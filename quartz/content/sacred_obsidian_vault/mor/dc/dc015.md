 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="../../img/tn_mor.jpg"></a><br><i>Doctrine and Covenants of the Church of Jesus Christ of the Latter-Day Saints</i>, at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">15. Revelation through Joseph Smith to John Whitmer, Fayette, New York, June 1829. <i><br> History of the Church</i> 1:50.</h3>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_15:1">15:1</a></font></span> Hearken, my servant John, and listen to the words of Jesus Christ, your Lord and your Redeemer.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_15:2">15:2</a></font></span> For behold, I speak unto you with sharpness and with power, for mine arm is over all the earth.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_15:3">15:3</a></font></span> And I will tell you that which no man knoweth save me and thee alone—</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_15:4">15:4</a></font></span> For many times you have desired of me to know that which would be of the most worth unto you.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_15:5">15:5</a></font></span> Behold, blessed are you for this thing, and for speaking my words which I have given you according to my commandments.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_15:6">15:6</a></font></span> And now, behold, I say unto you, that the thing which will be of the most worth unto you will be to declare repentance unto this people, that you may bring souls unto me, that you may rest with them in the kingdom of my Father. Amen. </p>
