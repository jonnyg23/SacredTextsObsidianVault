 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="../../img/tn_mor.jpg"></a><br><i>Doctrine and Covenants of the Church of Jesus Christ of the Latter-Day Saints</i>, at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">4. Revelation through Joseph Smith to his father, Joseph Smith, Sr., Harmony, Pennsylvania, February 1829. <i><br> History of the Church</i> 1:28.</h3>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_4:1">4:1</a></font></span> Now behold, a marvelous work is about to come forth among the children of men.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_4:2">4:2</a></font></span> Therefore, O ye that embark in the service of God, see that ye serve him with all your heart, might, mind and strength, that ye may stand blameless before God at the last day.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_4:3">4:3</a></font></span> Therefore, if ye have desires to serve God ye are called to the work;</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_4:4">4:4</a></font></span> For behold the field is white already to harvest; and lo, he that thrusteth in his sickle with his might, the same layeth up in store that he perisheth not, but bringeth salvation to his soul;</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_4:5">4:5</a></font></span> And faith, hope, charity and love, with an eye single to the glory of God, qualify him for the work.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_4:6">4:6</a></font></span> Remember faith, virtue, knowledge, temperance, patience, brotherly kindness, godliness, charity, humility, diligence.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_4:7">4:7</a></font></span> Ask, and ye shall receive; knock, and it shall be opened unto you. Amen. </p>
