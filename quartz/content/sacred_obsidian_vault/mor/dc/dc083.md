 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="../../img/tn_mor.jpg"></a><br><i>Doctrine and Covenants of the Church of Jesus Christ of the Latter-Day Saints</i>, at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">83. Revelation through Joseph Smith, Independence, Missouri, April 30, 1832. <i><br> History of the Church</i> 1:269-70.</h3>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_83:1">83:1</a></font></span> Verily, thus saith the Lord, in addition to the laws of the church concerning women and children, those who belong to the church, who have lost their husbands or fathers:</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_83:2">83:2</a></font></span> Women have claim on their husbands for their maintenance, until their husbands are taken; and if they are not found transgressors they shall have fellowship in the church.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_83:3">83:3</a></font></span> And if they are not faithful they shall not have fellowship in the church; yet they may remain upon their inheritances according to the laws of the land.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_83:4">83:4</a></font></span> All children have claim upon their parents for their maintenance until they are of age.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_83:5">83:5</a></font></span> And after that, they have claim upon the church, or in other words upon the Lord's storehouse, if their parents have not wherewith to give them inheritances.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_83:6">83:6</a></font></span> And the storehouse shall be kept by the consecrations of the church; and widows and orphans shall be provided for, as also the poor. Amen. </p>
