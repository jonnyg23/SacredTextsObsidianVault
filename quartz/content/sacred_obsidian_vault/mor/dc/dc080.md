 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="../../img/tn_mor.jpg"></a><br><i>Doctrine and Covenants of the Church of Jesus Christ of the Latter-Day Saints</i>, at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">80. Revelation through Joseph Smith, Hiram, Ohio, March 1832. <i><br> History of the Church</i> 1:257.</h3>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_80:1">80:1</a></font></span> Verily, thus saith the Lord unto you my servant Stephen Burnett: Go ye, go ye into the world and preach the gospel to every creature that cometh under the sound of your voice.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_80:2">80:2</a></font></span> And inasmuch as you desire a companion, I will give unto you my servant Eden Smith.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_80:3">80:3</a></font></span> Wherefore, go ye and preach my gospel, whether to the north or to the south, to the east or to the west, it mattereth not, for ye cannot go amiss.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_80:4">80:4</a></font></span> Therefore, declare the things which ye have heard, and verily believe, and know to be true.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_80:5">80:5</a></font></span> Behold, this is the will of him who hath called you, your Redeemer, even Jesus Christ. Amen. </p>
