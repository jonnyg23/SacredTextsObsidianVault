 <p>
 This is a short introduction to the Baha'i religion, part of the
 <a href="../../woe/index.htm">Wisdom of the East</a> series.
 It includes short but profound texts by the founders
 of the religion, including the Bab, Baha'u'llh and Abdul Baha
 (also known as Abbas Effendi).
 It includes a longer text, <cite>The Seven Valleys</cite> an allegorical
 tale of the journey of the soul.
 </p>
