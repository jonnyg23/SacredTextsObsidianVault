 <p>
 This is a short book on telling fortunes, primarily with a standard
 deck of playing cards.
 There is a short two-chapter section at the end about the Tarot.
 Described are several different spreads, including the 32-card method,
 the French and Italian methods, the Grand Star, and Etteilla's Tarot spread.
 </p>
