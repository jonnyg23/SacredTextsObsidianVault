 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_pe06"></a>
 <span align="CENTER">
 <a href="img/pe06.jpg"><img src="tn/pe06.jpg"><br><font size="-3">Click to enlarge</font></a></span></p> 
 <p>A person in the guise of a merchant weighs money in a pair of scales and distributes it to the needy and distressed. It is a testimony to his own success in life, as well as to his goodness of heart. <i>Divinatory Meanings</i>: Presents, gifts, gratification another account says attention, vigilance now is the accepted time, present prosperity, etc. <i>Reversed</i>: Desire, cupidity, envy, jealousy, illusion.</p>
