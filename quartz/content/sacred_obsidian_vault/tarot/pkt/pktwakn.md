 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_wakn"></a>
 <span align="CENTER">
 <a href="img/wakn.jpg"><img src="tn/wakn.jpg"><br><font size="-3">Click to enlarge</font></a></span></p> 
 <p>He is shewn as if upon a journey, armed with a short wand, and although mailed is not on a warlike errand. He is passing mounds or pyramids. The motion of the horse is a key to the character of its rider, and suggests the precipitate mood, or things connected therewith. <i>Divinatory Meanings</i>: Departure, absence, flight, emigration. A dark young man, friendly. Change of residence. <i>Reversed</i>: Rupture, division, interruption, discord.</p>
