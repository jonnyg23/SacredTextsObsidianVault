 <p>In a scene similar to the former, a young man stands in the act of proclamation. He is unknown but faithful, and his tidings are strange. <i>Divinatory Meanings</i>: Dark young man, faithful, a lover, an envoy, a postman. Beside a man, he will bear favourable testimony concerning him. A dangerous rival, if followed by the Page of Cups. Has the chief qualities of his suit. He may signify family intelligence. <i>Reversed</i>: Anecdotes, announcements, evil news. Also indecision and the instability which accompanies it.</p>
