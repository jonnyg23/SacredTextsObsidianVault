 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_wa07"></a>
 <span align="CENTER">
 <a href="img/wa07.jpg"><img src="tn/wa07.jpg"><br><font size="-3">Click to enlarge</font></a></span></p> 
 <p>A young man on a craggy eminence brandishing a staff; six other staves are raised towards him from below. <i>Divinatory Meanings</i>: It is a card of valour, for, on the surface, six are attacking one, who has, however, the vantage position. On the intellectual plane, it signifies discussion, wordy strife; in business--negotiations, war of trade, barter, competition. It is further a card of success, for the combatant is on the top and his enemies may be unable to reach him. <i>Reversed</i>: Perplexity, embarrassments, anxiety. It is also a caution against indecision.</p>
