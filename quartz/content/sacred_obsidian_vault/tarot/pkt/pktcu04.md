 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_cu04"></a>
 <span align="CENTER">
 <a href="img/cu04.jpg"><img src="tn/cu04.jpg"><br><font size="-3">Click to enlarge</font></a></span></p> 
 <p>A young man is seated under a tree and contemplates three cups set on the grass before him; an arm issuing from a cloud offers him another cup. His expression notwithstanding is one of discontent with his environment. <i>Divinatory Meanings</i>: Weariness, disgust, aversion, imaginary vexations, as if the wine of this world had caused satiety only; another wine, as if a fairy gift, is now offered the wastrel, but he sees no consolation therein. This is also a card of blended pleasure. <i>Reversed</i>: Novelty, presage, new instruction, new relations.</p>
