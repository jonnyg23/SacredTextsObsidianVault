 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_sw09"></a>
 <span align="CENTER">
 <a href="img/sw09.jpg"><img src="tn/sw09.jpg"><br><font size="-3">Click to enlarge</font></a></span></p> 
 <p>One seated on her couch in lamentation, with the swords over her. She is as one who knows no sorrow which is like unto hers. It is a card of utter desolation. <i>Divinatory Meanings</i>: Death, failure, miscarriage, delay, deception, disappointment, despair. <i>Reversed</i>: Imprisonment, suspicion, doubt, reasonable fear, shame.</p>
