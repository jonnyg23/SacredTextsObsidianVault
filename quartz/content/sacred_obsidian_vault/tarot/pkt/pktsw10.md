 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_sw10"></a>
 <span align="CENTER">
 <a href="img/sw10.jpg"><img src="tn/sw10.jpg"><br><font size="-3">Click to enlarge</font></a></span></p> 
 <p>A prostrate figure, pierced by all the swords belonging to the card. <i>Divinatory Meanings</i>: Whatsoever is intimated by the design; also pain, affliction, tears, sadness, desolation. It is not especially a card of violent death. <i>Reversed</i>: Advantage, profit, success, favour, but none of these are permanent; also power and authority.</p>
