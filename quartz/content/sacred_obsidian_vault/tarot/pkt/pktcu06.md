 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_cu06"></a>
 <span align="CENTER">
 <a href="img/cu06.jpg"><img src="tn/cu06.jpg"><br><font size="-3">Click to enlarge</font></a></span></p> 
 <p>Children in an old garden, their cups filled with flowers. <i>Divinatory Meanings</i>: A card of the past and of memories, looking back, as--for example--on childhood; happiness, enjoyment, but coming rather from the past; things that have vanished. Another reading reverses this, giving new relations, new knowledge, new environment, and then the children are disporting in an unfamiliar precinct. <i>Reversed</i>: The future, renewal, that which will come to pass presently.</p>
