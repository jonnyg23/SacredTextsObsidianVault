 <h3>III 1</h3>

 The great Neptune in his highest belfry:<br>
 Red adversary will become pale with fear,<br>
 Putting the great Ocean in dread.<br>

 <p>
 The divine word will give to the sustenance,<br>
 Including heaven, earth, gold hidden in the mystic milk:<br>
 Body, soul, spirit having all power,<br>
 As much under its feet as the Heavenly see.<br>

 <p>
 Mars and Mercury, and the silver joined together,<br>
 Towards the south extreme drought:<br>

 Dans Auignon tout le chef de l'empire,<br>
 Fera apprest pour Paris desolé :<br>
 Tricast tiendra l'Annibalique ire,<br>
 Luon par change sera mal consolé.<br>
 <p>
 In Avignon the chief of the whole empire<br>
 Will make a stop on the way to desolated Paris:<br>
 Tricast will hold the anger of Hannibal:<br>
 Lyons will be poorly consoled for the change.<br>
 </p><p>
 </p><h3>III 94</h3>
 De cinq cens ans plus compte l'on tiendra,<br>
 Celuy qu'estoit l'ornement de son temps :<br>
 Puis à vn coup grande clarté donrra,<br>
 Que par ce siecle les rendra trescontens.<br>
 <p>
 For five hundred years more one will keep count of him<br>
 Who was the ornament of his time:<br>
 Then suddenly great light will he give,<br>
 He who for this century will render them very satisfied.<br>
 </p><p>
 </p><h3>III 95</h3>
 La loy Moricque on verra deffaillir,<br>
 Apres vne autre beaucoup plus seductiue,<br>
 Boristhenes premier viendra faillir,<br>
 Par dons &amp; Langues vne plus attractiue.<br>
 <p>
 The law of More will be seen to decline:<br>
 After another much more seductive:<br>
 Dnieper first will come to give way:<br>
 Through gifts and tongue another more attractive.<br>
 </p><p>
 </p><h3>III 96</h3>
 Chef de Fossan aura gorge couppee,<br>
 Par le ducteur du limier &amp; leurier :<br>
 La fait paré par ceux du mont Tarpee,<br>
 Saturne en leo treziesme Feurier.<br>
 <p>
 The Chief of Fossano will have his throat cut<br>
 By the leader of the bloodhound and greyhound:<br>
 The deed executed by those of the Tarpeian Rock,<br>
 Saturn in Leo February 13.<br>
 </p><p>
 </p><h3>III 97</h3>
 Nouuelle loy terre neuue occuper,<br>
 Vers la Syrie, Iudee, &amp; Palestine,<br>
 Le grand Empire Barbare corruer,<br>
 Auant que Pheses son siecle determeine.<br>
 <p>
 New law to occupy the new land<br>
 Towards Syria, Judea and Palestine:<br>
 The great barbarian empire to decay,<br>
 Before the Moon completes it cycle.<br>
 </p><p>

 <p>

 Qu'entr'eux sera la guerre si mortelle,<br>
 Qu'vn chacun places fortes occuperont,<br>
 De regne &amp; vie sera leur grand querelle.<br>
 </p><p>
 Deux royals freres i forr guerroyeront,<br>
 Qu'entre eux era la guerre i mortelle,<br>
 Qu'vn chacun place-fortes occuperont :<br>
 De regne &amp; vie era leur grand querelle.<br>
 </p><p>
 Two royal brothers will wage war so fiercely<br>
 That between them the war will be so mortal<br>
 That both will occupy the strong places:<br>
 Their great quarrel will fill realm and life.<br>
 </p><p>
 </p><h3>III 99</h3>
 Aux champs herbeux d'alein &amp; du Varneigne,<br>
 Dumont Lebron proche de la Durance<br>
 Camp des deux parts conflit sera si aigre,<br>
 Mesopotamie deffaillira en la France.<br>
 <p>
 In the grassy fields of Alleins and Vernègues<br>
 Of the Lubéron range near the Durance,<br>
 The conflict will be very sharp for both armies,<br>
 Mesopotamia will fail in France.<br>
 </p><p>
 </p><h3>III 100</h3>
 Entre Gaulois le dernier honoré,<br>
 D'homme ennemy  sera victorieux,<br>
 Force &amp; terroir en moment exploré,<br>
 D'vn coup de trait quand mourra l'enuieux.<br>
 <p>
 The last one honored amongst the Gauls,<br>
 Over the enemy man will he be victorious:<br>
 Force and land in a moment explored,<br>
 When the envious one will die from an arrow shot.<br>
