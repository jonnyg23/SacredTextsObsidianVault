 <p><a name="page_19"><font size="1" color="green">p. 19</font></a></p>  <h2 align="center">THE LIFE AND MORALS</h2>  <h6 align="center">OF</h6>  <h1 align="center">JESUS OF NAZARETH.</h1>  
 <p align="center">I.</p>  
 <p align="center"><small><i>Joseph and Mary go to Bethlehem, Where Jesus is Born</i>.</small></p>  
 <p>AND it came to pass in those days, that there went out a decree from Caesar Augustus, that all the world should be taxed.</p>  
 <p>2 <i>(And</i> this taxing ways first made when Cyrenius was governor of Syria.)</p>  
 <p>3 And all went to be taxed, every one into his own city.</p>  
 <p>4 And Joseph also went up from Galilee, out of the city of Nazareth, into Judaea, unto the city of</p>  
 <p><a name="page_20"><font size="1" color="green">p. 20</font></a></p>  
 <p><span class="contnote"><font size="-2" color="GREEN">[paragraph continues]</font></span> David, which is called Bethlehem, (because he was of the house and lineage of David,)</p>  
 <p>5 To be taxed with Mary his espoused wife, being great with child.</p>  
 <p>6 And so it was, that, while they were there, the days were accomplished that she should be delivered.</p>  
 <p>7 And she brought forth her firstborn son, and wrapped him in swaddling clothes, and laid him in a manger; because there was no room for them in the inn.</p>
