 <p><a name="page_45"><font size="1" color="green">p. 45</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">XII.</h3>  
 <p align="center"><small><i>Exhorts</i>.</small></p>  
 <p>WHEN he was come down from the mountain, great multitudes followed him.</p>  
 <p>2 And he marvelled because of their unbelief. And he went round about the villages, teaching.</p>  
 <p>3 ¶ Come unto me, all <i>ye</i> that labour and are heavy laden, and I will give you rest.</p>  
 <p>4 Take my yoke upon you, and learn of me; for I am meek and lowly in heart: and ye shall find rest unto your souls.</p>  
 <p>5 For my yoke <i>is</i> easy, and my burden is light.</p>
