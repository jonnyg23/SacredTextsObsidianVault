 <p>24. And the Lord opened seven flood-gates <a name="fr_201"></a><a href="#fn_201"><font size="1">5</font></a> of heaven,<br> And the mouths of the fountains of the great deep, seven mouths in number.</p>  
 <p>25. And the flood-gates began to pour down water from the heaven forty days and forty nights,<br> And the fountains of the deep also sent up waters, until the whole world was full of water.</p>  
 <p>26.
