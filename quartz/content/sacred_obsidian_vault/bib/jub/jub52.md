   <h3 align="center">Renewed Study of the Law followed by a Renewal of Mankind. The Messianic Kingdom and the Blessedness of the Righteous (xxiii. 26-32; cf. Isa. lxv. 17 ff. [Eschatological.]</h3>  
 <p>26. And in those days the children will begin to study the laws,<br> And to seek the commandments,<br> And to return to the path of righteousness.<br> 27. And the days will begin to grow many and increase amongst those children of men, <a name="page_132"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 132</font></a><br> Till their days draw nigh to one thousand years, <a name="fr_630"></a><a href="#fn_628"><font size="1">1</font></a><br> And to a greater number of years than (before) was the number of the days.<br> 28. And there will be no old man<br> Nor one who is not satisfied with his days,<br> For all will be (as) children and youths. <a name="fr_631"></a><a href="#fn_629"><font size="1">2</font></a><br> 29. And all their days they will complete and live in peace and in joy, <a name="fr_632"></a><a href="#fn_630"><font size="1">3</font></a><br> And there will be no Satan <a name="fr_633"></a><a href="#fn_631"><font size="1">4</font></a> nor any evil destroyer;<br> For all their days will be days of blessing and healing, <a name="fr_634"></a><a href="#fn_632"><font size="1">5</font></a><br> 30. And at that time the Lord will heal His servants,<br> And they will rise up <a name="fr_635"></a><a href="#fn_633"><font size="1">6</font></a> and see <a name="fr_636"></a><a href="#fn_634"><font size="1">7</font></a> great peace,<br> And drive out their adversaries.<br> And the righteous will see and be thankful,<br> And rejoice with joy for ever and ever,<br> And will see all their judgments and all their curses on their enemies.<br> 31. And their bones will rest in the earth,<br> And their spirits will have much joy, <a name="fr_637"></a><a href="#fn_635"><font size="1">8</font></a><br> And they will know that it is the Lord who executeth judgment,<br> And showeth mercy to hundreds and thousands and to all that love Him. <a name="fr_638"></a><a href="#fn_636"><font size="1">9</font></a></p>  
 <p>32. And do thou, Moses, write down these words; for thus are they written, and they record (them) on the heavenly tables for a testimony for the generations for ever.</p>
