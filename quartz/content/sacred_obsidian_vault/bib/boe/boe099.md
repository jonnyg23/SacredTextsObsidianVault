 <p align="CENTER">XCV. <i>Enoch's Grief: fresh Woes against the Sinners</i>.</p> <h3 align="CENTER">CHAPTER XCV.</h3><dir> <dir>  
 <p>1. Oh that mine eyes were <font color="GREEN">[</font>a cloud of<font color="GREEN">]</font> waters<br> That I might weep over you,<br> And pour down my tears as a cloud †of† waters:<br> That so I might rest from my trouble of heart!</p> 
 <p>2. †Who has permitted you to practice reproaches and wickedness?<br> And so judgement shall overtake you, sinners. †</p> 
 <p>3. Fear not the sinners, ye righteous;<br> For again will the Lord deliver them into your hands,<br> That ye may execute judgement upon them according to your desires.</p> 
 <p>4. Woe to you who fulminate anathemas which cannot be reversed:<br> Healing shall therefore be far from you because of your sins.</p> 
 <p>5. Woe to you who requite your neighbour with evil;<br> For ye shall be requited according to your works.</p> 
 <p>6. Woe to you, lying witnesses,<br> And to those who weigh out injustice,<br> For suddenly shall ye perish.</p> 
 <p>7. Woe to you, sinners, for ye persecute the righteous;<br> For ye shall be delivered up and persecuted because of injustice,<br> And heavy shall its yoke be upon you.</p>
