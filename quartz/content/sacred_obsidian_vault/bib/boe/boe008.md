 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Book of Enoch</i>, by R.H. Charles, [1917], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="CENTER">CHAPTER V.</h3> 
 <p>1. Observe <font color="GREEN">⌈⌈</font>ye<font color="GREEN">⌉⌉</font> how the trees cover themselves with green leaves and bear fruit: wherefore give ye heed <font color="GREEN">⌈</font>and know<font color="GREEN">⌉</font> with regard to all <font color="GREEN">⌈</font>His works<font color="GREEN">⌉</font>, and recognize how He that liveth for ever hath made them so.</p> 
 <p>2. And <font color="GREEN">⌈</font>all<font color="GREEN">⌉</font> His works go on <font color="GREEN">⌈</font>thus<font color="GREEN">⌉</font> from year to year for ever, and all the tasks <font color="GREEN">⌈</font>which<font color="GREEN">⌉</font> they accomplish for Him, and <font color="GREEN">⌈</font>their tasks<font color="GREEN">⌉</font> change not, but according as <font color="GREEN">⌈⌈</font>God<font color="GREEN">⌉⌉</font> hath ordained so is it done.</p> 
 <p>3. And behold how the sea and the rivers in like manner accomplish and <font color="GREEN">⌈</font>change not<font color="GREEN">⌉</font> their tasks <font color="GREEN">⌈</font>from His commandments<font color="GREEN">⌉</font>.</p><dir> <dir>  
 <p>4. But ye--ye have not been steadfast, nor done the commandments of the Lord,<br> But ye have turned away and spoken proud and hard words<br> With your impure mouths against His greatness.<br> Oh, ye hard-hearted, ye shall find no peace.</p> 
 <p>5. Therefore shall ye execrate your days,<br> And the years of your life shall perish,<br> And <font color="GREEN">⌈</font>the years of your destruction<font color="GREEN">⌉</font> shall be multiplied in eternal execration,<br> And ye shall find no mercy.</p> 
 <p>6<i>a</i>. In those days ye shall make your names an eternal execration unto all the righteous,<br> <i>b</i>. And by you shall <font color="GREEN">⌈</font>all<font color="GREEN">⌉</font> who curse, curse,<br> And all the sinners <font color="GREEN">⌈</font>and godless<font color="GREEN">⌉</font> shall imprecate by you,<br> 7<i>c</i>. And for you the godless there shall be a curse.</p> 
 <p>6<i>d</i>. And all the . . . shall rejoice,<br> <i>e</i>. And there shall be forgiveness of sins,<br> <i>f</i>. And every mercy and peace and forbearance: <a name="page_34"><font size="1" color="green">p. 34</font></a><br> <i>g</i>. There shall be salvation unto them, a goodly light.</p> <i>
 </i><p>i. And for all of you sinners there shall be no salvation,<br> <i>j</i> But on you all shall abide a curse.<br> 7<i>a</i>. But for the elect there shall be light and joy and peace,<br> <i>b</i>. And they shall inherit the earth.</p> 
 <p>8 And then there shall be bestowed upon the elect wisdom,<br> And they shall all live and never again sin,<br> Either through ungodliness or through pride:<br> But they who are wise shall be humble.</p> 
 <p>9 And they shall not again transgress,<br> Nor shall they sin all the days of their life,<br> Nor shall they die of (the divine) anger or wrath,<br> But they shall complete the number of the days of their life.</p> 
 <p>And their lives shall be increased in peace,<br> And the years of their joy shall be multiplied,<br> In eternal gladness and peace,<br> All the days of their life.</p></dir> </dir>
