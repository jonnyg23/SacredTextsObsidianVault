 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Geneva Bible Translation Notes</i>, [1599], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/kg1005.htm#001">3 Kings (1 Kings) Chapter 5</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/kg1005.htm#004">3 Kings (1 Kings) 5:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">kg1 5:4</a></font></span></p>But now the LORD my God hath given me (a) rest on every side, [so that there is] neither adversary nor evil occurrent. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(a) He declares that he was bound to set forth God's glory for as much as the Lord had sent him rest and peace. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/kg1005.htm#006">3 Kings (1 Kings) 5:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">kg1 5:6</a></font></span></p>Now therefore command thou that they hew me cedar trees out of Lebanon; and my servants shall be with thy servants: and unto thee will I give (b) hire for thy servants according to all that thou shalt appoint: for thou knowest that [there is] not among us any that can skill to hew timber like unto the Sidonians. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(b) This was his equity, that he would not receive a benefit without some recompence. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/kg1005.htm#007">3 Kings (1 Kings) 5:7</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_007">kg1 5:7</a></font></span></p>And it came to pass, when (c) Hiram heard the words of Solomon, that he rejoiced greatly, and said, Blessed [be] the LORD this day, which hath given unto David a wise son over this great people. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(c) In Hiram is prefigured the calling of the Gentiles who would help build the spiritual temple. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/kg1005.htm#009">3 Kings (1 Kings) 5:9</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_009">kg1 5:9</a></font></span></p>My servants shall bring [them] down from Lebanon unto the sea: and I will convey them by sea in floats unto the place that thou shalt appoint me, and will cause them to be discharged there, and thou shalt receive [them]: and thou shalt accomplish my desire, in giving food for (d) my household. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(d) While my servants are occupied with your business. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/kg1005.htm#012">3 Kings (1 Kings) 5:12</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_012">kg1 5:12</a></font></span></p>And the LORD gave Solomon wisdom, as he promised him: and there was peace between Hiram and Solomon; and they (e) two made a league together. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(e) Concerning the furniture of wood. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/kg1005.htm#018">3 Kings (1 Kings) 5:18</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_018">kg1 5:18</a></font></span></p>And Solomon's builders and Hiram's (f) builders did hew [them], and the stonesquarers: so they prepared timber and stones to build the house. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(f) The Hebrew word is Giblim, which some say were excellent masons.
