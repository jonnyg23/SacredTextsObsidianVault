 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Geneva Bible Translation Notes</i>, [1599], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro031.htm#001">Proverbs Chapter 31</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro031.htm#001">Proverbs 31:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">pro 31:1</a></font></span></p>The words of king (a) Lemuel, the (b) prophecy that his mother taught him. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(a) That is, of Solomon who was called Lemuel, that is, of God, because God had ordained him to be king over Israel. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(b) The doctrine which his mother Bathsheba taught him.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro031.htm#002">Proverbs 31:2</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_002">pro 31:2</a></font></span></p>What, my son? and what, the son of (c) my womb? and what, the son of my vows? 
 <p>
 </p><p>(c) By this often repetition of one thing, she declares her motherly affection.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro031.htm#003">Proverbs 31:3</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_003">pro 31:3</a></font></span></p>Give not thy strength to women, (d) nor thy ways to that which destroyeth kings. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(d) Meaning, that women are the destruction of kings, if they hunt them.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro031.htm#004">Proverbs 31:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">pro 31:4</a></font></span></p>[It is] not for kings, O Lemuel, [it is] not for kings to drink wine; nor for princes (e) strong drink: 
 <p>
 </p><p>(e) That is, the king must not give himself to wantonness, and neglect of his office, which is to execute judgment.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro031.htm#007">Proverbs 31:7</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_007">pro 31:7</a></font></span></p>Let him drink, and forget (f) his poverty, and remember his misery no more. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(f) For wine comforts the heart as in (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa104.htm#015">Psa 104:15</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro031.htm#008">Proverbs 31:8</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_008">pro 31:8</a></font></span></p>Open thy mouth for the (g) dumb in the cause of all such as are appointed to destruction. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(g) Defend their cause that are not able to help themselves.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro031.htm#011">Proverbs 31:11</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_011">pro 31:11</a></font></span></p>The heart of her husband doth safely trust in her, so that he shall have no need of (h) spoil. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(h) He will not need to use any unlawful means to gain his living.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro031.htm#015">Proverbs 31:15</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_015">pro 31:15</a></font></span></p>She riseth also while it is yet night, and giveth food to her household, and a (i) portion to her maidens. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(i) She prepares their food early.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro031.htm#016">Proverbs 31:16</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_016">pro 31:16</a></font></span></p>She considereth a field, and (k) buyeth it: with the fruit of her hands she planteth a vineyard. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(k) She purchases it with the gains of her travail.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro031.htm#023">Proverbs 31:23</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_023">pro 31:23</a></font></span></p>Her husband is known in the (l) gates, when he sitteth among the elders of the land. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(l) In the assemblies and places of judgment.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro031.htm#025">Proverbs 31:25</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_025">pro 31:25</a></font></span></p>(m) Strength and honour [are] her clothing; and she shall rejoice in time to come. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(m) After he had spoken of the apparel of the body, he now declares the apparel of the spirit.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro031.htm#026">Proverbs 31:26</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_026">pro 31:26</a></font></span></p>She openeth her mouth with wisdom; and on her tongue [is] the (n) law of kindness. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(n) Her tongue is a book by which one might learn many good things: for she delights to talk of the word of God.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro031.htm#028">Proverbs 31:28</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_028">pro 31:28</a></font></span></p>Her children rise up, and (o) call her blessed; her husband [also], and he praiseth her. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(o) That is, do her reverence.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro031.htm#031">Proverbs 31:31</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_031">pro 31:31</a></font></span></p>Give (p) her of the fruit of her hands; and let her own works praise her in the (q) gates. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(p) Confess her diligent labours, and commend her therefore. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(q) Forasmuch as the most honourable are clad in the apparel that she made.  
 </p><p></p>
