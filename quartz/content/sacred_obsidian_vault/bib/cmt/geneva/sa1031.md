 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Geneva Bible Translation Notes</i>, [1599], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa1031.htm#001">1 Kings (1 Samuel) Chapter 31</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa1031.htm#004">1 Kings (1 Samuel) 31:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">sa1 31:4</a></font></span></p>Then said Saul unto his armourbearer, (a) Draw thy sword, and thrust me through therewith; lest these uncircumcised come and thrust me through, and abuse me. But his armourbearer would not; for he was sore afraid. Therefore Saul took a sword, and fell upon it. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(a) So we see that his cruel life has a desperate end, as is commonly seen in those who persecute the children of God. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa1031.htm#007">1 Kings (1 Samuel) 31:7</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_007">sa1 31:7</a></font></span></p>And when the men of Israel that [were] on the other side of the (b) valley, and [they] that [were] on the other side (c) Jordan, saw that the men of Israel fled, and that Saul and his sons were dead, they forsook the cities, and fled; and the Philistines came and dwelt in them. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(b) Near to Gilboa. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(c) The tribes of Reuben and Gad, and half the tribe of Manasseh. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa1031.htm#009">1 Kings (1 Samuel) 31:9</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_009">sa1 31:9</a></font></span></p>And they cut off his head, and stripped off his armour, and sent into the land of the Philistines round about, to (d) publish [it in] the house of their idols, and among the people. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(d) In token of victory and triumph. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa1031.htm#011">1 Kings (1 Samuel) 31:11</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_011">sa1 31:11</a></font></span></p>And when the inhabitants of (e) Jabeshgilead heard of that which the Philistines had done to Saul; 
 <p>
 </p><p>(e) Whom he had delivered from their enemies, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa1011.htm#011">Sa1 11:11</a>). 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa1031.htm#013">1 Kings (1 Samuel) 31:13</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_013">sa1 31:13</a></font></span></p>And they took their bones, and buried [them] under a tree at Jabesh, and (f) fasted seven days. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(f) According to the custom of mourners.
