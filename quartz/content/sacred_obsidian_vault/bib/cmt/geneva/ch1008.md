 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Geneva Bible Translation Notes</i>, [1599], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1008.htm#001">1 Chronicles Chapter 8</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1008.htm#001">1 Chronicles 8:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">ch1 8:1</a></font></span></p>Now Benjamin (a) begat Bela his firstborn, Ashbel the second, and Aharah the third, 
 <p>
 </p><p>(a) He continues in the description of the tribe of Benjamin, because his purpose is to set forth the genealogy of Saul. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1008.htm#006">1 Chronicles 8:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">ch1 8:6</a></font></span></p>And these [are] the sons of Ehud: these are the heads of the fathers of the inhabitants of Geba, and (b) they removed them to Manahath: 
 <p>
 </p><p>(b) Meaning, the inhabitants of the city of Geba. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1008.htm#007">1 Chronicles 8:7</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_007">ch1 8:7</a></font></span></p>And Naaman, and Ahiah, and Gera, he removed them, and (c) begat Uzza, and Ahihud. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(c) That is, Ehud. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1008.htm#008">1 Chronicles 8:8</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_008">ch1 8:8</a></font></span></p>And Shaharaim begat [children] in the country of Moab, after he had sent them (d) away; Hushim and Baara [were] his wives. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(d) After he had put away his two wives. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1008.htm#028">1 Chronicles 8:28</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_028">ch1 8:28</a></font></span></p>These [were] heads of the (e) fathers, by their generations, chief [men]. These dwelt in Jerusalem. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(e) The chief of the tribe of Benjamin, who dwelt in Jerusalem. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1008.htm#033">1 Chronicles 8:33</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_033">ch1 8:33</a></font></span></p>And (f) Ner begat Kish, and Kish begat Saul, and Saul begat Jonathan, and Malchishua, and Abinadab, and (g) Eshbaal. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(f) Who in (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa1009.htm#002">Sa1 9:2</a>) is called Abiel. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(g) He is likewise called Mephibosheth, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa2009.htm#006">Sa2 9:6</a>). 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1008.htm#034">1 Chronicles 8:34</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_034">ch1 8:34</a></font></span></p>And the son of Jonathan [was] (h) Meribbaal; and Meribbaal begat Micah. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(h) He is also called Mephibosheth, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa2009.htm#006">Sa2 9:6</a>).
