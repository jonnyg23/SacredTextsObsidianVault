 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Geneva Bible Translation Notes</i>, [1599], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1009.htm#001">1 Chronicles Chapter 9</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1009.htm#001">1 Chronicles 9:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">ch1 9:1</a></font></span></p>So all Israel were reckoned by genealogies; and, behold, they [were] written in the book of the kings of Israel and Judah, [who] were (a) carried away to Babylon for their transgression. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(a) Until now he has described their genealogies before they went into captivity, and now he describes their history after their return. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1009.htm#002">1 Chronicles 9:2</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_002">ch1 9:2</a></font></span></p>Now the first inhabitants that [dwelt] in their possessions in their cities [were], the Israelites, the priests, Levites, and the (b) Nethinims. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(b) Meaning, the Gibeonites, who served in the temple, read (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/jos009.htm#023">Jos 9:23</a>). 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1009.htm#011">1 Chronicles 9:11</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_011">ch1 9:11</a></font></span></p>And Azariah the son of Hilkiah, the son of Meshullam, the son of Zadok, the son of Meraioth, the son of Ahitub, the (c) ruler of the house of God; 
 <p>
 </p><p>(c) That is, he was the high priest. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1009.htm#013">1 Chronicles 9:13</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_013">ch1 9:13</a></font></span></p>And their brethren, heads of the house of their fathers, a thousand and seven hundred and threescore; very able men for the (d) work of the service of the house of God. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(d) To serve in the temple, every one according to his office. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1009.htm#018">1 Chronicles 9:18</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_018">ch1 9:18</a></font></span></p>Who hitherto [waited] in the (e) king's gate eastward: they [were] porters in the companies of the children of Levi. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(e) So called because the king came into the temple by it, and not the common people. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1009.htm#019">1 Chronicles 9:19</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_019">ch1 9:19</a></font></span></p>And Shallum the son of Kore, the son of Ebiasaph, the son of Korah, and his brethren, of the house of his father, the Korahites, [were] over the work of the service, keepers of the gates of the (f) tabernacle: and their fathers, [being] over the host of the LORD, [were] keepers of the entry. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(f) Their charge was that no one should enter those places, which were only appointed for the priests to minister in. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1009.htm#025">1 Chronicles 9:25</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_025">ch1 9:25</a></font></span></p>And their brethren, [which were] in their villages, [were] to come after (g) seven days from time to time with them. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(g) They served weekly, as in (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo029.htm#030">Exo 29:30</a>). 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1009.htm#029">1 Chronicles 9:29</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_029">ch1 9:29</a></font></span></p>[Some] of them also [were] appointed to oversee the vessels, and all the instruments of the sanctuary, and the fine (h) flour, and the wine, and the oil, and the frankincense, and the spices. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(h) Of which the meat offering was made, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev002.htm#008">Lev 2:8</a>). 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1009.htm#033">1 Chronicles 9:33</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_033">ch1 9:33</a></font></span></p>And these [are] the singers, chief of the fathers of the Levites, [who remaining] in the chambers (i) [were] free: for they were employed in [that] work day and night. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(i) But were continually occupied in singing praises to God. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1009.htm#042">1 Chronicles 9:42</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_042">ch1 9:42</a></font></span></p>And Ahaz begat (k) Jarah; and Jarah begat Alemeth, and Azmaveth, and Zimri; and Zimri begat Moza; 
 <p>
 </p><p>(k) Who was also called Jehoiada, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1008.htm#036">Ch1 8:36</a>).
