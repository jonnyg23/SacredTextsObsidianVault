 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Geneva Bible Translation Notes</i>, [1599], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jos015.htm#001">Joshua Chapter 15</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jos015.htm#002">Joshua 15:2</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_002">jos 15:2</a></font></span></p>And their south border was from the shore of the salt sea, from the (a) bay that looketh southward: 
 <p>
 </p><p>(a) The Hebrew word signifies tongue, by which it means either the arm of the sea that comes into the land, or a rock, or cape that goes into the sea.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jos015.htm#005">Joshua 15:5</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_005">jos 15:5</a></font></span></p>And the east border [was] the salt sea, [even] unto the (b) end of Jordan. And [their] border in the north quarter [was] from the bay of the sea at the uttermost part of Jordan: 
 <p>
 </p><p>(b) Meaning the mouth of the river where it runs into the Salt sea.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jos015.htm#006">Joshua 15:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">jos 15:6</a></font></span></p>And the border went up to Bethhogla, and passed along by the north of Betharabah; and the border went up to the (c) stone of Bohan the son of Reuben: 
 <p>
 </p><p>(c) Which was a mark to part their countries.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jos015.htm#011">Joshua 15:11</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_011">jos 15:11</a></font></span></p>And the border went out unto the side of Ekron northward: and the border was drawn to Shicron, and passed along to mount Baalah, and went out unto Jabneel; and the goings out of the border were at the (d) sea. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(d) Meaning, toward Syria.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jos015.htm#014">Joshua 15:14</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_014">jos 15:14</a></font></span></p>And Caleb (e) drove thence the three sons of Anak, Sheshai, and Ahiman, and Talmai, the children of Anak. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(e) This was done after the death of Joshua, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/jdg001.htm#010">Jdg 1:10</a>, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/jdg001.htm#020">Jdg 1:20</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jos015.htm#018">Joshua 15:18</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_018">jos 15:18</a></font></span></p>And it came to pass, as she came [unto him], that she moved him to ask of her father a field: (f) and she lighted off [her] ass; and Caleb said unto her, What wouldest thou? 
 <p>
 </p><p>(f) Because her husband tarried too long.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jos015.htm#019">Joshua 15:19</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_019">jos 15:19</a></font></span></p>Who answered, Give me a blessing; for thou hast given me a south land; (g) give me also springs of water. And he gave her the upper springs, and the nether springs. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(g) Because her country was barren, she wanted a field that had springs from her father. (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/jdg001.htm#014">Jdg 1:14-15</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jos015.htm#030">Joshua 15:30</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_030">jos 15:30</a></font></span></p>And Eltolad, and Chesil, and (h) Hormah, 
 <p>
 </p><p>(h) Which before was called Zephath, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/jdg001.htm#017">Jdg 1:17</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jos015.htm#047">Joshua 15:47</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_047">jos 15:47</a></font></span></p>Ashdod with her towns and her villages, Gaza with her towns and her villages, unto the (i) river of Egypt, and the great sea, and the border [thereof]: 
 <p>
 </p><p>(i) Meaning, the Nile (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/jos013.htm#003">Jos 13:3</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jos015.htm#049">Joshua 15:49</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_049">jos 15:49</a></font></span></p>And Dannah, and (k) Kirjathsannah, which [is] Debir, 
 <p>
 </p><p>(k) Which is also called Kirjath-sepher, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/jos015.htm#015">Jos 15:15</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jos015.htm#062">Joshua 15:62</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_062">jos 15:62</a></font></span></p>And Nibshan, and the (l) city of Salt, and Engedi; six cities with their villages. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(l) Of this city the salt sea has it's name.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jos015.htm#063">Joshua 15:63</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_063">jos 15:63</a></font></span></p>As for the Jebusites the inhabitants of Jerusalem, the children of Judah could not drive them (m) out: but the Jebusites dwell with the children of Judah at Jerusalem unto this day. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(m) That is, utterly, though they slew the most part, and burnt their city, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/jdg001.htm#008">Jdg 1:8</a>).
