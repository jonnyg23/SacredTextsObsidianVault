 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Geneva Bible Translation Notes</i>, [1599], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa030.htm#001">Psalms Chapter 30</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa030.htm#001">Psalms 30:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">psa 30:1</a></font></span></p>"A Psalm [and] Song [at] the dedication of the (a) house of David." I will extol thee, O LORD; (b) for thou hast lifted me up, and hast not made my foes to rejoice over me. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(a) After Absalom had polluted it with most filthy fornication. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(b) He condemns them for great ingratitude who do not praise God for his benefits.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa030.htm#002">Psalms 30:2</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_002">psa 30:2</a></font></span></p>O LORD my God, I cried unto thee, and thou hast (c) healed me. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(c) Restored from the rebellion of Absalom.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa030.htm#003">Psalms 30:3</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_003">psa 30:3</a></font></span></p>O LORD, thou hast brought up my (d) soul from the grave: thou hast kept me alive, that I should not go down to the pit. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(d) Meaning, that he escaped death most narrowly.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa030.htm#004">Psalms 30:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">psa 30:4</a></font></span></p>Sing unto the LORD, O ye (e) saints of his, and give thanks at the remembrance of his holiness. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(e) The word signifies them who have received mercy, and show mercy liberally to others.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa030.htm#006">Psalms 30:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">psa 30:6</a></font></span></p>And in my (g) prosperity I said, I shall never be moved. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(g) I put too much confidence in my quiet state as in (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer031.htm#018">Jer 31:18</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch2032.htm#024">Ch2 32:24-25</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa030.htm#007">Psalms 30:7</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_007">psa 30:7</a></font></span></p>LORD, by thy favour thou hast made my (h) mountain to stand strong: thou didst hide thy face, [and] I (i) was troubled. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(h) I thought you had established me in Zion most surely. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(i) After you had withdrawn your help, I felt my misery.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa030.htm#009">Psalms 30:9</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_009">psa 30:9</a></font></span></p>What profit [is there] in my blood, when I go down to the pit? Shall the (k) dust praise thee? shall it declare thy truth? 
 <p>
 </p><p>(k) David means that the dead are not profitable to the congregation of the Lord here in the earth: therefore he would live to praise his Name, which is the end of man's creation.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa030.htm#012">Psalms 30:12</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_012">psa 30:12</a></font></span></p>To the end that [my] (l) glory may sing praise to thee, and not be silent. O LORD my God, I will give thanks unto thee for ever. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(l) Because you have preserved me that my tongue should praise you, I will not be unmindful of my duty.
