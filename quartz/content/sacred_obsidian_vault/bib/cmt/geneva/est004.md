 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Geneva Bible Translation Notes</i>, [1599], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/est004.htm#001">Esther Chapter 4</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/est004.htm#002">Esther 4:2</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_002">est 4:2</a></font></span></p>And came even before the king's (a) gate: for none [might] enter into the king's gate clothed with sackcloth. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(a) Because he would inform Esther of this cruel proclamation.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/est004.htm#014">Esther 4:14</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_014">est 4:14</a></font></span></p>For if thou altogether holdest thy peace at this time, [then] shall there enlargement and deliverance (b) arise to the Jews from another place; but thou and thy father's house shall be destroyed: and who knoweth whether thou art come to the kingdom for (c) [such] a time as this? 
 <p>
 </p><p>(b) Thus Mordecai spoke in the confidence of that faith which all God's children should have; which is that God will deliver them, though all worldly means fail. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(c) To deliver God's Church out of these present dangers.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/est004.htm#016">Esther 4:16</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_016">est 4:16</a></font></span></p>Go, gather together all the Jews that are present in Shushan, and fast ye for me, and neither eat nor drink three days, night or day: I also and my maidens will fast likewise; and so will I go in unto the king, which [is] not according to the law: and if I perish, (d) I perish. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(d) I will put my life in danger and refer the success to God, seeing it is for his glory and the deliverance of his Church.
