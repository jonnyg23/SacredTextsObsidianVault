 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Geneva Bible Translation Notes</i>, [1599], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa023.htm#001">Psalms Chapter 23</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa023.htm#001">Psalms 23:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">psa 23:1</a></font></span></p>"A Psalm of David." The LORD [is] my shepherd; (a) I shall not want. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(a) He has care over me and ministers all things to me.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa023.htm#003">Psalms 23:3</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_003">psa 23:3</a></font></span></p>He (b) restoreth my soul: he leadeth me in the (c) paths of righteousness for his name's sake. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(b) He comforts or refreshes me. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(c) Plain or straight ways.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa023.htm#004">Psalms 23:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">psa 23:4</a></font></span></p>Yea, though I walk through the valley of the (d) shadow of death, I will fear no evil: for thou [art] with me; thy rod and thy staff they comfort me. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(d) Though he was in danger of death, as the sheep that wanders in the dark valley without his shepherd.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa023.htm#005">Psalms 23:5</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_005">psa 23:5</a></font></span></p>Thou preparest a (e) table before me in the presence of mine enemies: thou (f) anointest my head with oil; my cup runneth over. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(e) Even though his enemies sought to destroy him, yet God delivers him, and deals most liberally with him in spite of them. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(f) As was the manner of great feasts.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa023.htm#006">Psalms 23:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">psa 23:6</a></font></span></p>Surely goodness and mercy shall follow me all the days of my life: and I will dwell in the (g) house of the LORD for ever. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(g) He does not set his happiness in the pleasures of this world, but in the fear and service of God.
