 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Geneva Bible Translation Notes</i>, [1599], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num027.htm#001">Numbers Chapter 27</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num027.htm#003">Numbers 27:3</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_003">num 27:3</a></font></span></p>Our father died in the wilderness, and he was not in the company of them that gathered themselves together against the LORD in the company of Korah; but died in his own (a) sin, and had no sons. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(a) According as all men die, for as much as they are sinners.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num027.htm#005">Numbers 27:5</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_005">num 27:5</a></font></span></p>And Moses brought their (b) cause before the LORD. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(b) That is, their matter to be judged, to know what he should determine, as he did all hard matters.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num027.htm#011">Numbers 27:11</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_011">num 27:11</a></font></span></p>And if his father have no brethren, then ye shall give his inheritance unto his kinsman that is next to him of his family, and he shall possess it: and it shall be unto the children of Israel a statute of (c) judgment, as the LORD commanded Moses. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(c) Meaning, an ordinance to judge by.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num027.htm#016">Numbers 27:16</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_016">num 27:16</a></font></span></p>Let the LORD, the God of the (d) spirits of all flesh, set a man over the congregation, 
 <p>
 </p><p>(d) Who as he has created, so he governs the hearts of all men.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num027.htm#017">Numbers 27:17</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_017">num 27:17</a></font></span></p>Which may (e) go out before them, and which may go in before them, and which may lead them out, and which may bring them in; that the congregation of the LORD be not as sheep which have no shepherd. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(e) That is, govern them and do his duty, as in (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch2001.htm#010">Ch2 1:10</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num027.htm#018">Numbers 27:18</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_018">num 27:18</a></font></span></p>And the LORD said unto Moses, Take thee Joshua the son of Nun, a man in whom [is] the spirit, and (f) lay thine hand upon him; 
 <p>
 </p><p>(f) And so appoint him governor.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num027.htm#020">Numbers 27:20</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_020">num 27:20</a></font></span></p>And thou shalt (g) put [some] of thine honour upon him, that all the congregation of the children of Israel may be obedient. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(g) Commend him to the people as suitable for the office and appointed by God.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num027.htm#021">Numbers 27:21</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_021">num 27:21</a></font></span></p>And he shall stand before Eleazar the priest, who shall ask [counsel] for him after the (h) judgment of Urim before the LORD: at his word shall they go out, and at his word they shall come in, [both] he, and all the children of Israel with him, even all the congregation. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(h) According to his office: signifying that the civil magistrate could execute nothing but that which he knew to be the will of God.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num027.htm#023">Numbers 27:23</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_023">num 27:23</a></font></span></p>And he laid his hands upon him, and gave him a (i) charge, as the LORD commanded by the hand of Moses. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(i) How he should govern himself in his office.
