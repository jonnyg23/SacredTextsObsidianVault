 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Geneva Bible Translation Notes</i>, [1599], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa003.htm#001">Psalms Chapter 3</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa003.htm#001">Psalms 3:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">psa 3:1</a></font></span></p>"A Psalm of David, when he fled from Absalom his son." LORD, how are they (a) increased that trouble me! many [are] they that rise up against me. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(a) This was a token of his stable faith, that for all his troubles he turned to God.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa003.htm#002">Psalms 3:2</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_002">psa 3:2</a></font></span></p>Many [there be] which say of my soul, [There is] no help for him in God. (b) Selah. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(b) Selah here signifies a lifting up of the voice, to cause us to consider the sentence as a thing of great importance.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa003.htm#006">Psalms 3:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">psa 3:6</a></font></span></p>I will not be afraid of (c) ten thousands of people, that have set [themselves] against me round about. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(c) When he considered the truth of God's promise, and tried the same, his faith increased marvellously.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa003.htm#008">Psalms 3:8</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_008">psa 3:8</a></font></span></p>(d) Salvation [belongeth] unto the LORD: thy blessing [is] upon thy people. Selah. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(d) No matter how great or many the dangers may be, yet God always has ways to deliver his.
