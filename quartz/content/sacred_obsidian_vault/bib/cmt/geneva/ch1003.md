 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Geneva Bible Translation Notes</i>, [1599], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1003.htm#001">1 Chronicles Chapter 3</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1003.htm#001">1 Chronicles 3:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">ch1 3:1</a></font></span></p>Now these were the sons of (a) David, which were born unto him in Hebron; the firstborn Amnon, of Ahinoam the Jezreelitess; the second (b) Daniel, of Abigail the Carmelitess: 
 <p>
 </p><p>(a) He returns to the genealogy of David, to show that Christ came from his stock. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(b) Who in (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa2003.htm#003">Sa2 3:3</a>) is called Chileab, born of her that was Nabal's wife the Carmelite. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1003.htm#005">1 Chronicles 3:5</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_005">ch1 3:5</a></font></span></p>And these were born unto him in Jerusalem; Shimea, and Shobab, and Nathan, and Solomon, four, of (c) Bathshua the daughter of Ammiel: 
 <p>
 </p><p>(c) Called also Bathsheba the daughter of Eliam: for they gave them various names. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1003.htm#006">1 Chronicles 3:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">ch1 3:6</a></font></span></p>Ibhar also, and (d) Elishama, and Eliphelet, 
 <p>
 </p><p>(d) Elishama, or Elishua, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa2005.htm#015">Sa2 5:15</a>) and Eliphelet died, and David named those sons who were born next by the same names; in the book of kings his living children are mentioned and here both they that were alive and dead. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1003.htm#015">1 Chronicles 3:15</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_015">ch1 3:15</a></font></span></p>And the sons of Josiah [were], the (e) firstborn Johanan, the second Jehoiakim, the third Zedekiah, the fourth Shallum. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(e) So called because he was preferred for the royal dignity before his brother Jehoiakim who was the elder. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1003.htm#019">1 Chronicles 3:19</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_019">ch1 3:19</a></font></span></p>And the sons of Pedaiah [were], (f) Zerubbabel, and Shimei: and the sons of Zerubbabel; Meshullam, and Hananiah, and Shelomith their sister: 
 <p>
 </p><p>(f) Matthew says that Zerubbabel was the son of Shealtiel, meaning that he was his nephew according to the Hebrew speech: for he was Pedaiah's son. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1003.htm#022">1 Chronicles 3:22</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_022">ch1 3:22</a></font></span></p>And the sons of Shechaniah; Shemaiah: and the sons of Shemaiah; Hattush, and Igeal, and Bariah, and Neariah, and Shaphat, (g) six. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(g) So that Shemaiah was Shechaniah's natural son, and the other five his nephews and in all there were six.
