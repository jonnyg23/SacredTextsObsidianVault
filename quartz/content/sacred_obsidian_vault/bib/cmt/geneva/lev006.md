 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Geneva Bible Translation Notes</i>, [1599], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev006.htm#001">Leviticus Chapter 6</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev006.htm#002">Leviticus 6:2</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_002">lev 6:2</a></font></span></p>If a soul sin, and commit a trespass against the LORD, and lie unto his neighbour in that which was delivered him to keep, or in (a) fellowship, or in a thing taken away by (b) violence, or hath deceived his neighbour; 
 <p>
 </p><p>(a) To give, and occupy for the use of him that gave it. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(b) By any deceit, or unlawful means.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev006.htm#003">Leviticus 6:3</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_003">lev 6:3</a></font></span></p>Or have found that which was lost, and lieth concerning it, and sweareth falsely; in any of all these that a man doeth, (c) sinning therein: 
 <p>
 </p><p>(c) In which he cannot but sin: or, in which a man accustoms to sin by perjury or such like thing.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev006.htm#009">Leviticus 6:9</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_009">lev 6:9</a></font></span></p>Command Aaron and his sons, saying, This [is] the (d) law of the burnt offering: It [is] the burnt offering, because of the burning upon the altar all night unto the morning, and the fire of the altar shall be burning in it. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(d) That is, the ceremony which ought to be observed in it.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev006.htm#010">Leviticus 6:10</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_010">lev 6:10</a></font></span></p>And the priest shall put on his linen garment, and his linen breeches shall he put upon (e) his flesh, and take up the ashes which the fire hath consumed with the burnt offering on the altar, and he shall put them beside the (f) altar. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(e) Upon his secret parts, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo028.htm#042">Exo 28:42</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(f) In the ash pans appointed for that use.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev006.htm#017">Leviticus 6:17</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_017">lev 6:17</a></font></span></p>It shall not be (g) baken with leaven. I have given it [unto them for] their portion of my offerings made by fire; it [is] most holy, as [is] the sin offering, and as the trespass offering. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(g) Or, kneaded with leaven and baked.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev006.htm#020">Leviticus 6:20</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_020">lev 6:20</a></font></span></p>This [is] the offering of Aaron and of his sons, which they shall offer unto the LORD in the day when he is anointed; the tenth part of an ephah of fine flour for a meat offering (h) perpetual, half of it in the morning, and half thereof at night. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(h) So oft as the high priest shall be elected and anointed.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev006.htm#022">Leviticus 6:22</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_022">lev 6:22</a></font></span></p>And the priest of his sons that is (i) anointed in his stead shall offer it: [it is] a statute for ever unto the LORD; it shall be wholly burnt. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(i) His son that shall succeed him.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev006.htm#027">Leviticus 6:27</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_027">lev 6:27</a></font></span></p>Whatsoever shall touch the flesh thereof shall be holy: and when there is sprinkled of the blood thereof upon any (k) garment, thou shalt wash that whereon it was sprinkled in the holy place. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(k) Meaning, the garment of the priest.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev006.htm#028">Leviticus 6:28</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_028">lev 6:28</a></font></span></p>But the earthen vessel wherein it is sodden shall be broken: and if it be sodden in a brasen pot, it shall be both scoured, and rinsed in (l) water. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(l) Which was in the laver, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo030.htm#028">Exo 30:28</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev006.htm#030">Leviticus 6:30</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_030">lev 6:30</a></font></span></p>And no sin offering, whereof [any] of the blood is brought into the tabernacle of the congregation to reconcile [withal] in the holy [place], shall be eaten: it shall be burnt in the (m) fire. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(m) Out of the camp (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev004.htm#012">Lev 4:12</a>).
