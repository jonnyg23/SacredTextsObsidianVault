 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Geneva Bible Translation Notes</i>, [1599], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa026.htm#001">Psalms Chapter 26</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa026.htm#001">Psalms 26:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">psa 26:1</a></font></span></p>"[A Psalm] of David." Judge me, (a) O LORD; for I have walked in mine integrity: I have trusted also in the LORD; [therefore] I shall not slide. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(a) He flees to God to be the judge of his just cause, seeing there is no equity among men.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa026.htm#002">Psalms 26:2</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_002">psa 26:2</a></font></span></p>Examine me, O LORD, and prove me; try my (b) reins and my heart. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(b) My very affections and inward motions of the heart.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa026.htm#003">Psalms 26:3</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_003">psa 26:3</a></font></span></p>For thy (c) lovingkindness [is] before mine eyes: and I have walked in thy truth. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(c) He shows what stayed him, that he did not recompense evil for evil.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa026.htm#004">Psalms 26:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">psa 26:4</a></font></span></p>I have not (d) sat with vain persons, neither will I go in with dissemblers. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(d) He declares that they cannot walk in simplicity before God, that delight in the company of the ungodly.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa026.htm#006">Psalms 26:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">psa 26:6</a></font></span></p>I will (e) wash mine hands in innocency: so will I compass thine altar, O LORD: 
 <p>
 </p><p>(e) I will serve you with a pure affection, and with the godly that sacrifice to you.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa026.htm#009">Psalms 26:9</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_009">psa 26:9</a></font></span></p>(f) Gather not my soul with sinners, nor my life with bloody men: 
 <p>
 </p><p>(f) Destroy me not in the overthrow of the wicked.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa026.htm#010">Psalms 26:10</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_010">psa 26:10</a></font></span></p>In whose hands [is] (g) mischief, and their right hand is full of bribes. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(g) Whose cruel hands execute the malicious devises of their hearts.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa026.htm#012">Psalms 26:12</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_012">psa 26:12</a></font></span></p>My foot standeth in (h) an even place: in the congregations will I bless the LORD. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(h) I am preserved from my enemies by the power of God, and therefore will praise him openly.
