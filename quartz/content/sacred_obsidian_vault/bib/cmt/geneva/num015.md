 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Geneva Bible Translation Notes</i>, [1599], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num015.htm#001">Numbers Chapter 15</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num015.htm#002">Numbers 15:2</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_002">num 15:2</a></font></span></p>Speak unto the children of Israel, and say unto them, When ye be come into the (a) land of your habitations, which I give unto you, 
 <p>
 </p><p>(a) Into the land of Canaan.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num015.htm#004">Numbers 15:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">num 15:4</a></font></span></p>Then shall he that offereth his offering unto the LORD bring a meat offering of a tenth deal of flour mingled with the fourth [part] of an (b) hin of oil. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(b) Read (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo029.htm#040">Exo 29:40</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num015.htm#007">Numbers 15:7</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_007">num 15:7</a></font></span></p>And for a (c) drink offering thou shalt offer the third [part] of an hin of wine, [for] a sweet savour unto the LORD. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(c) The liquor was so called, because it was poured on the thing that was offered.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num015.htm#012">Numbers 15:12</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_012">num 15:12</a></font></span></p>According to the number (d) that ye shall prepare, so shall ye do to every one according to their number. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(d) Every sacrifice of beasts must have their meat offering and drink offering according to this proportion.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num015.htm#020">Numbers 15:20</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_020">num 15:20</a></font></span></p>Ye shall offer up a cake of the first of your (e) dough [for] an heave offering: as [ye do] the heave offering of the threshingfloor, so shall ye heave it. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(e) Which is made from the first corn you harvest.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num015.htm#022">Numbers 15:22</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_022">num 15:22</a></font></span></p>And if ye (f) have erred, and not observed all these commandments, which the LORD hath spoken unto Moses, 
 <p>
 </p><p>(f) By oversight or ignorance, read (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev004.htm#002">Lev 4:2</a>, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev004.htm#013">Lev 4:13</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num015.htm#031">Numbers 15:31</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_031">num 15:31</a></font></span></p>Because he hath despised the word of the LORD, and hath broken his commandment, that soul shall utterly be cut off; his (h) iniquity [shall be] upon him. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(h) He shall sustain the punishment of his sin.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num015.htm#039">Numbers 15:39</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_039">num 15:39</a></font></span></p>And it shall be unto you for a fringe, that ye may look upon it, and remember all the commandments of the LORD, and do them; and that ye seek not after your own heart and your own eyes, after which ye use to go a (i) whoring: 
 <p>
 </p><p>(i) By leaving God's commandments and following your own fantasies.
