 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Geneva Bible Translation Notes</i>, [1599], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa020.htm#001">Psalms Chapter 20</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa020.htm#001">Psalms 20:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">psa 20:1</a></font></span></p>"To the chief Musician, A Psalm of David." The (a) LORD hear thee in the day of trouble; the (b) name of the God of Jacob defend thee; 
 <p>
 </p><p>(a) By this kings are also admonished to call to God in their affairs. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(b) The virtue, power and grace of God.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa020.htm#003">Psalms 20:3</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_003">psa 20:3</a></font></span></p>Remember all thy offerings, and (c) accept thy burnt sacrifice; Selah. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(c) In token that they are acceptable to him.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa020.htm#005">Psalms 20:5</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_005">psa 20:5</a></font></span></p>We will rejoice in thy (d) salvation, and in the name of our God we will set up [our] banners: the LORD fulfil all thy petitions. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(d) Granted to the king in whose wealth our happiness stands.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa020.htm#006">Psalms 20:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">psa 20:6</a></font></span></p>Now (e) know I that the LORD saveth his anointed; he will hear him from his (f) holy heaven with the saving strength of his right hand. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(e) The Church feels that God had heard their petition. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(f) As by the visible sanctuary God's familiarity appeared toward his people, so by the heavenly is meant his power and majesty.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa020.htm#008">Psalms 20:8</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_008">psa 20:8</a></font></span></p>(g) They are brought down and fallen: but we are risen, and stand upright. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(g) The worldlings who do not put their trust in God alone.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa020.htm#009">Psalms 20:9</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_009">psa 20:9</a></font></span></p>Save, LORD: (h) let the king hear us when we call. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(h) Let the king be able to deliver us by your strength, when we seek him for help.
