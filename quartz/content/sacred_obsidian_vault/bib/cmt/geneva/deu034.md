 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Geneva Bible Translation Notes</i>, [1599], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/deu034.htm#001">Deuteronomy Chapter 34</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/deu034.htm#001">Deuteronomy 34:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">deu 34:1</a></font></span></p>And Moses went up from the plains of Moab unto the mountain of (a) Nebo, to the top of Pisgah, that [is] over against Jericho. And the LORD shewed him all the land of Gilead, unto Dan, 
 <p>
 </p><p>(a) Which was a part of mount Abarim, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/num027.htm#012">Num 27:12</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/deu034.htm#002">Deuteronomy 34:2</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_002">deu 34:2</a></font></span></p>And all Naphtali, and the land of Ephraim, and Manasseh, and all the land of Judah, unto the utmost (b) sea, 
 <p>
 </p><p>(b) Called Mediterranean.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/deu034.htm#006">Deuteronomy 34:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">deu 34:6</a></font></span></p>And (c) he buried him in a valley in the land of Moab, over against Bethpeor: but no man knoweth of his sepulchre unto (d) this day. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(c) That is, the angel of the Lord, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/jde001.htm#009">Jde 1:9</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(d) That the Jews might not have opportunity to commit idolatry by it.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/deu034.htm#009">Deuteronomy 34:9</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_009">deu 34:9</a></font></span></p>And (e) Joshua the son of Nun was full of the spirit of wisdom; for Moses had laid his hands upon him: and the children of Israel hearkened unto him, and did as the LORD commanded Moses. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(e) By this the favour of God is demonstrated, in that he does not leave his Church destitute of a governor.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/deu034.htm#010">Deuteronomy 34:10</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_010">deu 34:10</a></font></span></p>And there arose not a prophet since in Israel like unto Moses, whom the LORD knew (f) face to face, 
 <p>
 </p><p>(f) To whom the Lord revealed himself so plainly as in (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo033.htm#011">Exo 33:11</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/deu034.htm#012">Deuteronomy 34:12</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_012">deu 34:12</a></font></span></p>And in all that mighty (g) hand, and in all the great terror which Moses shewed in the sight of all Israel. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(g) Meaning, the power of God working by Moses in the wilderness.  
 </p><p></p>
