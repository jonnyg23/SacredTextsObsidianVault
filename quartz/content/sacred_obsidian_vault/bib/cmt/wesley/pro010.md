 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible</i>, by John Wesley, [1754-65], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro010.htm#001">Proverbs Chapter 10</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro010.htm#002">Proverbs 10:2</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_002">pro 10:2</a></font></span></p>Of wickedness - Such as are got by any sort of wicked practices. Death - Often from temporal, and always from eternal death. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro010.htm#005">Proverbs 10:5</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_005">pro 10:5</a></font></span></p>Gathereth - The fruits of his field. In summer - In harvest. He that improved the opportunities of doing good to himself and others. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro010.htm#006">Proverbs 10:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">pro 10:6</a></font></span></p>Violence - Their own violence or injustice. This may be an allusion to the ancient custom of covering the mouths and faces of condemned malefactors. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro010.htm#008">Proverbs 10:8</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_008">pro 10:8</a></font></span></p>Who receive - Is ready to hear and obey the precepts of God and men. Fall - Into mischief. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro010.htm#009">Proverbs 10:9</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_009">pro 10:9</a></font></span></p>Surely - Or, securely, or confidently, as the word properly signifies; quietly resting upon God's favour and gracious providence for his protections. Known - His wickedness shall be publickly discovered. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro010.htm#010">Proverbs 10:10</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_010">pro 10:10</a></font></span></p>Winketh - That secretly designs mischief against others, as this phrase is used, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa035.htm#019">Psa 35:19</a>. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro010.htm#013">Proverbs 10:13</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_013">pro 10:13</a></font></span></p>Wisdom - This wisdom shews itself in his speech. A rod - Rebukes from God and men. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro010.htm#014">Proverbs 10:14</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_014">pro 10:14</a></font></span></p>Lay up - In their minds, to be brought forth upon fit occasions. Knowledge - Whereby they may be enabled to speak both what, and when it is seasonable. But - Fools are more forward to lay out than to lay up, and for want of knowledge speak much and foolishly, whereby they frequently bring destruction upon themselves. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro010.htm#015">Proverbs 10:15</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_015">pro 10:15</a></font></span></p>Wealth - It often redeems him from dangers and calamities. Poverty - Is the cause of their ruin. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro010.htm#016">Proverbs 10:16</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_016">pro 10:16</a></font></span></p>The labour - The fruit of his labour, to the preservation of this life, and obtaining eternal life. Fruit - The fruit of all their labour tends to sin, and serves only for fuel to mens pride and luxury. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro010.htm#018">Proverbs 10:18</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_018">pro 10:18</a></font></span></p>Lying lips - With flattering words. He here condemns two opposite vices, secret hatred, and manifest slander. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro010.htm#020">Proverbs 10:20</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_020">pro 10:20</a></font></span></p>Is - Of great worth and use. Heart - And consequently the tongue. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro010.htm#021">Proverbs 10:21</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_021">pro 10:21</a></font></span></p>Feed - By their wise discourses and counsels. Die - They have not wisdom to preserve themselves, much less to feed others. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro010.htm#026">Proverbs 10:26</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_026">pro 10:26</a></font></span></p>So - Unserviceable and vexatious. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro010.htm#028">Proverbs 10:28</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_028">pro 10:28</a></font></span></p>Gladness - Shall be accomplished, and turned into enjoyment. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro010.htm#029">Proverbs 10:29</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_029">pro 10:29</a></font></span></p>The way - The way of God's precepts. Strength - Gives strength, support, and protection. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro010.htm#030">Proverbs 10:30</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_030">pro 10:30</a></font></span></p>Never be removed - They shall live happily here, and eternally in heaven. The earth - They shall not have so much as a quiet abode upon earth; much less shall they have any possession in heaven. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro010.htm#032">Proverbs 10:32</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_032">pro 10:32</a></font></span></p>The lips - Knowledge is here ascribed to the lips, as it is to the hands, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa078.htm#072">Psa 78:72</a>, because they are conducted by knowledge and wisdom. Acceptable - What is truly worthy of acceptation.
