 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible</i>, by John Wesley, [1754-65], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa057.htm#001">Psalms Chapter 57</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa057.htm#003">Psalms 57:3</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_003">psa 57:3</a></font></span></p>Send forth - Will discover them, by affording his gracious help in pursuance of his promises. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa057.htm#004">Psalms 57:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">psa 57:4</a></font></span></p>Lions - Fierce and bloody men. I lie - I have my abode. On fire - From hell. Who are mere fire - brands, breathing out wrath and threatenings, and incensing Saul against me. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa057.htm#005">Psalms 57:5</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_005">psa 57:5</a></font></span></p>Excited - Glorify thy power, and goodness, and justice, and faithfulness, by my deliverance. Above, &amp;c. - To the highest degree possible. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa057.htm#007">Psalms 57:7</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_007">psa 57:7</a></font></span></p>Fixed - In a stedfast belief of thy promises. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa057.htm#008">Psalms 57:8</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_008">psa 57:8</a></font></span></p>My glory - My tongue, the instrument of singing. Awake - I will employ all the powers of my soul and body. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa057.htm#009">Psalms 57:9</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_009">psa 57:9</a></font></span></p>The people - Among the Israelites, and among the Heathens, as I shall have occasion.
