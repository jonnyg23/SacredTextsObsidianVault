 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible</i>, by John Wesley, [1754-65], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa077.htm#001">Psalms Chapter 77</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa077.htm#002">Psalms 77:2</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_002">psa 77:2</a></font></span></p>Night - Which to others was a time of rest and quietness. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa077.htm#003">Psalms 77:3</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_003">psa 77:3</a></font></span></p>Troubled - Yea, the thoughts of God were now a matter of trouble, because he was angry with me. Overwhelmed - So far was I from finding relief. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa077.htm#004">Psalms 77:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">psa 77:4</a></font></span></p>Waking - By continual grief. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa077.htm#005">Psalms 77:5</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_005">psa 77:5</a></font></span></p>The days - The mighty works of God in former times. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa077.htm#006">Psalms 77:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">psa 77:6</a></font></span></p>My song - The mercies of God vouchsafed to me, and to his people, which have obliged me to sing his praises, not only in the day, but also by night. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa077.htm#007">Psalms 77:7</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_007">psa 77:7</a></font></span></p>Cut off - His peculiar people. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa077.htm#010">Psalms 77:10</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_010">psa 77:10</a></font></span></p>I said - These suspicions of God's faithfulness proceed from the weakness of my faith. The years - The years wherein God hath done great and glorious works, which are often ascribed to God's right - hand. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa077.htm#013">Psalms 77:13</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_013">psa 77:13</a></font></span></p>In holiness - God is holy and just, and true in all his works. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa077.htm#016">Psalms 77:16</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_016">psa 77:16</a></font></span></p>Afraid - And stood still, as men astonished, do. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa077.htm#017">Psalms 77:17</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_017">psa 77:17</a></font></span></p>Poured - When the Israelites passed over the sea. Arrows - Hail - stones or lightnings. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa077.htm#019">Psalms 77:19</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_019">psa 77:19</a></font></span></p>Not known - Because the water returned and covered them. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa077.htm#020">Psalms 77:20</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_020">psa 77:20</a></font></span></p>Leddest - First through the sea, and afterwards through the wilderness, with singular care and tenderness, as a shepherd doth his sheep.
