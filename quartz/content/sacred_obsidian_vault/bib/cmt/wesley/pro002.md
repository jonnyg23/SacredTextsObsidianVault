 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible</i>, by John Wesley, [1754-65], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro002.htm#001">Proverbs Chapter 2</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro002.htm#001">Proverbs 2:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">pro 2:1</a></font></span></p>Hide - Lay them up in thy heart with care, as men do their choicest treasures. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro002.htm#003">Proverbs 2:3</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_003">pro 2:3</a></font></span></p>Criest - To God, the only giver of it. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro002.htm#004">Proverbs 2:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">pro 2:4</a></font></span></p>Seek her - With unwearied diligence and earnest desire and patient expectation. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro002.htm#008">Proverbs 2:8</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_008">pro 2:8</a></font></span></p>Path of judgment - Righteous paths, judgment being here put for righteousness. And keeping of paths may be put for keeping them in their paths. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro002.htm#009">Proverbs 2:9</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_009">pro 2:9</a></font></span></p>Then - When God in answer to thy desires hath given thee wisdom. Equity - All the parts of thy duty to man, as well as the fear of God. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro002.htm#010">Proverbs 2:10</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_010">pro 2:10</a></font></span></p>When - Thou dost truly love wisdom. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro002.htm#013">Proverbs 2:13</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_013">pro 2:13</a></font></span></p>Darkness - Of sin. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro002.htm#015">Proverbs 2:15</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_015">pro 2:15</a></font></span></p>Crooked - Who swerve from the straight way of God's law, and have windings and turnings, to escape conviction. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro002.htm#016">Proverbs 2:16</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_016">pro 2:16</a></font></span></p>Strange woman - From the adulteress or whore. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro002.htm#017">Proverbs 2:17</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_017">pro 2:17</a></font></span></p>Forsaketh - Her husband whom she took to be her guide and governor, in her youth. The covenant - The marriage covenant: so called because God is the author of that mutual obligation: and because God is called to be the witness and judge of that solemn promise and covenant. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro002.htm#018">Proverbs 2:18</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_018">pro 2:18</a></font></span></p>For - Conversation with her (which was most usual in her own house) is the certain way to death, which it brings many ways, and undoubtedly, without repentance, to God's wrath and the second death. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro002.htm#020">Proverbs 2:20</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_020">pro 2:20</a></font></span></p>That - This depends upon <a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro002.htm#011">Pro 2:11</a>, and is mentioned as another happy fruit of wisdom.
