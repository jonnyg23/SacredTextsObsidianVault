 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible</i>, by John Wesley, [1754-65], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa130.htm#001">Psalms Chapter 130</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa130.htm#003">Psalms 130:3</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_003">psa 130:3</a></font></span></p>Mark - Observe them accurately and punish them as they deserve. Stand - At thy tribunal. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa130.htm#004">Psalms 130:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">psa 130:4</a></font></span></p>Forgiveness - Thou art able and ready to forgive repenting sinners. Feared - Not with a slavish, but with a childlike fear. This mercy of thine is the foundation of all religion, without which men would desperately proceed in their impious courses. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa130.htm#005">Psalms 130:5</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_005">psa 130:5</a></font></span></p>I wait - That he would pardon my sins. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa130.htm#006">Psalms 130:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">psa 130:6</a></font></span></p>They - Whether soldiers that keep the night - watches in an army, or the priests or Levites who did so in the temple. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa130.htm#007">Psalms 130:7</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_007">psa 130:7</a></font></span></p>Israel - Every true Israelite. Plenteous - Abundantly sufficient for all persons who accept it upon God's terms.
