 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible</i>, by John Wesley, [1754-65], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro001.htm#001">Proverbs Chapter 1</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro001.htm#002">Proverbs 1:2</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_002">pro 1:2</a></font></span></p>To know - Written to help men to know throughly and practically. Both human wisdom to conduct our affairs in this life, and Divine wisdom. Instruction - The instructions delivered either by God, or men, in order to the attainment of wisdom. To perceive - Which teach a man true understanding. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro001.htm#003">Proverbs 1:3</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_003">pro 1:3</a></font></span></p>To receive - Willing to receive the counsels of others. Such as makes men wise and prudent, and to teach just judgments or equity. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro001.htm#004">Proverbs 1:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">pro 1:4</a></font></span></p>Simple - Such as want wisdom. Young man - Who wants both experience and self - government. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro001.htm#005">Proverbs 1:5</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_005">pro 1:5</a></font></span></p>Will hear - Is willing to learn. Attain to - The art of governing himself or others. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro001.htm#007">Proverbs 1:7</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_007">pro 1:7</a></font></span></p>The fear - Reverence and obedience to God. Beginning - The foundation without which all other knowledge is vain. Fools - That is, wicked men, are so far from attaining true wisdom, that they despise it, and all the means of getting it. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro001.htm#008">Proverbs 1:8</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_008">pro 1:8</a></font></span></p>My son - He speaks to his scholars with paternal authority and affection. Of thy mother - Those pious instructions, which thy mother instilled into thee in thy tender years. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro001.htm#017">Proverbs 1:17</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_017">pro 1:17</a></font></span></p>In vain - The fowler who spreads, his net in the sight of the bird looseth his labour. But these, are more foolish than the silly birds, and though they are not ignorant of the mischief which these evil courses will bring upon themselves, yet they will not take warning. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro001.htm#018">Proverbs 1:18</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_018">pro 1:18</a></font></span></p>Their own blood - The destruction which they design to others, fall upon themselves. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro001.htm#019">Proverbs 1:19</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_019">pro 1:19</a></font></span></p>Greedy - That seeks gain by wicked practices. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro001.htm#020">Proverbs 1:20</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_020">pro 1:20</a></font></span></p>Wisdom - Having expressed the counsels of wicked men, he now declares the voice of wisdom. By wisdom he understands the wisdom of God revealed to men in his word. And this is said to cry with a loud voice, to intimate God's earnestness in inviting sinners to repentance. Abroad - Or, in the streets or open places. Not in corners, but openly before all the world. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro001.htm#021">Proverbs 1:21</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_021">pro 1:21</a></font></span></p>Gates - Where magistrates sit in judgment, and people are assembled. The city - Not only in the gate, but in every part of the city. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro001.htm#022">Proverbs 1:22</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_022">pro 1:22</a></font></span></p>Scoffers - That scoff at religion and contemn the word and faithful ministers of God. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro001.htm#023">Proverbs 1:23</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_023">pro 1:23</a></font></span></p>My words - By my spirit I will cause you to understand my word. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro001.htm#024">Proverbs 1:24</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_024">pro 1:24</a></font></span></p>Called - By my ministers, by my judgments, and by the motions of my spirit and your own conscience. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro001.htm#026">Proverbs 1:26</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_026">pro 1:26</a></font></span></p>Your fear - The misery you do or should fear. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro001.htm#028">Proverbs 1:28</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_028">pro 1:28</a></font></span></p>Early - With diligence and fervency. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro001.htm#029">Proverbs 1:29</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_029">pro 1:29</a></font></span></p>Knowledge - The practical knowledge of God and of their duty. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro001.htm#031">Proverbs 1:31</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_031">pro 1:31</a></font></span></p>Eat - They should receive punishment answerable to their sins. Filled - With the fruits of them. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro001.htm#032">Proverbs 1:32</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_032">pro 1:32</a></font></span></p>Turning - From God. Destroy - Shall prove the occasion of their ruin, by making them presumptuous, and secure.
