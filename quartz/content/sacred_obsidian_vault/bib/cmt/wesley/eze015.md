 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible</i>, by John Wesley, [1754-65], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze015.htm#001">Ezekiel Chapter 15</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze015.htm#002">Ezekiel 15:2</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_002">eze 15:2</a></font></span></p>The vine - tree - Israel is here compared to a vine, which, when fruitless, is utterly unprofitable. This the prophet minds them of to humble them, and awaken them to fruitfulness. A branch - One branch of a tree in the forest is of more use than the whole vine - tree is, except for its fruit. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze015.htm#003">Ezekiel 15:3</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_003">eze 15:3</a></font></span></p>A pin - Will it afford even a pin to drive into a wall or post, on which you may safely fasten any weight. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze015.htm#004">Ezekiel 15:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">eze 15:4</a></font></span></p>For fuel - When for its barrenness it is cut down, it is fit only to burn. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze015.htm#006">Ezekiel 15:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">eze 15:6</a></font></span></p>Given - Doomed for food to the fire. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze015.htm#008">Ezekiel 15:8</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_008">eze 15:8</a></font></span></p>Because - They have been so perpetually trespassing, that it seems a continued act.
