 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible</i>, by John Wesley, [1754-65], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/lam001.htm#001">Lamentations Chapter 1</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/lam001.htm#001">Lamentations 1:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">lam 1:1</a></font></span></p>A widow - She that had a king, or rather a God, that was an husband to her, now was forsaken of God, and her king taken from her. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/lam001.htm#003">Lamentations 1:3</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_003">lam 1:3</a></font></span></p>Because - Because of the servitude and oppression exercised among them: oppression by their rulers, and servitude more generally; keeping their servants beyond the year of jubilee, when they ought to be set at liberty. The straits - Those that pursued them overtook them in places where they could not escape. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/lam001.htm#004">Lamentations 1:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">lam 1:4</a></font></span></p>She - Persons of all ages and ranks are in bitterness. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/lam001.htm#010">Lamentations 1:10</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_010">lam 1:10</a></font></span></p>Pleasant things - Has laid violent hands on them. The things of the sanctuary were always pleasant things to those that feared God. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/lam001.htm#011">Lamentations 1:11</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_011">lam 1:11</a></font></span></p>Bread - Even in a land that ordinarily flowed with milk and honey, they were at a loss for bread to eat. Given - And gave any thing for something to satisfy their hunger. Vile - Miserable or contemptible. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/lam001.htm#012">Lamentations 1:12</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_012">lam 1:12</a></font></span></p>Is it nothing - The prophet speaks in the name of the Jewish church. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/lam001.htm#013">Lamentations 1:13</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_013">lam 1:13</a></font></span></p>Fire - A judgment as consuming, and afflictive as fire. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/lam001.htm#014">Lamentations 1:14</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_014">lam 1:14</a></font></span></p>Is bound - Put upon my neck on account of my transgressions. Wreathed - My punishments are twisted as cords; I have a complication of judgments upon me, sword, famine, pestilence, captivity. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/lam001.htm#015">Lamentations 1:15</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_015">lam 1:15</a></font></span></p>An assembly - God had called an assembly of Chaldeans against the city, to crush the inhabitants of it. Trodden - God had trodden upon the Jews as men use to stamp grapes in a wine - press. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/lam001.htm#016">Lamentations 1:16</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_016">lam 1:16</a></font></span></p>The comforter - God. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/lam001.htm#017">Lamentations 1:17</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_017">lam 1:17</a></font></span></p>Jerusalem - Is become loathsome and filthy. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/lam001.htm#019">Lamentations 1:19</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_019">lam 1:19</a></font></span></p>Deceived - They did not answer my expectation. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/lam001.htm#020">Lamentations 1:20</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_020">lam 1:20</a></font></span></p>Death - By famine and pestilence. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/lam001.htm#021">Lamentations 1:21</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_021">lam 1:21</a></font></span></p>They - The neighbouring nations. Like me - But thou hast foretold their destruction also, and hast by me proclaimed it: and thou shalt in that day bring them into as sad a condition as I am in now.
