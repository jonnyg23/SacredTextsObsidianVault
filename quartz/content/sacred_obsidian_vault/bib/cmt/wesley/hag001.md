 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible</i>, by John Wesley, [1754-65], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/hag001.htm#001">Haggai Chapter 1</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/hag001.htm#001">Haggai 1:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">hag 1:1</a></font></span></p>Son - Adoptive son to Shealtiel, being of the royal line, but by nature, son of Pedaiah. Governor - Appointed to this by the Persian king, over the remnant returned out of Babylon. Joshua - A type of the great deliverer; one Joshua leads them into Canaan, another restores the temple. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/hag001.htm#004">Haggai 1:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">hag 1:4</a></font></span></p>Ceiled - Arched and richly adorned. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/hag001.htm#006">Haggai 1:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">hag 1:6</a></font></span></p>Have not enough - But what you eat doth not nourish or satisfy you. Are not filled - Your water quenches not your thirst, your wine does not revive your spirit. None warm - You have no comfort therein. With holes - Loses all his labour. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/hag001.htm#008">Haggai 1:8</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_008">hag 1:8</a></font></span></p>Take pleasure - I will accept your offerings, and hear your prayers. Glorified - Shew my majesty and account myself glorified by you also. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/hag001.htm#009">Haggai 1:9</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_009">hag 1:9</a></font></span></p>Came to little - But it answered not the expectation. I did blow - I blasted it. Ye run - You with eagerness carry on your own particular buildings. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/hag001.htm#010">Haggai 1:10</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_010">hag 1:10</a></font></span></p>Is stayed - God hath forbidden them, to drop down dew. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/hag001.htm#011">Haggai 1:11</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_011">hag 1:11</a></font></span></p>Upon men - The very blood, and constitutions of men were changed, and many diseases afflicted them. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/hag001.htm#013">Haggai 1:13</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_013">hag 1:13</a></font></span></p>In the Lord's message - ln the words of his master. The people - The whole assembly. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/hag001.htm#014">Haggai 1:14</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_014">hag 1:14</a></font></span></p>The Lord of hosts - By which name he delights to be known among the returned captives; and it was a name best suited to their present state, compassed on all hands with enemies.
