 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible</i>, by John Wesley, [1754-65], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa099.htm#001">Psalms Chapter 99</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa099.htm#001">Psalms 99:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">psa 99:1</a></font></span></p>People - Such as are enemies to God and his people. Sitteth - Upon the ark. He is present with his people. Earth - The people of the earth. Moved - With fear and trembling. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa099.htm#003">Psalms 99:3</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_003">psa 99:3</a></font></span></p>Them - All people. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa099.htm#004">Psalms 99:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">psa 99:4</a></font></span></p>Judgment - Though his dominion be absolute, and his power irresistible, yet he manages it with righteousness. The king's strength is by a known Hebraism put for the strong, or powerful king. Equity - In all thy proceedings. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa099.htm#005">Psalms 99:5</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_005">psa 99:5</a></font></span></p>Foot - stool - Before the ark, which is so called, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1028.htm#002">Ch1 28:2</a>. Holy - It is consecrated to be a pledge of God's presence. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa099.htm#006">Psalms 99:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">psa 99:6</a></font></span></p>Moses - Moses before the institution of the priesthood executed that office, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo024.htm#006">Exo 24:6</a>. That call - Who used frequently and solemnly to intercede with God on the behalf of the people. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa099.htm#007">Psalms 99:7</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_007">psa 99:7</a></font></span></p>Spake - To some of them: to Moses and Aaron, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo019.htm#024">Exo 19:24</a>, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo033.htm#009">Exo 33:9-11</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa1007.htm#009">Sa1 7:9</a>, &amp;c. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa099.htm#008">Psalms 99:8</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_008">psa 99:8</a></font></span></p>Them - The intercessors before mentioned. Forgavest - The people for whom they prayed, so far as not to inflict that total destruction upon them which they deserved;
