 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible</i>, by John Wesley, [1754-65], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa088.htm#001">Psalms Chapter 88</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa088.htm#004">Psalms 88:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">psa 88:4</a></font></span></p>Counted - l am given up by my friends for a lost man. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa088.htm#005">Psalms 88:5</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_005">psa 88:5</a></font></span></p>Free - Well nigh discharged from the warfare of the present life, and entered as a member into the society of the dead. Whom - Thou seemest to neglect and bury in oblivion. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa088.htm#007">Psalms 88:7</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_007">psa 88:7</a></font></span></p>Waves - With they judgments, breaking in furiously upon me like the waves of the sea. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa088.htm#010">Psalms 88:10</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_010">psa 88:10</a></font></span></p>Wonders - In raising them to life. To praise thee - In this world? 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa088.htm#012">Psalms 88:12</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_012">psa 88:12</a></font></span></p>Forgetfulness - In the grave, where men are forgotten by their nearest relations. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa088.htm#013">Psalms 88:13</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_013">psa 88:13</a></font></span></p>Prevent - Come to thee before the dawning of the day, or the rising of the sun. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa088.htm#017">Psalms 88:17</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_017">psa 88:17</a></font></span></p>Water - As the waters of the sea encompass him who is in the midst of it.
