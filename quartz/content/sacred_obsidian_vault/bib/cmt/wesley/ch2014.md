 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible</i>, by John Wesley, [1754-65], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch2014.htm#001">2 Chronicles Chapter 14</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch2014.htm#001">2 Chronicles 14:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">ch2 14:1</a></font></span></p>Quiet - There was no open war, but there were private hostilities between his and Baasha's subjects. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch2014.htm#006">2 Chronicles 14:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">ch2 14:6</a></font></span></p>The land had rest - Those have rest indeed, to whom God gives rest; peace indeed, to whom Christ gives peace. We find by experience, it is good to seek the Lord. While we pursue the world, we meet with nothing but vexation. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch2014.htm#007">2 Chronicles 14:7</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_007">ch2 14:7</a></font></span></p>Before us - In our power. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch2014.htm#009">2 Chronicles 14:9</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_009">ch2 14:9</a></font></span></p>Ethiopian - Or, the Arabian, as the Hebrew word Cush is commonly used: these being much nearer to Asa than the Ethiopians. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch2014.htm#011">2 Chronicles 14:11</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_011">ch2 14:11</a></font></span></p>Let not man prevail - If he prevails against us, he prevails, as it were, against thee; because thou art our God. And we rest on thee, and go forth in thy name, which thou hast encouraged us to do. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch2014.htm#012">2 Chronicles 14:12</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_012">ch2 14:12</a></font></span></p>Smote - With terror, and an unaccountable consternation. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch2014.htm#014">2 Chronicles 14:14</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_014">ch2 14:14</a></font></span></p>Smote the cities - because they had joined, with Zerah in this war.
