 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible</i>, by John Wesley, [1754-65], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro023.htm#001">Proverbs Chapter 23</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro023.htm#001">Proverbs 23:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">pro 23:1</a></font></span></p>Ruler - With a great man. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro023.htm#002">Proverbs 23:2</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_002">pro 23:2</a></font></span></p>Put a knife - Restrain thine appetite, as if a man stood with a knife at thy throat. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro023.htm#003">Proverbs 23:3</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_003">pro 23:3</a></font></span></p>Deceitful - They do not yield thee that satisfaction which thou didst expect from them. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro023.htm#004">Proverbs 23:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">pro 23:4</a></font></span></p>Thine own wisdom - From worldly wisdom, which persuades men to use all possible means to get riches. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro023.htm#005">Proverbs 23:5</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_005">pro 23:5</a></font></span></p>Set thine eyes - Look upon it with earnestness and desire. Is not - Which has no solid and settled being. Eagle - Swiftly, strongly, and irrecoverably. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro023.htm#006">Proverbs 23:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">pro 23:6</a></font></span></p>Evil eye - Of the envious or covetous man. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro023.htm#007">Proverbs 23:7</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_007">pro 23:7</a></font></span></p>So is he - You are not to judge of him by his words, but by the constant temper of his mind. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro023.htm#008">Proverbs 23:8</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_008">pro 23:8</a></font></span></p>Vomit up - When thou perceivest his churlish disposition, his meat will be loathsome to thee. Sweet words - Thy pleasant discourse wherewith thou didst design to delight and profit him. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro023.htm#009">Proverbs 23:9</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_009">pro 23:9</a></font></span></p>Of a fool - Cast not away good counsels upon incorrigible sinners. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro023.htm#010">Proverbs 23:10</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_010">pro 23:10</a></font></span></p>Enter not - To possess their lands. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro023.htm#011">Proverbs 23:11</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_011">pro 23:11</a></font></span></p>Redeemer - Heb. their near kinsman, to whom it belongs to avenge their wrongs, and to recover and maintain their rights. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro023.htm#013">Proverbs 23:13</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_013">pro 23:13</a></font></span></p>Shall not die - It is a likely way to prevent his destruction. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro023.htm#016">Proverbs 23:16</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_016">pro 23:16</a></font></span></p>My reins - I shall rejoice with all my soul. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro023.htm#018">Proverbs 23:18</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_018">pro 23:18</a></font></span></p>An end - An expected and happy end for such as fear God. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro023.htm#019">Proverbs 23:19</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_019">pro 23:19</a></font></span></p>Guide - Order the whole course of thine affections and actions. In the way - In God's way. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro023.htm#023">Proverbs 23:23</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_023">pro 23:23</a></font></span></p>Buy - Purchase it upon any terms, spare no pains or cost. Truth - The true and saving knowledge of God's will. Sell it not - Do not forsake it for any worldly advantage. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro023.htm#026">Proverbs 23:26</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_026">pro 23:26</a></font></span></p>Give me - Solomon here speaks in God's name. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro023.htm#030">Proverbs 23:30</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_030">pro 23:30</a></font></span></p>Mixt wine - Either mixed with water, or with other ingredients to make it strong and delicious. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro023.htm#031">Proverbs 23:31</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_031">pro 23:31</a></font></span></p>Red - Which was the colour of the best wines in that country. Aright - When it sparkles, and seems to smile upon a man. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro023.htm#034">Proverbs 23:34</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_034">pro 23:34</a></font></span></p>Lieth down - To sleep. Sea - In a ship in the midst of the sea. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro023.htm#035">Proverbs 23:35</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_035">pro 23:35</a></font></span></p>Sick - I was not sensible of it. Again - At present my condition requires sleep to settle myself, and when I am composed, I purpose to return to my former course.
