 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible</i>, by John Wesley, [1754-65], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/zac008.htm#001">Zechariah Chapter 8</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/zac008.htm#002">Zechariah 8:2</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_002">zac 8:2</a></font></span></p>Jealousy - With great care that she should not, as formerly, sin against my love, and her own welfare, and with a great desire to do her good, and to rescue her from her enemies. Fury - With heat of anger against her enemies. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/zac008.htm#003">Zechariah 8:3</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_003">zac 8:3</a></font></span></p>Shall be called a city of truth - Her citizens shall love the truth and speak it, shall worship me in truth of heart, as well as in the true manner prescribed to them. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/zac008.htm#004">Zechariah 8:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">zac 8:4</a></font></span></p>Old men - Formerly war, or famine or pestilence, and wasting diseases, cut off men and women before they came to old age. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/zac008.htm#006">Zechariah 8:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">zac 8:6</a></font></span></p>Marvellous - These things may seem strange to this people. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/zac008.htm#007">Zechariah 8:7</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_007">zac 8:7</a></font></span></p>The east - country - Persia and Media, which lay east from Jerusalem, and were now masters of Babylon. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/zac008.htm#008">Zechariah 8:8</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_008">zac 8:8</a></font></span></p>In truth and in righteousness - This signifies both God's part, and their part; on God's part truth, on their's righteousness, obedience to God's righteous law. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/zac008.htm#009">Zechariah 8:9</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_009">zac 8:9</a></font></span></p>The prophets - Haggai and Zechariah. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/zac008.htm#010">Zechariah 8:10</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_010">zac 8:10</a></font></span></p>Before these days - For eighteen years together. No hire - No profit by the labour of man or beast, no sowing or planting. Affliction - Distress, and want, through the barrenness, which attended all their labour. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/zac008.htm#011">Zechariah 8:11</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_011">zac 8:11</a></font></span></p>I will not be - That is, I will not deal with them as in former days. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/zac008.htm#014">Zechariah 8:14</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_014">zac 8:14</a></font></span></p>I repented not - I did not fail to do it. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/zac008.htm#015">Zechariah 8:15</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_015">zac 8:15</a></font></span></p>So - So with like steadiness of mind I have purposed to do well to you. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/zac008.htm#016">Zechariah 8:16</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_016">zac 8:16</a></font></span></p>Judgment of truth - True judgment. Peace - That may restore, and settle peace among you. Gates - The places of judicature, where the judges sat. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/zac008.htm#019">Zechariah 8:19</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_019">zac 8:19</a></font></span></p>Thus saith the Lord - This verse is a final decision of the case: provided they do these things required, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/zac008.htm#016">Zac 8:16-17</a>, then shall the fasting cease, and turn into joyful feasts. The fourth month - Wherein the city was taken by the Babylonians. The fifth - In which the temple was burnt. The seventh - Wherein Gedaliah was killed. The tenth - On the tenth day whereof the king of Babylon's army sat down before the city. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/zac008.htm#020">Zechariah 8:20</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_020">zac 8:20</a></font></span></p>People - Multitudes. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/zac008.htm#021">Zechariah 8:21</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_021">zac 8:21</a></font></span></p>I will go also - The invited, shall with as much zeal embrace the motion, as others made it. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/zac008.htm#022">Zechariah 8:22</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_022">zac 8:22</a></font></span></p>In Jerusalem - Literally understood, you have the first fruits of them mentioned, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/act002.htm#010">Act 2:10-12</a>. Mystically, Jerusalem is the church of Christ. To pray - To perform all gospel - worship. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/zac008.htm#023">Zechariah 8:23</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_023">zac 8:23</a></font></span></p>Ten men - That is, many men. All languages - No nation is any longer excluded. A Jew - To whom the gospel was first preached. We have heard - And now see, and are assured. That God - The true God, the only true God, whom to know is life eternal.
