 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible</i>, by John Wesley, [1754-65], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro024.htm#001">Proverbs Chapter 24</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro024.htm#002">Proverbs 24:2</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_002">pro 24:2</a></font></span></p>Destruction - How they may oppress and destroy others. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro024.htm#003">Proverbs 24:3</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_003">pro 24:3</a></font></span></p>Through wisdom - There is no need that thou shouldest raise thy family by ruining others, which thou mayest more effectually do by wisdom. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro024.htm#004">Proverbs 24:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">pro 24:4</a></font></span></p>By knowledge - Which in scripture phrase includes the love and practice of what we know. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro024.htm#005">Proverbs 24:5</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_005">pro 24:5</a></font></span></p>Is strong - Is courageous and resolute, and able by wisdom to do greater things than others can accomplish by strength. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro024.htm#006">Proverbs 24:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">pro 24:6</a></font></span></p>Thy war - War is better managed by wisdom than by strength. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro024.htm#007">Proverbs 24:7</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_007">pro 24:7</a></font></span></p>A fool - For a wicked man, whose sins enfeeble his mind, and make it incapable of wisdom. Openeth not - He knows not how to speak acceptably, and profitably in the publick assembly. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro024.htm#008">Proverbs 24:8</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_008">pro 24:8</a></font></span></p>Shall be called - Though he cover his wicked devices with fair pretences, he shall be branded with that infamy which is due to him. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro024.htm#009">Proverbs 24:9</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_009">pro 24:9</a></font></span></p>The thought - The very inward thought or contrivance of evil, is a sin in God's sight. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro024.htm#011">Proverbs 24:11</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_011">pro 24:11</a></font></span></p>Deliver - When it is in thy power. Drawn - By the violence of lawless men. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro024.htm#012">Proverbs 24:12</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_012">pro 24:12</a></font></span></p>Consider - That this is only a frivolous excuse. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro024.htm#013">Proverbs 24:13</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_013">pro 24:13</a></font></span></p>Eat - This is not a command, but a concession, and is only here expressed to illustrate the following verse. Honey in those parts was an usual food. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro024.htm#014">Proverbs 24:14</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_014">pro 24:14</a></font></span></p>A reward - It is not only sweet for the present, but brings a sure and everlasting reward. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro024.htm#016">Proverbs 24:16</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_016">pro 24:16</a></font></span></p>Falleth - Into calamities, of which he evidently speaks both in the foregoing verse, and in the following branch of this verse. Fall - Frequently into irrecoverable destruction. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro024.htm#017">Proverbs 24:17</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_017">pro 24:17</a></font></span></p>Falleth - Into mischief. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro024.htm#018">Proverbs 24:18</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_018">pro 24:18</a></font></span></p>From him - Understand, upon thee, which is implied in the Hebrew phrase. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro024.htm#020">Proverbs 24:20</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_020">pro 24:20</a></font></span></p>No reward - All his happiness shall quickly and eternally perish. The candle - All their comfort and glory shall cease. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro024.htm#021">Proverbs 24:21</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_021">pro 24:21</a></font></span></p>To change - That are unstable in their obedience to God or to the king. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro024.htm#022">Proverbs 24:22</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_022">pro 24:22</a></font></span></p>Who knoweth - Who can conceive how sore and sudden will be the ruin of them that fear not God, and the king. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro024.htm#023">Proverbs 24:23</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_023">pro 24:23</a></font></span></p>These - The counsels following, to the end of the chapter, no less than those hitherto mentioned, are worthy of their consideration. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro024.htm#025">Proverbs 24:25</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_025">pro 24:25</a></font></span></p>Rebuke - That publickly and judicially rebuke and condemn the wicked. Delight - The peace of a good conscience. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro024.htm#026">Proverbs 24:26</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_026">pro 24:26</a></font></span></p>Shall kiss - Shall respect him. A right answer - That speaks pertinently and plainly, and truly. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro024.htm#032">Proverbs 24:32</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_032">pro 24:32</a></font></span></p>Received - I learned wisdom by his folly.
