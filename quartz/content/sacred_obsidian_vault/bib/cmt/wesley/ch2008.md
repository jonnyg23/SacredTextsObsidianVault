 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch2008.htm#011">2 Chronicles 8:11</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_011">ch2 8:11</a></font></span></p>The house - He built this house for her; because the ark was now in the house of David, which therefore ought to be kept pure and free from the very danger and appearance of pollution. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch2008.htm#014">2 Chronicles 8:14</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_014">ch2 8:14</a></font></span></p>Man of God - A prophet inspired by God in these matters, whose commands therefore are the commands of God. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch2008.htm#016">2 Chronicles 8:16</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_016">ch2 8:16</a></font></span></p>Prepared - All the materials were procured, and in all points fitted and compleated before - hand.
