 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible</i>, by John Wesley, [1754-65], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk005.htm#001">Luke Chapter 5</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk005.htm#001">Luke 5:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">luk 5:1</a></font></span></p><a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat004.htm#018">Mat 4:18</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/mar001.htm#016">Mar 1:16</a>. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk005.htm#006">Luke 5:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">luk 5:6</a></font></span></p>Their net brake - Began to tear. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk005.htm#008">Luke 5:8</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_008">luk 5:8</a></font></span></p>Depart from me, for I am a sinful man - And therefore not worthy to be in thy presence. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk005.htm#011">Luke 5:11</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_011">luk 5:11</a></font></span></p>They forsook all and followed him - They had followed him before, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/joh001.htm#043">Joh 1:43</a>, but not so as to forsake all. Till now, they wrought at their ordinary calling. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk005.htm#012">Luke 5:12</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_012">luk 5:12</a></font></span></p><a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat008.htm#002">Mat 8:2</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/mar001.htm#040">Mar 1:40</a>. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk005.htm#014">Luke 5:14</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_014">luk 5:14</a></font></span></p><a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev014.htm#002">Lev 14:2</a>. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk005.htm#016">Luke 5:16</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_016">luk 5:16</a></font></span></p>He withdrew - The expression in the original implies, that he did so frequently. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk005.htm#017">Luke 5:17</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_017">luk 5:17</a></font></span></p>Sitting by - As being more honourable than the bulk of the congregation, who stood. And the power of the Lord was present to heal them - To heal the sickness of their souls, as well as all bodily diseases. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk005.htm#018">Luke 5:18</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_018">luk 5:18</a></font></span></p><a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat009.htm#002">Mat 9:2</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/mar002.htm#003">Mar 2:3</a>. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk005.htm#019">Luke 5:19</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_019">luk 5:19</a></font></span></p>Not being able to bring him in through the multitude, they went round about by a back passage, and going up the stairs on the outside, they came upon the flat - roofed house, and let him down through the trap door, such as was on the top of most of the Jewish houses: doubtless, with such circumspection as the circumstances plainly required. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk005.htm#026">Luke 5:26</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_026">luk 5:26</a></font></span></p>We have seen strange things to. day - Sins forgiven, miracles wrought. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk005.htm#027">Luke 5:27</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_027">luk 5:27</a></font></span></p><a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat009.htm#009">Mat 9:9</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/mar002.htm#014">Mar 2:14</a>. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk005.htm#028">Luke 5:28</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_028">luk 5:28</a></font></span></p>Leaving all - His business and gain. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk005.htm#029">Luke 5:29</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_029">luk 5:29</a></font></span></p>And Levi made him a great entertainment - It was necessarily great, because of the great number of guests. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk005.htm#033">Luke 5:33</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_033">luk 5:33</a></font></span></p>Make prayers - Long and solemn. <a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat009.htm#014">Mat 9:14</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/mar002.htm#018">Mar 2:18</a>. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk005.htm#034">Luke 5:34</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_034">luk 5:34</a></font></span></p>Can ye make - That is, is it proper to make men fast and mourn, during a festival solemnity? 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk005.htm#036">Luke 5:36</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_036">luk 5:36</a></font></span></p>He spake also a parable - Taken from clothes and wine; therefore peculiarly proper at a feast. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk005.htm#039">Luke 5:39</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_039">luk 5:39</a></font></span></p>And no man having drunk old wine - And beside, men are not wont to be immediately freed from old prejudices.
