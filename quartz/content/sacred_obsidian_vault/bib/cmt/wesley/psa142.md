 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible</i>, by John Wesley, [1754-65], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa142.htm#001">Psalms Chapter 142</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa142.htm#003">Psalms 142:3</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_003">psa 142:3</a></font></span></p>Knowest - So as to direct me to it. My path - What paths I should chuse whereby I might escape. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa142.htm#004">Psalms 142:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">psa 142:4</a></font></span></p>Right - hand - The place where the patron or assistant used to stand. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa142.htm#005">Psalms 142:5</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_005">psa 142:5</a></font></span></p>Portion - Even in this life. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa142.htm#007">Psalms 142:7</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_007">psa 142:7</a></font></span></p>Prison - Set me at liberty. Compass - Shall flock to me from all parts, to rejoice and bless God with me and for me.
