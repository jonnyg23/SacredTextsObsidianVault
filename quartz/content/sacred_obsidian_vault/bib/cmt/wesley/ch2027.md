 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch2027.htm#002">2 Chronicles 27:2</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_002">ch2 27:2</a></font></span></p>He did - He did according to all that his father Uzziah did; except in his miscarriages. We must not imitate those we have the greatest esteem for, any farther than we do well; but their failings must be warnings to us, to walk more circumspectly. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch2027.htm#003">2 Chronicles 27:3</a></p>
