 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible</i>, by John Wesley, [1754-65], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch2004.htm#001">2 Chronicles Chapter 4</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch2004.htm#007">2 Chronicles 4:7</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_007">ch2 4:7</a></font></span></p>Their form - The old form which God prescribed to Moses. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch2004.htm#008">2 Chronicles 4:8</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_008">ch2 4:8</a></font></span></p>Ten tables - Whereon the shew - bread was set, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch2004.htm#019">Ch2 4:19</a>. Perhaps each of these had twelve loaves on it. As the house was enlarged, so was the provision. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch2004.htm#016">2 Chronicles 4:16</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_016">ch2 4:16</a></font></span></p>His father - He is so called because Solomon usually called him by that name out of that great respect which he bare to him for his excellent art and service which he did for him: it being usual to call great artists and inventors of things by this name. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch2004.htm#020">2 Chronicles 4:20</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_020">ch2 4:20</a></font></span></p>The manner - According to the prescription of God to Moses. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch2004.htm#022">2 Chronicles 4:22</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_022">ch2 4:22</a></font></span></p>Of gold - In part; they were made of wood, but covered with golden plates.
