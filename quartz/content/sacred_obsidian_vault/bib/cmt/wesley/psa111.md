 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible</i>, by John Wesley, [1754-65], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa111.htm#001">Psalms Chapter 111</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa111.htm#002">Psalms 111:2</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_002">psa 111:2</a></font></span></p>Sought - Diligently meditated upon. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa111.htm#003">Psalms 111:3</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_003">psa 111:3</a></font></span></p>Work - Either all his works, or that eminent branch of those works, his providence towards his people. Righteousness - His justice or faithfulness in performing his word. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa111.htm#004">Psalms 111:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">psa 111:4</a></font></span></p>Remembered - By their own nature, and the lasting benefits flowing from them, which are such as cannot easily be forgotten. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa111.htm#005">Psalms 111:5</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_005">psa 111:5</a></font></span></p>Meat - All necessary provisions for their being and well - being. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa111.htm#007">Psalms 111:7</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_007">psa 111:7</a></font></span></p>The works - All that he doth on the behalf of his people, or against their enemies. Truth - Are exactly agreeable to his promises, and to justice. Commandments - His laws given to the Israelites, especially the moral law. Sure - Constant and unchangeable. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa111.htm#008">Psalms 111:8</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_008">psa 111:8</a></font></span></p>Done - Constituted or ordered. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa111.htm#009">Psalms 111:9</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_009">psa 111:9</a></font></span></p>Redemption - The deliverance out of Egypt, which was a type of that higher redemption by Christ. Commanded - Appointed, or established firmly by his power and authority. For ever - Through all successive generations of his people to the end of the world. Reverend - Terrible to his enemies, venerable in his peoples eyes, and holy in all his dealings with all men. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa111.htm#010">Psalms 111:10</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_010">psa 111:10</a></font></span></p>The fear - True religion. Beginning - Is the only foundation of, and introduction to, true wisdom.
