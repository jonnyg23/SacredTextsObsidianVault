 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible</i>, by John Wesley, [1754-65], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa043.htm#001">Psalms Chapter 43</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa043.htm#001">Psalms 43:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">psa 43:1</a></font></span></p>Nation - So he calls the company of his enemies for their great numbers. Man - Probably Achitophel or Absalom. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa043.htm#003">Psalms 43:3</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_003">psa 43:3</a></font></span></p>Send out - That is, actually discover them. Truth - Thy favour, or the light of thy countenance, and the truth of thy promises made to me; or the true - light, the illumination of thy spirit, and the direction of thy gracious providence, whereby I may be led in the right way, to thy holy hill. Hill - Of Zion, the place of God's presence and worship.
