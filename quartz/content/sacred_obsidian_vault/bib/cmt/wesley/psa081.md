 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible</i>, by John Wesley, [1754-65], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa081.htm#001">Psalms Chapter 81</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa081.htm#005">Psalms 81:5</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_005">psa 81:5</a></font></span></p>Joseph - Among the people of Israel. Testimony - For a witness of that glorious deliverance. He - God. Went - As a captain at the head of his people. Egypt - To execute his judgments upon that land. I - My progenitors, for all the successive generations of Israel make one body, and are sometimes spoken of as one person. A language - The Egyptian language, which at first was unknown to the Israelites, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/gen042.htm#013">Gen 42:13</a>, and probably continued so for some considerable time, because they were much separated both in place and conversation from the Egyptians. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa081.htm#006">Psalms 81:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">psa 81:6</a></font></span></p>Pots - This word denotes all those vessels wherein they carried water, straw, lime, or bricks. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa081.htm#007">Psalms 81:7</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_007">psa 81:7</a></font></span></p>Calledst - At the Red Sea. Secret place - From the dark and cloudy pillar, whence I thundered against the Egyptians. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa081.htm#008">Psalms 81:8</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_008">psa 81:8</a></font></span></p>Testify - This God did presently after he brought them from Meribah, even at Sinai. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa081.htm#010">Psalms 81:10</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_010">psa 81:10</a></font></span></p>Wide - Either to pray for mercies, or to receive the mercies which I am ready to give you. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa081.htm#015">Psalms 81:15</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_015">psa 81:15</a></font></span></p>Him - Unto Israel. Their time - Their happy time. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa081.htm#016">Psalms 81:16</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_016">psa 81:16</a></font></span></p>Honey - With all pleasant and precious fruits.
