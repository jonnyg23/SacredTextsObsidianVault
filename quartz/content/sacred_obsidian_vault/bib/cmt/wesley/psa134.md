 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa134.htm#001">Psalms 134:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">psa 134:1</a></font></span></p>Servant - Peculiarly so called, priests and Levites. Night - Not only by day, but also by night, when their watch was more necessary. Stand - Serve or minister. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa134.htm#003">Psalms 134:3</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_003">psa 134:3</a></font></span></p>Thee - Thee whosoever thou art who dost faithfully perform the duty here commanded.
