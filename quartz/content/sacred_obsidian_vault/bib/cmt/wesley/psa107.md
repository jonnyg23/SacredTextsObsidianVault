 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible</i>, by John Wesley, [1754-65], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa107.htm#001">Psalms Chapter 107</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa107.htm#003">Psalms 107:3</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_003">psa 107:3</a></font></span></p>Gathered - Into their own land. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa107.htm#004">Psalms 107:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">psa 107:4</a></font></span></p>No city - Or rather, no town inhabited, where they might refresh themselves. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa107.htm#006">Psalms 107:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">psa 107:6</a></font></span></p>The Lord - Heb. Unto Jehovah, to the true God. For the Heathens had, many of them, some knowledge of the true God. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa107.htm#007">Psalms 107:7</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_007">psa 107:7</a></font></span></p>Forth - Out of the wilderness. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa107.htm#010">Psalms 107:10</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_010">psa 107:10</a></font></span></p>Darkness - In dark prisons or dungeons. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa107.htm#012">Psalms 107:12</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_012">psa 107:12</a></font></span></p>Heart - The pride and obstinacy of their hearts. Fell - They fell into hopeless miseries. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa107.htm#017">Psalms 107:17</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_017">psa 107:17</a></font></span></p>Afflicted - With sickness. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa107.htm#020">Psalms 107:20</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_020">psa 107:20</a></font></span></p>Word - His command, or blessing. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa107.htm#032">Psalms 107:32</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_032">psa 107:32</a></font></span></p>Exalt him - In public assemblies, and before all persons, as they have opportunity. Elders - The magistrates or rulers; let them not be ashamed nor afraid to speak of God's wonderful works, before the greatest of men. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa107.htm#033">Psalms 107:33</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_033">psa 107:33</a></font></span></p>Rivers - Those grounds which are well watered, and therefore fruitful. And so the water - springs, here, and the standing water, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa107.htm#035">Psa 107:35</a> are taken. Into - Into a dry ground, which is like a parched and barren wilderness. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa107.htm#034">Psalms 107:34</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_034">psa 107:34</a></font></span></p>For - He doth not inflict these judgments without cause, but for the punishment of sin in some, and the prevention of it in others. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa107.htm#035">Psalms 107:35</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_035">psa 107:35</a></font></span></p>Water - Into a well - watered and fruitful land. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa107.htm#036">Psalms 107:36</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_036">psa 107:36</a></font></span></p>Hungry - Poor people who could not provide for themselves. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa107.htm#039">Psalms 107:39</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_039">psa 107:39</a></font></span></p>They - These men, who when they are exalted by God, grow insolent and secure. Low - By God's just judgment. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa107.htm#040">Psalms 107:40</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_040">psa 107:40</a></font></span></p>Contempt - Renders them despicable. Wander - Banishes them from their own courts and kingdoms, and forces them to flee into desolate wildernesses for shelter.
