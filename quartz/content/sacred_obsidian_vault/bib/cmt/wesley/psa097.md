 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible</i>, by John Wesley, [1754-65], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa097.htm#001">Psalms Chapter 97</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa097.htm#001">Psalms 97:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">psa 97:1</a></font></span></p>Isles - The Gentile nations, as this word, used <a href="../../../bib/kjv/isa042.htm#004">Isa 42:4</a>, is expounded, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat012.htm#021">Mat 12:21</a>. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa097.htm#002">Psalms 97:2</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_002">psa 97:2</a></font></span></p>Darkness - A dark cloud doth encompass him. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa097.htm#006">Psalms 97:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">psa 97:6</a></font></span></p>Heavens - The angels, yea God himself from heaven. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa097.htm#007">Psalms 97:7</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_007">psa 97:7</a></font></span></p>Confounded - Let them be ashamed of their folly. Gods - All you whom the Gentiles have made the objects of their worship. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa097.htm#008">Psalms 97:8</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_008">psa 97:8</a></font></span></p>Zion - Thy people dwelling in Zion or Jerusalem, to whom Christ came. Heard - The fame of thy judgments, and the setting up the kingdom of the Messiah. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa097.htm#011">Psalms 97:11</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_011">psa 97:11</a></font></span></p>Light - Joy and happiness. Sown - Is laid up for them.
