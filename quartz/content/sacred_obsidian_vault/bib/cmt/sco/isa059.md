 <p>
 </p><p>Redeemer 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Redemption: Kinsman type, summary. The goel, or Kinsman-Redeemer, is a beautiful type of Christ. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(1) The kinsman redemption was of persons, and an inheritance (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev025.htm#048">Lev 25:48</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev025.htm#025">Lev 25:25</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/gal004.htm#005">Gal 4:5</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/eph001.htm#007">Eph 1:7</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/eph001.htm#011">Eph 1:11</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/eph001.htm#014">Eph 1:14</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(2) The Redeemer must be a kinsman (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev025.htm#048">Lev 25:48</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev025.htm#049">Lev 25:49</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rut003.htm#012">Rut 3:12</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rut003.htm#013">Rut 3:13</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/gal004.htm#004">Gal 4:4</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/heb002.htm#014">Heb 2:14</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/heb002.htm#015">Heb 2:15</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(3) The Redeemer must be able to redeem (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rut004.htm#004">Rut 4:4-6</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer050.htm#034">Jer 50:34</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/joh010.htm#011">Joh 10:11</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/joh010.htm#018">Joh 10:18</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(4) Redemption is effected by the goel paying the just demand in full (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev025.htm#027">Lev 25:27</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/pe1001.htm#018">Pe1 1:18</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/pe1001.htm#019">Pe1 1:19</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/gal003.htm#013">Gal 3:13</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo014.htm#030">Exo 14:30</a>). 
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom003.htm#024">Rom 3:24</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>
 </p><p>come to Zion 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>The time when the "Redeemer shall come to Zion" is fixed, relatively, by (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom011.htm#023">Rom 11:23-29</a>) as following the completion of the Gentile Church. That is also the order of the great dispensational passage, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/act015.htm#014">Act 15:14-17</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>In both, the return of the Lord to Zion follows the outcalling of the Church.
