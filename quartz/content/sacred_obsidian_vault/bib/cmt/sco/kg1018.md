 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/kg1018.htm#003">3 Kings (1 Kings) 18:3</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_003">kg1 18:3</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>Obadiah 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>In such a time as the reign of Ahab and Jezebel a believer's true place was by Elijah's side. Obadiah is a warning type of the men of God who adhere to the world while still seeking to serve God. The secret of the Lord, and the power of the Lord were with Elijah, the separated servant. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Compare (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/ti2002.htm#020">Ti2 2:20-21</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>
