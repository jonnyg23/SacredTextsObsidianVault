 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer046.htm#001">Jeremiah 46:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">jer 46:1</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>Gentiles 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>A near and a far fulfilment of these prophecies against Gentile powers are to be distinguished. In Chapter 46, the near vision is of a Babylonian invasion of Egypt, but verses (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer046.htm#027">Jer 46:27</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer046.htm#028">Jer 46:28</a>) look forward to the judgment of the nations. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat025.htm#032">Mat 25:32</a>), 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>after Armageddon (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rev016.htm#014">Rev 16:14</a>); 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rev019.htm#017">Rev 19:17</a>) 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>and the deliverance of Israel ("Israel," (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/gen012.htm#002">Gen 12:2</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/gen012.htm#003">Gen 12:3</a> 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom011.htm#026">Rom 11:26</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(<a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer050.htm#004">Jer 50:4-7</a>) also looks forward to the last days.
