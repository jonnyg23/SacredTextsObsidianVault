 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Scofield Bible Commentary</i>, by Cyrus Ingerson Scofield, [1917], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk007.htm#001">Luke Chapter 7</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk007.htm#024">Luke 7:24</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_024">luk 7:24</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>were departed 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Having gently removed His servant's doubt, the Lord bears witness to him before others: He knows when to reprove, and where, and when, to praise.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk007.htm#034">Luke 7:34</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_034">luk 7:34</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>sinners 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Sin 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom003.htm#023">Rom 3:23</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk007.htm#037">Luke 7:37</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_037">luk 7:37</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>sinner 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>That is, in the sense of unchaste.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk007.htm#044">Luke 7:44</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_044">luk 7:44</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>Simon 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>See (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/jam002.htm#014">Jam 2:14-26</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>When Jesus would justify the woman in the eyes of Simon, He points to her works, for only through her works could Simon see the proof of her faith; but when He would send the woman away in peace, He points to her faith, not her works. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>See (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/tit002.htm#014">Tit 2:14</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/tit003.htm#004">Tit 3:4-8</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>His own works can never be to the believer his own ground of assurance, which must rest upon the work of Christ. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Compare (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat007.htm#022">Mat 7:22</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat007.htm#023">Mat 7:23</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>See "Assurance"; (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/isa032.htm#017">Isa 32:17</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/jde001.htm#001">Jde 1:1</a>).
