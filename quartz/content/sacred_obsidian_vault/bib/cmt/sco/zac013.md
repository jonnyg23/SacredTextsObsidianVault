 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Scofield Bible Commentary</i>, by Cyrus Ingerson Scofield, [1917], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/zac013.htm#001">Zechariah Chapter 13</a></h3> 
 <p>
 </p><p>And it shall come to pass 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Zechariah 13 now returns to the subject of (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/zac012.htm#010">Zac 12:10</a>). (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/zac013.htm#008">Zac 13:8</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/zac013.htm#009">Zac 13:9</a>) refer to the sufferings of the remnant; (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/isa001.htm#009">Isa 1:9</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom011.htm#005">Rom 11:5</a>) preceding the great battle. Zechariah 14 is a recapitulation of the whole matter. The order is: 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(1) The gathering of the nation, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/zac013.htm#002">Zac 13:2</a>) (see "Armageddon," (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rev016.htm#014">Rev 16:14</a>) (See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rev019.htm#011">Rev 19:11</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(2) the deliverance, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/zac013.htm#003">Zac 13:3</a>); 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(3) the return of Christ to the Mount of Olives, and the physical change of the scene, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/zac013.htm#004">Zac 13:4-8</a>); 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(4) the setting up of the kingdom, and full earthly blessing, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/zac013.htm#009">Zac 13:9-21</a>).
