 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/mic001.htm#001">Micah 1:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">mic 1:1</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>Therefore 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>In (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mic001.htm#006">Mic 1:6-16</a>), the Assyrian invasion is described. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Compare (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/kg2017.htm#001">Kg2 17:1-18</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>This is the local circumstance which gives rise to the prophecy of the greater invasion in the last days. (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mic004.htm#009">Mic 4:9-13</a>) and of the Lord's deliverance at Armageddon. (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rev016.htm#014">Rev 16:14</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rev019.htm#017">Rev 19:17</a>).
