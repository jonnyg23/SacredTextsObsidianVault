 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/isa017.htm#001">Isaiah 17:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">isa 17:1</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>burden of Damascus 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>As in the burden of Moab, there was doubtless a near fulfilment in Sennacherib's approaching invasion, but 17:12-14 as evidently look forward to the final invasion and battle. ("Armageddon," (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rev016.htm#014">Rev 16:14</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rev019.htm#017">Rev 19:17</a>) 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Compare (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/isa010.htm#026">Isa 10:26-34</a>).
