 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom013.htm#002">Romans 13:2</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_002">rom 13:2</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>damnation 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Condemnation, that is, in the sense of judgment by the magistrate. 
 </p><p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom013.htm#008">Romans 13:8</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_008">rom 13:8</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>Owe 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>See (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev019.htm#013">Lev 19:13</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro022.htm#007">Pro 22:7</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom013.htm#011">Romans 13:11</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_011">rom 13:11</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>nearer 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>"Nearer" in the sense of the full result of salvation in glory. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom001.htm#016">Rom 1:16</a>).
