 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa039.htm#001">Psalms 39:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">psa 39:1</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>Jeduthun 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Jeduthun, a Levite, chief singer and instructor. See (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1009.htm#016">Ch1 9:16</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1016.htm#038">Ch1 16:38</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1016.htm#041">Ch1 16:41</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1016.htm#042">Ch1 16:42</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1025.htm#001">Ch1 25:1</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1025.htm#003">Ch1 25:3</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1025.htm#006">Ch1 25:6</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch2005.htm#012">Ch2 5:12</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch2035.htm#015">Ch2 35:15</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/neh011.htm#017">Neh 11:17</a>). He is mentioned in Psalms 39, 62, 77. Jeduthun was first called Ethan.
