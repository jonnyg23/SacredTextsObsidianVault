 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/isa052.htm#003">Isaiah 52:3</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_003">isa 52:3</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>redeemed 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Hebrew, "goel," Redemption (Kinsman type). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/isa059.htm#020">Isa 59:20</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/isa052.htm#012">Isaiah 52:12</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_012">isa 52:12</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>For ye shall 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Contrast (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo012.htm#033">Exo 12:33</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo012.htm#039">Exo 12:39</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/isa052.htm#014">Isaiah 52:14</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_014">isa 52:14</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>so marred 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>The literal rendering is terrible: "So marred from the form of man was His aspect that His appearance was not that of a son of man" -- that is, not human -- the effect of the brutalities described in (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat026.htm#067">Mat 26:67</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat026.htm#068">Mat 26:68</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat027.htm#027">Mat 27:27-30</a>).
