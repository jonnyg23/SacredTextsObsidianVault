 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Scofield Bible Commentary</i>, by Cyrus Ingerson Scofield, [1917], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev014.htm#001">Leviticus Chapter 14</a></h3> 
 <p>
 </p><p>go forth 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>As a type of Gospel salvation the points are: 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>1. The leper does nothing (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom004.htm#005">Rom 4:5</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom004.htm#005">Rom 4:5</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>2. the priest seeks the leper, not the leper the priest (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk019.htm#010">Luk 19:10</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>3. "with-out shedding of blood is no remission" (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/heb009.htm#022">Heb 9:22</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>4. "and if Christ be not raised, your faith is vain" (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/co1015.htm#017">Co1 15:17</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev014.htm#004">Leviticus 14:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">lev 14:4</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>birds 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>The bird slain, and the live bird, dipped in blood and released, present the two aspects of salvation in (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom004.htm#025">Rom 4:25</a>) "delivered for our offences, and raised again for our justification."  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev014.htm#005">Leviticus 14:5</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_005">lev 14:5</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>vessel 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>The earthen vessel typifies the humanity of Christ, as the running water typifies the Holy Spirit as the "Spirit of life" (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom008.htm#002">Rom 8:2</a>) "put to death in the flesh, but quickened by the Spirit." (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/pe1003.htm#018">Pe1 3:18</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev014.htm#020">Leviticus 14:20</a></p>
