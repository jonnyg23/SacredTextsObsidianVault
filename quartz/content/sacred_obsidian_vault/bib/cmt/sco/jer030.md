 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Scofield Bible Commentary</i>, by Cyrus Ingerson Scofield, [1917], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer030.htm#001">Jeremiah Chapter 30</a></h3> 
 <p>
 </p><p>The word 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>The writings of Jeremiah in chapters 30 to 36, cannot with certainty be arranged in consecutive order. Certain dates are mentioned (for example (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer032.htm#001">Jer 32:1</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer033.htm#001">Jer 33:1</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer034.htm#001">Jer 34:1</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer034.htm#008">Jer 34:8</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer035.htm#001">Jer 35:1</a>) but retrospectively. The narrative, so far as Jeremiah gives a narrative, is resumed at (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer037.htm#001">Jer 37:1</a>). These chapters constitute a kind of summary of prophecy concerning Israel as a nation, looking on especially to the last days, the day of the Lord, and the kingdom-age to follow. If the marginal references are carefully followed the order will become clear. But these prophecies are interspersed with much historical matter concerning Jeremiah and his time.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer030.htm#002">Jeremiah 30:2</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_002">jer 30:2</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>Write 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Three "writings" by Jeremiah are to be distinguished: 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(1) This is impersonal -- a general prophecy, and probably the earliest. (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer030.htm#001">Jer 30:1-31</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer030.htm#040">Jer 30:40</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(2) The prophecy destroyed by Jehoiakim. (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer001.htm#001">Jer 1:1-36</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer001.htm#023">Jer 1:23</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(3) The destroyed writing re-written doubtless the writing preserved to us. (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer036.htm#027">Jer 36:27</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer030.htm#006">Jeremiah 30:6</a></p>
