 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Scofield Bible Commentary</i>, by Cyrus Ingerson Scofield, [1917], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa046.htm#001">Psalms Chapter 46</a></h3> 
 <p>
 </p><p>Alamoth 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Alamoth, "soprano," from almah, a virgin. Some have thought the alamoth, "virgins," were a temple choir, singing antiphonally to the sheminith, or male choir. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa006.htm#001">Psa 6:1</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>But contr, see (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1015.htm#020">Ch1 15:20</a>).
