 </p><p>
 </p><p>(<a href="../../../bib/kjv/gen022.htm#001">Gen 22:1-3</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/jam002.htm#021">Jam 2:21-23</a>) 
 </p><p>

 </p><p>

 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/neh009.htm#014">Nehemiah 9:14</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_014">neh 9:14</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>madest known unto them 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>This important passage fixes beyond all cavil the time when the sabbath, God's rest (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/gen002.htm#001">Gen 2:1-3</a>) was given to man. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Compare (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo020.htm#009">Exo 20:9-11</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>
 </p><p>f In (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo031.htm#013">Exo 31:13-17</a>) the sabbath is invested with the character of a sign between Jehovah and Israel. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat012.htm#001">Mat 12:1</a>).
