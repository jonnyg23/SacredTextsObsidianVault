 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Scofield Bible Commentary</i>, by Cyrus Ingerson Scofield, [1917], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo038.htm#001">Exodus Chapter 38</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo038.htm#001">Exodus 38:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">exo 38:1</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>shittim 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>That is, acacia.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo038.htm#027">Exodus 38:27</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_027">exo 38:27</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>silver 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Silver thus receives its symbolic meaning -- redemption. The sockets were made of the redemption money of the children of Israel. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Compare (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo026.htm#019">Exo 26:19</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo030.htm#013">Exo 30:13-16</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/num003.htm#044">Num 3:44-51</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>
 </p><p>sockets 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo026.htm#019">Exo 26:19</a>).
