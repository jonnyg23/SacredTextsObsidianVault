 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev005.htm#006">Leviticus 5:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">lev 5:6</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>trespass offering 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>The trespass-offerings have in view rather the injury which sin does than its guilt - which is the sin-offering aspect. What is due to God's rights in every human being is here meant. (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa051.htm#004">Psa 51:4</a>); is a perfect expression of this.
