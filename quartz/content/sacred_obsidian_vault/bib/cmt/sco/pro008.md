 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro008.htm#013">Proverbs 8:13</a></p>

 </p><p>fear 
 </p><p>

 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro008.htm#022">Proverbs 8:22</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_022">pro 8:22</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>The Lord possessed me 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>That wisdom is more than the personification of an attribute of God, or of the will of God as best for man, but is a distinct adumbration of Christ, is sure to the devout mind. (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro008.htm#022">Pro 8:22-36</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/joh001.htm#001">Joh 1:1-3</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/col001.htm#017">Col 1:17</a>) can refer to nothing less than the Eternal Son of God.
