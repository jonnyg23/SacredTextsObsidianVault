 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Scofield Bible Commentary</i>, by Cyrus Ingerson Scofield, [1917], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/dan001.htm#001">Daniel Chapter 1</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/dan001.htm#006">Daniel 1:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">dan 1:6</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>Daniel 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>deported 8 years before Ezekiel. (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/kg2024.htm#001">Kg2 24:1</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/kg2024.htm#002">Kg2 24:2</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch2036.htm#005">Ch2 36:5-7</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer025.htm#001">Jer 25:1</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer052.htm#012">Jer 52:12-30</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/dan001.htm#007">Daniel 1:7</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_007">dan 1:7</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>Belteshazzar 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>That is, the king's leader, or attendant. (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/dan002.htm#026">Dan 2:26</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/dan004.htm#008">Dan 4:8</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/dan004.htm#009">Dan 4:9</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/dan004.htm#018">Dan 4:18</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/dan004.htm#019">Dan 4:19</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/dan005.htm#012">Dan 5:12</a>). Identical in meaning with Belshazzar.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/dan001.htm#021">Daniel 1:21</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_021">dan 1:21</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>continued 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>That is, to see the return of the remnant of Judah at the end of the 70 years, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer025.htm#011">Jer 25:11</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer025.htm#012">Jer 25:12</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer029.htm#010">Jer 29:10</a>). Daniel actually lived beyond the first year of Cyrus. (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/dan010.htm#001">Dan 10:1</a>).
