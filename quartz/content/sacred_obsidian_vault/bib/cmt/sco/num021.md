 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Scofield Bible Commentary</i>, by Cyrus Ingerson Scofield, [1917], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num021.htm#001">Numbers Chapter 21</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num021.htm#003">Numbers 21:3</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_003">num 21:3</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>Hormah 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>That is, utter destruction.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num021.htm#009">Numbers 21:9</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_009">num 21:9</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>serpent 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/gen003.htm#014">Gen 3:14</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>The serpent is a symbol of sin judged; brass speaks of the divine judgment, as in the brazen altar 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo027.htm#001">Exo 27:1</a>)", 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>note (2) and self-judgment, as in the laver of brass. The brazen serpent is a type of Christ "made sin for us"; (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/joh003.htm#014">Joh 3:14</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/joh003.htm#015">Joh 3:15</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/co2005.htm#021">Co2 5:21</a>) in bearing our judgment. Historically, the moment is indicated in the cry: "My God, My God, why hast Thou forsaken Me?" (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat027.htm#046">Mat 27:46</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num021.htm#017">Numbers 21:17</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_017">num 21:17</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>Then Israel 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>The spiritual order here is beautiful: 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(1) atonement (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/num021.htm#008">Num 21:8</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/num021.htm#009">Num 21:9</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/joh003.htm#014">Joh 3:14</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/joh003.htm#015">Joh 3:15</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(2) water, symbol of the Spirit bestowed (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/num021.htm#016">Num 21:16</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/joh007.htm#037">Joh 7:37-39</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(3) joy (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/num021.htm#017">Num 21:17</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/num021.htm#018">Num 21:18</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom014.htm#017">Rom 14:17</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(4) power (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/num021.htm#021">Num 21:21-24</a>).
