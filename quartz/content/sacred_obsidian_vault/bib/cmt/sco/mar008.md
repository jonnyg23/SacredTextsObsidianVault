 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/mar008.htm#023">Mark 8:23</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_023">mar 8:23</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>And he took 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Our Lord's action here is most significant, Having abandoned Bethsaida to judgment (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat011.htm#012">Mat 11:12-24</a>). He would neither heal in that village, nor permit further testimony to be borne there (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mar008.htm#026">Mar 8:26</a>). The probation of Bethsaida as a community was ended, but He would still show mercy to individuals. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Compare (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rev003.htm#020">Rev 3:20</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Christ is outside the door of that church, but "If any man hear My voice," etc.
