 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Scofield Bible Commentary</i>, by Cyrus Ingerson Scofield, [1917], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jo1002.htm#001">1 John Chapter 2</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jo1002.htm#001">1 John 2:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">jo1 2:1</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>advocate 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Advocacy is that work of Jesus Christ for sinning saints which He carries on with the Father whereby, because of the eternal efficacy of His own sacrifice, He restores them to fellowship. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Compare (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa023.htm#003">Psa 23:3</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/joh013.htm#010">Joh 13:10</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>
 </p><p>sin 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Sin 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom003.htm#023">Rom 3:23</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jo1002.htm#002">1 John 2:2</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_002">jo1 2:2</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>propitiation 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(Greek, "hilasmos", "that which propitiates"). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom003.htm#025">Rom 3:25</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>
 </p><p>the sins 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Omit words "the sins". 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>
 </p><p>world 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Greek, "kosmos", means "mankind". 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat004.htm#008">Mat 4:8</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jo1002.htm#003">1 John 2:3</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_003">jo1 2:3</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>commandments 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>John uses "commandments" 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(1) in the general sense of the divine will, however revealed, "his word"; (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/jo1002.htm#005">Jo1 2:5</a>); and 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(2) especially of the law of Christ. (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/gal006.htm#002">Gal 6:2</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/jo2001.htm#005">Jo2 1:5</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>See also (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/joh015.htm#010">Joh 15:10-12</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jo1002.htm#005">1 John 2:5</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_005">jo1 2:5</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>perfect 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat005.htm#048">Mat 5:48</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jo1002.htm#012">1 John 2:12</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_012">jo1 2:12</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>sins 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Sin 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom003.htm#023">Rom 3:23</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jo1002.htm#013">1 John 2:13</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_013">jo1 2:13</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>little children 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>The little ones of the family. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/jo1002.htm#028">Jo1 2:28</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jo1002.htm#015">1 John 2:15</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_015">jo1 2:15</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>world 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Greek, "kosmos", means "world-system". (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/jo1003.htm#013">Jo1 3:13</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/joh007.htm#007">Joh 7:7</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rev013.htm#008">Rev 13:8</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jo1002.htm#019">1 John 2:19</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_019">jo1 2:19</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>went out from us 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>"Went out from us," that is, doctrinally. Doubtless then, as now, the deniers of the Son (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/jo1002.htm#022">Jo1 2:22</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/jo1002.htm#023">Jo1 2:23</a>) still called themselves Christians. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Compare (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/ti2001.htm#015">Ti2 1:15</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jo1002.htm#028">1 John 2:28</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_028">jo1 2:28</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>little children 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>The general term for all children.
