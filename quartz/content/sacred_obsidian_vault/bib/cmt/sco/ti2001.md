 </p><p>saved 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom001.htm#016">Rom 1:16</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>
 </p><p>grace 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Grace (in salvation). (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/tit002.htm#011">Tit 2:11</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom003.htm#024">Rom 3:24</a>). 
 </p><p>

 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ti2001.htm#010">2 Timothy 1:10</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_010">ti2 1:10</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>Saviour 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom001.htm#016">Rom 1:16</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ti2001.htm#012">2 Timothy 1:12</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_012">ti2 1:12</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>suffer 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>The believer's resources in a day of general declension and apostasy are: 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(1) Faith (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/ti2001.htm#005">Ti2 1:5</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(2) the Spirit (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/ti2001.htm#006">Ti2 1:6</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/ti2001.htm#007">Ti2 1:7</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(3) the word of God (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/ti2001.htm#013">Ti2 1:13</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/ti2003.htm#001">Ti2 3:1-17</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/ti2004.htm#003">Ti2 4:3</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/ti2004.htm#004">Ti2 4:4</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(4) the grace of Christ (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/ti2002.htm#001">Ti2 2:1</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(5) separation from vessels unto dishonour (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/ti2002.htm#004">Ti2 2:4</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/ti2002.htm#020">Ti2 2:20</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/ti2002.htm#021">Ti2 2:21</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(6) the Lord's sure reward (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/ti2004.htm#007">Ti2 4:7</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/ti2004.htm#008">Ti2 4:8</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(7) the Lord's faithfulness and power (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/ti2002.htm#013">Ti2 2:13</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/ti2002.htm#019">Ti2 2:19</a>).
