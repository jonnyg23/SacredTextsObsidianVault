 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Scofield Bible Commentary</i>, by Cyrus Ingerson Scofield, [1917], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/gen009.htm#001">Genesis Chapter 9</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/gen009.htm#001">Genesis 9:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">gen 9:1</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>and said unto them 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>The Noahic Covenant. The elements are: 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(1) The relation of man to the earth under the Adamic Covenant is confirmed 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/gen008.htm#021">Gen 8:21</a>) 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(2) The order of nature is confirmed (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/gen008.htm#022">Gen 8:22</a>) 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(3) Human government is established (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/gen009.htm#001">Gen 9:1-6</a>) 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(4) Earth is secured against another universal judgment by water (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/gen008.htm#021">Gen 8:21</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/gen009.htm#011">Gen 9:11</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(5) A prophetic declaration is made that from Ham will descend an inferior and servile posterity (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/gen009.htm#024">Gen 9:24</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/gen009.htm#025">Gen 9:25</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(6) A prophetic declaration is made that Shem will have a peculiar relation to Jehovah (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/gen009.htm#026">Gen 9:26</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/gen009.htm#027">Gen 9:27</a>). All divine revelation is through Semitic men, and Christ, after the flesh, descends from Shem. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(7) A prophetic declaration is made that from Japheth will descend the "enlarged" races (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/gen009.htm#027">Gen 9:27</a>). Government, science, and art, speaking broadly, are and have been Japhetic, so that history is the indisputable record of the exact fulfilment of these declarations. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/gen008.htm#021">Gen 8:21</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>for the other seven covenants: 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>EDENIC (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/gen001.htm#028">Gen 1:28</a>) 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>ADAMIC (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/gen003.htm#015">Gen 3:15</a>) 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>ABRAHAMIC (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/gen015.htm#018">Gen 15:18</a>) 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>MOSAIC (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo019.htm#025">Exo 19:25</a>) 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>PALESTINIAN (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/deu030.htm#003">Deu 30:3</a>) 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>DAVIDIC (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa2007.htm#016">Sa2 7:16</a>) 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>NEW (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/heb008.htm#008">Heb 8:8</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/gen009.htm#008">Genesis 9:8</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_008">gen 9:8</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>saying 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/gen008.htm#021">Gen 8:21</a>). 
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/gen009.htm#001">Gen 9:1</a>) 
 </p><p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/gen009.htm#013">Genesis 9:13</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_013">gen 9:13</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>bow 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>The bow is not said to have come into existence at this time, but only to have been here invested with the character of a sign. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Compare (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo031.htm#013">Exo 31:13</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Typically, the bow, seen upon the storm clouds of judgment (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/gen007.htm#011">Gen 7:11</a>) has been thought to speak of the cross where judgment, never to be repeated has been visted upon the believer's sins. (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/gal003.htm#010">Gal 3:10-14</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/heb010.htm#014">Heb 10:14-18</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/gen009.htm#025">Genesis 9:25</a></p>
