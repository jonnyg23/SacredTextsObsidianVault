 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer004.htm#023">Jeremiah 4:23</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_023">jer 4:23</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>without form and void 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Compare (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/gen001.htm#002">Gen 1:2</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>"Without form and void" describes the condition of the earth as the result of judgment; (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer004.htm#024">Jer 4:24-26</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/isa024.htm#001">Isa 24:1</a>) which overthrew the primal order of (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/gen001.htm#001">Gen 1:1</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer004.htm#028">Jeremiah 4:28</a></p>
