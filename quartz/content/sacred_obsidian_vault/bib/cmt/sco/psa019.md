 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa019.htm#009">Psalms 19:9</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_009">psa 19:9</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>fear of the Lord 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>The "fear of the Lord," a phrase of the Old Testament piety, meaning reverential trust, with hatred of evil.
