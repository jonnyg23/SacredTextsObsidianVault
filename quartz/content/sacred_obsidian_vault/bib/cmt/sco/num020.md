 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Scofield Bible Commentary</i>, by Cyrus Ingerson Scofield, [1917], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num020.htm#001">Numbers Chapter 20</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num020.htm#001">Numbers 20:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">num 20:1</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>first month 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>That is, April.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num020.htm#005">Numbers 20:5</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_005">num 20:5</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>water 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/num020.htm#008">Num 20:8</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num020.htm#008">Numbers 20:8</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_008">num 20:8</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>speak ye unto the rock before their eyes 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>See (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo017.htm#005">Exo 17:5</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo017.htm#005">Exo 17:5</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>The rock (Christ) (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/co1010.htm#004">Co1 10:4</a>) once smitten, needs not to be smitten (crucified) again. Moses' act exalted himself (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/num020.htm#010">Num 20:10</a>) and implied (in type) that the one sacrifice was ineffectual, thus denying the eternal efficacy of the blood; (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/heb009.htm#025">Heb 9:25</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/heb009.htm#026">Heb 9:26</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/heb010.htm#003">Heb 10:3</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/heb010.htm#011">Heb 10:11</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/heb010.htm#012">Heb 10:12</a>). The abundant water (grace reaching the need of the people, despite the error of their leader) tells of refreshing and power through the Spirit.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num020.htm#016">Numbers 20:16</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_016">num 20:16</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>angel 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/heb001.htm#004">Heb 1:4</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num020.htm#028">Numbers 20:28</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_028">num 20:28</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>died 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>The death of Aaron marks the end of the wanderings. Henceforth, Israel marches or halts, but does not wander. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/num015.htm#001">Num 15:1</a>).
