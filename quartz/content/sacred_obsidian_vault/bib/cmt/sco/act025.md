 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Scofield Bible Commentary</i>, by Cyrus Ingerson Scofield, [1917], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/act025.htm#001">Acts Chapter 25</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/act025.htm#006">Acts 25:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">act 25:6</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>more than ten 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Or, as some copies read, no more than eight or ten days.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/act025.htm#013">Acts 25:13</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_013">act 25:13</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>Agrippa 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>This (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/act005.htm#013">Act 5:13</a>) was Herod Agrippa II, son of the Herod Agrippa I of (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/act012.htm#001">Act 12:1</a>); and great-grandson of Herod the Great. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat002.htm#001">Mat 2:1</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Bernice, or Berenice, was the sister of Herod Agrippa II. (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/act005.htm#013">Act 5:13</a>).
