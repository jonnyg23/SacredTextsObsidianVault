 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Scofield Bible Commentary</i>, by Cyrus Ingerson Scofield, [1917], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/gen037.htm#001">Genesis Chapter 37</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/gen037.htm#002">Genesis 37:2</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_002">gen 37:2</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>Joseph 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>While it is nowhere asserted that Joseph was a type of Christ, the analogies are too numerous to be accidental. They are: 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(1) both were especial objects of a father's love (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/gen037.htm#003">Gen 37:3</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat003.htm#017">Mat 3:17</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/joh003.htm#035">Joh 3:35</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/joh005.htm#020">Joh 5:20</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(2) both were hated by their brethren (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/gen037.htm#004">Gen 37:4</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/joh015.htm#025">Joh 15:25</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(3) the superior claims of both were rejected by their brethren (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/gen037.htm#008">Gen 37:8</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat021.htm#037">Mat 21:37-39</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/joh015.htm#024">Joh 15:24</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/joh015.htm#025">Joh 15:25</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(4) the brethren of both conspired against them to slay them (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/gen037.htm#018">Gen 37:18</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat026.htm#003">Mat 26:3</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat026.htm#004">Mat 26:4</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(5) Joseph was, in intent and figure, slain by his brethren, as was Christ (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/gen037.htm#024">Gen 37:24</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat027.htm#035">Mat 27:35-37</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(6) each became a blessing among the Gentiles, and gained a Gentile bride (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/gen041.htm#001">Gen 41:1-45</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/act015.htm#014">Act 15:14</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/eph005.htm#025">Eph 5:25-32</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(7) as Joseph reconciled his brethren to himself, and afterward exalted them, so will it be with Christ and His Jewish brethren (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/gen045.htm#001">Gen 45:1-15</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/deu030.htm#001">Deu 30:1-10</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/hos002.htm#014">Hos 2:14-18</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom011.htm#001">Rom 11:1</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom011.htm#015">Rom 11:15</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom011.htm#025">Rom 11:25</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom011.htm#026">Rom 11:26</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/gen037.htm#035">Genesis 37:35</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_035">gen 37:35</a></font></span></p>

 </p><p>

 </p><p>
