 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Scofield Bible Commentary</i>, by Cyrus Ingerson Scofield, [1917], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/act016.htm#001">Acts Chapter 16</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/act016.htm#007">Acts 16:7</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_007">act 16:7</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>suffered 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>R.V. adds "of Jesus", as in the best authorities.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/act016.htm#009">Acts 16:9</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_009">act 16:9</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>prayed him 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>beseeching him. Here the Gospel turns toward Europe.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/act016.htm#010">Acts 16:10</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_010">act 16:10</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>we 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>The change here from "they," as in the preceding verses, to "we" indicates that at Troas, Luke, the narrator, joined Paul's company.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/act016.htm#012">Acts 16:12</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_012">act 16:12</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>colony 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>That is, a Roman colony.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/act016.htm#013">Acts 16:13</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_013">act 16:13</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>was wont 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>might legally, that is, a legal meeting-place for Jews where there was no synagogue.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/act016.htm#016">Acts 16:16</a></p>

 <p>
 </p><p>spirit 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(Greek, "pneuma", "a Python").  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/act016.htm#017">Acts 16:17</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_017">act 16:17</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>most high God 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat007.htm#022">Mat 7:22</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>This marks the "spirit", (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/act016.htm#018">Act 16:18</a>); as being a demon. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>
 </p><p>salvation
