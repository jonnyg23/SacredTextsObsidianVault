 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jon004.htm#006">Jonah 4:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">jon 4:6</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>prepared 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Four prepared things. (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/jon001.htm#017">Jon 1:17</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/jon004.htm#006">Jon 4:6</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/jon004.htm#007">Jon 4:7</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/jon004.htm#008">Jon 4:8</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jon004.htm#008">Jonah 4:8</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_008">jon 4:8</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>fainted 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Compare (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/kg1019.htm#004">Kg1 19:4-8</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Taken as a lesson in service we have in Jonah a servant, 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(1) disobedient, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/jon001.htm#001">Jon 1:1-11</a>); 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(2) afflicted, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/jon001.htm#012">Jon 1:12-17</a>); 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(3) praying (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/jon002.htm#001">Jon 2:1-9</a>(; 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(4) delivered, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/jon002.htm#010">Jon 2:10</a>); 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(5) recommissioned, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/jon003.htm#001">Jon 3:1-3</a>); 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(6) powerful, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/jon003.htm#004">Jon 3:4-9</a>); 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(7) perplexed and fainting but not forsaken, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/jon004.htm#001">Jon 4:1-11</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jon004.htm#011">Jonah 4:11</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_011">jon 4:11</a></font></span></p>
