 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Scofield Bible Commentary</i>, by Cyrus Ingerson Scofield, [1917], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/zac002.htm#001">Zechariah Chapter 2</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/zac002.htm#001">Zechariah 2:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">zac 2:1</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>again 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>As in (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/zac001.htm#008">Zac 1:8-11</a>); the "man" of (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/zac002.htm#001">Zac 2:1</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/zac002.htm#001">Zac 2:1</a>) is "the angel that talked with me" of (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/zac001.htm#003">Zac 1:3</a>). The measuring-line (or reed) is used by Ezekiel (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze040.htm#003">Eze 40:3</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze040.htm#005">Eze 40:5</a>) as a symbol of preparation for rebuilding the city and temple in the kingdom-age. Here also it has that meaning, as the context (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/zac002.htm#004">Zac 2:4-13</a>) shows. The subject of the vision is the restoration of nation and city. In no sense has this prophecy been fulfilled. The order is: 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(1) The Lord in glory in Jerusalem, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/zac002.htm#005">Zac 2:5</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Compare (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat024.htm#029">Mat 24:29</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat024.htm#030">Mat 24:30</a>) 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(2) the restoration of Israel, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/zac002.htm#006">Zac 2:6</a>); 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(3) the judgment of Jehovah upon the nations, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/zac002.htm#008">Zac 2:8</a>); "after the glory" (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat025.htm#031">Mat 25:31</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat025.htm#032">Mat 25:32</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(4) the full blessing of the earth in the kingdom, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/zac002.htm#010">Zac 2:10-13</a>); 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>See "Kingdom (Old Testament)" (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/gen001.htm#026">Gen 1:26</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/zac012.htm#008">Zac 12:8</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>"Israel,"; (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/gen012.htm#002">Gen 12:2</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom011.htm#026">Rom 11:26</a>).
