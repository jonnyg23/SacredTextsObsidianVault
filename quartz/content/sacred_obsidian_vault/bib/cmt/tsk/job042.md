 <p>Therefore:  From this it appears that Job was considered as a priest, not only to his own family, but also to others.  For his children he offered burnt offerings (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/job001.htm#005">Job 1:5</a>), and now he is to make the same kind of offerings, accompanied with intercession, in behalf of his three friends.  This is a full proof of the innocence and integrity of Job.
