 </p><p>Zedekiah:  As the sons of Jeconiah are enumerated in the succeeding verse, and as Zedekiah is no where else mentioned as the son of Jeconiah, but as the son of Josiah, it is highly probable that son here means successor. <a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1003.htm#015">Ch1 3:15</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/kg2024.htm#017">Kg2 24:17</a>, being his uncle

 <p>Assir:  As Salathiel was not the son of Assir, but of Jeconiah, it is probable that the word assir, which signifies a prisoner, is an ephithet applied to Jeconiah, who was a long time prisoner at Babylon.
