 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_000">psa 117:0</a></font></span></p>
 <p>Overview
 </p><p><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa117.htm#001">Psa 117:1</a>, An exhortation to praise God for his mercy and truth. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa117.htm#001">Psalms 117:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">psa 117:1</a></font></span></p>
 <p>(Title), This Psalm, the shortest in the whole collection, is written as a part of the preceding in thirty-two manuscripts.  It celebrates the deliverance from the Babylonian captivity, the grand type of the redemption of the world by the Messiah.
