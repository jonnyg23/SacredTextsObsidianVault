 <p>A day of darkness:  "The quantity of these insects," says a French author,"is incredible to all who have not themselves witnessed their astonishing numbers; the whole earth is covered with them for the space of several leagues.  The noise they make in browsing on the trees and herbage may be heard at a great distance, and resembles that of an army in secret.
