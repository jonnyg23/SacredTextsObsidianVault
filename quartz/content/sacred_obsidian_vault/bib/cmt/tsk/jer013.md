 <p>Overview
 </p><p><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer013.htm#001">Jer 13:1</a>, By the type of a linen girdle, hidden at Euphrates, God prefigures the destruction of his people; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer013.htm#012">Jer 13:12</a>, Under the parable of the bottles filled with wine he foretells their drunkenness in misery; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer013.htm#015">Jer 13:15</a>, He exhorts to prevent their future judgments; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer013.htm#022">Jer 13:22</a>, He shews their abominations are the cause thereof.
