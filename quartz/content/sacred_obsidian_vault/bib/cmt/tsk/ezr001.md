 <p>Overview
 </p><p><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ezr001.htm#001">Ezr 1:1</a>, The proclamation of Cyrus for the building of the temple; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/ezr001.htm#005">Ezr 1:5</a>, The people provide for their return; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/ezr001.htm#007">Ezr 1:7</a>, Cyrus restores the vessels of the temple to Sheshbazzar. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ezr001.htm#001">Ezra 1:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">ezr 1:1</a></font></span></p>
 <p>Now in the:  <a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch2036.htm#022">Ch2 36:22</a>, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch2036.htm#023">Ch2 36:23</a> 
 </p><p>Cyrus:  Cyrus is said to have been the son of Cambyses, king of Persia, and Mandane, daughter of Astyages, king of Media.  he was born about 600 years before Christ, and died at the age of 70, after a reign of 30 years.  He was mentioned by name, and his conquests foretold, by the prophet Isaiah, about a century before his birth.  Josephus states the partiality he evinced towards the Jews, arose from the circumstance of these prophecies being shown him, probably by Daniel.
 </p><p>by the mouth:  <a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer025.htm#012">Jer 25:12-14</a>, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer029.htm#010">Jer 29:10</a>, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer033.htm#007">Jer 33:7-13</a>

 </p><p>five thousand:  Instead of 5,400, the enumeration of the articles in <a href="../../../bib/kjv/ti2002.htm#009">Ti2 2:9</a>, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/ti2002.htm#010">Ti2 2:10</a>, only amounts to 2,499; but in the account, Esdras <a href="../../../bib/kjv/ti2002.htm#013">Ti2 2:13</a>, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/ti2002.htm#014">Ti2 2:14</a>, the amount is 5,469, as will be evident from the following statements:
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Temple Vessels  In Ezra In Esdras  Gold chargers 30  Gold cups 1,000   Silver chargers 1,000  Silver cups 1,000   Knives 29  Silver censers 29   Gold basons 30  Gold vials 30   Silver basons 410  Silver vials 2,410   Other vessels 1,000  Other vessels 1,000   Said to be 5,400  Total 5,469   But only 2,499      Deficiency 2,901  Surplus 69   It is supposed that they actually amounted to 5,400, but that only the chief of there specified, the spoons, etc. being omitted.
