 <p>Overview
 </p><p><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze015.htm#001">Eze 15:1</a>, By the unfitness of the vine branch for any work, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze015.htm#006">Eze 15:6</a>, is shewn the rejection of Jerusalem.
