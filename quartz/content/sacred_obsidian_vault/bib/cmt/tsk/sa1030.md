 </p><p>the brook Besor:  This brook or torrent, it is evident from the circumstances of the history, must be in the south-west part of Judea, and must empty itself into the Mediterranean Sea.  In the more particular situation of it writers are not agreed.  Some suppose it to be between Gaza and Rhinocorura; but Jerome places it between Rhinocorura and Egypt.  It is supposed by some to be the same as the river of the wilderness (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/amo006.htm#014">Amo 6:14</a>), and the river of Egypt, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/jos015.htm#004">Jos 15:4</a>.
