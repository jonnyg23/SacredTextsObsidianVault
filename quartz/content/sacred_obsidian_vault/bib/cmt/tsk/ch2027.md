 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Treasury of Scriptural Knowledge</i>, by R.A. Torrey, [ca. 1880], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch2027.htm#001">2 Chronicles Chapter 27</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch2001.htm#001">2 Chronicles</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_000">ch2 27:0</a></font></span></p>
 <p>Overview
 </p><p><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch2027.htm#001">Ch2 27:1</a>, Jotham reigning well, prospers; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch2027.htm#005">Ch2 27:5</a>, He subdues the Ammonites; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch2027.htm#007">Ch2 27:7</a>, His reign and death; Ahaz succeeds him. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch2027.htm#001">2 Chronicles 27:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">ch2 27:1</a></font></span></p>
 <p>am 3246-3262, bc 753-742
 </p><p>twenty and five:  <a href="../../../bib/kjv/kg2015.htm#032">Kg2 15:32</a>, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/kg2015.htm#033">Kg2 15:33-38</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1003.htm#012">Ch1 3:12</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/isa001.htm#001">Isa 1:1</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/hos001.htm#001">Hos 1:1</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/mic001.htm#001">Mic 1:1</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat001.htm#009">Mat 1:9</a>, Joatham 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch2027.htm#002">2 Chronicles 27:2</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_002">ch2 27:2</a></font></span></p>
 <p>And he did:  <a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch2026.htm#004">Ch2 26:4</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/kg2015.htm#034">Kg2 15:34</a> 
 </p><p>he entered not:  He copied his father's conduct as far as it was pious and constitutional; and avoided his transgression. <a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch2026.htm#016">Ch2 26:16-21</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa119.htm#120">Psa 119:120</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/act005.htm#013">Act 5:13</a> 
 </p><p>the people:  <a href="../../../bib/kjv/kg2015.htm#035">Kg2 15:35</a> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch2027.htm#003">2 Chronicles 27:3</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_003">ch2 27:3</a></font></span></p>
 <p>high gate:  <a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch2023.htm#020">Ch2 23:20</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer020.htm#002">Jer 20:2</a> 
 </p><p>Ophel:  or, the tower, "The wall," says the Targum, "of the interior palace." Ophel appears to have been a tower, or fort, on the city wall, in which we read "the Nehthinim dwelt." <a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch2033.htm#014">Ch2 33:14</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/neh003.htm#026">Neh 3:26</a>, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/neh003.htm#027">Neh 3:27</a> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch2027.htm#004">2 Chronicles 27:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">ch2 27:4</a></font></span></p>
 <p>he built cities:  <a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch2011.htm#005">Ch2 11:5-10</a>, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch2014.htm#007">Ch2 14:7</a>, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch2026.htm#009">Ch2 26:9</a>, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch2026.htm#010">Ch2 26:10</a> 
 </p><p>the mountains:  <a href="../../../bib/kjv/jos014.htm#012">Jos 14:12</a>, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/jos014.htm#013">Jos 14:13</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk001.htm#039">Luk 1:39</a> 
 </p><p>castles and towers:  These castles and towers he doubtless built built for the protection of the country people against marauders. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch2027.htm#005">2 Chronicles 27:5</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_005">ch2 27:5</a></font></span></p>
 <p>the king of the Ammonites:  We find here, that he brought the Ammonites under a heavy tribute for three years; but whether this was the effect of his prevailing against them, is not so evident.  Some think that they paid this tribute for three years, and then revolted; that, in consequence, he attacked them, and their utter subjection was the result. <a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch2020.htm#001">Ch2 20:1</a>; Judg. 11:4-33; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa2010.htm#001">Sa2 10:1-14</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer049.htm#001">Jer 49:1-6</a> 
 </p><p>ten thousand:  Rather, "ten thousand cors (korim) of wheat."  The cor was same as the homer, and contained about 32 pecks 1 pint.
 </p><p>So much:  Heb. This 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch2027.htm#006">2 Chronicles 27:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">ch2 27:6</a></font></span></p>
 <p>Jotham:  <a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch2026.htm#005">Ch2 26:5</a> 
 </p><p>prepared:  or, established, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch2019.htm#003">Ch2 19:3</a> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch2027.htm#007">2 Chronicles 27:7</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_007">ch2 27:7</a></font></span></p>
 <p>Now the rest:  <a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch2020.htm#034">Ch2 20:34</a>, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch2026.htm#022">Ch2 26:22</a>, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch2026.htm#023">Ch2 26:23</a>, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch2032.htm#032">Ch2 32:32</a>, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch2032.htm#033">Ch2 32:33</a>, am 3262, bc 742
 </p><p>they are written:  There is not so much found in the book of Kings, which we have now, as here.  in both places we have abridged accounts; the larger histories having been lost.
