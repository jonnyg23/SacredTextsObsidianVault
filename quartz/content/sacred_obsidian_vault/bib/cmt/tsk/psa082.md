 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Treasury of Scriptural Knowledge</i>, by R.A. Torrey, [ca. 1880], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa082.htm#001">Psalms Chapter 82</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa001.htm#001">Psalms</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_000">psa 82:0</a></font></span></p>
 <p>Overview
 </p><p><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa082.htm#001">Psa 82:1</a>, The psalmist, having exhorted the judges, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa082.htm#005">Psa 82:5</a>, and reproved their negligence, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa082.htm#008">Psa 82:8</a>, prays God to judge.
 </p><p>Some refer this psalm to the time of David, and others to that of Hezekiah; but it is more probable that it was composed when Jehoshaphat reformed the courts throughout his kingdom. <a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch2019.htm#006">Ch2 19:6</a>, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch2019.htm#007">Ch2 19:7</a> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa082.htm#001">Psalms 82:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">psa 82:1</a></font></span></p>
 <p>of Asaph:  or, for Asaph
 </p><p>God:  etc. Or, "God standeth in the assembly of God, ail, he judgeth among the judges."  Elohim̀  God is among His own people; and presides especially in those courts of justice which Himself has established. <a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo018.htm#021">Exo 18:21</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch2019.htm#006">Ch2 19:6</a>, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch2019.htm#007">Ch2 19:7</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/ecc005.htm#008">Ecc 5:8</a> 
 </p><p>the gods:  <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa082.htm#006">Psa 82:6</a>, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa082.htm#007">Psa 82:7</a>, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa138.htm#001">Psa 138:1</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo021.htm#006">Exo 21:6</a>, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo022.htm#028">Exo 22:28</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/joh010.htm#035">Joh 10:35</a>
