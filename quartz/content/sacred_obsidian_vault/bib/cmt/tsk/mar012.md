 <p>Overview
 </p><p><a href="../../../bib/kjv/mar012.htm#001">Mar 12:1</a>, In a parable of the vineyard let out to wicked husbandmen Christ foretells the reprobation of the Jews, and the calling of the Gentiles; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/mar012.htm#013">Mar 12:13</a>, He avoids the snare of the Pharisees and Herodians about paying tribute to Caesar; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/mar012.htm#018">Mar 12:18</a>, convicts the Sadducees of error, who denied the resurrection; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/mar012.htm#028">Mar 12:28</a>, resolves the scribe, who questioned of the first commandment; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/mar012.htm#035">Mar 12:35</a>, refutes the opinion that the scribes held of Christ; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/mar012.htm#038">Mar 12:38</a>, bidding the people to beware of their ambition and hypocrisy; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/mar012.htm#041">Mar 12:41</a>, and commends the poor widow for her two mites, above all.
