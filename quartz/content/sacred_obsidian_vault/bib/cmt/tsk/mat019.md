 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_000">mat 19:0</a></font></span></p>
 <p>Overview
 </p><p><a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat019.htm#001">Mat 19:1</a>, Christ heals the sick; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat019.htm#003">Mat 19:3</a>, answers the Pharisees concerning divorcement; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat019.htm#010">Mat 19:10</a>, shows when marriage is necessary; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat019.htm#013">Mat 19:13</a>, receives little children; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat019.htm#016">Mat 19:16</a>, instructs the young man how to attain eternal life; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat019.htm#020">Mat 19:20</a>, and how to be perfect; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat019.htm#023">Mat 19:23</a>, tells his disciples how hard it is for a rich man to enter into the kingdom of God; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat019.htm#027">Mat 19:27</a>, and promises reward to those that forsake anything to follow him. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat019.htm#001">Matthew 19:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">mat 19:1</a></font></span></p>
 <p>that when:  <a href="../../../bib/kjv/mar010.htm#001">Mar 10:1</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/joh010.htm#040">Joh 10:40</a> 
 </p><p>he departed:  This was our Lord's final departure from Galilee, previous to his crucifixion; but he appears to have taken in a large compass in his journey, and passed through the districts east of Jordan.  Some learned men, however, are of opinion, that instead of "beyond Jordan," we should render, "by the side of Jordan," as περαν [Strong's G4008], especially with a genitive, sometimes signifies.
