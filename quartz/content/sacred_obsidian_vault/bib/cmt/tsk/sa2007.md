 </p><p>any of the tribes:  <a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1017.htm#006">Ch1 17:6</a>, any of the judges, Instead of שׁבטי, shivtey, "tribes," we should probably read, with Houbigant, Drs. Waterland and A. Clarke, and others, שׁפטי, shophtey, "judges;" which is the reading in the parallel passage.  Indeed there is but one letter of difference between them, and letters which might be easily mistaken for each other; the apex under the upper stroke of the פ, pay, being the only mark to distinguish it from the ב, baith.  Compare with <a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa2007.htm#011">Sa2 7:11</a>.
