 <p>Overview
 </p><p><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa061.htm#001">Psa 61:1</a>, David flees to God upon his former experience; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa061.htm#004">Psa 61:4</a>, He vows perpetual service unto him, because of his promises.
