 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num001.htm#001">Numbers</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_000">num 0:0</a></font></span></p>
 <p>The book of Numbers is a book containing a series of the most astonishing providences and events.  Every where and in every circumstance God appears; and yet there is no circumstance or occasion which does not justify those signal displays of his grace and mercy; and in every relation we perceive the consistency of the divine intentions, and the propriety of those laws which he established.
