 </p><p>as the trees:  Ahalim, "lign-aloe trees."  This tree, which grows in the East Indies, is described as being eight or ten feet in height, with a stem the thickness of a man's thigh. The blossoms are red, intermingled with yellow, and double like cloves; from which comes a red and white fruit, of the size of a pea, oblong and triangular, with three apartments filled with seed.
