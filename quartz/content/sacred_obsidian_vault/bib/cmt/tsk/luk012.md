 </p><p><a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk012.htm#001">Luk 12:1</a>, Christ preaches to his apostles to avoid hypocrisy, and fearfulness in publishing his doctrine; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk012.htm#013">Luk 12:13</a>, warns the people to beware of covetousness, by the parable of the rich man who set up greater barns; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk012.htm#022">Luk 12:22</a>, We must not be over careful of earthly things, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk012.htm#031">Luk 12:31</a>. but seek the kingdom of God; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk012.htm#033">Luk 12:33</a>, give alms; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk012.htm#035">Luk 12:35</a>, be ready at a knock to open to our Lord whensoever he comes; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk012.htm#041">Luk 12:41</a>, Christ's ministers are to see to their charges, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk012.htm#049">Luk 12:49</a>. and look for persecution; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk012.htm#054">Luk 12:54</a>, The people must take this time of grace; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk012.htm#057">Luk 12:57</a>, because it is a fearful thing to die without reconciliation.
