Fausset and David Brown [1882] at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa145.htm#001">Psalms Chapter 145</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa145.htm#001">Psalms 145:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">psa 145:1</a></font></span></p>A Psalm of praise to God for His mighty, righteous, and gracious government of all men, and of His humble and suffering people in particular. (Psa. 145:1-21)
 <p>
 </p><p>(Compare <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa030.htm#001">Psa 30:1</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>bless thy name--celebrate Thy perfections (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa005.htm#011">Psa 5:11</a>). God is addressed as king, alluding to His government of men.
 </p><p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa145.htm#003">Psalms 145:3</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_003">psa 145:3</a></font></span></p>(Compare <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa018.htm#003">Psa 18:3</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa048.htm#001">Psa 48:1</a>). 
 <p>
 </p><p>greatness--as displayed in His works.
 </p><p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa145.htm#004">Psalms 145:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">psa 145:4</a></font></span></p>shall declare--literally, "they shall declare," that is, all generations.
 <p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa145.htm#005">Psalms 145:5</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_005">psa 145:5</a></font></span></p>I will speak--or, "muse" (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa077.htm#012">Psa 77:12</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa119.htm#015">Psa 119:15</a>). 
 <p>
 </p><p>thy wondrous works--or, "words of thy wonders," that is, which described them (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa105.htm#027">Psa 105:27</a>, Margin).
 </p><p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa145.htm#006">Psalms 145:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">psa 145:6</a></font></span></p>terrible acts--which produce dread or fear.
 <p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa145.htm#007">Psalms 145:7</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_007">psa 145:7</a></font></span></p>memory-- (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa006.htm#005">Psa 6:5</a>), remembrance, or what causes to be remembered. 
 <p>
 </p><p>righteousness--as in <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa143.htm#001">Psa 143:1</a>, goodness according to covenant engagement.
 </p><p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa145.htm#008">Psalms 145:8</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_008">psa 145:8</a></font></span></p>(Compare <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa103.htm#008">Psa 103:8</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa111.htm#004">Psa 111:4</a>). 
 <p>
 </p><p>over all, &amp;c.--rests on all His works.
 </p><p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa145.htm#010">Psalms 145:10</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_010">psa 145:10</a></font></span></p>bless--as in <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa145.htm#001">Psa 145:1</a>, to praise with reverence, more than merely to praise.
 <p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa145.htm#011">Psalms 145:11</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_011">psa 145:11</a></font></span></p>The declaration of God's glory is for the extension of His knowledge and perfections in the world.
 <p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa145.htm#013">Psalms 145:13</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_013">psa 145:13</a></font></span></p>(Compare <a href="../../../bib/kjv/dan004.htm#003">Dan 4:3</a>, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/dan004.htm#034">Dan 4:34</a>).
 <p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa145.htm#014">Psalms 145:14</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_014">psa 145:14</a></font></span></p>(Compare <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa037.htm#017">Psa 37:17</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa054.htm#004">Psa 54:4</a>).
 <p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa145.htm#015">Psalms 145:15</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_015">psa 145:15</a></font></span></p>eyes of . . . thee--or, look with expecting faith (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa104.htm#027">Psa 104:27-28</a>).
 <p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa145.htm#017">Psalms 145:17</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_017">psa 145:17</a></font></span></p>holy . . . works--literally, "merciful" or "kind, goodness" (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa144.htm#002">Psa 144:2</a>) is the corresponding noun. 
 <p>
 </p><p>righteous--in a similar relation of meaning to "righteousness" (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa145.htm#007">Psa 145:7</a>).
 </p><p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa145.htm#018">Psalms 145:18</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_018">psa 145:18</a></font></span></p>(Compare <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa034.htm#007">Psa 34:7</a>, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa034.htm#010">Psa 34:10</a>).
 <p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa145.htm#020">Psalms 145:20</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_020">psa 145:20</a></font></span></p>Those who fear Him (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa145.htm#019">Psa 145:19</a>) are those who are here said to love Him.
 <p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa145.htm#021">Psalms 145:21</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_021">psa 145:21</a></font></span></p>(Compare <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa033.htm#021">Psa 33:21</a>). 
 <p>
 </p><p>all flesh-- (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa065.htm#002">Psa 65:2</a>). The Psalm ends, as it began, with ascriptions of praise, in which the pious will ever delight to join.
 </p><p>
