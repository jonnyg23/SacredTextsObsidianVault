Fausset and David Brown [1882] at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa082.htm#001">Psalms Chapter 82</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa082.htm#001">Psalms 82:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">psa 82:1</a></font></span></p>Before the great Judge, the judges of the earth are rebuked, exhorted, and threatened. (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa082.htm#001">Psa 82:1-8</a>)
 <p>
 </p><p>congregation--(Compare <a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo012.htm#003">Exo 12:3</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo016.htm#001">Exo 16:1</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>of the mighty--that is, of God, of His appointment. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>the gods--or, "judges" (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo021.htm#006">Exo 21:6</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo022.htm#009">Exo 22:9</a>), God's representatives.
 </p><p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa082.htm#002">Psalms 82:2</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_002">psa 82:2</a></font></span></p>accept the persons--literally, "lift up the faces," that is, from dejection, or admit to favor and communion, regardless of merit (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev019.htm#015">Lev 19:15</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro018.htm#005">Pro 18:5</a>).
 <p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa082.htm#003">Psalms 82:3</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_003">psa 82:3</a></font></span></p>So must good judges act (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa010.htm#014">Psa 10:14</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/job029.htm#012">Job 29:12</a>).
 <p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa082.htm#004">Psalms 82:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">psa 82:4</a></font></span></p>poor and needy--(Compare <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa034.htm#010">Psa 34:10</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa041.htm#001">Psa 41:1</a>).
 <p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa082.htm#005">Psalms 82:5</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_005">psa 82:5</a></font></span></p>By the wilful ignorance and negligence of judges, anarchy ensues (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa011.htm#003">Psa 11:3</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa075.htm#003">Psa 75:3</a>). 
 <p>
 </p><p>out of course--(Compare Margin; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa009.htm#006">Psa 9:6</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa062.htm#002">Psa 62:2</a>).
 </p><p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa082.htm#006">Psalms 82:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">psa 82:6</a></font></span></p>Though God admitted their official dignity (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/joh010.htm#034">Joh 10:34</a>), He reminds them of their mortality.
 <p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa082.htm#007">Psalms 82:7</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_007">psa 82:7</a></font></span></p>fall like, &amp;c.--be cut off suddenly (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa020.htm#008">Psa 20:8</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa091.htm#007">Psa 91:7</a>).
 <p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa082.htm#008">Psalms 82:8</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_008">psa 82:8</a></font></span></p>As rightful sovereign of earth, God is invoked personally to correct the evils of His representatives.
