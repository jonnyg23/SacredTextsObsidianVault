 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa112.htm#001">Psalms 112:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">psa 112:1</a></font></span></p>This Psalm may be regarded as an exposition of <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa111.htm#010">Psa 111:10</a>, presenting the happiness of those who fear and obey God, and contrasting the fate of the ungodly. (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa112.htm#001">Psa 112:1-10</a>)
 <p>
 </p><p>True fear produces obedience and this happiness.
 </p><p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa112.htm#002">Psalms 112:2</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_002">psa 112:2</a></font></span></p>Temporal blessings follow the service of God, exceptions occurring only as they are seen by God to be inconsistent with those spiritual blessings which are better.
 <p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa112.htm#004">Psalms 112:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">psa 112:4</a></font></span></p>light--figurative for relief (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa027.htm#001">Psa 27:1</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa097.htm#011">Psa 97:11</a>). 
 <p>
 </p><p>the upright--are like God (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk006.htm#036">Luk 6:36</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa111.htm#004">Psa 111:4</a>).
 </p><p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa112.htm#005">Psalms 112:5</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_005">psa 112:5</a></font></span></p>Generosity, sound judgment in business, and confidence in God, form a character which preserves from fear of evil and ensures success against enemies. While a man thus truly pious is liberal, he increases in substance.
 <p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa112.htm#006">Psalms 112:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">psa 112:6</a></font></span></p>not be moved--(compare <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa013.htm#004">Psa 13:4</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa015.htm#005">Psa 15:5</a>).
 <p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa112.htm#008">Psalms 112:8</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_008">psa 112:8</a></font></span></p>heart is established--or, firm in right principles. 
 <p>
 </p><p>see his desire-- (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa050.htm#023">Psa 50:23</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa054.htm#007">Psa 54:7</a>).
 </p><p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa112.htm#010">Psalms 112:10</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_010">psa 112:10</a></font></span></p>Disappointed in their malevolent wishes by the prosperity of the pious, the wicked are punished by the working of their evil passions, and come to naught.
