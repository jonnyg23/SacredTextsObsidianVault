 </i><p align="CENTER">Of the dew and of the olive-oil, and various flowers.</p> 
 <p>THEY showed me the treasure-house of the dew, like oil of the olive, and the appearance of its form, as of all the flowers of the earth; further many angels guarding the treasure-houses of these <i>things</i>, <i>and</i> how they are made to shut and open.</p>
