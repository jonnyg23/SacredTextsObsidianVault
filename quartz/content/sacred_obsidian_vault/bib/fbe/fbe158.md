 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Forgotten Books of Eden</i>, by Rutherford H. Platt, Jr., [1926], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="CENTER">LI.</h3> <i>
 </i><p>Enoch instructs his sons, that they hide not treasures in the earth, but bids them give alms to the poor.</p> 
 <p>STRETCH out your hands to the poor according to your strength.</p> 
 <p>2 Hide not your silver in the earth.</p> 
 <p>3 Help the faithful man in affliction, and affliction will not find you in the time of your trouble.</p> 
 <p>4 And every grievous and cruel yoke that come upon you bear all for the sake of the Lord, and thus you will find your reward in the day of judgement.</p> 
 <p>5 It is good to go morning, midday, and evening into the Lord's dwelling, for the glory of your creator.</p> 
 <p>6 Because every breathing thing glorifies him, and every creature visible and invisible returns him praise.</p>
