 </i><p align="CENTER">An elementary Ode.</p> 
 <p>1 I stretched out my hands to my Lord: and to the Most I High I raised my voice:<br> 2 And I spake with the lips of my heart; and He heard me, when my voice reached I Him:<br> 3 His answer came to me, and gave me the fruits of my labours;<br> 4 And it gave me rest by the grace of the Lord. Hallelujah.</p>
