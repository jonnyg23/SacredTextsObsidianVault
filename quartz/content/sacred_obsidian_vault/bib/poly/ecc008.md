 <p align="center"><a href="../kjv/ecc008.htm">KJV</a></p><p>1  Who<sup><a href="h4310.htm">4310</a></sup> is as the wise<sup><a href="h2450.htm">2450</a></sup> man? and who<sup><a href="h4310.htm">4310</a></sup> knoweth<sup><a href="h3045.htm">3045</a></sup> the interpretation<sup><a href="h6592.htm">6592</a></sup> of a thing?<sup><a href="h1697.htm">1697</a></sup> a man's<sup><a href="h0120.htm">120</a></sup> wisdom<sup><a href="h2451.htm">2451</a></sup> maketh his face<sup><a href="h6440.htm">6440</a></sup> to shine,<sup><a href="h0215.htm">215</a></sup> and the boldness<sup><a href="h5797.htm">5797</a></sup> of his face<sup><a href="h6440.htm">6440</a></sup> shall be changed.<sup><a href="h8132.htm">8132</a></sup> </p>
 <p>2  I<sup><a href="h0589.htm">589</a></sup> counsel thee to keep<sup><a href="h8104.htm">8104</a></sup> the king's<sup><a href="h4428.htm">4428</a></sup> commandment,<sup><a href="h6310.htm">6310</a></sup> and that in regard<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> <sup><a href="h1700.htm">1700</a></sup> of the oath<sup><a href="h7621.htm">7621</a></sup> of God.<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> </p>
 <p>3  Be not<sup><a href="h0408.htm">408</a></sup> hasty<sup><a href="h0926.htm">926</a></sup> to go<sup><a href="h1980.htm">1980</a></sup> out of his sight:<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h6440.htm">6440</a></sup> stand<sup><a href="h5975.htm">5975</a></sup> not<sup><a href="h0408.htm">408</a></sup> in an evil<sup><a href="h7451.htm">7451</a></sup> thing;<sup><a href="h1697.htm">1697</a></sup> for<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> he doeth<sup><a href="h6213.htm">6213</a></sup> whatsoever<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> pleaseth<sup><a href="h2654.htm">2654</a></sup> him. </p>
 <p>4  Where<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> the word<sup><a href="h1697.htm">1697</a></sup> of a king<sup><a href="h4428.htm">4428</a></sup> is, there is power:<sup><a href="h7983.htm">7983</a></sup> and who<sup><a href="h4310.htm">4310</a></sup> may say<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> unto him, What<sup><a href="h4100.htm">4100</a></sup> doest<sup><a href="h6213.htm">6213</a></sup> thou? </p>
 <p>5  Whoso keepeth<sup><a href="h8104.htm">8104</a></sup> the commandment<sup><a href="h4687.htm">4687</a></sup> shall feel<sup><a href="h3045.htm">3045</a></sup> no<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> evil<sup><a href="h7451.htm">7451</a></sup> thing:<sup><a href="h1697.htm">1697</a></sup> and a wise man's<sup><a href="h2450.htm">2450</a></sup> heart<sup><a href="h3820.htm">3820</a></sup> discerneth<sup><a href="h3045.htm">3045</a></sup> both time<sup><a href="h6256.htm">6256</a></sup> and judgment.<sup><a href="h4941.htm">4941</a></sup> </p>
 <p>6  Because<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> to every<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> purpose<sup><a href="h2656.htm">2656</a></sup> there is<sup><a href="h3426.htm">3426</a></sup> time<sup><a href="h6256.htm">6256</a></sup> and judgment,<sup><a href="h4941.htm">4941</a></sup> therefore<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> the misery<sup><a href="h7451.htm">7451</a></sup> of man<sup><a href="h0120.htm">120</a></sup> is great<sup><a href="h7227.htm">7227</a></sup> upon<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> him. </p>
 <p>7  For<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> he knoweth<sup><a href="h3045.htm">3045</a></sup> not<sup><a href="h0369.htm">369</a></sup> that<sup><a href="h4100.htm">4100</a></sup> which shall be:<sup><a href="h7945.htm">7945</a></sup> <sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> for<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> who<sup><a href="h4310.htm">4310</a></sup> can tell<sup><a href="h5046.htm">5046</a></sup> him when<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> it shall be?<sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> </p>
 <p>8  There is no<sup><a href="h0369.htm">369</a></sup> man<sup><a href="h0120.htm">120</a></sup> that hath power<sup><a href="h7989.htm">7989</a></sup> over the spirit<sup><a href="h7307.htm">7307</a></sup> to retain<sup><a href="h3607.htm">3607</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> the spirit;<sup><a href="h7307.htm">7307</a></sup> neither<sup><a href="h0369.htm">369</a></sup> hath he power<sup><a href="h7983.htm">7983</a></sup> in the day<sup><a href="h3117.htm">3117</a></sup> of death:<sup><a href="h4194.htm">4194</a></sup> and there is no<sup><a href="h0369.htm">369</a></sup> discharge<sup><a href="h4917.htm">4917</a></sup> in that war;<sup><a href="h4421.htm">4421</a></sup> neither<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> shall wickedness<sup><a href="h7562.htm">7562</a></sup> deliver<sup><a href="h4422.htm">4422</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> those that are given<sup><a href="h1167.htm">1167</a></sup> to it. </p>
 <p>9  All<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> this<sup><a href="h2088.htm">2088</a></sup> have I seen,<sup><a href="h7200.htm">7200</a></sup> and applied<sup><a href="h5414.htm">5414</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> my heart<sup><a href="h3820.htm">3820</a></sup> unto every<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> work<sup><a href="h4639.htm">4639</a></sup> that<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> is done<sup><a href="h6213.htm">6213</a></sup> under<sup><a href="h8478.htm">8478</a></sup> the sun:<sup><a href="h8121.htm">8121</a></sup> there is a time<sup><a href="h6256.htm">6256</a></sup> wherein<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> one man<sup><a href="h0120.htm">120</a></sup> ruleth over<sup><a href="h7980.htm">7980</a></sup> another<sup><a href="h0120.htm">120</a></sup> to his own hurt.<sup><a href="h7451.htm">7451</a></sup> </p>
 <p>10  And so<sup><a href="h3651.htm">3651</a></sup> I saw<sup><a href="h7200.htm">7200</a></sup> the wicked<sup><a href="h7563.htm">7563</a></sup> buried,<sup><a href="h6912.htm">6912</a></sup> who had come<sup><a href="h0935.htm">935</a></sup> and gone<sup><a href="h1980.htm">1980</a></sup> from the place<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h4725.htm">4725</a></sup> of the holy,<sup><a href="h6918.htm">6918</a></sup> and they were forgotten<sup><a href="h7911.htm">7911</a></sup> in the city<sup><a href="h5892.htm">5892</a></sup> where<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> they had so<sup><a href="h3651.htm">3651</a></sup> done:<sup><a href="h6213.htm">6213</a></sup> this<sup><a href="h2088.htm">2088</a></sup> is also<sup><a href="h1571.htm">1571</a></sup> vanity.<sup><a href="h1892.htm">1892</a></sup> </p>
 <p>11  Because<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> sentence against<sup><a href="h6599.htm">6599</a></sup> an evil<sup><a href="h7451.htm">7451</a></sup> work<sup><a href="h4639.htm">4639</a></sup> is not<sup><a href="h0369.htm">369</a></sup> executed<sup><a href="h6213.htm">6213</a></sup> speedily,<sup><a href="h4120.htm">4120</a></sup> therefore<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3651.htm">3651</a></sup> the heart<sup><a href="h3820.htm">3820</a></sup> of the sons<sup><a href="h1121.htm">1121</a></sup> of men<sup><a href="h0120.htm">120</a></sup> is fully set<sup><a href="h4390.htm">4390</a></sup> in them to do<sup><a href="h6213.htm">6213</a></sup> evil.<sup><a href="h7451.htm">7451</a></sup> </p>
 <p>12  Though<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> a sinner<sup><a href="h2398.htm">2398</a></sup> do<sup><a href="h6213.htm">6213</a></sup> evil<sup><a href="h7451.htm">7451</a></sup> a hundred times,<sup><a href="h3967.htm">3967</a></sup> and his days be prolonged,<sup><a href="h0748.htm">748</a></sup> yet<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> surely<sup><a href="h1571.htm">1571</a></sup> I<sup><a href="h0589.htm">589</a></sup> know<sup><a href="h3045.htm">3045</a></sup> that<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> it shall be<sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> well<sup><a href="h2896.htm">2896</a></sup> with them that fear<sup><a href="h3373.htm">3373</a></sup> God,<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> which<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> fear<sup><a href="h3372.htm">3372</a></sup> before<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h6440.htm">6440</a></sup> him: </p>
 <p>13  But it shall not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> be<sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> well<sup><a href="h2896.htm">2896</a></sup> with the wicked,<sup><a href="h7563.htm">7563</a></sup> neither<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> shall he prolong<sup><a href="h0748.htm">748</a></sup> his days,<sup><a href="h3117.htm">3117</a></sup> which are as a shadow;<sup><a href="h6738.htm">6738</a></sup> because<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> he feareth<sup><a href="h3372.htm">3372</a></sup> not<sup><a href="h0369.htm">369</a></sup> before<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h6440.htm">6440</a></sup> God.<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> </p>
 <p>14  There is<sup><a href="h3426.htm">3426</a></sup> a vanity<sup><a href="h1892.htm">1892</a></sup> which<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> is done<sup><a href="h6213.htm">6213</a></sup> upon<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> the earth;<sup><a href="h0776.htm">776</a></sup> that<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> there be<sup><a href="h3426.htm">3426</a></sup> just<sup><a href="h6662.htm">6662</a></sup> men, unto whom<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> it happeneth<sup><a href="h5060.htm">5060</a></sup> according to the work<sup><a href="h4639.htm">4639</a></sup> of the wicked;<sup><a href="h7563.htm">7563</a></sup> again, there be<sup><a href="h3426.htm">3426</a></sup> wicked<sup><a href="h7563.htm">7563</a></sup> men, to<sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> whom it happeneth<sup><a href="h7945.htm">7945</a></sup> <sup><a href="h5060.htm">5060</a></sup> according to the work<sup><a href="h4639.htm">4639</a></sup> of the righteous:<sup><a href="h6662.htm">6662</a></sup> I said<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> that this also<sup><a href="h2088.htm">2088</a></sup> <sup><a href="h7945.htm">7945</a></sup> <sup><a href="h1571.htm">1571</a></sup> is vanity.<sup><a href="h1892.htm">1892</a></sup> </p>
 <p>15  Then I<sup><a href="h0589.htm">589</a></sup> commended<sup><a href="h7623.htm">7623</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> mirth,<sup><a href="h8057.htm">8057</a></sup> because<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> a man<sup><a href="h0120.htm">120</a></sup> hath no<sup><a href="h0369.htm">369</a></sup> better<sup><a href="h2896.htm">2896</a></sup> thing under<sup><a href="h8478.htm">8478</a></sup> the sun,<sup><a href="h8121.htm">8121</a></sup> than<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0518.htm">518</a></sup> to eat,<sup><a href="h0398.htm">398</a></sup> and to drink,<sup><a href="h8354.htm">8354</a></sup> and to be merry:<sup><a href="h8055.htm">8055</a></sup> for that<sup><a href="h1931.htm">1931</a></sup> shall abide<sup><a href="h3867.htm">3867</a></sup> with him of his labor<sup><a href="h5999.htm">5999</a></sup> the days<sup><a href="h3117.htm">3117</a></sup> of his life,<sup><a href="h2416.htm">2416</a></sup> which<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> God<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> giveth<sup><a href="h5414.htm">5414</a></sup> him under<sup><a href="h8478.htm">8478</a></sup> the sun.<sup><a href="h8121.htm">8121</a></sup> </p>
 <p>16  When<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> I applied<sup><a href="h5414.htm">5414</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> mine heart<sup><a href="h3820.htm">3820</a></sup> to know<sup><a href="h3045.htm">3045</a></sup> wisdom,<sup><a href="h2451.htm">2451</a></sup> and to see<sup><a href="h7200.htm">7200</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> the business<sup><a href="h6045.htm">6045</a></sup> that<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> is done<sup><a href="h6213.htm">6213</a></sup> upon<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> the earth:<sup><a href="h0776.htm">776</a></sup> (for<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> also<sup><a href="h1571.htm">1571</a></sup> there is that neither<sup><a href="h0369.htm">369</a></sup> day<sup><a href="h3117.htm">3117</a></sup> nor night<sup><a href="h3915.htm">3915</a></sup> seeth<sup><a href="h7200.htm">7200</a></sup> sleep<sup><a href="h8142.htm">8142</a></sup> with his eyes:)<sup><a href="h5869.htm">5869</a></sup> </p>
 <p>17  Then I beheld<sup><a href="h7200.htm">7200</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> the work<sup><a href="h4639.htm">4639</a></sup> of God,<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> that<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> a man<sup><a href="h0120.htm">120</a></sup> cannot<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3201.htm">3201</a></sup> find out<sup><a href="h4672.htm">4672</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> the work<sup><a href="h4639.htm">4639</a></sup> that<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> is done<sup><a href="h6213.htm">6213</a></sup> under<sup><a href="h8478.htm">8478</a></sup> the sun:<sup><a href="h8121.htm">8121</a></sup> because<sup><a href="h7945.htm">7945</a></sup> though<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> a man<sup><a href="h0120.htm">120</a></sup> labor<sup><a href="h5998.htm">5998</a></sup> to seek it out,<sup><a href="h1245.htm">1245</a></sup> yet he shall not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> find<sup><a href="h4672.htm">4672</a></sup> it; yea<sup><a href="h1571.htm">1571</a></sup> further; though<sup><a href="h0518.htm">518</a></sup> a wise<sup><a href="h2450.htm">2450</a></sup> man think<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> to know<sup><a href="h3045.htm">3045</a></sup> it, yet shall he not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> be able<sup><a href="h3201.htm">3201</a></sup> to find<sup><a href="h4672.htm">4672</a></sup> it. </p>

 <p align="center"><a href="../sep/ecc008.htm">Sep</a></p><p>1 τίς οἶδεν σοφούς καὶ τίς οἶδεν λύσιν ῥήματος σοφία ἀνθρώπου φωτιεῖ πρόσωπον αὐτοῦ καὶ ἀναιδὴς προσώπῳ αὐτοῦ μισηθήσεται </p>
 <p>2 στόμα βασιλέως φύλαξον καὶ περὶ λόγου ὅρκου θεοῦ μὴ σπουδάσῃς </p>
 <p>3 ἀπὸ προσώπου αὐτοῦ πορεύσῃ μὴ στῇς ἐν λόγῳ πονηρῷ ὅτι πᾶν ὃ ἐὰν θελήσῃ ποιήσει </p>
 <p>4 καθὼς λαλεῖ βασιλεὺς ἐξουσιάζων καὶ τίς ἐρεῖ αὐτῷ τί ποιήσεις </p>
 <p>5 ὁ φυλάσσων ἐντολὴν οὐ γνώσεται ῥῆμα πονηρόν καὶ καιρὸν κρίσεως γινώσκει καρδία σοφοῦ </p>
 <p>6 ὅτι παντὶ πράγματι ἔστιν καιρὸς καὶ κρίσις ὅτι γνῶσις τοῦ ἀνθρώπου πολλὴ ἐπ᾽ αὐτόν </p>
 <p>7 ὅτι οὐκ ἔστιν γινώσκων τί τὸ ἐσόμενον ὅτι καθὼς ἔσται τίς ἀναγγελεῖ αὐτῷ </p>
 <p>8 οὐκ ἔστιν ἄνθρωπος ἐξουσιάζων ἐν πνεύματι τοῦ κωλῦσαι σὺν τὸ πνεῦμα καὶ οὐκ ἔστιν ἐξουσία ἐν ἡμέρᾳ τοῦ θανάτου καὶ οὐκ ἔστιν ἀποστολὴ ἐν τῷ πολέμῳ καὶ οὐ διασώσει ἀσέβεια τὸν παρ᾽ αὐτῆς </p>
 <p>9 καὶ σὺν πᾶν τοῦτο εἶδον καὶ ἔδωκα τὴν καρδίαν μου εἰς πᾶν ποίημα ὃ πεποίηται ὑπὸ τὸν ἥλιον τὰ ὅσα ἐξουσιάσατο ὁ ἄνθρωπος ἐν ἀνθρώπῳ τοῦ κακῶσαι αὐτόν </p>
 <p>10 καὶ τότε εἶδον ἀσεβεῖς εἰς τάφους εἰσαχθέντας καὶ ἐκ τόπου ἁγίου ἐπορεύθησαν καὶ ἐπῃνέθησαν ἐν τῇ πόλει ὅτι οὕτως ἐποίησαν καί γε τοῦτο ματαιότης </p>
 <p>11 ὅτι οὐκ ἔστιν γινομένη ἀντίρρησις ἀπὸ τῶν ποιούντων τὸ πονηρὸν ταχύ διὰ τοῦτο ἐπληροφορήθη καρδία υἱῶν τοῦ ἀνθρώπου ἐν αὐτοῖς τοῦ ποιῆσαι τὸ πονηρόν </p>
 <p>12 ὃς ἥμαρτεν ἐποίησεν τὸ πονηρὸν ἀπὸ τότε καὶ ἀπὸ μακρότητος αὐτῷ ὅτι καί γε γινώσκω ἐγὼ ὅτι ἔσται ἀγαθὸν τοῖς φοβουμένοις τὸν θεόν ὅπως φοβῶνται ἀπὸ προσώπου αὐτοῦ </p>
 <p>13 καὶ ἀγαθὸν οὐκ ἔσται τῷ ἀσεβεῖ καὶ οὐ μακρυνεῖ ἡμέρας ἐν σκιᾷ ὃς οὐκ ἔστιν φοβούμενος ἀπὸ προσώπου τοῦ θεοῦ </p>
 <p>14 ἔστιν ματαιότης ἣ πεποίηται ἐπὶ τῆς γῆς ὅτι εἰσὶ δίκαιοι ὅτι φθάνει πρὸς αὐτοὺς ὡς ποίημα τῶν ἀσεβῶν καὶ εἰσὶν ἀσεβεῖς ὅτι φθάνει πρὸς αὐτοὺς ὡς ποίημα τῶν δικαίων εἶπα ὅτι καί γε τοῦτο ματαιότης </p>
 <p>15 καὶ ἐπῄνεσα ἐγὼ σὺν τὴν εὐφροσύνην ὅτι οὐκ ἔστιν ἀγαθὸν τῷ ἀνθρώπῳ ὑπὸ τὸν ἥλιον ὅτι εἰ μὴ τοῦ φαγεῖν καὶ τοῦ πιεῖν καὶ τοῦ εὐφρανθῆναι καὶ αὐτὸ συμπροσέσται αὐτῷ ἐν μόχθῳ αὐτοῦ ἡμέρας ζωῆς αὐτοῦ ὅσας ἔδωκεν αὐτῷ ὁ θεὸς ὑπὸ τὸν ἥλιον </p>
 <p>16 ἐν οἷς ἔδωκα τὴν καρδίαν μου τοῦ γνῶναι σοφίαν καὶ τοῦ ἰδεῖν τὸν περισπασμὸν τὸν πεποιημένον ἐπὶ τῆς γῆς ὅτι καί γε ἐν ἡμέρᾳ καὶ ἐν νυκτὶ ὕπνον ἐν ὀφθαλμοῖς αὐτοῦ οὐκ ἔστιν βλέπων </p>
 <p>17 καὶ εἶδον σὺν πάντα τὰ ποιήματα τοῦ θεοῦ ὅτι οὐ δυνήσεται ἄνθρωπος τοῦ εὑρεῖν σὺν τὸ ποίημα τὸ πεποιημένον ὑπὸ τὸν ἥλιον ὅσα ἂν μοχθήσῃ ὁ ἄνθρωπος τοῦ ζητῆσαι καὶ οὐχ εὑρήσει καί γε ὅσα ἂν εἴπῃ ὁ σοφὸς τοῦ γνῶναι οὐ δυνήσεται τοῦ εὑρεῖν </p>

 <p align="center"><a href="../vul/ecc008.htm">Vul</a></p><p>1 [Sapientia hominis lucet in vultu ejus,<br> et potentissimus faciem illius commutabit.<br></p>
 <p>2 Ego os regis observo,<br> et præcepta juramenti Dei.<br></p>
 <p>3 Ne festines recedere a facie ejus,<br> neque permaneas in opere malo:<br> quia omne quod voluerit faciet.<br></p>
 <p>4 Et sermo illius potestate plenus est,<br> nec dicere ei quisquam potest: Quare ita facis?<br></p>
 <p>5 Qui custodit præceptum non experietur quidquam mali.<br> Tempus et responsionem cor sapientis intelligit.<br></p>
 <p>6 Omni negotio tempus est, et opportunitas:<br> et multa hominis afflictio,<br></p>
 <p>7 quia ignorat præterita,<br> et futura nullo scire potest nuntio.<br></p>
 <p>8 Non est in hominis potestate prohibere spiritum,<br> nec habet potestatem in die mortis:<br> nec sinitur quiescere ingruente bello,<br> neque salvabit impietas impium.]<br>
 </p>
 <p>9 [Omnia hæc consideravi,<br> et dedi cor meum in cunctis operibus quæ fiunt sub sole.<br> Interdum dominatur homo homini in malum suum.<br></p>
 <p>10 Vidi impios sepultos,<br> qui etiam cum adhuc viverent<br> in loco sancto erant,<br> et laudabantur in civitate<br> quasi justorum operum.<br> Sed et hoc vanitas est.<br></p>
 <p>11 Etenim quia non profertur cito contra malos sententia,<br> absque timore ullo<br> filii hominum perpetrant mala.<br></p>
 <p>12 Attamen peccator ex eo quod centies facit malum,<br> et per patientiam sustentatur;<br> ego cognovi quod erit bonum timentibus Deum,<br> qui verentur faciem ejus.<br></p>
 <p>13 Non sit bonum impio,<br> nec prolongentur dies ejus,<br> sed quasi umbra transeant qui non timent faciem Domini.<br></p>
 <p>14 Est et alia vanitas quæ fit super terram:<br> sunt justi quibus mala proveniunt<br> quasi opera egerint impiorum:<br> et sunt impii qui ita securi sunt<br> quasi justorum facta habeant.<br> Sed et hoc vanissimum judico.<br></p>
 <p>15 Laudavi igitur lætitiam;<br> quod non esset homini bonum sub sole,<br> nisi quod comederet, et biberet, atque gauderet,<br> et hoc solum secum auferret de labore suo,<br> in diebus vitæ suæ quos dedit ei Deus sub sole.<br></p>
 <p>16 Et apposui cor meum ut scirem sapientiam,<br> et intelligerem distentionem quæ versatur in terra.<br> Est homo qui diebus et noctibus somnum non capit oculis.<br></p>
 <p>17 Et intellexi quod omnium operum Dei<br> nullam possit homo invenire rationem<br> eorum quæ fiunt sub sole;<br> et quanto plus laboraverit ad quærendum,<br> tanto minus inveniat:<br> etiam si dixerit sapiens se nosse, non poterit reperire.]</p>
