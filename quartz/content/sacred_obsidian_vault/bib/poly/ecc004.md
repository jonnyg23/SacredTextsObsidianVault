 <p align="center"><a href="../kjv/ecc004.htm">KJV</a></p><p>1  So I<sup><a href="h0589.htm">589</a></sup> returned,<sup><a href="h7725.htm">7725</a></sup> and considered<sup><a href="h7200.htm">7200</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> the oppressions<sup><a href="h6217.htm">6217</a></sup> that<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> are done<sup><a href="h6213.htm">6213</a></sup> under<sup><a href="h8478.htm">8478</a></sup> the sun:<sup><a href="h8121.htm">8121</a></sup> and behold<sup><a href="h2009.htm">2009</a></sup> the tears<sup><a href="h1832.htm">1832</a></sup> of such as were oppressed,<sup><a href="h6217.htm">6217</a></sup> and they had no<sup><a href="h0369.htm">369</a></sup> comforter;<sup><a href="h5162.htm">5162</a></sup> and on the side<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3027.htm">3027</a></sup> of their oppressors<sup><a href="h6231.htm">6231</a></sup> there was power;<sup><a href="h3581.htm">3581</a></sup> but they had no<sup><a href="h0369.htm">369</a></sup> comforter.<sup><a href="h5162.htm">5162</a></sup> </p>
 <p>2  Wherefore I<sup><a href="h0589.htm">589</a></sup> praised<sup><a href="h7623.htm">7623</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> the dead<sup><a href="h4191.htm">4191</a></sup> which are already<sup><a href="h7945.htm">7945</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3528.htm">3528</a></sup> dead<sup><a href="h4191.htm">4191</a></sup> more than<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> the living<sup><a href="h2416.htm">2416</a></sup> which<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> <sup><a href="h1992.htm">1992</a></sup> are yet<sup><a href="h5728.htm">5728</a></sup> alive.<sup><a href="h2416.htm">2416</a></sup> </p>
 <p>3  Yea, better<sup><a href="h2896.htm">2896</a></sup> is he than both<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h8147.htm">8147</a></sup> they, <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> which<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> hath not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> yet<sup><a href="h5728.htm">5728</a></sup> been,<sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> who<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> hath not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> seen<sup><a href="h7200.htm">7200</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> the evil<sup><a href="h7451.htm">7451</a></sup> work<sup><a href="h4639.htm">4639</a></sup> that<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> is done<sup><a href="h6213.htm">6213</a></sup> under<sup><a href="h8478.htm">8478</a></sup> the sun.<sup><a href="h8121.htm">8121</a></sup> </p>
 <p>4  Again, I<sup><a href="h0589.htm">589</a></sup> considered<sup><a href="h7200.htm">7200</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> travail,<sup><a href="h5999.htm">5999</a></sup> and every<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> right<sup><a href="h3788.htm">3788</a></sup> work,<sup><a href="h4639.htm">4639</a></sup> that<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> for this<sup><a href="h1931.htm">1931</a></sup> a man<sup><a href="h0376.htm">376</a></sup> is envied<sup><a href="h7068.htm">7068</a></sup> of his neighbor.<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h7453.htm">7453</a></sup> This<sup><a href="h2088.htm">2088</a></sup> is also<sup><a href="h1571.htm">1571</a></sup> vanity<sup><a href="h1892.htm">1892</a></sup> and vexation<sup><a href="h7469.htm">7469</a></sup> of spirit.<sup><a href="h7307.htm">7307</a></sup> </p>
 <p>5  The fool<sup><a href="h3684.htm">3684</a></sup> foldeth his hands together,<sup><a href="h2263.htm">2263</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3027.htm">3027</a></sup> and eateth<sup><a href="h0398.htm">398</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> his own flesh.<sup><a href="h1320.htm">1320</a></sup> </p>

 <p>7  Then I<sup><a href="h0589.htm">589</a></sup> returned,<sup><a href="h7725.htm">7725</a></sup> and I saw<sup><a href="h7200.htm">7200</a></sup> vanity<sup><a href="h1892.htm">1892</a></sup> under<sup><a href="h8478.htm">8478</a></sup> the sun.<sup><a href="h8121.htm">8121</a></sup> </p>
 <p>8  There is<sup><a href="h3426.htm">3426</a></sup> one<sup><a href="h0259.htm">259</a></sup> alone, and there is not<sup><a href="h0369.htm">369</a></sup> a second;<sup><a href="h8145.htm">8145</a></sup> yea,<sup><a href="h1571.htm">1571</a></sup> he hath neither<sup><a href="h0369.htm">369</a></sup> child<sup><a href="h1121.htm">1121</a></sup> nor brother:<sup><a href="h0251.htm">251</a></sup> yet is there no<sup><a href="h0369.htm">369</a></sup> end<sup><a href="h7093.htm">7093</a></sup> of all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> his labor;<sup><a href="h5999.htm">5999</a></sup> neither<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> is his eye<sup><a href="h5869.htm">5869</a></sup> satisfied<sup><a href="h7646.htm">7646</a></sup> with riches;<sup><a href="h6239.htm">6239</a></sup> neither saith he, For whom<sup><a href="h4310.htm">4310</a></sup> do I<sup><a href="h0589.htm">589</a></sup> labor,<sup><a href="h6001.htm">6001</a></sup> and bereave<sup><a href="h2637.htm">2637</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> my soul<sup><a href="h5315.htm">5315</a></sup> of good?<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h2896.htm">2896</a></sup> This<sup><a href="h2088.htm">2088</a></sup> is also<sup><a href="h1571.htm">1571</a></sup> vanity,<sup><a href="h1892.htm">1892</a></sup> yea, it<sup><a href="h1931.htm">1931</a></sup> is a sore<sup><a href="h7451.htm">7451</a></sup> travail.<sup><a href="h6045.htm">6045</a></sup> </p>
 <p>9  Two<sup><a href="h8147.htm">8147</a></sup> are better<sup><a href="h2896.htm">2896</a></sup> than<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> one;<sup><a href="h0259.htm">259</a></sup> because<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> they have<sup><a href="h3426.htm">3426</a></sup> a good<sup><a href="h2896.htm">2896</a></sup> reward<sup><a href="h7939.htm">7939</a></sup> for their labor.<sup><a href="h5999.htm">5999</a></sup> </p>
 <p>10  For<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> if<sup><a href="h0518.htm">518</a></sup> they fall,<sup><a href="h5307.htm">5307</a></sup> the one<sup><a href="h0259.htm">259</a></sup> will lift up<sup><a href="h6965.htm">6965</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> his fellow:<sup><a href="h2270.htm">2270</a></sup> but woe<sup><a href="h0337.htm">337</a></sup> to him that is alone<sup><a href="h0259.htm">259</a></sup> when he falleth;<sup><a href="h7945.htm">7945</a></sup> <sup><a href="h5307.htm">5307</a></sup> for he hath not<sup><a href="h0369.htm">369</a></sup> another<sup><a href="h8145.htm">8145</a></sup> to help him up.<sup><a href="h6965.htm">6965</a></sup> </p>
 <p>11  Again,<sup><a href="h1571.htm">1571</a></sup> if<sup><a href="h0518.htm">518</a></sup> two<sup><a href="h8147.htm">8147</a></sup> lie together,<sup><a href="h7901.htm">7901</a></sup> then they have heat:<sup><a href="h2552.htm">2552</a></sup> but how<sup><a href="h0349.htm">349</a></sup> can one<sup><a href="h0259.htm">259</a></sup> be warm<sup><a href="h3179.htm">3179</a></sup> alone? </p>
 <p>12  And if<sup><a href="h0518.htm">518</a></sup> one<sup><a href="h0259.htm">259</a></sup> prevail against<sup><a href="h8630.htm">8630</a></sup> him, two<sup><a href="h8147.htm">8147</a></sup> shall withstand<sup><a href="h5975.htm">5975</a></sup> <sup><a href="h5048.htm">5048</a></sup> him; and a threefold<sup><a href="h8027.htm">8027</a></sup> cord<sup><a href="h2339.htm">2339</a></sup> is not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> quickly<sup><a href="h4120.htm">4120</a></sup> broken.<sup><a href="h5423.htm">5423</a></sup> </p>

 <p>14  For<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> out of prison<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h1004.htm">1004</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0631.htm">631</a></sup> he cometh<sup><a href="h3318.htm">3318</a></sup> to reign;<sup><a href="h4427.htm">4427</a></sup> whereas<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> also<sup><a href="h1571.htm">1571</a></sup> he that is born<sup><a href="h3205.htm">3205</a></sup> in his kingdom<sup><a href="h4438.htm">4438</a></sup> becometh poor.<sup><a href="h7326.htm">7326</a></sup> </p>
 <p>15  I considered<sup><a href="h7200.htm">7200</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> the living<sup><a href="h2416.htm">2416</a></sup> which walk<sup><a href="h1980.htm">1980</a></sup> under<sup><a href="h8478.htm">8478</a></sup> the sun,<sup><a href="h8121.htm">8121</a></sup> with<sup><a href="h5973.htm">5973</a></sup> the second<sup><a href="h8145.htm">8145</a></sup> child<sup><a href="h3206.htm">3206</a></sup> that<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> shall stand up<sup><a href="h5975.htm">5975</a></sup> in his stead.<sup><a href="h8478.htm">8478</a></sup> </p>

 <p align="center"><a href="../sep/ecc004.htm">Sep</a></p><p>1 καὶ ἐπέστρεψα ἐγὼ καὶ εἶδον σὺν πάσας τὰς συκοφαντίας τὰς γινομένας ὑπὸ τὸν ἥλιον καὶ ἰδοὺ δάκρυον τῶν συκοφαντουμένων καὶ οὐκ ἔστιν αὐτοῖς παρακαλῶν καὶ ἀπὸ χειρὸς συκοφαντούντων αὐτοὺς ἰσχύς καὶ οὐκ ἔστιν αὐτοῖς παρακαλῶν </p>
 <p>2 καὶ ἐπῄνεσα ἐγὼ σὺν τοὺς τεθνηκότας τοὺς ἤδη ἀποθανόντας ὑπὲρ τοὺς ζῶντας ὅσοι αὐτοὶ ζῶσιν ἕως τοῦ νῦν </p>
 <p>3 καὶ ἀγαθὸς ὑπὲρ τοὺς δύο τούτους ὅστις οὔπω ἐγένετο ὃς οὐκ εἶδεν σὺν τὸ ποίημα τὸ πονηρὸν τὸ πεποιημένον ὑπὸ τὸν ἥλιον </p>
 <p>4 καὶ εἶδον ἐγὼ σὺν πάντα τὸν μόχθον καὶ σὺν πᾶσαν ἀνδρείαν τοῦ ποιήματος ὅτι αὐτὸ ζῆλος ἀνδρὸς ἀπὸ τοῦ ἑταίρου αὐτοῦ καί γε τοῦτο ματαιότης καὶ προαίρεσις πνεύματος </p>
 <p>5 ὁ ἄφρων περιέλαβεν τὰς χεῖρας αὐτοῦ καὶ ἔφαγεν τὰς σάρκας αὐτοῦ </p>
 <p>6 ἀγαθὸν πλήρωμα δρακὸς ἀναπαύσεως ὑπὲρ πλήρωμα δύο δρακῶν μόχθου καὶ προαιρέσεως πνεύματος </p>
 <p>7 καὶ ἐπέστρεψα ἐγὼ καὶ εἶδον ματαιότητα ὑπὸ τὸν ἥλιον </p>
 <p>8 ἔστιν εἷς καὶ οὐκ ἔστιν δεύτερος καί γε υἱὸς καὶ ἀδελφὸς οὐκ ἔστιν αὐτῷ καὶ οὐκ ἔστιν περασμὸς τῷ παντὶ μόχθῳ αὐτοῦ καί γε ὀφθαλμὸς αὐτοῦ οὐκ ἐμπίπλαται πλούτου καὶ τίνι ἐγὼ μοχθῶ καὶ στερίσκω τὴν ψυχήν μου ἀπὸ ἀγαθωσύνης καί γε τοῦτο ματαιότης καὶ περισπασμὸς πονηρός ἐστιν </p>
 <p>9 ἀγαθοὶ οἱ δύο ὑπὲρ τὸν ἕνα οἷς ἔστιν αὐτοῖς μισθὸς ἀγαθὸς ἐν μόχθῳ αὐτῶν </p>
 <p>10 ὅτι ἐὰν πέσωσιν ὁ εἷς ἐγερεῖ τὸν μέτοχον αὐτοῦ καὶ οὐαὶ αὐτῷ τῷ ἑνί ὅταν πέσῃ καὶ μὴ ᾖ δεύτερος τοῦ ἐγεῖραι αὐτόν </p>
 <p>11 καί γε ἐὰν κοιμηθῶσιν δύο καὶ θέρμη αὐτοῖς καὶ ὁ εἷς πῶς θερμανθῇ </p>
 <p>12 καὶ ἐὰν ἐπικραταιωθῇ ὁ εἷς οἱ δύο στήσονται κατέναντι αὐτοῦ καὶ τὸ σπαρτίον τὸ ἔντριτον οὐ ταχέως ἀπορραγήσεται </p>
 <p>13 ἀγαθὸς παῖς πένης καὶ σοφὸς ὑπὲρ βασιλέα πρεσβύτερον καὶ ἄφρονα ὃς οὐκ ἔγνω τοῦ προσέχειν ἔτι </p>
 <p>14 ὅτι ἐξ οἴκου τῶν δεσμίων ἐξελεύσεται τοῦ βασιλεῦσαι ὅτι καί γε ἐν βασιλείᾳ αὐτοῦ ἐγεννήθη πένης </p>
 <p>15 εἶδον σὺν πάντας τοὺς ζῶντας τοὺς περιπατοῦντας ὑπὸ τὸν ἥλιον μετὰ τοῦ νεανίσκου τοῦ δευτέρου ὃς στήσεται ἀντ᾽ αὐτοῦ </p>
 <p>16 οὐκ ἔστιν περασμὸς τῷ παντὶ λαῷ τοῖς πᾶσιν ὅσοι ἐγένοντο ἔμπροσθεν αὐτῶν καί γε οἱ ἔσχατοι οὐκ εὐφρανθήσονται ἐν αὐτῷ ὅτι καί γε τοῦτο ματαιότης καὶ προαίρεσις πνεύματος </p>
 <p>17 φύλαξον πόδα σου ἐν ᾧ ἐὰν πορεύῃ εἰς οἶκον τοῦ θεοῦ καὶ ἐγγὺς τοῦ ἀκούειν ὑπὲρ δόμα τῶν ἀφρόνων θυσία σου ὅτι οὔκ εἰσιν εἰδότες τοῦ ποιῆσαι κακόν </p>

 <p align="center"><a href="../vul/ecc004.htm">Vul</a></p><p>1 [Verti me ad alia, et vidi calumnias<br> quæ sub sole geruntur,<br> et lacrimas innocentium,<br> et neminem consolatorem,<br> nec posse resistere eorum violentiæ,<br> cunctorum auxilio destitutos,<br></p>
 <p>2 et laudavi magis mortuos quam viventes;<br></p>
 <p>3 et feliciorem utroque judicavi<br> qui necdum natus est,<br> nec vidit mala quæ sub sole fiunt.<br></p>
 <p>4 Rursum contemplatus sum omnes labores hominum,<br> et industrias animadverti patere invidiæ proximi;<br> et in hoc ergo vanitas et cura superflua est.<br></p>
 <p>5 Stultus complicat manus suas,<br> et comedit carnes suas, dicens:<br></p>
 <p>6 Melior est pugillus cum requie,<br> quam plena utraque manus cum labore et afflictione animi.<br></p>
 <p>7 Considerans, reperi et aliam vanitatem sub sole.<br></p>
 <p>8 Unus est, et secundum non habet,<br> non filium, non fratrem,<br> et tamen laborare non cessat,<br> nec satiantur oculi ejus divitiis;<br> nec recogitat, dicens:<br> Cui laboro, et fraudo animam meam bonis?<br> In hoc quoque vanitas est et afflictio pessima.]<br>
 </p>
 <p>9 [Melius est ergo duos esse simul quam unum;<br> habent enim emolumentum societatis suæ.<br></p>
 <p>10 Si unus ceciderit, ab altero fulcietur.<br> Væ soli, quia cum ceciderit, non habet sublevantem se.<br></p>
 <p>11 Et si dormierint duo, fovebuntur mutuo;<br> unus quomodo calefiet?<br></p>
 <p>12 Et si quispiam prævaluerit contra unum,<br> duo resistunt ei;<br> funiculus triplex difficile rumpitur.<br></p>
 <p>13 Melior est puer pauper et sapiens,<br> rege sene et stulto,<br> qui nescit prævidere in posterum.<br></p>
 <p>14 Quod de carcere catenisque interdum quis egrediatur ad regnum;<br> et alius, natus in regno, inopia consumatur.<br></p>
 <p>15 Vidi cunctos viventes qui ambulant sub sole<br> cum adolescente secundo, qui consurget pro eo.<br></p>
 <p>16 Infinitus numerus est populi<br> omnium qui fuerunt ante eum,<br> et qui postea futuri sunt non lætabuntur in eo;<br> sed et hoc vanitas et afflictio spiritus.]<br>
 </p>
 <p>17 [Custodi pedem tuum ingrediens domum Dei,<br> et appropinqua ut audias.<br> Multo enim melior est obedientia quam stultorum victimæ,<br> qui nesciunt quid faciunt mali.]</p>
