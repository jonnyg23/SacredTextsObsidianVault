  <p>H7673<br>  שׁבת  <br> שָׁבַת  ‎  shâbath  <br><i>shaw-bath‘ </i><br>A primitive root; to <i>repose</i>, that is, <i>desist</i> from exertion; used in many implied relations (causatively, figuratively or specifically): - (cause to, let, make to) cease, celebrate, cause (make) to fail, keep (sabbath), suffer to be lacking, leave, put away (down), (make to) rest, rid, still, take away.<br></p>
