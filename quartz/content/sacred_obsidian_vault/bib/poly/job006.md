 <p align="center"><a href="../kjv/job006.htm">KJV</a></p><p>1  But Job<sup><a href="h0347.htm">347</a></sup> answered<sup><a href="h6030.htm">6030</a></sup> and said,<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> </p>

 <p>3  For<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> now<sup><a href="h6258.htm">6258</a></sup> it would be heavier<sup><a href="h3513.htm">3513</a></sup> than the sand<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h2344.htm">2344</a></sup> of the sea:<sup><a href="h3220.htm">3220</a></sup> therefore<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3651.htm">3651</a></sup> my words<sup><a href="h1697.htm">1697</a></sup> are swallowed up.<sup><a href="h3886.htm">3886</a></sup> </p>

 <p>5  Doth the wild ass<sup><a href="h6501.htm">6501</a></sup> bray<sup><a href="h5101.htm">5101</a></sup> when he hath grass?<sup><a href="h1877.htm">1877</a></sup> or<sup><a href="h0518.htm">518</a></sup> loweth<sup><a href="h1600.htm">1600</a></sup> the ox<sup><a href="h7794.htm">7794</a></sup> over<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> his fodder?<sup><a href="h1098.htm">1098</a></sup> </p>
 <p>6  Can that which is unsavory<sup><a href="h8602.htm">8602</a></sup> be eaten<sup><a href="h0398.htm">398</a></sup> without<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h1097.htm">1097</a></sup> salt?<sup><a href="h4417.htm">4417</a></sup> or<sup><a href="h0518.htm">518</a></sup> is there<sup><a href="h3426.htm">3426</a></sup> any taste<sup><a href="h2940.htm">2940</a></sup> in the white<sup><a href="h7388.htm">7388</a></sup> of an egg?<sup><a href="h2495.htm">2495</a></sup> </p>
 <p>7  The things<sup><a href="h1992.htm">1992</a></sup> that my soul<sup><a href="h5315.htm">5315</a></sup> refused<sup><a href="h3985.htm">3985</a></sup> to touch<sup><a href="h5060.htm">5060</a></sup> are as my sorrowful<sup><a href="h1741.htm">1741</a></sup> meat.<sup><a href="h3899.htm">3899</a></sup> </p>
 <p>8  Oh that<sup><a href="h4310.htm">4310</a></sup> I might<sup><a href="h5414.htm">5414</a></sup> have<sup><a href="h0935.htm">935</a></sup> my request;<sup><a href="h7596.htm">7596</a></sup> and that God<sup><a href="h0433.htm">433</a></sup> would grant<sup><a href="h5414.htm">5414</a></sup> me the thing that I long for!<sup><a href="h8615.htm">8615</a></sup> </p>
 <p>9  Even that it would please<sup><a href="h2974.htm">2974</a></sup> God<sup><a href="h0433.htm">433</a></sup> to destroy<sup><a href="h1792.htm">1792</a></sup> me; that he would let loose<sup><a href="h5425.htm">5425</a></sup> his hand,<sup><a href="h3027.htm">3027</a></sup> and cut me off!<sup><a href="h1214.htm">1214</a></sup> </p>
 <p>10  Then should I yet<sup><a href="h5750.htm">5750</a></sup> have<sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> comfort;<sup><a href="h5165.htm">5165</a></sup> yea, I would harden<sup><a href="h5539.htm">5539</a></sup> myself in sorrow:<sup><a href="h2427.htm">2427</a></sup> let him not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> spare;<sup><a href="h2550.htm">2550</a></sup> for<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> I have not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> concealed<sup><a href="h3582.htm">3582</a></sup> the words<sup><a href="h0561.htm">561</a></sup> of the Holy One.<sup><a href="h6918.htm">6918</a></sup> </p>
 <p>11  What<sup><a href="h4100.htm">4100</a></sup> is my strength,<sup><a href="h3581.htm">3581</a></sup> that<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> I should hope?<sup><a href="h3176.htm">3176</a></sup> and what<sup><a href="h4100.htm">4100</a></sup> is mine end,<sup><a href="h7093.htm">7093</a></sup> that<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> I should prolong<sup><a href="h0748.htm">748</a></sup> my life?<sup><a href="h5315.htm">5315</a></sup> </p>
 <p>12  Is my strength<sup><a href="h3581.htm">3581</a></sup> the strength<sup><a href="h3581.htm">3581</a></sup> of stones?<sup><a href="h0068.htm">68</a></sup> or is my flesh<sup><a href="h1320.htm">1320</a></sup> of brass?<sup><a href="h5153.htm">5153</a></sup> </p>
 <p>13  Is not<sup><a href="h0369.htm">369</a></sup> my help<sup><a href="h5833.htm">5833</a></sup> in me? and is wisdom<sup><a href="h8454.htm">8454</a></sup> driven<sup><a href="h5080.htm">5080</a></sup> quite from<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> me? </p>
 <p>14  To him that is afflicted<sup><a href="h4523.htm">4523</a></sup> pity<sup><a href="h2617.htm">2617</a></sup> should be showed from his friend;<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h7453.htm">7453</a></sup> but he forsaketh<sup><a href="h5800.htm">5800</a></sup> the fear<sup><a href="h3374.htm">3374</a></sup> of the Almighty.<sup><a href="h7706.htm">7706</a></sup> </p>
 <p>15  My brethren<sup><a href="h0251.htm">251</a></sup> have dealt deceitfully<sup><a href="h0898.htm">898</a></sup> as<sup><a href="h3644.htm">3644</a></sup> a brook,<sup><a href="h5158.htm">5158</a></sup> and as the stream<sup><a href="h0650.htm">650</a></sup> of brooks<sup><a href="h5158.htm">5158</a></sup> they pass away;<sup><a href="h5674.htm">5674</a></sup> </p>
 <p>16  Which are blackish<sup><a href="h6937.htm">6937</a></sup> by reason of<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> the ice,<sup><a href="h7140.htm">7140</a></sup> and wherein<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> the snow<sup><a href="h7950.htm">7950</a></sup> is hid:<sup><a href="h5956.htm">5956</a></sup> </p>
 <p>17  What time<sup><a href="h6256.htm">6256</a></sup> they wax warm,<sup><a href="h2215.htm">2215</a></sup> they vanish:<sup><a href="h6789.htm">6789</a></sup> when it is hot,<sup><a href="h2522.htm">2522</a></sup> they are consumed<sup><a href="h1846.htm">1846</a></sup> out of their place.<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h4725.htm">4725</a></sup> </p>
 <p>18  The paths<sup><a href="h0734.htm">734</a></sup> of their way<sup><a href="h1870.htm">1870</a></sup> are turned aside;<sup><a href="h3943.htm">3943</a></sup> they go<sup><a href="h5927.htm">5927</a></sup> to nothing,<sup><a href="h8414.htm">8414</a></sup> and perish.<sup><a href="h0006.htm">6</a></sup> </p>
 <p>19  The troops<sup><a href="h0734.htm">734</a></sup> of Tema<sup><a href="h8485.htm">8485</a></sup> looked,<sup><a href="h5027.htm">5027</a></sup> the companies<sup><a href="h1979.htm">1979</a></sup> of Sheba<sup><a href="h7614.htm">7614</a></sup> waited<sup><a href="h6960.htm">6960</a></sup> for<sup><a href="h3926.htm">3926</a></sup> them. </p>
 <p>20  They were confounded<sup><a href="h0954.htm">954</a></sup> because<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> they had hoped;<sup><a href="h0982.htm">982</a></sup> they came<sup><a href="h0935.htm">935</a></sup> thither,<sup><a href="h5704.htm">5704</a></sup> and were ashamed.<sup><a href="h2659.htm">2659</a></sup> </p>
 <p>21  For<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> now<sup><a href="h6258.htm">6258</a></sup> ye are<sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> nothing;<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> ye see<sup><a href="h7200.htm">7200</a></sup> my casting down,<sup><a href="h2866.htm">2866</a></sup> and are afraid.<sup><a href="h3372.htm">3372</a></sup> </p>
 <p>22  Did<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> I say,<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> Bring<sup><a href="h3051.htm">3051</a></sup> unto me? or, Give a reward<sup><a href="h7809.htm">7809</a></sup> for<sup><a href="h1157.htm">1157</a></sup> me of your substance?<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3581.htm">3581</a></sup> </p>
 <p>23  Or, Deliver<sup><a href="h4422.htm">4422</a></sup> me from the enemy's hand?<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3027.htm">3027</a></sup> <sup><a href="h6862.htm">6862</a></sup> or, Redeem<sup><a href="h6299.htm">6299</a></sup> me from the hand<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3027.htm">3027</a></sup> of the mighty?<sup><a href="h6184.htm">6184</a></sup> </p>
 <p>24  Teach<sup><a href="h3384.htm">3384</a></sup> me, and I<sup><a href="h0589.htm">589</a></sup> will hold my tongue:<sup><a href="h2790.htm">2790</a></sup> and cause me to understand<sup><a href="h0995.htm">995</a></sup> wherein<sup><a href="h4100.htm">4100</a></sup> I have erred.<sup><a href="h7686.htm">7686</a></sup> </p>
 <p>25  How<sup><a href="h4100.htm">4100</a></sup> forcible<sup><a href="h4834.htm">4834</a></sup> are right<sup><a href="h3476.htm">3476</a></sup> words!<sup><a href="h0561.htm">561</a></sup> but what<sup><a href="h4100.htm">4100</a></sup> doth your arguing<sup><a href="h3198.htm">3198</a></sup> reprove?<sup><a href="h3198.htm">3198</a></sup> <sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> </p>
 <p>26  Do ye imagine<sup><a href="h2803.htm">2803</a></sup> to reprove<sup><a href="h3198.htm">3198</a></sup> words,<sup><a href="h4405.htm">4405</a></sup> and the speeches<sup><a href="h0561.htm">561</a></sup> of one that is desperate,<sup><a href="h2976.htm">2976</a></sup> which are as wind?<sup><a href="h7307.htm">7307</a></sup> </p>
 <p>27  Yea,<sup><a href="h0637.htm">637</a></sup> ye overwhelm<sup><a href="h5307.htm">5307</a></sup> <sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> the fatherless,<sup><a href="h3490.htm">3490</a></sup> and ye dig<sup><a href="h3738.htm">3738</a></sup> a pit for<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> your friend.<sup><a href="h7453.htm">7453</a></sup> </p>
 <p>28  Now<sup><a href="h6258.htm">6258</a></sup> therefore be content,<sup><a href="h2974.htm">2974</a></sup> look<sup><a href="h6437.htm">6437</a></sup> upon me; for it is evident unto<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> <sup><a href="h6440.htm">6440</a></sup> you if<sup><a href="h0518.htm">518</a></sup> I lie.<sup><a href="h3576.htm">3576</a></sup> </p>

 <p>2 εἰ γάρ τις ἱστῶν στήσαι μου τὴν ὀργήν τὰς δὲ ὀδύνας μου ἄραι ἐν ζυγῷ ὁμοθυμαδόν </p>
 <p>3 καὶ δὴ ἄμμου παραλίας βαρυτέρα ἔσται ἀλλ᾽ ὡς ἔοικεν τὰ ῥήματά μού ἐστιν φαῦλα </p>
 <p>4 βέλη γὰρ κυρίου ἐν τῷ σώματί μού ἐστιν ὧν ὁ θυμὸς αὐτῶν ἐκπίνει μου τὸ αἷμα ὅταν ἄρξωμαι λαλεῖν κεντοῦσί με </p>
 <p>5 τί γάρ μὴ διὰ κενῆς κεκράξεται ὄνος ἄγριος ἀλλ᾽ ἢ τὰ σῖτα ζητῶν εἰ δὲ καὶ ῥήξει φωνὴν βοῦς ἐπὶ φάτνης ἔχων τὰ βρώματα </p>
 <p>6 εἰ βρωθήσεται ἄρτος ἄνευ ἁλός εἰ δὲ καὶ ἔστιν γεῦμα ἐν ῥήμασιν κενοῖς </p>
 <p>7 οὐ δύναται γὰρ παύσασθαί μου ἡ ψυχή βρόμον γὰρ ὁρῶ τὰ σῖτά μου ὥσπερ ὀσμὴν λέοντος </p>
 <p>8 εἰ γὰρ δῴη καὶ ἔλθοι μου ἡ αἴτησις καὶ τὴν ἐλπίδα μου δῴη ὁ κύριος </p>
 <p>9 ἀρξάμενος ὁ κύριος τρωσάτω με εἰς τέλος δὲ μή με ἀνελέτω </p>
 <p>10 εἴη δέ μου πόλις τάφος ἐφ᾽ ἧς ἐπὶ τειχέων ἡλλόμην ἐπ᾽ αὐτῆς οὐ μὴ φείσωμαι οὐ γὰρ ἐψευσάμην ῥήματα ἅγια θεοῦ μου </p>
 <p>11 τίς γάρ μου ἡ ἰσχύς ὅτι ὑπομένω ἢ τίς μου ὁ χρόνος ὅτι ἀνέχεταί μου ἡ ψυχή </p>
 <p>12 μὴ ἰσχὺς λίθων ἡ ἰσχύς μου ἢ αἱ σάρκες μού εἰσιν χάλκειαι </p>
 <p>13 ἦ οὐκ ἐπ᾽ αὐτῷ ἐπεποίθειν βοήθεια δὲ ἀπ᾽ ἐμοῦ ἄπεστιν </p>
 <p>14 ἀπείπατό με ἔλεος ἐπισκοπὴ δὲ κυρίου ὑπερεῖδέν με </p>
 <p>15 οὐ προσεῖδόν με οἱ ἐγγύτατοί μου ὥσπερ χειμάρρους ἐκλείπων ἢ ὥσπερ κῦμα παρῆλθόν με </p>
 <p>16 οἵτινές με διευλαβοῦντο νῦν ἐπιπεπτώκασίν μοι ὥσπερ χιὼν ἢ κρύσταλλος πεπηγώς </p>
 <p>17 καθὼς τακεῖσα θέρμης γενομένης οὐκ ἐπεγνώσθη ὅπερ ἦν </p>
 <p>18 οὕτως κἀγὼ κατελείφθην ὑπὸ πάντων ἀπωλόμην δὲ καὶ ἔξοικος ἐγενόμην </p>
 <p>19 ἴδετε ὁδοὺς Θαιμανων ἀτραποὺς Σαβων οἱ διορῶντες </p>
 <p>20 καὶ αἰσχύνην ὀφειλήσουσιν οἱ ἐπὶ πόλεσιν καὶ χρήμασιν πεποιθότες </p>
 <p>21 ἀτὰρ δὲ καὶ ὑμεῖς ἐπέβητέ μοι ἀνελεημόνως ὥστε ἰδόντες τὸ ἐμὸν τραῦμα φοβήθητε </p>
 <p>22 τί γάρ μή τι ὑμᾶς ᾔτησα ἢ τῆς παρ᾽ ὑμῶν ἰσχύος ἐπιδέομαι </p>
 <p>23 ὥστε σῶσαί με ἐξ ἐχθρῶν ἢ ἐκ χειρὸς δυναστῶν ῥύσασθαί με </p>
 <p>24 διδάξατέ με ἐγὼ δὲ κωφεύσω εἴ τι πεπλάνημαι φράσατέ μοι </p>
 <p>25 ἀλλ᾽ ὡς ἔοικεν φαῦλα ἀληθινοῦ ῥήματα οὐ γὰρ παρ᾽ ὑμῶν ἰσχὺν αἰτοῦμαι </p>
 <p>26 οὐδὲ ὁ ἔλεγχος ὑμῶν ῥήμασίν με παύσει οὐδὲ γὰρ ὑμῶν φθέγμα ῥήματος ἀνέξομαι </p>
 <p>27 πλὴν ὅτι ἐπ᾽ ὀρφανῷ ἐπιπίπτετε ἐνάλλεσθε δὲ ἐπὶ φίλῳ ὑμῶν </p>
 <p>28 νυνὶ δὲ εἰσβλέψας εἰς πρόσωπα ὑμῶν οὐ ψεύσομαι </p>
 <p>29 καθίσατε δὴ καὶ μὴ εἴη ἄδικον καὶ πάλιν τῷ δικαίῳ συνέρχεσθε </p>
 <p>30 οὐ γάρ ἐστιν ἐν γλώσσῃ μου ἄδικον ἢ ὁ λάρυγξ μου οὐχὶ σύνεσιν μελετᾷ </p>

 <p align="center"><a href="../vul/job006.htm">Vul</a></p><p>1 Respondens autem Job, dixit:</p>
 <p>2 [Utinam appenderentur peccata mea quibus iram merui,<br> et calamitas quam patior, in statera!<br></p>
 <p>3 Quasi arena maris hæc gravior appareret;<br> unde et verba mea dolore sunt plena:<br></p>
 <p>4 quia sagittæ Domini in me sunt,<br> quarum indignatio ebibit spiritum meum;<br> et terrores Domini militant contra me.<br></p>
 <p>5 Numquid rugiet onager cum habuerit herbam?<br> aut mugiet bos cum ante præsepe plenum steterit?<br></p>
 <p>6 aut poterit comedi insulsum, quod non est sale conditum?<br> aut potest aliquis gustare quod gustatum affert mortem?<br></p>
 <p>7 Quæ prius nolebat tangere anima mea,<br> nunc, præ angustia, cibi mei sunt.<br></p>
 <p>8 Quis det ut veniat petitio mea,<br> et quod expecto tribuat mihi Deus?<br></p>
 <p>9 et qui cpit, ipse me conterat;<br> solvat manum suam, et succidat me?<br></p>
 <p>10 Et hæc mihi sit consolatio, ut affligens me dolore, non parcat,<br> nec contradicam sermonibus Sancti.<br></p>
 <p>11 Quæ est enim fortitudo mea, ut sustineam?<br> aut quis finis meus, ut patienter agam?<br></p>
 <p>12 Nec fortitudo lapidum fortitudo mea,<br> nec caro mea ænea est.<br></p>
 <p>13 Ecce non est auxilium mihi in me,<br> et necessarii quoque mei recesserunt a me.<br></p>
 <p>14 Qui tollit ab amico suo misericordiam,<br> timorem Domini derelinquit.<br></p>
 <p>15 Fratres mei præterierunt me,<br> sicut torrens qui raptim transit in convallibus.<br></p>
 <p>16 Qui timent pruinam,<br> irruet super eos nix.<br></p>
 <p>17 Tempore quo fuerint dissipati, peribunt;<br> et ut incaluerit, solventur de loco suo.<br></p>
 <p>18 Involutæ sunt semitæ gressuum eorum;<br> ambulabunt in vacuum, et peribunt.<br></p>
 <p>19 Considerate semitas Thema, itinera Saba,<br> et expectate paulisper.<br></p>
 <p>20 Confusi sunt, quia speravi:<br> venerunt quoque usque ad me, et pudore cooperti sunt.<br></p>
 <p>21 Nunc venistis;<br> et modo videntes plagam meam, timetis.<br></p>
 <p>22 Numquid dixi: Afferte mihi,<br> et de substantia vestra donate mihi?<br></p>
 <p>23 vel: Liberate me de manu hostis,<br> et de manu robustorum eruite me?<br></p>
 <p>24 Docete me, et ego tacebo:<br> et si quid forte ignoravi, instruite me.<br></p>
 <p>25 Quare detraxistis sermonibus veritatis,<br> cum e vobis nullus sit qui possit arguere me?<br></p>
 <p>26 Ad increpandum tantum eloquia concinnatis,<br> et in ventum verba profertis.<br></p>
 <p>27 Super pupillum irruitis,<br> et subvertere nitimini amicum vestrum.<br></p>
 <p>28 Verumtamen quod cpistis explete:<br> præbete aurem, et videte an mentiar.<br></p>
 <p>29 Respondete, obsecro, absque contentione;<br> et loquentes id quod justum est, judicate.<br></p>
 <p>30 Et non invenietis in lingua mea iniquitatem,<br> nec in faucibus meis stultitia personabit.]</p>
