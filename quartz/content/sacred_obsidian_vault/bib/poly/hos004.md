 <p align="center"><a href="../kjv/hos004.htm">KJV</a></p><p>1  Hear<sup><a href="h8085.htm">8085</a></sup> the word<sup><a href="h1697.htm">1697</a></sup> of the LORD,<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> ye children<sup><a href="h1121.htm">1121</a></sup> of Israel:<sup><a href="h3478.htm">3478</a></sup> for<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> the LORD<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> hath a controversy<sup><a href="h7379.htm">7379</a></sup> with<sup><a href="h5973.htm">5973</a></sup> the inhabitants<sup><a href="h3427.htm">3427</a></sup> of the land,<sup><a href="h0776.htm">776</a></sup> because<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> there is no<sup><a href="h0369.htm">369</a></sup> truth,<sup><a href="h0571.htm">571</a></sup> nor<sup><a href="h0369.htm">369</a></sup> mercy,<sup><a href="h2617.htm">2617</a></sup> nor<sup><a href="h0369.htm">369</a></sup> knowledge<sup><a href="h1847.htm">1847</a></sup> of God<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> in the land.<sup><a href="h0776.htm">776</a></sup> </p>
 <p>2  By swearing,<sup><a href="h0422.htm">422</a></sup> and lying,<sup><a href="h3584.htm">3584</a></sup> and killing,<sup><a href="h7523.htm">7523</a></sup> and stealing,<sup><a href="h1589.htm">1589</a></sup> and committing adultery,<sup><a href="h5003.htm">5003</a></sup> they break out,<sup><a href="h6555.htm">6555</a></sup> and blood<sup><a href="h1818.htm">1818</a></sup> toucheth<sup><a href="h5060.htm">5060</a></sup> blood.<sup><a href="h1818.htm">1818</a></sup> </p>
 <p>3  Therefore<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3651.htm">3651</a></sup> shall the land<sup><a href="h0776.htm">776</a></sup> mourn,<sup><a href="h0056.htm">56</a></sup> and every one<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> that dwelleth<sup><a href="h3427.htm">3427</a></sup> therein shall languish,<sup><a href="h0535.htm">535</a></sup> with the beasts<sup><a href="h2416.htm">2416</a></sup> of the field,<sup><a href="h7704.htm">7704</a></sup> and with the fowls<sup><a href="h5775.htm">5775</a></sup> of heaven;<sup><a href="h8064.htm">8064</a></sup> yea, the fishes<sup><a href="h1709.htm">1709</a></sup> of the sea<sup><a href="h3220.htm">3220</a></sup> also<sup><a href="h1571.htm">1571</a></sup> shall be taken away.<sup><a href="h0622.htm">622</a></sup> </p>
 <p>4  Yet<sup><a href="h0389.htm">389</a></sup> let no<sup><a href="h0408.htm">408</a></sup> man<sup><a href="h0376.htm">376</a></sup> strive,<sup><a href="h7378.htm">7378</a></sup> nor<sup><a href="h0408.htm">408</a></sup> reprove<sup><a href="h3198.htm">3198</a></sup> another:<sup><a href="h0376.htm">376</a></sup> for thy people<sup><a href="h5971.htm">5971</a></sup> are as they that strive<sup><a href="h7378.htm">7378</a></sup> with the priest.<sup><a href="h3548.htm">3548</a></sup> </p>
 <p>5  Therefore shalt thou fall<sup><a href="h3782.htm">3782</a></sup> in the day,<sup><a href="h3117.htm">3117</a></sup> and the prophet<sup><a href="h5030.htm">5030</a></sup> also<sup><a href="h1571.htm">1571</a></sup> shall fall<sup><a href="h3782.htm">3782</a></sup> with<sup><a href="h5973.htm">5973</a></sup> thee in the night,<sup><a href="h3915.htm">3915</a></sup> and I will destroy<sup><a href="h1820.htm">1820</a></sup> thy mother.<sup><a href="h0517.htm">517</a></sup> </p>
 <p>6  My people<sup><a href="h5971.htm">5971</a></sup> are destroyed<sup><a href="h1820.htm">1820</a></sup> for lack<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h1097.htm">1097</a></sup> of knowledge:<sup><a href="h1847.htm">1847</a></sup> because<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> thou<sup><a href="h0859.htm">859</a></sup> hast rejected<sup><a href="h3988.htm">3988</a></sup> knowledge,<sup><a href="h1847.htm">1847</a></sup> I will also reject<sup><a href="h3988.htm">3988</a></sup> thee, that thou shalt be no priest<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3547.htm">3547</a></sup> to me: seeing thou hast forgotten<sup><a href="h7911.htm">7911</a></sup> the law<sup><a href="h8451.htm">8451</a></sup> of thy God,<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> I<sup><a href="h0589.htm">589</a></sup> will also<sup><a href="h1571.htm">1571</a></sup> forget<sup><a href="h7911.htm">7911</a></sup> thy children.<sup><a href="h1121.htm">1121</a></sup> </p>
 <p>7  As they were increased,<sup><a href="h7231.htm">7231</a></sup> so<sup><a href="h3651.htm">3651</a></sup> they sinned<sup><a href="h2398.htm">2398</a></sup> against me: therefore will I change<sup><a href="h4171.htm">4171</a></sup> their glory<sup><a href="h3519.htm">3519</a></sup> into shame.<sup><a href="h7036.htm">7036</a></sup> </p>
 <p>8  They eat up<sup><a href="h0398.htm">398</a></sup> the sin<sup><a href="h2403.htm">2403</a></sup> of my people,<sup><a href="h5971.htm">5971</a></sup> and they set<sup><a href="h5375.htm">5375</a></sup> their heart<sup><a href="h5315.htm">5315</a></sup> on<sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> their iniquity.<sup><a href="h5771.htm">5771</a></sup> </p>
 <p>9  And there shall be,<sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> like people,<sup><a href="h5971.htm">5971</a></sup> like priest:<sup><a href="h3548.htm">3548</a></sup> and I will punish<sup><a href="h6485.htm">6485</a></sup> <sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> them for their ways,<sup><a href="h1870.htm">1870</a></sup> and reward<sup><a href="h7725.htm">7725</a></sup> them their doings.<sup><a href="h4611.htm">4611</a></sup> </p>
 <p>10  For they shall eat,<sup><a href="h0398.htm">398</a></sup> and not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> have enough:<sup><a href="h7646.htm">7646</a></sup> they shall commit whoredom,<sup><a href="h2181.htm">2181</a></sup> and shall not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> increase:<sup><a href="h6555.htm">6555</a></sup> because<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> they have left off<sup><a href="h5800.htm">5800</a></sup> to take heed<sup><a href="h8104.htm">8104</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> to the LORD.<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> </p>
 <p>11  Whoredom<sup><a href="h2184.htm">2184</a></sup> and wine<sup><a href="h3196.htm">3196</a></sup> and new wine<sup><a href="h8492.htm">8492</a></sup> take away<sup><a href="h3947.htm">3947</a></sup> the heart.<sup><a href="h3820.htm">3820</a></sup> </p>
 <p>12  My people<sup><a href="h5971.htm">5971</a></sup> ask counsel<sup><a href="h7592.htm">7592</a></sup> at their stocks,<sup><a href="h6086.htm">6086</a></sup> and their staff<sup><a href="h4731.htm">4731</a></sup> declareth<sup><a href="h5046.htm">5046</a></sup> unto them: for<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> the spirit<sup><a href="h7307.htm">7307</a></sup> of whoredoms<sup><a href="h2183.htm">2183</a></sup> hath caused them to err,<sup><a href="h8582.htm">8582</a></sup> and they have gone a whoring<sup><a href="h2181.htm">2181</a></sup> from under<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h8478.htm">8478</a></sup> their God.<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> </p>
 <p>13  They sacrifice<sup><a href="h2076.htm">2076</a></sup> upon<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> the tops<sup><a href="h7218.htm">7218</a></sup> of the mountains,<sup><a href="h2022.htm">2022</a></sup> and burn incense<sup><a href="h6999.htm">6999</a></sup> upon<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> the hills,<sup><a href="h1389.htm">1389</a></sup> under<sup><a href="h8478.htm">8478</a></sup> oaks<sup><a href="h0437.htm">437</a></sup> and poplars<sup><a href="h3839.htm">3839</a></sup> and elms,<sup><a href="h0424.htm">424</a></sup> because<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> the shadow<sup><a href="h6738.htm">6738</a></sup> thereof is good:<sup><a href="h2896.htm">2896</a></sup> therefore<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3651.htm">3651</a></sup> your daughters<sup><a href="h1323.htm">1323</a></sup> shall commit whoredom,<sup><a href="h2181.htm">2181</a></sup> and your spouses<sup><a href="h3618.htm">3618</a></sup> shall commit adultery.<sup><a href="h5003.htm">5003</a></sup> </p>
 <p>14  I will not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> punish<sup><a href="h6485.htm">6485</a></sup> <sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> your daughters<sup><a href="h1323.htm">1323</a></sup> when<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> they commit whoredom,<sup><a href="h2181.htm">2181</a></sup> nor your spouses<sup><a href="h3618.htm">3618</a></sup> when<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> they commit adultery:<sup><a href="h5003.htm">5003</a></sup> for<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> themselves<sup><a href="h1992.htm">1992</a></sup> are separated<sup><a href="h6504.htm">6504</a></sup> with<sup><a href="h5973.htm">5973</a></sup> whores,<sup><a href="h2181.htm">2181</a></sup> and they sacrifice<sup><a href="h2076.htm">2076</a></sup> with<sup><a href="h5973.htm">5973</a></sup> harlots:<sup><a href="h6948.htm">6948</a></sup> therefore the people<sup><a href="h5971.htm">5971</a></sup> that doth not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> understand<sup><a href="h0995.htm">995</a></sup> shall fall.<sup><a href="h3832.htm">3832</a></sup> </p>

 <p>16  For<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> Israel<sup><a href="h3478.htm">3478</a></sup> slideth back<sup><a href="h5637.htm">5637</a></sup> as a backsliding<sup><a href="h5637.htm">5637</a></sup> heifer:<sup><a href="h6510.htm">6510</a></sup> now<sup><a href="h6258.htm">6258</a></sup> the LORD<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> will feed<sup><a href="h7462.htm">7462</a></sup> them as a lamb<sup><a href="h3532.htm">3532</a></sup> in a large place.<sup><a href="h4800.htm">4800</a></sup> </p>

 <p>18  Their drink<sup><a href="h5435.htm">5435</a></sup> is sour:<sup><a href="h5493.htm">5493</a></sup> they have committed whoredom continually:<sup><a href="h2181.htm">2181</a></sup> <sup><a href="h2181.htm">2181</a></sup> her rulers<sup><a href="h4043.htm">4043</a></sup> with shame<sup><a href="h7036.htm">7036</a></sup> do love,<sup><a href="h0157.htm">157</a></sup> Give<sup><a href="h3051.htm">3051</a></sup> ye. </p>

 <p align="center"><a href="../sep/hos004.htm">Sep</a></p><p>1 ἀκούσατε λόγον κυρίου υἱοὶ Ισραηλ διότι κρίσις τῷ κυρίῳ πρὸς τοὺς κατοικοῦντας τὴν γῆν διότι οὐκ ἔστιν ἀλήθεια οὐδὲ ἔλεος οὐδὲ ἐπίγνωσις θεοῦ ἐπὶ τῆς γῆς </p>
 <p>2 ἀρὰ καὶ ψεῦδος καὶ φόνος καὶ κλοπὴ καὶ μοιχεία κέχυται ἐπὶ τῆς γῆς καὶ αἵματα ἐφ᾽ αἵμασιν μίσγουσιν </p>
 <p>3 διὰ τοῦτο πενθήσει ἡ γῆ καὶ σμικρυνθήσεται σὺν πᾶσιν τοῖς κατοικοῦσιν αὐτήν σὺν τοῖς θηρίοις τοῦ ἀγροῦ καὶ σὺν τοῖς ἑρπετοῖς τῆς γῆς καὶ σὺν τοῖς πετεινοῖς τοῦ οὐρανοῦ καὶ οἱ ἰχθύες τῆς θαλάσσης ἐκλείψουσιν </p>
 <p>4 ὅπως μηδεὶς μήτε δικάζηται μήτε ἐλέγχῃ μηδείς ὁ δὲ λαός μου ὡς ἀντιλεγόμενος ἱερεύς </p>
 <p>5 καὶ ἀσθενήσεις ἡμέρας καὶ ἀσθενήσει καὶ προφήτης μετὰ σοῦ νυκτὶ ὡμοίωσα τὴν μητέρα σου </p>
 <p>6 ὡμοιώθη ὁ λαός μου ὡς οὐκ ἔχων γνῶσιν ὅτι σὺ ἐπίγνωσιν ἀπώσω κἀγὼ ἀπώσομαι σὲ τοῦ μὴ ἱερατεύειν μοι καὶ ἐπελάθου νόμον θεοῦ σου κἀγὼ ἐπιλήσομαι τέκνων σου </p>
 <p>7 κατὰ τὸ πλῆθος αὐτῶν οὕτως ἥμαρτόν μοι τὴν δόξαν αὐτῶν εἰς ἀτιμίαν θήσομαι </p>
 <p>8 ἁμαρτίας λαοῦ μου φάγονται καὶ ἐν ταῖς ἀδικίαις αὐτῶν λήμψονται τὰς ψυχὰς αὐτῶν </p>
 <p>9 καὶ ἔσται καθὼς ὁ λαὸς οὕτως καὶ ὁ ἱερεύς καὶ ἐκδικήσω ἐπ᾽ αὐτὸν τὰς ὁδοὺς αὐτοῦ καὶ τὰ διαβούλια αὐτοῦ ἀνταποδώσω αὐτῷ </p>
 <p>10 καὶ φάγονται καὶ οὐ μὴ ἐμπλησθῶσιν ἐπόρνευσαν καὶ οὐ μὴ κατευθύνωσιν διότι τὸν κύριον ἐγκατέλιπον τοῦ φυλάξαι </p>
 <p>11 πορνείαν καὶ οἶνον καὶ μέθυσμα ἐδέξατο καρδία λαοῦ μου </p>
 <p>12 ἐν συμβόλοις ἐπηρώτων καὶ ἐν ῥάβδοις αὐτοῦ ἀπήγγελλον αὐτῷ πνεύματι πορνείας ἐπλανήθησαν καὶ ἐξεπόρνευσαν ἀπὸ τοῦ θεοῦ αὐτῶν </p>
 <p>13 ἐπὶ τὰς κορυφὰς τῶν ὀρέων ἐθυσίαζον καὶ ἐπὶ τοὺς βουνοὺς ἔθυον ὑποκάτω δρυὸς καὶ λεύκης καὶ δένδρου συσκιάζοντος ὅτι καλὸν σκέπη διὰ τοῦτο ἐκπορνεύσουσιν αἱ θυγατέρες ὑμῶν καὶ αἱ νύμφαι ὑμῶν μοιχεύσουσιν </p>
 <p>14 καὶ οὐ μὴ ἐπισκέψωμαι ἐπὶ τὰς θυγατέρας ὑμῶν ὅταν πορνεύωσιν καὶ ἐπὶ τὰς νύμφας ὑμῶν ὅταν μοιχεύωσιν διότι καὶ αὐτοὶ μετὰ τῶν πορνῶν συνεφύροντο καὶ μετὰ τῶν τετελεσμένων ἔθυον καὶ ὁ λαὸς ὁ συνίων συνεπλέκετο μετὰ πόρνης </p>
 <p>15 σὺ δέ Ισραηλ μὴ ἀγνόει καὶ Ιουδα μὴ εἰσπορεύεσθε εἰς Γαλγαλα καὶ μὴ ἀναβαίνετε εἰς τὸν οἶκον Ων καὶ μὴ ὀμνύετε ζῶντα κύριον </p>
 <p>16 ὅτι ὡς δάμαλις παροιστρῶσα παροίστρησεν Ισραηλ νῦν νεμήσει αὐτοὺς κύριος ὡς ἀμνὸν ἐν εὐρυχώρῳ </p>
 <p>17 μέτοχος εἰδώλων Εφραιμ ἔθηκεν ἑαυτῷ σκάνδαλα </p>
 <p>18 ᾑρέτισεν Χαναναίους πορνεύοντες ἐξεπόρνευσαν ἠγάπησαν ἀτιμίαν ἐκ φρυάγματος αὐτῶν </p>
 <p>19 συστροφὴ πνεύματος σὺ εἶ ἐν ταῖς πτέρυξιν αὐτῆς καὶ καταισχυνθήσονται ἐκ τῶν θυσιαστηρίων αὐτῶν </p>

 <p align="center"><a href="../vul/hos004.htm">Vul</a></p><p>1 [Audite verbum Domini, filii Israël,<br> quia judicium Domino cum habitatoribus terræ:<br> non est enim veritas, et non est misericordia,<br> et non est scientia Dei in terra.<br></p>
 <p>2 Maledictum, et mendacium,<br> et homicidium, et furtum, et adulterium inundaverunt,<br> et sanguis sanguinem tetigit.<br></p>
 <p>3 Propter hoc lugebit terra,<br> et infirmabitur omnis qui habitat in ea,<br> in bestia agri, et in volucre cæli;<br> sed et pisces maris congregabuntur.<br></p>
 <p>4 Verumtamen unusquisque non judicet,<br> et non arguatur vir:<br> populus enim tuus sicut hi qui contradicunt sacerdoti.<br></p>
 <p>5 Et corrues hodie,<br> et corruet etiam propheta tecum.<br> Nocte tacere feci matrem tuam.<br></p>
 <p>6 Conticuit populus meus,<br> eo quod non habuerit scientiam:<br> quia tu scientiam repulisti,<br> repellam te, ne sacerdotio fungaris mihi;<br> et oblita es legis Dei tui,<br> obliviscar filiorum tuorum et ego.<br></p>
 <p>7 Secundum multitudinem eorum sic peccaverunt mihi:<br> gloriam eorum in ignominiam commutabo.<br></p>
 <p>8 Peccata populi mei comedent,<br> et ad iniquitatem eorum sublevabunt animas eorum.<br></p>
 <p>9 Et erit sicut populus, sic sacerdos;<br> et visitabo super eum vias ejus,<br> et cogitationes ejus reddam ei.<br></p>
 <p>10 Et comedent, et non saturabuntur;<br> fornicati sunt, et non cessaverunt:<br> quoniam Dominum dereliquerunt in non custodiendo.<br></p>
 <p>11 Fornicatio, et vinum, et ebrietas auferunt cor.<br></p>
 <p>12 Populus meus in ligno suo interrogavit,<br> et baculus ejus annuntiavit ei;<br> spiritus enim fornicationum decepit eos,<br> et fornicati sunt a Deo suo.<br></p>
 <p>13 Super capita montium sacrificabant,<br> et super colles ascendebant thymiama;<br> subtus quercum, et populum, et terebinthum,<br> quia bona erat umbra ejus;<br> ideo fornicabuntur filiæ vestræ,<br> et sponsæ vestræ adulteræ erunt.<br></p>
 <p>14 Non visitabo super filias vestras cum fuerint fornicatæ,<br> et super sponsas vestras cum adulteraverint,<br> quoniam ipsi cum meretricibus conversabantur,<br> et cum effeminatis sacrificabant;<br> et populus non intelligens vapulabit.<br></p>
 <p>15 Si fornicaris tu, Israël,<br> non delinquat saltem Juda;<br> et nolite ingredi in Galgala,<br> et ne ascenderitis in Bethaven,<br> neque juraveritis: Vivit Dominus!<br></p>
 <p>16 Quoniam sicut vacca lasciviens declinavit Israël;<br> nunc pascet eos Dominus, quasi agnum in latitudine.<br></p>
 <p>17 Particeps idolorum Ephraim: dimitte eum.<br></p>
 <p>18 Separatum est convivium eorum;<br> fornicatione fornicati sunt:<br> dilexerunt afferre ignominiam protectores ejus.<br></p>
 <p>19 Ligavit eum spiritus in alis suis,<br> et confundentur a sacrificiis suis.]</p>
