 <h3 align="CENTER">Psalms 92</h3><table border="1">
 <tr>
 <td valign="top">
 <p align="center"><a href="../kjv/psa092.htm">KJV</a></p><p>1  A Psalm<sup><a href="h4210.htm">4210</a></sup> or Song<sup><a href="h7892.htm">7892</a></sup> for the sabbath<sup><a href="h7676.htm">7676</a></sup> day.<sup><a href="h3117.htm">3117</a></sup> It is a good<sup><a href="h2896.htm">2896</a></sup> thing to give thanks<sup><a href="h3034.htm">3034</a></sup> unto the LORD,<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> and to sing praises<sup><a href="h2167.htm">2167</a></sup> unto thy name,<sup><a href="h8034.htm">8034</a></sup> O most High:<sup><a href="h5945.htm">5945</a></sup> </p>
 <p>2  To show forth<sup><a href="h5046.htm">5046</a></sup> thy lovingkindness<sup><a href="h2617.htm">2617</a></sup> in the morning,<sup><a href="h1242.htm">1242</a></sup> and thy faithfulness<sup><a href="h0530.htm">530</a></sup> every night,<sup><a href="h3915.htm">3915</a></sup> </p>
 <p>3  Upon<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> an instrument of ten strings,<sup><a href="h6218.htm">6218</a></sup> and upon<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> the psaltery;<sup><a href="h5035.htm">5035</a></sup> upon the harp<sup><a href="h3658.htm">3658</a></sup> with<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> a solemn sound.<sup><a href="h1902.htm">1902</a></sup> </p>
 <p>4  For<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> thou, LORD,<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> hast made me glad<sup><a href="h8055.htm">8055</a></sup> through thy work:<sup><a href="h6467.htm">6467</a></sup> I will triumph<sup><a href="h7442.htm">7442</a></sup> in the works<sup><a href="h4639.htm">4639</a></sup> of thy hands.<sup><a href="h3027.htm">3027</a></sup> </p>
 <p>5  O LORD,<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> how<sup><a href="h4100.htm">4100</a></sup> great<sup><a href="h1431.htm">1431</a></sup> are thy works!<sup><a href="h4639.htm">4639</a></sup> and thy thoughts<sup><a href="h4284.htm">4284</a></sup> are very<sup><a href="h3966.htm">3966</a></sup> deep.<sup><a href="h6009.htm">6009</a></sup> </p>
 <p>6  A brutish<sup><a href="h1198.htm">1198</a></sup> man<sup><a href="h0376.htm">376</a></sup> knoweth<sup><a href="h3045.htm">3045</a></sup> not;<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> neither<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> doth a fool<sup><a href="h3684.htm">3684</a></sup> understand<sup><a href="h0995.htm">995</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> this.<sup><a href="h2063.htm">2063</a></sup> </p>
 <p>7  When the wicked<sup><a href="h7563.htm">7563</a></sup> spring<sup><a href="h6524.htm">6524</a></sup> as<sup><a href="h3644.htm">3644</a></sup> the grass,<sup><a href="h6212.htm">6212</a></sup> and when all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> the workers<sup><a href="h6466.htm">6466</a></sup> of iniquity<sup><a href="h0205.htm">205</a></sup> do flourish;<sup><a href="h6692.htm">6692</a></sup> it is that they shall be destroyed<sup><a href="h8045.htm">8045</a></sup> forever:<sup><a href="h5703.htm">5703</a></sup> <sup><a href="h5704.htm">5704</a></sup> </p>
 <p>8  But thou,<sup><a href="h0859.htm">859</a></sup> LORD,<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> art most high<sup><a href="h4791.htm">4791</a></sup> forevermore.<sup><a href="h5769.htm">5769</a></sup> </p>
 <p>9  For,<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> lo,<sup><a href="h2009.htm">2009</a></sup> thine enemies,<sup><a href="h0341.htm">341</a></sup> O LORD,<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> for,<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> lo,<sup><a href="h2009.htm">2009</a></sup> thine enemies<sup><a href="h0341.htm">341</a></sup> shall perish;<sup><a href="h0006.htm">6</a></sup> all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> the workers<sup><a href="h6466.htm">6466</a></sup> of iniquity<sup><a href="h0205.htm">205</a></sup> shall be scattered.<sup><a href="h6504.htm">6504</a></sup> </p>
 <p>10  But my horn<sup><a href="h7161.htm">7161</a></sup> shalt thou exalt<sup><a href="h7311.htm">7311</a></sup> like the horn of a unicorn:<sup><a href="h7214.htm">7214</a></sup> I shall be anointed<sup><a href="h1101.htm">1101</a></sup> with fresh<sup><a href="h7488.htm">7488</a></sup> oil.<sup><a href="h8081.htm">8081</a></sup> </p>
 <p>11  Mine eye<sup><a href="h5869.htm">5869</a></sup> also shall see<sup><a href="h5027.htm">5027</a></sup> my desire on mine enemies,<sup><a href="h7790.htm">7790</a></sup> and mine ears<sup><a href="h0241.htm">241</a></sup> shall hear<sup><a href="h8085.htm">8085</a></sup> my desire of the wicked<sup><a href="h7489.htm">7489</a></sup> that rise up<sup><a href="h6965.htm">6965</a></sup> against<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> me. </p>
 <p>12  The righteous<sup><a href="h6662.htm">6662</a></sup> shall flourish<sup><a href="h6524.htm">6524</a></sup> like the palm tree:<sup><a href="h8558.htm">8558</a></sup> he shall grow<sup><a href="h7685.htm">7685</a></sup> like a cedar<sup><a href="h0730.htm">730</a></sup> in Lebanon.<sup><a href="h3844.htm">3844</a></sup> </p>
 <p>13  Those that be planted<sup><a href="h8362.htm">8362</a></sup> in the house<sup><a href="h1004.htm">1004</a></sup> of the LORD<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> shall flourish<sup><a href="h6524.htm">6524</a></sup> in the courts<sup><a href="h2691.htm">2691</a></sup> of our God.<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> </p>
 <p>14  They shall still<sup><a href="h5750.htm">5750</a></sup> bring forth fruit<sup><a href="h5107.htm">5107</a></sup> in old age;<sup><a href="h7872.htm">7872</a></sup> they shall be<sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> fat<sup><a href="h1879.htm">1879</a></sup> and flourishing;<sup><a href="h7488.htm">7488</a></sup> </p>
 <p>15  To show<sup><a href="h5046.htm">5046</a></sup> that<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> the LORD<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> is upright:<sup><a href="h3477.htm">3477</a></sup> he is my rock,<sup><a href="h6697.htm">6697</a></sup> and there is no<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> unrighteousness<sup><a href="h5766.htm">5766</a></sup> in him. </p>
