 <p align="center"><a href="../kjv/isa032.htm">KJV</a></p><p>1  Behold,<sup><a href="h2005.htm">2005</a></sup> a king<sup><a href="h4428.htm">4428</a></sup> shall reign<sup><a href="h4427.htm">4427</a></sup> in righteousness,<sup><a href="h6664.htm">6664</a></sup> and princes<sup><a href="h8269.htm">8269</a></sup> shall rule<sup><a href="h8323.htm">8323</a></sup> in judgment.<sup><a href="h4941.htm">4941</a></sup> </p>
 <p>2  And a man<sup><a href="h0376.htm">376</a></sup> shall be<sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> as a hiding place<sup><a href="h4224.htm">4224</a></sup> from the wind,<sup><a href="h7307.htm">7307</a></sup> and a covert<sup><a href="h5643.htm">5643</a></sup> from the tempest;<sup><a href="h2230.htm">2230</a></sup> as rivers<sup><a href="h6388.htm">6388</a></sup> of water<sup><a href="h4325.htm">4325</a></sup> in a dry place,<sup><a href="h6724.htm">6724</a></sup> as the shadow<sup><a href="h6738.htm">6738</a></sup> of a great<sup><a href="h3515.htm">3515</a></sup> rock<sup><a href="h5553.htm">5553</a></sup> in a weary<sup><a href="h5889.htm">5889</a></sup> land.<sup><a href="h0776.htm">776</a></sup> </p>
 <p>3  And the eyes<sup><a href="h5869.htm">5869</a></sup> of them that see<sup><a href="h7200.htm">7200</a></sup> shall not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> be dim,<sup><a href="h8159.htm">8159</a></sup> and the ears<sup><a href="h0241.htm">241</a></sup> of them that hear<sup><a href="h8085.htm">8085</a></sup> shall hearken.<sup><a href="h7181.htm">7181</a></sup> </p>
 <p>4  The heart<sup><a href="h3824.htm">3824</a></sup> also of the rash<sup><a href="h4116.htm">4116</a></sup> shall understand<sup><a href="h0995.htm">995</a></sup> knowledge,<sup><a href="h3045.htm">3045</a></sup> and the tongue<sup><a href="h3956.htm">3956</a></sup> of the stammerers<sup><a href="h5926.htm">5926</a></sup> shall be ready<sup><a href="h4116.htm">4116</a></sup> to speak<sup><a href="h1696.htm">1696</a></sup> plainly.<sup><a href="h6703.htm">6703</a></sup> </p>
 <p>5  The vile person<sup><a href="h5036.htm">5036</a></sup> shall be no<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> more<sup><a href="h5750.htm">5750</a></sup> called<sup><a href="h7121.htm">7121</a></sup> liberal,<sup><a href="h5081.htm">5081</a></sup> nor<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> the churl<sup><a href="h3596.htm">3596</a></sup> said<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> to be bountiful.<sup><a href="h7771.htm">7771</a></sup> </p>
 <p>6  For<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> the vile person<sup><a href="h5036.htm">5036</a></sup> will speak<sup><a href="h1696.htm">1696</a></sup> villainy,<sup><a href="h5039.htm">5039</a></sup> and his heart<sup><a href="h3820.htm">3820</a></sup> will work<sup><a href="h6213.htm">6213</a></sup> iniquity,<sup><a href="h0205.htm">205</a></sup> to practice<sup><a href="h6213.htm">6213</a></sup> hypocrisy,<sup><a href="h2612.htm">2612</a></sup> and to utter<sup><a href="h1696.htm">1696</a></sup> error<sup><a href="h8442.htm">8442</a></sup> against<sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> the LORD,<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> to make empty<sup><a href="h7324.htm">7324</a></sup> the soul<sup><a href="h5315.htm">5315</a></sup> of the hungry,<sup><a href="h7457.htm">7457</a></sup> and he will cause the drink<sup><a href="h4945.htm">4945</a></sup> of the thirsty<sup><a href="h6771.htm">6771</a></sup> to fail.<sup><a href="h2637.htm">2637</a></sup> </p>
 <p>7  The instruments<sup><a href="h3627.htm">3627</a></sup> also of the churl<sup><a href="h3596.htm">3596</a></sup> are evil:<sup><a href="h7451.htm">7451</a></sup> he<sup><a href="h1931.htm">1931</a></sup> deviseth<sup><a href="h3289.htm">3289</a></sup> wicked devices<sup><a href="h2154.htm">2154</a></sup> to destroy<sup><a href="h2254.htm">2254</a></sup> the poor<sup><a href="h6041.htm">6041</a></sup> with lying<sup><a href="h8267.htm">8267</a></sup> words,<sup><a href="h0561.htm">561</a></sup> even when the needy<sup><a href="h0034.htm">34</a></sup> speaketh<sup><a href="h1696.htm">1696</a></sup> right.<sup><a href="h4941.htm">4941</a></sup> </p>
 <p>8  But the liberal<sup><a href="h5081.htm">5081</a></sup> deviseth<sup><a href="h3289.htm">3289</a></sup> liberal things;<sup><a href="h5081.htm">5081</a></sup> and by<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> liberal things<sup><a href="h5081.htm">5081</a></sup> shall he stand.<sup><a href="h6965.htm">6965</a></sup> </p>
 <p>9  Rise up,<sup><a href="h6965.htm">6965</a></sup> ye women<sup><a href="h0802.htm">802</a></sup> that are at ease;<sup><a href="h7600.htm">7600</a></sup> hear<sup><a href="h8085.htm">8085</a></sup> my voice,<sup><a href="h6963.htm">6963</a></sup> ye careless<sup><a href="h0982.htm">982</a></sup> daughters;<sup><a href="h1323.htm">1323</a></sup> give ear<sup><a href="h0238.htm">238</a></sup> unto my speech.<sup><a href="h0565.htm">565</a></sup> </p>
 <p>10  Many days<sup><a href="h3117.htm">3117</a></sup> and years<sup><a href="h8141.htm">8141</a></sup> shall ye be troubled,<sup><a href="h7264.htm">7264</a></sup> ye careless women:<sup><a href="h0982.htm">982</a></sup> for<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> the vintage<sup><a href="h1210.htm">1210</a></sup> shall fail,<sup><a href="h3615.htm">3615</a></sup> the gathering<sup><a href="h0625.htm">625</a></sup> shall not<sup><a href="h1097.htm">1097</a></sup> come.<sup><a href="h0935.htm">935</a></sup> </p>
 <p>11  Tremble,<sup><a href="h2729.htm">2729</a></sup> ye women that are at ease;<sup><a href="h7600.htm">7600</a></sup> be troubled,<sup><a href="h7264.htm">7264</a></sup> ye careless ones:<sup><a href="h0982.htm">982</a></sup> strip<sup><a href="h6584.htm">6584</a></sup> you, and make you bare,<sup><a href="h6209.htm">6209</a></sup> and gird<sup><a href="h2290.htm">2290</a></sup> sackcloth upon<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> your loins.<sup><a href="h2504.htm">2504</a></sup> </p>
 <p>12  They shall lament<sup><a href="h5594.htm">5594</a></sup> for<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> the teats,<sup><a href="h7699.htm">7699</a></sup> for<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> the pleasant<sup><a href="h2531.htm">2531</a></sup> fields,<sup><a href="h7704.htm">7704</a></sup> for<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> the fruitful<sup><a href="h6509.htm">6509</a></sup> vine.<sup><a href="h1612.htm">1612</a></sup> </p>
 <p>13  Upon<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> the land<sup><a href="h0127.htm">127</a></sup> of my people<sup><a href="h5971.htm">5971</a></sup> shall come up<sup><a href="h5927.htm">5927</a></sup> thorns<sup><a href="h6975.htm">6975</a></sup> and briers;<sup><a href="h8068.htm">8068</a></sup> yea,<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> upon<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> the houses<sup><a href="h1004.htm">1004</a></sup> of joy<sup><a href="h4885.htm">4885</a></sup> in the joyous<sup><a href="h5947.htm">5947</a></sup> city:<sup><a href="h7151.htm">7151</a></sup> </p>
 <p>14  Because<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> the palaces<sup><a href="h0759.htm">759</a></sup> shall be forsaken;<sup><a href="h5203.htm">5203</a></sup> the multitude<sup><a href="h1995.htm">1995</a></sup> of the city<sup><a href="h5892.htm">5892</a></sup> shall be left;<sup><a href="h5800.htm">5800</a></sup> the forts<sup><a href="h6076.htm">6076</a></sup> and towers<sup><a href="h0975.htm">975</a></sup> shall be<sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> for<sup><a href="h1157.htm">1157</a></sup> dens<sup><a href="h4631.htm">4631</a></sup> forever,<sup><a href="h5704.htm">5704</a></sup> <sup><a href="h5769.htm">5769</a></sup> a joy<sup><a href="h4885.htm">4885</a></sup> of wild asses,<sup><a href="h6501.htm">6501</a></sup> a pasture<sup><a href="h4829.htm">4829</a></sup> of flocks;<sup><a href="h5739.htm">5739</a></sup> </p>
 <p>15  Until<sup><a href="h5704.htm">5704</a></sup> the spirit<sup><a href="h7307.htm">7307</a></sup> be poured<sup><a href="h6168.htm">6168</a></sup> upon<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> us from on high,<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h4791.htm">4791</a></sup> and the wilderness<sup><a href="h4057.htm">4057</a></sup> be<sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> a fruitful field,<sup><a href="h3759.htm">3759</a></sup> and the fruitful field<sup><a href="h3759.htm">3759</a></sup> be counted<sup><a href="h2803.htm">2803</a></sup> for a forest.<sup><a href="h3293.htm">3293</a></sup> </p>
 <p>16  Then judgment<sup><a href="h4941.htm">4941</a></sup> shall dwell<sup><a href="h7931.htm">7931</a></sup> in the wilderness,<sup><a href="h4057.htm">4057</a></sup> and righteousness<sup><a href="h6666.htm">6666</a></sup> remain<sup><a href="h3427.htm">3427</a></sup> in the fruitful field.<sup><a href="h3759.htm">3759</a></sup> </p>
 <p>17  And the work<sup><a href="h4639.htm">4639</a></sup> of righteousness<sup><a href="h6666.htm">6666</a></sup> shall be<sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> peace;<sup><a href="h7965.htm">7965</a></sup> and the effect<sup><a href="h5656.htm">5656</a></sup> of righteousness<sup><a href="h6666.htm">6666</a></sup> quietness<sup><a href="h8252.htm">8252</a></sup> and assurance<sup><a href="h0983.htm">983</a></sup> forever.<sup><a href="h5704.htm">5704</a></sup> <sup><a href="h5769.htm">5769</a></sup> </p>
 <p>18  And my people<sup><a href="h5971.htm">5971</a></sup> shall dwell<sup><a href="h3427.htm">3427</a></sup> in a peaceable<sup><a href="h7965.htm">7965</a></sup> habitation,<sup><a href="h5116.htm">5116</a></sup> and in sure<sup><a href="h4009.htm">4009</a></sup> dwellings,<sup><a href="h4908.htm">4908</a></sup> and in quiet<sup><a href="h7600.htm">7600</a></sup> resting places;<sup><a href="h4496.htm">4496</a></sup> </p>
 <p>19  When it shall hail,<sup><a href="h1258.htm">1258</a></sup> coming down<sup><a href="h3381.htm">3381</a></sup> on the forest;<sup><a href="h3293.htm">3293</a></sup> and the city<sup><a href="h5892.htm">5892</a></sup> shall be low<sup><a href="h8213.htm">8213</a></sup> in a low place.<sup><a href="h8218.htm">8218</a></sup> </p>
 <p>20  Blessed<sup><a href="h0835.htm">835</a></sup> are ye that sow<sup><a href="h2232.htm">2232</a></sup> beside<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> waters,<sup><a href="h4325.htm">4325</a></sup> that send forth<sup><a href="h7971.htm">7971</a></sup> thither the feet<sup><a href="h7272.htm">7272</a></sup> of the ox<sup><a href="h7794.htm">7794</a></sup> and the ass.<sup><a href="h2543.htm">2543</a></sup> </p>

 <p align="center"><a href="../sep/isa032.htm">Sep</a></p><p>1 ἰδοὺ γὰρ βασιλεὺς δίκαιος βασιλεύσει καὶ ἄρχοντες μετὰ κρίσεως ἄρξουσιν </p>
 <p>2 καὶ ἔσται ὁ ἄνθρωπος κρύπτων τοὺς λόγους αὐτοῦ καὶ κρυβήσεται ὡς ἀφ᾽ ὕδατος φερομένου καὶ φανήσεται ἐν Σιων ὡς ποταμὸς φερόμενος ἔνδοξος ἐν γῇ διψώσῃ </p>
 <p>3 καὶ οὐκέτι ἔσονται πεποιθότες ἐπ᾽ ἀνθρώποις ἀλλὰ τὰ ὦτα δώσουσιν ἀκούειν </p>
 <p>4 καὶ ἡ καρδία τῶν ἀσθενούντων προσέξει τοῦ ἀκούειν καὶ αἱ γλῶσσαι αἱ ψελλίζουσαι ταχὺ μαθήσονται λαλεῖν εἰρήνην </p>
 <p>5 καὶ οὐκέτι μὴ εἴπωσιν τῷ μωρῷ ἄρχειν καὶ οὐκέτι μὴ εἴπωσιν οἱ ὑπηρέται σου σίγα </p>
 <p>6 ὁ γὰρ μωρὸς μωρὰ λαλήσει καὶ ἡ καρδία αὐτοῦ μάταια νοήσει τοῦ συντελεῖν ἄνομα καὶ λαλεῖν πρὸς κύριον πλάνησιν τοῦ διασπεῖραι ψυχὰς πεινώσας καὶ τὰς ψυχὰς τὰς διψώσας κενὰς ποιῆσαι </p>
 <p>7 ἡ γὰρ βουλὴ τῶν πονηρῶν ἄνομα βουλεύσεται καταφθεῖραι ταπεινοὺς ἐν λόγοις ἀδίκοις καὶ διασκεδάσαι λόγους ταπεινῶν ἐν κρίσει </p>
 <p>8 οἱ δὲ εὐσεβεῖς συνετὰ ἐβουλεύσαντο καὶ αὕτη ἡ βουλὴ μενεῖ </p>
 <p>9 γυναῖκες πλούσιαι ἀνάστητε καὶ ἀκούσατε τῆς φωνῆς μου θυγατέρες ἐν ἐλπίδι ἀκούσατε τοὺς λόγους μου </p>
 <p>10 ἡμέρας ἐνιαυτοῦ μνείαν ποιήσασθε ἐν ὀδύνῃ μετ᾽ ἐλπίδος ἀνήλωται ὁ τρύγητος πέπαυται ὁ σπόρος καὶ οὐκέτι μὴ ἔλθῃ </p>
 <p>11 ἔκστητε λυπήθητε αἱ πεποιθυῖαι ἐκδύσασθε γυμναὶ γένεσθε περιζώσασθε σάκκους τὰς ὀσφύας </p>
 <p>12 καὶ ἐπὶ τῶν μαστῶν κόπτεσθε ἀπὸ ἀγροῦ ἐπιθυμήματος καὶ ἀμπέλου γενήματος </p>
 <p>13 ἡ γῆ τοῦ λαοῦ μου ἄκανθα καὶ χόρτος ἀναβήσεται καὶ ἐκ πάσης οἰκίας εὐφροσύνη ἀρθήσεται πόλις πλουσία </p>
 <p>14 οἶκοι ἐγκαταλελειμμένοι πλοῦτον πόλεως καὶ οἴκους ἐπιθυμητοὺς ἀφήσουσιν καὶ ἔσονται αἱ κῶμαι σπήλαια ἕως τοῦ αἰῶνος εὐφροσύνη ὄνων ἀγρίων βοσκήματα ποιμένων </p>
 <p>15 ἕως ἂν ἐπέλθῃ ἐφ᾽ ὑμᾶς πνεῦμα ἀφ᾽ ὑψηλοῦ καὶ ἔσται ἔρημος ὁ Χερμελ καὶ ὁ Χερμελ εἰς δρυμὸν λογισθήσεται </p>
 <p>16 καὶ ἀναπαύσεται ἐν τῇ ἐρήμῳ κρίμα καὶ δικαιοσύνη ἐν τῷ Καρμήλῳ κατοικήσει </p>
 <p>17 καὶ ἔσται τὰ ἔργα τῆς δικαιοσύνης εἰρήνη καὶ κρατήσει ἡ δικαιοσύνη ἀνάπαυσιν καὶ πεποιθότες ἕως τοῦ αἰῶνος </p>
 <p>18 καὶ κατοικήσει ὁ λαὸς αὐτοῦ ἐν πόλει εἰρήνης καὶ ἐνοικήσει πεποιθώς καὶ ἀναπαύσονται μετὰ πλούτου </p>
 <p>19 ἡ δὲ χάλαζα ἐὰν καταβῇ οὐκ ἐφ᾽ ὑμᾶς ἥξει καὶ ἔσονται οἱ ἐνοικοῦντες ἐν τοῖς δρυμοῖς πεποιθότες ὡς οἱ ἐν τῇ πεδινῇ </p>
 <p>20 μακάριοι οἱ σπείροντες ἐπὶ πᾶν ὕδωρ οὗ βοῦς καὶ ὄνος πατεῖ </p>

 <p align="center"><a href="../vul/isa032.htm">Vul</a></p><p>1 [Ecce in justitia regnabit rex,<br> et principes in judicio præerunt.<br></p>
 <p>2 Et erit vir sicut qui absconditur a vento,<br> et celat se a tempestate;<br> sicut rivi aquarum in siti,<br> et umbra petræ prominentis in terra deserta.<br></p>
 <p>3 Non caligabunt oculi videntium,<br> et aures audientium diligenter auscultabunt.<br></p>
 <p>4 Et cor stultorum intelliget scientiam,<br> et lingua balborum velociter loquetur et plane.<br></p>
 <p>5 Non vocabitur ultra is qui insipiens est, princeps,<br> neque fraudulentus appellabitur major;<br></p>
 <p>6 stultus enim fatua loquetur,<br> et cor ejus faciet iniquitatem,<br> ut perficiat simulationem,<br> et loquatur ad Dominum fraudulenter,<br> et vacuam faciat animam esurientis,<br> et potum sitienti auferat.<br></p>
 <p>7 Fraudulenti vasa pessima sunt;<br> ipse enim cogitationes concinnavit<br> ad perdendos mites in sermone mendacii,<br> cum loqueretur pauper judicium.<br></p>
 <p>8 Princeps vero ea quæ digna sunt principe cogitabit,<br> et ipse super duces stabit.]<br>
 </p>
 <p>9 [Mulieres opulentæ, surgite,<br> et audite vocem meam;<br> filiæ confidentes,<br> percipite auribus eloquium meum.<br></p>
 <p>10 Post dies enim et annum,<br> vos conturbabimini confidentes;<br> consummata est enim vindemia,<br> collectio ultra non veniet.<br></p>
 <p>11 Obstupescite, opulentæ;<br> conturbamini, confidentes:<br> exuite vos et confundimini;<br> accingite lumbos vestros.<br></p>
 <p>12 Super ubera plangite,<br> super regione desiderabili,<br> super vinea fertili.<br></p>
 <p>13 Super humum populi mei<br> spinæ et vepres ascendent:<br> quanto magis super omnes domos gaudii<br> civitatis exultantis!<br></p>
 <p>14 Domus enim dimissa est,<br> multitudo urbis relicta est,<br> tenebræ et palpatio<br> factæ sunt super speluncas usque in æternum;<br> gaudium onagrorum,<br> pascua gregum.<br></p>
 <p>15 Donec effundatur super nos<br> spiritus de excelso,<br> et erit desertum in carmel,<br> et carmel in saltum reputabitur.<br></p>
 <p>16 Et habitabit in solitudine judicium,<br> et justitia in carmel sedebit.<br></p>
 <p>17 Et erit opus justitiæ pax,<br> et cultus justitiæ silentium,<br> et securitas usque in sempiternum.<br></p>
 <p>18 Et sedebit populus meus in pulchritudine pacis,<br> et in tabernaculis fiduciæ,<br> et in requie opulenta.<br></p>
 <p>19 Grando autem in descensione saltus,<br> et humilitate humiliabitur civitas.<br></p>
 <p>20 Beati qui seminatis super omnes aquas,<br> immittentes pedem bovis et asini.]</p>
