 <p>2  Suffer<sup><a href="h3803.htm">3803</a></sup> me a little,<sup><a href="h2191.htm">2191</a></sup> and I will show<sup><a href="h2331.htm">2331</a></sup> thee that<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> I have yet<sup><a href="h5750.htm">5750</a></sup> to speak<sup><a href="h4405.htm">4405</a></sup> on God's<sup><a href="h0433.htm">433</a></sup> behalf. </p>
 <p>3  I will fetch<sup><a href="h5375.htm">5375</a></sup> my knowledge<sup><a href="h1843.htm">1843</a></sup> from afar,<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h7350.htm">7350</a></sup> and will ascribe<sup><a href="h5414.htm">5414</a></sup> righteousness<sup><a href="h6664.htm">6664</a></sup> to my Maker.<sup><a href="h6466.htm">6466</a></sup> </p>

 <p>5  Behold,<sup><a href="h2005.htm">2005</a></sup> God<sup><a href="h0410.htm">410</a></sup> is mighty,<sup><a href="h3524.htm">3524</a></sup> and despiseth<sup><a href="h3988.htm">3988</a></sup> not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> any: he is mighty<sup><a href="h3524.htm">3524</a></sup> in strength<sup><a href="h3581.htm">3581</a></sup> and wisdom.<sup><a href="h3820.htm">3820</a></sup> </p>
 <p>6  He preserveth not the life<sup><a href="h2421.htm">2421</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> of the wicked:<sup><a href="h7563.htm">7563</a></sup> but giveth<sup><a href="h5414.htm">5414</a></sup> right<sup><a href="h4941.htm">4941</a></sup> to the poor.<sup><a href="h6041.htm">6041</a></sup> </p>
 <p>7  He withdraweth<sup><a href="h1639.htm">1639</a></sup> not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> his eyes<sup><a href="h5869.htm">5869</a></sup> from the righteous:<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h6662.htm">6662</a></sup> but with<sup><a href="h0854.htm">854</a></sup> kings<sup><a href="h4428.htm">4428</a></sup> are they on the throne;<sup><a href="h3678.htm">3678</a></sup> yea, he doth establish<sup><a href="h3427.htm">3427</a></sup> them forever,<sup><a href="h5331.htm">5331</a></sup> and they are exalted.<sup><a href="h1361.htm">1361</a></sup> </p>
 <p>8  And if<sup><a href="h0518.htm">518</a></sup> they be bound<sup><a href="h0631.htm">631</a></sup> in fetters,<sup><a href="h2131.htm">2131</a></sup> and be holden<sup><a href="h3920.htm">3920</a></sup> in cords<sup><a href="h2256.htm">2256</a></sup> of affliction;<sup><a href="h6040.htm">6040</a></sup> </p>
 <p>9  Then he showeth<sup><a href="h5046.htm">5046</a></sup> them their work,<sup><a href="h6467.htm">6467</a></sup> and their transgressions<sup><a href="h6588.htm">6588</a></sup> that<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> they have exceeded.<sup><a href="h1396.htm">1396</a></sup> </p>
 <p>10  He openeth<sup><a href="h1540.htm">1540</a></sup> also their ear<sup><a href="h0241.htm">241</a></sup> to discipline,<sup><a href="h4148.htm">4148</a></sup> and commandeth<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> that<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> they return<sup><a href="h7725.htm">7725</a></sup> from iniquity.<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0205.htm">205</a></sup> </p>
 <p>11  If<sup><a href="h0518.htm">518</a></sup> they obey<sup><a href="h8085.htm">8085</a></sup> and serve<sup><a href="h5647.htm">5647</a></sup> him, they shall spend<sup><a href="h3615.htm">3615</a></sup> their days<sup><a href="h3117.htm">3117</a></sup> in prosperity,<sup><a href="h2896.htm">2896</a></sup> and their years<sup><a href="h8141.htm">8141</a></sup> in pleasures.<sup><a href="h5273.htm">5273</a></sup> </p>
 <p>12  But if<sup><a href="h0518.htm">518</a></sup> they obey<sup><a href="h8085.htm">8085</a></sup> not,<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> they shall perish<sup><a href="h5674.htm">5674</a></sup> by the sword,<sup><a href="h7973.htm">7973</a></sup> and they shall die<sup><a href="h1478.htm">1478</a></sup> without<sup><a href="h1097.htm">1097</a></sup> knowledge.<sup><a href="h1847.htm">1847</a></sup> </p>
 <p>13  But the hypocrites<sup><a href="h2611.htm">2611</a></sup> in heart<sup><a href="h3820.htm">3820</a></sup> heap up<sup><a href="h7760.htm">7760</a></sup> wrath:<sup><a href="h0639.htm">639</a></sup> they cry<sup><a href="h7768.htm">7768</a></sup> not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> when<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> he bindeth<sup><a href="h0631.htm">631</a></sup> them. </p>
 <p>14  They<sup><a href="h5315.htm">5315</a></sup> die<sup><a href="h4191.htm">4191</a></sup> in youth,<sup><a href="h5290.htm">5290</a></sup> and their life<sup><a href="h2416.htm">2416</a></sup> is among the unclean.<sup><a href="h6945.htm">6945</a></sup> </p>
 <p>15  He delivereth<sup><a href="h2502.htm">2502</a></sup> the poor<sup><a href="h6041.htm">6041</a></sup> in his affliction,<sup><a href="h6040.htm">6040</a></sup> and openeth<sup><a href="h1540.htm">1540</a></sup> their ears<sup><a href="h0241.htm">241</a></sup> in oppression.<sup><a href="h3906.htm">3906</a></sup> </p>
 <p>16  Even so<sup><a href="h0637.htm">637</a></sup> would he have removed<sup><a href="h5496.htm">5496</a></sup> thee out of the strait<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h6310.htm">6310</a></sup> <sup><a href="h6862.htm">6862</a></sup> into a broad place,<sup><a href="h7338.htm">7338</a></sup> where<sup><a href="h8478.htm">8478</a></sup> there is no<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> straitness;<sup><a href="h4164.htm">4164</a></sup> and that which should be set<sup><a href="h5183.htm">5183</a></sup> on thy table<sup><a href="h7979.htm">7979</a></sup> should be full<sup><a href="h4390.htm">4390</a></sup> of fatness.<sup><a href="h1880.htm">1880</a></sup> </p>
 <p>17  But thou hast fulfilled<sup><a href="h4390.htm">4390</a></sup> the judgment<sup><a href="h1779.htm">1779</a></sup> of the wicked:<sup><a href="h7563.htm">7563</a></sup> judgment<sup><a href="h1779.htm">1779</a></sup> and justice<sup><a href="h4941.htm">4941</a></sup> take hold<sup><a href="h8551.htm">8551</a></sup> on thee. </p>
 <p>18  Because<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> there is wrath,<sup><a href="h2534.htm">2534</a></sup> beware lest<sup><a href="h6435.htm">6435</a></sup> he take thee away<sup><a href="h5496.htm">5496</a></sup> with his stroke:<sup><a href="h5607.htm">5607</a></sup> then a great<sup><a href="h7227.htm">7227</a></sup> ransom<sup><a href="h3724.htm">3724</a></sup> cannot<sup><a href="h0408.htm">408</a></sup> deliver<sup><a href="h5186.htm">5186</a></sup> thee. </p>
 <p>19  Will he esteem<sup><a href="h6186.htm">6186</a></sup> thy riches?<sup><a href="h7769.htm">7769</a></sup> no, not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> gold,<sup><a href="h1222.htm">1222</a></sup> nor all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> the forces<sup><a href="h3981.htm">3981</a></sup> of strength.<sup><a href="h3581.htm">3581</a></sup> </p>
 <p>20  Desire<sup><a href="h7602.htm">7602</a></sup> not<sup><a href="h0408.htm">408</a></sup> the night,<sup><a href="h3915.htm">3915</a></sup> when people<sup><a href="h5971.htm">5971</a></sup> are cut off<sup><a href="h5927.htm">5927</a></sup> in their place.<sup><a href="h8478.htm">8478</a></sup> </p>
 <p>21  Take heed,<sup><a href="h8104.htm">8104</a></sup> regard not<sup><a href="h6437.htm">6437</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0408.htm">408</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> iniquity:<sup><a href="h0205.htm">205</a></sup> for<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> <sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> this<sup><a href="h2088.htm">2088</a></sup> hast thou chosen<sup><a href="h0977.htm">977</a></sup> rather than affliction.<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h6040.htm">6040</a></sup> </p>
 <p>22  Behold,<sup><a href="h2005.htm">2005</a></sup> God<sup><a href="h0410.htm">410</a></sup> exalteth<sup><a href="h7682.htm">7682</a></sup> by his power:<sup><a href="h3581.htm">3581</a></sup> who<sup><a href="h4310.htm">4310</a></sup> teacheth<sup><a href="h3384.htm">3384</a></sup> like him?<sup><a href="h3644.htm">3644</a></sup> </p>
 <p>23  Who<sup><a href="h4310.htm">4310</a></sup> hath enjoined<sup><a href="h6485.htm">6485</a></sup> <sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> him his way?<sup><a href="h1870.htm">1870</a></sup> or who<sup><a href="h4310.htm">4310</a></sup> can say,<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> Thou hast wrought<sup><a href="h6466.htm">6466</a></sup> iniquity?<sup><a href="h5766.htm">5766</a></sup> </p>
 <p>24  Remember<sup><a href="h2142.htm">2142</a></sup> that<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> thou magnify<sup><a href="h7679.htm">7679</a></sup> his work,<sup><a href="h6467.htm">6467</a></sup> which<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> men<sup><a href="h0376.htm">376</a></sup> behold.<sup><a href="h7789.htm">7789</a></sup> </p>
 <p>25  Every<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> man<sup><a href="h0120.htm">120</a></sup> may see<sup><a href="h2372.htm">2372</a></sup> it; man<sup><a href="h0582.htm">582</a></sup> may behold<sup><a href="h5027.htm">5027</a></sup> it afar off.<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h7350.htm">7350</a></sup> </p>
 <p>26  Behold,<sup><a href="h2005.htm">2005</a></sup> God<sup><a href="h0410.htm">410</a></sup> is great,<sup><a href="h7689.htm">7689</a></sup> and we know<sup><a href="h3045.htm">3045</a></sup> him not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> neither<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> can the number<sup><a href="h4557.htm">4557</a></sup> of his years<sup><a href="h8141.htm">8141</a></sup> be searched out.<sup><a href="h2714.htm">2714</a></sup> </p>
 <p>27  For<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> he maketh small<sup><a href="h1639.htm">1639</a></sup> the drops<sup><a href="h5198.htm">5198</a></sup> of water:<sup><a href="h4325.htm">4325</a></sup> they pour down<sup><a href="h2212.htm">2212</a></sup> rain<sup><a href="h4306.htm">4306</a></sup> according to the vapor<sup><a href="h0108.htm">108</a></sup> thereof: </p>
 <p>28  Which<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> the clouds<sup><a href="h7834.htm">7834</a></sup> do drop<sup><a href="h5140.htm">5140</a></sup> and distill<sup><a href="h7491.htm">7491</a></sup> upon<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> man<sup><a href="h0120.htm">120</a></sup> abundantly.<sup><a href="h7227.htm">7227</a></sup> </p>
 <p>29  Also<sup><a href="h0637.htm">637</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0518.htm">518</a></sup> can any understand<sup><a href="h0995.htm">995</a></sup> the spreadings<sup><a href="h4666.htm">4666</a></sup> of the clouds,<sup><a href="h5645.htm">5645</a></sup> or the noise<sup><a href="h8663.htm">8663</a></sup> of his tabernacle?<sup><a href="h5521.htm">5521</a></sup> </p>
 <p>30  Behold,<sup><a href="h2005.htm">2005</a></sup> he spreadeth<sup><a href="h6566.htm">6566</a></sup> his light<sup><a href="h0216.htm">216</a></sup> upon<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> it, and covereth<sup><a href="h3680.htm">3680</a></sup> the bottom<sup><a href="h8328.htm">8328</a></sup> of the sea.<sup><a href="h3220.htm">3220</a></sup> </p>
 <p>31  For<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> by them judgeth<sup><a href="h1777.htm">1777</a></sup> he the people;<sup><a href="h5971.htm">5971</a></sup> he giveth<sup><a href="h5414.htm">5414</a></sup> meat<sup><a href="h0400.htm">400</a></sup> in abundance.<sup><a href="h4342.htm">4342</a></sup> </p>
 <p>32  With<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> clouds<sup><a href="h3709.htm">3709</a></sup> he covereth<sup><a href="h3680.htm">3680</a></sup> the light;<sup><a href="h0216.htm">216</a></sup> and commandeth<sup><a href="h6680.htm">6680</a></sup> it not to shine by<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> the cloud that cometh between.<sup><a href="h6293.htm">6293</a></sup> </p>
 <p>33  The noise<sup><a href="h7452.htm">7452</a></sup> thereof showeth<sup><a href="h5046.htm">5046</a></sup> concerning<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> it, the cattle<sup><a href="h4735.htm">4735</a></sup> also<sup><a href="h0637.htm">637</a></sup> concerning<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> the vapor.<sup><a href="h5927.htm">5927</a></sup> </p>

 <p>12 ἀσεβεῖς δὲ οὐ διασῴζει παρὰ τὸ μὴ βούλεσθαι εἰδέναι αὐτοὺς τὸν κύριον καὶ διότι νουθετούμενοι ἀνήκοοι ἦσαν </p>

 <p>15 ἀνθ᾽ ὧν ἔθλιψαν ἀσθενῆ καὶ ἀδύνατον κρίμα δὲ πραέων ἐκθήσει </p>
 <p>16 καὶ προσέτι ἠπάτησέν σε ἐκ στόματος ἐχθροῦ ἄβυσσος κατάχυσις ὑποκάτω αὐτῆς καὶ κατέβη τράπεζά σου πλήρης πιότητος </p>
 <p>17 οὐχ ὑστερήσει δὲ ἀπὸ δικαίων κρίμα </p>

 <p>24 μνήσθητι ὅτι μεγάλα ἐστὶν αὐτοῦ τὰ ἔργα ὧν ἦρξαν ἄνδρες </p>
 <p>25 πᾶς ἄνθρωπος εἶδεν ἐν ἑαυτῷ ὅσοι τιτρωσκόμενοί εἰσιν βροτοί </p>
 <p>26 ἰδοὺ ὁ ἰσχυρὸς πολύς καὶ οὐ γνωσόμεθα ἀριθμὸς ἐτῶν αὐτοῦ καὶ ἀπέραντος </p>

 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎4 ‏כִּֽי־אָ֭מְנָם לֹא־שֶׁ֣קֶר מִלָּ֑י תְּמִ֖ים דֵּע֣וֹת עִמָּֽךְ׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎5 ‏הֶן־אֵ֣ל כַּ֭בִּיר וְלֹ֣א יִמְאָ֑ס כַּ֝בִּ֗יר כֹּ֣חַֽ לֵֽב׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎6 ‏לֹא־יְחַיֶּ֥ה רָשָׁ֑ע וּמִשְׁפַּ֖ט עֲנִיִּ֣ים יִתֵּֽן׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎7 ‏לֹֽא־יִגְרַ֥ע מִצַּדִּ֗יק עֵ֫ינָ֥יו וְאֶת־מְלָכִ֥ים לַכִּסֵּ֑א וַיֹּשִׁיבֵ֥ם לָ֝נֶ֗צַח וַיִּגְבָּֽהוּ׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎8 ‏וְאִם־אֲסוּרִ֥ים בַּזִּקִּ֑ים יִ֝לָּכְד֗וּן בְּחַבְלֵי־עֹֽנִי׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎9 ‏וַיַּגֵּ֣ד לָהֶ֣ם פָּעֳלָ֑ם וּ֝פִשְׁעֵיהֶ֗ם כִּ֣י יִתְגַּבָּֽרוּ׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎10 ‏וַיִּ֣גֶל אָ֭זְנָם לַמּוּסָ֑ר וַ֝יֹּ֗אמֶר כִּֽי־יְשֻׁב֥וּן מֵאָֽוֶן׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎11 ‏אִֽם־יִשְׁמְע֗וּ וְֽיַ֫עֲבֹ֥דוּ יְכַלּ֣וּ יְמֵיהֶ֣ם בַּטּ֑וֹב וּ֝שְׁנֵיהֶ֗ם בַּנְּעִימִֽים׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎12 ‏וְאִם־לֹ֣א יִ֭שְׁמְעוּ בְּשֶׁ֣לַח יַעֲבֹ֑רוּ וְ֝יִגְוְע֗וּ כִּבְלִי־דָֽעַת׃<font color="red"><sup>1</sup></font> </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎13 ‏וְֽחַנְפֵי־לֵ֭ב יָשִׂ֣ימוּ אָ֑ף לֹ֥א יְ֝שַׁוְּע֗וּ כִּ֣י אֲסָרָֽם׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎14 ‏תָּמֹ֣ת בַּנֹּ֣עַר נַפְשָׁ֑ם וְ֝חַיָּתָ֗ם בַּקְּדֵשִֽׁים׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎15 ‏יְחַלֵּ֣ץ עָנִ֣י בְעָנְי֑וֹ וְיִ֖גֶל בַּלַּ֣חַץ אָזְנָֽם׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎16 ‏וְאַ֤ף הֲסִיתְךָ֨׀<font color="red"><sup>3</sup></font> מִפִּי־צָ֗ר רַ֭חַב לֹא־מוּצָ֣ק תַּחְתֶּ֑יהָ וְנַ֥חַת שֻׁ֝לְחָנְךָ֗ מָ֣לֵא דָֽשֶׁן׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎17 ‏וְדִין־רָשָׁ֥ע מָלֵ֑אתָ דִּ֖ין וּמִשְׁפָּ֣ט יִתְמֹֽכוּ׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎18 ‏כִּֽי־חֵ֭מָה פֶּן־יְסִֽיתְךָ֣ בְסָ֑פֶק וְרָב־כֹּ֝֗פֶר אַל־יַטֶּֽךָּ׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎19 ‏הֲיַעֲרֹ֣ךְ שׁ֖וּעֲךָ לֹ֣א בְצָ֑ר וְ֝כֹ֗ל מַאֲמַצֵּי־כֹֽחַ׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎20 ‏אַל־תִּשְׁאַ֥ף הַלָּ֑יְלָה לַעֲל֖וֹת עַמִּ֣ים תַּחְתָּֽם׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎21 ‏הִ֭שָּׁמֶר אַל־תֵּ֣פֶן אֶל־אָ֑וֶן כִּֽי־עַל־זֶ֝֗ה<font color="red"><sup>3</sup></font> בָּחַ֥רְתָּ מֵעֹֽנִי׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎22 ‏הֶן־אֵ֭ל יַשְׂגִּ֣יב בְּכֹח֑וֹ מִ֖י כָמֹ֣הוּ מוֹרֶֽה׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎23 ‏מִֽי־פָקַ֣ד עָלָ֣יו דַּרְכּ֑וֹ וּמִֽי־אָ֝מַ֗ר פָּעַ֥לְתָּ עַוְלָֽה׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎24 ‏זְ֭כֹר כִּֽי־תַשְׂגִּ֣יא פָעֳל֑וֹ אֲשֶׁ֖ר שֹׁרְר֣וּ אֲנָשִֽׁים׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎25 ‏כָּל־אָדָ֥ם חָֽזוּ־ב֑וֹ אֱ֝נ֗וֹשׁ יַבִּ֥יט מֵרָחֽוֹק׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎26 ‏הֶן־אֵ֣ל שַׂ֭גִּיא וְלֹ֣א נֵדָ֑ע מִסְפַּ֖ר שָׁנָ֣יו וְלֹא־חֵֽקֶר׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎27 ‏כִּ֭י יְגָרַ֣ע נִטְפֵי־מָ֑יִם יָזֹ֖קּוּ מָטָ֣ר לְאֵדֽוֹ׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎28 ‏אֲשֶֽׁר־יִזְּל֥וּ שְׁחָקִ֑ים יִ֝רְעֲפ֗וּ עֲלֵ֤י׀ אָדָ֬ם רָֽב׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎29 ‏אַ֣ף אִם־יָ֭בִין מִפְרְשֵׂי־עָ֑ב תְּ֝שֻׁא֗וֹת סֻכָּתֽוֹ׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎30 ‏הֵן־פָּרַ֣שׂ עָלָ֣יו אוֹר֑וֹ וְשָׁרְשֵׁ֖י הַיָּ֣ם כִּסָּֽה׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎31 ‏כִּי־בָ֭ם יָדִ֣ין עַמִּ֑ים יִֽתֶּן־אֹ֥כֶל לְמַכְבִּֽיר׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎32 ‏עַל־כַּפַּ֥יִם כִּסָּה־א֑וֹר וַיְצַ֖ו עָלֶ֣יהָ בְמַפְגִּֽיעַ׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎33 ‏יַגִּ֣יד עָלָ֣יו רֵע֑וֹ מִ֝קְנֶ֗ה אַ֣ף עַל־עוֹלֶֽה׃ </span></p>

 <p align="center"><a href="../vul/job036.htm">Vul</a></p><p>1 Addens quoque Eliu, hæc locutus est:</p>
 <p>2 [Sustine me paululum, et indicabo tibi:<br> adhuc enim habeo quod pro Deo loquar.<br></p>
 <p>3 Repetam scientiam meam a principio,<br> et operatorem meum probabo justum.<br></p>
 <p>4 Vere enim absque mendacio sermones mei,<br> et perfecta scientia probabitur tibi.<br></p>
 <p>5 Deus potentes non abjicit,<br> cum et ipse sit potens:<br></p>
 <p>6 sed non salvat impios,<br> et judicium pauperibus tribuit.<br></p>
 <p>7 Non auferet a justo oculos suos:<br> et reges in solio collocat in perpetuum,<br> et illi eriguntur.<br></p>
 <p>8 Et si fuerint in catenis,<br> et vinciantur funibus paupertatis,<br></p>
 <p>9 indicabit eis opera eorum,<br> et scelera eorum, quia violenti fuerunt.<br></p>
 <p>10 Revelabit quoque aurem eorum, ut corripiat:<br> et loquetur, ut revertantur ab iniquitate.<br></p>
 <p>11 Si audierint et observaverint, complebunt dies suos in bono,<br> et annos suos in gloria:<br></p>
 <p>12 si autem non audierint,<br> transibunt per gladium,<br> et consumentur in stultitia.<br></p>
 <p>13 Simulatores et callidi provocant iram Dei,<br> neque clamabunt cum vincti fuerint.<br></p>
 <p>14 Morietur in tempestate anima eorum,<br> et vita eorum inter effeminatos.<br></p>
 <p>15 Eripiet de angustia sua pauperem,<br> et revelabit in tribulatione aurem ejus.<br></p>
 <p>16 Igitur salvabit te de ore angusto latissime,<br> et non habente fundamentum subter se:<br> requies autem mensæ tuæ erit plena pinguedine.<br></p>
 <p>17 Causa tua quasi impii judicata est:<br> causam judiciumque recipies.<br></p>
 <p>18 Non te ergo superet ira ut aliquem opprimas:<br> nec multitudo donorum inclinet te.<br></p>
 <p>19 Depone magnitudinem tuam absque tribulatione,<br> et omnes robustos fortitudine.<br></p>
 <p>20 Ne protrahas noctem,<br> ut ascendant populi pro eis.<br></p>
 <p>21 Cave ne declines ad iniquitatem:<br> hanc enim cpisti sequi post miseriam.<br></p>
 <p>22 Ecce Deus excelsus in fortitudine sua,<br> et nullus ei similis in legislatoribus.<br></p>
 <p>23 Quis poterit scrutari vias ejus?<br> aut quis potest ei dicere: Operatus es iniquitatem?<br></p>
 <p>24 Memento quod ignores opus ejus,<br> de quo cecinerunt viri.<br></p>
 <p>25 Omnes homines vident eum:<br> unusquisque intuetur procul.<br></p>
 <p>26 Ecce Deus magnus vincens scientiam nostram:<br> numerus annorum ejus inæstimabilis.<br></p>
 <p>27 Qui aufert stillas pluviæ,<br> et effundit imbres ad instar gurgitum,<br></p>
 <p>28 qui de nubibus fluunt<br> quæ prætexunt cuncta desuper.<br></p>
 <p>29 Si voluerit extendere nubes quasi tentorium suum,<br></p>
 <p>30 et fulgurare lumine suo desuper,<br> cardines quoque maris operiet.<br></p>
 <p>31 Per hæc enim judicat populos,<br> et dat escas multis mortalibus.<br></p>
 <p>32 In manibus abscondit lucem,<br> et præcepit ei ut rursus adveniat.<br></p>
 <p>33 Annuntiat de ea amico suo, quod possessio ejus sit,<br> et ad eam possit ascendere.]</p>
