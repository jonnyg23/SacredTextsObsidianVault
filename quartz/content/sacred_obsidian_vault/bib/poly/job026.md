 <p>8  He bindeth up<sup><a href="h6887.htm">6887</a></sup> the waters<sup><a href="h4325.htm">4325</a></sup> in his thick clouds;<sup><a href="h5645.htm">5645</a></sup> and the cloud<sup><a href="h6051.htm">6051</a></sup> is not rent<sup><a href="h1234.htm">1234</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> under<sup><a href="h8478.htm">8478</a></sup> them. </p>

 <p>13  By his spirit<sup><a href="h7307.htm">7307</a></sup> he hath garnished<sup><a href="h8235.htm">8235</a></sup> the heavens;<sup><a href="h8064.htm">8064</a></sup> his hand<sup><a href="h3027.htm">3027</a></sup> hath formed<sup><a href="h2490.htm">2490</a></sup> the crooked<sup><a href="h1281.htm">1281</a></sup> serpent.<sup><a href="h5175.htm">5175</a></sup> </p>
 <p>14  Lo,<sup><a href="h2005.htm">2005</a></sup> these<sup><a href="h0428.htm">428</a></sup> are parts<sup><a href="h7098.htm">7098</a></sup> of his ways:<sup><a href="h1870.htm">1870</a></sup> but how<sup><a href="h4100.htm">4100</a></sup> little<sup><a href="h8102.htm">8102</a></sup> a portion<sup><a href="h1697.htm">1697</a></sup> is heard<sup><a href="h8085.htm">8085</a></sup> of him? but the thunder<sup><a href="h7482.htm">7482</a></sup> of his power<sup><a href="h1369.htm">1369</a></sup> who<sup><a href="h4310.htm">4310</a></sup> can understand?<sup><a href="h0995.htm">995</a></sup> </p>
