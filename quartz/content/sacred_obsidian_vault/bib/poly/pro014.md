 <p>13  Even<sup><a href="h1571.htm">1571</a></sup> in laughter<sup><a href="h7814.htm">7814</a></sup> the heart<sup><a href="h3820.htm">3820</a></sup> is sorrowful;<sup><a href="h3510.htm">3510</a></sup> and the end<sup><a href="h0319.htm">319</a></sup> of that mirth<sup><a href="h8057.htm">8057</a></sup> is heaviness.<sup><a href="h8424.htm">8424</a></sup> </p>

 <p>15  The simple<sup><a href="h6612.htm">6612</a></sup> believeth<sup><a href="h0539.htm">539</a></sup> every<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> word:<sup><a href="h1697.htm">1697</a></sup> but the prudent<sup><a href="h6175.htm">6175</a></sup> man looketh well<sup><a href="h0995.htm">995</a></sup> to his going.<sup><a href="h0838.htm">838</a></sup> </p>
 <p>16  A wise<sup><a href="h2450.htm">2450</a></sup> man feareth,<sup><a href="h3373.htm">3373</a></sup> and departeth<sup><a href="h5493.htm">5493</a></sup> from evil:<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h7451.htm">7451</a></sup> but the fool<sup><a href="h3684.htm">3684</a></sup> rageth,<sup><a href="h5674.htm">5674</a></sup> and is confident.<sup><a href="h0982.htm">982</a></sup> </p>
 <p>17  He that is soon<sup><a href="h7116.htm">7116</a></sup> angry<sup><a href="h0639.htm">639</a></sup> dealeth<sup><a href="h6213.htm">6213</a></sup> foolishly:<sup><a href="h0200.htm">200</a></sup> and a man<sup><a href="h0376.htm">376</a></sup> of wicked devices<sup><a href="h4209.htm">4209</a></sup> is hated.<sup><a href="h8130.htm">8130</a></sup> </p>
 <p>18  The simple<sup><a href="h6612.htm">6612</a></sup> inherit<sup><a href="h5157.htm">5157</a></sup> folly:<sup><a href="h0200.htm">200</a></sup> but the prudent<sup><a href="h6175.htm">6175</a></sup> are crowned<sup><a href="h3803.htm">3803</a></sup> with knowledge.<sup><a href="h1847.htm">1847</a></sup> </p>

 <p>20  The poor<sup><a href="h7326.htm">7326</a></sup> is hated<sup><a href="h8130.htm">8130</a></sup> even<sup><a href="h1571.htm">1571</a></sup> of his own neighbor:<sup><a href="h7453.htm">7453</a></sup> but the rich<sup><a href="h6223.htm">6223</a></sup> hath many<sup><a href="h7227.htm">7227</a></sup> friends.<sup><a href="h0157.htm">157</a></sup> </p>

 <p>24  The crown<sup><a href="h5850.htm">5850</a></sup> of the wise<sup><a href="h2450.htm">2450</a></sup> is their riches:<sup><a href="h6239.htm">6239</a></sup> but the foolishness<sup><a href="h0200.htm">200</a></sup> of fools<sup><a href="h3684.htm">3684</a></sup> is folly.<sup><a href="h0200.htm">200</a></sup> </p>
 <p>25  A true<sup><a href="h0571.htm">571</a></sup> witness<sup><a href="h5707.htm">5707</a></sup> delivereth<sup><a href="h5337.htm">5337</a></sup> souls:<sup><a href="h5315.htm">5315</a></sup> but a deceitful<sup><a href="h4820.htm">4820</a></sup> witness speaketh<sup><a href="h6315.htm">6315</a></sup> lies.<sup><a href="h3577.htm">3577</a></sup> </p>
 <p>26  In the fear<sup><a href="h3374.htm">3374</a></sup> of the LORD<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> is strong<sup><a href="h5797.htm">5797</a></sup> confidence:<sup><a href="h4009.htm">4009</a></sup> and his children<sup><a href="h1121.htm">1121</a></sup> shall have<sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> a place of refuge.<sup><a href="h4268.htm">4268</a></sup> </p>
 <p>27  The fear<sup><a href="h3374.htm">3374</a></sup> of the LORD<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> is a fountain<sup><a href="h4726.htm">4726</a></sup> of life,<sup><a href="h2416.htm">2416</a></sup> to depart<sup><a href="h5493.htm">5493</a></sup> from the snares<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h4170.htm">4170</a></sup> of death.<sup><a href="h4194.htm">4194</a></sup> </p>

 <p>30  A sound<sup><a href="h4832.htm">4832</a></sup> heart<sup><a href="h3820.htm">3820</a></sup> is the life<sup><a href="h2416.htm">2416</a></sup> of the flesh:<sup><a href="h1320.htm">1320</a></sup> but envy<sup><a href="h7068.htm">7068</a></sup> the rottenness<sup><a href="h7538.htm">7538</a></sup> of the bones.<sup><a href="h6106.htm">6106</a></sup> </p>
 <p>31  He that oppresseth<sup><a href="h6231.htm">6231</a></sup> the poor<sup><a href="h1800.htm">1800</a></sup> reproacheth<sup><a href="h2778.htm">2778</a></sup> his Maker:<sup><a href="h6213.htm">6213</a></sup> but he that honoreth<sup><a href="h3513.htm">3513</a></sup> him hath mercy<sup><a href="h2603.htm">2603</a></sup> on the poor.<sup><a href="h0034.htm">34</a></sup> </p>

 <p align="center"><a href="../sep/pro014.htm">Sep</a></p><p>1 σοφαὶ γυναῖκες ᾠκοδόμησαν οἴκους ἡ δὲ ἄφρων κατέσκαψεν ταῖς χερσὶν αὐτῆς </p>
 <p>2 ὁ πορευόμενος ὀρθῶς φοβεῖται τὸν κύριον ὁ δὲ σκολιάζων ταῖς ὁδοῖς αὐτοῦ ἀτιμασθήσεται </p>
 <p>3 ἐκ στόματος ἀφρόνων βακτηρία ὕβρεως χείλη δὲ σοφῶν φυλάσσει αὐτούς </p>
 <p>4 οὗ μή εἰσιν βόες φάτναι καθαραί οὗ δὲ πολλὰ γενήματα φανερὰ βοὸς ἰσχύς </p>
 <p>5 μάρτυς πιστὸς οὐ ψεύδεται ἐκκαίει δὲ ψεύδη μάρτυς ἄδικος </p>
 <p>6 ζητήσεις σοφίαν παρὰ κακοῖς καὶ οὐχ εὑρήσεις αἴσθησις δὲ παρὰ φρονίμοις εὐχερής </p>
 <p>7 πάντα ἐναντία ἀνδρὶ ἄφρονι ὅπλα δὲ αἰσθήσεως χείλη σοφά </p>
 <p>8 σοφία πανούργων ἐπιγνώσεται τὰς ὁδοὺς αὐτῶν ἄνοια δὲ ἀφρόνων ἐν πλάνῃ </p>
 <p>9 οἰκίαι παρανόμων ὀφειλήσουσιν καθαρισμόν οἰκίαι δὲ δικαίων δεκταί </p>
 <p>10 καρδία ἀνδρὸς αἰσθητική λυπηρὰ ψυχὴ αὐτοῦ ὅταν δὲ εὐφραίνηται οὐκ ἐπιμείγνυται ὕβρει </p>
 <p>11 οἰκίαι ἀσεβῶν ἀφανισθήσονται σκηναὶ δὲ κατορθούντων στήσονται </p>
 <p>12 ἔστιν ὁδὸς ἣ δοκεῖ ὀρθὴ εἶναι παρὰ ἀνθρώποις τὰ δὲ τελευταῖα αὐτῆς ἔρχεται εἰς πυθμένα ᾅδου </p>
 <p>13 ἐν εὐφροσύναις οὐ προσμείγνυται λύπη τελευταία δὲ χαρὰ εἰς πένθος ἔρχεται </p>
 <p>14 τῶν ἑαυτοῦ ὁδῶν πλησθήσεται θρασυκάρδιος ἀπὸ δὲ τῶν διανοημάτων αὐτοῦ ἀνὴρ ἀγαθός </p>
 <p>15 ἄκακος πιστεύει παντὶ λόγῳ πανοῦργος δὲ ἔρχεται εἰς μετάνοιαν </p>
 <p>16 σοφὸς φοβηθεὶς ἐξέκλινεν ἀπὸ κακοῦ ὁ δὲ ἄφρων ἑαυτῷ πεποιθὼς μείγνυται ἀνόμῳ </p>
 <p>17 ὀξύθυμος πράσσει μετὰ ἀβουλίας ἀνὴρ δὲ φρόνιμος πολλὰ ὑποφέρει </p>
 <p>18 μεριοῦνται ἄφρονες κακίαν οἱ δὲ πανοῦργοι κρατήσουσιν αἰσθήσεως </p>
 <p>19 ὀλισθήσουσιν κακοὶ ἔναντι ἀγαθῶν καὶ ἀσεβεῖς θεραπεύσουσιν θύρας δικαίων </p>
 <p>20 φίλοι μισήσουσιν φίλους πτωχούς φίλοι δὲ πλουσίων πολλοί </p>
 <p>21 ὁ ἀτιμάζων πένητας ἁμαρτάνει ἐλεῶν δὲ πτωχοὺς μακαριστός </p>
 <p>22 πλανώμενοι τεκταίνουσι κακά ἔλεον δὲ καὶ ἀλήθειαν τεκταίνουσιν ἀγαθοί οὐκ ἐπίστανται ἔλεον καὶ πίστιν τέκτονες κακῶν ἐλεημοσύναι δὲ καὶ πίστεις παρὰ τέκτοσιν ἀγαθοῖς </p>
 <p>23 ἐν παντὶ μεριμνῶντι ἔνεστιν περισσόν ὁ δὲ ἡδὺς καὶ ἀνάλγητος ἐν ἐνδείᾳ ἔσται </p>
 <p>24 στέφανος σοφῶν πανοῦργος ἡ δὲ διατριβὴ ἀφρόνων κακή </p>
 <p>25 ῥύσεται ἐκ κακῶν ψυχὴν μάρτυς πιστός ἐκκαίει δὲ ψεύδη δόλιος </p>
 <p>26 ἐν φόβῳ κυρίου ἐλπὶς ἰσχύος τοῖς δὲ τέκνοις αὐτοῦ καταλείπει ἔρεισμα </p>
 <p>27 πρόσταγμα κυρίου πηγὴ ζωῆς ποιεῖ δὲ ἐκκλίνειν ἐκ παγίδος θανάτου </p>
 <p>28 ἐν πολλῷ ἔθνει δόξα βασιλέως ἐν δὲ ἐκλείψει λαοῦ συντριβὴ δυνάστου </p>
 <p>29 μακρόθυμος ἀνὴρ πολὺς ἐν φρονήσει ὁ δὲ ὀλιγόψυχος ἰσχυρῶς ἄφρων </p>
 <p>30 πραΰθυμος ἀνὴρ καρδίας ἰατρός σὴς δὲ ὀστέων καρδία αἰσθητική </p>
 <p>31 ὁ συκοφαντῶν πένητα παροξύνει τὸν ποιήσαντα αὐτόν ὁ δὲ τιμῶν αὐτὸν ἐλεᾷ πτωχόν </p>
 <p>32 ἐν κακίᾳ αὐτοῦ ἀπωσθήσεται ἀσεβής ὁ δὲ πεποιθὼς τῇ ἑαυτοῦ ὁσιότητι δίκαιος </p>
 <p>33 ἐν καρδίᾳ ἀγαθῇ ἀνδρὸς σοφία ἐν δὲ καρδίᾳ ἀφρόνων οὐ διαγινώσκεται </p>
 <p>34 δικαιοσύνη ὑψοῖ ἔθνος ἐλασσονοῦσι δὲ φυλὰς ἁμαρτίαι </p>
 <p>35 δεκτὸς βασιλεῖ ὑπηρέτης νοήμων τῇ δὲ ἑαυτοῦ εὐστροφίᾳ ἀφαιρεῖται ἀτιμίαν </p>

 <p align="center"><a href="../tan/pro014.htm">Tan</a></p><p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎1 ‏חַכְמ֣וֹת נָ֭שִׁים בָּנְתָ֣ה בֵיתָ֑הּ וְ֝אִוֶּ֗לֶת בְּיָדֶ֥יהָ תֶהֶרְסֶֽנּוּ׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎2 ‏הוֹלֵ֣ךְ בְּ֭יָשְׁרוֹ יְרֵ֣א יְהוָ֑ה וּנְל֖וֹז דְּרָכָ֣יו בּוֹזֵֽהוּ׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎3 ‏בְּֽפִי־אֱ֭וִיל חֹ֣טֶר גַּאֲוָ֑ה וְשִׂפְתֵ֥י חֲ֝כָמִ֗ים תִּשְׁמוּרֵֽם׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎4 ‏בְּאֵ֣ין אֲ֭לָפִים אֵב֣וּס בָּ֑ר וְרָב־תְּ֝בוּא֗וֹת בְּכֹ֣חַ שֽׁוֹר׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎5 ‏עֵ֣ד אֱ֭מוּנִים לֹ֣א יְכַזֵּ֑ב וְיָפִ֥יחַ כְּ֝זָבִ֗ים עֵ֣ד שָֽׁקֶר׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎6 ‏בִּקֶּשׁ־לֵ֣ץ חָכְמָ֣ה וָאָ֑יִן וְדַ֖עַת לְנָב֣וֹן נָקָֽל׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎7 ‏לֵ֣ךְ מִ֭נֶּגֶד לְאִ֣ישׁ כְּסִ֑יל וּבַל־יָ֝דַ֗עְתָּ שִׂפְתֵי־דָֽעַת׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎8 ‏חָכְמַ֣ת עָ֭רוּם הָבִ֣ין דַּרְכּ֑וֹ וְאִוֶּ֖לֶת כְּסִילִ֣ים מִרְמָֽה׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎9 ‏אֱ֭וִלִים יָלִ֣יץ אָשָׁ֑ם וּבֵ֖ין יְשָׁרִ֣ים רָצֽוֹן׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎10 ‏לֵ֗ב<font color="red"><sup>3</sup></font> י֭וֹדֵעַ מָרַּ֣ת נַפְשׁ֑וֹ וּ֝בְשִׂמְחָת֗וֹ לֹא־יִתְעָ֥רַב זָֽר׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎11 ‏בֵּ֣ית רְ֭שָׁעִים יִשָּׁמֵ֑ד וְאֹ֖הֶל יְשָׁרִ֣ים יַפְרִֽיחַ׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎12 ‏יֵ֤שׁ דֶּ֣רֶךְ יָ֭שָׁר לִפְנֵי־אִ֑ישׁ וְ֝אַחֲרִיתָ֗הּ דַּרְכֵי־מָֽוֶת׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎13 ‏גַּם־בִּשְׂח֥וֹק יִכְאַב־לֵ֑ב וְאַחֲרִיתָ֖הּ<font color="red"><sup>3</sup></font> שִׂמְחָ֣ה תוּגָֽה׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎14 ‏מִדְּרָכָ֣יו יִ֭שְׂבַּע ס֣וּג לֵ֑ב וּ֝מֵעָלָ֗יו אִ֣ישׁ טֽוֹב׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎15 ‏פֶּ֭תִי יַאֲמִ֣ין לְכָל־דָּבָ֑ר וְ֝עָר֗וּם יָבִ֥ין לַאֲשֻׁרֽוֹ׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎16 ‏חָכָ֣ם יָ֭רֵא וְסָ֣ר מֵרָ֑ע וּ֝כְסִ֗יל מִתְעַבֵּ֥ר וּבוֹטֵֽחַ׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎17 ‏קְֽצַר־אַ֭פַּיִם יַעֲשֶׂ֣ה אִוֶּ֑לֶת וְאִ֥ישׁ מְ֝זִמּ֗וֹת יִשָּׂנֵֽא׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎18 ‏נָחֲל֣וּ פְתָאיִ֣ם אִוֶּ֑לֶת וַֽ֝עֲרוּמִ֗ים יַכְתִּ֥רוּ דָֽעַת׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎19 ‏שַׁח֣וּ רָ֭עִים לִפְנֵ֣י טוֹבִ֑ים וּ֝רְשָׁעִ֗ים עַֽל־שַׁעֲרֵ֥י צַדִּֽיק׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎20 ‏גַּם־לְ֭רֵעֵהוּ יִשָּׂ֣נֵא רָ֑שׁ וְאֹהֲבֵ֖י עָשִׁ֣יר רַבִּֽים׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎21 ‏בָּז־לְרֵעֵ֥הוּ חוֹטֵ֑א וּמְחוֹנֵ֖ן <font color="brown">עניים</font> <font color="blue">עֲנָוִ֣ים</font> אַשְׁרָֽיו׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎22 ‏הֲ‍ֽלוֹא־יִ֭תְעוּ חֹ֣רְשֵׁי רָ֑ע וְחֶ֥סֶד וֶ֝אֱמֶ֗ת חֹ֣רְשֵׁי טֽוֹב׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎23 ‏בְּכָל־עֶ֭צֶב יִהְיֶ֣ה מוֹתָ֑ר וּדְבַר־שְׂ֝פָתַ֗יִם אַךְ־לְמַחְסֽוֹר׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎24 ‏עֲטֶ֣רֶת חֲכָמִ֣ים עָשְׁרָ֑ם אִוֶּ֖לֶת כְּסִילִ֣ים אִוֶּֽלֶת׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎25 ‏מַצִּ֣יל נְ֭פָשׁוֹת עֵ֣ד אֱמֶ֑ת וְיָפִ֖חַ כְּזָבִ֣ים מִרְמָֽה׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎26 ‏בְּיִרְאַ֣ת יְ֭הוָה מִבְטַח־עֹ֑ז וּ֝לְבָנָ֗יו יִהְיֶ֥ה מַחְסֶֽה׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎27 ‏יִרְאַ֣ת יְ֭הוָה מְק֣וֹר חַיִּ֑ים לָ֝ס֗וּר מִמֹּ֥קְשֵׁי מָֽוֶת׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎28 ‏בְּרָב־עָ֥ם הַדְרַת־מֶ֑לֶךְ וּבְאֶ֥פֶס לְ֝אֹ֗ם מְחִתַּ֥ת רָזֽוֹן׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎29 ‏אֶ֣רֶךְ אַ֭פַּיִם רַב־תְּבוּנָ֑ה וּקְצַר־ר֝֗וּחַ מֵרִ֥ים אִוֶּֽלֶת׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎30 ‏חַיֵּ֣י בְ֭שָׂרִים לֵ֣ב מַרְפֵּ֑א וּרְקַ֖ב עֲצָמ֣וֹת קִנְאָֽה׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎31 ‏עֹ֣שֵֽׁק־דָּ֭ל<font color="red"><sup>1</sup></font> חֵרֵ֣ף עֹשֵׂ֑הוּ וּ֝מְכַבְּד֗וֹ חֹנֵ֥ן אֶבְיֽוֹן׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎32 ‏בְּֽ֭רָעָתוֹ יִדָּחֶ֣ה רָשָׁ֑ע וְחֹסֶ֖ה בְמוֹת֣וֹ צַדִּֽיק׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎33 ‏בְּלֵ֣ב נָ֭בוֹן תָּנ֣וּחַ חָכְמָ֑ה וּבְקֶ֥רֶב כְּ֝סִילִ֗ים תִּוָּדֵֽעַ׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎34 ‏צְדָקָ֥ה תְרֽוֹמֵֽם־גּ֑וֹי וְחֶ֖סֶד לְאֻמִּ֣ים חַטָּֽאת׃ </span></p>

 <p align="center"><a href="../vul/pro014.htm">Vul</a></p><p>1 [Sapiens mulier ædificat domum suam;<br> insipiens exstructam quoque manibus destruet.<br></p>
 <p>2 Ambulans recto itinere, et timens Deum,<br> despicitur ab eo qui infami graditur via.<br></p>
 <p>3 In ore stulti virga superbiæ;<br> labia autem sapientium custodiunt eos.<br></p>
 <p>4 Ubi non sunt boves, præsepe vacuum est;<br> ubi autem plurimæ segetes, ibi manifesta est fortitudo bovis.<br></p>
 <p>5 Testis fidelis non mentitur;<br> profert autem mendacium dolosus testis.<br></p>
 <p>6 Quærit derisor sapientiam, et non invenit;<br> doctrina prudentium facilis.<br></p>
 <p>7 Vade contra virum stultum,<br> et nescit labia prudentiæ.<br></p>
 <p>8 Sapientia callidi est intelligere viam suam,<br> et imprudentia stultorum errans.<br></p>
 <p>9 Stultus illudet peccatum,<br> et inter justos morabitur gratia.<br></p>
 <p>10 Cor quod novit amaritudinem animæ suæ,<br> in gaudio ejus non miscebitur extraneus.<br></p>
 <p>11 Domus impiorum delebitur:<br> tabernacula vero justorum germinabunt.<br></p>
 <p>12 Est via quæ videtur homini justa,<br> novissima autem ejus deducunt ad mortem.<br></p>
 <p>13 Risus dolore miscebitur,<br> et extrema gaudii luctus occupat.<br></p>
 <p>14 Viis suis replebitur stultus,<br> et super eum erit vir bonus.]<br>
 </p>
 <p>15 [Innocens credit omni verbo;<br> astutus considerat gressus suos.<br> Filio doloso nihil erit boni;<br> servo autem sapienti prosperi erunt actus,<br> et dirigetur via ejus.<br></p>
 <p>16 Sapiens timet, et declinat a malo;<br> stultus transilit, et confidit.<br></p>
 <p>17 Impatiens operabitur stultitiam,<br> et vir versutus odiosus est.<br></p>
 <p>18 Possidebunt parvuli stultitiam,<br> et exspectabunt astuti scientiam.<br></p>
 <p>19 Jacebunt mali ante bonos,<br> et impii ante portas justorum.<br></p>
 <p>20 Etiam proximo suo pauper odiosus erit:<br> amici vero divitum multi.<br></p>
 <p>21 Qui despicit proximum suum peccat;<br> qui autem miseretur pauperis beatus erit.<br> Qui credit in Domino misericordiam diligit.<br></p>
 <p>22 Errant qui operantur malum;<br> misericordia et veritas præparant bona.<br></p>
 <p>23 In omni opere erit abundantia;<br> ubi autem verba sunt plurima, ibi frequenter egestas.<br></p>
 <p>24 Corona sapientium divitiæ eorum;<br> fatuitas stultorum imprudentia.<br></p>
 <p>25 Liberat animas testis fidelis,<br> et profert mendacia versipellis.]<br>
 </p>
 <p>26 [In timore Domini fiducia fortitudinis,<br> et filiis ejus erit spes.<br></p>
 <p>27 Timor Domini fons vitæ,<br> ut declinent a ruina mortis.<br></p>
 <p>28 In multitudine populi dignitas regis,<br> et in paucitate plebis ignominia principis.<br></p>
 <p>29 Qui patiens est multa gubernatur prudentia;<br> qui autem impatiens est exaltat stultitiam suam.<br></p>
 <p>30 Vita carnium sanitas cordis;<br> putredo ossium invidia.<br></p>
 <p>31 Qui calumniatur egentem exprobrat factori ejus;<br> honorat autem eum qui miseretur pauperis.<br></p>
 <p>32 In malitia sua expelletur impius:<br> sperat autem justus in morte sua.<br></p>
 <p>33 In corde prudentis requiescit sapientia,<br> et indoctos quosque erudiet.<br></p>
 <p>34 Justitia elevat gentem;<br> miseros autem facit populos peccatum.<br></p>
 <p>35 Acceptus est regi minister intelligens;<br> iracundiam ejus inutilis sustinebit.]</p>
