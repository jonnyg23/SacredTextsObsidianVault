 <p>2  O ye sons<sup><a href="h1121.htm">1121</a></sup> of men,<sup><a href="h0376.htm">376</a></sup> how long<sup><a href="h5704.htm">5704</a></sup> <sup><a href="h4100.htm">4100</a></sup> will ye turn my glory<sup><a href="h3519.htm">3519</a></sup> into shame?<sup><a href="h3639.htm">3639</a></sup> how long will ye love<sup><a href="h0157.htm">157</a></sup> vanity,<sup><a href="h7385.htm">7385</a></sup> and seek after<sup><a href="h1245.htm">1245</a></sup> leasing?<sup><a href="h3577.htm">3577</a></sup> Selah.<sup><a href="h5542.htm">5542</a></sup> </p>
 <p>3  But know<sup><a href="h3045.htm">3045</a></sup> that<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> the LORD<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> hath set apart<sup><a href="h6395.htm">6395</a></sup> him that is godly<sup><a href="h2623.htm">2623</a></sup> for himself: the LORD<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> will hear<sup><a href="h8085.htm">8085</a></sup> when I call<sup><a href="h7121.htm">7121</a></sup> unto<sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> him. </p>
 <p>4  Stand in awe,<sup><a href="h7264.htm">7264</a></sup> and sin<sup><a href="h2398.htm">2398</a></sup> not:<sup><a href="h0408.htm">408</a></sup> commune<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> with your own heart<sup><a href="h3824.htm">3824</a></sup> upon<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> your bed,<sup><a href="h4904.htm">4904</a></sup> and be still.<sup><a href="h1826.htm">1826</a></sup> Selah.<sup><a href="h5542.htm">5542</a></sup> </p>
 <p>5  Offer<sup><a href="h2076.htm">2076</a></sup> the sacrifices<sup><a href="h2077.htm">2077</a></sup> of righteousness,<sup><a href="h6664.htm">6664</a></sup> and put your trust<sup><a href="h0982.htm">982</a></sup> in<sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> the LORD.<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> </p>
 <p>6  There be many<sup><a href="h7227.htm">7227</a></sup> that say,<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> Who<sup><a href="h4310.htm">4310</a></sup> will show<sup><a href="h7200.htm">7200</a></sup> us any good?<sup><a href="h2896.htm">2896</a></sup> LORD,<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> lift thou up<sup><a href="h5375.htm">5375</a></sup> the light<sup><a href="h0216.htm">216</a></sup> of thy countenance<sup><a href="h6440.htm">6440</a></sup> upon<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> us. </p>
 <p>7  Thou hast put<sup><a href="h5414.htm">5414</a></sup> gladness<sup><a href="h8057.htm">8057</a></sup> in my heart,<sup><a href="h3820.htm">3820</a></sup> more than in the time<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h6256.htm">6256</a></sup> that their corn<sup><a href="h1715.htm">1715</a></sup> and their wine<sup><a href="h8492.htm">8492</a></sup> increased.<sup><a href="h7231.htm">7231</a></sup> </p>
 <p>8  I will both<sup><a href="h3162.htm">3162</a></sup> lay me down<sup><a href="h7901.htm">7901</a></sup> in peace,<sup><a href="h7965.htm">7965</a></sup> and sleep:<sup><a href="h3462.htm">3462</a></sup> for<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> thou,<sup><a href="h0859.htm">859</a></sup> LORD,<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> only<sup><a href="h0910.htm">910</a></sup> makest me dwell<sup><a href="h3427.htm">3427</a></sup> in safety.<sup><a href="h0983.htm">983</a></sup> </p>

 <p>2 ἐν τῷ ἐπικαλεῖσθαί με εἰσήκουσέν μου ὁ θεὸς τῆς δικαιοσύνης μου ἐν θλίψει ἐπλάτυνάς μοι οἰκτίρησόν με καὶ εἰσάκουσον τῆς προσευχῆς μου </p>
 <p>3 υἱοὶ ἀνθρώπων ἕως πότε βαρυκάρδιοι ἵνα τί ἀγαπᾶτε ματαιότητα καὶ ζητεῖτε ψεῦδος διάψαλμα </p>
 <p>4 καὶ γνῶτε ὅτι ἐθαυμάστωσεν κύριος τὸν ὅσιον αὐτοῦ κύριος εἰσακούσεταί μου ἐν τῷ κεκραγέναι με πρὸς αὐτόν </p>
 <p>5 ὀργίζεσθε καὶ μὴ ἁμαρτάνετε λέγετε ἐν ταῖς καρδίαις ὑμῶν καὶ ἐπὶ ταῖς κοίταις ὑμῶν κατανύγητε διάψαλμα </p>
 <p>6 θύσατε θυσίαν δικαιοσύνης καὶ ἐλπίσατε ἐπὶ κύριον </p>
 <p>7 πολλοὶ λέγουσιν τίς δείξει ἡμῖν τὰ ἀγαθά ἐσημειώθη ἐφ᾽ ἡμᾶς τὸ φῶς τοῦ προσώπου σου κύριε </p>
 <p>8 ἔδωκας εὐφροσύνην εἰς τὴν καρδίαν μου ἀπὸ καιροῦ σίτου καὶ οἴνου καὶ ἐλαίου αὐτῶν ἐπληθύνθησαν </p>
 <p>9 ἐν εἰρήνῃ ἐπὶ τὸ αὐτὸ κοιμηθήσομαι καὶ ὑπνώσω ὅτι σύ κύριε κατὰ μόνας ἐπ᾽ ἐλπίδι κατῴκισάς με </p>
