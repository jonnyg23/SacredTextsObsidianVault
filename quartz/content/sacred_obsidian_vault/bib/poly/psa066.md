 <td valign="top">
 <p align="center"><a href="../kjv/psa066.htm">KJV</a></p><p>1  To the chief Musician,<sup><a href="h5329.htm">5329</a></sup> A Song<sup><a href="h7892.htm">7892</a></sup> or Psalm.<sup><a href="h4210.htm">4210</a></sup> Make a joyful noise<sup><a href="h7321.htm">7321</a></sup> unto God,<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> ye lands:<sup><a href="h0776.htm">776</a></sup> </p>
 <p>2  Sing forth<sup><a href="h2167.htm">2167</a></sup> the honor<sup><a href="h3519.htm">3519</a></sup> of his name:<sup><a href="h8034.htm">8034</a></sup> make<sup><a href="h7760.htm">7760</a></sup> his praise<sup><a href="h8416.htm">8416</a></sup> glorious.<sup><a href="h3519.htm">3519</a></sup> </p>
 <p>3  Say<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> unto God,<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> How<sup><a href="h4100.htm">4100</a></sup> terrible<sup><a href="h3372.htm">3372</a></sup> art thou in thy works!<sup><a href="h4639.htm">4639</a></sup> through the greatness<sup><a href="h7230.htm">7230</a></sup> of thy power<sup><a href="h5797.htm">5797</a></sup> shall thine enemies<sup><a href="h0341.htm">341</a></sup> submit<sup><a href="h3584.htm">3584</a></sup> themselves unto thee. </p>
 <p>4  All<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> the earth<sup><a href="h0776.htm">776</a></sup> shall worship<sup><a href="h7812.htm">7812</a></sup> thee, and shall sing<sup><a href="h2167.htm">2167</a></sup> unto thee; they shall sing<sup><a href="h2167.htm">2167</a></sup> to thy name.<sup><a href="h8034.htm">8034</a></sup> Selah.<sup><a href="h5542.htm">5542</a></sup> </p>
 <p>5  Come<sup><a href="h1980.htm">1980</a></sup> and see<sup><a href="h7200.htm">7200</a></sup> the works<sup><a href="h4659.htm">4659</a></sup> of God:<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> he is terrible<sup><a href="h3372.htm">3372</a></sup> in his doing<sup><a href="h5949.htm">5949</a></sup> toward<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> the children<sup><a href="h1121.htm">1121</a></sup> of men.<sup><a href="h0120.htm">120</a></sup> </p>
 <p>6  He turned<sup><a href="h2015.htm">2015</a></sup> the sea<sup><a href="h3220.htm">3220</a></sup> into dry<sup><a href="h3004.htm">3004</a></sup> land: they went<sup><a href="h5674.htm">5674</a></sup> through the flood<sup><a href="h5104.htm">5104</a></sup> on foot:<sup><a href="h7272.htm">7272</a></sup> there<sup><a href="h8033.htm">8033</a></sup> did we rejoice<sup><a href="h8055.htm">8055</a></sup> in him. </p>
 <p>7  He ruleth<sup><a href="h4910.htm">4910</a></sup> by his power<sup><a href="h1369.htm">1369</a></sup> forever;<sup><a href="h5769.htm">5769</a></sup> his eyes<sup><a href="h5869.htm">5869</a></sup> behold<sup><a href="h6822.htm">6822</a></sup> the nations:<sup><a href="h1471.htm">1471</a></sup> let not<sup><a href="h0408.htm">408</a></sup> the rebellious<sup><a href="h5637.htm">5637</a></sup> exalt<sup><a href="h7311.htm">7311</a></sup> themselves. Selah.<sup><a href="h5542.htm">5542</a></sup> </p>
 <p>8  O bless<sup><a href="h1288.htm">1288</a></sup> our God,<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> ye people,<sup><a href="h5971.htm">5971</a></sup> and make the voice<sup><a href="h6963.htm">6963</a></sup> of his praise<sup><a href="h8416.htm">8416</a></sup> to be heard:<sup><a href="h8085.htm">8085</a></sup> </p>
 <p>9  Which holdeth<sup><a href="h7760.htm">7760</a></sup> our soul<sup><a href="h5315.htm">5315</a></sup> in life,<sup><a href="h2416.htm">2416</a></sup> and suffereth<sup><a href="h5414.htm">5414</a></sup> not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> our feet<sup><a href="h7272.htm">7272</a></sup> to be moved.<sup><a href="h4132.htm">4132</a></sup> </p>
 <p>10  For<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> thou, O God,<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> hast proved<sup><a href="h0974.htm">974</a></sup> us: thou hast tried<sup><a href="h6884.htm">6884</a></sup> us, as silver<sup><a href="h3701.htm">3701</a></sup> is tried.<sup><a href="h6884.htm">6884</a></sup> </p>
 <p>11  Thou broughtest<sup><a href="h0935.htm">935</a></sup> us into the net;<sup><a href="h4686.htm">4686</a></sup> thou laidst<sup><a href="h7760.htm">7760</a></sup> affliction<sup><a href="h4157.htm">4157</a></sup> upon our loins.<sup><a href="h4975.htm">4975</a></sup> </p>
 <p>12  Thou hast caused men<sup><a href="h0582.htm">582</a></sup> to ride<sup><a href="h7392.htm">7392</a></sup> over our heads;<sup><a href="h7218.htm">7218</a></sup> we went<sup><a href="h0935.htm">935</a></sup> through fire<sup><a href="h0784.htm">784</a></sup> and through water:<sup><a href="h4325.htm">4325</a></sup> but thou broughtest us out<sup><a href="h3318.htm">3318</a></sup> into a wealthy<sup><a href="h7310.htm">7310</a></sup> place. </p>
 <p>13  I will go<sup><a href="h0935.htm">935</a></sup> into thy house<sup><a href="h1004.htm">1004</a></sup> with burnt offerings:<sup><a href="h5930.htm">5930</a></sup> I will pay<sup><a href="h7999.htm">7999</a></sup> thee my vows,<sup><a href="h5088.htm">5088</a></sup> </p>
 <p>14  Which<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> my lips<sup><a href="h8193.htm">8193</a></sup> have uttered,<sup><a href="h6475.htm">6475</a></sup> and my mouth<sup><a href="h6310.htm">6310</a></sup> hath spoken,<sup><a href="h1696.htm">1696</a></sup> when I was in trouble.<sup><a href="h6862.htm">6862</a></sup> </p>
 <p>15  I will offer<sup><a href="h5927.htm">5927</a></sup> unto thee burnt sacrifices<sup><a href="h5930.htm">5930</a></sup> of fatlings,<sup><a href="h4220.htm">4220</a></sup> with<sup><a href="h5973.htm">5973</a></sup> the incense<sup><a href="h7004.htm">7004</a></sup> of rams;<sup><a href="h0352.htm">352</a></sup> I will offer<sup><a href="h6213.htm">6213</a></sup> bullocks<sup><a href="h1241.htm">1241</a></sup> with<sup><a href="h5973.htm">5973</a></sup> goats.<sup><a href="h6260.htm">6260</a></sup> Selah.<sup><a href="h5542.htm">5542</a></sup> </p>
 <p>16  Come<sup><a href="h1980.htm">1980</a></sup> and hear,<sup><a href="h8085.htm">8085</a></sup> all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> ye that fear<sup><a href="h3373.htm">3373</a></sup> God,<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> and I will declare<sup><a href="h5608.htm">5608</a></sup> what<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> he hath done<sup><a href="h6213.htm">6213</a></sup> for my soul.<sup><a href="h5315.htm">5315</a></sup> </p>
 <p>17  I cried<sup><a href="h7121.htm">7121</a></sup> unto<sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> him with my mouth,<sup><a href="h6310.htm">6310</a></sup> and he was extolled<sup><a href="h7318.htm">7318</a></sup> with<sup><a href="h8478.htm">8478</a></sup> my tongue.<sup><a href="h3956.htm">3956</a></sup> </p>
 <p>18  If<sup><a href="h0518.htm">518</a></sup> I regard<sup><a href="h7200.htm">7200</a></sup> iniquity<sup><a href="h0205.htm">205</a></sup> in my heart,<sup><a href="h3820.htm">3820</a></sup> the Lord<sup><a href="h0136.htm">136</a></sup> will not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> hear<sup><a href="h8085.htm">8085</a></sup> me: </p>
 <p>19  But verily<sup><a href="h0403.htm">403</a></sup> God<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> hath heard<sup><a href="h8085.htm">8085</a></sup> me; he hath attended<sup><a href="h7181.htm">7181</a></sup> to the voice<sup><a href="h6963.htm">6963</a></sup> of my prayer.<sup><a href="h8605.htm">8605</a></sup> </p>
 <p>20  Blessed<sup><a href="h1288.htm">1288</a></sup> be God,<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> which<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> hath not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> turned away<sup><a href="h5493.htm">5493</a></sup> my prayer,<sup><a href="h8605.htm">8605</a></sup> nor his mercy<sup><a href="h2617.htm">2617</a></sup> from<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0854.htm">854</a></sup> me. </p>
