 <p align="center"><a href="../kjv/hos010.htm">KJV</a></p><p>1  Israel<sup><a href="h3478.htm">3478</a></sup> is an empty<sup><a href="h1238.htm">1238</a></sup> vine,<sup><a href="h1612.htm">1612</a></sup> he bringeth forth<sup><a href="h7737.htm">7737</a></sup> fruit<sup><a href="h6529.htm">6529</a></sup> unto himself: according to the multitude<sup><a href="h7230.htm">7230</a></sup> of his fruit<sup><a href="h6529.htm">6529</a></sup> he hath increased<sup><a href="h7235.htm">7235</a></sup> the altars;<sup><a href="h4196.htm">4196</a></sup> according to the goodness<sup><a href="h2896.htm">2896</a></sup> of his land<sup><a href="h0776.htm">776</a></sup> they have made goodly<sup><a href="h3190.htm">3190</a></sup> images.<sup><a href="h4676.htm">4676</a></sup> </p>
 <p>2  Their heart<sup><a href="h3820.htm">3820</a></sup> is divided;<sup><a href="h2505.htm">2505</a></sup> now<sup><a href="h6258.htm">6258</a></sup> shall they be found faulty:<sup><a href="h0816.htm">816</a></sup> he<sup><a href="h1931.htm">1931</a></sup> shall break down<sup><a href="h6202.htm">6202</a></sup> their altars,<sup><a href="h4196.htm">4196</a></sup> he shall spoil<sup><a href="h7703.htm">7703</a></sup> their images.<sup><a href="h4676.htm">4676</a></sup> </p>
 <p>3  For<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> now<sup><a href="h6258.htm">6258</a></sup> they shall say,<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> We have no<sup><a href="h0369.htm">369</a></sup> king,<sup><a href="h4428.htm">4428</a></sup> because<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> we feared<sup><a href="h3372.htm">3372</a></sup> not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> the LORD;<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> what<sup><a href="h4100.htm">4100</a></sup> then should a king<sup><a href="h4428.htm">4428</a></sup> do<sup><a href="h6213.htm">6213</a></sup> to us? </p>
 <p>4  They have spoken<sup><a href="h1696.htm">1696</a></sup> words,<sup><a href="h1697.htm">1697</a></sup> swearing<sup><a href="h0422.htm">422</a></sup> falsely<sup><a href="h7723.htm">7723</a></sup> in making<sup><a href="h3772.htm">3772</a></sup> a covenant:<sup><a href="h1285.htm">1285</a></sup> thus judgment<sup><a href="h4941.htm">4941</a></sup> springeth up<sup><a href="h6524.htm">6524</a></sup> as hemlock<sup><a href="h7219.htm">7219</a></sup> in<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> the furrows<sup><a href="h8525.htm">8525</a></sup> of the field.<sup><a href="h7704.htm">7704</a></sup> </p>
 <p>5  The inhabitants<sup><a href="h7934.htm">7934</a></sup> of Samaria<sup><a href="h8111.htm">8111</a></sup> shall fear<sup><a href="h1481.htm">1481</a></sup> because of the calves<sup><a href="h5697.htm">5697</a></sup> of Beth-aven:<sup><a href="h1007.htm">1007</a></sup> for<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> the people<sup><a href="h5971.htm">5971</a></sup> thereof shall mourn<sup><a href="h0056.htm">56</a></sup> over<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> it, and the priests<sup><a href="h3649.htm">3649</a></sup> thereof that rejoiced<sup><a href="h1523.htm">1523</a></sup> on<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> it, for<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> the glory<sup><a href="h3519.htm">3519</a></sup> thereof, because<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> it is departed<sup><a href="h1540.htm">1540</a></sup> from<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> it. </p>
 <p>6  It shall be also<sup><a href="h1571.htm">1571</a></sup> carried<sup><a href="h2986.htm">2986</a></sup> unto Assyria<sup><a href="h0804.htm">804</a></sup> for a present<sup><a href="h4503.htm">4503</a></sup> to king<sup><a href="h4428.htm">4428</a></sup> Jareb:<sup><a href="h3377.htm">3377</a></sup> Ephraim<sup><a href="h0669.htm">669</a></sup> shall receive<sup><a href="h3947.htm">3947</a></sup> shame,<sup><a href="h1317.htm">1317</a></sup> and Israel<sup><a href="h3478.htm">3478</a></sup> shall be ashamed<sup><a href="h0954.htm">954</a></sup> of his own counsel.<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h6098.htm">6098</a></sup> </p>
 <p>7  As for Samaria,<sup><a href="h8111.htm">8111</a></sup> her king<sup><a href="h4428.htm">4428</a></sup> is cut off<sup><a href="h1820.htm">1820</a></sup> as the foam<sup><a href="h7110.htm">7110</a></sup> upon<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> <sup><a href="h6440.htm">6440</a></sup> the water.<sup><a href="h4325.htm">4325</a></sup> </p>
 <p>8  The high places<sup><a href="h1116.htm">1116</a></sup> also of Aven,<sup><a href="h0206.htm">206</a></sup> the sin<sup><a href="h2403.htm">2403</a></sup> of Israel,<sup><a href="h3478.htm">3478</a></sup> shall be destroyed:<sup><a href="h8045.htm">8045</a></sup> the thorn<sup><a href="h6975.htm">6975</a></sup> and the thistle<sup><a href="h1863.htm">1863</a></sup> shall come up<sup><a href="h5927.htm">5927</a></sup> on<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> their altars;<sup><a href="h4196.htm">4196</a></sup> and they shall say<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> to the mountains,<sup><a href="h2022.htm">2022</a></sup> Cover<sup><a href="h3680.htm">3680</a></sup> us; and to the hills,<sup><a href="h1389.htm">1389</a></sup> Fall<sup><a href="h5307.htm">5307</a></sup> on<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> us. </p>
 <p>9  O Israel,<sup><a href="h3478.htm">3478</a></sup> thou hast sinned<sup><a href="h2398.htm">2398</a></sup> from the days<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3117.htm">3117</a></sup> of Gibeah:<sup><a href="h1390.htm">1390</a></sup> there<sup><a href="h8033.htm">8033</a></sup> they stood:<sup><a href="h5975.htm">5975</a></sup> the battle<sup><a href="h4421.htm">4421</a></sup> in Gibeah<sup><a href="h1390.htm">1390</a></sup> against<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> the children<sup><a href="h1121.htm">1121</a></sup> of iniquity<sup><a href="h5932.htm">5932</a></sup> did not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> overtake<sup><a href="h5381.htm">5381</a></sup> them. </p>
 <p>10  It is in my desire<sup><a href="h0185.htm">185</a></sup> that I should chastise<sup><a href="h3256.htm">3256</a></sup> them; and the people<sup><a href="h5971.htm">5971</a></sup> shall be gathered<sup><a href="h0622.htm">622</a></sup> against<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> them, when they shall bind<sup><a href="h0631.htm">631</a></sup> themselves in their two<sup><a href="h8147.htm">8147</a></sup> furrows.<sup><a href="h5869.htm">5869</a></sup> </p>
 <p>11  And Ephraim<sup><a href="h0669.htm">669</a></sup> is as a heifer<sup><a href="h5697.htm">5697</a></sup> that is taught,<sup><a href="h3925.htm">3925</a></sup> and loveth<sup><a href="h0157.htm">157</a></sup> to tread out<sup><a href="h1758.htm">1758</a></sup> the corn; but I<sup><a href="h0589.htm">589</a></sup> passed over<sup><a href="h5674.htm">5674</a></sup> upon<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> her fair<sup><a href="h2898.htm">2898</a></sup> neck:<sup><a href="h6677.htm">6677</a></sup> I will make Ephraim<sup><a href="h0669.htm">669</a></sup> to ride;<sup><a href="h7392.htm">7392</a></sup> Judah<sup><a href="h3063.htm">3063</a></sup> shall plow,<sup><a href="h2790.htm">2790</a></sup> and Jacob<sup><a href="h3290.htm">3290</a></sup> shall break his clods.<sup><a href="h7702.htm">7702</a></sup> </p>
 <p>12  Sow<sup><a href="h2232.htm">2232</a></sup> to yourselves in righteousness,<sup><a href="h6666.htm">6666</a></sup> reap<sup><a href="h7114.htm">7114</a></sup> in<sup><a href="h6310.htm">6310</a></sup> mercy;<sup><a href="h2617.htm">2617</a></sup> break up<sup><a href="h5214.htm">5214</a></sup> your fallow ground:<sup><a href="h5215.htm">5215</a></sup> for it is time<sup><a href="h6256.htm">6256</a></sup> to seek<sup><a href="h1875.htm">1875</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> the LORD,<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> till<sup><a href="h5704.htm">5704</a></sup> he come<sup><a href="h0935.htm">935</a></sup> and rain<sup><a href="h3384.htm">3384</a></sup> righteousness<sup><a href="h6664.htm">6664</a></sup> upon you. </p>
 <p>13  Ye have plowed<sup><a href="h2790.htm">2790</a></sup> wickedness,<sup><a href="h7562.htm">7562</a></sup> ye have reaped<sup><a href="h7114.htm">7114</a></sup> iniquity;<sup><a href="h5766.htm">5766</a></sup> ye have eaten<sup><a href="h0398.htm">398</a></sup> the fruit<sup><a href="h6529.htm">6529</a></sup> of lies:<sup><a href="h3585.htm">3585</a></sup> because<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> thou didst trust<sup><a href="h0982.htm">982</a></sup> in thy way,<sup><a href="h1870.htm">1870</a></sup> in the multitude<sup><a href="h7230.htm">7230</a></sup> of thy mighty men.<sup><a href="h1368.htm">1368</a></sup> </p>
 <p>14  Therefore shall a tumult<sup><a href="h7588.htm">7588</a></sup> arise<sup><a href="h6965.htm">6965</a></sup> among thy people,<sup><a href="h5971.htm">5971</a></sup> and all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> thy fortresses<sup><a href="h4013.htm">4013</a></sup> shall be spoiled,<sup><a href="h7703.htm">7703</a></sup> as Shalman<sup><a href="h8020.htm">8020</a></sup> spoiled<sup><a href="h7701.htm">7701</a></sup> Beth-arbel<sup><a href="h1009.htm">1009</a></sup> in the day<sup><a href="h3117.htm">3117</a></sup> of battle:<sup><a href="h4421.htm">4421</a></sup> the mother<sup><a href="h0517.htm">517</a></sup> was dashed in pieces<sup><a href="h7376.htm">7376</a></sup> upon<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> her children.<sup><a href="h1121.htm">1121</a></sup> </p>
 <p>15  So<sup><a href="h3602.htm">3602</a></sup> shall Bethel<sup><a href="h1008.htm">1008</a></sup> do<sup><a href="h6213.htm">6213</a></sup> unto you because<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h6440.htm">6440</a></sup> of your great wickedness:<sup><a href="h7451.htm">7451</a></sup> <sup><a href="h7451.htm">7451</a></sup> in a morning<sup><a href="h7837.htm">7837</a></sup> shall the king<sup><a href="h4428.htm">4428</a></sup> of Israel<sup><a href="h3478.htm">3478</a></sup> utterly be cut off.<sup><a href="h1820.htm">1820</a></sup> <sup><a href="h1820.htm">1820</a></sup> </p>

 <p align="center"><a href="../sep/hos010.htm">Sep</a></p><p>1 ἄμπελος εὐκληματοῦσα Ισραηλ ὁ καρπὸς αὐτῆς εὐθηνῶν κατὰ τὸ πλῆθος τῶν καρπῶν αὐτοῦ ἐπλήθυνεν τὰ θυσιαστήρια κατὰ τὰ ἀγαθὰ τῆς γῆς αὐτοῦ ᾠκοδόμησεν στήλας </p>
 <p>2 ἐμέρισαν καρδίας αὐτῶν νῦν ἀφανισθήσονται αὐτὸς κατασκάψει τὰ θυσιαστήρια αὐτῶν ταλαιπωρήσουσιν αἱ στῆλαι αὐτῶν </p>
 <p>3 διότι νῦν ἐροῦσιν οὐκ ἔστιν βασιλεὺς ἡμῖν ὅτι οὐκ ἐφοβήθημεν τὸν κύριον ὁ δὲ βασιλεὺς τί ποιήσει ἡμῖν </p>
 <p>4 λαλῶν ῥήματα προφάσεις ψευδεῖς διαθήσεται διαθήκην ἀνατελεῖ ὡς ἄγρωστις κρίμα ἐπὶ χέρσον ἀγροῦ </p>
 <p>5 τῷ μόσχῳ τοῦ οἴκου Ων παροικήσουσιν οἱ κατοικοῦντες Σαμάρειαν ὅτι ἐπένθησεν ὁ λαὸς αὐτοῦ ἐπ᾽ αὐτόν καὶ καθὼς παρεπίκραναν αὐτόν ἐπιχαροῦνται ἐπὶ τὴν δόξαν αὐτοῦ ὅτι μετῳκίσθη ἀπ᾽ αὐτοῦ </p>
 <p>6 καὶ αὐτὸν εἰς Ἀσσυρίους δήσαντες ἀπήνεγκαν ξένια τῷ βασιλεῖ Ιαριμ ἐν δόματι Εφραιμ δέξεται καὶ αἰσχυνθήσεται Ισραηλ ἐν τῇ βουλῇ αὐτοῦ </p>
 <p>7 ἀπέρριψεν Σαμάρεια βασιλέα αὐτῆς ὡς φρύγανον ἐπὶ προσώπου ὕδατος </p>
 <p>8 καὶ ἐξαρθήσονται βωμοὶ Ων ἁμαρτήματα τοῦ Ισραηλ ἄκανθαι καὶ τρίβολοι ἀναβήσονται ἐπὶ τὰ θυσιαστήρια αὐτῶν καὶ ἐροῦσιν τοῖς ὄρεσιν καλύψατε ἡμᾶς καὶ τοῖς βουνοῖς πέσατε ἐφ᾽ ἡμᾶς </p>
 <p>9 ἀφ᾽ οὗ οἱ βουνοί ἥμαρτεν Ισραηλ ἐκεῖ ἔστησαν οὐ μὴ καταλάβῃ αὐτοὺς ἐν τῷ βουνῷ πόλεμος ἐπὶ τὰ τέκνα ἀδικίας </p>
 <p>10 ἦλθεν παιδεῦσαι αὐτούς καὶ συναχθήσονται ἐπ᾽ αὐτοὺς λαοὶ ἐν τῷ παιδεύεσθαι αὐτοὺς ἐν ταῖς δυσὶν ἀδικίαις αὐτῶν </p>
 <p>11 Εφραιμ δάμαλις δεδιδαγμένη ἀγαπᾶν νεῖκος ἐγὼ δὲ ἐπελεύσομαι ἐπὶ τὸ κάλλιστον τοῦ τραχήλου αὐτῆς ἐπιβιβῶ Εφραιμ καὶ παρασιωπήσομαι Ιουδαν ἐνισχύσει αὐτῷ Ιακωβ </p>
 <p>12 σπείρατε ἑαυτοῖς εἰς δικαιοσύνην τρυγήσατε εἰς καρπὸν ζωῆς φωτίσατε ἑαυτοῖς φῶς γνώσεως ἐκζητήσατε τὸν κύριον ἕως τοῦ ἐλθεῖν γενήματα δικαιοσύνης ὑμῖν </p>
 <p>13 ἵνα τί παρεσιωπήσατε ἀσέβειαν καὶ τὰς ἀδικίας αὐτῆς ἐτρυγήσατε ἐφάγετε καρπὸν ψευδῆ ὅτι ἤλπισας ἐν τοῖς ἅρμασίν σου ἐν πλήθει δυνάμεώς σου </p>
 <p>14 καὶ ἐξαναστήσεται ἀπώλεια ἐν τῷ λαῷ σου καὶ πάντα τὰ περιτετειχισμένα σου οἰχήσεται ὡς ἄρχων Σαλαμαν ἐκ τοῦ οἴκου Ιεροβααλ ἐν ἡμέραις πολέμου μητέρα ἐπὶ τέκνοις ἠδάφισαν </p>
 <p>15 οὕτως ποιήσω ὑμῖν οἶκος τοῦ Ισραηλ ἀπὸ προσώπου κακιῶν ὑμῶν ὄρθρου ἀπερρίφησαν ἀπερρίφη βασιλεὺς Ισραηλ </p>

 <p align="center"><a href="../vul/hos010.htm">Vul</a></p><p>1 [Vitis frondosa Israël,<br> fructus adæquatus est ei:<br> secundum multitudinem fructus sui multiplicavit altaria,<br> juxta ubertatem terræ suæ exuberavit simulacris.<br></p>
 <p>2 Divisum est cor eorum, nunc interibunt;<br> ipse confringet simulacra eorum,<br> depopulabitur aras eorum.<br></p>
 <p>3 Quia nunc dicent: Non est rex nobis,<br> non enim timemus Dominum;<br> et rex quid faciet nobis?<br></p>
 <p>4 Loquimini verba visionis inutilis, et ferietis fdus;<br> et germinabit quasi amaritudo judicium super sulcos agri.<br></p>
 <p>5 Vaccas Bethaven coluerunt habitatores Samariæ;<br> quia luxit super eum populus ejus,<br> et æditui ejus super eum exsultaverunt<br> in gloria ejus, quia migravit ab eo.<br></p>
 <p>6 Siquidem et ipse in Assur delatus est,<br> munus regi ultori.<br> Confusio Ephraim capiet,<br> et confundetur Israël in voluntate sua.<br></p>
 <p>7 Transire fecit Samaria regem suum<br> quasi spumam super faciem aquæ.<br></p>
 <p>8 Et disperdentur excelsa idoli, peccatum Israël;<br> lappa et tribulus ascendet super aras eorum:<br> et dicent montibus: Operite nos,<br> et collibus: Cadite super nos.<br></p>
 <p>9 Ex diebus Gabaa peccavit Israël;<br> ibi steterunt.<br> Non comprehendet eos in Gabaa<br> prælium super filios iniquitatis.<br></p>
 <p>10 Juxta desiderium meum corripiam eos:<br> congregabuntur super eos populi,<br> cum corripientur propter duas iniquitates suas.<br></p>
 <p>11 Ephraim vitula docta diligere trituram,<br> et ego transivi super pulchritudinem colli ejus:<br> ascendam super Ephraim,<br> arabit Judas;<br> confringet sibi sulcos Jacob.<br></p>
 <p>12 Seminate vobis in justitia,<br> et metite in ore misericordiæ.<br> Innovate vobis novale;<br> tempus autem requirendi Dominum,<br> cum venerit qui docebit vos justitiam.<br></p>
 <p>13 Arastis impietatem,<br> iniquitatem messuistis:<br> comedistis frugem mendacii,<br> quia confisus es in viis tuis,<br> in multitudine fortium tuorum.<br></p>
 <p>14 Consurget tumultus in populo tuo;<br> et omnes munitiones tuæ vastabuntur,<br> sicut vastatus est Salmana a domo ejus qui judicavit Baal<br> in die prælii, matre super filios allisa.<br></p>
 <p>15 Sic fecit vobis Bethel,<br> a facie malitiæ nequitiarum vestrarum.]</p>
