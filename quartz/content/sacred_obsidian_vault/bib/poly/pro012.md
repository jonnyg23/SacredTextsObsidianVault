 <h3 align="CENTER">Proverbs 12</h3><table border="1">
 <tr>
 <td valign="top">
 <p align="center"><a href="../kjv/pro012.htm">KJV</a></p><p>1  Whoso loveth<sup><a href="h0157.htm">157</a></sup> instruction<sup><a href="h4148.htm">4148</a></sup> loveth<sup><a href="h0157.htm">157</a></sup> knowledge:<sup><a href="h1847.htm">1847</a></sup> but he that hateth<sup><a href="h8130.htm">8130</a></sup> reproof<sup><a href="h8433.htm">8433</a></sup> is brutish.<sup><a href="h1198.htm">1198</a></sup> </p>
 <p>2  A good<sup><a href="h2896.htm">2896</a></sup> man obtaineth<sup><a href="h6329.htm">6329</a></sup> favor<sup><a href="h7522.htm">7522</a></sup> of the LORD:<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> but a man<sup><a href="h0376.htm">376</a></sup> of wicked devices<sup><a href="h4209.htm">4209</a></sup> will he condemn.<sup><a href="h7561.htm">7561</a></sup> </p>
 <p>3  A man<sup><a href="h0120.htm">120</a></sup> shall not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> be established<sup><a href="h3559.htm">3559</a></sup> by wickedness:<sup><a href="h7562.htm">7562</a></sup> but the root<sup><a href="h8328.htm">8328</a></sup> of the righteous<sup><a href="h6662.htm">6662</a></sup> shall not<sup><a href="h1077.htm">1077</a></sup> be moved.<sup><a href="h4131.htm">4131</a></sup> </p>
 <p>4  A virtuous<sup><a href="h2428.htm">2428</a></sup> woman<sup><a href="h0802.htm">802</a></sup> is a crown<sup><a href="h5850.htm">5850</a></sup> to her husband:<sup><a href="h1167.htm">1167</a></sup> but she that maketh ashamed<sup><a href="h0954.htm">954</a></sup> is as rottenness<sup><a href="h7538.htm">7538</a></sup> in his bones.<sup><a href="h6106.htm">6106</a></sup> </p>
 <p>5  The thoughts<sup><a href="h4284.htm">4284</a></sup> of the righteous<sup><a href="h6662.htm">6662</a></sup> are right:<sup><a href="h4941.htm">4941</a></sup> but the counsels<sup><a href="h8458.htm">8458</a></sup> of the wicked<sup><a href="h7563.htm">7563</a></sup> are deceit.<sup><a href="h4820.htm">4820</a></sup> </p>
 <p>6  The words<sup><a href="h1697.htm">1697</a></sup> of the wicked<sup><a href="h7563.htm">7563</a></sup> are to lie in wait<sup><a href="h0693.htm">693</a></sup> for blood:<sup><a href="h1818.htm">1818</a></sup> but the mouth<sup><a href="h6310.htm">6310</a></sup> of the upright<sup><a href="h3477.htm">3477</a></sup> shall deliver<sup><a href="h5337.htm">5337</a></sup> them. </p>
 <p>7  The wicked<sup><a href="h7563.htm">7563</a></sup> are overthrown,<sup><a href="h2015.htm">2015</a></sup> and are not:<sup><a href="h0369.htm">369</a></sup> but the house<sup><a href="h1004.htm">1004</a></sup> of the righteous<sup><a href="h6662.htm">6662</a></sup> shall stand.<sup><a href="h5975.htm">5975</a></sup> </p>
 <p>8  A man<sup><a href="h0376.htm">376</a></sup> shall be commended<sup><a href="h1984.htm">1984</a></sup> according<sup><a href="h6310.htm">6310</a></sup> to his wisdom:<sup><a href="h7922.htm">7922</a></sup> but he that is of a perverse<sup><a href="h5753.htm">5753</a></sup> heart<sup><a href="h3820.htm">3820</a></sup> shall be<sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> despised.<sup><a href="h0937.htm">937</a></sup> </p>
 <p>9  He that is despised,<sup><a href="h7034.htm">7034</a></sup> and hath a servant,<sup><a href="h5650.htm">5650</a></sup> is better<sup><a href="h2896.htm">2896</a></sup> than he that honoreth himself,<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3513.htm">3513</a></sup> and lacketh<sup><a href="h2638.htm">2638</a></sup> bread.<sup><a href="h3899.htm">3899</a></sup> </p>
 <p>10  A righteous<sup><a href="h6662.htm">6662</a></sup> man regardeth<sup><a href="h3045.htm">3045</a></sup> the life<sup><a href="h5315.htm">5315</a></sup> of his beast:<sup><a href="h0929.htm">929</a></sup> but the tender mercies<sup><a href="h7356.htm">7356</a></sup> of the wicked<sup><a href="h7563.htm">7563</a></sup> are cruel.<sup><a href="h0394.htm">394</a></sup> </p>
 <p>11  He that tilleth<sup><a href="h5647.htm">5647</a></sup> his land<sup><a href="h0127.htm">127</a></sup> shall be satisfied<sup><a href="h7646.htm">7646</a></sup> with bread:<sup><a href="h3899.htm">3899</a></sup> but he that followeth<sup><a href="h7291.htm">7291</a></sup> vain<sup><a href="h7386.htm">7386</a></sup> persons is void<sup><a href="h2638.htm">2638</a></sup> of understanding.<sup><a href="h3820.htm">3820</a></sup> </p>
 <p>12  The wicked<sup><a href="h7563.htm">7563</a></sup> desireth<sup><a href="h2530.htm">2530</a></sup> the net<sup><a href="h4685.htm">4685</a></sup> of evil<sup><a href="h7451.htm">7451</a></sup> men: but the root<sup><a href="h8328.htm">8328</a></sup> of the righteous<sup><a href="h6662.htm">6662</a></sup> yieldeth<sup><a href="h5414.htm">5414</a></sup> fruit. </p>
 <p>13  The wicked<sup><a href="h7451.htm">7451</a></sup> is snared<sup><a href="h4170.htm">4170</a></sup> by the transgression<sup><a href="h6588.htm">6588</a></sup> of his lips:<sup><a href="h8193.htm">8193</a></sup> but the just<sup><a href="h6662.htm">6662</a></sup> shall come out<sup><a href="h3318.htm">3318</a></sup> of trouble.<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h6869.htm">6869</a></sup> </p>
 <p>14  A man<sup><a href="h0376.htm">376</a></sup> shall be satisfied<sup><a href="h7646.htm">7646</a></sup> with good<sup><a href="h2896.htm">2896</a></sup> by the fruit<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h6529.htm">6529</a></sup> of his mouth:<sup><a href="h6310.htm">6310</a></sup> and the recompense<sup><a href="h1576.htm">1576</a></sup> of a man's<sup><a href="h0120.htm">120</a></sup> hands<sup><a href="h3027.htm">3027</a></sup> shall be rendered<sup><a href="h7725.htm">7725</a></sup> unto him. </p>
 <p>15  The way<sup><a href="h1870.htm">1870</a></sup> of a fool<sup><a href="h0191.htm">191</a></sup> is right<sup><a href="h3477.htm">3477</a></sup> in his own eyes:<sup><a href="h5869.htm">5869</a></sup> but he that hearkeneth<sup><a href="h8085.htm">8085</a></sup> unto counsel<sup><a href="h6098.htm">6098</a></sup> is wise.<sup><a href="h2450.htm">2450</a></sup> </p>
 <p>16  A fool's<sup><a href="h0191.htm">191</a></sup> wrath<sup><a href="h3708.htm">3708</a></sup> is presently<sup><a href="h3117.htm">3117</a></sup> known:<sup><a href="h3045.htm">3045</a></sup> but a prudent<sup><a href="h6175.htm">6175</a></sup> man covereth<sup><a href="h3680.htm">3680</a></sup> shame.<sup><a href="h7036.htm">7036</a></sup> </p>
 <p>17  He that speaketh<sup><a href="h6315.htm">6315</a></sup> truth<sup><a href="h0530.htm">530</a></sup> showeth forth<sup><a href="h5046.htm">5046</a></sup> righteousness:<sup><a href="h6664.htm">6664</a></sup> but a false<sup><a href="h8267.htm">8267</a></sup> witness<sup><a href="h5707.htm">5707</a></sup> deceit.<sup><a href="h4820.htm">4820</a></sup> </p>
 <p>18  There is<sup><a href="h3426.htm">3426</a></sup> that speaketh<sup><a href="h0981.htm">981</a></sup> like the piercings<sup><a href="h4094.htm">4094</a></sup> of a sword:<sup><a href="h2719.htm">2719</a></sup> but the tongue<sup><a href="h3956.htm">3956</a></sup> of the wise<sup><a href="h2450.htm">2450</a></sup> is health.<sup><a href="h4832.htm">4832</a></sup> </p>
 <p>19  The lip<sup><a href="h8193.htm">8193</a></sup> of truth<sup><a href="h0571.htm">571</a></sup> shall be established<sup><a href="h3559.htm">3559</a></sup> forever:<sup><a href="h5703.htm">5703</a></sup> but<sup><a href="h5704.htm">5704</a></sup> a lying<sup><a href="h8267.htm">8267</a></sup> tongue<sup><a href="h3956.htm">3956</a></sup> is but for a moment.<sup><a href="h7280.htm">7280</a></sup> </p>
 <p>20  Deceit<sup><a href="h4820.htm">4820</a></sup> is in the heart<sup><a href="h3820.htm">3820</a></sup> of them that imagine<sup><a href="h2790.htm">2790</a></sup> evil:<sup><a href="h7451.htm">7451</a></sup> but to the counselors<sup><a href="h3289.htm">3289</a></sup> of peace<sup><a href="h7965.htm">7965</a></sup> is joy.<sup><a href="h8057.htm">8057</a></sup> </p>
 <p>21  There shall no<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> evil<sup><a href="h0205.htm">205</a></sup> happen<sup><a href="h0579.htm">579</a></sup> to the just:<sup><a href="h6662.htm">6662</a></sup> but the wicked<sup><a href="h7563.htm">7563</a></sup> shall be filled<sup><a href="h4390.htm">4390</a></sup> with mischief.<sup><a href="h7451.htm">7451</a></sup> </p>
 <p>22  Lying<sup><a href="h8267.htm">8267</a></sup> lips<sup><a href="h8193.htm">8193</a></sup> are abomination<sup><a href="h8441.htm">8441</a></sup> to the LORD:<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> but they that deal<sup><a href="h6213.htm">6213</a></sup> truly<sup><a href="h0530.htm">530</a></sup> are his delight.<sup><a href="h7522.htm">7522</a></sup> </p>
 <p>23  A prudent<sup><a href="h6175.htm">6175</a></sup> man<sup><a href="h0120.htm">120</a></sup> concealeth<sup><a href="h3680.htm">3680</a></sup> knowledge:<sup><a href="h1847.htm">1847</a></sup> but the heart<sup><a href="h3820.htm">3820</a></sup> of fools<sup><a href="h3684.htm">3684</a></sup> proclaimeth<sup><a href="h7121.htm">7121</a></sup> foolishness.<sup><a href="h0200.htm">200</a></sup> </p>
 <p>24  The hand<sup><a href="h3027.htm">3027</a></sup> of the diligent<sup><a href="h2742.htm">2742</a></sup> shall bear rule:<sup><a href="h4910.htm">4910</a></sup> but the slothful<sup><a href="h7423.htm">7423</a></sup> shall be<sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> under tribute.<sup><a href="h4522.htm">4522</a></sup> </p>
 <p>25  Heaviness<sup><a href="h1674.htm">1674</a></sup> in the heart<sup><a href="h3820.htm">3820</a></sup> of man<sup><a href="h0376.htm">376</a></sup> maketh it stoop:<sup><a href="h7812.htm">7812</a></sup> but a good<sup><a href="h2896.htm">2896</a></sup> word<sup><a href="h1697.htm">1697</a></sup> maketh it glad.<sup><a href="h8055.htm">8055</a></sup> </p>
 <p>26  The righteous<sup><a href="h6662.htm">6662</a></sup> is more excellent<sup><a href="h8446.htm">8446</a></sup> than his neighbor:<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h7453.htm">7453</a></sup> but the way<sup><a href="h1870.htm">1870</a></sup> of the wicked<sup><a href="h7563.htm">7563</a></sup> seduceth<sup><a href="h8582.htm">8582</a></sup> them. </p>
 <p>27  The slothful<sup><a href="h7423.htm">7423</a></sup> man roasteth<sup><a href="h2760.htm">2760</a></sup> not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> that which he took in hunting:<sup><a href="h6718.htm">6718</a></sup> but the substance<sup><a href="h1952.htm">1952</a></sup> of a diligent<sup><a href="h2742.htm">2742</a></sup> man<sup><a href="h0120.htm">120</a></sup> is precious.<sup><a href="h3368.htm">3368</a></sup> </p>
 <p>28  In the way<sup><a href="h0734.htm">734</a></sup> of righteousness<sup><a href="h6666.htm">6666</a></sup> is life;<sup><a href="h2416.htm">2416</a></sup> and in the pathway<sup><a href="h1870.htm">1870</a></sup> <sup><a href="h5410.htm">5410</a></sup> thereof there is no<sup><a href="h0408.htm">408</a></sup> death.<sup><a href="h4194.htm">4194</a></sup> </p>

 <p align="center"><a href="../sep/pro012.htm">Sep</a></p><p>1 ὁ ἀγαπῶν παιδείαν ἀγαπᾷ αἴσθησιν ὁ δὲ μισῶν ἐλέγχους ἄφρων </p>
 <p>2 κρείσσων ὁ εὑρὼν χάριν παρὰ κυρίῳ ἀνὴρ δὲ παράνομος παρασιωπηθήσεται </p>
 <p>3 οὐ κατορθώσει ἄνθρωπος ἐξ ἀνόμου αἱ δὲ ῥίζαι τῶν δικαίων οὐκ ἐξαρθήσονται </p>
 <p>4 γυνὴ ἀνδρεία στέφανος τῷ ἀνδρὶ αὐτῆς ὥσπερ δὲ ἐν ξύλῳ σκώληξ οὕτως ἄνδρα ἀπόλλυσιν γυνὴ κακοποιός </p>
 <p>5 λογισμοὶ δικαίων κρίματα κυβερνῶσιν δὲ ἀσεβεῖς δόλους </p>
 <p>6 λόγοι ἀσεβῶν δόλιοι στόμα δὲ ὀρθῶν ῥύσεται αὐτούς </p>
 <p>7 οὗ ἐὰν στραφῇ ἀσεβὴς ἀφανίζεται οἶκοι δὲ δικαίων παραμένουσιν </p>
 <p>8 στόμα συνετοῦ ἐγκωμιάζεται ὑπὸ ἀνδρός νωθροκάρδιος δὲ μυκτηρίζεται </p>
 <p>9 κρείσσων ἀνὴρ ἐν ἀτιμίᾳ δουλεύων ἑαυτῷ ἢ τιμὴν ἑαυτῷ περιτιθεὶς καὶ προσδεόμενος ἄρτου </p>
 <p>10 δίκαιος οἰκτίρει ψυχὰς κτηνῶν αὐτοῦ τὰ δὲ σπλάγχνα τῶν ἀσεβῶν ἀνελεήμονα </p>
 <p>11 ὁ ἐργαζόμενος τὴν ἑαυτοῦ γῆν ἐμπλησθήσεται ἄρτων οἱ δὲ διώκοντες μάταια ἐνδεεῖς φρενῶν  <br>
  ὅς ἐστιν ἡδὺς ἐν οἴνων διατριβαῖς ἐν τοῖς ἑαυτοῦ ὀχυρώμασιν καταλείψει ἀτιμίαν </p>
 <p>12 ἐπιθυμίαι ἀσεβῶν κακαί αἱ δὲ ῥίζαι τῶν εὐσεβῶν ἐν ὀχυρώμασιν </p>
 <p>13 δι᾽ ἁμαρτίαν χειλέων ἐμπίπτει εἰς παγίδας ἁμαρτωλός ἐκφεύγει δὲ ἐξ αὐτῶν δίκαιος  <br>
  ὁ βλέπων λεῖα ἐλεηθήσεται ὁ δὲ συναντῶν ἐν πύλαις ἐκθλίψει ψυχάς </p>
 <p>14 ἀπὸ καρπῶν στόματος ψυχὴ ἀνδρὸς πλησθήσεται ἀγαθῶν ἀνταπόδομα δὲ χειλέων αὐτοῦ δοθήσεται αὐτῷ </p>
 <p>15 ὁδοὶ ἀφρόνων ὀρθαὶ ἐνώπιον αὐτῶν εἰσακούει δὲ συμβουλίας σοφός </p>
 <p>16 ἄφρων αὐθημερὸν ἐξαγγέλλει ὀργὴν αὐτοῦ κρύπτει δὲ τὴν ἑαυτοῦ ἀτιμίαν πανοῦργος </p>
 <p>17 ἐπιδεικνυμένην πίστιν ἀπαγγέλλει δίκαιος ὁ δὲ μάρτυς τῶν ἀδίκων δόλιος </p>
 <p>18 εἰσὶν οἳ λέγοντες τιτρώσκουσιν μαχαίρᾳ γλῶσσαι δὲ σοφῶν ἰῶνται </p>
 <p>19 χείλη ἀληθινὰ κατορθοῖ μαρτυρίαν μάρτυς δὲ ταχὺς γλῶσσαν ἔχει ἄδικον </p>
 <p>20 δόλος ἐν καρδίᾳ τεκταινομένου κακά οἱ δὲ βουλόμενοι εἰρήνην εὐφρανθήσονται </p>
 <p>21 οὐκ ἀρέσει τῷ δικαίῳ οὐδὲν ἄδικον οἱ δὲ ἀσεβεῖς πλησθήσονται κακῶν </p>
 <p>22 βδέλυγμα κυρίῳ χείλη ψευδῆ ὁ δὲ ποιῶν πίστεις δεκτὸς παρ᾽ αὐτῷ </p>
 <p>23 ἀνὴρ συνετὸς θρόνος αἰσθήσεως καρδία δὲ ἀφρόνων συναντήσεται ἀραῖς </p>
 <p>24 χεὶρ ἐκλεκτῶν κρατήσει εὐχερῶς δόλιοι δὲ ἔσονται εἰς προνομήν </p>
 <p>25 φοβερὸς λόγος καρδίαν ταράσσει ἀνδρὸς δικαίου ἀγγελία δὲ ἀγαθὴ εὐφραίνει αὐτόν </p>
 <p>26 ἐπιγνώμων δίκαιος ἑαυτοῦ φίλος ἔσται αἱ δὲ γνῶμαι τῶν ἀσεβῶν ἀνεπιεικεῖς ἁμαρτάνοντας καταδιώξεται κακά ἡ δὲ ὁδὸς τῶν ἀσεβῶν πλανήσει αὐτούς </p>
 <p>27 οὐκ ἐπιτεύξεται δόλιος θήρας κτῆμα δὲ τίμιον ἀνὴρ καθαρός </p>
 <p>28 ἐν ὁδοῖς δικαιοσύνης ζωή ὁδοὶ δὲ μνησικάκων εἰς θάνατον </p>

 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎11 ‏עֹבֵ֣ד אַ֭דְמָתוֹ יִֽשְׂבַּֽע־לָ֑חֶם וּמְרַדֵּ֖ף רֵיקִ֣ים חֲסַר־לֵֽב׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎12 ‏חָמַ֣ד רָ֭שָׁע מְצ֣וֹד רָעִ֑ים וְשֹׁ֖רֶשׁ צַדִּיקִ֣ים יִתֵּֽן׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎13 ‏בְּפֶ֣שַׁע שְׂ֭פָתַיִם מוֹקֵ֣שׁ רָ֑ע וַיֵּצֵ֖א מִצָּרָ֣ה צַדִּֽיק׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎14 ‏מִפְּרִ֣י פִי־אִ֭ישׁ יִשְׂבַּע־ט֑וֹב וּגְמ֥וּל יְדֵי־אָ֝דָ֗ם <font color="brown">ישוב</font> <font color="blue">יָשִׁ֥יב</font> לֽוֹ׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎15 ‏דֶּ֣רֶךְ אֱ֭וִיל יָשָׁ֣ר בְּעֵינָ֑יו וְשֹׁמֵ֖עַ לְעֵצָ֣ה חָכָֽם׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎16 ‏אֱוִ֗יל בַּ֭יּוֹם יִוָּדַ֣ע כַּעְס֑וֹ וְכֹסֶ֖ה קָל֣וֹן עָרֽוּם׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎17 ‏יָפִ֣יחַ אֱ֭מוּנָה יַגִּ֣יד צֶ֑דֶק וְעֵ֖ד שְׁקָרִ֣ים מִרְמָֽה׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎18 ‏יֵ֣שׁ בּ֖וֹטֶה כְּמַדְקְר֣וֹת חָ֑רֶב וּלְשׁ֖וֹן חֲכָמִ֣ים מַרְפֵּֽא׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎19 ‏שְֽׂפַת־אֱ֭מֶת תִּכּ֣וֹן לָעַ֑ד וְעַד־אַ֝רְגִּ֗יעָה לְשׁ֣וֹן שָֽׁקֶר׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎20 ‏מִ֭רְמָה בְּלֶב־חֹ֣רְשֵׁי רָ֑ע וּֽלְיֹעֲצֵ֖י שָׁל֣וֹם שִׂמְחָֽה׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎21 ‏לֹא־יְאֻנֶּ֣ה לַצַּדִּ֣יק כָּל־אָ֑וֶן וּ֝רְשָׁעִ֗ים מָ֣לְאוּ רָֽע׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎22 ‏תּוֹעֲבַ֣ת יְ֭הוָה שִׂפְתֵי־שָׁ֑קֶר וְעֹשֵׂ֖י אֱמוּנָ֣ה רְצוֹנֽוֹ׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎23 ‏אָדָ֣ם עָ֭רוּם כֹּ֣סֶה דָּ֑עַת וְלֵ֥ב כְּ֝סִילִ֗ים יִקְרָ֥א אִוֶּֽלֶת׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎24 ‏יַד־חָרוּצִ֥ים תִּמְשׁ֑וֹל וּ֝רְמִיָּ֗ה תִּהְיֶ֥ה לָמַֽס׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎25 ‏דְּאָגָ֣ה בְלֶב־אִ֣ישׁ יַשְׁחֶ֑נָּה וְדָבָ֖ר ט֣וֹב יְשַׂמְּחֶֽנָּה׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎26 ‏יָתֵ֣ר מֵרֵעֵ֣הוּ צַדִּ֑יק וְדֶ֖רֶךְ רְשָׁעִ֣ים תַּתְעֵֽם׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎27 ‏לֹא־יַחֲרֹ֣ךְ רְמִיָּ֣ה צֵיד֑וֹ וְהוֹן־אָדָ֖ם יָקָ֣ר חָרֽוּץ׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎28 ‏בְּאֹֽרַח־צְדָקָ֥ה חַיִּ֑ים וְדֶ֖רֶךְ נְתִיבָ֣ה אַל־מָֽוֶת׃ </span></p>

 <p align="center"><a href="../vul/pro012.htm">Vul</a></p><p>1 [Qui diligit disciplinam diligit scientiam;<br> qui autem odit increpationes insipiens est.<br></p>
 <p>2 Qui bonus est hauriet gratiam a Domino;<br> qui autem confidit in cogitationibus suis impie agit.<br></p>
 <p>3 Non roborabitur homo ex impietate,<br> et radix justorum non commovebitur.<br></p>
 <p>4 Mulier diligens corona est viro suo;<br> et putredo in ossibus ejus, quæ confusione res dignas gerit.<br></p>
 <p>5 Cogitationes justorum judicia,<br> et consilia impiorum fraudulenta.<br></p>
 <p>6 Verba impiorum insidiantur sanguini;<br> os justorum liberabit eos.<br></p>
 <p>7 Verte impios, et non erunt;<br> domus autem justorum permanebit.<br></p>
 <p>8 Doctrina sua noscetur vir;<br> qui autem vanus et excors est patebit contemptui.<br></p>
 <p>9 Melior est pauper et sufficiens sibi<br> quam gloriosus et indigens pane.<br></p>
 <p>10 Novit justus jumentorum suorum animas;<br> viscera autem impiorum crudelia.<br></p>
 <p>11 Qui operatur terram suam satiabitur panibus;<br> qui autem sectatur otium stultissimus est.<br> Qui suavis est in vini demorationibus,<br> in suis munitionibus relinquit contumeliam.<br></p>
 <p>12 Desiderium impii munimentum est pessimorum;<br> radix autem justorum proficiet.]<br>
 </p>
 <p>13 [Propter peccata labiorum ruina proximat malo;<br> effugiet autem justus de angustia.<br></p>
 <p>14 De fructu oris sui unusquisque replebitur bonis,<br> et juxta opera manuum suarum retribuetur ei.<br></p>
 <p>15 Via stulti recta in oculis ejus;<br> qui autem sapiens est audit consilia.<br></p>
 <p>16 Fatuus statim indicat iram suam;<br> qui autem dissimulat injuriam callidus est.<br></p>
 <p>17 Qui quod novit loquitur, index justitiæ est;<br> qui autem mentitur, testis est fraudulentus.<br></p>
 <p>18 Est qui promittit, et quasi gladio pungitur conscientiæ:<br> lingua autem sapientium sanitas est.<br></p>
 <p>19 Labium veritatis firmum erit in perpetuum;<br> qui autem testis est repentinus, concinnat linguam mendacii.<br></p>
 <p>20 Dolus in corde cogitantium mala;<br> qui autem pacis ineunt consilia, sequitur eos gaudium.<br></p>
 <p>21 Non contristabit justum quidquid ei acciderit:<br> impii autem replebuntur malo.<br></p>
 <p>22 Abominatio est Domino labia mendacia;<br> qui autem fideliter agunt placent ei.<br></p>
 <p>23 Homo versatus celat scientiam,<br> et cor insipientium provocat stultitiam.]<br>
 </p>
 <p>24 [Manus fortium dominabitur;<br> quæ autem remissa est, tributis serviet.<br></p>
 <p>25 Mror in corde viri humiliabit illum,<br> et sermone bono lætificabitur.<br></p>
 <p>26 Qui negligit damnum propter amicum, justus est;<br> iter autem impiorum decipiet eos.<br></p>
 <p>27 Non inveniet fraudulentus lucrum,<br> et substantia hominis erit auri pretium.<br></p>
 <p>28 In semita justitiæ vita;<br> iter autem devium ducit ad mortem.]</p>
