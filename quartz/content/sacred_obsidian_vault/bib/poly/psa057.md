 <p align="center"><a href="../kjv/psa057.htm">KJV</a></p><p>1  To the chief Musician,<sup><a href="h5329.htm">5329</a></sup> Al-taschith,<sup><a href="h0516.htm">516</a></sup> Michtam<sup><a href="h4387.htm">4387</a></sup> of David,<sup><a href="h1732.htm">1732</a></sup> when he fled<sup><a href="h1272.htm">1272</a></sup> from<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h6440.htm">6440</a></sup> Saul<sup><a href="h7586.htm">7586</a></sup> in the cave.<sup><a href="h4631.htm">4631</a></sup> Be merciful<sup><a href="h2603.htm">2603</a></sup> unto me, O God,<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> be merciful<sup><a href="h2603.htm">2603</a></sup> unto me: for<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> my soul<sup><a href="h5315.htm">5315</a></sup> trusteth<sup><a href="h2620.htm">2620</a></sup> in thee: yea, in the shadow<sup><a href="h6738.htm">6738</a></sup> of thy wings<sup><a href="h3671.htm">3671</a></sup> will I make my refuge,<sup><a href="h2620.htm">2620</a></sup> until<sup><a href="h5704.htm">5704</a></sup> these calamities<sup><a href="h1942.htm">1942</a></sup> be overpast.<sup><a href="h5674.htm">5674</a></sup> </p>
 <p>2  I will cry<sup><a href="h7121.htm">7121</a></sup> unto God<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> most high;<sup><a href="h5945.htm">5945</a></sup> unto God<sup><a href="h0410.htm">410</a></sup> that performeth<sup><a href="h1584.htm">1584</a></sup> all things for<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> me. </p>
 <p>3  He shall send<sup><a href="h7971.htm">7971</a></sup> from heaven,<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h8064.htm">8064</a></sup> and save<sup><a href="h3467.htm">3467</a></sup> me from the reproach<sup><a href="h2778.htm">2778</a></sup> of him that would swallow me up.<sup><a href="h7602.htm">7602</a></sup> Selah.<sup><a href="h5542.htm">5542</a></sup> God<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> shall send forth<sup><a href="h7971.htm">7971</a></sup> his mercy<sup><a href="h2617.htm">2617</a></sup> and his truth.<sup><a href="h0571.htm">571</a></sup> </p>
 <p>4  My soul<sup><a href="h5315.htm">5315</a></sup> is among<sup><a href="h8432.htm">8432</a></sup> lions:<sup><a href="h3833.htm">3833</a></sup> and I lie<sup><a href="h7901.htm">7901</a></sup> even among them that are set on fire,<sup><a href="h3857.htm">3857</a></sup> even the sons<sup><a href="h1121.htm">1121</a></sup> of men,<sup><a href="h0120.htm">120</a></sup> whose teeth<sup><a href="h8127.htm">8127</a></sup> are spears<sup><a href="h2595.htm">2595</a></sup> and arrows,<sup><a href="h2671.htm">2671</a></sup> and their tongue<sup><a href="h3956.htm">3956</a></sup> a sharp<sup><a href="h2299.htm">2299</a></sup> sword.<sup><a href="h2719.htm">2719</a></sup> </p>
 <p>5  Be thou exalted,<sup><a href="h7311.htm">7311</a></sup> O God,<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> above<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> the heavens;<sup><a href="h8064.htm">8064</a></sup> let thy glory<sup><a href="h3519.htm">3519</a></sup> be above<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> the earth.<sup><a href="h0776.htm">776</a></sup> </p>
 <p>6  They have prepared<sup><a href="h3559.htm">3559</a></sup> a net<sup><a href="h7568.htm">7568</a></sup> for my steps;<sup><a href="h6471.htm">6471</a></sup> my soul<sup><a href="h5315.htm">5315</a></sup> is bowed down:<sup><a href="h3721.htm">3721</a></sup> they have digged<sup><a href="h3738.htm">3738</a></sup> a pit<sup><a href="h7882.htm">7882</a></sup> before<sup><a href="h6440.htm">6440</a></sup> me, into the midst<sup><a href="h8432.htm">8432</a></sup> whereof they are fallen<sup><a href="h5307.htm">5307</a></sup> themselves. Selah.<sup><a href="h5542.htm">5542</a></sup> </p>
 <p>7  My heart<sup><a href="h3820.htm">3820</a></sup> is fixed,<sup><a href="h3559.htm">3559</a></sup> O God,<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> my heart<sup><a href="h3820.htm">3820</a></sup> is fixed:<sup><a href="h3559.htm">3559</a></sup> I will sing<sup><a href="h7891.htm">7891</a></sup> and give praise.<sup><a href="h2167.htm">2167</a></sup> </p>
 <p>8  Awake up,<sup><a href="h5782.htm">5782</a></sup> my glory;<sup><a href="h3519.htm">3519</a></sup> awake,<sup><a href="h5782.htm">5782</a></sup> psaltery<sup><a href="h5035.htm">5035</a></sup> and harp:<sup><a href="h3658.htm">3658</a></sup> I myself will awake<sup><a href="h5782.htm">5782</a></sup> early.<sup><a href="h7837.htm">7837</a></sup> </p>
 <p>9  I will praise<sup><a href="h3034.htm">3034</a></sup> thee, O Lord,<sup><a href="h0136.htm">136</a></sup> among the people:<sup><a href="h5971.htm">5971</a></sup> I will sing<sup><a href="h2167.htm">2167</a></sup> unto thee among the nations.<sup><a href="h3816.htm">3816</a></sup> </p>
 <p>10  For<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> thy mercy<sup><a href="h2617.htm">2617</a></sup> is great<sup><a href="h1419.htm">1419</a></sup> unto<sup><a href="h5704.htm">5704</a></sup> the heavens,<sup><a href="h8064.htm">8064</a></sup> and thy truth<sup><a href="h0571.htm">571</a></sup> unto<sup><a href="h5704.htm">5704</a></sup> the clouds.<sup><a href="h7834.htm">7834</a></sup> </p>
 <p>11  Be thou exalted,<sup><a href="h7311.htm">7311</a></sup> O God,<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> above<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> the heavens:<sup><a href="h8064.htm">8064</a></sup> let thy glory<sup><a href="h3519.htm">3519</a></sup> be above<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> the earth.<sup><a href="h0776.htm">776</a></sup> </p>

 <p>2 εἰ ἀληθῶς ἄρα δικαιοσύνην λαλεῖτε εὐθεῖα κρίνετε οἱ υἱοὶ τῶν ἀνθρώπων </p>
 <p>3 καὶ γὰρ ἐν καρδίᾳ ἀνομίας ἐργάζεσθε ἐν τῇ γῇ ἀδικίαν αἱ χεῖρες ὑμῶν συμπλέκουσιν </p>
 <p>4 ἀπηλλοτριώθησαν οἱ ἁμαρτωλοὶ ἀπὸ μήτρας ἐπλανήθησαν ἀπὸ γαστρός ἐλάλησαν ψεύδη </p>
 <p>5 θυμὸς αὐτοῖς κατὰ τὴν ὁμοίωσιν τοῦ ὄφεως ὡσεὶ ἀσπίδος κωφῆς καὶ βυούσης τὰ ὦτα αὐτῆς </p>
 <p>6 ἥτις οὐκ εἰσακούσεται φωνὴν ἐπᾳδόντων φαρμάκου τε φαρμακευομένου παρὰ σοφοῦ </p>
 <p>7 ὁ θεὸς συνέτριψεν τοὺς ὀδόντας αὐτῶν ἐν τῷ στόματι αὐτῶν τὰς μύλας τῶν λεόντων συνέθλασεν κύριος </p>
 <p>8 ἐξουδενωθήσονται ὡς ὕδωρ διαπορευόμενον ἐντενεῖ τὸ τόξον αὐτοῦ ἕως οὗ ἀσθενήσουσιν </p>
 <p>9 ὡσεὶ κηρὸς ὁ τακεὶς ἀνταναιρεθήσονται ἐπέπεσε πῦρ καὶ οὐκ εἶδον τὸν ἥλιον </p>
 <p>10 πρὸ τοῦ συνιέναι τὰς ἀκάνθας ὑμῶν τὴν ῥάμνον ὡσεὶ ζῶντας ὡσεὶ ἐν ὀργῇ καταπίεται ὑμᾶς </p>
 <p>11 εὐφρανθήσεται δίκαιος ὅταν ἴδῃ ἐκδίκησιν ἀσεβῶν τὰς χεῖρας αὐτοῦ νίψεται ἐν τῷ αἵματι τοῦ ἁμαρτωλοῦ </p>
 <p>12 καὶ ἐρεῖ ἄνθρωπος εἰ ἄρα ἔστιν καρπὸς τῷ δικαίῳ ἄρα ἐστὶν ὁ θεὸς κρίνων αὐτοὺς ἐν τῇ γῇ </p>

 <p>3 Etenim in corde iniquitates operamini;<br> in terra injustitias manus vestræ concinnant.<br></p>
 <p>4 Alienati sunt peccatores a vulva;<br> erraverunt ab utero:<br> locuti sunt falsa.<br></p>
 <p>5 Furor illis secundum similitudinem serpentis,<br> sicut aspidis surdæ et obturantis aures suas,<br></p>
 <p>6 quæ non exaudiet vocem incantantium,<br> et venefici incantantis sapienter.<br></p>
 <p>7 Deus conteret dentes eorum in ore ipsorum;<br> molas leonum confringet Dominus.<br></p>
 <p>8 Ad nihilum devenient tamquam aqua decurrens;<br> intendit arcum suum donec infirmentur.<br></p>
 <p>9 Sicut cera quæ fluit auferentur;<br> supercecidit ignis, et non viderunt solem.<br></p>
 <p>10 Priusquam intelligerent spinæ vestræ rhamnum,<br> sicut viventes sic in ira absorbet eos.<br></p>
 <p>11 Lætabitur justus cum viderit vindictam;<br> manus suas lavabit in sanguine peccatoris.<br></p>
 <p>12 Et dicet homo: Si utique est fructus justo,<br> utique est Deus judicans eos in terra.]</p>
