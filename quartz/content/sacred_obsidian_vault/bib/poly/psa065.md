 <p align="center"><a href="../kjv/psa065.htm">KJV</a></p><p>1  To the chief Musician,<sup><a href="h5329.htm">5329</a></sup> A Psalm<sup><a href="h4210.htm">4210</a></sup> and Song<sup><a href="h7892.htm">7892</a></sup> of David.<sup><a href="h1732.htm">1732</a></sup> Praise<sup><a href="h8416.htm">8416</a></sup> waiteth<sup><a href="h1747.htm">1747</a></sup> for thee, O God,<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> in Zion:<sup><a href="h6726.htm">6726</a></sup> and unto thee shall the vow<sup><a href="h5088.htm">5088</a></sup> be performed.<sup><a href="h7999.htm">7999</a></sup> </p>
 <p>2  O thou that hearest<sup><a href="h8085.htm">8085</a></sup> prayer,<sup><a href="h8605.htm">8605</a></sup> unto<sup><a href="h5704.htm">5704</a></sup> thee shall all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> flesh<sup><a href="h1320.htm">1320</a></sup> come.<sup><a href="h0935.htm">935</a></sup> </p>
 <p>3  Iniquities<sup><a href="h1697.htm">1697</a></sup> <sup><a href="h5771.htm">5771</a></sup> prevail<sup><a href="h1396.htm">1396</a></sup> against<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> me: as for our transgressions,<sup><a href="h6588.htm">6588</a></sup> thou<sup><a href="h0859.htm">859</a></sup> shalt purge them away.<sup><a href="h3722.htm">3722</a></sup> </p>
 <p>4  Blessed<sup><a href="h0835.htm">835</a></sup> is the man whom thou choosest,<sup><a href="h0977.htm">977</a></sup> and causest to approach<sup><a href="h7126.htm">7126</a></sup> unto thee, that he may dwell<sup><a href="h7931.htm">7931</a></sup> in thy courts:<sup><a href="h2691.htm">2691</a></sup> we shall be satisfied<sup><a href="h7646.htm">7646</a></sup> with the goodness<sup><a href="h2898.htm">2898</a></sup> of thy house,<sup><a href="h1004.htm">1004</a></sup> even of thy holy<sup><a href="h6918.htm">6918</a></sup> temple.<sup><a href="h1964.htm">1964</a></sup> </p>
 <p>5  By terrible things<sup><a href="h3372.htm">3372</a></sup> in righteousness<sup><a href="h6664.htm">6664</a></sup> wilt thou answer<sup><a href="h6030.htm">6030</a></sup> us, O God<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> of our salvation;<sup><a href="h3468.htm">3468</a></sup> who art the confidence<sup><a href="h4009.htm">4009</a></sup> of all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> the ends<sup><a href="h7099.htm">7099</a></sup> of the earth,<sup><a href="h0776.htm">776</a></sup> and of them that are afar off<sup><a href="h7350.htm">7350</a></sup> upon the sea:<sup><a href="h3220.htm">3220</a></sup> </p>
 <p>6  Which by his strength<sup><a href="h3581.htm">3581</a></sup> setteth fast<sup><a href="h3559.htm">3559</a></sup> the mountains;<sup><a href="h2022.htm">2022</a></sup> being girded<sup><a href="h0247.htm">247</a></sup> with power:<sup><a href="h1369.htm">1369</a></sup> </p>
 <p>7  Which stilleth<sup><a href="h7623.htm">7623</a></sup> the noise<sup><a href="h7588.htm">7588</a></sup> of the seas,<sup><a href="h3220.htm">3220</a></sup> the noise<sup><a href="h7588.htm">7588</a></sup> of their waves,<sup><a href="h1530.htm">1530</a></sup> and the tumult<sup><a href="h1995.htm">1995</a></sup> of the people.<sup><a href="h3816.htm">3816</a></sup> </p>
 <p>8  They also that dwell<sup><a href="h3427.htm">3427</a></sup> in the uttermost parts<sup><a href="h7098.htm">7098</a></sup> are afraid<sup><a href="h3372.htm">3372</a></sup> at thy tokens:<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0226.htm">226</a></sup> thou makest the outgoings<sup><a href="h4161.htm">4161</a></sup> of the morning<sup><a href="h1242.htm">1242</a></sup> and evening<sup><a href="h6153.htm">6153</a></sup> to rejoice.<sup><a href="h7442.htm">7442</a></sup> </p>
 <p>9  Thou visitest<sup><a href="h6485.htm">6485</a></sup> the earth,<sup><a href="h0776.htm">776</a></sup> and waterest<sup><a href="h7783.htm">7783</a></sup> it: thou greatly<sup><a href="h7227.htm">7227</a></sup> enrichest<sup><a href="h6238.htm">6238</a></sup> it with the river<sup><a href="h6388.htm">6388</a></sup> of God,<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> which is full<sup><a href="h4390.htm">4390</a></sup> of water:<sup><a href="h4325.htm">4325</a></sup> thou preparest<sup><a href="h3559.htm">3559</a></sup> them corn,<sup><a href="h1715.htm">1715</a></sup> when<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> thou hast so<sup><a href="h3651.htm">3651</a></sup> provided<sup><a href="h3559.htm">3559</a></sup> for it. </p>
 <p>10  Thou waterest the ridges<sup><a href="h8525.htm">8525</a></sup> thereof abundantly:<sup><a href="h7301.htm">7301</a></sup> thou settlest<sup><a href="h5181.htm">5181</a></sup> the furrows<sup><a href="h1417.htm">1417</a></sup> thereof: thou makest it soft<sup><a href="h4127.htm">4127</a></sup> with showers:<sup><a href="h7241.htm">7241</a></sup> thou blessest<sup><a href="h1288.htm">1288</a></sup> the springing<sup><a href="h6780.htm">6780</a></sup> thereof. </p>
 <p>11  Thou crownest<sup><a href="h5849.htm">5849</a></sup> the year<sup><a href="h8141.htm">8141</a></sup> with thy goodness;<sup><a href="h2896.htm">2896</a></sup> and thy paths<sup><a href="h4570.htm">4570</a></sup> drop<sup><a href="h7491.htm">7491</a></sup> fatness.<sup><a href="h1880.htm">1880</a></sup> </p>
 <p>12  They drop<sup><a href="h7491.htm">7491</a></sup> upon the pastures<sup><a href="h4999.htm">4999</a></sup> of the wilderness:<sup><a href="h4057.htm">4057</a></sup> and the little hills<sup><a href="h1389.htm">1389</a></sup> rejoice<sup><a href="h1524.htm">1524</a></sup> on every side.<sup><a href="h2296.htm">2296</a></sup> </p>
 <p>13  The pastures<sup><a href="h3733.htm">3733</a></sup> are clothed<sup><a href="h3847.htm">3847</a></sup> with flocks;<sup><a href="h6629.htm">6629</a></sup> the valleys<sup><a href="h6010.htm">6010</a></sup> also are covered over<sup><a href="h5848.htm">5848</a></sup> with corn;<sup><a href="h1250.htm">1250</a></sup> they shout for joy,<sup><a href="h7321.htm">7321</a></sup> they also<sup><a href="h0637.htm">637</a></sup> sing.<sup><a href="h7891.htm">7891</a></sup> </p>

 <p align="center"><a href="../sep/psa065.htm">Sep</a></p><p>1 εἰς τὸ τέλος ᾠδὴ ψαλμοῦ ἀναστάσεως ἀλαλάξατε τῷ θεῷ πᾶσα ἡ γῆ </p>
 <p>2 ψάλατε δὴ τῷ ὀνόματι αὐτοῦ δότε δόξαν αἰνέσει αὐτοῦ </p>
 <p>3 εἴπατε τῷ θεῷ ὡς φοβερὰ τὰ ἔργα σου ἐν τῷ πλήθει τῆς δυνάμεώς σου ψεύσονταί σε οἱ ἐχθροί σου </p>
 <p>4 πᾶσα ἡ γῆ προσκυνησάτωσάν σοι καὶ ψαλάτωσάν σοι ψαλάτωσαν τῷ ὀνόματί σου διάψαλμα </p>
 <p>5 δεῦτε καὶ ἴδετε τὰ ἔργα τοῦ θεοῦ φοβερὸς ἐν βουλαῖς ὑπὲρ τοὺς υἱοὺς τῶν ἀνθρώπων </p>
 <p>6 ὁ μεταστρέφων τὴν θάλασσαν εἰς ξηράν ἐν ποταμῷ διελεύσονται ποδί ἐκεῖ εὐφρανθησόμεθα ἐπ᾽ αὐτῷ </p>
 <p>7 τῷ δεσπόζοντι ἐν τῇ δυναστείᾳ αὐτοῦ τοῦ αἰῶνος οἱ ὀφθαλμοὶ αὐτοῦ ἐπὶ τὰ ἔθνη ἐπιβλέπουσιν οἱ παραπικραίνοντες μὴ ὑψούσθωσαν ἐν ἑαυτοῖς διάψαλμα </p>
 <p>8 εὐλογεῖτε ἔθνη τὸν θεὸν ἡμῶν καὶ ἀκουτίσασθε τὴν φωνὴν τῆς αἰνέσεως αὐτοῦ </p>
 <p>9 τοῦ θεμένου τὴν ψυχήν μου εἰς ζωὴν καὶ μὴ δόντος εἰς σάλον τοὺς πόδας μου </p>
 <p>10 ὅτι ἐδοκίμασας ἡμᾶς ὁ θεός ἐπύρωσας ἡμᾶς ὡς πυροῦται τὸ ἀργύριον </p>
 <p>11 εἰσήγαγες ἡμᾶς εἰς τὴν παγίδα ἔθου θλίψεις ἐπὶ τὸν νῶτον ἡμῶν </p>
 <p>12 ἐπεβίβασας ἀνθρώπους ἐπὶ τὰς κεφαλὰς ἡμῶν διήλθομεν διὰ πυρὸς καὶ ὕδατος καὶ ἐξήγαγες ἡμᾶς εἰς ἀναψυχήν </p>
 <p>13 εἰσελεύσομαι εἰς τὸν οἶκόν σου ἐν ὁλοκαυτώμασιν ἀποδώσω σοι τὰς εὐχάς μου </p>
 <p>14 ἃς διέστειλεν τὰ χείλη μου καὶ ἐλάλησεν τὸ στόμα μου ἐν τῇ θλίψει μου </p>
 <p>15 ὁλοκαυτώματα μεμυαλωμένα ἀνοίσω σοι μετὰ θυμιάματος καὶ κριῶν ποιήσω σοι βόας μετὰ χιμάρων διάψαλμα </p>
 <p>16 δεῦτε ἀκούσατε καὶ διηγήσομαι πάντες οἱ φοβούμενοι τὸν θεόν ὅσα ἐποίησεν τῇ ψυχῇ μου </p>
 <p>17 πρὸς αὐτὸν τῷ στόματί μου ἐκέκραξα καὶ ὕψωσα ὑπὸ τὴν γλῶσσάν μου </p>
 <p>18 ἀδικίαν εἰ ἐθεώρουν ἐν καρδίᾳ μου μὴ εἰσακουσάτω κύριος </p>
 <p>19 διὰ τοῦτο εἰσήκουσέν μου ὁ θεός προσέσχεν τῇ φωνῇ τῆς δεήσεώς μου </p>
 <p>20 εὐλογητὸς ὁ θεός ὃς οὐκ ἀπέστησεν τὴν προσευχήν μου καὶ τὸ ἔλεος αὐτοῦ ἀπ᾽ ἐμοῦ </p>

 <p align="center"><a href="../vul/psa065.htm">Vul</a></p><p>1 In finem. Canticum psalmi resurrectionis. [Jubilate Deo, omnis terra;<br></p>
 <p>2 psalmum dicite nomini ejus;<br> date gloriam laudi ejus.<br></p>
 <p>3 Dicite Deo: Quam terribilia sunt opera tua, Domine!<br> in multitudine virtutis tuæ mentientur tibi inimici tui.<br></p>
 <p>4 Omnis terra adoret te, et psallat tibi;<br> psalmum dicat nomini tuo.<br></p>
 <p>5 Venite, et videte opera Dei:<br> terribilis in consiliis super filios hominum.<br></p>
 <p>6 Qui convertit mare in aridam;<br> in flumine pertransibunt pede:<br> ibi lætabimur in ipso.<br></p>
 <p>7 Qui dominatur in virtute sua in æternum;<br> oculi ejus super gentes respiciunt:<br> qui exasperant non exaltentur in semetipsis.<br></p>
 <p>8 Benedicite, gentes, Deum nostrum,<br> et auditam facite vocem laudis ejus:<br></p>
 <p>9 qui posuit animam meam ad vitam,<br> et non dedit in commotionem pedes meos.<br></p>
 <p>10 Quoniam probasti nos, Deus;<br> igne nos examinasti, sicut examinatur argentum.<br></p>
 <p>11 Induxisti nos in laqueum;<br> posuisti tribulationes in dorso nostro;<br></p>
 <p>12 imposuisti homines super capita nostra.<br> Transivimus per ignem et aquam,<br> et eduxisti nos in refrigerium.<br></p>
 <p>13 Introibo in domum tuam in holocaustis;<br> reddam tibi vota mea</p>
 <p>14 quæ distinxerunt labia mea:<br> et locutum est os meum in tribulatione mea.<br></p>
 <p>15 Holocausta medullata offeram tibi, cum incenso arietum;<br> offeram tibi boves cum hircis.<br></p>
 <p>16 Venite, audite, et narrabo, omnes qui timetis Deum,<br> quanta fecit animæ meæ.<br></p>
 <p>17 Ad ipsum ore meo clamavi,<br> et exaltavi sub lingua mea.<br></p>
 <p>18 Iniquitatem si aspexi in corde meo,<br> non exaudiet Dominus.<br></p>
 <p>19 Propterea exaudivit Deus,<br> et attendit voci deprecationis meæ.<br></p>
 <p>20 Benedictus Deus, qui non amovit orationem meam,<br> et misericordiam suam a me.]</p>
