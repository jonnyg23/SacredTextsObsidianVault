 <p>9 <ghimel>Conclusit vias meas lapidibus quadris;<br> semitas meas subvertit.<br></ghimel></p>

 <p>11 <daleth>Semitas meas subvertit, et confregit me;<br> posuit me desolatam.<br></daleth></p>
