 <p align="center"><a href="../kjv/psa058.htm">KJV</a></p><p>1  To the chief Musician,<sup><a href="h5329.htm">5329</a></sup> Al-taschith,<sup><a href="h0516.htm">516</a></sup> Michtam<sup><a href="h4387.htm">4387</a></sup> of David.<sup><a href="h1732.htm">1732</a></sup> Do ye indeed<sup><a href="h0552.htm">552</a></sup> speak<sup><a href="h1696.htm">1696</a></sup> righteousness,<sup><a href="h6664.htm">6664</a></sup> O congregation?<sup><a href="h0482.htm">482</a></sup> do ye judge<sup><a href="h8199.htm">8199</a></sup> uprightly,<sup><a href="h4339.htm">4339</a></sup> O ye sons<sup><a href="h1121.htm">1121</a></sup> of men?<sup><a href="h0120.htm">120</a></sup> </p>
 <p>2  Yea,<sup><a href="h0637.htm">637</a></sup> in heart<sup><a href="h3820.htm">3820</a></sup> ye work<sup><a href="h6466.htm">6466</a></sup> wickedness;<sup><a href="h5766.htm">5766</a></sup> ye weigh<sup><a href="h6424.htm">6424</a></sup> the violence<sup><a href="h2555.htm">2555</a></sup> of your hands<sup><a href="h3027.htm">3027</a></sup> in the earth.<sup><a href="h0776.htm">776</a></sup> </p>
 <p>3  The wicked<sup><a href="h7563.htm">7563</a></sup> are estranged<sup><a href="h2114.htm">2114</a></sup> from the womb:<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h7358.htm">7358</a></sup> they go astray<sup><a href="h8582.htm">8582</a></sup> as soon as they be born,<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0990.htm">990</a></sup> speaking<sup><a href="h1696.htm">1696</a></sup> lies.<sup><a href="h3577.htm">3577</a></sup> </p>
 <p>4  Their poison<sup><a href="h2534.htm">2534</a></sup> is like<sup><a href="h1823.htm">1823</a></sup> the poison<sup><a href="h2534.htm">2534</a></sup> of a serpent:<sup><a href="h5175.htm">5175</a></sup> they are like<sup><a href="h3644.htm">3644</a></sup> the deaf<sup><a href="h2795.htm">2795</a></sup> adder<sup><a href="h6620.htm">6620</a></sup> that stoppeth<sup><a href="h0331.htm">331</a></sup> her ear;<sup><a href="h0241.htm">241</a></sup> </p>
 <p>5  Which<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> will not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> hearken<sup><a href="h8085.htm">8085</a></sup> to the voice<sup><a href="h6963.htm">6963</a></sup> of charmers,<sup><a href="h3907.htm">3907</a></sup> charming<sup><a href="h2266.htm">2266</a></sup> <sup><a href="h2267.htm">2267</a></sup> never so wisely.<sup><a href="h2449.htm">2449</a></sup> </p>
 <p>6  Break<sup><a href="h2040.htm">2040</a></sup> their teeth,<sup><a href="h8127.htm">8127</a></sup> O God,<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> in their mouth:<sup><a href="h6310.htm">6310</a></sup> break out<sup><a href="h5422.htm">5422</a></sup> the great teeth<sup><a href="h4459.htm">4459</a></sup> of the young lions,<sup><a href="h3715.htm">3715</a></sup> O LORD.<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> </p>
 <p>7  Let them melt away<sup><a href="h3988.htm">3988</a></sup> as<sup><a href="h3644.htm">3644</a></sup> waters<sup><a href="h4325.htm">4325</a></sup> which run<sup><a href="h1980.htm">1980</a></sup> continually: when he bendeth<sup><a href="h1869.htm">1869</a></sup> his bow to shoot his arrows,<sup><a href="h2671.htm">2671</a></sup> let them be as<sup><a href="h3644.htm">3644</a></sup> cut in pieces.<sup><a href="h4135.htm">4135</a></sup> </p>
 <p>8  As<sup><a href="h3644.htm">3644</a></sup> a snail<sup><a href="h7642.htm">7642</a></sup> which melteth,<sup><a href="h8557.htm">8557</a></sup> let every one of them pass away:<sup><a href="h1980.htm">1980</a></sup> like the untimely birth<sup><a href="h5309.htm">5309</a></sup> of a woman,<sup><a href="h0802.htm">802</a></sup> that they may not<sup><a href="h1077.htm">1077</a></sup> see<sup><a href="h2372.htm">2372</a></sup> the sun.<sup><a href="h8121.htm">8121</a></sup> </p>
 <p>9  Before<sup><a href="h2962.htm">2962</a></sup> your pots<sup><a href="h5518.htm">5518</a></sup> can feel<sup><a href="h0995.htm">995</a></sup> the thorns,<sup><a href="h0329.htm">329</a></sup> he shall take them away as with a whirlwind,<sup><a href="h8175.htm">8175</a></sup> both<sup><a href="h3644.htm">3644</a></sup> living,<sup><a href="h2416.htm">2416</a></sup> and in<sup><a href="h3644.htm">3644</a></sup> his wrath.<sup><a href="h2740.htm">2740</a></sup> </p>
 <p>10  The righteous<sup><a href="h6662.htm">6662</a></sup> shall rejoice<sup><a href="h8055.htm">8055</a></sup> when<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> he seeth<sup><a href="h2372.htm">2372</a></sup> the vengeance:<sup><a href="h5359.htm">5359</a></sup> he shall wash<sup><a href="h7364.htm">7364</a></sup> his feet<sup><a href="h6471.htm">6471</a></sup> in the blood<sup><a href="h1818.htm">1818</a></sup> of the wicked.<sup><a href="h7563.htm">7563</a></sup> </p>
 <p>11  So that a man<sup><a href="h0120.htm">120</a></sup> shall say,<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> Verily<sup><a href="h0389.htm">389</a></sup> there is a reward<sup><a href="h6529.htm">6529</a></sup> for the righteous:<sup><a href="h6662.htm">6662</a></sup> verily<sup><a href="h0389.htm">389</a></sup> he is<sup><a href="h3426.htm">3426</a></sup> a God<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> that judgeth<sup><a href="h8199.htm">8199</a></sup> in the earth.<sup><a href="h0776.htm">776</a></sup> </p>

 <p align="center"><a href="../sep/psa058.htm">Sep</a></p><p>1 εἰς τὸ τέλος μὴ διαφθείρῃς τῷ Δαυιδ εἰς στηλογραφίαν ὁπότε ἀπέστειλεν Σαουλ καὶ ἐφύλαξεν τὸν οἶκον αὐτοῦ τοῦ θανατῶσαι αὐτόν </p>
 <p>2 ἐξελοῦ με ἐκ τῶν ἐχθρῶν μου ὁ θεός καὶ ἐκ τῶν ἐπανιστανομένων ἐπ᾽ ἐμὲ λύτρωσαί με </p>
 <p>3 ῥῦσαί με ἐκ τῶν ἐργαζομένων τὴν ἀνομίαν καὶ ἐξ ἀνδρῶν αἱμάτων σῶσόν με </p>
 <p>4 ὅτι ἰδοὺ ἐθήρευσαν τὴν ψυχήν μου ἐπέθεντο ἐπ᾽ ἐμὲ κραταιοί οὔτε ἡ ἀνομία μου οὔτε ἡ ἁμαρτία μου κύριε </p>
 <p>5 ἄνευ ἀνομίας ἔδραμον καὶ κατεύθυναν ἐξεγέρθητι εἰς συνάντησίν μου καὶ ἰδέ </p>
 <p>6 καὶ σύ κύριε ὁ θεὸς τῶν δυνάμεων ὁ θεὸς Ισραηλ πρόσχες τοῦ ἐπισκέψασθαι πάντα τὰ ἔθνη μὴ οἰκτιρήσῃς πάντας τοὺς ἐργαζομένους τὴν ἀνομίαν διάψαλμα </p>
 <p>7 ἐπιστρέψουσιν εἰς ἑσπέραν καὶ λιμώξουσιν ὡς κύων καὶ κυκλώσουσιν πόλιν </p>
 <p>8 ἰδοὺ ἀποφθέγξονται ἐν τῷ στόματι αὐτῶν καὶ ῥομφαία ἐν τοῖς χείλεσιν αὐτῶν ὅτι τίς ἤκουσεν </p>
 <p>9 καὶ σύ κύριε ἐκγελάσῃ αὐτούς ἐξουδενώσεις πάντα τὰ ἔθνη </p>
 <p>10 τὸ κράτος μου πρὸς σὲ φυλάξω ὅτι ὁ θεὸς ἀντιλήμπτωρ μου εἶ </p>
 <p>11 ὁ θεός μου τὸ ἔλεος αὐτοῦ προφθάσει με ὁ θεὸς δείξει μοι ἐν τοῖς ἐχθροῖς μου </p>
 <p>12 μὴ ἀποκτείνῃς αὐτούς μήποτε ἐπιλάθωνται τοῦ λαοῦ μου διασκόρπισον αὐτοὺς ἐν τῇ δυνάμει σου καὶ κατάγαγε αὐτούς ὁ ὑπερασπιστής μου κύριε </p>
 <p>13 ἁμαρτίαν στόματος αὐτῶν λόγον χειλέων αὐτῶν καὶ συλλημφθήτωσαν ἐν τῇ ὑπερηφανίᾳ αὐτῶν καὶ ἐξ ἀρᾶς καὶ ψεύδους διαγγελήσονται συντέλειαι </p>
 <p>14 ἐν ὀργῇ συντελείας καὶ οὐ μὴ ὑπάρξωσιν καὶ γνώσονται ὅτι ὁ θεὸς δεσπόζει τοῦ Ιακωβ τῶν περάτων τῆς γῆς διάψαλμα </p>
 <p>15 ἐπιστρέψουσιν εἰς ἑσπέραν καὶ λιμώξουσιν ὡς κύων καὶ κυκλώσουσιν πόλιν </p>
 <p>16 αὐτοὶ διασκορπισθήσονται τοῦ φαγεῖν ἐὰν δὲ μὴ χορτασθῶσιν καὶ γογγύσουσιν </p>
 <p>17 ἐγὼ δὲ ᾄσομαι τῇ δυνάμει σου καὶ ἀγαλλιάσομαι τὸ πρωὶ τὸ ἔλεός σου ὅτι ἐγενήθης ἀντιλήμπτωρ μου καὶ καταφυγὴ ἐν ἡμέρᾳ θλίψεώς μου </p>
 <p>18 βοηθός μου σοὶ ψαλῶ ὅτι ὁ θεός ἀντιλήμπτωρ μου εἶ ὁ θεός μου τὸ ἔλεός μου </p>

 <p align="center"><a href="../vul/psa058.htm">Vul</a></p><p>1 In finem, ne disperdas. David in tituli inscriptionem, quando misit Saul et custodivit domum ejus ut eum interficeret.</p>
 <p>2 [Eripe me de inimicis meis, Deus meus,<br> et ab insurgentibus in me libera me.<br></p>
 <p>3 Eripe me de operantibus iniquitatem,<br> et de viris sanguinum salva me.<br></p>
 <p>4 Quia ecce ceperunt animam meam;<br> irruerunt in me fortes.<br></p>
 <p>5 Neque iniquitas mea, neque peccatum meum, Domine;<br> sine iniquitate cucurri, et direxi.<br></p>
 <p>6 Exsurge in occursum meum, et vide:<br> et tu, Domine Deus virtutum, Deus Israël,<br> intende ad visitandas omnes gentes:<br> non miserearis omnibus qui operantur iniquitatem.<br></p>
 <p>7 Convertentur ad vesperam, et famem patientur ut canes:<br> et circuibunt civitatem.<br></p>
 <p>8 Ecce loquentur in ore suo,<br> et gladius in labiis eorum: quoniam quis audivit?<br></p>
 <p>9 Et tu, Domine, deridebis eos;<br> ad nihilum deduces omnes gentes.<br></p>
 <p>10 Fortitudinem meam ad te custodiam,<br> quia, Deus, susceptor meus es:<br></p>
 <p>11 Deus meus misericordia ejus præveniet me.<br></p>
 <p>12 Deus ostendet mihi super inimicos meos:<br> ne occidas eos, nequando obliviscantur populi mei.<br> Disperge illos in virtute tua,<br> et depone eos, protector meus, Domine:<br></p>
 <p>13 delictum oris eorum, sermonem labiorum ipsorum;<br> et comprehendantur in superbia sua.<br> Et de execratione et mendacio annuntiabuntur</p>
 <p>14 in consummatione:<br> in ira consummationis, et non erunt.<br> Et scient quia Deus dominabitur Jacob, et finium terræ.<br></p>
 <p>15 Convertentur ad vesperam, et famem patientur ut canes:<br> et circuibunt civitatem.</p>
 <p>16 Ipsi dispergentur ad manducandum;<br> si vero non fuerint saturati, et murmurabunt.<br></p>
 <p>17 Ego autem cantabo fortitudinem tuam,<br> et exsultabo mane misericordiam tuam:<br> quia factus es susceptor meus,<br> et refugium meum in die tribulationis meæ.<br></p>
 <p>18 Adjutor meus, tibi psallam,<br> quia Deus susceptor meus es;<br> Deus meus, misericordia mea.]</p>
