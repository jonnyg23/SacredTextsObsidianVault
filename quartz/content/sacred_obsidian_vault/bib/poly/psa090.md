 <p>7  For<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> we are consumed<sup><a href="h3615.htm">3615</a></sup> by thine anger,<sup><a href="h0639.htm">639</a></sup> and by thy wrath<sup><a href="h2534.htm">2534</a></sup> are we troubled.<sup><a href="h0926.htm">926</a></sup> </p>
 <p>8  Thou hast set<sup><a href="h7896.htm">7896</a></sup> our iniquities<sup><a href="h5771.htm">5771</a></sup> before<sup><a href="h5048.htm">5048</a></sup> thee, our secret<sup><a href="h5956.htm">5956</a></sup> sins in the light<sup><a href="h3974.htm">3974</a></sup> of thy countenance.<sup><a href="h6440.htm">6440</a></sup> </p>
 <p>9  For<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> our days<sup><a href="h3117.htm">3117</a></sup> are passed away<sup><a href="h6437.htm">6437</a></sup> in thy wrath:<sup><a href="h5678.htm">5678</a></sup> we spend<sup><a href="h3615.htm">3615</a></sup> our years<sup><a href="h8141.htm">8141</a></sup> as<sup><a href="h3644.htm">3644</a></sup> a tale<sup><a href="h1899.htm">1899</a></sup> that is told. </p>

 <p>11  Who<sup><a href="h4310.htm">4310</a></sup> knoweth<sup><a href="h3045.htm">3045</a></sup> the power<sup><a href="h5797.htm">5797</a></sup> of thine anger?<sup><a href="h0639.htm">639</a></sup> even according to thy fear,<sup><a href="h3374.htm">3374</a></sup> so is thy wrath.<sup><a href="h5678.htm">5678</a></sup> </p>

 <p align="center"><a href="../vul/psa090.htm">Vul</a></p><p>1 Laus cantici David. [Qui habitat in adjutorio Altissimi,<br> in protectione Dei cæli commorabitur.<br></p>
 <p>2 Dicet Domino: Susceptor meus es tu, et refugium meum;<br> Deus meus, sperabo in eum.<br></p>
 <p>3 Quoniam ipse liberavit me de laqueo venantium,<br> et a verbo aspero.<br></p>
 <p>4 Scapulis suis obumbrabit tibi,<br> et sub pennis ejus sperabis.<br></p>
 <p>5 Scuto circumdabit te veritas ejus:<br> non timebis a timore nocturno;<br></p>
 <p>6 a sagitta volante in die,<br> a negotio perambulante in tenebris,<br> ab incursu, et dæmonio meridiano.<br></p>
 <p>7 Cadent a latere tuo mille, et decem millia a dextris tuis;<br> ad te autem non appropinquabit.<br></p>
 <p>8 Verumtamen oculis tuis considerabis,<br> et retributionem peccatorum videbis.<br></p>
 <p>9 Quoniam tu es, Domine, spes mea;<br> Altissimum posuisti refugium tuum.<br></p>
 <p>10 Non accedet ad te malum,<br> et flagellum non appropinquabit tabernaculo tuo.<br></p>
 <p>11 Quoniam angelis suis mandavit de te,<br> ut custodiant te in omnibus viis tuis.<br></p>
 <p>12 In manibus portabunt te,<br> ne forte offendas ad lapidem pedem tuum.<br></p>
 <p>13 Super aspidem et basiliscum ambulabis,<br> et conculcabis leonem et draconem.<br></p>
 <p>14 Quoniam in me speravit, liberabo eum;<br> protegam eum, quoniam cognovit nomen meum.<br></p>
 <p>15 Clamabit ad me, et ego exaudiam eum;<br> cum ipso sum in tribulatione:<br> eripiam eum, et glorificabo eum.<br></p>
 <p>16 Longitudine dierum replebo eum,<br> et ostendam illi salutare meum.]</p>
