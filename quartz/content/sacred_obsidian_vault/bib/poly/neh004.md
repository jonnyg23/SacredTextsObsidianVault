 <p align="center"><a href="../kjv/neh004.htm">KJV</a></p><p>1  But it came to pass,<sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> that when<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> Sanballat<sup><a href="h5571.htm">5571</a></sup> heard<sup><a href="h8085.htm">8085</a></sup> that<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> we<sup><a href="h0587.htm">587</a></sup> built<sup><a href="h1129.htm">1129</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> the wall,<sup><a href="h2346.htm">2346</a></sup> he was wroth,<sup><a href="h2734.htm">2734</a></sup> and took great indignation,<sup><a href="h7235.htm">7235</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3707.htm">3707</a></sup> and mocked<sup><a href="h3932.htm">3932</a></sup> <sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> the Jews.<sup><a href="h3064.htm">3064</a></sup> </p>
 <p>2  And he spoke<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> before<sup><a href="h6440.htm">6440</a></sup> his brethren<sup><a href="h0251.htm">251</a></sup> and the army<sup><a href="h2428.htm">2428</a></sup> of Samaria,<sup><a href="h8111.htm">8111</a></sup> and said,<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> What<sup><a href="h4100.htm">4100</a></sup> do<sup><a href="h6213.htm">6213</a></sup> these feeble<sup><a href="h0537.htm">537</a></sup> Jews?<sup><a href="h3064.htm">3064</a></sup> will they fortify<sup><a href="h5800.htm">5800</a></sup> themselves? will they sacrifice?<sup><a href="h2076.htm">2076</a></sup> will they make an end<sup><a href="h3615.htm">3615</a></sup> in a day?<sup><a href="h3117.htm">3117</a></sup> will they revive<sup><a href="h2421.htm">2421</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> the stones<sup><a href="h0068.htm">68</a></sup> out of the heaps<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h6194.htm">6194</a></sup> of the rubbish<sup><a href="h6083.htm">6083</a></sup> which<sup><a href="h1992.htm">1992</a></sup> are burned?<sup><a href="h8313.htm">8313</a></sup> </p>
 <p>3  Now Tobiah<sup><a href="h2900.htm">2900</a></sup> the Ammonite<sup><a href="h5984.htm">5984</a></sup> was by<sup><a href="h0681.htm">681</a></sup> him, and he said,<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> Even<sup><a href="h1571.htm">1571</a></sup> that which<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> they<sup><a href="h1992.htm">1992</a></sup> build,<sup><a href="h1129.htm">1129</a></sup> if<sup><a href="h0518.htm">518</a></sup> a fox<sup><a href="h7776.htm">7776</a></sup> go up,<sup><a href="h5927.htm">5927</a></sup> he shall even break down<sup><a href="h6555.htm">6555</a></sup> their stone<sup><a href="h0068.htm">68</a></sup> wall.<sup><a href="h2346.htm">2346</a></sup> </p>
 <p>4  Hear,<sup><a href="h8085.htm">8085</a></sup> O our God;<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> for<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> we are<sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> despised:<sup><a href="h0939.htm">939</a></sup> and turn<sup><a href="h7725.htm">7725</a></sup> their reproach<sup><a href="h2781.htm">2781</a></sup> upon<sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> their own head,<sup><a href="h7218.htm">7218</a></sup> and give<sup><a href="h5414.htm">5414</a></sup> them for a prey<sup><a href="h0961.htm">961</a></sup> in the land<sup><a href="h0776.htm">776</a></sup> of captivity:<sup><a href="h7633.htm">7633</a></sup> </p>
 <p>5  And cover<sup><a href="h3680.htm">3680</a></sup> <sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> not<sup><a href="h0408.htm">408</a></sup> their iniquity,<sup><a href="h5771.htm">5771</a></sup> and let not<sup><a href="h0408.htm">408</a></sup> their sin<sup><a href="h2403.htm">2403</a></sup> be blotted out<sup><a href="h4229.htm">4229</a></sup> from before<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h6440.htm">6440</a></sup> thee: for<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> they have provoked thee to anger<sup><a href="h3707.htm">3707</a></sup> before<sup><a href="h5048.htm">5048</a></sup> the builders.<sup><a href="h1129.htm">1129</a></sup> </p>
 <p>6  So built<sup><a href="h1129.htm">1129</a></sup> we <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> the wall;<sup><a href="h2346.htm">2346</a></sup> and all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> the wall<sup><a href="h2346.htm">2346</a></sup> was joined together<sup><a href="h7194.htm">7194</a></sup> unto<sup><a href="h5704.htm">5704</a></sup> the half<sup><a href="h2677.htm">2677</a></sup> thereof: for the people<sup><a href="h5971.htm">5971</a></sup> had<sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> a mind<sup><a href="h3820.htm">3820</a></sup> to work.<sup><a href="h6213.htm">6213</a></sup> </p>
 <p>7  But it came to pass,<sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> that when<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> Sanballat,<sup><a href="h5571.htm">5571</a></sup> and Tobiah,<sup><a href="h2900.htm">2900</a></sup> and the Arabians,<sup><a href="h6163.htm">6163</a></sup> and the Ammonites,<sup><a href="h5984.htm">5984</a></sup> and the Ashdodites,<sup><a href="h0796.htm">796</a></sup> heard<sup><a href="h8085.htm">8085</a></sup> that<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> the walls<sup><a href="h2346.htm">2346</a></sup> of Jerusalem<sup><a href="h3389.htm">3389</a></sup> were made up,<sup><a href="h5927.htm">5927</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0724.htm">724</a></sup> and that<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> the breaches<sup><a href="h6555.htm">6555</a></sup> began<sup><a href="h2490.htm">2490</a></sup> to be stopped,<sup><a href="h5640.htm">5640</a></sup> then they were very<sup><a href="h3966.htm">3966</a></sup> wroth,<sup><a href="h2734.htm">2734</a></sup> </p>
 <p>8  And conspired<sup><a href="h7194.htm">7194</a></sup> all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> of them together<sup><a href="h3162.htm">3162</a></sup> to come<sup><a href="h0935.htm">935</a></sup> and to fight<sup><a href="h3898.htm">3898</a></sup> against Jerusalem,<sup><a href="h3389.htm">3389</a></sup> and to hinder<sup><a href="h6213.htm">6213</a></sup> <sup><a href="h8442.htm">8442</a></sup> it. </p>
 <p>9  Nevertheless we made our prayer<sup><a href="h6419.htm">6419</a></sup> unto<sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> our God,<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> and set<sup><a href="h5975.htm">5975</a></sup> a watch<sup><a href="h4929.htm">4929</a></sup> against<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> them day<sup><a href="h3119.htm">3119</a></sup> and night,<sup><a href="h3915.htm">3915</a></sup> because of<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h6440.htm">6440</a></sup> them. </p>
 <p>10  And Judah<sup><a href="h3063.htm">3063</a></sup> said,<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> The strength<sup><a href="h3581.htm">3581</a></sup> of the bearers of burdens<sup><a href="h5449.htm">5449</a></sup> is decayed,<sup><a href="h3782.htm">3782</a></sup> and there is much<sup><a href="h7235.htm">7235</a></sup> rubbish;<sup><a href="h6083.htm">6083</a></sup> so that we<sup><a href="h0587.htm">587</a></sup> are not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> able<sup><a href="h3201.htm">3201</a></sup> to build<sup><a href="h1129.htm">1129</a></sup> the wall.<sup><a href="h2346.htm">2346</a></sup> </p>
 <p>11  And our adversaries<sup><a href="h6862.htm">6862</a></sup> said,<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> They shall not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> know,<sup><a href="h3045.htm">3045</a></sup> neither<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> see,<sup><a href="h7200.htm">7200</a></sup> till<sup><a href="h5704.htm">5704</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> we come<sup><a href="h0935.htm">935</a></sup> in<sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> the midst among<sup><a href="h8432.htm">8432</a></sup> them, and slay<sup><a href="h2026.htm">2026</a></sup> them, and cause <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> the work<sup><a href="h4399.htm">4399</a></sup> to cease.<sup><a href="h7673.htm">7673</a></sup> </p>
 <p>12  And it came to pass,<sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> that when<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> the Jews<sup><a href="h3064.htm">3064</a></sup> which dwelt<sup><a href="h3427.htm">3427</a></sup> by<sup><a href="h0681.htm">681</a></sup> them came,<sup><a href="h0935.htm">935</a></sup> they said<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> unto us ten<sup><a href="h6235.htm">6235</a></sup> times,<sup><a href="h6471.htm">6471</a></sup> From all<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> places<sup><a href="h4725.htm">4725</a></sup> whence<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> ye shall return<sup><a href="h7725.htm">7725</a></sup> unto<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> us they will be upon you. </p>
 <p>13  Therefore set<sup><a href="h5975.htm">5975</a></sup> I in the lower<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h8482.htm">8482</a></sup> places<sup><a href="h4725.htm">4725</a></sup> behind<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0310.htm">310</a></sup> the wall,<sup><a href="h2346.htm">2346</a></sup> and on the higher places,<sup><a href="h6706.htm">6706</a></sup> I even set<sup><a href="h5975.htm">5975</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> the people<sup><a href="h5971.htm">5971</a></sup> after their families<sup><a href="h4940.htm">4940</a></sup> with<sup><a href="h5973.htm">5973</a></sup> their swords,<sup><a href="h2719.htm">2719</a></sup> their spears,<sup><a href="h7420.htm">7420</a></sup> and their bows.<sup><a href="h7198.htm">7198</a></sup> </p>
 <p>14  And I looked,<sup><a href="h7200.htm">7200</a></sup> and rose up,<sup><a href="h6965.htm">6965</a></sup> and said<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> unto<sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> the nobles,<sup><a href="h2715.htm">2715</a></sup> and to<sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> the rulers,<sup><a href="h5461.htm">5461</a></sup> and to<sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> the rest<sup><a href="h3499.htm">3499</a></sup> of the people,<sup><a href="h5971.htm">5971</a></sup> Be not<sup><a href="h0408.htm">408</a></sup> ye afraid<sup><a href="h3372.htm">3372</a></sup> of<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h6440.htm">6440</a></sup> them: remember<sup><a href="h2142.htm">2142</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> the Lord,<sup><a href="h0136.htm">136</a></sup> which is great<sup><a href="h1419.htm">1419</a></sup> and terrible,<sup><a href="h3372.htm">3372</a></sup> and fight<sup><a href="h3898.htm">3898</a></sup> for<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> your brethren,<sup><a href="h0251.htm">251</a></sup> your sons,<sup><a href="h1121.htm">1121</a></sup> and your daughters,<sup><a href="h1323.htm">1323</a></sup> your wives,<sup><a href="h0802.htm">802</a></sup> and your houses.<sup><a href="h1004.htm">1004</a></sup> </p>
 <p>15  And it came to pass,<sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> when<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> our enemies<sup><a href="h0341.htm">341</a></sup> heard<sup><a href="h8085.htm">8085</a></sup> that<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> it was known<sup><a href="h3045.htm">3045</a></sup> unto us, and God<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> had brought their counsel to naught,<sup><a href="h6565.htm">6565</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> <sup><a href="h6098.htm">6098</a></sup> that we returned<sup><a href="h7725.htm">7725</a></sup> all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> of us to<sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> the wall,<sup><a href="h2346.htm">2346</a></sup> every one<sup><a href="h0376.htm">376</a></sup> unto<sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> his work.<sup><a href="h4399.htm">4399</a></sup> </p>
 <p>16  And it came to pass<sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> from<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> that<sup><a href="h1931.htm">1931</a></sup> time<sup><a href="h3117.htm">3117</a></sup> forth, that the half<sup><a href="h2677.htm">2677</a></sup> of my servants<sup><a href="h5288.htm">5288</a></sup> wrought<sup><a href="h6213.htm">6213</a></sup> in the work,<sup><a href="h4399.htm">4399</a></sup> and the other half<sup><a href="h2677.htm">2677</a></sup> of them held<sup><a href="h2388.htm">2388</a></sup> both the spears,<sup><a href="h7420.htm">7420</a></sup> the shields,<sup><a href="h4043.htm">4043</a></sup> and the bows,<sup><a href="h7198.htm">7198</a></sup> and the habergeons;<sup><a href="h8302.htm">8302</a></sup> and the rulers<sup><a href="h8269.htm">8269</a></sup> were behind<sup><a href="h0310.htm">310</a></sup> all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> the house<sup><a href="h1004.htm">1004</a></sup> of Judah.<sup><a href="h3063.htm">3063</a></sup> </p>
 <p>17  They which built<sup><a href="h1129.htm">1129</a></sup> on the wall,<sup><a href="h2346.htm">2346</a></sup> and they that bore<sup><a href="h5375.htm">5375</a></sup> burdens,<sup><a href="h5447.htm">5447</a></sup> with those that laded,<sup><a href="h6006.htm">6006</a></sup> every one with one<sup><a href="h0259.htm">259</a></sup> of his hands<sup><a href="h3027.htm">3027</a></sup> wrought<sup><a href="h6213.htm">6213</a></sup> in the work,<sup><a href="h4399.htm">4399</a></sup> and with the other<sup><a href="h0259.htm">259</a></sup> hand held<sup><a href="h2388.htm">2388</a></sup> a weapon.<sup><a href="h7973.htm">7973</a></sup> </p>
 <p>18  For the builders,<sup><a href="h1129.htm">1129</a></sup> every one<sup><a href="h0376.htm">376</a></sup> had his sword<sup><a href="h2719.htm">2719</a></sup> girded<sup><a href="h0631.htm">631</a></sup> by<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> his side,<sup><a href="h4975.htm">4975</a></sup> and so built.<sup><a href="h1129.htm">1129</a></sup> And he that sounded<sup><a href="h8628.htm">8628</a></sup> the trumpet<sup><a href="h7782.htm">7782</a></sup> was by<sup><a href="h0681.htm">681</a></sup> me. </p>
 <p>19  And I said<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> unto<sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> the nobles,<sup><a href="h2715.htm">2715</a></sup> and to<sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> the rulers,<sup><a href="h5461.htm">5461</a></sup> and to<sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> the rest<sup><a href="h3499.htm">3499</a></sup> of the people,<sup><a href="h5971.htm">5971</a></sup> The work<sup><a href="h4399.htm">4399</a></sup> is great<sup><a href="h7235.htm">7235</a></sup> and large,<sup><a href="h7342.htm">7342</a></sup> and we<sup><a href="h0587.htm">587</a></sup> are separated<sup><a href="h6504.htm">6504</a></sup> upon<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> the wall,<sup><a href="h2346.htm">2346</a></sup> one<sup><a href="h0376.htm">376</a></sup> far<sup><a href="h7350.htm">7350</a></sup> from another.<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0251.htm">251</a></sup> </p>
 <p>20  In what<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> place<sup><a href="h4725.htm">4725</a></sup> therefore ye hear<sup><a href="h8085.htm">8085</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> the sound<sup><a href="h6963.htm">6963</a></sup> of the trumpet,<sup><a href="h7782.htm">7782</a></sup> resort<sup><a href="h6908.htm">6908</a></sup> ye thither<sup><a href="h8033.htm">8033</a></sup> unto<sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> us: our God<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> shall fight<sup><a href="h3898.htm">3898</a></sup> for us. </p>
 <p>21  So we<sup><a href="h0587.htm">587</a></sup> labored<sup><a href="h6213.htm">6213</a></sup> in the work:<sup><a href="h4399.htm">4399</a></sup> and half<sup><a href="h2677.htm">2677</a></sup> of them held<sup><a href="h2388.htm">2388</a></sup> the spears<sup><a href="h7420.htm">7420</a></sup> from the rising<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h5927.htm">5927</a></sup> of the morning<sup><a href="h7837.htm">7837</a></sup> till<sup><a href="h5704.htm">5704</a></sup> the stars<sup><a href="h3556.htm">3556</a></sup> appeared.<sup><a href="h3318.htm">3318</a></sup> </p>
 <p>22  Likewise<sup><a href="h1571.htm">1571</a></sup> at the same<sup><a href="h1931.htm">1931</a></sup> time<sup><a href="h6256.htm">6256</a></sup> said<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> I unto the people,<sup><a href="h5971.htm">5971</a></sup> Let every one<sup><a href="h0376.htm">376</a></sup> with his servant<sup><a href="h5288.htm">5288</a></sup> lodge<sup><a href="h3885.htm">3885</a></sup> within<sup><a href="h8432.htm">8432</a></sup> Jerusalem,<sup><a href="h3389.htm">3389</a></sup> that in the night<sup><a href="h3915.htm">3915</a></sup> they may be<sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> a guard<sup><a href="h4929.htm">4929</a></sup> to us, and labor<sup><a href="h4399.htm">4399</a></sup> on the day.<sup><a href="h3117.htm">3117</a></sup> </p>
 <p>23  So neither<sup><a href="h0369.htm">369</a></sup> I,<sup><a href="h0589.htm">589</a></sup> nor my brethren,<sup><a href="h0251.htm">251</a></sup> nor my servants,<sup><a href="h5288.htm">5288</a></sup> nor the men<sup><a href="h0376.htm">376</a></sup> of the guard<sup><a href="h4929.htm">4929</a></sup> which<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> followed<sup><a href="h0310.htm">310</a></sup> me, none<sup><a href="h0369.htm">369</a></sup> of us<sup><a href="h0587.htm">587</a></sup> put off<sup><a href="h6584.htm">6584</a></sup> our clothes,<sup><a href="h0899.htm">899</a></sup> saving that every one<sup><a href="h0376.htm">376</a></sup> put them off<sup><a href="h7971.htm">7971</a></sup> for washing.<sup><a href="h4325.htm">4325</a></sup> </p>

 <p align="center"><a href="../vul/neh004.htm">Vul</a></p><p>1 Factum est autem, cum audisset Sanaballat quod ædificaremus murum, iratus est valde: et motus nimis subsannavit Judæos,</p>
 <p>2 et dixit coram fratribus suis, et frequentia Samaritanorum: Quid Judæi faciunt imbecilles? num dimittent eos gentes? num sacrificabunt, et complebunt in una die? numquid ædificare poterunt lapides de acervis pulveris, qui combusti sunt?</p>
 <p>3 Sed et Tobias Ammanites, proximus ejus, ait: Ædificent: si ascenderit vulpes, transiliet murum eorum lapideum.</p>
 <p>4 Audi, Deus noster, quia facti sumus despectui: converte opprobrium super caput eorum, et da eos in despectionem in terra captivitatis.</p>
 <p>5 Ne operias iniquitatem eorum, et peccatum eorum coram facie tua non deleatur, quia irriserunt ædificantes.</p>
 <p>6 Itaque ædificavimus murum, et conjunximus totum usque ad partem dimidiam: et provocatum est cor populi ad operandum.</p>
 <p>7 Factum est autem, cum audisset Sanaballat, et Tobias, et Arabes, et Ammanitæ, et Azotii, quod obducta esset cicatrix muri Jerusalem, et quod cpissent interrupta concludi, irati sunt nimis.</p>
 <p>8 Et congregati sunt omnes pariter ut venirent, et pugnarent contra Jerusalem, et molirentur insidias.</p>
 <p>9 Et oravimus Deum nostrum, et posuimus custodes super murum die ac nocte contra eos.</p>
 <p>10 Dixit autem Judas: Debilitata est fortitudo portantis, et humus nimia est, et nos non poterimus ædificare murum.</p>
 <p>11 Et dixerunt hostes nostri: Nesciant, et ignorent donec veniamus in medium eorum, et interficiamus eos, et cessare faciamus opus.</p>
 <p>12 Factum est autem venientibus Judæis qui habitabant juxta eos, et dicentibus nobis per decem vices, ex omnibus locis quibus venerant ad nos,</p>
 <p>13 statui in loco post murum per circuitum populum in ordinem cum gladiis suis, et lanceis, et arcubus.</p>
 <p>14 Et perspexi atque surrexi: et aio ad optimates et magistratus, et ad reliquam partem vulgi: Nolite timere a facie eorum: Domini magni et terribilis mementote, et pugnate pro fratribus vestris, filiis vestris, et filiabus vestris, et uxoribus vestris, et domibus vestris.</p>
 <p>15 Factum est autem, cum audissent inimici nostri nuntiatum esse nobis, dissipavit Deus consilium eorum. Et reversi sumus omnes ad muros, unusquisque ad opus suum.</p>
 <p>16 Et factum est a die illa, media pars juvenum eorum faciebat opus, et media parata erat ad bellum: et lanceæ, et scuta, et arcus, et loricæ, et principes post eos in omni domo Juda.</p>
 <p>17 Ædificantium in muro, et portantium onera, et imponentium: una manu sua faciebat opus, et altera tenebat gladium:</p>
 <p>18 ædificentium enim unusquisque gladio erat accinctus renes. Et ædificabant, et clangebant buccina juxta me.</p>
 <p>19 Et dixi ad optimates, et ad magistratus, et ad reliquam partem vulgi: Opus grande est et latum, et nos separati sumus in muro procul alter ab altero:</p>
 <p>20 in loco quocumque audieritis clangorem tubæ, illuc concurrite ad nos: Deus noster pugnabit pro nobis.</p>
 <p>21 Et nos ipsi faciamus opus, et media pars nostrum teneat lanceas ab ascensu auroræ donec egrediantur astra.</p>
 <p>22 In tempore quoque illo dixi populo: Unusquisque cum puero suo maneat in medio Jerusalem, et sint nobis vices per noctem et diem ad operandum.</p>
 <p>23 Ego autem et fratres mei, et pueri mei, et custodes, qui erant post me, non deponebamus vestimenta nostra: unusquisque tantum nudabatur ad baptismum.</p>
