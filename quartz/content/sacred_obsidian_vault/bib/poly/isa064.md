 <p align="center"><a href="../kjv/isa064.htm">KJV</a></p><p>1  Oh that<sup><a href="h3863.htm">3863</a></sup> thou wouldest rend<sup><a href="h7167.htm">7167</a></sup> the heavens,<sup><a href="h8064.htm">8064</a></sup> that thou wouldest come down,<sup><a href="h3381.htm">3381</a></sup> that the mountains<sup><a href="h2022.htm">2022</a></sup> might flow down<sup><a href="h2151.htm">2151</a></sup> at thy presence,<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h6440.htm">6440</a></sup> </p>
 <p>2  As when the melting<sup><a href="h2003.htm">2003</a></sup> fire<sup><a href="h0784.htm">784</a></sup> burneth,<sup><a href="h6919.htm">6919</a></sup> the fire<sup><a href="h0784.htm">784</a></sup> causeth the waters<sup><a href="h4325.htm">4325</a></sup> to boil,<sup><a href="h1158.htm">1158</a></sup> to make thy name<sup><a href="h8034.htm">8034</a></sup> known<sup><a href="h3045.htm">3045</a></sup> to thine adversaries,<sup><a href="h6862.htm">6862</a></sup> that the nations<sup><a href="h1471.htm">1471</a></sup> may tremble<sup><a href="h7264.htm">7264</a></sup> at thy presence!<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h6440.htm">6440</a></sup> </p>
 <p>3  When thou didst<sup><a href="h6213.htm">6213</a></sup> terrible things<sup><a href="h3372.htm">3372</a></sup> which we looked not for,<sup><a href="h6960.htm">6960</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> thou camest down,<sup><a href="h3381.htm">3381</a></sup> the mountains<sup><a href="h2022.htm">2022</a></sup> flowed down<sup><a href="h2151.htm">2151</a></sup> at thy presence.<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h6440.htm">6440</a></sup> </p>
 <p>4  For since the beginning of the world<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h5769.htm">5769</a></sup> men have not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> heard,<sup><a href="h8085.htm">8085</a></sup> nor<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> perceived by the ear,<sup><a href="h0238.htm">238</a></sup> neither<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> hath the eye<sup><a href="h5869.htm">5869</a></sup> seen,<sup><a href="h7200.htm">7200</a></sup> O God,<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> beside<sup><a href="h2108.htm">2108</a></sup> thee, what he hath prepared<sup><a href="h6213.htm">6213</a></sup> for him that waiteth<sup><a href="h2442.htm">2442</a></sup> for him. </p>
 <p>5  Thou meetest<sup><a href="h6293.htm">6293</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> him that rejoiceth<sup><a href="h7797.htm">7797</a></sup> and worketh<sup><a href="h6213.htm">6213</a></sup> righteousness,<sup><a href="h6664.htm">6664</a></sup> those that remember<sup><a href="h2142.htm">2142</a></sup> thee in thy ways:<sup><a href="h1870.htm">1870</a></sup> behold,<sup><a href="h2005.htm">2005</a></sup> thou<sup><a href="h0859.htm">859</a></sup> art wroth;<sup><a href="h7107.htm">7107</a></sup> for we have sinned:<sup><a href="h2398.htm">2398</a></sup> in those is continuance,<sup><a href="h5769.htm">5769</a></sup> and we shall be saved.<sup><a href="h3467.htm">3467</a></sup> </p>
 <p>6  But we are<sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> as an unclean<sup><a href="h2931.htm">2931</a></sup> thing, and all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> our righteousnesses<sup><a href="h6666.htm">6666</a></sup> are as filthy<sup><a href="h5708.htm">5708</a></sup> rags;<sup><a href="h0899.htm">899</a></sup> and we all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> do fade<sup><a href="h5034.htm">5034</a></sup> as a leaf;<sup><a href="h5929.htm">5929</a></sup> and our iniquities,<sup><a href="h5771.htm">5771</a></sup> like the wind,<sup><a href="h7307.htm">7307</a></sup> have taken us away.<sup><a href="h5375.htm">5375</a></sup> </p>
 <p>7  And there is none<sup><a href="h0369.htm">369</a></sup> that calleth<sup><a href="h7121.htm">7121</a></sup> upon thy name,<sup><a href="h8034.htm">8034</a></sup> that stirreth up himself<sup><a href="h5782.htm">5782</a></sup> to take hold<sup><a href="h2388.htm">2388</a></sup> of thee: for<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> thou hast hid<sup><a href="h5641.htm">5641</a></sup> thy face<sup><a href="h6440.htm">6440</a></sup> from<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> us, and hast consumed<sup><a href="h4127.htm">4127</a></sup> us, because<sup><a href="h3027.htm">3027</a></sup> of our iniquities.<sup><a href="h5771.htm">5771</a></sup> </p>
 <p>8  But now,<sup><a href="h6258.htm">6258</a></sup> O LORD,<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> thou<sup><a href="h0859.htm">859</a></sup> art our father;<sup><a href="h0001.htm">1</a></sup> we<sup><a href="h0587.htm">587</a></sup> are the clay,<sup><a href="h2563.htm">2563</a></sup> and thou<sup><a href="h0859.htm">859</a></sup> our potter;<sup><a href="h3335.htm">3335</a></sup> and we all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> are the work<sup><a href="h4639.htm">4639</a></sup> of thy hand.<sup><a href="h3027.htm">3027</a></sup> </p>
 <p>9  Be not wroth<sup><a href="h7107.htm">7107</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0408.htm">408</a></sup> very sore,<sup><a href="h5704.htm">5704</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3966.htm">3966</a></sup> O LORD,<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> neither<sup><a href="h0408.htm">408</a></sup> remember<sup><a href="h2142.htm">2142</a></sup> iniquity<sup><a href="h5771.htm">5771</a></sup> forever:<sup><a href="h5703.htm">5703</a></sup> behold,<sup><a href="h2005.htm">2005</a></sup> see,<sup><a href="h5027.htm">5027</a></sup> we beseech thee,<sup><a href="h4994.htm">4994</a></sup> we are all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> thy people.<sup><a href="h5971.htm">5971</a></sup> </p>
 <p>10  Thy holy<sup><a href="h6944.htm">6944</a></sup> cities<sup><a href="h5892.htm">5892</a></sup> are<sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> a wilderness,<sup><a href="h4057.htm">4057</a></sup> Zion<sup><a href="h6726.htm">6726</a></sup> is<sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> a wilderness,<sup><a href="h4057.htm">4057</a></sup> Jerusalem<sup><a href="h3389.htm">3389</a></sup> a desolation.<sup><a href="h8077.htm">8077</a></sup> </p>
 <p>11  Our holy<sup><a href="h6944.htm">6944</a></sup> and our beautiful<sup><a href="h8597.htm">8597</a></sup> house,<sup><a href="h1004.htm">1004</a></sup> where<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> our fathers<sup><a href="h0001.htm">1</a></sup> praised<sup><a href="h1984.htm">1984</a></sup> thee, is<sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> burned up<sup><a href="h8316.htm">8316</a></sup> with fire:<sup><a href="h0784.htm">784</a></sup> and all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> our pleasant things<sup><a href="h4261.htm">4261</a></sup> are<sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> laid waste.<sup><a href="h2723.htm">2723</a></sup> </p>
 <p>12  Wilt thou refrain thyself<sup><a href="h0662.htm">662</a></sup> for<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> these<sup><a href="h0428.htm">428</a></sup> things, O LORD?<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> wilt thou hold thy peace,<sup><a href="h2814.htm">2814</a></sup> and afflict<sup><a href="h6031.htm">6031</a></sup> us very sore?<sup><a href="h5704.htm">5704</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3966.htm">3966</a></sup> </p>

 <p>2 ὅταν ποιῇς τὰ ἔνδοξα τρόμος λήμψεται ἀπὸ σοῦ ὄρη </p>
 <p>3 ἀπὸ τοῦ αἰῶνος οὐκ ἠκούσαμεν οὐδὲ οἱ ὀφθαλμοὶ ἡμῶν εἶδον θεὸν πλὴν σοῦ καὶ τὰ ἔργα σου ἃ ποιήσεις τοῖς ὑπομένουσιν ἔλεον </p>
 <p>4 συναντήσεται γὰρ τοῖς ποιοῦσιν τὸ δίκαιον καὶ τῶν ὁδῶν σου μνησθήσονται ἰδοὺ σὺ ὠργίσθης καὶ ἡμεῖς ἡμάρτομεν διὰ τοῦτο ἐπλανήθημεν </p>
 <p>5 καὶ ἐγενήθημεν ὡς ἀκάθαρτοι πάντες ἡμεῖς ὡς ῥάκος ἀποκαθημένης πᾶσα ἡ δικαιοσύνη ἡμῶν καὶ ἐξερρύημεν ὡς φύλλα διὰ τὰς ἀνομίας ἡμῶν οὕτως ἄνεμος οἴσει ἡμᾶς </p>
 <p>6 καὶ οὐκ ἔστιν ὁ ἐπικαλούμενος τὸ ὄνομά σου καὶ ὁ μνησθεὶς ἀντιλαβέσθαι σου ὅτι ἀπέστρεψας τὸ πρόσωπόν σου ἀφ᾽ ἡμῶν καὶ παρέδωκας ἡμᾶς διὰ τὰς ἁμαρτίας ἡμῶν </p>
 <p>7 καὶ νῦν κύριε πατὴρ ἡμῶν σύ ἡμεῖς δὲ πηλὸς ἔργον τῶν χειρῶν σου πάντες </p>
 <p>8 μὴ ὀργίζου ἡμῖν σφόδρα καὶ μὴ ἐν καιρῷ μνησθῇς ἁμαρτιῶν ἡμῶν καὶ νῦν ἐπίβλεψον ὅτι λαός σου πάντες ἡμεῖς </p>
 <p>9 πόλις τοῦ ἁγίου σου ἐγενήθη ἔρημος Σιων ὡς ἔρημος ἐγενήθη Ιερουσαλημ εἰς κατάραν </p>
 <p>10 ὁ οἶκος τὸ ἅγιον ἡμῶν καὶ ἡ δόξα ἣν ηὐλόγησαν οἱ πατέρες ἡμῶν ἐγενήθη πυρίκαυστος καὶ πάντα τὰ ἔνδοξα συνέπεσεν </p>
 <p>11 καὶ ἐπὶ πᾶσι τούτοις ἀνέσχου κύριε καὶ ἐσιώπησας καὶ ἐταπείνωσας ἡμᾶς σφόδρα </p>

 <p>3 Cum feceris mirabilia, non sustinebimus;<br> descendisti, et a facie tua montes defluxerunt.<br></p>
 <p>4 A sæculo non audierunt, neque auribus perceperunt;<br> oculus non vidit, Deus, absque te,<br> quæ præparasti exspectantibus te.<br></p>
 <p>5 Occurristi lætanti, et facienti justitiam;<br> in viis tuis recordabuntur tui.<br> Ecce tu iratus es, et peccavimus;<br> in ipsis fuimus semper, et salvabimur.<br></p>
 <p>6 Et facti sumus ut immundus omnes nos,<br> et quasi pannus menstruatæ universæ justitiæ nostræ;<br> et cecidimus quasi folium universi,<br> et iniquitates nostræ quasi ventus abstulerunt nos.<br></p>
 <p>7 Non est qui invocet nomen tuum;<br> qui consurgat, et teneat te.<br> Abscondisti faciem tuam a nobis,<br> et allisisti nos in manu iniquitatis nostræ.<br></p>
 <p>8 Et nunc, Domine, pater noster es tu,<br> nos vero lutum;<br> et fictor noster tu,<br> et opera manuum tuarum omnes nos.<br></p>
 <p>9 Ne irascaris, Domine, satis,<br> et ne ultra memineris iniquitatis nostræ;<br> ecce, respice, populus tuus omnes nos.<br></p>
 <p>10 Civitas Sancti tui facta est deserta,<br> Sion deserta facta est,<br> Jerusalem desolata est.<br></p>
 <p>11 Domus sanctificationis nostræ et gloriæ nostræ,<br> ubi laudaverunt te patres nostri,<br> facta est in exustionem ignis,<br> et omnia desiderabilia nostra versa sunt in ruinas.<br></p>
 <p>12 Numquid super his continebis te, Domine;<br> tacebis, et affliges nos vehementer?]</p>
