 <p>2  And said,<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> I cried<sup><a href="h7121.htm">7121</a></sup> by reason of mine affliction<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h6869.htm">6869</a></sup> unto<sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> the LORD,<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> and he heard<sup><a href="h6030.htm">6030</a></sup> me; out of the belly<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0990.htm">990</a></sup> of hell<sup><a href="h7585.htm">7585</a></sup> cried<sup><a href="h7768.htm">7768</a></sup> I, and thou heardest<sup><a href="h8085.htm">8085</a></sup> my voice.<sup><a href="h6963.htm">6963</a></sup> </p>
 <p>3  For thou hadst cast<sup><a href="h7993.htm">7993</a></sup> me into the deep,<sup><a href="h4688.htm">4688</a></sup> in the midst<sup><a href="h3824.htm">3824</a></sup> of the seas;<sup><a href="h3220.htm">3220</a></sup> and the floods<sup><a href="h5104.htm">5104</a></sup> compassed me about:<sup><a href="h5437.htm">5437</a></sup> all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> thy billows<sup><a href="h4867.htm">4867</a></sup> and thy waves<sup><a href="h1530.htm">1530</a></sup> passed<sup><a href="h5674.htm">5674</a></sup> over<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> me. </p>
 <p>4  Then I<sup><a href="h0589.htm">589</a></sup> said,<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> I am cast out<sup><a href="h1644.htm">1644</a></sup> of<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h5048.htm">5048</a></sup> thy sight;<sup><a href="h5869.htm">5869</a></sup> yet<sup><a href="h0389.htm">389</a></sup> I will look<sup><a href="h5027.htm">5027</a></sup> again<sup><a href="h3254.htm">3254</a></sup> toward<sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> thy holy<sup><a href="h6944.htm">6944</a></sup> temple.<sup><a href="h1964.htm">1964</a></sup> </p>
 <p>5  The waters<sup><a href="h4325.htm">4325</a></sup> compassed me about,<sup><a href="h0661.htm">661</a></sup> even to<sup><a href="h5704.htm">5704</a></sup> the soul:<sup><a href="h5315.htm">5315</a></sup> the depth<sup><a href="h8415.htm">8415</a></sup> closed me round about,<sup><a href="h5437.htm">5437</a></sup> the weeds<sup><a href="h5488.htm">5488</a></sup> were wrapped<sup><a href="h2280.htm">2280</a></sup> about my head.<sup><a href="h7218.htm">7218</a></sup> </p>
 <p>6  I went down<sup><a href="h3381.htm">3381</a></sup> to the bottoms<sup><a href="h7095.htm">7095</a></sup> of the mountains;<sup><a href="h2022.htm">2022</a></sup> the earth<sup><a href="h0776.htm">776</a></sup> with her bars<sup><a href="h1280.htm">1280</a></sup> was about<sup><a href="h1157.htm">1157</a></sup> me forever:<sup><a href="h5769.htm">5769</a></sup> yet hast thou brought up<sup><a href="h5927.htm">5927</a></sup> my life<sup><a href="h2416.htm">2416</a></sup> from corruption,<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h7845.htm">7845</a></sup> O LORD<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> my God.<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> </p>
 <p>7  When my soul<sup><a href="h5315.htm">5315</a></sup> fainted<sup><a href="h5848.htm">5848</a></sup> within<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> me I remembered<sup><a href="h2142.htm">2142</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> the LORD:<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> and my prayer<sup><a href="h8605.htm">8605</a></sup> came in<sup><a href="h0935.htm">935</a></sup> unto<sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> thee, into<sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> thine holy<sup><a href="h6944.htm">6944</a></sup> temple.<sup><a href="h1964.htm">1964</a></sup> </p>
 <p>8  They that observe<sup><a href="h8104.htm">8104</a></sup> lying<sup><a href="h7723.htm">7723</a></sup> vanities<sup><a href="h1892.htm">1892</a></sup> forsake<sup><a href="h5800.htm">5800</a></sup> their own mercy.<sup><a href="h2617.htm">2617</a></sup> </p>
 <p>9  But I<sup><a href="h0589.htm">589</a></sup> will sacrifice<sup><a href="h2076.htm">2076</a></sup> unto thee with the voice<sup><a href="h6963.htm">6963</a></sup> of thanksgiving;<sup><a href="h8426.htm">8426</a></sup> I will pay<sup><a href="h7999.htm">7999</a></sup> that that<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> I have vowed.<sup><a href="h5087.htm">5087</a></sup> Salvation<sup><a href="h3444.htm">3444</a></sup> is of the LORD.<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> </p>
 <p>10  And the LORD<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> spoke<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> unto the fish,<sup><a href="h1709.htm">1709</a></sup> and it vomited out<sup><a href="h6958.htm">6958</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> Jonah<sup><a href="h3124.htm">3124</a></sup> upon<sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> the dry<sup><a href="h3004.htm">3004</a></sup> land. </p>

 <p align="center"><a href="../sep/jon002.htm">Sep</a></p><p>1 καὶ προσέταξεν κύριος κήτει μεγάλῳ καταπιεῖν τὸν Ιωναν καὶ ἦν Ιωνας ἐν τῇ κοιλίᾳ τοῦ κήτους τρεῖς ἡμέρας καὶ τρεῖς νύκτας </p>
 <p>2 καὶ προσηύξατο Ιωνας πρὸς κύριον τὸν θεὸν αὐτοῦ ἐκ τῆς κοιλίας τοῦ κήτους </p>
 <p>3 καὶ εἶπεν ἐβόησα ἐν θλίψει μου πρὸς κύριον τὸν θεόν μου καὶ εἰσήκουσέν μου ἐκ κοιλίας ᾅδου κραυγῆς μου ἤκουσας φωνῆς μου </p>
 <p>4 ἀπέρριψάς με εἰς βάθη καρδίας θαλάσσης καὶ ποταμοί με ἐκύκλωσαν πάντες οἱ μετεωρισμοί σου καὶ τὰ κύματά σου ἐπ᾽ ἐμὲ διῆλθον </p>
 <p>5 καὶ ἐγὼ εἶπα ἀπῶσμαι ἐξ ὀφθαλμῶν σου ἆρα προσθήσω τοῦ ἐπιβλέψαι πρὸς τὸν ναὸν τὸν ἅγιόν σου </p>
 <p>6 περιεχύθη ὕδωρ μοι ἕως ψυχῆς ἄβυσσος ἐκύκλωσέν με ἐσχάτη ἔδυ ἡ κεφαλή μου εἰς σχισμὰς ὀρέων </p>
 <p>7 κατέβην εἰς γῆν ἧς οἱ μοχλοὶ αὐτῆς κάτοχοι αἰώνιοι καὶ ἀναβήτω φθορὰ ζωῆς μου κύριε ὁ θεός μου </p>
 <p>8 ἐν τῷ ἐκλείπειν ἀπ᾽ ἐμοῦ τὴν ψυχήν μου τοῦ κυρίου ἐμνήσθην καὶ ἔλθοι πρὸς σὲ ἡ προσευχή μου εἰς ναὸν ἅγιόν σου </p>
 <p>9 φυλασσόμενοι μάταια καὶ ψευδῆ ἔλεος αὐτῶν ἐγκατέλιπον </p>
 <p>10 ἐγὼ δὲ μετὰ φωνῆς αἰνέσεως καὶ ἐξομολογήσεως θύσω σοι ὅσα ηὐξάμην ἀποδώσω σοι σωτηρίου τῷ κυρίῳ </p>
 <p>11 καὶ προσετάγη τῷ κήτει καὶ ἐξέβαλεν τὸν Ιωναν ἐπὶ τὴν ξηράν </p>

 <p align="center"><a href="../vul/jon002.htm">Vul</a></p><p>1 Et præparavit Dominus piscem grandem ut deglutiret Jonam: et erat Jonas in ventre piscis tribus diebus et tribus noctibus.</p>
 <p>2 Et oravit Jonas ad Dominum Deum suum de ventre piscis,</p>
 <p>3 et dixit: [Clamavi de tribulatione mea ad Dominum,<br> et exaudivit me;<br> de ventre inferi clamavi,<br> et exaudisti vocem meam.<br></p>
 <p>4 Et projecisti me in profundum in corde maris,<br> et flumen circumdedit me:<br> omnes gurgites tui, et fluctus tui super me transierunt.<br></p>
 <p>5 Et ego dixi:<br> Abjectus sum a conspectu oculorum tuorum;<br> verumtamen rursus videbo templum sanctum tuum.<br></p>
 <p>6 Circumdederunt me aquæ usque ad animam:<br> abyssus vallavit me,<br> pelagus operuit caput meum.<br></p>
 <p>7 Ad extrema montium descendi;<br> terræ vectes concluserunt me in æternum:<br> et sublevabis de corruptione vitam meam, Domine Deus meus.<br></p>
 <p>8 Cum angustiaretur in me anima mea,<br> Domini recordatus sum:<br> ut veniat ad te oratio mea,<br> ad templum sanctum tuum.<br></p>
 <p>9 Qui custodiunt vanitates frustra,<br> misericordiam suam derelinquunt.<br></p>
 <p>10 Ego autem in voce laudis immolabo tibi:<br> quæcumque vovi, reddam pro salute Domino.]</p>
 <p>11 Et dixit Dominus pisci, et evomuit Jonam in aridam.</p>
