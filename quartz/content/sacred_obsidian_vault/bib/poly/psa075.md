 <p>2  When<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> I shall receive<sup><a href="h3947.htm">3947</a></sup> the congregation<sup><a href="h4150.htm">4150</a></sup> I<sup><a href="h0589.htm">589</a></sup> will judge<sup><a href="h8199.htm">8199</a></sup> uprightly.<sup><a href="h4339.htm">4339</a></sup> </p>

 <p>8  For<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> in the hand<sup><a href="h3027.htm">3027</a></sup> of the LORD<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> there is a cup,<sup><a href="h3563.htm">3563</a></sup> and the wine<sup><a href="h3196.htm">3196</a></sup> is red;<sup><a href="h2560.htm">2560</a></sup> it is full<sup><a href="h4392.htm">4392</a></sup> of mixture;<sup><a href="h4538.htm">4538</a></sup> and he poureth out<sup><a href="h5064.htm">5064</a></sup> of the same:<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h2088.htm">2088</a></sup> but<sup><a href="h0389.htm">389</a></sup> the dregs<sup><a href="h8105.htm">8105</a></sup> thereof, all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> the wicked<sup><a href="h7563.htm">7563</a></sup> of the earth<sup><a href="h0776.htm">776</a></sup> shall wring them out,<sup><a href="h4680.htm">4680</a></sup> and drink<sup><a href="h8354.htm">8354</a></sup> them. </p>
 <p>9  But I<sup><a href="h0589.htm">589</a></sup> will declare<sup><a href="h5046.htm">5046</a></sup> forever;<sup><a href="h5769.htm">5769</a></sup> I will sing praises<sup><a href="h2167.htm">2167</a></sup> to the God<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> of Jacob.<sup><a href="h3290.htm">3290</a></sup> </p>

 <p>13 τῷ φοβερῷ καὶ ἀφαιρουμένῳ πνεύματα ἀρχόντων φοβερῷ παρὰ τοῖς βασιλεῦσι τῆς γῆς </p>

 <p>2 [Notus in Judæa Deus;<br> in Israël magnum nomen ejus.<br></p>
 <p>3 Et factus est in pace locus ejus,<br> et habitatio ejus in Sion.<br></p>
 <p>4 Ibi confregit potentias arcuum,<br> scutum, gladium, et bellum.<br></p>
 <p>5 Illuminans tu mirabiliter a montibus æternis;<br></p>
 <p>6 turbati sunt omnes insipientes corde.<br> Dormierunt somnum suum,<br> et nihil invenerunt omnes viri divitiarum in manibus suis.<br></p>
 <p>7 Ab increpatione tua, Deus Jacob,<br> dormitaverunt qui ascenderunt equos.<br></p>
 <p>8 Tu terribilis es; et quis resistet tibi?<br> ex tunc ira tua.<br></p>
 <p>9 De cælo auditum fecisti judicium:<br> terra tremuit et quievit</p>
 <p>10 cum exsurgeret in judicium Deus,<br> ut salvos faceret omnes mansuetos terræ.<br></p>
 <p>11 Quoniam cogitatio hominis confitebitur tibi,<br> et reliquiæ cogitationis diem festum agent tibi.<br></p>
 <p>12 Vovete et reddite Domino Deo vestro,<br> omnes qui in circuitu ejus affertis munera:<br> terribili,</p>
 <p>13 et ei qui aufert spiritum principum:<br> terribili apud reges terræ.]</p>
