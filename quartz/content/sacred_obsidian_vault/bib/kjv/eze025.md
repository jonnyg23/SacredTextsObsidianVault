 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Ezekiel Chapter 25</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> The word of the LORD came again unto me, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Son of man, set thy face against the Ammonites, and prophesy against them;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And say unto the Ammonites, Hear the word of the Lord GOD; Thus saith the Lord GOD; Because thou saidst, <a href="../ebd/ebd013.htm#001">Aha</a>, against my <a href="../ebd/ebd321.htm#005">sanctuary</a>, when it was profaned; and against the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, when it was desolate; and against the house of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>, when they went into <a href="../ebd/ebd072.htm#002">captivity</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Behold, therefore I will deliver thee to the men of the <a href="../ebd/ebd111.htm#000">east</a> for a possession, and they shall set their palaces in thee, and make their <a href="../ebd/ebd110.htm#001">dwellings</a> in thee: they shall eat thy <a href="../ebd/ebd139.htm#000">fruit</a>, and they shall <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a> thy <a href="../ebd/ebd255.htm#000">milk</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And I will make <a href="../ebd/ebd304.htm#005">Rabbah</a> a stable for camels, and the Ammonites a couching place for flocks: and ye shall know that I am the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> For thus saith the Lord GOD; Because thou hast clapped thine hands, and stamped with the feet, and rejoiced in heart with all thy despite against the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Behold, therefore I will stretch out mine hand upon thee, and will deliver thee for a spoil to the <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#005">heathen</a>; and I will cut thee off from the people, and I will cause thee to perish out of the countries: I will destroy thee; and thou shalt know that I am the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> Thus saith the Lord GOD; Because that <a href="../ebd/ebd258.htm#005">Moab</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd326.htm#004">Seir</a> do say, Behold, the house of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a> is like unto all the <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#005">heathen</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> Therefore, behold, I will open the side of <a href="../ebd/ebd258.htm#005">Moab</a> from the cities, from his cities which are on his frontiers, the <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a> of the country, Bethjeshimoth, Baalmeon, and Kiriathaim,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> Unto the men of the <a href="../ebd/ebd111.htm#000">east</a> with the Ammonites, and will give them in possession, that the Ammonites may not be remembered among the nations.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And I will execute judgments upon <a href="../ebd/ebd258.htm#005">Moab</a>; and they shall know that I am the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> Thus saith the Lord GOD; Because that <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#001">Edom</a> hath dealt against the house of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a> by taking vengeance, and hath greatly offended, and revenged himself upon them;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> Therefore thus saith the Lord GOD; I will also stretch out mine hand upon <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#001">Edom</a>, and will cut off man and <a href="../ebd/ebd049.htm#001">beast</a> from it; and I will make it desolate from <a href="../ebd/ebd361.htm#001">Teman</a>; and they of <a href="../ebd/ebd100.htm#005">Dedan</a> shall fall by the <a href="../ebd/ebd354.htm#003">sword</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> And I will lay my vengeance upon <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#001">Edom</a> by the hand of my people <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>: and they shall do in <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#001">Edom</a> according to mine <a href="../ebd/ebd024.htm#002">anger</a> and according to my <a href="../ebd/ebd140.htm#002">fury</a>; and they shall know my vengeance, saith the Lord GOD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> Thus saith the Lord GOD; Because the <a href="../ebd/ebd294.htm#002">Philistines</a> have dealt by revenge, and have taken vengeance with a despiteful heart, to destroy it for the old <a href="../ebd/ebd167.htm#002">hatred</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> Therefore thus saith the Lord GOD; Behold, I will stretch out mine hand upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd294.htm#002">Philistines</a>, and I will cut off the Cherethims, and destroy the remnant of the sea coast.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> And I will execute great vengeance upon them with furious rebukes; and they shall know that I am the LORD, when I shall lay my vengeance upon them.<br>
 </p>
