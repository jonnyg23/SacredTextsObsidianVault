 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Galatians Chapter 6</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Brethren, if a man be overtaken in a fault, ye which are spiritual, restore such an one in the <a href="../ebd/ebd349.htm#009">spirit</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd246.htm#003">meekness</a>; considering thyself, lest thou also be tempted.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Bear ye one another's burdens, and so fulfil the law of <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> For if a man think himself to be something, when he is nothing, he deceiveth himself.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> But let every man prove his own work, and then shall he have rejoicing in himself alone, and not in another.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> For every man shall bear his own <a href="../ebd/ebd066.htm#007">burden</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Let him that is taught in the word communicate unto him that teacheth in all good things.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Be not deceived; God is not mocked: for whatsoever a man soweth, that shall he also reap.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> For he that soweth to his <a href="../ebd/ebd135.htm#007">flesh</a> shall of the <a href="../ebd/ebd135.htm#007">flesh</a> reap corruption; but he that soweth to the <a href="../ebd/ebd349.htm#009">Spirit</a> shall of the <a href="../ebd/ebd349.htm#009">Spirit</a> reap life <a href="../ebd/ebd127.htm#007">everlasting</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And let us not be weary in well doing: for in due season we shall reap, if we faint not.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> As we have therefore opportunity, let us do good unto all men, especially unto them who are of the household of faith.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> Ye see how large a letter I have written unto you with mine own hand.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> As many as desire to make a fair shew in the <a href="../ebd/ebd135.htm#007">flesh</a>, they constrain you to be circumcised; only lest they should suffer <a href="../ebd/ebd290.htm#007">persecution</a> for the <a href="../ebd/ebd093.htm#000">cross</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> For neither they themselves who are circumcised keep the law; but desire to have you circumcised, that they may <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a> in your <a href="../ebd/ebd135.htm#007">flesh</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> But God forbid that I should <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a>, save in the <a href="../ebd/ebd093.htm#000">cross</a> of our Lord <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a>, by whom the world is crucified unto me, and I unto the world.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> For in <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a> neither <a href="../ebd/ebd083.htm#007">circumcision</a> availeth any thing, nor uncircumcision, but a new <a href="../ebd/ebd092.htm#004">creature</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> And as many as walk according to this rule, peace be on them, and <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a>, and upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> of God.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> From henceforth let no man trouble me: for I bear in my body the marks of the Lord <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> Brethren, the <a href="../ebd/ebd154.htm#000">grace</a> of our Lord <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a> be with your <a href="../ebd/ebd349.htm#009">spirit</a>. <a href="../ebd/ebd020.htm#001">Amen</a>.<br>
 </p>
