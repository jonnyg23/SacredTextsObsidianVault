 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: 1 Chronicles Chapter 8</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Now <a href="../ebd/ebd052.htm#008">Benjamin</a> begat <a href="../ebd/ebd051.htm#006">Bela</a> his firstborn, Ashbel the second, and Aharah the third,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Nohah the fourth, and <a href="../ebd/ebd307.htm#006">Rapha</a> the fifth.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd051.htm#006">Bela</a> were, <a href="../ebd/ebd008.htm#004">Addar</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd146.htm#003">Gera</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd003.htm#005">Abihud</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd004.htm#006">Abishua</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd263.htm#009">Naaman</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd015.htm#003">Ahoah</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd146.htm#003">Gera</a>, and Shephuphan, and Huram.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And these are the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd114.htm#000">Ehud</a>: these are the heads of the fathers of the inhabitants of <a href="../ebd/ebd144.htm#004">Geba</a>, and they removed them to Manahath:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd263.htm#009">Naaman</a>, and Ahiah, and <a href="../ebd/ebd146.htm#003">Gera</a>, he removed them, and begat <a href="../ebd/ebd376.htm#000">Uzza</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd013.htm#009">Ahihud</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And Shaharaim begat children in the country of <a href="../ebd/ebd258.htm#005">Moab</a>, after he had sent them away; Hushim and Baara were his wives.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And he begat of Hodesh his wife, <a href="../ebd/ebd208.htm#003">Jobab</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd392.htm#005">Zibia</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd250.htm#006">Mesha</a>, and Malcham,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And Jeuz, and Shachia, and Mirma. These were his sons, heads of the fathers.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And of Hushim he begat <a href="../ebd/ebd004.htm#009">Abitub</a>, and Elpaal.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> The sons of Elpaal; <a href="../ebd/ebd112.htm#001">Eber</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd256.htm#008">Misham</a>, and Shamed, who built <a href="../ebd/ebd279.htm#002">Ono</a>, and Lod, with the towns thereof:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd053.htm#005">Beriah</a> also, and <a href="../ebd/ebd334.htm#003">Shema</a>, who were heads of the fathers of the inhabitants of Aijalon, who drove away the inhabitants of <a href="../ebd/ebd143.htm#009">Gath</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd014.htm#007">Ahio</a>, Shashak, and Jeremoth,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd388.htm#005">Zebadiah</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd028.htm#000">Arad</a>, and Ader,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd252.htm#009">Michael</a>, and Ispah, and Joha, the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd053.htm#005">Beriah</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd388.htm#005">Zebadiah</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd251.htm#001">Meshullam</a>, and Hezeki, and <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#008">Heber</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> Ishmerai also, and Jezliah, and <a href="../ebd/ebd208.htm#003">Jobab</a>, the sons of Elpaal;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd196.htm#004">Jakim</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd392.htm#007">Zichri</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd385.htm#008">Zabdi</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> And Elienai, and <a href="../ebd/ebd393.htm#006">Zilthai</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd116.htm#006">Eliel</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> And Adaiah, and Beraiah, and <a href="../ebd/ebd338.htm#005">Shimrath</a>, the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd338.htm#004">Shimhi</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> And Ishpan, and <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#008">Heber</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd116.htm#006">Eliel</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd001.htm#002">Abdon</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd392.htm#007">Zichri</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd162.htm#001">Hanan</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd162.htm#004">Hananiah</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd114.htm#003">Elam</a>, and Antothijah,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd188.htm#009">Iphedeiah</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd289.htm#005">Penuel</a>, the sons of Shashak;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> And Shamsherai, and Shehariah, and <a href="../ebd/ebd036.htm#002">Athaliah</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> And Jaresiah, and Eliah, and <a href="../ebd/ebd392.htm#007">Zichri</a>, the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#001">Jeroham</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="028">28</a> These were heads of the fathers, by their generations, chief men. These dwelt in <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="029">29</a> And at <a href="../ebd/ebd148.htm#002">Gibeon</a> dwelt the father of <a href="../ebd/ebd148.htm#002">Gibeon</a>; whose wife's name was <a href="../ebd/ebd234.htm#004">Maachah</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="030">30</a> And his firstborn son <a href="../ebd/ebd001.htm#002">Abdon</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd396.htm#005">Zur</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd220.htm#007">Kish</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd038.htm#004">Baal</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd264.htm#008">Nadab</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="031">31</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd145.htm#003">Gedor</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd014.htm#007">Ahio</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd386.htm#007">Zacher</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="032">32</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd254.htm#005">Mikloth</a> begat <a href="../ebd/ebd338.htm#001">Shimeah</a>. And these also dwelt with their brethren in <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>, over against them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="033">33</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd270.htm#007">Ner</a> begat <a href="../ebd/ebd220.htm#007">Kish</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd220.htm#007">Kish</a> begat <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#002">Saul</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#002">Saul</a> begat <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#000">Jonathan</a>, and Malchishua, and <a href="../ebd/ebd004.htm#001">Abinadab</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd124.htm#009">Eshbaal</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="034">34</a> And the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#000">Jonathan</a> was Meribbaal; and Meribbaal begat <a href="../ebd/ebd252.htm#005">Micah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="035">35</a> And the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd252.htm#005">Micah</a> were, Pithon, and <a href="../ebd/ebd247.htm#005">Melech</a>, and Tarea, and <a href="../ebd/ebd013.htm#005">Ahaz</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="036">36</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd013.htm#005">Ahaz</a> begat Jehoadah; and Jehoadah begat <a href="../ebd/ebd016.htm#008">Alemeth</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd038.htm#000">Azmaveth</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd393.htm#009">Zimri</a>; and <a href="../ebd/ebd393.htm#009">Zimri</a> begat <a href="../ebd/ebd261.htm#007">Moza</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="037">37</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd261.htm#007">Moza</a> begat Binea: <a href="../ebd/ebd307.htm#006">Rapha</a> was his son, Eleasah his son, <a href="../ebd/ebd037.htm#009">Azel</a> his son:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="038">38</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd037.htm#009">Azel</a> had six sons, whose names are these, Azrikam, Bocheru, and <a href="../ebd/ebd190.htm#005">Ishmael</a>, and Sheariah, and <a href="../ebd/ebd276.htm#001">Obadiah</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd162.htm#001">Hanan</a>. All these were the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd037.htm#009">Azel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="039">39</a> And the sons of Eshek his brother were, Ulam his firstborn, Jehush the second, and <a href="../ebd/ebd117.htm#007">Eliphelet</a> the third.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="040">40</a> And the sons of Ulam were mighty men of valour, archers, and had many sons, and sons' sons, an hundred and fifty. All these are of the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd052.htm#008">Benjamin</a>.<br>
 </p>
