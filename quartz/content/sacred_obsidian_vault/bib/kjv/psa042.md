 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 42</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> As the <a href="../ebd/ebd165.htm#004">hart</a> panteth after the water brooks, so panteth my soul after thee, O God.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> My soul thirsteth for God, for the living God: when shall I come and appear before God?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> My tears have been my meat day and night, while they continually say unto me, Where is thy God?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> When I remember these things, I pour out my soul in me: for I had gone with the multitude, I went with them to the house of God, with the voice of joy and praise, with a multitude that kept holyday.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Why art thou cast down, O my soul? and why art thou disquieted in me?  hope thou in God: for I shall yet praise him for the help of his countenance.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> O my God, my soul is cast down within me: therefore will I remember thee from the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#004">Jordan</a>, and of the Hermonites, from the hill <a href="../ebd/ebd257.htm#009">Mizar</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd100.htm#008">Deep</a> calleth unto <a href="../ebd/ebd100.htm#008">deep</a> at the noise of thy <a href="../ebd/ebd379.htm#008">waterspouts</a>: all thy waves and thy billows are gone over me.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> Yet the LORD will command his lovingkindness in the day time, and in the night his song shall be with me, and my <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#000">prayer</a> unto the God of my life.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> I will say unto God my <a href="../ebd/ebd315.htm#001">rock</a>, Why hast thou forgotten me? why go I mourning because of the oppression of the enemy?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> As with a <a href="../ebd/ebd354.htm#003">sword</a> in my bones, mine enemies reproach me; while they say daily unto me, Where is thy God?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> Why art thou cast down, O my soul? and why art thou disquieted within me? hope thou in God: for I shall yet praise him, who is the health of my countenance, and my God.<br>
 </p>
