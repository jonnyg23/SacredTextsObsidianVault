 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: 1 Corinthians Chapter 3</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> And I, brethren, could not speak unto you as unto spiritual, but as unto <a href="../ebd/ebd072.htm#008">carnal</a>, even as unto babes in <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> I have fed you with <a href="../ebd/ebd255.htm#000">milk</a>, and not with meat: for hitherto ye were not able to bear it, neither yet now are ye able.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> For ye are yet <a href="../ebd/ebd072.htm#008">carnal</a>: for whereas there is among you envying, and strife, and divisions, are ye not <a href="../ebd/ebd072.htm#008">carnal</a>, and walk as men?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> For while one saith, I am of <a href="../ebd/ebd287.htm#002">Paul</a>; and another, I am of <a href="../ebd/ebd026.htm#006">Apollos</a>; are ye not <a href="../ebd/ebd072.htm#008">carnal</a>?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Who then is <a href="../ebd/ebd287.htm#002">Paul</a>, and who is <a href="../ebd/ebd026.htm#006">Apollos</a>, but ministers by whom ye believed, even as the Lord gave to every man?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> I have planted, <a href="../ebd/ebd026.htm#006">Apollos</a> watered; but God gave the increase.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> So then neither is he that planteth any thing, neither he that watereth; but God that giveth the increase.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> Now he that planteth and he that watereth are one: and every man shall receive his own reward according to his own labour.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> For we are labourers together with God: ye are God's husbandry, ye are God's building.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> According to the <a href="../ebd/ebd154.htm#000">grace</a> of God which is given unto me, as a <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#004">wise</a> masterbuilder, I have laid the foundation, and another buildeth thereon. But let every man take heed how he buildeth thereupon.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> For other foundation can no man lay than that is laid, which is <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> Now if any man build upon this foundation <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#000">silver</a>, precious stones, <a href="../ebd/ebd383.htm#003">wood</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd168.htm#002">hay</a>, stubble;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> Every man's work shall be made manifest: for the day shall declare it, because it shall be revealed by fire; and the fire shall try every man's work of what sort it is.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> If any man's work abide which he hath built thereupon, he shall receive a reward.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> If any man's work shall be burned, he shall suffer loss: but he himself shall be saved; yet so as by fire.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> Know ye not that ye are the <a href="../ebd/ebd361.htm#004">temple</a> of God, and that the <a href="../ebd/ebd349.htm#009">Spirit</a> of God dwelleth in you?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> If any man defile the <a href="../ebd/ebd361.htm#004">temple</a> of God, him shall God destroy; for the <a href="../ebd/ebd361.htm#004">temple</a> of God is holy, which <a href="../ebd/ebd361.htm#004">temple</a> ye are.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> Let no man deceive himself. If any man among you seemeth to be <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#004">wise</a> in this world, let him become a fool, that he may be <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#004">wise</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> For the wisdom of this world is foolishness with God. For it is written, He taketh the <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#004">wise</a> in their own craftiness.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> And again, The Lord knoweth the thoughts of the <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#004">wise</a>, that they are vain.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> Therefore let no man <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a> in men. For all things are your's;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> Whether <a href="../ebd/ebd287.htm#002">Paul</a>, or <a href="../ebd/ebd026.htm#006">Apollos</a>, or <a href="../ebd/ebd075.htm#005">Cephas</a>, or the world, or life, or death, or things present, or things to come; all are your's;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> And ye are Christ's; and <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a> is God's.<br>
 </p>
