 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Revelation Chapter 15</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> And I saw another sign in <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#006">heaven</a>, great and marvellous, <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> angels having the <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> last plagues; for in them is filled up the wrath of God.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> And I saw as it were a sea of glass mingled with fire: and them that had gotten the victory over the <a href="../ebd/ebd049.htm#001">beast</a>, and over his image, and over his mark, and over the number of his name, stand on the sea of glass, having the harps of God.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And they sing the song of <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> the servant of God, and the song of the <a href="../ebd/ebd223.htm#003">Lamb</a>, saying, Great and marvellous are thy works, Lord God Almighty; just and true are thy ways, thou <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">King</a> of saints.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Who shall not fear thee, O Lord, and <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#005">glorify</a> thy name? for thou only art holy: for all nations shall come and <a href="../ebd/ebd384.htm#002">worship</a> before thee; for thy judgments are made manifest.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And after that I looked, and, behold, the <a href="../ebd/ebd361.htm#004">temple</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd362.htm#008">testimony</a> in <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#006">heaven</a> was opened:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> angels came out of the <a href="../ebd/ebd361.htm#004">temple</a>, having the <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> plagues, clothed in pure and white <a href="../ebd/ebd229.htm#008">linen</a>, and having their breasts girded with golden girdles.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And one of the four beasts gave unto the <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> angels <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> golden vials full of the wrath of God, who liveth for ever and ever.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd361.htm#004">temple</a> was filled with smoke from the <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a> of God, and from his power; and no man was able to enter into the <a href="../ebd/ebd361.htm#004">temple</a>, till the <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> plagues of the <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> angels were fulfilled.<br>
 </p>
