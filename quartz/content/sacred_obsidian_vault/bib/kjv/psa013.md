 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 13</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> How long wilt thou forget me, O LORD? for ever? how long wilt thou hide thy face from me?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> How long shall I take counsel in my soul, having sorrow in my heart daily? how long shall mine enemy be exalted over me?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Consider and hear me, O LORD my God: lighten mine eyes, lest I sleep the sleep of death;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Lest mine enemy say, I have prevailed against him; and those that trouble me rejoice when I am moved.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> But I have trusted in thy <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a>; my heart shall rejoice in thy <a href="../ebd/ebd320.htm#003">salvation</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> I will sing unto the LORD, because he hath dealt bountifully with me.<br>
 </p>
