 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Exodus Chapter 6</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Then the LORD said unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, Now shalt thou see what I will do to <a href="../ebd/ebd292.htm#005">Pharaoh</a>: for with a strong hand shall he let them go, and with a strong hand shall he drive them out of his land.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> And God spake unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, and said unto him, I am the LORD:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And I appeared unto <a href="../ebd/ebd005.htm#005">Abraham</a>, unto <a href="../ebd/ebd189.htm#006">Isaac</a>, and unto <a href="../ebd/ebd194.htm#007">Jacob</a>, by the name of God Almighty, but by my name <a href="../ebd/ebd201.htm#009">JEHOVAH</a> was I not known to them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And I have also established my <a href="../ebd/ebd091.htm#008">covenant</a> with them, to give them the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#005">Canaan</a>, the land of their pilgrimage, wherein they were strangers.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And I have also heard the groaning of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, whom the Egyptians keep in <a href="../ebd/ebd062.htm#002">bondage</a>; and I have remembered my <a href="../ebd/ebd091.htm#008">covenant</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Wherefore say unto the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, I am the LORD, and I will bring you out from under the burdens of the Egyptians, and I will rid you out of their <a href="../ebd/ebd062.htm#002">bondage</a>, and I will redeem you with a stretched out arm, and with great judgments:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And I will take you to me for a people, and I will be to you a God: and ye shall know that I am the LORD your God, which bringeth you out from under the burdens of the Egyptians.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And I will bring you in unto the land, concerning the which I did swear to give it to <a href="../ebd/ebd005.htm#005">Abraham</a>, to <a href="../ebd/ebd189.htm#006">Isaac</a>, and to <a href="../ebd/ebd194.htm#007">Jacob</a>; and I will give it you for an heritage: I am the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> spake so unto the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>: but they hearkened not unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> for anguish of <a href="../ebd/ebd349.htm#009">spirit</a>, and for cruel <a href="../ebd/ebd062.htm#002">bondage</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And the LORD spake unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> Go in, speak unto <a href="../ebd/ebd292.htm#005">Pharaoh</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a>, that he let the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> go out of his land.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> spake before the LORD, saying, Behold, the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> have not hearkened unto me; how then shall <a href="../ebd/ebd292.htm#005">Pharaoh</a> hear me, who am of uncircumcised lips?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> And the LORD spake unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> and unto <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a>, and gave them a charge unto the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, and unto <a href="../ebd/ebd292.htm#005">Pharaoh</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a>, to bring the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> out of the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> These be the heads of their fathers' houses: The sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd311.htm#009">Reuben</a> the firstborn of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>; Hanoch, and <a href="../ebd/ebd283.htm#000">Pallu</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd177.htm#007">Hezron</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd072.htm#007">Carmi</a>: these be the families of <a href="../ebd/ebd311.htm#009">Reuben</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#002">Simeon</a>; Jemuel, and Jamin, and <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#003">Ohad</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd194.htm#004">Jachin</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd395.htm#006">Zohar</a>, and Shaul the son of a Canaanitish woman: these are the families of <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#002">Simeon</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> And these are the names of the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd227.htm#006">Levi</a> according to their generations; <a href="../ebd/ebd146.htm#009">Gershon</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd222.htm#001">Kohath</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#000">Merari</a>: and the years of the life of <a href="../ebd/ebd227.htm#006">Levi</a> were an hundred thirty and <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> years.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> The sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd146.htm#009">Gershon</a>; <a href="../ebd/ebd228.htm#005">Libni</a>, and Shimi, according to their families.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> And the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd222.htm#001">Kohath</a>; <a href="../ebd/ebd022.htm#001">Amram</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd192.htm#001">Izhar</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd171.htm#004">Hebron</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd376.htm#005">Uzziel</a>: and the years of the life of <a href="../ebd/ebd222.htm#001">Kohath</a> were an hundred thirty and three years.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> And the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#000">Merari</a>; Mahali and <a href="../ebd/ebd262.htm#005">Mushi</a>: these are the families of <a href="../ebd/ebd227.htm#006">Levi</a> according to their generations.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd022.htm#001">Amram</a> took him <a href="../ebd/ebd208.htm#004">Jochebed</a> his father's sister to wife; and she bare him <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>: and the years of the life of <a href="../ebd/ebd022.htm#001">Amram</a> were an hundred and thirty and <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> years.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> And the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd192.htm#001">Izhar</a>; <a href="../ebd/ebd222.htm#003">Korah</a>, and Nepheg, and <a href="../ebd/ebd392.htm#007">Zichri</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> And the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd376.htm#005">Uzziel</a>; <a href="../ebd/ebd256.htm#006">Mishael</a>, and Elzaphan, and <a href="../ebd/ebd394.htm#009">Zithri</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> took him <a href="../ebd/ebd118.htm#003">Elisheba</a>, daughter of <a href="../ebd/ebd020.htm#008">Amminadab</a>, sister of Naashon, to wife; and she bare him <a href="../ebd/ebd264.htm#008">Nadab</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd003.htm#004">Abihu</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd115.htm#000">Eleazar</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#005">Ithamar</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> And the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd222.htm#003">Korah</a>; Assir, and <a href="../ebd/ebd118.htm#005">Elkanah</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd002.htm#004">Abiasaph</a>: these are the families of the <a href="../ebd/ebd222.htm#006">Korhites</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd115.htm#000">Eleazar</a> Aaron's son took him one of the daughters of Putiel to wife; and she bare him <a href="../ebd/ebd294.htm#003">Phinehas</a>: these are the heads of the fathers of the Levites according to their families.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> These are that <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, to whom the LORD said, Bring out the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> from the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a> according to their armies.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> These are they which spake to <a href="../ebd/ebd292.htm#005">Pharaoh</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a>, to bring out the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> from <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a>: these are that <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="028">28</a> And it came to pass on the day when the LORD spake unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> in the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="029">29</a> That the LORD spake unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, saying, I am the LORD: speak thou unto <a href="../ebd/ebd292.htm#005">Pharaoh</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a> all that I say unto thee.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="030">30</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> said before the LORD, Behold, I am of uncircumcised lips, and how shall <a href="../ebd/ebd292.htm#005">Pharaoh</a> hearken unto me?<br>
 </p>
