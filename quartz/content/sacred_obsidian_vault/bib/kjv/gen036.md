 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Genesis Chapter 36</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Now these are the generations of <a href="../ebd/ebd124.htm#005">Esau</a>, who is <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#001">Edom</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd124.htm#005">Esau</a> took his wives of the daughters of <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#005">Canaan</a>; <a href="../ebd/ebd007.htm#006">Adah</a> the daughter of <a href="../ebd/ebd119.htm#000">Elon</a> the Hittite, and <a href="../ebd/ebd015.htm#008">Aholibamah</a> the daughter of <a href="../ebd/ebd022.htm#004">Anah</a> the daughter of <a href="../ebd/ebd392.htm#004">Zibeon</a> the Hivite;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd046.htm#006">Bashemath</a> Ishmael's daughter, sister of Nebajoth.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd007.htm#006">Adah</a> bare to <a href="../ebd/ebd124.htm#005">Esau</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd117.htm#005">Eliphaz</a>; and <a href="../ebd/ebd046.htm#006">Bashemath</a> bare <a href="../ebd/ebd312.htm#001">Reuel</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd015.htm#008">Aholibamah</a> bare <a href="../ebd/ebd206.htm#003">Jeush</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd192.htm#006">Jaalam</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd222.htm#003">Korah</a>: these are the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd124.htm#005">Esau</a>, which were born unto him in the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#005">Canaan</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd124.htm#005">Esau</a> took his wives, and his sons, and his daughters, and all the persons of his house, and his <a href="../ebd/ebd074.htm#002">cattle</a>, and all his beasts, and all his substance, which he had got in the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#005">Canaan</a>; and went into the country from the face of his brother <a href="../ebd/ebd194.htm#007">Jacob</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> For their riches were more than that they might dwell together; and the land wherein they were strangers could not bear them because of their <a href="../ebd/ebd074.htm#002">cattle</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> Thus dwelt <a href="../ebd/ebd124.htm#005">Esau</a> in <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#007">mount</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd326.htm#004">Seir</a>: <a href="../ebd/ebd124.htm#005">Esau</a> is <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#001">Edom</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And these are the generations of <a href="../ebd/ebd124.htm#005">Esau</a> the father of the Edomites in <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#007">mount</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd326.htm#004">Seir</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> These are the names of Esau's sons; <a href="../ebd/ebd117.htm#005">Eliphaz</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd007.htm#006">Adah</a> the wife of <a href="../ebd/ebd124.htm#005">Esau</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd312.htm#001">Reuel</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd046.htm#006">Bashemath</a> the wife of <a href="../ebd/ebd124.htm#005">Esau</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd117.htm#005">Eliphaz</a> were <a href="../ebd/ebd361.htm#001">Teman</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd278.htm#003">Omar</a>, Zepho, and Gatam, and <a href="../ebd/ebd217.htm#003">Kenaz</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And Timna was <a href="../ebd/ebd087.htm#007">concubine</a> to <a href="../ebd/ebd117.htm#005">Eliphaz</a> Esau's son; and she bare to <a href="../ebd/ebd117.htm#005">Eliphaz</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#009">Amalek</a>: these were the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd007.htm#006">Adah</a> Esau's wife.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> And these are the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd312.htm#001">Reuel</a>; <a href="../ebd/ebd265.htm#004">Nahath</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd391.htm#001">Zerah</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd331.htm#002">Shammah</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd258.htm#003">Mizzah</a>: these were the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd046.htm#006">Bashemath</a> Esau's wife.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> And these were the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd015.htm#008">Aholibamah</a>, the daughter of <a href="../ebd/ebd022.htm#004">Anah</a> the daughter of <a href="../ebd/ebd392.htm#004">Zibeon</a>, Esau's wife: and she bare to <a href="../ebd/ebd124.htm#005">Esau</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd206.htm#003">Jeush</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd192.htm#006">Jaalam</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd222.htm#003">Korah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> These were dukes of the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd124.htm#005">Esau</a>: the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd117.htm#005">Eliphaz</a> the firstborn son of <a href="../ebd/ebd124.htm#005">Esau</a>; <a href="../ebd/ebd108.htm#009">duke</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd361.htm#001">Teman</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd108.htm#009">duke</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd278.htm#003">Omar</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd108.htm#009">duke</a> Zepho, <a href="../ebd/ebd108.htm#009">duke</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd217.htm#003">Kenaz</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd108.htm#009">Duke</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd222.htm#003">Korah</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd108.htm#009">duke</a> Gatam, and <a href="../ebd/ebd108.htm#009">duke</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#009">Amalek</a>: these are the dukes that came of <a href="../ebd/ebd117.htm#005">Eliphaz</a> in the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#001">Edom</a>; these were the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd007.htm#006">Adah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> And these are the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd312.htm#001">Reuel</a> Esau's son; <a href="../ebd/ebd108.htm#009">duke</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd265.htm#004">Nahath</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd108.htm#009">duke</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd391.htm#001">Zerah</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd108.htm#009">duke</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd331.htm#002">Shammah</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd108.htm#009">duke</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd258.htm#003">Mizzah</a>: these are the dukes that came of <a href="../ebd/ebd312.htm#001">Reuel</a> in the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#001">Edom</a>; these are the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd046.htm#006">Bashemath</a> Esau's wife.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> And these are the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd015.htm#008">Aholibamah</a> Esau's wife; <a href="../ebd/ebd108.htm#009">duke</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd206.htm#003">Jeush</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd108.htm#009">duke</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd192.htm#006">Jaalam</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd108.htm#009">duke</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd222.htm#003">Korah</a>: these were the dukes that came of <a href="../ebd/ebd015.htm#008">Aholibamah</a> the daughter of <a href="../ebd/ebd022.htm#004">Anah</a>, Esau's wife.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> These are the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd124.htm#005">Esau</a>, who is <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#001">Edom</a>, and these are their dukes.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> These are the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd326.htm#004">Seir</a> the Horite, who inhabited the land; <a href="../ebd/ebd232.htm#003">Lotan</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd340.htm#004">Shobal</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd392.htm#004">Zibeon</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd022.htm#004">Anah</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> And Dishon, and <a href="../ebd/ebd129.htm#004">Ezer</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd104.htm#005">Dishan</a>: these are the dukes of the <a href="../ebd/ebd182.htm#001">Horites</a>, the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd326.htm#004">Seir</a> in the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#001">Edom</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> And the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd232.htm#003">Lotan</a> were Hori and Hemam; and Lotan's sister was Timna.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> And the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd340.htm#004">Shobal</a> were these; Alvan, and Manahath, and <a href="../ebd/ebd111.htm#007">Ebal</a>, Shepho, and Onam.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> And these are the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd392.htm#004">Zibeon</a>; both Ajah, and <a href="../ebd/ebd022.htm#004">Anah</a>: this was that <a href="../ebd/ebd022.htm#004">Anah</a> that found the mules in the <a href="../ebd/ebd381.htm#005">wilderness</a>, as he fed the asses of <a href="../ebd/ebd392.htm#004">Zibeon</a> his father.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> And the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd022.htm#004">Anah</a> were these; Dishon, and <a href="../ebd/ebd015.htm#008">Aholibamah</a> the daughter of <a href="../ebd/ebd022.htm#004">Anah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> And these are the children of Dishon; Hemdan, and Eshban, and Ithran, and Cheran.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> The children of <a href="../ebd/ebd129.htm#004">Ezer</a> are these; Bilhan, and <a href="../ebd/ebd385.htm#004">Zaavan</a>, and Akan.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="028">28</a> The children of <a href="../ebd/ebd104.htm#005">Dishan</a> are these; <a href="../ebd/ebd375.htm#007">Uz</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd028.htm#004">Aran</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="029">29</a> These are the dukes that came of the <a href="../ebd/ebd182.htm#001">Horites</a>; <a href="../ebd/ebd108.htm#009">duke</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd232.htm#003">Lotan</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd108.htm#009">duke</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd340.htm#004">Shobal</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd108.htm#009">duke</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd392.htm#004">Zibeon</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd108.htm#009">duke</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd022.htm#004">Anah</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="030">30</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd108.htm#009">Duke</a> Dishon, <a href="../ebd/ebd108.htm#009">duke</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd129.htm#004">Ezer</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd108.htm#009">duke</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd104.htm#005">Dishan</a>: these are the dukes that came of Hori, among their dukes in the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd326.htm#004">Seir</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="031">31</a> And these are the kings that reigned in the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#001">Edom</a>, before there reigned any <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> over the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="032">32</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd051.htm#006">Bela</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd052.htm#009">Beor</a> reigned in <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#001">Edom</a>: and the name of his city was <a href="../ebd/ebd104.htm#000">Dinhabah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="033">33</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd051.htm#006">Bela</a> died, and <a href="../ebd/ebd208.htm#003">Jobab</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd391.htm#001">Zerah</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd063.htm#009">Bozrah</a> reigned in his stead.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="034">34</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd208.htm#003">Jobab</a> died, and Husham of the land of Temani reigned in his stead.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="035">35</a> And Husham died, and <a href="../ebd/ebd157.htm#000">Hadad</a> the son of Bedad, who smote <a href="../ebd/ebd253.htm#007">Midian</a> in the field of <a href="../ebd/ebd258.htm#005">Moab</a>, reigned in his stead: and the name of his city was Avith.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="036">36</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd157.htm#000">Hadad</a> died, and Samlah of <a href="../ebd/ebd243.htm#004">Masrekah</a> reigned in his stead.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="037">37</a> And Samlah died, and <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#002">Saul</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd309.htm#008">Rehoboth</a> by the river reigned in his stead.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="038">38</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#002">Saul</a> died, and Baalhanan the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd006.htm#007">Achbor</a> reigned in his stead.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="039">39</a> And Baalhanan the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd006.htm#007">Achbor</a> died, and <a href="../ebd/ebd157.htm#003">Hadar</a> reigned in his stead: and the name of his city was <a href="../ebd/ebd287.htm#001">Pau</a>; and his wife's name was <a href="../ebd/ebd246.htm#006">Mehetabel</a>, the daughter of Matred, the daughter of <a href="../ebd/ebd252.htm#000">Mezahab</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="040">40</a> And these are the names of the dukes that came of <a href="../ebd/ebd124.htm#005">Esau</a>, according to their families, after their places, by their names; <a href="../ebd/ebd108.htm#009">duke</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd366.htm#005">Timnah</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd108.htm#009">duke</a> Alvah, <a href="../ebd/ebd108.htm#009">duke</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#008">Jetheth</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="041">41</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd108.htm#009">Duke</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd015.htm#008">Aholibamah</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd108.htm#009">duke</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd114.htm#002">Elah</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd108.htm#009">duke</a> Pinon,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="042">42</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd108.htm#009">Duke</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd217.htm#003">Kenaz</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd108.htm#009">duke</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd361.htm#001">Teman</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd108.htm#009">duke</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd252.htm#004">Mibzar</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="043">43</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd108.htm#009">Duke</a> Magdiel, <a href="../ebd/ebd108.htm#009">duke</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd189.htm#002">Iram</a>: these be the dukes of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#001">Edom</a>, according to their habitations in the land of their possession: he is <a href="../ebd/ebd124.htm#005">Esau</a> the father of the Edomites.<br>
 </p>
