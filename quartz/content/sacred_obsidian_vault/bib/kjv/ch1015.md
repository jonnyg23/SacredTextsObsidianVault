 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: 1 Chronicles Chapter 15</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> made him houses in the city of <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a>, and prepared a place for the <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#000">ark</a> of God, and pitched for it a <a href="../ebd/ebd361.htm#009">tent</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Then <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> said, None ought to carry the <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#000">ark</a> of God but the Levites: for them hath the LORD <a href="../ebd/ebd081.htm#008">chosen</a> to carry the <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#000">ark</a> of God, and to <a href="../ebd/ebd255.htm#007">minister</a> unto him for ever.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> gathered all <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> together to <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>, to bring up the <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#000">ark</a> of the LORD unto his place, which he had prepared for it.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> assembled the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a>, and the Levites:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Of the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd222.htm#001">Kohath</a>; <a href="../ebd/ebd375.htm#003">Uriel</a> the chief, and his brethren an hundred and twenty:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Of the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#000">Merari</a>; Asaiah the chief, and his brethren two hundred and twenty:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Of the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd146.htm#008">Gershom</a>; <a href="../ebd/ebd208.htm#005">Joel</a> the chief and his brethren an hundred and thirty:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> Of the sons of Elizaphan; <a href="../ebd/ebd334.htm#005">Shemaiah</a> the chief, and his brethren two hundred:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> Of the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd171.htm#004">Hebron</a>; <a href="../ebd/ebd116.htm#006">Eliel</a> the chief, and his brethren fourscore:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> Of the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd376.htm#005">Uzziel</a>; <a href="../ebd/ebd020.htm#008">Amminadab</a> the chief, and his brethren an hundred and twelve.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> called for <a href="../ebd/ebd386.htm#008">Zadok</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd002.htm#005">Abiathar</a> the priests, and for the Levites, for <a href="../ebd/ebd375.htm#003">Uriel</a>, Asaiah, and <a href="../ebd/ebd208.htm#005">Joel</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd334.htm#005">Shemaiah</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd116.htm#006">Eliel</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd020.htm#008">Amminadab</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And said unto them, Ye are the chief of the fathers of the Levites: sanctify yourselves, both ye and your brethren, that ye may bring up the <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#000">ark</a> of the LORD God of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> unto the place that I have prepared for it.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> For because ye did it not at the first, the LORD our God made a <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#005">breach</a> upon us, for that we sought him not after the due order.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> So the priests and the Levites sanctified themselves to bring up the <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#000">ark</a> of the LORD God of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And the children of the Levites bare the <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#000">ark</a> of God upon their shoulders with the staves thereon, as <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> commanded according to the word of the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> spake to the chief of the Levites to appoint their brethren to be the singers with instruments of musick, psalteries and harps and <a href="../ebd/ebd095.htm#001">cymbals</a>, sounding, by lifting up the voice with joy.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> So the Levites appointed <a href="../ebd/ebd173.htm#009">Heman</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd208.htm#005">Joel</a>; and of his brethren, <a href="../ebd/ebd033.htm#002">Asaph</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd053.htm#003">Berechiah</a>; and of the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#000">Merari</a> their brethren, <a href="../ebd/ebd126.htm#002">Ethan</a> the son of Kushaiah;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> And with them their brethren of the second degree, <a href="../ebd/ebd389.htm#006">Zechariah</a>, Ben, and <a href="../ebd/ebd193.htm#004">Jaaziel</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd334.htm#009">Shemiramoth</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd200.htm#003">Jehiel</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd374.htm#008">Unni</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd115.htm#008">Eliab</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd052.htm#003">Benaiah</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd234.htm#007">Maaseiah</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd244.htm#006">Mattithiah</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd117.htm#006">Elipheleh</a>, and Mikneiah, and Obededom, and <a href="../ebd/ebd203.htm#000">Jeiel</a>, the porters.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> So the singers, <a href="../ebd/ebd173.htm#009">Heman</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd033.htm#002">Asaph</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd126.htm#002">Ethan</a>, were appointed to sound with <a href="../ebd/ebd095.htm#001">cymbals</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#003">brass</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd389.htm#006">Zechariah</a>, and Aziel, and <a href="../ebd/ebd334.htm#009">Shemiramoth</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd200.htm#003">Jehiel</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd374.htm#008">Unni</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd115.htm#008">Eliab</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd234.htm#007">Maaseiah</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd052.htm#003">Benaiah</a>, with psalteries on <a href="../ebd/ebd016.htm#006">Alamoth</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd244.htm#006">Mattithiah</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd117.htm#006">Elipheleh</a>, and Mikneiah, and Obededom, and <a href="../ebd/ebd203.htm#000">Jeiel</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd037.htm#007">Azaziah</a>, with harps on the <a href="../ebd/ebd334.htm#008">Sheminith</a> to excel.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> And Chenaniah, chief of the Levites, was for song: he instructed about the song, because he was skilful.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd053.htm#003">Berechiah</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd118.htm#005">Elkanah</a> were doorkeepers for the <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#000">ark</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd332.htm#007">Shebaniah</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd201.htm#006">Jehoshaphat</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd271.htm#003">Nethaneel</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd019.htm#004">Amasai</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd389.htm#006">Zechariah</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd052.htm#003">Benaiah</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd116.htm#007">Eliezer</a>, the priests, did blow with the <a href="../ebd/ebd373.htm#001">trumpets</a> before the <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#000">ark</a> of God: and Obededom and Jehiah were doorkeepers for the <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#000">ark</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> So <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a>, and the elders of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, and the captains over <a href="../ebd/ebd364.htm#008">thousands</a>, went to bring up the <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#000">ark</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd091.htm#008">covenant</a> of the LORD out of the house of Obededom with joy.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> And it came to pass, when God helped the Levites that bare the <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#000">ark</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd091.htm#008">covenant</a> of the LORD, that they offered <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> bullocks and <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> rams.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> was clothed with a robe of fine <a href="../ebd/ebd229.htm#008">linen</a>, and all the Levites that bare the <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#000">ark</a>, and the singers, and Chenaniah the master of the song with the singers: <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> also had upon him an <a href="../ebd/ebd122.htm#008">ephod</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd229.htm#008">linen</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="028">28</a> Thus all <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> brought up the <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#000">ark</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd091.htm#008">covenant</a> of the LORD with shouting, and with sound of the <a href="../ebd/ebd090.htm#009">cornet</a>, and with <a href="../ebd/ebd373.htm#001">trumpets</a>, and with <a href="../ebd/ebd095.htm#001">cymbals</a>, making a noise with psalteries and harps.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="029">29</a> And it came to pass, as the <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#000">ark</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd091.htm#008">covenant</a> of the LORD came to the city of <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a>, that <a href="../ebd/ebd253.htm#001">Michal</a>, the daughter of <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#002">Saul</a> looking out at a window saw <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> dancing and playing: and she despised him in her heart.<br>
 </p>
