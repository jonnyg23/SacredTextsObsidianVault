 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Joshua Chapter 22</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Then <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#006">Joshua</a> called the Reubenites, and the Gadites, and the half <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd240.htm#002">Manasseh</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> And said unto them, Ye have kept all that <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> the servant of the LORD commanded you, and have obeyed my voice in all that I commanded you:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Ye have not left your brethren these many days unto this day, but have kept the charge of the commandment of the LORD your God.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And now the LORD your God hath given rest unto your brethren, as he promised them: therefore now return ye, and get you unto your tents, and unto the land of your possession, which <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> the servant of the LORD gave you on the other side <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#004">Jordan</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> But take diligent heed to do the commandment and the law, which <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> the servant of the LORD charged you, to love the LORD your God, and to walk in all his ways, and to keep his commandments, and to cleave unto him, and to serve him with all your heart and with all your soul.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> So <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#006">Joshua</a> blessed them, and sent them away: and they went unto their tents.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Now to the one half of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd240.htm#002">Manasseh</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> had given possession in <a href="../ebd/ebd046.htm#003">Bashan</a>: but unto the other half thereof gave <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#006">Joshua</a> among their brethren on this side <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#004">Jordan</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd380.htm#008">westward</a>. And when <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#006">Joshua</a> sent them away also unto their tents, then he blessed them,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And he spake unto them, saying, Return with much riches unto your tents, and with very much <a href="../ebd/ebd074.htm#002">cattle</a>, with <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#000">silver</a>, and with <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a>, and with <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#003">brass</a>, and with <a href="../ebd/ebd189.htm#004">iron</a>, and with very much raiment: divide the spoil of your enemies with your brethren.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd311.htm#009">Reuben</a> and the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd140.htm#007">Gad</a> and the half <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd240.htm#002">Manasseh</a> returned, and departed from the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> out of <a href="../ebd/ebd337.htm#008">Shiloh</a>, which is in the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#005">Canaan</a>, to go unto the country of <a href="../ebd/ebd148.htm#009">Gilead</a>, to the land of their possession, whereof they were possessed, according to the word of the LORD by the hand of <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And when they came unto the borders of <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#004">Jordan</a>, that are in the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#005">Canaan</a>, the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd311.htm#009">Reuben</a> and the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd140.htm#007">Gad</a> and the half <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd240.htm#002">Manasseh</a> built there an <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> by <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#004">Jordan</a>, a great <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> to see to.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> heard say, Behold, the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd311.htm#009">Reuben</a> and the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd140.htm#007">Gad</a> and the half <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd240.htm#002">Manasseh</a> have built an <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> over against the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#005">Canaan</a>, in the borders of <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#004">Jordan</a>, at the <a href="../ebd/ebd286.htm#003">passage</a> of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And when the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> heard of it, the whole <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> gathered themselves together at <a href="../ebd/ebd337.htm#008">Shiloh</a>, to go up to war against them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> And the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> sent unto the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd311.htm#009">Reuben</a>, and to the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd140.htm#007">Gad</a>, and to the half <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd240.htm#002">Manasseh</a>, into the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd148.htm#009">Gilead</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd294.htm#003">Phinehas</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd115.htm#000">Eleazar</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> And with him ten princes, of each chief house a <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#004">prince</a> throughout all the tribes of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>; and each one was an head of the house of their fathers among the <a href="../ebd/ebd364.htm#008">thousands</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And they came unto the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd311.htm#009">Reuben</a>, and to the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd140.htm#007">Gad</a>, and to the half <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd240.htm#002">Manasseh</a>, unto the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd148.htm#009">Gilead</a>, and they spake with them, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> Thus saith the whole <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> of the LORD, What trespass is this that ye have committed against the God of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, to turn away this day from following the LORD, in that ye have builded you an <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>, that ye might rebel this day against the LORD?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> Is the iniquity of <a href="../ebd/ebd289.htm#006">Peor</a> too little for us, from which we are not cleansed until this day, although there was a <a href="../ebd/ebd297.htm#001">plague</a> in the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> of the LORD,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> But that ye must turn away this day from following the LORD? and it will be, seeing ye rebel to day against the LORD, that to morrow he will be wroth with the whole <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> Notwithstanding, if the land of your possession be unclean, then pass ye over unto the land of the possession of the LORD, wherein the LORD's <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> dwelleth, and take possession among us: but rebel not against the LORD, nor rebel against us, in building you an <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> beside the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> of the LORD our God.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> Did not <a href="../ebd/ebd006.htm#006">Achan</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd391.htm#001">Zerah</a> commit a trespass in the accursed thing, and wrath fell on all the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>? and that man perished not alone in his iniquity.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> Then the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd311.htm#009">Reuben</a> and the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd140.htm#007">Gad</a> and the half <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd240.htm#002">Manasseh</a> answered, and said unto the heads of the <a href="../ebd/ebd364.htm#008">thousands</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> The LORD God of gods, the LORD God of gods, he knoweth, and <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> he shall know; if it be in rebellion, or if in transgression against the LORD, (save us not this day,)<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> That we have built us an <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> to turn from following the LORD, or if to offer thereon burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> or meat <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, or if to offer peace offerings thereon, let the LORD himself require it;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> And if we have not rather done it for fear of this thing, saying, In time to come your children might speak unto our children, saying, What have ye to do with the LORD God of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> For the LORD hath made <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#004">Jordan</a> a border between us and you, ye children of <a href="../ebd/ebd311.htm#009">Reuben</a> and children of <a href="../ebd/ebd140.htm#007">Gad</a>; ye have no part in the LORD: so shall your children make our children cease from fearing the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> Therefore we said, Let us now prepare to build us an <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>, not for burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, nor for <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#002">sacrifice</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> But that it may be a <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#008">witness</a> between us, and you, and our generations after us, that we might do the service of the LORD before him with our burnt offerings, and with our sacrifices, and with our peace offerings; that your children may not say to our children in time to come, Ye have no part in the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="028">28</a> Therefore said we, that it shall be, when they should so say to us or to our generations in time to come, that we may say again, Behold the pattern of the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> of the LORD, which our fathers made, not for burnt offerings, nor for sacrifices; but it is a <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#008">witness</a> between us and you.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="029">29</a> God forbid that we should rebel against the LORD, and turn this day from following the LORD, to build an <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> for burnt offerings, for meat offerings, or for sacrifices, beside the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> of the LORD our God that is before his <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="030">30</a> And when <a href="../ebd/ebd294.htm#003">Phinehas</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a>, and the princes of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> and heads of the <a href="../ebd/ebd364.htm#008">thousands</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> which were with him, heard the words that the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd311.htm#009">Reuben</a> and the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd140.htm#007">Gad</a> and the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd240.htm#002">Manasseh</a> spake, it pleased them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="031">31</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd294.htm#003">Phinehas</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd115.htm#000">Eleazar</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> said unto the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd311.htm#009">Reuben</a>, and to the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd140.htm#007">Gad</a>, and to the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd240.htm#002">Manasseh</a>, This day we perceive that the LORD is among us, because ye have not committed this trespass against the LORD: now ye have delivered the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> out of the hand of the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="032">32</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd294.htm#003">Phinehas</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd115.htm#000">Eleazar</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a>, and the princes, returned from the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd311.htm#009">Reuben</a>, and from the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd140.htm#007">Gad</a>, out of the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd148.htm#009">Gilead</a>, unto the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#005">Canaan</a>, to the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, and brought them word again.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="033">33</a> And the thing pleased the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>; and the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> blessed God, and did not intend to go up against them in battle, to destroy the land wherein the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd311.htm#009">Reuben</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd140.htm#007">Gad</a> dwelt.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="034">34</a> And the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd311.htm#009">Reuben</a> and the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd140.htm#007">Gad</a> called the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd112.htm#007">Ed</a>: for it shall be a <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#008">witness</a> between us that the LORD is God.<br>
 </p>
