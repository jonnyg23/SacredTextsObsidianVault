 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Judges Chapter 10</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> And after <a href="../ebd/ebd004.htm#000">Abimelech</a> there arose to defend <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd369.htm#007">Tola</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd301.htm#008">Puah</a>, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd105.htm#005">Dodo</a>, a man of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#002">Issachar</a>; and he dwelt in <a href="../ebd/ebd331.htm#001">Shamir</a> in <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#007">mount</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd123.htm#000">Ephraim</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> And he judged <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> twenty and three years, and died, and was buried in <a href="../ebd/ebd331.htm#001">Shamir</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And after him arose <a href="../ebd/ebd196.htm#001">Jair</a>, a Gileadite, and judged <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> twenty and two years.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And he had thirty sons that rode on thirty <a href="../ebd/ebd035.htm#001">ass</a> colts, and they had thirty cities, which are called Havothjair unto this day, which are in the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd148.htm#009">Gilead</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd196.htm#001">Jair</a> died, and was buried in <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#001">Camon</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> did evil again in the sight of the LORD, and served <a href="../ebd/ebd039.htm#007">Baalim</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd034.htm#004">Ashtaroth</a>, and the gods of <a href="../ebd/ebd355.htm#002">Syria</a>, and the gods of <a href="../ebd/ebd393.htm#000">Zidon</a>, and the gods of <a href="../ebd/ebd258.htm#005">Moab</a>, and the gods of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd021.htm#002">Ammon</a>, and the gods of the <a href="../ebd/ebd294.htm#002">Philistines</a>, and forsook the LORD, and served not him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd024.htm#002">anger</a> of the LORD was hot against <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, and he sold them into the hands of the <a href="../ebd/ebd294.htm#002">Philistines</a>, and into the hands of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd021.htm#002">Ammon</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And that year they vexed and oppressed the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>: eighteen years, all the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> that were on the other side <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#004">Jordan</a> in the land of the <a href="../ebd/ebd021.htm#006">Amorites</a>, which is in <a href="../ebd/ebd148.htm#009">Gilead</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> Moreover the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd021.htm#002">Ammon</a> passed over <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#004">Jordan</a> to fight also against <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>, and against <a href="../ebd/ebd052.htm#008">Benjamin</a>, and against the house of <a href="../ebd/ebd123.htm#000">Ephraim</a>; so that <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> was sore distressed.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> cried unto the LORD, saying, We have sinned against thee, both because we have forsaken our God, and also served <a href="../ebd/ebd039.htm#007">Baalim</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And the LORD said unto the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, Did not I deliver you from the Egyptians, and from the <a href="../ebd/ebd021.htm#006">Amorites</a>, from the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd021.htm#002">Ammon</a>, and from the <a href="../ebd/ebd294.htm#002">Philistines</a>?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> The Zidonians also, and the Amalekites, and the Maonites, did oppress you; and ye cried to me, and I delivered you out of their hand.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> Yet ye have forsaken me, and served other gods: wherefore I will deliver you no more.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> Go and cry unto the gods which ye have <a href="../ebd/ebd081.htm#008">chosen</a>; let them deliver you in the time of your <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#005">tribulation</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> said unto the LORD, We have sinned: do thou unto us whatsoever seemeth good unto thee; deliver us only, we pray thee, this day.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> And they put away the strange gods from among them, and served the LORD: and his soul was grieved for the misery of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> Then the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd021.htm#002">Ammon</a> were gathered together, and encamped in <a href="../ebd/ebd148.htm#009">Gilead</a>. And the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> assembled themselves together, and encamped in Mizpeh.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> And the people and princes of <a href="../ebd/ebd148.htm#009">Gilead</a> said one to another, What man is he that will begin to fight against the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd021.htm#002">Ammon</a>? he shall be head over all the inhabitants of <a href="../ebd/ebd148.htm#009">Gilead</a>.<br>
 </p>
