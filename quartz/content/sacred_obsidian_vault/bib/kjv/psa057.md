 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 57</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Be merciful unto me, O God, be merciful unto me: for my soul trusteth in thee: yea, in the <a href="../ebd/ebd330.htm#002">shadow</a> of thy wings will I make my refuge, until these calamities be overpast.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> I will cry unto God most high; unto God that performeth all things for me.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> He shall send from <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#006">heaven</a>, and save me from the reproach of him that would <a href="../ebd/ebd353.htm#009">swallow</a> me up. <a href="../ebd/ebd326.htm#008">Selah</a>. God shall send forth his <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> and his truth.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> My soul is among <a href="../ebd/ebd230.htm#001">lions</a>: and I lie even among them that are set on fire, even the sons of men, whose teeth are spears and <a href="../ebd/ebd032.htm#005">arrows</a>, and their tongue a sharp <a href="../ebd/ebd354.htm#003">sword</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Be thou exalted, O God, above the heavens; let thy <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a> be above all the earth.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> They have prepared a <a href="../ebd/ebd271.htm#002">net</a> for my steps; my soul is bowed down: they have digged a <a href="../ebd/ebd296.htm#007">pit</a> before me, into the midst whereof they are fallen themselves. <a href="../ebd/ebd326.htm#008">Selah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> My heart is fixed, O God, my heart is fixed: I will sing and give praise.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> Awake up, my <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a>; awake, <a href="../ebd/ebd301.htm#006">psaltery</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd165.htm#001">harp</a>: I myself will awake early.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> I will praise thee, O Lord, among the people: I will sing unto thee among the nations.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> For thy <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> is great unto the heavens, and thy truth unto the clouds.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> Be thou exalted, O God, above the heavens: let thy <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a> be above all the earth.<br>
 </p>
