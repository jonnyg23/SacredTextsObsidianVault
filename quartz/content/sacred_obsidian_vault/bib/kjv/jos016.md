 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Joshua Chapter 16</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> And the lot of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#005">Joseph</a> fell from <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#004">Jordan</a> by <a href="../ebd/ebd203.htm#008">Jericho</a>, unto the water of <a href="../ebd/ebd203.htm#008">Jericho</a> on the <a href="../ebd/ebd111.htm#000">east</a>, to the <a href="../ebd/ebd381.htm#005">wilderness</a> that goeth up from <a href="../ebd/ebd203.htm#008">Jericho</a> throughout <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#007">mount</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd055.htm#006">Bethel</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> And goeth out from <a href="../ebd/ebd055.htm#006">Bethel</a> to <a href="../ebd/ebd233.htm#006">Luz</a>, and passeth along unto the borders of <a href="../ebd/ebd029.htm#004">Archi</a> to <a href="../ebd/ebd036.htm#000">Ataroth</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And goeth down <a href="../ebd/ebd380.htm#008">westward</a> to the coast of Japhleti, unto the coast of Bethhoron the nether, and to <a href="../ebd/ebd147.htm#004">Gezer</a>; and the goings out thereof are at the sea.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> So the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#005">Joseph</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd240.htm#002">Manasseh</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd123.htm#000">Ephraim</a>, took their inheritance.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And the border of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd123.htm#000">Ephraim</a> according to their families was thus: even the border of their inheritance on the <a href="../ebd/ebd111.htm#000">east</a> side was Atarothaddar, unto Bethhoron the upper;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And the border went out toward the sea to <a href="../ebd/ebd253.htm#003">Michmethah</a> on the north side; and the border went about eastward unto Taanathshiloh, and passed by it on the <a href="../ebd/ebd111.htm#000">east</a> to Janohah;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And it went down from Janohah to <a href="../ebd/ebd036.htm#000">Ataroth</a>, and to <a href="../ebd/ebd264.htm#004">Naarath</a>, and came to <a href="../ebd/ebd203.htm#008">Jericho</a>, and went out at <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#004">Jordan</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> The border went out from <a href="../ebd/ebd358.htm#008">Tappuah</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd380.htm#008">westward</a> unto the river <a href="../ebd/ebd215.htm#006">Kanah</a>; and the goings out thereof were at the sea. This is the inheritance of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd123.htm#000">Ephraim</a> by their families.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And the separate cities for the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd123.htm#000">Ephraim</a> were among the inheritance of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd240.htm#002">Manasseh</a>, all the cities with their <a href="../ebd/ebd377.htm#003">villages</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And they drave not out the <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#007">Canaanites</a> that dwelt in <a href="../ebd/ebd147.htm#004">Gezer</a>: but the <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#007">Canaanites</a> dwell among the Ephraimites unto this day, and serve under <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#006">tribute</a>.<br>
 </p>
