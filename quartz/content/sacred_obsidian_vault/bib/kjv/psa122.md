 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 122</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> I was glad when they said unto me, Let us go into the house of the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Our feet shall stand within thy gates, O <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a> is builded as a city that is compact together:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Whither the tribes go up, the tribes of the LORD, unto the <a href="../ebd/ebd362.htm#008">testimony</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, to give thanks unto the name of the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> For there are set thrones of judgment, the thrones of the house of <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Pray for the peace of <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>: they shall prosper that love thee.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Peace be within thy walls, and prosperity within thy palaces.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> For my brethren and companions' sakes, I will now say, Peace be within thee.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> Because of the house of the LORD our God I will seek thy good.<br>
 </p>
