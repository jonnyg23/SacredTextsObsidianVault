 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Exodus Chapter 39</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> And of the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#003">blue</a>, and purple, and <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#005">scarlet</a>, they made cloths of service, to do service in the holy place, and made the holy garments for <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a>; as the LORD commanded <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> And he made the <a href="../ebd/ebd122.htm#008">ephod</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#003">blue</a>, and purple, and <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#005">scarlet</a>, and fine twined <a href="../ebd/ebd229.htm#008">linen</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And they did beat the <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a> into thin plates, and cut it into wires, to work it in the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#003">blue</a>, and in the purple, and in the <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#005">scarlet</a>, and in the fine <a href="../ebd/ebd229.htm#008">linen</a>, with cunning work.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> They made shoulderpieces for it, to couple it together: by the two edges was it coupled together.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And the curious <a href="../ebd/ebd149.htm#005">girdle</a> of his <a href="../ebd/ebd122.htm#008">ephod</a>, that was upon it, was of the same, according to the work thereof; of <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#003">blue</a>, and purple, and <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#005">scarlet</a>, and fine twined <a href="../ebd/ebd229.htm#008">linen</a>; as the LORD commanded <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And they wrought <a href="../ebd/ebd279.htm#004">onyx</a> stones inclosed in <a href="../ebd/ebd281.htm#004">ouches</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a>, graven, as signets are graven, with the names of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And he put them on the shoulders of the <a href="../ebd/ebd122.htm#008">ephod</a>, that they should be stones for a memorial to the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>; as the LORD commanded <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And he made the <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#007">breastplate</a> of cunning work, like the work of the <a href="../ebd/ebd122.htm#008">ephod</a>; of <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#003">blue</a>, and purple, and <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#005">scarlet</a>, and fine twined <a href="../ebd/ebd229.htm#008">linen</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> It was foursquare; they made the <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#007">breastplate</a> double: a span was the length thereof, and a span the breadth thereof, being doubled.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And they set in it four rows of stones: the first row was a sardius, a <a href="../ebd/ebd370.htm#005">topaz</a>, and a <a href="../ebd/ebd072.htm#003">carbuncle</a>: this was the first row.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And the second row, an <a href="../ebd/ebd119.htm#007">emerald</a>, a <a href="../ebd/ebd322.htm#002">sapphire</a>, and a <a href="../ebd/ebd103.htm#001">diamond</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And the third row, a <a href="../ebd/ebd229.htm#004">ligure</a>, an <a href="../ebd/ebd012.htm#000">agate</a>, and an <a href="../ebd/ebd020.htm#002">amethyst</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> And the fourth row, a <a href="../ebd/ebd053.htm#008">beryl</a>, an <a href="../ebd/ebd279.htm#004">onyx</a>, and a <a href="../ebd/ebd198.htm#003">jasper</a>: they were inclosed in <a href="../ebd/ebd281.htm#004">ouches</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a> in their inclosings.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> And the stones were according to the names of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, twelve, according to their names, like the engravings of a <a href="../ebd/ebd343.htm#001">signet</a>, every one with his name, according to the twelve tribes.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And they made upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#007">breastplate</a> chains at the ends, of wreathen work of pure <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> And they made two <a href="../ebd/ebd281.htm#004">ouches</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a>, and two <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a> rings; and put the two rings in the two ends of the <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#007">breastplate</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> And they put the two wreathen chains of <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a> in the two rings on the ends of the <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#007">breastplate</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> And the two ends of the two wreathen chains they fastened in the two <a href="../ebd/ebd281.htm#004">ouches</a>, and put them on the shoulderpieces of the <a href="../ebd/ebd122.htm#008">ephod</a>, before it.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> And they made two rings of <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a>, and put them on the two ends of the <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#007">breastplate</a>, upon the border of it, which was on the side of the <a href="../ebd/ebd122.htm#008">ephod</a> inward.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> And they made two other golden rings, and put them on the two sides of the <a href="../ebd/ebd122.htm#008">ephod</a> underneath, toward the forepart of it, over against the other coupling thereof, above the curious <a href="../ebd/ebd149.htm#005">girdle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd122.htm#008">ephod</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> And they did bind the <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#007">breastplate</a> by his rings unto the rings of the <a href="../ebd/ebd122.htm#008">ephod</a> with a lace of <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#003">blue</a>, that it might be above the curious <a href="../ebd/ebd149.htm#005">girdle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd122.htm#008">ephod</a>, and that the <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#007">breastplate</a> might not be loosed from the <a href="../ebd/ebd122.htm#008">ephod</a>; as the LORD commanded <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> And he made the robe of the <a href="../ebd/ebd122.htm#008">ephod</a> of woven work, all of <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#003">blue</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> And there was an hole in the midst of the robe, as the hole of an <a href="../ebd/ebd156.htm#006">habergeon</a>, with a band round about the hole, that it should not rend.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> And they made upon the hems of the robe pomegranates of <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#003">blue</a>, and purple, and <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#005">scarlet</a>, and twined <a href="../ebd/ebd229.htm#008">linen</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> And they made bells of pure <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a>, and put the bells between the pomegranates upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd173.htm#008">hem</a> of the robe, round about between the pomegranates;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> A <a href="../ebd/ebd051.htm#008">bell</a> and a <a href="../ebd/ebd298.htm#000">pomegranate</a>, a <a href="../ebd/ebd051.htm#008">bell</a> and a <a href="../ebd/ebd298.htm#000">pomegranate</a>, round about the <a href="../ebd/ebd173.htm#008">hem</a> of the robe to <a href="../ebd/ebd255.htm#007">minister</a> in; as the LORD commanded <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> And they made coats of fine <a href="../ebd/ebd229.htm#008">linen</a> of woven work for <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a>, and for his sons,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="028">28</a> And a <a href="../ebd/ebd257.htm#006">mitre</a> of fine <a href="../ebd/ebd229.htm#008">linen</a>, and goodly bonnets of fine <a href="../ebd/ebd229.htm#008">linen</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd229.htm#008">linen</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#008">breeches</a> of fine twined <a href="../ebd/ebd229.htm#008">linen</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="029">29</a> And a <a href="../ebd/ebd149.htm#005">girdle</a> of fine twined <a href="../ebd/ebd229.htm#008">linen</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#003">blue</a>, and purple, and <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#005">scarlet</a>, of needlework; as the LORD commanded <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="030">30</a> And they made the plate of the holy <a href="../ebd/ebd093.htm#001">crown</a> of pure <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a>, and wrote upon it a <a href="../ebd/ebd384.htm#005">writing</a>, like to the engravings of a <a href="../ebd/ebd343.htm#001">signet</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd180.htm#006">HOLINESS</a> TO THE LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="031">31</a> And they tied unto it a lace of <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#003">blue</a>, to fasten it on high upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd257.htm#006">mitre</a>; as the LORD commanded <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="032">32</a> Thus was all the work of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd361.htm#009">tent</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> finished: and the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> did according to all that the LORD commanded <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, so did they.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="033">33</a> And they brought the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, the <a href="../ebd/ebd361.htm#009">tent</a>, and all his furniture, his <a href="../ebd/ebd357.htm#001">taches</a>, his boards, his bars, and his pillars, and his sockets,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="034">34</a> And the covering of rams' skins dyed red, and the covering of badgers' skins, and the vail of the covering,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="035">35</a> The <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#000">ark</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd362.htm#008">testimony</a>, and the staves thereof, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> seat,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="036">36</a> The table, and all the vessels thereof, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd336.htm#008">shewbread</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="037">37</a> The pure <a href="../ebd/ebd071.htm#001">candlestick</a>, with the lamps thereof, even with the lamps to be set in order, and all the vessels thereof, and the oil for light,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="038">38</a> And the golden <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>, and the anointing oil, and the sweet <a href="../ebd/ebd188.htm#002">incense</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd163.htm#001">hanging</a> for the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> door,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="039">39</a> The brasen <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>, and his <a href="../ebd/ebd154.htm#007">grate</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#003">brass</a>, his staves, and all his vessels, the <a href="../ebd/ebd224.htm#009">laver</a> and his foot,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="040">40</a> The hangings of the court, his pillars, and his sockets, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd163.htm#001">hanging</a> for the court <a href="../ebd/ebd143.htm#008">gate</a>, his cords, and his pins, and all the vessels of the service of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a>, for the <a href="../ebd/ebd361.htm#009">tent</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="041">41</a> The cloths of service to do service in the holy place, and the holy garments for <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a>, and his sons' garments, to <a href="../ebd/ebd255.htm#007">minister</a> in the priest's office.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="042">42</a> According to all that the LORD commanded <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, so the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> made all the work.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="043">43</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> did look upon all the work, and, behold, they had done it as the LORD had commanded, even so had they done it: and <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> blessed them.<br>
 </p>
