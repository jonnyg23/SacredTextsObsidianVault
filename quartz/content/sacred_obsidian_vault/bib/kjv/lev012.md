 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Leviticus Chapter 12</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> And the LORD spake unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Speak unto the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, saying, If a woman have conceived seed, and born a man child: then she shall be unclean <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> days; according to the days of the separation for her infirmity shall she be unclean.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And in the eighth day the <a href="../ebd/ebd135.htm#007">flesh</a> of his foreskin shall be circumcised.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And she shall then continue in the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> of her purifying three and thirty days; she shall touch no hallowed thing, nor come into the <a href="../ebd/ebd321.htm#005">sanctuary</a>, until the days of her purifying be fulfilled.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> But if she bear a maid child, then she shall be unclean two weeks, as in her separation: and she shall continue in the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> of her purifying threescore and six days.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And when the days of her purifying are fulfilled, for a son, or for a daughter, she shall bring a <a href="../ebd/ebd223.htm#003">lamb</a> of the first year for a burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, and a young <a href="../ebd/ebd295.htm#004">pigeon</a>, or a turtledove, for a <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, unto the door of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>, unto the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Who shall offer it before the LORD, and make an <a href="../ebd/ebd036.htm#004">atonement</a> for her; and she shall be cleansed from the issue of her <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a>. This is the law for her that hath born a male or a female.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And if she be not able to bring a <a href="../ebd/ebd223.htm#003">lamb</a>, then she shall bring two turtles, or two young pigeons; the one for the burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, and the other for a <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>: and the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> shall make an <a href="../ebd/ebd036.htm#004">atonement</a> for her, and she shall be <a href="../ebd/ebd084.htm#005">clean</a>.<br>
 </p>
