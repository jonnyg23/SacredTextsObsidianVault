 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Numbers Chapter 10</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> And the LORD spake unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Make thee two <a href="../ebd/ebd373.htm#001">trumpets</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#000">silver</a>; of a whole piece shalt thou make them: that thou mayest use them for the <a href="../ebd/ebd069.htm#007">calling</a> of the assembly, and for the journeying of the camps.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And when they shall blow with them, all the assembly shall assemble themselves to thee at the door of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And if they blow but with one trumpet, then the princes, which are heads of the <a href="../ebd/ebd364.htm#008">thousands</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, shall gather themselves unto thee.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> When ye blow an <a href="../ebd/ebd016.htm#007">alarm</a>, then the camps that lie on the <a href="../ebd/ebd111.htm#000">east</a> parts shall go forward.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> When ye blow an <a href="../ebd/ebd016.htm#007">alarm</a> the second time, then the camps that lie on the south side shall take their <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#001">journey</a>: they shall blow an <a href="../ebd/ebd016.htm#007">alarm</a> for their journeys.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> But when the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> is to be gathered together, ye shall blow, but ye shall not sound an <a href="../ebd/ebd016.htm#007">alarm</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a>, the priests, shall blow with the <a href="../ebd/ebd373.htm#001">trumpets</a>; and they shall be to you for an ordinance for ever throughout your generations.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And if ye go to war in your land against the enemy that oppresseth you, then ye shall blow an <a href="../ebd/ebd016.htm#007">alarm</a> with the <a href="../ebd/ebd373.htm#001">trumpets</a>; and ye shall be remembered before the LORD your God, and ye shall be saved from your enemies.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> Also in the day of your gladness, and in your solemn days, and in the beginnings of your months, ye shall blow with the <a href="../ebd/ebd373.htm#001">trumpets</a> over your burnt offerings, and over the sacrifices of your peace offerings; that they may be to you for a memorial before your God: I am the LORD your God.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And it came to pass on the twentieth day of the second month, in the second year, that the <a href="../ebd/ebd085.htm#001">cloud</a> was taken up from off the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd362.htm#008">testimony</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> took their journeys out of the <a href="../ebd/ebd381.htm#005">wilderness</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#009">Sinai</a>; and the <a href="../ebd/ebd085.htm#001">cloud</a> rested in the <a href="../ebd/ebd381.htm#005">wilderness</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd284.htm#006">Paran</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> And they first took their <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#001">journey</a> according to the commandment of the LORD by the hand of <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> In the first place went the standard of the <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#002">camp</a> of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a> according to their armies: and over his <a href="../ebd/ebd183.htm#007">host</a> was <a href="../ebd/ebd265.htm#007">Nahshon</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd020.htm#008">Amminadab</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And over the <a href="../ebd/ebd183.htm#007">host</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#002">Issachar</a> was <a href="../ebd/ebd271.htm#003">Nethaneel</a> the son of Zuar.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> And over the <a href="../ebd/ebd183.htm#007">host</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd389.htm#003">Zebulun</a> was <a href="../ebd/ebd115.htm#008">Eliab</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd173.htm#005">Helon</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> was taken down; and the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd146.htm#009">Gershon</a> and the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#000">Merari</a> set forward, bearing the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> And the standard of the <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#002">camp</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd311.htm#009">Reuben</a> set forward according to their armies: and over his <a href="../ebd/ebd183.htm#007">host</a> was Elizur the son of Shedeur.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> And over the <a href="../ebd/ebd183.htm#007">host</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#002">Simeon</a> was Shelumiel the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd396.htm#007">Zurishaddai</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> And over the <a href="../ebd/ebd183.htm#007">host</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd140.htm#007">Gad</a> was Eliasaph the son of Deuel.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd222.htm#002">Kohathites</a> set forward, bearing the <a href="../ebd/ebd321.htm#005">sanctuary</a>: and the other did set up the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> against they came.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> And the standard of the <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#002">camp</a> of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd123.htm#000">Ephraim</a> set forward according to their armies: and over his <a href="../ebd/ebd183.htm#007">host</a> was <a href="../ebd/ebd118.htm#001">Elishama</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd020.htm#007">Ammihud</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> And over the <a href="../ebd/ebd183.htm#007">host</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd240.htm#002">Manasseh</a> was <a href="../ebd/ebd142.htm#006">Gamaliel</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd288.htm#000">Pedahzur</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> And over the <a href="../ebd/ebd183.htm#007">host</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd052.htm#008">Benjamin</a> was <a href="../ebd/ebd002.htm#008">Abidan</a> the son of Gideoni.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> And the standard of the <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#002">camp</a> of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd096.htm#009">Dan</a> set forward, which was the <a href="../ebd/ebd311.htm#004">rereward</a> of all the camps throughout their hosts: and over his <a href="../ebd/ebd183.htm#007">host</a> was <a href="../ebd/ebd013.htm#008">Ahiezer</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd021.htm#000">Ammishaddai</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> And over the <a href="../ebd/ebd183.htm#007">host</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd033.htm#008">Asher</a> was <a href="../ebd/ebd282.htm#005">Pagiel</a> the son of Ocran.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> And over the <a href="../ebd/ebd183.htm#007">host</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd266.htm#006">Naphtali</a> was <a href="../ebd/ebd014.htm#008">Ahira</a> the son of Enan.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="028">28</a> Thus were the journeyings of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> according to their armies, when they set forward.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="029">29</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> said unto <a href="../ebd/ebd180.htm#000">Hobab</a>, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd305.htm#005">Raguel</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd253.htm#008">Midianite</a>, Moses' father in law, We are journeying unto the place of which the LORD said, I will give it you: come thou with us, and we will do thee good: for the LORD hath spoken good concerning <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="030">30</a> And he said unto him, I will not go; but I will depart to mine own land, and to my kindred.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="031">31</a> And he said, Leave us not, I pray thee; forasmuch as thou knowest how we are to <a href="../ebd/ebd120.htm#003">encamp</a> in the <a href="../ebd/ebd381.htm#005">wilderness</a>, and thou mayest be to us instead of eyes.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="032">32</a> And it shall be, if thou go with us, yea, it shall be, that what <a href="../ebd/ebd153.htm#000">goodness</a> the LORD shall do unto us, the same will we do unto thee.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="033">33</a> And they departed from the <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#007">mount</a> of the LORD three days' <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#001">journey</a>: and the <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#000">ark</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd091.htm#008">covenant</a> of the LORD went before them in the three days' <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#001">journey</a>, to search out a resting place for them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="034">34</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd085.htm#001">cloud</a> of the LORD was upon them by day, when they went out of the <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#002">camp</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="035">35</a> And it came to pass, when the <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#000">ark</a> set forward, that <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> said, Rise up, LORD, and let thine enemies be scattered; and let them that hate thee flee before thee.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="036">36</a> And when it rested, he said, Return, O LORD, unto the many <a href="../ebd/ebd364.htm#008">thousands</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>.<br>
 </p>
