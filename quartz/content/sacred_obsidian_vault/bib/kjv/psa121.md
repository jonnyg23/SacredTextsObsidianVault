 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 121</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> I will lift up mine eyes unto the hills, from whence cometh my help.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> My help cometh from the LORD, which made <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#006">heaven</a> and earth.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> He will not suffer thy foot to be moved: he that keepeth thee will not slumber.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Behold, he that keepeth <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> shall neither slumber nor sleep.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> The LORD is thy keeper: the LORD is thy shade upon thy right hand.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> The <a href="../ebd/ebd353.htm#001">sun</a> shall not smite thee by day, nor the <a href="../ebd/ebd259.htm#004">moon</a> by night.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> The LORD shall preserve thee from all evil: he shall preserve thy soul.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> The LORD shall preserve thy going out and thy coming in from this time forth, and even for evermore.<br>
 </p>
