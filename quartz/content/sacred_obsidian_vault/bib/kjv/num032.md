 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Numbers Chapter 32</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Now the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd311.htm#009">Reuben</a> and the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd140.htm#007">Gad</a> had a very great multitude of <a href="../ebd/ebd074.htm#002">cattle</a>: and when they saw the land of Jazer, and the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd148.htm#009">Gilead</a>, that, behold, the place was a place for <a href="../ebd/ebd074.htm#002">cattle</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> The children of <a href="../ebd/ebd140.htm#007">Gad</a> and the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd311.htm#009">Reuben</a> came and spake unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, and to <a href="../ebd/ebd115.htm#000">Eleazar</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a>, and unto the princes of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd036.htm#000">Ataroth</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd103.htm#005">Dibon</a>, and Jazer, and <a href="../ebd/ebd273.htm#002">Nimrah</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd176.htm#009">Heshbon</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd114.htm#009">Elealeh</a>, and Shebam, and <a href="../ebd/ebd268.htm#004">Nebo</a>, and Beon,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Even the country which the LORD smote before the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, is a land for <a href="../ebd/ebd074.htm#002">cattle</a>, and thy servants have <a href="../ebd/ebd074.htm#002">cattle</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Wherefore, said they, if we have found <a href="../ebd/ebd154.htm#000">grace</a> in thy sight, let this land be given unto thy servants for a possession, and bring us not over <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#004">Jordan</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> said unto the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd140.htm#007">Gad</a> and to the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd311.htm#009">Reuben</a>, Shall your brethren go to war, and shall ye sit here?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And wherefore discourage ye the heart of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> from going over into the land which the LORD hath given them?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> Thus did your fathers, when I sent them from Kadeshbarnea to see the land.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> For when they went up unto the <a href="../ebd/ebd376.htm#008">valley</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd125.htm#000">Eshcol</a>, and saw the land, they discouraged the heart of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, that they should not go into the land which the LORD had given them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And the LORD's <a href="../ebd/ebd024.htm#002">anger</a> was kindled the same time, and he sware, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> Surely none of the men that came up out of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a>, from twenty years old and upward, shall see the land which I sware unto <a href="../ebd/ebd005.htm#005">Abraham</a>, unto <a href="../ebd/ebd189.htm#006">Isaac</a>, and unto <a href="../ebd/ebd194.htm#007">Jacob</a>; because they have not wholly followed me:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> Save <a href="../ebd/ebd069.htm#003">Caleb</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd203.htm#004">Jephunneh</a> the Kenezite, and <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#006">Joshua</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd275.htm#006">Nun</a>: for they have wholly followed the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> And the LORD's <a href="../ebd/ebd024.htm#002">anger</a> was kindled against <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, and he made them wander in the <a href="../ebd/ebd381.htm#005">wilderness</a> forty years, until all the <a href="../ebd/ebd145.htm#008">generation</a>, that had done evil in the sight of the LORD, was consumed.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> And, behold, ye are risen up in your fathers' stead, an increase of sinful men, to augment yet the fierce <a href="../ebd/ebd024.htm#002">anger</a> of the LORD toward <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> For if ye turn away from after him, he will yet again leave them in the <a href="../ebd/ebd381.htm#005">wilderness</a>; and ye shall destroy all this people.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> And they came near unto him, and said, We will build sheepfolds here for our <a href="../ebd/ebd074.htm#002">cattle</a>, and cities for our little ones:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> But we ourselves will go ready armed before the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, until we have brought them unto their place: and our little ones shall dwell in the fenced cities because of the inhabitants of the land.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> We will not return unto our houses, until the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> have inherited every man his inheritance.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> For we will not inherit with them on yonder side <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#004">Jordan</a>, or forward; because our inheritance is fallen to us on this side <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#004">Jordan</a> eastward.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> said unto them, If ye will do this thing, if ye will go armed before the LORD to war,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> And will go all of you armed over <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#004">Jordan</a> before the LORD, until he hath driven out his enemies from before him,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> And the land be subdued before the LORD: then afterward ye shall return, and be guiltless before the LORD, and before <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>; and this land shall be your possession before the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> But if ye will not do so, behold, ye have sinned against the LORD: and be sure your <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> will find you out.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> Build you cities for your little ones, and folds for your <a href="../ebd/ebd333.htm#005">sheep</a>; and do that which hath proceeded out of your mouth.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> And the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd140.htm#007">Gad</a> and the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd311.htm#009">Reuben</a> spake unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, saying, Thy servants will do as my lord commandeth.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> Our little ones, our wives, our flocks, and all our <a href="../ebd/ebd074.htm#002">cattle</a>, shall be there in the cities of <a href="../ebd/ebd148.htm#009">Gilead</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> But thy servants will pass over, every man armed for war, before the LORD to battle, as my lord saith.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="028">28</a> So concerning them <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> commanded <a href="../ebd/ebd115.htm#000">Eleazar</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#006">Joshua</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd275.htm#006">Nun</a>, and the chief fathers of the tribes of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="029">29</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> said unto them, If the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd140.htm#007">Gad</a> and the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd311.htm#009">Reuben</a> will pass with you over <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#004">Jordan</a>, every man armed to battle, before the LORD, and the land shall be subdued before you; then ye shall give them the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd148.htm#009">Gilead</a> for a possession:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="030">30</a> But if they will not pass over with you armed, they shall have possessions among you in the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#005">Canaan</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="031">31</a> And the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd140.htm#007">Gad</a> and the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd311.htm#009">Reuben</a> answered, saying, As the LORD hath said unto thy servants, so will we do.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="032">32</a> We will pass over armed before the LORD into the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#005">Canaan</a>, that the possession of our inheritance on this side <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#004">Jordan</a> may be ours.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="033">33</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> gave unto them, even to the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd140.htm#007">Gad</a>, and to the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd311.htm#009">Reuben</a>, and unto half the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd240.htm#002">Manasseh</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#005">Joseph</a>, the kingdom of <a href="../ebd/ebd343.htm#002">Sihon</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd021.htm#006">Amorites</a>, and the kingdom of <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#002">Og</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd046.htm#003">Bashan</a>, the land, with the cities thereof in the coasts, even the cities of the country round about.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="034">34</a> And the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd140.htm#007">Gad</a> built <a href="../ebd/ebd103.htm#005">Dibon</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd036.htm#000">Ataroth</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd032.htm#001">Aroer</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="035">35</a> And Atroth, <a href="../ebd/ebd341.htm#001">Shophan</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd193.htm#002">Jaazer</a>, and Jogbehah,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="036">36</a> And Bethnimrah, and Bethharan, fenced cities: and folds for <a href="../ebd/ebd333.htm#005">sheep</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="037">37</a> And the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd311.htm#009">Reuben</a> built <a href="../ebd/ebd176.htm#009">Heshbon</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd114.htm#009">Elealeh</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd220.htm#001">Kirjathaim</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="038">38</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd268.htm#004">Nebo</a>, and Baalmeon, (their names being changed,) and <a href="../ebd/ebd337.htm#000">Shibmah</a>: and gave other names unto the cities which they builded.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="039">39</a> And the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd235.htm#006">Machir</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd240.htm#002">Manasseh</a> went to <a href="../ebd/ebd148.htm#009">Gilead</a>, and took it, and dispossessed the Amorite which was in it.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="040">40</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> gave <a href="../ebd/ebd148.htm#009">Gilead</a> unto <a href="../ebd/ebd235.htm#006">Machir</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd240.htm#002">Manasseh</a>; and he dwelt therein.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="041">41</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd196.htm#001">Jair</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd240.htm#002">Manasseh</a> went and took the small towns thereof, and called them Havothjair.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="042">42</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd274.htm#004">Nobah</a> went and took <a href="../ebd/ebd217.htm#002">Kenath</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd377.htm#003">villages</a> thereof, and called it <a href="../ebd/ebd274.htm#004">Nobah</a>, after his own name.<br>
 </p>
