 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Zechariah Chapter 2</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> I lifted up mine eyes again, and looked, and behold a man with a measuring line in his hand.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Then said I, Whither goest thou? And he said unto me, To measure <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>, to see what is the breadth thereof, and what is the length thereof.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And, behold, the <a href="../ebd/ebd024.htm#001">angel</a> that talked with me went forth, and another <a href="../ebd/ebd024.htm#001">angel</a> went out to meet him,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And said unto him, Run, speak to this young man, saying, <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a> shall be inhabited as towns without walls for the multitude of men and <a href="../ebd/ebd074.htm#002">cattle</a> therein:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> For I, saith the LORD, will be unto her a wall of fire round about, and will be the <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a> in the midst of her.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Ho, ho, come forth, and flee from the land of the north, saith the LORD: for I have spread you abroad as the four <a href="../ebd/ebd381.htm#009">winds</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#006">heaven</a>, saith the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Deliver thyself, O <a href="../ebd/ebd394.htm#002">Zion</a>, that dwellest with the daughter of <a href="../ebd/ebd041.htm#001">Babylon</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> For thus saith the LORD of hosts; After the <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a> hath he sent me unto the nations which spoiled you: for he that toucheth you toucheth the <a href="../ebd/ebd027.htm#004">apple</a> of his eye.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> For, behold, I will shake mine hand upon them, and they shall be a spoil to their servants: and ye shall know that the LORD of hosts hath sent me.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> Sing and rejoice, O daughter of <a href="../ebd/ebd394.htm#002">Zion</a>: for, lo, I come, and I will dwell in the midst of thee, saith the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And many nations shall be joined to the LORD in that day, and shall be my people: and I will dwell in the midst of thee, and thou shalt know that the LORD of hosts hath sent me unto thee.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And the LORD shall inherit <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a> his portion in the holy land, and shall choose <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a> again.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> Be silent, O all <a href="../ebd/ebd135.htm#007">flesh</a>, before the LORD: for he is raised up out of his holy <a href="../ebd/ebd156.htm#007">habitation</a>.<br>
 </p>
