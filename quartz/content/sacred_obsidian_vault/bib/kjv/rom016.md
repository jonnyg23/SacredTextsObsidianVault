 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Romans Chapter 16</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> I commend unto you <a href="../ebd/ebd293.htm#000">Phebe</a> our sister, which is a servant of the church which is at <a href="../ebd/ebd075.htm#001">Cenchrea</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> That ye receive her in the Lord, as becometh saints, and that ye assist her in whatsoever business she hath need of you: for she hath been a succourer of many, and of myself also.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Greet <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#005">Priscilla</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd027.htm#006">Aquila</a> my helpers in <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Who have for my life laid down their own necks: unto whom not only I give thanks, but also all the churches of the <a href="../ebd/ebd146.htm#001">Gentiles</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Likewise greet the church that is in their house. Salute my well-beloved <a href="../ebd/ebd122.htm#002">Epaenetus</a>, who is the firstfruits of <a href="../ebd/ebd006.htm#004">Achaia</a> unto <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Greet <a href="../ebd/ebd242.htm#009">Mary</a>, who bestowed much labour on us.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Salute <a href="../ebd/ebd023.htm#008">Andronicus</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd214.htm#004">Junia</a>, my kinsmen, and my fellow-prisoners, who are of note among the apostles, who also were in <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a> before me.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> Greet <a href="../ebd/ebd022.htm#000">Amplias</a> my beloved in the Lord.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> Salute Urbane, our helper in <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd350.htm#004">Stachys</a> my beloved.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> Salute <a href="../ebd/ebd025.htm#009">Apelles</a> approved in <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a>. Salute them which are of Aristobulus' household.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> Salute <a href="../ebd/ebd176.htm#007">Herodion</a> my <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#006">kinsman</a>. Greet them that be of the household of <a href="../ebd/ebd267.htm#001">Narcissus</a>, which are in the Lord.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> Salute Tryphena and Tryphosa, who labour in the Lord. Salute the beloved <a href="../ebd/ebd291.htm#000">Persis</a>, which laboured much in the Lord.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> Salute <a href="../ebd/ebd316.htm#004">Rufus</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd081.htm#008">chosen</a> in the Lord, and his mother and mine.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> Salute Asyncritus, <a href="../ebd/ebd294.htm#004">Phlegon</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd175.htm#003">Hermas</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd287.htm#000">Patrobas</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd175.htm#004">Hermes</a>, and the brethren which are with them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> Salute Philologus, and <a href="../ebd/ebd214.htm#002">Julia</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd270.htm#008">Nereus</a>, and his sister, and <a href="../ebd/ebd278.htm#002">Olympas</a>, and all the saints which are with them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> Salute one another with an holy <a href="../ebd/ebd221.htm#000">kiss</a>. The churches of <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a> salute you.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> Now I beseech you, brethren, mark them which cause divisions and offences contrary to the doctrine which ye have learned; and avoid them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> For they that are such serve not our Lord <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a>, but their own <a href="../ebd/ebd052.htm#000">belly</a>; and by good words and fair speeches deceive the hearts of the simple.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> For your obedience is come abroad unto all men. I am glad therefore on your behalf: but yet I would have you <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#004">wise</a> unto that which is good, and simple concerning evil.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> And the God of peace shall bruise <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#000">Satan</a> under your feet shortly. The <a href="../ebd/ebd154.htm#000">grace</a> of our Lord <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a> be with you. <a href="../ebd/ebd020.htm#001">Amen</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd367.htm#001">Timotheus</a> my workfellow, and <a href="../ebd/ebd232.htm#007">Lucius</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd198.htm#002">Jason</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd348.htm#008">Sosipater</a>, my kinsmen, salute you.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> I <a href="../ebd/ebd362.htm#005">Tertius</a>, who wrote this epistle, salute you in the Lord.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd141.htm#003">Gaius</a> mine <a href="../ebd/ebd183.htm#007">host</a>, and of the whole church, saluteth you. <a href="../ebd/ebd124.htm#001">Erastus</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd076.htm#005">chamberlain</a> of the city saluteth you, and <a href="../ebd/ebd303.htm#005">Quartus</a> a brother.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> The <a href="../ebd/ebd154.htm#000">grace</a> of our Lord <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a> be with you all. <a href="../ebd/ebd020.htm#001">Amen</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> Now to him that is of power to stablish you according to my <a href="../ebd/ebd153.htm#003">gospel</a>, and the preaching of <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a>, according to the <a href="../ebd/ebd312.htm#002">revelation</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd263.htm#006">mystery</a>, which was kept secret since the world began,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> But now is made manifest, and by the scriptures of the prophets, according to the commandment of the <a href="../ebd/ebd127.htm#007">everlasting</a> God, made known to all nations for the obedience of faith:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> To God only <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#004">wise</a>, be <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a> through <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a> for ever. <a href="../ebd/ebd020.htm#001">Amen</a>.<br>
 </p>
