 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 108</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> O god, my heart is fixed; I will sing and give praise, even with my <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Awake, <a href="../ebd/ebd301.htm#006">psaltery</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd165.htm#001">harp</a>: I myself will awake early.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> I will praise thee, O LORD, among the people: and I will sing praises unto thee among the nations.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> For thy <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> is great above the heavens: and thy truth reacheth unto the clouds.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Be thou exalted, O God, above the heavens: and thy <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a> above all the earth;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> That thy beloved may be delivered: save with thy right hand, and answer me.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> God hath spoken in his <a href="../ebd/ebd180.htm#006">holiness</a>; I will rejoice, I will divide <a href="../ebd/ebd333.htm#003">Shechem</a>, and mete out the <a href="../ebd/ebd376.htm#008">valley</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd352.htm#008">Succoth</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd148.htm#009">Gilead</a> is mine; <a href="../ebd/ebd240.htm#002">Manasseh</a> is mine; <a href="../ebd/ebd123.htm#000">Ephraim</a> also is the strength of mine head; <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a> is my lawgiver;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd258.htm#005">Moab</a> is my washpot; over <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#001">Edom</a> will I cast out my <a href="../ebd/ebd340.htm#009">shoe</a>; over <a href="../ebd/ebd294.htm#001">Philistia</a> will I triumph.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> Who will bring me into the strong city? who will lead me into <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#001">Edom</a>?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> Wilt not thou, O God, who hast cast us off? and wilt not thou, O God, go forth with our hosts?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> Give us help from trouble: for vain is the help of man.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> Through God we shall do valiantly: for he it is that shall tread down our enemies.<br>
 </p>
