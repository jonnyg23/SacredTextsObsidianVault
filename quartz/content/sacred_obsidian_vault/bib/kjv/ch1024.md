 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: 1 Chronicles Chapter 24</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Now these are the divisions of the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a>. The sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a>; <a href="../ebd/ebd264.htm#008">Nadab</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd003.htm#004">Abihu</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd115.htm#000">Eleazar</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#005">Ithamar</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> But <a href="../ebd/ebd264.htm#008">Nadab</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd003.htm#004">Abihu</a> died before their father, and had no children: therefore <a href="../ebd/ebd115.htm#000">Eleazar</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#005">Ithamar</a> executed the priest's office.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> distributed them, both <a href="../ebd/ebd386.htm#008">Zadok</a> of the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd115.htm#000">Eleazar</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd014.htm#004">Ahimelech</a> of the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#005">Ithamar</a>, according to their offices in their service.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And there were more chief men found of the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd115.htm#000">Eleazar</a> than of the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#005">Ithamar</a>, and thus were they divided. Among the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd115.htm#000">Eleazar</a> there were sixteen chief men of the house of their fathers, and eight among the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#005">Ithamar</a> according to the house of their fathers.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Thus were they divided by lot, one sort with another; for the governors of the <a href="../ebd/ebd321.htm#005">sanctuary</a>, and governors of the house of God, were of the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd115.htm#000">Eleazar</a>, and of the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#005">Ithamar</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd334.htm#005">Shemaiah</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd271.htm#003">Nethaneel</a> the scribe, one of the Levites, wrote them before the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a>, and the princes, and <a href="../ebd/ebd386.htm#008">Zadok</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd014.htm#004">Ahimelech</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd002.htm#005">Abiathar</a>, and before the chief of the fathers of the priests and Levites: one principal household being taken for <a href="../ebd/ebd115.htm#000">Eleazar</a>, and one taken for <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#005">Ithamar</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Now the first lot came forth to <a href="../ebd/ebd201.htm#002">Jehoiarib</a>, the second to <a href="../ebd/ebd199.htm#006">Jedaiah</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> The third to <a href="../ebd/ebd164.htm#003">Harim</a>, the fourth to <a href="../ebd/ebd327.htm#006">Seorim</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> The fifth to Malchijah, the sixth to Mijamin,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> The seventh to <a href="../ebd/ebd159.htm#004">Hakkoz</a>, the eighth to <a href="../ebd/ebd003.htm#006">Abijah</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> The ninth to Jeshuah, the tenth to <a href="../ebd/ebd333.htm#001">Shecaniah</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> The eleventh to <a href="../ebd/ebd116.htm#003">Eliashib</a>, the twelfth to <a href="../ebd/ebd196.htm#004">Jakim</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> The thirteenth to Huppah, the fourteenth to <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#000">Jeshebeab</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> The fifteenth to <a href="../ebd/ebd058.htm#007">Bilgah</a>, the sixteenth to <a href="../ebd/ebd187.htm#008">Immer</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> The seventeenth to <a href="../ebd/ebd177.htm#005">Hezir</a>, the eighteenth to Aphses,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> The nineteenth to <a href="../ebd/ebd291.htm#005">Pethahiah</a>, the twentieth to Jehezekel,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> The one and twentieth to <a href="../ebd/ebd194.htm#004">Jachin</a>, the two and twentieth to <a href="../ebd/ebd142.htm#009">Gamul</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> The three and twentieth to <a href="../ebd/ebd101.htm#001">Delaiah</a>, the four and twentieth to <a href="../ebd/ebd235.htm#000">Maaziah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> These were the orderings of them in their service to come into the house of the LORD, according to their manner, under <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> their father, as the LORD God of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> had commanded him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> And the rest of the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd227.htm#006">Levi</a> were these: Of the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd022.htm#001">Amram</a>; Shubael: of the sons of Shubael; <a href="../ebd/ebd200.htm#002">Jehdeiah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> Concerning <a href="../ebd/ebd309.htm#005">Rehabiah</a>: of the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd309.htm#005">Rehabiah</a>, the first was Isshiah.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> Of the Izharites; Shelomoth: of the sons of Shelomoth; <a href="../ebd/ebd195.htm#004">Jahath</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> And the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd171.htm#004">Hebron</a>; Jeriah the first, <a href="../ebd/ebd019.htm#002">Amariah</a> the second, <a href="../ebd/ebd195.htm#006">Jahaziel</a> the third, Jekameam the fourth.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> Of the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd376.htm#005">Uzziel</a>; Michah: of the sons of Michah; <a href="../ebd/ebd331.htm#001">Shamir</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> The brother of Michah was Isshiah: of the sons of Isshiah; <a href="../ebd/ebd389.htm#006">Zechariah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> The sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#000">Merari</a> were Mahli and <a href="../ebd/ebd262.htm#005">Mushi</a>: the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd193.htm#003">Jaaziah</a>; Beno.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> The sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#000">Merari</a> by <a href="../ebd/ebd193.htm#003">Jaaziah</a>; Beno, and Shoham, and <a href="../ebd/ebd386.htm#004">Zaccur</a>, and Ibri.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="028">28</a> Of Mahli came <a href="../ebd/ebd115.htm#000">Eleazar</a>, who had no sons.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="029">29</a> Concerning <a href="../ebd/ebd220.htm#007">Kish</a>: the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd220.htm#007">Kish</a> was <a href="../ebd/ebd203.htm#005">Jerahmeel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="030">30</a> The sons also of <a href="../ebd/ebd262.htm#005">Mushi</a>; Mahli, and <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#000">Eder</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd203.htm#009">Jerimoth</a>. These were the sons of the Levites after the house of their fathers.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="031">31</a> These likewise cast lots over against their brethren the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> in the presence of <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd386.htm#008">Zadok</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd014.htm#004">Ahimelech</a>, and the chief of the fathers of the priests and Levites, even the principal fathers over against their younger brethren.<br>
 </p>
