 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Isaiah Chapter 12</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> And in that day thou shalt say, O LORD, I will praise thee: though thou wast angry with me, thine <a href="../ebd/ebd024.htm#002">anger</a> is turned away, and thou comfortedst me.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Behold, God is my <a href="../ebd/ebd320.htm#003">salvation</a>; I will trust, and not be afraid: for the LORD <a href="../ebd/ebd201.htm#009">JEHOVAH</a> is my strength and my song; he also is become my <a href="../ebd/ebd320.htm#003">salvation</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Therefore with joy shall ye draw water out of the wells of <a href="../ebd/ebd320.htm#003">salvation</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And in that day shall ye say, Praise the LORD, call upon his name, declare his doings among the people, make mention that his name is exalted.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Sing unto the LORD; for he hath done excellent things: this is known in all the earth.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Cry out and shout, thou inhabitant of <a href="../ebd/ebd394.htm#002">Zion</a>: for great is the Holy One of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> in the midst of thee.<br>
 </p>
