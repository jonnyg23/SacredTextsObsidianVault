 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Malachi Chapter 4</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> For, behold, the day cometh, that shall burn as an <a href="../ebd/ebd281.htm#005">oven</a>; and all the proud, yea, and all that do wickedly, shall be stubble: and the day that cometh shall burn them up, saith the LORD of hosts, that it shall leave them neither root nor <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#002">branch</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> But unto you that fear my name shall the <a href="../ebd/ebd353.htm#001">Sun</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd313.htm#004">righteousness</a> arise with healing in his wings; and ye shall go forth, and grow up as calves of the stall.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And ye shall tread down the wicked; for they shall be <a href="../ebd/ebd034.htm#000">ashes</a> under the soles of your feet in the day that I shall do this, saith the LORD of hosts.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Remember ye the law of <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> my servant, which I commanded unto him in <a href="../ebd/ebd181.htm#009">Horeb</a> for all <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, with the statutes and judgments.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Behold, I will send you <a href="../ebd/ebd116.htm#009">Elijah</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#008">prophet</a> before the coming of the great and dreadful day of the LORD:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And he shall turn the heart of the fathers to the children, and the heart of the children to their fathers, lest I come and smite the earth with a <a href="../ebd/ebd094.htm#003">curse</a>.<br>
 </p>
