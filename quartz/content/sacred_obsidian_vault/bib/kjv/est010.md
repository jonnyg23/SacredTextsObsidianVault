 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Esther Chapter 10</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd013.htm#003">Ahasuerus</a> laid a <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#006">tribute</a> upon the land, and upon the isles of the sea.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> And all the acts of his power and of his might, and the declaration of the greatness of <a href="../ebd/ebd259.htm#005">Mordecai</a>, whereunto the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> advanced him, are they not written in the book of the <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#003">chronicles</a> of the kings of <a href="../ebd/ebd246.htm#001">Media</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd290.htm#009">Persia</a>?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> For <a href="../ebd/ebd259.htm#005">Mordecai</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd206.htm#004">Jew</a> was next unto <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd013.htm#003">Ahasuerus</a>, and great among the Jews, and accepted of the multitude of his brethren, seeking the wealth of his people, and speaking peace to all his seed.<br>
 </p>
