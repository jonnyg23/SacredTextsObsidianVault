 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 80</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Give <a href="../ebd/ebd110.htm#004">ear</a>, O <a href="../ebd/ebd335.htm#006">Shepherd</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, thou that leadest <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#005">Joseph</a> like a flock; thou that dwellest between the cherubims, shine forth.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Before <a href="../ebd/ebd123.htm#000">Ephraim</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd052.htm#008">Benjamin</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd240.htm#002">Manasseh</a> stir up thy strength, and come and save us.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Turn us again, O God, and cause thy face to shine; and we shall be saved.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> O LORD God of hosts, how long wilt thou be angry against the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#000">prayer</a> of thy people?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Thou feedest them with the <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#006">bread</a> of tears; and givest them tears to <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a> in great measure.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Thou makest us a strife unto our neighbours: and our enemies laugh among themselves.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Turn us again, O God of hosts, and cause thy face to shine; and we shall be saved.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> Thou hast brought a <a href="../ebd/ebd377.htm#004">vine</a> out of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a>: thou hast cast out the <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#005">heathen</a>, and planted it.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> Thou preparedst room before it, and didst cause it to take <a href="../ebd/ebd100.htm#008">deep</a> root, and it filled the land.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> The hills were covered with the <a href="../ebd/ebd330.htm#002">shadow</a> of it, and the boughs thereof were like the goodly cedars.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> She sent out her boughs unto the sea, and her branches unto the river.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> Why hast thou then broken down her hedges, so that all they which pass by the way do pluck her?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> The <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#005">boar</a> out of the <a href="../ebd/ebd383.htm#003">wood</a> doth waste it, and the wild <a href="../ebd/ebd049.htm#001">beast</a> of the field doth devour it.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> Return, we beseech thee, O God of hosts: look down from <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#006">heaven</a>, and behold, and visit this <a href="../ebd/ebd377.htm#004">vine</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And the vineyard which thy right hand hath planted, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#002">branch</a> that thou madest strong for thyself.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> It is burned with fire, it is cut down: they perish at the rebuke of thy countenance.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> Let thy hand be upon the man of thy right hand, upon the son of man whom thou madest strong for thyself.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> So will not we go back from thee: quicken us, and we will call upon thy name.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> Turn us again, O LORD God of hosts, cause thy face to shine; and we shall be saved.<br>
 </p>
