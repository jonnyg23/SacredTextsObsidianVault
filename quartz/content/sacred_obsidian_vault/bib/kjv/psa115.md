 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 115</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Not unto us, O LORD, not unto us, but unto thy name give <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a>, for thy <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a>, and for thy truth's sake.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Wherefore should the <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#005">heathen</a> say, Where is now their God?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> But our God is in the heavens: he hath done whatsoever he hath pleased.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Their idols are <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#000">silver</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a>, the work of men's hands.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> They have mouths, but they speak not: eyes have they, but they see not:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> They have ears, but they hear not: noses have they, but they smell not:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> They have hands, but they handle not: feet have they, but they walk not: neither speak they through their throat.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> They that make them are like unto them; so is every one that trusteth in them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> O <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, trust thou in the LORD: he is their help and their <a href="../ebd/ebd337.htm#001">shield</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> O house of <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a>, trust in the LORD: he is their help and their <a href="../ebd/ebd337.htm#001">shield</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> Ye that fear the LORD, trust in the LORD: he is their help and their <a href="../ebd/ebd337.htm#001">shield</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> The LORD hath been mindful of us: he will <a href="../ebd/ebd060.htm#008">bless</a> us; he will <a href="../ebd/ebd060.htm#008">bless</a> the house of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>; he will <a href="../ebd/ebd060.htm#008">bless</a> the house of <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> He will <a href="../ebd/ebd060.htm#008">bless</a> them that fear the LORD, both small and great.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> The LORD shall increase you more and more, you and your children.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> Ye are blessed of the LORD which made <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#006">heaven</a> and earth.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> The <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#006">heaven</a>, even the heavens, are the LORD's: but the earth hath he given to the children of men.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> The dead praise not the LORD, neither any that go down into silence.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> But we will <a href="../ebd/ebd060.htm#008">bless</a> the LORD from this time forth and for evermore. Praise the LORD.<br>
 </p>
