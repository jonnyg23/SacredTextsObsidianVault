 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Genesis Chapter 15</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> After these things the word of the LORD came unto <a href="../ebd/ebd005.htm#004">Abram</a> in a <a href="../ebd/ebd378.htm#000">vision</a>, saying, Fear not, <a href="../ebd/ebd005.htm#004">Abram</a>: I am thy <a href="../ebd/ebd337.htm#001">shield</a>, and thy exceeding great reward.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd005.htm#004">Abram</a> said, LORD God, what wilt thou give me, seeing I go childless, and the steward of my house is this <a href="../ebd/ebd116.htm#007">Eliezer</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd096.htm#007">Damascus</a>?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd005.htm#004">Abram</a> said, Behold, to me thou hast given no seed: and, lo, one born in my house is mine <a href="../ebd/ebd171.htm#007">heir</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And, behold, the word of the LORD came unto him, saying, This shall not be thine <a href="../ebd/ebd171.htm#007">heir</a>; but he that shall come forth out of thine own <a href="../ebd/ebd063.htm#004">bowels</a> shall be thine <a href="../ebd/ebd171.htm#007">heir</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And he brought him forth abroad, and said, Look now toward <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#006">heaven</a>, and tell the <a href="../ebd/ebd350.htm#008">stars</a>, if thou be able to number them: and he said unto him, So shall thy seed be.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And he believed in the LORD; and he counted it to him for <a href="../ebd/ebd313.htm#004">righteousness</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And he said unto him, I am the LORD that brought thee out of <a href="../ebd/ebd375.htm#001">Ur</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd076.htm#002">Chaldees</a>, to give thee this land to inherit it.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And he said, LORD God, whereby shall I know that I shall inherit it?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And he said unto him, Take me an <a href="../ebd/ebd171.htm#006">heifer</a> of three years old, and a she <a href="../ebd/ebd151.htm#001">goat</a> of three years old, and a <a href="../ebd/ebd306.htm#003">ram</a> of three years old, and a turtledove, and a young <a href="../ebd/ebd295.htm#004">pigeon</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And he took unto him all these, and divided them in the midst, and laid each piece one against another: but the birds divided he not.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And when the fowls came down upon the carcases, <a href="../ebd/ebd005.htm#004">Abram</a> drove them away.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And when the <a href="../ebd/ebd353.htm#001">sun</a> was going down, a <a href="../ebd/ebd100.htm#008">deep</a> sleep fell upon <a href="../ebd/ebd005.htm#004">Abram</a>; and, lo, an horror of great <a href="../ebd/ebd097.htm#008">darkness</a> fell upon him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> And he said unto <a href="../ebd/ebd005.htm#004">Abram</a>, Know of a <a href="../ebd/ebd353.htm#005">surety</a> that thy seed shall be a <a href="../ebd/ebd351.htm#009">stranger</a> in a land that is not their's, and shall serve them; and they shall afflict them four hundred years;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> And also that nation, whom they shall serve, will I <a href="../ebd/ebd213.htm#005">judge</a>: and afterward shall they come out with great substance.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And thou shalt go to thy fathers in peace; thou shalt be buried in a good old age.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> But in the fourth <a href="../ebd/ebd145.htm#008">generation</a> they shall come hither again: for the iniquity of the <a href="../ebd/ebd021.htm#006">Amorites</a> is not yet full.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> And it came to pass, that, when the <a href="../ebd/ebd353.htm#001">sun</a> went down, and it was dark, behold a smoking <a href="../ebd/ebd140.htm#000">furnace</a>, and a burning <a href="../ebd/ebd223.htm#007">lamp</a> that passed between those <a href="../ebd/ebd295.htm#002">pieces</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> In the same day the LORD made a <a href="../ebd/ebd091.htm#008">covenant</a> with <a href="../ebd/ebd005.htm#004">Abram</a>, saying, Unto thy seed have I given this land, from the river of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a> unto the great river, the river <a href="../ebd/ebd127.htm#001">Euphrates</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> The <a href="../ebd/ebd217.htm#004">Kenites</a>, and the Kenizzites, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd215.htm#005">Kadmonites</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd179.htm#006">Hittites</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd290.htm#006">Perizzites</a>, and the Rephaims,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd021.htm#006">Amorites</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#007">Canaanites</a>, and the Girgashites, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd199.htm#004">Jebusites</a>.<br>
 </p>
