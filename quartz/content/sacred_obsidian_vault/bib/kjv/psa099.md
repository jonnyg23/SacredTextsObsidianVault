 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 99</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> The LORD reigneth; let the people tremble: he sitteth between the cherubims; let the earth be moved.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> The LORD is great in <a href="../ebd/ebd394.htm#002">Zion</a>; and he is high above all the people.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Let them praise thy great and terrible name; for it is holy.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> The king's strength also loveth judgment; thou dost establish equity, thou executest judgment and <a href="../ebd/ebd313.htm#004">righteousness</a> in <a href="../ebd/ebd194.htm#007">Jacob</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Exalt ye the LORD our God, and <a href="../ebd/ebd384.htm#002">worship</a> at his <a href="../ebd/ebd136.htm#009">footstool</a>; for he is holy.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> among his priests, and <a href="../ebd/ebd321.htm#001">Samuel</a> among them that call upon his name; they called upon the LORD, and he answered them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> He spake unto them in the cloudy <a href="../ebd/ebd295.htm#007">pillar</a>: they kept his testimonies, and the ordinance that he gave them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> Thou answeredst them, O LORD our God: thou wast a God that forgavest them, though thou tookest vengeance of their inventions.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> Exalt the LORD our God, and <a href="../ebd/ebd384.htm#002">worship</a> at his holy hill; for the LORD our God is holy.<br>
 </p>
