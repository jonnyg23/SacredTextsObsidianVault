 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Numbers Chapter 8</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> And the LORD spake unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Speak unto <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> and say unto him, When thou lightest the lamps, the <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> lamps shall give light over against the <a href="../ebd/ebd071.htm#001">candlestick</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> did so; he lighted the lamps thereof over against the <a href="../ebd/ebd071.htm#001">candlestick</a>, as the LORD commanded <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And this work of the <a href="../ebd/ebd071.htm#001">candlestick</a> was of beaten <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a>, unto the shaft thereof, unto the <a href="../ebd/ebd136.htm#002">flowers</a> thereof, was beaten work: according unto the pattern which the LORD had shewed <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, so he made the <a href="../ebd/ebd071.htm#001">candlestick</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And the LORD spake unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Take the Levites from among the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, and cleanse them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And thus shalt thou do unto them, to cleanse them: Sprinkle water of purifying upon them, and let them shave all their <a href="../ebd/ebd135.htm#007">flesh</a>, and let them wash their clothes, and so make themselves <a href="../ebd/ebd084.htm#005">clean</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> Then let them take a young <a href="../ebd/ebd066.htm#003">bullock</a> with his meat <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, even fine <a href="../ebd/ebd136.htm#001">flour</a> mingled with oil, and another young <a href="../ebd/ebd066.htm#003">bullock</a> shalt thou take for a <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And thou shalt bring the Levites before the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>: and thou shalt gather the whole assembly of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> together:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And thou shalt bring the Levites before the LORD: and the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> shall put their hands upon the Levites:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> shall offer the Levites before the LORD for an <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, that they may execute the service of the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And the Levites shall lay their hands upon the heads of the bullocks: and thou shalt offer the one for a <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, and the other for a burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, unto the LORD, to make an <a href="../ebd/ebd036.htm#004">atonement</a> for the Levites.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> And thou shalt set the Levites before <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a>, and before his sons, and offer them for an <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> unto the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> Thus shalt thou separate the Levites from among the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>: and the Levites shall be mine.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And after that shall the Levites go in to do the service of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>: and thou shalt cleanse them, and offer them for an <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> For they are wholly given unto me from among the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>; instead of such as open every womb, even instead of the firstborn of all the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, have I taken them unto me.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> For all the firstborn of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> are mine, both man and <a href="../ebd/ebd049.htm#001">beast</a>: on the day that I smote every firstborn in the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a> I sanctified them for myself.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> And I have taken the Levites for all the firstborn of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> And I have given the Levites as a <a href="../ebd/ebd148.htm#005">gift</a> to <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> and to his sons from among the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, to do the service of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> in the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>, and to make an <a href="../ebd/ebd036.htm#004">atonement</a> for the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>: that there be no <a href="../ebd/ebd297.htm#001">plague</a> among the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, when the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> come nigh unto the <a href="../ebd/ebd321.htm#005">sanctuary</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a>, and all the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, did to the Levites according unto all that the LORD commanded <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> concerning the Levites, so did the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> unto them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> And the Levites were purified, and they washed their clothes; and <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> offered them as an <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> before the LORD; and <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> made an <a href="../ebd/ebd036.htm#004">atonement</a> for them to cleanse them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> And after that went the Levites in to do their service in the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> before <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a>, and before his sons: as the LORD had commanded <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> concerning the Levites, so did they unto them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> And the LORD spake unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> This is it that belongeth unto the Levites: from twenty and five years old and upward they shall go in to wait upon the service of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> And from the age of fifty years they shall cease waiting upon the service thereof, and shall serve no more:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> But shall <a href="../ebd/ebd255.htm#007">minister</a> with their brethren in the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>, to keep the charge, and shall do no service. Thus shalt thou do unto the Levites touching their charge.<br>
 </p>
