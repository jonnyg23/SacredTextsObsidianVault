 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Isaiah Chapter 7</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> And it came to pass in the days of <a href="../ebd/ebd013.htm#005">Ahaz</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#000">Jotham</a>, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd376.htm#004">Uzziah</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>, that <a href="../ebd/ebd312.htm#006">Rezin</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd355.htm#002">Syria</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd288.htm#002">Pekah</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd310.htm#003">Remaliah</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, went up toward <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a> to war against it, but could not prevail against it.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> And it was told the house of <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a>, saying, <a href="../ebd/ebd355.htm#002">Syria</a> is confederate with <a href="../ebd/ebd123.htm#000">Ephraim</a>. And his heart was moved, and the heart of his people, as the trees of the <a href="../ebd/ebd383.htm#003">wood</a> are moved with the wind.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Then said the LORD unto <a href="../ebd/ebd189.htm#007">Isaiah</a>, Go forth now to meet <a href="../ebd/ebd013.htm#005">Ahaz</a>, thou, and Shearjashub thy son, at the end of the <a href="../ebd/ebd087.htm#009">conduit</a> of the upper pool in the <a href="../ebd/ebd178.htm#005">highway</a> of the fuller's field;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And say unto him, Take heed, and be quiet; fear not, neither be fainthearted for the two tails of these smoking firebrands, for the fierce <a href="../ebd/ebd024.htm#002">anger</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd312.htm#006">Rezin</a> with <a href="../ebd/ebd355.htm#002">Syria</a>, and of the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd310.htm#003">Remaliah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Because <a href="../ebd/ebd355.htm#002">Syria</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd123.htm#000">Ephraim</a>, and the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd310.htm#003">Remaliah</a>, have taken evil counsel against thee, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Let us go up against <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>, and vex it, and let us make a <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#005">breach</a> therein for us, and set a <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> in the midst of it, even the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd355.htm#009">Tabeal</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Thus saith the Lord GOD, It shall not stand, neither shall it come to pass.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> For the head of <a href="../ebd/ebd355.htm#002">Syria</a> is <a href="../ebd/ebd096.htm#007">Damascus</a>, and the head of <a href="../ebd/ebd096.htm#007">Damascus</a> is <a href="../ebd/ebd312.htm#006">Rezin</a>; and within threescore and five years shall <a href="../ebd/ebd123.htm#000">Ephraim</a> be broken, that it be not a people.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And the head of <a href="../ebd/ebd123.htm#000">Ephraim</a> is <a href="../ebd/ebd320.htm#004">Samaria</a>, and the head of <a href="../ebd/ebd320.htm#004">Samaria</a> is Remaliah's son. If ye will not believe, surely ye shall not be established.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> Moreover the LORD spake again unto <a href="../ebd/ebd013.htm#005">Ahaz</a>, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> Ask thee a sign of the LORD thy God; ask it either in the depth, or in the height above.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> But <a href="../ebd/ebd013.htm#005">Ahaz</a> said, I will not ask, neither will I tempt the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> And he said, Hear ye now, O house of <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a>; Is it a small thing for you to weary men, but will ye weary my God also?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> Therefore the Lord himself shall give you a sign; Behold, a <a href="../ebd/ebd377.htm#009">virgin</a> shall conceive, and bear a son, and shall call his name <a href="../ebd/ebd187.htm#007">Immanuel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd067.htm#002">Butter</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd181.htm#001">honey</a> shall he eat, that he may know to refuse the evil, and choose the good.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> For before the child shall know to refuse the evil, and choose the good, the land that thou abhorrest shall be forsaken of both her kings.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> The LORD shall bring upon thee, and upon thy people, and upon thy father's house, days that have not come, from the day that <a href="../ebd/ebd123.htm#000">Ephraim</a> departed from <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>; even the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd035.htm#005">Assyria</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> And it shall come to pass in that day, that the LORD shall <a href="../ebd/ebd179.htm#005">hiss</a> for the <a href="../ebd/ebd136.htm#004">fly</a> that is in the uttermost part of the rivers of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a>, and for the <a href="../ebd/ebd050.htm#000">bee</a> that is in the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd035.htm#005">Assyria</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> And they shall come, and shall rest all of them in the desolate valleys, and in the holes of the rocks, and upon all thorns, and upon all bushes.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> In the same day shall the Lord shave with a <a href="../ebd/ebd307.htm#009">razor</a> that is hired, namely, by them beyond the river, by the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd035.htm#005">Assyria</a>, the head, and the hair of the feet: and it shall also consume the <a href="../ebd/ebd049.htm#000">beard</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> And it shall come to pass in that day, that a man shall nourish a young <a href="../ebd/ebd092.htm#001">cow</a>, and two <a href="../ebd/ebd333.htm#005">sheep</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> And it shall come to pass, for the abundance of <a href="../ebd/ebd255.htm#000">milk</a> that they shall give he shall eat <a href="../ebd/ebd067.htm#002">butter</a>: for <a href="../ebd/ebd067.htm#002">butter</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd181.htm#001">honey</a> shall every one eat that is left in the land.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> And it shall come to pass in that day, that every place shall be, where there were a thousand vines at a thousand silverlings, it shall even be for briers and thorns.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> With <a href="../ebd/ebd032.htm#005">arrows</a> and with bows shall men come thither; because all the land shall become briers and thorns.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> And on all hills that shall be digged with the <a href="../ebd/ebd244.htm#007">mattock</a>, there shall not come thither the fear of briers and thorns: but it shall be for the sending forth of oxen, and for the treading of lesser <a href="../ebd/ebd074.htm#002">cattle</a>.<br>
 </p>
