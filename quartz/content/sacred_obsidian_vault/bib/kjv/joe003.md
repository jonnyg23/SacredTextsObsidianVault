 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Joel Chapter 3</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> For, behold, in those days, and in that time, when I shall bring again the <a href="../ebd/ebd072.htm#002">captivity</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> I will also gather all nations, and will bring them down into the <a href="../ebd/ebd376.htm#008">valley</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd201.htm#006">Jehoshaphat</a>, and will plead with them there for my people and for my heritage <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, whom they have scattered among the nations, and parted my land.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And they have cast lots for my people; and have given a boy for an <a href="../ebd/ebd164.htm#005">harlot</a>, and sold a girl for <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#000">wine</a>, that they might <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Yea, and what have ye to do with me, O <a href="../ebd/ebd374.htm#001">Tyre</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd393.htm#000">Zidon</a>, and all the coasts of <a href="../ebd/ebd282.htm#009">Palestine</a>? will ye render me a recompence? and if ye recompense me, swiftly and speedily will I return your recompence upon your own head;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Because ye have taken my <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#000">silver</a> and my <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a>, and have carried into your temples my goodly pleasant things:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> The children also of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a> and the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a> have ye sold unto the <a href="../ebd/ebd155.htm#002">Grecians</a>, that ye might remove them far from their border.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Behold, I will raise them out of the place whither ye have sold them, and will return your recompence upon your own head:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And I will sell your sons and your daughters into the hand of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>, and they shall sell them to the <a href="../ebd/ebd317.htm#006">Sabeans</a>, to a people far off: for the LORD hath spoken it.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> Proclaim ye this among the <a href="../ebd/ebd146.htm#001">Gentiles</a>; Prepare war, wake up the mighty men, let all the men of war draw near; let them come up:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> Beat your plowshares into swords and your pruninghooks into spears: let the weak say, I am strong.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> Assemble yourselves, and come, all ye <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#005">heathen</a>, and gather yourselves together round about: thither cause thy mighty ones to come down, O LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> Let the <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#005">heathen</a> be wakened, and come up to the <a href="../ebd/ebd376.htm#008">valley</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd201.htm#006">Jehoshaphat</a>: for there will I sit to <a href="../ebd/ebd213.htm#005">judge</a> all the <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#005">heathen</a> round about.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> Put ye in the <a href="../ebd/ebd342.htm#008">sickle</a>, for the <a href="../ebd/ebd165.htm#008">harvest</a> is ripe: come, get you down; for the press is full, the fats overflow; for their wickedness is great.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> Multitudes, multitudes in the <a href="../ebd/ebd376.htm#008">valley</a> of decision: for the day of the LORD is near in the <a href="../ebd/ebd376.htm#008">valley</a> of decision.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> The <a href="../ebd/ebd353.htm#001">sun</a> and the <a href="../ebd/ebd259.htm#004">moon</a> shall be darkened, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd350.htm#008">stars</a> shall withdraw their shining.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> The LORD also shall roar out of <a href="../ebd/ebd394.htm#002">Zion</a>, and utter his voice from <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>; and the heavens and the earth shall shake: but the LORD will be the hope of his people, and the strength of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> So shall ye know that I am the LORD your God dwelling in <a href="../ebd/ebd394.htm#002">Zion</a>, my holy mountain: then shall <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a> be holy, and there shall no strangers pass through her any more.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> And it shall come to pass in that day, that the mountains shall drop down new <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#000">wine</a>, and the hills shall flow with <a href="../ebd/ebd255.htm#000">milk</a>, and all the rivers of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a> shall flow with waters, and a <a href="../ebd/ebd138.htm#000">fountain</a> shall come forth out of the house of the LORD, and shall water the <a href="../ebd/ebd376.htm#008">valley</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd339.htm#009">Shittim</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a> shall be a desolation, and <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#001">Edom</a> shall be a desolate <a href="../ebd/ebd381.htm#005">wilderness</a>, for the violence against the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>, because they have shed innocent <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> in their land.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> But <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a> shall dwell for ever, and <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a> from <a href="../ebd/ebd145.htm#008">generation</a> to <a href="../ebd/ebd145.htm#008">generation</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> For I will cleanse their <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> that I have not cleansed: for the LORD dwelleth in <a href="../ebd/ebd394.htm#002">Zion</a>.<br>
 </p>
