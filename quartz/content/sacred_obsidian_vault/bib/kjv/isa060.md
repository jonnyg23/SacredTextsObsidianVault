 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Isaiah Chapter 60</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Arise, shine; for thy light is come, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a> of the LORD is risen upon thee.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> For, behold, the <a href="../ebd/ebd097.htm#008">darkness</a> shall cover the earth, and gross <a href="../ebd/ebd097.htm#008">darkness</a> the people: but the LORD shall arise upon thee, and his <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a> shall be seen upon thee.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd146.htm#001">Gentiles</a> shall come to thy light, and kings to the brightness of thy rising.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Lift up thine eyes round about, and see: all they gather themselves together, they come to thee: thy sons shall come from far, and thy daughters shall be nursed at thy side.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Then thou shalt see, and flow together, and thine heart shall fear, and be enlarged; because the abundance of the sea shall be converted unto thee, the <a href="../ebd/ebd137.htm#000">forces</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd146.htm#001">Gentiles</a> shall come unto thee.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> The multitude of camels shall cover thee, the dromedaries of <a href="../ebd/ebd253.htm#007">Midian</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd122.htm#003">Ephah</a>; all they from <a href="../ebd/ebd332.htm#006">Sheba</a> shall come: they shall bring <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd188.htm#002">incense</a>; and they shall shew forth the praises of the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> All the flocks of <a href="../ebd/ebd216.htm#003">Kedar</a> shall be gathered together unto thee, the rams of <a href="../ebd/ebd268.htm#001">Nebaioth</a> shall <a href="../ebd/ebd255.htm#007">minister</a> unto thee: they shall come up with acceptance on mine <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>, and I will <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#005">glorify</a> the house of my <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> Who are these that <a href="../ebd/ebd136.htm#004">fly</a> as a <a href="../ebd/ebd085.htm#001">cloud</a>, and as the doves to their windows?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> Surely the isles shall wait for me, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd339.htm#003">ships</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd359.htm#002">Tarshish</a> first, to bring thy sons from far, their <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#000">silver</a> and their <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a> with them, unto the name of the LORD thy God, and to the Holy One of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, because he hath glorified thee.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And the sons of strangers shall build up thy walls, and their kings shall <a href="../ebd/ebd255.htm#007">minister</a> unto thee: for in my wrath I smote thee, but in my favour have I had <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> on thee.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> Therefore thy gates shall be open continually; they shall not be shut day nor night; that men may bring unto thee the <a href="../ebd/ebd137.htm#000">forces</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd146.htm#001">Gentiles</a>, and that their kings may be brought.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> For the nation and kingdom that will not serve thee shall perish; yea, those nations shall be utterly wasted.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> The <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd226.htm#001">Lebanon</a> shall come unto thee, the <a href="../ebd/ebd133.htm#005">fir</a> tree, the pine tree, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd063.htm#007">box</a> together, to beautify the place of my <a href="../ebd/ebd321.htm#005">sanctuary</a>; and I will make the place of my feet glorious.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> The sons also of them that afflicted thee shall come bending unto thee; and all they that despised thee shall <a href="../ebd/ebd063.htm#003">bow</a> themselves down at the soles of thy feet; and they shall call thee; The city of the LORD, The <a href="../ebd/ebd394.htm#002">Zion</a> of the Holy One of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> Whereas thou has been forsaken and hated, so that no man went through thee, I will make thee an eternal excellency, a joy of many generations.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> Thou shalt also suck the <a href="../ebd/ebd255.htm#000">milk</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd146.htm#001">Gentiles</a>, and shalt suck the breast of kings: and thou shalt know that I the LORD am thy <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#003">Saviour</a> and thy <a href="../ebd/ebd308.htm#008">Redeemer</a>, the mighty One of <a href="../ebd/ebd194.htm#007">Jacob</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> For <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#003">brass</a> I will bring <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a>, and for <a href="../ebd/ebd189.htm#004">iron</a> I will bring <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#000">silver</a>, and for <a href="../ebd/ebd383.htm#003">wood</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#003">brass</a>, and for stones <a href="../ebd/ebd189.htm#004">iron</a>: I will also make thy officers peace, and thine exactors <a href="../ebd/ebd313.htm#004">righteousness</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> Violence shall no more be heard in thy land, wasting nor <a href="../ebd/ebd102.htm#004">destruction</a> within thy borders; but thou shalt call thy walls <a href="../ebd/ebd320.htm#003">Salvation</a>, and thy gates Praise.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> The <a href="../ebd/ebd353.htm#001">sun</a> shall be no more thy light by day; neither for brightness shall the <a href="../ebd/ebd259.htm#004">moon</a> give light unto thee: but the LORD shall be unto thee an <a href="../ebd/ebd127.htm#007">everlasting</a> light, and thy God thy <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> Thy <a href="../ebd/ebd353.htm#001">sun</a> shall no more go down; neither shall thy <a href="../ebd/ebd259.htm#004">moon</a> withdraw itself: for the LORD shall be thine <a href="../ebd/ebd127.htm#007">everlasting</a> light, and the days of thy mourning shall be ended.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> Thy people also shall be all righteous: they shall inherit the land for ever, the <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#002">branch</a> of my planting, the work of my hands, that I may be glorified.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> A little one shall become a thousand, and a small one a strong nation: I the LORD will hasten it in his time.<br>
 </p>
