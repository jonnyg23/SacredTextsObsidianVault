 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 9</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> I will praise thee, O LORD, with my whole heart; I will shew forth all thy marvellous works.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> I will be glad and rejoice in thee: I will sing praise to thy name, O thou most High.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> When mine enemies are turned back, they shall fall and perish at thy presence.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> For thou hast maintained my right and my cause; thou satest in the <a href="../ebd/ebd365.htm#001">throne</a> judging right.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Thou hast rebuked the <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#005">heathen</a>, thou hast destroyed the wicked, thou hast put out their name for ever and ever.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> O thou enemy, destructions are come to a perpetual end: and thou hast destroyed cities; their memorial is perished with them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> But the LORD shall endure for ever: he hath prepared his <a href="../ebd/ebd365.htm#001">throne</a> for judgment.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And he shall <a href="../ebd/ebd213.htm#005">judge</a> the world in <a href="../ebd/ebd313.htm#004">righteousness</a>, he shall <a href="../ebd/ebd255.htm#007">minister</a> judgment to the people in uprightness.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> The LORD also will be a refuge for the oppressed, a refuge in times of trouble.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And they that know thy name will put their trust in thee: for thou, LORD, hast not forsaken them that seek thee.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> Sing praises to the LORD, which dwelleth in <a href="../ebd/ebd394.htm#002">Zion</a>: declare among the people his doings.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> When he maketh inquisition for <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a>, he remembereth them: he forgetteth not the cry of the humble.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> Have <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> upon me, O LORD; consider my trouble which I suffer of them that hate me, thou that liftest me up from the gates of death:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> That I may shew forth all thy praise in the gates of the daughter of <a href="../ebd/ebd394.htm#002">Zion</a>: I will rejoice in thy <a href="../ebd/ebd320.htm#003">salvation</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> The <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#005">heathen</a> are sunk down in the <a href="../ebd/ebd296.htm#007">pit</a> that they made: in the <a href="../ebd/ebd271.htm#002">net</a> which they hid is their own foot taken.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> The LORD is known by the judgment which he executeth: the wicked is snared in the work of his own hands. <a href="../ebd/ebd178.htm#002">Higgaion</a>. <a href="../ebd/ebd326.htm#008">Selah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> The wicked shall be turned into <a href="../ebd/ebd173.htm#003">hell</a>, and all the nations that forget God.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> For the needy shall not alway be forgotten: the expectation of the <a href="../ebd/ebd298.htm#006">poor</a> shall not perish for ever.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> Arise, O LORD; let not man prevail: let the <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#005">heathen</a> be judged in thy sight.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> Put them in fear, O LORD: that the nations may know themselves to be but men. <a href="../ebd/ebd326.htm#008">Selah</a>.<br>
 </p>
