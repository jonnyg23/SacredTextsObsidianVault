 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Exodus Chapter 31</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> And the LORD spake unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> See, I have called by name <a href="../ebd/ebd057.htm#009">Bezaleel</a> the son of Uri, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd184.htm#009">Hur</a>, of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And I have filled him with the <a href="../ebd/ebd349.htm#009">spirit</a> of God, in wisdom, and in understanding, and in knowledge, and in all manner of workmanship,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> To devise cunning works, to work in <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a>, and in <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#000">silver</a>, and in <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#003">brass</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And in <a href="../ebd/ebd095.htm#000">cutting</a> of stones, to set them, and in carving of timber, to work in all manner of workmanship.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And I, behold, I have given with him <a href="../ebd/ebd015.htm#006">Aholiab</a>, the son of Ahisamach, of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd096.htm#009">Dan</a>: and in the hearts of all that are <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#004">wise</a> hearted I have put wisdom, that they may make all that I have commanded thee;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> The <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#000">ark</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd362.htm#008">testimony</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> seat that is thereupon, and all the furniture of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And the table and his furniture, and the pure <a href="../ebd/ebd071.htm#001">candlestick</a> with all his furniture, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd188.htm#002">incense</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> of burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> with all his furniture, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd224.htm#009">laver</a> and his foot,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And the cloths of service, and the holy garments for <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a>, and the garments of his sons, to <a href="../ebd/ebd255.htm#007">minister</a> in the priest's office,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And the anointing oil, and sweet <a href="../ebd/ebd188.htm#002">incense</a> for the holy place: according to all that I have commanded thee shall they do.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And the LORD spake unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> Speak thou also unto the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, saying, Verily my sabbaths ye shall keep: for it is a sign between me and you throughout your generations; that ye may know that I am the LORD that doth sanctify you.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> Ye shall keep the <a href="../ebd/ebd317.htm#003">sabbath</a> therefore; for it is holy unto you: every one that defileth it shall surely be put to death: for whosoever doeth any work therein, that soul shall be cut off from among his people.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> Six days may work be done; but in the seventh is the <a href="../ebd/ebd317.htm#003">sabbath</a> of rest, holy to the LORD: whosoever doeth any work in the <a href="../ebd/ebd317.htm#003">sabbath</a> day, he shall surely be put to death.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> Wherefore the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> shall keep the <a href="../ebd/ebd317.htm#003">sabbath</a>, to observe the <a href="../ebd/ebd317.htm#003">sabbath</a> throughout their generations, for a perpetual <a href="../ebd/ebd091.htm#008">covenant</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> It is a sign between me and the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> for ever: for in six days the LORD made <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#006">heaven</a> and earth, and on the seventh day he rested, and was refreshed.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> And he gave unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, when he had made an end of communing with him upon <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#007">mount</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#009">Sinai</a>, two <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#006">tables</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd362.htm#008">testimony</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#006">tables</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd351.htm#005">stone</a>, written with the finger of God.<br>
 </p>
