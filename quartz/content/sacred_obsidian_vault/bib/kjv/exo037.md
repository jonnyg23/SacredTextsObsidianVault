 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Exodus Chapter 37</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd057.htm#009">Bezaleel</a> made the <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#000">ark</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd339.htm#009">shittim</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd383.htm#003">wood</a>: two cubits and a half was the length of it, and a <a href="../ebd/ebd093.htm#006">cubit</a> and a half the breadth of it, and a <a href="../ebd/ebd093.htm#006">cubit</a> and a half the height of it:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> And he overlaid it with pure <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a> within and without, and made a <a href="../ebd/ebd093.htm#001">crown</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a> to it round about.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And he cast for it four rings of <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a>, to be set by the four corners of it; even two rings upon the one side of it, and two rings upon the other side of it.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And he made staves of <a href="../ebd/ebd339.htm#009">shittim</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd383.htm#003">wood</a>, and overlaid them with <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And he put the staves into the rings by the sides of the <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#000">ark</a>, to bear the <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#000">ark</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And he made the <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> seat of pure <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a>: two cubits and a half was the length thereof, and one <a href="../ebd/ebd093.htm#006">cubit</a> and a half the breadth thereof.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And he made two cherubims of <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a>, beaten out of one piece made he them, on the two ends of the <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> seat;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> One <a href="../ebd/ebd079.htm#003">cherub</a> on the end on this side, and another <a href="../ebd/ebd079.htm#003">cherub</a> on the other end on that side: out of the <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> seat made he the cherubims on the two ends thereof.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And the cherubims spread out their wings on high, and covered with their wings over the <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> seat, with their faces one to another; even to the <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> seatward were the faces of the cherubims.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And he made the table of <a href="../ebd/ebd339.htm#009">shittim</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd383.htm#003">wood</a>: two cubits was the length thereof, and a <a href="../ebd/ebd093.htm#006">cubit</a> the breadth thereof, and a <a href="../ebd/ebd093.htm#006">cubit</a> and a half the height thereof:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And he overlaid it with pure <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a>, and made thereunto a <a href="../ebd/ebd093.htm#001">crown</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a> round about.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> Also he made thereunto a border of an <a href="../ebd/ebd162.htm#006">handbreadth</a> round about; and made a <a href="../ebd/ebd093.htm#001">crown</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a> for the border thereof round about.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> And he cast for it four rings of <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a>, and put the rings upon the four corners that were in the four feet thereof.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> Over against the border were the rings, the places for the staves to bear the table.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And he made the staves of <a href="../ebd/ebd339.htm#009">shittim</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd383.htm#003">wood</a>, and overlaid them with <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a>, to bear the table.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> And he made the vessels which were upon the table, his dishes, and his spoons, and his bowls, and his covers to cover withal, of pure <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> And he made the <a href="../ebd/ebd071.htm#001">candlestick</a> of pure <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a>: of beaten work made he the <a href="../ebd/ebd071.htm#001">candlestick</a>; his shaft, and his <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#002">branch</a>, his bowls, his knops, and his <a href="../ebd/ebd136.htm#002">flowers</a>, were of the same:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> And six branches going out of the sides thereof; three branches of the <a href="../ebd/ebd071.htm#001">candlestick</a> out of the one side thereof, and three branches of the <a href="../ebd/ebd071.htm#001">candlestick</a> out of the other side thereof:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> Three bowls made after the fashion of almonds in one <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#002">branch</a>, a <a href="../ebd/ebd221.htm#009">knop</a> and a flower; and three bowls made like almonds in another <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#002">branch</a>, a <a href="../ebd/ebd221.htm#009">knop</a> and a flower: so throughout the six branches going out of the <a href="../ebd/ebd071.htm#001">candlestick</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> And in the <a href="../ebd/ebd071.htm#001">candlestick</a> were four bowls made like almonds, his knops, and his <a href="../ebd/ebd136.htm#002">flowers</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> And a <a href="../ebd/ebd221.htm#009">knop</a> under two branches of the same, and a <a href="../ebd/ebd221.htm#009">knop</a> under two branches of the same, and a <a href="../ebd/ebd221.htm#009">knop</a> under two branches of the same, according to the six branches going out of it.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> Their knops and their branches were of the same: all of it was one beaten work of pure <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> And he made his <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> lamps, and his snuffers, and his snuffdishes, of pure <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> Of a <a href="../ebd/ebd357.htm#009">talent</a> of pure <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a> made he it, and all the vessels thereof.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> And he made the <a href="../ebd/ebd188.htm#002">incense</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd339.htm#009">shittim</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd383.htm#003">wood</a>: the length of it was a <a href="../ebd/ebd093.htm#006">cubit</a>, and the breadth of it a <a href="../ebd/ebd093.htm#006">cubit</a>; it was foursquare; and two cubits was the height of it; the horns thereof were of the same.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> And he overlaid it with pure <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a>, both the top of it, and the sides thereof round about, and the horns of it: also he made unto it a <a href="../ebd/ebd093.htm#001">crown</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a> round about.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> And he made two rings of <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a> for it under the <a href="../ebd/ebd093.htm#001">crown</a> thereof, by the two corners of it, upon the two sides thereof, to be places for the staves to bear it withal.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="028">28</a> And he made the staves of <a href="../ebd/ebd339.htm#009">shittim</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd383.htm#003">wood</a>, and overlaid them with <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="029">29</a> And he made the holy anointing oil, and the pure <a href="../ebd/ebd188.htm#002">incense</a> of sweet <a href="../ebd/ebd349.htm#005">spices</a>, according to the work of the <a href="../ebd/ebd026.htm#009">apothecary</a>.<br>
 </p>
