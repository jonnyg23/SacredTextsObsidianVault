 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Deuteronomy Chapter 14</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Ye are the children of the LORD your God: ye shall not cut yourselves, nor make any <a href="../ebd/ebd042.htm#008">baldness</a> between your eyes for the dead.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> For thou art an holy people unto the LORD thy God, and the LORD hath <a href="../ebd/ebd081.htm#008">chosen</a> thee to be a <a href="../ebd/ebd287.htm#008">peculiar</a> people unto himself, above all the nations that are upon the earth.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Thou shalt not eat any abominable thing.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> These are the beasts which ye shall eat: the <a href="../ebd/ebd281.htm#007">ox</a>, the <a href="../ebd/ebd333.htm#005">sheep</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd151.htm#001">goat</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> The <a href="../ebd/ebd165.htm#004">hart</a>, and the roebuck, and the fallow deer, and the wild <a href="../ebd/ebd151.htm#001">goat</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd303.htm#001">pygarg</a>, and the wild <a href="../ebd/ebd281.htm#007">ox</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd076.htm#007">chamois</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And every <a href="../ebd/ebd049.htm#001">beast</a> that parteth the <a href="../ebd/ebd181.htm#003">hoof</a>, and cleaveth the cleft into two claws, and cheweth the cud among the beasts, that ye shall eat.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Nevertheless these ye shall not eat of them that chew the cud, or of them that divide the cloven <a href="../ebd/ebd181.htm#003">hoof</a>; as the <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#000">camel</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd163.htm#009">hare</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#000">coney</a>: for they chew the cud, but divide not the <a href="../ebd/ebd181.htm#003">hoof</a>; therefore they are unclean unto you.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd354.htm#002">swine</a>, because it divideth the <a href="../ebd/ebd181.htm#003">hoof</a>, yet cheweth not the cud, it is unclean unto you: ye shall not eat of their <a href="../ebd/ebd135.htm#007">flesh</a>, nor touch their dead <a href="../ebd/ebd072.htm#004">carcase</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> These ye shall eat of all that are in the waters: all that have fins and scales shall ye eat:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And whatsoever hath not fins and scales ye may not eat; it is unclean unto you.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> Of all <a href="../ebd/ebd084.htm#005">clean</a> birds ye shall eat.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> But these are they of which ye shall not eat: the <a href="../ebd/ebd110.htm#003">eagle</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd281.htm#000">ossifrage</a>, and the ospray,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#004">glede</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd221.htm#001">kite</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd378.htm#002">vulture</a> after his kind,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> And every <a href="../ebd/ebd307.htm#008">raven</a> after his kind,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd281.htm#006">owl</a>, and the night <a href="../ebd/ebd168.htm#001">hawk</a>, and the cuckow, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd168.htm#001">hawk</a> after his kind,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> The little <a href="../ebd/ebd281.htm#006">owl</a>, and the great <a href="../ebd/ebd281.htm#006">owl</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd354.htm#000">swan</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> And the pelican, and the gier <a href="../ebd/ebd110.htm#003">eagle</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd090.htm#005">cormorant</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd351.htm#007">stork</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd176.htm#008">heron</a> after her kind, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd224.htm#003">lapwing</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd047.htm#002">bat</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> And every creeping thing that flieth is unclean unto you: they shall not be eaten.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> But of all <a href="../ebd/ebd084.htm#005">clean</a> fowls ye may eat.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> Ye shall not eat of anything that dieth of itself: thou shalt give it unto the <a href="../ebd/ebd351.htm#009">stranger</a> that is in thy gates, that he may eat it; or thou mayest sell it unto an <a href="../ebd/ebd017.htm#003">alien</a>: for thou art an holy people unto the LORD thy God. Thou shalt not <a href="../ebd/ebd326.htm#001">seethe</a> a kid in his mother's <a href="../ebd/ebd255.htm#000">milk</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> Thou shalt truly <a href="../ebd/ebd368.htm#005">tithe</a> all the increase of thy seed, that the field bringeth forth year by year.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> And thou shalt eat before the LORD thy God, in the place which he shall choose to place his name there, the <a href="../ebd/ebd368.htm#005">tithe</a> of thy <a href="../ebd/ebd090.htm#006">corn</a>, of thy <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#000">wine</a>, and of thine oil, and the firstlings of thy herds and of thy flocks; that thou mayest learn to fear the LORD thy God always.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> And if the way be too long for thee, so that thou art not able to carry it; or if the place be too far from thee, which the LORD thy God shall choose to set his name there, when the LORD thy God hath blessed thee:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> Then shalt thou turn it into money, and bind up the money in thine hand, and shalt go unto the place which the LORD thy God shall choose:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> And thou shalt bestow that money for whatsoever thy soul lusteth after, for oxen, or for <a href="../ebd/ebd333.htm#005">sheep</a>, or for <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#000">wine</a>, or for strong <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a>, or for whatsoever thy soul desireth: and thou shalt eat there before the LORD thy God, and thou shalt rejoice, thou, and thine household,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd227.htm#009">Levite</a> that is within thy gates; thou shalt not forsake him; for he hath no part nor inheritance with thee.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="028">28</a> At the end of three years thou shalt bring forth all the <a href="../ebd/ebd368.htm#005">tithe</a> of thine increase the same year, and shalt lay it up within thy gates:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="029">29</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd227.htm#009">Levite</a>, (because he hath no part nor inheritance with thee,) and the <a href="../ebd/ebd351.htm#009">stranger</a>, and the fatherless, and the widow, which are within thy gates, shall come, and shall eat and be satisfied; that the LORD thy God may <a href="../ebd/ebd060.htm#008">bless</a> thee in all the work of thine hand which thou doest.<br>
 </p>
