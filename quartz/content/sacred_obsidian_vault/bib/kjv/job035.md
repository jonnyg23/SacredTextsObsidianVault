 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Job Chapter 35</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd116.htm#008">Elihu</a> spake moreover, and said,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Thinkest thou this to be right, that thou saidst, My <a href="../ebd/ebd313.htm#004">righteousness</a> is more than God's?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> For thou saidst, What advantage will it be unto thee? and, What profit shall I have, if I be cleansed from my <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a>?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> I will answer thee, and thy companions with thee.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Look unto the heavens, and see; and behold the clouds which are higher than thou.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> If thou sinnest, what doest thou against him? or if thy transgressions be multiplied, what doest thou unto him?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> If thou be righteous, what givest thou him? or what receiveth he of thine hand?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> Thy wickedness may hurt a man as thou art; and thy <a href="../ebd/ebd313.htm#004">righteousness</a> may profit the son of man.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> By reason of the multitude of oppressions they make the oppressed to cry: they cry out by reason of the arm of the mighty.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> But none saith, Where is God my maker, who giveth <a href="../ebd/ebd348.htm#002">songs</a> in the night;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> Who teacheth us more than the beasts of the earth, and maketh us wiser than the fowls of <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#006">heaven</a>?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> There they cry, but none giveth answer, because of the pride of evil men.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> Surely God will not hear vanity, neither will the Almighty regard it.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> Although thou sayest thou shalt not see him, yet judgment is before him; therefore trust thou in him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> But now, because it is not so, he hath visited in his <a href="../ebd/ebd024.htm#002">anger</a>; yet he knoweth it not in great extremity:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> Therefore doth Job open his mouth in vain; he multiplieth words without knowledge.<br>
 </p>
