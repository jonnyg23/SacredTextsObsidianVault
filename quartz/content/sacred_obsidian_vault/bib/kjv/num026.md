 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Numbers Chapter 26</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> And it came to pass after the <a href="../ebd/ebd297.htm#001">plague</a>, that the LORD spake unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> and unto <a href="../ebd/ebd115.htm#000">Eleazar</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a>, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Take the sum of all the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, from twenty years old and upward, throughout their fathers' house, all that are able to go to war in <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd115.htm#000">Eleazar</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> spake with them in the plains of <a href="../ebd/ebd258.htm#005">Moab</a> by <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#004">Jordan</a> near <a href="../ebd/ebd203.htm#008">Jericho</a>, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Take the sum of the people, from twenty years old and upward; as the LORD commanded <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> and the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, which went forth out of the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd311.htm#009">Reuben</a>, the eldest son of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>: the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd311.htm#009">Reuben</a>; Hanoch, of whom cometh the family of the Hanochites: of <a href="../ebd/ebd283.htm#000">Pallu</a>, the family of the Palluites:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Of <a href="../ebd/ebd177.htm#007">Hezron</a>, the family of the Hezronites: of <a href="../ebd/ebd072.htm#007">Carmi</a>, the family of the Carmites.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> These are the families of the Reubenites: and they that were numbered of them were forty and three thousand and <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> hundred and thirty.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd283.htm#000">Pallu</a>; <a href="../ebd/ebd115.htm#008">Eliab</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd115.htm#008">Eliab</a>; <a href="../ebd/ebd270.htm#004">Nemuel</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#002">Dathan</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd004.htm#003">Abiram</a>. This is that <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#002">Dathan</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd004.htm#003">Abiram</a>, which were famous in the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>, who strove against <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> and against <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> in the company of <a href="../ebd/ebd222.htm#003">Korah</a>, when they strove against the LORD:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And the earth opened her mouth, and swallowed them up together with <a href="../ebd/ebd222.htm#003">Korah</a>, when that company died, what time the fire devoured two hundred and fifty men: and they became a sign.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> Notwithstanding the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd222.htm#003">Korah</a> died not.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> The sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#002">Simeon</a> after their families: of <a href="../ebd/ebd270.htm#004">Nemuel</a>, the family of the Nemuelites: of Jamin, the family of the Jaminites: of <a href="../ebd/ebd194.htm#004">Jachin</a>, the family of the Jachinites:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> Of <a href="../ebd/ebd391.htm#001">Zerah</a>, the family of the Zarhites: of Shaul, the family of the Shaulites.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> These are the families of the Simeonites, twenty and two thousand and two hundred.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> The children of <a href="../ebd/ebd140.htm#007">Gad</a> after their families: of Zephon, the family of the Zephonites: of Haggi, the family of the Haggites: of Shuni, the family of the Shunites:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> Of <a href="../ebd/ebd282.htm#001">Ozni</a>, the family of the Oznites: of Eri, the family of the Erites:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> Of Arod, the family of the Arodites: of Areli, the family of the Arelites.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> These are the families of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd140.htm#007">Gad</a> according to those that were numbered of them, forty thousand and five hundred.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> The sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a> were Er and <a href="../ebd/ebd278.htm#008">Onan</a>: and Er and <a href="../ebd/ebd278.htm#008">Onan</a> died in the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#005">Canaan</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> And the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a> after their families were; of <a href="../ebd/ebd334.htm#000">Shelah</a>, the family of the Shelanites: of <a href="../ebd/ebd292.htm#007">Pharez</a>, the family of the Pharzites: of <a href="../ebd/ebd391.htm#001">Zerah</a>, the family of the Zarhites.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> And the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd292.htm#007">Pharez</a> were; of <a href="../ebd/ebd177.htm#007">Hezron</a>, the family of the Hezronites: of <a href="../ebd/ebd161.htm#008">Hamul</a>, the family of the Hamulites.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> These are the families of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a> according to those that were numbered of them, threescore and sixteen thousand and five hundred.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> Of the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#002">Issachar</a> after their families: of <a href="../ebd/ebd369.htm#007">Tola</a>, the family of the <a href="../ebd/ebd369.htm#009">Tolaites</a>: of Pua, the family of the Punites:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> Of <a href="../ebd/ebd198.htm#001">Jashub</a>, the family of the Jashubites: of <a href="../ebd/ebd338.htm#008">Shimron</a>, the family of the Shimronites.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> These are the families of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#002">Issachar</a> according to those that were numbered of them, threescore and four thousand and three hundred.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> Of the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd389.htm#003">Zebulun</a> after their families: of <a href="../ebd/ebd328.htm#005">Sered</a>, the family of the Sardites: of <a href="../ebd/ebd119.htm#000">Elon</a>, the family of the Elonites: of Jahleel, the family of the Jahleelites.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> These are the families of the Zebulunites according to those that were numbered of them, threescore thousand and five hundred.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="028">28</a> The sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#005">Joseph</a> after their families were <a href="../ebd/ebd240.htm#002">Manasseh</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd123.htm#000">Ephraim</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="029">29</a> Of the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd240.htm#002">Manasseh</a>: of <a href="../ebd/ebd235.htm#006">Machir</a>, the family of the Machirites: and <a href="../ebd/ebd235.htm#006">Machir</a> begat <a href="../ebd/ebd148.htm#009">Gilead</a>: of <a href="../ebd/ebd148.htm#009">Gilead</a> come the family of the Gileadites.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="030">30</a> These are the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd148.htm#009">Gilead</a>: of Jeezer, the family of the Jeezerites: of <a href="../ebd/ebd172.htm#005">Helek</a>, the family of the Helekites:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="031">31</a> And of Asriel, the family of the Asrielites: and of <a href="../ebd/ebd333.htm#003">Shechem</a>, the family of the Shechemites:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="032">32</a> And of Shemida, the family of the Shemidaites: and of <a href="../ebd/ebd174.htm#006">Hepher</a>, the family of the Hepherites.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="033">33</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd390.htm#002">Zelophehad</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd174.htm#006">Hepher</a> had no sons, but daughters: and the names of the daughters of <a href="../ebd/ebd390.htm#002">Zelophehad</a> were <a href="../ebd/ebd238.htm#000">Mahlah</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd274.htm#002">Noah</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd180.htm#003">Hoglah</a>, Milcah, and Tirzah.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="034">34</a> These are the families of <a href="../ebd/ebd240.htm#002">Manasseh</a>, and those that were numbered of them, fifty and two thousand and <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> hundred.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="035">35</a> These are the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd123.htm#000">Ephraim</a> after their families: of Shuthelah, the family of the Shuthalhites: of <a href="../ebd/ebd049.htm#005">Becher</a>, the family of the Bachrites: of Tahan, the family of the Tahanites.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="036">36</a> And these are the sons of Shuthelah: of Eran, the family of the Eranites.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="037">37</a> These are the families of the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd123.htm#000">Ephraim</a> according to those that were numbered of them, thirty and two thousand and five hundred. These are the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#005">Joseph</a> after their families.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="038">38</a> The sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd052.htm#008">Benjamin</a> after their families: of <a href="../ebd/ebd051.htm#006">Bela</a>, the family of the Belaites: of Ashbel, the family of the Ashbelites: of Ahiram, the family of the Ahiramites:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="039">39</a> Of Shupham, the family of the Shuphamites: of Hupham, the family of the Huphamites.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="040">40</a> And the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd051.htm#006">Bela</a> were <a href="../ebd/ebd029.htm#008">Ard</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd263.htm#009">Naaman</a>: of <a href="../ebd/ebd029.htm#008">Ard</a>, the family of the Ardites: and of <a href="../ebd/ebd263.htm#009">Naaman</a>, the family of the Naamites.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="041">41</a> These are the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd052.htm#008">Benjamin</a> after their families: and they that were numbered of them were forty and five thousand and six hundred.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="042">42</a> These are the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd096.htm#009">Dan</a> after their families: of Shuham, the family of the Shuhamites. These are the families of <a href="../ebd/ebd096.htm#009">Dan</a> after their families.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="043">43</a> All the families of the Shuhamites, according to those that were numbered of them, were threescore and four thousand and four hundred.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="044">44</a> Of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd033.htm#008">Asher</a> after their families: of Jimna, the family of the Jimnites: of Jesui, the family of the Jesuites: of <a href="../ebd/ebd053.htm#005">Beriah</a>, the family of the Beriites.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="045">45</a> Of the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd053.htm#005">Beriah</a>: of <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#008">Heber</a>, the family of the Heberites: of Malchiel, the family of the Malchielites.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="046">46</a> And the name of the daughter of <a href="../ebd/ebd033.htm#008">Asher</a> was <a href="../ebd/ebd322.htm#003">Sarah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="047">47</a> These are the families of the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd033.htm#008">Asher</a> according to those that were numbered of them; who were fifty and three thousand and four hundred.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="048">48</a> Of the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd266.htm#006">Naphtali</a> after their families: of <a href="../ebd/ebd195.htm#008">Jahzeel</a>, the family of the Jahzeelites: of Guni, the family of the Gunites:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="049">49</a> Of Jezer, the family of the Jezerites: of Shillem, the family of the Shillemites.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="050">50</a> These are the families of <a href="../ebd/ebd266.htm#006">Naphtali</a> according to their families: and they that were numbered of them were forty and five thousand and four hundred.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="051">51</a> These were the numbered of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, six hundred thousand and a thousand <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> hundred and thirty.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="052">52</a> And the LORD spake unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="053">53</a> Unto these the land shall be divided for an inheritance according to the number of names.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="054">54</a> To many thou shalt give the more inheritance, and to few thou shalt give the less inheritance: to every one shall his inheritance be given according to those that were numbered of him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="055">55</a> Notwithstanding the land shall be divided by lot: according to the names of the tribes of their fathers they shall inherit.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="056">56</a> According to the lot shall the possession thereof be divided between many and few.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="057">57</a> And these are they that were numbered of the Levites after their families: of <a href="../ebd/ebd146.htm#009">Gershon</a>, the family of the Gershonites: of <a href="../ebd/ebd222.htm#001">Kohath</a>, the family of the <a href="../ebd/ebd222.htm#002">Kohathites</a>: of <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#000">Merari</a>, the family of the <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#001">Merarites</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="058">58</a> These are the families of the Levites: the family of the Libnites, the family of the Hebronites, the family of the Mahlites, the family of the Mushites, the family of the Korathites. And <a href="../ebd/ebd222.htm#001">Kohath</a> begat <a href="../ebd/ebd022.htm#001">Amram</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="059">59</a> And the name of Amram's wife was <a href="../ebd/ebd208.htm#004">Jochebed</a>, the daughter of <a href="../ebd/ebd227.htm#006">Levi</a>, whom her mother bare to <a href="../ebd/ebd227.htm#006">Levi</a> in <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a>: and she bare unto <a href="../ebd/ebd022.htm#001">Amram</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd256.htm#003">Miriam</a> their sister.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="060">60</a> And unto <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> was born <a href="../ebd/ebd264.htm#008">Nadab</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd003.htm#004">Abihu</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd115.htm#000">Eleazar</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#005">Ithamar</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="061">61</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd264.htm#008">Nadab</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd003.htm#004">Abihu</a> died, when they offered strange fire before the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="062">62</a> And those that were numbered of them were twenty and three thousand, all males from a month old and upward: for they were not numbered among the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, because there was no inheritance given them among the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="063">63</a> These are they that were numbered by <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd115.htm#000">Eleazar</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a>, who numbered the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> in the plains of <a href="../ebd/ebd258.htm#005">Moab</a> by <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#004">Jordan</a> near <a href="../ebd/ebd203.htm#008">Jericho</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="064">64</a> But among these there was not a man of them whom <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> numbered, when they numbered the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> in the <a href="../ebd/ebd381.htm#005">wilderness</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#009">Sinai</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="065">65</a> For the LORD had said of them, They shall surely die in the <a href="../ebd/ebd381.htm#005">wilderness</a>. And there was not left a man of them, save <a href="../ebd/ebd069.htm#003">Caleb</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd203.htm#004">Jephunneh</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#006">Joshua</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd275.htm#006">Nun</a>.<br>
 </p>
