 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: 1 Chronicles Chapter 1</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd007.htm#007">Adam</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd336.htm#004">Sheth</a>, Enosh,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Kenan, <a href="../ebd/ebd237.htm#001">Mahalaleel</a>, Jered,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd174.htm#005">Henoch</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd251.htm#009">Methuselah</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd223.htm#004">Lamech</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd274.htm#002">Noah</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd334.htm#002">Shem</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd160.htm#003">Ham</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd197.htm#002">Japheth</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> The sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd197.htm#002">Japheth</a>; <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#006">Gomer</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd236.htm#009">Magog</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd235.htm#008">Madai</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd198.htm#005">Javan</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd373.htm#005">Tubal</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd250.htm#008">Meshech</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd367.htm#008">Tiras</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#006">Gomer</a>; Ashchenaz, and <a href="../ebd/ebd313.htm#008">Riphath</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd369.htm#004">Togarmah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd198.htm#005">Javan</a>; <a href="../ebd/ebd118.htm#000">Elishah</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd359.htm#002">Tarshish</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd221.htm#004">Kittim</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd105.htm#004">Dodanim</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> The sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd160.htm#003">Ham</a>; <a href="../ebd/ebd094.htm#005">Cush</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd258.htm#002">Mizraim</a>, Put, and <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#005">Canaan</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd094.htm#005">Cush</a>; <a href="../ebd/ebd325.htm#004">Seba</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd167.htm#009">Havilah</a>, and Sabta, and <a href="../ebd/ebd304.htm#002">Raamah</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd317.htm#008">Sabtecha</a>. And the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd304.htm#002">Raamah</a>; <a href="../ebd/ebd332.htm#006">Sheba</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd100.htm#005">Dedan</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd094.htm#005">Cush</a> begat <a href="../ebd/ebd273.htm#004">Nimrod</a>: he began to be mighty upon the earth.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd258.htm#002">Mizraim</a> begat <a href="../ebd/ebd233.htm#000">Ludim</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd022.htm#007">Anamim</a>, and Lehabim, and <a href="../ebd/ebd266.htm#009">Naphtuhim</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And Pathrusim, and <a href="../ebd/ebd073.htm#005">Casluhim</a>, (of whom came the <a href="../ebd/ebd294.htm#002">Philistines</a>,) and Caphthorim.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#005">Canaan</a> begat <a href="../ebd/ebd393.htm#000">Zidon</a> his firstborn, and <a href="../ebd/ebd177.htm#001">Heth</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> The Jebusite also, and the Amorite, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd149.htm#006">Girgashite</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And the Hivite, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#001">Arkite</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd345.htm#002">Sinite</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> And the Arvadite, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd390.htm#005">Zemarite</a>, and the Hamathite.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> The sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd334.htm#002">Shem</a>; <a href="../ebd/ebd114.htm#003">Elam</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd035.htm#002">Asshur</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd032.htm#004">Arphaxad</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd232.htm#009">Lud</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd028.htm#001">Aram</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd375.htm#007">Uz</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd184.htm#004">Hul</a>, and Gether, and <a href="../ebd/ebd250.htm#008">Meshech</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd032.htm#004">Arphaxad</a> begat <a href="../ebd/ebd334.htm#000">Shelah</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd334.htm#000">Shelah</a> begat <a href="../ebd/ebd112.htm#001">Eber</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> And unto <a href="../ebd/ebd112.htm#001">Eber</a> were born two sons: the name of the one was <a href="../ebd/ebd288.htm#007">Peleg</a>; because in his days the earth was divided: and his brother's name was <a href="../ebd/ebd210.htm#004">Joktan</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd210.htm#004">Joktan</a> begat <a href="../ebd/ebd017.htm#009">Almodad</a>, and Sheleph, and Hazarmaveth, and Jerah,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd158.htm#001">Hadoram</a> also, and <a href="../ebd/ebd375.htm#009">Uzal</a>, and Diklah,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd111.htm#007">Ebal</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd003.htm#009">Abimael</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd332.htm#006">Sheba</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd279.htm#007">Ophir</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd167.htm#009">Havilah</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd208.htm#003">Jobab</a>. All these were the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd210.htm#004">Joktan</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd334.htm#002">Shem</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd032.htm#004">Arphaxad</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd334.htm#000">Shelah</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd112.htm#001">Eber</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd288.htm#007">Peleg</a>, Reu,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#001">Serug</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd265.htm#006">Nahor</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd362.htm#001">Terah</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd005.htm#004">Abram</a>; the same is <a href="../ebd/ebd005.htm#005">Abraham</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="028">28</a> The sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd005.htm#005">Abraham</a>; <a href="../ebd/ebd189.htm#006">Isaac</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd190.htm#005">Ishmael</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="029">29</a> These are their generations: The firstborn of <a href="../ebd/ebd190.htm#005">Ishmael</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd268.htm#001">Nebaioth</a>; then <a href="../ebd/ebd216.htm#003">Kedar</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd008.htm#003">Adbeel</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd252.htm#003">Mibsam</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="030">30</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd257.htm#000">Mishma</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd109.htm#001">Dumah</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd243.htm#005">Massa</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd157.htm#000">Hadad</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd361.htm#000">Tema</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="031">31</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd206.htm#001">Jetur</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd266.htm#005">Naphish</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd216.htm#004">Kedemah</a>. These are the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd190.htm#005">Ishmael</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="032">32</a> Now the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd218.htm#001">Keturah</a>, Abraham's <a href="../ebd/ebd087.htm#007">concubine</a>: she bare <a href="../ebd/ebd393.htm#008">Zimran</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd210.htm#003">Jokshan</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd245.htm#008">Medan</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd253.htm#007">Midian</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd190.htm#001">Ishbak</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd341.htm#006">Shuah</a>. And the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd210.htm#003">Jokshan</a>; <a href="../ebd/ebd332.htm#006">Sheba</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd100.htm#005">Dedan</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="033">33</a> And the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd253.htm#007">Midian</a>; <a href="../ebd/ebd122.htm#003">Ephah</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd122.htm#004">Epher</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd174.htm#005">Henoch</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd002.htm#007">Abida</a>, and Eldaah. All these are the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd218.htm#001">Keturah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="034">34</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd005.htm#005">Abraham</a> begat <a href="../ebd/ebd189.htm#006">Isaac</a>. The sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd189.htm#006">Isaac</a>; <a href="../ebd/ebd124.htm#005">Esau</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="035">35</a> The sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd124.htm#005">Esau</a>; <a href="../ebd/ebd117.htm#005">Eliphaz</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd312.htm#001">Reuel</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd206.htm#003">Jeush</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd192.htm#006">Jaalam</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd222.htm#003">Korah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="036">36</a> The sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd117.htm#005">Eliphaz</a>; <a href="../ebd/ebd361.htm#001">Teman</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd278.htm#003">Omar</a>, Zephi, and Gatam, <a href="../ebd/ebd217.htm#003">Kenaz</a>, and Timna, and <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#009">Amalek</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="037">37</a> The sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd312.htm#001">Reuel</a>; <a href="../ebd/ebd265.htm#004">Nahath</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd391.htm#001">Zerah</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd331.htm#002">Shammah</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd258.htm#003">Mizzah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="038">38</a> And the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd326.htm#004">Seir</a>; <a href="../ebd/ebd232.htm#003">Lotan</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd340.htm#004">Shobal</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd392.htm#004">Zibeon</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd022.htm#004">Anah</a>, and Dishon, and Ezar, and <a href="../ebd/ebd104.htm#005">Dishan</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="039">39</a> And the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd232.htm#003">Lotan</a>; Hori, and Homam: and Timna was Lotan's sister.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="040">40</a> The sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd340.htm#004">Shobal</a>; Alian, and Manahath, and <a href="../ebd/ebd111.htm#007">Ebal</a>, Shephi, and Onam. And the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd392.htm#004">Zibeon</a>; Aiah, and <a href="../ebd/ebd022.htm#004">Anah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="041">41</a> The sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd022.htm#004">Anah</a>; Dishon. And the sons of Dishon; <a href="../ebd/ebd022.htm#001">Amram</a>, and Eshban, and Ithran, and Cheran.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="042">42</a> The sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd129.htm#004">Ezer</a>; Bilhan, and Zavan, and Jakan. The sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd104.htm#005">Dishan</a>; <a href="../ebd/ebd375.htm#007">Uz</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd028.htm#004">Aran</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="043">43</a> Now these are the kings that reigned in the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#001">Edom</a> before any <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> reigned over the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>; <a href="../ebd/ebd051.htm#006">Bela</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd052.htm#009">Beor</a>: and the name of his city was <a href="../ebd/ebd104.htm#000">Dinhabah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="044">44</a> And when <a href="../ebd/ebd051.htm#006">Bela</a> was dead, <a href="../ebd/ebd208.htm#003">Jobab</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd391.htm#001">Zerah</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd063.htm#009">Bozrah</a> reigned in his stead.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="045">45</a> And when <a href="../ebd/ebd208.htm#003">Jobab</a> was dead, Husham of the land of the Temanites reigned in his stead.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="046">46</a> And when Husham was dead, <a href="../ebd/ebd157.htm#000">Hadad</a> the son of Bedad, which smote <a href="../ebd/ebd253.htm#007">Midian</a> in the field of <a href="../ebd/ebd258.htm#005">Moab</a>, reigned in his stead: and the name of his city was Avith.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="047">47</a> And when <a href="../ebd/ebd157.htm#000">Hadad</a> was dead, Samlah of <a href="../ebd/ebd243.htm#004">Masrekah</a> reigned in his stead.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="048">48</a> And when Samlah was dead, Shaul of <a href="../ebd/ebd309.htm#008">Rehoboth</a> by the river reigned in his stead.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="049">49</a> And when Shaul was dead, Baalhanan the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd006.htm#007">Achbor</a> reigned in his stead.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="050">50</a> And when Baalhanan was dead, <a href="../ebd/ebd157.htm#000">Hadad</a> reigned in his stead: and the name of his city was Pai; and his wife's name was <a href="../ebd/ebd246.htm#006">Mehetabel</a>, the daughter of Matred, the daughter of <a href="../ebd/ebd252.htm#000">Mezahab</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="051">51</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd157.htm#000">Hadad</a> died also. And the dukes of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#001">Edom</a> were; <a href="../ebd/ebd108.htm#009">duke</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd366.htm#005">Timnah</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd108.htm#009">duke</a> Aliah, <a href="../ebd/ebd108.htm#009">duke</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#008">Jetheth</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="052">52</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd108.htm#009">Duke</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd015.htm#008">Aholibamah</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd108.htm#009">duke</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd114.htm#002">Elah</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd108.htm#009">duke</a> Pinon,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="053">53</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd108.htm#009">Duke</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd217.htm#003">Kenaz</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd108.htm#009">duke</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd361.htm#001">Teman</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd108.htm#009">duke</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd252.htm#004">Mibzar</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="054">54</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd108.htm#009">Duke</a> Magdiel, <a href="../ebd/ebd108.htm#009">duke</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd189.htm#002">Iram</a>. These are the dukes of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#001">Edom</a>.<br>
 </p>
