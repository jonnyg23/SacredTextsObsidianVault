 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 129</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Many a time have they afflicted me from my youth, may <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> now say:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Many a time have they afflicted me from my youth: yet they have not prevailed against me.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> The plowers plowed upon my back: they made long their furrows.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> The LORD is righteous: he hath cut asunder the cords of the wicked.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Let them all be confounded and turned back that hate <a href="../ebd/ebd394.htm#002">Zion</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Let them be as the <a href="../ebd/ebd154.htm#005">grass</a> upon the housetops, which withereth afore it groweth up:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Wherewith the mower filleth not his hand; nor he that bindeth sheaves his <a href="../ebd/ebd062.htm#008">bosom</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> Neither do they which go by say, The blessing of the LORD be upon you: we <a href="../ebd/ebd060.htm#008">bless</a> you in the name of the LORD.<br>
 </p>
