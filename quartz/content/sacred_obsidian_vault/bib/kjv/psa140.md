 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 140</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Deliver me, O LORD, from the evil man: preserve me from the violent man;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Which imagine mischiefs in their heart; continually are they gathered together for war.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> They have sharpened their tongues like a <a href="../ebd/ebd328.htm#009">serpent</a>; adders' <a href="../ebd/ebd297.htm#009">poison</a> is under their lips. <a href="../ebd/ebd326.htm#008">Selah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Keep me, O LORD, from the hands of the wicked; preserve me from the violent man; who have purposed to overthrow my goings.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> The proud have hid a <a href="../ebd/ebd346.htm#009">snare</a> for me, and cords; they have spread a <a href="../ebd/ebd271.htm#002">net</a> by the wayside; they have set gins for me. <a href="../ebd/ebd326.htm#008">Selah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> I said unto the LORD, Thou art my God: hear the voice of my supplications, O LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> O GOD the Lord, the strength of my <a href="../ebd/ebd320.htm#003">salvation</a>, thou hast covered my head in the day of battle.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> Grant not, O LORD, the desires of the wicked: further not his wicked device; lest they exalt themselves. <a href="../ebd/ebd326.htm#008">Selah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> As for the head of those that compass me about, let the mischief of their own lips cover them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> Let burning coals fall upon them: let them be cast into the fire; into <a href="../ebd/ebd100.htm#008">deep</a> pits, that they rise not up again.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> Let not an evil speaker be established in the earth: evil shall hunt the violent man to overthrow him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> I know that the LORD will maintain the cause of the afflicted, and the right of the <a href="../ebd/ebd298.htm#006">poor</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> Surely the righteous shall give thanks unto thy name: the upright shall dwell in thy presence.<br>
 </p>
