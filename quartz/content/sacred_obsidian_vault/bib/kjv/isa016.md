 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Isaiah Chapter 16</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Send ye the <a href="../ebd/ebd223.htm#003">lamb</a> to the ruler of the land from <a href="../ebd/ebd326.htm#006">Sela</a> to the <a href="../ebd/ebd381.htm#005">wilderness</a>, unto the <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#007">mount</a> of the daughter of <a href="../ebd/ebd394.htm#002">Zion</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> For it shall be, that, as a <a href="../ebd/ebd378.htm#008">wandering</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd059.htm#000">bird</a> cast out of the nest, so the daughters of <a href="../ebd/ebd258.htm#005">Moab</a> shall be at the fords of <a href="../ebd/ebd032.htm#000">Arnon</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Take counsel, execute judgment; make thy <a href="../ebd/ebd330.htm#002">shadow</a> as the night in the midst of the noonday; hide the outcasts; <a href="../ebd/ebd057.htm#007">bewray</a> not him that wandereth.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Let mine outcasts dwell with thee, <a href="../ebd/ebd258.htm#005">Moab</a>; be thou a covert to them from the face of the spoiler: for the extortioner is at an end, the spoiler ceaseth, the oppressors are consumed out of the land.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And in <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> shall the <a href="../ebd/ebd365.htm#001">throne</a> be established: and he shall sit upon it in truth in the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a>, judging, and seeking judgment, and hasting <a href="../ebd/ebd313.htm#004">righteousness</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> We have heard of the pride of <a href="../ebd/ebd258.htm#005">Moab</a>; he is very proud: even of his haughtiness, and his pride, and his wrath: but his lies shall not be so.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Therefore shall <a href="../ebd/ebd258.htm#005">Moab</a> howl for <a href="../ebd/ebd258.htm#005">Moab</a>, every one shall howl: for the foundations of Kirhareseth shall ye <a href="../ebd/ebd261.htm#004">mourn</a>; surely they are stricken.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> For the fields of <a href="../ebd/ebd176.htm#009">Heshbon</a> languish, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd377.htm#004">vine</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd342.htm#006">Sibmah</a>: the lords of the <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#005">heathen</a> have broken down the principal plants thereof, they are come even unto Jazer, they wandered through the <a href="../ebd/ebd381.htm#005">wilderness</a>: her branches are stretched out, they are gone over the sea.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> Therefore I will bewail with the weeping of Jazer the <a href="../ebd/ebd377.htm#004">vine</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd342.htm#006">Sibmah</a>: I will water thee with my tears, O <a href="../ebd/ebd176.htm#009">Heshbon</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd114.htm#009">Elealeh</a>: for the shouting for thy summer fruits and for thy <a href="../ebd/ebd165.htm#008">harvest</a> is fallen.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And gladness is taken away, and joy out of the plentiful field; and in the vineyards there shall be no singing, neither shall there be shouting: the treaders shall tread out no <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#000">wine</a> in their presses; I have made their vintage shouting to cease.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> Wherefore my <a href="../ebd/ebd063.htm#004">bowels</a> shall sound like an <a href="../ebd/ebd165.htm#001">harp</a> for <a href="../ebd/ebd258.htm#005">Moab</a>, and mine inward parts for Kirharesh.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And it shall come to pass, when it is seen that <a href="../ebd/ebd258.htm#005">Moab</a> is weary on the high place, that he shall come to his <a href="../ebd/ebd321.htm#005">sanctuary</a> to pray; but he shall not prevail.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> This is the word that the LORD hath spoken concerning <a href="../ebd/ebd258.htm#005">Moab</a> since that time.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> But now the LORD hath spoken, saying, Within three years, as the years of an <a href="../ebd/ebd179.htm#004">hireling</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd258.htm#005">Moab</a> shall be contemned, with all that great multitude; and the remnant shall be very small and feeble.<br>
 </p>
