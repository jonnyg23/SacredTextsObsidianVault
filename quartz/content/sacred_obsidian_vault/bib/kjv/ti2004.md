 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: 2 Timothy Chapter 4</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> I charge thee therefore before God, and the Lord <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a>, who shall <a href="../ebd/ebd213.htm#005">judge</a> the quick and the dead at his appearing and his kingdom;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Preach the word; be instant in season, out of season; reprove, rebuke, exhort with all longsuffering and doctrine.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> For the time will come when they will not endure sound doctrine; but after their own lusts shall they <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#000">heap</a> to themselves teachers, having itching ears;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And they shall turn away their ears from the truth, and shall be turned unto fables.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> But watch thou in all things, endure <a href="../ebd/ebd011.htm#006">afflictions</a>, do the work of an <a href="../ebd/ebd127.htm#004">evangelist</a>, make full proof of thy ministry.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> For I am now ready to be offered, and the time of my departure is at hand.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> I have fought a good fight, I have finished my course, I have kept the faith:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> Henceforth there is laid up for me a <a href="../ebd/ebd093.htm#001">crown</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd313.htm#004">righteousness</a>, which the Lord, the righteous <a href="../ebd/ebd213.htm#005">judge</a>, shall give me at that day: and not to me only, but unto all them also that love his appearing.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> Do thy diligence to come shortly unto me:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> For <a href="../ebd/ebd101.htm#004">Demas</a> hath forsaken me, having loved this present world, and is departed unto <a href="../ebd/ebd364.htm#000">Thessalonica</a>; <a href="../ebd/ebd092.htm#005">Crescens</a> to <a href="../ebd/ebd141.htm#004">Galatia</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd368.htm#007">Titus</a> unto <a href="../ebd/ebd096.htm#005">Dalmatia</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> Only <a href="../ebd/ebd233.htm#002">Luke</a> is with me. Take Mark, and bring him with thee: for he is profitable to me for the ministry.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd373.htm#008">Tychicus</a> have I sent to <a href="../ebd/ebd122.htm#007">Ephesus</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> The cloke that I left at <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#008">Troas</a> with Carpus, when thou comest, bring with thee, and the books, but especially the parchments.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd016.htm#009">Alexander</a> the coppersmith did me much evil: the Lord reward him according to his works:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> Of whom be thou ware also; for he hath greatly withstood our words.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> At my first answer no man stood with me, but all men forsook me: I pray God that it may not be laid to their charge.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> Notwithstanding the Lord stood with me, and strengthened me; that by me the preaching might be fully known, and that all the <a href="../ebd/ebd146.htm#001">Gentiles</a> might hear: and I was delivered out of the mouth of the lion.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> And the Lord shall deliver me from every evil work, and will preserve me unto his heavenly kingdom: to whom be <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a> for ever and ever. <a href="../ebd/ebd020.htm#001">Amen</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> Salute Prisca and <a href="../ebd/ebd027.htm#006">Aquila</a>, and the household of <a href="../ebd/ebd279.htm#000">Onesiphorus</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd124.htm#001">Erastus</a> abode at <a href="../ebd/ebd090.htm#002">Corinth</a>: but <a href="../ebd/ebd373.htm#000">Trophimus</a> have I left at Miletum sick.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> Do thy diligence to come before winter. Eubulus greeteth thee, and <a href="../ebd/ebd302.htm#001">Pudens</a>, and Linus, and <a href="../ebd/ebd084.htm#002">Claudia</a>, and all the brethren.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> The Lord <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a> be with thy <a href="../ebd/ebd349.htm#009">spirit</a>. <a href="../ebd/ebd154.htm#000">Grace</a> be with you. <a href="../ebd/ebd020.htm#001">Amen</a>.<br>
 </p>
