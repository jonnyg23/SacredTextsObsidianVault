 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Zechariah Chapter 14</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Behold, the day of the LORD cometh, and thy spoil shall be divided in the midst of thee.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> For I will gather all nations against <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a> to battle; and the city shall be taken, and the houses rifled, and the women ravished; and half of the city shall go forth into <a href="../ebd/ebd072.htm#002">captivity</a>, and the residue of the people shall not be cut off from the city.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Then shall the LORD go forth, and fight against those nations, as when he fought in the day of battle.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And his feet shall stand in that day upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#007">mount</a> of Olives, which is before <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a> on the <a href="../ebd/ebd111.htm#000">east</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#007">mount</a> of Olives shall cleave in the midst thereof toward the <a href="../ebd/ebd111.htm#000">east</a> and toward the west, and there shall be a very great <a href="../ebd/ebd376.htm#008">valley</a>; and half of the mountain shall remove toward the north, and half of it toward the south.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And ye shall flee to the <a href="../ebd/ebd376.htm#008">valley</a> of the mountains; for the <a href="../ebd/ebd376.htm#008">valley</a> of the mountains shall reach unto <a href="../ebd/ebd037.htm#004">Azal</a>: yea, ye shall flee, like as ye fled from before the <a href="../ebd/ebd110.htm#009">earthquake</a> in the days of <a href="../ebd/ebd376.htm#004">Uzziah</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>: and the LORD my God shall come, and all the saints with thee.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And it shall come to pass in that day, that the light shall not be clear, nor dark:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> But it shall be one day which shall be known to the LORD, not day, nor night: but it shall come to pass, that at evening time it shall be light.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And it shall be in that day, that living waters shall go out from <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>; half of them toward the former sea, and half of them toward the hinder sea: in summer and in winter shall it be.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And the LORD shall be <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> over all the earth: in that day shall there be one LORD, and his name one.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> All the land shall be turned as a <a href="../ebd/ebd297.htm#002">plain</a> from <a href="../ebd/ebd144.htm#004">Geba</a> to <a href="../ebd/ebd313.htm#005">Rimmon</a> south of <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>: and it shall be lifted up, and inhabited in her place, from Benjamin's <a href="../ebd/ebd143.htm#008">gate</a> unto the place of the first <a href="../ebd/ebd143.htm#008">gate</a>, unto the corner <a href="../ebd/ebd143.htm#008">gate</a>, and from the tower of <a href="../ebd/ebd162.htm#002">Hananeel</a> unto the king's winepresses.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And men shall dwell in it, and there shall be no more utter <a href="../ebd/ebd102.htm#004">destruction</a>; but <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a> shall be safely inhabited.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And this shall be the <a href="../ebd/ebd297.htm#001">plague</a> wherewith the LORD will smite all the people that have fought against <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>; Their <a href="../ebd/ebd135.htm#007">flesh</a> shall consume away while they stand upon their feet, and their eyes shall consume away in their holes, and their tongue shall consume away in their mouth.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> And it shall come to pass in that day, that a great tumult from the LORD shall be among them; and they shall lay hold every one on the hand of his neighbour, and his hand shall rise up against the hand of his neighbour.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a> also shall fight at <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>; and the wealth of all the <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#005">heathen</a> round about shall be gathered together, <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#000">silver</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd027.htm#000">apparel</a>, in great abundance.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And so shall be the <a href="../ebd/ebd297.htm#001">plague</a> of the horse, of the <a href="../ebd/ebd262.htm#001">mule</a>, of the <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#000">camel</a>, and of the <a href="../ebd/ebd035.htm#001">ass</a>, and of all the beasts that shall be in these tents, as this <a href="../ebd/ebd297.htm#001">plague</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> And it shall come to pass, that every one that is left of all the nations which came against <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a> shall even go up from year to year to <a href="../ebd/ebd384.htm#002">worship</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">King</a>, the LORD of hosts, and to keep the <a href="../ebd/ebd132.htm#000">feast</a> of tabernacles.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> And it shall be, that whoso will not come up of all the families of the earth unto <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a> to <a href="../ebd/ebd384.htm#002">worship</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">King</a>, the LORD of hosts, even upon them shall be no <a href="../ebd/ebd305.htm#008">rain</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> And if the family of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a> go not up, and come not, that have no <a href="../ebd/ebd305.htm#008">rain</a>; there shall be the <a href="../ebd/ebd297.htm#001">plague</a>, wherewith the LORD will smite the <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#005">heathen</a> that come not up to keep the <a href="../ebd/ebd132.htm#000">feast</a> of tabernacles.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> This shall be the <a href="../ebd/ebd302.htm#005">punishment</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd302.htm#005">punishment</a> of all nations that come not up to keep the <a href="../ebd/ebd132.htm#000">feast</a> of tabernacles.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> In that day shall there be upon the bells of the horses, <a href="../ebd/ebd180.htm#006">HOLINESS</a> UNTO THE LORD; and the pots in the LORD's house shall be like the bowls before the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> Yea, every pot in <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a> and in <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a> shall be <a href="../ebd/ebd180.htm#006">holiness</a> unto the LORD of hosts: and all they that <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#002">sacrifice</a> shall come and take of them, and <a href="../ebd/ebd326.htm#001">seethe</a> therein: and in that day there shall be no more the <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#008">Canaanite</a> in the house of the LORD of hosts.<br>
 </p>
