 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Exodus Chapter 26</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Moreover thou shalt make the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> with ten curtains of fine twined <a href="../ebd/ebd229.htm#008">linen</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#003">blue</a>, and purple, and <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#005">scarlet</a>: with cherubims of cunning work shalt thou make them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> The length of one <a href="../ebd/ebd094.htm#004">curtain</a> shall be eight and twenty cubits, and the breadth of one <a href="../ebd/ebd094.htm#004">curtain</a> four cubits: and every one of the curtains shall have one measure.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> The five curtains shall be coupled together one to another; and other five curtains shall be coupled one to another.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And thou shalt make loops of <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#003">blue</a> upon the edge of the one <a href="../ebd/ebd094.htm#004">curtain</a> from the selvedge in the coupling; and likewise shalt thou make in the uttermost edge of another <a href="../ebd/ebd094.htm#004">curtain</a>, in the coupling of the second.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Fifty loops shalt thou make in the one <a href="../ebd/ebd094.htm#004">curtain</a>, and fifty loops shalt thou make in the edge of the <a href="../ebd/ebd094.htm#004">curtain</a> that is in the coupling of the second; that the loops may take hold one of another.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And thou shalt make fifty <a href="../ebd/ebd357.htm#001">taches</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a>, and couple the curtains together with the <a href="../ebd/ebd357.htm#001">taches</a>: and it shall be one <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And thou shalt make curtains of goats' hair to be a covering upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a>: eleven curtains shalt thou make.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> The length of one <a href="../ebd/ebd094.htm#004">curtain</a> shall be thirty cubits, and the breadth of one <a href="../ebd/ebd094.htm#004">curtain</a> four cubits: and the eleven curtains shall be all of one measure.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And thou shalt couple five curtains by themselves, and six curtains by themselves, and shalt double the sixth <a href="../ebd/ebd094.htm#004">curtain</a> in the forefront of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And thou shalt make fifty loops on the edge of the one <a href="../ebd/ebd094.htm#004">curtain</a> that is outmost in the coupling, and fifty loops in the edge of the <a href="../ebd/ebd094.htm#004">curtain</a> which coupleth the second.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And thou shalt make fifty <a href="../ebd/ebd357.htm#001">taches</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#003">brass</a>, and put the <a href="../ebd/ebd357.htm#001">taches</a> into the loops, and couple the <a href="../ebd/ebd361.htm#009">tent</a> together, that it may be one.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And the remnant that remaineth of the curtains of the <a href="../ebd/ebd361.htm#009">tent</a>, the half <a href="../ebd/ebd094.htm#004">curtain</a> that remaineth, shall hang over the backside of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> And a <a href="../ebd/ebd093.htm#006">cubit</a> on the one side, and a <a href="../ebd/ebd093.htm#006">cubit</a> on the other side of that which remaineth in the length of the curtains of the <a href="../ebd/ebd361.htm#009">tent</a>, it shall hang over the sides of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> on this side and on that side, to cover it.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> And thou shalt make a covering for the <a href="../ebd/ebd361.htm#009">tent</a> of rams' skins dyed red, and a covering above of badgers' skins.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And thou shalt make boards for the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd339.htm#009">shittim</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd383.htm#003">wood</a> standing up.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> Ten cubits shall be the length of a board, and a <a href="../ebd/ebd093.htm#006">cubit</a> and a half shall be the breadth of one board.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> Two tenons shall there be in one board, set in order one against another: thus shalt thou make for all the boards of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> And thou shalt make the boards for the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a>, twenty boards on the south side southward.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> And thou shalt make forty sockets of <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#000">silver</a> under the twenty boards; two sockets under one board for his two tenons, and two sockets under another board for his two tenons.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> And for the second side of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> on the north side there shall be twenty boards:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> And their forty sockets of <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#000">silver</a>; two sockets under one board, and two sockets under another board.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> And for the sides of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd380.htm#008">westward</a> thou shalt make six boards.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> And two boards shalt thou make for the corners of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> in the two sides.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> And they shall be coupled together beneath, and they shall be coupled together above the head of it unto one <a href="../ebd/ebd313.htm#007">ring</a>: thus shall it be for them both; they shall be for the two corners.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> And they shall be eight boards, and their sockets of <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#000">silver</a>, sixteen sockets; two sockets under one board, and two sockets under another board.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> And thou shalt make bars of <a href="../ebd/ebd339.htm#009">shittim</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd383.htm#003">wood</a>; five for the boards of the one side of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> And five bars for the boards of the other side of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a>, and five bars for the boards of the side of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a>, for the two sides <a href="../ebd/ebd380.htm#008">westward</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="028">28</a> And the middle bar in the midst of the boards shall reach from end to end.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="029">29</a> And thou shalt overlay the boards with <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a>, and make their rings of <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a> for places for the bars: and thou shalt overlay the bars with <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="030">30</a> And thou shalt rear up the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> according to the fashion thereof which was shewed thee in the <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#007">mount</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="031">31</a> And thou shalt make a vail of <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#003">blue</a>, and purple, and <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#005">scarlet</a>, and fine twined <a href="../ebd/ebd229.htm#008">linen</a> of cunning work: with cherubims shall it be made:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="032">32</a> And thou shalt hang it upon four pillars of <a href="../ebd/ebd339.htm#009">shittim</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd383.htm#003">wood</a> overlaid with <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a>: their hooks shall be of <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a>, upon the four sockets of <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#000">silver</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="033">33</a> And thou shalt hang up the vail under the <a href="../ebd/ebd357.htm#001">taches</a>, that thou mayest bring in thither within the vail the <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#000">ark</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd362.htm#008">testimony</a>: and the vail shall divide unto you between the holy place and the most holy.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="034">34</a> And thou shalt put the <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> seat upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#000">ark</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd362.htm#008">testimony</a> in the most holy place.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="035">35</a> And thou shalt set the table without the vail, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd071.htm#001">candlestick</a> over against the table on the side of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> toward the south: and thou shalt put the table on the north side.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="036">36</a> And thou shalt make an <a href="../ebd/ebd163.htm#001">hanging</a> for the door of the <a href="../ebd/ebd361.htm#009">tent</a>, of <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#003">blue</a>, and purple, and <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#005">scarlet</a>, and fine twined <a href="../ebd/ebd229.htm#008">linen</a>, wrought with needlework.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="037">37</a> And thou shalt make for the <a href="../ebd/ebd163.htm#001">hanging</a> five pillars of <a href="../ebd/ebd339.htm#009">shittim</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd383.htm#003">wood</a>, and overlay them with <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a>, and their hooks shall be of <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a>: and thou shalt cast five sockets of <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#003">brass</a> for them.<br>
 </p>
