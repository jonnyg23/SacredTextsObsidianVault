 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: 1 Chronicles Chapter 27</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Now the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> after their number, to wit, the chief fathers and captains of <a href="../ebd/ebd364.htm#008">thousands</a> and hundreds, and their officers that served the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> in any matter of the <a href="../ebd/ebd091.htm#006">courses</a>, which came in and went out month by month throughout all the months of the year, of every course were twenty and four thousand.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Over the first course for the first month was <a href="../ebd/ebd198.htm#000">Jashobeam</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd385.htm#009">Zabdiel</a>: and in his course were twenty and four thousand.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd289.htm#009">Perez</a> was the chief of all the captains of the <a href="../ebd/ebd183.htm#007">host</a> for the first month.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And over the course of the second month was <a href="../ebd/ebd105.htm#003">Dodai</a> an <a href="../ebd/ebd015.htm#004">Ahohite</a>, and of his course was <a href="../ebd/ebd254.htm#005">Mikloth</a> also the ruler: in his course likewise were twenty and four thousand.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> The third captain of the <a href="../ebd/ebd183.htm#007">host</a> for the third month was <a href="../ebd/ebd052.htm#003">Benaiah</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd200.htm#009">Jehoiada</a>, a chief <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a>: and in his course were twenty and four thousand.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> This is that <a href="../ebd/ebd052.htm#003">Benaiah</a>, who was mighty among the thirty, and above the thirty: and in his course was <a href="../ebd/ebd021.htm#001">Ammizabad</a> his son.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> The fourth captain for the fourth month was <a href="../ebd/ebd033.htm#001">Asahel</a> the brother of <a href="../ebd/ebd207.htm#006">Joab</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd388.htm#005">Zebadiah</a> his son after him: and in his course were twenty and four thousand.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> The fifth captain for the fifth month was Shamhuth the <a href="../ebd/ebd192.htm#002">Izrahite</a>: and in his course were twenty and four thousand.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> The sixth captain for the sixth month was <a href="../ebd/ebd189.htm#000">Ira</a> the son of Ikkesh the Tekoite: and in his course were twenty and four thousand.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> The seventh captain for the seventh month was <a href="../ebd/ebd172.htm#008">Helez</a> the Pelonite, of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd123.htm#000">Ephraim</a>: and in his course were twenty and four thousand.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> The eighth captain for the eighth month was <a href="../ebd/ebd342.htm#005">Sibbecai</a> the Hushathite, of the Zarhites: and in his course were twenty and four thousand.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> The ninth captain for the ninth month was <a href="../ebd/ebd003.htm#000">Abiezer</a> the Anetothite, of the Benjamites: and in his course were twenty and four thousand.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> The tenth captain for the tenth month was Maharai the Netophathite, of the Zarhites: and in his course were twenty and four thousand.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> The eleventh captain for the eleventh month was <a href="../ebd/ebd052.htm#003">Benaiah</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd296.htm#003">Pirathonite</a>, of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd123.htm#000">Ephraim</a>: and in his course were twenty and four thousand.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> The twelfth captain for the twelfth month was <a href="../ebd/ebd172.htm#002">Heldai</a> the Netophathite, of <a href="../ebd/ebd281.htm#003">Othniel</a>: and in his course were twenty and four thousand.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> Furthermore over the tribes of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>: the ruler of the Reubenites was <a href="../ebd/ebd116.htm#007">Eliezer</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd392.htm#007">Zichri</a>: of the Simeonites, <a href="../ebd/ebd335.htm#005">Shephatiah</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd234.htm#004">Maachah</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> Of the Levites, <a href="../ebd/ebd166.htm#001">Hashabiah</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd217.htm#001">Kemuel</a>: of the <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#002">Aaronites</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd386.htm#008">Zadok</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> Of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd116.htm#008">Elihu</a>, one of the brethren of <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a>: of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#002">Issachar</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd278.htm#006">Omri</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd252.htm#009">Michael</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> Of <a href="../ebd/ebd389.htm#003">Zebulun</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd190.htm#006">Ishmaiah</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd276.htm#001">Obadiah</a>: of <a href="../ebd/ebd266.htm#006">Naphtali</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd203.htm#009">Jerimoth</a> the son of Azriel:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> Of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd123.htm#000">Ephraim</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd183.htm#006">Hoshea</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd037.htm#007">Azaziah</a>: of the half <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd240.htm#002">Manasseh</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd208.htm#005">Joel</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd288.htm#001">Pedaiah</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> Of the half <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd240.htm#002">Manasseh</a> in <a href="../ebd/ebd148.htm#009">Gilead</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd186.htm#005">Iddo</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd389.htm#006">Zechariah</a>: of <a href="../ebd/ebd052.htm#008">Benjamin</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd193.htm#000">Jaasiel</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd005.htm#002">Abner</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> Of <a href="../ebd/ebd096.htm#009">Dan</a>, Azareel the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#001">Jeroham</a>. These were the princes of the tribes of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> But <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> took not the number of them from twenty years old and under: because the LORD had said he would increase <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> like to the <a href="../ebd/ebd350.htm#008">stars</a> of the heavens.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd207.htm#006">Joab</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd391.htm#009">Zeruiah</a> began to number, but he finished not, because there fell wrath for it against <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>; neither was the number put in the account of the <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#003">chronicles</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> And over the king's treasures was <a href="../ebd/ebd038.htm#000">Azmaveth</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd008.htm#008">Adiel</a>: and over the storehouses in the fields, in the cities, and in the <a href="../ebd/ebd377.htm#003">villages</a>, and in the castles, was <a href="../ebd/ebd201.htm#004">Jehonathan</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd376.htm#004">Uzziah</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> And over them that did the work of the field for tillage of the ground was <a href="../ebd/ebd129.htm#009">Ezri</a> the son of Chelub:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> And over the vineyards was <a href="../ebd/ebd338.htm#002">Shimei</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd307.htm#000">Ramathite</a>: over the increase of the vineyards for the <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#000">wine</a> cellars was <a href="../ebd/ebd385.htm#008">Zabdi</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd339.htm#004">Shiphmite</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="028">28</a> And over the <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#009">olive</a> trees and the sycomore trees that were in the low plains was Baalhanan the Gederite: and over the cellars of oil was <a href="../ebd/ebd208.htm#000">Joash</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="029">29</a> And over the herds that fed in <a href="../ebd/ebd331.htm#009">Sharon</a> was Shitrai the Sharonite: and over the herds that were in the valleys was <a href="../ebd/ebd331.htm#005">Shaphat</a> the son of Adlai:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="030">30</a> Over the camels also was <a href="../ebd/ebd276.htm#007">Obil</a> the Ishmaelite: and over the asses was <a href="../ebd/ebd200.htm#002">Jehdeiah</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd250.htm#004">Meronothite</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="031">31</a> And over the flocks was Jaziz the Hagerite. All these were the rulers of the substance which was <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> David's.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="032">32</a> Also <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#000">Jonathan</a> David's uncle was a <a href="../ebd/ebd091.htm#005">counsellor</a>, a <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#004">wise</a> man, and a scribe: and <a href="../ebd/ebd200.htm#003">Jehiel</a> the son of Hachmoni was with the king's sons:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="033">33</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd015.htm#000">Ahithophel</a> was the king's <a href="../ebd/ebd091.htm#005">counsellor</a>: and <a href="../ebd/ebd185.htm#003">Hushai</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd029.htm#006">Archite</a> was the king's companion:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="034">34</a> And after <a href="../ebd/ebd015.htm#000">Ahithophel</a> was <a href="../ebd/ebd200.htm#009">Jehoiada</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd052.htm#003">Benaiah</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd002.htm#005">Abiathar</a>: and the general of the king's army was <a href="../ebd/ebd207.htm#006">Joab</a>.<br>
 </p>
