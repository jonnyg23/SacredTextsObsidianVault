 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Exodus Chapter 23</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Thou shalt not raise a false report: put not thine hand with the wicked to be an unrighteous <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#008">witness</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Thou shalt not follow a multitude to do evil; neither shalt thou speak in a cause to decline after many to wrest judgment:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Neither shalt thou countenance a <a href="../ebd/ebd298.htm#006">poor</a> man in his cause.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> If thou meet thine enemy's <a href="../ebd/ebd281.htm#007">ox</a> or his <a href="../ebd/ebd035.htm#001">ass</a> going astray, thou shalt surely bring it back to him again.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> If thou see the <a href="../ebd/ebd035.htm#001">ass</a> of him that hateth thee lying under his <a href="../ebd/ebd066.htm#007">burden</a>, and wouldest forbear to help him, thou shalt surely help with him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Thou shalt not wrest the judgment of thy <a href="../ebd/ebd298.htm#006">poor</a> in his cause.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Keep thee far from a false matter; and the innocent and righteous slay thou not: for I will not justify the wicked.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And thou shalt take no <a href="../ebd/ebd148.htm#005">gift</a>: for the <a href="../ebd/ebd148.htm#005">gift</a> blindeth the <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#004">wise</a>, and perverteth the words of the righteous.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> Also thou shalt not oppress a <a href="../ebd/ebd351.htm#009">stranger</a>: for ye know the heart of a <a href="../ebd/ebd351.htm#009">stranger</a>, seeing ye were strangers in the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And six years thou shalt sow thy land, and shalt gather in the fruits thereof:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> But the seventh year thou shalt let it rest and lie still; that the <a href="../ebd/ebd298.htm#006">poor</a> of thy people may eat: and what they leave the beasts of the field shall eat. In like manner thou shalt deal with thy vineyard, and with thy oliveyard.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> Six days thou shalt do thy work, and on the seventh day thou shalt rest: that thine <a href="../ebd/ebd281.htm#007">ox</a> and thine <a href="../ebd/ebd035.htm#001">ass</a> may rest, and the son of thy <a href="../ebd/ebd162.htm#008">handmaid</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd351.htm#009">stranger</a>, may be refreshed.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> And in all things that I have said unto you be circumspect: and make no mention of the name of other gods, neither let it be heard out of thy mouth.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> Three times thou shalt keep a <a href="../ebd/ebd132.htm#000">feast</a> unto me in the year.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> Thou shalt keep the <a href="../ebd/ebd132.htm#000">feast</a> of unleavened <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#006">bread</a>: (thou shalt eat unleavened <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#006">bread</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> days, as I commanded thee, in the time appointed of the month <a href="../ebd/ebd002.htm#006">Abib</a>; for in it thou camest out from <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a>: and none shall appear before me empty:)<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd132.htm#000">feast</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd165.htm#008">harvest</a>, the firstfruits of thy labours, which thou hast sown in the field: and the <a href="../ebd/ebd132.htm#000">feast</a> of ingathering, which is in the end of the year, when thou hast gathered in thy labours out of the field.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> Three items in the year all thy males shall appear before the LORD God.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> Thou shalt not offer the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> of my <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#002">sacrifice</a> with leavened <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#006">bread</a>; neither shall the <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#005">fat</a> of my <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#002">sacrifice</a> remain until the morning.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> The first of the firstfruits of thy land thou shalt bring into the house of the LORD thy God. Thou shalt not <a href="../ebd/ebd326.htm#001">seethe</a> a kid in his mother's <a href="../ebd/ebd255.htm#000">milk</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> Behold, I send an <a href="../ebd/ebd024.htm#001">Angel</a> before thee, to keep thee in the way, and to bring thee into the place which I have prepared.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> Beware of him, and obey his voice, provoke him not; for he will not <a href="../ebd/ebd285.htm#001">pardon</a> your transgressions: for my name is in him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> But if thou shalt indeed obey his voice, and do all that I speak; then I will be an enemy unto thine enemies, and an <a href="../ebd/ebd011.htm#001">adversary</a> unto thine adversaries.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> For mine <a href="../ebd/ebd024.htm#001">Angel</a> shall go before thee, and bring thee in unto the <a href="../ebd/ebd021.htm#006">Amorites</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd179.htm#006">Hittites</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd290.htm#006">Perizzites</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#007">Canaanites</a>, the <a href="../ebd/ebd179.htm#007">Hivites</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd199.htm#004">Jebusites</a>: and I will cut them off.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> Thou shalt not <a href="../ebd/ebd063.htm#003">bow</a> down to their gods, nor serve them, nor do after their works: but thou shalt utterly overthrow them, and quite break down their images.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> And ye shall serve the LORD your God, and he shall <a href="../ebd/ebd060.htm#008">bless</a> thy <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#006">bread</a>, and thy water; and I will take sickness away from the midst of thee.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> There shall nothing cast their young, nor be <a href="../ebd/ebd045.htm#007">barren</a>, in thy land: the number of thy days I will fulfil.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> I will send my fear before thee, and will destroy all the people to whom thou shalt come, and I will make all thine enemies turn their backs unto thee.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="028">28</a> And I will send hornets before thee, which shall drive out the Hivite, the <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#008">Canaanite</a>, and the Hittite, from before thee.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="029">29</a> I will not drive them out from before thee in one year; lest the land become desolate, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd049.htm#001">beast</a> of the field multiply against thee.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="030">30</a> By little and little I will drive them out from before thee, until thou be increased, and inherit the land.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="031">31</a> And I will set thy bounds from the Red sea even unto the sea of the <a href="../ebd/ebd294.htm#002">Philistines</a>, and from the <a href="../ebd/ebd102.htm#000">desert</a> unto the river: for I will deliver the inhabitants of the land into your hand; and thou shalt drive them out before thee.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="032">32</a> Thou shalt make no <a href="../ebd/ebd091.htm#008">covenant</a> with them, nor with their gods.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="033">33</a> They shall not dwell in thy land, lest they make thee <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> against me: for if thou serve their gods, it will surely be a <a href="../ebd/ebd346.htm#009">snare</a> unto thee.<br>
 </p>
