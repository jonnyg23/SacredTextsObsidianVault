 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Deuteronomy Chapter 23</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> He that is wounded in the stones, or hath his privy member cut off, shall not enter into the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> of the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> A <a href="../ebd/ebd047.htm#000">bastard</a> shall not enter into the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> of the LORD; even to his tenth <a href="../ebd/ebd145.htm#008">generation</a> shall he not enter into the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> of the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> An <a href="../ebd/ebd021.htm#003">Ammonite</a> or <a href="../ebd/ebd258.htm#006">Moabite</a> shall not enter into the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> of the LORD; even to their tenth <a href="../ebd/ebd145.htm#008">generation</a> shall they not enter into the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> of the LORD for ever:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Because they met you not with <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#006">bread</a> and with water in the way, when ye came forth out of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a>; and because they hired against thee <a href="../ebd/ebd042.htm#003">Balaam</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd052.htm#009">Beor</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd291.htm#006">Pethor</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd251.htm#003">Mesopotamia</a>, to <a href="../ebd/ebd094.htm#003">curse</a> thee.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Nevertheless the LORD thy God would not hearken unto <a href="../ebd/ebd042.htm#003">Balaam</a>; but the LORD thy God turned the <a href="../ebd/ebd094.htm#003">curse</a> into a blessing unto thee, because the LORD thy God loved thee.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Thou shalt not seek their peace nor their prosperity all thy days for ever.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Thou shalt not abhor an Edomite; for he is thy brother: thou shalt not abhor an Egyptian; because thou wast a <a href="../ebd/ebd351.htm#009">stranger</a> in his land.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> The children that are begotten of them shall enter into the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> of the LORD in their third <a href="../ebd/ebd145.htm#008">generation</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> When the <a href="../ebd/ebd183.htm#007">host</a> goeth forth against thine enemies, then keep thee from every wicked thing.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> If there be among you any man, that is not <a href="../ebd/ebd084.htm#005">clean</a> by reason of uncleanness that chanceth him by night, then shall he go abroad out of the <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#002">camp</a>, he shall not come within the <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#002">camp</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> But it shall be, when evening cometh on, he shall wash himself with water: and when the <a href="../ebd/ebd353.htm#001">sun</a> is down, he shall come into the <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#002">camp</a> again.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> Thou shalt have a place also without the <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#002">camp</a>, whither thou shalt go forth abroad:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> And thou shalt have a paddle upon thy weapon; and it shall be, when thou wilt ease thyself abroad, thou shalt dig therewith, and shalt turn back and cover that which cometh from thee:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> For the LORD thy God walketh in the midst of thy <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#002">camp</a>, to deliver thee, and to give up thine enemies before thee; therefore shall thy <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#002">camp</a> be holy: that he see no unclean thing in thee, and turn away from thee.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> Thou shalt not deliver unto his master the servant which is escaped from his master unto thee:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> He shall dwell with thee, even among you, in that place which he shall choose in one of thy gates, where it liketh him best: thou shalt not oppress him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> There shall be no whore of the daughters of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, nor a sodomite of the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> Thou shalt not bring the hire of a whore, or the price of a dog, into the house of the LORD thy God for any vow: for even both these are <a href="../ebd/ebd005.htm#003">abomination</a> unto the LORD thy God.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> Thou shalt not lend upon <a href="../ebd/ebd375.htm#006">usury</a> to thy brother; <a href="../ebd/ebd375.htm#006">usury</a> of money, <a href="../ebd/ebd375.htm#006">usury</a> of victuals, <a href="../ebd/ebd375.htm#006">usury</a> of any thing that is lent upon <a href="../ebd/ebd375.htm#006">usury</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> Unto a <a href="../ebd/ebd351.htm#009">stranger</a> thou mayest lend upon <a href="../ebd/ebd375.htm#006">usury</a>; but unto thy brother thou shalt not lend upon <a href="../ebd/ebd375.htm#006">usury</a>: that the LORD thy God may <a href="../ebd/ebd060.htm#008">bless</a> thee in all that thou settest thine hand to in the land whither thou goest to possess it.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> When thou shalt vow a vow unto the LORD thy God, thou shalt not slack to pay it: for the LORD thy God will surely require it of thee; and it would be <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> in thee.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> But if thou shalt forbear to vow, it shall be no <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> in thee.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> That which is gone out of thy lips thou shalt keep and perform; even a freewill <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, according as thou hast vowed unto the LORD thy God, which thou hast promised with thy mouth.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> When thou comest into thy neighbour's vineyard, then thou mayest eat grapes thy fill at thine own pleasure; but thou shalt not put any in thy vessel.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> When thou comest into the standing <a href="../ebd/ebd090.htm#006">corn</a> of thy neighbour, then thou mayest pluck the ears with thine hand; but thou shalt not move a <a href="../ebd/ebd342.htm#008">sickle</a> unto thy neighbour's standing <a href="../ebd/ebd090.htm#006">corn</a>.<br>
 </p>
