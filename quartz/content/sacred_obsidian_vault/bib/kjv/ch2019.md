 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: 2 Chronicles Chapter 19</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd201.htm#006">Jehoshaphat</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a> returned to his house in peace to <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd202.htm#007">Jehu</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd162.htm#003">Hanani</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd326.htm#000">seer</a> went out to meet him, and said to <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd201.htm#006">Jehoshaphat</a>, Shouldest thou help the ungodly, and love them that hate the LORD? therefore is wrath upon thee from before the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Nevertheless there are good things found in thee, in that thou hast taken away the groves out of the land, and hast prepared thine heart to seek God.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd201.htm#006">Jehoshaphat</a> dwelt at <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>: and he went out again through the people from <a href="../ebd/ebd050.htm#008">Beersheba</a> to <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#007">mount</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd123.htm#000">Ephraim</a>, and brought them back unto the LORD God of their fathers.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And he set judges in the land throughout all the fenced cities of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>, city by city,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And said to the judges, Take heed what ye do: for ye <a href="../ebd/ebd213.htm#005">judge</a> not for man, but for the LORD, who is with you in the judgment.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Wherefore now let the fear of the LORD be upon you; take heed and do it: for there is no iniquity with the LORD our God, nor respect of persons, nor taking of gifts.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> Moreover in <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a> did <a href="../ebd/ebd201.htm#006">Jehoshaphat</a> set of the Levites, and of the priests, and of the chief of the fathers of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, for the judgment of the LORD, and for controversies, when they returned to <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And he charged them, saying, Thus shall ye do in the fear of the LORD, faithfully, and with a perfect heart.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And what cause soever shall come to you of your brethren that dwell in your cities, between <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a>, between law and commandment, statutes and judgments, ye shall even warn them that they trespass not against the LORD, and so wrath come upon you, and upon your brethren: this do, and ye shall not trespass.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And, behold, <a href="../ebd/ebd019.htm#002">Amariah</a> the chief <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> is over you in all matters of the LORD; and <a href="../ebd/ebd388.htm#005">Zebadiah</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd190.htm#005">Ishmael</a>, the ruler of the house of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>, for all the king's matters: also the Levites shall be officers before you. Deal courageously, and the LORD shall be with the good.<br>
 </p>
