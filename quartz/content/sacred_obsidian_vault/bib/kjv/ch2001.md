 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: 2 Chronicles Chapter 1</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd347.htm#007">Solomon</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> was strengthened in his kingdom, and the LORD his God was with him, and magnified him exceedingly.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Then <a href="../ebd/ebd347.htm#007">Solomon</a> spake unto all <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, to the captains of <a href="../ebd/ebd364.htm#008">thousands</a> and of hundreds, and to the judges, and to every governor in all <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, the chief of the fathers.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> So <a href="../ebd/ebd347.htm#007">Solomon</a>, and all the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> with him, went to the high place that was at <a href="../ebd/ebd148.htm#002">Gibeon</a>; for there was the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> of God, which <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> the servant of the LORD had made in the <a href="../ebd/ebd381.htm#005">wilderness</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> But the <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#000">ark</a> of God had <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> brought up from Kirjathjearim to the place which <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> had prepared for it: for he had pitched a <a href="../ebd/ebd361.htm#009">tent</a> for it at <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Moreover the brasen <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>, that <a href="../ebd/ebd057.htm#009">Bezaleel</a> the son of Uri, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd184.htm#009">Hur</a>, had made, he put before the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the LORD: and <a href="../ebd/ebd347.htm#007">Solomon</a> and the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> sought unto it.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd347.htm#007">Solomon</a> went up thither to the brasen <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> before the LORD, which was at the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>, and offered a thousand burnt offerings upon it.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> In that night did God appear unto <a href="../ebd/ebd347.htm#007">Solomon</a>, and said unto him, Ask what I shall give thee.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd347.htm#007">Solomon</a> said unto God, Thou hast shewed great <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> unto <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> my father, and hast made me to reign in his stead.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> Now, O LORD God, let thy promise unto <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> my father be established: for thou hast made me <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> over a people like the <a href="../ebd/ebd109.htm#008">dust</a> of the earth in multitude.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> Give me now wisdom and knowledge, that I may go out and come in before this people: for who can <a href="../ebd/ebd213.htm#005">judge</a> this thy people, that is so great?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And God said to <a href="../ebd/ebd347.htm#007">Solomon</a>, Because this was in thine heart, and thou hast not asked riches, wealth, or honour, nor the life of thine enemies, neither yet hast asked long life; but hast asked wisdom and knowledge for thyself, that thou mayest <a href="../ebd/ebd213.htm#005">judge</a> my people, over whom I have made thee <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> Wisdom and knowledge is granted unto thee; and I will give thee riches, and wealth, and honour, such as none of the kings have had that have been before thee, neither shall there any after thee have the like.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> Then <a href="../ebd/ebd347.htm#007">Solomon</a> came from his <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#001">journey</a> to the high place that was at <a href="../ebd/ebd148.htm#002">Gibeon</a> to <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>, from before the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>, and reigned over <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd347.htm#007">Solomon</a> gathered chariots and horsemen: and he had a thousand and four hundred chariots, and twelve thousand horsemen, which he placed in the <a href="../ebd/ebd077.htm#008">chariot</a> cities, and with the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> at <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> made <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#000">silver</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a> at <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a> as plenteous as stones, and <a href="../ebd/ebd074.htm#007">cedar</a> trees made he as the sycomore trees that are in the vale for abundance.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd347.htm#007">Solomon</a> had horses brought out of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd229.htm#008">linen</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd384.htm#006">yarn</a>: the king's merchants received the <a href="../ebd/ebd229.htm#008">linen</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd384.htm#006">yarn</a> at a price.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> And they fetched up, and brought forth out of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a> a <a href="../ebd/ebd077.htm#008">chariot</a> for six hundred shekels of <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#000">silver</a>, and an horse for an hundred and fifty: and so brought they out horses for all the kings of the <a href="../ebd/ebd179.htm#006">Hittites</a>, and for the kings of <a href="../ebd/ebd355.htm#002">Syria</a>, by their means.<br>
 </p>
