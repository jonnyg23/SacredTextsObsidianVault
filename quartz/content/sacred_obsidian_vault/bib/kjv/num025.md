 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Numbers Chapter 25</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> abode in <a href="../ebd/ebd339.htm#009">Shittim</a>, and the people began to commit whoredom with the daughters of <a href="../ebd/ebd258.htm#005">Moab</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> And they called the people unto the sacrifices of their gods: and the people did eat, and bowed down to their gods.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> joined himself unto Baalpeor: and the <a href="../ebd/ebd024.htm#002">anger</a> of the LORD was kindled against <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And the LORD said unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, Take all the heads of the people, and hang them up before the LORD against the <a href="../ebd/ebd353.htm#001">sun</a>, that the fierce <a href="../ebd/ebd024.htm#002">anger</a> of the LORD may be turned away from <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> said unto the judges of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, Slay ye every one his men that were joined unto Baalpeor.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And, behold, one of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> came and brought unto his brethren a Midianitish woman in the sight of <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, and in the sight of all the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, who were weeping before the door of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And when <a href="../ebd/ebd294.htm#003">Phinehas</a>, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd115.htm#000">Eleazar</a>, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a>, saw it, he rose up from among the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>, and took a <a href="../ebd/ebd198.htm#006">javelin</a> in his hand;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And he went after the man of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> into the <a href="../ebd/ebd361.htm#009">tent</a>, and thrust both of them through, the man of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, and the woman through her <a href="../ebd/ebd052.htm#000">belly</a>. So the <a href="../ebd/ebd297.htm#001">plague</a> was stayed from the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And those that died in the <a href="../ebd/ebd297.htm#001">plague</a> were twenty and four thousand.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And the LORD spake unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd294.htm#003">Phinehas</a>, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd115.htm#000">Eleazar</a>, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a>, hath turned my wrath away from the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, while he was zealous for my sake among them, that I consumed not the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> in my <a href="../ebd/ebd198.htm#008">jealousy</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> Wherefore say, Behold, I give unto him my <a href="../ebd/ebd091.htm#008">covenant</a> of peace:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> And he shall have it, and his seed after him, even the <a href="../ebd/ebd091.htm#008">covenant</a> of an <a href="../ebd/ebd127.htm#007">everlasting</a> priesthood; because he was zealous for his God, and made an <a href="../ebd/ebd036.htm#004">atonement</a> for the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> Now the name of the Israelite that was slain, even that was slain with the Midianitish woman, was <a href="../ebd/ebd393.htm#009">Zimri</a>, the son of Salu, a <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#004">prince</a> of a chief house among the Simeonites.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And the name of the Midianitish woman that was slain was Cozbi, the daughter of <a href="../ebd/ebd396.htm#005">Zur</a>; he was head over a people, and of a chief house in <a href="../ebd/ebd253.htm#007">Midian</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> And the LORD spake unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> Vex the Midianites, and smite them:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> For they vex you with their wiles, wherewith they have beguiled you in the matter of <a href="../ebd/ebd289.htm#006">Peor</a>, and in the matter of Cozbi, the daughter of a <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#004">prince</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd253.htm#007">Midian</a>, their sister, which was slain in the day of the <a href="../ebd/ebd297.htm#001">plague</a> for Peor's sake.<br>
 </p>
