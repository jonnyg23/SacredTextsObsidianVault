 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 86</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd063.htm#003">Bow</a> down thine <a href="../ebd/ebd110.htm#004">ear</a>, O LORD, hear me: for I am <a href="../ebd/ebd298.htm#006">poor</a> and needy.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Preserve my soul; for I am holy: O thou my God, save thy servant that trusteth in thee.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Be merciful unto me, O Lord: for I cry unto thee daily.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Rejoice the soul of thy servant: for unto thee, O Lord, do I lift up my soul.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> For thou, Lord, art good, and ready to forgive; and plenteous in <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> unto all them that call upon thee.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Give <a href="../ebd/ebd110.htm#004">ear</a>, O LORD, unto my <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#000">prayer</a>; and attend to the voice of my supplications.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> In the day of my trouble I will call upon thee: for thou wilt answer me.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> Among the gods there is none like unto thee, O Lord; neither are there any works like unto thy works.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> All nations whom thou hast made shall come and <a href="../ebd/ebd384.htm#002">worship</a> before thee, O Lord; and shall <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#005">glorify</a> thy name.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> For thou art great, and doest wondrous things: thou art God alone.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> Teach me thy way, O LORD; I will walk in thy truth: unite my heart to fear thy name.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> I will praise thee, O Lord my God, with all my heart: and I will <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#005">glorify</a> thy name for evermore.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> For great is thy <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> toward me: and thou hast delivered my soul from the lowest <a href="../ebd/ebd173.htm#003">hell</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> O God, the proud are risen against me, and the assemblies of violent men have sought after my soul; and have not set thee before them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> But thou, O Lord, art a God full of compassion, and gracious, long suffering, and plenteous in <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> and truth.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> O turn unto me, and have <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> upon me; give thy strength unto thy servant, and save the son of thine <a href="../ebd/ebd162.htm#008">handmaid</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> Shew me a token for good; that they which hate me may see it, and be ashamed: because thou, LORD, hast holpen me, and comforted me.<br>
 </p>
