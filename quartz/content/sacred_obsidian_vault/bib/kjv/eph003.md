 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Ephesians Chapter 3</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> For this cause I <a href="../ebd/ebd287.htm#002">Paul</a>, the prisoner of <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a> for you <a href="../ebd/ebd146.htm#001">Gentiles</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> If ye have heard of the <a href="../ebd/ebd104.htm#006">dispensation</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd154.htm#000">grace</a> of God which is given me to you-ward:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> How that by <a href="../ebd/ebd312.htm#002">revelation</a> he made known unto me the <a href="../ebd/ebd263.htm#006">mystery</a>; (as I wrote afore in few words,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Whereby, when ye read, ye may understand my knowledge in the <a href="../ebd/ebd263.htm#006">mystery</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a>)<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Which in other ages was not made known unto the sons of men, as it is now revealed unto his holy apostles and prophets by the <a href="../ebd/ebd349.htm#009">Spirit</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> That the <a href="../ebd/ebd146.htm#001">Gentiles</a> should be fellowheirs, and of the same body, and partakers of his promise in <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a> by the <a href="../ebd/ebd153.htm#003">gospel</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Whereof I was made a <a href="../ebd/ebd255.htm#007">minister</a>, according to the <a href="../ebd/ebd148.htm#005">gift</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd154.htm#000">grace</a> of God given unto me by the effectual working of his power.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> Unto me, who am less than the least of all saints, is this <a href="../ebd/ebd154.htm#000">grace</a> given, that I should preach among the <a href="../ebd/ebd146.htm#001">Gentiles</a> the unsearchable riches of <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And to make all men see what is the <a href="../ebd/ebd132.htm#002">fellowship</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd263.htm#006">mystery</a>, which from the beginning of the world hath been hid in God, who created all things by <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> To the intent that now unto the principalities and powers in heavenly places might be known by the church the manifold wisdom of God,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> According to the eternal purpose which he purposed in <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a> our Lord:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> In whom we have boldness and access with confidence by the faith of him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> Wherefore I desire that ye faint not at my tribulations for you, which is your <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> For this cause I <a href="../ebd/ebd063.htm#003">bow</a> my knees unto the Father of our Lord <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> Of whom the whole family in <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#006">heaven</a> and earth is named,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> That he would grant you, according to the riches of his <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a>, to be strengthened with might by his <a href="../ebd/ebd349.htm#009">Spirit</a> in the inner man;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> That <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a> may dwell in your hearts by faith; that ye, being rooted and grounded in love,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> May be able to comprehend with all saints what is the breadth, and length, and depth, and height;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> And to know the love of <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a>, which passeth knowledge, that ye might be filled with all the <a href="../ebd/ebd139.htm#007">fulness</a> of God.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> Now unto him that is able to do exceeding abundantly above all that we ask or think, according to the power that worketh in us,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> Unto him be <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a> in the church by <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a> throughout all ages, world without end. <a href="../ebd/ebd020.htm#001">Amen</a>.<br>
 </p>
