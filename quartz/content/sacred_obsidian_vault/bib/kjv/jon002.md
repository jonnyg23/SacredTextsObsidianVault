 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Jonah Chapter 2</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Then <a href="../ebd/ebd210.htm#007">Jonah</a> prayed unto the LORD his God out of the fish's <a href="../ebd/ebd052.htm#000">belly</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> And said, I cried by reason of mine affliction unto the LORD, and he heard me; out of the <a href="../ebd/ebd052.htm#000">belly</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd173.htm#003">hell</a> cried I, and thou heardest my voice.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> For thou hadst cast me into the <a href="../ebd/ebd100.htm#008">deep</a>, in the midst of the seas; and the floods compassed me about: all thy billows and thy waves passed over me.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Then I said, I am cast out of thy sight; yet I will look again toward thy holy <a href="../ebd/ebd361.htm#004">temple</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> The waters compassed me about, even to the soul: the depth closed me round about, the weeds were wrapped about my head.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> I went down to the bottoms of the mountains; the earth with her bars was about me for ever: yet hast thou brought up my life from corruption, O LORD my God.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> When my soul fainted within me I remembered the LORD: and my <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#000">prayer</a> came in unto thee, into thine holy <a href="../ebd/ebd361.htm#004">temple</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> They that observe lying vanities forsake their own <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> But I will <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#002">sacrifice</a> unto thee with the voice of thanksgiving; I will pay that that I have vowed. <a href="../ebd/ebd320.htm#003">Salvation</a> is of the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And the LORD spake unto the <a href="../ebd/ebd134.htm#005">fish</a>, and it vomited out <a href="../ebd/ebd210.htm#007">Jonah</a> upon the dry land.<br>
 </p>
