 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Lamentations Chapter 5</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Remember, O LORD, what is come upon us: consider, and behold our reproach.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Our inheritance is turned to strangers, our houses to aliens.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> We are <a href="../ebd/ebd280.htm#008">orphans</a> and fatherless, our mothers are as <a href="../ebd/ebd381.htm#003">widows</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> We have drunken our water for money; our <a href="../ebd/ebd383.htm#003">wood</a> is sold unto us.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Our necks are under <a href="../ebd/ebd290.htm#007">persecution</a>: we labour, and have no rest.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> We have given the hand to the Egyptians, and to the Assyrians, to be satisfied with <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#006">bread</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Our fathers have sinned, and are not; and we have borne their iniquities.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> Servants have ruled over us: there is none that doth deliver us out of their hand.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> We gat our <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#006">bread</a> with the peril of our lives because of the <a href="../ebd/ebd354.htm#003">sword</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd381.htm#005">wilderness</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> Our skin was black like an <a href="../ebd/ebd281.htm#005">oven</a> because of the terrible <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#000">famine</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> They ravished the women in <a href="../ebd/ebd394.htm#002">Zion</a>, and the maids in the cities of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> Princes are hanged up by their hand: the faces of elders were not honoured.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> They took the young men to <a href="../ebd/ebd155.htm#006">grind</a>, and the children fell under the <a href="../ebd/ebd383.htm#003">wood</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> The elders have ceased from the <a href="../ebd/ebd143.htm#008">gate</a>, the young men from their musick.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> The joy of our heart is ceased; our <a href="../ebd/ebd097.htm#000">dance</a> is turned into mourning.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> The <a href="../ebd/ebd093.htm#001">crown</a> is fallen from our head: woe unto us, that we have sinned!<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> For this our heart is faint; for these things our eyes are dim.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> Because of the mountain of <a href="../ebd/ebd394.htm#002">Zion</a>, which is desolate, the foxes walk upon it.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> Thou, O LORD, remainest for ever; thy <a href="../ebd/ebd365.htm#001">throne</a> from <a href="../ebd/ebd145.htm#008">generation</a> to <a href="../ebd/ebd145.htm#008">generation</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> Wherefore dost thou forget us for ever, and forsake us so long time?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> Turn thou us unto thee, O LORD, and we shall be turned; renew our days as of old.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> But thou hast utterly rejected us; thou art very wroth against us.<br>
 </p>
