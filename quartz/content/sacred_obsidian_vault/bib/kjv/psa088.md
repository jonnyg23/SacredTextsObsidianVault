 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 88</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> O lord God of my <a href="../ebd/ebd320.htm#003">salvation</a>, I have cried day and night before thee:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Let my <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#000">prayer</a> come before thee: incline thine <a href="../ebd/ebd110.htm#004">ear</a> unto my cry;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> For my soul is full of troubles: and my life draweth nigh unto the <a href="../ebd/ebd154.htm#008">grave</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> I am counted with them that go down into the <a href="../ebd/ebd296.htm#007">pit</a>: I am as a man that hath no strength:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Free among the dead, like the slain that lie in the <a href="../ebd/ebd154.htm#008">grave</a>, whom thou rememberest no more: and they are cut off from thy hand.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Thou hast laid me in the lowest <a href="../ebd/ebd296.htm#007">pit</a>, in <a href="../ebd/ebd097.htm#008">darkness</a>, in the deeps.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Thy wrath lieth hard upon me, and thou hast afflicted me with all thy waves. <a href="../ebd/ebd326.htm#008">Selah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> Thou hast put away mine acquaintance far from me; thou hast made me an <a href="../ebd/ebd005.htm#003">abomination</a> unto them: I am shut up, and I cannot come forth.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> Mine eye mourneth by reason of affliction: LORD, I have called daily upon thee, I have stretched out my hands unto thee.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> Wilt thou shew wonders to the dead? shall the dead arise and praise thee? <a href="../ebd/ebd326.htm#008">Selah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> Shall thy lovingkindness be declared in the <a href="../ebd/ebd154.htm#008">grave</a>? or thy faithfulness in <a href="../ebd/ebd102.htm#004">destruction</a>?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> Shall thy wonders be known in the dark? and thy <a href="../ebd/ebd313.htm#004">righteousness</a> in the land of forgetfulness?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> But unto thee have I cried, O LORD; and in the morning shall my <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#000">prayer</a> prevent thee.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> LORD, why castest thou off my soul? why hidest thou thy face from me?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> I am afflicted and ready to die from my youth up: while I suffer thy terrors I am distracted.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> Thy fierce wrath goeth over me; thy terrors have cut me off.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> They came round about me daily like water; they compassed me about together.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> Lover and friend hast thou put far from me, and mine acquaintance into <a href="../ebd/ebd097.htm#008">darkness</a>.<br>
 </p>
