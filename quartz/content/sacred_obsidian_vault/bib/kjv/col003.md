 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Colossians Chapter 3</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> If ye then be risen with <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a>, seek those things which are above, where <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a> sitteth on the right hand of God.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Set your <a href="../ebd/ebd011.htm#004">affection</a> on things above, not on things on the earth.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> For ye are dead, and your life is hid with <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a> in God.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> When <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a>, who is our life, shall appear, then shall ye also appear with him in <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Mortify therefore your members which are upon the earth; <a href="../ebd/ebd137.htm#008">fornication</a>, uncleanness, inordinate <a href="../ebd/ebd011.htm#004">affection</a>, evil <a href="../ebd/ebd087.htm#008">concupiscence</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd092.htm#000">covetousness</a>, which is <a href="../ebd/ebd186.htm#007">idolatry</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> For which things' sake the wrath of God cometh on the children of disobedience:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> In the which ye also walked some time, when ye lived in them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> But now ye also put off all these; <a href="../ebd/ebd024.htm#002">anger</a>, wrath, malice, <a href="../ebd/ebd060.htm#005">blasphemy</a>, filthy communication out of your mouth.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> Lie not one to another, seeing that ye have put off the old man with his deeds;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And have put on the new man, which is renewed in knowledge after the image of him that created him:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> Where there is neither <a href="../ebd/ebd155.htm#004">Greek</a> nor <a href="../ebd/ebd206.htm#004">Jew</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd083.htm#007">circumcision</a> nor uncircumcision, <a href="../ebd/ebd044.htm#006">Barbarian</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd324.htm#006">Scythian</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd062.htm#001">bond</a> nor free: but <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a> is all, and in all.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> Put on therefore, as the elect of God, holy and beloved, <a href="../ebd/ebd063.htm#004">bowels</a> of mercies, kindness, humbleness of mind, <a href="../ebd/ebd246.htm#003">meekness</a>, longsuffering;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> Forbearing one another, and forgiving one another, if any man have a quarrel against any: even as <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a> forgave you, so also do ye.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> And above all these things put on <a href="../ebd/ebd077.htm#009">charity</a>, which is the <a href="../ebd/ebd062.htm#001">bond</a> of perfectness.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And let the peace of God rule in your hearts, to the which also ye are called in one body; and be ye thankful.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> Let the word of <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a> dwell in you richly in all wisdom; teaching and admonishing one another in <a href="../ebd/ebd301.htm#005">psalms</a> and hymns and spiritual <a href="../ebd/ebd348.htm#002">songs</a>, singing with <a href="../ebd/ebd154.htm#000">grace</a> in your hearts to the Lord.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> And whatsoever ye do in word or deed, do all in the name of the Lord <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a>, giving thanks to God and the Father by him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> Wives, submit yourselves unto your own husbands, as it is fit in the Lord.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> Husbands, love your wives, and be not <a href="../ebd/ebd059.htm#009">bitter</a> against them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> Children, obey your parents in all things: for this is well pleasing unto the Lord.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> Fathers, provoke not your children to <a href="../ebd/ebd024.htm#002">anger</a>, lest they be discouraged.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> Servants, obey in all things your masters according to the <a href="../ebd/ebd135.htm#007">flesh</a>; not with eyeservice, as menpleasers; but in singleness of heart, fearing God;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> And whatsoever ye do, do it heartily, as to the Lord, and not unto men;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> Knowing that of the Lord ye shall receive the reward of the inheritance: for ye serve the Lord <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> But he that doeth wrong shall receive for the wrong which he hath done: and there is no respect of persons.<br>
 </p>
