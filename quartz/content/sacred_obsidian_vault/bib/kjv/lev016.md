 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Leviticus Chapter 16</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> And the LORD spake unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> after the death of the two sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a>, when they offered before the LORD, and died;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> And the LORD said unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, Speak unto <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> thy brother, that he come not at all times into the holy place within the vail before the <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> seat, which is upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#000">ark</a>; that he die not: for I will appear in the <a href="../ebd/ebd085.htm#001">cloud</a> upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> seat.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Thus shall <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> come into the holy place: with a young <a href="../ebd/ebd066.htm#003">bullock</a> for a <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, and a <a href="../ebd/ebd306.htm#003">ram</a> for a burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> He shall put on the holy <a href="../ebd/ebd229.htm#008">linen</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd085.htm#004">coat</a>, and he shall have the <a href="../ebd/ebd229.htm#008">linen</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#008">breeches</a> upon his <a href="../ebd/ebd135.htm#007">flesh</a>, and shall be girded with a <a href="../ebd/ebd229.htm#008">linen</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd149.htm#005">girdle</a>, and with the <a href="../ebd/ebd229.htm#008">linen</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd257.htm#006">mitre</a> shall he be attired: these are holy garments; therefore shall he wash his <a href="../ebd/ebd135.htm#007">flesh</a> in water, and so put them on.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And he shall take of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> two kids of the goats for a <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, and one <a href="../ebd/ebd306.htm#003">ram</a> for a burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> shall offer his <a href="../ebd/ebd066.htm#003">bullock</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, which is for himself, and make an <a href="../ebd/ebd036.htm#004">atonement</a> for himself, and for his house.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And he shall take the two goats, and present them before the LORD at the door of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> shall cast lots upon the two goats; one lot for the LORD, and the other lot for the <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#004">scapegoat</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> shall bring the <a href="../ebd/ebd151.htm#001">goat</a> upon which the LORD's lot fell, and offer him for a <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> But the <a href="../ebd/ebd151.htm#001">goat</a>, on which the lot fell to be the <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#004">scapegoat</a>, shall be presented alive before the LORD, to make an <a href="../ebd/ebd036.htm#004">atonement</a> with him, and to let him go for a <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#004">scapegoat</a> into the <a href="../ebd/ebd381.htm#005">wilderness</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> shall bring the <a href="../ebd/ebd066.htm#003">bullock</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, which is for himself, and shall make an <a href="../ebd/ebd036.htm#004">atonement</a> for himself, and for his house, and shall kill the <a href="../ebd/ebd066.htm#003">bullock</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> which is for himself:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And he shall take a <a href="../ebd/ebd075.htm#002">censer</a> full of burning coals of fire from off the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> before the LORD, and his hands full of sweet <a href="../ebd/ebd188.htm#002">incense</a> beaten small, and bring it within the vail:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> And he shall put the <a href="../ebd/ebd188.htm#002">incense</a> upon the fire before the LORD, that the <a href="../ebd/ebd085.htm#001">cloud</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd188.htm#002">incense</a> may cover the <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> seat that is upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd362.htm#008">testimony</a>, that he die not:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> And he shall take of the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd066.htm#003">bullock</a>, and sprinkle it with his finger upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> seat eastward; and before the <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> seat shall he sprinkle of the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> with his finger <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> times.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> Then shall he kill the <a href="../ebd/ebd151.htm#001">goat</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, that is for the people, and bring his <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> within the vail, and do with that <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> as he did with the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd066.htm#003">bullock</a>, and sprinkle it upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> seat, and before the <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> seat:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> And he shall make an <a href="../ebd/ebd036.htm#004">atonement</a> for the holy place, because of the uncleanness of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, and because of their transgressions in all their sins: and so shall he do for the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>, that remaineth among them in the midst of their uncleanness.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> And there shall be no man in the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> when he goeth in to make an <a href="../ebd/ebd036.htm#004">atonement</a> in the holy place, until he come out, and have made an <a href="../ebd/ebd036.htm#004">atonement</a> for himself, and for his household, and for all the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> And he shall go out unto the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> that is before the LORD, and make an <a href="../ebd/ebd036.htm#004">atonement</a> for it; and shall take of the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd066.htm#003">bullock</a>, and of the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd151.htm#001">goat</a>, and put it upon the horns of the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> round about.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> And he shall sprinkle of the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> upon it with his finger <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> times, and cleanse it, and <a href="../ebd/ebd160.htm#001">hallow</a> it from the uncleanness of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> And when he hath made an end of reconciling the holy place, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>, he shall bring the live <a href="../ebd/ebd151.htm#001">goat</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> shall lay both his hands upon the head of the live <a href="../ebd/ebd151.htm#001">goat</a>, and confess over him all the iniquities of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, and all their transgressions in all their sins, putting them upon the head of the <a href="../ebd/ebd151.htm#001">goat</a>, and shall send him away by the hand of a fit man into the <a href="../ebd/ebd381.htm#005">wilderness</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd151.htm#001">goat</a> shall bear upon him all their iniquities unto a land not inhabited: and he shall let go the <a href="../ebd/ebd151.htm#001">goat</a> in the <a href="../ebd/ebd381.htm#005">wilderness</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> shall come into the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>, and shall put off the <a href="../ebd/ebd229.htm#008">linen</a> garments, which he put on when he went into the holy place, and shall leave them there:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> And he shall wash his <a href="../ebd/ebd135.htm#007">flesh</a> with water in the holy place, and put on his garments, and come forth, and offer his burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, and the burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> of the people, and make an <a href="../ebd/ebd036.htm#004">atonement</a> for himself, and for the people.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#005">fat</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> shall he burn upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> And he that let go the <a href="../ebd/ebd151.htm#001">goat</a> for the <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#004">scapegoat</a> shall wash his clothes, and bathe his <a href="../ebd/ebd135.htm#007">flesh</a> in water, and afterward come into the <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#002">camp</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd066.htm#003">bullock</a> for the <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd151.htm#001">goat</a> for the <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, whose <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> was brought in to make <a href="../ebd/ebd036.htm#004">atonement</a> in the holy place, shall one carry forth without the <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#002">camp</a>; and they shall burn in the fire their skins, and their <a href="../ebd/ebd135.htm#007">flesh</a>, and their <a href="../ebd/ebd109.htm#003">dung</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="028">28</a> And he that burneth them shall wash his clothes, and bathe his <a href="../ebd/ebd135.htm#007">flesh</a> in water, and afterward he shall come into the <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#002">camp</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="029">29</a> And this shall be a statute for ever unto you: that in the seventh month, on the tenth day of the month, ye shall afflict your souls, and do no work at all, whether it be one of your own country, or a <a href="../ebd/ebd351.htm#009">stranger</a> that sojourneth among you:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="030">30</a> For on that day shall the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> make an <a href="../ebd/ebd036.htm#004">atonement</a> for you, to cleanse you, that ye may be <a href="../ebd/ebd084.htm#005">clean</a> from all your sins before the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="031">31</a> It shall be a <a href="../ebd/ebd317.htm#003">sabbath</a> of rest unto you, and ye shall afflict your souls, by a statute for ever.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="032">32</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a>, whom he shall <a href="../ebd/ebd024.htm#008">anoint</a>, and whom he shall consecrate to <a href="../ebd/ebd255.htm#007">minister</a> in the priest's office in his father's stead, shall make the <a href="../ebd/ebd036.htm#004">atonement</a>, and shall put on the <a href="../ebd/ebd229.htm#008">linen</a> clothes, even the holy garments:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="033">33</a> And he shall make an <a href="../ebd/ebd036.htm#004">atonement</a> for the holy <a href="../ebd/ebd321.htm#005">sanctuary</a>, and he shall make an <a href="../ebd/ebd036.htm#004">atonement</a> for the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>, and for the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>, and he shall make an <a href="../ebd/ebd036.htm#004">atonement</a> for the priests, and for all the people of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="034">34</a> And this shall be an <a href="../ebd/ebd127.htm#007">everlasting</a> statute unto you, to make an <a href="../ebd/ebd036.htm#004">atonement</a> for the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> for all their sins once a year. And he did as the LORD commanded <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>.<br>
 </p>
