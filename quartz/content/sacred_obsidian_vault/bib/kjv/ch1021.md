 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: 1 Chronicles Chapter 21</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#000">Satan</a> stood up against <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, and provoked <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> to number <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> said to <a href="../ebd/ebd207.htm#006">Joab</a> and to the rulers of the people, Go, number <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> from <a href="../ebd/ebd050.htm#008">Beersheba</a> even to <a href="../ebd/ebd096.htm#009">Dan</a>; and bring the number of them to me, that I may know it.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd207.htm#006">Joab</a> answered, The LORD make his people an hundred times so many more as they be: but, my lord the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a>, are they not all my lord's servants?  why then doth my lord require this thing? why will he be a cause of trespass to <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Nevertheless the king's word prevailed against <a href="../ebd/ebd207.htm#006">Joab</a>. Wherefore <a href="../ebd/ebd207.htm#006">Joab</a> departed, and went throughout all <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, and came to <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd207.htm#006">Joab</a> gave the sum of the number of the people unto <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a>. And all they of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> were a thousand thousand and an hundred thousand men that drew <a href="../ebd/ebd354.htm#003">sword</a>: and <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a> was four hundred threescore and ten thousand men that drew <a href="../ebd/ebd354.htm#003">sword</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> But <a href="../ebd/ebd227.htm#006">Levi</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd052.htm#008">Benjamin</a> counted he not among them: for the king's word was abominable to <a href="../ebd/ebd207.htm#006">Joab</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And God was displeased with this thing; therefore he smote <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> said unto God, I have sinned greatly, because I have done this thing: but now, I beseech thee, do away the iniquity of thy servant; for I have done very foolishly.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And the LORD spake unto <a href="../ebd/ebd140.htm#007">Gad</a>, David's <a href="../ebd/ebd326.htm#000">seer</a>, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> Go and tell <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a>, saying, Thus saith the LORD, I offer thee three things: choose thee one of them, that I may do it unto thee.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> So <a href="../ebd/ebd140.htm#007">Gad</a> came to <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a>, and said unto him, Thus saith the LORD, Choose thee<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> Either three years' <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#000">famine</a>; or three months to be destroyed before thy foes, while that the <a href="../ebd/ebd354.htm#003">sword</a> of thine enemies overtaketh thee; or else three days the <a href="../ebd/ebd354.htm#003">sword</a> of the LORD, even the pestilence, in the land, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd024.htm#001">angel</a> of the LORD destroying throughout all the coasts of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>. Now therefore advise thyself what word I shall bring again to him that sent me.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> said unto <a href="../ebd/ebd140.htm#007">Gad</a>, I am in a great strait: let me fall now into the hand of the LORD; for very great are his mercies: but let me not fall into the hand of man.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> So the LORD sent pestilence upon <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>: and there fell of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> seventy thousand men.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And God sent an <a href="../ebd/ebd024.htm#001">angel</a> unto <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a> to destroy it: and as he was destroying, the LORD beheld, and he repented him of the evil, and said to the <a href="../ebd/ebd024.htm#001">angel</a> that destroyed, It is enough, stay now thine hand. And the <a href="../ebd/ebd024.htm#001">angel</a> of the LORD stood by the threshingfloor of <a href="../ebd/ebd280.htm#006">Ornan</a> the Jebusite.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> lifted up his eyes, and saw the <a href="../ebd/ebd024.htm#001">angel</a> of the LORD stand between the earth and the <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#006">heaven</a>, having a drawn <a href="../ebd/ebd354.htm#003">sword</a> in his hand stretched out over <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>. Then <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> and the elders of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, who were clothed in <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#001">sackcloth</a>, fell upon their faces.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> said unto God, Is it not I that commanded the people to be numbered? even I it is that have sinned and done evil indeed; but as for these <a href="../ebd/ebd333.htm#005">sheep</a>, what have they done? let thine hand, I pray thee, O LORD my God, be on me, and on my father's house; but not on thy people, that they should be plagued.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> Then the <a href="../ebd/ebd024.htm#001">angel</a> of the LORD commanded <a href="../ebd/ebd140.htm#007">Gad</a> to say to <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a>, that <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> should go up, and set up an <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> unto the LORD in the threshingfloor of <a href="../ebd/ebd280.htm#006">Ornan</a> the Jebusite.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> went up at the saying of <a href="../ebd/ebd140.htm#007">Gad</a>, which he spake in the name of the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd280.htm#006">Ornan</a> turned back, and saw the <a href="../ebd/ebd024.htm#001">angel</a>; and his four sons with him hid themselves. Now <a href="../ebd/ebd280.htm#006">Ornan</a> was <a href="../ebd/ebd364.htm#009">threshing</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd381.htm#000">wheat</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> And as <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> came to <a href="../ebd/ebd280.htm#006">Ornan</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd280.htm#006">Ornan</a> looked and saw <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a>, and went out of the threshingfloor, and bowed himself to <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> with his face to the ground.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> Then <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> said to <a href="../ebd/ebd280.htm#006">Ornan</a>, Grant me the place of this threshingfloor, that I may build an <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> therein unto the LORD: thou shalt grant it me for the full price: that the <a href="../ebd/ebd297.htm#001">plague</a> may be stayed from the people.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd280.htm#006">Ornan</a> said unto <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a>, Take it to thee, and let my lord the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> do that which is good in his eyes: lo, I give thee the oxen also for burnt offerings, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd364.htm#009">threshing</a> instruments for <a href="../ebd/ebd383.htm#003">wood</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd381.htm#000">wheat</a> for the meat <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>; I give it all.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> said to <a href="../ebd/ebd280.htm#006">Ornan</a>, Nay; but I will verily buy it for the full price: for I will not take that which is thine for the LORD, nor offer burnt offerings without cost.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> So <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> gave to <a href="../ebd/ebd280.htm#006">Ornan</a> for the place six hundred shekels of <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a> by weight.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> built there an <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> unto the LORD, and offered burnt offerings and peace offerings, and called upon the LORD; and he answered him from <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#006">heaven</a> by fire upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> of burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> And the LORD commanded the <a href="../ebd/ebd024.htm#001">angel</a>; and he put up his <a href="../ebd/ebd354.htm#003">sword</a> again into the sheath thereof.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="028">28</a> At that time when <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> saw that the LORD had answered him in the threshingfloor of <a href="../ebd/ebd280.htm#006">Ornan</a> the Jebusite, then he sacrificed there.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="029">29</a> For the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the LORD, which <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> made in the <a href="../ebd/ebd381.htm#005">wilderness</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> of the burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, were at that season in the high place at <a href="../ebd/ebd148.htm#002">Gibeon</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="030">30</a> But <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> could not go before it to enquire of God: for he was afraid because of the <a href="../ebd/ebd354.htm#003">sword</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd024.htm#001">angel</a> of the LORD.<br>
 </p>
