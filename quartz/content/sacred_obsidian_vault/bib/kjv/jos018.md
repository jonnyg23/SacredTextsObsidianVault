 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Joshua Chapter 18</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> And the whole <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> assembled together at <a href="../ebd/ebd337.htm#008">Shiloh</a>, and set up the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> there. And the land was subdued before them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> And there remained among the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> tribes, which had not yet received their inheritance.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#006">Joshua</a> said unto the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, How long are ye slack to go to possess the land, which the LORD God of your fathers hath given you?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Give out from among you three men for each <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a>: and I will send them, and they shall rise, and go through the land, and describe it according to the inheritance of them; and they shall come again to me.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And they shall divide it into <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> parts: <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a> shall abide in their coast on the south, and the house of <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#005">Joseph</a> shall abide in their coasts on the north.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Ye shall therefore describe the land into <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> parts, and bring the description hither to me, that I may cast lots for you here before the LORD our God.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> But the Levites have no part among you; for the priesthood of the LORD is their inheritance: and <a href="../ebd/ebd140.htm#007">Gad</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd311.htm#009">Reuben</a>, and half the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd240.htm#002">Manasseh</a>, have received their inheritance beyond <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#004">Jordan</a> on the <a href="../ebd/ebd111.htm#000">east</a>, which <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> the servant of the LORD gave them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And the men arose, and went away: and <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#006">Joshua</a> charged them that went to describe the land, saying, Go and walk through the land, and describe it, and come again to me, that I may here cast lots for you before the LORD in <a href="../ebd/ebd337.htm#008">Shiloh</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And the men went and passed through the land, and described it by cities into <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> parts in a book, and came again to <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#006">Joshua</a> to the <a href="../ebd/ebd183.htm#007">host</a> at <a href="../ebd/ebd337.htm#008">Shiloh</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#006">Joshua</a> cast lots for them in <a href="../ebd/ebd337.htm#008">Shiloh</a> before the LORD: and there <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#006">Joshua</a> divided the land unto the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> according to their divisions.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And the lot of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd052.htm#008">Benjamin</a> came up according to their families: and the coast of their lot came forth between the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a> and the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#005">Joseph</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And their border on the north side was from <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#004">Jordan</a>; and the border went up to the side of <a href="../ebd/ebd203.htm#008">Jericho</a> on the north side, and went up through the mountains <a href="../ebd/ebd380.htm#008">westward</a>; and the goings out thereof were at the <a href="../ebd/ebd381.htm#005">wilderness</a> of Bethaven.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> And the border went over from thence toward <a href="../ebd/ebd233.htm#006">Luz</a>, to the side of <a href="../ebd/ebd233.htm#006">Luz</a>, which is <a href="../ebd/ebd055.htm#006">Bethel</a>, southward; and the border descended to Atarothadar, near the hill that lieth on the south side of the nether Bethhoron.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> And the border was drawn thence, and compassed the corner of the sea southward, from the hill that lieth before Bethhoron southward; and the goings out thereof were at Kirjathbaal, which is Kirjathjearim, a city of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>: this was the west quarter.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And the south quarter was from the end of Kirjathjearim, and the border went out on the west, and went out to the well of waters of <a href="../ebd/ebd270.htm#006">Nephtoah</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> And the border came down to the end of the mountain that lieth before the <a href="../ebd/ebd376.htm#008">valley</a> of the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd179.htm#002">Hinnom</a>, and which is in the <a href="../ebd/ebd376.htm#008">valley</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd147.htm#006">giants</a> on the north, and descended to the <a href="../ebd/ebd376.htm#008">valley</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd179.htm#002">Hinnom</a>, to the side of Jebusi on the south, and descended to Enrogel,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> And was drawn from the north, and went forth to Enshemesh, and went forth toward <a href="../ebd/ebd145.htm#006">Geliloth</a>, which is over against the going up of <a href="../ebd/ebd011.htm#000">Adummim</a>, and descended to the <a href="../ebd/ebd351.htm#005">stone</a> of Bohan the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd311.htm#009">Reuben</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> And passed along toward the side over against <a href="../ebd/ebd027.htm#008">Arabah</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd275.htm#002">northward</a>, and went down unto <a href="../ebd/ebd027.htm#008">Arabah</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> And the border passed along to the side of Bethhoglah <a href="../ebd/ebd275.htm#002">northward</a>: and the outgoings of the border were at the north <a href="../ebd/ebd048.htm#001">bay</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd319.htm#008">salt</a> sea at the south end of <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#004">Jordan</a>: this was the south coast.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#004">Jordan</a> was the border of it on the <a href="../ebd/ebd111.htm#000">east</a> side. This was the inheritance of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd052.htm#008">Benjamin</a>, by the coasts thereof round about, according to their families.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> Now the cities of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd052.htm#008">Benjamin</a> according to their families were <a href="../ebd/ebd203.htm#008">Jericho</a>, and Bethhoglah, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd376.htm#008">valley</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd218.htm#004">Keziz</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> And Betharabah, and <a href="../ebd/ebd390.htm#004">Zemaraim</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd055.htm#006">Bethel</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd037.htm#001">Avim</a>, and Pharah, and <a href="../ebd/ebd279.htm#009">Ophrah</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> And Chepharhaammonai, and <a href="../ebd/ebd279.htm#008">Ophni</a>, and Gaba; twelve cities with their <a href="../ebd/ebd377.htm#003">villages</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd148.htm#002">Gibeon</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd306.htm#005">Ramah</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd050.htm#006">Beeroth</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> And Mizpeh, and <a href="../ebd/ebd079.htm#000">Chephirah</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd261.htm#008">Mozah</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd310.htm#002">Rekem</a>, and Irpeel, and Taralah,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="028">28</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd390.htm#000">Zelah</a>, Eleph, and Jebusi, which is <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>, Gibeath, and <a href="../ebd/ebd220.htm#000">Kirjath</a>; fourteen cities with their <a href="../ebd/ebd377.htm#003">villages</a>. This is the inheritance of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd052.htm#008">Benjamin</a> according to their families.<br>
 </p>
