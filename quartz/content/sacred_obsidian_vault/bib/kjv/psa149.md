 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 149</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Praise ye the LORD. Sing unto the LORD a new song, and his praise in the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> of saints.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Let <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> rejoice in him that made him: let the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd394.htm#002">Zion</a> be joyful in their <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">King</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Let them praise his name in the <a href="../ebd/ebd097.htm#000">dance</a>: let them sing praises unto him with the <a href="../ebd/ebd366.htm#004">timbrel</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd165.htm#001">harp</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> For the LORD taketh pleasure in his people: he will beautify the meek with <a href="../ebd/ebd320.htm#003">salvation</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Let the saints be joyful in <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a>: let them sing aloud upon their beds.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Let the high praises of God be in their mouth, and a two-edged <a href="../ebd/ebd354.htm#003">sword</a> in their hand;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> To execute vengeance upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#005">heathen</a>, and punishments upon the people;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> To bind their kings with chains, and their nobles with fetters of <a href="../ebd/ebd189.htm#004">iron</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> To execute upon them the judgment written: this honour have all his saints. Praise ye the LORD.<br>
 </p>
