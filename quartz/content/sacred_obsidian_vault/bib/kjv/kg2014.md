 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: 4 Kings (2 Kings) Chapter 14</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> In the second year of <a href="../ebd/ebd208.htm#000">Joash</a> son of <a href="../ebd/ebd200.htm#006">Jehoahaz</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> reigned <a href="../ebd/ebd019.htm#007">Amaziah</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd208.htm#000">Joash</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> He was twenty and five years old when he began to reign, and reigned twenty and nine years in <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>. And his mother's name was <a href="../ebd/ebd200.htm#005">Jehoaddan</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And he did that which was right in the sight of the LORD, yet not like <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> his father: he did according to all things as <a href="../ebd/ebd208.htm#000">Joash</a> his father did.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Howbeit the high places were not taken away: as yet the people did <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#002">sacrifice</a> and burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd188.htm#002">incense</a> on the high places.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And it came to pass, as soon as the kingdom was confirmed in his hand, that he slew his servants which had slain the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> his father.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> But the children of the murderers he slew not: according unto that which is written in the book of the law of <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, wherein the LORD commanded, saying, The fathers shall not be put to death for the children, nor the children be put to death for the fathers; but every man shall be put to death for his own <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> He slew of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#001">Edom</a> in the <a href="../ebd/ebd376.htm#008">valley</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd319.htm#008">salt</a> ten thousand, and took <a href="../ebd/ebd326.htm#008">Selah</a> by war, and called the name of it <a href="../ebd/ebd210.htm#005">Joktheel</a> unto this day.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> Then <a href="../ebd/ebd019.htm#007">Amaziah</a> sent messengers to <a href="../ebd/ebd200.htm#007">Jehoash</a>, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd200.htm#006">Jehoahaz</a> son of <a href="../ebd/ebd202.htm#007">Jehu</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, saying, Come, let us look one another in the face.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd200.htm#007">Jehoash</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> sent to <a href="../ebd/ebd019.htm#007">Amaziah</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>, saying, The <a href="../ebd/ebd364.htm#004">thistle</a> that was in <a href="../ebd/ebd226.htm#001">Lebanon</a> sent to the <a href="../ebd/ebd074.htm#007">cedar</a> that was in <a href="../ebd/ebd226.htm#001">Lebanon</a>, saying, Give thy daughter to my son to wife: and there passed by a wild <a href="../ebd/ebd049.htm#001">beast</a> that was in <a href="../ebd/ebd226.htm#001">Lebanon</a>, and trode down the <a href="../ebd/ebd364.htm#004">thistle</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> Thou hast indeed smitten <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#001">Edom</a>, and thine heart hath lifted thee up: <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a> of this, and tarry at home: for why shouldest thou meddle to thy hurt, that thou shouldest fall, even thou, and <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a> with thee?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> But <a href="../ebd/ebd019.htm#007">Amaziah</a> would not hear. Therefore <a href="../ebd/ebd200.htm#007">Jehoash</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> went up; and he and <a href="../ebd/ebd019.htm#007">Amaziah</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a> looked one another in the face at Bethshemesh, which belongeth to <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a> was put to the worse before <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>; and they fled every man to their tents.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd200.htm#007">Jehoash</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> took <a href="../ebd/ebd019.htm#007">Amaziah</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd200.htm#007">Jehoash</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd013.htm#006">Ahaziah</a>, at Bethshemesh, and came to <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>, and brake down the wall of <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a> from the <a href="../ebd/ebd143.htm#008">gate</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd123.htm#000">Ephraim</a> unto the corner <a href="../ebd/ebd143.htm#008">gate</a>, four hundred cubits.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> And he took all the <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#000">silver</a>, and all the vessels that were found in the house of the LORD, and in the treasures of the king's house, and hostages, and returned to <a href="../ebd/ebd320.htm#004">Samaria</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> Now the rest of the acts of <a href="../ebd/ebd200.htm#007">Jehoash</a> which he did, and his might, and how he fought with <a href="../ebd/ebd019.htm#007">Amaziah</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>, are they not written in the book of the <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#003">chronicles</a> of the kings of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd200.htm#007">Jehoash</a> slept with his fathers, and was buried in <a href="../ebd/ebd320.htm#004">Samaria</a> with the kings of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>; and <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#000">Jeroboam</a> his son reigned in his stead.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd019.htm#007">Amaziah</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd208.htm#000">Joash</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a> lived after the death of <a href="../ebd/ebd200.htm#007">Jehoash</a> son of <a href="../ebd/ebd200.htm#006">Jehoahaz</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> fifteen years.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> And the rest of the acts of <a href="../ebd/ebd019.htm#007">Amaziah</a>, are they not written in the book of the <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#003">chronicles</a> of the kings of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> Now they made a conspiracy against him in <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>: and he fled to <a href="../ebd/ebd222.htm#009">Lachish</a>; but they sent after him to <a href="../ebd/ebd222.htm#009">Lachish</a>, and slew him there.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> And they brought him on horses: and he was buried at <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a> with his fathers in the city of <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> And all the people of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a> took <a href="../ebd/ebd037.htm#005">Azariah</a>, which was sixteen years old, and made him <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> instead of his father <a href="../ebd/ebd019.htm#007">Amaziah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> He built <a href="../ebd/ebd114.htm#005">Elath</a>, and restored it to <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>, after that the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> slept with his fathers.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> In the fifteenth year of <a href="../ebd/ebd019.htm#007">Amaziah</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd208.htm#000">Joash</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#000">Jeroboam</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd208.htm#000">Joash</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> began to reign in <a href="../ebd/ebd320.htm#004">Samaria</a>, and reigned forty and one years.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> And he did that which was evil in the sight of the LORD: he departed not from all the sins of <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#000">Jeroboam</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd268.htm#003">Nebat</a>, who made <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> to <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> He restored the coast of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> from the entering of <a href="../ebd/ebd160.htm#005">Hamath</a> unto the sea of the <a href="../ebd/ebd297.htm#002">plain</a>, according to the word of the LORD God of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, which he spake by the hand of his servant <a href="../ebd/ebd210.htm#007">Jonah</a>, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd020.htm#003">Amittai</a>, the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#008">prophet</a>, which was of Gathhepher.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> For the LORD saw the affliction of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, that it was very <a href="../ebd/ebd059.htm#009">bitter</a>: for there was not any shut up, nor any left, nor any helper for <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> And the LORD said not that he would <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#002">blot</a> out the name of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> from under <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#006">heaven</a>: but he saved them by the hand of <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#000">Jeroboam</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd208.htm#000">Joash</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="028">28</a> Now the rest of the acts of <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#000">Jeroboam</a>, and all that he did, and his might, how he warred, and how he recovered <a href="../ebd/ebd096.htm#007">Damascus</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd160.htm#005">Hamath</a>, which belonged to <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>, for <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, are they not written in the book of the <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#003">chronicles</a> of the kings of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="029">29</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#000">Jeroboam</a> slept with his fathers, even with the kings of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>; and <a href="../ebd/ebd386.htm#005">Zachariah</a> his son reigned in his stead.<br>
 </p>
