 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Deuteronomy Chapter 16</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Observe the month of <a href="../ebd/ebd002.htm#006">Abib</a>, and keep the <a href="../ebd/ebd286.htm#005">passover</a> unto the LORD thy God: for in the month of <a href="../ebd/ebd002.htm#006">Abib</a> the LORD thy God brought thee forth out of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a> by night.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Thou shalt therefore <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#002">sacrifice</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd286.htm#005">passover</a> unto the LORD thy God, of the flock and the <a href="../ebd/ebd174.htm#009">herd</a>, in the place which the LORD shall choose to place his name there.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Thou shalt eat no leavened <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#006">bread</a> with it; <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> days shalt thou eat unleavened <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#006">bread</a> therewith, even the <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#006">bread</a> of affliction; for thou camest forth out of the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a> in haste: that thou mayest remember the day when thou camest forth out of the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a> all the days of thy life.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And there shall be no leavened <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#006">bread</a> seen with thee in all thy coast <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> days; neither shall there any thing of the <a href="../ebd/ebd135.htm#007">flesh</a>, which thou sacrificedst the first day at even, remain all night until the morning.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Thou mayest not <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#002">sacrifice</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd286.htm#005">passover</a> within any of thy gates, which the LORD thy God giveth thee:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> But at the place which the LORD thy God shall choose to place his name in, there thou shalt <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#002">sacrifice</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd286.htm#005">passover</a> at even, at the going down of the <a href="../ebd/ebd353.htm#001">sun</a>, at the season that thou camest forth out of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And thou shalt roast and eat it in the place which the LORD thy God shall choose: and thou shalt turn in the morning, and go unto thy tents.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> Six days thou shalt eat unleavened <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#006">bread</a>: and on the seventh day shall be a solemn assembly to the LORD thy God: thou shalt do no work therein.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">Seven</a> weeks shalt thou number unto thee: begin to number the <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> weeks from such time as thou beginnest to put the <a href="../ebd/ebd342.htm#008">sickle</a> to the <a href="../ebd/ebd090.htm#006">corn</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And thou shalt keep the <a href="../ebd/ebd132.htm#000">feast</a> of weeks unto the LORD thy God with a <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#006">tribute</a> of a freewill <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> of thine hand, which thou shalt give unto the LORD thy God, according as the LORD thy God hath blessed thee:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And thou shalt rejoice before the LORD thy God, thou, and thy son, and thy daughter, and thy manservant, and thy maidservant, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd227.htm#009">Levite</a> that is within thy gates, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd351.htm#009">stranger</a>, and the fatherless, and the widow, that are among you, in the place which the LORD thy God hath <a href="../ebd/ebd081.htm#008">chosen</a> to place his name there.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And thou shalt remember that thou wast a bondman in <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a>: and thou shalt observe and do these statutes.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> Thou shalt observe the <a href="../ebd/ebd132.htm#000">feast</a> of tabernacles <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> days, after that thou hast gathered in thy <a href="../ebd/ebd090.htm#006">corn</a> and thy <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#000">wine</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> And thou shalt rejoice in thy <a href="../ebd/ebd132.htm#000">feast</a>, thou, and thy son, and thy daughter, and thy manservant, and thy maidservant, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd227.htm#009">Levite</a>, the <a href="../ebd/ebd351.htm#009">stranger</a>, and the fatherless, and the widow, that are within thy gates.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">Seven</a> days shalt thou keep a solemn <a href="../ebd/ebd132.htm#000">feast</a> unto the LORD thy God in the place which the LORD shall choose: because the LORD thy God shall <a href="../ebd/ebd060.htm#008">bless</a> thee in all thine increase, and in all the works of thine hands, therefore thou shalt surely rejoice.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> Three times in a year shall all thy males appear before the LORD thy God in the place which he shall choose; in the <a href="../ebd/ebd132.htm#000">feast</a> of unleavened <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#006">bread</a>, and in the <a href="../ebd/ebd132.htm#000">feast</a> of weeks, and in the <a href="../ebd/ebd132.htm#000">feast</a> of tabernacles: and they shall not appear before the LORD empty:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> Every man shall give as he is able, according to the blessing of the LORD thy God which he hath given thee.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> Judges and officers shalt thou make thee in all thy gates, which the LORD thy God giveth thee, throughout thy tribes: and they shall <a href="../ebd/ebd213.htm#005">judge</a> the people with just judgment.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> Thou shalt not wrest judgment; thou shalt not respect persons, neither take a <a href="../ebd/ebd148.htm#005">gift</a>: for a <a href="../ebd/ebd148.htm#005">gift</a> doth <a href="../ebd/ebd060.htm#009">blind</a> the eyes of the <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#004">wise</a>, and pervert the words of the righteous.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> That which is altogether just shalt thou follow, that thou mayest live, and inherit the land which the LORD thy God giveth thee.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> Thou shalt not plant thee a <a href="../ebd/ebd155.htm#008">grove</a> of any trees near unto the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> of the LORD thy God, which thou shalt make thee.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> Neither shalt thou set thee up any image; which the LORD thy God hateth.<br>
 </p>
