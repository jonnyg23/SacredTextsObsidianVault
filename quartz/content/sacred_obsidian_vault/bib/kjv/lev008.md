 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Leviticus Chapter 8</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> And the LORD spake unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Take <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> and his sons with him, and the garments, and the anointing oil, and a <a href="../ebd/ebd066.htm#003">bullock</a> for the <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, and two rams, and a <a href="../ebd/ebd046.htm#009">basket</a> of unleavened <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#006">bread</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And gather thou all the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> together unto the door of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> did as the LORD commanded him; and the assembly was gathered together unto the door of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> said unto the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>, This is the thing which the LORD commanded to be done.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> brought <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> and his sons, and washed them with water.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And he put upon him the <a href="../ebd/ebd085.htm#004">coat</a>, and girded him with the <a href="../ebd/ebd149.htm#005">girdle</a>, and clothed him with the robe, and put the <a href="../ebd/ebd122.htm#008">ephod</a> upon him, and he girded him with the curious <a href="../ebd/ebd149.htm#005">girdle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd122.htm#008">ephod</a>, and bound it unto him therewith.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And he put the <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#007">breastplate</a> upon him: also he put in the <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#007">breastplate</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd375.htm#005">Urim</a> and the <a href="../ebd/ebd365.htm#002">Thummim</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And he put the <a href="../ebd/ebd257.htm#006">mitre</a> upon his head; also upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd257.htm#006">mitre</a>, even upon his forefront, did he put the golden plate, the holy <a href="../ebd/ebd093.htm#001">crown</a>; as the LORD commanded <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> took the anointing oil, and anointed the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> and all that was therein, and sanctified them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And he sprinkled thereof upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> times, and anointed the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> and all his vessels, both the <a href="../ebd/ebd224.htm#009">laver</a> and his foot, to sanctify them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And he poured of the anointing oil upon Aaron's head, and anointed him, to sanctify him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> brought Aaron's sons, and put coats upon them, and girded them with girdles, and put bonnets upon them; as the LORD commanded <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> And he brought the <a href="../ebd/ebd066.htm#003">bullock</a> for the <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>: and <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> and his sons laid their hands upon the head of the <a href="../ebd/ebd066.htm#003">bullock</a> for the <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And he slew it; and <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> took the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a>, and put it upon the horns of the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> round about with his finger, and purified the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>, and poured the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> at the bottom of the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>, and sanctified it, to make <a href="../ebd/ebd308.htm#004">reconciliation</a> upon it.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> And he took all the <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#005">fat</a> that was upon the inwards, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd074.htm#003">caul</a> above the <a href="../ebd/ebd230.htm#004">liver</a>, and the two kidneys, and their <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#005">fat</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> burned it upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> But the <a href="../ebd/ebd066.htm#003">bullock</a>, and his hide, his <a href="../ebd/ebd135.htm#007">flesh</a>, and his <a href="../ebd/ebd109.htm#003">dung</a>, he burnt with fire without the <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#002">camp</a>; as the LORD commanded <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> And he brought the <a href="../ebd/ebd306.htm#003">ram</a> for the burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>: and <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> and his sons laid their hands upon the head of the <a href="../ebd/ebd306.htm#003">ram</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> And he killed it; and <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> sprinkled the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> round about.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> And he cut the <a href="../ebd/ebd306.htm#003">ram</a> into <a href="../ebd/ebd295.htm#002">pieces</a>; and <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> burnt the head, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd295.htm#002">pieces</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#005">fat</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> And he washed the inwards and the legs in water; and <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> burnt the whole <a href="../ebd/ebd306.htm#003">ram</a> upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>: it was a burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#002">sacrifice</a> for a sweet savour, and an <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> made by fire unto the LORD; as the LORD commanded <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> And he brought the other <a href="../ebd/ebd306.htm#003">ram</a>, the <a href="../ebd/ebd306.htm#003">ram</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#007">consecration</a>: and <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> and his sons laid their hands upon the head of the <a href="../ebd/ebd306.htm#003">ram</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> And he slew it; and <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> took of the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> of it, and put it upon the tip of Aaron's right <a href="../ebd/ebd110.htm#004">ear</a>, and upon the thumb of his right hand, and upon the great toe of his right foot.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> And he brought Aaron's sons, and <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> put of the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> upon the tip of their right <a href="../ebd/ebd110.htm#004">ear</a>, and upon the thumbs of their right hands, and upon the great toes of their right feet: and <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> sprinkled the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> round about.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> And he took the <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#005">fat</a>, and the rump, and all the <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#005">fat</a> that was upon the inwards, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd074.htm#003">caul</a> above the <a href="../ebd/ebd230.htm#004">liver</a>, and the two kidneys, and their <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#005">fat</a>, and the right shoulder:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> And out of the <a href="../ebd/ebd046.htm#009">basket</a> of unleavened <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#006">bread</a>, that was before the LORD, he took one unleavened <a href="../ebd/ebd068.htm#009">cake</a>, and a <a href="../ebd/ebd068.htm#009">cake</a> of oiled <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#006">bread</a>, and one wafer, and put them on the <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#005">fat</a>, and upon the right shoulder:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> And he put all upon Aaron's hands, and upon his sons' hands, and waved them for a wave <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> before the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="028">28</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> took them from off their hands, and burnt them on the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> upon the burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>: they were consecrations for a sweet savour: it is an <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> made by fire unto the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="029">29</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> took the breast, and waved it for a wave <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> before the LORD: for of the <a href="../ebd/ebd306.htm#003">ram</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#007">consecration</a> it was Moses' part; as the LORD commanded <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="030">30</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> took of the anointing oil, and of the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> which was upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>, and sprinkled it upon <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a>, and upon his garments, and upon his sons, and upon his sons' garments with him; and sanctified <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a>, and his garments, and his sons, and his sons' garments with him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="031">31</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> said unto <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> and to his sons, <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#008">Boil</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd135.htm#007">flesh</a> at the door of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>: and there eat it with the <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#006">bread</a> that is in the <a href="../ebd/ebd046.htm#009">basket</a> of consecrations, as I commanded, saying, <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> and his sons shall eat it.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="032">32</a> And that which remaineth of the <a href="../ebd/ebd135.htm#007">flesh</a> and of the <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#006">bread</a> shall ye burn with fire.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="033">33</a> And ye shall not go out of the door of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> in <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> days, until the days of your <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#007">consecration</a> be at an end: for <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> days shall he consecrate you.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="034">34</a> As he hath done this day, so the LORD hath commanded to do, to make an <a href="../ebd/ebd036.htm#004">atonement</a> for you.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="035">35</a> Therefore shall ye abide at the door of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> day and night <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> days, and keep the charge of the LORD, that ye die not: for so I am commanded.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="036">36</a> So <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> and his sons did all things which the LORD commanded by the hand of <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>.<br>
 </p>
