 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Exodus Chapter 24</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> And he said unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, Come up unto the LORD, thou, and <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd264.htm#008">Nadab</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd003.htm#004">Abihu</a>, and seventy of the elders of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>; and <a href="../ebd/ebd384.htm#002">worship</a> ye afar off.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> alone shall come near the LORD: but they shall not come nigh; neither shall the people go up with him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> came and told the people all the words of the LORD, and all the judgments: and all the people answered with one voice, and said, All the words which the LORD hath said will we do.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> wrote all the words of the LORD, and rose up early in the morning, and builded an <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> under the hill, and twelve pillars, according to the twelve tribes of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And he sent young men of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, which offered burnt offerings, and sacrificed peace offerings of oxen unto the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> took half of the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a>, and put it in basons; and half of the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> he sprinkled on the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And he took the book of the <a href="../ebd/ebd091.htm#008">covenant</a>, and read in the audience of the people: and they said, All that the LORD hath said will we do, and be obedient.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> took the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a>, and sprinkled it on the people, and said, Behold the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd091.htm#008">covenant</a>, which the LORD hath made with you concerning all these words.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> Then went up <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd264.htm#008">Nadab</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd003.htm#004">Abihu</a>, and seventy of the elders of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And they saw the God of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>: and there was under his feet as it were a paved work of a <a href="../ebd/ebd322.htm#002">sapphire</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd351.htm#005">stone</a>, and as it were the body of <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#006">heaven</a> in his clearness.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And upon the nobles of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> he laid not his hand: also they saw God, and did eat and <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And the LORD said unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, Come up to me into the <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#007">mount</a>, and be there: and I will give thee <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#006">tables</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd351.htm#005">stone</a>, and a law, and commandments which I have written; that thou mayest teach them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> rose up, and his <a href="../ebd/ebd255.htm#007">minister</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#006">Joshua</a>: and <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> went up into the <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#007">mount</a> of God.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> And he said unto the elders, Tarry ye here for us, until we come again unto you: and, behold, <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd184.htm#009">Hur</a> are with you: if any man have any matters to do, let him come unto them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> went up into the <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#007">mount</a>, and a <a href="../ebd/ebd085.htm#001">cloud</a> covered the <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#007">mount</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a> of the LORD abode upon <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#007">mount</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#009">Sinai</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd085.htm#001">cloud</a> covered it six days: and the seventh day he called unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> out of the midst of the <a href="../ebd/ebd085.htm#001">cloud</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> And the sight of the <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a> of the LORD was like devouring fire on the top of the <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#007">mount</a> in the eyes of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> went into the midst of the <a href="../ebd/ebd085.htm#001">cloud</a>, and gat him up into the <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#007">mount</a>: and <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> was in the <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#007">mount</a> forty days and forty nights.<br>
 </p>
