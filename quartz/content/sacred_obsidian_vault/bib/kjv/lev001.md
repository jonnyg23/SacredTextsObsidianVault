 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Leviticus Chapter 1</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> And the LORD called unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, and spake unto him out of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Speak unto the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, and say unto them, If any man of you bring an <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> unto the LORD, ye shall bring your <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd074.htm#002">cattle</a>, even of the <a href="../ebd/ebd174.htm#009">herd</a>, and of the flock.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> If his <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> be a burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#002">sacrifice</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd174.htm#009">herd</a>, let him offer a male without <a href="../ebd/ebd060.htm#007">blemish</a>: he shall offer it of his own voluntary will at the door of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> before the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And he shall put his hand upon the head of the burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>; and it shall be accepted for him to make <a href="../ebd/ebd036.htm#004">atonement</a> for him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And he shall kill the <a href="../ebd/ebd066.htm#003">bullock</a> before the LORD: and the priests, Aaron's sons, shall bring the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a>, and sprinkle the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> round about upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> that is by the door of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And he shall flay the burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, and cut it into his <a href="../ebd/ebd295.htm#002">pieces</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> shall put fire upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>, and lay the <a href="../ebd/ebd383.htm#003">wood</a> in order upon the fire:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And the priests, Aaron's sons, shall lay the parts, the head, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#005">fat</a>, in order upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd383.htm#003">wood</a> that is on the fire which is upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> But his inwards and his legs shall he wash in water: and the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> shall burn all on the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>, to be a burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#002">sacrifice</a>, an <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> made by fire, of a sweet savour unto the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And if his <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> be of the flocks, namely, of the <a href="../ebd/ebd333.htm#005">sheep</a>, or of the goats, for a burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#002">sacrifice</a>; he shall bring it a male without <a href="../ebd/ebd060.htm#007">blemish</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And he shall kill it on the side of the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd275.htm#002">northward</a> before the LORD: and the priests, Aaron's sons, shall sprinkle his <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> round about upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And he shall cut it into his <a href="../ebd/ebd295.htm#002">pieces</a>, with his head and his <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#005">fat</a>: and the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> shall lay them in order on the <a href="../ebd/ebd383.htm#003">wood</a> that is on the fire which is upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> But he shall wash the inwards and the legs with water: and the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> shall bring it all, and burn it upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>: it is a burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#002">sacrifice</a>, an <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> made by fire, of a sweet savour unto the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> And if the burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#002">sacrifice</a> for his <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> to the LORD be of fowls, then he shall bring his <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> of turtledoves, or of young pigeons.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> shall bring it unto the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>, and wring off his head, and burn it on the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>; and the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> thereof shall be wrung out at the side of the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> And he shall pluck away his crop with his feathers, and cast it beside the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> on the <a href="../ebd/ebd111.htm#000">east</a> part, by the place of the <a href="../ebd/ebd034.htm#000">ashes</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> And he shall cleave it with the wings thereof, but shall not divide it asunder: and the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> shall burn it upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>, upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd383.htm#003">wood</a> that is upon the fire: it is a burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#002">sacrifice</a>, an <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> made by fire, of a sweet savour unto the LORD.<br>
 </p>
