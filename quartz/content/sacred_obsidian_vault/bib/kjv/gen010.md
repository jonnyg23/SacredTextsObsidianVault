 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Genesis Chapter 10</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Now these are the generations of the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd274.htm#002">Noah</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd334.htm#002">Shem</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd160.htm#003">Ham</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd197.htm#002">Japheth</a>: and unto them were sons born after the <a href="../ebd/ebd136.htm#000">flood</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> The sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd197.htm#002">Japheth</a>; <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#006">Gomer</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd236.htm#009">Magog</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd235.htm#008">Madai</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd198.htm#005">Javan</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd373.htm#005">Tubal</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd250.htm#008">Meshech</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd367.htm#008">Tiras</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#006">Gomer</a>; <a href="../ebd/ebd034.htm#002">Ashkenaz</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd313.htm#008">Riphath</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd369.htm#004">Togarmah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd198.htm#005">Javan</a>; <a href="../ebd/ebd118.htm#000">Elishah</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd359.htm#002">Tarshish</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd221.htm#004">Kittim</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd105.htm#004">Dodanim</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> By these were the isles of the <a href="../ebd/ebd146.htm#001">Gentiles</a> divided in their lands; every one after his tongue, after their families, in their nations.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd160.htm#003">Ham</a>; <a href="../ebd/ebd094.htm#005">Cush</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd258.htm#002">Mizraim</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd294.htm#007">Phut</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#005">Canaan</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd094.htm#005">Cush</a>; <a href="../ebd/ebd325.htm#004">Seba</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd167.htm#009">Havilah</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd317.htm#007">Sabtah</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd304.htm#002">Raamah</a>, and Sabtechah: and the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd304.htm#002">Raamah</a>; <a href="../ebd/ebd332.htm#006">Sheba</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd100.htm#005">Dedan</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd094.htm#005">Cush</a> begat <a href="../ebd/ebd273.htm#004">Nimrod</a>: he began to be a mighty one in the earth.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> He was a mighty hunter before the LORD: wherefore it is said, Even as <a href="../ebd/ebd273.htm#004">Nimrod</a> the mighty hunter before the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And the beginning of his kingdom was Babel, and <a href="../ebd/ebd124.htm#002">Erech</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd006.htm#000">Accad</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd069.htm#008">Calneh</a>, in the land of Shinar.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> Out of that land went forth <a href="../ebd/ebd035.htm#002">Asshur</a>, and builded <a href="../ebd/ebd273.htm#006">Nineveh</a>, and the city <a href="../ebd/ebd309.htm#008">Rehoboth</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd069.htm#000">Calah</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd311.htm#005">Resen</a> between <a href="../ebd/ebd273.htm#006">Nineveh</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd069.htm#000">Calah</a>: the same is a great city.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd258.htm#002">Mizraim</a> begat <a href="../ebd/ebd233.htm#000">Ludim</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd022.htm#007">Anamim</a>, and Lehabim, and <a href="../ebd/ebd266.htm#009">Naphtuhim</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> And Pathrusim, and <a href="../ebd/ebd073.htm#005">Casluhim</a>, (out of whom came Philistim,) and Caphtorim.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#005">Canaan</a> begat <a href="../ebd/ebd343.htm#000">Sidon</a> his first born, and <a href="../ebd/ebd177.htm#001">Heth</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> And the Jebusite, and the Amorite, and the Girgasite,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> And the Hivite, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#001">Arkite</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd345.htm#002">Sinite</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> And the Arvadite, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd390.htm#005">Zemarite</a>, and the Hamathite: and afterward were the families of the <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#007">Canaanites</a> spread abroad.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> And the border of the <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#007">Canaanites</a> was from <a href="../ebd/ebd343.htm#000">Sidon</a>, as thou comest to <a href="../ebd/ebd146.htm#005">Gerar</a>, unto <a href="../ebd/ebd144.htm#003">Gaza</a>; as thou goest, unto <a href="../ebd/ebd347.htm#004">Sodom</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#007">Gomorrah</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd009.htm#003">Admah</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd388.htm#009">Zeboim</a>, even unto <a href="../ebd/ebd224.htm#005">Lasha</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> These are the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd160.htm#003">Ham</a>, after their families, after their tongues, in their countries, and in their nations.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> Unto <a href="../ebd/ebd334.htm#002">Shem</a> also, the father of all the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd112.htm#001">Eber</a>, the brother of <a href="../ebd/ebd197.htm#002">Japheth</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd114.htm#008">elder</a>, even to him were children born.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> The children of <a href="../ebd/ebd334.htm#002">Shem</a>; <a href="../ebd/ebd114.htm#003">Elam</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd035.htm#002">Asshur</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd032.htm#004">Arphaxad</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd232.htm#009">Lud</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd028.htm#001">Aram</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> And the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd028.htm#001">Aram</a>; <a href="../ebd/ebd375.htm#007">Uz</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd184.htm#004">Hul</a>, and Gether, and <a href="../ebd/ebd243.htm#001">Mash</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd032.htm#004">Arphaxad</a> begat Salah; and Salah begat <a href="../ebd/ebd112.htm#001">Eber</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> And unto <a href="../ebd/ebd112.htm#001">Eber</a> were born two sons: the name of one was <a href="../ebd/ebd288.htm#007">Peleg</a>; for in his days was the earth divided; and his brother's name was <a href="../ebd/ebd210.htm#004">Joktan</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd210.htm#004">Joktan</a> begat <a href="../ebd/ebd017.htm#009">Almodad</a>, and Sheleph, and Hazarmaveth, and Jerah,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd158.htm#001">Hadoram</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd375.htm#009">Uzal</a>, and Diklah,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="028">28</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd276.htm#003">Obal</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd003.htm#009">Abimael</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd332.htm#006">Sheba</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="029">29</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd279.htm#007">Ophir</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd167.htm#009">Havilah</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd208.htm#003">Jobab</a>: all these were the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd210.htm#004">Joktan</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="030">30</a> And their dwelling was from <a href="../ebd/ebd250.htm#006">Mesha</a>, as thou goest unto <a href="../ebd/ebd327.htm#007">Sephar</a> a <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#007">mount</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd111.htm#000">east</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="031">31</a> These are the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd334.htm#002">Shem</a>, after their families, after their tongues, in their lands, after their nations.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="032">32</a> These are the families of the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd274.htm#002">Noah</a>, after their generations, in their nations: and by these were the nations divided in the earth after the <a href="../ebd/ebd136.htm#000">flood</a>.<br>
 </p>
