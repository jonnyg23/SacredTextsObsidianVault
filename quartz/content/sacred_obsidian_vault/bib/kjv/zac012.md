 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Zechariah Chapter 12</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> The <a href="../ebd/ebd066.htm#007">burden</a> of the word of the LORD for <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, saith the LORD, which stretcheth forth the heavens, and layeth the foundation of the earth, and formeth the <a href="../ebd/ebd349.htm#009">spirit</a> of man within him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Behold, I will make <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a> a <a href="../ebd/ebd094.htm#000">cup</a> of trembling unto all the people round about, when they shall be in the siege both against <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a> and against <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And in that day will I make <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a> a burdensome <a href="../ebd/ebd351.htm#005">stone</a> for all people: all that <a href="../ebd/ebd066.htm#007">burden</a> themselves with it shall be cut in <a href="../ebd/ebd295.htm#002">pieces</a>, though all the people of the earth be gathered together against it.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> In that day, saith the LORD, I will smite every horse with astonishment, and his rider with <a href="../ebd/ebd236.htm#002">madness</a>: and I will open mine eyes upon the house of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>, and will smite every horse of the people with blindness.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And the governors of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a> shall say in their heart, The inhabitants of <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a> shall be my strength in the LORD of hosts their God.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> In that day will I make the governors of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a> like an <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#002">hearth</a> of fire among the <a href="../ebd/ebd383.htm#003">wood</a>, and like a torch of fire in a sheaf; and they shall devour all the people round about, on the right hand and on the left: and <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a> shall be inhabited again in her own place, even in <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> The LORD also shall save the tents of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a> first, that the <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a> of the house of <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> and the <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a> of the inhabitants of <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a> do not magnify themselves against <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> In that day shall the LORD defend the inhabitants of <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>; and he that is feeble among them at that day shall be as <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a>; and the house of <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> shall be as God, as the <a href="../ebd/ebd024.htm#001">angel</a> of the LORD before them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And it shall come to pass in that day, that I will seek to destroy all the nations that come against <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And I will pour upon the house of <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a>, and upon the inhabitants of <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>, the <a href="../ebd/ebd349.htm#009">spirit</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd154.htm#000">grace</a> and of supplications: and they shall look upon me whom they have pierced, and they shall <a href="../ebd/ebd261.htm#004">mourn</a> for him, as one mourneth for his only son, and shall be in bitterness for him, as one that is in bitterness for his firstborn.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> In that day shall there be a great mourning in <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>, as the mourning of Hadadrimmon in the <a href="../ebd/ebd376.htm#008">valley</a> of Megiddon.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And the land shall <a href="../ebd/ebd261.htm#004">mourn</a>, every family apart; the family of the house of <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> apart, and their wives apart; the family of the house of <a href="../ebd/ebd267.htm#002">Nathan</a> apart, and their wives apart;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> The family of the house of <a href="../ebd/ebd227.htm#006">Levi</a> apart, and their wives apart; the family of <a href="../ebd/ebd338.htm#002">Shimei</a> apart, and their wives apart;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> All the families that remain, every family apart, and their wives apart.<br>
 </p>
