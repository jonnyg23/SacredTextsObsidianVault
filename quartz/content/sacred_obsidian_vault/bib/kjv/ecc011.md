 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Ecclesiastes Chapter 11</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Cast thy <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#006">bread</a> upon the waters: for thou shalt find it after many days.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Give a portion to <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a>, and also to eight; for thou knowest not what evil shall be upon the earth.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> If the clouds be full of <a href="../ebd/ebd305.htm#008">rain</a>, they empty themselves upon the earth: and if the tree fall toward the south, or toward the north, in the place where the tree falleth, there it shall be.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> He that observeth the wind shall not sow; and he that regardeth the clouds shall not reap.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> As thou knowest not what is the way of the <a href="../ebd/ebd349.htm#009">spirit</a>, nor how the bones do grow in the womb of her that is with child: even so thou knowest not the works of God who maketh all.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> In the morning sow thy seed, and in the evening withhold not thine hand: for thou knowest not whether shall prosper, either this or that, or whether they both shall be alike good.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Truly the light is sweet, and a pleasant thing it is for the eyes to behold the <a href="../ebd/ebd353.htm#001">sun</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> But if a man live many years, and rejoice in them all; yet let him remember the days of <a href="../ebd/ebd097.htm#008">darkness</a>; for they shall be many. All that cometh is vanity.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> Rejoice, O young man, in thy youth; and let thy heart cheer thee in the days of thy youth, and walk in the ways of thine heart, and in the sight of thine eyes: but know thou, that for all these things God will bring thee into judgment.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> Therefore remove sorrow from thy heart, and put away evil from thy <a href="../ebd/ebd135.htm#007">flesh</a>: for childhood and youth are vanity.<br>
 </p>
