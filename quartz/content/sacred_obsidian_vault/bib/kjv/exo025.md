 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Exodus Chapter 25</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> And the LORD spake unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Speak unto the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, that they bring me an <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>: of every man that giveth it willingly with his heart ye shall take my <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And this is the <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> which ye shall take of them; <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#000">silver</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#003">brass</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#003">blue</a>, and purple, and <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#005">scarlet</a>, and fine <a href="../ebd/ebd229.htm#008">linen</a>, and goats' hair,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And rams' skins dyed red, and badgers' skins, and <a href="../ebd/ebd339.htm#009">shittim</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd383.htm#003">wood</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Oil for the light, <a href="../ebd/ebd349.htm#005">spices</a> for anointing oil, and for sweet <a href="../ebd/ebd188.htm#002">incense</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd279.htm#004">Onyx</a> stones, and stones to be set in the <a href="../ebd/ebd122.htm#008">ephod</a>, and in the <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#007">breastplate</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And let them make me a <a href="../ebd/ebd321.htm#005">sanctuary</a>; that I may dwell among them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> According to all that I shew thee, after the pattern of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a>, and the pattern of all the instruments thereof, even so shall ye make it.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And they shall make an <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#000">ark</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd339.htm#009">shittim</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd383.htm#003">wood</a>: two cubits and a half shall be the length thereof, and a <a href="../ebd/ebd093.htm#006">cubit</a> and a half the breadth thereof, and a <a href="../ebd/ebd093.htm#006">cubit</a> and a half the height thereof.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And thou shalt overlay it with pure <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a>, within and without shalt thou overlay it, and shalt make upon it a <a href="../ebd/ebd093.htm#001">crown</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a> round about.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And thou shalt cast four rings of <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a> for it, and put them in the four corners thereof; and two rings shall be in the one side of it, and two rings in the other side of it.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> And thou shalt make staves of <a href="../ebd/ebd339.htm#009">shittim</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd383.htm#003">wood</a>, and overlay them with <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> And thou shalt put the staves into the rings by the sides of the <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#000">ark</a>, that the <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#000">ark</a> may be borne with them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> The staves shall be in the rings of the <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#000">ark</a>: they shall not be taken from it.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> And thou shalt put into the <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#000">ark</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd362.htm#008">testimony</a> which I shall give thee.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> And thou shalt make a <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> seat of pure <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a>: two cubits and a half shall be the length thereof, and a <a href="../ebd/ebd093.htm#006">cubit</a> and a half the breadth thereof.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> And thou shalt make two cherubims of <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a>, of beaten work shalt thou make them, in the two ends of the <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> seat.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> And make one <a href="../ebd/ebd079.htm#003">cherub</a> on the one end, and the other <a href="../ebd/ebd079.htm#003">cherub</a> on the other end: even of the <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> seat shall ye make the cherubims on the two ends thereof.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> And the cherubims shall stretch forth their wings on high, covering the <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> seat with their wings, and their faces shall look one to another; toward the <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> seat shall the faces of the cherubims be.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> And thou shalt put the <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> seat above upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#000">ark</a>; and in the <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#000">ark</a> thou shalt put the <a href="../ebd/ebd362.htm#008">testimony</a> that I shall give thee.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> And there I will meet with thee, and I will commune with thee from above the <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> seat, from between the two cherubims which are upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#000">ark</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd362.htm#008">testimony</a>, of all things which I will give thee in commandment unto the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> Thou shalt also make a table of <a href="../ebd/ebd339.htm#009">shittim</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd383.htm#003">wood</a>: two cubits shall be the length thereof, and a <a href="../ebd/ebd093.htm#006">cubit</a> the breadth thereof, and a <a href="../ebd/ebd093.htm#006">cubit</a> and a half the height thereof.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> And thou shalt overlay it with pure <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a>, and make thereto a <a href="../ebd/ebd093.htm#001">crown</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a> round about.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> And thou shalt make unto it a border of an hand breadth round about, and thou shalt make a golden <a href="../ebd/ebd093.htm#001">crown</a> to the border thereof round about.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> And thou shalt make for it four rings of <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a>, and put the rings in the four corners that are on the four feet thereof.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> Over against the border shall the rings be for places of the staves to bear the table.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="028">28</a> And thou shalt make the staves of <a href="../ebd/ebd339.htm#009">shittim</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd383.htm#003">wood</a>, and overlay them with <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a>, that the table may be borne with them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="029">29</a> And thou shalt make the dishes thereof, and spoons thereof, and covers thereof, and bowls thereof, to cover withal: of pure <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a> shalt thou make them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="030">30</a> And thou shalt set upon the table <a href="../ebd/ebd336.htm#008">shewbread</a> before me alway.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="031">31</a> And thou shalt make a <a href="../ebd/ebd071.htm#001">candlestick</a> of pure <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a>: of beaten work shall the <a href="../ebd/ebd071.htm#001">candlestick</a> be made: his shaft, and his branches, his bowls, his knops, and his <a href="../ebd/ebd136.htm#002">flowers</a>, shall be of the same.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="032">32</a> And six branches shall come out of the sides of it; three branches of the <a href="../ebd/ebd071.htm#001">candlestick</a> out of the one side, and three branches of the <a href="../ebd/ebd071.htm#001">candlestick</a> out of the other side:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="033">33</a> Three bowls made like unto almonds, with a <a href="../ebd/ebd221.htm#009">knop</a> and a flower in one <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#002">branch</a>; and three bowls made like almonds in the other <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#002">branch</a>, with a <a href="../ebd/ebd221.htm#009">knop</a> and a flower: so in the six branches that come out of the <a href="../ebd/ebd071.htm#001">candlestick</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="034">34</a> And in the candlesticks shall be four bowls made like unto almonds, with their knops and their <a href="../ebd/ebd136.htm#002">flowers</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="035">35</a> And there shall be a <a href="../ebd/ebd221.htm#009">knop</a> under two branches of the same, and a <a href="../ebd/ebd221.htm#009">knop</a> under two branches of the same, and a <a href="../ebd/ebd221.htm#009">knop</a> under two branches of the same, according to the six branches that proceed out of the <a href="../ebd/ebd071.htm#001">candlestick</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="036">36</a> Their knops and their branches shall be of the same: all it shall be one beaten work of pure <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="037">37</a> And thou shalt make the <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> lamps thereof: and they shall light the lamps thereof, that they may give light over against it.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="038">38</a> And the tongs thereof, and the snuffdishes thereof, shall be of pure <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="039">39</a> Of a <a href="../ebd/ebd357.htm#009">talent</a> of pure <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a> shall he make it, with all these vessels.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="040">40</a> And look that thou make them after their pattern, which was shewed thee in the <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#007">mount</a>.<br>
 </p>
