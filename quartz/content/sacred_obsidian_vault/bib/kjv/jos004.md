 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Joshua Chapter 4</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> And it came to pass, when all the people were <a href="../ebd/ebd084.htm#005">clean</a> passed over <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#004">Jordan</a>, that the LORD spake unto <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#006">Joshua</a>, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Take you twelve men out of the people, out of every <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> a man,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And command ye them, saying, Take you hence out of the midst of <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#004">Jordan</a>, out of the place where the priests' feet stood firm, twelve stones, and ye shall carry them over with you, and leave them in the lodging place, where ye shall <a href="../ebd/ebd231.htm#002">lodge</a> this night.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Then <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#006">Joshua</a> called the twelve men, whom he had prepared of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, out of every <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> a man:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#006">Joshua</a> said unto them, Pass over before the <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#000">ark</a> of the LORD your God into the midst of <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#004">Jordan</a>, and take you up every man of you a <a href="../ebd/ebd351.htm#005">stone</a> upon his shoulder, according unto the number of the tribes of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> That this may be a sign among you, that when your children ask their fathers in time to come, saying, What mean ye by these stones?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Then ye shall answer them, That the waters of <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#004">Jordan</a> were cut off before the <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#000">ark</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd091.htm#008">covenant</a> of the LORD; when it passed over <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#004">Jordan</a>, the waters of <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#004">Jordan</a> were cut off: and these stones shall be for a memorial unto the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> for ever.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> did so as <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#006">Joshua</a> commanded, and took up twelve stones out of the midst of <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#004">Jordan</a>, as the LORD spake unto <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#006">Joshua</a>, according to the number of the tribes of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, and carried them over with them unto the place where they lodged, and laid them down there.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#006">Joshua</a> set up twelve stones in the midst of <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#004">Jordan</a>, in the place where the feet of the priests which bare the <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#000">ark</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd091.htm#008">covenant</a> stood: and they are there unto this day.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> For the priests which bare the <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#000">ark</a> stood in the midst of <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#004">Jordan</a>, until everything was finished that the LORD commanded <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#006">Joshua</a> to speak unto the people, according to all that <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> commanded <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#006">Joshua</a>: and the people hasted and passed over.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And it came to pass, when all the people were <a href="../ebd/ebd084.htm#005">clean</a> passed over, that the <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#000">ark</a> of the LORD passed over, and the priests, in the presence of the people.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd311.htm#009">Reuben</a>, and the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd140.htm#007">Gad</a>, and half the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd240.htm#002">Manasseh</a>, passed over armed before the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, as <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> spake unto them:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> About forty thousand prepared for war passed over before the LORD unto battle, to the plains of <a href="../ebd/ebd203.htm#008">Jericho</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> On that day the LORD magnified <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#006">Joshua</a> in the sight of all <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>; and they feared him, as they feared <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, all the days of his life.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And the LORD spake unto <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#006">Joshua</a>, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> Command the priests that bear the <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#000">ark</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd362.htm#008">testimony</a>, that they come up out of <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#004">Jordan</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#006">Joshua</a> therefore commanded the priests, saying, Come ye up out of <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#004">Jordan</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> And it came to pass, when the priests that bare the <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#000">ark</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd091.htm#008">covenant</a> of the LORD were come up out of the midst of <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#004">Jordan</a>, and the soles of the priests' feet were lifted up unto the dry land, that the waters of <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#004">Jordan</a> returned unto their place, and flowed over all his banks, as they did before.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> And the people came up out of <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#004">Jordan</a> on the tenth day of the first month, and encamped in <a href="../ebd/ebd149.htm#001">Gilgal</a>, in the <a href="../ebd/ebd111.htm#000">east</a> border of <a href="../ebd/ebd203.htm#008">Jericho</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> And those twelve stones, which they took out of <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#004">Jordan</a>, did <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#006">Joshua</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd296.htm#008">pitch</a> in <a href="../ebd/ebd149.htm#001">Gilgal</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> And he spake unto the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, saying, When your children shall ask their fathers in time to come, saying, What mean these stones?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> Then ye shall let your children know, saying, <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> came over this <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#004">Jordan</a> on dry land.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> For the LORD your God dried up the waters of <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#004">Jordan</a> from before you, until ye were passed over, as the LORD your God did to the Red sea, which he dried up from before us, until we were gone over:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> That all the people of the earth might know the hand of the LORD, that it is mighty: that ye might fear the LORD your God for ever.<br>
 </p>
