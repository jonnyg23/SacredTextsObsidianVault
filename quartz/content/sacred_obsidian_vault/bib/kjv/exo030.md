 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Exodus Chapter 30</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> And thou shalt make an <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> to burn <a href="../ebd/ebd188.htm#002">incense</a> upon: of <a href="../ebd/ebd339.htm#009">shittim</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd383.htm#003">wood</a> shalt thou make it.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> A <a href="../ebd/ebd093.htm#006">cubit</a> shall be the length thereof, and a <a href="../ebd/ebd093.htm#006">cubit</a> the breadth thereof; foursquare shall it be: and two cubits shall be the height thereof: the horns thereof shall be of the same.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And thou shalt overlay it with pure <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a>, the top thereof, and the sides thereof round about, and the horns thereof; and thou shalt make unto it a <a href="../ebd/ebd093.htm#001">crown</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a> round about.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And two golden rings shalt thou make to it under the <a href="../ebd/ebd093.htm#001">crown</a> of it, by the two corners thereof, upon the two sides of it shalt thou make it; and they shall be for places for the staves to bear it withal.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And thou shalt make the staves of <a href="../ebd/ebd339.htm#009">shittim</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd383.htm#003">wood</a>, and overlay them with <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And thou shalt put it before the vail that is by the <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#000">ark</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd362.htm#008">testimony</a>, before the <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> seat that is over the <a href="../ebd/ebd362.htm#008">testimony</a>, where I will meet with thee.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> shall burn thereon sweet <a href="../ebd/ebd188.htm#002">incense</a> every morning: when he dresseth the lamps, he shall burn <a href="../ebd/ebd188.htm#002">incense</a> upon it.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And when <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> lighteth the lamps at even, he shall burn <a href="../ebd/ebd188.htm#002">incense</a> upon it, a perpetual <a href="../ebd/ebd188.htm#002">incense</a> before the LORD throughout your generations.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> Ye shall offer no strange <a href="../ebd/ebd188.htm#002">incense</a> thereon, nor burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#002">sacrifice</a>, nor meat <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>; neither shall ye pour <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> thereon.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> shall make an <a href="../ebd/ebd036.htm#004">atonement</a> upon the horns of it once in a year with the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> of atonements: once in the year shall he make <a href="../ebd/ebd036.htm#004">atonement</a> upon it throughout your generations: it is most holy unto the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And the LORD spake unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> When thou takest the sum of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> after their number, then shall they give every man a <a href="../ebd/ebd307.htm#005">ransom</a> for his soul unto the LORD, when thou numberest them; that there be no <a href="../ebd/ebd297.htm#001">plague</a> among them, when thou numberest them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> This they shall give, every one that passeth among them that are numbered, half a <a href="../ebd/ebd333.htm#009">shekel</a> after the <a href="../ebd/ebd333.htm#009">shekel</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd321.htm#005">sanctuary</a>: (a <a href="../ebd/ebd333.htm#009">shekel</a> is twenty gerahs:) an half <a href="../ebd/ebd333.htm#009">shekel</a> shall be the <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> of the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> Every one that passeth among them that are numbered, from twenty years old and above, shall give an <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> unto the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> The rich shall not give more, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd298.htm#006">poor</a> shall not give less than half a <a href="../ebd/ebd333.htm#009">shekel</a>, when they give an <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> unto the LORD, to make an <a href="../ebd/ebd036.htm#004">atonement</a> for your souls.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> And thou shalt take the <a href="../ebd/ebd036.htm#004">atonement</a> money of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, and shalt appoint it for the service of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>; that it may be a memorial unto the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> before the LORD, to make an <a href="../ebd/ebd036.htm#004">atonement</a> for your souls.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> And the LORD spake unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> Thou shalt also make a <a href="../ebd/ebd224.htm#009">laver</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#003">brass</a>, and his foot also of <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#003">brass</a>, to wash withal: and thou shalt put it between the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> and the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>, and thou shalt put water therein.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> For <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> and his sons shall wash their hands and their feet thereat:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> When they go into the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>, they shall wash with water, that they die not; or when they come near to the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> to <a href="../ebd/ebd255.htm#007">minister</a>, to burn <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> made by fire unto the LORD:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> So they shall wash their hands and their feet, that they die not: and it shall be a statute for ever to them, even to him and to his seed throughout their generations.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> Moreover the LORD spake unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> Take thou also unto thee principal <a href="../ebd/ebd349.htm#005">spices</a>, of pure <a href="../ebd/ebd263.htm#003">myrrh</a> five hundred shekels, and of sweet <a href="../ebd/ebd083.htm#004">cinnamon</a> half so much, even two hundred and fifty shekels, and of sweet <a href="../ebd/ebd069.htm#001">calamus</a> two hundred and fifty shekels,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> And of <a href="../ebd/ebd073.htm#006">cassia</a> five hundred shekels, after the <a href="../ebd/ebd333.htm#009">shekel</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd321.htm#005">sanctuary</a>, and of oil <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#009">olive</a> an hin:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> And thou shalt make it an oil of holy <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#007">ointment</a>, an <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#007">ointment</a> compound after the art of the <a href="../ebd/ebd026.htm#009">apothecary</a>: it shall be an holy anointing oil.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> And thou shalt <a href="../ebd/ebd024.htm#008">anoint</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> therewith, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#000">ark</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd362.htm#008">testimony</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> And the table and all his vessels, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd071.htm#001">candlestick</a> and his vessels, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd188.htm#002">incense</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="028">28</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> of burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> with all his vessels, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd224.htm#009">laver</a> and his foot.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="029">29</a> And thou shalt sanctify them, that they may be most holy: whatsoever toucheth them shall be holy.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="030">30</a> And thou shalt <a href="../ebd/ebd024.htm#008">anoint</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> and his sons, and consecrate them, that they may <a href="../ebd/ebd255.htm#007">minister</a> unto me in the priest's office.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="031">31</a> And thou shalt speak unto the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, saying, This shall be an holy anointing oil unto me throughout your generations.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="032">32</a> Upon man's <a href="../ebd/ebd135.htm#007">flesh</a> shall it not be poured, neither shall ye make any other like it, after the composition of it: it is holy, and it shall be holy unto you.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="033">33</a> Whosoever compoundeth any like it, or whosoever putteth any of it upon a <a href="../ebd/ebd351.htm#009">stranger</a>, shall even be cut off from his people.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="034">34</a> And the LORD said unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, Take unto thee sweet <a href="../ebd/ebd349.htm#005">spices</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd350.htm#005">stacte</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd279.htm#003">onycha</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd141.htm#006">galbanum</a>; these sweet <a href="../ebd/ebd349.htm#005">spices</a> with pure <a href="../ebd/ebd138.htm#004">frankincense</a>: of each shall there be a like weight:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="035">35</a> And thou shalt make it a perfume, a <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#001">confection</a> after the art of the <a href="../ebd/ebd026.htm#009">apothecary</a>, tempered together, pure and holy:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="036">36</a> And thou shalt beat some of it very small, and put of it before the <a href="../ebd/ebd362.htm#008">testimony</a> in the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>, where I will meet with thee: it shall be unto you most holy.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="037">37</a> And as for the perfume which thou shalt make, ye shall not make to yourselves according to the composition thereof: it shall be unto thee holy for the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="038">38</a> Whosoever shall make like unto that, to smell thereto, shall even be cut off from his people.<br>
 </p>
