 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 72</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Give the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> thy judgments, O God, and thy <a href="../ebd/ebd313.htm#004">righteousness</a> unto the king's son.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> He shall <a href="../ebd/ebd213.htm#005">judge</a> thy people with <a href="../ebd/ebd313.htm#004">righteousness</a>, and thy <a href="../ebd/ebd298.htm#006">poor</a> with judgment.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> The mountains shall bring peace to the people, and the little hills, by <a href="../ebd/ebd313.htm#004">righteousness</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> He shall <a href="../ebd/ebd213.htm#005">judge</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd298.htm#006">poor</a> of the people, he shall save the children of the needy, and shall break in <a href="../ebd/ebd295.htm#002">pieces</a> the oppressor.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> They shall fear thee as long as the <a href="../ebd/ebd353.htm#001">sun</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd259.htm#004">moon</a> endure, throughout all generations.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> He shall come down like <a href="../ebd/ebd305.htm#008">rain</a> upon the mown <a href="../ebd/ebd154.htm#005">grass</a>: as showers that water the earth.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> In his days shall the righteous flourish; and abundance of peace so long as the <a href="../ebd/ebd259.htm#004">moon</a> endureth.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> He shall have dominion also from sea to sea, and from the river unto the ends of the earth.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> They that dwell in the <a href="../ebd/ebd381.htm#005">wilderness</a> shall <a href="../ebd/ebd063.htm#003">bow</a> before him; and his enemies shall lick the <a href="../ebd/ebd109.htm#008">dust</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> The kings of <a href="../ebd/ebd359.htm#002">Tarshish</a> and of the isles shall bring presents: the kings of <a href="../ebd/ebd332.htm#006">Sheba</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd325.htm#004">Seba</a> shall offer gifts.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> Yea, all kings shall fall down before him: all nations shall serve him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> For he shall deliver the needy when he crieth; the <a href="../ebd/ebd298.htm#006">poor</a> also, and him that hath no helper.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> He shall spare the <a href="../ebd/ebd298.htm#006">poor</a> and needy, and shall save the souls of the needy.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> He shall redeem their soul from deceit and violence: and precious shall their <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> be in his sight.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And he shall live, and to him shall be given of the <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd332.htm#006">Sheba</a>: <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#000">prayer</a> also shall be made for him continually; and daily shall he be praised.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> There shall be an handful of <a href="../ebd/ebd090.htm#006">corn</a> in the earth upon the top of the mountains; the <a href="../ebd/ebd139.htm#000">fruit</a> thereof shall shake like <a href="../ebd/ebd226.htm#001">Lebanon</a>: and they of the city shall flourish like <a href="../ebd/ebd154.htm#005">grass</a> of the earth.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> His name shall endure for ever: his name shall be continued as long as the <a href="../ebd/ebd353.htm#001">sun</a>: and men shall be blessed in him: all nations shall call him blessed.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> Blessed be the LORD God, the God of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, who only doeth wondrous things.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> And blessed be his glorious name for ever: and let the whole earth be filled with his <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a>; <a href="../ebd/ebd020.htm#001">Amen</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd020.htm#001">Amen</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> The prayers of <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#005">Jesse</a> are ended.<br>
 </p>
