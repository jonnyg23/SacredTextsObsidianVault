 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: 1 Kings (1 Samuel) Chapter 15</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd321.htm#001">Samuel</a> also said unto <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#002">Saul</a>, The LORD sent me to <a href="../ebd/ebd024.htm#008">anoint</a> thee to be <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> over his people, over <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>: now therefore hearken thou unto the voice of the words of the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Thus saith the LORD of hosts, I remember that which <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#009">Amalek</a> did to <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, how he laid wait for him in the way, when he came up from <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Now go and smite <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#009">Amalek</a>, and utterly destroy all that they have, and spare them not; but slay both man and woman, infant and suckling, <a href="../ebd/ebd281.htm#007">ox</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd333.htm#005">sheep</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#000">camel</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd035.htm#001">ass</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#002">Saul</a> gathered the people together, and numbered them in <a href="../ebd/ebd360.htm#005">Telaim</a>, two hundred thousand footmen, and ten thousand men of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#002">Saul</a> came to a city of <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#009">Amalek</a>, and laid wait in the <a href="../ebd/ebd376.htm#008">valley</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#002">Saul</a> said unto the <a href="../ebd/ebd217.htm#004">Kenites</a>, Go, depart, get you down from among the Amalekites, lest I destroy you with them: for ye shewed kindness to all the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, when they came up out of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a>. So the <a href="../ebd/ebd217.htm#004">Kenites</a> departed from among the Amalekites.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#002">Saul</a> smote the Amalekites from <a href="../ebd/ebd167.htm#009">Havilah</a> until thou comest to <a href="../ebd/ebd342.htm#002">Shur</a>, that is over against <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And he took <a href="../ebd/ebd011.htm#008">Agag</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of the Amalekites alive, and utterly destroyed all the people with the edge of the <a href="../ebd/ebd354.htm#003">sword</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> But <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#002">Saul</a> and the people spared <a href="../ebd/ebd011.htm#008">Agag</a>, and the best of the <a href="../ebd/ebd333.htm#005">sheep</a>, and of the oxen, and of the fatlings, and the lambs, and all that was good, and would not utterly destroy them: but every thing that was vile and refuse, that they destroyed utterly.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> Then came the word of the LORD unto <a href="../ebd/ebd321.htm#001">Samuel</a>, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> It repenteth me that I have set up <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#002">Saul</a> to be <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a>: for he is turned back from following me, and hath not performed my commandments. And it grieved <a href="../ebd/ebd321.htm#001">Samuel</a>; and he cried unto the LORD all night.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And when <a href="../ebd/ebd321.htm#001">Samuel</a> rose early to meet <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#002">Saul</a> in the morning, it was told <a href="../ebd/ebd321.htm#001">Samuel</a>, saying, <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#002">Saul</a> came to <a href="../ebd/ebd072.htm#006">Carmel</a>, and, behold, he set him up a place, and is gone about, and passed on, and gone down to <a href="../ebd/ebd149.htm#001">Gilgal</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd321.htm#001">Samuel</a> came to <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#002">Saul</a>: and <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#002">Saul</a> said unto him, Blessed be thou of the LORD: I have performed the commandment of the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd321.htm#001">Samuel</a> said, What meaneth then this bleating of the <a href="../ebd/ebd333.htm#005">sheep</a> in mine ears, and the lowing of the oxen which I hear?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#002">Saul</a> said, They have brought them from the Amalekites: for the people spared the best of the <a href="../ebd/ebd333.htm#005">sheep</a> and of the oxen, to <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#002">sacrifice</a> unto the LORD thy God; and the rest we have utterly destroyed.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> Then <a href="../ebd/ebd321.htm#001">Samuel</a> said unto <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#002">Saul</a>, Stay, and I will tell thee what the LORD hath said to me this night. And he said unto him, Say on.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd321.htm#001">Samuel</a> said, When thou wast little in thine own sight, wast thou not made the head of the tribes of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, and the LORD anointed thee <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> over <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> And the LORD sent thee on a <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#001">journey</a>, and said, Go and utterly destroy the sinners the Amalekites, and fight against them until they be consumed.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> Wherefore then didst thou not obey the voice of the LORD, but didst <a href="../ebd/ebd136.htm#004">fly</a> upon the spoil, and didst evil in the sight of the LORD?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#002">Saul</a> said unto <a href="../ebd/ebd321.htm#001">Samuel</a>, Yea, I have obeyed the voice of the LORD, and have gone the way which the LORD sent me, and have brought <a href="../ebd/ebd011.htm#008">Agag</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#009">Amalek</a>, and have utterly destroyed the Amalekites.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> But the people took of the spoil, <a href="../ebd/ebd333.htm#005">sheep</a> and oxen, the chief of the things which should have been utterly destroyed, to <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#002">sacrifice</a> unto the LORD thy God in <a href="../ebd/ebd149.htm#001">Gilgal</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd321.htm#001">Samuel</a> said, Hath the LORD as great delight in burnt offerings and sacrifices, as in obeying the voice of the LORD? Behold, to obey is better than <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#002">sacrifice</a>, and to hearken than the <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#005">fat</a> of rams.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> For rebellion is as the <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#007">witchcraft</a>, and stubbornness is as iniquity and <a href="../ebd/ebd186.htm#007">idolatry</a>. Because thou hast rejected the word of the LORD, he hath also rejected thee from being <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#002">Saul</a> said unto <a href="../ebd/ebd321.htm#001">Samuel</a>, I have sinned: for I have transgressed the commandment of the LORD, and thy words: because I feared the people, and obeyed their voice.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> Now therefore, I pray thee, <a href="../ebd/ebd285.htm#001">pardon</a> my <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a>, and turn again with me, that I may <a href="../ebd/ebd384.htm#002">worship</a> the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd321.htm#001">Samuel</a> said unto <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#002">Saul</a>, I will not return with thee: for thou hast rejected the word of the LORD, and the LORD hath rejected thee from being <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> over <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> And as <a href="../ebd/ebd321.htm#001">Samuel</a> turned about to go away, he laid hold upon the skirt of his <a href="../ebd/ebd240.htm#009">mantle</a>, and it <a href="../ebd/ebd310.htm#007">rent</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="028">28</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd321.htm#001">Samuel</a> said unto him, The LORD hath <a href="../ebd/ebd310.htm#007">rent</a> the kingdom of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> from thee this day, and hath given it to a neighbour of thine, that is better than thou.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="029">29</a> And also the Strength of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> will not lie nor repent: for he is not a man, that he should repent.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="030">30</a> Then he said, I have sinned: yet honour me now, I pray thee, before the elders of my people, and before <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, and turn again with me, that I may <a href="../ebd/ebd384.htm#002">worship</a> the LORD thy God.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="031">31</a> So <a href="../ebd/ebd321.htm#001">Samuel</a> turned again after <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#002">Saul</a>; and <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#002">Saul</a> worshipped the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="032">32</a> Then said <a href="../ebd/ebd321.htm#001">Samuel</a>, Bring ye hither to me <a href="../ebd/ebd011.htm#008">Agag</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of the Amalekites. And <a href="../ebd/ebd011.htm#008">Agag</a> came unto him delicately. And <a href="../ebd/ebd011.htm#008">Agag</a> said, Surely the bitterness of death is past.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="033">33</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd321.htm#001">Samuel</a> said, As the <a href="../ebd/ebd354.htm#003">sword</a> hath made women childless, so shall thy mother be childless among women. And <a href="../ebd/ebd321.htm#001">Samuel</a> hewed <a href="../ebd/ebd011.htm#008">Agag</a> in <a href="../ebd/ebd295.htm#002">pieces</a> before the LORD in <a href="../ebd/ebd149.htm#001">Gilgal</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="034">34</a> Then <a href="../ebd/ebd321.htm#001">Samuel</a> went to <a href="../ebd/ebd306.htm#005">Ramah</a>; and <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#002">Saul</a> went up to his house to <a href="../ebd/ebd147.htm#008">Gibeah</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#002">Saul</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="035">35</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd321.htm#001">Samuel</a> came no more to see <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#002">Saul</a> until the day of his death: nevertheless <a href="../ebd/ebd321.htm#001">Samuel</a> mourned for <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#002">Saul</a>: and the LORD repented that he had made <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#002">Saul</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> over <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>.<br>
 </p>
