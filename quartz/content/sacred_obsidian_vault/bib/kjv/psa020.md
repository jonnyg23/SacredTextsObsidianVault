 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 20</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> The LORD hear thee in the day of trouble; the name of the God of <a href="../ebd/ebd194.htm#007">Jacob</a> defend thee;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Send thee help from the <a href="../ebd/ebd321.htm#005">sanctuary</a>, and strengthen thee out of <a href="../ebd/ebd394.htm#002">Zion</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Remember all thy offerings, and accept thy burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#002">sacrifice</a>; <a href="../ebd/ebd326.htm#008">Selah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Grant thee according to thine own heart, and fulfil all thy counsel.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> We will rejoice in thy <a href="../ebd/ebd320.htm#003">salvation</a>, and in the name of our God we will set up our banners: the LORD fulfil all thy petitions.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Now know I that the LORD saveth his anointed; he will hear him from his holy <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#006">heaven</a> with the saving strength of his right hand.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Some trust in chariots, and some in horses: but we will remember the name of the LORD our God.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> They are brought down and fallen: but we are risen, and stand upright.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> Save, LORD: let the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> hear us when we call.<br>
 </p>
