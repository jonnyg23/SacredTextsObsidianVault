 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Leviticus Chapter 19</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> And the LORD spake unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Speak unto all the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, and say unto them, Ye shall be holy: for I the LORD your God am holy.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Ye shall fear every man his mother, and his father, and keep my sabbaths: I am the LORD your God.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Turn ye not unto idols, nor make to yourselves molten gods: I am the LORD your God.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And if ye offer a <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#002">sacrifice</a> of peace offerings unto the LORD, ye shall offer it at your own will.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> It shall be eaten the same day ye offer it, and on the morrow: and if ought remain until the third day, it shall be burnt in the fire.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And if it be eaten at all on the third day, it is abominable; it shall not be accepted.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> Therefore every one that eateth it shall bear his iniquity, because he hath profaned the hallowed thing of the LORD: and that soul shall be cut off from among his people.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And when ye reap the <a href="../ebd/ebd165.htm#008">harvest</a> of your land, thou shalt not wholly reap the corners of thy field, neither shalt thou gather the gleanings of thy <a href="../ebd/ebd165.htm#008">harvest</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And thou shalt not <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#003">glean</a> thy vineyard, neither shalt thou gather every <a href="../ebd/ebd154.htm#004">grape</a> of thy vineyard; thou shalt leave them for the <a href="../ebd/ebd298.htm#006">poor</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd351.htm#009">stranger</a>: I am the LORD your God.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> Ye shall not steal, neither deal falsely, neither lie one to another.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And ye shall not swear by my name falsely, neither shalt thou profane the name of thy God: I am the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> Thou shalt not defraud thy neighbour, neither rob him: the <a href="../ebd/ebd378.htm#004">wages</a> of him that is hired shall not abide with thee all night until the morning.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> Thou shalt not <a href="../ebd/ebd094.htm#003">curse</a> the deaf, nor put a stumblingblock before the <a href="../ebd/ebd060.htm#009">blind</a>, but shalt fear thy God: I am the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> Ye shall do no unrighteousness in judgment: thou shalt not respect the person of the <a href="../ebd/ebd298.htm#006">poor</a>, nor honor the person of the mighty: but in <a href="../ebd/ebd313.htm#004">righteousness</a> shalt thou <a href="../ebd/ebd213.htm#005">judge</a> thy neighbour.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> Thou shalt not go up and down as a talebearer among thy people: neither shalt thou stand against the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> of thy neighbour; I am the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> Thou shalt not hate thy brother in thine heart: thou shalt in any <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#004">wise</a> rebuke thy neighbour, and not suffer <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> upon him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> Thou shalt not avenge, nor bear any grudge against the children of thy people, but thou shalt love thy neighbour as thyself: I am the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> Ye shall keep my statutes. Thou shalt not let thy <a href="../ebd/ebd074.htm#002">cattle</a> gender with a diverse kind: thou shalt not sow thy field with mingled seed: neither shall a garment mingled of <a href="../ebd/ebd229.htm#008">linen</a> and woollen come upon thee.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> And whosoever lieth carnally with a woman, that is a bondmaid, betrothed to an husband, and not at all redeemed, nor freedom given her; she shall be scourged; they shall not be put to death, because she was not free.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> And he shall bring his trespass <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> unto the LORD, unto the door of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>, even a <a href="../ebd/ebd306.htm#003">ram</a> for a trespass <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> shall make an <a href="../ebd/ebd036.htm#004">atonement</a> for him with the <a href="../ebd/ebd306.htm#003">ram</a> of the trespass <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> before the LORD for his <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> which he hath done: and the <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> which he hath done shall be forgiven him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> And when ye shall come into the land, and shall have planted all manner of trees for food, then ye shall count the <a href="../ebd/ebd139.htm#000">fruit</a> thereof as uncircumcised: three years shall it be as uncircumcised unto you: it shall not be eaten of.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> But in the fourth year all the <a href="../ebd/ebd139.htm#000">fruit</a> thereof shall be holy to praise the LORD withal.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> And in the fifth year shall ye eat of the <a href="../ebd/ebd139.htm#000">fruit</a> thereof, that it may yield unto you the increase thereof: I am the LORD your God.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> Ye shall not eat any thing with the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a>: neither shall ye use enchantment, nor observe times.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> Ye shall not round the corners of your heads, neither shalt thou mar the corners of thy <a href="../ebd/ebd049.htm#000">beard</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="028">28</a> Ye shall not make any cuttings in your <a href="../ebd/ebd135.htm#007">flesh</a> for the dead, nor print any marks upon you: I am the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="029">29</a> Do not prostitute thy daughter, to cause her to be a whore; lest the land fall to whoredom, and the land become full of wickedness.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="030">30</a> Ye shall keep my sabbaths, and reverence my <a href="../ebd/ebd321.htm#005">sanctuary</a>: I am the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="031">31</a> Regard not them that have familiar spirits, neither seek after wizards, to be defiled by them: I am the LORD your God.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="032">32</a> Thou shalt rise up before the hoary head, and honour the face of the old man, and fear thy God: I am the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="033">33</a> And if a <a href="../ebd/ebd351.htm#009">stranger</a> sojourn with thee in your land, ye shall not vex him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="034">34</a> But the <a href="../ebd/ebd351.htm#009">stranger</a> that dwelleth with you shall be unto you as one born among you, and thou shalt love him as thyself; for ye were strangers in the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a>: I am the LORD your God.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="035">35</a> Ye shall do no unrighteousness in judgment, in meteyard, in weight, or in measure.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="036">36</a> Just balances, just <a href="../ebd/ebd380.htm#006">weights</a>, a just <a href="../ebd/ebd122.htm#003">ephah</a>, and a just hin, shall ye have: I am the LORD your God, which brought you out of the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="037">37</a> Therefore shall ye observe all my statutes, and all my judgments, and do them: I am the LORD.<br>
 </p>
