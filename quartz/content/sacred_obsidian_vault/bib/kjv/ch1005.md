 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: 1 Chronicles Chapter 5</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Now the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd311.htm#009">Reuben</a> the firstborn of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, (for he was the firstborn; but forasmuch as he defiled his father's bed, his <a href="../ebd/ebd059.htm#004">birthright</a> was given unto the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#005">Joseph</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>: and the genealogy is not to be reckoned after the <a href="../ebd/ebd059.htm#004">birthright</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> For <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a> prevailed above his brethren, and of him came the chief ruler; but the <a href="../ebd/ebd059.htm#004">birthright</a> was Joseph's:)<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> The sons, I say, of <a href="../ebd/ebd311.htm#009">Reuben</a> the firstborn of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> were, Hanoch, and <a href="../ebd/ebd283.htm#000">Pallu</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd177.htm#007">Hezron</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd072.htm#007">Carmi</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> The sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd208.htm#005">Joel</a>; <a href="../ebd/ebd334.htm#005">Shemaiah</a> his son, <a href="../ebd/ebd151.htm#009">Gog</a> his son, <a href="../ebd/ebd338.htm#002">Shimei</a> his son,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd252.htm#005">Micah</a> his son, Reaia his son, <a href="../ebd/ebd038.htm#004">Baal</a> his son,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Beerah his son, whom Tilgathpilneser <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd035.htm#005">Assyria</a> carried away <a href="../ebd/ebd072.htm#001">captive</a>: he was <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#004">prince</a> of the Reubenites.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And his brethren by their families, when the genealogy of their generations was reckoned, were the chief, <a href="../ebd/ebd203.htm#000">Jeiel</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd389.htm#006">Zechariah</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd051.htm#006">Bela</a> the son of Azaz, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd334.htm#003">Shema</a>, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd208.htm#005">Joel</a>, who dwelt in <a href="../ebd/ebd032.htm#001">Aroer</a>, even unto <a href="../ebd/ebd268.htm#004">Nebo</a> and Baalmeon:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And eastward he inhabited unto the entering in of the <a href="../ebd/ebd381.htm#005">wilderness</a> from the river <a href="../ebd/ebd127.htm#001">Euphrates</a>: because their <a href="../ebd/ebd074.htm#002">cattle</a> were multiplied in the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd148.htm#009">Gilead</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And in the days of <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#002">Saul</a> they made war with the Hagarites, who fell by their hand: and they dwelt in their tents throughout all the <a href="../ebd/ebd111.htm#000">east</a> land of <a href="../ebd/ebd148.htm#009">Gilead</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd140.htm#007">Gad</a> dwelt over against them, in the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd046.htm#003">Bashan</a> unto <a href="../ebd/ebd319.htm#000">Salcah</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd208.htm#005">Joel</a> the chief, and Shapham the next, and <a href="../ebd/ebd192.htm#007">Jaanai</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd331.htm#005">Shaphat</a> in <a href="../ebd/ebd046.htm#003">Bashan</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> And their brethren of the house of their fathers were, <a href="../ebd/ebd252.htm#009">Michael</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd251.htm#001">Meshullam</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd332.htm#006">Sheba</a>, and Jorai, and <a href="../ebd/ebd194.htm#003">Jachan</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd392.htm#002">Zia</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#008">Heber</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> These are the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd003.htm#003">Abihail</a> the son of Huri, the son of Jaroah, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd148.htm#009">Gilead</a>, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd252.htm#009">Michael</a>, the son of Jeshishai, the son of Jahdo, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd067.htm#003">Buz</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> Ahi the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd001.htm#001">Abdiel</a>, the son of Guni, chief of the house of their fathers.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> And they dwelt in <a href="../ebd/ebd148.htm#009">Gilead</a> in <a href="../ebd/ebd046.htm#003">Bashan</a>, and in her towns, and in all the <a href="../ebd/ebd352.htm#007">suburbs</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd331.htm#009">Sharon</a>, upon their borders.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> All these were reckoned by genealogies in the days of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#000">Jotham</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>, and in the days of <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#000">Jeroboam</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> The sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd311.htm#009">Reuben</a>, and the Gadites, and half the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd240.htm#002">Manasseh</a>, of valiant men, men able to bear <a href="../ebd/ebd066.htm#000">buckler</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd354.htm#003">sword</a>, and to shoot with <a href="../ebd/ebd063.htm#003">bow</a>, and skilful in war, were four and forty thousand <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> hundred and threescore, that went out to the war.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> And they made war with the Hagarites, with <a href="../ebd/ebd206.htm#001">Jetur</a>, and Nephish, and <a href="../ebd/ebd274.htm#007">Nodab</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> And they were helped against them, and the Hagarites were delivered into their hand, and all that were with them: for they cried to God in the battle, and he was intreated of them; because they put their trust in him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> And they took away their <a href="../ebd/ebd074.htm#002">cattle</a>; of their camels fifty thousand, and of <a href="../ebd/ebd333.htm#005">sheep</a> two hundred and fifty thousand, and of asses two thousand, and of men an hundred thousand.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> For there fell down many slain, because the war was of God. And they dwelt in their steads until the <a href="../ebd/ebd072.htm#002">captivity</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> And the children of the half <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd240.htm#002">Manasseh</a> dwelt in the land: they increased from <a href="../ebd/ebd046.htm#003">Bashan</a> unto Baalhermon and <a href="../ebd/ebd327.htm#004">Senir</a>, and unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#007">mount</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd175.htm#006">Hermon</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> And these were the heads of the house of their fathers, even <a href="../ebd/ebd122.htm#004">Epher</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd190.htm#004">Ishi</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd116.htm#006">Eliel</a>, and Azriel, and <a href="../ebd/ebd203.htm#006">Jeremiah</a>, and Hodaviah, and Jahdiel, mighty men of valour, famous men, and heads of the house of their fathers.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> And they transgressed against the God of their fathers, and went a whoring after the gods of the people of the land, whom God destroyed before them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> And the God of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> stirred up the <a href="../ebd/ebd349.htm#009">spirit</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd302.htm#002">Pul</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd035.htm#005">Assyria</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd349.htm#009">spirit</a> of Tilgathpilneser <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd035.htm#005">Assyria</a>, and he carried them away, even the Reubenites, and the Gadites, and the half <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd240.htm#002">Manasseh</a>, and brought them unto <a href="../ebd/ebd159.htm#005">Halah</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd156.htm#008">Habor</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd163.htm#005">Hara</a>, and to the river <a href="../ebd/ebd153.htm#009">Gozan</a>, unto this day.<br>
 </p>
