 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Proverbs Chapter 2</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> My son, if thou wilt receive my words, and hide my commandments with thee;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> So that thou incline thine <a href="../ebd/ebd110.htm#004">ear</a> unto wisdom, and apply thine heart to understanding;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Yea, if thou criest after knowledge, and liftest up thy voice for understanding;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> If thou seekest her as <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#000">silver</a>, and searchest for her as for hid treasures;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Then shalt thou understand the fear of the LORD, and find the knowledge of God.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> For the LORD giveth wisdom: out of his mouth cometh knowledge and understanding.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> He layeth up sound wisdom for the righteous: he is a <a href="../ebd/ebd066.htm#000">buckler</a> to them that walk uprightly.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> He keepeth the paths of judgment, and preserveth the way of his saints.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> Then shalt thou understand <a href="../ebd/ebd313.htm#004">righteousness</a>, and judgment, and equity; yea, every good path.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> When wisdom entereth into thine heart, and knowledge is pleasant unto thy soul;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> Discretion shall preserve thee, understanding shall keep thee:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> To deliver thee from the way of the evil man, from the man that speaketh froward things;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> Who leave the paths of uprightness, to walk in the ways of <a href="../ebd/ebd097.htm#008">darkness</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> Who rejoice to do evil, and delight in the frowardness of the wicked;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> Whose ways are crooked, and they froward in their paths:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> To deliver thee from the strange woman, even from the <a href="../ebd/ebd351.htm#009">stranger</a> which flattereth with her words;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> Which forsaketh the guide of her youth, and forgetteth the <a href="../ebd/ebd091.htm#008">covenant</a> of her God.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> For her house inclineth unto death, and her paths unto the dead.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> None that go unto her return again, neither take they hold of the paths of life.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> That thou mayest walk in the way of good men, and keep the paths of the righteous.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> For the upright shall dwell in the land, and the perfect shall remain in it.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> But the wicked shall be cut off from the earth, and the transgressors shall be rooted out of it.<br>
 </p>
