 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 4</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Hear me when I call, O God of my <a href="../ebd/ebd313.htm#004">righteousness</a>: thou hast enlarged me when I was in distress; have <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> upon me, and hear my <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#000">prayer</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> O ye sons of men, how long will ye turn my <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a> into shame? how long will ye love vanity, and seek after <a href="../ebd/ebd225.htm#008">leasing</a>? <a href="../ebd/ebd326.htm#008">Selah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> But know that the LORD hath set apart him that is godly for himself: the LORD will hear when I call unto him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Stand in awe, and <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> not: commune with your own heart upon your bed, and be still. <a href="../ebd/ebd326.htm#008">Selah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Offer the sacrifices of <a href="../ebd/ebd313.htm#004">righteousness</a>, and put your trust in the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> There be many that say, Who will shew us any good? LORD, lift thou up the light of thy countenance upon us.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Thou hast put gladness in my heart, more than in the time that their <a href="../ebd/ebd090.htm#006">corn</a> and their <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#000">wine</a> increased.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> I will both lay me down in peace, and sleep: for thou, LORD, only makest me dwell in safety.<br>
 </p>
