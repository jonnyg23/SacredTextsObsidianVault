 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 136</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> O give thanks unto the LORD; for he is good: for his <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> endureth for ever.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> O give thanks unto the God of gods: for his <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> endureth for ever.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> O give thanks to the Lord of lords: for his <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> endureth for ever.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> To him who alone doeth great wonders: for his <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> endureth for ever.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> To him that by wisdom made the heavens: for his <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> endureth for ever.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> To him that stretched out the earth above the waters: for his <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> endureth for ever.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> To him that made great lights: for his <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> endureth for ever:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> The <a href="../ebd/ebd353.htm#001">sun</a> to rule by day: for his <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> endureth for ever:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> The <a href="../ebd/ebd259.htm#004">moon</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd350.htm#008">stars</a> to rule by night: for his <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> endureth for ever.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> To him that smote <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a> in their firstborn: for his <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> endureth for ever:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And brought out <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> from among them: for his <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> endureth for ever:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> With a strong hand, and with a stretched out arm: for his <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> endureth for ever.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> To him which divided the Red sea into parts: for his <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> endureth for ever:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> And made <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> to pass through the midst of it: for his <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> endureth for ever:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> But overthrew <a href="../ebd/ebd292.htm#005">Pharaoh</a> and his <a href="../ebd/ebd183.htm#007">host</a> in the Red sea: for his <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> endureth for ever.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> To him which led his people through the <a href="../ebd/ebd381.htm#005">wilderness</a>: for his <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> endureth for ever.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> To him which smote great kings: for his <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> endureth for ever:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> And slew famous kings: for his <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> endureth for ever:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd343.htm#002">Sihon</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd021.htm#006">Amorites</a>: for his <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> endureth for ever:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#002">Og</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd046.htm#003">Bashan</a>: for his <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> endureth for ever:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> And gave their land for an heritage: for his <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> endureth for ever:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> Even an heritage unto <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> his servant: for his <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> endureth for ever.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> Who remembered us in our low estate: for his <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> endureth for ever:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> And hath redeemed us from our enemies: for his <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> endureth for ever.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> Who giveth food to all <a href="../ebd/ebd135.htm#007">flesh</a>: for his <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> endureth for ever.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> O give thanks unto the God of <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#006">heaven</a>: for his <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> endureth for ever.<br>
 </p>
