 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: 2 Timothy Chapter 3</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> This know also, that in the last days perilous times shall come.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> For men shall be lovers of their own selves, covetous, boasters, proud, blasphemers, disobedient to parents, unthankful, unholy,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Without natural <a href="../ebd/ebd011.htm#004">affection</a>, trucebreakers, false accusers, incontinent, fierce, despisers of those that are good,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Traitors, heady, highminded, lovers of pleasures more than lovers of God;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Having a form of <a href="../ebd/ebd151.htm#007">godliness</a>, but denying the power thereof: from such turn away.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> For of this sort are they which creep into houses, and lead <a href="../ebd/ebd072.htm#001">captive</a> silly women laden with sins, led away with divers lusts,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Ever learning, and never able to come to the knowledge of the truth.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> Now as <a href="../ebd/ebd196.htm#009">Jannes</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd196.htm#006">Jambres</a> withstood <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, so do these also resist the truth: men of corrupt minds, <a href="../ebd/ebd311.htm#003">reprobate</a> concerning the faith.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> But they shall proceed no further: for their folly shall be manifest unto all men, as their's also was.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> But thou hast fully known my doctrine, manner of life, purpose, faith, longsuffering, <a href="../ebd/ebd077.htm#009">charity</a>, patience,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> Persecutions, <a href="../ebd/ebd011.htm#006">afflictions</a>, which came unto me at <a href="../ebd/ebd025.htm#001">Antioch</a>, at <a href="../ebd/ebd186.htm#003">Iconium</a>, at <a href="../ebd/ebd234.htm#003">Lystra</a>; what persecutions I endured: but out of them all the Lord delivered me.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> Yea, and all that will live godly in <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a> shall suffer <a href="../ebd/ebd290.htm#007">persecution</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> But evil men and seducers shall <a href="../ebd/ebd380.htm#000">wax</a> worse and worse, deceiving, and being deceived.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> But continue thou in the things which thou hast learned and hast been assured of, knowing of whom thou hast learned them;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And that from a child thou hast known the holy scriptures, which are able to make thee <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#004">wise</a> unto <a href="../ebd/ebd320.htm#003">salvation</a> through faith which is in <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> All <a href="../ebd/ebd324.htm#005">scripture</a> is given by <a href="../ebd/ebd188.htm#006">inspiration</a> of God, and is profitable for doctrine, for reproof, for correction, for instruction in <a href="../ebd/ebd313.htm#004">righteousness</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> That the man of God may be perfect, throughly furnished unto all good works.<br>
 </p>
