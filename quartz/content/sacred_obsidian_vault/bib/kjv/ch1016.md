 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: 1 Chronicles Chapter 16</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> So they brought the <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#000">ark</a> of God, and set it in the midst of the <a href="../ebd/ebd361.htm#009">tent</a> that <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> had pitched for it: and they offered burnt sacrifices and peace offerings before God.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> And when <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> had made an end of <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> the burnt offerings and the peace offerings, he blessed the people in the name of the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And he dealt to every one of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, both man and woman, to every one a loaf of <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#006">bread</a>, and a good piece of <a href="../ebd/ebd135.htm#007">flesh</a>, and a <a href="../ebd/ebd135.htm#002">flagon</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#000">wine</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And he appointed certain of the Levites to <a href="../ebd/ebd255.htm#007">minister</a> before the <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#000">ark</a> of the LORD, and to record, and to thank and praise the LORD God of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd033.htm#002">Asaph</a> the chief, and next to him <a href="../ebd/ebd389.htm#006">Zechariah</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd203.htm#000">Jeiel</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd334.htm#009">Shemiramoth</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd200.htm#003">Jehiel</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd244.htm#006">Mattithiah</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd115.htm#008">Eliab</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd052.htm#003">Benaiah</a>, and Obededom: and <a href="../ebd/ebd203.htm#000">Jeiel</a> with psalteries and with harps; but <a href="../ebd/ebd033.htm#002">Asaph</a> made a sound with <a href="../ebd/ebd095.htm#001">cymbals</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd052.htm#003">Benaiah</a> also and <a href="../ebd/ebd195.htm#006">Jahaziel</a> the priests with <a href="../ebd/ebd373.htm#001">trumpets</a> continually before the <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#000">ark</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd091.htm#008">covenant</a> of God.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Then on that day <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> delivered first this psalm to thank the LORD into the hand of <a href="../ebd/ebd033.htm#002">Asaph</a> and his brethren.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> Give thanks unto the LORD, call upon his name, make known his deeds among the people.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> Sing unto him, sing <a href="../ebd/ebd301.htm#005">psalms</a> unto him, talk ye of all his wondrous works.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">Glory</a> ye in his holy name: let the heart of them rejoice that seek the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> Seek the LORD and his strength, seek his face continually.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> Remember his marvellous works that he hath done, his wonders, and the judgments of his mouth;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> O ye seed of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> his servant, ye children of <a href="../ebd/ebd194.htm#007">Jacob</a>, his <a href="../ebd/ebd081.htm#008">chosen</a> ones.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> He is the LORD our God; his judgments are in all the earth.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> Be ye mindful always of his <a href="../ebd/ebd091.htm#008">covenant</a>; the word which he commanded to a thousand generations;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> Even of the <a href="../ebd/ebd091.htm#008">covenant</a> which he made with <a href="../ebd/ebd005.htm#005">Abraham</a>, and of his <a href="../ebd/ebd276.htm#000">oath</a> unto <a href="../ebd/ebd189.htm#006">Isaac</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> And hath confirmed the same to <a href="../ebd/ebd194.htm#007">Jacob</a> for a law, and to <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> for an <a href="../ebd/ebd127.htm#007">everlasting</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd091.htm#008">covenant</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> Saying, Unto thee will I give the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#005">Canaan</a>, the lot of your inheritance;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> When ye were but few, even a few, and strangers in it.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> And when they went from nation to nation, and from one kingdom to another people;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> He suffered no man to do them wrong: yea, he reproved kings for their sakes,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> Saying, Touch not mine anointed, and do my prophets no harm.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> Sing unto the LORD, all the earth; shew forth from day to day his <a href="../ebd/ebd320.htm#003">salvation</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> Declare his <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a> among the <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#005">heathen</a>; his marvellous works among all nations.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> For great is the LORD, and greatly to be praised: he also is to be feared above all gods.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> For all the gods of the people are idols: but the LORD made the heavens.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">Glory</a> and honour are in his presence; strength and gladness are in his place.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="028">28</a> Give unto the LORD, ye kindreds of the people, give unto the LORD <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a> and strength.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="029">29</a> Give unto the LORD the <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a> due unto his name: bring an <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, and come before him: <a href="../ebd/ebd384.htm#002">worship</a> the LORD in the beauty of <a href="../ebd/ebd180.htm#006">holiness</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="030">30</a> Fear before him, all the earth: the world also shall be stable, that it be not moved.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="031">31</a> Let the heavens be glad, and let the earth rejoice: and let men say among the nations, The LORD reigneth.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="032">32</a> Let the sea roar, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd139.htm#007">fulness</a> thereof: let the fields rejoice, and all that is therein.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="033">33</a> Then shall the trees of the <a href="../ebd/ebd383.htm#003">wood</a> sing out at the presence of the LORD, because he cometh to <a href="../ebd/ebd213.htm#005">judge</a> the earth.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="034">34</a> O give thanks unto the LORD; for he is good; for his <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> endureth for ever.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="035">35</a> And say ye, Save us, O God of our <a href="../ebd/ebd320.htm#003">salvation</a>, and gather us together, and deliver us from the <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#005">heathen</a>, that we may give thanks to thy holy name, and <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a> in thy praise.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="036">36</a> Blessed be the LORD God of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> for ever and ever. And all the people said, <a href="../ebd/ebd020.htm#001">Amen</a>, and praised the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="037">37</a> So he left there before the <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#000">ark</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd091.htm#008">covenant</a> of the LORD <a href="../ebd/ebd033.htm#002">Asaph</a> and his brethren, to <a href="../ebd/ebd255.htm#007">minister</a> before the <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#000">ark</a> continually, as every day's work required:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="038">38</a> And Obededom with their brethren, threescore and eight; Obededom also the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd199.htm#009">Jeduthun</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd183.htm#005">Hosah</a> to be porters:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="039">39</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd386.htm#008">Zadok</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a>, and his brethren the priests, before the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the LORD in the high place that was at <a href="../ebd/ebd148.htm#002">Gibeon</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="040">40</a> To offer burnt offerings unto the LORD upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> of the burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> continually morning and evening, and to do according to all that is written in the law of the LORD, which he commanded <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="041">41</a> And with them <a href="../ebd/ebd173.htm#009">Heman</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd199.htm#009">Jeduthun</a>, and the rest that were <a href="../ebd/ebd081.htm#008">chosen</a>, who were expressed by name, to give thanks to the LORD, because his <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> endureth for ever;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="042">42</a> And with them <a href="../ebd/ebd173.htm#009">Heman</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd199.htm#009">Jeduthun</a> with <a href="../ebd/ebd373.htm#001">trumpets</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd095.htm#001">cymbals</a> for those that should make a sound, and with musical instruments of God. And the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd199.htm#009">Jeduthun</a> were porters.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="043">43</a> And all the people departed every man to his house: and <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> returned to <a href="../ebd/ebd060.htm#008">bless</a> his house.<br>
 </p>
