 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Job Chapter 38</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Then the LORD answered Job out of the whirlwind, and said,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Who is this that darkeneth counsel by words without knowledge?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Gird up now thy loins like a man; for I will demand of thee, and answer thou me.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Where wast thou when I laid the foundations of the earth? declare, if thou hast understanding.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Who hath laid the measures thereof, if thou knowest? or who hath stretched the line upon it?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Whereupon are the foundations thereof fastened? or who laid the corner <a href="../ebd/ebd351.htm#005">stone</a> thereof;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> When the morning <a href="../ebd/ebd350.htm#008">stars</a> sang together, and all the sons of God shouted for joy?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> Or who shut up the sea with <a href="../ebd/ebd105.htm#009">doors</a>, when it brake forth, as if it had issued out of the womb?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> When I made the <a href="../ebd/ebd085.htm#001">cloud</a> the garment thereof, and thick <a href="../ebd/ebd097.htm#008">darkness</a> a swaddlingband for it,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And brake up for it my decreed place, and set bars and <a href="../ebd/ebd105.htm#009">doors</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And said, Hitherto shalt thou come, but no further: and here shall thy proud waves be stayed?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> Hast thou commanded the morning since thy days; and caused the <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#009">dayspring</a> to know his place;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> That it might take hold of the ends of the earth, that the wicked might be shaken out of it?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> It is turned as <a href="../ebd/ebd084.htm#004">clay</a> to the <a href="../ebd/ebd325.htm#002">seal</a>; and they stand as a garment.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And from the wicked their light is withholden, and the high arm shall be broken.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> Hast thou entered into the springs of the sea? or hast thou walked in the search of the depth?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> Have the gates of death been opened unto thee? or hast thou seen the <a href="../ebd/ebd105.htm#009">doors</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd330.htm#002">shadow</a> of death?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> Hast thou perceived the breadth of the earth? declare if thou knowest it all.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> Where is the way where light dwelleth? and as for <a href="../ebd/ebd097.htm#008">darkness</a>, where is the place thereof,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> That thou shouldest take it to the bound thereof, and that thou shouldest know the paths to the house thereof?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> Knowest thou it, because thou wast then born? or because the number of thy days is great?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> Hast thou entered into the treasures of the <a href="../ebd/ebd347.htm#000">snow</a>? or hast thou seen the treasures of the <a href="../ebd/ebd159.htm#002">hail</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> Which I have reserved against the time of trouble, against the day of battle and war?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> By what way is the light parted, which scattereth the <a href="../ebd/ebd111.htm#000">east</a> wind upon the earth?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> Who hath divided a watercourse for the overflowing of waters, or a way for the <a href="../ebd/ebd229.htm#002">lightning</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd365.htm#003">thunder</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> To cause it to <a href="../ebd/ebd305.htm#008">rain</a> on the earth, where no man is; on the <a href="../ebd/ebd381.htm#005">wilderness</a>, wherein there is no man;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> To satisfy the desolate and waste ground; and to cause the bud of the tender <a href="../ebd/ebd174.htm#008">herb</a> to spring forth?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="028">28</a> Hath the <a href="../ebd/ebd305.htm#008">rain</a> a father? or who hath begotten the drops of <a href="../ebd/ebd102.htm#008">dew</a>?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="029">29</a> Out of whose womb came the <a href="../ebd/ebd186.htm#001">ice</a>? and the hoary <a href="../ebd/ebd138.htm#009">frost</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#006">heaven</a>, who hath gendered it?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="030">30</a> The waters are hid as with a <a href="../ebd/ebd351.htm#005">stone</a>, and the face of the <a href="../ebd/ebd100.htm#008">deep</a> is frozen.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="031">31</a> Canst thou bind the sweet influences of <a href="../ebd/ebd297.htm#006">Pleiades</a>, or loose the <a href="../ebd/ebd043.htm#003">bands</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd280.htm#005">Orion</a>?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="032">32</a> Canst thou bring forth <a href="../ebd/ebd244.htm#009">Mazzaroth</a> in his season? or canst thou guide <a href="../ebd/ebd029.htm#007">Arcturus</a> with his sons?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="033">33</a> Knowest thou the ordinances of <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#006">heaven</a>? canst thou set the dominion thereof in the earth?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="034">34</a> Canst thou lift up thy voice to the clouds, that abundance of waters may cover thee?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="035">35</a> Canst thou send lightnings, that they may go and say unto thee, Here we are?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="036">36</a> Who hath put wisdom in the inward parts? or who hath given understanding to the heart?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="037">37</a> Who can number the clouds in wisdom? or who can stay the bottles of <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#006">heaven</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="038">38</a> When the <a href="../ebd/ebd109.htm#008">dust</a> groweth into hardness, and the clods cleave <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#004">fast</a> together?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="039">39</a> Wilt thou hunt the prey for the lion? or fill the appetite of the young <a href="../ebd/ebd230.htm#001">lions</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="040">40</a> When they <a href="../ebd/ebd091.htm#002">couch</a> in their dens, and abide in the covert to lie in wait?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="041">41</a> Who provideth for the <a href="../ebd/ebd307.htm#008">raven</a> his food? when his young ones cry unto God, they wander for lack of meat.<br>
 </p>
