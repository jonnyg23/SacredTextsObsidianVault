 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Hosea Chapter 3</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Then said the LORD unto me, Go yet, love a woman beloved of her friend, yet an adulteress, according to the love of the LORD toward the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, who look to other gods, and love flagons of <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#000">wine</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> So I bought her to me for fifteen <a href="../ebd/ebd295.htm#002">pieces</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#000">silver</a>, and for an <a href="../ebd/ebd181.htm#000">homer</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd045.htm#003">barley</a>, and an half <a href="../ebd/ebd181.htm#000">homer</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd045.htm#003">barley</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And I said unto her, Thou shalt abide for me many days; thou shalt not play the <a href="../ebd/ebd164.htm#005">harlot</a>, and thou shalt not be for another man: so will I also be for thee.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> For the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> shall abide many days without a <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a>, and without a <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#004">prince</a>, and without a <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#002">sacrifice</a>, and without an image, and without an <a href="../ebd/ebd122.htm#008">ephod</a>, and without <a href="../ebd/ebd362.htm#002">teraphim</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Afterward shall the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> return, and seek the LORD their God, and <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> their <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a>; and shall fear the LORD and his <a href="../ebd/ebd153.htm#000">goodness</a> in the latter days.<br>
 </p>
