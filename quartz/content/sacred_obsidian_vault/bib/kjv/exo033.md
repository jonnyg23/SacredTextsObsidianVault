 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Exodus Chapter 33</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> And the LORD said unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, Depart, and go up hence, thou and the people which thou hast brought up out of the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a>, unto the land which I sware unto <a href="../ebd/ebd005.htm#005">Abraham</a>, to <a href="../ebd/ebd189.htm#006">Isaac</a>, and to <a href="../ebd/ebd194.htm#007">Jacob</a>, saying, Unto thy seed will I give it:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> And I will send an <a href="../ebd/ebd024.htm#001">angel</a> before thee; and I will drive out the <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#008">Canaanite</a>, the Amorite, and the Hittite, and the Perizzite, the Hivite, and the Jebusite:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Unto a land flowing with <a href="../ebd/ebd255.htm#000">milk</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd181.htm#001">honey</a>: for I will not go up in the midst of thee; for thou art a stiffnecked people: lest I consume thee in the way.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And when the people heard these evil tidings, they mourned: and no man did put on him his ornaments.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> For the LORD had said unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, Say unto the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, Ye are a stiffnecked people: I will come up into the midst of thee in a moment, and consume thee: therefore now put off thy ornaments from thee, that I may know what to do unto thee.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> stripped themselves of their ornaments by the <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#007">mount</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd181.htm#009">Horeb</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> took the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a>, and pitched it without the <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#002">camp</a>, afar off from the <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#002">camp</a>, and called it the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">Tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>. And it came to pass, that every one which sought the LORD went out unto the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>, which was without the <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#002">camp</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And it came to pass, when <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> went out unto the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a>, that all the people rose up, and stood every man at his <a href="../ebd/ebd361.htm#009">tent</a> door, and looked after <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, until he was gone into the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And it came to pass, as <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> entered into the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a>, the cloudy <a href="../ebd/ebd295.htm#007">pillar</a> descended, and stood at the door of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a>, and the Lord talked with <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And all the people saw the cloudy <a href="../ebd/ebd295.htm#007">pillar</a> stand at the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> door: and all the people rose up and worshipped, every man in his <a href="../ebd/ebd361.htm#009">tent</a> door.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And the LORD spake unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> face to face, as a man speaketh unto his friend. And he turned again into the <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#002">camp</a>: but his servant <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#006">Joshua</a>, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd275.htm#006">Nun</a>, a young man, departed not out of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> said unto the LORD, See, thou sayest unto me, Bring up this people: and thou hast not let me know whom thou wilt send with me. Yet thou hast said, I know thee by name, and thou hast also found <a href="../ebd/ebd154.htm#000">grace</a> in my sight.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> Now therefore, I pray thee, if I have found <a href="../ebd/ebd154.htm#000">grace</a> in thy sight, shew me now thy way, that I may know thee, that I may find <a href="../ebd/ebd154.htm#000">grace</a> in thy sight: and consider that this nation is thy people.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> And he said, My presence shall go with thee, and I will give thee rest.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And he said unto him, If thy presence go not with me, carry us not up hence.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> For wherein shall it be known here that I and thy people have found <a href="../ebd/ebd154.htm#000">grace</a> in thy sight? is it not in that thou goest with us? so shall we be separated, I and thy people, from all the people that are upon the face of the earth.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> And the LORD said unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, I will do this thing also that thou hast spoken: for thou hast found <a href="../ebd/ebd154.htm#000">grace</a> in my sight, and I know thee by name.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> And he said, I beseech thee, shew me thy <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> And he said, I will make all my <a href="../ebd/ebd153.htm#000">goodness</a> pass before thee, and I will proclaim the name of the LORD before thee; and will be gracious to whom I will be gracious, and will shew <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> on whom I will shew <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> And he said, Thou canst not see my face: for there shall no man see me, and live.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> And the LORD said, Behold, there is a place by me, and thou shalt stand upon a <a href="../ebd/ebd315.htm#001">rock</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> And it shall come to pass, while my <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a> passeth by, that I will put thee in a clift of the <a href="../ebd/ebd315.htm#001">rock</a>, and will cover thee with my hand while I pass by:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> And I will take away mine hand, and thou shalt see my back parts: but my face shall not be seen.<br>
 </p>
