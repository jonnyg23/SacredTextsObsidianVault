 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 134</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Behold, <a href="../ebd/ebd060.htm#008">bless</a> ye the LORD, all ye servants of the LORD, which by night stand in the house of the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Lift up your hands in the <a href="../ebd/ebd321.htm#005">sanctuary</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd060.htm#008">bless</a> the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> The LORD that made <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#006">heaven</a> and earth <a href="../ebd/ebd060.htm#008">bless</a> thee out of <a href="../ebd/ebd394.htm#002">Zion</a>.<br>
