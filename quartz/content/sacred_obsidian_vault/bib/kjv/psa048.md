 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 48</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Great is the LORD, and greatly to be praised in the city of our God, in the mountain of his <a href="../ebd/ebd180.htm#006">holiness</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Beautiful for situation, the joy of the whole earth, is <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#007">mount</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd394.htm#002">Zion</a>, on the sides of the north, the city of the great <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">King</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> God is known in her palaces for a refuge.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> For, lo, the kings were assembled, they passed by together.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> They saw it, and so they marvelled; they were troubled, and hasted away.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Fear took hold upon them there, and pain, as of a woman in travail.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Thou breakest the <a href="../ebd/ebd339.htm#003">ships</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd359.htm#002">Tarshish</a> with an <a href="../ebd/ebd111.htm#000">east</a> wind.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> As we have heard, so have we seen in the city of the LORD of hosts, in the city of our God: God will establish it for ever. <a href="../ebd/ebd326.htm#008">Selah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> We have thought of thy lovingkindness, O God, in the midst of thy <a href="../ebd/ebd361.htm#004">temple</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> According to thy name, O God, so is thy praise unto the ends of the earth: thy right hand is full of <a href="../ebd/ebd313.htm#004">righteousness</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> Let <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#007">mount</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd394.htm#002">Zion</a> rejoice, let the daughters of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a> be glad, because of thy judgments.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> Walk about <a href="../ebd/ebd394.htm#002">Zion</a>, and go round about her: tell the <a href="../ebd/ebd371.htm#002">towers</a> thereof.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> Mark ye well her <a href="../ebd/ebd066.htm#005">bulwarks</a>, consider her palaces; that ye may tell it to the <a href="../ebd/ebd145.htm#008">generation</a> following.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> For this God is our God for ever and ever: he will be our guide even unto death.<br>
 </p>
