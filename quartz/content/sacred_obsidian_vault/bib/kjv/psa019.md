 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 19</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> The heavens declare the <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a> of God; and the <a href="../ebd/ebd134.htm#000">firmament</a> sheweth his handywork.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Day unto day uttereth speech, and night unto night sheweth knowledge.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> There is no speech nor language, where their voice is not heard.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Their line is gone out through all the earth, and their words to the end of the world. In them hath he set a <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> for the <a href="../ebd/ebd353.htm#001">sun</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Which is as a bridegroom coming out of his <a href="../ebd/ebd076.htm#003">chamber</a>, and rejoiceth as a strong man to run a race.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> His going forth is from the end of the <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#006">heaven</a>, and his <a href="../ebd/ebd083.htm#006">circuit</a> unto the ends of it: and there is nothing hid from the heat thereof.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> The law of the LORD is perfect, converting the soul: the <a href="../ebd/ebd362.htm#008">testimony</a> of the LORD is sure, making <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#004">wise</a> the simple.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> The statutes of the LORD are right, rejoicing the heart: the commandment of the LORD is pure, enlightening the eyes.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> The fear of the LORD is <a href="../ebd/ebd084.htm#005">clean</a>, enduring for ever: the judgments of the LORD are true and righteous altogether.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> More to be desired are they than <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a>, yea, than much fine <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a>: sweeter also than <a href="../ebd/ebd181.htm#001">honey</a> and the honeycomb.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> Moreover by them is thy servant warned: and in keeping of them there is great reward.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> Who can understand his errors? cleanse thou me from secret faults.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> Keep back thy servant also from presumptuous sins; let them not have dominion over me: then shall I be upright, and I shall be innocent from the great transgression.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> Let the words of my mouth, and the meditation of my heart, be acceptable in thy sight, O LORD, my strength, and my <a href="../ebd/ebd308.htm#008">redeemer</a>.<br>
 </p>
