 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Numbers Chapter 4</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> And the LORD spake unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> and unto <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a>, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Take the sum of the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd222.htm#001">Kohath</a> from among the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd227.htm#006">Levi</a>, after their families, by the house of their fathers,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> From thirty years old and upward even until fifty years old, all that enter into the <a href="../ebd/ebd183.htm#007">host</a>, to do the work in the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> This shall be the service of the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd222.htm#001">Kohath</a> in the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>, about the most holy things:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And when the <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#002">camp</a> setteth forward, <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> shall come, and his sons, and they shall take down the covering vail, and cover the <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#000">ark</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd362.htm#008">testimony</a> with it:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And shall put thereon the covering of badgers' skins, and shall spread over it a cloth wholly of <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#003">blue</a>, and shall put in the staves thereof.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And upon the table of <a href="../ebd/ebd336.htm#008">shewbread</a> they shall spread a cloth of <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#003">blue</a>, and put thereon the dishes, and the spoons, and the bowls, and covers to cover withal: and the continual <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#006">bread</a> shall be thereon:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And they shall spread upon them a cloth of <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#005">scarlet</a>, and cover the same with a covering of badgers' skins, and shall put in the staves thereof.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And they shall take a cloth of <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#003">blue</a>, and cover the <a href="../ebd/ebd071.htm#001">candlestick</a> of the light, and his lamps, and his tongs, and his snuffdishes, and all the oil vessels thereof, wherewith they <a href="../ebd/ebd255.htm#007">minister</a> unto it:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And they shall put it and all the vessels thereof within a covering of badgers' skins, and shall put it upon a bar.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And upon the golden <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> they shall spread a cloth of <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#003">blue</a>, and cover it with a covering of badgers' skins, and shall put to the staves thereof:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And they shall take all the instruments of ministry, wherewith they <a href="../ebd/ebd255.htm#007">minister</a> in the <a href="../ebd/ebd321.htm#005">sanctuary</a>, and put them in a cloth of <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#003">blue</a>, and cover them with a covering of badgers' skins, and shall put them on a bar:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> And they shall take away the <a href="../ebd/ebd034.htm#000">ashes</a> from the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>, and spread a purple cloth thereon:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> And they shall put upon it all the vessels thereof, wherewith they <a href="../ebd/ebd255.htm#007">minister</a> about it, even the censers, the fleshhooks, and the shovels, and the basons, all the vessels of the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>; and they shall spread upon it a covering of badgers' skins, and put to the staves of it.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And when <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> and his sons have made an end of covering the <a href="../ebd/ebd321.htm#005">sanctuary</a>, and all the vessels of the <a href="../ebd/ebd321.htm#005">sanctuary</a>, as the <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#002">camp</a> is to set forward; after that, the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd222.htm#001">Kohath</a> shall come to bear it: but they shall not touch any holy thing, lest they die. These things are the <a href="../ebd/ebd066.htm#007">burden</a> of the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd222.htm#001">Kohath</a> in the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> And to the office of <a href="../ebd/ebd115.htm#000">Eleazar</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> pertaineth the oil for the light, and the sweet <a href="../ebd/ebd188.htm#002">incense</a>, and the daily meat <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, and the anointing oil, and the oversight of all the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a>, and of all that therein is, in the <a href="../ebd/ebd321.htm#005">sanctuary</a>, and in the vessels thereof.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> And the LORD spake unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> and unto <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> Cut ye not off the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of the families of the <a href="../ebd/ebd222.htm#002">Kohathites</a> from among the Levites:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> But thus do unto them, that they may live, and not die, when they approach unto the most holy things: <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> and his sons shall go in, and appoint them every one to his service and to his <a href="../ebd/ebd066.htm#007">burden</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> But they shall not go in to see when the holy things are covered, lest they die.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> And the LORD spake unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> Take also the sum of the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd146.htm#009">Gershon</a>, throughout the houses of their fathers, by their families;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> From thirty years old and upward until fifty years old shalt thou number them; all that enter in to perform the service, to do the work in the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> This is the service of the families of the Gershonites, to serve, and for burdens:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> And they shall bear the curtains of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>, his covering, and the covering of the badgers' skins that is above upon it, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd163.htm#001">hanging</a> for the door of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> And the hangings of the court, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd163.htm#001">hanging</a> for the door of the <a href="../ebd/ebd143.htm#008">gate</a> of the court, which is by the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> and by the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> round about, and their cords, and all the instruments of their service, and all that is made for them: so shall they serve.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> At the appointment of <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> and his sons shall be all the service of the sons of the Gershonites, in all their burdens, and in all their service: and ye shall appoint unto them in charge all their burdens.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="028">28</a> This is the service of the families of the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd146.htm#009">Gershon</a> in the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>: and their charge shall be under the hand of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#005">Ithamar</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="029">29</a> As for the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#000">Merari</a>, thou shalt number them after their families, by the house of their fathers;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="030">30</a> From thirty years old and upward even unto fifty years old shalt thou number them, every one that entereth into the service, to do the work of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="031">31</a> And this is the charge of their <a href="../ebd/ebd066.htm#007">burden</a>, according to all their service in the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>; the boards of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a>, and the bars thereof, and the pillars thereof, and sockets thereof,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="032">32</a> And the pillars of the court round about, and their sockets, and their pins, and their cords, with all their instruments, and with all their service: and by name ye shall reckon the instruments of the charge of their <a href="../ebd/ebd066.htm#007">burden</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="033">33</a> This is the service of the families of the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#000">Merari</a>, according to all their service, in the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>, under the hand of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#005">Ithamar</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="034">34</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> and the chief of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> numbered the sons of the <a href="../ebd/ebd222.htm#002">Kohathites</a> after their families, and after the house of their fathers,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="035">35</a> From thirty years old and upward even unto fifty years old, every one that entereth into the service, for the work in the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="036">36</a> And those that were numbered of them by their families were two thousand <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> hundred and fifty.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="037">37</a> These were they that were numbered of the families of the <a href="../ebd/ebd222.htm#002">Kohathites</a>, all that might do service in the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>, which <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> did number according to the commandment of the LORD by the hand of <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="038">38</a> And those that were numbered of the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd146.htm#009">Gershon</a>, throughout their families, and by the house of their fathers,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="039">39</a> From thirty years old and upward even unto fifty years old, every one that entereth into the service, for the work in the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="040">40</a> Even those that were numbered of them, throughout their families, by the house of their fathers, were two thousand and six hundred and thirty.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="041">41</a> These are they that were numbered of the families of the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd146.htm#009">Gershon</a>, of all that might do service in the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>, whom <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> did number according to the commandment of the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="042">42</a> And those that were numbered of the families of the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#000">Merari</a>, throughout their families, by the house of their fathers,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="043">43</a> From thirty years old and upward even unto fifty years old, every one that entereth into the service, for the work in the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="044">44</a> Even those that were numbered of them after their families, were three thousand and two hundred.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="045">45</a> These be those that were numbered of the families of the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#000">Merari</a>, whom <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> numbered according to the word of the LORD by the hand of <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="046">46</a> All those that were numbered of the Levites, whom <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> and the chief of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> numbered, after their families, and after the house of their fathers,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="047">47</a> From thirty years old and upward even unto fifty years old, every one that came to do the service of the ministry, and the service of the <a href="../ebd/ebd066.htm#007">burden</a> in the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="048">48</a> Even those that were numbered of them, were eight thousand and five hundred and fourscore,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="049">49</a> According to the commandment of the LORD they were numbered by the hand of <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, every one according to his service, and according to his <a href="../ebd/ebd066.htm#007">burden</a>: thus were they numbered of him, as the LORD commanded <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>.<br>
 </p>
