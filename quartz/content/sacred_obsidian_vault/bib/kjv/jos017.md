 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Joshua Chapter 17</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> There was also a lot for the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd240.htm#002">Manasseh</a>; for he was the firstborn of <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#005">Joseph</a>; to wit, for <a href="../ebd/ebd235.htm#006">Machir</a> the firstborn of <a href="../ebd/ebd240.htm#002">Manasseh</a>, the father of <a href="../ebd/ebd148.htm#009">Gilead</a>: because he was a man of war, therefore he had <a href="../ebd/ebd148.htm#009">Gilead</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd046.htm#003">Bashan</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> There was also a lot for the rest of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd240.htm#002">Manasseh</a> by their families; for the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd003.htm#000">Abiezer</a>, and for the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd172.htm#005">Helek</a>, and for the children of Asriel, and for the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd333.htm#003">Shechem</a>, and for the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd174.htm#006">Hepher</a>, and for the children of Shemida: these were the male children of <a href="../ebd/ebd240.htm#002">Manasseh</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#005">Joseph</a> by their families.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> But <a href="../ebd/ebd390.htm#002">Zelophehad</a>, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd174.htm#006">Hepher</a>, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd148.htm#009">Gilead</a>, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd235.htm#006">Machir</a>, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd240.htm#002">Manasseh</a>, had no sons, but daughters: and these are the names of his daughters, <a href="../ebd/ebd238.htm#000">Mahlah</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd274.htm#002">Noah</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd180.htm#003">Hoglah</a>, Milcah, and Tirzah.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And they came near before <a href="../ebd/ebd115.htm#000">Eleazar</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a>, and before <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#006">Joshua</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd275.htm#006">Nun</a>, and before the princes, saying, The LORD commanded <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> to give us an inheritance among our brethren. Therefore according to the commandment of the LORD he gave them an inheritance among the brethren of their father.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And there fell ten portions to <a href="../ebd/ebd240.htm#002">Manasseh</a>, beside the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd148.htm#009">Gilead</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd046.htm#003">Bashan</a>, which were on the other side <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#004">Jordan</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Because the daughters of <a href="../ebd/ebd240.htm#002">Manasseh</a> had an inheritance among his sons: and the rest of Manasseh's sons had the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd148.htm#009">Gilead</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And the coast of <a href="../ebd/ebd240.htm#002">Manasseh</a> was from <a href="../ebd/ebd033.htm#008">Asher</a> to <a href="../ebd/ebd253.htm#003">Michmethah</a>, that lieth before <a href="../ebd/ebd333.htm#003">Shechem</a>; and the border went along on the right hand unto the inhabitants of Entappuah.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> Now <a href="../ebd/ebd240.htm#002">Manasseh</a> had the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd358.htm#008">Tappuah</a>: but <a href="../ebd/ebd358.htm#008">Tappuah</a> on the border of <a href="../ebd/ebd240.htm#002">Manasseh</a> belonged to the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd123.htm#000">Ephraim</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And the coast descended unto the river <a href="../ebd/ebd215.htm#006">Kanah</a>, southward of the river: these cities of <a href="../ebd/ebd123.htm#000">Ephraim</a> are among the cities of <a href="../ebd/ebd240.htm#002">Manasseh</a>: the coast of <a href="../ebd/ebd240.htm#002">Manasseh</a> also was on the north side of the river, and the outgoings of it were at the sea:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> Southward it was Ephraim's, and <a href="../ebd/ebd275.htm#002">northward</a> it was Manasseh's, and the sea is his border; and they met together in <a href="../ebd/ebd033.htm#008">Asher</a> on the north, and in <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#002">Issachar</a> on the <a href="../ebd/ebd111.htm#000">east</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd240.htm#002">Manasseh</a> had in <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#002">Issachar</a> and in <a href="../ebd/ebd033.htm#008">Asher</a> Bethshean and her towns, and <a href="../ebd/ebd185.htm#009">Ibleam</a> and her towns, and the inhabitants of <a href="../ebd/ebd106.htm#003">Dor</a> and her towns, and the inhabitants of <a href="../ebd/ebd120.htm#006">Endor</a> and her towns, and the inhabitants of <a href="../ebd/ebd355.htm#005">Taanach</a> and her towns, and the inhabitants of <a href="../ebd/ebd246.htm#004">Megiddo</a> and her towns, even three countries.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> Yet the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd240.htm#002">Manasseh</a> could not drive out the inhabitants of those cities; but the <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#007">Canaanites</a> would dwell in that land.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> Yet it came to pass, when the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> were waxen strong, that they put the <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#007">Canaanites</a> to <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#006">tribute</a>, but did not utterly drive them out.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> And the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#005">Joseph</a> spake unto <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#006">Joshua</a>, saying, Why hast thou given me but one lot and one portion to inherit, seeing I am a great people, forasmuch as the LORD hath blessed me hitherto?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#006">Joshua</a> answered them, If thou be a great people, then get thee up to the <a href="../ebd/ebd383.htm#003">wood</a> country, and cut down for thyself there in the land of the <a href="../ebd/ebd290.htm#006">Perizzites</a> and of the <a href="../ebd/ebd147.htm#006">giants</a>, if <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#007">mount</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd123.htm#000">Ephraim</a> be too narrow for thee.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> And the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#005">Joseph</a> said, The hill is not enough for us: and all the <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#007">Canaanites</a> that dwell in the land of the <a href="../ebd/ebd376.htm#008">valley</a> have chariots of <a href="../ebd/ebd189.htm#004">iron</a>, both they who are of Bethshean and her towns, and they who are of the <a href="../ebd/ebd376.htm#008">valley</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd206.htm#008">Jezreel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#006">Joshua</a> spake unto the house of <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#005">Joseph</a>, even to <a href="../ebd/ebd123.htm#000">Ephraim</a> and to <a href="../ebd/ebd240.htm#002">Manasseh</a>, saying, Thou art a great people, and hast great power: thou shalt not have one lot only:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> But the mountain shall be thine; for it is a <a href="../ebd/ebd383.htm#003">wood</a>, and thou shalt cut it down: and the outgoings of it shall be thine: for thou shalt drive out the <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#007">Canaanites</a>, though they have <a href="../ebd/ebd189.htm#004">iron</a> chariots, and though they be strong.<br>
 </p>
