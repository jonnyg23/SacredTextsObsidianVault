 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: 1 Corinthians Chapter 16</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Now concerning the <a href="../ebd/ebd086.htm#005">collection</a> for the saints, as I have given order to the churches of <a href="../ebd/ebd141.htm#004">Galatia</a>, even so do ye.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Upon the first day of the week let every one of you lay by him in store, as God hath prospered him, that there be no gatherings when I come.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And when I come, whomsoever ye shall approve by your letters, them will I send to bring your liberality unto <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And if it be meet that I go also, they shall go with me.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Now I will come unto you, when I shall pass through <a href="../ebd/ebd235.htm#003">Macedonia</a>: for I do pass through <a href="../ebd/ebd235.htm#003">Macedonia</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And it may be that I will abide, yea, and winter with you, that ye may bring me on my <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#001">journey</a> whithersoever I go.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> For I will not see you now by the way; but I trust to tarry a while with you, if the Lord permit.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> But I will tarry at <a href="../ebd/ebd122.htm#007">Ephesus</a> until <a href="../ebd/ebd289.htm#004">Pentecost</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> For a great door and effectual is opened unto me, and there are many adversaries.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> Now if <a href="../ebd/ebd367.htm#001">Timotheus</a> come, see that he may be with you without fear: for he worketh the work of the Lord, as I also do.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> Let no man therefore despise him: but conduct him forth in peace, that he may come unto me: for I look for him with the brethren.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> As touching our brother <a href="../ebd/ebd026.htm#006">Apollos</a>, I greatly desired him to come unto you with the brethren: but his will was not at all to come at this time; but he will come when he shall have convenient time.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> Watch ye, stand <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#004">fast</a> in the faith, quit you like men, be strong.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> Let all your things be done with <a href="../ebd/ebd077.htm#009">charity</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> I beseech you, brethren, (ye know the house of <a href="../ebd/ebd351.htm#001">Stephanas</a>, that it is the firstfruits of <a href="../ebd/ebd006.htm#004">Achaia</a>, and that they have addicted themselves to the ministry of the saints,)<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> That ye submit yourselves unto such, and to every one that helpeth with us, and laboureth.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> I am glad of the coming of <a href="../ebd/ebd351.htm#001">Stephanas</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd137.htm#009">Fortunatus</a> and Achaicus: for that which was lacking on your part they have supplied.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> For they have refreshed my <a href="../ebd/ebd349.htm#009">spirit</a> and your's: therefore acknowledge ye them that are such.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> The churches of <a href="../ebd/ebd034.htm#008">Asia</a> salute you. <a href="../ebd/ebd027.htm#006">Aquila</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#005">Priscilla</a> salute you much in the Lord, with the church that is in their house.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> All the brethren greet you. Greet ye one another with an holy <a href="../ebd/ebd221.htm#000">kiss</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> The <a href="../ebd/ebd320.htm#002">salutation</a> of me <a href="../ebd/ebd287.htm#002">Paul</a> with mine own hand.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> If any man love not the Lord <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a>, let him be <a href="../ebd/ebd023.htm#003">Anathema</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd241.htm#005">Maranatha</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> The <a href="../ebd/ebd154.htm#000">grace</a> of our Lord <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a> be with you.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> My love be with you all in <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a>. <a href="../ebd/ebd020.htm#001">Amen</a>.<br>
 </p>
