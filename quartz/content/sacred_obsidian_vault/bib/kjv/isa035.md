 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Isaiah Chapter 35</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> The <a href="../ebd/ebd381.htm#005">wilderness</a> and the solitary place shall be glad for them; and the <a href="../ebd/ebd102.htm#000">desert</a> shall rejoice, and blossom as the rose.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> It shall blossom abundantly, and rejoice even with joy and singing: the <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd226.htm#001">Lebanon</a> shall be given unto it, the excellency of <a href="../ebd/ebd072.htm#006">Carmel</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd331.htm#009">Sharon</a>, they shall see the <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a> of the LORD, and the excellency of our God.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Strengthen ye the weak hands, and confirm the feeble knees.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Say to them that are of a fearful heart, Be strong, fear not: behold, your God will come with vengeance, even God with a recompence; he will come and save you.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Then the eyes of the <a href="../ebd/ebd060.htm#009">blind</a> shall be opened, and the ears of the deaf shall be unstopped.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Then shall the lame man leap as an <a href="../ebd/ebd165.htm#004">hart</a>, and the tongue of the <a href="../ebd/ebd109.htm#002">dumb</a> sing: for in the <a href="../ebd/ebd381.htm#005">wilderness</a> shall waters break out, and streams in the <a href="../ebd/ebd102.htm#000">desert</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And the parched ground shall become a pool, and the thirsty land springs of water: in the <a href="../ebd/ebd156.htm#007">habitation</a> of dragons, where each lay, shall be <a href="../ebd/ebd154.htm#005">grass</a> with reeds and rushes.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And an <a href="../ebd/ebd178.htm#005">highway</a> shall be there, and a way, and it shall be called The way of <a href="../ebd/ebd180.htm#006">holiness</a>; the unclean shall not pass over it; but it shall be for those: the wayfaring men, though fools, shall not err therein.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> No lion shall be there, nor any ravenous <a href="../ebd/ebd049.htm#001">beast</a> shall go up thereon, it shall not be found there; but the redeemed shall walk there:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And the ransomed of the LORD shall return, and come to <a href="../ebd/ebd394.htm#002">Zion</a> with <a href="../ebd/ebd348.htm#002">songs</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd127.htm#007">everlasting</a> joy upon their heads: they shall obtain joy and gladness, and sorrow and sighing shall flee away.<br>
 </p>
