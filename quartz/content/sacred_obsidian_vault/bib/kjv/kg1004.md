 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: 3 Kings (1 Kings) Chapter 4</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> So <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd347.htm#007">Solomon</a> was <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> over all <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> And these were the princes which he had; <a href="../ebd/ebd037.htm#005">Azariah</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd386.htm#008">Zadok</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Elihoreph and Ahiah, the sons of Shisha, <a href="../ebd/ebd324.htm#003">scribes</a>; <a href="../ebd/ebd201.htm#006">Jehoshaphat</a> the son of Ahilud, the <a href="../ebd/ebd308.htm#005">recorder</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd052.htm#003">Benaiah</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd200.htm#009">Jehoiada</a> was over the <a href="../ebd/ebd183.htm#007">host</a>: and <a href="../ebd/ebd386.htm#008">Zadok</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd002.htm#005">Abiathar</a> were the priests:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd037.htm#005">Azariah</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd267.htm#002">Nathan</a> was over the officers: and <a href="../ebd/ebd386.htm#000">Zabud</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd267.htm#002">Nathan</a> was principal officer, and the king's friend:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd014.htm#009">Ahishar</a> was over the household: and <a href="../ebd/ebd009.htm#008">Adoniram</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#008">Abda</a> was over the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#006">tribute</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd347.htm#007">Solomon</a> had twelve officers over all <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, which provided victuals for the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> and his household: each man his month in a year made provision.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And these are their names: The son of <a href="../ebd/ebd184.htm#009">Hur</a>, in <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#007">mount</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd123.htm#000">Ephraim</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> The son of Dekar, in Makaz, and in Shaalbim, and Bethshemesh, and Elonbethhanan:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> The son of Hesed, in Aruboth; to him pertained Sochoh, and all the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd174.htm#006">Hepher</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> The son of <a href="../ebd/ebd004.htm#001">Abinadab</a>, in all the region of <a href="../ebd/ebd106.htm#003">Dor</a>; which had Taphath the daughter of <a href="../ebd/ebd347.htm#007">Solomon</a> to wife:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd040.htm#006">Baana</a> the son of Ahilud; to him pertained <a href="../ebd/ebd355.htm#005">Taanach</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd246.htm#004">Megiddo</a>, and all Bethshean, which is by Zartanah beneath <a href="../ebd/ebd206.htm#008">Jezreel</a>, from Bethshean to Abelmeholah, even unto the place that is beyond <a href="../ebd/ebd210.htm#002">Jokneam</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> The son of <a href="../ebd/ebd144.htm#007">Geber</a>, in Ramothgilead; to him pertained the towns of <a href="../ebd/ebd196.htm#001">Jair</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd240.htm#002">Manasseh</a>, which are in <a href="../ebd/ebd148.htm#009">Gilead</a>; to him also pertained the region of <a href="../ebd/ebd030.htm#003">Argob</a>, which is in <a href="../ebd/ebd046.htm#003">Bashan</a>, threescore great cities with walls and brasen bars:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd014.htm#005">Ahinadab</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd186.htm#005">Iddo</a> had <a href="../ebd/ebd237.htm#005">Mahanaim</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd014.htm#002">Ahimaaz</a> was in <a href="../ebd/ebd266.htm#006">Naphtali</a>; he also took Basmath the daughter of <a href="../ebd/ebd347.htm#007">Solomon</a> to wife:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd040.htm#007">Baanah</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd185.htm#003">Hushai</a> was in <a href="../ebd/ebd033.htm#008">Asher</a> and in Aloth:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd201.htm#006">Jehoshaphat</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd285.htm#008">Paruah</a>, in <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#002">Issachar</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd338.htm#002">Shimei</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd114.htm#002">Elah</a>, in <a href="../ebd/ebd052.htm#008">Benjamin</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd144.htm#007">Geber</a> the son of Uri was in the country of <a href="../ebd/ebd148.htm#009">Gilead</a>, in the country of <a href="../ebd/ebd343.htm#002">Sihon</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd021.htm#006">Amorites</a>, and of <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#002">Og</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd046.htm#003">Bashan</a>; and he was the only officer which was in the land.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> were many, as the sand which is by the sea in multitude, <a href="../ebd/ebd111.htm#006">eating</a> and drinking, and making merry.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd347.htm#007">Solomon</a> reigned over all kingdoms from the river unto the land of the <a href="../ebd/ebd294.htm#002">Philistines</a>, and unto the border of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a>: they brought presents, and served <a href="../ebd/ebd347.htm#007">Solomon</a> all the days of his life.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> And Solomon's provision for one day was thirty measures of fine <a href="../ebd/ebd136.htm#001">flour</a>, and threescore measures of meal,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> Ten <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#005">fat</a> oxen, and twenty oxen out of the pastures, and an hundred <a href="../ebd/ebd333.htm#005">sheep</a>, beside harts, and roebucks, and fallowdeer, and fatted fowl.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> For he had dominion over all the region on this side the river, from <a href="../ebd/ebd367.htm#007">Tiphsah</a> even to Azzah, over all the kings on this side the river: and he had peace on all sides round about him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> dwelt safely, every man under his <a href="../ebd/ebd377.htm#004">vine</a> and under his <a href="../ebd/ebd133.htm#001">fig</a> tree, from <a href="../ebd/ebd096.htm#009">Dan</a> even to <a href="../ebd/ebd050.htm#008">Beersheba</a>, all the days of <a href="../ebd/ebd347.htm#007">Solomon</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd347.htm#007">Solomon</a> had forty thousand stalls of horses for his chariots, and twelve thousand horsemen.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> And those officers provided victual for <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd347.htm#007">Solomon</a>, and for all that came unto <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> Solomon's table, every man in his month: they lacked nothing.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="028">28</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd045.htm#003">Barley</a> also and <a href="../ebd/ebd352.htm#000">straw</a> for the horses and dromedaries brought they unto the place where the officers were, every man according to his charge.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="029">29</a> And God gave <a href="../ebd/ebd347.htm#007">Solomon</a> wisdom and understanding exceeding much, and largeness of heart, even as the sand that is on the sea shore.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="030">30</a> And Solomon's wisdom excelled the wisdom of all the children of the <a href="../ebd/ebd111.htm#000">east</a> country, and all the wisdom of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="031">31</a> For he was wiser than all men; than <a href="../ebd/ebd126.htm#002">Ethan</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd129.htm#008">Ezrahite</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd173.htm#009">Heman</a>, and Chalcol, and <a href="../ebd/ebd097.htm#005">Darda</a>, the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd238.htm#002">Mahol</a>: and his fame was in all nations round about.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="032">32</a> And he spake three thousand proverbs: and his <a href="../ebd/ebd348.htm#002">songs</a> were a thousand and five.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="033">33</a> And he spake of trees, from the <a href="../ebd/ebd074.htm#007">cedar</a> tree that is in <a href="../ebd/ebd226.htm#001">Lebanon</a> even unto the <a href="../ebd/ebd185.htm#007">hyssop</a> that springeth out of the wall: he spake also of beasts, and of fowl, and of creeping things, and of fishes.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="034">34</a> And there came of all people to hear the wisdom of <a href="../ebd/ebd347.htm#007">Solomon</a>, from all kings of the earth, which had heard of his wisdom.<br>
 </p>
