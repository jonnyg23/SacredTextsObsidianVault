 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 133</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Behold, how good and how pleasant it is for brethren to dwell together in unity!<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> It is like the precious <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#007">ointment</a> upon the head, that ran down upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd049.htm#000">beard</a>, even Aaron's <a href="../ebd/ebd049.htm#000">beard</a>: that went down to the skirts of his garments;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> As the <a href="../ebd/ebd102.htm#008">dew</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd175.htm#006">Hermon</a>, and as the <a href="../ebd/ebd102.htm#008">dew</a> that descended upon the mountains of <a href="../ebd/ebd394.htm#002">Zion</a>: for there the LORD commanded the blessing, even life for evermore.<br>
 </p>
