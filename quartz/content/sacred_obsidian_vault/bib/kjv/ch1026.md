 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: 1 Chronicles Chapter 26</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Concerning the divisions of the porters: Of the <a href="../ebd/ebd222.htm#006">Korhites</a> was <a href="../ebd/ebd250.htm#009">Meshelemiah</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd222.htm#005">Kore</a>, of the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd033.htm#002">Asaph</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> And the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd250.htm#009">Meshelemiah</a> were, <a href="../ebd/ebd389.htm#006">Zechariah</a> the firstborn, <a href="../ebd/ebd199.htm#007">Jediael</a> the second, <a href="../ebd/ebd388.htm#005">Zebadiah</a> the third, Jathniel the fourth,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd114.htm#003">Elam</a> the fifth, <a href="../ebd/ebd200.htm#008">Jehohanan</a> the sixth, <a href="../ebd/ebd117.htm#003">Elioenai</a> the seventh.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Moreover the sons of Obededom were, <a href="../ebd/ebd334.htm#005">Shemaiah</a> the firstborn, <a href="../ebd/ebd202.htm#005">Jehozabad</a> the second, <a href="../ebd/ebd207.htm#007">Joah</a> the third, and Sacar the fourth, and <a href="../ebd/ebd271.htm#003">Nethaneel</a> the fifth.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd020.htm#006">Ammiel</a> the sixth, <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#002">Issachar</a> the seventh, <a href="../ebd/ebd291.htm#009">Peulthai</a> the eighth: for God blessed him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Also unto <a href="../ebd/ebd334.htm#005">Shemaiah</a> his son were sons born, that ruled throughout the house of their father: for they were mighty men of valour.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> The sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd334.htm#005">Shemaiah</a>; <a href="../ebd/ebd281.htm#002">Othni</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd310.htm#009">Rephael</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd276.htm#004">Obed</a>, Elzabad, whose brethren were strong men, <a href="../ebd/ebd116.htm#008">Elihu</a>, and Semachiah.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> All these of the sons of Obededom: they and their sons and their brethren, able men for strength for the service, were threescore and two of Obededom.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd250.htm#009">Meshelemiah</a> had sons and brethren, strong men, eighteen.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> Also <a href="../ebd/ebd183.htm#005">Hosah</a>, of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#000">Merari</a>, had sons; <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#005">Simri</a> the chief, (for though he was not the firstborn, yet his father made him the chief;)<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd178.htm#006">Hilkiah</a> the second, Tebaliah the third, <a href="../ebd/ebd389.htm#006">Zechariah</a> the fourth: all the sons and brethren of <a href="../ebd/ebd183.htm#005">Hosah</a> were thirteen.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> Among these were the divisions of the porters, even among the chief men, having wards one against another, to <a href="../ebd/ebd255.htm#007">minister</a> in the house of the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> And they cast lots, as well the small as the great, according to the house of their fathers, for every <a href="../ebd/ebd143.htm#008">gate</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> And the lot eastward fell to <a href="../ebd/ebd334.htm#001">Shelemiah</a>. Then for <a href="../ebd/ebd389.htm#006">Zechariah</a> his son, a <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#004">wise</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd091.htm#005">counsellor</a>, they cast lots; and his lot came out <a href="../ebd/ebd275.htm#002">northward</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> To Obededom southward; and to his sons the house of <a href="../ebd/ebd035.htm#008">Asuppim</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> To Shuppim and <a href="../ebd/ebd183.htm#005">Hosah</a> the lot came forth <a href="../ebd/ebd380.htm#008">westward</a>, with the <a href="../ebd/ebd143.htm#008">gate</a> Shallecheth, by the <a href="../ebd/ebd074.htm#005">causeway</a> of the going up, <a href="../ebd/ebd379.htm#001">ward</a> against <a href="../ebd/ebd379.htm#001">ward</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> Eastward were six Levites, <a href="../ebd/ebd275.htm#002">northward</a> four a day, southward four a day, and toward <a href="../ebd/ebd035.htm#008">Asuppim</a> two and two.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> At <a href="../ebd/ebd284.htm#008">Parbar</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd380.htm#008">westward</a>, four at the <a href="../ebd/ebd074.htm#005">causeway</a>, and two at <a href="../ebd/ebd284.htm#008">Parbar</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> These are the divisions of the porters among the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd222.htm#005">Kore</a>, and among the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#000">Merari</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> And of the Levites, <a href="../ebd/ebd014.htm#000">Ahijah</a> was over the treasures of the house of God, and over the treasures of the dedicated things.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> As concerning the sons of Laadan; the sons of the Gershonite Laadan, chief fathers, even of Laadan the Gershonite, were Jehieli.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> The sons of Jehieli; <a href="../ebd/ebd392.htm#000">Zetham</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd208.htm#005">Joel</a> his brother, which were over the treasures of the house of the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> Of the Amramites, and the Izharites, the Hebronites, and the Uzzielites:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd333.htm#000">Shebuel</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd146.htm#008">Gershom</a>, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, was ruler of the treasures.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> And his brethren by <a href="../ebd/ebd116.htm#007">Eliezer</a>; <a href="../ebd/ebd309.htm#005">Rehabiah</a> his son, and <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#007">Jeshaiah</a> his son, and <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#003">Joram</a> his son, and <a href="../ebd/ebd392.htm#007">Zichri</a> his son, and Shelomith his son.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> Which Shelomith and his brethren were over all the treasures of the dedicated things, which <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a>, and the chief fathers, the captains over <a href="../ebd/ebd364.htm#008">thousands</a> and hundreds, and the captains of the <a href="../ebd/ebd183.htm#007">host</a>, had dedicated.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> Out of the spoils won in battles did they dedicate to maintain the house of the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="028">28</a> And all that <a href="../ebd/ebd321.htm#001">Samuel</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd326.htm#000">seer</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#002">Saul</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd220.htm#007">Kish</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd005.htm#002">Abner</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd270.htm#007">Ner</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd207.htm#006">Joab</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd391.htm#009">Zeruiah</a>, had dedicated; and whosoever had dedicated any thing, it was under the hand of Shelomith, and of his brethren.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="029">29</a> Of the Izharites, Chenaniah and his sons were for the outward business over <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, for officers and judges.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="030">30</a> And of the Hebronites, <a href="../ebd/ebd166.htm#001">Hashabiah</a> and his brethren, men of valour, a thousand and <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> hundred, were officers among them of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> on this side <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#004">Jordan</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd380.htm#008">westward</a> in all the business of the LORD, and in the service of the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="031">31</a> Among the Hebronites was Jerijah the chief, even among the Hebronites, according to the generations of his fathers. In the fortieth year of the reign of <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> they were sought for, and there were found among them mighty men of valour at Jazer of <a href="../ebd/ebd148.htm#009">Gilead</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="032">32</a> And his brethren, men of valour, were two thousand and <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> hundred chief fathers, whom <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> made rulers over the Reubenites, the Gadites, and the half <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd240.htm#002">Manasseh</a>, for every matter pertaining to God, and affairs of the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a>.<br>
 </p>
