 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 141</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Lord, I cry unto thee: make haste unto me; give <a href="../ebd/ebd110.htm#004">ear</a> unto my voice, when I cry unto thee.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Let my <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#000">prayer</a> be set forth before thee as <a href="../ebd/ebd188.htm#002">incense</a>; and the lifting up of my hands as the evening <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#002">sacrifice</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Set a watch, O LORD, before my mouth; keep the door of my lips.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Incline not my heart to any evil thing, to practise wicked works with men that work iniquity: and let me not eat of their dainties.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Let the righteous smite me; it shall be a kindness: and let him reprove me; it shall be an excellent oil, which shall not break my head: for yet my <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#000">prayer</a> also shall be in their calamities.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> When their judges are overthrown in stony places, they shall hear my words; for they are sweet.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Our bones are scattered at the grave's mouth, as when one cutteth and cleaveth <a href="../ebd/ebd383.htm#003">wood</a> upon the earth.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> But mine eyes are unto thee, O GOD the Lord: in thee is my trust; leave not my soul destitute.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> Keep me from the snares which they have laid for me, and the gins of the workers of iniquity.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> Let the wicked fall into their own nets, whilst that I withal escape.<br>
 </p>
