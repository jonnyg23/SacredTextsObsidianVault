 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 97</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> The LORD reigneth; let the earth rejoice; let the multitude of isles be glad thereof.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Clouds and <a href="../ebd/ebd097.htm#008">darkness</a> are round about him: <a href="../ebd/ebd313.htm#004">righteousness</a> and judgment are the <a href="../ebd/ebd156.htm#007">habitation</a> of his <a href="../ebd/ebd365.htm#001">throne</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> A fire goeth before him, and burneth up his enemies round about.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> His lightnings enlightened the world: the earth saw, and trembled.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> The hills melted like <a href="../ebd/ebd380.htm#000">wax</a> at the presence of the LORD, at the presence of the Lord of the whole earth.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> The heavens declare his <a href="../ebd/ebd313.htm#004">righteousness</a>, and all the people see his <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Confounded be all they that serve graven images, that boast themselves of idols: <a href="../ebd/ebd384.htm#002">worship</a> him, all ye gods.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd394.htm#002">Zion</a> heard, and was glad; and the daughters of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a> rejoiced because of thy judgments, O LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> For thou, LORD, art high above all the earth: thou art exalted far above all gods.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> Ye that love the LORD, hate evil: he preserveth the souls of his saints; he delivereth them out of the hand of the wicked.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> Light is sown for the righteous, and gladness for the upright in heart.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> Rejoice in the LORD, ye righteous; and give thanks at the remembrance of his <a href="../ebd/ebd180.htm#006">holiness</a>.<br>
 </p>
