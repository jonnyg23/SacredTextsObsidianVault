 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: 1 Chronicles Chapter 9</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> So all <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> were reckoned by genealogies; and, behold, they were written in the book of the kings of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>, who were carried away to <a href="../ebd/ebd041.htm#001">Babylon</a> for their transgression.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Now the first inhabitants that dwelt in their possessions in their cities were, the Israelites, the priests, Levites, and the Nethinims.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And in <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a> dwelt of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>, and of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd052.htm#008">Benjamin</a>, and of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd123.htm#000">Ephraim</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd240.htm#002">Manasseh</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Uthai the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd020.htm#007">Ammihud</a>, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd278.htm#006">Omri</a>, the son of Imri, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd043.htm#004">Bani</a>, of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd292.htm#007">Pharez</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And of the Shilonites; Asaiah the firstborn, and his sons.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And of the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd391.htm#001">Zerah</a>; <a href="../ebd/ebd206.htm#002">Jeuel</a>, and their brethren, six hundred and ninety.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And of the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd052.htm#008">Benjamin</a>; <a href="../ebd/ebd319.htm#004">Sallu</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd251.htm#001">Meshullam</a>, the son of Hodaviah, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd166.htm#000">Hasenuah</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And Ibneiah the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#001">Jeroham</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd114.htm#002">Elah</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd376.htm#003">Uzzi</a>, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd253.htm#004">Michri</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd251.htm#001">Meshullam</a> the son of Shephathiah, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd312.htm#001">Reuel</a>, the son of Ibnijah;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And their brethren, according to their generations, nine hundred and fifty and six. All these men were chief of the fathers in the house of their fathers.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And of the priests; <a href="../ebd/ebd199.htm#006">Jedaiah</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd201.htm#002">Jehoiarib</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd194.htm#004">Jachin</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd037.htm#005">Azariah</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd178.htm#006">Hilkiah</a>, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd251.htm#001">Meshullam</a>, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd386.htm#008">Zadok</a>, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd248.htm#009">Meraioth</a>, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd015.htm#001">Ahitub</a>, the ruler of the house of God;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And Adaiah the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#001">Jeroham</a>, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd286.htm#002">Pashur</a>, the son of Malchijah, and <a href="../ebd/ebd234.htm#008">Maasiai</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd008.htm#008">Adiel</a>, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd195.htm#009">Jahzerah</a>, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd251.htm#001">Meshullam</a>, the son of Meshillemith, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd187.htm#008">Immer</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> And their brethren, heads of the house of their fathers, a thousand and <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> hundred and threescore; very able men for the work of the service of the house of God.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> And of the Levites; <a href="../ebd/ebd334.htm#005">Shemaiah</a> the son of Hasshub, the son of Azrikam, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd166.htm#001">Hashabiah</a>, of the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#000">Merari</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And Bakbakkar, Heresh, and Galal, and <a href="../ebd/ebd243.htm#009">Mattaniah</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd252.htm#005">Micah</a>, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd392.htm#007">Zichri</a>, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd033.htm#002">Asaph</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd276.htm#001">Obadiah</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd334.htm#005">Shemaiah</a>, the son of Galal, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd199.htm#009">Jeduthun</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd053.htm#003">Berechiah</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd033.htm#000">Asa</a>, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd118.htm#005">Elkanah</a>, that dwelt in the <a href="../ebd/ebd377.htm#003">villages</a> of the Netophathites.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> And the porters were, <a href="../ebd/ebd330.htm#008">Shallum</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd016.htm#003">Akkub</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd358.htm#002">Talmon</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd014.htm#003">Ahiman</a>, and their brethren: <a href="../ebd/ebd330.htm#008">Shallum</a> was the chief;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> Who hitherto waited in the king's <a href="../ebd/ebd143.htm#008">gate</a> eastward: they were porters in the companies of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd227.htm#006">Levi</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd330.htm#008">Shallum</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd222.htm#005">Kore</a>, the son of Ebiasaph, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd222.htm#003">Korah</a>, and his brethren, of the house of his father, the <a href="../ebd/ebd222.htm#004">Korahites</a>, were over the work of the service, keepers of the gates of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a>: and their fathers, being over the <a href="../ebd/ebd183.htm#007">host</a> of the LORD, were keepers of the entry.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd294.htm#003">Phinehas</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd115.htm#000">Eleazar</a> was the ruler over them in time past, and the LORD was with him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd389.htm#006">Zechariah</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd250.htm#009">Meshelemiah</a> was <a href="../ebd/ebd299.htm#000">porter</a> of the door of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> All these which were <a href="../ebd/ebd081.htm#008">chosen</a> to be porters in the gates were two hundred and twelve. These were reckoned by their genealogy in their <a href="../ebd/ebd377.htm#003">villages</a>, whom <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd321.htm#001">Samuel</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd326.htm#000">seer</a> did ordain in their set office.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> So they and their children had the oversight of the gates of the house of the LORD, namely, the house of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a>, by wards.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> In four quarters were the porters, toward the <a href="../ebd/ebd111.htm#000">east</a>, west, north, and south.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> And their brethren, which were in their <a href="../ebd/ebd377.htm#003">villages</a>, were to come after <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> days from time to time with them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> For these Levites, the four chief porters, were in their set office, and were over the chambers and treasuries of the house of God.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> And they lodged round about the house of God, because the charge was upon them, and the opening thereof every morning pertained to them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="028">28</a> And certain of them had the charge of the ministering vessels, that they should bring them in and out by <a href="../ebd/ebd357.htm#008">tale</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="029">29</a> Some of them also were appointed to oversee the vessels, and all the instruments of the <a href="../ebd/ebd321.htm#005">sanctuary</a>, and the fine <a href="../ebd/ebd136.htm#001">flour</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#000">wine</a>, and the oil, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd138.htm#004">frankincense</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd349.htm#005">spices</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="030">30</a> And some of the sons of the priests made the <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#007">ointment</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd349.htm#005">spices</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="031">31</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd244.htm#006">Mattithiah</a>, one of the Levites, who was the firstborn of <a href="../ebd/ebd330.htm#008">Shallum</a> the Korahite, had the set office over the things that were made in the pans.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="032">32</a> And other of their brethren, of the sons of the <a href="../ebd/ebd222.htm#002">Kohathites</a>, were over the <a href="../ebd/ebd336.htm#008">shewbread</a>, to prepare it every <a href="../ebd/ebd317.htm#003">sabbath</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="033">33</a> And these are the singers, chief of the fathers of the Levites, who remaining in the chambers were free: for they were employed in that work day and night.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="034">34</a> These chief fathers of the Levites were chief throughout their generations; these dwelt at <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="035">35</a> And in <a href="../ebd/ebd148.htm#002">Gibeon</a> dwelt the father of <a href="../ebd/ebd148.htm#002">Gibeon</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd200.htm#003">Jehiel</a>, whose wife's name was <a href="../ebd/ebd234.htm#004">Maachah</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="036">36</a> And his firstborn son <a href="../ebd/ebd001.htm#002">Abdon</a>, then <a href="../ebd/ebd396.htm#005">Zur</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd220.htm#007">Kish</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd038.htm#004">Baal</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd270.htm#007">Ner</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd264.htm#008">Nadab</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="037">37</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd145.htm#003">Gedor</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd014.htm#007">Ahio</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd389.htm#006">Zechariah</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd254.htm#005">Mikloth</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="038">38</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd254.htm#005">Mikloth</a> begat Shimeam. And they also dwelt with their brethren at <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>, over against their brethren.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="039">39</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd270.htm#007">Ner</a> begat <a href="../ebd/ebd220.htm#007">Kish</a>; and <a href="../ebd/ebd220.htm#007">Kish</a> begat <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#002">Saul</a>; and <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#002">Saul</a> begat <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#000">Jonathan</a>, and Malchishua, and <a href="../ebd/ebd004.htm#001">Abinadab</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd124.htm#009">Eshbaal</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="040">40</a> And the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#000">Jonathan</a> was Meribbaal: and Meribbaal begat <a href="../ebd/ebd252.htm#005">Micah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="041">41</a> And the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd252.htm#005">Micah</a> were, Pithon, and <a href="../ebd/ebd247.htm#005">Melech</a>, and Tahrea, and <a href="../ebd/ebd013.htm#005">Ahaz</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="042">42</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd013.htm#005">Ahaz</a> begat Jarah; and Jarah begat <a href="../ebd/ebd016.htm#008">Alemeth</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd038.htm#000">Azmaveth</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd393.htm#009">Zimri</a>; and <a href="../ebd/ebd393.htm#009">Zimri</a> begat <a href="../ebd/ebd261.htm#007">Moza</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="043">43</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd261.htm#007">Moza</a> begat Binea; and Rephaiah his son, Eleasah his son, <a href="../ebd/ebd037.htm#009">Azel</a> his son.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="044">44</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd037.htm#009">Azel</a> had six sons, whose names are these, Azrikam, Bocheru, and <a href="../ebd/ebd190.htm#005">Ishmael</a>, and Sheariah, and <a href="../ebd/ebd276.htm#001">Obadiah</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd162.htm#001">Hanan</a>: these were the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd037.htm#009">Azel</a>.<br>
 </p>
