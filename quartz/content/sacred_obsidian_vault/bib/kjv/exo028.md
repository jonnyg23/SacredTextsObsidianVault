 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Exodus Chapter 28</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> And take thou unto thee <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> thy brother, and his sons with him, from among the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, that he may <a href="../ebd/ebd255.htm#007">minister</a> unto me in the priest's office, even <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd264.htm#008">Nadab</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd003.htm#004">Abihu</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd115.htm#000">Eleazar</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#005">Ithamar</a>, Aaron's sons.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> And thou shalt make holy garments for <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> thy brother for <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a> and for beauty.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And thou shalt speak unto all that are <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#004">wise</a> hearted, whom I have filled with the <a href="../ebd/ebd349.htm#009">spirit</a> of wisdom, that they may make Aaron's garments to consecrate him, that he may <a href="../ebd/ebd255.htm#007">minister</a> unto me in the priest's office.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And these are the garments which they shall make; a <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#007">breastplate</a>, and an <a href="../ebd/ebd122.htm#008">ephod</a>, and a robe, and a broidered <a href="../ebd/ebd085.htm#004">coat</a>, a <a href="../ebd/ebd257.htm#006">mitre</a>, and a <a href="../ebd/ebd149.htm#005">girdle</a>: and they shall make holy garments for <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> thy brother, and his sons, that he may <a href="../ebd/ebd255.htm#007">minister</a> unto me in the priest's office.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And they shall take <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#003">blue</a>, and purple, and <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#005">scarlet</a>, and fine <a href="../ebd/ebd229.htm#008">linen</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And they shall make the <a href="../ebd/ebd122.htm#008">ephod</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a>, of <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#003">blue</a>, and of purple, of <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#005">scarlet</a>, and fine twined <a href="../ebd/ebd229.htm#008">linen</a>, with cunning work.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> It shall have the two shoulderpieces thereof joined at the two edges thereof; and so it shall be joined together.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And the curious <a href="../ebd/ebd149.htm#005">girdle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd122.htm#008">ephod</a>, which is upon it, shall be of the same, according to the work thereof; even of <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a>, of <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#003">blue</a>, and purple, and <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#005">scarlet</a>, and fine twined <a href="../ebd/ebd229.htm#008">linen</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And thou shalt take two <a href="../ebd/ebd279.htm#004">onyx</a> stones, and <a href="../ebd/ebd154.htm#008">grave</a> on them the names of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> Six of their names on one <a href="../ebd/ebd351.htm#005">stone</a>, and the other six names of the rest on the other <a href="../ebd/ebd351.htm#005">stone</a>, according to their <a href="../ebd/ebd059.htm#002">birth</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> With the work of an <a href="../ebd/ebd121.htm#001">engraver</a> in <a href="../ebd/ebd351.htm#005">stone</a>, like the engravings of a <a href="../ebd/ebd343.htm#001">signet</a>, shalt thou engrave the two stones with the names of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>: thou shalt make them to be set in <a href="../ebd/ebd281.htm#004">ouches</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And thou shalt put the two stones upon the shoulders of the <a href="../ebd/ebd122.htm#008">ephod</a> for stones of memorial unto the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>: and <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> shall bear their names before the LORD upon his two shoulders for a memorial.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> And thou shalt make <a href="../ebd/ebd281.htm#004">ouches</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> And two chains of pure <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a> at the ends; of wreathen work shalt thou make them, and fasten the wreathen chains to the <a href="../ebd/ebd281.htm#004">ouches</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And thou shalt make the <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#007">breastplate</a> of judgment with cunning work; after the work of the <a href="../ebd/ebd122.htm#008">ephod</a> thou shalt make it; of <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a>, of <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#003">blue</a>, and of purple, and of <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#005">scarlet</a>, and of fine twined <a href="../ebd/ebd229.htm#008">linen</a>, shalt thou make it.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> Foursquare it shall be being doubled; a span shall be the length thereof, and a span shall be the breadth thereof.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> And thou shalt set in it settings of stones, even four rows of stones: the first row shall be a sardius, a <a href="../ebd/ebd370.htm#005">topaz</a>, and a <a href="../ebd/ebd072.htm#003">carbuncle</a>: this shall be the first row.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> And the second row shall be an <a href="../ebd/ebd119.htm#007">emerald</a>, a <a href="../ebd/ebd322.htm#002">sapphire</a>, and a <a href="../ebd/ebd103.htm#001">diamond</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> And the third row a <a href="../ebd/ebd229.htm#004">ligure</a>, an <a href="../ebd/ebd012.htm#000">agate</a>, and an <a href="../ebd/ebd020.htm#002">amethyst</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> And the fourth row a <a href="../ebd/ebd053.htm#008">beryl</a>, and an <a href="../ebd/ebd279.htm#004">onyx</a>, and a <a href="../ebd/ebd198.htm#003">jasper</a>: they shall be set in <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a> in their inclosings.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> And the stones shall be with the names of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, twelve, according to their names, like the engravings of a <a href="../ebd/ebd343.htm#001">signet</a>; every one with his name shall they be according to the twelve tribes.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> And thou shalt make upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#007">breastplate</a> chains at the ends of wreathen work of pure <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> And thou shalt make upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#007">breastplate</a> two rings of <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a>, and shalt put the two rings on the two ends of the <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#007">breastplate</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> And thou shalt put the two wreathen chains of <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a> in the two rings which are on the ends of the <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#007">breastplate</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> And the other two ends of the two wreathen chains thou shalt fasten in the two <a href="../ebd/ebd281.htm#004">ouches</a>, and put them on the shoulderpieces of the <a href="../ebd/ebd122.htm#008">ephod</a> before it.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> And thou shalt make two rings of <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a>, and thou shalt put them upon the two ends of the <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#007">breastplate</a> in the border thereof, which is in the side of the <a href="../ebd/ebd122.htm#008">ephod</a> inward.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> And two other rings of <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a> thou shalt make, and shalt put them on the two sides of the <a href="../ebd/ebd122.htm#008">ephod</a> underneath, toward the forepart thereof, over against the other coupling thereof, above the curious <a href="../ebd/ebd149.htm#005">girdle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd122.htm#008">ephod</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="028">28</a> And they shall bind the <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#007">breastplate</a> by the rings thereof unto the rings of the <a href="../ebd/ebd122.htm#008">ephod</a> with a lace of <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#003">blue</a>, that it may be above the curious <a href="../ebd/ebd149.htm#005">girdle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd122.htm#008">ephod</a>, and that the <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#007">breastplate</a> be not loosed from the <a href="../ebd/ebd122.htm#008">ephod</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="029">29</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> shall bear the names of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> in the <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#007">breastplate</a> of judgment upon his heart, when he goeth in unto the holy place, for a memorial before the LORD continually.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="030">30</a> And thou shalt put in the <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#007">breastplate</a> of judgment the <a href="../ebd/ebd375.htm#005">Urim</a> and the <a href="../ebd/ebd365.htm#002">Thummim</a>; and they shall be upon Aaron's heart, when he goeth in before the LORD: and <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> shall bear the judgment of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> upon his heart before the LORD continually.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="031">31</a> And thou shalt make the robe of the <a href="../ebd/ebd122.htm#008">ephod</a> all of <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#003">blue</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="032">32</a> And there shall be an hole in the top of it, in the midst thereof: it shall have a binding of woven work round about the hole of it, as it were the hole of an <a href="../ebd/ebd156.htm#006">habergeon</a>, that it be not <a href="../ebd/ebd310.htm#007">rent</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="033">33</a> And beneath upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd173.htm#008">hem</a> of it thou shalt make pomegranates of <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#003">blue</a>, and of purple, and of <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#005">scarlet</a>, round about the <a href="../ebd/ebd173.htm#008">hem</a> thereof; and bells of <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a> between them round about:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="034">34</a> A golden <a href="../ebd/ebd051.htm#008">bell</a> and a <a href="../ebd/ebd298.htm#000">pomegranate</a>, a golden <a href="../ebd/ebd051.htm#008">bell</a> and a <a href="../ebd/ebd298.htm#000">pomegranate</a>, upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd173.htm#008">hem</a> of the robe round about.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="035">35</a> And it shall be upon <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> to <a href="../ebd/ebd255.htm#007">minister</a>: and his sound shall be heard when he goeth in unto the holy place before the LORD, and when he cometh out, that he die not.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="036">36</a> And thou shalt make a plate of pure <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd154.htm#008">grave</a> upon it, like the engravings of a <a href="../ebd/ebd343.htm#001">signet</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd180.htm#006">HOLINESS</a> TO THE LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="037">37</a> And thou shalt put it on a <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#003">blue</a> lace, that it may be upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd257.htm#006">mitre</a>; upon the forefront of the <a href="../ebd/ebd257.htm#006">mitre</a> it shall be.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="038">38</a> And it shall be upon Aaron's <a href="../ebd/ebd137.htm#002">forehead</a>, that <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> may bear the iniquity of the holy things, which the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> shall <a href="../ebd/ebd160.htm#001">hallow</a> in all their holy gifts; and it shall be always upon his <a href="../ebd/ebd137.htm#002">forehead</a>, that they may be accepted before the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="039">39</a> And thou shalt <a href="../ebd/ebd119.htm#006">embroider</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd085.htm#004">coat</a> of fine <a href="../ebd/ebd229.htm#008">linen</a>, and thou shalt make the <a href="../ebd/ebd257.htm#006">mitre</a> of fine <a href="../ebd/ebd229.htm#008">linen</a>, and thou shalt make the <a href="../ebd/ebd149.htm#005">girdle</a> of needlework.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="040">40</a> And for Aaron's sons thou shalt make coats, and thou shalt make for them girdles, and bonnets shalt thou make for them, for <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a> and for beauty.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="041">41</a> And thou shalt put them upon <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> thy brother, and his sons with him; and shalt <a href="../ebd/ebd024.htm#008">anoint</a> them, and consecrate them, and sanctify them, that they may <a href="../ebd/ebd255.htm#007">minister</a> unto me in the priest's office.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="042">42</a> And thou shalt make them <a href="../ebd/ebd229.htm#008">linen</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#008">breeches</a> to cover their nakedness; from the loins even unto the thighs they shall reach:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="043">43</a> And they shall be upon <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a>, and upon his sons, when they come in unto the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>, or when they come near unto the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> to <a href="../ebd/ebd255.htm#007">minister</a> in the holy place; that they bear not iniquity, and die: it shall be a statute for ever unto him and his seed after him.<br>
 </p>
