 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: 1 Chronicles Chapter 25</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Moreover <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> and the captains of the <a href="../ebd/ebd183.htm#007">host</a> separated to the service of the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd033.htm#002">Asaph</a>, and of <a href="../ebd/ebd173.htm#009">Heman</a>, and of <a href="../ebd/ebd199.htm#009">Jeduthun</a>, who should prophesy with harps, with psalteries, and with <a href="../ebd/ebd095.htm#001">cymbals</a>: and the number of the workmen according to their service was:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Of the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd033.htm#002">Asaph</a>; <a href="../ebd/ebd386.htm#004">Zaccur</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#005">Joseph</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd271.htm#004">Nethaniah</a>, and Asarelah, the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd033.htm#002">Asaph</a> under the hands of <a href="../ebd/ebd033.htm#002">Asaph</a>, which prophesied according to the order of the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Of <a href="../ebd/ebd199.htm#009">Jeduthun</a>: the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd199.htm#009">Jeduthun</a>; <a href="../ebd/ebd144.htm#009">Gedaliah</a>, and Zeri, and <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#007">Jeshaiah</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd166.htm#001">Hashabiah</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd244.htm#006">Mattithiah</a>, six, under the hands of their father <a href="../ebd/ebd199.htm#009">Jeduthun</a>, who prophesied with a <a href="../ebd/ebd165.htm#001">harp</a>, to give thanks and to praise the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Of <a href="../ebd/ebd173.htm#009">Heman</a>: the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd173.htm#009">Heman</a>: Bukkiah, <a href="../ebd/ebd243.htm#009">Mattaniah</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd376.htm#005">Uzziel</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd333.htm#000">Shebuel</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd203.htm#009">Jerimoth</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd162.htm#004">Hananiah</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd162.htm#003">Hanani</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd116.htm#004">Eliathah</a>, Giddalti, and Romamtiezer, Joshbekashah, <a href="../ebd/ebd239.htm#004">Mallothi</a>, Hothir, and <a href="../ebd/ebd237.htm#008">Mahazioth</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> All these were the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd173.htm#009">Heman</a> the king's <a href="../ebd/ebd326.htm#000">seer</a> in the words of God, to lift up the <a href="../ebd/ebd182.htm#003">horn</a>. And God gave to <a href="../ebd/ebd173.htm#009">Heman</a> fourteen sons and three daughters.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> All these were under the hands of their father for song in the house of the LORD, with <a href="../ebd/ebd095.htm#001">cymbals</a>, psalteries, and harps, for the service of the house of God, according to the king's order to <a href="../ebd/ebd033.htm#002">Asaph</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd199.htm#009">Jeduthun</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd173.htm#009">Heman</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> So the number of them, with their brethren that were instructed in the <a href="../ebd/ebd348.htm#002">songs</a> of the LORD, even all that were cunning, was two hundred fourscore and eight.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And they cast lots, <a href="../ebd/ebd379.htm#001">ward</a> against <a href="../ebd/ebd379.htm#001">ward</a>, as well the small as the great, the teacher as the scholar.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> Now the first lot came forth for <a href="../ebd/ebd033.htm#002">Asaph</a> to <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#005">Joseph</a>: the second to <a href="../ebd/ebd144.htm#009">Gedaliah</a>, who with his brethren and sons were twelve:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> The third to <a href="../ebd/ebd386.htm#004">Zaccur</a>, he, his sons, and his brethren, were twelve:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> The fourth to Izri, he, his sons, and his brethren, were twelve:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> The fifth to <a href="../ebd/ebd271.htm#004">Nethaniah</a>, he, his sons, and his brethren, were twelve:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> The sixth to Bukkiah, he, his sons, and his brethren, were twelve:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> The seventh to <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#009">Jesharelah</a>, he, his sons, and his brethren, were twelve:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> The eighth to <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#007">Jeshaiah</a>, he, his sons, and his brethren, were twelve:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> The ninth to <a href="../ebd/ebd243.htm#009">Mattaniah</a>, he, his sons, and his brethren, were twelve:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> The tenth to <a href="../ebd/ebd338.htm#002">Shimei</a>, he, his sons, and his brethren, were twelve:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> The eleventh to Azareel, he, his sons, and his brethren, were twelve:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> The twelfth to <a href="../ebd/ebd166.htm#001">Hashabiah</a>, he, his sons, and his brethren, were twelve:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> The thirteenth to Shubael, he, his sons, and his brethren, were twelve:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> The fourteenth to <a href="../ebd/ebd244.htm#006">Mattithiah</a>, he, his sons, and his brethren, were twelve:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> The fifteenth to Jeremoth, he, his sons, and his brethren, were twelve:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> The sixteenth to <a href="../ebd/ebd162.htm#004">Hananiah</a>, he, his sons, and his brethren, were twelve:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> The seventeenth to Joshbekashah, he, his sons, and his brethren, were twelve:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> The eighteenth to <a href="../ebd/ebd162.htm#003">Hanani</a>, he, his sons, and his brethren, were twelve:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> The nineteenth to <a href="../ebd/ebd239.htm#004">Mallothi</a>, he, his sons, and his brethren, were twelve:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> The twentieth to <a href="../ebd/ebd116.htm#004">Eliathah</a>, he, his sons, and his brethren, were twelve:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="028">28</a> The one and twentieth to Hothir, he, his sons, and his brethren, were twelve:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="029">29</a> The two and twentieth to Giddalti, he, his sons, and his brethren, were twelve:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="030">30</a> The three and twentieth to <a href="../ebd/ebd237.htm#008">Mahazioth</a>, he, his sons, and his brethren, were twelve:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="031">31</a> The four and twentieth to Romamtiezer, he, his sons, and his brethren, were twelve.<br>
 </p>
