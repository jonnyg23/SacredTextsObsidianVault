 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Joshua Chapter 13</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Now <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#006">Joshua</a> was old and stricken in years; and the LORD said unto him, Thou art old and stricken in years, and there remaineth yet very much land to be possessed.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> This is the land that yet remaineth: all the borders of the <a href="../ebd/ebd294.htm#002">Philistines</a>, and all Geshuri,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> From <a href="../ebd/ebd343.htm#003">Sihor</a>, which is before <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a>, even unto the borders of <a href="../ebd/ebd114.htm#001">Ekron</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd275.htm#002">northward</a>, which is counted to the <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#008">Canaanite</a>: five lords of the <a href="../ebd/ebd294.htm#002">Philistines</a>; the Gazathites, and the Ashdothites, the Eshkalonites, the Gittites, and the Ekronites; also the Avites:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> From the south, all the land of the <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#007">Canaanites</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd245.htm#003">Mearah</a> that is beside the Sidonians unto Aphek, to the borders of the <a href="../ebd/ebd021.htm#006">Amorites</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And the land of the Giblites, and all <a href="../ebd/ebd226.htm#001">Lebanon</a>, toward the sunrising, from Baalgad under <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#007">mount</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd175.htm#006">Hermon</a> unto the entering into <a href="../ebd/ebd160.htm#005">Hamath</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> All the inhabitants of the hill country from <a href="../ebd/ebd226.htm#001">Lebanon</a> unto Misrephothmaim, and all the Sidonians, them will I drive out from before the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>: only divide thou it by lot unto the Israelites for an inheritance, as I have commanded thee.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Now therefore divide this land for an inheritance unto the nine tribes, and the half <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd240.htm#002">Manasseh</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> With whom the Reubenites and the Gadites have received their inheritance, which <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> gave them, beyond <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#004">Jordan</a> eastward, even as <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> the servant of the LORD gave them;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> From <a href="../ebd/ebd032.htm#001">Aroer</a>, that is upon the bank of the river <a href="../ebd/ebd032.htm#000">Arnon</a>, and the city that is in the midst of the river, and all the <a href="../ebd/ebd297.htm#002">plain</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd246.htm#000">Medeba</a> unto <a href="../ebd/ebd103.htm#005">Dibon</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And all the cities of <a href="../ebd/ebd343.htm#002">Sihon</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd021.htm#006">Amorites</a>, which reigned in <a href="../ebd/ebd176.htm#009">Heshbon</a>, unto the border of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd021.htm#002">Ammon</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd148.htm#009">Gilead</a>, and the border of the <a href="../ebd/ebd147.htm#002">Geshurites</a> and Maachathites, and all <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#007">mount</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd175.htm#006">Hermon</a>, and all <a href="../ebd/ebd046.htm#003">Bashan</a> unto <a href="../ebd/ebd319.htm#000">Salcah</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> All the kingdom of <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#002">Og</a> in <a href="../ebd/ebd046.htm#003">Bashan</a>, which reigned in <a href="../ebd/ebd034.htm#004">Ashtaroth</a> and in <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#002">Edrei</a>, who remained of the remnant of the <a href="../ebd/ebd147.htm#006">giants</a>: for these did <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> smite, and cast them out.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> Nevertheless the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> expelled not the <a href="../ebd/ebd147.htm#002">Geshurites</a>, nor the Maachathites: but the <a href="../ebd/ebd147.htm#002">Geshurites</a> and the Maachathites dwell among the Israelites until this day.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> Only unto the tribes of <a href="../ebd/ebd227.htm#006">Levi</a> he gave none inheritance; the sacrifices of the LORD God of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> made by fire are their inheritance, as he said unto them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> gave unto the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd311.htm#009">Reuben</a> inheritance according to their families.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> And their coast was from <a href="../ebd/ebd032.htm#001">Aroer</a>, that is on the bank of the river <a href="../ebd/ebd032.htm#000">Arnon</a>, and the city that is in the midst of the river, and all the <a href="../ebd/ebd297.htm#002">plain</a> by <a href="../ebd/ebd246.htm#000">Medeba</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd176.htm#009">Heshbon</a>, and all her cities that are in the <a href="../ebd/ebd297.htm#002">plain</a>; <a href="../ebd/ebd103.htm#005">Dibon</a>, and Bamothbaal, and Bethbaalmeon,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> And Jahaza, and <a href="../ebd/ebd216.htm#005">Kedemoth</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd248.htm#005">Mephaath</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd220.htm#001">Kirjathaim</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd342.htm#006">Sibmah</a>, and Zarethshahar in the <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#007">mount</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd376.htm#008">valley</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> And Bethpeor, and Ashdothpisgah, and Bethjeshimoth,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> And all the cities of the <a href="../ebd/ebd297.htm#002">plain</a>, and all the kingdom of <a href="../ebd/ebd343.htm#002">Sihon</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd021.htm#006">Amorites</a>, which reigned in <a href="../ebd/ebd176.htm#009">Heshbon</a>, whom <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> smote with the princes of <a href="../ebd/ebd253.htm#007">Midian</a>, Evi, and <a href="../ebd/ebd310.htm#002">Rekem</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd396.htm#005">Zur</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd184.htm#009">Hur</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd308.htm#000">Reba</a>, which were dukes of <a href="../ebd/ebd343.htm#002">Sihon</a>, dwelling in the country.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd042.htm#003">Balaam</a> also the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd052.htm#009">Beor</a>, the <a href="../ebd/ebd348.htm#003">soothsayer</a>, did the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> slay with the <a href="../ebd/ebd354.htm#003">sword</a> among them that were slain by them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> And the border of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd311.htm#009">Reuben</a> was <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#004">Jordan</a>, and the border thereof. This was the inheritance of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd311.htm#009">Reuben</a> after their families, the cities and the <a href="../ebd/ebd377.htm#003">villages</a> thereof.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> gave inheritance unto the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd140.htm#007">Gad</a>, even unto the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd140.htm#007">Gad</a> according to their families.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> And their coast was Jazer, and all the cities of <a href="../ebd/ebd148.htm#009">Gilead</a>, and half the land of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd021.htm#002">Ammon</a>, unto <a href="../ebd/ebd032.htm#001">Aroer</a> that is before <a href="../ebd/ebd304.htm#005">Rabbah</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> And from <a href="../ebd/ebd176.htm#009">Heshbon</a> unto Ramathmizpeh, and Betonim; and from <a href="../ebd/ebd237.htm#005">Mahanaim</a> unto the border of <a href="../ebd/ebd099.htm#007">Debir</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> And in the <a href="../ebd/ebd376.htm#008">valley</a>, Betharam, and Bethnimrah, and <a href="../ebd/ebd352.htm#008">Succoth</a>, and Zaphon, the rest of the kingdom of <a href="../ebd/ebd343.htm#002">Sihon</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd176.htm#009">Heshbon</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#004">Jordan</a> and his border, even unto the edge of the sea of <a href="../ebd/ebd081.htm#000">Chinnereth</a> on the other side <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#004">Jordan</a> eastward.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="028">28</a> This is the inheritance of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd140.htm#007">Gad</a> after their families, the cities, and their <a href="../ebd/ebd377.htm#003">villages</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="029">29</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> gave inheritance unto the half <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd240.htm#002">Manasseh</a>: and this was the possession of the half <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd240.htm#002">Manasseh</a> by their families.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="030">30</a> And their coast was from <a href="../ebd/ebd237.htm#005">Mahanaim</a>, all <a href="../ebd/ebd046.htm#003">Bashan</a>, all the kingdom of <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#002">Og</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd046.htm#003">Bashan</a>, and all the towns of <a href="../ebd/ebd196.htm#001">Jair</a>, which are in <a href="../ebd/ebd046.htm#003">Bashan</a>, threescore cities:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="031">31</a> And half <a href="../ebd/ebd148.htm#009">Gilead</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd034.htm#004">Ashtaroth</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#002">Edrei</a>, cities of the kingdom of <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#002">Og</a> in <a href="../ebd/ebd046.htm#003">Bashan</a>, were pertaining unto the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd235.htm#006">Machir</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd240.htm#002">Manasseh</a>, even to the one half of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd235.htm#006">Machir</a> by their families.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="032">32</a> These are the countries which <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> did distribute for inheritance in the plains of <a href="../ebd/ebd258.htm#005">Moab</a>, on the other side <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#004">Jordan</a>, by <a href="../ebd/ebd203.htm#008">Jericho</a>, eastward.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="033">33</a> But unto the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd227.htm#006">Levi</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> gave not any inheritance: the LORD God of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> was their inheritance, as he said unto them.<br>
 </p>
