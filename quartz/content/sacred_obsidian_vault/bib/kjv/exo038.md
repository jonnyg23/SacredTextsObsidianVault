 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Exodus Chapter 38</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> And he made the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> of burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd339.htm#009">shittim</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd383.htm#003">wood</a>: five cubits was the length thereof, and five cubits the breadth thereof; it was foursquare; and three cubits the height thereof.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> And he made the horns thereof on the four corners of it; the horns thereof were of the same: and he overlaid it with <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#003">brass</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And he made all the vessels of the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>, the pots, and the shovels, and the basons, and the fleshhooks, and the firepans: all the vessels thereof made he of <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#003">brass</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And he made for the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> a brasen <a href="../ebd/ebd154.htm#007">grate</a> of network under the compass thereof beneath unto the midst of it.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And he cast four rings for the four ends of the <a href="../ebd/ebd154.htm#007">grate</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#003">brass</a>, to be places for the staves.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And he made the staves of <a href="../ebd/ebd339.htm#009">shittim</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd383.htm#003">wood</a>, and overlaid them with <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#003">brass</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And he put the staves into the rings on the sides of the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>, to bear it withal; he made the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> hollow with boards.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And he made the <a href="../ebd/ebd224.htm#009">laver</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#003">brass</a>, and the foot of it of <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#003">brass</a>, of the lookingglasses of the women assembling, which assembled at the door of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And he made the court: on the south side southward the hangings of the court were of fine twined <a href="../ebd/ebd229.htm#008">linen</a>, an hundred cubits:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> Their pillars were twenty, and their brasen sockets twenty; the hooks of the pillars and their <a href="../ebd/ebd133.htm#002">fillets</a> were of <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#000">silver</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And for the north side the hangings were an hundred cubits, their pillars were twenty, and their sockets of <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#003">brass</a> twenty; the hooks of the pillars and their <a href="../ebd/ebd133.htm#002">fillets</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#000">silver</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And for the west side were hangings of fifty cubits, their pillars ten, and their sockets ten; the hooks of the pillars and their <a href="../ebd/ebd133.htm#002">fillets</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#000">silver</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> And for the <a href="../ebd/ebd111.htm#000">east</a> side eastward fifty cubits.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> The hangings of the one side of the <a href="../ebd/ebd143.htm#008">gate</a> were fifteen cubits; their pillars three, and their sockets three.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And for the other side of the court <a href="../ebd/ebd143.htm#008">gate</a>, on this hand and that hand, were hangings of fifteen cubits; their pillars three, and their sockets three.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> All the hangings of the court round about were of fine twined <a href="../ebd/ebd229.htm#008">linen</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> And the sockets for the pillars were of <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#003">brass</a>; the hooks of the pillars and their <a href="../ebd/ebd133.htm#002">fillets</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#000">silver</a>; and the overlaying of their chapiters of <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#000">silver</a>; and all the pillars of the court were filleted with <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#000">silver</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd163.htm#001">hanging</a> for the <a href="../ebd/ebd143.htm#008">gate</a> of the court was needlework, of <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#003">blue</a>, and purple, and <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#005">scarlet</a>, and fine twined <a href="../ebd/ebd229.htm#008">linen</a>: and twenty cubits was the length, and the height in the breadth was five cubits, answerable to the hangings of the court.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> And their pillars were four, and their sockets of <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#003">brass</a> four; their hooks of <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#000">silver</a>, and the overlaying of their chapiters and their <a href="../ebd/ebd133.htm#002">fillets</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#000">silver</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> And all the pins of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a>, and of the court round about, were of <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#003">brass</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> This is the sum of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a>, even of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd362.htm#008">testimony</a>, as it was counted, according to the commandment of <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, for the service of the Levites, by the hand of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#005">Ithamar</a>, son to <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd057.htm#009">Bezaleel</a> the son Uri, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd184.htm#009">Hur</a>, of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>, made all that the LORD commanded <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> And with him was <a href="../ebd/ebd015.htm#006">Aholiab</a>, son of Ahisamach, of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd096.htm#009">Dan</a>, an <a href="../ebd/ebd121.htm#001">engraver</a>, and a cunning workman, and an embroiderer in <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#003">blue</a>, and in purple, and in <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#005">scarlet</a>, and fine <a href="../ebd/ebd229.htm#008">linen</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> All the <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a> that was occupied for the work in all the work of the holy place, even the <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, was twenty and nine talents, and <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> hundred and thirty shekels, after the <a href="../ebd/ebd333.htm#009">shekel</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd321.htm#005">sanctuary</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#000">silver</a> of them that were numbered of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> was an hundred talents, and a thousand <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> hundred and threescore and fifteen shekels, after the <a href="../ebd/ebd333.htm#009">shekel</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd321.htm#005">sanctuary</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> A <a href="../ebd/ebd051.htm#004">bekah</a> for every man, that is, half a <a href="../ebd/ebd333.htm#009">shekel</a>, after the <a href="../ebd/ebd333.htm#009">shekel</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd321.htm#005">sanctuary</a>, for every one that went to be numbered, from twenty years old and upward, for six hundred thousand and three thousand and five hundred and fifty men.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> And of the hundred talents of <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#000">silver</a> were cast the sockets of the <a href="../ebd/ebd321.htm#005">sanctuary</a>, and the sockets of the vail; an hundred sockets of the hundred talents, a <a href="../ebd/ebd357.htm#009">talent</a> for a socket.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="028">28</a> And of the thousand <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> hundred seventy and five shekels he made hooks for the pillars, and overlaid their chapiters, and filleted them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="029">29</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#003">brass</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> was seventy talents, and two thousand and four hundred shekels.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="030">30</a> And therewith he made the sockets to the door of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>, and the brasen <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>, and the brasen <a href="../ebd/ebd154.htm#007">grate</a> for it, and all the vessels of the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="031">31</a> And the sockets of the court round about, and the sockets of the court <a href="../ebd/ebd143.htm#008">gate</a>, and all the pins of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a>, and all the pins of the court round about.<br>
 </p>
