 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 38</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> O lord, rebuke me not in thy wrath: neither chasten me in thy hot displeasure.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> For thine <a href="../ebd/ebd032.htm#005">arrows</a> stick <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#004">fast</a> in me, and thy hand presseth me sore.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> There is no soundness in my <a href="../ebd/ebd135.htm#007">flesh</a> because of thine <a href="../ebd/ebd024.htm#002">anger</a>; neither is there any rest in my bones because of my <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> For mine iniquities are gone over mine head: as an heavy <a href="../ebd/ebd066.htm#007">burden</a> they are too heavy for me.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> My wounds stink and are corrupt because of my foolishness.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> I am troubled; I am bowed down greatly; I go mourning all the day long.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> For my loins are filled with a loathsome disease: and there is no soundness in my <a href="../ebd/ebd135.htm#007">flesh</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> I am feeble and sore broken: I have roared by reason of the disquietness of my heart.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> Lord, all my desire is before thee; and my groaning is not hid from thee.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> My heart panteth, my strength faileth me: as for the light of mine eyes, it also is gone from me.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> My lovers and my friends stand aloof from my sore; and my kinsmen stand afar off.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> They also that seek after my life lay snares for me: and they that seek my hurt speak mischievous things, and imagine deceits all the day long.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> But I, as a deaf man, heard not; and I was as a <a href="../ebd/ebd109.htm#002">dumb</a> man that openeth not his mouth.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> Thus I was as a man that heareth not, and in whose mouth are no reproofs.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> For in thee, O LORD, do I hope: thou wilt hear, O Lord my God.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> For I said, Hear me, lest otherwise they should rejoice over me: when my foot slippeth, they magnify themselves against me.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> For I am ready to <a href="../ebd/ebd160.htm#002">halt</a>, and my sorrow is continually before me.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> For I will declare mine iniquity; I will be sorry for my <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> But mine enemies are lively, and they are strong: and they that hate me wrongfully are multiplied.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> They also that render evil for good are mine adversaries; because I follow the thing that good is.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> Forsake me not, O LORD: O my God, be not far from me.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> Make haste to help me, O Lord my <a href="../ebd/ebd320.htm#003">salvation</a>.<br>
 </p>
