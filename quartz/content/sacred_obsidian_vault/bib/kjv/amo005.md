 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Amos Chapter 5</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Hear ye this word which I take up against you, even a <a href="../ebd/ebd223.htm#005">lamentation</a>, O house of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> The <a href="../ebd/ebd377.htm#009">virgin</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> is fallen; she shall no more rise: she is forsaken upon her land; there is none to raise her up.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> For thus saith the Lord GOD; The city that went out by a thousand shall leave an hundred, and that which went forth by an hundred shall leave ten, to the house of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> For thus saith the LORD unto the house of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, Seek ye me, and ye shall live:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> But seek not <a href="../ebd/ebd055.htm#006">Bethel</a>, nor enter into <a href="../ebd/ebd149.htm#001">Gilgal</a>, and pass not to <a href="../ebd/ebd050.htm#008">Beersheba</a>: for <a href="../ebd/ebd149.htm#001">Gilgal</a> shall surely go into <a href="../ebd/ebd072.htm#002">captivity</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd055.htm#006">Bethel</a> shall come to nought.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Seek the LORD, and ye shall live; lest he break out like fire in the house of <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#005">Joseph</a>, and devour it, and there be none to quench it in <a href="../ebd/ebd055.htm#006">Bethel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Ye who turn judgment to <a href="../ebd/ebd384.htm#001">wormwood</a>, and leave off <a href="../ebd/ebd313.htm#004">righteousness</a> in the earth,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> Seek him that maketh the <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd350.htm#008">stars</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd280.htm#005">Orion</a>, and turneth the <a href="../ebd/ebd330.htm#002">shadow</a> of death into the morning, and maketh the day dark with night: that calleth for the waters of the sea, and poureth them out upon the face of the earth: The LORD is his name:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> That strengtheneth the spoiled against the strong, so that the spoiled shall come against the fortress.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> They hate him that rebuketh in the <a href="../ebd/ebd143.htm#008">gate</a>, and they abhor him that speaketh uprightly.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> Forasmuch therefore as your treading is upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd298.htm#006">poor</a>, and ye take from him burdens of <a href="../ebd/ebd381.htm#000">wheat</a>: ye have built houses of hewn <a href="../ebd/ebd351.htm#005">stone</a>, but ye shall not dwell in them; ye have planted pleasant vineyards, but ye shall not <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#000">wine</a> of them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> For I know your manifold transgressions and your mighty sins: they afflict the just, they take a <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#009">bribe</a>, and they turn aside the <a href="../ebd/ebd298.htm#006">poor</a> in the <a href="../ebd/ebd143.htm#008">gate</a> from their right.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> Therefore the prudent shall keep silence in that time; for it is an evil time.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> Seek good, and not evil, that ye may live: and so the LORD, the God of hosts, shall be with you, as ye have spoken.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> Hate the evil, and love the good, and establish judgment in the <a href="../ebd/ebd143.htm#008">gate</a>: it may be that the LORD God of hosts will be gracious unto the remnant of <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#005">Joseph</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> Therefore the LORD, the God of hosts, the LORD, saith thus; Wailing shall be in all streets; and they shall say in all the highways, Alas! alas!  and they shall call the <a href="../ebd/ebd185.htm#002">husbandman</a> to mourning, and such as are skilful of <a href="../ebd/ebd223.htm#005">lamentation</a> to wailing.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> And in all vineyards shall be wailing: for I will pass through thee, saith the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> Woe unto you that desire the day of the LORD! to what end is it for you? the day of the LORD is <a href="../ebd/ebd097.htm#008">darkness</a>, and not light.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> As if a man did flee from a lion, and a bear met him; or went into the house, and leaned his hand on the wall, and a <a href="../ebd/ebd328.htm#009">serpent</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd059.htm#006">bit</a> him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> Shall not the day of the LORD be <a href="../ebd/ebd097.htm#008">darkness</a>, and not light? even very dark, and no brightness in it?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> I hate, I despise your <a href="../ebd/ebd132.htm#000">feast</a> days, and I will not smell in your solemn assemblies.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> Though ye offer me burnt offerings and your meat offerings, I will not accept them: neither will I regard the peace offerings of your <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#005">fat</a> beasts.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> Take thou away from me the noise of thy <a href="../ebd/ebd348.htm#002">songs</a>; for I will not hear the melody of thy viols.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> But let judgment run down as waters, and <a href="../ebd/ebd313.htm#004">righteousness</a> as a mighty stream.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> Have ye offered unto me sacrifices and offerings in the <a href="../ebd/ebd381.htm#005">wilderness</a> forty years, O house of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> But ye have borne the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of your <a href="../ebd/ebd259.htm#000">Moloch</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd081.htm#004">Chiun</a> your images, the star of your god, which ye made to yourselves.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> Therefore will I cause you to go into <a href="../ebd/ebd072.htm#002">captivity</a> beyond <a href="../ebd/ebd096.htm#007">Damascus</a>, saith the LORD, whose name is The God of hosts.<br>
 </p>
