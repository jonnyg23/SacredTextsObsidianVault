 <p><a name="page_4"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 4</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">MY HEART IS TURNED INTO A WAILING CHILD</h3>  <h3 align="center">BY N. KOUCHAK</h3>  
 <p align="center">(<i>Fifteenth Century</i>)</p>  
 <p></p><dir>MY heart is turned into a wailing child,<br>      In vain with sweets I seek to still its cries;<br> Sweet love, it calls for thee in sobbings wild<br>      All day and night, with longing and with sighs.<br>           What solace can I give it?  
 <p>I showed my eyes the fair ones of this earth<br>      And tried to please them--but I tried in vain.<br> Sweet love, for them all those were nothing worth--<br>      Thee--only thee my heart would have again.<br>           What solace can I give it?</p></dir>
