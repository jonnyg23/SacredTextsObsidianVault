 <p><a name="page_31"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 31</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">BIRTHDAY SONG</h3>  <h5 align="center">BY NAHABED KOUCHAK</h5>  
 <p></p><dir>ON the morning of thy birth<br>      We were glad but thou wert wailing,<br> See that when thou leav’st the earth<br>      Thou art glad and we bewailing.  
 <p>Let me speak unto thy heart,--<br>      List if thou hast understanding;<br> Keep thyself from fools apart,<br>      All their flatteries withstanding.</p>  
 <p>For the fool, like fire and heat,<br>      Scorcheth everything, and burneth;<br> But the wise, like water sweet,<br>      Deserts into gardens turneth.</p></dir>
