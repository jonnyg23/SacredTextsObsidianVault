 <p><a name="page_113"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 113</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">THE CHRIST-CHILD</h3>  <h4 align="center">BY SAINT GREGORY OF NAREK</h4>  
 <p align="center">(951-1009)</p>  
 <p></p><dir>THE lips of the Christ-child are like to twin leaves;<br> They let roses fall when he smiles tenderly.<br> The tears of the Christ-child are pearls when he grieves;<br> The eyes of the Christ-child are deep as the sea.<br> Like pomegranate grains are the dimples he hath,<br> And clustering lilies spring up in his path.  
 <p>                               <i>Translated by Alice Stone Blackwell</i>.</p></dir>
