 <p><a name="page_30"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 30</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">THE SUN WENT DOWN</h3>  <h5 align="center">BY AVETIS ISAHAKIAN</h5>  
 <p></p><dir>THE sun went down behind the hill,<br>      No light was on the lea,<br> The fowls and birds slept calm and still,<br>      But sleep came not to me.  
 <p>The moon peeped in beneath the eaves,<br>      The Balance rose on high,<br> The fresh night-wind that stirred the leaves<br>      Spoke to the starry sky.</p>  
 <p>Ah, gentle winds and stars of light,<br>      Where is my love to-night?<br> Ye painted eyes of heaven so bright,--<br>      Saw you my love to-night?</p>  
 <p>Day dawned,--unbolted was our door:--<br>      The snowflakes whirled like foam,<br> ’Tis cloud and storm, the wild winds roar<br>      Why comes my love not home?</p></dir>
