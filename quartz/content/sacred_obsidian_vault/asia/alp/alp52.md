 <p><a name="page_73"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 73</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">THE EVE OF ASCENSION DAY <a name="fr_8"></a><a href="#fn_8"><font size="1">1</font></a></h3>  <h5 align="center">BY DERENIK DEMIRJIAN</h5>  
 <p></p><dir>NIGHT with her ebon hair and starry crown<br>   Upon the hills came down.<br> Her loosened tresses floated all unbound<br>   And veiled her form around.  
 <p>The fountain murmured like an endless tale<br>   On her entrancing lips; and it would seem<br> As if God spake within the silent vale,<br>   And sleeping Earth were listening, in a dream.</p>  
 <p>Like blackened clouds, in Jorokh's stream arise<br>   Those rocks that through her savage waters pierce;<br> Like dragons twain, they glare with threatening eyes,<br>   Facing each other, arrogant and fierce.</p>  
 <p>Wild Jorokh through that fearsome valley flows--<br>   Flows like a caravan that onward sweeps;<br> First roaring loud, then hushed into repose,<br>   Groping its way through darkness, on it creeps.</p>  
 <p>The sounds of Earth are melted into rest,<br>   While strikes the hour of expectation deep;<br> Earth's waters heave, against each other pressed,<br>   And breathless listening, all their vigils keep.</p>  
 <p>Decked out like lovely brides stand all the flowers;<br>   With nuptial joy the forests trembling wait:<br> Until Heaven's blessing fall in sacred showers,<br>   And whispering softly, each may clasp its mate.</p></dir>
