 <p><a name="page_101"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 101</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">DANCE SONG <a name="fr_12"></a><a href="#fn_12"><font size="1">1</font></a></h3>  <h5 align="center">FOLK SONG</h5>  
 <p></p><dir>"I HAVE loved your winsome face,<br> And your never-fading grace.<br> If they give you not to me<br> May God send them black disgrace!"  
 <p>"Mountain sorrel, fresh with dew,<br> Sweets I send and honey new;<br> Is a dainty maid like me<br> Fit to wed a youth like you?"</p>  
 <p>"You are arch, my little maid,<br> In four plaits your hair you braid,<br> Make no more pretence to me--<br> For you love me, I'm afraid!"</p>  
 <p>"Drive your plough ahead, and go;<br> Underneath it thistles grow.<br> You are reckless, young, and wild--<br> She is mad would wed you so!"</p>  
 <p>"Near your house a field I'll sow<br> And I'll stone the ill-starred crow.<br> When I have the girl I love<br> I'll let all my folly go."</p></dir>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p><a name="page_102"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 102</font></a></p>  
 <p></p><dir>"On its way the water flows,<br> Washing with its waves the rose.<br> My beloved amidst the youths,<br> Like a mighty fortress shows."  
 <p>"In the vineyard you have grown,<br> Where the melon plants are sown,<br> Day and night upon my lute,<br> You I sing, and you alone."</p>  
 <p>"Sing a minstrel's song to me,<br> Or the blackbird's rhapsody;<br> All your praises I deserve,<br> And my bridegroom you shall be!"</p></dir>
