 <p><a name="page_20"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 20</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">SPRING</h3>  <h5 align="center">BY HOVHANNES HOVHANNESSIAN</h5>  
 <p align="center">(<i>Born</i> 1869)</p>  
 <p></p><dir>NONE await thy smiling rays;<br> Whither comest thou, O Spring<br> None are left to sing thy praise--<br> Vain thy coming now, O Spring!  
 <p>All the world is wrapped in gloom,<br> Earth in blood is weltering:<br> This year brought us blackest doom--<br> Whither comest thou, O Spring?</p>  
 <p>No rose for the nightingale,<br> No flower within park or dale,<br> Every face with anguish pale--<br> Whither comest thou, O Spring?</p></dir>
