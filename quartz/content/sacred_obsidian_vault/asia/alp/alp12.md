 <p><a name="page_6"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 6</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">BLACK EYES</h3>  <h5 align="center">BY AVETIS ISAHAKIAN</h5>  
 <p align="center">(<i>Born </i>1875)</p>  
 <p></p><dir>Do not trust black eyes, but fear them:--<br>     Gloom they are, and endless night;<br> Woes and perils lurking near them<br>     Love not thou their gleaming bright!  
 <p>In my heart a sea of blood wells,<br>     Called up by their cruel might,<br> No calm ever in that flood dwells<br>     Love not thou their gleaming bright!</p></dir>
