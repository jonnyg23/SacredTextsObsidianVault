 <h1 align="CENTER">NOTE.</h1>
 <font face="Times Roman,Times New Roman"><p>The present volume was intended to include a collection of Kamchadal texts. Owing to the war, it has been impossible to communicate with Mr. Bogoras; and since the volume has been in type for over two years, it seems best to publish the collection of Koryak texts alone.</p>
 <p>There is some inconsistency in spelling the verbal endings -<i>lin</i> and -<i>łen</i>. These ought to be read consistently as given here. The forms -<i>łin</i> and -<i>len</i> are incorrect. There is no <i>g</i> in Koryak. Wherever this occurs, it should be read <i>ġ</i>.</p>
 <p align="RIGHT">EDITOR.</p>
 <p>November, 1916.</p>
