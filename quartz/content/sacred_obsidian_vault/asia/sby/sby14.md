 <p><a name="page_73"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 73</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">THE PRINCIPAL PRAYER OF THE YEZIDIS</h3>  
 <p></p><dir>Amen, Amen, Amen!<br> Through the intermediation of Šams-ad-Dîn,<br> Fahr ad-Dîn, Nasir-ad-Dîn,<br> Sajad ad-Dîn, Šeiḫ Sin (Husein),<br> Šeiḫ Bakr, Ḳâdir ar-Raḥmân.<br> Lord, thou art gracious, thou art merciful;<br> Thou art God, king of kings and lands,<br> King of joy and happiness,<br> King of good possession (eternal life).<br> From eternity thou art eternal.<br> Thou art the seat of luck (happiness) and life;<br> Thou art lord of grace and good luck.<br> Thou art king of jinns and human beings,<br> King of the holy men (saints),<br> Lord of terror and praise,<br> The abode of religious duty and praise,<br> Worthy of praise and thanks.<br> Lord! Protector in journeys,<br> Sovereign of the moon and of the darkness,<br> God of the sun and of the fire,<br> God of the great throne,<br> Lord of goodness.<br> Lord! No one knows how thou art.<br> Thou hast no beauty; thou hast no height.<br> Thou hast no going forth; thou hast no number.<br> Lord! judge of kings and beggars,<br> Judge of society and of the world,<br> Thou hast revealed the repentance of Adam.<br> Lord, thou hast no house; thou hast no money; <a name="page_74"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 74</font></a><br> Thou hast no wings, hast no feathers;<br> Thou hast no voice, thou hast no color.<br> Thou hast made us lucky and satisfied.<br> Thou hast created Jesus and Mary.<br> Lord, thou art gracious,<br> Merciful, faithful.<br> Thou art Lord; I am nothingness.<br> I am a fallen sinner,<br> A sinner by thee remembered.<br> Thou hast led us out of darkness into light.<br> Lord! My sin and my guilt,<br> Take them and remove them.<br> O God, O God, O God, Amen!</dir>
