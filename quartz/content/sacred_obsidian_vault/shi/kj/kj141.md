 <p><a name="page_363"><font size="1" color="green">p. 363</font></a></p>  <h2 align="center">[SECT. CXXXIV.—EMPEROR RI-CHIU (PART IV.—VARIOUS DEEDS).]</h2>  
 <p>The Heavenly Sovereign thereupon first appointed the Suzerain of Achi to the office of Treasurer, <a name="fr_2173"></a><a href="#fn_2168"><font size="1">1</font></a> and likewise bestowed on him domains. <a name="fr_2174"></a><a href="#fn_2169"><font size="1">2</font></a> Again in this august reign the name of Waka-sakura Tribe <a name="fr_2175"></a><a href="#fn_2170"><font size="1">3</font></a> was granted to the Grandees of the Waka-sakura Tribe. <a name="fr_2176"></a><a href="#fn_2171"><font size="1">4</font></a> Again the gentile name <a name="fr_2177"></a><a href="#fn_2172"><font size="1">5</font></a> of Dukes of Himeda <a name="fr_2178"></a><a href="#fn_2173"><font size="1">6</font></a> was granted to the Dukes of Himeda. Again the Ihare Clan <a name="fr_2179"></a><a href="#fn_2174"><font size="1">7</font></a> was established.</p>
