 <p>So the Heavenly Sovereign died, and His Augustness Ohoke ruled the succession of Heaven's sun. <a name="fr_2409"></a><a href="#fn_2404"><font size="1">1</font></a> The Heavenly Sovereign's august years were thirty-eight</p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p><a name="page_422"><font size="1" color="green">p. 422</font></a></p>  
 <p>years. His august mausoleum is on the mound of Ihatsuki at Katawoka. <a name="fr_2410"></a><a href="#fn_2405"><font size="1">2</font></a></p>
