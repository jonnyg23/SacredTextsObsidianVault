 <p><a name="page_298"><font size="1" color="green">p. 298</font></a> [240]</p>  <h2 align="center">[SECT. CIII.—EMPEROR CHIŪ-AI (PART IX.—HIS DEATH AND THAT OF THE EMPRESS JIN-GŌ).]</h2>  
 <p>Altogether the august years of this Heavenly Sovereign Tarashi-naka-tsu-hiko <a name="fr_1815"></a><a href="#fn_1810"><font size="1">1</font></a> were fifty-two. His august mausoleum is at Nagaye, <a name="fr_1816"></a><a href="#fn_1811"><font size="1">2</font></a> near Wega, <a name="fr_1817"></a><a href="#fn_1812"><font size="1">3</font></a> in Kafuchi. (<small>The Empress died at the august age of one hundred. She was buried in the mausoleum of Tatanami in Saki. <a name="fr_1818"></a><a href="#fn_1813"><font size="1">4</font></a></small>)</p>
