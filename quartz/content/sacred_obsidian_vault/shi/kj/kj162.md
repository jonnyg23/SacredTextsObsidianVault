 <p><a name="page_395"><font size="1" color="green">p. 395</font></a></p>  <h2 align="center">[SECT. CLV.—EMPEROR YŪ-RIYAKU (PART VI.—HE MAKES A PROGRESS TO YESHINU).]</h2>  
 <p>When the Heavenly Sovereign made a progress to the palace of Yeshinu, <a name="fr_2326"></a><a href="#fn_2321"><font size="1">1</font></a> there was on the bank of the Yeshinu</p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p><a name="page_396"><font size="1" color="green">p. 396</font></a></p>  
 <p>river a girl of beautiful appearance. So having wedded this girl, he returned to the Palace. Afterwards, when he again made a progress to Yeshinu, he halted where he had met the girl, and in that place raised a great august throne, <a name="fr_2327"></a><a href="#fn_2322"><font size="1">2a</font></a> seated himself on that august throne, played on his august lute, and made the maiden dance. Then he composed an august Song on account of the maiden's good dancing. That Song said:</p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">"Oh! that the maiden dancing to the lute-playing, of the august hand of the Deity seated on the throne might continue for ever!" <a name="fr_2328"></a><a href="#fn_2323"><font size="1">3</font></a></div>  
 <p> </p>
