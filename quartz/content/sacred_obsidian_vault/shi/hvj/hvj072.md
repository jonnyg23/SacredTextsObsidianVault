 <img src="img/07100.jpg"></p>  <h3 align="center">71</h3>  <h3 align="center">THE FIRST ADVISER OF STATE TSUNE-NOBU</h3>  <h3 align="center">DAI-NAGON TSUNE-NOBU</h3>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">  Yūsareba<br> Kado-da no inaba<br>   Otozurete<br> Ashi no maroya ni<br> Aki kaze zo fuku.  
 <p>THIS autumn night the wind blows shrill,<br>   And would that I could catch<br> Its message, as it whistles through<br>   The rushes in the thatch<br>   And leaves of my rice-patch.</p></div>  
 <p><small>Tsune-nobu, a member of the Minamoto family, was famous as a man of letters in the eleventh century, and died in the year 1096.</small></p>
