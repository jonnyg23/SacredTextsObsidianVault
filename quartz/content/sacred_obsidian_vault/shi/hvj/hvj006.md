 <img src="img/00500.jpg"></p>  <h3 align="center">5</h3>  <h3 align="center">SARU MARU, A SHINTO OFFICIAL</h3>  <h3 align="center">SARU MARU TAIU</h3>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">  Oku yama ni<br> Momiji fumi wake<br>   Naku shika no<br> Koe kiku toki zo<br> Aki wa kanashiki.  
 <p>HEAR the stag's pathetic call<br>   Far up the mountain side,<br> While tramping o'er the maple leaves<br>   Wind-scattered far and wide<br>   This sad, sad autumn tide.</p></div>  
 <p><small>Very little is known of this writer, but he probably lived not later than A.D. 800. Stags and the crimson leaves of the maple are frequently used symbolically of autumn.</small></p>
