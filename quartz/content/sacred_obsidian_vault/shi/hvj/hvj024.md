 <img src="img/02300.jpg"></p>  <h3 align="center">23</h3>  <h3 align="center">CHISATO ŌYE</h3>  <h3 align="center">ŌYE NO CHISATO</h3>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">  Tsuki mireba<br> Chiji ni mono koso<br>   Kanashi kere<br> Waga mi hitotsu no<br> Aki ni wa aranedo.  
 <p>THIS night the cheerless autumn moon<br>   Doth all my mind enthrall;<br> But others also have their griefs,<br>   For autumn on us all<br>   Hath cast her gloomy pall.</p></div>  
 <p><small>Chisato Ōye is said to have lived about the end of the ninth century; he was the son of a Councillor, and a very fertile poet. He was also famous as a philosopher, and acted as tutor to the Emperor Sei-wa, who reigned A.D. 859-876.</small></p>
