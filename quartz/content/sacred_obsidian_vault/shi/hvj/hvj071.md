 <img src="img/07000.jpg"></p>  <h3 align="center">70</h3>  <h3 align="center">THE PRIEST RIYŌ-ZEN</h3>  <h3 align="center">RIYŌ-ZEN HOSHI</h3>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">  Sabishisa ni<br> Yado wo, tachi-idete<br>   Nagamureba<br> Izuko mo onaji<br> Aki no yūgure.  
 <p>THE prospect from my cottage shows<br>   No other hut in sight;<br> The solitude depresses me,<br>   Like deepening twilight<br>   On a chill autumn night.</p></div>  
 <p><small>Nothing is known of this author, but he appears to have lived during the eleventh century. The Priest appears in the illustration, looking out over the bare landscape, with his tiny hut in the background.</small></p>
