 <img src="img/05200.jpg"></p>  <h3 align="center">52</h3>  <h3 align="center">THE MINISTER MICHI-NOBU FUJIWARA</h3>  <h3 align="center">FUJIWARA NO MICHI-NOBU ASON</h3>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">  Akenureba<br> Kururu mono to wa<br>   Shiri nagara<br> Nao urameshiki<br> Asaborake kana.  
 <p>ALTHOUGH I know the gentle night<br>   Will surely follow morn,<br> Yet, when I'm wakened by the sun,<br>   Turn over, stretch and yawn—<br>   How I detest the dawn!</p></div>  
 <p><small>Michi-nobu lived in the tenth century. He is shown in the illustration with his wife on the verandah, watching the day break.</small></p>
