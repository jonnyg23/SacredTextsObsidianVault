 <p></p>  <h3 align="center">ANIMALS</h3>  
 <p>(<i>Dog: for example</i>)</p>  
 <p align="center"></p>  <h4 align="center">AFFIRMATIONS</h4>  
 <p>I deny any appearance of disorder. This dog is a perfect idea in Divine Mind and now expresses God's perfect Idea of a perfect dog.</p>  
 <p align="center"></p>  
 <p>Infinite Intelligence illumines and directs this animal. It is a perfect idea in Divine Mind and is always in its right place.</p>
