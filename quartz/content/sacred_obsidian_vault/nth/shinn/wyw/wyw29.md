 <p></p>  <h3 align="center">CONCLUSION</h3>  
 <p>Choose the affirmation which appeals to you the most and <i>wave it over the situation which confronts you</i>.</p>  
 <p>It is your <i>magic wand</i>, for your <i>word</i> is God in <i>action</i>.</p>  
 <p>"It shall not return unto me void but shall accomplish that whereunto it is sent." (Isaiah 55-11.)</p>  
 <p>"But I say, have they not heard? Yes, verily, their sound went into all the earth and <i>their words unto the end of the world</i>." (Romans 10-18.)</p>
