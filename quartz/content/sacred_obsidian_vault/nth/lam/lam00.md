 <p align="center">AFFECTION AND GRATITUDE FOR HAVING<br> SHOWN US THE WAY</p>

 <p>O that twere   possible<br>   After long grief and pain<br>   To find the arms of my true love<br>   Round me once again.<br>   A shadow flits before me<br>   Not thou, but like to thee.<br>   Ah, Christ, that it were possible<br>   For one short hour to see<br>   The souls we loved, that they might tell us<br>   What and where they be!<i>Tennyson</i>.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>
