 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Edinburgh Lectures on Mental Science</i>, by Thomas Troward, [1909], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">FOREWORD.</h3>  
 <p>This book contains the substance of a course of lectures recently given by the writer in the Queen Street Hall, Edinburgh. Its purpose is to indicate the <i>Natural Principles</i> governing the relation between Mental Action and Material Conditions, and thus to afford the student an intelligible starting-point for the practical study of the subject.</p>  
 <p align="right">T.T.</p>  
 <p><small>March, 1904.</small></p>
