 </p><p>PLATE XXVII</p>
 
 <p><a name="img_27"></a>
 <img src="img/27.jpg"></p>
 
 <p>How the Jews crucified our Lord. The Virgin Mary stands on His right and St. John on His left. Between the Virgin and the Cross stands Longinus, the soldier who pierced our Lord's side. The Crusaders are said to have found the body of Longinus in the church of St. Peter at Antioch in the XIth century</p>
