 </p><h3 align="CENTER">4. <i>Concerning Envy</i></h3>
 
 <p>   And when they had grown up together, <small>SATAN</small> had envy of him, and he cast this envy into the heart of <small>CAIN</small>, who was envious [of <small>ABEL</small>] first, because of the words of his father <small>ADAM</small>, who said, "He who hath the good-tempered face shall be the heir of my kingdom"; and secondly, because of his sister with the beautiful face, who was born with him and who had been given unto <small>ABEL</small>, even as God commanded them to multiply and fill the earth—now the face of the sister who had been born with <small>ABEL</small> resembled that of <small>CAIN</small>, and their father had transferred them (i.e., the two sisters) when giving them [in marriage];—and thirdly, because when the two [brothers] offered up sacrifice, God accepted the offering of <small>ABEL</small> and rejected the offering of <small>CAIN</small>. And because of this envy <small>CAIN</small> killed <small>ABEL</small>. Thus fratricide was first created through <small>SATAN'S</small> envy of the children of <small>ADAM</small>. And having killed his brother, <small>CAIN</small> fell into a state of trembling and horrible fright, and he was repulsed by his father and his Lord. And [then] <small>SETH</small> was born, and <small>ADAM</small>
 
 
 
 <a name="page_4"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 4</font></a>
 
 looked upon him and said, "Now hath God shown compassion upon me, and He hath given unto me the light of my face. In sorrowful remembrance I will console myself (?) with him. The name of him that shall slay my heir shall be blotted out, even to his ninth generation."</p>
