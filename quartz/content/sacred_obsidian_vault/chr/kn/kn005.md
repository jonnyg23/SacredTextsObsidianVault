 </p><h3 align="CENTER">5. <i>Concerning the Kingdom of </i><small>SETH</small></h3>
 
 <p>   And <small>ADAM</small> died, and <small>SETH</small> reigned in righteousness. And <small>SETH</small> died, and <small>HÊNÔS</small> (<small>ENOS</small>) reigned. And <small>HÊNÔS</small> (<small>ENOS</small>) died, and <small>ḲÂYNÂN</small> (<small>CAINAN</small>) reigned. And <small>ḲÂYNÂN</small> (<small>CAINAN</small>) died, and <small>MALÂL’ÊL</small> (<small>MAHALALEEL</small>) reigned. And <small>MALÂL’ÊL</small> (<small>MAHALALEEL</small>) died, and <small>YÂRÔD</small> (<small>JARED</small>) reigned. And <small>YÂRÔD</small> died, and <small>HÊNÔKH</small> (<small>ENOCH</small>) reigned in righteousness, and he feared God, and [God] hid him so that he might not see death. And he became a king in his flesh in the Land of the Living. And after <small>ENOCH</small> disappeared <small>MÂTÛSÂLÂ</small> (<small>METHUSELAH</small>) reigned. And <small>MÂTÛSÂLÂ</small> died, and <small>LÂMÊKH</small> (<small>LAMECH</small>) reigned. And <small>LÂMÊKH</small> died, and <small>NÔḤ</small> (<small>NOAH</small>) reigned in righteousness, and he pleased God in all his works.</p>
