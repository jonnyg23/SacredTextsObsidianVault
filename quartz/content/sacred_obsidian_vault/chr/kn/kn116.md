 </p><h3 align="CENTER">116. <i>Concerning the Chariot of </i><small>ETHIOPIA</small></h3>
 
 <p>   And the Archbishops answered and said unto <small>GREGORY</small>, the Worker of Wonders, "Behold now, thou hast told us concerning the vanquisher of the enemy of <small>RÔMÊ</small>, and now [tell us] of the chariot of <small>ETHIOPIA</small> and whether it shall remain henceforward, to the Coming of <small>CHRIST</small>, as thou hast told us concerning <small>ZION</small>, and concerning the faith of the people of <small>ETHIOPIA</small>, and likewise if their chariot shall remain." And <small>GREGORY</small> said unto them, "It shall assuredly not disappear. And again, hearken ye unto me and I will declare this unto you: A few <small>JEWS</small> shall lift up their heads against our faith in <small>NÂGRÂN</small> and in <small>ARMENIA</small> in the days after this, and this God will do by His Will so that He may destroy them, for <small>ARMENIA</small> is a territory of <small>RÔMÊ</small> and <small>NÂGRÂN</small> is a territory of <small>ETHIOPIA</small>."</p>
