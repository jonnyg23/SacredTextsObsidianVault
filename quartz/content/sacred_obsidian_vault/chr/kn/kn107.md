 <h3 align="CENTER">107. <i>Concerning His entrance into </i><small>JERUSALEM</small><i> in Glory</i></h3>
 
 <p>   And the Prophets have prophesied concerning His glorious entry into <small>JERUSALEM</small>, and <small>ISAIAH</small> the Prophet said, "Shine thou, shine thou, <small>JERUSALEM</small>, thy light hath come and the glory of God hath risen upon thee."<a name="page_209_fr_1"></a><a href="#page_209_note_1"><sup><small>1</small></sup></a></p>
 
 <p>   Thus the Prophet <small>ZECHARIAH</small> prophesied and said, "Rejoice, rejoice, daughter of <small>ZION</small>, and let <small>JERUSALEM</small> shout for joy."<a name="page_209_fr_2"></a><a href="#page_209_note_2"><sup><small>2</small></sup></a></p>
 
 <p>   Thus <small>DAVID</small> prophesied and said, "Out of the mouth of children and babes Thou hast prepared praise because of the enemy, so that Thou mightest overthrow the enemy and the avenger."<a name="page_209_fr_3"></a><a href="#page_209_note_3"><sup><small>3</small></sup></a></p>
 
 <p>   Thus <small>SOLOMON</small> prophesied and said, "The children are taught by God, and the peoples rejoice within thee."<a name="page_209_fr_4"></a><a href="#page_209_note_4"><sup><small>4</small></sup></a></p>
 
 <p>   Thus <small>DAVID</small> his father prophesied and said, "Blow ye the horn in <small>ZION</small>, on the day of the new moon, on the appointed day of our festival, for it is an ordinance for <small>ISRAEL</small>."<a name="page_209_fr_5"></a><a href="#page_209_note_5"><sup><small>5</small></sup></a></p>
 
 <p>   Thus <small>EZRA</small> the Scribe prophesied and said, "Get ye out, make ye a festival in gladness, and say unto the daughter of <small>ZION</small>, Rejoice thou, behold thy King hath come."<a name="page_209_fr_6"></a><a href="#page_209_note_6"><sup><small>6</small></sup></a></p>
 
 <p>   Thus <small>ISAIAH</small> the Prophet prophesied and said, "Rejoice thou, <small>JERUSALEM</small>, rejoice thou. Behold, thy King hath come riding upon an ass. His reward is with Him, and His work is before His face."<a name="page_209_fr_7"></a><a href="#page_209_note_7"><sup><small>7</small></sup></a></p>
 
 <p>   Thus <small>DAVID</small> the Prophet prophesied and said, "Blessed is He who cometh in the name of the Lord."<a name="page_209_fr_8"></a><a href="#page_209_note_8"><sup><small>8</small></sup></a></p>
 
 <p>   Thus <small>JACOB</small> the son of <small>ISAAC</small> prophesied and said, "<small>JUDAH</small>, thy brethren have praised thee. Thine hand is upon the back of thine enemy, and the children of thy mother shall worship thee. And the dominion
 
 
 
 <a name="page_210"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 210</font></a>
 
 shall not diminish from <small>JUDAH</small>, and the government shall not depart from his kin, until he shall find Him Who hath been waited for, and Who is the Hope of the nations."<a name="page_210_fr_1"></a><a href="#page_210_note_1"><sup><small>1</small></sup></a> And he also prophesied and said, "His teeth are white as with snow, and His eyes are glad as with wine, and He shall wash His apparel in wine and His tunic in the blood of clusters of grapes."<a name="page_210_fr_2"></a><a href="#page_210_note_2"><sup><small>2</small></sup></a> And again he prophesied, saying, "<small>JUDAH</small> is a lion's whelp; thou hast lain down, and thou hast slept; no one shall wake him up except him that hunteth until he findeth him; rise up from thy strong place."<a name="page_210_fr_3"></a><a href="#page_210_note_3"><sup><small>3</small></sup></a> And again <small>JACOB</small> blessed his son <small>JUDAH</small>, and said unto him, "There is a King who shall go forth from thee and shall wash His apparel in wine, and glorious is the place of rest of the Beloved"; now, by "Beloved" <small>CHRIST</small> is meant, and by "Messiah" <small>CHRIST</small> is meant, and <small>JESUS</small> meaneth "Saviour of the people". Now the Prophets mention <small>CHRIST</small> under a secret name and they call Him "the Beloved".</p>
 
 <p>   And <small>ISAIAH</small> spake concerning His Ascension in his prophecy, saying, "On that day the Beloved shall come down from heaven, and shall choose for Himself twelve Apostles."<a name="page_210_fr_4"></a><a href="#page_210_note_4"><sup><small>4</small></sup></a> And again he said, "I have seen the ascension of the beloved Son to the seventh heaven, and the Angels and the Archangels receiving Him, He being very much higher than they."</p>
 
 <p>   And <small>DAVID</small> said, "The beloved is like the son of the unicorn";<a name="page_210_fr_5"></a><a href="#page_210_note_5"><sup><small>5</small></sup></a> and again he said, "And thine only one from the horns of the unicorn."<a name="page_210_fr_6"></a><a href="#page_210_note_6"><sup><small>6</small></sup></a> And again he said, "Let my horn be exalted like that of the unicorn."<a name="page_210_fr_7"></a><a href="#page_210_note_7"><sup><small>7</small></sup></a> "Horns" meaneth the kingdoms of the world; and "unicorn" meaneth He Who is over His kingdom Whom no one can resist, for He is the governor of
 
 
 
 <a name="page_211"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 211</font></a>
 
 kings; He destroyeth whom He will and He setteth up him whom He will. Even as <small>DAVID</small> saith, "I will make thee to rejoice more than those who are mighty through their horns,"<a name="page_211_fr_1"></a><a href="#page_211_note_1"><sup><small>1</small></sup></a> which meaneth, "Thou art nobler than the noble kings, and thou dost rejoice."</p>
 
 <p>   And <small>HABAKKUK</small> prophesied, saying, "Horns are in his hands,<a name="page_211_fr_2"></a><a href="#page_211_note_2"><sup><small>2</small></sup></a> and he hath placed the beloved in the strength of his power," which meaneth, "The palms of the hand, wherein the life of all is held, of the holder of the dominion of kings, are pierced with nails, which <small>CHRIST</small>, the beloved, hath endured in the strength of His might."</p>
