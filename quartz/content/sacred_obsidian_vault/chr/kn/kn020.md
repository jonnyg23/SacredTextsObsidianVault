 </p><h3 align="CENTER">20. <i>Concerning the Division of the Earth</i></h3>
 
 <p>   From the middle of <small>JERUSALEM</small>, and from the north thereof to the south-east is the portion of the Emperor of <small>RÔM</small>; and from the middle of <small>JERUSALEM</small> from the north thereof to the south and to <small>WESTERN INDIA</small> is the portion of the Emperor of <small>ETHIOPIA</small>. For both of them are of the seed of <small>SHEM</small>, the son of <small>NOAH</small>, the seed of <small>ABRAHAM</small>, the seed of <small>DAVID</small>, the children of <small>SOLOMON</small>. For God gave the seed of <small>SHEM</small> glory because of the blessing of their father <small>NOAH</small>. The Emperor of <small>RÔM</small> is the son of <small>SOLOMON</small>, and the Emperor of <small>ETHIOPIA</small> is the firstborn and eldest son of <small>SOLOMON</small>.</p>
