 <p><a name="v.v-p1"></a>    
 </p><p class="HDG-C-Gr-16-Pref08"><a name="v.v-p2"></a>CHAPTER 5</p>    
 <p><a name="v.v-p3"></a><b>1 </b>Stand fast, therefore, in the liberty with which Christ hath made us free; and be not again entangled by the yoke of bondage.</p>    
 <p><a name="v.v-p4"></a><b>2 </b>Behold, I Paul protest to you, that, if ye be circumcised, Christ will profit you nothing.</p>    
 <p><a name="v.v-p5"></a><b>3 </b>For I testify again to every man who is circumcised that he is a debtor to do the whole law.</p>    
 <p><a name="v.v-p6"></a><b>4 </b>Whosoever of you are justified by the law, ye have separated yourselves from Christ; ye are fallen from grace.</p>    
 <p><a name="v.v-p7"></a><b>5 </b>For we, through the Spirit, by faith, wait for the hope of righteousness.</p>    
 <p><a name="v.v-p8"></a><b>6 </b>For in Christ Jesus neither circumcision availeth any thing, nor uncircumcision; but faith working by love.</p>    
 <p><a name="v.v-p9"></a><b>7 </b>Ye were running well. Who hindered you, that ye should not obey the truth?</p>    
 <p><a name="v.v-p10"></a><b>8 </b>This persuasion is not from him who called you.</p>    
 <p><a name="v.v-p11"></a><b>9 </b>A little leaven leaveneth the whole lump.</p>    
 <p><a name="v.v-p12"></a><b>10 </b>I am persuaded concerning you in the Lord, that ye will think nothing else;   	  <a name="fr_193"></a><a href="#fn_193"><font size="1">194</font></a>
  	  but he that troubleth you shall bear the condemnation, whoever he be.</p>    
 <p><a name="v.v-p14"></a><b>11 </b>And I,   	  <a name="fr_194"></a><a href="#fn_194"><font size="1">195</font></a>
  	  brethren, if I still preach circumcision, why do I still suffer persecution? The offense of the cross is abolished.</p>    
 <p><a name="v.v-p16"></a><b>12 </b>Would that they were even cut off who trouble you!</p>    
 <p><a name="v.v-p17"></a><b>13 </b>For ye, brethren, have been called to liberty; only make not the liberty an occasion to the flesh,   	  <a name="fr_195"></a><a href="#fn_195"><font size="1">196</font></a>
  	  but by love serve one another.</p>    
 <p><a name="v.v-p19"></a><b>14 </b>For the whole law is fulfilled in one word, namely, this: Thou shalt love thy neighbor as thyself.</p>    
 <p><a name="v.v-p20"></a><b>15 </b>But if ye bite and devour one another, see that ye be not consumed by one another.</p>    
 <p><a name="v.v-p21"></a><b>16 </b>Now I say: Walk in the Spirit, and ye shall not fulfill the lust of the flesh.</p>    
 <p><a name="v.v-p22"></a><b>17 </b>For the flesh lusteth against the Spirit; and the Spirit against the flesh; and these are contrary to one another, so that ye cannot do those things which ye would wish.</p>    
 <p><a name="v.v-p23"></a><b>18 </b>But if ye are led by the Spirit, ye are not under the law.</p>    
 <p><a name="v.v-p24"></a><b>19 </b>Now the works of the flesh are manifest, which are adultery, fornication, uncleanness, lasciviousness,</p>    
 <p><a name="v.v-p25"></a><b>20 </b>Idolatry, witchcraft, enmities, strife, emulations, wrath, quarrellings, seditions, heresies,</p>    
 <p><a name="v.v-p26"></a><b>21 </b>Envyings, murders, drunkenness, revellings, and such like; of which I foretell to you, as I also have foretold, that they who do such things shall not inherit the kingdom of God.</p>    
 <p><a name="v.v-p27"></a><b>22 </b>But the fruit of the Spirit is love, joy, peace, long-suffering,   	  <a name="fr_196"></a><a href="#fn_196"><font size="1">197</font></a>
  	  gentleness, kindness, fidelity,</p>    
 <p><a name="v.v-p29"></a><b>23 </b>Meekness, temperance; against such there is no law.</p>    
 <p><a name="v.v-p30"></a><b>24 </b>And they who are Christ’s have crucified the flesh with the affections and lusts.</p>    
 <p><a name="v.v-p31"></a><b>25 </b>If we live by the Spirit, let us also walk by the Spirit.</p>    
 <p><a name="v.v-p32"></a><b>26 </b>Let us not be desirous of vain-glory, provoking one another, envying one another.</p>
