 <p class="TableCaption14"><a name="v.i.i-p2"></a><a name="v.i.i-p2.1"></a><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo020.htm#008">Exodus 20:8-11</a><a name="v.i.i-p2.1"></a></p> 				    				 				     				  
 <p><a name="v.i.i-p3"></a><b>8.</b> Remember the sabbath day, to keep it holy.</p> 				     				  
 <p><a name="v.i.i-p4"></a><b>8.</b> Recordare diei Sabbathi, ut sanctifices eum:</p> 				    				 				     				  
 <p><a name="v.i.i-p5"></a><b>9.</b> Six days shalt thou labour, and do all thy work:</p> 				     				  
 <p><a name="v.i.i-p6"></a><b>9.</b> Sex diebus operaberis et facies universum opus tuum.</p> 				    				 				     				  
 <p><a name="v.i.i-p7"></a><b>10.</b> But the seventh day <i>is</i> the sabbath of the LORD thy God: <i>in it </i>thou shalt not do any work, thou, nor thy son, nor thy daughter, thy manservant, nor thy maidservant, nor thy cattle, nor thy stranger that <i>is </i>within thy gates:</p> 				     				  
 <p><a name="v.i.i-p8"></a><b>10.</b> Dies autem septimus Sabbathum Jehovae Dei tui est. Non facies ullum opus, tu, et filius tuus, et filia tua, servus tuus, et ancilla tua, et inquilinus tuus qui est in portis tuis:</p> 				    				 				     				  
 <p><a name="v.i.i-p9"></a><b>11.</b> For <i>in</i> six days the LORD made heaven and earth, the sea, and all that in them <i>is, </i>and rested the seventh day: wherefore the LORD blessed the sabbath day, and hallowed it.</p> 				     				  
 <p><a name="v.i.i-p10"></a><b>11.</b> Quoniam sex diebus fecit Jehova coelum et terram, mare et quaecunque in illis sunt, et quievit die septimo: propterea benedixit Jehova diem sabbathi, et sanctificavit eum.</p>
