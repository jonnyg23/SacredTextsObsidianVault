 </p><p class="c33"><a name="I_1-p1"></a><span class="c6"><a name="I_1-p1.1"></a>Book I.</span></p>  
 <p class="c8"><a name="I_1-p2"></a>————————————</p>  
 <p class="c40"><a name="I_1-p3"></a>Commencing with the invocation of God, Augustin relates in detail the beginning of his life, his infancy and boyhood, up to his fifteenth year; at which age he acknowledges that he was more inclined to all youthful pleasures and vices than to the study of letters.</p>
