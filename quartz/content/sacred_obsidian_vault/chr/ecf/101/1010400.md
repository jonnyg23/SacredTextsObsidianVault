 </p><p class="c41"><a name="vii.1.XCIV-p1"></a><span class="c2"><a name="vii.1.XCIV-p1.1"></a>Letter XCIV.</span></p>  
 <p class="c1"><a name="vii.1.XCIV-p2"></a>(<span class="c9"><a name="vii.1.XCIV-p2.1"></a>a.d.</span> 408.)</p>  
 <p><a name="vii.1.XCIV-p3"></a>A letter to Augustin from Paulinus and Therasia, the substance of which is sufficiently stated in the next letter, which contains the reply of Augustin to his friends questions concerning the present life, the nature of the bodies of the blessed in the life to come, and the functions of the members of the body after the resurrection.</p>
