 <p> <a name="viii.iii.iv"></a>  </p><h4 align="center"><a name="viii.iii.iv-p0.1"></a>Chapter IV. <a name="fr_2408"></a><a href="#fn_2408"><font size="1">2409</font></a>
 —Various Precepts.</h4>  
 <p class="BodyText"><a name="viii.iii.iv-p2"></a>1. My child, him that speaketh to thee the word of God remember night and day; and thou shalt honour him as the Lord; <a name="fr_2409"></a><a href="#fn_2409"><font size="1">2410</font></a>
  for <span><a name="viii.iii.iv-p3.3"></a>in the place</span> whence lordly rule is uttered, <a name="fr_2410"></a><a href="#fn_2410"><font size="1">2411</font></a>
  there is the Lord. 2. And thou shalt seek out day by day the faces of the saints, in order that thou mayest rest upon <a name="fr_2411"></a><a href="#fn_2411"><font size="1">2412</font></a>
  their words. 3. Thou shalt not long for <a name="fr_2412"></a><a href="#fn_2412"><font size="1">2413</font></a>
  division, but shalt bring those who contend to peace. Thou shalt judge righteously, thou shalt not respect persons in reproving for transgressions. 4. Thou shalt not be undecided whether it shall be or no. <a name="fr_2413"></a><a href="#fn_2413"><font size="1">2414</font></a>
  5. Be not a stretcher forth of the hands to receive and a drawer of them back to give. <a name="fr_2414"></a><a href="#fn_2414"><font size="1">2415</font></a>
  6. If thou hast <span><a name="viii.iii.iv-p8.5"></a>aught</span>, through thy hands thou shalt give ransom for thy sins. <a name="fr_2415"></a><a href="#fn_2415"><font size="1">2416</font></a>
  7. Thou shalt not hesitate to give, nor murmur when thou givest; for thou shalt know who is the good repayer of the hire. 8. Thou shalt not turn away from him that is in want, but thou shalt share all things with thy brother, and shalt not say that they are thine own; for if ye are partakers in that which is immortal, how much more in things which are mortal? <a name="fr_2416"></a><a href="#fn_2416"><font size="1">2417</font></a>
  9. Thou shalt not remove thy hand from thy son or from thy daughter, but from <span><a name="viii.iii.iv-p10.5"></a>their</span> youth shalt teach <span><a name="viii.iii.iv-p10.6"></a>them</span> the fear of God. <a name="fr_2417"></a><a href="#fn_2417"><font size="1">2418</font></a>
  10. Thou shalt not enjoin aught in thy bitterness upon thy bondman or maidservant, who hope in the same God, lest ever they shall fear not God who is over both; <a name="fr_2418"></a><a href="#fn_2418"><font size="1">2419</font></a>
  for he cometh not to call according to the outward appearance, but unto them whom the Spirit hath prepared. 11. And ye bondmen shall be subject to your <a name="fr_2419"></a><a href="#fn_2419"><font size="1">2420</font></a>
  masters as to a type of God, in modesty and fear. <a name="fr_2420"></a><a href="#fn_2420"><font size="1">2421</font></a>
  12. Thou shalt hate all hypocrisy and everything which is not pleasing to the Lord. 13. Do thou in no wise forsake the commandments of the Lord; but thou shalt keep what thou hast received, neither adding <span><a name="viii.iii.iv-p14.3"></a>thereto</span> nor taking away <span><a name="viii.iii.iv-p14.4"></a>therefrom</span>. <a name="fr_2421"></a><a href="#fn_2421"><font size="1">2422</font></a>
  14. In the church <a name="fr_2422"></a><a href="#fn_2422"><font size="1">2423</font></a>
  thou shalt acknowledge thy transgressions, and thou shalt not come near for thy prayer <a name="fr_2423"></a><a href="#fn_2423"><font size="1">2424</font></a>
  with an evil conscience. <a name="fr_2424"></a><a href="#fn_2424"><font size="1">2425</font></a>
  This is the way of life. <a name="fr_2425"></a><a href="#fn_2425"><font size="1">2426</font></a>
    </p>
