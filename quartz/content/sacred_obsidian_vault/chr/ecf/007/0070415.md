 <p> <a name="viii.iii.vii"></a>  </p><h4 align="center"><a name="viii.iii.vii-p0.1"></a>Chapter VII.—Concerning Baptism.</h4>  
 <p class="BodyText"><a name="viii.iii.vii-p1"></a>1. And concerning baptism, <a name="fr_2440"></a><a href="#fn_2440"><font size="1">2441</font></a>
  thus baptize ye: <a name="fr_2441"></a><a href="#fn_2441"><font size="1">2442</font></a>
  Having first said all these things, baptize into the name of the Father, and of the Son, and of the Holy Spirit, <a name="fr_2442"></a><a href="#fn_2442"><font size="1">2443</font></a>
  in living water. <a name="fr_2443"></a><a href="#fn_2443"><font size="1">2444</font></a>
  2. But if thou have not living water, baptize into other water; and if thou canst not in cold, in warm. 3. But if thou have not either, pour out water thrice <a name="fr_2444"></a><a href="#fn_2444"><font size="1">2445</font></a>
  upon the head into the name of Father and Son and Holy Spirit. 4. But before the baptism let the baptizer fast, and the baptized, and whatever others can; but thou shalt order the baptized to fast one or two days before. <a name="fr_2445"></a><a href="#fn_2445"><font size="1">2446</font></a>
    </p>
