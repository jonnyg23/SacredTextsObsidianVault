    <h2 align="CENTER"><a></a> IV.-A Point in the Same Disputation.<sup><a href="footnote/fn31.htm">1</a></sup> </h2>  
 <p>------------</p>  
 <p>Did I not say before that you do not admit that the only-begotten Son, who is from all eternity before every creature, was made substantially existent<sup><a href="footnote/fn31.htm#P3188_814458">2</a></sup> in the whole person of the Saviour;<sup><a href="footnote/fn31.htm#P3189_814539">3</a></sup> that is to say, was united <i>with Him</i> according to substance?</p>
