 <p class="c35"><a name="xiv.iii.lviii-p5"></a>No one should offer honey or milk at the altar.</p> 
 <p class="c18"><a name="xiv.iii.lviii-p6"></a>See canon iij. of the Apostles, canon xxviij. of the African code, also canon xxviij. of this synod.  The Greeks apparently do not recognize the exception specified in the canon of the African Code.</p>
