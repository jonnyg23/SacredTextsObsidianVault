 </p><p class="c46"><a name="xv.iv.iv.xli-p1"></a> <span class="c4"><a name="xv.iv.iv.xli-p1.1"></a>Canon XL.  (Greek xliii.)</span></p> 
 <p class="c35"><a name="xv.iv.iv.xli-p2"></a>Concerning the non-frequenting of taverns by the clergy, except when travelling.</p> 
 <p class="c18"><a name="xv.iv.iv.xli-p3"></a><span class="sc"><a name="xv.iv.iv.xli-p3.1"></a>That</span> the clergy are not to enter taverns for eating or drinking, nor unless compelled to do so by the necessity of their journey.</p> 
 <p class="c31"><a name="xv.iv.iv.xli-p4"></a><span class="c4"><a name="xv.iv.iv.xli-p4.1"></a>Notes.</span></p> 
 <p class="c36"><a name="xv.iv.iv.xli-p5"></a><span class="sc"><a name="xv.iv.iv.xli-p5.1"></a>Ancient Epitome of Canon XL.</span></p> 
 <p class="c35"><a name="xv.iv.iv.xli-p6"></a>A cleric on a journey may enter a tavern, otherwise not.</p> 
 <p class="c18"><a name="xv.iv.iv.xli-p7"></a>This canon is Canon xxvj. of the Synod of Hippo, <span class="sc"><a name="xv.iv.iv.xli-p7.1"></a>a.d.</span> 393.</p>
