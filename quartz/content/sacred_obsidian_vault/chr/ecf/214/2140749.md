 </p><p class="c14"><a name="xvii.xxix-p1"></a> <a name="page_615"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 615</font></a> <a name="xvii.xxix-Page_615"></a><span class="c4"><a name="xvii.xxix-p1.1"></a>The Narrative of the Same concerning Those Called Cathari.</span></p> 
 <p class="c18"><a name="xvii.xxix-p2"></a>Because the great synod held at Nice has decreed, That [the clergymen] who come over to the Church from the Novatians be ordained; do you ordain those that come over, if their life be upright, and there be no objection.</p>
