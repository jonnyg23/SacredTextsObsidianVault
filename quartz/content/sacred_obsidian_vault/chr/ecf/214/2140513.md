 <p> <a name="xv.iii.iv.xix"></a>
 </p><p class="c34"><a name="xv.iii.iv.xix-p1"></a> <span class="c4"><a name="xv.iii.iv.xix-p1.1"></a>Canon XVIII.</span></p> 
 <p class="c31"><a name="xv.iii.iv.xix-p2"></a>(<i>Greek</i>.)</p> 
 <p class="c18"><a name="xv.iii.iv.xix-p3"></a><span class="sc"><a name="xv.iii.iv.xix-p3.1"></a>Bishop</span> <span class="sc"><a name="xv.iii.iv.xix-p3.2"></a>Gaudentius</span> said:  Thou knowest, brother Aëtius, that since thou wast made bishop, peace hath continued to rule [in thy diocese].  In order that no remnants of discord concerning ecclesiastics remain, it seems good that those who were ordained by Musæus and by Eutychianus, provided no fault be found in them, should all be received.</p> 
 <p class="c31"><a name="xv.iii.iv.xix-p4"></a>(This canon is wanting in the <i>Latin</i>.)</p>
