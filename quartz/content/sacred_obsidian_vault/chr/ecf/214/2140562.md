 </p><p class="c34"><a name="xv.iv.iv.xxxix-p1"></a> <span class="c4"><a name="xv.iv.iv.xxxix-p1.1"></a>Canon XXXVIII.  (Greek xli.)</span></p> 
 <p class="c50"><a name="xv.iv.iv.xxxix-p2"></a>That clerics or those who are continent shall not visit virgins or widows.</p> 
 <p class="c18"><a name="xv.iv.iv.xxxix-p3"></a><span class="sc"><a name="xv.iv.iv.xxxix-p3.1"></a>Neither</span> clerics nor those who profess continence should enter the houses of widows or virgins without the bidding or consent of the bishops or presbyters:  and then let them not go alone, but with some other of the clergy, or with those assigned by the bishop or presbyter for this purpose; not even bishops and presbyters shall go alone to women of this sort, except some of the clergy are present or some other grave Christian men.</p> 
 <p class="c31"><a name="xv.iv.iv.xxxix-p4"></a><span class="c4"><a name="xv.iv.iv.xxxix-p4.1"></a>Notes.</span></p> 
 <p class="c36"><a name="xv.iv.iv.xxxix-p5"></a><span class="sc"><a name="xv.iv.iv.xxxix-p5.1"></a>Ancient Epitome of Canon XXXVIII.</span></p> 
 <p class="c35"><a name="xv.iv.iv.xxxix-p6"></a>Clerics and those who are continent shall not go to widows or virgins, unless at the bidding of the bishop and presbyter:  and even then not alone, but with those with whom presbyters and deacons visit them.</p> 
 <p class="c18"><a name="xv.iv.iv.xxxix-p7"></a>This canon is canon xxiv. of the Synod of Hippo, <span class="sc"><a name="xv.iv.iv.xxxix-p7.1"></a>a.d.</span> 393.</p>
