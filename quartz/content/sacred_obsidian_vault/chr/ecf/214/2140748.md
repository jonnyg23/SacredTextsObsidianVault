 <p> <a name="xvii.xxviii"></a>
 </p><p class="c14"><a name="xvii.xxviii-p1"></a> <span class="c4"><a name="xvii.xxviii-p1.1"></a>Of the Same to Menas the Bishop.</span></p> 
 <p class="c18"><a name="xvii.xxviii-p2"></a>Theophilus was informed, that the priest in Geminus, a village, had repelled Kyradium (a woman) from the communion:  Theophilus approves of it, because she had done wrong, and was unwilling to make satisfaction; but orders her to be admitted to communion upon repentance.</p>
