 <p> <a name="xiii.ix"></a>
 </p><p class="c14"><a name="xiii.ix-p1"></a> <span class="c4"><a name="xiii.ix-p1.1"></a>Session XVI.</span></p> 
 <p class="c31"><a name="xiii.ix-p2"></a>(Labbe and Cossart, <i>Concilia</i>, Tom. VI., col. 1010.)</p> 
 <p class="c18"><a name="xiii.ix-p3"></a>[<i>The Acclamations of the Fathers</i>.]</p> 
 <p class="c18"><a name="xiii.ix-p4"></a>Many years to the Emperor!  Many years to Constantine, our great Emperor!  Many years to the Orthodox King!  Many years to our Emperor that maketh peace!  Many years to Constantine, a second Martian!  Many years to Constantine, a new Theodosius!  Many years to Constantine, a new Justinian!  Many years to the keeper of the orthodox faith!  O Lord preserve the foundation of the Churches!  O Lord preserve the keeper of the faith!</p> 
 <p class="c19"><a name="xiii.ix-p5"></a>Many years to Agatho, Pope of Rome!  Many years to George, Patriarch of Constantinople!  Many years to Theophanus, Patriarch of Antioch!  Many years to the orthodox council!  Many years to the orthodox Senate!</p> 
 <p class="c19"><a name="xiii.ix-p6"></a>To Theodore of Pharan, the heretic, anathema!  To Sergius, the heretic, anathema!  To Cyrus, the heretic, anathema!  To Honorius, the heretic, anathema!  To Pyrrhus, the heretic, anathema!</p> 
 <p class="c18"><a name="xiii.ix-p7"></a>To Paul the heretic, anathema!</p> 
 <p class="c19"><a name="xiii.ix-p8"></a>To Peter the heretic, anathema!</p> 
 <p class="c19"><a name="xiii.ix-p9"></a>To Macarius the heretic, anathema!</p> 
 <p class="c19"><a name="xiii.ix-p10"></a>To Stephen the heretic, anathema!</p> 
 <p class="c19"><a name="xiii.ix-p11"></a>To Polychronius the heretic, anathema!</p> 
 <p class="c19"><a name="xiii.ix-p12"></a>To Apergius of Perga the heretic, anathema!</p> 
 <p class="c18"><a name="xiii.ix-p13"></a>To all heretics, anathema!  To all who side with heretics, anathema!</p> 
 <p class="c19"><a name="xiii.ix-p14"></a>May the faith of the Christians increase, and long years to the orthodox and Ecumenical Council!</p>
