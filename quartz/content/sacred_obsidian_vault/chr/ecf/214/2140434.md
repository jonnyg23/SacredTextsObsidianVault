 <p> <a name="xiv.iii.li"></a>
 </p><p class="c34"><a name="xiv.iii.li-p1"></a> <span class="c4"><a name="xiv.iii.li-p1.1"></a>Canon L.</span></p> 
 <p class="c18"><a name="xiv.iii.li-p2"></a><span class="sc"><a name="xiv.iii.li-p2.1"></a>No</span> one at all, whether cleric or layman, is from this time forward to play at dice.  And if any one hereafter shall be found doing so, if he be a cleric he is to be deposed, if a layman let him be cut off.</p> 
 <p class="c31"><a name="xiv.iii.li-p3"></a><span class="c4"><a name="xiv.iii.li-p3.1"></a>Notes.</span></p> 
 <p class="c36"><a name="xiv.iii.li-p4"></a><span class="sc"><a name="xiv.iii.li-p4.1"></a>Ancient Epitome of Canon L.</span></p> 
 <p class="c35"><a name="xiv.iii.li-p5"></a>A layman should not play at dice.</p> 
 <p class="c18"><a name="xiv.iii.li-p6"></a>This renews canons xlii. and xliij. of the Apostolic canons.</p>
