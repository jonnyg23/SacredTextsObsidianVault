 <p> <a name="xiv.iii.lxxix"></a>
 </p><p class="c34"><a name="xiv.iii.lxxix-p1"></a> <span class="c4"><a name="xiv.iii.lxxix-p1.1"></a>Canon LXXVIII.</span></p> 
 <p class="c18"><a name="xiv.iii.lxxix-p2"></a><span class="sc"><a name="xiv.iii.lxxix-p2.1"></a>It</span> behoves those who are illuminated to learn the Creed by heart and to recite it to the bishop or presbyters on the Fifth Feria of the Week.</p> 
 <p class="c31"><a name="xiv.iii.lxxix-p3"></a><span class="c4"><a name="xiv.iii.lxxix-p3.1"></a>Notes.</span></p> 
 <p class="c36"><a name="xiv.iii.lxxix-p4"></a><span class="sc"><a name="xiv.iii.lxxix-p4.1"></a>Ancient Epitome of Canon LXXVIII.</span></p> 
 <p class="c18"><a name="xiv.iii.lxxix-p5"></a><i>He that is illuminated is to recite</i> (<span class="Greek"><a name="xiv.iii.lxxix-p5.1"></a>ἀπαγγελλέτω</span> ) <i>the faith on the fifth feria of the week.</i></p> 
 <p class="c18"><a name="xiv.iii.lxxix-p6"></a>This is a renewal of canon xlvi. of Laodicea.</p>
