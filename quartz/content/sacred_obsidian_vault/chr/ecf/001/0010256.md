 <p> <a name="v.xiv.iv"></a>  </p><h3 align="center"><a name="v.xiv.iv-p0.1"></a>Chapter IV.—Continuation.</h3>  
 <p><a name="v.xiv.iv-p1"></a>And [know ye, moreover], that He who was born of a woman was the Son of God, and He that was crucified was “the first-born of every creature,” <a name="fr_1189"></a><a href="#fn_1189"><font size="1">1190</font></a>
  and God the Word, who also created all things. For says the apostle, “There is one God, the Father, of whom are all things; and  <a name="page_108"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 108</font></a> <a name="v.xiv.iv-Page_108"></a>  one Lord Jesus Christ, by whom are all things.” <a name="fr_1190"></a><a href="#fn_1190"><font size="1">1191</font></a>
  And again, “For there is one God, and one Mediator between God and man, the man Christ Jesus;” <a name="fr_1191"></a><a href="#fn_1191"><font size="1">1192</font></a>
  and, “By Him were all things created that are in heaven, and on earth, visible and invisible; and He is before all things, and by Him all things consist.” <a name="fr_1192"></a><a href="#fn_1192"><font size="1">1193</font></a>
 </p>
