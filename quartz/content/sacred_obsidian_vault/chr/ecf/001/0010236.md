 <p> <a name="v.xi.iv"></a>  </p><h3 align="center"><a name="v.xi.iv-p0.1"></a>Chapter IX.</h3>  
 <p><a name="v.xi.iv-p1"></a><a name="v.xi.iv-p1.1"></a>And ye are prepared for the building of God the Father, and ye are raised up on high by the instrument of Jesus Christ, which is the cross; and ye are drawn by the rope, which is the Holy Spirit; and your pulley is your faith, and your love is the way which leadeth up on high to God.</p>
