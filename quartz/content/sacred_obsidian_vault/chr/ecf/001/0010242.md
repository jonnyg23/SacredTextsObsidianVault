 <p> <a name="v.xii"></a>  <a name="page_103"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 103</font></a> <a name="v.xii-Page_103"></a>  </p><h2 align="center"><a name="v.xii-p0.1"></a>The Third Epistle of the Same St. Ignatius <a name="fr_1159"></a><a href="#fn_1159"><font size="1">1160</font></a>
  </h2>    
 <p><a name="v.xii-p2"></a><a name="v.xii-p2.1"></a><i>Ignatius, who is [also called] Theophorus, to the Church which has received grace through the greatness of the Father Most High; to her who presideth in the place of the region of the Romans, who is worthy of God, and worthy of life, and happiness, and praise, and remembrance, and is worthy of prosperity, and presideth in love, and is perfected in the law of Christ unblameable: [wishes] abundance of peace.</i></p>
