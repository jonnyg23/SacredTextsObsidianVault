 <p> <a name="viii.ix.v"></a>  </p><h3 align="center"><a name="viii.ix.v-p0.1"></a>V.</h3>  
 <p><a name="viii.ix.v-p1"></a>And Justin of Neapolis, a man who was not far separated from the apostles either in age or excellence, says that that which is mortal is inherited, but that which is immortal inherits; and that the flesh indeed dies, but the kingdom of heaven lives.—<i>From</i> <span class="upper"><a name="viii.ix.v-p1.1"></a>Methodius</span> <i>On the Resurrection, in Photius</i>.</p>
