 <p> <a name="v.iii.xiii"></a>  </p><h3 align="center"><a name="v.iii.xiii-p0.1"></a>Chapter XIII.—Be established in faith and unity.</h3>  
 <p><a name="v.iii.xiii-p1"></a><a name="v.iii.xiii-p1.1"></a><a name="v.iii.xiii-p1.2"></a>Study, therefore, to be established in the doctrines of the Lord and the apostles, that so all things, whatsoever ye do, may prosper both in the flesh and spirit; in faith and love; in the Son, and in the Father, and in the Spirit; in the beginning and in the end; with your most admirable bishop, and the well-compacted spiritual crown of your presbytery, and the deacons who are according to God. Be ye subject to the bishop, and to one another, as Jesus Christ to the Father,  <a name="page_65"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 65</font></a> <a name="v.iii.xiii-Page_65"></a>  according to the flesh, and the apostles to Christ, and to the Father, and to the Spirit; that so there may be a union both fleshly and spiritual.</p>  
 <p><a name="v.iii.xiii-p2"></a><a name="v.iii.xiii-p2.1"></a><a name="v.iii.xiii-p2.2"></a><a name="v.iii.xiii-p2.3"></a>Study, therefore, to be established in the doctrines of the Lord and the apostles, that so all things, whatsoever ye do, may prosper, both in the flesh and spirit, in faith and love, with your most admirable bishop, and the well-compacted <a name="fr_724"></a><a href="#fn_724"><font size="1">725</font></a>
  spiritual crown of your presbytery, and the deacons who are according to God. Be ye subject to the bishop, and to one another, as Christ to the Father, that there may be a unity according to God among you.</p>
