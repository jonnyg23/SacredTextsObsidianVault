 <p> <a name="iv.ii.xiv"></a>  </p><h3 align="center"><a name="iv.ii.xiv-p0.1"></a>Chapter XIV.—Conclusion.</h3>  
 <p><a name="iv.ii.xiv-p1"></a>These things I have written to you by Crescens, whom up to the present <a name="fr_416"></a><a href="#fn_416"><font size="1">417</font></a>
  time I have recommended unto you, and do now recommend. For he has acted blamelessly among us, and I believe also among you. Moreover, ye will hold his sister in esteem when she comes to you. Be ye safe in the Lord Jesus Christ. Grace be with you all. <a name="fr_417"></a><a href="#fn_417"><font size="1">418</font></a>
  Amen.</p>
