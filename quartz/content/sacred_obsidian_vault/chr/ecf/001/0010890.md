 <p> <a name="ix.viii.xv"></a>  </p><h3 align="center"><a name="ix.viii.xv-p0.1"></a>XV.</h3>  
 <p><a name="ix.viii.xv-p1"></a><a name="ix.viii.xv-p1.1"></a>When, <a name="fr_4820"></a><a href="#fn_4820"><font size="1">4821</font></a>
  in times of old, Balaam spake these things in parables, he was not acknowledged; and now, when Christ has appeared and fulfilled them, He was not believed. Wherefore [Balaam], foreseeing this, and wondering at it, exclaimed, “Alas! alas! who shall live when God brings these things to pass?” <a name="fr_4821"></a><a href="#fn_4821"><font size="1">4822</font></a>
