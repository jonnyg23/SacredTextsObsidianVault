 <p><a name="iv.ii-p2"></a><a name="iv.ii-p2.1"></a><a name="iv.ii-p2.2"></a><a name="iv.ii-p2.3"></a><span class="upper"><a name="iv.ii-p2.4"></a>Polycarp</span>, and the presbyters <a name="fr_337"></a><a href="#fn_337"><font size="1">338</font></a>
