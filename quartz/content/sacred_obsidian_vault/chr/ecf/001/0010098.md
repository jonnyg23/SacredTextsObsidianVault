 <p> <a name="iv.iv"></a>  <a name="page_39"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 39</font></a> <a name="iv.iv-Page_39"></a>  </p><h2 align="center"><a name="iv.iv-p0.1"></a>The Encyclical Epistle of the Church at Smyrna Concerning the Martyrdom of the Holy Polycarp</h2>    
 <p><a name="iv.iv-p1"></a><a name="iv.iv-p1.1"></a><a name="iv.iv-p1.2"></a><span class="upper"><a name="iv.iv-p1.3"></a>The</span> Church of God which sojourns at Smyrna, to the Church of God sojourning in Philomelium, <a name="fr_419"></a><a href="#fn_419"><font size="1">420</font></a>
  and to all the congregations <a name="fr_420"></a><a href="#fn_420"><font size="1">421</font></a>
  of the Holy and Catholic Church in every place: Mercy, peace, and love from God the Father, and our Lord Jesus Christ, be multiplied.</p>
