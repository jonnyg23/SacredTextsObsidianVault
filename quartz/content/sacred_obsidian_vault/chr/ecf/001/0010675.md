 <p> <a name="viii.ix.viii"></a>  </p><h3 align="center"><a name="viii.ix.viii-p0.1"></a>VIII.</h3>  
 <p><a name="viii.ix.viii-p1"></a>The unskilfulness of the teacher proves destructive to his disciples, and the carelessness of the disciples entails danger on the teacher, and especially should they owe their negligence to his want of knowledge.</p>
