 <p> <a name="v.xiv.viii"></a>  </p><h3 align="center"><a name="v.xiv.viii-p0.1"></a>Chapter VIII.—Exhortations to holiness and good order.</h3>  
 <p><a name="v.xiv.viii-p1"></a>May I have joy of you in the Lord! Be ye sober. <a name="v.xiv.viii-p1.1"></a>Lay aside, every one of you, all malice and beast-like fury, evil-speaking, calumny, filthy speaking, ribaldry, whispering, arrogance, drunkenness, lust, avarice, vainglory, envy, and everything akin to these. “But put ye on the Lord Jesus Christ, and make no provision for the flesh, to fulfil the lusts thereof.” <a name="fr_1210"></a><a href="#fn_1210"><font size="1">1211</font></a>
  Ye presbyters, be subject to the bishop; ye deacons, to the presbyters; and ye, the people, to the presbyters and the deacons. Let my soul be for theirs who preserve this good order; and may the Lord be with them continually!</p>
