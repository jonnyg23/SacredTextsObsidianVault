 <p> <a name="viii.ii.xxxiv"></a>  </p><h3 align="center"><a name="viii.ii.xxxiv-p0.1"></a>Chapter XXXIV.—Place of Christs birth foretold.</h3>  
 <p><a name="viii.ii.xxxiv-p1"></a><a name="viii.ii.xxxiv-p1.1"></a><a name="viii.ii.xxxiv-p1.2"></a>And hear what part of earth He was to be born in, as another prophet, Micah, foretold. He spoke thus: “And thou, Bethlehem, the land of Judah, art not the least among the princes of Judah; for out of thee shall come forth a Governor, who shall feed My people.” <a name="fr_1832"></a><a href="#fn_1832"><font size="1">1833</font></a>
  Now there is a village in the land of the Jews, thirty-five stadia from Jerusalem, in which Jesus Christ was born, as you can ascertain also from the registers of the taxing made under Cyrenius, your first procurator in Judæa.</p>
