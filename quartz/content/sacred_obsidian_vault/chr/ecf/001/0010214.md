 <p> <a name="v.viii.i"></a>  </p><h3 align="center"><a name="v.viii.i-p0.1"></a>Chapter I.—Commendation and exhortation.</h3>  
 <p><a name="v.viii.i-p1"></a><span class="upper"><a name="v.viii.i-p1.1"></a>Having</span> obtained good proof that thy mind is fixed in God as upon an immoveable rock, I loudly glorify [His name] that I have been thought worthy [to behold] thy blameless face, <a name="fr_1062"></a><a href="#fn_1062"><font size="1">1063</font></a>
  which may I ever enjoy in God! I entreat thee, by the grace with which thou art clothed, to press forward in thy course, and to exhort all that they may be saved. Maintain thy position with all care, both in the flesh and spirit. Have a regard to preserve unity, than which nothing is better. Bear with all, even as the Lord does with thee. Support <a name="fr_1063"></a><a href="#fn_1063"><font size="1">1064</font></a>
  all in love, as also thou doest. Give thyself to prayer without ceasing. <a name="fr_1064"></a><a href="#fn_1064"><font size="1">1065</font></a>
  Implore additional understanding to what thou already hast. Be watchful, possessing a sleepless spirit. Speak to every man separately, as God enables thee. <a name="fr_1065"></a><a href="#fn_1065"><font size="1">1066</font></a>
  Bear the infirmities of all, as being a perfect athlete [in the Christian life]: where the labour is great, the gain is all the more.</p>  
 <p><a name="v.viii.i-p6"></a><span class="upper"><a name="v.viii.i-p6.1"></a>Having</span> obtained good proof that thy mind is fixed in God as upon an immoveable rock, I loudly glorify [His name] that I have been thought worthy to behold thy blameless face, <a name="fr_1066"></a><a href="#fn_1066"><font size="1">1067</font></a>
  which may I ever enjoy in God! I entreat thee, by the grace with which thou art clothed, to press forward in thy course, and to exhort all that they may be saved. Maintain thy position with all care, both in the flesh and spirit. Have a regard to preserve unity, than which nothing is better. Bear with all even as the Lord does with thee. Support <a name="fr_1067"></a><a href="#fn_1067"><font size="1">1068</font></a>
  all in love, as also thou doest. Give thyself to prayer without ceasing. <a name="fr_1068"></a><a href="#fn_1068"><font size="1">1069</font></a>
  Implore additional understanding to what thou already hast. Be watchful, possessing a sleepless spirit. Speak to every man separately, as God enables thee. <a name="fr_1069"></a><a href="#fn_1069"><font size="1">1070</font></a>
  Bear the infirmities of all, as being a perfect athlete [in the Christian life], even as does the Lord of all. For says [the Scripture], “He Himself took our infirmities, and bare our sicknesses.” <a name="fr_1070"></a><a href="#fn_1070"><font size="1">1071</font></a>
  Where the labour is great, the gain is all the more.</p>
