 <p> <a name="ix.viii.xxiii"></a>  </p><h3 align="center"><a name="ix.viii.xxiii-p0.1"></a>XXIII.</h3>  
 <p><a name="ix.viii.xxiii-p1"></a><a name="ix.viii.xxiii-p1.1"></a>“And he mounted upon his ass.” <a name="fr_4833"></a><a href="#fn_4833"><font size="1">4834</font></a>
  The ass was the type of the body of Christ, upon whom all men, resting from their labours, are borne as in a chariot. For the Saviour has taken up the burden of our sins. <a name="fr_4834"></a><a href="#fn_4834"><font size="1">4835</font></a>
  Now the angel who appeared to Balaam was the Word Himself; and in His hand He held a sword, to indicate the power which He had from above.</p>
