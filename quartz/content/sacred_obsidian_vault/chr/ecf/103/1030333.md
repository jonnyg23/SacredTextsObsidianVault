 </p><p class="c39"><a name="iv.ii.lxxxv-p1"></a>  <span class="c2"><a name="iv.ii.lxxxv-p1.1"></a>Chapter 83.—The Man Who Despises the Mercy of God is Guilty of the Sin Against the Holy Ghost.</span></p>  
 <p class="c10"><a name="iv.ii.lxxxv-p2"></a>Now the man who, not believing that sins are remitted in the Church, despises this great gift of Gods mercy, and persists to the last day of his life in his obstinacy of heart, is guilty of the unpardonable sin against the Holy Ghost, in whom Christ forgives sins. <a name="fr_1265"></a><a href="#fn_1265"><font size="1">1266</font></a>
  But this difficult question I have discussed as clearly as I could in a book devoted exclusively to this one point.</p>
