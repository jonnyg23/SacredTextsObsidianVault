 </p><p class="c42"><a name="iv.iv.ii.iii-p1"></a> <span class="c1"><a name="iv.iv.ii.iii-p1.1"></a>Ep. LXXIII.</span></p>  
 <p class="c23"><a name="iv.iv.ii.iii-p2"></a>As to the subject of your letter, these are my sentiments.  I am not angry at being overlooked, but I am glad when I am honoured.  The one is my own desert, the other is a proof of your respect.  Pray for me.  Excuse this short letter, for anyhow, though it is short, it is longer than silence.</p>
