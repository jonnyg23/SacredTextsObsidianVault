 </p><p class="c42"><a name="iv.iv.viii.vi-p1"></a> <span class="c1"><a name="iv.iv.viii.vi-p1.1"></a>Ep. LV.</span></p>  
 <p class="c23"><a name="iv.iv.viii.vi-p2"></a>An Invitation.  You flee when I pursue you:  perhaps in accordance with the laws of love, to make yourself more valuable.  Come then, and fill up at last the loss I have suffered by your long delay.  And if any home affairs detain you, you shall leave us again, and so make yourself more precious as an object of desire.</p>
