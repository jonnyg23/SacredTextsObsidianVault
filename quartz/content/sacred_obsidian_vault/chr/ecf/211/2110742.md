 <p> <a name="iv.iv.viii.xxxiii"></a>  </p><h4 align="center"><a name="iv.iv.viii.xxxiii-p0.1"></a>Chapter XXXIII.</h4>  
 <p class="subh"><a name="iv.iv.viii.xxxiii-p1"></a>A question as to the origin of such differences in powers of evil in the sky.</p>  
 <p class="skip"><a name="iv.iv.viii.xxxiii-p2"></a><span class="sc"><a name="iv.iv.viii.xxxiii-p2.1"></a>Germanus</span>: We certainly do not doubt that those orders which the Apostle enumerates refer to them: “For we wrestle not against flesh and blood, but against principalities,  <a name="page_375"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 375</font></a> <a name="iv.iv.viii.xxxiii-Page_375"></a>against powers, against the world-rulers of this darkness, against spirits of wickedness in heavenly places:” <a name="fr_1514"></a><a href="#fn_1514"><font size="1">1515</font></a>
  but we want to know whence comes such a difference between them, or how such grades of wickedness exist? Were they created for this, to meet with these orders of evil, and in some way to serve this wickedness?</p>
