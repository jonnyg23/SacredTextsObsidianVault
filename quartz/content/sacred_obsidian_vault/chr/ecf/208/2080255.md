 </p><p class="c26"><a name="ix.clxxxiii-p1"></a>  <span class="c18"><a name="ix.clxxxiii-p1.1"></a>Letter CLXXXII. <a name="fr_2595"></a><a href="#fn_2595"><font size="1">2596</font></a>
 </span></p>  
 <p class="c55"><a name="ix.clxxxiii-p3"></a><i>To the presbyters of Samosata</i>.</p>  
 <p class="c20"><a name="ix.clxxxiii-p4"></a><span class="c14"><a name="ix.clxxxiii-p4.1"></a>Grieved</span> as I am at the desolation of the Church, <a name="fr_2596"></a><a href="#fn_2596"><font size="1">2597</font></a>
  I none the less congratulate you on having been brought so soon to this extreme limit of your hard struggle.  God grant that you may pass through it with patience, to the end that in return for your faithful stewardship, and the noble constancy which you have shewn in Christs cause, you may receive the great reward.</p>
