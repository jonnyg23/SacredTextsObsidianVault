 </p><p class="c26"><a name="ix.cccli-p1"></a>  <span class="c18"><a name="ix.cccli-p1.1"></a>Letter CCCLVI.</span></p>  
 <p class="c54"><a name="ix.cccli-p2"></a>Basil to Libanius.</p>  
 <p class="c20"><a name="ix.cccli-p3"></a><span class="c14"><a name="ix.cccli-p3.1"></a>I am</span> delighted at receiving what you write, but when you ask me to reply, I am in a difficulty.  What could I say in answer to so Attic a tongue, except that I confess, and confess with joy, that I am a pupil of fishermen?</p>
