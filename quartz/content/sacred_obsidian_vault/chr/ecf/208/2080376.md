 </p><p class="c26"><a name="ix.ccciv-p1"></a>  <span class="c18"><a name="ix.ccciv-p1.1"></a>Letter CCCIII.</span></p>  
 <p class="c54"><a name="ix.ccciv-p2"></a>To the Comes Privatarum.</p>  
 <p class="c20"><a name="ix.ccciv-p3"></a><span class="c14"><a name="ix.ccciv-p3.1"></a>You</span> have, I think, been led to impose a contribution of mares <a name="fr_3260"></a><a href="#fn_3260"><font size="1">3261</font></a>
  on these people by false information on the part of the inhabitants.  What is going on is quite unfair.  It cannot but be displeasing to your excellency, and is distressing to me on account of my intimate connexion with the victims of the wrong.  I have therefore lost no time in begging your Lordship not to allow these promoters of iniquity to succeed in their malevolence.</p>
