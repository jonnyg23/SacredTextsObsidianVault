 </p><p class="c46"><a name="v.iv.xxxiii-p1"></a>  <span class="c12"><a name="v.iv.xxxiii-p1.1"></a>Chapter XXXII.</span></p>  
 <p class="c31"><a name="v.iv.xxxiii-p2"></a><span class="c12"><a name="v.iv.xxxiii-p2.1"></a>Paul</span> the bishop wrote a short work <i>On penitence</i> in which he lays down this law for penitents; that they ought to repent for their sins in such manner that they be not beyond measure overwhelmed with despairing sadness.</p>
