 </p><p class="c46"><a name="v.iii.cxvi-p1"></a>  <span class="c12"><a name="v.iii.cxvi-p1.1"></a>Chapter CXIV.</span></p>  
 <p class="c31"><a name="v.iii.cxvi-p2"></a><span class="c12"><a name="v.iii.cxvi-p2.1"></a>Epiphanius</span>, <a name="fr_2546"></a><a href="#fn_2546"><font size="1">2547</font></a>
  bishop of Salamina in Cyprus, wrote books <i>Against all heresies</i> and many others which are eagerly read by the learned, on account of their subject matter, and also by the plain people, on account of their language. He is still living, and in his extreme old age composes various brief works.</p>
