 </p><p class="c46"><a name="v.iii.cxiv-p1"></a>  <span class="c12"><a name="v.iii.cxiv-p1.1"></a>Chapter CXII.</span></p>  
 <p class="c31"><a name="v.iii.cxiv-p2"></a><span class="c12"><a name="v.iii.cxiv-p2.1"></a>Cyril</span>, <a name="fr_2544"></a><a href="#fn_2544"><font size="1">2545</font></a>
  bishop of Jerusalem often expelled by the church, and at last received, held the episcopate for eight consecutive years, in the reign of Theodosius. Certain <i>Catachetical lectures</i> of his, composed while he was a young man, are extant.</p>
