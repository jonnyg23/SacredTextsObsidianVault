 </p><p class="c46"><a name="iv.x.lvi-p1"></a>  <i>LVI. Festal.</i></p>  
 <p class="c31"><a name="iv.x.lvi-p2"></a>My grief is now at its height and my mind is seriously affected by it, but I have thought it right to fulfil the custom of the feast, so now I take my pen to salute your reverence and pay the debt of affection.</p>
