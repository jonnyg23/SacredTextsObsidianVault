 </p><p class="c46"><a name="v.iii.xciv-p1"></a>  <span class="c12"><a name="v.iii.xciv-p1.1"></a>Chapter XCII.</span></p>  
 <p class="c31"><a name="v.iii.xciv-p2"></a><span class="c12"><a name="v.iii.xciv-p2.1"></a>Triphylius</span>, <a name="fr_2517"></a><a href="#fn_2517"><font size="1">2518</font></a>
  bishop of Ledra or Leucotheon, <a name="fr_2518"></a><a href="#fn_2518"><font size="1">2519</font></a>
  in Cyprus, was the most eloquent man of his age, and was distinguished during the reign of Constantius. I have read his <i>Commentary on the Song of Songs.</i> He is said to have written many other works, none of which have come to our hand.</p>
