 <p> <a name="vi.xi.iii.xxi"></a>
 </p><p class="c24"><a name="vi.xi.iii.xxi-p1"></a>  17. Once more, in his letter to Marcella he says: <a name="fr_2964"></a><a href="#fn_2964"><font size="1">2965</font></a>
 </p>  
 <p class="c70"><a name="vi.xi.iii.xxi-p3"></a>“Ambrose, who supplied the paper, the money and the secretaries by the aid of which our Adamantius <a name="fr_2965"></a><a href="#fn_2965"><font size="1">2966</font></a>
  and Chalcenterus <a name="fr_2966"></a><a href="#fn_2966"><font size="1">2967</font></a>
  completed his innumerable books, in a certain letter written to the same person from Athens, declares that he never had a meal, when Origen was present, without something being read, and that he never went to bed without having some brother read aloud from the holy Scriptures. This he said he continued day and night, so that prayer waited upon reading and reading upon prayer.”</p>
