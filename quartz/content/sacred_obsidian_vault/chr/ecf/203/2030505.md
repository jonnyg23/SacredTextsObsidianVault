 <p> <a name="v.iii.cxxiv"></a>
 </p><p class="c46"><a name="v.iii.cxxiv-p1"></a>  <span class="c12"><a name="v.iii.cxxiv-p1.1"></a>Chapter CXXII.</span></p>  
 <p class="c31"><a name="v.iii.cxxiv-p2"></a><span class="c12"><a name="v.iii.cxxiv-p2.1"></a>Latronianus</span>, <a name="fr_2554"></a><a href="#fn_2554"><font size="1">2555</font></a>
  of Spain, a man of great learning, and in the matter of versification worthy to be compared with the poets of ancient time, was also put to death at Trèves with Priscillianus, Felicissimus, Julianus, and Euchrotia, coöriginators with him of schism. Various fruits of his genius written in different metres are extant.</p>
