 <p class="c31"><a name="v.iv.lxxv-p2"></a><span class="c12"><a name="v.iv.lxxv-p2.1"></a>Asclepius, <a name="fr_2700"></a><a href="#fn_2700"><font size="1">2701</font></a>
 </span> the African, bishop of a large see <a name="fr_2701"></a><a href="#fn_2701"><font size="1">2702</font></a>
  within the borders of Bagais, wrote against the Arians, and is said to be now writing against the Donatists. He is famous for his extemporaneous teaching.</p>
