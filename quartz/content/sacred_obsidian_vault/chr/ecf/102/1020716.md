 </p><p class="c34"><a name="v.iv.xx-p1"></a><span class="c2"><a name="v.iv.xx-p1.1"></a>Chapter 20.—The Resurrection to Damnation.</span></p>  
 <p class="c21"><a name="v.iv.xx-p2"></a>19.  Now he whose soul does not die to this world and begin here to be conformed to the truth, falls when the body dies into a more terrible death, and shall revive, not to change his earthly for a heavenly habitation, but to endure the penalty of his sin.</p>
