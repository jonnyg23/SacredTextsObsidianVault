 <p class="c21"><a name="ix.v.v-p2"></a>71. and Narcissus. <a name="fr_2949"></a><a href="#fn_2949"><font size="1">2950</font></a>
   For they did not suffer that selection of the Seventy-two to be wanting, as likewise neither that of the Twelve.  This <i>man</i> was of the Seventy-two:  perhaps he was a disciple of Addæus the apostle.</p>
