 </p><p class="c34"><a name="ix.v.viii-p1"></a> <span class="c1"><a name="ix.v.viii-p1.1"></a>VIII.</span></p> 
 <p class="c35"><a name="ix.v.viii-p2"></a><i>From the homily about the town of Antioch.</i> <a name="fr_2955"></a><a href="#fn_2955"><font size="1">2956</font></a>
 </p> 
 <p class="c21"><a name="ix.v.viii-p4"></a>To Simon was allotted Rome, <a name="fr_2956"></a><a href="#fn_2956"><font size="1">2957</font></a>
  and to John Ephesus; to Thomas India, and to Addæus the country of the Assyrians. <a name="fr_2957"></a><a href="#fn_2957"><font size="1">2958</font></a>
   And, when they were sent each one of them to the district which had been allotted to him, they devoted themselves <a name="fr_2958"></a><a href="#fn_2958"><font size="1">2959</font></a>
  to bring the <i>several</i> countries to discipleship.</p>
