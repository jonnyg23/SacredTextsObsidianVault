 <p> <a name="iv.iii.xxxiii"></a>
 </p><p class="c27"><a name="iv.iii.xxxiii-p1"></a> <span class="c6"><a name="iv.iii.xxxiii-p1.1"></a>Section XXXIII.</span></p> 
 <p class="c12"><a name="iv.iii.xxxiii-p2"></a>[1] [Arabic, p. 125]  <a name="fr_2240"></a><a href="#fn_2240"><font size="1">2241</font></a>
 And when evening came, Jesus went forth outside of the city, he and his [2] disciples.   <a name="fr_2241"></a><a href="#fn_2241"><font size="1">2242</font></a>
 And as they passed in the morning, the disciples saw that fig tree [3] withered away from its root.   <a name="fr_2242"></a><a href="#fn_2242"><font size="1">2243</font></a>
 And they passed by, and said, How did the fig tree dry [4] up immediately?   <a name="fr_2243"></a><a href="#fn_2243"><font size="1">2244</font></a>
 And Simon remembered, and said unto him, My Master, behold, <a name="page_94"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 94</font></a> <a name="iv.iii.xxxiii-Page_94"></a>[5] that fig tree which thou didst curse hath dried up.   <a name="fr_2244"></a><a href="#fn_2244"><font size="1">2245</font></a>
 And Jesus answered and said [6] unto them, Let there be in you the faith of God.   <a name="fr_2245"></a><a href="#fn_2245"><font size="1">2246</font></a>
 Verily I say unto you, if ye believe, and doubt not in your hearts, and assure yourselves that that will be which [7] ye say, ye shall have what ye say.   <a name="fr_2246"></a><a href="#fn_2246"><font size="1">2247</font></a>
 And if ye say to this mountain, Remove, and [8] fall <a name="fr_2247"></a><a href="#fn_2247"><font size="1">2248</font></a>
  into the sea, it shall be.   <a name="fr_2248"></a><a href="#fn_2248"><font size="1">2249</font></a>
 And all that ye ask God in prayer, and believe, he [9, 10] will give you.   <a name="fr_2249"></a><a href="#fn_2249"><font size="1">2250</font></a>
 And the apostles <a name="fr_2250"></a><a href="#fn_2250"><font size="1">2251</font></a>
  said unto our Lord, Increase our <a name="fr_2251"></a><a href="#fn_2251"><font size="1">2252</font></a>
  faith.   <a name="fr_2252"></a><a href="#fn_2252"><font size="1">2253</font></a>
 He said unto them, If there be in you faith like a grain of mustard, ye shall say to this fig tree, Be thou torn up, and be thou planted in the sea; and it will obey you.  [11]  <a name="fr_2253"></a><a href="#fn_2253"><font size="1">2254</font></a>
 Who of you hath a servant driving a yoke of oxen or tending sheep, and if he [12] come from the field, will say unto him straightway, Go and sit down?   <a name="fr_2254"></a><a href="#fn_2254"><font size="1">2255</font></a>
 Nay, <a name="fr_2255"></a><a href="#fn_2255"><font size="1">2256</font></a>
  he will say unto him, Make ready for me wherewith I may sup, and gird thy waist, and serve me, till I eat and drink; and afterwards thou shalt eat and drink also.  [13]  <a name="fr_2256"></a><a href="#fn_2256"><font size="1">2257</font></a>
 Doth that servant haply, who did what he was bid, receive his praise?  I think [14] not.   <a name="fr_2257"></a><a href="#fn_2257"><font size="1">2258</font></a>
 So ye also, when ye have done all that ye were bid, say, We are idle servants; what it was our duty to do, we have done.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.xxxiii-p21"></a>[15]  <a name="fr_2258"></a><a href="#fn_2258"><font size="1">2259</font></a>
 For this reason I say unto you, Whatever ye pray and ask, believe that ye [16] [Arabic, p. 126] receive, and ye shall have.   <a name="fr_2259"></a><a href="#fn_2259"><font size="1">2260</font></a>
 And when ye stand to pray, forgive what is in your heart against <i>any</i> man; and your Father which is in heaven will [17] forgive you also your wrong-doings.   <a name="fr_2260"></a><a href="#fn_2260"><font size="1">2261</font></a>
 But if ye forgive not men their wrong-doings, neither will your Father forgive you also your wrong-doings.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.xxxiii-p25"></a>[18]  <a name="fr_2261"></a><a href="#fn_2261"><font size="1">2262</font></a>
 And he spake unto them a parable also, that they should pray at all times, and [19] not be slothful:   <a name="fr_2262"></a><a href="#fn_2262"><font size="1">2263</font></a>
 There was a judge in a city, who feared not God, nor was ashamed [20] for men:   <a name="fr_2263"></a><a href="#fn_2263"><font size="1">2264</font></a>
 and there was a widow in that city; and she came unto him, and said, [21] Avenge me of mine adversary.   <a name="fr_2264"></a><a href="#fn_2264"><font size="1">2265</font></a>
 And he would not for a long time:  but afterwards he said within himself, If of God I have no fear, and before men I have no shame; [22]  <a name="fr_2265"></a><a href="#fn_2265"><font size="1">2266</font></a>
 <i>yet</i> because this widow vexeth <i>me</i>, I will avenge her, that she come not at all times [23, 24] and annoy me.   <a name="fr_2266"></a><a href="#fn_2266"><font size="1">2267</font></a>
 And our Lord said, Hear ye what the judge of injustice said.   <a name="fr_2267"></a><a href="#fn_2267"><font size="1">2268</font></a>
 And shall not God <i>still</i> more do vengeance for his elect, who call upon him in the night [25] and <i>in</i> the day, and grant them respite?   <a name="fr_2268"></a><a href="#fn_2268"><font size="1">2269</font></a>
 I say unto you, He will do vengeance for them speedily. Thinkest thou the Son of man will come and find faith on the earth?</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.xxxiii-p34"></a>[26, 27]  <a name="fr_2269"></a><a href="#fn_2269"><font size="1">2270</font></a>
 And they came again to Jerusalem.   <a name="fr_2270"></a><a href="#fn_2270"><font size="1">2271</font></a>
 And it came to pass, on one of the days, as Jesus was walking in the temple, and teaching the people, and preaching <i>the</i> [28] <i>gospel</i>,  <a name="fr_2271"></a><a href="#fn_2271"><font size="1">2272</font></a>
 that the chief priests and the scribes with the elders came upon him, and said unto him, Tell us:   <a name="fr_2272"></a><a href="#fn_2272"><font size="1">2273</font></a>
 By what power doest thou this? and who gave thee this [29] power to do that?   <a name="fr_2273"></a><a href="#fn_2273"><font size="1">2274</font></a>
 And Jesus said unto them, I also will ask you one word, and if [30] ye tell me, I also shall tell you by what power I do that.   <a name="fr_2274"></a><a href="#fn_2274"><font size="1">2275</font></a>
 The baptism of John, from [31] what place <i>is</i> it? from heaven or of men?   <a name="fr_2275"></a><a href="#fn_2275"><font size="1">2276</font></a>
 Tell me.   <a name="fr_2276"></a><a href="#fn_2276"><font size="1">2277</font></a>
 And they reflected within themselves, [Arabic, p. 127] and said, If we shall say unto him, From heaven; he will say unto [32] us, For what reason did ye not believe him?   <a name="fr_2277"></a><a href="#fn_2277"><font size="1">2278</font></a>
 But <a name="fr_2278"></a><a href="#fn_2278"><font size="1">2279</font></a>
  if we shall say, Of men; [33] we fear <a name="fr_2279"></a><a href="#fn_2279"><font size="1">2280</font></a>
  that the people will stone us, all of them.   <a name="fr_2280"></a><a href="#fn_2280"><font size="1">2281</font></a>
 And all of them were holding <a name="fr_2281"></a><a href="#fn_2281"><font size="1">2282</font></a>
  [34] to John, that he was a true prophet.   <a name="fr_2282"></a><a href="#fn_2282"><font size="1">2283</font></a>
 They answered and said unto him, We know [35] not.   <a name="fr_2283"></a><a href="#fn_2283"><font size="1">2284</font></a>
 Jesus said unto them, Neither tell I you also by what power I work.  What think ye?  A man had two sons; and he went to the first, and said unto him, My [36] son, go to-day, and till in the vineyard.   <a name="fr_2284"></a><a href="#fn_2284"><font size="1">2285</font></a>
 And he answered and said, I do not wish [37] to:  but finally he repented, and went.   <a name="fr_2285"></a><a href="#fn_2285"><font size="1">2286</font></a>
 And he went to the other, and said unto [38] him likewise.   <a name="fr_2286"></a><a href="#fn_2286"><font size="1">2287</font></a>
 And he answered and said, Yea, my lord:  and went not.  Which of these two did the will of his father?  They said unto him, The first.  Jesus said unto them, Verily I say unto you, The publicans and harlots go before you into [39] the kingdom of God.   <a name="fr_2287"></a><a href="#fn_2287"><font size="1">2288</font></a>
 John came unto you in the way of righteousness, and ye believed him not; but the publicans and harlots believed him; and ye, not even when ye saw, did ye repent at last, that ye might believe in him.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.xxxiii-p54"></a>[40]  <a name="fr_2288"></a><a href="#fn_2288"><font size="1">2289</font></a>
 Hear another parable:  A man was a householder, and planted a vineyard, and surrounded it with a hedge, and digged in it a winepress, and built in it a tower, [41, 42]  <a name="fr_2289"></a><a href="#fn_2289"><font size="1">2290</font></a>
 and gave it to husbandmen, and went to a distance for a long time.   <a name="fr_2290"></a><a href="#fn_2290"><font size="1">2291</font></a>
 So when the time of the fruits came, he sent his servants <a name="fr_2291"></a><a href="#fn_2291"><font size="1">2292</font></a>
  unto the husbandmen, that they might <a name="page_95"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 95</font></a> <a name="iv.iii.xxxiii-Page_95"></a>[43] send him of the produce <a name="fr_2292"></a><a href="#fn_2292"><font size="1">2293</font></a>
  of his vineyard.   <a name="fr_2293"></a><a href="#fn_2293"><font size="1">2294</font></a>
 And those husbandmen beat him, and [44] sent him away empty.   <a name="fr_2294"></a><a href="#fn_2294"><font size="1">2295</font></a>
 And he sent unto them another servant also; and they [45] stoned him, and wounded <a name="fr_2295"></a><a href="#fn_2295"><font size="1">2296</font></a>
  him, and sent him away with shameful handling.   <a name="fr_2296"></a><a href="#fn_2296"><font size="1">2297</font></a>
 And he sent again another; and they slew him.  And he sent many other servants unto [46] them.   <a name="fr_2297"></a><a href="#fn_2297"><font size="1">2298</font></a>
 And the husbandmen took his servants, and one they beat, and another they [47] stoned, and another they slew.   <a name="fr_2298"></a><a href="#fn_2298"><font size="1">2299</font></a>
 So he sent again other servants more than the first; and [48] [Arabic, p. 128] they did likewise with them.   <a name="fr_2299"></a><a href="#fn_2299"><font size="1">2300</font></a>
 So the owner of the vineyard said, What shall I do?  I will send my beloved son:  it may be they will see him and be [49, 50] ashamed.   <a name="fr_2300"></a><a href="#fn_2300"><font size="1">2301</font></a>
 So at last he sent unto them his beloved son that he had.   <a name="fr_2301"></a><a href="#fn_2301"><font size="1">2302</font></a>
 But the husbandmen, when they saw the son, said amongst themselves, This is the heir.  [51, 52]  <a name="fr_2302"></a><a href="#fn_2302"><font size="1">2303</font></a>
 And they said, We will slay him, and so the inheritance will be ours.   <a name="fr_2303"></a><a href="#fn_2303"><font size="1">2304</font></a>
 So they took [53] him, and put him forth without the vineyard, and slew him.   <a name="fr_2304"></a><a href="#fn_2304"><font size="1">2305</font></a>
 When then the lord [54] of the vineyard shall come, what will he do with those husbandmen?   <a name="fr_2305"></a><a href="#fn_2305"><font size="1">2306</font></a>
 They said unto him, He will destroy them in the worst of ways, <a name="fr_2306"></a><a href="#fn_2306"><font size="1">2307</font></a>
  and give the vineyard to [55] other husbandmen, who will give him fruit in its season.   <a name="fr_2307"></a><a href="#fn_2307"><font size="1">2308</font></a>
 Jesus said unto them, Have ye never read in the scripture,</p> 
 <p class="c25"><a name="iv.iii.xxxiii-p75"></a>The stone which the builders declared to be base,</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.xxxiii-p76"></a> <a name="fr_2308"></a><a href="#fn_2308"><font size="1">2309</font></a>
 The same came to be at the head of the corner:</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.xxxiii-p78"></a>[56]  <a name="fr_2309"></a><a href="#fn_2309"><font size="1">2310</font></a>
 From God was this,</p> 
 <p class="c21"><a name="iv.iii.xxxiii-p80"></a>And it is wonderful in our eyes?</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.xxxiii-p81"></a>[57]  <a name="fr_2310"></a><a href="#fn_2310"><font size="1">2311</font></a>
 Therefore I say unto you, The kingdom of God shall be taken from you, and [58] given to a people that will produce fruit.   <a name="fr_2311"></a><a href="#fn_2311"><font size="1">2312</font></a>
 And whosoever falleth on this stone shall be broken in pieces:  but on whomsoever it falleth, it will grind him to [59] powder.   <a name="fr_2312"></a><a href="#fn_2312"><font size="1">2313</font></a>
 And when the chief priests and the Pharisees heard his parables, they [60] perceived that it was concerning them he spake.   <a name="fr_2313"></a><a href="#fn_2313"><font size="1">2314</font></a>
 And they sought to seize him; and they feared the multitude, because they were holding to him as the prophet.</p>
