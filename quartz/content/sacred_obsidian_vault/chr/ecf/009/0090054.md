 <p> <a name="iv.iii.xlv"></a>
 </p><p class="c27"><a name="iv.iii.xlv-p1"></a> <span class="c6"><a name="iv.iii.xlv-p1.1"></a>Section XLV.</span></p> 
 <p class="c12"><a name="iv.iii.xlv-p2"></a>[1, 2] [Arabic, p. 170]  <a name="fr_3085"></a><a href="#fn_3085"><font size="1">3086</font></a>
 And one of his disciples was sitting <a name="fr_3086"></a><a href="#fn_3086"><font size="1">3087</font></a>
  in his bosom, <i>he</i> whom Jesus loved.   <a name="fr_3087"></a><a href="#fn_3087"><font size="1">3088</font></a>
 To him Simon Cephas beckoned, that he should ask him who this <i>was</i>, concerning [3] whom he spake.   <a name="fr_3088"></a><a href="#fn_3088"><font size="1">3089</font></a>
 And that disciple leaned <a name="fr_3089"></a><a href="#fn_3089"><font size="1">3090</font></a>
  on Jesus breast, and said unto him, [4] My Lord, who is this?   <a name="fr_3090"></a><a href="#fn_3090"><font size="1">3091</font></a>
 Jesus answered and said, He to whom I shall dip bread, and give it.  And Jesus dipped bread, and gave to Judas, the son of Simon Iscariot.  [5]  <a name="fr_3091"></a><a href="#fn_3091"><font size="1">3092</font></a>
 And after the bread, Satan entered him.  And Jesus said unto him, What thou [6] desirest to do, hasten the doing of it.   <a name="fr_3092"></a><a href="#fn_3092"><font size="1">3093</font></a>
 And no man of them that sat knew why he [7] said this unto him.   <a name="fr_3093"></a><a href="#fn_3093"><font size="1">3094</font></a>
 And some of them thought, because Judas had the box, that he was bidding him buy what would be needed for the feast; or, that he might pay [8] something to the poor.   <a name="fr_3094"></a><a href="#fn_3094"><font size="1">3095</font></a>
 Judas the betrayer answered and said, Can it be I, my [9] Master?  Jesus said unto him, Thou hast said.   <a name="fr_3095"></a><a href="#fn_3095"><font size="1">3096</font></a>
 And Judas took the bread straightway, and went forth without:  and it was still night.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.xlv-p14"></a>[10]  <a name="fr_3096"></a><a href="#fn_3096"><font size="1">3097</font></a>
 And Jesus said, Now is the Son of man being glorified, <a name="fr_3097"></a><a href="#fn_3097"><font size="1">3098</font></a>
  and God is being glorified <a name="fr_3098"></a><a href="#fn_3098"><font size="1">3099</font></a>
  [11] in him;  <a name="fr_3099"></a><a href="#fn_3099"><font size="1">3100</font></a>
 and if God is glorified in him, God also will glorify him in him, and straightway will glorify him.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.xlv-p19"></a>[12]  <a name="fr_3100"></a><a href="#fn_3100"><font size="1">3101</font></a>
 And while they were eating, Jesus took bread, and blessed, and divided;  <a name="fr_3101"></a><a href="#fn_3101"><font size="1">3102</font></a>
 and he <a name="page_113"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 113</font></a> <a name="iv.iii.xlv-Page_113"></a>[13] gave to his disciples, and said unto them, Take and eat; this is my body.   <a name="fr_3102"></a><a href="#fn_3102"><font size="1">3103</font></a>
 And he [Arabic, p. 171] took a cup, and gave thanks, and blessed, and gave them,  <a name="fr_3103"></a><a href="#fn_3103"><font size="1">3104</font></a>
 and said, Take [14, 15] and drink of it, all of you.   <a name="fr_3104"></a><a href="#fn_3104"><font size="1">3105</font></a>
 And they drank of it, all of them.   <a name="fr_3105"></a><a href="#fn_3105"><font size="1">3106</font></a>
 And he said unto them,  <a name="fr_3106"></a><a href="#fn_3106"><font size="1">3107</font></a>
 This is my blood, the new covenant, that is shed for many for the [16] forgiveness of sins.   <a name="fr_3107"></a><a href="#fn_3107"><font size="1">3108</font></a>
 I say unto you, I shall not drink henceforth of this, the juice of the vine, until the day in which I drink <a name="fr_3108"></a><a href="#fn_3108"><font size="1">3109</font></a>
  with you new <i>wine</i> in the kingdom of [17] God.   <a name="fr_3109"></a><a href="#fn_3109"><font size="1">3110</font></a>
 And thus do ye in remembrance of me.   <a name="fr_3110"></a><a href="#fn_3110"><font size="1">3111</font></a>
 And Jesus said unto Simon, Simon, [18] behold, Satan asketh that he may sift you like wheat:   <a name="fr_3111"></a><a href="#fn_3111"><font size="1">3112</font></a>
 but I entreat <a name="fr_3112"></a><a href="#fn_3112"><font size="1">3113</font></a>
  for thee, that thou lose not thy faith: <a name="fr_3113"></a><a href="#fn_3113"><font size="1">3114</font></a>
   and do thou, at some time, turn <a name="fr_3114"></a><a href="#fn_3114"><font size="1">3115</font></a>
  and strengthen thy brethren.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.xlv-p35"></a>[19]  <a name="fr_3115"></a><a href="#fn_3115"><font size="1">3116</font></a>
 My children, another little <i>while</i> am I with you.  And ye shall seek me:  and as [20] I said unto the Jews, Whither I go, ye cannot come; I say unto you now also.   <a name="fr_3116"></a><a href="#fn_3116"><font size="1">3117</font></a>
 A new commandment I give you, that ye may love one another; and as I have loved [21] you, so shall ye also love one another.   <a name="fr_3117"></a><a href="#fn_3117"><font size="1">3118</font></a>
 By this shall every man know that ye are [22] my disciples, if ye have love one to another.   <a name="fr_3118"></a><a href="#fn_3118"><font size="1">3119</font></a>
 Simon Cephas said unto him, Our Lord, whither goest thou?  Jesus answered and said unto him, Whither I go, thou canst not now follow me; but later thou shalt come.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.xlv-p40"></a>[23]  <a name="fr_3119"></a><a href="#fn_3119"><font size="1">3120</font></a>
 Then said Jesus unto them, Ye all shall desert <a name="fr_3120"></a><a href="#fn_3120"><font size="1">3121</font></a>
  me this night: <a name="fr_3121"></a><a href="#fn_3121"><font size="1">3122</font></a>
   it is written, I [24] will smite the shepherd, and the sheep of the flock shall be scattered.   <a name="fr_3122"></a><a href="#fn_3122"><font size="1">3123</font></a>
 But after my [25] rising, I shall go before you into Galilee.   <a name="fr_3123"></a><a href="#fn_3123"><font size="1">3124</font></a>
 Simon Cephas answered and said unto [26] him, My Lord, if every man desert thee, I shall at no time desert thee.   <a name="fr_3124"></a><a href="#fn_3124"><font size="1">3125</font></a>
 I am with thee ready for imprisonment and for death.   <a name="fr_3125"></a><a href="#fn_3125"><font size="1">3126</font></a>
 And my life will I give up for thee.  [27] [Arabic, p. 172]  <a name="fr_3126"></a><a href="#fn_3126"><font size="1">3127</font></a>
 Jesus said unto him, Wilt thou give up thy life for me?   <a name="fr_3127"></a><a href="#fn_3127"><font size="1">3128</font></a>
 Verily, verily, I say unto thee, Thou shalt to-day, during this night, before the cock crow [28] twice, three times deny me, that thou knowest me not.   <a name="fr_3128"></a><a href="#fn_3128"><font size="1">3129</font></a>
 But Cephas said the more, <a name="fr_3129"></a><a href="#fn_3129"><font size="1">3130</font></a>
  Even if it lead to <a name="fr_3130"></a><a href="#fn_3130"><font size="1">3131</font></a>
  death with thee, I shall not deny thee, my Lord.  And in like manner said all the disciples also.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.xlv-p53"></a>[29]  <a name="fr_3131"></a><a href="#fn_3131"><font size="1">3132</font></a>
 Then Jesus said unto them, Let not your hearts be troubled: <a name="fr_3132"></a><a href="#fn_3132"><font size="1">3133</font></a>
   believe in God, [30] and believe in me.   <a name="fr_3133"></a><a href="#fn_3133"><font size="1">3134</font></a>
 The stations <a name="fr_3134"></a><a href="#fn_3134"><font size="1">3135</font></a>
  in my Fathers house are many, else I should [31] have told <a name="fr_3135"></a><a href="#fn_3135"><font size="1">3136</font></a>
  you.  I <a name="fr_3136"></a><a href="#fn_3136"><font size="1">3137</font></a>
  go to prepare for you a place.   <a name="fr_3137"></a><a href="#fn_3137"><font size="1">3138</font></a>
 And if I go <i>to</i> prepare for you a place, I shall return again, and take you unto me:  and so where I am, there ye [32, 33] shall be also.   <a name="fr_3138"></a><a href="#fn_3138"><font size="1">3139</font></a>
 And the place that I go ye know, <a name="fr_3139"></a><a href="#fn_3139"><font size="1">3140</font></a>
  and the way ye know. <a name="fr_3140"></a><a href="#fn_3140"><font size="1">3141</font></a>
    <a name="fr_3141"></a><a href="#fn_3141"><font size="1">3142</font></a>
 Thomas said unto him, Our Lord, we know not whither thou goest; and how is the way for [34] us to the knowledge of that? <a name="fr_3142"></a><a href="#fn_3142"><font size="1">3143</font></a>
    <a name="fr_3143"></a><a href="#fn_3143"><font size="1">3144</font></a>
 Jesus said unto him, I am the way, and the truth, [35] and the life:  and no man cometh unto my Father, but through me.   <a name="fr_3144"></a><a href="#fn_3144"><font size="1">3145</font></a>
 And if ye had known me, ye should have known my Father:  and from henceforth ye know <a name="fr_3145"></a><a href="#fn_3145"><font size="1">3146</font></a>
  him, [36] and have seen him.   <a name="fr_3146"></a><a href="#fn_3146"><font size="1">3147</font></a>
 Philip <a name="fr_3147"></a><a href="#fn_3147"><font size="1">3148</font></a>
  said unto him, Our Lord, shew us the Father, and it sufficeth [37] us.   <a name="fr_3148"></a><a href="#fn_3148"><font size="1">3149</font></a>
 Jesus said unto him, Have I been all this time with you, and dost thou not know <a name="fr_3149"></a><a href="#fn_3149"><font size="1">3150</font></a>
  me, Philip? <a name="fr_3150"></a><a href="#fn_3150"><font size="1">3151</font></a>
  whosoever hath seen me hath seen the Father; how then sayest [38] thou, Shew us the Father?   <a name="fr_3151"></a><a href="#fn_3151"><font size="1">3152</font></a>
 Believest thou not that I am in my Father, and my Father in me? and the saying that I say, I say not of myself:  but my Father who dwelleth in [39] me, he doeth these deeds.   <a name="fr_3152"></a><a href="#fn_3152"><font size="1">3153</font></a>
 Believe that I am in my Father, and my Father in me:  [40] [Arabic, p. 173] or else believe for the sake of the deeds.   <a name="fr_3153"></a><a href="#fn_3153"><font size="1">3154</font></a>
 Verily, verily, I say unto you, Whosoever believeth in me, the deeds that I do shall he do also; and [41] more than that shall he do:  I go unto the Father.   <a name="fr_3154"></a><a href="#fn_3154"><font size="1">3155</font></a>
 And what ye shall ask in my [42] name, I shall do unto you, that the Father may be glorified in his Son.   <a name="fr_3155"></a><a href="#fn_3155"><font size="1">3156</font></a>
 And if ye [43, 44] ask me <a name="fr_3156"></a><a href="#fn_3156"><font size="1">3157</font></a>
  in my name, I will do <i>it</i>.   <a name="fr_3157"></a><a href="#fn_3157"><font size="1">3158</font></a>
 If ye love me, keep my commandments.   <a name="fr_3158"></a><a href="#fn_3158"><font size="1">3159</font></a>
 And I will entreat of my Father, and he will send unto you another Paraclete, that he [45] may be with you for ever, <i>even</i> the Spirit of truth:   <a name="fr_3159"></a><a href="#fn_3159"><font size="1">3160</font></a>
 whom the world cannot receive; for it hath not seen him, nor known him:  but ye know him; for he hath dwelt <a name="fr_3160"></a><a href="#fn_3160"><font size="1">3161</font></a>
  <a name="page_114"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 114</font></a> <a name="iv.iii.xlv-Page_114"></a>[46] with you, and is in you.   <a name="fr_3161"></a><a href="#fn_3161"><font size="1">3162</font></a>
 I will not leave you orphans:  I will come unto you.  [47]  <a name="fr_3162"></a><a href="#fn_3162"><font size="1">3163</font></a>
 Another little <i>while</i>, and the world seeth me not; but ye see me that I live, and ye [48] shall live also.   <a name="fr_3163"></a><a href="#fn_3163"><font size="1">3164</font></a>
 And in that day ye shall know that I am in my Father, and ye in me, and I in you.</p>
