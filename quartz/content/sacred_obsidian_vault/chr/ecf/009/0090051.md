 <p> <a name="iv.iii.xlii"></a>
 </p><p class="c27"><a name="iv.iii.xlii-p1"></a> <span class="c6"><a name="iv.iii.xlii-p1.1"></a>Section XLII.</span></p> 
 <p class="c12"><a name="iv.iii.xlii-p2"></a>[1]  <a name="fr_2880"></a><a href="#fn_2880"><font size="1">2881</font></a>
 But when ye see Jerusalem with the army compassing it about, then know that [2] its desolation is come near.   <a name="fr_2881"></a><a href="#fn_2881"><font size="1">2882</font></a>
 Those then that are in Judæa at that time shall flee to the mountain; and those that are within her shall flee; and those that are in the [3] villages shall not enter her.   <a name="fr_2882"></a><a href="#fn_2882"><font size="1">2883</font></a>
 For these days are the days of vengeance, that all that [4] is written may be fulfilled.   <a name="fr_2883"></a><a href="#fn_2883"><font size="1">2884</font></a>
 And when ye see the unclean sign of desolation, <a name="fr_2884"></a><a href="#fn_2884"><font size="1">2885</font></a>
  spoken of in Daniel the prophet, standing in the pure place, he that readeth shall understand, [5, 6]  <a name="fr_2885"></a><a href="#fn_2885"><font size="1">2886</font></a>
 and then he that is in Judæa shall flee in to the mountain:   <a name="fr_2886"></a><a href="#fn_2886"><font size="1">2887</font></a>
 and let him that is on the [7] roof not go down, nor enter in to take anything from his house:   <a name="fr_2887"></a><a href="#fn_2887"><font size="1">2888</font></a>
 and let him that is in [8] [Arabic, p. 159] the field not turn behind him to take his garment.   <a name="fr_2888"></a><a href="#fn_2888"><font size="1">2889</font></a>
 Woe to them that are with child and to them that give suck in those days! there shall be great [9] distress in the land, and wrath against this nation.   <a name="fr_2889"></a><a href="#fn_2889"><font size="1">2890</font></a>
 And they shall fall on the edge of the sword, <a name="fr_2890"></a><a href="#fn_2890"><font size="1">2891</font></a>
  and shall be taken captive to every land:  and Jerusalem shall be trodden down of the nations, until the times of the nations be ended.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.xlii-p14"></a>[10]  <a name="fr_2891"></a><a href="#fn_2891"><font size="1">2892</font></a>
 Then if any man say unto you, The Messiah is here; or, Lo, he is there; believe [11] him not:   <a name="fr_2892"></a><a href="#fn_2892"><font size="1">2893</font></a>
 there shall rise then false Messiahs and prophets of lying, and shall do signs and wonders, in order that they may lead astray even the elect also, if they [12] be able.   <a name="fr_2893"></a><a href="#fn_2893"><font size="1">2894</font></a>
 But as for you, beware:  for I have acquainted you with everything [13] beforehand.   <a name="fr_2894"></a><a href="#fn_2894"><font size="1">2895</font></a>
 If then they say unto you, Lo, he is in the desert; go not out, lest ye [14] be taken:  and if they say unto you, Lo, he is in the chamber; believe not.   <a name="fr_2895"></a><a href="#fn_2895"><font size="1">2896</font></a>
 And as the lightning appeareth from the east, and is seen unto the west; so shall be the [15] coming of the Son of man.   <a name="fr_2896"></a><a href="#fn_2896"><font size="1">2897</font></a>
 But first he must suffer much and be rejected by this [16] generation. <a name="fr_2897"></a><a href="#fn_2897"><font size="1">2898</font></a>
    <a name="fr_2898"></a><a href="#fn_2898"><font size="1">2899</font></a>
 Pray therefore that your flight be not in winter, nor on a sabbath:  [17]  <a name="fr_2899"></a><a href="#fn_2899"><font size="1">2900</font></a>
 there shall be then great tribulation, <a name="fr_2900"></a><a href="#fn_2900"><font size="1">2901</font></a>
  the like of which there hath not been from the [18] beginning of the world till now, nor shall be.   <a name="fr_2901"></a><a href="#fn_2901"><font size="1">2902</font></a>
 And except the Lord had shortened those days, no flesh would have lived:  but because of the elect, whom he elected, [19] he shortened those days.   <a name="fr_2902"></a><a href="#fn_2902"><font size="1">2903</font></a>
 And there shall be signs in the sun and the moon and the stars; and upon the earth affliction <a name="fr_2903"></a><a href="#fn_2903"><font size="1">2904</font></a>
  of the nations, and rubbing of hands for the confusion <a name="fr_2904"></a><a href="#fn_2904"><font size="1">2905</font></a>
  [20] [Arabic, p. 160] of the noise of the sea, and an earthquake:   <a name="fr_2905"></a><a href="#fn_2905"><font size="1">2906</font></a>
 the souls of men shall [21] go forth from fear of that which is to come upon the earth.   <a name="fr_2906"></a><a href="#fn_2906"><font size="1">2907</font></a>
 And in those days, straightway after the distress of those days, the sun shall become dark, and the moon shall not shew its light, and the stars shall fall from heaven, and the powers [22] of heaven shall be convulsed:   <a name="fr_2907"></a><a href="#fn_2907"><font size="1">2908</font></a>
 and then shall appear the sign of the Son of man in heaven:  and at that time all the tribes of the earth shall wail, and look unto the Son [23] of man coming on the clouds of heaven with power and much glory.   <a name="fr_2908"></a><a href="#fn_2908"><font size="1">2909</font></a>
 And he shall send his angels with the great trumpet, and they shall gather his elect from the four [24] winds, from one end of heaven to the other. <a name="fr_2909"></a><a href="#fn_2909"><font size="1">2910</font></a>
    <a name="fr_2910"></a><a href="#fn_2910"><font size="1">2911</font></a>
 But when these things begin to be, be of good cheer, and lift up your heads; for your salvation <a name="fr_2911"></a><a href="#fn_2911"><font size="1">2912</font></a>
  is come near.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.xlii-p36"></a>[25]  <a name="fr_2912"></a><a href="#fn_2912"><font size="1">2913</font></a>
 Learn the example of the fig tree:  when it letteth down its branches, <a name="fr_2913"></a><a href="#fn_2913"><font size="1">2914</font></a>
  and putteth [26] forth its leaves, ye know that the summer is come;  <a name="fr_2914"></a><a href="#fn_2914"><font size="1">2915</font></a>
 so ye also, when ye see these things begun to be, know ye that the kingdom of God hath arrived at the [27] door.   <a name="fr_2915"></a><a href="#fn_2915"><font size="1">2916</font></a>
 Verily I say unto you, This generation shall not pass away, until all these [28] <i>things</i> shall be.   <a name="fr_2916"></a><a href="#fn_2916"><font size="1">2917</font></a>
 Heaven and earth shall pass away, but my sayings shall not pass away.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.xlii-p42"></a>[29]  <a name="fr_2917"></a><a href="#fn_2917"><font size="1">2918</font></a>
 Take heed to yourselves, that your hearts become not heavy with inordinate desire, <a name="fr_2918"></a><a href="#fn_2918"><font size="1">2919</font></a>
  and drunkenness, and the care of the world at any time, and that day come <a name="page_109"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 109</font></a> <a name="iv.iii.xlii-Page_109"></a>[30] upon you suddenly:   <a name="fr_2919"></a><a href="#fn_2919"><font size="1">2920</font></a>
 for it is as a shock that shocks all the inhabitants that are on the [31] face of the whole earth.   <a name="fr_2920"></a><a href="#fn_2920"><font size="1">2921</font></a>
 Watch at all times, and pray, that ye may be worthy to escape [Arabic, p. 161] from all the things that are to be, and that ye may stand before the Son of [32] man.   <a name="fr_2921"></a><a href="#fn_2921"><font size="1">2922</font></a>
 Of that day and of that hour hath no man learned, not even the angels [33] of heaven, neither the Son, but the Father.   <a name="fr_2922"></a><a href="#fn_2922"><font size="1">2923</font></a>
 See ye, and watch and pray:  for ye know [34] not when that time <i>will be</i>.   <a name="fr_2923"></a><a href="#fn_2923"><font size="1">2924</font></a>
 <i>It is</i> as a man, who journeyed, and left his house, and gave his authority to his servants, and appointed every man to his work, and [35] charged the porter to be wakeful.   <a name="fr_2924"></a><a href="#fn_2924"><font size="1">2925</font></a>
 Be wakeful then: <a name="fr_2925"></a><a href="#fn_2925"><font size="1">2926</font></a>
   since ye know not when the lord of the house cometh, in the evening, or in the middle of the night, or when the [36] cock croweth, or in the morning;  <a name="fr_2926"></a><a href="#fn_2926"><font size="1">2927</font></a>
 lest he come unexpectedly, and find you sleeping.  [37]  <a name="fr_2927"></a><a href="#fn_2927"><font size="1">2928</font></a>
 The thing that I say unto you, unto all of you do I say it, Be ye watchful.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.xlii-p54"></a>[38]  <a name="fr_2928"></a><a href="#fn_2928"><font size="1">2929</font></a>
 For as it was in the days of Noah, so shall the coming of the Son of man be.  [39]  <a name="fr_2929"></a><a href="#fn_2929"><font size="1">2930</font></a>
 As they were before the flood eating and drinking, and taking wives, and giving [40] wives to men,  <a name="fr_2930"></a><a href="#fn_2930"><font size="1">2931</font></a>
 until the day in which Noah entered into the ark, and they perceived not till the flood came, and took them all; so shall the coming of the Son of man [41] be.   <a name="fr_2931"></a><a href="#fn_2931"><font size="1">2932</font></a>
 And as it was in the days of Lot; they were eating and drinking, and selling [42] and buying, and planting and building,  <a name="fr_2932"></a><a href="#fn_2932"><font size="1">2933</font></a>
 on the day in which Lot went out from Sodom, and the Lord rained fire and brimstone from heaven, and destroyed them [43, 44] all:   <a name="fr_2933"></a><a href="#fn_2933"><font size="1">2934</font></a>
 so shall it be in the day in which the Son of man is revealed. <a name="fr_2934"></a><a href="#fn_2934"><font size="1">2935</font></a>
    <a name="fr_2935"></a><a href="#fn_2935"><font size="1">2936</font></a>
 And in that day, whosoever is on the roof, and his garments <a name="fr_2936"></a><a href="#fn_2936"><font size="1">2937</font></a>
  in the house, let him not go down to [45] take them:  and he that is in the field shall not turn behind him.   <a name="fr_2937"></a><a href="#fn_2937"><font size="1">2938</font></a>
 Remember Lots [46] wife.   <a name="fr_2938"></a><a href="#fn_2938"><font size="1">2939</font></a>
 Whosoever shall desire to save his life shall destroy it:  but whosoever shall [47] destroy his life shall save it.   <a name="fr_2939"></a><a href="#fn_2939"><font size="1">2940</font></a>
 Verily I say unto you, In that night there shall be two on [48] [Arabic, p. 162] one bed; one shall be taken, and another left.   <a name="fr_2940"></a><a href="#fn_2940"><font size="1">2941</font></a>
 And two <i>women</i> shall be grinding [49] at one mill; one shall be taken, and another left.   <a name="fr_2941"></a><a href="#fn_2941"><font size="1">2942</font></a>
 And two shall be in the [50] field; one shall be taken, and another left.   <a name="fr_2942"></a><a href="#fn_2942"><font size="1">2943</font></a>
 They answered and said unto him, To what place, our Lord?  He said unto them, Where the body is, there will the eagles [51, 52] gather.   <a name="fr_2943"></a><a href="#fn_2943"><font size="1">2944</font></a>
 Be attentive now:  for ye know not at what hour your Lord cometh.   <a name="fr_2944"></a><a href="#fn_2944"><font size="1">2945</font></a>
 Know this:  if the master of the house had known in what watch the thief would come, he would have been attentive, and would not make it possible that his house should be [53] broken through.   <a name="fr_2945"></a><a href="#fn_2945"><font size="1">2946</font></a>
 Therefore be ye also ready:  for in the hour that ye think not the Son of man cometh.</p>
