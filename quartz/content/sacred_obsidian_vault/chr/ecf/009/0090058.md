 <p> <a name="iv.iii.xlix"></a>
 </p><p class="c27"><a name="iv.iii.xlix-p1"></a> <span class="c6"><a name="iv.iii.xlix-p1.1"></a>Section XLIX.</span></p> 
 <p class="c12"><a name="iv.iii.xlix-p2"></a>[1, 2]  <a name="fr_3386"></a><a href="#fn_3386"><font size="1">3387</font></a>
 And the chief priest asked Jesus about his disciples, and about his doctrine. <a name="fr_3387"></a><a href="#fn_3387"><font size="1">3388</font></a>
    <a name="fr_3388"></a><a href="#fn_3388"><font size="1">3389</font></a>
 And Jesus said unto him, I was speaking <a name="fr_3389"></a><a href="#fn_3389"><font size="1">3390</font></a>
  openly to the people; and I ever taught in the synagogue, and in the temple, where all the Jews gather; and I have spoken nothing in [3] [Arabic, p. 185] secret.   <a name="fr_3390"></a><a href="#fn_3390"><font size="1">3391</font></a>
 Why askest thou me? ask those that have heard, what I spake unto [4] them:  for they know all that I said.   <a name="fr_3391"></a><a href="#fn_3391"><font size="1">3392</font></a>
 And when he had said that, one of the soldiers which were standing <i>there</i> struck the cheek <a name="fr_3392"></a><a href="#fn_3392"><font size="1">3393</font></a>
  of Jesus, and said unto him, <a name="page_119"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 119</font></a> <a name="iv.iii.xlix-Page_119"></a>[5] Dost thou thus answer the chief priest?   <a name="fr_3393"></a><a href="#fn_3393"><font size="1">3394</font></a>
 Jesus answered and said unto him, If I [6] have spoken evil, bear witness of evil: <a name="fr_3394"></a><a href="#fn_3394"><font size="1">3395</font></a>
   but if well, why didst thou smite me?   <a name="fr_3395"></a><a href="#fn_3395"><font size="1">3396</font></a>
 And Annas sent Jesus bound unto Caiaphas the chief priest.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.xlix-p13"></a>[7]  <a name="fr_3396"></a><a href="#fn_3396"><font size="1">3397</font></a>
 And when Jesus went out, Simon Cephas was standing in the outer court warming [8] himself.   <a name="fr_3397"></a><a href="#fn_3397"><font size="1">3398</font></a>
 And that maid saw him again, and began to say to those that stood [9] <i>by</i>, This <i>man</i> also was there with Jesus the Nazarene.   <a name="fr_3398"></a><a href="#fn_3398"><font size="1">3399</font></a>
 And those that stood <i>by</i> [10] came forward and said to Cephas, Truly thou art one of his disciples.   <a name="fr_3399"></a><a href="#fn_3399"><font size="1">3400</font></a>
 And he [11] denied again with an oath, I know not the man.   <a name="fr_3400"></a><a href="#fn_3400"><font size="1">3401</font></a>
 And after a little one of the servants of the chief priest, the kinsman of him whose ear Simon cut off, saw him; and [12] he disputed <a name="fr_3401"></a><a href="#fn_3401"><font size="1">3402</font></a>
  and said, Truly this <i>man</i> was with him:   <a name="fr_3402"></a><a href="#fn_3402"><font size="1">3403</font></a>
 and he also is a Galilæan; [13] and his speech resembles. <a name="fr_3403"></a><a href="#fn_3403"><font size="1">3404</font></a>
    <a name="fr_3404"></a><a href="#fn_3404"><font size="1">3405</font></a>
 And he said unto Simon, Did not I see thee with him [14] in the garden?   <a name="fr_3405"></a><a href="#fn_3405"><font size="1">3406</font></a>
 Then began Simon to curse, <a name="fr_3406"></a><a href="#fn_3406"><font size="1">3407</font></a>
  and to swear, I know not this man [15] whom ye have mentioned.   <a name="fr_3407"></a><a href="#fn_3407"><font size="1">3408</font></a>
 And immediately, while he was speaking, the cock crew [16] twice.   <a name="fr_3408"></a><a href="#fn_3408"><font size="1">3409</font></a>
 And in that hour Jesus turned, he being without, and looked stedfastly at Cephas.  And Simon remembered the word of our Lord, which he said unto him, [17, 18]  <a name="fr_3409"></a><a href="#fn_3409"><font size="1">3410</font></a>
 Before the cock crow twice, thou shalt deny me thrice.   <a name="fr_3410"></a><a href="#fn_3410"><font size="1">3411</font></a>
 And Simon went forth without, and wept bitterly.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.xlix-p29"></a>[19] [Arabic, p. 186]  <a name="fr_3411"></a><a href="#fn_3411"><font size="1">3412</font></a>
 And when the morning approached, the servants of all the chief priests and the scribes and the elders of the people and all the multitude assembled, [20, 21] and made a plot;  <a name="fr_3412"></a><a href="#fn_3412"><font size="1">3413</font></a>
 and they took counsel against Jesus to put him to death.   <a name="fr_3413"></a><a href="#fn_3413"><font size="1">3414</font></a>
 And they sought false witnesses who should witness against him, that they might put him to [22, 23] death, and they found not;  <a name="fr_3414"></a><a href="#fn_3414"><font size="1">3415</font></a>
 but many false witnesses came,  <a name="fr_3415"></a><a href="#fn_3415"><font size="1">3416</font></a>
 but their witness did not [24, 25] agree.   <a name="fr_3416"></a><a href="#fn_3416"><font size="1">3417</font></a>
 But at last there came two lying witnesses,  <a name="fr_3417"></a><a href="#fn_3417"><font size="1">3418</font></a>
 and said, We heard him say, I will destroy this <a name="fr_3418"></a><a href="#fn_3418"><font size="1">3419</font></a>
  temple of God that is made with hands, and will build another not [26, 27] made with hands after three days.   <a name="fr_3419"></a><a href="#fn_3419"><font size="1">3420</font></a>
 And not even so did their witness agree.  But Jesus was silent.   <a name="fr_3420"></a><a href="#fn_3420"><font size="1">3421</font></a>
 And the chief priest rose in the midst, and asked Jesus, and said, [28]  <a name="fr_3421"></a><a href="#fn_3421"><font size="1">3422</font></a>
 Answerest thou not a word concerning anything? what do these <a name="fr_3422"></a><a href="#fn_3422"><font size="1">3423</font></a>
  witness against [29, 30] thee?   <a name="fr_3423"></a><a href="#fn_3423"><font size="1">3424</font></a>
 But Jesus was silent, and answered him nothing.   <a name="fr_3424"></a><a href="#fn_3424"><font size="1">3425</font></a>
 And they took him up [31] into their assembly, <a name="fr_3425"></a><a href="#fn_3425"><font size="1">3426</font></a>
  and said unto him, If thou art the Messiah, tell us.   <a name="fr_3426"></a><a href="#fn_3426"><font size="1">3427</font></a>
 He said [32] unto them, If I tell you, ye will not believe me:   <a name="fr_3427"></a><a href="#fn_3427"><font size="1">3428</font></a>
 and if I ask you, ye will not answer [33] me a word, nor let me go.   <a name="fr_3428"></a><a href="#fn_3428"><font size="1">3429</font></a>
 And the chief priest answered and said unto him, I adjure thee by the living God, that thou tell us whether thou art the Messiah, the [34, 35] Son of the living God.   <a name="fr_3429"></a><a href="#fn_3429"><font size="1">3430</font></a>
 Jesus said unto him, Thou hast said that I am he.   <a name="fr_3430"></a><a href="#fn_3430"><font size="1">3431</font></a>
 They all said unto him, Then thou art now the Son of God?  Jesus said, Ye have said [36] that I am he.   <a name="fr_3431"></a><a href="#fn_3431"><font size="1">3432</font></a>
 I say unto you, that henceforth ye shall see the Son of man sitting [37] [Arabic, p. 187] at the right hand of power, and coming on the clouds of heaven.   <a name="fr_3432"></a><a href="#fn_3432"><font size="1">3433</font></a>
 Then the [38] chief priest rent his tunic, <a name="fr_3433"></a><a href="#fn_3433"><font size="1">3434</font></a>
  and said, He hath blasphemed.   <a name="fr_3434"></a><a href="#fn_3434"><font size="1">3435</font></a>
 And they all said, Why should we seek now witnesses? we have heard now the blasphemy from his mouth.  [39, 40] What then think ye?   <a name="fr_3435"></a><a href="#fn_3435"><font size="1">3436</font></a>
 They all answered and said, He is worthy of death.   <a name="fr_3436"></a><a href="#fn_3436"><font size="1">3437</font></a>
 Then some of them drew near, and spat in his face, and struck him, and scoffed at him.  [41]  <a name="fr_3437"></a><a href="#fn_3437"><font size="1">3438</font></a>
 And the soldiers struck him on his cheeks, <a name="fr_3438"></a><a href="#fn_3438"><font size="1">3439</font></a>
  and said, Prophesy unto us, <i>thou</i> Messiah:  [42] who is he that struck thee?   <a name="fr_3439"></a><a href="#fn_3439"><font size="1">3440</font></a>
 And many other things spake they falsely, <a name="fr_3440"></a><a href="#fn_3440"><font size="1">3441</font></a>
  and said against him.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.xlix-p60"></a>[43]  <a name="fr_3441"></a><a href="#fn_3441"><font size="1">3442</font></a>
 And all of their assembly arose, <a name="fr_3442"></a><a href="#fn_3442"><font size="1">3443</font></a>
  and took Jesus, and brought him bound <a name="fr_3443"></a><a href="#fn_3443"><font size="1">3444</font></a>
  to [44] the prætorium, <a name="fr_3444"></a><a href="#fn_3444"><font size="1">3445</font></a>
  and delivered him up to Pilate the judge;  <a name="fr_3445"></a><a href="#fn_3445"><font size="1">3446</font></a>
 but they entered not into the prætorium, that they might not be defiled when they should eat the passover.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.xlix-p66"></a>[45]  <a name="fr_3446"></a><a href="#fn_3446"><font size="1">3447</font></a>
 And Jesus stood before the judge.  And Pilate went forth unto them without, and [46] said unto them, What accusation <a name="fr_3447"></a><a href="#fn_3447"><font size="1">3448</font></a>
  have ye against this man?   <a name="fr_3448"></a><a href="#fn_3448"><font size="1">3449</font></a>
 They answered and said unto him, If he had not been doing evils, neither should we have delivered [47] him up unto thee.   <a name="fr_3449"></a><a href="#fn_3449"><font size="1">3450</font></a>
 We found this <i>man</i> leading our people astray, and restraining from giving tribute to Cæsar, and saying of himself that he is the King, the Messiah.  [48]  <a name="fr_3450"></a><a href="#fn_3450"><font size="1">3451</font></a>
 Pilate said unto them, Then take ye him, and judge him according to your law.  <a name="page_120"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 120</font></a> <a name="iv.iii.xlix-Page_120"></a>[Arabic, p. 188] The Jews said unto him, We have no authority to put a man to death:  [49]  <a name="fr_3451"></a><a href="#fn_3451"><font size="1">3452</font></a>
 that the word might be fulfilled, which Jesus spake, when he made known by what manner of death he was to die.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.xlix-p73"></a>[50]  <a name="fr_3452"></a><a href="#fn_3452"><font size="1">3453</font></a>
 And Pilate entered into the prætorium, and called Jesus, and said unto him, Art [51] thou the King of the Jews?   <a name="fr_3453"></a><a href="#fn_3453"><font size="1">3454</font></a>
 Jesus said unto him, Of thyself saidst thou this, or [52] did others tell it thee concerning me?   <a name="fr_3454"></a><a href="#fn_3454"><font size="1">3455</font></a>
 Pilate said unto him, Am I, forsooth, <a name="fr_3455"></a><a href="#fn_3455"><font size="1">3456</font></a>
  a Jew?  The sons of thy nation <a name="fr_3456"></a><a href="#fn_3456"><font size="1">3457</font></a>
  and the chief priests delivered thee unto me:  what [53] hast thou done?   <a name="fr_3457"></a><a href="#fn_3457"><font size="1">3458</font></a>
 Jesus said unto him, My kingdom is not of this world:  if my kingdom were of this world, then would my servants fight, that I should not be [54] delivered to the Jews:  now my kingdom is not from hence.   <a name="fr_3458"></a><a href="#fn_3458"><font size="1">3459</font></a>
 Pilate said unto him, Then <i>thou art</i> a king?  Jesus said unto him, Thou hast said that I am a king.  And for this was I born, and for this came I into the world, that I should bear witness [55] of the truth.   <a name="fr_3459"></a><a href="#fn_3459"><font size="1">3460</font></a>
 And every one that is of the truth heareth my voice.  Pilate said unto him, And what is the truth?  And when he said that, he went out again unto the Jews.</p>
