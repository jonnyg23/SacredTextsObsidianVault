 <p> <a name="iv.iii.lv"></a>
 </p><p class="c27"><a name="iv.iii.lv-p1"></a> <span class="c6"><a name="iv.iii.lv-p1.1"></a>Section LV.</span></p> 
 <p class="c12"><a name="iv.iii.lv-p2"></a>[1]  <a name="fr_3847"></a><a href="#fn_3847"><font size="1">3848</font></a>
 But the eleven disciples went into Galilee, to the mountain <a name="fr_3848"></a><a href="#fn_3848"><font size="1">3849</font></a>
  where Jesus had [2] appointed them.   <a name="fr_3849"></a><a href="#fn_3849"><font size="1">3850</font></a>
 And when they saw him, they worshipped him:  but there were of [3] them who doubted.   <a name="fr_3850"></a><a href="#fn_3850"><font size="1">3851</font></a>
 And while they sat there he appeared to them again, and upbraided <i>them</i> for their lack of faith and the hardness of their hearts, those that saw him when he was risen, and believed not. <a name="fr_3851"></a><a href="#fn_3851"><font size="1">3852</font></a>
 </p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.lv-p8"></a>[4] [Arabic, p. 209]  <a name="fr_3852"></a><a href="#fn_3852"><font size="1">3853</font></a>
 Then said Jesus unto them, I have been given all authority in heaven [5] and earth; and as my Father hath sent me, so I also send you.   <a name="fr_3853"></a><a href="#fn_3853"><font size="1">3854</font></a>
 Go now into [6] all the world, and preach my gospel in all the creation;  <a name="fr_3854"></a><a href="#fn_3854"><font size="1">3855</font></a>
 and teach <a name="fr_3855"></a><a href="#fn_3855"><font size="1">3856</font></a>
  all the peoples, and [7] baptize them in the name of the Father and the Son and the Holy Spirit;  <a name="fr_3856"></a><a href="#fn_3856"><font size="1">3857</font></a>
 and teach them to keep all whatsoever I commanded you:  and lo, I am with you all the days, unto [8] the end of the world.   <a name="fr_3857"></a><a href="#fn_3857"><font size="1">3858</font></a>
 For whosoever believeth and is baptized shall be saved; but [9] whosoever believeth not shall be rejected.   <a name="fr_3858"></a><a href="#fn_3858"><font size="1">3859</font></a>
 And the signs <a name="fr_3859"></a><a href="#fn_3859"><font size="1">3860</font></a>
  which shall attend those that believe in me are these:  that they shall cast out devils in my name; and they [10] shall speak with new tongues;  <a name="fr_3860"></a><a href="#fn_3860"><font size="1">3861</font></a>
 and they shall take <i>up</i> serpents, and if they drink <a name="page_129"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 129</font></a> <a name="iv.iii.lv-Page_129"></a>deadly poison, <a name="fr_3861"></a><a href="#fn_3861"><font size="1">3862</font></a>
  it shall not injure them; and they shall lay their hands on the diseased, [11] and they shall be healed.   <a name="fr_3862"></a><a href="#fn_3862"><font size="1">3863</font></a>
 But ye, abide in the city of Jerusalem, until ye be clothed with power from on high.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.lv-p20"></a>[12]  <a name="fr_3863"></a><a href="#fn_3863"><font size="1">3864</font></a>
 And our Lord Jesus, after speaking to them, took them out to Bethany:  and he [13] lifted up his hands, and blessed them.   <a name="fr_3864"></a><a href="#fn_3864"><font size="1">3865</font></a>
 And while he blessed them, he was separated from them, and ascended into heaven, and sat down at the right hand of God.  [14, 15]  <a name="fr_3865"></a><a href="#fn_3865"><font size="1">3866</font></a>
 And they worshipped him, and returned to Jerusalem with great joy:   <a name="fr_3866"></a><a href="#fn_3866"><font size="1">3867</font></a>
 and at all times they were in the temple, praising and blessing God.  Amen.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.lv-p25"></a>[16]  <a name="fr_3867"></a><a href="#fn_3867"><font size="1">3868</font></a>
 And from thence they went forth, and preached in every place; and our Lord helped them, and confirmed their sayings by the signs which they did. <a name="fr_3868"></a><a href="#fn_3868"><font size="1">3869</font></a>
 </p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.lv-p28"></a>[17]  <a name="fr_3869"></a><a href="#fn_3869"><font size="1">3870</font></a>
 And here are also many other things which Jesus did, which if they were written every one of them, not even the world, according to my opinion, would contain the books which should be written. <a name="fr_3870"></a><a href="#fn_3870"><font size="1">3871</font></a>
 </p>
