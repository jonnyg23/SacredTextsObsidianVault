 <p> <a name="iv.iii.v"></a>
 </p><p class="c27"><a name="iv.iii.v-p1"></a> <span class="c6"><a name="iv.iii.v-p1.1"></a>Section V.</span></p> 
 <p class="c12"><a name="iv.iii.v-p2"></a>[1]  <a name="fr_375"></a><a href="#fn_375"><font size="1">376</font></a>
 Jesus answered and said unto him, Get thee hence, Satan:  for it is written, Thou [2] shalt worship the Lord thy God, and him alone shalt thou serve.   <a name="fr_376"></a><a href="#fn_376"><font size="1">377</font></a>
 And when the [3] devil <a name="fr_377"></a><a href="#fn_377"><font size="1">378</font></a>
  had completed all his temptations, he departed from him for a season.   <a name="fr_378"></a><a href="#fn_378"><font size="1">379</font></a>
 And behold, the angels drew near and ministered unto him.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.v-p7"></a>[4, 5]  <a name="fr_379"></a><a href="#fn_379"><font size="1">380</font></a>
 And next day John was standing, and two of his disciples;  <a name="fr_380"></a><a href="#fn_380"><font size="1">381</font></a>
 and he saw Jesus as [6] he was walking, and said, Behold, the Lamb of God!   <a name="fr_381"></a><a href="#fn_381"><font size="1">382</font></a>
 And his two disciples heard [7] him saying <i>this</i>, <a name="fr_382"></a><a href="#fn_382"><font size="1">383</font></a>
  and they followed Jesus.   <a name="fr_383"></a><a href="#fn_383"><font size="1">384</font></a>
 And Jesus turned and saw them coming after him, and said unto them, What seek ye?  They said unto him, Our master, [8] where art thou staying?   <a name="fr_384"></a><a href="#fn_384"><font size="1">385</font></a>
 And he said unto them, Come and see.  And they came and saw his place, and abode with him that day:  and it was about the tenth hour.  [9]  <a name="fr_385"></a><a href="#fn_385"><font size="1">386</font></a>
 One of the two which heard from <a name="fr_386"></a><a href="#fn_386"><font size="1">387</font></a>
  John, and followed Jesus, was Andrew the [10] brother of Simon.   <a name="fr_387"></a><a href="#fn_387"><font size="1">388</font></a>
 And he saw first Simon his brother, and said unto him, We have [11] found the Messiah.   <a name="fr_388"></a><a href="#fn_388"><font size="1">389</font></a>
 And he brought him unto Jesus.  And Jesus looked upon him and said, Thou art Simon, son of Jonah:  thou shalt be called Cephas. <a name="fr_389"></a><a href="#fn_389"><font size="1">390</font></a>
 </p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.v-p19"></a>[12]  <a name="fr_390"></a><a href="#fn_390"><font size="1">391</font></a>
 And on the next day Jesus desired to go forth to Galilee, and he found Philip, [13] [Arabic, p. 19] and said unto him, Follow me.   <a name="fr_391"></a><a href="#fn_391"><font size="1">392</font></a>
 Now Philip was of Bethsaida, of the city [14] of Andrew and Simon.   <a name="fr_392"></a><a href="#fn_392"><font size="1">393</font></a>
 And Philip found Nathanael, and said unto him, He of whom Moses did write in the law and in the prophets, we have found that [15] he is Jesus the son of Joseph of Nazareth.   <a name="fr_393"></a><a href="#fn_393"><font size="1">394</font></a>
 Nathanael said unto him, Is it possible <a name="page_51"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 51</font></a> <a name="iv.iii.v-Page_51"></a>that there can be any good thing from Nazareth?  Philip said unto him, Come and [16] see.   <a name="fr_394"></a><a href="#fn_394"><font size="1">395</font></a>
 And Jesus saw Nathanael coming to him, and said of him, This is indeed a <a name="fr_395"></a><a href="#fn_395"><font size="1">396</font></a>
  [17] son of Israel in whom is no guile.   <a name="fr_396"></a><a href="#fn_396"><font size="1">397</font></a>
 And Nathanael said unto him, Whence knowest thou me?  Jesus said unto him, Before Philip called thee, while thou wast under the [18] fig tree, I saw thee.   <a name="fr_397"></a><a href="#fn_397"><font size="1">398</font></a>
 Nathanael answered and said unto him, My Master, thou art [19] the Son of God; thou art the King of Israel.   <a name="fr_398"></a><a href="#fn_398"><font size="1">399</font></a>
 Jesus said unto him, Because I said unto thee, I saw thee under the fig tree, hast thou believed? thou shalt see what is [20] greater than this.   <a name="fr_399"></a><a href="#fn_399"><font size="1">400</font></a>
 And he said unto him, Verily, verily, I say unto you, Henceforth ye shall see the heavens opened, and the angels of God ascending and descending upon the Son of man.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.v-p30"></a>[21]  <a name="fr_400"></a><a href="#fn_400"><font size="1">401</font></a>
 And Jesus returned in the power of the Spirit to Galilee.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.v-p32"></a>[22]  <a name="fr_401"></a><a href="#fn_401"><font size="1">402</font></a>
 And on the third day there was a feast in Cana, <a name="fr_402"></a><a href="#fn_402"><font size="1">403</font></a>
  a <a name="fr_403"></a><a href="#fn_403"><font size="1">404</font></a>
  city of Galilee; and the [23] mother of Jesus was there:   <a name="fr_404"></a><a href="#fn_404"><font size="1">405</font></a>
 and Jesus also and his disciples were invited to the [24] feast.   <a name="fr_405"></a><a href="#fn_405"><font size="1">406</font></a>
 And they lacked wine:  and his mother said unto Jesus, They have no wine.  [25]  <a name="fr_406"></a><a href="#fn_406"><font size="1">407</font></a>
 And Jesus said unto her, What have I to do with thee, woman? hath not mine [26] hour come? <a name="fr_407"></a><a href="#fn_407"><font size="1">408</font></a>
    <a name="fr_408"></a><a href="#fn_408"><font size="1">409</font></a>
 And his mother said unto the servants, What he saith unto you, do.  [27]  <a name="fr_409"></a><a href="#fn_409"><font size="1">410</font></a>
 And there were there six vessels of stone, placed for the Jews purification, such as [28] [Arabic, p. 20] would contain two or three jars.   <a name="fr_410"></a><a href="#fn_410"><font size="1">411</font></a>
 And Jesus said unto them, Fill the vessels [29] with water.  And they filled them to the top.   <a name="fr_411"></a><a href="#fn_411"><font size="1">412</font></a>
 He said unto them, Draw [30] out now, and present to the ruler of the feast. And they did <i>so</i>.   <a name="fr_412"></a><a href="#fn_412"><font size="1">413</font></a>
 And when the ruler of the company tasted that water which had become wine, and knew not whence it was (but the servants knew, because they filled up the water), the ruler of the company called [31] the bridegroom,  <a name="fr_413"></a><a href="#fn_413"><font size="1">414</font></a>
 and said unto him, Every man presenteth first the good wine, and on intoxication he bringeth what is poor; but thou hast kept the good wine until [32] now.   <a name="fr_414"></a><a href="#fn_414"><font size="1">415</font></a>
 And this is the first sign <a name="fr_415"></a><a href="#fn_415"><font size="1">416</font></a>
  which Jesus did in Cana of Galilee, and manifested [33] his glory; and his disciples believed on him.   <a name="fr_416"></a><a href="#fn_416"><font size="1">417</font></a>
 And his fame spread in all the country [34] which was around them.   <a name="fr_417"></a><a href="#fn_417"><font size="1">418</font></a>
 And he taught in their synagogues, and was glorified [35] by <a name="fr_418"></a><a href="#fn_418"><font size="1">419</font></a>
  every man.   <a name="fr_419"></a><a href="#fn_419"><font size="1">420</font></a>
 And he came to Nazareth, where he had been brought up, and entered, according to his custom, into the synagogue on the sabbath day, and stood [36] up to read.   <a name="fr_420"></a><a href="#fn_420"><font size="1">421</font></a>
 And he was given the book of Isaiah the prophet.  And Jesus opened the book and found the place where it was written,</p> 
 <p class="c28"><a name="iv.iii.v-p53"></a>[37]  <a name="fr_421"></a><a href="#fn_421"><font size="1">422</font></a>
 The Spirit of the Lord is upon me,</p> 
 <p class="c29"><a name="iv.iii.v-p55"></a>And for this anointed he me, to preach good tidings to the poor;</p> 
 <p class="c29"><a name="iv.iii.v-p56"></a>And he hath sent me to heal the broken-hearted,</p> 
 <p class="c29"><a name="iv.iii.v-p57"></a>And to proclaim forgiveness to the evil-doers, <a name="fr_422"></a><a href="#fn_422"><font size="1">423</font></a>
  and sight to the blind,</p> 
 <p class="c29"><a name="iv.iii.v-p59"></a>And to bring the broken into forgiveness, <a name="fr_423"></a><a href="#fn_423"><font size="1">424</font></a>
 </p> 
 <p class="c30"><a name="iv.iii.v-p61"></a>[38]  <a name="fr_424"></a><a href="#fn_424"><font size="1">425</font></a>
 And to proclaim an acceptable year of the Lord.</p> 
 <p><a name="iv.iii.v-p63"></a>[39]  <a name="fr_425"></a><a href="#fn_425"><font size="1">426</font></a>
 And he rolled up the book and gave it to the servant, and went and sat down:  [40] and the eyes of all that were in the synagogue were observing him.   <a name="fr_426"></a><a href="#fn_426"><font size="1">427</font></a>
 And he began to say unto them, To-day hath this scripture been fulfilled which ye have heard with [41] your ears.   <a name="fr_427"></a><a href="#fn_427"><font size="1">428</font></a>
 And they all bare him witness, and wondered at the words of grace which were proceeding from his mouth.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.v-p67"></a>[42] [Arabic, p. 21]  <a name="fr_428"></a><a href="#fn_428"><font size="1">429</font></a>
 And from that time began Jesus to proclaim the gospel of the kingdom [43] of God, and to say, Repent ye, and believe in the gospel.   <a name="fr_429"></a><a href="#fn_429"><font size="1">430</font></a>
 The time is fulfilled, and the kingdom of heaven hath come near.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.v-p70"></a>[44]  <a name="fr_430"></a><a href="#fn_430"><font size="1">431</font></a>
 And while he was walking on the shore of the sea of Galilee, he saw two brethren, Simon who was called Cephas, and Andrew his brother, casting their nets into [45] the sea; for they were fishers.   <a name="fr_431"></a><a href="#fn_431"><font size="1">432</font></a>
 And Jesus said unto them, Follow me, and I will [46] make you fishers of men.   <a name="fr_432"></a><a href="#fn_432"><font size="1">433</font></a>
 And they immediately left their nets there and followed [47] him.   <a name="fr_433"></a><a href="#fn_433"><font size="1">434</font></a>
 And when he went on from thence, he saw other two brothers, James the son of Zebedee, and John his brother, in the ship with Zebedee their father, mending [48] their nets; and Jesus called them.   <a name="fr_434"></a><a href="#fn_434"><font size="1">435</font></a>
 And they immediately forsook the ship and their father Zebedee, and followed him.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.v-p76"></a><a name="page_52"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 52</font></a> <a name="iv.iii.v-Page_52"></a>[49]  <a name="fr_435"></a><a href="#fn_435"><font size="1">436</font></a>
 And when the multitude gathered unto him to hear the word of God, while he [50] was standing on the shore of the sea of Gennesaret,  <a name="fr_436"></a><a href="#fn_436"><font size="1">437</font></a>
 he saw two boats standing beside the sea, while <a name="fr_437"></a><a href="#fn_437"><font size="1">438</font></a>
  the two fishers which were gone out of them were washing their [51] nets.   <a name="fr_438"></a><a href="#fn_438"><font size="1">439</font></a>
 And one of them belonged to Simon Cephas.  And Jesus went up and sat down in it, and commanded that they should move away a little from the land into [52] the water.  And he sat down and taught the multitudes from the boat.   <a name="fr_439"></a><a href="#fn_439"><font size="1">440</font></a>
 And when he had left off his speaking, he said unto Simon, Put out into the deep, and cast your [53] net for a draught.   <a name="fr_440"></a><a href="#fn_440"><font size="1">441</font></a>
 And Simon answered and said unto him, My Master, we toiled [54] all night and caught nothing; now <a name="fr_441"></a><a href="#fn_441"><font size="1">442</font></a>
  at thy word I will cast the net.   <a name="fr_442"></a><a href="#fn_442"><font size="1">443</font></a>
 And when they did this, there were enclosed <a name="fr_443"></a><a href="#fn_443"><font size="1">444</font></a>
  a great many fishes; and their net was on the [55] point of breaking.   <a name="fr_444"></a><a href="#fn_444"><font size="1">445</font></a>
 And they beckoned to their comrades that were in the other boat, to come and help them.  And when they came, they filled both boats, so that they were on the point of sinking.</p>
