 </p><p class="c35"><a name="xii.iv.xviii-p1"></a> <span class="c2"><a name="xii.iv.xviii-p1.1"></a>Chapter XVIII.—David as an Example of Humility.</span></p> 
 <p class="c12"><a name="xii.iv.xviii-p2"></a>But what shall we say concerning David, to whom such testimony was borne, and of whom <a name="fr_4086"></a><a href="#fn_4086"><font size="1">4087</font></a>
  God said, “I have found a man after mine own heart, David the son of Jesse; and in everlasting mercy have I anointed him?” <a name="fr_4087"></a><a href="#fn_4087"><font size="1">4088</font></a>
   Yet this very man saith to God, “Have mercy on me, O Lord, according to Thy great mercy; and according to the multitude of Thy compassions, blot out my transgression. <a name="fr_4088"></a><a href="#fn_4088"><font size="1">4089</font></a>
   Wash me still more from mine iniquity, and cleanse me from my sin.  For I acknowledge mine iniquity, and my sin is ever before me.  Against Thee only have I sinned, and done that which is evil in Thy sight; that Thou mayest be justified in Thy sayings, and mayest overcome when Thou <a name="fr_4089"></a><a href="#fn_4089"><font size="1">4090</font></a>
  art judged.  For, behold, I was conceived in transgressions, and in sins did my mother conceive me.  For, behold, Thou hast loved truth; the secret and hidden things of wisdom hast Thou shown me.  Thou shalt sprinkle me with hyssop, and I shall be cleansed; Thou shalt wash me, and I shall be whiter than snow.  Thou shalt make me to hear joy and gladness; my bones, which have been humbled, shall exult.  Turn away Thy face from my sins, and blot out all mine iniquities.  Create in me a clean heart, O God, and <a name="page_235"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 235</font></a> <a name="xii.iv.xviii-Page_235"></a>renew a right spirit within me. <a name="fr_4090"></a><a href="#fn_4090"><font size="1">4091</font></a>
   Cast me not away from Thy presence, and take not Thy Holy Spirit from me.  Restore to me the joy of Thy salvation, and establish me by Thy governing Spirit.  I will teach transgressors Thy ways, and the ungodly shall be converted unto Thee.  Deliver me from blood-guiltiness, <a name="fr_4091"></a><a href="#fn_4091"><font size="1">4092</font></a>
  O God, the God of my salvation:  my tongue shall exult in Thy righteousness.  O Lord, Thou shalt open my mouth, and my lips shall show forth Thy praise.  For if Thou hadst desired sacrifice, I would have given it; Thou wilt not delight in burnt-offerings.  The sacrifice [acceptable] to God is a bruised spirit; a broken and a contrite heart God will not despise.” <a name="fr_4092"></a><a href="#fn_4092"><font size="1">4093</font></a>
 </p>
