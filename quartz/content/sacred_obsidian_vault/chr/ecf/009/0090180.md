 </p><p class="c35"><a name="xii.vi.xviii-p1"></a> <span class="c2"><a name="xii.vi.xviii-p1.1"></a>Chapter XVIII.—The Author Sinful, Yet Pursuing.</span></p> 
 <p class="c12"><a name="xii.vi.xviii-p2"></a>And let us, then, be of the number of those who give thanks, who have served God, and not of the ungodly who are judged.  For I myself, though a sinner every whit and not yet fleeing temptation but continuing in the midst of the tools of the devil, study to follow after righteousness, that I may make, be it only some, approach to it, fearing the judgment to come.</p>
