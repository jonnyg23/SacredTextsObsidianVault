 <p> <a name="iv.iii.iii"></a>
 </p><p class="c27"><a name="iv.iii.iii-p1"></a> <span class="c6"><a name="iv.iii.iii-p1.1"></a>Section III.</span></p> 
 <p class="c12"><a name="iv.iii.iii-p2"></a>[1, 2]  <a name="fr_233"></a><a href="#fn_233"><font size="1">234</font></a>
 And after that, <a name="fr_234"></a><a href="#fn_234"><font size="1">235</font></a>
  the Magi came from the east to Jerusalem,  <a name="fr_235"></a><a href="#fn_235"><font size="1">236</font></a>
 and said, Where is the King of the Jews which was born?  We have seen his star in the east, and have [3] come to worship him.   <a name="fr_236"></a><a href="#fn_236"><font size="1">237</font></a>
 And Herod the king heard, and he was troubled, and all [4] Jerusalem with him.   <a name="fr_237"></a><a href="#fn_237"><font size="1">238</font></a>
 And he gathered all the chief priests and the scribes of the [5] people, and asked them in what place <a name="fr_238"></a><a href="#fn_238"><font size="1">239</font></a>
  the Messiah should be born.   <a name="fr_239"></a><a href="#fn_239"><font size="1">240</font></a>
 They said, In Bethlehem of Judæa:  thus it is written in the prophet,</p> 
 <p class="c28"><a name="iv.iii.iii-p10"></a>[6]  <a name="fr_240"></a><a href="#fn_240"><font size="1">241</font></a>
 Thou also, Bethlehem of Judah,</p> 
 <p class="c29"><a name="iv.iii.iii-p12"></a>Art not contemptible among the kings of Judah:</p> 
 <p class="c29"><a name="iv.iii.iii-p13"></a>From thee shall go forth a king,</p> 
 <p class="c21"><a name="iv.iii.iii-p14"></a>And he shall be a shepherd to my people Israel.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.iii-p15"></a>[7]  <a name="fr_241"></a><a href="#fn_241"><font size="1">242</font></a>
 Then Herod called the Magi secretly, and inquired of them the time at which [8] the star appeared to them.   <a name="fr_242"></a><a href="#fn_242"><font size="1">243</font></a>
 And he sent them to Bethlehem, and said unto them, Go and search about the child diligently; and when ye have found him, come and [9] make known to me, that I also may go and worship him.   <a name="fr_243"></a><a href="#fn_243"><font size="1">244</font></a>
 And they, when they [Arabic, p. 11] heard the king, departed; and lo, the star which they had seen in the east went before them, until it came and stood above the place where the child [10, 11] was.   <a name="fr_244"></a><a href="#fn_244"><font size="1">245</font></a>
 And when they beheld the star, they rejoiced with very great joy.   <a name="fr_245"></a><a href="#fn_245"><font size="1">246</font></a>
 And they entered the house and beheld the child with Mary his mother, and fell down worshipping him, and opened their saddle-bags and offered to him offerings, gold and [12] myrrh and frankincense.   <a name="fr_246"></a><a href="#fn_246"><font size="1">247</font></a>
 And they saw in a dream <a name="fr_247"></a><a href="#fn_247"><font size="1">248</font></a>
  that they should not return to Herod, and they travelled by another way in going to their country.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.iii-p23"></a>[13]  <a name="fr_248"></a><a href="#fn_248"><font size="1">249</font></a>
 And when they had departed, the angel of the Lord appeared in a dream to Joseph, and said unto him, Rise, take the child and his mother, and flee into Egypt, and be thou there until I speak to thee; for Herod is determined to seek the child [14] to slay him.   <a name="fr_249"></a><a href="#fn_249"><font size="1">250</font></a>
 And Joseph arose and took the child and his mother in the night, and [15] fled into Egypt,  <a name="fr_250"></a><a href="#fn_250"><font size="1">251</font></a>
 and remained in it until the time of the death of Herod:  that that might be fulfilled which was said by the Lord in the prophet, which said, From [16] Egypt did I call my son.   <a name="fr_251"></a><a href="#fn_251"><font size="1">252</font></a>
 And Herod then, when he saw that he was mocked of the Magi, was very angry, and sent and killed all the male children which were in Bethlehem and all its borders, from two years old and under, according to the time [17] which he had inquired from the Magi.   <a name="fr_252"></a><a href="#fn_252"><font size="1">253</font></a>
 Then was fulfilled the saying in Jeremiah the prophet, which said,</p> 
 <p class="c28"><a name="iv.iii.iii-p29"></a>[18]  <a name="fr_253"></a><a href="#fn_253"><font size="1">254</font></a>
 A voice was heard in Ramah,</p> 
 <p class="c29"><a name="iv.iii.iii-p31"></a>Weeping and much lamentation;</p> 
 <p class="c29"><a name="iv.iii.iii-p32"></a>Rachel weeping <a name="fr_254"></a><a href="#fn_254"><font size="1">255</font></a>
  for her children,</p> 
 <p class="c21"><a name="iv.iii.iii-p34"></a>And not willing to be consoled for their loss.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.iii-p35"></a>[19]  <a name="fr_255"></a><a href="#fn_255"><font size="1">256</font></a>
 But when Herod the king died, the angel of the Lord appeared in a dream to [20] Joseph in Egypt,  <a name="fr_256"></a><a href="#fn_256"><font size="1">257</font></a>
 and said unto him, Rise and take the child and his mother, and [Arabic, p. 12] go into the land of Israel; for they have died who sought the childs life.  [21]  <a name="fr_257"></a><a href="#fn_257"><font size="1">258</font></a>
 And Joseph rose and took the child and his mother, and came to the land [22] of Israel.   <a name="fr_258"></a><a href="#fn_258"><font size="1">259</font></a>
 But when he heard that Archelaus had become king over Judæa instead of Herod his father, he feared to go thither; and he saw in a dream that he should <a name="page_48"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 48</font></a> <a name="iv.iii.iii-Page_48"></a>[23] go into the land of Galilee,  <a name="fr_259"></a><a href="#fn_259"><font size="1">260</font></a>
 and that he should abide in a city called Nazareth:  that the saying in the prophet might be fulfilled, that he should be called a Nazarene.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.iii-p41"></a>[24]  <a name="fr_260"></a><a href="#fn_260"><font size="1">261</font></a>
 And the child grew, and became strong in spirit, becoming filled with wisdom; and the grace of God was upon him.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.iii-p43"></a>[25]  <a name="fr_261"></a><a href="#fn_261"><font size="1">262</font></a>
 And his kinsfolk <a name="fr_262"></a><a href="#fn_262"><font size="1">263</font></a>
  used to go every year to Jerusalem at the feast of the passover.  [26]  <a name="fr_263"></a><a href="#fn_263"><font size="1">264</font></a>
 And when he was twelve years old, they went up according to their custom, [27] to the feast.   <a name="fr_264"></a><a href="#fn_264"><font size="1">265</font></a>
 And when the days were accomplished, they returned; and the child [28] Jesus remained in Jerusalem, and Joseph and his mother knew not:   <a name="fr_265"></a><a href="#fn_265"><font size="1">266</font></a>
 and they supposed that he was with the children of their company.  And when they had gone one days journey, they sought him beside their people and those who knew them, [29] and they found him not;  <a name="fr_266"></a><a href="#fn_266"><font size="1">267</font></a>
 so they returned to Jerusalem and sought him again.  [30]  <a name="fr_267"></a><a href="#fn_267"><font size="1">268</font></a>
 And after three days they found him in the temple, sitting in the midst of the teachers, [31] hearing them and asking them <i>questions</i>;  <a name="fr_268"></a><a href="#fn_268"><font size="1">269</font></a>
 and all who heard him wondered at [32] his wisdom and his words.   <a name="fr_269"></a><a href="#fn_269"><font size="1">270</font></a>
 And when they saw him they wondered, and his mother said unto him, My son, why hast thou dealt with us thus? behold, I and thy father [33] have been seeking for thee with much anxiety.   <a name="fr_270"></a><a href="#fn_270"><font size="1">271</font></a>
 And he said unto them, Why were [34] ye seeking me? know <a name="fr_271"></a><a href="#fn_271"><font size="1">272</font></a>
  ye not that I must be in the house of my Father?   <a name="fr_272"></a><a href="#fn_272"><font size="1">273</font></a>
 And they [35] understood not the word which he spake unto them.   <a name="fr_273"></a><a href="#fn_273"><font size="1">274</font></a>
 And he went down with them, and came to Nazareth; and he was obedient to them:  and his mother used to keep all these sayings in her heart.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.iii-p57"></a>[36] [Arabic, p. 13]  <a name="fr_274"></a><a href="#fn_274"><font size="1">275</font></a>
 And Jesus grew in his stature and wisdom, and in grace with God and men.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.iii-p59"></a>[37]  <a name="fr_275"></a><a href="#fn_275"><font size="1">276</font></a>
 And in the fifteenth year of the reign of Tiberius Cæsar, when Pontius Pilate was governor in Judæa, and one of the four rulers, Herod, in Galilee; and Philip his brother, one of the four rulers, in Ituræa and in the district of Trachonitis; and [38] Lysanias, one of the four rulers, in Abilene;  <a name="fr_276"></a><a href="#fn_276"><font size="1">277</font></a>
 in the chief-priesthood of Annas and Caiaphas, the command <a name="fr_277"></a><a href="#fn_277"><font size="1">278</font></a>
  of God went forth to John the son of Zacharias in the [39] desert.   <a name="fr_278"></a><a href="#fn_278"><font size="1">279</font></a>
 And he came into all the region which is about Jordan, proclaiming the [40] baptism of repentance unto <a name="fr_279"></a><a href="#fn_279"><font size="1">280</font></a>
  the forgiveness of sins.   <a name="fr_280"></a><a href="#fn_280"><font size="1">281</font></a>
 And he was preaching in the [41] wilderness of Judæa, and saying,  <a name="fr_281"></a><a href="#fn_281"><font size="1">282</font></a>
 Repent ye; the kingdom of heaven is come near.  [42]  <a name="fr_282"></a><a href="#fn_282"><font size="1">283</font></a>
 This is he that was spoken <i>of</i> in Isaiah the prophet,</p> 
 <p class="c28"><a name="iv.iii.iii-p68"></a>The voice which crieth in the desert,</p> 
 <p class="c29"><a name="iv.iii.iii-p69"></a>[43]  <a name="fr_283"></a><a href="#fn_283"><font size="1">284</font></a>
 Prepare ye the way of the Lord,</p> 
 <p class="c29"><a name="iv.iii.iii-p71"></a>And make straight in the plain, paths for our God.</p> 
 <p class="c29"><a name="iv.iii.iii-p72"></a>[44]  <a name="fr_284"></a><a href="#fn_284"><font size="1">285</font></a>
 All the valleys shall become filled,</p> 
 <p class="c29"><a name="iv.iii.iii-p74"></a>And all the mountains and hills shall become low;</p> 
 <p class="c29"><a name="iv.iii.iii-p75"></a>And the rough shall become plain, And the difficult place, easy;</p> 
 <p class="c21"><a name="iv.iii.iii-p76"></a>[45]  <a name="fr_285"></a><a href="#fn_285"><font size="1">286</font></a>
 And all flesh shall see the salvation <a name="fr_286"></a><a href="#fn_286"><font size="1">287</font></a>
  of God.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.iii-p79"></a>[46]  <a name="fr_287"></a><a href="#fn_287"><font size="1">288</font></a>
 This <i>man</i> came to bear witness, that he might bear witness to the light, that [47] every man might believe through his mediation.   <a name="fr_288"></a><a href="#fn_288"><font size="1">289</font></a>
 He was not the light, but that he [48] might bear witness to the light,  <a name="fr_289"></a><a href="#fn_289"><font size="1">290</font></a>
 which was the light of truth, that giveth light to [49] every man coming into the world.   <a name="fr_290"></a><a href="#fn_290"><font size="1">291</font></a>
 He was in the world, and the world was made [50] by him, and the world knew [51] him not.   <a name="fr_291"></a><a href="#fn_291"><font size="1">292</font></a>
 He came unto his own, and his own received him not.   <a name="fr_292"></a><a href="#fn_292"><font size="1">293</font></a>
 And those who received him, to them gave he the power <a name="fr_293"></a><a href="#fn_293"><font size="1">294</font></a>
  that they might [52] be sons of God,—those which believe in his name:   <a name="fr_294"></a><a href="#fn_294"><font size="1">295</font></a>
 which were born, not of blood, [53] nor of the will of the flesh, nor of the will of a man, but of God.   <a name="fr_295"></a><a href="#fn_295"><font size="1">296</font></a>
 And the Word became flesh, and took up his abode among us; and we saw his glory as the glory [54] of the only <i>Son</i> from the Father, which is full of grace and equity. <a name="fr_296"></a><a href="#fn_296"><font size="1">297</font></a>
    <a name="fr_297"></a><a href="#fn_297"><font size="1">298</font></a>
 John bare witness [Arabic, p. 14] of him, and cried, and said, This is he that I said cometh after me and [55] was before me, because he was before me. <a name="fr_298"></a><a href="#fn_298"><font size="1">299</font></a>
    <a name="fr_299"></a><a href="#fn_299"><font size="1">300</font></a>
 And of his fullness received [56] we all grace for grace.   <a name="fr_300"></a><a href="#fn_300"><font size="1">301</font></a>
 For the law was given through the mediation of Moses, but truth and grace were <a name="fr_301"></a><a href="#fn_301"><font size="1">302</font></a>
  through Jesus Christ.</p>
