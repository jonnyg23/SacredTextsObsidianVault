 </p><p class="c35"><a name="xii.iv.v-p1"></a> <span class="c2"><a name="xii.iv.v-p1.1"></a>Chapter V.—No Less Evils Have Arisen from the Same Source in the Most Recent Times.  The Martyrdom of Peter and Paul.</span></p> 
 <p class="c12"><a name="xii.iv.v-p2"></a>But not to dwell upon ancient examples, let us come to the most recent spiritual heroes. <a name="fr_4022"></a><a href="#fn_4022"><font size="1">4023</font></a>
   Let us take the noble examples furnished in our own generation.  Through envy <a name="fr_4023"></a><a href="#fn_4023"><font size="1">4024</font></a>
  and jealousy the greatest and most righteous pillars [of the church] have been persecuted and put to death. <a name="fr_4024"></a><a href="#fn_4024"><font size="1">4025</font></a>
   Let us set before our eyes the illustrious <a name="fr_4025"></a><a href="#fn_4025"><font size="1">4026</font></a>
  apostles.  Peter, through unrighteous envy, endured not one or two, but numerous labours; and when he had at length suffered martyrdom, departed to the place of glory due to him.  Owing to envy, Paul also obtained <a name="fr_4026"></a><a href="#fn_4026"><font size="1">4027</font></a>
  the reward of patient endurance, after being seven times thrown into captivity, <a name="fr_4027"></a><a href="#fn_4027"><font size="1">4028</font></a>
  compelled <a name="fr_4028"></a><a href="#fn_4028"><font size="1">4029</font></a>
  to flee, and <a name="page_231"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 231</font></a> <a name="xii.iv.v-Page_231"></a>stoned.  After preaching both in the east and west, he gained the illustrious reputation due to his faith, having taught righteousness <a name="fr_4029"></a><a href="#fn_4029"><font size="1">4030</font></a>
  to the whole world, and come to the extreme limit of the west, <a name="fr_4030"></a><a href="#fn_4030"><font size="1">4031</font></a>
  and suffered martyrdom under the prefects. <a name="fr_4031"></a><a href="#fn_4031"><font size="1">4032</font></a>
   Thus was he removed from the world, and went into the holy place, having proved himself a striking example of patience.</p>
