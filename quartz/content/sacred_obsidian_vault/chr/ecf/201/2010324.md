 </p><p class="c18"><a name="iii.xvii.xvii-p1"></a>  On Bk. VI. chap. 12, § 6.</p>  
 <p class="c18"><a name="iii.xvii.xvii-p2"></a>Since this passage was printed, I have seen Westcotts translation of this fragment of Serapions epistle in his <i>Canon of the New Testament</i>, 5th ed. p. 390 sq. (cf. especially p. 391, note), and am glad to note that his rendering of the words <span class="Greek"><a name="iii.xvii.xvii-p2.1"></a>καταρξαμένων αὐτοῦ</span> is the same as my own. His interpretation of one or two other points I am unable to adopt.</p>
