 <p> <a name="iii.xvii.xxvi"></a>
 </p><p class="c17"><a name="iii.xvii.xxvi-p1"></a><a name="page_402"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 402</font></a> <a name="iii.xvii.xxvi-Page_402"></a><i>Bishops of Antioch.</i></p>  
 <p class="c80"><a name="iii.xvii.xxvi-p2"></a>(Dates taken from the table given by Harnack in his <i>Zeit des Ignatius</i>, p. 62.)</p>     
 <p><a name="iii.xvii.xxvi-p3"></a>Evodius.</p>   
 <p><a name="iii.xvii.xxvi-p4"></a>Zebinus, died between 238 and 249.</p>      
 <p><a name="iii.xvii.xxvi-p5"></a>Ignatius.</p>   
 <p><a name="iii.xvii.xxvi-p6"></a>Babylas, died in 250, during the persecution of Decius.</p>      
 <p><a name="iii.xvii.xxvi-p7"></a>Hero.</p>   
 <p><a name="iii.xvii.xxvi-p8"></a>Fabius, died toward the end of 252 or early in 253.</p>      
 <p><a name="iii.xvii.xxvi-p9"></a>Cornelius.</p>   
 <p><a name="iii.xvii.xxvi-p10"></a>Demetrian, died between 257 and 260.</p>      
 <p><a name="iii.xvii.xxvi-p11"></a>Eros.</p>   
 <p><a name="iii.xvii.xxvi-p12"></a>Paul, deposed between 266 and 269 (probably in 268).</p>      
 <p><a name="iii.xvii.xxvi-p13"></a>Theophilus, died not earlier than 182.</p>   
 <p><a name="iii.xvii.xxvi-p14"></a>Domnus.</p>      
 <p><a name="iii.xvii.xxvi-p15"></a>Maximinus, died between 189 and 192.</p>   
 <p><a name="iii.xvii.xxvi-p16"></a>Timæus, died about 280.</p>      
 <p><a name="iii.xvii.xxvi-p17"></a>Serapion, died about 209.</p>   
 <p><a name="iii.xvii.xxvi-p18"></a>Cyril, sent to the mines in 303, and died probably toward the end of 306.</p>      
 <p><a name="iii.xvii.xxvi-p19"></a>Asclepiades, died between 211 and 222.</p>   
 <p><a name="iii.xvii.xxvi-p20"></a>Tyrannus, succeeded Cyril probably in 303, possibly not until 306, and lived until the close of the persecution.</p>      
 <p><a name="iii.xvii.xxvi-p21"></a>Philetus, died not long before 229–231.</p>
