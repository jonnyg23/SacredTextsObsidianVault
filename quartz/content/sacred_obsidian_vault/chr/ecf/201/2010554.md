 </p><p class="c47"><a name="iv.vi.iii.xxxv-p1"></a>  <span class="c11"><a name="iv.vi.iii.xxxv-p1.1"></a>Chapter XXXV</span>.—<i>Description of the Atrium and Porticos.</i></p>  
 <p class="c18"><a name="iv.vi.iii.xxxv-p2"></a><span class="c11"><a name="iv.vi.iii.xxxv-p2.1"></a>The</span> next object of his attention was a space of ground of great extent, and open to the pure air of heaven. This he adorned with a pavement of finely polished stone, and enclosed it on three sides with porticos of great length.</p>
