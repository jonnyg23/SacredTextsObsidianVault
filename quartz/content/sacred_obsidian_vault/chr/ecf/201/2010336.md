 </p><p class="c17"><a name="iii.xvii.xxix-p1"></a>  <span class="c11"><a name="iii.xvii.xxix-p1.1"></a>Table of Macedonian Months</span></p>  
 <p class="c100"><a name="iii.xvii.xxix-p2"></a>The months of the Macedonian year, as commonly employed in the time of Eusebius, corresponded exactly to the Roman months, but the year began with the first of September. The names of the months were as follows:—</p>     
 <p class="c101"><a name="iii.xvii.xxix-p3"></a><span class="c11"><a name="iii.xvii.xxix-p3.1"></a>Macedonian.</span></p>   
 <p class="c101"><a name="iii.xvii.xxix-p4"></a><span class="c11"><a name="iii.xvii.xxix-p4.1"></a>Roman.</span></p>   
 <p class="c101"><a name="iii.xvii.xxix-p5"></a><span class="c11"><a name="iii.xvii.xxix-p5.1"></a>Macedonian.</span></p>   
 <p class="c101"><a name="iii.xvii.xxix-p6"></a><span class="c11"><a name="iii.xvii.xxix-p6.1"></a>Roman.</span></p>      
 <p><a name="iii.xvii.xxix-p7"></a>1. Gorpiæus.</p>   
 <p><a name="iii.xvii.xxix-p8"></a>September.</p>   
 <p><a name="iii.xvii.xxix-p9"></a>7. Dystrus.</p>   
 <p><a name="iii.xvii.xxix-p10"></a>March.</p>      
 <p><a name="iii.xvii.xxix-p11"></a>2. Hyperberetæus.</p>   
 <p><a name="iii.xvii.xxix-p12"></a>October.</p>   
 <p><a name="iii.xvii.xxix-p13"></a>8. Xanthicus.</p>   
 <p><a name="iii.xvii.xxix-p14"></a>April.</p>      
 <p><a name="iii.xvii.xxix-p15"></a>3. Dius.</p>   
 <p><a name="iii.xvii.xxix-p16"></a>November.</p>   
 <p><a name="iii.xvii.xxix-p17"></a>9. Artemisius.</p>   
 <p><a name="iii.xvii.xxix-p18"></a>May.</p>      
 <p><a name="iii.xvii.xxix-p19"></a>4. Apellæus.</p>   
 <p><a name="iii.xvii.xxix-p20"></a>December.</p>   
 <p><a name="iii.xvii.xxix-p21"></a>10. Dæsius.</p>   
 <p><a name="iii.xvii.xxix-p22"></a>June.</p>      
 <p><a name="iii.xvii.xxix-p23"></a>5. Audynæus.</p>   
 <p><a name="iii.xvii.xxix-p24"></a>January.</p>   
 <p><a name="iii.xvii.xxix-p25"></a>11. Panemus.</p>   
 <p><a name="iii.xvii.xxix-p26"></a>July.</p>      
 <p><a name="iii.xvii.xxix-p27"></a>6. Peritius.</p>   
 <p><a name="iii.xvii.xxix-p28"></a>February.</p>   
 <p><a name="iii.xvii.xxix-p29"></a>12. Loüs.</p>   
 <p><a name="iii.xvii.xxix-p30"></a>August.</p>
