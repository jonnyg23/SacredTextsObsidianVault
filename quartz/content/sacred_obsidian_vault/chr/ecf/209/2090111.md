 </p><p class="c33"><a name="iii.iv.iii.xxix-p1"></a> <span class="c12"><a name="iii.iv.iii.xxix-p1.1"></a>Chapter XXIX</span>.—<i>Concerning the Descent to Hades.</i></p> 
 <p class="c18"><a name="iii.iv.iii.xxix-p2"></a>The soul <a name="fr_2244"></a><a href="#fn_2244"><font size="1">2245</font></a>
  when it was deified descended into Hades, in order that, just as the Sun of Righteousness <a name="fr_2245"></a><a href="#fn_2245"><font size="1">2246</font></a>
  rose for those upon the earth, so likewise He might bring light to those who sit under the earth in darkness <a name="page_73b"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 73b</font></a> <a name="iii.iv.iii.xxix-Page_73b"></a>and shadow of death <a name="fr_2246"></a><a href="#fn_2246"><font size="1">2247</font></a>
 :  in order that just as He brought the message of peace to those upon the earth, and of release to the prisoners, and of sight to the blind <a name="fr_2247"></a><a href="#fn_2247"><font size="1">2248</font></a>
 , and became to those who believed the Author of everlasting salvation and to those who did not believe a reproach of their unbelief <a name="fr_2248"></a><a href="#fn_2248"><font size="1">2249</font></a>
 , so He might become the same to those in Hades <a name="fr_2249"></a><a href="#fn_2249"><font size="1">2250</font></a>
 :  <i>That every knee should bow to Him, of things in heaven, and things in earth and things under the earth</i> <a name="fr_2250"></a><a href="#fn_2250"><font size="1">2251</font></a>
 . And thus after He had freed those who had been bound for ages, straightway He rose again from the dead, shewing us the way of resurrection.</p>
