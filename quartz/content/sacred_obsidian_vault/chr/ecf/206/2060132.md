 <p> <a name="v.CXXI"></a>
 </p><p class="c30"><a name="v.CXXI-p1"></a>  <span class="c1"><a name="v.CXXI-p1.1"></a>Letter CXXI. To Algasia.</span></p>  
 <p class="c16"><a name="v.CXXI-p2"></a>Jerome writes to a lady of Gaul named Algasia to answer eleven questions which she had submitted to him. They were as follows:—</p>  
 <p><a name="v.CXXI-p3"></a>(1) How is <a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk007.htm#018">Luke 7:18</a>, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk007.htm#019">19</a><a name="v.CXXI-p3.1"></a>, to be reconciled with <a href="../../../bib/kjv/joh001.htm#036">John i. 36</a><a name="v.CXXI-p3.2"></a>?</p>  
 <p><a name="v.CXXI-p4"></a>(2) What is the meaning of <a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat012.htm#020">Matt. xii. 20</a><a name="v.CXXI-p4.1"></a>?</p>  
 <p><a name="v.CXXI-p5"></a>(3) And of <a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat016.htm#024">Matt. xvi. 24</a><a name="v.CXXI-p5.1"></a>?</p>  
 <p><a name="v.CXXI-p6"></a>(4) And of <a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat024.htm#019">Matt. 24:19</a>, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat024.htm#020">20</a><a name="v.CXXI-p6.1"></a>?</p>  
 <p><a name="v.CXXI-p7"></a>(5) And of <a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk009.htm#053">Luke ix. 53</a><a name="v.CXXI-p7.1"></a>?</p>  
 <p><a name="v.CXXI-p8"></a>(6) What is the meaning of the parable of the unjust steward?</p>  
 <p><a name="v.CXXI-p9"></a>(7) What is the meaning of <a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom005.htm#007">Rom. v. 7</a><a name="v.CXXI-p9.1"></a>?</p>  
 <p><a name="v.CXXI-p10"></a>(8) And of <a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom007.htm#008">Rom. vii. 8</a><a name="v.CXXI-p10.1"></a>?</p>  
 <p><a name="v.CXXI-p11"></a>(9) And of <a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom009.htm#003">Rom. ix. 3</a><a name="v.CXXI-p11.1"></a>?</p>  
 <p><a name="v.CXXI-p12"></a>(10) And of <a href="../../../bib/kjv/col002.htm#018">Col. ii. 18</a><a name="v.CXXI-p12.1"></a>?</p>  
 <p><a name="v.CXXI-p13"></a>(11) And of <a href="../../../bib/kjv/th2002.htm#003">2 Thes. ii. 3</a><a name="v.CXXI-p13.1"></a>?</p>  
 <p><a name="v.CXXI-p14"></a>The date of the letter is 406 <span class="c17"><a name="v.CXXI-p14.1"></a>a.d.</span></p>
