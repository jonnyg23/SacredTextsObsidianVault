 </p><p class="c26"><a name="vii.iv.vii-p1"></a>  <span class="c1"><a name="vii.iv.vii-p1.1"></a>Titus.</span></p>  
 <p class="c16"><a name="vii.iv.vii-p2"></a><i>Addressed to Paula and Eustochium, <span class="c17"><a name="vii.iv.vii-p2.1"></a>a.d.</span> 387. The Preface speaks of the rejection of the Epistle by Marcion and Basilides, its acceptance by Tatius, but without assigning reasons. It ought, Jerome says, to be of special interest to Paula and Eustochium, as being written from Nicopolis, near Actium, where their property lay.</i></p>
