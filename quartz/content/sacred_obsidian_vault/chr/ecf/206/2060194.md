 <p class="c16"><a name="vii.iii.v-p2"></a><i>This Preface is almost wholly a repetition of the arguments adduced in the Preface to Genesis. It is addressed to Chromatius, bishop of Aquileia, who took great interest in the work and provided funds for its continuance. The date is <span class="c17"><a name="vii.iii.v-p2.1"></a>a.d.</span> 395.</i></p>
