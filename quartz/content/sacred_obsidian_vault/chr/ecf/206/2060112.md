 </p><p class="c30"><a name="v.CI-p1"></a>  <span class="c1"><a name="v.CI-p1.1"></a>Letter CI. From Augustine.</span></p>  
 <p class="c16"><a name="v.CI-p2"></a>A letter from Augustine in which he denies that he has written a book against Jerome and sent it to Rome but confesses that he has criticized him although without giving details. Written in 402 <span class="c17"><a name="v.CI-p2.1"></a>a.d.</span> This and the following letters are to be found in the First Volume of the First Series of this Library. Letter LXVII.</p>
