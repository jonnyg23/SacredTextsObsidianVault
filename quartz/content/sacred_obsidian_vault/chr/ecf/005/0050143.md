    <h2 align="CENTER"><a></a> A Roman Council Celebrated Under Stephen.</h2>    <ul>
 <p><a></a> From the Synodal Roll.</p>    </ul>
 <p>A Divine and sacred provincial synod, gathered together at Rome by Stephen, the blessed martyr and <i>father</i><sup><a href="footnote/fn105.htm#P10562_3333453">1</a></sup> which excommunicated those who in an African synod had, without reason, conceded that they who came to the Catholic Church from any heresy should be re-baptized.<sup><a href="footnote/fn105.htm#P10563_3333673">2</a></sup> </p>
