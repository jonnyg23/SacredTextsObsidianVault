 <p> <a name="v.iii.xxxvi"></a>
 </p><p class="c30"><a name="v.iii.xxxvi-p1"></a> <span class="c1"><a name="v.iii.xxxvi-p1.1"></a>Chapter XXXVI.—The Apostolic Churches the Voice of the Apostles. Let the Heretics Examine Their Apostolic Claims, in Each Case, Indisputable. The Church of Rome Doubly Apostolic; Its Early Eminence and Excellence. Heresy, as Perverting the Truth, is Connected Therewith.</span></p> 
 <p class="c19"><a name="v.iii.xxxvi-p2"></a>Come now, you who would indulge a better curiosity, if you would apply it to the business of your salvation, run over the apostolic churches, in which the very thrones <a name="fr_2229"></a><a href="#fn_2229"><font size="1">2230</font></a>
  of the apostles are still pre-eminent in their places, <a name="fr_2230"></a><a href="#fn_2230"><font size="1">2231</font></a>
  in which their own authentic writings <a name="fr_2231"></a><a href="#fn_2231"><font size="1">2232</font></a>
  are read, uttering the voice and representing the face of each of them severally. Achaia is very near you, (in which) you find Corinth. Since you are not far from Macedonia, you have Philippi; (and there too) you have the Thessalonians. Since you are able to cross to Asia, you get Ephesus. Since, moreover, you are close upon Italy, <a name="fr_2232"></a><a href="#fn_2232"><font size="1">2233</font></a>
  you have Rome, from which there comes even into our own hands the very authority (of apostles themselves). <a name="fr_2233"></a><a href="#fn_2233"><font size="1">2234</font></a>
  How happy is its church, on which apostles poured forth all their doctrine along with their blood! where Peter endures a passion like his Lords! where Paul wins his crown in a death like Johns <a name="fr_2234"></a><a href="#fn_2234"><font size="1">2235</font></a>
  where the Apostle John was first plunged, unhurt, into boiling oil, and thence remitted to his island-exile! See what she has learned, what taught, what fellowship has had with even (our) churches in Africa! <a name="fr_2235"></a><a href="#fn_2235"><font size="1">2236</font></a>
  One Lord God does she acknowledge, the Creator of the universe, and Christ Jesus (born) of the Virgin Mary, the Son of God the Creator; and the Resurrection of the flesh; the law and the prophets she unites <a name="fr_2236"></a><a href="#fn_2236"><font size="1">2237</font></a>
  in one volume with the writings of evangelists and apostles, from which she drinks in her faith. This she seals with the water (of baptism), arrays with the Holy <a name="page_261"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 261</font></a> <a name="v.iii.xxxvi-Page_261"></a>Ghost, feeds with the Eucharist, cheers with martyrdom, <a name="fr_2237"></a><a href="#fn_2237"><font size="1">2238</font></a>
  and against such a discipline thus (maintained) she admits no gainsayer. This is the discipline which I no longer say foretold that heresies should come, but from <a name="fr_2238"></a><a href="#fn_2238"><font size="1">2239</font></a>
  which they proceeded. However, they were not of her, because they were opposed to her. <a name="fr_2239"></a><a href="#fn_2239"><font size="1">2240</font></a>
  Even the rough wild-olive arises from the germ <a name="fr_2240"></a><a href="#fn_2240"><font size="1">2241</font></a>
  of the fruitful, rich, and genuine <a name="fr_2241"></a><a href="#fn_2241"><font size="1">2242</font></a>
  olive; also from the seed <a name="fr_2242"></a><a href="#fn_2242"><font size="1">2243</font></a>
  of the mellowest and sweetest fig there springs the empty and useless wild-fig. In the same way heresies, too, come from our plant, <a name="fr_2243"></a><a href="#fn_2243"><font size="1">2244</font></a>
  although not of our kind; (they come) from the grain of truth, <a name="fr_2244"></a><a href="#fn_2244"><font size="1">2245</font></a>
  but, owing to their falsehood, they have only wild leaves to show. <a name="fr_2245"></a><a href="#fn_2245"><font size="1">2246</font></a>
 </p>
