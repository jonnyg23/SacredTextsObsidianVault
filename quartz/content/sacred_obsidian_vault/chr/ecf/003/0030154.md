 </p><p class="c30"><a name="iv.viii.i.xiv-p1"></a> <span class="c1"><a name="iv.viii.i.xiv-p1.1"></a>Chapter XIV. <a name="fr_678"></a><a href="#fn_678"><font size="1">679</font></a>
 —The Vile Calumny About Onocoetes Retorted on the Heathen by Tertullian.</span></p> 
 <p class="c19"><a name="iv.viii.i.xiv-p3"></a>Report has introduced a new calumny respecting our God. Not so long ago, a most abandoned wretch in that city of yours, <a name="fr_679"></a><a href="#fn_679"><font size="1">680</font></a>
  a man who had deserted indeed his own religion—a Jew, in fact, who had only lost his skin, flayed of course by wild beasts, <a name="fr_680"></a><a href="#fn_680"><font size="1">681</font></a>
  against which he enters the lists for hire day after day with a sound body, and so in a condition to lose his skin <a name="fr_681"></a><a href="#fn_681"><font size="1">682</font></a>
 —carried about in public a caricature of us with this label: <i>Onocoetes</i>. <a name="fr_682"></a><a href="#fn_682"><font size="1">683</font></a>
  This (figure) had asss ears, and was dressed in a <i>toga</i> with a book, having a hoof on one of his feet. And the crowd believed this infamous Jew. For what other set of men is the seed-plot <a name="fr_683"></a><a href="#fn_683"><font size="1">684</font></a>
  of all the calumny against us?  Throughout the city, therefore, Onocoetes is all the talk. As, however, it is less then “a nine days wonder,” <a name="fr_684"></a><a href="#fn_684"><font size="1">685</font></a>
  and so destitute of all authority from time, and weak enough from the character of its author, I shall gratify myself by using it simply in the way of a retort. Let us then see whether you are not here also found in our company. Now it matters not what their form may be, when our concern is about deformed images. You have amongst you gods with a dogs head, and a lions head, with the horns of a cow, and a ram, and a goat, goat-shaped or serpent-shaped, and winged in foot, head, and back. Why therefore brand our one God so conspicuously? Many an <i>Onocoetes</i> is found amongst yourselves.</p>
