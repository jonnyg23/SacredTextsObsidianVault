 </p><p class="c41"><a name="v.iv.ix.xxviii-p1"></a> <span class="c11"><a name="v.iv.ix.xxviii-p1.1"></a>Chapter 28.—</span>54.  Another Saturninus of Abitini <a name="fr_1834"></a><a href="#fn_1834"><font size="1">1835</font></a>
  said:  "If Antichrist can give to any one the grace of Christ, then can heretics also baptize, who are called Antichrists." <a name="fr_1835"></a><a href="#fn_1835"><font size="1">1836</font></a>
 </p>  
 <p class="c10"><a name="v.iv.ix.xxviii-p4"></a>55.  What if another were to say, If a murderer can give the grace of Christ, then can they also baptize that hate their brethren who are called murderers?  For certainly he would seem in a way to speak the truth, and yet they can baptize; in like manner, therefore, can the heretics as well.</p>
