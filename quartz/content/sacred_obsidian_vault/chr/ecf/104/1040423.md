 </p><p class="c41"><a name="v.iv.ix.xxiv-p1"></a> <span class="c11"><a name="v.iv.ix.xxiv-p1.1"></a>Chapter 24.—</span>46.  Rogatianus of Nova <a name="fr_1824"></a><a href="#fn_1824"><font size="1">1825</font></a>
  said:  "Christ established the Church, the devil heresy:  how can the synagogue of Satan have the baptism of Christ?" <a name="fr_1825"></a><a href="#fn_1825"><font size="1">1826</font></a>
 </p>  
 <p class="c10"><a name="v.iv.ix.xxiv-p4"></a>47.  To him our answer is:  Is it true that because Christ established the well-affectioned, and the devil the envious, therefore the party of the devil, which is proved to be among the envious, cannot have the baptism of Christ?</p>
