 </p><p class="c41"><a name="v.iv.ix.ix-p1"></a> <span class="c11"><a name="v.iv.ix.ix-p1.1"></a>Chapter 9.—</span>16.  Jader of Midila <a name="fr_1779"></a><a href="#fn_1779"><font size="1">1780</font></a>
  said:  "We know that there is but one baptism in the Catholic Church, and therefore we ought not <a name="page_503"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 503</font></a> <a name="v.iv.ix.ix-Page_503"></a>to admit a heretic unless he has been baptized in our body, lest he should think that he has been baptized outside the Catholic Church." <a name="fr_1780"></a><a href="#fn_1780"><font size="1">1781</font></a>
 </p>  
 <p class="c10"><a name="v.iv.ix.ix-p4"></a>17.  To him our answer is, that if this were said of those unrighteous men who are outside the rock, it certainly would be falsely said.  And so it is therefore also in the case of heretics.</p>
