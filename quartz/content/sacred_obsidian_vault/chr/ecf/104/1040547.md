 </p><p class="c41"><a name="v.v.iv.lx-p1"></a> <span class="c11"><a name="v.v.iv.lx-p1.1"></a>Chapter</span>  60.—135.  <span class="c11"><a name="v.v.iv.lx-p1.2"></a>Petilianus</span> said:  "Under what law, then, do you make out that you are Christians, seeing that you do what is contrary to the law?"</p>
