 </p><p class="c41"><a name="v.iv.ix.xxxi-p1"></a> <span class="c11"><a name="v.iv.ix.xxxi-p1.1"></a>Chapter 31.—</span>60.  Tenax of Horrea Celiæ <a name="fr_1841"></a><a href="#fn_1841"><font size="1">1842</font></a>
  said:  "There is one baptism, but of the Church; and where the Church is not, there baptism also cannot be." <a name="fr_1842"></a><a href="#fn_1842"><font size="1">1843</font></a>
 </p>  
 <p class="c10"><a name="v.iv.ix.xxxi-p4"></a>61.  To him we answer:  How then comes it that it may be where the rock is not, but only sand; seeing that the Church is on the rock, and not on sand?</p>
