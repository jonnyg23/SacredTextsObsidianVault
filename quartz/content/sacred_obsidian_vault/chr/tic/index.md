 <p>
 <cite>El Castillo Interior</cite> (The Interior Castle)
 or <cite>Las Moradas</cite> (The Mansions)
 was written by St. Teresa of Avila in 1577.
 According to contemporary accounts, St. Teresa had a revelation from
 God of a crystal globe in the shape of a castle containing seven
 mansions.
 This is St. Teresa's interpretation of that vision, describing the
 journey of faith through seven stages, ending with union with God.
 </p>
