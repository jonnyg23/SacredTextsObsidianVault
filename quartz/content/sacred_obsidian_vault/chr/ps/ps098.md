 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>Pistis Sophia</i>, by G.R.S. Mead, [1921], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">CHAPTER 93</h3>  
 <p>Jesus continued again and said unto his disciples: "Yet further, O my disciples and companions and brethren, let every one be sober in the spirit which is in him, let him understand and comprehend all the words which I shall say unto you; for from now on will I begin to discourse with you concerning all the gnoses of that Ineffable.</p>  
 <p>"That mystery knoweth why the west hath arisen and why the east hath arisen.</p>  
 <p>"And that mystery knoweth why the south hath arisen and why the north hath arisen.</p>  
 <p>Yet further, O my disciples, hearken and continue to be sober and hearken to the total gnosis of the mystery of the Ineffable.</p>  
 <p>"That mystery knoweth why the demons have arisen and why mankind hath arisen.</p>  
 <p>"And that mystery knoweth why the heat hath arisen and why the pleasant air hath arisen.</p>  
 <p>"And that mystery knoweth why the stars have arisen and why the clouds have arisen. |<span class="rmargnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><b>212</b>.</font></span></p>  
 <p>"And that mystery knoweth why the earth became deep and why the water came thereon.</p>  
 <p>"And that mystery knoweth why the earth became dry and why the water came thereon.</p>  
 <p>"And that mystery knoweth why famine hath arisen and why superfluity hath arisen.</p>  
 <p>"And that mystery knoweth why the hoar-frost</p>  
 <p><a name="page_178"><font size="1" color="green">p. 178</font></a></p>  
 <p>hath arisen and why the healthful dew hath arisen.</p>  
 <p>"And that mystery knoweth why the dust hath arisen and why the delightsome freshness hath arisen.</p>  
 <p>"And that mystery knoweth why the hail hath arisen and why the pleasant snow hath arisen.</p>  
 <p>"And that mystery knoweth why the west wind hath arisen and why the east wind hath arisen.</p>  
 <p>("And that mystery knoweth why the fire of the height hath arisen and why the waters have arisen.</p>  
 <p>"And that mystery knoweth why the east wind hath arisen. [? miscopied.])</p>  
 <p>"And that mystery knoweth why the south wind hath arisen and why the north wind hath arisen.</p>  
 <p>"And that mystery knoweth why the stars of the heaven and the |<span class="rmargnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><b>213</b>.</font></span> disks of the light-givers have arisen and why the firmament with all its veils hath arisen.</p>  
 <p>"And that mystery knoweth why the rulers of the spheres have arisen and why the sphere with all its regions hath arisen.</p>  
 <p>"And that mystery knoweth why the rulers of the æons have arisen and why the æons with their veils have arisen.</p>  
 <p>"And that mystery knoweth why the tyrant rulers of the æons have arisen and why the rulers who have repented have arisen.</p>  
 <p>"And that mystery knoweth why the servitors have arisen and why the decans have arisen.</p>  
 <p>"And that mystery knoweth why the angels</p>  
 <p>have arisen and why the archangels have arisen.</p>  
 <p><a name="page_179"><font size="1" color="green">p. 179</font></a></p>  
 <p>"And that mystery knoweth why the lords have arisen and why the gods have arisen.</p>  
 <p>"And that mystery knoweth why the jealousy in the height hath arisen and why concord hath arisen.</p>  
 <p>"And that mystery knoweth why hate hath arisen and why love hath arisen.</p>  
 <p>"And that mystery knoweth why discord hath arisen and why concord hath arisen.</p>  
 <p>"And that mystery knoweth why avarice |<span class="rmargnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><b>214</b>.</font></span> hath arisen and why renunciation of all hath arisen and love of possessions hath arisen.</p>  
 <p>"And that mystery knoweth why love of the belly hath arisen and why satiety hath arisen.</p>  
 <p>"And that mystery knoweth why the paired have arisen and why the unpaired have arisen.</p>  
 <p>"And that mystery knoweth why impiety hath arisen and why fear of God hath arisen.</p>  
 <p>"And that mystery knoweth why the light-givers have arisen and why the sparks have arisen.</p>  
 <p>"And that mystery knoweth why the thrice-powerful have arisen and why the invisibles have arisen.</p>  
 <p>"And that mystery knoweth why the fore-fathers have arisen and why the purities have arisen.</p>  
 <p>"And that mystery knoweth why the great self-willed hath arisen and why his faithful have arisen.</p>  
 <p>"And that mystery knoweth why the great triple-powerful hath arisen and why the great invisible forefather hath arisen.</p>  
 <p>"And that mystery knoweth why the thirteenth æon hath arisen and why the region |<span class="rmargnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><b>215</b>.</font></span> of those of the Midst hath arisen.</p>  
 <p><a name="page_180"><font size="1" color="green">p. 180</font></a></p>  
 <p>"And that mystery knoweth why receivers of the Midst have arisen and why the virgins of the light have arisen.</p>  
 <p>"And that mystery knoweth why the ministers of the Midst have arisen and why the angels of the Midst have arisen.</p>  
 <p>"And that mystery knoweth why the light-land hath arisen and why the great receiver of the light hath arisen.</p>  
 <p>"And that mystery knoweth why the guards of the region of the Right have arisen and why the leaders of them have arisen.</p>  
 <p>"And that mystery knoweth why the gate of life hath arisen and why Sabaōth, the Good, hath arisen.</p>  
 <p>"And that mystery knoweth why the region of the Right hath arisen and why the light-land, which is the treasury of the light, hath arisen.</p>  
 <p>"And that mystery knoweth why the emanations of the light have arisen and why the twelve saviours have arisen.</p>  
 <p>"And that mystery knoweth why the three gates of the treasury of the light have arisen and why the nine guards have arisen.</p>  
 <p>"And |<span class="rmargnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><b>216</b>.</font></span> that mystery knoweth why the twin-saviours have arisen and why the three Amēns have arisen.</p>  
 <p>"And that mystery knoweth why the five Trees have arisen and why the seven Amēns have arisen.</p>  
 <p>"And that mystery knoweth why the Mixture which existeth not, hath arisen and why it is purified."</p>
