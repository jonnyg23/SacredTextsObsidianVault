 <p><a name="iv.XV-p1"></a> 		  </p><h3 align="center"><a name="iv.XV-p1.1"></a>CHAPTER XV.</h3>   		  
 <p><a name="iv.XV-p2"></a>  		  
 </p><p><a name="iv.XV-p3"></a>He is greater than can be conceived.</p>  		  
 <p><a name="iv.XV-p4"></a>  		  
 </p><p><a name="iv.XV-p5"></a>THEREFORE, O Lord, thou art not only that than which a greater cannot be conceived, but thou art a being greater than can be conceived. For, since it can be conceived that there is such a being, if thou art not this very being, a greater than thou can be conceived. But this is impossible.</p>
