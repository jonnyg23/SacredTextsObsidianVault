 <p><a name="page_138"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 138</font></a></p>  <h1 align="center">The Seven Last Plagues or Vial Judgments.</h1>  
 <p align="center"><a name="img_13800"></a>
 <img src="img/13800.jpg"></p>  
 <p align="center">Rev. 15:1.</p>  
 <p></p><dir><font size="1">"And I saw another <b>SIGN</b> in Heaven, great and marvellous, S<b>EVEN ANGELS</b> having the '<b>SEVEN LAST PLAGUES</b>'; for in them is <b>filled up</b> the <b>WRATH OF GOD</b>."</font></dir>  
 <p>This is another "<b>SIGN</b>" or "<b>Wonder</b>." It was great and marvellous, because it "<b>FILLED UP THE WRATH OF GOD</b>," that is, it <b>completed</b> the pouring out of the <b>accumulated</b> "<b>WRATH OF GOD</b>."</p>
