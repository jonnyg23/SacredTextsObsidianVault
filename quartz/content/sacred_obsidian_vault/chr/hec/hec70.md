 <p align="center"><font size="-1" color="green">Canon for Ascension Day</font></p>  <h3 align="center">ODE IX.</h3>  
 <p align="center">ὦ τῶν δωρεῶν.</p>  
 <p>H   oly gift, surpassing comprehension!<br>     Wond’rous mystery of each fiery tongue!<br>     <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Christ</span> made good His Promise in Ascension:<br>     O’er the Twelve the cloven flames have hung!</p>  
 <p>S   pake the <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Lord</span>, or ere He left the Eleven:<br>     "Here in Salem wait the Gift I send:<br>     Till the <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Paraclete</span> come down from Heaven:<br>     Everlasting Guide and Guard and Friend."</p>  
 <p><a name="page_227"><font size="1" color="green">p. 227</font></a></p>  
 <p>O   that shame, now ended in that glory!<br>     Pain untold, now lost in joy unknown!<br>     Tell it out with praise, the whole glad story,<br>     Human nature at the <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Father's</span> Throne!</p>  
 <p align="center"><i>Catavasía</i>.</p>  
 <p>D   eclare, ye Angel Bands that dwell on high,<br>     How saw ye Him, the Victor, drawing nigh?<br>     What strange new visions burst upon your sight?<br>     One in the form of Man, That claims by right<br>     The very throne of <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">God</span>, the unapproached Light!</p>  
 <p align="center"><i>Exaposteilarion</i>.</p>  
 <p>E   ternal! After Thine own will<br>       Thou born in time would’st be: <a name="page_228"><font size="1" color="green">p. 228</font></a><br>     After the self-same counsel still<br>       Was Thine Epiphany:<br>     Thou in our flesh didst yield Thy breath,<br>       Immortal <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">God</span>, for man:<br>     Thou by Thy death didst conquer Death,<br>       Through Thine Almighty plan:<br>     Thou, rising Victor to the sky,<br>       Fill’st Heav’n and earth above<br>     And send’st the Promise from on high,<br>       The <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Spirit</span> of Thy love!</p>
