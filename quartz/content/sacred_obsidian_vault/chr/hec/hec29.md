 <p align="center"><font size="-1" color="green">Canon for Easter Day</font></p>  <h3 align="center">ODE IX.</h3>  
 <p align="center"><font size="-1" color="green">by S. John Damascene.</font></p>  
 <p align="center">φωτίζου, φωτίζου.</p>  
 <p>Thou New Jerusalem, arise and shine!<br> The glory of the Lord on thee hath risen!<br> Sion, exult! rejoice with joy divine,<br> Mother of <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">God</span>! Thy Son hath burst His prison!</p>  
 <p>O heavenly Voice! O word of purest love!<br> 'Lo! I am with you alway to the end!'<br> This is the anchor, steadfast from above,<br> The golden anchor, whence our hopes depend.</p>  
 <p>O <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Christ</span>, our Pascha! greatest, holiest, best!<br> <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">God's</span> Word and Wisdom and effectual Might!<br> Thy fuller, lovelier presence manifest,<br> In that eternal realm, that knows no night!</p>
