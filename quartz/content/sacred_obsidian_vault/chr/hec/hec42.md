 <p align="center"><font size="-1" color="green">Canon for Christmas Day</font></p>  <h3 align="center">ODE VII.</h3>  
 <p align="center"><font size="-1" color="green">by S. Cosmas</font></p>  
 <p align="center">οἱ παῖδες εὐσεβείᾳ.</p>  
 <p>The Holy Children boldly stand<br> Against the tyrant's fierce command:<br> The kindled furnace they defy,—<br> No doom can shake their constancy<br> They in the midmost flame confess’d,<br> "<span style="font-variant:small-caps;">God</span> of our Fathers! Thou art bless’d!"</p>  
 <p>The Shepherds keep their flocks by night;<br> The Heav’n glows out with wondrous light;<br> The glory of the <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Lord</span> is there,<br> The Angel-bands their King declare:<br> The watchers of the night confess’d,<br> "<span style="font-variant:small-caps;">God</span> of our Fathers! Thou art bless’d!"</p>  
 <p><a name="page_144"><font size="1" color="green">p. 144</font></a></p>  
 <p>The Angel ceas’d; and suddenly<br> Seraphic legions fill’d the sky:<br> "Glory to <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">God</span>," they cry again:<br> "Peace upon earth, good will to men:<br> "<span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Christ</span> comes! "—And they that heard confess’d,<br> "<span style="font-variant:small-caps;">God</span> of our fathers! Thou art bless’d!"</p>  
 <p>What said the Shepherds?—"Let us turn<br> This new-born miracle to learn."<br> To Bethlehem’s gate their footsteps drew:<br> The Mother with the Child they view:<br> They knelt and worshipp’d, and confess’d,<br> "<span style="font-variant:small-caps;">God</span> of our Fathers! Thou art bless’d!"</p>
