 <p align="center"><font size="-1" color="green">Canon for Easter Day</font></p>  <h3 align="center">ODE V.</h3>  
 <p align="center"><font size="-1" color="green">by S. John Damascene.</font></p>  
 <p align="center">ὀρθρίσωμεν ὄρθρου βαθέος.</p>  
 <p>Let us rise in early morning,<br>   And, instead of ointments, bring<br> Hymns of praises to our Master,<br>   And His Resurrection sing:<br> We shall see the Sun of Justice<br>   Risen with healing on His wing.</p>  
 <p>Thy unbounded loving-kindness,<br>   They that groaned in Hades’ chain,<br> Prisoners, from afar beholding,<br>   Hasten to the light again<br> And to that eternal Pascha<br>   Wove the dance and raised the strain.</p>  
 <p><a name="page_101"><font size="1" color="green">p. 101</font></a></p>  
 <p>Go ye forth, His Saints, to meet Him!<br>   Go with lamps in every hand!<br> From the sepulchre He riseth:<br>   Ready for the Bridegroom stand:<br> And the Pascha of salvation<br>   Hail, with His triumphant band.</p>
