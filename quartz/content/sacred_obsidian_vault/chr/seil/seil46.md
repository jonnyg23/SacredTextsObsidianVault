 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Spiritual Exercises of St. Ignatius of Loyola</i>, [1914], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <a></a> <h3 align="center">OF THE FLIGHT TO EGYPT</h3> St. Matthew writes in the second Chapter [13-16].
 <p> <b>First Point</b>. First: Herod wanted to kill the Child Jesus, and so killed the Innocents, and before their death the Angel warned Joseph to fly into Egypt: "`Arise and take the Child and His Mother, and fly to Egypt.'"
 </p><p> <b>Second Point</b>. Second: He departed for Egypt. "Who arising by night departed to Egypt."
 </p><p> <b>Third Point</b>. Third: He was there until the death of Herod.
