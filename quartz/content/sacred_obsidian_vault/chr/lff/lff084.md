 <p align="center"><small>How a holy man, being in prayer, beheld the soul of Friar Giles go to the life eternal</small></p>  
 <p>A GOOD man, being in prayer, when Friar Giles passed from this life, saw his soul go up to heaven, together with a multitude of other souls which</p>  
 <p><a name="page_232"><font size="1" color="green">p. 232</font></a></p>  
 <p>at that hour came forth from Purgatory; and he saw Jesus Christ come to meet the soul of Friar Giles, and, with a multitude of Angels and all those souls, ascend into the glory of Paradise, to a great sound of sweet music.</p>
