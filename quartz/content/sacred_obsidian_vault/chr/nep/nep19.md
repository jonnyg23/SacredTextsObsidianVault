 <p><a name="page_21"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 21</font></a></p>
 <p>Q Who made you? A. <i>God</i>.<br>
 Q. Who redeemed you? A. JESUS CHRIST.<br>
 Q. Who sanctifies and preserves you?<br>
 A. <i>The Holy Ghost</i>.<br>
 Q. Of what are you made? A. <i>Dust</i>.<br>
 Q. What doth that teach you?<br>
 A. <i>To be humble, and mindful of death</i>.<br>
 Q. For what end were you made?<br>
 A. <i>To serve God</i>.<br>
 Q. How must you serve him?<br>
 A. <i>In spirit and in truth</i>.</p>
