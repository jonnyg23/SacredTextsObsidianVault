 <font size="4"><p align="CENTER">AGUR'S PRAYER</p></font>.
 <p>Remove far from me vanity and lies; give me neither poverty nor riches; feed me with food convenient for me; lest I be full and deny thee, and say, "Who is the Lord?" or lest I be poor, and steal, and take the name of God in vain.</p>
