 <p align="center">THE CANON OF THE MASS</p>  <div align="center">  <table border="0" width="100%">  <tr>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="49%">   
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Te</span> ígitur, clementíssime Pater, per Jesum   Christum Fílium tuum   Dóminum nostrum súpplices   rogámus ac pétimus   (<i>osculatur</i><i> altare</i>) uti accépta hábeas, et benedícas   (<i>jungit</i><i> manus, deinde signat ter super oblata</i>), hæc <img src="img/cross.jpg"> dona, hæc <img src="img/cross.jpg"> múnera, hæc <img src="img/cross.jpg">   sancta sacrifícia illibáta   (<i>extensis</i><i> manibus prosequitur</i>): in primis quæ   tibi offérimus pro   Ecclésia tua sancta cathólica:   quam pacificáre, custodíre,   adunáre, et régere   dignéris toto orbe terrárum, una cum famulo tuo Papa nostro <i>N</i>. et Antístite   nostro <i>N</i>.   et ómnibus orthodóxis,   atque cathólicæ et apostólicæ   fídei cultóribus.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="2%">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="49%">   
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Wherefore</span>, O most merciful Father, we humbly pray and   beseech thee, through Jesus Christ thy Son, our Lord (<i>he kisses the altar</i>), that thou <a name="page_465"><font size="1" color="green">p. 465</font></a> wouldst vouchsafe to   receive and bless (<i>he joins his hands   together, and then makes the sign of the cross thrice over the offerings</i>)   these <img src="img/cross.jpg"> gifts, these <img src="img/cross.jpg"> offerings, this <img src="img/cross.jpg"> holy and unblemished sacrifice (he   extends his hands and continues), which in the first place we offer thee for   thy holy Catholic Church, that it may please thee to grant her peace: as also   to protect, unite, and govern her throughout the world, together with thy   servant <i>N</i>., our Pope <i>N</i>., our bishop, as also all orthodox   believers who keep the catholic and apostolic faith.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>  
 <p align="center"><i>The Commemoration for the living</i>.</p>  <div align="center">  <table border="0" width="100%">  <tr>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="48%">   
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Meménto</span>, Dómine, famulórum   famularúmque tuárum   <i>N</i>. et <i>N</i>.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="2%">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="48%">   
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Be</span> mindful, O Lord, of thy servants and handmaids, <i>N</i>. and <i>N</i>.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>  
 <p align="center"><i>He joins his hands, prays a little while for those he wishes to pray for, then with his hands stretched out he continues:</i> </p>  <div align="center">  <table border="0" width="100%">  <tr>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="49%">   
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Et</span> ómnium circumstántium, quorum tibi   fides cógnita est, et nota devótio: pro quibus tibi offérimus, vel qui tibi ófferunt hoc sacrifícium   laudis, pro se, suísque   ómnibus, pro redemptióne   animárum suárum,   pro spe salútis et   incolumitátis suæ;   tibíque reddunt vota sua ætérno   Deo, vivo et vero.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="2%">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="49%">   
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">And</span> of all here present, whose faith and devotion are   known unto thee; for whom we offer, or who offer up to thee, this sacrifice   of praise for themselves and theirs, for the redeeming of their souls, for   the hope of their safety and salvation, and who pay their vows to thee, the   eternal, living, and true God.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>  
 <p align="center"> </p>  <div align="center">  <table border="0" width="100%">  <tr>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="48%">   
 <p align="center"><i>Infra   actionem</i>.</p>   
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Communicántes</span>, et memóriam venerántes,   in primis gloriósæ semper Vírginis Maríæ,   genitrícis Dei et Dómini   nostri Jesu Christi: sed et beatórum Apostolórum ac Mártyrum   tuórum, Petri et Pauli, Andréæ,   Jacóbi, Joánnis,   Thomæ, Jacóbi,   Philíppi, Bartholomæi,   Matthæi, Simónis   et Thaddæi, Cleti, Cleméntis, Xysti, Cornélii, Cypriáni,   Lauréntii, Chrysógoni,   Joánnis et Pauli, Cosmæ   et Damiáni et ómnium   sanctórum tuórum:   quorum méritis precibúsque   concédas, ut in ómnibus   protectiónis tuæ   muniámur auxílio.   (<i>Jungit</i><i> manus</i>.) Per eúmdem   Christum Dóminum nostrum. Amen.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="2%">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="48%">   
 <p align="center"><i>Within   the action</i>.</p>   
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Communicating</span>, and reverencing the memory first of   the glorious Mary, ever a virgin, Mother of our God and Lord Jesus Christ;   likewise of thy blessed apostles and martyrs, Peter and Paul, Andrew, James,   John, Thomas, James, Philip, Bartholomew, <a name="page_466"><font size="1" color="green">p. 466</font></a> Matthew, Simon and   Thaddeus; of Linus, Cletus, Clement, Xystus, Cornelius, Cyprian, Lawrence, Chrysogonus,   john and Paul, Cosmas and Damian, and of all thy   saints; by whose merits and prayers grant that in all things we may be guarded   by thy protecting help. (<i>He joins his   hands together</i>.) Through the same Christ our Lord. Amen.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>  
 <p align="center"><i>With his hands spread over the offerings, he says:</i> </p>  <div align="center">  <table border="0" width="100%">  <tr>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="48%">   
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Hanc</span> ígitur   oblatiónem servitútis   nostræ, sed et cunctæ famíliæ   tuæ, quæsumus   Dómine, ut placátus   accípias, diésque   nostros in tua pace dispónas, atque ab ætérna damnatióne   nos éripi, et in electórum tuórum júbeas grege numerári.   (<i>Jungit</i><i> manus</i>.) Per Christum Dóminum nostrum. Amen.</p>   
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Quam</span> oblatiónem tu, Deus, in ómnibus, quæsumus,</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="2%">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="48%">   
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">We</span> therefore beseech thee, O Lord, to be appeased, and to receive   this offering of our bounden duty, as also of thy whole household; order our   days in thy peace; grant that we be rescued from eternal damnation and   counted within the fold of thine elect. (<i>He joins his hands together</i>.) Through   Christ our Lord. Amen.</p>   
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Which</span> offering do thou, O God, vouchsafe in all   things.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>  
 <p align="center"><i>He makes the sign of the cross three times over the offerings</i>.</p>  <div align="center">  <table border="0" width="100%">  <tr>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="48%">   
 <p>bene<img src="img/cross.jpg">díctam, adscrí<img src="img/cross.jpg">ptam, ra<img src="img/cross.jpg">tam, rationábilem, acceptabilémque   fácere dignéris:</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="2%">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="48%">   
 <p>to bless <img src="img/cross.jpg">, consecrate <img src="img/cross.jpg">, approve <img src="img/cross.jpg">, make reasonable and   acceptable:</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>  
 <p align="center"><i>He makes the sign of the cross once over the host and once over the chalice</i>.</p>  <div align="center">  <table border="0" width="100%">  <tr>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="48%">   
 <p>ut nobis Cor<img src="img/cross.jpg">pus et San<img src="img/cross.jpg">guis fiat dilectíssimi   Fílii tui Dómini nostri Jesu Christi.</p>   
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Qui</span> prídie quam paterétur (<i>accipit</i><i> hostiam</i>), accépit panem in sanctas ac venerábiles manus suas (<i>elevat</i><i> oculos ad cœlum</i>), et elevátis   óculis in cœlum,   ad te Deum Patrem suum omnipoténtem, tibi grátias agens,</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="2%">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="48%">   
 <p>that it may become for us the Body <img src="img/cross.jpg"> and <img src="img/cross.jpg"> Blood of thy most beloved Son   our Lord Jesus Christ.</p>   
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Who</span> the day before he suffered took bread (<i>he takes the host</i>) into his holy and   venerable hands (<i>he raises </i><a name="page_467"><font size="1" color="green">p. 467</font></a>   <i>his eyes to heaven</i>), and with his   eyes lifted up to heaven, unto thee, God, his almighty Father, giving thanks   to thee,</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>  
 <p align="center"><i>He makes the sign of the cross over the host</i>.</p>  <div align="center">  <table border="0" width="100%">  <tr>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="48%">   
 <p>bene<img src="img/cross.jpg">díxit, fregit, dedítque discípulis suis, dicens: Accípite, et manducáte ex hoc omnes.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="2%">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="48%">   
 <p>he blessed <img src="img/cross.jpg">, brake, and gave to his disciples, saying:   Take and eat ye all of this,</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>  
 <p><i>Holding the host between the first fingers and thumbs of both hands, he says the words of consecration, silently with clearness and attention, over the host, and at the same time over all the other hosts, if several are to be consecrated</i>.</p>  <div align="center">  <table border="0" width="100%">  <tr>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="48%">   
 <p>Hoc est enim Corpus meum.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="2%">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="48%">   
 <p>For this is my Body.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>  
 <p><i>As soon as the words of consecration have been said, he kneels and adores the consecrated host. He rises, shows it to the people, puts it on the corporal, and again adores. Then, uncovering the chalice, he says:</i> </p>  <div align="center">  <table border="0" width="100%">  <tr>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="48%">   
 <p>Símili modo postquam cœnátum est,</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="2%">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="48%">   
 <p>In like manner, after he had supped,</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>  
 <p align="center"><i>He takes the chalice with both hands</i>.</p>  <div align="center">  <table border="0" width="100%">  <tr>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="48%">   
 <p>accípiens et hunc præclárum Cálicem   in sanctas ac venerábiles   manus suas, item tibi grátias agens,</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="2%">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="48%">   
 <p>taking also this excellent chalice into his holy and adorable   hands; also giving thanks to thee,</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>  
 <p align="center"><i>Holding the chalice with his left hand, he makes the sign of the cross over it with his right</i>.</p>  <div align="center">  <table border="0" width="100%">  <tr>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="48%">   
 <p>bene<img src="img/cross.jpg">díxit, dedítque discípulis   suis, dicens: Accípite, et bíbite   ex eo omnes:</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="2%">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="48%">   
 <p>he blessed <img src="img/cross.jpg">, and gave it to his disciples, saying: Take,   and drink ye all of this;</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>  
 <p align="center"><i>He utters the words of consecration over the chalice silently, attentively, carefully, and without pausing, holding it slightly raised</i>.</p>  <div align="center">  <table border="0" width="100%">  <tr>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="48%">   
 <p>Hic est enim Calix   Sánguinis mei, novi et ætérni testaménti; mystérium   fidei: qui pro vobis et   pro multis effundétur   in remissiónem peccatórum.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="2%">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="48%">   
 <p>For this is the Chalice of my Blood, of the new and   eternal testament; the mystery of faith: which shall be shed for you and for   many unto the remission of sins.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>  
 <p align="center"><i>As soon as the words of consecration have been said, he puts the chalice on the corporal, and says silently:</i> </p>  <div align="center">  <table border="0" width="100%">  <tr>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="49%">   
 <p>Hæc quotiescúmque   fecéritis, in mei memóriam faciétis.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="2%">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="49%">   
 <p>As often as ye shall do these things, ye shall do them in   memory of me.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>  
 <p><a name="page_468"><font size="1" color="green">p. 468</font></a></p>  
 <p align="center"><i>He kneels and adores; then rises, shows it to the people, puts it down, covers it, and again adores. Then holding his hands apart, he says:</i> </p>  <div align="center">  <table border="0" width="100%">  <tr>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="48%">   
 <p>Unde et mémores,   Dómine, nos servi tui, sed   et plebs tua sancta, ejúsdem Christi Fílii tui Dómini nostri, tam beatæ passiónis, necnon et ab ínferis resurrectiónis, sed et   in cœlos gloriósæ ascensiónis: offérimus   præcláræ majestáti   tuæ de tuis donis ac datis,</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="2%">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="48%">   
 <p>Wherefore, O Lord, we thy servants, as also thy holy   people, calling to mind the blessed passion of the same Christ thy Son our   Lord, and also his rising up from hell, and his glorious ascension into   heaven, do offer unto thy most excellent majesty, of thine   own gifts bestowed upon us,</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>  
 <p align="center"><i>He joins his hands and makes the sign of the cross three times over the host and chalice together</i>.</p>  <div align="center">  <table border="0" width="100%">  <tr>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="48%">   
 <p>hóstiam <img src="img/cross.jpg"> puram, hóstiam <img src="img/cross.jpg"> sanctam, hóstiam <img src="img/cross.jpg"> immaculátam,</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="2%">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="48%">   
 <p>a pure <img src="img/cross.jpg"> victim, a holy <img src="img/cross.jpg"> victim, a spotless <img src="img/cross.jpg"> victim,</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>  
 <p align="center"><i>He makes the sign of the cross once over the host and once over the chalice</i>.</p>  <div align="center">  <table border="0" width="100%">  <tr>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="48%">   
 <p>Panem <img src="img/cross.jpg"> sanctum vitæ ætérnæ, et Cálicem   <img src="img/cross.jpg"> salútis perpétuæ.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="2%">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="48%">   
 <p>the holy <img src="img/cross.jpg"> Bread of eternal life, and the Chalice <img src="img/cross.jpg"> of everlasting   salvation.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>  
 <p align="center"><i>He continues with his hands stretched out:</i> </p>  <div align="center">  <table border="0" width="100%">  <tr>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="48%">   
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Supra</span> quæ propítio ac seréno   vultu respícere dignéris: et accépta   habére, sícuti   accépta habére   dignátus es múnera púeri tui justi Abel, et sacrifícium patriárchæ   nostri Ábrahæ,   et quod tibi óbtulit   summus sacérdos tuus Melchísedech   sanctum sacrifícium, immaculátam   hóstiam.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="2%">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="48%">   
 <p>Upon which do thou vouchsafe to look with a propitious and   serene countenance, and to accept them, as thou wert graciously pleased to   accept the gifts of thy just servant Abel, and the sacrifice of our patriarch   Abraham, and that which thy high priest Melchisedech   offered to thee, a holy sacrifice, a spotless victim.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>  
 <p><i>Bowing low with his hands joined together and then laid on the altar, he says:</i> </p>  <div align="center">  <table border="0" width="100%">  <tr>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="49%">   
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Súpplices</span> te rogámus, omnípotens   Deus: jube hæc perférri per manus sancti   Ángeli tui in sublíme altáre tuum, in conspéctu divínæ majestátis   tuæ: ut quotquot (<i>osculatur</i><i> altare</i>), ex   hac altáris participatióne, sacrosánctum   Fílii tui,</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="2%">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="49%">   
 <p>We most humbly beseech thee, almighty God, to command that   these things be borne by the hands of thy holy angel to thine   altar On high, in the sight of thy divine majesty, that as many of us <i>(he kisses the altar</i>) as, at this   altar, shall partake of and receive the</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>  
 <p><a name="page_469"><font size="1" color="green">p. 469</font></a></p>  
 <p><i>He joins his hands together and makes the sign of the cross over the host and once over the chalice</i>.</p>  <div align="center">  <table border="0" width="100%">  <tr>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="48%">   
 <p>Cor<img src="img/cross.jpg">pus et Sán<img src="img/cross.jpg">guinem   sumpsérimus (<i>seipsum</i><i> signat</i>), omni   benedictióne cœlésti,   et grátia repleámur   (<i>jungit</i><i> manus</i>). Per eúndem   Christum Dóminum nostrum. Amen.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="2%">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="48%">   
 <p>most holy Body <img src="img/cross.jpg"> and <img src="img/cross.jpg"> Blood of thy Son (h<i>e makes the sign of the cross on himself</i>),   may be filled with every heavenly blessing and grace (<i>he joins his hands together</i>). Through the same Christ our Lord.   Amen.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>  
 <p align="center"><i>The Commemoration for the dead</i>.</p>  <div align="center">  <table border="0" width="100%">  <tr>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="48%">   
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Meménto</span> étiam, Dómine, famulórum famularúmque   tuárum <i>N</i>.   et <i>N</i>. qui nos   præcessérunt cum signo   fídei, et dórmiunt   in somno pacis.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="2%">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="48%">   
 <p>Be mindful, O Lord, of thy servants and handmaids <i>N</i>. and <i>N</i>., who are gone before us, with the sign of faith, and sleep in   the sleep of peace.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>  
 <p><i>He joins his hands, prays a little while for those dead whom he means to pray for, then with his hands stretched out, continues:</i> </p>  <div align="center">  <table border="0" width="100%">  <tr>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="48%">   
 <p>Ipsis, Dómine,   et ómnibus in Christo   quiescéntibus, locum refrigérii,   lucis et pacis, ut indúlgeas, deprecámur.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="2%">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="48%">   
 <p>To these, O Lord, and to all that rest in Christ, we   beseech thee, grant a place of refreshment, light, and peace.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>  
 <p align="center"><i>He joins his hands together, and bows his head</i>.</p>  <div align="center">  <table border="0" width="100%">  <tr>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="48%">   
 <p>Per eúmdem Christum Dóminum nostrum. Amen.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="2%">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="48%">   
 <p>Through the same Christ our Lord. Amen.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>  
 <p align="center"><i>He strikes his breast with his right hand, and slightly raising his voice, says:</i> </p>  <div align="center">  <table border="0" width="100%">  <tr>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="48%">   
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Nobis</span> quoque   peccatóribus, fámulis   tuis, de multitúdine   miseratiónum tuárum   sperántibus, partem   áliquam et societátem   donáre dignéris,   cum tuis sanctis Apóstolis et Martyribus:   cum Joánne, Stéphano,   Mathía, Bárnaba,   Ignátio, Alexándro,   Marcellíno, Petro, Felicitáte,   Perpétua, Ágatha,   Lúcia, Agnéte,   Cæcília, Anastásia,   et ómnibus sanctis   tuis; intra quorum nos consórtium, non æstimátor   mériti, sed véniæ, quæsumus,   largítor admítte.   Per Christum Dóminum nostrum. Per quem hæc ómnia, Dómine,   semper bona creas, sanctíficas,   vivíficas, benedícis,   et præstas nobis.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="2%">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="48%">

 <p><i>After saying, </i>Ite missa est<i> or </i>Benedicámus Dómino<i>, the priest bows down at the middle of the altar, and with his hands joined above it, says:</i> </p>  <div align="center">  <table border="0" width="100%">  <tr>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="48%">   
 <p>Pláceat tibi, sancta Trínitas, obséquium servitútis   meæ: et præsta;   ut sacrifícium quod óculis   tuæ majestátis   indígnus óbtuli,   tibi sit acceptábile,   mihíque, et ómnibus   pro quibus illud óbtuli, sit, te miseránte,   propitiábile. Per Christum Dóminum nostrum. Amen.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="2%">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="48%">   
 <p>May the homage of my service be pleasing to thee, O holy   Trinity; and grant that the sacrifice which I, though unworthy, have offered   in the sight of thy majesty, may be acceptable to thee: and through thy mercy   win forgiveness for me and for all those for whom I have offered it. Through   Christ our Lord. Amen.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>  
 <p><i>Then he kisses the altar, and raising his eyes upward, stretching out, lifting up, and joining his hands, bowing his head before the cross, he says:</i> </p>  <div align="center">  <table border="0" width="100%">  <tr>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="48%">   
 <p>Benedícat vos omnípotens Deus,</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="2%">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="48%">   
 <p>May God almighty bless you,</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>  
 <p><i>and turning towards the people, he blesses them once only, even at high mass, and continues:</i> </p>  <div align="center">  <table border="0" width="100%">  <tr>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="48%">   
 <p>Pater, et Fílius <img src="img/cross.jpg"> et   Spíritus sanctus. R. Amen.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="2%">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="48%">   
 <p>Father, and Son and Holy <img src="img/cross.jpg"> Ghost. R. Amen.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>  
 <p><i>At a bishop's mass a triple blessing is given. At mass for the dead no blessing is given</i>.</p>  
 <p><i>Then at the Gospel corner, after saying </i>Dóminus vobíscum<i>, and </i>Inítium<i> or </i>Sequéntia sancti Evangélii<i>, and making the sign of the cross on the altar, or on the book and on himself as at the Gospel in the mass, he reads the Gospel of St. John, as below, or another Gospel as appointed</i>.</p>  <div align="center">  <table border="0" width="100%">  <tr>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="48%">   
 <p><img src="img/cross.jpg"> Inítium sancti   Evangélii secúndum   Joánnem. R. Glória   tibi, Dómine.</p>   
 <p>In princípio erat   Verbum, et Verbum erat apud   Deum, et Deus erat Verbum. Hoc erat   in princípio apud   Deum. Ómnia per ipsum   facta sunt, et sine ipso factum est nihil quod   factum est. In ipso vita erat, et vita erat lux hóminum,   et lux in ténebris   lucet, et ténebræ   eam non comprehendérunt.   Fuit homo missus a Deo, cui nomen   erat Joánnes. Hic venit in testimónium, ut   testimónium perhibéret   de lúmine, ut omnes   créderent per illum.   Non erat ille lux, sed ut testimónium   perhibéret de lúmine.   Erat lux vera quæ illúminat omnem hóminem veniéntem   in hunc mundum. In mundo erat, et mundus per ipsum factus est, et mundus eum non cognóvit. In própria   venit, et sui eum non recepérunt; quotquot autem recepérunt eum, dedit eis   potestátem fílios   Dei fíeri; his qui credunt   in nómine ejus,   qui non ex sanguínibus, neque   ex voluntáte carnis,   neque ex voluntáte   viri, sed ex Deo nati sunt. (<i>Hic genuflectitur</i>.) <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Et verbum caro   factum est</span>, et habitávit in nobis: et vídimus glóriam ejus, glóriam quasi Unigéniti   a Patre, plenum grátiæ   et veritátis.</p>   
 <p>R. Deo grátias.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="2%">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="48%">   
 <p><img src="img/cross.jpg"> The beginning of the holy Gospel according to St. John. R. Glory be to   thee, O Lord.</p>   
 <p>In the beginning was the Word, and the Word was with God, and the Word was   God: the same was in the beginning with God. All things were made by him, and   without him was made nothing that was made: in him was life, and the life was   the light of men; and the light shineth in   darkness, and the darkness did not comprehend <a name="page_476"><font size="1" color="green">p. 476</font></a> it. There was a man   sent from God, whose name was John. This man came for a witness to give   testimony of the light, that all men might believe through him. He was not   the light, but was to give testimony of the light, that was the true light   which enlighteneth every man that cometh into this   world. He was in the world, and the world was made by him, and the world knew   him not. He came unto his own, and his own received him not. But as many as   received him, he gave them power to become the sons of God: to them that   believe in his name, who are born not of blood, nor of the will of the flesh,   nor of the will of man, but of God. <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">And the Word was made flesh</span> (<i>here the people kneel down</i>), and dwelt   among us; and we saw his glory, the glory as it were of the only-begotten of   the Father, full of grace and truth.</p>   
 <p>R. Thanks be to God.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>  
 <p><i>While leaving the altar the priest says silently the antiphon </i>Trium puerórum<i>, &amp;c</i>.</p>
