   <h3 align="center">THE EIGHTH ERA.</h3>  
 <p>The child of Uli, of Uli of Ke,<br> The child in the time of numerous night,<br> The child in the time of riding distant surf in the night.<br> Beings were born to increase.<br> Male was born of Waiololi,<br> Female was born of Waiolola,<br> Then was born the night of gods,<br> Men that stood,<br> Of men lying down,<br> They slept long sleep in the distant time,<br> And went staggering when they walked.<br> The forehead of the gods is red. <a name="page_23"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 23</font></a><br> That of man is dark.<br> Their chins are light.<br> Then calmness spread in the time of Kapokinikini--<br> Calm in the time of Kapoheenalu mamao,<br> And it was called there Lailai.<br> Lailai was born a woman,<br> Kii was born a man.<br> Kane a god was born.<br> Kanaloa was born a god, the great Kaheehaunawela (Octopus).<br>                            ’Tis day.</p>  
 <p>The drums were born,<br> Called Moanaliha.<br> Kawaomaaukele came next.<br> The last was Kupololiilialiimuaoloipo,<br> A man of long life and very high rank.<br> O night! O God of Night!<br> O kupa, Kupakupa kupa, the settler!<br> Then Kupakupa the settler, the woman who sat sideway,<br> That woman was Lailai of the distant night,<br> Lailai, the woman. Kapokinikini<br> Dwelt with people of Kapokinikini.<br> Hahapoele was born a woman,<br> Hapopo was born a woman.<br> Maila was born and called Lopalapala (ingenious),<br> Her other name was void or nakedness,<br> And lived in the land of Lua (deep hole).<br> So the place was called Olohelohe lua.<br> Then Olohelohe was born in the day a man,<br> And Olohelohe was born at the time, a woman,<br> And lived with Kane.<br> Laiolo was born by Kane.<br> Kapopo was born a woman,<br> Poelei and Poelea were born twins.<br> After them was born Wehiloa.<br> From them these were born, <a name="page_24"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 24</font></a><br> The little beings who were cross-eyed,<br> That stood in numbers and moved in myriads.<br> These men that flew naked were the men of the day.<br>                             ’Tis day</p>
