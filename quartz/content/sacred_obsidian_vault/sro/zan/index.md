 <p>
 <cite>Zanoni</cite> introduced the concept of the
 wandering, eternal adepts into popular culture, with this tale of tragic love.
 Bulwer Lytton also wrote the fantasy
 <a href="../../atl/vril/index.htm">Vril, The Power of the Coming Race</a>,
 a prototype for fictions of lost civilizations to come.
 <cite>Zanoni</cite> had a huge influence on Theosophists
 and other 19th century occult groups.
