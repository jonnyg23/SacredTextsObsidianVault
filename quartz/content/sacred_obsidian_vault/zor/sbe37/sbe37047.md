 <p>1. The Vendîdâ<i>d</i> <a name="fr_361"></a><a href="#fn_359"><font size="1">2</font></a> <i>contains</i> particulars <i>of</i> Aûha<i>r</i>ma<i>z</i><i>d</i> <i>having</i> produced the pleasure of mankind by</p>

 <p><a name="page_153"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 153</font></a></p>  
 <p>that place where they specially make a residence, and the advantage from the same production <a name="fr_362"></a><a href="#fn_360"><font size="1">1</font></a>. 2. About the formation of sixteen perfect places specially enumerated, <i>and</i> also the adversity which has happened to each separately <a name="fr_363"></a><a href="#fn_361"><font size="1">2</font></a>.</p>  
 <p>3. About Aûha<i>r</i>ma<i>z</i><i>d</i>'s disclosing the religion first <i>among</i> mankind to Yim <a name="fr_364"></a><a href="#fn_362"><font size="1">3</font></a>; <i>its</i> non-acceptance by Yim owing to attachment (asrûnŏîh) <i>to</i> the religion of the ancients; <i>and</i> the acceptance <i>of</i> other <i>things</i> to develope, extend, and improve the world thereby.  <a name="fr_365"></a><a href="#fn_363"><font size="1">4</font></a>. About the reason of the needfulness <i>of</i> making the enclosure that Yim made (var-î Yim ka<i>r</i><i>d</i>), the command <i>and</i> instruction by Aûha<i>r</i>ma<i>z</i><i>d</i> to Yim, the making by Yim just as Aûha<i>r</i>ma<i>z</i><i>d</i> commanded <i>and</i> instructed, <i>and</i> whatever is on the same subject <a name="fr_366"></a><a href="#fn_364"><font size="1">5</font></a>.</p>  
 <p>5. About what the comfort of the spirit of the earth is most owing to, what <i>its</i> discomfort is more particularly owing to, and from what <i>its</i> greatest gratification has arisen <a name="fr_367"></a><a href="#fn_365"><font size="1">6</font></a>.</p>  
 <p>6. About the sin of pollution owing to carrying a corpse by a single person, <i>relating</i>, however, to that which a dog <i>has</i> not seen <a name="fr_368"></a><a href="#fn_366"><font size="1">7</font></a>. 7. About the food,</p>
