 <p><a name="page_263"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 263</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">CHAPTER XXXIV.</h3>  
 <p align="center"><a href="errata.htm#5"><i>Var</i>s<i>tmânsar</i></a> <i>Nask</i>.</p>  
 <p>. . . . . . . . . . . . . . it is possible to come through virtuous deeds <i>and</i> through virtuous thoughts.' <i>n</i>. And this, too, <i>he spoke</i>, namely: 'That Good Thought <a name="fr_941"></a><a href="#fn_934"><font size="1">1</font></a> of mine proceeds <i>and</i> notices the thoughts of the embodied existence, <i>and</i> of the good words and the deeds he reports again those referring to me, as often as three times in the same day, both <i>of those</i> who are liberal <i>to</i> thee <a name="fr_942"></a><a href="#fn_934"><font size="1">1</font></a>, <i>O</i> Spîtâmân! and <i>of those</i> who are illiberal to thee.'</p>  
 <p><i>o</i>. The struggling of the demons <a name="fr_943"></a><a href="#fn_935"><font size="1">2</font></a>, for the putting down of all benefit from mankind, <i>has</i> not produced the obtainment of their capability for that benefit which arises <i>for</i> mankind through the future existence; so that that one evil is more grievous than every evil which the demons imagine for mankind, when <i>the latter</i> are frightened by them from the way of the sacred beings, and are wicked; and harder for them <i>are</i> the praisers of righteousness among the apostates <i>and</i> the rest <i>of</i> the creation, through <i>their</i> praise of righteousness, even when very many praise <i>it</i>.</p>  
 <p><i>p</i>. About the progress of Ârmat <a name="fr_944"></a><a href="#fn_936"><font size="1">3</font></a> and Târôkmat perpetually among the creatures, the disclosure of Ârmat <i>to</i> mankind, and <i>of</i> righteousness <i>to</i> Târôkmat;</p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p><a name="page_264"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 264</font></a></p>  
 <p>the listening of that vile Târôkmat to falsehood, and the distance <a name="fr_945"></a><a href="#fn_937"><font size="1">1</font></a> <i>of</i> righteousness from him who is vile is like <i>that of</i> a sheep fled (sî<i>s</i>d) <a name="fr_946"></a><a href="#fn_938"><font size="1">2</font></a> from mankind. <i>q</i>. And this, too, that the evil spirit is beaten <a name="fr_947"></a><a href="#fn_939"><font size="1">3</font></a> by complete mindfulness, in the struggle of those having mighty ones, just as a powerful man beats him who is a reverent creation (nîyâyîn dahi<i>s</i>nŏ) <a name="fr_948"></a><a href="#fn_940"><font size="1">4</font></a>; <i>and</i> the pure Zaratû<i>s</i>t is produced <i>by</i> Aûha<i>r</i>ma<i>z</i><i>d</i>, <i>as well as</i> the power of Khû<i>r</i>da<i>d</i> <i>and</i> Amû<i>r</i>da<i>d</i> <a name="fr_949"></a><a href="#fn_941"><font size="1">5</font></a> which <i>acts</i> forcibly for giving value (far<i>g</i>) and preparing the creatures.</p>  
 <p><i>r</i>. About the opposition of Aûha<i>r</i>ma<i>z</i><i>d</i> to the demons <a name="fr_950"></a><a href="#fn_942"><font size="1">6</font></a>, and the valuation of the deeds of mankind <i>which</i> exist for greater jurisdiction <a name="fr_951"></a><a href="#fn_943"><font size="1">7</font></a> <i>and</i> more advantage of the primitive good creation; and in any doubtfulness <i>one is</i> to perform the ceremonial of the sacred beings. <i>s</i>. About <i>cases</i> where the good-will <i>of</i> the spirit of complete mindfulness makes mankind attain to the good religion; and their spiritual joy <a name="fr_952"></a><a href="#fn_944"><font size="1">8</font></a> arises from the purification of their own religion through virtuous exercise of will.</p>  
 <p><i>t</i>. About the desire <i>for</i> a reward <i>for</i> anything whatever, and the great advantage owing to a reward of the desires <i>of</i> mankind; <i>also</i> the appropriation of the reward through the operation of the sacred beings:'Even through the ruler (pa<i>d</i>) of that dominion of yours do I produce the renovation <i>of</i> the existences by <i>my</i> will <a name="fr_953"></a><a href="#fn_945"><font size="1">9</font></a>, I who am Aûha<i>r</i>ma<i>z</i><i>d</i>.'</p>  
 <p><i>u</i>. Excellence is righteousness <i>that is</i> perfect.</p>
