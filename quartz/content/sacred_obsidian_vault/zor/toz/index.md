 <p>
 This is a short study of the Zoroastrian religion, part of the
 Wisdom of the East series.
 S.A. Kapadia, who along with L. Cranmer-Byng, edited or wrote
 many of the books in the <a href="../../woe/index.htm">Wisdom of the East</a>,
 was a member of the Indian Zoroastrian community, and his
 personal insights into the religion are invaluable.
 </p>
