 <p><a name="page_88"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 88</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">CHAPTER XXIV.</h3>  
 <p>1. On the chieftainship of men and animals <i>and</i> every single thing it says in revelation, that first of the human species Gâyômar<i>d</i> was produced, brilliant and white, with eyes which looked out for the great <i>one</i>, him who <i>was</i> here the Zaratû<i>s</i>trôtûm (chief high-priest); the chieftainship of all things was from Zaratû<i>s</i>t <a name="fr_479"></a><a href="#fn_475"><font size="1">1</font></a>. 2. The white ass-goat <a name="fr_480"></a><a href="#fn_476"><font size="1">2</font></a>, which holds <i>its</i> head down, is the chief of goats, the first of those species created <a name="fr_481"></a><a href="#fn_477"><font size="1">3</font></a>. 3. The black sheep which is fat and white-jawed is the chief of sheep; it was the first of those species created <a name="fr_482"></a><a href="#fn_477"><font size="1">3</font></a>. 4. The camel with white-haired knees <i>and</i> two humps is the chief of camels. 5. First the black-haired ox with yellow knees was created; he is the chief of oxen. 6. First the dazzling white (arûs) horse, with yellow ears, glossy hair, <i>and</i> white eyes, was produced; he is the chief of horses. 7. The white, cat-footed <a name="fr_483"></a><a href="#fn_478"><font size="1">4</font></a> ass is the chief of asses. 8. First of dogs the fair (arûs) dog with yellow hair was produced; he is the chief of dogs. 9. The hare was produced brown</p>

 <p><span class="contnote"><font size="-2" color="GREEN">[paragraph continues]</font></span> (bûr); he is the chief of the wide-travellers. 10. Those beasts which have no dread whatever of the hand are evil. 11. First of birds the griffon of three natures <a name="fr_484"></a><a href="#fn_479"><font size="1">1</font></a> was created, not for here (this world), for the Kar<i>s</i>ipt <a name="fr_485"></a><a href="#fn_480"><font size="1">2</font></a> is the chief, which they call the falcon (<i>k</i>ark), that which revelation says was brought to the enclosure formed by Yim. 12. First of fur animals the white ermine was produced; he is the chief of fur animals; as it says that <i>it is</i> the white ermine which came unto the assembly of the archangels. 13. The Kar-fish, <i>or</i> Ari<i>z</i> <a name="fr_486"></a><a href="#fn_481"><font size="1">3</font></a>, is the chief of the water-creatures. 14. The Dâîtîk <a name="fr_487"></a><a href="#fn_482"><font size="1">4</font></a> river is the chief of streams. 15. The Dâra<i>g</i>a <a name="fr_488"></a><a href="#fn_483"><font size="1">5</font></a> river is the chief of exalted rivers, for the dwelling of the father of Zaratû<i>s</i>t was on its banks <a name="fr_489"></a><a href="#fn_484"><font size="1">6</font></a>, <i>and</i> Zaratû<i>s</i>t was born there. 16. The hoary forest <a name="fr_490"></a><a href="#fn_485"><font size="1">7</font></a> is the chief of forests. 17. Hûgar the lofty <a name="fr_491"></a><a href="#fn_486"><font size="1">8</font></a>, on which the water of Arêdvîvsûr flows <i>and</i> leaps, is the chief of summits, since <i>it is that</i> above which is the revolution of the constellation Satavês <a name="fr_492"></a><a href="#fn_487"><font size="1">9</font></a>, the chief of</p>

 <p>reservoirs <a name="fr_493"></a><a href="#fn_488"><font size="1">1</font></a>. 18. The Hôm which is out-squeezed is the chief of medicinal plants <a name="fr_494"></a><a href="#fn_489"><font size="1">2</font></a>. 19. Wheat is the chief of large-seeded <a name="fr_495"></a><a href="#fn_490"><font size="1">3</font></a> grains. 20. The desert wormwood is the chief of unmedicinal <a name="fr_496"></a><a href="#fn_491"><font size="1">4</font></a> <i>plants</i>. 21. The summer vetch, which they also call 'pag' (gâvirs), is the chief of small-seeded grains <a name="fr_497"></a><a href="#fn_492"><font size="1">5</font></a>. 22. The Kûstîk (sacred thread-girdle) is the chief of clothes. 23. The Bâzâyvâna <a name="fr_498"></a><a href="#fn_493"><font size="1">6</font></a> is the chief of seas. 24. Of two men, when they come forward together, the wiser and more truthful is chief.</p>  
 <p>25. This, too, it says in revelation, that Aûharmazd created the whole material world one abode, so that all may be one; for there is much splendour and glory of industry in the world. 26. Whatsoever he performs, who practises that which is good, is the value of the water of life <a name="fr_499"></a><a href="#fn_494"><font size="1">7</font></a>; since water is not created alike <a name="fr_500"></a><a href="#fn_495"><font size="1">8</font></a> in value, for the undefiled water of Arêdvîvsûr is worth the whole water of the sky and earth of Khvanîras <a name="fr_501"></a><a href="#fn_496"><font size="1">9</font></a>, except the Arag river <a name="fr_502"></a><a href="#fn_497"><font size="1">10</font></a>, created by Aûharmazd. 27.  <p>on which <i>model</i>, it is said, trees were formed, are worth all the trees of Khvanîras, except the Gôkar<i>d</i> tree <a name="fr_503"></a><a href="#fn_498"><font size="1">1</font></a> with which they restore the dead.</p>  
 <p>28. Of mountains Mount Apârsên's beginning is <i>in</i> Sagastân <i>and</i> end in Khû<i>g</i>îstân, some say <i>it is</i> all the mountains of Pârs, <i>and</i> is chief of all mountains except Albûr<i>z</i>. 29. Of birds <i>K</i>amrô<i>s</i> <a name="fr_504"></a><a href="#fn_499"><font size="1">2</font></a> is chief, who is worth all the birds in Khvanîras, except the griffon of three natures. 30. The conclusion is this, that every one who performs a great duty has then much value.</p>
