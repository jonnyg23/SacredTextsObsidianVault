 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Zend Avesta, Part III (SBE31)</i>, L.H. Mills, tr. [1886], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">VISPARAD XX (SP. XXIII) <a name="fr_1511"></a><a href="#fn_1494"><font size="1">3</font></a>.</h3>  
 <p>1. (Homage to the Gâtha Vohu-khshathra <a name="fr_1512"></a><a href="#fn_1495"><font size="1">4</font></a>! We sacrifice to the Vohu-khshathra), (the good kingdom) even the Khshathra-vairya, the kingdom to be desired; and we sacrifice to the iron-founding <a name="fr_1513"></a><a href="#fn_1496"><font size="1">5</font></a>, and to the (sacrificial) words <a name="fr_1514"></a><a href="#fn_1497"><font size="1">6</font></a> correctly spoken which smite (the foe) with victory, and which hold the Daêvas subject.</p>  
 <p>And we worship that reward and that health, that healing and that progress, that growth and that victorious smiting <a name="fr_1515"></a><a href="#fn_1498"><font size="1">7</font></a> (2) which are between the Vohu-khshathra and the Vahi<i>s</i>tôi<i>s</i>ti <a name="fr_1516"></a><a href="#fn_1499"><font size="1">8</font></a>, (and which are acquired by us) by the memorised recital of the good thoughts, good words, and good deeds, for the withstanding of evil thoughts, and words, and deeds; yea, for the undoing of all treacherous thoughts (directed) against me, and of all false words, and unfair deeds. 3. [And we sacrifice to the later Yasna, the heroic Haptanghâiti <a name="fr_1517"></a><a href="#fn_1500"><font size="1">9</font></a>, (and which as it recurs becomes) the holy ritual chief.]</p>
