 <p><a name="page_280"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 280</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">YASNA XXVII.</h3>  <h4 align="center">PRELUDE TO THE CHIEF RECITAL OF THE AHUNA-VAIRYA.</h4>  
 <p>1. This is to render <a name="fr_1180"></a><a href="#fn_1171"><font size="1">1</font></a> Him who is of all the greatest, our lord <a name="fr_1181"></a><a href="#fn_1172"><font size="1">2</font></a> and master <a name="fr_1182"></a><a href="#fn_1173"><font size="1">3</font></a> (even) Ahura Mazda. And this to smite <a name="fr_1183"></a><a href="#fn_1174"><font size="1">4</font></a> the wicked Angra Mainyu, and to smite Aêshma of the bloody spear, and the Mazainya Daêvas, and to smite all the wicked Varenya Daêvas.</p>  
 <p>2. And this is to further Ahura Mazda, the resplendent, the glorious, to further the Bountiful Immortals, and the influences of the star Ti<i>s</i>trya, the resplendent, the glorious, (and) to the furtherance of the holy man, and of all the (bountiful and) holy creatures of the Bounteous Spirit.</p>  
 <p>3-5 <a name="fr_1184"></a><a href="#fn_1175"><font size="1">5</font></a>. 6. The Haomas are crushed, O Mazda, Khshathra, and Asha, O ye Lords! Good is Sraosha who accompanies the sacrifice with the great glory <a name="fr_1185"></a><a href="#fn_1176"><font size="1">6</font></a>, and may he be present affording strenuous help.</p>  
 <p>7. We are offering saving acts of wisdom and of worship with the sacred gift of the Ahuna-vairya intoned with sanctity, and of the two mortars here</p>

 <p>brought forward <a name="fr_1186"></a><a href="#fn_1177"><font size="1">1</font></a> with holy act, and with that of the correctly uttered words likewise; and therefore may they be to us the more saving in their wise significance</p>  
 <p>8-12 <a name="fr_1187"></a><a href="#fn_1178"><font size="1">2</font></a>. 13. As the Ahû is excellent, so is the Ratu (one who rules) from (his) sanctity, a creator of mental goodness, and of life's actions done for Mazda; and the Kingdom (is) for Ahura, which to the poor may offer a nurturer. 14. (What is Your Kingdom, Your riches; how may I be Your own in my actions, to nourish Your poor, O Mazda? Beyond; yea, beyond all we declare You, far from Daêvas and Khrafstra-accursed mortals <a name="fr_1188"></a><a href="#fn_1179"><font size="1">3</font></a>!)</p>  
 <p>15. We worship the Ahuna-vairya. We worship Asha Vahi<i>s</i>ta, the most beautiful <a name="fr_1189"></a><a href="#fn_1180"><font size="1">4</font></a>, the Bountiful Immortal <a name="fr_1190"></a><a href="#fn_1181"><font size="1">5</font></a>.</p>
