LIX).</h3>  <h4 align="center">PRAYERS FOR THE DWELLING OF THE SACRIFICER <a name="fr_1303"></a><a href="#fn_1293"><font size="1">2</font></a>.</h4>  
 <p>1. Thus that better than the good may he approach, who shows to us straight paths of profit appertaining to this bodily life and to the mental likewise, in the eternal (?) realms where dwells Ahura; yea, may he approach it, who is Thy worthy servant, and good citizen, O Great giver Lord <a name="fr_1304"></a><a href="#fn_1294"><font size="1">3</font></a>!</p>  
 <p>2. May these blessings approach this house, which are the wise perceptions of the saints, the sacred blessings bestowed through the ritual, their guileless characteristics, together with their recognition of what is due; and may the Righteous Order appear for this village, and the Divine Sovereign Power, together with the benefit and glorious welfare (which ensues),</p>  
 <p>3. And with these the long enduring prominence of this Religion of Ahura's, the Zarathu<i>s</i>trian Faith. And may the Kine <a name="fr_1305"></a><a href="#fn_1295"><font size="1">4</font></a> be now with greatest speed within (the farm-yard of) this house, most speedily</p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p><a name="page_311"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 311</font></a></p>  
 <p>may the rewarded sanctity and the strength of the holy man be here, most speedily as well Ahura's lore. 4. And may the good and heroic and bountiful Fravashis of the saints come here, and may they go hand in hand with us with the healing virtues of (their) blessed gifts as wide-spread as the earth, as far-spread as the rivers, as high-reaching <a name="fr_1306"></a><a href="#fn_1296"><font size="1">1</font></a> as the sun, for the furtherance <a name="fr_1307"></a><a href="#fn_1297"><font size="1">2</font></a> of the better men, for the hindrance of the hostile, and for the abundant growth of riches and of glory.</p>  
 <p>5. May Sraosha (Obedience) conquer disobedience <a name="fr_1308"></a><a href="#fn_1298"><font size="1">3</font></a> within this house, and may peace triumph over discord here, and generous giving over avarice, reverence <a name="fr_1309"></a><a href="#fn_1298"><font size="1">3</font></a> over contempt, speech with truthful words over lying utterance. May the Righteous Order gain the victory over the Demon of the Lie <a name="fr_1310"></a><a href="#fn_1299"><font size="1">4</font></a>.</p>  
 <p>6. As in this (house) the Bountiful Immortals seek for good Yasnas and good praises from the blessed Sraosha (who governs here), and as they seek for (one) good sacrifice and act of homage (more especially their own) which is a good offering <a name="fr_1311"></a><a href="#fn_1300"><font size="1">5</font></a> (to them) for (our) salvation, and a good offering in praise, together with a long continued offering of the entire self <a name="fr_1312"></a><a href="#fn_1301"><font size="1">6</font></a>, (7) let not then (their) brilliant glory <a name="fr_1313"></a><a href="#fn_1302"><font size="1">7</font></a> ever desert this house, nor the bright abundance, nor an illustrious <a name="fr_1314"></a><a href="#fn_1303"><font size="1">8</font></a> offspring legitimately <a name="fr_1315"></a><a href="#fn_1304"><font size="1">9</font></a> born, nor that long continued companionship which is the</p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p><a name="page_312"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 312</font></a></p>  
 <p>furtherance of that good blessedness which teaches concerning glory <a name="fr_1316"></a><a href="#fn_1305"><font size="1">1</font></a>. 8-10 (= Y. VIII, 5-7).</p>  
 <p>11. In order that our minds may be <a name="fr_1317"></a><a href="#fn_1306"><font size="1">2</font></a> delighted, and our souls the best, let our bodies be glorified as well, and let them, O Mazda! go likewise openly (unto Heaven) as the best world <a name="fr_1318"></a><a href="#fn_1307"><font size="1">3</font></a> of the saints as devoted to Ahura, (12) and accompanied by Asha Vahi<i>s</i>ta (who is Righteousness the Best), and the most beautiful! And may we see Thee, and may we, approaching, come around about Thee, and attain to entire companionship with Thee! And we sacrifice to the Righteous Order, the best, the most beautiful, the bounteous Immortal!</p>
