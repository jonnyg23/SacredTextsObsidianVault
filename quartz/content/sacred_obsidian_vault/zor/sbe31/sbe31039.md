 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Zend Avesta, Part III (SBE31)</i>, L.H. Mills, tr. [1886], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">YASNA XVII.</h3>  <h4 align="center">TO THE FIRES, WATERS, PLANTS, &amp;c.</h4>  
 <p>1-10 <a name="fr_1090"></a><a href="#fn_1081"><font size="1">1</font></a>, 11. We worship thee, the Fire, O Ahura Mazda's son! We worship the fire Berezi-savangha (of the lofty use <a name="fr_1091"></a><a href="#fn_1082"><font size="1">2</font></a>), and the fire Vohu-fryâna (the good and friendly <a name="fr_1092"></a><a href="#fn_1083"><font size="1">3</font></a>), and the fire Urvâzi<i>s</i>ta (the most beneficial and most helpful <a name="fr_1093"></a><a href="#fn_1084"><font size="1">4</font></a>), and the fire Vâzi<i>s</i>ta (the most supporting <a name="fr_1094"></a><a href="#fn_1085"><font size="1">5</font></a>), and the fire Sp<i>e</i>ni<i>s</i>ta (the most bountiful <a name="fr_1095"></a><a href="#fn_1086"><font size="1">6</font></a>), and Nairya-sangha the Yazad of the royal lineage <a name="fr_1096"></a><a href="#fn_1087"><font size="1">7</font></a>, and that fire which is the house-lord of all houses and Mazda-made, even the son of Ahura Mazda, the holy lord of the ritual order, with all the fires.</p>  
 <p>12. And we worship the good and best waters Mazda-made, holy, all the waters Mazda-made and holy, and all the plants which Mazda made, and which are holy.</p>

 <p>13. And we worship the Mãthra-spe<i>n</i>ta (the bounteous word-of-reason), the Zarathu<i>s</i>trian law against the Daêvas, and its long descent.</p>  
 <p>14. And we worship Mount Ushi-darena which is Mazda-made and shining with its holiness, and all the mountains shining with holiness, and of abundant glory, and which Mazda made—.</p>  
 <p>15. And we worship the good and pious prayer for blessings, (16) and these waters and (these lands), (17) and all the greatest chieftains, lords of the ritual order <a name="fr_1097"></a><a href="#fn_1088"><font size="1">1</font></a>;</p>  
 <p>18. And I praise, invoke, and glorify the good, heroic, bountiful Fravashis of the saints, those of the house, the Vîs, the Zantuma, the Da<i>h</i><i>v</i>yuma <a name="fr_1098"></a><a href="#fn_1089"><font size="1">2</font></a>, and the Zarathu<i>s</i>trôtema, and all the holy Yazads <a name="fr_1099"></a><a href="#fn_1090"><font size="1">3</font></a>!</p>
