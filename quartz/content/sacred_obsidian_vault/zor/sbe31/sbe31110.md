 <p>1. I confess myself a Mazda-worshipper—for the praise of Thraêtaona, the Âthwyan. Let them declare it—Propitiation be to the Fravashi of Thraêtaona, the Âthwyan, the saint. 2. We sacrifice to Thraêtaona, the Âthwyan, the holy lord of the ritual order; and may we be free from the dog Kuro <a name="fr_1591"></a><a href="#fn_1563"><font size="1">3</font></a>, and the Tarewani <a name="fr_1592"></a><a href="#fn_1563"><font size="1">3</font></a>, and the Karpan, (we who are) of <a name="fr_1593"></a><a href="#fn_1564"><font size="1">4</font></a> those who sacrifice in order. 3.
