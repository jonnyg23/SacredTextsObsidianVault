 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Pahlavi Texts, Part II (SBE18)</i>, E.W. West, tr. [1882], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">CHAPTER XCII.</h3>  
 <p>1. <i>As to</i> the ninety-first question <i>and</i> reply, that which you ask is thus: Of waters and rivers, and whatever water is good, is Arekdvisûr <a name="fr_994"></a><a href="#fn_991"><font size="1">2</font></a> the greatest (mas), or <i>some</i> other water or good river; and, again, where is the place of Arekdvisûr?</p>  
 <p>2. The reply is this, that <i>it is</i> the water of Arekdvisûr; and <i>what has</i> gushed from Arekdvisûr is as large a mass as all the water in the world <a name="fr_995"></a><a href="#fn_992"><font size="1">3</font></a> except the Arvand <a name="fr_996"></a><a href="#fn_993"><font size="1">4</font></a>; within the wide-formed ocean <a name="fr_997"></a><a href="#fn_994"><font size="1">5</font></a> <i>it</i> is dominant over the thousand cascades (pashan) <i>and</i> thousand lakes <a name="fr_998"></a><a href="#fn_995"><font size="1">6</font></a> of the waters, and its place is most <i>renowned</i> throughout the spheres. 3. There flows the water of Arekdvisûr in a forest, the source of all seeds, whereby the species <i>which</i> plants possess are assimilated (aê<i>d</i>ûnagî<i>d</i>ŏ) by it, <i>and</i> healing existences of all kinds are mingled <i>with it</i> from medicinal plants. 4. The abundant power of the</p>

 <p>coming of healing to the purifying water is like the nature of the existences which it acquires, <i>and</i> then the nature which it thus acquires for its own the water draws up by the power which is drawing water to itself.</p>  
 <p>5. The water of Arekdvisûr is on Albûr<i>z</i> <a name="fr_999"></a><a href="#fn_996"><font size="1">1</font></a>, and flows even to the summit of the star station during the coming of the healing of purification, even unto Hûkhîr the lofty <a name="fr_1000"></a><a href="#fn_997"><font size="1">2</font></a>, all-gorgeous <i>and</i> brilliant; thence <i>its</i> flowing is effected <a name="fr_1001"></a><a href="#fn_998"><font size="1">3</font></a> into the lake of a summit to Albûr<i>z</i>, Mount <a href="errata.htm#1">Aûshin<i>d</i>ûm</a> <a name="fr_1002"></a><a href="#fn_999"><font size="1">4</font></a>, which is in the middle of the wide-formed ocean. 6. And from that flowing of waters that destined river, the utter destruction of every night, comes on in the light of a dawn; by the sprinkling of spray (pash-pashanŏ) it extends through the seven regions <i>of the earth</i>, and from it arise the growth of their plants and the coming of the healing of purification; that which is called a drop (srî<i>s</i>k) of the primeval creatures <i>being</i> a particle (aham) of water of the bulk of a horse <a name="fr_1003"></a><a href="#fn_1000"><font size="1">5</font></a>.</p>
