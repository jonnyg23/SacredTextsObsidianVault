 <p><a name="page_70"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 70</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">CHAPTER XXXIV.</h3>  
 <p>1. The sage asked the spirit of wisdom (2) thus: '<i>Has</i> the creator Aûha<i>r</i>ma<i>z</i><i>d</i> produced the creation of anything whatever for the worldly <i>existence</i> <a name="fr_377"></a><a href="#fn_372"><font size="1">1</font></a>, unto which Aharman is not able to bring disturbance?'</p>  
 <p>3. The spirit of wisdom answered (4) thus: 'To him who is a wise and contented man it is <i>but</i> little possible to bring disturbance.'</p>
