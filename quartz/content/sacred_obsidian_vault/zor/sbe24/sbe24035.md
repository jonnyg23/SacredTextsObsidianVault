 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Pahlavi Texts, Part III (SBE24)</i>, E.W. West, tr. [1885], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">CHAPTER XXIX.</h3>  
 <p>1. The sage asked the spirit of wisdom (2) thus: 'What is it necessary to keep with more regard and more protection?'</p>  
 <p><a name="page_67"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 67</font></a></p>  
 <p>3. The spirit of wisdom answered (4) thus: 'It is necessary to keep a young serving-boy (rasîk) <a name="fr_361"></a><a href="#fn_356"><font size="1">1</font></a>, a wife, a beast <i>of burden</i>, and a fire with more protection and more regard.'</p>
