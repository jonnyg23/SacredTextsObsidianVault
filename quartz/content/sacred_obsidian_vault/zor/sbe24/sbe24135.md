 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Pahlavi Texts, Part III (SBE24)</i>, E.W. West, tr. [1885], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">CHAPTER XLVII.</h3>  
 <p>1. The forty-seventh subject is this, that, when a child of seven years shall die, an order is necessary <i>that</i> it is requisite to perform a ceremony (ya<i>s</i>t) <i>for</i> Srôsh <a name="fr_1337"></a><a href="#fn_1326"><font size="1">1</font></a> on account of it, <i>and</i> to consecrate the sacred cake of the fourth night <a name="fr_1338"></a><a href="#fn_1327"><font size="1">2</font></a>.</p>  
 <p>2. For it says in revelation that the souls of children go with the souls of <i>their</i> fathers and mothers; if the father is <i>fit</i> for heaven <i>the child</i> goes to heaven with him, if he be <i>fit</i> for hell it arrives in hell; if the mother be <i>fit</i> for heaven it reaches heaven with her, if she be <i>fit</i> for hell it reaches hell with her <a name="fr_1339"></a><a href="#fn_1328"><font size="1">3</font></a>.</p>  
 <p>3. Therefore, every time that they accomplish the ceremony <i>for</i> Srôsh, the soul of that child becomes separated from the souls of <i>its</i> father and mother, and goes to heaven, and is imploring intercession (<i>s</i>ifâhat) for <i>its</i> father and mother in the presence of the sacred beings in that <i>other</i> world.</p>
