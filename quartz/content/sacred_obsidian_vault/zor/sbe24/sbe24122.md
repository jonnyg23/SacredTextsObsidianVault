 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Pahlavi Texts, Part III (SBE24)</i>, E.W. West, tr. [1885], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">CHAPTER XXXIV.</h3>  
 <p>1. The thirty-fourth subject is this, that it is greatly necessary to refrain from much slaughter of animals and the cattle species <a name="fr_1267"></a><a href="#fn_1257"><font size="1">1</font></a>. 2. Because it says in revelation <a name="fr_1268"></a><a href="#fn_1258"><font size="1">2</font></a> that, <i>for</i> every one who slaughters many animals <i>and</i> cattle <a name="fr_1269"></a><a href="#fn_1259"><font size="1">3</font></a>, every fibre of the hair of a goat becomes, in that <i>other</i> world, like a sharp sword, <i>and</i> adheres in the soul of that person.</p>  
 <p>3. And there are several things the slaughter of which is very bad, and the sin very abundant, <i>as</i> the lamb, the kid, the ploughing ox, the war horse, the swallow bird that catches the locust, and the cock; and of the whole of these the sin is most <i>as regards</i> the cock. 4. If it becomes a necessity <a name="fr_1270"></a><a href="#fn_1260"><font size="1">4</font></a>, it is proper to kill a cock <i>that</i> does not crow <a name="fr_1271"></a><a href="#fn_1261"><font size="1">5</font></a>, and it is necessary to consecrate their heads. 5. Any head of an animal, not consecrated, it is not desirable to eat, so that it becomes so far <a name="fr_1272"></a><a href="#fn_1262"><font size="1">6</font></a> a righteous gift. 6. If <i>one</i> be not able to consecrate the head, it is requisite to consecrate one kidney <i>as</i> a substitute (badal) for it.</p>
