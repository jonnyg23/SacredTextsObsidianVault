 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Pahlavi Texts, Part III (SBE24)</i>, E.W. West, tr. [1885], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">CHAPTER XXXVIII.</h3>  
 <p>1. The sage asked the spirit of wisdom (2) thus: 'Wherefore <i>is it</i> when they do not allot the happiness</p>  
 <p><a name="page_76"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 76</font></a></p>  
 <p>of the worldly <i>existence</i> according to worthiness, and they make the soul a seizer upon the spiritual <i>existences</i> by worthiness of action?'</p>  
 <p>3. The spirit of wisdom answered (4) thus: 'On account of the compassion of Aûha<i>r</i>ma<i>z</i><i>d</i>, the lord, as regards the creatures, he allots all happiness alike among the good and alike among the bad. 5. But when it does not always come upon them, <i>it is</i> on account of the oppression of Aharman and the demons, and the extortion of those seven planets <a name="fr_405"></a><a href="#fn_400"><font size="1">1</font></a>.</p>  
 <p>6. 'And they make <i>one</i> <a name="fr_406"></a><a href="#fn_401"><font size="1">2</font></a> a seizer upon the spiritual <i>existences</i>, by worthiness of action, on this account, because the wickedness of any <a name="fr_407"></a><a href="#fn_402"><font size="1">3</font></a> one arises through the performance of his own actions.'</p>
