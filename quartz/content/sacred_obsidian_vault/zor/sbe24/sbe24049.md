 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Pahlavi Texts, Part III (SBE24)</i>, E.W. West, tr. [1885], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">CHAPTER XLIII.</h3>  
 <p>1. The sage asked the spirit of wisdom (2) thus: 'How is it possible to make Aûha<i>r</i>ma<i>z</i><i>d</i>, the archangels, and the fragrant, well-pleasing heaven more fully for oneself? 3. And how is it possible to make Aharman, the wicked, and the demons confounded, and to escape from hell, the depreciated <a name="fr_443"></a><a href="#fn_438"><font size="1">2</font></a> and dark?'</p>  
 <p>4. The spirit of wisdom answered (5) thus: 'To make Aûha<i>r</i>ma<i>z</i><i>d</i>, the lord, and the archangels, and</p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p><a name="page_84"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 84</font></a></p>  
 <p>the fragrant, well-pleasing heaven for oneself, and <a name="fr_444"></a><a href="#fn_439"><font size="1">1</font></a> Aharman, the wicked, and the. demons confounded, and to escape from hell, the dark and depreciated, are possible thus: (6) that is, when they make the spirit of wisdom a protection for the back (pû<i>s</i>tîkpânakîh), (7) and wear the spirit of contentment on the body, like arms and armour and valour, (8) and make the spirit of truth <a name="fr_445"></a><a href="#fn_440"><font size="1">2</font></a> a shield, (9) the spirit of thankfulness a club, (To) the spirit of complete mindfulness a bow, (11) and the spirit of liberality an arrow; (12) and they make the spirit of moderation like a spear, (13) the spirit of perseverance a gauntlet, and they put forth the spirit of destiny as a protection <a name="fr_446"></a><a href="#fn_441"><font size="1">3</font></a>. 14. In this manner it is possible to come to heaven and the sight of the sacred beings, and to escape from Aharman, the wicked, and hell, the depreciated.'</p>
