 <p>
 This is a Theosophical manual of the phenomena of Clairvoyance, the mental
 viewing of things far removed in space and/or time.
 Leadbeater appeals to the fourth dimension, the Akashic record,
 and the assistance of unseen spirits, as explanations of the phenomena.
 Students of the occult will find this a satisfying review, but more
 rationalist minded investigators will find this more of a
 <i>counter-example</i> of how to research psi.
 </p>
