 <p><a name="page_145"><font size="1" color="green">p. 145</font></a></p>  <h4 align="center"><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">The Seventh Tablet of the Story of the Creation</span></h4>  
 <p align="center"><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Obverse</span></p>  
 <p>1. At that time the gods in their assembly created (the beasts).<br> 2. They made perfect the mighty (monsters).<br> 3. They caused the living creatures (of the field) to come forth,<br> 4 the cattle of the field, (the wild beasts) of the field and the creeping things (of the field).<br> 5. (They fixed their habitations) for the living creatures (of the field).<br> 6. They distributed <a name="fr_291"></a><a href="#fn_289"><font size="1">1</font></a> (in their dwelling-places) the cattle and the creeping things of the city.<br> 7, (They made strong) the multitude of creeping things, all the offspring (of the earth).<br> 8. …… in the assembly of my family.<br> 9. …… <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Ea</span> the god of the illustrious face.<br> 10. … the multitude of creeping things did I make strong.<br> 11. … the seed of <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Lakhama</span> did I destroy.</p>  
 <p align="center"><i>The rest is lost</i>.</p>
