 <p><a name="page_109"><font size="1" color="green">p. 109</font></a></p>  <h4 align="center">XI. <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Inscriptions of Dungi, King of Ur</span></h4>  
 <p align="center">No. 1.—<span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Inscription On A Tablet</span> <a name="fr_257"></a><a href="#fn_257"><font size="1">1</font></a></p>  
 <p>1. For the god <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Nin-girsu</span>,<br> 2. the powerful warrior<br> 3. of the god <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Ellilla</span>,<br> 4. for his king,<br> 5. Dungi<br> 6. the puissant prince,<br> 7. the king of <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Ur</span>, <a name="fr_258"></a><a href="#fn_258"><font size="1">2</font></a><br> 8. the king of <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Shumer</span> and <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Accad</span>, <a name="fr_259"></a><a href="#fn_259"><font size="1">3</font></a><br> 9. the temple of <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">E-ninnû</span><br> 10. his favourite temple<br> 11. has constructed.</p>  
 <p align="center">No. 2.—<span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Inscription on a Tablet</span> <a name="fr_260"></a><a href="#fn_260"><font size="1">4</font></a></p>  
 <p>1. For the goddess <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Ninâ</span>,<br> 2. the lady of destinies (?),<br> 3. the lady of oracles (?),<br> 4. for his mistress,<br> 5. Dungi<br> 6. the puissant prince,<br> 7. the king of <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Ur</span>,<br> 8. the king of <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Shumer</span> and <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Accad</span>,<br> 9. the temple of <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">E-shish-shish-e-ma-ra</span>,<br> 10. her favourite temple,<br> 11. has constructed.</p>
