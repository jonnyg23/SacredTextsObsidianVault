 <p><a name="page_106"><font size="1" color="green">p. 106</font></a></p>  <h4 align="center">VIII. <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Inscriptions of Ur-nin-girsu</span> <a name="fr_249"></a><a href="#fn_249"><font size="1">1</font></a></h4>  
 <p align="center">No. 1.—<span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Inscription on a Brick</span> <a name="fr_250"></a><a href="#fn_250"><font size="1">2</font></a></p>  
 <p>1. Ur-nin-girsu,<br> 2. the priest of the god <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Anna</span>,<br> 3. the priest of the god <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">En-ki</span>, <a name="fr_251"></a><a href="#fn_251"><font size="1">3</font></a><br> 4. the favourite priest of the goddess <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Ninâ</span>.</p>  
 <p align="center">No. 2.—<span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Inscription on a Brick</span> <a name="fr_252"></a><a href="#fn_252"><font size="1">4</font></a></p>  
 <p>1. To the god <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Nin-girsu</span>,<br> 2. the powerful warrior<br> 3. of the god <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Ellilla</span>,<br> 4. for his king,<br> 5. Ur-nin-girsu,<br> 6. the patesi<br> 7. of <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Shirpurla</span><br> 8. the son of Gudea,<br> 9. the patesi<br> 10. of <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Shirpurla</span><br> 11. who the temple of <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">E-ninnû</span><br> 12. of the god <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Nin-girsu</span><br> 13. has constructed.<br> 14. His favourite <i>gigunû</i> <a name="fr_253"></a><a href="#fn_253"><font size="1">5</font></a><br> 15. of cedar-wood<br> 16. he has constructed for him.</p>
