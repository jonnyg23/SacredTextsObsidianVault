 <p><a name="page_27"><font size="1" color="green">p. 27</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">THE SYNCHRONOUS HISTORY OF ASSYRIA AND <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Babylonia</span></h3>  <h4 align="center"><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Obverse</span></h4>  <h5 align="center">COLUMN I.—<span style="font-variant:small-caps;">The Commencement is destroyed</span></h5>
