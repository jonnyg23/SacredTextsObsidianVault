 <p><a name="page_7"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 7</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">ADDENDA ET CORRIGENDA.</h3>  
 <p>P. 5 (Preface), <i>l</i>. 13, after "metre" add "except the twelfth which contains about half that number."</p>  
 <p>, <i>l</i>. 15, read "name."</p>  
 <p>, <i>l</i>. 36, and pp. 4349, Siduri should perhaps be better described as "a seller of wine." Mr. Gadd suggests to me the old word "ale-wife."</p>  
 <p>P. 11, <i>l</i>. 48, read "(and)."</p>  
 <p>P. 15, add to note 1 "Or should the line be restored from Col. VI, <i>ll</i>. 16-17?"</p>  
 <p>, <i>l</i>. 16, delete square bracket before "[all]."</p>  
 <p>P. 18, <i>l</i>. 14, delete dash at end of line.</p>  
 <p>P. 22, <i>l</i>. 5, add "(forsooth)," after the dash.</p>  
 <p>, <i>l</i>. 21, read "friend."</p>  
 <p>P. 27, <i>l</i>. 46, read "Nippur."</p>  
 <p>P. 31, <i>l</i>. 3, read "seven mutilated."</p>  
 <p>P. 51, <i>l</i>. 87, for "2" read "3."</p>
