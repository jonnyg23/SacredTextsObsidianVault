 <h3 align="CENTER">CATHEDRAL ROCKS</h3>
 <p>Above the Bridal Veil Fall towers Cathedral Rocks, one of the salient points of this end of the Valley, holding for the real cliff lover a personality and attractiveness all their own, though dwarfed by their proximity to the overshadowing hulk of El Capitan.</p>
