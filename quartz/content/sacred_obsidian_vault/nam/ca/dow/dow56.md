 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Dawn of the World</i>, by C. Hart Merriam, [1910], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">A HOLE IN THE NOSE SAVES TURNING INTO A FISH</h3>  
 <p><i>The Southern Mewuk of the Mariposa region say</i>:</p>  
 <p>If a person dies without a hole in his nose he will turn into a fish, but if the nose is perforated</p>  
 <p><a name="page_219"><font size="1" color="green">p. 219</font></a></p>  
 <p>for the <i>kun-no-wah</i> <a name="fr_23"></a><a href="#fn_23"><font size="1">25</font></a> he will not turn into a fish.</p>  
 <p>

 <p><a name="fn_23"></a><a href="dow56.htm#fr_23">219:25</a> The <i>kun-no-wah</i> is a short white rod of shell or stone worn in the nose by both men and women of this tribe.</p>
