 <p><i>The Olayome of Putah Creek say</i>:</p>  
 <p>Under the earth is a great giant named <i>He-wow'-wah-tin</i>.</p>  
 <p><a name="page_224"><font size="1" color="green">p. 224</font></a></p>  
 <p><span class="contnote"><font size="-2" color="GREEN">[paragraph continues]</font></span> When angry he shakes the earth, causing <i>Yo'-wan-hew'-wah</i> the Earthquake.</p>
