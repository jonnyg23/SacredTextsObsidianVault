 <p><a name="page_125"><font size="1" color="green">p. 125</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">NEK'-NA-KA'-TAH THE ROCK MAIDEN</h3>  
 <p>IN the mountains among the rocks by the river lives <i>Nek'-na-kā'-tah</i>, the little rock girl. She is herself a rock and always lives in rocky places by the river. In some way she produces or gives off people; these people are hard like rocks and you can not cut them or shoot them with an arrow.</p>  
 <p>A long time ago <i>Oo-soo'-ma-te</i> the Grizzly Bear and <i>Hoi-yah'-ko</i> the FIRST PEOPLE, made the Chaw'-se or mortar holes in the big flat-topped rocks. Then <i>Nek'-na-kā'-tah</i> the rock maiden came and helped make the <i>Kah-wah'-che</i> or stone pestles for the people to pound acorns with.</p>
