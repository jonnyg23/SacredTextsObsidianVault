 <h3 align="CENTER">BETTING EYES <a name="fr_172"></a><a href="#fn_171"><font size="1">1</font></a></h3>
 <p>Spider and Dung Beetle were playing, and they bet their eyes upon the game. Therefore Spider has four eyes and Beetle none.</p>
