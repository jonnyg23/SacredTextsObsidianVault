 <h3 align="CENTER">THE SONG OF NOOEE WHEN HE WENT TO THE SUN</h3>
 <p align="CENTER">The Rising (Sun) I am going to meet.</p>
 <p align="CENTER">(Repeated many times)</p>
 <h3 align="CENTER">WHEN NOOEE <a href="errata.htm#18">KILLED</a> EE-EE-TOY <a name="fr_3"></a><a href="#fn_3"><font size="1">1</font></a></h3>
 <p align="CENTER">(A Song)</p>
 <p>The gun, he gave it to me as a cane;<br>
 With it I killed the Brother's heart.</p>
 <p>
 </p>
 <hr>
 <h3 align="CENTER">Footnotes</h3>
 <p><a name="fn_3"></a><a href="ain23.htm#fr_3">131:1</a> The reference to the "gun" shows clearly that this song was made after the advent of the white man.</p>
