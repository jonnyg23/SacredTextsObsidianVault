 <h3 align="CENTER">PLATE 7</h3>
 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_pl07a"></a>
 </p><p align="CENTER">
 <a href="img/pl07a.jpg"><img src="tn/pl07a.jpg" alt="Plate 7, Figure 1"><br><font size="-3">Click to enlarge</font></a><br>Plate 7, Figure 1<br></p>
 <p>FIGURE 1. Chakoya katsina. Mask black bordered with brown; eye-slits yellow; blue feathers from duck head around the mouth; teeth of corn husks; beard of human hair. A skirt of an entire buckskin hanging almost to the feet will be worn.</p>
 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_pl07b"></a>
 </p><p align="CENTER">
 <a href="img/pl07b.jpg"><img src="tn/pl07b.jpg" alt="Plate 7, Figure 2"><br><font size="-3">Click to enlarge</font></a><br>Plate 7, Figure 2<br></p>
 <p>FIGURE 2. Shumaashka, Corn clan katsina. Squash blossom (red) on side; horn frame (green) strung with cotton; blue (left side of mask) for sky; yellow (right side) for earth; median line represents a 4-day trail with black and white for night and day; sash collar (white) with pendant ornaments (blue and red).</p>
