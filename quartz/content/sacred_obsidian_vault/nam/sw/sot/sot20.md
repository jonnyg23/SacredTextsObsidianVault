 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>Songs of the Tewa</i>, by Herbert Joseph Spinden, [1933], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h5 align="center">XVII</h5>  <h5 align="center">THE RABBIT BY THE RIVER</h5>  
 <p>There by the river runs a little rabbit.<br> Why did you not catch him?<br> Why did you not kill him?<br> We feel like doing just that!<br> Bent over like a little old man<br> Off he goes with a watermelon;<br> Bent over like a little old man<br> Off he goes with a muskmelon!</p>
