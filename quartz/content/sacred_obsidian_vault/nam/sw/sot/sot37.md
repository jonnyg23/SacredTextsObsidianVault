 <p><a name="page_97"><font size="1" color="green">p. 97</font></a></p>  <h5 align="center">XXXIII</h5>  <h5 align="center">SONGS IN THE TURTLE DANCE AT SANTA CLARA</h5>  
 <p><i>First song</i></p>  
 <p>Long ago in the north<br> Lies the road of emergence!<br> Yonder our ancestors live,<br> Yonder we take our being.<br> Yet now we come southwards<br> For cloud flowers blossom here<br> Here the lightning flashes,<br> Rain water here is falling!</p>  
 <p><i>Second song</i></p>  
 <p><i>A ha haya, ehe he,<br> A ha haya, ehe he!</i><br> Fog clouds and cloud flowers<br> On various mountains lie,<br> Cloud flowers are blooming now<br> <i>Te he kwa, te he kwa!</i><br> From the north the lightning<br> Now makes flashes,<br> The thunder rumbles now,<br> Rain water now is falling!<br> <i>A ha we ahe, aha we ahe,<br> Aha a a a, e e he e heye!</i></p>
