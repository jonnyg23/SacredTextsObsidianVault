 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>Apu Ollantay</i>, by Clements Markham, [1910], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> 
 <p><a name="page_337"><font size="1" color="green">p. 337</font></a></p> <h1 align="CENTER">OLLANTAY</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">ACTS AND SCENES</h3> 
 <p>ACT 1. Sc. 1.--<i>Open space near Cuzco</i>.<br>                     Ollantay, Piqui Chaqui, Uillac Uma.<br>          Sc. 2.--<i>Hall in the Colcampata</i>.<br>                     Anahuarqui, Cusi Coyllur, Inca Pachacuti, Boys and Girls, Singers.<br>          Sc. 3.--<i>Hall in the Inca's palace</i>.<br>                     Pachacuti, Rumi-ñaui, Ollantay.<br>          Sc. 4.--<i>Height above Cuzco</i>.<br>                     Ollantay, Piqui Chaqui, Unseen Singer.<br>          Sc. 5.--<i>Hall in the Inca's palace</i>.<br>                     Pachacuti, Rumi-ñaui, and a Chasqui.</p> 
 <p>ACT II. Sc. 1.--<i>Ollantay-tampu Hall</i>.<br>                     Ollantay, Urco Huaranca, Hanco Huayllu, People and Soldiers.<br>          Sc. 2.--<i>A wild place in the mountains</i>.<br>                     Rumi-ñaui's soliloquy.<br>          Sc. 3.--<i>Gardens of the Virgins</i>.<br>                     Yma Sumac, Pitu Salla, Mama Ccacca.</p> 
 <p>ACT III. Sc. 1.--<i>Pampa Maroni at Cuzco</i>.<br>                     Uillac Uma and Piqui Chaqui.<br>          Sc. 2.--<i>Palace of Tupac Yupanqui</i>.<br>                     Tupac Yupanqui, Uillac Uma, Rumi-ñaui.</p> 
 <p><a name="page_338"><font size="1" color="green">p. 338</font></a></p> 
 <p>         Sc. 3.--<i>Ollantay-tampu, Terrace</i>.<br>                     Rumi-ñaui, Ollantay, Guards.<br>          Sc. 4.--<i>House of Virgins, Corridor</i>.<br>                     Yma Sumac, Pitu Salla.<br>          Sc. 5.--<i>House of Virgins, Garden</i>.<br>                     Yma Sumac, Pitu Salla, Cusi Coyllur.<br>          Sc. 6.--<i>Palace of Tupac Yupanqui</i>.<br>                     Tupac Yupanqui, Uillac Uma, a Chasqui, Rumi-ñaui, Ollantay, Urco Huaranca, Hanco Huayllu, Piqui Chaqui, Chiefs and Guards. then Yma Sumac.<br>          Sc. 7.--House of Virgins, Garden.<br>                     All of Scene 6, and Mama Ccacca, Cusi Coyllur, Pitu Salla.</p>
