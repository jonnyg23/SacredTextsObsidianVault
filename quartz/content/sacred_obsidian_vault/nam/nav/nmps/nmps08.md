 <a name="page_61"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 61</font></a><h1 align="CENTER">PROTECTION SONG.</h1>
 <p>(To be sung on going into battle.)</p>
 <h3 align="CENTER">I.</h3>
 <p>Now, Slayer of the Alien Gods, among men am I.<br>
 Now among the alien gods with weapons of magic am I.<br>
 Rubbed with the summits of the mountains,<br>
 Now among the alien gods with weapons of magic am I.<br>
 Now upon the beautiful trail of old age,<br>
 Now among the alien gods with weapons of magic am I.</p>
 <h3 align="CENTER">II.</h3>
 <p>Now, Offspring of the Water, among men am I.<br>
 Now among the alien gods with weapons of magic am I.<br>
 Rubbed with the water of the summits,<br>
 Now among the alien gods with weapons of magic am I.<br>
 Now upon the beautiful trail of old age,<br>
 Now among the alien gods with weapons of magic am I.</p>
 <h3 align="CENTER">III.</h3>
 <p>Now, Lightning of the Thunder, among men am I.<br>
 Now among the alien gods with weapons of magic am I.<br>
 Rubbed with the summit of the sky,<br>
 Now among the alien gods with weapons of magic am I.<br>
 Now upon the beautiful trail of old age,<br>
 Now among the alien gods with weapons of magic am I.</p>
 <h3 align="CENTER">IV.</h3>
 <p>Now, Altsodoniglehi, among men am I.<br>
 Now among the alien gods with weapons of magic am I.<br>
 Rubbed with the summits of the earth,<br>
 Now among the alien gods with weapons of magic am I.<br>
 Now upon the beautiful trail of old age,<br>
 Now among the alien gods with weapons of magic am I.</p>
