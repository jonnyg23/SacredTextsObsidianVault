 <p align="center"><b>ANCIENT INCA</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Beautiful Princess<br> Lo, thy brother<br> Breaks thy vessel<br> Now in fragments.<br> From the blow comes<br> Thunder, lightning,<br> Strokes of lightning.<br> And thou, princess,<br> Take the water;<br> As the rain, fall;<br> As the hail, fall;<br> As the snow, fall;<br> Viracocha<br> World-dispenser.</div>
