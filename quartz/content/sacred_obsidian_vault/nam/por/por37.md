 <p><a name="page_129"><font size="1" color="green">p. 129</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">SONGS FROM CALIFORNIA</h3>  <h3 align="center">SONGS OF SPIRITS</h3>  <h4 align="center">WINTU</h4>  
 <p align="center"><b>I</b></p>  
 <p align="center"><b>LIGHTNING</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">I bear the sucker-torch to the western tree-ridge.<br> Behold me! first born and greatest.</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>II</b></p>  
 <p align="center"><b>OLELBIS (THE CREATOR)</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">    I am great above.<br>     I tan the black cloud.</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>III</b></p>  
 <p align="center"><b>HAU (RED FOX)</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">On the stone ridge east I go.<br> On the white road I, Hau, crouching go.<br> I, Hau, whistle on the road of stars.</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>IV</b></p>  
 <p align="center"><b>POLAR STAR</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">The circuit of earth which you see,<br> The scattering of stars in the sky which you see,<br> All that is the place for my hair.</div>
