 <p><a name="page_130"><font size="1" color="green">p. 130</font></a></p>  <h4 align="center">SONGS OF KUMASTAMXO</h4>  
 <p align="center"><b>YUMA</b></p>  
 <p align="center"><b>I</b></p>  
 <p align="center"><b>WHEN THE SPIRIT-WIND APPROACHED</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">"The Wind is wandering, is wandering;<br> The Wind is wandering, is wandering."</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>II</b></p>  
 <p align="center"><b>WHEN HE BURNT THE UNCLEAN HOUSE</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">"The house will burn, will burn.<br> The house will be crackling, will be crackling.<br> It will blaze.<br> We are going to dance.<br> It is going to be lighted.<br> It is going to be lighted.<br> It will blaze.<br> We are going to dance.<br> Something bird-like is coming. <a name="fr_3"></a><a href="#fn_3"><font size="1">*</font></a>  
 <p>Bird-like tracks will be about the place.<br> We are going to light this unclean house.<br> It will blaze, blaze."</p></div>  
 <p align="center"><b>III</b></p>  
 <p align="center"><b>WHEN HE MADE THE RIVER</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">    "This is my water, my water.<br>     This is my river, my river.<br>     We love its water. </div>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p><a name="page_131"><font size="1" color="green">p. 131</font></a></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">We love its foamwood.<br> It shall flow forever.<br> It shall flow forever.<br> When the weather grows hot, it shall rise and overflow its banks.<br> It shall flow forever."</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>IV</b></p>  
 <p align="center"><b>AFTER HE HAD FINISHED HIS CREATING</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">"Into the earth I go down, go down.<br> Nothing but earth will I be seeing, will I be seeing.<br> I sink down into the old river-bed,<br> Down into the interior."</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>V</b></p>  
 <p align="center"><b>WHEN HE BECAME THE EAGLE</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">    "I am springing, springing.<br>     Wing-feathers!<br>     Body-feathers!<br>     On my hands wing-feathers.<br>     On my body body-feathers."</div>
