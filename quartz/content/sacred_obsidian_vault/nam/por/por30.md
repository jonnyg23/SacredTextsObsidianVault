 <p><a name="page_118"><font size="1" color="green">p. 118</font></a></p>  <h4 align="center">SONG OF THE EARTH</h4>  
 <p align="center"><b>NAVAJO</b></p>  
 <p>      </p><div style="margin-left: 32px">All is beautiful,<br>       All is beautiful,<br>       All is beautiful, indeed.</div>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Now the Mother Earth<br> And the Father Sky,<br>     Meeting, joining one another,<br>     Helpmates ever, they.<br>       All is beautiful,<br>       All is beautiful,<br>       All is beautiful, indeed.</div>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Sisnajinni,<br> Tsodsichl,<br>     Meeting, joining one another,<br>     Helpmates ever, they.<br>       All is beautiful,<br>       All is beautiful,<br>       All is beautiful, indeed.</div>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Now Doko-oslid<br> And Depenitsa,<br>     Meeting, joining one another,<br>     Helpmates ever, they.<br>       All is beautiful,<br>       All is beautiful,<br>       All is beautiful, indeed.</div>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">And the night of darkness<br> And the dawn of light,<br>     Meeting, joining one another,<br>     Helpmates ever, they.<br>       All is beautiful,<br>       All is beautiful,</div>  
 <p><a name="page_119"><font size="1" color="green">p. 119</font></a></p>  
 <p>      </p><div style="margin-left: 32px">All is beautiful, indeed.</div>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Now Hastyeyalli<br> And Hastyehogan,<br>     Meeting, joining one another,<br>     Helpmates ever, they.<br>       All is beautiful,<br>       All is beautiful,<br>       All is beautiful, indeed.</div>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">And the white corn<br> And the yellow corn,<br>     Meeting, joining one another,<br>     Helpmates ever, they.<br>       All is beautiful,<br>       All is beautiful,<br>       All is beautiful, indeed.</div>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">And the corn-pollen<br> And the Ripener,<br>     Meeting, joining one another,<br>     Helpmates ever, they.<br>       All is beautiful,<br>       All is beautiful,<br>       All is beautiful, indeed.</div>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Life-that-never-passeth,<br> Happiness-of-all-things,<br>     Meeting, joining one another,<br>     Helpmates ever, they.<br>       All is beautiful,<br>       All is beautiful,<br>       All is beautiful, indeed.</div>  
 <p>      </p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Now all is beautiful,<br>       All is beautiful,<br>       All is beautiful, indeed.</div>
