 <p><a name="page_161"><font size="1" color="green">p. 161</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">ESKIMO SONGS</h3>  <h4 align="center">OXAITOQ'S SONG</h4>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">I walk so long thus<br>     inland, inland.<br> I walk so long thus<br>     inland, inland, and inland.<br> I am not loved<br>     inland, inland.<br> I am not loved<br>     she is the greatest of all<br>     inland and inland.<br> They love best<br>     inland, inland.<br> They love best<br>     what I obtain<br>     inland and inland.<br> They love best<br>     inland, inland.<br> They love best<br>     the food that I bring<br>     inland and inland.</div>  <h4 align="center">SUMMER SONG</h4>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Ajaja, it is pleasant,<br>     it is pleasant at last<br>     the great world<br>     when it is summer at last.<br> Ajaja, it is pleasant,<br>     it is pleasant at last<br>     the great world<br>     when our caribous begin to come.<br> Ajaja, they make great noise,</div>  
 <p><a name="page_162"><font size="1" color="green">p. 162</font></a></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">    they make great noise,<br>     the brooks there in our country<br>     when it is summer.<br> Ajaja, this great water<br>     has spread over the ice;<br>     I cannot walk<br>     to the rock across there.<br> Ajaja, I feel sorry for them,<br>     I feel sorry for them,<br>     not being able to speak,<br>     these gulls.<br> Ajaja, I feel sorry for them,<br>     I feel sorry for them,<br>     not being able to speak,<br>     these ravens.<br> A great animal comes now;<br>     no one observes it;<br>     I keep it secret;<br>     the ravens do not tell.<br> Food like that I cannot obtain.<br>     but quickly I got<br>     little sculpins.<br> Ajaja, he has found a smooth slope,<br>     he has found a smooth slope,<br>     to burrow into,<br>     the bad old fox.</div>
