 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Path on the Rainbow</i>, edited by George W. Cronyn, [1918], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h4 align="center">PRAYER TO THE MOUNTAIN SPIRIT</h4>  
 <p align="center"><b>FROM THE NAVAJO</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Lord of the Mountain,<br> Reared within the Mountain<br> Young Man, Chieftain,<br> Hear a young man's prayer!<br> Hear a prayer for cleanness.<br> Keeper of the strong rain,<br> Drumming on the mountain;<br> Lord of the small rain<br> That restores the earth in newness;<br> Keeper of the clean rain,<br> Hear a prayer for wholeness,  
 <p>Young Man, Chieftain,<br> Hear a prayer for fleetness.</p></div>  
 <p><a name="page_187"><font size="1" color="green">p. 187</font></a></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Keeper of the deer's way,<br> Reared among the eagles,<br> Clear my feet of slothness.<br> Keeper of the paths of men,<br> Hear a prayer for straightness.  
 <p>Hear a prayer for courage.<br> Lord of the thin peaks,<br> Reared amid the thunders;<br> Keeper of the headlands<br> Holding up the harvest,<br> Keeper of the strong rocks<br> Hear a prayer for staunchness.</p>  
 <p>Young Man, Chieftain,<br> Spirit of the Mountain!</p></div>
