 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Path on the Rainbow</i>, edited by George W. Cronyn, [1918], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">SONGS OF THE CHIPPEWA</h3>  
 <p align="center"><b>HEALING SONG</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">They are in close consultation<br>     with their heads together<br>     Wenabojo<br>     and his grandmother.</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>LOVE-CHARM</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">What are you saying to me?<br> I am arrayed like the roses<br>     and beautiful as they.</div>  
 <p><a name="page_14"><font size="1" color="green">p. 14</font></a></p>  
 <p align="center"><b>I AM WALKING</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Toward calm and shady places<br>     I am walking<br>     on the earth.</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>THEY THINK ME UNWORTHY</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">They think me unworthy<br>     my Midé brethren<br>     but look and see<br>     the length of my wigwam.</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>THE SOUND IS FADING AWAY</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">The sound is fading away<br> It is of five sounds<br>     freedom<br> The sound is fading away<br> It is of five sounds.</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>DOCTOR'S SONG</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">I am singing and dreaming in my poor way<br>     over the earth<br>     I who will again disembark<br>     upon earth.</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>SONG OF THE TREES</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">The wind<br>     only<br>     I am afraid of.</div>  
 <p><a name="page_15"><font size="1" color="green">p. 15</font></a></p>  
 <p align="center"><b>SONG OF THE THUNDERS</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Sometimes<br>     I go about pitying<br>     myself<br>     while I am carried by the wind<br>     across the sky.</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>THE APPROACH OF THE STORM</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">From the half<br>     of the sky<br>     that which lives there<br>     is coming, and makes a noise.</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>I AM AFRAID OF THE OWL</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Very much<br>     also<br>     I<br>     of the owl<br>     am afraid<br>     whenever I am sitting alone<br>         in the wigwam.</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>I AM AS BRAVE AS OTHER MEN</b></p>  
 <p>Men who are brave and heroic<br>     as you esteem them to be<br> Like them<br>     I also<br>     consider myself to be.</p>  
 <p><a name="page_16"><font size="1" color="green">p. 16</font></a></p>  
 <p align="center"><b>MY MUSIC REACHES TO THE SKY</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">My music<br>          reaches<br>         to the sky.</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>FAREWELL TO THE WARRIORS</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Come<br>     it is time for you to depart<br> We are going on a long journey.</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>SONG OF THE MAN WHO WAS TO BE HUNG</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">The thunders will take me home,<br>     whenever I mind to go home,<br>     my friends,<br>     and the wind<br>     it will take me home, too.</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>FRIENDLY SONG</b></p>  
 <p align="center"><b>I</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Over there<br>     in the sky<br>     they have taken pity on me.</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>II</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">The sky<br>     loves to hear me.</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>GAMING SONG</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">I will go home<br>     if I am beaten<br>     after more articles<br>     to wager.</div>  
 <p><a name="page_17"><font size="1" color="green">p. 17</font></a></p>  
 <p align="center"><b>WAR SONGS</b></p>  
 <p>At Co’goben's<br>     village<br>     they are weeping<br>     the men</p>  
 <p>At Co’goben's<br>     village<br>     they are wailing<br>     the women.</p>  
 <p>I feel no fear<br>     when the Great River man<br>     death<br>     speaks of.</p>  
 <p align="center"><b>THE MAN WHO STAYED HOME</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Although<br>     Jinwábe<br>     considers himself<br>     a man<br>     his wife<br>     certainly<br>     takes all his attention.</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>ARROW SONG</b></p>  
 <p>Scarlet<br>     is its head</p>  
 <p align="center"><b>SONG OF THE BUFFALO</b></p>  
 <p>Strike ye<br>     our land<br>     with curved horns</p>  
 <p><a name="page_18"><font size="1" color="green">p. 18</font></a></p>  
 <p align="center"><b>SONG OF THE MINISINOWUCK</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">It is uncertain what will happen<br>     to the one from whom I fly<br> It is uncertain what will happen<br>     to the one from whom I rise.</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>DRUM SONG</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">I make them dance<br>     those brave men<br> those brave men <br>     every one of them</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>SONG OF WOMAN-OF-THE-RED-SKY, WHO WENT WITH HER HUSBAND, SINGING INTO BATTLE</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">At that time<br>     if I had been a man<br>     truly<br>     a man<br>     I would have seized.</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>DEATH SONG OF NAMEBINES</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">The odor of death<br> I discern the odor of death<br>     in the front of my body</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>THE SIOUX WOMEN GATHER UP THEIR WOUNDED</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">The Sioux women<br>     pass to and fro wailing<br>     as they gather up<br>     their wounded men<br> The voice of their weeping comes back<br>     to us.</div>  
 <p><a name="page_19"><font size="1" color="green">p. 19</font></a></p>  
 <p align="center"><b>THEY ARE PLAYING A GAME</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">The noise of passing feet<br>     on the prairie<br> They are playing a game as they come<br>     those men.</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>SCALP SONG</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">I wonder<br>     if she is humiliated<br>     the Sioux woman<br>     that I cut off her head.</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>SONG OF REJOICING</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">It shall be<br>     that I rejoice<br> O, my son<br>     your elder brother<br>     you have brought back<br> O, my son<br>     it shall be<br>     that I rejoice<br> O, my son.</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>DEATH SONG</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Is there anyone who<br>     would weep for me?<br> My wife<br>     would weep for me.</div>  
 <p align="center">SONG OF THE BUTTERFLY</p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">In the coming heat<br>     of the day<br>     I stood there.</div>  
 <p><a name="page_20"><font size="1" color="green">p. 20</font></a></p>  
 <p align="center"><b>MAPLE SUGAR</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Maple sugar<br>     is the only thing<br>     that satisfies me.</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>A SONG OF SPRING</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">As my eyes<br>     search<br>     the prairie<br>     I feel the summer in the spring.</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>CARRIED AROUND THE SKY</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">As the wind is carrying me<br>     around the sky.</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>THE SKY WILL RESOUND</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">It will resound finely<br>     the sky<br>     when I come making a noise.</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>AN OVERHANGING CLOUD</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">An overhanging<br>     cloud<br>     repeats my words with pleasing sound.</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>HEAPS OF CLOUDS</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Great heaps<br>     of clouds<br>     in the direction I am looking.</div>  
 <p><a name="page_21"><font size="1" color="green">p. 21</font></a></p>  
 <p align="center"><b>THE NOISE OF THE VILLAGE</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Whenever I pause<br>     the noise<br>     of the village.</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>MIDÉ BURIAL SONG</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Neniwá<br>     let us stand<br>     and you shall see<br>     my body<br>     as I desire.</div>  <h4 align="center">LOVE SONGS</h4>  
 <p align="center"><b>MY LOVE HAS DEPARTED</b></p>  
 <p align="center"><b>I</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">A loon<br>     I thought it was<br>     but it was<br>     my love's<br>     splashing oar.</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>II</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">To Sault Ste. Marie<br>     he has departed<br> My love has gone on before me,<br> Never again<br>     can I see him.</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>THE GENEROUS ONE</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Why should<br>     I, even I</div>  
 <p><a name="page_22"><font size="1" color="green">p. 22</font></a></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">be jealous<br> because of that bad boy?</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>RECOVERY</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Do not weep<br> I am not going to die.</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>LOVE-HURT</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Although he said it<br>     still<br> I am filled with longing<br> when I think of him.</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>GLAD-PARTING</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Come<br>     I am going away<br> I pray you<br>     let me go<br> I will soon return<br> Do not<br>     weep for me<br> Behold<br>     we will be very glad<br>     to meet each other<br>     when I return<br> Do not<br>     weep for me.</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>INVITATION</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">My sweetheart<br>     a long time<br>     I have been waiting for you</div>  
 <p><a name="page_23"><font size="1" color="green">p. 23</font></a></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">to come over<br>     where I am.</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>YOU DESIRE VAINLY</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">You desire vainly<br>     that I seek you<br>     the reason is<br>     I come<br>     to see your younger sister.</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>HE IS GONE</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">I might grieve<br> I am sad<br>     that he has gone<br>     my lover.</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>SONG OF AN AMBITIOUS MOTHER</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">I am asking for<br>     Bugac's<br>     daughter<br> My big<br>     brass kettle<br>     he is giving.</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>NONSENSE SONG OF THE GAME OF SILENCE</b></p>  
 <p><i><div style="margin-left: 32px">(If you speak or laugh you are defeated)<br> It is hanging<br>     in the edge of the sunshine<br> It is a pig I see<br>     with its double (cloven) hoofs<br> It is a very fat pig.<br> The people who live in a hollow tree<br>     are fighting</div></i></p>  
 <p><a name="page_24"><font size="1" color="green">p. 24</font></a></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">They are fighting bloodily<br> He is rich<br> He will carry a pack toward the great water.<br> <i>(The rabbit speaks)</i><br> At the end of the point of land<br> I eat the bark off the tree<br> I see the track of a lynx<br> I don't care, I can get away from him<br> It is a jumping trail<br> sep!</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>WORK STEADILY</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Be very careful<br>     to work steadily<br> I am afraid they will take you away from me.</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>I HAVE FOUND MY LOVER</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Oh<br>     I am thinking<br> Oh<br>     I am thinking<br>     I have found .<br>     my lover<br> Oh<br>     I think it is so.</div>  <h4 align="center">MIDÉ SONGS</h4>  
 <p align="center"><b>THESE REPRESENT THE EXPRESSION OF RELIGIOUS IDEAS</b></p>  
 <p align="center"><b>I</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Is it that<br>     which my voice resembles?</div>  
 <p><a name="page_25"><font size="1" color="green">p. 25</font></a></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Even metal<br>     the sounding of my voice?</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>II</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">From beneath the high hill<br>     my voice echoes forth.</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>III</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">The strength of metal<br>     has entered into my arrow point.<br> A spirit<br>     I could kill.<br> The strength of iron<br>     has entered into my arrow point.</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>IV</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Beautiful as a star hanging in the sky<br>     is our Midé lodge.</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>V</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">I have gained such spirit-power<br>     that I can tame it in my hand<br> It is true<br>     even our white shell<br>     I can tame it in my hand.</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>VI</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Do not speak ill of the Midé<br>     my Midé brethren.<br> Wherever you may be<br>     do not speak ill of a woman<br>     my Midé brethren.</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>VII</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">We may live by it always<br>     my Midé brethren<br> It is spiritual<br>     the inspiration we receive.</div>  
 <p><a name="page_26"><font size="1" color="green">p. 26</font></a></p>  
 <p align="center"><b>VIII</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">In the middle of the sea<br>     in the lengthy room of the sea<br>     there I am standing.</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>IX</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">I that hasten around<br> I shoot at a man and he falls in a trance<br> Then I feel with my hand<br>     to see if he is still alive.</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>X</b></p>  
 <p>My Midé brother<br>     is searched<br> In his heart is found<br>     that which I seek to remove<br>     a white shell.</p>  
 <p align="center"><b>XI</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Into thy body<br>     I shoot<br>     the spirit</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>XII</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">What is this<br>     I promise you?<br> The skies shall be bright and clear for you<br> This is what I promise you.</div>
