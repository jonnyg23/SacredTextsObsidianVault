 <h3 align="CENTER">Origin of the Perch. <a name="fr_22"></a><a href="#fn_22"><font size="1">3</font></a></h3>
 <p>Then he saw a perch. "Don't come near me on the water. You are secular." <a name="fr_23"></a><a href="#fn_23"><font size="1">4</font></a>--"I am really the greatest thrower of all, <a name="fr_24"></a><a href="#fn_24"><font size="1">5</font></a> Ô'lalahahë'", great friend!" Thus he said to him. "You are a great shaman," said Q!â'nêqi?la<u>x</u><sup>u</sup> to him. "Hê'hehehe! you only say that I sing my sacred shaman's song. I feel only glad on the water because it is calm."--"Come, friend, and let us meet," said Q!â'nêqi?la<u>x</u><sup>u</sup> to him. The perch came ashore, and his cedar-bark head-ring</p>
