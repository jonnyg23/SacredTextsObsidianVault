 <h3 align="CENTER">Greedy-One steals Crabapples.</h3>
 <p>Then he went search in of food. He found Crabapple-Owner. Crabapple-Owner was climbing about. Then he became a child again. "Wuâ', wuâ', wuâ'!" said Greedy-One. Then the woman saw the child.
