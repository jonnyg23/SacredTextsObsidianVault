 <h3 align="CENTER">Q!â'nêqi?la<u>x</u><sup>u</sup> begins his wanderings. <a name="fr_9"></a><a href="#fn_9"><font size="1">4</font></a></h3>
 <p>"Don't go away!" said Q!â'nêqi?la<u>x</u><sup>u</sup> to Only-One. "I shall go to see the child of Gwâ'?nâlâlis." Q!â'nêqi?la<u>x</u><sup>u</sup> started. He saw Shaman-Woman. Q!â'nêqi?la<u>x</u><sup>u</sup> was afraid of Shaman-Woman, and he went past inland from Shaman-Woman. Q!â'nêqi?la<u>x</u><sup>u</sup> started, and he saw</p>
 <p>
 </p>
 <p><a name="page_195"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 195</font></a></p>
 <p>something that was wrong. It was not a real man. It was not a real man, what was seen by Q!â'nêqi?la<u>x</u><sup>u</sup>. He started, and he saw <strike>L</strike>Exyâ'lik*a?layû. He was afraid of him, and went past inland from <strike>L</strike>Exyâ'lik*a?layû. Q!â'nêqi?la<u>x</u><sup>u</sup> started. He was Greatest-Shaman at Having-Dead-Ones, the place of Greatest-Shaman. He passed inland from Greatest-Shaman.</p>
