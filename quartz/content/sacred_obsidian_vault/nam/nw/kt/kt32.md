 <h3 align="CENTER">Q!â'nêqi?la<u>x</u><sup>u</sup> makes a House for Only-One. <a name="fr_7"></a><a href="#fn_7"><font size="1">2</font></a></h3>
 <p>Q!â'nêqi?la<u>x</u><sup>u</sup> saw much dirt. He took it and made a house. He finished the house. Then he took water and blew it on the house. Then the house made of dirt became large. It was the house of Only-One. He took cedar-sticks and put them up outside the house, and Q!â'nêqi?la<u>x</u><sup>u</sup> took many men and put them inside as posts. <a name="fr_8"></a><a href="#fn_8"><font size="1">3</font></a></p>
