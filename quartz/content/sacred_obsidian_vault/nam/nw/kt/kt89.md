 <h1 align="CENTER">47. Q!ê'x*Lala.</h1>
 <p align="CENTER">Tradition of the <u>L</u>ê'<u>Lg</u>êd, a Clan of the ?wâ'las Kwâ'g*u<strike>l</strike>.</p>
 <p>The ancestor of the was <u>L</u>ê'<u>Lg</u>êd was Q!ê'x*Lala. He came into existence at TSEdâ'sas in Hardy Bay, when the world was first lighted. He wore a very large head-ring, so that it had to be supported on each side. As a shaman his name was Hai'alik*ila<strike>l</strike>. His son was Hë'lik*illg*imis, whose son was Hê'x*ak*in<u>î</u>s. His son was Awâ'lasEla<strike>l</strike>, whose son was TsEx?wî'd. He died recently.</p>
