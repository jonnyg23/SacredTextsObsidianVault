 <font face="Times Roman,Times New Roman"><p align="CENTER">(Masset Dialect.)</p>

 <h3 align="CENTER">101.</h3>
 <font face="Times Roman,Times New Roman"><p>Â'gua Sk<u>î</u>'lsîs l'nagâ'-i <sup>?</sup>ai'ya <u>g</u>a<u>g</u>ô'das hô.<br>
 Here | Sk<u>î</u>'lsîs's | town | lies.<br>
 DAñ gua gû'la dô'na-i (repeated four times).<br>
