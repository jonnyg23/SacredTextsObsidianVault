 <h3 align="CENTER">100. <a name="fr_97"></a><a href="#fn_91"><font size="1">1</font></a></h3>
 <font face="Times Roman,Times New Roman"><p>DalA'ñ gu k<u>î</u>'lsta-us dî L!ao gAm kî'lstastA dî gudA'ñhAñgAn.<br>
 Your | ? | voices are tired, | I | but | not | have a tired voice | I | want to.</p>
 <p>Your voices are tired, but I do not want to have a tired voice (i. e., I do not want to cease wailing).</p>
