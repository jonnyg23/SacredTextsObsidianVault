 <h3 align="CENTER">6.</h3>
 <font face="Times Roman,Times New Roman"><p>A+ya+ña'+ ayâ'ñê â'+yañô.<br>
 Be careful, | be careful, | be careful.<br>
 La hao îldja'oga+n.<br>
 He | is a nobleman.</p>
 <p><a name="page_9"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 9</font></a></p>
 <p>I'+lgiañ wAga'ñ(añ) kû'+skî+twas sê+ Lû tcî'nañ qôniga'-i<br>
 (Face) changed | like it | will be wherever your place is | his grand-father | powerful<br>
     gî a la qea<strike>l</strike>dî+ga.<br>
     for | he looks expectantly.<br>
 Ayâ'ñô a o <u>î</u>ldja'oga+n.<br>
 Be careful, this is a nobleman.</p>
 <p>Be careful (of the child), be careful, be careful.<br>
 This is going to be a great man.<br>
 His face will be changed wherever he may be, when he<br>
 looks for the coming of his powerful grandfather <a name="fr_6"></a><a href="#fn_6"><font size="1">1</font></a> (and sees him).<br>
 Be careful, this is going to be a great man.</p>

 <h3 align="CENTER">Footnotes</h3>
 <p><a name="fn_6"></a><a href="hs008.htm#fr_6">9:1</a> Skedans and his people called Djê'basa, the Tsimshian chief at Kitkatla, "grand father," and vice versa. After he has become a man, the child's face will look joyous when he sees his Tsimshian friends approach.</p>
