 <p>Are you crying for it, chief?<br>
 Are you crying for it, chief?<br>
 Are you crying for your sisters, that you be seated up higher (on a pile of blankets)?<br>
 For your cousins, that there be people in front of you as numerous as if people made sea-gulls cry, being obliged to step on them,--<br>
 For those things are you crying here, chief?</p>
