 <p>Perhaps when the chief-woman's fathers return from being angry with the Bella Bella people, that one (captured slave) next the fire will take care of you while you are lying about.<br>
 For what do you cry as you sit like a noble's child?</p>
 <p>Perhaps when the chief-woman's fathers return from being angry with the Ninstints people, that one next to the door will take care of you while you are lying about.<br>
 For what do you cry as you sit like a noble's child?</p>
