 <h3 align="CENTER">18.</h3>
 <font face="Times Roman,Times New Roman"><p>HalA' ga'g<u>î</u>ñ gao-o (eight times)<br>
 Come, | let her sit on my lap (or "let us have her")<br>
 Ga ga'os wa<u>L</u>û'xAn la gu'tgi gagaga'ñgao gu'tgî ska'ndî<u>g</u>o <strike>l</strike>A.<br>
 The villages | all | to each other | hand (her) | to each other hand.<br>
 <strike>L</strike>a s<u>g</u>un ga'g<u>î</u>ñûgwâ'ñ, <strike>l</strike>a sgun ga'g<u>î</u>ñûgwâ'ñ<br>
 I | only | take care of her now, | I | only | take care of her now.<br>
 HalA' ga'g<u>î</u>ñ gao-o (several times).</p>
 <p>come, let her sit on my lap.<br>
 Come, let her sit on my lap! (eight times)<br>
 All the villages used to hand her to one another.<br>
 Now only I take care of her, now only I take care of her. <a name="fr_24"></a><a href="#fn_20"><font size="1">1</font></a><br>
 Come, let her sit on my lap! (several times)</p>
