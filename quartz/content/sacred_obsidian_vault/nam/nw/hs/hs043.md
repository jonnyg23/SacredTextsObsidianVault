 <h3 align="CENTER">41.</h3>
 <font face="Times Roman,Times New Roman"><p>HalA' gagî'ñ gu, halA' gagî'ñ gu,<br>
 Come, | let us take (the baby) on our knees | there; come, | let us take (the baby) on our knees | there.<br>
 <u>G</u>ô'ñ<u>g</u>a na'<u>g</u>a qA<strike>l</strike>i gutgî <u>g</u>a<u>g</u>aga'ñ, gutgî <u>g</u>a<u>g</u>aga'ñ.<br>
 Its father's | house | inside | to each other | hand it, | to each other | hand it.<br>
 HalA' gagî'ñ gu, halA' gagî'ñ gu,<br>
 Come, | let us take it on our knees, | come, | let us take it on our knees.</p>
 <p>Come, let us take (the baby) on our knees! Come, let us take (the baby) on our knees!<br>
 Hand it to one another inside of its father's house, hand it to one another!<br>
 Come, let us take it on our knees! Come, let us take it on our knees!</p>
