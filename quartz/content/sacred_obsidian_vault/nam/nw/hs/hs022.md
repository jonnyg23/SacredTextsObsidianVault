 <h3 align="CENTER">20.</h3>
 <font face="Times Roman,Times New Roman"><p>L!a s<u>g</u>A'nxAn L! <u>g</u>êidAñ sû'ga. L!a s<u>g</u>A'nxAn hao <u>g</u>êidAñ <br>
 Those | only | are that way, | they | say. Those | only | are that way,<br>
     L! sû'ga.<br>
     they say.<br>
 GAm iL! La geitgâ'ñañ L! sû'ga.<br>
 Not | (with) us | however | it was that way | they | say.</p>
 <p>They alone belong to a high family, they say. They alone belong to a high family, they say.<br>
 But it is not that way with us, they say.</p>
