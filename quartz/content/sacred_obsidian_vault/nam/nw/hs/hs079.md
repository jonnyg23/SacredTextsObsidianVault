 <h3 align="CENTER">77.</h3>
 <font face="Times Roman,Times New Roman"><p>Â'gua nâ'nAñ Lga gut dî'nañ ku-i-<u>ê</u>'ndalane.<br>
 It was | his grand-mother's | land | upon | my child | walks (a proud word).<br>
 Wa'<u>g</u>an st!a'<u>g</u>a kudja'oanê.<br>
 For it | his foot | is dear.<br>
 GAm <strike>l</strike>a ku'ñg<u>î</u>ñañ Añ.<br>
 Not | you | cry (excl.)!</p>
 <p>My child walks proudly upon his grandmother's land.<br>
 His dear foot is for that (i. e., to walk on it).<br>
 Do not cry!</p>
