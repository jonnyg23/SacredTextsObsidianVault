 <h3 align="CENTER">90.</h3>
 <font face="Times Roman,Times New Roman"><p>Qeda'o gu wa qeda'o gu wa, gada'-i <strike>l</strike>i qêga'na.<br>
 War men | ? | killed <a name="fr_89"></a><a href="#fn_83"><font size="1">2</font></a> you, |war men | ? | killed dear daughter | I | bore you <br>
 Qeda'o gwa wa, qêda'o gwa wa.<br>
 War men | ? | killed you, | war men | ? | killed you.</p>
 <p>Did warriors kill you, did warriors kill you, dear daughter that I bore?<br>
 Did warriors kill you, did warriors kill you?</p>
