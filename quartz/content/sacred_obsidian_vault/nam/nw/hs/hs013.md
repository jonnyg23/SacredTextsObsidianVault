 <p>What are you for, what are you for?<br>
 You are to have a supernatural helper.<br>
 What are you for, what are you for?<br>
 You will not let canoes pass in front of you. <a name="fr_16"></a><a href="#fn_12"><font size="1">1</font></a><br>
 That is what you are for.<br>
 What are you for, what are you for?<br>
 You are to have a supernatural helper.</p>
