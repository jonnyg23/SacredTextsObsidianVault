 <h3 align="CENTER">42.</h3>
 <font face="Times Roman,Times New Roman"><p>Hao da'lAñ La'a hao da'lAñ La tcîna'-i lanâ'ga <a name="fr_53"></a><a href="#fn_48"><font size="1">2</font></a> gut gîda<br>
 You | you | grand-father's | town | upon | chiefs' children<br>
     gAn<strike>l</strike><u>g</u>a<strike>l</strike>diâ'n,<br>
     walking about,</p>
 <p>
 </p>
 <p><a name="page_32"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 32</font></a></p>
 <p>DalA'n s<u>g</u>un ya'dañs gî'tsîs, dalA'ñ ya'daga.<br>
 You (are the) | only (ones) | fit to be | chiefs' children, | you | are fit to be.<br>
 Sqada'ls dalA'ñ ya'da<u>g</u>a,<br>
 Great ones | you | are fit to be,<br>
 G<u>î</u>ts<u>î</u>'s dalA'ñ ya'da<u>g</u>a.<br>
 Chiefs' children | you | are fit to be.</p>
 <p>You walk about as chiefs' children in your grandfather's town.<br>
 Only you are fit to be chiefs' children.<br>
 You are fit to be sqada'l (close relatives of chiefs).<br>
 You are fit to be chiefs' children.</p>
