 <h3 align="CENTER">58.</h3>
 <font face="Times Roman,Times New Roman"><p>Îhî îhî hî, etc.<br>
 Gû'gus t!a'<u>g</u>ahas dînA'ñ kûñg<u>î</u>ñâ'ñodi<u>g</u>añ?<br>
 What | for | my child | sits crying,<br>
 Gu'lxas t!a'<u>g</u>a gwA dînA'ñ kuñg<u>î</u>ña'ño?<br>
 Abalone-shells for my child cries?<br>
 DAñ gaña'ñ <u>g</u>wA dî k<u>î</u>lsLia'wus qâñ <strike>l</strike> qê'gAn.<br>
 You | like | ? | I | sit a chief, | my uncle | I | bore.</p>
 <p><a name="page_41"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 41</font></a></p>
 <p>Îhî îhî hî, etc.<br>
 For what does my child cry?<br>
 Does my child cry for abalone-shells?<br>
 Like you I sit as a chief, uncle <a name="fr_65"></a><a href="#fn_60"><font size="1">1</font></a> that I bore?</p>
