 <h3 align="CENTER">Song used by the <sup>?</sup>ao sL!an l'nagâ'-i in making Peace.</h3>
 <h3 align="CENTER">105.</h3>
 <font face="Times Roman,Times New Roman"><p>Xâ'<u>L</u>a gwai'yê <u>g</u>ô'<strike>l</strike><u>g</u>a<strike>l</strike> q!ê'aosgi<u>ê</u>n.<br>
 Haidas' | island | green | has become.</p>
 <p>The island of the Haida has become green (i. e. the hats have appeared as when spring comes and the foliage turns green.)</p>
