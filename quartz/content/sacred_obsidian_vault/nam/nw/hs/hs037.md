 <h3 align="CENTER">35.</h3>
 <u><font face="Times Roman,Times New Roman"><p>L</p></font></u>û'gua nAñ na'n<u>g</u>a sL!a-i dja'kia q<u>î</u>ndjâ'waxañ: wagi<br>
 At that time | someone's | grandmother | hand | wooden tray with square sides | has been hurt, I hear: | for it<br>
 gag<u>î</u>ña'ñ, wagi <u>g</u>â'<strike>l</strike><u>g</u>a<strike>l</strike>wa'ñ q!oldjida, q!oldjida, gadja'o<br>
 (you) cry, | for it | (you) sit and move around (i. e., the body), | chief-woman | chief-woman, | sit and move <a name="fr_45"></a><a href="#fn_41"><font size="1">1</font></a>
 <p>Perhaps you are crying and are moving around for your grandmother's hand, which was hurt on a wooden tray, chief-woman, chief-woman.</p>
