 <h3 align="CENTER">28. <a name="fr_38"></a><a href="#fn_34"><font size="1">1</font></a></h3>
 <font face="Times Roman,Times New Roman"><p>Giä'<u>L</u>û Xâ'na qâ'<strike>l</strike>i, giä'<u>l</u>û Xâ'na qâ'<strike>l</strike>i,<br>
 At the time | Skidegate | Inlet, | at the time | Skidegate | Inlet,<br>
 DA'ñ<strike>l</strike>iai dâ L!da'o<u>g</u>o-u<strike>l</strike>aiya,<br>
 To swell up | you | burst (on that day),<br>
 S<u>g</u>a<strike>l</strike>ê <u>î</u>stâ'û<strike>l</strike>i kî'lsLa-i (four times).<br>
 Secret Society | having one day [or morning], | chief.<br>
 Ga-i <strike>l</strike>A <u>î</u>'stâ-û<strike>l</strike> kî'lsLa-i (four times).<br>
 That | do one day (again), | chief.<br>
 Ga'odjaos ga'-i<strike>l</strike>a <u>î</u>stâ'û<strike>l</strike> kî'lsLa-i.<br>
 Drum (town) | at that place | do it some day, | chief.</p>
 <p>Skidegate Inlet, Skidegate Inlet.<br>
 When you burst with swelling,<br>
 You had the Secret Society perform one day, chief.<br>
 Do it again, chief!<br>
 Do it one day at Drum Town, chief! <a name="fr_39"></a><a href="#fn_35"><font size="1">2</font></a></p>
