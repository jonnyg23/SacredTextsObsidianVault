 <h3 align="CENTER">SECTION 79</h3>
 <p>"Now another message.</p>
 <p>"This will happen.</p>
 <p>"You will sing three times and the third time you sing you will step into oyâ?dedion'diade?, the other world. <a name="fr_75"></a><a href="#fn_75"><font size="1">1</font></a> That you go there will be the earnest wish of all who have heard your message."</p>
 <p>So they said and he said. Eniaiehuk.</p>
 <p>

 <h3 align="CENTER">Footnotes</h3>
 <p><a name="fn_75"></a><a href="cohl090.htm#fr_75">60:1</a> It was customary for the friends and relatives to address the body of the dead and give expression to one's desires, etc. The soul when it reached the heaven-world would then tell the Great Ruler who would attend to the wishes expressed.</p>
