 <h3 align="CENTER">SECTION 118</h3>
 <p>"So they took up their journey again and after a ways the four messengers said, 'We have arrived at the point where you must return. Here there is a house prepared for your eternal abode but should you now enter a room you could never go back to the earth-world.'"</p>
 <p>So they said. Eniaiehuk.</p>
