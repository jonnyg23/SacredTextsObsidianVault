 <h3 align="CENTER">DESWADENYATIONDOTTÛ?, THE WOMAN'S SOCIETY</h3>
 <p>This society preserves the ritual by which good fortune and health are obtained for women. The singers, fourteen in number at Cattaraugus, are all men. During their singing the women dance. The office of chief singer is hereditary. The women join in a chorus as the men sing. Horn rattles and water drums are used.</p>
