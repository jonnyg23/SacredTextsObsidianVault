 <h3 align="CENTER">SECTION 39</h3>
 <p>"'Now another message to tell your people.</p>
 <p>"'We, the messengers of the Creator, are of the opinion that the world will continue for three generations longer (or three hundred years). <a name="fr_43"></a><a href="#fn_43"><font size="1">1</font></a> Then will Gai'wiio` be fulfilled.'"</p>
 <p>So they said and he said. Eniaiehuk.</p>
