 <h3 align="CENTER">SECTION 71</h3>
 <p>"Now another message.</p>
 <p>"Now we think that a time will come when a great plague will kill man people and no one will know its cause. Then will you know that the end is near and those who do not believe will suffer great hardships."</p>
 <p>So they said and he said. Eniaiehuk.</p>
