 <h3 align="CENTER">SECTION 126</h3>
 <p>"Now then Ganio`dai'io` started on his journey and a large number followed him that they might hear him speak. They had no conveyances but traveled afoot.</p>
 <p>"Now when they came to their camping spot at Ganowa'gês, <a name="fr_93"></a><a href="#fn_93"><font size="1">2</font></a> he said to them in a commanding voice, 'Assemble early in the morning.' Now when they did he offered thanks and afterward he said, 'I have had a dream, a wondrous vision. I seemed to see a pathway, a trail overgrown and covered with grass so that it appeared not to have been traveled in a long time.' Now no one spoke but</p>
 <p>
 </p>
 <p><a name="page_79"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 79</font></a></p>
 <p>when all had heard and he had finished they dispersed and they continued on their journey."</p>
