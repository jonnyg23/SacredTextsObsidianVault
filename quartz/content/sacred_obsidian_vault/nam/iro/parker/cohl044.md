 <h3 align="CENTER">SECTION 33</h3>
 <p>"'So now another.</p>
 <p>"'Tell your relations this. The Creator has sanctioned a feast to a medicine animal on a great day."'</p>
 <p>So they said and he said. Eniaiehuk.</p>
