 <h3 align="CENTER">SECTION 36</h3>
 <p>"'Now another message to tell your people.</p>
 <p>"'This will happen.</p>
 <p>"'We have told you to watch.</p>
 <p><a name="page_43"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 43</font></a></p>
 <p>"'The Honon'diont will go out in fours for game for the feasts. You may think that they are fulfilling their duty to Gai'wiio`.</p>
 <p>"'The animals that fall must be thirty.</p>
 <p>"'But this will happen when Gai'wiio` is new. The Honon'diont will kill twenty-nine and the twenty-ninth will be a cub bear. So there will not be thirty.</p>
 <p>"'So this will be done when Gai'wiio` is new. It will be done at Adekwe'o<sup>n</sup>ge, the Green Corn thanksgiving ceremony."'</p>
 <p>So they said and he said. Eniaiehuk.</p>
