 <h3 align="CENTER">SECTION 116</h3>
 <p>"Now that person spoke and said, 'I brought them with me to testify to the truth that those of the lower world when they pass away come hither.'</p>
 <p><a name="page_76"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 76</font></a></p>
 <p>"Then spoke the daughter of Gaiän'twakâ, 'I send a message. It is this: It grieves me to know that my brothers on the earth disagree with my father. Bid them cease their disagreement.' So she said."</p>
 <p>Eniaiehuk.</p>
