 <h3 align="CENTER">SECTION 110</h3>
 <p>"Verily he saw those who were upon the earth and those who were alive and he saw the wicked in the house of torment. He saw Gowono<sup>n</sup>?'gowa [she great talker], Gâkon'go? [she-glutton animal], Gânonjoni'yon [hanging kettle] and Hano'e:s [head-eater]. Verily he saw these four persons.</p>
 <p>"Then said the four messengers, 'These four have committed the great sin and can not be forgiven.'"</p>
 <p>So they said. Eniaiehuk.</p>
