 <h3 align="CENTER">SECTION 70</h3>
 <p>"Now another message.</p>
 <p>"Now we say that you must tell your friends and relatives that there will be a time when all the earth will withhold its sustaining foods. Then will come the end of the world and those who refuse to believe in Gai'wiio` will suffer great hardships."</p>
 <p>So they said and he said. Eniaiehuk.</p>
