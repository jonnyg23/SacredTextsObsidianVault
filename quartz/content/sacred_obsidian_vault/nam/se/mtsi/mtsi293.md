 <h3 align="CENTER">28. THE TURTLE <a name="fr_86"></a><a href="mtsi295.htm#fn_85"><font size="1">1</font></a></h3>
 <p>A Turtle came out of the Water and began sunning himself on a log. While he was doing so he looked up and saw that a rainstorm was coming. He said to himself, "It is going to wet me." So he jumped into the water.</p>
