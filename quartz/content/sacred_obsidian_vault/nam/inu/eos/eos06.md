 <p align="center">  </p><h3 align="center">5. Creation of the World. <a name="fr_2"></a><a href="#fn_2"><font size="1">1</font></a></h3>  
 <p>   The Raven and his wife created the world. They made the cape of Uñi´sak out of a nose of an eider-duck (<i>Somatheria spectabilis</i>); the peninsula of Alaska, of a long belt-knife; and the island Ima´lik (one of the Diomedes), of a button of the scabbard (with which it is clasped around the hip). They made reindeer of their hair, and dogs of their nails, and sea-water of their urine.</p>  
 <p align="right">Told by Ñịpe´wġi, an Asiatic Eskimo man, in the village of Uñi´sak, at Indian Point, May, 1901.</p>
