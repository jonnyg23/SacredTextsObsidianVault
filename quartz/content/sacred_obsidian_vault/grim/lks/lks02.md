 <i></i><p align="CENTER">PRELIMINARY INVOCATION.</p>
 <i></i><p>Thee I invoke, the Bornless one.<br>
 Thee that didst create the Earth and the Heavens:<br>
 Thee that didst create the Night and the Day.<br>
 Thee that didst create the Darkness and the Light.<br>
 Thou art Osorronophris: Whom no man has seen at any time.<br>
 Thou art Jäbas<br>
 Thou art Jäpôs:<br>
 Thou hast distinguished between the Just and the Unjust.<br>
 Thou didst make the Female and the Male.<br>
 Thou didst produce the Seed and the Fruit.<br>
 Thou didst form Men to love one another, and to hate one another.</p>
 <i></i><p>I am Mosheh Thy Prophet, unto Whom Thou didst commit Thy Mysteries, the Ceremonies of Ishrael:<br>
 Thou didst produce the moist and the, dry, and that which nourisheth all created Life.<br>
 Hear Thou Me, for I am the Angel of Paphrô Osorronophris: this is Thy True Name, handed down to the Prophets of Ishrael.</p>
 <p align="CENTER">****</p>
 <i></i><p>Hear Me:--<br>
 Ar: Thiao: Rheibet: Atheleberseth:<br>
 A: Blatha: Abeu: Ebeu: Phi:<br>
 Thitasoe: Ib: Thiao.</p>
 <p><a name="page_6"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 6</font></a></p>
 <i></i><p>Hear Me, and make all Spirits subject unto Me: so that every Spirit of the Firmament and of the Ether; upon the Earth and under the Earth: on dry Land and in the Water: of Whirling Air, and of rushing Fire: and every Spell and Scourge of God may be obedient unto Me.</p>
 <p align="CENTER">****</p>
 <i></i><p>I invoke Thee, the Terrible and Invisible God: Who dwellest in the Void Place of the Spirit:--<br>
 Arogogorobraô: Sothou:<br>
 Modoriô: Phalarthaô: Döö: Apé, The Bornless One:<br>
 Hear Me: etc.</p>
 <i></i><p>Hear me:--<br>
 Roubriaô: Mariôdam: Balbnabaoth: Assalonai: Aphniaô: I: Thoteth: Abrasar: Aëöôü: Ischure, Mighty and Bornless One!<br>
 Hear me: etc.</p>
 <i></i><p>I invoke thee:--<br>
     Ma: Barraiô: Jôêl: Kotha:<br>
     Athorêbalô: Abraoth:<br>
 Hear Me: etc.</p>
 <p>Hear me!<br>
 Aôth: Abaôth: Basum: Isak:<br>
 Sabaoth: Iao:</p>
 <p>This is the Lord of the Gods:<br>
 This is the Lord of the Universe:<br>
 This is He Whom the Winds fear.</p>
 <p><a name="page_7"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 7</font></a></p>
 <i></i><p>This is He, Who having made Voice by His Commandment, is Lord of All Things; King, Ruler and Helper.<br>
 Hear Me, etc.</p>
 <i></i><p>Hear Me:--<br>
 Ieou: Pûr: Iou: Pûr: Iaôt: Iaeô: Ioou: Abrasar: Sabriam: Do: Uu: Adonaie: Ede: Edu: Angelos ton Theon: Aniaia Lai: Gaia: Ape: Diathanna Thorun.</p>
 <i></i><p>I am He! the Bornless Spirit! having sight in the feet: Strong, and the Immortal Fire!<br>
 I am He! the Truth!<br>
 I am He! Who hate that evil should be wrought in the, World!<br>
 I am He, that lighteneth and thundereth.<br>
 I am He, from Whom is the Shower of the Life of Earth:<br>
 I am He, Whose mouth ever flameth:<br>
 I am He, the Begetter and Manifester unto the Light:<br>
 I am He; the Grace of the World:</p>
 <p>"The Heart Girt with a Serpent" is My Name!</p>
 <p>Come Thou forth, and follow Me: and make all Spirits subject unto Me so that every Spirit of the Firmament, and of the Ether: upon the Earth and under the Earth: on dry Land, or in the Water: of whirling Air or of rushing Fire: and every Spell and Scourge of God, may be obedient unto Me!</p>
 <p align="CENTER">Iao: Sabao:</p>
 <p align="CENTER">Such are the Words!</p>
