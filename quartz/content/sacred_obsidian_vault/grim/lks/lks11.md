 <h3 align="CENTER">THE PENTAGRAM OF SOLOMON.</h3>
 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_fig156"></a>
 <a href="img/fig156.jpg"><img src="tn/generic.jpg"></a></p>
 <p>THIS is the Form of Pentagram of Solomon, the figure whereof is to be made in Sol or Luna (Gold or Silver), and worn upon thy breast; having the Seal of the Spirit required upon the other side thereof. It is to preserve thee from danger, and also to command the Spirits by.</p>
 <p>(Colours.--Circle and pentagram outlined in black. Names and Sigils within Pentagram black also. "Tetragrammaton" in red letters. Ground of centre of Pentagram, where "Soluzen" is written, green. External angles of Pentagram where "Abdia", "Ballaton," "Halliza," etc., are written, blue.)</p>
