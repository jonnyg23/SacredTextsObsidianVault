 <h3 align="CENTER">THE EXPLANATION OF THE TWO TRIANGLES <a name="fr_39"></a><a href="#fn_39"><font size="1">1</font></a> IN THE PARCHMENT.</h3>
 <i></i><p>Alpha And Omega.--Thou, O great God, Who art the beginning and the end:</p>
 <i></i><p>Tetragrammaton.--Thou God of Almighty power, be ever present with us to guard and protect us, and let Thy Holy Spirit and presence be now and always with us:</p>
 <i></i><p>Soluzen.--I command thee, thou Spirit of whatsoever region thou art, to come unto this circle:</p>
 <i></i><p>Halliza.--And appear in human shape:</p>
 <i></i><p>Bellator (or Ballaton).--And speak unto us audibly in our mother-tongue:</p>
 <p>
 </p>
 <p><a name="page_70"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 70</font></a></p>
 <i></i><p>Bellonoy (or Bellony).--And show, and discover unto us all treasure that thou knowest of, or that is in thy keeping, and deliver it unto us quietly:</p>
 <i></i><p>Hallii. Hra.--And answer all such questions as we may demand without any defect now at this time.</p>
