 <p> </p>
 <font size="1"><p align="CENTER">THE</p>
 </font><font size="4"><p align="CENTER">TRUE SECRET OF THE PHILOSOPHERS' STONE;</p>
 </font><font size="1"><p align="CENTER">OR,</p>
 </font><font size="6"><p align="CENTER">JEWEL OF ALCHYMY.</p>
 </font><font size="1"><p align="CENTER">WHEREIN</p>
 </font><font size="4"><p align="CENTER">THE PROCESS OF MAKING THE GREAT ELIXIR</p>
 </font><i><font size="1"><p align="CENTER">is discovered;</p>
 </font></i><font size="2"><p align="CENTER">BY WHICH BASE METALS MAY BE TURNED INTO PURE GOLD; CONTAINING THE MOST EXCELLENT AND PROFITABLE INSTRUCTIONS IN THE</p>
 </font><i><font size="4"><p align="CENTER">HERMETIC ART;</p>
 </font></i><font size="2"><p align="CENTER">DISCOVERING THAT VALUABLE AND SECRET</p>
 </font><font size="4"><p align="CENTER">MEDICINE OF THE PHILOSOPHERS</p></font>,
 <i><font size="2"><p align="CENTER">To make Men Healthy, Wise, and Happy</p></font></i>.
 <font size="4"><p align="CENTER">BY F. BARRETT</p></font>,
 <font size="1"><p align="CENTER">STUDENT OF CHEMISTRY, NATURAL PHILOSOPHY, &amp;c.</p></font>,
 <p align="CENTER">1801.</p>
