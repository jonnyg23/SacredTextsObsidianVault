 <font size="4"><p align="CENTER">CHAP. XVII</p></font>.
 <font size="2"><p align="CENTER">THERE IS ANOTHER KIND OF CHARACTERS, OR MARKS OF SPIRITS, WHICH ARE RECEIVED ONLY BY REVELATION</p></font>.
 <p>THERE is another kind of character received by revelation only, which can be found out no other way; the virtue of which characters is from the Deity revealing; of whom there are some secret works breathing out a harmony of some divinity, or they are, as it were, some certain agreements or compacts of a league between us and them. Of this kind there was a sign shewed to <i>Constantine</i>, which was this, <i>in hoc vince</i>; there was another revealed to <i>Antiochus</i> in the figure of a Pentangle, which signifies health; for,</p>
 <p> </p>
 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_pl18"></a>
 <a href="img/pl18.jpg"><img src="tn/generic.jpg"><br><font size="-3">Click to view</font></a></p>

 <p><a name="page_67"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 67</font></a></p>
 <p>being resolved into letters, it speaks the word ὑγίεια i. e. health: in the faith and virtue of which signs, both kings obtained a great victory against their enemies. So Judas, who by reason of that, was afterwards surnamed Machabeus, being to fight with the Jews against <i>Antiochus Eupator</i>, received from an angel a notable sign, מכבי in the virtue of which they first slew 11,000, with an infinite number of elephants, then again 35,000 of their enemies: for that sign did represent the name of <i>Jehovah</i>, and was a memorable emblem of the name of seventy-two letters by the equality of number; and the exposition thereof is מי בטיד באלי מיהיח <i>i. e.</i> who is there among thee strong as <i>Jehovah</i>? See Plate, fig. F.</p>
