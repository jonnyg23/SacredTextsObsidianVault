 <p align="CENTER"> </p>
 <p>X. BUER, <a name="fr_190"></a><a href="#fn_189"><font size="1">1</font></a> a great president, who appears when the sun is in Sagittarius and teaches philosophy, logic, the virtues of herbs, &amp;c. He heals all diseases and gives good familiars.</p>
 <p>XI. GUSION, <a name="fr_191"></a><a href="#fn_190"><font size="1">2</font></a> a mighty duke, who appears like a cynocephalus, discerns the past, present and future, answers all questions, reconciles enemies and gives honour and dignities.</p>

 <p align="CENTER"> </p>
 <p>XII. SYTRY, a great prince, who appears with a leopard's head and the wings of a griffin, but assumes a beautiful human form at the Magician's command. He procures love between the two sexes, and causes women to shew themselves naked. <a name="fr_192"></a><a href="#fn_191"><font size="1">3</font></a></p>
 <p>XIII. BELETH, <a name="fr_193"></a><a href="#fn_192"><font size="1">4</font></a> a terrible and mighty king, riding on a pale horse, preceded by all manner of musicians. He is very</p>
 <p>
 </p>
 <p>
 </p>
 <p>
 </p>
 <p>
 </p>
 <p><a name="page_200"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 200</font></a></p>
 <p>furious when first summoned, and must be commanded into a triangle or circle with the hazel wand of the Magician pointed to the South-East. He must be received courteously and with homage, but a silver ring must be worn on the middle finger of the left hand, which must he held against the face.</p>
 <p>He procures love between man and woman and is of the Order of the Powers. <a name="fr_194"></a><a href="#fn_193"><font size="1">1</font></a></p>

 <p align="CENTER"> </p>
 <p>XIV. LERAJIE, a powerful marquis, coming in the likeness of an archer, clad in green and bearing bow and quiver. He occasions battles and causes arrow-wounds to putrefy.</p>
 <p>XV. ELIGOR, a great duke, appearing as a goodly knight carrying a lance, pennon and sceptre. He discovers hidden things, causes war, marshals armies, kindles love and lust. <a name="fr_195"></a><a href="#fn_194"><font size="1">2</font></a></p>

 <p align="CENTER"> </p>
 <p>XVI. ZEPAR, a great duke, who appears in red apparel and armed like a soldier. He inflames women with love for men</p>
 <p>
 </p>
 <p>
 </p>
 <p><a name="page_201"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 201</font></a></p>
 <p>and can transform them into other shapes till they have been enjoyed by their lovers. <a name="fr_196"></a><a href="#fn_195"><font size="1">1</font></a></p>
 <p>XVII. BOTIS, a great president and earl, who appears like a horrid viper, but, when commanded, assumes a human shape, with large teeth and horns. He bears a sharp sword in his hand, discerns past, present and future reconciles friends and foes.</p>
 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_20100"></a>
 <a href="img/20100.jpg"><img src="tn/generic.jpg"><br><font size="-3">Click to view</font></a><br>
 <font size="2">THE SEAL OF BATHIN</font></p>
 <p>XVIII. BATHIN, <a name="fr_197"></a><a href="#fn_196"><font size="1">2</font></a> a mighty duke, who appears like a strong man with a serpent's tail, riding on a pale horse. He knows the virtues of herbs and precious stones, and can transport men swiftly from one country to another.</p>
 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_20101"></a>
 <a href="img/20101.jpg"><img src="tn/generic.jpg"><br><font size="-3">Click to view</font></a><br>
 <font size="2">THE SEAL OF SALEOS</font></p>
 <p>XIX SALEOS, <a name="fr_198"></a><a href="#fn_197"><font size="1">3</font></a> a great duke, who appears like a brave soldier, riding on a crocodile and wearing a duke's crown. He promotes love between the sexes.</p>
 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_20102"></a>
 <a href="img/20102.jpg"><img src="tn/generic.jpg"><br><font size="-3">Click to view</font></a><br>
 <font size="2">THE SEAL OF PURSON</font></p>
 <p>XX. PURSON, <a name="fr_199"></a><a href="#fn_198"><font size="1">4</font></a> a great king, who appears like a lion-headed man, carrying a viper in his hand and riding on a bear, preceded by many trumpeters. He conceals and discovers treasure, discerns past, present and future, gives true answers respecting things human and divine, and provides good familiars. <a name="fr_200"></a><a href="#fn_199"><font size="1">5</font></a></p>

 <h3 align="CENTER">Footnotes</h3>
 <p><a name="fn_189"></a><a href="bcm55.htm#fr_190">199:1</a> The seventh spirit of the <i>Pseudo-Monarchia</i>. He is also an exponent of ethics.</p>
 <p><a name="fn_190"></a><a href="bcm55.htm#fr_191">199:2</a> Otherwise, <i>Gusayn</i>, the eighth spirit of Wierus, and <i>Pirsoyn</i>, according to the variant of another MS.</p>
 <p><a name="fn_191"></a><a href="bcm55.htm#fr_192">199:3</a> <i>Jussus secreta libenter detegit feminarum, eas ridens ludificansque ut se luxorise nudent</i>.--<i>Wierus</i>.</p>
 <p><a name="fn_192"></a><a href="bcm55.htm#fr_193">199:4</a> Otherwise, <i>Byleth</i>. Some codices give considerable space to this demon and the precautions to be taken concerning him.</p>
 <p><a name="fn_193"></a><a href="bcm55.htm#fr_194">200:1</a> He yet hopes to be restored to the Seventh Thrones--"which is not altogether credible," according to one of the versions.</p>
 <p><a name="fn_194"></a><a href="bcm55.htm#fr_195">200:2</a> Alternatively, he procures the favour of lords and knights.</p>
 <p><a name="fn_195"></a><a href="bcm55.htm#fr_196">201:1</a> The last statement is on the authority of Wierus. According to another reading, <i>Zepar</i> makes women barren.</p>
 <p><a name="fn_196"></a><a href="bcm55.htm#fr_197">201:2</a> Otherwise, <i>Bathym</i> or <i>Marthim</i>.</p>
 <p><a name="fn_197"></a><a href="bcm55.htm#fr_198">201:3</a> Called also <i>Zaleos</i>.</p>
 <p><a name="fn_198"></a><a href="bcm55.htm#fr_199">201:4</a> Otherwise, <i>Curson</i>.</p>
 <p><a name="fn_199"></a><a href="bcm55.htm#fr_200">201:5</a> He also utters sound doctrine concerning the creation of the world.</p>
