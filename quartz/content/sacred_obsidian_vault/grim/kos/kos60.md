 <h1 align="CENTER">THE QABALISTICAL INVOCATION OF SOLOMON</h1>
 <i></i><p>Given by Eliphaz Lévi in 'Rituel de la Haute Magie,' Chapter xiii.</p>
 <p>POWERS of the Kingdom, be beneath my left foot, and within my right hand.</p>
 <p>Glory and Eternity touch my shoulders, and guide me in the Paths of Victory.</p>
 <p>Mercy and justice be ye the Equilibrium and splendour of my life.</p>
 <p>Understanding and Wisdom give unto me the Crown.</p>
 <p>Spirits of Malkuth conduct me between the two columns whereon is supported the whole edifice of the Temple.</p>
 <p>Angels of Netzach and of Hod strengthen me upon the Cubical Stone of Yesod.</p>
 <p>O GEDULAHEL! O GEBURAHEL! O TIPHERETH!</p>
 <p>BINAHEL, be Thou my Love!</p>
 <p>RUACH CHOKMAHEL, be Thou my Light!</p>
 <p>Be that which Thou art, and that which thou willest to be, O KETHERIEL!</p>
 <i></i><p>Ishim, assist me in the Name Of SHADDAL</p>
 <i></i><p>Cherubim, be my strength in the Name of ADONAL</p>
 <i></i><p>Beni Elohim, be ye my brethren in the Name of the Son, and by the virtues of TZABAOTH.</p>
 <i></i><p>Elohim, fight for me in the Name of TETRAGRAMMATON.</p>
 <i></i><p>Malachim, protect me in the Name Of YOD HE VAU HE.</p>
 <i></i><p>Seraphim, purify my love in the Name of ELOAH.</p>
 <i></i><p>Chaschmalim, enlighten me with the splendours of ELOHI, and of SCHECHINAH.</p>
 <i></i><p>Aralim, act ye; <i>Auphanim</i>, revolve and shine.</p>
 <i></i><p>Chaioth Ha-Qadosch, cry aloud, speak, roar, and groan; Qadosch, Qadosch, Qadosch., SHADDAI, ADONAI, YOD CHAVAH, EHEIEH ASHER EHEIEH!</p>
 <p>Halelu-Yah! Halelu-Yah! Halelu-Yah. Amen.</p>
 <p align="CENTER">THE END.</p>
