   <h3 align="center">THE TWENTIETH CHAPTER.</h3>  
 <p><a name="img_tinit"></a>
 </p><table align="LEFT"><tr><td align="CENTER" valign="CENTER"><img src="img/tinit.jpg" align="LEFT"></td></tr></table>O excite every Description of Hatred and Enmity, Discords, Quarrels, Contentions, Combats, Battles, Loss, and Damage.  
 <p>(1) To excite Quarrels and Fights.</p>  
 <p>(2) For Enmity in general.</p>  
 <p>(3) For Enmities of Kings and of the Great.</p>  
 <p>(4) For particular Enmities.</p>  
 <p>(5) For Enmities among Women.</p>  
 <p>(6) To cause a General War.</p>  
 <p>(7) To render any one unfortunate in Combat.</p>  
 <p>(8) To put Discord in an Army.</p>  
 <p>(9) For a particular Discord.</p>  
 <p>(10) To sow Discord among Ecclesiastics.</p>  
 <p>(11) For every description of Vengeance.</p>  
 <p>(12) To cause Battles, Losses, etc.</p>
