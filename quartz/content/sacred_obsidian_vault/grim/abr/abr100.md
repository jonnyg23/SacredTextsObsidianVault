   <h3 align="center">THE TWENTY-EIGHTH CHAPTER.</h3>  
 <p><a name="img_tinit"></a>
 </p><table align="LEFT"><tr><td align="CENTER" valign="CENTER"><img src="img/tinit.jpg" align="LEFT"></td></tr></table>O have as much Gold and Silver as one may wish, both to provide for one's necessities, and to live in opulence.  
 <p>(1) To have coined Gold.</p>  
 <p>(2) To have coined Silver.</p>  
 <p>(3) To have Silver in small coins.</p>  
 <p>(4) To have small change in Copper (or Bronze).</p>
