 <p><a name="page_194"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 194</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">THE TWELFTH CHAPTER.</h3>  
 <p><a name="img_tinit"></a>
 </p><table align="LEFT"><tr><td align="CENTER" valign="CENTER"><img src="img/tinit.jpg" align="LEFT"></td></tr></table>O know the Secrets of any person.  
 <p>(1) To know the Secret of Letters.</p>  
 <p>(2) To know the Secret of Words.</p>  
 <p>(3) To know Secret Operations.</p>  
 <p>(4) For the Military Counsels of a Captain.</p>  
 <p>(5) To know the Secrets of Love.</p>  
 <p>(6) To know what riches a person possesseth.</p>  
 <p>(7) To know the Secret of all Arts.</p>
