   <h3 align="center">NOTES TO CHAPTER IV.</h3>  
 <p>(<i>a</i>) The Symbols of this Chapter are manifested only by the Angels or by the Guardian Angel.</p>  
 <p>(<i>b</i>) ORIENS, PAIMON, ARITON, AMAYMON execute the Operations hereof by means of their Common Ministers.</p>  
 <p>(<i>c</i>) The Operations of this Chapter can also be to an extent performed by the Familiar Spirits.</p>  
 <p>(<i>d</i>) No especial instructions are given regarding this Chapter in the Second Book.</p>  
 <p>(<i>e</i>) No. 1 is a Gnomonic Square of 15 Squares taken from a Square of 64 Squares.--GILIONIN = Chaldaic GLIVNIM = Mirrors.</p>  
 <p>No. 2 is a Gnomonic Square of 13 Squares out of a Square of 49 Squares.--ETHANIM = AThVNIM, Heb. = Vaults, Ovens.</p>  
 <p>No. 3 is a Gnomonic Square of 13 Squares out of a Square of 49 Squares.--APPARET, Latin = Let it appear.</p>  
 <p>No. 4 is a Square of 36 Squares.--BEDSER = Hebrew BTzR a Gold Ornament.--ELIELE = ALI ALI Hebrew = Towards me.</p>  
 <p><a name="page_178"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 178</font></a></p>  
 <p><font size="-2" color="GREEN">[paragraph continues]</font> SEPPED Hebrew SPD = He struck.--RES DEB = perhaps Hebrew, RSh DB head or chief point of a discourse.</p>  
 <p>No. 5 is a Square of 25 Squares.--NEGOT = perhaps Heb. NHG, He leads.--ERASO = probably Greek, sec. pers. sing. from EROMAI for EIROMAI, to demand or interrogate. GARAG = perhaps from Heb. GRO, to diminish. OMARE = perhaps Greek, an Assembly or Synthesis.--TOGEN = perhaps from Greek TOGE = Why or Wherefore when used in an adverbial sense.</p>  
 <p>No. 6 is a Square of 16 Squares.--NASI = Hebrew NSI = My Banner or Symbol.--APIS = the Egyptian sacred bull. SIPA perhaps from SPH = Hebrew to consume.--ISAN = perhaps from Heb. IShN = To sleep.</p>  
 <p>No. 7 is a Square of 25 Squares.--GOHEN should perhaps be COHEN = a Jewish priest.--ORARE, Latin = to pray.--HASAH, Heb. HSH = to keep silence. ERARO perhaps from Heb. ARR to curse. NEHOG = perhaps Heb. NHG = to lead.</p>  
 <p>No. 8 is a Gnomonic Square of 9 Squares out of 25 Squares.--ADMON = perhaps from Heb. DMO = Tears but also Liquids or fluids.</p>  
 <p>No. 9 is a Gnomonic Square of 9 Squares out of 25 Squares.--LELEH Hebrew LILH = Night, Darkness.</p>
