 <p><a name="page_211"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 211</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">THE EIGHTEENTH CHAPTER.</h3>  
 <p><a name="img_tinit"></a>
 </p><table align="LEFT"><tr><td align="CENTER" valign="CENTER"><img src="img/tinit.jpg" align="LEFT"></td></tr></table>O heal divers Maladies.  
 <p>(1) To heal Leprosy.</p>  
 <p>(2) For chapped hands, etc.</p>  
 <p>(3) For old Ulcers.</p>  
 <p>(4) For pestilential diseases.</p>  
 <p>(5) For inveterate Paralysis.</p>  
 <p>(6) For Malignant Fevers.</p>  
 <p>(7) For bodily pains.</p>  
 <p>(8) For Sea Sickness.</p>  
 <p>(9) For Vertigo (and Giddiness).</p>  
 <p>(10) For the "Miserere" <a name="fr_231"></a><a href="#fn_231"><font size="1">1</font></a> (a most violent and dangerous kind of colic), accompanied by dreadful vomiting.</p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p>(11) For Dropsy.</p>  
 <p>(12) For all kinds of Wounds.</p>
