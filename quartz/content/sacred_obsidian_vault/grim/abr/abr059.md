   <h3 align="center">NOTES TO CHAPTER VII.</h3>  
 <p>(<i>a</i>) The Symbols of this Chapter are manifested only by the Angels, or by the Guardian Angel.</p>  
 <p>(<i>b</i>) ASHTAROTH and ASMODEUS together execute the Operations of this Chapter.</p>  
 <p>(<i>c</i>) The Familiar Spirits cannot well execute the Operations of this Chapter.</p>  
 <p>(<i>d</i>) No especial instructions are given regarding this Chapter in the Second Book.</p>  
 <p>(<i>e</i>) No. 1 is a Gnomon of 11 Squares taken from a Square of 36 Squares. METALO from Greek METALLON = in metal, mineral, or mining work.</p>  
 <p>No. 2 is a Square of 36 Squares. TABBAT, Chaldaic ThIBVTh = Heads or Sections of classification of operations. ARUUCA perhaps from ARUQ, adhering to.</p>  
 <p><a name="page_186"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 186</font></a></p>  
 <p>No. 3 is a Square of 49 Squares.--IPOMANO, probably from Greek HIPPOMANES, an ingredient used in philtres, etc., perhaps put here for chemical drugs in general.</p>
