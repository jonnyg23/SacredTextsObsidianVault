 <h3 align="CENTER">CHAPTER V. CONJURATION OF THE LAWS OF MOSES</h3>
 <p align="CENTER"><img src="img/08702.jpg"></p>
 <p align="CENTER">Conjuration</p>
 <p>KEISEHU, NISCHBA, LAWEMSO--How to be God, so swarest Thou to our parents.</p>
 <p align="CENTER">Prayer</p>
 <p>Eternal of Eternals! Jehovah of Light, Adonai of Truth! Messiah of the All Merciful! Jesus Christ the Beloved and All Redemption and Love! Thou hast said: Who seeth me seeth also the Father. Father, eternal Father of the old and new convenants. Triune Father, Triune Son, Triune Spirit, our Father, I beseech and conjure Thee by the eternal words of Thy eternal truth.</p>
 <p>Now read the 17th chapter of John or Jesus' prayer.</p>
 <p align="CENTER">Closing Prayer of the Conjuration of the Law</p>
 <p>Eternal God Jehovah, Thou hast said: Ask and it shall be given you. I pray that Thou mayest hear Thy servants Caspar, Melchior and Balthasar, the archpriest of Thy fountain of light! I pray that I thou mayest bid thine angels to purify me from all sin; that they may breathe upon me in love, and that they may cover me with the shadow of their wings. Send them down! This is my prayer in peace!</p>
