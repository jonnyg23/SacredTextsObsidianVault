 <h3 align="CENTER">DISMISSION OF ELEAZAR, THE SON OF AARON</h3>
 <p align="CENTER"><img src="img/06401.jpg"></p>
 <p>The inscriptions on the seal are to be read as follows:</p>
 <p align="CENTER">LEAY YLI ZIARITE ZELOHABE ET NEGORAMY ZIEN LATEBM DAMA MECHA RA METI OZIRA</p>
 <p>Through this dismission all things dissolve into nothing.</p>
