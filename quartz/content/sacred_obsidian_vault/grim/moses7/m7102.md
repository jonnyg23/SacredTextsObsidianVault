 <h3 align="CENTER">Citation of the Seven Great Princes</h3>
 <p align="CENTER">REMARKS</p>
 <p>Following is some information regarding the appearances and provinces of the various spirits.</p>
 <p align="CENTER"></p><center><table cellspacing="0" border="0" cellpadding="9" width="779">
 <tr><td width="27%" valign="TOP">
 <p>AZIEL</p></td>
 <td width="73%" valign="TOP">
 <p>is a very prompt treasure spirit of the earth and of the sea. He appears in the form of a wild ox.</p></td>
 </tr>
 <tr><td width="27%" valign="TOP">
 <p>ARIEL</p></td>
 <td width="73%" valign="TOP">
 <p>is a very serviceable spirit, and appears in the form of a ferocious dog. He commands the lost treasures of the land and sea.</p></td>
 </tr>
 <tr><td width="27%" valign="TOP">
 <p>MARBUEL</p></td>
 <td width="73%" valign="TOP">
 <p>appears in the form of an old lion. He delivers the treasures of the water and of the land, and assists in obtaining all secret knowledge and honors.</p></td>
 </tr>
 <tr><td width="27%" valign="TOP">
 <p>MEPHISTOPHILIS</p></td>
 <td width="73%" valign="TOP">
 <p>is ready to serve, and appears in the form of a youth. He is willing to serve in all skilled arts, and gives the spiritus Servos, otherwise called "familiars." He brings treasures from the earth and from the deep very quickly.</p></td>
 </tr>
 <tr><td width="27%" valign="TOP">
 <p>BARBUEL</p></td>
 <td width="73%" valign="TOP">
 <p>is a master of all arts and all secret knowledge, a great master of all treasure. He is very accommodating, and appears with alacrity in the form of a wild hog.</p></td>
 </tr>
 <tr><td width="27%" valign="TOP">
 <p>AZIABEL</p></td>
 <td width="73%" valign="TOP">
 <p>is a prince of the water and mountain spirits and their treasures. He is amiable and wears a large pearl crown.</p></td>
 </tr>
 <tr><td width="27%" valign="TOP">
 <p>ANITUEL</p></td>
 <td width="73%" valign="TOP">
 <p>appears in the form of a serpent of paradise. He confers great wealth and honors according to wish.</p></td>
 </tr>
 </table>
 </center>
