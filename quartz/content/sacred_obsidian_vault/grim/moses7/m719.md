 <h3 align="CENTER">BREASTPLATE OF AARON</h3>
 <p align="CENTER"><img src="img/06201.jpg"></p>
 <p>The inscriptions on the seal are to be pronounced as follows:</p>
 <p align="CENTER">SADAJAI AMARA ELON HEJIANA VANANEL PHENATON EBCOEL MERAI</p>
 <p>That is, a Prince of Miens, the other leads to Jehova. Through this, God spoke to Moses.</p>
