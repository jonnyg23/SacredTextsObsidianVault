 <h3 align="CENTER">THE TENTH TABLE OF MERCURY</h3>
 <p align="CENTER">Conjuration</p>
 <p>Petasa, Ahor, Havaashar, N. N., cites Thee Spirit Yloij * through God, God Adonaij <img src="img/cross.jpg"> Ymah, through God Tetragrammaton <img src="img/cross.jpg"> Rawa, through God Emanuel * Ahaij, through Athanatos <img src="img/cross.jpg"> that Thou appear before me as truly in and through the name of Adonai, which Moses mentioned, and there appeared grasshoppers. fiat, fiat, fiat.</p>
 <p align="CENTER"><img src="img/05300.jpg"></p>
 <p>The Tenth Table of the Spirits of Mercury gives wealth in Chemistry. These spirits contribute treasures of the mines.</p>
