 <h3 align="CENTER">GENERATION SEAL</h3>
 <p align="CENTER"><img src="img/05700.jpg"></p>
 <p>This Generation Seal, also known as Moloch familiarum or Ammonitarium Ministering Spirits, makes its spirits obedient in all services. At the time of Citation, it must be written on parchment and held in the right hand, but it must not be read.</p>
