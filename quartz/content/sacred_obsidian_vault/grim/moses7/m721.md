 <h3 align="CENTER">THE INSCRIPTION ON THE CHALICE OF HOLINESS</h3>
 <p align="CENTER"><img src="img/06301.jpg"></p>
 <p>The inscriptions on the seal are to be read as follows:</p>
 <p align="CENTER">ELIAON JOENA EBREEL ELOIJELA AIJEL AGONI SOCHADON</p>
 <p>These words are great and mighty. They are names of the Creator and the characters on the Ark of the Covenant.</p>
