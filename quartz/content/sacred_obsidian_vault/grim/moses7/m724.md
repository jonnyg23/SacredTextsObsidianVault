 <h3 align="CENTER">CITATION OF GERMUTHSAI OR LEVIATHAN</h3>
 <p align="CENTER"><img src="img/06500.jpg"></p>
 <p>The inscriptions on the seal are to be read as follows:</p>
 <p align="CENTER">LAGUMEN EMANUEL THEREFORI MECHELAG LAIGEL YAZI ZAZAEL</p>
 <p>With these names Eleazar bound and unbound the spirits of the air.</p>
