 <h3 align="CENTER">HELMET OF MOSES AND AARON</h3>
 <p align="CENTER"><img src="img/06200.jpg"></p>
 <p>The Hebrew inscriptions are to be pronounced as follows:</p><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <p>HIEBEL MARE ACTITAS BARNE DONENE ARIAERCH</p></dir>
 </dir>
 
 <p>These are the names which the old Egyptians used instead of the unutterable name of Asser Criel, and are called the "Fire of God," and "Strong Rock of Faith." Whoever wears them on his person will not die a sudden death.</p>
