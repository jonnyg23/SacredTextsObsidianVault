 <h3 align="CENTER">* CHAPTER VII. GENERAL CITATION OF MOSES ON ALL SPIRITS</h3>
 <p align="CENTER"><img src="img/08900.jpg"></p>
 <p align="CENTER">Conjuration</p>
 <p align="CENTER">TUBATLU! BUALU! TULATU! LABUS! UBLISI!--Let there appear and bring before me the spirit of N.N.</p>
 <p>Each of these five omnipotent angels must be called three times toward the four quarters of the world, first with the voice then with the horn, to make a total of six calls.</p>
 <p align="CENTER">DISMISSAL OF MOSES</p>
 <p align="CENTER"><img src="img/08901.jpg"></p>
 <p align="CENTER">Conjuration</p>
 <p align="CENTER">UBELUTUSI! KADUKULITI! KEBUTZI!--Take away from my Presence the spirit of N.N.</p>
 <p>Twelve calls with the voice and twelve calls with the horn for each name.</p>
