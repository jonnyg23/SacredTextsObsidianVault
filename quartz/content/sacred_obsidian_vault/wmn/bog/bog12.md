 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Book of the Goddess</i>, by Anna Livia Plurabelle, [© 2002 Anna Livia Plurabelle, All rights reserved], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h1 align="center">KALI</h1>  <h3 align="center">I.</h3>  
 <p>O Goddess Kali, give me of Thy wisdom,<br> O Goddess Kali, give me of Thy mercy,<br> O Goddess Kali, give me of Thy fullness,<br> And of Thy guidance in face of every strait.<br> O Goddess Kali, give me of Thy holiness,<br> O Goddess Kali, give me of Thy shielding,<br> O Goddess Kali, give me of Thy surrounding,<br> And of Thy peace in the knot of my death.<br> Oh give me of Thy surrounding,<br> And of Thy peace at the hour of my death!</p>  <h3 align="center">II.</h3>  
 <p>Death with joy and with forgiveness,<br> Death without horror or repulsion,<br> Death without fear or shrinking.<br> Dying the death of the avatars,<br> The Healer of my soul by my side,<br> The death of peace and tranquillity,<br> And grant Thou me a good day of burial.<br> The seven devas of the Holy Goddess<br> Be shielding me, and be this night the night<br> Till brightness and summer-tide shall come!</p>  <h3 align="center">III.</h3>  
 <p>I am going home with thee<br> I am going home with thee<br> To thy home! to thy home!<br> I am going home with thee<br> To thy home of winter.<br> I am going home with thee<br> To thy home! to thy home!<br> I am going home with thee<br> To thy home of autumn, of spring and of summer.<br> I am going home with thee,<br> Thou child of my love,<br> To thine eternal bed,<br> To thy bodies perpetual sleep.<br> I am going home with thee,<br> Thou child of my love,<br> To the dear Daughter of blessings,<br> To Kali, Mother of grace.</p>  <h3 align="center">IV.</h3>  
 <p>Thou goest home this night to thy home of winter,<br> To thy home of autumn, of spring, and of summer;<br> Thou goest home this night to thy perpetual home,<br> To thine eternal bed, to thine eternal slumber.<br> Sleep thou, sleep, and away with thy sorrow,<br> Sleep thou, sleep, and away with thy sorrow,<br> Sleep thou, sleep, and away with thy sorrow;<br> Sleep, thou beloved, in the Rock of the fold.<br> Sleep this night in the breast of thy Mother,<br> Sleep, thou beloved, while she herself soothes thee;<br> Sleep thou this night on Kali's arm,<br> Sleep, thou beloved, while she herself kisses thee.<br> The sleep of the seven lights be thine, beloved,<br> The sleep of the seven joys be thine, beloved,<br> The sleep of the seven slumbers be thine, beloved,<br> The shade of death lies upon thy face, beloved,<br> In nearness to Kali, farewell to thy pains,<br> Kali stands before thee and peace is in Her mind.<br> Sleep, O sleep in the calm of all calm,<br> Sleep, O sleep in the guidance of guidance,<br> Sleep, O sleep in the love of all loves;<br> Sleep, O beloved, in the Goddess of life,<br> Sleep, O beloved, in the Goddess of life!</p>  <h3 align="center">V.</h3>  
 <p>Give us, O Kali, the needs of the body,<br> Give us, O Kali, the needs of the soul;<br> Give us, O Kali, the healing balsam of the body,<br> Give us, O Kali, the healing balsam of the soul.<br> Give us, O Kali, the joy of forgiveness,<br> Wash Thou from us the pain of jealousy,<br> Cleanse Thou from us the stain of karma.<br> That reincarnation may cease<br> And we may live forever in your summerland.<br> O great Goddess, Who art on the throne,<br> Weigh mine heart on your scales,<br> Give to us, O Kali, strong love,<br> And that beautiful crown of the Queen;<br> Give us, O Kali, the home of salvation<br> Within the beauteous gates of Thy kingdom.<br> Give us hospitality in the brightness of peace.</p>
