 <p><a name="page_35"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 35</font></a></p>  <h1 align="center">Chapter 20</h1>  <h3 align="center">THE ROYAL SWADDLING OF JESUS AND JOHN THE BAPTIST</h3>  
 <p>John The Baptist wears The Monarch's GIRDLE about his loins.</p>  
 <p>And Jesus Christ wore the Supreme Swaddling, Both loins and head.</p>  
 <p>And we, Black Supremacy, wears them The Three ways.</p>  
 <p>First on Our heads The Royal Wrap, (12 yards) King of Kings.</p>  
 <p>The MONARCH'S GIRDLE, the first to the Skin, Through the Rectum on both legs, closely fitted and all around the waist. Both MALE and FEMALE for life, DAY AND NIGHT.<br> Ethiopia must do the Same for life, and Safe-Guard your Dynasty, and Black Supremacy, The Church Triumphant, K.A.Q.O.K.O.K.</p>  
 <p>I AND MY CREATOR ARE ONE in PURPOSE, as MY Boy Jesus said he and I are one, the True Vine, and I the Husband-MAN His Tri-Divinity and Queen Lula Pettersburgh, Her Tri-Virginity My Head and Pillow companion, K.A.Q.O.K.O.K.</p>  
 <p>This is Officially called the MONARCH CROWN WRAP. Place of Writing, from the THRONE World's Capital The Bible House in The Furnace ROOM.<br> January 10, 1926 A.D. 2000. (4 O.C.P.M.)</p>
