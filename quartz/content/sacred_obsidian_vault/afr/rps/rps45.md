 <p><a name="page_68"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 68</font></a></p>  <h1 align="center">Chapter 43</h1>  <h3 align="center">THE MAN BEFORE ADAM WAS</h3>  
 <p>I am His Majesty King Alpha The King of Kings, alone with my Own BONA FIDE Lion-Hearted Wife Queen Omega, THE BIBLICAL SOVEREIGN OF THIS WORLD.<br> We are Black Peoples if you please.<br> The Copyright of this World if you Please!!.<br> Race &amp; Nations, Languages &amp; Tongues &amp; other peoples, will come and go, from off the FACE of this EARTH BUT His Majesty King Alpha and His Wife Queen Omega, We be here always if you please.</p>  
 <p>Alpha &amp; Omega, The Black man &amp; his wife, was here on Earth before Adam and Eve &amp; ABARAHAM &amp; Anglo-Saxon if you please.<br> And we, that is Our SEEDS will be here, in gross PROSPERITY as SOON AS THE ANGLO-SAXON PEOPLES ALL DIE OUT IF YOU PLEASE.<br> We are the Type-Setters for Time and Eternity, if you please.</p>  
 <p>Our appointment is an Eternal Appointment if you please.<br> We are, THE Keeper of the Tree of Life if you please.<br> We are the Owner of the Zodiac if you please.<br> We are The Ethiopian Kingdom Owner if you please.</p>  
 <p><a name="page_69"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 69</font></a></p>  
 <p>We are His &amp; Her Register General of Black Supremacy if you please.<br> Adam-Abraham-Angle-Saxon The Lepor, has no PLACE in this World, if you please.<br> Sign The Copyright of Creation. The Monarch F.B. Pettersburgh<br> A.B.C., M.A.</p>
