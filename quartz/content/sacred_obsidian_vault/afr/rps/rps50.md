 <p>Owing to the long Delay, of my BIBLE ATLAS SURVAYOR, and other DIFFICULTIES with the Engraving Department on this side of the Globe!!!<br> I had to alter the ENTIRE CONSTRUCTION of the CANON.<br> Therefore 70, Seventy chapters are left out of this Volume.<br> Those you will get in One Full volume, as soon as Our Survayor &amp; Atlas Engravers, can get through their work.</p>  
 <p>Has the Royal Honour to be, your Biblical Architect. His &amp; Her ARCHITECT Queen Lula May Fitz Balintine Pettersburgh, A.B.C., S.J.W.K.O.K.A.Q.O. ATLAS SURVAYORS. June 1st 1926. ETHIOPIAN BIBLE PIONEER.<br> P.S.--The entire Bible Scroll of Black Supremacy The Church Triumphant--and all the ROYAL DOCUMENTS Along with 76 chapters of The New Canon was destroyed by a MAD BRAINED REVIVAL man CALLED ALEXANDER HABAKKUK COOMBS and his Wife.</p>  
 <p>It is impossible for such a man to ESCAPE punishment because his acts are WILFULL.<br> The Rev. Fitz Balintine Pettersburgh (Lawgiver) and Clergyman.</p>
