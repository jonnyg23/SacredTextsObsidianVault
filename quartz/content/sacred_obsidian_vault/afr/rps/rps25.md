 <p><a name="page_39"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 39</font></a></p>  <h1 align="center">Chapter 23</h1>  <h3 align="center">ETHIOPIA'S BANQUETING CHAMBER</h3>  
 <p>Lady Black Supremacy, The Church Triumphant INVITED me into her Royal Banqueting Universe.<br> May Be, you might find a Royal Lover for your Own Heart in This Banquet, at this LOVE SEARCHING Battle GROUND.</p>  
 <p>If you do, let us know, when it ripe.<br> At the Banquet in Egypt, King Solomon, The Black Man, made love, with King Pharaoh The Black man's virgin princess.</p>  <h3 align="center">A BANQUETING CHAMBER, HAS MANY ROYAL SECTIONS.</h3>  
 <p>You may have a Thanks-Giving Banquet for Marriage Life.</p>  
 <p>A Memorial Banquet for a Member gone to REST.</p>  
 <p>You will also have a Banquet of Black Supremacy.</p>  
 <p>Banquet for CHILD'S BIRTH.</p>  
 <p>New Home or Business, or for Sick Recovery.</p>  
 <p>For a Friend, or, An Assembly.</p>  
 <p>For Souls Having Peace with GOD.</p>  
 <p>For New Year, Xmas and HOLIDAYS.</p>  
 <p><a name="page_40"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 40</font></a></p>  
 <p>For Business Properties.</p>  
 <p>For Advertising your Business.</p>  
 <p>For King Alpha and Queen Omega.</p>  
 <p>A Love Banquet to The Glory of God.</p>
