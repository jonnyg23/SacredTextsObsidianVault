 <h1 align="CENTER">THE SAXICOLA CASTOR<small>[1]</small> AND THE WILD CAT.</h1>
 <p>It (the Saxicola Castor) says: "<i>Tcha, tcha, tcha, tcha,</i>" when it is laughing at the wild cat, when it has espied the cat, while the cat is lying down, lying asleep; and it is laughing at the cat, on account of it.</p>
 <p>The other little birds (hearing it) go to it, they are all laughing at the cat.</p>
 <p><small>[1. The <i>|ka-kau</i> or <i>Saxicola Castor</i> is a little bird found in Bushmanland. It lives in trees and flies about. It is not eaten by Bushmen.]</small></p>
