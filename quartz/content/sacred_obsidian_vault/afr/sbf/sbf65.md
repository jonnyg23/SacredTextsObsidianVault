 <h1 align="CENTER">THE SHAPED RIB BONE.</h1>
 <p>A bone (it) is; a rib (it.) is; a Bushman is the one who makes it.<small>[1]</small> He works it; he shapes it with a knife.</p>
 <p>"Kambro" is that which we eat (with) it.</p>
 <p><small>[1. He works two ribs, with a knife.]</small></p>
