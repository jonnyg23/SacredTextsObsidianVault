 <h1 align="CENTER">THE #NERRU, AS A BIRD</h1>
 <p>The <i>#nerru</i>'s bill is very short. The male <i>#nerru </i>is the one whose plumage resembles (that of) the ostrich; it is black like the male ostrich. The female <i>#nerru</i> is the one whose plumage is white like (that of) the female ostrich. Thus, they resemble the ostriches; because the male <i>#nerru</i> are black, the female <i>#nerru</i> white.</p>
 <p>They eat the things which little birds usually eat, which they pick up on the ground.</p>
 <p>They make grass nests on the ground, by the root of a bush.</p>
 <p>When not breeding, they are found in large numbers.</p>
