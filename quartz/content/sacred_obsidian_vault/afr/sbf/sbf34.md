 <h1 align="CENTER">A SONG SUNG BY THE STAR !GAUNU, AND ESPECIALLY BY BUSHMAN WOMEN.</h1><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <p>Does the <i>||garraken</i><small>[1]</small> flower open?<br>
 The <i>#ku-Yam</i><small>[2]</small> is the one which opens.<br>
 Dost thou open?<br>
 The <i>#ku-Yam</i> is the one which opens.</p></dir>
 </dir>
 
 <p><small>[1. The <i>||garraken</i> are bulbs; the Bushmen dig them out.</small></p>
 <p>2. <i>Dimorphotheca annua</i>, a daisy-like flower, in bloom at Mowbray in August, 1879.]</p>
