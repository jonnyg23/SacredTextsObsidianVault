 <h1 align="CENTER">THE SONG OF THE BUSTARD.</h1><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <p>My younger brother-in-law,<br>
 Put my head in the fire.<small>[1]</small><br>
 My younger brother-in-law,<br>
 My younger brother-in-law,<br>
 Put my head in the fire.</p></dir>
 </dir>
 
 <p>When we startle it up, it flies away; it (cries): "<i>Wara ||khau, wara ||khau, wara ||khau, ||khau ||khau, ||khau, wara ||khau, wara ||khau, wara ||khau, ||khau ||khau, ||khau!"</i></p>
 <p>When it stands on the ground, it says: "<i>A wa,a wa, a wa, a wa!"</i> when it stands on the ground.</p>
 <p><small>[1. When the "Knorhaan Brandkop" was still a man, his head was thrust into the fire by his brother-in-law, in order to punish him for having surreptitiously married a sister. Since then he is only a bustard.]</small></p>
