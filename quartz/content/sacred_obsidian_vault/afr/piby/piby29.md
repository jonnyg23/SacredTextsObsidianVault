 <h1 align="CENTER">QUESTIONS AND ANSWERS</h1>
 <p>Questions and answers concerning the works of God among the children of Ethiopia and the principles of Athlicanity:</p>
 <p>Q1. What is the house of Athlyi?</p>
 <p>A. A house created by the Almighty God through his apostle Athlyi, giver of the Gospel and the law commanded by the Lord of the salvation of Ethiopia's posterities.</p>
 <p>Q2. What is the right wing to the house of Athlyi?</p>
 <p>A. The Afro Athlican Constructive Church.</p>
 <p>Q3. What is the left wing to the house of Athlyi?</p>
 <p>A. The Athlican green pasture.</p>
 <p>Q4. Can there be any other church or denomination save the Afro Athlican constructive church upon the law or the Gospel commanded by God to save the generations of Ethiopia?</p>
 <p>A. No.</p>
 <p><a name="page_101"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 101</font></a></p>
 <p>Q5. What is Athlicanity?</p>
 <p>A. The Law and the Gospel administered by Athlyi.</p>
 <p>Q6. Why can no other church be established upon this gospel?</p>
 <p>A. Because the Athlicanity is sent by the Lord our God not to teach confusion and hatred but to set up a real religious and material brotherhood among the children of Ethiopia.</p>
 <p>Q7. What is the Holy Piby?</p>
 <p>A. Holy book of God written unto the generations of Ethiopia.</p>
 <p>Q8. Do the Athlyians believe in Jesus Christ?</p>
 <p>A. As a true servant sent by God to seek and to save the lost house of Israel.</p>
 <p>Q9. Who did Athlyi, Marcus Garvey and colleagues come to save?</p>
 <p>A. The down trodden children of Ethiopia that they may raise to be a great power among the nations and the glory of their God.</p>
 <p>Q10. What is the Dictuary?</p>
 <p>A. The most holy place in the Afro Athlican Constructive Church or in the house of <a name="page_102"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 102</font></a> Athlyi, where the Shepherd or his apostles occupies to administer the gospel.</p>
 <p>Q11. What is God's Holy law to the children of Ethiopia?</p>
 <p>A. A written document handed to Athlyi by an angel of the Lord whose name was Douglas.</p>
 <p>Q12. What is the difference between God's holy law to the children of Israel and God's Holy Law to the children of Ethiopia?</p>
 <p>A. There is much difference, the holy law to the Israelites was given to Moses, but God's Holy Law given to the children of Ethiopia was handed to Athlyi by a messenger of the Lord our God, notwithstanding in the law given to the Israelites there are ten commandments, but in the law given to the children of Ethiopia there are twelve.</p>
 <p>Q13. What church is already established on God's Holy Law given to the children of Ethiopia?</p>
 <p>A. The Afro Athlican Constructive Church.</p>
 <p>Q14. By what other name shall this church be known?</p>
 <p>A. The house of Athlyi.</p>
