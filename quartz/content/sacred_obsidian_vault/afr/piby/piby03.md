 <h1 align="CENTER">PROCLAMATION OF THE HOUSE OF ATHLYI</h1>
 <p align="CENTER">Woodbridge, N.J., U.S.A. March 1924</p>
 <p>His Holiness, Shepherd Athlyi, 1st of the House of Athlyi, supreme lord of the Afro Athlican Constructive Church, requests that all Negroes the world over celebrate concord in its fullness.</p>
 <p>The first of August all members of the A.A.C. Church shall join in with the Universal Negro Improvement Association that there be a united day of joviality.</p>
 <p>O God, bless thine Apostles, and help us to establish a Heaven on earth.</p>
 <p align="CENTER">Proclamated by the<br>
 HOUSE OF ATHLYI</p>
