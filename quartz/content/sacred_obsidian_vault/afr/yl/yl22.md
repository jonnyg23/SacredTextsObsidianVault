 <h2 align="center">XX<br> ANOTHER STORY OF LEOPARD’S SPOTS</h2>
 
 <p>A<small>CCORDING</small> to another story, Leopard once had a very dark skin. He was prowling one day in a beautiful compound, when he noticed a little hut in which a lady was taking her bath.</p>
 
 <p> Round and round the hut Leopard walked, waiting for an opportunity to spring into the hut and seize his victim, for he was hungry.</p>
 
 <p> But as he passed the opening of the hut, the lady saw him, and, uttering a scream
 
 <!------------------------page 38------------------------>
 
 <a name="page_38"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 38</font></a>
 
 of terror, she threw at him her loofah, which was full of soap.</p>
 
 <table align="center" cellpadding="10">
 
 <tr><td><small>“She flung it at him and he fled,<br> But to this day the Leopard still<br> Is flecked with soap from foot to head!”<br></small></td></tr>
