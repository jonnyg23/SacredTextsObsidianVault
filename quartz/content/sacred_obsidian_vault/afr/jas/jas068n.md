 <center><i>NOTE:</i> </center><h3 align="CENTER">68. Sammy the Comferee.</h3>
 <p>This curious story seems to be a cross between the Potiphar's wife episode with which the ballad of "Young Seidal" opens, and the African tale of the lard girl who melts if exposed to the sun.</p>
 <p>Compare Dayrell, 1-2; Tremearne, 192; Parsons, Andros Island, 125 and note 2 for references.</p>
