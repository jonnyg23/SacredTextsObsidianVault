 <h3 align="CENTER">131. Dancing to Anansi's Fiddle.</h3>
 <i></i><p align="CENTER">Sarah Vassel, Bog, Westmoreland.</p>
 <p>Assono a run a gang. Assono sen' one of de men for water. When he go a take water, him couldn't take it; Anansi play fiddle into de water-hole--</p><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <font size="2"><p>"Zing a little ting!"</p></font></dir>
 </dir>
 
 <p>T'row down de gourd an' begin to dance. Assono a come to look fe de man. When he come, (Anansi stop playing). He call to him say, "Massa, no quarrel!" Him come give de massa de gourd a go fill it. Anansi begin playing, De Massa t'row down de gourd, begin dance. Assono dance till him drop. Anansi cut off him head an' tek de head make a water-cup.</p>
 <p>{p. 163}</p>
