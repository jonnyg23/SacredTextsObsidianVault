 <h3 align="CENTER">145. Fowl and Pretty Poll.[1]</h3>
 <i></i><p align="CENTER">Mary Jane Roden, Brownstown, St. Ann.</p>
 <p>Fowl invite Pretty Poll to chapel, den Pretty Poll said to Fowl, "Kyan't go to chapel for me soso fedder." Poll said to Kyan-crow, "Make go to chapel." Kyan-crow said to Pretty Poll him kyan't to go chapel for him peel-head young man.</p><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <font size="2"><p><a name="17601.jpg"><img src="img/17601.jpg"></a><br>
 Come we go to church, Pret-ty Poll.<br>
 Come we go to church, Pret-ty Poll.<br>
 Pret-ty Poll, wan' get mar-ried<br>
 Same time as a so so fed-der.<br>
 John Crow say he be de bride, same time he, peel-head young man.</p></font></dir>
 </dir>
 
 <p>[1. Jamaica negroes speak of the groom at a wedding as "the bride."]</p>
