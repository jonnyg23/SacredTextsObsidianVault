 <h3 align="CENTER">140. In Come Murray.</h3>
 <i></i><p align="CENTER">William Forbes, Dry River, Cock-pit country.</p>
 <p>It was said when you go you see a man going to play dat Nansi story. Get anodder wid a pint a water, den him gwine to turn a drunkard, begin to totter, say, "Tiger, tiger, lie down"--</p><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <font size="2"><p><a name="17000.jpg"><img src="img/17000.jpg"></a><br>
 Zin come Mur-ray. I take drink, lay down, Zin come Mur-ray.<br>
 Mur-ray, ah, lay down, Zin come Mur-ray. I take drink lay down,<br>
 Zin come Mur-ray. Oh, poor Mur-ray. Zin come Mur-ray.<br>
 I take drink, lay down, Zin come Mur-ray.</p></font></dir>
