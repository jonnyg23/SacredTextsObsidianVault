 <h3 align="CENTER">142. Anansi makes a Dance.</h3>
 <i></i><p align="CENTER">William Forbes, Dry River, Cock-pit country.</p>
 <p>Again, Anansi make a dance. Him playing de fiddle,</p><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <font size="2"><p><a name="17100.jpg"><img src="img/17100.jpg"></a><br>
 Ke-lly bam bom ba, Ke-lly bam ya Mor-ree!<br>
 Ke-lly bam bom ba, Ke-lly bam ya Mor-ree!<br>
 Ke-lly bam born ba, Ke-lly bam ya Mor-ree!<br>
 Kel-ly bam born ba, Kel-ly bam ya Mor-ree!<br>
 Dem dat kyan' run, dey no hea-rie! (etc.[3])<br>
 Kel-ly bam bom ba, Kel-ly bam bom ba,<br>
 Kel-ly bam bom ba, Kel-ly bam ya Mor-ree!<br>
 Kel-ly</p></font></dir>
 </dir>
 
 <p>[3. The record was faulty.]</p>
 <p>{p. 172}</p><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <font size="2"><p><a name="17200.jpg"><img src="img/17200.jpg"></a><br>
 bam bom ba, Kel-ly barn bom ba,<br>
 Kel-ly barn ya Mor-ree!<br>
 Kel-ly bam bom ba, Kel-ly bam bom ba,<br>
 Kel-ly barn bom ba, Kel-ly bam ya Mor-ree! --- --- --- --- --- --- --- --- --- --- etc.</p></font></dir>
 </dir>
 
 <p>Goat dere a dance, say,</p><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <font size="2"><p>"Me kyan' run, but me cunnie do!"</p></font></dir>
 </dir>
 
 <p>Dog begin to sing,</p><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <font size="2"><p>"Na way you lie, Samedy,<br>
 Pussy no dead at all!"</p></font></dir>
 </dir>
 
 <p>Den Puss an' Rat begin to dance an' say,</p><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <font size="2"><p>"Massa Puss an' Massa Rat a jump shandelay,[1]</p>
 <p><a name="17201.jpg"><img src="img/17201.jpg"></a><br>
 Oh, jump shan-de- lay, jump shan-de-lay, den a jump shan-de-lay.<br>
 Oh, oh, jump shan-de-lay--Mis-sa Rat a jump den 'a jump shan-de lay!<br>
 Oh, oh jump shan-de-lay. Jump shan--de--lay, will you jump shan-de-lay!<br>
 Oh, oh, jump shan-de-lay, ob, Mis-sa Rat a jump, will you jump, shan-de-lay!<br>
 Den she went an' jump it, den she went an' jump it, den she went an' jump it,<br>
 den she went an' jump it, den she went an' jump it, den she went an' jump it.</p></font></dir>
 </dir>
 
 <p>Well, Anansi boy was blowing de fife,</p><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <font size="2"><p>"Ti-li-harry-ham, handsome ben-in-ben!"</p></font></dir>
 </dir>
 
 <p>[1. Part of the first of the song is missing; phonograph needle was put down too late.]</p>
 <p>{p. 173}</p>
 <p>Den de bull was coming along, hear dem, say,</p><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <font size="2"><p><a name="17300.jpg"><img src="img/17300.jpg"></a><br>
 Oh who dey cal me Tim-mo Lim-mo?<br>
 Tim-mo Lim-mo, oh?<br>
 Zin ku-ma Ya ya ya, oh, Zin ku-ma.<br>
 Tim-mo Lim-mo, oh, Tim-mo Lim-mo oh, Zin ku ma.<br>
 Ya, ya, ya, oh, Zin ku-ma.<br>
 Ah, who dey ca' me Tim-mo Lim-mo?<br>
 Ah, who dey ca' me Tim-mo Lim-mo, oh, Zin ku-ma. Ya, ya, ya oh,<br>
 Zin ku-ma Tim-mo Lim-mo, oh, Zin ku-ma.<br>
 Ah who dey ca' me Tim-mo Lim-mo?<br>
 Ah, who dey ca' me, Tim-mo Lim-mo,<br>
 Tim-mo Lim-mo, oh, Zin ku-ma. Ya, ya, ya, oh, Zin ku-ma.</p></font></dir>
 </dir>
