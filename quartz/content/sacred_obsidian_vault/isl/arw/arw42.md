 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>Arabian Wisdom</i>, by John Wortabet, [1913], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center"><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Rashness</span></h3>  
 <p>Beware of rashness, for it has well been called the Mother of Regrets.</p>  
 <p>He who acts hastily either makes a blunder, or comes very near it.</p>  
 <p><a name="page_50"><font size="1" color="green">p. 50</font></a></p>  
 <p>He who is deliberate is either right, or very nearly so.</p>  
 <p>A hasty act comes from the Evil One, and a deliberate act from God.</p>  
 <p>Haste is the resort of the weak.</p>
