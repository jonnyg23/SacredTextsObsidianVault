 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 1:813</h3><p><i>Narrated 'Ata':</i></p>
 I heard Jabir bin 'Abdullah saying, "The Prophet said, 'Whoever eats 
 (from) this plant (he meant garlic) should keep away from our mosque."
 I said, "What does he mean by that?" He replied, "I think he means 
 only raw garlic."
