 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 1:671</h3><p><i>Narrated Abu Huraira:</i></p>
 Allah's Apostle said, "If anyone of you leads the people in the 
 prayer, he should shorten it for amongst them are the weak, the sick 
 and the old; and if anyone among your prays alone then he may prolong 
 (the prayer) as much as he wishes. "
