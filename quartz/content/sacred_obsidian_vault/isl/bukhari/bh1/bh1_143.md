 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 1:143</h3><p><i>Narrated Ibn 'Abbas:</i></p>
 The Prophet said, "If anyone of you on having sexual relations with 
 his wife said (and he must say it before starting) 'In the name of 
 Allah. O Allah! Protect us from Satan and also protect what you bestow
 upon us (i.e. the coming offspring) from Satan, and if it is destined 
 that they should have a child then, Satan will never be able to harm 
 that offspring."
