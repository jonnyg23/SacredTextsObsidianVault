 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 1:193</h3><p><i>Narrated Jabir:</i></p>
 Allah's Apostle came to visit me while I was sick and unconscious. He 
 performed ablution and sprinkled the remaining water on me and I 
 became conscious and said, "O Allah's Apostle! To whom will my 
 inheritance go as I have neither ascendants nor descendants?" Then the
 Divine verses regarding Fara'id (inheritance) were revealed.
