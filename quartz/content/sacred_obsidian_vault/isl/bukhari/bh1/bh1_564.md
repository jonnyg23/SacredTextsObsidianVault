 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 1:562</h3><p><i>Narrated Abu Huraira:</i></p>
 Allah's Apostle forbade the offering of two prayers:
 <p>
 1. after the morning prayer till the sunrises.
 </p><p>
 2. after the 'Asr prayer till the sun sets.
 </p><p>
