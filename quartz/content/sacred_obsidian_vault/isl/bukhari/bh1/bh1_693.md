 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 1:691</h3><p><i>Narrated Anas bin Malik:</i></p>
 I arrived at Medina and was asked whether I found any change since the
 days of Allah's Apostle. I said, "I have not found any change except 
 that you do not stand in alignment in your prayers."
