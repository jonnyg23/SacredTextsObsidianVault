 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 1:227</h3><p><i>Narrated Asma':</i></p>
 A woman came to the Prophet and said, "If anyone of us gets menses in 
 her clothes then what should she do?" He replied, "She should (take 
 hold of the soiled place), rub it and put it in the water and rub it 
 in order to remove the traces of blood and then pour water over it. 
 Then she can pray in it."
