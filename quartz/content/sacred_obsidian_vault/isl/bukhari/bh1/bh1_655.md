 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 1:653</h3><p><i>Narrated Malik bin Huwairth:</i></p>
 We went to the Prophet and we were all young men and stayed with him 
 for about twenty nights. The Prophet was very merciful. He said, "When
 you return home, impart religious teachings to your families and tell 
 them to offer perfectly such and such a prayer at such and such a time
 and such and such a prayer at such and such a time. And al the time of
 the prayer one of you should pronounce the Adhan and the oldest of you
 should lead the prayer."
