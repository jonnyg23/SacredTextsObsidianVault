 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 1:697</h3><p><i>Narrated 'Aisha:</i></p>
 The Prophet had a mat which he used to spread during the day and use 
 as a curtain at night. So a number of people gathered at night facing 
 it and prayed behind him.
