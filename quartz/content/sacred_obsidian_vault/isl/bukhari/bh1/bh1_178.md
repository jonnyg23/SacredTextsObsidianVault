 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 1:178</h3><p><i>Narrated 'Ali:</i></p>
 I used to get emotional urethral discharges frequently and felt shy to
 ask Allah's Apostle about it. So I requested Al-Miqdad bin Al-Aswad to
 ask (the Prophet ) about it. Al-Miqdad asked him and he replied, "On 
 has to perform ablution (after it)."
