 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 1:236</h3><p><i>Narrated Maimuna:</i></p>
 Allah's Apostle was asked regarding ghee (cooking butter) in which a 
 mouse had fallen. He said, "Take out the mouse and throw away the ghee
 around it and use the rest."
