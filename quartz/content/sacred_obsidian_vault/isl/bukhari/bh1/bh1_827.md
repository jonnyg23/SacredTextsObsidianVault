 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 1:825</h3><p><i>Narrated Um Salama:</i></p>
 (the wife of the Prophet) In the lifetime of Allah's Apostle the women
 used to get up when they finished their compulsory prayers with 
 Taslim. The Prophet and the men would stay on at their places as long 
 as Allah will. When the Prophet got up, the men would then get up.
