 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 1:638</h3><p><i>Narrated Abu Sa'id Al-Khudri:</i></p>
 A cloud came and it rained till the roof started leaking and in those 
 days the roof used to be of the branches of date-palms. Iqama was 
 pronounced and I saw Allah's Apostles prostrating in water and mud and
 even I saw the mark of mud on his forehead.
