 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 1:498</h3><p><i>Narrated 'Aisha:</i></p>
 It is not good that you people have made us (women) equal to dogs and 
 donkeys. No doubt I saw Allah's Apostle praying while I used to lie 
 between him and the Qibla and when he wanted to prostrate, he pushed 
 my legs and I withdrew them.
