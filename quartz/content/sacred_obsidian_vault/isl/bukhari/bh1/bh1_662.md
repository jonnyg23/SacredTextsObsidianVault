 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 1:660</h3><p><i>Narrated Abu Huraira:</i></p>
 The Prophet said, "Isn't he who raises his head before the Imam afraid
 that Allah may transform his head into that of a donkey or his figure 
 (face) into that of a donkey?"
