 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 1:644</h3><p><i>Narrated Al-Aswad:</i></p>
 That he asked 'Aisha "What did the Prophet use to do in his house?" 
 She replied, "He used to keep himself busy serving his family and when
 it was the time for prayer he would go for it."
