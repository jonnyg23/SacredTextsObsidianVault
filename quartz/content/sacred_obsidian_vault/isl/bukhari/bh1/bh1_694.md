 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 1:692</h3><p><i>Narrated Anas bin Malik:</i></p>
 The Prophet said, "Straighten your rows for I see you from behind my 
 back." Anas added, "Everyone of us used to put his shoulder with the 
 shoulder of his companion and his foot with the foot of his 
 companion."
