 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 1:632</h3><p><i>Narrated Malik Ibn Buhaina:</i></p>
 Allah's Apostle passed by a man praying two Rakat after the Iqama (had
 been pronounced). When Allah's Apostle completed the prayer, the 
 people gathered around him (the Prophet) or that man and Allah's 
 Apostle said to him (protesting), Are there four Rakat in Fajr prayer?
 Are there four Rakat in Fajr prayer?"
