 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 1:77</h3><p><i>Narrated Mahmud bin Rabi'a:</i></p>
 When I was a boy of five, I remember, the Prophet took water from a 
 bucket (used far getting water out of a well) with his mouth and threw
 it on my face.
