 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 1:821</h3><p><i>Narrated 'Aisha:</i></p>
 Once Allah's Apostle delayed the 'Isha' prayer till 'Umar informed him
 that the women and children had slept. Then Allah's Apostle came out 
 and said: "None from amongst the dwellers of earth have prayed this 
 prayer except you." In those days none but the people of Medina 
 prayed.
