 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 1:492</h3><p><i>Narrated 'Aisha:</i></p>
 the wife of the Prophet, "I used to sleep in front of Allah's Apostle 
 with my legs opposite his Qibla (facing him); and whenever he 
 prostrated, he pushed my feet and I withdrew them and whenever he 
 stood, I stretched them." 'Aisha added, "In those days there were no 
 lamps in the houses."
