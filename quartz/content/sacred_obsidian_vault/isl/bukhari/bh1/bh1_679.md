 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 1:677</h3><p><i>Narrated Anas bin Malik:</i></p>
 The Prophet said, "When I start the prayer I intend to prolong it, but
 on hearing the cries of a child, I cut short the prayer because I know
 that the cries of the child will incite its mother's passions."
