 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 1:827</h3><p><i>Narrated 'Abdullah bin Abi Qatada Al-Ansari:</i></p>
 My father said, "Allah's Apostle said, "Whenever I stand for prayer, I
 want to prolong it but on hearing the cries of a child, I would 
 shorten it as I dislike to put its mother in trouble."
