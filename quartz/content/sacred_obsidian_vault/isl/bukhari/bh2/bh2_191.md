 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 2:192</h3><p><i>Narrated Ibn 'Umar:</i></p>
 The Prophet said, "A woman should not travel for more than three days 
 except with a Dhi-Mahram (i.e. a male with whom she cannot marry at 
 all, e.g. her brother, father, grandfather, etc.) or her own 
 husband.)"
