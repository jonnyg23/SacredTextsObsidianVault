 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 2:205</h3><p><i>Narrated Hafs bin 'Asim:</i></p>
 Ibn 'Umar went on a journey and said, "I accompanied the Prophet and 
 he did not offer optional prayers during the journey, and Allah says: 
 'Verily! In Allah's Apostle you have a good example to follow.' " 
 (33.21)
