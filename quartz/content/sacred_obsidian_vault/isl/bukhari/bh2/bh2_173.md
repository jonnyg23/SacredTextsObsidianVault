 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 2:174</h3><p><i>Narrated Abu Huraira:</i></p>
 On Fridays the Prophet used to recite Alf Lam Mim Tanzil-As-Sajda (in 
 the first Raka) and Hal ata'alal-lnsani i.e. Suratad-Dahr (LXXVI) (in 
 the second Raka), in the Fajr prayer.
