 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 2:463</h3><p><i>Narrated Anas bin Malik</i></p>
 Allah's Apostle (p.b.u.h) said, "Any Muslim whose three children died 
 before the age of puberty will be granted Paradise by Allah because of
 His mercy to them."
