 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 2:584</h3><p><i>Narrated Abu Sa'id Al-Khudri:</i></p>
 In the life-time of the Prophet we used to give one Sa' of food or one
 Sa' of dates or one Sa' of barley or one Sa' of Raisins (dried grapes)
 as Sadaqat-ul-Fitr. And when Muawiya became the Caliph and the wheat 
 was (available in abundance) he said, "I think (observe) that one Mudd
 (of wheat) equals two Mudds (of any of the above mentioned things).
