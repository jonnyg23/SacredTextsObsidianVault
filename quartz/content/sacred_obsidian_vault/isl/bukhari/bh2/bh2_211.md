 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 2:212</h3><p><i>Narrated Anas bin Malik:</i></p>
 Whenever the Prophet started a journey before noon, he used to delay 
 the Zuhr prayer till the time of 'Asr and then offer them together; 
 and if the sun declined (at noon) he used to offer the Zuhr prayer and
 then ride (for the journey).
