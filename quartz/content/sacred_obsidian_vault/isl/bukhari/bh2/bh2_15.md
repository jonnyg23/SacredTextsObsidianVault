 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 2:16</h3><p><i>Narrated Abu Huraira:</i></p>
 The Prophet used to recite the following in the Fajr prayer of Friday,
 "Alif, Lam, Mim, Tanzil" (Surat-as-Sajda #32) and 
 "Hal-ata-ala-l-Insani" (i.e. Surah-Ad-Dahr #76).
