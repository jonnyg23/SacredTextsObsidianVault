 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 2:29</h3><p><i>Narrated Anas bin Malik:</i></p>
 The Prophet used to offer the prayer earlier if it was very cold; and 
 if it was very hot he used to delay the prayer, i.e. the Jumua prayer.
