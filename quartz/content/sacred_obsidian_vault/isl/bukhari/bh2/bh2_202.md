 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 2:203</h3><p><i>Narrated Jabir bin 'Abdullah</i></p>
 The Prophet used to pray (the Nawafil) on his Mount facing east and 
 whenever he wanted to offer the compulsory prayer, he used to dismount
 and face the Qibla.
