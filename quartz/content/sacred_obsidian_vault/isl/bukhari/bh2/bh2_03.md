 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 2:4</h3><p><i>Narrated Abu Said Al-Khudri:</i></p>
 Allah's Apostle (p.b.u.h) said, "The taking of a bath on Friday is 
 compulsory for every male (Muslim) who has attained the age of 
 puberty."
