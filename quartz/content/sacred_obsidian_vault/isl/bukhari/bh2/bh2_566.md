 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 2:565</h3><p><i>Narrated Anas bin Malik:</i></p>
 Allah's Apostle forbade the selling of fruits until they were ripe. 
 The Prophet (p.b.u.h) added, "It means that they become red ."
