 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 2:240</h3><p><i>Narrated Masruq:</i></p>
 I asked Aisha about the night prayer of Allah's Apostle and she said, 
 "It was seven, nine or eleven Rakat besides the two Rakat of the Fajr 
 prayer (i.e. Sunna). "
