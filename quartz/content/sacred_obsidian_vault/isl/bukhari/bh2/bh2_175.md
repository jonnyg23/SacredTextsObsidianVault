 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 2:176</h3><p><i>Narrated 'Abdullah bin Masud:</i></p>
 The Prophet recited Surat-an-Najm (53) and prostrated while reciting 
 it and all the people prostrated and a man amongst the people took a 
 handful of stones or earth and raised it to his face and said, "This 
 is sufficient for me. Later on I saw him killed as a Nobel ever. "
