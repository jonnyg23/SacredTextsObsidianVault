 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 2:790</h3><p><i>Narrated Ibn Abbas:</i></p>
 The Prophet was asked about the slaughtering, shaving (of the head), 
 and the doing of Rami before or after the due times. He said, "There 
 is no harm in that."
