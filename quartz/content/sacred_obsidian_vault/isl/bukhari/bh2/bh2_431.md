 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 2:430</h3><p><i>Narrated Jabir:</i></p>
 The Prophet said, "Bury them (i.e. martyrs) with their blood." (that 
 was) On the day of the Battle of Uhud. He did not get them washed.
