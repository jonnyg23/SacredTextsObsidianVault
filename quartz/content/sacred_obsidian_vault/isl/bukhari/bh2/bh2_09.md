 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 2:10</h3><p><i>Narrated Tawus:</i></p>
 Ibn 'Abbas mentioned the statement of the Prophet regarding the taking
 of a bath on Friday and then I asked him whether the Prophet (p.b.u.h)
 had ordered perfume or (hair) oil to be used if they could be found in
 one's house. He (Ibn 'Abbas) replied that he did not know about it.
