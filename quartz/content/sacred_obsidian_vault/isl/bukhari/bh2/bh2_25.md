 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 2:26</h3><p><i>Narrated Yahya bin Said:</i></p>
 I asked 'Amra about taking a bath on Fridays. She replied, "  Aisha 
 said, 'The people used to work (for their livelihood) and whenever 
 they went for the Jumua prayer, they used to go to the mosque in the 
 same shape as they had been in work. So they were asked to take a bath
 on Friday.' "
