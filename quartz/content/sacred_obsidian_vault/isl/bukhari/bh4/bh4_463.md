 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 4:459</h3><p><i>Narrated Samura:</i></p>
 The Prophet said, "Last night I saw (in a dream) two men coming to me.
 One of them said, "The person who kindles the fire is Malik, the 
 gate-keeper of the (Hell) Fire, and I am Gabriel, and this is 
 Michael."
