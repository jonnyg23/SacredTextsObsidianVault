 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 4:536</h3><p><i>Narrated Abu Huraira:</i></p>
 Allah's Apostle said, "Once while a prophet amongst the prophets was 
 taking a rest underneath a tree, an ant bit him. He, therefore, 
 ordered that his luggage be taken away from underneath that tree and 
 then ordered that the dwelling place of the ants should be set on 
 fire. Allah sent him a revelation:-- "Wouldn't it have been sufficient
 to burn a single ant? (that bit you): (See Page 162, chapter No. 
 153).
