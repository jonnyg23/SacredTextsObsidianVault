 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 4:528</h3><p><i>Narrated 'Aisha:</i></p>
 The Prophet ordered that a short-tailed or mutilated-tailed snake 
 (i.e. Abtar) should be killed, for it blinds the on-looker and causes 
 abortion."
