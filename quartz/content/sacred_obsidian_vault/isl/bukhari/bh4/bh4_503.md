 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 4:499</h3><p><i>Narrated 'Abdullah bin 'Umar:</i></p>
 I saw Allah's Apostle pointing towards the east saying, "Lo! 
 Afflictions will verily emerge hence; afflictions will verily emerge 
 hence where the (side of the head of) Satan appears."
