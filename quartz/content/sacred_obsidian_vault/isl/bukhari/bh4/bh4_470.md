 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 4:466</h3><p><i>Narrated 'Abdullah bin Qais Al-Ashari:</i></p>
 The Prophet said, "A tent (in Paradise) is like a hollow pearl which 
 is thirty miles in height and on every corner of the tent the believer
 Imran in another narration, "(The tent is) sixty miles (in height)")
