 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 4:506</h3><p><i>Narrated Abu Huraira:</i></p>
 The Prophet said, "When any human being is born. Satan touches him at 
 both sides of the body with his two fingers, except Jesus, the son of 
 Mary, whom Satan tried to touch but failed, for he touched the 
 placenta-cover instead."
