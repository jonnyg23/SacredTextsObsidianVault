 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 4:573</h3><p><i>Narrated Samura:</i></p>
 Allah's Apostle said, "Two persons came to me at night (in dream) (and
 took me along with them). We passed by a tall man who was so tall that
 I was not able to see his head and that person was Abraham."
