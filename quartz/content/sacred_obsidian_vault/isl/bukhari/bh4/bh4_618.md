 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 4:615</h3><p><i>Narrated Abu Huraira:</i></p>
 Allah's Apostle said, "It was said to Bani Israel, Enter the gate (of 
 the town) with humility (prostrating yourselves) and saying: 
 "Repentance", but they changed the word and entered the town crawling 
 on their buttocks and saying: "A wheat grain in the hair."
