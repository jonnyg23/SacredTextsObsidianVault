 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 4:439</h3><p><i>Narrated Abu Huraira:</i></p>
 I heard the Prophet saying, "Who ever spends a couple (of objects) in 
 Allah's cause, will be called by the Gatekeepers of Paradise who will 
 say, "O so-and-so, come on!" Abu Bakr said, "Such a person will never 
 perish or be miserable' The Prophet said, "I hope you will be among 
 such person."
