 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 4:531</h3><p><i>Narrated 'Aisha:</i></p>
 The Prophet said, "Five kinds of animals are mischief-doers and can be
 killed even in the Sanctuary: They are the rat the scorpion, the kite,
 the crow and the rabid dog."
