 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 4:415</h3><p><i>Narrated Abu Huraira:</i></p>
 Allah's Apostle said, "Allah the Most Superior said, "The son of Adam 
 slights Me, and he should not slight Me, and he disbelieves in Me, and
 he ought not to do so. As for his slighting Me, it is that he says 
 that I have a son; and his disbelief in Me is his statement that I 
 shall not recreate him as I have created (him) before."
