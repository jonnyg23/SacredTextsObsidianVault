 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 4:545</h3><p><i>Narrated Abu Salama:</i></p>
 Um Salama said, "Um Salaim said, 'O Allah's Apostle! Allah does not 
 refrain from saying the truth! Is it obligatory for a woman to take a 
 bath after she gets nocturnal discharge?' He said, 'Yes, if she 
 notices the water (i.e. discharge).' Um Salama smiled and said, 'Does 
 a woman get discharge?' Allah's Apostle said. 'Then why does a child 
 resemble (its mother)?"
