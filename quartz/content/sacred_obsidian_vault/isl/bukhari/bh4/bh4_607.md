 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 4:604</h3><p><i>Narrated Abu Huraira:</i></p>
 The Prophet said, "While Job was naked, taking a bath, a swarm of gold
 locusts fell on him and he started collecting them in his garment. His
 Lord called him, 'O Job! Have I not made you rich enough to need what 
 you see? He said, 'Yes, O Lord! But I cannot dispense with your 
 Blessing."'
