 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 4:50</h3><p><i>Narrated Anas bin Malik:</i></p>
 The Prophet said, "A single endeavor (of fighting) in Allah's Cause in
 the forenoon or in the afternoon is better than the world and whatever
 is in it."
