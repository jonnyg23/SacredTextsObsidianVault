 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 4:259</h3><p><i>Narrated Abu Huraira:</i></p>
 Allah's Apostle sent us in a mission (i.e. am army-unit) and said, "If
 you find so-and-so and so-and-so, burn both of them with fire." When 
 we intended to depart, Allah's Apostle said, "I have ordered you to 
 burn so-and-so and so-and-so, and it is none but Allah Who punishes 
 with fire, so, if you find them, kill them."
