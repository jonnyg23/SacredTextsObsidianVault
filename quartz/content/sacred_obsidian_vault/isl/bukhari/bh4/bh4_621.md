 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 4:618</h3><p><i>Narrated Jabir bin 'Abdullah:</i></p>
 We were with Allah's Apostle picking the fruits of the 'Arak trees, 
 and Allah's Apostle said, "Pick the black fruit, for it is the best." 
 The companions asked, "Were you a shepherd?" He replied, "There was no
 prophet who was not a shepherd."
