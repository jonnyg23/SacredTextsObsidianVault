 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 4:632</h3><p><i>Narrated Mujahid:</i></p>
 I asked Ibn 'Abbas, "Should we perform a prostration on reciting 
 Surat-Sad?" He recited (the Sura) including: 'And among his progeny, 
 David, Solomon..(up to)...so follow their guidance (6.84-91) And then 
 he said, "Your Prophet is amongst those people who have been ordered 
 to follow them (i.e. the preceding apostles).
