 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 3:54</h3><p><i>Narrated Hafsa:</i></p>
 Allah's Apostle said, "It is not sinful (of a Muhrim) to kill five 
 kinds of animals, namely: the crow, the kite, the mouse, the scorpion 
 and the rabid dog."
