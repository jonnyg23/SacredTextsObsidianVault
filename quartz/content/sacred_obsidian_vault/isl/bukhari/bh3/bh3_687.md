 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 3:688</h3><p><i>Narrated Abu Huraira:</i></p>
 The Prophet said, "One can ride the mortgaged animal because of what 
 one spends on it, and one can drink the milk of a milch animal as long
 as it is mortgaged. "
