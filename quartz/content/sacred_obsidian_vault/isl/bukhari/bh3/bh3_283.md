 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 3:286</h3><p><i>Narrated Al-Miqdam:</i></p>
 The Prophet said, "Nobody has ever eaten a better meal than that which
 one has earned by working with one's own hands. The Prophet of Allah, 
 David used to eat from the earnings of his manual labor."
