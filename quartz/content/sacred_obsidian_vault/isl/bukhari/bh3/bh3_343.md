 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 3:346</h3><p><i>Narrated Ibn 'Umar:</i></p>
 The Prophet said, "The buyer of foodstuff should not sell it before it
 has been measured for him." Isma'il narrated instead, "He should not 
 sell it before receiving it."
