 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 3:633</h3><p><i>Narrated Abu Salama:</i></p>
 That there was a dispute between him and some people (about a piece of
 land). When he told 'Aisha about it, she said, "O Abu Salama! Avoid 
 taking the land unjustly, for the Prophet said, 'Whoever usurps even 
 one span of the land of somebody, his neck will be encircled with it 
 down the seven earths."
