 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 3:24</h3><p><i>Narrated Ibn Abbas:</i></p>
 When the Prophet arrived at Mecca, some boys of the tribe of Bani 
 'Abdul Muttalib went to receive him, and the Prophet made one of them 
 ride in front of him and the other behind him.
