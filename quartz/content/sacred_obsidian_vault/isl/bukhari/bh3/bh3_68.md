 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 3:69</h3><p><i>Narrated Ibn Abbas:</i></p>
 The Prophet delivered a sermon at 'Arafat and said, "Whoever does not 
 get an Izar can wear trousers, and whoever cannot get a pair of shoes 
 can wear Khuffs."
