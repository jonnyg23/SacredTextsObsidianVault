 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 3:372</h3><p><i>Narrated Tawus:</i></p>
 I asked Ibn 'Abbas, "What is the meaning of, 'No town dweller should 
 sell (or buy) for a desert dweller'?" Ibn 'Abbas said, "It means he 
 should not become his broker."
