 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 3:72</h3><p><i>Narrated Anas bin Malik:</i></p>
 Allah's Apostle entered Mecca in the year of its Conquest wearing an 
 Arabian helmet on his head and when the Prophet took it off, a person 
 came and said, "Ibn Khatal is holding the covering of the Ka'ba 
 (taking refuge in the Ka'ba)." The Prophet said, "Kill him."
