 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 3:172</h3><p><i>Narrated Abu Said:</i></p>
 The Prophet said, "Isn't it true that a woman does not pray and does 
 not fast on menstruating? And that is the defect (a loss) in her 
 religion."
