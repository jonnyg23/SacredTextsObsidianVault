 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 3:624</h3><p><i>Narrated Anas:</i></p>
 Allah's Apostle said, "Help your brother, whether he is an oppressor 
 or he is an oppressed one. People asked, "O Allah's Apostle! It is all
 right to help him if he is oppressed, but how should we help him if he
 is an oppressor?" The Prophet said, "By preventing him from oppressing
 others."
