 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 3:247</h3><p><i>Narrated 'Aisha:</i></p>
 The Prophet used to embrace me during my menses. He also used to put 
 his head out of the mosque while he was in Itikaf, and I would wash it
 during my menses.
