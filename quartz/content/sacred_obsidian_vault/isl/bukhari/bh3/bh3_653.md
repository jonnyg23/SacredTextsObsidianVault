 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 3:654</h3><p><i>Narrated 'Abdullah bin Yazid Al-Ansari:</i></p>
 The Prophet forbade robbery (taking away what belongs to others 
 without their permission), and also forbade mutilation (or maiming) of
 bodies.
