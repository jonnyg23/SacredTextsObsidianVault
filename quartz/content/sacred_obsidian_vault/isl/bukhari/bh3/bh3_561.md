 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 3:562</h3><p><i>Narrated Abu Huraira:</i></p>
 Allah's Apostle said, "No doubt, you had better gather a bundle of 
 wood and carry it on your back (and earn your living thereby) rather 
 than ask somebody who may give you or not."
