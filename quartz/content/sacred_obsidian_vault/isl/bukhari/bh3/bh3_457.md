 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 3:460</h3><p><i>Narrated Aisha:</i></p>
 I said, "O Allah's Apostle! I have two neighbors and would like to 
 know to which of them I should give presents." He replied, "To the one
 whose door is nearer to you."
