 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 3:640</h3><p><i>Narrated Aisha:</i></p>
 Hind bint 'Utba (Abu Sufyan's wife) came and said, "O Allah's Apostle!
 Abu Sufyan is a miser. Is there any harm if I spend something from his
 property for our children?" He said, there is no harm for you if you 
 feed them from it justly and reasonably (with no extravagance)."
