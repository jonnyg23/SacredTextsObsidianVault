 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 3:642</h3><p><i>Narrated 'Umar:</i></p>
 When Allah took away the soul of His Prophet at his death, the Ansar 
 assembled In the shed of Bani Sa'ida. I said to Abu Bakr, "Let us go."
 So, we come to them (i.e. to Ansar) at the shed of Bani Sa'ida.  (See 
 Hadith No. 19, Vol. 5 for details)
