 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 3:78</h3><p><i>Narrated Ibn 'Abbas:</i></p>
 A woman from the tribe of Khath'am came in the year (of 
 ,Hajjat-ul-wada' of the Prophet ) and said, "O Allah's Apostle! My 
 father has come under Allah's obligation of performing Hajj but he is 
 a very old man and cannot sit properly on his Mount. Will the 
 obligation be fulfilled if I perform Hajj on his behalf?" The Prophet 
 replied in the affirmative.
