 <h1 align="CENTER">100. al-`Adiyat: The Assaulters</h1>
 <a name="an_100_001">1</a> By the snorting courses, 
 <a name="an_100_002">2</a> Striking sparks of fire 
 <a name="an_100_003">3</a> And scouring to the raid at dawn, 
 <a name="an_100_004">4</a> Then, therewith, with their trail of dust, 
 <a name="an_100_005">5</a> Cleaving, as one, the centre (of the foe), 
 <a name="an_100_006">6</a> Lo! man is an ingrate unto his Lord 
 <a name="an_100_007">7</a> And lo! he is a witness unto that; 
 <a name="an_100_008">8</a> And lo! in the love of wealth he is violent. 
 <a name="an_100_009">9</a> Knoweth he not that, when the contents of the graves are poured forth 
 <a name="an_100_010">10</a> And the secrets of the breasts are made known, 
 <a name="an_100_011">11</a> On that day will their Lord be perfectly informed concerning them.
