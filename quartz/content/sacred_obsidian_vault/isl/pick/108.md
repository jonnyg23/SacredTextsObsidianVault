 <h1 align="CENTER">108. al-Kauthar: Abundance</h1>
 <a name="an_108_001">1</a> Lo! We have given thee Abundance; 
 <a name="an_108_002">2</a> So pray unto thy Lord, and sacrifice. 
 <a name="an_108_003">3</a> Lo! it is thy insulter (and not thou) who is without posterity.
