 <h1 align="CENTER">82. al-Infitar: The Cleaving Asunder</h1>
 <a name="an_082_001">1</a> When the heaven is cleft asunder, 
 <a name="an_082_002">2</a> When the planets are dispersed, 
 <a name="an_082_003">3</a> When the seas are poured forth, 
 <a name="an_082_004">4</a> And the sepulchres are overturned, 
 <a name="an_082_005">5</a> A soul will know what it hath sent before (it) and what left behind. 
 <a name="an_082_006">6</a> O man! What hath made thee careless concerning thy Lord, the Bountiful, 
 <a name="an_082_007">7</a> Who created thee, then fashioned, then proportioned thee ? 
 <a name="an_082_008">8</a> Into whatsoever form He will, He casteth thee. 
 <a name="an_082_009">9</a> Nay, but ye deny the Judgment. 
 <a name="an_082_010">10</a> Lo! there are above you guardians, 
 <a name="an_082_011">11</a> Generous and recording, 
 <a name="an_082_012">12</a> Who know (all) that ye do. 
 <a name="an_082_013">13</a> Lo! the righteous verily will be in delight. 
 <a name="an_082_014">14</a> And lo! the wicked verily will be in hell; 
 <a name="an_082_015">15</a> They will burn therein on the Day of Judgment, 
 <a name="an_082_016">16</a> And will not be absent thence. 
 <a name="an_082_017">17</a> Ah, what will convey unto thee what the Day of Judgment is! 
 <a name="an_082_018">18</a> Again, what will convey unto thee what the Day of Judgment is! 
 <a name="an_082_019">19</a> A day on which no soul hath power at all for any (other) soul. The (absolute)  command on that day is Allah's.
