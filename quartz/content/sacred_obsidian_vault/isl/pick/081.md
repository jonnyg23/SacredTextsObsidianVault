 <h1 align="CENTER">81. at-Takwir: The Cessation</h1>
 <a name="an_081_001">1</a> When the sun is overthrown, 
 <a name="an_081_002">2</a> And when the stars fall, 
 <a name="an_081_003">3</a> And when the hills are moved, 
 <a name="an_081_004">4</a> And when the camels big with young are abandoned, 
 <a name="an_081_005">5</a> And when the wild beasts are herded together, 
 <a name="an_081_006">6</a> And when the seas rise, 
 <a name="an_081_007">7</a> And when souls are reunited, 
 <a name="an_081_008">8</a> And when the girl-child that was buried alive is asked 
 <a name="an_081_009">9</a> For what sin she was slain, 
 <a name="an_081_010">10</a> And when the pages are laid open, 
 <a name="an_081_011">11</a> And when the sky is torn away, 
 <a name="an_081_012">12</a> And when hell is lighted, 
 <a name="an_081_013">13</a> And when the Garden is brought nigh, 
 <a name="an_081_014">14</a> (Then) every soul will know what it hath made ready. 
 <a name="an_081_015">15</a> Oh, but I call to witness the planets, 
 <a name="an_081_016">16</a> The stars which rise and set, 
 <a name="an_081_017">17</a> And the close of night, 
 <a name="an_081_018">18</a> And the breath of morning 
 <a name="an_081_019">19</a> That this is in truth the word of an honoured messenger, 
 <a name="an_081_020">20</a> Mighty, established in the presence of the Lord of the Throne, 
 <a name="an_081_021">21</a> (One) to be obeyed, and trustworthy; 
 <a name="an_081_022">22</a> And your comrade is not mad. 
 <a name="an_081_023">23</a> Surely he beheld Him on the clear horizon. 
 <a name="an_081_024">24</a> And he is not avid of the Unseen. 
 <a name="an_081_025">25</a> Nor is this the utterance of a devil worthy to be stoned. 
 <a name="an_081_026">26</a> Whither then go ye ? 
 <a name="an_081_027">27</a> This is naught else than a reminder unto creation, 
 <a name="an_081_028">28</a> Unto whomsoever of you willeth to walk straight. 
 <a name="an_081_029">29</a> And ye will not, unless (it be) that Allah willeth, the Lord of Creation.
