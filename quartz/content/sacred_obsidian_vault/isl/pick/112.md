 <h1 align="CENTER">112. al-Ikhlas: The Unity</h1>
 <a name="an_112_001">1</a> Say: He is Allah, the One! 
 <a name="an_112_002">2</a> Allah, the eternally Besought of all! 
 <a name="an_112_003">3</a> He begetteth not nor was begotten. 
 <a name="an_112_004">4</a> And there is none comparable unto Him.
