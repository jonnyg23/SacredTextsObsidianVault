 <h1 align="CENTER">77. al-Mursalat: The Emissaries</h1>
 <a name="an_077_001">1</a> By the emissary winds, (sent) one after another 
 <a name="an_077_002">2</a> By the raging hurricanes, 
 <a name="an_077_003">3</a> By those which cause earth's vegetation to revive; 
 <a name="an_077_004">4</a> By those who winnow with a winnowing, 
 <a name="an_077_005">5</a> By those who bring down the Reminder, 
 <a name="an_077_006">6</a> To excuse or to warn, 
 <a name="an_077_007">7</a> Surely that which ye are promised will befall. 
 <a name="an_077_008">8</a> So when the stars are put out, 
 <a name="an_077_009">9</a> And when the sky is riven asunder, 
 <a name="an_077_010">10</a> And when the mountains are blown away, 
 <a name="an_077_011">11</a> And when the messengers are brought unto their time appointed - 
 <a name="an_077_012">12</a> For what day is the time appointed ? 
 <a name="an_077_013">13</a> For the Day of Decision. 
 <a name="an_077_014">14</a> And what will convey unto thee what the Day of Decision is! - 
 <a name="an_077_015">15</a> Woe unto the repudiators on that day! 
 <a name="an_077_016">16</a> Destroyed We not the former folk, 
 <a name="an_077_017">17</a> Then caused the latter folk to follow after ? 
 <a name="an_077_018">18</a> Thus deal We ever with the guilty. 
 <a name="an_077_019">19</a> Woe unto the repudiators on that day! 
 <a name="an_077_020">20</a> Did We not create you from a base fluid 
 <a name="an_077_021">21</a> Which We laid up in a safe abode 
 <a name="an_077_022">22</a> For a known term ? 
 <a name="an_077_023">23</a> Thus We arranged. How excellent is Our arranging! 
 <a name="an_077_024">24</a> Woe unto the repudiators on that day! 
 <a name="an_077_025">25</a> Have We not made the earth a receptacle 
 <a name="an_077_026">26</a> Both for the living and the dead, 
 <a name="an_077_027">27</a> And placed therein high mountains and given you to drink sweet water  therein ? 
 <a name="an_077_028">28</a> Woe unto the repudiators on that day! 
 <a name="an_077_029">29</a> (It will be said unto them:) Depart unto that (doom) which ye used to deny; 
 <a name="an_077_030">30</a> Depart unto the shadow falling threefold, 
 <a name="an_077_031">31</a> (Which yet is) no relief nor shelter from the flame. 
 <a name="an_077_032">32</a> Lo! it throweth up sparks like the castles, 
 <a name="an_077_033">33</a> (Or) as it might be camels of bright yellow hue. 
 <a name="an_077_034">34</a> Woe unto the repudiators on that day! 
 <a name="an_077_035">35</a> This is a day wherein they speak not, 
 <a name="an_077_036">36</a> Nor are they suffered to put forth excuses. 
 <a name="an_077_037">37</a> Woe unto the repudiators on that day! 
 <a name="an_077_038">38</a> This is the Day of Decision, We have brought you and the men of old  together. 
 <a name="an_077_039">39</a> If now ye have any wit, outwit Me. 
 <a name="an_077_040">40</a> Woe unto the repudiators on that day! 
 <a name="an_077_041">41</a> Lo! those who kept their duty are amid shade and fountains, 
 <a name="an_077_042">42</a> And fruits such as they desire. 
 <a name="an_077_043">43</a> (Unto them it is said:) Eat, drink and welcome, O ye blessed, in return for  what ye did. 
 <a name="an_077_044">44</a> Thus do We reward the good. 
 <a name="an_077_045">45</a> Woe unto the repudiators on that day! 
 <a name="an_077_046">46</a> Eat and take your ease (on earth) a little. Lo! ye are guilty. 
 <a name="an_077_047">47</a> Woe unto the repudiators on that day! 
 <a name="an_077_048">48</a> When it is said unto them: Bow down, they bow not down! 
 <a name="an_077_049">49</a> Woe unto the repudiators on that day! 
 <a name="an_077_050">50</a> In what statement, after this, will they believe ?
