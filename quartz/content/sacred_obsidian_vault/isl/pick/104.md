 <h1 align="CENTER">104. al-Humazah: The Slanderer</h1>
 <a name="an_104_001">1</a> Woe unto every slandering traducer, 
 <a name="an_104_002">2</a> Who hath gathered wealth (of this world) and arranged it. 
 <a name="an_104_003">3</a> He thinketh that his wealth will render him immortal. 
 <a name="an_104_004">4</a> Nay, but verily he will be flung to the Consuming One. 
 <a name="an_104_005">5</a> Ah, what will convey unto thee what the Consuming One is! 
 <a name="an_104_006">6</a> (It is) the fire of Allah, kindled, 
 <a name="an_104_007">7</a> Which leapeth up over the hearts (of men). 
 <a name="an_104_008">8</a> Lo! it is closed in on them 
 <a name="an_104_009">9</a> In outstretched columns.
