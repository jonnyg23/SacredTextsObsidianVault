 <h1 align="CENTER">109. al-Kafirun: The Unbelievers</h1>
 <a name="an_109_001">1</a> Say: O disbelievers! 
 <a name="an_109_002">2</a> I worship not that which ye worship; 
 <a name="an_109_003">3</a> Nor worship ye that which I worship. 
 <a name="an_109_004">4</a> And I shall not worship that which ye worship. 
 <a name="an_109_005">5</a> Nor will ye worship that which I worship. 
 <a name="an_109_006">6</a> Unto you your religion, and unto me my religion.
