 <h1 align="CENTER">91. ash-Shams: The Sun</h1>
 <a name="an_091_001">1</a> By the sun and his brightness, 
 <a name="an_091_002">2</a> And the moon when she followeth him, 
 <a name="an_091_003">3</a> And the day when it revealeth him, 
 <a name="an_091_004">4</a> And the night when it enshroudeth him, 
 <a name="an_091_005">5</a> And the heaven and Him Who built it, 
 <a name="an_091_006">6</a> And the earth and Him Who spread it, 
 <a name="an_091_007">7</a> And a soul and Him Who perfected it 
 <a name="an_091_008">8</a> And inspired it (with conscience of) what is wrong for it and (what is) right  for it. 
 <a name="an_091_009">9</a> He is indeed successful who causeth it to grow, 
 <a name="an_091_010">10</a> And he is indeed a failure who stunteth it. 
 <a name="an_091_011">11</a> (The tribe of) Thamud denied (the truth) in their rebellious pride, 
 <a name="an_091_012">12</a> When the basest of them broke forth 
 <a name="an_091_013">13</a> And the messenger of Allah said: It is the she-camel of Allah, so let her  drink! 
 <a name="an_091_014">14</a> But they denied him, and they hamstrung her, so Allah doomed them for  their sin and rased (their dwellings). 
 <a name="an_091_015">15</a> He dreadeth not the sequel (of events).
