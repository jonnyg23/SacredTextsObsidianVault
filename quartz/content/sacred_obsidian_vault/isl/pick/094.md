 <h1 align="CENTER">94. al-Inshirah: The Expansion</h1>
 <a name="an_094_001">1</a> Have We not caused thy bosom to dilate, 
 <a name="an_094_002">2</a> And eased thee of the burden 
 <a name="an_094_003">3</a> Which weighed down thy back; 
 <a name="an_094_004">4</a> And exalted thy fame ? 
 <a name="an_094_005">5</a> But lo! with hardship goeth ease, 
 <a name="an_094_006">6</a> Lo! with hardship goeth ease; 
 <a name="an_094_007">7</a> So when thou art relieved, still toil 
 <a name="an_094_008">8</a> And strive to please thy Lord.
