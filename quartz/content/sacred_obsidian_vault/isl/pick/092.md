 <h1 align="CENTER">92. al-Layl: The Night</h1>
 <a name="an_092_001">1</a> By the night enshrouding 
 <a name="an_092_002">2</a> And the day resplendent 
 <a name="an_092_003">3</a> And Him Who hath created male and female, 
 <a name="an_092_004">4</a> Lo! your effort is dispersed (toward divers ends). 
 <a name="an_092_005">5</a> As for him who giveth and is dutiful (toward Allah) 
 <a name="an_092_006">6</a> And believeth in goodness; 
 <a name="an_092_007">7</a> Surely We will ease his way unto the state of ease. 
 <a name="an_092_008">8</a> But as for him who hoardeth and deemeth himself independent, 
 <a name="an_092_009">9</a> And disbelieveth in goodness; 
 <a name="an_092_010">10</a> Surely We will ease his way unto adversity. 
 <a name="an_092_011">11</a> His riches will not save him when he perisheth. 
 <a name="an_092_012">12</a> Lo! Ours it is (to give) the guidance 
 <a name="an_092_013">13</a> And lo! unto Us belong the latter portion and the former. 
 <a name="an_092_014">14</a> Therefor have I warned you of the flaming Fire 
 <a name="an_092_015">15</a> Which only the most wretched must endure, 
 <a name="an_092_016">16</a> He who denieth and turneth away. 
 <a name="an_092_017">17</a> Far removed from it will be the righteous 
 <a name="an_092_018">18</a> Who giveth his wealth that he may grow (in goodness). 
 <a name="an_092_019">19</a> And none hath with him any favour for reward, 
 <a name="an_092_020">20</a> Except as seeking (to fulfil) the purpose of his Lord Most High. 
 <a name="an_092_021">21</a> He verily will be content.
