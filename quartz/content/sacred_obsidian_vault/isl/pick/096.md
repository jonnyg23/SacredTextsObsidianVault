 <h1 align="CENTER">96. al-`Alaq: The Clot</h1>
 <a name="an_096_001">1</a> Read: In the name of thy Lord Who createth, 
 <a name="an_096_002">2</a> Createth man from a clot. 
 <a name="an_096_003">3</a> Read: And thy Lord is the Most Bounteous, 
 <a name="an_096_004">4</a> Who teacheth by the pen, 
 <a name="an_096_005">5</a> Teacheth man that which he knew not. 
 <a name="an_096_006">6</a> Nay, but verily man is rebellious 
 <a name="an_096_007">7</a> That he thinketh himself independent! 
 <a name="an_096_008">8</a> Lo! unto thy Lord is the return. 
 <a name="an_096_009">9</a> Hast thou seen him who dissuadeth 
 <a name="an_096_010">10</a> A slave when he prayeth ? 
 <a name="an_096_011">11</a> Hast thou seen if he relieth on the guidance (of Allah) 
 <a name="an_096_012">12</a> Or enjoineth piety ? 
 <a name="an_096_013">13</a> Hast thou seen if he denieth (Allah's guidance) and is froward ? 
 <a name="an_096_014">14</a> Is he then unaware that Allah seeth ? 
 <a name="an_096_015">15</a> Nay, but if he cease not We will seize him by the forelock - 
 <a name="an_096_016">16</a> The lying, sinful forelock - 
 <a name="an_096_017">17</a> Then let him call upon his henchmen! 
 <a name="an_096_018">18</a> We will call the guards of hell. 
 <a name="an_096_019">19</a> Nay, Obey not thou him. But prostrate thyself, and draw near (unto Allah).
