 <h1 align="CENTER">105. al-Fil: The Elephant</h1>
 <a name="an_105_001">1</a> Hast thou not seen how thy Lord dealt with the owners of the Elephant ? 
 <a name="an_105_002">2</a> Did He not bring their stratagem to naught, 
 <a name="an_105_003">3</a> And send against them swarms of flying creatures, 
 <a name="an_105_004">4</a> Which pelted them with stones of baked clay, 
 <a name="an_105_005">5</a> And made them like green crops devoured (by cattle) ?
