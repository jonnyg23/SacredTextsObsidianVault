 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>Sadi's Scroll of Wisdom</i>, by Arthur N. Wollaston, [1906], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">ADDRESS TO THE SOUL</h3>  
 <p>Forty years of thy precious existence have expired; <a name="page_31"><font size="1" color="green">p. 31</font></a><br> Yet thy life hath not passed beyond childhood.<br> Thou hast spent all in lust and licence;<br> Not a moment hast thou acted according to righteousness.<br> Rely not upon unstable life;<br> Be not confident that thou art safe from the sport of fortune.</p>
