 <p><a name="page_49"><font size="1" color="green">p. 49</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">IN CONDEMNATION OF SATAN</h3>  
 <p>O soul! whoever is overpowered by Satan,<br> Is night and day in the snare of sin.<br> Whoever hath Satan for his ruler,<br> How can he return to the way of God?<br> O soul! beware that thou dost not give way to sin,<br> That to-morrow thou mayest be free as from fire.<br> A wise man avoideth offence,<br> As sugar melteth in water.<br> A man of good disposition doth not commit sin,<br> Lest he becometh as the light of the sun concealed by the clouds.<br> Do not give way to thy lusts,<br> Lest thou be suddenly snatched to perdition.<br> If thine heart doth not turn aside from sin,<br> Amongst the lowest of the low will be thine abode. <a name="page_50"><font size="1" color="green">p. 50</font></a><br> Do not destroy the house of life<br> With the torrent of bad and improper actions.<br> If thou keepest away from sin and iniquity,<br> Thou wilt not be far from the garden of Paradise.</p>
