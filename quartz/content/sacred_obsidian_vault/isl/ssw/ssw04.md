 <p><a name="page_28"><font size="1" color="green">p. 28</font></a> <a name="page_29"><font size="1" color="green">p. 29</font></a></p>  <h1 align="center">SADI'S SCROLL OF WISDOM</h1>  
 <p align="center"><i>In the Name of God, the Merciful and Compassionate!</i></p>  
 <p>O merciful Being! take pity on our condition;<br> For we are captives in the snare of lust!<br> We have no protector save Thee!<br> Thou art the all-sufficient Forgiver of sins to us sinners!<br> Keep us from the path of error;<br> Forgive us our trespasses, and show us righteousness.</p>
