 <p><a name="page_30"><font size="1" color="green">p. 30</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">IN PRAISE OF MUHAMMAD</h3>  
 <p>The Peace of God be upon him and his Posterity!<br> So long as the tongue is fixed in the mouth,<br> May the praise of Muhammad be a source of delight.<br> The beloved of God, the most exalted of Prophets,<br> Whose pillow is the glorious firmament.<br> The earth-conquering horseman with his chestnut Buraq, <a name="fr_5"></a><a href="#fn_5"><font size="1">1</font></a><br> Which passed beyond the palace of the cerulean portico!</p>
