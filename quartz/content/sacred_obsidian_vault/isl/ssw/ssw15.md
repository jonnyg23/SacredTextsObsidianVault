 <p><a name="page_42"><font size="1" color="green">p. 42</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">IN CONDEMNATION OF OPPRESSION</h3>  
 <p>The world witnesseth desolation owing to failure of justice,<br> As it were a beautiful garden from the autumn gale.<br> Do not give way to oppression in any case,<br> Lest the sun of monarchy suffer decline.<br> He who raiseth the fire of oppression in the world<br> Occasioneth a sigh on the part of the people of the land.<br> If a tyrant raiseth a sigh from the soul<br> The anguish createth a flame over land and water.<br> Do not oppress poor helpless people,<br> Without further thought as to the narrowness of the grave.<br> Be not disposed towards the market-place of oppression; <a name="page_43"><font size="1" color="green">p. 43</font></a><br> Be not neglectful of the smoke of men's hearts.<br> O man of haste! be not an oppressor of humanity,<br> For of a sudden the wrath of God will overtake thee.<br> Do not oppress the poor humble people,<br> For without doubt the tyrant passeth to perdition.</p>
