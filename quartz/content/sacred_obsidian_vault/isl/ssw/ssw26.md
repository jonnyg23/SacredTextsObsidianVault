 <p><a name="page_57"><font size="1" color="green">p. 57</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">ON THE VICISSITUDES OF FORTUNE AND DIFFERENCES OF STATION</h3>  
 <p>Regard this globe, resplendent like gold,<br> The roof of which is firm without pillars!<br> Regard the curtain of the revolving sphere! <br> Regard its glittering lamps! <a name="fr_12"></a><a href="#fn_12"><font size="1">1</font></a>
 <br> One is a shepherd, and another a sovereign;<br> One is a suppliant for justice, another covets a throne;<br> One is happy, and another miserable;<br> One is prosperous, and another unfortunate;<br> One is a payer of taxes, and another sits on a throne;<br> One is exalted, and another debased;<br> One sitteth on a mat, and another upon a throne;<br> One is in rags, and another is clad in silk;<br> One hath no bread, and another revels in wealth; <a name="page_58"><font size="1" color="green">p. 58</font></a><br> One is disappointed, and another prosperous;<br> One is afflicted, and another rolleth in riches;<br> One hath a transient fate, and another is established throughout eternity;<br> One is full of health, and another is sickly;<br> One is full of years, and another is a mere stripling;<br> One is filled with righteousness, and another lives in sin;<br> One is given to prayer, and another is addicted to deceit;<br> One is upright and religious,<br> Another is immersed in an ocean of crime and wickedness;<br> One is of good disposition, and another is of hasty temper;<br> One is patient, and another is quarrelsome;<br> One is at ease, another in pain;<br> One is in difficulties, another is prosperous;<br> One is a chieftain in the world of luxury, <a name="page_59"><font size="1" color="green">p. 59</font></a><br> Another is a captive in the snares of adversity;<br> One is established in the rose garden of comfort,<br> Another is associated with anguish, pain, and distress;<br> One is like a rose resplendent with joy,<br> Another is distressed at heart, and pained in soul;<br> One girdeth his loins with obedience,<br> Another bringeth his life to an end in sin;<br> One passeth day and night with the Koran in his hands,<br> Another sleepeth intoxicated in the corner of a wine shop;<br> One is fixed firm as a peg at the door of religion,<br> Another is a sinner in the way of infidelity;<br> One is prosperous, learned, and intelligent,<br> Another is unfortunate, ignorant, and abashed; <a name="page_60"><font size="1" color="green">p. 60</font></a><br> One is a champion, agile, and a warrior,<br> Another is faint-hearted, indolent, and without courage;<br> One exceedeth all limits in the possession of wealth,<br> Another is in want of bread and means for his family;<br> One hath the taper of joy resplendent,<br> To another bright daylight is as night.</p>
