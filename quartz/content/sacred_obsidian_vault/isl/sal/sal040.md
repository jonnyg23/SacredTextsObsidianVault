 <p>(39)</p>  
 <p>His supplication for cure from dropsy</p>  
 <p><i>In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful</i></p>  
 <p>In the name of Allah and by Allah, I seek refuge from the Honoured name</p>  
 <p>of Allah, by the great name and with its help. I seek refuge from all the</p>  
 <p>blood supplying veins and from all the heat of every fire.</p>
