 <p>(22)</p>  
 <p>Dua seeking forgiveness</p>  
 <p><i>In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful</i></p>  
 <p>O the One (Lord) Who has forgiven those evil deeds which I have</p>  
 <p>committed in my house or secretly in other houses. O Allah Who did not</p>  
 <p>punish me when I initiated the sin. Your forgiveness! Your forgiveness!</p>  
 <p>O Generous, Your forgiveness.</p>
