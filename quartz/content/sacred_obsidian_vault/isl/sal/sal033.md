 <p>(32)</p>  
 <p>His supplication at the time of breaking the</p>  
 <p>fast</p>  
 <p><i>In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful</i></p>  
 <p>O Allah! We had kept this fast for You, and have ended the fast with</p>  
 <p>Your sustenance. You accept it from us, as You are Hearing and</p>  
 <p>Knowing.</p>
