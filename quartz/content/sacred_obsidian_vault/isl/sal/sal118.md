 <p>(117)</p>  
 <p>His supplication after waking</p>  
 <p><i>In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful</i></p>  
 <p>Sufficient is my Lord from all the people, That is sufficient for me, one</p>  
 <p>Who is sufficient. He is sufficient since the time of my existence. Allah is</p>  
 <p>sufficient and the best caretaker.</p>
