 <p>(104)</p>  
 <p>Another of His supplication after the</p>  
 <p>morning prayers</p>  
 <p><i>In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful</i></p>  
 <p>O Allah! I have recited this prayer not with any desire or fulfilling of any</p>  
 <p>hopes but it is only for the obedience of Your commands. O Allah if there</p>  
 <p>is any deficiency, whether in intention or in standing position or in</p>  
 <p>recitation or in bowing on in prostration do not hold me responsible but</p>  
 <p>accept it and bestow forgiveness and oblige me, by Your mercy, O most</p>  
 <p>merciful of all the mercifuls.</p>
