 <p>(93)</p>  
 <p>His supplication while rinsing the mouth</p>  
 <p><i>In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful</i></p>  
 <p>O Allah! The day when I will present myself in Your presence grant me</p>  
 <p>Your proof and mercy and move my tongue for Your remembrance.</p>
