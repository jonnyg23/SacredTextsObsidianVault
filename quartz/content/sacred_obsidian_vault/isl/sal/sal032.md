 <p>(31)</p>  
 <p>His supplication on sighting the moon of the</p>  
 <p>month of Ramazan recited facing the Qibla</p>  
 <p><i>In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful</i></p>  
 <p>O Allah! Make this moon as a faith and protection for us in it we are safe</p>  
 <p>and sustenance is increased and the sick are cured. O Allah! By Your</p>  
 <p>grace, grant us the power to fast, pray and recite the Quran in it. O</p>  
 <p>Allah! Make it safe for us and from our side and grant us Your protection</p>  
 <p>in this month.</p>
