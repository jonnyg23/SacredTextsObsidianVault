 <p>(88)</p>  
 <p>His supplication while riding his horse to</p>  
 <p>battlefield</p>  
 <p><i>In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful</i></p>  
 <p>Glory be to Him who made this subservient to us and we were not able</p>  
 <p>to do it and surely to our Lord we must return. Praise is for Allah on all</p>  
 <p>His bounties upon us and mighty Grace, which He bestowed upon us.</p>
