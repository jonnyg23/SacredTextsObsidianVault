 <p align="right">(116)</p>  
 <p>His supplication when turning sideways on</p>  
 <p>bed</p>  
 <p><i>In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful</i></p>  
 <p>There is no god but Allah. He is the living, the self Existing. Glory be to</p>  
 <p>Allah Lord of the Prophets and Messengers. Glory be to Allah, Lord of</p>  
 <p>the Heavens and everything in it and he is the Lord of the Great Throne</p>  
 <p>and peace be upon the Messenger and praise be to Allah, Lord of the</p>  
 <p>Worlds.</p>
