 <p>(46)</p>  
 <p>His supplication to save from drowning or</p>  
 <p>burning</p>  
 <p><i>In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful</i></p>  
 <p>Surely my guardian is Allah Who revealed the Book and He loves the</p>  
 <p>doer of good. These (Jews) people did not care for Allah although the</p>  
 <p>whole earth and the skies are in the control of Allah, and He is far</p>  
 <p>greater than what people ascribe to Him.</p>
