 <p>(123)</p>  
 <p>His Supplication after completing the Holy</p>  
 <p>Quran</p>  
 <p><i>In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful</i></p>  
 <p>O Allah! Expand my heart by the Holy Quran, and make me use my body</p>  
 <p>for the Holy Quran, illuminate my eyes with the Holy Quran, make my</p>  
 <p>tongue speak the Holy Quran, and give me Your grace to adhere to its</p>  
 <p>commands the whole life, for surely there is no power or might except in</p>  
 <p>You.</p>
