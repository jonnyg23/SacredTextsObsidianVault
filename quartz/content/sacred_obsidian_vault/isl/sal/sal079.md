 <p>(78)</p>  
 <p>His supplication for reforming his enemies</p>  
 <p><i>In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful</i></p>  
 <p>O Allah! Protect ours and theirs blood and reform their and our affairs</p>  
 <p>bring them out of deviation on a straight path, so that truth is</p>  
 <p>distinguished from falsehood and they are away from oppression and</p>  
 <p>ignorance.</p>
