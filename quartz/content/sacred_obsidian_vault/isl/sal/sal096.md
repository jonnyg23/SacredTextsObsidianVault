 <p>(95)</p>  
 <p>His supplication while washing the face</p>  
 <p><i>In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful</i></p>  
 <p>O Allah! Illuminate my face on that day when other faces are blackened</p>  
 <p>and do not blacken my face on the day when other faces are illuminated.</p>
