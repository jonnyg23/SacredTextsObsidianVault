 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">The Qur'ân, Rodwell edition [1876]; at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">SURA CVII.--RELIGION [XIV.]</h3>  
 <p align="center">MECCA.--7 Verses</p>  
 <p>In the name of God, the Compassionate, the Merciful<br> WHAT thinkest thou of him who treateth our RELIGION as a lie?<br> He it is who trusteth away the orphan,<br> And stirreth not others up to feed the poor.<br> Woe to those who pray,<br> But in their prayer are careless;<br> Who make a shew of devotion,<br> But refuse help to the needy.</p>
