 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">The Qur'ân, Rodwell edition [1876]; at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">SURA XC.--THE SOIL [XVIII.]</h3>  
 <p align="center">MECCA.--20 Verses</p>  
 <p>In the Name of God, the Compassionate, the Merciful<br> I NEED not to swear by this SOIL,<br> This soil on which thou dost dwell,<br> Or by sire and offspring! <a name="fr_57"></a><a href="#fn_57"><font size="1">1</font></a><br> Surely in trouble have we created man.<br> What! thinketh he that no one hath power over him?<br> "I have wasted," saith he, "enormous riches!"<br> What! thinketh he that no one regardeth him?<br> What! have we not made him eyes,<br> And tongue, and lips,<br> And guided him to the two highways? <a name="fr_58"></a><a href="#fn_58"><font size="1">2</font></a><br> Yet he attempted not the steep.<br> And who shall teach thee what the steep is?<br> It is to ransom the captive, <a name="fr_59"></a><a href="#fn_59"><font size="1">3</font></a><br> Or to feed in the day of famine,<br> The orphan who is near of kin, or the poor that lieth in the dust;<br> Beside this, to be of those who believe, and enjoin stedfastness on each other, and enjoin compassion on each other.<br> These shall be the people of the right hand:<br> While they who disbelieve our signs,<br> Shall be the people of the left.<br> Around them the fire shall close.</p>
