 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">The Qur'ân, Rodwell edition [1876]; at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">SURA CIV.--THE BACKBITER [XII.]</h3>  
 <p align="center">MECCA.--9 Verses</p>  
 <p>In the Name of God, the Compassionate, the Merciful<br> Woe to every BACKBITER, Defamer!<br> Who amasseth wealth and storeth it against the future!<br> He thinketh surely that his wealth shall be with him for ever.<br> Nay! for verily he shall be flung into the Crushing Fire;<br> And who shall teach thee what the Crushing Fire is?<br> It is God's kindled fire,<br> Which shall mount above the hearts of the damned;<br> It shall verily rise over them like a vault,<br> On outstretched columns.</p>
