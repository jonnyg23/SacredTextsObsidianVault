 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">The Qur'ân, Rodwell edition [1876]; at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">SURA XCIX.--THE EARTHQUAKE [XXX.]</h3>  
 <p align="center">MECCA.--8 Verses</p>  
 <p>In the Name of God, the Compassionate, the Merciful<br> WHEN the Earth with her quaking shall quake<br> And the Earth shall cast forth her burdens,<br> And man shall say, What aileth her?<br> On that day shall she tell out her tidings,<br> Because thy Lord shall have inspired her.<br> On that day shall men come forward in throngs to behold their works,<br> And whosoever shall have wrought an atom's weight of good shall behold it,<br> And whosoever shall have wrought an atom's weight of evil shall behold it.</p>
