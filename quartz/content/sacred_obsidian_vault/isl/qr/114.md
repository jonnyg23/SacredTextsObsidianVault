 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">The Qur'ân, Rodwell edition [1876]; at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">SURA CXIV.--MEN [VII.]</h3>  
 <p align="center">MECCA OR MEDINA.--6 Verses</p>  
 <p>In the Name of God, the Compassionate, the Merciful<br> SAY: I betake me for refuge to the Lord of MEN,<br> The King of men,<br> The God of men,<br> Against the mischief of the stealthily withdrawing whisperer, <a name="fr_40"></a><a href="#fn_40"><font size="1">1</font></a><br> Who whispereth in man's breast--<br> Against djinn and men.</p>
