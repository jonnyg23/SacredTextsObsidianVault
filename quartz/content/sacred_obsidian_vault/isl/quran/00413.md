 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_092.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>92.  Wam<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>na limu/minin an yaqtula mu/minan ill<u>a</u> kha<u>t</u>aan waman qatala mu/minan kha<u>t</u>aan fata<u>h</u>reeru raqabatin mu/minatin wadiyatun musallamatun il<u>a</u> ahlihi ill<u>a</u> an ya<u>ss</u>addaqoo fa-in k<u>a</u>na min qawmin AAaduwwin lakum wahuwa mu/minun fata<u>h</u>reeru raqabatin mu/minatin wa-in k<u>a</u>na min qawmin baynakum wabaynahum meeth<u>a</u>qun fadiyatun musallamatun il<u>a</u> ahlihi wata<u>h</u>reeru raqabatin mu/minatin faman lam yajid fa<u>s</u>iy<u>a</u>mu shahrayni mutat<u>a</u>biAAayni tawbatan mina All<u>a</u>hi wak<u>a</u>na All<u>a</u>hu AAaleeman <u>h</u>akeem<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>92.  Never should a Believer<br> Kill a Believer; but<br> (If it so happens) by mistake,<br> (Compensation is due):<br> If one (so) kills a Believer,<br> It is ordained that he<br> Should free a believing slave,<br> And pay compensation<br> To the deceased's family,<br> Unless they remit it freely.<br> If the deceased belonged<br> To a people at war with you,<br> And he was a Believer,<br> The freeing of a believing slave<br> (Is enough). If he belonged<br> To a people with whom<br> Ye have a treaty of mutual<br> Alliance, compensation should<br> Be paid to his family,<br> And a believing slave be freed.<br> For those who find this<br> Beyond their means, (is prescribed)<br> A fast for two months<br> Running: by way of repentance<br> To God: for God hath<br> All knowledge and all wisdom.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_093.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>93.  Waman yaqtul mu/minan mutaAAammidan fajaz<u>a</u>ohu jahannamu kh<u>a</u>lidan feeh<u>a</u> wagha<u>d</u>iba All<u>a</u>hu AAalayhi walaAAanahu waaAAadda lahu AAa<u>tha</u>ban AAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<u>a</u><b>n </b>   </p>
 <p>93.  1f a man kills a Believer<br> Intentionally, his recompense<br> Is Hell, to abide therein<br> (For ever): and the wrath<br> And the curse of God<br> Are upon him, and<br> A dreadful penalty<br> Is prepared for him.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_094.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>94.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo i<u>tha</u> <u>d</u>arabtum fee sabeeli All<u>a</u>hi fatabayyanoo wal<u>a</u> taqooloo liman alq<u>a</u> ilaykumu a<b>l</b>ssal<u>a</u>ma lasta mu/minan tabtaghoona AAara<u>d</u>a al<u>h</u>ay<u>a</u>ti a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> faAAinda All<u>a</u>hi magh<u>a</u>nimu katheeratun ka<u>tha</u>lika kuntum min qablu famanna All<u>a</u>hu AAalaykum fatabayyanoo inna All<u>a</u>ha k<u>a</u>na bim<u>a</u> taAAmaloona khabeer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>94.  O ye who believe!<br> When ye go abroad<br> In the cause of God,<br> Investigate carefully,<br> And say not to any one<br> Who offers you a salutation:<br> "Thou art none of a Believer!"<br> Coveting the perishable goods<br> Of this life: with God<br> Are profits and spoils abundant.<br> Even thus were ye yourselves<br> Before, till God conferred<br> On you His favours: therefore<br> Carefully investigate.<br> For God is well aware<br> Of all that ye do.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_095.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>95.  L<u>a</u> yastawee alq<u>a</u>AAidoona mina almu/mineena ghayru olee a<b>l</b><u>dd</u>arari wa<b>a</b>lmuj<u>a</u>hidoona fee sabeeli All<u>a</u>hi bi-amw<u>a</u>lihim waanfusihim fa<u>dd</u>ala All<u>a</u>hu almuj<u>a</u>hideena bi-amw<u>a</u>lihim waanfusihim AAal<u>a</u> alq<u>a</u>AAideena darajatan wakullan waAAada All<u>a</u>hu al<u>h</u>usn<u>a</u> wafa<u>dd</u>ala All<u>a</u>hu almuj<u>a</u>hideena AAal<u>a</u> alq<u>a</u>AAideena ajran AAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>95.  Not equal are those<br> Believers who sit (at home)<br> And receive no hurt,<br> And those who strive<br> And fight in the cause<br> Of God with their goods<br> And their persons.<br> God hath granted<br> A grade higher to those<br> Who strive and fight<br>With their goods and persons<br> Than to those who sit (at home).<br> Unto all (in Faith)<br> Hath God promised good:<br> But those who strive and fight<br> Hath He distinguished<br> Above those who sit (at home)<br> By a special reward,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_096.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>96.  Daraj<u>a</u>tin minhu wamaghfiratan wara<u>h</u>matan wak<u>a</u>na All<u>a</u>hu ghafooran ra<u>h</u>eem<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>96.  Ranks specially bestowed<br> By Him. And Forgiveness<br> And Mercy. For God is<br> Oft forgiving, Most Merciful.</p> <div class="filenav">
