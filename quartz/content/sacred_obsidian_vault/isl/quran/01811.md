 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XVIII.: Kahf, or the Cave.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 11</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_083.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>83.  Wayas-aloonaka AAan <u>th</u>ee alqarnayni qul saatloo AAalaykum minhu <u>th</u>ikr<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>83.  They ask thee concerning<br> Ẓul-qarnain. Say,<br>"I will rehearse to you<br> Something of his story."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_084.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>84.  Inn<u>a</u> makkann<u>a</u> lahu fee al-ar<u>d</u>i wa<u>a</u>tayn<u>a</u>hu min kulli shay-in sabab<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>84.  Verily We established his power<br> On earth, and We gave him<br> The ways and the means<br> To all ends.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_085.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>85.  FaatbaAAa sabab<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>85.  One (such) way he followed,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_086.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>86.  <u>H</u>att<u>a</u> i<u>tha</u> balagha maghriba a<b>l</b>shshamsi wajadah<u>a</u> taghrubu fee AAaynin <u>h</u>ami-atin wawajada AAindah<u>a</u> qawman quln<u>a</u> y<u>a</u> <u>tha</u> alqarnayni imm<u>a</u> an tuAAa<u>thth</u>iba wa-imm<u>a</u> an tattakhi<u>th</u>a feehim <u>h</u>usn<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>86.  Until, when he reached<br> The setting of the sun,<br> He found it set<br> In a spring of murky water:<br> Near it he found a People:<br> We said: "O Ẓul-qarnain!<br> (Thou hast authority,) either<br> To punish them, or<br> To treat them with kindness."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_087.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>87.  Q<u>a</u>la amm<u>a</u> man <i><u>th</u></i>alama fasawfa nuAAa<u>thth</u>ibuhu thumma yuraddu il<u>a</u> rabbihi fayuAAa<u>thth</u>ibuhu AAa<u>tha</u>ban nukr<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>87.  He said: "Whoever doth wrong,<br> Him shall we punish; then<br> Shall he be sent back<br> To his Lord; and He will<br> Punish him with a punishment<br> Unheard-of (before).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_088.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>88.  Waamm<u>a</u> man <u>a</u>mana waAAamila <u>sa</u>li<u>h</u>an falahu jaz<u>a</u>an al<u>h</u>usn<u>a</u> wasanaqoolu lahu min amrin<u>a</u> yusr<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>88.  "But whoever believes,<br> And works righteousness,—<br>He shall have a goodly<br> Reward, and easy will be<br> His task as we order it<br> By our command."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_089.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>89.  Thumma atbaAAa sabab<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>89.  Then followed he (another) way,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_090.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>90.  <u>H</u>att<u>a</u> i<u>tha</u> balagha ma<u>t</u>liAAa a<b>l</b>shshamsi wajadah<u>a</u> ta<u>t</u>luAAu AAal<u>a</u> qawmin lam najAAal lahum min doonih<u>a</u> sitr<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>90.  Until, when he came<br> To the rising of the sun,<br> He found it rising<br> On a people for whom<br> We had provided<br> No covering protection<br> Against the sun.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_091.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>91.  Ka<u>tha</u>lika waqad a<u>h</u>a<u>t</u>n<u>a</u> bim<u>a</u> ladayhi khubr<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>91.  (He left them) as they were:<br> We completely understood<br> What was before him.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_092.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>92.  Thumma atbaAAa sabab<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>92.  Then followed he (another) way,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_093.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>93.  <u>H</u>att<u>a</u> i<u>tha</u> balagha bayna a<b>l</b>ssaddayni wajada min doonihim<u>a</u> qawman l<u>a</u> yak<u>a</u>doona yafqahoona qawl<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>93.  Until, when he reached<br> (A tract) between two mountains,<br> He found, beneath them, a people<br> Who scarcely understood a word.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_094.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>94.  Q<u>a</u>loo y<u>a</u> <u>tha</u> alqarnayni inna ya/jooja wama/jooja mufsidoona fee al-ar<u>d</u>i fahal najAAalu laka kharjan AAal<u>a</u> an tajAAala baynan<u>a</u> wabaynahum sadd<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>94.  They said: "O Ẓul-qarnain!<br> The Gog and Magog (people)<br> Do great mischief on earth:<br> Shall we then render thee<br> Tribute in order that<br> Thou mightest erect a barrier<br> Between us and them?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_095.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>95.  Q<u>a</u>la m<u>a</u> makkannee feehi rabbee khayrun faaAAeenoonee biquwwatin ajAAal baynakum wabaynahum radm<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>95.  He said: "(The power) in which<br> My Lord has established me<br> Is better (than tribute):<br> Help me therefore with strength<br> (And labour): I will<br> Erect a strong barrier<br> Between you and them:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_096.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>96.  <u>A</u>toonee zubara al<u>h</u>adeedi <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> i<u>tha</u> s<u>a</u>w<u>a</u> bayna a<b>l</b><u>s</u>adafayni q<u>a</u>la onfukhoo <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> i<u>tha</u> jaAAalahu n<u>a</u>ran q<u>a</u>la <u>a</u>toonee ofrigh AAalayhi qi<u>t</u>r<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>96.  "Bring me blocks of iron."<br> At length, when he had<br> Filled up the space between<br> The two steep mountain-sides,<br> He said, "Blow (with your bellows)"<br> Then, when he had made<br> It (red) as fire, he said:<br> "Bring me, that I may<br> Pour over it, molten lead."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_097.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>97.  Fam<u>a</u> is<u>ta</u>AAoo an ya<i><u>th</u></i>haroohu wam<u>a</u> ista<u>ta</u>AAoo lahu naqb<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>97.  Thus were they made<br> Powerless to scale it<br> Or to dig through it.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_098.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>98.  Q<u>a</u>la h<u>atha</u> ra<u>h</u>matun min rabbee fa-i<u>tha</u> j<u>a</u>a waAAdu rabbee jaAAalahu dakk<u>a</u>a wak<u>a</u>na waAAdu rabbee <u>h</u>aqq<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>98.  He said: "This is<br> A mercy from my Lord:<br> But when the promise<br> Of my Lord comes to pass,<br> He will make it into dust;<br> And the promise of<br> My Lord is true."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_099.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>99.  Watarakn<u>a</u> baAA<u>d</u>ahum yawma-i<u>th</u>in yamooju fee baAA<u>d</u>in wanufikha fee a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>oori fajamaAAn<u>a</u>hum jamAA<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>99.  On that day We shall<br> Leave them to surge<br> Like waves on one another<br> The trumpet will be blown,<br> And We shall collect them<br> All together.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_100.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>100.  WaAAara<u>d</u>n<u>a</u> jahannama yawma-i<u>th</u>in lilk<u>a</u>fireena AAar<u>da</u><b>n </b>   </p>
 <p>100.  And We shall present<br> Hell that day for Unbelievers<br> To see, all spread out,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_101.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>101.  Alla<u>th</u>eena k<u>a</u>nat aAAyunuhum fee ghi<u>ta</u>-in AAan <u>th</u>ikree wak<u>a</u>noo l<u>a</u> yasta<u>t</u>eeAAoona samAA<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>101.  (Unbelievers) whose eyes<br> Had been under a veil<br> From Remembrance of Me,<br> And who had been unable<br> Even to hear.</p> <div class="filenav">
