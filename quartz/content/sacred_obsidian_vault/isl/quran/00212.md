 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra II.: Baqara, or the Heifer.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 12</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_097.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>97.  Qul man k<u>a</u>na AAaduwwan lijibreela fa-innahu nazzalahu AAal<u>a</u> qalbika bi-i<u>th</u>ni All<u>a</u>hi mu<u>s</u>addiqan lim<u>a</u> bayna yadayhi wahudan wabushr<u>a</u> lilmu/mineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>97.  Say: Whoever is an enemy<br> To Gabriel—for he brings down<br>The (revelation) to thy heart<br> By God's will, a confirmation<br> Of what went before,<br> And guidance and glad tidings<br> For those who believe,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_098.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>98.  Man k<u>a</u>na AAaduwwan lill<u>a</u>hi wamal<u>a</u>-ikatihi warusulihi wajibreela wameek<u>a</u>la fa-inna All<u>a</u>ha AAaduwwun lilk<u>a</u>fireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>98.  Whoever is an enemy to God<br> And His angels and apostles,<br> To Gabriel and Michael,—<br> Lo! God is an enemy to those<br> Who reject Faith.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_099.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>99.  Walaqad anzaln<u>a</u> ilayka <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin bayyin<u>a</u>tin wam<u>a</u> yakfuru bih<u>a</u> ill<u>a</u> alf<u>a</u>siqoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>99.  We have sent down to thee<br> Manifest Signs (āyāt);<br> And none reject them<br> But those who are perverse.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_100.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>100.  Awa kullam<u>a</u> AA<u>a</u>hadoo AAahdan naba<u>th</u>ahu fareequn minhum bal aktharuhum l<u>a</u> yu/minoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>100.  Is it not (the case) that<br> Every time they make a Covenant,<br> Some party among them<br> Throw it aside?—Nay,<br> Most of them are faithless.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_101.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>101.  Walamm<u>a</u> j<u>a</u>ahum rasoolun min AAindi All<u>a</u>hi mu<u>s</u>addiqun lim<u>a</u> maAAahum naba<u>th</u>a fareequn mina alla<u>th</u>eena ootoo alkit<u>a</u>ba kit<u>a</u>ba All<u>a</u>hi war<u>a</u>a <i><u>th</u></i>uhoorihim kaannahum l<u>a</u> yaAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>101.  And when there came to them<br> An Apostle from God,<br> Confirming what was with them,<br> A party of the People of the Book<br> Threw away the Book of God<br> Behind their backs,<br> As if (it had been something)<br> They did not know!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_102.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>102.  Wa<b>i</b>ttabaAAoo m<u>a</u> tatloo a<b>l</b>shshay<u>at</u>eenu AAal<u>a</u> mulki sulaym<u>a</u>na wam<u>a</u> kafara sulaym<u>a</u>nu wal<u>a</u>kinna a<b>l</b>shshay<u>at</u>eena kafaroo yuAAallimoona a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>sa a<b>l</b>ssi<u>h</u>ra wam<u>a</u> onzila AAal<u>a</u> almalakayni bib<u>a</u>bila h<u>a</u>roota wam<u>a</u>roota wam<u>a</u> yuAAallim<u>a</u>ni min a<u>h</u>adin <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> yaqool<u>a</u> innam<u>a</u> na<u>h</u>nu fitnatun fal<u>a</u> takfur fayataAAallamoona minhum<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> yufarriqoona bihi bayna almar-i wazawjihi wam<u>a</u> hum bi<u>da</u>rreena bihi min a<u>h</u>adin ill<u>a</u> bi-i<u>th</u>ni All<u>a</u>hi wayataAAallamoona m<u>a</u> ya<u>d</u>urruhum wal<u>a</u> yanfaAAuhum walaqad AAalimoo lamani ishtar<u>a</u>hu m<u>a</u> lahu fee al-<u>a</u>khirati min khal<u>a</u>qin walabi/sa m<u>a</u> sharaw bihi anfusahum law k<u>a</u>noo yaAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>102.  They followed what the evil ones<br> Gave out (falsely)<br> Against the power<br> Of Solomon: the blasphemers<br> Were, not Solomon, but<br> The evil ones, teaching men<br>Magic, and such things<br> As came down at Babylon<br> To the angels Hārūt and Mārūt.<br> But neither of these taught anyone<br> (Such things) without saying:<br> "We are only for trial;<br> So do not blaspheme."<br> They learned from them<br> The means to sow discord<br> Between man and wife.<br> But they could not thus<br> Harm anyone except<br> By God's permission.<br> And they learned what harmed them,<br> Not what profited them.<br> And they knew that the buyers<br> Of (magic) would have<br> No share in the happiness<br> Of the Hereafter. And vile<br> Was the price for which<br> They did sell their souls,<br> If they but knew!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_103.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>103.  Walaw annahum <u>a</u>manoo wa<b>i</b>ttaqaw lamathoobatun min AAindi All<u>a</u>hi khayrun law k<u>a</u>noo yaAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>103.  If they had kept their Faith<br> And guarded themselves from evil,<br>Far better had been<br> The reward from their Lord,<br> If they but knew!</p> <div class="filenav">
