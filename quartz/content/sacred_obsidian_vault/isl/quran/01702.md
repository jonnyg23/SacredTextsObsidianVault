 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XVII.: Banī Isrā-īl, or the Children of Israel,</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 2</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_011.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>11.  WayadAAu al-ins<u>a</u>nu bi<b>al</b>shsharri duAA<u>a</u>ahu bi<b>a</b>lkhayri wak<u>a</u>na al-ins<u>a</u>nu AAajool<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>11.  The prayer that man<br> Should make for good,<br> He maketh for evil;<br> For man is given to<br> Hasty (deeds).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_012.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>12.  WajaAAaln<u>a</u> allayla wa<b>al</b>nnah<u>a</u>ra <u>a</u>yatayni fama<u>h</u>awn<u>a</u> <u>a</u>yata allayli wajaAAaln<u>a</u> <u>a</u>yata a<b>l</b>nnah<u>a</u>ri mub<u>s</u>iratan litabtaghoo fa<u>d</u>lan min rabbikum walitaAAlamoo AAadada a<b>l</b>ssineena wa<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>is<u>a</u>ba wakulla shay-in fa<u>ss</u>aln<u>a</u>hu taf<u>s</u>eel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>12.  We have made the Night<br> And the Day as two<br>(Of Our) Signs: the Sign<br> Of the Night have We obscured,<br> While the Sign of the Day<br> We have made to enlighten<br> You; that ye may seek<br> Bounty from your Lord,<br> And that ye may know<br> The number and count<br> Of the years: all things<br> Have We explained in detail.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_013.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>13.  Wakulla ins<u>a</u>nin alzamn<u>a</u>hu <u>ta</u>-irahu fee AAunuqihi wanukhriju lahu yawma alqiy<u>a</u>mati kit<u>a</u>ban yalq<u>a</u>hu manshoor<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>13.  Every man's fate<br> We have fastened<br> On his own neck:<br> On the Day of Judgment<br> We shall bring out<br> For him a scroll,<br> Which he will see<br> Spread open.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_014.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>14.  Iqra/ kit<u>a</u>baka kaf<u>a</u> binafsika alyawma AAalayka <u>h</u>aseeb<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>14.  (It will be said to him:)<br> "Read thine (own) record:<br> Sufficient is thy soul<br> This day to make out<br> An account against thee."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_015.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>15.  Mani ihtad<u>a</u> fa-innam<u>a</u> yahtadee linafsihi waman <u>d</u>alla fa-innam<u>a</u> ya<u>d</u>illu AAalayh<u>a</u> wal<u>a</u> taziru w<u>a</u>ziratun wizra okhr<u>a</u> wam<u>a</u> kunn<u>a</u> muAAa<u>thth</u>ibeena <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> nabAAatha rasool<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>15.  Who receiveth guidance,<br> Receiveth it for his own<br> Benefit: who goeth astray<br> Doth so to his own loss:<br> No bearer of burdens<br> Can bear the burden<br> Of another: nor would We<br> Visit with Our Wrath<br> Until We had sent<br> An apostle (to give warning).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_016.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>16.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> aradn<u>a</u> an nuhlika qaryatan amarn<u>a</u> mutrafeeh<u>a</u> fafasaqoo feeh<u>a</u> fa<u>h</u>aqqa AAalayh<u>a</u> alqawlu fadammarn<u>a</u>h<u>a</u> tadmeer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>16.  When We decide to destroy<br> A population, We (first) send<br> A definite order to those<br> Among them who are given<br> The good things of this life<br> And yet transgress; so that<br> The word is proved true<br> Against them: then (it is)<br> We destroy them utterly.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_017.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>17.  Wakam ahlakn<u>a</u> mina alqurooni min baAAdi noo<u>h</u>in wakaf<u>a</u> birabbika bi<u>th</u>unoobi AAib<u>a</u>dihi khabeeran ba<u>s</u>eer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>17.  How many generations<br> Have We destroyed. after Noah?<br> And enough is thy Lord<br> To note and see<br> The sins of His servants</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_018.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>18.  Man k<u>a</u>na yureedu alAA<u>a</u>jilata AAajjaln<u>a</u> lahu feeh<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> nash<u>a</u>o liman nureedu thumma jaAAaln<u>a</u> lahu jahannama ya<u>s</u>l<u>a</u>h<u>a</u> ma<u>th</u>mooman mad<u>h</u>oor<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>18.  If any do wish<br> For the transitory things<br> (Of this life), We readily<br> Grant them—such things<br> As We will, to such persons<br> As We will: in the end<br> Have We provided Hell<br> For them: they will burn<br> Therein, disgraced and rejected.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_019.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>19.  Waman ar<u>a</u>da al-<u>a</u>khirata wasaAA<u>a</u> lah<u>a</u> saAAyah<u>a</u> wahuwa mu/minun faol<u>a</u>-ika k<u>a</u>na saAAyuhum mashkoor<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>19.  Those who do wish<br> For the (things of) the<br> Hereafter,<br> And strive therefor<br> With all due striving,<br> And have Faith,—<br> They are the ones<br> Whose striving is acceptable<br> (To God).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_020.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>20.  Kullan numiddu h<u>a</u>ol<u>a</u>-i wah<u>a</u>ol<u>a</u>-i min AAa<u>ta</u>-i rabbika wam<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>na AAa<u>ta</u>o rabbika ma<u>h</u><i><u>th</u></i>oor<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>20.  Of the bounties of thy Lord<br> We bestow freely on all—<br> These as well as those:<br> The bounties of thy Lord<br> Are not closed (to anyone).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_021.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>21.  On<i><u>th</u></i>ur kayfa fa<u>dd</u>aln<u>a</u> baAA<u>d</u>ahum AAal<u>a</u> baAA<u>d</u>in walal-<u>a</u>khiratu akbaru daraj<u>a</u>tin waakbaru taf<u>d</u>eel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>21.  See how We have bestowed<br> More on some than on others;<br> But verily the Hereafter<br> Is more in rank and gradation<br>And more in excellence.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_022.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>22.  L<u>a</u> tajAAal maAAa All<u>a</u>hi il<u>a</u>han <u>a</u>khara fataqAAuda ma<u>th</u>mooman makh<u>th</u>ool<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>22.  Take not with God<br> Another object of worship;<br> Or thou (O man!) wilt sit<br> In disgrace and destitution.'</p> <div class="filenav">
