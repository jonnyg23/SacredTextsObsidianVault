 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XVII.: Banī Isrā-īl, or the Children of Israel,</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 8</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_071.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>71.  Yawma nadAAoo kulla on<u>a</u>sin bi-im<u>a</u>mihim faman ootiya kit<u>a</u>bahu biyameenihi faol<u>a</u>-ika yaqraoona kit<u>a</u>bahum wal<u>a</u> yu<i><u>th</u></i>lamoona fateel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>71.  One day We shall call<br> Together all human beings<br> With their (respective) Imāms:<br> Those who are given their record<br> In their right hand<br> Will read it (with pleasure),<br> And they will not be<br> Dealt with unjustly<br> In the least.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_072.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>72.  Waman k<u>a</u>na fee h<u>ath</u>ihi aAAm<u>a</u> fahuwa fee al-<u>a</u>khirati aAAm<u>a</u> waa<u>d</u>allu sabeel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>72.  But those who were blind<br> In this world, will he<br> Blind in the Hereafter,<br> And most astray<br> From the Path.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_073.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>73.  Wa-in k<u>a</u>doo layaftinoonaka AAani alla<u>th</u>ee aw<u>h</u>ayn<u>a</u> ilayka litaftariya AAalayn<u>a</u> ghayrahu wa-i<u>th</u>an la<b>i</b>ttakha<u>th</u>ooka khaleel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>73.  And their purpose was<br> To tempt thee away<br> From that which We<br> Had revealed unto thee,<br> To substitute in Our name<br> Something quite different:<br> (In that case), behold!<br> They would certainly have<br> Made thee (their) friend!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_074.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>74.  Walawl<u>a</u> an thabbatn<u>a</u>ka laqad kidta tarkanu ilayhim shay-an qaleel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>74.  And had We not<br> Given thee strength,<br>Thou wouldst nearly<br> Have inclined to them<br> A little.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_075.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>75.  I<u>th</u>an laa<u>th</u>aqn<u>a</u>ka <u>d</u>iAAfa al<u>h</u>ay<u>a</u>ti wa<u>d</u>iAAfa almam<u>a</u>ti thumma l<u>a</u> tajidu laka AAalayn<u>a</u> na<u>s</u>eer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>75.  In that case We should<br> Have made thee taste<br> An equal portion (of punishment)<br> In this life, and an equal portion<br> In death: and moreover<br> Thou wouldst have found<br> None to help thee against Us!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_076.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>76.  Wa-in k<u>a</u>doo layastafizzoonaka mina al-ar<u>d</u>i liyukhrijooka minh<u>a</u> wa-i<u>th</u>an l<u>a</u> yalbathoona khil<u>a</u>faka ill<u>a</u> qaleel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>76.  Their purpose was to scare<br> Thee off the land,<br> In order to expel thee;<br> But in that case they<br> Would not have stayed<br> (Therein) after thee,<br> Except for a little while.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_077.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>77.  Sunnata man qad arsaln<u>a</u> qablaka min rusulin<u>a</u> wal<u>a</u> tajidu lisunnatin<u>a</u> ta<u>h</u>weel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>77.  (This was Our) way<br> With the apostles We sent<br> Before thee: thou wilt find<br> No change in Our ways.</p> <div class="filenav">
