 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra II.: Baqara, or the Heifer.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 9</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_072.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>72.  Wa-i<u>th</u> qataltum nafsan fa<b>i</b>dd<u>a</u>ra/tum feeh<u>a</u> wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu mukhrijun m<u>a </u>kuntum taktumoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>72.  Remember ye slew a man<br> And fell into a dispute<br> Among yourselves as to the crime:<br> But God was to bring forth<br> What ye did hide.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_073.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>73.  Faquln<u>a</u> i<u>d</u>riboohu bibaAA<u>d</u>ih<u>a</u> ka<u>tha</u>lika yu<u>h</u>yee All<u>a</u>hu almawt<u>a</u> wayureekum <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tihi laAAallakum taAAqiloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>73.  So We said: "Strike the (body)<br> With a piece of the (heifer)."<br> Thus God bringeth the dead<br> To life and showeth you His Signs:<br> Perchance ye may understand.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_074.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>74.  Thumma qasat quloobukum min baAAdi <u>tha</u>lika fahiya ka<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>ij<u>a</u>rati aw ashaddu qaswatan wa-inna mina al<u>h</u>ij<u>a</u>rati lam<u>a</u> yatafajjaru minhu al-anh<u>a</u>ru wa-inna minh<u>a</u> lam<u>a</u> yashshaqqaqu fayakhruju minhu alm<u>a</u>o wa-inna minh<u>a</u> lam<u>a</u> yahbi<u>t</u>u min khashyati All<u>a</u>hi wam<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hu bigh<u>a</u>filin AAamm<u>a</u> taAAmaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>74.  Thenceforth were your hearts<br> Hardened: they became<br> Like a rock and even worse<br> In hardness. For among rocks<br> There are some from which<br> Rivers gush forth; others<br> There are which when split<br> Asunder send forth water;<br> And others which sink<br>For fear of God. And God is<br> Not unmindful of what ye do.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_075.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>75.  Afata<u>t</u>maAAoona an yu/minoo lakum waqad k<u>a</u>na fareequn minhum yasmaAAoona kal<u>a</u>ma All<u>a</u>hi thumma yu<u>h</u>arrifoonahu min baAAdi m<u>a</u> AAaqaloohu wahum yaAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>75.  Can ye (O ye men of Faith)<br> Entertain the hope that they<br> Will believe in you?—<br> Seeing that a party of them<br> Heard the Word of God,<br> And perverted it knowingly<br> After they understood it.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_076.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>76.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> laqoo alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo q<u>a</u>loo <u>a</u>mann<u>a</u> wa-i<u>tha</u> khal<u>a</u> baAA<u>d</u>uhum il<u>a</u> baAA<u>d</u>in q<u>a</u>loo atu<u>h</u>addithoonahum bim<u>a</u> fata<u>h</u>a All<u>a</u>hu AAalaykum liyu<u>ha</u>jjookum bihi AAinda rabbikum afal<u>a</u> taAAqiloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>76.  Behold! when they meet<br> The men of Faith, they say:<br> "We believe": but when<br> They meet each other in private,<br> They say: "Shall you tell them<br> What God hath revealed to you,<br> That they may engage you<br> In argument about it<br> Before your Lord?"—<br> Do ye not understand (their aim)?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_077.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>77.  Awa l<u>a</u> yaAAlamoona anna All<u>a</u>ha yaAAlamu m<u>a</u> yusirroona wam<u>a</u> yuAAlinoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>77.  Know they not that God<br> Knoweth what they conceal<br> And what they reveal?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_078.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>78.  Waminhum ommiyyoona l<u>a</u> yaAAlamoona alkit<u>a</u>ba ill<u>a</u> am<u>a</u>niyya wa-in hum ill<u>a</u> ya<i><u>th</u></i>unnoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>78.  And there are among them<br> Illiterates, who know not the Book,<br> But (see therein their own) desires,<br> And they do nothing but conjecture.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_079.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>79.  Fawaylun lilla<u>th</u>eena yaktuboona alkit<u>a</u>ba bi-aydeehim thumma yaqooloona h<u>atha</u> min AAindi All<u>a</u>hi liyashtaroo bihi thamanan qaleelan fawaylun lahum mimm<u>a</u> katabat aydeehim wawaylun lahum mimm<u>a</u> yaksiboon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>79.  Then woe to those who write<br> The Book with their own hands,<br> And then say: "This is from God,"<br> To traffic with it<br> For a miserable price!—<br> Woe to them for what their hands<br> Do write, and for the gain<br> They make thereby.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_080.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>80.  Waq<u>a</u>loo lan tamassan<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>ru ill<u>a</u> ayy<u>a</u>man maAAdoodatan qul attakha<u>th</u>tum AAinda All<u>a</u>hi AAahdan falan yukhlifa All<u>a</u>hu AAahdahu am taqooloona AAal<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi m<u>a</u> l<u>a</u> taAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>80.  And they say: "The Fire<br> Shall not touch us<br> But for a few numbered days:"<br> Say: "Have ye taken a promise<br> From God, for He never<br> Breaks His promise?<br> Or is it that ye say of God<br> What ye do not know?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_081.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>81.  Bal<u>a</u> man kasaba sayyi-atan waa<u>hat</u>at bihi kha<u>t</u>ee-atuhu faol<u>a</u>-ika a<u>s</u>-<u>ha</u>bu a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>ri hum feeh<u>a</u> kh<u>a</u>lidoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>81.  Nay, those who seek gain<br> In Evil, and are girt round<br> By their sins,<br> They are Companions of the Fire:<br> Therein shall they abide<br> (For ever).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_082.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>82.  Wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo waAAamiloo a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>li<u>ha</u>ti ol<u>a</u>-ika a<u>s</u>-<u>ha</u>bu aljannati hum feeh<u>a</u> kh<u>a</u>lidoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>82.  But those who have faith<br> And work righteousness,<br>They are Companions of the Garden:<br> Therein shall they abide<br> (For ever).</p> <div class="filenav">
