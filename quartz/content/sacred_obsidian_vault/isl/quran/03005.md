 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXX.: Rūm, or The Roman Empire.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 5</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/030/030_041.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>41.  <i><u>Th</u></i>ahara alfas<u>a</u>du fee albarri wa<b>a</b>lba<u>h</u>ri bim<u>a</u> kasabat aydee a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si liyu<u>th</u>eeqahum baAA<u>d</u>a alla<u>th</u>ee AAamiloo laAAallahum yarjiAAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>41.  Mischief has appeared<br> On land and sea because<br> Of (the meed) that the hands<br> Of men have earned,<br> That (God) may give them<br> A taste of some of their<br> Deeds: in order that the<br> May turn back (from Evil).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/030/030_042.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>42.  Qul seeroo fee al-ar<u>d</u>i fa<b>o</b>n<i><u>th</u></i>uroo kayfa k<u>a</u>na AA<u>a</u>qibatu alla<u>th</u>eena min qablu k<u>a</u>na aktharuhum mushrikeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>42.  Say: "Travel through the earth<br> And see what was the End<br> Of those before (you):<br> Most of them worshipped<br> Others besides God."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/030/030_043.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>43.  Faaqim wajhaka li<b>l</b>ddeeni alqayyimi min qabli an ya/tiya yawmun l<u>a</u> maradda lahu mina All<u>a</u>hi yawma-i<u>th</u>in ya<u>ss</u>addaAAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>43.  But set thou thy face<br> To the right Religion,<br> Before there come from God<br> The Day which there is<br>No chance of averting:<br> On that Day shall men<br> Be divided (in two).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/030/030_044.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>44.  Man kafara faAAalayhi kufruhu waman AAamila <u>sa</u>li<u>h</u>an fali-anfusihim yamhadoon<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>44.  Those who reject Faith<br> Will suffer from that rejection:<br> And those who work righteousness<br> Will spread their couch<br> (Of repose) for themselves<br> (In heaven):</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/030/030_045.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>45.  Liyajziya alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo waAAamiloo a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>li<u>ha</u>ti min fa<u>d</u>lihi innahu l<u>a</u> yu<u>h</u>ibbu alk<u>a</u>fireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>45.  That He may reward those<br> Who believe and work righteous<br> Deeds, out of His Bounty.<br> For He loves not those<br> Who reject Faith.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/030/030_046.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>46.  Wamin <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tihi an yursila a<b>l</b>rriy<u>ah</u>a mubashshir<u>a</u>tin waliyu<u>th</u>eeqakum min ra<u>h</u>matihi walitajriya alfulku bi-amrihi walitabtaghoo min fa<u>d</u>lihi walaAAallakum tashkuroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>46.  Among His Signs is this,<br> That He sends the Winds,<br> As heralds of Glad Tidings,<br> Giving you a taste<br> Of His (Grace and) Mercy,—<br> That the ships may sail<br> (Majestically) by His Command<br> And that ye may seek<br>Of His Bounty: in order<br> That ye may be grateful.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/030/030_047.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>47.  Walaqad arsaln<u>a</u> min qablika rusulan il<u>a</u> qawmihim faj<u>a</u>oohum bi<b>a</b>lbayyin<u>a</u>ti fa<b>i</b>ntaqamn<u>a</u> mina alla<u>th</u>eena ajramoo wak<u>a</u>na <u>h</u>aqqan AAalayn<u>a</u> na<u>s</u>ru almu/mineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>47.  We did indeed send,<br> Before thee, apostles<br> To their (respective) peoples,<br> And they came to them<br> With Clear Signs: then,<br> To those who transgressed,<br> We meted out Retribution:<br> And it was due from us<br> To aid those who believed.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/030/030_048.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>48.  All<u>a</u>hu alla<u>th</u>ee yursilu a<b>l</b>rriy<u>ah</u>a fatutheeru sa<u>ha</u>ban fayabsu<u>t</u>uhu fee a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>-i kayfa yash<u>a</u>o wayajAAaluhu kisafan fatar<u>a</u> alwadqa yakhruju min khil<u>a</u>lihi fa-i<u>tha</u> a<u>sa</u>ba bihi man yash<u>a</u>o min AAib<u>a</u>dihi i<u>tha</u> hum yastabshiroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>48.  It is God Who sends<br> The Winds, and they raise<br> The Clouds: then does He<br> Spread them in the sky<br> As He wills, and break them<br> Into fragments, until thou seest<br> Rain-drops issue from the midst<br> Thereof: then when He has<br> Made them reach such<br> His servants as He wills,<br> Behold, they do rejoice!—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/030/030_049.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>49.  Wa-in k<u>a</u>noo min qabli an yunazzala AAalayhim min qablihi lamubliseen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>49.  Even though, before they received<br> (The rain)—just before this—<br> They were dumb with despair!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/030/030_050.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>50.  Fa<b>o</b>n<i><u>th</u></i>ur il<u>a</u> <u>a</u>th<u>a</u>ri ra<u>h</u>mati All<u>a</u>hi kayfa yu<u>h</u>yee al-ar<u>d</u>a baAAda mawtih<u>a</u> inna <u>tha</u>lika lamu<u>h</u>yee almawt<u>a</u> wahuwa AAal<u>a</u> kulli shay-in qadeer<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>50.  Then contemplate (O man!)<br> The memorials of God's Mercy!—<br> How He gives life<br> To the earth after<br> Its death: verily the Same<br> Will give life to the men<br>Who are dead: for He<br> Has power over all things.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/030/030_051.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>51.  Wala-in arsaln<u>a</u> ree<u>h</u>an faraawhu mu<u>s</u>farran la<i><u>th</u></i>alloo min baAAdihi yakfuroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>51.  And if We (but) send<br> A Wind from which<br> They see (their tilth)<br> Turn yellow,—behold,<br> They become, thereafter,<br> Ungrateful (Unbelievers)!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/030/030_052.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>52.  Fa-innaka l<u>a</u> tusmiAAu almawt<u>a</u> wal<u>a</u> tusmiAAu a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>umma a<b>l</b>dduAA<u>a</u>a i<u>tha</u> wallaw mudbireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>52.  So verily thou canst not<br> Make the dead to hear,<br> Nor canst thou make<br> The deaf to hear<br> The call, when they show<br> Their backs and turn away.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/030/030_053.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>53.  Wam<u>a</u> anta bih<u>a</u>di alAAumyi AAan <u>d</u>al<u>a</u>latihim in tusmiAAu ill<u>a</u> man yu/minu bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin<u>a</u> fahum muslimoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>53.  Nor canst thou lead back<br> The blind from their straying:<br> Only those wilt thou make<br> To hear, who believe<br> In Our Signs and submit<br> (Their wills in Islam).</p> <div class="filenav">
