 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_217.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>217.  Yas-aloonaka AAani a<b>l</b>shshahri al<u>h</u>ar<u>a</u>mi qit<u>a</u>lin feehi qul qit<u>a</u>lun feehi kabeerun wa<u>s</u>addun AAan sabeeli All<u>a</u>hi wakufrun bihi wa<b>a</b>lmasjidi al<u>h</u>ar<u>a</u>mi wa-ikhr<u>a</u>ju ahlihi minhu akbaru AAinda All<u>a</u>hi wa<b>a</b>lfitnatu akbaru mina alqatli wal<u>a</u> yaz<u>a</u>loona yuq<u>a</u>tiloonakum <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> yaruddookum AAan deenikum ini ista<u>ta</u>AAoo waman yartadid minkum AAan deenihi fayamut wahuwa k<u>a</u>firun faol<u>a</u>-ika <u>h</u>abi<u>t</u>at aAAm<u>a</u>luhum fee a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> wa<b>a</b>l-<u>a</u>khirati waol<u>a</u>-ika a<u>s</u>-<u>ha</u>bu a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>ri hum feeh<u>a</u> kh<u>a</u>lidoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>217.  They ask thee<br> Concerning fighting<br> In the Prohibited Month.<br> Say: "Fighting therein<br> Is a grave (offence);<br> But graver is it<br> In the sight of God<br> To prevent access<br> To the path of God,<br> To deny Him,<br> To prevent access<br> To the Sacred Mosque,<br> And drive out its members.<br> Tumult and oppression<br> Are worse than slaughter.<br> Nor will they cease<br> Fighting you until<br> They turn you back<br> From your faith<br> If they can.<br> And if any of you<br> Turn back from their faith<br> And die in unbelief,<br> Their works will bear no fruit<br> In this life<br> And in the Hereafter;<br> They will be<br> Companions of the Fire<br> And will abide therein.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_218.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>218.  Inna alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena h<u>a</u>jaroo waj<u>a</u>hadoo fee sabeeli All<u>a</u>hi ol<u>a</u>-ika yarjoona ra<u>h</u>mata All<u>a</u>hi wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu ghafoorun ra<u>h</u>eem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>218.  Those who believed<br> And those who suffered exile<br> And fought (and strove and struggled)<br> In the path of God,—<br> They have the hope<br> Of the Mercy of God;<br> And God is Oft-forgiving,<br> Most Merciful.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_219.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>219.  Yas-aloonaka AAani alkhamri wa<b>a</b>lmaysiri qul feehim<u>a</u> ithmun kabeerun waman<u>a</u>fiAAu li<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si wa-ithmuhum<u>a</u> akbaru min nafAAihim<u>a</u> wayas-aloonaka m<u>atha</u> yunfiqoona quli alAAafwa ka<u>tha</u>lika yubayyinu All<u>a</u>hu lakumu al-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>ti laAAallakum tatafakkaroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>219.  They ask thee<br> Concerning wine and gambling.<br> Say: "In them is great sin,<br> And some profit, for men;<br> But the sin is greater<br> Than the profit."<br> Whey ask thee how much<br> They are to spend;<br> Say: "What is beyond<br> Your needs."<br> Thus doth God<br> Make clear to you<br> His Signs: in order that<br> Ye may consider—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_220.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>220.  Fee a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> wa<b>a</b>l-<u>a</u>khirati wayas-aloonaka AAani alyat<u>a</u>m<u>a</u> qul i<u>s</u>l<u>ah</u>un lahum khayrun wa-in tukh<u>a</u>li<u>t</u>oohum fa-ikhw<u>a</u>nukum wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu yaAAlamu almufsida mina almu<u>s</u>li<u>h</u>i walaw sh<u>a</u>a All<u>a</u>hu laaAAnatakum inna All<u>a</u>ha AAazeezun <u>h</u>akeem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>220.  (Their bearings) on<br> This life and the Hereafter.<br>They ask thee<br> Concerning orphans.<br> Say: "The best thing to do<br> Is what is for their good;<br> If ye mix<br> Their affairs with yours,<br> They are your brethren;<br> But God knows<br>The man who means mischief<br> From the man who means good.<br> And if God had wished,<br> He could have put you<br> Into difficulties: He is indeed<br> Exalted in Power, Wise.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_221.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>221.  Wal<u>a</u> tanki<u>h</u>oo almushrik<u>a</u>ti <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> yu/minna walaamatun mu/minatun khayrun min mushrikatin walaw aAAjabatkum wal<u>a</u> tunki<u>h</u>oo almushrikeena <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> yu/minoo walaAAabdun mu/minun khayrun min mushrikin walaw aAAjabakum ol<u>a</u>-ika yadAAoona il<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>ri wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu yadAAoo il<u>a</u> aljannati wa<b>a</b>lmaghfirati bi-i<u>th</u>nihi wayubayyinu <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tihi li<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si laAAallahum yata<u>th</u>akkaroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>221.  Do not marry<br> Unbelieving women (idolaters),<br> Until they believe:<br> A slave woman who believes<br> Is better than an unbelieving woman,<br> Even though she allure you.<br> Nor marry (your girls)<br> To unbelievers until<br> They believe:<br> A man slave who believes<br> Is better than an unbeliever,<br> Even though he allure you.<br> Unbelievers do (but)<br> Beckon you to the Fire.<br> But God beckons by His Grace<br> To the Garden (of Bliss)<br> And forgiveness,<br> And makes His Signs<br> Clear to mankind:<br> That they may<br> Celebrate His praise.</p> <div class="filenav">
