 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XV.: Al-Hijr, or The Rocky Tract.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 2</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_016.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>16.  Walaqad jaAAaln<u>a</u> fee a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>-i buroojan wazayyann<u>a</u>h<u>a</u> li<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u><i><u>th</u></i>ireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>16.  It is We Who have set out<br> The Zodiacal Signs in the heavens,<br> And made them fair-seeming<br> To (all) beholders;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_017.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>17.  Wa<u>h</u>afi<i><u>th</u></i>n<u>a</u>h<u>a</u> min kulli shay<u>ta</u>nin rajeem<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>17.  And (moreover) We have guarded them<br>From every evil spirit accursed:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_018.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>18.  Ill<u>a</u> mani istaraqa a<b>l</b>ssamAAa faatbaAAahu shih<u>a</u>bun mubeen<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>18.  But any that gains a hearing<br> By stealth, is pursued<br> By a flaming fire, bright (to see).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_019.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>19.  Wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>a madadn<u>a</u>h<u>a</u> waalqayn<u>a</u> feeh<u>a</u> raw<u>a</u>siya waanbatn<u>a</u> feeh<u>a</u> min kulli shay-in mawzoon<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>19.  And the earth We have spread out<br> (Like a carpet); set thereon<br> Mountains firm and immovable;<br> And produced therein all kinds<br> Of things in due balance.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_020.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>20.  WajaAAaln<u>a</u> lakum feeh<u>a</u> maAA<u>a</u>yisha waman lastum lahu bir<u>a</u>ziqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>20.  And We have provided therein<br> Means of subsistence,—for you<br> And for those for whose sustenance<br> Ye are not responsible.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_021.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>21.  Wa-in min shay-in ill<u>a</u> AAindan<u>a</u> khaz<u>a</u>-inuhu wam<u>a</u> nunazziluhu ill<u>a</u> biqadarin maAAloom<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>21.  And there is not a thing<br> But its (sources and) treasures<br>(Inexhaustible) are with Us;<br> But We only send down<br> Thereof in due and ascertainable measures.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_022.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>22.  Waarsaln<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>rriy<u>ah</u>a law<u>a</u>qi<u>h</u>a faanzaln<u>a</u> mina a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>-i m<u>a</u>an faasqayn<u>a</u>kumoohu wam<u>a</u> antum lahu bikh<u>a</u>zineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>22.  And We send the fecundating winds,<br> Then cause the rain to descend<br> From the sky, therewith providing<br> You with water (in abundance),<br> Though ye are not the guardians<br> Of its stores.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_023.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>23.  Wa-inn<u>a</u> lana<u>h</u>nu nu<u>h</u>yee wanumeetu wana<u>h</u>nu alw<u>a</u>rithoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>23.  And verily, it is We<br> Who give life, and Who give<br> Death: it is We Who remain Inheritors<br> (After all else passes away).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_024.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>24.  Walaqad AAalimn<u>a</u> almustaqdimeena minkum walaqad AAalimn<u>a</u> almusta/khireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>24.  To Us are known those of you<br> Who hasten forward, and those<br> Who lag behind.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_025.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>25.  Wa-inna rabbaka huwa ya<u>h</u>shuruhum innahu <u>h</u>akeemun AAaleem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>25.  Assuredly it is thy Lord<br> Who will gather them together:<br> For He is Perfect in Wisdom<br> And Knowledge.</p> <div class="filenav">
