 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra VII.: A’rāf, or the Heights</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 2</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_011.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>11.  Walaqad khalaqn<u>a</u>kum thumma <u>s</u>awwarn<u>a</u>kum thumma quln<u>a</u> lilmal<u>a</u>-ikati osjudoo li-<u>a</u>dama fasajadoo ill<u>a</u> ibleesa lam yakun mina a<b>l</b>ss<u>a</u>jideen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>11.  It is We Who created you<br> And gave you shape;<br> Then We bade the angels<br> Bow down to Adam, and they<br> Bowed down; not so Iblīs;<br> He refused to be of those<br> Who bow down.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_012.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>12.  Q<u>a</u>la m<u>a</u> manaAAaka all<u>a</u> tasjuda i<u>th</u> amartuka q<u>a</u>la an<u>a</u> khayrun minhu khalaqtanee min n<u>a</u>rin wakhalaqtahu min <u>t</u>een<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>12.  (God) said: "What prevented<br> Thee from bowing down<br> When I commanded thee?"<br> He said: "I am better<br> Than he: Thou didst create<br> Me from fire, and him from clay."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_013.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>13.  Q<u>a</u>la fa<b>i</b>hbi<u>t</u> minh<u>a</u> fam<u>a</u> yakoonu laka an tatakabbara feeh<u>a</u> fa<b>o</b>khruj innaka mina a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>ghireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>13.  (God) said: "Get thee down<br> From this: it is not<br> For thee to be arrogant<br> Here: get out, for thou<br> Art of the meanest (of creatures)."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_014.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>14.  Q<u>a</u>la an<i><u>th</u></i>irnee il<u>a</u> yawmi yubAAathoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>14.  He said: "Give me respite<br> Till the day they are<br> Raised up."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_015.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>15.  Q<u>a</u>la innaka mina almun<i><u>th</u></i>areen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>15.  (God) said: "Be thou<br> Among those who have respite."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_016.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>16.  Q<u>a</u>la fabim<u>a</u> aghwaytanee laaqAAudanna lahum <u>s</u>ir<u>at</u>aka almustaqeem<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>16.  He said: "Because thou<br> Hast thrown me out<br> Of the Way, lo! I will<br> Lie in wait for them<br> On Thy Straight Way:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_017.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>17.  Thumma la<u>a</u>tiyannahum min bayni aydeehim wamin khalfihim waAAan aym<u>a</u>nihim waAAan sham<u>a</u>-ilihim wal<u>a</u> tajidu aktharahum sh<u>a</u>kireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>17.  "Then will I assault them<br> From before them and behind them,<br> From their right and their left:<br> Nor wilt Thou find,<br> In most of them,<br> Gratitude (for Thy mercies).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_018.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>18.  Q<u>a</u>la okhruj minh<u>a</u> ma<u>th</u>ooman mad<u>h</u>ooran laman tabiAAaka minhum laamlaanna jahannama minkum ajmaAAeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>18.  (God) said: "Get out<br> From this, disgraced<br> And expelled. If any<br> Of them follow thee,—<br> Hell will I fill<br> With you all.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_019.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>19.  Way<u>a</u> <u>a</u>damu oskun anta wazawjuka aljannata fakul<u>a</u> min <u>h</u>aythu shi/tum<u>a</u> wal<u>a</u> taqrab<u>a</u> h<u>ath</u>ihi a<b>l</b>shshajarata fatakoon<u>a</u> mina a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>19.  "O Adam! dwell thou<br> And thy wife in the Garden,<br> And enjoy (its good things)<br> As ye wish: but approach not<br> This tree, or ye run<br> Into harm and transgression."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_020.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>20.  Fawaswasa lahum<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>shshay<u>ta</u>nu liyubdiya lahum<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> wooriya AAanhum<u>a</u> min saw-<u>a</u>tihim<u>a</u> waq<u>a</u>la m<u>a</u> nah<u>a</u>kum<u>a</u> rabbukum<u>a</u> AAan h<u>ath</u>ihi a<b>l</b>shshajarati ill<u>a</u> an takoon<u>a</u> malakayni aw takoon<u>a</u> mina alkh<u>a</u>lideen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>20.  Then began Satan to whisper<br> Suggestions to them, bringing<br> Openly before their minds<br> All their shame<br>That was hidden from them<br> (Before): he said: "Your Lord<br> Only forbade you this tree,<br> Lest ye should become angels<br> Or such beings as live for ever."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_021.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>21.  Waq<u>a</u>samahum<u>a</u> innee lakum<u>a</u> lamina a<b>l</b>nn<u>as</u>i<u>h</u>een<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>21.  And he swore to them<br> Both, that he was<br> Their sincere adviser.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_022.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>22.  Fadall<u>a</u>hum<u>a</u> bighuroorin falamm<u>a</u> <u>tha</u>q<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>shshajarata badat lahum<u>a</u> saw-<u>a</u>tuhum<u>a</u> wa<u>t</u>afiq<u>a</u> yakh<u>s</u>if<u>a</u>ni AAalayhim<u>a</u> min waraqi aljannati wan<u>a</u>d<u>a</u>hum<u>a</u> rabbuhum<u>a</u> alam anhakum<u>a</u> AAan tilkum<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>shshajarati waaqul lakum<u>a</u> inna a<b>l</b>shshay<u>ta</u>na lakum<u>a</u> AAaduwwun mubeen<b>un</b>    </p>
 <p>22.  So by deceit he brought about<br> Their fall: when they<br> Tasted of the tree,<br> Their shame became manifest<br> To them, and they began<br> To sew together the leaves<br> Of the Garden over their bodies.<br> And their Lord called<br> Unto them: "Did I not<br> Forbid you that tree,<br> And tell you that Satan<br> Was an avowed<br> Enemy unto you?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_023.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>23.  Q<u>a</u>l<u>a</u> rabban<u>a</u> <i><u>th</u></i>alamn<u>a</u> anfusan<u>a</u> wa-in lam taghfir lan<u>a</u> watar<u>h</u>amn<u>a </u>lanakoonanna mina alkh<u>a</u>sireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>23.  They said: "Our Lord!<br> We have wronged our own souls:<br> If Thou forgive us not<br> And bestow not upon us<br> Thy Mercy, we shall<br> Certainly be lost."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_024.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>24.  Q<u>a</u>la ihbi<u>t</u>oo baAA<u>d</u>ukum libaAA<u>d</u>in AAaduwwun walakum fee al-ar<u>d</u>i mustaqarrun wamat<u>a</u>AAun il<u>a</u> <u>h</u>een<b>in</b>    </p>
 <p>24.  (God) said: "Get ye down,<br> With enmity between yourselves.<br> On earth will be your dwelling-place<br> And your means of livelihood,—<br> For a time."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_025.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>25.  Q<u>a</u>la feeh<u>a</u> ta<u>h</u>yawna wafeeh<u>a</u> tamootoona waminh<u>a</u> tukhrajoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>25.  He said: "Therein shall ye<br> Live, and therein shall ye<br> Die; but from it shall ye<br> Be taken out (at last)."</p> <div class="filenav">
