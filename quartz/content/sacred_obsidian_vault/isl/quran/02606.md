 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXVI.: Shu‘arāa, or The Poets.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 6</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_105.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>105.  Ka<u>thth</u>abat qawmu noo<u>h</u>in almursaleen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>105.  The people of Noah rejected<br> The apostles.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_106.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>106.  I<u>th</u> q<u>a</u>la lahum akhoohum noo<u>h</u>un al<u>a</u> tattaqoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>106.  Behold, their brother Noah<br> Said to them: "Will ye not<br> Fear (God)?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_107.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>107.  Innee lakum rasoolun ameen<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>107.  "I am to you an apostle<br> Worthy of all trust:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_108.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>108.  Fa<b>i</b>ttaqoo All<u>a</u>ha waa<u>t</u>eeAAoon<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>108.  "So fear God, and obey me.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_109.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>109.  Wam<u>a</u> as-alukum AAalayhi min ajrin in ajriya ill<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> rabbi alAA<u>a</u>lameen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>109.  "No reward do I ask<br> Of you for it: my reward<br> Is only from the Lord<br> Of the Worlds:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_110.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>110.  Fa<b>i</b>ttaqoo All<u>a</u>ha waa<u>t</u>eeAAoon<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>110.  "So fear God, and obey me."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_111.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>111.  Q<u>a</u>loo anu/minu laka wa<b>i</b>ttabaAAaka al-ar<u>th</u>aloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>111.  They said: "Shall we<br> Believe in thee when it is<br> The meanest that follow thee?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_112.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>112.  Q<u>a</u>la wam<u>a</u> AAilmee bim<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo yaAAmaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>112.  He said: "And what<br> Do I know as to<br> What they do?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_113.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>113.  In <u>h</u>is<u>a</u>buhum ill<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> rabbee law tashAAuroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>113.  "Their account is only<br> With my Lord, if ye<br> Could (but) understand.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_114.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>114.  Wam<u>a</u> an<u>a</u> bi<u>ta</u>ridi almu/mineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>114.  "I am not one to drive away<br> Those who believe.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_115.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>115.  In an<u>a</u> ill<u>a</u> na<u>th</u>eerun mubeen<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>115.  "I am sent only<br> To warn plainly in public."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_116.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>116.  Q<u>a</u>loo la-in lam tantahi y<u>a</u> noo<u>h</u>u latakoonanna mina almarjoomeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>116.  They said: "If thou<br> Desist not, O Noah!<br> Thou shalt be stoned<br> (to death)."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_117.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>117.  Q<u>a</u>la rabbi inna qawmee ka<u>thth</u>aboon<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>117.  He said: "O my Lord!<br> Truly my people have<br> Rejected me.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_118.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>118.  Fa<b>i</b>fta<u>h</u> baynee wabaynahum fat<u>h</u>an wanajjinee waman maAAiya mina almu/mineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>118.  "Judge thou, then, between me<br> And them openly, and deliver<br>Me and those of the Believers<br> Who are with me."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_119.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>119.  Faanjayn<u>a</u>hu waman maAAahu fee alfulki almashhoon<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>119.  So We delivered him<br> And those with him,<br> In the Ark filled<br> (With all creatures).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_120.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>120.  Thumma aghraqn<u>a</u> baAAdu alb<u>a</u>qeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>120.  Thereafter We drowned those<br> Who remained behind.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_121.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>121.  Inna fee <u>tha</u>lika la<u>a</u>yatan wam<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>na aktharuhum mu/mineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>121.  Verily in this is a Sign:<br> But most of them<br> Do not believe.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_122.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>122.  Wa-inna rabbaka lahuwa alAAazeezu a<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>eem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>122.  And verily thy Lord<br> Is He, the Exalted in Might,<br> Most Merciful.</p> <div class="filenav">
