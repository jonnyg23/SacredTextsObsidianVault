 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra II.: Baqara, or the Heifer.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 5</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_040.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>40.  Y<u>a</u> banee isr<u>a</u>-eela o<u>th</u>kuroo niAAmatiya allatee anAAamtu AAalaykum waawfoo biAAahdee oofi biAAahdikum wa-iyy<u>a</u>ya fa<b>i</b>rhaboon<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>40.  O Children of Israel! call to mind<br> The (special) favour which I bestowed<br> Upon you, and fulfil your Covenants<br> With Me as I fulfil My Covenant<br> With you, and fear none but Me.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_041.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>41.  Wa<u>a</u>minoo bim<u>a</u> anzaltu mu<u>s</u>addiqan lim<u>a</u> maAAakum wal<u>a</u> takoonoo awwala k<u>a</u>firin bihi wal<u>a</u> tashtaroo bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tee thamanan qaleelan wa-iyy<u>a</u>ya fa<b>i</b>ttaqoon<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>41.  And believe in what I reveal,<br> Confirming the revelation<br> Which is with you,<br> And be not the first to reject<br> Faith therein, nor sell My Signs<br> For a small price; and fear Me,<br> And Me alone.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_042.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>42.  Wal<u>a</u> talbisoo al<u>h</u>aqqa bi<b>a</b>lb<u>at</u>ili wataktumoo al<u>h</u>aqqa waantum taAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>42.  And cover not Truth<br> With falsehood, nor conceal<br> The Truth when ye know (what it is).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_043.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>43.  Waaqeemoo a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>al<u>a</u>ta wa<u>a</u>too a<b>l</b>zzak<u>a</u>ta wa<b>i</b>rkaAAoo maAAa a<b>l</b>rr<u>a</u>kiAAeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>43.  And be steadfast in prayer;<br> Practise regular charity;<br> And bow down your heads<br> With those who bow down (in worship).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_044.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>44.  Ata/muroona a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>sa bi<b>a</b>lbirri watansawna anfusakum waantum tatloona alkit<u>a</u>ba afal<u>a</u> taAAqiloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>44.  Do ye enjoin right conduct<br> On the people, and forget<br> (To practise it) yourselves,<br> And yet ye study the Scripture?<br> Will ye not understand?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_045.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>45.  Wa<b>i</b>staAAeenoo bi<b>al</b><u>ss</u>abri wa<b>al</b><u>ss</u>al<u>a</u>ti wa-innah<u>a</u> lakabeeratun ill<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> alkh<u>a</u>shiAAeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>45.  Nay, seek (God's) help<br> With patient perseverance<br> And prayer:<br> It is indeed hard, except<br> To those who bring a lowly spirit,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_046.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>46.  Alla<u>th</u>eena ya<i><u>th</u></i>unnoona annahum mul<u>a</u>qoo rabbihim waannahum ilayhi r<u>a</u>jiAAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>46.  Who bear in mind the certainty<br> That they are to meet their Lord,<br> And that they are to return to Him.</p> <div class="filenav">
