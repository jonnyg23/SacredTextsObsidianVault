 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra X.: Yūnus, or Jonah.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 5</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/010/010_041.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>41.  Wa-in ka<u>thth</u>abooka faqul lee AAamalee walakum AAamalukum antum baree-oona mimm<u>a</u> aAAmalu waan<u>a</u> baree-on mimm<u>a</u> taAAmaloon<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>41.  If they charge thee<br> With falsehood, say:<br> "My work to me,<br> And yours to you!<br>Ye are free from responsibility<br> For what I do, and I<br> For what ye do!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/010/010_042.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>42.  Waminhum man yastamiAAoona ilayka afaanta tusmiAAu a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>umma walaw k<u>a</u>noo l<u>a</u> yaAAqiloon<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>42.  Among them are some who<br> (Pretend to) listen to thee:<br> But canst thou make the deaf<br> To hear,—even though<br> They are without understanding?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/010/010_043.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>43.  Waminhum man yan<i><u>th</u></i>uru ilayka afaanta tahdee alAAumya walaw k<u>a</u>noo l<u>a</u> yub<u>s</u>iroon<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>43.  And among them are some<br> Who look at thee:<br> But canst thou guide<br> The blind,—even though<br> They will not see?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/010/010_044.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>44.  Inna All<u>a</u>ha l<u>a</u> ya<i><u>th</u></i>limu a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>sa shay-an wal<u>a</u>kinna a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>sa anfusahum ya<i><u>th</u></i>limoon<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>44.  Verily God will not deal<br> Unjustly with man in aught:<br> It is man that wrongs<br> His own soul.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/010/010_045.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>45.  Wayawma ya<u>h</u>shuruhum kaan lam yalbathoo ill<u>a</u> s<u>a</u>AAatan mina a<b>l</b>nnah<u>a</u>ri yataAA<u>a</u>rafoona baynahum qad khasira alla<u>th</u>eena ka<u>thth</u>aboo biliq<u>a</u>-i All<u>a</u>hi wam<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo muhtadeen<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>45.  One day He will<br> Gather them together:<br> (It will be) as if<br> They had tarried<br> But an hour of a day:<br> They will recognise each other:<br> Assuredly those will be lost<br> Who denied the meeting<br> With God and refused<br> To receive true guidance.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/010/010_046.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>46.  Wa-imm<u>a</u> nuriyannaka baAA<u>d</u>a alla<u>th</u>ee naAAiduhum aw natawaffayannaka fa-ilayn<u>a</u> marjiAAuhum thumma All<u>a</u>hu shaheedun AAal<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> yafAAaloon<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>46.  Whether We show thee<br> (Realised in thy life-time)<br> Some part of what We<br> Promise them,—or We<br>Take thy soul (to Our Mercy)<br> (Before that),—in any case,<br> To Us is their return:<br> Ultimately God is witness.<br> To all that they do.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/010/010_047.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>47.  Walikulli ommatin rasoolun fa-i<u>tha</u> j<u>a</u>a rasooluhum qu<u>d</u>iya baynahum bi<b>a</b>lqis<u>t</u>i wahum l<u>a</u> yu<i><u>th</u></i>lamoon<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>47.  To every people (was sent)<br> An Apostle: when their Apostle<br> Comes (before them), the matter<br> Will be judged between them<br> With justice, and they<br> Will not be wronged.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/010/010_048.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>48.  Wayaqooloona mat<u>a</u> h<u>atha</u> alwaAAdu in kuntum <u>sa</u>diqeen<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>48.  They say: "When<br> Will this promise<br> Come to pass,<br> If ye speak the truth?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/010/010_049.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>49.  Qul l<u>a</u> amliku linafsee <u>d</u>arran wal<u>a</u> nafAAan ill<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> sh<u>a</u>a All<u>a</u>hu likulli ommatin ajalun i<u>tha</u> j<u>a</u>a ajaluhum fal<u>a</u> yasta/khiroona s<u>a</u>AAatan wal<u>a</u> yastaqdimoon<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>49.  Say: "I have no power<br> Over any harm or profit<br> To myself except as God<br> Willeth. To every People<br> Is a term appointed:<br> When their term is reached,<br> Not an hour can they cause<br> Delay, nor (an hour) can they<br> Advance (it in anticipation)."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/010/010_050.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>50.  Qul araaytum in at<u>a</u>kum AAa<u>tha</u>buhu bay<u>a</u>tan aw nah<u>a</u>ran m<u>atha</u> yastaAAjilu minhu almujrimoon<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>50.  Say: "Do ye see,<br> If His punishment should come<br> To you by night or by day,—<br> What portion of it<br> Would the Sinners<br> Wish to hasten?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/010/010_051.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>51.  Athumma i<u>tha</u> m<u>a</u> waqaAAa <u>a</u>mantum bihi <u>a</u>l-<u>a</u>na waqad kuntum bihi tastaAAjiloon<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>51.  "Would ye then believe in it<br> At last, when it actually cometh<br> To pass? (It will then be said:)<br> 'Ah! now? and ye wanted<br> (Aforetime) to hasten it on!'</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/010/010_052.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>52.  Thumma qeela lilla<u>th</u>eena <i><u>th</u></i>alamoo <u>th</u>ooqoo AAa<u>tha</u>ba alkhuldi hal tujzawna ill<u>a</u> bim<u>a</u> kuntum taksiboon<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>52.  "At length will be said<br> To the wrong-doers: 'Taste ye<br> The enduring punishment!<br> Ye get but the recompense<br> Of what ye earned!'"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/010/010_053.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>53.  Wayastanbi-oonaka a<u>h</u>aqqun huwa qul ee warabbee innahu la<u>h</u>aqqun wam<u>a</u> antum bimuAAjizeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>53.  They seek to be informed<br> By thee: "Is that true?"<br> Say: "Aye! by my Lord!<br> It is the very truth!<br> And ye cannot frustrate it!"</p> <div class="filenav">
