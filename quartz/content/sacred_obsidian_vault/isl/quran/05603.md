 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra LVI.: Wāqi‘a, or The Inevitable Event.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 3</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_075.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>75.  Fal<u>a</u> oqsimu bimaw<u>a</u>qiAAi a<b>l</b>nnujoom<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>75.  Furthermore I call<br> To witness the setting<br> Of the Stars,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_076.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>76.  Wa-innahu laqasamun law taAAlamoona AAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>76.  And that is indeed<br> A mighty adjuration<br> If ye but knew,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_077.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>77.  Innahu laqur-<u>a</u>nun kareem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>77.  That this is indeed<br> A Qur-ān most honourable,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_078.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>78.  Fee kit<u>a</u>bin maknoon<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>78.  In a Book well-guarded,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_079.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>79.  L<u>a</u> yamassuhu ill<u>a</u> almu<u>t</u>ahharoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>79.  Which none shall touch<br> But those who are clean:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_080.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>80.  Tanzeelun min rabbi alAA<u>a</u>lameen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>80.  A Revelation from the Lord<br> Of the Worlds.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_081.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>81.  Afabih<u>atha</u> al<u>h</u>adeethi antum mudhinoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>81.  Is it such a Message<br> That ye would hold<br> In light esteem?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_082.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>82.  WatajAAaloona rizqakum annakum tuka<u>thth</u>iboon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>82.  And have ye made it<br> Your livelihood that ye<br> Should declare it false?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_083.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>83.  Falawl<u>a</u> i<u>tha</u> balaghati al<u>h</u>ulqoom<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>83.  Then why do ye not<br> (Intervene) when (the soul<br> Of the dying man)<br> Reaches the throat,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_084.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>84.  Waantum <u>h</u>eena-i<u>th</u>in tan<i><u>th</u></i>uroona   </p>
 <p>84.  And ye the while<br> (Sit) looking on,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_085.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>85.  Wana<u>h</u>nu aqrabu ilayhi minkum wal<u>a</u>kin l<u>a</u> tub<u>s</u>iroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>85.  But We are nearer<br> To him than ye,<br> And yet see not,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_086.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>86.  Falawl<u>a</u> in kuntum ghayra madeeneen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>86.  Then why do ye not,—<br> If you are exempt<br> From (future) account,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_087.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>87.  TarjiAAoonah<u>a</u> in kuntum <u>sa</u>diqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>87.  Call back the soul,<br> If ye are true<br> (In your claim of Independence)?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_088.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>88.  Faamm<u>a</u> in k<u>a</u>na mina almuqarrabeen<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>88.  Thus, then, if he<br> Be of those Nearest to God,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_089.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>89.  Faraw<u>h</u>un waray<u>ha</u>nun wajannatu naAAeem<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>89.  (There is for him) Rest<br> And Satisfaction, and<br> A Garden of Delights.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_090.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>90.  Waamm<u>a</u> in k<u>a</u>na min a<u>s</u>-<u>ha</u>bi alyameen<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>90.  And if he be<br> Of the Companions of<br> The Right Hand,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_091.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>91.  Fasal<u>a</u>mun laka min a<u>s</u>-<u>ha</u>bi alyameen<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>91.  (For him is the salutation),<br> "Peace be unto thee",<br> From the Companions<br> Of the Right Hand.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_092.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>92.  Waamm<u>a</u> in k<u>a</u>na mina almuka<u>thth</u>ibeena a<b>l</b><u>dda</u>lleen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>92.  And if he be<br> Of those who treat<br> (Truth) as Falsehood,<br> Who go wrong,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_093.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>93.  Fanuzulun min <u>h</u>ameem<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>93.  For him is Entertainment<br> With Boiling Water,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_094.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>94.  Wata<u>s</u>liyatu ja<u>h</u>eem<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>94.  And burning in Hell-Fire.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_095.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>95.  Inna h<u>atha</u> lahuwa <u>h</u>aqqu alyaqeen<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>95.  Verily, this is<br> The Very Truth<br> And Certainty.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_096.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>96.  Fasabbi<u>h</u> bi<b>i</b>smi rabbika alAAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>96.  So celebrate with praises<br> The name of thy Lord,<br> The Supreme.</p>  <div class="filenav">
