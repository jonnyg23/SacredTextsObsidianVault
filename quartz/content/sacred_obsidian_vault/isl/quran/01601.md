 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XVI.: Naḥl or The Bee.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  At<u>a</u> amru All<u>a</u>hi fal<u>a</u> tastaAAjiloohu sub<u>ha</u>nahu wataAA<u>a</u>l<u>a</u> AAamm<u>a</u> yushrikoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>1.  (Inevitable) cometh (to pass)<br> The Command of God:<br> Seek ye not then<br> To hasten it: glory to Him,<br> And far is He above<br> Having the partners<br> They ascribe unto Him!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  Yunazzilu almal<u>a</u>-ikata bi<b>al</b>rroo<u>h</u>i min amrihi AAal<u>a</u> man yash<u>a</u>o min AAib<u>a</u>dihi an an<u>th</u>iroo annahu l<u>a</u> il<u>a</u>ha ill<u>a</u> an<u>a</u> fa<b>i</b>ttaqoon<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>2.  He doth send down His angels<br> With inspiration of His Command,<br> To such of His servants<br> As He pleaseth, (saying):<br> "Warn (Man) that there is<br> No god but I: so do<br> Your duty unto Me."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  Khalaqa a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>a bi<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>aqqi taAA<u>a</u>l<u>a</u> AAamm<u>a</u> yushrikoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>3.  He has created the heavens<br> And the earth for just ends:<br> Far is He above having<br> The partners they ascribe to Him!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  Khalaqa al-ins<u>a</u>na min nu<u>t</u>fatin fa-i<u>tha</u> huwa kha<u>s</u>eemun mubeen<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>4.  He has created man<br> From a sperm-drop;<br> And behold this same (man)<br> Becomes an open disputer!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  Wa<b>a</b>l-anAA<u>a</u>ma khalaqah<u>a</u> lakum feeh<u>a</u> dif-on waman<u>a</u>fiAAu waminh<u>a</u> ta-kuloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>5.  And cattle He has created<br> For you (men): from them<br> Ye derive warmth,<br> And numerous benefits,<br> And of their (meat) ye eat.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  Walakum feeh<u>a</u> jam<u>a</u>lun <u>h</u>eena turee<u>h</u>oona wa<u>h</u>eena tasra<u>h</u>oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>6.  And ye have a sense<br> Of pride and beauty in them<br> As ye drive them home<br> In the evening, and as ye<br> Lead them forth to pasture<br> In the morning.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  Wata<u>h</u>milu athq<u>a</u>lakum il<u>a</u> baladin lam takoonoo b<u>a</u>ligheehi ill<u>a</u> bishiqqi al-anfusi inna rabbakum laraoofun ra<u>h</u>eem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>7.  And they carry your heavy loads<br> To lands that ye could not<br> (Otherwise) reach except with<br> Souls distressed: for your Lord<br> Is indeed Most Kind, Most Merciful</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  Wa<b>a</b>lkhayla wa<b>a</b>lbigh<u>a</u>la wa<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>ameera litarkabooh<u>a</u> wazeenatan wayakhluqu m<u>a</u> l<u>a</u> taAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>8.  And (He has created) horses,<br> Mules, and donkeys, for you<br> To ride and use for show;<br> And He has created (other) things<br> Of which ye have no knowledge.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_009.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>9.  WaAAal<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi qa<u>s</u>du a<b>l</b>ssabeeli waminh<u>a</u> j<u>a</u>-irun walaw sh<u>a</u>a lahad<u>a</u>kum ajmaAAeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>9.  and unto God leads straight<br> The Way, but there are ways<br> That turn aside: if God<br> Had willed, He could have<br> Guided all of you.</p> <div class="filenav">
