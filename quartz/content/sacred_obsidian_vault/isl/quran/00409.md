 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra IV.: Nisāa, or The Women.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 9</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_060.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>60.  Alam tara il<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena yazAAumoona annahum <u>a</u>manoo bim<u>a</u> onzila ilayka wam<u>a</u> onzila min qablika yureedoona an yata<u>ha</u>kamoo il<u>a</u> a<b>l</b><u>tta</u>ghooti waqad omiroo an yakfuroo bihi wayureedu a<b>l</b>shshay<u>ta</u>nu an yu<u>d</u>illahum <u>d</u>al<u>a</u>lan baAAeed<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>60.  Hast thou not turned<br> Thy vision to those<br> Who declare that they believe<br> In the revelations<br> That have come to thee<br> And to those before thee?<br> Their (real) wish is<br> To resort together for judgment<br> (In their disputes)<br> To the Evil One,<br> Though they were ordered<br> To reject him.<br> But Satan's wish<br> Is to lead them astray<br> Far away (from the Right).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_061.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>61.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> qeela lahum taAA<u>a</u>law il<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> anzala All<u>a</u>hu wa-il<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>rrasooli raayta almun<u>a</u>fiqeena ya<u>s</u>uddoona AAanka <u>s</u>udood<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>61.  When it is said to them:<br> "Come to what God hath revealed,<br>And to the Apostle":<br> Thou seest the Hypocrites avert<br> Their faces from thee in disgust.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_062.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>62.  Fakayfa i<u>tha</u> a<u>sa</u>bat-hum mu<u>s</u>eebatun bim<u>a</u> qaddamat aydeehim thumma j<u>a</u>ooka ya<u>h</u>lifoona bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi in aradn<u>a</u> ill<u>a</u> i<u>h</u>s<u>a</u>nan watawfeeq<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>62.  How then, when they are<br> Seized by misfortune,<br> Because of the deeds<br> Which their hands have sent forth?<br> Then they come to thee,<br> Swearing by God:<br> "We meant no more<br> Than good-will and conciliation!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_063.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>63.  Ol<u>a</u>-ika alla<u>th</u>eena yaAAlamu All<u>a</u>hu m<u>a</u> fee quloobihim faaAAri<u>d</u> AAanhum waAAi<i><u>th</u></i>hum waqul lahum fee anfusihim qawlan baleegh<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>63.  Those men,—God knows<br> What is in their hearts;<br> So keep clear of them,ss:<br> But admonish them,<br> And speak to them a word<br> To reach their very souls.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_064.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>64.  Wam<u>a</u> arsaln<u>a</u> min rasoolin ill<u>a</u> liyu<u>ta</u>AAa bi-i<u>th</u>ni All<u>a</u>hi walaw annahum i<u>th</u> <i><u>th</u></i>alamoo anfusahum j<u>a</u>ooka fa<b>i</b>staghfaroo All<u>a</u>ha wa<b>i</b>staghfara lahumu a<b>l</b>rrasoolu lawajadoo All<u>a</u>ha taww<u>a</u>ban ra<u>h</u>eem<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>64.  We sent not an Apostle,<br> But to be obeyed, in accordance<br> With the Will of God.<br> If they had only,<br> When they were unjust<br> To themselves,<br> Come unto thee<br> And asked God's forgiveness,<br> And the Apostle had asked<br> Forgiveness for them,<br> They would have found<br> God indeed Oft-returning,<br> Most Merciful.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_065.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>65.  Fal<u>a</u> warabbika l<u>a</u> yu/minoona <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> yu<u>h</u>akkimooka feem<u>a</u> shajara baynahum thumma l<u>a</u> yajidoo fee anfusihim <u>h</u>arajan mimm<u>a</u> qa<u>d</u>ayta wayusallimoo tasleem<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>65.  But no, by thy Lord,<br> They can have<br> No (real) Faith,<br> Until they make thee judge<br> In all disputes between them,<br> And find in their souls<br> No resistance against<br> Thy decisions, but accept<br> Them with the fullest conviction.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_066.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>66.  Walaw ann<u>a</u> katabn<u>a</u> AAalayhim ani oqtuloo anfusakum awi okhrujoo min diy<u>a</u>rikum m<u>a</u> faAAaloohu ill<u>a</u> qaleelun minhum walaw annahum faAAaloo m<u>a</u> yooAAa<i><u>th</u></i>oona bihi lak<u>a</u>na khayran lahum waashadda tathbeet<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>66.  If We had ordered them<br> To sacrifice their lives<br> Or to leave their homes,<br> Very few of them<br> Would have done it:<br> But if they had done<br> What they were (actually) told,<br> It would have been best<br> For them, and would have gone<br> Farthest to strengthen their (faith);</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_067.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>67.  Wa-i<u>th</u>an la<u>a</u>tayn<u>a</u>hum min ladunn<u>a</u> ajran AAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>67.  And We should then have<br> Given them from Our Presence<br> A great reward;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_068.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>68.  Walahadayn<u>a</u>hum <u>s</u>ir<u>at</u>an mustaqeem<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>68.  And We should have<br> Shown them the Straight Way.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_069.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>69.  Waman yu<u>t</u>iAAi All<u>a</u>ha wa<b>al</b>rrasoola faol<u>a</u>-ika maAAa alla<u>th</u>eena anAAama All<u>a</u>hu AAalayhim mina a<b>l</b>nnabiyyeena wa<b>al</b><u>ss</u>iddeeqeena wa<b>al</b>shshuhad<u>a</u>-i wa<b>al</b><u>ssa</u>li<u>h</u>eena wa<u>h</u>asuna ol<u>a</u>-ika rafeeq<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>69.  All who obey God<br> And the Apostle<br> Are in the company<br> Of those on whom<br> Is the Grace of God,—<br> Of the Prophets (who teach),<br> The Sincere (lovers of Truth),<br> The Witnesses (who testify),<br> And the Righteous (who do good):<br> Ah! what a beautiful Fellowship!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_070.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>70.  <u>Tha</u>lika alfa<u>d</u>lu mina All<u>a</u>hi wakaf<u>a</u> bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi AAaleem<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>70.  Such is the Bounty<br> From God: and sufficient<br> Is it that God knoweth all.</p> <div class="filenav">
