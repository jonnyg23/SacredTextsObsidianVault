 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra V.: Māïda, or The Table Spread.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 16</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_116.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>116.  Wa-i<u>th</u> q<u>a</u>la All<u>a</u>hu y<u>a</u> AAees<u>a</u> ibna maryama aanta qulta li<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si ittakhi<u>th</u>oonee waommiya il<u>a</u>hayni min dooni All<u>a</u>hi q<u>a</u>la sub<u>ha</u>naka m<u>a</u> yakoonu lee an aqoola m<u>a</u> laysa lee bi<u>h</u>aqqin in kuntu qultuhu faqad AAalimtahu taAAlamu m<u>a</u> fee nafsee wal<u>a</u> aAAlamu m<u>a</u> fee nafsika innaka anta AAall<u>a</u>mu alghuyoob<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>116.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">119</font></span> And behold! God will say:<br> "O Jesus the son of Mary!<br> Didst thou say unto men,<br> Worship me and my mother<br> As gods in derogation of God'?"<br> He will say: "Glory to Thee!<br> Never could I say<br> What I had no right<br> (To say). Had I said<br> Such a thing, Thou wouldst<br> Indeed have known it.<br> Thou knowest what is<br> In my heart, though I<br> Know not what is<br> In Thine. For Thou<br> Knowest in full<br> All that is hidden.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_117.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>117.  M<u>a</u> qultu lahum ill<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> amartanee bihi ani oAAbudoo All<u>a</u>ha rabbee warabbakum wakuntu AAalayhim shaheedan m<u>a</u> dumtu feehim falamm<u>a</u> tawaffaytanee kunta anta a<b>l</b>rraqeeba AAalayhim waanta AAal<u>a</u> kulli shay-in shaheed<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>117.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">120</font></span> "Never said I to them<br> Aught except what Thou<br> Didst command me<br> To say, to wit, 'Worship<br> God, my Lord and your Lord';<br> And I was a witness<br> Over them whilst I dwelt<br> Amongst them; when thou<br> Didst take me up<br> Thou wast the Watcher<br> Over them, and Thou<br> Art a witness to all things.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_118.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>118.  In tuAAa<u>thth</u>ibhum fa-innahum AAib<u>a</u>duka wa-in taghfir lahum fa-innaka anta alAAazeezu al<u>h</u>akeem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>118.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">121</font></span> "If Thou dost punish them,<br> They are Thy servants:<br>If Thou dost forgive them,<br> Thou art the Exalted in power,<br> The Wise."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_119.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>119.  Q<u>a</u>la All<u>a</u>hu h<u>atha</u> yawmu yanfaAAu a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>diqeena <u>s</u>idquhum lahum jann<u>a</u>tun tajree min ta<u>h</u>tih<u>a</u> al-anh<u>a</u>ru kh<u>a</u>lideena feeh<u>a</u> abadan ra<u>d</u>iya All<u>a</u>hu AAanhum wara<u>d</u>oo AAanhu <u>tha</u>lika alfawzu alAAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>119.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">122</font></span> God will say: "This is<br> A day on which<br> The truthful will profit<br> From their truth: theirs<br> Are Gardens, with rivers<br> Flowing beneath,—their eternal<br> Home: God well-pleased<br> With them, and they with God:<br> That is the great Salvation,<br> (The fulfilment of all desires).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_120.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>120.  Lill<u>a</u>hi mulku a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i wam<u>a</u> feehinna wahuwa AAal<u>a</u> kulli shay-in qadeer<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>120.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">123</font></span> To God doth belong the dominion<br> Of the heavens and the earth,<br> And all that is therein,<br> And it is He who hath power<br> Over all things.</p>  <div class="filenav">
