 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XI.: Hūd (The Prophet Hūd).</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 7</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_069.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>69.  Walaqad j<u>a</u>at rusulun<u>a</u> ibr<u>a</u>heema bi<b>a</b>lbushr<u>a</u> q<u>a</u>loo sal<u>a</u>man q<u>a</u>la sal<u>a</u>mun fam<u>a</u> labitha an j<u>a</u>a biAAijlin <u>h</u>anee<u>th</u><b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>69.  There came Our Messengers<br> To Abraham with glad tidings.<br>They said, "Peace!" He answered,<br> "Peace!" and hastened<br> To entertain them<br> With a roasted calf.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_070.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>70.  Falamm<u>a</u> ra<u>a</u> aydiyahum l<u>a</u> ta<u>s</u>ilu ilayhi nakirahum waawjasa minhum kheefatan q<u>a</u>loo l<u>a</u> takhaf inn<u>a</u> orsiln<u>a</u> il<u>a</u> qawmi loo<u>t</u><b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>70.  But when he saw<br> Their hands went not<br> Towards the (meal), he felt<br> Some mistrust of them,<br> And conceived a fear of them.<br> They said: "Fear not:<br> We have been sent<br> Against the people of Lūt."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_071.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>71.  Wa<b>i</b>mraatuhu q<u>a</u>-imatun fa<u>d</u>a<u>h</u>ikat fabashsharn<u>a</u>h<u>a</u> bi-is<u>ha</u>qa wamin war<u>a</u>-i is<u>ha</u>qa yaAAqoob<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>71.  And his wife was standing<br> (There), and she laughed:<br> But We gave her<br> Glad tidings of Isaac,<br> And after him, of Jacob.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_072.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>72.  Q<u>a</u>lat y<u>a</u> waylat<u>a</u> aalidu waan<u>a</u> AAajoozun wah<u>atha</u> baAAlee shaykhan inna h<u>atha</u> lashay-on AAajeeb<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>72.  She said: "Alas for me!<br> Shall I bear a <a href="errata.htm#3">child</a>,<br> Seeing I am an old woman,<br> And my husband here<br> Is an old man?<br> That would indeed<br> Be a wonderful thing!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_073.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>73.  Q<u>a</u>loo ataAAjabeena min amri All<u>a</u>hi ra<u>h</u>matu All<u>a</u>hi wabarak<u>a</u>tuhu AAalaykum ahla albayti innahu <u>h</u>ameedun majeed<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>73.  They said: "Dost thou<br> Wonder at God's decree?<br> The grace of God<br> And His blessings on you,<br> O ye people of the house!<br> For He is indeed<br> Worthy of all praise,<br> Full of all glory!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_074.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>74.  Falamm<u>a</u> <u>th</u>ahaba AAan ibr<u>a</u>heema a<b>l</b>rrawAAu waj<u>a</u>at-hu albushr<u>a </u>yuj<u>a</u>dilun<u>a</u> fee qawmi loo<u>t</u><b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>74.  When fear had passed<br> From (the mind of) Abraham<br> And the glad tidings<br> Had reached him, he<br> Began to plead with Us<br> For Lūt's people.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_075.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>75.  Inna ibr<u>a</u>heema la<u>h</u>aleemun aww<u>a</u>hun muneeb<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>75.  For Abraham was,<br> Without doubt, forbearing<br> (Of faults), compassionate,<br> And given to look to God.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_076.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>76.  Y<u>a</u> ibr<u>a</u>heemu aAAri<u>d</u> AAan h<u>atha</u> innahu qad j<u>a</u>a amru rabbika wa-innahum <u>a</u>teehim AAa<u>tha</u>bun ghayru mardood<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>76.  O Abraham! Seek not this.<br> The decree of thy Lord<br> Hath gone forth: for them<br> There cometh a Penalty<br>That cannot be turned back!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_077.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>77.  Walamm<u>a</u> j<u>a</u>at rusulun<u>a</u> loo<u>t</u>an see-a bihim wa<u>da</u>qa bihim <u>th</u>arAAan waq<u>a</u>la h<u>atha</u> yawmun AAa<u>s</u>eeb<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>77.  When Our Messengers<br> Came to Lūt, he was<br> Grieved on their account<br> And felt himself powerless<br> (To protect) them. He said:<br> "This is a distressful day."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_078.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>78.  Waj<u>a</u>ahu qawmuhu yuhraAAoona ilayhi wamin qablu k<u>a</u>noo yaAAmaloona a<b>l</b>ssayyi-<u>a</u>ti q<u>a</u>la y<u>a</u> qawmi h<u>a</u>ol<u>a</u>-i ban<u>a</u>tee hunna a<u>t</u>haru lakum fa<b>i</b>ttaqoo All<u>a</u>ha wal<u>a</u> tukhzooni fee <u>d</u>ayfee alaysa minkum rajulun rasheed<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>78.  And his people came<br> Rushing towards him,<br> And they had been long<br> In the habit of practising<br> Abominations. He said:<br> "O my people! Here are<br> My daughters: they are purer<br> For you (if ye marry)!<br> Now fear God, and cover me not<br> With shame about my guests!<br> Is there not among you<br> A single right-minded man?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_079.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>79.  Q<u>a</u>loo laqad AAalimta m<u>a</u> lan<u>a</u> fee ban<u>a</u>tika min <u>h</u>aqqin wa-innaka lataAAlamu m<u>a</u> nureed<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>79.  They said: "Well dost thou<br> Know we have no need<br> Of thy daughters: indeed<br> Thou knowest quite well<br> What we want!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_080.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>80.  Q<u>a</u>la law anna lee bikum quwwatan aw <u>a</u>wee il<u>a</u> ruknin shadeed<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>80.  He said: "Would that I<br> Had power to suppress you<br>Or that I could betake<br> Myself to some powerful support."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_081.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>81.  Q<u>a</u>loo y<u>a</u> loo<u>t</u>u inn<u>a</u> rusulu rabbika lan ya<u>s</u>iloo ilayka faasri bi-ahlika biqi<u>t</u>AAin mina allayli wal<u>a</u> yaltafit minkum a<u>h</u>adun ill<u>a</u> imraataka innahu mu<u>s</u>eebuh<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> a<u>sa</u>bahum inna mawAAidahumu a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>ub<u>h</u>u alaysa a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>ub<u>h</u>u biqareeb<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>81.  (The Messengers) said: "O Lūt<br> We are Messengers from thy Lord!<br> By no means shall they<br> Reach thee! Now travel<br> With thy family while yet<br> A part of the night remains,<br> And let not any of you<br> Look back: but thy wife<br> (Will remain behind):<br> To her will happen<br> What happens to the people.<br> Morning is their time appointed:<br> Is not the morning nigh?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_082.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>82.  Falamm<u>a</u> j<u>a</u>a amrun<u>a</u> jaAAaln<u>a</u> AA<u>a</u>liyah<u>a</u> s<u>a</u>filah<u>a</u> waam<u>t</u>arn<u>a</u> AAalayh<u>a</u> <u>h</u>ij<u>a</u>ratan min sijjeelin man<u>d</u>ood<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>82.  When Our decree issued,<br> We turned (the cities)<br> Upside down, and rained down<br> On them brimstones<br> Hard as baked clay,<br> Spread, layer on layer,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_083.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>83.  Musawwamatan AAinda rabbika wam<u>a</u> hiya mina a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limeena bibaAAeed<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>83.  Marked as from thy Lord:<br>Nor are they ever far<br> From those who do wrong!</p> <div class="filenav">
