 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XVI.: Naḥl or The Bee.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 11</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_077.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>77.  Walill<u>a</u>hi ghaybu a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i wam<u>a</u> amru a<b>l</b>ss<u>a</u>AAati ill<u>a</u> kalam<u>h</u>i alba<u>s</u>ari aw huwa aqrabu inna All<u>a</u>ha AAal<u>a</u> kulli shay-in qadeer<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>77.  To God belongeth the Mystery<br> Of the heavens and the earth.<br> And the Decision of the Hour<br> (Of Judgment) is as<br> The twinkling of an eye,<br> Or even quicker:<br> For God hath power<br> Over all things.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_078.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>78.  Wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu akhrajakum min bu<u>t</u>ooni ommah<u>a</u>tikum l<u>a</u> taAAlamoona shay-an wajaAAala lakumu a<b>l</b>ssamAAa wa<b>a</b>l-ab<u>sa</u>ra wa<b>a</b>l-af-idata laAAallakum tashkuroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>78.  It is He Who brought you<br> Forth from the wombs<br> Of your mothers when<br> Ye knew nothing; and He<br> Gave you hearing and sight<br> And intelligence and affections:<br> That ye may give thanks<br> (To God).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_079.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>79.  Alam yaraw il<u>a</u> a<b>l</b><u>tt</u>ayri musakhkhar<u>a</u>tin fee jawwi a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>-i m<u>a</u> yumsikuhunna ill<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hu inna fee <u>tha</u>lika la<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin liqawmin yu/minoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>79.  Do they not look at<br> The birds, held poised<br> In the midst of (the air<br> And) the sky? Nothing<br> Holds them up but (the power<br> Of) God. Verily in this<br> Are Signs for those who believe.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_080.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>80.  Wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu jaAAala lakum min buyootikum sakanan wajaAAala lakum min juloodi al-anAA<u>a</u>mi buyootan tastakhiffoonah<u>a</u> yawma <i><u>th</u></i>aAAnikum wayawma iq<u>a</u>matikum wamin a<u>s</u>w<u>a</u>fih<u>a</u> waawb<u>a</u>rih<u>a</u> waashAA<u>a</u>rih<u>a</u> ath<u>a</u>than wamat<u>a</u>AAan il<u>a</u> <u>h</u>een<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>80.  It is God Who made your habitations<br> Homes of rest and quiet<br> For you; and made for you,<br> Out of the skins of animals,<br> (Tents for) dwellings, which<br> Ye find so light (and handy)<br> When ye travel and when<br> Ye stop (in your travels);<br> And out of their wool,<br> And their soft fibres<br> (Between wool and hair),<br> And their hair, rich stuff<br> And articles of convenience<br> (To serve you) for a time.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_081.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>81.  Wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu jaAAala lakum mimm<u>a</u> khalaqa <i><u>th</u></i>il<u>a</u>lan wajaAAala lakum mina aljib<u>a</u>li akn<u>a</u>nan wajaAAala lakum sar<u>a</u>beela taqeekumu al<u>h</u>arra wasar<u>a</u>beela taqeekum ba/sakum ka<u>tha</u>lika yutimmu niAAmatahu AAalaykum laAAallakum tuslimoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>81.  It is God Who made.<br> Out of the things He created,<br> Some things to give you shade;<br> Of the hills He made some<br> For your shelter; He made you<br> Garments to protect you<br> From heat, and coats of mail<br> To protect you from<br> Your (mutual) violence.<br> Thus does He complete<br> His favours on you, that<br> Ye may bow to His Will<br> (In Islām).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_082.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>82.  Fa-in tawallaw fa-innam<u>a</u> AAalayka albal<u>a</u>ghu almubeen<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>82.  But if they turn away,<br> Thy duty is only to preach<br> The Clear Message.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_083.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>83.  YaAArifoona niAAmata All<u>a</u>hi thumma yunkiroonah<u>a</u> waaktharuhumu alk<u>a</u>firoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>83.  They recognise the favours<br> Of God; then they deny them;<br> And most of them<br> Are (creatures) ungrateful.</p> <div class="filenav">
