 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXVII.: Naml, or the Ants.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 7</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_083.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>83.  Wayawma na<u>h</u>shuru min kulli ommatin fawjan mimman yuka<u>thth</u>ibu bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin<u>a</u> fahum yoozaAAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>83.  One Day We shall gather<br> Together from every people<br> A troop of those who reject<br> Our Signs, and they shall<br> Be kept in ranks,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_084.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>84.  <u>H</u>att<u>a</u> i<u>tha</u> j<u>a</u>oo q<u>a</u>la aka<u>thth</u>abtum bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tee walam tu<u>h</u>ee<u>t</u>oo bih<u>a</u> AAilman amm<u>atha</u> kuntum taAAmaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>84.  Until, when they come<br> (Before the Judgment-seat),<br> (God) will say: "Did ye<br> Reject My Signs, though ye<br>Comprehended them not<br> In knowledge, or what<br> Was it ye did?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_085.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>85.  WawaqaAAa alqawlu AAalayhim bim<u>a</u> <i><u>th</u></i>alamoo fahum l<u>a</u> yan<u>t</u>iqoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>85.  And the Word will be<br> Fulfilled against them, because<br> Of their wrong-doing, and they<br> Will be unable to speak<br> (In plea).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_086.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>86.  Alam yaraw ann<u>a</u> jaAAaln<u>a</u> allayla liyaskunoo feehi wa<b>al</b>nnah<u>a</u>ra mub<u>s</u>iran inna fee <u>tha</u>lika la<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin liqawmin yu/minoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>86.  See they not that We<br> Have made the Night<br> For them to rest in<br> And the Day to give<br> Them light? Verily in this<br> Are Signs for any people<br> That believe!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_087.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>87.  Wayawma yunfakhu fee a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>oori fafaziAAa man fee a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti waman fee al-ar<u>d</u>i ill<u>a</u> man sh<u>a</u>a All<u>a</u>hu wakullun atawhu d<u>a</u>khireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>87.  And the Day that the Trumpet<br> Will be sounded—then will be<br> Smitten with terror those<br> Who are in the heavens,<br> And those who are on earth,<br> Except such as God will please<br> (To exempt): and all shall come<br> To His (Presence) as beings<br> Conscious of their lowliness.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_088.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>88.  Watar<u>a</u> aljib<u>a</u>la ta<u>h</u>sabuh<u>a</u> j<u>a</u>midatan wahiya tamurru marra a<b>l</b>ssa<u>ha</u>bi <u>s</u>unAAa All<u>a</u>hi alla<u>th</u>ee atqana kulla shay-in innahu khabeerun bim<u>a</u> tafAAaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>88.  Thou seest the mountains<br> And thinkest them firmly fixed:<br> But they shall pass away<br> As the clouds pass away:<br>(Such is) the artistry of God,<br> Who disposes of all things<br> In perfect order: for He is<br> Well acquainted with all that ye do.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_089.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>89.  Man j<u>a</u>a bi<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>asanati falahu khayrun minh<u>a</u> wahum min fazaAAin yawma-i<u>th</u>in <u>a</u>minoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>89.  If any do good, good will<br> (Accrue) to them therefrom;<br> And they will be secure<br> From terror that Day.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_090.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>90.  Waman j<u>a</u>a bi<b>al</b>ssayyi-ati fakubbat wujoohuhum fee a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>ri hal tujzawna ill<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> kuntum taAAmaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>90.  And if any do evil,<br> Their faces will be thrown<br> Headlong into the Fire:<br> "Do ye receive a reward<br> Other than that which ye<br> Have earned by your deeds?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_091.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>91.  Innam<u>a</u> omirtu an aAAbuda rabba h<u>ath</u>ihi albaldati alla<u>th</u>ee <u>h</u>arramah<u>a</u> walahu kullu shay-in waomirtu an akoona mina almuslimeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>91.  For me, I have been<br> Commanded to serve the Lord<br> Of this City, Him Who has<br> Sanctified it and to Whom<br> (Belong) all things:<br> And I am commanded<br> To be of those who bow<br> In Islam to God's Will,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_092.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>92.  Waan atluwa alqur-<u>a</u>na famani ihtad<u>a</u> fa-innam<u>a</u> yahtadee linafsihi waman <u>d</u>alla faqul innam<u>a</u> an<u>a</u> mina almun<u>th</u>ireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>92.  And to rehearse the Qur-ān:<br> And if any accept guidance,<br>They do it for the good<br> Of their own souls,<br> And if any stray, say:<br> "I am only a Warner".</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_093.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>93.  Waquli al<u>h</u>amdu lill<u>a</u>hi sayureekum <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tihi fataAArifoonah<u>a</u> wam<u>a</u> rabbuka bigh<u>a</u>filin AAamm<u>a</u> taAAmaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>93.  And say: "Praise be to God,<br> Who will soon show you<br> His Signs, so that ye<br> Shall know them"; and thy Lord<br> Is not unmindful<br> Of all that ye do.</p>  <div class="filenav">
