 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra II.: Baqara, or the Heifer.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 19</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_153.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>153.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo istaAAeenoo bi<b>al</b><u>ss</u>abri wa<b>al</b><u>ss</u>al<u>a</u>ti inna All<u>a</u>ha maAAa a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>bireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>153.  O ye who believe! seek help<br> With patient Perseverance<br> And Prayer: for God is with those<br> Who patiently persevere.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_154.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>154.  Wal<u>a</u> taqooloo liman yuqtalu fee sabeeli All<u>a</u>hi amw<u>a</u>tun bal a<u>h</u>y<u>a</u>on wal<u>a</u>kin l<u>a</u> tashAAuroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>154.  And say not of those<br> Who are slain in the way<br>Of God: "They are dead."<br> Nay, they are living,<br> Though ye perceive (it) not.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_155.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>155.  Walanabluwannakum bishay-in mina alkhawfi wa<b>a</b>ljooAAi wanaq<u>s</u>in mina al-amw<u>a</u>li wa<b>a</b>l-anfusi wa<b>al</b>ththamar<u>a</u>ti wabashshiri a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>bireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>155.  Be sure we shall test you<br> With something of fear<br> And hunger, some loss<br> In goods or lives or the fruits<br> (Of your toil), but give<br> Glad tidings to those<br> Who patiently persevere,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_156.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>156.  Alla<u>th</u>eena i<u>tha</u> a<u>sa</u>bat-hum mu<u>s</u>eebatun q<u>a</u>loo inn<u>a</u> lill<u>a</u>hi wa-inn<u>a</u> ilayhi r<u>a</u>jiAAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>156.  Who say, when afflicted<br> With calamity: "To God<br> We belong, and to Him<br> Is our return":—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_157.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>157.  Ol<u>a</u>-ika AAalayhim <u>s</u>alaw<u>a</u>tun min rabbihim wara<u>h</u>matun waol<u>a</u>-ika humu almuhtadoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>157.  They are those on whom<br> (Descend) blessings from God,<br> And Mercy,<br> And they are the ones<br> That receive guidance.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_158.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>158.  Inna a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>af<u>a</u> wa<b>a</b>lmarwata min shaAA<u>a</u>-iri All<u>a</u>hi faman <u>h</u>ajja albayta awi iAAtamara fal<u>a</u> jun<u>ah</u>a AAalayhi an ya<u>tt</u>awwafa bihim<u>a</u> waman ta<u>t</u>awwaAAa khayran fa-inna All<u>a</u>ha sh<u>a</u>kirun AAaleem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>158.  Behold! Ṣafa and Marwa<br> Are among the Symbols<br> Of God. So if those who visit<br> The House in the Season<br> Or at other times,<br> Should compass them round,<br> It is no sin in them.<br> And if any one obeyeth his own<br>Impulse to Good,—<br> Be sure that God<br> Is He Who recogniseth<br> And knoweth.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_159.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>159.  Inna alla<u>th</u>eena yaktumoona m<u>a</u> anzaln<u>a</u> mina albayyin<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>lhud<u>a</u> min baAAdi m<u>a</u> bayyann<u>a</u>hu li<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si fee alkit<u>a</u>bi ol<u>a</u>-ika yalAAanuhumu All<u>a</u>hu wayalAAanuhumu all<u>a</u>AAinoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>159.  Those who conceal<br> The clear (Signs) We have<br> Sent down, and the Guidance,<br> After We have made it<br> Clear for the People<br> In the Book,—on them<br> Shall be God's curse,<br> And the curse of those<br> Entitled to curse,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_160.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>160.  Ill<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena t<u>a</u>boo waa<u>s</u>la<u>h</u>oo wabayyanoo faol<u>a</u>-ika atoobu AAalayhim waan<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>ttaww<u>a</u>bu a<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>eem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>160.  Except those who repent<br> And make amends<br> And openly declare (the Truth):<br> To them I turn;<br> For I am Oft-returning,<br> Most Merciful.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_161.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>161.  Inna alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo wam<u>a</u>too wahum kuff<u>a</u>run ol<u>a</u>-ika AAalayhim laAAnatu All<u>a</u>hi wa<b>a</b>lmal<u>a</u>-ikati wa<b>al</b>nn<u>a</u>si ajmaAAeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>161.  Those who reject Faith,<br> And die rejecting,—<br> On them is God's curse,<br> And the curse of angels,<br> And of all mankind;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_162.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>162.  Kh<u>a</u>lideena feeh<u>a</u> l<u>a</u> yukhaffafu AAanhumu alAAa<u>tha</u>bu wal<u>a</u> hum yun<i><u>th</u></i>aroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>162.  They will abide therein:<br> Their penalty will not<br> Be lightened, nor will<br> Respite be their (lot).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_163.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>163.  Wa-il<u>a</u>hukum il<u>a</u>hun w<u>ah</u>idun l<u>a</u> il<u>a</u>ha ill<u>a</u> huwa a<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>m<u>a</u>nu a<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>eem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>163.  And your God<br> Is One God:<br> There is no god<br> But He,<br>Most Gracious,<br> Most Merciful.</p> <div class="filenav">
