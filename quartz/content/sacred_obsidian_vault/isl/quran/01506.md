 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XV.: Al-Hijr, or The Rocky Tract.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 6</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_080.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>80.  Walaqad ka<u>thth</u>aba a<u>s</u>-<u>ha</u>bu al<u>h</u>ijri almursaleen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>80.  The Companions of the Rocky Tract<br> Also rejected the apostles:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_081.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>81.  Wa<u>a</u>tayn<u>a</u>hum <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin<u>a</u> fak<u>a</u>noo AAanh<u>a</u> muAAri<u>d</u>een<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>81.  We sent them Our Signs,<br> But they persisted<br> In turning away from them.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_082.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>82.  Wak<u>a</u>noo yan<u>h</u>itoona mina aljib<u>a</u>li buyootan <u>a</u>mineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>82.  Out of the mountains<br> Did they hew (their) edifices,<br> (Feeling themselves) secure.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_083.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>83.  Faakha<u>th</u>at-humu a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>ay<u>h</u>atu mu<u>s</u>bi<u>h</u>een<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>83.  But the (mighty) Blast<br> Seized them of a morning,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_084.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>84.  Fam<u>a</u> aghn<u>a</u> AAanhum m<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo yaksiboon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>84.  And of no avail to them<br> Was all that they did<br> (With such art and care)!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_085.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>85.  Wam<u>a</u> khalaqn<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>a wam<u>a</u> baynahum<u>a</u> ill<u>a</u> bi<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>aqqi wa-inna a<b>l</b>ss<u>a</u>AAata la<u>a</u>tiyatun fa<b>i</b><u>s</u>fa<u>h</u>i a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>af<u>h</u>a aljameel<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>85.  We created not the heavens,<br> The earth, and all between them,<br>But for just ends.<br> And the Hour is surely<br> Coming (when this will be manifest).<br> So overlook (any human faults)<br> With gracious forgiveness.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_086.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>86.  Inna rabbaka huwa alkhall<u>a</u>qu alAAaleem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>86.  For verily it is thy Lord<br> Who is the Master-Creator,<br> Knowing all things.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_087.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>87.  Walaqad <u>a</u>tayn<u>a</u>ka sabAAan mina almath<u>a</u>nee wa<b>a</b>lqur-<u>a</u>na alAAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>87.  And We have bestowed<br> Upon thee the Seven<br> Oft-repeated (Verses)<br> And the Grand Qur-ān.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_088.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>88.  L<u>a</u> tamuddanna AAaynayka il<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> mattaAAn<u>a</u> bihi azw<u>a</u>jan minhum wal<u>a</u> ta<u>h</u>zan AAalayhim wa<b>i</b>khfi<u>d</u> jan<u>ah</u>aka lilmu/mineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>88.  Strain not thine eyes.<br> (Wistfully) at what We<br> Have bestowed on certain classes<br> Of them, nor grieve over them:<br>But lower thy wing<br> (in gentleness)<br> To the Believers.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_089.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>89.  Waqul innee an<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nna<u>th</u>eeru almubeen<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>89.  And say: "I am indeed he<br> That warneth openly<br> And without ambiguity,"—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_090.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>90.  Kam<u>a</u> anzaln<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> almuqtasimeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>90.  (Of just such wrath)<br> As We sent down<br> On those who divided<br> (Scripture into arbitrary parts),—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_091.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>91.  Alla<u>th</u>eena jaAAaloo alqur-<u>a</u>na AAi<u>d</u>een<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>91.  (So also on such)<br> As have made Qur-ān<br> Into shreds (as they please).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_092.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>92.  Fawarabbika lanas-alannahum ajmaAAeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>92.  Therefore, by the Lord,<br> We will, of a surety,<br> Call them to account,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_093.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>93.  AAamm<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo yaAAmaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>93.  For all their deeds.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_094.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>94.  Fa<b>i</b><u>s</u>daAA bim<u>a</u> tu/maru waaAAri<u>d</u> AAani almushrikeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>94.  Therefore expound openly<br> What thou art commanded,<br> And turn away from those<br> Who join false gods with God.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_095.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>95.  Inn<u>a</u> kafayn<u>a</u>ka almustahzi-een<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>95.  For sufficient are We<br> Unto thee against those<br>Who scoff,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_096.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>96.  Alla<u>th</u>eena yajAAaloona maAAa All<u>a</u>hi il<u>a</u>han <u>a</u>khara fasawfa yaAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>96.  Those who adopt, with God,<br> Another god: but soon<br> Will they come to know.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_097.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>97.  Walaqad naAAlamu annaka ya<u>d</u>eequ <u>s</u>adruka bim<u>a</u> yaqooloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>97.  We do indeed know<br> How thy heart is distressed<br> At what they say.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_098.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>98.  Fasabbi<u>h</u> bi<u>h</u>amdi rabbika wakun mina a<b>l</b>ss<u>a</u>jideen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>98.  But celebrate the praises<br> Of thy Lord, and be of those<br> Who prostrate themselves<br> In adoration.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_099.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>99.  Wa<b>o</b>AAbud rabbaka <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> ya/tiyaka alyaqeen<b>u</b>      </p>
 <p>99.  And serve thy Lord<br> Until there come unto thee<br> The Hour that is Certain.</p>  <div class="filenav">
