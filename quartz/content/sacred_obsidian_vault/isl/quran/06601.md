 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra LXVI.: Taḥrīm, or Holding (something) to be Forbidden.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/066/066_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nnabiyyu lima tu<u>h</u>arrimu m<u>a</u> a<u>h</u>alla All<u>a</u>hu laka tabtaghee mar<u>da</u>ta azw<u>a</u>jika wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu ghafoorun ra<u>h</u>eem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>1.  O Prophet! Why<br> Holdest thou to be forbidden<br> That which God has<br> Made lawful to thee?<br> Thou seekest to please<br> Thy consorts. But God<br> Is Oft-Forgiving, Most Merciful.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/066/066_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  Qad fara<u>d</u>a All<u>a</u>hu lakum ta<u>h</u>illata aym<u>a</u>nikum wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu mawl<u>a</u>kum wahuwa alAAaleemu al<u>h</u>akeem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>2.  God has already ordained<br> For you, (O men),<br> The dissolution of your oaths<br> (In some cases): and God<br> Is your Protector, and He<br> Is Full of Knowledge<br> And Wisdom.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/066/066_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  Wa-i<u>th</u> asarra a<b>l</b>nnabiyyu il<u>a</u> baAA<u>d</u>i azw<u>a</u>jihi <u>h</u>adeethan falamm<u>a</u> nabbaat bihi waa<i><u>th</u></i>harahu All<u>a</u>hu AAalayhi AAarrafa baAA<u>d</u>ahu waaAAra<u>d</u>a AAan baAA<u>d</u>in falamm<u>a</u> nabbaah<u>a</u> bihi q<u>a</u>lat man anbaaka h<u>atha</u> q<u>a</u>la nabbaaniya alAAaleemu alkhabeer<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>3.  When the Prophet disclosed<br> A matter in confidence<br>To one of his consorts,<br> And she then divulged it<br> (To another), and God made it<br> Known to him, he confirmed<br> Part thereof and repudiated<br> A part. Then when he<br> Told her thereof, she said,<br> "Who told thee this?"<br> He said, "He told me<br> Who knows and is well-acquainted<br> (With all things)"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/066/066_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  In tatoob<u>a</u> il<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi faqad <u>s</u>aghat quloobukum<u>a</u> wa-in ta<i><u>th</u></i><u>a</u>har<u>a</u> AAalayhi fa-inna All<u>a</u>ha huwa mawl<u>a</u>hu wajibreelu wa<u>sa</u>li<u>h</u>u almu/mineena wa<b>a</b>lmal<u>a</u>-ikatu baAAda <u>tha</u>lika <i><u>th</u></i>aheer<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>4.  If ye two turn in repentance<br> To Him, your hearts<br> Are indeed so inclined;<br> But if ye back up<br> Each other against him,<br> Truly God is his Protector,<br> And Gabriel, and (every)<br> Righteous one among those<br> Who believe,—and furthermore,<br> The angels—will back (him) up.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/066/066_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  AAas<u>a</u> rabbuhu in <u>t</u>allaqakunna an yubdilahu azw<u>a</u>jan khayran minkunna muslim<u>a</u>tin mu/min<u>a</u>tin q<u>a</u>nit<u>a</u>tin t<u>a</u>-ib<u>a</u>tin AA<u>a</u>bid<u>a</u>tin s<u>a</u>-i<u>ha</u>tin thayyib<u>a</u>tin waabk<u>a</u>r<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>5.  It may be, if he<br> Divorced you (all)<br>That God will give him<br> In exchange Consorts<br> Better than you,<br> Who submit (their wills),<br> Who believe, who are devout,<br> Who turn to God in repentance,<br> Who worship (in humility),<br> Who travel (for Faith) and fast,—<br> Previously married or virgins.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/066/066_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo qoo anfusakum waahleekum n<u>a</u>ran waqooduh<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>su wa<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>ij<u>a</u>ratu AAalayh<u>a</u> mal<u>a</u>-ikatun ghil<u>a</u><i><u>th</u></i>un shid<u>a</u>dun l<u>a</u> yaAA<u>s</u>oona All<u>a</u>ha m<u>a</u> amarahum wayafAAaloona m<u>a</u> yu/maroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>6.  O ye who believe!<br> Save yourselves and your<br> Families from a Fire<br> Whose fuel is Men<br> And Stones, over which<br> Are (appointed) angels<br> Stern (and) severe,<br> Who flinch not (from<br> Executing) the Commands<br> They receive from God,<br> But do (precisely) what<br> They are commanded.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/066/066_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo l<u>a</u> taAAta<u>th</u>iroo alyawma innam<u>a</u> tujzawna m<u>a</u> kuntum taAAmaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>7.  (They will say),<br> "O ye Unbelievers!<br> Make no excuses<br> This Day! Ye are being<br>But requited for<br> All that ye did!"</p> <div class="filenav">
