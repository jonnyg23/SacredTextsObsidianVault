 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra L.: Qāf.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 2</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/050/050_016.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>16.  Walaqad khalaqn<u>a</u> al-ins<u>a</u>na wanaAAlamu m<u>a</u> tuwaswisu bihi nafsuhu wana<u>h</u>nu aqrabu ilayhi min <u>h</u>abli alwareed<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>16.  It was We Who<br> Created man, and We know<br> What dark suggestions his soul<br> Makes to him: for We<br> Are nearer to him<br> Than (his) jugular vein.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/050/050_017.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>17.  I<u>th</u> yatalaqq<u>a</u> almutalaqqiy<u>a</u>ni AAani alyameeni waAAani a<b>l</b>shshim<u>a</u>li qaAAeed<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>17.  Behold, two (guardian angels)<br> Appointed to learn (his doings)<br> Learn (and note them),<br> One sitting on the right<br> And one on the left.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/050/050_018.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>18.  M<u>a</u> yalfi<i><u>th</u></i>u min qawlin ill<u>a</u> ladayhi raqeebun AAateed<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>18.  Not a word does he<br> Utter but there is<br> A sentinel by him,<br> Ready (to note it).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/050/050_019.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>19.  Waj<u>a</u>at sakratu almawti bi<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>aqqi <u>tha</u>lika m<u>a</u> kunta minhu ta<u>h</u>eed<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>19.  And the stupor of death<br> Will bring truth (before<br> His eyes): "This was<br> The thing which thou<br> Wast trying to escape!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/050/050_020.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>20.  Wanufikha fee a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>oori <u>tha</u>lika yawmu alwaAAeed<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>20.  And the Trumpet<br> Shall be blown:<br> That will be the Day<br> Whereof Warning (had been given).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/050/050_021.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>21.  Waj<u>a</u>at kullu nafsin maAAah<u>a</u> s<u>a</u>-iqun washaheed<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>21.  And there will come forth<br> Every soul: with each<br> Will be an (angel) to drive,<br> And an (angel) to<br> Bear witness.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/050/050_022.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>22.  Laqad kunta fee ghaflatin min h<u>atha</u> fakashafn<u>a</u> AAanka ghi<u>ta</u>aka faba<u>s</u>aruka alyawma <u>h</u>adeed<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>22.  (It will be said:)<br> "Thou wast heedless<br> Of this; now have We<br> Removed thy veil,<br>And sharp is thy sight<br> This Day!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/050/050_023.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>23.  Waq<u>a</u>la qareenuhu h<u>atha</u> m<u>a</u> ladayya AAateed<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>23.  And his Companion will say:<br> "Here is (his Record) ready<br> With me!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/050/050_024.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>24.  Alqiy<u>a</u> fee jahannama kulla kaff<u>a</u>rin AAaneed<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>24.  (The sentence will be:)<br> "Throw, throw into Hell<br> Every contumacious Rejecter<br> (Of God)!—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/050/050_025.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>25.  Mann<u>a</u>AAin lilkhayri muAAtadin mureeb<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>25.  "Who forbade what was good,<br> Transgressed all bounds,<br> Cast doubts and suspicions;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/050/050_026.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>26.  Alla<u>th</u>ee jaAAala maAAa All<u>a</u>hi il<u>a</u>han <u>a</u>khara faalqiy<u>a</u>hu fee alAAa<u>tha</u>bi a<b>l</b>shshadeed<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>26.  "Who set up another god<br> Beside God: throw him<br> Into a severe Penalty."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/050/050_027.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>27.  Q<u>a</u>la qareenuhu rabban<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> a<u>t</u>ghaytuhu wal<u>a</u>kin k<u>a</u>na fee <u>d</u>al<u>a</u>lin baAAeed<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>27.  His Companion will say:<br> "Our Lord! I did not<br> Make him transgress,<br> But he was (himself)<br> Far astray."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/050/050_028.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>28.  Q<u>a</u>la l<u>a</u> takhta<u>s</u>imoo ladayya waqad qaddamtu ilaykum bi<b>a</b>lwaAAeed<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>28.  He will say: "Dispute not<br> With each other<br> In My Presence:<br> I had already in advance<br> Sent you Warning.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/050/050_029.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>29.  M<u>a</u> yubaddalu alqawlu ladayya wam<u>a</u> an<u>a</u> bi<i><u>th</u></i>all<u>a</u>min lilAAabeed<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>29.  "The Word changes not<br> Before Me, and I do not<br> The least injustice<br> To My Servants."</p> <div class="filenav">
