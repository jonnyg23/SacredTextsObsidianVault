 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra IX.: Tauba (Repentance) or Barāat (Immunity).</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 10</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_073.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>73.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nnabiyyu j<u>a</u>hidi alkuff<u>a</u>ra wa<b>a</b>lmun<u>a</u>fiqeena wa<b>o</b>ghlu<i><u>th</u></i> AAalayhim wama/w<u>a</u>hum jahannamu wabi/sa alma<u>s</u>eer<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>73.  O Prophet! strive hard against<br> The Unbelievers and the Hypocrites,<br> And be firm against them.<br> Their abode is Hell,—<br> An evil refuge indeed.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_074.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>74.  Ya<u>h</u>lifoona bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi m<u>a</u> q<u>a</u>loo walaqad q<u>a</u>loo kalimata alkufri wakafaroo baAAda isl<u>a</u>mihim wahammoo bim<u>a</u> lam yan<u>a</u>loo wam<u>a</u> naqamoo ill<u>a</u> an aghn<u>a</u>humu All<u>a</u>hu warasooluhu min fa<u>d</u>lihi fa-in yatooboo yaku khayran lahum wa-in yatawallaw yuAAa<u>thth</u>ibhumu All<u>a</u>hu AAa<u>tha</u>ban aleeman fee a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> wa<b>a</b>l-<u>a</u>khirati wam<u>a</u> lahum fee al-ar<u>d</u>i min waliyyin wal<u>a</u> na<u>s</u>eer<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>74.  They swear by God that they<br> Said nothing (evil), but indeed<br> They uttered blasphemy,<br> And they did it after accepting<br> Islam; and they meditated<br> A plot which they were unable<br> To carry out: this revenge<br> Of theirs was (their) only return<br> For the bounty with which<br> God and His Apostle had enriched<br> Them! If they repent,<br> It will be best for them;<br> But if they turn back<br> (To their evil ways),<br> God will punish them<br> With a grievous penalty<br> In this life and in the Hereafter:<br> They shall have none on earth<br> To protect or help them.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_075.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>75.  Waminhum man AA<u>a</u>hada All<u>a</u>ha la-in <u>a</u>t<u>a</u>n<u>a</u> min fa<u>d</u>lihi lana<u>ss</u>addaqanna walanakoonanna mina a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>li<u>h</u>een<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>75.  Amongst them are men<br> Who made a Covenant with God,<br> That if He bestowed on them<br> Of His bounty, they would give<br> (Largely) in charity, and be truly<br> Amongst those who are righteous.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_076.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>76.  Falamm<u>a</u> <u>a</u>t<u>a</u>hum min fa<u>d</u>lihi bakhiloo bihi watawallaw wahum muAAri<u>d</u>oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>76.  But when He did bestow<br> Of His bounty, they became<br> Covetous, and turned back<br> (From their Covenant), averse<br> (From its fulfilment).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_077.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>77.  FaaAAqabahum nif<u>a</u>qan fee quloobihim il<u>a</u> yawmi yalqawnahu bim<u>a</u> akhlafoo All<u>a</u>ha m<u>a</u> waAAadoohu wabim<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo yak<u>th</u>iboon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>77.  So He hath put as a consequence<br> Hypocrisy into their hearts,<br> (To last) till the Day whereon<br> They shall meet Him: because<br> They broke their Covenant<br> With God, and because they<br> Lied (again and again).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_078.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>78.  Alam yaAAlamoo anna All<u>a</u>ha yaAAlamu sirrahum wanajw<u>a</u>hum waanna All<u>a</u>ha AAall<u>a</u>mu alghuyoob<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>78.  Know they not that God<br> Doth know their secret (thoughts)<br> And their secret counsels,<br> And that God knoweth well<br> All things unseen?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_079.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>79.  Alla<u>th</u>eena yalmizoona almu<u>tt</u>awwiAAeena mina almu/mineena fee a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>adaq<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena l<u>a</u> yajidoona ill<u>a</u> juhdahum fayaskharoona minhum sakhira All<u>a</u>hu minhum walahum AAa<u>tha</u>bun aleem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>79.  Those who slander such<br> Of the Believers as give themselves<br> Freely to (deeds of) charity,<br> As well as such as can find<br> Nothing to give except<br> The fruits of their labour,—<br> And throw ridicule on them,—<br> God will throw back<br>Their ridicule on them:<br> And they shall have<br> A grievous penalty.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_080.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>80.  Istaghfir lahum aw l<u>a</u> tastaghfir lahum in tastaghfir lahum sabAAeena marratan falan yaghfira All<u>a</u>hu lahum <u>tha</u>lika bi-annahum kafaroo bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi warasoolihi wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu l<u>a</u> yahdee alqawma alf<u>a</u>siqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>80.  Whether thou ask<br> For their forgiveness,<br> Or not, (their sin is unforgivable):<br> If thou ask seventy times<br> For their forgiveness, God<br> Will not forgive them:<br> Because they have rejected<br> God and His Apostle: and God<br> Guideth not those<br> Who are perversely rebellious.</p> <div class="filenav">
