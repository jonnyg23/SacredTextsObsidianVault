 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra C.: ’Adiyāt, or Those that run.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/100/100_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  Wa<b>a</b>lAA<u>a</u>diy<u>a</u>ti <u>d</u>ab<u>ha</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>1.  By the (Steeds)<br> That run, with panting (breath),</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/100/100_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  Fa<b>a</b>lmooriy<u>a</u>ti qad<u>ha</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>2.  And strike sparks of fire,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/100/100_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  Fa<b>a</b>lmugheer<u>a</u>ti <u>s</u>ub<u>ha</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>3.  And push home the charge<br> In the morning,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/100/100_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  Faatharna bihi naqAA<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>4.  And raise the dust<br> In clouds the while,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/100/100_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  Fawasa<u>t</u>na bihi jamAA<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>5.  And penetrate forthwith<br> Into the midst (of the foe)<br> En masse;—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/100/100_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  Inna al-ins<u>a</u>na lirabbihi lakanood<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>6.  Truly Man is,<br> To his Lord,<br> Ungrateful;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/100/100_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  Wa-innahu AAal<u>a</u> <u>tha</u>lika lashaheed<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>7.  And to that (fact)<br> He bears witness<br> (By his deeds);</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/100/100_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  Wa-innahu li<u>h</u>ubbi alkhayri lashadeed<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>8.  And violent is he<br> In his love of wealth.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/100/100_009.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>9.  Afal<u>a</u> yaAAlamu i<u>tha</u> buAAthira m<u>a</u> fee alquboor<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>9.  Does he not know,—<br> When that which is<br> In the graves is<br> Scattered abroad</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/100/100_010.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>10.  Wa<u>h</u>u<u>ss</u>ila m<u>a</u> fee a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>udoor<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>10.  And that which is<br> (Locked up) in (human) breasts<br> Is made manifest—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/100/100_011.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>11.  Inna rabbahum bihim yawma-i<u>th</u>in lakhabeer<b>un</b>      </p>
 <p>11.  That their Lord had been<br> Well-acquainted with them,<br> (Even to) that Day?</p>  <div class="filenav">
