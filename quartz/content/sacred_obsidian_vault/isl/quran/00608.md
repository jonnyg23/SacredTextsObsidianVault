 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra VI.: An’ām, or Cattle.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 8</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_061.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>61.  Wahuwa alq<u>a</u>hiru fawqa AAib<u>a</u>dihi wayursilu AAalaykum <u>h</u>afa<i><u>th</u></i>atan <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> i<u>tha</u> j<u>a</u>a a<u>h</u>adakumu almawtu tawaffat-hu rusulun<u>a</u> wahum l<u>a</u> yufarri<u>t</u>oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>61.  He is the Irresistible, (watching)<br> From above over His worshippers,<br> And He sets guardians<br> Over you. At length,<br> When death approaches<br> One of you, Our angels<br> Take his soul, and they<br> Never fail in their duty.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_062.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>62.  Thumma ruddoo il<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi mawl<u>a</u>humu al<u>h</u>aqqi al<u>a</u> lahu al<u>h</u>ukmu wahuwa asraAAu al<u>ha</u>sibeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>62.  Then are men returned<br> Unto God, their Protector,<br> The (only) Reality:<br> Is not His the Command?<br> And He is the Swiftest<br> In taking account.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_063.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>63.  Qul man yunajjeekum min <i><u>th</u></i>ulum<u>a</u>ti albarri wa<b>a</b>lba<u>h</u>ri tadAAoonahu ta<u>d</u>arruAAan wakhufyatan la-in anj<u>a</u>n<u>a</u> min h<u>ath</u>ihi lanakoonanna mina a<b>l</b>shsh<u>a</u>kireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>63.  Say: "Who is it<br> That delivereth you<br> From the dark recesses<br> Of land and sea,<br> When ye call upon Him<br> In humility<br>And silent terror:<br> If He only delivers us<br> From these (dangers),<br> (We vow) we shall truly<br> Show our gratitude?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_064.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>64.  Quli All<u>a</u>hu yunajjeekum minh<u>a</u> wamin kulli karbin thumma antum tushrikoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>64.  Say: "It is God<br> That delivereth you<br> From these and all (other)<br> Distresses: and yet<br> Ye worship false gods!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_065.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>65.  Qul huwa alq<u>a</u>diru AAal<u>a</u> an yabAAatha AAalaykum AAa<u>tha</u>ban min fawqikum aw min ta<u>h</u>ti arjulikum aw yalbisakum shiyaAAan wayu<u>th</u>eeqa baAA<u>d</u>akum ba/sa baAA<u>d</u>in on<i><u>th</u></i>ur kayfa nu<u>s</u>arrifu al-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>ti laAAallahum yafqahoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>65.  Say: "He hath power<br> To send calamities<br> On you, from above<br> And below, or to cover<br> You with confusion<br> In party strife,<br> Giving you a taste<br> Of mutual vengeance—<br> Each from the other."<br> See how We explain<br> The Signs by various (symbols);<br> That they may understand.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_066.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>66.  Waka<u>thth</u>aba bihi qawmuka wahuwa al<u>h</u>aqqu qul lastu AAalaykum biwakeel<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>66.  But thy people reject<br> This, though it is<br> The Truth. Say: "Not mine<br> Is the responsibility<br> For arranging your affairs;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_067.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>67.  Likulli naba-in mustaqarrun wasawfa taAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>67.  For every Message<br> Is a limit of time,<br> And soon shall ye<br> Know it."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_068.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>68.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> raayta alla<u>th</u>eena yakhoo<u>d</u>oona fee <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin<u>a</u> faaAAri<u>d</u> AAanhum <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> yakhoo<u>d</u>oo fee <u>h</u>adeethin ghayrihi wa-imm<u>a</u> yunsiyannaka a<b>l</b>shshay<u>ta</u>nu fal<u>a</u> taqAAud baAAda a<b>l</b><u>thth</u>ikr<u>a</u> maAAa alqawmi a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>68.  When thou seest men<br> Engaged in vain discourse<br> About Our Signs, turn<br> Away from them unless<br> They turn to a different<br> Theme. If Satan ever<br> Makes thee forget, then<br> After recollection, sit not<br> Thou in the company<br> Of those who do wrong.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_069.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>69.  Wam<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena yattaqoona min <u>h</u>is<u>a</u>bihim min shay-in wal<u>a</u>kin <u>th</u>ikr<u>a</u> laAAallahum yattaqoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>69.  On their account<br> No responsibility<br> Falls on the righteous,<br> But (their duty)<br> Is to remind them,<br> That they may (learn<br> To) fear God.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_070.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>70.  Wa<u>th</u>ari alla<u>th</u>eena ittakha<u>th</u>oo deenahum laAAiban walahwan wagharrat-humu al<u>h</u>ay<u>a</u>tu a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> wa<u>th</u>akkir bihi an tubsala nafsun bim<u>a</u> kasabat laysa lah<u>a</u> min dooni All<u>a</u>hi waliyyun wal<u>a</u> shafeeAAun wa-in taAAdil kulla AAadlin l<u>a</u> yu/kha<u>th</u> minh<u>a</u> ol<u>a</u>-ika alla<u>th</u>eena obsiloo bim<u>a</u> kasaboo lahum shar<u>a</u>bun min <u>h</u>ameemin waAAa<u>tha</u>bun aleemun bim<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo yakfuroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>70.  Leave alone those<br> Who take their religion<br> To be mere play<br> And amusement,<br> And are deceived<br> By the life of this world.<br> But proclaim (to them)<br> This (truth): that every soul<br> Delivers itself to ruin<br> By its own acts:<br> It will find for itself<br> No protector or intercessor<br> Except God: if it offered<br> Every ransom, (or<br> Reparation), none<br> Will be accepted: such is<br> (The end of) those who<br> Deliver themselves to ruin<br> By their own acts:<br> They will have for drink<br> (Only) boiling water,<br>And for punishment,<br> One most grievous:<br> For they persisted<br> In rejecting God.</p> <div class="filenav">
