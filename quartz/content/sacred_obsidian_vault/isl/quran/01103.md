 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XI.: Hūd (The Prophet Hūd).</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 3</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_025.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>25.  Walaqad arsaln<u>a</u> noo<u>h</u>an il<u>a</u> qawmihi innee lakum na<u>th</u>eerun mubeen<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>25.  We sent Noah to his People<br> (With a mission): "I have come<br> To you with a Clear Warning:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_026.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>26.  An l<u>a</u> taAAbudoo ill<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>ha innee akh<u>a</u>fu AAalaykum AAa<u>tha</u>ba yawmin aleem<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>26.  "That ye serve none but God:<br> Verily I do fear for you<br>The Penalty of a Grievous Day."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_027.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>27.  Faq<u>a</u>la almalao alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo min qawmihi m<u>a</u> nar<u>a</u>ka ill<u>a</u> basharan mithlan<u>a</u> wam<u>a</u> nar<u>a</u>ka ittabaAAaka ill<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena hum ar<u>ath</u>ilun<u>a</u> b<u>a</u>diya a<b>l</b>rra/yi wam<u>a</u> nar<u>a</u> lakum AAalayn<u>a</u> min fa<u>d</u>lin bal na<i><u>th</u></i>unnukum k<u>ath</u>ibeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>27.  But the Chiefs of the Unbelievers<br> Among his People said:<br> "We see (in) thee nothing<br> But a man like ourselves:<br> Nor do we see that any<br> Follow thee but the meanest<br> Among us, in judgment immature:<br> Nor do we see in you (all)<br> Any merit above us:<br> In fact we think ye are liars!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_028.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>28.  Q<u>a</u>la y<u>a</u> qawmi araaytum in kuntu AAal<u>a</u> bayyinatin min rabbee wa<u>a</u>t<u>a</u>nee ra<u>h</u>matan min AAindihi faAAummiyat AAalaykum anulzimukumooh<u>a</u> waantum lah<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>rihoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>28.  He said: "O my People!<br> See ye if (it be that)<br> I have a Clear Sign<br> From my Lord, and that He<br> Hath sent Mercy unto me<br> From His own Presence, but<br> That the Mercy hath been<br> Obscured from your sight?<br> Shall we compel you<br> To accept it when ye<br> Are averse to it?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_029.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>29.  Way<u>a</u> qawmi l<u>a</u> as-alukum AAalayhi m<u>a</u>lan in ajriya ill<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi wam<u>a</u> an<u>a</u> bi<u>ta</u>ridi alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo innahum mul<u>a</u>qoo rabbihim wal<u>a</u>kinnee ar<u>a</u>kum qawman tajhaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>29.  "And O my People!<br> I ask you for no wealth<br>In return: my reward<br> Is from none but God:<br> But I will not drive away<br> (In contempt) those who believe:<br> For verily they are<br> To meet their Lord, and ye<br> I see are the ignorant ones!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_030.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>30.  Way<u>a</u> qawmi man yan<u>s</u>urunee mina All<u>a</u>hi in <u>t</u>aradtuhum afal<u>a</u> ta<u>th</u>akkaroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>30.  "And O my People!<br> Who would help me against God<br> If I drove them away?<br> Will ye not then take heed?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_031.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>31.  Wal<u>a</u> aqoolu lakum AAindee khaz<u>a</u>-inu All<u>a</u>hi wal<u>a</u> aAAlamu alghayba wal<u>a</u> aqoolu innee malakun wal<u>a</u> aqoolu lilla<u>th</u>eena tazdaree aAAyunukum lan yu/tiyahumu All<u>a</u>hu khayran All<u>a</u>hu aAAlamu bim<u>a</u> fee anfusihim innee i<u>th</u>an lamina a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>31.  "I tell you not that<br> With me are the Treasures<br> Of God, nor do I know<br> What is hidden,<br> Nor claim I to be<br> An angel. Nor yet<br> Do I say, of those whom<br> Your eyes do despise<br> That God will not grant them<br> (All) that is good:<br> God knoweth best<br> What is in their souls:<br> I should, if I did,<br> Indeed be a wrong-doer."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_032.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>32.  Q<u>a</u>loo y<u>a</u> noo<u>h</u>u qad j<u>a</u>daltan<u>a</u> faaktharta jid<u>a</u>lan<u>a</u> fa/tin<u>a</u> bim<u>a</u> taAAidun<u>a</u> in kunta mina a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>diqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>32.  They said: "O Noah!<br> Thou hast disputed with us,<br>And (much) hast thou prolonged<br> The dispute with us: now<br> Bring upon us what thou<br> Threatenest us with, if thou<br> Speakest the truth!?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_033.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>33.  Q<u>a</u>la innam<u>a</u> ya/teekum bihi All<u>a</u>hu in sh<u>a</u>a wam<u>a</u> antum bimuAAjizeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>33.  He said: "Truly, God<br> Will bring it on you<br> If He wills,—and then,<br> Ye will not be able<br> To frustrate it!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_034.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>34.  Wal<u>a</u> yanfaAAukum nu<u>sh</u>ee in aradtu an an<u>s</u>a<u>h</u>a lakum in k<u>a</u>na All<u>a</u>hu yureedu an yughwiyakum huwa rabbukum wa-ilayhi turjaAAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>34.  "Of no profit will be<br> My counsel to you,<br> Much as I desire<br> To give you (good) counsel,<br> If it be that God<br> Willeth to leave you astray:<br> He is your Lord!<br> And to Him will ye return!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_035.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>35.  Am yaqooloona iftar<u>a</u>hu qul ini iftaraytuhu faAAalayya ijr<u>a</u>mee waan<u>a</u> baree-on mimm<u>a</u> tujrimoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>35.  Or do they say,<br> "He has forged it "? Say:<br> "If I had forged it,<br> On me were my sin!<br> And I am free<br> Of the sins of which<br> Ye are guilty!</p> <div class="filenav">
