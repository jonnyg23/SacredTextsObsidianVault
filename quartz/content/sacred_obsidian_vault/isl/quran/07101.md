 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra LXXI.: Nūḥ, or Noah.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/071/071_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  Inn<u>a</u> arsaln<u>a</u> noo<u>h</u>an il<u>a</u> qawmihi an an<u>th</u>ir qawmaka min qabli an ya/tiyahum AAa<u>tha</u>bun aleem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>1.  We sent Noah<br> To his People<br> (With the Command):<br> "Do thou warn thy People<br> Before there comes to them<br> A grievous Penalty."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/071/071_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  Q<u>a</u>la y<u>a</u> qawmi innee lakum na<u>th</u>eerun mubeen<b>un</b>    </p>
 <p>2.  He said: "O my People!<br> I am to you<br> A Warner, clear and open:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/071/071_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  Ani oAAbudoo All<u>a</u>ha wa<b>i</b>ttaqoohu waa<u>t</u>eeAAoon<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>3.  "That ye should worship<br> God, fear Him,<br> And obey me:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/071/071_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  Yaghfir lakum min <u>th</u>unoobikum wayu-akhkhirkum il<u>a</u> ajalin musamman inna ajala All<u>a</u>hi i<u>tha</u> j<u>a</u>a l<u>a</u> yu-akhkharu law kuntum taAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>4.  "So He may forgive you<br> Your sins and give you<br> Respite for a stated Term:<br> For when the Term given<br> By God is accomplished,<br> It cannot be put forward:<br> If ye only knew."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/071/071_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  Q<u>a</u>la rabbi innee daAAawtu qawmee laylan wanah<u>a</u>r<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>5.  He, said: "O my Lord!<br> I have called to my People<br> Night and day:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/071/071_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  Falam yazidhum duAA<u>a</u>-ee ill<u>a</u> fir<u>a</u>r<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>6.  "But my call only<br> Increases (their) flight<br> (From the Right).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/071/071_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  Wa-innee kullam<u>a</u> daAAawtuhum litaghfira lahum jaAAaloo a<u>sa</u>biAAahum fee <u>atha</u>nihim wa<b>i</b>staghshaw thiy<u>a</u>bahum waa<u>s</u>arroo wa<b>i</b>stakbaroo istikb<u>a</u>r<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>7.  "And every time I have<br> Called to them, that Thou<br> Mightest forgive them,<br> They have (only) thrust<br> Their fingers into their ears,<br> Covered themselves up with<br> Their garments, grown obstinate,<br> And given themselves up<br> To arrogance.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/071/071_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  Thumma innee daAAawtuhum jih<u>a</u>r<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>8.  "So I have called to them<br> Aloud;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/071/071_009.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>9.  Thumma innee aAAlantu lahum waasrartu lahum isr<u>a</u>r<u>a</u><b>n</b>    </p>
 <p>9.  "Further I have spoken<br> To them in public<br> And secretly in private,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/071/071_010.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>10.  Faqultu istaghfiroo rabbakum innahu k<u>a</u>na ghaff<u>a</u>r<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>10.  "Saying, "Ask forgiveness<br> From your Lord;<br> For He is Oft-Forgiving;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/071/071_011.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>11.  Yursili a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>a AAalaykum midr<u>a</u>r<u>a</u><b>n </b>   </p>
 <p>11.  "He will send rain<br> To you in abundance;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/071/071_012.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>12.  Wayumdidkum bi-amw<u>a</u>lin wabaneena wayajAAal lakum jann<u>a</u>tin wayajAAal lakum anh<u>a</u>r<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>12.  Give you increase<br> In wealth and sons;<br> And bestow on you<br> Gardens and bestow on you<br> Rivers (of flowing water).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/071/071_013.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>13.  M<u>a</u> lakum l<u>a</u> tarjoona lill<u>a</u>hi waq<u>a</u>r<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>13.  "What is the matter<br> With you, that ye<br> Place not your hope<br> For kindness and long-suffering<br> In God,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/071/071_014.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>14.  Waqad khalaqakum a<u>t</u>w<u>a</u>r<u>a</u><b>n</b>    </p>
 <p>14.  "Seeing that it is He<br> That has created you<br> In diverse stages?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/071/071_015.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>15.  Alam taraw kayfa khalaqa All<u>a</u>hu sabAAa sam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>tin <u>t</u>ib<u>a</u>q<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>15.  "See ye not<br> How God has created<br> The seven heavens<br> One above another,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/071/071_016.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>16.  WajaAAala alqamara feehinna nooran wajaAAala a<b>l</b>shshamsa sir<u>a</u>j<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>16.  "And made the moon<br> A light in their midst,<br> And made the sun<br> As a (Glorious) Lamp?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/071/071_017.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>17.  Wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu anbatakum mina al-ar<u>d</u>i nab<u>a</u>t<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>17.  "And God has produced<br> You from the earth,<br> Growing (gradually),</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/071/071_018.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>18.  Thumma yuAAeedukum feeh<u>a</u> wayukhrijukum ikhr<u>a</u>j<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>18.  "And in the End<br> He will return you<br> Into the (earth),<br> And raise you forth<br> (Again at the Resurrection)?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/071/071_019.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>19.  Wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu jaAAala lakumu al-ar<u>d</u>a bis<u>ata</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>19.  "And God has made<br> The earth for you<br> As a carpet (spread out),</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/071/071_020.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>20.  Litaslukoo minh<u>a</u> subulan fij<u>a</u>j<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>20.  "That ye may go about<br> Therein, in spacious roads."</p> <div class="filenav">
