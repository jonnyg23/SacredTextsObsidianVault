 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra LXV.: Ṭalāq, or Divorce.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/065/065_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nnabiyyu i<u>tha</u> <u>t</u>allaqtumu a<b>l</b>nnis<u>a</u>a fa<u>t</u>alliqoohunna liAAiddatihinna waa<u>hs</u>oo alAAiddata wa<b>i</b>ttaqoo All<u>a</u>ha rabbakum l<u>a</u> tukhrijoohunna min buyootihinna wal<u>a</u> yakhrujna ill<u>a</u> an ya/teena bif<u>ah</u>ishatin mubayyinatin watilka <u>h</u>udoodu All<u>a</u>hi waman yataAAadda <u>h</u>udooda All<u>a</u>hi faqad <i><u>th</u></i>alama nafsahu l<u>a</u> tadree laAAalla All<u>a</u>ha yu<u>h</u>dithu baAAda <u>tha</u>lika amr<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>1.  O Prophet! When ye<br> Do divorce women,<br> Divorce them at their<br> Prescribed periods,<br> And count (accurately)<br> Their prescribed periods:<br> And fear God your Lord:<br> And turn them not out<br> Of their houses, nor shall<br> They (themselves) leave,<br> Except in case they are<br> Guilty of some open lewdness,<br> Those are limits<br> Set by God: and any<br> Who transgresses the limits<br> Of God, does verily<br> Wrong his (own) soul:<br> Thou knowest not if<br>Perchance God will<br> Bring about thereafter<br> Some new situation.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/065/065_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  Fa-i<u>tha</u> balaghna ajalahunna faamsikoohunna bimaAAroofin aw f<u>a</u>riqoohunna bimaAAroofin waashhidoo <u>th</u>away AAadlin minkum waaqeemoo a<b>l</b>shshah<u>a</u>data lill<u>a</u>hi <u>tha</u>likum yooAAa<i><u>th</u></i>u bihi man k<u>a</u>na yu/minu bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi wa<b>a</b>lyawmi al-<u>a</u>khiri waman yattaqi All<u>a</u>ha yajAAal lahu makhraj<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>2.  Thus when they fulfil<br> Their term appointed,<br> Either take them back<br> On equitable terms<br> Or part with them<br> On equitable terms;<br> And take for witness<br> Two persons from among you,<br> Endued with justice,<br> And establish the evidence<br> (As) before God. Such<br> Is the admonition given<br> To him who believes<br> In God and the Last Day.<br> And for those who fear<br> God, He (ever) prepares<br> A way out,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/065/065_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  Wayarzuqhu min <u>h</u>aythu l<u>a</u> ya<u>h</u>tasibu waman yatawakkal AAal<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi fahuwa <u>h</u>asbuhu inna All<u>a</u>ha b<u>a</u>lighu amrihi qad jaAAala All<u>a</u>hu likulli shay-in qadr<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>3.  And He provides for him<br> From (sources) he never<br> Could imagine. And if<br> Any one puts his trust<br> In God, sufficient is (God)<br> For him. For God will<br>Surely accomplish His purpose:<br> Verily, for all things<br> Has God appointed<br> A due proportion.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/065/065_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  Wa<b>a</b>ll<u>a</u>-ee ya-isna mina alma<u>h</u>ee<u>d</u>i min nis<u>a</u>-ikum ini irtabtum faAAiddatuhunna thal<u>a</u>thatu ashhurin wa<b>a</b>ll<u>a</u>-ee lam ya<u>h</u>i<u>d</u>na waol<u>a</u>tu al-a<u>h</u>m<u>a</u>li ajaluhunna an ya<u>d</u>aAAna <u>h</u>amlahunna waman yattaqi All<u>a</u>ha yajAAal lahu min amrihi yusr<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>4.  Such of your women<br> As have passed the age<br> Of monthly courses, for them<br> The prescribed period, if ye<br> Have any doubts, is<br> Three months, and for those<br> Who have no courses<br> (It is the same):<br> For those who carry<br> (Life within their wombs),<br> Their period is until<br> They deliver their burdens:<br> And for those who<br> Fear God, He will<br> Make their path easy.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/065/065_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  <u>Tha</u>lika amru All<u>a</u>hi anzalahu ilaykum waman yattaqi All<u>a</u>ha yukaffir AAanhu sayyi-<u>a</u>tihi wayuAA<i><u>th</u></i>im lahu ajr<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>5.  That is the Command<br> Of God, which He<br> Has sent down to you:<br> And if any one fears God,<br> He will remove his ills<br> From him, and will enlarge<br> His reward.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/065/065_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  Askinoohunna min <u>h</u>aythu sakantum min wujdikum wal<u>a</u> tu<u>da</u>rroohunna litu<u>d</u>ayyiqoo AAalayhinna wa-in kunna ol<u>a</u>ti <u>h</u>amlin faanfiqoo AAalayhinna <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> ya<u>d</u>aAAna <u>h</u>amlahunna fa-in ar<u>d</u>aAAna lakum fa<u>a</u>toohunna ojoorahunna wa/tamiroo baynakum bimaAAroofin wa-in taAA<u>a</u>sartum fasatur<u>d</u>iAAu lahu okhr<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>6.  Let the women live<br> (In ‘iddat) in the same<br>Style as ye live,<br> According to your means:<br> Annoy them not, so as<br> To restrict them.<br> And if they carry (life<br> In their wombs), then<br> Spend (your substance) on them<br> Until they deliver<br> Their burden: and if<br> They suckle your (offspring),<br> Give them their recompense:<br> And take mutual counsel<br> Together, according to<br> What is just and reasonable.<br> And if ye find yourselves<br> In difficulties, let another<br> Woman suckle (the child)<br> On the (father's) behalf.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/065/065_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  Liyunfiq <u>th</u>oo saAAatin min saAAatihi waman qudira AAalayhi rizquhu falyunfiq mimm<u>a</u> <u>a</u>t<u>a</u>hu All<u>a</u>hu l<u>a</u> yukallifu All<u>a</u>hu nafsan ill<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> <u>a</u>t<u>a</u>h<u>a</u> sayajAAalu All<u>a</u>hu baAAda AAusrin yusr<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>7.  Let the man of means<br> Spend according to<br> His means: and the man<br> Whose resources are restricted,<br> Let him spend according<br> To what God has given him.<br> God puts no burden<br> On any person beyond<br> What He has given him.<br> After a difficulty, God<br> Will soon grant relief.</p> <div class="filenav">
