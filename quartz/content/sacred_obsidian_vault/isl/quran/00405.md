 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra IV.: Nisāa, or The Women.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 5</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_026.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>26.  Yureedu All<u>a</u>hu liyubayyina lakum wayahdiyakum sunana alla<u>th</u>eena min qablikum wayatooba AAalaykum wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu AAaleemun <u>h</u>akeem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>26.  God doth wish<br> To make clear to you<br> And to show you<br> The ordinances of those<br> Before you; and (He<br> Doth wish to) turn to you<br> (In Mercy): and God<br> Is All-knowing, All-wise.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_027.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>27.  Wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu yureedu an yatooba AAalaykum wayureedu alla<u>th</u>eena yattabiAAoona a<b>l</b>shshahaw<u>a</u>ti an tameeloo maylan AAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<b>an</b>   </p>
 <p>27.  God doth wish<br> To turn to you,<br> But the wish of those<br> Who follow their lusts<br> Is that ye should turn<br> Away (from Him),<br> Far, far away.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_028.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>28.  Yureedu All<u>a</u>hu an yukhaffifa AAankum wakhuliqa al-ins<u>a</u>nu <u>d</u>aAAeef<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>28.  God doth wish<br> To lighten your (difficulties);<br> For man was created<br> Weak (in flesh).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_029.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>29.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo l<u>a</u> ta/kuloo amw<u>a</u>lakum baynakum bi<b>a</b>lb<u>at</u>ili ill<u>a</u> an takoona tij<u>a</u>ratan AAan tar<u>ad</u>in minkum wal<u>a</u> taqtuloo anfusakum inna All<u>a</u>ha k<u>a</u>na bikum ra<u>h</u>eem<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>29.  O ye who believe! sot<br> Eat not up your property<br>Among yourselves in vanities:<br> But let there be amongst you<br> Traffic and trade<br> By mutual good-will:<br> Nor kill (or destroy)<br> Yourselves: for verily<br> God hath been to you<br> Most Merciful!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_030.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>30.  Waman yafAAal <u>tha</u>lika AAudw<u>a</u>nan wa<i><u>th</u></i>ulman fasawfa nu<u>s</u>leehi n<u>a</u>ran wak<u>a</u>na <u>tha</u>lika AAal<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi yaseer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>30.  If any do that<br> In rancour and injustice,—<br> Soon shall We cast them<br> Into the Fire: and easy<br> It is for God.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_031.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>31.  In tajtaniboo kab<u>a</u>-ira m<u>a</u> tunhawna AAanhu nukaffir AAankum sayyi-<u>a</u>tikum wanudkhilkum mudkhalan kareem<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>31.  If ye (but) eschew<br> The most heinous<br> Of the things<br> Which ye are forbidden to do,<br> We shall expel<br> Out of you<br> All the evil in you,<br> And admit you to a Gate<br> Of great honour.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_032.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>32.  Wal<u>a</u> tatamannaw m<u>a</u> fa<u>dd</u>ala All<u>a</u>hu bihi baAA<u>d</u>akum AAal<u>a</u> baAA<u>d</u>in li<b>l</b>rrij<u>a</u>li na<u>s</u>eebun mimm<u>a</u> iktasaboo wali<b>l</b>nnis<u>a</u>-i na<u>s</u>eebun mimm<u>a</u> iktasabna wa<b>i</b>s-aloo All<u>a</u>ha min fa<u>d</u>lihi inna All<u>a</u>ha k<u>a</u>na bikulli shay-in AAaleem<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>32.  And in no wise covet<br> Those things in which God<br> Hath bestowed His gifts<br> More freely on some of you<br> Than on others: to men<br> Is allotted what they earn,<br> And to women what they earn:<br> But ask God of His bounty.<br> For God hath full knowledge<br> Of all things.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_033.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>33.  Walikullin jaAAaln<u>a</u> maw<u>a</u>liya mimm<u>a</u> taraka alw<u>a</u>lid<u>a</u>ni wa<b>a</b>l-aqraboona wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena AAaqadat aym<u>a</u>nukum fa<u>a</u>toohum na<u>s</u>eebahum inna All<u>a</u>ha k<u>a</u>na AAal<u>a</u> kulli shay-in shaheed<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>33.  To (benefit) every one,<br> We have appointed<br> Sharers and heirs<br> To property left<br>By parents and relatives.<br> To those, also, to whom<br> Your right hand was pledged,<br> Give their due portion.<br> For truly God is witness<br> To all things.</p> <div class="filenav">
