 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XCVI.: Iqraa, or Read! or Proclaim!</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/096/096_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  Iqra/ bi-ismi rabbika alla<u>th</u>ee khalaq<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>1.  Proclaim! (or Read!)<br> In the name<br> Of thy Lord and Cherisher,<br> Who created—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/096/096_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  Khalaqa al-ins<u>a</u>na min AAalaq<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>2.  Created man, out of<br> A (mere) clot<br> Of congealed blood:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/096/096_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  Iqra/ warabbuka al-akram<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>3.  Proclaim! And thy Lord<br> Is Most Bountiful,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/096/096_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  Alla<u>th</u>ee AAallama bi<b>a</b>lqalam<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>4.  He Who taught<br> (The use of) the Pen,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/096/096_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  AAallama al-ins<u>a</u>na m<u>a</u> lam yaAAlam   </p>
 <p>5.  Taught man that<br> Which he knew not.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/096/096_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  Kall<u>a</u> inna al-ins<u>a</u>na laya<u>t</u>gh<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>6.  Nay, but man doth<br> Transgress all bounds,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/096/096_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  An ra<u>a</u>hu istaghn<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>7.  In that he looketh<br> Upon himself as self-sufficient.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/096/096_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  Inna il<u>a</u> rabbika a<b>l</b>rrujAA<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>8.  Verily, to thy Lord<br> Is the return (of all).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/096/096_009.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>9.  Araayta alla<u>th</u>ee yanh<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>9.  Seest thou one<br> Who forbids—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/096/096_010.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>10.  AAabdan i<u>tha</u> <u>s</u>all<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>10.  A votary when he<br> (Turns) to pray?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/096/096_011.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>11.  Araayta in k<u>a</u>na AAal<u>a</u> alhud<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>11.  Seest thou if<br> He is on (the road<br> Of) Guidance?—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/096/096_012.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>12.  Aw amara bi<b>al</b>ttaqw<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>12.  Or enjoins Righteousness?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/096/096_013.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>13.  Araayta in ka<u>thth</u>aba watawall<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>13.  Seest thou if he<br> Denies (Truth) and turns away?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/096/096_014.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>14.  Alam yaAAlam bi-anna All<u>a</u>ha yar<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>14.  Knoweth he not<br> That God doth see?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/096/096_015.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>15.  Kall<u>a</u> la-in lam yantahi lanasfaAAan bi<b>al</b>nn<u>as</u>iya<b>ti</b>   </p>
 <p>15.  Let him beware! If he<br> Desist not, We will<br> Drag him by the forelock,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/096/096_016.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>16.  N<u>as</u>iyatin k<u>ath</u>ibatin kh<u>at</u>i-a<b>tin</b>    </p>
 <p>16.  A lying, sinful forelock!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/096/096_017.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>17.  FalyadAAu n<u>a</u>diyah<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>17.  Then, let him call<br> (For help) to his council<br> (Of comrades):</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/096/096_018.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>18.  SanadAAu a<b>l</b>zzab<u>a</u>niya<b>ta</b>   </p>
 <p>18.  We will call<br> On the angels of punishment<br> (To deal with him)!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/096/096_019.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>19.  Kall<u>a</u> l<u>a</u> tu<u>t</u>iAAhu wa<b>o</b>sjud wa<b>i</b>qtarib      </p>
 <p>19.  Nay, heed him not:<br> But bow down in adoration,<br> And bring thyself<br> The closer (to God)!</p>  <div class="filenav">
