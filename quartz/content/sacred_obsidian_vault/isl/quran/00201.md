 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra II.: Baqara, or the Heifer.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  Alif-l<u>a</u>m-meem    </p>
 <p>1.  A. L. M.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  <u>Tha</u>lika alkit<u>a</u>bu l<u>a</u> rayba feehi hudan lilmuttaqeen<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>2.  This is the Book;<br> In it is guidance sure, without doubt,<br> To those who fear God;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  Alla<u>th</u>eena yu/minoona bi<b>a</b>lghaybi wayuqeemoona a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>al<u>a</u>ta wamimm<u>a</u> razaqn<u>a</u>hum yunfiqoon<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>3.  Who believe in the Unseen,<br> Are steadfast in prayer,<br> And spend out of what We<br> Have provided for them;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  Wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena yu/minoona bim<u>a</u> onzila ilayka wam<u>a</u> onzila min qablika wabi<b>a</b>l-<u>a</u>khirati hum yooqinoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>4.  And who believe in the Revelation<br> Sent to thee,<br>And sent before thy time,<br> And (in their hearts)<br> Have the assurance of the Hereafter.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  Ol<u>a</u>-ika AAal<u>a</u> hudan min rabbihim waol<u>a</u>-ika humu almufli<u>h</u>oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>5.  They are on (true) guidance,<br> From their Lord, and it is<br> These who will prosper.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  Inna alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo saw<u>a</u>on AAalayhim aan<u>th</u>artahum am lam tun<u>th</u>irhum l<u>a</u> yu/minoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>6.  As to those who reject Faith,<br> It is the same to them<br> Whether thou warn them<br> Or do not warn them;<br> They will not believe.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  Khatama All<u>a</u>hu AAal<u>a</u> quloobihim waAAal<u>a</u> samAAihim waAAal<u>a</u> ab<u>sa</u>rihim ghish<u>a</u>watun walahum AAa<u>tha</u>bun AAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>7.  God hath set a seal<br> On their hearts and on their hearing,<br> And on their eyes is a veil;<br> Great is the penalty they (incur).</p> <div class="filenav">
