 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra II.: Baqara, or the Heifer.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 18</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_148.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>148.  Walikullin wijhatun huwa muwalleeh<u>a</u> fa<b>i</b>stabiqoo alkhayr<u>a</u>ti aynam<u>a</u> takoonoo ya/ti bikumu All<u>a</u>hu jameeAAan inna All<u>a</u>ha AAal<u>a</u> kulli shay-in qadeer<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>148.  To each is a goal<br> To which God turns him;<br> Then strive together (as in a race)<br> Towards all that is good.<br> Wheresoever ye are,<br> God will bring you<br> Together, For God<br> Hath power over all things.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_149.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>149.  Wamin <u>h</u>aythu kharajta fawalli wajhaka sha<u>t</u>ra almasjidi al<u>h</u>ar<u>a</u>mi wa-innahu lal<u>h</u>aqqu min rabbika wam<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hu bigh<u>a</u>filin AAamm<u>a</u> taAAmaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>149.  From whencesoever<br> Thou startest forth, turn<br> Thy face in the direction<br> Of the Sacred Mosque;<br> That is indeed the truth<br> From thy Lord. And God<br> Is not unmindful<br> Of what ye do.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_150.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>150.  Wamin <u>h</u>aythu kharajta fawalli wajhaka sha<u>t</u>ra almasjidi al<u>h</u>ar<u>a</u>mi wa<u>h</u>aythu m<u>a</u> kuntum fawalloo wujoohakum sha<u>t</u>rahu li-all<u>a</u> yakoona li<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si AAalaykum <u>h</u>ujjatun ill<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <i><u>th</u></i>alamoo minhum fal<u>a</u> takhshawhum wa<b>i</b>khshawnee wali-otimma niAAmatee AAalaykum walaAAallakum tahtadoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>150.  So from whencesoever<br> Thou startest forth, turn<br> Thy face in the direction<br> Of the Sacred Mosque;<br> And wheresoever ye are,<br> Turn your face thither:<br> That there be no ground<br> Of dispute against you<br> Among the people,<br> Except those of them that are<br> Bent on wickedness; so fear<br> Them not, but fear Me;<br> And that I may complete<br> My favours on you, and ye<br> May (consent to) be guided;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_151.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>151.  Kam<u>a</u> arsaln<u>a</u> feekum rasoolan minkum yatloo AAalaykum <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin<u>a</u> wayuzakkeekum wayuAAallimukumu alkit<u>a</u>ba wa<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>ikmata wayuAAallimukum m<u>a</u> lam takoonoo taAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>151.  A similar (favour<br> Have ye already received)<br> In that We have sent<br> Among you an Apostle<br> Of your own, rehearsing to you<br> Our Signs, and sanctifying<br> You, and instructing you<br> In Scripture and Wisdom,<br> And in new Knowledge.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_152.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>152.  Fa<b>o</b><u>th</u>kuroonee a<u>th</u>kurkum wa<b>o</b>shkuroo lee wal<u>a</u> takfuroon<b>i</b>    </p>
 <p>152.  Then do ye remember<br> Me; I will remember<br> You. Be grateful to Me,<br> And reject not Faith.</p> <div class="filenav">
