 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/077/077_041.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>41.  Inna almuttaqeena fee <i><u>th</u></i>il<u>a</u>lin waAAuyoon<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>41.  As to the Righteous,<br> They shall be amidst<br> (Cool) shades and springs<br> (Of water),</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/077/077_042.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>42.  Wafaw<u>a</u>kiha mimm<u>a</u> yashtahoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>42.  And (they shall have)<br> Fruits,—all they desire.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/077/077_043.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>43.  Kuloo wa<b>i</b>shraboo hanee-an bim<u>a</u> kuntum taAAmaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>43.  "Eat ye and drink ye<br> To your heart's content:<br> For that ye worked<br> (Righteousness).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/077/077_044.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>44.  Inn<u>a</u> ka<u>tha</u>lika najzee almu<u>h</u>sineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>44.  Thus do We certainly<br> Reward the Doers of Good.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/077/077_045.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>45.  Waylun yawma-i<u>th</u>in lilmuka<u>thth</u>ibeen<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>45.  Ah woe, that Day,<br> To the Rejecters of Truth!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/077/077_046.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>46.  Kuloo watamattaAAoo qaleelan innakum mujrimoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>46.  (O ye Unjust!)<br> Eat ye and enjoy yourselves<br> (But) a little while,<br> For that ye are Sinners.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/077/077_047.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>47.  Waylun yawma-i<u>th</u>in lilmuka<u>thth</u>ibeen<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>47.  Ah woe, that Day,<br> To the Rejecters of Truth!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/077/077_048.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>48.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> qeela lahumu irkaAAoo l<u>a</u> yarkaAAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>48.  And when it is said<br> To them, "Prostrate yourselves!"<br> They do not so.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/077/077_049.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>49.  Waylun yawma-i<u>th</u>in lilmuka<u>thth</u>ibeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>49.  Ah woe, that Day,<br> To the Rejecters of Truth!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/077/077_050.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>50.  Fabi-ayyi <u>h</u>adeethin baAAdahu yu/minoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>50.  Then what Message,<br> After that,<br> Will they believe in?</p>  <div class="filenav">
