 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXVII.: Naml, or the Ants.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 4</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_045.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>45.  Walaqad arsaln<u>a</u> il<u>a</u> thamooda akh<u>a</u>hum <u>sa</u>li<u>h</u>an ani oAAbudoo All<u>a</u>ha fa-i<u>tha</u> hum fareeq<u>a</u>ni yakhta<u>s</u>imoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>45.  We sent (aforetime),<br> To the <u>Th</u>amūd, their brother<br> Ṣaliḥ, saying, "Serve God":<br> But behold, they became<br> Two factions quarrelling<br> With each other.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_046.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>46.  Q<u>a</u>la y<u>a</u> qawmi lima tastaAAjiloona bi<b>al</b>ssayyi-ati qabla al<u>h</u>asanati lawl<u>a</u> tastaghfiroona All<u>a</u>ha laAAallakum tur<u>h</u>amoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>46.  He said: "O my people!<br> Why ask ye to hasten on<br>The evil in preference to the good?<br> If only ye ask God for forgiveness,<br> Ye may hope to receive mercy.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_047.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>47.  Q<u>a</u>loo i<u>tt</u>ayyarn<u>a</u> bika wabiman maAAaka q<u>a</u>la <u>ta</u>-irukum AAinda All<u>a</u>hi bal antum qawmun tuftanoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>47.  They said: "Ill omen<br> Do we augur from thee<br> And those that are with thee".<br> He said: "Your ill omen<br> Is with God; yea, ye are<br> A people under trial."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_048.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>48.  Wak<u>a</u>na fee almadeenati tisAAatu rah<u>t</u>in yufsidoona fee al-ar<u>d</u>i wal<u>a</u> yu<u>s</u>li<u>h</u>oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>48.  There were in the City<br> Nine men of a family,<br> Who made mischief in the land,<br> And would not reform.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_049.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>49.  Q<u>a</u>loo taq<u>a</u>samoo bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi lanubayyitannahu waahlahu thumma lanaqoolanna liwaliyyihi m<u>a</u> shahidn<u>a</u> mahlika ahlihi wa-inn<u>a</u> la<u>sa</u>diqoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>49.  They said: "Swear<br> A mutual oath by God<br> That we shall make<br> A secret night attack<br> On him and his people,<br> And that we shall then<br> Say to his heir (when he<br> Seeks vengeance), 'We were not<br> Present at the slaughter<br> Of his people, and we are<br> Positively telling the truth'."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_050.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>50.  Wamakaroo makran wamakarn<u>a</u> makran wahum l<u>a</u> yashAAuroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>50.  They plotted and planned,<br> But We too planned,<br> Even while they perceived it not.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_051.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>51.  Fa<b>o</b>n<i><u>th</u></i>ur kayfa k<u>a</u>na AA<u>a</u>qibatu makrihim ann<u>a</u> dammarn<u>a</u>hum waqawmahum ajmaAAeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>51.  Then see what was the end<br> Of their plot!—this,<br> That We destroyed them<br> And their people, all (of them).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_052.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>52.  Fatilka buyootuhum kh<u>a</u>wiyatan bim<u>a</u> <i><u>th</u></i>alamoo inna fee <u>tha</u>lika la<u>a</u>yatan liqawmin yaAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>52.  Now such were their houses,—<br> In utter ruin,—because<br> They practised wrong-doing.<br> Verily in this is a Sign<br> For people of knowledge.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_053.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>53.  Waanjayn<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo wak<u>a</u>noo yattaqoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>53.  And We saved those<br> Who believed and practised<br> Righteousness.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_054.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>54.  Waloo<u>t</u>an i<u>th</u> q<u>a</u>la liqawmihi ata/toona alf<u>ah</u>ishata waantum tub<u>s</u>iroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>54.  (We also sent) Lūt<br> (As an apostle): behold,<br> He said to his people,<br> "Do ye do what is shameful<br> Though ye see (its iniquity)?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_055.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>55.  A-innakum lata/toona a<b>l</b>rrij<u>a</u>la shahwatan min dooni a<b>l</b>nnis<u>a</u>-i bal antum qawmun tajhaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>55.  Would ye really approach men<br> In your lusts rather than<br> Women? Nay, ye are<br> A people (grossly) ignorant!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_056.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>56.  Fam<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>na jaw<u>a</u>ba qawmihi ill<u>a</u> an q<u>a</u>loo akhrijoo <u>a</u>la loo<u>t</u>in min qaryatikum innahum on<u>a</u>sun yata<u>t</u>ahharoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>56.  But his people gave<br> No other answer but this:<br> They said, "Drive out<br>The followers of Lūt from<br> Your city: these are<br> Indeed men who want<br> To be clean and pure!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_057.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>57.  Faanjayn<u>a</u>hu waahlahu ill<u>a</u> imraatahu qaddarn<u>a</u>h<u>a</u> mina algh<u>a</u>bireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>57.  But We saved him<br> And his family, except<br> His wife: her We destined<br> To be of those<br> Who lagged behind.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_058.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>58.  Waam<u>t</u>arn<u>a</u> AAalayhim ma<u>t</u>aran fas<u>a</u>a ma<u>t</u>aru almun<u>th</u>areen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>58.  And We rained down on them<br> A shower (of brimstone):<br> And evil was the shower<br> On those who were admonished<br> (But heeded not)!</p> <div class="filenav">
