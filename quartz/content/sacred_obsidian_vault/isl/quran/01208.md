 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XII.: Yūsuf, or Joseph.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 8</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_058.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>58.  Waj<u>a</u>a ikhwatu yoosufa fadakhaloo AAalayhi faAAarafahum wahum lahu munkiroon<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>58.  Then came Joseph's brethren:<br> They entered his presence,<br> And he knew them,<br> But they knew him not.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_059.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>59.  Walamm<u>a</u> jahhazahum bijah<u>a</u>zihim q<u>a</u>la i/toonee bi-akhin lakum min abeekum al<u>a</u> tarawna annee oofee alkayla waan<u>a</u> khayru almunzileen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>59.  And when he had furnished<br> Them forth with provisions<br> (Suitable) for them, he said:<br> "Bring unto me a brother<br> Ye have, of the same father<br> As yourselves, (but a different mother):<br> See ye not that I pay out<br> Full measure, and that I<br> Do provide the best hospitality?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_060.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>60.  Fa-in lam ta/toonee bihi fal<u>a</u> kayla lakum AAindee wal<u>a</u> taqraboon<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>60.  "Now if ye bring him not<br> To me, ye shall have<br> No measure (of corn) from me,<br> Nor shall ye (even) come<br> Near me."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_061.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>61.  Q<u>a</u>loo sanur<u>a</u>widu AAanhu ab<u>a</u>hu wa-inn<u>a</u> laf<u>a</u>AAiloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>61.  They said: "We shall<br> Certainly seek to get<br> Our wish about him<br> From his father:<br> Indeed we shall do it."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_062.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>62.  Waq<u>a</u>la lifity<u>a</u>nihi ijAAaloo bi<u>da</u>AAatahum fee ri<u>ha</u>lihim laAAallahum yaAArifoonah<u>a</u> i<u>tha</u> inqalaboo il<u>a</u> ahlihim laAAallahum yarjiAAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>62.  And (Joseph) told his servants<br> To put their stock-in-trade<br> (With which they had bartered)<br> Into their saddle-bags,<br> So they should know it only<br> When they returned to their people,<br>In order that they<br> Might come back.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_063.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>63.  Falamm<u>a</u> rajaAAoo il<u>a</u> abeehim q<u>a</u>loo y<u>a</u> ab<u>a</u>n<u>a</u> muniAAa minn<u>a</u> alkaylu faarsil maAAan<u>a</u> akh<u>a</u>n<u>a</u> naktal wa-inn<u>a</u> lahu la<u>ha</u>fi<i><u>th</u></i>oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>63.  Now when they returned<br> To their father, they said:<br> "O our father! No more<br> Measure of grain shall we get<br> (Unless we take our brother):<br> So send our brother with us,<br> That we may get our measure;<br> And we will indeed<br> Take every care of him."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_064.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>64.  Q<u>a</u>la hal <u>a</u>manukum AAalayhi ill<u>a</u> kam<u>a</u> amintukum AAal<u>a</u> akheehi min qablu fa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu khayrun <u>ha</u>fi<i><u>th</u></i>an wahuwa ar<u>h</u>amu a<b>l</b>rr<u>ah</u>imeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>64.  He said: "Shall I trust you<br> With him with any result<br> Other than when I trusted you<br> With his brother aforetime?<br> But God is the best<br> To take care (of him),<br> And He is the Most Merciful<br> Of those who show mercy!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_065.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>65.  Walamm<u>a</u> fata<u>h</u>oo mat<u>a</u>AAahum wajadoo bi<u>da</u>AAatahum ruddat ilayhim q<u>a</u>loo y<u>a</u> ab<u>a</u>n<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> nabghee h<u>ath</u>ihi bi<u>da</u>AAatun<u>a</u> ruddat ilayn<u>a</u> wanameeru ahlan<u>a</u> wana<u>h</u>fa<i><u>th</u></i>u akh<u>a</u>n<u>a</u> wanazd<u>a</u>du kayla baAAeerin <u>tha</u>lika kaylun yaseer<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>65.  Then when they opened<br> Their baggage, they found<br> Their stock-in-trade had been<br> Returned to them. They said:<br> "O our father! What (more)<br> Can we desire? This our<br> Stock-in-trade has been returned<br> To us: so we shall get<br>(More) food for our family;<br> We shall take care of our brother;<br> And add (at the same time)<br> A full camel's load (of grain<br> To our provisions).<br> This is but a small quantity.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_066.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>66.  Q<u>a</u>la lan orsilahu maAAakum <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> tu/tooni mawthiqan mina All<u>a</u>hi lata/tunnanee bihi ill<u>a</u> an yu<u>hat</u>a bikum falamm<u>a</u> <u>a</u>tawhu mawthiqahum q<u>a</u>la All<u>a</u>hu AAal<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> naqoolu wakeel<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>66.  (Jacob) said: "Never will I<br> Send him with you until<br> Ye swear a solemn oath to me,<br> In God's name, that ye<br> Will be sure to bring him back<br> To me unless ye are yourselves<br> Hemmed in (and made powerless)."<br> And when they had sworn<br> Their solemn oath,<br> He said: "Over all<br> That we say, be God<br> The Witness and Guardian!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_067.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>67.  Waq<u>a</u>la y<u>a</u> baniyya l<u>a</u> tadkhuloo min b<u>a</u>bin w<u>ah</u>idin wa<b>o</b>dkhuloo min abw<u>a</u>bin mutafarriqatin wam<u>a</u> oghnee AAankum mina All<u>a</u>hi min shay-in ini al<u>h</u>ukmu ill<u>a</u> lill<u>a</u>hi AAalayhi tawakkaltu waAAalayhi falyatawakkali almutawakkiloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>67.  Further he said:<br> "O my sons! enter not<br> All by one gate: enter ye<br> By different gates. Not that<br>I can profit you aught<br> Against God (with my advice):<br> None can command except God:<br> On Him do I put my trust:<br> And let all that trust<br> Put their trust on Him."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_068.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>68.  Walamm<u>a</u> dakhaloo min <u>h</u>aythu amarahum aboohum m<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>na yughnee AAanhum mina All<u>a</u>hi min shay-in ill<u>a</u> <u>ha</u>jatan fee nafsi yaAAqooba qa<u>da</u>h<u>a</u> wa-innahu la<u>th</u>oo AAilmin lim<u>a</u> AAallamn<u>a</u>hu wal<u>a</u>kinna akthara a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si l<u>a</u> yaAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>68.  And when they entered<br> In the manner their father<br> Had enjoined, it did not<br> Profit them in the least<br> Against (the Plan of) God:<br> It was but a necessity<br> Of Jacob's soul, which he<br> Discharged. For he was,<br> By Our instruction, full<br> Of knowledge (and experience):<br> But most men know not.</p> <div class="filenav">
