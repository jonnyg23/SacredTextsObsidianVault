 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_119.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>119.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo ittaqoo All<u>a</u>ha wakoonoo maAAa a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>diqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>119.  O ye who believe! Fear God<br> And be with those<br> Who are true (in word and deed).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_120.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>120.  M<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>na li-ahli almadeenati waman <u>h</u>awlahum mina al-aAAr<u>a</u>bi an yatakhallafoo AAan rasooli All<u>a</u>hi wal<u>a</u> yarghaboo bi-anfusihim AAan nafsihi <u>tha</u>lika bi-annahum l<u>a</u> yu<u>s</u>eebuhum <i><u>th</u></i>amaon wal<u>a</u> na<u>s</u>abun wal<u>a</u> makhma<u>s</u>atun fee sabeeli All<u>a</u>hi wal<u>a</u> ya<u>t</u>aoona maw<u>t</u>i-an yaghee<i><u>th</u></i>u alkuff<u>a</u>ra wal<u>a</u> yan<u>a</u>loona min AAaduwwin naylan ill<u>a</u> kutiba lahum bihi AAamalun <u>sa</u>li<u>h</u>un inna All<u>a</u>ha l<u>a</u> yu<u>d</u>eeAAu ajra almu<u>h</u>sineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>120.  It was not fitting<br> For the people of Medina<br> And the Bedouin Arabs<br> Of the neighbourhood, to refuse<br> To follow God's Apostle,<br> Nor to prefer their own lives<br> To his: because nothing<br> Could they suffer or do,<br> But was reckoned to their credit<br> As a deed of righteousness,—<br> Whether they suffered thirst,<br> Or fatigue, or hunger, in the Cause<br> Of God, or trod paths<br> To raise the ire of the Unbelievers,<br> Or received any injury<br>Whatever from an enemy:<br> For God suffereth not<br> The reward to be lost<br> Of those who do good;—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_121.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>121.  Wal<u>a</u> yunfiqoona nafaqatan <u>s</u>agheeratan wal<u>a</u> kabeeratan wal<u>a</u> yaq<u>t</u>aAAoona w<u>a</u>diyan ill<u>a</u> kutiba lahum liyajziyahumu All<u>a</u>hu a<u>h</u>sana m<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo yaAAmaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>121.  </p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_122.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>122.  Wam<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>na almu/minoona liyanfiroo k<u>a</u>ffatan falawl<u>a</u> nafara min kulli firqatin minhum <u>ta</u>-ifatun liyatafaqqahoo fee a<b>l</b>ddeeni waliyun<u>th</u>iroo qawmahum i<u>tha</u> rajaAAoo ilayhim laAAallahum ya<u>hth</u>aroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>122.  Nor should the Believers<br> All go forth together:<br> If a contingent<br> From every expedition<br> Remained behind,<br> They could devote themselves<br> To studies in religion,<br> And admonish the people<br> When they return to them,—<br> That thus they (may learn)<br> To guard themselves (against evil)</p> <div class="filenav">
