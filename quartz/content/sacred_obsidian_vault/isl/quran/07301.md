 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra LXXIII.: Muzzammil, or Folded in Garments.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/073/073_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> almuzzammil<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>1.  O thou folded<br> In garments!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/073/073_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  Qumi allayla ill<u>a</u> qaleel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>2.  Stand (to prayer) by night,<br> But not all night,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/073/073_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  Ni<u>s</u>fahu awi onqu<u>s</u> minhu qaleel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>3.  Half of it,—<br> Or a little less,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/073/073_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  Aw zid AAalayhi warattili alqur-<u>a</u>na tarteel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>4.  Or a little more;<br> And recite the Qur-ān<br> In slow, measured rhythmic tones.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/073/073_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  Inn<u>a</u> sanulqee AAalayka qawlan thaqeel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>5.  Soon shall We send down<br> To thee a weighty Message.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/073/073_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  Inna n<u>a</u>shi-ata allayli hiya ashaddu wa<u>t</u>-an waaqwamu qeel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>6.  Truly the rising by night<br> Is most potent for governing<br> (The soul), and most suitable<br> For (framing) the Word<br> (Of Prayer and Praise).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/073/073_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  Inna laka fee a<b>l</b>nnah<u>a</u>ri sab<u>h</u>an <u>t</u>aweel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>7.  True, there is for thee<br> By day prolonged occupation<br> With ordinary duties:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/073/073_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  Wa<b>o</b><u>th</u>kuri isma rabbika watabattal ilayhi tabteel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>8.  But keep in remembrance<br> The name of thy Lord<br> And devote thyself<br> To Him whole-heartedly.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/073/073_009.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>9.  Rabbu almashriqi wa<b>a</b>lmaghribi l<u>a</u> il<u>a</u>ha ill<u>a</u> huwa fa<b>i</b>ttakhi<u>th</u>hu wakeel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>9.  (He is) Lord of the East<br> And the West: there is<br> No god but He:<br> Take Him therefore<br> For (thy) Disposer of Affairs.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/073/073_010.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>10.  Wa<b>i</b><u>s</u>bir AAal<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> yaqooloona wa<b>o</b>hjurhum hajran jameel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>10.  And have patience with what<br> They say, and leave them<br> With noble (dignity).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/073/073_011.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>11.  Wa<u>th</u>arnee wa<b>a</b>lmuka<u>thth</u>ibeena olee a<b>l</b>nnaAAmati wamahhilhum qaleel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>11.  And leave Me<br> (Alone to deal with)<br> Those in possession of<br> The good things of life,<br> Who (yet) deny the Truth;<br> And bear with them<br> For a little while.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/073/073_012.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>12.  Inna ladayn<u>a</u> ank<u>a</u>lan waja<u>h</u>eem<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>12.  With Us are Fetters<br>(To bind them), and a Fire<br> (To burn them),</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/073/073_013.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>13.  Wa<u>t</u>aAA<u>a</u>man <u>tha</u> ghu<u>ss</u>atin waAAa<u>tha</u>ban aleem<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>13.  And a Food that chokes,<br> And a Penalty Grievous.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/073/073_014.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>14.  Yawma tarjufu al-ar<u>d</u>u wa<b>a</b>ljib<u>a</u>lu wak<u>a</u>nati aljib<u>a</u>lu katheeban maheel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>14.  One Day the earth<br> And the mountains<br> Will be in violent commotion.<br> And the mountains will be<br> As a heap of sand<br> Poured out and flowing down.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/073/073_015.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>15.  Inn<u>a</u> arsaln<u>a</u> ilaykum rasoolan sh<u>a</u>hidan AAalaykum kam<u>a</u> arsaln<u>a</u> il<u>a</u> firAAawna rasool<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>15.  We have sent to you,<br> (O men!) an apostle,<br> To be a witness concerning you,<br> Even as We sent<br> An apostle to Pharaoh.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/073/073_016.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>16.  FaAAa<u>sa</u> firAAawnu a<b>l</b>rrasoola faakha<u>th</u>n<u>a</u>hu akh<u>th</u>an wabeel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>16.  But Pharaoh disobeyed<br> The apostle; so We<br> Seized him with<br> A heavy Punishment.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/073/073_017.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>17.  Fakayfa tattaqoona in kafartum yawman yajAAalu alwild<u>a</u>na sheeb<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>17.  Then how shall ye,<br> If ye deny (God),<br> Guard yourselves against<br> A Day that will make<br> Children hoary-headed?—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/073/073_018.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>18.  A<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>o munfa<u>t</u>irun bihi k<u>a</u>na waAAduhu mafAAool<u>a</u><b>n</b>    </p>
 <p>18.  Whereon the sky will be<br> Cleft asunder?<br> His Promise needs must<br> Be accomplished.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/073/073_019.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>19.  Inna h<u>ath</u>ihi ta<u>th</u>kiratun faman sh<u>a</u>a ittakha<u>th</u>a il<u>a</u> rabbihi sabeel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>19.  Verily this is an Admonition:<br> Therefore, whoso will, let him<br> Take a (straight) path<br> To his Lord!</p> <div class="filenav">
