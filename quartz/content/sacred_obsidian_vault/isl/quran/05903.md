 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra LIX.: Ḥa<u>sh</u>r, or The Gathering</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 3</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/059/059_018.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>18.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo ittaqoo All<u>a</u>ha waltan<i><u>th</u></i>ur nafsun m<u>a</u> qaddamat lighadin wa<b>i</b>ttaqoo All<u>a</u>ha inna All<u>a</u>ha khabeerun bim<u>a</u> taAAmaloon<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>18.  O ye who believe!<br> Fear God,<br>And let every soul look<br> To what (provision) he has<br> Sent forth for the morrow.<br> Yea, fear God:<br> For God is well-acquainted<br> With (all) that ye do.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/059/059_019.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>19.  Wal<u>a</u> takoonoo ka<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena nasoo All<u>a</u>ha faans<u>a</u>hum anfusahum ol<u>a</u>-ika humu alf<u>a</u>siqoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>19.  And be ye not like<br> Those who forgot God;<br> And He made them forget<br> Their own souls! Such<br> Are the rebellious transgressors!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/059/059_020.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>20.  L<u>a</u> yastawee a<u>s</u>-<u>ha</u>bu a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>ri waa<u>s</u>-<u>ha</u>bu aljannati a<u>s</u>-<u>ha</u>bu aljannati humu alf<u>a</u>-izoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>20.  Not equal are<br> The Companions of the Fire<br> And the Companions<br> Of the Garden:<br> It is the Companions<br> Of the Garden,<br> That will achieve Felicity.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/059/059_021.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>21.  Law anzaln<u>a</u> h<u>atha</u> alqur-<u>a</u>na AAal<u>a</u> jabalin laraaytahu kh<u>a</u>shiAAan muta<u>s</u>addiAAan min khashyati All<u>a</u>hi watilka al-amth<u>a</u>lu na<u>d</u>ribuh<u>a</u> li<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si laAAallahum yatafakkaroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>21.  Had We sent down<br> This Qur-ān on a mountain,<br> Verily, thou wouldst have seen<br> It humble itself and cleave<br>Asunder for fear of God.<br> Such are the similitudes<br> Which We propound to men,<br> That they may reflect.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/059/059_022.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>22.  Huwa All<u>a</u>hu alla<u>th</u>ee l<u>a</u> il<u>a</u>ha ill<u>a</u> huwa AA<u>a</u>limu alghaybi wa<b>al</b>shshah<u>a</u>dati huwa a<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>m<u>a</u>nu a<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>eem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>22.  God is He, than Whom<br> There is no other god;—<br> Who knows (all things)<br> Both secret and open;<br> He, Most Gracious,<br> Most Merciful.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/059/059_023.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>23.  Huwa All<u>a</u>hu alla<u>th</u>ee l<u>a</u> il<u>a</u>ha ill<u>a</u> huwa almaliku alquddoosu a<b>l</b>ssal<u>a</u>mu almu/minu almuhayminu alAAazeezu aljabb<u>a</u>ru almutakabbiru sub<u>ha</u>na All<u>a</u>hi AAamm<u>a</u> yushrikoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>23.  God is He, than Whom<br> There is no other god;—<br> The Sovereign, the Holy One,<br> The Source of Peace<br> (and Perfection),<br> The Guardian of Faith,<br> The Preserver of Safety,<br> The Exalted in Might,<br> The Irresistible, the Supreme:<br>Glory to God!<br> (High is He)<br> Above the partners<br> They attribute to Him.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/059/059_024.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>24.  Huwa All<u>a</u>hu alkh<u>a</u>liqu alb<u>a</u>ri-o almu<u>s</u>awwiru lahu al-asm<u>a</u>o al<u>h</u>usn<u>a</u> yusabbi<u>h</u>u lahu m<u>a</u> fee a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i wahuwa alAAazeezu al<u>h</u>akeem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>24.  He is God, the Creator,<br> The Evolver,<br> The Bestower of Forms<br> (Or Colours).<br> To Him belongs<br> The Most Beautiful Names:<br> Whatever is in<br> The heavens and on earth,<br> Doth declare<br> His Praises and Glory:<br> And He is the Exalted<br> In Might, the Wise.</p>  <div class="filenav">
