 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXXIX.: Zumar, or the Crowds.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 7</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/039/039_064.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>64.  Qul afaghayra All<u>a</u>hi ta/muroonnee aAAbudu ayyuh<u>a</u> alj<u>a</u>hiloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>64.  Say: "Is it<br> Some one other than God<br> That ye order me<br> To worship, O ye<br> Ignorant ones?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/039/039_065.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>65.  Walaqad oo<u>h</u>iya ilayka wa-il<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena min qablika la-in ashrakta laya<u>h</u>ba<u>t</u>anna AAamaluka walatakoonanna mina alkh<u>a</u>sireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>65.  But it has already<br> Been revealed to thee,—<br> As it was to those<br> Before thee,—"If thou<br> Wert to join (gods<br> With God), truly fruitless<br> Will be thy work (in life),<br> And thou wilt surely<br> Be in the ranks of those<br> Who lose (all spiritual good)".</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/039/039_066.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>66.  Bali All<u>a</u>ha fa<b>o</b>AAbud wakun mina a<b>l</b>shsh<u>a</u>kireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>66.  Nay, but worship God,<br> And be of those who<br> Give thanks.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/039/039_067.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>67.  Wam<u>a</u> qadaroo All<u>a</u>ha <u>h</u>aqqa qadrihi wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>u jameeAAan qab<u>d</u>atuhu yawma alqiy<u>a</u>mati wa<b>al</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>tu ma<u>t</u>wiyy<u>a</u>tun biyameenihi sub<u>ha</u>nahu wataAA<u>a</u>l<u>a</u> AAamm<u>a</u> yushrikoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>67.  No just estimate<br> Have they made of God,<br> Such as is due to Him:<br> On the Day of Judgment<br> The whole of the earth<br> Will be but His handful,<br> And the heavens will be<br>Rolled up in His right hand:<br> Glory to Him!<br> High is He above<br> The Partners they attribute<br> To Him!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/039/039_068.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>68.  Wanufikha fee a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>oori fa<u>s</u>aAAiqa man fee a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti waman fee al-ar<u>d</u>i ill<u>a</u> man sh<u>a</u>a All<u>a</u>hu thumma nufikha feehi okhr<u>a</u> fa-i<u>tha</u> hum qiy<u>a</u>mun yan<i><u>th</u></i>uroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>68.  The Trumpet will (just)<br> Be sounded, when all<br> That are in the heavens<br> And on earth will swoon,<br> Except such as it will<br> Please God (to exempt).<br> Then will a second one<br> Be sounded, when, behold,<br> They will be standing<br> And looking on!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/039/039_069.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>69.  Waashraqati al-ar<u>d</u>u binoori rabbih<u>a</u> wawu<u>d</u>iAAa alkit<u>a</u>bu wajee-a bi<b>al</b>nnabiyyeena wa<b>al</b>shshuhad<u>a</u>-i waqu<u>d</u>iya baynahum bi<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>aqqi wahum l<u>a</u> yu<i><u>th</u></i>lamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>69.  And the Earth will shine<br> With the glory of its Lord:<br> The Record (of Deeds)<br> Will be placed (open);<br> The prophets and the witnesses<br> Will be brought forward;<br> And a just decision<br> Pronounced between them;<br> And they will not<br> Be wronged (in the least).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/039/039_070.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>70.  Wawuffiyat kullu nafsin m<u>a</u> AAamilat wahuwa aAAlamu bim<u>a</u> yafAAaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>70.  And to every soul will be<br> Paid in full (the fruit)<br>Of its deeds; and (God)<br> Knoweth best all that<br> They do.</p> <div class="filenav">
