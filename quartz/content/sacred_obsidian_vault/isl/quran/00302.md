 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra III.: Āl-i-’Imrān, or The Family of ’Imrān.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 2</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_010.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>10.  Inna alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo lan tughniya AAanhum amw<u>a</u>luhum wal<u>a</u> awl<u>a</u>duhum mina All<u>a</u>hi shay-an waol<u>a</u>-ika hum waqoodu a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>r<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>10.  Whose who reject Faith,—<br> Neither their possessions<br> Nor their (numerous) progeny<br> Will avail them aught<br> Against God: they are themselves<br> But fuel for the Fire.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_011.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>11.  Kada/bi <u>a</u>li firAAawna wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena min qablihim ka<u>thth</u>aboo bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin<u>a</u> faakha<u>th</u>ahumu All<u>a</u>hu bi<u>th</u>unoobihim wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu shadeedu alAAiq<u>a</u>b<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>11.  (Their plight will be)<br> No better than that<br> Of the people of Pharaoh,<br> And their predecessors:<br> They denied our Signs,<br> And God called them to account<br> For their sins.<br> For God is strict<br> In punishment.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_012.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>12.  Qul lilla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo satughlaboona watu<u>h</u>sharoona il<u>a</u> jahannama wabi/sa almih<u>a</u>d<b>u</b>    </p>
 <p>12.  Say to those who reject Faith:<br> "Soon will ye be vanquished<br> And gathered together<br> To Hell,—an evil bed<br> Indeed (to lie on)!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_013.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>13.  Qad k<u>a</u>na lakum <u>a</u>yatun fee fi-atayni iltaqat<u>a</u> fi-atun tuq<u>a</u>tilu fee sabeeli All<u>a</u>hi waokhr<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>firatun yarawnahum mithlayhim ra/ya alAAayni wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu yu-ayyidu bina<u>s</u>rihi man yash<u>a</u>o inna fee <u>tha</u>lika laAAibratan li-olee al-ab<u>sa</u>r<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>13.  "There has already been<br> For you a Sign<br>In the two armies<br> That met (in combat):<br> One was fighting in the Cause<br> Of God, the other<br> Resisting God; these saw<br> With their own eyes<br> Twice their number.<br> But God doth support<br> With His aid whom He pleaseth.<br> In this is a warning<br> For such as have eyes to see."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_014.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>14.  Zuyyina li<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si <u>h</u>ubbu a<b>l</b>shshahaw<u>a</u>ti mina a<b>l</b>nnis<u>a</u>-i wa<b>a</b>lbaneena wa<b>a</b>lqan<u>at</u>eeri almuqan<u>t</u>arati mina a<b>l</b><u>thth</u>ahabi wa<b>a</b>lfi<u>dd</u>ati wa<b>a</b>lkhayli almusawwamati wa<b>a</b>l-anAA<u>a</u>mi wa<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>arthi <u>tha</u>lika mat<u>a</u>AAu al<u>h</u>ay<u>a</u>ti a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu AAindahu <u>h</u>usnu alma<u>a</u>b<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>14.  Fair in the eyes of men<br> Is the love of things they covet:<br> Women and sons;<br> Heaped-up hoards<br> Of gold and silver; horses<br> Branded (for blood and excellence);<br> And (wealth of) cattle<br> And well-tilled land.<br> Such are the possessions<br> Of this world's life;<br> But in nearness to God<br> Is the best of the goals<br> (To return to).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_015.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>15.  Qul aonabbi-okum bikhayrin min <u>tha</u>likum lilla<u>th</u>eena ittaqaw AAinda rabbihim jann<u>a</u>tun tajree min ta<u>h</u>tih<u>a</u> al-anh<u>a</u>ru kh<u>a</u>lideena feeh<u>a</u> waazw<u>a</u>jun mu<u>t</u>ahharatun wari<u>d</u>w<u>a</u>nun mina All<u>a</u>hi wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu ba<u>s</u>eerun bi<b>a</b>lAAib<u>a</u>d<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>15.  Say: Shall I give you<br> Glad tidings of things<br> Far better than those?<br> For the righteous are Gardens<br>In nearness to their Lord,<br> With rivers flowing beneath;<br> Therein is their eternal home;<br> With Companions pure (and holy);<br> And the good pleasure of God.<br> For in God's sight<br> Are (all) His servants,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_016.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>16.  Alla<u>th</u>eena yaqooloona rabban<u>a</u> innan<u>a</u> <u>a</u>mann<u>a</u> fa<b>i</b>ghfir lan<u>a</u> <u>th</u>unooban<u>a</u> waqin<u>a</u> AAa<u>tha</u>ba a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>r<b>i</b>    </p>
 <p>16.  (Namely), those who say:<br> "Our Lord! we have indeed<br> Believed: forgive us, then,<br> Our sins, and save us<br> From the agony of the Fire;"—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_017.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>17.  A<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>bireena wa<b>al</b><u>ssa</u>diqeena wa<b>a</b>lq<u>a</u>niteena wa<b>a</b>lmunfiqeena wa<b>a</b>lmustaghfireena bi<b>a</b>l-as<u>ha</u>r<b>i</b>    </p>
 <p>17.  Those who show patience,<br> Firmness and self control;<br> Who are true (in word and deed);<br> Who worship devoutly;<br> Who spend (in the way of God);<br> And who pray for forgiveness<br> In the early hours of the morning.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_018.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>18.  Shahida All<u>a</u>hu annahu l<u>a</u> il<u>a</u>ha ill<u>a</u> huwa wa<b>a</b>lmal<u>a</u>-ikatu waoloo alAAilmi q<u>a</u>-iman bi<b>a</b>lqis<u>t</u>i l<u>a</u> il<u>a</u>ha ill<u>a</u> huwa alAAazeezu al<u>h</u>akeem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>18.  There is no god but He<br> That is the witness of God,<br> His angels, and those endued<br> With knowledge, standing firm<br> On justice. There is no god but He,<br> The Exalted in Power,<br> The Wise.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_019.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>19.  Inna a<b>l</b>ddeena AAinda All<u>a</u>hi al-isl<u>a</u>mu wam<u>a</u> ikhtalafa alla<u>th</u>eena ootoo alkit<u>a</u>ba ill<u>a</u> min baAAdi m<u>a</u> j<u>a</u>ahumu alAAilmu baghyan baynahum waman yakfur bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>ti All<u>a</u>hi fa-inna All<u>a</u>ha sareeAAu al<u>h</u>is<u>a</u>b<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>19.  The Religion before God<br> Is Islam (submission to His Will):<br> Nor did the People of the Book<br> Dissent therefrom except<br>Through envy of each other,<br> After knowledge had come to them.<br> But if any deny the Signs of God,<br> God is swift in calling to account.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_020.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>20.  Fa-in <u>ha</u>jjooka faqul aslamtu wajhiya lill<u>a</u>hi wamani ittabaAAani waqul lilla<u>th</u>eena ootoo alkit<u>a</u>ba wa<b>a</b>l<b>-</b>ommiyyeena aaslamtum fa-in aslamoo faqadi ihtadaw wa-in tawallaw fa-innam<u>a</u> AAalayka albal<u>a</u>ghu wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu ba<u>s</u>eerun bi<b>a</b>lAAib<u>a</u>d<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>20.  So if they dispute with thee,<br> Say: "I have submitted<br> My whole self to God<br> And so have those<br> Who follow me."<br> And say to the People of the Book<br> And to those who are unlearned:<br> "Do ye (also) submit yourselves?"<br> If they do, they are in right guidance,<br> But if they turn back,<br> Thy duty is to convey the Message;<br> And in God's sight<br> Are (all) His servants.</p> <div class="filenav">
