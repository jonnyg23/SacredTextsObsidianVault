 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra LXX.: Ma‘ārij, or the Ways of Ascent.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 2</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/070/070_036.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>36.  Fam<u>a</u>li alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo qibalaka muh<u>t</u>iAAeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>36.  Now what is<br> The matter with the Unbelievers<br> That they rush madly<br> Before thee—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/070/070_037.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>37.  AAani alyameeni waAAani a<b>l</b>shshim<u>a</u>li AAizeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>37.  From the right<br> And from the left,<br> In crowds?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/070/070_038.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>38.  Aya<u>t</u>maAAu kullu imri-in minhum an yudkhala jannata naAAeem<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>38.  Does every man of them<br> Long to enter<br> The Garden of Bliss?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/070/070_039.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>39.  Kall<u>a</u> inn<u>a</u> khalaqn<u>a</u>hum mimm<u>a</u> yaAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>39.  By no means!<br> For We have created them<br> Out of the (base matter)<br> They know!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/070/070_040.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>40.  Fal<u>a</u> oqsimu birabbi almash<u>a</u>riqi wa<b>a</b>lmagh<u>a</u>ribi inn<u>a</u> laq<u>a</u>diroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>40.  Now I do<br> Call to witness<br>The Lord of all points<br> In the East and the West<br> That We can certainly—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/070/070_041.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>41.  AAal<u>a</u> an nubaddila khayran minhum wam<u>a</u> na<u>h</u>nu bimasbooqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>41.  Substitute for them<br> Better (men) than they;<br> And We are not<br> To be defeated<br> (In Our Plan).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/070/070_042.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>42.  Fa<u>th</u>arhum yakhoo<u>d</u>oo wayalAAaboo <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> yul<u>a</u>qoo yawmahumu alla<u>th</u>ee yooAAadoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>42.  So leave them<br> To plunge in vain talk<br> And play about,<br> Until they encounter<br> That Day of theirs which<br> They have been promised!—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/070/070_043.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>43.  Yawma yakhrujoona mina al-ajd<u>a</u>thi sir<u>a</u>AAan kaannahum il<u>a</u> nu<u>s</u>ubin yoofi<u>d</u>oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>43.  The Day whereon<br> They will issue<br> From their sepulchres<br> In sudden haste<br> As if they were<br> Rushing to a goal-post<br> (Fixed for them),—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/070/070_044.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>44.  Kh<u>a</u>shiAAatan ab<u>sa</u>ruhum tarhaquhum <u>th</u>illatun <u>tha</u>lika alyawmu alla<u>th</u>ee k<u>a</u>noo yooAAadoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>44.  Their eyes lowered<br> In dejection,<br> Ignominy covering them<br> (All over)!<br> Such is the Day<br> The which they<br> Are promised!</p>  <div class="filenav">
