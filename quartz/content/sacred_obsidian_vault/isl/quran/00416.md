 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra IV.: Nisāa, or The Women.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 16</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_105.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>105.  Inn<u>a</u> anzaln<u>a</u> ilayka alkit<u>a</u>ba bi<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>aqqi lita<u>h</u>kuma bayna a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si bim<u>a</u> ar<u>a</u>ka All<u>a</u>hu wal<u>a</u> takun lilkh<u>a</u>-ineena kha<u>s</u>eem<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>105.  We have sent down<br> To thee the Book in truth,<br> That thou might test judge<br> Between men, as guided<br> By God: so be not (used)<br> As an advocate by those<br> Who betray their trust;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_106.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>106.  Wa<b>i</b>staghfiri All<u>a</u>ha inna All<u>a</u>ha k<u>a</u>na ghafooran ra<u>h</u>eem<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>106.  But seek the forgiveness<br> Of God; for God is<br> Oft-forgiving, Most Merciful.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_107.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>107.  Wal<u>a</u> tuj<u>a</u>dil AAani alla<u>th</u>eena yakht<u>a</u>noona anfusahum inna All<u>a</u>ha l<u>a</u> yu<u>h</u>ibbu man k<u>a</u>na khaww<u>a</u>nan atheem<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>107.  Contend not on behalf<br> Of such as betray<br> Their own souls;<br> For God loveth not<br> One given to perfidy<br> And crime;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_108.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>108.  Yastakhfoona mina a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si wal<u>a</u> yastakhfoona mina All<u>a</u>hi wahuwa maAAahum i<u>th</u> yubayyitoona m<u>a</u> l<u>a</u> yar<u>da</u> mina alqawli wak<u>a</u>na All<u>a</u>hu bim<u>a</u> yaAAmaloona mu<u>h</u>ee<u>ta</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>108.  They may hide<br> (Their crimes) from men,<br> But they cannot hide<br> (Them) from God, seeing that<br> He is in their midst<br> When they plot by night,<br> In words that He cannot<br> Approve: and God<br> Doth compass round<br> All that they do.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_109.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>109.  H<u>a</u>antum h<u>a</u>ol<u>a</u>-i j<u>a</u>daltum AAanhum fee al<u>h</u>ay<u>a</u>ti a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> faman yuj<u>a</u>dilu All<u>a</u>ha AAanhum yawma alqiy<u>a</u>mati am man yakoonu AAalayhim wakeel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>109.  Ah! these are the sort<br> Of men on whose behalf<br> Ye may contend in this world;<br> But who will contend with God<br> On their behalf on the Day<br> Of Judgment, or who<br> Will carry their affairs through?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_110.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>110.  Waman yaAAmal soo-an aw ya<i><u>th</u></i>lim nafsahu thumma yastaghfiri All<u>a</u>ha yajidi All<u>a</u>ha ghafooran ra<u>h</u>eem<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>110.  If any one does evil<br> Or wrongs his own soul<br> But afterwards seeks<br> God's forgiveness, he will find<br> God Oft-forgiving,<br> Most Merciful.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_111.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>111.  Waman yaksib ithman fa-innam<u>a</u> yaksibuhu AAal<u>a</u> nafsihi wak<u>a</u>na All<u>a</u>hu AAaleeman <u>h</u>akeem<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>111.  And if any one earns<br> Sin, he earns it against<br>His own soul: for God<br> Is full of knowledge and wisdom.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_112.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>112.  Waman yaksib kha<u>t</u>ee-atan aw ithman thumma yarmi bihi baree-an faqadi i<u>h</u>tamala buht<u>a</u>nan wa-ithman mubeen<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>112.  But if any one earns<br> A fault or a sin<br> And throws it on to one<br> That is innocent,<br> He carries (on himself)<br> (Both) a falsehood<br> And a flagrant sin.</p> <div class="filenav">
