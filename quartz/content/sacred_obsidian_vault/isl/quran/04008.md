 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XL.: Mū-min, or The Believer.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 8</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/040/040_069.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>69.  Alam tara il<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena yuj<u>a</u>diloona fee <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>ti All<u>a</u>hi ann<u>a</u> yu<u>s</u>rafoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>69.  Seest thou not those<br> That dispute concerning<br> The Signs of God?<br> How are they turned away<br> (From Reality)?—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/040/040_070.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>70.  Alla<u>th</u>eena ka<u>thth</u>aboo bi<b>a</b>lkit<u>a</u>bi wabim<u>a</u> arsaln<u>a</u> bihi rusulan<u>a</u> fasawfa yaAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>70.  Those who reject the Book<br> And the (revelations) with which<br> We sent Our apostles:<br> But soon shall they know,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/040/040_071.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>71.  I<u>th</u>i al-aghl<u>a</u>lu fee aAAn<u>a</u>qihim wa<b>al</b>ssal<u>a</u>silu yus<u>h</u>aboon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>71.  When the yokes (shall be)<br> Round their necks,<br> And the chains;<br> They shall be dragged along—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/040/040_072.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>72.  Fee al<u>h</u>ameemi thumma fee a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>ri yusjaroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>72.  In the boiling fetid fluid<br> Then in the Fire<br> Shall they be burned;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/040/040_073.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>73.  Thumma qeela lahum ayna m<u>a</u> kuntum tushrikoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>73.  Then shall it be said<br> To them: "Where are<br> The (deities) to which<br> Ye gave part-worship—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/040/040_074.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>74.  Min dooni All<u>a</u>hi q<u>a</u>loo <u>d</u>alloo AAann<u>a</u> bal lam nakun nadAAoo min qablu shay-an ka<u>tha</u>lika yu<u>d</u>illu All<u>a</u>hu alk<u>a</u>fireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>74.  "In derogation of God?"<br> They will reply: "They have<br> Left us in the lurch:<br> Nay, we invoked not,<br> Of old, anything (that had<br> Real existence)." Thus<br> Does God leave<br> The Unbelievers to stray.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/040/040_075.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>75.  <u>Tha</u>likum bim<u>a</u> kuntum tafra<u>h</u>oona fee al-ar<u>d</u>i bighayri al<u>h</u>aqqi wabim<u>a</u> kuntum tamra<u>h</u>oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>75.  "That was because<br> Ye were wont to rejoice<br> On the earth in things<br> Other than the Truth,<br> And that ye were wont<br> To be insolent.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/040/040_076.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>76.  Odkhuloo abw<u>a</u>ba jahannama kh<u>a</u>lideena feeh<u>a</u> fabi/sa mathw<u>a</u> almutakabbireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>76.  "Enter ye the gates<br> Of Hell, to dwell therein:<br> And evil is (this) abode<br> Of the arrogant!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/040/040_077.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>77.  Fa<b>i</b><u>s</u>bir inna waAAda All<u>a</u>hi <u>h</u>aqqun fa-imm<u>a</u> nuriyannaka baAA<u>d</u>a alla<u>th</u>ee naAAiduhum aw natawaffayannaka fa-ilayn<u>a</u> yurjaAAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>77.  So persevere in patience;<br> For the Promise of God<br> Is true: and whether<br> We show thee (in this life)<br> Some part of what We<br> Promise them,—or We<br> Take thy soul (to Our Mercy)<br> (Before that),—(in any case)<br> It is to Us that<br> They shall (all) return.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/040/040_078.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>78.  Walaqad arsaln<u>a</u> rusulan min qablika minhum man qa<u>s</u>a<u>s</u>n<u>a</u> AAalayka waminhum man lam naq<u>s</u>u<u>s</u> AAalayka wam<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>na lirasoolin an ya/tiya bi-<u>a</u>yatin ill<u>a</u> bi-i<u>th</u>ni All<u>a</u>hi fa-i<u>tha</u> j<u>a</u>a amru All<u>a</u>hi qu<u>d</u>iya bi<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>aqqi wakhasira hun<u>a</u>lika almub<u>t</u>iloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>78.  We did aforetime send<br> Apostles before thee: of them<br> There are some whose story<br> We have related to thee,<br> And some whose story<br> We have not related<br> To thee. It was not<br> (Possible) for any apostle<br> To bring a Sign except<br> By the leave of God:<br> But when the Command<br> Of God issued,<br> The matter was decided<br> In truth and justice,<br> And there perished,<br> There and then, those<br> Who stood on Falsehoods.</p> <div class="filenav">
