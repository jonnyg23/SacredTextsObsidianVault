 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra X.: Yūnus, or Jonah.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 10</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/010/010_093.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>93.  Walaqad bawwa/n<u>a</u> banee isr<u>a</u>-eela mubawwaa <u>s</u>idqin warazaqn<u>a</u>hum mina a<b>l</b><u>tt</u>ayyib<u>a</u>ti fam<u>a</u> ikhtalafoo <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> j<u>a</u>ahumu alAAilmu inna rabbaka yaq<u>d</u>ee baynahum yawma alqiy<u>a</u>mati feem<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo feehi yakhtalifoon<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>93.  We settled the Children<br> Of Israel in a beautiful<br> Dwelling-place, and provided<br> For them sustenance of the best:<br> It was after knowledge had been<br> Granted to them, that they<br> Fell into schisms. Verily<br> God will judge between them<br> As to the schisms amongst them,<br> On the Day of Judgment.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/010/010_094.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>94.  Fa-in kunta fee shakkin mimm<u>a</u> anzalnn<u>a</u> ilayka fa<b>i</b>s-ali alla<u>th</u>eena yaqraoona alkit<u>a</u>ba min qablika laqad j<u>a</u>aka al<u>h</u>aqqu min rabbika fal<u>a</u> takoonanna mina almumtareen<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>94.  If thou wert in doubt<br> As to what We have revealed<br> Unto thee, then ask those<br> Who have been reading<br> The Book from before thee:<br> The Truth hath indeed come<br> To thee from thy Lord:<br> So be in no wise<br> Of those in doubt.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/010/010_095.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>95.  Wal<u>a</u> takoonanna mina alla<u>th</u>eena ka<u>thth</u>aboo bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>ti All<u>a</u>hi fatakoona mina alkh<u>a</u>sireen<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>95.  Nor be of those who reject<br> The Signs of God,<br> Or thou shalt be of those<br> Who perish.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/010/010_096.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>96.  Inna alla<u>th</u>eena <u>h</u>aqqat AAalayhim kalimatu rabbika l<u>a</u> yu/minoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>96.  Whose against whom the Word<br> Of thy Lord hath been verified<br>Would not believe—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/010/010_097.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>97.  Walaw j<u>a</u>at-hum kullu <u>a</u>yatin <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> yarawoo alAAa<u>tha</u>ba al-aleem<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>97.  Even if every Sign was brought<br> Unto them,—until they see<br> (For themselves) the Penalty Grievous.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/010/010_098.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>98.  Falawl<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>nat qaryatun <u>a</u>manat fanafaAAah<u>a</u> eem<u>a</u>nuh<u>a</u> ill<u>a</u> qawma yoonusa lamm<u>a</u> <u>a</u>manoo kashafn<u>a</u> AAanhum AAa<u>tha</u>ba alkhizyi fee al<u>h</u>ay<u>a</u>ti a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> wamattaAAn<u>a</u>hum il<u>a</u> <u>h</u>een<b>in</b>    </p>
 <p>98.  Why was there not<br> A single township (among those<br> We warned), which believed,—<br> So its Faith should have<br> Profited it,—except the People<br> Of Jonah? When they believed,<br> We removed from them<br> The Penalty of Ignominy<br> In the life of the Present,<br> And permitted them to enjoy<br> (Their life) for a while.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/010/010_099.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>99.  Walaw sh<u>a</u>a rabbuka la<u>a</u>mana man fee al-ar<u>d</u>i kulluhum jameeAAan afaanta tukrihu a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>sa <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> yakoonoo mu/mineen<b>a </b>   </p>
 <p>99.  If it had been thy Lord's Will,<br> They would all have believed,—<br> All who are on earth!<br> Wilt thou then compel mankind,<br>Against their will, to believe!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/010/010_100.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>100.  Wam<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>na linafsin an tu/mina ill<u>a</u> bi-i<u>th</u>ni All<u>a</u>hi wayajAAalu a<b>l</b>rrijsa AAal<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena l<u>a</u> yaAAqiloon<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>100.  No soul can believe, except<br> By the Will of God,<br> And He will place Doubt<br> (Or obscurity) on those<br> Who will not understand.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/010/010_101.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>101.  Quli on<i><u>th</u></i>uroo m<u>atha</u> fee a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i wam<u>a</u> tughnee al-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tu wa<b>al</b>nnu<u>th</u>uru AAan qawmin l<u>a</u> yu/minoon<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>101.  Say: "Behold all that is<br> In the heavens and on earth ";<br> But neither Signs nor Warners<br> Profit those who believe not.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/010/010_102.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>102.  Fahal yanta<i><u>th</u></i>iroona ill<u>a</u> mithla ayy<u>a</u>mi alla<u>th</u>eena khalaw min qablihim qul fa<b>i</b>nta<i><u>th</u></i>iroo innee maAAakum mina almunta<i><u>th</u></i>ireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>102.  Do they then expect<br> (Any thing) but (what happened<br> In) the days of the men<br> Who passed away before them?<br> Say: "Wait ye then:<br> For I, too, will wait with you."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/010/010_103.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>103.  Thumma nunajjee rusulan<u>a</u> wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo ka<u>tha</u>lika <u>h</u>aqqan AAalayn<u>a</u> nunjee almu/mineen<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>103.  In the end We deliver<br> Our apostles and those who believe:<br> Thus is it fitting on Our part<br> That We should deliver<br> Those who believe!</p> <div class="filenav">
