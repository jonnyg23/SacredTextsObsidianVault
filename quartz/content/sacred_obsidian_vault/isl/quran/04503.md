 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XLV.: Ja<u>th</u>iya, or Bowing the Knee.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 3</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/045/045_022.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>22.  Wakhalaqa All<u>a</u>hu a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>a bi<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>aqqi walitujz<u>a</u> kullu nafsin bim<u>a</u> kasabat wahum l<u>a</u> yu<i><u>th</u></i>lamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>22.  God created the heavens<br> And the earth for<br> Just ends, and in order<br> That each soul may find<br> The recompense of what<br> It has earned, and none<br> Of them be wronged.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/045/045_023.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>23.  Afaraayta mani ittakha<u>th</u>a il<u>a</u>hahu haw<u>a</u>hu waa<u>d</u>allahu All<u>a</u>hu AAal<u>a</u> AAilmin wakhatama AAal<u>a</u> samAAihi waqalbihi wajaAAala AAal<u>a</u> ba<u>s</u>arihi ghish<u>a</u>watan faman yahdeehi min baAAdi All<u>a</u>hi afal<u>a</u> ta<u>th</u>akkaroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>23.  Then seest thou such<br> A one as takes<br> As his god his own<br> Vain desire? God has,<br> Knowing (him as such),<br> Left him astray, and sealed<br> His hearing and his heart<br> (And understanding), and put<br>A cover on his sight.<br> Who, then, will guide him<br> After God (has withdrawn<br> Guidance)? Will ye not<br> Then receive admonition?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/045/045_024.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>24.  Waq<u>a</u>loo m<u>a</u> hiya ill<u>a</u> <u>h</u>ay<u>a</u>tun<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> namootu wana<u>h</u>y<u>a</u> wam<u>a</u> yuhlikun<u>a</u> ill<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>ddahru wam<u>a</u> lahum bi<u>tha</u>lika min AAilmin in hum ill<u>a</u> ya<i><u>th</u></i>unnoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>24.  And they say: "What is<br> There but our life<br> In this world?<br> We shall die and we live,<br> And nothing but Time<br> Can destroy us." But<br> Of that they have no<br> Knowledge: they merely conjecture:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/045/045_025.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>25.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> tutl<u>a</u> AAalayhim <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tun<u>a</u> bayyin<u>a</u>tin m<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>na <u>h</u>ujjatahum ill<u>a</u> an q<u>a</u>loo i/too bi-<u>a</u>b<u>a</u>-in<u>a</u> in kuntum <u>sa</u>diqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>25.  And when Our Clear<br> Signs are rehearsed to them,<br> Their argument is nothing<br> But this: they say, "Bring<br> (Back) our forefathers, if<br> What ye say is true!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/045/045_026.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>26.  Quli All<u>a</u>hu yu<u>h</u>yeekum thumma yumeetukum thumma yajmaAAukum il<u>a</u> yawmi alqiy<u>a</u>mati l<u>a</u> rayba feehi wal<u>a</u>kinna akthara a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si l<u>a</u> yaAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>26.  Say: "It is God Who<br> Gives you life, then<br> Gives you death; then<br> He will gather you together<br> For the Day of Judgment<br> About which there is<br> No doubt": but most<br> Men do not understand.</p> <div class="filenav">
