 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra VII.: A’rāf, or the Heights</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 18</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_148.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>148.  Wa<b>i</b>ttakha<u>th</u>a qawmu moos<u>a</u> min baAAdihi min <u>h</u>uliyyihim AAijlan jasadan lahu khuw<u>a</u>run alam yaraw annahu l<u>a</u> yukallimuhum wal<u>a </u>yahdeehim sabeelan ittakha<u>th</u>oohu wak<u>a</u>noo <i><u>th</u></i><u>a</u>limeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>148.  The people of Moses made,<br> In his absence, out of their ornaments,<br>The image of a calf,<br> (for worship):<br> It seemed to low: did they<br> Not see that it could<br> Neither speak to them, nor<br> Show them the Way?<br> They took it for worship<br> And they did wrong.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_149.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>149.  Walamm<u>a</u> suqi<u>t</u>a fee aydeehim waraaw annahum qad <u>d</u>alloo q<u>a</u>loo la-in lam yar<u>h</u>amn<u>a</u> rabbun<u>a</u> wayaghfir lan<u>a</u> lanakoonanna mina alkh<u>a</u>sireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>149.  When they repented, and saw<br> That they had erred,<br> They said: "if our Lord<br> Have not mercy upon us<br> And forgive us, we shall<br> Indeed be of those who perish."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_150.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>150.  Walamm<u>a</u> rajaAAa moos<u>a</u> il<u>a</u> qawmihi gha<u>d</u>b<u>a</u>na asifan q<u>a</u>la bi/sam<u>a</u> khalaftumoonee min baAAdee aAAajiltum amra rabbikum waalq<u>a </u>al-alw<u>ah</u>a waakha<u>th</u>a bira/si akheehi yajurruhu ilayhi q<u>a</u>la ibna omma inna alqawma ista<u>d</u>AAafoonee wak<u>a</u>doo yaqtuloonanee fal<u>a</u> tushmit biya al-aAAd<u>a</u>a wal<u>a</u> tajAAalnee maAAa alqawmi a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>150.  When Moses came back<br> To his people, angry and grieved,<br> He said: "Evil it is that ye<br> Have done in my place<br> In my absence: did ye<br> Make haste to bring on<br> The judgment of your Lord?"<br> He put down the Tablets,<br>Seized his brother by (the hair<br> Of) his head, and dragged him<br> To him. Aaron said:<br> "Son of my mother! The people<br> Did indeed reckon me<br> As naught, and went near<br> To slaying me! Make not<br> The enemies rejoice over<br> My misfortune, nor count thou<br> Me amongst the people<br> Of sin."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_151.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>151.  Q<u>a</u>la rabbi ighfir lee wali-akhee waadkhiln<u>a</u> fee ra<u>h</u>matika waanta ar<u>h</u>amu a<b>l</b>rr<u>ah</u>imeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>151.  Moses prayed: "O my Lord!<br> Forgive me and my brother!<br> Admit us to Thy mercy!<br> For Thou art the Most Merciful<br> Of those who show mercy!"</p> <div class="filenav">
