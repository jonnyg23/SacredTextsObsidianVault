 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XLII.: Shūrā, or Consultation.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 3</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/042/042_020.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>20.  Man k<u>a</u>na yureedu <u>h</u>artha al-<u>a</u>khirati nazid lahu fee <u>h</u>arthihi waman k<u>a</u>na yureedu <u>h</u>artha a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> nu/tihi minh<u>a</u> wam<u>a</u> lahu fee al-<u>a</u>khirati min na<u>s</u>eeb<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>20.  To any that desires<br> The tilth of the Hereafter,<br> We give increase<br> In his tilth; and to any<br> That desires the tilth<br> Of this world, We grant<br> Somewhat thereof, but he<br> Has no share or lot<br> In the Hereafter.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/042/042_021.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>21.  Am lahum shurak<u>a</u>o sharaAAoo lahum mina a<b>l</b>ddeeni m<u>a</u> lam ya/<u>th</u>an bihi All<u>a</u>hu walawl<u>a</u> kalimatu alfa<u>s</u>li laqu<u>d</u>iya baynahum wa-inna a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limeena lahum AAa<u>tha</u>bun aleem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>21.  What! Have they partners<br> (In godhead), who have<br> Established for them some<br> Religion without the permission<br> Of God? Had it not<br> Been for the Decree<br> Of Judgment, the matter<br> Would have been decided<br> Between them (at once).<br> But verily the wrong-doers<br> Will have a grievous Penalty.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/042/042_022.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>22.  Tar<u>a</u> a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limeena mushfiqeena mimm<u>a</u> kasaboo wahuwa w<u>a</u>qiAAun bihim wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo waAAamiloo a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>li<u>ha</u>ti fee raw<u>da</u>ti aljann<u>a</u>ti lahum m<u>a</u> yash<u>a</u>oona AAinda rabbihim <u>tha</u>lika huwa alfa<u>d</u>lu alkabeer<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>22.  Thou wilt see the wrong-doers<br> In fear on account of what<br>They have earned, and (the burden<br> Of) that must (necessarily)<br> Fall on them. But those<br> Who believe and work<br> Righteous deeds will be<br> In the luxuriant meads<br> Of the Gardens: they shall<br> Have, before their Lord,<br> All that they wish for.<br> That will indeed be<br> The magnificent Bounty<br> (Of God).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/042/042_023.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>23.  <u>Tha</u>lika alla<u>th</u>ee yubashshiru All<u>a</u>hu AAib<u>a</u>dahu alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo waAAamiloo a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>li<u>ha</u>ti qul l<u>a</u> as-alukum AAalayhi ajran ill<u>a</u> almawaddata fee alqurb<u>a</u> waman yaqtarif <u>h</u>asanatan nazid lahu feeh<u>a</u> <u>h</u>usnan inna All<u>a</u>ha ghafoorun shakoor<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>23.  That is (the Bounty) whereof<br> God gives Glad Tidings<br> To His Servants who<br> Believe and do righteous deeds.<br> Say: "No reward do I<br> Ask of you for this<br> Except the love<br> Of those near of kin."<br> And if any one earns<br> Any good, We shall give<br> Him an increase of good<br> In respect thereof: for God<br> Is Oft-Forgiving, Most Ready<br> To appreciate (service).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/042/042_024.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>24.  Am yaqooloona iftar<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi ka<u>th</u>iban fa-in yasha-i All<u>a</u>hu yakhtim AAal<u>a</u> qalbika wayam<u>h</u>u All<u>a</u>hu alb<u>at</u>ila wayu<u>h</u>iqqu al<u>h</u>aqqa bikalim<u>a</u>tihi innahu AAaleemun bi<u>tha</u>ti a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>udoor<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>24.  What! Do they say,<br> "He has forged a falsehood<br> Against God"? But if God<br> Willed, He could seal up<br>Thy heart. And God<br> Blots out Vanity, and proves<br> The Truth by His Words.<br> For He knows well<br> The secrets of all hearts.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/042/042_025.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>25.  Wahuwa alla<u>th</u>ee yaqbalu a<b>l</b>ttawbata AAan AAib<u>a</u>dihi wayaAAfoo AAani a<b>l</b>ssayyi-ati wayaAAlamu m<u>a</u> tafAAaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>25.  He is the One that accepts<br> Repentance from His Servants<br> And forgives sins:<br> And He knows all<br> That ye do.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/042/042_026.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>26.  Wayastajeebu alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo waAAamiloo a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>li<u>ha</u>ti wayazeeduhum min fa<u>d</u>lihi wa<b>a</b>lk<u>a</u>firoona lahum AAa<u>tha</u>bun shadeed<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>26.  And He listens to<br> Those who believe and<br> Do deeds of righteousness,<br> And gives them increase<br> Of His Bounty: but<br> For the Unbelievers there is<br> A terrible Penalty.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/042/042_027.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>27.  Walaw basa<u>t</u>a All<u>a</u>hu a<b>l</b>rrizqa liAAib<u>a</u>dihi labaghaw fee al-ar<u>d</u>i wal<u>a</u>kin yunazzilu biqadarin m<u>a</u> yash<u>a</u>o innahu biAAib<u>a</u>dihi khabeerun ba<u>s</u>eer<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>27.  If God were to enlarge<br> The provision for His Servants,<br> They would indeed transgress<br> Beyond all bounds<br> Through the earth;<br> But He sends (it) down<br> In due measure<br> As He pleases.<br> For He is with His Servants<br> Well-acquainted, Watchful.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/042/042_028.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>28.  Wahuwa alla<u>th</u>ee yunazzilu alghaytha min baAAdi m<u>a</u> qana<u>t</u>oo wayanshuru ra<u>h</u>matahu wahuwa alwaliyyu al<u>h</u>ameed<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>28.  He is the One that sends down<br> Rain (even) after (men) have<br>Given up all hope,<br> And scatters His Mercy<br> (Far and wide). And He<br> Is the Protector, Worthy<br> Of all Praise.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/042/042_029.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>29.  Wamin <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tihi khalqu a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i wam<u>a</u> baththa feehim<u>a</u> min d<u>a</u>bbatin wahuwa AAal<u>a</u> jamAAihim i<u>tha</u> yash<u>a</u>o qadeer<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>29.  And among His Signs<br> Is the creation of<br> The heavens and the earth,<br> And the living creatures<br> That He has scattered<br> Through them: and He<br> Has power to gather them<br> Together when He wills.</p> <div class="filenav">
