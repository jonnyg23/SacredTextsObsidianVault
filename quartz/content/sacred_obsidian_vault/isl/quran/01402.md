 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XIV.: Ibrāhīm, or Abraham.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 2</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/014/014_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  Wa-i<u>th</u> taa<u>thth</u>ana rabbukum la-in shakartum laazeedannakum wala-in kafartum inna AAa<u>tha</u>bee lashadeed<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>7.  And remember! your Lord<br> Caused to be declared (publicly):<br> "If ye are grateful, I will<br> Add more (favours) unto you;<br> But if ye show ingratitude,<br> Truly my punishment<br> Is terrible indeed."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/014/014_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  Waq<u>a</u>la moos<u>a</u> in takfuroo antum waman fee al-ar<u>d</u>i jameeAAan fa-inna All<u>a</u>ha laghaniyyun <u>h</u>ameed<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>8.  And Moses said: "If ye<br> Show ingratitude, ye and all<br> On earth together,—yet<br> Is God Free of all wants,<br> Worthy of all praise.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/014/014_009.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>9.  Alam ya/tikum nabao alla<u>th</u>eena min qablikum qawmi noo<u>h</u>in waAA<u>a</u>din wathamooda wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena min baAAdihim l<u>a</u> yaAAlamuhum ill<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hu j<u>a</u>at-hum rusuluhum bi<b>a</b>lbayyin<u>a</u>ti faraddoo aydiyahum fee afw<u>a</u>hihim waq<u>a</u>loo inn<u>a</u> kafarn<u>a</u> bim<u>a</u> orsiltum bihi wa-inn<u>a</u> lafee shakkin mimm<u>a</u> tadAAoonan<u>a</u> ilayhi mureeb<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>9.  Has not the story<br> Reached you, (O people!), of those<br> Who (went) before you?—<br> Of the People of Noah,<br> And ’Ād, and <u>Th</u>amūd?—<br> And of those who (came)<br> After them? None knows them<br> But God. To them came<br>Apostles with Clear (Signs);<br> But they put their hands<br> Up to their mouths, and said:<br> "We do deny (the mission)<br> On which ye have been sent,<br> And we are really<br> In suspicious (disquieting) doubt<br> As to that to which<br> Ye invite us."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/014/014_010.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>10.  Q<u>a</u>lat rusuluhum afee All<u>a</u>hi shakkun f<u>at</u>iri a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i yadAAookum liyaghfira lakum min <u>th</u>unoobikum wayu-akhkhirakum il<u>a</u> ajalin musamman q<u>a</u>loo in antum ill<u>a</u> basharun mithlun<u>a</u> tureedoona an ta<u>s</u>uddoon<u>a</u> AAamm<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>na yaAAbudu <u>a</u>b<u>a</u>on<u>a</u> fa/toon<u>a</u> bisul<u>ta</u>nin mubeen<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>10.  Their apostles said: "Is there<br> A doubt about God,<br> The Creator of the heavens<br> And the earth? It is He<br> Who invites you, in order<br> That He may forgive you<br> Your sins and give you<br> Respite for a term appointed!"<br> They said: "Ah! ye are<br> No more than human,<br> Like ourselves! Ye wish<br> To turn us away from<br> The (gods) our fathers<br> Used to worship: then<br> Bring us some clear authority."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/014/014_011.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>11.  Q<u>a</u>lat lahum rusuluhum in na<u>h</u>nu ill<u>a</u> basharun mithlukum wal<u>a</u>kinna All<u>a</u>ha yamunnu AAal<u>a</u> man yash<u>a</u>o min AAib<u>a</u>dihi wam<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>na lan<u>a</u> an na/tiyakum bisul<u>ta</u>nin ill<u>a</u> bi-i<u>th</u>ni All<u>a</u>hi waAAal<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi falyatawakkali almu/minoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>11.  Their apostles said to them:<br> "True, we are human<br> Like yourselves, but God<br> Doth grant His grace<br>To such of His servants<br> As He pleases. It is not<br> For us to bring you<br> An authority except as God<br> Permits. And on God<br> Let all men of faith<br> Put their trust.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/014/014_012.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>12.  Wam<u>a</u> lan<u>a</u> all<u>a</u> natawakkala AAal<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi waqad had<u>a</u>n<u>a</u> subulan<u>a</u> walana<u>s</u>biranna AAal<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> <u>ath</u>aytumoon<u>a</u> waAAal<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi falyatawakkali almutawakkiloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>12.  "No reason have we why<br> We should not put our trust<br> On God. Indeed He<br> Has guided us to the Ways<br> We (follow). We shall certainly<br> Bear with patience all<br> The hurt you may cause us.<br> For those who put their trust<br> Should put their trust on God."</p> <div class="filenav">
