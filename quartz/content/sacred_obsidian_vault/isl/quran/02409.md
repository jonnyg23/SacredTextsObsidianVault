 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/024/024_062.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>62.  Innam<u>a</u> almu/minoona alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi warasoolihi wa-i<u>tha</u> k<u>a</u>noo maAAahu AAal<u>a</u> amrin j<u>a</u>miAAin lam ya<u>th</u>haboo <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> yasta/<u>th</u>inoohu inna alla<u>th</u>eena yasta/<u>th</u>inoonaka ol<u>a</u>-ika alla<u>th</u>eena yu/minoona bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi warasoolihi fa-i<u>tha</u> ista/<u>th</u>anooka libaAA<u>d</u>i sha/nihim fa/<u>th</u>an liman shi/ta minhum wa<b>i</b>staghfir lahumu All<u>a</u>ha inna All<u>a</u>ha ghafoorun ra<u>h</u>eem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>62.  Only those are Believers,<br> Who believe in God and<br> His Apostle: when they are<br> With him on a matter<br> Requiring <a href="errata.htm#4">collective</a> action,<br> They do not depart until<br> They have asked for his leave;<br> Those who ask for thy leave<br> Are those who believe in God<br> And His Apostle; so when<br> They ask for thy leave,<br> For some business of theirs,<br> Give leave to those of them<br> Whom thou wilt, and ask<br> God for their forgiveness:<br> For God is Oft-Forgiving,<br> Most Merciful.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/024/024_063.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>63.  L<u>a</u> tajAAaloo duAA<u>a</u>a a<b>l</b>rrasooli baynakum kaduAA<u>a</u>-i baAA<u>d</u>ikum baAA<u>d</u>an qad yaAAlamu All<u>a</u>hu alla<u>th</u>eena yatasallaloona minkum liw<u>ath</u>an falya<u>hth</u>ari alla<u>th</u>eena yukh<u>a</u>lifoona AAan amrihi an tu<u>s</u>eebahum fitnatun aw yu<u>s</u>eebahum AAa<u>tha</u>bun aleem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>63.  Deem not the summons<br> Of the Apostle among yourselves<br> Like the summons of one<br> Of you to another: God<br> Doth know those of you<br> Who slip away under shelter<br> Of some excuse: then<br> Let those beware who<br>Withstand the Apostle's order,<br> Lest some trial befall them,<br> Or a grievous Penalty<br> Be inflicted on them.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/024/024_064.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>64.  Al<u>a</u> inna lill<u>a</u>hi m<u>a</u> fee a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i qad yaAAlamu m<u>a</u> antum AAalayhi wayawma yurjaAAoona ilayhi fayunabbi-ohum bim<u>a</u> AAamiloo wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu bikulli shay-in AAaleem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>64.  Be quite sure that<br> To God doth belong<br> Whatever is in the heavens<br> And on earth. Well doth He<br> Know what ye are intent upon:<br> And one day they will be<br> Brought back to Him, and He<br> Will tell them the truth<br> Of what they did:<br> For God doth know<br> All things.</p>  <div class="filenav">
