 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra LXXV.: Qiyāmat, or the Resurrection.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 2</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/075/075_031.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>31.  Fal<u>a</u> <u>s</u>addaqa wal<u>a</u> <u>s</u>all<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>31.  So he gave nothing<br> In charity, nor<br> Did he pray!—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/075/075_032.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>32.  Wal<u>a</u>kin ka<u>thth</u>aba watawall<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>32.  But on the contrary,<br> He rejected Truth<br> And turned away!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/075/075_033.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>33.  Thumma <u>th</u>ahaba il<u>a</u> ahlihi yatama<u>tta</u>   </p>
 <p>33.  Then did he stalk<br> To his family<br> In full conceit!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/075/075_034.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>34.  Awl<u>a</u> laka faawl<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>34.  Woe to thee,<br> (O man!, yea, woe!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/075/075_035.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>35.  Thumma awl<u>a</u> laka faawl<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>35.  Again, woe to thee,<br> (O man!), yea, woe!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/075/075_036.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>36.  Aya<u>h</u>sabu al-ins<u>a</u>nu an yutraka sud<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>36.  Does Man think<br> That he will be left<br> Uncontrolled, (without purpose)?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/075/075_037.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>37.  Alam yaku nu<u>t</u>fatan min manayyin yumn<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>37.  Was he not a drop<br> Of sperm emitted<br> (In lowly form)?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/075/075_038.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>38.  Thumma k<u>a</u>na AAalaqatan fakhalaqa fasaww<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>38.  Then did he become<br> A leech-like clot;<br> Then did (God) make<br> And fashion (him)<br> In due proportion.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/075/075_039.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>39.  FajaAAala minhu a<b>l</b>zzawjayni a<b>l</b><u>thth</u>akara wa<b>a</b>l-onth<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>39.  And of him He made<br> Two sexes, male<br> And female.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/075/075_040.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>40.  Alaysa <u>tha</u>lika biq<u>a</u>dirin AAal<u>a</u> an yu<u>h</u>yiya almawt<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>40.  Has not He, (the same),<br> The power to give life<br> To the dead?</p>  <div class="filenav">
