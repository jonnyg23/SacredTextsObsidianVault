 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra VII.: A’rāf, or the Heights</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 21</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_163.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>163.  Wa<b>i</b>s-alhum AAani alqaryati allatee k<u>a</u>nat <u>had</u>irata alba<u>h</u>ri i<u>th</u> yaAAdoona fee a<b>l</b>ssabti i<u>th</u> ta/teehim <u>h</u>eet<u>a</u>nuhum yawma sabtihim shurraAAan wayawma l<u>a</u> yasbitoona l<u>a</u> ta/teehim ka<u>tha</u>lika nabloohum bim<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo yafsuqoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>163.  Ask them concerning the town<br> Standing close by the sea.<br> Behold! they transgressed<br>In the matter of the Sabbath.<br> For on the day of their Sabbath<br> Their fish did come to them,<br> Openly holding up their heads,<br> But on the day they had<br> No Sabbath, they came not:<br> Thus did We make a trial<br> Of them, for they were<br> Given to transgression.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_164.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>164.  Wa-i<u>th</u> q<u>a</u>lat ommatun minhum lima taAAi<i><u>th</u></i>oona qawman All<u>a</u>hu muhlikuhum aw muAAa<u>thth</u>ibuhum AAa<u>tha</u>ban shadeedan q<u>a</u>loo maAA<u>th</u>iratan il<u>a</u> rabbikum walaAAallahum yattaqoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>164.  When some of them said:<br> "Why do ye preach<br> To a people whom God<br> Will destroy or visit<br> With a terrible punishment?"—<br> Said the preachers: "To discharge<br> Our duty to your Lord,<br> And perchance they may fear Him."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_165.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>165.  Falamm<u>a</u> nasoo m<u>a</u> <u>th</u>ukkiroo bihi anjayn<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena yanhawna AAani a<b>l</b>ssoo-i waakha<u>th</u>n<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <i><u>th</u></i>alamoo biAAa<u>tha</u>bin ba-eesin bim<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo yafsuqoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>165.  When they disregarded the warnings<br> That had been given them,<br> We rescued those who forbade<br> Evil; but We visited<br> The wrong-doers with a<br> Grievous <a href="errata.htm#1">punishment</a>, because<br> They were given to transgression.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_166.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>166.  Falamm<u>a</u> AAataw AAan m<u>a</u> nuhoo AAanhu quln<u>a</u> lahum koonoo qiradatan kh<u>a</u>si-een<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>166.  When in their insolence<br> They transgressed (all) prohibitions,<br> We said to them:<br> "Be ye apes,<br> Despised and rejected."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_167.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>167.  Wa-i<u>th</u> taa<u>thth</u>ana rabbuka layabAAathanna AAalayhim il<u>a</u> yawmi alqiy<u>a</u>mati man yasoomuhum soo-a alAAa<u>tha</u>bi inna rabbaka lasareeAAu alAAiq<u>a</u>bi wa-innahu laghafoorun ra<u>h</u>eem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>167.  Behold! thy Lord did declare<br> That He would send<br> Against them, to the Day<br> Of Judgment, those who would<br> Afflict them with grievous<br> Penalty. Thy Lord is quick<br> In retribution, but He is also<br> Oft-forgiving, Most Merciful.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_168.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>168.  Waqa<u>tt</u>aAAn<u>a</u>hum fee al-ar<u>d</u>i omaman minhumu a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>li<u>h</u>oona waminhum doona <u>tha</u>lika wabalawn<u>a</u>hum bi<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>asan<u>a</u>ti wa<b>al</b>ssayyi-<u>a</u>ti laAAallahum yarjiAAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>168.  We broke them up<br> Into sections on this earth.<br> There are among them some<br> That are the righteous, and some<br> That are the opposite.<br> We have tried them<br> With both prosperity and adversity:<br> In order that they<br> Might turn (to Us).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_169.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>169.  Fakhalafa min baAAdihim khalfun warithoo alkit<u>a</u>ba ya/khu<u>th</u>oona AAara<u>d</u>a h<u>atha</u> al-adn<u>a</u> wayaqooloona sayughfaru lan<u>a</u> wa-in ya/tihim AAara<u>d</u>un mithluhu ya/khu<u>th</u>oohu alam yu/kha<u>th</u> AAalayhim meeth<u>a</u>qu alkit<u>a</u>bi an l<u>a</u> yaqooloo AAal<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi ill<u>a</u> al<u>h</u>aqqa wadarasoo m<u>a</u> feehi wa<b>al</b>dd<u>a</u>ru al-<u>a</u>khiratu khayrun lilla<u>th</u>eena yattaqoona afal<u>a</u> taAAqiloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>169.  After them succeeded<br> An (evil) generation: they<br> Inherited the Book, but<br> They chose (for themselves)<br> The vanities of this world,<br> Saying (for excuse): "(Everything)<br> Will be forgiven us."<br> (Even so), if similar vanities<br> Came their way, they would<br> (Again) seize them.<br> Was not the Covenant<br> Of the Book taken from them,<br> That they would not<br> Ascribe to God anything<br>But the truth? And they<br> Study what is in the Book.<br> But best for the righteous<br> Is the Home in the Hereafter.<br> Will ye not understand?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_170.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>170.  Wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena yumassikoona bi<b>a</b>lkit<u>a</u>bi waaq<u>a</u>moo a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>al<u>a</u>ta inn<u>a</u> l<u>a</u> nu<u>d</u>eeAAu ajra almu<u>s</u>li<u>h</u>een<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>170.  As to those who hold fast<br> By the Book and establish<br> Regular Prayer,—never<br> Shall we suffer the reward<br> Of the righteous to perish.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_171.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>171.  Wa-i<u>th</u> nataqn<u>a</u> aljabala fawqahum kaannahu <i><u>th</u></i>ullatun wa<i><u>th</u></i>annoo annahu w<u>a</u>qiAAun bihim khu<u>th</u>oo m<u>a</u> <u>a</u>tayn<u>a</u>kum biquwwatin wa<b>o</b><u>th</u>kuroo m<u>a</u> feehi laAAallakum tattaqoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>171.  When We shook the Mount<br> Over them, as if it had been<br> A canopy, and they thought<br> It was going to fall on them<br> (We said): "Hold firmly<br> To what We have given you,<br> And bring (ever) to remembrance<br> What is therein;<br> Perchance ye may fear God."</p> <div class="filenav">
