 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra VIII.: Anfāl, or the Spoils of War.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 8</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/008/008_059.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>59.  Wal<u>a</u> ya<u>h</u>sabanna alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo sabaqoo innahum l<u>a</u> yuAAjizoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>59.  Let not the Unbelievers<br> Think that they can<br> Get the better (of the godly):<br> They will never frustrate (them).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/008/008_060.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>60.  WaaAAiddoo lahum m<u>a</u> ista<u>t</u>aAAtum min quwwatin wamin rib<u>at</u>i alkhayli turhiboona bihi AAaduwwa All<u>a</u>hi waAAaduwwakum wa<u>a</u>khareena min doonihim l<u>a</u> taAAlamoonahumu All<u>a</u>hu yaAAlamuhum wam<u>a</u> tunfiqoo min shay-in fee sabeeli All<u>a</u>hi yuwaffa ilaykum waantum l<u>a</u> tu<i><u>th</u></i>lamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>60.  Against them make ready<br> Your strength to the utmost<br> Of your power, including<br> Steeds of war, to strike terror<br> Into (the hearts of) the enemies,<br> Of God and your enemies,<br> And others besides, whom<br> Ye may not know, but whom<br> God doth know. Whatever<br> Ye shall spend in the Cause<br> Of God, shall be repaid<br> Unto you, and ye shall not<br> Be treated unjustly.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/008/008_061.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>61.  Wa-in jana<u>h</u>oo li<b>l</b>ssalmi fa<b>i</b>jna<u>h</u> lah<u>a</u> watawakkal AAal<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi innahu huwa a<b>l</b>ssameeAAu alAAaleem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>61.  But if the enemy<br> Incline towards peace,<br> Do thou (also) incline<br> Towards peace, and trust<br> In God: for He is the One<br> That heareth and knoweth<br> (All things).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/008/008_062.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>62.  Wa-in yureedoo an yakhdaAAooka fa-inna <u>h</u>asbaka All<u>a</u>hu huwa alla<u>th</u>ee ayyadaka bina<u>s</u>rihi wabi<b>a</b>lmu/mineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>62.  Should they intend<br> To deceive thee,—verily God<br> Sufficeth thee: He it is<br> That hath strengthened thee<br> With his aid and<br> With (the company of)<br> The Believers;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/008/008_063.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>63.  Waallafa bayna quloobihim law anfaqta m<u>a</u> fee al-ar<u>d</u>i jameeAAan m<u>a</u> allafta bayna quloobihim wal<u>a</u>kinna All<u>a</u>ha allafa baynahum innahu AAazeezun <u>h</u>akeem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>63.  And (moreover) He hath put<br> Affection between their hearts:<br>Not if thou hadst spent<br> All that is in the earth,<br> Couldst thou have produced<br> That affection, but God<br> Hath done it: for He<br> Is Exalted in might, Wise.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/008/008_064.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>64.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nnabiyyu <u>h</u>asbuka All<u>a</u>hu wamani ittabaAAaka mina almu/mineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>64.  O Apostle! Sufficient<br> Unto thee is God,—<br> (Unto thee) and unto those<br> Who follow thee<br> Among the Believers.</p> <div class="filenav">
