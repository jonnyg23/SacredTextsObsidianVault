 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra II.: Baqara, or the Heifer.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 4</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_030.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>30.  Wa-i<u>th</u> q<u>a</u>la rabbuka lilmal<u>a</u>-ikati innee j<u>a</u>AAilun fee al-ar<u>d</u>i khaleefatan q<u>a</u>loo atajAAalu feeh<u>a</u> man yufsidu feeh<u>a</u> wayasfiku a<b>l</b>ddim<u>a</u>a wana<u>h</u>nu nusabbi<u>h</u>u bi<u>h</u>amdika wanuqaddisu laka q<u>a</u>la innee aAAlamu m<u>a</u> l<u>a</u> taAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>30.  Behold, thy Lord said to the angels: "I will create<br> A vicegerent on earth." They said:<br> "Wilt Thou place therein one who will make<br> Mischief therein and shed blood?—<br> Whilst we do celebrate Thy praises<br> And glorify Thy holy (name)?"<br> He said: "I know what ye know not."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_031.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>31.  WaAAallama <u>a</u>dama al-asm<u>a</u>a kullah<u>a</u> thumma AAara<u>d</u>ahum AAal<u>a</u> almal<u>a</u>-ikati faq<u>a</u>la anbi-oonee bi-asm<u>a</u>-i h<u>a</u>ol<u>a</u>-i in kuntum <u>sa</u>diqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>31.  And He taught Adam the nature<br> Of all things; then He placed them<br> Before the angels, and said: "Tell Me<br> The nature of these if ye are right."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_032.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>32.  Q<u>a</u>loo sub<u>ha</u>naka l<u>a</u> AAilma lan<u>a</u> ill<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> AAallamtan<u>a</u> innaka anta alAAaleemu al<u>h</u>akeem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>32.  They said: "Glory to Thee: of knowledge<br>We have none, save what Thou<br> Hast taught us: in truth it is Thou<br> Who art perfect in knowledge and wisdom."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_033.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>33.  Q<u>a</u>la y<u>a</u> <u>a</u>damu anbi/hum bi-asm<u>a</u>-ihim falamm<u>a</u> anbaahum bi-asm<u>a</u>-ihim q<u>a</u>la alam aqul lakum innee aAAlamu ghayba a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i waaAAlamu m<u>a</u> tubdoona wam<u>a</u> kuntum taktumoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>33.  He said: "O Adam! tell them<br> Their natures." When he had told them,<br> God said: "Did I not tell you<br> That I know the secrets of heaven<br> And earth, and I know what ye reveal<br> And what ye conceal?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_034.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>34.  Wa-i<u>th</u> quln<u>a</u> lilmal<u>a</u>-ikati osjudoo li-<u>a</u>dama fasajadoo ill<u>a</u> ibleesa ab<u>a</u> wa<b>i</b>stakbara wak<u>a</u>na mina alk<u>a</u>fireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>34.  And behold, We said to the angels:<br> "Bow down to Adam: "and they bowed down:<br> Not so Iblīs: he refused and was haughty:<br> He was of those who reject Faith.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_035.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>35.  Waquln<u>a</u> y<u>a</u> <u>a</u>damu oskun anta wazawjuka aljannata wakul<u>a</u> minh<u>a</u> raghadan <u>h</u>aythu shi/tum<u>a</u> wal<u>a</u> taqrab<u>a</u> h<u>ath</u>ihi a<b>l</b>shshajarata fatakoon<u>a</u> mina a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>35.  We said: "O Adam! dwell thou<br> And thy wife in the Garden;<br> And eat of the bountiful things therein<br> As (where and when) ye will; but approach not this tree,<br> Or ye run into harm and transgression."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_036.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>36.  Faazallahum<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>shshay<u>ta</u>nu AAanh<u>a</u> faakhrajahum<u>a</u> mimm<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>n<u>a</u> feehi waquln<u>a</u> ihbi<u>t</u>oo baAA<u>d</u>ukum libaAA<u>d</u>in AAaduwwun walakum fee al-ar<u>d</u>i mustaqarrun wamat<u>a</u>AAun il<u>a</u> <u>h</u>een<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>36.  Then did Satan make them slip<br> From the (Garden), and get them out<br>Of the state (of felicity) in which<br> They had been. We said:<br> "Get ye down, all (ye people),<br> With enmity between yourselves.<br> On earth will be your dwelling-place<br> And your means of livelihood—<br> For a time."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_037.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>37.  Fatalaqq<u>a</u> <u>a</u>damu min rabbihi kalim<u>a</u>tin fat<u>a</u>ba AAalayhi innahu huwa a<b>l</b>ttaww<u>a</u>bu a<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>eem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>37.  Then learnt Adam from his Lord<br> Words of inspiration, and his Lord<br> Turned towards him; for He<br> Is Oft-Returning, Most Merciful.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_038.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>38.  Quln<u>a</u> ihbi<u>t</u>oo minh<u>a</u> jameeAAan fa-imm<u>a</u> ya/tiyannakum minnee hudan faman tabiAAa hud<u>a</u>ya fal<u>a</u> khawfun AAalayhim wal<u>a</u> hum ya<u>h</u>zanoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>38.  We said: "Get ye down all from here;<br> And if, as is sure, there comes to you<br> Guidance from Me, whosoever<br> Follows My guidance, on them<br> Shall be no fear, nor shall they grieve.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_039.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>39.  Wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo waka<u>thth</u>aboo bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin<u>a</u> ol<u>a</u>-ika a<u>s</u>-<u>ha</u>bu a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>ri hum feeh<u>a</u> kh<u>a</u>lidoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>39.  "But those who reject Faith<br> And belie Our Signs,<br> They shall be Companions of the Fire;<br> They shall abide therein."</p> <div class="filenav">
