 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/033/033_035.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>35.  Inna almuslimeena wa<b>a</b>lmuslim<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>lmu/mineena wa<b>a</b>lmu/min<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>lq<u>a</u>niteena wa<b>a</b>lq<u>a</u>nit<u>a</u>ti wa<b>al</b><u>ssa</u>diqeena wa<b>al</b><u>ssa</u>diq<u>a</u>ti wa<b>al</b><u>ssa</u>bireena wa<b>al</b><u>ssa</u>bir<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>lkh<u>a</u>shiAAeena wa<b>a</b>lkh<u>a</u>shiAA<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>lmuta<u>s</u>addiqeena wa<b>a</b>lmuta<u>s</u>addiq<u>a</u>ti wa<b>al</b><u>ssa</u>-imeena wa<b>al</b><u>ssa</u>-im<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l<u>ha</u>fi<i><u>th</u></i>eena furoojahum wa<b>a</b>l<u>ha</u>fi<i><u>th</u></i><u>a</u>ti wa<b>al</b><u>ththa</u>kireena All<u>a</u>ha katheeran wa<b>al</b><u>ththa</u>kir<u>a</u>ti aAAadda All<u>a</u>hu lahum maghfiratan waajran AAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>35.  For Muslim men and women,—<br> For believing men and women,<br> For devout men and women,<br> For true men and women,<br> For men and women who are<br> Patient and constant, for men<br> And women who humble themselves<br> For men and women who give<br> In charity, for men and women<br> Who fast (and deny themselves),<br> For men and women who<br> Guard their chastity, and<br> For men and women who<br>Engage much in God's praise,—<br> For them has God prepared<br> Forgiveness and great reward.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/033/033_036.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>36.  Wam<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>na limu/minin wal<u>a</u> mu/minatin i<u>tha</u> qa<u>da</u> All<u>a</u>hu warasooluhu amran an yakoona lahumu alkhiyaratu min amrihim waman yaAA<u>s</u>i All<u>a</u>ha warasoolahu faqad <u>d</u>alla <u>d</u>al<u>a</u>lan mubeen<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>36.  It is not fitting<br> For a Believer, man or woman.<br> When a matter has been decided<br> By God and His Apostle,<br> To have any option<br> About their decision:<br> If any one disobeys God<br> And His Apostle, he is indeed<br> On a clearly wrong Path.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/033/033_037.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>37.  Wa-i<u>th</u> taqoolu lilla<u>th</u>ee anAAama All<u>a</u>hu AAalayhi waanAAamta AAalayhi amsik AAalayka zawjaka wa<b>i</b>ttaqi All<u>a</u>ha watukhfee fee nafsika m<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hu mubdeehi watakhsh<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>sa wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu a<u>h</u>aqqu an takhsh<u>a</u>hu falamm<u>a</u> qa<u>da</u> zaydun minh<u>a</u> wa<u>t</u>aran zawwajn<u>a</u>kah<u>a</u> likay l<u>a</u> yakoona AAal<u>a</u> almu/mineena <u>h</u>arajun fee azw<u>a</u>ji adAAiy<u>a</u>-ihim i<u>tha</u> qa<u>d</u>aw minhunna wa<u>t</u>aran wak<u>a</u>na amru All<u>a</u>hi mafAAool<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>37.  Behold! thou didst say<br> To one who had received<br> The grace of God<br> And thy favour: "Retain thou<br> (In wedlock) thy wife,<br> And fear God." But thou<br> Didst hide in thy heart<br> That which God was about<br> To make manifest: thou didst<br> Fear the people, but it is<br>More fitting that thou shouldst<br> Fear God. Then when Zaid<br> Had dissolved (his marriage)<br> With her, with the necessary<br> (Formality), We joined her<br> In marriage to thee:<br> In order that (in future)<br> There may be no difficulty<br> To the Believers in (the matter<br> Of) marriage with the vives<br> Of their adopted sons, when<br> The latter have dissolved<br> With the necessary (formality)<br> (Their marriage) with them.<br> And God's command must<br> Be fulfilled.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/033/033_038.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>38.  M<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>na AAal<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nnabiyyi min <u>h</u>arajin feem<u>a</u> fara<u>d</u>a All<u>a</u>hu lahu sunnata All<u>a</u>hi fee alla<u>th</u>eena khalaw min qablu wak<u>a</u>na amru All<u>a</u>hi qadaran maqdoor<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>38.  There can be no difficulty<br> To the Prophet in what<br> God has indicated to him<br> As a duty. It was<br> The practice (approved) of God<br> Amongst those of old<br> That have passed away.<br> And the command of God<br> Is a decree determined.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/033/033_039.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>39.  Alla<u>th</u>eena yuballighoona ris<u>a</u>l<u>a</u>ti All<u>a</u>hi wayakhshawnahu wal<u>a</u> yakhshawna a<u>h</u>adan ill<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>ha wakaf<u>a</u> bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi <u>h</u>aseeb<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>39.  (It is the practice of those)<br> Who preach the Messages<br> Of God, and fear Him,<br> And fear none but God.<br>And enough is God<br> To call (men) to account.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/033/033_040.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>40.  M<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>na mu<u>h</u>ammadun ab<u>a</u> a<u>h</u>adin min rij<u>a</u>likum wal<u>a</u>kin rasoola All<u>a</u>hi wakh<u>a</u>tama a<b>l</b>nnabiyyeena wak<u>a</u>na All<u>a</u>hu bikulli shay-in AAaleem<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>40.  Muhammad is not<br> The father of any<br> Of your men, but (he is)<br> The Apostle of God,<br> And the Seal of the Prophets:<br> And God has full knowledge<br> Of all things.</p> <div class="filenav">
