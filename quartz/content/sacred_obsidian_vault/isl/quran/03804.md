 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXXVIII.: Ṣād (being one of the Abbreviated Letters).</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 4</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/038/038_041.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>41.  Wa<b>o</b><u>th</u>kur AAabdan<u>a</u> ayyooba i<u>th</u> n<u>a</u>d<u>a</u> rabbahu annee massaniya a<b>l</b>shshay<u>ta</u>nu binu<u>s</u>bin waAAa<u>tha</u>b<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>41.  Commemorate Our Servant Job.<br>Behold he cried to his Lord:<br> "The Evil One has afflicted<br> Me with distress and suffering!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/038/038_042.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>42.  Orku<u>d</u> birijlika h<u>atha</u> mughtasalun b<u>a</u>ridun washar<u>a</u>b<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>42.  (The command was given:)<br> "Strike with thy foot:<br> Here is (water) wherein<br> To wash, coal and refreshing,<br> And (water) to drink."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/038/038_043.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>43.  Wawahabn<u>a</u> lahu ahlahu wamithlahum maAAahum ra<u>h</u>matan minn<u>a </u>wa<u>th</u>ikr<u>a</u> li-olee al-alb<u>a</u>b<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>43.  And We gave him (back)<br> His people, and doubled<br> Their number,—as a Grace<br> From Ourselves, and a thing<br> For commemoration, for all<br> Who have Understanding.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/038/038_044.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>44.  Wakhu<u>th</u> biyadika <u>d</u>ighthan fa<b>i</b><u>d</u>rib bihi wal<u>a</u> ta<u>h</u>nath inn<u>a</u> wajadn<u>a</u>hu <u>sa</u>biran niAAma alAAabdu innahu aww<u>a</u>b<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>44.  "And take in thy hand<br> A little grass, and strike<br> Therewith: and break not<br> (Thy oath)." Truly We found<br> Him full of patience and constancy.<br> How excellent in Our service!<br> Ever did he turn (to Us)!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/038/038_045.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>45.  Wa<b>o</b><u>th</u>kur AAib<u>a</u>dan<u>a</u> ibr<u>a</u>heema wa-is<u>ha</u>qa wayaAAqooba olee al-aydee wa<b>a</b>l-ab<u>sa</u>r<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>45.  And commemorate Our Servants<br> Abraham, Isaac, and Jacob,<br> Possessors of Power and Vision.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/038/038_046.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>46.  Inn<u>a</u> akhla<u>s</u>n<u>a</u>hum bikh<u>a</u>li<u>s</u>atin <u>th</u>ikr<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>dd<u>a</u>r<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>46.  Verily We did choose them<br> For a special (purpose)—<br> Proclaiming the Message<br> Of the Hereafter.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/038/038_047.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>47.  Wa-innahum AAindan<u>a</u> lamina almu<u>st</u>afayna al-akhy<u>a</u>r<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>47.  They were, in Our sight,<br> Truly, of the company<br> Of the Elect and the Good.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/038/038_048.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>48.  Wa<b>o</b><u>th</u>kur ism<u>a</u>AAeela wa-ilyasaAAa wa<u>tha</u> alkifli wakullun mina al-akhy<u>a</u>r<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>48.  And commemorate Ismā‘īl,<br> Elisha, and Ẓul-Kifl:<br> Each of them was<br> Of the company of the Good.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/038/038_049.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>49.  H<u>atha</u> <u>th</u>ikrun wa-inna lilmuttaqeena la<u>h</u>usna ma<u>a</u>b<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>49.  This is a Message<br> (Of admonition): and verily,<br> For the Righteous,<br> Is a beautiful place<br> Of (final) Return,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/038/038_050.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>50.  Jann<u>a</u>ti AAadnin mufatta<u>h</u>atan lahumu al-abw<u>a</u>b<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>50.  Gardens of Eternity,<br> Whose doors will (ever)<br> Be open to them;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/038/038_051.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>51.  Muttaki-eena feeh<u>a</u> yadAAoona feeh<u>a</u> bif<u>a</u>kihatin katheeratin washar<u>a</u>b<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>51.  Therein will they<br> Recline (at ease);<br> Therein can they<br> Call (at pleasure)<br> For fruit in abundance,<br> And (delicious) drink;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/038/038_052.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>52.  WaAAindahum q<u>as</u>ir<u>a</u>tu a<b>l</b><u>tt</u>arfi atr<u>a</u>b<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>52.  And beside them will be<br> Chaste women restraining<br> Their glances, (companions)<br> Of equal age.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/038/038_053.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>53.  H<u>atha</u> m<u>a</u> tooAAadoona liyawmi al<u>h</u>is<u>a</u>b<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>53.  Such is the Promise<br> Made to you<br> For the Day of Account!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/038/038_054.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>54.  Inna h<u>atha</u> larizqun<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> lahu min naf<u>a</u>d<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>54.  Truly such will be<br> Our Bounty (to you);<br> It will never fail;—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/038/038_055.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>55.  H<u>atha</u> wa-inna li<b>l</b><u>tta</u>gheena lasharra ma<u>a</u>b<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>55.  ''Yea, such! But<br> For the wrong-doers<br> Will be an evil place<br> Of (final) Return!—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/038/038_056.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>56.  Jahannama ya<u>s</u>lawnah<u>a</u> fabi/sa almih<u>a</u>d<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>56.  Hell!—they will burn<br> Therein,—an evil bed<br> (Indeed, to lie on)!—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/038/038_057.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>57.  H<u>atha</u> falya<u>th</u>ooqoohu <u>h</u>ameemun waghass<u>a</u>q<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>57.  Yea, such!—Then<br> Shall they taste it,<br> A boiling fluid, and a fluid<br> Dark, murky, intensely cold!—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/038/038_058.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>58.  Wa<u>a</u>kharu min shaklihi azw<u>a</u>j<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>58.  And other Penalties<br> Of a similar kind,<br> To match them!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/038/038_059.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>59.  H<u>atha</u> fawjun muqta<u>h</u>imun maAAakum l<u>a</u> mar<u>h</u>aban bihim innahum <u>sa</u>loo a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>r<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>59.  Here is a troop<br> Rushing headlong with you!<br> No welcome for them!<br> Truly, they shall burn<br> In the Fire!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/038/038_060.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>60.  Q<u>a</u>loo bal antum l<u>a</u> mar<u>h</u>aban bikum antum qaddamtumoohu lan<u>a</u> fabi/sa alqar<u>a</u>r<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>60.  (The followers shall cry<br> To the misleaders:)<br> "Nay, ye (too)! No welcome<br> For you! It is ye who<br> Have brought this upon us!<br> Now evil is (this) place<br> To stay in!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/038/038_061.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>61.  Q<u>a</u>loo rabban<u>a</u> man qaddama lan<u>a</u> h<u>atha</u> fazidhu AAa<u>tha</u>ban <u>d</u>iAAfan fee a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>r<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>61.  They will say: "Our Lord!<br> Whoever brought this upon us,—<br> Add to him a double<br> Penalty in the Fire!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/038/038_062.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>62.  Waq<u>a</u>loo m<u>a</u> lan<u>a</u> l<u>a</u> nar<u>a</u> rij<u>a</u>lan kunn<u>a</u> naAAudduhum mina al-ashr<u>a</u>r<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>62.  And they will say:<br> "What has happened to us<br> That we see not men<br> Whom we used to number<br> Among the bad ones?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/038/038_063.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>63.  Attakha<u>th</u>n<u>a</u>hum sikhriyyan am z<u>a</u>ghat AAanhumu al-ab<u>sa</u>r<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>63.  "Did we treat them<br> (As such) in ridicule,<br> Or have (our) eyes<br> Failed to perceive them?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/038/038_064.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>64.  Inna <u>tha</u>lika la<u>h</u>aqqun takh<u>as</u>umu ahli a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>r<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>64.  Truly that is just and fitting,—<br> The mutual recriminations<br> Of the People of the Fire!</p> <div class="filenav">
