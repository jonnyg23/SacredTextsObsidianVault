 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra LV.: Raḥmān, or (God) Most Gracious.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 2</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/055/055_026.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>26.  Kullu man AAalayh<u>a</u> f<u>a</u>n<b>in</b>    </p>
 <p>26.  All that is on earth<br> Will perish:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/055/055_027.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>27.  Wayabq<u>a</u> wajhu rabbika <u>th</u>oo aljal<u>a</u>li wa<b>a</b>l-ikr<u>a</u>m<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>27.  But will abide (for ever)<br> The Face of thy Lord,—<br>Full of Majesty,<br> Bounty and Honour.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/055/055_028.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>28.  Fabi-ayyi <u>a</u>l<u>a</u>-i rabbikum<u>a</u> tuka<u>thth</u>ib<u>a</u>n<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>28.  Then which of the favours<br> Of your Lord will ye deny?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/055/055_029.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>29.  Yas-aluhu man fee a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i kulla yawmin huwa fee sha/n<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>29.  Of Him seeks (its need)<br> Every creature in the heavens<br> And on earth:<br> Every day in (new) Splendour<br> Doth He (shine)!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/055/055_030.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>30.  Fabi-ayyi <u>a</u>l<u>a</u>-i rabbikum<u>a</u> tuka<u>thth</u>ib<u>a</u>n<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>30.  Then which of the favours<br> Of your Lord will ye deny?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/055/055_031.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>31.  Sanafrughu lakum ayyuh<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>ththaqal<u>a</u>n<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>31.  Soon shall We<br> Settle your affairs,<br> O both ye worlds!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/055/055_032.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>32.  Fabi-ayyi <u>a</u>l<u>a</u>-i rabbikum<u>a</u> tuka<u>thth</u>ib<u>a</u>n<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>32.  Then which of the favours<br> Of your Lord will ye deny?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/055/055_033.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>33.  Y<u>a</u> maAAshara aljinni wa<b>a</b>l-insi ini ista<u>t</u>aAAtum an tanfu<u>th</u>oo min aq<u>ta</u>ri a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i fa<b>o</b>nfu<u>th</u>oo l<u>a</u> tanfu<u>th</u>oona ill<u>a</u> bisul<u>ta</u>n<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>33.  O ye assembly of Jinns,<br> And men! If it be<br>Ye can pass beyond<br> The zones of the heavens<br> And the earth, pass ye!<br> Not without authority<br> Shall ye be able to pass!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/055/055_034.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>34.  Fabi-ayyi <u>a</u>l<u>a</u>-i rabbikum<u>a</u> tuka<u>thth</u>ib<u>a</u>n<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>34.  Then which of the favours<br> Of your Lord will ye deny?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/055/055_035.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>35.  Yursalu AAalaykum<u>a</u> shuw<u>a</u><i><u>th</u></i>un min n<u>a</u>rin wanu<u>ha</u>sun fal<u>a</u> tanta<u>s</u>ir<u>a</u>n<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>35.  On you will be sent<br> (O ye evil ones twain!)<br> A flame of fire (to burn)<br> And a smoke (to choke):<br> No defence will ye have:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/055/055_036.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>36.  Fabi-ayyi <u>a</u>l<u>a</u>-i rabbikum<u>a</u> tuka<u>thth</u>ib<u>a</u>n<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>36.  Then which of the favours<br> Of your Lord will ye deny?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/055/055_037.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>37.  Fa-i<u>tha</u> inshaqqati a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>o fak<u>a</u>nat wardatan ka<b>al</b>ddih<u>a</u>n<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>37.  When the sky is rent<br> Asunder, and it becomes red<br> Like ointment:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/055/055_038.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>38.  Fabi-ayyi <u>a</u>l<u>a</u>-i rabbikum<u>a</u> tuka<u>thth</u>ib<u>a</u>n<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>38.  Then which of the favours<br> Of your Lord will ye deny?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/055/055_039.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>39.  Fayawma-i<u>th</u>in l<u>a</u> yus-alu AAan <u>th</u>anbihi insun wal<u>a</u> j<u>a</u>n<b>nun</b>   </p>
 <p>39.  On that Day<br> No question will be asked<br> Of man or Jinn<br> As to his sin,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/055/055_040.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>40.  Fabi-ayyi <u>a</u>l<u>a</u>-i rabbikum<u>a</u> tuka<u>thth</u>ib<u>a</u>n<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>40.  Then which of the favours<br> Of your Lord will ye deny?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/055/055_041.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>41.  YuAArafu almujrimoona biseem<u>a</u>hum fayu/kha<u>th</u>u bi<b>al</b>nnaw<u>as</u>ee wa<b>a</b>l-aqd<u>a</u>m<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>41.  (For) the sinners will be<br> Known by their Marks:<br> And they will be seized<br> By their forelocks and<br> Their feet.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/055/055_042.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>42.  Fabi-ayyi <u>a</u>l<u>a</u>-i rabbikum<u>a</u> tuka<u>thth</u>ib<u>a</u>n<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>42.  Then which of the favours<br> Of your Lord will ye deny?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/055/055_043.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>43.  H<u>ath</u>ihi jahannamu allatee yuka<u>thth</u>ibu bih<u>a</u> almujrimoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>43.  This is the Hell which<br> The Sinners deny:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/055/055_044.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>44.  Ya<u>t</u>oofoona baynah<u>a</u> wabayna <u>h</u>ameemin <u>a</u>n<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>44.  In its midst<br> And in the midst<br> Of boiling hot water<br> Will they wander round!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/055/055_045.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>45.  Fabi-ayyi <u>a</u>l<u>a</u>-i rabbikum<u>a</u> tuka<u>thth</u>ib<u>a</u>n<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>45.  Then which of the favours<br> Of your Lord will ye deny?</p> <div class="filenav">
