 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XIV.: Ibrāhīm, or Abraham.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 6</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/014/014_035.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>35.  Wa-i<u>th</u> q<u>a</u>la ibr<u>a</u>heemu rabbi ijAAal h<u>atha</u> albalada <u>a</u>minan wa<b>o</b>jnubnee wabaniyya an naAAbuda al-a<u>s</u>n<u>a</u>m<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>35.  Remember Abraham said:<br> "O my Lord! make this city<br> One of peace and security:<br> And preserve me and my sons<br> From worshipping idols.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/014/014_036.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>36.  Rabbi innahunna a<u>d</u>lalna katheeran mina a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si faman tabiAAanee fa-innahu minnee waman AAa<u>sa</u>nee fa-innaka ghafoorun ra<u>h</u>eem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>36.  "O my Lord! they have indeed<br> Led astray many among mankind;<br> He then who follows my (ways)<br> Is of me, and he that<br> Disobeys me,—but Thou<br> Art indeed Oft-Forgiving,<br> Most Merciful.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/014/014_037.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>37.  Rabban<u>a</u> innee askantu min <u>th</u>urriyyatee biw<u>a</u>din ghayri <u>th</u>ee zarAAin AAinda baytika almu<u>h</u>arrami rabban<u>a</u> liyuqeemoo a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>al<u>a</u>ta fa<b>i</b>jAAal af-idatan mina a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si tahwee ilayhim wa<b>o</b>rzuqhum mina a<b>l</b>ththamar<u>a</u>ti laAAallahum yashkuroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>37.  "O our Lord! I have made<br> Some of my offspring to dwell<br> In a valley without cultivation,<br> By Thy Sacred House;<br>In order, O our Lord, that they<br> May establish regular Prayer:<br> So till the hearts of some<br> Among men with love towards them,<br> And feed them with Fruits:<br> So that they may give thanks.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/014/014_038.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>38.  Rabban<u>a</u> innaka taAAlamu m<u>a</u> nukhfee wam<u>a</u> nuAAlinu wam<u>a</u> yakhf<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi min shay-in fee al-ar<u>d</u>i wal<u>a</u> fee a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>/-<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>38.  "O our Lord! truly Thou<br> Dost know what we conceal<br> And what we reveal:<br> For nothing whatever is hidden<br> From God, whether on earth<br> Or in heaven.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/014/014_039.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>39.  Al<u>h</u>amdu lill<u>a</u>hi alla<u>th</u>ee wahaba lee AAal<u>a</u> alkibari ism<u>a</u>AAeela wa-is<u>ha</u>qa inna rabbee lasameeAAu a<b>l</b>dduAA<u>a</u>/-<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>39.  "Praise be to God, Who hath<br> Granted unto me in old age<br> Ismā’īl and Isaac: for truly<br> My Lord is He, the Hearer<br> Of Prayer!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/014/014_040.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>40.  Rabbi ijAAalnee muqeema a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>al<u>a</u>ti wamin <u>th</u>urriyyatee rabban<u>a</u> wtaqabbal duAA<u>a</u>/-<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>40.  "O my Lord! make me<br> One who establishes regular Prayer,<br> And also (raise such)<br> Among my offspring<br> O our Lord!<br> And accept Thou my Prayer.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/014/014_041.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>41.  Rabban<u>a</u> ighfir lee waliw<u>a</u>lidayya walilmu/mineena yawma yaqoomu al<u>h</u>is<u>a</u>b<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>41.  "O our Lord! cover (us)<br> With Thy Forgiveness—me,<br>My parents, and (all) Believers,<br> On the Day that the Reckoning<br> Will be established!</p> <div class="filenav">
