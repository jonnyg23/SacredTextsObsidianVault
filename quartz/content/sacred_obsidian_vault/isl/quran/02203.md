 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/022/022_023.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>23.  Inna All<u>a</u>ha yudkhilu alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo waAAamiloo a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>li<u>ha</u>ti jann<u>a</u>tin tajree min ta<u>h</u>tih<u>a</u> al-anh<u>a</u>ru yu<u>h</u>allawna feeh<u>a</u> min as<u>a</u>wira min <u>th</u>ahabin walu/lu-an walib<u>a</u>suhum feeh<u>a</u> <u>h</u>areer<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>23.  God will admit those<br> Who believe and work righteous deeds,<br> To Gardens beneath which<br> Rivers flow: they shall be<br> Adorned therein with bracelets<br> Of gold and pearls; and<br> Their garments there<br> Will be of silk.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/022/022_024.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>24.  Wahudoo il<u>a</u> a<b>l</b><u>tt</u>ayyibi mina alqawli wahudoo il<u>a</u> <u>s</u>ir<u>at</u>i al<u>h</u>ameed<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>24.  For they have been guided<br> (In this life) to the purest<br> Of speeches; they have been<br> Guided to the Path of Him<br> Who is Worthy of (all) Praise.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/022/022_025.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>25.  Inna alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo waya<u>s</u>uddoona AAan sabeeli All<u>a</u>hi wa<b>a</b>lmasjidi al<u>h</u>ar<u>a</u>mi alla<u>th</u>ee jaAAaln<u>a</u>hu li<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si saw<u>a</u>an alAA<u>a</u>kifu feehi wa<b>a</b>lb<u>a</u>di waman yurid feehi bi-il<u>ha</u>din bi<i><u>th</u></i>ulmin nu<u>th</u>iqhu min AAa<u>tha</u>bin aleem<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>25.  As to those who have rejected<br> (God), and would keep back (men)<br> From the Way of God, and<br> From the Sacred Mosque, which<br> We have made (open) to (all) men—<br> Equal is the dweller there<br> And the visitor from the country<br> And any whose purpose therein<br> Is profanity or wrong-doing—<br> Them will We cause to taste<br> Of a most grievous Penalty.</p> <div class="filenav">
