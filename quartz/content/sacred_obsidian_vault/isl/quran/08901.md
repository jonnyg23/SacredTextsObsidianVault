 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra LXXXIX.: Fajr, or The Break of Day.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/089/089_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  Wa<b>a</b>lfajr<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>1.  By the Break of Day;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/089/089_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  Walay<u>a</u>lin AAashr<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>2.  By the Nights twice five;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/089/089_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  Wa<b>al</b>shshafAAi wa<b>a</b>lwatr<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>3.  By the Even<br> And Odd (contrasted);</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/089/089_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  Wa<b>a</b>llayli i<u>tha</u> yasr<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>4.  And by the Night<br> When it passeth away;—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/089/089_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  Hal fee <u>tha</u>lika qasamun li<u>th</u>ee <u>h</u>ijr<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>5.  Is there (not) in these<br> An adjuration (or evidence)<br> For those who understand?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/089/089_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  Alam tara kayfa faAAala rabbuka biAA<u>a</u>d<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>6.  Seest thou not<br> How thy Lord dealt<br> With the ‘Ād (people),—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/089/089_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  Irama <u>tha</u>ti alAAim<u>a</u>d<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>7.  Of the (city of) Iram,<br> With lofty pillars,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/089/089_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  Allatee lam yukhlaq mithluh<u>a</u> fee albil<u>a</u>d<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>8.  The like of which<br> Were not produced<br> In (all) the land?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/089/089_009.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>9.  Wathamooda alla<u>th</u>eena j<u>a</u>boo a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>akhra bi<b>a</b>lw<u>a</u>d<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>9.  And with the <u>Th</u>amūd<br> (People), who cut out<br> (Huge) rocks in the-valley?—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/089/089_010.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>10.  WafirAAawna <u>th</u>ee al-awt<u>a</u>d<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>10.  And with Pharaoh,<br> Lord of Stakes?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/089/089_011.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>11.  Alla<u>th</u>eena <u>t</u>aghaw fee albil<u>a</u>d<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>11.  (All) these transgressed<br> Beyond bounds in the lands.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/089/089_012.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>12.  Faaktharoo feeh<u>a</u> alfas<u>a</u>d<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>12.  And heaped therein<br> Mischief (on mischief).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/089/089_013.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>13.  Fa<u>s</u>abba AAalayhim rabbuka saw<u>t</u>a AAa<u>tha</u>b<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>13.  Therefore did thy Lord<br> Pour on them a scourge<br> Of diverse chastisements:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/089/089_014.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>14.  Inna rabbaka labi<b>a</b>lmir<u>sa</u>d<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>14.  For thy Lord is<br> (As a Guardian)<br> On a watch-tower.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/089/089_015.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>15.  Faamm<u>a</u> al-ins<u>a</u>nu i<u>tha</u> m<u>a</u> ibtal<u>a</u>hu rabbuhu faakramahu wanaAAAAamahu fayaqoolu rabbee akraman<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>15.  Now, as for man,<br> When his Lord trieth him,<br> Giving him honour and gifts,<br> Then saith he, (puffed up),<br> "My Lord hath honoured me"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/089/089_016.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>16.  Waamm<u>a</u> i<u>tha</u> m<u>a</u> ibtal<u>a</u>hu faqadara AAalayhi rizqahu fayaqoolu rabbee ah<u>a</u>nan<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>16.  But when he trieth him,<br> Restricting his subsistence<br> For him, then saith he<br> (In despair), "My Lord<br> Hath humiliated me I."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/089/089_017.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>17.  Kall<u>a</u> bal l<u>a</u> tukrimoona alyateem<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>17.  Nay, nay! But ye<br> Honour not the orphans!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/089/089_018.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>18.  Wal<u>a</u> ta<u>hadd</u>oona AAal<u>a</u> <u>t</u>aAA<u>a</u>mi almiskeen<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>18.  Nor do ye encourage<br> One another<br> To feed the poor!—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/089/089_019.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>19.  Wata/kuloona a<b>l</b>ttur<u>a</u>tha aklan lamm<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>19.  And ye devour Inheritance—<br> All with greed,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/089/089_020.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>20.  Watu<u>h</u>ibboona alm<u>a</u>la <u>h</u>ubban jamm<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>20.  And ye love wealth<br> With inordinate love!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/089/089_021.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>21.  Kall<u>a</u> i<u>tha</u> dukkati al-ar<u>d</u>u dakkan dakk<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>21.  Nay! When the earth<br> Is pounded to powder,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/089/089_022.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>22.  Waj<u>a</u>a rabbuka wa<b>a</b>lmalaku <u>s</u>affan <u>s</u>aff<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>22.  And thy Lord cometh,<br> And His angels,<br> Rank upon rank,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/089/089_023.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>23.  Wajee-a yawma-i<u>th</u>in bijahannama yawma-i<u>th</u>in yata<u>th</u>akkaru al-ins<u>a</u>nu waann<u>a</u> lahu a<b>l</b><u>thth</u>ikr<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>23.  And Hell, that Day,<br> Is brought (face to face),—<br> On that Day will man<br> Remember, but how will<br> That remembrance profit him?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/089/089_024.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>24.  Yaqoolu y<u>a</u> laytanee qaddamtu li<u>h</u>ay<u>a</u>tee   </p>
 <p>24.  He will say: "Ah!<br> Would that I had<br> Sent forth (Good Deeds)<br> For (this) my (Future) Life!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/089/089_025.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>25.  Fayawma-i<u>th</u>in l<u>a</u> yuAAa<u>thth</u>ibu AAa<u>tha</u>bahu a<u>h</u>ad<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>25.  For, that Day,<br> His Chastisement will be<br>Such as none (else)<br> Can inflict,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/089/089_026.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>26.  Wal<u>a</u> yoothiqu wath<u>a</u>qahu a<u>h</u>ad<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>26.  And His bonds<br> Will be such as<br> None (other) can bind.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/089/089_027.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>27.  Y<u>a</u> ayyatuh<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nnafsu almu<u>t</u>ma-inna<b>tu</b>   </p>
 <p>27.  (To the righteous soul<br> Will be said:)<br> "O (thou) soul,<br> In (complete) rest<br> And satisfaction!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/089/089_028.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>28.  IrjiAAee il<u>a</u> rabbiki r<u>ad</u>iyatan mar<u>d</u>iyya<b>tan</b>   </p>
 <p>28.  "Come back thou<br> To thy Lord,—<br> Well pleased (thyself),<br> And well-pleasing<br> Unto Him!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/089/089_029.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>29.  Fa<b>o</b>dkhulee fee AAib<u>a</u>dee   </p>
 <p>29.  "Enter thou, then,<br> Among my Devotees!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/089/089_030.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>30.  Wa<b>o</b>dkhulee jannatee      </p>
 <p>30.  "Yea, enter thou<br> My Heaven!</p>  <div class="filenav">
