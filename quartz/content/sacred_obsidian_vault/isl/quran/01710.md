 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XVII.: Banī Isrā-īl, or the Children of Israel,</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 10</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_085.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>85.  Wayas-aloonaka AAani a<b>l</b>rroo<u>h</u>i quli a<b>l</b>rroo<u>h</u>u min amri rabbee wam<u>a</u> ooteetum mina alAAilmi ill<u>a</u> qaleel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>85.  They ask thee concerning<br> The Spirit (of inspiration).<br> Say: "The Spirit (cometh)<br> By command of my Lord:<br> Of knowledge it is only<br> A little that is communicated<br> To you, (O men!)"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_086.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>86.  Wala-in shi/n<u>a</u> lana<u>th</u>habanna bi<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>ee aw<u>h</u>ayn<u>a</u> ilayka thumma l<u>a</u> tajidu laka bihi AAalayn<u>a</u> wakeel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>86.  If it were Our Will,<br> We could take away<br> That which We have<br> Sent thee by inspiration<br> Then wouldst thou find<br> None to plead thy affair<br> In that matter as against Us,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_087.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>87.  Ill<u>a</u> ra<u>h</u>matan min rabbika inna fa<u>d</u>lahu k<u>a</u>na AAalayka kabeer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>87.  Except for Mercy from thy Lord:<br> For His Bounty is<br> To thee (indeed) great.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_088.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>88.  Qul la-ini ijtamaAAati al-insu wa<b>a</b>ljinnu AAal<u>a</u> an ya/too bimithli h<u>atha</u> alqur-<u>a</u>ni l<u>a</u> ya/toona bimithlihi walaw k<u>a</u>na baAA<u>d</u>uhum libaAA<u>d</u>in <i><u>th</u></i>aheer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>88.  Say: "If the whole<br> Of mankind and Jinns<br> Were to gather together<br> To produce the like<br> Of this Qur-ān, they<br> Could not produce<br> The like thereof, even if<br> They backed up each other<br>With help and support.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_089.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>89.  Walaqad <u>s</u>arrafn<u>a</u> li<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si fee h<u>atha</u> alqur-<u>a</u>ni min kulli mathalin faab<u>a</u> aktharu a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si ill<u>a</u> kufoor<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>89.  And We have explained<br> To man, in this Qur-ān,<br> Every kind of similitude:<br> Yet the greater part of men<br> Refuse (to receive it)<br> Except with ingratitude!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_090.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>90.  Waq<u>a</u>loo lan nu/mina laka <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> tafjura lan<u>a</u> mina al-ar<u>d</u>i yanbooAA<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>90.  They say: "We shall not<br> Believe in thee, until thou<br> Cause a spring to gush<br> Forth for us from the earth,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_091.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>91.  Aw takoona laka jannatun min nakheelin waAAinabin fatufajjira al-anh<u>a</u>ra khil<u>a</u>lah<u>a</u> tafjeer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>91.  "Or (until) thou have<br> A garden of date trees<br> And vines, and cause rivers<br> To gush forth in their midst,<br> Carrying abundant water;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_092.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>92.  Aw tusqi<u>t</u>a a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>a kam<u>a</u> zaAAamta AAalayn<u>a</u> kisafan aw ta/tiya bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi wa<b>a</b>lmal<u>a</u>-ikati qabeel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>92.  "Or thou cause the sky<br> To fall in pieces, as thou<br> Sayest (will happen), against us;<br>Or thou bring God<br> And the angels before (us)<br> Face to face;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_093.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>93.  Aw yakoona laka baytun min zukhrufin aw tarq<u>a</u> fee a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>-i walan nu/mina liruqiyyika <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> tunazzila AAalayn<u>a</u> kit<u>a</u>ban naqraohu qul sub<u>ha</u>na rabbee hal kuntu ill<u>a</u> basharan rasool<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>93.  "Or thou have a house<br> Adorned with gold,<br> Or thou mount a ladder<br> Right into the skies.<br> No, we shall not even believe<br> In thy mounting until thou<br> Send down to us a book<br> That we could read."<br> Say: "Glory to my Lord!<br> Am I aught but a man,—<br> An apostle?"</p> <div class="filenav">
