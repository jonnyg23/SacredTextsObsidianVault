 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra LXXII.: Jinn, or the Spirits.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 2</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/072/072_020.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>20.  Qul innam<u>a</u> adAAoo rabbee wal<u>a</u> oshriku bihi a<u>h</u>ad<u>a</u><b>n</b>    </p>
 <p>20.  Say: "I do<br> No more than invoke<br> My Lord, and I join not<br> With Him any (false god)."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/072/072_021.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>21.  Qul innee l<u>a</u> amliku lakum <u>d</u>arran wal<u>a</u> rashad<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>21.  Say: "It is not<br> In my power to cause<br> You harm, or to bring<br> You to right conduct."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/072/072_022.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>22.  Qul innee lan yujeeranee mina All<u>a</u>hi a<u>h</u>adun walan ajida min doonihi multa<u>h</u>ad<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>22.  Say: "No one can<br> Deliver me from God<br> (If I were to disobey Him),<br> Nor should I find refuge<br> Except in Him,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/072/072_023.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>23.  Ill<u>a</u> bal<u>a</u>ghan mina All<u>a</u>hi waris<u>a</u>l<u>a</u>tihi waman yaAA<u>s</u>i All<u>a</u>ha warasoolahu fa-inna lahu n<u>a</u>ra jahannama kh<u>a</u>lideena feeh<u>a</u> abad<u>a</u><b>n</b>    </p>
 <p>23.  "Unless I proclaim what<br> I receive from God<br> And His Messages:<br> For any that disobey God<br> And His Apostle,—for them<br> Is Hell: they shall dwell<br> Therein for ever."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/072/072_024.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>24.  <u>H</u>att<u>a</u> i<u>tha</u> raaw m<u>a</u> yooAAadoona fasayaAAlamoona man a<u>d</u>AAafu n<u>as</u>iran waaqallu AAadad<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>24.  At length, when they<br> See (with their own eyes)<br> That which they are promised,—<br> Then will they know<br> Who it is that is<br> Weakest in (his) helper<br> And least important<br> In point of numbers.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/072/072_025.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>25.  Qul in adree aqareebun m<u>a</u> tooAAadoona am yajAAalu lahu rabbee amad<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>25.  Say: "I know not whether<br> The (Punishment) which ye<br> Are promised is near,<br> Or whether my Lord<br> Will appoint for it<br> A distant term.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/072/072_026.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>26.  AA<u>a</u>limu alghaybi fal<u>a</u> yu<i><u>th</u></i>hiru AAal<u>a</u> ghaybihi a<u>h</u>ad<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>26.  "He (alone) knows the Unseen,<br> Nor does He make any one<br> Acquainted with his Mysteries,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/072/072_027.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>27.  Ill<u>a</u> mani irta<u>da</u> min rasoolin fa-innahu yasluku min bayni yadayhi wamin khalfihi ra<u>s</u>ad<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>27.  "Except an apostle<br> Whom He has chosen:<br>And then He makes<br> A band of watchers<br> March before him<br> And behind him,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/072/072_028.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>28.  LiyaAAlama an qad ablaghoo ris<u>a</u>l<u>a</u>ti rabbihim waa<u>hat</u>a bim<u>a</u> ladayhim waa<u>hsa</u> kulla shay-in AAadad<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>28.  "That he may know<br> That they have (truly)<br> Brought and delivered<br> The Messages of their Lord:<br> And He surrounds<br> (All the mysteries) that are<br> With them, and takes account<br> Of every single thing."</p>  <div class="filenav">
