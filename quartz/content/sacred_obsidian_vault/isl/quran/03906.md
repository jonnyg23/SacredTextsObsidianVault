 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXXIX.: Zumar, or the Crowds.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 6</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/039/039_053.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>53.  Qul y<u>a</u> AAib<u>a</u>diya alla<u>th</u>eena asrafoo AAal<u>a</u> anfusihim l<u>a</u> taqna<u>t</u>oo min ra<u>h</u>mati All<u>a</u>hi inna All<u>a</u>ha yaghfiru a<b>l</b><u>thth</u>unooba jameeAAan innahu huwa alghafooru a<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>eem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>53.  Say: "O my Servants who<br> Have transgressed against their souls<br> Despair not of the Mercy<br> Of God: for God forgives<br> All sins: for He is<br> Oft-Forgiving, Most Merciful.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/039/039_054.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>54.  Waaneeboo il<u>a</u> rabbikum waaslimoo lahu min qabli an ya/tiyakumu alAAa<u>tha</u>bu thumma l<u>a</u> tun<u>s</u>aroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>54.  "Turn ye to your Lord<br> (In repentance) and bow<br> To His (Will), before<br> The Penalty comes on you:<br> After that ye shall not<br> Be helped.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/039/039_055.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>55.  Wa<b>i</b>ttabiAAoo a<u>h</u>sana m<u>a</u> onzila ilaykum min rabbikum min qabli an ya/tiyakumu alAAa<u>tha</u>bu baghtatan waantum l<u>a</u> tashAAuroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>55.  "And follow the Best<br> Of (the courses) revealed<br> To you from your Lord,<br> Before the Penalty comes<br> On you—of a sudden,<br> While ye perceive not!—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/039/039_056.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>56.  An taqoola nafsun y<u>a</u> <u>h</u>asrat<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> farra<u>t</u>tu fee janbi All<u>a</u>hi wa-in kuntu lamina a<b>l</b>ss<u>a</u>khireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>56.  "Lest the soul should (then)<br> Say: "Ah! woe is me!—<br> In that I neglected<br> (My Duty) towards God,<br> And was but among those<br> Who mocked!"—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/039/039_057.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>57.  Aw taqoola law anna All<u>a</u>ha had<u>a</u>nee lakuntu mina almuttaqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>57.  "Or (lest) it should say:<br> "If only God had guided<br> Me, I should certainly<br> Have been among the righteous!"—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/039/039_058.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>58.  Aw taqoola <u>h</u>eena tar<u>a</u> alAAa<u>tha</u>ba law anna lee karratan faakoona mina almu<u>h</u>sineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>58.  "Or (lest) it should say<br> When it (actually) sees<br> The Penalty, "If only<br> I had another chance,<br> I should certainly be<br> Among those who do good!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/039/039_059.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>59.  Bal<u>a</u> qad j<u>a</u>atka <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tee faka<u>thth</u>abta bih<u>a</u> wa<b>i</b>stakbarta wakunta mina alk<u>a</u>fireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>59.  "(The reply will be:) "Nay,<br> But there came to thee<br>My Signs, and thou didst<br> Reject them: thou vast<br> Haughty, and became one<br> Of those who reject Faith!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/039/039_060.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>60.  Wayawma alqiy<u>a</u>mati tar<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena ka<u>th</u>aboo AAal<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi wujoohuhum muswaddatun alaysa fee jahannama mathwan lilmutakabbireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>60.  (On the Day of Judgment<br> Wilt thou see those<br> Who told lies against God;—<br> Their faces will be turned<br> Black; is there not<br> In Hell an abode<br> For the Haughty?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/039/039_061.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>61.  Wayunajjee All<u>a</u>hu alla<u>th</u>eena ittaqaw bimaf<u>a</u>zatihim l<u>a</u> yamassuhumu a<b>l</b>ssoo-o wal<u>a</u> hum ya<u>h</u>zanoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>61.  But God will deliver<br> The righteous to their place<br> Of salvation: no evil<br> Shall touch them,<br> Nor shall they grieve.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/039/039_062.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>62.  All<u>a</u>hu kh<u>a</u>liqu kulli shay-in wahuwa AAal<u>a</u> kulli shay-in wakeel<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>62.  God is the Creator<br> Of all things, and He<br> Is the Guardian and Disposer<br> Of all affairs.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/039/039_063.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>63.  Lahu maq<u>a</u>leedu a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>ti All<u>a</u>hi ol<u>a</u>-ika humu alkh<u>a</u>siroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>63.  To Him belong the keys<br> Of the heavens<br> And the earth:<br> And those who reject<br> The Signs of God,—<br> It is they who will<br> Be in loss.</p> <div class="filenav">
