 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XI.: Hūd (The Prophet Hūd).</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 6</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_061.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>61.  Wa-il<u>a</u> thamooda akh<u>a</u>hum <u>sa</u>li<u>h</u>an q<u>a</u>la y<u>a</u> qawmi oAAbudoo All<u>a</u>ha m<u>a</u> lakum min il<u>a</u>hin ghayruhu huwa anshaakum mina al-ar<u>d</u>i wa<b>i</b>staAAmarakum feeh<u>a</u> fa<b>i</b>staghfiroohu thumma tooboo ilayhi inna rabbee qareebun mujeeb<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>61.  To the Thamūd People<br> (We sent) Sālih, one<br> Of their own brethren.<br> He said: "O my People!<br> Worship God: ye have<br> No other God but Him.<br> It is He Who hath produced you<br> From the earth and settled you<br> Therein: then ask forgiveness<br> Of Him, and turn to Him<br> (In repentance): for my Lord<br> Is (always) near, ready<br> To answer."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_062.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>62.  Q<u>a</u>loo y<u>a</u> <u>sa</u>li<u>h</u>u qad kunta feen<u>a</u> marjuwwan qabla h<u>atha</u> atanh<u>a</u>n<u>a</u> an naAAbuda m<u>a</u> yaAAbudu <u>a</u>b<u>a</u>on<u>a</u> wa-innan<u>a</u> lafee shakkin mimm<u>a</u> tadAAoon<u>a</u> ilayhi mureeb<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>62.  They said: "O Sālib!<br> Thou hast been of us!—<br>A centre of our hopes<br> Hitherto! Dost thou (now)<br> Forbid us the worship<br> Of what our fathers worshipped?<br> But we are really<br> In suspicious (disquieting)<br> Doubt as to that to which<br> Thou invitest us."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_063.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>63.  Q<u>a</u>la y<u>a</u> qawmi araaytum in kuntu AAal<u>a</u> bayyinatin min rabbee wa<u>a</u>t<u>a</u>nee minhu ra<u>h</u>matan faman yan<u>s</u>urunee mina All<u>a</u>hi in AAa<u>s</u>aytuhu fam<u>a</u> tazeedoonanee ghayra takhseer<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>63.  He said: "O my people!<br> Do ye see?—If I have<br> A Clear (Sign) from my Lord<br> And He hath sent Mercy<br> Unto me from Himself,—who<br> Then can help me<br> Against God if I were<br> To disobey Him? What<br> Then would ye add<br> To my (portion) but perdition?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_064.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>64.  Way<u>a</u> qawmi h<u>ath</u>ihi n<u>a</u>qatu All<u>a</u>hi lakum <u>a</u>yatan fa<u>th</u>arooh<u>a</u> ta/kul fee ar<u>d</u>i All<u>a</u>hi wal<u>a</u> tamassooh<u>a</u> bisoo-in faya/khu<u>th</u>akum AAa<u>tha</u>bun qareeb<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>64.  "And O my people!<br> This she-camel of God is<br> A symbol to you:<br> Leave her to feed<br> On God's (free) earth,<br> And inflict no harm<br> On her, or a swift Penalty<br> Will seize you!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_065.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>65.  FaAAaqarooh<u>a</u> faq<u>a</u>la tamattaAAoo fee d<u>a</u>rikum thal<u>a</u>thata ayy<u>a</u>min <u>tha</u>lika waAAdun ghayru mak<u>th</u>oob<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>65.  But they did ham-string her.<br> So he said: "Enjoy yourselves<br> In your homes for three days:<br> (Then will be your ruin):<br>(Behold) there a promise<br> Not to be belied!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_066.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>66.  Falamm<u>a</u> j<u>a</u>a amrun<u>a</u> najjayn<u>a</u> <u>sa</u>li<u>h</u>an wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo maAAahu bira<u>h</u>matin minn<u>a</u> wamin khizyi yawmi-i<u>th</u>in inna rabbaka huwa alqawiyyu alAAazeez<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>66.  When Our Decree issued,<br> We saved Sālib and those<br> Who believed with him,<br> By (special) Grace from<br> Ourselves—<br> And from the Ignominy<br> Of that Day. For thy Lord—<br> He is the Strong One, and Able<br> To enforce His Will.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_067.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>67.  Waakha<u>th</u>a alla<u>th</u>eena <i><u>th</u></i>alamoo a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>ay<u>h</u>atu faa<u>s</u>ba<u>h</u>oo fee diy<u>a</u>rihim j<u>a</u>thimeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>67.  The (mighty) Blast overtook<br> The wrong-doers, and they<br> Lay prostrate in their homes<br> Before the morning,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_068.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>68.  Kaan lam yaghnaw feeh<u>a</u> al<u>a</u> inna thamooda kafaroo rabbahum al<u>a</u> buAAdan lithamood<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>68.  As if they had never<br> Dwelt and flourished there.<br> Ah! Behold! For the Thamūd<br> Rejected their Lord and Cherisher!<br> Ah! Behold! Removed<br> (From sight) were the Thamūd!</p> <div class="filenav">
