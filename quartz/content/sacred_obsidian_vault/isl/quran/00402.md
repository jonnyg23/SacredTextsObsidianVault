 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_011.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>11.  Yoo<u>s</u>eekumu All<u>a</u>hu fee awl<u>a</u>dikum li<b>l</b><u>thth</u>akari mithlu <u>h</u>a<i><u>thth</u></i>i alonthayayni fa-in kunna nis<u>a</u>an fawqa ithnatayni falahunna thuluth<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> taraka wa-in k<u>a</u>nat w<u>ah</u>idatan falah<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nni<u>s</u>fu wali-abawayhi likulli w<u>ah</u>idin minhum<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>ssudusu mimm<u>a</u> taraka in k<u>a</u>na lahu waladun fa-in lam yakun lahu waladun wawarithahu abaw<u>a</u>hu fali-ommihi a<b>l</b>ththuluthu fa-in k<u>a</u>na lahu ikhwatun fali-ommihi a<b>l</b>ssudusu min baAAdi wa<u>s</u>iyyatin yoo<u>s</u>ee bih<u>a</u> aw daynin <u>a</u>b<u>a</u>okum waabn<u>a</u>okum l<u>a</u> tadroona ayyuhum aqrabu lakum nafAAan faree<u>d</u>atan mina All<u>a</u>hi inna All<u>a</u>ha k<u>a</u>na AAaleeman <u>h</u>akeem<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>11.  God (thus) directs you<br> As regards your children's<br> (Inheritance): to the male,<br> A portion equal to that<br> Of two females: if only<br> Daughters, two or more,<br> Their share is two-thirds<br> Of the inheritance;<br> If only one, her share<br> Is a half.<br>For parents, a sixth share<br> Of the inheritance to each,<br> If the deceased left children;<br> If no children, and the parents<br> Are the (only) heirs, the mother<br> Has a third; if the deceased<br> Left brothers (or sisters)<br> The mother has a sixth.<br> (The distribution in all cases<br> Is) after the payment<br> Of legacies and debts.<br> Ye know not whether<br> Your parents or your children<br> Are nearest to you<br> In benefit. These are<br> Settled portions ordained<br>By God; and God is<br> All-knowing, All-wise.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_012.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>12.  Walakum ni<u>s</u>fu m<u>a</u> taraka azw<u>a</u>jukum in lam yakun lahunna waladun fa-in k<u>a</u>na lahunna waladun falakumu a<b>l</b>rrubuAAu mimm<u>a</u> tarakna min baAAdi wa<u>s</u>iyyatin yoo<u>s</u>eena bih<u>a</u> aw daynin walahunna a<b>l</b>rrubuAAu mimm<u>a</u> taraktum in lam yakun lakum waladun fa-in k<u>a</u>na lakum waladun falahunna a<b>l</b>ththumunu mimm<u>a</u> taraktum min baAAdi wa<u>s</u>iyyatin too<u>s</u>oona bih<u>a</u> aw daynin wa-in k<u>a</u>na rajulun yoorathu kal<u>a</u>latan awi imraatun walahu akhun aw okhtun falikulli w<u>ah</u>idin minhum<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>ssudusu fa-in k<u>a</u>noo akthara min <u>tha</u>lika fahum shurak<u>a</u>o fee a<b>l</b>ththuluthi min baAAdi wa<u>s</u>iyyatin yoo<u>sa</u> bih<u>a</u> aw daynin ghayra mu<u>da</u>rrin wa<u>s</u>iyyatan mina All<u>a</u>hi wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu AAaleemun <u>h</u>aleem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>12.  In what your wives leave,<br> Your share is a half,<br> 1f they leave no child;<br> But if they leave a child,<br> Ye get a fourth; after payment<br> Of legacies and debts.<br> In what ye leave,<br> Their share is a fourth,<br> If ye leave no child;<br> But if ye leave a child,<br> They get an eighth; after payment<br> Of legacies and debts.<br>If the man or woman<br> Whose inheritance is in question,<br> Has left neither ascendants nor descendants,<br> But has left a brother<br> Or a sister, each one of the two<br> Gets a sixth; but if more<br> Than two, they share in a third;<br> After payment of legacies<br> And debts; so that no loss<br> Is caused (to any one).<br> Thus is it ordained by God;<br> And God is All-knowing,<br> Most Forbearing.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_013.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>13.  Tilka <u>h</u>udoodu All<u>a</u>hi waman yu<u>t</u>iAAi All<u>a</u>ha warasoolahu yudkhilhu jann<u>a</u>tin tajree min ta<u>h</u>tih<u>a</u> al-anh<u>a</u>ru kh<u>a</u>lideena feeh<u>a</u> wa<u>tha</u>lika alfawzu alAAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>13.  Those are limits<br> Set by God: those who<br> Obey God and His Apostle<br> Will be admitted to Gardens<br> With rivers flowing beneath,<br> To abide therein (for ever)<br> And that will be<br> The Supreme achievement.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_014.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>14.  Waman yaAA<u>s</u>i All<u>a</u>ha warasoolahu wayataAAadda <u>h</u>udoodahu yudkhilhu n<u>a</u>ran kh<u>a</u>lidan feeh<u>a</u> walahu AAa<u>tha</u>bun muheen<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>14.  But those who disobey<br> God and His Apostle<br> And transgress His limits<br> Will be admitted<br> To a Fire, to abide therein:<br> And they shall have<br> A humiliating punishment.</p> <div class="filenav">
