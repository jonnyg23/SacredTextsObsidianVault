 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXXVIII.: Ṣād (being one of the Abbreviated Letters).</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/038/038_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  <u>Sa</u>d wa<b>a</b>lqur-<u>a</u>ni <u>th</u>ee a<b>l</b><u>thth</u>ikr<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>1.  Sād:<br>By the Qur-ān,<br> Full of Admonition:<br> (This is the Truth).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/038/038_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  Bali alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo fee AAizzatin washiq<u>a</u>q<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>2.  But the Unbelievers<br> (Are steeped) in Self-glory<br> And Separatism.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/038/038_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  Kam ahlakn<u>a</u> min qablihim min qarnin fan<u>a</u>daw wal<u>a</u>ta <u>h</u>eena man<u>as</u><b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>3.  How many generations<br> Before them did We destroy?<br> In the end they cried<br> (For mercy)—when<br> There was no longer time<br> For being saved!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/038/038_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  WaAAajiboo an j<u>a</u>ahum mun<u>th</u>irun minhum waq<u>a</u>la alk<u>a</u>firoona h<u>atha </u>s<u>ah</u>irun ka<u>ththa</u>b<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>4.  So they wonder<br> That a Warner has come<br>To them from among themselves!<br> And the Unbelievers say,<br> "This is a sorcerer<br> Telling lies!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/038/038_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  AjaAAala al-<u>a</u>lihata il<u>a</u>han w<u>ah</u>idan inna h<u>atha</u> lashay-on AAuj<u>a</u>b<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>5.  "Has he made the gods<br> (All) into one God?<br> Truly this is<br> A wonderful thing!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/038/038_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  Wa<b>i</b>n<u>t</u>alaqa almalao minhum ani imshoo wa<b>i</b><u>s</u>biroo AAal<u>a</u> <u>a</u>lihatikum inna h<u>atha</u> lashay-on yur<u>a</u>d<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>6.  And the leaders among them<br> Go away (impatiently), (saying),<br> "Walk ye away, and remain<br> Constant to your gods!<br> For this is truly<br> A thing designed (against you)!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/038/038_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  M<u>a</u> samiAAn<u>a</u> bih<u>atha</u> fee almillati al-<u>a</u>khirati in h<u>atha</u> ill<u>a</u> ikhtil<u>a</u>q<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>7.  "We never heard (the like)<br> Of this among the people<br> Of these latter days:<br> This is nothing but<br> A made-up tale!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/038/038_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  Aonzila AAalayhi a<b>l</b><u>thth</u>ikru min baynin<u>a</u> bal hum fee shakkin min <u>th</u>ikree bal lamm<u>a</u> ya<u>th</u>ooqoo AAa<u>tha</u>b<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>8.  "What! Has the Message<br> Been sent to him—<br>(Of all persons) among us?"…<br> But they are in doubt<br> Concerning My (own) Message!<br> Nay, they have not yet<br> Tasted My Punishment!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/038/038_009.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>9.  Am AAindahum khaz<u>a</u>-inu ra<u>h</u>mati rabbika alAAazeezi alwahh<u>a</u>b<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>9.  Or have they the Treasures<br> Of the Mercy of thy Lord,—<br> The Exalted in Power,<br> The Grantor of Bounties<br> Without measure?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/038/038_010.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>10.  Am lahum mulku a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i wam<u>a</u> baynahum<u>a</u> falyartaqoo fee al-asb<u>a</u>b<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>10.  Or have they the dominion<br> Of the heavens and the earth<br> And all between? If so,<br> Let them mount up<br> With the ropes and means<br> (To reach that end)!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/038/038_011.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>11.  Jundun m<u>a</u> hun<u>a</u>lika mahzoomun mina al-a<u>h</u>z<u>a</u>b<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>11.  But there—will be<br> Put to flight even a host<br> Of confederates.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/038/038_012.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>12.  Ka<u>thth</u>abat qablahum qawmu noo<u>h</u>in waAA<u>a</u>dun wafirAAawnu <u>th</u>oo al-awt<u>a</u>d<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>12.  Before them (were many<br> Who) rejected apostles,—<br> The People of Noah,<br> And ‘Ād, and Pharaoh<br> The Lord of Stakes,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/038/038_013.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>13.  Wathamoodu waqawmu loo<u>t</u>in waa<u>s</u>-<u>ha</u>bu al-aykati ol<u>a</u>-ika al-a<u>h</u>z<u>a</u>b<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>13.  And <u>Th</u>amūd, and the People<br> Of Lūṭ, and the Companions<br> Of the Wood;—such were<br> The Confederates.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/038/038_014.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>14.  In kullun ill<u>a</u> ka<u>thth</u>aba a<b>l</b>rrusula fa<u>h</u>aqqa AAiq<u>a</u>b<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>14.  Not one (of them) but<br> Rejected the apostles,<br> But My Punishment<br> Came justly and inevitably<br> (On them).</p> <div class="filenav">
