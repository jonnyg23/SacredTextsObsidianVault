 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XVIII.: Kahf, or the Cave.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 6</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_045.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>45.  Wa<b>i</b><u>d</u>rib lahum mathala al<u>h</u>ay<u>a</u>ti a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> kam<u>a</u>-in anzaln<u>a</u>hu mina a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>-i fa<b>i</b>khtala<u>t</u>a bihi nab<u>a</u>tu al-ar<u>d</u>i faa<u>s</u>ba<u>h</u>a hasheeman ta<u>th</u>roohu a<b>l</b>rriy<u>ah</u>u wak<u>a</u>na All<u>a</u>hu AAal<u>a</u> kulli shay-in muqtadir<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>45.  Set forth to them<br> The similitude of the life<br> Of this world: it is like<br> The rain which We send<br> Down from the skies:<br> The earth's vegetation absorbs it,<br> But soon it becomes<br> Dry stubble, which the winds<br> Do scatter: it is (only) God<br> Who prevails over all things.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_046.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>46.  Alm<u>a</u>lu wa<b>a</b>lbanoona zeenatu al<u>h</u>ay<u>a</u>ti a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> wa<b>a</b>lb<u>a</u>qiy<u>a</u>tu a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>li<u>ha</u>tu khayrun AAinda rabbika thaw<u>a</u>ban wakhayrun amal<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>46.  Wealth and sons are allurements<br> Of the life of this world:<br> But the things that endure,<br> Good Deeds, are best<br> In the sight of thy Lord,<br> As rewards, and best<br> As (the foundation for) hopes.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_047.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>47.  Wayawma nusayyiru aljib<u>a</u>la watar<u>a</u> al-ar<u>d</u>a b<u>a</u>rizatan wa<u>h</u>asharn<u>a</u>hum falam nugh<u>a</u>dir minhum a<u>h</u>ad<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>47.  One Day We shall<br> Remove the mountains, and thou<br> Wilt see the earth<br> As a level stretch,<br> And We shall gather them,<br> All together, nor shall We<br> Leave out any one of them.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_048.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>48.  WaAAuri<u>d</u>oo AAal<u>a</u> rabbika <u>s</u>affan laqad ji/tumoon<u>a</u> kam<u>a</u> khalaqn<u>a</u>kum awwala marratin bal zaAAamtum allan najAAala lakum mawAAid<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>48.  And they will be marshalled<br> Before thy Lord in ranks,<br> (With the announcement),<br> "Now have ye come to Us<br> (Bare) as We created you<br> First: aye, ye thought<br> We shall not fulfil<br> The appointment made to you<br> To meet (Us)!":</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_049.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>49.  Wawu<u>d</u>iAAa alkit<u>a</u>bu fatar<u>a</u> almujrimeena mushfiqeena mimm<u>a</u> feehi wayaqooloona y<u>a</u> waylatan<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> lih<u>atha</u> alkit<u>a</u>bi l<u>a</u> yugh<u>a</u>diru <u>s</u>agheeratan wal<u>a</u> kabeeratan ill<u>a</u> a<u>hsa</u>h<u>a</u> wawajadoo m<u>a</u> AAamiloo <u>had</u>iran wal<u>a</u> ya<i><u>th</u></i>limu rabbuka a<u>h</u>ad<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>49.  And the Book (of Deeds)<br> Will be placed (before you);<br> And thou wilt see<br> The sinful in great terror<br> Because of what is (recorded)<br> Therein; they will say,<br> "Ah! woe to us!<br> What a book is this!<br> It leaves out nothing<br> Small or great, but<br> Takes account thereof!"<br> They will find all that they<br> Did, placed before them:<br> And not one will thy Lord<br> Treat with injustice.</p> <div class="filenav">
