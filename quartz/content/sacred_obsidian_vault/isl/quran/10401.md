 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra CIV.: Humaza, or the Scandal-monger.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/104/104_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  Waylun likulli humazatin lumaza<b>tin</b>   </p>
 <p>1.  Woe to every<br> (Kind of) scandal-monger<br> And backbiter,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/104/104_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  Alla<u>th</u>ee jamaAAa m<u>a</u>lan waAAaddadah<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>2.  Who pileth up wealth<br> And layeth it by,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/104/104_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  Ya<u>h</u>sabu anna m<u>a</u>lahu akhladah<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>3.  Thinking that his wealth<br> Would make him last<br> For ever!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/104/104_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  Kall<u>a</u> layunba<u>th</u>anna fee al<u>h</u>u<u>t</u>ama<b>ti</b>   </p>
 <p>4.  By no means! He will<br> Be sure to be thrown into<br> That which Breaks to Pieces.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/104/104_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  Wam<u>a</u> adr<u>a</u>ka m<u>a</u> al<u>h</u>u<u>t</u>ama<b>tu</b>   </p>
 <p>5.  And what will explain<br> To thee That which Breaks<br> To Pieces?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/104/104_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  N<u>a</u>ru All<u>a</u>hi almooqada<b>tu</b>   </p>
 <p>6.  (It is) the Fire<br> Of (the Wrath of) God<br> Kindled (to a blaze),</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/104/104_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  Allatee ta<u>tt</u>aliAAu AAal<u>a</u> al-af-ida<b>ti</b>   </p>
 <p>7.  The which doth mount<br> (Right) to the Hearts:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/104/104_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  lnnah<u>a</u> AAalayhim mu/<u>s</u>ada<b>tun</b>   </p>
 <p>8.  It shall be made<br> Into a vault over them,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/104/104_009.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>9.  Fee AAamadin mumaddada<b>tin</b>      </p>
 <p>9.  In columns outstretched.</p>  <div class="filenav">
