 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XVI.: Naḥl or The Bee.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 12</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_084.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>84.  Wayawma nabAAathu min kulli ommatin shaheedan thumma l<u>a</u> yu/<u>th</u>anu lilla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo wal<u>a</u> hum yustaAAtaboon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>84.  One Day We shall raise<br> From all Peoples a Witness:<br> Then will no excuse be accepted<br> From Unbelievers, nor will they<br> Receive any favours.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_085.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>85.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> ra<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <i><u>th</u></i>alamoo alAAa<u>tha</u>ba fal<u>a</u> yukhaffafu AAanhum wal<u>a</u> hum yun<i><u>th</u></i>aroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>85.  When the wrong-doers<br> (Actually) see the Penalty,<br> Then will it in no way<br> Be mitigated, nor will they<br> Then receive respite.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_086.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>86.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> ra<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena ashrakoo shurak<u>a</u>ahum q<u>a</u>loo rabban<u>a</u> h<u>a</u>ol<u>a</u>-i shurak<u>a</u>on<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena kunn<u>a</u> nadAAoo min doonika faalqaw ilayhimu alqawla innakum lak<u>ath</u>iboon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>86.  When those who gave partners<br> To God will see their "partners",<br>They will say: "Our Lord!<br> These are our "partners," those<br> Whom we used to invoke<br> Besides Thee." But they will<br> Throw back their word at them<br> (And say): "Indeed ye are liars!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_087.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>87.  Waalqaw il<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi yawma-i<u>th</u>in a<b>l</b>ssalama wa<u>d</u>alla AAanhum m<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo yaftaroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>87.  That day shall they (openly) show<br> (Their) submission to God; and all<br> Their inventions shall leave<br> Them in the lurch.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_088.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>88.  Alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo wa<u>s</u>addoo AAan sabeeli All<u>a</u>hi zidn<u>a</u>hum AAa<u>tha</u>ban fawqa alAAa<u>tha</u>bi bim<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo yufsidoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>88.  Those who reject God<br> And hinder (men) from the Path<br> Of God—for them<br> Will We add Penalty<br> To Penalty; for that they<br> Used to spread mischief.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_089.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>89.  Wayawma nabAAathu fee kulli ommatin shaheedan AAalayhim min anfusihim waji/n<u>a</u> bika shaheedan AAal<u>a</u> h<u>a</u>ol<u>a</u>-i wanazzaln<u>a</u> AAalayka alkit<u>a</u>ba tiby<u>a</u>nan likulli shay-in wahudan wara<u>h</u>matan wabushr<u>a</u> lilmuslimeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>89.  One day We shall raise<br> From all Peoples a witness<br> Against them, from amongst themselves:<br> And We shall bring thee<br> As a witness against these<br> (Thy people): and We have sent down<br> To thee the Book explaining<br> All things, a Guide, a Mercy,<br> And Glad Tidings to Muslims.</p> <div class="filenav">
