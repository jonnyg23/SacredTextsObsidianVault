 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra IX.: Tauba (Repentance) or Barāat (Immunity).</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 11</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_081.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>81.  Fari<u>h</u>a almukhallafoona bimaqAAadihim khil<u>a</u>fa rasooli All<u>a</u>hi wakarihoo an yuj<u>a</u>hidoo bi-amw<u>a</u>lihim waanfusihim fee sabeeli All<u>a</u>hi waq<u>a</u>loo l<u>a</u> tanfiroo fee al<u>h</u>arri qul n<u>a</u>ru jahannama ashaddu <u>h</u>arran law k<u>a</u>noo yafqahoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>81.  Those who were left behind<br> (In the Tabūk expedition)<br> Rejoiced in their inaction<br> Behind the back of the Apostle<br> Of God: they hated to strive<br> And fight, with their goods<br> And their persons, in the Cause<br> Of God: they said,<br> "Go not forth in the heat."<br> Say, "The fire of Hell<br> Is fiercer in heat." If<br> Only they could understand!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_082.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>82.  Falya<u>dh</u>akoo qaleelan walyabkoo katheeran jaz<u>a</u>an bim<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo yaksiboon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>82.  Let them laugh a little:<br> Much will they weep:<br>A recompense for the (evil)<br> That they do.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_083.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>83.  Fa-in rajaAAaka All<u>a</u>hu il<u>a</u> <u>ta</u>-ifatin minhum fa<b>i</b>sta/<u>th</u>anooka lilkhurooji faqul lan takhrujoo maAAiya abadan walan tuq<u>a</u>tiloo maAAiya AAaduwwan innakum ra<u>d</u>eetum bi<b>a</b>lquAAoodi awwala marratin fa<b>o</b>qAAudoo maAAa alkh<u>a</u>lifeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>83.  If, then, God bring thee back<br> To any of them, and they ask<br> Thy permission to come out<br> (With thee), say: "Never shall ye<br> Come out with me, nor fight<br> An enemy with me:<br> For ye preferred to sit<br> Inactive on the first occasion:<br> Then sit ye (now)<br> With those who lag behind."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_084.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>84.  Wal<u>a</u> tu<u>s</u>alli AAal<u>a</u> a<u>h</u>adin minhum m<u>a</u>ta abadan wal<u>a</u> taqum AAal<u>a</u> qabrihi innahum kafaroo bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi warasoolihi wam<u>a</u>too wahum f<u>a</u>siqoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>84.  Nor do thou ever pray<br> For any of them that dies,<br> Nor stand at his grave;<br> For they rejected God<br> And His Apostle, and died<br> In a state of perverse rebellion.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_085.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>85.  Wal<u>a</u> tuAAjibka amw<u>a</u>luhum waawl<u>a</u>duhum innam<u>a</u> yureedu All<u>a</u>hu an yuAAa<u>thth</u>ibahum bih<u>a</u> fee a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> watazhaqa anfusuhum wahum k<u>a</u>firoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>85.  Nor let their wealth<br> Nor their (following in) sons<br> Dazzle thee: God's Plan<br> Is to punish them<br> With these things in this world,<br> And that their souls may perish<br> In their (very) denial of God.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_086.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>86.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> onzilat sooratun an <u>a</u>minoo bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi waj<u>a</u>hidoo maAAa rasoolihi ista/<u>th</u>anaka oloo a<b>l</b><u>tt</u>awli minhum waq<u>a</u>loo <u>th</u>arn<u>a</u> nakun maAAa alq<u>a</u>AAideen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>86.  When a Sūra comes down,<br> Enjoining them to believe<br> In God and to strive and fight<br> Along with His Apostle,<br> Those with wealth and influence<br> Among them ask thee<br> For exemption, and say:<br> "Leave us (behind): we<br> Would be with those<br> Who sit (at home)."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_087.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>87.  Ra<u>d</u>oo bi-an yakoonoo maAAa alkhaw<u>a</u>lifi wa<u>t</u>ubiAAa AAal<u>a</u> quloobihim fahum l<u>a</u> yafqahoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>87.  They prefer to be with (the women),<br> Who remain behind (at home):<br> Their hearts are sealed<br> And so they understand not.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_088.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>88.  L<u>a</u>kini a<b>l</b>rrasoolu wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo maAAahu j<u>a</u>hadoo bi-amw<u>a</u>lihim waanfusihim waol<u>a</u>-ika lahumu alkhayr<u>a</u>tu waol<u>a</u>-ika humu almufli<u>h</u>oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>88.  But the Apostle, and those<br> Who believe with him,<br> Strive and fight with their wealth<br> And their persons: for them<br> Are (all) good things:<br> And it is they<br> Who will prosper.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_089.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>89.  aAAadda All<u>a</u>hu lahum jann<u>a</u>tin tajree min ta<u>h</u>tih<u>a</u> al-anh<u>a</u>ru kh<u>a</u>lideena feeh<u>a</u> <u>tha</u>lika alfawzu alAAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>89.  God hath prepared for them<br> Gardens under which rivers flow,<br>To dwell therein:<br> That is the supreme felicity.</p> <div class="filenav">
