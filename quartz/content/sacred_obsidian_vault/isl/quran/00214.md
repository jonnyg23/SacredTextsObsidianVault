 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra II.: Baqara, or the Heifer.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 14</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_113.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>113.  Waq<u>a</u>lati alyahoodu laysati a<b>l</b>nna<u>sa</u>r<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> shay-in waq<u>a</u>lati a<b>l</b>nna<u>sa</u>r<u>a</u> laysati alyahoodu AAal<u>a</u> shay-in wahum yatloona alkit<u>a</u>ba ka<u>tha</u>lika q<u>a</u>la alla<u>th</u>eena l<u>a</u> yaAAlamoona mithla qawlihim fa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu ya<u>h</u>kumu baynahum yawma alqiy<u>a</u>mati feem<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo feehi yakhtalifoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>113.  The Jews say: "The Christians<br> Have naught (to stand) upon;<br> And the Christians say:<br> "The Jews have naught<br> (To stand) upon." Yet they<br> (Profess to) study the (same) Book.<br> Like unto their word<br> Is what those say who know not;<br>But God will judge<br> Between them in their quarrel<br> On the Day of Judgment.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_114.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>114.  Waman a<i><u>th</u></i>lamu mimman manaAAa mas<u>a</u>jida All<u>a</u>hi an yu<u>th</u>kara feeh<u>a</u> ismuhu wasaAA<u>a</u> fee khar<u>a</u>bih<u>a</u> ol<u>a</u>-ika m<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>na lahum an yadkhulooh<u>a</u> ill<u>a</u> kh<u>a</u>-ifeena lahum fee a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> khizyun walahum fee al-<u>a</u>khirati AAa<u>tha</u>bun AAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>114.  And who is more unjust<br> Than he who forbids<br> That in places for the worship<br> Of God, God's name should be<br> Celebrated?—whose zeal<br> Is (in fact) to ruin them?<br> It was not fitting that such<br> Should themselves enter them<br> Except in fear. For them<br> There is nothing but disgrace<br> In this world, and in the world<br> To come, an exceeding torment.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_115.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>115.  Walill<u>a</u>hi almashriqu wa<b>a</b>lmaghribu faaynam<u>a</u> tuwalloo fathamma wajhu All<u>a</u>hi inna All<u>a</u>ha w<u>a</u>siAAun AAaleem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>115.  To God belong the East<br> And the West: whithersoever<br> Ye turn, there is the Presence<br> Of God. For God is All-Pervading,<br> All-Knowing.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_116.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>116.  Waq<u>a</u>loo itakha<u>th</u>a All<u>a</u>hu waladan sub<u>ha</u>nahu bal lahu m<u>a</u> fee a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i kullun lahu q<u>a</u>nitoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>116.  They say: "God hath begotten<br> A son": Glory be to Him.—Nay,<br> To Him belongs all<br> That is in the heavens<br> And on earth: everything<br> Renders worship to Him.'"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_117.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>117.  BadeeAAu a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i wa-i<u>tha</u> qa<u>da</u> amran fa-innam<u>a</u> yaqoolu lahu kun fayakoon<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>117.  To Him is due<br> The primal origin<br>Of the heavens and the earth:<br> When He decreeth a matter,<br> He saith to it: "Be,"<br> And it is.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_118.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>118.  Waq<u>a</u>la alla<u>th</u>eena l<u>a</u> yaAAlamoona lawl<u>a</u> yukallimun<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hu aw ta/teen<u>a</u> <u>a</u>yatun ka<u>tha</u>lika q<u>a</u>la alla<u>th</u>eena min qablihim mithla qawlihim tash<u>a</u>bahat quloobuhum qad bayyann<u>a</u> al-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>ti liqawmin yooqinoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>118.  Say those without knowledge:<br> "Why speaketh not God<br> Unto us? Or why cometh not<br> Unto us a Sign?"<br> So said the people before them<br> Words of similar import.<br> Their hearts are alike.<br> We have indeed made clear<br> The Signs unto any people<br> Who hold firmly<br> To Faith (in their hearts).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_119.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>119.  Inn<u>a</u> arsaln<u>a</u>ka bi<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>aqqi basheeran wana<u>th</u>eeran wal<u>a</u> tus-alu Aaan a<u>s</u>-<u>ha</u>bi alja<u>h</u>eem<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>119.  Verily We have sent thee<br> In truth as a bearer<br> Of glad tidings and a warner:<br> But of thee no question<br> Shall be asked of the Companions<br> Of the Blazing Fire.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_120.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>120.  Walan tar<u>da</u> AAanka alyahoodu wal<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nna<u>sa</u>r<u>a</u> <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> tattabiAAa millatahum qul inna hud<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi huwa alhud<u>a</u> wala-ini ittabaAAta ahw<u>a</u>ahum baAAda alla<u>th</u>ee j<u>a</u>aka mina alAAilmi m<u>a</u> laka mina All<u>a</u>hi min waliyyin wal<u>a</u> na<u>s</u>eer<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>120.  Never will the Jews<br> Or the Christians be satisfied<br> With thee unless thou follow<br> Their form of religion. Say:<br>"The Guidance of God,—that<br> Is the (only) Guidance."<br> Wert thou to follow their desires<br> After the knowledge<br> Which hath reached thee,<br> Then wouldst thou find<br> Neither Protector nor Helper<br> Against God.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_121.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>121.  Alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>tayn<u>a</u>humu alkit<u>a</u>ba yatloonahu <u>h</u>aqqa til<u>a</u>watihi ol<u>a</u>-ika yu/minoona bihi waman yakfur bihi faol<u>a</u>-ika humu alkh<u>a</u>siroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>121.  Those to whom We have sent<br> The Book study it as it<br> Should be studied: they are<br> The ones that believe therein:<br> Those who reject faith therein,—<br> The loss is their own.</p> <div class="filenav">
