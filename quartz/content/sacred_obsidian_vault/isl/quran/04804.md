 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/048/048_027.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>27.  Laqad <u>s</u>adaqa All<u>a</u>hu rasoolahu a<b>l</b>rru/y<u>a</u> bi<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>aqqi latadkhulunna almasjida al<u>h</u>ar<u>a</u>ma in sh<u>a</u>a All<u>a</u>hu <u>a</u>mineena mu<u>h</u>alliqeena ruoosakum wamuqa<u>ss</u>ireena l<u>a</u> takh<u>a</u>foona faAAalima m<u>a</u> lam taAAlamoo fajaAAala min dooni <u>tha</u>lika fat<u>h</u>an qareeb<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>27.  Truly did God fulfil<br> The vision for His Apostle:<br> Ye shall enter the Sacred<br> Mosque, if God wills,<br> With minds secure, heads shaved,<br> Hair cut short, and without fear.<br> For He knew what ye<br> Knew not, and He granted,<br> Besides this, a speedy victory.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/048/048_028.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>28.  Huwa alla<u>th</u>ee arsala rasoolahu bi<b>a</b>lhud<u>a</u> wadeeni al<u>h</u>aqqi liyu<i><u>th</u></i>hirahu AAal<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>ddeeni kullihi wakaf<u>a</u> bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi shaheed<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>28.  It is He Who has sent<br> His Apostle with Guidance<br> And the Religion of Truth,<br> To proclaim it over<br>All religion: and enough<br> Is God for a Witness.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/048/048_029.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>29.  Mu<u>h</u>ammadun rasoolu All<u>a</u>hi wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena maAAahu ashidd<u>a</u>o AAal<u>a</u> alkuff<u>a</u>ri ru<u>h</u>am<u>a</u>o baynahum tar<u>a</u>hum rukkaAAan sujjadan yabtaghoona fa<u>d</u>lan mina All<u>a</u>hi wari<u>d</u>w<u>a</u>nan seem<u>a</u>hum fee wujoohihim min athari a<b>l</b>ssujoodi <u>tha</u>lika mathaluhum fee a<b>l</b>ttawr<u>a</u>ti wamathaluhum fee al-injeeli kazarAAin akhraja sha<u>t</u>-ahu fa<u>a</u>zarahu fa<b>i</b>staghla<i><u>th</u></i>a fa<b>i</b>staw<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> sooqihi yuAAjibu a<b>l</b>zzurr<u>a</u>AAa liyaghee<i><u>th</u></i>a bihimu alkuff<u>a</u>ra waAAada All<u>a</u>hu alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo waAAamiloo a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>li<u>ha</u>ti minhum maghfiratan waajran AAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>29.  Muhammad is the Apostle<br> Of God; and those who are<br> With him are strong<br> Against Unbelievers, (but)<br> Compassionate amongst each other.<br> Thou wilt see them bow<br> And prostrate themselves<br> (In prayer), seeking Grace<br> From God and (His) Good<br> Pleasure.<br> On their faces are their<br> Marks, (being) the traces<br> Of their prostration.<br> This is their similitude<br> In the Taurāt;<br> And their similitude<br> In the Gospel is:<br> Like a seed which sends<br> Forth its blade, then<br> Makes it strong; it then<br> Becomes thick, and it stands<br>On its own stem, (filling)<br> The sowers with wonder<br> And delight. As a result,<br> It fills the Unbelievers<br> With rage at them.<br> God has promised those<br> Among them who believe<br> And do righteous deeds<br> Forgiveness,<br> And a great Reward.</p>  <div class="filenav">
