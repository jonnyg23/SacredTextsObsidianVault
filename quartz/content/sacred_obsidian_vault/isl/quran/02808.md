 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXVIII.: Qaṣaṣ, or the Narration.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 8</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/028/028_076.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>76.  Inna q<u>a</u>roona k<u>a</u>na min qawmi moos<u>a</u> fabagh<u>a</u> AAalayhim wa<u>a</u>tayn<u>a</u>hu mina alkunoozi m<u>a</u> inna maf<u>a</u>ti<u>h</u>ahu latanoo-o bi<b>a</b>lAAu<u>s</u>bati olee alquwwati i<u>th</u> q<u>a</u>la lahu qawmuhu l<u>a</u> tafra<u>h</u> inna All<u>a</u>ha l<u>a</u> yu<u>h</u>ibbu alfari<u>h</u>een<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>76.  Qārūn was doubtless,<br> Of the people of Moses; but<br> He acted insolently towards them:<br> Such were the treasures We<br> Had bestowed on him, that<br> Their very keys would<br> Have been a burden to<br> A body of strong men.<br> Behold, his people said to him:<br> "Exult not, for God loveth not<br> Those who exult (in riches).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/028/028_077.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>77.  Wa<b>i</b>btaghi feem<u>a</u> <u>a</u>t<u>a</u>ka All<u>a</u>hu a<b>l</b>dd<u>a</u>ra al-<u>a</u>khirata wal<u>a</u> tansa na<u>s</u>eebaka mina a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> waa<u>h</u>sin kam<u>a</u> a<u>h</u>sana All<u>a</u>hu ilayka wal<u>a</u> tabghi alfas<u>a</u>da fee al-ar<u>d</u>i inna All<u>a</u>ha l<u>a</u> yu<u>h</u>ibbu almufsideen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>77.  "But seek, with the (wealth)<br> Which God has bestowed on thee,<br> The Home of the Hereafter,<br> Nor forget thy portion in this<br>World: but do thou good,<br> As God has been good<br> To thee, and seek not<br> (Occasions for) mischief in the land:<br> For God loves not those<br> Who do mischief."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/028/028_078.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>78.  Q<u>a</u>la innam<u>a</u> ooteetuhu AAal<u>a</u> AAilmin AAindee awa lam yaAAlam anna All<u>a</u>ha qad ahlaka min qablihi mina alqurooni man huwa ashaddu minhu quwwatan waaktharu jamAAan wal<u>a</u> yus-alu AAan <u>th</u>unoobihimu almujrimoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>78.  He said: "This has been given<br> To me because of a certain<br> Knowledge which I have."<br> Did he not know that God<br> Had destroyed, before him,<br> (Whole) generations,—which were<br> Superior to him in strength<br> And greater in the amount<br> (Of riches) they had collected?<br> But the wicked are not<br> Called (immediately) to account<br> For their sins.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/028/028_079.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>79.  Fakharaja AAal<u>a</u> qawmihi fee zeenatihi q<u>a</u>la alla<u>th</u>eena yureedoona al<u>h</u>ay<u>a</u>ta a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> y<u>a</u> layta lan<u>a</u> mithla m<u>a</u> ootiya q<u>a</u>roonu innahu la<u>th</u>oo <u>h</u>a<i><u>thth</u></i>in AAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>79.  So he went forth among<br> His people in the (pride<br> Of his worldly) glitter.<br> Said those whose aim is<br> The Life of this World:<br> "Oh! that we had the like<br> Of what Qārūn has got!<br> For he is truly a lord<br> Of mighty good fortune!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/028/028_080.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>80.  Waq<u>a</u>la alla<u>th</u>eena ootoo alAAilma waylakum thaw<u>a</u>bu All<u>a</u>hi khayrun liman <u>a</u>mana waAAamila <u>sa</u>li<u>h</u>an wal<u>a</u> yulaqq<u>a</u>h<u>a</u> ill<u>a</u> a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>biroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>80.  But those who had been granted<br> (True) knowledge said: "Alas<br> For you! The reward of God<br> (In the Hereafter) is best<br> For those who believe<br> And work righteousness: but this<br>None shall attain, save those<br> Who steadfastly persevere<br> (in good)."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/028/028_081.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>81.  Fakhasafn<u>a</u> bihi wabid<u>a</u>rihi al-ar<u>d</u>a fam<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>na lahu min fi-atin yan<u>s</u>uroonahu min dooni All<u>a</u>hi wam<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>na mina almunta<u>s</u>ireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>81.  Then We caused the earth<br> To swallow up him and<br> His house; and he had not<br> (The least little) party<br> To help him against God,<br> Nor could he defend himself.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/028/028_082.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>82.  Waa<u>s</u>ba<u>h</u>a alla<u>th</u>eena tamannaw mak<u>a</u>nahu bi<b>a</b>l-amsi yaqooloona waykaanna All<u>a</u>ha yabsu<u>t</u>u a<b>l</b>rrizqa liman yash<u>a</u>o min AAib<u>a</u>dihi wayaqdiru lawl<u>a</u> an manna All<u>a</u>hu AAalayn<u>a</u> lakhasafa bin<u>a</u> waykaannahu l<u>a</u> yufli<u>h</u>u alk<u>a</u>firoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>82.  And those who had envied<br> His position the day before<br> Began to say on the morrow:<br> "Ah! It is indeed God<br> Who enlarges the provision"'<br> Or restricts it, to any<br> Of His servants He pleases!<br> Had it not been that God<br> Was gracious to us, He<br> Could have caused the earth<br> To swallow us up! Ah!<br> Those who reject God<br> Will assuredly never prosper."</p> <div class="filenav">
