 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXXIII.: Aḥzāb, or The Confederates.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 3</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/033/033_021.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>21.  Laqad k<u>a</u>na lakum fee rasooli All<u>a</u>hi oswatun <u>h</u>asanatun liman k<u>a</u>na yarjoo All<u>a</u>ha wa<b>a</b>lyawma al-<u>a</u>khira wa<u>th</u>akara All<u>a</u>ha katheer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>21.  Ye have indeed<br> In the Apostle of God<br> A beautiful pattern (of conduct)<br> For any one whose hope is<br> In God and the Final Day,<br> And who engages much<br> In the praise of God.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/033/033_022.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>22.  Walamm<u>a</u> ra<u>a</u> almu/minoona al-a<u>h</u>z<u>a</u>ba q<u>a</u>loo h<u>atha</u> m<u>a</u> waAAadan<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hu warasooluhu wa<u>s</u>adaqa All<u>a</u>hu warasooluhu wam<u>a</u> z<u>a</u>dahum ill<u>a</u> eem<u>a</u>nan watasleem<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>22.  When the Believers saw<br> The Confederate forces,<br> They said: "This is<br> What God and His Apostle is<br> Had promised us, and God<br> And His Apostle told us<br> What was true." And it<br> Only added to their faith<br> And their zeal in obedience.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/033/033_023.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>23.  Mina almu/mineena rij<u>a</u>lun <u>s</u>adaqoo m<u>a</u> AA<u>a</u>hadoo All<u>a</u>ha AAalayhi faminhum man qa<u>da</u> na<u>h</u>bahu waminhum man yanta<i><u>th</u></i>iru wam<u>a</u> baddaloo tabdeel<u>a</u><b>n</b>    </p>
 <p>23.  Among the Believers are then<br> Who have been true to<br> Their Covenant with God:<br> Of them some have completed<br> Their vow (to the extreme),<br> And some (still) wait:<br> But they have never changed<br> (Their determination) in the least:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/033/033_024.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>24.  Liyajziya All<u>a</u>hu a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>diqeena bi<u>s</u>idqihim wayuAAa<u>thth</u>iba almun<u>a</u>fiqeena in sh<u>a</u>a aw yatooba AAalayhim inna All<u>a</u>ha k<u>a</u>na ghafooran ra<u>h</u>eem<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>24.  That God may reward<br> The men of Truth for<br> Their Truth, and punish<br> The Hypocrites if that be<br>His Will, or turn to them<br> In Mercy: for God is<br> Oft-Forgiving, Most Merciful.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/033/033_025.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>25.  Waradda All<u>a</u>hu alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo bighay<i><u>th</u></i>ihim lam yan<u>a</u>loo khayran wakaf<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hu almu/mineena alqit<u>a</u>la wak<u>a</u>na All<u>a</u>hu qawiyyan AAazeez<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>25.  And God turned back<br> The Unbelievers for (all)<br> Their fury: no advantage<br> Did they gain; and enough<br> Is God for the Believers<br> In their fight. And God<br> Is full of Strength, Able<br> To enforce His Will.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/033/033_026.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>26.  Waanzala alla<u>th</u>eena <i><u>th</u></i><u>a</u>haroohum min ahli alkit<u>a</u>bi min <u>s</u>ay<u>as</u>eehim waqa<u>th</u>afa fee quloobihimu a<b>l</b>rruAAba fareeqan taqtuloona wata/siroona fareeq<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>26.  And those of the people<br> Of the Book who aided<br> Them—God did take them<br> Down from their strongholds<br>And cast terror into<br> Their hearts, (so that)<br> Some ye slew, and some<br> Ye made prisoners,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/033/033_027.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>27.  Waawrathakum ar<u>d</u>ahum wadiy<u>a</u>rahum waamw<u>a</u>lahum waar<u>d</u>an lam ta<u>t</u>aooh<u>a</u> wak<u>a</u>na All<u>a</u>hu AAal<u>a</u> kulli shay-in qadeer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>27.  And He made you heirs<br> Of their lands, their houses,<br> And their goods,<br> And of a land which<br>Ye had not frequented<br> (Before). And God has<br> Power over all things.</p> <div class="filenav">
