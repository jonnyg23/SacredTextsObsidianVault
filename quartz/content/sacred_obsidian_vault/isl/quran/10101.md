 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra CI.: Al-Qāri’a, or The Day of Noise and Clamour.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/101/101_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  Alq<u>a</u>riAAa<b>tu</b>   </p>
 <p>1.  The (Day) of<br> Noise and Clamour:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/101/101_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  M<u>a</u> alq<u>a</u>riAAa<b>tu</b>   </p>
 <p>2.  What is the (Day)<br> Of Noise and Clamour?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/101/101_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  Wam<u>a</u> adr<u>a</u>ka m<u>a</u> alq<u>a</u>riAAa<b>tu</b>   </p>
 <p>3.  And what will explain<br> To thee what the (Day)<br> Of Noise and Clamour is?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/101/101_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  Yawma yakoonu a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>su ka<b>a</b>lfar<u>a</u>shi almabthooth<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>4.  (It is) a Day whereon<br> Men will be like moths<br> Scattered about,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/101/101_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  Watakoonu aljib<u>a</u>lu ka<b>a</b>lAAihni almanfoosh<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>5.  And the mountains<br> Will be like carded wool.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/101/101_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  Faamm<u>a</u> man thaqulat maw<u>a</u>zeenuh<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>6.  Then, he whose<br> Balance (of good deeds)<br> Will be (found) heavy,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/101/101_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  Fahuwa fee AAeeshatin r<u>ad</u>iya<b>tin</b>   </p>
 <p>7.  Will be in a Life<br> Of good pleasure and<br> satisfaction.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/101/101_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  Waamm<u>a</u> man khaffat maw<u>a</u>zeenuh<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>8.  But he whose<br> Balance (of good deeds)<br> Will be (found) light,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/101/101_009.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>9.  Faommuhu h<u>a</u>wiya<b>tun</b>   </p>
 <p>9.  Will have his home<br> In a (bottomless) Pit.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/101/101_010.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>10.  Wam<u>a</u> adr<u>a</u>ka m<u>a</u> hiya<b>h</b>   </p>
 <p>10.  And what will explain<br> To thee what this is?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/101/101_011.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>11.  N<u>a</u>run <u>ha</u>miya<b>tun</b>   </p>
 <p>11.  (It is) a Fire<br> Blazing fiercely!</p>  <div class="filenav">
