 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra LXXVIII.: Nabaa, or The (Great) News.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 2</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/078/078_031.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>31.  Inna lilmuttaqeena maf<u>a</u>z<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>31.  Verily for the Righteous<br> There will be<br> A fulfilment of<br> (The Heart's) desires;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/078/078_032.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>32.  <u>H</u>ad<u>a</u>-iqa waaAAn<u>a</u>b<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>32.  Gardens enclosed, and Grapevines;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/078/078_033.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>33.  Wakaw<u>a</u>AAiba atr<u>a</u>b<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>33.  Companions of Equal age;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/078/078_034.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>34.  Waka/san dih<u>a</u>q<u>a</u><b>n</b>    </p>
 <p>34.  And a Cup full<br> (To the brim).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/078/078_035.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>35.  L<u>a</u> yasmaAAoona feeh<u>a</u> laghwan wal<u>a</u> ki<u>ththa</u>b<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>35.  No Vanity shall they hear<br> Therein, nor Untruth;—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/078/078_036.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>36.  Jaz<u>a</u>an min rabbika AAa<u>ta</u>an <u>h</u>is<u>a</u>b<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>36.  Recompense from thy Lord,<br> A Gift, (amply) sufficient,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/078/078_037.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>37.  Rabbi a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i wam<u>a</u> baynahum<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>m<u>a</u>ni l<u>a</u> yamlikoona minhu khi<u>ta</u>b<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>37.  (From the Lord<br> Of the heavens<br> And the earth, and all between,—<br> (God) Most Gracious:<br> None shall have power<br> To argue with Him.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/078/078_038.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>38.  Yawma yaqoomu a<b>l</b>rroo<u>h</u>u wa<b>a</b>lmal<u>a</u>-ikatu <u>s</u>affan l<u>a</u> yatakallamoona ill<u>a</u> man a<u>th</u>ina lahu a<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>m<u>a</u>nu waq<u>a</u>la <u>s</u>aw<u>a</u>b<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>38.  The Day that<br> The Spirit and the angels<br> Will stand forth in ranks,<br> None shall speak<br> Except any who is<br> Permitted by (God) Most Gracious,<br> And he will say<br> What is right.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/078/078_039.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>39.  <u>Tha</u>lika alyawmu al<u>h</u>aqqu faman sh<u>a</u>a ittakha<u>th</u>a il<u>a</u> rabbihi ma<u>a</u>b<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>39.  that day will be<br> The sure Reality:<br> Therefore, whoso will let him<br> Take a (straight) Return<br> To his Lord!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/078/078_040.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>40.  Inn<u>a</u> an<u>th</u>arn<u>a</u>kum AAa<u>tha</u>ban qareeban yawma yan<i><u>th</u></i>uru almaro m<u>a</u> qaddamat yad<u>a</u>hu wayaqoolu alk<u>a</u>firu y<u>a</u> laytanee kuntu tur<u>a</u>b<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>40.  Verily, we have warned you<br> Of a Penalty near,—<br> The Day when man will<br> See (the Deeds) which<br> His hands have sent forth,<br> And the Unbeliever will say,<br> "Woe unto me! Would that<br> I were mere dust!"</p>  <div class="filenav">
