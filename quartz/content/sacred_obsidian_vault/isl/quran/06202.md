 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra LXII.: Jumu‘a, or the Assembly (Friday) Prayer.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 2</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/062/062_009.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>9.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo i<u>tha</u> noodiya li<b>l</b><u>ss</u>al<u>a</u>ti min yawmi aljumuAAati fa<b>i</b>sAAaw il<u>a</u> <u>th</u>ikri All<u>a</u>hi wa<u>th</u>aroo albayAAa <u>tha</u>likum khayrun lakum in kuntum taAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>9.  O ye who believe!<br> When the call is proclaimed<br> To prayer on Friday<br> (The Day of Assembly),<br>Hasten earnestly to the<br> Remembrance<br> Of God, and leave off<br> Business (and traffic):<br> That is best for you<br> If ye but knew!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/062/062_010.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>10.  Fa-i<u>tha</u> qu<u>d</u>iyati a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>al<u>a</u>tu fa<b>i</b>ntashiroo fee al-ar<u>d</u>i wa<b>i</b>btaghoo min fa<u>d</u>li All<u>a</u>hi wa<b>o</b><u>th</u>kuroo All<u>a</u>ha katheeran laAAallakum tufli<u>h</u>oon<b>a </b>   </p>
 <p>10.  And when the Prayer<br> Is finished, then may ye<br> Disperse through the land,<br> And seek of the Bounty<br> Of God: and celebrate<br> The Praises of God<br> Often (and without stint):<br> That ye may prosper.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/062/062_011.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>11.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> raaw tij<u>a</u>ratan aw lahwan infa<u>dd</u>oo ilayh<u>a</u> watarakooka q<u>a</u>-iman qul m<u>a</u> AAinda All<u>a</u>hi khayrun mina allahwi wamina a<b>l</b>ttij<u>a</u>rati wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu khayru a<b>l</b>rr<u>a</u>ziqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>11.  But when they see<br> Some bargain or some<br> Amusement, they disperse<br> Headlong to it, and leave<br> Thee standing. Say:<br> "The (blessing) from the Presence<br> Of God is better than<br> Any amusement or bargain!<br> And God is the Best<br> To provide (for all needs)."</p>  <div class="filenav">
