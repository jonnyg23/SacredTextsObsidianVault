 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXXIX.: Zumar, or the Crowds.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 8</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/039/039_071.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>71.  Waseeqa alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo il<u>a</u> jahannama zumaran <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> i<u>tha</u> j<u>a</u>ooh<u>a</u> futi<u>h</u>at abw<u>a</u>buh<u>a</u> waq<u>a</u>la lahum khazanatuh<u>a</u> alam ya/tikum rusulun minkum yatloona AAalaykum <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>ti rabbikum wayun<u>th</u>iroonakum liq<u>a</u>a yawmikum h<u>atha</u> q<u>a</u>loo bal<u>a</u> wal<u>a</u>kin <u>h</u>aqqat kalimatu alAAa<u>tha</u>bi AAal<u>a</u> alk<u>a</u>fireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>71.  The Unbelievers will be<br> Led to Hell in crowd:<br> Until, when they arrive there,<br> Its gates will be opened.<br> And its Keepers will say,<br> "Did not apostles come<br> To you from among yourselves,<br> Rehearsing to you the Signs<br> Of your Lord, and warning you<br> Of the Meeting of this Day<br> Of yours?" The answer<br> Will be: "True: but<br> The Decree of Punishment<br> Has been proved true<br> Against the Unbelievers!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/039/039_072.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>72.  Qeela odkhuloo abw<u>a</u>ba jahannama kh<u>a</u>lideena feeh<u>a</u> fabi/sa mathw<u>a</u> almutakabbireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>72.  (To them) will be said:<br> "Enter ye the gates of Hell,<br> To dwell therein:<br> And evil is (this)<br> Abode of the arrogant!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/039/039_073.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>73.  Waseeqa alla<u>th</u>eena ittaqaw rabbahum il<u>a</u> aljannati zumaran <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> i<u>tha</u> j<u>a</u>ooh<u>a</u> wafuti<u>h</u>at abw<u>a</u>buh<u>a</u> waq<u>a</u>la lahum khazanatuh<u>a</u> sal<u>a</u>mun AAalaykum <u>t</u>ibtum fa<b>o</b>dkhulooh<u>a</u> kh<u>a</u>lideen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>73.  And those who feared<br> Their Lord will be led<br> To the Garden in crowds:<br> Until behold, they arrive there;<br>Its gates will be opened;<br> And its Keepers will say:<br> "Peace be upon you!<br> Well have ye done!"<br> Enter ye here,<br> To dwell therein."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/039/039_074.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>74.  Waq<u>a</u>loo al<u>h</u>amdu lill<u>a</u>hi alla<u>th</u>ee <u>s</u>adaqan<u>a</u> waAAdahu waawrathan<u>a</u> al-ar<u>d</u>a natabawwao mina aljannati <u>h</u>aythu nash<u>a</u>o faniAAma ajru alAA<u>a</u>mileen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>74.  They will say: "Praise be<br> To God, Who has<br> Truly fulfilled His promise<br> To us, and has given us<br> (This) land in heritage:<br> We can dwell in the Garden<br> As we will: how excellent<br> A reward for those<br> Who work (righteousness)!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/039/039_075.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>75.  Watar<u>a</u> almal<u>a</u>-ikata <u>ha</u>ffeena min <u>h</u>awli alAAarshi yusabbi<u>h</u>oona bi<u>h</u>amdi rabbihim waqu<u>d</u>iya baynahum bi<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>aqqi waqeela al<u>h</u>amdu lill<u>a</u>hi rabbi alAA<u>a</u>lameen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>75.  And thou wilt see<br> The angels surrounding<br> The Throne (Divine)<br> On all sides, singing Glory<br> And Praise to their Lord.<br> The Decision between them<br> (At Judgment) will be<br> In (perfect) justice,<br> And the cry (on all sides)<br> Will be, "Praise be to God,<br> The Lord of the Worlds!"</p>  <div class="filenav">
