 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra LXXII.: Jinn, or the Spirits.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/072/072_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  Qul oo<u>h</u>iya ilayya annahu istamaAAa nafarun mina aljinni faq<u>a</u>loo inn<u>a</u> samiAAn<u>a</u> qur-<u>a</u>nan AAajab<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>1.  Say: It has been<br> Revealed to me that<br> A company of Jinns<br> Listened (to the Qur-ān).<br> They said, 'We have<br> Really heard a wonderful Recital!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/072/072_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  Yahdee il<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>rrushdi fa<u>a</u>mann<u>a</u> bihi walan nushrika birabbin<u>a</u> a<u>h</u>ad<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>2.  'It gives guidance<br> To the Right,<br> And we have believed therein:<br> We shall not join (in worship)<br> Any (gods) with our Lord.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/072/072_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  Waannahu taAA<u>a</u>l<u>a</u> jaddu rabbin<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> ittakha<u>th</u>a <u>sah</u>ibatan wal<u>a</u> walad<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>3.  'And exalted is the Majesty<br> Of our Lord: He has<br> Taken neither a wife<br> Nor a son.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/072/072_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  Waannahu k<u>a</u>na yaqoolu safeehun<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi sha<u>t</u>a<u>ta</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>4.  'There were some foolish ones<br> Among us, who used<br> To utter extravagant lies<br> Against God;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/072/072_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  Waann<u>a</u> <i><u>th</u></i>anann<u>a</u> an lan taqoola al-insu wa<b>a</b>ljinnu AAal<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi ka<u>th</u>ib<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>5.  'But we do think<br> That no man or spirit<br>Should say aught that is<br> Untrue against God.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/072/072_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  Waannahu k<u>a</u>na rij<u>a</u>lun mina al-insi yaAAoo<u>th</u>oona birij<u>a</u>lin mina aljinni faz<u>a</u>doohum rahaq<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>6.  'True, there were persons<br> Among mankind who took shelter<br> With persons among the Jinns,<br> But they increased them<br> In folly.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/072/072_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  Waannahum <i><u>th</u></i>annoo kam<u>a</u> <i><u>th</u></i>anantum an lan yabAAatha All<u>a</u>hu a<u>h</u>ad<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>7.  'And they (came to) think<br> As ye thought, that God<br> Would not raise up<br> Any one (to Judgment).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/072/072_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  Waann<u>a</u> lamasn<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>a fawajadn<u>a</u>h<u>a</u> muli-at <u>h</u>arasan shadeedan washuhub<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>8.  'And we pried into<br> The secrets of heaven;<br> But we found it filled<br> With stern guards<br> And flaming fires.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/072/072_009.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>9.  Waann<u>a</u> kunn<u>a</u> naqAAudu minh<u>a</u> maq<u>a</u>AAida li<b>l</b>ssamAAi faman yastamiAAi al-<u>a</u>na yajid lahu shih<u>a</u>ban ra<u>s</u>ad<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>9.  'We used, indeed, to sit there<br> In (hidden) stations, to (steal)<br> A hearing; but any<br> Who listens now<br> Will find a flaming fire<br> Watching him in ambush.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/072/072_010.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>10.  Waann<u>a</u> l<u>a</u> nadree asharrun oreeda biman fee al-ar<u>d</u>i am ar<u>a</u>da bihim rabbuhum rashad<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>10.  'And we understand not<br> Whether ill is intended<br> To those on earth,<br> Or whether their Lord<br> (Really) intends to guide<br> Them to right conduct.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/072/072_011.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>11.  Waann<u>a</u> minn<u>a</u> a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>li<u>h</u>oona waminn<u>a</u> doona <u>tha</u>lika kunn<u>a</u> <u>t</u>ar<u>a</u>-iqa qidad<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>11.  'There are among us<br> Some that are righteous,<br> And some the contrary:<br> We follow divergent paths.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/072/072_012.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>12.  Wann<u>a</u> <i><u>th</u></i>anann<u>a</u> an lan nuAAjiza All<u>a</u>ha fee al-ar<u>d</u>i walan nuAAjizahu harab<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>12.  'But we think that we<br> Can by no means frustrate<br> God throughout the earth,<br> Nor can we frustrate Him<br> By flight.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/072/072_013.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>13.  Waann<u>a</u> lamm<u>a</u> samiAAn<u>a</u> alhud<u>a</u> <u>a</u>mann<u>a</u> bihi faman yu/min birabbihi fal<u>a</u> yakh<u>a</u>fu bakhsan wal<u>a</u> rahaq<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>13.  'And as for us,<br> Since we have listened<br> To the Guidance, we have<br> Accepted it: and any<br> Who believes in his Lord<br> Has no fear, either<br> Of a short (account)<br> Or of any injustice.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/072/072_014.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>14.  Waann<u>a</u> minn<u>a</u> almuslimoona waminn<u>a</u> alq<u>a</u>si<u>t</u>oona faman aslama faol<u>a</u>-ika ta<u>h</u>arraw rashad<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>14.  'Amongst us are some<br> That submit their wills<br> (To God), and some<br> That swerve from justice.<br> Now those who submit<br> Their wills—they have<br>Sought out (the path)<br> Of right conduct:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/072/072_015.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>15.  Waam<u>a</u> alq<u>a</u>si<u>t</u>oona fak<u>a</u>noo lijahannama <u>h</u>a<u>t</u>ab<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>15.  'But those who swerve,—<br> They are (but) fuel<br> For Hell-fire'—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/072/072_016.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>16.  Waallawi istaq<u>a</u>moo AAal<u>a</u> a<b>l</b><u>tt</u>areeqati laasqayn<u>a</u>hum m<u>a</u>an ghadaq<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>16.  (And God's Message is):<br> "If they (the Pagans)<br> Had (only) remained<br> On the (right) Way,<br> We should certainly have<br> Bestowed on them Rain<br> In abundance.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/072/072_017.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>17.  Linaftinahum feehi waman yuAAri<u>d</u> AAan <u>th</u>ikri rabbihi yasluk-hu AAa<u>tha</u>ban <u>s</u>aAAad<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>17.  "That We might try them<br> By that (means).<br> But if any turns away<br> From the remembrance<br> Of his Lord, He will<br> Cause him to undergo<br> A severe Penalty.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/072/072_018.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>18.  Waanna almas<u>a</u>jida lill<u>a</u>hi fal<u>a</u> tadAAoo maAAa All<u>a</u>hi a<u>h</u>ad<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>18.  "And the places of worship<br> Are for God (alone):<br> So invoke not any one<br> Along with God;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/072/072_019.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>19.  Waannahu lamm<u>a</u> q<u>a</u>ma AAabdu All<u>a</u>hi yadAAoohu k<u>a</u>doo yakoonoona AAalayhi libad<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>19.  "Yet when the Devotee<br> Of God stands forth<br> To invoke Him, they just<br> Make round him a dense crowd."</p> <div class="filenav">
