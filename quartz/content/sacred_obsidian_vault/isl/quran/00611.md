 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_091.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>91.  Wam<u>a</u> qadaroo All<u>a</u>ha <u>h</u>aqqa qadrihi i<u>th</u> q<u>a</u>loo m<u>a</u> anzala All<u>a</u>hu AAal<u>a</u> basharin min shay-in qul man anzala alkit<u>a</u>ba alla<u>th</u>ee j<u>a</u>a bihi moos<u>a</u> nooran wahudan li<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si tajAAaloonahu qar<u>at</u>eesa tubdoonah<u>a</u> watukhfoona katheeran waAAullimtum m<u>a</u> lam taAAlamoo antum wal<u>a</u> <u>a</u>b<u>a</u>okum quli All<u>a</u>hu thumma <u>th</u>arhum fee khaw<u>d</u>ihim yalAAaboon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>91.  No just estimate of God<br> Do they make when they say:<br>"Nothing doth God send down<br> To man (by way of revelation)":<br> Say: "Who then sent down<br> The Book which Moses brought?—<br> A light and guidance to man:<br> But ye make it into<br> (Separate) sheets for show,<br> While ye conceal much<br> (Of its contents): therein<br> Were ye taught that<br> Which ye knew not<br> Neither ye nor your fathers."<br> Say: "God (sent it down)":<br> Then leave them to plunge<br> In vain discourse and trifling.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_092.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>92.  Wah<u>atha</u> kit<u>a</u>bun anzaln<u>a</u>hu mub<u>a</u>rakun mu<u>s</u>addiqu alla<u>th</u>ee bayna yadayhi walitun<u>th</u>ira omma alqur<u>a</u> waman <u>h</u>awlah<u>a</u> wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena yu/minoona bi<b>a</b>l-<u>a</u>khirati yu/minoona bihi wahum AAal<u>a</u> <u>s</u>al<u>a</u>tihim yu<u>ha</u>fi<i><u>th</u></i>oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>92.  And this is a Book<br> Which We have sent down,<br> Bringing blessings, and confirming<br> (The revelations) which came<br> Before it: that thou<br> Mayest warn the Mother<br> Of Cities and all around her.<br> Those who believe<br> In the Hereafter<br> Believe in this (Book),<br> And they are constant<br> In guarding their Prayers.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_093.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>93.  Waman a<i><u>th</u></i>lamu mimmani iftar<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi ka<u>th</u>iban aw q<u>a</u>la oo<u>h</u>iya ilayya walam yoo<u>h</u>a ilayhi shay-on waman q<u>a</u>la saonzilu mithla m<u>a</u> anzala All<u>a</u>hu walaw tar<u>a</u> i<u>th</u>i a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limoona fee ghamar<u>a</u>ti almawti wa<b>a</b>lmal<u>a</u>-ikatu b<u>a</u>si<u>t</u>oo aydeehim akhrijoo anfusakumu alyawma tujzawna AAa<u>tha</u>ba alhooni bim<u>a</u> kuntum taqooloona AAal<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi ghayra al<u>h</u>aqqi wakuntum AAan <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tihi tastakbiroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>93.  Who can be more wicked<br> Than one who inventeth<br>A lie against God,<br> Or saith, "I have<br> Received inspiration,"<br> When he hath received<br> None, or (again) who saith,<br> "I can reveal the like<br> Of what God hath revealed"?<br> If thou couldst but see<br> How the wicked (do fare)<br> In the flood of confusion<br> At death!—the angels<br> Stretch forth their hands,<br> (Saying), "Yield up your souls:<br> This day shall ye receive<br> Your reward,—a penalty<br> Of shame, for that ye used<br> To tell lies against God,<br> And scornfully to reject<br> Of His Signs!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_094.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>94.  Walaqad ji/tumoon<u>a</u> fur<u>a</u>d<u>a</u> kam<u>a</u> khalaqn<u>a</u>kum awwala marratin wataraktum m<u>a</u> khawwaln<u>a</u>kum war<u>a</u>a <i><u>th</u></i>uhoorikum wam<u>a</u> nar<u>a</u> maAAakum shufaAA<u>a</u>akumu alla<u>th</u>eena zaAAamtum annahum feekum shurak<u>a</u>o laqad taqa<u>tt</u>aAAa baynakum wa<u>d</u>alla AAankum m<u>a</u> kuntum tazAAumoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>94.  "And behold! ye come<br> To Us bare and alone<br> As We created you<br> For the first time:<br> Ye have left behind you<br> All (the favours) which<br> We bestowed on you:<br> We see not with you<br> Your intercessors<br> Whom ye thought to be<br> Partners in your affairs:<br> So now all relations<br> Between you have been<br> Cut off, and your (pet) fancies<br> Have left you in the lurch!</p> <div class="filenav">
