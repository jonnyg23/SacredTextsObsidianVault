 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXX.: Rūm, or The Roman Empire.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 3</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/030/030_020.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>20.  Wamin <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tihi an khalaqakum min tur<u>a</u>bin thumma i<u>tha</u> antum basharun tantashiroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>20.  Among His Signs is this,<br> That He created you<br>From dust; and then,—<br> Behold, ye are men<br> Scattered (far and wide)!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/030/030_021.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>21.  Wamin <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tihi an khalaqa lakum min anfusikum azw<u>a</u>jan litaskunoo ilayh<u>a</u> wajaAAala baynakum mawaddatan wara<u>h</u>matan inna fee <u>tha</u>lika la<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin liqawmin yatafakkaroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>21.  And among His Signs<br> Is this, that He created<br> For you mates from among<br> Yourselves, that ye may<br> Dwell in tranquillity with them,<br> And He has put love<br> And mercy between your (hearts):<br> Verily in that are Signs<br> For those who reflect.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/030/030_022.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>22.  Wamin <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tihi khalqu a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i wa<b>i</b>khtil<u>a</u>fu alsinatikum waalw<u>a</u>nikum inna fee <u>tha</u>lika la<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin lilAA<u>a</u>limeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>22.  And among His Signs<br> Is the creation of the heavens<br> And the earth, and the variations<br> In your languages<br> And your colours: verily<br> In that are Signs<br> For those who know.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/030/030_023.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>23.  Wamin <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tihi man<u>a</u>mukum bi<b>a</b>llayli wa<b>al</b>nnah<u>a</u>ri wa<b>i</b>btigh<u>a</u>okum min fa<u>d</u>lihi inna fee <u>tha</u>lika la<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin liqawmin yasmaAAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>23.  And among His Signs<br> Is the sleep that ye take<br> By night and by day,<br> And the quest that ye<br>(Make for livelihood)<br> Out of His Bounty: verily<br> In that are Signs<br> For those who hearken.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/030/030_024.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>24.  Wamin <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tihi yureekumu albarqa khawfan wa<u>t</u>amaAAan wayunazzilu mina a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>-i m<u>a</u>an fayu<u>h</u>yee bihi al-ar<u>d</u>a baAAda mawtih<u>a</u> inna fee <u>tha</u>lika la<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin liqawmin yaAAqiloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>24.  And among His Signs,<br> He shows you the lightning,<br> By way both of fear<br> And of hope, and He sends<br> Down rain from the sky<br> And with it gives life to<br> The earth after it is dead:<br> Verily in that are Signs<br> For those who are wise.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/030/030_025.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>25.  Wamin <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tihi an taqooma a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>o wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>u bi-amrihi thumma i<u>tha</u> daAA<u>a</u>kum daAAwatan mina al-ar<u>d</u>i i<u>tha</u> antum takhrujoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>25.  And among His Signs is this,<br> That heaven and earth<br> Stand by His Command:<br> Then when He calls you,<br> By a single call, from the earth,<br> Behold, ye (straightway) come forth.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/030/030_026.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>26.  Walahu man fee a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i kullun lahu q<u>a</u>nitoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>26.  To Him belongs every being<br> That is in the heavens<br> And on earth: all are<br> Devoutly obedient to Him.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/030/030_027.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>27.  Wahuwa alla<u>th</u>ee yabdao alkhalqa thumma yuAAeeduhu wahuwa ahwanu AAalayhi walahu almathalu al-aAAl<u>a</u> fee a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i wahuwa alAAazeezu al<u>h</u>akeem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>27.  It is He Who begins<br> (The process of) creation;<br> Then repeats it; and<br> For Him it is most easy.<br> To Him belongs the loftiest<br> Similitude (we can think of)<br> In the heavens and the earth:<br> For He is Exalted in Might,<br> Full of wisdom.</p> <div class="filenav">
