 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXXV.: Fāṭir, or The Originator of Creation; or Malāïka, or The Angels.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/035/035_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  Al<u>h</u>amdu lill<u>a</u>hi f<u>at</u>iri a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i j<u>a</u>AAili almal<u>a</u>-ikati rusulan olee ajni<u>h</u>atin mathn<u>a</u> wathul<u>a</u>tha warub<u>a</u>AAa yazeedu fee alkhalqi m<u>a</u> yash<u>a</u>o inna All<u>a</u>ha AAal<u>a</u> kulli shay-in qadeer<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>1.  Praise be to God,<br> Who created (out of nothing)<br> The heavens and the earth,<br> Who made the angels<br> Messengers with wings,—<br> Two, or three, or four (Pairs):<br> He adds to Creation<br> As He pleases: for God<br> Has power over all things.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/035/035_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  M<u>a</u> yafta<u>h</u>i All<u>a</u>hu li<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si min ra<u>h</u>matin fal<u>a</u> mumsika lah<u>a</u> wam<u>a</u> yumsik fal<u>a</u> mursila lahu min baAAdihi wahuwa alAAazeezu al<u>h</u>akeem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>2.  What God out of His Mercy<br> Doth bestow on mankind<br> There is none can withhold:<br> What He doth withhold,<br>There is none can grant,<br> Apart from Him:<br> And He is the Exalted<br> In Power, Full of Wisdom.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/035/035_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>su o<u>th</u>kuroo niAAmata All<u>a</u>hi AAalaykum hal min kh<u>a</u>liqin ghayru All<u>a</u>hi yarzuqukum mina a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>-i wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i l<u>a</u> il<u>a</u>ha ill<u>a</u> huwa faann<u>a</u> tu/fakoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>3.  O men! call to mind<br> The grace of God unto you!<br> Is there a Creator, other<br> Than God, to give you<br> Sustenance from heaven<br> Or earth? There is<br> No god but He: how<br> Then are ye deluded<br> Away from the Truth?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/035/035_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  Wa-in yuka<u>thth</u>ibooka faqad kut<u>hth</u>ibat rusulun min qablika wa-il<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi turjaAAu al-omoor<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>4.  And if they reject thee,<br> So were apostles rejected<br> Before thee: to God<br> Go back for decision<br> All affairs.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/035/035_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>su inna waAAda All<u>a</u>hi <u>h</u>aqqun fal<u>a</u> taghurrannakumu al<u>h</u>ay<u>a</u>tu a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> wal<u>a</u> yaghurrannakum bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi algharoor<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>5.  O men! certainly<br> The promise of God<br> Is true. Let not then<br> This present life deceive you,<br>Nor let the Chief Deceiver<br> Deceive you about God.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/035/035_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  Inna a<b>l</b>shshay<u>ta</u>na lakum AAaduwwun fa<b>i</b>ttakhi<u>th</u>oohu AAaduwwan innam<u>a</u> yadAAoo <u>h</u>izbahu liyakoonoo min a<u>s</u>-<u>ha</u>bi a<b>l</b>ssaAAeer<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>6.  Verily Satan is an enemy<br> To you: so treat him<br> As an enemy. He only<br> Invites his adherents,<br> That they may become<br> Companions of the Blazing Fire.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/035/035_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  Alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo lahum AAa<u>tha</u>bun shadeedun wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo waAAamiloo a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>li<u>ha</u>ti lahum maghfiratun waajrun kabeer<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>7.  For those who reject God,<br> Is a terrible Penalty: but<br> For those who believe<br> And work righteous deeds,<br> Is Forgiveness, and<br> A magnificent Reward.</p> <div class="filenav">
