 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra LXI.: Ṣaff, or Battle Array.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 2</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/061/061_010.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>10.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo hal adullukum AAal<u>a</u> tij<u>a</u>ratin tunjeekum min AAa<u>tha</u>bin aleem<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>10.  O ye who believe!<br> Shall I lead you<br> To a bargain that will<br> Save you from<br> A grievous Penalty?—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/061/061_011.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>11.  Tu/minoona bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi warasoolihi watuj<u>a</u>hidoona fee sabeeli All<u>a</u>hi bi-amw<u>a</u>likum waanfusikum <u>tha</u>likum khayrun lakum in kuntum taAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>11.  That ye believe in God<br> And His Apostle, and that<br> Ye strive (your utmost)<br> In the Cause of God,<br> With your property<br> And your persons:<br> That will be best for you,<br> If ye but knew!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/061/061_012.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>12.  Yaghfir lakum <u>th</u>unoobakum wayudkhilkum jann<u>a</u>tin tajree min ta<u>h</u>tih<u>a</u> al-anh<u>a</u>ru wamas<u>a</u>kina <u>t</u>ayyibatan fee jann<u>a</u>ti AAadnin <u>tha</u>lika alfawzu alAAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>12.  He will forgive you<br> Your sins, and admit you<br> To Gardens beneath which<br> Rivers flow, and to beautiful<br> Mansions in Gardens<br> Of Eternity: that is indeed<br> The supreme Achievement.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/061/061_013.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>13.  Waokhr<u>a</u> tu<u>h</u>ibboonah<u>a</u> na<u>s</u>run mina All<u>a</u>hi wafat<u>h</u>un qarreebun wabashshiri almu/mineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>13.  And another (favour<br> Will He bestow), which ye<br> Do love,—help from God<br> And a speedy victory.<br> So give the Glad Tidings<br> To the Believers.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/061/061_014.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>14.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo koonoo an<u>sa</u>ra All<u>a</u>hi kam<u>a</u> q<u>a</u>la AAees<u>a</u> ibnu maryama lil<u>h</u>aw<u>a</u>riyyeena man an<u>sa</u>ree il<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi q<u>a</u>la al<u>h</u>aw<u>a</u>riyyoona na<u>h</u>nu an<u>sa</u>ru All<u>a</u>hi fa<u>a</u>manat <u>ta</u>-ifatun min banee isr<u>a</u>-eela wakafarat <u>ta</u>-ifatun faayyadn<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo AAal<u>a</u> AAaduwwihim faa<u>s</u>ba<u>h</u>oo <i><u>th</u></i><u>a</u>hireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>14.  O ye who believe!<br> Be ye helpers of God:<br> As said Jesus the son of Mary<br> To the Disciples, "Who will be<br> My helpers to (the work<br> Of) God?" Said the Disciples,<br>"We are God's helpers!"<br> Then a portion of the Children<br> Of Israel believed, and<br> A portion disbelieved:<br> But We gave power<br> To those who believed,<br> Against their enemies,<br> And they became<br> The ones that prevailed.</p>  <div class="filenav">
