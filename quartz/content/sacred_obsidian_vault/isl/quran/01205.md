 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_036.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>36.  Wadakhala maAAahu a<b>l</b>ssijna fatay<u>a</u>ni q<u>a</u>la a<u>h</u>aduhum<u>a</u> innee ar<u>a</u>nee aAA<u>s</u>iru khamran waq<u>a</u>la al-<u>a</u>kharu innee ar<u>a</u>nee a<u>h</u>milu fawqa ra/see khubzan ta/kulu a<b>l</b><u>tt</u>ayru minhu nabbi/n<u>a</u> bita/weelihi inn<u>a</u> nar<u>a</u>ka mina almu<u>h</u>sineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>36.  Now with him there came<br> Into the prison two young men.<br> Said one of them: "I see<br> Myself (in a dream)<br> Pressing wine." Said the other:<br> "I see myself (in a dream)<br> Carrying bread on my head,<br> And birds are eating thereof."<br> "Tell us" (they said) "the truth<br> And meaning thereof: for we<br> See thou art one<br> That doth good (to all)."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_037.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>37.  Q<u>a</u>la l<u>a</u> ya/teekum<u>a</u> <u>t</u>aAA<u>a</u>mun turzaq<u>a</u>nihi ill<u>a</u> nabba/tukum<u>a</u> bita/weelihi qabla an ya/tiyakum<u>a</u> <u>tha</u>likum<u>a</u> mimm<u>a</u> AAallamanee rabbee innee taraktu millata qawmin l<u>a</u> yu/minoona bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi wahum bi<b>a</b>l-<u>a</u>khirati hum k<u>a</u>firoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>37.  He said: "Before any food<br> Comes (in due course)<br> To feed either of you,<br> I will surely reveal<br> To you the truth<br> And meaning of this<br> Ere it befall you<br> That is part of the (Duty)<br> Which my Lord hath taught me.<br> I have (I assure you)<br>Abandoned the ways<br> Of a people that believe not<br> In God and that (even)<br> Deny the Hereafter.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_038.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>38.  Wa<b>i</b>ttabaAAtu millata <u>a</u>b<u>a</u>-ee ibr<u>a</u>heema wa-is<u>ha</u>qa wayaAAqooba m<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>na lan<u>a</u> an nushrika bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi min shay-in <u>tha</u>lika min fa<u>d</u>li All<u>a</u>hi AAalayn<u>a</u> waAAal<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si wal<u>a</u>kinna akthara a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si l<u>a</u> yashkuroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>38.  "And I follow the ways<br> Of my fathers,—Abraham,<br> Isaac, and Jacob; and never<br> Could we attribute any partners<br> Whatever to God: that (comes)<br> Of the grace of God to us<br> And to mankind: yet<br> Most men are not grateful</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_039.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>39.  Y<u>a</u> <u>sah</u>ibayi a<b>l</b>ssijni aarb<u>a</u>bun mutafarriqoona khayrun ami All<u>a</u>hu alw<u>ah</u>idu alqahh<u>a</u>r<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>39.  "O my two companions.<br> Of the prison! (I ask you):<br> Are many lords differing<br> Among themselves better,<br> Or the One God,<br> Supreme and Irresistible?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_040.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>40.  M<u>a</u> taAAbudoona min doonihi ill<u>a</u> asm<u>a</u>an sammaytumooh<u>a</u> antum wa<u>a</u>b<u>a</u>okum m<u>a</u> anzala All<u>a</u>hu bih<u>a</u> min sul<u>ta</u>nin ini al<u>h</u>ukmu ill<u>a</u> lill<u>a</u>hi amara all<u>a</u> taAAbudoo ill<u>a</u> iyy<u>a</u>hu <u>tha</u>lika a<b>l</b>ddeenu alqayyimu wal<u>a</u>kinna akthara a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si l<u>a</u> yaAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>40.  "If not Him, ye worship nothing<br> But names which ye have named,—<br> Ye and your fathers,—<br> For which God hath sent down<br> No authority: the Command<br> Is for none but God: He<br>Hath commanded that ye worship<br> None but Him: that is<br> The right religion, but<br> Most men understand not…</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_041.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>41.  Y<u>a</u> <u>sah</u>ibayi a<b>l</b>ssijni amm<u>a</u> a<u>h</u>adukum<u>a</u> fayasqee rabbahu khamran waamm<u>a</u> al-<u>a</u>kharu fayu<u>s</u>labu fata/kulu a<b>l</b><u>tt</u>ayru min ra/sihi qu<u>d</u>iya al-amru alla<u>th</u>ee feehi tastaftiy<u>a</u>n<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>41.  "O my two companions<br> Of the prison! As to one<br> Of you, he will pour out<br> The wine for his lord to drink:<br> As for the other, he will<br> Hang from the cross, and the birds<br> Will eat from off his head.<br> (So) hath been decreed<br> That matter whereof<br> Ye twain do enquire"…</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_042.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>42.  Waq<u>a</u>la lilla<u>th</u>ee <i><u>th</u></i>anna annahu n<u>a</u>jin minhum<u>a</u> o<u>th</u>kurnee AAinda rabbika faans<u>a</u>hu a<b>l</b>shshay<u>ta</u>nu <u>th</u>ikra rabbihi falabitha fee a<b>l</b>ssijni bi<u>d</u>AAa sineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>42.  And of the two,<br> To that one whom he considered<br> About to be saved, he said:<br> "Mention me to thy lord."<br>But Satan made him forget<br> To mention him to his lord:<br> And (Joseph) lingered in prison<br> A few (more) years.</p> <div class="filenav">
