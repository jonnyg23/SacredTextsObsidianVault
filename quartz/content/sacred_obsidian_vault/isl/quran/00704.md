 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra VII.: A’rāf, or the Heights</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 4</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_032.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>32.  Qul man <u>h</u>arrama zeenata All<u>a</u>hi allatee akhraja liAAib<u>a</u>dihi wa<b>al</b><u>tt</u>ayyib<u>a</u>ti mina a<b>l</b>rrizqi qul hiya lilla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo fee al<u>h</u>ay<u>a</u>ti a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> kh<u>a</u>li<u>s</u>atan yawma alqiy<u>a</u>mati ka<u>tha</u>lika nufa<u>ss</u>ilu al-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>ti liqawmin yaAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>32.  Say: Who hath forbidden<br> The beautiful (gifts) of God,<br> Which He hath produced<br> For His servants,<br> And the things, clean and pure,<br> (Which He hath provided)<br> For sustenance?<br> Say: They are, in the life<br> Of this world, for those<br> Who believe, (and) purely<br> For them on the Day<br> Of Judgment. Thus do We<br> Explain the Signs in detail<br> For those who understand.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_033.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>33.  Qul innam<u>a</u> <u>h</u>arrama rabbiya alfaw<u>ah</u>isha m<u>a</u> <i><u>th</u></i>ahara minh<u>a</u> wam<u>a</u> ba<u>t</u>ana wa<b>a</b>l-ithma wa<b>a</b>lbaghya bighayri al<u>h</u>aqqi waan tushrikoo bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi m<u>a</u> lam yunazzil bihi sul<u>ta</u>nan waan taqooloo AAal<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi m<u>a</u> l<u>a</u> taAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>33.  Say: The things that my Lord<br> Hath indeed forbidden are:<br> Shameful deeds, whether open<br> Or secret; sins and trespasses<br> Against truth or reason; assigning<br> Of partners to God, for which<br> He hath given no authority;<br> And saying things about God<br> Of which ye have no knowledge.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_034.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>34.  Walikulli ommatin ajalun fa-i<u>tha</u> j<u>a</u>a ajaluhum l<u>a</u> yasta/khiroona s<u>a</u>AAatan wal<u>a</u> yastaqdimoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>34.  To every People is a term.<br> Appointed: when their term<br> Is reached, not an hour<br> Can they cause delay,<br> Nor (an hour) can they<br> Advance (it in anticipation).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_035.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>35.  Y<u>a</u> banee <u>a</u>dama imm<u>a</u> ya/tiyannakum rusulun minkum yaqu<u>ss</u>oona AAalaykum <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tee famani ittaq<u>a</u> waa<u>s</u>la<u>h</u>a fal<u>a</u> khawfun AAalayhim wal<u>a</u> hum ya<u>h</u>zanoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>35.  O ye Children of Adam!<br> Whenever there come to you<br> Apostles from amongst you,<br> Rehearsing My Signs unto you,<br> Those who are righteous<br> And mend (their lives),—<br> On them shall be no fear<br> Nor shall they grieve.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_036.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>36.  Wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena ka<u>thth</u>aboo bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin<u>a</u> wa<b>i</b>stakbaroo AAanh<u>a</u> ol<u>a</u>-ika a<u>s</u>-<u>ha</u>bu a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>ri hum feeh<u>a</u> kh<u>a</u>lidoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>36.  But those who reject<br> Our Signs and treat them<br> With arrogance,—they<br> Are Companions of the Fire,<br> To dwell therein (for ever).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_037.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>37.  Faman a<i><u>th</u></i>lamu mimmani iftar<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi ka<u>th</u>iban aw ka<u>thth</u>aba bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tihi ol<u>a</u>-ika yan<u>a</u>luhum na<u>s</u>eebuhum mina alkit<u>a</u>bi <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> i<u>tha</u> j<u>a</u>at-hum rusulun<u>a</u> yatawaffawnahum q<u>a</u>loo ayna m<u>a</u> kuntum tadAAoona min dooni All<u>a</u>hi q<u>a</u>loo <u>d</u>alloo AAann<u>a</u> washahidoo AAal<u>a</u> anfusihim annahum k<u>a</u>noo k<u>a</u>fireen<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>37.  Who is more unjust<br> Than one who invents<br> A lie against God<br> Or rejects His Signs?<br> For such, their portion<br> Appointed must reach them<br> From the Book (of Decrees):<br> Until, when Our messengers<br> (Of death) arrive and take<br> Their souls, they say:<br> "Where are the things<br> That ye used to invoke<br> Besides God?"<br> They will reply, "They<br>Have left us in the lurch,"<br> And they will bear witness<br> Against themselves, that they<br> Had rejected God.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_038.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>38.  Q<u>a</u>la odkhuloo fee omamin qad khalat min qablikum mina aljinni wa<b>a</b>l-insi fee a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>ri kullam<u>a</u> dakhalat ommatun laAAanat okhtah<u>a</u> <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> i<u>tha</u> idd<u>a</u>rakoo feeh<u>a</u> jameeAAan q<u>a</u>lat okhr<u>a</u>hum li-ool<u>a</u>hum rabban<u>a</u> h<u>a</u>ol<u>a</u>-i a<u>d</u>alloon<u>a</u> fa<u>a</u>tihim AAa<u>tha</u>ban <u>d</u>iAAfan mina a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>ri q<u>a</u>la likullin <u>d</u>iAAfun wal<u>a</u>kin l<u>a</u> taAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>38.  He will say: "Enter ye<br> In the company of<br> The Peoples who passed away<br> Before you—men and Jinns,—<br> Into the Fire. Every time<br> A new People enters,<br> It curses its sister-People<br> (That went before), until<br> They follow each other, all<br> Into the Fire. Saith the last<br> About the first: "Our Lord!<br> It is these that misled us:<br> So give them a double<br> Penalty in the Fire."<br> He will say: "Doubled<br> For all": but this<br> Ye do not understand.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_039.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>39.  Waq<u>a</u>lat ool<u>a</u>hum li-okhr<u>a</u>hum fam<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>na lakum AAalayn<u>a</u> min fa<u>d</u>lin fa<u>th</u>ooqoo alAAa<u>tha</u>ba bim<u>a</u> kuntum taksiboon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>39.  Then the first will say<br> To the last: "See then!<br> No advantage have ye<br> Over us; so taste ye<br> Of the Penalty for all<br> That ye did!"</p> <div class="filenav">
