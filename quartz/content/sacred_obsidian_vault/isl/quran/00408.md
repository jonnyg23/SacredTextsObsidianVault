 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra IV.: Nisāa, or The Women.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 8</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_051.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>51.  Alam tara il<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena ootoo na<u>s</u>eeban mina alkit<u>a</u>bi yu/minoona bi<b>a</b>ljibti wa<b>al</b><u>tta</u>ghooti wayaqooloona lilla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo h<u>a</u>ol<u>a</u>-i ahd<u>a</u> mina alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo sabeel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>51.  Hast thou not turned<br> Thy vision to those<br> Who were given a portion<br> Of the Book? They believe<br> In Sorcery and Evil,<br> And say to the Unbelievers<br> That they are better guided<br> In the (right) way<br> Than the Believers!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_052.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>52.  Ol<u>a</u>-ika alla<u>th</u>eena laAAanahumu All<u>a</u>hu waman yalAAani All<u>a</u>hu falan tajida lahu na<u>s</u>eer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>52.  They are (men) whom<br> God hath cursed:<br> And those whom God<br> Hath cursed, thou wilt find,<br> Have no one to help.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_053.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>53.  Am lahum na<u>s</u>eebun mina almulki fa-i<u>th</u>an l<u>a</u> yu/toona a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>sa naqeer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>53.  Have they a share<br> In dominion or power?<br> Behold, they give not a farthing<br> To their fellow-men?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_054.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>54.  Am ya<u>h</u>sudoona a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>sa AAal<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> <u>a</u>t<u>a</u>humu All<u>a</u>hu min fa<u>d</u>lihi faqad <u>a</u>tayn<u>a</u> <u>a</u>la ibr<u>a</u>heema alkit<u>a</u>ba wa<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>ikmata wa<u>a</u>tayn<u>a</u>hum mulkan AAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>54.  Or do they envy mankind<br> For what God hath given them<br> Of his bounty? But We<br> Had already given the people<br> Of Abraham the Book<br> And Wisdom, and conferred<br> Upon them a great kingdom.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_055.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>55.  Faminhum man <u>a</u>mana bihi waminhum man <u>s</u>adda AAanhu wakaf<u>a</u> bijahannama saAAeer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>55.  Some of them believed,<br> And some of them averted<br> Their faces from him: and enough<br> Is Hell for a burning fire.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_056.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>56.  Inna alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin<u>a</u> sawfa nu<u>s</u>leehim n<u>a</u>ran kullam<u>a</u> na<u>d</u>ijat julooduhum baddaln<u>a</u>hum juloodan ghayrah<u>a</u> liya<u>th</u>ooqoo alAAa<u>tha</u>ba inna All<u>a</u>ha k<u>a</u>na AAazeezan <u>h</u>akeem<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>56.  Those who reject<br> Our Signs, We shall soon<br> Cast into the Fire:<br> As often as their skins<br> Are roasted through,<br> We shall change them<br> For fresh skins,<br> That they may taste<br> The Penalty: for God<br> Is Exalted in Power, Wise.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_057.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>57.  Wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo waAAamiloo a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>li<u>ha</u>ti sanudkhiluhum jann<u>a</u>tin tajree min ta<u>h</u>tih<u>a</u> al-anh<u>a</u>ru kh<u>a</u>lideena feeh<u>a</u> abadan lahum feeh<u>a</u> azw<u>a</u>jun mu<u>t</u>ahharatun wanudkhiluhum <i><u>th</u></i>illan <i><u>th</u></i>aleel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>57.  But those who believe<br> And do deeds of righteousness,<br> We shall soon admit to Gardens,<br> With rivers flowing beneath,—<br> Their eternal home:<br> Therein shall they have<br> Companions pure and holy: ye<br> We shall admit them<br> To shades, cool and ever deepening.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_058.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>58.  Inna All<u>a</u>ha ya/murukum an tu-addoo al-am<u>a</u>n<u>a</u>ti il<u>a</u> ahlih<u>a</u> wa-i<u>tha</u> <u>h</u>akamtum bayna a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si an ta<u>h</u>kumoo bi<b>a</b>lAAadli inna All<u>a</u>ha niAAimm<u>a</u> yaAAi<i><u>th</u></i>ukum bihi inna All<u>a</u>ha k<u>a</u>na sameeAAan ba<u>s</u>eer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>58.  God doth command you<br> To render back your Trusts<br> To those to whom they are due;<br> And when ye judge<br>Between man and man,<br> That ye judge with justice:<br> Verily how excellent<br> Is the teaching which He giveth you!<br> For God is He Who heareth<br> And seeth all things.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_059.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>59.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo a<u>t</u>eeAAoo All<u>a</u>ha waa<u>t</u>eeAAoo a<b>l</b>rrasoola waolee al-amri minkum fa-in tan<u>a</u>zaAAtum fee shay-in faruddoohu il<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi wa<b>al</b>rrasooli in kuntum tu/minoona bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi wa<b>a</b>lyawmi al-<u>a</u>khiri <u>tha</u>lika khayrun waa<u>h</u>sanu ta/weel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>59.  O ye who believe!<br> Obey God, and obey the Apostle,<br> And those charged<br> With authority among you.<br> If ye differ in anything<br> Among yourselves, refer it<br> To God and His Apostle,<br> If ye do believe in God<br> And the Last Day:<br> That is best, and most suitable<br> For final determination.</p> <div class="filenav">
