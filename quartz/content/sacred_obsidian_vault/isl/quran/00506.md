 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra V.: Māïda, or The Table Spread.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 6</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_035.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>35.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo ittaqoo All<u>a</u>ha wa<b>i</b>btaghoo ilayhi alwaseelata waj<u>a</u>hidoo fee sabeelihi laAAallakum tufli<u>h</u>oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>35.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">38</font></span> O ye who believe!<br> Do your duty to God,<br> Seek the means<br> Of approach unto Him,<br> And strive with might<br> And main in His cause:<br> That ye may presper.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_036.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>36.  Inna alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo law anna lahum m<u>a</u> fee al-ar<u>d</u>i jameeAAan wamithlahu maAAahu liyaftadoo bihi min AAa<u>tha</u>bi yawmi alqiy<u>a</u>mati m<u>a</u> tuqubbila minhum walahum AAa<u>tha</u>bun aleem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>36.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">39</font></span> As to those who reject<br> Faith,—if they had<br> Everything on earth,<br> And twice repeated,<br> To give as ransom<br> For the penalty of the Day<br> Of Judgment, it would<br> Never be accepted of them.<br> Theirs would be<br> A grievous Penalty.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_037.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>37.  Yureedoona an yakhrujoo mina a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>ri wam<u>a</u> hum bikh<u>a</u>rijeena minh<u>a</u> walahum AAa<u>tha</u>bun muqeem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>37.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">40</font></span> Their wish will be<br> To get out of the Fire,<br> But never will they<br> Get out therefrom:<br>Their Penalty will be<br> One that endures.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_038.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>38.  Wa<b>al</b>ss<u>a</u>riqu wa<b>al</b>ss<u>a</u>riqatu fa<b>i</b>q<u>t</u>aAAoo aydiyahum<u>a</u> jaz<u>a</u>an bim<u>a</u> kasab<u>a</u> nak<u>a</u>lan mina All<u>a</u>hi wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu AAazeezun <u>h</u>akeem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>38.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">41</font></span> As to the thief,<br> Male or female,<br> Cut off his or her hands:<br> A punishment by way<br> Of example, from God,<br> For their crime:<br> And God is Exalted in Power.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_039.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>39.  Faman t<u>a</u>ba min baAAdi <i><u>th</u></i>ulmihi waa<u>s</u>la<u>h</u>a fa-inna All<u>a</u>ha yatoobu AAalayhi inna All<u>a</u>ha ghafoorun ra<u>h</u>eem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>39.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">42</font></span> But if the thief repent<br> After his crime,<br> And amend his conduct,<br> God turneth to him<br> In forgiveness; for God<br> Is Oft-forgiving, Most Merciful.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_040.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>40.  Alam taAAlam anna All<u>a</u>ha lahu mulku a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i yuAAa<u>thth</u>ibu man yash<u>a</u>o wayaghfiru liman yash<u>a</u>o wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu AAal<u>a</u> kulli shay-in qadeer<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>40.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">43</font></span> Knowest thou not<br> That to God (alone)<br> Belongeth the dominion<br> Of the heavens and the earth?<br> He punisheth whom He pleaseth,<br> And He forgiveth whom He pleaseth:<br> And God hath power<br> Over all things.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_041.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>41.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>rrasoolu l<u>a</u> ya<u>h</u>zunka alla<u>th</u>eena yus<u>a</u>riAAoona fee alkufri mina alla<u>th</u>eena q<u>a</u>loo <u>a</u>mann<u>a</u> bi-afw<u>a</u>hihim walam tu/min quloobuhum wamina alla<u>th</u>eena h<u>a</u>doo samm<u>a</u>AAoona lilka<u>th</u>ibi samm<u>a</u>AAoona liqawmin <u>a</u>khareena lam ya/tooka yu<u>h</u>arrifoona alkalima min baAAdi maw<u>ad</u>iAAihi yaqooloona in ooteetum h<u>atha</u> fakhu<u>th</u>oohu wa-in lam tu/tawhu fa<b>i</b><u>hth</u>aroo waman yuridi All<u>a</u>hu fitnatahu falan tamlika lahu mina All<u>a</u>hi shay-an ol<u>a</u>-ika alla<u>th</u>eena lam yuridi All<u>a</u>hu an yu<u>t</u>ahhira quloobahum lahum fee a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> khizyun walahum fee al-<u>a</u>khirati AAa<u>tha</u>bun AAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>41.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">44</font></span> O Apostle! let not<br> Those grieve thee, who race<br> Each other into Unbelief:<br> (Whether it be) among those<br> Who say "We believe"<br> With their lips but<br> Whose hearts have no faith;<br>Or it be among the Jews,—<br> Men who will listen<br> To any lie,—will listen<br> Even to others who have<br> Never so much as come<br> To thee. They change the words<br> From their (right) times<br> And places; they say,<br> "If ye are given this,<br> Take it, but if not,<br> Beware!" If any one's trial<br> Is intended by God, thou hast<br> No authority in the least<br> For him against God.<br> For such—it is not<br> God's will to purify<br> Their hearts. For them<br> There is disgrace<br> In this world, and<br> In the Hereafter<br> A heavy punishment.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_042.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>42.  Samm<u>a</u>AAoona lilka<u>th</u>ibi akk<u>a</u>loona li<b>l</b>ssu<u>h</u>ti fa-in j<u>a</u>ooka fa<b>o</b><u>h</u>kum baynahum aw aAAri<u>d</u> AAanhum wa-in tuAAri<u>d</u> AAanhum falan ya<u>d</u>urrooka shay-an wa-in <u>h</u>akamta fa<b>o</b><u>h</u>kum baynahum bi<b>a</b>lqis<u>t</u>i inna All<u>a</u>ha yu<u>h</u>ibbu almuqsi<u>t</u>een<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>42.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">45</font></span> (They are fond of) listening<br> To falsehood, of devouring<br> Anything forbidden.<br> If they do come to thee,<br> Either judge between them,<br> Or decline to interfere.<br> If thou decline, they cannot<br> Hurt thee in the least.<br> If thou judge, judge<br> In equity between them.<br> For God loveth those<br> Who judge in equity.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_043.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>43.  Wakayfa yu<u>h</u>akkimoonaka waAAindahumu a<b>l</b>ttawr<u>a</u>tu feeh<u>a</u> <u>h</u>ukmu All<u>a</u>hi thumma yatawallawna min baAAdi <u>tha</u>lika wam<u>a</u> ol<u>a</u>-ika bi<b>a</b>lmu/mineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>43.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">46</font></span> But why do they come<br> To thee for decision,<br> When they have (their own)<br> Law before them?<br> Therein is the (plain)<br> Command of God; yet<br> Even after that, they would<br> Turn away. For they<br> Are not (really)<br> People of Faith.</p> <div class="filenav">
