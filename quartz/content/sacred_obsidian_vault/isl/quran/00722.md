 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra VII.: A’rāf, or the Heights</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 22</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_172.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>172.  Wa-i<u>th</u> akha<u>th</u>a rabbuka min banee <u>a</u>dama min <i><u>th</u></i>uhoorihim <u>th</u>urriyyatahum waashhadahum AAal<u>a</u> anfusihim alastu birabbikum q<u>a</u>loo bal<u>a</u> shahidn<u>a</u> an taqooloo yawma alqiy<u>a</u>mati inn<u>a</u> kunn<u>a</u> AAan h<u>atha</u> gh<u>a</u>fileen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>172.  When thy Lord drew forth<br> From the Children of Adam—<br>From their loins—<br> Their descendants, and made them<br> Testify concerning themselves, (saying):<br> "Am I not your Lord<br> (Who cherishes and sustains you)?"—<br> They said: "Yea!<br> We do testify!" (This), lest<br> Ye should say on the Day<br> Of Judgment: "Of this we<br> Were never mindful":</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_173.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>173.  Aw taqooloo innam<u>a</u> ashraka <u>a</u>b<u>a</u>on<u>a</u> min qablu wakunn<u>a</u> <u>th</u>urriyyatan min baAAdihim afatuhlikun<u>a</u> bim<u>a</u> faAAala almub<u>t</u>iloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>173.  Or lest ye should say:<br> "Our fathers before us<br> May have taken false gods,<br> But we are (their) descendants<br> After them: wilt Thou then<br> Destroy us because of the deeds<br> Of men who were futile?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_174.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>174.  Waka<u>tha</u>lika nufa<u>ss</u>ilu al-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>ti walaAAallahum yarjiAAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>174.  Thus do We explain<br> The Signs in detail;<br> And perchance they may turn<br> (Unto Us).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_175.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>175.  Wa<b>o</b>tlu AAalayhim nabaa alla<u>th</u>ee <u>a</u>tayn<u>a</u>hu <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin<u>a</u> fa<b>i</b>nsalakha minh<u>a</u> faatbaAAahu a<b>l</b>shshay<u>ta</u>nu fak<u>a</u>na mina algh<u>a</u>ween<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>175.  Relate to them the story<br> Of the man to whom<br> We sent Our Signs,<br> But he passed them by:<br>So Satan followed him up,<br> And he went astray.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_176.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>176.  Walaw shi/n<u>a</u> larafaAAn<u>a</u>hu bih<u>a</u> wal<u>a</u>kinnahu akhlada il<u>a</u> al-ar<u>d</u>i wa<b>i</b>ttabaAAa haw<u>a</u>hu famathaluhu kamathali alkalbi in ta<u>h</u>mil AAalayhi yalhath aw tatruk-hu yalhath <u>tha</u>lika mathalu alqawmi alla<u>th</u>eena ka<u>thth</u>aboo bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin<u>a</u> fa<b>o</b>q<u>s</u>u<u>s</u>i alqa<u>s</u>a<u>s</u>a laAAallahum yatafakkaroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>176.  If it had been Our Will,<br> We should have elevated him<br> With Our Signs; but he<br> Inclined to the earth,<br> And followed his own vain desires.<br> His similitude is that<br> Of a dog: if you attack<br> Him, he lolls out his tongue,<br> Or if you leave him alone,<br> He (still) lolls out his tongue.<br> That is the similitude<br> Of those who reject Our Signs;<br> So relate the story;<br> Perchance they may reflect.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_177.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>177.  S<u>a</u>a mathalan alqawmu alla<u>th</u>eena ka<u>thth</u>aboo bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin<u>a</u> waanfusahum k<u>a</u>noo ya<i><u>th</u></i>limoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>177.  Evil as an example are<br> People who reject Our Signs<br> And wrong their own souls.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_178.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>178.  Man yahdi All<u>a</u>hu fahuwa almuhtadee waman yu<u>d</u>lil faol<u>a</u>-ika humu alkh<u>a</u>siroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>178.  Whom God doth guide,<br> He is on the right path:<br> Whom He rejects from His guidance,—<br> Such are the persons who perish.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_179.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>179.  Walaqad <u>th</u>ara/n<u>a</u> lijahannama katheeran mina aljinni wa<b>a</b>l-insi lahum quloobun l<u>a</u> yafqahoona bih<u>a</u> walahum aAAyunun l<u>a</u> yub<u>s</u>iroona bih<u>a</u> walahum <u>atha</u>nun l<u>a</u> yasmaAAoona bih<u>a</u> ol<u>a</u>-ika ka<b>a</b>l-anAA<u>a</u>mi bal hum a<u>d</u>allu ol<u>a</u>-ika humu algh<u>a</u>filoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>179.  Many are the Jinns and men<br> We have made for Hell:<br> They have hearts wherewith they<br> Understand not, eyes wherewith<br>They see not, and ears wherewith<br> They hear not. They are<br> Like cattle,—nay more<br> Misguided: for they<br> Are heedless (of warning).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_180.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>180.  Walill<u>a</u>hi al-asm<u>a</u>o al<u>h</u>usn<u>a</u> fa<b>o</b>dAAoohu bih<u>a</u> wa<u>th</u>aroo alla<u>th</u>eena yul<u>h</u>idoona fee asm<u>a</u>-ihi sayujzawna m<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo yaAAmaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>180.  The most beautiful names<br> Belong to God:<br> So call on him by them;<br> But shun such men as<br> Use profanity in His names:<br> For what they do, they will<br> Soon be requited.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_181.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>181.  Wamimman khalaqn<u>a</u> ommatun yahdoona bi<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>aqqi wabihi yaAAdiloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>181.  Of those We have created<br> Are people who direct<br> (Others) with truth.<br> And dispense justice therewith,</p> <div class="filenav">
