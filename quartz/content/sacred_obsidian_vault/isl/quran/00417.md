 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_113.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>113.  Walawl<u>a</u> fa<u>d</u>lu All<u>a</u>hi AAalayka wara<u>h</u>matuhu lahammat <u>ta</u>-ifatun minhum an yu<u>d</u>illooka wam<u>a</u> yu<u>d</u>illoona ill<u>a</u> anfusahum wam<u>a</u> ya<u>d</u>urroonaka min shay-in waanzala All<u>a</u>hu AAalayka alkit<u>a</u>ba wa<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>ikmata waAAallamaka m<u>a</u> lam takun taAAlamu wak<u>a</u>na fa<u>d</u>lu All<u>a</u>hi AAalayka AAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>113.  But for the Grace of God<br> To thee and His Mercy,<br> A party of them would<br> Certainly have plotted<br> To lead thee astray.<br> But (in fact) they will only<br> Lead their own souls astray,<br> And to thee they can do<br> No harm in the least.<br> For God hath sent down<br> To thee the Book and Wisdom<br> And taught thee what thou<br> Knewest not (before):<br> And great is the Grace<br> Of God unto thee.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_114.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>114.  L<u>a</u> khayra fee katheerin min najw<u>a</u>hum ill<u>a</u> man amara bi<u>s</u>adaqatin aw maAAroofin aw i<u>s</u>l<u>ah</u>in bayna a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si waman yafAAal <u>tha</u>lika ibtigh<u>a</u>a mar<u>da</u>ti All<u>a</u>hi fasawfa nu/teehi ajran AAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>114.  In most of their secret talks<br> There is no good: but if<br> One exhorts to a deed<br> Of charity or justice<br> Or conciliation between men,<br> (Secrecy is permissible):<br> To him who does this,<br> Seeking the good pleasure<br> Of God, We shall soon give<br> A reward of the highest (value).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_115.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>115.  Waman yush<u>a</u>qiqi a<b>l</b>rrasoola min baAAdi m<u>a</u> tabayyana lahu alhud<u>a</u> wayattabiAA ghayra sabeeli almu/mineena nuwallihi m<u>a</u> tawall<u>a</u> wanu<u>s</u>lihi jahannama was<u>a</u>at ma<u>s</u>eer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>115.  If anyone contends with<br> The Apostle even after<br> Guidance has been plainly<br> Conveyed to him, and follows<br> A path other than that<br> Becoming to men of Faith,<br> We shall leave him<br> In the path he has chosen,<br> And land him in Hell,—<br> What an evil refuge!</p> <div class="filenav">
