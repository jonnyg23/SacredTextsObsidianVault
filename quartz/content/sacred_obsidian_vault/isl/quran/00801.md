 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra VIII.: Anfāl, or the Spoils of War.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/008/008_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  Yas-aloonaka AAani al-anf<u>a</u>li quli al-anf<u>a</u>lu lill<u>a</u>hi wa<b>al</b>rrasooli fa<b>i</b>ttaqoo All<u>a</u>ha waa<u>s</u>li<u>h</u>oo <u>tha</u>ta baynikum waa<u>t</u>eeAAoo All<u>a</u>ha warasoolahu in kuntum mu/mineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>1.  They ask thee concerning<br> (Things taken as) spoils of war.<br> Say: "(Such) spoils are<br> At the disposal of God<br> And the Apostle: so fear<br> God, and keep straight<br> The relations between yourselves:<br> Obey God and His Apostle,<br> If ye do believe."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/008/008_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  Innam<u>a</u> almu/minoona alla<u>th</u>eena i<u>tha</u> <u>th</u>ukira All<u>a</u>hu wajilat quloobuhum wa-i<u>tha</u> tuliyat AAalayhim <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tuhu z<u>a</u>dat-hum eem<u>a</u>nan waAAal<u>a</u> rabbihim yatawakkaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>2.  For, Believers are those<br> Who, when God is mentioned,<br> Feel a tremor in their hearts,<br> And when they hear<br> His Signs rehearsed, find<br> Their faith strengthened,<br> And put (all) their trust<br> In their Lord;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/008/008_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  Alla<u>th</u>eena yuqeemoona a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>al<u>a</u>ta wamimm<u>a</u> razaqn<u>a</u>hum yunfiqoon<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>3.  Who establish regular prayers<br> And spend (freely) out of<br> The gifts We have given<br> Them for sustenance:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/008/008_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  Ol<u>a</u>-ika humu almu/minoona <u>h</u>aqqan lahum daraj<u>a</u>tun AAinda rabbihim wamaghfiratun warizqun kareem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>4.  Such in truth are the Believers:<br> They have grades of dignity<br> With their Lord, and forgiveness,<br> And generous sustenance:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/008/008_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  Kam<u>a</u> akhrajaka rabbuka min baytika bi<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>aqqi wa-inna fareeqan mina almu/mineena lak<u>a</u>rihoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>5.  Just as thy Lord ordered thee<br> Out of thy house in truth,<br> Even though a party among<br> The Believers disliked it,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/008/008_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  Yuj<u>a</u>diloonaka fee al<u>h</u>aqqi baAAda m<u>a</u> tabayyana kaannam<u>a</u> yus<u>a</u>qoona il<u>a</u> almawti wahum yan<i><u>th</u></i>uroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>6.  Disputing with thee concerning<br> The truth after it was made<br> Manifest, as if they were<br> Being driven to death<br> And they (actually) saw it.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/008/008_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  Wa-i<u>th</u> yaAAidukumu All<u>a</u>hu i<u>h</u>d<u>a</u> a<b>l</b><u>tta</u>-ifatayni annah<u>a</u> lakum watawaddoona anna ghayra <u>tha</u>ti a<b>l</b>shshawkati takoonu lakum wayureedu All<u>a</u>hu an yu<u>h</u>iqqa al<u>h</u>aqqa bikalim<u>a</u>tihi wayaq<u>t</u>aAAa d<u>a</u>bira alk<u>a</u>fireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>7.  Behold! God promised you<br> One of the two (enemy) parties,<br> That it should be yours:<br> Ye wished that the one<br> Unarmed should be yours,<br> But God willed<br> To justify the Truth<br> According to His words,<br> And to cut off the roots<br> Of the Unbelievers;—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/008/008_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.   Liyu<u>h</u>iqqa al<u>h</u>aqqa wayub<u>t</u>ila alb<u>at</u>ila walaw kariha almujrimoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>8.  That He might justify Truth<br> And prove Falsehood false,<br> Distasteful though it be<br> To those in guilt.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/008/008_009.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>9.  I<u>th</u> tastagheethoona rabbakum fa<b>i</b>staj<u>a</u>ba lakum annee mumiddukum bi-alfin mina almal<u>a</u>-ikati murdifeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>9.  Remember ye implored<br> The assistance of your Lord,<br> And He answered you:<br> "I will assist you<br> With a thousand of the angels,<br> Ranks on ranks."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/008/008_010.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>10.  Wam<u>a</u> jaAAalahu All<u>a</u>hu ill<u>a</u> bushr<u>a</u> walita<u>t</u>ma-inna bihi quloobukum wam<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nna<u>s</u>ru ill<u>a</u> min AAindi All<u>a</u>hi inna All<u>a</u>ha AAazeezun <u>h</u>akeem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>10.  God made it but a message<br> Of hope, and an assurance<br> To your hearts: (in any case)<br> There is no help.<br> Except from God:<br> And God is Exalted in Power,<br> Wise.</p> <div class="filenav">
