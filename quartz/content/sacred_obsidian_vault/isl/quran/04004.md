 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XL.: Mū-min, or The Believer.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 4</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/040/040_028.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>28.  Waq<u>a</u>la rajulun mu/minun min <u>a</u>li firAAawna yaktumu eem<u>a</u>nahu ataqtuloona rajulan an yaqoola rabbiyya All<u>a</u>hu waqad j<u>a</u>akum bi<b>a</b>lbayyin<u>a</u>ti min rabbikum wa-in yaku k<u>ath</u>iban faAAalayhi ka<u>th</u>ibuhu wa-in yaku <u>sa</u>diqan yu<u>s</u>ibkum baAA<u>d</u>u alla<u>th</u>ee yaAAidukum inna All<u>a</u>ha l<u>a</u> yahdee man huwa musrifun ka<u>ththa</u>b<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>28.  A Believer, a man<br> From among the people<br> Of Pharaoh, who had concealed<br> His faith, said: "Will ye<br> Slay a man because he<br> Says, "My Lord is God"?—<br> When he has indeed come<br> To you with Clear (Signs)<br> From your Lord? And if<br> He be a liar, on him<br> Is (the sin of) his lie;<br> But, if he is telling<br> The Truth, then will<br> Fall on you something<br> Of the (calamity) of which<br> He warns you: truly<br> God guides not one<br> Who transgresses and lies!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/040/040_029.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>29.  Y<u>a</u> qawmi lakumu almulku alyawma <i><u>th</u></i><u>a</u>hireena fee al-ar<u>d</u>i faman yan<u>s</u>urun<u>a</u> min ba/si All<u>a</u>hi in j<u>a</u>an<u>a</u> q<u>a</u>la firAAawnu m<u>a</u> oreekum ill<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> ar<u>a</u> wam<u>a</u> ahdeekum ill<u>a</u> sabeela a<b>l</b>rrash<u>a</u>d<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>29.  "O my People! yours<br> Is the dominion this day:<br>Ye have the upper hand<br> In the land: but who<br> Will help us from<br> The Punishment of God,<br> Should it befall us?"<br> Pharaoh said: "I but<br> Point out to you that<br> Which I see (myself);<br> Nor do I guide you<br> But to the Path of Right!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/040/040_030.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>30.  Waq<u>a</u>la alla<u>th</u>ee <u>a</u>mana y<u>a</u> qawmi innee akh<u>a</u>fu AAalaykum mithla yawmi al-a<u>h</u>z<u>a</u>b<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>30.  Then said the man<br> Who believed: "O my People!<br> Truly I do fear<br> For you something like<br> The Day (of disaster)<br> Of the Confederates (in sin)!—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/040/040_031.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>31.  Mithla da/bi qawmi noo<u>h</u>in waAA<u>a</u>din wathamooda wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena min baAAdihim wam<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hu yureedu <i><u>th</u></i>ulman lilAAib<u>a</u>d<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>31.  "Something like the fate<br> Of the People of Noah,<br> The ‘Ād, and the <u>Th</u>amūd,<br> And those who came<br> After them: but God<br> Never wishes injustice<br> To His Servants.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/040/040_032.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>32.  Way<u>a</u> qawmi innee akh<u>a</u>fu AAalaykum yawma a<b>l</b>ttan<u>a</u>d<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>32.  "And O my People!<br> I fear for you a Day<br> When there will be<br> Mutual calling (and wailing),—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/040/040_033.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>33.  Yawma tuwalloona mudbireena m<u>a</u> lakum mina All<u>a</u>hi min AA<u>as</u>imin waman yu<u>d</u>lili All<u>a</u>hu fam<u>a</u> lahu min h<u>a</u>d<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>33.  "A Day when ye<br> Shall turn your backs<br> And flee: no defender<br> Shall ye have from God:<br> Any whom God leaves<br> To stray, there is none<br> To guide…</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/040/040_034.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>34.  Walaqad j<u>a</u>akum yoosufu min qablu bi<b>a</b>lbayyin<u>a</u>ti fam<u>a</u> ziltum fee shakkin mimm<u>a</u> j<u>a</u>akum bihi <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> i<u>tha</u> halaka qultum lan yabAAatha All<u>a</u>hu min baAAdihi rasoolan ka<u>tha</u>lika yu<u>d</u>illu All<u>a</u>hu man huwa musrifun murt<u>a</u>b<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>34.  "And to you there came<br> Joseph in times gone by,<br> With Clear Signs, but<br> Ye ceased not to doubt<br> Of the (mission) for which<br> He had come: at length,<br> When he died, ye said:<br> "No apostle will God send<br> After him." Thus doth God<br> Leave to stray such as<br> Transgress and live in doubt,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/040/040_035.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>35.  Alla<u>th</u>eena yuj<u>a</u>diloona fee <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>ti All<u>a</u>hi bighayri sul<u>ta</u>nin at<u>a</u>hum kabura maqtan AAinda All<u>a</u>hi waAAinda alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo ka<u>tha</u>lika ya<u>t</u>baAAu All<u>a</u>hu AAal<u>a</u> kulli qalbi mutakabbirin jabb<u>a</u>r<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>35.  "(Such) as dispute about<br> The Signs of God,<br> Without any authority<br> That hath reached them.<br> Grievous and odious<br> (Is such conduct)<br> In the sight of God<br> And of the Believers.<br> Thus doth God seal up<br> Every heart—of arrogant<br> And obstinate transgressors."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/040/040_036.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>36.  Waq<u>a</u>la firAAawnu y<u>a</u> h<u>a</u>m<u>a</u>nu ibni lee <u>s</u>ar<u>h</u>an laAAallee ablughu al-asb<u>a</u>b<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>36.  Pharaoh said: "O Hāmān!<br> Build me a lofty palace,<br> That I may attain<br> The ways and means—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/040/040_037.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>37.  Asb<u>a</u>ba a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti faa<u>tt</u>aliAAa il<u>a</u> il<u>a</u>hi moos<u>a</u> wa-innee laa<i><u>th</u></i>unnuhu k<u>ath</u>iban waka<u>tha</u>lika zuyyina lifirAAawna soo-o AAamalihi wa<u>s</u>udda AAani a<b>l</b>ssabeeli wam<u>a</u> kaydu firAAawna ill<u>a</u> fee tab<u>a</u>b<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>37.  "The ways and means<br> Of (reaching) the heavens,<br> And that I may mount up<br> To the God of Moses:<br> But as far as I am concerned,<br> I think (Moses) is a liar!"<br> Thus was made alluring,<br> In Pharaoh's eyes,<br> The evil of his deeds,<br> And he was hindered<br> From the Path; and the plot<br> Of Pharaoh led to nothing<br> But perdition (for him).</p> <div class="filenav">
