 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_190.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>190.  Inna fee khalqi a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i wa<b>i</b>khtil<u>a</u>fi allayli wa<b>al</b>nnah<u>a</u>ri la<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin li-olee al-alb<u>a</u>b<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>190.  Behold! In the creation<br> Of the heavens and the earth,<br> And the alternation<br> Of Night and Day,—<br> There are indeed Signs<br> For men of understanding,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_191.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>191.  Alla<u>th</u>eena ya<u>th</u>kuroona All<u>a</u>ha qiy<u>a</u>man waquAAoodan waAAal<u>a</u> junoobihim wayatafakkaroona fee khalqi a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i rabban<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> khalaqta h<u>atha</u> b<u>at</u>ilan sub<u>ha</u>naka faqin<u>a</u> AAa<u>tha</u>ba a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>r<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>191.  Men who celebrate<br> The praises of God,<br> Standing, sitting,<br> And lying down on their sides,<br> And contemplate<br> The (wonders of) creation<br>In the heavens and the earth,<br> (With the thought):<br> "Our Lord! not for naught<br> Hast Thou created (all) this!<br> Glory to Thee! Give us<br> Salvation from the Penalty<br> Of the Fire.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_192.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>192.  Rabban<u>a</u> innaka man tudkhili a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>ra faqad akhzaytahu wam<u>a</u> li<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limeena min an<u>sa</u>r<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>192.  "Our Lord! any whom Thou<br> Dost admit to the Fire,<br> Truly Thou coverest with shame,<br> And never will wrong-doers<br> Find any helpers!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_193.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>193.  Rabban<u>a</u> innan<u>a</u> samiAAn<u>a</u> mun<u>a</u>diyan yun<u>a</u>dee lil-eem<u>a</u>ni an <u>a</u>minoo birabbikum fa<u>a</u>mann<u>a</u> rabban<u>a</u> fa<b>i</b>ghfir lan<u>a</u> <u>th</u>unooban<u>a</u> wakaffir AAann<u>a</u> sayyi-<u>a</u>tin<u>a</u> watawaffan<u>a</u> maAAa al-abr<u>a</u>r<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>193.  "Our Lord! we have heard<br> The call of one calling<br> (Us) to Faith, 'Believe ye<br> In the Lord,' and we<br> Have believed. Our Lord!<br> Forgive us our sins,<br> Blot out from us<br> Our iniquities, and take<br> To Thyself our souls<br> In the company of the righteous.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_194.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>194.  Rabban<u>a</u> wa<u>a</u>tin<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> waAAadtan<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> rusulika wal<u>a</u> tukhzin<u>a</u> yawma alqiy<u>a</u>mati innaka l<u>a</u> tukhlifu almeeAA<u>a</u>d<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>194.  "Our Lord! Grant us<br> What Thou didst promise<br> Unto us through Thine Apostles,<br> And save us from shame<br> On the Day of Judgment:<br> For Thou never breakest<br> Thy promise."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_195.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>195.  Fa<b>i</b>staj<u>a</u>ba lahum rabbuhum annee l<u>a</u> o<u>d</u>eeAAu AAamala AA<u>a</u>milin minkum min <u>th</u>akarin aw onth<u>a</u> baAA<u>d</u>ukum min baAA<u>d</u>in fa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena h<u>a</u>jaroo waokhrijoo min diy<u>a</u>rihim waoo<u>th</u>oo fee sabeelee waq<u>a</u>taloo waqutiloo laokaffiranna AAanhum sayyi-<u>a</u>tihim walaodkhilannahum jann<u>a</u>tin tajree min ta<u>h</u>tih<u>a</u> al-anh<u>a</u>ru thaw<u>a</u>ban min AAindi All<u>a</u>hi wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu AAindahu <u>h</u>usnu a<b>l</b>ththaw<u>a</u>b<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>195.  And their Lord hath accepted<br> Of them, and answered them:<br> "Never will I suffer to be lost<br> The work of any of you,<br>Be he male or female:<br> Ye are members, one of another:<br> Those who have left their homes,<br> Or been driven out therefrom,<br> Or suffered harm in My Cause,<br> Or fought or been slain,—<br> Verily, I will blot out<br> From them their iniquities,<br> And admit them into Gardens<br> With rivers flowing beneath;—<br> A reward from the Presence<br> Of God, and from His Presence<br> Is the best of rewards."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_196.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>196.  L<u>a</u> yaghurrannaka taqallubu alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo fee albil<u>a</u>d<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>196.  Let not the strutting about<br> Of the Unbelievers<br> Through the land<br> Deceive thee:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_197.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>197.  Mat<u>a</u>AAun qaleelun thumma ma/w<u>a</u>hum jahannamu wabi/sa almih<u>a</u>d<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>197.  Little is it for enjoyment:<br> Their ultimate abode<br> Is Hell: what an evil bed<br> (To lie on)!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_198.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>198.  L<u>a</u>kini alla<u>th</u>eena ittaqaw rabbahum lahum jann<u>a</u>tun tajree min ta<u>h</u>tih<u>a</u> al-anh<u>a</u>ru kh<u>a</u>lideena feeh<u>a</u> nuzulan min AAindi All<u>a</u>hi wam<u>a</u> AAinda All<u>a</u>hi khayrun lil-abr<u>a</u>r<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>198.  On the other hand, for those<br> Who fear their Lord,<br> Are Gardens, with rivers<br> Flowing beneath; therein<br> Are they to dwell (for ever),—<br> A gift from the Presence<br> Of God; and that which is<br> In the Presence of God<br> Is the best (bliss)<br> For the righteous.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_199.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>199.  Wa-inna min ahli alkit<u>a</u>bi laman yu/minu bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi wam<u>a</u> onzila ilaykum wam<u>a</u> onzila ilayhim kh<u>a</u>shiAAeena lill<u>a</u>hi l<u>a</u> yashtaroona bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>ti All<u>a</u>hi thamanan qaleelan ol<u>a</u>-ika lahum ajruhum AAinda rabbihim inna All<u>a</u>ha sareeAAu al<u>h</u>is<u>a</u>b<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>199.  And there are, certainly,<br> Among the People of the Book,<br>Those who believe in God,<br> In the revelation to you,<br> And in the revelation to them,<br> Bowing in humility to God:<br> They will not sell<br> The Signs of God<br> For a miserable gain!<br> For them is a reward<br> With their Lord,<br> And God is swift in account.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_200.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>200.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo i<u>s</u>biroo wa<u>sa</u>biroo war<u>a</u>bi<u>t</u>oo wa<b>i</b>ttaqoo All<u>a</u>ha laAAallakum tufli<u>h</u>oon<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>200.  O ye who believe!<br> Persevere in patience<br> And constancy; vie<br> In such perseverance;<br> Strengthen each other;<br> And fear God;<br> That ye may prosper.</p>  <div class="filenav">
