 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXXV.: Fāṭir, or The Originator of Creation; or Malāïka, or The Angels.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 2</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/035/035_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  Afaman zuyyina lahu soo-o AAamalihi fara<u>a</u>hu <u>h</u>asanan fa-inna All<u>a</u>ha yu<u>d</u>illu man yash<u>a</u>o wayahdee man yash<u>a</u>o fal<u>a</u> ta<u>th</u>hab nafsuka AAalayhim <u>h</u>asar<u>a</u>tin inna All<u>a</u>ha AAaleemun bim<u>a</u> ya<u>s</u>naAAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>8.  Is he, then, to whom<br> The evil of his conduct<br> Is made alluring, so<br> That he looks upon it<br> As good, (equal to one<br> Who is rightly guided)?<br> For God leaves to stray<br> Whom He wills, and guides<br> Whom He wills. So<br> Let not thy soul go out<br> In (vainly) sighing after them:<br> For God knows well<br> All that they do!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/035/035_009.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>9.  Wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu alla<u>th</u>ee arsala a<b>l</b>rriy<u>ah</u>a fatutheeru sa<u>ha</u>ban fasuqn<u>a</u>hu il<u>a</u> baladin mayyitin faa<u>h</u>yayn<u>a</u> bihi al-ar<u>d</u>a baAAda mawtih<u>a</u> ka<u>tha</u>lika a<b>l</b>nnushoor<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>9.  It is God Who sends<br> Forth the Winds, so that<br>They raise up the Clouds,<br> And We drive them<br> To a land that is dead,<br> And revive the earth therewith<br> After its death: even so<br> (Will be) the Resurrection!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/035/035_010.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>10.  Man k<u>a</u>na yureedu alAAizzata falill<u>a</u>hi alAAizzatu jameeAAan ilayhi ya<u>s</u>AAadu alkalimu a<b>l</b><u>tt</u>ayyibu wa<b>a</b>lAAamalu a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>li<u>h</u>u yarfaAAuhu wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena yamkuroona a<b>l</b>ssayyi-<u>a</u>ti lahum AAa<u>tha</u>bun shadeedun wamakru ol<u>a</u>-ika huwa yaboor<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>10.  If any do seek<br> For glory and power,—<br> To God belong<br> All glory and power.<br> To Him mount up<br> (All) Words of Purity:<br> It is He Who exalts<br> Each Deed of Righteousness.<br> Those that lay Plots<br> Of Evil,—for them<br> Is a Penalty terrible;<br> And the plotting of such<br> Will be void (of result).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/035/035_011.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>11.  Wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu khalaqakum min tur<u>a</u>bin thumma min nu<u>t</u>fatin thumma jaAAalakum azw<u>a</u>jan wam<u>a</u> ta<u>h</u>milu min onth<u>a</u> wal<u>a</u> ta<u>d</u>aAAu ill<u>a</u> biAAilmihi wam<u>a</u> yuAAammaru min muAAammarin wal<u>a</u> yunqa<u>s</u>u min AAumurihi ill<u>a</u> fee kit<u>a</u>bin inna <u>tha</u>lika AAal<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi yaseer<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>11.  And God did create<br> You from dust;<br> Then from a sperm-drop;<br> Then He made you<br> In pairs. And no female<br> Conceives, or lays down<br> (Her load), but with His<br> Knowledge. Nor is a man<br>Long-lived granted length<br> Of days, nor is a part<br> Cut off from his life,<br> But is in a Decree<br> (Ordained). All this<br> Is easy to God.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/035/035_012.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>12.  Wam<u>a</u> yastawee alba<u>h</u>r<u>a</u>ni h<u>atha</u> AAa<u>th</u>bun fur<u>a</u>tun s<u>a</u>-ighun shar<u>a</u>buhu wah<u>atha</u> mil<u>h</u>un oj<u>a</u>jun wamin kullin ta/kuloona la<u>h</u>man <u>t</u>ariyyan watastakhrijoona <u>h</u>ilyatan talbasoonah<u>a</u> watar<u>a</u> alfulka feehi maw<u>a</u>khira litabtaghoo min fa<u>d</u>lihi walaAAallakum tashkuroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>12.  Nor are the two bodies<br> Of flowing water alike,—<br> The one palatable, sweet,<br> And pleasant to drink,<br> And the other, salt<br> And bitter. Yet from each<br> (Kind of water) do ye<br> Eat flesh fresh and tender,<br> And ye extract ornaments<br> To wear; and thou seest<br> The ships therein that plough<br> The waves, that ye may<br> Seek (thus) of the Bounty<br> Of God that ye<br> May be grateful.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/035/035_013.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>13.  Yooliju allayla fee a<b>l</b>nnah<u>a</u>ri wayooliju a<b>l</b>nnah<u>a</u>ra fee allayli wasakhkhara a<b>l</b>shshamsa wa<b>a</b>lqamara kullun yajree li-ajalin musamman <u>tha</u>likumu All<u>a</u>hu rabbukum lahu almulku wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena tadAAoona min doonihi m<u>a</u> yamlikoona min qi<u>t</u>meer<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>13.  He merges Night into Day,<br> And He merges Day<br>Into Night, and He has<br> Subjected the sun and<br> The moon (to His Law):<br> Each one runs its course<br> For a term appointed.<br> Such is God your Lord:<br> To Him belongs all Dominion.<br> And those whom ye invoke<br> Besides Him have not<br> The least power.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/035/035_014.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>14.  In tadAAoohum l<u>a</u> yasmaAAoo duAA<u>a</u>akum walaw samiAAoo m<u>a</u> istaj<u>a</u>boo lakum wayawma alqiy<u>a</u>mati yakfuroona bishirkikum wal<u>a</u> yunabbi-oka mithlu khabeer<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>14.  If ye invoke them,<br> They will not listen<br> To your call, and if<br> They were to listen,<br> They cannot answer<br> Your (prayer). On the Day<br> Of Judgment they will reject<br> Your "Partnership". And none,<br> (O man!) can tell thee<br> (The Truth) like the One<br> Who is acquainted with all things.</p> <div class="filenav">
