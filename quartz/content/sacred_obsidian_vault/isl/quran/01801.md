 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XVIII.: Kahf, or the Cave.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  Al<u>h</u>amdu lill<u>a</u>hi alla<u>th</u>ee anzala AAal<u>a</u> AAabdihi alkit<u>a</u>ba walam yajAAal lahu AAiwaj<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>1.  Praise be to God,<br> Who hath sent to His Servant<br> The Book, and hath allowed<br> Therein no Crookedness:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  Qayyiman liyun<u>th</u>ira ba/san shadeedan min ladunhu wayubashshira almu/mineena alla<u>th</u>eena yaAAmaloona a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>li<u>ha</u>ti anna lahum ajran <u>h</u>asan<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>2.  (He hath made it) Straight<br> (And Clear) in order that<br> He may warn (the godless)<br> Of a terrible Punishment<br> From Him, and that He<br> May give Glad Tidings<br> To the Believers who work<br> Righteous deeds, that they<br> Shall have a goodly Reward,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.   M<u>a</u>kitheena feehi abad<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>3.  Wherein they shall<br> Remain for ever:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.   Wayun<u>th</u>ira alla<u>th</u>eena q<u>a</u>loo ittakha<u>th</u>a All<u>a</u>hu walad<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>4.  Further; that He may warn<br> Those (also) who say,<br>"God hath begotten a son"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  M<u>a</u> lahum bihi min AAilmin wal<u>a</u> li-<u>a</u>b<u>a</u>-ihim kaburat kalimatan takhruju min afw<u>a</u>hihim in yaqooloona ill<u>a</u> ka<u>th</u>ib<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>5.  No knowledge have they<br> Of such a thing, nor<br> Had their fathers. It is<br> A grievous thing that issues<br> From their mouths as a saying.<br> What they say is nothing<br> But falsehood!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  FalaAAallaka b<u>a</u>khiAAun nafsaka AAal<u>a</u> <u>a</u>th<u>a</u>rihim in lam yu/minoo bih<u>atha</u> al<u>h</u>adeethi asaf<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>6.  Thou wouldst only, perchance,<br> Fret thyself to death,<br> Following after them, in grief,<br> If they believe not<br> In this Message.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  Inn<u>a</u> jaAAaln<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> al-ar<u>d</u>i zeenatan lah<u>a</u> linabluwahum ayyuhum a<u>h</u>sanu AAamal<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>7.  That which is on earth<br> We have made but as<br> A glittering show for the earth,<br> In order that We may test<br> Them—as to which of them<br> Are best in conduct.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  Wa-inn<u>a</u> laj<u>a</u>AAiloona m<u>a</u> AAalayh<u>a</u> <u>s</u>aAAeedan juruz<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>8.  Verily what is on earth<br> We shall make but as<br>Dust and dry soil<br> (Without growth or herbage).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_009.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>9.  Am <u>h</u>asibta anna a<u>s</u>-<u>ha</u>ba alkahfi wa<b>al</b>rraqeemi k<u>a</u>noo min <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin<u>a</u> AAajab<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>9.  Or dost thou reflect<br> That the Companions of the Cave<br> And of the Inscription<br> Were wonders among Our Signs?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_010.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>10.  I<u>th</u> aw<u>a</u> alfityatu il<u>a</u> alkahfi faq<u>a</u>loo rabban<u>a</u> <u>a</u>tin<u>a</u> min ladunka ra<u>h</u>matan wahayyi/ lan<u>a</u> min amrin<u>a</u> rashad<u>a</u><b>n</b><u> </u>   </p>
 <p>10.  Behold, the youths betook themselves<br>To the Cave: they said,<br> "Our Lord! bestow on us<br> Mercy from Thyself,<br> And dispose of our affair<br> For us in the right way!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_011.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>11.  Fa<u>d</u>arabn<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> <u>atha</u>nihim fee alkahfi sineena AAadad<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>11.  Then We draw (a veil)<br> Over their ears, for a number<br> Of years, in the Cave,<br> (So that they heard not):</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_012.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>12.  Thumma baAAathn<u>a</u>hum linaAAlama ayyu al<u>h</u>izbayni a<u>hsa</u> lim<u>a</u> labithoo amad<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>12.  Then We roused them,<br> In order to test which<br> Of the two parties was best<br> At calculating the term<br> Of years they had tarried!</p> <div class="filenav">
