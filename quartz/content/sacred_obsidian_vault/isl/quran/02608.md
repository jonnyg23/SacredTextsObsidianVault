 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXVI.: Shu‘arāa, or The Poets.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 8</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_141.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>141.  Ka<u>thth</u>abat thamoodu almursaleen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>141.  The <u>Th</u>amūd (people) rejected<br> The apostles.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_142.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>142.  I<u>th</u> q<u>a</u>la lahum akhoohum <u>sa</u>li<u>h</u>un al<u>a</u> tattaqoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>142.  Behold, their brother Ṣāliḥ<br> Said to them: "Will you not<br> Fear (God)?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_143.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>143.  Innee lakum rasoolun ameen<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>143.  "I am to you an apostle<br> Worthy of all trust.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_144.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>144.  Fa<b>i</b>ttaqoo All<u>a</u>ha waa<u>t</u>eeAAoon<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>144.  "So fear God, and obey me.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_145.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>145.  Wam<u>a</u> as-alukum AAalayhi min ajrin in ajriya ill<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> rabbi alAA<u>a</u>lameen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>145.  "No reward do I ask<br> Of you for it: my reward<br> Is only from the Lord<br> Of the Worlds.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_146.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>146.  Atutrakoona fee m<u>a</u> h<u>a</u>hun<u>a</u> <u>a</u>mineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>146.  "Will ye be left secure,<br> In (the enjoyment of) all<br> That ye have here?—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_147.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>147.  Fee jann<u>a</u>tin waAAuyoon<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>147.  "Gardens and Springs,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_148.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>148.  WazurooAAin wanakhlin <u>t</u>alAAuh<u>a</u> ha<u>d</u>eem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>148.  "And corn-fields and date palms<br> With spathes near breaking<br> (With the weight of fruit)?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_149.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>149.  Watan<u>h</u>itoona mina aljib<u>a</u>li buyootan f<u>a</u>riheen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>149.  "And ye carve houses<br> Out of (rocky) mountains<br> With great skill.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_150.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>150.  Fa<b>i</b>ttaqoo All<u>a</u>ha waa<u>t</u>eeAAoon<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>150.  "But fear God and obey me;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_151.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>151.  Wal<u>a</u> tu<u>t</u>eeAAoo amra almusrifeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>151.  "And follow not the bidding<br> Of those who are extravagant,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_152.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>152.  Alla<u>th</u>eena yufsidoona fee al-ar<u>d</u>i wal<u>a</u> yu<u>s</u>li<u>h</u>oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>152.  "Who make mischief in the land,<br> And mend not (their ways)."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_153.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>153.  Q<u>a</u>loo innam<u>a</u> anta mina almusa<u>hh</u>areen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>153.  Whey said: "Thou art only<br> One of those bewitched!'</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_154.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>154.  M<u>a</u> anta ill<u>a</u> basharun mithlun<u>a</u> fa/ti bi-<u>a</u>yatin in kunta mina a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>diqeena   </p>
 <p>154.  "Thou art no more than<br> A mortal like us:<br> Then bring us a Sign,<br> If thou tellest the truth!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_155.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>155.  Q<u>a</u>la h<u>ath</u>ihi n<u>a</u>qatun lah<u>a</u> shirbun walakum shirbu yawmin maAAloom<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>155.  He said: "Here is<br> A she-camel: she has<br> A right of watering,<br> And ye have a right<br> Of watering, (severally)<br> On a day appointed.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_156.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>156.  Wal<u>a</u> tamassooh<u>a</u> bisoo-in faya/khu<u>th</u>akum AAa<u>tha</u>bu yawmin AAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>156.  "Touch her not with harm,<br> Lest the Penalty<br> Of a Great Day<br> Seize you."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_157.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>157.  FaAAaqarooh<u>a</u> faa<u>s</u>ba<u>h</u>oo n<u>a</u>dimeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>157.  But they ham-strung her:<br> Then did they become<br> Full of regrets.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_158.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>158.  Faakha<u>th</u>ahumu alAAa<u>tha</u>bu inna fee <u>tha</u>lika la<u>a</u>yatan wam<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>na aktharuhum mu/mineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>158.  But the Penalty seized them.<br> Verily in this is a Sign:<br> But most of them<br> Do not believe.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_159.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>159.  Wa-inna rabbaka lahuwa alAAazeezu a<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>eem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>159.  And verily thy Lord<br> Is He, the Exalted in Might,<br> Most Merciful.</p> <div class="filenav">
