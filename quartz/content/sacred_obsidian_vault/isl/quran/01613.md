 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XVI.: Naḥl or The Bee.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 13</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_090.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>90.  Inna All<u>a</u>ha ya/muru bi<b>a</b>lAAadli wa<b>a</b>l-i<u>h</u>s<u>a</u>ni wa-eet<u>a</u>-i <u>th</u>ee alqurb<u>a</u> wayanh<u>a</u> AAani alfa<u>h</u>sh<u>a</u>-i wa<b>a</b>lmunkari wa<b>a</b>lbaghyi yaAAi<i><u>th</u></i>ukum laAAallakum ta<u>th</u>akkaroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>90.  God commands justice, the doing<br> Of good, and liberality to kith<br>And kin, and He forbids<br> All shameful deeds, and injustice<br> And rebellion: He instructs you,<br> That ye may receive admonition.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_091.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>91.  Waawfoo biAAahdi All<u>a</u>hi i<u>tha</u> AA<u>a</u>hadtum wal<u>a</u> tanqu<u>d</u>oo al-aym<u>a</u>na baAAda tawkeedih<u>a</u> waqad jaAAaltumu All<u>a</u>ha AAalaykum kafeelan inna All<u>a</u>ha yaAAlamu m<u>a</u> tafAAaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>91.  Fulfil the Covenant of God<br> When ye have entered into it,<br> And break not your oaths<br> After ye have confirmed them;<br> Indeed ye have made<br> God your surety; for God<br> Knoweth all that ye do.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_092.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>92.  Wal<u>a</u> takoonoo ka<b>a</b>llatee naqa<u>d</u>at ghazlah<u>a</u> min baAAdi quwwatin ank<u>a</u>than tattakhi<u>th</u>oona aym<u>a</u>nakum dakhalan baynakum an takoona ommatun hiya arb<u>a</u> min ommatin innam<u>a</u> yablookumu All<u>a</u>hu bihi walayubayyinanna lakum yawma alqiy<u>a</u>mati m<u>a</u> kuntum feehi takhtalifoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>92.  And be not like a woman<br> Who breaks into untwisted strands<br> The yarn which she has spun,<br> After it has become strong<br> Nor take your oaths to practise<br> Deception between yourselves,<br> Lest one party should be<br> More numerous than another:<br> For God will test you by this;<br> And on the Day of Judgment<br>He will certainly make clear<br> To you (the truth of) that<br> Wherein ye disagree.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_093.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>93.  Walaw sh<u>a</u>a All<u>a</u>hu lajaAAalakum ommatan w<u>ah</u>idatan wal<u>a</u>kin yu<u>d</u>illu man yash<u>a</u>o wayahdee man yash<u>a</u>o walatus-alunna AAamm<u>a</u> kuntum taAAmaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>93.  If God so willed, He<br> Could make you all one People:<br> But He leaves straying<br> Whom He pleases, and He guides<br> Whom He pleases: but ye<br> Shall certainly be called to account<br> For all your actions.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_094.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>94.  Wal<u>a</u> tattakhi<u>th</u>oo aym<u>a</u>nakum dakhalan baynakum fatazilla qadamun baAAda thubootih<u>a</u> wata<u>th</u>ooqoo a<b>l</b>ssoo-a bim<u>a</u> <u>s</u>adadtum AAan sabeeli All<u>a</u>hi walakum AAa<u>tha</u>bun AAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>94.  And take not your oaths,<br> To practise deception between<br> yourselves,<br> With the result that someone's foot<br> May slip after it was<br> Firmly planted, and ye may<br> Have to taste the evil<br> (consequences)<br> Of having hindered (men)<br> From the Path of God,<br> And a mighty Wrath<br> Descend on you.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_095.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>95.  Wal<u>a</u> tashtaroo biAAahdi All<u>a</u>hi thamanan qaleelan innam<u>a</u> AAinda All<u>a</u>hi huwa khayrun lakum in kuntum taAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>95.  Nor sell the Covenant of God<br> For a miserable price:<br> For with God is (a prize)<br> Far better for you,<br> If ye only knew.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_096.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>96.  M<u>a</u> AAindakum yanfadu wam<u>a</u> AAinda All<u>a</u>hi b<u>a</u>qin walanajziyanna alla<u>th</u>eena <u>s</u>abaroo ajrahum bi-a<u>h</u>sani m<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo yaAAmaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>96.  What is with you must vanish:<br> What is with God will endure.<br> And We will certainly bestow,<br> On those who patiently persevere,<br> Their reward according to<br> The best of their actions.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_097.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>97.  Man AAamila <u>sa</u>li<u>h</u>an min <u>th</u>akarin aw onth<u>a</u> wahuwa mu/minun falanu<u>h</u>yiyannahu <u>h</u>ay<u>a</u>tan <u>t</u>ayyibatan walanajziyannahum ajrahum bi-a<u>h</u>sani m<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo yaAAmaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>97.  Whoever works righteousness,<br> Man or woman, and has Faith,<br> Verily, to him will We give<br> A new Life, a life<br> That is good and pure, and We<br> Will bestow on such their reward<br> According to the best<br> Of their actions.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_098.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>98.  Fa-i<u>tha</u> qara/ta alqur-<u>a</u>na fa<b>i</b>staAAi<u>th</u> bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi mina a<b>l</b>shshay<u>ta</u>ni a<b>l</b>rrajeem<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>98.  When thou dost read<br> The Qur-ān, seek God's protection<br> From Satan the Rejected One.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_099.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>99.  Innahu laysa lahu sul<u>ta</u>nun AAal<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo waAAal<u>a</u> rabbihim yatawakkaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>99.  No authority has he over those<br> Who believe and put their trust<br> In their Lord.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_100.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>100.  Innam<u>a</u> sul<u>ta</u>nuhu AAal<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena yatawallawnahu wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena hum bihi mushrikoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>100.  His authority is over those<br> Only, who take him as patron<br> And who join partners with God.</p> <div class="filenav">
