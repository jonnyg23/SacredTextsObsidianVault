 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/024/024_035.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>35.  All<u>a</u>hu nooru a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i mathalu noorihi kamishk<u>a</u>tin feeh<u>a</u> mi<u>s</u>b<u>ah</u>un almi<u>s</u>b<u>ah</u>u fee zuj<u>a</u>jatin a<b>l</b>zzuj<u>a</u>jatu kaannah<u>a</u> kawkabun durriyyun yooqadu min shajaratin mub<u>a</u>rakatin zaytoonatin l<u>a</u> sharqiyyatin wal<u>a</u> gharbiyyatin yak<u>a</u>du zaytuh<u>a</u> yu<u>d</u>ee-o walaw lam tamsas-hu n<u>a</u>run noorun AAal<u>a</u> noorin yahdee All<u>a</u>hu linoorihi man yash<u>a</u>o waya<u>d</u>ribu All<u>a</u>hu al-amth<u>a</u>la li<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu bikulli shay-in AAaleem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>35.  God is the Light<br> Of the heavens and the earth.<br> The parable of His Light<br> Is as if there were a Niche<br> And within it a Lamp:<br> The Lamp enclosed in Glass:<br>The glass as it were<br> A brilliant star:<br> Lit from a blessed Tree,<br> An Olive, neither of the East<br> Nor of the West,<br> Whose Oil is well-nigh<br> Luminous,<br> Though fire scarce touched it<br> Light upon Light!<br> God doth guide<br> Whom He will<br> To His Light:<br> God doth set forth Parables<br> For men: and God<br> Doth know all things.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/024/024_036.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>36.  Fee buyootin a<u>th</u>ina All<u>a</u>hu an turfaAAa wayu<u>th</u>kara feeh<u>a</u> ismuhu yusabbi<u>h</u>u lahu feeh<u>a</u> bi<b>a</b>lghuduwwi wa<b>a</b>l-<u>asa</u>l<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>36.  (Lit is such a Light)<br> In houses, which God<br> Hath permitted to be raised<br> To honour; for the celebration,<br>In them, of His name:<br> In them is He glorified<br> In the mornings and<br> In the evenings, (again and again),</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/024/024_037.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>37.  Rij<u>a</u>lun l<u>a</u> tulheehim tij<u>a</u>ratun wal<u>a</u> bayAAun AAan <u>th</u>ikri All<u>a</u>hi wa-iq<u>a</u>mi a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>al<u>a</u>ti wa-eet<u>a</u>-i a<b>l</b>zzak<u>a</u>ti yakh<u>a</u>foona yawman tataqallabu feehi alquloobu wa<b>a</b>l-ab<u>sa</u>r<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>37.  By men whom neither<br> Traffic nor merchandise<br> Can divert from the Remembrance<br> Of God, nor from regular Prayer,<br> Nor from the practice<br> Of regular Charity:<br> Their (only) fear is<br> For the Day when<br> Hearts and eyes<br> Will be transformed<br> (In a world wholly new),—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/024/024_038.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>38.  Liyajziyahumu All<u>a</u>hu a<u>h</u>sana m<u>a</u> AAamiloo wayazeedahum min fa<u>d</u>lihi wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu yarzuqu man yash<u>a</u>o bighayri <u>h</u>is<u>a</u>b<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>38.  That God may reward them<br> According to the best<br> Of their deeds, and add<br> Even more for them<br> Out of His Grace:<br> For God doth provide<br> For those whom He will,<br> Without measure.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/024/024_039.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>39.  Wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo aAAm<u>a</u>luhum kasar<u>a</u>bin biqeeAAatin ya<u>h</u>sabuhu a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i>am-<u>a</u>nu m<u>a</u>an <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> i<u>tha</u> j<u>a</u>ahu lam yajidhu shay-an wawajada All<u>a</u>ha AAindahu fawaff<u>a</u>hu <u>h</u>is<u>a</u>bahu wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu sareeAAu al<u>h</u>is<u>a</u>b<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>39.  but the Unbelievers,—<br> Their deeds are like a mirage<br>In sandy deserts, which<br> The man parched with thirst<br> Mistakes for water; until<br> When he comes up to it,<br> He finds it to be nothing:<br> But he finds God<br> (Ever) with him, and God<br> Will pay him his account:<br> And God is swift<br> In taking account.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/024/024_040.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>40.  Aw ka<i><u>th</u></i>ulum<u>a</u>tin fee ba<u>h</u>rin lujjiyyin yaghsh<u>a</u>hu mawjun min fawqihi mawjun min fawqihi sa<u>ha</u>bun <i><u>th</u></i>ulum<u>a</u>tun baAA<u>d</u>uh<u>a</u> fawqa baAA<u>d</u>in i<u>tha</u> akhraja yadahu lam yakad yar<u>a</u>h<u>a</u> waman lam yajAAali All<u>a</u>hu lahu nooran fam<u>a</u> lahu min noor<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>40.  Or (the Unbelievers’ state)<br> Is like the depths of darkness<br> In a vast deep ocean,<br> Overwhelmed with billow<br> Topped by billow,<br> Topped by (dark) clouds:<br> Depths of darkness, one<br> Above another: if a man<br> Stretches out his hand,<br> He can hardly see it!<br> For any to whom God<br> Giveth not light,<br> There is no light!</p> <div class="filenav">
