 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXXIV.: Sabā, or the City of Sabā</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 5</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/034/034_037.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>37.  Wam<u>a</u> amw<u>a</u>lukum wal<u>a</u> awl<u>a</u>dukum bi<b>a</b>llatee tuqarribukum AAindan<u>a</u> zulf<u>a</u> ill<u>a</u> man <u>a</u>mana waAAamila <u>sa</u>li<u>h</u>an faol<u>a</u>-ika lahum jaz<u>a</u>o a<b>l</b><u>dd</u>iAAfi bim<u>a</u> AAamiloo wahum fee alghuruf<u>a</u>ti <u>a</u>minoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>37.  It is not your wealth<br> Nor your sons, that will<br> Bring you nearer to Us<br> In degree: but only<br> Those who believe and work<br> Righteousness—these are<br> The ones for whom there is<br> A multiplied Reward<br>For their deeds, while<br> Secure they (reside)<br> In the dwellings on high!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/034/034_038.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>38.  Wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena yasAAawna fee <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin<u>a</u> muAA<u>a</u>jizeena ol<u>a</u>-ika fee alAAa<u>tha</u>bi mu<u>hd</u>aroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>38.  Those who strive against<br> Our Signs, to frustrate them,<br> Will be given over<br> Into Punishment.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/034/034_039.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>39.  Qul inna rabbee yabsu<u>t</u>u a<b>l</b>rrizqa liman yash<u>a</u>o min AAib<u>a</u>dihi wayaqdiru lahu wam<u>a</u> anfaqtum min shay-in fahuwa yukhlifuhu wahuwa khayru a<b>l</b>rr<u>a</u>ziqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>39.  Say: "Verily my Lord enlarges<br> And restricts the Sustenance<br> To such of His servants<br> As He pleases: and nothing<br> Do ye spend in the least<br> (In His Cause) but He<br> Replaces it: for He is<br> The Best of those who<br> Grant Sustenance.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/034/034_040.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>40.  Wayawma ya<u>h</u>shuruhum jameeAAan thumma yaqoolu lilmal<u>a</u>-ikati ah<u>a</u>ol<u>a</u>-i iyy<u>a</u>kum k<u>a</u>noo yaAAbudoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>40.  One Day He will<br> Gather them all together,<br> And say to the angels,<br> "Was it you that these<br> Men used to worship?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/034/034_041.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>41.  Q<u>a</u>loo sub<u>ha</u>naka anta waliyyun<u>a</u> min doonihim bal k<u>a</u>noo yaAAbudoona aljinna aktharuhum bihim mu/minoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>41.  They will say, "Glory to Thee!<br> Our (tie) is with Thee<br> As Protector—not with them.<br> Nay, but they worshipped<br>The Jinns: most of them<br> Believed in them."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/034/034_042.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>42.  Fa<b>a</b>lyawma l<u>a</u> yamliku baAA<u>d</u>ukum libaAA<u>d</u>in nafAAan wal<u>a</u> <u>d</u>arran wanaqoolu lilla<u>th</u>eena <i><u>th</u></i>alamoo <u>th</u>ooqoo AAa<u>tha</u>ba a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>ri allatee kuntum bih<u>a</u> tuka<u>thth</u>iboon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>42.  So on that Day<br> No power shall they have<br> Over each other, for profit<br> Or harm: and We shall<br> Say to the wrong-doers,<br> "Taste ye the Penalty's<br> Of the Fire,—the which<br> Ye were wont to deny!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/034/034_043.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>43.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> tutl<u>a</u> AAalayhim <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tun<u>a</u> bayyin<u>a</u>tin q<u>a</u>loo m<u>a</u> h<u>atha</u> ill<u>a</u> rajulun yureedu an ya<u>s</u>uddakum AAamm<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>na yaAAbudu <u>a</u>b<u>a</u>okum waq<u>a</u>loo m<u>a</u> h<u>atha</u> ill<u>a</u> ifkun muftaran waq<u>a</u>la alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo lil<u>h</u>aqqi lamm<u>a</u> j<u>a</u>ahum in h<u>atha</u> ill<u>a</u> si<u>h</u>run mubeen<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>43.  When Our Clear Signs<br> Are rehearsed to them,<br> They say, "This is only<br> A man who wishes<br> To hinder you from the (worship)<br> Which your fathers practised."<br> And they say, "This is<br> Only a falsehood invented!"<br> And the Unbelievers say<br> Of the Truth when it comes<br> To them, "This is nothing<br> But evident magic!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/034/034_044.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>44.  Wam<u>a</u> <u>a</u>tayn<u>a</u>hum min kutubin yadrusoonah<u>a</u> wam<u>a</u> arsaln<u>a</u> ilayhim qablaka min na<u>th</u>eer<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>44.  But We had not given<br> Them Books which they could<br> Study, nor sent apostles<br> To them before thee<br> As Warners.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/034/034_045.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>45.  Waka<u>thth</u>aba alla<u>th</u>eena min qablihim wam<u>a</u> balaghoo miAAsh<u>a</u>ra m<u>a</u> <u>a</u>tayn<u>a</u>hum faka<u>thth</u>aboo rusulee fakayfa k<u>a</u>na nakeer<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>45.  And their predecessors rejected<br> (The Truth); these have<br> Not received a tenth<br> Of what We had granted<br> To those: yet when they rejected<br> My apostles, how (terrible)<br> Was My rejection (of them)!</p> <div class="filenav">
