 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra LII.: Ṭūr, or the Mount.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 2</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/052/052_029.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>29.  Fa<u>th</u>akkir fam<u>a</u> anta biniAAmati rabbika bik<u>a</u>hin wal<u>a</u> majnoon<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>29.  Wherefore proclaim thou<br> The praises (of thy Lord):<br> For by the Grace<br> Of thy Lord, thou art<br> No (vulgar) soothsayer, nor<br> Art thou one possessed.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/052/052_030.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>30.  Am yaqooloona sh<u>a</u>AAirun natarabba<u>s</u>u bihi rayba almanoon<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>30.  Or do they say:—<br> "A Poet! we await<br> For him some calamity<br> (Hatched) by Time!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/052/052_031.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>31.  Qul tarabba<u>s</u>oo fa-innee maAAakum mina almutarabbi<u>s</u>een<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>31.  Say thou: "Await ye!—<br> I too will wait<br> Along with you!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/052/052_032.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>32.  Am ta/muruhum a<u>h</u>l<u>a</u>muhum bih<u>atha</u> am hum qawmun <u>ta</u>ghoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>32.  Is it that their faculties<br> Of understanding urge them<br> To this, or are they<br> But a people transgressing<br> Beyond bounds?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/052/052_033.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>33.  Am yaqooloona taqawwalahu bal l<u>a</u> yu/minoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>33.  Or do they say,<br> "He fabricated the (Message)"?<br> Nay, they have no faith!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/052/052_034.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>34.  Falya/too bi<u>h</u>adeethin mithlihi in k<u>a</u>noo <u>sa</u>diqeena   </p>
 <p>34.  Let them then produce<br> A recital like unto it,—<br> If (it be) they speak<br> The Truth!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/052/052_035.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>35.  Am khuliqoo min ghayri shay-in am humu alkh<u>a</u>liqoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>35.  Were they created of<br> nothing,<br> Or were they themselves<br> The creators?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/052/052_036.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>36.  Am khalaqoo a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>a bal l<u>a</u> yooqinoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>36.  Or did they create<br> The heavens and the earth?<br> Nay, they have<br> No firm belief.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/052/052_037.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>37.  Am AAindahum khaz<u>a</u>-inu rabbika am humu almu<u>s</u>ay<u>t</u>iroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>37.  Or are the Treasures<br> Of thy Lord with them,<br> Or are they the managers<br> (Of affairs)?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/052/052_038.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>38.  Am lahum sullamun yastamiAAoona feehi falya/ti mustamiAAuhum bisul<u>ta</u>nin mubeen<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>38.  Or have they a ladder,<br> By which they can (climb<br> Up to heaven and) listen<br> (To its secrets)? Then let<br> (Such a) listener of theirs<br> Produce a manifest proof.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/052/052_039.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>39.  Am lahu alban<u>a</u>tu walakumu albanoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>39.  Or has He only daughters<br> And ye have sons?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/052/052_040.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>40.  Am tas-aluhum ajran fahum min maghramin muthqaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>40.  Or is it that thou<br> Dost ask for a reward,<br> So that they are burdened<br> With a load of debt?—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/052/052_041.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>41.  Am AAindahumu alghaybu fahum yaktuboon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>41.  Or that the Unseen<br> Is in their hands,<br> And they write it down?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/052/052_042.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>42.  Am yureedoona kaydan fa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo humu almakeedoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>42.  Or do they intend<br> A plot (against thee)?<br> But those who defy God<br> Are themselves involved<br> In a Plot!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/052/052_043.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>43.  Am lahum il<u>a</u>hun ghayru All<u>a</u>hi sub<u>ha</u>na All<u>a</u>hi AAamm<u>a</u> yushrikoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>43.  Or have they a god<br> Other than God?<br> Exalted is God<br> Far above the things<br> They associate with Him!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/052/052_044.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>44.  Wa-in yaraw kisfan mina a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>-i s<u>a</u>qi<u>t</u>an yaqooloo sa<u>ha</u>bun markoom<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>44.  Were they to see<br> A piece of the sky<br> Falling (on them), they<br> Would (only) say: "Clouds<br> Gathered in heaps!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/052/052_045.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>45.  Fa<u>th</u>arhum <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> yul<u>a</u>qoo yawmahumu alla<u>th</u>ee feehi yu<u>s</u>AAaqoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>45.  So leave them alone<br> Until they encounter<br> That Day of theirs,<br> Wherein they shall (perforce)<br> Swoon (with terror),—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/052/052_046.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>46.  Yawma l<u>a</u> yughnee AAanhum kayduhum shay-an wal<u>a</u> hum yun<u>s</u>aroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>46.  The Day when their plotting<br> Will avail them nothing<br> And no help shall be<br> Given them.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/052/052_047.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>47.  Wa-inna lilla<u>th</u>eena <i><u>th</u></i>alamoo AAa<u>tha</u>ban doona <u>tha</u>lika wal<u>a</u>kinna aktharahum l<u>a</u> yaAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>47.  And verily, for those<br> Who do wrong, there is<br> Another punishment besides this:<br> But most of them<br> Understand not.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/052/052_048.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>48.  Wa<b>i</b><u>s</u>bir li<u>h</u>ukmi rabbika fa-innaka bi-aAAyunin<u>a</u> wasabbi<u>h</u> bi<u>h</u>amdi rabbika <u>h</u>eena taqoom<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>48.  Now await in patience<br> The command of thy Lord:<br>For verily thou art<br> In Our eyes:<br> And celebrate the praises<br> Of thy Lord the while<br> Thou standest forth,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/052/052_049.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>49.  Wamina allayli fasabbi<u>h</u>hu wa-idb<u>a</u>ra a<b>l</b>nnujoom<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>49.  And for part of the night<br> Also praise thou Him,—<br> And at the retreat<br> Of the stars!</p>  <div class="filenav">
