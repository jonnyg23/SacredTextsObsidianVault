 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra VII.: A’rāf, or the Heights</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 11</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_085.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>85.  Wa-il<u>a</u> madyana akh<u>a</u>hum shuAAayban q<u>a</u>la y<u>a</u> qawmi oAAbudoo All<u>a</u>ha m<u>a</u> lakum min il<u>a</u>hin ghayruhu qad j<u>a</u>atkum bayyinatun min rabbikum faawfoo alkayla wa<b>a</b>lmeez<u>a</u>na wal<u>a</u> tabkhasoo a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>sa ashy<u>a</u>ahum wal<u>a</u> tufsidoo fee al-ar<u>d</u>i baAAda i<u>s</u>l<u>ah</u>ih<u>a</u> <u>tha</u>likum khayrun lakum in kuntum mu/mineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>85.  To the Madyan people<br>We sent Shu‘aib, one<br> Of their own brethren: he said:<br> "O my people! worship God;<br> Ye have no other god<br> But Him. Now hath come<br> Unto you a clear (Sign)<br> From your Lord! Give just<br> Measure and weight, nor withhold<br> From the people the things<br> That are their due; and do<br> No mischief on the earth<br> After it has been set<br> In order: that will be best<br> For you, if ye have Faith.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_086.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>86.  Wal<u>a</u> taqAAudoo bikulli <u>s</u>ir<u>at</u>in tooAAidoona wata<u>s</u>uddoona AAan sabeeli All<u>a</u>hi man <u>a</u>mana bihi watabghoonah<u>a</u> AAiwajan wa<b>o</b><u>th</u>kuroo i<u>th</u> kuntum qaleelan fakaththarakum wa<b>o</b>n<i><u>th</u></i>uroo kayfa k<u>a</u>na AA<u>a</u>qibatu almufsideen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>86.  "And squat not on every road,<br> Breathing threats, hindering<br> From the path of God<br> Those who believe in Him,<br> And seeking in it<br> Something crooked;<br> But remember how ye were<br> Little, and He gave you increase.<br> And hold in your mind's eye<br> What was the end<br>Of those who did mischief.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_087.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>87.  Wa-in k<u>a</u>na <u>ta</u>-ifatun minkum <u>a</u>manoo bi<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>ee orsiltu bihi wa<u>ta</u>-ifatun lam yu/minoo fa<b>i</b><u>s</u>biroo <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> ya<u>h</u>kuma All<u>a</u>hu baynan<u>a</u> wahuwa khayru al<u>ha</u>kimeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>87.  "And if there is a party<br> Among you who believes<br> In the Message with which<br> I have been sent, and a party<br> Which does not believe,<br> Hold yourselves in patience<br> Until God doth decide<br> Between us: for He<br> Is the best to decide."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_088.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>88.  Q<u>a</u>la almalao alla<u>th</u>eena istakbaroo min qawmihi lanukhrijannaka y<u>a</u> shuAAaybu wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo maAAaka min qaryatin<u>a</u> aw lataAAoodunna fee millatin<u>a</u> q<u>a</u>la awa law kunn<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>riheen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>88.  The leaders, the arrogant<br> Party among his people, said:<br> "O Shu’aib! we shall<br> Certainly drive thee out<br> Of our city—(thee) and those<br> Who believe with thee;<br> Or else ye (thou and they)<br> Shall have to return<br> To our ways and religion."<br> He said: "What! even<br> Though we do detest (them)?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_089.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>89.  Qadi iftarayn<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi ka<u>th</u>iban in AAudn<u>a</u> fee millatikum baAAda i<u>th</u> najj<u>a</u>n<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hu minh<u>a</u> wam<u>a</u> yakoonu lan<u>a</u> an naAAooda feeh<u>a</u> ill<u>a</u> an yash<u>a</u>a All<u>a</u>hu rabbun<u>a</u> wasiAAa rabbun<u>a</u> kulla shay-in AAilman AAal<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi tawakkaln<u>a</u> rabban<u>a</u> ifta<u>h</u> baynan<u>a</u> wabayna qawmin<u>a</u> bi<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>aqqi waanta khayru alf<u>a</u>ti<u>h</u>een<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>89.  "We should indeed invent<br> A lie against God,<br> If we returned to your ways<br> After God hath rescued<br> Us therefrom; nor could we<br> By any manner of means<br> Return thereto unless it be<br> As in the will and plan of God,<br> Our Lord. Our Lord<br> Can reach out to the utmost<br> Recesses of things by His<br> knowledge.<br> In God is our trust.<br> Our Lord! Decide thou<br> Between us and our people<br>In truth, for thou<br> Art the best to decide."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_090.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>90.  Waq<u>a</u>la almalao alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo min qawmihi la-ini ittabaAAtum shuAAayban innakum i<u>th</u>an lakh<u>a</u>siroon<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>90.  The leaders, the Unbelievers<br> Among his people, said:<br> "If ye follow Shu’aib,<br> Be sure then ye are ruined!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_091.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>91.  Faakha<u>th</u>at-humu a<b>l</b>rrajfatu faa<u>s</u>ba<u>h</u>oo fee d<u>a</u>rihim j<u>a</u>thimeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>91.  But the earthquake took them<br> Unawares, and they lay<br> Prostrate in their homes<br> Before the morning!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_092.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>92.  Alla<u>th</u>eena ka<u>thth</u>aboo shuAAayban kaan lam yaghnaw feeh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena ka<u>thth</u>aboo shuAAayban k<u>a</u>noo humu alkh<u>a</u>sireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>92.  The men who rejected<br> Shu’aib became as if<br> They had never been<br> In the homes where they<br> Had flourished: the men<br> Who rejected Shu’aib—<br> It was they who were ruined!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_093.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>93.  Fatawall<u>a</u> AAanhum waq<u>a</u>la y<u>a</u> qawmi laqad ablaghtukum ris<u>a</u>l<u>a</u>ti rabbee wana<u>s</u>a<u>h</u>tu lakum fakayfa <u>a</u>s<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> qawmin k<u>a</u>fireen<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>93.  So Shu’aib left them,<br> Saying: "O my people!<br> I did indeed convey to you<br> The Messages for which<br> I was sent by my Lord:<br> I gave you good counsel,<br> But how shall I lament<br>Over a people who refuse<br> To believe!"</p> <div class="filenav">
