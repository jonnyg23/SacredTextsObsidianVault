 <p><a name="page_1810"><font size="1" color="green">p. 1810</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">Sūra CXIV.</h3>  <h3 align="center"><i>Nās,</i> or Mankind.</h3>  
 <p><i>In the name of God, Most Gracious,<br> Most Merciful.</i></p>  
 <p>1. Say: I seek refuge<br> With the Lord<br> And Cherisher of Mankind,</p>  
 <p>2. The King (or Ruler)<br> Of Mankind,</p>  
 <p>3. The God (or Judge)<br> Of Mankind,—</p>  
 <p>4. From the mischief<br> Of the Whisperer<br> (Of Evil), who withdraws<br> (After his whisper),—</p>  
 <p>5. (The same) who whispers<br> Into the hearts of Mankind,—</p>  
 <p>6. Among Jinns<br> And among Men.</p>
