 <p><a name="page_1737"><font size="1" color="green">p. 1737</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">Sūra XC.</h3>  <h3 align="center"><i>Balad, </i>or The City.</h3>  
 <p><i>In the name of God, Most Gracious,<br> Most Merciful.</i></p>  
 <p>1. I do call to witness<br> This City;—</p>  
 <p>2. And thou art a freeman<br> Of this City;—</p>  
 <p>3. And (the mystic ties<br> of) Parent and Child;—</p>  
 <p>4. Verily We have created<br> Man into toil and struggle.</p>  
 <p>5. Thinketh he, that none<br> Hath power over him?</p>  
 <p><a name="page_1738"><font size="1" color="green">p. 1738</font></a></p>  
 <p>6. He may say (boastfully):<br> "Wealth have I squandered<br> In abundance!"</p>  
 <p>7. Thinketh he that none<br> Beholdeth him?</p>  
 <p>8. Have We not made<br> For him a pair of eyes?—</p>  
 <p>9. And a tongue,<br> And a pair of lips?—</p>  
 <p>10. And shown him<br> The two highways?</p>  
 <p>11. But he hath made no haste<br> On the path that is steep.</p>  
 <p>12. And what will explain<br> To thee the path that is steep?—</p>  
 <p><a name="page_1739"><font size="1" color="green">p. 1739</font></a></p>  
 <p>13. (It is:) freeing the bondman;</p>  
 <p>14. Or the giving of food<br> In a day of privation</p>  
 <p>15. To the orphan<br> With claims of relationship,</p>  
 <p>16. Or to the indigent<br> (Down) in the dust.</p>  
 <p>17. Then will he be<br> Of those who believe,<br> And enjoin patience, (constancy,<br> And self-restraint), and enjoin<br> Deeds of kindness and compassion.</p>  
 <p>18. Such are the Companions<br> Of the Right Hand.</p>  
 <p><a name="page_1740"><font size="1" color="green">p. 1740</font></a></p>  
 <p>19. But those who reject<br> Our Signs, they are<br> The (unhappy) Companions<br> Of the Left Hand.</p>  
 <p>20. On them will be Fire<br> Vaulted over (all round).</p>
