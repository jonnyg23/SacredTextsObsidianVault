 <p><a name="page_1699"><font size="1" color="green">p. 1699</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">Sūra LXXXII.</h3>  <h3 align="center"><i>Infiṭār,</i> or The Cleaving Asunder.</h3>  
 <p><i>In the name of God, Most Gracious,<br> Most Merciful.</i></p>  
 <p>1. When the Sky<br> Is cleft asunder;</p>  
 <p>2. When the Stars<br> Are scattered;</p>  
 <p>3. When the Oceans<br> Are suffered to burst forth;</p>  
 <p>4. And when the Graves<br> Are turned upside down;—</p>  
 <p><a name="page_1700"><font size="1" color="green">p. 1700</font></a></p>  
 <p>5. (Then) shall each soul know<br> What it hath sent forward<br> And (what it hath) kept back.</p>  
 <p>6. O man! what has<br> Seduced thee from<br> Thy Lord Most Beneficent?—</p>  
 <p>7. Him Who created thee.<br> Fashioned thee in due proportion<br> And gave thee a just bias;</p>  
 <p>8. In whatever Form He wills,<br> Does He put thee together.</p>  
 <p>9. Nay! but ye do<br> Reject Right and Judgment!</p>  
 <p>10. But verily over you<br> (Are appointed angels)<br> To protect you,—</p>  
 <p>11. Kind and honourable,—<br> Writing down (your deeds):</p>  
 <p><a name="page_1701"><font size="1" color="green">p. 1701</font></a></p>  
 <p>12. They know (and understand)<br> All that ye do.</p>  
 <p>13. As for the Righteous,<br> They will be in Bliss;</p>  
 <p>14. And the Wicked—<br> They will be in the Fire,</p>  
 <p>15. Which they will enter<br> On the Day of Judgment,</p>  
 <p>16. And they will not be<br> Able to keep away therefrom.</p>  
 <p>17. And what will explain<br> To thee what the Day<br> Of Judgment is?</p>  
 <p>18. Again, what will explain<br> To thee what the Day<br> Of Judgment is?</p>  
 <p>19. (It will be) the Day<br> When no soul shall have<br> Power (to do) aught<br> For another <br> For the Command, that Day,<br> Will be (wholly) with God.</p>
