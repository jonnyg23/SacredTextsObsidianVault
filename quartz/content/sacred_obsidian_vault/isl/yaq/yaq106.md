 <p><a name="page_1794"><font size="1" color="green">p. 1794</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">Sūra CVI.</h3>  <h3 align="center"><i>Quraish</i> or The Quraish, (Custodians of the Ka’ba).</h3>  
 <p><i>In the name of God, Most Gracious,<br> Most Merciful.</i></p>  
 <p>1. For the covenants<br> (Of security and safeguard<br> Enjoyed) by the Quraish,</p>  
 <p>2. Their covenants (covering) journeys<br> By winter and summer,—</p>  
 <p>3. Let them adore the Lord<br> Of this House</p>  
 <p>4. Who provides them<br> With food against hunger,<br> And with security<br> Against fear (of danger).</p>
