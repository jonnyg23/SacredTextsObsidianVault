 <p><a name="page_1796"><font size="1" color="green">p. 1796</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">Sūra CVII.</h3>  <h3 align="center"><i>Mā’ūn</i>, or Neighbourly Needs.</h3>  
 <p><i>In the name of God, Most Gracious,<br> Most Merciful.</i></p>  
 <p>1. Seest thou one<br> Who denies the Judgment<br> (To come)?</p>  
 <p>2. Then such is the (man)<br> Who repulses the orphan<br> (With harshness),</p>  
 <p>3. And encourages not<br> The feeding of the indigent.</p>  
 <p>4. So woe to the worshippers</p>  
 <p>5. Who are neglectful<br> Of their Prayers,</p>  
 <p>6. Those who (want but)<br> To be seen (of men),</p>  
 <p>7. But refuse (to supply)<br> (Even) neighbourly needs.</p>
