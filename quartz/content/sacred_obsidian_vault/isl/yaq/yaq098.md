 <p><a name="page_1767"><font size="1" color="green">p. 1767</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">Sūra XCVIII.</h3>  <h3 align="center"><i>Baiyina,</i> or The Clear Evidence.</h3>  
 <p><i>In the name of God, Most Gracious,<br> Most Merciful.</i></p>  
 <p>1. Those who reject (Truth),<br> Among the People of the Book<br> And among the Polytheists,<br> Were not going to depart<br> (From their ways) until<br> There should come to them<br> Clear Evidence,—</p>  
 <p>2. An apostle from God,<br> Rehearsing scriptures<br> Kept pure and holy:</p>  
 <p>3. Wherein are laws (or decrees)<br> Right and straight.</p>  
 <p>4. Nor did the People<br> Of the Book</p>  
 <p><a name="page_1768"><font size="1" color="green">p. 1768</font></a></p>  
 <p>Make schisms,<br> Until after there came<br> To them Clear Evidence.</p>  
 <p>5. And they have been commanded<br> No more than this:<br> To worship God,<br> Offering Him sincere devotion,<br> Being True (in faith);<br> To establish regular Prayer;<br> And to practise regular Charity;<br> And that is the Religion<br> Right and Straight.</p>  
 <p>6. Those who reject (Truth),<br> Among the People of the Book<br> And among the Polytheists,<br> Will be in hell-fire,<br> To dwell therein (for aye).<br> They are the worst<br> Of creatures.</p>  
 <p>7. Those who have faith<br> And do righteous deeds,—<br> They are the best<br> Of creatures.</p>  
 <p><a name="page_1769"><font size="1" color="green">p. 1769</font></a></p>  
 <p>8. Their reward is with God:<br> Gardens of Eternity,<br> Beneath which rivers flow;<br> They will dwell therein<br> For ever; God well pleased<br> With them, and they with Him;<br> All this for such as<br> Fear their Lord and Cherisher.</p>
