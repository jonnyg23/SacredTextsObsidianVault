 <p><a name="page_1755"><font size="1" color="green">p. 1755</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">Sūra XCIV.</h3>  <h3 align="center"><i>Inshirāḥ,</i> or The Expansion.</h3>  
 <p><i>In the name of God, Most Gracious,<br> Most Merciful.</i></p>  
 <p>1. Have We not<br> Expanded thee thy breast?—</p>  
 <p>2. And removed from thee<br> Thy burden</p>  
 <p>3. The which did gall<br> Thy back?—</p>  
 <p>4. And raised high the esteem<br> (In which) thou (art held)?</p>  
 <p>5. So, verily,<br> With every difficulty,<br> There is relief:</p>  
 <p>6. Verily, with every difficulty<br> There is relief.</p>  
 <p><a name="page_1756"><font size="1" color="green">p. 1756</font></a></p>  
 <p>7. Therefore, when thou art<br> Free (from thine immediate task),<br> Still labour hard,</p>  
 <p>8. And to thy Lord<br> Turn (all) thy attention.</p>
