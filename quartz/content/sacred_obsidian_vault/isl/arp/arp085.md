 <p><a name="page_167"><font size="1" color="green">p. 167</font></a></p>  <h5 align="center">ON THE</h5>  <h4 align="center">EARLY DEATH OF ABU ALHASSAN ALI,</h4>  <h5 align="center">SON OF THE KHALIF ALNASSAR LEDIN ALLAH.</h5>  <h5 align="center">BY CAMAL EDDIN BEN ALNABIT.</h5>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">SOON hast thou run the race of life,<br>   Nor could our tears thy speed control:<br> Still in the coursers’ gen’rous strife<br>   The best will soonest reach the goal.  
 <p>As Death upon his hand turns o’er<br>   The different gems the world displays,<br> He seizes first, to swell his store,<br>   The brightest jewel he surveys.</p>  
 <p>Thy name, by every breath conveyed,<br>   Stretched o’er the globe its boundless flight;<br> Alas! in eve the length’ning shade<br>   But lengthens to be lost in night!</p>  
 <p>If gracious <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Allah</span> bade thee close<br>   Thy youthful eyes so soon on day,<br> ’Tis that he readiest welcomes those<br>   Who love him best, and best obey.</p></div>
