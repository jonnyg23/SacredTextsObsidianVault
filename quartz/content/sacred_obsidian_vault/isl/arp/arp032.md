 <p><a name="page_104"><font size="1" color="green">p. 104</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">ON TEMPER.</h3>  <h4 align="center">BY NABEGAT BENI JAID.</h4>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">YES, Leila, I swore, by the fire of thine eyes,<br>   I ne’er could a sweetness unvaried endure;<br> The bubbles of spirit that sparkling arise<br>   Forbid life to stagnate, and render it pure.  
 <p>But yet, my dear maid, though thy spirit's my pride,<br>   I'd wish for some sweetness to temper the bowl:<br> If life be ne’er suffered to rest or subside,<br>   It may not be flat, but I fear ’t will be foul.</p></div>
