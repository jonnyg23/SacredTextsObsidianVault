 <p><a name="page_115"><font size="1" color="green">p. 115</font></a></p>  <h4 align="center">TO A FEMALE CUPBEARER.</h4>  <h5 align="center">BY ABD ALSALAM BEN RAGBAN.</h5>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">COME, Leila, fill the goblet up—<br>   Reach round the rosy wine;<br> Think not that we will take the cup<br>   From any hand but thine.  
 <p>A draught like this ‘twere vain to seek,<br>   No grape can such supply<br> It steals its tint from Leila's cheek,<br>   Its brightness from her eye.</p></div>
