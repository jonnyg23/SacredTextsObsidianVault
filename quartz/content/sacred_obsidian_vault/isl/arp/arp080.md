 <p><a name="page_162"><font size="1" color="green">p. 162</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">TO YOUTH.</h3>  <h4 align="center">BY EBN ALRABIA, IN HIS OLD AGE.</h4>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">YES, Youth, thou’rt fled, and I am left,<br>   Like yonder desolated bower,<br> By Winter's ruthless hand bereft<br>   Of every leaf and every flower.  
 <p>With heaving heart and streaming eyes,<br>   I wooed thee to prolong thy stay,<br> But vain were all my tears and sighs—<br>   Thou only fled’st more fast away.</p>  
 <p>Yet though thou fled’st away so fast,<br>   I can recall thee if I will;<br> For I can talk of what is past,<br>   And while I talk, enjoy thee still.</p></div>
