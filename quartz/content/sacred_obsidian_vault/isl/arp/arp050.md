 <p><a name="page_122"><font size="1" color="green">p. 122</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">TO CASSIM OBID ALLAH,</h3>  <h4 align="center">ON THE DEATH OF ONE OF HIS SONS.</h4>  <h5 align="center">BY ALI BEN AHMED BEN MANSOUR.</h5>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">POOR Cassim! thou art doomed to mourn,<br>   By Destiny's decree;<br> Whatever happen, it must turn<br>   To misery for thee.  
 <p>Two sons hadst thou, the one thy pride,<br>   The other was thy pest;<br> Ah, why did cruel Death decide<br>   To snatch away the best?</p>  
 <p>No wonder thou shouldst droop with woe,<br>   Of such a child bereft:<br> But now thy tears must doubly flow,<br>   For ah,—the other's left!</p></div>
