 <p><a name="page_114"><font size="1" color="green">p. 114</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">VERSES</h3>  <h4 align="center">ADDRESSED TO HIS MISTRESS, WHO HAD FOUND FAULT WITH HIM FOR PROFUSION.</h4>  <h5 align="center">BY ABU TEMAN HABIB.</h5>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">UNGENEROUS and mistaken maid,<br>   To scorn me thus because I'm poor<br> Canst thou a liberal hand upbraid,<br>   For dealing round some worthless ore?  
 <p>To spare's the wish of little souls;<br>   The great but gather to bestow:<br> Yon current don the mountain rolls,<br>   And stagnates in the swamp belay.</p></div>
