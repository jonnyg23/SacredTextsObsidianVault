 <p><a name="page_152"><font size="1" color="green">p. 152</font></a></p>  <h4 align="center">TO A LITTLE MAN WITH A VERY LARGE BEARD.</h4>  <h5 align="center">BY ISAAC BEN KHALIF.</h5>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">HOW can thy chin that burden bear?<br>   Is it all gravity to shock?<br> Is it to make the people stare,<br>   And be thyself a laughing-stock?  
 <p>When I behold thy little feet<br>   After thy beard obsequious run,<br> I always fancy that I meet<br>   Some father followed by his son.</p>  
 <p>A man like thee scarce e’er appeared;<br>   A beard like thine, where shall we find it?<br> Surely thou cherishest thy beard,<br>   In hopes to hide thyself behind it!</p></div>
