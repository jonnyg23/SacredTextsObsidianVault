 <p><a name="page_165"><font size="1" color="green">p. 165</font></a></p>  <h4 align="center">VERSES</h4>  <h5 align="center">ADDRESSED BY THE KHALIF ALMOKTAFI LIAMRILLAH</h5>  <h5 align="center">TO A LADY, WHO PRETENDED A PASSION FOR</h5>  <h5 align="center">HIM IN HIS OLD AGE.</h5>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">THOUGH such unbounded love you swear,<br>   ’Tis only art I see:<br> Can I believe that one so fair<br>   Should ever doat on me?  
 <p>Say that you hate, and freely show<br>   That Age displeases Youth;<br> And I may love you, when I know<br>   That you can tell the truth.</p></div>
