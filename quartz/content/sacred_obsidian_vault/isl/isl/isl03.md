 <p><a name="page_13"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 13</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">THE OPENING</h3>  
 <p> </p>  
 <p></p><dir><i>In the Name of God, the Merciful, the Compassionate</i>.  
 <p><i>Praise be to God, the Lord of the Worlds,<br> The Merciful One, the Compassionate One,<br> Master of the Day of Doom</i>.</p>  
 <p><i>Thee alone we serve, to Thee alone we cry for help.<br> Guide us in the straight path<br> The path of them Thou hast blessed.<br> Not of those with whom Thou art angry<br> Nor of those who go astray</i>.</p></dir>  
 <p>The Fattiḥa, the "Lord's Prayer" of Islam, is the opening <i>sūra</i> of the Qur’ān. It is recited for each new beginning.</p>
