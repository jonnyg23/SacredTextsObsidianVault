 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">The Qur'ân, part II (Sacred Books of the East volume 9), Palmer edition [1880]; at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">THE CHAPTER OF UNITY <a name="fr_508"></a><a href="#fn_508"><font size="1">2</font></a>.</h3>  <h3 align="center">(CXII. Place of origin doubtful.)</h3>  
 <p>IN the name of the merciful and compassionate God.<br> Say, 'He is God alone!<br> God the Eternal!<br> He begets not and is not begotten!<br> Nor is there like unto Him any one!'</p>
