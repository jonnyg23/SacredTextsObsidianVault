 <p><a name="page_342"><font size="1" color="green">p. 342</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">THE CHAPTER OF THE QURÂI<i>S</i>.</h3>  <h3 align="center">(CVI. Mecca.)</h3>  
 <p>IN the name of the merciful and compassionate God.<br> For the uniting of the Qurâi<i>s</i>; uniting them for the caravan of winter and summer.<br> So let them serve the Lord of this house who feeds them against hunger and makes them safe against fear <a name="fr_502"></a><a href="#fn_502"><font size="1">1</font></a>.</p>
