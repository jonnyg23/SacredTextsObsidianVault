 <p>"<i>The Universe is the mirror of God, and the heart of man is the mirror of the Universe.</i>"--<a href="omy15.htm#page_41">p. 41</a></p>
 <p>
 This is a concise study of the key concepts of Sufi philosophy, written
 by a western scholar in the mid-19th century.
 Palmer discusses the spiritual journey that Sufis take, the path to
 oneness with God.
 A very useful feature of this book is the glossary of poetic symbols used in
 Persian Sufi literature.
 Palmer also translated the Qur'an for the Sacred Books of the East
 in two volumes, also at this site
 <a href="../sbe06/index.htm">here</a> and <a href="../sbe09/index.htm">here</a>.
 </p>
