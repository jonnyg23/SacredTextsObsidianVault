 <h1 align="CENTER">FORTUNE AND LUCK</h1><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <p>If Fortune favor you, be not elated;<br>
 If she frown, do not despond.<br>
      --AUSONIUS.</p>
 <p>When Fortune means to men most good,<br>
 She looks upon them with a threatening eye.<br>
      --King John, III. 4, 119.</p>
 <p>When smiling Fortune spreads her golden ray,<br>
 All crowd around to flatter and obey;<br>
 But when she thunders from the angry sky,<br>
 Our friends, our flatterers, our lovers fly.<br>
      --OVID.</p>
 <p>Since Fortune is not in our power,<br>
 Let us be as little as possible in hers.<br>
      --STEELE.</p></dir>
