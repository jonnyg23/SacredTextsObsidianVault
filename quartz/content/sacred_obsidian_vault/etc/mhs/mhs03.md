 <h1 align="CENTER">THE MAGIC OF THE HORSE-SHOE</h1>
 <p>And still o'er many a neighboring door</p>
 <p>She saw the horse-shoe's curvèd charm.</p>
 <p>-WHITTIER, The Witch's Daughter.</p>
 <p>Happy art thou, as if every day thou hadst picked up a horse-shoe.</p>
 <p>-LONGFELLOW, Evangeline.</p>
