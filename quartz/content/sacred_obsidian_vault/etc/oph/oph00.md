 <h1 align="CENTER">Ophiolatreia</h1>
 <h5 align="CENTER">An account of the rites and mysteries connected with the origin, rise and development of serpent worship in various parts of the world, enriched with interesting traditions, and a full description of the celebrated serpent mounds &amp; temples, the whole forming an exposition of one of the phases of phallic, or sex worship.</h5>
 <h4 align="CENTER">Anonymous</h4>
 <h4 align="CENTER">London, 1889</h4>
