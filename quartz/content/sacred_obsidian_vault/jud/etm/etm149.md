 <p><a name="page_322"><font size="1" color="green">p. 322</font></a></p>  <h1 align="center">‏סדר נזיקין‎</h1>  <h6 align="center">OR, DAMAGES; TREATS OF THE RIGHTS OF PERSONS AND THINGS.</h6>  
 <p align="center">_________</p>  <h1 align="center">XXX. TREATISE BABAH KAMAN,</h1>  <h6 align="center">THE FIRST GATE.</h6>  
 <p align="center">_________</p>  <h1 align="center">XXXI. TREATISE BABAH MEZIAH,</h1>  <h6 align="center">THE MIDDLE GATE.</h6>  
 <p align="center">_________</p>  <h1 align="center">XXXII. TREATISE BABAH BATHRA,</h1>  <h6 align="center">THE LAST GATE.</h6>  
 <p>[The above Treatises contain civil laws. They are called "Gates," because, in the East, justice is administered in the city gates.]</p>  
 <p align="center">_________</p>  <h1 align="center">XXXIII. TREATISE SANHEDRIN.</h1>  
 <p>[Contains ceremonial laws. It also treats of the institution of the municipal and provincial Sanhedrin, and of the great Bethdin, or Sanhedrin at Jerusalem; whence its name.]</p>  
 <p align="center">_________</p>  <h1 align="center">XXXIV. TREATISE MACOOTH.</h1>  
 <p>[Treats of corporeal punishments; also, of false witnesses, and of the involuntary homicide, and the cities of refuge.]</p>  
 <p align="center">_________</p>  <h1 align="center">XXXV. TREATISE SHEBUOTH.</h1>  
 <p align="center">[Contains precepts for the administration of oaths.]</p>  
 <p><a name="page_323"><font size="1" color="green">p. 323</font></a></p>  <h1 align="center">XXXVI. TREATISE EDIOTH,</h1>  <h6 align="center">TESTIMONIES.</h6>  
 <p>[Is so called because it consists of laws which tried and trustworthy teachers attested to have been adopted by the elder teachers, in Sanhedrin assembled.]</p>  
 <p align="center">_________</p>  <h1 align="center">XXXVII. TREATISE ABODAH ZARAH.</h1>  
 <p>[Contains laws relating to idolatry, heresy, and the enticers or seducers to either.]</p>  
 <p align="center">_________</p>  <h1 align="center">XXXVIII. TREATISE ABOTH,</h1>  <h6 align="center">ETHICS OF THE FATHERS.</h6>  
 <p>[Contains moral precepts, maxims, and apothegms, of the elder, or Mishnic teachings.]</p>  
 <p align="center">_________</p>  <h1 align="center">XXXIX. TREATISE HORIOTH,</h1>  <h6 align="center">PRECEPTS.</h6>  
 <p>[Treats of such errors in judgment committed by the great Sanhedrin as require a sin-offering. Vide Lev. iv. 13–21, and 22–26.]</p>  
 <p align="center">Which closes the Seder Nezekin.</p>

 <p align="center">[Having laws relating to sacrifices generally.]</p>  
 <p align="center">_________</p>  <h1 align="center">XLI. TREATISE MINCHOTH,</h1>  <h6 align="center">MEAT-OFFERINGS.</h6>  
 <p align="center">[Containing laws relating to offerings of flour.]</p>
