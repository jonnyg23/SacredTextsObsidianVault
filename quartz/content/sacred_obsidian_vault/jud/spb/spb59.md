 <p><a name="page_470"><font size="1" color="green">p. 470</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">THE MOURNER'S KADDISH</h3>  
 <p align="center"><i>Mourner</i></p>  
 <p>Yis-gad-dal v’yis-kad-dash sh’meh rab-bo, b’ol-mo di-v’ro kir’-u-seh v’yam-lich mal-chu-seh, b’cha-ye-chon u-v’yo-me-chon u-v’cha-yeh d’chol bes yis-ro-el, ba-a-go-lo u-viz-man ko-riv, v’im-ru O-men:</p>  
 <p align="center"><i>Cong. and Mourner</i></p>  
 <p>Y’heh sh’meh rab-bo m’vo-rach, l’o-lam ul’-ol-meh ol-ma-yo:</p>  
 <p align="center"><i>Mourner</i></p>  
 <p>Yis-bo-rach, v’yish-tab-bach, v’yis-po-ar, v’yis-ro-mam, v’yis-nas-seh, v’yis-had-dor, v’yis-al-leh, v’yis-hal-lol, sh’meh d’kud’-sho, b’rich hu. L’e-lo min kol bir-cho-so, v’shi-ro-so, tush-b’cho-so, v’ne-ch’-mo-so, di-a-mi-ron b’ol-mo, v’im-ru O-men:</p>  
 <p>Y’heh sh’lo-mo rab-bo min sh’ma-yo v’cha-yim, o-le-nu v’al kol yis-ro-el, v’im-ru O-men:</p>  
 <p>O-seh sho-lom bim’-ro-mov, hu ya-a-seh, sho-lom, o-le-nu v’al kol yis-ro-el, v’imru O-men:</p>
