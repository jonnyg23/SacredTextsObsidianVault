 <p><a name="page_1"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 1</font></a><a name="page_2"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 2</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">1</h3>  <h3 align="center">AT THE DAWN</h3>  
 <p>At the dawn I seek Thee,<br>   Rock and refuge tried,<br> In due service speak Thee<br>   Morn and eventide.</p>  
 <p>Neath Thy greatness shrinking,<br>   Stand I sore afraid,<br> All my secret thinking<br>   Bare before Thee laid.</p>  
 <p>  Little to Thy glory<br> Heart or tongue can do;<br>   Small remains the story,<br> Add we spirit too.</p>  
 <p>  Yet since mans praise ringing<br> May seem good to Thee,<br>   I will praise Thee singing<br> While Thy breaths in me.</p>
