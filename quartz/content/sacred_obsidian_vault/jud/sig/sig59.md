 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Selected Religious Poems of Solomon ibn Gabirol</i>, tr. by Israel Zangwill, [1923], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h4 align="center">VII.</h4>  
 <p>Thou art Light celestial, and the eyes of the pure shall behold Thee<br> But the clouds of sin shall veil Thee from the eyes of the sinners.<br> Thou art Light, hidden in this world but to be revealed in the visible world on high.<br> "On the mount of the Lord shall it be seen."<br> Light Eternal art Thou, and the eye of the intellect longeth and yearneth for Thee.<br> "Yet only a part shall it see, the whole it shall not behold."</p>
