 <p><a name="page_85"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 85</font></a></p>  <h4 align="center">IV.</h4>  
 <p>Thou livest, but not from any restricted season nor from any known period.<br> Thou livest, but not through breath and soul, for Thou art soul of the soul.<br> Thou livest, but not with the life of man, which is like unto vanity and its end the moth and the worm.<br> Thou livest, and he who layeth hold of Thy secret shall find eternal delight:<br> "He shall eat and live for ever."</p>
