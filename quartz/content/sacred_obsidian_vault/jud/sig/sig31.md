 <p><a name="page_45"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 45</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">28</h3>  <h3 align="center">LET THE ISLES REJOICE</h3>  
 <p>Let the numerous isles rejoice with trembling,<br> For He is high and exalted and acknowledged as One<br> In the height of the firmament.<br> The Lord reigneth, let the earth rejoice.<br> The clouds acclaim Thee beyond every other power,<br> In every mouth is thy unity uttered,<br> And by the people of God is Thy praise proclaimed.<br> And who is like to Thy people Israel,<br> The one nation on earth,<br> To give thanks to Thee upstanding,<br> O God inhabiting the heights,<br> And to proclaim Thee as One?<br> The Lord reigneth, let the nations quake.<br> He sitteth among the Cherubim, let the earth tremble.<br> The scattered shalt Thou assemble and the sighing redeem,<br> To Thy holy house Thou shalt lead them with rejoicing,<br> And from earths four corners gather the exiles.</p>
