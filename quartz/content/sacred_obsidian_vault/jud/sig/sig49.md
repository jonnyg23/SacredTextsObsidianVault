 <p><a name="page_75"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 75</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">46</h3>  <h3 align="center">ARISE, O MY RAPTURE</h3>  
 <p>Arise, O my rapture, at dawn I exclaim,<br>   Go seeking the face of my love, the King,<br> I thirst at the thought of Him, burn as with flame,<br>   And chatter like swallow upon the wing.</p>  
 <p>No gifts can I bring save of heart or of wit,<br>   My cause to my lips I can only trust.<br> Desires my Redeemer a ritual fit,<br>   How should I suffice who am based on dust?</p>  
 <p>When I with my self seek communion, I shrink,<br>   Were I mightier far, I should still be small,<br> Soul and strength in adoring Thee faint and sink,<br>   Yet sing Thee I must till the end of all.</p>
