 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Selected Religious Poems of Solomon ibn Gabirol</i>, tr. by Israel Zangwill, [1923], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h4 align="center">XXVI.</h4>  
 <p>Who can approach Thy seat?<br> For beyond the sphere of Intelligence hast Thou established the throne of Thy glory;<br> There standeth the splendour of Thy veiled habitation,<br> And the mystery and the foundation.<br> Thus far reacheth Intelligence, but cometh here to a standstill,<br> For higher still hast Thou mounted, and ascended Thy mighty throne,<br> "And no man may go up with Thee."</p>
