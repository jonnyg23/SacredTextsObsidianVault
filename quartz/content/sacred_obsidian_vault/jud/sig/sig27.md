 <p><a name="page_38"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 38</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">24</h3>  <h3 align="center">TWO THINGS HAVE MET</h3>  
 <p>Two things have met in me, one in their ways,<br>   And stand within me, above or below,<br> My tongue that hastes to proclaim Thy praise,<br>   My heart Thy greatness to see and know.<br> The angels on high cannot speak of Thy glory,<br>   Then how shall contemptible man tell its story?</p>  
 <p>When men bring tribute, an ox, say, or dove,<br>   The lean or the fat gives Thee equal delight,<br> If but tis brought by a heart full of love.<br>   So too take my prayer as priestly rite,<br> For my soul and spirit unite in Thy praise,<br>   Two things having met in me, one in their ways.</p>
