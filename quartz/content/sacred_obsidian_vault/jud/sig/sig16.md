 <p><a name="page_16"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 16</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">13</h3>  <h3 align="center">I HAVE SOUGHT THEE DAILY</h3>  
 <p>I have sought Thee daily at dawn and twilight,<br>   I have stretched my hands to Thee, turned my face,<br> Now the cry of a heart athirst I will utter,<br>   Like the beggar who cries at my door for grace.<br> The infinite heights are too small to contain Thee,<br>   Yet perchance Thou canst niche in the clefts of me.<br> Shall my heart not treasure the hope to gain Thee,<br>   Or my yearning fail till my tongues last plea?<br>   Nay, surely Thy name I will worship, while breath in my nostrils be.</p>
