 <p><a name="page_7"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 7</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">4</h3>  <h3 align="center">INVITATION</h3>  
 <p>Come up to me at early dawn,<br> Come up to me, for I am drawn,<br> Belovèd, by my spirits spell,<br> To see the sons of Israël.<br> For thee, my darling, I will spread<br> Within my court a golden bed,<br> And I will set a table there<br> And bread for thee I will prepare,<br> For thee my goblet I will fill<br> With juices that my vines distil:<br> And thou shalt drink to hearts delight,<br> Of all my flavours day and night.<br> The joy in thee I will evince<br> With which a people greets its prince.<br> O son of Jesse, holy stem,<br> Gods servant, born of Bethlehem!</p>
