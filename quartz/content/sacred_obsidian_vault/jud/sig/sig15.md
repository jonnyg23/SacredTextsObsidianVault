 <p><a name="page_15"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 15</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">12</h3>  <h3 align="center">ECSTASY</h3>  
 <p>My thoughts astounded asked me why<br> Towards the whirling wheels on high<br> In ecstasy I rush and fly.</p>  
 <p>The living God is my desire,<br> It carries me on wings of fire,<br> Body and soul to Him aspire.</p>  
 <p>God is at once my joy and fate,<br> This yearning me He did create,<br> At thought of Him I palpitate.</p>  
 <p>Shall song with all its loveliness<br> Submerge my soul with happiness<br> Before the God of Gods it bless?</p>
