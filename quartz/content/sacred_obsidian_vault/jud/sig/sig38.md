 <p><a name="page_60"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 60</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">35</h3>  <h3 align="center">LAMENTATION</h3>  
 <p>Strayed in mid-youth, rouse up, nor sleep, for lo!<br> The days of youth like clouds of smoke will pass.<br> Ere evening falls, thou shalt be withered grass,<br> Though morning saw thee like a lily blow.</p>  
 <p>Why waste on ancestors a heated breath,<br> Or note which progeny was Abrahams?<br> Whether his food be herbs or Bashan rams,<br> Man, wretched wight, is on his way to death.</p>
