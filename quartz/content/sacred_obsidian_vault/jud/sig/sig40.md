 <p><a name="page_64"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 64</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">37</h3>  <h3 align="center">ALMIGHTY GOD</h3>  
 <p>Almighty God, who sufferest Thyself<br> To be entreated, and who payest heed<br> Unto the poor, how long wilt Thou from me<br> Be far and hidden? Night and day I turn<br> And with a steadfast heart I call to Thee,<br> And pour incessant gratitude for Thy<br> Excelling goodness. O my King, with pain<br> For Thee my heart is torn, in Thee it trusts.<br> Dreaming this shut-in dream, it looks to Thee<br> For lifes interpretation. This I ask,<br> This is the plea to which I beg assent,<br> My sole petition, neither more nor less.</p>
