 <p><a name="page_19"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 19</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">16</h3>  <h3 align="center">THE SUN</h3>  
 <p>Like a bridegroom the sun<br> Dons his robe that is spun<br>        Of light,<br> Which from Thee emanated<br> Yet in no wise abated<br>        Thy light.</p>  
 <p>Taught to go westward round<br> With obeisance profound<br>        To his Lord,<br> He by service so loyal<br> To a master so royal<br>        Is a lord.</p>  
 <p>While his homage each day<br> Serves to mark and display<br>        Thy glory,<br> Tis Thy hand that investeth<br> The robe on which resteth<br>        His glory.</p>
