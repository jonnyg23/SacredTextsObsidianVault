 <p><a name="page_71"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 71</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">43</h3>  <h3 align="center">INVOCATION</h3>  
 <p>Root of our saviour,<br> The scion of Jesse,<br> Till when wilt thou linger,<br> Invisible, buried?<br> Bring forth a flower,<br> For winter is over!</p>  
 <p>Why should a slave rule<br> The lineage of princes,<br> A hairy barbarian<br> Replace our young sovran?</p>  
 <p>The years are a thousand<br> Since, broken and scattered,<br> We wander in exile,<br> Like waterfowl lost in<br> The depths of the desert.</p>  
 <p>No man in white linen<br> Reveals at our asking<br> The end of our Exile.<br> God sealed up the matter,<br> And closed up the knowledge.</p>
