 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Tractate Sanhedrin</i>, Herbert Danby tr. [1919], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h1 align="center">IV.--OFFENDERS LIABLE TO CAPITAL PUNISHMENT</h1>  <h3 align="center">A.--THOSE WHO ARE PUNISHABLE BY STONING</h3>  
 <p>VII. 4. These are they who are to be stoned: the man who has criminal connexion with his</p>  
 <p><a name="page_96"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 96</font></a></p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><b>M</b>.</font></span>mother, his father's wife, his daughter-in-law, a male, or a beast; the woman who has criminal connexion with a beast; a blasphemer; an idolater; he who offers one of his children to Moloch; the <i>Ba‘al ‘Ob;</i> the <i>Yidd‘oni;</i> <a name="fr_296"></a><a href="tsa19.htm#fn_293"><font size="1">1</font></a> he who defiles the Sabbath; he who curses his father or mother; he who has criminal connexion with a betrothed damsel; the beguiler to idolatry; he who leads a town astray; the sorcerer; and the stubborn and rebellious son.</p>
