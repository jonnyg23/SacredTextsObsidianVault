 <p><span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><b>M</b>.</font></span>. IX. 3.  <h3 align="CENTER">Footnotes</h3>
 <p><a name="fn_469"></a><a href="tbr58.htm#fr_470">78:3</a> <i>tools</i>. The word includes vessels and implements of all kinds.</p>
 <p><a name="fn_470"></a><a href="tbr58.htm#fr_471">78:4</a> has, "who has given us life, and has established us, and has allowed us to reach this time."—A service for the consecration of a house is given in SA, pp. 300-303, but the formula here quoted is on p. ccxix. Abrahams, in his note, says it is "prescribed for use on the enjoyment of a new possession or pleasure for the first time."</p>
