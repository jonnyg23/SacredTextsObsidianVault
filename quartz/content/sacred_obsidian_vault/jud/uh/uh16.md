 <img src="img/templ00.jpg"></p>  <h3 align="center">Hallel</h3>  
 <p align="center"><small><small>PSALM CXIII</small></small></p>  
 <p align="center"><small><i>Leader:</i></small></p>  
 <p><a name="img_hinit"></a>
 </p><table align="LEFT"><tr><td align="CENTER" valign="CENTER"><img src="img/hinit.jpg" align="LEFT"></td></tr></table>HALLELUJAH.  
 <p>Praise, O ye servants of the Lord,<br> Praise the name of the Lord.</p>  
 <p align="center"><small><i>Company:</i></small></p>  
 <p>Blessed be the name of the Lord<br> From this time forth and for ever.</p>  
 <p align="center"><small><i>Leader:</i></small></p>  
 <p>From the rising of the sun unto the going down thereof<br> The Lord's name is to be praised.</p>  
 <p align="center"><small><i>Company:</i></small></p>  
 <p>The Lord is high above all nations,<br> His glory is above the heavens.</p>  
 <p align="center"><small><i>Leader:</i></small></p>  
 <p>Who is like unto the Lord our God,<br> That is enthroned on high,</p>  
 <p align="center"><small><i>Company:</i></small></p>  
 <p>That looketh down low<br> Upon heaven and upon earth?</p>  
 <p align="center"><small><i>Leader:</i></small></p>  
 <p>Who raiseth up the poor out of the dust,<br> And lifteth up the needy out of the dunghill;</p>  
 <p align="center"><small><i>Company:</i></small></p>  
 <p>That He may set him with princes,<br> Even with the princes of His people.</p>  
 <p align="center"><small><i>Leader:</i></small></p>  
 <p>Who maketh the barren woman to dwell in her house<br> As a joyful mother of children.</p>  
 <p align="center"><small><i>Company:</i></small></p>  
 <p>Hallelujah.</p>

 <p align="center"><small><small>PSALM CXIV</small></small></p>  
 <p align="center"><small><i>Leader:</i></small></p>  
 <p>WHEN Israel came forth out of Egypt,<br> The house of Jacob from a people of strange language;</p>  
 <p align="center"><small><i>Company:</i></small></p>  
 <p>Judah became His sanctuary,<br> Israel His dominion.</p>  
 <p align="center"><small><i>Leader:</i></small></p>  
 <p>The sea saw it, and fled;<br> The Jordan turned backward.</p>  
 <p align="center"><small><i>Company:</i></small></p>  
 <p>The mountains skipped like rams,<br> The hills like young sheep,</p>  
 <p align="center"><small><i>Leader:</i></small></p>  
 <p>What aileth thee, O thou sea, that thou fleest?<br> Thou Jordan, that thou turnest backward?</p>  
 <p align="center"><small><i>Company:</i></small></p>  
 <p>Ye mountains that ye skip like rams;<br> Ye hills, like young sheep?</p>  
 <p align="center"><small><i>Leader:</i></small></p>  
 <p>Tremble, thou earth, at the presence of the Lord,<br> At the presence of the God of Jacob;</p>  
 <p align="center"><small><i>Company:</i></small></p>  
 <p>Who turned the rock into a pool of water,<br> The flint into a fountain of waters.</p>
