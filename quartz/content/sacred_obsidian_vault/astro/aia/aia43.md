   <h3 align="center">OF THE ✶ AND △ ASPECTS BETWEEN THE SIGNIFICATORS.</h3>  
 <p align="center">♄ ✶ <i>or</i> △ ♃</p>  
 <p>If ♄ be significator, it gives riches by means of agriculture; and he is of a sedate and religious disposition.</p>  
 <p><a name="page_324"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 324</font></a></p>  
 <p>If ♃ be significator, he is extremely grave, and frequently gains riches by legacies or mining concerns.</p>  
 <p align="center">♄ ✶ <i>or</i> △ ♂</p>  
 <p>If ♄ be significator, it increases the courage of the person so signified, and renders him more open in his resentment.</p>  
 <p>If ♂ be significator, he is prudent and cautious, bigotted in religion, and, should other aspects befriend ♂, he may gain an estate.</p>  
 <p align="center">♄ ✶ <i>or</i> △ ☉</p>  
 <p>If ♄ be significator, he is generous and noble, though somewhat austere in his behaviour.</p>  
 <p>If ☉ be significator, he is ostentatious, boastful, and conceited; he may be expected to gain by legacies, or to be successful as a farmer.</p>  
 <p align="center">♄ ✶ <i>or</i> △ ♀</p>  
 <p>If ♄ be significator, he is prodigal and extravagant, wasting his money among females.</p>  
 <p>If ♀ be significator, he is modest, shy, and retired in his manners; he gains the favour of elderly people, and sometimes inherits their property.</p>  
 <p align="center">♄ ✶ <i>or</i> △ ☿</p>  
 <p>If ♄ be significator, it gives ingenuity and subtilty, though his talents are mostly employed to little purpose.</p>  
 <p>If ☿ be significator, he is very cautious and prudent, and is addicted to the study of arts and sciences.</p>  
 <p align="center">♄ ✶ or △ ☽</p>  
 <p>Of ♄ be significator, the querent is changeable, jealous, and mistrustful.</p>  
 <p>If ☽ be significator, he is vain and conceited, mean in his actions, though without the excuse of rashness, as he does nothing without much deliberation.</p>  
 <p><a name="page_326"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 326</font></a></p>  
 <p align="center">♃ ✶ <i>or</i> △ ♂</p>  
 <p>If ♃ be significator, it gives bravery, and the spirit of military adventure; he is a good soldier, surgeon, or chemist.</p>  
 <p>If ♂ be significator, he is noble, generous, and ambitious, and will rise rapidly in the army.</p>  
 <p align="center">♃ ✶ <i>or</i> △ ☉</p>  
 <p>If ♃ be significator, it makes one extremely fortunate, and very noble and courageous in his disposition.</p>  
 <p>If ☉ be significator, he gains money rapidly, is always respected, and possesses a most excellent disposition.</p>  
 <p align="center">♃ ✶ <i>or</i> △ ♀</p>  
 <p>If ♃ be significator, it causes beauty, love, riches, and real goodness of heart: this is the most fortunate aspect that can be formed.</p>  
 <p>If ♀ be significator, the person is virtuous, amiable, of a noble disposition, incapable of fraud or malice.</p>  
 <p align="center">♃ ✶ <i>or</i> △ ☿</p>  
 <p>If ♃ be significator, it gives great learning, sound judgment, and excellent abilities.</p>  
 <p>If ☿ be significator, he possesses solid sense, an open, generous disposition, and real good fortune.</p>  
 <p align="center">♃ ✶ <i>or</i> △ ☽</p>  
 <p>If ♃ be significator, it makes a man very fortunate, beloved by females, and much respected by the poorer classes of society.</p>  
 <p>If ☽ be significator, he is just and charitable, sincere in his friendships, and generous to the full extent of his means.</p>  
 <p align="center">♂ ✶ <i>or</i> △ ☉</p>  
 <p>If ♂ be significator, it gives a very noble disposition, and</p>  
 <p><a name="page_326"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 326</font></a></p>  
 <p>great mind; it causes one to rise rapidly in the army; he a uncommonly successful in war, and will gain much by the patronage of men in power.</p>  
 <p>If ☉ be significator, it confers great bravery, and a high spirit; he rises to grandeur by means of his courage and invincible military talents.</p>  
 <p align="center">♂ ✶ <i>or</i> △ ♀</p>  
 <p>If ♂ be significator, it causes lewdness and dissipation; his disposition is not radically bad, but he is extremely thoughtless and improvident; he may gain by females, for he seems to possess a fascinating influence, which he never fails to exert to the utmost with the female sex.</p>  
 <p>If ♀ be significator, he is handsome, but proud, rash, and inconsiderate; and neither remarkable for prudence nor principle.</p>  
 <p align="center">♂ ✶ <i>or</i> △ ☿</p>  
 <p>If ♂ be significator, this aspect gives great acuteness, penetration, and learning; the querent, however, is crafty, rather hasty, and extremely confident.</p>  
 <p>If ☿ be significator, he possesses great courage, is very ingenious, in any mechanical trade, a good engraver or mathematician, and will succeed in any thing that requires presence of mind, acuteness, and ready wit.</p>  
 <p>If ☿ receive any aspect of ♅, he is extremely fitted to become a good astrologer, especially if ☽ assist ☿.</p>  
 <p align="center">♂ ✶ <i>or</i> △ ☽</p>  
 <p>If ♂ be significator, it makes one restless and changeable, servile and talkative; he travels much, and receives much assistance from females.</p>  
 <p>If ☽ be significator, he is very passionate and changeable, with a high spirit and good abilities.</p>  
 <p><a name="page_327"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 327</font></a></p>  
 <p align="center">☉ ✶ <i>or</i> △ ☽</p>  
 <p>If ☉ be significator, it confers riches and honour; the querent is fortunate with women, and is much respected by the multitude.</p>  
 <p>If ☽ be significator, he is proud and aspiring; he is generally successful, but his fortune is not permanent, unless both ☉ and ☽ be in fixed signs.</p>  
 <p align="center">♀ ✶ <i>or</i> △ ☿</p>  
 <p>If ♀ be significator, this aspect gives ingenuity subtlety, and good nature.</p>  
 <p>If ☿ be significator, the querent possesses a refined and accomplished mind; he is neat in his person, and elegant in his manners; a lover of music and the fine arts in general.</p>  
 <p align="center">♀ ✶ <i>or</i> △ ☽</p>  
 <p>If ♀ be significator, it is a very fortunate aspect; it shews a person who is much assisted by female friends; and one who, though unstable, often obtains considerable property.</p>  
 <p>If ☽ be significator, the querent is gentle, obliging, amiable, and genteel in his manners, and is much admired by females; whose condition in life depends on the strength or debility of ♀.</p>  
 <p align="center">☿ ✶ <i>or</i> △ ☽</p>  
 <p>If ☿ be significator, the person signified is witty, ingenious, subtle, easily learning any thing to which he applies, and frequently acquiring many sciences without any assistance. He is somewhat reserved, and a little melancholy, but, from his extensive knowledge, he is always a useful and sometimes a pleasant companion.</p>  
 <p>If ☽ be significator, this is the most favourable aspect for learning or scientific speculation.</p>
