 <p>
 This is Donald Mackenzie's able retelling of the Northern mythological cycle.
 He weaves a coherent narrative from
 the Eddas, the Niebelunglied, the Volsung Saga, Beowulf,
 the primordial Hamlet myths, and Medieval German tales of chivalry.
 MacKenzie also wrote
 <a href="../../egy/eml/index.htm">Egyptian Myth and Legend</a>
 and
 <a href="../../cla/moc/index.htm">Myths of Crete and Pre-Hellenic Europe</a>.
