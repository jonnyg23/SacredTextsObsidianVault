 <p><a name="page_21"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 21</font></a></p>  <h2 align="center">PART III.</h2>  <h3 align="center">I. ÍGOŔ'S ESCAPE.</h3>  
 <p> </p>  <table border="0" width="100%">  <tr>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="50%">   
 <p>Прысну море   полунощи;<br>   <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="664">664</a></font></span> идутъ   сморци   мьглами:<br>   Игореви   князю Богъ путь   кажетъ<br>   изъ земли   Половецкой<br>   на землю   Рускую,<br>   <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="668">668</a></font></span> къ отню   злату столу.<br>       Погасоша   вечеру зари.<br>   Игорь с<i>ъ</i>пить, Игорь   б<i>ъ</i>дитъ,<br>   Игорь мыслію   поля мѣритъ<br>   <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="672">672</a></font></span> отъ великого   Дону<br>   до малаго   Донца.<br>       <i>Гомонь</i>   въ полуночи:<br>   Овлуръ   свисну за рѣкою;<br>   <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="676">676</a></font></span> велить   князю<br>       Разумѣти князю   Игорю не <i>бысть</i>;<br>   Кликну. Стукну   земля:<br>   въшумѣ   трава.<br>   <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="680">680</a></font></span> Вежи [ся]   половецкыя   подвизашася;<br>   Игорь князь   поскочи<br>   горнастаемъ   къ тростію<br>   и бѣлымъ   гоголемъ на   воду;<br>   <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="684">684</a></font></span> въвержеся   на борзъ   комонь,<br>   и скочи съ   него босымъ   волкомъ,<br>   и потече къ   лугу Донца,<br>   и полетѣ   соколомъ   подъ мьлами,<br>   <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="688">688</a></font></span> избивая   гуси и   лебеди<br>   завтроку и   обѣду   и ужинѣ.<br>       Коли Игорь   соколомъ   полетѣ,<br>   тогда Влуръ   влъкомъ   потече,<br>   <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="692">692</a></font></span> труся   собою   студеную   росу.<br>   Претръгоста   бо борзая   комоня.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="50%">   
 <p>The sea spurted   at midnight; the waterspouts pass like mists. God manifests the road to   Prince Ígoŕ from the Polovsk land to the Russian land, to his   fathers' golden throne.</p>   
 <p>The twilight   dimmed at even-time. Ígoŕ sleeps, Ígoŕ wakes,   Ígoŕ in his mind measures the plains from the mighty Don to the   little Donéts.</p>   
 <p>[There is]   clamour at midnight; Ovlur whistled beyond the stream, summons the prince;   Prince Ígoŕ could not understand.</p>   
 <p>[Ovlur] called   out loud; the earth throbbed; the grass rustled. The Polovsk tents began to   stir. Ígoŕ the Prince raced like an ermine to the brushwood, like   a white duck to the water, cast himself on his swift horse and leapt from it   like a swift-footed wolf and fled to the meadow of the Donéts, and   flew like a hawk in the mists, slaying geese and swans for breakfast, dinner   and supper.</p>   
 <p>When Ígoŕ flew like a hawk, then Vlur fled   like a wolf, shaking off himself the cold dew. For they had over-ridden their   swift steeds.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  
 <p> </p>  
 <p><a name="page_22"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 22</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">II.</h3>  <h4 align="center">(1) ÍGOŔ SPEAKS WITH THE RIVER DONÉTS.</h4>  
 <p> </p>  <table border="0" width="100%">  <tr>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="50%">   
 <p>Донецъ   рече: "Княже Игорю!<br>   не мало ти   величія,<br>   <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="696">696</a></font></span> а Кончаку   нелюбія,<br>   а Руской   земли   веселиа."<br>       Игорь   рече: "О Донче,<br>   не мало ти   величія,<br>   <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="700">700</a></font></span> лелѣявшу   князя на волнахъ,<br>   стлавшу ему   зелену   траву<br>   на своихъ   сребреныхъ   брезѣхъ;<br>   одѣвавшу   его теплыми   м<i>ъ</i>глами<br>   <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="704">704</a></font></span> подъ сѣнію   зелен<i>а</i>   древу.<br>   Стрежаше е гоголемъ   на водѣ,<br>   чайцами на   струяхъ,<br>   чрьнядьми   на вѣтрѣхъ."</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="50%">   
 <p>Donéts   [the river] said:--"Prince Ígoŕ, not mean is thy greatness,   nor Končák's hatred, nor the joy of the Russian land!</p>   
 <p>Ígoŕ said:--"Oh Donéts! Not mean   is thy greatness, thou who swayest the Prince on thy waves, and hast spread   out for him [a bed of] green grass by thy silvery banks, clothing him with   warm mists beneath the shade of the green tree; thou hast guarded him with a   duck on the water, with gulls on the billows, with mallards on the winds.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  
 <p> </p>  <h4 align="center">(2) RECOLLECTION OF ROSTÍSLAV VSÉVOLODIČ,</h4>  
 <p> </p>  <table border="0" width="100%">  <tr>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="50%">   
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="708">708</a></font></span> "Не тако-ли,"--рече,   "рѣка   Стугна,<br>   худу струю   имѣя,<br>   пожръши   чужи ручьи,<br>   и стругы, ростре   на кусту?<br>   <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="712">712</a></font></span> Унош<i>ѣ</i> князю   Ростиславу<br>   затвори Днѣпрь   темнѣ   березѣ.<br>       Плачется   мати   Ростиславля<br>   по уноши   князи   Ростиславѣ."<br>       <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="716">716</a></font></span> Уныша цвѣты   жалобою,<br>   и древо с<i>ъ</i> тугою<br>   къ земли преклонило.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="50%">   
 <p>"Was it   not thus," he said, "that the river Stugná, having an evil   stream, swallowing strange brooks ground down the barges on the bushes?</p>   
 <p>The Dnĕpr   closed his dark banks to the youth Prince Rostíslav.   Rostíslav's mother wails for the youth Prince Rostíslav."</p>   
 <p>The flowers   drooped for sorrow and the tree for grief bowed low to earth.</p>   
 <p> </p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  
 <p> </p>  <h3 align="center">III.</h3>  <h4 align="center">(1) THE PURSUIT AFTER ÍGOŔ.</h4>  
 <p> </p>  <table border="0" width="100%">  <tr>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="50%">   
 <p>(1)   не сорокы   втроскоташа,<br>   <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="720">720</a></font></span> на слѣду   Игоревѣ<br>   ѣздитъ   Гзакъ съ   Кончакомъ.<br>       Тогда врани   не граяхуть,<br>   галици помолкоша,<br>   <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="724">724</a></font></span> сорок<i>и</i> не   троскоташа,<br>   по лозію   ползаша;<br>   только дятлове   тектомъ<br>   путь къ рѣцѣ   кажутъ;</p>   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="50%">   
 <p>It was not the   magpies chirping; in pursuit of Ígoŕ, Gzak rides with   Končák.</p>   
 <p>Then the crows   did not croak, the jackdaws were still, the magpies did not chirp; they crept   in the boughs. Only the woodpeckers by their pecking show the road to the   river;</p>   
 <p> </p>   </td>  </tr>  <tr>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="50%">   
 <p><a name="page_23"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 23</font></a></p>   
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="728">728</a></font></span> соловии   веселыми пѣсньми<br>   свѣтъ   повѣдаютъ.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="50%">   
 <p> </p>   
 <p>the   nightingales with their merry song announce the dawn.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  
 <p> </p>  
 <p> </p>  <h4 align="center">(2) THE CONVERSATION OF GZAK AND KONČÁK.</h4>  
 <p> </p>  <table border="0" width="100%">  <tr>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="50%">   
 <p>(2) Молъвитъ   Гза къ   Кончакови:--<br>   "Аже соколъ   къ гнѣзду   летитъ,<br>   <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="732">732</a></font></span> соколича   рострѣляевѣ<br>   своими   злачеными   стрелами."<br>       Рече   Кончакъ ко   Гзѣ:--<br>   "Аже соколъ   къ гнѣзду   летитъ,<br>   <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="736">736</a></font></span> а вѣ   сокольца   опутаевѣ<br>   красньою дѣвицею"<br>       И рече   Гзакъ къ   Кончакови:--<br>   "Аше его   опутаевѣ<br>   <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="740">740</a></font></span> красною дѣвицею,<br>   ни нама   будетъ   сокольца<br>   ни нама   красны дѣвице:<br>   то почнутъ   наю птици   бити<br>   <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="744">744</a></font></span> въ полѣ   Половецкомъ."</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="50%">   
 <p>Gzak speaks to   Končák:--"If the hawk is flying to his nest, we two will   shoot down the fledgeling with our gilded arrows!"</p>   
 <p>Končák   said to Gzak:--"If the hawk is flying to his nest we will fetter the   fledgeling with a maiden fair."</p>   
 <p>And Gzak said to Končák:--"If we fetter   him with a maiden fair, then we shall have neither the fledgeling nor the   fair maiden; but the birds will begin to assail us in the Polovsk   plains."</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>

 <p> </p>  <h3 align="center">IV. REMINISCENCE OF BOYÁN.</h3>  
 <p> </p>  <table border="0" width="100%">  <tr>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="50%">   
 <p>Рече Боянъ   и ходы<br>   Святъславл<i>и</i> на Коган<i>а</i>:--<br>   "Пѣснотвор<i>ъцъ</i> <i>азъ</i> стараго   времени<br>   <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="748">748</a></font></span> "Ярославля   <i>и</i> Ольгова:<br>       'Хоти тяжко   ти, голов<i>ѣ</i>,<br>       кромѣ плечю,<br>       зло ти, тѣлу,<br>       <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="752">752</a></font></span> кромѣ   головы;"--<br>     --Руской   земли безъ   Игоря!</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="50%">   
 <p>Boyán has told of the expeditions of   Svyatosláv [the First] against the Kogan: "I am the poet of the   ancient time [i.e. Vladímir I], of the time of Yarosláv [the   First], and Olég [of Tmutarakáń]." 'Though it be   heavy to thee, the head, parted from the shoulders; ill is to thee, body,   parted from the head:--to the Russian land without Ígoŕ!'</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  
 <p> </p>  
 <p> </p>  <h3 align="center">V. IGOŔ'S RETURN TO RUSSIA.</h3>  
 <p> </p>  <table border="0" width="100%">  <tr>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="50%">   
 <p>Солнце   свѣтится   на небесѣ;<br>   Игорь князь   въ Руской   земли.<br>   <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="756">756</a></font></span> Дѣвици   поютъ на   Дунаи;<br>   вьются   голоси   чрезъ море   до Кіева.</p>   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="50%">   
 <p>The sun shines   in the heavens. Ígoŕ the prince is in the Russian land. The   maidens sing on the Danube; their voices mingle across the waters [and are   borne] to Kíev.</p>   
 <p> </p>   </td>  </tr>  <tr>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="50%">   
 <p><a name="page_24"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 24</font></a></p>   
 <p>Игорь   идетъ по   Боричеву<br>   къ святѣй   Богородици Пирогощей.<br>   <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="760">760</a></font></span> Страны ради,   гради   весели,<br>   пѣвше   пѣснь   старымъ   княземъ<br>   а потомъ   молодымъ пѣти.</p>   
 <p>Слава   Игорю Святъславличь,<br>   <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="764">764</a></font></span> буй-туру   Всеволоде,<br>   Владиміру   Игоревичь!</p>   
 <p>Здрави   князи, и   дружина,<br>   <i>побарючи</i>   за Христьяны<br>   <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="768">768</a></font></span> на поганыя   полк<i>ы</i>!</p>   
 <p>Княземъ   слава а <i>дружик</i><i>ѣ</i> хвала!</p>   
 <p> </p>   
 <p> </p>   
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="770">770</a></font></span> Аминь!</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="50%">   
 <p> </p>   
 <p>Ígoŕ   repairs up [the hill] Boríčev to the Holy Mother of God at   Pirogóšč.</p>   
 <p>The countries   are happy, the cities rejoicing; singing a song to the princes of yore: and   hereafter the the young shall sing it.</p>   
 <p>Glory, oh   Ígoŕ Svyatoslávič, brave bull Vsévolod,   Vladímir Ígorevič!</p>   
 <p>The princes   prosper and the družína fighting for the Christians against the   Pagans!</p>   
 <p>Glory to the   Princes and (praise) to the <i>družína</i>!</p>
