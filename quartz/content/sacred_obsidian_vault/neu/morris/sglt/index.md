 <p>
 This fantasy novel by William Morris is the tale of the quest of
 Hallblithe to rescue his fiance from pirates.
 He finally catches up with her in the utopian Land of the Glittering Plain.
 This locale is also known as the Land of the Living Men, inhabitants
 of which are immortal.
 </p>
