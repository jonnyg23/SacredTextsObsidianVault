 <h1>Riddle 70</h1>
 <p><cite>Verse Indeterminate Saxon</cite></p>
 <dl compact>
 <dt></dt>
 <dd>
 Wiht is wrætlic      þam þe <strong>hyre</strong> wisan ne conn. <br>
 Singeð þurh sidan.      Is se sweora woh, <br>
 orþoncum geworht;      hafaþ eaxle tua <br>
 scearp on gescyldrum.      His gesceapo <strong>dreogeð</strong> <br>
 </dd>
 <dt>5</dt>
 <dd>
 þe swa wrætlice      be wege stonde <br>
 heah ond hleortorht      hæleþum to nytte. <br>
