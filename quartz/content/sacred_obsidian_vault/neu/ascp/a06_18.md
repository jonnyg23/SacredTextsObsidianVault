 <h1>The Meters of Boethius: Meter 18</h1>
 <p><cite>Verse Indeterminate Saxon</cite></p>
 <dl compact>
 <dt></dt>
 <dd>
 Eala ðæt se yfla      unrihta gedeð <br>
 wraða willa      wohhæmetes, <br>
 þæt he mid ealle gedræfð      anra gehwylces <br>
 monna cynnes      mod fulneah ðon. <br>
 </dd>
 <dt>5</dt>
 <dd>
 Hwæt, sio wilde beo,      þeah wis sie, <br>
 anunga sceal      eall forweorðan <br>
 gif hio yrringa      awuht stingeð. <br>
 Swa sceal sawla gehwilc      siððan losian, <br>
 gif se lichoma      forlegen weorðeð <br>
 </dd>
 <dt>10</dt>
 <dd>
 unrihthæmede,      bute him ær cume <br>
 hreow to heortan,      ær he hionan wende. <br>
 </dd>
