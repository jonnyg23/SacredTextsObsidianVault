 <h1>Riddle 38</h1>
 <p><cite>Verse Indeterminate Saxon</cite></p>
 <dl compact>
 <dt></dt>
 <dd>
 Ic þa wiht geseah      wæpnedcynnes, <br>
 <strong>geoguðmyrþe</strong> grædig;      him on gafol forlet <br>
 ferðfriþende      feower wellan <br>
 scire sceotan,      on gesceap þeotan. <br>
 </dd>
 <dt>5</dt>
 <dd>
 Mon maþelade,      se þe me gesægde: <br>
 "Seo wiht, gif hio gedygeð,      duna briceð; <br>
 gif he tobirsteð,      bindeð cwice." <br>
 </dd>
