 <h1>Metrical Charm 7: For the Water-Elf Disease</h1>
 <p><cite>Verse Indeterminate Saxon</cite></p>
 <dl compact>
 <dt></dt>
 <dd>
 Gif mon biþ on wæterælfadle, þonne beoþ him þa hand- <br>
 næglas wonne and þa eagan tearige and wile locian niþer. <br>
 Do him þis to læcedome: eoforþrote, cassuc, fone nioþo- <br>
 weard, eowberge, elehtre, eolone, merscmealwan crop, <br>
 </dd>
 <dt>5</dt>
 <dd>
 fenminte, dile, lilie, attorlaþe, polleie, marubie, docce, ellen, <br>
 felterre, wermod, streawbergean leaf, consolde; ofgeot mid <br>
 ealaþ, do hæligwæter to, sing þis gealdor ofer þriwa: <br>
 Ic <strong>benne</strong> awrat      betest beadowræda, <br>
 swa benne ne burnon,      ne burston, <br>
 </dd>
 <dt>10</dt>
 <dd>
 ne fundian,      ne feologan, <br>
 ne hoppettan,      ne wund waxsian, <br>
 ne dolh diopian;      ac him self healde halewæge, <br>
 ne ace þe þon ma,      þe eorþan on eare ace. <br>
 Sing þis manegum siþum: Eorþe þe onbere eallum hire <br>
 </dd>
 <dt>15</dt>
 <dd>
 mihtum and mægenum. þas galdor mon mæg singan on <br>
 wunde. <br>
 </dd>
