 <h1>Riddle 10</h1>
 <p><cite>Verse Indeterminate Saxon</cite></p>
 <dl compact>
 <dt></dt>
 <dd>
 Neb wæs min on nearwe,      ond ic neoþan wætre, <br>
 flode underflowen,      firgenstreamum <br>
 swiþe besuncen,      ond on sunde awox <br>
 ufan yþum þeaht,      anum getenge <br>
 </dd>
 <dt>5</dt>
 <dd>
 liþendum wuda      lice mine. <br>
 Hæfde feorh cwico,      þa ic of fæðmum cwom <br>
 brimes ond beames      on blacum <strong>hrægle;</strong> <br>
 sume wæron hwite      hyrste mine, <br>
 þa mec lifgende      lyft upp ahof, <br>
 </dd>
 <dt>10</dt>
 <dd>
 wind of wæge,      siþþan wide bær <br>
 ofer seolhbaþo.      Saga hwæt ic hatte. <br>
 </dd>
