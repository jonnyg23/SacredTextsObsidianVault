 <h1>Metrical Charm 3: Against a Dwarf</h1>
 <p><cite>Verse Indeterminate Saxon</cite></p>
 <dl compact>
 <dt></dt>
 <dd>
 Wið dweorh man sceal niman <em>VII</em> lytle oflætan, swylce <br>
 man mid ofrað, and <strong>writan</strong> þas naman on ælcre oflætan: <br>
 Maximianus, Malchus, Iohannes, Martimianus, Dioni- <br>
 sius, Constantinus, Serafion. þænne eft þæt galdor, þæt <br>
 </dd>
 <dt>5</dt>
 <dd>
 her æfter cweð, man sceal singan, ærest on þæt wynstre <br>
 eare, þænne on þæt swiðre eare, þænne <strong>bufan</strong> þæs mannes <br>
 moldan. And ga þænne an mædenman to and ho hit on <br>
 his sweoran, and do man swa þry dagas; him bið sona sel. <br>
 Her com in gangan,      in <strong>spiderwiht,</strong> <br>
 </dd>
 <dt>10</dt>
 <dd>
 hæfde him his haman on handa,      cwæð þæt þu his hæncgest wære, <br>
 <strong>legde</strong> þe his teage an sweoran.      Ongunnan him of þæm lande liþan; <br>
 sona swa hy of þæm lande coman,      þa ongunnan him ða liþu colian. <br>
 þa com in gangan      <strong>dweores</strong> sweostar; <br>
 þa geændade heo      and aðas swor <br>
 </dd>
 <dt>15</dt>
 <dd>
 ðæt næfre þis ðæm adlegan      derian ne moste, <br>
 ne þæm þe þis galdor      begytan mihte, <br>
 oððe þe þis galdor      ongalan cuþe. <br>
 Amen. Fiað. <br>
