 <h1>Thureth</h1>
 <p><cite>Verse Indeterminate Saxon</cite></p>
 <dl compact>
 <dt></dt>
 <dd>
 Ic eom halgungboc;      healde hine dryhten <br>
 þe me fægere þus      frætewum belegde. <br>
 þureð to þance      þus het me wyrcean, <br>
 to loue and to wurðe,      þam þe leoht gesceop. <br>
 </dd>
 <dt>5</dt>
 <dd>
 Gemyndi is he      mihta gehwylcre <br>
 þæs þe he on foldan      gefremian mæg, <br>
 and him geþancie      þeoda waldend <br>
 þæs þe he on gemynde      madma manega <br>
 wyle gemearcian      metode to lace; <br>
 </dd>
 <dt>10</dt>
 <dd>
 and he sceal ece lean      ealle findan <br>
 þæs þe he on foldan      fremaþ to ryhte. <br>
 </dd>
