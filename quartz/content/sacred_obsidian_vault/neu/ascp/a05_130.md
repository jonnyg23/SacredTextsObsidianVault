 <h1>The Paris Psalter: Psalm 130</h1>
 <p><cite>Verse Indeterminate Saxon</cite></p>
 <dl compact>
 <dt></dt>
 <dd>
 Nis min heorte wið þe      ahafen, drihten, <br>
 ne mine eagan wið þe      on oferhygde. <br>
 Ne ic on mægene      miclum gange <br>
 ne wundur ofer me      wuniað ænig. <br>
 </dd>
 <dt>5</dt>
 <dd>
 Ac ic mid eaðmedum      eall geþafige; <br>
 is min sawl on ðon      swyþe gefeonde. <br>
 Swa man æt meder      bið miclum feded, <br>
 swa þu minre sawle      symble gyldest. <br>
 Israhelas on drihten      a getreowigen <br>
 </dd>
 <dt>10</dt>
 <dd>
 of ðyssum nu      awa to worulde. <br>
 </dd>
