 <h1>The Paris Psalter: Psalm 51</h1>
 <p><cite>Verse Indeterminate Saxon</cite></p>
 <dl compact>
 <dt></dt>
 <dd>
 fore ænigre      egesan næfde, <br>
 ne him fultum þær      fæstne gelyfde; <br>
 ac he on his welan spede      wræste getruwode, <br>
 and on idel gylp      ealra geornost. <br>
 </dd>
 <dt>5</dt>
 <dd>
 Ic þonne swa elebeam      up weaxende <br>
 on godes huse      ece gewene, <br>
 and on milde mod      mines drihtnes, <br>
 and me þæt to worulde      wat to helpe. <br>
 Ic þe andette      awa to feore <br>
 </dd>
 <dt>10</dt>
 <dd>
 on þære worulde      ðe þu geworhtest her; <br>
 forþan þu eart se gooda,      gleaw on gesyhðe, <br>
 þe þinne held curan,      þara haligra. <br>
 </dd>
