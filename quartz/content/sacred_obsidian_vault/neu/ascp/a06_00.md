 <h1>The Meters of Boethius: Proem</h1>
 <p><cite>Verse Indeterminate Saxon</cite></p>

 <dd>
 ðus ælfred us      ealdspell reahte, <br>
 cyning Westsexna,      cræft meldode, <br>
 leoðwyrhta list.      Him wæs lust micel <br>
 ðæt he ðiossum leodum      leoð spellode, <br>
 </dd>
 <dt>5</dt>
 <dd>
 monnum myrgen,      mislice cwidas, <br>
 þy læs ælinge      ut adrife <br>
 selflicne secg,      þonne he swelces lyt <br>
 gymð for his gilpe.      Ic sceal giet sprecan, <br>
 fon on fitte,      folccuðne ræd <br>
 </dd>

 <dd>
 hæleðum secgean.      Hliste se þe wille! <br>
