 <h1>Riddle 49</h1>
 <p><cite>Verse Indeterminate Saxon</cite></p>
 <dl compact>
 <dt></dt>
 <dd>
 Ic wat eardfæstne      anne standan, <br>
 deafne, dumban,      se oft dæges swilgeð <br>
 þurh gopes hond      gifrum lacum. <br>
 Hwilum <strong>on</strong> þam wicum      se wonna þegn, <br>
 </dd>
 <dt>5</dt>
 <dd>
 sweart ond saloneb,      sendeð oþre <br>
 under goman him      golde dyrran, <br>
 þa æþelingas      oft wilniað, <br>
 cyningas ond cwene.      Ic þæt cyn nu gen <br>
 nemnan ne wille,      þe him to nytte swa <br>
 </dd>
 <dt>10</dt>
 <dd>
 ond to dugþum doþ      þæt se dumba her, <br>
 eorp unwita,      ær <strong>forswilgeð.</strong> <br>
 </dd>
