 <h1>Fragments of Psalms: Psalm 89</h1>
 <p><cite>Verse Indeterminate Saxon</cite></p>
 <dl compact>
 <dt></dt>
 <dd>
 <em>Conuertere, domine, aliquantulum, et deprecabilis esto super seruos tuos.</em><br>
 Gehweorf us hwæthwygu,           halig drihten;  <br>
 wes ðinum scealcum           wel eaðbene.  <br>
 <em>Respice in seruos tuos et in opera tua, domine, et dirige filios eorum.</em><br>
 Geseoh þine scealcas           swæsum eagum,  <br>
 and on þin agen weorc,           ece drihten,  <br>
 </dd>
 <dt>5</dt>
 <dd>
 and heora bearn gerece           bliðum mode.  <br>
 <em>Et <b>sit</b> splendor domini dei nostri super nos, et opera manuum nostrarum dirige super nos, et opus manuum nostrarum dirige.</em><br>
 Wese us beorhtnys ofer           bliðan drihtnes,  <br>
 ures þæs godan godes           georne ofer ealle;  <br>
 gerece ure handgeweorc           heah ofer usic.  <br>
 </dd>
