 <h1>Fragments of Psalms: Psalm 58</h1>
 <p><cite>Verse Indeterminate Saxon</cite></p>
 <dl compact>
 <dt></dt>
 <dd>
 <em>Eripe me de inimicis meis, deus meus, et ab insurgentibus in me libera me.</em><br>
 Ahrede me, halig god,           hefiges niðes  <br>
 feonda minra,           þe me feohtað to;  <br>
 alys me fram laðum           þe me <b>lungre</b> on  <br>
 risan willað,           nymþe þu me ræd gife.  <br>
 </dd>
 <dt>5</dt>
 <dd>
 <em>Eripe me de operantibus iniquitatem, et de <b>uiris</b> sanguinum salua me.</em><br>
 Genere me fram niðe           nahtfremmendra  <br>
 þe her unrihtes           ealle wyrceað,  <br>
 and me wið blodhreowes weres           bealuwe gehæle.  <br>
 </dd>
