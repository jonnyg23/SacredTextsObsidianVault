 <h1>Riddle 85</h1>
 <p><cite>Verse Indeterminate Saxon</cite></p>
 <dl compact>
 <dt></dt>
 <dd>
 Nis min sele swige,      ne ic sylfa hlud <br>
 ymb ......      unc <strong>dryhten</strong> scop <br>
 siþ ætsomne.      Ic eom <strong>swiftre</strong> þonne he, <br>
 þragum strengra,      he þreohtigra. <br>
 </dd>
 <dt>5</dt>
 <dd>
 Hwilum ic me reste;      he sceal yrnan forð. <br>
 Ic him in wunige      a þenden ic lifge; <br>
 gif wit unc gedælað,      me bið deað witod. <br>
 </dd>
