 <p align="CENTER">THE BLACK CAT'S TAIL</p>
 <p>THOSE little gatherings which occur on the eyelids of children,
 locally called "whilks," are cured by passing a black cat's tail nine times
 over the place. If a ram cat, the cure is more certain.</p>
