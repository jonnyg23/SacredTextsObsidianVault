 <p align="CENTER">ON THE BIRTHS OF CHILDREN.</p>
 <p>
  "SUNDAY'S child is full of grace,<br>
 Monday's child is full in the face,<br>
 Tuesday's child is solemn and sad,<br>
 Wednesday's child is merry and glad,<br>
 Thursday's child is inclined to thieving,<br>
 Friday's child is free in giving,<br>
 Saturday's child works hard for his living."</p>
