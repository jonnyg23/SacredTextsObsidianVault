 <p align="CENTER">THE MAGPIE.</p>
 <p>
  "ONE is asign of anger,<br>
 Two is a sign of mirth,<br>
 Three is a sign of a wedding,<br>
 Four is a sign of a birth/death."</p>
 <p>A scolding woman is called a magpie. Whenever you see a
 magpie, take off your hat to it; this will turnaway the anger.</p>
