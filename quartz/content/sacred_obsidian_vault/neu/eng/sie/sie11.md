 <img src="img/14.jpg"></p>  <h3 align="center">The Little Boy found</h3>  
 <p>The little boy lost in the lonely fen,<br> Led by the wand’ring light,<br> Began to cry, but God ever nigh,<br> Appeard like his father in white.</p>  
 <p>He kissed the child &amp; by the hand led<br> And to his mother brought,<br> Who in sorrow pale, thro’ the lonely dale<br> Her little boy weeping sought.</p>
