 <img src="img/43.jpg"></p>  <h3 align="center">My Pretty ROSE TREE</h3>  
 <p>A flower was offerd to me:<br> Such a flower as May never bore.<br> But I said I've a Pretty Rose-tree,<br> And I passed the sweet flower o’er.</p>  
 <p>Then I went to my Pretty Rose-tree:<br> To tend her by day and by night.<br> But my Rose turnd away with jealousy:<br> And her thorns were my only delight.</p>  <h3 align="center">AH! SUN-FLOWER</h3>  
 <p>Ah Sun-flower! weary of time,<br> Who countest the steps of the Sun:<br> Seeking after that sweet golden clime,<br> Where the travellers journey is done.</p>  
 <p>Where the Youth pined away with desire,<br> And the pale Virgin shrouded in snow:<br> Arise from their graves and aspire,<br> Where my Sun-flower wishes to go.</p>  <h3 align="center">THE LILLY</h3>  
 <p>The modest Rose puts forth a thorn:<br> The humble Sheep, a threatning horn:<br> While the Lilly white, shall in Love delight,<br> Nor a thorn nor a threat stain her beauty bright.</p>
