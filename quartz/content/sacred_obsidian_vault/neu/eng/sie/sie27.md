 <img src="img/33.jpg"></p>  <h3 align="center">HOLY THURSDAY</h3>  
 <p>Is this a holy thing to see,<br> In a rich and fruitful land,<br> Babes reducd to misery,<br> Fed with cold and usurous hand?</p>  
 <p>Is that trembling cry a song?<br> Can it be a song of joy?<br> And so many children poor?<br> It is a land of poverty!</p>  
 <p>And their sun does never shine.<br> And their fields are bleak &amp; bare.<br> And their ways are fill’d with thorns<br> It is eternal winter there.</p>  
 <p>For where-e’er the sun does shine,<br> And where-e’er the rain does fall:<br> Babe can never hunger there,<br> Nor poverty the mind appall.</p>
