 <h1 align="CENTER">PART III</h1>
 <h1 align="CENTER">The Madness of Sir Tristram</h1>
 <i></i><p>HERE followeth the story of how Sir Tristram was driven out of Cornwall and of how he went mad because of his troubles. Likewise it shall be told how he performed several very wonderful adventures whilst he was in that state, and of how he was brought back into his senses again.</p>
