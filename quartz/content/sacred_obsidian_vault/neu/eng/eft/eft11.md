 <h1 align="CENTER">Mouse and Mouser</h1>
 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_015"></a>
 <a href="img/015.jpg"><img src="tn/015.jpg"><br><font size="-3">Click to enlarge</font></a></p>
 <p align="CENTER">THE Mouse went to visit the Cat, and found her sitting behind the hall door, spinning.</p>
 <p> </p>
 <p align="CENTER">MOUSE<br>
 What are you doing, my lady, my lady,<br>
 What are you doing, my lady?</p>
 <p> </p>
 <p align="CENTER">CAT <i>(sharply)<br>
 </i>I'm spinning old breeches, good body, good body,<br>
 I'm spinning old breeches, good body.</p>
 <p> </p>
 <p align="CENTER">MOUSE<br>
 Long may you wear them, my lady, my lady,<br>
 Long may you wear them, my lady.</p>
 <p> </p>
 <p align="CENTER">CAT (<i>gruffly</i>)<br>
 I'll wear 'em and tear 'em, good body, good body,<br>
 I'll wear 'em and tear 'em, good body.</p>
 <p> </p>
 <p align="CENTER">MOUSE<br>
 I was sweeping my room, my lady, my lady,<br>
 I was sweeping my room, my lady.</p>
 <p> </p>
 <p align="CENTER">CAT<br>
 The cleaner you'd be, good body, good body,<br>
 The cleaner you'd be, good body.</p>
 <p> </p>
 <p align="CENTER">MOUSE<br>
 I found a silver sixpence, my lady, my lady,<br>
 I found a silver sixpence, my lady.</p>
 <p> </p>
 <p align="CENTER">CAT<br>
 The richer you were, good body, good body,<br>
 The richer you were, good body.</p>
 <p> </p>
 <p align="CENTER">MOUSE<br>
 I went to the market, my lady, my lady,<br>
 I went to the market, my lady.</p>
 <p> </p>
 <p align="CENTER">CAT<br>
 The further you went, good body, good body,<br>
 The further you went, good body.</p>
 <p> </p>
 <p align="CENTER">MOUSE<br>
 I bought me a pudding, my lady, my lady,<br>
 I bought me a pudding, my lady.</p>
 <p> </p>
 <p align="CENTER">CAT <i>(snarling)<br>
 </i>The more meat you had, good body, good body,<br>
 The more meat you had, good body.</p>
 <p> </p>
 <p align="CENTER">MOUSE<br>
 I put it in the window to cool, my lady,<br>
 I put it in the window to cool.</p>
 <p> </p>
 <p align="CENTER">CAT <i>(sharply)<br>
 </i>The faster you'd eat it, good body, good body,<br>
 The faster you'd eat it, good body.</p>
 <p align="CENTER"> </p>
 <p align="CENTER">MOUSE <i>(timidly)<br>
 </i>The cat came and ate it, my lady, my lady,<br>
 The cat came and ate it, my lady.</p>
 <p align="CENTER"> </p>
 <p align="CENTER">CAT <i>(pouncingly)<br>
 </i>And I'll eat you, good body, good body,<br>
 And I'll eat you, good body.</p>
 <p align="CENTER"> </p>
 <i></i><p align="CENTER">(Springs upon the mouse and kills it.)</p>
