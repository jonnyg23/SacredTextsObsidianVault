 <h1 align="CENTER">CHRONOLOGY OF THE STORY.</h1>
 <p>872. The battle of Hafrs-firth.</p>
 <p>874. Begins the settlement of Iceland.</p>
 <p>cca. 897. Thrand and Ufeigh Grettir settle Gnup-Wardsrape.</p>
 <p>cca. 900. Onund Treefoot comes to Iceland.</p>
 <p>cca. 920. Death of Onund Treefoot.</p>
 <p>929. The Althing established.</p>
 <p>997 (?). Grettir born.</p>
 <p>I000. Christianity sanctioned by law.</p>
 <p>1004. Skapti Thorodson made law-man.</p>
 <p>1011. Grettir slays Skeggi; goes abroad, banished for three Years.</p>
 <p>1012. Slaying of Thorir Paunch and his fellows in Haramsey. Earl Eric goes to Denmark.</p>
 <p>1013. Slaying of Biorn at the Island of Gartar. Slaying of Thorgils Makson. Illugi Asmundson born. Death of Thorkel Krafla.</p>
 <p>1014. Slaying of Gunnar in Tunsberg. Grettir goes back to Iceland; fights with the men of Meal on Ramfirth-neck. Heath-slayings. Thorgeir Havarson outlawed. Fight with Glam the ghost.</p>
 <p>1015. Fight of Nesjar in Norway. Slaying of Thorbiorn Tardy. Grettir fares abroad. Burning</p>
 <p><a name="page_xviii"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. xviii</font></a></p>
 <p>of the sons of Thorir of Garth. Death of Asmund the Greyhaired.</p>
 <p>1016. Grettir meets King Olaf; fails to bear iron; goes east to Tunsberg to Thorstein Dromund. Slaying of Atli of Biarg. Grettir outlawed at the Thing for the burning of the sons of Thorir; his return to Iceland. Slaying of Thorbiorn Oxmain and his son Arnor.</p>
 <p>1017. Grettir at Reek-knolls. Law-suit for the slaying of Thorbiorn Oxmain. Grettir taken by the Icefirth churls.</p>
 <p>1018. Grettir at Liarskogar with Thorstein Kuggson his travels to the East to Skapti the lawman and Thorhall of Tongue, and thence to the Keel-mountain, where he met Hallmund (Air) for the first time.</p>
 <p>1019-1021. Grettir on Ernewater-heath.</p>
 <p>1021. Grettir goes to the Marshes.</p>
 <p>1022-1024. Grettir in Fairwoodfell.</p>
 <p>1024. Grettir visits Hallmund again.</p>
 <p>1025. Grettir discovers Thorirsdale.</p>
 <p>1025-1026. Grettir travels round by the East; haunts Madderdale-heath and Reek heath.</p>
 <p>1026. Thorstein Kuggson slain.</p>
 <p>1027. Grettir at Sandheaps in Bard-dale.</p>
 <p>1028. Grettir haunts the west by Broadfirth-dales, meets Thorod Snorrison.</p>
 <p>1028-1031. Grettir in Drangey.</p>
 <p>1029. Grettir visits Heronness-thing.</p>
 <p>1030. Grettir fetches fire from Reeks. Skapti the lawman dies.</p>
 <p>1031. Death of Snorri Godi and Grettir Asmundson.</p>
 <p>1033. Thorbiorn Angle slain.</p>
