 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">An Arthurian Miscellany at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <center> <h2 align="CENTER"> A SONG THAT TROSTAN MADE </h2>  <h3 align="CENTER">by</h3>  <h2 align="CENTER"> ELLA YOUNG </h2>  </center>  <br>  <br>  If I were a king's son<br>  I would give you a white hound<br>  In a leash of silver,<br>  I would give you a white stallion<br>  From over the sea.<br>  I would give you a cloak of purple<br>  Wrought with findruiny,<br>  And shoes of white bronze,<br>  If I were a king's son.<br>  <br>  You would talk with me<br>  In the bright-coloured palace:<br>  You would be glad at my coming,<br>  If I were a king's son.<br>  <br>
