 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">An Arthurian Miscellany at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <center> <h2 align="CENTER">TRISTRAM AND ISOLT</h2>  <h3 align="CENTER">by</h3>  <h2 align="CENTER">MADISON CAWEIN</h2>  </center>  <br>  <br> Night, and vast caverns of rock and of iron:<br> Voices like water, and voices like wind:<br> Horror, and tempests of hail that environ<br> Shapes and the shadows of two who have sinned.<br> <br> Wan on the whirlwind, in loathing uplifting<br> Faces that loved once, forever they go,<br> Tristram and Isolt, the lovers, go drifting,<br> The simmer and laughter of Hell below.<br>
