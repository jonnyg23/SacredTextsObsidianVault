 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">An Arthurian Miscellany at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <center> <h2 align="CENTER"> QUEEN GUENNIVAR'S ROUND </h2>  <h3 align="CENTER">by</h3>  <h2 align="CENTER">ROBERT STEPHEN HAWKER </h2>  </center>  <br>                    I.<br>  Naid for Grecian waters!<br>     Nymph for the fountain-side!<br>  But old Cornwall's bounding daughters<br>     For gay Dundagel's tide.<br>  <br>                    II.<br>  The wild wind proudly gathers<br>     Round the ladies of the land;<br>  And the blue wave of their fathers<br>     Is joyful where they stand.<br>  <br>                    III.<br>  Naiad for Grecian waters!<br>     Nymph for the foutain-side!<br>  But old Cornwall's bounding daughters<br>     For gray Dundagel's tide.<br>  <br>                    IV.<br>  Yes! when memory rejoices<br>     In her long-belovèd theme,<br>  Fair forms and thrilling voices<br>     Will mingle with my dream.<br>  <br>                    V.<br>  Naiad for Grecian waters!<br>     Nymph for the foutain-side!<br>  But old Cornwall's bounding daughters<br>     For gray Dundagel's tide.
