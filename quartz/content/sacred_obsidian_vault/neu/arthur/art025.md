 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">An Arthurian Miscellany at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <center> <h2 align="CENTER">MERLIN'S PROPHECY</h2>  <h3 align="CENTER">by</h3>  <h2 align="CENTER">MELISSA<br>  [pseudonym of Jane Brereton]</h2>  </center>  <br>   <center> <i> Humbly inscrib'd to his</i>  R.H. <i> the</i>  PRINCE  <i> of</i>  WALES. </center> <br> <br>   ROYAL  <i> FREDERICK! Britain'</i> s Pride!<br>  <i> Prince</i> , for future Safety giv'n;<br>     For Thee 's decree'd a Virtuous <i> Bride,</i> <br>  Choicest <i> Gift</i>  of bounteous Heav'n.<br>     To reward thy filial Duty,<br>  To perpetuate <i> Brunswick'</i> s Race,<br>     Wit, and Learning, Youth, and Beauty,<br>  Heav'n prepares for thy Embrace.<br>  <br>     No blust'ring Storms, affright the Fair,<br>  No raging Billows, dare to rise;<br>     Safe by <i> Heav'n'</i> s, and <i> George'</i> s Care,<br>  May She bless our longing Eyes!<br>     <i> Neptune</i> , sooth old Father <i> Ocean</i> ,<br>  Mild <i> Favonius</i> , waft thy Gales;<br>     May <i> One</i>  repell each theat'ning Motion,<br>  For <i> Albion</i> , t' <i> other</i>  swell the Sails!<br>  <br>     Behold!  She comes, enrich'd with Charms,<br>  Indulgent to thy plighted Love!<br>     Receive the Blessing to thy Arms,<br>  And<i>  Hymen'</i> s hallow'd Rites approve.<br>     <i> Illustrious Pair!</i>  wou'd You in Story,<br>  Thro' succeeding Ages shine?<br>     Wou'd You tread the Paths to Glory?<br>  Follow <i> George</i> , and <i> Caroline</i> .<br>  <br>     And lo!  I see a <i> glorious Race,</i> <br>  Successive rising to Renown!<br>     Decree'd <i> Britannia</i> 's Throne to grace;<br>  And give new Lustre to a Crown.<br>     Ordain'd, to wield the Sceptre Royal,<br>  With righteous Pow'r, and gentle Sway;<br>     And rule o'er <i> Britons</i> , Brave, and Loyal,<br>  'Till Heavn, and Earth, shall melt away.
