 <h2 align="center">THE THREE WISHES.</h2>
 
 <p>T<small>HIS</small> well-known story appears in one of its commonest forms in the tale of “Loppi and Lappi” (Kreutzwald), a quarrelsome couple who are granted three wishes by a fairy. At supper-time the wife wishes for a sausage, which is wished on and off her nose, and the couple remain as poor as before.</p>
