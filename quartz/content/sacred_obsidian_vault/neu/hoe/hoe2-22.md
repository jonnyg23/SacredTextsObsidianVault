 <h1 align="center">SECTION IV</h1>
 
 <h3 align="center"><i>FAMILIAR STORIES OF NORTHERN EUROPE</i></h3>
 
 <p>U<small>NDER</small> this heading we include variants of well-known but not cosmopolitan tales, some of which are of considerable interest. Among them is a variant of “Melusina,” close in some points, but presenting many features of difference.</p>
