 <h1 align="CENTER">APPENDIX A.</h1>
 <p>The following are the Russian books to which I am principally indebted. In alluding to them in the foot-notes to the present volume I have frequently given only the initials of their titles, just as I have often represented the words <i>Deutsche Mythologie</i> by the letters D. M.</p>
 <p>AFANASIEF. <i>Poeticheskiya Vozzryeniya Slavyan na Prirodu</i>. [Poetic Views of the Slavonians about Nature.] 3 vols. Moscow, 1865-69. 8vo.</p>
 <p>---- <i>Narodnuiya Russkiya Skazki</i>. [Popular Russian Tales.] Third edition. 8 Pts. Moscow, 1863. 8vo.</p>
 <p>BEZSONOF. <i>Kalyeki Perekhozhie</i>. [Wandering Psalm-singers. A collection of their songs.] 6 pts. Moscow. 1860-62. 8vo.</p>
 <p>BUSLAEF. <i>Istoricheskie Ocherki</i>, etc. [Historical Sketches of National Literature and Art.] 2 vols. St. Petersburg, 1861. fol.</p>
 <p>---- <i>O Vliyanii Khristianstva na Slavyansky Yazuik</i>. [On the Influence of Christianity on Slavonic Language.] Moscow, 1848. 8vo.</p>
 <p>DAHL. <i>Poslovitsui Russkago Naroda</i>. [Proverbs of the Russian People.] Moscow, 1862. fol.</p>
 <p>ERLENBEIN. <i>Narodnuiya Skazki</i>. [Popular Tales collected by village Schoolmasters.] Moscow, 1863. 8vo.</p>
 <p>KASTORSKY. <i>Nachertanie Slovanskoi Mithologii</i>. [Outline of Slavonic Mythology.] St. Petersburg, 1841. 8vo.</p>
 <p>KAVELIN. <i>Sochineniya</i>. [Collected works.] 4 vols. Moscow, 1859.</p>
 <p>KHUDYAKOF. <i>Velikorusskiya Skazzki</i>. [Great-Russian Popular Tales.] 3 pts. Moscow, 1860-62.</p>
 <p><a name="page_438"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 438</font></a></p>
 <p>KIRYEEVSKY. <i>Pyesni sobrannuiya. P. V. Kiryeevskim, etc</i>. [Songs collected by P. V. Kiryeevsky. Edited by P. A. Bezsonof and others.] Second edition. Parts 1-8. Moscow, 1868, etc. 8vo.</p>
 <p>KOTLYAREVSKY. <i>O Pogrebal'nuikh Obuichayakh Yazuicheskikh Slavyan</i>. [On the Funeral Customs of the heathen Slavonians.] Moscow, 1868. 8vo.</p>
 <p>MAIKOF. <i>O Builinakh Vladimirova Tsikla</i>. [On the Builínas of the Vladímir Cycle.] St. Petersburg, 1863. 8vo.</p>
 <p>---- <i>Velikorusskiya Zaklinaniya</i>. [Great-Russian Spells.] St. Petersburg, 1869. 8vo.</p>
 <p>OREST MILLER. <i>Opuit Istoricheskago Obozryeniya Russkoi Slovesnosti</i>. [Attempt at an Historical Survey of Russian literature Part 1. Section 1. Second edition.] St. Petersburg, 1866. 8vo.</p>
 <p>---- <i>Khristomatiya, etc</i>. [Chrestomathy, appended to the "Attempt, etc."] Part 1. Section 1. Second edition. St. Petersburg, 1866. 8vo.</p>
 <p>---- <i>Ilya Muromets</i>. [Ilya of Murom. and the Heroes of Kief.] St . Petersburg, 1869. Royal 8vo.</p>
 <p>RUIBNIKOF, <i>Pyesni sobrannuiya P. N. Ruibnikovuim</i>. [Songs collected by P. N. Ruibnikof Edited by P. A. Bezsonof, etc.] 4 vols. Moscow, 1861-67. 8vo.</p>
 <p>SAKHAROF. <i>Skazaniya Russkago Naroda</i>. [Utterances of the Russian People. Third edition.] 2 vols. <a name="fr_338"></a><a href="#fn_338"><font size="1">1</font></a> St. Petersburg, 1841. Royal 8vo.</p>
 <p>---- <i>Pyesni Russkago Naroda</i>. [Songs of the Russian People]. 5 vols. St. Petersburg, 1838-39. 12mo.</p>
 <p>SCHOEPPIING (or Shepping). <i>Mithui Slavyanskago Yazuichestva</i>. [Myths of Slavonic Heathendom.] Moscow, 1849. 8vo.</p>
 <p>---- <i>Russkaya Narodnost'</i>, etc. [Russian Nationality in its Superstitions, Rites, and Popular Stories.] Moscow, 1862. 8vo.</p>
 <p>SHCHEPKIN. <i>Ob Istochnikakh i formakh Russkago Basnosloviya</i>. [On the Sources and Forms of Russian Mythology.] 2 pts. Moscow, 1859-61.</p>
 <p><a name="page_439"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 439</font></a></p>
 <p>SHEN. <i>Russkiya Narodnuiya Pyesni</i>. [Russian Popular Songs, collected and arranged by P. V. Shein.] Vol. I. Moscow, 1870. Royal 8vo.</p>
 <p>SNEGIREF. <i>Russkie Prostonarodnuie Prazdniki</i>, etc. [Russian Popular Festivals and Superstitious Rites.] 4 vols. Moscow, 1837-39. 8vo.</p>
 <p>SNEGIREF. <i>Russkie v svoikh Poslovitsakh</i>. [The Russians in their Proverbs.] 4 vols. Moscow, 1832. 12mo.</p>
 <p>SOLOVIEF. <i>Istoriya Rossii</i>. [History of Russia. <a name="fr_339"></a><a href="#fn_339"><font size="1">2</font></a>] Fourth edition. Moscow, 1856, etc. 8vo.</p>
 <p>TERESHCHENKO. <i>Buit Russkago Naroda</i>. [Manners and Customs of the Russian People.] 7 vols. St. Petersburg, 1848. 8vo. <a name="fr_340"></a><a href="#fn_340"><font size="1">3</font></a></p>

 <p>WOJCICKI. "Songs of the White-Croatians, Masures, etc." 2 vols. Warsaw, 1836. 8vo.</p>
 <p>KARAJIC. "Servian Popular Songs." 5 vols. Vienna, 1841-65. 8vo.</p>
 <p>SUSIL. "Moravian Popular Songs." Brunn, 1860. 8vo.</p>
 <p>HAUPT AND SCHMALER. "Folk-Songs of the Wends in Upper and Lower Lusatia " [in Wendish and German.] Grimma, 1841-43. 4to.</p>
 <p>ZEGOTA PAULI. "Songs of the Ruthenians in Galicia." 2 vols. Lwow, 1839-40. 12mo.</p>
