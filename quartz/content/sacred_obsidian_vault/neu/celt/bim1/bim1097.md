 <p><a name="page_222"><font size="1" color="green">p. 222</font></a> <a name="page_223"><font size="1" color="green">p. 223</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">THE CIRCLES. <a name="fr_235"></a><a href="#fn_234"><font size="1">1</font></a></h3>  
 <p>The Circle of Abred, in which are all corporal and dead existences.</p>  
 <p>The Circle of Gwynvyd, in which are all animated and immortal beings.</p>  
 <p>The Circle of Ceugant, where there is only God. The wise men describe them thus, in three Circles.</p>
