 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Barddas of Iolo Morganwg, Vol. I.</i>, ed. by J. Williams Ab Ithel, [1862], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">YEARS OF THE SUN AND MOON.</h3>  
 <p>The years of the sun and moon are as follows:--The year of the sun, 366 days. The year of the moon, 354 days.</p>  
 <p><a name="page_424"><font size="1" color="green">p. 424</font></a> <a name="page_425"><font size="1" color="green">p. 425</font></a></p>  
 <p>The year of memorial and computation, 364 days.</p>  
 <p>Twenty-nine years of the sun are thirty years of the moon.</p>  
 <p>In another place, thus:--</p>  
 <p>Thirty-one years of the moon make thirty years of the sun.</p>  
 <p align="right">From the Book of Brith y Coed.</p>
