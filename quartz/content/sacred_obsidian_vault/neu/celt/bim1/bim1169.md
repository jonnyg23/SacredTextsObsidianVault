 <p>The four parts of the day: morning, that is, the first six hours; vapourlessness, the second six hours; noon, the third six hours; and the fourth, evanescence, <a name="fr_421"></a><a href="#fn_417"><font size="1">1</font></a> reaching till the morning.</p>
