 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Barddas of Iolo Morganwg, Vol. I.</i>, ed. by J. Williams Ab Ithel, [1862], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">THE BARDIC SECRET.--FORMATION OF LETTERS.</h3>  
 <p>It is very remarkable that the Druidical secrets consisted in the use of the letters <a name="img_07700"></a>
 <img src="img/07700.jpg"> or <a name="img_07701"></a>
 <img src="img/07701.jpg">; anciently <a name="img_07702"></a>
 <img src="img/07702.jpg">, of the twelve letters <a name="fr_109"></a><a href="#fn_108"><font size="1">*</font></a>, <a name="img_07703"></a>
 <img src="img/07703.jpg">;or thus <a name="img_07704"></a>
 <img src="img/07704.jpg">, A. E. I. O, and where <a name="img_07705"></a>
 <img src="img/07705.jpg"> was made, it was cut fully across the Coelbren; or thus, <a name="img_07706"></a>
 <img src="img/07706.jpg">, which were five vowels. With them in the second age there were seven strongly vocal letters, namely <a name="img_07707"></a>
 <img src="img/07707.jpg"> or <a name="img_07708"></a>
 <img src="img/07708.jpg">. Instead of M there were two <a name="img_07709"></a>
 <img src="img/07709.jpg">.'s together, as <a name="img_07710"></a>
 <img src="img/07710.jpg">; and instead of P, <a name="img_07711"></a>
 <img src="img/07711.jpg"> upside down, thus <a name="img_07712"></a>
 <img src="img/07712.jpg">; and instead of Mh, two <a name="img_07713"></a>
 <img src="img/07713.jpg">'s, thus, <a name="img_07714"></a>
 <img src="img/07714.jpg">; and instead of F, <a name="img_07715"></a>
 <img src="img/07715.jpg">, and an aspirate thus <a name="img_07716"></a>
 <img src="img/07716.jpg">, and subsequently, thus <a name="img_07717"></a>
 <img src="img/07717.jpg">; and instead of <a name="img_07718"></a>
 <img src="img/07718.jpg">, <a name="img_07719"></a>
 <img src="img/07719.jpg"> fully across, thus, <a name="img_07720"></a>
 <img src="img/07720.jpg">. And <a name="img_07721"></a>
 <img src="img/07721.jpg"> A. E. I. O; and then <a name="img_07722"></a>
 <img src="img/07722.jpg"> A. E. I. O. W. Some persons maintain that there were only three vowels from the beginning, namely, <a name="img_07723"></a>
 <img src="img/07723.jpg">. O. I. W; after that, <a name="img_07724"></a>
 <img src="img/07724.jpg">; in other books, <a name="img_07725"></a>
 <img src="img/07725.jpg"> and <a name="img_07726"></a>
 <img src="img/07726.jpg"> making <a name="img_07727"></a>
 <img src="img/07727.jpg"> E.</p>
