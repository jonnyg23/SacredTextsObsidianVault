 <p><a name="page_94"><font size="1" color="green">p. 94</font></a> <a name="page_95"><font size="1" color="green">p. 95</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">THE BIRDS OF RHIANON <a name="fr_129"></a><a href="bim1049.htm#fn_128"><font size="1">1</font></a></h3>  
 <p>The Birds of Rhianon sang until the Angels of heaven came to listen to them; and it was from their songs that were first obtained vocal song and instrumental music; vocal song being that which is sung by the lips to melody and harp.</p>  
 <p align="right">Evan Evans.</p>
