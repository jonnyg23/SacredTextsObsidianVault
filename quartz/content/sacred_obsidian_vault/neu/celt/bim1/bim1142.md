 <p align="center">From the Book of Mr. Cobb of Cardiff.</p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">From the earth, the flesh;<br> From the water, the blood;<br> From the air, the breath;<br> From the calas, the bones;<br> From the salt, the feeling;<br> From the sun, that is, the fire, his agitation;<br> From the truth, his understanding;<br> From the Holy Ghost, that is, God, his soul,<br>   or life.</div>
