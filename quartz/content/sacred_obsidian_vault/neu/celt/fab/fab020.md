 <h3 align="CENTER">IV.</h3>
 <h3 align="CENTER">BOOK OF TALIESSIN XLI.</h3>
 <p align="CENTER"></p>
 <p>MADAWG, the joy of the wall,<br>
 Madawg, before he was in the grave,<br>
 Was a fortress of abundance<br>
 Of games, and society.<br>
 The son of Uthyr before he was slain,<br>
 From his hand he pledged thee.<br>
 Erof the cruel came,<br>
 Of impotent joy;<br>
 Of impotent sorrow.<br>
 10 Erof the cruel caused<br>
 Treacheries to Jesus.<br>
 Though he believed.<br>
 The earth quaking,<br>
 And the elements darkening,<br>
 And a shadow on the world,<br>
 And baptism trembling.<br>
 An impotent stop<br>
 Was taken by fierce Erof,<br>
 Going in the course of things<br>
 20 Among the hideous fiends<br>
 Even to the bottom of Uffern.</p>
