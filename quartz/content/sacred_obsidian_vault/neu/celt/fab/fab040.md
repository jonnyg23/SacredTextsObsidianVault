 <h2 align="CENTER">F.</h2>
 <h2 align="CENTER">POEMS RELATING TO CITIES OF THE CYMRY AND THEIR LEGENDARY HEROES.</h2>
 <h3 align="CENTER">XXIV.</h3>
 <h3 align="CENTER">BLACK BOOK OF CAERMARTHEN XV.</h3>
 <p align="CENTER"></p>
 <p>I. DINAS MAON, may God the blessed Sovereign defend it!<br>
 What the sun will dry, Edar will moisten.</p>
 <p>II. Dinas Maon, the dislike of Sovereigns, where kings were hewed down in the obstinate conflict.<br>
 What the sun will dry, Mervin will moisten.</p>
 <p>III. Dinas Maon, the security of the country, may the protection of God surround it!<br>
 What the sun will dry, Nynaw will moisten.</p>
 <p>IV. Mad put his thigh on Merchin the gray steed,<br>
 The fort of the brave will defend me.<br>
 What the sun will dry, Maelgwn will moisten.</p>
