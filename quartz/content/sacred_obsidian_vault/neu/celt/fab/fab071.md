 <h2 align="CENTER">M.</h2>
 <h2 align="CENTER">POEMS RELATING TO CADWALLAWN.</h2>
 <h3 align="CENTER">LVI.</h3>
 <h3 align="CENTER">BOOK OF TALIESSIN XLIX.</h3>
 <p align="CENTER"></p>
 <p>A BRIGHT festivity<br>
 About the two lakes,<br>
 The lake on my side.<br>
 The side about the Caer,<br>
 The Caer in urgency<br>
 Has been described.<br>
 A comely flight from it;<br>
 And the legion of the band<br>
 Augmented stones.<br>
 10 The dragon will flow around,<br>
 Above the places,<br>
 Vessels of liquor,<br>
 Liquor in golden horns,<br>
 Golden horns in hand,<br>
 Hand on the knife,<br>
 The knife on the rallying point.<br>
 Truly I implore thee,<br>
 Victorious Beli,<br>
 Son of Manogan, the king,<br>
 20 That will preserve the qualities<br>
 Of the honey isle of Beli,<br>
 He had a right to it.<br>
 Five chiefs there will be,<br>
 Of the Gwyddyl Ffichti, <a name="page_432"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 432</font></a><br>
 Of a sinner's disposition,<br>
 Of the race of the knife.<br>
 Five others there will be,<br>
 Of the Norddmyn's place,<br>
 The sixth a wonderful king,<br>
 30 From sowing to reaping.<br>
 The seventh proceeded<br>
 To land over the flood.<br>
 The eighth of the line of Dyvi.<br>
 Shall not be separated from prosperity,<br>
 Before the shout of Venni.<br>
 The calls of Eryri.<br>
 With difficulty thou wilt come.<br>
 Let us implore Eloi,<br>
 When we may be with Celi,<br>
 40 A dwelling of heaven will be to me.</p>
