 <h3 align="CENTER">LXXXIV.</h3>
 <h3 align="CENTER">BLACK BOOK OF CAERMARTHEN XXV.</h3>
 <p align="CENTER"></p>
 <p>AS long as we sojourn among excess and pride,<br>
 Let our work be perfect;<br>
 Let us seek deliverance through faith,<br>
 And religion and belief, as long as there is a belief in <a name="page_516"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 516</font></a><br>
 God through obtaining faith,<br>
 And by doing great penance daily,<br>
 Soul, why askest thou me<br>
 What my end, and will the grave be my portion?</p>
