 <p>TA mi liuthail   m’ aodainn<br>   An caora caon na greine,<br>   Mar a liuthail Moire Criosd<br>   Am bainne miamh na h-Eiphit.</p>   
 <p>Meilc bhi   ’na mo bhial,<br>   Ciall bhi ’na mo chainn,<br>   An gaol thug Moire mhin dha Mac<br>   Bhi an cridhe gach mire dhomhsa.</p>   
 <p>Gradh Chriosd   am chom,<br>   Cruth Chriosd am chomhnadh,<br>   Chan ’eil am muir no ’m fonn<br>   Na bheir buaidh air Righ an Domhnuich.</p>   
 <p>Bas Bhride mu   m’ mhuineal,<br>   Bas Mhuire mu m’ bhraghad,<br>   Bas Mhicheil dha m’ liuthail,<br>   Bas Chriosda dha m’ thearnadh.</p>   
 <p>Doigh eile--<br>     Bith a bhith ’na m’ bheul,<br>     Ceil a bhith ’na m’ chainn,<br>     Blas na sile ’na mo bhile<br>     Gon an till mi nall.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>I AM bathing my   face<br>   In the mild rays of the sun,<br>   As Mary bathed Christ<br>   In the rich milk of Egypt.</p>   
 <p>Sweetness be in   my mouth,<br>   Wisdom be in my speech,<br>   The love the fair Mary gave her Son<br>   Be in the heart of all flesh for me.</p>   
 <p>The love of   Christ in my breast,<br>   The form of Christ protecting me,<br>   There is not in sea nor on land<br>   That can overcome the King of the Lord's Day.</p>   
 <p>The hand of   Bride about my neck,<br>   The hand of Mary about my breast,<br>   The hand of Michael laving me,<br>   The hand of Christ saving me.</p>   
 <p>Variant--<br>     Force in my mouth,<br>     Sense be in my speech,<br>     The taste of nectar on my lips,<br>     Till I return hither.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>
