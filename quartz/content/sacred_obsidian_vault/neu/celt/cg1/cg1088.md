 <p>DI-LUAIN thig   an doireann trom,<br>   A shileas am bith eutrom,<br>   Bithidh sinn umhail gach greis,<br>        Gach uile na dh’ eisdeas.</p>   
 <p>Di-mairt thig   an t-sian eile,<br>   Cradh chridheach, cruaidh pheinneach,<br>   A shileas na gruaidheana glana,<br>        Frasa fala fiona.</p>   
 <p>Di-ciadain a   sheideas gaoth,<br>   Sguaba lom air shrath is raon,<br>   Dortadh oiteag barra theann,<br>        Beithir bheur ’s reubadh bheann.</p>   
 <p>Di-ardaoin a   shileas an cith,<br>   Chuireas daoine ’n an dalla ruith,<br>   Na ’s luaithe na ’n duil air an fhiodh,<br>        Mar bharr mhic-Muir air bhalla-chrith.</p>   
 <p>Di-haoine thig   an coinneal dubh,<br>   Is eitiche thainig fo’n t-saoghal;<br>   Fagar an sluagh braon am beachd,<br>        Fiar agus iasg fo’n aon leac.</p>   
 <p>Di-sathuirne   thig am muir mor,<br>   Ag iomairt air alt aibhne;<br>   Bithidh gach uile mar a shnodh<br>        Ag altachadh gu sliabh slighinn.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>ON Monday will   come the great storm<br>   Which the airy firmament will pour,<br>   We shall be obedient the while,<br>        All who will hearken.</p>   
 <p>On Tuesday will   come the other element,<br>   Heart paining, hard piercing,<br>   Wringing from pure pale cheeks<br>        Blood, like showers of wine.</p>   
 <p>On Wednesday   will blow the wind,<br>   Sweeping bare strath and plain,<br>   Showering gusts of galling grief,<br>        Thunder bursts and rending hills.</p>   
 <p>On Thursday   will pour the shower,<br>   Driving people into blind flight,<br>   Faster than the foliage on the trees,<br>        Like the leaves of Mary's plant in terror   trembling.</p>   
 <p>On Friday will   come the dool cloud of darkness,<br>   The direst dread that ever came over the world,<br>   Leaving multitudes bereft of reason,<br>        Grass and fish beneath the same flagstone.</p>   
 <p>On Saturday   will come the great sea,<br>   Rushing like a mighty river;<br>   All will be at their best<br>        Hastening to a hill of safety.</p>   </td>  </tr>  <tr>   <td valign="top" align="top">

 <p>Di-domhnaich a   dh’ eireas mo Righ,<br>   Lan feirge agus iminidh,<br>   Ag eisdeachd ri searbh ghloir gach fir,<br>        Crois dhearg air gach guala dheis.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>On Sunday will   arise my King,<br>   Full of ire and tribulation,<br>   Listening to the bitter talk of each man,<br>        A red cross on each right shoulder.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>
