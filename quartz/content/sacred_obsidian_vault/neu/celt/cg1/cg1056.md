 <p>O ’S tus   a Chriosd a cheannaich an t-anam--<br>   Ri linn dioladh na beatha,<br>   Ri linn bruchdadh na falluis,<br>   Ri linn iobar na creadha,<br>   Ri linn dortadh na fala,<br>   Ri linn cothrom na meidhe,<br>   Ri linn sgathadh na h-anal,<br>   Ri linn tabhar na breithe,<br>   Biodh a shith air do theannal fein;<br>   Iosa Criosda Mhic Moire mine,<br>   Biodh a shith air do theannal fein,<br>        O Ios! air do theannal fein.</p>   
 <p>Is bitheadh   Micheal geal caomh,<br>   Ard righ nan aingeal naomh,<br>   An cinnseal an anama ghaoil,<br>   Ga dhion dh’an Triu barra-chaon,<br>        O! dh’an Triu barra-chaon.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>SINCE Thou   Christ it was who didst buy the soul---<br>   At the time of yielding the life,<br>   At the time of pouring the sweat,<br>   At the time of offering the clay,<br>   At the time of shedding the blood,<br>   At the time of balancing the beam,<br>   At the time of severing the breath,<br>   At the time of delivering the judgment,<br>   Be its peace upon Thine own ingathering;<br>   Jesus Christ Son of gentle Mary,<br>   Be its peace upon Thine own ingathering,<br>        O Jesus! upon Thine own ingathering.</p>   
 <p>And may Michael   white kindly,<br>   High king of the holy angels,<br>   Take possession of the beloved soul,<br>   And shield it home to the Three of surpassing love,<br>        Oh! to the Three of surpassing love.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>
