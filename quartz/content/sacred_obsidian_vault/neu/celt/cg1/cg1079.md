 <p>A MHOIRE, a   mhathair nan naomh,<br>   Beannaich an t-al ’s an crodh-laoigh;<br>   Na leig fuath no foirne, ’n ar gaoith,<br>   Fuadaich oirnne doigh nan daoi.</p>   
 <p>Cum do shuil   gach Luan is Mart,<br>   Air crodh-laoigh's air aighean dair;<br>   Iomachair leinn o bheinn gu sal,<br>   Tionail fein an treud ’s an t-al.</p>   
 <p>Gach Ciadaon   agus Daorn bi leo,<br>   Biodh do lamh chaon a chaoidh ’n an coir;<br>   Cuallaich buar d’am buabhal bho,<br>   Cuallaich cuanal d’an cual chro.</p>   
 <p>Gach Aona bi-sa,   a Naoimh, ’n an ceann,<br>   Treoraich caoraich a aodann bheann,<br>   Le ’n al beag ba as an deigh,<br>   Cuartaich ’ad le cuartachd Dhe.</p>   
 <p>Gach Sathurna   bith leo mar chach,<br>   Tabhair gobhair a steach le ’n al,<br>   Gach meann is maos gu taobh sal,<br>   Is Lioc a h-Eigir gu h-ard,<br>   Le biolair uaine shuas m’a barr.</p>   
 <p>Treoir na   Trianailt d’ ar dian ’s gach cas,<br>   Treoir Chriosda le shith ’s le Phais,<br>   Treoir an Spioraid, Ligh na slaint,<br>   Is Athar priseil, Righ nan gras.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>MARY, thou   mother of saints,<br>   Bless our flocks and bearing kine;<br>   Hate nor scath let not come near us,<br>   Drive from us the ways of the wicked.</p>   
 <p>Keep thine eye   every Monday and Tuesday<br>   On the bearing kine and the pairing queys;<br>   Accompany us from hill to sea,<br>   Gather thyself the sheep and their progeny.</p>   
 <p>Every Wednesday   and Thursday be with them,<br>   Be thy gracious hand always about them;<br>   Tend the cows down to their stalls,<br>   Tend the sheep down to their folds!</p>   
 <p>Every Friday be   thou, O Saint, at their head,<br>   Lead the sheep from the face of the bens,<br>   With their innocent little lambs following them,<br>   Encompass them with God's encompassing.</p>   
 <p>Every Saturday   be likewise with them,<br>   Bring the goats in with their young,<br>   Every kid and goat to the sea side,<br>   And from the Rock of Aegir on high,<br>   With tresses green about its summit.</p>   
 <p>The strength of   the Triune be our shield in distress,<br>   The strength of Christ, His peace and His Pasch,<br>   The strength of the Spirit, Physician of health,<br>   And of the precious Father, the King of grace.</p>   </td>  </tr>  <tr>   <td valign="top" align="top">

 <p>*       *       *       *<br>   ’S gach naomh eile bha nan deigh<br>   ’S a choisinn suamhnas rioghachd De.</p>   
 <p>Beannaich sinn   fein agus ar cloinn,<br>   Beannaich gach creubh a thig o’r loinn,<br>   Beannaich am fear sin air an sloinn,<br>   Beannaich a Dhe, an te a rug o’n bhroinn.</p>   
 <p>Gach naomhachd,   beannachd agus buaidh,<br>   Bhi ’g aomadh leinn gach am ’s gach uair,<br>   An ainm Trithinn Naomha shuas,<br>   Athar, Mic, is Spiorad buan.</p>   
 <p>Crois Chriosd   bhi d’ ar dion a nuas,<br>   Crois Chriosd bhi d’ ar dion a suas,<br>   <a href="errata.htm#2">Crois</a> Chriosd bhi d’ ar dion mu   ’r cuart,<br>   Gabhail beannachd Bealltain uainn,<br>         Gabhail beannachd Bealltain uainn.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>*       *       *       *<br>   And of every other saint who succeeded them<br>   And who earned the repose of the kingdom of God.</p>   
 <p>Bless ourselves   and our children,<br>   Bless every one who shall come from our loins,<br>   Bless him whose name we bear,<br>   Bless, O God, her from whose womb we came.</p>   
 <p>Every holiness,   blessing and power,<br>   Be yielded to us every time and every hour,<br>   In name of the Holy Threefold above,<br>   Father, Son, and Spirit everlasting.</p>   
 <p>Be the Cross of   Christ to shield us downward,<br>   Be the Cross of Christ to shield us upward,<br>   Be the Cross of Christ to shield us roundward,<br>   Accepting our Beltane blessing from us,<br>         Accepting our Beltane blessing from us.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>
