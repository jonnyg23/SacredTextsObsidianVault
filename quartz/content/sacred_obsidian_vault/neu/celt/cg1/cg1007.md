 <p>DHE, eisd ri   m’ urnuigh,<br>   Lub rium do chluas,<br>   Leig m’ achan agus m’ urnuigh.<br>   T' ionnsuidh a suas.<br>   Thig, a Righ na glorach<br>   Da m’ chomhnadh a nuas,<br>   A Righ na bith ’s na trocair,<br>   Le comhnadh an Uain,<br>   A Mhic na Muire Oighe<br>   Da m’ chomhnadh le buadh,<br>   A Mhic na Muire mine<br>   Is finne-ghile snuadh.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>GOD, listen to   my prayer,<br>   Bend to me Thine ear,<br>   Let my supplications and my prayers<br>   Ascend to Thee upwards,<br>   Come, Thou King of Glory,<br>   To protect me down,<br>   Thou King of life and mercy<br>   With the aid of the Lamb.<br>   Thou Son of Mary Virgin<br>   To protect me with power,<br>   Thou Son of the lovely Mary<br>   Of purest fairest beauty.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>
