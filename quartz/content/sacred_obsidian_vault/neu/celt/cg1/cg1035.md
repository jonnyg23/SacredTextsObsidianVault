 <p>AN ainm an   Tighearn Iosa,<br>   Agus Spiorad ìocshlain aigh,<br>   An ainm Athar Israil,<br>       Sinim sios gu tamh.</p>   
 <p>Ma tha musal na   dusal,<br>   Na run air bith dhomh ’n dan,<br>   Dhia fuasgail orm is cuartaich orm,<br>       Is fuadaich uam mo namh.</p>   
 <p>An ainm Athar   priseil,<br>   Is Spiorad iocshlain aigh,<br>   An ainm Tighearn Iosa,<br>       Sinim sios gu tamh.<br>   *         *         *         *<br>   Dhia, cobhair mi is cuartaich mi,<br>       O ’n uair ’s gu uair mo bhais.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>IN name of the   Lord Jesus,<br>   And of the Spirit of healing balm,<br>   In name of the Father of Israel,<br>       I lay me down to rest.</p>   
 <p>If there be   evil threat or quirk,<br>   Or covert act intent on me,<br>   God free me and encompass me,<br>       And drive from me mine enemy.</p>   
 <p>In name of the   Father precious,<br>   And of the Spirit of healing balm,<br>   In name of the Lord Jesus,<br>       I lay me down to rest.<br>   *         *         *         *<br>   God, help me and encompass me,<br>       From this hour till the hour of my death.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>
