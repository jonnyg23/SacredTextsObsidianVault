 <p>NIST o thaine   sinn dh’ an duthaich,<br>   Dh’ urachadh dhuibh na Callaig,<br>   Cha ruig uine dhuinn bhi ’g innse,<br>           Bha i ann ri linn ar seanar.</p>   
 <p>A direadh ri tobht   an taighe,<br>   A teurnadh aig an dorus,<br>   Mo dhuan a ghabhail modhail,<br>           Mar b’ eol domh aig a   Challaig.</p>   
 <p>Caisein Callaig   ’na mo phocaid,<br>   Is mor an ceo thig as an fhear ud,<br>   Chan ’eil aon a gheobh de aile,<br>           Nach bi gu brath de fallain.</p>   
 <p>Gheobh fear an   taighe ’na dhorn e,<br>   Cuiridh e shron anns an teallach;<br>   Theid e deiseil air na paisdean,<br>           Is seachd araid bean an   taighe.</p>   
 <p>Gheobh a bhean   e, is i ’s t-fhiach e,<br>   Lamh a riarachadh na Callaig,<br>   Lamh a bhairig cais is im duinn,<br>           Lamh gun spiocaireachd, gun   ghainne.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>Now since we   came to the country<br>   To renew to you the Hogmanay,<br>   Time will not allow us to explain,<br>           It has been since the age of   our fathers.</p>   
 <p>Ascending the   wall of the house,<br>   Descending at the door,<br>   My carol to say modestly,<br>           As becomes me at the   Hogmanay.</p>   
 <p>The Hogmanay   skin is in my pocket,<br>   Great the fume that will come from that;<br>   No one who shall inhale its odour,<br>           But shall be for ever from it   healthy.</p>   
 <p>The house-man   will get it in his grasp,<br>   He will put its point in the fire;<br>   He will go sunwise round the children,<br>           And very specially round the   goodwife.</p>   
 <p>The wife will   get it, she it is who deserves it,<br>   The hand to distribute the Hogmanay,<br>   The hand to bestow upon us cheese and butter,<br>           The hand without   niggardliness, without meanness.</p>   </td>  </tr>  <tr>   <td valign="top" align="top">

 <p>Bho ’n ta   tart air tighinn an duthaich,<br>   Is nach bi duil againn ri annas,<br>   Rud beag a shugh an t-samhraidh,<br>           B’ annsa leinn e leis   an aran.</p>   
 <p>Mur bheil sin   againn ri fhaotainn,<br>   Ma dh’ fhaodas tu, na cum maill oirnn;<br>   Mise gille Mhic De air Chollaig,<br>           Eirich fein is fosgail dorus,<br>                Callain   seo! Callain seo!</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>Since drought   has come upon the land,<br>   And that we do not expect rarity,<br>   A little of the substance of the summer,<br>           Would we desire with the   bread.</p>   
 <p>If that we are   not to have it,<br>   If thou mayest, do not detain us;<br>   I am the servant of God's Son on Hogmanay,<br>           Arise thyself and open the   door.<br>                Hogmanay   here! Hogmanay here!</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>
