 <p>A RIGH na gile,<br>   A Righ na greine,<br>   A Righ na rinne,<br>   A Righ na reula,<br>   A Righ na cruinne,<br>   A Righ na speura,<br>   Is aluinn do ghnuis,<br>   A lub eibhinn.</p>   
 <p>Da lub shioda<br>   Shios ri d’ leasraich<br>   Mhinich, chraicich;<br>   Usgannan buidhe<br>   Agus dolach<br>   As gach sath dhiubh</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>THOU King of   the moon,<br>   Thou King of the sun,<br>   Thou King of the planets,<br>   Thou King of the stars,<br>   Thou King of the globe,<br>   Thou King of the sky,<br>   Oh! lovely Thy countenance,<br>   Thou beauteous Beam.</p>   
 <p>Two loops of   silk<br>   Down by thy limbs,<br>   Smooth-skinned;<br>   Yellow jewels<br>   And a handful<br>   Out of every stock of them.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>
