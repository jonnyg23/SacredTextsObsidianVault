 <p>SPIORAID,   tiubhair dhomh do phailteas,<br>   Athair, tiubhair dhomh do ghliocas,<br>   Mhic, tiubhair dhomh na m’ airceas,<br>        Iosa fo fhasga do sgeith.</p>   
 <p>Laigheam sios a   nochd,<br>   Le Trithinn mo neart,<br>   Le Athair, le Iosa,<br>        Le Spiorad nam feart.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>SPIRIT, give me   of Thine abundance,<br>   Father, give me of Thy wisdom,<br>   Son, give me in my need,<br>        Jesus beneath the shelter of Thy shield.</p>   
 <p>I lie down   to-night,<br>   With the Triune of my strength,<br>   With the Father, with Jesus,<br>        With the Spirit of might.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>
