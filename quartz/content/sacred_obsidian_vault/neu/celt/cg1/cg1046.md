 <p>DHE, teasruig   an tigh, an teine, ’s an tan,<br>   Gach aon ta gabhail tamh an seo an nochd.<br>   Teasruig mi fein ’s mo chroilean graidh,<br>   Is gleidh sinn bho lamh ’s bho lochd;<br>   Gleidh sinn bho namh an nochd,<br>   Air sgath Mhic Mhuire Mhathar<br>   ’S an ait-s ’s gach ait a bheil an tamh an nochd,<br>   Air an oidhche nochd ’s gach aon oidhche,<br>         An oidhche nochd ’s gach aon   oidhche.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>GOD shield the   house, the fire, the kine,<br>   Every one who dwells herein to-night.<br>   Shield myself and my beloved group,<br>   Preserve us from violence and from harm;<br>   Preserve us from foes this night,<br>   For the sake of the Son of the Mary Mother,<br>   In this place, and in every place wherein they dwell to-night,<br>   On this night and on every night,<br>         This night and every night.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>
