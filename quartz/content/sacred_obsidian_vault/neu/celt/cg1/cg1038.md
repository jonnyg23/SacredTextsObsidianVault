 <p>THA mis a nis a   dol dh’ an chadal,<br>   Gu mu slan a dhuisgeas mi;<br>   Ma ’s a bas domh anns a bhas chadail,<br>   Gun ann air do ghairdean fein<br>   A Dhe nan gras a dhuisgeas mi;<br>       O air do ghairdean gradhach fein,<br>       A Dhe nan gras a dhuisgeas mi!</p>   
 <p>M’ anam   air do laimh dheis, a Dhe,<br>   A Re nan neamha neomh;<br>   Is tu fein a cheannaich mi le t’fhuil,<br>   Is tu thug do bheatha air mo shon,<br>       Comraig mis an nochd, a Dhe,<br>       Is na h-eireadh dhomh beud no cron.</p>   
 <p>Am feadh bhios   a cholann a tamh ’s a chadal,<br>   Biodh an t-anam a snamh an sgath nam flathas,<br>   Micheal cra-gheal an dail an anama,<br>       Moch agus amnoch, oidhche agus latha,<br>       Moch agus anmoch, oidhche agus latha.<br>                                               Amen.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>I AM now going   into the sleep,<br>   Be it that I in health shall waken;<br>   If death be to me in the death-sleep,<br>   Be it that on Thine own arm,<br>   O God of Grace, I in peace shall waken;<br>       Be it on Thine own beloved arm,<br>       O God of Grace, that I in peace shall waken.</p>   
 <p>Be my soul on   Thy right hand, O God,<br>   Thou King of the heaven of heavens;<br>   Thou it was who bought'st me with Thy blood,<br>   Thou it was who gavest Thy life for me,<br>       Encompass Thou me this night, O God,<br>       That no harm, no evil shall me befall.</p>   
 <p>Whilst the body   is dwelling in the sleep,<br>   The soul is soaring in the shadow of heaven,<br>   Be the red-white Michael meeting the soul,<br>       Early and late, night and day,<br>       Early and late, night and day.<br>                                               Amen.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>
