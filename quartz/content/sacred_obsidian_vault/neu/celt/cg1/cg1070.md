 <p>THAINE sinne   chon an doruis,<br>   Feuch am feairrde sinn an turas,<br>   Dh’ innis a mhnathan coir a bhaile,<br>   Gur e maireach La Cullaig.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>WE are come to   the door,<br>   To see if we be the better of our visit,<br>   To tell the generous women of the townland<br>   That to-morrow is Calendae Day.</p>   </td>  </tr>  <tr>   <td valign="top" colspan="3" align="top">   
 <p><small>After being   entertained the guisers go sunwise <a name="page_157"><font size="1" color="green">p. 157</font></a> round the fire singing--</small></p>   </td>  </tr>  <tr>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>Gum   beannaicheadh Dia an t-ardrach,<br>   Eadar chlach, is chuaille, is chrann,<br>   Eadar bhithe, bhliochd, is aodach,<br>   Slainte dhaoin bhi daonnan ann,</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>May God bless   the dwelling,<br>   Each stone, and beam, and stave,<br>   All food, and drink, and clothing,<br>   May health of men he always there.</p>   </td>  </tr>  <tr>   <td valign="top" colspan="3" align="top">   
 <p><small>Should the   guisers be inhospitably treated, they file round the fire withershins and   walk out, and raise a cairn in or near the door, called 'carnan mollachd,'   cairn of malison, 'carnan cronachd,' scaith cairn. <a name="page_157"><font size="1" color="green">p. 157</font></a></small></p>   
 <p>They tramp   loudly, shaking the dust of the place off their feet, and intoning with a   deep voice the following and other maledictions:--</p>   </td>  </tr>  <tr>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>Mallachd Dhe is   Challaig oirbh,<br>   ’S cronachd chlaimhein chiuchaich,<br>   Fioinn, fithich agus fiolair,<br>   ’S cronachd sionnaich liugaich.</p>   
 <p>Cronachd chon   is chat oirbh,<br>   Thorc is bhroc is bhrugha,<br>   Mhaghain mais ’s mhadaidh-alla,<br>   ’S cronachd thaghain tutaidh.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>The malison of   God and of Hogmanay be on you,<br>   And the scath of the plaintive buzzard,<br>   Of the hen-harrier, of the raven, of the eagle,<br>   And the scath of the sneaking fox.</p>   
 <p>The scath of   the dog and of the cat be on you,<br>   Of the boar, of the badger, and of the 'brugha,'<br>   Of the hipped bear and of the wild wolf,<br>   And the scath of the foul foumart.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>
