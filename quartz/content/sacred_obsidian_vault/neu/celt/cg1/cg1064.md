 <p>IS mise Bannag,   is mise Bochd,<br>   Is mise Fear na h-oidhche nochd.</p>   
 <p>Is mise Mac De   anns an dorus,<br>   Di-luain air thuaradh nam bannag.</p>   
 <p>Is uasal Bride   mhin-gheal air a glun,<br>   Is uasal High nan dul ’na h-uchd.</p>   
 <p>Mac na gile,   Mac na greine,<br>   Mac Moire mor na De-meine,</p>   
 <p>Crois air gach   guala dheis,<br>   Mis is dorus, fosgail thusa.</p>   
 <p>Is leir   ’omh tulach, is leir ’omh traigh,<br>   Is leir ’omh ainghlean tighinn air snamh.</p>   
 <p>Is leir   ’omh calaman, cuimir, caon,<br>   Tighinn le caomh is cairdeas duinn.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>I AM the Gift, I   am the Poor,<br>   I am the Man of this night.</p>   
 <p>I am the Son of   God in the door,<br>   On Monday seeking the gifts.</p>   
 <p>Noble is Bride   the gentle fair on her knee,<br>   Noble the King of glory on her breast.</p>   
 <p>Son of the   moon, Son of the sung<br>   Great Son of Mary of God-like mind.</p>   
 <p>A cross on each   right shoulder,<br>   I am in the door, open thou.</p>   
 <p>I see the   hills, I see the strand,<br>   I see angels heralding on high.</p>   
 <p>I see the dove   shapely, benign,<br>   Coming with kindness and friendship to us.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>
