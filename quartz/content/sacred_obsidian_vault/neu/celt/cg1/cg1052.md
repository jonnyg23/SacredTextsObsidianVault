 <p>IOSA Mhic   Mhoire eighim air th’ ainm,<br>   Is air ainm Eoin ostail ghradhaich,<br>   Is air ainm gach naoimh ’s an domhan dearg,<br>   Mo thearmad ’s a chath nach tainig,<br>        Mo thearmad ’s a chath nach tainig.</p>   
 <p>Duair theid am   beul a dhunadh,<br>   Duair theid an t-suil a dhruideadh,<br>   Duair sguireas an anail da struladh,<br>   Duair sguireas an cridhe da bhuille,<br>        Sguireas an cridhe de bhuille.</p>   
 <p>Duair theid am   Breitheamh dh’ an chathair,<br>   Is a theid an tagradh a shuidheach,<br>   Iosa Mhic Mhoire cobhair air m’ anam,<br>   A Mhicheil mhin gobh ri mo shiubhal.<br>        Iosa Mhic Mhoire cobhair air m’ anam!<br>        A Mhicheil mhin gobh ri mo shiubhal!</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>JESUS, Thou Son   of Mary, I call on Thy name,<br>   And on the name of John the apostle beloved,<br>   And on the names of all the saints in the red domain,<br>   To shield me in the battle to come,<br>        To shield me in the battle to come.</p>   
 <p>When the mouth   shall be closed,<br>   When the eye shall be shut,<br>   When the breath shall cease to rattle,<br>   When the heart shall cease to throb,<br>        When the heart shall cease to throb.</p>   
 <p>When the Judge   shall take the throne,<br>   And when the cause is fully pleaded,<br>   O Jesu, Son of Mary, shield Thou my soul,<br>   O Michael fair, acknowledge my departure.<br>        O Jesu, Son of Mary, shield Thou my soul!<br>        O Michael fair, receive my departure!</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>
