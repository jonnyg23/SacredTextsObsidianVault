 <p>THIGEAM an   diugh ’an t-Athair,<br>   Thigeam an diugh ’an Mhac,<br>   Thigeam ’an Spiorad neartor naomh;<br>   Thigeam an diugh le Dia,<br>   Thigeam an diugh le Criosd,<br>   Thigeam le Spiorad iocshlaint chaomh.</p>   
 <p>Dia, agus   Spiorad, agus Ios,<br>   Bho mhullach mo chinn,<br>   Gu iochdar mo bhonn;<br>   Thigeam le mo chliu,<br>   Falbham le mo theasd,<br>   Thigeam thugad, Iosa--<br>        Iosa, dean mo leasd.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>COME I this day   to the Father,<br>   Come I this day to the Son,<br>   Come I to the Holy Spirit powerful;<br>   Come I this day with God,<br>   Come I this day with Christ,<br>   Come I with the Spirit of kindly balm,</p>   
 <p>God, and   Spirit, and Jesus,<br>   From the crown of my head<br>   To the soles of my feet;<br>   Come I with my reputation,<br>   Come I with my testimony,<br>   Come I to Thee, Jesu--<br>        Jesu, shelter me.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>
