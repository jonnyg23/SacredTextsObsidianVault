 <p>FAILT, a   Mhoire! failt, a Mhoire!<br>         Righinn nan gras, Mathair na trocair;<br>   Failt, a Mhoire, air mhodh gun choimeas,<br>         Geil ar slainte, fath ar solais.</p>   
 <p>Riut tha sinne,   dh’ oidhch ’s a latha,<br>         Sliochd seachranach Adhamh is Eubha,<br>   Togail ar guth ’s ag achan,<br>         An gul ’s an gal ’s an deura.</p>   
 <p>Tabhair duinn,   a Fhreimh an aigh,<br>         O ’s tu copan nan grasa fial,<br>   Creid Eoin, is Pheaid, is Phail,<br>         Le sgeith Airil an aird nan nial.</p>   
 <p>Deoin dhuinn, a   gheug dhonn,<br>         Aros ann am Fonn na sith,<br>   Tamh o ghabhadh ’s o anradh thonn,<br>         Fo sgath toraidh do bhronn, Ios.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>HAIL, Mary!   hail, Mary!<br>         Queen of grace, Mother of mercy;<br>   Hail, Mary, in manner surpassing,<br>         Fount of our health, source of our joy.</p>   
 <p>To thee we,   night and day,<br>         Erring children of Adam and Eve,<br>   Lift our voice in supplication,<br>         In groans and grief and tears.</p>   
 <p>Bestow upon us,   thou Root of gladness,<br>         Since thou art the cup of generous   graces,<br>   The faith of John, and Peter, and Paul,<br>         With the wings of Ariel on the heights of   the clouds.</p>   
 <p>Vouchsafe to us,   thou golden branch,<br>         A mansion in the Realm of peace,<br>   Rest from the perils and stress of waves,<br>         Beneath the shade of the fruit of thy   womb, Jesu.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>
