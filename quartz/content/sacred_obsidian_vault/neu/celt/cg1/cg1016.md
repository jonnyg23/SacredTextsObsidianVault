 <p>BHO is tu is   Buachiaill thar an treuid<br>   Iomain fein shin do chleidh ’s do chaimir,<br>   Seun sinn fo do bhrot riomhach reidh;<br>         A Sgeith dhidinn, dion ri ’r   mairionn.</p>   
 <p>Bi-sa do   chlaidheamh cruaidh, cosgarra,<br>   Chon sinne dhion a irinn arrais,<br>   Bho fhigeirich is bho fheadaine frinne fuara,<br>         ’S bho dheathach ruadh an aigeil.</p>   
 <p>M’ anam   an urrachd an Ard Righ,<br>   Micheil murrach an comhdhail m’ anama.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>As Thou art the   Shepherd over the flock<br>   Tend Thou us to the cot and the fold,<br>   Sain us beneath Thine own glorious mantle;<br>         Thou Shield of protection, guard us for   ever,</p>   
 <p>Be Thou a hard   triumphant slave<br>   To shield us securely from wicked hell,<br>   From the fiends and from the stieve snell gullies,<br>         And from the lurid smoke of the abyss.</p>   
 <p>Be my soul in   the trustance of the High King,<br>   Be Michael the powerful meeting my soul.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>
