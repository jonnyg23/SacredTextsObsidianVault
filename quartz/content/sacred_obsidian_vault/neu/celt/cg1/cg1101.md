 <p>OIDHCH Inid<br>   Bi feoil againn,<br>   ’S bu choir ’uinn sin<br>   Bu choir ’uinn sin.</p>   
 <p>Leth-cheann   circe,<br>   ’S da ghreim eorna,<br>   ’S bu leoir ’uinn sin<br>   Bu leoir ’uinn sin.</p>   
 <p>Bi bin againn,<br>   Bi beoir againn,<br>   Bi fion againn,<br>   Bi roic againn.<br>   Meilc is marrum,<br>   Mil is bainne,<br>   Sile fallain,<br>   Meall dheth sin,<br>   Meall dheth sin.</p>   
 <p>Bi cruit   againn,<br>   Bi clar againn,<br>   Bi dus againn,<br>   Bi das againn;<br>   Bi saltair ghrinn,<br>   Nan teuda binn,<br>   ’S hi fairchil, righ’nn<br>   Nan dan againn,<br>   Nan dan againn.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>ON Ash Eve<br>   We shall have flesh,<br>   We should have that<br>   We should have that.</p>   
 <p>The cheek of   hen,<br>   Two bits of barley,<br>   That were enough<br>   That were enough.</p>   
 <p>We shall have   mead,<br>   We shall have spruce,<br>   We shall have wine,<br>   We shall have feast.<br>   We shall have sweetness and milk produce,<br>   Honey and milk,<br>   Wholesome ambrosia,<br>   Abundance of that,<br>   Abundance of that.</p>   
 <p>We shall have   harp, (small?)<br>   We shall have harp, (pedal?)<br>   We shall have lute,<br>   We shall have horn.<br>   We shall have sweet psaltery<br>   Of the melodious strings<br>   And the regal lyre,<br>   Of the songs we shall have,<br>   Of the songs we shall have.</p>   </td>  </tr>  <tr>   <td valign="top" align="top">
