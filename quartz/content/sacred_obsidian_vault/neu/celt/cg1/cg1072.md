 <p>CRIOSDA   Cleireach os ar cionn,<br>   Dh’ orduich Ti nan dul do gach dull a t’ann.<br>           Criosda Cleireach os ar   cionn.</p>   
 <p>Nochd oidhch a   chrochaidh chruaidh,<br>   Crann cruaidh ris na chrochadh Criosd.<br>           Criosda Cleireach os ar   cionn.</p>   
 <p>Is uasal   Bannag, is uasal Bochd,<br>   Is uasal Fear na h-oidhche nochd.<br>           Criosda Cleireach os ar   cionn.</p>   
 <p>Is i Bride mhin   chaidh air a glun,<br>   Is e Righ nan dul a ta ’na h-uchd,<br>           Criosda Cleireach os ar   cionn.</p>   
 <p>Chluinn mi   tulach, chluinn mi traigh,<br>   Chluinn mi ainghlean air an t-snamh,<br>           Criosda Cleireach os ar   cionn.</p>   
 <p>Chluinn mi   Cairbre cuimir, cruinn,<br>   Tighinn cluimh le cairdeas duinn.<br>           Criosda Cleireach os ar   cionn.</p>   
 <p>Is ioma   tionailt air an tulaich,<br>   Gun farmad duine ri cheile.<br>           Criosda Cleireach os ar   cionn.</p>   
 <p>Is mise gille   Mic De is an dorus,<br>   Eirich fein is fosgail domh e.<br>           Criosda Cleireach os ar   cionn.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>CHRIST the   Priest above us,<br>   Ordained of God for all living.<br>           Christ the Priest above us.</p>   
 <p>To-night, the   night of the cross of agony,<br>   The cross of anguish to which Christ was crucified.<br>           Christ the Priest above us.</p>   
 <p>Noble the Gift!   noble the Poor!<br>   Noble the Man of this night.<br>           Christ the Priest above us.</p>   
 <p>It was Bride   the fair who went on her knee,<br>   It is the King of glory who is in her lap.<br>           Christ the Priest above us.</p>   
 <p>I hear the   hills, I hear the seas,<br>   I hear the angels heralding to earth<br>           Christ the Priest above us.</p>   
 <p>I hear Cairbre   of the shapely, rounded limbs,<br>   Coming softly in friendship to us.<br>           Christ the Priest above us.</p>   
 <p>Great the   assemblage upon this knoll,<br>   Without the envy of man to another.<br>           Christ the Priest above us.</p>   
 <p>I am servant of   God the Son at the door,<br>   Oh! arise thou thyself and open to me.<br>           Christ the Priest above us.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>
