 <p align="CENTER">VILAS</p>
 <blockquote> </blockquote><dir>
 
 <blockquote>CHERRY! dearest Cherry!<br>
 Higher lift thy branches,<br>
 Under which the Vilas<br>
 Dance their magic roundels.<br>
 Them before Radisha<br>
 Dew from flowers, lashes,<br>
 Leadeth on two Vilas,<br>
 To the third he sayeth--<br>
 "Be thou mine, O Vila!<br>
 Thou shalt, with my mother,<br>
 In the cool shade seat thee;<br>
 Soft silk deftly spinning<br>
 From the golden distaff." [a]</blockquote>
 <blockquote> </blockquote></dir>
 
 <p></p><hr>
 <blockquote>[a] We have made this translation from a German version in the Wiener Jahrbucher, vol. xxx. which is evidently more faithful than Bowring's.</blockquote>
