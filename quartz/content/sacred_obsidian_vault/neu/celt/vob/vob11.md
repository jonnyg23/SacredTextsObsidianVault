   <h3 align="center">VIII</h3>  <h3 align="center">Irische Texte iii. p. 87.</h3>  
 <p></p><dir>[5] 'I m-Bendchur<br> atá Mongán mac Fíachna:<br> is le[is] atá Conchobur<br> ar grafaind scáilte scíathcba.'  
 <p>'In Bangor<br> [10] Is Mongán son of Fiachna:<br> With him is Conchobur<br> At the contest of shield-splitting.'</p></dir>  
 <p>Quoted as an example of the metre called <i>ae freslíge becc</i>. <i>Is leis</i> is Stokes' conjecture for isle of the isle of the MS.</p>
