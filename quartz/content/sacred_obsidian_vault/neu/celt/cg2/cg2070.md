 <p>BHA Peadail is   Pol a dol seachad,<br>   Is eilid ’s an ro a cur laoigh;<br>   'Tha eilid a breith,' osa Peadail;<br>   'Chi mi gu bheil,' osa Pol.</p>   
 <p>'Mar a thuiteas   a duille bho ’n chraoibh,<br>   Gun ann a thuiteadh a seile gu lar,<br>   An ainm Athar an aigh agus Mhic an aoibh,<br>   Agus Spiorad a ghliocais ghraidh;<br>        Athar an aigh agus Mhic an aoibh,<br>        Agus Spioraid a ghliocais ghraidh.'</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>PETER and Paul   were passing by,<br>   While a hind in the path was bearing a fawn;<br>   'A hind is bearing there,' said Peter;<br>   'I see it is so,' said Paul.</p>   
 <p>'As her foliage   falls from the tree,<br>   So may her placenta fall to the ground,<br>   In name of the Father of love and of the Son of grace,<br>   And of the Spirit of loving wisdom;<br>        Father of love and Son of grace,<br>        And Spirit of loving wisdom.'</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>
