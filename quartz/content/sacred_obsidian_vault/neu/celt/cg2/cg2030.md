 <p>SALTRUIGHIDH   mis air an t-suil,<br>   Mar a shaltruigheas eal air tigh nocht,    [traigh?<br>   Ta neart gaoithe agam air,<br>   Ta neart greine agam air,<br>   Ta neart Mhic Righ Neamh<br>   Agus talmhainn agam air,<br>   Trian air na clacha glasa,</p>   
 <p>  *       *       *       *<br>   ’S trian air a mhuir mhoir,<br>   Is i fein acfhuinn is fearr ’g a ghiulan.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>TRAMPLE I upon   the eye,<br>   As tramples the swan on a bare house,    [strand?<br>   Power of wind I have over it,<br>   The power of the Son of the King of Heaven<br>   And of earth I have over it,<br>   A portion of it on the grey   stones,         [third</p>   
 <p>  *       *       *       *<br>   And a portion on the great sea,<br>   She herself is the instrument most able to bear it.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>
