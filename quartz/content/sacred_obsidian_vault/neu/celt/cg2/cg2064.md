 <p>TA mis a nis   air leirg,<br>   Traogh’ fraoich is feirg,<br>   Deanamh eolas a bhun deirg,<br>        Dh’ an bho bhailg dhuibh.</p>   
 <p>Air bhliochd,   air bhlachd, air bhlath,<br>   Air omhan agus ais,<br>   Air slaman agus slaig,<br>        Air im, air cais, air gruth.</p>   
 <p>Air aghar agus   agh,<br>   Air damhair agus dair,<br>   Air taghar agus tan,<br>        Air rathaich agus ruth.</p>   
 <p>Naoi tobraiche   Mhic-an-Lir,<br>   Cobhair ort a shil,<br>   Casg a chur air t-fhuil,<br>   Ruith a chur air t-fhual,<br>        A bho bhuar, dhubh.</p>   
 <p>         Muir   mor,<br>            Eas ruadh,<br>            Casg fuil,<br>            Ruith fual.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>I AM now on the   plain,<br>   Reducing wrath and fury,<br>   Making the charm of the red water,<br>        To the beauteous black cow.</p>   
 <p>For milk, for   milk substance, for milk produce,<br>   For whisked whey, for milk riches,<br>   For curdled milk, for milk plenty,<br>        For butter, for cheese, for curds.</p>   
 <p>For progeny and   prosperity,<br>   For rutting time and rutting,<br>   For desire and kine,<br>        For passion and prosperity.</p>   
 <p>The nine wells   of Mac-Lir,<br>   Relief on thee to pour,<br>   Put stop to thy blood,<br>   Put run to thy urine,<br>        Thou cow of cows, black cow.</p>   
 <p>         Great   sea,<br>            Red cascade,<br>            Stop blood,<br>            Flow urine.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>
