 <p>CHAR Bride mach<br>   Maduinn mhoch,<br>   Le caraid each;<br>   Bhris each a chas,<br>   Le uinich och,<br>   Bha sid mu seach,<br>   Chuir i cnamh ri cnamh,<br>   Chuir i feoil ri feoil,<br>   Chuir i feithe ri feithe,<br>   Chuir i cuisle ri cuisle;<br>   Mar a leighis ise sin<br>   Gun leighis mise seo.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>BRIDE went out<br>   In the morning early,<br>   With a pair of horses;<br>   One broke his leg,<br>   With much ado,<br>   That was apart,<br>   She put bone to bone,<br>   She put flesh to flesh,<br>   She put sinew to sinew,<br>   She put vein to vein;<br>   As she healed that<br>   May I heal this.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>
