 <p>BUAINIDH mi an   torranan,<br>   Le toradh mara ’s tir,<br>   Ri lionadh gun traghadh,<br>   Le d’ laimh, a Mhoire mhin.</p>   
 <p>Calum caomh da   m’ sheoladh,<br>   Odhran naomh da m’ dhion,<br>   ’S Bride nam ban buadhach<br>   Cur bhuadh anns an ni.</p>   
 <p>Mar a dh’   orduich Righ nan righ,<br>   Bainne chur an cich ’s an carr,<br>   Mar a dh’ orduich Ri nan dul,<br>   Sugh a chur an uth ’s an ar.</p>   
 <p>Ann an uth   bruc,<br>   Ann an uth brac,<br>   Ann an uth murc,<br>   Ann an uth marc.</p>   
 <p>Ann an uth urc,<br>   Ann an uth arc,<br>   An uth gobhar, othasg, agus caora,<br>   Maoiseach, agus mart.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>I WILL pluck   the figwort,<br>   With the fullness of sea and land,<br>   At the flow, not the ebb of the tide,<br>   By thine hand, gentle Mary.</p>   
 <p>The kindly   Colum directing me,<br>   The holy Oran protecting me,<br>   Whilst Bride of women beneficent<br>   Shall put fruitage in the kine.</p>   
 <p>As the King of   kings ordained,<br>   To put milk in breast and gland,<br>   As the Being of life ordained,<br>   To put sap in udder and teat.</p>   
 <p>In udder of   badger,<br>   In udder of reindeer,<br>   In udder of sow (?),<br>   In udder of mare.</p>   
 <p>In udder of sow   (?),<br>   In udder of heifer,<br>   In udder of goat, ewe, and sheep,<br>   Of roe, and of cow.</p>   </td>  </tr>  <tr>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">

 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>Le bliochd, le   blachd, le bladh,<br>   Le bair, le dair, le toradh,<br>   Le laoigh bhoirionn, bharr,<br>   Le al, le agh, le sonadh.</p>   
 <p>Gun fear   mi-ruin,<br>   Gun bhean mi-shuil,<br>   Gun ghnu, gun tnu,<br>   Gun aon donadh.</p>   
 <p>An ainm nan   ostal deug,          [da<br>   An ainm Mathar De,<br>   An ainm Chriosda fein,<br>   Agus Phadruig.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>With milk, with   cream, with substance,<br>   With rutting, with begetting, with fruitfulness,<br>   With female calves excelling,<br>   With progeny, with joyance, with blessing.</p>   
 <p>Without man of   evil wish,<br>   Without woman of evil eye,<br>   Without malice, without envy,<br>   Without one evil.</p>   
 <p>In name of the   apostles twelve,<br>   In name of the Mother of God,<br>   In name of Christ Himself,<br>   And of Patrick.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>
