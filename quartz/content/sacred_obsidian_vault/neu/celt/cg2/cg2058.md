 <p>A CHEUS-CHRANN   chaomh nam buadh,<br>   A naomhaich fuil naomh an Uain,<br>   Mac Moire min, Dalta Bride nam buar,<br>   Mac Moire mor, Mathair chobhair an t-sluaigh.</p>   
 <p>Ni bheil tur,   no tir,<br>   Ni bheil cith, no cuan,<br>   Ni bheil lod, no li,<br>   Ni bheil frith, no fruan,<br>   Nach bheil domh-sa reidh,<br>   Le comhnadh ceus nam buadh,<br>         Nach bheil domh-sa reidh,<br>         Le comhnadh ceus nam buadh.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>Thou   passion-flower of virtues beloved,<br>   Sanctified by the holy blood of the Lamb,<br>   Son of Mary fair, Foster Son of Bride of kine,<br>   Son of Mary great, helpful Mother of the people.</p>   
 <p>There is no   earth, no land,<br>   There is no lake, no ocean,<br>   There is no pool, no water,<br>   There is no forest, no steep,<br>   That is not to me full safe,<br>   By the protection of the passion-flower of virtues,<br>         But is to me full safe,<br>         By the protection of the passion-flower   of virtues.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>
