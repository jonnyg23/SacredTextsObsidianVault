 <p><small><a name="page_210"><font size="1" color="green">p. 210</font></a></small></p>  
 <p>After more questions and more answers, the boy was carried round in procession sunwise to a wailing march, in which all the boys joined. The boy was then laid upon a rock or knoll for an altar. After more singing and more <a name="page_211"><font size="1" color="green">p. 211</font></a> ceremonial the victim was laid in some convenient hollow for a grave, to the music of another eerie lament and the laughter of the boys. The writer was an actor in this boyish drama, but what the drama represented he does not know.</p>
