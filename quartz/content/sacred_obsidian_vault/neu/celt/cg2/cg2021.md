 <p>EOLAS gradhaidh   dut,<br>   Uisge thraghadh thromh shop,<br>   Blaths an fhir [te] thig riut,<br>        Le ghradh a tharsainn ort.</p>   
 <p>Eirich moch   Di-domhnaich,<br>   Gu lic chomhnard chladaich<br>   Beir leat beannach pubaill,<br>        Agus currachd sagairt.</p>   
 <p>Deannan beag a   ghriosaich<br>   An iochdar do bhadain,<br>   Dolman corr a ghruaigean<br>        Ann an sluasaid mhaide.</p>   
 <p>Tri cnamhan   seann-duine,<br>   An deigh an creann a uaigh,<br>   Naoi goisne reann-roinnich,<br>        An deigh an treann le tuaigh.</p>   
 <p>Loisg iad air   teine crionaich<br>   Is dean gu leir diubh luath;<br>   Crath am brollach broth do leannain,<br>        An aghaidh gath gaoth tuath.</p>   
 <p>Rach ruaig rath   an alachd,<br>   Car nan coig cuart,<br>   ’S bheirim brath is baran duit<br>        Nach falbh am fear [bean] sin uat.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>A LOVE charm   for thee,<br>   Water drawn through a straw,<br>   The warmth of him [her] thou lovest,<br>        With love to draw on thee.</p>   
 <p>Arise betimes   on Lord's day,<br>   To the flat rock of the shore<br>   Take with thee the pointed canopy,   [butter-bur (?)<br>        And the cap of a   priest.           [fox-glove   (?)</p>   
 <p>A small   quantity of embers<br>   In the skirt of thy kirtle,<br>   A special handful of sea-weed<br>        In a wooden shovel.</p>   
 <p>Three bones of   an old man,<br>   Newly torn from the grave,<br>   Nine stalks of royal fern,<br>        Newly trimmed with an axe.</p>   
 <p>Burn them on a   fire of faggots<br>   And make them all into ashes;<br>   Sprinkle in the fleshy breast of thy lover,<br>        Against the venom of the north wind.</p>   
 <p>Go round the   'rath' of procreation,<br>   The circuit of the five turns,<br>   And I will vow and warrant thee<br>        That man [woman] shall never leave thee.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>
