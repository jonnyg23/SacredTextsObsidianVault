 <p>I TRAMPLE upon   the eye,<br>   As tramples the duck upon the lake,<br>   As tramples the swan upon the water,<br>   As tramples the horse upon the plain,<br>   As tramples the cow upon the 'iuc,'<br>   As tramples the host of the elements,<br>        As tramples the host of the elements.</p>
