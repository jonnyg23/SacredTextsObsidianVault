 <p>BUAINIDH mi an   torranan,<br>   Le mile beannachd, le mile buaidh,<br>   Bride bhith dha chonall dhomh,<br>   Moire mhin dha thoradh dhomh,<br>   Moire mhor, Mathair chobhair an t-sluaigh.</p>   
 <p>Thainig na naoi   sonais,<br>   Le na naoi marannan,<br>   A bhuain an torranain,<br>   Le mile beannachd, le mile buaidh--<br>   Le mile beannachd, le mile buaidh.</p>   
 <p>Lamh Chriosda   liom,<br>   Fath Chriosda rium,<br>   Sgath Chriosda tharam,<br>   Tha mo lus allail an allos a bhuain--<br>   Tha mo lus allail an allos a bhuain.</p>   
 <p>An ainm Athar   ais,<br>   An ainm Criosda Phais,<br>   An ainm Spiorad grais,<br>   An agallaich mo bhais,<br>   Nach fag mi gu Luan--<br>        An agallaich mo bhais,<br>        Nach fag mi gu Luan.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>I WILL cull the   figwort,<br>   Of thousand blessings, of thousand virtues,<br>   The calm Bride endowing it to me,<br>   The fair Mary enriching it to me,<br>   The great Mary, aid-Mother of the people.</p>   
 <p>Came the nine   joys,<br>   With the nine waves,<br>   To cull the figwort,<br>   Of thousand blessings, of thousand virtues--<br>   Of thousand blessings, of thousand virtues.</p>   
 <p>The arm of   Christ about me,<br>   The face of Christ before me,<br>   The shade of Christ over me,<br>   My noble plant is being culled--<br>   My noble plant is being culled.</p>   
 <p>In name of the   Father of wisdom,<br>   In name of the Christ of Pasch,<br>   In name of the Spirit of grace,<br>   Who in the struggles of my death,<br>   Will not leave me till Doom<br>        Who in the struggles of my death,<br>        Will not leave me till Doom.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>
