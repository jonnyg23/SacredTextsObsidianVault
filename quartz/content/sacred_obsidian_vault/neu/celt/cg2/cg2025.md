 <p>UIBE gheal   chuir Muire mhin,<br>   A nail air allt, air muir, ’s air tir,<br>   Air bhrig, ’s air ghat fharmaid,<br>   Air mhac armaid,<br>   Air fiacaill coin-ghiorr,<br>   Air siadhadh coin-ghearr,<br>   Air tri chorracha-cri,<br>   Air tri chorracha cnamh,<br>   Air tri chorracha creothail,<br>   ’S air lion leothair lair. [? leobhar</p>   
 <p>Ge be co rinn   dut an t-suil,<br>   Gun laigh i air fein,<br>   Gun laigh i air a thur,<br>   Gun laigh i air a spreidh,<br>   Gun laigh i air a shult,<br>   Gun laigh i air a shaill,<br>   Gun laigh i air a chuid,<br>   Gun laigh i air a chlainn,<br>   Gun laigh i air a bhean,<br>   Gun laigh i air a loinn.</p>   
 <p>Clomhaidh mise   an t-suil,<br>   Somhaidh mise an t-suil,<br>   Imirichidh mi ’n t-suil,<br>   A thri feithean feiche,<br>   ’S teang eug an iomalain.<br>   Tri maighdeana beaga caomh,<br>   A rugadh ’s an aon oidhche ri Criosd,<br>   Ma's beo dh’an triuir sin air an oidhche nochd,<br>   Beo bhith d’ ire-sa, bheothaich bhochd.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>THE fair spell   that lovely Mary sent,<br>   Over stream, over sea, over land,<br>   Against incantations, against withering glance,<br>   Against inimical power,<br>   Against the teeth of wolf,<br>   Against the testicles of wolf,<br>   Against the three crooked cranes,<br>   Against the three crooked bones,<br>   Against the three crooked 'creothail,'<br>   And against lint 'leothair' of the ground. [? long lint</p>   
 <p>Whoso made to   thee the eye,'<br>   May it lie upon himself,<br>   May it lie upon his house,<br>   May it lie upon his flocks,<br>   May it lie upon his substance,<br>   May it lie upon his fatness,<br>   May it lie upon his means,<br>   May it lie upon his children,<br>   May it lie upon his wife,<br>   May it lie upon his descendants.</p>   
 <p>I will subdue   the eye,<br>   I will suppress the eye,<br>   And I will banish the eye,<br>   The three arteries inviting (?),<br>   And the tongue of death completely.<br>   Three lovely little maidens,<br>   Born the same night with Christ,<br>   If alive be these three to-night,<br>   Life be anear thee, poor beast.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>
