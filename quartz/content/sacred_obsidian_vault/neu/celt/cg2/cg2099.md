 <p>IS min a has,<br>   Is fin a cas,<br>   Is caomh a cruth,<br>   Is caoin a guth,<br>   Is binn a cainn,<br>   Is grinn a meinn,<br>   Is blath sealladh a sul,<br>   Is tlath meaghail a gnuis,<br>   ’S a brollach graidh-gheal a snamh ’n a com<br>   Mar chra-fhaoileag air bharr nan tonn.</p>   
 <p>Is naomhar an   oigh is or-dhealta cul,<br>   Le maotharan og am bonn nan stuc,<br>   Gun lon dhaibh le cheil fo chorr nan speur,<br>   Gun sgoth fo ’n ghrein bho ’n namhaid.</p>   
 <p>Ta sgiath Mhic   De da comhdach,<br>   Ta ciall Mhic De da seoladh,<br>   Ta briathar Mhic De mar bhiadh di fein,<br>   Ta reul ’n a leirsinn mhoir di.</p>   
 <p>Ta duibhre na   h-oidhche dhi mar shoillse an lo,<br>   Ta an lo dhi a ghnath ’n a sholas,<br>   Ta Moir oigh nan gras ’s a h-uile h-ait,<br>   Le na seachd graidh ’g a comhnadh,<br>        Na seachd graidh ’g a comhnadh.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>SMOOTH her   hand,<br>   Fair her foot,<br>   Graceful her form,<br>   Winsome her voice,<br>   Gentle her speech,<br>   Stately her mien,<br>   Warm the look of her eye,<br>   Mild the expression of her face,<br>   While her lovely white breast heaves on her bosom<br>   Like the black-headed sea-gull on the gently heaving wave.</p>   
 <p>Holy is the   virgin of gold-mist hair,<br>   With tenderest babe at the base of the bens,<br>   No food for either of them under the arch of the sky,<br>   No shelter under the sun to shield them from the foe.</p>   
 <p>The shield of   the Son of God covers her,<br>   The inspiration of the Son of God guides her,<br>   The word of the Son of God is food to her,<br>   His star is a bright revealing light to her.</p>   
 <p>The darkness of   night is to her as the brightness of day,<br>   The day to her gaze is always a joy,<br>   While the Mary of grace is in every place,<br>   With the seven beatitudes compassing her,<br>        The seven beatitudes compassing her.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>
