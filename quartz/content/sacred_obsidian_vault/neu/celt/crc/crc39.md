 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Cattle Raid of Cualnge</i>, by L. Winifred Faraday, [1904], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center"><font size="-1" color="green">The Peace</font></h3>  
 <p>Ailill and Medb made peace with the Ulstermen and with Cuchulainn. For seven years after there was no wounding of men between them. Findabair stayed with Cuchulainn, and the Connaughtmen went to their country, and the Ulstermen to Emain Macha with their great triumph. Finit, amen.</p>
