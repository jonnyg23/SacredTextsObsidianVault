 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Feuds of the Clans</i>, by Alexander MacGregor, [1907], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">NOTE.</h3>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">The</span> first part of this book was written by the late Rev. Alexander Macgregor, M.A., on 21st October, 1875, and delivered as a lecture to the Working Men's Club at Inverness on 19th November, 1875.</p>  
 <p>The second part is from a MS. written in the reign of King James VI., and first published in 1764 by Messrs. Foulis, Glasgow; reprinted by Messrs. Robertson, Glasgow, in 1780; and again reprinted in <i>Miscellanea Scotica</i>, Vols. I.–II., 1818. It was also reprinted in the <i>Celtic Magazine</i>, Vol. XI., 1885–1886.</p>
