 <p>
 This is an account of the feuds of the Highlanders of Scotland.
 While most feuds stem from trivial causes, they have always been
 a source for high literature, from the Iliad to the Mahabharata.
 These Scottish feuds take on an epic quality of their own, as they play
 out their grim logic of retribution.
