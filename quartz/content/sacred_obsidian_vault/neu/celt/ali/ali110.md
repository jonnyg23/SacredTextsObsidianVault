 <p align="CENTER">For the Night-Fire (the Fever)</p>
 <blockquote>"God save thee, Michael, archangel! God save thee!</blockquote>
 <blockquote>"What aileth thee, O man?"</blockquote>
 <blockquote>"A headache and a sickness and a weakness of the heart. O Michael, archangel, canst thou cure me, O angel of the Lord?"</blockquote>
 <blockquote>"May three things cure thee, O man. May the shadow of Christ fall on thee! May the garment of Christ cover thee May the breath of Christ breathe on thee! And when I come again thou wilt be healed."</blockquote>
 <blockquote>These words are said over the patient while his arms are lifted in the form of a cross, and water is sprinkled on his head.</blockquote>
