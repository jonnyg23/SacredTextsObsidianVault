 <p align="CENTER">A Prayer Against the Plague</p>
 <blockquote>"O Star of Heaven, beloved of the Lord, drive away time foul constellation that has slain the people with the wound of dreadful death. O Star of time Sea, save us from the poison-breath that kills, from the enemy that slays in the night. AMEN."</blockquote>
