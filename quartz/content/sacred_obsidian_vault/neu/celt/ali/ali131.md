 <p align="CENTER">How to Go Invisible</p>
 <blockquote>Get a raven's heart, split it open with a black-hafted knife; make three cuts and place a black bean in each cut. Then plant it, and when the beans sprout put one in your mouth and say--</blockquote><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <blockquote>"By virtue of Satan's heart,<br>
 And by strength of my great art,<br>
 I desire to be invisible."</blockquote>
 <blockquote>And so it will be as long as the bean is kept in the mouth.</blockquote></dir>
