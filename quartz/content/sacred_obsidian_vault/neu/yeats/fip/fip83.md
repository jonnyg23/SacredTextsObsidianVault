 <h3 align="CENTER">STOLEN CHILD.--Page <a href="fip18.htm#page_59">59</a>.</h3>
 <p>The places mentioned are round about Sligo. Further Rosses is a very noted fairy locality. There is here a little point of rocks where, if anyone falls asleep, there is danger of their waking silly, the fairies having carried off their souls.</p>
