 <p><a name="page_31"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 31</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">HE TELLS OF THE PERFECT BEAUTY</h3>  
 <p>O CLOUD-PALE eyelids, dream-dimmed eyes,<br> The poets labouring all their days<br> To build a perfect beauty in rhyme<br> Are overthrown by a woman's gaze<br> And by the unlabouring brood of the skies:<br> And therefore my heart will bow, when dew<br> Is dropping sleep, until God burn time,<br> Before the unlabouring stars and you.</p>
