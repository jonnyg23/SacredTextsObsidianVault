 <p><a name="page_86"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 86</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">O DO NOT LOVE TOO LONG</h3>  
 <p>SWEETHEART, do not love too long:<br> I loved long and long,<br> And grew to be out of fashion<br> Like an old song.</p>  
 <p>All through the years of our youth<br> Neither could have known<br> Their own thought from the other's,<br> We were so much at one.</p>  
 <p>But, O in a minute she changed--<br> O do not love too long,<br> Or you will grow out of fashion<br> Like an old song.</p>
