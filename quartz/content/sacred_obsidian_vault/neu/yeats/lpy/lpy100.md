 <p><a name="page_222"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 222</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">I</h3>  <h3 align="center">TO A CHILD DANCING IN THE WIND</h3>  
 <p>DANCE there upon the shore;<br> What need have you to care<br> For wind or water's roar?<br> And tumble out your hair<br> That the salt drops have wet;<br> Being young you have not known<br> The fool's triumph, nor yet<br> Love lost as soon as won,<br> Nor the best labourer dead<br> And all the sheaves to bind.<br> What need have you to dread<br> The monstrous crying of wind?</p>
