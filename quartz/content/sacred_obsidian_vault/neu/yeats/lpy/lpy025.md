 <p><a name="page_30"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 30</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">HE TELLS OF A VALLEY FULL OF LOVERS</h3>  
 <p>I DREAMED that I stood in a valley, and amid sighs,<br> For happy lovers passed two by two where I stood;<br> And I dreamed my lost love came stealthily out of the wood<br> With her cloud-pale eyelids falling on dream-dimmed eyes:<br> I cried in my dream, <i>O women, bid the young men lay<br> Their heads on your knees, and drown their eyes with your hair,<br> Or remembering hers they will find no other face fair<br> Till all the valleys of the world have been withered away</i>.</p>
