 <p><a name="page_40"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 40</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">THE LOVER PLEADS WITH HIS FRIEND FOR OLD FRIENDS</h3>  
 <p>THOUGH you are in your shining days,<br> Voices among the crowd<br> And new friends busy with your praise,<br> Be not unkind or proud,<br> But think about old friends the most:<br> Time's bitter flood will rise,<br> Your beauty perish and be lost<br> For all eyes but these eyes.</p>
