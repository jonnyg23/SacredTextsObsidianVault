 <p><a name="page_265"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 265</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">LINES WRITTEN IN DEJECTION</h3>  
 <p>WHEN have I last looked on<br> The round green eyes and the long wavering bodies<br> Of the dark leopards of the moon?<br> All the wild witches those most noble ladies,<br> For all their broom-sticks and their tears,<br> Their angry tears, are gone.<br> The holy centaurs of the hills are vanished;<br> I have nothing but the embittered sun;<br> Banished heroic mother moon and vanished,<br> And now that I have come to fifty years<br> I must endure the timid sun.</p>
