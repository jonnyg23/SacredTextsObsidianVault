 <p><a name="page_32"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 32</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">HE HEARS THE CRY OF THE SEDGE</h3>  
 <p>I WANDER by the edge<br> Of this desolate lake<br> Where wind cries in the sedge:<br> <i>Until the axle break<br> That keeps the stars in their round,<br> And hands hurl in the deep<br> The banners of East and West,<br> And the girdle of light is unbound,<br> Your breast will not lie by the breast<br> Of your beloved in sleep</i>.</p>
