 <p><a name="page_24"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 24</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">TO HIS HEART, BIDDING IT HAVE NO FEAR</h3>  
 <p>BE you still, be you still, trembling heart;<br> Remember the wisdom out of the old days:<br> <i>Him who trembles before the flame and the flood,<br> find the winds that blow through the starry ways,<br> Let the starry winds and the flame and the flood<br> Cover over and hide, for he has no part<br> With the lonely, majestical multitude</i>.</p>
