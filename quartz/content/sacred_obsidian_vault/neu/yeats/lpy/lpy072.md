 <p><a name="page_162"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 162</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">THE MASK</h3>  
 <p>"PUT off that mask of burning gold<br> With emerald eyes."<br> "O no, my dear, you make so bold<br> To find if hearts be wild and wise,<br> And yet not cold."</p>  
 <p>"I would but find what's there to find,<br> Love or deceit."<br> "It was the mask engaged your mind,<br> And after set your heart to beat,<br> Not what's behind."</p>  
 <p>"But lest you are my enemy,<br> I must enquire."<br> "O no, my dear, let all that be,<br> What matter, so there is but fire<br> In you, in me?"</p>
