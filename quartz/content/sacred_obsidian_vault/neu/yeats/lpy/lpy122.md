 <p><a name="page_254"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 254</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">TO A YOUNG GIRL</h3>  
 <p>MY dear, my dear, I know<br> More than another<br> What makes your heart beat so;<br> Not even your own mother<br> Can know it as I know,<br> Who broke my heart for her<br> When the wild thought,<br> That she denies<br> And has forgot,<br> Set all her blood astir<br> And glittered in her eyes.</p>
