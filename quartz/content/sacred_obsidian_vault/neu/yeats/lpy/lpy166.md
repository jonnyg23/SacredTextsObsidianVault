 <p><a name="page_353"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 353</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">TO BE CARVED ON A STONE AT THOOR BALLYLEE</h3>  
 <p>I, THE poet William Yeats,<br> With old mill boards and sea-green slates,<br> And smithy work from the Gort forge,<br> Restored this tower for my wife George;<br> And may these characters remain<br> When all is ruin once again.</p>
