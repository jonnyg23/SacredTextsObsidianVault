 <p><a name="page_161"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 161</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">TO A POET, WHO WOULD HAVE ME PRAISE CERTAIN BAD POETS, IMITATORS OF HIS AND MINE</h3>  
 <p>You say, as I have often given tongue<br> In praise of what another's said or sung,<br> ’Twere politic to do the like by these;<br> But was there ever dog that praised his fleas?</p>
