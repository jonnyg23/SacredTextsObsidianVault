 <p><a name="page_202"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 202</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">ON THOSE THAT HATED "THE PLAYBOY OF THE WESTERN WORLD," 1907</h3>  
 <p>ONCE, when midnight smote the air,<br> Eunuchs ran through Hell and met<br> On every crowded street to stare<br> Upon great Juan riding by:<br> Even like these to rail and sweat<br> Staring upon his sinewy thigh.</p>
