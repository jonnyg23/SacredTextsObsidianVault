 <p><a name="page_158"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 158</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">A DRINKING SONG</h3>  
 <p>WINE comes in at the mouth<br> And love comes in at the eye;<br> That's all we shall know for truth<br> Before we grow old and die.<br> I lift the glass to my mouth,<br> I look at you, and I sigh.</p>
