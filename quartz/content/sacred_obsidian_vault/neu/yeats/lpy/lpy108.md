 <p><a name="page_231"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 231</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">I</h3>  <h3 align="center">THE MAGI</h3>  
 <p>Now as at all times I can see in the mind's eye,<br> In their stiff, painted clothes, the pale unsatisfied ones<br> Appear and disappear in the blue depth of the sky<br> With all their ancient faces like rain-beaten stones,<br> And all their helms of silver hovering side by side,<br> And all their eyes still fixed, hoping to find once more,<br> Being by Calvary's turbulence unsatisfied,<br> The uncontrollable mystery on the bestial floor.</p>
