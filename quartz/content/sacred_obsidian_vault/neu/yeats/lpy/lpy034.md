 <p><a name="page_41"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 41</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">A LOVER SPEAKS TO THE HEARERS OF HIS SONGS IN COMING DAYS</h3>  
 <p>O, WOMEN, kneeling by your altar rails long hence,<br> When songs I wove for my beloved hide the prayer,<br> And smoke from this dead heart drifts through the violet air<br> And covers away the smoke of myrrh and frankincense;<br> Bend down and pray for all that sin I wove in song,<br> Till the Attorney for Lost Souls cry her sweet cry,<br> And call to my beloved and me: "No longer fly<br> Amid the hovering, piteous, penitential throng."</p>
