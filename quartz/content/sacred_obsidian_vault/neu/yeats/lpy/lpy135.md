 <p><a name="page_280"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 280</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">A THOUGHT FROM PROPERTIUS</h3>  
 <p>SHE might, so noble from head<br> To great shapely knees<br> The long flowing line,<br> Have walked to the altar<br> Through the holy images<br> At Pallas Athene's side,<br> Or been fit spoil for a centaur<br> Drunk with the unmixed wine.</p>
