 <p><a name="an_iii-p1"></a>“What the world, which truly knows   <i>nothing,</i>    calls ‘mysticism’ is the science of   <i>ultimates, . . .</i>    the science of self-evident Reality, which cannot be ‘reasoned  about,’ because it is the object of pure reason or perception.  The Babe sucking its mother’s breast, and the Lover returning,  after twenty years’ separation, to his home and food in the same  bosom, are the types and princes of Mystics.”</p>
