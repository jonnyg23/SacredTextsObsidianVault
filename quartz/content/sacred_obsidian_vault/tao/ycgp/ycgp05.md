 <h2 align="center">CHAPTER V<br> <small>FALSE VIRTUES</small></h2>
 
 <p>Y<small>ANG</small> C<small>HU</small> said:</p>
 
 <p>   "Po Yi was not without desire, for being too proud of his purity of mind, he was led to death by starvation.</p>
 
 <p>   "Chan-Chi<a name="page_41_fr_2"></a><a href="#page_41_note_2"><font size="1">2</font></a> was not passionless, for being too proud of his virtue he happened to reduce his family.</p>
 
 <p>   "Those who in pursuit of purity and virtue do good in a false way resemble these men."</p>
