 <h3 align="CENTER">(A Simile.)</h3>
 <p>Those who seize property, are, to use an illustration, like those who relieve their hunger by eating tainted meat,<sup>[63]</sup> or quench their thirst by drinking poisoned liquor. Though they are not without temporary gratification, death will anon overcome them. (1146-1169)</p>
