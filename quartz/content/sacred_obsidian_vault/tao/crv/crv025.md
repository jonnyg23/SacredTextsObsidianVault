 <h3 align="CENTER">19. RETURNING TO SIMPLICITY.</h3>
 <p><a name="img_tao19"></a>
 </p><table align="LEFT"><tr><td align="CENTER" valign="CENTER"><img src="img/tao19.jpg" align="LEFT"></td></tr></table>1. Abandon your saintliness; put away your prudence; and the people will gain a hundredfold!
 <p>2. Abandon your benevolence; put away your justice; and the people will return to filial piety and paternal devotion.</p>
 <p>3. Abandon smartness; give up greed; and thieves and robbers will no longer exist.</p>
 <p>4. These are three things for which culture is insufficient. Therefore it is said:</p><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <p>"Hold fast to that which will endure,<br>
 Show thyself simple, preserve thee pure,<br>
 And lessen self with desires fewer."</p>
