 <h3 align="CENTER">21. EMPTYING THE HEART.</h3>
 <p><a name="img_tao21"></a>
 </p><table align="LEFT"><tr><td align="CENTER" valign="CENTER"><img src="img/tao21.jpg" align="LEFT"></td></tr></table><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <p>1. "Vast virtue's form<br>
 Follows Reason's norm.</p>
 <p>2. "And Reason's nature<br>
 Is vague and eluding.</p>
 <p>3. "How eluding and vague<br>
 All types including!<br>
 How vague and eluding,<br>
 All beings including!<br>
 How deep and how obscure.<br>
 It harbors the spirit pure,<br>
 Whose truth is ever sure,<br>
 Whose faith abides for aye<br>
 From of yore until to-day.</p>
 <p>4. "Its name is never vanishing,<br>
 It heeds the good of everything."</p></dir>
 </dir>
 
 <p>5. Through what do I know that "it heeds the good of everything"? In this way, verily: Through IT.</p>
