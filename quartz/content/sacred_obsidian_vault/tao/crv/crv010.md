 <h3 align="CENTER">4. SOURCELESS.</h3>
 <p><a name="img_tao04"></a>
 </p><table align="LEFT"><tr><td align="CENTER" valign="CENTER"><img src="img/tao04.jpg" align="LEFT"></td></tr></table>1. Reason is empty, but its use is inexhaustible. In its profundity, verily, it <a name="page_76"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 76</font></a> resembleth the arch-father of the ten thousand things.<dir>
 <dir>
 
 <p>2. "It will blunt its own sharpness,<br>
 Will its tangles adjust;<br>
 It will dim its own radiance<br>
 And be one with its dust."</p></dir>
 </dir>
 
 <p>3. Oh, how calm it seems to remain! I know not whose son it is. Apparently even the Lord it precedes.</p>
