 <h3 align="CENTER">47. VIEWING THE DISTANT.</h3>
 <p><a name="img_tao47"></a>
 </p><table align="LEFT"><tr><td align="CENTER" valign="CENTER"><img src="img/tao47.jpg" align="LEFT"></td></tr></table><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <p>1. "Without passing out of the gate<br>
 The world's course I prognosticate.<br>
 Without peeping through the window<br>
 The heavenly Reason I contemplate.<br>
 The further one goes,<br>
 The less one knows."</p></dir>
 </dir>
 
 <p>2. Therefore the holy man does not travel, and yet he has knowledge. He does not see things, and yet he defines them. He does not labor, and yet he completes.</p>
