 <h3 align="CENTER">9. PRACTISING PLACIDITY.</h3>
 <p><a name="img_tao09"></a>
 </p><table align="LEFT"><tr><td align="CENTER" valign="CENTER"><img src="img/tao09.jpg" align="LEFT"></td></tr></table>1. Grasp to the full, are you not likely foiled? Scheme too sharply, can you wear long? If gold and jewels fill the hall no one can protect it.
 <p>2. Rich and high but proud, brings about its own doom. To accomplish merit and acquire fame, then to withdraw, that is Heaven's Way.</p>
