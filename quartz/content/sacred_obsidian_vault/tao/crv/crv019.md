 <h3 align="CENTER">13. LOATHING SHAME.</h3>
 <p><a name="img_tao13"></a>
 </p><table align="LEFT"><tr><td align="CENTER" valign="CENTER"><img src="img/tao13.jpg" align="LEFT"></td></tr></table><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <p>1. "Favor bodes disgrace; it is like trembling.<br>
 Rank bodes great heartache. It is like the body."</p></dir>
 </dir>
 
 <p><a name="page_81"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 81</font></a></p>
 <p>2. What means "Favor bodes disgrace; it is like trembling?"</p>
 <p>Favor humiliates. Its acquisition causes trembling, its loss causes trembling. This is meant by "Favor bodes disgrace; it is like trembling."</p>
 <p>3. What means "Rank bodes great heartache, it is like the body?"</p>
 <p>I suffer great heartache because I have a body. When I have no body, what heartache remains?</p>
 <p>4. Therefore who administers the empire as he takes care of his body can be entrusted with the empire.</p>
