 <h3 align="CENTER">5. THE FUNCTION OF EMPTINESS.</h3>
 <p><a name="img_tao05"></a>
 </p><table align="LEFT"><tr><td align="CENTER" valign="CENTER"><img src="img/tao05.jpg" align="LEFT"></td></tr></table>1. But for heaven and earth's humaneness, the ten thousand things are straw dogs. But for the holy man's humaneness, the hundred families are straw dogs.
 <p>2. Is not the space between heaven and earth like unto a bellows? It is empty; yet it collapses not. It moves, and more and more comes forth. [But]</p><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <p>3. "How soon exhausted is<br>
 A gossip's fulsome talk!<br>
 And should we not prefer<br>
 On the middle path to walk?"</p>
