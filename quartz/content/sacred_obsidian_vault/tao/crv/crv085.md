 <h3 align="CENTER">79. KEEP YOUR OBLIGATIONS.</h3>
 <p><a name="img_tao79"></a>
 </p><table align="LEFT"><tr><td align="CENTER" valign="CENTER"><img src="img/tao79.jpg" align="LEFT"></td></tr></table>1. When a great hatred is reconciled, naturally some hatred will remain. How can this be made good?
 <p>2. Therefore the sage keeps the obligations of his contract and exacts not from others. Those who have virtue attend to their obligations; those who have no virtue attend to their claims.</p>
 <p>3. Heaven's Reason shows no preference but always assists the good man.</p>
