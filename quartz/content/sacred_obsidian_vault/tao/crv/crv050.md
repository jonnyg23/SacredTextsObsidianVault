 <h3 align="CENTER">44. SETTING UP PRECEPTS.</h3>
 <p><a name="img_tao44"></a>
 </p><table align="LEFT"><tr><td align="CENTER" valign="CENTER"><img src="img/tao44.jpg" align="LEFT"></td></tr></table><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <p>1. "Name or person, which is more near?<br>
 Person or fortune, which is more dear?<br>
 Gain or loss, which is more sear?</p>
 <p>2. "Extreme dotage leadeth to squandering.<br>
 Hoarded wealth inviteth plundering.</p>
 <p>3. "Who is content incurs no humiliation,<br>
 Who knows when to stop risks no vitiation,<br>
 Forever lasteth his duration."</p>
