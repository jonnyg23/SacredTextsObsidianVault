 <p>This book presents details of Masonic initiation rituals,
 along with grips, passwords and regalia.
 Written in the 19th century, <cite>Duncan's Ritual</cite>, as it
 is known, has been republished numerous times.
 It includes the three basic degrees of the Ancient York Rite,
 and four additional advanced degrees.
 There are over a hundred illustrations, all reproduced here, which
 show important details of the rituals, including gestures
 and symbolic pictures.
 <cite>Duncan's Ritual</cite> is careful to note known variations
 where they exist.
 This book will be of interest to beginning Masons who
 want a roadmap of the craft, as well as experienced Masons
 who need a review.
 </p>
