 <p>
 This is a set of essays which discuss the esoteric side of Masonry.
 The author, Walter Leslie Wilmshurst, (b. 1867, d. 1939) attempts to
 demonstrate that Masonry has a deeper meaning, specifically the
 striving for human perfection, and is firmly in the mainstream
 of traditional mystery teachings.
 </p>
