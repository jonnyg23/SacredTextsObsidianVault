 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>Shibboleth: A Templar Monitor</i>, by George Cooper Connor, [1894], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">POPULAR ABBREVIATIONS.</h3>  <h4 align="center">SPECIALLY FOR RECORDERS OF COMMANDERIES.</h4>  
 <p>A. C. (<i>Ante Christum</i>), Before Christ.</p>  
 <p>B. C., Before Christ.</p>  
 <p>A. D. (<i>Anno Domini</i>), Year of our Lord.</p>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Æt</span>., Aged.</p>  
 <p>A. V., Authorized Version.</p>  
 <p>D. V. (<i>Deo Volente</i>), God willing.</p>  
 <p>G. M., Grand Master.</p>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Ibid</span>. (<i>Ibidem</i>), In the same place.</p>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Id</span>. (<i>Idem</i>), The same.</p>  
 <p>I. E. (<i>Id est</i>), That is.</p>  
 <p>I. H. S. (<i>Jesus Hominum Salvator</i>, or <i>In Hoc Salus</i>), Jesus Saviour of men, or In Him is Salvation.</p>  
 <p>I. N. R. I. (<i>Iesus Nazarenus Rex Judæorum</i>), Jesus of Nazareth King of the Jews.</p>  
 <p>K. T., Knight Templar.</p>  
 <p>K. M., Knight of Malta.</p>  
 <p>L. S. (<i>Locus Sigilli</i>), Place of the seal.</p>  
 <p>N. B. (<i>Nota Bene</i>), Take notice.</p>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Nem. Con.</span> (<i>Nemine contradicente</i>), Unanimously.</p>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Ob</span>. (<i>Obiit</i>), Died.</p>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Pro Tem</span>. (<i>Pro tempore</i>), For the time being.</p>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Prox</span>. (<i>Proximo</i>), Next.</p>  
 <p>Q. V. (<i>Quod vide</i>), Which see.</p>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Sc</span>. (<i>Sculpsit</i>), Engraved.</p>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Ult</span>. (<i>Ultimo</i>), Last.</p>  
 <p>V. (<i>Vide</i>), See.</p>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Viz</span>. (<i>Videlicet</i>), Namely.</p>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Etc</span>. (<i>Et cetera</i>), And so forth.</p>
