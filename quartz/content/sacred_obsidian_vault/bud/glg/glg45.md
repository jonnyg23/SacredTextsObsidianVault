 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Gateless Gate</i>, by Ekai, called Mu-mon, tr. Nyogen Senzaki and Paul Reps [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">45. Who Is He?</h3>  
 <p>Hoen said: "The past and future Buddhas, both are his servants. Who is he?"</p>  
 <p> </p>  
 <p><i>Mumon's comment:</i> If you realize clearly who he is, it is as if you met your own father on a busy street. There is no need to ask anyone whether or not your recognition is true.</p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px"><i>Do not fight with another's bow and arrow.<br> Do not ride another's horse.<br> Do not discuss another's faults.<br> Do not interfere with another's work</i>.</div>
