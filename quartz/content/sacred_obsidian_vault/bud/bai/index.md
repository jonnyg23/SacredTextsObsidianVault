 <p>
 This extended essay was the 1908 Ingersoll Lecture on Immortality.
 Bigelow explores the nature of Karma, and the ultimate mental state, Nirvana.
 He relates this to concepts expressed by Western thinkers such as
 <a href="../../aor/darwin/index.htm">Darwin</a> and <a href="../../phi/index.htm">Emerson</a>.
