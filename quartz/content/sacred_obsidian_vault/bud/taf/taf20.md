   <h4 align="center"><i>1. The Revelation of the True Doctrine</i>.</h4>  
 <p>In the one soul we may distinguish two aspects.</p>  
 <p>The one is the Soul as suchness (<i>bhûtatathatâ</i>), the other is the soul as birth-and-death (<i>samsâra</i>). Each in itself constitutes all things, and both are so closely interrelated that one cannot be separated from the other.</p>
