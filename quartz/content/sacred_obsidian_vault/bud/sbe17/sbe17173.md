   <h3 align="center">19.</h3>  
 <p>1. When he had accomplished the Mânatta he told the Bhikkhus [all that happened, from chapter 13 downwards].</p>  
 <p><a name="page_408"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 408</font></a></p>  
 <p>They told this matter to the Blessed One.</p>  
 <p>'Let then the Sa<i>m</i>gha rehabilitate Udâyi the Bhikkhu.</p>  
 <p>'Now thus, O Bhikkhus, [here follows the kammavâ<i>k</i>â as in chapter 5.]'</p>  
 <p align="center">__________________</p>  
 <p align="center">Here end the proceedings on the breach of the first Sa<i>m</i>ghâdisesa.</p>
