 <p><a name="page_401"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 401</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">5.</h3>  
 <p>1. When he had accomplished the Mânatta he told the Bhikkhus, saying, 'I committed (&amp;c., as 4. 1, down to) for one day concealed. I asked, &amp;c. . . . The Sa<i>m</i>gha imposed a probation, &amp;c. . . . When I passed through that probation the Sa<i>m</i>gha imposed a Mânatta of &amp;c. . . . for &amp;c. . . . I have accomplished that Mânatta. What now shall I do?'</p>  
 <p>They told that matter to the Blessed One.</p>  
 <p>'Let then the Sa<i>m</i>gha, O Bhikkhus, rehabilitate Udâyi the Bhikkhu.'</p>  
 <p>2, 3. [Here follows the kammavâ<i>k</i>â as in chapter 2, §§ 2, 3, to the end.]</p>
