 <p><a name="page_383"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 383</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">35.</h3>  
 <p>'Now thus, O Bhikkhus, should it be carried out,' &amp;c. [Here follows the Kammavâ<i>k</i>â for the re-vocation of a Kamma precisely as in chapter 8, with the necessary alterations.]</p>  
 <p align="center">Here ends the seventh (Kamma), the Ukkhepaniya-kamma on not renouncing a sinful doctrine.</p>  
 <p align="center">Here ends the First Khandhaka, the Khandhaka on the Kammas.</p>
