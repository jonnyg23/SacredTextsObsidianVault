   <h3 align="center">4.</h3>  
 <p>1. When he had passed through the probation he told the Bhikkhus, saying, 'I committed, friends, an offence--the first Sa<i>m</i>ghâdisesa--which for one day I concealed. I asked the Sa<i>m</i>gha to impose upon me a probation of one day for the offence . . . . concealed. The Sa<i>m</i>gha imposed . . . . concealed. I have passed through that probation. What now should I do?'</p>  
 <p>They told this thing to the Blessed One.</p>  
 <p>'Let then the Sa<i>m</i>gha impose upon Udâyi the Bhikkhu a Mânatta of six days' duration.'</p>  
 <p>2, 3. [Here follows the kammavâ<i>k</i>â as in chapter 1, §§ 2, 3, to the end.]</p>
