 <h4></h4><p align="center">§ 30. THE SIX ORGANS OF SENSE.</p>
 
 <p align="center"><small>Translated from the Samyutta-Nikâya (xii.2<sup>11</sup>).</small></p>
 
 <p>   And what, O priests, are the six organs of sense?</p>
 
 <p>   Eye, ear, nose, tongue, body, and mind--these, O priests, are called the six organs of sense.</p>
