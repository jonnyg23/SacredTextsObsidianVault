 <p>
 This is a retelling of stories from the Jataka, the treasury of
 tales of Buddha's previous animal reincarnations.
 Babbitt wrote this with young adults and children in mind, but the simple
 language and sprightly black and white illustrations add appeal
 for readers of all ages, Buddhist or not.
 </p>
