 <p>
 This is a translation of the Dhammapada, one of the central
 sacred texts of Buddhism.
 This primary document embodies key concepts of Buddhism expressed
 in simple parables and metaphors.
 This clarity has led to it being one of the most popular
 of all Buddhist documents.
 This is part of the <a href="../../woe/index.htm">Wisdom of the East</a>
 series.
 </p>
