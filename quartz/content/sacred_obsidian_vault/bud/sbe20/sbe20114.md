   <h3 align="center">23.</h3>  
 <p>Now at that time Bhikkhunîs dwelt in the forest, and men of abandoned life violated them.</p>  
 <p><a name="page_363"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 363</font></a></p>  
 <p>They told this matter to the Blessed One.</p>  
 <p>'A Bhikkhunî is not, O Bhikkhus, to adopt the forest life. Whosoever does so, shall be guilty of a dukka<i>t</i>a <a name="fr_947"></a><a href="#fn_931"><font size="1">1</font></a>.'</p>
