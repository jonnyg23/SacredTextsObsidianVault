   <h3 align="center">4.</h3>  
 <p>1. Now Mahâ-pa<i>g</i>âpatî the Gotamî went up to the place where the Blessed One was, and bowed down before him, and stood respectfully on one side. And, so standing, Mahâ-pa<i>g</i>âpatî the Gotamî said to the Blessed One: 'What course, Lord, shall we pursue with reference to those precepts for the</p>  
 <p><a name="page_329"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 329</font></a></p>  
 <p><span class="contnote"><font size="-2" color="GREEN">[paragraph continues]</font></span> Bhikkhunîs which are applicable also to the Bhikkhus?'</p>  
 <p>'Train yourselves, Gotamî, therein in the same manner as the Bhikkhus do.'</p>  
 <p>'And what course, Lord, should we pursue in reference to those precepts for the Bhikkhunîs which are not applicable also to the Bhikkhus?'</p>  
 <p>'Train yourselves, Gotamî, therein according to the substance thereof, as they are laid down.</p>
