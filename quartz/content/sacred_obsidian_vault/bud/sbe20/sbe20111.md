   <h3 align="center">20.</h3>  
 <p>Now at that time Bhikkhunîs inhibited Bhikkhus from the Uposatha, or from the Pavâra<i>n</i>â, or from the Exhortation, or issued commands to them, or asked them to give them leave to rebuke them, or warned them of some offence they supposed they</p>  
 <p><a name="page_359"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 359</font></a></p>  
 <p>were committing, or called upon them to remember whether or not they had committed an offence <a name="fr_934"></a><a href="#fn_919"><font size="1">1</font></a>.</p>  
 <p>They told this matter to the Blessed One.</p>  
 <p>'A Bhikkhunî is not, O Bhikkhus, to perform any one of these official acts towards a Bhikkhu. Should she do so, the act is itself invalid, and she is guilty of a dukka<i>t</i>a.'</p>  
 <p>Now at that time Bhikkhus inhibited Bhikkhunîs [&amp;c., as before, giving the contrary decision].</p>
