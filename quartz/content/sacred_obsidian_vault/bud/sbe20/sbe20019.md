 <p><a name="page_73"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 73</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">4.</h3>  
 <p>1. Now at that time the <i>Kh</i>abbaggiya Bhikkhus used to wear woollen cloth with long fleece to it <a name="fr_151"></a><a href="#fn_146"><font size="1">1</font></a>.</p>  
 <p>The people murmured . . . (&amp;c., down to) They told this matter to the Blessed One.</p>  
 <p>'You are not, O Bhikkhus, to wear woollen cloth with long fleece to it. Whosoever does so, shall be guilty of a dukka<i>t</i>a.'</p>
