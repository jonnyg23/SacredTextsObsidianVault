 <p><a name="an_j111"></a></p>  <h3 align="center">No. 111.</h3>  <h3 align="center">GADRABHA-PAÑHA.</h3>  
 <p>"<i>Thou think’st thyself a swan</i>."--This Question as to the Ass will also be set out at length in the Ummagga-jātaka. This is the end of the Question as to the Ass.</p>
