 <p><a name="page_95"><font size="1" color="green">p. 95</font></a></p>  <h4 align="center">X</h4>  
 <p></p><div style="font-size: 90%">The <i>Shan Yu Fu Su;</i> allusive. A lady mocking her lover.</div>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 20%; margin-right: 20%; font-size: 110%;"><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1">1</a></font></span>On mountain grows the mulberry tree;<br>   The lotus flower in meadow damp.<br> It is not Tzŭ-tu that I see,<br>   But only you, you foolish scamp!  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2">2</a></font></span>Polygonums the damp meads cover;<br>   To lofty pines on mountains view.<br> It is not Tzŭ-ch‘ung comes as lover;<br>   You artful boy, ‘tis only you!</p></div>
