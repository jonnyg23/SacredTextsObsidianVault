 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Book of Poetry</i>, tr. by James Legge, [1876], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h4 align="center">III</h4>  
 <p></p><div style="font-size: 90%">The <i>Yü Li;</i> allusive and narrative. An ode used at district entertainments, celebrating the abundance of everything, and the prosperity of the times.</div>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 20%; margin-right: 20%; font-size: 110%;"><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1">1</a></font></span>By means of simplest fish trap that men make,<br> Sand blowers small and yellow jaws they take;—<br>   Such the result good rule insures.<br> And here our host his spirits forth has set,<br> Good and abundant; proof we now are met,<br>   When ordered rule large wealth secures. <a name="page_204"><font size="1" color="green">p. 204</font></a>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2">2</a></font></span>In simplest fish traps that might useless seem,<br> They take abundant store of tench and bream;—<br>   Such issue from our good rule springs.<br> And here our host his spirits forth has set,<br> Abundant, good;—sure proof we now are met,<br>   When ordered rule great riches brings.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_3">3</a></font></span>Into the simplest fish traps largely go<br> Mudfish and carp;—captures that clearly show<br>   How with good rule the land is crowned.<br> And here our host his spirits forth has set,<br> Good and most ample;—proof we here are met,<br>   When ordered rule makes wealth abound.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_4">4</a></font></span>The mats in great abundance viands show;<br> And these of rarest quality we know.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_5">5</a></font></span>The viands excellent are here revealed;<br> Both land and sea their contributions yield.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_6">6</a></font></span>Viands in ample store the feast displays;<br> And all in season, all beyond our praise.</p></div>
