 <p><a name="page_125"><font size="1" color="green">p. 125</font></a></p>  <h4 align="center">VII</h4>  
 <p></p><div style="font-size: 90%">The <i>Shih Shu;</i> metaphorical. Against the oppression and extortion of the government of Wei.</div>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 20%; margin-right: 20%; font-size: 110%;"><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1">1</a></font></span>Large rats, large rats, let us entreat<br> That you our millet will not eat.<br> But the large rats we mean are you,<br> With whom three years we've had to do,<br> And all that time have never known<br> One look of kindness on us thrown.<br> We take our leave of Wei and you;<br> That happier land we long to view.<br> O happy land! O happy land!<br> There in our proper place we'll stand.  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2">2</a></font></span>Large rats, large rats, let us entreat<br> You'll not devour our crops of wheat.<br> But the large rats we mean are you,<br> With whom three years we've had to do;<br> And all that time you never wrought<br> One kindly act to cheer our lot.<br> To you and Wei we bid farewell,<br> Soon in that happier state to dwell.<br> O happy state! O happy state!<br> There shall we learn to bless our fate. <a name="page_126"><font size="1" color="green">p. 126</font></a></p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_3">3</a></font></span>Large rats, large rats, let us entreat<br> Our springing grain you will not eat.<br> But the large rats we mean are you,<br> With whom three years we've had to do.<br> From you there came not all that while<br> One word of comfort ’mid our toil.<br> We take our leave of you and Wei;<br> And to those happier coasts we flee.<br> O happy coasts, to you we wend!<br> There shall our groans and sorrows end.</p></div>
