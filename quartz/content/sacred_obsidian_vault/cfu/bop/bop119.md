 <p><a name="page_129"><font size="1" color="green">p. 129</font></a></p>  <h4 align="center">III</h4>  
 <p></p><div style="font-size: 90%">The <i>Yang Chih Shui;</i> allusive. Rebellion plotted against the Marquis of Chin by the chief of Ch‘ü-wu and his partisans.</div>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 20%; margin-right: 20%; font-size: 110%;"><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1">1</a></font></span>  ’Midst the fretted waters<br>     The white rocks grandly stand.<br>   To Wu we'll follow you,<br>     With white silk robe in hand,<br>   And collar of red hue,<br>     Your master to invest,<br>   And hail him lord of Chin,<br>     Of the whole state possest.<br> The sight of him, our princely lord,<br> Will joy to all our hearts afford.  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2">2</a></font></span>  ’Midst the fretted waters<br>     The white rocks glist’ning stand.<br>   To Ku we'll follow you,<br>     With white silk robe in hand,<br>   And collar of red hue,<br>     And broidered. axes fine,<br>   Your master to invest<br> As chief of T‘ang’s great line.<br> When him, our princely lord, we see,<br> From every pain we shall be free. <a name="page_130"><font size="1" color="green">p. 130</font></a></p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_3">3</a></font></span>’Midst the fretted waters<br>   The white rocks grandly show.<br> Your orders we have heard,<br> But not a single word<br>   Shall any from us know.</p></div>
