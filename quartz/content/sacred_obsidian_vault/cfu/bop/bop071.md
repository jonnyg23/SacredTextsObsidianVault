 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Book of Poetry</i>, tr. by James Legge, [1876], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h4 align="center">IV</h4>  
 <p></p><div style="font-size: 90%">The <i>Yang Chih Shui;</i> allusive. The troops of Chou, kept on duty in Shin, murmur at their separation from their families.</div>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 20%; margin-right: 20%; font-size: 110%;"><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1">1</a></font></span>    Fretted the waters seem,<br>     Yet gently flows the stream;—<br> Unable a fagot to bear.<br>     Guarding Shin here we roam,<br>     Wives and children at home,<br> All absent our toils who should share. <a name="page_78"><font size="1" color="green">p. 78</font></a><br>     We think of them ever;<br>     Thought parts from them never;—<br> What month shall we homeward repair?  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2">2</a></font></span>    Fretted the waters seem,<br>     Yet gently flows the stream;—<br> A bundle of thorns twill not bear.<br>     Guarding Fu here we roam,<br>     Wives and children at home,<br> All absent our toils who should share.<br>     We think of them ever;<br>     Thought parts from them never;—<br> What month shall we homeward repair?</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_3">3</a></font></span>    Fretted the waters seem,<br>     Yet gently flows the stream;<br> The reed bundle powerless to bear.<br>     Guarding Hsü here we roam,<br>     Wives and children at home,<br> All absent our toils who should share.<br>     We think of them ever;<br>     Thought parts from them never;—<br> What month shall we homeward repair?</p></div>
