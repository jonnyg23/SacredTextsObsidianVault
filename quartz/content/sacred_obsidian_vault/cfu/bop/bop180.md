 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Book of Poetry</i>, tr. by James Legge, [1876], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h4 align="center">VII</h4>  
 <p></p><div style="font-size: 90%">The <i>Nan Shan Yu T‘ai;</i> allusive. A festal ode, where the ruler, as the host, celebrates the virtues of his ministers, who are the guests, and supplicates blessings on them.</div>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 20%; margin-right: 20%; font-size: 110%;"><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1">1</a></font></span>  The southern hills the <i>t‘ai</i> plant show,<br>     The northern yield the <i>lai</i>.<br>   Your presence here, my noble guests,<br>     Fills me with rapture high.<br> ’Tis on your strength that all my states depend;<br> Myriads of years be yours, years without end!  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2">2</a></font></span>  On southern hills are mulberry trees,<br>     On northern willows grow.<br>   Your presence here, my noble guests,<br>     Makes my joy overflow.<br> Your virtue's rays through all my regions shine;<br> Myriads of years be yours in boundless line! <a name="page_207"><font size="1" color="green">p. 207</font></a></p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_3">3</a></font></span>  On southern hills the medlars thrive,<br>     And plum trees in the north.<br>   Your presence here, my noble guests,<br>     The richest joy calls forth.<br> Parental love ye for my people show,<br> And may your virtuous fame decay ne’er know!</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_4">4</a></font></span>  The southern hills the <i>k‘ao</i> display,<br>     The northern have the <i>niu</i>.<br>   Here at our feast, my noble guests,<br>     My heart rests glad in you.<br> The eyebrows of long life your foreheads crown;<br> Still wider be your virtuous fame's renown!</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_5">5</a></font></span>  The honey trees on southern hills,<br>     The <i>yü</i> on northern rise.<br>   Your presence here, my noble guests,<br>     Rare happiness supplies.<br> Gray hair and wrinkled face yours yet shall be:<br> May future times your sons as prosperous see!</p></div>
