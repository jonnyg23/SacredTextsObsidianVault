 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Book of Poetry</i>, tr. by James Legge, [1876], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h4 align="center">V</h4>  
 <p></p><div style="font-size: 90%">The <i>Han Lu;</i> allusive and narrative. In praise of the virtue of King Wan, blessed by his ancestors, and raised to the highest dignity without seeking of his own.</div>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 20%; margin-right: 20%; font-size: 110%;"><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1">1</a></font></span>Round the foot of Mount Han<br>     Grow the hazel and thorn.<br> Self-possession and ease<br>     Did our monarch adorn.<br> Striving for his height of place,<br> These around him threw their grace.  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2">2</a></font></span>From the large cup of jade<br>     Fragrant spirits down flow.<br> Self-possession and ease<br>     Did our prince ever show.<br> Could there but on him descend<br> Rank and blessing without end? <a name="page_344"><font size="1" color="green">p. 344</font></a></p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_3">3</a></font></span>  Up to heaven flies the hawk;<br>     Fishes spring in the deep.<br>   Self-possession and ease<br>     Did our prince ever keep.<br> Grand the influence he shed,<br> As those virtues he displayed!</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_4">4</a></font></span>  In the vessel behold<br>     How his spirits shine clear!<br>   The red bull is ready,<br>     Nor the knife views with fear.<br> Thus he paid the sacred rite,<br> Brighter blessing to invite!</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_5">5</a></font></span>  Oaks and shrubs thickly grow,<br>     Which for firewood men hew.<br>   Self-possession and ease<br>     In our prince all could view.<br> Spirits cheered him in his course,<br> Nerving him with secret force.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_6">6</a></font></span>  How the creepers close twine<br>     Round the branches and stems!<br>   Self-possession and ease<br>     Robed our prince as with gems.<br> Happiness increased unsought,<br> Nor by crooked ways was bought.</p></div>
