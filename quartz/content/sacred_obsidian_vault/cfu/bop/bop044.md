 <p><a name="page_44"><font size="1" color="green">p. 44</font></a></p>  <h4 align="center">XVI</h4>  
 <p></p><div style="font-size: 90%">The <i>Pei Fêng;</i> metaphorical and narrative. Some one of Wei presses his friends to leave the country with him at once, in consequence of the prevailing oppression and misery.</div>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 20%; margin-right: 20%; font-size: 110%;"><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1">1</a></font></span>As when the north winds keenly blow,.<br> And all around fast falls the snow,<br> The source of pain and suffering great,<br> So now it is in Wei's poor state.<br>   Let us join hands and haste away,<br>     My friends and lovers all.<br>   ’Tis not a time will brook delay;<br>     Things for prompt action call.  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2">2</a></font></span>As when the north winds whistle shrill,<br> And drifting snows each hollow fill,<br> The source of pain and suffering great,<br> So now it is in Wei's poor state.<br>   Let us join hands, and leave for aye,<br>     My friends and lovers all,<br>   ’Tis not a time will brook delay;<br>     Things for prompt action call.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_3">3</a></font></span>We look for red, and foxes meet;<br> For black, and crows our vision greet.<br> The creatures, both of omen bad,<br> Well suit the state of Wei so sad. <a name="page_45"><font size="1" color="green">p. 45</font></a><br>   Let us join hands and mount our cars,<br>     My friends and lovers all.<br>   No time remains for wordy jars;<br>     Things for prompt action call.</p></div>
