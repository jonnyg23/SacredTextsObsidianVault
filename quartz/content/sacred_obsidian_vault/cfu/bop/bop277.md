 <p></p><div style="font-size: 90%">The <i>Wei Ch‘ing;</i> narrative. Appropriate at some sacrifice to King Wên, and celebrating his statutes.</div>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 20%; margin-right: 20%; font-size: 110%;">The statutes of King Wên are pure and clear;<br> Them we must guard, and in our lives revere. <a name="page_435"><font size="1" color="green">p. 435</font></a><br> Since first we sacrificed to him till now<br> When all the states obedience yield to Chou,<br> The fortune of our house comes from his laws,<br> Its happy omen first, and then its cause.</div>
