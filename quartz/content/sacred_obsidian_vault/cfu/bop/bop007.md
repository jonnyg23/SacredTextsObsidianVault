 <p><a name="page_5"><font size="1" color="green">p. 5</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">IV</h3>  
 <p></p><div style="font-size: 90%">The <i>Chiu Mu;</i> allusive. Celebrating T‘ai Ssŭ’s freedom from jealousy, and offering fervent wishes for her happiness.</div>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 20%; margin-right: 20%; font-size: 110%;"><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1">1</a></font></span>In the south are the trees whose branches are bent,<br> And droop in such fashion that o’er their extent<br>   All the dolichos’ creepers fast cling.<br> See <i>our</i> princely lady, from whom we have got<br> Rejoicing that's endless! May her happy lot<br>   And her honors repose ever bring!  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2">2</a></font></span>In the south are the trees whose branches are bent,<br> And droop in such fashion that o’er their extent<br>   All the dolichos’ creepers are spread.<br> See our princely lady, from whom we have got<br> Rejoicing that's endless! Of her happy lot<br>   And her honors the greatness ne’er fade!</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_3">3</a></font></span>In the south are the trees whose branches are bent,<br> And droop in such fashion that o’er their extent<br>   All the dolichos’ creepers entwine.<br> See our princely lady, from whom we have got<br> Rejoicing that's endless. May her happy lot<br>   And her honors complete ever shine!</p></div>
