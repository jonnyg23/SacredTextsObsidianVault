 <p></p><div style="font-size: 90%">The <i>T‘ien Tso;</i> narrative. Appropriate to a sacrifice to King Ta.</div>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 20%; margin-right: 20%; font-size: 110%;">’Twas Heaven that made the lofty hill,<br>   And there King Ta his work began,<br> Laboring the land all round to till.<br>   Then in tranquillity King Wên<br> Pursued the work, till that mount Ch'i,<br>   Rugged and craggy though it frowned,<br> Was reached by the smooth roads we see.<br>   Long be it by their offspring owned!</div>
