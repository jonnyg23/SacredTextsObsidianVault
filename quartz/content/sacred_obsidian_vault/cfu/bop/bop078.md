 <p><a name="page_85"><font size="1" color="green">p. 85</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center"><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Book VII. The Odes of Chêng</span></h3>  <h4 align="center">I</h4>  
 <p></p><div style="font-size: 90%">The <i>Tzŭ I;</i> narrative. The people of the capital express their admiration of, and regard for, Duke Wu of Chêng.</div>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 20%; margin-right: 20%; font-size: 110%;"><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1">1</a></font></span>The black robes well your form befit;<br>   When they are worn we'll make you. new.<br> Now for your court! oh! there we'll sit,<br>   And watch how you your duties do.<br> And when we to our homes repair,<br> We'll send to you our richest fare,<br> Such is the love to you we bear!  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2">2</a></font></span>Those robes well with your virtue match;<br>   When they are worn we'll make you new.<br> Now for your court! There will we watch,<br>   Well pleased, how you your duties do.<br> And when we to our homes repair,<br> We'll send to you our richest fare,<br> Such is the love to you we bear! <a name="page_86"><font size="1" color="green">p. 86</font></a></p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_3">3</a></font></span>Those robes your character beseem;<br>   When they are worn we'll make you new.<br> Now for your court! oh! there we deem<br>   It pleasure great your form to view.<br> And when we to our homes repair,<br> We'll send to you our richest fare,<br> Such is the love to you we bear!</p></div>
