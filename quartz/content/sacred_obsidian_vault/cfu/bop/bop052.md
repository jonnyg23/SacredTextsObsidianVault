 <p><a name="page_53"><font size="1" color="green">p. 53</font></a></p>  <h4 align="center">V</h4>  
 <p></p><div style="font-size: 90%">The <i>Shun Chih Pên Pên;</i> allusive. Against Hsüan Chiang and Chao Po, as worse than beasts.</div>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 20%; margin-right: 20%; font-size: 110%;"><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1">1</a></font></span>How bold the quails together rush,<br>   Each fighting for his mate!<br> How strong the magpies, battling fierce<br>   Upon the same debate!<br> This man, without a trait that's good,<br>   Is stained by vicious crime;<br> Yet him as brother I regard:—<br>   Alas! woe worth the time!  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2">2</a></font></span>How strong the magpies, battling fierce,<br>   Each one to keep his mate!<br> How fierce the quails together rush,<br>   Upon the same debate!<br> This woman, with no trait that's good,<br>   Is stained by vicious crime,<br> Yet her I hail as marchioness:—<br>   Alas! woe worth the time!</p></div>
