 <p><a name="page_58"><font size="1" color="green">p. 58</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">[30] RAIN</h3>  <h4 align="center">(A.D. <i>815</i>)</h4>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Since</span> I lived a stranger in the City of Hsün-yang<br> Hour by hour bitter rain has poured.<br> On few days has the dark sky cleared;<br> In listless sleep I have spent much time.<br> The lake has widened till it almost joins the sky;<br> The clouds sink till they touch the water's face.<br> Beyond my hedge I hear the boatmen's talk;<br> At the street-end I hear the fisher's song.<br> Misty birds are lost in yellow air;<br> Windy sails kick the white waves.<br> In front of my gate the horse and carriage-way<br> In a single night has turned into a river-bed.</p>
