 <p><a name="page_78"><font size="1" color="green">p. 78</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">[46] GOOD-BYE TO THE PEOPLE OF HANGCHOW</h3>  <h4 align="center">(A.D. <i>824</i>)</h4>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Elders</span> and officers line the returning road;<br> Wine and soup load the parting table.<br> I have not ruled you with the wisdom of Shao Kung; <a name="fr_30"></a><a href="#fn_28"><font size="1">1</font></a><br> What is the reason your tears should fall so fast?<br> My taxes were heavy, though many of the people were poor;<br> The farmers were hungry, for often their fields were dry.<br> All I did was to dam the water of the Lake <a name="fr_31"></a><a href="#fn_29"><font size="1">2</font></a><br> And help a little in a year when things were bad.</p>
