 <p><a name="page_31"><font size="1" color="green">p. 31</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">[8] SELF–ABANDONMENT</h3>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">I sat</span> drinking and did not notice the dusk,<br> Till falling petals filled the folds of my dress.<br> Drunken I rose and walked to the moonlit stream;<br> The birds were gone, and men also few.</p>
