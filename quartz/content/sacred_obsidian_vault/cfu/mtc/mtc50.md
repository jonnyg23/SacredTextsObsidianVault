 <p><a name="page_81"><font size="1" color="green">p. 81</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">[49] LOSING A SLAVE-GIRL</h3>  <h4 align="center"><i>(Date uncertain)</i></h4>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Around</span> my garden the little wall is low;<br> In the bailiff's lodge the lists are seldom checked.<br> I am ashamed to think we were not always kind;<br> I regret your labours, that will never be repaid.<br> The caged bird owes no allegiance;<br> The wind-tossed flower does not cling to the tree.</p>  
 <p>Where to-night she lies none can give us news;<br> Nor any knows, save the bright watching moon.</p>
