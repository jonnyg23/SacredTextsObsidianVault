 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">At dawn the crickets shrill, then cease their ’plain,<br> The dying candle flickers through my eaves;<br> Though windows bar the wild dust and the rain,<br> I hear the drip, drip, dripping on the broad banana leaves.</div>
