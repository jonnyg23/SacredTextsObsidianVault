 <p><a name="page_62"><font size="1" color="green">p. 62</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">TS‘UI HAO</h3>  <h4 align="center">A.D. 703-755</h4>  <h3 align="center">BOATING SONG OF THE YO EH</h3>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px"><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">O light</span> we glide through forest green,<br> By misty shore and gaunt ravine.<br>     And whether we tarry or drift along<br>     The clouds and the birds around us throng,<br> And mirrored mountains' nodding brows<br> Follow the wake of our flying prows.  
 <p>Now song returns from rock to rock;<br> Now soundless glades our silence mock.<br>     Sunbeam and shadow elves at play<br>     Beckon our wandering wills to stray.<br> Ah furl your sails! ah furl your sails!<br> The last wind down the valley fails.</p></div>
