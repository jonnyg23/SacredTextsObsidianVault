 <p><a name="page_71"><font size="1" color="green">p. 71</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">THE PAVILION OF ABOUNDING JOY</h3>  
 <p>Red trees, green hills in the sunset, and steppes of boundless grass.<br> O little the pilgrim reckons of the Spring about to pass.<br> In front of the Joy Pavilion, in the drift of scented showers<br> To and fro I come and go on a carpet of fallen flowers.</p>
