 <p><a name="page_76"><font size="1" color="green">p. 76</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">ON THE RIVER AT HUI-CH‘UNG</h3>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Beyond the twilight grove some sprays<br> Of peach-bloom charm the lingering days.<br> In spring, when first the waters warm,<br> The wild duck on the river swarm.<br> When artemisia lights the land<br> Young reeds break through the dappled sand.</div>
