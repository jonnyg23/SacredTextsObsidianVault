 <p><a name="page_94"><font size="1" color="green">p. 94</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">A CHALLENGE FROM THE MOON</h3>  
 <p>The moon leans mirrored on the dark guitar<br> As though she fears its cadences unheard<br> May lapse into the night. Oh! I am stirred<br> By some some rare tone afar<br> Caught from the drifting Palaces of Cold, <a name="fr_3"></a><a href="fol69.htm#fn_3"><font size="1">1</font></a><br> Where pale musicians through the moon-mists peer,<br> And challenged into song. Of waters rolled<br> Seaward I sing. Now clear<br> Now muffled in the wreathèd haze, now fall <br> My chords far strangled down the forest. All<br> My cares are centred in the strings, and I forget<br> That night and dawn on the long grey line have met.</p>
