 <p><a name="page_89"><font size="1" color="green">p. 89</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">CALYCANTHUS FLOWER</h3>  
 <p>Robed in pale yellow gown she leans apart,<br> Guarding her secret trust inviolate;<br> With mouth that, scarce unclosed, but faintly breathes<br> Its fragrance, like a tender grief remains<br> Half-told, half-treasured still. See how she droops<br> From delicate stem; while her close petals keep<br> Their shy demeanour. Think not that the fear<br> Of great cold winds can hinder her from bloom,<br> Who hides the rarest wonders of the spring<br> To vie with all the flowers of Chiang Nan.</p>
