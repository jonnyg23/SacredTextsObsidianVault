 <p><a name="page_83"><font size="1" color="green">p. 83</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">ANON</h3>  <h4 align="center"><i>(From the Sung Collection)</i></h4>  <h3 align="center">RIDING BY MOONLIGHT</h3>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px"><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">From</span> the tall hill-top some great star<br> Falls to the west afar and afar.  
 <p>Out of the glistening gorge below<br> The orient moon swims full and slow.</p>  
 <p>Hair dishevelled and sleeves blown wide,<br> Into the kind cool night I ride.</p>  
 <p>Faint winds free strange scents anew<br> Moon-paled maples bright with dew,</p>  
 <p>Dripping dreams from bough to bough<br> Sigh to my lute, Why sleepest thou?</p>  
 <p>Hands on the waiting strings fall mute.<br> Low my heart answers—"I am the lute."</p></div>
