 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>A Feast of Lanterns</i>, by L. Cranmer-Byng, [1916], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">ALONG THE STREAM</h3>  
 <p>The rustling nightfall strews my gown with roses,<br> And wine-flushed petals bring forgetfulness <br> Of shadow after shadow striding past.<br> I arise with the stars exultantly and follow<br> The sweep of the moon along the hushing stream,<br> Where no birds wake; only the far-drawn sigh<br> Of wary voices whispering farewell.</p>
