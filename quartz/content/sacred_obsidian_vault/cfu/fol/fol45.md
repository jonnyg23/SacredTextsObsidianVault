 <p><a name="page_75"><font size="1" color="green">p. 75</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">AT THE KUANG-LI PAVILION</h3>  
 <p>Red-skirted ladies, robed for fairyland, all have flown,<br> But my heart to the wail of their long reed-pipes lilts on:<br> Their clarion songs ’mid the wandering clouds were blown,<br> The tiny-waisted, dreamily-dancing girls are gone.</p>
