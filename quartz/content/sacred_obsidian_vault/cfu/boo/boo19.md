 <p><a name="page_34"><font size="1" color="green">p. 34</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center"><i>THE PEAR-TREE</i></h3>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">This</span> shade-bestowing pear-tree, thou<br> Hurt not, nor lay its leafage low;<br> Beneath it slept the Duke of Shaou.</p>  
 <p>This shade-bestowing pear-tree, thou<br> Hurt not, nor break one leafy bough;<br> Beneath it stayed the Duke of Shaou.</p>  
 <p>This shade-bestowing pear-tree, thou<br> Hurt not, nor bend one leafy bough;<br> Beneath it paused the Duke of Shaou.</p>
