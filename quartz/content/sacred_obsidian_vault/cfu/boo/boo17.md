 <p><a name="page_31"><font size="1" color="green">p. 31</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center"><i>THE STRAGGLER</i></h3>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">There</span> is that little oriole<br> At rest where the mound doth rise;<br> Oh, but the way is long,<br> Long that before me lies.<br> There is no rest for me,<br> None for my tired feet;<br> Give me to drink and eat,<br> Do what is best for me.<br> Order an ambulance car,<br> And carry me, carry me on.</p>  
 <p>There is that little oriole<br> At rest where the mound doth bend;<br> Oh, but I know no fear<br> Save if the march will end.<br> There is no rest for me,<br> None for my tired feet;<br> Give me to drink and eat,<br> Do what is best for me.<br> Order an ambulance car,<br> And carry me, carry me on.</p>  
 <p>There is that little oriole<br> At rest on the hillock grey;<br> Oh, but I know no fear<br> Save that I fall by the way.</p>
