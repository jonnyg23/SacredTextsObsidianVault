 <p><a name="page_18"><font size="1" color="green">p. 18</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center"><i>KING'S MESSENGER</i></h3>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Galloping</span>, galloping, gallant steed;<br> Six reins slackened and dull with sweat,<br> Galloping, galloping still we speed,<br> Seeking, counselling, onward set.</p>  
 <p>Galloping, galloping, piebald steed;<br> Six reins, silken reins, start and strain,<br> Galloping, galloping, still we speed,<br> News—what news—from the King's domain.</p>  
 <p>Galloping, galloping, white and black;<br> Six reins glossy and flaked with foam,<br> Galloping, galloping, look not back!<br> On for the King—for the King we roam.</p>  
 <p>Galloping, galloping, dappled grey;<br> Six reins true to the hand alone,<br> Galloping, galloping, night and day,<br> Seeking, questioning, galloping, gone!</p>
