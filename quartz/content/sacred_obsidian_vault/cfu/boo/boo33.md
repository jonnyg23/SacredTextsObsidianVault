 <p><a name="page_48"><font size="1" color="green">p. 48</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center"><i>THE BRIDE COMETH</i></h3>  
 <p><i><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">The</span> turtle-dove dwells in the magpie's nest.<br> </i>One cometh as a bride to be caressed;<br> A hundred carriages have gone in quest.</p>  
 <p><i>The magpie's home the young dove hath possessed</i>.<br> This lady cometh as a life-long guest;<br> A hundred chariots on the road have pressed.</p>  
 <p><i>The turtle-dove shall fill the magpie's nest.<br> </i>She travels far from home to love and rest;<br> A hundred carriages her rank attest.</p>
