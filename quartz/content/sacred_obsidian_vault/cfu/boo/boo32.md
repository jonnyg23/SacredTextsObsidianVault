 <p><a name="page_47"><font size="1" color="green">p. 47</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center"><i>BULWARKS OF EMPIRE</i></h3>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Good</span> men are bulwarks; while the multitudes<br> Are walls that ring the land; great states are screens;<br> Each family a buttress; the pursuit<br> Of righteousness secures repose; like towers<br> Of strong defence the royal kinsmen stand<br> Immune from peril. May they still remain<br> Nor leave the king, a lonely citadel<br> Abandoned to his enemies.</p>  
 <p>                              Give heed<br> Unto the wrath of Heaven! nor presume<br> To idle; but revere the Heavenly moods,<br> Ephemeral though they seem. Be not of those<br> That roam at random. Heaven understands<br> And doth companion all the ways we go,<br> And seeth all things clearly…</p>
