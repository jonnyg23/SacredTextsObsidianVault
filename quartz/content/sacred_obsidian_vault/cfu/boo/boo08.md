 <p><a name="page_21"><font size="1" color="green">p. 21</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center"><i>DRIFTING</i></h3>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Two</span> youths into their boats descend,<br> Whose shadows on the waters sway;<br> Ah! light hearts bravely sped away,<br> My heavy heart forbodes the end.</p>  
 <p>Two youths into their boats descend,<br> Two lives go drifting far from me;<br> Between the willow glooms I see<br> Death lurking at the river's bend.</p>
