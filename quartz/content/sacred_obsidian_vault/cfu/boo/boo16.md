 <p><a name="page_30"><font size="1" color="green">p. 30</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center"><i>THROUGH EASTERN GATES</i></h3>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Through</span> eastern gates I wandered far,<br> Where cloud-like beauties thronged the way;<br> Although like clouds their faces are,<br> My thoughts among them would not stay.<br> She in rough silk and kerchief blue<br> Gave me the only joy I knew.</p>  
 <p>I wandered by the curtain tower,<br> Like flowering rushes were the maids;<br> Although they match the rushes' flower,<br> Soon from my mind their beauty fades.<br> In humble silk and madder dye,<br> She fills my heart with ecstasy.</p>
