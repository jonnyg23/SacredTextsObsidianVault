 <p>
 This is M.A. Czaplicka's review of the literature about Shamanism
 in Siberia, excerpted from a longer ethnographic work.
 Siberian Shamanism is important topic because
 the practice continued there up until historic times, and so there
 are reliable modern accounts.
 The details of the shamanistic journey appear to be largely consistent
 throughout this culture region.
 This extended description contains important source material on Shamanism.
 </p>
