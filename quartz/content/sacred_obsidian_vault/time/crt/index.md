 <p>
 This is a study of the origin of modern Christmas in ancient pagan
 traditions, how the festival was adopted by Christianity,
 and its evolution through history.
 Miles includes descriptions of folk-lore related to
 the Christmas season from a wide range of European countries,
 including an extensive collection of folk songs.
 It is important to learn about these traditions to remind ourselves
 that before Christmas became a hyper-commercialized festival of
 consumption, it was a joyous celebration of renewal and friendship.
 </p>
