 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Fifty Christmas Poems for Children</i>, by Florence B. Hyett, [1923], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">THE HOLLY</h3>  
 <p>HOW happy the holly-tree looks, and how strong,<br> Where he stands like a sentinel all the year long.</p>  
 <p>Neither dry summer heat nor cold winter hail<br> Can make that gay warrior tremble or quail.</p>  
 <p>He has beamed all the year, but bright scarlet he'll glow<br> When the ground glitters white with the fresh fallen snow.</p>  
 <p>--EDITH KING</p>
