 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Fifty Christmas Poems for Children</i>, by Florence B. Hyett, [1923], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">THE BIRDS</h3>  
 <p>WHEN Jesus Christ was four years old,<br> The angels brought Him toys of gold,<br> Which no man ever had bought or sold.</p>  
 <p>And yet with these He would not play,<br> He made Him small fowl out of clay,<br> And blessed them till they flew away:<br> Tu Creasti Domine.</p>  
 <p>Jesus Christ, Thou child so wise,<br> Bless mine hands and fill mine eyes,<br> And bring my soul to Paradise.</p>  
 <p>--HILAIRE BELLOC</p>
