 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Fifty Christmas Poems for Children</i>, by Florence B. Hyett, [1923], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">THE CHRISTMAS CHILD</h3>  
 <p>LITTLE one, who straight hast come<br> Down the heavenly stair,<br> Tell us all about your home,<br> And the father there."</p>  
 <p>"He is such a one as I<br> Like as like can be.<br> Do his will, and, by and by,<br> Home and him you'll see."</p>  
 <p>--GEORGE MACDONALD</p>
