 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Fifty Christmas Poems for Children</i>, by Florence B. Hyett, [1923], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">SHEPHERD'S SONG</h3>  
 <p>AS I rode out this enderes' night,<br> Of three jolly shepherds I saw a sight<br> And all about their fold a star shone bright;<br> They sang, Terli, terlow;<br> So merrily the shepherds their pipes can blow.</p>  
 <p>Down from heaven, from heaven so high,<br> Of angels there came a great company.<br> With mirth, and joy, and great solemnity<br> They sang, Terli, terlow;<br> So merrily the shepherds their pipes can blow.</p>  
 <p>--OLD SONG</p>
