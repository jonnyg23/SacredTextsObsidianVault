 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Hymns of the Atharva Veda</i>, by Ralph T.H. Griffith, [1895], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">HYMN X</h3>  <h4 align="center">A prayer for the same</h4>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1901001">1</a></font></span>Befriend us with their aids Indra and Agni, Indra and Varuna<br>    who receive oblations!<br>   Indra and Soma give health, wealth and comfort, Indra and<br>   Pūshan be our strength in battle!<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1901002">2</a></font></span>Auspicious friends to us be Bhaga. Sansa, auspicious be Pur-<br>    andhi and all Riches,<br>   The blessing of the true and well-conducted and Aryaman in<br>    many forms apparent.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1901003">3</a></font></span>Kind unto us be Maker and Sustainer and the far-reaching<br>   One with godlike nature.<br>   Auspicious unto us be Earth and Heaven, the Mountain and the<br>   Gods' fair invocations. <a name="page_221"><font size="1" color="green">p. 221</font></a><br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1901004">4</a></font></span>Favour us Agni with his face of splendour, and Varuna and'<br>   Mitra and the Asvins.<br>   Favour us noble actions of the pious; impetuous Vāta blow on,<br>    us with favour!<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1901005">5</a></font></span>Early invoked may Heaven and Earth be friendly, and Air's<br>    mid-region good for us to look on.<br>   To us may herbs and forest trees be gracious, gracious the Lord<br>    victorious of the region.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1901006">6</a></font></span>Be the God Indra with the Vasus friendly, and with Ādityas<br>   Varuna who blesseth.<br>   Kind with the Rudras be the healer Rudra, and with the Dames<br>    here Tvashtar kindly hear us.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1901007">7</a></font></span>Kind unto us be Soma and Devotions, kind be the sacrifice and<br>   Stones for pressing.<br>   Kind be the fixing of the Sacred Pillars, kind be the tender<br>   Grass, and kind the Altar.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1901008">8</a></font></span>May the far-seeing Sun rise up to bless us: be the four quarters-<br>    of the heaven auspicious.<br>   Auspicious be the firmly-seated Mountains, auspicious be the<br>   Rivers and the Waters.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1901009">9</a></font></span>May Aditi through holy works be gracious, and may the Maruts,_<br>    loud in song, be friendly.<br>   May Vishnu give felicity, and, Pūshan, and Air that cherisheth.<br>    our lives, and Vāyu.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1901010">10</a></font></span>Prosper us Savitar, the God who rescues, and let the radiant.<br>   Mornings be propitious.<br>   Propitious to our children be Pārjanya, kind to us be the field's<br>    benign Protector!</p>
