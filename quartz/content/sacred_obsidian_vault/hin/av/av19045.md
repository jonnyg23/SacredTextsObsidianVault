 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Hymns of the Atharva Veda</i>, by Ralph T.H. Griffith, [1895], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">HYMN XLV</h3>  <h4 align="center">A curative and protective charm</h4>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1904501">1</a></font></span>As debt from debt repay and send sorcery to the sorcerer's<br>    house.<br>   Split, Salve! the cruel villain's ribs whose evil eye bewitches us.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1904502">2</a></font></span>Whatever evil dream we have, what'er befall our kine or home,<br>   Be this that is salubrity, the evil-hearted's foe applied.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1904503">3</a></font></span>Increasing from the Waters' strength and vigour, sprung into life<br>    from Agni Jātavedas,<br>   Strong as four heroes, mountain born, this Ointment make for<br>    thee quarters and mind-points auspicious!<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1904504">4</a></font></span>On thee is laid the Chaturvira Ointment: let all the regions give<br>    thee peace and safety.<br>   Secure like precious Savitar thou standest: to thee let all these<br>    regions bring their tribute.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1904505">5</a></font></span>Make one thy salve, thine amulet another, drink one, and with<br>    another bathe thy body.<br>   So let the Chaturvira keep us guarded from the four bonds of<br>   Nirriti and Grāhi.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1904506">6</a></font></span>May Agni protect me with fire for inspiration and expiration,<br>    for strength, for energy, for vigour, for weal and prosperity.<br>   All Hail!<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1904507">7</a></font></span>May Indra protect me with his Indra-power for inspiration, etc. <a name="page_248"><font size="1" color="green">p. 248</font></a><br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1904508">8</a></font></span>May Soma protect me with Soma-power, etc.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1904509">9</a></font></span>May Bhaga with good fortune protect me, etc.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1904510">10</a></font></span>May the Maruts protect me with their troops for inspiration<br>    and expiration, for strength, for energy, for vigour, for weal<br>    and prosperity. All Hail!</p>
