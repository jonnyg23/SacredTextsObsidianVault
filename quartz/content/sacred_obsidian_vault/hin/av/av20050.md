 <p><a name="page_307"><font size="1" color="green">p. 307</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">HYMN L</h3>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2005001">1</a></font></span>What newest of imploring hymns shall, then, the zealous mortal<br>    sing?<br>   For have not they who laud his might and Indra-power won for<br>    themselves the light of heaven?<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2005002">2</a></font></span>When shall they keep the Law and praise thee mid the Gods?<br>   Who counts as Rishi and as sage?<br>   When wilt thou ever, Indra, Bounteous Lord, come nigh to<br>    presser's or to praiser's call?</p>
