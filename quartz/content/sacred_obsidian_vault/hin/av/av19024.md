 <p><a name="page_231"><font size="1" color="green">p. 231</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">HYMN XXIV</h3>  <h4 align="center">A benediction on a newly elected King</h4>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1902401">1</a></font></span>Do ye, O Brāhmanaspati, invest for the royal sway this man.<br>   With that wherewith the Deities invested Savitar the God.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1902402">2</a></font></span>Invest this Indra for long life, invest him for great princely<br>    power,<br>   That I may lead him on to eld, that the man watch his<br>    princedom long.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1902403">3</a></font></span>Invest this Soma for long life, invest him for great hearing<br>    power.<br>   That I may lead him on to eld, that he may watch o'er hearing<br>    long.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1902404">4</a></font></span>For us, surround him, cover him with splendour, give him long<br>    life, and death when age removes him.<br>   This garment hath Brihaspati presented to Soma, to the King,<br>    to wrap about him.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1902405">5</a></font></span>Advance to good old age: endue the mantle. Be thou our<br>    heifers' guard from imprecation.<br>   Live thou a hundred full and plenteous autumns, and wrap thee<br>    in prosperity of riches.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1902406">6</a></font></span>Thou for weal hast clothed thee in this garment: thou hast<br>    become our cows' sure guard from curses.<br>   Live thou a hundred full and plenteous autumns: then living,<br>    fair thyself, shalt deal forth treasures.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1902407">7</a></font></span>In every need, in every fray we call, as friends, to succour us,<br>   Indra the mightiest of all.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1902408">8</a></font></span>Gold-coloured, undecaying, blest with heroes, dwell, dying in<br>    old age, with children round thee.<br>   This is the spoken word of Agni, Soma, Brihaspati and Savitar,<br>    and Indra.</p>
