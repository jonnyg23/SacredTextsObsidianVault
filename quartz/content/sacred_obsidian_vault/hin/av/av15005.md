 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Hymns of the Atharva Veda</i>, by Ralph T.H. Griffith, [1895], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">HYMN V</h3>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1500501">1</a></font></span>For him they made the Archer Bhava a deliverer from the inter-<br>    mediate space of the eastern region. Bhava the Archer, a<br>    deliverer, delivers him from the intermediate space of the<br>    eastern region. Neither Sarva nor Bhava nor Isāna slays him<br>    who possesses this knowledge, or his cattle, or his kinsmen.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1500502">2</a></font></span>They made Sarva the Archer his deliverer from the intermediate<br>    space of the southern region, etc, as in verse 1.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1500503">3</a></font></span>They made Pasupati the Archer his deliverer from the inter-<br>    mediate space of the western region, etc.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1500504">4</a></font></span>They made the Awful God, the Archer, his deliverer from the<br>    intermediate space of the northern region, etc, as above.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1500505">5</a></font></span>They made Rudra the Archer his deliverer from the intermediate<br>    space of the region of the nadir etc.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1500506">6</a></font></span>They made Mahādeva his deliverer from the intermediate space<br>    of the region of the zenith, etc.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1500507">7</a></font></span>They made Isana the Archer his deliverer from all the inter-<br>    mediate regions. Isāna the Archer, a deliverer, delivers him<br>    from all the intermediate regions. Neither Sarva nor Bhava,<br>    nor Isana slays him who possesses this knowledge, or his cattle,<br>    or his kinsmen.</p>
