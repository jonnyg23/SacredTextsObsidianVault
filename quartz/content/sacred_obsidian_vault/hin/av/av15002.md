 <p><a name="page_150"><font size="1" color="green">p. 150</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">HYMN II</h3>  <h4 align="center">The same, continued</h4>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1500201">1</a></font></span>He arose and went his way to the eastern region. The Brihat,<br>    the Rathantara, the Ādityas and all the Gods followed him.<br>   That man is alienated from the Brihat, the Rathantara, the<br>   Ādityas, and all Gods who reviles the Vrātya who possesses<br>    this knowledge. He who hath this knowledge becomes the<br>    beloved home of the Brihat, the Rathantara the Ādityas, and<br>    all the Gods. In the eastern region Faith is his leman, the<br>    hymn his panegyrist, knowledge his vesture, day his turban,<br>    night his hair, Indra's two Bays his circular ornaments, the<br>    splendour of the stars his jewel. Present and Future are his<br>    running footmen, mind is his war-chariot, Mātarisvan and<br>   Pavamāna are they who draw it, Vita is his charioteer, Storm<br>    his goad, Fame and Glory are his harbingers. Fame and<br>   Glory come to him who hath this knowledge.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1500202">2</a></font></span>He arose and went away to the southern region. Yajnāyajniya<br>    and Vāmadevya and Sacrifice and Sacrificer and sacrificial<br>    victims followed him. The man who reviles the Vrātya<br>    possessing this knowledge is alienated from Yajnāyajniya and<br>   Vāmadevya, Sacrifice, Sacrificer and sacrificial victims. He<br>    who hath this knowledge becomes the beloved home of<br>   Yajnāyajniya,Vāmadevya, Sacrifice, Sacrificer, and sacrificial<br>    victims. In the southern region Dawn is his leman, Mitra his<br>    panegyrist, knowledge his vesture, day his turban, night his<br>    hair, Indra's two Bays are his circular ornaments, New Moon<br>   Night and Full Moon Night are his running attendants, Mind,<br>    etc. as in stanza 1.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1500203">3</a></font></span>He arose and went away to the western region. Vairūpa and<br>   Vairāja, the Waters, and King Varuna followed him. He who<br>    reviles the Vrātya possessing this knowledge is alienated from<br>   Vairūpa and Vairāja, the Waters and Varuna the King. He<br>    who possesses this knowledge becomes the dear home of<br>   Vairūpa and Vairāja, the Waters and King Varuna. In the <a name="page_151"><font size="1" color="green">p. 151</font></a><br>    western region Irā is his leman, Laughter his panegyrist,<br>    knowledge, etc., as above. Day and Night are his running<br>    attendants, Mind, etc., as above.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1500204">4</a></font></span>He arose and went away to the northern region. Syaita and<br>   Naudhasa, the Seven Rishis, and King Soma followed him.<br>   He who reviles the Vrātya possessing this knowledge is<br>    alienated from Syaita, etc. He who hath this knowledge be-<br>    comes the dear home of Syaita, etc. In the northern region<br>   Lightning is his leman, thunder his panegyrist, etc. as above.<br>   Revelation and Tradition are his running attendants, Mind,<br>    etc., as above.</p>
