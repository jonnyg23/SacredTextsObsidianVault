 <p><a name="page_375"><font size="1" color="green">p. 375</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">HYMN CXXXVIII</h3>  <h4 align="center">In praise of Indra</h4>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2013801">1</a></font></span>Indra, great in his power and might and, like Parjanya, rich in<br>    rain,<br>   Is magnified by Vatsa's lauds,<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2013802">2</a></font></span>When the priests, strengthening the Son of holy Law, present<br>    their gifts,<br>   Singers with Order's hymn of praise.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2013803">3</a></font></span>Since Kanvas with their lauds have made Indra complete the<br>    sacrifice,<br>   Words are their own appropriate arms.</p>
