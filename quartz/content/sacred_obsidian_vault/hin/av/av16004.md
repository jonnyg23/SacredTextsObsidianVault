 <p><a name="page_165"><font size="1" color="green">p. 165</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">HYMN IV</h3>  <h4 align="center">A charm to secure long life and success</h4>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1600401">1</a></font></span>I am the:centre of riches. Fain would I be the centre of mine<br>    equals.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1600402">2</a></font></span>Pleasant art thou to sit by one, a mother: immortal among<br>    mortals.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1600403">3</a></font></span>Let not inward breath desert me; let not outward breath depart<br>    and leave me.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1600404">4</a></font></span>Let Sūrya protect me from Day, Agni from Earth, Vāyu from<br>   Firmament, Yama from men, Sarasvatī from dwellers on the<br>    earth.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1600405">5</a></font></span>Let not outward and inward breath fail me. Be not thou destruc-<br>    tive among the men.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1600406">6</a></font></span>Propitious to-day be dawns and evenings. May I drink water with<br>    all my people safe around me.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1600407">7</a></font></span>Mighty are ye, domestic creatures. May Mitra-Varuna stand<br>    beside me. May Agni give me inward and outward breath.<br>   May,he give me ability.</p>
