 <p><a name="page_320"><font size="1" color="green">p. 320</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">HYMN LXX</h3>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2007001">1</a></font></span>Thou, Indra, with the Tempest-Gods, the breakers down of<br>    what is firm,<br>   Foundest the kine even in the cave.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2007002">2</a></font></span>Worshipping even as they list, singers laud him who findeth<br>    wealth,<br>   The far-renowned, the mighty One.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2007003">3</a></font></span>Then, faring on by Indra's side, the fearless, let thyself be seen,.<br>   Both gracious and in splendour peers.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2007004">4</a></font></span>With Indra's well-beloved hosts, the blameless, tending heaven-<br>    ward,<br>   The sacrificer cries aloud.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2007005">5</a></font></span>Come from this place, O wanderer, or downward from the light<br>    of heaven!<br>   Our songs of praise all yearn for this.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2007006">6</a></font></span>Or Indra we implore for help from here, from heaven above the<br>    earth,<br>   Or from the spacious firmament.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2007007">7</a></font></span>Indra the singers with high praise, Indra reciters with their<br>    lauds,<br>   Indra the choirs have glorified.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2007008">8</a></font></span>Indra hath ever close to him his two bay steeds and word-yoked<br>    car,<br>   Indra the golden, Thunder-armed.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2007009">9</a></font></span>Indra hath raisedjthe Sun on high in heaven, that he may see<br>    afar:<br>   He burst the mountain for the kine.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2007010">10</a></font></span>Help us, O Indra, in the frays, yea, frays where thousand spoils<br>    are gained,<br>   With awful aids, O awful One.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2007011">11</a></font></span>In mighty battle we invoke, Indra, Indra in lesser fight,<br>   The friend who bends his bolt at fiends.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2007012">12</a></font></span>Unclose, our manly Hero, thou for ever bounteous, yonder<br>    cloud,<br>   For us, thou irresistible.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2007013">13</a></font></span>Still higher, at each strain of mine, thunder-armed Indra's,<br>    praises rise:<br>   I find no laud worthy of him. <a name="page_321"><font size="1" color="green">p. 321</font></a><br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2007014">14</a></font></span>Even as the bull drives on the herds, he drives the people with<br>    his might,<br>   The ruler irresistible:<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2007015">15</a></font></span>Indra who rules with single sway men, riches, and the fivefold<br>    race.<br>   Of those who dwell upon the earth.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2007016">16</a></font></span>For your sake from each side we call Indra away from other<br>    men:<br>   Ours, and none others', may he be.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2007017">17</a></font></span>Indra, bring wealth that gives delight, the victor's ever-conquer-<br>    ing wealth,<br>   Most excellent, to be our aid;<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2007018">18</a></font></span>By means of which we may repel our foes in battle hand to<br>    hand.<br>   By thee assisted with the car.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2007019">19</a></font></span>Aided by thee, the Thunder-armed, Indra; may we lit up the<br>    bolt,<br>   And conquer all our foes in fight.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2007020">20</a></font></span>With thee, O Indra, for ally, with missile-darting heroes may<br>   We conquer our embattled foes.</p>
