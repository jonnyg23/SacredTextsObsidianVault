 <p><a name="page_269"><font size="1" color="green">p. 269</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">HYMN VIII</h3>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2000801">1</a></font></span>Drink as of old, and let the draught delight thee: hear thou my<br>    prayer and let our songs exalt thee.<br>   Make the Sun visible, make food abundant: slaughter the foes,.<br>    pierce through and free the cattle.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2000802">2</a></font></span>Come to us; they have called thee Soma-lover. Here is the<br>    pressed juice: drink thereof for rapture.<br>   Widely-capacious, pour it down within thee, and invocated hear<br>    us like a father.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2000803">3</a></font></span>Full is his chalice. Blessing! Like a pourer I have filled up the<br>    vessel for his drinking.<br>   Presented on the right, dear Soma juices have brought us Indra,.<br>    to rejoice him, hither.</p>
