 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Hymns of the Atharva Veda</i>, by Ralph T.H. Griffith, [1895], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">HYMN LXXI</h3>  <h4 align="center">A hymn, accompanying libations, for wealth and prosperity</h4>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1907101">1</a></font></span>Let my libations, giving boons, adoring, further the Twice-born's<br>    song that honours Soma.<br>   Go ye to Brahma's world having enriched me with life and<br>    breath, with children and with cattle, with fame and wealth,<br>    and with a Brāhman's lustre.</p>
