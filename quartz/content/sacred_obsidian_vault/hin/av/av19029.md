 <p><a name="page_235"><font size="1" color="green">p. 235</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">HYMN XXIX</h3>  <h4 align="center">A charm for the destruction of enemies, continued from 28</h4>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1902901">1</a></font></span>Pierce thou my rivals, Darbha, pierce the men who fain would<br>    fight with me.<br>   Pierce all who wish me evil, pierce the men who hate me,.<br>   Amulet!<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1902902">2</a></font></span>Split thou my rivals, Darbha, etc. (as in 1, with 'split' for<br>   'pierce' throughout).<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1902903">3</a></font></span>Check thou, etc.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1902904">4</a></font></span>Crush thou, etc.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1902905">5</a></font></span>Shake thou, etc.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1902906">6</a></font></span>Bruise thou, etc.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1902907">7</a></font></span>Burn thou, etc.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1902908">8</a></font></span>Consume, etc.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1902909">9</a></font></span>Slay thou my rivals. Darbha, slay the men who fain would fight<br>    with me.<br>   Slay all who wish me evil, slay the men who hate me, Amulet.</p>
