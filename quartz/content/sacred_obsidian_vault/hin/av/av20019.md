 <p><a name="page_279"><font size="1" color="green">p. 279</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">HYMN XIX</h3>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2001901">1</a></font></span>O Indra, for the strength that slays the foe and conquers in the<br>    fight<br>   We turn thee hitherward to us.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2001902">2</a></font></span>O Indra, Lord of Hundred Powers, may those who praise thee<br>    hitherward.<br>   Direct thy spirit and thine eye.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2001903">3</a></font></span>O Indra, Lord of Hundred Powers, with all our songs we<br>    invocate.<br>   Thy names for triumph over foes.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2001904">4</a></font></span>We strive for glory through the powers immense of him whom<br>    many praise,<br>   Of Indra who supports mankind.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2001905">5</a></font></span>For the foe's slaughter I address Indra whom many invocate,<br>   To win us booty in the wars.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2001906">6</a></font></span>In battles be victorious. We seek thee, Lord of Hundred Powers,<br>   O Indra, that the foe may fall.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2001907">7</a></font></span>In splendid combats of the hosts, in glories where the fight is<br>    won,<br>   Indra, be victor over foes.</p>
