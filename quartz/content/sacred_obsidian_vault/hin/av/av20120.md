 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2012101">1</a></font></span>Though, Indra, thou art called by men eastward and westward,<br>    north and south, <a name="page_357"><font size="1" color="green">p. 357</font></a><br>   Thou chiefly art with Anava and Turvasa, brave Champion!<br>    urged by men to come.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2011802">2</a></font></span>Or, Indra, when with Ruma, Rusama, Syāvaka, and Kripa thou<br>    rejoicest thee,<br>   Still do the Kanvas bring praises, with their prayers, O Indra,<br>    draw thee hither: come.</p>
