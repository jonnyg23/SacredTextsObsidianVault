 <p><a name="page_270"><font size="1" color="green">p. 270</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">HYMN X</h3>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2001001">1</a></font></span>These songs of ours exceeding sweet, these hymns of praise as-<br>    cend to thee,<br>   Like ever-conquering chariots that display their strength, gain<br>    wealth and give unfailing aid.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2001002">2</a></font></span>The Bhrigus are like suns, like Kanvas, and have gained all that<br>    their thoughts were bent to win.<br>   The living men of Priyamedha's race have sung exalting Indra<br>    with their lauds.</p>
