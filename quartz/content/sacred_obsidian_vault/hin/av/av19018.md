 <p><a name="page_227"><font size="1" color="green">p. 227</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">HYMN XVIII</h3>  <h4 align="center">A prayer for security and peace on all sides</h4>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1901801">1</a></font></span>Let those who vex me from the eastern region, sinners, praise<br>   Agni followed by the Vasus.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1901802">2</a></font></span>Let those who vex me from this quarter, sinners, praise Vāyu<br>    compassed by the Air's mid region.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1901803">3</a></font></span>Let those who vex me from the southern quarter, sinners, sing<br>    praise to Soma with the Rudras.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1901804">4</a></font></span>Let those who vex me from this quarter, sinners, praise Varuna<br>    connected with Ādityas.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1901805">5</a></font></span>Let those who vex me from the western quarter, sinners, praise<br>   Sūrya linked with Earth and Heaven.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1901806">6</a></font></span>Let those who vex me from this quarter, sinners give praise to<br>    plants' associates the Waters.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1901807">7</a></font></span>Let those who from the north side vex me, sinners, praise<br>   Visvakarman with the Seven Rishis.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1901808">8</a></font></span>Let those who vex me from this quarter, sinners, praise Indra<br>    with the Marut host about him.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1901809">9</a></font></span>Let those who vex me from the nadir, sinners, extol Prajāpati<br>    of genial power.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1901810">10</a></font></span>Let those who vex me from the zenith, sinners, extol Brihaspati<br>    with the Visve Devas.</p>
