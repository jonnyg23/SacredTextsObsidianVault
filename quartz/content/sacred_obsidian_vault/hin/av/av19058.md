 <p><a name="page_259"><font size="1" color="green">p. 259</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">HYMN LVIII</h3>  <h4 align="center">A prayer for prosperity, accompanying a sacrifice</h4>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1905801">1</a></font></span>Still equal be the flow of butter ever causing the Year to prosper<br>    with oblation.<br>   Still be our hearing, sight, and breath uninjured: let us lose<br>    nothing of our life and vigour.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1905802">2</a></font></span>Let lively breath invite us: we call vital breath to visit us.<br>   Earth and air's middle realm have gathered, Soma, Brihaspati<br>    and Dhartar gathered vigour.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1905803">3</a></font></span>The earth and heaven have come to be two gatherers up of vigo-<br>    rous might.<br>   So let us gather vigour up and closely follow after Earth.<br>   With glory come the cows and stand beside the master of the<br>    herd. Let us when we have gathered fame and glory closely<br>    follow Earth.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1905804">4</a></font></span>Prepare the cow-stall, for there drink your heroes: stitch ye the<br>    coats of armour wide and many.<br>   Make iron forts defying all assailants: let not your pitcher leak;<br>    stay it securely.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1905805">5</a></font></span>The eye of sacrifice, source and beginning with voice, car, spirit<br>    unto him I offer.<br>   To this our sacrifice, wrought by Visvakarman, may the Gods<br>    come gracious and kindly-hearted.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1905806">6</a></font></span>Let the Gods' Priests and those who merit worship, to whom<br>    oblation as their share is offered,<br>   Come to this holy service with their Consorts, and all Gods<br>    revel in the food we bring them.</p>
