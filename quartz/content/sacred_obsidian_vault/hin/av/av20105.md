 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2010501">1</a></font></span>Thou in the battles, Indra, art subduer of all hostile bands.<br>   Father art thou, all-conquering, cancelling the curse, thou victor<br>    of the vanquisher.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2010502">2</a></font></span>The earth, and heaven cling close to thy victorious might, as sire<br>    and mother to their child.<br>   When thou attackest Vritra all the hostile bands shrink and<br>    faint, Indra at thy wrath.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2010503">3</a></font></span>Bring to your aid the Eternal One, who shoots and none may<br>    shoot at him,<br>   Inciter, swift, victorious, best of charioteers, Tugrya's unvan-<br>    quished strengthener;</p>
