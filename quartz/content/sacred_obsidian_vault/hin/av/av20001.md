 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2000101">1</a></font></span>Thee, Indra, we invoke, the Bull, what time the Soma hath been<br>    pressed.<br>   Drink of the sweetly-flavoured juice.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2000102">2</a></font></span>The best of guardian hath the man within whose dwelling-place<br>    ye drink,<br>   O Maruts, giants of the sky.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2000103">3</a></font></span>Let us serve Agni with our hyms, Sage who consumeth ox and<br>    cow,<br>   Who beareth Soma on his back.</p>
