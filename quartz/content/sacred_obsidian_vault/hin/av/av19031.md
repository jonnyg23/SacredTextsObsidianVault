 <p><a name="page_236"><font size="1" color="green">p. 236</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">HYMN XXXI</h3>  <h4 align="center">A charm to ensure general prosperity, accompanying self-investiture with an amulet of Udumbara</h4>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1903101">1</a></font></span>Savitar make all cattle grow and prosper in my stable with.<br>   Amulet of Udumbara, helper of him who longs for wealth!<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1903102">2</a></font></span>May he who was our Household Fire, the ruler of our cattle,<br>    strong.<br>   Amulet of Udumbara endow us with prosperity.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1903103">3</a></font></span>By power of the Udumbara Charm may Dhātar give me plenty,<br>    rich.<br>   In the kine's droppings and in fruit, and, in our dwelling, food,<br>    and drink.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1903104">4</a></font></span>I win great plenty, while I wear the Amulet of Udumbara.<br>   Of quadrupeds and bipeds, of juices and food of every sort.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1903105">5</a></font></span>I have obtained abundant wealth of cattle, bipeds and quad-<br>    rupeds, and corn in plenty.<br>   Savitar and Brihaspati vouchsafe me the milk of kine and herbs'<br>    refreshing juices!<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1903106">6</a></font></span>Fain would I be the lord of herds of cattle: may he who rules<br>    o'er riches send me riches!<br>   May the Udumbara Amulet vouchsafe possessions unto me.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1903107">7</a></font></span>To me with wealth and children come the Amulet of Udumbara.<br>   With splendour come the Amulet hastened by Indra on its way!<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1903108">8</a></font></span>Divine, foe-quelling Amulet, wealth-winner for the gain of<br>    wealth—<br>   May it give store of beasts and food and cause our kine to<br>    multiply.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1903109">9</a></font></span>As thou, O Forest Tree, wast born with increase when thy life<br>    began,<br>   So let Sarasvati bestow abundant growth of wealth on me.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1903110">10</a></font></span>Sarasvati vouchsafe me wealth, household prosperity, and corn!<br>   Let Sinivāli bring them, and this Amulet of Udumbara.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1903111">11</a></font></span>The Lord of amulets art thou, most mighty: in thee wealth's<br>    ruler hath engendered riches,<br>   These gains are lodged in thee, and all great treasures. Amulet,<br>    conquer thou: far from us banish malignity and indigence,<br>    and hunger. <a name="page_237"><font size="1" color="green">p. 237</font></a><br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1903112">12</a></font></span>Vigour art thou, in me do thou plant vigour: riches art thou, so<br>    do thou grant me riches.<br>   Plenty art thou, so prosper me with plenty: House-holder, hear"<br>    a householder's petition.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1903113">13</a></font></span>Amulet of Udumbara, enrich us: vouchsafe us wealth with all<br>    good men about us. I bind thee on for increase of possessions.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1903114">14</a></font></span>For hero is this hero bound, Amulet of Udumbara.<br>   So may he make our offering sweetly-savoured, and grant us<br>    wealth with all good men about us.</p>
