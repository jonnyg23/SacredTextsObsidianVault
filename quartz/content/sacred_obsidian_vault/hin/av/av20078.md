 <p><a name="page_329"><font size="1" color="green">p. 329</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">HYMN LXXVIII</h3>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2007801">1</a></font></span>Sing this, what time the juice is pressed, to him your Hero<br>    much-invoked,<br>   To please him as a mighty, Bull.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2007802">2</a></font></span>He, excellent, withholdeth not his gift of power and wealth in<br>    kine,<br>   When he hath listened to our songs.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2007803">3</a></font></span>May he with might disclose for us the cows' stall, whosesoe'er<br>    it be,<br>   To which the Dasyu-slayer goes.</p>
