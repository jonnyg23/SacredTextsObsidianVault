 <p><a name="page_318"><font size="1" color="green">p. 318</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">HYMN LXVIII</h3>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2006804">4</a></font></span>Go to the wise unconquered One, ask thou of Indra, skilled in<br>    song,<br>   Him who is better than thy friends.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2006805">5</a></font></span>Whether the men who mock us say, Depart unto another place,<br>   Ye who serve Indra and none else;<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2006806">6</a></font></span>Or whether, God of wondrous deeds, all our true people call us<br>    blest,<br>   Still may we dwell in Indra's care.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2006807">7</a></font></span>Unto the swift One bring the swift, man-cheering, grace of<br>    sacrifice.<br>   That to the Friend gives wings and joy.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2006808">8</a></font></span>Thou, Satakratu, drankest this and wast the Vritras' slayer;<br>    thou.<br>   Helpest the warrior in the fray.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2006809">9</a></font></span>We strengthen, Satakratu, thee, yea, thee the powerfull in fight,<br>   That, Indra, we may win us wealth.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2006810">10</a></font></span>To him the mighty stream of wealth, prompt Friend of him who<br>    pours the juice,<br>   Yea, to this Indra sing your song.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2006841">41</a></font></span>O come ye hither, sit ye down: to Indra sing ye forth your<br>    song,<br>   Companions, bringing hymns of praise.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2006812">12</a></font></span>To him the richest of the rich, the Lord of treasures excellent,<br>   Indra, with Soma juice outpoured.</p>
