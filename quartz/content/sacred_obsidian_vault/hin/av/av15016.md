 <p><a name="page_161"><font size="1" color="green">p. 161</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">HYMN XVI</h3>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1501601">1</a></font></span>His first downward breath is the time of Full Moon.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1501602">2</a></font></span>His second downward breath is the eighth day after Full Moon.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1501603">3</a></font></span>His third downward breath is the night of New Moon.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1501604">4</a></font></span>His fourth downward breath is Faith.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1501605">5</a></font></span>His fifth downward breath is Consecration.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1501606">6</a></font></span>His sixth downward breath is Sacrifice.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1501607">7</a></font></span>His seventh downward breath are these sacrificial fees.</p>
