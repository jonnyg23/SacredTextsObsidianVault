 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2007901">1</a></font></span>O Indra, give us wisdom as a sire gives wisdom to his sons.<br>   Guide us, O Much-invoked, on this our foray: may we, living,<br>    still enjoy the light.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2007902">2</a></font></span>Grant that no mighty foes, unknown, malevolent, unhallowed,<br>    tread us to the ground.<br>   With thine assistance, Hero! may we pass through all the waters<br>    that are rushing down.</p>
