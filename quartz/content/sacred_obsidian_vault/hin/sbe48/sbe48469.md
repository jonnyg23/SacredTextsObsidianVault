 <p>22. And on account of the words denoting becoming.</p>
 <p>That the texts under discussion have an injunctive purport also follows from the fact that they contain verbal forms denoting becoming or origination--'he is to meditate' and the like; for all such forms have injunctive force. All these texts therefore are meant to enjoin special forms of meditation.--Here terminates the adhikara<i>n</i>a of mere glorification.'</p>
