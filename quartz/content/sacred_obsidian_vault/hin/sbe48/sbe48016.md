 <h4 align="CENTER">Being and consciousness are not one.</h4>
 <p>Hence mere Being does not alone constitute reality. And as the distinction between consciousness and its objects--which rests just on this relation of object and that for which the object is--is proved by perception, the assertion that only consciousness has real existence is also disposed of.</p>
