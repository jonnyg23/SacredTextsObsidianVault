 <p>2. And on account of an inferential mark.</p>
 <p>Inferential mark here means Sm<i>ri</i>ti. Sm<i>ri</i>ti also declares that that knowledge which effects Release is of the nature of continued representation. Meditation therefore has to be repeated.--Here terminates the adhikara<i>n</i>a of 'repetition.'</p>
