 <p>21. The third term includes that which springs from heat.</p>
 <p><a name="page_596"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 596</font></a></p>
 <p>Creatures sprung from heat are included in the third term--viz. that which springs from a germ--which is exhibited in the text quoted. The settled conclusion therefore is that the evil-doers do not go to the moon.--Here terminates the adhikara<i>n</i>a of 'those who do not perform sacrifices.'</p>
