 <p>51. And it is thus also in the case of purposes and so on.</p>
 <p>For the same reason there can be no definite restriction in the case of purposes and so on which are the causes of the,different ad<i>ri</i>sh<i>t</i>as. (For they also cannot introduce plurality into Brahman that is fundamentally one.)</p>
