 <p>15. Moreover there are seven.</p>
 <p>The Sm<i>ri</i>tis moreover declare that there are seven hells, called Raurava, and so on, to which evil-doers have to go.--But how do they, if moving about in those seven places, reach the palace of Yama?</p>
