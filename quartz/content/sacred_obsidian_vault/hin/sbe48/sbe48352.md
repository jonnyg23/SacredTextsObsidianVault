 <p>8. Hence the awaking from that.</p>
 <p>Since Brahman alone directly is the place of deep sleep, Scripture is able to declare that the souls awake from that, i.e. Brahman; compare 'Having come back from the True they do not know that they come from the True' (<i>Kh</i>. Up. VI, io, 2), and other texts.--Here terminates the adhikara<i>n</i>a of 'the absence of that.'</p>
