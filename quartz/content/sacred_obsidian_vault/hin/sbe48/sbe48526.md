 <p>10. Hence not in the way of destruction of bondage.</p>
 <p>It thus appears that the text 'when all desires which once entered his heart are undone, then does the mortal become immortal, then he obtains Brahman ' (B<i>ri</i>. Up. IV, 4, 7), does not mean such immortality as would imply complete destruction of the state of bondage.</p>
