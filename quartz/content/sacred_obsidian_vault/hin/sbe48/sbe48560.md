 <p>9. And for this very reason without another ruler.</p>
 <p>Since the released soul realises all its wishes, it does not stand under another ruler. For to be under a ruler means to be subject to injunction and prohibition, and to be such is opposed to being free in the realisation of all one's wishes. Hence Scripture says, 'he is a Self-ruler' (<i>Kh</i>. Up. VII, 25).--Here terminates the adhikara<i>n</i>a of 'wishes.'</p>
