 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Rig Veda</i>, tr. by Ralph T.H. Griffith, [1896], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">HYMN CLXXIV. The King.</h3>  
 <p>1. WITH offering for success in fight whence Indra was victorious.<br> With this, O Brahmaṇaspati, let us attain to royal sway.<br> 2 Subduing those who rival us, subduing all malignities,<br> Withstand the man who menaces, withstand the man who angers us.<br> 3 Soma and Savitar the God have made thee a victorious King<br> All elements have aided thee, to make thee general conqueror.<br> 4 Oblation, that which Indra. gave and thus grew glorious and most high,<br> This have I offered, Gods! and hence now, verily, am rivalless.<br> 5 Slayer of rivals, rivalless, victorious, with royal sway,<br> Over these beings may I rule, may I be Sovran of the folk.</p>
