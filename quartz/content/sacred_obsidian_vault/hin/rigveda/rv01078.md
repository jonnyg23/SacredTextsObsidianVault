 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Rig Veda</i>, tr. by Ralph T.H. Griffith, [1896], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">HYMN LXXVIII. Agni.</h3>  
 <p>1. O JĀTAVEDAS, keen and swift, we Gotamas with sacred song exalt thee for thy glories' sake.<br> 2 Thee, as thou art, desiring wealth Gotama worships with his song:<br> We laud thee for thy glories' sake.<br> 3 As such, like Aṅgiras we call on thee best winner of the spoil:<br> We laud thee for thy glories' sake.<br> 4 Thee, best of Vṛtra-slayers, thee who shakest off our Dasyu foes:<br> We laud thee for thy glories' sake.<br> 5 A pleasant song to Agni we, sons of Rahūgaṅa, have sung:<br> We laud thee for thy glories' sake.</p>
