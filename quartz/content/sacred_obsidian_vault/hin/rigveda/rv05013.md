 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Rig Veda</i>, tr. by Ralph T.H. Griffith, [1896], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">HYMN XIII. Agni.</h3>  
 <p>1. WITH songs of praise we call on thee, we kindle thee with songs of praise,<br> Agni, with songs of praise, for help.<br> 2 Eager for wealth, we meditate Agni's effectual praise to-day,<br> Praise of the God who touches heaven.<br> 3 May Agni, Priest among mankind, take pleasure in our songs of praise,<br> And worship the Celestial Folk.<br> 4 Thou, Agni, art spread widely forth, Priest dear and excellent; through thee<br> Men make the sacrifice complete.<br> 5 Singers exalt thee, Agni, well lauded, best giver of our strength:<br> So grant thou us heroic might.<br> 6 Thou Agni, as the felly rings the spokes, encompassest the Gods.<br> I yearn for bounty manifold.</p>
