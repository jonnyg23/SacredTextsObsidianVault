 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Rig Veda</i>, tr. by Ralph T.H. Griffith, [1896], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">HYMN XXXV. Soma Pavamana.</h3>  
 <p>1. Pour forth on us abundant wealth, O Pavamana, with thy stream.<br> Wherewith thou mayest find us light<br> 2 O Indu, swayer of the sea, shaker of all things, flow thou on,<br> Bearer of wealth to us with might.<br> 3 With thee for Hero, Valiant One! may we subdue our enemies:<br> Let what is precious flow to us.<br> 4 Indu arouses strength the Sage who strives for victory, winning power,<br> Discovering holy works and means.<br> 5 Mover of speech, we robe him with our songs as he is purified<br> Soma, the Guardian of the folk;<br> 6 On whose way, Lord of Holy Law, most richi as he is purified.<br> The people all have set their hearts.</p>
