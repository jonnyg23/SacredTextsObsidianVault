 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Rig Veda</i>, tr. by Ralph T.H. Griffith, [1896], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">HYMN LI. Soma Pavamana.</h3>  
 <p>1. ADHVARYU, on the filter pour the Soma juice expressed with stones,<br> And make it pure for Indra's drink.<br> 2 Pour out for Indra, Thunder-armed, the milk of heaven,, the Soma's juice,<br> Most excellent, most rich in sweets.<br> 3 These Gods and all the Marut host, Indu enjoy this juice of thine,<br> This Pavamana's flowing meath.<br> 4 For, Soma, thou hast been effused, strengthening for the wild carouse,<br> O Steer, the singer, for our help.<br> 5 Flow with thy stream, Far-sighted One, effused, into the cleansing sieve:<br> Flow on to give us strength and fame.</p>
