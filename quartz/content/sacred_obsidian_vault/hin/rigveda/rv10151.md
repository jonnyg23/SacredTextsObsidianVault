 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Rig Veda</i>, tr. by Ralph T.H. Griffith, [1896], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">HYMN CLI. Faith.</h3>  
 <p>1. By Faith is Agni kindled, through Faith is oblation offered up.<br> We celebrate with praises Faith upon the height of happiness.<br> 2 Bless thou the man who gives, O Faith; Faith, bless the man who fain would give.<br> Bless thou the liberal worshippers: bless thou the word that I have said.<br> 3 Even as the Deities maintained Faith in the mighty Asuras,<br> So make this uttered wish of mine true for the liberal worshippers.<br> 4 Guarded by Vāyu, Gods and men who sacrifice draw near to Faith.<br> Man winneth Faith by yearnings of the heart, and opulence by Faith.<br> 5 Faith in the early morning, Faith at noonday will we invocate,<br> Faith at the setting of the Sun. O Faith, endow us with belief.</p>
