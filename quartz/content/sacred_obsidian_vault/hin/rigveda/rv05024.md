 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Rig Veda</i>, tr. by Ralph T.H. Griffith, [1896], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">HYMN XXIV. Agni.</h3>  
 <p>1. O AGNI, be our nearest Friend, be thou a kind deliverer and a gracious Friend.<br> 2 Excellent Agni, come thou nigh to us, and give us wealth most splendidly renowned.<br> 3 So hear us, listen to this call of ours, and keep us far from every sinful man.<br> 4 To thee then, O Most Bright, O Radiant God, we come with prayer for happiness for our friends.</p>
