 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Rig Veda</i>, tr. by Ralph T.H. Griffith, [1896], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">HYMN CLXXV. Press-stones.</h3>  
 <p>1. MAY Savitar the God, O Stones, stir you according to the Law:<br> Be harnessed to the shafts, and press.<br> 2 Stones, drive calamity away, drive ye away malevolence:<br> Make ye the Cows our medicine.<br> 3 Of one accord the upper Stones, giving the Bull his bull-like strength,<br> Look down with pride on those below.<br> 4 May Savitar the God, O Stones, stir you as Law commands for him<br> Who sacrifices, pouring juice.</p>
