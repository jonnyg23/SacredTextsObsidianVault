 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Rig Veda</i>, tr. by Ralph T.H. Griffith, [1896], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">HYMN CLXXXII. Bṛhaspati.</h3>  
 <p>1. BṚHASPATI lead us safely over troubles and turn his evil thought against the sinner;<br> Repel the curse, and drive away ill-feeling, and give the sacrificer peace and comfort!<br> 2 May Narāśaṁsa aid us at Prayāja: blest be our Anuyāja at invokings.<br> May he repel the curse, and chase ill-feeling, and give the sacrificer peace and comfort.<br> 3 May he whose head is flaming burn the demons, haters of prayer, so that the arrow slay them.<br> May he repel the curse and chase ill-feeling, and give the sacrificer peace and comfort.</p>
