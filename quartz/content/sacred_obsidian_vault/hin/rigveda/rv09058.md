 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Rig Veda</i>, tr. by Ralph T.H. Griffith, [1896], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">HYMN LVIII. Soma Pavamana.</h3>  
 <p>1. SWIFT runs this giver of delight, even the stream of flowing juice:<br> Swift runs this giver of delight.<br> 2 The Morning knows all precious things, the Goddess knows her grace to man:<br> Swift runs this giver of delight.<br> 3 We have accepted thousands from Dhvasra's and Puruṣanti's hands:<br> Swift runs this giver of delight.<br> 4 From whom we have accepted thus thousands and three times ten beside:<br> Swift runs this giver of delight.</p>
