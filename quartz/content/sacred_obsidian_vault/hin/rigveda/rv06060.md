 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Rig Veda</i>, tr. by Ralph T.H. Griffith, [1896], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">HYMN LX. Indra-Agni.</h3>  
 <p>1. HE slays the foe and wins the spoil who worships Indra and Agni, strong and mighty Heroes,<br> Who rule as Sovrans over ample riches, victorious, showing forth their power in conquest.<br> 2 So battle now, O Indra and thou, Agni, for cows and waters, sunlight, stolen Mornings.<br> Team-borne, thou makest kine thine own, O Agni: thou, Indra, light, Dawns, regions, wondrous waters.<br> 3 With Vṛtra-slaying might, Indra and Agni, come, drawn by homage, O ye Vṛtra-slayers.<br> Indra and Agni, show yourselves among us with your supreme and unrestricted bounties.<br> 4 I call the Twain whose deeds of old have all been famed in ancient days<br> O Indra-Agni, harm us not.<br> 5 The Strong, the scatterers of the foe, Indra and Agni, we invoke;<br> May they be kind to one like me.<br> 6 They slay our Ārya foes, these Lords of heroes, slay our Dasyu foes<br> And drive our enemies away.<br> 7 Indra and Agni, these our songs of praise have sounded forth to you:<br> Ye who bring blessings! drink the juice.<br> 8 Come, Indra-Agni, with those teams, desired of many, which ye have,<br> O Heroes, for the worshipper.<br> 9 With those to this libation poured, ye Heroes, Indra-Agni, come:<br> Come ye to drink the Soma juice.<br> 10 Glorify him who compasses all forests with his glowing flame,<br> And leaves them blackened with his tongue.<br> 11 He who gains Indra's bliss with fire enkindled finds an easy way<br> Over the floods to happiness.<br> 12 Give us fleet coursers to convey Indra and Agni, and bestow<br> Abundant strengthening food on us.<br> 13 Indra and Agni, I will call you hither and make you joyful with the gifts I offer.<br> Ye Twain are givers both of food and riches: to win me strength and vigour I invoke you.<br> 14 Come unto us with riches, come with wealth in horses and in kine.<br> Indra and Agni, we invoke you both, the Gods, as Friends for friendship, bringing bliss.<br> 15 Indra and Agni, hear his call who worships. with libations poured.<br> Come and enjoy the offerings, drink the sweetly-flavoured Soma juice.</p>
