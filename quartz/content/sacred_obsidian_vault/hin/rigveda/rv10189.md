 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Rig Veda</i>, tr. by Ralph T.H. Griffith, [1896], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">HYMN CLXXXIX. Sūrya.</h3>  
 <p>1. THIS spotted Bull hath come, and sat before the Mother in the east,<br> Advancing to his Father heaven.<br> 2 Expiring when he draws his breath, she moves along the lucid spheres:<br> The Bull shines out through all the sky.<br> 3 Song is bestowed upon the Bird: it rules supreme through thirty realms<br> Throughout the days at break of morn.</p>
