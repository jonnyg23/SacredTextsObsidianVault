 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Rig Veda</i>, tr. by Ralph T.H. Griffith, [1896], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">HYMN LXII. Indra and Others.</h3>  
 <p>1. YOUR well-known prompt activities aforetime needed no impulse from your faithful servant.<br> Where, Indra-Varuṇa, is now that glory wherewith ye brought support to those who loved you?<br> 2 This man, most diligent, seeking after riches, incessantly invokes you for your favour.<br> Accordant, Indra-Varuṇa, with Maruts, with Heaven and Earth, hear ye mine invocation.<br> 3 O Indra-Varuṇa, ours be this treasure ours be wealth, Maruts, with full store of heroes.<br> May the Varūtrīs with their shelter aid us, and Bhāratī and Hotrā with the Mornings.<br> 4 Be pleased! with our oblations, thou loved of all Gods, Bṛhaspati:<br> Give wealth to him who brings thee gifts.<br> 5 At sacrifices, with your hymns worship the pure Bṛhaspati<br> I pray for power which none may bend<br> 6 The Bull of men, whom none deceive, the wearer of each shape at will,<br> Bṛhaspati Most Excellent.<br> 7 Divine, resplendent Pūṣan, this our newest hymn of eulogy,<br> By us is chanted forth to thee.<br> 8 Accept with favour this my song, be gracious to the earnest thought,<br> Even as a bridegroom to his bride.<br> 9 May he who sees all living things, see, them together at a glance,<br> May he, may Pūṣan be our help.<br> 10 May we attain that excellent glory of Savitar the God:<br> So May he stimulate our prayers.<br> 11 With understanding, earnestly, of Savitar the God we crave<br> Our portion of prosperity.<br> 12 Men, singers worship Savitar the God with hymn and holy rites,<br> Urged by the impulse of their thoughts.<br> 13 Soma who gives success goes forth, goes to the gathering place of Gods,<br> To seat him at the seat of Law.<br> 14 To us and to our cattle may Soma give salutary food,<br> To biped and to quadruped.<br> 15 May Soma, strengthening our power of life, and conquering our foes,<br> In our assembly take his seat.<br> 16 May Mitra-Varuṇa, sapient Pair, bedew our pasturage with oil,<br> With meatb the regions of the air.<br> 17 Far-ruling, joyful when adored, ye reign through majesty of might,<br> With pure laws everlastingly.<br> 18 Lauded by Jamadagni's song, sit in the place of holy Law:</p>
