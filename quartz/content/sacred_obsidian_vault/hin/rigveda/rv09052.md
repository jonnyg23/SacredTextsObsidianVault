 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Rig Veda</i>, tr. by Ralph T.H. Griffith, [1896], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">HYMN LII. Soma Pavamana.</h3>  
 <p>1. WEALTH-WINNER, dwelling in the sky, bringing us vigour with the juice,<br> Flow to the filter when effused.<br> 2 So, in thine ancient ways, may he, beloved, with a thousand streams<br> Run oer the fleecy straining-cloth.<br> 3 Him who is like a caldron shake: O Indu, shake thy gift to us<br> Shake it, armed Warrior! with thine arms.<br> 4 Indu, invoked with many a prayer, bring down the vigour of these men,<br> Of him who threatens us with war.<br> 5 Indu, Wealth-giver, with thine help pour out for us a hundred, yea,<br> A thousand of thy pure bright streams.</p>
