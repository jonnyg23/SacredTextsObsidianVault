 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Rig Veda</i>, tr. by Ralph T.H. Griffith, [1896], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">HYMN LVIII. Mānas or Spirit.</h3>  
 <p>1. THY spirit, that went far away to Yama to Vivasvān's Son,<br> We cause to come to thee again that thou mayst live and sojourn here.<br> 2 Thy spirit, that went far away, that passed away to earth and heaven,<br> We cause to come to thee again that thou mayst live and sojourn here.<br> 3 Thy spirit, that went far away, away to the four-cornered earth,<br> We cause to come to thee again that thou mayst live and sojourn here.<br> 4 Thy spirit, that went far away to the four quarters of the world,<br> We cause to come to thee again that thou mayst live and sojourn here.<br> 5 Thy spirit, that went far away, away unto the billowy sea,<br> We cause to come to thee again that thou mayst live and sojourn here.<br> 6 Thy spirit, that went far away to beams of light that flash and flow,<br> We cause to come to thee again that thou mayst live and sojourn here.<br> 7 Thy spirit, that went far away, went to the waters and the plants,<br> We cause to come to thee again that thou mayst live and sojourn here.<br> 8 Thy spirit, that went far away, that visited the Sun and Dawn.<br> We cause to come to thee again that thou mayst live and sojourn here.<br> 9 Thy spirit, that went far away, away to lofty mountain heights,<br> We cause to come to thee again that thou mayst live and sojourn here.<br> 10 Thy spirit, that went far away into this All, that lives and moves,<br> We cause to come to thee again that thou mayst live and sojourn here.<br> 11 Thy spirit, that went far away to distant realms beyond our ken,<br> We cause to come to thee again that thou mayst live and sojourn here.<br> 12 Thy spirit, that went far away to all that is and is to be,<br> We cause to come to thee again that thou mayst live and sojourn heie.</p>
