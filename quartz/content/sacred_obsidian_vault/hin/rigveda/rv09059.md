 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Rig Veda</i>, tr. by Ralph T.H. Griffith, [1896], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">HYMN LIX. Soma Pavamana.</h3>  
 <p>1. FLOW onward, Soma, winning kine, and steeds, and all that gives delight:<br> Bring hither wealth with progeny.<br> 2 Flow onward from the waters, flow, inviolable, from the plants:<br> Flow onward from the pressing-boards.<br> 3 Soma, as Pavamana, pass over all trouble and distress:<br> Sit on the sacred grass, a Sage.<br> 4 Thou, Pavamana, foundest light; thou at thy birth becamest great:<br> O Indu, thou art over all.</p>
