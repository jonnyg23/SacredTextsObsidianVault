 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Rig Veda</i>, tr. by Ralph T.H. Griffith, [1896], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">HYMN IV. Soma Pavamana.</h3>  
 <p>1. O Soma flowing on thy way, win thou and conquer high renown;<br> And make us better than we are.<br> 2 Win thou the light, win heavenly light, and, Soma, all felicities;<br> And make us better than we are.<br> 3 Win skilful strength and mental power. O Soma, drive away our foes;<br> And make us better than we are.<br> 4 Ye purifiers, purify Soma for Indra, for his drink:<br> Make thou us better than we are.<br> 5 Give us our portion in the Sun through thine own mental power and aids;<br> And make us better than we are.<br> 6 Through thine own mental power and aid long may we look upon the Sun;<br> Make thou us better than we are.<br> 7 Well-weaponed Soma, pour to usa stream of riches doubly great;<br> And make us better than we are.<br> 8 As one victorious unsubdued in battle pour forth wealth to us;<br> And make us better than we are.<br> 9 By worship, Pavamana! men have strengthened thee to prop the Law:<br> Make thou us better than we are.<br> 10 O Indu, bring us wealth in steeds, manifold. quickening all life;<br> And mate us better than we are.</p>
