 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Rig Veda</i>, tr. by Ralph T.H. Griffith, [1896], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">HYMN LI. Viśvedevas.</h3>  
 <p>1. THAT mighty eye of Varuṇa and Mitra, infallible and dear, is moving upward.<br> The pure and lovely face of holy Order hath shone like gold of heaven in its arising.<br> 2 The Sage who knows these Gods three ranks and orders, and all their generations near and distant,<br> Beholding good and evil acts of mortals, Sūra marks well the doing of the pious.<br> 3 I praise you Guards of mighty Law eternal, Aditi, Mitra, Varuṇa, the noble,<br> Aryaman, Bhaga, all whose thoughts are faithful: hither I call the Bright who share in common.<br> 4 Lords of the brave, infallible, foe-destroyers, great Kings, bestowers of fair homes to dwell in,<br> Young, Heroes, ruling heaven with strong dominion, Ādityas, Aditi I seek with worship.<br> 5 O Heaven our Father, Earth our guileless Mother, O Brother Agni, and ye Vasus, bless us.<br> Grant us, O Aditi and ye Ādityas, all of one mind, your manifold protection.<br> 6 Give us not up to any evil creature, as spoil to wolf or she-wolf, O ye Holy.<br> For ye are they who guide aright our bodies, ye are the rulers of our speech and vigour.<br> 7 Let us not suffer for the sin of others, nor do the deed which ye, O Vasus, punish.<br> Ye, Universal Gods! are all-controllers: may he do harm unto himself who hates Me.<br> 8 Mighty is homage: I adopt and use it. Homage hath held in place the earth and heaven.<br> Homage to Gods! Homage commands and rules them. I banish even committed sin by homage<br> 9 You Furtherers of Law, pure in your spirit, infallible, dwellers in the home of Order,<br> To you all Heroes mighty and far-seeing I bow me down, O Holy Ones, with homage.<br> 10 For these are they who shine with noblest splendour; through all our troubles these conduct us safely-<br> Varuṇa, Mitra, Agni, mighty Rulers, trueminded, faithful to the hymn's controllers.<br> 11 May they, Earth, Aditi, Indra, Bhaga, Pūṣan increase our laud, increase the Fivefold people.<br> Giving good help, good refuge, goodly guidance, be they our good deliverers, good protectors.<br> 12 Come now, O Gods, to your celestial station: the Bharadvājas' priest entreats your favour.<br> He, sacrificing, fain for wealth, hath honoured the Gods vath those who sit and share oblations.<br> 13 Agni, drive thou the wicked foe, the evil-hearted thief away,<br> Far, far, Lord of the brave I and give us easy paths.<br> 14 Soma, these pressing-stones have called aloud to win thee for our Friend.<br> Destroy the greedy Paṇi, for a wolf is he.<br> 15 Ye, O most bountiful, are they who, led by Indra, seek the sky.<br> Give us good paths for travel: guard us ivell at home.<br> 16 Now have we entered on the road that leads to bliss, without a foe,<br> The road whereon a man escapes all enemies and gathers wealth.</p>
