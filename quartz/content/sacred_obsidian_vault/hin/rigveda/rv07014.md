 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Rig Veda</i>, tr. by Ralph T.H. Griffith, [1896], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">HYMN XIV Agni.</h3>  
 <p>1. WITH reverence and with offered gifts serve we the God whose flame is bright:<br> Let us bring Jātavedas fuel, and adore Agni when we invoke the Gods.<br> 2 Agni, may we perform thy rites with fuel, and honour thee, O Holy one, with praises:<br> Honour thee, Priest of sacrifice! with butter, thee, God of blessed light! with our oblation.<br> 3 Come, Agni, with the Gods to our invoking, come, pleased, to offerings sanctified with Vaṣaṭ.<br> May we be his who pays thee, God, due honour. Ye Gods, preserve us evermore with blessings.</p>
