 </small><p>II.  <a href="sbe46166.htm#an_lmip">LIST OF THE MORE IMPORTANT PASSAGES QUOTED IN THE NOTES.</a></p></div>  
 <p><a name="page_426"><font size="1" color="green">p. 426</font></a></p>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">The</span> following is not (like the Index to vol. xxxii) a complete Index verborum to the hymns translated in this volume, but only an Index of all the words which can be of any importance. It contains all rare, difficult, and doubtful words, all words of any mythological importance, and especially all words about which something is said in the Notes.</p>  
 <p>Three figures refer to Ma<i>n</i><i>d</i>ala, hymn, and verse, a small figure to a note. If a word occurs in a note only, the passage is put in parentheses.</p>
