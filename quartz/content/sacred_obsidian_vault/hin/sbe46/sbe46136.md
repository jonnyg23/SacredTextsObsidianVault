 <p>i: ayate, he proceeds, I, 127, 3:6; yánta<i>h</i>, corrupt for vyánta<i>h</i>? I, 140, 13:2; saním yaté, striving for gain, V, 27, 4;—ádhi ihi, think thou (of us), 1, 71, 10:2;—sá<i>h</i> nú îyate (Sa<i>m</i>hitâ: sâ nvî̀yate), conj. sâ´nu (= sá ánu) îyate, I, 145, 1:1;—ánta<i>h</i> î´yase, thou passest between, II, 6, 7;—ápa aíye<i>h</i>, V, 2, 8;—úpa â´ imasi, we approach, I, 1, 7;—pari-etâ´, will overtake, I, 27, 8;—pra-yatí ya<i>g</i><i>ñ</i>é, adhvaré, while the sacrifice is going on, III, 29, 16; V, 28, 6; pra-yaí devébhya<i>h</i>, that the gods may come forth, I, 142, 6; éti prá, I, 144, 1:2;—éti práti-itaye, not to be withstood, I, 36, 20;—sám yanti, come together, I, 31, 10; see sam-yát.</p>  
 <p>í<i>d</i>, nourishing power, sacred food: i<i>l</i>â´ k<i>ri</i>tâ´ni, I, 128, 7; i<i>l</i>á<i>h</i> padé, I, 128, 1; II, 10, 1:2; hótâram i<i>l</i>á<i>h</i>, III, 4, 3.</p>  
 <p>Í<i>l</i>â, 'Nourishment,' N. of a goddess, I, 13, 9:1; 31, 11:3; 142, 9; 188, 8; II, 1, 11:1; 3, 8; III, 1, 23:1; 4, 8; 7, 5; 27, 10:2; V, 4, 4; 5, 8; í<i>l</i>âyâ<i>h</i> padé, III, 23, 4:3; 29, 4; i<i>l</i>â´, instr., III, 24, 2; i<i>l</i>àya<i>h</i> putrá<i>h</i>, III, 29, 3:3.</p>  
 <p>í<i>l</i>à-vat, rich in nourishment, IV, 2, 5.</p>  
 <p>itá<i>h</i>-ûti, who from here distributes his blessings, I, 146, 2.</p>  
 <p>íti, thus: íti krátvâ, with this intention, IV, 1, 1:1.</p>  
 <p>itthâ´, truly, I, 36, 7; 141, I; V, 17, I; itthâ´ dhiyâ´, with right thought, III, 27, 6.</p>  
 <p>itthâ´-dhî, thus minded, IV, 11, 3.</p>  
 <p>idám, this: asmai (after the plural yé), I, 67, 8:1; asmai (verb to be supplied), I, 70, 4:1; yádi idám, when here (all this happened), 1, 79, 2:3; yádi idám, as I am here, IV, 5, 11:2; <a name="page_434"><font size="1" color="green">p. 434</font></a> there, II, 5, 5; eshâm (conj. eshám), IV, 2, 4:1.</p>  
 <p>idâ´ <i>k</i>it-idâ´ <i>k</i>it, now-now, IV, 10, 5.</p>  
 <p>idhmá, fuel, I, 94, 4; III, 18, 3.</p>  
 <p>in: inóshi, thou drivest away, IV, 10, 7;—prá ainot, he has driven forward, I, 66, 10.</p>  
 <p>iná, strong, I, 149, I.</p>  
 <p>índu, Soma, V, 18, 2.</p>  
 <p>Índra, I, 13, 12; 142, 4; 5; 12; 13; II, 1, 3; 3, 3; 8, 6; III, 4, 6; 11; 22, 1; 25, 4; IV, 2, 17; V, 2, 8; 3, 1; 5, 3; 11; 11, 2; índram agním, III, 12, 3.</p>

 <p>iva: svéna-iva, read svéna eva? I, 145, 2:2.</p>  
 <p>ish, to long, seek: i<i>k</i><i>kh</i>ánta, I, 68, 8; i<i>k</i><i>kh</i>ánta<i>h</i>, I, 72, 2; îshu<i>h</i>, III, 1, 2.</p>
