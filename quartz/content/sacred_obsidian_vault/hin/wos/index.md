 <p>
 Shankara, an Indian philosopher, probably lived in the 8th century CE.
 He taught the unity of the soul and God, the 'Advaita' (non-dualistic)
 branch of Vedanta Hinduism.
 This is a short sampler of some of his important texts,
 originally published as a Sanskrit primer with each verse
 also given in Sanskrit.
 This etext includes the English translation only.--<small>J.B. Hare, December 28th, 2008.</small>
