 <p>4. Because scripture directly states that.</p>  
 <p>'What a man does with knowledge, faith and the Upanishad is more powerful' (<i>Kh</i>. Up. I, 1, 10): this passage directly states that knowledge is subordinate to work <a name="fr_187"></a><a href="#fn_187"><font size="1">1</font></a>, and from this it follows that mere knowledge cannot effect the purpose of man.</p>
