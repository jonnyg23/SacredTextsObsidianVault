 <img src="img/00100.jpg"></p>  <h1 align="center">THE HAṬHA YOGA PRADIPIKA.</h1>  <h3 align="center"><font color="green">हठ-योग-परदीपिका</font></h3>  <h3 align="center"><font color="green">haṭha-yogha-pradīpikā</font></h3>  <h3 align="center">CHAPTER 1.</h3>  
 <p align="center"><i>On Âsanas</i>.</p>  
 <p align="center"><font color="green">|| १ || परथमोपदेशः</font></p>  
 <p align="center"><font color="green">|| 1 || prathamopadeśaḥ</font></p>  
 <p><font color="green">शरी-आदि-नाथाय नमो|अस्तु तस्मै<br> येनोपदिष्ह्टा हठ-योग-विद्या |<br> विभ्राजते परोन्नत-राज-योगम<br> आरोढुमिछ्छोरधिरोहिणीव || १ || || १ ||</font></p>  
 <p><font color="green">śrī-ādi-nāthāya namo|astu tasmai<br> yenopadiṣhṭā haṭha-yogha-vidyā |<br> vibhrājate pronnata-rāja-yogham<br> āroḍhumichchoradhirohiṇīva || 1 || || 1 ||</font></p>  
 <p>Salutation to Âdinâtha (Śiva) who expounded the knowledge of Haṭha Yoga, which like a staircase leads the aspirant to the high pinnacled Râja Yoga. <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1.">1.</a></font></span></p>  
 <p><font color="green">परणम्य शरी-गुरुं नाथं सवात्मारामेण योगिना |<br> केवलं राज-योगाय हठ-विद्योपदिश्यते || २ ||</font></p>  
 <p><font color="green">praṇamya śrī-ghuruṃ nāthaṃ svātmārāmeṇa yoghinā |<br> kevalaṃ rāja-yoghāya haṭha-vidyopadiśyate || 2 ||</font></p>  
 <p>Yogin Swâtmârâma, after saluting first his Gurû Srinâtha explains Haṭha Yoga for the attainment of Raja Yoga. <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2.">2.</a></font></span></p>  
 <p><font color="green">भरान्त्या बहुमत-धवान्ते राज-योगमजानताम |<br> हठ-परदीपिकां धत्ते सवात्मारामः कॄपाकरः || ३ |</font></p>  
 <p><font color="green">bhrāntyā bahumata-dhvānte rāja-yoghamajānatām |<br> haṭha-pradīpikāṃ dhatte svātmārāmaḥ kṝpākaraḥ || 3 |</font></p>  
 <p>Owing to the darkness arising from the multiplicity of opinions people are unable to know the Râja Yoga. Compassionate Swâtmârâma composes the Haṭha Yoga Pradipikâ like a torch to dispel it. <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_3.">3.</a></font></span></p>  
 <p><font color="green">हठ-विद्यां हि मत्स्येन्द्र-गोरक्ष्हाद्या विजानते |<br> सवात्मारामो|अथवा योगी जानीते तत-परसादतः || ४ ||</font></p>  
 <p><font color="green">haṭha-vidyāṃ hi matsyendra-ghorakṣhādyā vijānate |<br> svātmārāmo|athavā yoghī jānīte tat-prasādataḥ || 4 ||</font></p>  
 <p>Matsyendra, Gorakṣa, etc., knew Haṭha Vidyâ, and by their favour Yogî Swâtmârâma also learnt it from them. <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_4.">4.</a></font></span></p>  
 <p>The following Siddhas (masters) are said to have existed in former times:</p>  
 <p><font color="green">शरी-आदिनाथ-मत्स्येन्द्र-शावरानन्द-भैरवाः |<br> छौरङ्गी-मीन-गोरक्ष्ह-विरूपाक्ष्ह-बिलेशयाः || ५ ||</font></p>  
 <p><font color="green">śrī-ādinātha-matsyendra-śāvarānanda-bhairavāḥ |<br> chaurangghī-mīna-ghorakṣha-virūpākṣha-bileśayāḥ || 5 ||</font></p>  
 <p>Sri Âdinâtha (Śiva), Matsyendra, Nâtha, Sâbar, Anand, Bhairava, Chaurangi, Mîna nâtha, Gorakṣanâtha, Virupâkṣa, Bileśaya. <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_5.">5.</a></font></span></p>  
 <p><font color="green">मन्थानो भैरवो योगी सिद्धिर्बुद्धश्छ कन्थडिः |<br> कोरंटकः सुरानन्दः सिद्धपादश्छ छर्पटिः || ६ ||</font></p>  
 <p><font color="green">manthāno bhairavo yoghī siddhirbuddhaścha kanthaḍiḥ |<br> koraṃṭakaḥ surānandaḥ siddhapādaścha charpaṭiḥ || 6 ||</font></p>  
 <p>Manthâna, Bhairava, Siddhi Buddha, Kanthadi, Karantaka, Surânanda, Siddhipâda, Charapati. <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_6.">6.</a></font></span></p>  
 <p><a name="page_2"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 2</font></a></p>  
 <p><font color="green">कानेरी पूज्यपादश्छ नित्य-नाथो निरञ्जनः |<br> कपाली बिन्दुनाथश्छ काकछण्डीश्वराह्वयः || ७ ||</font></p>  
 <p><font color="green">kānerī pūjyapādaścha nitya-nātho nirañjanaḥ |<br> kapālī bindunāthaścha kākachaṇḍīśvarāhvayaḥ || 7 ||</font></p>  
 <p>Kânerî, Pûjyapâda, Nityanâtha, Nirañjana, Kapâli, Vindunâtha, Kâka Chandîśwara. <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_7.">7.</a></font></span></p>  
 <p><font color="green">अल्लामः परभुदेवश्छ घोडा छोली छ टिंटिणिः |<br> भानुकी नारदेवश्छ खण्डः कापालिकस्तथा || ८ ||</font></p>  
 <p><font color="green">allāmaḥ prabhudevaścha ghoḍā cholī cha ṭiṃṭiṇiḥ |<br> bhānukī nāradevaścha khaṇḍaḥ kāpālikastathā || 8 ||</font></p>  
 <p>Allâma, Prabhudeva, Ghodâ, Cholî, Tintiṇi, Bhânukî Nârdeva, Khanda Kâpâlika, etc. <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_8.">8.</a></font></span></p>  
 <p><font color="green">इत्यादयो महासिद्धा हठ-योग-परभावतः |<br> खण्डयित्वा काल-दण्डं बरह्माण्डे विछरन्ति ते || ९ ||</font></p>  
 <p><font color="green">ityādayo mahāsiddhā haṭha-yogha-prabhāvataḥ |<br> khaṇḍayitvā kāla-daṇḍaṃ brahmāṇḍe vicharanti te || 9 ||</font></p>  
 <p>These Mahâsiddhas (great masters), breaking the sceptre of death, are roaming in the universe. <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_9.">9.</a></font></span></p>  
 <p><font color="green">अशेष्ह-ताप-तप्तानां समाश्रय-मठो हठः |<br> अशेष्ह-योग-युक्तानामाधार-कमठो हठः || १० ||</font></p>  
 <p><font color="green">aśeṣha-tāpa-taptānāṃ samāśraya-maṭho haṭhaḥ |<br> aśeṣha-yogha-yuktānāmādhāra-kamaṭho haṭhaḥ || 10 ||</font></p>  
 <p>Like a house protecting one from the heat of the sun, Haṭha Yoga protects its practiser from the burning heat of the three Tâpas; and, similarly, it is the supporting tortoise, as it were, for those who are constantly devoted to the practice of Yoga. <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_10.">10.</a></font></span></p>  
 <p><font color="green">हठ-विद्या परं गोप्या योगिना सिद्धिमिछ्छता |<br> भवेद्वीर्यवती गुप्ता निर्वीर्या तु परकाशिता || ११ ||</font></p>  
 <p><font color="green">haṭha-vidyā paraṃ ghopyā yoghinā siddhimichchatā |<br> bhavedvīryavatī ghuptā nirvīryā tu prakāśitā || 11 ||</font></p>  
 <p>A Yogî desirous of success should keep the knowledge of Haṭha Yoga secret; for it becomes potent by concealing, and impotent by exposing. <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_11.">11.</a></font></span></p>  
 <p><font color="green">सुराज्ये धार्मिके देशे सुभिक्ष्हे निरुपद्रवे |<br> धनुः परमाण-पर्यन्तं शिलाग्नि-जल-वर्जिते |<br> एकान्ते मठिका-मध्ये सथातव्यं हठ-योगिना || १२ ||</font></p>  
 <p><font color="green">surājye dhārmike deśe subhikṣhe nirupadrave |<br> dhanuḥ pramāṇa-paryantaṃ śilāghni-jala-varjite |<br> ekānte maṭhikā-madhye sthātavyaṃ haṭha-yoghinā || 12 ||</font></p>  
 <p>The Yogî should practise Haṭha Yoga in a small room, situated in a solitary place, being 4 cubits square, and free from stones, fire, water, disturbances of all kinds, and in a country where justice is properly administered, where good people live, and food can be obtained easily and plentifully. <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_12.">12.</a></font></span></p>  
 <p><a name="page_3"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 3</font></a></p>  
 <p><font color="green">अल्प-दवारमरन्ध्र-गर्त-विवरं नात्युछ्छ-नीछायतं<br> सम्यग-गोमय-सान्द्र-लिप्तममलं निःशेस-जन्तूज्झितम |<br> बाह्ये मण्डप-वेदि-कूप-रुछिरं पराकार-संवेष्ह्टितं<br> परोक्तं योग-मठस्य लक्ष्हणमिदं सिद्धैर्हठाभ्यासिभिः || १३ ||</font></p>  
 <p><font color="green">alpa-dvāramarandhra-gharta-vivaraṃ nātyuchcha-nīchāyataṃ<br> samyagh-ghomaya-sāndra-liptamamalaṃ niḥśesa-jantūjjhitam |<br> bāhye maṇḍapa-vedi-kūpa-ruchiraṃ prākāra-saṃveṣhṭitaṃ<br> proktaṃ yogha-maṭhasya lakṣhaṇamidaṃ siddhairhaṭhābhyāsibhiḥ || 13 ||</font></p>  
 <p>The room should have a small door, be free from holes, hollows, neither too high nor too low, well plastered with cow-dung and free from dirt, filth and insects. On its outside there should be bowers, raised platform (chabootrâ), a well, and a compound. These characteristics of a room for Haṭha Yogîs have been described by adepts in the practice of Haṭha. <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_13.">13.</a></font></span></p>  
 <p><font color="green">एवं विधे मठे सथित्वा सर्व-छिन्ता-विवर्जितः |<br> गुरूपदिष्ह्ट-मार्गेण योगमेव समभ्यसेत || १४ ||</font></p>  
 <p><font color="green">evaṃ vidhe maṭhe sthitvā sarva-chintā-vivarjitaḥ |<br> ghurūpadiṣhṭa-mārgheṇa yoghameva samabhyaset || 14 ||</font></p>  
 <p>Having seated in such a room and free from all anxieties, he should practise Yoga, as instructed by his <i>guru</i>. <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_14.">14.</a></font></span></p>  
 <p><font color="green">अत्याहारः परयासश्छ परजल्पो नियमाग्रहः |<br> जन-सङ्गश्छ लौल्यं छ षहड्भिर्योगो विनश्यति || १५ ||</font></p>  
 <p><font color="green">atyāhāraḥ prayāsaścha prajalpo niyamāghrahaḥ |<br> jana-sangghaścha laulyaṃ cha ṣhaḍbhiryogho vinaśyati || 15 ||</font></p>  
 <p>Yoga is destroyed by the following six causes:Over-eating, exertion, talkativeness, adhering to rules, <i>i.e.</i>, cold bath in the morning, eating at night, or eating fruits only, company of men, and unsteadiness. <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_15.">15.</a></font></span></p>  
 <p><font color="green">उत्साहात्साहसाद्धैर्यात्तत्त्व-जञानाश्छ निश्छयात |<br> जन-सङ्ग-परित्यागात्ष्हड्भिर्योगः परसिद्ध्यति || १६ ||</font></p>  
 <p><font color="green">utsāhātsāhasāddhairyāttattva-jñānāścha niśchayāt |<br> jana-sanggha-parityāghātṣhaḍbhiryoghaḥ prasiddhyati || 16 ||</font></p>  
 <p>The following six bring speedy success:Courage, daring, perseverance, discriminative knowledge, faith, aloofness. from company. <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_16.">16.</a></font></span></p>  
 <p><font color="green">अथ यम-नियमाः<br> अहिंसा सत्यमस्तेयं बरह्मछर्यं कष्हमा धॄतिः |<br> दयार्जवं मिताहारः शौछं छैव यमा दश || १७ ||</font></p>  
 <p><font color="green">atha yama-niyamāḥ<br> ahiṃsā satyamasteyaṃ brahmacharyaṃ kṣhamā dhṝtiḥ |<br> dayārjavaṃ mitāhāraḥ śauchaṃ chaiva yamā daśa || 17 ||</font></p>  
 <p>The ten rules of conduct are: ahiṃsâ (non-injuring), truth, non-stealing, continence, forgiveness, endurance, compassion, meekness, sparing diet and cleanliness. <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_17.">17.</a></font></span></p>  
 <p><font color="green">तपः सन्तोष्ह आस्तिक्यं दानमीश्वर-पूजनम |<br> सिद्धान्त-वाक्य-शरवणं हरीमती छ तपो हुतम |<br> नियमा दश सम्प्रोक्ता योग-शास्त्र-विशारदैः || १८ ||</font></p>  
 <p><font color="green">tapaḥ santoṣha āstikyaṃ dānamīśvara-pūjanam |<br> siddhānta-vākya-śravaṇaṃ hrīmatī cha tapo hutam |<br> niyamā daśa samproktā yogha-śāstra-viśāradaiḥ || 18 ||</font></p>  
 <p>The ten niyamas mentioned by those proficient in the knowledge of yoga are: Tapa, patience, belief in God, charity, adoration of God, hearing discourses on the principles of religion, shame, intellect, Tapa and Yajña. <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_18.">18.</a></font></span></p>  
 <p><a name="page_4"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 4</font></a></p>  
 <p align="center"><i>Âsanas</i>.</p>  
 <p><font color="green">अथ आसनम<br> हठस्य परथमाङ्गत्वादासनं पूर्वमुछ्यते |<br> कुर्यात्तदासनं सथैर्यमारोग्यं छाङ्ग-लाघवम || १९ ||</font></p>  
 <p><font color="green">atha āsanam<br> haṭhasya prathamāngghatvādāsanaṃ pūrvamuchyate |<br> kuryāttadāsanaṃ sthairyamāroghyaṃ chānggha-lāghavam || 19 ||</font></p>  
 <p>Being the first accessory of Haṭha Yoga, âsana is described first. It should be practised for gaining steady posture, health and lightness of body. <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_19.">19.</a></font></span></p>  
 <p><font color="green">वशिष्ह्ठाद्यैश्छ मुनिभिर्मत्स्येन्द्राद्यैश्छ योगिभिः |<br> अङ्गीकॄतान्यासनानि कथ्यन्ते कानिछिन्मया || २० ||</font></p>  
 <p><font color="green">vaśiṣhṭhādyaiścha munibhirmatsyendrādyaiścha yoghibhiḥ |<br> angghīkṝtānyāsanāni kathyante kānichinmayā || 20 ||</font></p>  
 <p>I am going to describe certain âsanas which have been adopted by Munîs like Vasiṣṭha, etc., and Yogîs like Matsyendra, etc. <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_20.">20.</a></font></span></p>  
 <p align="center"><i>Swastika-âsana</i>.</p>  
 <p><font color="green">जानूर्वोरन्तरे सम्यक्कॄत्वा पाद-तले उभे |<br> ॠजु-कायः समासीनः सवस्तिकं तत्प्रछक्ष्हते || २१ ||</font></p>  
 <p><font color="green">jānūrvorantare samyakkṝtvā pāda-tale ubhe |<br> ṝju-kāyaḥ samāsīnaḥ svastikaṃ tatprachakṣhate || 21 ||</font></p>  
 <p>Having kept both the hands under both the thighs, with the body straight, when one sits calmly in this posture, it is called Swastika. <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_21.">21.</a></font></span></p>  
 <p align="center"><i>Gomukha-âsana</i>.</p>  
 <p><font color="green">सव्ये दक्ष्हिण-गुल्कं तु पॄष्ह्ठ-पार्श्वे नियोजयेत |<br> दक्ष्हिणे|अपि तथा सव्यं गोमुखं गोमुखाकॄतिः || २२ ||</font></p>  
 <p><font color="green">savye dakṣhiṇa-ghulkaṃ tu pṝṣhṭha-pārśve niyojayet |<br> dakṣhiṇe|api tathā savyaṃ ghomukhaṃ ghomukhākṝtiḥ || 22 ||</font></p>  
 <p>Placing the right ankle on the left side and the left ankle on the right side, makes Gomukha-âsana, having the appearance of a cow. <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_22.">22.</a></font></span></p>  
 <p align="center"><i>Vîrâsana</i>.</p>  
 <p><font color="green">एकं पादं तथैकस्मिन्विन्यसेदुरुणि सथिरम |<br> इतरस्मिंस्तथा छोरुं वीरासनमितीरितम || २३ ||</font></p>  
 <p><font color="green">ekaṃ pādaṃ tathaikasminvinyaseduruṇi sthiram |<br> itarasmiṃstathā choruṃ vīrāsanamitīritam || 23 ||</font></p>  
 <p>One foot is to be placed on the thigh of the opposite side; and so also the other foot on the opposite thigh. This is called Vîrâsana. <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_23.">23.</a></font></span></p>  
 <p align="center"><i>Kurmâsana</i>.</p>  
 <p><font color="green">गुदं निरुध्य गुल्फाभ्यां वयुत्क्रमेण समाहितः |<br> कूर्मासनं भवेदेतदिति योग-विदो विदुः || २४ ||</font></p>  
 <p><font color="green">ghudaṃ nirudhya ghulphābhyāṃ vyutkrameṇa samāhitaḥ |<br> kūrmāsanaṃ bhavedetaditi yogha-vido viduḥ || 24 ||</font></p>  
 <p>Placing the right ankle on the left side of anus, and the left ankle on the right side of it, makes what the Yogîs call Kûrma-âsana. <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_24.">24.</a></font></span></p>  
 <p align="center"><i>Kukkuṭa âsana</i>.</p>  
 <p><font color="green">पद्मासनं तु संस्थाप्य जानूर्वोरन्तरे करौ |<br> निवेश्य भूमौ संस्थाप्य वयोमस्थं कुक्कुटासनम || २५ ||</font></p>  
 <p><font color="green">padmāsanaṃ tu saṃsthāpya jānūrvorantare karau |<br> niveśya bhūmau saṃsthāpya vyomasthaṃ kukkuṭāsanam || 25 ||</font></p>  
 <p>Taking the posture of Padma-âsana and carrying the hands under the thighs, when the Yogî raises himself above the ground, with his palms resting on the ground, it becomes Kukkuṭa-âsana. <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_25.">25.</a></font></span></p>  
 <p><a name="page_5"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 5</font></a></p>  
 <p align="center"><i>Uttâna Kûrma-âsana</i>.</p>  
 <p><font color="green">कुक्कुटासन-बन्ध-सथो दोर्भ्यां सम्बद्य कन्धराम |<br> भवेद्कूर्मवदुत्तान एतदुत्तान-कूर्मकम || २६ ||</font></p>  
 <p><font color="green">kukkuṭāsana-bandha-stho dorbhyāṃ sambadya kandharām |<br> bhavedkūrmavaduttāna etaduttāna-kūrmakam || 26 ||</font></p>  
 <p>Having assumed Kukkuṭa-âsana, when one grasps his neck by crossing his hands behind his head, and lies in this posture with his back touching the ground, it becomes Uttâna Kûrma-âsana, from its appearance like that of a tortoise. <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_26.">26.</a></font></span></p>  
 <p align="center"><i>Dhanura âsana</i>.</p>  
 <p><font color="green">पादाङ्गुष्ह्ठौ तु पाणिभ्यां गॄहीत्वा शरवणावधि |<br> धनुराकर्ष्हणं कुर्याद्धनुर-आसनमुछ्यते || २७ ||</font></p>  
 <p><font color="green">pādāngghuṣhṭhau tu pāṇibhyāṃ ghṝhītvā śravaṇāvadhi |<br> dhanurākarṣhaṇaṃ kuryāddhanur-āsanamuchyate || 27 ||</font></p>  
 <p>Having caught the toes of the feet with both the hands and carried them to the ears by drawing the body like a bow, it becomes Dhanura âsana. <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_27.">27.</a></font></span></p>  
 <p align="center"><i>Matsya-âsana</i>.</p>  
 <p><font color="green">वामोरु-मूलार्पित-दक्ष्ह-पादं<br> जानोर्बहिर्वेष्ह्टित-वाम-पादम |<br> परगॄह्य तिष्ह्ठेत्परिवर्तिताङ्गः<br> शरी-मत्य्सनाथोदितमासनं सयात || २८ ||<br> मत्स्येन्द्र-पीठं जठर-परदीप्तिं<br> परछण्ड-रुग्मण्डल-खण्डनास्त्रम |<br> अभ्यासतः कुण्डलिनी-परबोधं<br> छन्द्र-सथिरत्वं छ ददाति पुंसाम || २९ ||</font></p>  
 <p><font color="green">vāmoru-mūlārpita-dakṣha-pādaṃ<br> jānorbahirveṣhṭita-vāma-pādam |<br> praghṝhya tiṣhṭhetparivartitāngghaḥ<br> śrī-matysanāthoditamāsanaṃ syāt || 28 ||<br> matsyendra-pīṭhaṃ jaṭhara-pradīptiṃ<br> prachaṇḍa-rughmaṇḍala-khaṇḍanāstram |<br> abhyāsataḥ kuṇḍalinī-prabodhaṃ<br> chandra-sthiratvaṃ cha dadāti puṃsām || 29 ||</font></p>  
 <p>Having placed the right foot at the root of the left thigh, let the toe be grasped with the right hand passing over the back, and having placed the left foot on the right thigh at its root, let it be grasped with the left hand passing behind the back. This is the âsana, as explained by Śri Matsyanâtha. It increases appetite and is an instrument for destroying the group of the most deadly diseases. Its practice awakens the Kundalinî, stops the nectar shedding from the moon in people. <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_28-29.">28-29.</a></font></span></p>  
 <p align="center"><i>Paśchima Tâna</i>.</p>  
 <p><font color="green">परसार्य पादौ भुवि दण्ड-रूपौ<br> दोर्भ्यां पदाग्र-दवितयं गॄहीत्वा |<br> जानूपरिन्यस्त-ललाट-देशो<br> वसेदिदं पश्छिमतानमाहुः || ३० ||</font></p>  
 <p><font color="green">prasārya pādau bhuvi daṇḍa-rūpau<br> dorbhyāṃ padāghra-dvitayaṃ ghṝhītvā |<br> jānūparinyasta-lalāṭa-deśo<br> vasedidaṃ paśchimatānamāhuḥ || 30 ||</font></p>  
 <p>Having stretched the feet on the ground, like a stick, and having grasped the toes of both the feet with both the hands, when one sits with his forehead resting on the thighs, it is called Paśchima Tâna. <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_30.">30.</a></font></span></p>  
 <p><font color="green">इति पश्छिमतानमासनाग्र्यं<br> पवनं पश्छिम-वाहिनं करोति |<br> उदयं जठरानलस्य कुर्याद<br> उदरे कार्श्यमरोगतां छ पुंसाम || ३१ ||</font></p>  
 <p><font color="green">iti paśchimatānamāsanāghryaṃ<br> pavanaṃ paśchima-vāhinaṃ karoti |<br> udayaṃ jaṭharānalasya kuryād<br> udare kārśyamaroghatāṃ cha puṃsām || 31 ||</font></p>  
 <p>This Paśchima Tâna carries the air from the front to the back part of the body (<i>i.e.</i>, to the suṣumna). It kindles gastric fire, reduces obesity and cures all diseases of men. <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_31.">31.</a></font></span></p>  
 <p><a name="page_6"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 6</font></a></p>  
 <p align="center"><i>Mayûra-âsana</i>.</p>  
 <p><font color="green">धरामवष्ह्टभ्य कर-दवयेन<br> तत-कूर्पर-सथापित-नाभि-पार्श्वः |<br> उछ्छासनो दण्डवदुत्थितः खे<br> मायूरमेतत्प्रवदन्ति पीठम || ३२ ||</font></p>  
 <p><font color="green">dharāmavaṣhṭabhya kara-dvayena<br> tat-kūrpara-sthāpita-nābhi-pārśvaḥ |<br> uchchāsano daṇḍavadutthitaḥ khe<br> māyūrametatpravadanti pīṭham || 32 ||</font></p>  
 <p>Place the palms of both the hands on the ground, and place the navel on both the elbows and balancing thus, the body should be stretched backward like a stick. This is called Mayûra-âsana. <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_32.">32.</a></font></span></p>  
 <p><font color="green">हरति सकल-रोगानाशु गुल्मोदरादीन<br> अभिभवति छ दोष्हानासनं शरी-मयूरम |<br> बहु कदशन-भुक्तं भस्म कुर्यादशेष्हं<br> जनयति जठराग्निं जारयेत्काल-कूटम || ३३ ||</font></p>  
 <p><font color="green">harati sakala-roghānāśu ghulmodarādīn<br> abhibhavati cha doṣhānāsanaṃ śrī-mayūram |<br> bahu kadaśana-bhuktaṃ bhasma kuryādaśeṣhaṃ<br> janayati jaṭharāghniṃ jārayetkāla-kūṭam || 33 ||</font></p>  
 <p>This Âsana soon destroys all diseases, and removes abdominal disorders, and also those arising from irregularities of phlegm, bile and wind, digests unwholesome food taken in excess, increases appetite and destroys the most deadly poison. <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_33.">33.</a></font></span></p>  
 <p align="center"><i>Śava-âsana</i>.</p>  
 <p><font color="green">उत्तानं शबवद्भूमौ शयनं तछ्छवासनम |<br> शवासनं शरान्ति-हरं छित्त-विश्रान्ति-कारकम || ३४ ||</font></p>  
 <p><font color="green">uttānaṃ śabavadbhūmau śayanaṃ tachchavāsanam |<br> śavāsanaṃ śrānti-haraṃ chitta-viśrānti-kārakam || 34 ||</font></p>  
 <p>Lying down on the ground, like a corpse, is called Śava-âsana. It removes fatigue and gives rest to the mind. <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_34.">34.</a></font></span></p>  
 <p><font color="green">छतुरशीत्यासनानि शिवेन कथितानि छ |<br> तेभ्यश्छतुष्ह्कमादाय सारभूतं बरवीम्यहम || ३५ ||</font></p>  
 <p><font color="green">chaturaśītyāsanāni śivena kathitāni cha |<br> tebhyaśchatuṣhkamādāya sārabhūtaṃ bravīmyaham || 35 ||</font></p>  
 <p>Śiva taught 84 âsanas. Of these the first four being essential ones, I am going to explain them here. <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_35.">35.</a></font></span></p>  
 <p><font color="green">सिद्धं पद्मं तथा सिंहं भद्रं वेति छतुष्ह्टयम |<br> शरेष्ह्ठं तत्रापि छ सुखे तिष्ह्ठेत्सिद्धासने सदा || ३६ ||</font></p>  
 <p><font color="green">siddhaṃ padmaṃ tathā siṃhaṃ bhadraṃ veti chatuṣhṭayam |<br> śreṣhṭhaṃ tatrāpi cha sukhe tiṣhṭhetsiddhāsane sadā || 36 ||</font></p>  
 <p>These four are:The Siddha, Padma, Sinha and Bhadra. Even of these, the Siddha-âsana, being very comfortable, one should always practise it. <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_36.">36.</a></font></span></p>  
 <p align="center"><i>The Siddhâsana</i></p>  
 <p><font color="green">अथ सिद्धासनम<br> योनि-सथानकमङ्घ्रि-मूल-घटितं कॄत्वा दॄढं विन्यसेत<br> मेण्ढ्रे पादमथैकमेव हॄदये कॄत्वा हनुं सुस्थिरम |<br> सथाणुः संयमितेन्द्रियो|अछल-दॄशा पश्येद्भ्रुवोरन्तरं<br> हयेतन्मोक्ष्ह-कपाट-भेद-जनकं सिद्धासनं परोछ्यते || ३७ ||</font></p>  
 <p><font color="green">atha siddhāsanam<br> yoni-sthānakamangghri-mūla-ghaṭitaṃ kṝtvā dṝḍhaṃ vinyaset<br> meṇḍhre pādamathaikameva hṝdaye kṝtvā hanuṃ susthiram |<br> sthāṇuḥ saṃyamitendriyo|achala-dṝśā paśyedbhruvorantaraṃ<br> hyetanmokṣha-kapāṭa-bheda-janakaṃ siddhāsanaṃ prochyate || 37 ||</font></p>  
 <p>Press firmly the heel of the left foot against the perineum, and the right heel above the male organ. With the chin pressing on the chest, one should sit calmly, having restrained the senses, and gaze steadily the space between the eyebrows. This is called the Siddha Âsana, the opener of the door of salvation. <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_37.">37.</a></font></span></p>  
 <p><a name="page_7"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 7</font></a></p>  
 <p><font color="green">मेण्ढ्रादुपरि विन्यस्य सव्यं गुल्फं तथोपरि |<br> गुल्फान्तरं छ निक्ष्हिप्य सिद्धासनमिदं भवेत || ३८ ||</font></p>  
 <p><font color="green">meṇḍhrādupari vinyasya savyaṃ ghulphaṃ tathopari |<br> ghulphāntaraṃ cha nikṣhipya siddhāsanamidaṃ bhavet || 38 ||</font></p>  
 <p>This Siddhâsana is performed also by placing the left heel on Meḍhra (above the male organ), and then placing the right one on it. <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_38.">38.</a></font></span></p>  
 <p><font color="green">एतत्सिद्धासनं पराहुरन्ये वज्रासनं विदुः |<br> मुक्तासनं वदन्त्येके पराहुर्गुप्तासनं परे || ३९ ||</font></p>  
 <p><font color="green">etatsiddhāsanaṃ prāhuranye vajrāsanaṃ viduḥ |<br> muktāsanaṃ vadantyeke prāhurghuptāsanaṃ pare || 39 ||</font></p>  
 <p>Some call this Siddhâsana, some Vajrâsana. Others call it Mukta Âsana or Gupta Âsana. <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_39.">39.</a></font></span></p>  
 <p><font color="green">यमेष्ह्विव मिताहारमहिंसा नियमेष्ह्विव |<br> मुख्यं सर्वासनेष्ह्वेकं सिद्धाः सिद्धासनं विदुः || ४० ||</font></p>  
 <p><font color="green">yameṣhviva mitāhāramahiṃsā niyameṣhviva |<br> mukhyaṃ sarvāsaneṣhvekaṃ siddhāḥ siddhāsanaṃ viduḥ || 40 ||</font></p>  
 <p>Just as sparing food is among Yamas, and Ahiṃsâ among the Niyamas, so is Siddhâsana called by adepts the chief of all the âsanas. <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_40.">40.</a></font></span></p>  
 <p><font color="green">छतुरशीति-पीठेष्हु सिद्धमेव सदाभ्यसेत |<br> दवासप्तति-सहस्राणां नाडीनां मल-शोधनम || ४१ ||</font></p>  
 <p><font color="green">chaturaśīti-pīṭheṣhu siddhameva sadābhyaset |<br> dvāsaptati-sahasrāṇāṃ nāḍīnāṃ mala-śodhanam || 41 ||</font></p>  
 <p>Out of the 84 Âsanas Siddhâsana should always be practised, because it cleanses the impurities of 72,000 nâḍîs. <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_41.">41.</a></font></span></p>  
 <p><font color="green">आत्म-धयायी मिताहारी यावद्द्वादश-वत्सरम |<br> सदा सिद्धासनाभ्यासाद्योगी निष्ह्पत्तिमाप्नुयात || ४२ ||</font></p>  
 <p><font color="green">ātma-dhyāyī mitāhārī yāvaddvādaśa-vatsaram |<br> sadā siddhāsanābhyāsādyoghī niṣhpattimāpnuyāt || 42 ||</font></p>  
 <p>By contemplating on oneself, by eating sparingly, and by practising Siddhâsana for 12 years, the Yogî obtains success. <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_42.">42.</a></font></span></p>  
 <p><font color="green">किमन्यैर्बहुभिः पीठैः सिद्धे सिद्धासने सति |<br> पराणानिले सावधाने बद्धे केवल-कुम्भके |<br> उत्पद्यते निरायासात्स्वयमेवोन्मनी कला || ४३ ||</font></p>  
 <p><font color="green">kimanyairbahubhiḥ pīṭhaiḥ siddhe siddhāsane sati |<br> prāṇānile sāvadhāne baddhe kevala-kumbhake |<br> utpadyate nirāyāsātsvayamevonmanī kalā || 43 ||</font></p>  
 <p>Other postures are of no use, when success has been achieved in Siddhâsana, and Prâṇa Vâyû becomes calm and restrained by Kevala Kumbhaka. <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_43.">43.</a></font></span></p>  
 <p><font color="green">तथैकास्मिन्नेव दॄढे सिद्धे सिद्धासने सति |<br> बन्ध-तरयमनायासात्स्वयमेवोपजायते || ४४ ||</font></p>  
 <p><font color="green">tathaikāsminneva dṝḍhe siddhe siddhāsane sati |<br> bandha-trayamanāyāsātsvayamevopajāyate || 44 ||</font></p>  
 <p>Success in one Siddhâsana alone becoming firmly established, one gets Unmanî at once, and the three bonds (Bandhas) are accomplished of themselves. <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_44.">44.</a></font></span></p>  
 <p><a name="page_8"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 8</font></a></p>  
 <p><font color="green">नासनं सिद्ध-सदॄशं न कुम्भः केवलोपमः |<br> न खेछरी-समा मुद्रा न नाद-सदॄशो लयः || ४५ ||</font></p>  
 <p><font color="green">nāsanaṃ siddha-sadṝśaṃ na kumbhaḥ kevalopamaḥ |<br> na khecharī-samā mudrā na nāda-sadṝśo layaḥ || 45 ||</font></p>  
 <p>There is no Âsana like the Siddhâsana and no Kumbhaka like the Kevala. There is no mudrâ like the Khechari and no <i>laya</i> like the Nâda (Anâhata Nâda.) <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_45.">45.</a></font></span></p>  
 <p align="center"><i>Padmâsana</i>.</p>  
 <p><font color="green">अथ पद्मासनम<br> वामोरूपरि दक्ष्हिणं छ छरणं संस्थाप्य वामं तथा<br> दक्ष्होरूपरि पश्छिमेन विधिना धॄत्वा कराभ्यां दॄढम |<br> अङ्गुष्ह्ठौ हॄदये निधाय छिबुकं नासाग्रमालोकयेत<br> एतद्व्याधि-विनाश-कारि यमिनां पद्मासनं परोछ्यते || ४६ ||</font></p>  
 <p><font color="green">atha padmāsanam<br> vāmorūpari dakṣhiṇaṃ cha charaṇaṃ saṃsthāpya vāmaṃ tathā<br> dakṣhorūpari paśchimena vidhinā dhṝtvā karābhyāṃ dṝḍham |<br> angghuṣhṭhau hṝdaye nidhāya chibukaṃ nāsāghramālokayet<br> etadvyādhi-vināśa-kāri yamināṃ padmāsanaṃ prochyate || 46 ||</font></p>  
 <p>Place the right foot on the left thigh and the left foot on the right thigh, and grasp the toes with the hands crossed over the back. Press the chin against the chest and gaze on the tip of the nose. This is called the Padmâsana, the destroyer of the diseases of the Yamîs. <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_46.">46.</a></font></span></p>  
 <p><font color="green">उत्तानौ छरणौ कॄत्वा ऊरु-संस्थौ परयत्नतः |<br> ऊरु-मध्ये तथोत्तानौ पाणी कॄत्वा ततो दॄशौ || ४७ ||</font></p>  
 <p><font color="green">uttānau charaṇau kṝtvā ūru-saṃsthau prayatnataḥ |<br> ūru-madhye tathottānau pāṇī kṝtvā tato dṝśau || 47 ||</font></p>  
 <p>Place the feet on the thighs, with the soles upwards, and place the hands on the thighs, with the palms upwards. <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_47.">47.</a></font></span></p>  
 <p><font color="green">नासाग्रे विन्यसेद्राजद-अन्त-मूले तु जिह्वया |<br> उत्तम्भ्य छिबुकं वक्ष्हस्युत्थाप्य पवनं शनैः || ४८ ||</font></p>  
 <p><font color="green">nāsāghre vinyasedrājad-anta-mūle tu jihvayā |<br> uttambhya chibukaṃ vakṣhasyutthāpy pavanaṃ śanaiḥ || 48 ||</font></p>  
 <p>Gaze on the tip of the nose, keeping the tongue pressed against the root of the teeth of the upper jaw, and the chin against the chest, and raise the air up slowly, <i>i.e.</i>, pull the apâna-vâyû gently upwards. <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_48.">48.</a></font></span></p>  
 <p><font color="green">इदं पद्मासनं परोक्तं सर्व-वयाधि-विनाशनम |<br> दुर्लभं येन केनापि धीमता लभ्यते भुवि || ४९ ||</font></p>  
 <p><font color="green">idaṃ padmāsanaṃ proktaṃ sarva-vyādhi-vināśanam |<br> durlabhaṃ yena kenāpi dhīmatā labhyate bhuvi || 49 ||</font></p>  
 <p>This is called the Padmâsana, the destroyer of all diseases. It is difficult of attainment by everybody, but can be learnt by intelligent people in this world. <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_49.">49.</a></font></span></p>  
 <p><font color="green">कॄत्वा सम्पुटितौ करौ दॄढतरं बद्ध्वा तु पद्ममासनं<br> गाढं वक्ष्हसि सन्निधाय छिबुकं धयायंश्छ तछ्छेतसि |<br> वारं वारमपानमूर्ध्वमनिलं परोत्सारयन्पूरितं<br> नयञ्छन्प्राणमुपैति बोधमतुलं शक्ति-परभावान्नरः || ५० ||</font></p>  
 <p><font color="green">kṝtvā sampuṭitau karau dṝḍhataraṃ baddhvā tu padmamāsanaṃ<br> ghāḍhaṃ vakṣhasi sannidhāya chibukaṃ dhyāyaṃścha tachchetasi |<br> vāraṃ vāramapānamūrdhvamanilaṃ protsārayanpūritaṃ<br> nyañchanprāṇamupaiti bodhamatulaṃ śakti-prabhāvānnaraḥ || 50 ||</font></p>  
 <p>Having kept both the hands together in the lap, performing the Padmâsana firmly, keeping the chin Fixed to the chest and contemplating on Him in the mind, by drawing the apâna-vâyû up (performing Mûla Bandha) and pushing down the air after inhaling it, joining thus the prâṇa</p>  
 <p><a name="page_9"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 9</font></a></p>  
 <p>and apâna in the navel, one gets the highest intelligence by awakening the śakti (kundalinî) thus. <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_50.">50.</a></font></span></p>  
 <p><font size="-1"><i>NḄ.</i>When Apâna Vâyû is drawn gently up and after filling in the lungs with the air from outside, the prâṇa is forced down by and by so as to join both of them in the navel, they both enter then the Kundalinî and, reaching the Brahma randhra (the great hole), they make the mind calm. Then the mind can contemplate on the nature of the âtmana and can enjoy the highest bliss.</font></p>  
 <p><font color="green">पद्मासने सथितो योगी नाडी-दवारेण पूरितम |<br> मारुतं धारयेद्यस्तु स मुक्तो नात्र संशयः || ५१ ||</font></p>  
 <p><font color="green">padmāsane sthito yoghī nāḍī-dvāreṇa pūritam |<br> mārutaṃ dhārayedyastu sa mukto nātra saṃśayaḥ || 51 ||</font></p>  
 <p>The Yogî who, sitting with Padmâsana, can control breathing, there is no doubt, is free from bondage. <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_51.">51.</a></font></span></p>  
 <p align="center"><i>The Siṃhâsana</i>.</p>  
 <p><font color="green">अथ सिंहासनम<br> गुल्फौ छ वॄष्हणस्याधः सीवन्त्याः पार्श्वयोः कष्हिपेत |<br> दक्ष्हिणे सव्य-गुल्फं तु दक्ष्ह-गुल्फं तु सव्यके || ५२ ||</font></p>  
 <p><font color="green">atha siṃhāsanam<br> ghulphau cha vṝṣhaṇasyādhaḥ sīvantyāḥ pārśvayoḥ kṣhipet |<br> dakṣhiṇe savya-ghulphaṃ tu dakṣha-ghulphaṃ tu savyake || 52 ||</font></p>  
 <p>Press the heels on both sides of the seam of Perineum, in such a way that the left heel touches the right side and the right heel touches the left side of it. <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_52.">52.</a></font></span></p>  
 <p><font color="green">हस्तौ तु जान्वोः संस्थाप्य सवाङ्गुलीः सम्प्रसार्य छ |<br> वयात्त-वक्तो निरीक्ष्हेत नासाग्रं सुसमाहितः || ५३ ||</font></p>  
 <p><font color="green">hastau tu jānvoḥ saṃsthāpya svāngghulīḥ samprasārya cha |<br> vyātta-vakto nirīkṣheta nāsāghraṃ susamāhitaḥ || 53 ||</font></p>  
 <p>Place the hands on the thighs, with stretched fingers, and keeping the mouth open and the mind collected, gaze on the tip of the nose. <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_53.">53.</a></font></span></p>  
 <p><font color="green">सिंहासनं भवेदेतत्पूजितं योगि-पुणगवैः |<br> बन्ध-तरितय-सन्धानं कुरुते छासनोत्तमम || ५४ ||</font></p>  
 <p><font color="green">siṃhāsanaṃ bhavedetatpūjitaṃ yoghi-pungghavaiḥ |<br> bandha-tritaya-sandhānaṃ kurute chāsanottamam || 54 ||</font></p>  
 <p>This is Siṃhâsana, held sacred by the best of Yogîs. This excellent Âsana effects the completion of the three Bandhas (The Mûlabandha, Kanṭha or Jâlandhar Bandha and Uḍḍiyâna Bandha). <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_54.">54.</a></font></span></p>  
 <p align="center"><i>The Bhadrâsana</i>.</p>  
 <p><font color="green">अथ भद्रासनम<br> गुल्फौ छ वॄष्हणस्याधः सीवन्त्याः पार्श्वयोः कष्हिप्ते |<br> सव्य-गुल्फं तथा सव्ये दक्ष्ह-गुल्फं तु दक्ष्हिणे || ५५ ||<br> पार्श्व-पादौ छ पाणिभ्यां दॄढं बद्ध्वा सुनिश्छलम |<br> भद्रासनं भवेदेतत्सर्व-वयाधि-विनाशनम |<br> गोरक्ष्हासनमित्याहुरिदं वै सिद्ध-योगिनः || ५६ ||</font></p>  
 <p><font color="green">atha bhadrāsanam<br> ghulphau cha vṝṣhaṇasyādhaḥ sīvantyāḥ pārśvayoḥ kṣhipte |<br> savya-ghulphaṃ tathā savye dakṣha-ghulphaṃ tu dakṣhiṇe || 55 ||<br> pārśva-pādau cha pāṇibhyāṃ dṝḍhaṃ baddhvā suniśchalam |<br> bhadrāsanaṃ bhavedetatsarva-vyādhi-vināśanam |<br> ghorakṣhāsanamityāhuridaṃ vai siddha-yoghinaḥ || 56 ||</font></p>  
 <p>Place the heels on either side of the seam of the Perineum, keeping the left heel on the left side and the right one on the right side, hold the</p>  
 <p><a name="page_10"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 10</font></a></p>  
 <p>feet firmly joined to one another with both the hands. This Bhadrâsana is the destroyer of all the diseases. <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_55%C2%A0and%C2%A056.">55 and 56.</a></font></span></p>  
 <p><font color="green">एवमासन-बन्धेष्हु योगीन्द्रो विगत-शरमः |<br> अभ्यसेन्नाडिका-शुद्धिं मुद्रादि-पवनी-करियाम || ५७ ||</font></p>  
 <p><font color="green">evamāsana-bandheṣhu yoghīndro vighata-śramaḥ |<br> abhyasennāḍikā-śuddhiṃ mudrādi-pavanī-kriyām || 57 ||</font></p>  
 <p>The expert Yogîs call this Gorakśa âsana. By sitting with this âsana, the Yogî gets rid of fatigue <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_57.">57.</a></font></span></p>  
 <p><font color="green">आसनं कुम्भकं छित्रं मुद्राख्यं करणं तथा |<br> अथ नादानुसन्धानमभ्यासानुक्रमो हठे || ५८ ||</font></p>  
 <p><font color="green">āsanaṃ kumbhakaṃ chitraṃ mudrākhyaṃ karaṇaṃ tathā |<br> atha nādānusandhānamabhyāsānukramo haṭhe || 58 ||</font></p>  
 <p>The Nâdis should be cleansed of their impurities by performing the mudrâs, etc., (which are the practices relating to the air) Âsanas, Kumbhakas and various curious mûdrâs. <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_58.">58.</a></font></span></p>  
 <p><font color="green">बरह्मछारी मिताहारी तयागी योग-परायणः |<br> अब्दादूर्ध्वं भवेद्सिद्धो नात्र कार्या विछारणा || ५९ ||</font></p>  
 <p><font color="green">brahmachārī mitāhārī tyāghī yogha-parāyaṇaḥ |<br> abdādūrdhvaṃ bhavedsiddho nātra kāryā vichāraṇā || 59 ||</font></p>  
 <p>By regular and close attention to Nâda (anâhata nâda) in Haṭha Yoga, a Brahmachari, sparing in diet, unattached to objects of enjoyment, and devoted to Yoga, gains success, no doubt, within a year. <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_59.">59.</a></font></span></p>  
 <p><font color="green">सुस्निग्ध-मधुराहारश्छतुर्थांश-विवर्जितः |<br> भुज्यते शिव-सम्प्रीत्यै मिताहारः स उछ्यते || ६० ||</font></p>  
 <p><font color="green">susnighdha-madhurāhāraśchaturthāṃśa-vivarjitaḥ |<br> bhujyate śiva-samprītyai mitāhāraḥ sa uchyate || 60 ||</font></p>  
 <p>Abstemious feeding is that in which ¾ of hunger is satisfied with food, well cooked with ghee and sweets, and eaten with the offering of it to Śiva. <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_60.">60.</a></font></span></p>  
 <p align="center"><i>Foods injurious to a Yogî</i>.</p>  
 <p><font color="green">कट्वाम्ल-तीक्ष्ह्ण-लवणोष्ह्ण-हरीत-शाक-<br> सौवीर-तैल-तिल-सर्ष्हप-मद्य-मत्स्यान |<br> आजादि-मांस-दधि-तक्र-कुलत्थकोल-<br> पिण्याक-हिङ्गु-लशुनाद्यमपथ्यमाहुः || ६१ ||</font></p>  
 <p><font color="green">kaṭvāmla-tīkṣhṇa-lavaṇoṣhṇa-harīta-śāka-<br> sauvīra-taila-tila-sarṣhapa-madya-matsyān |<br> ājādi-māṃsa-dadhi-takra-kulatthakola-<br> piṇyāka-hingghu-laśunādyamapathyamāhuḥ || 61 ||</font></p>  
 <p>Bitter, sour, saltish, hot, green vegetables, fermented, oily, mixed with til seed, rape seed, intoxicating liquors, fish, meat, curds, chhaasa pulses, plums, oil-cake, asaftida (hînga), garlic, onion, etc., should not be eaten. <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_61.">61.</a></font></span></p>  
 <p><font color="green">भोजनमहितं विद्यात्पुनरस्योष्ह्णी-कॄतं रूक्ष्हम |<br> अतिलवणमम्ल-युक्तं कदशन-शाकोत्कं वर्ज्यम || ६२ ||</font></p>  
 <p><font color="green">bhojanamahitaṃ vidyātpunarasyoṣhṇī-kṝtaṃ rūkṣham |<br> atilavaṇamamla-yuktaṃ kadaśana-śākotkaṃ varjyam || 62 ||</font></p>  
 <p>Food heated again, dry, having too much salt, sour, minor grains, and vegetables that cause burning sensation, should not be eaten, Fire, women, travelling, etc., should be avoided. <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_62.">62.</a></font></span></p>  
 <p><a name="page_11"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 11</font></a></p>  
 <p><font color="green">वह्नि-सत्री-पथि-सेवानामादौ वर्जनमाछरेत || ६३ ||</font></p>  
 <p><font color="green">vahni-strī-pathi-sevānāmādau varjanamācharet || 63 ||</font></p>  
 <p>As said by Gorakṣa, one should keep aloof from the society of the evil-minded, fire, women, travelling, early morning bath, fasting, and all kinds of bodily exertion. <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_63.">63.</a></font></span></p>  
 <p><font color="green">तथा हि गोरक्ष्ह-वछनम<br> वर्जयेद्दुर्जन-परान्तं वह्नि-सत्री-पथि-सेवनम |<br> परातः-सनानोपवासादि काय-कलेश-विधिं तथा || ६४ ||</font></p>  
 <p><font color="green">tathā hi ghorakṣha-vachanam<br> varjayeddurjana-prāntaṃ vahni-strī-pathi-sevanam |<br> prātaḥ-snānopavāsādi kāya-kleśa-vidhiṃ tathā || 64 ||</font></p>  
 <p>Wheat, rice, barley, shâstik (a kind of rice), good corns, milk, ghee, sugar, butter, sugarcandy, honey, dried ginger, Parwal (a vegetable) the five vegetables, moong, pure water, these are very beneficial to those who practise Yoga. <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_64.">64.</a></font></span></p>  
 <p><font color="green">गोधूम-शालि-यव-षहाष्ह्टिक-शोभनान्नं<br> कष्हीराज्य-खण्ड-नवनीत-सिद्धा-मधूनि |<br> शुण्ठी-पटोल-कफलादिक-पञ्छ-शाकं<br> मुद्गादि-दिव्यमुदकं छ यमीन्द्र-पथ्यम || ६५ ||</font></p>  
 <p><font color="green">ghodhūma-śāli-yava-ṣhāṣhṭika-śobhanānnaṃ<br> kṣhīrājya-khaṇḍa-navanīta-siddhā-madhūni |<br> śuṇṭhī-paṭola-kaphalādika-pañcha-śākaṃ<br> mudghādi-divyamudakaṃ cha yamīndra-pathyam || 65 ||</font></p>  
 <p>A Yogî should eat tonics (things giving strength), well sweetened, greasy (made with ghee), milk, butter, etc., which may increase humors of the body, according to his desire. <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_65.">65.</a></font></span></p>  
 <p><font color="green">पुष्ह्टं सुमधुरं सनिग्धं गव्यं धातु-परपोष्हणम |<br> मनोभिलष्हितं योग्यं योगी भोजनमाछरेत || ६६ ||</font></p>  
 <p><font color="green">puṣhṭaṃ sumadhuraṃ snighdhaṃ ghavyaṃ dhātu-prapoṣhaṇam |<br> manobhilaṣhitaṃ yoghyaṃ yoghī bhojanamācharet || 66 ||</font></p>  
 <p>Whether young, old or too old, sick or lean, one who discards laziness, gets success if he practises Yoga. <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_66.">66.</a></font></span></p>  
 <p><font color="green">युवो वॄद्धो|अतिवॄद्धो वा वयाधितो दुर्बलो|अपि वा |<br> अभ्यासात्सिद्धिमाप्नोति सर्व-योगेष्ह्वतन्द्रितः || ६७ ||</font></p>  
 <p><font color="green">yuvo vṝddho|ativṝddho vā vyādhito durbalo|api vā |<br> abhyāsātsiddhimāpnoti sarva-yogheṣhvatandritaḥ || 67 ||</font></p>  
 <p>Success comes to him who is engaged in the practice. How can one get success without practice; for by merely reading books on Yoga, one can never get success. <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_67.">67.</a></font></span></p>  
 <p><font color="green">करिया-युक्तस्य सिद्धिः सयादक्रियस्य कथं भवेत |<br> न शास्त्र-पाठ-मात्रेण योग-सिद्धिः परजायते || ६८ ||</font></p>  
 <p><font color="green">kriyā-yuktasya siddhiḥ syādakriyasya kathaṃ bhavet |<br> na śāstra-pāṭha-mātreṇa yogha-siddhiḥ prajāyate || 68 ||</font></p>  
 <p>Success cannot be attained by adopting a particular dress (Veṣa). It cannot be gained by telling tales. Practice alone is the means to success. This is true, there is no doubt. <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_68.">68.</a></font></span></p>  
 <p><a name="page_12"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 12</font></a></p>  
 <p><font color="green">न वेष्ह-धारणं सिद्धेः कारणं न छ तत-कथा |<br> करियैव कारणं सिद्धेः सत्यमेतन्न संशयः || ६९ ||<br> पीठानि कुम्भकाश्छित्रा दिव्यानि करणानि छ |<br> सर्वाण्यपि हठाभ्यासे राज-योग-फलावधि || ७० ||</font></p>  
 <p><font color="green">na veṣha-dhāraṇaṃ siddheḥ kāraṇaṃ na cha tat-kathā |<br> kriyaiva kāraṇaṃ siddheḥ satyametanna saṃśayaḥ || 69 ||<br> pīṭhāni kumbhakāśchitrā divyāni karaṇāni cha |<br> sarvāṇyapi haṭhābhyāse rāja-yogha-phalāvadhi || 70 ||</font></p>  
 <p>Âsanas (postures), various Kumbhakas, and other divine means, all should be practised in the practice of Haṭha Yoga, till the fruitRâja Yogais obtained. <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_69.">69.</a></font></span></p>  
 <p align="center">End of chapter 1st, on the method of forming the Âsanas.</p>
