 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Grihya Sutras, Part 1 (SBE29)</i>, by Hermann Oldenberg, [1886], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">KA<i>N</i><i>D</i>IKÂ 16.</h3>  
 <p>1 <a name="fr_444"></a><a href="#fn_443"><font size="1">1</font></a>. In the sixth month the Annaprâ<i>s</i>ana (i.e. the first feeding with solid food).</p>  
 <p>2. Goat's flesh, if he is desirous of nourishment,</p>  
 <p>3. Flesh of partridge, if desirous of holy lustre,</p>  
 <p>4. Boiled rice with ghee, if desirous of splendour:</p>  
 <p>5. (Such) food, mixed with curds, honey and ghee he should give (to the child) to eat with (the verse), 'Lord of food, give us food painless and strong;</p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p><a name="page_184"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 184</font></a></p>  
 <p>bring forward the giver; bestow power on us, on men and animals.'</p>  
 <p>6. The rite only (without the Mantra) for a girl.</p>
