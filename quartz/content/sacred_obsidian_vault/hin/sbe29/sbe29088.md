 <p>1 <a name="fr_306"></a><a href="#fn_305"><font size="1">1</font></a>. If (his wife) gives birth to a child, without the Sîmantonnayana having been performed,</p>  
 <p>2 <a name="fr_307"></a><a href="#fn_306"><font size="1">2</font></a>. (Or if) the <i>G</i>âtakarman has not been performed (for the child),</p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p><a name="page_138"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 138</font></a></p>  
 <p>3 <a name="fr_308"></a><a href="#fn_307"><font size="1">7_3</font></a>. He places, when ten days have elapsed since (the delivery), the little child in the mother's lap,</p>
