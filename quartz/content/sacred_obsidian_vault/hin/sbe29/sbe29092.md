 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Grihya Sutras, Part 1 (SBE29)</i>, by Hermann Oldenberg, [1886], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">KHA<i>N</i><i>D</i>A 11.</h3>  
 <p>1. If an anthill arises in his house, the house should be abandoned.</p>  
 <p>2 <a name="fr_319"></a><a href="#fn_318"><font size="1">2</font></a>. Then, after having fasted three nights (and days), he should perform the great expiation.</p>  
 <p align="center">Here ends the Fifth Adhyâya.</p>
