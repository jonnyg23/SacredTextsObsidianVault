 II. atha dvitīyodhyāyaḥ.  (sāṅkhyayogaḥ)<br>
 <br>
 sañjaya uvāca<br>
 <br>
 taṃ tathā kṛpayāviṣṭam aśrupūrṇākulekṣaṇam<br>
 viṣīdantam idaṃ vākyam uvāca madhusūdanaḥ 2.1<br>
 <br>
 śrībhagavān uvāca<br>
 <br>
 kutas tvā kaśmalam idaṃ viṣame samupasthitam<br>
 anāryajuṣṭam asvargyam akīrtikaram arjuna 2.2<br>
 <br>
 klaibyaṃ mā sma gamaḥ pārtha naitat tvayy<br>
 upapadyate<br>
 kṣudraṃ hṛdayadaurbalyaṃ tyaktvottiṣṭha paraṃtapa<br>
 2.3<br>
 <br>
 arjuna uvāca<br>
 <br>
 kathaṃ bhīṣmam ahaṃ sāṅkhye droṇaṃ ca<br>
 madhusūdana<br>
 iṣubhiḥ pratiyotsyāmi pūjārhāv arisūdana 2.4<br>
 <br>
 gurūn ahatvā hi mahānubhāvān<br>
 śreyo bhoktuṃ bhaikṣyam apīha loke<br>
 hatvārthakāmāṃstu gurunihaiva<br>
 bhuñjjīya bhogān rudhirapradigdhān 2.5<br>
 <br>
 na caitad vidmaḥ kataran no garīyo<br>
 yad vā jayema yadi vā no jayeyuḥ<br>
 yān eva hatvā na jijīviṣāmas<br>
 tevasthitāḥ pramukhe dhārtarāṣṭrāḥ 2.6<br>
 <br>
 kārpaṇyadoṣopahatasvabhāvaḥ<br>
 pṛcchāmi tvāṃ dharmasaṃmūḍhacetāḥ<br>
 yac chreyaḥ syān niścitaṃ brūhi tan me<br>
 śiṣyastehaṃ śādhi māṃ tvāṃ prapannam 2.7<br>
 <br>
 na hi prapaśyāmi mamāpanudyād<br>
 yac chokam ucchoṣaṇam indriyāṇām<br>
 avāpya bhūmāv asapatnam ṛddhaṃ<br>
 rājyaṃ surāṇām api cādhipatyam 2.8<br>
 <br>
 sañjaya uvāca<br>
 <br>
 evam uktvā hṛṣīkeśaṃ guḍākeśaḥ paraṃtapaḥ<br>
 na yotsya iti govindam uktvā tūṣṇīṃ babhūva ha 2.9<br>
 <br>
 tam uvāca hṛṣīkeśaḥ prahasann iva bhārata<br>
 senayor ubhayor madhye viṣīdantam idaṃ vacaḥ 2.10<br>
 <br>
 śrībhagavān uvāca<br>
 <br>
 aśocyān anvaśocas tvaṃ prajñāvādāṃś ca bhāṣase<br>
 gatāsūn agatāsūṃś ca nānuśocanti paṇḍitāḥ 2.11<br>
 <br>
 natv evāhaṃ jātu nāsaṃ na tvaṃ neme janādhipāḥ<br>
 na caiva na bhaviṣyāmaḥ sarve vayam ataḥ param<br>
 2.12<br>
 <br>
 dehinosmin yathā dehe kaumāraṃ yauvanaṃ jarā<br>
 tathā dehāntaraprāptir dhīras tatra na muhyati 2.13<br>
 <br>
 mātrāsparśās tu kaunteya śītoṣṇasukhaduḥkhadāḥ<br>
 āgamāpāyinonityās tāṃs titikṣasva bhārata 2.14<br>
 <br>
 yaṃ hi na vyathayanty ete puruṣaṃ puruṣarṣabha<br>
 samaduḥkhasukhaṃ dhīraṃ somṛtatvāya kalpate 2.15<br>
 <br>
 nāsato vidyate bhāvo nābhāvo vidyate sataḥ<br>
 ubhayor api dṛṣṭo.antas tv anayos tattvadarśibhiḥ 2.16<br>
 <br>
 avināśi tu tad viddhi yena sarvam idaṃ tatam<br>
 vināśam avyayasyāsya na kaścit kartum arhati 2.17<br>
 <br>
 antavanta ime dehā nityasyoktāḥ śarīriṇaḥ<br>
 anāśinoprameyasya tasmād yudhyasva bhārata 2.18<br>
 <br>
 ya enaṃ vetti hantāraṃ yaś cainaṃ manyate hatam<br>
 ubhau tau na vijānīto nāyaṃ hanti na hanyate 2.19<br>
 <br>
 na jāyate mriyate vā kadācin<br>
 nāyaṃ bhūtvā bhavitā vā na bhūyaḥ<br>
 ajo nityaḥ śāśvatoyaṃ purāṇo<br>
 na hanyate hanyamāne śarīre 2.20   <br>
 <br>
 vedāvināśinaṃ nityaṃ ya enam ajam avyayam<br>
 kathaṃ sa puruṣaḥ pārtha kaṃ ghātayati hanti kam<br>
 2.21<br>
 <br>
 vāsāṃsi jīrṇāni yathā vihāya<br>
 navāni gṛhṇāti naroparāṇi<br>
 tathā śarīrāṇi vihāya jīrṇāni<br>
 anyāni saṃyāti navāni dehī 2.20<br>
 <br>
 nainaṃ chindanti śastrāṇi nainaṃ dahati pāvakaḥ<br>
 na cainaṃ kledayanty āpo na śoṣayati mārutaḥ 2.23<br>
 <br>
 acchedyoyam adāhyoyam akledyośoṣya eva ca<br>
 nityaḥ sarvagataḥ sthāṇur acaloyaṃ sanātanaḥ 2.24<br>
 <br>
 avyaktoyam acintyoyam avikaryoyam ucyate<br>
 tasmād evaṃ viditvainaṃ nānuśocitum arhasi 2.25<br>
 <br>
 atha cainaṃ nityajātaṃ nityaṃ vā manyase mṛtam<br>
 tathāpi tvaṃ mahābāho naivaṃ śocitum arhasi 2.26<br>
 <br>
 jātasya hi dhruvo mṛtyur dhruvaṃ janma mṛtasya ca<br>
 tasmād aparihāryerthe na tvaṃ śocitum arhasi 2.27<br>
 <br>
 avyaktādīni bhūtāni vyaktamadhyāni bhārata<br>
 avyaktanidhanāny eva tatra kā paridevanā 2.28<br>
 <br>
 āścaryavat paśyati kaścid enam<br>
 āścaryavad vadati tathaiva cānyaḥ<br>
 āścaryavac cainam anyaḥ śṛṇoti<br>
 śrutvāpy enaṃ veda na caiva kaścit 2.29<br>
 <br>
 dehī nityam avadhyoyaṃ dehe sarvasya bhārata<br>
 tasmāt sarvāṇi bhūtāni na tvaṃ śocitum arhasi 2.30<br>
 <br>
 svadharmam api cāvekṣya na vikampitum arhasi<br>
 dharmyād dhi yuddhāc chreyonyat kṣatriyasya na<br>
 vidyate 2.31<br>
 <br>
 yadṛcchayā copapannaṃ svargadvāram apāvṛtam<br>
 sukhinaḥ kṣatriyāḥ pārtha labhante yuddham īdṛśam<br>
 2.32<br>
 <br>
 atha cet tvam imaṃ dhārmyaṃ saṅgrāmaṃ na<br>
 kariṣyasi<br>
 tataḥ svadharmaṃ kīrtiṃ ca hitvā pāpam avāpsyasi<br>
 2.33<br>
 <br>
 akīrtiṃ cāpi bhūtāni kathayiṣyanti tevyayām<br>
 saṃbhāvitasya cākīrtir maraṇād atiricyate 2.34<br>
 <br>
 bhayād raṇād uparataṃ maṃsyante tvāṃ mahārathāḥ<br>
 yeṣāṃ ca tvaṃ bahumato bhūtvā yāsyasi lāghavam<br>
 2.35<br>
 <br>
 avācyavādāṃś ca bahūn vadiṣyanti tavāhitāḥ<br>
 nindantas tava sāmarthyaṃ tato duḥkhataraṃ nu kim<br>
 2.36<br>
 <br>
 hato vā prāpsyasi svargaṃ jitvā vā bhokṣyase mahīm<br>
 tasmād uttiṣṭha kaunteya yuddhāya kṛtaniścayaḥ 2.37<br>
 <br>
 sukhaduḥkhe same kṛtvā lābhālābhau jayājayau<br>
 tato yuddhāya yujyasva naivaṃ pāpam avāpsyasi 2.38<br>
 <br>
 eṣā tebhihitā sāṅkhye buddhir yoge tv imāṃ śṛṇu<br>
 buddhyā yukto yayā pārtha karmabandhaṃ<br>
 prahāsyasi 2.39<br>
 <br>
 nehābhikramanāśosti pratyavāyo na vidyate<br>
 svalpam apy asya dharmasya trāyate mahato bhayāt<br>
 2.40<br>
 <br>
 vyavasāyātmikā buddhir ekeha kurunandana<br>
 bahuśākhā hy anantāś ca buddhayovyavasāyinām 2.41<br>
 <br>
 yām imāṃ puṣpitāṃ vācaṃ pravadanty avipaścitaḥ<br>
 vedavādaratāḥ pārtha nānyad astīti vādinaḥ 2.42<br>
 <br>
 kāmātmānaḥ svargaparā janmakarmaphalapradām<br>
 kriyāviśeṣabahulāṃ bhogaiśvaryagatiṃ prati 2.43<br>
 <br>
 bhogaiśvaryaprasaktānāṃ tayāpahṛtacetasām<br>
 vyavasāyātmikā buddhiḥ samādhau na vidhīyate 2.44<br>
 <br>
 traiguṇyaviṣayā vedā nistraiguṇyo bhavārjuna<br>
 nirdvandvo nityasatvastho niryogakṣema ātmavān 2.45<br>
 <br>
 yāvān artha udapāne sarvataḥ saṃplutodake<br>
 tāvān sarveṣu vedeṣu brāhmaṇasya vijānataḥ 2.46<br>
 <br>
 karmaṇy evādhikāras te mā phaleṣu kadācana<br>
 mā karmaphalahetur bhūr mā te saṅgostv akarmaṇi<br>
 2.47<br>
 <br>
 yogasthaḥ kuru karmāṇi saṅgaṃ tyaktvā dhanañjaya<br>
 siddhyasiddhyoḥ samo bhūtvā samatvaṃ yoga ucyate<br>
 2.48<br>
 <br>
 dūreṇa hy avaraṃ karma buddhiyogād dhanañjaya<br>
 buddhau śaraṇam anviccha kṛpaṇāḥ phalahetavaḥ 2.49<br>
 <br>
 buddhiyukto jahātīha ubhe sukṛtaduṣkṛte<br>
 tasmād yogāya yujyasva yogaḥ karmasu kauśalam 2.50<br>
 <br>
 karmajaṃ buddhiyuktā hi phalaṃ tyaktvā manīṣiṇaḥ<br>
 janmabandhavinirmuktāḥ padaṃ gacchhanty<br>
 anāmayam 2.51<br>
 <br>
 yadā te mohakalilaṃ buddhir vyatitariṣyati<br>
 tadā gantāsi nirvedaṃ śrotavyasya śrutasya ca 2.52<br>
 <br>
 śrutivipratipannā te yadā sthāsyati niścalā<br>
 samādhāv acalā buddhis tadā yogam avāpsyasi 2.53<br>
 <br>
 arjuna uvāca<br>
 <br>
 sthitaprajñasya kā bhāṣā samādhisthasya keśava<br>
 sthitadhīḥ kiṃ prabhāṣeta kim āsīta vrajeta kim 2.54<br>
 <br>
 śrībhagavān uvāca<br>
 <br>
 prajahāti yadā kāmān sarvān pārtha manogatān<br>
 ātmany evātmanā tuṣṭaḥ sthitaprajñas tadocyate 2.55<br>
 <br>
 duḥkheṣv anudvignamanāḥ sukheṣu vigataspṛhaḥ<br>
 vītarāgabhayakrodhaḥ sthitadhīr munir ucyate 2.56<br>
 <br>
 yaḥ sarvatrānabhisnehas tattatprāpya śubhāśubham<br>
 nābhinandati na dveṣṭi tasya prajñā pratiṣṭhitā 2.57<br>
 <br>
 yadā saṃharate cāyaṃ kūrmoṅgānīva sarvaśaḥ<br>
 indriyāṇīndriyārthebhyas tasya prajñā pratiṣṭhitā 2.58<br>
 <br>
 viṣayā vinivartante nirāhārasya dehinaḥ<br>
 rasavarjaṃ rasopy asya paraṃ dṛṣṭvā nivartate 2.59<br>
 <br>
 yatato hy api kaunteya puruṣasya vipaścitaḥ<br>
 indriyāṇi pramāthīni haranti prasabhaṃ manaḥ 2.60<br>
 <br>
 tāni sarvāṇi saṃyamya yukta āsīta matparaḥ<br>
 vaśe hi yasyendriyāṇi tasya prajñā pratiṣṭhitā 2.61<br>
 <br>
 dhyāyato viṣayān puṃsaḥ saṅgas teṣūpajāyate<br>
 saṅgāt sañjāyate kāmaḥ kāmāt krodhobhijāyate 2.62<br>
 <br>
 krodhād bhavati saṃmohaḥ saṃmohāt<br>
 smṛtivibhramaḥ<br>
 smṛtibhraṃśād buddhināśo buddhināśāt praṇaśyati<br>
 2.63<br>
 <br>
 rāgadveṣavimuktais tu viṣayān indriyaiś caran<br>
 ātmavaśyair vidheyātmā prasādam adhigacchati 2.64<br>
 <br>
 prasāde sarvaduḥkhānāṃ hānir asyopajāyate<br>
 prasannacetaso hy āśu buddhiḥ paryavatiṣṭhate 2.65<br>
 <br>
 nāsti buddhir ayuktasya na cāyuktasya bhāvanā<br>
 na cābhāvayataḥ śāntir aśāntasya kutaḥ sukham 2.66.<br>
 <br>
 indriyāṇāṃ hi caratāṃ yan manonuvidhīyate<br>
 tad asya harati prajñāṃ vāyur nāvam ivāmbhasi 2.67<br>
 <br>
 tasmād yasya mahābāho nigṛhītāni sarvaśaḥ<br>
 indriyāṇīndriyārthebhyas tasya prajñā pratiṣṭhitā 2.68<br>
 <br>
 yā niśā sarvabhūtānāṃ tasyāṃ jāgarti saṃyamī<br>
 yasyāṃ jāgrati bhūtāni sā niśā paśyato muneḥ 2.69<br>
 <br>
 āpūryamāṇam acalapratiṣṭhaṃ<br>
 samudram āpaḥ praviśanti yadvat<br>
 tadvat kāmā yaṃ praviśanti sarve<br>
 sa śāntim āpnoti na kāmakāmī 2.70<br>
 <br>
 vihāya kāmān yaḥ sarvān pumāṃś carati niḥspṛhaḥ<br>
 nirmamo nirahaṃkāraḥ sa śāntim adhigacchhati 2.71<br>
 <br>
 eṣā brāhmī sthitiḥ pārtha naināṃ prāpya vimuhyati<br>
 sthitvāsyām antakālepi brahmanirvāṇam ṛcchati 2.72<br>
 <br>
