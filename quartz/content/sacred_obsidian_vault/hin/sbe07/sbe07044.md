 <a name="page_140"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 140</font></a><h1 align="CENTER">XLII.</h1>
 <p>1. Miscellaneous crimes are those which have not been mentioned before.</p>
 <p>2. Having committed one out of the number of miscellaneous crimes, a prudent man should always perform a penance, by the advice of a Brâhma<i>n</i>a, after the higher or less degree of his guilt has been ascertained.</p>
