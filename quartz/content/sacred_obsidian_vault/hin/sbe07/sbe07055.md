 <a name="page_174"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 174</font></a><h1 align="CENTER">LIII.</h1>
 <p>1. One who has (unawares) had illicit sexual intercourse<sup>[1]</sup>, must perform the Prâ<i>g</i>âpatya penance for a year, according to the rule of the Mahâvrata, clad in a garment of bark, and living in a forest.</p>
 <p>2. The same (penance is ordained) for sexual intercourse with the wife of another man (who belongs to his own caste, but is no Guru of his).</p>
 <p>3. For intercourse with a cow, the Govrata (must be performed).</p>
 <p>4. For intercourse with a man, for unnatural crimes with a woman, (for wasting his manhood) in the air, (for intercourse with a woman) in water, by day, or in a go-cart<sup>[1]</sup>, he must bathe dressed in his clothes.</p>
 <p>5. By intercourse (knowingly) with a <i>K</i>a<i>nd</i>âla woman[1], he becomes her equal in caste.</p>
 <p>6. For intercourse unawares with such, he must perform the <i>K</i>ândrâya<i>n</i>a twice.</p>
 <p>7. For intercourse (knowingly) with cattle (other) than cows) or with a public prostitute, (he must perform) the Prâ<i>g</i>âpatya penance.</p>
 <p>8. A woman who has committed adultery once,</p>
 <font size="2"><p>[LIII. 1-8. M. XI, 106, 171-177.--4. Y. III, 291.--9. M. XI, 179.</p>
 <p>1. <sup>1</sup> The crime intended here is explained by Nand. as being illicit intercourse with a step-mother, who belongs to the <i>S</i>ûdra caste.</p>
 <p>3. See L, 16-24.</p>
 <p>4. <sup>1</sup> 'Or in a cart drawn by asses or by other beasts of draught, as the particle <i>k</i>a implies.' (Nand.)</p>
 <p>5. <sup>1</sup> 'Or with a woman of an equally degraded caste, such as tile Svapaka caste and others.' (Nand.)</p>
 <p>8. See Sûtra 2.]</p>
 </font><p><a name="page_175"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 175</font></a></p>
 <p>must perform that penance which has been prescribed for an adulterer.</p>
 <p>9. That guilt which a Brâhma<i>n</i>a incurs by intercourse with a <i>K</i>a<i>nd</i>âla woman one night, he can only remove by subsisting upon alms, and constantly repeating (the Gâyatrî) for three years.</p>
