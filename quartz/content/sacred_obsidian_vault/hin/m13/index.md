 <h1 align="CENTER">The Mahabharata</h1>
 <h2 align="CENTER">Book 13: Anusasana Parva</h2>
 <h3 align="CENTER">Kisari Mohan Ganguli, tr.</h3>
 <h4 align="CENTER">[1883-1896]</h4>
 <hr>
 <p>
 The Anusasana Parva, like the Santi Parva before it, is a long hiatus
 in the narrative of the Mahabharata.
 Set on the deathbed of Bhishma, the Anusasana Parva is a huge treasure-trove
 of Brahmanic lore which is obviously a later interpolation in the epic.
 </p>
