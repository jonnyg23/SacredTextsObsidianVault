 <p>1. Let a man meditate on the fivefold Sâman in all waters. When the clouds gather, that is the hiṅkâra; when it rains, that is the prastâva that which flows in the east <a name="fr_129"></a><a href="#fn_128"><font size="1">2</font></a>, that is the udgîtha that which flows in the west <a name="fr_130"></a><a href="#fn_129"><font size="1">3</font></a>, that is the pratihâra the sea is the nidhana.</p>  
 <p>2. He does not die in water <a name="fr_131"></a><a href="#fn_130"><font size="1">4</font></a>, nay, he is rich in</p>
