 <p>1. 'The altar is man, O Gautama; its fuel speech itself, the smoke the breath, the light the tongue, the coals the eye, the sparks the ear.</p>  
 <p>2. 'On that altar the Devas (prâ<i>n</i>as) offer food. From that oblation rises seed.</p>
