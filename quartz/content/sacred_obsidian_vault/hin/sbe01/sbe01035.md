 <p><a name="page_23"><font size="1" color="green">p. 23</font></a></p>  <h2 align="center">SECOND PRAPÂTHAKA.</h2>  <h3 align="center">FIRST KHA<i>N</i><i>D</i>A.</h3>  
 <p>1. Meditation on the whole <a name="fr_125"></a><a href="#fn_124"><font size="1">1</font></a> of the Sâman is good, and people, when anything is good, say it is Sâman; when it is not good, it is not Sâman.</p>  
 <p>2. Thus they also say, he approached him with Sâman, i. e. becomingly; and he approached him without Sâman, i. e. unbecomingly.</p>  
 <p>:3. And they also say, truly this is Sâman for us, i.e. it is good for us, when it is good; and truly that is not Sâman for us, i. e. it is not good for us, when it is not good.</p>  
 <p>4. If any one knowing this meditates on the Sâman as good, depend upon it all good qualities will approach quickly, aye, they will become his own <a name="fr_126"></a><a href="#fn_125"><font size="1">2</font></a>.</p>
