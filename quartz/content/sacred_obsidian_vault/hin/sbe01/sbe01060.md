 <p><a name="page_39"><font size="1" color="green">p. 39</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">SECOND KHA<i>N</i><i>D</i>A.</h3>  
 <p>1. The southern rays of the sun are the honey-cells on the right. The Ya<i>g</i>us verses are the bees, the Ya<i>g</i>ur-veda sacrifice is the flower, the water (of the sacrificial libations) is the nectar (of the flower).</p>  
 <p>2. Those very Ya<i>g</i>us verses (as bees) brooded over the Ya<i>g</i>ur-veda sacrifice (the flower); and from it, thus brooded on, sprang as its (nectar) essence, fame, glory of countenance, vigour, strength, and health.</p>  
 <p>3. That flowed forth and went towards the sun. And that forms what we call the white (<i>s</i>ukla) light of the sun.</p>
