 <p>I. 50. <i>bhram kâ tâlâ lagâ mahal re</i></p> 
 <p>THE lock of error shuts the gate, open it with the key of love: Thus, by opening the door, thou shalt wake the Beloved. <a name="page_87"><font size="1" color="green">p. 87</font></a><br> Kabîr says: "O brother! do not pass by such good fortune as this."</p>
