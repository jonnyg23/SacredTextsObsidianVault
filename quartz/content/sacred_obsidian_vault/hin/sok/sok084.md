 <p>III. 84. <i>jhî jhî jantar bâjai</i></p> 
 <p>THE harp gives forth murmurous music; and the dance goes on without hands and feet.<br> It is played without fingers, it is heard without ears: for He is the ear, and He is the listener.<br> The gate is locked, but within there is fragrance: and there the meeting is seen of none.<br> The wise shall understand it.</p>
