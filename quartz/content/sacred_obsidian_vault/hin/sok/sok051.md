 <p>I. 126. <i>muralî bajat akha<b>nd</b> sadâye</i></p> 
 <p>THE flute of the Infinite is played without ceasing, and its sound is love:<br> When love renounces all limits, it reaches truth.<br> How widely the fragrance spreads! It has no end, nothing stands in its way.<br> The form of this melody is bright like <a name="page_97"><font size="1" color="green">p. 97</font></a> a million suns: incomparably sounds the vina, the vina of the notes of truth.</p>
