 <p>III. 69. <i>satta nâm hai sab te<u>n</u> nyârâ</i></p> 
 <p>THE true Name is like none other name!<br> The distinction of the Conditioned from the Unconditioned is but a word:<br> The Unconditioned is the seed, the Conditioned is the flower and the fruit.<br> Knowledge is the branch, and the Name is the root.<br> Look, and see where the root is: happiness shall be yours when you come to the root.<br> The root will lead you to the branch, the leaf, the flower, and the fruit:<br> It is the encounter with the Lord, it is the attainment of bliss, it is the reconciliation of the Conditioned and the Unconditioned.</p>
