 <p>I. 101. <i>is gha<b>t</b> antar bâg bagîce</i></p> 
 <p>WITHIN this earthen vessel are bowers and groves, and within it is the Creator:<br> Within this vessel are the seven oceans and the unnumbered stars.<br> The touchstone and the jewel-appraiser are within;<br> And within this vessel the Eternal soundeth, and the spring wells up.<br> Kabîr says: "Listen tome, my Friend! My beloved Lord is within."</p>
