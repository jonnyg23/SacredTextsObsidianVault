   <h2 align="center">VI, 105. Charm against cough.</h2>  
 <p>1. As the-soul with the soul's desires swiftly to a distance flies, thus do thou, O cough, fly forth along the soul's course of flight!<br> 2. As a well-sharpened arrow swiftly to a distance flies, thus do thou, O cough, fly forth along the expanse of the earth!<br> 3. As the rays of the sun swiftly to a distance fly, thus do thou, O cough, fly forth along the flood of the sea!</p>
