   <h2 align="center">VI, 59. Prayer to the plant arundhatî for protection to cattle.</h2>  
 <p>1. Thy foremost protection, O Arundhatî, do thou bestow upon steer and milch-kine, upon (cattle of) the age when weaned from their mother, upon (all) four-footed creatures!<br> 2. May Arundhatî, the herb, bestow protection along with the gods, render full of sap the stable, free from disease our men!<br> 3. The variegated, lovely, life-giving (plant) do I invoke. May she carry away for us, far from the cattle, the missile hurled by Rudra!</p>
