   <h2 align="center">VI, 17. Charm to prevent miscarriage.</h2>  
 <p>1. As this great earth conceives the germs of the beings, thus shalt thy embryo be-beld fast, to produce a child after pregnancy!<br> 2. As this great earth holds these trees, thus shall thy embryo be held fast, to produce a child after pregnancy!<br> 3. As this great earth holds the mountains and the peaks, thus shall thy embryo be held fast, to produce a child after pregnancy!<br> 4. As this great earth holds the animals scattered far, thus shall thy embryo be held fast, to produce a child after pregnancy!</p>
