 <h1 align="CENTER">Book 5
 </h1><h3 align="CENTER">Chapter 1
 </h3><table width="100%">
 <td width="50%">
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">1</font>
  tato rāvaṇanītāyāḥ sītāyāḥ śatrukarśanaḥ<br>
   iyeṣa padam anveṣṭuṃ cāraṇācarite pathi<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">2</font>
  atha vaidūryavarṇeṣu śādvaleṣu mahābalaḥ<br>
   dhīraḥ salilakalpeṣu vicacāra yathāsukham<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">3</font>
  dvijān vitrāsayan dhīmān urasā pādapān haran<br>
   mṛgāṃś ca subahūn nighnan pravṛddha iva kesarī<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">4</font>
  nīlalohitamāñjiṣṭhapadmavarṇaiḥ sitāsitaiḥ<br>
   svabhāva vihitaiś citrair dhātubhiḥ samalaṃkṛtam<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">5</font>
  kāmarūpibhir āviṣṭam abhīkṣṇaṃ saparicchadaiḥ<br>
   yakṣakiṃnaragandharvair devakalpaiś ca pannagaiḥ<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">6</font>
  sa tasya girivaryasya tale nāgavarāyute<br>
   tiṣṭhan kapivaras tatra hrade nāga ivābabhau<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">7</font>
  sa sūryāya mahendrāya pavanāya svayambhuve<br>
   bhūtebhyaś cāñjaliṃ kṛtvā cakāra gamane matim<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">8</font>
  añjaliṃ prāṅmukhaḥ kurvan pavanāyātmayonaye<br>
   tato hi vavṛdhe gantuṃ dakṣiṇo dakṣiṇāṃ diśam<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">9</font>
  plavaṃgapravarair dṛṣṭaḥ plavane kṛtaniścayaḥ<br>
   vavṛdhe rāmavṛddhyarthaṃ samudra iva parvasu<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">10</font>
  niṣpramāṇa śarīraḥ saṃl lilaṅghayiṣur arṇavam<br>
    bāhubhyāṃ pīḍayām āsa caraṇābhyāṃ ca parvatam<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">11</font>
  sa cacālācalāś cāru muhūrtaṃ kapipīḍitaḥ<br>
    tarūṇāṃ puṣpitāgrāṇāṃ sarvaṃ puṣpam aśātayat<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">12</font>
  tena pādapamuktena puṣpaugheṇa sugandhinā<br>
    sarvataḥ saṃvṛtaḥ śailo babhau puṣpamayo yathā<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">13</font>
  tena cottamavīryeṇa pīḍyamānaḥ sa parvataḥ<br>
    salilaṃ saṃprasusrāva madaṃ matta iva dvipaḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">14</font>
  pīḍyamānas tu balinā mahendras tena parvataḥ<br>
    rītir nirvartayām āsa kāñcanāñjanarājatīḥ<br>
    mumoca ca śilāḥ śailo viśālāḥ samanaḥśilāḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">15</font>
  giriṇā pīḍyamānena pīḍyamānāni sarvaśaḥ<br>
    guhāviṣṭāni bhūtāni vinedur vikṛtaiḥ svaraiḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">16</font>
  sa mahāsattvasaṃnādaḥ śailapīḍānimittajaḥ<br>
    pṛthivīṃ pūrayām āsa diśaś copavanāni ca<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">17</font>
  śirobhiḥ pṛthubhiḥ sarpā vyaktasvastikalakṣaṇaiḥ<br>
    vamantaḥ pāvakaṃ ghoraṃ dadaṃśur daśanaiḥ śilāḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">18</font>
  tās tadā saviṣair daṣṭāḥ kupitais tair mahāśilāḥ<br>
    jajvaluḥ pāvakoddīptā vibhiduś ca sahasradhā<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">19</font>
  yāni cauṣadhajālāni tasmiñ jātāni parvate<br>
    viṣaghnāny api nāgānāṃ na śekuḥ śamituṃ viṣam<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">20</font>
  bhidyate 'yaṃ girir bhūtair iti matvā tapasvinaḥ<br>
    trastā vidyādharās tasmād utpetuḥ strīgaṇaiḥ saha<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">21</font>
  pānabhūmigataṃ hitvā haimam āsanabhājanam<br>
    pātrāṇi ca mahārhāṇi karakāṃś ca hiraṇmayān<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">22</font>
  lehyān uccāvacān bhakṣyān māṃsāni vividhāni ca<br>
    ārṣabhāṇi ca carmāṇi khaḍgāṃś ca kanakatsarūn<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">23</font>
  kṛtakaṇṭhaguṇāḥ kṣībā raktamālyānulepanāḥ<br>
    raktākṣāḥ puṣkarākṣāś ca gaganaṃ pratipedire<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">24</font>
  hāranūpurakeyūra pārihārya dharāḥ striyaḥ<br>
    vismitāḥ sasmitās tasthur ākāśe ramaṇaiḥ saha<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">25</font>
  darśayanto mahāvidyāṃ vidyādharamaharṣayaḥ<br>
    sahitās tasthur ākāśe vīkṣāṃ cakruś ca parvatam<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">26</font>
  śuśruvuś ca tadā śabdam ṛṣīṇāṃ bhāvitātmanām<br>
    cāraṇānāṃ ca siddhānāṃ sthitānāṃ vimale 'mbare<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">27</font>
  eṣa parvatasaṃkāśo hanūmān mārutātmajaḥ<br>
    titīrṣati mahāvegaṃ samudraṃ makarālayam<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">28</font>
  rāmārthaṃ vānarārthaṃ ca cikīrṣan karma duṣkaram<br>
    samudrasya paraṃ pāraṃ duṣprāpaṃ prāptum icchati<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">29</font>
  dudhuve ca sa romāṇi cakampe cācalopamaḥ<br>
    nanāda ca mahānādaṃ sumahān iva toyadaḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">30</font>
  ānupūrvyāc ca vṛttaṃ ca lāṅgūlaṃ romabhiś citam<br>
    utpatiṣyan vicikṣepa pakṣirāja ivoragam<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">31</font>
  tasya lāṅgūlam āviddham ativegasya pṛṣṭhataḥ<br>
    dadṛśe garuḍeneva hriyamāṇo mahoragaḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">32</font>
  bāhū saṃstambhayām āsa mahāparighasaṃnibhau<br>
    sasāda ca kapiḥ kaṭyāṃ caraṇau saṃcukopa ca<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">33</font>
  saṃhṛtya ca bhujau śrīmāṃs tathaiva ca śirodharām<br>
    tejaḥ sattvaṃ tathā vīryam āviveśa sa vīryavān<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">34</font>
  mārgam ālokayan dūrād ūrdhvapraṇihitekṣaṇaḥ<br>
    rurodha hṛdaye prāṇān ākāśam avalokayan<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">35</font>
  padbhyāṃ dṛḍham avasthānaṃ kṛtvā sa kapikuñjaraḥ<br>
    nikuñcya karṇau hanumān utpatiṣyan mahābalaḥ<br>
    vānarān vānaraśreṣṭha idaṃ vacanam abravīt<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">36</font>
  yathā rāghavanirmuktaḥ śaraḥ śvasanavikramaḥ<br>
    gacchet tadvad gamiṣyāmi laṅkāṃ rāvaṇapālitām<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">37</font>
  na hi drakṣyāmi yadi tāṃ laṅkāyāṃ janakātmajām<br>
    anenaiva hi vegena gamiṣyāmi surālayam<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">38</font>
  yadi vā tridive sītāṃ na drakṣyāmi kṛtaśramaḥ<br>
    baddhvā rākṣasarājānam ānayiṣyāmi rāvaṇam<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">39</font>
  sarvathā kṛtakāryo 'ham eṣyāmi saha sītayā<br>
    ānayiṣyāmi vā laṅkāṃ samutpāṭya sarāvaṇām<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">40</font>
  evam uktvā tu hanumān vānarān vānarottamaḥ<br>
    utpapātātha vegena vegavān avicārayan<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">41</font>
  samutpatati tasmiṃs tu vegāt te nagarohiṇaḥ<br>
    saṃhṛtya viṭapān sarvān samutpetuḥ samantataḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">42</font>
  sa mattakoyaṣṭibhakān pādapān puṣpaśālinaḥ<br>
    udvahann ūruvegena jagāma vimale 'mbare<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">43</font>
  ūruvegoddhatā vṛkṣā muhūrtaṃ kapim anvayuḥ<br>
    prasthitaṃ dīrgham adhvānaṃ svabandhum iva bāndhavāḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">44</font>
  tam ūruvegonmathitāḥ sālāś cānye nagottamāḥ<br>
    anujagmur hanūmantaṃ sainyā iva mahīpatim<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">45</font>
  supuṣpitāgrair bahubhiḥ pādapair anvitaḥ kapiḥ<br>
    hanumān parvatākāro babhūvādbhutadarśanaḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">46</font>
  sāravanto 'tha ye vṛkṣā nyamajjaṁl lavaṇāmbhasi<br>
    bhayād iva mahendrasya parvatā varuṇālaye<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">47</font>
  sa nānākusumaiḥ kīrṇaḥ kapiḥ sāṅkurakorakaiḥ<br>
    śuśubhe meghasaṃkāśaḥ khadyotair iva parvataḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">48</font>
  vimuktās tasya vegena muktvā puṣpāṇi te drumāḥ<br>
    avaśīryanta salile nivṛttāḥ suhṛdo yathā<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">49</font>
  laghutvenopapannaṃ tad vicitraṃ sāgare 'patat<br>
    drumāṇāṃ vividhaṃ puṣpaṃ kapivāyusamīritam<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">50</font>
  puṣpaugheṇānubaddhena nānāvarṇena vānaraḥ<br>
    babhau megha ivodyan vai vidyudgaṇavibhūṣitaḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">51</font>
  tasya vegasamudbhūtaiḥ puṣpais toyam adṛśyata<br>
    tārābhir abhirāmābhir uditābhir ivāmbaram<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">52</font>
  tasyāmbaragatau bāhū dadṛśāte prasāritau<br>
    parvatāgrād viniṣkrāntau pañcāsyāv iva pannagau<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">53</font>
  pibann iva babhau cāpi sormijālaṃ mahārṇavam<br>
    pipāsur iva cākāśaṃ dadṛśe sa mahākapiḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">54</font>
  tasya vidyutprabhākāre vāyumārgānusāriṇaḥ<br>
    nayane viprakāśete parvatasthāv ivānalau<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">55</font>
  piṅge piṅgākṣamukhyasya bṛhatī parimaṇḍale<br>
    cakṣuṣī saṃprakaśete candrasūryāv iva sthitau<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">56</font>
  mukhaṃ nāsikayā tasya tāmrayā tāmram ābabhau<br>
    saṃdhyayā samabhispṛṣṭaṃ yathā sūryasya maṇḍalam<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">57</font>
  lāṅgalaṃ ca samāviddhaṃ plavamānasya śobhate<br>
    ambare vāyuputrasya śakradhvaja ivocchritaḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">58</font>
  lāṅgūlacakreṇa mahāñ śukladaṃṣṭro 'nilātmajaḥ<br>
    vyarocata mahāprājñaḥ pariveṣīva bhāskaraḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">59</font>
  sphigdeśenābhitāmreṇa rarāja sa mahākapiḥ<br>
    mahatā dāriteneva girir gairikadhātunā<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">60</font>
  tasya vānarasiṃhasya plavamānasya sāgaram<br>
    kakṣāntaragato vāyur jīmūta iva garjati<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">61</font>
  khe yathā nipataty ulkā uttarāntād viniḥsṛtā<br>
    dṛśyate sānubandhā ca tathā sa kapikuñjaraḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">62</font>
  patatpataṃgasaṃkāśo vyāyataḥ śuśubhe kapiḥ<br>
    pravṛddha iva mātaṃgaḥ kakṣyayā badhyamānayā<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">63</font>
  upariṣṭāc charīreṇa chāyayā cāvagāḍhayā<br>
    sāgare mārutāviṣṭā naur ivāsīt tadā kapiḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">64</font>
  yaṃ yaṃ deśaṃ samudrasya jagāma sa mahākapiḥ<br>
    sa sa tasyāṅgavegena sonmāda iva lakṣyate<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">65</font>
  sāgarasyormijālānām urasā śailavarṣmaṇām<br>
    abhighnaṃs tu mahāvegaḥ pupluve sa mahākapiḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">66</font>
  kapivātaś ca balavān meghavātaś ca niḥsṛtaḥ<br>
    sāgaraṃ bhīmanirghoṣaṃ kampayām āsatur bhṛśam<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">67</font>
  vikarṣann ūrmijālāni bṛhanti lavaṇāmbhasi<br>
    atyakrāman mahāvegas taraṅgān gaṇayann iva<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">68</font>
  plavamānaṃ samīkṣyātha bhujaṅgāḥ sāgarālayāḥ<br>
    vyomni taṃ kapiśārdūlaṃ suparṇam iti menire<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">69</font>
  daśayojanavistīrṇā triṃśadyojanam āyatā<br>
    chāyā vānarasiṃhasya jale cārutarābhavat<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">70</font>
  śvetābhraghanarājīva vāyuputrānugāminī<br>
    tasya sā śuśubhe chāyā vitatā lavaṇāmbhasi<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">71</font>
  plavamānaṃ tu taṃ dṛṣṭvā plavagaṃ tvaritaṃ tadā<br>
    vavṛṣuḥ puṣpavarṣāṇi devagandharvadānavāḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">72</font>
  tatāpa na hi taṃ sūryaḥ plavantaṃ vānareśvaram<br>
    siṣeve ca tadā vāyū rāmakāryārthasiddhaye<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">73</font>
  ṛṣayas tuṣṭuvuś cainaṃ plavamānaṃ vihāyasā<br>
    jaguś ca devagandharvāḥ praśaṃsanto mahaujasaṃ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">74</font>
  nāgāś ca tuṣṭuvur yakṣā rakṣāṃsi vibudhāḥ khagāḥ<br>
    prekṣyākāśe kapivaraṃ sahasā vigataklamam<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">75</font>
  tasmin plavagaśārdūle plavamāne hanūmati<br>
    ikṣvākukulamānārthī cintayām āsa sāgaraḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">76</font>
  sāhāyyaṃ vānarendrasya yadi nāhaṃ hanūmataḥ<br>
    kariṣyāmi bhaviṣyāmi sarvavācyo vivakṣatām<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">77</font>
  aham ikṣvākunāthena sagareṇa vivardhitaḥ<br>
    ikṣvākusacivaś cāyaṃ nāvasīditum arhati<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">78</font>
  tathā mayā vidhātavyaṃ viśrameta yathā kapiḥ<br>
    śeṣaṃ ca mayi viśrāntaḥ sukhenātipatiṣyati<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">79</font>
  iti kṛtvā matiṃ sādhvīṃ samudraś channam ambhasi<br>
    hiraṇyanābhaṃ mainākam uvāca girisattamam<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">80</font>
  tvam ihāsurasaṃghānāṃ pātālatalavāsinām<br>
    devarājñā giriśreṣṭha parighaḥ saṃniveśitaḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">81</font>
  tvam eṣāṃ jñātavīryāṇāṃ punar evotpatiṣyatām<br>
    pātālasyāprameyasya dvāram āvṛtya tiṣṭhasi<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">82</font>
  tiryag ūrdhvam adhaś caiva śaktis te śailavardhitum<br>
    tasmāt saṃcodayāmi tvām uttiṣṭha nagasattama<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">83</font>
  sa eṣa kapiśārdūlas tvām uparyeti vīryavān<br>
    hanūmān rāmakāryārthaṃ bhīmakarmā kham āplutaḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">84</font>
  tasya sāhyaṃ mayā kāryam ikṣvākukulavartinaḥ<br>
    mama ikṣvākavaḥ pūjyāḥ paraṃ pūjyatamās tava<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">85</font>
  kuru sācivyam asmākaṃ na naḥ kāryam atikramet<br>
    kartavyam akṛtaṃ kāryaṃ satāṃ manyum udīrayet<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">86</font>
  salilād ūrdhvam uttiṣṭha tiṣṭhatv eṣa kapis tvayi<br>
    asmākam atithiś caiva pūjyaś ca plavatāṃ varaḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">87</font>
  cāmīkaramahānābha devagandharvasevita<br>
    hanūmāṃs tvayi viśrāntas tataḥ śeṣaṃ gamiṣyati<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">88</font>
  kākutsthasyānṛśaṃsyaṃ ca maithilyāś ca vivāsanam<br>
    śramaṃ ca plavagendrasya samīkṣyotthātum arhasi<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">89</font>
  hiraṇyanābho maināko niśamya lavaṇāmbhasaḥ<br>
    utpapāta jalāt tūrṇaṃ mahādrumalatāyutaḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">90</font>
  sa sāgarajalaṃ bhittvā babhūvātyutthitas tadā<br>
    yathā jaladharaṃ bhittvā dīptaraśmir divākaraḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">91</font>
  śātakumbhamayaiḥ śṛṅgaiḥ sakiṃnaramahoragaiḥ<br>
    ādityodayasaṃkāśair ālikhadbhir ivāmbaram<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">92</font>
  tasya jāmbūnadaiḥ śṛṅgaiḥ parvatasya samutthitaiḥ<br>
    ākāśaṃ śastrasaṃkāśam abhavat kāñcanaprabham<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">93</font>
  jātarūpamayaiḥ śṛṅgair bhrājamānaiḥ svayaṃ prabhaiḥ<br>
    ādityaśatasaṃkāśaḥ so 'bhavad girisattamaḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">94</font>
  tam utthitam asaṃgena hanūmān agrataḥ sthitam<br>
    madhye lavaṇatoyasya vighno 'yam iti niścitaḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">95</font>
  sa tam ucchritam atyarthaṃ mahāvego mahākapiḥ<br>
    urasā pātayām āsa jīmūtam iva mārutaḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">96</font>
  sa tadā pātitas tena kapinā parvatottamaḥ<br>
    buddhvā tasya kaper vegaṃ jaharṣa ca nananda ca<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">97</font>
  tam ākāśagataṃ vīram ākāśe samavasthitam<br>
    prīto hṛṣṭamanā vākyam abravīt parvataḥ kapim<br>
    mānuṣaṃ dharayan rūpam ātmanaḥ śikhare sthitaḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">98</font>
  duṣkaraṃ kṛtavān karma tvam idaṃ vānarottama<br>
    nipatya mama śṛṅgeṣu viśramasva yathāsukham<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">99</font>
  rāghāvasya kule jātair udadhiḥ parivardhitaḥ<br>
    sa tvāṃ rāmahite yuktaṃ pratyarcayati sāgaraḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">100</font>
  kṛte ca pratikartavyam eṣa dharmaḥ sanātanaḥ<br>
    so 'yaṃ tat pratikārārthī tvattaḥ saṃmānam arhati<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">101</font>
  tvannimittam anenāhaṃ bahumānāt pracoditaḥ<br>
    yojanānāṃ śataṃ cāpi kapir eṣa samāplutaḥ<br>
    tava sānuṣu viśrāntaḥ śeṣaṃ prakramatām iti<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">102</font>
  tiṣṭha tvaṃ hariśārdūla mayi viśramya gamyatām<br>
    tad idaṃ gandhavat svādu kandamūlaphalaṃ bahu<br>
    tad āsvādya hariśreṣṭha viśrānto 'nugamiṣyasi<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">103</font>
  asmākam api saṃbandhaḥ kapimukhyas tvayāsti vai<br>
    prakhyatas triṣu lokeṣu mahāguṇaparigrahaḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">104</font>
  vegavantaḥ plavanto ye plavagā mārutātmaja<br>
    teṣāṃ mukhyatamaṃ manye tvām ahaṃ kapikuñjara<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">105</font>
  atithiḥ kila pūjārhaḥ prākṛto 'pi vijānatā<br>
    dharmaṃ jijñāsamānena kiṃ punar yādṛśo bhavān<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">106</font>
  tvaṃ hi devavariṣṭhasya mārutasya mahātmanaḥ<br>
    putras tasyaiva vegena sadṛśaḥ kapikuñjara<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">107</font>
  pūjite tvayi dharmajña pūjāṃ prāpnoti mārutaḥ<br>
    tasmāt tvaṃ pūjanīyo me śṛṇu cāpy atra kāraṇam<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">108</font>
  pūrvaṃ kṛtayuge tāta parvatāḥ pakṣiṇo 'bhavan<br>
    te 'pi jagmur diśaḥ sarvā garuḍānilaveginaḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">109</font>
  tatas teṣu prayāteṣu devasaṃghāḥ saharṣibhiḥ<br>
    bhūtāni ca bhayaṃ jagmus teṣāṃ patanaśaṅkayā<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">110</font>
  tataḥ kruddhaḥ sahasrākṣaḥ parvatānāṃ śatakratuḥ<br>
    pakṣāṃś ciccheda vajreṇa tatra tatra sahasraśaḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">111</font>
  sa mām upagataḥ kruddho vajram udyamya devarāṭ<br>
    tato 'haṃ sahasā kṣiptaḥ śvasanena mahātmanā<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">112</font>
  asmiṁl lavaṇatoye ca prakṣiptaḥ plavagottama<br>
    guptapakṣaḥ samagraś ca tava pitrābhirakṣitaḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">113</font>
  tato 'haṃ mānayāmi tvāṃ mānyo hi mama mārutaḥ<br>
    tvayā me hy eṣa saṃbandhaḥ kapimukhya mahāguṇaḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">114</font>
  asminn evaṃgate kārye sāgarasya mamaiva ca<br>
    prītiṃ prītamanā kartuṃ tvam arhasi mahākape<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">115</font>
  śramaṃ mokṣaya pūjāṃ ca gṛhāṇa kapisattama<br>
    prītiṃ ca bahumanyasva prīto 'smi tava darśanāt<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">116</font>
  evam uktaḥ kapiśreṣṭhas taṃ nagottamam abravīt<br>
    prīto 'smi kṛtam ātithyaṃ manyur eṣo 'panīyatām<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">117</font>
  tvarate kāryakālo me ahaś cāpy ativartate<br>
    pratijñā ca mayā dattā na sthātavyam ihāntarā<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">118</font>
  ity uktvā pāṇinā śailam ālabhya haripuṃgavaḥ<br>
    jagāmākāśam āviśya vīryavān prahasann iva<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">119</font>
  sa parvatasamudrābhyāṃ bahumānād avekṣitaḥ<br>
    pūjitaś copapannābhir āśīrbhir anilātmajaḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">120</font>
  athordhvaṃ dūram utpatya hitvā śailamahārṇavau<br>
    pituḥ panthānam āsthāya jagāma vimale 'mbare<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">121</font>
  bhūyaś cordhvagatiṃ prāpya giriṃ tam avalokayan<br>
    vāyusūnur nirālambe jagāma vimale 'mbare<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">122</font>
  tad dvitīyaṃ hanumato dṛṣṭvā karma suduṣkaram<br>
    praśaśaṃsuḥ surāḥ sarve siddhāś ca paramarṣayaḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">123</font>
  devatāś cābhavan hṛṣṭās tatrasthās tasya karmaṇā<br>
    kāñcanasya sunābhasya sahasrākṣaś ca vāsavaḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">124</font>
  uvāca vacanaṃ dhīmān paritoṣāt sagadgadam<br>
    sunābhaṃ parvataśreṣṭhaṃ svayam eva śacīpatiḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">125</font>
  hiraṇyanābhaśailendraparituṣṭo 'smi te bhṛśam<br>
    abhayaṃ te prayacchāmi tiṣṭha saumya yathāsukham<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">126</font>
  sāhyaṃ kṛtaṃ te sumahad vikrāntasya hanūmataḥ<br>
    kramato yojanaśataṃ nirbhayasya bhaye sati<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">127</font>
  rāmasyaiṣa hi dautyena yāti dāśarather hariḥ<br>
    satkriyāṃ kurvatā śakyā toṣito 'smi dṛḍhaṃ tvayā<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">128</font>
  tataḥ praharṣam alabhad vipulaṃ parvatottamaḥ<br>
    devatānāṃ patiṃ dṛṣṭvā parituṣṭaṃ śatakratum<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">129</font>
  sa vai dattavaraḥ śailo babhūvāvasthitas tadā<br>
    hanūmāṃś ca muhūrtena vyaticakrāma sāgaram<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">130</font>
  tato devāḥ sagandharvāḥ siddhāś ca paramarṣayaḥ<br>
    abruvan sūryasaṃkāśāṃ surasāṃ nāgamātaram<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">131</font>
  ayaṃ vātātmajaḥ śrīmān plavate sāgaropari<br>
    hanūmān nāma tasya tvaṃ muhūrtaṃ vighnam ācara<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">132</font>
  rākṣasaṃ rūpam āsthāya sughoraṃ parvatopamam<br>
    daṃṣṭrākarālaṃ piṅgākṣaṃ vaktraṃ kṛtvā nabhaḥspṛśam<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">133</font>
  balam icchāmahe jñātuṃ bhūyaś cāsya parākramam<br>
    tvāṃ vijeṣyaty upāyena viṣadaṃ vā gamiṣyati<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">134</font>
  evam uktā tu sā devī daivatair abhisatkṛtā<br>
    samudramadhye surasā bibhratī rākṣasaṃ vapuḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">135</font>
  vikṛtaṃ ca virūpaṃ ca sarvasya ca bhayāvaham<br>
    plavamānaṃ hanūmantam āvṛtyedam uvāca ha<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">136</font>
  mama bhakṣaḥ pradiṣṭas tvam īśvarair vānararṣabha<br>
    ahaṃ tvāṃ bhakṣayiṣyāmi praviśedaṃ mamānanam<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">137</font>
  evam uktaḥ surasayā prāñjalir vānararṣabhaḥ<br>
    prahṛṣṭavadanaḥ śrīmān idaṃ vacanam abravīt<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">138</font>
  rāmo dāśarathir nāma praviṣṭo daṇḍakāvanam<br>
    lakṣmaṇena saha bhrātrā vaidehyā cāpi bhāryayā<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">139</font>
  asya kāryaviṣaktasya baddhavairasya rākṣasaiḥ<br>
    tasya sītā hṛtā bhāryā rāvaṇena yaśasvinī<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">140</font>
  tasyāḥ sakāśaṃ dūto 'haṃ gamiṣye rāmaśāsanāt<br>
    kartum arhasi rāmasya sāhyaṃ viṣayavāsini<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">141</font>
  atha vā maithilīṃ dṛṣṭvā rāmaṃ cākliṣṭakāriṇam<br>
    āgamiṣyāmi te vaktraṃ satyaṃ pratiśṛṇomi te<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">142</font>
  evam uktā hanumatā surasā kāmarūpiṇī<br>
    abravīn nātivarten māṃ kaś cid eṣa varo mama<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">143</font>
  evam uktaḥ surasayā kruddho vānarapuṃgavaḥ<br>
    abravīt kuru vai vaktraṃ yena māṃ viṣahiṣyase<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">144</font>
  ity uktvā surasāṃ kruddho daśayojanam āyataḥ<br>
    daśayojanavistāro babhūva hanumāṃs tadā<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">145</font>
  taṃ dṛṣṭvā meghasaṃkāśaṃ daśayojanam āyatam<br>
    cakāra surasāpy āsyaṃ viṃśadyojanam āyatam<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">146</font>
  hanumāṃs tu tataḥ kruddhas triṃśadyojanam āyataḥ<br>
    cakāra surasā vaktraṃ catvāriṃśat tathocchritam<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">147</font>
  babhūva hanumān vīraḥ pañcāśadyojanocchritaḥ<br>
    cakāra surasā vaktraṃ ṣaṣṭiyojanam āyatam<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">148</font>
  tathaiva hanumān vīraḥ saptatiṃ yojanocchritaḥ<br>
    cakāra surasā vaktram aśītiṃ yojanāyatam<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">149</font>
  hanūmān acala prakhyo navatiṃ yojanocchritaḥ<br>
    cakāra surasā vaktraṃ śatayojanam āyatam<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">150</font>
  tad dṛṣṭvā vyāditaṃ tv āsyaṃ vāyuputraḥ sa buddhimān<br>
    dīrghajihvaṃ surasayā sughoraṃ narakopamam<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">151</font>
  sa saṃkṣipyātmanaḥ kāyaṃ jīmūta iva mārutiḥ<br>
    tasmin muhūrte hanumān babhūvāṅguṣṭhamātrakaḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">152</font>
  so 'bhipatyāśu tad vaktraṃ niṣpatya ca mahājavaḥ<br>
    antarikṣe sthitaḥ śrīmān idaṃ vacanam abravīt<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">153</font>
  praviṣṭo 'smi hi te vaktraṃ dākṣāyaṇi namo 'stu te<br>
    gamiṣye yatra vaidehī satyaṃ cāstu vacas tava<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">154</font>
  taṃ dṛṣṭvā vadanān muktaṃ candraṃ rāhumukhād iva<br>
    abravīt surasā devī svena rūpeṇa vānaram<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">155</font>
  arthasiddhyai hariśreṣṭha gaccha saumya yathāsukham<br>
    samānaya ca vaidehīṃ rāghaveṇa mahātmanā<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">156</font>
  tat tṛtīyaṃ hanumato dṛṣṭvā karma suduṣkaram<br>
    sādhu sādhv iti bhūtāni praśaśaṃsus tadā harim<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">157</font>
  sa sāgaram anādhṛṣyam abhyetya varuṇālayam<br>
    jagāmākāśam āviśya vegena garuṇopamaḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">158</font>
  sevite vāridhāribhiḥ patagaiś ca niṣevite<br>
    carite kaiśikācāryair airāvataniṣevite<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">159</font>
  siṃhakuñjaraśārdūlapatagoragavāhanaiḥ<br>
    vimānaiḥ saṃpatadbhiś ca vimalaiḥ samalaṃkṛte<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">160</font>
  vajrāśanisamāghātaiḥ pāvakair upaśobhite<br>
    kṛtapuṇyair mahābhāgaiḥ svargajidbhir alaṃkṛte<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">161</font>
  bahatā havyam atyantaṃ sevite citrabhānunā<br>
    grahanakṣatracandrārkatārāgaṇavibhūṣite<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">162</font>
  maharṣigaṇagandharvanāgayakṣasamākule<br>
    vivikte vimale viśve viśvāvasuniṣevite<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">163</font>
  devarājagajākrānte candrasūryapathe śive<br>
    vitāne jīvalokasya vitato brahmanirmite<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">164</font>
  bahuśaḥ sevite vīrair vidyādharagaṇair varaiḥ<br>
    kapinā kṛṣyamāṇāni mahābhrāṇi cakāśire<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">165</font>
  praviśann abhrajālāni niṣpataṃś ca punaḥ punaḥ<br>
    prāvṛṣīndur ivābhāti niṣpatan praviśaṃs tadā<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">166</font>
  plavamānaṃ tu taṃ dṛṣṭvā siṃhikā nāma rākṣasī<br>
    manasā cintayām āsa pravṛddhā kāmarūpiṇī<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">167</font>
  adya dīrghasya kālasya bhaviṣyāmy aham āśitā<br>
    idaṃ hi me mahat sattvaṃ cirasya vaśam āgatam<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">168</font>
  iti saṃcintya manasā chāyām asya samakṣipat<br>
    chāyāyāṃ saṃgṛhītāyāṃ cintayām āsa vānaraḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">169</font>
  samākṣipto 'smi sahasā paṅgūkṛtaparākramaḥ<br>
    pratilomena vātena mahānaur iva sāgare<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">170</font>
  tiryag ūrdhvam adhaś caiva vīkṣamāṇas tataḥ kapiḥ<br>
    dadarśa sa mahāsattvam utthitaṃ lavaṇāmbhasi<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">171</font>
  kapirājñā yad ākhyātaṃ sattvam adbhutadarśanam<br>
    chāyāgrāhi mahāvīryaṃ tad idaṃ nātra saṃśayaḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">172</font>
  sa tāṃ buddhvārthatattvena siṃhikāṃ matimān kapiḥ<br>
    vyavardhata mahākāyaḥ prāvṛṣīva balāhakaḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">173</font>
  tasya sā kāyam udvīkṣya vardhamānaṃ mahākapeḥ<br>
    vaktraṃ prasārayām āsa pātālāmbarasaṃnibham<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">174</font>
  sa dadarśa tatas tasyā vikṛtaṃ sumahan mukham<br>
    kāyamātraṃ ca medhāvī marmāṇi ca mahākapiḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">175</font>
  sa tasyā vivṛte vaktre vajrasaṃhananaḥ kapiḥ<br>
    saṃkṣipya muhur ātmānaṃ niṣpapāta mahābalaḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">176</font>
  āsye tasyā nimajjantaṃ dadṛśuḥ siddhacāraṇāḥ<br>
    grasyamānaṃ yathā candraṃ pūrṇaṃ parvaṇi rāhuṇā<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">177</font>
  tatas tasya nakhais tīkṣṇair marmāṇy utkṛtya vānaraḥ<br>
    utpapātātha vegena manaḥsaṃpātavikramaḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">178</font>
  tāṃ hatāṃ vānareṇāśu patitāṃ vīkṣya siṃhikām<br>
    bhūtāny ākāśacārīṇi tam ūcuḥ plavagarṣabham<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">179</font>
  bhīmam adya kṛtaṃ karma mahat sattvaṃ tvayā hatam<br>
    sādhayārtham abhipretam ariṣṭaṃ plavatāṃ vara<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">180</font>
  yasya tv etāni catvāri vānarendra yathā tava<br>
    dhṛtir dṛṣṭir matir dākṣyaṃ sa karmasu na sīdati<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">181</font>
  sa taiḥ saṃbhāvitaḥ pūjyaḥ pratipannaprayojanaḥ<br>
    jagāmākāśam āviśya pannagāśanavat kapiḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">182</font>
  prāptabhūyiṣṭha pāras tu sarvataḥ pratilokayan<br>
    yojanānāṃ śatasyānte vanarājiṃ dadarśa saḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">183</font>
  dadarśa ca patann eva vividhadrumabhūṣitam<br>
    dvīpaṃ śākhāmṛgaśreṣṭho malayopavanāni ca<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">184</font>
  sāgaraṃ sāgarānūpān sāgarānūpajān drumān<br>
    sāgarasya ca patnīnāṃ mukhāny api vilokayan<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">185</font>
  sa mahāmeghasaṃkāśaṃ samīkṣyātmānam ātmanā<br>
    nirundhantam ivākāśaṃ cakāra matimān matim<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">186</font>
  kāyavṛddhiṃ pravegaṃ ca mama dṛṣṭvaiva rākṣasāḥ<br>
    mayi kautūhalaṃ kuryur iti mene mahākapiḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">187</font>
  tataḥ śarīraṃ saṃkṣipya tan mahīdharasaṃnibham<br>
    punaḥ prakṛtim āpede vītamoha ivātmavān<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">188</font>
  sa cārunānāvidharūpadhārī; paraṃ samāsādya samudratīram<br>
    parair aśakyapratipannarūpaḥ; samīkṣitātmā samavekṣitārthaḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">189</font>
  tataḥ sa lambasya gireḥ samṛddhe; vicitrakūṭe nipapāta kūṭe<br>
    saketakoddālakanālikere; mahādrikūṭapratimo mahātmā<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">190</font>
  sa sāgaraṃ dānavapannagāyutaṃ; balena vikramya mahormimālinam<br>
    nipatya tīre ca mahodadhes tadā; dadarśa laṅkām amarāvatīm iva<br>

    नयने विप्रकाशेते पर्वतस्थाव इवानलौ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">55</font>

 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">69</font>
  दशयॊजनविस्तीर्णा तरिंशद्यॊजनम आयता<br>
    छाया वानरसिंहस्य जले चारुतराभवत<br>

 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">146</font>
  हनुमांस तु ततः करुद्धस तरिंशद्यॊजनम आयतः<br>
    चकार सुरसा वक्त्रं चत्वारिंशत तथॊच्छ्रितम<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">147</font>
  बभूव हनुमान वीरः पञ्चाशद्यॊजनॊच्छ्रितः<br>
    चकार सुरसा वक्त्रं षष्टियॊजनम आयतम<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">148</font>
  तथैव हनुमान वीरः सप्ततिं यॊजनॊच्छ्रितः<br>
    चकार सुरसा वक्त्रम अशीतिं यॊजनायतम<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">149</font>
  हनूमान अचल परख्यॊ नवतिं यॊजनॊच्छ्रितः<br>
    चकार सुरसा वक्त्रं शतयॊजनम आयतम<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">150</font>
  तद दृष्ट्वा वयादितं तव आस्यं वायुपुत्रः स बुद्धिमान<br>
    दीर्घजिह्वं सुरसया सुघॊरं नरकॊपमम<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">151</font>
  स संक्षिप्यात्मनः कायं जीमूत इव मारुतिः<br>
    तस्मिन मुहूर्ते हनुमान बभूवाङ्गुष्ठमात्रकः<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">152</font>
  सॊ ऽभिपत्याशु तद वक्त्रं निष्पत्य च महाजवः<br>
    अन्तरिक्षे सथितः शरीमान इदं वचनम अब्रवीत<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">153</font>
  परविष्टॊ ऽसमि हि ते वक्त्रं दाक्षायणि नमॊ ऽसतु ते<br>
    गमिष्ये यत्र वैदेही सत्यं चास्तु वचस तव<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">154</font>
  तं दृष्ट्वा वदनान मुक्तं चन्द्रं राहुमुखाद इव<br>
    अब्रवीत सुरसा देवी सवेन रूपेण वानरम<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">155</font>
  अर्थसिद्ध्यै हरिश्रेष्ठ गच्छ सौम्य यथासुखम<br>
    समानय च वैदेहीं राघवेण महात्मना<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">156</font>
  तत तृतीयं हनुमतॊ दृष्ट्वा कर्म सुदुष्करम<br>
    साधु साध्व इति भूतानि परशशंसुस तदा हरिम<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">157</font>
  स सागरम अनाधृष्यम अभ्येत्य वरुणालयम<br>
    जगामाकाशम आविश्य वेगेन गरुणॊपमः<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">158</font>
  सेविते वारिधारिभिः पतगैश च निषेविते<br>
    चरिते कैशिकाचार्यैर ऐरावतनिषेविते<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">159</font>
  सिंहकुञ्जरशार्दूलपतगॊरगवाहनैः<br>
    विमानैः संपतद्भिश च विमलैः समलंकृते<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">160</font>
  वज्राशनिसमाघातैः पावकैर उपशॊभिते<br>
    कृतपुण्यैर महाभागैः सवर्गजिद्भिर अलंकृते<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">161</font>
  बहता हव्यम अत्यन्तं सेविते चित्रभानुना<br>
    गरहनक्षत्रचन्द्रार्कतारागणविभूषिते<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">162</font>
  महर्षिगणगन्धर्वनागयक्षसमाकुले<br>
    विविक्ते विमले विश्वे विश्वावसुनिषेविते<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">163</font>
  देवराजगजाक्रान्ते चन्द्रसूर्यपथे शिवे<br>
    विताने जीवलॊकस्य विततॊ बरह्मनिर्मिते<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">164</font>
  बहुशः सेविते वीरैर विद्याधरगणैर वरैः<br>
    कपिना कृष्यमाणानि महाभ्राणि चकाशिरे<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">165</font>
  परविशन्न अभ्रजालानि निष्पतंश च पुनः पुनः<br>
    परावृषीन्दुर इवाभाति निष्पतन परविशंस तदा<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">166</font>
  पलवमानं तु तं दृष्ट्वा सिंहिका नाम राक्षसी<br>
    मनसा चिन्तयाम आस परवृद्धा कामरूपिणी<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">167</font>
  अद्य दीर्घस्य कालस्य भविष्याम्य अहम आशिता<br>
    इदं हि मे महत सत्त्वं चिरस्य वशम आगतम<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">168</font>
  इति संचिन्त्य मनसा छायाम अस्य समक्षिपत<br>
    छायायां संगृहीतायां चिन्तयाम आस वानरः<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">169</font>
  समाक्षिप्तॊ ऽसमि सहसा पङ्गूकृतपराक्रमः<br>
    परतिलॊमेन वातेन महानौर इव सागरे<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">170</font>
  तिर्यग ऊर्ध्वम अधश चैव वीक्षमाणस ततः कपिः<br>
    ददर्श स महासत्त्वम उत्थितं लवणाम्भसि<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">171</font>
  कपिराज्ञा यद आख्यातं सत्त्वम अद्भुतदर्शनम<br>
    छायाग्राहि महावीर्यं तद इदं नात्र संशयः<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">172</font>
  स तां बुद्ध्वार्थतत्त्वेन सिंहिकां मतिमान कपिः<br>
    वयवर्धत महाकायः परावृषीव बलाहकः<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">173</font>
  तस्य सा कायम उद्वीक्ष्य वर्धमानं महाकपेः<br>
    वक्त्रं परसारयाम आस पातालाम्बरसंनिभम<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">174</font>
  स ददर्श ततस तस्या विकृतं सुमहन मुखम<br>
    कायमात्रं च मेधावी मर्माणि च महाकपिः<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">175</font>
  स तस्या विवृते वक्त्रे वज्रसंहननः कपिः<br>
    संक्षिप्य मुहुर आत्मानं निष्पपात महाबलः<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">176</font>
  आस्ये तस्या निमज्जन्तं ददृशुः सिद्धचारणाः<br>
    गरस्यमानं यथा चन्द्रं पूर्णं पर्वणि राहुणा<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">177</font>
  ततस तस्य नखैस तीक्ष्णैर मर्माण्य उत्कृत्य वानरः<br>
    उत्पपाताथ वेगेन मनःसंपातविक्रमः<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">178</font>
  तां हतां वानरेणाशु पतितां वीक्ष्य सिंहिकाम<br>
    भूतान्य आकाशचारीणि तम ऊचुः पलवगर्षभम<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">179</font>
  भीमम अद्य कृतं कर्म महत सत्त्वं तवया हतम<br>
    साधयार्थम अभिप्रेतम अरिष्टं पलवतां वर<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">180</font>
  यस्य तव एतानि चत्वारि वानरेन्द्र यथा तव<br>
    धृतिर दृष्टिर मतिर दाक्ष्यं स कर्मसु न सीदति<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">181</font>
  स तैः संभावितः पूज्यः परतिपन्नप्रयॊजनः<br>
    जगामाकाशम आविश्य पन्नगाशनवत कपिः<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">182</font>
  पराप्तभूयिष्ठ पारस तु सर्वतः परतिलॊकयन<br>
    यॊजनानां शतस्यान्ते वनराजिं ददर्श सः<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">183</font>
  ददर्श च पतन्न एव विविधद्रुमभूषितम<br>
    दवीपं शाखामृगश्रेष्ठॊ मलयॊपवनानि च<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">184</font>
  सागरं सागरानूपान सागरानूपजान दरुमान<br>
    सागरस्य च पत्नीनां मुखान्य अपि विलॊकयन<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">185</font>
  स महामेघसंकाशं समीक्ष्यात्मानम आत्मना<br>
    निरुन्धन्तम इवाकाशं चकार मतिमान मतिम<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">186</font>
  कायवृद्धिं परवेगं च मम दृष्ट्वैव राक्षसाः<br>
    मयि कौतूहलं कुर्युर इति मेने महाकपिः<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">187</font>
  ततः शरीरं संक्षिप्य तन महीधरसंनिभम<br>
    पुनः परकृतिम आपेदे वीतमॊह इवात्मवान<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">188</font>
  स चारुनानाविधरूपधारी; परं समासाद्य समुद्रतीरम<br>
    परैर अशक्यप्रतिपन्नरूपः; समीक्षितात्मा समवेक्षितार्थः<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">189</font>
  ततः स लम्बस्य गिरेः समृद्धे; विचित्रकूटे निपपात कूटे<br>
    सकेतकॊद्दालकनालिकेरे; महाद्रिकूटप्रतिमॊ महात्मा<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">190</font>
  स सागरं दानवपन्नगायुतं; बलेन विक्रम्य महॊर्मिमालिनम<br>
    निपत्य तीरे च महॊदधेस तदा; ददर्श लङ्काम अमरावतीम इव<br>
