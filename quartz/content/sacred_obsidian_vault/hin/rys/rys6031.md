 <h3 align="CENTER">Book 6<br>Chapter 31
 </h3><table width="100%">
 <td width="50%">
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">1</font>
  atha tasmin nimittāni dṛṣṭvā lakṣmaṇapūrvajaḥ<br>
   lakṣmaṇaṃ lakṣmisaṃpannam idaṃ vacanam abravīt<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">2</font>
  parigṛhyodakaṃ śītaṃ vanāni phalavanti ca<br>
   balaughaṃ saṃvibhajyemaṃ vyūhya tiṣṭhema lakṣmaṇa<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">3</font>
  lokakṣayakaraṃ bhīmaṃ bhayaṃ paśyāmy upasthitam<br>
   nibarhaṇaṃ pravīrāṇām ṛkṣavānararakṣasām<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">4</font>
  vātāś ca paruṣaṃ vānti kampate ca vasuṃdharā<br>
   parvatāgrāṇi vepante patanti dharaṇīdharāḥ<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">5</font>
  meghāḥ kravyādasaṃkāśāḥ paruṣāḥ paruṣasvanāḥ<br>
   krūrāḥ krūraṃ pravarṣanti miśraṃ śoṇitabindubhiḥ<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">6</font>
  raktacandanasaṃkāśā saṃdhyāparamadāruṇā<br>
   jvalac ca nipataty etad ādityād agnimaṇḍalam<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">7</font>
  ādityam abhivāśyante janayanto mahad bhayam<br>
   dīnā dīnasvarā ghorā apraśastā mṛgadvijāḥ<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">8</font>
  rajanyām aprakāśaś ca saṃtāpayati candramāḥ<br>
   kṛṣṇaraktāṃśuparyanto yathā lokasya saṃkṣaye<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">9</font>
  hrasvo rūkṣo 'praśastaś ca pariveṣaḥ sulohitaḥ<br>
   ādityamaṇḍale nīlaṃ lakṣma lakṣmaṇa dṛśyate<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">10</font>
  dṛśyante na yathāvac ca nakṣatrāṇy abhivartate<br>
    yugāntam iva lokasya paśya lakṣmaṇa śaṃsati<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">11</font>
  kākāḥ śyenās tathā gṛdhrā nīcaiḥ paripatanti ca<br>
    śivāś cāpy aśivā vācaḥ pravadanti mahāsvanāḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">12</font>
  kṣipram adya durādharṣāṃ purīṃ rāvaṇapālitām<br>
    abhiyāma javenaiva sarvato haribhir vṛtāḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">13</font>
  ity evaṃ tu vadan vīro lakṣmaṇaṃ lakṣmaṇāgrajaḥ<br>
    tasmād avātarac chīghraṃ parvatāgrān mahābalaḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">14</font>
  avatīrya tu dharmātmā tasmāc chailāt sa rāghavaḥ<br>
    paraiḥ paramadurdharṣaṃ dadarśa balam ātmanaḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">15</font>
  saṃnahya tu sasugrīvaḥ kapirājabalaṃ mahat<br>
    kālajño rāghavaḥ kāle saṃyugāyābhyacodayat<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">16</font>
  tataḥ kāle mahābāhur balena mahatā vṛtaḥ<br>
    prasthitaḥ purato dhanvī laṅkām abhimukhaḥ purīm<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">17</font>
  taṃ vibhīṣaṇa sugrīvau hanūmāñ jāmbavān nalaḥ<br>
    ṛkṣarājas tathā nīlo lakṣmaṇaś cānyayus tadā<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">18</font>
  tataḥ paścāt sumahatī pṛtanarkṣavanaukasām<br>
    pracchādya mahatīṃ bhūmim anuyāti sma rāghavam<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">19</font>
  śailaśṛṅgāṇi śataśaḥ pravṛddhāṃś ca mahīruhām<br>
    jagṛhuḥ kuñjaraprakhyā vānarāḥ paravāraṇāḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">20</font>
  tau tv adīrgheṇa kālena bhrātarau rāmalakṣmaṇau<br>
    rāvaṇasya purīṃ laṅkām āsedatur ariṃdamau<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">21</font>
  patākāmālinīṃ ramyām udyānavanaśobhitām<br>
    citravaprāṃ suduṣprāpām uccaprākāratoraṇām<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">22</font>
  tāṃ surair api durdharṣāṃ rāmavākyapracoditāḥ<br>
    yathānideśaṃ saṃpīḍya nyaviśanta vanaukasaḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">23</font>
  laṅkāyās tūttaradvāraṃ śailaśṛṅgam ivonnatam<br>
    rāmaḥ sahānujo dhanvī jugopa ca rurodha ca<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">24</font>
  laṅkām upaniviṣṭaś ca rāmo daśarathātmajaḥ<br>
    lakṣmaṇānucaro vīraḥ purīṃ rāvaṇapālitām<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">25</font>
  uttaradvāram āsādya yatra tiṣṭhati rāvaṇaḥ<br>
    nānyo rāmād dhi tad dvāraṃ samarthaḥ parirakṣitum<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">26</font>
  rāvaṇādhiṣṭhitaṃ bhīmaṃ varuṇeneva sāgaram<br>
    sāyudhau rākṣasair bhīmair abhiguptaṃ samantataḥ<br>
    laghūnāṃ trāsajananaṃ pātālam iva dānavaiḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">27</font>
  vinyastāni ca yodhānāṃ bahūni vividhāni ca<br>
    dadarśāyudhajālāni tathaiva kavacāni ca<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">28</font>
  pūrvaṃ tu dvāram āsādya nīlo haricamūpatiḥ<br>
    atiṣṭhat saha maindena dvividena ca vīryavān<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">29</font>
  aṅgado dakṣiṇadvāraṃ jagrāha sumahābalaḥ<br>
    ṛṣabheṇa gavākṣeṇa gajena gavayena ca<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">30</font>
  hanūmān paścimadvāraṃ rarakṣa balavān kapiḥ<br>
    pramāthi praghasābhyāṃ ca vīrair anyaiś ca saṃgataḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">31</font>
  madhyame ca svayaṃ gulme sugrīvaḥ samatiṣṭhata<br>
    saha sarvair hariśreṣṭhaiḥ suparṇaśvasanopamaiḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">32</font>
  vānarāṇāṃ tu ṣaṭtriṃśat koṭyaḥ prakhyātayūthapāḥ<br>
    nipīḍyopaniviṣṭāś ca sugrīvo yatra vānaraḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">33</font>
  śāsanena tu rāmasya lakṣmaṇaḥ savibhīṣaṇaḥ<br>
    dvāre dvāre harīṇāṃ tu koṭiṃ koṭiṃ nyaveśayat<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">34</font>
  paścimena tu rāmasya sugrīvaḥ saha jāmbavān<br>
    adūrān madhyame gulme tasthau bahubalānugaḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">35</font>
  te tu vānaraśārdūlāḥ śārdūlā iva daṃṣṭriṇaḥ<br>
    gṛhītvā drumaśailāgrān hṛṣṭā yuddhāya tasthire<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">36</font>
  sarve vikṛtalāṅgūlāḥ sarve daṃṣṭrānakhāyudhāḥ<br>
    sarve vikṛtacitrāṅgāḥ sarve ca vikṛtānanāḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">37</font>
  daśanāgabalāḥ ke cit ke cid daśaguṇottarāḥ<br>
    ke cin nāgasahasrasya babhūvus tulyavikramāḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">38</font>
  santi caughā balāḥ ke cit ke cic chataguṇottarāḥ<br>
    aprameyabalāś cānye tatrāsan hariyūthapāḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">39</font>
  adbhutaś ca vicitraś ca teṣām āsīt samāgamaḥ<br>
    tatra vānarasainyānāṃ śalabhānām ivodgamaḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">40</font>
  paripūrṇam ivākāśaṃ saṃchanneva ca medinī<br>
    laṅkām upaniviṣṭaiś ca saṃpatadbhiś ca vānaraiḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">41</font>
  śataṃ śatasahasrāṇāṃ pṛthag ṛkṣavanaukasām<br>
    laṅkā dvārāṇy upājagmur anye yoddhuṃ samantataḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">42</font>
  āvṛtaḥ sa giriḥ sarvais taiḥ samantāt plavaṃgamaiḥ<br>
    ayutānāṃ sahasraṃ ca purīṃ tām abhyavartata<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">43</font>
  vānarair balavadbhiś ca babhūva drumapāṇibhiḥ<br>
    sarvataḥ saṃvṛtā laṅkā duṣpraveśāpi vāyunā<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">44</font>
  rākṣasā vismayaṃ jagmuḥ sahasābhinipīḍitāḥ<br>
    vānarair meghasaṃkāśaiḥ śakratulyaparākramaiḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">45</font>
  mahāñ śabdo 'bhavat tatra balaughasyābhivartataḥ<br>
    sāgarasyeva bhinnasya yathā syāt salilasvanaḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">46</font>
  tena śabdena mahatā saprākārā satoraṇā<br>
    laṅkā pracalitā sarvā saśailavanakānanā<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">47</font>
  rāmalakṣmaṇaguptā sā sugrīveṇa ca vāhinī<br>
    babhūva durdharṣatarā sarvair api surāsuraiḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">48</font>
  rāghavaḥ saṃniveśyaivaṃ sainyaṃ svaṃ rakṣasāṃ vadhe<br>
    saṃmantrya mantribhiḥ sārdhaṃ niścitya ca punaḥ punaḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">49</font>
  ānantaryam abhiprepsuḥ kramayogārthatattvavit<br>
    vibhīṣaṇasyānumate rājadharmam anusmaran<br>
    aṅgadaṃ vālitanayaṃ samāhūyedam abravīt<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">50</font>
  gatvā saumya daśagrīvaṃ brūhi madvacanāt kape<br>
    laṅghayitvā purīṃ laṅkāṃ bhayaṃ tyaktvā gatavyathaḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">51</font>
  bhraṣṭaśrīkagataiśvaryamumūrṣo naṣṭacetanaḥ<br>
    ṛṣīṇāṃ devatānāṃ ca gandharvāpsarasāṃ tathā<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">52</font>
  nāgānām atha yakṣāṇāṃ rājñāṃ ca rajanīcara<br>
    yac ca pāpaṃ kṛtaṃ mohād avaliptena rākṣasa<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">53</font>
  nūnam adya gato darpaḥ svayambhū varadānajaḥ<br>
    yasya daṇḍadharas te 'haṃ dārāharaṇakarśitaḥ<br>
    daṇḍaṃ dhārayamāṇas tu laṅkādvare vyavasthitaḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">54</font>
  padavīṃ devatānāṃ ca maharṣīṇāṃ ca rākṣasa<br>
    rājarṣīṇāṃ ca sarveṇāṃ gamiṣyasi mayā hataḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">55</font>
  balena yena vai sītāṃ māyayā rākṣasādhama<br>
    mām atikrāmayitvā tvaṃ hṛtavāṃs tad vidarśaya<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">56</font>
  arākṣasam imaṃ lokaṃ kartāsmi niśitaiḥ śaraiḥ<br>
    na cec charaṇam abhyeṣi mām upādāya maithilīm<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">57</font>
  dharmātmā rakṣasāṃ śreṣṭhaḥ saṃprāpto 'yaṃ vibhīṣaṇaḥ<br>
    laṅkaiśvaryaṃ dhruvaṃ śrīmān ayaṃ prāpnoty akaṇṭakam<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">58</font>
  na hi rājyam adharmeṇa bhoktuṃ kṣaṇam api tvayā<br>
    śakyaṃ mūrkhasahāyena pāpenāvijitātmanā<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">59</font>
  yudhyasva vā dhṛtiṃ kṛtvā śauryam ālambya rākṣasa<br>
    maccharais tvaṃ raṇe śāntas tataḥ pūto bhaviṣyasi<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">60</font>
  yady āviśasi lokāṃs trīn pakṣibhūto manojavaḥ<br>
    mama cakṣuṣpathaṃ prāpya na jīvan pratiyāsyasi<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">61</font>
  bravīmi tvāṃ hitaṃ vākyaṃ kriyatām aurdhvadekikam<br>
    sudṛṣṭā kriyatāṃ laṅkā jīvitaṃ te mayi sthitam<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">62</font>
  ity uktaḥ sa tu tāreyo rāmeṇākliṣṭakarmaṇā<br>
    jagāmākāśam āviśya mūrtimān iva havyavāṭ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">63</font>
  so 'tipatya muhūrtena śrīmān rāvaṇamandiram<br>
    dadarśāsīnam avyagraṃ rāvaṇaṃ sacivaiḥ saha<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">64</font>
  tatas tasyāvidūreṇa nipatya haripuṃgavaḥ<br>
    dīptāgnisadṛśas tasthāv aṅgadaḥ kanakāṅgadaḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">65</font>
  tad rāmavacanaṃ sarvam anyūnādhikam uttamam<br>
    sāmātyaṃ śrāvayām āsa nivedyātmānam ātmanā<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">66</font>
  dūto 'haṃ kosalendrasya rāmasyākliṣṭakarmaṇaḥ<br>
    vāliputro 'ṅgado nāma yadi te śrotram āgataḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">67</font>
  āha tvāṃ rāghavo rāmaḥ kausalyānandavardhanaḥ<br>
    niṣpatya pratiyudhyasva nṛśaṃsaṃ puruṣādhama<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">68</font>
  hantāsmi tvāṃ sahāmātyaṃ saputrajñātibāndhavam<br>
    nirudvignās trayo lokā bhaviṣyanti hate tvayi<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">69</font>
  devadānavayakṣāṇāṃ gandharvoragarakṣasām<br>
    śatrum adyoddhariṣyāmi tvām ṛṣīṇāṃ ca kaṇṭakam<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">70</font>
  vibhīṣaṇasya caiśvaryaṃ bhaviṣyati hate tvayi<br>
    na cet satkṛtya vaidehīṃ praṇipatya pradāsyasi<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">71</font>
  ity evaṃ paruṣaṃ vākyaṃ bruvāṇe haripuṃgave<br>
    amarṣavaśam āpanno niśācaragaṇeśvaraḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">72</font>
  tataḥ sa roṣatāmrākṣaḥ śaśāsa sacivāṃs tadā<br>
    gṛhyatām eṣa durmedhā vadhyatām iti cāsakṛt<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">73</font>
  rāvaṇasya vacaḥ śrutvā dīptāgnisamatejasaḥ<br>
    jagṛhus taṃ tato ghorāś catvāro rajanīcarāḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">74</font>
  grāhayām āsa tāreyaḥ svayam ātmānam ātmanā<br>
    balaṃ darśayituṃ vīro yātudhānagaṇe tadā<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">75</font>
  sa tān bāhudvaye saktān ādāya patagān iva<br>
    prāsādaṃ śailasaṃkāśam utpāpātāṅgadas tadā<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">76</font>
  te 'ntarikṣād vinirdhūtās tasya vegena rākṣasāḥ<br>
    bhumau nipatitāḥ sarve rākṣasendrasya paśyataḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">77</font>
  tataḥ prāsādaśikharaṃ śailaśṛṅgam ivonnatam<br>
    tat paphāla tadākrāntaṃ daśagrīvasya paśyataḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">78</font>
  bhaṅktvā prāsādaśikharaṃ nāma viśrāvya cātmanaḥ<br>
    vinadya sumahānādam utpapāta vihāyasā<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">79</font>
  rāvaṇas tu paraṃ cakre krodhaṃ prāsādadharṣaṇāt<br>
    vināśaṃ cātmanaḥ paśyan niḥśvāsaparamo 'bhavat<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">80</font>
  rāmas tu bahubhir hṛṣṭair ninadadbhiḥ plavaṃgamaiḥ<br>
    vṛto ripuvadhākāṅkṣī yuddhāyaivābhyavartata<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">81</font>
  suṣeṇas tu mahāvīryo girikūṭopamo hariḥ<br>
    bahubhiḥ saṃvṛtas tatra vānaraiḥ kāmarūpibhiḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">82</font>
  caturdvārāṇi sarvāṇi sugrīvavacanāt kapiḥ<br>
    paryākramata durdharṣo nakṣatrāṇīva candramāḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">83</font>
  teṣām akṣauhiṇiśataṃ samavekṣya vanaukasām<br>
    laṅkām upaniviṣṭānāṃ sāgaraṃ cātivartatām<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">84</font>
  rākṣasā vismayaṃ jagmus trāsaṃ jagmus tathāpare<br>
    apare samaroddharṣād dharṣam evopapedire<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">85</font>
  kṛtsnaṃ hi kapibhir vyāptaṃ prākāraparikhāntaram<br>
    dadṛśū rākṣasā dīnāḥ prākāraṃ vānarīkṛtam<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">86</font>
  tasmin mahābhīṣaṇake pravṛtte; kolāhale rākṣasarājadhānyām<br>
    pragṛhya rakṣāṃsi mahāyudhāni; yugāntavātā iva saṃviceruḥ<br>

  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">1</font>
  अथ तस्मिन निमित्तानि दृष्ट्वा लक्ष्मणपूर्वजः<br>
   लक्ष्मणं लक्ष्मिसंपन्नम इदं वचनम अब्रवीत<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">2</font>
  परिगृह्यॊदकं शीतं वनानि फलवन्ति च<br>
   बलौघं संविभज्येमं वयूह्य तिष्ठेम लक्ष्मण<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">3</font>
  लॊकक्षयकरं भीमं भयं पश्याम्य उपस्थितम<br>
   निबर्हणं परवीराणाम ऋक्षवानररक्षसाम<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">4</font>
  वाताश च परुषं वान्ति कम्पते च वसुंधरा<br>
   पर्वताग्राणि वेपन्ते पतन्ति धरणीधराः<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">5</font>
  मेघाः करव्यादसंकाशाः परुषाः परुषस्वनाः<br>
   करूराः करूरं परवर्षन्ति मिश्रं शॊणितबिन्दुभिः<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">6</font>

  स तान बाहुद्वये सक्तान आदाय पतगान इव<br>
    परासादं शैलसंकाशम उत्पापाताङ्गदस तदा<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">76</font>
  ते ऽनतरिक्षाद विनिर्धूतास तस्य वेगेन राक्षसाः<br>
    भुमौ निपतिताः सर्वे राक्षसेन्द्रस्य पश्यतः<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">77</font>
  ततः परासादशिखरं शैलशृङ्गम इवॊन्नतम<br>
    तत पफाल तदाक्रान्तं दशग्रीवस्य पश्यतः<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">78</font>
  भङ्क्त्वा परासादशिखरं नाम विश्राव्य चात्मनः<br>
    विनद्य सुमहानादम उत्पपात विहायसा<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">79</font>
  रावणस तु परं चक्रे करॊधं परासादधर्षणात<br>
    विनाशं चात्मनः पश्यन निःश्वासपरमॊ ऽभवत<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">80</font>
  रामस तु बहुभिर हृष्टैर निनदद्भिः पलवंगमैः<br>
    वृतॊ रिपुवधाकाङ्क्षी युद्धायैवाभ्यवर्तत<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">81</font>
  सुषेणस तु महावीर्यॊ गिरिकूटॊपमॊ हरिः<br>
    बहुभिः संवृतस तत्र वानरैः कामरूपिभिः<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">82</font>
  चतुर्द्वाराणि सर्वाणि सुग्रीववचनात कपिः<br>
    पर्याक्रमत दुर्धर्षॊ नक्षत्राणीव चन्द्रमाः<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">83</font>
  तेषाम अक्षौहिणिशतं समवेक्ष्य वनौकसाम<br>
    लङ्काम उपनिविष्टानां सागरं चातिवर्तताम<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">84</font>
  राक्षसा विस्मयं जग्मुस तरासं जग्मुस तथापरे<br>
    अपरे समरॊद्धर्षाद धर्षम एवॊपपेदिरे<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">85</font>
  कृत्स्नं हि कपिभिर वयाप्तं पराकारपरिखान्तरम<br>
    ददृशू राक्षसा दीनाः पराकारं वानरीकृतम<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">86</font>
  तस्मिन महाभीषणके परवृत्ते; कॊलाहले राक्षसराजधान्याम<br>
    परगृह्य रक्षांसि महायुधानि; युगान्तवाता इव संविचेरुः<br>
 </td>
