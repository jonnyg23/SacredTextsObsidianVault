 <h1 align="CENTER">The Mahabharata in Sanskrit</h1>
 <h3 align="CENTER">Book 12<br>Chapter 136
 </h3><table width="100%">
 <td width="50%" valign="TOP">
   <font color="GREEN" size="-1">1</font>
   [य]<br>
       सर्वत्र बुद्धिः कथिता शरेष्ठा ते भरतर्षभ<br>
       अनागता तथॊत्पन्ना दीर्घसूत्रा विनाशिनी<br>
   <font color="GREEN" size="-1">2</font>
  तद इच्छामि परां बुद्धिं शरॊतुं भरतसत्तम<br>
       यथा राजन न मुह्येत शत्रुभिः परिवारितः<br>
   <font color="GREEN" size="-1">3</font>
  धर्मार्थकुशलप्राज्ञ सर्वशास्त्रविशारद<br>
       पृच्छामि तवा कुरु कश्रेष्ठ तन मे वयाख्यातुम अर्हसि<br>
   <font color="GREEN" size="-1">4</font>
  शत्रुभिर बहुभिर गरस्तॊ यथा वर्तेत पार्थिवः<br>
       एतद इच्छाम्य अहं शरॊतुं सर्वम एव यथाविधि<br>
   <font color="GREEN" size="-1">5</font>
  विषमस्थं हि राजानं शत्रवः परिपन्थिनः<br>
       बहवॊ ऽपय एकम उद्धर्तुं यतन्ते पूर्वतापिताः<br>
   <font color="GREEN" size="-1">6</font>
  सर्वतः परार्थ्यमानेन दुर्बलेन महाबलैः<br>
       एकेनैवासहायेन शक्यं सथातुं कथं भवेत<br>
   <font color="GREEN" size="-1">7</font>
  कथं मित्रम अरिं चैव विन्देत भरतर्षभ<br>
       चेष्टितव्यं कथं चाथ शत्रॊर मित्रस्य चान्तरे<br>
   <font color="GREEN" size="-1">8</font>
  परज्ञात लक्षणे राजन्न अमित्रे मित्रतां गते<br>
       कथं नु पुरुषः कुर्यात किं वा कृत्वा सुखी भवेत<br>
   <font color="GREEN" size="-1">9</font>
  विग्रहं केन वा कुर्यात संधिं वा केन यॊजयेत<br>
       कथं वा शत्रुमध्यस्थॊ वर्तेताबलवान इति<br>
   <font color="GREEN" size="-1">10</font>
  एतद वै सर्वकृत्यानां परं कृत्यं परंतप<br>
      नैतस्य कश चिद वक्तास्ति शरॊता चापि सुदुर्लभः<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">11</font>
  ऋते शांतनवाद भीष्मात सत्यसंधाज जितेन्द्रियात<br>
      तद अन्विष्य महाबाहॊ सर्वम एतद वदस्व मे<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">12</font>
   [भ]<br>
      तवद युक्तॊ ऽयम अनुप्रश्नॊ युधिष्ठिर गुणॊदयः<br>
      शृणु मे पुत्र कार्त्स्न्येन गुह्यम आपत्सु भारत<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">13</font>
  अमित्रॊ मित्रतां याति मित्रं चापि परदुष्यति<br>
      सामर्थ्य यॊगात कार्याणां तद्गत्या हि सदागतिः<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">14</font>
  तस्माद विश्वसितव्यं च विग्रहं च समाचरेत<br>
      देशं कालं च विज्ञाय कार्याकार्यविनिश्चये<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">15</font>
  संधातव्यं बुधैर नित्यं वयवस्यं च हितार्थिभिः<br>
      अमित्रैर अपि संधेयं पराणा रक्ष्याश च भारत<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">16</font>
  यॊ हय अमित्रैर नरॊ नित्यं न संदध्याद अपण्डितः<br>
      न सॊ ऽरथम आप्नुयात किं चित फलान्य अपि च भारत<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">17</font>
  यस तव अमित्रेण संधत्ते मित्रेण च विरुध्यते<br>
      अर्थयुक्तिं समालॊक्य सुमहद विन्दते फलम<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">18</font>
  अत्राप्य उदाहरन्तीमम इतिहासं पुरातनम<br>
      मार्जारस्य च संवादं नयग्रॊधे मूषकस्य च<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">19</font>
  वने महति कस्मिंश चिन नयग्रॊधः सुमहान अभूत<br>
      लता जालपरिच्छन्नॊ नानाद्विज गणायुतः<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">20</font>
  सकन्धवान मेघसंकाशः शीतच छायॊ मनॊरमः<br>
      वैरन्त्यम अभितॊ जातस तरुर वयालमृगाकुलः<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">21</font>
  तस्य मूलं समाश्रित्य कृत्वा शतमुखं बिलम<br>
      वसति सम महाप्राज्ञः पलितॊ नाम मूषकः<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">22</font>
  शाखाश च तस्य संश्रित्य वसति सम सुखं पुरः<br>
      लॊमशॊ नाम मार्जारः पक्षिसत्त्वावसादकः<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">23</font>
  तत्र चागत्य चाण्डालॊ वैरन्त्य कृतकेतनः<br>
      अयॊजयत तम उन्माथं नित्यम अस्तं गते रवौ<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">24</font>
  तत्र सनायुमयान पाशान यथावत संनिधाय सः<br>
      गृहं गत्वा सुखं शेते परभाताम एति शर्वरीम<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">25</font>
  तत्र सम नित्यं बध्यन्ते नक्तं बहुविधा मृगाः<br>
      कदा चित तत्र मार्जारस तव अप्रमत्तॊ ऽपय अबध्यत<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">26</font>
  तस्मिन बद्धे महाप्राज्ञः शत्रौ नित्याततायिनि<br>
      तं कालं पलितॊ जञात्वा विचचार सुनिर्भयः<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">27</font>
  तेनानुचरता तस्मिन वने विश्वस्तचारिणा<br>
      भक्षं विचरमाणेन नचिराद दृष्टम आमिषम<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">28</font>
  स तम उन्माथम आरुह्य तद आमिषम अभक्षयत<br>
      तस्यॊपरि सपत्नस्य बद्धस्य मनसा हसन<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">29</font>
  आमिषे तु परसक्तः स कदा चिद अवलॊकयन<br>
      अपश्यद अपरं घॊरम आत्मनः शत्रुम आगतम<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">30</font>
  शरप्रसून संकाशं मही विवर शायिनम<br>
      नकुलं हरिकं नाम चपलं ताम्रलॊचनम<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">31</font>
  तेन मूषक गन्धेन तवरमाणम उपागतम<br>
      भक्षार्थं लेलिहद वक्त्रं भूमाव ऊर्ध्वमुखं सथितम<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">32</font>
  शखा गतम अरिं चान्यद अपश्यत कॊटरालयम<br>
      उलूकं चन्द्रकं नाम तीक्ष्णतुण्डं कषपाचरम<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">33</font>
  गतस्य विषयं तस्य नकुलॊलूकयॊस तदा<br>
      अथास्यासीद इयं चिन्ता तत पराप्य सुमहद भयम<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">34</font>
  आपद्य अस्यां सुकष्टायां मरणे समुपस्थिते<br>
      समन्ताद भय उत्पन्ने कथं कार्यं हितैषिणा<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">35</font>
  स तथा सर्वतॊ रुद्धः सर्वत्र समदर्शनः<br>
      अभवद भयसंतप्तश चक्रे चेमां परां गतिम<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">36</font>
  आपद विनाशभूयिष्ठा शतैकीयं च जीवितम<br>
      समन्त संशया चेयम अस्मान आपद उपस्थिता<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">37</font>
  गतं हि सहसा भूमिं नकुलॊ मां समाप्नुयात<br>
      उलूकश चेह तिष्ठन्तं मार्जारः पाशसंक्षयात<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">38</font>
  न तव एवास्मद विधः पराज्ञः संमॊहं गन्तुम अर्हति<br>
      करिष्ये जीविते यत्नं यावद उच्छ्वासनिग्रहम<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">39</font>
  न हि बुद्ध्यान्विताः पराज्ञा नीतिशास्त्रविशारदाः<br>
      संभ्रमन्त्य आपदं पराप्य महतॊ ऽरथान अवाप्य च<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">40</font>
  न तव अन्याम इह मार्जाराद गतिं पश्यामि सांप्रतम<br>
      विषमस्थॊ हय अयं जन्तुः कृत्यं चास्य महन मया<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">41</font>
  जीवितार्थी कथं तव अद्य परार्थितः शत्रुभिस तरिभिः<br>
      तस्माद इमम अहं शत्रुं मार्जारं संश्रयामि वै<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">42</font>
  कषत्रविद्यां समाश्रित्य हितम अस्यॊपधारये<br>
      येनेमं शत्रुसंघातं मतिपूर्वेण वञ्चये<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">43</font>
  अयम अत्यन्तशत्रुर मे वैषम्यं परमं गतः<br>
      मूढॊ गराहयितुं सवार्थं संगत्या यदि शक्यते<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">44</font>
  कदा चिद वयसनं पराप्य संधिं कुर्यान मया सह<br>
      बलिना संनिविष्टस्य शत्रॊर अपि परिग्रहः<br>
      कार्य इत्य आहुर आचार्या विषमे जीवितार्थिना<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">45</font>
  शरेयान हि पण्डितः शत्रुर न च मित्रम अपण्डितम<br>
      मम हय अमित्रे मार्जारे जीवितं संप्रतिष्ठितम<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">46</font>
  हन्तैनं संप्रवक्ष्यामि हेतुम आत्माभिरक्षणे<br>
      अपीदानीम अयं शत्रुः संगत्या पण्डितॊ भवेत<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">47</font>
  ततॊ ऽरथगतितत्त्वज्ञः संधिविग्रहकालवित<br>
      सान्त्वपूर्वम इदं वाक्यं मार्जारं मूषकॊ ऽबरवीत<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">48</font>
  सौहृदेन भिभाषे तवा कच चिन मार्जारजीवसि<br>
      जीवितं हि तवेच्छामि शरेयः साधारणं हि नौ<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">49</font>
  न ते सौम्य विषत्तव्यं जीविष्यसि यथा पुरा<br>
      अहं तवाम उद्धरिष्यामि पराणाञ जह्यां हि ते कृते<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">50</font>
  अस्ति कश चिद उपायॊ ऽतर पुष्कलः परतिभाति माम<br>
      येन शक्यस तवया मॊक्षः पराप्तुं शरेयॊ यथा मया<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">51</font>
  मया हय उपायॊ दृष्टॊ ऽयं विचार्य मतिम आत्मनः<br>
      आत्मार्थं च तवदर्थं च शरेयः साधारणं हि नौ<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">52</font>
  इदं हि नकुलॊलूकं पापबुद्ध्य अभितः सथितम<br>
      न धर्षयति मार्जारतेन मे सवस्ति सांप्रतम<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">53</font>
  कूजंश चपल नेत्रॊ ऽयं कौशिकॊ मां निरीक्षते<br>
      नगशाखा गरहस तिष्ठंस तस्याहं भृशम उद्विजे<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">54</font>
  सतां साप्तपदं सख्यं स वासॊ मे ऽसि पण्डितः<br>
      सांवास्यकं करिष्यामि नास्ति ते मृत्युतॊ भयम<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">55</font>
  न हि शक्नॊषि मार्जारपाशं छेत्तुं विना मया<br>
      अहं छेत्स्यामि ते पाशं यदि मां तवं न हिंससि<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">56</font>
  तवम आश्रितॊ नगस्याग्रं मूलं तव अहम उपाश्रितः<br>
      चिरॊषिताव इहावां वै वृक्षे ऽसमिन विदितं हि ते<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">57</font>
  यस्मिन्न आश्वसते कश चिद यश च नाश्वसते कव चित<br>
      न तौ धीराः परशंसन्ति नित्यम उद्विग्नचेतसौ<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">58</font>
  तस्माद विवर्धतां परीतिः सत्या संगतिर अस्तु नौ<br>
      कालातीतम अपार्थं हि न परशंसन्ति पण्डिताः<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">59</font>
  अर्थयुक्तिम इमां तावद यथा भूतां निशामय<br>
      तव जीवितम इच्छामि तवं ममेच्छसि जीवितम<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">60</font>
  कश चित तरति काष्ठेन सुगम्भीरां महानदीम<br>
      स तारयति तत काष्ठं स च काष्ठेन तार्यते<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">61</font>
  ईदृशॊ नौ समायॊगॊ भविष्यति सुनिस्तरम<br>
      अहं तवां तारयिष्यामि तवं च मां तारयिष्यसि<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">62</font>
  एवम उक्त्वा तु पलितस तदर्थम उभयॊर हितम<br>
      हेतुमद गरहणीयं च कालाकाङ्क्षी वयपैक्षत<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">63</font>
  अथ सुव्याहृतं तस्य शरुत्वा शत्रुर विचक्षणः<br>
      हेतुमद गरहणीयार्थं मार्जारॊ वाक्यम अब्रवीत<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">64</font>
  बुद्धिमान वाक्यसंपन्नस तद वाक्यम अनुवर्णयन<br>
      ताम अवस्थाम अवेक्ष्यान्त्यां साम्नैव परत्यपूजयत<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">65</font>
  ततस तीक्ष्णाग्रदशनॊ वैडूर्यमणिलॊचनः<br>
      मूषकं मन्दम उद्वीक्ष्य मार्जारॊ लॊमशॊ ऽबरवीत<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">66</font>
  नन्दामि सौम्य भद्रं ते यॊ मां जीवन्तम इच्छसि<br>
      शरेयश च यदि जानीषे करियतां मा विचारय<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">67</font>

  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">68</font>

  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">74</font>

  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">75</font>

  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">78</font>

  अकालविप्रमुक्तान मे तवत्त एव भयं भवेत<br>

  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">91</font>

      ततश छेत्स्यामि ते पाशं पराप्ते साधारणे भये<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">92</font>

      न हि ते जीविताद अन्यत किं चित कृत्यं भविष्यति<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">93</font>
  ततॊ भवत्य अतिक्रान्ते तरस्ते भीते च लॊमश<br>
      अहं बिलं परवेक्ष्यामि भवाट शाखां गमिष्यति<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">94</font>

  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">95</font>

  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">96</font>

  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">97</font>
  ताथैव तवरमाणेन तवया कार्यं हितं मम<br>
      यत्नं कुरु महाप्राज्ञ यथा सवस्त्य आवयॊर भवेत<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">98</font>
  अथ वा पूर्ववैरं तवं समरन कालं विकर्षसि<br>

  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">99</font>

  छिन्नं तु तन्तु बाहुल्यं तन्तुर एकॊ ऽवशेषितः<br>

       yathā rājan na muhyeta śatrubhiḥ parivāritaḥ<br>
   <font color="GREEN" size="-1">3</font>

       pṛcchāmi tvā kuru kśreṣṭha tan me vyākhyātum arhasi<br>

       etad icchāmy ahaṃ śrotuṃ sarvam eva yathāvidhi<br>
   <font color="GREEN" size="-1">5</font>

       bahavo 'py ekam uddhartuṃ yatante pūrvatāpitāḥ<br>
   <font color="GREEN" size="-1">6</font>
  sarvataḥ prārthyamānena durbalena mahābalaiḥ<br>
       ekenaivāsahāyena śakyaṃ sthātuṃ kathaṃ bhavet<br>
   <font color="GREEN" size="-1">7</font>
  kathaṃ mitram ariṃ caiva vindeta bharatarṣabha<br>
       ceṣṭitavyaṃ kathaṃ cātha śatror mitrasya cāntare<br>
   <font color="GREEN" size="-1">8</font>

       kathaṃ nu puruṣaḥ kuryāt kiṃ vā kṛtvā sukhī bhavet<br>
   <font color="GREEN" size="-1">9</font>
  vigrahaṃ kena vā kuryāt saṃdhiṃ vā kena yojayet<br>
       kathaṃ vā śatrumadhyastho vartetābalavān iti<br>
   <font color="GREEN" size="-1">10</font>
  etad vai sarvakṛtyānāṃ paraṃ kṛtyaṃ paraṃtapa<br>
      naitasya kaś cid vaktāsti śrotā cāpi sudurlabhaḥ<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">11</font>
  ṛte śāṃtanavād bhīṣmāt satyasaṃdhāj jitendriyāt<br>
      tad anviṣya mahābāho sarvam etad vadasva me<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">12</font>
   [bh]<br>
      tvad yukto 'yam anupraśno yudhiṣṭhira guṇodayaḥ<br>
      śṛṇu me putra kārtsnyena guhyam āpatsu bhārata<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">13</font>
  amitro mitratāṃ yāti mitraṃ cāpi praduṣyati<br>
      sāmarthya yogāt kāryāṇāṃ tadgatyā hi sadāgatiḥ<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">14</font>
  tasmād viśvasitavyaṃ ca vigrahaṃ ca samācaret<br>
      deśaṃ kālaṃ ca vijñāya kāryākāryaviniścaye<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">15</font>
  saṃdhātavyaṃ budhair nityaṃ vyavasyaṃ ca hitārthibhiḥ<br>
      amitrair api saṃdheyaṃ prāṇā rakṣyāś ca bhārata<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">16</font>
  yo hy amitrair naro nityaṃ na saṃdadhyād apaṇḍitaḥ<br>
      na so 'rtham āpnuyāt kiṃ cit phalāny api ca bhārata<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">17</font>
  yas tv amitreṇa saṃdhatte mitreṇa ca virudhyate<br>
      arthayuktiṃ samālokya sumahad vindate phalam<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">18</font>
  atrāpy udāharantīmam itihāsaṃ purātanam<br>
      mārjārasya ca saṃvādaṃ nyagrodhe mūṣakasya ca<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">19</font>
  vane mahati kasmiṃś cin nyagrodhaḥ sumahān abhūt<br>
      latā jālaparicchanno nānādvija gaṇāyutaḥ<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">20</font>
  skandhavān meghasaṃkāśaḥ śītac chāyo manoramaḥ<br>
      vairantyam abhito jātas tarur vyālamṛgākulaḥ<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">21</font>
  tasya mūlaṃ samāśritya kṛtvā śatamukhaṃ bilam<br>
      vasati sma mahāprājñaḥ palito nāma mūṣakaḥ<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">22</font>
  śākhāś ca tasya saṃśritya vasati sma sukhaṃ puraḥ<br>
      lomaśo nāma mārjāraḥ pakṣisattvāvasādakaḥ<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">23</font>
  tatra cāgatya cāṇḍālo vairantya kṛtaketanaḥ<br>
      ayojayat tam unmāthaṃ nityam astaṃ gate ravau<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">24</font>
  tatra snāyumayān pāśān yathāvat saṃnidhāya saḥ<br>
      gṛhaṃ gatvā sukhaṃ śete prabhātām eti śarvarīm<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">25</font>
  tatra sma nityaṃ badhyante naktaṃ bahuvidhā mṛgāḥ<br>
      kadā cit tatra mārjāras tv apramatto 'py abadhyata<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">26</font>
  tasmin baddhe mahāprājñaḥ śatrau nityātatāyini<br>
      taṃ kālaṃ palito jñātvā vicacāra sunirbhayaḥ<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">27</font>
  tenānucaratā tasmin vane viśvastacāriṇā<br>
      bhakṣaṃ vicaramāṇena nacirād dṛṣṭam āmiṣam<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">28</font>
  sa tam unmātham āruhya tad āmiṣam abhakṣayat<br>
      tasyopari sapatnasya baddhasya manasā hasan<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">29</font>
  āmiṣe tu prasaktaḥ sa kadā cid avalokayan<br>
      apaśyad aparaṃ ghoram ātmanaḥ śatrum āgatam<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">30</font>
  śaraprasūna saṃkāśaṃ mahī vivara śāyinam<br>
      nakulaṃ harikaṃ nāma capalaṃ tāmralocanam<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">31</font>
  tena mūṣaka gandhena tvaramāṇam upāgatam<br>
      bhakṣārthaṃ lelihad vaktraṃ bhūmāv ūrdhvamukhaṃ sthitam<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">32</font>
  śakhā gatam ariṃ cānyad apaśyat koṭarālayam<br>
      ulūkaṃ candrakaṃ nāma tīkṣṇatuṇḍaṃ kṣapācaram<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">33</font>
  gatasya viṣayaṃ tasya nakulolūkayos tadā<br>
      athāsyāsīd iyaṃ cintā tat prāpya sumahad bhayam<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">34</font>
  āpady asyāṃ sukaṣṭāyāṃ maraṇe samupasthite<br>
      samantād bhaya utpanne kathaṃ kāryaṃ hitaiṣiṇā<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">35</font>
  sa tathā sarvato ruddhaḥ sarvatra samadarśanaḥ<br>
      abhavad bhayasaṃtaptaś cakre cemāṃ parāṃ gatim<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">36</font>
  āpad vināśabhūyiṣṭhā śataikīyaṃ ca jīvitam<br>
      samanta saṃśayā ceyam asmān āpad upasthitā<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">37</font>
  gataṃ hi sahasā bhūmiṃ nakulo māṃ samāpnuyāt<br>
      ulūkaś ceha tiṣṭhantaṃ mārjāraḥ pāśasaṃkṣayāt<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">38</font>
  na tv evāsmad vidhaḥ prājñaḥ saṃmohaṃ gantum arhati<br>
      kariṣye jīvite yatnaṃ yāvad ucchvāsanigraham<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">39</font>
  na hi buddhyānvitāḥ prājñā nītiśāstraviśāradāḥ<br>
      saṃbhramanty āpadaṃ prāpya mahato 'rthān avāpya ca<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">40</font>
  na tv anyām iha mārjārād gatiṃ paśyāmi sāṃpratam<br>

  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">41</font>
  jīvitārthī kathaṃ tv adya prārthitaḥ śatrubhis tribhiḥ<br>
      tasmād imam ahaṃ śatruṃ mārjāraṃ saṃśrayāmi vai<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">42</font>
  kṣatravidyāṃ samāśritya hitam asyopadhāraye<br>
      yenemaṃ śatrusaṃghātaṃ matipūrveṇa vañcaye<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">43</font>
  ayam atyantaśatrur me vaiṣamyaṃ paramaṃ gataḥ<br>
      mūḍho grāhayituṃ svārthaṃ saṃgatyā yadi śakyate<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">44</font>
  kadā cid vyasanaṃ prāpya saṃdhiṃ kuryān mayā saha<br>
      balinā saṃniviṣṭasya śatror api parigrahaḥ<br>
      kārya ity āhur ācāryā viṣame jīvitārthinā<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">45</font>
  śreyān hi paṇḍitaḥ śatrur na ca mitram apaṇḍitam<br>
      mama hy amitre mārjāre jīvitaṃ saṃpratiṣṭhitam<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">46</font>
  hantainaṃ saṃpravakṣyāmi hetum ātmābhirakṣaṇe<br>
      apīdānīm ayaṃ śatruḥ saṃgatyā paṇḍito bhavet<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">47</font>
  tato 'rthagatitattvajñaḥ saṃdhivigrahakālavit<br>
      sāntvapūrvam idaṃ vākyaṃ mārjāraṃ mūṣako 'bravīt<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">48</font>
  sauhṛdena bhibhāṣe tvā kac cin mārjārajīvasi<br>

  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">49</font>
  na te saumya viṣattavyaṃ jīviṣyasi yathā purā<br>

  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">50</font>

      yena śakyas tvayā mokṣaḥ prāptuṃ śreyo yathā mayā<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">51</font>

  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">52</font>

  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">53</font>

      nagaśākhā grahas tiṣṭhaṃs tasyāhaṃ bhṛśam udvije<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">54</font>
  satāṃ sāptapadaṃ sakhyaṃ sa vāso me 'si paṇḍitaḥ<br>
      sāṃvāsyakaṃ kariṣyāmi nāsti te mṛtyuto bhayam<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">55</font>
  na hi śaknoṣi mārjārapāśaṃ chettuṃ vinā mayā<br>
      ahaṃ chetsyāmi te pāśaṃ yadi māṃ tvaṃ na hiṃsasi<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">56</font>
  tvam āśrito nagasyāgraṃ mūlaṃ tv aham upāśritaḥ<br>
      ciroṣitāv ihāvāṃ vai vṛkṣe 'smin viditaṃ hi te<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">57</font>
  yasminn āśvasate kaś cid yaś ca nāśvasate kva cit<br>
      na tau dhīrāḥ praśaṃsanti nityam udvignacetasau<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">58</font>
  tasmād vivardhatāṃ prītiḥ satyā saṃgatir astu nau<br>

  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">60</font>

  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">63</font>

  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">65</font>

  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">66</font>
  nandāmi saumya bhadraṃ te yo māṃ jīvantam icchasi<br>

  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">68</font>

  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">69</font>

  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">71</font>

  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">72</font>

  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">73</font>

  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">74</font>

      harṣād udvīkṣya palitaṃ svāgatenābhyapūjayat<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">75</font>
  sa taṃ saṃpūjya palitaṃ mārjāraḥ sauhṛde sthitaḥ<br>
      suvicintyābravīd dhīraḥ prītas tvarita eva hi<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">76</font>
  kṣipram āgaccha bhadraṃ te tvaṃ me prāṇasamaḥ sakhā<br>
      tava prājña prasādād dhi kṣipraṃ prāpsyāmi jīvitam<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">77</font>
  yad yad evaṃgatenādya śakyaṃ kartuṃ mayā tava<br>
      tad ājñāpaya kartāhaṃ saṃdhir evāstu nau sakhe<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">78</font>
  asmāt te saṃśayān muktaḥ sa mitra gaṇabāndhavaḥ<br>
      sarvakāryāṇi kartāhaṃ priyāṇi ca hitāni ca<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">79</font>
  muktaś ca vyasanād asmāt saumyāham api nāma te<br>
      prītim utpādayeyaṃ ca pratikartuṃ ca śaknuyām<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">80</font>
  grāhayitvā tu taṃ svārthaṃ mārjāraṃ mūṣakas tadā<br>
      praviveśa suvisrabdhaḥ samyag arthāṃś cacāra ha<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">81</font>
  evam āśvasito vidvān mārjāreṇa sa mūṣakaḥ<br>
      mārjārorasi visrabdhaḥ suṣvāpa pitṛmātṛvat<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">82</font>
  līnaṃ tu tasya gātreṣu mārjārasyātha mūṣakam<br>
      tau dṛṣṭvā nakulolūkau nirāśau jagmatur gṛhān<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">83</font>
  līnas tu tasya gātreṣu palito deśakālavit<br>
      ciccheda pāśān nṛpate kālākāṅkṣī śanaiḥ śanaiḥ<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">84</font>
  atha bandhaparikliṣṭo mārjāro vīkṣya mūṣakam<br>
      chindantaṃ vai tadā pāśān atvarantaṃ tvarānvitaḥ<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">85</font>
  tam atvarantaṃ palitaṃ pāśānāṃ chedane tadā<br>
      saṃcodayitum ārebhe mārjāro mūṣakaṃ tadā<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">86</font>
  kiṃ saumya nābhitvarase kiṃ kṛtārtho 'vamanyase<br>
      chindhi pāśān amitraghna purā śvapaca eti saḥ<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">87</font>
  ity uktas tvaratā tena matimān palito 'bravīt<br>
      mārjāram akṛtaprajñaṃ vaśyam ātmahitaṃ vacaḥ<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">88</font>
  tūṣṇīṃ bhava na te saumya tvarā kāryā na saṃbhramaḥ<br>
      vayam evātra kālajñā na kālaḥ parihāsyate<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">89</font>
  akāle kṛtyam ārabdhaṃ kartuṃ nārthāya kalpate<br>
      tad eva kāla ārabdhaṃ mahate 'rthāya kalpate<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">90</font>
  akālavipramuktān me tvatta eva bhayaṃ bhavet<br>
      tasmāt kālaṃ pratīkṣasva kim iti tvarase sakhe<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">91</font>
  yāvat paśyāmi caṇḍālam āyāntaṃ śastrapāṇinam<br>
      tataś chetsyāmi te pāśaṃ prāpte sādhāraṇe bhaye<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">92</font>
  tasmin kāle pramuktas tvaṃ tarum evādhirohasi<br>
      na hi te jīvitād anyat kiṃ cit kṛtyaṃ bhaviṣyati<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">93</font>
  tato bhavaty atikrānte traste bhīte ca lomaśa<br>
      ahaṃ bilaṃ pravekṣyāmi bhavāṭ śākhāṃ gamiṣyati<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">94</font>
  evam uktas tu mārjāro mūṣakeṇātmano hitam<br>
      vacanaṃ vākyatattvajño jīvitārthī mahāmatiḥ<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">95</font>
  athātmakṛtya tvaritaḥ samyak praśrayam ācaran<br>
      uvāca lomaśo vākyaṃ mūṣakaṃ cirakāriṇam<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">96</font>
  na hy evaṃ mitrakāryāṇi prītyā kurvanti sādhavaḥ<br>
      yathā tvaṃ mokṣitaḥ kṛcchrāt tvaramāṇena vai mayā<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">97</font>
  tāthaiva tvaramāṇena tvayā kāryaṃ hitaṃ mama<br>
      yatnaṃ kuru mahāprājña yathā svasty āvayor bhavet<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">98</font>
  atha vā pūrvavairaṃ tvaṃ smaran kālaṃ vikarṣasi<br>
      paśya duṣkṛtakarmatvaṃ vyaktam āyuḥ kṣayo mama<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">99</font>
  yac ca kiṃ cin mayājñānāt purastād vipriyaṃ kṛtam<br>
      na tan manasi kartavyaṃ kṣamaye tvāṃ prasīda me<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">100</font>
  tam evaṃ vādinaṃ prājñaḥ śāstravid buddhisaṃmataḥ<br>
     uvācedaṃ vacaḥ śreṣṭhaṃ mārjāraṃ mūṣakas tadā<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">101</font>
  śrutaṃ me tava mārjārasvam arthaṃ parigṛhṇataḥ<br>
     mamāpi tvaṃ vijānīhi svam arthaṃ parigṛhṇataḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">102</font>
  yan mitraṃ bhītavat sādhyaṃ yan mitra bhayasaṃhitam<br>
     surakṣitaṃ tataḥ kāryaṃ pāṇiḥ sarpamukhād iva<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">103</font>
  kṛtvā balavatā saṃdhim ātmānaṃ yo na rakṣati<br>
     apathyam iva tad bhuktaṃ tasyānarthāya kalpate<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">104</font>
  na kaś cit kasya cin mitraṃ na kaś cit kasya cit suhṛt<br>
     arthair arthā nibadhyante gajair vanagajā iva<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">105</font>
  na hi kaś cit kṛte kārye kartāraṃ samavekṣate<br>
     tasmāt sarvāṇi kāryāṇi sāvaśeṣāṇi kārayet<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">106</font>
  tasmin kāle 'pi ca bhavān divā kīrtibhayānvitaḥ<br>
     mama na grahaṇe śaktaḥ palāyanaparāyaṇaḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">107</font>
  chinnaṃ tu tantu bāhulyaṃ tantur eko 'vaśeṣitaḥ<br>
     chetsyāmy ahaṃ tad apy āśu nirvṛto bhava lomaśa<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">108</font>
  tayoḥ saṃvadator evaṃ tathaivāpannayor dvayoḥ<br>
     kṣayaṃ jagāma sā rātrir lomaśaṃ cāviśad bhayam<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">109</font>
  tataḥ prabhātasamaye vikṛtaḥ kṛṣṇapiṅgalaḥ<br>
     sthūlasphig vikaco rūkṣaḥ śvacakraparivāritaḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">110</font>
  śaṅkukarṇo mahāvaktraḥ palito ghoradarśanaḥ<br>
     parigho nāma caṇḍālaḥ śastrapāṇir adṛśyata<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">111</font>
  taṃ dṛṣṭvā yamadūtābhaṃ mārjāras trastacetanaḥ<br>
     uvāca palitaṃ bhītaḥ kim idānīṃ kariṣyasi<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">112</font>
  atha cāpi susaṃtrastau taṃ dṛṣṭvā ghoradarśanam<br>
     kṣaṇena nakulolūkau nairāśyaṃ jagmatus tadā<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">113</font>
  balinau matimantau ca saṃghātaṃ cāpy upāgatau<br>
     aśakyau sunayāt tasmāt saṃpradharṣayituṃ balāt<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">114</font>
  kāryārthaṃ kṛtasaṃdhī tau dṛṣṭvā mārjāramūṣakau<br>
     ulūka nakulau tūrṇaṃ jagmatuḥ svaṃ svam ālayam<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">115</font>
  tataś ciccheda taṃ tantuṃ mārjārasya sa mūṣakaḥ<br>
     vipramukto 'tha mārjāras tam evābhyapatad drumam<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">116</font>
  sa ca tasmād bhayān mukto mukto ghoreṇa śatruṇā<br>
     bilaṃ viveśa palitaḥ śākhāṃ bheje ca lomaśaḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">117</font>
  unmātham apy athādāya caṇḍālo vīkṣya sarvaśaḥ<br>
     vihatāśaḥ kṣaṇenātha tasmād deśād apākramat<br>
     jagāma ca svabhavanaṃ caṇḍālo bharatarṣabha<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">118</font>
  tatas tasmād bhayān mukto durlabhaṃ prāpya jīvitam<br>
     bilasthaṃ pādapāgrasthaḥ palitaṃ lomaśo 'bravīt<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">119</font>
  akṛtvā saṃvidaṃ kāṃ cit sahasāham upaplutaḥ<br>
     kṛtajñaṃ kṛtakalyāṇaṃ kac cin māṃ nābhiśaṅkase<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">120</font>
  gatvā ca mama viśvāsaṃ dattvā ca mama jīvitam<br>
     mitropabhoga samaye kiṃ tvaṃ naivopasarpasi<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">121</font>
  kṛtvā hi pūrvaṃ mitrāṇi yaḥ paścān nānutiṣṭhati<br>
     na sa mitrāṇi labhate kṛcchrāsv āpatsu durmatiḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">122</font>
  tat kṛto 'haṃ tvayā mitraṃ sāmarthyād ātmanaḥ sakhe<br>
     sa māṃ mitratvam āpannam upabhoktuṃ tvam arhasi<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">123</font>
  yāni me santi mitrāṇi ye ca me santi bāndhavāḥ<br>
     sarve tvāṃ pūjayiṣyanti śiṣyā gurum iva priyam<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">124</font>
  ahaṃ ca pūjayiṣye tvāṃ samitragaṇabāndhavam<br>
     jīvitasya pradātāraṃ kṛtajñaḥ ko na pūjayet<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">125</font>
  īśravo me bhavān astu śarīrasya gṛhasya ca<br>
     arthānāṃ caiva sarveṣām anuśāstā ca me bhava<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">126</font>
  amātyo me bhava prājña piteva hi praśādhi mām<br>
     na te 'sti bhayam asmatto jīvitenātmanaḥ śape<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">127</font>
  buddhyā tvam uśanāḥ sākṣād bale tv adhikṛtā vayam<br>
     tvanmantrabalayukto hi vindeta jayam eva ha<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">128</font>
  evam uktaḥ paraṃ sāntvaṃ mārjāreṇa sa mūṣakaḥ<br>
     uvāca paramārthajñaḥ ślakṣṇam ātmahitaṃ vacaḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">129</font>
  yad bhavān āha tat sarvaṃ mayā te lomaśa śrutam<br>
     mamāpi tāvad bruvataḥ śṛṇu yat pratibhāti mām<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">130</font>
  veditavyāni mitrāṇi boddhavyāś cāpi śatravaḥ<br>
     etat susūkṣmaṃ loke 'smin dṛśyate prājñasaṃmatam<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">131</font>
  śatrurūpāś ca suhṛdo mitrarūpāś ca śatravaḥ<br>
     sāntvitās te na budhyante rāgalobha vaśaṃgatāḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">132</font>
  nāsti jātyā ripur nāma mitraṃ nāma na vidyate<br>
     sāmarthya yogāj jāyante mitrāṇi ripavas tathā<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">133</font>
  yo yasmiñ jīvati svārthaṃ paśyet tāvat sa jīvati<br>
     sa tasya tāvan mitraṃ syād yāvan na syād viparyayaḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">134</font>
  nāsti maitrī sthirā nāma na ca dhruvam asauhṛdam<br>
     arthayuktyā hi jāyante mitrāṇi ripavas tathā<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">135</font>
  mitraṃ ca śatrutām eti kasmiṃś cit kālaparyaye<br>
     śatruś ca mitratām eti svārtho hi balavattaraḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">136</font>
  yo viśvasati mitreṣu na cāśvasati śatruṣu<br>
     arthayuktim avijñāya calitaṃ tasya jīvitam<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">137</font>
  arthayuktim avijñāya yaḥ śubhe kurute matim<br>
     mitre vā yadi vā śatrau tasyāpi calitā matiḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">138</font>
  na viśvased aviśvaste viśvaste 'pi na viśvaset<br>
     viśvāsād bhayam utpannaṃ mūlāny api nikṛntati<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">139</font>
  arthayuktyā hi deśyante pitā mātā sutās tathā<br>
     mātulā bhāgineyāś ca tathā saṃbandhibāndhavāḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">140</font>
  putraṃ hi mātā pitaru tyajataḥ patitaṃ priyam<br>
     loko rakṣati cātmānaṃ paśya svārthasya sāratām<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">141</font>
  taṃ manye nikṛtiprajñaṃ yo mokṣaṃ pratyanantaram<br>
     kṛtyaṃ mṛgayase kartuṃ sukhopāyam asaṃśayam<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">142</font>
  asmin nilaya eva tvaṃ nyagrodhād avatāritaḥ<br>
     pūrvaṃ niviṣṭam unmāthaṃ capalatvān na buddhivān<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">143</font>
  ātmanaś capalo nāsti kuto 'nyeṣāṃ bhaviṣyati<br>
     tasmāt sarvāṇi kāryāṇi capalo hanty asaṃśayam<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">144</font>
  bravīti madhuraṃ kaṃ cit priyo me ha bhavān iti<br>
     tan mithyā karaṇaṃ sarvaṃ vistareṇāpi me śṛṇu<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">145</font>
  kāraṇāt priyatām eti dveṣyo bhavati kāraṇāt<br>
     arthārthī jīvaloko 'yaṃ na kaś cit kasya cit priyaḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">146</font>
  sakhyaṃ sodarayor bhrātror dampatyor vā parasparam<br>
     kasya cin nābhijānāmi prītiṃ niṣkāraṇām iha<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">147</font>
  yady api bhrātaraḥ kruddhā māryā vā kāraṇāntare<br>
     svabhāvatas te prīyante netaraḥ prīyate janaḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">148</font>
  priyo bhavati dānena priyavādena cāparaḥ<br>
     mantrahoma japair anyaḥ kāryārthaṃ prīyate janaḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">149</font>
  utpanne kāraṇe prītir nāsti nau kāraṇāntare<br>
     pradhvaste kāraṇasthāne sā prītir vinivartate<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">150</font>
  kiṃ nu tat kāraṇaṃ manye yanāhaṃ bhavataḥ priyaḥ<br>
     anyatrābhyavahārārthāt tatrāpi ca budhā vayam<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">151</font>
  kālo hetuṃ vikurute svārthas tam anuvartate<br>
     svārthaṃ prājño 'bhijānāti prājñaṃ loko 'nuvartate<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">152</font>
  na tv īdṛśaṃ tvayā vācyaṃ viduṣi svārthapaṇḍite<br>
     akāle 'viṣamasthasya svārthahetur ayaṃ tava<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">153</font>
  tasmān nāhaṃ cale svārthāt susthitaḥ saṃdhivigrahe<br>
     abhrāṇām iva rūpāṇi vikurvanti kṣaṇe kṣaṇe<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">154</font>
  adyaiva hi ripur bhūtvā punar adyaiva sauhṛdam<br>
     punaś ca ripur adyaiva yuktīnāṃ paśya cāpalam<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">155</font>
  āsīt tāvat tu maitrī nau yāvad dhetur abhūt purā<br>
     sā gatā saha tenaiva kālayuktena hetunā<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">156</font>
  tvaṃ hi me 'tyantataḥ śatruḥ sāmarthyān mitratāṃ gataḥ<br>
     tat kṛtyam abhinirvṛttaṃ prakṛtiḥ śatrutāṃ gatā<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">157</font>
  so 'ham evaṃ praṇītāni jñātvā śāstrāṇi tattvataḥ<br>
     praviśeyaṃ kathaṃ pāśaṃ tvatkṛtaṃ tad vadasva me<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">158</font>
  tvadvīryeṇa vimukto 'haṃ madvīryeṇa tathā bhavān<br>
     anyonyānugrahe vṛtte nāsti bhūyaḥ samāgamaḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">159</font>
  tvaṃ hi saumya kṛtārtho 'dya nirvṛttārthās tathā vayam<br>
     na te 'sty anyan mayā kṛtyaṃ kiṃ cid anyatra bhakṣaṇāt<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">160</font>
  aham annaṃ bhavān bhoktā durbalo 'haṃ bhavān balī<br>
     nāvayor vidyate saṃdhir niyukte viṣame bale<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">161</font>
  saṃmanye 'haṃ tava prajñāṃ yan mokṣāt pratyanantaram<br>
     bhakṣyaṃ mṛgayase nūnaṃ sukhopāyam asaṃśayam<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">162</font>
  bhakṣyārtham eva baddhas tvaṃ sa muktaḥ prasṛtaḥ kṣudhā<br>
     śāstrajñam abhisaṃdhāya nūnaṃ bhakṣayitādya mām<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">163</font>
  jānāmi kṣudhitaṃ hi tvām āhārasamayaś ca te<br>
     sa tvaṃ mām abhisaṃdhāya bhakṣyaṃ mṛgayase punaḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">164</font>
  yac cāpi putradāraṃ svaṃ tat saṃnisṛjase mayi<br>
     śuśrūṣāṃ nāma me kartuṃ sakhe mama na tatkṣamam<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">165</font>
  tvayā māṃ sahitaṃ dṛṣṭvā priyā bhāryā sutāś ca ye<br>
     kasmān māṃ te na khādeyur hṛṣṭāḥ praṇayinas tvayi<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">166</font>
  nāhaṃ tvayā sameṣyāmi vṛtto hetuḥ samāgame<br>
     śivaṃ dhyāyasva me 'trasthaḥ sukṛtaṃ smaryate yadi<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">167</font>
  śatror annādya bhūtaḥ san kliṣṭasya kṣudhitasya ca<br>
     bhakṣyaṃ mṛgayamāṇasya kaḥ prājño viṣayaṃ vrajet<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">168</font>
  svasti te 'stu gamiṣyāmi dūrād api tavodvije<br>
     nāhaṃ tvayā sameṣyāmi nirvṛto bhava lomaśa<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">169</font>
  balavat saṃnikarṣo hi na kadā cit praśasyate<br>
     praśāntād api me prājña bhetavyaṃ balinaḥ sadā<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">170</font>
  yadi tv arthena me kāryaṃ brūhi kiṃ karavāṇi te<br>
     kāmaṃ sarvaṃ pradāsyāmi na tv ātmānaṃ kadā cana<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">171</font>
  ātmārthe saṃtatis tyājyā rājyaṃ ratnaṃ dhanaṃ tathā<br>
     api sarvasvam utsṛjya rakṣed ātmānam ātmanā<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">172</font>
  aiśvaryadhanaratnānāṃ pratyamitre 'pi tiṣṭhatām<br>
     dṛṣṭā hi punar āvṛttir jīvitām iti naḥ śrutam<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">173</font>
  na tv ātmanaḥ saṃpradānaṃ dhanaratnavad iṣyate<br>
     ātmā tu sarvato rakṣyo dārair api dhanair api<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">174</font>
  ātmarakṣita tantrāṇāṃ suparīkṣita kāriṇām<br>
     āpado nopapadyante puruṣāṇāṃ svadoṣajāḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">175</font>
  śatrūn samyag vijānanti durbalā ye balīyasaḥ<br>
     teṣāṃ na cālyate buddhir ātmārthaṃ kṛtaniścayā<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">176</font>
  ity abhivyaktam evāsau palitenāvabhartsitaḥ<br>
     mārjāro vrīḍito bhūtvā mūṣakaṃ vākyam abravīt<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">177</font>
  saṃmanye 'haṃ tava prajñāṃ yas tvaṃ mama hite rataḥ<br>
     uktavān arthatattvena mayā saṃbhinnadarśanaḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">178</font>
  na tu mām anyathā sādho tvaṃ vijñātum ihārhasi<br>
     prāṇapradānajaṃ tvatto mama sauhṛdam āgatam<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">179</font>
  dharmajño 'smi guṇajño 'smi kṛtajño 'smi viśeṣataḥ<br>
     mitreṣu vatsalaś cāsmi tvadvidheṣu viśeṣataḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">180</font>
  tan mām evaṃgate sādho na yāvayitum arhasi<br>
     tvayā hi yāvyamāno 'haṃ prāṇāñ jahyāṃ sabāndhavaḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">181</font>
  dhik śabdo hi budhair dṛṣṭo madvidheṣu manasviṣu<br>
     maraṇaṃ dharmatattvajña na māṃ śaṅkitum arhasi<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">182</font>
  iti saṃstūyamāno hi mārjāreṇa sa mūṣakaḥ<br>
     manasā bhāvagambhīraṃ mārjāraṃ vākyam abravīt<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">183</font>
  sādhur bhavāñ śrutārtho 'smi prīyate na ca viśvase<br>
     saṃstavair vā dhanaughair vā nāhaṃ śakyaḥ punas tvayā<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">184</font>
  na hy amitravaśaṃ yānti prājñā niṣkaraṇaṃ sakhe<br>
     asminn arthe ca gāthe dve nibodhośanasā kṛte<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">185</font>
  śatrusādhāraṇe kṛtye kṛtvā saṃdhiṃ balīyasā<br>
     samāhitaś cared yuktyā kṛtārthaś ca na viśvaset<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">186</font>
  tasmāt sarvāsv avasthāsu rakṣeñ jīvitam ātmanaḥ<br>
     dravyāṇi saṃtatiś caiva sarvaṃ bhavati jīvataḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">187</font>
  saṃkṣepo nītiśāstrāṇām aviśvāsaḥ paro mataḥ<br>
     nṛṣu tasmād aviśvāsaḥ puṣkalaṃ hitam ātmanaḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">188</font>
  vadhyante na hy aviśvastāḥ śatrubhir durbalā api<br>
     viśvastās tv āśu vadhyante balavanto 'pi durbalaiḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">189</font>
  tvadvidhebhyo mayā hy ātmā rakṣyo mārjārasarvadā<br>
     rakṣa tvam api cātmānaṃ caṇḍālāñ jātikilbiṣāt<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">190</font>
  sa tasya bruvatas tv evaṃ saṃtrāsāñ jātasādhvasaḥ<br>
     svabaliṃ hi javenāśu mārjāraḥ prayayau tataḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">191</font>
  tataḥ śāstrārthatattvajño buddhisāmarthyam ātmanaḥ<br>
     viśrāvya palitaḥ prājño bilam anyañ jagāma ha<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">192</font>
  evaṃ prajñāvatā buddhyā durbalena mahābalāḥ<br>
     ekena bahavo 'mitrāḥ saṃdhiṃ kurvīta paṇḍitaḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">193</font>
  ariṇāpi samarthena saṃdhiṃ kurvīta paṇḍitaḥ<br>
     mūṣakaś ca viḍālaś ca muktāv anyonyasaṃśrayāt<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">194</font>
  ity eṣa kṣatradharmasya mayā mārgo 'nudarśitaḥ<br>
     vistareṇa mahīpāla saṃkṣepeṇa punaḥ śṛṇu<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">195</font>
  anyonyakṛtavairau tu cakratuḥ prītim uttamām<br>
     anyonyam abhisaṃdhātum abhūc caiva tayor matiḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">196</font>
  tatra prājño 'bhisaṃdhatte samyag buddhibalāśrayāt<br>
     abhisaṃdhīyate prājñaḥ pramādād api cābudhaiḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">197</font>
  tasmād abhītavad bhīto viśvastavad aviśvasan<br>
     na hy apramattaś calati calito vā vinaśyati<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">198</font>
  kālena ripuṇā saṃdhiḥ kāle mitreṇa vigrahaḥ<br>
     kārya ity eva tattvajñāḥ prājur nityaṃ yudhiṣṭhira<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">199</font>
  evaṃ matvā mahārāja śāstrārtham abhigamya ca<br>
     abhiyukto 'pramattaś ca prāg bhayād bhītavac caret<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">200</font>
  bhītavat saṃvidhiḥ kāryaḥ pratisaṃdhis tathaiva ca<br>
     bhayād utpadyate buddhir apramattābhiyogajā<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">201</font>
  na bhayaṃ vidyate rājan bhītasyānāgate bhaye<br>
     abhītasya tu visrambhāt sumahāñ jāyate bhayam<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">202</font>
  na bhīrur iti cātyantaṃ mantro 'deyaḥ kathaṃ cana<br>
     avijñānād dhi vijñāte gacched āspada darśiṣu<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">203</font>
  tasmād abhītavad bhīto viśvastavad aviśvasan<br>
     kāryāṇāṃ gurutāṃ buddhvā nānṛtaṃ kiṃ cid ācaret<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">204</font>
  evam etan mayā proktam itihāsaṃ yudhiṣṭhira<br>
     śrutvā tvaṃ suhṛdāṃ madhye yathāvat samupācara<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">205</font>
  upalabhya matiṃ cāgryām arimitrāntaraṃ tathā<br>
     saṃdhivigrahakālaṃ ca mokṣopāyaṃ tathāpadi<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">206</font>
  śatrusādhāraṇe kṛtye kṛtvā saṃdhiṃ balīyasā<br>
     samāgamaṃ cared yuktyā kṛtārtho na ca viśvaset<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">207</font>
  aviruddhāṃ trivargeṇa nītim etāṃ yudhiṣṭhira<br>
     abhyuttiṣṭha śrutād asmād bhūyas tvaṃ rañjayan prajāḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">208</font>
  brāhmaṇaiś cāpi te sārdhaṃ yātrā bhavatu pāṇḍava<br>
     brāhmaṇā hi paraṃ śreyo divi ceha ca bhārata<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">209</font>
  ete dharmasya vettāraḥ kṛtajñāḥ satataṃ prabho<br>
     pūjitāḥ śubhakarmāṇaḥ pūrvajityā narādhipa<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">210</font>
  rājyaṃ śreyaḥ paraṃ rājan yaśaḥ kīrtiṃ ca lapsyase<br>
     kulasya saṃtatiṃ caiva yathānyāyaṃ yathākramam<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">211</font>
  dvayor imaṃ bhārata saṃdhivigrahaṃ; subhāṣitaṃ buddhiviśeṣakāritam<br>
     tathānvavekṣya kṣitipena sarvadā; niṣevitavyaṃ nṛpa śatrumaṇḍale<br>
