 <p>éka, alone: éka<i>h</i>, X, 121, I; 3; 7:2; 8; I, 165, 3; ékam, I, 165, 6; VIII, 20, 13; ékasya <i>k</i>it me, I, 165, 10.—éka<i>h</i>-eka<i>h</i>, one by one, V, 61, 1.</p>  
 <p>ékam-eka: ékam-ekâ <i>s</i>atâ´, each a hundred, V, 52, 17.</p>  
 <p>e<i>g</i>: e<i>g</i>ati, (the earth) trembles, V, 59, 2.—é<i>g</i>atha, you stir, VIII, 20, 4.</p>  
 <p>e<i>d</i>a, a kind of sheep: (I, 166, 10:3; 235.)</p>  
 <p>e<i>n</i>a, a kind of antelope: (I, 166, 10:3; 235.)</p>  
 <p>éta, the fallow deer: étâ<i>h</i>, (I, 165, 1:3); V, 54, 5; étân, I, 165, 5:2.—étâ<i>h</i>, speckled deerskins, I, 166, 10:3; (<a href="sbe3227.htm#page_232">232</a>; <a href="sbe3227.htm#page_234">234</a> seq.)—étâ<i>h</i> ná, like harts, X, 77, 2.—See ena.</p>  
 <p>etád, this …: etâ´ni ví<i>s</i>vâ <i>g</i>âtâ´ni, all these created things, X, 121, 10.—etâ´n and étân, (I, 165, 5:2.)—eté, there they are, I, 165, 12.—etát tyát, I, 88, 5; eshâ´ syâ´, I, 88, 6.—etâ´ni ni<i>n</i>yâ´, these secrets, VII, 56, 4:1.</p>  
 <p>éta<i>s</i>a, the (solar) horse: éta<i>s</i>a<i>h</i>, I, 168, 5:4.</p>  
 <p>etâ´vat, so much: etâ´vat, VII, 57, 3.—etâ´vata<i>h</i> <i>k</i>it eshâm, of that immense (host) of them, VIII, 7, 15:1.</p>  
 <p>ena, fem. enî: enyã<i>h</i>, spotted deer, V, 53, 7:2—See éta, (<a href="sbe3227.htm#page_234">234</a> seq.)</p>  
 <p>enad, this: enâ´, instr., II, 34, 14; V, 53, 12; enân, V, 52, 6.</p>  
 <p>énas, sin: éna<i>h</i>, VII, 58, 5; VI, 74, 3.</p>  
 <p>éman, march: éma-bhi<i>h</i>, V, 59, 2.</p>  
 <p>éva, horse: évâsa<i>h</i>, I, 166, 4; (I, 168, 1:2.)</p>  
 <p>evá: evá ít, truly, I, 165, 12; evá and evám, (I, 168, 1:2.)—evá, thus, II, 33, 15.</p>  
 <p>evám, thus: adverb of éva, (I, 168, 1:2.)</p>  
 <p>evayâ´, quickly moving, quick: eva-yâ´<i>h</i>, I, 168, 1:2 (conjecture for deva-yâ´<i>h</i>); (<a href="sbe3244.htm#page_365">365</a>.)</p>  
 <p>evayâ´marut, Evayâmarut, a sacrificial shout: evayâ´marut, V, 87, 1 to 9; (<a href="sbe3244.htm#page_365">365</a>); (I, 168, 1:2.)</p>  
 <p>eva-yâ´van, the constant wanderer: eva-yâ´vna<i>h</i>, II, 34, 11.—fem. eva-yâ´varî, (I, 168, 1:2); (<a href="sbe3244.htm#page_365">365</a>.)</p>  
 <p>eshá, rapid: eshásya (vísh<i>n</i>o<i>h</i>), II, 34, 11:1; VIII, 20, 3:2.</p>
