 <p align="center"><a name="img_12900"></a>
 <img src="img/12900.jpg"></p>  <h5 align="center">CONSONANTS</h5>  
 <p><i>NḄ.</i>—The Tamil alphabet is not fully phonetic as are the Sanskrit and the other Dravidian alphabets. Several letters indicate different sounds in different connections.</p>  
 <p align="center"><a name="img_12901"></a>
 <img src="img/12901.jpg"></p>
