To the Moon Svâhâ! To the Stars<br> Svâhâ! To the Waters Svâhâ! To Varuna Svâhâ! To<br> the Navel Svâhâ! To the Purified Svâhâ!<br> 3 To Speech Svâhâ! To Breath Svâhâ! To Breath Svâhâ!<br> To Sight Svâhâ! To Sight Svâhâ! To Hearing Svâhâ!<br> To Hearing Svâhâ!<br> 4 The wish and purpose of the mind and truth of speech may<br> I obtain.<br> Bestowed on me be cattle's form, swept taste of food, and<br> fame and grace. Svâhâ!<br> 5 Prajâpati while in preparation; Samrâj when prepared;<br> All-Gods when seated; Gharma when heated with fire;<br> Splendour when lifted up; the Asvins while milk is<br> poured in; Pûshan's when the butter trickles down it;<br> the Maruts when the milk is clotting; Mitra's when the<br> milk's skin is spreading; Vâyu's when it is carried off;<br> Agni's while offered as oblation; Vâk when it has been<br> offered.<br> 6 Savitar on the first day; Agni on the second; Vâyu on the<br> third; Âditya (the Sun) on the fourth; Chandramâs (the<br> Moon) on the fifth; Ritu on the sixth; the Maruts on<br> the seventh; Brihaspati on the eighth; Mitra on the<br> ninth; Varuna on the tenth; Indra on the eleventh;<br> the All-Gods on the twelfth.</p>

 <p>7 Fierce; Terrible; The Resonant; The Roarer; Victorious;<br> Assailant; and Dispeller. Svâhâ.<br> 8 Agni with the heart; Lightning with the heart's point;<br> Pasupati with the whole heart; Bhava with the liver.<br> Sarva with the two cardiac bones; Îsâna with Passion;<br> Mahâdeva with the intercostal flesh; the Fierce God with<br> the rectum; Vasishtha-hanuh, Singis with two lumps of<br> flesh near the heart.<br> 9 The Fierce with blood; Mitra with obedience, Rudra with<br> disobedience; Indra with pastime; the Maruts with<br> strength; the Sâdhyas with enjoyment.<br> Bhava's is what is on the throat; Rudra's what is between<br> the ribs; Mahâdeva's is the liver; Sarva's the rectum;<br> Pasupati's the pericardium.<br> 10 To the hair Svâhâ! To the hair Svâhâ! To the skin Svâhâ!<br> To the skin Svâhâ! To the blood Svâhâ! To the blood<br> Svâhâ! To the fats Svâhâ! To the fats Svâhâ! To the<br> fleshy parts Svâhâ! To the fleshy parts Svâhâ! To the<br> sinews Svâhâ! To the sinews Svâhâ! Svâhâ to the bones!<br> Svâhâ to the bones! To the marrows Svâhâ! To the<br> marrows Svâhâ! To the seed Svâhâ! To the anus Svâhâ!</p>
