   <h3 align="center">PRA<i>S</i>NA I, ADHYÂYA 8, KA<i>N</i><i>D</i>IKÂ 16.</h3>  
 <p>1. There are four castes (var<i>n</i>a, viz.) Brâhma<i>n</i>as, Kshatriyas, Vai<i>s</i>yas, and <i>S</i>ûdras. <a name="fr_975"></a><a href="#fn_975"><font size="1">1</font></a></p>  
 <p>2. (Males) belonging to them (may take) wives according to the order of the castes, (viz.) a Brâhma<i>n</i>a four, <a name="fr_976"></a><a href="#fn_976"><font size="1">2</font></a></p>  
 <p>3. A Kshatriya three,</p>  
 <p>4. A Vai<i>s</i>ya two,</p>  
 <p>5. A <i>S</i>ûdra one.</p>  
 <p>6. Sons begotten on (wives) of equal or of the next lower castes (are called) Savar<i>n</i>as (of equal caste). <a name="fr_977"></a><a href="#fn_977"><font size="1">6</font></a></p>  
 <p>7. (Those born) of (wives) of the second or third lower castes (become) Ambash<i>th</i>as, Ugras, and Nishâdas. <a name="fr_978"></a><a href="#fn_978"><font size="1">7</font></a></p>  
 <p>8. Of females wedded in the inverse order of the castes (are born) Âyogavas, Mâgadhas, Vai<i>n</i>as, Kshatt<i>ri</i>s, Pulkasas, Kukku<i>t</i>akas, Vaidehakas, and <i>K</i>a<i>n</i><i>d</i>âlas. <a name="fr_979"></a><a href="#fn_979"><font size="1">8</font></a></p>  
 <p>9. An Ambash<i>th</i>a (begets) on a female of the first (caste) a <i>S</i>vapâka,</p>  
 <p>10. An Ugra on a female of the second (caste) a Vai<i>n</i>a,</p>  
 <p>11. A Nishâda on a female of the third (caste) a Pulkasa.</p>

 <p>13.      .      .      .      .      .      .      .      .      .      .      .      .      .      .      .      .      . <a name="fr_980"></a><a href="#fn_980"><font size="1">13</font></a></p>  
 <p>14.      .      .      .      .      .      .      .      .      .      .      .      .      .      .      .      .      .</p>  
 <p>16. Now they quote also (the following verse).: But those sons whom an uninitiated man begets, the wise call Vrâtyas, who are excluded from the Sâvitrî; (that is a rule which refers) in an equal manner to the three (highest) castes.' <a name="fr_981"></a><a href="#fn_981"><font size="1">16</font></a></p>
