   <h3 align="center">PRA<i>S</i>NA I, ADHYÂYA 9, KA<i>N</i><i>D</i>IKÂ 17.</h3>  
 <p>1. The Rathakâra (carpenter), the Ambash<i>th</i>a, the Sûta (charioteer), the Ugra, the Mâgadha, the Âyogava, the Vai<i>n</i>a, the Kshatt<i>ri</i>, the Pulkasa, the Kukku<i>t</i>a, the Vaidehaka, the <i>K</i>a<i>n</i><i>d</i>âla, and so forth, <a name="fr_982"></a><a href="#fn_982"><font size="1">1</font></a></p>  
 <p>2. Among these, sons of equal caste (spring) from women of equal caste.</p>  
 <p>3. A Brâhma<i>n</i>a (begets) on a female of the Kshatriya caste a Brâhma<i>n</i>a, on a female of the Vai<i>s</i>ya caste an Ambash<i>th</i>a, on a female of the <i>S</i>ûdra caste a Nishâda, <a name="fr_983"></a><a href="#fn_983"><font size="1">3</font></a></p>  
 <p>4. (According to) some a Pâra<i>s</i>ava.</p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p><a name="page_198"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 198</font></a></p>  
 <p>5. A Kshatriya (begets) on a female of the Vai<i>s</i>ya caste a Kshatriya, on a female of the <i>S</i>ûdra caste an Ugra.</p>  
 <p>6. A Vai<i>s</i>ya (begets) on a female of the <i>S</i>ûdra caste a Rathakâra.</p>  
 <p>7. A <i>S</i>ûdra begets on a female of the Vai<i>s</i>ya caste a Mâgadha, on a female of the Kshatriya caste a Kshatt<i>ri</i>, but on a female of the Brâhma<i>n</i>a caste a <i>K</i>a<i>n</i><i>d</i>âla. <a name="fr_984"></a><a href="#fn_984"><font size="1">7</font></a></p>  
 <p>8. A Vai<i>s</i>ya begets on a female of the Kshatriya caste an Âyogava, on a female of the Brâhma<i>n</i>a caste a Sûta.</p>  
 <p>9. If among these an Ambash<i>th</i>a (male) and an Ugra (female) unite, (their son) will be born in the direct order of the castes (Anuloma). <a name="fr_985"></a><a href="#fn_985"><font size="1">9</font></a></p>  
 <p>10. If a Kshatt<i>ri</i> (male) and a Vaidehaka (female) unite, (their son will be) born against the order of the castes (Pratiloma).</p>  
 <p>11. An Ugra (begets) on a female of the Kshatt<i>ri</i> caste a <i>S</i>vapâka, <a name="fr_986"></a><a href="#fn_986"><font size="1">11</font></a></p>  
 <p>12. A Vaidehaka on a female of the Ambash<i>th</i>a caste a Vai<i>n</i>a,</p>  
 <p>13. A Nishâda on a female of the <i>S</i>ûdra caste a Pulkasa, <a name="fr_987"></a><a href="#fn_987"><font size="1">13</font></a></p>  
 <p>14. A <i>S</i>ûdra on a female of the Nishâda caste a Kukku<i>t</i>aka.</p>  
 <p>15. The wise declare those sprung from an inter-mixture of the castes to be Vrâtyas. <a name="fr_988"></a><a href="#fn_988"><font size="1">15</font></a></p>
