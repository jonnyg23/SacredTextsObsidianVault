 <h1 align="CENTER">The Book of Gates</h1>
 <h2 align="CENTER">by E. A. Wallis Budge</h2>
 <h4 align="CENTER">[1905]</h4>
 <h4 align="CENTER">(original title) The short form of the Book of am-tuat and the Book of Gates</h4>
 <hr>
 <p>The <i>Book of Gates</i> is an Ancient Egyptian cosmological
 treatise describing the architecture and inhabitants
 of the Tuat, the underworld which the boat of the Sun God, Ra,
 traverses during the night hours.
 This is the second volume of the three volume Budge series
 which deals with the books of the Underworld,
 the <i>Egyptian Heaven and Hell</i>.
 It also includes a short summary of the Book of Am-Tuat, the
 longer version of which comprises the
 <a href="../bat/index.htm">first volume</a>.
 </p>
