 <p><a name="page_81"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 81</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">LX. HOW A VIGILANT KING.</h3>  
 <p align="center"><a name="img_08100"></a>
 <img src="img/08100.jpg"></p>  
 <p>And otherwise to denote a <i>vigilant king</i>, they depict the SERPENT (UPON THE WATCH), and in the place of the king's name they depict a watcher: for he is the guardian of the whole world; and the king ought to be vigilant everywhere.</p>
