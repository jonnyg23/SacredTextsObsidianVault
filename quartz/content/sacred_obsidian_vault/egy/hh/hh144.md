 <p align="center"><a name="img_12901"></a>
 <img src="img/12901.jpg"></p>  
 <p>To denote <i>a man who overcomes his private enemy</i>, they</p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p><a name="page_130"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 130</font></a></p>  
 <p>delineate an HYÆNA TURNING TO THE RIGHT; but if <i>himself overcome</i>, they on the contrary depict ONE TURNING TO THE LEFT; for should this animal, when pursued, turn to the right, it slays the pursuer, but if to the left, it is slain by the pursuer.</p>
