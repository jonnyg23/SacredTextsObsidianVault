 <p><a name="page_54"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 54</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">XXXIV. HOW A SOUL CONTINUING A LONG TIME HERE.</h3>  
 <p align="center"><a name="img_05400"></a>
 <img src="img/05400.jpg"> <a name="fr_58"></a><a href="#fn_56"><font size="1">1</font></a></p>  
 <p>When they would denote a <i>soul continuing a long time here</i>, <a name="fr_59"></a><a href="#fn_57"><font size="1">2</font></a> or an <i>inundation</i>, they depict the PHŒNIX the bird: <a name="fr_60"></a><a href="#fn_58"><font size="1">3</font></a> and they denote the <i>soul</i> by it, because this is the longest lived of all creatures in the world; and an <i>inundation</i>, because the Phœnix is a symbol of the sun, than which nothing is greater in the universe. For the sun passes over all and</p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p><a name="page_55"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 55</font></a></p>  
 <p>scrutinises all, hence he is called . . . . . Polys <a name="fr_61"></a><a href="#fn_59"><font size="1">1</font></a> (much).</p>
