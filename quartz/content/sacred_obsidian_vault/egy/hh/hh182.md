 <p align="center"><a name="img_15200"></a>
 <img src="img/15200.jpg"></p>  
 <p>When they would symbolise <i>a man addicted to gluttony</i>, they delineate A CHARFISH;</p>  
 <p><a name="page_153"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 153</font></a></p>  
 <p>for this is the only fish which ruminates, and eats all the little fishes which fall in its way.</p>
