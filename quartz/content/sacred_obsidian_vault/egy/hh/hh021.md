 <p><a name="page_40"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 40</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">XIX. HOW THEY DENOTE A WATCHFUL PERSON.</h3>  
 <p align="center"><a name="img_04000"></a>
 <img src="img/04000.jpg"> <a name="fr_45"></a><a href="#fn_43"><font size="1">1</font></a></p>  
 <p>To denote a <i>watchful person</i>, or even a <i>guard</i>, they pourtray the HEAD OF A LION, because the lion, when awake, closes his eyes, but when asleep keeps them open, which is a sign of watching. Wherefore at the gates of the temples they have symbolically appropriated lions as guardians.</p>
