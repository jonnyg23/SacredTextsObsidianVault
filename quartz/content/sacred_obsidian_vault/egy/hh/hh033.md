 <p><a name="page_52"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 52</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">XXXI. HOW TASTE.</h3>  
 <p align="center"><a name="img_05200"></a>
 <img src="img/05200.jpg"></p>  
 <p>To denote <i>taste</i> they delineate the EXTREMITY OF THE GULLET, for all taste is preserved thus far: I am speaking however of <i>perfect taste</i>. But to denote <i>imperfect taste</i> they delineate the TONGUE UPON THE TEETH, inasmuch as all taste is effected by these.</p>
