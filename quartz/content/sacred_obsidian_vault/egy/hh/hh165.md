 <p align="center"><a name="img_14200"></a>
 <img src="img/14200.jpg"></p>  
 <p>When they would symbolise <i>the presage of a plentiful vintage</i>, they depict the HOU-POO; for if this bird sings [moans?) before the season of the vines, it is a sign of a good vintage.</p>
