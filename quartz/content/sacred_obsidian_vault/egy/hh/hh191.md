 <p><a name="page_158"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 158</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">CXVIII. HOW A MAN WHO DISTRIBUTES JUSTICE IMPARTIALLY TO ALL.</h3>  
 <p align="center"><a name="img_15800"></a>
 <img src="img/15800.jpg"> <a name="fr_146"></a><a href="#fn_141"><font size="1">1</font></a></p>  
 <p>When they would symbolise <i>a man who distributes justice impartially to all</i>, they depict THE FEATHER OF AN OSTRICH; for this bird has the feathers of its wings equal on every side, beyond all other birds.</p>
