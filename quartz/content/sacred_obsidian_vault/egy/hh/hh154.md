 <p><a name="page_136"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 136</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">LXXXI. HOW THEY DENOTE A RAPACIOUS AND INACTIVE MAN.</h3>  
 <p align="center"><a name="img_13600"></a>
 <img src="img/13600.jpg"></p>  
 <p>When they would denote <i>a rapacious and inactive man</i>, they portray a CROCODILE WITH THE WING OF AN IBIS ON HIS HEAD; for if you touch him with the wing of an Ibis you will find him motionless.</p>
