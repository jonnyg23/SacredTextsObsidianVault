 <p align="center"><a name="img_13900"></a>
 <img src="img/13900.jpg"></p>  
 <p>When they would symbolise <i>a man that is providing</i> </p>  
 <p><a name="page_140"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 140</font></a></p>  
 <p><i>his own tomb</i>, they depict an ELEPHANT <a name="fr_138"></a><a href="#fn_133"><font size="1">1</font></a> BURYING ITS OWN TEETH; for when his teeth fall out, he takes them up and buries them.</p>
