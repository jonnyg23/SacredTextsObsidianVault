 <p align="center"><a name="img_06400"></a>
 <img src="img/06400.jpg"> <a name="fr_76"></a><a href="#fn_74"><font size="1">1</font></a></p>  
 <p>To signify an Horoscopus [observer of the hours], they delineate a MAN EATING THE HOURS, not that the man eats the hours, for that is impossible, but because food is prepared for men according to the hours.</p>
