 <p align="center"><a name="img_12400"></a>
 <img src="img/12400.jpg"></p>  
 <p>When they would symbolise <i>a man who is reproached with accusations and thence falls sick</i>, they delineate a BASILISK; for he kills those that approach him with his breath.</p>
