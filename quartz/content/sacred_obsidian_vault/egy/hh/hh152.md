 <p><a name="page_135"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 135</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">LXXIX. HOW A SLAYER OF SHEEP AND GOATS.</h3>  
 <p align="center"><a name="img_13500"></a>
 <img src="img/13500.jpg"></p>  
 <p>When they would symbolise <i>a slayer of sheep and goats</i>, they portray THESE ANIMALS EATING FLEABANE; for if they eat fleabane they die, being cut off by thirst.</p>
