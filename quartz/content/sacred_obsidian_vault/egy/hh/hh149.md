 <p align="center"><a name="img_13300"></a>
 <img src="img/13300.jpg"></p>  
 <p>When they would denote <i>a feverish man who cures himself</i>, they depict A LION DEVOURING AN APE; for if, when in a fever, he devours an ape, he recovers.</p>
