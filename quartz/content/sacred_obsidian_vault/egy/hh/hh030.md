 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Hieroglyphics of Horapollo</i>, tr. Alexander Turner Cory, [1840], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">XXVIII. HOW DUMBNESS.</h3>  
 <p align="center"><a name="img_05000"></a>
 <img src="img/05000.jpg"></p>  
 <p>To denote <i>dumbness</i>, they depict the number 1095, which is the number of days in the space of three years, the year consisting of 365 days, within which time, if a child does not speak, it chews that it has an impediment in its tongue.</p>
