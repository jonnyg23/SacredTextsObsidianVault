 <p align="center"><a name="img_14402"></a>
 <img src="img/14402.jpg"> <a name="fr_140"></a><a href="#fn_135"><font size="1">1</font></a></p>  
 <p>When they would symbolise <i>a man living perpetually</i> </p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p><a name="page_145"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 145</font></a></p>  
 <p><i>in motion, and agitation of mind, and not even remaining quiet during meals</i>, they depict THE YOUNG ONES OF A CROW <a name="fr_141"></a><a href="#fn_136"><font size="1">1</font></a> [a crow with its young]; for whilst she flies she feeds her young.</p>
