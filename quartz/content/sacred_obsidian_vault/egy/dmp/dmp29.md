   <h3 align="center">COL. XXVI</h3>  
 <p>(1) Another invocation again of this cup of wine: (2) 'Birakethat, (3) Samara, (4) Pilpioun, (5) Iahout, (6) Sabaouth, (7, 8) Saipounithas.'</p>  
 <p>(9) Another invocation belonging to it again, in another book: (10) 'I am Biraka-that, (11) Lathat, (12) Sasmira,(13) Plipron, (14) Takou, (15) Sabakhot, (16) Sasoupounitha, (17) send the heart of N. after (18) Sasoupounithas.'</p>
