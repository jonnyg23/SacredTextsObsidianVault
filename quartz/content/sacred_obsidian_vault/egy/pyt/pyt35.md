 <h3 align="center">32. A PURIFICATION LITANY, UTTERANCE 563.</h3>
 
 <p align="center"><i>Utterance
 563</i>.</p>
 
 <p>1408a. To say: The double doors of heaven are open, the double doors of <i>ḳbḥ.w</i> are open for Horus of the Gods,</p>
 
 <p>1408b. that he may ascend and purify himself in the Marsh of Reeds.</p>
 
 <p>1408c. The double doors of heaven are open for N., the double doors of <i>ḳbḥ.w</i> are open for N.,</p>
 
 <p>1408d. that he may ascend and purify himself in the Marsh of Reeds.</p>
 
 <p>1409a. The double doors of heaven are open, the double doors of <i>ḳbḥ.w</i> are open for Horus <i>Šsm.t</i>-land,</p>
 
 <p>1409b. that he may ascend and purify himself in the Marsh of Reeds.</p>
 
 <p>1409c. The double doors of heaven are open for N., the double doors of <i>ḳbḥ.w</i> are open for N.,</p>
 
 <p>1409d. that he may ascend and purify himself in the Marsh of Reeds.</p>
 
 <p>1410a. The double doors of heaven are open, the double doors of <i>ḳbḥ.w</i> are open for Horus of the East,</p>
 
 <p>1410b. that he may ascend and purify himself in the Marsh of Reeds.</p>
 
 <p>1410c. The double doors of heaven are open for N., the double doors of <i>ḳbḥ.w</i> are open for N.,</p>
 
 <p>1410d. that he may ascend and purify himself in the Marsh of Reeds.</p>
 
 <p>1411a. The double doors of heaven are open, the double doors of <i>ḳbḥ.w</i> are open for Horus of the
 Horizon,</p>
 
 <p>1411b. that he may ascend and purify himself in the Marsh of Reeds.</p>
 
 <p><a name="page_226"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 226</font></a></p>
 
 <p>1411c. The double doors of heaven are open, the double doors of <i>ḳbḥ.w</i> are open for N.,</p>
 
 <p>1411d. that he may ascend and purify himself in the Marsh of Reeds.</p>
 
 <p>1412a. He who ascended, ascended, Horus of the Gods, that he might purify
 himself in the Marsh of Reeds.</p>
 
 <p>1412b. He who ascended, ascended, N., that he might purify himself in the
 Marsh of Reeds.</p>
 
 <p>1413a. He who ascended, ascended, Horus, of the <i>Šsm.t</i>-land, that he might purify himself in the Marsh of Reeds.</p>
 
 <p>1413b. He who ascended, ascended, N., that he might purify himself in the
 Marsh of Reeds.</p>
 
 <p>1414a. He who ascended, ascended, Horus of the East, that he might purify
 himself in the Marsh of Reeds.</p>
 
 <p>1414b. He who ascended, ascended, N., that he might purify himself in the
 Marsh of Reeds.</p>
 
 <p>1415a. He who ascended, ascended, Horus of the Horizon, that he might purify
 himself in the Marsh of Reeds.</p>
 
 <p>1415b. He who ascended, ascended, N., that he might purify himself in the
 Marsh of Reeds.</p>
 
 <p>1416a. N. is purified; N. has taken the <i>śwḥ</i>-vestment.</p>
 
 <p>1416b. N. truly ascends to heaven, permanent like the earth.</p>
 
 <p>1416c. It is grievous (?) for thy body, O Nut, because of the divine seed,
 which shall be in thee (or, in thy mother).</p>
 
 <p>1417a. N., this one, he is the divine seed, which shall be in thy mother,
 Nut.</p>
 
 <p>1417b. Receive him, this N., as thou didst receive thy divine son.</p>
 
 <p>1418a. <i>Hpȝ<u>t</u></i>, <i>Hpȝ<u>t</u></i>, <i>Hnni</i>, <i>Hnni</i>,</p>
 
 <p>1418b. take him with you; let N. be established among you.</p>
 
 <p>1419a. <i>Ḥftn.t</i>, mother of the
 gods,</p>
 
 <p>1419b. give thy hand to N.; take his hand (or, take to thee the hand of N.),
 for life;</p>
 
 <p>1419c. draw him to heaven, like as, thou hast drawn this one, Osiris, to
 heaven.</p>
 
 <p>1420a. <i>Hnni</i>, <i>Hnni</i>, <i>Hpȝ<u>t</u></i>, <i>Hpȝ<u>t</u></i>,</p>
 
 <p>1420b. take N. with you; let N. be established among you.</p>
