 <h3 align="center">41. A LITANY-LIKE INCANTATION FOR THE ENDURANCE OF A PYRAMID AND TEMPLE, UTTERANCE 601.</h3>
 
 <p align="center"><i>Utterance
 601</i>.</p>
 
 <p>1660a. To say: O Great Ennead, who are in Heliopolis, make N. endure;</p>
 
 <p>1660b. make this pyramid of N. endure, and this his temple, for ever and
 ever,</p>
 
 <p>1660c. as the name of Atum, chief of the Great Ennead, endures.</p>
 
 <p>1661a. As the name of Shu, lord of the upper <i>mnś.t</i> in Heliopolis,
 endures,</p>
 
 <p>1661b. so may the name of N. endure,</p>
 
 <p>1661c. so may this his pyramid endure, and this his temple, likewise, for
 ever and ever.</p>
 
 <p>1662a. As the name of Tefnut, lady of the lower <i>mnś.t</i> in Heliopolis,
 is established,</p>
 
 <p>1662b. so may the name of N. be established,</p>
 
 <p><a name="page_255"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 255</font></a></p>
 
 <p>1662c. so may this pyramid be established, likewise, for ever and ever.</p>
 
 <p>1663a. As the name of Geb, even the soul of the earth, endures,</p>
 
 <p>1663b. so may the name of N. endure,</p>
 
 <p>1663c. so may this pyramid of N. endure,</p>
 
 <p>1663d. so may this his temple endure, likewise, even for ever and ever.</p>
 
 <p>1664a. As the name of Nut, in the encircled mansion in Heliopolis, endures,</p>
 
 <p>1664b. so may the name of N. endure,</p>
 
 <p>1664c. so may this his pyramid endure,</p>
 
 <p>1664d. so may this his temple endure, likewise, for ever and ever.</p>
 
 <p>1665a. As the name of Osiris, in Abydos, endures,</p>
 
 <p>1665b. so may the name of N. endure,</p>
 
 <p>1665c. so may this pyramid of N. endure,</p>
 
 <p>1665d. so may this his temple endure, likewise, even for ever and ever.</p>
 
 <p>1666a. As the name of Osiris, as First of the Westerners, endures,</p>
 
 <p>1666b. so may the name of N. endure,</p>
 
 <p>1666c. so may this pyramid of N. endure,</p>
 
 <p>1666d. so may this his temple endure, likewise, for ever and ever.</p>
 
 <p>1667a. As the name of Set, in Ombos, endures,</p>
 
 <p>1667b. so may the name of N. endure,</p>
 
 <p>1667c. so may this pyramid of N. endure,</p>
 
 <p>1667d. so may this his temple endure, likewise, for ever and ever.</p>
 
 <p>1668a. As the name of Horus endures, in Buto,</p>
 
 <p>1668b. so may the name of N. endure,</p>
 
 <p>1668c. so may this pyramid of N. endure,</p>
 
 <p>1668d. so may this his temple endure, likewise, for ever and ever.</p>
 
 <p>1669a. As the name of Rē, on the horizon, endures,</p>
 
 <p>1669b. so may the name of N. endure,</p>
 
 <p>1669c. so may this pyramid of N. endure,</p>
 
 <p>1669d. so may this his temple endure, likewise, for ever and ever.</p>
 
 <p>1670a. As the name of <i>Mḫnti-irti</i>,
 of Letopolis, is established,</p>
 
 <p>1670b. so may the name of N. endure,</p>
 
 <p>1670c. so may this his pyramid endure,</p>
 
 <p>1670d. so may this temple
  of N. endure, likewise,
 for ever and ever.</p>
 
 <p>1671a. As the name of <i>Wȝ<u>d</u>.t</i>, in
 Buto, endures,</p>
 
 <p>1671b. so may the name of N. endure,</p>
 
 <p><a name="page_256"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 256</font></a></p>
 
 <p>1671c. so may this pyramid of N. endure,</p>
 
 <p>1671d. so may this his temple endure, likewise, for ever and ever.</p>
