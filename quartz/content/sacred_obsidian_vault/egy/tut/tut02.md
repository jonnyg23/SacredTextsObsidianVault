 <h1 align="CENTER">NOTES</h1>
 <font face="Times Roman,Times New Roman"><p>The kings of the XVIIIth dynasty reigned about 230 years, <i>i.e.</i>, from about B.C. 1580 to 1350; their names are as follows:--</p><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <p>Aahmes 1580, reigned about 22 years.</p>
 <p>Amenhetep I, about 1558-7, reigned about 10 years.</p>
 <p>Thothmes I, about 1546, reigned about 30 years.</p>
 <p>Thothmes II, about 1500, reigned about 3 years.</p>
 <p>Hatshepsut, Thothmes III about 1500 to 1447.</p>
 <p>Amenhetep II, about 1448, reigned about 26 years.</p>
 <p>Thothmes IV, about 1420, reigned about 8 years.</p>
 <p>Amenhetep III, about 1412, reigned 36 years.</p>
 <p>Amenhetep IV, about 1376, reigned 17 years.</p>
 <p>Sakara, Tutankhamen, Ai reigned 8-12 years.</p>
 <p>Heremheb, about 1350, reigned 34 years.</p></dir>
 </dir>
 
 <i></i><p>Redactors Note: the diacritical marks in the original have been omitted.</p>
