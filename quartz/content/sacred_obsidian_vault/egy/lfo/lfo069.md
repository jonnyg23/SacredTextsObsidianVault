 <h3 align="CENTER">THE SEVENTIETH CEREMONY.</h3>
 <font face="Times Roman,Times New Roman"><p>A loin of beef (?), <i>Aa</i>, with a formula, two versions of which are extant. The rendering of one is:--</p>
 <p>"Osiris Unas, [here is] the marked piece of flesh and bone from which Seb cuts not off the <i>aau</i> joint," <a name="fr_74"></a><a href="#fn_74"><font size="1">1</font></a></p>
