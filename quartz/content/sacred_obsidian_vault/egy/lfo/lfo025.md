 <h3 align="CENTER">THE TWENTY-FIRST CEREMONY.</h3>
 <font face="Times Roman,Times New Roman"><p>In the next ceremonies the various kinds of bread and cakes on the "Tchesert" table were offered one by one. The first was the <i>tept</i>, and as the SEM priest presented it the Kher heb said:--</p>
 <p>"Unas, the Eye of Horus hath been presented unto thee for thy tasting."</p>
 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_08100"></a>
 <img src="img/08100.jpg"><br>
 </p></font><font face="Times Roman,Times New Roman" size="1">The Sem priest presenting the Tept cake</font><font face="Times Roman,Times New Roman">.
 <p>Here there is a play on the words <i>tept</i>, a "kind of bread," and <i>tep</i>, "to taste."</p>
