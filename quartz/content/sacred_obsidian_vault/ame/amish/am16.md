 <p><a name="page_17b"><font size="1" color="green">p. 17b</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">BOOKS ABOUT THE AMISH AND THE MENNONITES</h3>  
 <p>• A number of books are to be had dealing with the Amish and the Mennonites. Space will not allow listing of more than a few of the titles, viz:</p>  
 <p><b>Story of the Mennonites, The</b>. By C. Henry Smith. 8vo cloth (new edition) Newton, Kansas 1950. (The story of the Mennonites from the days of the Dutch Reformer, Menno Simons, to our present time; an important book).</p>  
 <p><b>Rosanna of the Amish</b>. By Joseph W. Yoder. 8vo cloth 319 pp Huntingdon, Pa. (An intimate story of the Conservative Amish, by one well-informed).</p>  
 <p><b>Rosanna's Boys</b>. By Joseph W. Yoder. 8vo cloth 345 pp. (A follow-up to <b>Rosanna of the Amish</b>).</p>  
 <p><b>Story of the Pennsylvania Dutch</b>, The. Interesting lithographs in color by C. H. DeWitt; text by Ann Hark. Quarto (Suitable to juvenile and adult).</p>  
 <p>• Prices of the above titles, and many others on the Pennsylvania Germans, may be had by writing for latest catalog. <b>The Aurand Press, Harrisburg, Pa</b>.</p>
