 <p align="CENTER">A GOOD REMEDY FOR BAD WOUNDS AND BURNS.</p> 
 <p>The word of God, the milk of Jesus' mother, and Christ's blood, is for all wounds and burnings good.</p> 
 <p align="RIGHT">+ + +</p> 
 <p>It is the safest way in all these cases to make the crosses with the hand or thumb three times over the affected parts; that is to say, over all those things to which the three crosses are attached.</p>
