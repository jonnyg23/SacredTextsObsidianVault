 <p align="CENTER">HOW TO TREAT A COW AFTER THE MILK IS TAKEN FROM HER.</p> 
 <p>Give to the cow three spoonfuls of her last milk, and say to the spirits in her blood: "Ninny has done it, and I have swallowed her in the name of God the Father, the Son, and the Holy Ghost. Amen." Pray what you choose at the same time.</p>
