 <p align="CENTER">A GOOD METHOD OF DESTROYING RATS AND MICE.</p> 
 <p>Every time you bring grain into your barn, you must, in putting down the three first sheaves, repeat the following words: "Rats and mice, these three sheaves I give to you, in order that you may not destroy any of my wheat." The name of the kind of grain must also be mentioned.</p>
