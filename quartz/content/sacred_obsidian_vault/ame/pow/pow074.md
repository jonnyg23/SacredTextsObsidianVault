 <p align="CENTER">TO MAKE GOOD BEER.</p> 
 <p>Take a handful of hops, five or six gallons of water, about three tablespoonfuls of ginger, half a gallon of molasses; filter the water, hops and ginger into a tub containing the molasses.</p>
