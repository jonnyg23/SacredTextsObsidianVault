 <p align="CENTER">TO PREVENT BAD PEOPLE FROM GETTING ABOUT THE CATTLE.</p> 
 <p>Take wormwood, gith, five-finger weed, and assaftida; three cents' worth of each; the straw of horse</p> 
 <p><a name="page_41"><font size="1" color="green">p. 41</font></a></p> 
 <p>beans, some dirt swept together behind the door of the stable and a little salt. Tie these all up together with a tape, and put the bundle in a hole about the threshold over which your cattle pass in and out, and cover it well with lignum-vitæ wood. This will certainly be of use.</p>
