 <p align="CENTER">TO REMOVE A WEN DURING THE CRESCENT MOON.</p> 
 <p>Look over the wen, directly towards the moon, and say "Whatever grows, does grow; and whatever diminishes, does diminish." This must be said three times in the same breath.</p>
