 <p>
 This is another classic fictional hollow earth adventure.
 The narrator, Olaf Jansen, is a Norwegian who sails with
 his father deep into the northern ice.
 There they sail over the lip of the hollow earth, and into
 the inner world, lit by a dim central sun (the 'smoky god' of the title).
 The inhabitants are an advanced race of giants who have electricity,
 monorails, and extremely long lifespans.
 The travelers return to the surface through the Antarctic, and
 as is usual in this genre, lose everything which could confirm their
 tale on the return voyage.
 The author, Willis George Emerson (1856-1918), was an American novelist;
 none of his other books appear to be on esoteric themes.
 </p>
