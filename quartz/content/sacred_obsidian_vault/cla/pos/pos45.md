 <p><a name="page_55"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 55</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">THE SACRIFICE</h3>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Upon</span> a cushion soft<br>   My limbs I place,<br> My every garment doffed<br>   For deeper grace;<br> From burning doves embalmed<br>   In baccharis,<br> The scented fumes have calmed<br>   Me like a kiss.</p>  
 <p>Beyond the phallic shrine<br>   That tripods light,<br> I pledge with holy wine<br>   An image white;<br> Anadyomene,<br>   Than foam more fair,<br> When from the ravished sea<br>   She rose to air.</p>  
 <p>Daughter of God, accept<br>   These gifts of mine!<br> Last night my body slept<br>   In arms divine.<br> These sated lips and eyes<br>   That erstwhile sued,<br> Accord this sacrifice<br>   In gratitude.</p>
