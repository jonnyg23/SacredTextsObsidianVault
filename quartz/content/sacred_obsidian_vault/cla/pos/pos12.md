 <p><a name="page_14"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 14</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">ANACREON'S SONG</h3>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Golden-Throned</span> Muse, sing the song that in olden<br> Days was sung of love and delight in Teos,<br> In the goodly land of the lovely women:<br>                Strains that in other</p>  
 <p>Years the hoary bard with the youthful fancy<br> Set to mirthful stir of flutes, when the dancing<br> Nymphs that poured the wine for the poet's banquet<br>                Mixed it with kisses;</p>  
 <p>Sing the song while I, in the arms of Atthis,<br> Seal her lips to mine with a lover's fervor,<br> Breathe her breath and drink her sighs to the honeyed<br>                Lull of the melics.</p>
