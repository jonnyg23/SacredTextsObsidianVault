 <p><a name="page_62"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 62</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">HYPORCHEME</h3>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Thus</span> contend the maidens<br>   In the cretic dance,<br> Rosy arms that glisten,<br>   Eyes that glance;</p>  
 <p>Cheeks as fair as blossoms,<br>   Parted lips that glow,<br> With their honeyed voices<br>   Chanting low;</p>  
 <p>With their plastic bodies<br>   Swaying to the flute,<br> Moving with the music<br>   Never mute;</p>  
 <p>Graceful the orchestric<br>   Figures they unfold,<br> While the vesper heaven<br>   Turns to gold.</p>
