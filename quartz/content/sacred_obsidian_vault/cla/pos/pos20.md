 <p><a name="page_25"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 25</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">EPITHALAMIUM</h3>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Vesper</span> is here! behold<br>   Faint gleams that welcome shine!<br> Rise from the feast, O youths,<br>   And chant the fescennine!</p>  
 <p>Before the porch we sing<br>   The hymeneal song;<br> Vesper is here, O youths!<br>   The star we waited long.</p>  
 <p>We lead the festal groups<br>   Across the bridegroom's porch;<br> Vesper is here, O youths!<br>   Wave high the bridal torch.</p>  
 <p>Hail, noble bridegroom, hail!<br>   The virgin fair has come;<br> Unlatch the door and lead<br>   Her timid footsteps home.</p>  
 <p>Hail, noble bridegroom, hail!<br>   Straight as a tender tree;<br> Fond as a folding vine<br>   Thy bride will cling to thee.</p>
