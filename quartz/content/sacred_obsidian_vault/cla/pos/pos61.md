 <p><a name="page_74"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 74</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">MEGARA</h3>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Thou</span> burnest us, Megara,<br> With thy passions wild;<br> Bringing from Panormus<br> Such unbridled fires.</p>  
 <p>Thou burnest us, a supple<br> Flow of tortured flame,<br> Raging, biting, searing,<br> Lawless of the will.</p>  
 <p>Thou burnest us, Megara,<br> Love must know reserve,<br> Curbing power to keep it<br> Keener for restraint.</p>
