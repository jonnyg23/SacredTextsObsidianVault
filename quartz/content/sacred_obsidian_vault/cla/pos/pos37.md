 <p><a name="page_44"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 44</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">THE DYE</h3>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">From</span> Scythian wood they brew<br> The dye whose yellow hue<br> Turns gold the lovely hair<br> Of Lesbians fair.</p>  
 <p>So, Zanthis, slave of mine,<br> Shall dip the fleeces fine,<br> And dye the robes I made<br> A saffron shade.</p>
