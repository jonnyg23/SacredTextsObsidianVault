 <p><a name="page_52"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 52</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">THE FIRST KISS</h3>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">And</span> down I set the cushion<br> Upon the couch that she,<br> Relaxed supine upon it,<br> Might give her lips to me.</p>  
 <p>As some enamored priestess<br> At Aphrodite's shrine,<br> Entranced I bent above her<br> With sense of the divine.</p>  
 <p>She had, by nature nubile,<br> In years a child, no hint<br> Of any secret knowledge<br> Of passion's least intent.</p>  
 <p>Her mouth for immolation<br> Was ripe, and mine the art;<br> And one long kiss of passion<br> Deflowered her virgin heart.</p>
