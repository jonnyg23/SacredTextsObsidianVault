 <p><a name="page_58"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 58</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">THE LOVE OF SELENE</h3>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Across</span> the still sea's moonlit wave<br>        Selene came<br> Softly to seek the Latmian cave,<br>        Her breast aflame</p>  
 <p>With secret passion's ruthless throe,<br>        Her scruples done,<br> And burning with desire to know<br>        Endymion.</p>
