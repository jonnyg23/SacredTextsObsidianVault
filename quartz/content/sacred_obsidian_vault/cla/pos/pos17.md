 <p><a name="page_20"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 20</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">LONG AGO</h3>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Long</span> ago beloved, thy memory, Atthis,<br> Saddens still my heart as the soft Æolic<br> Twilight deepens down on the sea, and fitful<br>           Winds that have wandered</p>  
 <p>Over groves of myrtle at Amathonte<br> Waft forgotten passion on breaths of perfume.<br> Long ago, how madly I loved thee, Atthis!<br>           Faithless, light-hearted</p>  
 <p>Loved one, mine no more, who lovest another<br> More than me; the silent flute and the faded<br> Garlands haunt the heart of me thou forgettest,<br>           Long since thy lover.</p>
