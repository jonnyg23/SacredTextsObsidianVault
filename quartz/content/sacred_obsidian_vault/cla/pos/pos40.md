 <p><a name="page_50"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 50</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">PASSION</h3>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Now</span> Love shakes my soul, a mighty<br>   Wind from the high mountain falling<br>   Full on the oaks of the forest;</p>  
 <p>Now, limb-relaxing, it masters<br>   My life and implacable thrills me,<br>   Rending with anguish and rapture.</p>  
 <p>Now my heart, paining my bosom,<br>   Pants with desire as a mænad<br>   Mad for the orgiac revel.</p>  
 <p>Now under my skin run subtle<br>   Arrows of flame, and my body<br>   Quivers with surge of emotion.</p>  
 <p>Now long importunate yearnings<br>   Vanquish with surfeit my reason;<br>   Fainting my senses forsake me.</p>
