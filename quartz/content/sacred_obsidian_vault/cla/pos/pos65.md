 <p><a name="page_78"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 78</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">ANAGORA</h3>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Anagora</span>, fairest<br> Spoil of fateful battle,<br> Babylonian temples<br> Knew thy luring song.</p>  
 <p>Wrested from barbaric<br> Captors for thy beauty,<br> Thou wert made a priestess<br> At Mylitta's shrine.</p>  
 <p>Once these flexile fingers<br> Clasped in mine so closely,<br> Neath the temple's arches<br> Thrummed the tabor soft.</p>  
 <p>Thou hast taught me secrets<br> Of the cryptic chambers,<br> How the zonahs worship<br> In the burning East;</p>  
 <p>Raptures that my wildest<br> Dreaming never pictured,<br> Arts of love that charmed me,<br> Subtle, new and strange.</p>  
 <p>Hearken to my earnest<br> Prayer, O Aphrodite!<br> May the night be doubled<br> Now for our delight.</p>
