 <p><a name="page_56"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 56</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">LEDA</h3>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Once</span> on a time<br>   They say that Leda found<br> Beneath the thyme<br>   An egg upon the ground;</p>  
 <p>And yet the swan<br>   She fondled long ago<br> Was whiter than<br>   Its shell of peeping snow.</p>
