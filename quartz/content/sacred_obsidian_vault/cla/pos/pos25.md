 <p><a name="page_30"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 30</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">PERSEPHONE</h3>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">I saw</span> a tender maiden plucking flowers<br> Once, long ago, in the bright morning hours;<br> And then from heaven I saw a sudden cloud<br> Fall swift and dark, and heard her cry aloud.</p>  
 <p>Again I looked, but from my open door<br> My anxious eyes espied the maid no more;<br> The cloud had vanished, bearing her away<br> To underlands beyond the smiling day.</p>
