 <p><a name="page_82"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 82</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">GOLDEN PULSE</h3>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Golden</span> pulse grew on the shore,<br>   Ferns along the hill,<br> And the red cliff roses bore<br>   Bees to drink their fill;</p>  
 <p>Bees that from the meadows bring<br>   Wine of melilot,<br> Honey-sups on golden wing<br>   To the garden grot.</p>  
 <p>But to me, neglected flower,<br>   Phaon will not see,<br> Passion brings no crowning hour,<br>   Honey nor the bee.</p>
