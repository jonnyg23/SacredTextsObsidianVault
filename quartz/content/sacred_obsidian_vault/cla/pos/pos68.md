 <p><a name="page_83"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 83</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">THE SWALLOW</h3>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Daughter</span> of Pandion, lovely<br> Swallow that veers at my window,<br> Swift on the flood of the sunshine<br>         Darting thy shadow;</p>  
 <p>What is thy innocent purpose,<br> Why dost thou hover and haunt me?<br> Is it a kinship of sorrow<br>         Brings thee anear me?</p>  
 <p>Must thou forever be tongueless,<br> Flying in fear of Tereus?<br> Must he for Itys pursue thee,<br>         Changed to a lapwing?</p>  
 <p>Tireless of pinion and never<br> Resting on bush or the branches,<br> Close to the earth, up the azure,<br>         Over the treetops;</p>  
 <p>After thy wing in its madness<br> Follows my glance, as a flitting<br> Child on the track of its mother<br>         Hastens in silence.</p>  
 <p>Daughter of Pandion, lovely<br> Swallow that veers at my window,<br> Hast thou a message from Cyprus<br>         Telling of Phaon?</p>
