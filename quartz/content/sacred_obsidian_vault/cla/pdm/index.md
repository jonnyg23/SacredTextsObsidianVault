 <p>
 This is a short biography of Pythagoras, written from a Theosophical,
 esoteric perspective.
 No actual texts of Pythagoras have survived:
 we only have second hand summaries and pastiches,
 such as the <a href="../gvp/index.htm">Golden Verses</a>.
 However, his impact on successive
 thinkers has been enormous.
 This work serves as a short, readable introduction to the man,
 his thought and times, and the
 modern esoteric interpretation of his place in the history of ideas.
 </p>
