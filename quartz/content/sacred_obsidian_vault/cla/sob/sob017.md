 <h3 align="CENTER">PHITTA MELIAI</h3>
 <p>As soon as the sun's heat diminishes, we will go and play on the banks of the river; we will struggle for a frail crocus, or for a sopping hyacinth.</p>
 <p>We will make a human necklace, and we'll weave a wreath of girls. We will take each other by the hand, and grasp each other's tunic-skirts.</p>
 <p>Phitta Meliai! give us honey! Phitta Naïades! let us bathe with you. Phitta Meliades! shade sweetly our perspiring bodies.</p>
 <p>And we will offer you, oh! beneficent nymphs, no shameful wine, but oil and milk and many crook-horned goats.</p>
