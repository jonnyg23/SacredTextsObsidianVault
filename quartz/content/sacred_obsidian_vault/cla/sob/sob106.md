 <h3 align="CENTER">CONVERSATION</h3>
 <p>Good-day. --Good-day to you. --You are in a hurry. --Perhaps less in a hurry than you think.</p>
 <p>--You are a pretty girl. --Perhaps more pretty than you even know.</p>
 <p>--What is your charming name? --I do not tell my name in such a hurry. --Have you some one for this evening? --I always have my lover. --And how do you love him? --As he would wish.</p>
 <p>--Shall we dine together? --If you wish. But what will you give? --This. --Five drachmae? That is for my slave. And me? --Say it yourself. --A hundred.</p>
 <p>--Where do you live? --In that blue house. --At what time do you wish me to come seek you? --At once if you should care. --At once. --Lead on.</p>
