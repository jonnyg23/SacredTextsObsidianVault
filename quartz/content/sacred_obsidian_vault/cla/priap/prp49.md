 <h1 align="CENTER">49</h1>
 <h3 align="CENTER">Priapus</h3>
 <p>Tu, quicumque vides circa tectoria nostra<br>
    non nimium casti carmina plena ioci,<br>
 versibus obscenis offendi desine: non est<br>
    mentula subducti nostra supercilii.</p>
 <p>Thou, who art 'customed to view around the walls of our temple<br>
 Verse of a strain jocose rather than modest and chaste,<br>
 Cease to be hurt by the song obscene, for verily ne'er was<br>
 Wont our mentule to wear eyebrow up-drawn in surprise.</p>
 <p>Thou who seest the walls of my temple covered round with jocose poems, not too chaste, cease to be shocked at the obscene verses: mine is not a mentule with raised eyebrow.</p>
