 <h1 align="CENTER">12</h1>
 <h3 align="CENTER">Priapus</h3>
 <p>Percidere puer, moneo: futuere puella:<br>
    barbatum furem tertia poena manet.</p>
 <p>Thou shalt be pedicate (lad!), thou also (lass!) shalt be rogered;<br>
 While for the bearded thief is the third penalty kept.</p>
 <p>I warn thee, my lad, thou wilt be sodomised; thee, my girl, I shall futter;[1] for the thief who is bearded, a third punishment[2] remains.</p>
 <font size="2"><p>[1. <i>Futuere</i>. Used frequently by Martial. Derived from <i>fundo</i>, to pour out (the semen).</p>
 <p>2. Tertia poene in the Latin original meaning irrumation, or coition with the mouth.]</p>
 </font><hr>
