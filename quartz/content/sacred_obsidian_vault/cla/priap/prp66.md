 <h1 align="CENTER">66</h1>
 <h3 align="CENTER">Priapus</h3>
 <p>Tu, quae ne videas notam virilem,<br>
 hinc averteris, ut decet pudicam:<br>
 nimirum, nisi quod times videre,<br>
 intra viscera habere concupiscis.</p>
 <p>Thou, who lest manly mark thy glances meet,<br>
 Hence fain avertest thee as suits the pure;<br>
 No wonder 'twere if that to see thou fear'st<br>
 Within thy vitals thou desire to feel.</p>
 <p>Thou who, lest thou behold the virile sip, hence withdrawest, as becomes a maiden of modesty: forsooth, unless what thou fearest to see, in thy bowels to have thou longest.</p>
