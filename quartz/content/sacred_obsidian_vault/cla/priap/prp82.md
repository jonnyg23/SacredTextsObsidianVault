 <h1 align="CENTER">82</h1>
 <h3 align="CENTER">To Priapus</h3>
 <p>Dum vivis, sperare decet: tu, rustice custos,<br>
    huc ades et nervis, tente Priape, fave.</p>
 <p>While there is life 'tis fitting to hope, O rustical guardian!<br>
 Here be thou present and thou aid us, Priapus stiff-nerved.</p>
 <p>Whilst there is life, 'tis fitting to hope; do thou, O rustic guardian, be present here; and, O stiff-nerved Priapus, be propitious.</p>
