 <h1 align="CENTER">44</h1>
 <h3 align="CENTER">Priapus</h3>
 <p>Velle quid hanc dicas, quamvis sim ligneus, hastam,<br>
    oscula dat medio si qua puella mihi?<br>
 augure non opus est: 'in me' mihi credite, dixit<br>
    'aptetur veris usibus hasta rudis'.</p>
 <p>What shouldest say this spear (although I'm wooden) be wishing<br>
 Whenas a maiden chance me in the middle to kiss?<br>
 Here none augur we: need: believe my word she is saying -<br>
 'Let the rude spear in me work with its natural wont!'</p>
 <p>What dost thou say this spear, although I be wooden, is wishing, if any girl give kisses to my middle? It needs no soothsayer, for, believe me, she has said, 'The rude spear will exercise its true functions on me.'[1]</p>
