 <h1 align="CENTER">94</h1>
 <h3 align="CENTER">A Wheaten Priapus</h3>
 <h3 align="CENTER">Also by Marcus Valerius Martialis</h3>
 <p>Si vis esse satur, nostrum potes esse Priapum:<br>
     Ipsa licet rodas inguina, purus eris.</p>
 <p>An thou would fain go filled thou mayest devour our Priapus;<br>
 Even consume his yard--pure thou shalt ever remain.</p>
 <p>If thou desirest to appease thine hunger, thou canst eat our Priapus; thou mayst munch even its privities, thou wilt still be pure.</p>
