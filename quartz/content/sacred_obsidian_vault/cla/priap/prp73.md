 <h1 align="CENTER">73</h1>
 <h3 align="CENTER">To Priapus</h3>
 <p>Tutelam pomarii, diligens Priape, facito:<br>
 rubricato furibus minare mutinio.</p>
 <p>Of vergers diligent guard (Priapus!), threat<br>
 These robbing loons with thy red amulet.</p>
 <p>O Priapus, faithful protector of orchards, warn off the thieves with thy red-painted amulet.</p>
