 <h1 align="CENTER">74</h1>
 <h3 align="CENTER">Priapus</h3>
 <p>Quod monear, non est, quia si furaberis ipse<br>
    grandia mala, tibi bracchia macra dabo.</p>
 <p>Not to be moved am I; shouldst thou, Thief, venture on thieving<br>
 These big apples, to thee <i>pommes de bragues</i> I will give.</p>
 <p>I am inflexible; if thou shalt steal my large apples, I will give thee the apples of the breeches[2]</p>
 <font size="2"><p>[1. By being sodomised. The text seems to infer that the god took pleasure in<br>
 punishing a thief.</p>
 <p>2. Apples meaning testicles.]</p>
 </font><hr>
