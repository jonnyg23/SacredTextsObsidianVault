 <h1 align="CENTER">58</h1>
 <h3 align="CENTER">Priapus</h3>
 <p>Quicumque nostram fur fefellerit curam,<br>
 effeminato verminet procul culo;<br>
 quaeque hic proterva carpserit manu poma<br>
 puella, nullum reperiat fututorem.</p>
 <p>Whatever thief shall trick my faith may he<br>
 Wither, far banisht from th' effeminate bum!<br>
 Whatever damsel plucks with wanton hand<br>
 This fruitage, never find she one to strum!</p>
 <p>Whatever thief who deceives my faith[1] may he wither away, far from the buttocks of a catamite. And whatso girl who with audacious hand plucks off these apples, may she meet with no futterer.</p>
