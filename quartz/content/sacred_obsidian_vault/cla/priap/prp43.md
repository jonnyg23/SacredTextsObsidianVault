 <h1 align="CENTER">43</h1>
 <h3 align="CENTER">Priapus</h3>
 <p>Nolite omnia, quae loquor, putare<br>
 per lusum mihi per iocumque dici.<br>
 deprensos ego ter quaterque fures<br>
 omnes, ne dubitetis, irrumabo.</p>
 <p>Refrain from deeming all my sayings be<br>
 In sport bespoken for mine own disport;<br>
 Thieves taken thrice or four tunes in the fact<br>
 (Believe my word) I'll surely irrumate.</p>
 <p>Think not that everything I say is spoken in jest and for my own amusement. That ye may not be in doubt, I tell ye this, that all thieves who are often caught I shall irrumate.</p>
