 <h1 align="CENTER">59</h1>
 <h3 align="CENTER">Priapus</h3>
 <p>Praedictum tibi ne negare possis:<br>
 si fur veneris, impudicus exis.</p>
 <p>Know, lest due warning be denied by thee,<br>
 An thief thou come male whore shalt surely flee.</p>
 <p>Know this, lest thou shouldst deny being warned, if thou comest a thief thou wilt go dishonoured.[2]</p>
