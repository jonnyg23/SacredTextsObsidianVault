 <p>
 Fabre d'Olivet (b. 1768, d. 1825), a French esoteric writer,
 originally published this book in 1813.
 The first section, an essay on esoteric Poetics, was dedicated
 to the Section of Literature of the Imperial Institute of France.
 The <a href="ogv11.htm">second portion</a> contains the Greek text
 of the Golden Verses, along with French and English translations.
 The final part of the book is an extended essay on the Golden Verses.
 </p>
 <p>
 The Golden Verses are also available at this site in a more recent English <a href="../gvp/index.htm">translation by Florence Firth</a>.
 </p>
