 <h1 align="CENTER">The Golden Verses of Pythagoras</h1>
 <h4 align="CENTER">And Other Pythagorean Fragments</h4>
 <h3 align="CENTER">Selected and Arranged by Florence M. Firth</h3>
 <h5 align="CENTER">With an Introduction by Annie Besant</h5>
 <h6 align="CENTER">[1904]</h6>
 <hr>
 <p>This little collection of sayings, attributed to the Greek
 philosopher and mathematician Pythagoras and his followers,
 embody what we know about his teachings today.
 Besides exhortations to live a moral, simple and contemplative life,
 these pithy aphorisms allow a glimpse of
 a bit of the Pythagorean schools' deeper knowledge.
 </p>
 <p><a href="../ogv/index.htm">A detailed study of the Golden Verses</a> by the French esoteric writer
 Fabre d'Olivet is also available at this site.
