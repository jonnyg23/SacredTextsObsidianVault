 <h1 align="CENTER"><a name="BM34">34</a></h1>
 <p>Οὐκ οἶδ᾽ ὄττι θέω, δύο μοι τὰ νοήματα. <font size="1"><a href="xlatall.htm#54">[transcription]</a></font>
 </p>
 <p>I know not what to do: I have two minds.</p>
 <p>In doubt I am, I have two minds,<br>
 I know not what to do.</p>
 <p>Quoted about 220 B.C. by Chrysippus, the Stoic philosopher.</p>
