 <h1 align="CENTER"><a name="BM8">8</a></h1>
 <p>Σοί δ᾽ ἔγο δεύκασ ἔπι βῶμον ἄιγοσ<br>
 ...<br>
 καπιλείψω τοι ... <font size="1"><a href="xlatall.htm#12">[transcription]</a></font>
 </p>
 <p>But for thee I will bring to the altar [the young] of a white goat... and add a libation for thee.</p>
 <p>Cited by Apollonius of Alexadria about A.D. 140. The reading is uncertain.</p>
