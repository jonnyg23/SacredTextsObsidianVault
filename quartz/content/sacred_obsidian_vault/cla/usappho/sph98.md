 <h1 align="CENTER"><a name="BM97">97</a></h1>
 <p>Μελλίχιοσ δ᾽ ἐπ᾽ ἰμμέρτῳ κέχυται προσώποῳ. <font size="1"><a href="xlatall.htm#123">[transcription]</a></font>
 </p>
 <p>And a sweet expression spreads over her fair face.</p>
 <p>From Hepaestion. Compare Catullus, "Mellitos oculos." and "Pulcher es neque te Venus negligit."</p>
