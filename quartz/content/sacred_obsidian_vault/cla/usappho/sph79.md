 <h1 align="CENTER"><a name="BM78">78</a></h1>
 <p>Ὀ πλοῦτοσ ἄνευ σεῦ γ᾽ ἀρέτα ᾽στ᾽ οὐκ ἀσίνησ πάροικοσ,<br>
 [ὴ δ ἐξ ἀμφοτέρων κρᾶσισ εὐδαιμονίασ ἔχει το ἄκρον.] <font size="1"><a href="xlatall.htm#101">[transcription]</a></font>
 </p>
 <p>Wealth without thee, Worthiness is no safe neighbor, [but the mixture of both is the height of happiness].</p>
 <p>From the Scholiast on Pindar. The second line is apparently a gloss of the commentator.</p>
