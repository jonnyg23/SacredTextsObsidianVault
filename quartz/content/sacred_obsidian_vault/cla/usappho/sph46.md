 <h1 align="CENTER"><a name="BM45">45</a></h1>
 <p>Μάλα δὴ κεκορημένασ Γόργωσ. <font size="1"><a href="xlatall.htm#66">[transcription]</a></font>
 </p>
 <p>Very weary of Gorgo.</p>
 <p>Quoted by Choeroboscus about A.D. 600 to show the Aeolic genitive with -ωσ <font size="1"><a href="xlatall.htm#67">[transcription]</a></font>
 . Gorgo is mentioned by Maximus Tyrius with Andromeda as being friends of Sappho.</p>
