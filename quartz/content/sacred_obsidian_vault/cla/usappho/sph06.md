 <h1 align="CENTER"><a name="BM5">5</a></h1>
 <p>     αμφὶ δ᾽ ὔδωρ<br>
 ψῖχρον ὤνεμοσ κελάδει δἰ ὔσδων<br>
 μαλίνων, αἰθυσσομένων δὲ φύλλων<br>
      κῶμα κατάρρει. <font size="1"><a href="xlatall.htm#9">[transcription]</a></font>
 </p>
 <p>And by the cool stream the breeze murmurs through apple branches and slumber pours down from quivering leaves.</p>
 <p> </p>
 <p>By the cool water the breeze murmurs, rustling<br>
 Through apple branches, while from quivering leaves<br>
      Streams down deep slumber.</p>
 <p>This beautiful fragment is quoted by Hermogenes about A.D. 170. Demetrius, about A.D. 150, says that it is part of Sappho's description of the garden of the nymphs.</p>
