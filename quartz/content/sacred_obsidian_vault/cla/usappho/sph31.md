 <h1 align="CENTER"><a name="BM30">30</a></h1>
 <p>Μνάσεσθαί τινά φαμι καὶ ὔστερον ἀμμέων. <font size="1"><a href="xlatall.htm#50">[transcription]</a></font>
 </p>
 <p>I think men will remember us even hereafter.</p>
 <p>From Dio Chrysostom, who, writing about A.D. 100, remarks that this is said "with perfect beauty."</p>
