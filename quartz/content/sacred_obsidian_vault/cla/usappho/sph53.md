 <h1 align="CENTER"><a name="BM52">52</a></h1>
 <p>Φαῖσι δὴ ποτα Λήδαν ὐακινθίνων<br>
 [ὐπ᾽ ἀνθέων] πεπυκαδμένον<br>
 εὔρην ὤιον. <font size="1"><a href="xlatall.htm#74">[transcription]</a></font>
 </p>
 <p>They say that Leda once found an egg under the hyacinths.</p>
 <p>From the "Etymologicum Magnum." It is uncertain what flower the Greeks described by the word "hyacinth." In this case the iris may be meant.</p>
