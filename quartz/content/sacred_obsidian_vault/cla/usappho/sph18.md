 <h1 align="CENTER"><a name="BM17">17</a></h1>
 <p>... κατ᾽ ἔμον στάλαγμον,<br>
 τὸν δ᾽ ἐπιπλάζοντεσ ἄμοι φέροιεν<br>
      καὶ μελεδώναισ. <font size="1"><a href="xlatall.htm#26">[transcription]</a></font>
 </p>
 <p>From my distress: let buffeting winds bear it and all care away.</p>
 <p>From the "Etymologicum Magnum" to show the Aeolic use of ζ <font size="1"><a href="xlatall.htm#27">[transcription]</a></font>
  in place of σσ <font size="1"><a href="xlatall.htm#28">[transcription]</a></font>
 . Bergk conjectures ἄμοι <font size="1"><a href="xlatall.htm#29">[transcription]</a></font>
  for ἄνεμοι <font size="1"><a href="xlatall.htm#30">[transcription]</a></font>
 , "winds". The fragment is tantilizingly incomplete, as so many others are, and the reading of one or two words in not certain.</p>
