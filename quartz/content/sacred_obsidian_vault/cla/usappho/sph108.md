 <h1 align="CENTER"><a name="BM107">107</a></h1>
 <p>Μήτ᾽ ἔμοι μέλι μήτε μέλισσα. <font size="1"><a href="xlatall.htm#136">[transcription]</a></font>
 </p>
 <p>Neither honey nor bee for me.</p>
 <p>This is a proverb quoted by a number of late authors. It is an example of Sappho's successful use of alliteration.</p>
