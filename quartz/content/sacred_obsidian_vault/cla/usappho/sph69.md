 <h1 align="CENTER"><a name="BM68">68</a></h1>
 <p>Ἤρων ἐξεδίδαξ᾽ εκ Γυάρων τὰν τανυσίδρομον. <font size="1"><a href="xlatall.htm#90">[transcription]</a></font>
 </p>
 <p>Hero of Gyara, that swift runner, I taught.</p>
 <p>Quoted by Choeroboscus to show an Aeolic form of the accusative.</p>
