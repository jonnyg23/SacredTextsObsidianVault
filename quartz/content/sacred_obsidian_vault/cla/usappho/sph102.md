 <h1 align="CENTER"><a name="BM101">101</a></h1>
 <p>Τίῳ, σ᾽, ὦ φίλε γάμβρε, κάλωσ ἔικάσω;<br>
 ὄρπακι βραδίνῳ σε κάλιστ᾽ ἔικάσδω. <font size="1"><a href="xlatall.htm#129">[transcription]</a></font>
 </p>
 <p>To what may I liken thee, dear bridegroom?<br>
 Best to a tender shoot may I liken thee.</p>
 <p>From Hephaestion as an example of metre.</p>
