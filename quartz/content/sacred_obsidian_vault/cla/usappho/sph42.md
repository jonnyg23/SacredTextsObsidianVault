 <h1 align="CENTER"><a name="BM41">41</a></h1>
 <p>Ὄτα πάννυχοσ ἄσφι κατάγρει. <font size="1"><a href="xlatall.htm#61">[transcription]</a></font>
 </p>
 <p>When all night long [sleep] holds them.</p>
 <p>Bergk suggest that the words ὄππατ᾽ ἄωροσ <font size="1"><a href="xlatall.htm#62">[transcription]</a></font>
  may have preceeded these words. The fragment quoted by Apollonius, and its sense may be "when all night long sleep holds their eyes,"</p>
