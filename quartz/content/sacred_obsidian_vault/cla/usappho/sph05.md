 <h1 align="CENTER"><a name="BM4">4</a></h1>
 <p>Αστερεσ μέν ἀμφι κάλαν σελάνναν<br>
 ἆιψ ἀπυκρύπτοισι φάεννον εἶδοσ,<br>
 ὄπποτα πλήθοισα μάλιστα λάμπησ<br>
 ἀργυρια γᾶν. <font size="1"><a href="xlatall.htm#8">[transcription]</a></font>
 </p>
 <p>The stars about the full moon lose their bright beauty when she, almost full, illumines all earth with silver.</p>
 <p> </p>
 <p>The gleaming stars all about the shining moon<br>
 Hide their bright faces, when full-orbed and splendid<br>
 In the sky she floats, flooding the shadowed earth<br>
      with clear silver light.</p>
 <p>Quoted by Eustathius of Thessalonica in the twelfth century.</p>
