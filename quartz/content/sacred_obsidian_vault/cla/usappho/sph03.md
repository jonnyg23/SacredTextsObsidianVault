 <h1 align="CENTER"><a name="BM2">2</a></h1>
 <p>φάινεταί μοι κῆνοσ ἴσοσ τηέοισιν<br>
 ἔμμεν ὤνερ ὄστισ ἐναντίοσ τοι<br>
 ἰζάνει καὶ πλασίον ἀδυ<br>
      φωνεύσασ ὐπακούει</p>
 <p>καὶ γαλαίσασ ἰμμερόεν τὸ δὴ ᾽μάν<br>
 καρδίαν ἐν στήθεσιν ἐπτόασεν,<br>
 ὠσ γὰρ εὔιδον βροχέωσ σε, φώνασ<br>
      οὐδὲν ἔτ᾽ ἔικει,</p>
 <p>ἀλλὰ κάμ μὲν γλῳσσα ϝέαγε, λέπτον<br>
 δ᾽ αὔτικα χρῷ πῦρ ὐπαδεδρόμακεν,<br>
 ὀππάτεσσι δ᾽ οὐδὲν ορημ᾽,<br>
      ἐπιρρόμβεισι δ᾽ ἄκουαι.</p>
 <p>ἀ δέ μ᾽ ί᾽δρωσ κακχέεται, τρόμοσ δὲ<br>
 παῖσαν ἄγρει χλωροτέρα δὲ ποίασ<br>
 ἔμμι, τεθνάκην δ᾽ ὀλιγω ᾽πιδεύϝην<br>
      φαίνομαι [ἄλλα].</p>
 <p>πᾶν τόλματον [......] <font size="1"><a href="xlatall.htm#1">[transcription]</a></font>
 </p>
 <p>That one seems to me the equal of the gods, who sits in thy presence and hears near him thy sweet voice and lovely laughter; that indeed makes my heart beat fast in my bosom. For when I see thee even a little I am bereft of utterance, my tongue is useless and at once a subtle fire races under my skin, my eyes see nothing, my ears ring, sweat pours forth and all my body is seized with trembling. I am paler than [dried] grass and seem in my madness little better than dead, but I must dare all ...</p>
 <p align="CENTER"> </p>
 <p align="CENTER">I</p>
 <p>Peer of the gods, the happiest man I seem<br>
 Sitting before thee, rapt at thy sight, hearing<br>
 Thy soft laughter and they voice most gentle,<br>
      Speaking so sweetly.</p>
 <p align="CENTER">II</p>
 <p>Then in my bosom my heart wildly flutters,<br>
 And, when on thee I gaze never so little,<br>
 Bereft am I of all power of utterance,<br>
      My tongue is useless.</p>
 <p align="CENTER">III</p>
 <p>There rushes at once through my flesh tingling fire,<br>
 My eyes are deprived of all power of vision,<br>
 My ears hear nothing by sounds of winds roaring,<br>
      And all is blackness.</p>
 <p align="CENTER">III</p>
 <p>Down courses in streams the sweat of emotion,<br>
 A dread trembling o'erwhelms me, paler than I<br>
 Than dried grass in autumn, and in my madness<br>
      Dead I seem almost.</p>
