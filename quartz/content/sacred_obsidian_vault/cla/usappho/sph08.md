 <h1 align="CENTER"><a name="BM7">7</a></h1>
 <p>Ἤ σε κύπροσ καὶ Πάφοσ ἢ Πάνορμοσ <font size="1"><a href="xlatall.htm#11">[transcription]</a></font>
 </p>
 <p>If thee, Cyprus or Paphos or Panormos [holds].</p>
 <p>This is from Strabo, early first century A.D. Panormos was a frequent name, and does not refer to Palermo, which was not founded in Sappho's time.</p>
