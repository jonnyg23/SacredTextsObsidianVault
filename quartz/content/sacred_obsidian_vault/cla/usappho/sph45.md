 <h1 align="CENTER">44</h1>
 <p>Γέλλωσ παιδοφιλωτέρα. <font size="1"><a href="xlatall.htm#65">[transcription]</a></font>
 </p>
 <p>More fond of children than Gello.</p>
 <p>Zenobius, about A.D. 130, quotes this as a proverb. The ghost of Gello was said by the Lesbians to pursue and carry off young children.</p>
