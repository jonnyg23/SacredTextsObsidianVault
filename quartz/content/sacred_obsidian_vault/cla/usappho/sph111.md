 <h1 align="CENTER">110</h1>
 <p>Ἠμιτύβιον σταλάσσον. <font size="1"><a href="xlatall.htm#141">[transcription]</a></font>
 </p>
 <p>A napkin dripping.</p>
 <p>From the Scholiast on the Plutus of Aristophanes to show the meaning of ἠμιτύβιον <font size="1"><a href="xlatall.htm#142">[transcription]</a></font>
 . This was a piece of soft linen for wiping the hands.</p>
