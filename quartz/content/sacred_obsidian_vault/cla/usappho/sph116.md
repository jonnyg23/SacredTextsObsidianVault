 <h1 align="CENTER"><a name="BM115">115</a></h1>
 <p>Πόλυ πάκιδοσ ᾽αδυμελεστέρα, χρύσω χρυσοτέρα. <font size="1"><a href="xlatall.htm#147">[transcription]</a></font>
 </p>
 <p>Than the lyre, far sweeter in tone, than gold, more golden.</p>
 <p> </p>
 <p>Far sweeter than the throbbing lyre in sound,<br>
 A voice more golden than gold, new found.</p>
 <p>Quoted by Demetrius to show the poetical value of hyperbolical phrase.</p>
