 <h1 align="CENTER"><a name="BM40">40</a></h1>
 <p>Ἕροσ δαὖτ᾽ ὲτίναξεν ἔμοι φρένασ,<br>
 ἄνεμοσ κατ ὄροσ δρύσιν ἐμπέσων. <font size="1"><a href="xlatall.htm#60">[transcription]</a></font>
 </p>
 <p>Now Eros shakes my soul, a wind on the mountain overwhelming the oaks.</p>
 <p> </p>
 <p>Now like a mountain wind the oaks o'erwhelming,<br>
 Eros shakes my soul.</p>
 <p>Quoted by Maximus Tyrius about 150 B.C. He speaks of Socrates exciting Phaedus to madness, when he speaks of love.</p>
