 <h4 align="CENTER">Section 9</h4>
 
 <p>9. But is there not something to be said for the memory of the
 various forms of beauty?
 
 </p><p>That is the resource of a man whose life is without beauty in
 the present, so that, for lack of it now, he grasps at the memory of
 what has been.
