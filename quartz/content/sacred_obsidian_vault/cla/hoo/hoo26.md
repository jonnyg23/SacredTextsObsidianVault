 <h6 align="CENTER">XXI.</h6>
 <h3 align="CENTER">TO THE SEA, OR TETHYS <a name="fr_79"></a><a href="#fn_79"><font size="1">*</font></a>.</h3>
 <h6 align="CENTER">The FUMIGATION from FRANKINCENSE and MANNA.</h6>
 <p>TETHYS I call, with eyes cærulean bright,<br>
 Hid in a veil obscure from human sight;<br>
 Great Ocean's empress, wand'ring thro' the deep,<br>
 And pleas'd with gentle gales, the earth to sweep;</p>
 <p><a name="page_147"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 147</font></a></p>
 <p>Whose blessed waves in swift succession go, 5<br>
 And lash the rocky shore with endless flow:<br>
 Delighting in the Sea serene to play,<br>
 In ships exulting and the wat'ry way.<br>
 Mother of Venus, and of clouds obscure,<br>
 Great nurse of beasts, and source of fountains pure. 10<br>
 O venerable Goddess, hear my pray'r,<br>
 And make benevolent my life thy care;<br>
 Send, blessed queen, to ships a prosp'rous breeze,<br>
 And waft them safely o'er the stormy seas.</p>
