 <h6 align="CENTER">XL.</h6>
 <h3 align="CENTER">TO THE CERALIAN MOTHER.</h3>
 <h6 align="CENTER">The FUMIGATION from AROMATICS.</h6>
 <p>CERALIAN queen, of celebrated name,<br>
 From whom both men, and Gods immortal came;<br>
 Who widely wand'ring once, oppress'd with grief,<br>
 In Eleusina's valley found'st relief,</p>
 <p><a name="page_173"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 173</font></a></p>
 <p>Discovering Proserpine thy daughter pure 5<br>
 In dread Avernus, dismal and obscure;<br>
 A sacred youth while thro' the world you stray<br>
 Bacchus, attending leader of the way;<br>
 The holy marriage of terrestrial Jove<br>
 Relating, while oppress'd with grief you rove; 10<br>
 Come, much invok'd, and to these rites inclin'd,<br>
 Thy mystic suppliant bless, with fav'ring mind.</p>
