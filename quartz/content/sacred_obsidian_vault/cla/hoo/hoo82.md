 <h6 align="CENTER">LXXVII.</h6>
 <h3 align="CENTER">TO AURORA.</h3>
 <h6 align="CENTER">The FUMIGATION from MANNA.</h6>
 <p>HEAR me, O Goddess! whose emerging ray<br>
 Leads on the broad refulgence of the day;<br>
 Blushing Aurora, whose celestial light<br>
 Beams on the world with red'ning splendours bright:<br>
 Angel of Titan, whom with constant round, 5<br>
 Thy orient beams recall from night profound:<br>
 Labour of ev'ry kind to lead is thine,<br>
 Of mortal life the minister divine.<br>
 Mankind in thee eternally delight,<br>
 And none presumes to shun thy beauteous sight. 10<br>
 Soon as thy splendours break the bands of rest,<br>
 And eyes unclose with pleasing sleep oppress'd;<br>
 Men, reptiles, birds, and beasts, with gen'ral voice,<br>
 And all the nations of the deep, rejoice;<br>
 For all the culture of our life is thine. 15<br>
 Come, blessed pow'r! and to these rites incline:<br>
 Thy holy light increase, and unconfin'd<br>
 Diffuse its radiance on thy mystic's mind.</p>
