 <h6 align="CENTER">XXIX.</h6>
 <h3 align="CENTER">TO BACCHUS.</h3>
 <h6 align="CENTER">The FUMIGATION from STORAX.</h6>
 <p>BACCHUS I call, loud-sounding and divine,<br>
 Fanatic God, a two-fold shape is thine:<br>
 Thy various names and attributes I sing,<br>
 O, first-born, thrice begotten, Bacchic king:  <a name="fr_86"></a><a href="#fn_86"><font size="1">4</font></a><br>
 Rural, ineffable, two-form'd, obscure, 5<br>
 Two-horn'd, with ivy crown'd, <b>euion</b>, pure.<br>
 Bull-fac'd, and martial, bearer of the vine,<br>
 Endu'd with counsel prudent and divine:<br>
 Triennial, whom the leaves of vines adorn,<br>
 Of Jove and Proserpine, occultly born. 10</p>
 <p><a name="page_156"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 156</font></a></p>
 <p>Immortal dæmon, hear my suppliant voice,<br>
 Give me in blameless plenty to rejoice;<br>
 And listen gracious to my mystic pray'r,<br>
 Surrounded with thy choir of nurses fair.</p>
