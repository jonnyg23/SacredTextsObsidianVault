 <h6 align="CENTER">LXX.</h6>
 <h3 align="CENTER">TO MELINOE.</h3>
 <h6 align="CENTER">The FUMIGATION from AROMATICS.</h6>
 <p>Call Melinoe, saffron-veil'd, terrene,<br>
 Who from infernal Pluto's sacred queen,<br>
 Mixt with Saturnian Jupiter, arose,<br>
 Near where Cocytus' mournful river flows;<br>
 When under Pluto's semblance, Jove divine 5<br>
 Deceiv'd with guileful arts dark Proserpine.<br>
 Hence, Partly black thy limbs and partly white,<br>
 From Pluto dark, from Jove etherial, bright<br>
 Thy colour'd members, men by night inspire<br>
 When seen in specter'd forms with terrors dire; 10<br>
 Now darkly visible, involv'd in night,<br>
 Perspicuous now they meet the fearful fight.<br>
 Terrestrial queen expel wherever found<br>
 The soul's mad fears to earth's remotest bound;<br>
 With holy aspect on our incense shrine, 15<br>
 And bless thy mystics, and the rites divine.</p>
