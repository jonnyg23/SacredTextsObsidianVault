 <h6 align="CENTER">XXXIV.</h6>
 <h3 align="CENTER">T O L A T O N A.</h3>
 <h6 align="CENTER">The FUMIGATION from MYRRH.</h6>
 <p>DARK veil'd Latona, much invoked queen,<br>
 Twin-bearing Goddess, of a noble mien;<br>
 Cæantis great, a mighty mind is thine,<br>
 Offspring prolific, blest of Jove divine:<br>
 Phbus proceeds from thee, the God of light, 5<br>
 And Dian fair, whom winged darts delight;<br>
 She in Ortygia's honor'd regions born,<br>
 In Delos he, which mountains high adorn.<br>
 Hear me, O Goddess, with propitious mind,<br>
 And end these holy rites, with aspect kind. 10</p>
