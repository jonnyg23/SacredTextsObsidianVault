 <h6 align="CENTER">XLVIII.</h6>
 <h3 align="CENTER">TO IPPA  <a name="fr_109"></a><a href="#fn_109"><font size="1">*</font></a></h3>
 <h6 align="CENTER">The FUMIGATION from STORAX.</h6>
 <p>Great nurse of Bacchus, to my pray'r incline,<br>
 For holy Sabus' secret rites are thine,<br>
 The mystic rites of Bacchus' nightly choirs,<br>
 Compos'd of sacred, loud-resounding fires:<br>
 Hear me, terrestrial mother, mighty queen, 5<br>
 Whether on Phyrgia's holy mountain  <a name="fr_110"></a><a href="#fn_110"><font size="1">+</font></a> seen,<br>
 Or if to dwell in Tmolus thee delights,<br>
 With holy aspect come, and bless these rites.</p>
