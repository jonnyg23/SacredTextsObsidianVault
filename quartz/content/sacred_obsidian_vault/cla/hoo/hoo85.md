 <h6 align="CENTER">LXXX.</h6>
 <h3 align="CENTER">TO THE WEST WIND.</h3>
 <h6 align="CENTER">The FUMIGATION from FRANKINCENSE.</h6>
 <p>SEA-born, aerial, blowing from the west,<br>
 Sweet gales, who give to weary'd labour rest:<br>
 Vernal and grassy, and of gentle found,<br>
 To ships delightful, thro' the sea profound;<br>
 For these, impell'd by you with gentle force, 5<br>
 Pursue with prosp'rous Fate their destin'd course.<br>
 With blameless gales regard my suppliant pray'r,<br>
 Zephyrs unseen, light-wing'd, and form'd from air.</p>
