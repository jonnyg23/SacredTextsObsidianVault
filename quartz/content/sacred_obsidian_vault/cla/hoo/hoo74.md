 <h6 align="CENTER">LXIX.</h6>
 <h3 align="CENTER">TO THE FURIES.</h3>
 <h6 align="CENTER">The FUMIGATION from AROMATICS.</h6>
 <p>HEAR me, illustrious Furies, mighty nam'd,<br>
 Terrific pow'rs, for prudent counsel fam'd;<br>
 Holy and pure, from Jove terrestrial born<br>
 And Proserpine, whom lovely locks adorn:<br>
 Whose piercing sight, with vision unconfin'd, 5<br>
 Surveys the deeds of all the impious kind:<br>
 On Fate attendant, punishing the race<br>
 (With wrath severe) of deeds unjust and base.<br>
 Dark-colour'd queens, whose glittering eyes, are bright<br>
 With dreadful, radiant, life-destroying, light: 10<br>
 Eternal rulers, terrible and strong,<br>
 To whom revenge, and tortures dire belong;<br>
 Fatal and horrid to the human sight,<br>
 With snaky tresses wand'ring in the night;<br>
 Either approach, and in these rites rejoice, 15<br>
 For ye, I call, with holy, suppliant voice.</p>
