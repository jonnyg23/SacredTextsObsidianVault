 <h6 align="CENTER">XLIX.</h6>
 <h3 align="CENTER">TO LYSIUS LENÆUS.</h3>
 <h6 align="CENTER">A HYMN.</h6>
 <p>Hear me, Jove's son, blest Bacchus, God of wine,<br>
 Born of two mothers, honor'd and divine;<br>
 Lysian, Euion  <a name="fr_111"></a><a href="#fn_111"><font size="1">*</font></a> Bacchus, various-nam'd,<br>
 Of Gods the offspring secret, holy, fam'd:<br>
 Fertile and nourishing whose liberal care 5<br>
 Earth's fruits increases, flourishing and fair;<br>
 Sounding, magnanimous, Lenæan pow'r<br>
 O various form'd, medic'nal, holy flow'r:<br>
 Mortals in thee, repose from labour find,<br>
 Delightful charm, desir'd by all mankind: 10<br>
 Fair-hair'd Euion, Bromian, joyful God,<br>
 Lysian, invested with the leafy rod.<br>
 To these our rites, benignant pow'r incline,<br>
 When fav'ring men, or when on Gods you shine;<br>
 Be present to thy mystic's suppliant pray'r, 15<br>
 Rejoicing come, and fruits abundant bear.</p>
