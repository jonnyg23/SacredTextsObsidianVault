 <h6 align="CENTER">XVI.</h6>
 <h3 align="CENTER">TO NEPTUNE.</h3>
 <h6 align="CENTER">The FUMIGATION from MYRRH</h6>
 <p>HEAR, Neptune, ruler of the sea profound,<br>
 Whose liquid grasp begirts the solid ground;<br>
 Who, at the bottom of the stormy main,<br>
 Dark and deep-bosom'd, hold'st thy wat'ry reign;<br>
 Thy awful hand the brazen trident bears,<br>
 And ocean's utmost bound, thy will reveres:<br>
 Thee I invoke, whose steeds the foam divide,<br>
 From whose dark locks the briny waters glide;<br>
 Whose voice loud founding thro' the roaring deep,<br>
 Drives all its billows, in a raging heap; 10<br>
 When fiercely riding thro' the boiling sea,<br>
 Thy hoarse command the trembling waves obey.</p>
 <p><a name="page_142"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 142</font></a></p>
 <p>Earth shaking, dark-hair'd God, the liquid plains<br>
 (The third division) Fate to thee ordains,<br>
 'Tis thine, cærulian dæmon, to survey<br>
 Well pleas'd the monsters of the ocean play, 15<br>
 Confirm earth's basis, and with prosp'rous gales<br>
 Waft ships along, and swell the spacious sails;<br>
 Add gentle Peace, and fair-hair'd Health beside,<br>
 And pour abundance in a blameless tide. 20</p>
