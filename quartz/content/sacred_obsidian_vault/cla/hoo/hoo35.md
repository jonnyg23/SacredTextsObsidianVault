 <h6 align="CENTER">XXX.</h6>
 <h3 align="CENTER">TO THE CURETES.</h3>
 <h6 align="CENTER">A HYMN.</h6>
 <p>LEAPING Curetes, who with dancing feet<br>
 And circling measures, armed footsteps beat:<br>
 Whose bosom's mad, fanatic transports fire,<br>
 Who move in rythm to the founding lyre:<br>
 Who traces deaf when lightly leaping tread,<br>
 Arm bearers, strong defenders, rulers dread:<br>
 Propitious omens, guards of Proserpine <a name="fr_87"></a><a href="#fn_87"><font size="1">*</font></a>,<br>
 Preserving rites, mysterious and divine<br>
 Come, and benevolent my words attend,<br>
 (In herds rejoicing), and my life defend. 10</p>
