 <h6 align="CENTER">LXVIII.</h6>
 <h3 align="CENTER">TO THE FURIES <a name="fr_120"></a><a href="#fn_120"><font size="1">*</font></a></h3>
 <h6 align="CENTER">The FUMIGATION from AROMATICS.</h6>
 <p>VOCIFEROUS Bacchanalian Furies, hear!<br>
 Ye, I invoke, dread pow'rs, whom all revere;<br>
 Nightly, profound, in secret who retire,<br>
 Tisiphone, Alecto, and Megara dire:<br>
 Deep in a cavern merg'd, involv'd in night, <a href="errata.htm#12">5</a><br>
 Near where Styx flows impervious to the sight;<br>
 Ever attendant on mysterious rites,<br>
 Furious and fierce, whom Fate's dread law delights;<br>
 Revenge and sorrows dire to you belong,<br>
 Hid in a savage veil, severe and strong, 10<br>
 Terrific virgins, who forever dwell<br>
 Endu'd with various forms, in deepest hell;<br>
 Aerial, and unseen by human kind,<br>
 And swiftly coursing, rapid as the mind.<br>
 In vain the Sun with wing'd refulgence bright, 15<br>
 In vain the Moon, far darting milder light,<br>
 Wisdom and Virtue may attempt in vain;<br>
 And pleasing, Art, our transport to obtain<br>
 Unless with these you readily conspire,<br>
 And far avert your all-destructive ire. 20</p>
 <p><a name="page_201"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 201</font></a></p>
 <p>The boundless tribes of mortals you descry,<br>
 And justly rule with Right's impartial eye.<br>
 Come, snaky-hair'd, Fates many-form'd, divine,<br>
 Suppress your rage, and to our rites incline.</p>
