 <h6 align="CENTER">LXXVIII.</h6>
 <h3 align="CENTER">TO THEMIS.</h3>
 <h6 align="CENTER">The FUMIGATION from FRANKINCENSE.</h6>
 <p>ILLUSTRIOUS Themis, of celestial birth,<br>
 Thee I invoke, young blossom of the earth;  <a name="fr_125"></a><a href="#fn_125"><font size="1">2</font></a><br>
 Beauteous-eyed virgin; first from thee alone,<br>
 Prophetic oracles to men were known,<br>
 Giv'n from the deep recesses of the fane 5<br>
 In sacred Pytho, where renown'd you reign;<br>
 From thee, Apollo's oracles arose,<br>
 And from thy pow'r his inspiration flows.<br>
 Honour'd by all, of form divinely bright,<br>
 Majestic virgin, wand'ring in the night: 10<br>
 Mankind from thee first learnt initial rites,<br>
 And Bacchus' nightly choirs thy soul delights;<br>
 For holy honours to disclose is thine,<br>
 With all the culture of the pow'rs divine.<br>
 Be present, Goddess, to my pray'r inclin'd,<br>
 And bless the mystic rites with fav'ring mind.</p>
