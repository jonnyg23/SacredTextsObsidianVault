 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Bôlus</span> of <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Mendê</span> in the Nile Delta, lived in the third century B.C.</p>  
 <p>He wrote works on <i>Potent Natural Products</i>, and on <i>Antipathetic and Sympathetic Substances</i>, which were often ascribed to Democritus; these were books of magic remedies. He also wrote other works of magical doctrine, on alchemy and divination.</p>
