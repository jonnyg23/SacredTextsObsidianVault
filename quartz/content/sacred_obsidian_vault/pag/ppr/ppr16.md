   <h3 align="center">HYMN TO AMUN-RA</h3>  
 <p align="center">(<i>Egyptian</i>)</p>  
 <p></p><dir>HAIL to thee, Amun-Ra, Lord of the thrones of the earth, the oldest existence, ancient of heaven, support of all things;<br> Chief of the gods, lord of truth; father of the gods, maker of men and beasts and herbs; maker of all things above and below;<br> Deliverer of the sufferer and oppressed, judging the poor;<br> Lord of wisdom, lord of mercy; most loving, opener of every eye, source of joy, in whose goodness the gods rejoice, thou whose name is hidden.<br> Thou art the one, maker of all that is, the one; the only one; maker of gods and men; giving food to all.<br> Hail to thee, thou one with many heads; sleepless when all others sleep, adoration to thee.<br> Hail to thee from all creatures from every land, from the height of heaven, from the depth of the sea.<br> The spirits thou hast made extol thee, saying, welcome to thee, father of the fathers of the gods; we worship thy spirit which is in us.</dir>
