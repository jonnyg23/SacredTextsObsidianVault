   <h1 align="center">PAGAN PRAYERS</h1>  <h3 align="center">ON THE WRITING OF A PRAYER OF PRAISE</h3>  
 <p align="center">(<i>Mexican</i>)</p>  
 <p><font size="-1">(This introduction to a volume of annals, written centuries ago by an unknown poet of ancient America, gives glimpse of the beauty of the native book craft, and the sacredness to them, of literature)</font></p>  
 <p align="center"><a name="img_aztec"></a>
 <img src="img/aztec.jpg"></p>  
 <p></p><dir>LIKE a red-winged heron of wonder, rising in flight, it shone.<br> The mist and the glow of the rainbow, it is there!<br> The harmony is as the tinkling turquoise bells on the silver drum: thus was a book of annals written and painted in colors.<br> I unwind my song!<br> I unwind my song like a string of jewels, all precious.</dir>
