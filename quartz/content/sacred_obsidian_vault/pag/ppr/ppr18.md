   <h3 align="center">HYMN TO PANU</h3>  
 <p align="center">(<i>Finnish</i>)</p>  
 <p align="center"><font size="-1">(Panu, God of fire, child of the Sun-mother)</font></p>  
 <p></p><dir>O PANU, Son of the Sun!<br> Offspring thou of the dear day.<br> Lift the fire up to the sky.<br> In the middle of the golden ring,<br> Within the rock of copper,<br> Carry it as a child to its mother<br> Into the lap of the ancient mother.<br> Place the fire to shine by day,<br> And to rest at night.<br> Let it rise every morning!<br> Let it rest every evening!</dir>
