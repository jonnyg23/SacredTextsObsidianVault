   <h3 align="center">HYMN OF THE GOD OF FLOWERS</h3>  
 <p align="center">(<i>Mexican</i>)</p>  
 <p align="center"><font size="-1">(High Priest, masked as the god, chants)<br> (Kin-teotl--God of maize)<br> (Tla-loc--God of storms and the wide plains)</font></p>  
 <p></p><dir>O FRIENDS! The quetzal bird sings--it sings its song at midnight to Kin-teotl.<br> The god now hears my song by night, he will hear my song as the night brings in the dawn.<br> I send forth the priests to the house of Tla-loc.<br> The priests to the house of Tla-loc do I send forth.<br> I shall go forth to the plains. I shall join myself to them. I shall go where is Kin-teotl. I shall follow the trail to him.<br> The priests go forth to the house of Tla-loc, to the home of the god of the plains!</dir>
