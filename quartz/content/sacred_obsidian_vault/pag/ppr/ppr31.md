   <h3 align="center">PRAYER TO THE GOD OF THIEVES</h3>  
 <p align="center">(<i>South Pacific Island</i>)</p>  
 <p></p><dir>O THOU divine Outre-reter!<br> We go out for plunder.<br> Cause all things to sleep in the house.<br> Owner of the house, sleep on!<br> Threshold of the house, sleep on!<br> Little insects of the house, sleep on!<br> Central-post, ridge-pole, rafters, thatch of the house, sleep on!<br> O Rongo, grant us success!</dir>
