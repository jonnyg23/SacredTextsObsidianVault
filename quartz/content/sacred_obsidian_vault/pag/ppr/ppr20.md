   <h3 align="center">PRAYER TO BUDDHA</h3>  
 <p align="center">(<a href="errata.htm#1"><i>Buddhist</i></a>)</p>  
 <p></p><dir>THOU in whom innumerable creatures believe!<br> Thou, Buddha, Victor over the hosts of evil!<br> Thou, all-wise Being, come down to our world!<br> Made perfect and glorified by in-numerable by-gone revolutions; always pitiful, always gracious toward all creatures!<br> Look down upon us; for the time has come to pour out blessings on all creatures.<br> Be gracious to us from thy throne built in thy heavenly world.<br> Thou art the eternal redemption of all creatures, therefore bow down to us with all thy unstained heavenly societies.</dir>
