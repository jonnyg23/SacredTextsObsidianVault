 <p></p><h3 align="CENTER">III.</h3>
 <p>
 </p><h3 align="CENTER">POLEMICS.--SPECULATION.</h3>
 <p>
 <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"> Against Speculators (1205. 1206).</font></span>Oh! speculators on things, boast not of knowing the things that
 nature ordinarily brings about; but rejoice if you know the end of
 those things which you yourself devise.
