 <h3 align="CENTER">310.</h3>
 <p><a name="page_170"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 170</font></a></p>
 <p></p><p>
 <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"> Proportions of the head and face (310-318).</font></span>The space between the parting of the lips [the mouth] and the base
 of the nose is one-seventh of the face.
 </p><p>
 The space from the mouth to the bottom of the chin <i>c d</i> is the
 fourth part of the face and equal to the width of the mouth.
 </p><p>
 The space from the chin to the base of the nose <i>e f</i> is the third
 part of the face and equal to the length of the nose and to the
 forehead.
 </p><p>
 The distance from the middle of the nose to the bottom of the chin
 <i>g h</i>, is half the length of the face.
 </p><p>
 The distance from the top of the nose, where the eyebrows begin, to
 the bottom of the chin, <i>i k</i>, is two thirds of the face.
 </p><p>
 The space from the parting of the lips to the top of the chin <i>l m</i>,
 that is where the chin ends and passes into the lower lip of the
 mouth, is the third of the distance from the parting of the lips to
 the bottom of the chin and is the twelfth part of the face. From the
 top to the bottom of the chin <i>m n</i> is the sixth part of the face
 and is the fifty fourth part of a man's height.
 </p><p>
 From the farthest projection of the chin to the throat <i>o p</i> is
 equal to the space between the mouth and the bottom of the chin, and
 a fourth of the face.
 </p><p>
 The distance from the top of the throat to the pit of the throat
 below <i>q r</i> is half the length of the face and the eighteenth part
 of a man's height.
 </p><p>
 From the chin to the back of the neck <i>s t</i>, is the same distance as
 between the mouth and the roots of the hair, that is three quarters
 of the head.
 </p><p>
 From the chin to the jaw bone <i>v x</i> is half the head and equal to
 the thickness of the neck in profile.
 </p><p>
 The thickness of the head from the brow to the nape is once and 3/4
 that of the neck.
 </p><p>
