 <h3 align="CENTER">937.</h3>
 <p>
 </p><h3 align="CENTER">WHETHER THE EARTH IS LESS THAN THE WATER.</h3>
 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_v218300"></a>
 <img src="img/v218300.jpg"></p>
 <p>
 <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"> Of the proportion of the mass of water to that of the earth (937. 938).</font></span>Some assert that it is true that the earth, which is not covered by
 water is much less than that covered by water. But considering the
 size of 7000 miles in diameter which is that of this earth, we may
 conclude the water to be of small depth.
