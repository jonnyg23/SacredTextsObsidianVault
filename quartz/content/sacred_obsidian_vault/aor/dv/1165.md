 <h3 align="CENTER">1165.</h3>
 <p>
 <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"> How to spend life (1165-1170).</font></span>0 sleepers! what a thing is slumber! Sleep resembles death. Ah, why
 then dost thou not work in such wise as that after death thou mayst
 retain a resemblance to perfect life, when, during life, thou art in
 sleep so like to the hapless dead?  <a name="fr_302"></a><a href="#fn_302"><font size="1">636</font></a>
