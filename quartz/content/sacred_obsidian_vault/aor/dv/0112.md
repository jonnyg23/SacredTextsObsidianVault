 <h3 align="CENTER">112.</h3>
 <p>
 <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"> Different principles and plans of treatment (112--116).</font></span>First I will treat of light falling through windows which I will
 call Restricted [Light] and then I will treat of light in the open
 country, to which I will give the name of diffused Light. Then I
 will treat of the light of luminous bodies.
