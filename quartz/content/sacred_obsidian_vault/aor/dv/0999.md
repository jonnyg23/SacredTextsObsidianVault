 <p><a name="fn_197"></a><a href="0999.htm#fr_197">220:531</a> : In connection
 with this text I may here mention a hygrometer, drawn and probably
 invented by Leonardo. A facsimile of this is given in Vol. I, p. 297
 with the note: <i>'Modi di pesare l'arie eddi sapere quando s'a
 arrompere il tepo'</i> (Mode of weighing the air and of knowing when
 the weather will change); by the sponge <i>"Spugnea"</i> is written.</p>
