 </p><h3 align="CENTER">OF THE VIBRATION OF THE EARTH.</h3>
 <p>
 <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"> On the alterations in the channels of rivers.</font></span>The subterranean channels of waters, like those which exist between
 the air and the earth, are those which unceasingly wear away and
 deepen the beds of their currents.
