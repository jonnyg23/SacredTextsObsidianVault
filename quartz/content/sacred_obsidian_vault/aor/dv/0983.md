 <h3 align="CENTER">983.</h3>
 <p>
 </p><h3 align="CENTER">OF THE SEA WHICH ENCIRCLES THE EARTH.</h3>
 <p>
 I find that of old, the state of the earth was that its plains were
 all covered up and hidden by salt water.  <a name="fr_185"></a><a href="#fn_185"><font size="1">519</font></a>
