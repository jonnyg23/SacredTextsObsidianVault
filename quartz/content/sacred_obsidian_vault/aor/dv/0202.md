 <h3 align="CENTER">202.</h3>
 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_v111201"></a>
 <img src="img/v111201.jpg"></p>
 <p>
 The shadow cast by an object on a plane will be smaller in
 proportion as that object is lighted by feebler rays. Let <i>d e</i> be
 the object and <i>d c</i> the plane surface; the number of times that <i>d
 e</i> will go into <i>f g</i> gives the proportion of light at <i>f h</i> to <i>d
 c</i>. The ray of light will be weaker in proportion to its distance
 from the hole through which it falls.
