 <h3 align="CENTER">335.</h3>
 <p>
 <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"> The relative proportions of the torso and of the leg (335. 336).</font></span><i>a b c</i> are equal to each other and to the space from the armpit of
 the shoulder to the genitals and to the distance from the tip of the
 fingers of the hand to the joint of the arm, and to the half of the
 breast; and you must know that <i>c b</i> is the third part of the height
 of a man from the shoulders to the ground; <i>d e f</i> are equal to each
 other and equal to the greatest width of the shoulders.
 </p><p>
