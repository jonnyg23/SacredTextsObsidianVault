 <h3 align="CENTER">141.</h3>
 <p>
 <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"> Light and shadow with regard to the position of the eye (141--145).</font></span>Every shaded body that is larger than the pupil and that interposes
 between the luminous body and the eye will be seen dark.
 </p><p>
 When the eye is placed between the luminous body and the objects
 illuminated by it, these objects will be seen without any shadow.
 </p><p>
