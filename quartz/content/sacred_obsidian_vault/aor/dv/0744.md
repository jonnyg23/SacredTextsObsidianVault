 <h3 align="CENTER">744.</h3>
 <p>
 </p><h3 align="CENTER">ON MOVING HOUSES.</h3>
 <p>
 Let the houses be moved and arranged in order; and this will be done
 with facility
 </p><p><a name="page_29"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 29</font></a></p>
 <p>because such houses are at first made in pieces on the
 open places, and can then be fitted together with their timbers in
 the site where they are to be permanent.
 </p><p align="CENTER"><a name="img_v202900"></a>
 </p><p align="CENTER">
 <a href="img/v202900.jpg"><img src="tn/v202900.jpg"><br><font size="-3">Click to enlarge</font></a></p>
 <p>
 [9] Let the men of the country [or the village] partly inhabit the
 new houses when the court is absent [12].
 </p><p>
