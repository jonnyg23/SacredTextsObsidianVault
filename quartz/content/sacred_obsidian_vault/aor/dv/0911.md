 </p><h3 align="CENTER">VI.</h3>
 <h3 align="CENTER">THE STARS.</h3>
 <p>
 <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"> On the light of the stars (911-913).</font></span>The stars are visible by night and not by day, because we are beneath
 the dense atmosphere, which is full of innumerable particles of moisture,
 each of which independently, when the rays of the sun fall upon it,
 reflects a radiance, and so these numberless bright particles conceal
 the stars; and if it were not for this atmosphere the sky would always
 display the stars against its darkness.
