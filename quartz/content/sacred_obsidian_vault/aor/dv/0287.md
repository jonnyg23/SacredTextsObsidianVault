 <h3 align="CENTER">287.</h3>
 <p>
 <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"> On the colours of the rainbow (287. 288).</font></span>Treat of the rainbow in the last book on Painting, but first write
 the book on colours produced by the mixture of other colours, so as
 to be able to prove by those painters' colours how the colours of
 the rainbow are produced.
