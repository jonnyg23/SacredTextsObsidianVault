 </p><h3 align="CENTER">A METHOD OF SEEING THE SUN ECLIPSED WITHOUT PAIN TO THE EYE.</h3>
 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_v215301"></a>
 <img src="img/v215301.jpg"></p>
 <p>

 at the sun through these holes.
