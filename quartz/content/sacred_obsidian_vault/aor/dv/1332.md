 <h3 align="CENTER">1332.</h3>
 <p>
 <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"> Irony (1332).</font></span>If Petrarch was so fond of bay, it was because it is of a good taste
 in sausages and with tunny; I cannot put any value on their foolery.
  <a name="fr_333"></a><a href="#fn_333"><font size="1">667</font></a>

 <h3 align="CENTER">Footnotes</h3>
