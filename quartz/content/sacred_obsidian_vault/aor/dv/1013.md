 <h3 align="CENTER">1013.</h3>
 <p>
 If it be not reported there that this is to be a public canal, it
 will be necessary to pay for the land;  <a name="fr_208"></a><a href="#fn_208"><font size="1">542</font></a>
  and the king will pay it by
 remitting the taxes for a year.
 </p><p>

 <h3 align="CENTER">Footnotes</h3>
 <p><a name="fn_208"></a><a href="1013.htm#fr_208">232:542</a>  3: <i>il re</i>. Louis
 XII or Francis I of France. It is hardly possible to doubt that the
 canals here spoken of were intended to be in the Milanese. Compare
 with this passage the rough copy of a letter by Leonardo, to the
 <i>"Presidente dell' Ufficio regolatore dell' acqua"</i> on No. 1350. See
 also the note to No. 745, 1. 12.</p>
