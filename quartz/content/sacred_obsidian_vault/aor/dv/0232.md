 <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"> On indistinctness at great distances (232-234).</font></span>Objects near to the eye will appear larger than those at a distance.
 </p><p>
 Objects seen with two eyes will appear rounder than if they are seen
 with only one.
 </p><p>
 Objects seen between light and shadow will show the most relief.
