 <h3 align="CENTER">394.</h3>
 <p>
 <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"> The relative thickness of the branches to the trunk (393--396).</font></span>All the branches of a tree at every stage of its height when put
 together are equal in thickness to the trunk [below them].
 </p><p>
 All the branches of a water [course] at every stage of its course,
 if they are of equal rapidity, are equal to the body of the main
 stream.
