 <h3 align="CENTER">549.</h3>
 <p>
 </p><h3 align="CENTER">HOW THE PAINTER MUST PLACE HIMSELF WITH REFERENCE TO THE LIGHT, TO GIVE THE EFFECT OF RELIEF.</h3>
 <p>
 <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"> On the choice of light for a picture (549-554).</font></span>Let <i>a b</i> be the window, <i>m</i> the point of light. I say that on
 whichever side the painter places himself he will be well placed
 </p><p align="CENTER"><a name="img_v127700"></a>
 <img src="img/v127700.jpg"></p>
 if only his eye is between the shaded and the illuminated portions of
 the object he is drawing; and this place you will find by putting
 yourself between the point <i>m</i> and the division between the shadow
 and the light on the object to be drawn.
