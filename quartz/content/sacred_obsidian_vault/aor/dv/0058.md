 </p><h3 align="CENTER">PERSPECTIVE.</h3>
 <p>
 <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"> The Production of pyramid of Vision (58-60).</font></span>The instant the atmosphere is illuminated it will be filled with an
 infinite number of images which are produced by the various bodies
 and colours assembled in it. And the eye is the target, a loadstone,
 of these images.
