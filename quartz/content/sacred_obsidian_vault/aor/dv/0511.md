 <h3 align="CENTER">511.</h3>
 <p>
 The different kinds of light afforded in cellars by various forms of
 windows. The
 </p><p><a name="page_256"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 256</font></a></p>
 <p>least useful and the coldest is the window at <i>a</i>. The
 most useful, the lightest and warmest
 </p><p align="CENTER"><a name="img_v125600"></a>
 <img src="img/v125600.jpg"></p>
 <p>and most open to the sky is
 the window at <i>b</i>. The window at <i>c</i> is of medium utility.
