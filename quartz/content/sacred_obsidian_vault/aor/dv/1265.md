 </p><h3 align="CENTER">FABLES.</h3>
 <p>
 </p><h3 align="CENTER">A FABLE.</h3>
 <p>
 <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"> Fables on animals (1265-1270).</font></span>An oyster being turned out together with other fish in the house of
 a fisherman near the sea, he entreated a rat to take him to the sea.
 The rat purposing to eat him bid him open; but as he bit him the
 oyster squeezed his head and closed; and the cat came and killed
 him.
