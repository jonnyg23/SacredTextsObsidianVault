 <h3 align="CENTER">351.</h3>
 <p>
 When the arm is bent at an angle at the elbow, it will produce some
 angle; the more acute the angle is, the more will the muscles within
 the bend be shortened; while
 </p><p><a name="page_187"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 187</font></a></p>
 <p>the muscles outside will become of
 greater length than before. As is shown in the example; <i>d c e</i> will
 shrink considerably; and <i>b n</i> will be much extended.
 </p><p>
