 <p>
 This is the second volume of three of G.R.S. Mead's comprehensive survey
 of the literature attributed to the legendary Egyptian sage,
 Hermes Trismegistus.
 This volume includes translations of the <cite>Corpus Hermeticum</cite>,
 including the <cite>Sheperd Poemandres</cite>,
 <cite>To Asclepius</cite>,
 <cite>The Secret Sermon on the Mountain</cite>, and more.
 </p>
