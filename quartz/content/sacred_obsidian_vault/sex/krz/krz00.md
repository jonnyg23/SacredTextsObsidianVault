 <h1 align="center">THE KAREZZA METHOD</h1>
 <h2 align="center">or MAGNETATION:</h2>
 <h4 align="center">The Art of Connubial Love</h4>
 <h2 align="center">by J. William Lloyd</h2>
 <h3 align="center">The Lover is the Artist in Touch</h3>
 <h4 align="center">Karezza in the perfect form is natural marriage - that clinging, satisfied union of body and soul which true love ever craves and in which ideal marriage consists - and with every repetition of the act the lovers are remarried, their unity renewed, deepened, intensified.</h4>
