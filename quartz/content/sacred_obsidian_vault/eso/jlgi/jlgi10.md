 <p><a name="page__001"></a></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">SYNOPSIS OF</h3>  <h3 align="center">The Fourteen Lessons</h3>  
 <p>LESSON I. On the ThresholdThe Constitution of ManThe Seven Principles of ManThe Physical BodyThe Astral BodyPrana or Vital Force.</p>  
 <p>LESSON II. The Fourth and Fifth PrinciplesThe Instinctive MindThe Intellect.</p>  
 <p>LESSON III. The Sixth and Seventh PrinciplesThe Spiritual MindSpiritIllumination, or Spiritual Consciousness.</p>  
 <p>LESSON IV. The Human AuraHealth AuraPranic AuraAurathe three Mental PrinciplesSpirit AuraAuric Colors, Phenomena etc.</p>  
 <p>LESSON V. Thought DynamicsThe nature, quality, and power of ThoughtThought FormsThought InfluencesThe Occult Teachings on this great subject.</p>  
 <p>LESSON VI. Clairvoyance, Clairaudience, Psychometry, Telepathy, etc.How to develop Psychic powers.</p>  
 <p>LESSON VII. Human MagnetismPranic EnergyUses and PropertiesDirections for development and use.</p>  
 <p>LESSON VIII. Occult TherapeuticsSpiritual HealingMental HealingPranic HealingTheory and Practice.</p>  
 <p>LESSON IX. Psychic InfluencePersonal MagnetismMesmerism, etc., explained, with instructions regarding self-protection and warnings against misuse of the powerA timely lesson upon an important subject.</p>  
 <p>LESSON X. The Astral World; Its PhenomenaThe Astral BodyAstral Helpers, etc.</p>  
 <p>LESSON XI. Beyond the BorderThe survival of the Ego after passing out of the physical bodyWhere it goes and what it does after the change called Death.</p>  
 <p>LESSON XII. Spiritual EvolutionThe Growth of the SoulIts TravelsIts PurposeIts Goal.</p>  
 <p>LESSON XIII. Spiritual Cause and EffectThe Yogi teachings regarding the puzzling questions of Human Life, ConductThe Sowing and the Reaping explained.</p>  
 <p>LESSON XIV. The Yogi's Path of AttainmentThe Threefold PathMethodsDirectionsPlansExercises, etc.Advice and Words of Encouragement to the Neophyte.</p>
