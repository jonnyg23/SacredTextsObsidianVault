 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Comte de Gabalis</i> [1913], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">CASSIODORUS RENIUS OR REYNA,</h3>  <h3 align="center">1520-30 A.D.--1594 A.D.</h3>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_PP">PP</a></font></span>Was born at Seville in Spain. His chief work was pp a scholarly translation of the Old and New Testaments</p>  
 <p><a name="page_293"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 293</font></a></p>  
 <p>into Spanish. His defence of Magdalen of the Cross occurs in a rare treatise on the fourth chapter of St. Matthew, "de periculis piorum ministrorum verbi in tempore cavendis," published with his "Evangelium Joannis" in 1573 at Frankfurt, where he lived for some years prior to his death.</p>
