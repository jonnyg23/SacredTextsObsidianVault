 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Comte de Gabalis</i> [1913], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">THE ORACLE OF DODONA.</h3>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_T">T</a></font></span>"They went therefore in their consternation to consult the Oracle at Dodona; for those who dwell on</p>  
 <p><a name="page_240"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 240</font></a></p>  
 <p>this mainland, as the Aetolians and their neighbours the Acarnanians and Epirots, believe in the oracular responses they get from doves and the oak there. <a name="fr_176"></a><a href="#fn_176"><font size="1">*</font></a> "The Peleae ('doves') at Dodona, also prophesied by divine inspiration." PAUSANIAS, BOOK X, CHAPTER xii.</p>
