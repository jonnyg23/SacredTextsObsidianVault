 <img src="img/35100.jpg"></p>  <h3 align="center">"MY PORT PATERNAL IN THE COURTS OF LIGHT."</h3>  <h3 align="center">HYMN TO THE SOVEREIGN SUN.</h3>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">’Tis thine by heat apparent to exalt<br> Corporeal natures from the sluggish earth,<br> Inspiring vivid, vegetative power;<br> And by a nature secretly divine,<br> And from the base alloy of matter free,<br> Inherent in thy all-productive rays,<br> Thou draw’st to union with thy wond’rous form,<br> Exalted souls, that in dark Hyle’s realms<br> Indignant struggle for the courts of light:<br> All beauteous, seven-rayed, supermundane god! <a name="page_352"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 352</font></a><br> Whose mystic essence secretly emits<br> The splendid fountains of celestial light.  
 <p>All-bounteous god, by whom the soul is freed<br> From Generation’s dark corporeal bands,<br> Assist THY OFFSPRING borne on mental wings,<br> Beyond the reach of guileful Nature’s hands<br> Swift to ascend, and gain thy beauteous world.<br> The subtle vestment of my soul refine,<br> Etherial, firm, and full of sacred light,<br> Her ancient vehicle by thee assign’d<br> In which invelop’d, thro’ the starry orbs,<br> Urg’d by the impulse of insane desire,<br> She fail’d precipitate, till Lethe’s shore,<br> Involv’d in night, unhappily she touch’d,<br> And lost all knowledge of her pristine state.</p>  
 <p>’Tis thine, from multitude exempt, t’ inspire<br> In forms subordinate, prophetic truth;<br> For truth and pure simplicity are one:<br> And of preserving unpolluted power,<br> Thy <i>liberated essence</i> is the source."</p></div>  
 <p> </p>  
 <p align="center"><small>EXTRACTS FROM THE EMPEROR JULIAN'S ORATION TO THE SOVEREIGN SUN.</small></p>
