 <p><a name="page_25"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 25</font></a></p>  <h1 align="center">THE SCIENCE OF MIND</h1>  
 <p><a name="page_26"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 26</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">YOURSELF</h3>  
 <p></p><dir>Oh, weary heart, laden with earth's weight and care,<br> Oh, feet, stumbling on the way, bleeding and bare,<br> Oh, arms outstretched, and hands upheld in prayer,<br> Oh, back, which so oft has felt the lash and rod,<br> Oh, soul, which cries aloud for the living God,<br> Oh, life, struggling to free itself from the clod;<br> Know this: there is no power from without,<br> Yourself must answer every fear and meet all doubt<br> With some divine, indwelling power<br> Which you yourself, upon yourself, shall shower;<br> And giving, take, and taking, give<br> Unto that life which you, yourself, shall live.</dir>
