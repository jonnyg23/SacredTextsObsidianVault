 <p><a name="page_xiii"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. xiii</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">THE ASCENSION</h3>  
 <p align="center"><i>By</i> CHANERA</p>  
 <p></p><dir>I feel My God Flame touch my brow,<br>   The Breath of Love—eternal now,<br> I raise my eyes and lo, I see<br>   My own Great God Self over me.  
 <p>A dazzling cloud envelopes all,<br>   I hear My Real God "Presence" call,<br> I feel a surge of Love's great might,<br>   I enter deep its Breath—its Light.</p>  
 <p>I see within this Pulsing Flame,<br>   I listen, and hear my Secret Name,<br> I feel the glow—the Great Flame Breath,<br>   I am the Victor over death.</p>  
 <p>I stand forth free-Ascended now,<br>   To my heart's Light, all things do bow:<br> I am a Being of Cause alone<br>   And That Cause, Love—The Sacred Tone.</p>  
 <p>I pour out Life—I lift, I raise,<br>   My heart o’erflows and sings its praise,<br> My power strengthens and inspires,<br>   My Great Light Rays are God's Own Fires.</p>  
 <p>I am a Sun, My Love—Its Light<br>   All else grows dim—earth lost to sight;<br> I know I am just God—The One<br>   The Source—The Great, Great Central Sun.</p></dir>
