 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>Numbers, Their Occult Power and Mystic Virtues</i>, by W. Wynn Westcott, [1911], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  
 <p><a name="page_126"><font size="1" color="green">p. 126</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">THE APOCALYPTIC NUMBERS.</h3>  
 <p>1st Resurrection, Revelation xx. 5.</p>  
 <p>2nd Death, xx. 14. 2 Witnesses, xi. 3.</p>  
 <p>2 Olive Trees, 2 Candlesticks before throne of God of the Earth, xi. 4.</p>  
 <p>2-Horned Beast who spoke like a Dragon, xiii. 11; his number is 666.</p>  
 <p>3 Woes, ix. 12.</p>  
 <p>1/3 part of Vegetation killed, viii. 7-do. of Sea became Blood, and do. of Fish died, viii. 8-do. of Waters became bitter, viii. 11-do. of Sun, Moon, Stars, viii. 12.</p>  
 <p>3½ days, Bodies lay unburied, xxi. 9.</p>  
 <p>4 quarters of the Earth, xx. 8.</p>  
 <p>4 Beasts, full of eyes and have 6 wings, iv. 6–9 ("Beasts" should be <i>living beings</i>.—W.).</p>  
 <p>4 Horses, White, Red, Pale, Black.</p>  
 <p>4 Horns of the golden altar before God, ix. 13.</p>  
 <p>4 Angels of the Euphrates, ix. 14.</p>  
 <p>4 Angels of the Winds of the 4 corners of the Earth, vii. 1.</p>  
 <p>5 Months the Locusts had power to hurt Men, ix. 5–10.</p>  
 <p>6 Wings of the Beasts (living beings), full of eyes, iv. 8.</p>  
 <p>7 Churches, i. 20.</p>  
 <p>7 Candlesticks, i. 20. Represent the 7 Churches.</p>  
 <p>7 Stars, i. 20; ii. 1. Represent 7 angels of the Churches.</p>  
 <p>7 Angels of the Churches, i. 20.</p>  
 <p>7 Lamps stand near the Throne, iv. 5.</p>  
 <p>7 Seals, v. 5, opened by the Lamb, produce 4 horses, etc.</p>  
 <p>7 Trumpets, viii. 2. Given to 7 Angels.</p>  
 <p>7 Thunders utter their voices, x. 3.</p>  
 <p>7 Plagues held by 7 Angels, xv. 1.</p>  
 <p>7 Vials of Wrath, xv. 7.</p>  
 <p>7 Spirits of God, v. 6.</p>  
 <p><a name="page_127"><font size="1" color="green">p. 127</font></a></p>  
 <p>7-horned and 7-eyed Lamb, v. 6; near the Throne are the 7 Spirits of God.</p>  
 <p>7-headed and 10-horned Scarlet Beast, on which is a Woman, xvii. 3.</p>  
 <p>7-headed and 10-horned Dragon with 7 Crowns, xii. 3.</p>  
 <p>7-headed and 10-horned Beast rose out of Sea, xiii. 1.</p>  
 <p>10 Crowns on Ten horns of beast which had 7 heads, xiii. 1.</p>  
 <p>10-horned Dragon with 7 heads, xii. 3.</p>  
 <p>10-horned Beast with 7 heads rose out of sea, xiii. 1.</p>  
 <p>10-horned Scarlet Beast with 7 heads, on which was a woman, xvii. 3.</p>  
 <p>12 Tribes of Israel.</p>  
 <p>12 Apostles of the Lamb, xxi. 14.</p>  
 <p>12 Gates of the New Jerusalem, 12 Angels guarding them xxi. 12.</p>  
 <p>12 Foundations of the Walls of the New Jerusalem.</p>  
 <p>12 Stars on the head of the Woman, xii. 1.</p>  
 <p>12 sorts of Fruit on the Tree of Life, xxii. 2.</p>  
 <p>24 Elders around the Throne, on 24 seats, iv. 4–10.</p>  
 <p>42 months the Gentiles tread over the outer court of Temple, xi. 2.</p>  
 <p>42 months the 7-headed Beast to have power to blaspheme, xiii. 5.</p>  
 <p>144 cubits, the height of the Walls of the New Jerusalem, xxi. 17.</p>  
 <p>666, the number of "The Beast."</p>  
 <p>1000 years, the Dragon bound for, xx. 2–3.</p>  
 <p>1000 years, Souls of the Faithful to live and reign, xx. 4.</p>  
 <p>1260 days, the two Witnesses prophesy, xi. 3.</p>  
 <p>1260 days, the Woman to be in the wilderness, xii. 6. 12,000 of each Tribe chosen.</p>  
 <p>12,000 furlongs, length of the sides of the New Jerusalem, xxi. 16.</p>  
 <p>144,000 chosen from the whole of the Tribes.</p>  
 <p>100,000,000, or ten thousand times ten thousand Angels, round the Throne, v. 11.</p>
