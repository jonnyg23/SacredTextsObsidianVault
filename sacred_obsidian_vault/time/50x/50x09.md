 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Fifty Christmas Poems for Children</i>, by Florence B. Hyett, [1923], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">CAROL</h3>  
 <p>WHEN the herds were watching<br> In the midnight chill,<br> Came a spotless lambkin<br> From the heavenly hill.</p>  
 <p>Snow was on the mountains,<br> And the wind was cold,<br> When from God's own garden<br> Dropped a rose of gold.</p>  
 <p>When 'twas bitter winter,<br> Houseless and forlorn<br> In a star-lit stable<br> Christ the Babe was born.</p>  
 <p>Welcome, heavenly lambkin,<br> Welcome, golden rose;<br> Alleluia, Baby<br> In the swaddling clothes!</p>  
 <p>--WILLIAM CANTON</p>
