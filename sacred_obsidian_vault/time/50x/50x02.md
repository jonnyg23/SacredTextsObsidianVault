 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Fifty Christmas Poems for Children</i>, by Florence B. Hyett, [1923], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">OLD CAROL</h3>  
 <p>HE came all so still<br> Where His mother was,<br> As dew in April<br> That falleth on the grass.</p>  
 <p>He came all so still<br> To His mother's bower,<br> As dew in April<br> That falleth on the flower.</p>  
 <p>He came all so still<br> Where His mother lay,<br> As dew in April<br> That falleth on the spray.</p>  
 <p>Mother and maiden<br> Was never none but she;<br> Well may such a lady<br> God's mother be.</p>  
 <p>--ANONYMOUS</p>
