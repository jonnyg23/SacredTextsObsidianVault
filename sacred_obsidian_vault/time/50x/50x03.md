 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Fifty Christmas Poems for Children</i>, by Florence B. Hyett, [1923], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">THE LAMB CHILD</h3>  
 <p>WHEN Christ the Babe was born,<br> Full many a little lamb<br> Upon the wintry hills forlorn<br> Was nestled near its dam:</p>  
 <p>And, waking or asleep,<br> Upon His Mother's breast,<br> For love of her, each mother-sheep<br> And baby-lamb He blessed.</p>  
 <p>--JOHN BANISTER TABB</p>
