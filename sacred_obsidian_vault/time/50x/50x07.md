 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Fifty Christmas Poems for Children</i>, by Florence B. Hyett, [1923], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">SONG</h3>  
 <p>WHY do the bells of Christmas ring?<br> Why do little children sing?</p>  
 <p>Once a lovely shining star,<br> Seen by shepherds from afar,<br> Gently moved until its light<br> Made a manger's cradle bright.</p>  
 <p>There a darling baby lay,<br> Pillowed soft upon the hay;<br> And its mother sung and smiled:<br> "This is Christ, the holy Child!"</p>  
 <p>Therefore bells for Christmas ring,<br> Therefore little children sing.</p>  
 <p>--EUGENE FIELD</p>
