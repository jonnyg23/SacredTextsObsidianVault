 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Fifty Christmas Poems for Children</i>, by Florence B. Hyett, [1923], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">CHRISTMAS DAY AND EVERY DAY</h3>  
 <p>STAR high<br> Baby low:<br> 'Twixt the two<br> Wise men go;<br> Find the baby,<br> Grasp the star<br> Heirs of all things<br> Near and far!</p>  
 <p>--GEORGE MACDONALD</p>
