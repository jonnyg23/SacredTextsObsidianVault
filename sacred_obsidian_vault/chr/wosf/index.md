 <p>
 This short anthology of the writings of St. Francis includes a wide sampling
 of his writings.
 Of general interest here is
 the transcendent <a href="wosf22.htm#canticle">Canticle of the Sun</a>,
 a prayer which lucidly describes a universe alive with joy, and praises
 'Brother Sun...Sister Moon...Mother Earth'.
 </p>
 <p>
 Also available at this site is the <a href="../lff/index.htm">Little Flowers of St. Francis</a>.
 </p>
