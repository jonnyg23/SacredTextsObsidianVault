 <p><a name="page_145"><font size="1" color="green">p. 145</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">III.</h3>  <h3 align="center">PRAYER TO OBTAIN DIVINE LOVE.</h3>  
 <p>The authenticity of this prayer, accepted by the Quaracchi editors, rests on the authority of St. Bernardine of Siena <a name="fr_548"></a><a href="#fn_548"><font size="1">1</font></a> and Ubertino da Casale, <a name="fr_549"></a><a href="#fn_549"><font size="1">2</font></a> both of whom are quoted in its behalf by Wadding. The prayer is here translated according to the text given by Ubertino in his <i>Arbor Vitae Crucifixae</i> composed on La Verna in 1305. <a name="fr_550"></a><a href="#fn_550"><font size="1">3</font></a> It follows:—</p>  <h4 align="center">PRAYER.</h4>  
 <p>I beseech Thee, O Lord, that the fiery and sweet strength of Thy love may absorb my soul from all things that are under heaven, that I may die for love of Thy love as Thou didst deign to die for love of my love.</p>
