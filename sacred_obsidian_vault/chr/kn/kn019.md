 </p><h3 align="CENTER">19. <i>How this Book came to be found</i></h3>
 
 <p>   And <small>DĔMÂTĔYÔS</small> (the Patriarch <small>TIMOTHEUS</small> (?) who sat from 511 to 517), the Archbishop of <small>RÔM</small> (i.e., <small>CONSTANTINOPLE</small>, <small>BYZANTIUM</small>), said, "I have found in the Church of [Saint] <small>SOPHIA</small> among the books and the royal treasures a manuscript [which stated] that the whole kingdom of the world [belonged] to the Emperor of <small>RÔM</small> and the Emperor of <small>ETHIOPIA</small>."</p>
