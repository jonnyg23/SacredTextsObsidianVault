 </p><h3 align="CENTER">110. <i>Concerning His Resurrection</i></h3>
 
 <p>   And the prophet <small>DAVID</small> also prophesied, concerning His Resurrection and said, "I will arise, saith the Lord,
 
 
 
 <a name="page_216"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 216</font></a>
 
 and I will make salvation and manifest it openly."<a name="page_216_fr_1"></a><a href="#page_216_note_1"><sup><small>1</small></sup></a> And again he saith, "Rise up, O Lord, and judge the earth, for Thou shalt inherit among the nations."<a name="page_216_fr_2"></a><a href="#page_216_note_2"><sup><small>2</small></sup></a> And he also prophesied and said, "Rise up, O Lord, help us, and deliver us for Thy Name's sake."<a name="page_216_fr_3"></a><a href="#page_216_note_3"><sup><small>3</small></sup></a> And again he said, "Let God arise and let His enemies be scattered, and let His enemies flee from before His face."<a name="page_216_fr_4"></a><a href="#page_216_note_4"><sup><small>4</small></sup></a> And he also prophesied and said, "God hath risen up like one who hath woke up from sleep, and like a mighty man who hath left [his] wine."<a name="page_216_fr_5"></a><a href="#page_216_note_5"><sup><small>5</small></sup></a></p>
 
 <p>   Thus <small>ISAIAH</small> the prophet prophesied and said, "He will remove sickness from his soul, for he hath not committed sin, and falsehood hath not been found in his mouth. And to him that hath served righteousness and good will he show light and he will justify him; and he shall do away the sins of many, for he hath not committed sin, and falsehood is not found in his mouth."<a name="page_216_fr_6"></a><a href="#page_216_note_6"><sup><small>6</small></sup></a></p>
 
 <p>   Thus prophesied <small>DAVID</small> the Prophet and said, "For My soul shall not be left in hell."<a name="page_216_fr_7"></a><a href="#page_216_note_7"><sup><small>7</small></sup></a></p>
 
 <p>   Thus <small>SOLOMON</small> his son prophesied and said, "The Sun of righteousness shall arise, and shall travel towards the right, and shall return into His place."<a name="page_216_fr_8"></a><a href="#page_216_note_8"><sup><small>8</small></sup></a></p>
