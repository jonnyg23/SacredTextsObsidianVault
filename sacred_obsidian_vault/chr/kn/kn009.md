 </p><h3 align="CENTER">9. <i>Concerning the Covenant of </i><small>NOAH</small></h3>
 
 <p>   And then <small>NOAH</small> the righteous man died, and <small>SHEM</small> reigned in wisdom and righteousness, for he was blessed by <small>NOAH</small>, saying, "Be God to thy brother." And to <small>HAM</small> he said, "Be servant to thy brother." And he said unto <small>JAPHET</small>, "Be thou servant to <small>SHEM</small> my heir, and be thou subject unto him."<a name="page_6_fr_2"></a><a href="#page_6_note_2"><sup><small>2</small></sup></a> And again, after the Flood, the Devil, our Enemy, did not cease from his hostility against the children of <small>NOAH</small>, but stirred up <small>CANAAN</small>, the son of <small>HAM</small>, and he became the violent tyrant (or usurper) who rent the kingdom from the children of <small>SHEM</small>. Now they had divided the earth among them, and <small>NOAH</small> had made them swear by the Name of his God that they would not encroach on each other's boundaries, and would not eat the beast that had died of itself or had been rent [by wild animals], and that they would not cultivate harlotry against the law, lest God should again become angry with them and punish them with a Flood. And as for <small>NOAH</small>, he humbled himself, and offered up sacrifice, and he cried out, and groaned, and wept. And God held converse with <small>NOAH</small>, who said [unto Him], "If Thou wilt destroy the earth a second time with a Flood, blot Thou me out with those who are to perish." And God said unto him, "I will make a covenant with thee that thou shalt tell thy children they shall not eat
 
 
 
 <a name="page_7"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 7</font></a>
 
 the beast that hath died of itself or that hath been torn by wild beasts, and they shall not cultivate harlotry against the law; and I, on My part, [covenant] that I will not destroy the earth a second time with a Flood, and that I will give unto thy children Winter and Summer, Seedtime and Harvest, Autumn and Spring.<a name="page_7_fr_1"></a><a href="#page_7_note_1"><sup><small>1</small></sup></a></p>
