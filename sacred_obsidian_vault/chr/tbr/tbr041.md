   <h3 align="center">THE ANGEL WARNING.</h3>  <h3 align="center">("Three Woes" Announced.)</h3>  
 <p align="center">Rev. 8:13.</p>  
 <p></p><dir><font size="1">"And I beheld, and heard an <b>ANGEL (R. V. Eagle)</b> flying through the midst of heaven, saying with a loud voice, <b>WOE, WOE, WOE,</b> to the <b>inhabiters of the earth</b> by reason of the other voices of the Trumpet of the <b>THREE ANGELS</b>, which are yet to sound."</font></dir>  
 <p>The Revised Version, and many Manuscripts substitute "<b>Eagle</b>" for <b>ANGEL</b>, but that does not affect the meaning, for if God could make Balaam's "<b>ass</b>" to speak, He can use an "<b>Eagle</b>" to announce a message.</p>
