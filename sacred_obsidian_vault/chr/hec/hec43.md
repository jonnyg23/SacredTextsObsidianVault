 <p align="center"><font size="-1" color="green">Canon for Christmas Day</font></p>  <h3 align="center">ODE VIII.</h3>  
 <p align="center"><font size="-1" color="green">by S. Cosmas</font></p>  
 <p align="center">θαύματος ὑπερφυοῦς ἡ δροσοβόλος.</p>  
 <p>The dewy freshness that the furnace flings<br> Works out a wond'rous type of future things:<br> Nor did the flame the Holy Three consume,<br> Nor did the Godhead's fire thy frame entomb,<br>   Thou, on Whose bosom hung the <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Word</span>:<br>   Wherefore we cry with hearts endeavour,<br>   "Let all Creation bless the Loin,<br>   And magnify His Name for ever!"</p>  
 <p>Babel's proud daughter once led David's race<br> From Sion, to their exile's woful place:<br> Babel now bids her wise men, gifts in hand,<br> Before King David's Royal Daughter stand, <a name="page_146"><font size="1" color="green">p. 146</font></a><br>   The Mother of the Incarnate Word:<br>   Wherefore we cry with heart's endeavour,<br>   "Let all Creation bless the <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Lord</span>,<br>   And magnify His Name for ever!"</p>  
 <p>From music grief held back the exile's hand:<br> "How sing the <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Lord's</span> song in an alien land?"<br> But Babel's exile here is done away,<br> And Bethlehem's harmony this glorious day<br>   By Thee, Incarnate <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">God</span>, restored:<br>   Wherefore we cry with heart's endeavour,<br>   "Let all Creation bless the <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Lord</span>,<br>   And magnify His Name for ever!"</p>  
 <p>Of old victorious Babel bore away,<br> The spoils of Royal Sion and her prey:<br> But Babel's treasure now, and Babel's kings,<br> <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Christ</span>, by the guiding star, to Sion brings, <a name="page_147"><font size="1" color="green">p. 147</font></a><br>   There they knelt, and there ador’d:<br>   Wherefore we cry with heart's endeavour,<br>   Let all Creation bless the <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Lord</span>,<br>   And magnify His Name for ever!"</p>
