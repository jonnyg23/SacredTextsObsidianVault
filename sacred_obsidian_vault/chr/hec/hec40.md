 <p align="center"><font size="-1" color="green">Canon for Christmas Day</font></p>  <h3 align="center">ODE V.</h3>  
 <p align="center"><font size="-1" color="green">by S. Cosmas</font></p>  
 <p align="center">Θεὸς ὢν εἰρήνης.</p>  
 <p>Father of Peace, and <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">God</span> of Consolation!<br>   The Angel of the Counsel dost Thou send<br> To herald peace, to manifest Salvation,<br>   Thy Light to pour, Thy knowledge to extend;<br> Whence, with the morning's earliest rays,<br> Lover of men! Thy Name we praise.</p>  
 <p>’Midst Cæsar's subjects Thou, at his decreeing,<br>   Obey’dst and was enrolled: our mortal race,<br> To sin and Satan slave, from bondage freeing,<br>   Our poverty in all points didst embrace:<br> And by that Union didst combine<br> The earthly with the All-Divine.</p>  
 <p><a name="page_140"><font size="1" color="green">p. 140</font></a></p>  
 <p>Lo! Mary, as the world's long day was waning,<br>   Incarnate Deity conceived and bore;<br> Virgin in birth, and after birth, remaining;<br>   And man to <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">God</span> is reconciled once more<br> Wherefore in faith her name we bless,<br> And Mother of our <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">God</span> confess.</p>
