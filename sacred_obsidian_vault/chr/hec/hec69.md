 <p align="center"><font size="-1" color="green">Canon for Ascension Day</font></p>  <h3 align="center">ODE VIII.</h3>  <h3 align="center"><i>Hirmos</i>. "HIM OF THE FATHER." <a name="fr_6"></a><a href="#fn_6"><font size="1">*</font></a></h3>  
 <p align="center"><font size="-1" color="green">by S. Joseph of the Studium</font></p>  
 <p align="center">τὸν ἐν δυσὶ ταῖς οὐσίαις.</p>  
 <p>O   f twofold natures, <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Christ</span>, the Giver<br>       Of immortality and love,<br>     Ascendeth to the <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Father's</span> glory,<br>       Ascendeth to the Throne above:<br>         Wherefore He, this glorious morn,<br>           Be by all ador'd:<br>         Thou That liftest up our horn,<br>           Holy art Thou, <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Lord</span>!</p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p><a name="page_224"><font size="1" color="green">p. 224</font></a></p>  
 <p>S   laves are set free, and captives ransom’d:<br>       The Nature that He made at first<br>     He now presenteth to the <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Father</span>,<br>       The chains of her damnation burst:<br>         This the cause that He was born,<br>           Adam's race restor’d:<br>         Thou That liftest up our horn,<br>           Holy art Thou, <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Lord</span>!</p>  
 <p>E   mptied awhile of all His brightness,<br>       He enter’d thus the glorious fight;<br>     O’erthrew the foe, mankind exalted<br>       Far above every Pow’r and Might:<br>         Therefore bare He pains and scorn,<br>           Calvary's heart-blood pour’d:—<br>         Thou That liftest up our horn,<br>           Holy art Thou, <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Lord</span>!</p>  
 <p><a name="page_225"><font size="1" color="green">p. 225</font></a></p>  
 <p align="center"><i>Catavasía</i>.</p>  
 <p>P   raising the <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Lord</span> they stood, the Martyr Three,<br>     Untouch’d amidst the fire, and wholly free:<br>     With them associate, let the world's wide frame<br>     To Him Whose healing dew restrain’d the flame,<br>     Send up the hymn of praise, and magnify His Name!</p>
