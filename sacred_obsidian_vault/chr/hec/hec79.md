 <p><a name="page_245"><font size="1" color="green">p. 245</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">THE PILGRIMS OF JESUS</h3>  
 <p align="center"><font size="-1" color="green">by S. Joseph of the Studium</font></p>  
 <p>O happy band of pilgrims,<br>   If onward ye will tread<br> With <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Jesus</span> as your Fellow<br>   To <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Jesus</span> as your Head!</p>  
 <p>O happy, if ye labour<br>   As <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Jesus</span> did for men:<br> O happy, if ye hunger<br>   As <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Jesus</span> hunger’d then!</p>  
 <p>The Cross that <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Jesus</span> carried<br>   He carried as your due:<br> The Crown that <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Jesus</span> weareth<br>   He weareth it for you.</p>  
 <p><a name="page_246"><font size="1" color="green">p. 246</font></a></p>  
 <p>The Faith by which ye see Him,<br>   The Hope, in which ye yearn,<br> The Love that through all troubles<br>   To Him alone will turn,—</p>  
 <p>What are they, but vaunt-couriers<br>   To lead you to His Sight?<br> What are they, save the effluence<br>   Of Uncreated Light?</p>  
 <p>The trials that beset you,<br>   The sorrows ye endure,<br> The manifold temptations<br>   That Death alone can cure,—</p>  
 <p>What are they, but His jewels<br>   Of right celestial worth?<br> What are they but the ladder,<br>   Set up to Heav’n on earth?</p>  
 <p><a name="page_247"><font size="1" color="green">p. 247</font></a></p>  
 <p>O happy band of pilgrims,<br>   Look upward to the skies:—<br> Where such a light affliction<br>   Shall win you such a prize!</p>
