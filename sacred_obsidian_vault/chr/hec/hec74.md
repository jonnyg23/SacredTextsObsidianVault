 <p><a name="page_236"><font size="1" color="green">p. 236</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center"><span style="font-family: Lucida Blackletter, Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; font-weight: bold;">Euthymius.</span></h3>  
 <p align="center">+ A.D. 910.</p>  
 <p>Euthymius, usually known as Syngelus, (the same as <i>Syncellus</i>, the confidential Deacon of the Patriarch of Constantinople,) who died about 916, is the author of a Penitential Canon to S Mary, which is highly esteemed in the East. It would scarcely, however, be possible to make even a Cento from it which would be acceptable to the English reader.</p>
