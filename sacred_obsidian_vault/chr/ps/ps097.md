 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>Pistis Sophia</i>, by G.R.S. Mead, [1921], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">CHAPTER 92</h3>  
 <p>It came to pass then, when Jesus had finished saying these words unto his disciples, that they rejoiced in great joy and exulted when they heard Jesus say these words.</p>  
 <p>And Jesus continued again in the discourse and said unto them: "Hearken, therefore, now still</p>  
 <p><a name="page_176"><font size="1" color="green">p. 176</font></a></p>  
 <p>further, O my disciples, so that I discourse with you concerning the whole gnosis of the mystery of the Ineffable.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">Of the gnosis of the mystery of the Ineffable.</font></span>"That mystery of the Ineffable knoweth why unmercifulness hath arisen and why mercifulness hath arisen.</p>  
 <p>"And that mystery knoweth why ruin hath arisen and why everlasting eternity hath arisen.</p>  
 <p>"And that mystery knoweth why the reptiles have arisen and why they will be destroyed.</p>  
 <p>"And that mystery knoweth why the wild beasts have arisen |<span class="rmargnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><b>210</b>.</font></span> and why they will be destroyed.</p>  
 <p>"And that mystery knoweth why the cattle have arisen and why the birds have arisen.</p>  
 <p>"And that mystery knoweth why the mountains have arisen and why the precious stones therein have arisen.</p>  
 <p>"And that mystery knoweth why the matter of gold hath arisen and why the matter of silver hath arisen.</p>  
 <p>"And that mystery knoweth why the matter of copper hath arisen and why the matter of iron and of stone hath arisen.</p>  
 <p>"And that mystery knoweth why the matter of lead hath arisen.</p>  
 <p>"And that mystery knoweth why the matter of glass hath arisen and why the matter of wax hath arisen.</p>  
 <p>"And that mystery knoweth why herbs, that is the vegetables, have arisen and why all matters have arisen.</p>  
 <p>"And the mystery knoweth why the waters of the earth and all things in them have arisen and why also the earth hath arisen.</p>  
 <p><a name="page_177"><font size="1" color="green">p. 177</font></a></p>  
 <p>"And that mystery knoweth why the seas |<span class="rmargnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><b>211</b>.</font></span> and the waters have arisen and why the wild beasts in the seas have arisen.</p>  
 <p>"And that mystery knoweth why the matter of the world hath arisen and why it [the world] will be utterly destroyed."</p>
