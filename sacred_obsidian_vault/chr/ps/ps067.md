 <p><a name="page_105"><font size="1" color="green">p. 105</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">[THE NOTE OF A SCRIBE]</h3>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">A note by a later hand, copied from another scripture.</font></span> |<span class="rmargnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><b>125</b>.</font></span>(Now these are the names which I will give from the Boundless onward. Write them with a sign, that the Sons of God may be revealed from here on.</p>  
 <p>This is the name of the Immortal: <i>aaa</i>, <i>ōōō</i>; and this is the name of the Voice, for the sake of which the Perfect Man hath set himself in motion: <i>iii</i>. And these are the interpretations of the names of these mysteries: the first [name], which is <i>aaa</i>, its interpretation is <i>fff</i>; the second, which is <i>mmm</i> or ōōō, its interpretation is <i>aaa</i>; the third, which is <i>ps ps ps</i>, its interpretation is <i>ooo</i>; the fourth, which is <i>fff</i>, its interpretation is <i>nnn</i>; the fifth, which is <i>ddd</i>, its interpretation is <i>aaa</i>. He on the throne is <i>aaa</i>. This is the interpretation of the second: <i>aaaa</i>, <i>aaaa</i>, <i>aaaa</i>; this is the interpretation of the whole name.)</p>
