 <p> <b>First Point.</b> First: The whole multitude of the Jews <a name="fr_27"></a><a href="seil83.htm#fn_27"><font size="1">28</font></a> take Him to Pilate and accuse Him before him, saying: "`We have found that this man tried to ruin our people and forbade to pay tribute to Caesar.'"
 </p><p> <b>Second Point.</b> Second: Pilate, after having examined Him once and again, said: "`I find no fault.'"
 </p><p> <b>Third Point.</b> Third: The robber Barabbas was preferred to Him. "They all cried, saying: `Give us not this man, but Barabbas!'"
