 <p>
 These are the Spiritual Exercises of St. Ignatius of Loyola, a plan
 of contemplation to be carried out over about a month.
 St. Ignatius of Loyola (1419-1556) was the founder of the Jesuits,
 and was canonized by Pope Gregory XV in 1622.
 He published the <cite>Spiritual Exercises</cite> in 1548.
 The <cite>Exercises</cite> were intended for use during a retreat;
 and are a central part of the first year training of Jesuit novitiates.
 However, one does not have to be a Jesuit-in-training to take
 advantage of the <cite>Exercises</cite>:
 Increasingly, lay people and even non-Catholics follow this path.
 </p>
