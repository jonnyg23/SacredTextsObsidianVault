 <h1 align="center">CHAPTER VI<a name="page_12_fr_1"></a><a href="#page_12_note_1"><sup><small>1</small></sup></a>.</h1>
 
 <p align="center">OF DARKNESS.</p>
 
 <p>   D<small>ARKNESS</small> is a self-existent nature; and if it had not had a nature, it would not have been reckoned among the seven natures which were created in the beginning in silence. Others say that darkness is not a self-existent nature, but that it is the shadow of bodies.</p>
