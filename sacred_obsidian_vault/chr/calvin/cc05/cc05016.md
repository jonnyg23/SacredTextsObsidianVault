 <p class="HDG-C-Gr-16-Pref12"><a name="ii.iii.iii-p1"></a>Supplements of the Seventh Commandment</p>  <a name="ii.iii.iii.i"></a>  				
 <p class="HDG-C-Gr-16-Pref12"><a name="ii.iii.iii.i-p1"></a><a name="ii.iii.iii.i-p1.1"></a><a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev018.htm#000">Leviticus 18</a><a name="ii.iii.iii.i-p1.1"></a></p>  				 				   					    					 
 <p class="TableCaption14"><a name="ii.iii.iii.i-p2"></a><a name="ii.iii.iii.i-p2.1"></a><a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev018.htm#022">Leviticus 18:22-30</a><a name="ii.iii.iii.i-p2.1"></a></p> 					   				   					    					 
 <p class="TableText15"><a name="ii.iii.iii.i-p3"></a><b>22.</b> Thou shalt not lie with mankind as with womankind: it <i>is</i> abomination.</p> 					    					 
 <p class="TableText15"><a name="ii.iii.iii.i-p4"></a><b>22.</b> Cure masculo ne concumbas concubitu mulieris: abominatio est.</p> 					   				   					    					 
 <p class="TableText15"><a name="ii.iii.iii.i-p5"></a><b>23.</b> Neither shalt thou lie with any beast, to defile thyself therewith; neither shall any woman stand before a beast to lie down thereto: it <i>is</i> confusion.</p> 					    					 
 <p class="TableText15"><a name="ii.iii.iii.i-p6"></a><b>23.</b> Cum animali non coibis ut polluaris cum eo: nec mulier prostituet se animali ut coeat cum eo: turpitudo est.</p> 					   				   					    					 
 <p class="TableText15"><a name="ii.iii.iii.i-p7"></a><b>24.</b> Defile not ye yourselves in any of these things: for in all these the nations are defiled which I cast out before you.</p> 					    					 
 <p class="TableText15"><a name="ii.iii.iii.i-p8"></a><b>24.</b> Ne polluamini in omnibus his: nam in his omnibus polluerunt se gentes quas ego ejiciam a facie vestra.</p> 					   				   					    					 
 <p class="TableText15"><a name="ii.iii.iii.i-p9"></a><b>25.</b> And the land is defiled: therefore I do visit the iniquity thereof upon it, and the land itself vomiteth out her inhabitants.</p> 					    					 
 <p class="TableText15"><a name="ii.iii.iii.i-p10"></a><b>25.</b> Polluta fuit terra, et visitavi iniquitatem ejus super eam, evomuitque terra habitatores suos.</p> 					   				   					    					 
 <p class="TableText15"><a name="ii.iii.iii.i-p11"></a><b>26.</b> Ye shall therefore keep my statutes and my judgments, and shall not commit <i>any</i> of these abominations; <i>neither</i> any of your own nation, nor any stranger that sojourneth among you;</p> 					    					 
 <p class="TableText15"><a name="ii.iii.iii.i-p12"></a><b>26.</b> Vos ergo custodite statuta mea, et judicia mea: et ne faciatis ex omnibus abominationibus istis, indigena, vel peregrinus qui peregrinatur in medio vestri.</p> 					   				   					    					 
 <p class="TableText15"><a name="ii.iii.iii.i-p13"></a><b>27.</b> (For all these abominations have the men of the land done which <i>were</i> before you, and the land is defiled;)</p> 					    					 
 <p class="TableText15"><a name="ii.iii.iii.i-p14"></a><b>27.</b> Omnes enim abominationes istas fecerunt homines terrae qui fuerunt ante vos, et polluta fuit terra.</p> 					   				   					    					 
 <p class="TableText15"><a name="ii.iii.iii.i-p15"></a><b>28.</b> That the land spur not you out also, when ye defile it, as it spewed out the nations that <i>were</i> before you.</p> 					    					 
 <p class="TableText15"><a name="ii.iii.iii.i-p16"></a><b>28.</b> Ne evomat vos terra quod contaminetis eam, quemadmodum evomuit gentem quae fuit ante vos.</p> 					   				   					    					 
 <p class="TableText15"><a name="ii.iii.iii.i-p17"></a><b>29.</b> For whosoever shall commit any of these abominations, even the souls that commit <i>them</i> shall be cut off from among their people.</p> 					    					 
 <p class="TableText15"><a name="ii.iii.iii.i-p18"></a><b>29.</b> Quisquis enim fecerit ex omnibus abominationibus istis, animae quae fecerint excidentur e medio populi sui.</p> 					   				   					    					 
 <p class="TableText15"><a name="ii.iii.iii.i-p19"></a><b>30.</b> Therefore shall ye keep mine ordinance, that ye commit not <i>any</i> <i>one</i> of these abominable customs, which were committed before you, and that ye defile not yourselves therein: I <i>am</i> the Lord your God.</p> 					    					 
 <p class="TableText15"><a name="ii.iii.iii.i-p20"></a><b>30.</b> Custodite custodias meas, nec facietis e statutis abominationum quae facta sunt ante vos, neque polluatis vos in illis: ego Jehova Deus vester.</p>
