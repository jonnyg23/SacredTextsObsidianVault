 <p><a name="v.i-p1"></a>    
 </p><p class="HDG-C-Gr-16-Pref08"><a name="v.i-p2"></a>CHAPTER 1</p>    
 <p><a name="v.i-p3"></a><b>1 </b>Paul, an apostle, not from men, neither by man, but by Jesus Christ, and God the Father, who raised him from the dead,</p>    
 <p><a name="v.i-p4"></a><b>2 </b>And all the brethren who are with met to the churches of Galatia;</p>    
 <p><a name="v.i-p5"></a><b>3 </b>Grace (be) to you, and peace from God the Father, and (from) our Lord Jesus Christ,</p>    
 <p><a name="v.i-p6"></a><b>4 </b>Who gave himself for our sins, that he might deliver us from the present wicked age, according to the will of God and our Father,</p>    
 <p><a name="v.i-p7"></a><b>5 </b>To whom (be) glory for ever and ever. Amen.</p>    
 <p><a name="v.i-p8"></a><b>6 </b>I wonder that ye are so soon removed from Christ who called you in grace,   	  <a name="fr_171"></a><a href="#fn_171"><font size="1">172</font></a>
  	  to another gospel;</p>    
 <p><a name="v.i-p10"></a><b>7 </b>Which is not another thing, than that there are some who trouble you, and wish to subvert the gospel of Christ.</p>    
 <p><a name="v.i-p11"></a><b>8 </b>But although we, or an angel from heaven preach the gospel to you otherwise than what we have preached to you, let him be accursed.</p>    
 <p><a name="v.i-p12"></a><b>9 </b>As we said before, now also I say again, if any one shall preach the gospel to you otherwise than what ye have received, let him be accursed.</p>    
 <p><a name="v.i-p13"></a><b>10 </b>For do I now persuade according to men, or according to God?   	  <a name="fr_172"></a><a href="#fn_172"><font size="1">173</font></a>
  	  or do I seek to please men? for if I still pleased men, I should not be a servant of Christ.</p>    
 <p><a name="v.i-p15"></a><b>11 </b>But I make known to you, brethren, concerning the gospel which was preached by me, that it is not according to man;</p>    
 <p><a name="v.i-p16"></a><b>12 </b>For I neither received nor learned it from man, but by the revelation of Jesus Christ.</p>    
 <p><a name="v.i-p17"></a><b>13 </b>For ye have heard of my conversation, which formerly was in Judaism;   	  <a name="fr_173"></a><a href="#fn_173"><font size="1">174</font></a>
  	  that, beyond measure, I persecuted the church of God, and wasted it,</p>    
 <p><a name="v.i-p19"></a><b>14 </b>And profited in Judaism   	  <a name="fr_174"></a><a href="#fn_174"><font size="1">175</font></a>
  	  above many my equals   	  <a name="fr_175"></a><a href="#fn_175"><font size="1">176</font></a>
  	  in my nation, being exceedingly zealous for the traditions of the fathers.   	  <a name="fr_176"></a><a href="#fn_176"><font size="1">177</font></a>
 </p>    
 <p><a name="v.i-p23"></a><b>15 </b>But after that it pleased God, (who had separated me from my mother’s womb, and (who) called me by his grace,)</p>    
 <p><a name="v.i-p24"></a><b>16 </b>To reveal his son to me, that I might preach him among the Gentiles, immediately I conferred not   	  <a name="fr_177"></a><a href="#fn_177"><font size="1">178</font></a>
  	  with flesh and blood,</p>    
 <p><a name="v.i-p26"></a><b>17 </b>Neither did I return to Jerusalem, to those who were apostles before me; but I went away into Arabia, and again returned to Damascus.</p>    
 <p><a name="v.i-p27"></a><b>18 </b>Next after three years, I returned to Jerusalem, that I might see Peter; and I abode with him fifteen days.</p>    
 <p><a name="v.i-p28"></a><b>19 </b>But I saw none other of the apostles, except James the Lord’s brother.</p>    
 <p><a name="v.i-p29"></a><b>20 </b>Now the things which I write to you, behold, before God, I do not speak falsely.</p>    
 <p><a name="v.i-p30"></a><b>21 </b>Afterwards I came into the countries of Syria and Cilicia;</p>    
 <p><a name="v.i-p31"></a><b>22 </b>And was unknown by face to the churches of Judea, which were in Christ.</p>    
 <p><a name="v.i-p32"></a><b>23 </b>But there was only this report among them:   	  <a name="fr_178"></a><a href="#fn_178"><font size="1">179</font></a>
  	  He who at one time persecuted us, now preacheth the faith which he formerly was destroying,</p>    
 <p><a name="v.i-p34"></a><b>24 </b>And they glorified God in me.</p>
