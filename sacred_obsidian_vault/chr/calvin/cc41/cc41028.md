 <p><a name="vi.ii-p1"></a>    
 </p><p class="HDG-C-Gr-16-Pref08"><a name="vi.ii-p2"></a>CHAPTER 2</p>    
 <p><a name="vi.ii-p3"></a><b>1 </b>And when ye were dead in your trespasses and sins;</p>    
 <p><a name="vi.ii-p4"></a><b>2 </b>In which for some time ye walked, according to the course   	  <a name="fr_206"></a><a href="#fn_206"><font size="1">207</font></a>
  	  of this world, according to the prince of the power of air, that is, of the spirit that now worketh   	  <a name="fr_207"></a><a href="#fn_207"><font size="1">208</font></a>
  	  in the children of disobedience;</p>    
 <p><a name="vi.ii-p7"></a><b>3 </b>Among whom we all also, for some time, had our conversation in the lusts of our flesh, executing what was agreeable to the flesh and to the mind;   	  <a name="fr_208"></a><a href="#fn_208"><font size="1">209</font></a>
  	  and were by nature children of wrath, even as others.</p>    
 <p><a name="vi.ii-p9"></a><b>4 </b>But God, who is rich in mercy, on account of his great love, with which he loved us,</p>    
 <p><a name="vi.ii-p10"></a><b>5 </b>Even when we were dead in sins, quickened (us) together with Christ; (by grace ye are saved;)</p>    
 <p><a name="vi.ii-p11"></a><b>6 </b>And hath raised us up together, and hath made us sit together in heavenly (places) in Christ Jesus;</p>    
 <p><a name="vi.ii-p12"></a><b>7 </b>That he might shew, in the ages to come, the surpassing riches of his grace, in (his) kindness   	  <a name="fr_209"></a><a href="#fn_209"><font size="1">210</font></a>
  	  toward us in Christ Jesus.</p>    
 <p><a name="vi.ii-p14"></a><b>8 </b>For by grace are ye saved through faith; and that not of yourselves; (it is) the gift of God.</p>    
 <p><a name="vi.ii-p15"></a><b>9 </b>Not of works, that no one may boast.</p>    
 <p><a name="vi.ii-p16"></a><b>10 </b>For we are his workmanship, having been created in Christ Jesus to good works, which God hath prepared, that we may walk in them.</p>    
 <p><a name="vi.ii-p17"></a><b>11 </b>Wherefore remember that you, at one time Gentiles in the flesh, who were called Uncircumcision by that which is called Circumcision, made by the hand in the flesh,</p>    
 <p><a name="vi.ii-p18"></a><b>12 </b>At that time were without Christ, having been alienated from the commonwealth of Israel   	  <a name="fr_210"></a><a href="#fn_210"><font size="1">211</font></a>
  	  strangers from the tables of promise,   	  <a name="fr_211"></a><a href="#fn_211"><font size="1">212</font></a>
  	  not having hope,   	  <a name="fr_212"></a><a href="#fn_212"><font size="1">213</font></a>
  	  and without God in the world;</p>    
 <p><a name="vi.ii-p22"></a><b>13 </b>But now in Christ Jesus   	  <a name="fr_213"></a><a href="#fn_213"><font size="1">214</font></a>
  	  you, who formerly were far off, have been made nigh through the blood of Christ.</p>    
 <p><a name="vi.ii-p24"></a><b>14 </b>For he is our peace, who hath made both one,   	  <a name="fr_214"></a><a href="#fn_214"><font size="1">215</font></a>
  	  and breaking down the middle wall of partition, the enmities in our flesh;</p>    
 <p><a name="vi.ii-p26"></a><b>15 </b>Abolishing in his flesh the law of commandments (which is) contained in ordinances, that he might unite in himself the two into one new man, making peace;</p>    
 <p><a name="vi.ii-p27"></a><b>16 </b>That he might reconcile both to God in one body through the cross, slaying   	  <a name="fr_215"></a><a href="#fn_215"><font size="1">216</font></a>
  	  the enmities by it;</p>    
 <p><a name="vi.ii-p29"></a><b>17 </b>And coming, preached peace to you who were far off, and peace to those who were nigh;</p>    
 <p><a name="vi.ii-p30"></a><b>18 </b>For through him we both have access in one Spirit to the Father.</p>    
 <p><a name="vi.ii-p31"></a><b>19 </b>Therefore ye are no longer strangers and foreigners, but fellow-citizens of the saints, and of the household of God,</p>    
 <p><a name="vi.ii-p32"></a><b>20 </b>Having been built on the foundation of the apostles and prophets, of which Jesus Christ himself is the chief corner-stone;</p>    
 <p><a name="vi.ii-p33"></a><b>21 </b>In whom the whole building joined together groweth into a holy temple in the Lord;</p>    
 <p><a name="vi.ii-p34"></a><b>22 </b>In whom ye also are built together   	  <a name="fr_216"></a><a href="#fn_216"><font size="1">217</font></a>
  	  for a habitation of God in the Spirit.</p>
