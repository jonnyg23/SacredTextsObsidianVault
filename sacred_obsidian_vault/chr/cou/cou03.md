 <p><a name="an_iv-p4"></a>Here Beginneth the Prayer on the Prologue</p>  
 <p><a name="an_iv-p5"></a> </p>  
 <p><a name="an_iv-p6"></a>GOD, unto whom all hearts be open, and unto whom all will speaketh, and unto whom no privy thing is hid. I beseech Thee so for to cleanse the intent of mine heart with the unspeakable gift of Thy grace, that I may perfectly love Thee, and worthily praise Thee. Amen.</p>
