If this, which is Oehler's and Migne's reading, be correct, the words <i>gelida</i> and <i>incanduit</i> seem to be intentionally contrasted, unless <i>incandescere</i> be used here in a supposed sense of "growing white," "turning pale." <i>Urere</i> is used in Latin of heat and cold indifferently.
