 <p class="c31"><a name="v.iv.lxiv-p2"></a><span class="c12"><a name="v.iv.lxiv-p2.1"></a>Philip, <a name="fr_2681"></a><a href="#fn_2681"><font size="1">2682</font></a>
 </span> the presbyter Jeromes best pupil, published a <i>Commentary on Job,</i> written in an unaffected style. I have read his <i>Familiar letters,</i> exceedingly witty, exhorting the endurance of poverty and sufferings. He died in the reign of Martianus and Avitus.</p>
