 </p><p class="c46"><a name="v.iv.lix-p1"></a>  <span class="c12"><a name="v.iv.lix-p1.1"></a>Chapter LVIII.</span></p>  
 <p class="c31"><a name="v.iv.lix-p2"></a><span class="c12"><a name="v.iv.lix-p2.1"></a>Cyril, <a name="fr_2673"></a><a href="#fn_2673"><font size="1">2674</font></a>
 </span> bishop of the church at Alexandria, published various treatises on various <i>Questions,</i> and also composed many homilies, which are recommended for preaching by the Greek bishops. Other books of his are; <i>On the downfall of the synagogue, On faith against the heretics,</i> and a work directed especially against Nestorius and entitled, <i>A Refutation,</i> in which all the secrets of Nestorius are exposed and his published opinions are refuted.</p>
