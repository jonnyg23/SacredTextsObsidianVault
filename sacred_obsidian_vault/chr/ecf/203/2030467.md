 <p> <a name="v.iii.lxxxvi"></a>
 </p><p class="c46"><a name="v.iii.lxxxvi-p1"></a>  <span class="c12"><a name="v.iii.lxxxvi-p1.1"></a>Chapter LXXXIV.</span></p>  
 <p class="c31"><a name="v.iii.lxxxvi-p2"></a><span class="c12"><a name="v.iii.lxxxvi-p2.1"></a>Juvencus</span>, <a name="fr_2506"></a><a href="#fn_2506"><font size="1">2507</font></a>
  a Spaniard of noble family and presbyter, translating the four gospels almost verbally in hexameter verses, composed four books. He wrote some other things in the same metre relating to the order of the sacraments. He flourished in the reign of Constantinus.</p>
