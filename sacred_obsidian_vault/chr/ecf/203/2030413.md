 <p class="c31"><a name="v.iii.xxxii-p2"></a><span class="c12"><a name="v.iii.xxxii-p2.1"></a>Philip <a name="fr_2431"></a><a href="#fn_2431"><font size="1">2432</font></a>
 </span> bishop of Crete, that is of the city of Gortina, whom Dionysius mentions in the epistle which he wrote to the church of the same city, published a remarkable book <i>Against Marcion</i> and flourished in the time of Marcus Antoninus Verus and Lucius Aurelius Commodus.</p>
