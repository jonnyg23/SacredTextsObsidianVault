 </p><p class="c46"><a name="v.iii.cviii-p1"></a>  <span class="c12"><a name="v.iii.cviii-p1.1"></a>Chapter CVI.</span></p>  
 <p class="c31"><a name="v.iii.cviii-p2"></a><span class="c12"><a name="v.iii.cviii-p2.1"></a>Pacianus</span>, <a name="fr_2534"></a><a href="#fn_2534"><font size="1">2535</font></a>
  bishop of Barcelona, in the Pyrenees Mountains, a man of chaste eloquence, and as distinguished by his life as by his speech, wrote various short works, among which are <i>The Deer</i>, <a name="fr_2535"></a><a href="#fn_2535"><font size="1">2536</font></a>
  and <i>Against the Novatians,</i> and died in the reign of Emperor Theodosian, in extreme old age.</p>
