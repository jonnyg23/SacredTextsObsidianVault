 </p><p class="c46"><a name="v.iii.xlv-p1"></a>  <span class="c12"><a name="v.iii.xlv-p1.1"></a>Chapter XLIII.</span></p>  
 <p class="c31"><a name="v.iii.xlv-p2"></a><span class="c12"><a name="v.iii.xlv-p2.1"></a>Theophilus</span>, <a name="fr_2451"></a><a href="#fn_2451"><font size="1">2452</font></a>
