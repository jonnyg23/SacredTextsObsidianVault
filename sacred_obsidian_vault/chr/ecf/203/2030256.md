 <p> <a name="iv.x.lxvii"></a>
 </p><p class="c46"><a name="iv.x.lxvii-p1"></a>  <i>LXVII. To Maranas.</i></p>  
 <p class="c31"><a name="iv.x.lxvii-p2"></a>It was thy work, my good Sir, to call the rest also to the feast of the dedication. Through thy zeal and energy the holy temple has been built, and the loud-voiced heralds of the truth have come to dwell therein, and guard them that approach thither in faith. Nevertheless I write and signify the season of the feast.</p>
