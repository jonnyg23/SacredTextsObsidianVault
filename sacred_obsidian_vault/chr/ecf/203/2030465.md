 <p class="c31"><a name="v.iii.lxxxiv-p2"></a><span class="c12"><a name="v.iii.lxxxiv-p2.1"></a>Reticius <a name="fr_2504"></a><a href="#fn_2504"><font size="1">2505</font></a>
 </span> bishop of Autun, among the Aedui, had a great reputation in Gaul in the reign of Constantine. I have read his commentaries <i>On the Song of Songs</i> and another great volume <i>Against Novatian</i> but besides these, I have found no works of his.</p>
