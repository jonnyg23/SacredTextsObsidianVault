 <p> <a name="vi.xii.iii.xlv"></a>
 </p><p class="c24"><a name="vi.xii.iii.xlv-p1"></a>  44. In the end of your letter you say: “I hope that you love peace.” To this I will answer in a few words: If you desire peace, lay down your arms. I can be at peace with one who shews kindness; I do not fear one who threatens me. Let us be at one in faith, and peace will follow immediately.</p>
