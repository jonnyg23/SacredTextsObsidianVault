 <p class="c31"><a name="v.iii.cxxvi-p2"></a><span class="c12"><a name="v.iii.cxxvi-p2.1"></a>Ambrose <a name="fr_2556"></a><a href="#fn_2556"><font size="1">2557</font></a>
 </span> a bishop of Milan, at the present time is still writing. I withhold my judgment of him, because he is still alive, fearing either to praise or blame lest in the one event, I should be blamed for adulation, and in the other for speaking the truth.</p>
