 <p class="c31"><a name="v.iii.cxiii-p2"></a><span class="c12"><a name="v.iii.cxiii-p2.1"></a>Acilius Severus <a name="fr_2542"></a><a href="#fn_2542"><font size="1">2543</font></a>
 </span> of Spain, of the family of that Severus to whom Lactantius two books of <i>Epistles</i> are addressed, composed a volume of mingled poetry and prose which is a sort of guide book to his whole life. This he called <i>Calamity or Trial</i>. <a name="fr_2543"></a><a href="#fn_2543"><font size="1">2544</font></a>
  He died in the reign of Valentinianus.</p>
