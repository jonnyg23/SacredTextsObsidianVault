 </p><p class="c46"><a name="v.iii.cxii-p1"></a>  <span class="c12"><a name="v.iii.cxii-p1.1"></a>Chapter CX.</span></p>  
 <p class="c31"><a name="v.iii.cxii-p2"></a><span class="c12"><a name="v.iii.cxii-p2.1"></a>Optatus <a name="fr_2540"></a><a href="#fn_2540"><font size="1">2541</font></a>
 </span> the African, bishop of Milevis, <a name="fr_2541"></a><a href="#fn_2541"><font size="1">2542</font></a>
  during the reign of the Emperors Valentinianus and Valens, wrote in behalf of the Catholic party six books against the calumny of the Donatian party, in which he asserts that the crime of the Donatists is falsely charged upon the catholic party.</p>
