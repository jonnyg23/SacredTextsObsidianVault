 </p><p class="c46"><a name="iv.x.lv-p1"></a>  <i>LV. Festal.</i></p>  
 <p class="c31"><a name="iv.x.lv-p2"></a>We are much distressed, for we are gifted with the nature not of rocks but of men, but the recollection of the Lords Epiphany has been to me a very potent medicine; so at once I write, according to the custom of the feast, and salute your magnificence with a prayer that you may live in prosperity and repute.</p>
