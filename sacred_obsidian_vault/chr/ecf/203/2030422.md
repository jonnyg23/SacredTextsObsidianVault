 </p><p class="c46"><a name="v.iii.xli-p1"></a>  <span class="c12"><a name="v.iii.xli-p1.1"></a>Chapter XXXIX.</span></p>  
 <p class="c31"><a name="v.iii.xli-p2"></a><span class="c12"><a name="v.iii.xli-p2.1"></a>Miltiades <a name="fr_2446"></a><a href="#fn_2446"><font size="1">2447</font></a>
 </span> of whom Rhodo gives an account in the work which he wrote against Montanus, Prisca and Maximilla, wrote a considerable volume against these same persons, and other books <i>Against the nations and the Jews</i> and addressed an <i>Apology</i> to the then ruling emperors. He flourished in the reign of Marcus Antoninus and Commodus.</p>
