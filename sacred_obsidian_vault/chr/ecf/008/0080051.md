 </p><p class="c35"><a name="v.iv.xii-p1"></a> <span class="c1"><a name="v.iv.xii-p1.1"></a>Chapter XII.—Solomons Infatuation Through Women.</span></p> 
 <p class="c21"><a name="v.iv.xii-p2"></a>Hast thou not read the history of Solomon, the son of David, the man to whom God gave wisdom, and knowledge, and largeness of mind, <a name="fr_483"></a><a href="#fn_483"><font size="1">484</font></a>
  and riches, and much glory, beyond all men?  Yet this same man, through women, came to ruin, <a name="fr_484"></a><a href="#fn_484"><font size="1">485</font></a>
  and departed from the Lord.</p>
