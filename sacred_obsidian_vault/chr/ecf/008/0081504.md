 </p><p class="c34"><a name="x.v.vii-p1"></a> <span class="c1"><a name="x.v.vii-p1.1"></a>VI. <a name="fr_3601"></a><a href="#fn_3601"><font size="1">3602</font></a>
 </span></p> 
 <p class="c21"><a name="x.v.vii-p3"></a>He that bore up the earth was borne up on a tree.  The Lord was subjected to ignominy with naked body—God put to death, the King of Israel slain!</p>
