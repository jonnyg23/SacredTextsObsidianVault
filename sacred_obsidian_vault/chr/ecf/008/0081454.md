 <p class="c21"><a name="ix.v.iv-p2"></a>Addæus <a name="fr_2947"></a><a href="#fn_2947"><font size="1">2948</font></a>
  preached at Edessa and in Mesopotamia (he was from Paneus <a name="fr_2948"></a><a href="#fn_2948"><font size="1">2949</font></a>
 ) in the days of Abgar the king.  And, when he was among the Zophenians, Severus the son of Abgar sent and slew him at Agel Hasna, as also a young man his disciple.</p>
