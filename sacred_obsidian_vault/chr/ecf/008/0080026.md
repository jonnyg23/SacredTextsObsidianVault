 <p class="c34"><a name="v.iii.i-p3"></a><span class="c6"><a name="v.iii.i-p3.1"></a>The First Epistle of the Blessed Clement, the Disciple of Peter the Apostle.</span></p> 
 <p class="c35"><a name="v.iii.i-p4"></a><span class="c1"><a name="v.iii.i-p4.1"></a>Chapter I.—The Salutation.</span></p> 
 <p class="c21"><a name="v.iii.i-p5"></a><span class="c9"><a name="v.iii.i-p5.1"></a>To</span> all those who love and cherish their life <i>which is</i> in Christ through God the Father, and obey the truth of God in hope of eternal life; to those who bear affection towards their brethren and towards their neighbours in the love of God; to the blessed <i>brother</i> virgins, <a name="fr_291"></a><a href="#fn_291"><font size="1">292</font></a>
  who devote themselves to preserve virginity “for the sake of the kingdom of heaven;” <a name="fr_292"></a><a href="#fn_292"><font size="1">293</font></a>
  and to the holy <i>sister</i> virgins:  the peace which is in God. <a name="fr_293"></a><a href="#fn_293"><font size="1">294</font></a>
 </p>
