 <p class="c35"><a name="x.v.x-p2"></a><span class="c1"><a name="x.v.x-p2.1"></a>From the Same Apology. <a name="fr_3619"></a><a href="#fn_3619"><font size="1">3620</font></a>
 </span></p> 
 <p class="c21"><a name="x.v.x-p4"></a>We are not those who pay homage to stones, that are without sensation; but of the only God, who is before all and over all, and, moreover, we are worshippers of His Christ, who is veritably God the Word <a name="fr_3620"></a><a href="#fn_3620"><font size="1">3621</font></a>
  <i>existing</i> before all time.</p>
