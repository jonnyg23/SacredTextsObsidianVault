 </p><p class="c35"><a name="vi.iv.vi.lvii-p1"></a> <span class="c1"><a name="vi.iv.vi.lvii-p1.1"></a>Chapter LVII.—Teaching of Christ.</span></p> 
 <p class="c21"><a name="vi.iv.vi.lvii-p2"></a>“But to those who are persuaded that He is evil, as the Scriptures say, He said, Call not <a name="page_249"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 249</font></a> <a name="vi.iv.vi.lvii-Page_249"></a>me good, for One <i>only</i> is good. <a name="fr_1015"></a><a href="#fn_1015"><font size="1">1016</font></a>
   And again, Be ye good and merciful, as your Father in the heavens, who makes the sun rise on good and evil men, and brings rain upon just and unjust. <a name="fr_1016"></a><a href="#fn_1016"><font size="1">1017</font></a>
   But to those who were misled to imagine many gods, as the Scriptures say, He said, Hear, O Israel; the Lord your God is one Lord.” <a name="fr_1017"></a><a href="#fn_1017"><font size="1">1018</font></a>
 </p>
