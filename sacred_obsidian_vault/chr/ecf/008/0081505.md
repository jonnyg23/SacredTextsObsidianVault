 <p class="c42"><a name="x.v.viii-p4"></a><span class="c1"><a name="x.v.viii-p4.1"></a>From the Work on the Passover. <a name="fr_3603"></a><a href="#fn_3603"><font size="1">3604</font></a>
 </span></p> 
 <p class="c21"><a name="x.v.viii-p6"></a>When Servilius Paulus was proconsul of Asia, at the time that Sagaris <a name="fr_3604"></a><a href="#fn_3604"><font size="1">3605</font></a>
  suffered martyrdom, there arose a great controversy at Laodicea concerning <i>the time of the celebration of</i> the Passover, which on that occasion had happened to fall at the proper season; <a name="fr_3605"></a><a href="#fn_3605"><font size="1">3606</font></a>
  and this <i>treatise</i> was <i>then</i> written. <a name="fr_3606"></a><a href="#fn_3606"><font size="1">3607</font></a>
 </p>
