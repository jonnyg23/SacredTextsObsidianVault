 </p><p class="c35"><a name="vi.iv.xiv.xxiv-p1"></a> <span class="c1"><a name="vi.iv.xiv.xxiv-p1.1"></a>Chapter XXIV.—“Born of Water.”</span></p> 
 <p class="c21"><a name="vi.iv.xiv.xxiv-p2"></a>“And now from inferior things learn the cause of all, reasoning that water makes all things, and water receives the production of its movement from spirit, and the spirit has its beginning from the God of all.  And thus you ought to have reasoned, in order that by reason you might attain to God, that, knowing your origin, and being born again by the first-born water, you may be constituted heir of the parents who have begotten you to incorruption.</p>
