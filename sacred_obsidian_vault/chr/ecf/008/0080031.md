 <p> <a name="v.iii.vi"></a>
 </p><p class="c35"><a name="v.iii.vi-p1"></a> <span class="c1"><a name="v.iii.vi-p1.1"></a>Chapter VI.—Divinity of Virginity.</span></p> 
 <p class="c21"><a name="v.iii.vi-p2"></a>The womb of a holy virgin <a name="fr_337"></a><a href="#fn_337"><font size="1">338</font></a>
  carried our Lord Jesus Christ, the Son of God; and the body <a name="page_57"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 57</font></a> <a name="v.iii.vi-Page_57"></a>which our Lord wore, and in which He carried on the conflict in this world, He put on from a holy virgin.  From this, therefore, understand the greatness and dignity of virginity.  Dost thou wish to be a Christian?  Imitate Christ in everything.  John, the ambassador, he who came before our Lord, he “than whom there was not a greater among those born of women,” <a name="fr_338"></a><a href="#fn_338"><font size="1">339</font></a>
  the holy messenger of our Lord, was a virgin.  Imitate, therefore, the ambassador of our Lord, and be his follower <a name="fr_339"></a><a href="#fn_339"><font size="1">340</font></a>
  in every thing.  <i>That</i> John, again, who “reclined on the bosom of our Lord, and whom He greatly loved,” <a name="fr_340"></a><a href="#fn_340"><font size="1">341</font></a>
 —he, too, was a holy person. <a name="fr_341"></a><a href="#fn_341"><font size="1">342</font></a>
   For it was not without reason that our Lord loved him.  Paul, also, and Barnabas, and Timothy, with all the others, “whose names are written in the book of life,” <a name="fr_342"></a><a href="#fn_342"><font size="1">343</font></a>
 —these, I say, all cherished and loved sanctity, <a name="fr_343"></a><a href="#fn_343"><font size="1">344</font></a>
  and ran in the contest, and finished their course without blemish, as imitators of Christ, and as sons of the living God.  Moreover, also, Elijah and Elisha, and many other holy men, we find to have lived a holy <a name="fr_344"></a><a href="#fn_344"><font size="1">345</font></a>
  and spotless life.  If, therefore, thou desirest to be like these, imitate them with all thy power.  For the Scripture has said, “The elders who are among you, honour; and, seeing their manner of life and conduct, imitate their faith.” <a name="fr_345"></a><a href="#fn_345"><font size="1">346</font></a>
   And again it saith, “Imitate me, my brethren, as I <i>imitate</i> Christ.” <a name="fr_346"></a><a href="#fn_346"><font size="1">347</font></a>
 </p>
