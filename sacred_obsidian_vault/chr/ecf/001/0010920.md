 <p> <a name="ix.viii.xlv"></a>  </p><h3 align="center"><a name="ix.viii.xlv-p0.1"></a>XLV.</h3>  
 <p><a name="ix.viii.xlv-p1"></a>“And <a name="fr_4888"></a><a href="#fn_4888"><font size="1">4889</font></a>
  Balaam the son of Beor they slew with the sword.” <a name="fr_4889"></a><a href="#fn_4889"><font size="1">4890</font></a>
  For, speaking no longer by the Spirit of God, but setting up another law of fornication contrary to the law of God, <a name="fr_4890"></a><a href="#fn_4890"><font size="1">4891</font></a>
  this man shall no longer be reckoned as a prophet, but as a soothsayer. For, as he did not continue in the commandment of God, he received the just reward of his evil devices.</p>
