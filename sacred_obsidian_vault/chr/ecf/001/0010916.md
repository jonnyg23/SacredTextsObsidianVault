 <p> <a name="ix.viii.xli"></a>  </p><h3 align="center"><a name="ix.viii.xli-p0.1"></a>XLI.</h3>  
 <p><a name="ix.viii.xli-p1"></a>This <a name="fr_4883"></a><a href="#fn_4883"><font size="1">4884</font></a>
  indicates the persecution against the Church set on foot by the nations who still continue in unbelief. But he (Samson) who suffered those things, trusted that there would be a retaliation against those waging this war. But retaliation through what means? First of all, by his betaking himself to the Rock <a name="fr_4884"></a><a href="#fn_4884"><font size="1">4885</font></a>
  not cognizable to the senses; <a name="fr_4885"></a><a href="#fn_4885"><font size="1">4886</font></a>
  secondly, by the finding of the jaw-bone of an ass. Now the type of the jaw-bone is the body of Christ.</p>
