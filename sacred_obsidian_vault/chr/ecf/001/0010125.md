 <p> <a name="v.ii.ii"></a>  <a name="page_50"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 50</font></a> <a name="v.ii.ii-Page_50"></a>  </p><h3 align="center"><a name="v.ii.ii-p0.1"></a>Chapter II.—Congratulations and entreaties.</h3>  
 <p><a name="v.ii.ii-p1"></a><a name="v.ii.ii-p1.1"></a><a name="v.ii.ii-p1.2"></a>As to my fellow-servant Burrhus, your deacon in regard to God and blessed in all things, <a name="fr_500"></a><a href="#fn_500"><font size="1">501</font></a>
  I beg that he may continue longer, both for your honour and that of your bishop. <a name="v.ii.ii-p2.1"></a>And Crocus also, worthy both of God and you, whom I have received as the manifestation <a name="fr_501"></a><a href="#fn_501"><font size="1">502</font></a>
  of your love, hath in all things refreshed <a name="fr_502"></a><a href="#fn_502"><font size="1">503</font></a>
  me, as the Father of our Lord Jesus Christ shall also refresh <a name="fr_503"></a><a href="#fn_503"><font size="1">504</font></a>
  him; together with Onesimus, and Burrhus, and Euplus, and Fronto, by means of whom, I have, as to love, beheld all of you. May I always have joy of you, if indeed I be worthy of it. <a name="v.ii.ii-p5.2"></a><a name="v.ii.ii-p5.3"></a>It is therefore befitting that you should in every way glorify Jesus Christ, who hath glorified you, that by a unanimous obedience “ye may be perfectly joined together in the same mind, and in the same judgment, and may all speak the same thing concerning the same thing,” <a name="fr_504"></a><a href="#fn_504"><font size="1">505</font></a>
  and that, being subject to the bishop and the presbytery, ye may in all respects be sanctified.</p>  
 <p><a name="v.ii.ii-p7"></a><a name="v.ii.ii-p7.1"></a>As to our fellow-servant Burrhus, your deacon in regard to God and blessed in all things, I pray that he may continue blameless for the honour of the Church, and of your most blessed bishop. <a name="v.ii.ii-p7.2"></a>Crocus also, worthy both of God and you, whom we have received as the manifestation <a name="fr_505"></a><a href="#fn_505"><font size="1">506</font></a>
  of your love to us, hath in all things refreshed <a name="fr_506"></a><a href="#fn_506"><font size="1">507</font></a>
  me, and “hath not been ashamed of my chain,” <a name="fr_507"></a><a href="#fn_507"><font size="1">508</font></a>
  as the Father of our Lord Jesus Christ will also refresh <a name="fr_508"></a><a href="#fn_508"><font size="1">509</font></a>
  him; together with Onesimus, and Burrhus, and Euplus, and Fronto, by means of whom I have, as to love, beheld all of you. May I always have joy of you, if indeed I be worthy of it. <a name="v.ii.ii-p11.2"></a><a name="v.ii.ii-p11.3"></a>It is therefore befitting that you should in every way glorify Jesus Christ, who hath glorified you, that by a unanimous obedience “ye may be perfectly joined together in the same mind and in the same judgment, and may all speak the same thing concerning the same thing,” <a name="fr_509"></a><a href="#fn_509"><font size="1">510</font></a>
  and that, being subject to the bishop and the presbytery, ye may in all respects be sanctified.</p>
