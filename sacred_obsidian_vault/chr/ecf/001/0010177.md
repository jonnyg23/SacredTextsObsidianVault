 <p> <a name="v.v.i"></a>  </p><h3 align="center"><a name="v.v.i-p0.1"></a>Chapter I.—As a prisoner, I hope to see you.</h3>  
 <p><a name="v.v.i-p1"></a><a name="v.v.i-p1.1"></a><span class="upper"><a name="v.v.i-p1.2"></a>Through</span> prayer <a name="fr_821"></a><a href="#fn_821"><font size="1">822</font></a>
  to God I have obtained the privilege of seeing your most worthy faces, <a name="fr_822"></a><a href="#fn_822"><font size="1">823</font></a>
  and have even <a name="fr_823"></a><a href="#fn_823"><font size="1">824</font></a>
  been granted more than I requested; for I hope as a prisoner in Christ Jesus to salute you, if indeed it be the will of God that I be thought worthy of attaining unto the end. For the beginning has been well ordered,  <a name="page_74"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 74</font></a> <a name="v.v.i-Page_74"></a>  if I may obtain grace to cling to <a name="fr_824"></a><a href="#fn_824"><font size="1">825</font></a>
  my lot without hindrance unto the end. For I am afraid of your love, <a name="fr_825"></a><a href="#fn_825"><font size="1">826</font></a>
  lest it should do me an injury. For it is easy for you to accomplish what you please; but it is difficult for me to attain to God, if ye spare me.</p>  
 <p><a name="v.v.i-p7"></a><a name="v.v.i-p7.1"></a><span class="upper"><a name="v.v.i-p7.2"></a>Through</span> prayer to God I have obtained the privilege of seeing your most worthy faces, <a name="fr_826"></a><a href="#fn_826"><font size="1">827</font></a>
  even as I earnestly begged might be granted me; for as a prisoner in Christ Jesus I hope to salute you, if indeed it be the will [of God] that I be thought worthy of attaining unto the end. For the beginning has been well ordered, if I may obtain grace to cling to <a name="fr_827"></a><a href="#fn_827"><font size="1">828</font></a>
  my lot without hindrance unto the end. For I am afraid of your love, <a name="fr_828"></a><a href="#fn_828"><font size="1">829</font></a>
  lest it should do me an injury. For it is easy for you to accomplish what you please; but it is difficult for me to attain to God, if ye do not spare me, <a name="fr_829"></a><a href="#fn_829"><font size="1">830</font></a>
  under the pretence of carnal affection.</p>
