 <p> <a name="ix.viii.xl"></a>  </p><h3 align="center"><a name="ix.viii.xl-p0.1"></a>XL.</h3>  
 <p><a name="ix.viii.xl-p1"></a><a name="ix.viii.xl-p1.1"></a>“And <a name="fr_4879"></a><a href="#fn_4879"><font size="1">4880</font></a>
  he found the jaw-bone of an ass.” <a name="fr_4880"></a><a href="#fn_4880"><font size="1">4881</font></a>
  It is to be observed that, after [Samson had committed] fornication, the holy Scripture no longer speaks of the things happily accomplished by him in connection with the formula, “The Spirit of the Lord came upon him.” <a name="fr_4881"></a><a href="#fn_4881"><font size="1">4882</font></a>
  For thus, according to the holy apostle, the sin of fornication is perpetrated against the body, as involving also sin against the temple of God. <a name="fr_4882"></a><a href="#fn_4882"><font size="1">4883</font></a>
 </p>
