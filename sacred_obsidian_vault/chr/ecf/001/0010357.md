 <p> <a name="vi.ii.xxi"></a>  </p><h3 align="center"><a name="vi.ii.xxi-p0.1"></a>Chapter XXI.—Conclusion.</h3>  
 <p><a name="vi.ii.xxi-p1"></a><a name="vi.ii.xxi-p1.1"></a>It is well, therefore, <a name="fr_1716"></a><a href="#fn_1716"><font size="1">1717</font></a>
  that he who has learned the judgments of the Lord, as many as have been written, should walk in them. For he who keepeth these shall be glorified in the kingdom of God; but he who chooseth other things <a name="fr_1717"></a><a href="#fn_1717"><font size="1">1718</font></a>
  shall be destroyed with his works. On this account there will be a resurrection, <a name="fr_1718"></a><a href="#fn_1718"><font size="1">1719</font></a>
  on this account a retribution. I beseech you who are superiors, if you will receive any counsel of my good-will, have among yourselves those to whom you may show kindness: do not forsake them. For the day is at hand on which all things shall perish with the evil [one]. The Lord is near, and His reward. Again, and yet again, I beseech you: be good lawgivers <a name="fr_1719"></a><a href="#fn_1719"><font size="1">1720</font></a>
  to one another; continue faithful counsellors of one another; take away from among you all hypocrisy. And may God, who ruleth over all the world, give to you wisdom, intelligence, understanding, knowledge of His judgments, <a name="fr_1720"></a><a href="#fn_1720"><font size="1">1721</font></a>
  with patience. And be ye <a name="fr_1721"></a><a href="#fn_1721"><font size="1">1722</font></a>
  taught of God, inquiring diligently what the Lord asks from you; and do it that ye maybe safe in the day of judgment. <a name="fr_1722"></a><a href="#fn_1722"><font size="1">1723</font></a>
  And if you have any remembrance of what is good, be mindful of me, meditating on these things, in order that both my desire and watchfulness may result in some good. I beseech you, entreating this as a favour. While yet you are in this fair vessel, <a name="fr_1723"></a><a href="#fn_1723"><font size="1">1724</font></a>
  do not fail in any one of those things, <a name="fr_1724"></a><a href="#fn_1724"><font size="1">1725</font></a>
  but unceasingly seek after them, and fulfil every commandment; for these things are worthy. <a name="fr_1725"></a><a href="#fn_1725"><font size="1">1726</font></a>
  Wherefore I have been the more earnest to write to you, as my ability served, <a name="fr_1726"></a><a href="#fn_1726"><font size="1">1727</font></a>
  that I might cheer you. <a name="vi.ii.xxi-p12.1"></a>Farewell, ye children of love and peace. The Lord of glory and of all grace be with your spirit. Amen. <a name="fr_1727"></a><a href="#fn_1727"><font size="1">1728</font></a>
 </p>
