 <p> <a name="v.xi.iii"></a>  </p><h3 align="center"><a name="v.xi.iii-p0.1"></a>Chapter VIII. <a name="fr_1152"></a><a href="#fn_1152"><font size="1">1153</font></a>
  </h3>  
 <p><a name="v.xi.iii-p2"></a>For, so long as there is not implanted in you any one lust which is able to torment you, behold, ye live in God. I rejoice in you, and offer supplication <a name="fr_1153"></a><a href="#fn_1153"><font size="1">1154</font></a>
  on account of you, Ephesians, a Church which is renowned in all ages. For those who are carnal are not able to do spiritual things, nor those that are spiritual carnal things; in like manner as neither can faith [do] those things which are foreign to faith, nor want of faith [do] what belongs to faith. For those things which ye have done in the flesh, even these are spiritual, because ye have done everything in Jesus Christ.</p>
