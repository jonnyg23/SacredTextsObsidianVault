 <p> <a name="v.vii.xii"></a>  </p><h3 align="center"><a name="v.vii.xii-p0.1"></a>Chapter XII.—Salutations.</h3>  
 <p><a name="v.vii.xii-p1"></a><a name="v.vii.xii-p1.1"></a>The love of the brethren at Troas salutes you; whence also I write to  <a name="page_92"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 92</font></a> <a name="v.vii.xii-Page_92"></a>  you by Burrhus, whom ye sent with me, together with the Ephesians, your brethren, and who has in all things refreshed me. And I would that all may imitate him, as being a pattern of a minister <a name="fr_1055"></a><a href="#fn_1055"><font size="1">1056</font></a>
  of God. <a name="v.vii.xii-p2.1"></a>Grace will reward him in all things. I salute your most worthy <a name="fr_1056"></a><a href="#fn_1056"><font size="1">1057</font></a>
  bishop, and your very venerable <a name="fr_1057"></a><a href="#fn_1057"><font size="1">1058</font></a>
  presbytery, and your deacons, my fellow-servants, and all of you individually, as well as generally, in the name of Jesus Christ, and in His flesh and blood, in His passion and resurrection, both corporeal and spiritual, in union with God and you. <a name="fr_1058"></a><a href="#fn_1058"><font size="1">1059</font></a>
  Grace, mercy, peace, and patience, be with you for evermore!</p>  
 <p><a name="v.vii.xii-p6"></a><a name="v.vii.xii-p6.1"></a>The love of your brethren at Troas salutes you; whence also I write to you by Burgus, whom ye sent with me, together with the Ephesians, your brethren, and who has in all things refreshed me. And I would that all may imitate him, as being a pattern of a minister of God. <a name="v.vii.xii-p6.2"></a>The grace of the Lord will reward him in all things. <a name="v.vii.xii-p6.3"></a>I salute your most worthy bishop Polycarp, and your venerable presbytery, and your Christ-bearing deacons, my fellow-servants, and all of you individually, as well as generally, in the name of Christ Jesus, and in His flesh and blood, in His passion and resurrection, both corporeal and spiritual, in union with God and you. Grace, mercy, peace, and patience, be with you in Christ for evermore!</p>
