 <p> <a name="ix.viii.xxvi"></a>  </p><h3 align="center"><a name="ix.viii.xxvi-p0.1"></a>XXVI.</h3>  
 <p><a name="ix.viii.xxvi-p1"></a><a name="ix.viii.xxvi-p1.1"></a>Know <a name="fr_4839"></a><a href="#fn_4839"><font size="1">4840</font></a>
  thou that every man is either empty or full. For if he has not the Holy Spirit, he has no knowledge of the Creator; he has not received Jesus Christ the Life; he knows not the Father who is in heaven; if he does not live after the dictates of reason, after the heavenly law, he is not a sober-minded person, nor does he act uprightly: such an one is empty. If, on the other hand, he receives God, who says, “I will dwell with them, and walk in them, and I will be their God,” <a name="fr_4840"></a><a href="#fn_4840"><font size="1">4841</font></a>
  such an one is not empty, but full.</p>
