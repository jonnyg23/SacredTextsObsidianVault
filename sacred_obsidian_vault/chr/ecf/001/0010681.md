 <p> <a name="viii.ix.xiv"></a>  </p><h3 align="center"><a name="viii.ix.xiv-p0.1"></a>XIV.</h3>  
 <p><a name="viii.ix.xiv-p1"></a>Neither shall light ever be darkness as long as light exists, nor shall the truth of the things pertaining to us be controverted. For truth is that than which nothing is more powerful. Every one who might speak the truth, and speaks it not, shall be judged by God.—<i>Manuscript and works of</i> <span class="upper"><a name="viii.ix.xiv-p1.1"></a>John of Damascus</span>.</p>
