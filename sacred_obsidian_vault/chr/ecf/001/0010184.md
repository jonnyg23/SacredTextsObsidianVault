 <p> <a name="v.v.viii"></a>  </p><h3 align="center"><a name="v.v.viii-p0.1"></a>Chapter VIII.—Be ye favourable to me.</h3>  
 <p><a name="v.v.viii-p1"></a>I no longer wish to live after the manner of men, and my desire shall be fulfilled if ye consent. Be ye willing, then, that ye also may have your desires fulfilled. I entreat you in this brief letter; do ye give credit to me. Jesus Christ will reveal these things to you, [so that ye shall know] that I speak truly. He <a name="fr_870"></a><a href="#fn_870"><font size="1">871</font></a>
  is the mouth altogether free from falsehood, by which the Father has truly spoken. <a name="v.v.viii-p2.1"></a>Pray ye for me, that I may attain [the object of my desire]. I have not written to you according to the flesh, but according to the will of God. If I shall suffer, ye have wished [well] to me; but if I am rejected, ye have hated me.</p>  
 <p><a name="v.v.viii-p3"></a>I no longer wish to live after the manner of men, and my desire shall be fulfilled if ye consent. “I am crucified with Christ: nevertheless I live; yet no longer I, since Christ liveth in me.” <a name="fr_871"></a><a href="#fn_871"><font size="1">872</font></a>
  I entreat you in this brief letter: do not refuse me; believe me that I love Jesus, who was delivered [to death] for my sake. “What shall I render to the Lord for all His benefits towards me?” <a name="fr_872"></a><a href="#fn_872"><font size="1">873</font></a>
  Now God, even the Father, and the Lord Jesus Christ, shall reveal these things to you, [so that ye shall know] that I speak truly. <a name="v.v.viii-p5.2"></a>And do ye pray along with me, that I may attain my aim in the Holy Spirit. I have not written to you according to the flesh, but according to the will of God. If I shall suffer, ye have loved me; but if I am rejected, ye have hated me.</p>
