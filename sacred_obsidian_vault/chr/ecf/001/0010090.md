 <p> <a name="iv.ii.viii"></a>  </p><h3 align="center"><a name="iv.ii.viii-p0.1"></a>Chapter VIII.—Persevere in hope and patience.</h3>  
 <p><a name="iv.ii.viii-p1"></a><a name="iv.ii.viii-p1.1"></a>Let us then continually persevere in our hope, and the earnest of our righteousness, which is Jesus Christ, “who bore our sins in His own body on the tree,” <a name="fr_383"></a><a href="#fn_383"><font size="1">384</font></a>
  “who did no sin, neither was guile found in His mouth,” <a name="fr_384"></a><a href="#fn_384"><font size="1">385</font></a>
  but endured all things for us, that we might live in Him. <a name="fr_385"></a><a href="#fn_385"><font size="1">386</font></a>
  <a name="iv.ii.viii-p4.2"></a><a name="iv.ii.viii-p4.3"></a>Let us then be imitators of His patience; and if we suffer <a name="fr_386"></a><a href="#fn_386"><font size="1">387</font></a>
  for His names sake, let us glorify Him. <a name="fr_387"></a><a href="#fn_387"><font size="1">388</font></a>
  For He has set us this example <a name="fr_388"></a><a href="#fn_388"><font size="1">389</font></a>
  in Himself, and we have believed that such is the case.</p>
