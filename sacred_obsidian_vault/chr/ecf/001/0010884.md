 <p> <a name="ix.viii.ix"></a>  </p><h3 align="center"><a name="ix.viii.ix-p0.1"></a>IX.</h3>  
 <p><a name="ix.viii.ix-p1"></a>Ever, <a name="fr_4812"></a><a href="#fn_4812"><font size="1">4813</font></a>
  indeed, speaking well of the deserving, but never ill of the undeserving, we also shall attain to the glory and kingdom of God.</p>    
 <hr>
 <h3 align="CENTER">Footnotes</h3>
 <a name="fn_4812"></a><a href="0010884.htm#fr_4812">570:4813</a> <a name="ix.viii.ix-p1.1"></a>
 <p class="endnote"><a name="ix.viii.ix-p2"></a> This fragment and the next three are from the <i>Parallela</i> of John of Damascus. Frag. ix. x. xii. seem to be quotations from the treatise of Irenæus on the resurrection. No. xi. is extracted from his <i>Miscellaneous Dissertations</i>, a work mentioned by Eusebius, <span class="Greek"><a name="ix.viii.ix-p2.1"></a>βιβλίον τι διαλεξέων διαφόρων</span>.</p>
