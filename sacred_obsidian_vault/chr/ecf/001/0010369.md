 <p> <a name="vii.ii.ix"></a>  </p><h3 align="center"><a name="vii.ii.ix-p0.1"></a>IX. <a name="fr_1762"></a><a href="#fn_1762"><font size="1">1763</font></a>
 </h3>  
 <p><a name="vii.ii.ix-p2"></a>Taking occasion from Papias of Hierapolis, the illustrious, a disciple of the apostle who leaned on the bosom of Christ, and Clemens, and Pantænus the priest of [the Church] of the Alexandrians, and the wise Ammonius, the ancient and first expositors, who agreed with each other, who understood the work of the six days as referring to Christ and the whole Church.</p>
