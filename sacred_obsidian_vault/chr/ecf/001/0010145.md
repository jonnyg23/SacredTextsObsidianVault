 <p> <a name="v.ii.xxii"></a>  </p><h3 align="center"><a name="v.ii.xxii-p0.1"></a>Chapter XXI.—Conclusion.</h3>  
 <p><a name="v.ii.xxii-p1"></a><a name="v.ii.xxii-p1.1"></a>My soul be for yours and theirs <a name="fr_626"></a><a href="#fn_626"><font size="1">627</font></a>
  whom, for the honour of God, ye have sent to Smyrna; whence also I write to you, giving thanks unto the Lord, and loving Polycarp even as I do you. <a name="v.ii.xxii-p2.1"></a>Remember me, as Jesus Christ also remembered you. <a name="v.ii.xxii-p2.2"></a>Pray ye for the Church which is in Syria, whence I am led bound to Rome, being the last of the faithful who are there, even as I have been thought worthy to be chosen <a name="fr_627"></a><a href="#fn_627"><font size="1">628</font></a>
  to show forth the honour of God. <a name="v.ii.xxii-p3.1"></a>Farewell in God the Father, and in Jesus Christ, our common hope.</p>  
 <p><a name="v.ii.xxii-p4"></a><a name="v.ii.xxii-p4.1"></a>My soul be for yours and theirs <a name="fr_628"></a><a href="#fn_628"><font size="1">629</font></a>
  whom, for the honour of God, ye have sent to Smyrna; whence also I write to you, giving thanks to the Lord, and loving Polycarp even as I do you. <a name="v.ii.xxii-p5.1"></a>Remember me, as Jesus Christ also remembers you, who is blessed for evermore. <a name="v.ii.xxii-p5.2"></a>Pray ye for the Church of Antioch which is in Syria, whence I am led bound to Rome, being the last of the faithful that are there, who <a name="fr_629"></a><a href="#fn_629"><font size="1">630</font></a>
  yet have been thought worthy to carry these chains to the honour of God. <a name="v.ii.xxii-p6.1"></a>Fare ye well in God the Father, and the Lord Jesus Christ, our common hope, and in the Holy Ghost. Fare ye well. Amen. Grace [be with you]. <a name="fr_630"></a><a href="#fn_630"><font size="1">631</font></a>
 </p>
