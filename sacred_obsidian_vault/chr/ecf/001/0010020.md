 <p> <a name="ii.ii.xv"></a>  <a name="page_9"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 9</font></a> <a name="ii.ii.xv-Page_9"></a>  </p><h3 align="center"><a name="ii.ii.xv-p0.1"></a>Chapter XV.—We must adhere to those who cultivate peace, not to those who merely pretend to do so.</h3>  
 <p><a name="ii.ii.xv-p1"></a><a name="ii.ii.xv-p1.1"></a>Let us cleave, therefore, to those who cultivate peace with godliness, and not to those who hypocritically profess to desire it. For [the Scripture] saith in a certain place, “This people honoureth Me with their lips, but their heart is far from Me.” <a name="fr_59"></a><a href="#fn_59"><font size="1">60</font></a>
  And again: “They bless with their mouth, but curse with their heart.” <a name="fr_60"></a><a href="#fn_60"><font size="1">61</font></a>
  And again it saith, “They loved Him with their mouth, and lied to Him with their tongue; but their heart was not right with Him, neither were they faithful in His covenant.” <a name="fr_61"></a><a href="#fn_61"><font size="1">62</font></a>
  “Let the deceitful lips become silent,” <a name="fr_62"></a><a href="#fn_62"><font size="1">63</font></a>
  [and “let the Lord destroy all the lying lips, <a name="fr_63"></a><a href="#fn_63"><font size="1">64</font></a>
 ] and the boastful tongue of those who have said, Let us magnify our tongue; our lips are our own; who is lord over us? For the oppression of the poor, and for the sighing of the needy, will I now arise, saith the Lord: I will place him in safety; I will deal confidently with him.” <a name="fr_64"></a><a href="#fn_64"><font size="1">65</font></a>
 </p>
