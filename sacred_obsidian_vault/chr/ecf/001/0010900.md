 <p> <a name="ix.viii.xxv"></a>  </p><h3 align="center"><a name="ix.viii.xxv-p0.1"></a>XXV.</h3>  
 <p><a name="ix.viii.xxv-p1"></a><a name="ix.viii.xxv-p1.1"></a>“To inflict vengeance from the Lord on Midian.” <a name="fr_4836"></a><a href="#fn_4836"><font size="1">4837</font></a>
  For this man (Balaam), when he speaks no longer in the Spirit of God, but contrary to Gods law, by setting up a different law with regard to fornication, <a name="fr_4837"></a><a href="#fn_4837"><font size="1">4838</font></a>
  is certainly not then to be counted as a prophet, but as a soothsayer. For he who did not keep to the commandment of God, received the just recompense of his own evil devices. <a name="fr_4838"></a><a href="#fn_4838"><font size="1">4839</font></a>
 </p>
