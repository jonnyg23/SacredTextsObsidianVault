 <p> <a name="v.v.vi"></a>  </p><h3 align="center"><a name="v.v.vi-p0.1"></a>Chapter VI.—By death I shall attain true life.</h3>  
 <p><a name="v.v.vi-p1"></a><a name="v.v.vi-p1.1"></a><a name="v.v.vi-p1.2"></a>All the pleasures of the world, and all the kingdoms of this earth, <a name="fr_859"></a><a href="#fn_859"><font size="1">860</font></a>
  shall profit me nothing. <a name="v.v.vi-p2.1"></a>It is better for me to die in behalf of <a name="fr_860"></a><a href="#fn_860"><font size="1">861</font></a>
  Jesus Christ, than to reign over all the ends of the earth. “For what shall a man be profited, if he gain the whole world, but lose his own soul?” <a name="fr_861"></a><a href="#fn_861"><font size="1">862</font></a>
  Him I seek, who died for us: Him I desire, who rose again for our sake. This is the gain which is laid up for me. Pardon me, brethren: do not hinder me from living, do not wish to keep me in a state of death; <a name="fr_862"></a><a href="#fn_862"><font size="1">863</font></a>
  and while I desire to belong to God, do not ye give me over to the world. Suffer me to obtain pure light: when I have gone thither, I shall indeed be a man of God. <a name="v.v.vi-p5.1"></a>Permit me to be an imitator of the passion of my God. If any one has Him within himself, let him consider what I desire, and let him have sympathy with me, as knowing how I am straitened.</p>  
 <p><a name="v.v.vi-p6"></a><a name="v.v.vi-p6.1"></a><a name="v.v.vi-p6.2"></a>All the ends of the world, and all the kingdoms of this earth, <a name="fr_863"></a><a href="#fn_863"><font size="1">864</font></a>
  shall profit me nothing. <a name="v.v.vi-p7.1"></a>It is better for me to die for the sake of Jesus Christ, than to reign over all the ends of the earth. “For what is a man profited, if he gain the whole world, but lose his own soul?” <a name="v.v.vi-p7.2"></a>I long after the Lord, the Son of the true God and Father, even Jesus Christ. Him I seek, who died for us and rose again. Pardon me, brethren: do not hinder me in attaining to life; for Jesus is the life of believers. Do not wish to keep me in a state of death, <a name="fr_864"></a><a href="#fn_864"><font size="1">865</font></a>
  for life without Christ is death. While I desire to belong to God, do not ye give me over to the world. Suffer me to obtain pure light: when I have gone thither, I shall indeed be a man of God. <a name="v.v.vi-p8.1"></a>Permit me to be an imitator of the passion of Christ, my God. If any one has Him within himself, let him consider what I desire, and let him have sympathy with me, as knowing how I am straitened.</p>
