 <p><a name="v.xv-p1"></a><a name="v.xv-p1.1"></a><a name="v.xv-p1.2"></a><i>Ignatius, who is also called Theophorus, to the Church sojourning in Syria, which has obtained mercy from God, and been elected by Christ, and which first</i> <a name="fr_1214"></a><a href="#fn_1214"><font size="1">1215</font></a>
 <i> received the name Christ, [wishes] happiness in God the Father, and the Lord Jesus Christ.</i></p>
