 <p> <a name="iv.ii.xi"></a>  </p><h3 align="center"><a name="iv.ii.xi-p0.1"></a>Chapter XI.—Expression of grief on account of Valens.</h3>  
 <p><a name="iv.ii.xi-p1"></a><a name="iv.ii.xi-p1.1"></a><a name="iv.ii.xi-p1.2"></a>I am greatly grieved for Valens, who was once a presbyter among you, because he so little understands the place that was given him [in the Church]. <a name="iv.ii.xi-p1.3"></a><a name="iv.ii.xi-p1.4"></a>I exhort you, therefore, that ye abstain from covetousness, <a name="fr_396"></a><a href="#fn_396"><font size="1">397</font></a>
  and that ye be chaste and truthful. <a name="iv.ii.xi-p2.2"></a>“Abstain from every form of evil.” <a name="fr_397"></a><a href="#fn_397"><font size="1">398</font></a>
  For if a man cannot govern himself in such matters, how shall he enjoin them on others? If a man does not keep himself from covetousness, <a name="fr_398"></a><a href="#fn_398"><font size="1">399</font></a>
  he shall be defiled by idolatry, and shall be judged as one of the heathen. But who of us are ignorant of the judgment of the Lord? “Do we not know that the saints shall judge the world?” <a name="fr_399"></a><a href="#fn_399"><font size="1">400</font></a>
  as Paul teaches. <a name="iv.ii.xi-p5.2"></a>But I have neither seen nor heard of any such thing among you, in the midst of whom the blessed Paul laboured, and who are commended <a name="fr_400"></a><a href="#fn_400"><font size="1">401</font></a>
  in the beginning of his Epistle. For he boasts of you in all those Churches which alone then knew the Lord; but we [of Smyrna] had not yet known Him. I am deeply grieved, therefore, brethren, for him (Valens) and his wife; to whom may the Lord grant true repentance! And be ye then moderate in regard to this matter, and “do not count such as enemies,” <a name="fr_401"></a><a href="#fn_401"><font size="1">402</font></a>
  but call them back as suffering and straying members, that ye may save your whole body. For by so acting ye shall edify yourselves. <a name="fr_402"></a><a href="#fn_402"><font size="1">403</font></a>
 </p>
