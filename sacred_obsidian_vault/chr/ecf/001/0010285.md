 <p> <a name="v.xvi.vii"></a>  </p><h3 align="center"><a name="v.xvi.vii-p0.1"></a>Chapter VII.—Solemn charge to Hero, as future bishop of Antioch.</h3>  
 <p><a name="v.xvi.vii-p1"></a>Keep God in remembrance, and thou shalt never sin. Be not double-minded <a name="fr_1289"></a><a href="#fn_1289"><font size="1">1290</font></a>
  in thy prayers; for blessed is he who doubteth not. For I believe in the Father of the Lord Jesus Christ, and in His only-begotten Son, that God will show me, Hero, upon my throne. Add speed, therefore, <a name="fr_1290"></a><a href="#fn_1290"><font size="1">1291</font></a>
  to thy course. I charge thee before the God of the universe, and before Christ, and in the presence of the Holy Spirit, and of the ministering ranks [of angels], keep in safety that deposit which I and Christ have committed to thee, and do not judge thyself unworthy of those things which have been shown by God [to me] concerning thee. I hand over to thee the Church of Antioch. I have commended you to Polycarp in the Lord Jesus Christ.</p>
