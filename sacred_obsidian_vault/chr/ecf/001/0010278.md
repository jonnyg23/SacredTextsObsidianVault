 <p> <a name="v.xvi"></a>  <a name="page_113"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 113</font></a> <a name="v.xvi-Page_113"></a>  </p><h2 align="center"><a name="v.xvi-p0.1"></a>The Epistle of Ignatius to Hero, a Deacon of Antioch</h2>    
 <p><a name="v.xvi-p1"></a><a name="v.xvi-p1.1"></a><a name="v.xvi-p1.2"></a><i>Ignatius, who is also called Theophorus, to Hero, the deacon of Christ, and the servant of God, a man honoured by God, and most dearly loved as well as esteemed, who carries Christ and the Spirit within him, and who is mine own son in faith and love: Grace, mercy, and peace from Almighty God, and from Christ Jesus our Lord, His only-begotten Son, “who gave Himself for our sins, that He might deliver us from the present evil world,”</i> <a name="fr_1264"></a><a href="#fn_1264"><font size="1">1265</font></a>
 <i> and preserve us unto His heavenly kingdom.</i></p>
