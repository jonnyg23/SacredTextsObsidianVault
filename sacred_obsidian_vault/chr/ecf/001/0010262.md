 <p> <a name="v.xiv.x"></a>  </p><h3 align="center"><a name="v.xiv.x-p0.1"></a>Chapter X.—Salutations.</h3>  
 <p><a name="v.xiv.x-p1"></a><a name="v.xiv.x-p1.1"></a>May I enjoy your prayers! Pray ye that may attain to Jesus. I commend unto you the Church which is at Antioch. The Churches of Philippi, <a name="fr_1212"></a><a href="#fn_1212"><font size="1">1213</font></a>
  whence also I write to you, salute you. Philo, your deacon, to whom also I give thanks as one who has zealously ministered to me in all things, salutes you. Agathopus, the deacon from Syria, who follows me in Christ, salutes you. “Salute ye one another with a holy kiss.” <a name="fr_1213"></a><a href="#fn_1213"><font size="1">1214</font></a>
  I salute you all, both male and female, who are in Christ. Fare ye well in body, and soul, and in one Spirit; and do not ye forget me. The Lord be with you!</p>
