 <p> <a name="iv.ii.ii"></a>  </p><h3 align="center"><a name="iv.ii.ii-p0.1"></a>Chapter II.—An exhortation to virtue.</h3>  
 <p><a name="iv.ii.ii-p1"></a><a name="iv.ii.ii-p1.1"></a><a name="iv.ii.ii-p1.2"></a>“Wherefore, girding up your loins,” <a name="fr_343"></a><a href="#fn_343"><font size="1">344</font></a>
  “serve the Lord in fear” <a name="fr_344"></a><a href="#fn_344"><font size="1">345</font></a>
  and truth, as those who have forsaken the vain, empty talk and error of the multitude, and “believed in Him who raised up our Lord Jesus Christ from the dead, and gave Him glory,” <a name="fr_345"></a><a href="#fn_345"><font size="1">346</font></a>
  and a throne at His right hand. To Him all things <a name="fr_346"></a><a href="#fn_346"><font size="1">347</font></a>
  in heaven and on earth are subject. Him every spirit serves. <a name="iv.ii.ii-p5.3"></a>He comes as the Judge of the living and the dead. <a name="fr_347"></a><a href="#fn_347"><font size="1">348</font></a>
  His blood will God require of those who do not believe in Him. <a name="fr_348"></a><a href="#fn_348"><font size="1">349</font></a>
  <a name="iv.ii.ii-p7.1"></a><a name="iv.ii.ii-p7.2"></a>But He who raised Him up from the dead will raise <a name="fr_349"></a><a href="#fn_349"><font size="1">350</font></a>
  up us also, if we do His will, and walk in His commandments, and love what He loved, keeping ourselves from all unrighteousness, covetousness, love of money, evil speaking, false witness; “not rendering evil for evil, or railing for railing,” <a name="fr_350"></a><a href="#fn_350"><font size="1">351</font></a>
  or blow for blow, or cursing for cursing, but being mindful of what the Lord said in His teaching: “Judge not, that ye be not judged; <a name="fr_351"></a><a href="#fn_351"><font size="1">352</font></a>
  forgive, and it shall be forgiven unto you; <a name="fr_352"></a><a href="#fn_352"><font size="1">353</font></a>
  be merciful, that ye may obtain mercy; <a name="fr_353"></a><a href="#fn_353"><font size="1">354</font></a>
  with what measure ye mete, it shall be measured to you again;” <a name="fr_354"></a><a href="#fn_354"><font size="1">355</font></a>
  and once more, “Blessed are the poor, and those that are persecuted for righteousness sake, for theirs is the kingdom of God.” <a name="fr_355"></a><a href="#fn_355"><font size="1">356</font></a>
 </p>
