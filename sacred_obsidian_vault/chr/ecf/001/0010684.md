 <p> <a name="viii.ix.xvii"></a>  </p><h3 align="center"><a name="viii.ix.xvii-p0.1"></a>XVII.</h3>  
 <p><a name="viii.ix.xvii-p1"></a>As the good of the body is health, so the good of the soul is knowledge, which is indeed a kind of health of soul, by which a likeness to God is attained.—<i>From the writings of</i> <span class="upper"><a name="viii.ix.xvii-p1.1"></a>John of Damascus</span>.</p>
