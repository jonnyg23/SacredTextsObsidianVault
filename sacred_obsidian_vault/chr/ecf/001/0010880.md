 <p> <a name="ix.viii.v"></a>  </p><h3 align="center"><a name="ix.viii.v-p0.1"></a>V.</h3>  
 <p><a name="ix.viii.v-p1"></a>The <a name="fr_4808"></a><a href="#fn_4808"><font size="1">4809</font></a>
  will and the energy of God is the effective and foreseeing cause of every time and place and age, and of every nature. The will is the reason (<span class="Greek"><a name="ix.viii.v-p2.1"></a>λόγος</span>) of the intellectual soul, which [reason] is within us, inasmuch as it is the faculty belonging to it which is endowed with freedom of action. The will is the mind desiring [some object], and an appetite possessed of intelligence, yearning after that thing which is desired.</p>
