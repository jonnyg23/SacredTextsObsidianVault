 <p> <a name="v.vii.x"></a>  <a name="page_91"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 91</font></a> <a name="v.vii.x-Page_91"></a>  </p><h3 align="center"><a name="v.vii.x-p0.1"></a>Chapter X.—Acknowledgment of their kindness.</h3>  
 <p><a name="v.vii.x-p1"></a>Ye have done well in receiving Philo and Rheus Agathopus as servants <a name="fr_1039"></a><a href="#fn_1039"><font size="1">1040</font></a>
  of Christ our God, who have followed me for the sake of God, and who give thanks to the Lord in your behalf, because ye have in every way refreshed them. None of these things shall be lost to you. <a name="v.vii.x-p2.1"></a>May my spirit be for you, <a name="fr_1040"></a><a href="#fn_1040"><font size="1">1041</font></a>
  and my bonds, which ye have not despised or been ashamed of; nor shall Jesus Christ, our perfect hope, be ashamed of you.</p>  
 <p><a name="v.vii.x-p4"></a>Ye have done well in receiving Philo, and Gaius, and Agathopus, who, being the servants <a name="fr_1041"></a><a href="#fn_1041"><font size="1">1042</font></a>
  of Christ, have followed me for the sake of God, and who greatly bless the Lord in your behalf, because ye have in every way refreshed them. None of those things which ye have done to them shall be passed by without being reckoned unto you. “The Lord grant” to you “that ye may find mercy of the Lord in that day!” <a name="fr_1042"></a><a href="#fn_1042"><font size="1">1043</font></a>
  <a name="v.vii.x-p6.2"></a>May my spirit be for you, <a name="fr_1043"></a><a href="#fn_1043"><font size="1">1044</font></a>
  and my bonds, which ye have not despised or been ashamed of. Wherefore, neither shall Jesus Christ, our perfect hope, be ashamed of you.</p>
