 <p> <a name="ix.viii.xxiv"></a>  </p><h3 align="center"><a name="ix.viii.xxiv-p0.1"></a>XXIV.</h3>  
 <p><a name="ix.viii.xxiv-p1"></a><a name="ix.viii.xxiv-p1.1"></a>“God is not as a man.” <a name="fr_4835"></a><a href="#fn_4835"><font size="1">4836</font></a>
  He thus shows that all men are indeed guilty of falsehood, inasmuch as they change from one thing to another (<span class="Greek"><a name="ix.viii.xxiv-p2.2"></a>μεταφερόμενοι</span>); but such is not the case with God, for He always continues true, perfecting whatever He wishes.</p>
