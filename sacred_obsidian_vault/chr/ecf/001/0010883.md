 <p> <a name="ix.viii.viii"></a>  </p><h3 align="center"><a name="ix.viii.viii-p0.1"></a>VIII.</h3>  
 <p><a name="ix.viii.viii-p1"></a><a name="ix.viii.viii-p1.1"></a>For <a name="fr_4811"></a><a href="#fn_4811"><font size="1">4812</font></a>
  as the ark [of the covenant] was gilded within and without with pure gold, so was also the body of Christ pure and resplendent; for it was adorned within by the Word, and shielded without by the Spirit, in order that from both [materials] the splendour of the natures might be clearly shown forth.</p>
