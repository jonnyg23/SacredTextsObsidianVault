 <p> <a name="iv.ii.xii"></a>  </p><h3 align="center"><a name="iv.ii.xii-p0.1"></a>Chapter XII.—Exhortation to various graces.</h3>  
 <p><a name="iv.ii.xii-p1"></a><a name="iv.ii.xii-p1.1"></a>For I trust that ye are well versed in the Sacred Scriptures, and that nothing is hid from you; but to me this privilege is not yet granted. <a name="fr_403"></a><a href="#fn_403"><font size="1">404</font></a>
  <a name="iv.ii.xii-p2.1"></a>It is declared then in these Scriptures, “Be ye angry, and sin not,” <a name="fr_404"></a><a href="#fn_404"><font size="1">405</font></a>
  and, “Let not the sun go down upon your wrath.” <a name="fr_405"></a><a href="#fn_405"><font size="1">406</font></a>
  Happy is he who remembers <a name="fr_406"></a><a href="#fn_406"><font size="1">407</font></a>
  this, which I believe to be the case with you. But may the God and Father of our Lord Jesus Christ, and Jesus Christ Himself, who is the Son of God, and our everlasting High Priest, build you up in faith and truth, and in all meekness, gentleness, patience, long-suffering, forbearance, and purity; and may He bestow on you a lot and portion among His saints, and on us with you, and on all that are under heaven, who shall believe in our Lord  <a name="page_36"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 36</font></a> <a name="iv.ii.xii-Page_36"></a>  Jesus Christ, and in His Father, who “raised Him from the dead.” <a name="fr_407"></a><a href="#fn_407"><font size="1">408</font></a>
  Pray for all the saints. Pray also for kings, <a name="fr_408"></a><a href="#fn_408"><font size="1">409</font></a>
  and potentates, and princes, and for those that persecute and hate you, <a name="fr_409"></a><a href="#fn_409"><font size="1">410</font></a>
  and for the enemies of the cross, that your fruit may be manifest to all, and that ye may be perfect in Him.</p>
