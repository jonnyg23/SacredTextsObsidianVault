 <p> <a name="v.ii.x"></a>  </p><h3 align="center"><a name="v.ii.x-p0.1"></a>Chapter X.—Exhortations to prayer, humility, etc.</h3>  
 <p><a name="v.ii.x-p1"></a><a name="v.ii.x-p1.1"></a><a name="v.ii.x-p1.2"></a><a name="v.ii.x-p1.3"></a>And pray ye without ceasing in behalf of other men. <a name="v.ii.x-p1.4"></a>For there is in them hope of repentance that they  <a name="page_54"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 54</font></a> <a name="v.ii.x-Page_54"></a>  may attain to God. See, <a name="fr_553"></a><a href="#fn_553"><font size="1">554</font></a>
  then, that they be instructed by your works, if in no other way. <a name="v.ii.x-p2.1"></a><a name="v.ii.x-p2.2"></a>Be ye meek in response to their wrath, humble in opposition to their boasting: to their blasphemies return <a name="fr_554"></a><a href="#fn_554"><font size="1">555</font></a>
  your prayers; in contrast to their error, be ye stedfast <a name="fr_555"></a><a href="#fn_555"><font size="1">556</font></a>
  in the faith; and for their cruelty, manifest your gentleness. <a name="v.ii.x-p4.2"></a><a name="v.ii.x-p4.3"></a>While we take care not to imitate their conduct, let us be found their brethren in all true kindness; and let us seek to be followers of the Lord (who ever more unjustly treated, more destitute, more condemned?), that so no plant of the devil may be found in you, but ye may remain in all holiness and sobriety in Jesus Christ, both with respect to the flesh and spirit.</p>  
 <p><a name="v.ii.x-p5"></a><a name="v.ii.x-p5.1"></a><a name="v.ii.x-p5.2"></a><a name="v.ii.x-p5.3"></a>And pray ye without ceasing in behalf of other men; for there is hope of the repentance, that they may attain to God. For “cannot he that falls arise again, and he that goes astray return?” <a name="fr_556"></a><a href="#fn_556"><font size="1">557</font></a>
  Permit them, then, to be instructed by you. Be ye therefore the ministers of God, and the mouth of Christ. For thus saith the Lord, “If ye take forth the precious from the vile, ye shall be as my mouth.” <a name="fr_557"></a><a href="#fn_557"><font size="1">558</font></a>
  <a name="v.ii.x-p7.2"></a><a name="v.ii.x-p7.3"></a>Be ye humble in response to their wrath; oppose to their blasphemies your earnest prayers; while they go astray, stand ye stedfast in the faith. Conquer ye their harsh temper by gentleness, their passion by meekness. For “blessed are the meek;” <a name="fr_558"></a><a href="#fn_558"><font size="1">559</font></a>
  and Moses was meek above all men; <a name="fr_559"></a><a href="#fn_559"><font size="1">560</font></a>
  and David was exceeding meek. <a name="fr_560"></a><a href="#fn_560"><font size="1">561</font></a>
  Wherefore Paul exhorts as follows: “The servant of the Lord must not strive, but be gentle towards all men, apt to teach, patient, in meekness instructing those that oppose themselves.” <a name="fr_561"></a><a href="#fn_561"><font size="1">562</font></a>
  Do not seek to avenge yourselves on those that injure you, for says [the Scripture], “If I have returned evil to those who returned evil to me.” <a name="fr_562"></a><a href="#fn_562"><font size="1">563</font></a>
  Let us make them brethren by our kindness. For say ye to those that hate you, Ye are our brethren, that the name of the Lord may be glorified. <a name="v.ii.x-p12.2"></a>And let us imitate the Lord, “who, when He was reviled, reviled not again;” <a name="fr_563"></a><a href="#fn_563"><font size="1">564</font></a>
  when He was crucified, He answered not; “when He suffered, He threatened not;” <a name="fr_564"></a><a href="#fn_564"><font size="1">565</font></a>
  but prayed for His enemies, “Father, forgive them; they know not what they do.” <a name="fr_565"></a><a href="#fn_565"><font size="1">566</font></a>
  If any one, the more he is injured, displays the more patience, blessed is he. If any one is defrauded, if any one is despised, for the name of the Lord, he truly is the servant of Christ. Take heed that no plant of the devil be found among you, for such a plant is bitter and salt. “Watch ye, and be ye sober,” <a name="fr_566"></a><a href="#fn_566"><font size="1">567</font></a>
  in Christ Jesus.</p>
