 <p> <a name="ix.viii.xlvii"></a>  </p><h3 align="center"><a name="ix.viii.xlvii-p0.1"></a>XLVII.</h3>  
 <p><a name="ix.viii.xlvii-p1"></a>The <a name="fr_4893"></a><a href="#fn_4893"><font size="1">4894</font></a>
  birth of John [the Baptist] brought the dumbness of Zacharias to an end. For he did not burden his father, when the voice issued forth from silence; but as when not believed it rendered him tongue-tied, so did the voice sounding out clearly set his father free, to whom he had both been announced and born. Now the voice and the burning light <a name="fr_4894"></a><a href="#fn_4894"><font size="1">4895</font></a>
  were a precursor of the Word and the Light.</p>
