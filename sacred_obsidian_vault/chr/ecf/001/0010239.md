 <p> <a name="v.xi.vii"></a>  </p><h3 align="center"><a name="v.xi.vii-p0.1"></a>Chapter XV.</h3>  
 <p><a name="v.xi.vii-p1"></a>It is better that a man should be silent while he is something, than that he should be talking when he is not; that by those things which be speaks he should act, and by those things of which he is silent he should be known.</p>
