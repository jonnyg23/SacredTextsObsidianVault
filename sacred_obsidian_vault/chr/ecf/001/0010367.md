 <p> <a name="vii.ii.vii"></a>  </p><h3 align="center"><a name="vii.ii.vii-p0.1"></a>VII. <a name="fr_1759"></a><a href="#fn_1759"><font size="1">1760</font></a>
 </h3>  
 <p><a name="vii.ii.vii-p2"></a><i>Papias thus speaks, word for word:</i> To some of them [angels] He gave dominion over the arrangement of the world, and He commissioned them to exercise their dominion well. <i>And he says, immediately after this:</i> but it happened that their arrangement came to nothing. <a name="fr_1760"></a><a href="#fn_1760"><font size="1">1761</font></a>
 </p>
