 <p> <a name="v.viii.iv"></a>  </p><h3 align="center"><a name="v.viii.iv-p0.1"></a>Chapter IV.—Exhortations.</h3>  
 <p><a name="v.viii.iv-p1"></a><a name="v.viii.iv-p1.1"></a><a name="v.viii.iv-p1.2"></a>Let not widows be neglected. Be thou, after the Lord, their protector <a name="fr_1083"></a><a href="#fn_1083"><font size="1">1084</font></a>
  and friend. Let nothing be done without thy consent; neither do thou anything without the approval of God, which indeed thou dost not, inasmuch as thou art stedfast. Let your assembling together be of frequent <a name="fr_1084"></a><a href="#fn_1084"><font size="1">1085</font></a>
  occurrence: seek after all by name. <a name="fr_1085"></a><a href="#fn_1085"><font size="1">1086</font></a>
  <a name="v.viii.iv-p4.1"></a>Do not despise either male or female slaves, yet neither let them be puffed up with conceit, but rather let them  <a name="page_95"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 95</font></a> <a name="v.viii.iv-Page_95"></a>  submit themselves <a name="fr_1086"></a><a href="#fn_1086"><font size="1">1087</font></a>
  the more, for the glory of God, that they may obtain from God a better liberty. Let them not long to be set free [from slavery] at the public expense, that they be not found slaves to their own desires.</p>  
 <p><a name="v.viii.iv-p6"></a><a name="v.viii.iv-p6.1"></a><a name="v.viii.iv-p6.2"></a>Let not the widows be neglected. Be thou, after the Lord, their protector and friend. Let nothing be done without thy consent; neither do thou anything without the approval of God, which indeed thou doest not. Be thou stedfast. Let your assembling together be of frequent <a name="fr_1087"></a><a href="#fn_1087"><font size="1">1088</font></a>
  occurrence: seek after all by name. <a name="fr_1088"></a><a href="#fn_1088"><font size="1">1089</font></a>
  <a name="v.viii.iv-p8.1"></a>Do not despise either male or female slaves, yet neither let them be puffed up with conceit, but rather let them submit themselves <a name="fr_1089"></a><a href="#fn_1089"><font size="1">1090</font></a>
  the more, for the glory of God, that they may obtain from God a better liberty. Let them not wish to be set free [from slavery] at the public expense, that they be not found slaves to their own desires.</p>
