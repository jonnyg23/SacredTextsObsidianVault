 <p> <a name="iv.vii.xiv"></a>
 </p><p class="c10"><a name="iv.vii.xiv-p1"></a>  14. It follows after commendation of the Trinity, “The Holy Church.” God is pointed out, and His temple. “For the temple of God is holy,” says the Apostle, “which (temple) are ye.” <a name="fr_1800"></a><a href="#fn_1800"><font size="1">1801</font></a>
  This same is the holy Church, the one Church, the true Church, the catholic Church, fighting against all heresies: fight, it can: be fought down, it cannot. As for heresies, they went all out of it, like as unprofitable branches pruned from the vine: but itself abideth in its root, in its Vine, in its charity. “The gates of hell shall not prevail against it.” <a name="fr_1801"></a><a href="#fn_1801"><font size="1">1802</font></a>
