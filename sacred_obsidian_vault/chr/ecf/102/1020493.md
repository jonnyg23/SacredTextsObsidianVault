 <p class="c19"><a name="iv.XVII-p2"></a> ————————————</p>  
 <p class="c31"><a name="iv.XVII-p3"></a>Argument—In this book the history of the city of God is traced during the period of the kings and prophets from Samuel to David, even to Christ; and the prophecies which are recorded in the books of Kings, Psalms, and those of Solomon, are interpreted of Christ and the church.</p>
