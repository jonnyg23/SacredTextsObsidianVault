 </p><p class="c29"><a name="iv.XXII-p1"></a><span class="c18"><a name="iv.XXII-p1.1"></a>Book XXII.</span></p>  
 <p class="c19"><a name="iv.XXII-p2"></a> ————————————</p>  
 <p class="c31"><a name="iv.XXII-p3"></a>Argument—This book treats of the end of the city of God, that is to say, of the eternal happiness of the saints; the faith of the resurrection of the body is established and explained; and the work concludes by showing how the saints, clothed in immortal and spiritual bodies, shall be employed.</p>
