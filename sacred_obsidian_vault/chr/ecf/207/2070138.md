 </p><p class="c42"><a name="iv.iv.v.vi-p1"></a>  <a name="page_468"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 468</font></a> <a name="iv.iv.v.vi-Page_468"></a><span class="c1"><a name="iv.iv.v.vi-p1.1"></a>Ep. XXVIII.</span></p>  
 <p class="c23"><a name="iv.iv.v.vi-p2"></a>In visiting the mountain cities which border on Pamphylia I fished up in the Mountains a sea Glaucus; I did not drag the fish out of the depths with a net of flax, but I snared my game with the love of a friend.  And having once taught my Glaucus to travel by land, I sent him as the bearer of a letter to Your Goodness.  Please receive him kindly, and honour him with the hospitality commended in the Bible, not forgetting the vegetables.</p>
