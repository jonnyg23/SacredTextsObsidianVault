 </p><p class="c42"><a name="iv.iv.vii.vi-p1"></a> <span class="c1"><a name="iv.iv.vii.vi-p1.1"></a>Ep. CXXIV.</span></p>  
 <p class="c23"><a name="iv.iv.vii.vi-p2"></a>(A little later on, when the weather was more settled, Gregory accepts the invitation and proposes to come at once, but declines to attend the Provincial Synod.)</p>  
 <p class="c23"><a name="iv.iv.vii.vi-p3"></a>You call me?  And I hasten, and that for a private visit.  Synods and Conventions I salute from afar, since I have experienced that most of them (to speak moderately) are but sorry affairs.  What then remains?  Help with your prayers my just desires that I may obtain that for which I am anxious.</p>
