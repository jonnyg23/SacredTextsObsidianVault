 </p><p class="c42"><a name="iv.iv.vii.v-p1"></a> <span class="c1"><a name="iv.iv.vii.v-p1.1"></a>Ep. CXXIII.</span></p>  
 <p class="c23"><a name="iv.iv.vii.v-p2"></a>(To excuse himself for postponing his acceptance of an invitation.)</p>  
 <p class="c23"><a name="iv.iv.vii.v-p3"></a>I reverence your presence, and I delight in your company; although otherwise I counsel<a name="page_473"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 473</font></a> <a name="iv.iv.vii.v-Page_473"></a>led myself to remain at home and philosophize in quiet, for I found this of all courses the most profitable for myself.  And since the winds are still somewhat rough, and my infirmity has not yet left me, I beg you to bear with me patiently for a little while, and to join me in my prayers for health; and as soon as the fit season comes I will attend upon your requests.</p>
