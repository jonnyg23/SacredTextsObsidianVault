 </p><p class="c18"><a name="vi.vi-p1"></a><span class="c37"><a name="vi.vi-p1.1"></a>Book III.</span></p>  		  
 <p class="c38"><a name="vi.vi-p2"></a>This book contains a demonstration of the harmony of the evangelists from the accounts of the Supper on to the end of the Gospel, the narratives given by the several writers being collated, and the whole arranged in one orderly connection.</p>
