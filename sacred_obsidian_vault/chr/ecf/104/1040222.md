 </p><p class="c38"><a name="iv.x.xl-p1"></a> <span class="c2"><a name="iv.x.xl-p1.1"></a>Chapter 38.—Eternal Fire Torturing the Wicked, Not Evil.</span></p>   
 <p class="c10"><a name="iv.x.xl-p2"></a>For neither is eternal fire itself, which is to torture the impious, an evil nature, since it has its measure, its form and its order depraved by no iniquity; but it is an evil torture for the damned, to whose sins it is due.  For neither is yonder light, because it tortures the blear-eyed, an evil nature.</p>
