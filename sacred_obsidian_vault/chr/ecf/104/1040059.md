 </p><p class="c34"><a name="iv.v.ii-p1"></a><span class="c7"><a name="iv.v.ii-p1.1"></a>On the Morals of the Manichæans.</span></p>   
 <p class="c28"><a name="iv.v.ii-p2"></a><span class="c2"><a name="iv.v.ii-p2.1"></a>[De Moribus Manichæorum.] <span class="c11"><a name="iv.v.ii-p2.2"></a>a.d.</span> 388.</span></p>   
 <p class="c37"><a name="iv.v.ii-p3"></a><i>Containing a particular refutation of the doctrine of these heretics regarding the origin and nature of evil; an exposure of their pretended symbolical customs of the mouth, of the hands, and of the breast; and a condemnation of their superstitious abstinence and unholy mysteries.  Lastly, some crimes brought to light among the Manichæans are mentioned.</i></p>
