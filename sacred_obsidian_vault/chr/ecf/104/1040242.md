 <p> <a name="v.iv.iii"></a>   
 </p><p class="c34"><a name="v.iv.iii-p1"></a> <span class="c7"><a name="v.iv.iii-p1.1"></a>Book I.</span></p>   
 <p class="c59"><a name="v.iv.iii-p2"></a>He proves that baptism can be conferred outside the Catholic communion by heretics or schismatics, but that it ought not to be received from them; and that it is of no avail to any while in a state of heresy or schism.</p>
