 </p><p class="c38"><a name="iv.x.xxxi-p1"></a> <span class="c2"><a name="iv.x.xxxi-p1.1"></a>Chapter 29.—That God is Not Defiled by Our Sins.</span></p>   
 <p class="c10"><a name="iv.x.xxxi-p2"></a>And yet, though all things that He established are in Him, those who sin do not defile Him, of whose wisdom it is said:  "She touches all things by reason of her purity, and nothing defiled assails her." <a name="fr_1109"></a><a href="#fn_1109"><font size="1">1110</font></a>
   For it behooves us to believe that as God is incorruptible and unchangeable, so also is He consequently undefilable.</p>
