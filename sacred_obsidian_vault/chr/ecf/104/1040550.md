 </p><p class="c41"><a name="v.v.iv.lxiii-p1"></a> <span class="c11"><a name="v.v.iv.lxiii-p1.1"></a>Chapter 63.—</span>141.  <span class="c11"><a name="v.v.iv.lxiii-p1.2"></a>Petilianus</span> said:  "But wherein do you fulfill the commandments of God?  The Lord Christ said, Blessed are the poor in spirit; for theirs is the kingdom of heaven.  But you by your malice in persecution breathe forth the riches of madness."</p>  
 <p class="c10"><a name="v.v.iv.lxiii-p2"></a>142.  <span class="c11"><a name="v.v.iv.lxiii-p2.1"></a>Augustin</span> answered:  Address that rather to your own Circumcelliones.</p>
