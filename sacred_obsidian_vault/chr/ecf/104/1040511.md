 </p><p class="c41"><a name="v.v.iv.xxiv-p1"></a> <span class="c11"><a name="v.v.iv.xxiv-p1.1"></a>Chapter 24.—</span>56.  <span class="c11"><a name="v.v.iv.xxiv-p1.2"></a>Petilianus</span> said:  "But you will answer that you abide by the same declaration, He that is once washed needeth not save to wash his feet. <a name="fr_2053"></a><a href="#fn_2053"><font size="1">2054</font></a>
   Now the once is once that has authority, once that is confirmed by the truth."</p>  
 <p class="c10"><a name="v.v.iv.xxiv-p3"></a>57.  <span class="c11"><a name="v.v.iv.xxiv-p3.1"></a>Augustin</span> answered:  Baptism in the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Ghost <a name="fr_2054"></a><a href="#fn_2054"><font size="1">2055</font></a>
  has Christ for its authority, not any man, whoever he may be; and Christ is the truth, not any man.</p>
