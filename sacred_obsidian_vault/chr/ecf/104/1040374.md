 </p><p class="c41"><a name="v.iv.viii.xx-p1"></a> <span class="c11"><a name="v.iv.viii.xx-p1.1"></a>Chapter 20.—</span>34.  Polianus of Mileum <a name="fr_1657"></a><a href="#fn_1657"><font size="1">1658</font></a>
  said:  "It is right that a heretic should be baptized in the holy Church." <a name="fr_1658"></a><a href="#fn_1658"><font size="1">1659</font></a>
 </p>  
 <p class="c10"><a name="v.iv.viii.xx-p4"></a>35.  Nothing, indeed, could be expressed more shortly.  But I think this too is short:  It is right that the baptism of Christ should not be depreciated in the Church of Christ.</p>
