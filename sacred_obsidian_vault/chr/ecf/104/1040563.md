 </p><p class="c41"><a name="v.v.iv.lxxvi-p1"></a> <span class="c11"><a name="v.v.iv.lxxvi-p1.1"></a>Chapter 76.—</span>167.  <span class="c11"><a name="v.v.iv.lxxvi-p1.2"></a>Petilianus</span> said:  "But to us the Lord Christ, in opposition to your deadly commands, commanded simple patience and harmlessness.  For what says He?  A new commandment I give unto you, That ye love one another; as I have loved you, that ye also love one another.  And again, By this shall all men know that ye are my disciples, if ye have love one to another."  <a name="fr_2179"></a><a href="#fn_2179"><font size="1">2180</font></a>
