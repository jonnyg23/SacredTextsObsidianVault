 </p><p class="c47"><a name="iii.x.x-p1"></a>  <span class="c11"><a name="iii.x.x-p1.1"></a>Chapter IX.—</span><i>The Bishops under Commodus.</i></p>  
 <p class="c18"><a name="iii.x.x-p2"></a>1. <span class="c13"><a name="iii.x.x-p2.1"></a>After</span> Antoninus <a name="fr_1500"></a><a href="#fn_1500"><font size="1">1501</font></a>
  had been emperor for nineteen years, Commodus received the government. <a name="fr_1501"></a><a href="#fn_1501"><font size="1">1502</font></a>
  In his first year Julian <a name="fr_1502"></a><a href="#fn_1502"><font size="1">1503</font></a>
  became bishop of the Alexandrian churches, after Agrippinus <a name="fr_1503"></a><a href="#fn_1503"><font size="1">1504</font></a>
  had held the office for twelve years.</p>
