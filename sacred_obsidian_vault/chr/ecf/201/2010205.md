 </p><p class="c47"><a name="iii.xi.xxxv-p1"></a>  <span class="c11"><a name="iii.xi.xxxv-p1.1"></a>Chapter XXXV.—</span><i>Dionysius succeeds Heraclas in the Episcopate.</i></p>  
 <p class="c18"><a name="iii.xi.xxxv-p2"></a><span class="c11"><a name="iii.xi.xxxv-p2.1"></a>In</span> the third year of this emperor, Heraclas <a name="fr_2042"></a><a href="#fn_2042"><font size="1">2043</font></a>
  died, having held his office for sixteen years, and Dionysius <a name="fr_2043"></a><a href="#fn_2043"><font size="1">2044</font></a>
  received the episcopate of the churches of Alexandria.</p>
