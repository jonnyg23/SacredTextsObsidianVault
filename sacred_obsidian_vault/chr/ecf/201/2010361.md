 <a name="fn_3046"></a><a href="2010361.htm#fr_3046">436:3047</a> <a name="iv.iii.iii.i-p4.2"></a>
 <p class="endnote"><a name="iv.iii.iii.i-p5"></a> It is curious that there should be no critical edition of the collected works of so considerable a writer. A large portion of his works are, to be sure, included in Mignes <i>Patrologia Latina,</i> vol. 84, Paris, 1844; but this <i>Opera Universa</i> is neither wholly complete nor in any sense critical, and this seems to be the only attempt at a collection. The works enumerated here are mostly in the edition of Migne, but not all.</p>
