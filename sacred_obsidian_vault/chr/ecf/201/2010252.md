 <p class="c47"><a name="iii.xiii.i-p2"></a><span class="c11"><a name="iii.xiii.i-p2.1"></a>Introduction.</span></p>  
 <p class="c18"><a name="iii.xiii.i-p3"></a><span class="c11"><a name="iii.xiii.i-p3.1"></a>As</span> we have described in seven books the events from the time of the apostles, <a name="fr_2491"></a><a href="#fn_2491"><font size="1">2492</font></a>
  we think it proper in this eighth book to record for the information of posterity a few of the most important occurrences of our own times, which are worthy of permanent record. Our account will begin at this point.</p>
