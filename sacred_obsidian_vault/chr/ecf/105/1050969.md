 </p><p class="c46"><a name="xix.iv.xxxv-p1"></a> <span class="c2"><a name="xix.iv.xxxv-p1.1"></a>Chapter 35.—Commendations of Love.</span></p>  
 <p><a name="xix.iv.xxxv-p2"></a>The Apostle Peter, likewise, says, “And, above all things, have fervent love among yourselves: for love shall cover the multitude of sins.” <a name="fr_3150"></a><a href="#fn_3150"><font size="1">3151</font></a>
  The Apostle James also says, “If ye <a name="page_459"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 459</font></a> <a name="xix.iv.xxxv-Page_459"></a>fulfil the royal law, according to the Scripture, Thou shalt love thy neighbour as thyself, ye do well.” <a name="fr_3151"></a><a href="#fn_3151"><font size="1">3152</font></a>
  So also the Apostle John says, “He that loveth his brother abideth in the right;” <a name="fr_3152"></a><a href="#fn_3152"><font size="1">3153</font></a>
  again, in another passage, “Whosoever doeth not righteousness is not of God, neither he that loveth not his brother; for this is the message which we have heard from the beginning, that we should love one another.” <a name="fr_3153"></a><a href="#fn_3153"><font size="1">3154</font></a>
  Then he says again, “This is His commandment, that we should believe on the name of His Son Jesus Christ, and love one another.” <a name="fr_3154"></a><a href="#fn_3154"><font size="1">3155</font></a>
  Once more: “And this commandment have we from Him that he who loveth God love his brother also.” <a name="fr_3155"></a><a href="#fn_3155"><font size="1">3156</font></a>
  Then shortly afterwards he adds, “By this we know that we love the children of God, when we love God, and keep His commandments; for this is the love of God, that we keep His commandments: and His commandments are not grievous.” <a name="fr_3156"></a><a href="#fn_3156"><font size="1">3157</font></a>
  While, in his second Epistle, it is written, “Not as though I wrote a new commandment unto thee, but that which we had from the beginning, that we love one another.” <a name="fr_3157"></a><a href="#fn_3157"><font size="1">3158</font></a>
 </p>
