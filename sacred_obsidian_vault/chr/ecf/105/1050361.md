 <p> <a name="xiii.xxix"></a>   
 </p><p><a name="xiii.xxix-p1"></a> (25.) Hence the force of the statement: “There was no injustice in my hands, but my prayer was pure.” <a name="fr_1467"></a><a href="#fn_1467"><font size="1">1468</font></a>
  For the purity of his prayer arose from this circumstance, that it was not improper for him to ask forgiveness in prayer, when he really bestowed forgiveness himself.</p>
