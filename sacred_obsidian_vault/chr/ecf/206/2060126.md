 </p><p class="c30"><a name="v.CXV-p1"></a>  <span class="c1"><a name="v.CXV-p1.1"></a>Letter CXV. To Augustine.</span></p>  
 <p class="c16"><a name="v.CXV-p2"></a>A short but most friendly letter in which Jerome excuses himself for the freedom with which he has dealt with Augustines questions (the allusion is to Letter CXII.) and hopes that henceforth they may be able to avoid controversy and to labour like brothers in the field of scripture.</p>  
 <p><a name="v.CXV-p3"></a>Written probably in 405 <span class="c17"><a name="v.CXV-p3.1"></a>a.d.</span></p>
