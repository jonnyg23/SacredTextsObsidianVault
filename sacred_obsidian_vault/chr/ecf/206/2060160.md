 </p><p class="c30"><a name="v.CXLIX-p1"></a>  <span class="c1"><a name="v.CXLIX-p1.1"></a>Letter CXLIX. On the Jewish Festivals.</span></p>  
 <p class="c16"><a name="v.CXLIX-p2"></a>The theme of this letter is the abrogation of the Jewish festivals by the evangelical law. It has no claim to be considered a work of Jerome.</p>
