 </p><p class="c30"><a name="v.CL-p1"></a>  <span class="c1"><a name="v.CL-p1.1"></a>Letter CL. From Procopius to Jerome.</span></p>  
 <p><a name="v.CL-p2"></a>This letter is extant also among those of Procopius of Gaza, to whose works it properly belongs. As this Procopius flourished a century later than Jerome, the letter cannot be addressed to him.</p>
