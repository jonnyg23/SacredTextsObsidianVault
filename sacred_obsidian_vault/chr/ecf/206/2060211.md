 </p><p class="c53"><a name="vii.iv.i-p1"></a>   <a name="page_495"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 495</font></a> <a name="vii.iv.i-Page_495"></a><span class="c14"><a name="vii.iv.i-p1.1"></a>The Commentaries.</span></p>  
 <p class="c31"><a name="vii.iv.i-p2"></a>The extant commentaries by Jerome on the books of Holy Scripture may be arranged thus, chronological sequence being observed as far as possible:</p>  
 <p class="c29"><a name="vii.iv.i-p3"></a>A. New Testament:</p>  
 <p class="c57"><a name="vii.iv.i-p4"></a>The Epistles to Philemon, Galatians, Ephesians, Titus. <span class="c17"><a name="vii.iv.i-p4.1"></a>a.d.</span> 387.</p>  
 <p class="c57"><a name="vii.iv.i-p5"></a>Origen on St. Luke. <span class="c17"><a name="vii.iv.i-p5.1"></a>a.d.</span> 389.</p>  
 <p class="c57"><a name="vii.iv.i-p6"></a>St. Matthew. <span class="c17"><a name="vii.iv.i-p6.1"></a>a.d.</span> 398.</p>  
 <p class="c29"><a name="vii.iv.i-p7"></a>B. Old Testament:</p>  
 <p class="c57"><a name="vii.iv.i-p8"></a>Ecclesiastes. <span class="c17"><a name="vii.iv.i-p8.1"></a>a.d.</span> 388.</p>  
 <p class="c57"><a name="vii.iv.i-p9"></a>1. The Twelve Minor Prophets:</p>  
 <p class="c58"><a name="vii.iv.i-p10"></a>Nahum, Michah, Zephaniah, Haggai, Habakkuk. <span class="c17"><a name="vii.iv.i-p10.1"></a>a.d.</span> 392.</p>  
 <p class="c58"><a name="vii.iv.i-p11"></a>Jonah. Begun three years after the foregoing (Preface). Finished between <span class="c17"><a name="vii.iv.i-p11.1"></a>a.d.</span> 395 and <span class="c17"><a name="vii.iv.i-p11.2"></a>a.d.</span> 397.</p>  
 <p class="c58"><a name="vii.iv.i-p12"></a>Obadiah. <span class="c17"><a name="vii.iv.i-p12.1"></a>a.d.</span> 403.</p>  
 <p class="c58"><a name="vii.iv.i-p13"></a>Zechariah, Malachi, Hosea, Joel, Amos. Finished by <span class="c17"><a name="vii.iv.i-p13.1"></a>a.d.</span> 406.</p>  
 <p class="c57"><a name="vii.iv.i-p14"></a>2. The Four Greater Prophets:</p>  
 <p class="c58"><a name="vii.iv.i-p15"></a>Daniel. <span class="c17"><a name="vii.iv.i-p15.1"></a>a.d</span>. 407.</p>  
 <p class="c58"><a name="vii.iv.i-p16"></a>Isaiah. <span class="c17"><a name="vii.iv.i-p16.1"></a>a.d.</span> 408–410.</p>  
 <p class="c58"><a name="vii.iv.i-p17"></a>Ezekiel. <span class="c17"><a name="vii.iv.i-p17.1"></a>a.d</span>. 410–414.</p>  
 <p class="c58"><a name="vii.iv.i-p18"></a>Jeremiah. Commenced after the death of Eustochium in <span class="c17"><a name="vii.iv.i-p18.1"></a>a.d.</span> 418. The commentary on this book, which stops short at chapter xxxii., was therefore written in <span class="c17"><a name="vii.iv.i-p18.2"></a>a.d.</span> 419, the year which intervened between Eustochiums death and Jeromes own.</p>  
 <p><a name="vii.iv.i-p19"></a>We have thought it best to give the Prefaces, as in those to the Vulgate, in the order of the books as they stand in our Bible, not in the order in which they were written.</p>
