 </p><p class="c30"><a name="v.XCIV-p1"></a>  <span class="c1"><a name="v.XCIV-p1.1"></a>Letter XCIV. From Dionysius to Theophilus.</span></p>  
 <p class="c16"><a name="v.XCIV-p2"></a>In this letter (translated into Latin by Jerome) Dionysius, bishop of Lydda, praises Theophilus for his signal victories over Origenism and urges him to continue his efforts against that heresy. Written in 400 <span class="c17"><a name="v.XCIV-p2.1"></a>a.d.</span></p>
