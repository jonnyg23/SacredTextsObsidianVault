 </p><p class="c34"><a name="xv.iv.iv.lxxiv-p1"></a> <span class="c4"><a name="xv.iv.iv.lxxiv-p1.1"></a>Canon LXXIII.  (Greek lxxvi.)</span></p> 
 <p class="c50"><a name="xv.iv.iv.lxxiv-p2"></a>The date of Easter and the date of the Council should be announced.</p> 
 <p class="c18"><a name="xv.iv.iv.lxxiv-p3"></a><span class="sc"><a name="xv.iv.iv.lxxiv-p3.1"></a>Item</span>, it seemed good that the day of the venerable Easter should be intimated to all by the subscription of formed letters; and that the same should be observed with regard to the date of the Council, according to the decree of the Council of Hippo, that <a name="page_479"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 479</font></a> <a name="xv.iv.iv.lxxiv-Page_479"></a>is to say the X. Calends of September, and that it should be written to the primates of each province so that when they summon their councils they do not impede this day.</p> 
 <p class="c31"><a name="xv.iv.iv.lxxiv-p4"></a><span class="c4"><a name="xv.iv.iv.lxxiv-p4.1"></a>Notes.</span></p> 
 <p class="c36"><a name="xv.iv.iv.lxxiv-p5"></a><span class="sc"><a name="xv.iv.iv.lxxiv-p5.1"></a>Ancient Epitome of Canon LXXIII.</span></p> 
 <p class="c35"><a name="xv.iv.iv.lxxiv-p6"></a>It seemed good that the day of the Holy Easter should be announced on the day of the annual Synod, or on the tenth day before the calends of September.</p> 
 <p class="c18"><a name="xv.iv.iv.lxxiv-p7"></a>This is Canon viij. of Carthage, September, <span class="sc"><a name="xv.iv.iv.lxxiv-p7.1"></a>a.d.</span> 401.</p> 
 <p class="c36"><a name="xv.iv.iv.lxxiv-p8"></a><span class="sc"><a name="xv.iv.iv.lxxiv-p8.1"></a>Johnson.</span></p> 
 <p class="c18"><a name="xv.iv.iv.lxxiv-p9"></a>See Can. 51 (55).</p> 
 <p class="c19"><a name="xv.iv.iv.lxxiv-p10"></a>“The time of council,” i.e., of the national council at Carthage.</p> 
 <p class="c19"><a name="xv.iv.iv.lxxiv-p11"></a>The Greek canon says <span class="Greek"><a name="xv.iv.iv.lxxiv-p11.1"></a>ἡ πρὸ δέκα καλανδῶν Σεπτεμβρίων</span>, and Zonaras makes this the 21st of August, but he mistakes in his calculation.</p>
