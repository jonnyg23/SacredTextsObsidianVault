 </p><p class="c14"><a name="viii.vii.iii.i-p1"></a> <a name="page_125"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 125</font></a> <a name="viii.vii.iii.i-Page_125"></a><span class="c4"><a name="viii.vii.iii.i-p1.1"></a>The Canons of the Synod Held in the City of Laodicea, in Phrygia Pacatiana, in which Many Blessed Fathers from Divers Provinces of Asia Were Gathered Together. <a name="fr_174"></a><a href="#fn_174"><font size="1">175</font></a>
 </span></p> 
 <p class="c18"><a name="viii.vii.iii.i-p3"></a>The holy synod which assembled at Laodicea in Phrygia Pacatiana, from divers regions of Asia; set forth the ecclesiastical definitions which are hereunder annexed.</p> 
 <p class="c31"><a name="viii.vii.iii.i-p4"></a><span class="c4"><a name="viii.vii.iii.i-p4.1"></a>Note.</span></p> 
 <p class="c18"><a name="viii.vii.iii.i-p5"></a>This brief preface, by some ancient collector, is found in the printed editions of Zonaras and of Balsamon and also in the Amerbachian manuscript.</p>
