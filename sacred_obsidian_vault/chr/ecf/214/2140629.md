 </p><p class="c34"><a name="xv.iv.iv.cvi-p1"></a> <span class="c4"><a name="xv.iv.iv.cvi-p1.1"></a>Canon CV.  (Greek cviii.)</span></p> 
 <p class="c50"><a name="xv.iv.iv.cvi-p2"></a>Of those who do not communicate in Africa and would go across seas.</p> 
 <p class="c18"><a name="xv.iv.iv.cvi-p3"></a><span class="sc"><a name="xv.iv.iv.cvi-p3.1"></a>Whoever</span> does not communicate in Africa, and goes to communicate across seas, let him be cast out of the clergy.</p> 
 <p class="c31"><a name="xv.iv.iv.cvi-p4"></a><span class="c4"><a name="xv.iv.iv.cvi-p4.1"></a>Notes.</span></p> 
 <p class="c36"><a name="xv.iv.iv.cvi-p5"></a><span class="sc"><a name="xv.iv.iv.cvi-p5.1"></a>Ancient Epitome of Canon CV.</span></p> 
 <p class="c35"><a name="xv.iv.iv.cvi-p6"></a>Whoever is cut off from communion in Africa, and goes to parts across seas that he may there communicate, is to be cast out of the clergy.</p> 
 <p class="c18"><a name="xv.iv.iv.cvi-p7"></a>This canon is Canon j. of Carthage, <span class="sc"><a name="xv.iv.iv.cvi-p7.1"></a>a.d.</span> 407.</p>
