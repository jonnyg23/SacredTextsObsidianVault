 </p><p class="c34"><a name="xv.iv.iv.lix-p1"></a> <span class="c4"><a name="xv.iv.iv.lix-p1.1"></a>Canon LVIII.  (Greek lxii.)</span></p> 
 <p class="c50"><a name="xv.iv.iv.lix-p2"></a>Of the remaining idols or temples which should be done away by the Emperors.</p> 
 <p class="c18"><a name="xv.iv.iv.lix-p3"></a><span class="sc"><a name="xv.iv.iv.lix-p3.1"></a>Wherefore</span> the most religious Emperors should be asked <a name="fr_457"></a><a href="#fn_457"><font size="1">458</font></a>
  that they order the remaining idols to be taken entirely away throughout all Africa; for in many maritime places and in divers possessions the iniquity of this error still flourishes:  that they command them to be taken away and their temples, (such as are no ornament, being set up in fields or out of the way places) be ordered to be altogether destroyed.</p> 
 <p class="c31"><a name="xv.iv.iv.lix-p5"></a><span class="c4"><a name="xv.iv.iv.lix-p5.1"></a>Notes.</span></p> 
 <p class="c36"><a name="xv.iv.iv.lix-p6"></a><span class="sc"><a name="xv.iv.iv.lix-p6.1"></a>Ancient Epitome of Canon LVIII.</span></p> 
 <p class="c35"><a name="xv.iv.iv.lix-p7"></a>The remains of the idols should be abolished altogether.</p> 
 <p class="c18"><a name="xv.iv.iv.lix-p8"></a>This is Canon ij. of the Synod of Carthage of June 15 (16), <span class="sc"><a name="xv.iv.iv.lix-p8.1"></a>a.d.</span> 401.</p>
