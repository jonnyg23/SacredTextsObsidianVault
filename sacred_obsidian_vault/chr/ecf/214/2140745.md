 <p class="c31"><a name="xvii.xxv-p2"></a><span class="c4"><a name="xvii.xxv-p2.1"></a>The Prosphonesus of Theophilus, Archbishop of Alexandria, When the Holy Epiphanies Happened to Fall on a Sunday. <a name="fr_604"></a><a href="#fn_604"><font size="1">605</font></a>
 </span></p> 
 <p class="c34"><a name="xvii.xxv-p4"></a><span class="c4"><a name="xvii.xxv-p4.1"></a>Canon I.</span></p> 
 <p class="c18"><a name="xvii.xxv-p5"></a>Because the fast of Epiphany chances to fall on a Lords day, let us take a few dates, and so break our fast, and honour the Lords day, and shew our dislike of heresy, and yet not wholly neglect the fast which should be observed on this day; eating no more till our evening assembly at three afternoon.</p>
