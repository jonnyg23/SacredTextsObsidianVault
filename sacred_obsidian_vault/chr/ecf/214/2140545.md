 </p><p class="c14"><a name="xv.iv.iv.xxii-p1"></a> <span class="c4"><a name="xv.iv.iv.xxii-p1.1"></a>Canon XXI.  (Greek xxiv.)</span></p> 
 <p class="c50"><a name="xv.iv.iv.xxii-p2"></a>That the sons of clergymen are not to be joined in marriage with heretics.</p> 
 <p class="c18"><a name="xv.iv.iv.xxii-p3"></a><span class="sc"><a name="xv.iv.iv.xxii-p3.1"></a>Likewise</span> it seemed good that the sons of clergymen should not be joined in matrimony with gentiles and heretics.</p>
