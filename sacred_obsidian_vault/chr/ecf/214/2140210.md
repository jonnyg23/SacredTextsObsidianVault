 <p> <a name="viii.vii.iii.xliv"></a>
 </p><p class="c34"><a name="viii.vii.iii.xliv-p1"></a> <span class="c4"><a name="viii.vii.iii.xliv-p1.1"></a>Canon XXXIX.</span></p> 
 <p class="c18"><a name="viii.vii.iii.xliv-p2"></a><span class="sc"><a name="viii.vii.iii.xliv-p2.1"></a>It</span> is not lawful to feast together with the heathen, and to be partakers of their godlessness.</p> 
 <p class="c31"><a name="viii.vii.iii.xliv-p3"></a><span class="c4"><a name="viii.vii.iii.xliv-p3.1"></a>Notes.</span></p> 
 <p class="c36"><a name="viii.vii.iii.xliv-p4"></a><span class="sc"><a name="viii.vii.iii.xliv-p4.1"></a>Ancient Epitome of Canons XXXVII., XXXVIII, and XXXIX.</span></p> 
 <p class="c35"><a name="viii.vii.iii.xliv-p5"></a>Thou shalt not keep feasts with Hebrews or heretics, nor receive festival offerings from them.</p> 
 <p class="c36"><a name="viii.vii.iii.xliv-p6"></a><span class="sc"><a name="viii.vii.iii.xliv-p6.1"></a>Balsamon.</span></p> 
 <p class="c18"><a name="viii.vii.iii.xliv-p7"></a>Read canon lxx. and canon lxxj. of the Holy Apostles, and Canon lx. <a name="fr_196"></a><a href="#fn_196"><font size="1">197</font></a>
  of the Synod of Carthage.</p> 
 <p class="c36"><a name="viii.vii.iii.xliv-p9"></a><span class="sc"><a name="viii.vii.iii.xliv-p9.1"></a>Aristenus.</span></p> 
 <p class="c18"><a name="viii.vii.iii.xliv-p10"></a>Light hath no communion with darkness.  Therefore no Christian should celebrate a feast with heretics or Jews, neither should he receive anything connected with these feasts such as azymes and the like.</p>
