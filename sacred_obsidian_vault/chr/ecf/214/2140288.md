 </p><p class="c34"><a name="x.xvi.vi-p1"></a> <span class="c4"><a name="x.xvi.vi-p1.1"></a>Canon IV.</span></p> 
 <p class="c18"><a name="x.xvi.vi-p2"></a><span class="sc"><a name="x.xvi.vi-p2.1"></a>If</span> any of the clergy should fall away, and publicly or privately presume to maintain the doctrines of Nestorius or Celestius, it is declared just by the holy Synod that these also should be deposed.</p>

  <i>or Nestorius, let them be deposed.</i></p>
