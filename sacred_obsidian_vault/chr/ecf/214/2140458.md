 <p> <a name="xiv.iii.lxxv"></a>
 </p><p class="c34"><a name="xiv.iii.lxxv-p1"></a> <span class="c4"><a name="xiv.iii.lxxv-p1.1"></a>Canon LXXIV.</span></p> 
 <p class="c18"><a name="xiv.iii.lxxv-p2"></a><span class="sc"><a name="xiv.iii.lxxv-p2.1"></a>It</span> is not permitted to hold what are called Agapæ, that is love-feasts, in the Lords houses or churches, nor to eat within the house, nor to spread couches.  If any dare to do so let him cease therefrom or be cut off.</p> 
 <p class="c31"><a name="xiv.iii.lxxv-p3"></a><span class="c4"><a name="xiv.iii.lxxv-p3.1"></a>Notes.</span></p> 
 <p class="c36"><a name="xiv.iii.lxxv-p4"></a><span class="sc"><a name="xiv.iii.lxxv-p4.1"></a>Ancient Epitome of Canon LXXIV.</span></p> 
 <p class="c35"><a name="xiv.iii.lxxv-p5"></a>Agapæ are not to be held in the churches, nor shall beds be put up.  Whoso refuse to give up these, let them be cut off.</p> 
 <p class="c18"><a name="xiv.iii.lxxv-p6"></a>This is a renewal of canon xxviij., of Laodicea, on which canon see the notes.</p>
