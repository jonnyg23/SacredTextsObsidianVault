 </p><p class="c34"><a name="xv.iv.iv.xlvii-p1"></a> <span class="c4"><a name="xv.iv.iv.xlvii-p1.1"></a>Canon XLVI.  (Greek l.)</span></p> 
 <p class="c50"><a name="xv.iv.iv.xlvii-p2"></a>Concerning the passions of the martyrs.</p> 
 <p class="c18"><a name="xv.iv.iv.xlvii-p3"></a><span class="sc"><a name="xv.iv.iv.xlvii-p3.1"></a>The</span> passions of the Martyrs may be read when their anniversary days are celebrated.</p> 
 <p class="c31"><a name="xv.iv.iv.xlvii-p4"></a><span class="c4"><a name="xv.iv.iv.xlvii-p4.1"></a>Note.</span></p> 
 <p class="c36"><a name="xv.iv.iv.xlvii-p5"></a><span class="sc"><a name="xv.iv.iv.xlvii-p5.1"></a>Ancient Epitome of Canon XLVI.</span></p> 
 <p class="c35"><a name="xv.iv.iv.xlvii-p6"></a>The passions of the martyrs are to be read on their commemorations.</p> 
 <p class="c18"><a name="xv.iv.iv.xlvii-p7"></a>This canon is the last part of Canon xxxvj. of the Synod of Hippo, <span class="sc"><a name="xv.iv.iv.xlvii-p7.1"></a>a.d.</span> 393.</p>
