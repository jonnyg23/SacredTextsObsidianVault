 </p><p class="c23"><a name="ii.iii.iv-p1"></a> <span class="c4"><a name="ii.iii.iv-p1.1"></a>Translations.</span></p>  
 <p class="c18"><a name="ii.iii.iv-p2"></a>1.  <i>Brights Leo on the Incarnation</i>, London, 1862 (2nd edn. enlarged, 1886, in which the Tome is translated), consists of xviii. sermons translated and the Tome in Latin, with many valuable notes.</p>  
 <p><a name="ii.iii.iv-p3"></a>2.  <i>Reithmayrs Bibliothek</i>  (1869) contains a German translation.</p>  
 <p><a name="ii.iii.iv-p4"></a>3.  <i>Dr. Neales History of the Alexandrian Patriarchate</i> embodies a translation of the Tome.</p>  
 <p><a name="ii.iii.iv-p5"></a>4.  Dr. Heurtley published a version of the Tome in 1886.</p>
