 <p> <a name="iv.iv.ix.ii"></a>  </p><h4 align="center"><a name="iv.iv.ix.ii-p0.1"></a>Chapter II.</h4>  
 <p class="subh"><a name="iv.iv.ix.ii-p1"></a>Statements on the different kinds of spiritual wickednesses.</p>  
 <p class="skip"><a name="iv.iv.ix.ii-p2"></a><span class="sc"><a name="iv.iv.ix.ii-p2.1"></a>Then</span> Germanus: We want to know what is the origin of the great variety of hostile powers opposed to men, and the difference between them, which the blessed Apostle sums up as follows: “We wrestle not against flesh and blood, but against principalities, against powers, against the world-rulers of this darkness, against spiritual wickedness in hea<a name="page_376"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 376</font></a> <a name="iv.iv.ix.ii-Page_376"></a>venly places:” <a name="fr_1516"></a><a href="#fn_1516"><font size="1">1517</font></a>
  and again: “Neither angels nor principalities nor powers nor any other creature, can separate us from the love of God which is in Christ Jesus our Lord.” <a name="fr_1517"></a><a href="#fn_1517"><font size="1">1518</font></a>
  Whence then arises the enmity of all this malice jealous of us? Are we to believe that those powers were created by the Lord for this; viz., to fight against men in these grades and orders?</p>
