 <p> <a name="iv.iv.vii.v"></a>  </p><h4 align="center"><a name="iv.iv.vii.v-p0.1"></a>Chapter V.</h4>  
 <p class="subh"><a name="iv.iv.vii.v-p1"></a>An objection, how God Himself can be said to create evil.</p>  
 <p class="skip"><a name="iv.iv.vii.v-p2"></a><span class="sc"><a name="iv.iv.vii.v-p2.1"></a>Germanus</span>: We often read in holy Scripture that God has created evil or brought it upon men, as is this passage: “There is none beside Me. I am the Lord, and there is none else: I form the light and create darkness, I make peace, and create evil.” <a name="fr_1378"></a><a href="#fn_1378"><font size="1">1379</font></a>
  And again: “Shall there be evil in a city which the Lord hath not done?” <a name="fr_1379"></a><a href="#fn_1379"><font size="1">1380</font></a>
