 </p><p class="c41"><a name="vi.XIII.XII-p1"></a><span class="c2"><a name="vi.XIII.XII-p1.1"></a>Chapter XII.—Allegorical Explanation of Genesis, Chap. I., Concerning the Origin of the Church and Its Worship.</span></p>  
 <p class="c10"><a name="vi.XIII.XII-p2"></a>13. Proceed in thy confession, say to the Lord thy God, O my faith, Holy, Holy, Holy, O Lord my God, in Thy name have we been baptized, Father, Son, and Holy Ghost, in Thy name do we baptize, Father, Son, and Holy Ghost, <a name="fr_1205"></a><a href="#fn_1205"><font size="1">1206</font></a>
  because among us also in His Christ did God make heaven and earth, namely, the spiritual and carnal people of His Church. <a name="fr_1206"></a><a href="#fn_1206"><font size="1">1207</font></a>
  Yea, and our earth, before it received the “form of doctrine,” <a name="fr_1207"></a><a href="#fn_1207"><font size="1">1208</font></a>
  was invisible and formless, and we were covered with the darkness of ignorance. For Thou correctest man for iniquity, <a name="fr_1208"></a><a href="#fn_1208"><font size="1">1209</font></a>
  and “Thy judgments are a great deep.” <a name="fr_1209"></a><a href="#fn_1209"><font size="1">1210</font></a>
  But because Thy Spirit was “borne over the waters,” <a name="fr_1210"></a><a href="#fn_1210"><font size="1">1211</font></a>
  Thy mercy forsook not our misery, <a name="fr_1211"></a><a href="#fn_1211"><font size="1">1212</font></a>
  and Thou saidst, “Let there be light,” “Repent ye, for the kingdom of heaven is at hand.” <a name="fr_1212"></a><a href="#fn_1212"><font size="1">1213</font></a>
  Repent ye, let there be light. <a name="fr_1213"></a><a href="#fn_1213"><font size="1">1214</font></a>
  And because our soul was troubled within us, <a name="fr_1214"></a><a href="#fn_1214"><font size="1">1215</font></a>
  we remembered Thee, O Lord, from the land of Jordan, and that mountain <a name="fr_1215"></a><a href="#fn_1215"><font size="1">1216</font></a>
  equal unto Thyself, but little for our sakes; and upon our being displeased with our darkness, we turned unto Thee, “and there was light.” And, behold, we were sometimes darkness, but now light in the Lord. <a name="fr_1216"></a><a href="#fn_1216"><font size="1">1217</font></a>
 </p>
