 <p class="c8"><a name="vi.XIII-p2"></a>————————————</p>  
 <p class="c40"><a name="vi.XIII-p3"></a>Of the goodness of God explained in the creation of things, and of the Trinity as found in the first words of Genesis. The story concerning the origin of the world (Gen. I.) is allegorically explained, and he applies it to those things which God works for sanctified and blessed man. Finally, he makes an end of this work, having implored eternal rest from God.</p>
