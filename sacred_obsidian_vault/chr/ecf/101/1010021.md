 <p class="c41"><a name="vi.I_1.V-p2"></a><span class="c2"><a name="vi.I_1.V-p2.1"></a>Chapter V.—He Seeks Rest in God, and Pardon of His Sins.</span></p>  
 <p class="c10"><a name="vi.I_1.V-p3"></a>5. Oh! how shall I find rest in Thee? Who will send Thee into my heart to inebriate it, so that I may forget my woes, and embrace Thee my only good? What art Thou to me? Have compassion on me, that I may speak. What am I to Thee that Thou demandest my love, and unless I give it Thee art angry, and threatenest me with great sorrows? Is it, then, a light sorrow not to love Thee? Alas! alas! tell me of Thy compassion, O Lord my God, what Thou art to me. “Say unto my soul, I am thy salvation.” <a name="fr_137"></a><a href="#fn_137"><font size="1">138</font></a>
  So speak that I may hear. Behold, Lord, the ears of my heart are before Thee; open Thou them, and “say unto my soul, I am thy salvation.” When I hear, may I run and lay hold on Thee. Hide not Thy face from me. Let me die, lest I die, if only I may see Thy face. <a name="fr_138"></a><a href="#fn_138"><font size="1">139</font></a>
 </p>  
 <p class="c10"><a name="vi.I_1.V-p6"></a>6. Cramped is the dwelling of my soul; do Thou expand it, that Thou mayest enter in. It is in ruins, restore Thou it. There is that about it which must offend Thine eyes; I confess and <a name="page_47"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 47</font></a> <a name="vi.I_1.V-Page_47"></a>know it, but who will cleanse it? or to whom shall I cry but to Thee? Cleanse me from my secret sins, <a name="fr_139"></a><a href="#fn_139"><font size="1">140</font></a>
  O Lord, and keep Thy servant from those of other men. I believe, and therefore do I speak; <a name="fr_140"></a><a href="#fn_140"><font size="1">141</font></a>
  Lord, Thou knowest. Have I not confessed my transgressions unto Thee, O my God; and Thou hast put away the iniquity of my heart? <a name="fr_141"></a><a href="#fn_141"><font size="1">142</font></a>
  I do not contend in judgment with Thee, <a name="fr_142"></a><a href="#fn_142"><font size="1">143</font></a>
  who art the Truth; and I would not deceive myself, lest my iniquity lie against itself. <a name="fr_143"></a><a href="#fn_143"><font size="1">144</font></a>
  I do not, therefore, contend in judgment with Thee, for “if Thou, Lord, shouldest mark iniquities, O Lord, who shall stand?” <a name="fr_144"></a><a href="#fn_144"><font size="1">145</font></a>
 </p>
