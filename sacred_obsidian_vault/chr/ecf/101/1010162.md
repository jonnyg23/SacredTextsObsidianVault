 <p> <a name="vi.X.V"></a>  
 </p><p class="c41"><a name="vi.X.V-p1"></a><span class="c2"><a name="vi.X.V-p1.1"></a>Chapter V.—That Man Knoweth Not Himself Wholly.</span></p>  
 <p class="c10"><a name="vi.X.V-p2"></a>7. For it is Thou, Lord, that judgest me; <a name="fr_834"></a><a href="#fn_834"><font size="1">835</font></a>
  for although no “man knoweth the things of a <a name="page_144"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 144</font></a> <a name="vi.X.V-Page_144"></a>man, save the spirit of man which is in him,” <a name="fr_835"></a><a href="#fn_835"><font size="1">836</font></a>
  yet is there something of man which “the spirit of man which is in him” itself knoweth not. But Thou, Lord, who hast made him, knowest him wholly. I indeed, though in Thy sight I despise myself, and reckon “myself but dust and ashes,” <a name="fr_836"></a><a href="#fn_836"><font size="1">837</font></a>
  yet know something concerning Thee, which I know not concerning myself. And assuredly “now we see through a glass darkly,” not yet “face to face.” <a name="fr_837"></a><a href="#fn_837"><font size="1">838</font></a>
  So long, therefore, as I be “absent” from Thee, I am more “present” with myself than with Thee; <a name="fr_838"></a><a href="#fn_838"><font size="1">839</font></a>
  and yet know I that Thou canst not suffer violence; <a name="fr_839"></a><a href="#fn_839"><font size="1">840</font></a>
  but for myself I know not what temptations I am able to resist, and what I am not able. <a name="fr_840"></a><a href="#fn_840"><font size="1">841</font></a>
  But there is hope, because Thou art faithful, who wilt not suffer us to be tempted above that we are able, but wilt with the temptation also make a way to escape, that we may be able to bear it. <a name="fr_841"></a><a href="#fn_841"><font size="1">842</font></a>
  I would therefore confess what I know concerning myself; I will confess also what I know not concerning myself. And because what I do know of myself, I know by Thee enlightening me; and what I know not of myself, so long I know not until the time when my “darkness be as the noonday” <a name="fr_842"></a><a href="#fn_842"><font size="1">843</font></a>
  in Thy sight.</p>
