 <p class="c8"><a name="vi.VIII-p2"></a>————————————</p>  
 <p class="c40"><a name="vi.VIII-p3"></a>He finally describes the thirty-second year of his age, the most memorable of his whole life, in which, being instructed by Simplicianus concerning the conversion of others, and the manner of acting, he is, after a severe struggle, renewed in his whole mind, and is converted unto God.</p>
