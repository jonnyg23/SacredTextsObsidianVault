 </p><p class="c20"><a name="iv.1.III-p1"></a>  III. <span class="c9"><a name="iv.1.III-p1.1"></a>special treatises on the system of augustin</span>.</p>  
 <p class="c10"><a name="iv.1.III-p2"></a>(1) The <i>Theology</i> of Augustin. The Church Histories of <span class="c9"><a name="iv.1.III-p2.1"></a>Neander, Baur, Hase</span> (his large work, 1885, vol. I. 514 sqq.), and the Doctrine Histories of <span class="c9"><a name="iv.1.III-p2.2"></a>Neander, Gieseler, Baur, Hagenbach, Shedd, Nitzsch, Schwane, Bach, Harnack</span> (in preparation, first vol., 1886).</p>  
 <p class="c10"><a name="iv.1.III-p3"></a>The voluminous literature on the Pelagian controversy embraces works of <span class="c9"><a name="iv.1.III-p3.1"></a>G. J. Voss, Garnier, Jansen</span> (died 1638; <i>Augustinus</i>, 1640, 3 vols.; he read Aug. twenty times and revived his system in the R. Cath. Church, but was condemned by the Pope), Cardinal <span class="c9"><a name="iv.1.III-p3.2"></a> Noris</span> (<i>Historia Pelagiana</i>, Florence, 1673), <span class="c9"><a name="iv.1.III-p3.3"></a>Walch</span> (<i>Ketzergeschichte</i>, vols. IV. and V., 1768 and 1770), <span class="c9"><a name="iv.1.III-p3.4"></a>Wiggers</span> (<i>Augustinismus und Pelagianismus</i>, 1821 and 1833), <span class="c9"><a name="iv.1.III-p3.5"></a> Bersot</span> (<i>Doctr. de St. Aug. sur la liberté et la Providence</i>, Paris, 1843), <span class="c9"><a name="iv.1.III-p3.6"></a>Jacobi</span> (<i>Lehre des Pelagius</i>, 1842), <span class="c9"><a name="iv.1.III-p3.7"></a>Jul. Müller</span> (<i>Lehre von der Sünde</i>, 5th ed. 1866, Engl. transl. by Urwick, 1868), <span class="c9"><a name="iv.1.III-p3.8"></a>Mozley</span> (<i>Augustinian Doctrine of Predestination</i>, London, 1855, very able), <span class="c9"><a name="iv.1.III-p3.9"></a>W. Bright</span> (Introduction to his ed. of the Anti-Pelag. writings of Aug. Oxford 1880), and others. See <span class="c9"><a name="iv.1.III-p3.10"></a>Schaff</span>, vol III. 783-785.</p>  
 <p class="c10"><a name="iv.1.III-p4"></a><span class="c9"><a name="iv.1.III-p4.1"></a>Van Goens</span>: <i>De Aur. August. apologeta, sec. 1 de Civitate Dei</i>. Amstel. 1838.</p>  
 <p class="c10"><a name="iv.1.III-p5"></a><span class="c9"><a name="iv.1.III-p5.1"></a>Nirschl</span> (Rom. Cath.). <i> Ursprung und Wesen des Bösen nach der Lehre des heil. Augustin</i>. 1854.</p>  
 <p class="c10"><a name="iv.1.III-p6"></a><span class="c9"><a name="iv.1.III-p6.1"></a>F. Ribbeck</span>: <i>Donatus und Augustinus, oder der erste entscheidende Kampf zwischen Separatismus und Kirche</i>. Elberfeld, 1858, 2 vols.</p>  
 <p class="c10"><a name="iv.1.III-p7"></a><span class="c9"><a name="iv.1.III-p7.1"></a>Fr. Nitzsch</span>: <i>Augustins Lehre vom Wunder</i>. Berlin, 1865.</p>  
 <p class="c10"><a name="iv.1.III-p8"></a><span class="c9"><a name="iv.1.III-p8.1"></a>Gangauf</span>: <i>Des heil. August. Lehre von Gott dem dreieinigen</i>. Augsburg, 1866. <span class="c9"><a name="iv.1.III-p8.2"></a>Emil Feuerlein</span>: <i>Ueber die Stellung Augustins in der Kirchen=und Kulturgeschichte</i>, in Sybels “Histor. Zeitschrift” for 1869, vol. XI. 270-313. <span class="c9"><a name="iv.1.III-p8.3"></a>Naville</span>: <i>Saint Augustin, Etude sur le développement de sa pensée</i>. Genève, 1872. <span class="c9"><a name="iv.1.III-p8.4"></a> Ernst</span>: <i>Die Werke und Tugenden der Ungläubigen nach Augustin</i>. Freiburg, 1872. <span class="c9"><a name="iv.1.III-p8.5"></a>Aug. Dorner</span> (son of Is. A. D.): <i>Augustinus, sein theol. System und seine religionsphilosophische Anschauung</i>. Berlin, 1873 (comp. his art. in Herzogs “Encycl.” 2d ed. I. 781-795, abridged in Schaff-Herzog I. 174 sqq.). <span class="c9"><a name="iv.1.III-p8.6"></a>Ch. H. Collett</span>: <i>St. Aug., a Sketch of his Life and Writings as affecting the controversy with Rome</i>. London, 1883. <span class="c9"><a name="iv.1.III-p8.7"></a>H. Reuter</span> (Prof. of Church <a name="page_3"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 3</font></a> <a name="iv.1.III-Page_3"></a>History in Göttingen): <i>Augustinische Studien</i>, in Briegers “Zeitschrift für Kirchengeschichte,” for 1880-86 (several articles on Aug.s doctrine of the church, of predestination, the kingdom of God, etc.,—very valuable).</p>

 <p class="c10"><a name="iv.1.III-p10"></a><span class="c9"><a name="iv.1.III-p10.1"></a>Theod. Gangauf</span>: <i> Metaphysische Psychologie des heil. Augustinus</i>. 1ste Abtheilung, Augsburg, 1852. <span class="c9"><a name="iv.1.III-p10.2"></a>T. Théry</span>: <i> Le génie philosophique et littéraire de saint Augustin</i>. Par. 1861.
