 <p> <a name="iv.iii.xlvi"></a>
 </p><p class="c27"><a name="iv.iii.xlvi-p1"></a> <span class="c6"><a name="iv.iii.xlvi-p1.1"></a>Section XLVI.</span></p> 
 <p class="c12"><a name="iv.iii.xlvi-p2"></a>[1]  <a name="fr_3164"></a><a href="#fn_3164"><font size="1">3165</font></a>
 Whosoever hath my commandments, and keepeth them, he it is that loveth me:  and he that loveth me shall be loved of my Father, and I will love him, and will [2] shew myself unto him.   <a name="fr_3165"></a><a href="#fn_3165"><font size="1">3166</font></a>
 Judas (not Iscariot) said unto him, My Lord, what is the [3] purpose of thy intention to shew thyself to us, and not to the world?   <a name="fr_3166"></a><a href="#fn_3166"><font size="1">3167</font></a>
 Jesus answered and said unto him, Whosoever loveth me will keep my word:  and my Father will love him, and to him will we come, and make our <a name="fr_3167"></a><a href="#fn_3167"><font size="1">3168</font></a>
  abode with him.  [4]  <a name="fr_3168"></a><a href="#fn_3168"><font size="1">3169</font></a>
 But he that loveth me not keepeth not my word:  and this word that ye hear is not my word, but the Fathers which sent me.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.xlvi-p8"></a>[5, 6]  <a name="fr_3169"></a><a href="#fn_3169"><font size="1">3170</font></a>
 This have I spoken unto you, while I was yet with you.   <a name="fr_3170"></a><a href="#fn_3170"><font size="1">3171</font></a>
 But the Paraclete, the Holy Spirit, whom my Father will send in my name, he will teach you everything, and [7] [Arabic, p. 174] he will bring to your remembrance all that I say unto you.   <a name="fr_3171"></a><a href="#fn_3171"><font size="1">3172</font></a>
 Peace I leave you; my peace I give unto you:  and not as this world giveth, give I unto you.  [8]  <a name="fr_3172"></a><a href="#fn_3172"><font size="1">3173</font></a>
 Let your heart not be troubled, <a name="fr_3173"></a><a href="#fn_3173"><font size="1">3174</font></a>
  nor fearful.  Ye heard that I said unto you, that I go away, and come unto you.  If <a name="fr_3174"></a><a href="#fn_3174"><font size="1">3175</font></a>
  ye loved me, ye would rejoice, that I go away to my [9] Father:  for my Father is greater than I.   <a name="fr_3175"></a><a href="#fn_3175"><font size="1">3176</font></a>
 And now I say unto you before it come [10] to pass, that, when it cometh to pass, ye may believe me.   <a name="fr_3176"></a><a href="#fn_3176"><font size="1">3177</font></a>
 Now I will not speak with you much:  the Archon of the world will come, and he will have nothing in [11] me:   <a name="fr_3177"></a><a href="#fn_3177"><font size="1">3178</font></a>
 but that the world may know that I love my Father, and as my Father charged me, so I do.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.xlvi-p18"></a>[12]  <a name="fr_3178"></a><a href="#fn_3178"><font size="1">3179</font></a>
 And he said unto them, When I sent you without purses, or wallets, and shoes, <a name="fr_3179"></a><a href="#fn_3179"><font size="1">3180</font></a>
  [13] lacked ye perchance anything?  They said unto him, Nothing.   <a name="fr_3180"></a><a href="#fn_3180"><font size="1">3181</font></a>
 He said unto them, Henceforth, whosoever hath a purse, let him take it, and likewise the wallet also:  and whosoever hath not a sword, shall sell his garment, and buy for himself a [14] sword.   <a name="fr_3181"></a><a href="#fn_3181"><font size="1">3182</font></a>
 I say unto you, that this scripture also must be fulfilled in me, that I should be reckoned <a name="fr_3182"></a><a href="#fn_3182"><font size="1">3183</font></a>
  with the transgressors:  for all that is said of me is fulfilled in [15] me.   <a name="fr_3183"></a><a href="#fn_3183"><font size="1">3184</font></a>
 His disciples said unto him, Our Lord, lo, here are two swords.  He said [16] unto them, They are sufficient.   <a name="fr_3184"></a><a href="#fn_3184"><font size="1">3185</font></a>
 Arise, let us go hence.  And they arose, and praised, and went forth, and went, according to their custom, to the mount of Olives, he and his disciples.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.xlvi-p26"></a>[17]  <a name="fr_3185"></a><a href="#fn_3185"><font size="1">3186</font></a>
 And he said unto them, I am the true vine, and my Father is the husbandman.  [18]  <a name="fr_3186"></a><a href="#fn_3186"><font size="1">3187</font></a>
 Every branch that produceth not fruit in me, he taketh it:  and that which giveth fruit, [19] he cleanseth it, that it may give much fruit.   <a name="fr_3187"></a><a href="#fn_3187"><font size="1">3188</font></a>
 Ye are already clean because of the word [20] that I have spoken unto you.   <a name="fr_3188"></a><a href="#fn_3188"><font size="1">3189</font></a>
 Abide in me, and I in you.  And as the branch of the [Arabic, p. 175] vine cannot produce fruit of itself, if it be not abiding in the vine; so too ye [21] also, if ye abide not in me.   <a name="fr_3189"></a><a href="#fn_3189"><font size="1">3190</font></a>
 I am the vine, and ye are the branches:  He then that abideth in me, and I in him, he giveth much fruit:  for without me ye cannot [22] do anything.   <a name="fr_3190"></a><a href="#fn_3190"><font size="1">3191</font></a>
 And if a man abide not in me, he is cast without, like a withered [23] branch; and it is gathered, and cast <a name="fr_3191"></a><a href="#fn_3191"><font size="1">3192</font></a>
  into the fire, that it may be burned.   <a name="fr_3192"></a><a href="#fn_3192"><font size="1">3193</font></a>
 If ye abide in me, and my word abide in you, everything that ye desire to ask shall be [24] <i>done</i> unto you.   <a name="fr_3193"></a><a href="#fn_3193"><font size="1">3194</font></a>
 And herein is the Father glorified, that ye may give much fruit; [25] and ye <i>shall</i> be my disciples.   <a name="fr_3194"></a><a href="#fn_3194"><font size="1">3195</font></a>
 And as my Father loved me, I loved you also:  [26] abide in my love.   <a name="fr_3195"></a><a href="#fn_3195"><font size="1">3196</font></a>
 If ye keep my commands, ye shall abide in my love; as I have [27] kept my Fathers commands, and abode in his love.   <a name="fr_3196"></a><a href="#fn_3196"><font size="1">3197</font></a>
 I have spoken that unto you, [28] that my joy <a name="fr_3197"></a><a href="#fn_3197"><font size="1">3198</font></a>
  may be in you, and your joy <a name="fr_3198"></a><a href="#fn_3198"><font size="1">3199</font></a>
  be fulfilled.   <a name="fr_3199"></a><a href="#fn_3199"><font size="1">3200</font></a>
 This is my commandment, [29] that ye love one another, as I loved you.   <a name="fr_3200"></a><a href="#fn_3200"><font size="1">3201</font></a>
 And no love is greater than this, namely, [30] that a man should give his life for his friends.   <a name="fr_3201"></a><a href="#fn_3201"><font size="1">3202</font></a>
 Ye are my friends, if ye do all that [31] I command you.   <a name="fr_3202"></a><a href="#fn_3202"><font size="1">3203</font></a>
 I call you not now servants; for the servant knoweth not what <a name="page_115"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 115</font></a> <a name="iv.iii.xlvi-Page_115"></a>his lord doeth:  my friends have I now called you; for everything that I heard from [32] my Father I have made known unto you.   <a name="fr_3203"></a><a href="#fn_3203"><font size="1">3204</font></a>
 Ye did not choose <a name="fr_3204"></a><a href="#fn_3204"><font size="1">3205</font></a>
  me, but I chose I you, and appointed you, that ye also should go and bear fruit, and <i>that</i> your fruit should <a name="fr_3205"></a><a href="#fn_3205"><font size="1">3206</font></a>
  [33] abide; and <i>that</i> all that ye shall ask my Father in my name, he may <a name="fr_3206"></a><a href="#fn_3206"><font size="1">3207</font></a>
  give you.   <a name="fr_3207"></a><a href="#fn_3207"><font size="1">3208</font></a>
 This [34] I command <a name="fr_3208"></a><a href="#fn_3208"><font size="1">3209</font></a>
  you, that ye love one another.   <a name="fr_3209"></a><a href="#fn_3209"><font size="1">3210</font></a>
 And if the world hate you, know that [35] before you it hated me.   <a name="fr_3210"></a><a href="#fn_3210"><font size="1">3211</font></a>
 If then ye were of the world, the world would love its own:  but ye are not of the world:  I chose you out of the world:  therefore the world [36] [Arabic, p. 176] hateth you.   <a name="fr_3211"></a><a href="#fn_3211"><font size="1">3212</font></a>
 Remember the word that I said unto you, that no servant is greater than his lord.  And if they persecuted <a name="fr_3212"></a><a href="#fn_3212"><font size="1">3213</font></a>
  me, you also will they [37] persecute; <a name="fr_3213"></a><a href="#fn_3213"><font size="1">3214</font></a>
  and if they kept my word, your word also will they keep.   <a name="fr_3214"></a><a href="#fn_3214"><font size="1">3215</font></a>
 But all these things will they do unto you for my names sake, for they have not known <a name="fr_3215"></a><a href="#fn_3215"><font size="1">3216</font></a>
  him [38] that sent me.   <a name="fr_3216"></a><a href="#fn_3216"><font size="1">3217</font></a>
 And if I had not come and spoken unto them, they had not had sin:  [39] but now they have no excuse for their sins.   <a name="fr_3217"></a><a href="#fn_3217"><font size="1">3218</font></a>
 Whosoever hateth me, also hateth my [40] Father.   <a name="fr_3218"></a><a href="#fn_3218"><font size="1">3219</font></a>
 And if I had not done the deeds before them that no other man did, they would not have had sin:  but now they have seen and hated me and my Father [41] also:  that the word may be fulfilled that is written in their law,  <a name="fr_3219"></a><a href="#fn_3219"><font size="1">3220</font></a>
 They hated me for [42] nothing.   <a name="fr_3220"></a><a href="#fn_3220"><font size="1">3221</font></a>
 But when the Paraclete is come, whom I will send unto you from my Father, even the Spirit of truth, which goeth forth from my Father, he shall bear witness of [43] me:   <a name="fr_3221"></a><a href="#fn_3221"><font size="1">3222</font></a>
 and ye also bear witness, because from the beginning ye <i>have been</i> with me.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.xlvi-p64"></a>[44, 45]  <a name="fr_3222"></a><a href="#fn_3222"><font size="1">3223</font></a>
 I have said that unto you, that ye may not stumble. <a name="fr_3223"></a><a href="#fn_3223"><font size="1">3224</font></a>
    <a name="fr_3224"></a><a href="#fn_3224"><font size="1">3225</font></a>
 And they shall put you out of their synagogues:  and there cometh an <a name="fr_3225"></a><a href="#fn_3225"><font size="1">3226</font></a>
  hour when every one that killeth [46] you shall think that he hath offered unto God an offering.   <a name="fr_3226"></a><a href="#fn_3226"><font size="1">3227</font></a>
 And they will do that, [47] because they do not know me, nor my Father.   <a name="fr_3227"></a><a href="#fn_3227"><font size="1">3228</font></a>
 I have said that unto you, so that [48] when its time is come, ye may remember it, that I told you.   <a name="fr_3228"></a><a href="#fn_3228"><font size="1">3229</font></a>
 And this hitherto I said not unto you, because I was with you.  But <a name="fr_3229"></a><a href="#fn_3229"><font size="1">3230</font></a>
  now I go unto him that sent me; and no [49] man of you asketh me whither I go.   <a name="fr_3230"></a><a href="#fn_3230"><font size="1">3231</font></a>
 I have said that unto you now, and grief hath [50] come and taken possession of your hearts    <a name="fr_3231"></a><a href="#fn_3231"><font size="1">3232</font></a>
 But I say the truth unto you; It is better <a name="fr_3232"></a><a href="#fn_3232"><font size="1">3233</font></a>
  for you that I go away:  for if I go not away, the Paraclete will not come unto you; [51] [Arabic, p. 177] but if I go away, I will send him unto you.   <a name="fr_3233"></a><a href="#fn_3233"><font size="1">3234</font></a>
 And when he cometh, he will reprove the world for sin, and for righteousness, and for judgement:  [52, 53] for sin,  <a name="fr_3234"></a><a href="#fn_3234"><font size="1">3235</font></a>
 because they have not believed in me;  <a name="fr_3235"></a><a href="#fn_3235"><font size="1">3236</font></a>
 and for righteousness, because I go [54] to my Father;  <a name="fr_3236"></a><a href="#fn_3236"><font size="1">3237</font></a>
 and for judgement, because <a name="fr_3237"></a><a href="#fn_3237"><font size="1">3238</font></a>
  the Archon of this world hath been [55] judged.   <a name="fr_3238"></a><a href="#fn_3238"><font size="1">3239</font></a>
 And further have I many things to speak unto you, but ye cannot tarry <a name="fr_3239"></a><a href="#fn_3239"><font size="1">3240</font></a>
  [56] now.   <a name="fr_3240"></a><a href="#fn_3240"><font size="1">3241</font></a>
 Howbeit <a name="fr_3241"></a><a href="#fn_3241"><font size="1">3242</font></a>
  when the Spirit of truth is come, he will remind <a name="fr_3242"></a><a href="#fn_3242"><font size="1">3243</font></a>
  you of all the truth:  he will say nothing from himself; but everything that he heareth, that shall [57] he say:  and he shall make known unto you the things that are to be.   <a name="fr_3243"></a><a href="#fn_3243"><font size="1">3244</font></a>
 And he shall [58] glorify me; for from me shall he take and shew you.   <a name="fr_3244"></a><a href="#fn_3244"><font size="1">3245</font></a>
 All that my Father hath is mine:  therefore said I unto you, that he taketh <a name="fr_3245"></a><a href="#fn_3245"><font size="1">3246</font></a>
  of mine, and shall shew <a name="fr_3246"></a><a href="#fn_3246"><font size="1">3247</font></a>
  you.</p>
