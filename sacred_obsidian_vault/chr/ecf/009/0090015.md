 <p> <a name="iv.iii.vi"></a>
 </p><p class="c27"><a name="iv.iii.vi-p1"></a> <span class="c6"><a name="iv.iii.vi-p1.1"></a>Section VI.</span></p> 
 <p class="c12"><a name="iv.iii.vi-p2"></a>[1] [Arabic, p. 22]  <a name="fr_445"></a><a href="#fn_445"><font size="1">446</font></a>
 But when Simon Cephas saw <i>this</i> he fell before the feet of Jesus, and said unto him, My Lord, I beseech of thee to depart from me, for I am [2] a sinful man.   <a name="fr_446"></a><a href="#fn_446"><font size="1">447</font></a>
 And amazement took possession of him, and of all who were with him, [3] because of the draught of the fishes which they had taken.   <a name="fr_447"></a><a href="#fn_447"><font size="1">448</font></a>
 And thus also were James and John the sons of Zebedee overtaken, <a name="fr_448"></a><a href="#fn_448"><font size="1">449</font></a>
  who were Simons partners.  And Jesus said [4] unto Simon, Fear not; henceforth thou shalt be a fisher of men unto life.   <a name="fr_449"></a><a href="#fn_449"><font size="1">450</font></a>
 And they brought the boats to the land; and they left everything, and followed him.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.vi-p8"></a>[5]  <a name="fr_450"></a><a href="#fn_450"><font size="1">451</font></a>
 And after that came Jesus and his disciples into the land of Judæa; and he went [6] about there with them, and baptized.   <a name="fr_451"></a><a href="#fn_451"><font size="1">452</font></a>
 And John also was baptizing in Ænon, which is beside Salim, because there was much water there:  and they came, and were baptized.  [7, 8]  <a name="fr_452"></a><a href="#fn_452"><font size="1">453</font></a>
 And John was not yet come into prison.   <a name="fr_453"></a><a href="#fn_453"><font size="1">454</font></a>
 And there was an inquiry between [9] one of Johns disciples and one of the Jews about purifying.   <a name="fr_454"></a><a href="#fn_454"><font size="1">455</font></a>
 And they <a name="fr_455"></a><a href="#fn_455"><font size="1">456</font></a>
  came unto John, and said unto him, Our master, he that was with thee beyond Jordan, to whom [10] thou hast borne witness, behold, he also baptizeth, and many come to him.   <a name="fr_456"></a><a href="#fn_456"><font size="1">457</font></a>
 John answered and said unto them, <a name="fr_457"></a><a href="#fn_457"><font size="1">458</font></a>
  A man can receive nothing of himself, except it be [11] given him <a name="fr_458"></a><a href="#fn_458"><font size="1">459</font></a>
  from heaven.   <a name="fr_459"></a><a href="#fn_459"><font size="1">460</font></a>
 Ye are they that bear witness unto me that I said, I am [12] not the Messiah, but I am one sent <a name="fr_460"></a><a href="#fn_460"><font size="1">461</font></a>
  before him.   <a name="fr_461"></a><a href="#fn_461"><font size="1">462</font></a>
 And he that hath a bride is a bridegroom:  and the friend of the bridegroom is he that standeth and listeneth to him, and rejoiceth greatly because of the bridegrooms voice.  Lo now, <a name="fr_462"></a><a href="#fn_462"><font size="1">463</font></a>
  behold, [13, 14] [Arabic, p. 23] my joy becometh complete. <a name="fr_463"></a><a href="#fn_463"><font size="1">464</font></a>
    <a name="fr_464"></a><a href="#fn_464"><font size="1">465</font></a>
 And he must increase and I decrease.   <a name="fr_465"></a><a href="#fn_465"><font size="1">466</font></a>
 For <a name="fr_466"></a><a href="#fn_466"><font size="1">467</font></a>
  he that is come from above is higher than everything; and he that is of the earth, of the earth he is, and of the earth he speaketh; and he that came down from heaven is [15] higher than all.   <a name="fr_467"></a><a href="#fn_467"><font size="1">468</font></a>
 And he beareth witness of what he hath seen and heard:  and no man [16] receiveth his witness.   <a name="fr_468"></a><a href="#fn_468"><font size="1">469</font></a>
 And he that hath received his witness hath asserted <a name="fr_469"></a><a href="#fn_469"><font size="1">470</font></a>
  that he is [17] truly God. <a name="fr_470"></a><a href="#fn_470"><font size="1">471</font></a>
    <a name="fr_471"></a><a href="#fn_471"><font size="1">472</font></a>
 And he whom God hath sent speaketh the words <a name="fr_472"></a><a href="#fn_472"><font size="1">473</font></a>
  of God:  God gave [18] not the Spirit by measure.   <a name="fr_473"></a><a href="#fn_473"><font size="1">474</font></a>
 The Father loveth the Son, and hath put everything in [19] his hands.   <a name="fr_474"></a><a href="#fn_474"><font size="1">475</font></a>
 Whosoever believeth in the Son hath eternal <a name="fr_475"></a><a href="#fn_475"><font size="1">476</font></a>
  life; but whosoever obeyeth not the Son shall not see life, but the wrath of God cometh <a name="fr_476"></a><a href="#fn_476"><font size="1">477</font></a>
  upon him.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.vi-p36"></a>[20]  <a name="fr_477"></a><a href="#fn_477"><font size="1">478</font></a>
 And Jesus learned <a name="fr_478"></a><a href="#fn_478"><font size="1">479</font></a>
  that the Pharisees had heard that he had received many disciples, [21] and that he was baptizing more than John  <a name="fr_479"></a><a href="#fn_479"><font size="1">480</font></a>
 (not that Jesus was himself baptizing, [22] but his disciples);  <a name="fr_480"></a><a href="#fn_480"><font size="1">481</font></a>
 and <i>so</i> he left Judæa.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.vi-p41"></a>[23]  <a name="fr_481"></a><a href="#fn_481"><font size="1">482</font></a>
 And Herod the governor, because he used to be rebuked by John because of <a name="page_53"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 53</font></a> <a name="iv.iii.vi-Page_53"></a>Herodias the wife of Philip his brother, and for all the sins which he was committing, [24] added to all that also this,  <a name="fr_482"></a><a href="#fn_482"><font size="1">483</font></a>
 that he shut up John in prison.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.vi-p44"></a>[25]  <a name="fr_483"></a><a href="#fn_483"><font size="1">484</font></a>
 And when Jesus heard that John was delivered up, he went away to Galilee.  [26]  <a name="fr_484"></a><a href="#fn_484"><font size="1">485</font></a>
 And he entered again into Cana, where he had made the water wine.  And there [27] was at Capernaum a kings servant, whose son was sick.   <a name="fr_485"></a><a href="#fn_485"><font size="1">486</font></a>
 And this <i>man</i> heard that Jesus was come from Judæa to Galilee; and he went to him, and besought of him that he would come down and heal his son; for he had come near unto death.  [28, 29]  <a name="fr_486"></a><a href="#fn_486"><font size="1">487</font></a>
 Jesus said unto him, Except ye see signs and wonders, ye do <a name="fr_487"></a><a href="#fn_487"><font size="1">488</font></a>
  not believe.   <a name="fr_488"></a><a href="#fn_488"><font size="1">489</font></a>
 The [Arabic, p. 24] kings servant said unto him, My Lord, come down, that the child die not.  [30]  <a name="fr_489"></a><a href="#fn_489"><font size="1">490</font></a>
 Jesus said unto him, Go; for thy son is alive.  And that man believed the [31] word which Jesus spake, and went.   <a name="fr_490"></a><a href="#fn_490"><font size="1">491</font></a>
 And when he went down, his servants met him [32] and told him, and <a name="fr_491"></a><a href="#fn_491"><font size="1">492</font></a>
  said unto him, Thy son is alive.   <a name="fr_492"></a><a href="#fn_492"><font size="1">493</font></a>
 And he asked them at what time he recovered.  They said unto him, Yesterday at the seventh hour the fever left [33] him.   <a name="fr_493"></a><a href="#fn_493"><font size="1">494</font></a>
 And his father knew that that was at that hour in which Jesus said unto him, [34] Thy son is alive.   <a name="fr_494"></a><a href="#fn_494"><font size="1">495</font></a>
 And he believed, he and the whole people of his house.  And this [35] is the second sign <a name="fr_495"></a><a href="#fn_495"><font size="1">496</font></a>
  which Jesus did when he returned from Judæa to Galilee.   <a name="fr_496"></a><a href="#fn_496"><font size="1">497</font></a>
 And he was preaching in the synagogues of Galilee.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.vi-p59"></a>[36]  <a name="fr_497"></a><a href="#fn_497"><font size="1">498</font></a>
 And he left Nazareth, and came and dwelt in Capernaum by the sea shore, in the [37] borders of Zebulun and Naphtali:   <a name="fr_498"></a><a href="#fn_498"><font size="1">499</font></a>
 that it might be fulfilled which was said in Isaiah the prophet, who said,</p> 
 <p class="c28"><a name="iv.iii.vi-p62"></a>[38]  <a name="fr_499"></a><a href="#fn_499"><font size="1">500</font></a>
 The land of Zebulun, the land of Naphtali,</p> 
 <p class="c29"><a name="iv.iii.vi-p64"></a>The way of the sea, the passage of the Jordan,</p> 
 <p class="c29"><a name="iv.iii.vi-p65"></a>Galilee of the nations:</p> 
 <p class="c29"><a name="iv.iii.vi-p66"></a>[39]  <a name="fr_500"></a><a href="#fn_500"><font size="1">501</font></a>
 The people sitting in darkness</p> 
 <p class="c29"><a name="iv.iii.vi-p68"></a>Saw a great light,</p> 
 <p class="c29"><a name="iv.iii.vi-p69"></a>And those sitting in the region and in the shadow of death,</p> 
 <p class="c21"><a name="iv.iii.vi-p70"></a>There appeared to them a light.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.vi-p71"></a>[40]  <a name="fr_501"></a><a href="#fn_501"><font size="1">502</font></a>
 And he taught them on the sabbaths.   <a name="fr_502"></a><a href="#fn_502"><font size="1">503</font></a>
 And they wondered because of his doctrine: <a name="fr_503"></a><a href="#fn_503"><font size="1">504</font></a>
   [41] for his word was as if it were authoritative.   <a name="fr_504"></a><a href="#fn_504"><font size="1">505</font></a>
 And there was in the synagogue [42] a man with an unclean devil, and he cried out with a loud voice, and said,  <a name="fr_505"></a><a href="#fn_505"><font size="1">506</font></a>
 Let me alone; what have I to do with thee, thou Jesus of Nazareth? art thou come for our [43] destruction?  I know thee who thou art, thou Holy One of God.   <a name="fr_506"></a><a href="#fn_506"><font size="1">507</font></a>
 And Jesus rebuked him, and said, Stop up thy mouth, and come out of him.  And the demon threw him [44] in the midst and came out of him, having done him no harm.   <a name="fr_507"></a><a href="#fn_507"><font size="1">508</font></a>
 And great amazement [Arabic, p. 25] took hold upon every man.  And they talked one with another, and said, What is this word that orders the unclean spirits with power and [45] authority, and they come out?   <a name="fr_508"></a><a href="#fn_508"><font size="1">509</font></a>
 And the news of him spread abroad in all the region which was around them.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.vi-p80"></a>[46]  <a name="fr_509"></a><a href="#fn_509"><font size="1">510</font></a>
 And when Jesus went out of the synagogue,  <a name="fr_510"></a><a href="#fn_510"><font size="1">511</font></a>
 he saw a man sitting among the publicans, <a name="fr_511"></a><a href="#fn_511"><font size="1">512</font></a>
  named Matthew:  and he said unto him, Come after me.  And he rose, and followed him.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.vi-p84"></a>[47, 48]  <a name="fr_512"></a><a href="#fn_512"><font size="1">513</font></a>
 And Jesus came to the house of Simon and Andrew with James and John.   <a name="fr_513"></a><a href="#fn_513"><font size="1">514</font></a>
 And Simons wifes mother was oppressed with a great fever, and they besought him for [49] her.   <a name="fr_514"></a><a href="#fn_514"><font size="1">515</font></a>
 And he stood over her and rebuked her fever, and it left her, and immediately [50] she rose and ministered to them.   <a name="fr_515"></a><a href="#fn_515"><font size="1">516</font></a>
 And at even they brought to him many that had [51] demons:  and he cast out their devils with the <a name="fr_516"></a><a href="#fn_516"><font size="1">517</font></a>
  word.   <a name="fr_517"></a><a href="#fn_517"><font size="1">518</font></a>
 And all that had sick, their diseases being divers <i>and</i> malignant, brought them unto him.  And he laid his hand [52] on them one by one <a name="fr_518"></a><a href="#fn_518"><font size="1">519</font></a>
  and healed them:   <a name="fr_519"></a><a href="#fn_519"><font size="1">520</font></a>
 that that might be fulfilled which was said [53] in the prophet Isaiah, who said, He taketh our pains and beareth our diseases.   <a name="fr_520"></a><a href="#fn_520"><font size="1">521</font></a>
 And [54] all the city was gathered together unto the door of Jesus.   <a name="fr_521"></a><a href="#fn_521"><font size="1">522</font></a>
 And he cast out devils also from many, as they were crying out and saying, Thou art the Messiah, the Son of God; and he rebuked them.  And he suffered not the demons to speak, because they knew him that he was the Lord the Messiah.</p>
