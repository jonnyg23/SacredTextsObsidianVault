 <p> <a name="iv.iii.xiii"></a>
 </p><p class="c27"><a name="iv.iii.xiii-p1"></a> <span class="c6"><a name="iv.iii.xiii-p1.1"></a>Section XIII.</span></p> 
 <p class="c12"><a name="iv.iii.xiii-p2"></a>[1]  <a name="fr_950"></a><a href="#fn_950"><font size="1">951</font></a>
 I am sending you as lambs among wolves:  be ye now wise as serpents, and [2] harmless <a name="fr_951"></a><a href="#fn_951"><font size="1">952</font></a>
  as doves.   <a name="fr_952"></a><a href="#fn_952"><font size="1">953</font></a>
 Beware of men:  they shall deliver you to the councils of the [3] magistrates, and scourge you in their synagogues;  <a name="fr_953"></a><a href="#fn_953"><font size="1">954</font></a>
 and shall bring you before governors and kings for my sake, for a testimony against them and against the nations.  [4]  <a name="fr_954"></a><a href="#fn_954"><font size="1">955</font></a>
 And when they deliver you up, be not <a name="fr_955"></a><a href="#fn_955"><font size="1">956</font></a>
  anxious, nor consider beforehand, what ye [5] shall say; but ye shall be given <a name="fr_956"></a><a href="#fn_956"><font size="1">957</font></a>
  in that hour what ye ought to speak.   <a name="fr_957"></a><a href="#fn_957"><font size="1">958</font></a>
 Ye do not [6] speak, but the Spirit of your Father speaketh in you.   <a name="fr_958"></a><a href="#fn_958"><font size="1">959</font></a>
 The brother shall deliver up his brother to death, and the father his son; and the sons shall rise against their [7] parents, and put them to death.   <a name="fr_959"></a><a href="#fn_959"><font size="1">960</font></a>
 And ye shall be hated of every man because of [8] my name; but he that endureth unto the end of the matter shall be saved. <a name="fr_960"></a><a href="#fn_960"><font size="1">961</font></a>
    <a name="fr_961"></a><a href="#fn_961"><font size="1">962</font></a>
 When they expel you from this city, flee to another.  Verily I say unto you, Ye shall not finish all the cities of the people of Israel, until the Son of man come.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.xiii-p15"></a>[9, 10]  <a name="fr_962"></a><a href="#fn_962"><font size="1">963</font></a>
 A disciple is not superior to his lord, nor a servant to his master.   <a name="fr_963"></a><a href="#fn_963"><font size="1">964</font></a>
 For it is enough then for the disciple that he be as his lord, and the servant as his master.  If they have called the master of the house Beelzebul, how much more the people <a name="page_64"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 64</font></a> <a name="iv.iii.xiii-Page_64"></a>[11] of his house!   <a name="fr_964"></a><a href="#fn_964"><font size="1">965</font></a>
 Fear them not therefore: <a name="fr_965"></a><a href="#fn_965"><font size="1">966</font></a>
   for there is nothing covered, that shall [12] [Arabic, p. 51] not be revealed; nor hid, that shall not be disclosed and published.   <a name="fr_966"></a><a href="#fn_966"><font size="1">967</font></a>
 What I say unto you in the darkness, speak ye in the light; and what ye have told [13] secretly in the ears in closets, let it be proclaimed on the housetops.   <a name="fr_967"></a><a href="#fn_967"><font size="1">968</font></a>
 I say unto you now, my beloved, Be not agitated at <a name="fr_968"></a><a href="#fn_968"><font size="1">969</font></a>
  those who kill the body, but have no power to [14] kill the soul.  I will inform you whom ye shall fear:  him <a name="fr_969"></a><a href="#fn_969"><font size="1">970</font></a>
  which is able to destroy [15] soul and body in hell.   <a name="fr_970"></a><a href="#fn_970"><font size="1">971</font></a>
 Yea, I say unto you, Be afraid of him especially.  Are not two sparrows sold for a farthing in a bond? <a name="fr_971"></a><a href="#fn_971"><font size="1">972</font></a>
  and one of them shall not fall on the [16] ground without your Father.   <a name="fr_972"></a><a href="#fn_972"><font size="1">973</font></a>
 But what concerns you:  even the hair of your heads [17, 18] also is numbered.   <a name="fr_973"></a><a href="#fn_973"><font size="1">974</font></a>
 Fear not therefore; ye are better than many sparrows.   <a name="fr_974"></a><a href="#fn_974"><font size="1">975</font></a>
 Every man who confesseth me now before men, I also will confess him before my Father [19] which is in heaven;  <a name="fr_975"></a><a href="#fn_975"><font size="1">976</font></a>
 but whosoever denieth me before men, I also will deny him before my Father which is in heaven.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.xiii-p30"></a>[20]  <a name="fr_976"></a><a href="#fn_976"><font size="1">977</font></a>
 Think ye that I am come to cast peace into the earth?  I came not to cast peace, [21] but to cast dissension.   <a name="fr_977"></a><a href="#fn_977"><font size="1">978</font></a>
 Henceforth there shall be five in one house, three of them [22] disagreeing with two, and the two with the three.   <a name="fr_978"></a><a href="#fn_978"><font size="1">979</font></a>
 The father shall become hostile to his son, and the son to his father; and the mother to her daughter, and the daughter to her mother; and the mother in law to her daughter in law, and the daughter [23] in law to her mother in law:   <a name="fr_979"></a><a href="#fn_979"><font size="1">980</font></a>
 and a mans enemies shall be the people of his house.  [24]  <a name="fr_980"></a><a href="#fn_980"><font size="1">981</font></a>
 Whosoever loveth father or mother better than me is not worthy of me; and whosoever [Arabic, p. 52] loveth son or daughter more than his love of me is not worthy of me.  [25]  <a name="fr_981"></a><a href="#fn_981"><font size="1">982</font></a>
 And every one that doth not take his cross and follow me is not worthy of [26] me.   <a name="fr_982"></a><a href="#fn_982"><font size="1">983</font></a>
 Whosoever findeth his life <a name="fr_983"></a><a href="#fn_983"><font size="1">984</font></a>
  shall lose it; and whosoever loseth his life <a name="fr_984"></a><a href="#fn_984"><font size="1">985</font></a>
  for my sake shall find it.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.xiii-p40"></a>[27]  <a name="fr_985"></a><a href="#fn_985"><font size="1">986</font></a>
 And whosoever receiveth you receiveth me; and whosoever receiveth me receiveth [28] him that sent me.   <a name="fr_986"></a><a href="#fn_986"><font size="1">987</font></a>
 And whosoever receiveth a prophet in the name of a prophet shall take <a name="fr_987"></a><a href="#fn_987"><font size="1">988</font></a>
  a prophets reward; and whosoever shall receive a righteous man [29] in the name of a righteous man shall take <a name="fr_988"></a><a href="#fn_988"><font size="1">989</font></a>
  a righteous mans reward.   <a name="fr_989"></a><a href="#fn_989"><font size="1">990</font></a>
 And every one that shall give to drink to one of these least ones a drink of water only, in the name of a disciple, verily I say unto you, he shall not lose his reward.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.xiii-p46"></a>[30]  <a name="fr_990"></a><a href="#fn_990"><font size="1">991</font></a>
 And when Jesus finished charging his twelve disciples, he removed thence to [31] teach and preach in their cities.   <a name="fr_991"></a><a href="#fn_991"><font size="1">992</font></a>
 And while they were going in the way they entered into a certain village; and a woman named Martha entertained him in her house.  [32]  <a name="fr_992"></a><a href="#fn_992"><font size="1">993</font></a>
 And she had a sister named Mary, and she came and sat at the feet of our Lord, [33] and heard his sayings.   <a name="fr_993"></a><a href="#fn_993"><font size="1">994</font></a>
 But Martha was disquieted by much serving; and she came and said unto him, My Lord, givest thou no heed that my sister left me alone to [34] serve? speak to her that she help me.   <a name="fr_994"></a><a href="#fn_994"><font size="1">995</font></a>
 Jesus answered and said unto her, Martha, [35] Martha, thou art solicitous and impatient <a name="fr_995"></a><a href="#fn_995"><font size="1">996</font></a>
  on account of many things:   <a name="fr_996"></a><a href="#fn_996"><font size="1">997</font></a>
 but what is sought is one <i>thing</i>.  But Mary hath chosen for herself a good portion, and that which shall not be taken from her.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.xiii-p54"></a>[36]  <a name="fr_997"></a><a href="#fn_997"><font size="1">998</font></a>
 And the apostles went forth, and preached to the people that they might repent.  [37]  <a name="fr_998"></a><a href="#fn_998"><font size="1">999</font></a>
 And they cast out many devils, and anointed many sick with oil, and healed them.  [38, 39]  <a name="fr_999"></a><a href="#fn_999"><font size="1">1000</font></a>
 And the disciples of John told him <a name="fr_1000"></a><a href="#fn_1000"><font size="1">1001</font></a>
  of all these things.   <a name="fr_1001"></a><a href="#fn_1001"><font size="1">1002</font></a>
 And when John heard in [Arabic, p. 53] the prison of the doings of the Messiah, he called two of his disciples, and sent them to Jesus, and said, Art thou he that cometh, or look we for [40] another?   <a name="fr_1002"></a><a href="#fn_1002"><font size="1">1003</font></a>
 And they came to Jesus, and said unto him, John the Baptist hath sent [41] us unto thee, and said, Art thou he that cometh, or look we for another?   <a name="fr_1003"></a><a href="#fn_1003"><font size="1">1004</font></a>
 And in that hour he cured many of diseases, and of plagues of an evil spirit; and he gave sight [42] to many blind.   <a name="fr_1004"></a><a href="#fn_1004"><font size="1">1005</font></a>
 Jesus answered and said unto them, Go and tell John everything ye have seen and heard:  the blind see, and the lame walk, and the lepers are cleansed, and the blind <a name="fr_1005"></a><a href="#fn_1005"><font size="1">1006</font></a>
  hear, and the dead rise, and the poor have the gospel preached to [43] them.   <a name="fr_1006"></a><a href="#fn_1006"><font size="1">1007</font></a>
 And blessed is he who doubteth not in me.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.xiii-p65"></a><a name="page_65"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 65</font></a> <a name="iv.iii.xiii-Page_65"></a>[44]  <a name="fr_1007"></a><a href="#fn_1007"><font size="1">1008</font></a>
 And when Johns disciples departed, Jesus began to say to the multitudes concerning John, What went ye out into the wilderness to see? a reed shaken with the [45] winds?   <a name="fr_1008"></a><a href="#fn_1008"><font size="1">1009</font></a>
 And if not, then what went ye out to see? a man clothed in soft raiment?  Behold, they that are in magnificent garments and in voluptuousness are in the abode [46] of kings.   <a name="fr_1009"></a><a href="#fn_1009"><font size="1">1010</font></a>
 And if not, then what went ye out to see? a prophet?  Yea, I say unto [47] you, and more than a prophet.   <a name="fr_1010"></a><a href="#fn_1010"><font size="1">1011</font></a>
 This is he of whom it is written,</p> 
 <p class="c28"><a name="iv.iii.xiii-p70"></a>I am sending my messenger before thy face</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.xiii-p71"></a>To prepare the way before thee.</p>
