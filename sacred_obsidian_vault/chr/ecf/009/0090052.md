 <p> <a name="iv.iii.xliii"></a>
 </p><p class="c27"><a name="iv.iii.xliii-p1"></a> <span class="c6"><a name="iv.iii.xliii-p1.1"></a>Section XLIII.</span></p> 
 <p class="c12"><a name="iv.iii.xliii-p2"></a>[1]  <a name="fr_2946"></a><a href="#fn_2946"><font size="1">2947</font></a>
 Simon Cephas said unto him, Our Lord, <i>is it</i> to us <i>that</i> thou hast spoken this [2] parable, or also to every man?   <a name="fr_2947"></a><a href="#fn_2947"><font size="1">2948</font></a>
 Jesus said unto him, Who, thinkest thou, is the servant, the master of the house, <a name="fr_2948"></a><a href="#fn_2948"><font size="1">2949</font></a>
  trusted with control, <a name="fr_2949"></a><a href="#fn_2949"><font size="1">2950</font></a>
  whom his lord set over his [3] household, to give them their food in its season?   <a name="fr_2950"></a><a href="#fn_2950"><font size="1">2951</font></a>
 Blessed is that servant, whom his [4] lord shall come and find having done so.   <a name="fr_2951"></a><a href="#fn_2951"><font size="1">2952</font></a>
 Verily I say unto you, He will set him [5] over all that he hath.   <a name="fr_2952"></a><a href="#fn_2952"><font size="1">2953</font></a>
 But if that evil servant say in his heart, My lord delayeth his [6] coming;  <a name="fr_2953"></a><a href="#fn_2953"><font size="1">2954</font></a>
 and shall begin to beat his servants and the maidservants of his lord, and [7] shall begin to eat and to drink with the drunken;  <a name="fr_2954"></a><a href="#fn_2954"><font size="1">2955</font></a>
 the lord of that servant shall come [8] in the day that he thinketh not, and in the hour that he knoweth not,  <a name="fr_2955"></a><a href="#fn_2955"><font size="1">2956</font></a>
 and shall [Arabic, p. 163] judge him, and appoint his portion with the hypocrites,  <a name="fr_2956"></a><a href="#fn_2956"><font size="1">2957</font></a>
 and with those that are not faithful:   <a name="fr_2957"></a><a href="#fn_2957"><font size="1">2958</font></a>
 there shall be weeping and gnashing of teeth.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.xliii-p15"></a>[9]  <a name="fr_2958"></a><a href="#fn_2958"><font size="1">2959</font></a>
 Then shall the kingdom of heaven be like unto ten virgins, those that took their [10] lamps, and went forth to meet the bridegroom and the bride.   <a name="fr_2959"></a><a href="#fn_2959"><font size="1">2960</font></a>
 Five of them were [11] wise, and five foolish.   <a name="fr_2960"></a><a href="#fn_2960"><font size="1">2961</font></a>
 And those foolish <i>ones</i> took their lamps, and took not with [12, 13] them oil:   <a name="fr_2961"></a><a href="#fn_2961"><font size="1">2962</font></a>
 but those wise <i>ones</i> took oil in vessels along with their lamps.   <a name="fr_2962"></a><a href="#fn_2962"><font size="1">2963</font></a>
 When then [14] the bridegroom delayed, they all slumbered and slept.   <a name="fr_2963"></a><a href="#fn_2963"><font size="1">2964</font></a>
 But in the middle of the night there occurred a cry, Behold, the bridegroom cometh!  Go forth therefore to [15, 16] meet him.   <a name="fr_2964"></a><a href="#fn_2964"><font size="1">2965</font></a>
 Then all those virgins arose, and made ready their lamps.   <a name="fr_2965"></a><a href="#fn_2965"><font size="1">2966</font></a>
 The foolish [17] said unto the wise, Give us of your oil; for our lamps are gone out.   <a name="fr_2966"></a><a href="#fn_2966"><font size="1">2967</font></a>
 But those wise answered and said, Perhaps <a name="fr_2967"></a><a href="#fn_2967"><font size="1">2968</font></a>
  there will not be enough for us and you:  but go ye to <a name="page_110"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 110</font></a> <a name="iv.iii.xliii-Page_110"></a>[18] the sellers, and buy for yourselves.   <a name="fr_2968"></a><a href="#fn_2968"><font size="1">2969</font></a>
 And when they went away to buy, the bridegroom came; and those that were ready went in with him to the marriage feast:  and [19] the door was shut.   <a name="fr_2969"></a><a href="#fn_2969"><font size="1">2970</font></a>
 And at last those other virgins also came and said, Our Lord, [20] our Lord, open unto us.   <a name="fr_2970"></a><a href="#fn_2970"><font size="1">2971</font></a>
 He answered and said unto them, Verily I say unto you, [21] I know you not.   <a name="fr_2971"></a><a href="#fn_2971"><font size="1">2972</font></a>
 Watch then, for ye know not that day nor that hour.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.xliii-p30"></a>[22]  <a name="fr_2972"></a><a href="#fn_2972"><font size="1">2973</font></a>
 <i>It is</i> as a man, who went on a journey, and called his servants, and delivered unto [23] them his possessions.   <a name="fr_2973"></a><a href="#fn_2973"><font size="1">2974</font></a>
 And unto one he gave five talents, <a name="fr_2974"></a><a href="#fn_2974"><font size="1">2975</font></a>
  and another two, and another [24] one; every one according to his strength; and went on <i>his</i> journey forthwith.   <a name="fr_2975"></a><a href="#fn_2975"><font size="1">2976</font></a>
 He [Arabic, p. 164] then that received the five talents went and traded with them, and gained [26] other five.   <a name="fr_2976"></a><a href="#fn_2976"><font size="1">2977</font></a>
 And so also he of the two gained other two.   <a name="fr_2977"></a><a href="#fn_2977"><font size="1">2978</font></a>
 But he that received [27] the one went and digged in the earth, and hid the money of his lord.   <a name="fr_2978"></a><a href="#fn_2978"><font size="1">2979</font></a>
 And after a long time the lord of those servants came, and took from them the account.  [28]  <a name="fr_2979"></a><a href="#fn_2979"><font size="1">2980</font></a>
 And he that received five talents came near and brought other five, and said, My lord, thou gavest me five talents:  lo, I have gained other five in addition to them.  [29]  <a name="fr_2980"></a><a href="#fn_2980"><font size="1">2981</font></a>
 His lord said unto him, Well done, thou good and faithful servant:  over a little hast [30] thou been faithful, over much will I set thee:  enter into the joy of thy lord.   <a name="fr_2981"></a><a href="#fn_2981"><font size="1">2982</font></a>
 And he that had the two came near and said, My lord, thou gavest me two talents:  lo, [31] other two have I gained in addition to them.   <a name="fr_2982"></a><a href="#fn_2982"><font size="1">2983</font></a>
 His lord said unto him, Good, <a name="fr_2983"></a><a href="#fn_2983"><font size="1">2984</font></a>
  thou faithful servant:  over a little hast thou been faithful, over much will I set thee:  enter [32] into the joy of thy lord.   <a name="fr_2984"></a><a href="#fn_2984"><font size="1">2985</font></a>
 And he also that received the one talent came forward and said, My lord, I knew thee that thou art a severe man, who reapest where thou [33] sowest not, and gatherest where thou didst not scatter:   <a name="fr_2985"></a><a href="#fn_2985"><font size="1">2986</font></a>
 and so I was afraid, and [34] went away and hid thy talent in the earth:  lo, thou hast what is thine.   <a name="fr_2986"></a><a href="#fn_2986"><font size="1">2987</font></a>
 His lord answered and said unto him, Thou wicked and slothful servant, thou knewest me [35] that I reap where I sowed not, and gather where I did not scatter;  <a name="fr_2987"></a><a href="#fn_2987"><font size="1">2988</font></a>
 it was incumbent on thee to put my money to the bank, <a name="fr_2988"></a><a href="#fn_2988"><font size="1">2989</font></a>
  and <i>then</i> I should come and seek it with its [36] gains.   <a name="fr_2989"></a><a href="#fn_2989"><font size="1">2990</font></a>
 Take now from him the talent, and give it to him that hath ten talents.  [37]  <a name="fr_2990"></a><a href="#fn_2990"><font size="1">2991</font></a>
 Whosoever hath shall be given, and he shall have more:  but he that hath not, even [38] [Arabic, p. 165] what he hath shall be taken from him.   <a name="fr_2991"></a><a href="#fn_2991"><font size="1">2992</font></a>
 And the unprofitable servant, put him forth into the outer darkness:  there shall be the weeping and gnashing of teeth.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.xliii-p51"></a>[39, 40]  <a name="fr_2992"></a><a href="#fn_2992"><font size="1">2993</font></a>
 Your loins shall be girded, and your lamps lit;  <a name="fr_2993"></a><a href="#fn_2993"><font size="1">2994</font></a>
 and ye shall be like the people that are looking for their lord, when he shall return from the feast; so that, when [41] he cometh and knocketh, they may at once open unto him.   <a name="fr_2994"></a><a href="#fn_2994"><font size="1">2995</font></a>
 Blessed are those servants, whom their lord shall come and find attentive:  verily I say unto you, that he will gird his waist, and make them sit down, and pass through <a name="fr_2995"></a><a href="#fn_2995"><font size="1">2996</font></a>
  <i>them</i> and serve [42] them.   <a name="fr_2996"></a><a href="#fn_2996"><font size="1">2997</font></a>
 And if he come in the second watch, or the third, and find thus, blessed are those servants.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.xliii-p57"></a>[43]  <a name="fr_2997"></a><a href="#fn_2997"><font size="1">2998</font></a>
 But when the Son of man cometh in his glory, and all his pure angels with him, [44] then shall he sit on the throne of his glory:   <a name="fr_2998"></a><a href="#fn_2998"><font size="1">2999</font></a>
 and he will gather before him all the nations, and separate them the one from the other, like the shepherd who separateth [45] the sheep from the goats;  <a name="fr_2999"></a><a href="#fn_2999"><font size="1">3000</font></a>
 and will set <a name="fr_3000"></a><a href="#fn_3000"><font size="1">3001</font></a>
  the sheep on his right, and the goats on his [46] left.   <a name="fr_3001"></a><a href="#fn_3001"><font size="1">3002</font></a>
 Then shall the King say to those that are at his right, Come, ye blessed of my Father, inherit the kingdom prepared for you from the foundations <a name="fr_3002"></a><a href="#fn_3002"><font size="1">3003</font></a>
  of the world:  [47]  <a name="fr_3003"></a><a href="#fn_3003"><font size="1">3004</font></a>
 I hungered, and ye gave me to eat; and I thirsted, and ye gave me to drink; and I [48] was a stranger, and ye took me in;  <a name="fr_3004"></a><a href="#fn_3004"><font size="1">3005</font></a>
 and I was naked, and ye clothed me; and I [49] was sick, and ye visited me; and I was in prison, and ye cared for me.   <a name="fr_3005"></a><a href="#fn_3005"><font size="1">3006</font></a>
 Then shall those righteous say unto him, Our Lord, when saw we thee hungry, and fed thee? [50] or thirsty, and gave thee to drink?   <a name="fr_3006"></a><a href="#fn_3006"><font size="1">3007</font></a>
 And when saw we thee a stranger, and took [51] thee in? or naked, and clothed thee?   <a name="fr_3007"></a><a href="#fn_3007"><font size="1">3008</font></a>
 And when saw we thee sick, or imprisoned, and [52] cared for thee?   <a name="fr_3008"></a><a href="#fn_3008"><font size="1">3009</font></a>
 The King shall answer and say <a name="fr_3009"></a><a href="#fn_3009"><font size="1">3010</font></a>
  unto them, Verily I say unto you, What [53] [Arabic, p. 166] ye did to one of these my brethren, the little ones, ye did unto me.   <a name="fr_3010"></a><a href="#fn_3010"><font size="1">3011</font></a>
 Then shall he say unto those that are on his left also, Depart from me, ye cursed, [54] into the eternal fire prepared for the devil and his hosts:   <a name="fr_3011"></a><a href="#fn_3011"><font size="1">3012</font></a>
 I hungered, and ye fed me [55] not; and I thirsted, and ye did not give me to drink;  <a name="fr_3012"></a><a href="#fn_3012"><font size="1">3013</font></a>
 and I was a stranger, and ye <a name="page_111"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 111</font></a> <a name="iv.iii.xliii-Page_111"></a>took me not in; and I was naked, and ye clothed me not; and I was sick, and imprisoned, [56] and ye visited me not.   <a name="fr_3013"></a><a href="#fn_3013"><font size="1">3014</font></a>
 Then shall those also answer and say, Our Lord, when saw we thee an hungred, or athirst, or naked, or a stranger, or sick, or imprisoned, [57] and did not minister unto thee?   <a name="fr_3014"></a><a href="#fn_3014"><font size="1">3015</font></a>
 Then shall he answer and say unto them, Verily I say unto you, When ye did <i>it</i> not unto one of these little <i>ones</i>, ye did <i>it</i> not [58] unto me also.   <a name="fr_3015"></a><a href="#fn_3015"><font size="1">3016</font></a>
 And these shall go away into eternal punishment:  but the righteous into eternal life.</p>
