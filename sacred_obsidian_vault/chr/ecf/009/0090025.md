 <p> <a name="iv.iii.xvi"></a>
 </p><p class="c27"><a name="iv.iii.xvi-p1"></a> <span class="c6"><a name="iv.iii.xvi-p1.1"></a>Section XVI.</span></p> 
 <p class="c12"><a name="iv.iii.xvi-p2"></a>[1]  <a name="fr_1141"></a><a href="#fn_1141"><font size="1">1142</font></a>
 Then answered certain of the scribes and Pharisees, that they might tempt him, [2] and said, Teacher, we desire to see a sign from thee.   <a name="fr_1142"></a><a href="#fn_1142"><font size="1">1143</font></a>
 He answered and said, This evil and adulterous generation <a name="fr_1143"></a><a href="#fn_1143"><font size="1">1144</font></a>
  seeketh a sign; and it shall not be given a sign, [3] except the sign of Jonah the prophet.   <a name="fr_1144"></a><a href="#fn_1144"><font size="1">1145</font></a>
 And as Jonah was a sign to the inhabitants [4] of Nineveh, so shall the Son of man also be to this generation.   <a name="fr_1145"></a><a href="#fn_1145"><font size="1">1146</font></a>
 And as Jonah was in the belly of the great fish three days and three nights, so shall the Son of man [5] be in the heart of the earth three days and three nights.   <a name="fr_1146"></a><a href="#fn_1146"><font size="1">1147</font></a>
 The queen of the south shall rise in the judgement with the people of this generation, and condemn <a name="fr_1147"></a><a href="#fn_1147"><font size="1">1148</font></a>
  them:  for she came from the ends of the earth that she might hear the wisdom of Solomon; [6] [Arabic, p. 62] and behold, here is a better than Solomon.   <a name="fr_1148"></a><a href="#fn_1148"><font size="1">1149</font></a>
 The men of Nineveh shall stand in the judgement with this generation, and condemn it:  for they repented at [7] the preaching of Jonah; and behold, here is a greater than Jonah.   <a name="fr_1149"></a><a href="#fn_1149"><font size="1">1150</font></a>
 The unclean spirit, when he goeth out of the man, departeth, and goeth about through places wherein are no waters, that he may find rest for himself; and when he findeth <i>it</i> not, he [8] saith, I will return to my house whence I came out.   <a name="fr_1150"></a><a href="#fn_1150"><font size="1">1151</font></a>
 And if he come and find it [9] adorned <i>and</i> set in order,  <a name="fr_1151"></a><a href="#fn_1151"><font size="1">1152</font></a>
 then he goeth, and associateth with himself seven other spirits worse than himself; and they enter and dwell in it:  and the end of that man [10] shall be worse than his beginning.   <a name="fr_1152"></a><a href="#fn_1152"><font size="1">1153</font></a>
 Thus shall it be unto this evil generation.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.xvi-p15"></a>[11]  <a name="fr_1153"></a><a href="#fn_1153"><font size="1">1154</font></a>
 And while he was saying that, a woman from the multitude lifted up her voice, and said unto him, Blessed is the womb that bare thee, and the breasts that nursed [12] thee.   <a name="fr_1154"></a><a href="#fn_1154"><font size="1">1155</font></a>
 But he said unto her, Blessed is he that heareth the word of God, and keepeth it.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.xvi-p18"></a>[13]  <a name="fr_1155"></a><a href="#fn_1155"><font size="1">1156</font></a>
 And while he was speaking unto the multitude, there came unto him his mother [14] and his brethren, and sought to speak with him;  <a name="fr_1156"></a><a href="#fn_1156"><font size="1">1157</font></a>
 and they were not able, because of [15] the multitude; and they stood without and sent, calling him unto them.   <a name="fr_1157"></a><a href="#fn_1157"><font size="1">1158</font></a>
 A man said unto him, Behold, thy mother and thy brethren <i>are</i> standing without, and seek to [16] speak with thee.   <a name="fr_1158"></a><a href="#fn_1158"><font size="1">1159</font></a>
 But he answered unto him that spake unto him, Who is my [17] mother? and who are my brethren?   <a name="fr_1159"></a><a href="#fn_1159"><font size="1">1160</font></a>
 And he beckoned with his hand, stretching it out towards his disciples, and said, Behold, my mother! and behold, my brethren!  [18]  <a name="fr_1160"></a><a href="#fn_1160"><font size="1">1161</font></a>
 And every man that shall do the will of my Father which is in heaven is my brother, and my sister, and my mother.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.xvi-p25"></a>[19]  <a name="fr_1161"></a><a href="#fn_1161"><font size="1">1162</font></a>
 And after that, Jesus was going about in the cities and in the villages, and proclaiming [Arabic, p. 63] and preaching the kingdom of God, and his <a name="fr_1162"></a><a href="#fn_1162"><font size="1">1163</font></a>
  twelve with him, [20]  <a name="fr_1163"></a><a href="#fn_1163"><font size="1">1164</font></a>
 and the women which had been healed of diseases and of evil spirits, Mary [21] that <i>was</i> called Magdalene, from whom he had cast out seven devils,  <a name="fr_1164"></a><a href="#fn_1164"><font size="1">1165</font></a>
 and Joanna the wife of Chuza Herods steward, and Susanna, and many others, who were ministering to them of their substance.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.xvi-p30"></a>[22]  <a name="fr_1165"></a><a href="#fn_1165"><font size="1">1166</font></a>
 And after that, Jesus went out of the house, and sat on the sea shore.   <a name="fr_1166"></a><a href="#fn_1166"><font size="1">1167</font></a>
 And there [23] gathered unto him great multitudes.  And when the press of the people was great upon him, he went up and sat in the boat; and all the multitude was standing on the [24] shore of the sea.   <a name="fr_1167"></a><a href="#fn_1167"><font size="1">1168</font></a>
 And he spake to them much in parables, and said, The sower [25] went forth to sow:  and when he sowed,  <a name="fr_1168"></a><a href="#fn_1168"><font size="1">1169</font></a>
 some fell on the beaten highway; and it was [26] trodden upon, and the birds ate it.   <a name="fr_1169"></a><a href="#fn_1169"><font size="1">1170</font></a>
 And other fell on the rocks:  and some, where <a name="page_69"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 69</font></a> <a name="iv.iii.xvi-Page_69"></a>there was not much earth; and straightway it sprang up, because it had no depth in [27] the earth:   <a name="fr_1170"></a><a href="#fn_1170"><font size="1">1171</font></a>
 and when the sun rose, it withered; and because it had no root, it dried [28] up.   <a name="fr_1171"></a><a href="#fn_1171"><font size="1">1172</font></a>
 And some fell among thorns; and the thorns sprang up with it, and choked it; [29] and it yielded no fruit.   <a name="fr_1172"></a><a href="#fn_1172"><font size="1">1173</font></a>
 And other fell into excellent <i>and</i> good <a name="fr_1173"></a><a href="#fn_1173"><font size="1">1174</font></a>
  ground; and it came up, and grew, and brought forth fruit, some thirty, and some sixty, and some [30] a hundred.   <a name="fr_1174"></a><a href="#fn_1174"><font size="1">1175</font></a>
 And when he said that, he cried, He that hath ears that hear, let him [31] hear.   <a name="fr_1175"></a><a href="#fn_1175"><font size="1">1176</font></a>
 And when they were alone, his disciples came, and asked him, and said unto [32] him, What is this parable? and why spakest thou unto them in parables?   <a name="fr_1176"></a><a href="#fn_1176"><font size="1">1177</font></a>
 He [Arabic, p. 64] answered and said unto them, Unto you is given the knowledge of the secrets of the kingdom of God; but it is not given unto them that are [33] without.   <a name="fr_1177"></a><a href="#fn_1177"><font size="1">1178</font></a>
 He that hath shall be given unto, and there shall be added; and he that [34] hath not, that which he hath shall be taken from him also.   <a name="fr_1178"></a><a href="#fn_1178"><font size="1">1179</font></a>
 For this <i>cause</i> therefore I speak unto them in parables; because they see, and see not; and hear, and hear [35] not, nor understand.   <a name="fr_1179"></a><a href="#fn_1179"><font size="1">1180</font></a>
 And in them is being fulfilled the prophecy of Isaiah, who said,</p> 
 <p class="c28"><a name="iv.iii.xvi-p46"></a>Hearing they shall hear, and shall not understand;</p> 
 <p class="c29"><a name="iv.iii.xvi-p47"></a>And seeing they shall see, and shall not perceive:</p> 
 <p class="c29"><a name="iv.iii.xvi-p48"></a>[36]  <a name="fr_1180"></a><a href="#fn_1180"><font size="1">1181</font></a>
 The heart of this people is waxed gross,</p> 
 <p class="c29"><a name="iv.iii.xvi-p50"></a>And their hearing with their ears is become heavy,</p> 
 <p class="c29"><a name="iv.iii.xvi-p51"></a>And they have closed their eyes;</p> 
 <p class="c29"><a name="iv.iii.xvi-p52"></a>Lest they should see with their eyes,</p> 
 <p class="c29"><a name="iv.iii.xvi-p53"></a>And hear with their ears,</p> 
 <p class="c29"><a name="iv.iii.xvi-p54"></a>And understand with their hearts,</p> 
 <p class="c29"><a name="iv.iii.xvi-p55"></a>And should return,</p> 
 <p class="c30"><a name="iv.iii.xvi-p56"></a>And I should heal them.</p> 
 <p><a name="iv.iii.xvi-p57"></a>[37, 38]  <a name="fr_1181"></a><a href="#fn_1181"><font size="1">1182</font></a>
 But ye, blessed are your eyes, which see; and your ears, which hear.   <a name="fr_1182"></a><a href="#fn_1182"><font size="1">1183</font></a>
 Blessed [39] are the eyes which see what ye see.   <a name="fr_1183"></a><a href="#fn_1183"><font size="1">1184</font></a>
 Verily I say unto you, Many of the prophets and the righteous longed to see what ye see, and saw not; and to hear what ye [40] hear, and heard not.   <a name="fr_1184"></a><a href="#fn_1184"><font size="1">1185</font></a>
 When ye know not this parable, how shall ye know all parables?  [41, 42]  <a name="fr_1185"></a><a href="#fn_1185"><font size="1">1186</font></a>
 Hear ye the parable of the sower.   <a name="fr_1186"></a><a href="#fn_1186"><font size="1">1187</font></a>
 The sower which sowed, sowed the word [43] of God.   <a name="fr_1187"></a><a href="#fn_1187"><font size="1">1188</font></a>
 Every one who heareth the word of the kingdom, and understandeth it not, the evil one cometh and snatcheth away the word <i>that hath been</i> sown in his [44] heart:  and this is that <i>which was</i> sown on the middle of the highway.   <a name="fr_1188"></a><a href="#fn_1188"><font size="1">1189</font></a>
 But <i>that</i> which was sown on the rocks is he that heareth the word, and straightway receiveth [45, 46] [Arabic, p. 65] it with joy;  <a name="fr_1189"></a><a href="#fn_1189"><font size="1">1190</font></a>
 only, it hath no root in his soul, but his belief in it <i>is</i> for a time;  <a name="fr_1190"></a><a href="#fn_1190"><font size="1">1191</font></a>
 and whenever there is distress or persecution because of a <a name="fr_1191"></a><a href="#fn_1191"><font size="1">1192</font></a>
  word, he [47] stumbleth <a name="fr_1192"></a><a href="#fn_1192"><font size="1">1193</font></a>
  quickly.   <a name="fr_1193"></a><a href="#fn_1193"><font size="1">1194</font></a>
 And <i>that which was</i> sown among the thorns is he that heareth the word;  <a name="fr_1194"></a><a href="#fn_1194"><font size="1">1195</font></a>
 and the care of this world, and the error of riches, and the rest of the [48] other lusts enter, and choke the word, and it becometh without fruit.   <a name="fr_1195"></a><a href="#fn_1195"><font size="1">1196</font></a>
 And that which was sown in good ground is he that heareth my word in a pure <i>and</i> good heart, and understandeth it, and holdeth to it, and bringeth forth fruit with patience,  <a name="fr_1196"></a><a href="#fn_1196"><font size="1">1197</font></a>
 and produceth either a hundredfold or sixtyfold or thirty.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.xvi-p74"></a>[49]  <a name="fr_1197"></a><a href="#fn_1197"><font size="1">1198</font></a>
 And he said, So is the kingdom of God, like a man who casteth seed into the [50] earth, and sleepeth and riseth by night and day,  <a name="fr_1198"></a><a href="#fn_1198"><font size="1">1199</font></a>
 and the seed groweth and cometh [51] up, whence <a name="fr_1199"></a><a href="#fn_1199"><font size="1">1200</font></a>
  he knoweth not.   <a name="fr_1200"></a><a href="#fn_1200"><font size="1">1201</font></a>
 And the earth bringeth it to the fruit; and first it [52] will be blade, and after it ear, and at last perfect wheat in the ear:   <a name="fr_1201"></a><a href="#fn_1201"><font size="1">1202</font></a>
 and whenever the fruit ripeneth, <a name="fr_1202"></a><a href="#fn_1202"><font size="1">1203</font></a>
  he bringeth immediately the sickle, for the harvest hath come.</p>
