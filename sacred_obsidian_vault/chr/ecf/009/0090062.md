 <p> <a name="iv.iii.liii"></a>
 </p><p class="c27"><a name="iv.iii.liii-p1"></a> <span class="c6"><a name="iv.iii.liii-p1.1"></a>Section LIII. <a name="fr_3702"></a><a href="#fn_3702"><font size="1">3703</font></a>
 </span></p> 
 <p class="c12"><a name="iv.iii.liii-p3"></a>[1]  <a name="fr_3703"></a><a href="#fn_3703"><font size="1">3704</font></a>
 And while they marvelled at that, behold, two men standing above them, their [2] raiment shining:   <a name="fr_3704"></a><a href="#fn_3704"><font size="1">3705</font></a>
 and they were seized with fright, and bowed down their face to [3] the earth:  and they said unto them, Why seek ye the living <i>one</i> with the dead?   <a name="fr_3705"></a><a href="#fn_3705"><font size="1">3706</font></a>
 He is not here; he is risen:  remember what he was speaking unto you while he was in [4] Galilee, and saying,  <a name="fr_3706"></a><a href="#fn_3706"><font size="1">3707</font></a>
 The Son of man is to be delivered up into the hands of sinners, [5] and to be crucified, and on the third day to rise.   <a name="fr_3707"></a><a href="#fn_3707"><font size="1">3708</font></a>
 But go in haste, and say to his disciples and to Cephas, He is risen from among the dead; and lo, he goeth before [6] you into Galilee;  <a name="fr_3708"></a><a href="#fn_3708"><font size="1">3709</font></a>
 and there ye shall see him, where <a name="fr_3709"></a><a href="#fn_3709"><font size="1">3710</font></a>
  he said unto you:  lo, I have [7] told you.   <a name="fr_3710"></a><a href="#fn_3710"><font size="1">3711</font></a>
 And they remembered his sayings; and they departed in haste from the [8] tomb with joy and great fear, and hastened and went;  <a name="fr_3711"></a><a href="#fn_3711"><font size="1">3712</font></a>
 and perplexity and fear [9] encompassed them; and they told no man anything, for they were afraid.   <a name="fr_3712"></a><a href="#fn_3712"><font size="1">3713</font></a>
 And Mary hastened, and came to Simon Cephas, and to that other disciple whom Jesus loved, and said unto them, They have taken our Lord from the sepulchre, and I [10] know not where they have laid him.   <a name="fr_3713"></a><a href="#fn_3713"><font size="1">3714</font></a>
 And Simon went out, and that other disciple, [11] and came to the sepulchre.   <a name="fr_3714"></a><a href="#fn_3714"><font size="1">3715</font></a>
 And they hastened both together:  and that disciple [12] outran <a name="fr_3715"></a><a href="#fn_3715"><font size="1">3716</font></a>
  Simon, and came first to the sepulchre;  <a name="fr_3716"></a><a href="#fn_3716"><font size="1">3717</font></a>
 and he looked down, and saw the [13] linen laid; but he went not in.   <a name="fr_3717"></a><a href="#fn_3717"><font size="1">3718</font></a>
 And Simon came after him, and entered into the [14] [Arabic, p. 201] sepulchre, and saw the linen laid;  <a name="fr_3718"></a><a href="#fn_3718"><font size="1">3719</font></a>
 and the scarf with which his head was bound was not with the linen, but wrapped and laid aside in a certain place.  [15]  <a name="fr_3719"></a><a href="#fn_3719"><font size="1">3720</font></a>
 Then entered that disciple which came first to the sepulchre, and saw, and believed.  [16]  <a name="fr_3720"></a><a href="#fn_3720"><font size="1">3721</font></a>
 And they knew not yet from the scriptures that the Messiah was to rise from among [17] the dead.   <a name="fr_3721"></a><a href="#fn_3721"><font size="1">3722</font></a>
 And those two disciples went to their place.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.liii-p23"></a>[18]  <a name="fr_3722"></a><a href="#fn_3722"><font size="1">3723</font></a>
 But Mary remained <a name="fr_3723"></a><a href="#fn_3723"><font size="1">3724</font></a>
  at the tomb weeping:  and while she wept, she looked [19] down into the tomb;  <a name="fr_3724"></a><a href="#fn_3724"><font size="1">3725</font></a>
 and she saw two angels sitting in white raiment, one of them toward his pillow, and the other toward his feet, where the body of Jesus had been [20] laid.   <a name="fr_3725"></a><a href="#fn_3725"><font size="1">3726</font></a>
 And they said unto her, Woman, why weepest thou?  She said unto them, [21] They have taken my Lord, and I know not where they have left him.   <a name="fr_3726"></a><a href="#fn_3726"><font size="1">3727</font></a>
 She said that, and turned behind her, and saw Jesus standing, and knew not that it was [22] Jesus.   <a name="fr_3727"></a><a href="#fn_3727"><font size="1">3728</font></a>
 Jesus said unto her, Woman, why weepest thou? whom seekest thou?  And she supposed <a name="fr_3728"></a><a href="#fn_3728"><font size="1">3729</font></a>
  him <i>to be</i> the gardener, and said, My lord, if thou hast taken him, [23] tell me where thou hast laid him, that I may go and take him.   <a name="fr_3729"></a><a href="#fn_3729"><font size="1">3730</font></a>
 Jesus said unto her, Mary.  She turned, and said unto him in Hebrew, Rabboni; which is, being [24] interpreted, Teacher.   <a name="fr_3730"></a><a href="#fn_3730"><font size="1">3731</font></a>
 Jesus said unto her, Touch me not; <a name="fr_3731"></a><a href="#fn_3731"><font size="1">3732</font></a>
  for I have not ascended yet unto my Father:  go to my brethren, and say unto them, I ascend unto my Father and your Father, and my God and your God.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.liii-p34"></a>[25]  <a name="fr_3732"></a><a href="#fn_3732"><font size="1">3733</font></a>
 And on the First-day on which he rose, he appeared first unto Mary Magdalene, from whom he had cast out seven demons.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.liii-p36"></a><a name="page_126"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 126</font></a> <a name="iv.iii.liii-Page_126"></a>[26]  <a name="fr_3733"></a><a href="#fn_3733"><font size="1">3734</font></a>
 And some of those guards <a name="fr_3734"></a><a href="#fn_3734"><font size="1">3735</font></a>
  came to the city, and informed the chief priests of [27] [Arabic, p. 202] all that had happened.   <a name="fr_3735"></a><a href="#fn_3735"><font size="1">3736</font></a>
 And they assembled with the elders, and took [28] counsel;  <a name="fr_3736"></a><a href="#fn_3736"><font size="1">3737</font></a>
 and they gave money, not a little, to the guards, and said unto them, Say ye, His disciples came and stole him by night, while we were sleeping.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.liii-p41"></a>[29]  <a name="fr_3737"></a><a href="#fn_3737"><font size="1">3738</font></a>
 And if the judge hear that, we will make a plea with him, and free you of blame.  [30]  <a name="fr_3738"></a><a href="#fn_3738"><font size="1">3739</font></a>
 And they, when they took the money, did according to what they taught them.  And this word spread among the Jews unto this day.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.liii-p44"></a>[31]  <a name="fr_3739"></a><a href="#fn_3739"><font size="1">3740</font></a>
 And then came Mary Magdalene, and announced to the disciples that she had seen our Lord, and that he had said that unto her.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.liii-p46"></a>[32]  <a name="fr_3740"></a><a href="#fn_3740"><font size="1">3741</font></a>
 And while the first <a name="fr_3741"></a><a href="#fn_3741"><font size="1">3742</font></a>
  women <a name="fr_3742"></a><a href="#fn_3742"><font size="1">3743</font></a>
  were going in the way to inform <a name="fr_3743"></a><a href="#fn_3743"><font size="1">3744</font></a>
  his disciples, <a name="fr_3744"></a><a href="#fn_3744"><font size="1">3745</font></a>
  [33] Jesus met them, and said unto them, Peace unto you.   <a name="fr_3745"></a><a href="#fn_3745"><font size="1">3746</font></a>
 And they came and took [34] hold of his feet, and worshipped him.   <a name="fr_3746"></a><a href="#fn_3746"><font size="1">3747</font></a>
 Then said Jesus unto them, Fear not:  but go and say to my brethren that they depart into Galilee, and there they shall see [35] me.   <a name="fr_3747"></a><a href="#fn_3747"><font size="1">3748</font></a>
 And those women returned, and told all that to the eleven, and to the rest of the disciples;  <a name="fr_3748"></a><a href="#fn_3748"><font size="1">3749</font></a>
 and to those that had been with him, for they were saddened and [36] weeping.   <a name="fr_3749"></a><a href="#fn_3749"><font size="1">3750</font></a>
 And those were Mary Magdalene, and Joanna, and Mary the mother of James, and the rest who were with them:  and they were those that told the apostles.  [37]  <a name="fr_3750"></a><a href="#fn_3750"><font size="1">3751</font></a>
 And they, when they heard them say that he was alive and had appeared unto them, [38] did not believe them:   <a name="fr_3751"></a><a href="#fn_3751"><font size="1">3752</font></a>
 and these sayings were before their eyes as the sayings of madness.  [39] [Arabic, p. 203]  <a name="fr_3752"></a><a href="#fn_3752"><font size="1">3753</font></a>
 And after that, he appeared to two of them, on that day, and while they were going to the village which was named Emmaus, and whose distance [40] from Jerusalem was sixty furlongs. <a name="fr_3753"></a><a href="#fn_3753"><font size="1">3754</font></a>
    <a name="fr_3754"></a><a href="#fn_3754"><font size="1">3755</font></a>
 And they were talking the one of them with the [41] other of all the things which had happened.   <a name="fr_3755"></a><a href="#fn_3755"><font size="1">3756</font></a>
 And during the time of their talking and [42] inquiring with one another, Jesus came and reached them, and walked with them.   <a name="fr_3756"></a><a href="#fn_3756"><font size="1">3757</font></a>
 But [43] their eyes were veiled that they should not know him.   <a name="fr_3757"></a><a href="#fn_3757"><font size="1">3758</font></a>
 And he said unto them, What are these sayings which ye address the one of you to the other, as ye walk and are [44] sad?   <a name="fr_3758"></a><a href="#fn_3758"><font size="1">3759</font></a>
 One of them, whose name was Cleopas, answered and said unto him, Art thou perchance alone a stranger to Jerusalem, since thou knowest not what was in [45] it in these days?  He said unto them, What was?   <a name="fr_3759"></a><a href="#fn_3759"><font size="1">3760</font></a>
 They said unto him, Concerning Jesus, he who was from Nazareth, a man who was a prophet, and powerful in [46] speech and deeds before God and before all the people:   <a name="fr_3760"></a><a href="#fn_3760"><font size="1">3761</font></a>
 and the chief priests and [47] the elders delivered him up to the sentence of <a name="fr_3761"></a><a href="#fn_3761"><font size="1">3762</font></a>
  death, and crucified him.   <a name="fr_3762"></a><a href="#fn_3762"><font size="1">3763</font></a>
 But we supposed that he was the one who was to deliver Israel.  And since all <a name="fr_3763"></a><a href="#fn_3763"><font size="1">3764</font></a>
  these [48] things happened there have passed three days.   <a name="fr_3764"></a><a href="#fn_3764"><font size="1">3765</font></a>
 But <i>certain</i> women of us also [49] informed us that they had come to the sepulchre;  <a name="fr_3765"></a><a href="#fn_3765"><font size="1">3766</font></a>
 and when they found not his body, they came and told us that they had seen there the angels, and they <a name="fr_3766"></a><a href="#fn_3766"><font size="1">3767</font></a>
  said [50] concerning him that he was alive.   <a name="fr_3767"></a><a href="#fn_3767"><font size="1">3768</font></a>
 And some of us also went to the sepulchre, and [51] found the matter as the women had said:  only they saw him not.   <a name="fr_3768"></a><a href="#fn_3768"><font size="1">3769</font></a>
 Then said Jesus [52] unto them, Ye lacking in discernment, and heavy in heart to believe!   <a name="fr_3769"></a><a href="#fn_3769"><font size="1">3770</font></a>
 Was it not in all the sayings of the prophets that the Messiah was to suffer these things, and to [53] [Arabic, p. 204] enter into his Glory?   <a name="fr_3770"></a><a href="#fn_3770"><font size="1">3771</font></a>
 And he began from Moses and from all the prophets, [54] and interpreted to them concerning himself from all the scriptures.   <a name="fr_3771"></a><a href="#fn_3771"><font size="1">3772</font></a>
 And they drew near unto the village, whither they were going:  and he was leading them to [55] imagine that he was as if going to a distant region.   <a name="fr_3772"></a><a href="#fn_3772"><font size="1">3773</font></a>
 And they pressed <a name="fr_3773"></a><a href="#fn_3773"><font size="1">3774</font></a>
  him, and said unto him, Abide with us:  for the day hath declined now to the darkness.  And he went [56] in to abide with them.   <a name="fr_3774"></a><a href="#fn_3774"><font size="1">3775</font></a>
 And when he sat with them, he took bread, and blessed, [57] and brake, and gave to them.   <a name="fr_3775"></a><a href="#fn_3775"><font size="1">3776</font></a>
 And straightway their eyes were opened, and they [58] knew him; and he was taken away from them. <a name="fr_3776"></a><a href="#fn_3776"><font size="1">3777</font></a>
    <a name="fr_3777"></a><a href="#fn_3777"><font size="1">3778</font></a>
 And they said the one to the other, Was not our heart heavy within us, while he was speaking to us in the way, and interpreting to us the scriptures?</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.liii-p85"></a>[59]  <a name="fr_3778"></a><a href="#fn_3778"><font size="1">3779</font></a>
 And they rose in that hour, and returned to Jerusalem, and found the eleven [60] gathered, and those that were with them, saying,  <a name="fr_3779"></a><a href="#fn_3779"><font size="1">3780</font></a>
 Truly our Lord is risen, and hath [61] appeared to Simon.   <a name="fr_3780"></a><a href="#fn_3780"><font size="1">3781</font></a>
 And they related what happened in the way, and how they knew him when he brake the bread.   <a name="fr_3781"></a><a href="#fn_3781"><font size="1">3782</font></a>
 Neither believed they that also.</p>
