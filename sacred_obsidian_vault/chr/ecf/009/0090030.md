 <p> <a name="iv.iii.xxi"></a>
 </p><p class="c27"><a name="iv.iii.xxi-p1"></a> <a name="page_76"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 76</font></a> <a name="iv.iii.xxi-Page_76"></a><span class="c6"><a name="iv.iii.xxi-p1.1"></a>Section XXI.</span></p> 
 <p class="c12"><a name="iv.iii.xxi-p2"></a>[1]  <a name="fr_1478"></a><a href="#fn_1478"><font size="1">1479</font></a>
 And Jesus went out again from the border<i>s</i> of Tyre and Sidon, and came to the [2] sea of Galilee, towards the borders of Decapolis.   <a name="fr_1479"></a><a href="#fn_1479"><font size="1">1480</font></a>
 And they brought unto him one dumb and deaf, and entreated him that he would lay his hand upon him and heal [3] him.   <a name="fr_1480"></a><a href="#fn_1480"><font size="1">1481</font></a>
 And he drew him away from the multitude, and went away alone, and spat [4] upon his fingers, and thrust <i>them</i> into his ears, and touched his tongue;  <a name="fr_1481"></a><a href="#fn_1481"><font size="1">1482</font></a>
 and looked [5] to heaven, and sighed, and said unto him, Be opened.   <a name="fr_1482"></a><a href="#fn_1482"><font size="1">1483</font></a>
 And in that hour his ears [6] were opened, and the bond of his tongue was loosed, and he spake with ease.   <a name="fr_1483"></a><a href="#fn_1483"><font size="1">1484</font></a>
 And Jesus charged them much that they should not tell this to any man:  but the more [7] he charged them, <i>the more</i> they increased in publishing, and marvelled much,  <a name="fr_1484"></a><a href="#fn_1484"><font size="1">1485</font></a>
 and [Arabic, p. 82] said, This <i>man</i> doeth everything well:  he made the deaf to hear, and those that lacked speech to speak.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.xxi-p10"></a>[8, 9]  <a name="fr_1485"></a><a href="#fn_1485"><font size="1">1486</font></a>
 And while he was passing through the land of Samaria,  <a name="fr_1486"></a><a href="#fn_1486"><font size="1">1487</font></a>
 he came to one of the cities of the Samaritans, called Sychar, beside the field which Jacob gave to Joseph to [10] his son.   <a name="fr_1487"></a><a href="#fn_1487"><font size="1">1488</font></a>
 And there was there a spring of water of Jacobs.  And Jesus was fatigued from the exertion of the way, and sat at the spring.  And the time was about the [11] sixth hour. <a name="fr_1488"></a><a href="#fn_1488"><font size="1">1489</font></a>
    <a name="fr_1489"></a><a href="#fn_1489"><font size="1">1490</font></a>
 And a woman of Samaria came to draw water; and Jesus said unto [12] her, Give me water, that I may drink.   <a name="fr_1490"></a><a href="#fn_1490"><font size="1">1491</font></a>
 And his disciples had entered into the city [13] to buy for themselves food.   <a name="fr_1491"></a><a href="#fn_1491"><font size="1">1492</font></a>
 And that Samaritan woman said unto him, How dost thou, being a Jew, ask me to give thee to drink, while I am a Samaritan woman?  [14]  <a name="fr_1492"></a><a href="#fn_1492"><font size="1">1493</font></a>
 (And the Jews mingle not with the Samaritans. <a name="fr_1493"></a><a href="#fn_1493"><font size="1">1494</font></a>
 )  Jesus answered and said unto her, If thou knewest the gift of God, and who this is that said unto thee, Give me [15] to drink; thou wouldest ask him, and he would give thee the water of life.   <a name="fr_1494"></a><a href="#fn_1494"><font size="1">1495</font></a>
 That woman said unto him, My Lord, thou hast no bucket, and the well is deep:  from [16] whence hast thou the water of life?   <a name="fr_1495"></a><a href="#fn_1495"><font size="1">1496</font></a>
 Can it be that thou art greater than our father Jacob, who gave us this well, and drank from it, and his children, and his sheep?  [17]  <a name="fr_1496"></a><a href="#fn_1496"><font size="1">1497</font></a>
 Jesus answered and said unto her, Every one that drinketh of this water shall thirst [18] again:   <a name="fr_1497"></a><a href="#fn_1497"><font size="1">1498</font></a>
 but whosoever drinketh of the water which I shall give him shall not thirst for ever:  but the water which I shall give him shall be in him a spring of water springing [19] up unto eternal life.   <a name="fr_1498"></a><a href="#fn_1498"><font size="1">1499</font></a>
 That woman said unto him, My Lord, give me of this water, that [20] I may not thirst again, neither come and draw water from here.   <a name="fr_1499"></a><a href="#fn_1499"><font size="1">1500</font></a>
 Jesus said unto her, [21] [Arabic, p. 83] Go and call thy husband, and come hither.   <a name="fr_1500"></a><a href="#fn_1500"><font size="1">1501</font></a>
 She said unto him, I have no [22] husband.   <a name="fr_1501"></a><a href="#fn_1501"><font size="1">1502</font></a>
 Jesus said unto her, Thou saidst well, I have no husband:  five husbands hast thou had, and this <i>man</i> whom thou hast now is not thy husband; and [23] in this thou saidst truly.   <a name="fr_1502"></a><a href="#fn_1502"><font size="1">1503</font></a>
 That woman said unto him, My Lord, I perceive thee to [24] be a prophet.   <a name="fr_1503"></a><a href="#fn_1503"><font size="1">1504</font></a>
 Our fathers worshipped in this mountain; and ye say that in Jerusalem [25] is the place in which worship must be.   <a name="fr_1504"></a><a href="#fn_1504"><font size="1">1505</font></a>
 Jesus said unto her, Woman, believe me, an hour cometh, when neither in this mountain, nor yet in Jerusalem, shall ye worship [26] the Father.   <a name="fr_1505"></a><a href="#fn_1505"><font size="1">1506</font></a>
 Ye worship that which ye know not:  but we worship that which [27] we know:  for salvation is of the Jews.   <a name="fr_1506"></a><a href="#fn_1506"><font size="1">1507</font></a>
 But an hour cometh, and now is, when the true worshippers shall worship the Father in spirit and truth:  and the Father also [28] seeketh such as these worshippers.   <a name="fr_1507"></a><a href="#fn_1507"><font size="1">1508</font></a>
 For God is a Spirit:  and they that worship him [29] must worship him in spirit and in truth.   <a name="fr_1508"></a><a href="#fn_1508"><font size="1">1509</font></a>
 That woman said unto him, I know that [30] the Messiah cometh:   <a name="fr_1509"></a><a href="#fn_1509"><font size="1">1510</font></a>
 and when he is come, he will teach us everything.  Jesus said unto her, I that speak unto thee am he.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.xxi-p36"></a>[31]  <a name="fr_1510"></a><a href="#fn_1510"><font size="1">1511</font></a>
 And while he was speaking, his disciples came; and they wondered how he would speak <a name="fr_1511"></a><a href="#fn_1511"><font size="1">1512</font></a>
  with a woman; but not one of them said unto him, What seekest thou? or, [32] What <a name="fr_1512"></a><a href="#fn_1512"><font size="1">1513</font></a>
  speakest thou with her?   <a name="fr_1513"></a><a href="#fn_1513"><font size="1">1514</font></a>
 And the woman left her waterpot, and went to the [33] city, and said to the people,  <a name="fr_1514"></a><a href="#fn_1514"><font size="1">1515</font></a>
 Come, and see a man who told me all that <i>ever</i> I did:  [34] perhaps then he is the Messiah.   <a name="fr_1515"></a><a href="#fn_1515"><font size="1">1516</font></a>
 And people went out from the city, and came to [35] him.   <a name="fr_1516"></a><a href="#fn_1516"><font size="1">1517</font></a>
 And in the mean while his disciples besought him, and said unto him, Our [36, 37] master, eat.   <a name="fr_1517"></a><a href="#fn_1517"><font size="1">1518</font></a>
 And he said unto them, I have food to eat that ye know not.   <a name="fr_1518"></a><a href="#fn_1518"><font size="1">1519</font></a>
 And the disciples said amongst themselves, Can any one have brought him aught to eat? <a name="fr_1519"></a><a href="#fn_1519"><font size="1">1520</font></a>
   <a name="page_77"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 77</font></a> <a name="iv.iii.xxi-Page_77"></a>[38]  <a name="fr_1520"></a><a href="#fn_1520"><font size="1">1521</font></a>
 Jesus said unto them, My food is to do the will of him that sent me, and to accomplish [39] [Arabic, p. 84] his work.   <a name="fr_1521"></a><a href="#fn_1521"><font size="1">1522</font></a>
 Said ye not that after four months cometh the harvest? behold, I therefore say unto you, Lift up your eyes, and behold the lands, [40] that they have become white, and the harvest is already come. <a name="fr_1522"></a><a href="#fn_1522"><font size="1">1523</font></a>
    <a name="fr_1523"></a><a href="#fn_1523"><font size="1">1524</font></a>
 And he that reapeth receiveth his wages, and gathereth the fruit of eternal life; <a name="fr_1524"></a><a href="#fn_1524"><font size="1">1525</font></a>
  and the sower and [41] the reaper rejoice together.   <a name="fr_1525"></a><a href="#fn_1525"><font size="1">1526</font></a>
 For in this is found the word of truth, One soweth, and [42] another reapeth.   <a name="fr_1526"></a><a href="#fn_1526"><font size="1">1527</font></a>
 And I sent you to reap that in which ye have not laboured:  others laboured, and ye have entered on their labour.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.xxi-p54"></a>[43]  <a name="fr_1527"></a><a href="#fn_1527"><font size="1">1528</font></a>
 And from that city many of the Samaritans believed in him because of the words [44] of that woman, who testified and said, He told me all that <i>ever</i> I did.   <a name="fr_1528"></a><a href="#fn_1528"><font size="1">1529</font></a>
 And when those Samaritans came unto him, they besought him to abide with them; and he [45, 46] abode with them two days.   <a name="fr_1529"></a><a href="#fn_1529"><font size="1">1530</font></a>
 And many believed in him because of his word;  <a name="fr_1530"></a><a href="#fn_1530"><font size="1">1531</font></a>
 and they said to that woman, Now not because of thy saying have we believed in him:  we have heard and known that this truly is the Messiah, the Saviour of the world.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.xxi-p59"></a>[47, 48]  <a name="fr_1531"></a><a href="#fn_1531"><font size="1">1532</font></a>
 And after two days Jesus went out thence and departed to Galilee.   <a name="fr_1532"></a><a href="#fn_1532"><font size="1">1533</font></a>
 And Jesus [49] testified that a prophet is not honoured in his own city.   <a name="fr_1533"></a><a href="#fn_1533"><font size="1">1534</font></a>
 And when he came to Galilee, the Galilæans received him.</p>
