 </p><p class="c35"><a name="xii.iv.xv-p1"></a> <span class="c2"><a name="xii.iv.xv-p1.1"></a>Chapter XV.—We Must Adhere to Those Who Cultivate Peace, Not to Those Who Merely Pretend to Do So.</span></p> 
 <p class="c12"><a name="xii.iv.xv-p2"></a>Let us cleave, therefore, to those who cultivate peace with godliness, and not to those who hypocritically profess to desire it.  For [the Scripture] saith in a certain place, “This people honoureth me with their lips, but their heart is far from me.” <a name="fr_4065"></a><a href="#fn_4065"><font size="1">4066</font></a>
   And again:  “They bless with their mouth, but curse with their heart.” <a name="fr_4066"></a><a href="#fn_4066"><font size="1">4067</font></a>
   And again it saith, “They loved Him with their month, and lied <a name="fr_4067"></a><a href="#fn_4067"><font size="1">4068</font></a>
  to Him with their tongue; but their heart was not right with Him, neither were they faithful in His covenant.” <a name="fr_4068"></a><a href="#fn_4068"><font size="1">4069</font></a>
   “Let the deceitful lips become silent, <a name="fr_4069"></a><a href="#fn_4069"><font size="1">4070</font></a>
  [and “let the Lord destroy all the lying lips, <a name="fr_4070"></a><a href="#fn_4070"><font size="1">4071</font></a>
 ] and the boastful tongue of those who have said, Let us magnify our tongue:  our lips are our own; who is lord over us?  For the oppression of the poor, and for the sighing of the needy, will I now arise, saith the Lord:  I will place him in safety; I will deal confidently with him.” <a name="fr_4071"></a><a href="#fn_4071"><font size="1">4072</font></a>
 </p>
