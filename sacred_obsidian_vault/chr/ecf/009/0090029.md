 <p> <a name="iv.iii.xx"></a>
 </p><p class="c27"><a name="iv.iii.xx-p1"></a> <span class="c6"><a name="iv.iii.xx-p1.1"></a>Section XX.</span></p> 
 <p class="c12"><a name="iv.iii.xx-p2"></a>[1]  <a name="fr_1401"></a><a href="#fn_1401"><font size="1">1402</font></a>
 And Jesus knew within himself that his disciples were murmuring because of [2] that, and he said unto them, Doth this trouble you?   <a name="fr_1402"></a><a href="#fn_1402"><font size="1">1403</font></a>
 <i>What</i> if ye should see the Son [3] of man then ascend to the place where he was of old?   <a name="fr_1403"></a><a href="#fn_1403"><font size="1">1404</font></a>
 It is the spirit that quickeneth, and the body profiteth nothing:  the words <a name="fr_1404"></a><a href="#fn_1404"><font size="1">1405</font></a>
  that I speak unto you are spirit [4] and life.   <a name="fr_1405"></a><a href="#fn_1405"><font size="1">1406</font></a>
 But there are some of you that do not believe.  And Jesus knew beforehand who they were who should <a name="fr_1406"></a><a href="#fn_1406"><font size="1">1407</font></a>
  not believe, and who it was that should betray [5] him.   <a name="fr_1407"></a><a href="#fn_1407"><font size="1">1408</font></a>
 And he said unto them, Therefore I said unto you, No man can come unto me, if that hath not been given him by the Father.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.xx-p10"></a>[6] [Arabic, p. 78]  <a name="fr_1408"></a><a href="#fn_1408"><font size="1">1409</font></a>
 And because of this word many of his disciples turned back and walked [7] not with him.   <a name="fr_1409"></a><a href="#fn_1409"><font size="1">1410</font></a>
 And Jesus said unto the twelve, Do ye haply also wish to [8] go away?   <a name="fr_1410"></a><a href="#fn_1410"><font size="1">1411</font></a>
 Simon Cephas answered and said, My Lord, to whom shall we go? thou [9] hast the words of eternal life.   <a name="fr_1411"></a><a href="#fn_1411"><font size="1">1412</font></a>
 And we have believed and known that thou art the [10] Messiah, the Son of the living God.   <a name="fr_1412"></a><a href="#fn_1412"><font size="1">1413</font></a>
 Jesus said unto them, Did not I choose you, [11] ye company of the twelve, and of you one is a devil?   <a name="fr_1413"></a><a href="#fn_1413"><font size="1">1414</font></a>
 He said that because of Judas the son of Simon Iscariot; for he, being of the twelve, was purposed to <a name="fr_1414"></a><a href="#fn_1414"><font size="1">1415</font></a>
  betray him.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.xx-p18"></a>[12]  <a name="fr_1415"></a><a href="#fn_1415"><font size="1">1416</font></a>
 And while he was speaking, one of the Pharisees came asking of him that he [13] would eat with him:  and he went in, and reclined <i>to meat</i>.   <a name="fr_1416"></a><a href="#fn_1416"><font size="1">1417</font></a>
 And that Pharisee, when [14] he saw it, <a name="fr_1417"></a><a href="#fn_1417"><font size="1">1418</font></a>
  marvelled that he had not first cleansed himself before his eating.   <a name="fr_1418"></a><a href="#fn_1418"><font size="1">1419</font></a>
 Jesus said unto him, Now do ye Pharisees wash the outside of the cup and the dish, and ye think that ye are cleansed; but your inside is full of injustice and wickedness.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.xx-p23"></a>[15, 16]  <a name="fr_1419"></a><a href="#fn_1419"><font size="1">1420</font></a>
 Ye of little mind, did not he that made the outside make the inside?   <a name="fr_1420"></a><a href="#fn_1420"><font size="1">1421</font></a>
 Now give what ye have <a name="fr_1421"></a><a href="#fn_1421"><font size="1">1422</font></a>
  in alms, and everything <i>shall be</i> clean unto you.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.xx-p27"></a>[17, 18]  <a name="fr_1422"></a><a href="#fn_1422"><font size="1">1423</font></a>
 And there came to him Pharisees and scribes, come from Jerusalem.   <a name="fr_1423"></a><a href="#fn_1423"><font size="1">1424</font></a>
 And when they saw some of his disciples eating bread while they had not washed their hands, [19] they found fault.   <a name="fr_1424"></a><a href="#fn_1424"><font size="1">1425</font></a>
 For all of the Jews and the Pharisees, if they wash not their [20] hands thoroughly, eat not; for they held <a name="fr_1425"></a><a href="#fn_1425"><font size="1">1426</font></a>
  to the ordinance <a name="fr_1426"></a><a href="#fn_1426"><font size="1">1427</font></a>
  of the elders.   <a name="fr_1427"></a><a href="#fn_1427"><font size="1">1428</font></a>
 And they ate not what was bought from the market, except they washed it; and many other things did they keep of what they had received, such as the washing of cups, and [21] measures, and vessels of brass, and couches.   <a name="fr_1428"></a><a href="#fn_1428"><font size="1">1429</font></a>
 And scribes <a name="fr_1429"></a><a href="#fn_1429"><font size="1">1430</font></a>
  and Pharisees asked him, <a name="page_75"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 75</font></a> <a name="iv.iii.xx-Page_75"></a>[Arabic, p. 79] Why do thy disciples not walk according to the ordinances of the elders, but [22] eat bread without washing their hands?   <a name="fr_1430"></a><a href="#fn_1430"><font size="1">1431</font></a>
 Jesus answered and said unto them, Why do ye also overstep the command of God by reason of your ordinance?  [23]  <a name="fr_1431"></a><a href="#fn_1431"><font size="1">1432</font></a>
 God said, Honour thy father and thy mother; and, Whosoever revileth his father and [24] his mother shall surely die.   <a name="fr_1432"></a><a href="#fn_1432"><font size="1">1433</font></a>
 But ye say, If a man say to his father or to his mother, [25] What thou receivest <a name="fr_1433"></a><a href="#fn_1433"><font size="1">1434</font></a>
  from me is an offering,— <a name="fr_1434"></a><a href="#fn_1434"><font size="1">1435</font></a>
 and ye <a name="fr_1435"></a><a href="#fn_1435"><font size="1">1436</font></a>
  suffer him not to do anything [26] for his father or his mother;  <a name="fr_1436"></a><a href="#fn_1436"><font size="1">1437</font></a>
 and ye <a name="fr_1437"></a><a href="#fn_1437"><font size="1">1438</font></a>
  make void and reject the word of God by reason of the ordinance that ye have ordained and commanded, such as the washing [27] of cups and measures, and what resembles that ye do much.   <a name="fr_1438"></a><a href="#fn_1438"><font size="1">1439</font></a>
 And ye forsook [28] the command of God, and held to the ordinance of men.   <a name="fr_1439"></a><a href="#fn_1439"><font size="1">1440</font></a>
 Do <a name="fr_1440"></a><a href="#fn_1440"><font size="1">1441</font></a>
  ye well to wrong <a name="fr_1441"></a><a href="#fn_1441"><font size="1">1442</font></a>
  [29] the command of God in order that ye may establish your ordinance?   <a name="fr_1442"></a><a href="#fn_1442"><font size="1">1443</font></a>
 Ye hypocrites, well did Isaiah the prophet prophesy concerning you, and say,</p> 
 <p class="c28"><a name="iv.iii.xx-p49"></a>[30]  <a name="fr_1443"></a><a href="#fn_1443"><font size="1">1444</font></a>
 This people honoureth me with its <a name="fr_1444"></a><a href="#fn_1444"><font size="1">1445</font></a>
  lips;</p> 
 <p class="c29"><a name="iv.iii.xx-p52"></a>But their heart is very far from me.</p> 
 <p class="c29"><a name="iv.iii.xx-p53"></a>[31]  <a name="fr_1445"></a><a href="#fn_1445"><font size="1">1446</font></a>
 But in vain do they fear me,</p> 
 <p class="c21"><a name="iv.iii.xx-p55"></a>In that they teach the commands of men.</p> 
 <p><a name="iv.iii.xx-p56"></a>[32]  <a name="fr_1446"></a><a href="#fn_1446"><font size="1">1447</font></a>
 And Jesus called all the multitude, and said unto them, Hear me, all of you, and [33] understand:   <a name="fr_1447"></a><a href="#fn_1447"><font size="1">1448</font></a>
 nothing without the man, which then enters him, is able to defile him; [34] but what goeth out of him, that it is which defileth the man.   <a name="fr_1448"></a><a href="#fn_1448"><font size="1">1449</font></a>
 He that hath ears [35] that hear, let him hear.   <a name="fr_1449"></a><a href="#fn_1449"><font size="1">1450</font></a>
 Then his disciples drew near, and said unto him, Knowest [36] thou that the Pharisees which heard this word were angry?   <a name="fr_1450"></a><a href="#fn_1450"><font size="1">1451</font></a>
 He answered and said unto them, Every plant which my Father which is in heaven planted not shall be [37] [Arabic, p. 80] uprooted.   <a name="fr_1451"></a><a href="#fn_1451"><font size="1">1452</font></a>
 Let them alone; for they are blind leading blind.  And if the blind lead <a name="fr_1452"></a><a href="#fn_1452"><font size="1">1453</font></a>
  the blind, both of them shall fall into a hollow.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.xx-p64"></a>[38]  <a name="fr_1453"></a><a href="#fn_1453"><font size="1">1454</font></a>
 And when Jesus entered the house from the multitude, Simon Cephas asked him, [39] and said unto him, My Lord, explain to us that parable.   <a name="fr_1454"></a><a href="#fn_1454"><font size="1">1455</font></a>
 He said unto them, Do ye also thus not understand?  Know ye not that everything that entereth into the [40] man from without cannot defile him;  <a name="fr_1455"></a><a href="#fn_1455"><font size="1">1456</font></a>
 because it entereth not into his heart; it entereth into his stomach only, and thence is cast forth in the cleansing which maketh [41] clean all the food? <a name="fr_1456"></a><a href="#fn_1456"><font size="1">1457</font></a>
    <a name="fr_1457"></a><a href="#fn_1457"><font size="1">1458</font></a>
 The thing which goeth forth from the mouth of the man proceedeth [42] from his heart, and it is that which defileth the man.   <a name="fr_1458"></a><a href="#fn_1458"><font size="1">1459</font></a>
 From within <a name="fr_1459"></a><a href="#fn_1459"><font size="1">1460</font></a>
  the [43] heart of men proceed evil thoughts,  <a name="fr_1460"></a><a href="#fn_1460"><font size="1">1461</font></a>
 fornication, adultery, theft, false witness, murder, injustice, wickedness, deceit, stupidity, evil eye, calumny, pride, foolishness:  [44]  <a name="fr_1461"></a><a href="#fn_1461"><font size="1">1462</font></a>
 these evils all of them from within proceed from the heart, and they are <i>the things</i> [45] which defile the man:   <a name="fr_1462"></a><a href="#fn_1462"><font size="1">1463</font></a>
 but if a man eat while he washeth not his hands, he is not defiled.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.xx-p75"></a>[46]  <a name="fr_1463"></a><a href="#fn_1463"><font size="1">1464</font></a>
 And Jesus went out thence, and came to the borders of Tyre and Sidon.  And he entered into a certain house, and desired that no man should know it; <a name="fr_1464"></a><a href="#fn_1464"><font size="1">1465</font></a>
  and [47] he could not be hid.   <a name="fr_1465"></a><a href="#fn_1465"><font size="1">1466</font></a>
 But straightway a Canaanitish woman, whose daughter had an [48, 49] unclean spirit, heard of him.   <a name="fr_1466"></a><a href="#fn_1466"><font size="1">1467</font></a>
 And that woman was a Gentile of Emesa of Syria.   <a name="fr_1467"></a><a href="#fn_1467"><font size="1">1468</font></a>
 And she came out after him, crying out, and saying, Have mercy upon me, my Lord, <i>thou</i> [50] son of David; for my daughter is seized in an evil way by Satan. <a name="fr_1468"></a><a href="#fn_1468"><font size="1">1469</font></a>
    <a name="fr_1469"></a><a href="#fn_1469"><font size="1">1470</font></a>
 And he answered [Arabic, p. 81] her not a word.  And his disciples came and besought him, and said, Send [51] her away:  for she crieth after us.   <a name="fr_1470"></a><a href="#fn_1470"><font size="1">1471</font></a>
 He answered and said unto them, I was [52] not sent except to the sheep that are gone astray of the house of Israel.   <a name="fr_1471"></a><a href="#fn_1471"><font size="1">1472</font></a>
 But she came and worshipped him, and said, My Lord, help me, have mercy upon me.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.xx-p85"></a>[53]  <a name="fr_1472"></a><a href="#fn_1472"><font size="1">1473</font></a>
 Jesus said unto her, It is not seemly that the childrens bread should be taken and [54] thrown to the dogs.   <a name="fr_1473"></a><a href="#fn_1473"><font size="1">1474</font></a>
 But she said, Yea, my Lord:  the dogs also eat of the crumbs [55] that fall from their masters tables, and live.   <a name="fr_1474"></a><a href="#fn_1474"><font size="1">1475</font></a>
 Then said Jesus unto her, O woman, [56] great is thy faith:  it shall be unto thee as thou hast desired.   <a name="fr_1475"></a><a href="#fn_1475"><font size="1">1476</font></a>
 Go then <i>thy way;</i> and [57] because of this word, the devil is gone out of thy daughter.   <a name="fr_1476"></a><a href="#fn_1476"><font size="1">1477</font></a>
 And her daughter was [58] healed in that hour.   <a name="fr_1477"></a><a href="#fn_1477"><font size="1">1478</font></a>
 And that woman went away to her house, and found her daughter laid upon the bed, and the devil gone out of her.</p>
