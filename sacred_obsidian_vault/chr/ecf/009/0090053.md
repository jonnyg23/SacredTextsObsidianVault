 <p> <a name="iv.iii.xliv"></a>
 </p><p class="c27"><a name="iv.iii.xliv-p1"></a> <span class="c6"><a name="iv.iii.xliv-p1.1"></a>Section XLIV.</span></p> 
 <p class="c12"><a name="iv.iii.xliv-p2"></a>[1, 2]  <a name="fr_3016"></a><a href="#fn_3016"><font size="1">3017</font></a>
 And when Jesus <a name="fr_3017"></a><a href="#fn_3017"><font size="1">3018</font></a>
  finished all these sayings, he said unto his disciples,  <a name="fr_3018"></a><a href="#fn_3018"><font size="1">3019</font></a>
 Ye know that after two days will be the passover, and the Son of man is delivered up to be [3] crucified.   <a name="fr_3019"></a><a href="#fn_3019"><font size="1">3020</font></a>
 Then gathered together the chief priests, and the scribes, and the elders [4] of the people, unto the court of the chief priest, who was called Caiaphas;  <a name="fr_3020"></a><a href="#fn_3020"><font size="1">3021</font></a>
 and they took counsel together concerning Jesus, that they might seize him by subtilty, and [5] kill him.   <a name="fr_3021"></a><a href="#fn_3021"><font size="1">3022</font></a>
 But they said, Not during the feast, lest there take place a disturbance among the people;  <a name="fr_3022"></a><a href="#fn_3022"><font size="1">3023</font></a>
 for they feared the people.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.xliv-p10"></a>[6]  <a name="fr_3023"></a><a href="#fn_3023"><font size="1">3024</font></a>
 And Satan entered into Judas who was called Iscariot, who was of the number [7] of the twelve.   <a name="fr_3024"></a><a href="#fn_3024"><font size="1">3025</font></a>
 And he went away, and communed with the chief priests, and the scribes, and those that held command in the temple, and said unto them, What [8] [Arabic, p. 167] would ye pay me, and I will deliver him unto you?   <a name="fr_3025"></a><a href="#fn_3025"><font size="1">3026</font></a>
 And they, when they heard <i>it</i>, were pleased, and made ready <a name="fr_3026"></a><a href="#fn_3026"><font size="1">3027</font></a>
  for him thirty <i>pieces</i> of money. <a name="fr_3027"></a><a href="#fn_3027"><font size="1">3028</font></a>
   [9]  <a name="fr_3028"></a><a href="#fn_3028"><font size="1">3029</font></a>
 And he promised <a name="fr_3029"></a><a href="#fn_3029"><font size="1">3030</font></a>
  them, and from that time he sought an opportunity <a name="fr_3030"></a><a href="#fn_3030"><font size="1">3031</font></a>
  that he might deliver unto them Jesus without the multitude.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.xliv-p19"></a>[10]  <a name="fr_3031"></a><a href="#fn_3031"><font size="1">3032</font></a>
 And on the first day of unleavened bread the disciples came to Jesus, and said unto him, Where wilt thou that we go and make ready for thee that thou mayest eat the passover?</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.xliv-p21"></a>[11]  <a name="fr_3032"></a><a href="#fn_3032"><font size="1">3033</font></a>
 And before the feast of the passover, Jesus knew that the hour was arrived for his departure from this world unto his Father; and he loved his own in this world, [12] and to the last he loved them.   <a name="fr_3033"></a><a href="#fn_3033"><font size="1">3034</font></a>
 And at the time of the feast, Satan put into the [13] heart of Judas, the son of Simon Iscariot, to deliver him up.   <a name="fr_3034"></a><a href="#fn_3034"><font size="1">3035</font></a>
 And Jesus, because he knew that the Father had delivered into his hands everything, and that he came [14] forth from the Father, and goeth unto God,  <a name="fr_3035"></a><a href="#fn_3035"><font size="1">3036</font></a>
 rose from supper, and laid <i>aside</i> his [15] garments;  <a name="fr_3036"></a><a href="#fn_3036"><font size="1">3037</font></a>
 and took a towel, <i>and</i> girded his waist, and poured water into a bason, and began to wash the feet of his disciples, and to wipe them with the towel wherewith [16] his waist was girded.   <a name="fr_3037"></a><a href="#fn_3037"><font size="1">3038</font></a>
 And when he came to Simon Cephas, Simon said unto [17] him, Dost thou, my Lord, wash for me my feet?   <a name="fr_3038"></a><a href="#fn_3038"><font size="1">3039</font></a>
 Jesus answered and said unto [18] him, What I do, now thou knowest not; but afterwards thou shalt learn.   <a name="fr_3039"></a><a href="#fn_3039"><font size="1">3040</font></a>
 Simon said unto him, Thou shalt never wash for me my feet.  Jesus said unto him, If I [19] wash thee not, thou hast no part with me.   <a name="fr_3040"></a><a href="#fn_3040"><font size="1">3041</font></a>
 Simon Cephas said unto him, Then, my [20] Lord, wash not for me my feet alone, but my hands also and my head.   <a name="fr_3041"></a><a href="#fn_3041"><font size="1">3042</font></a>
 Jesus said unto him, He that batheth <a name="fr_3042"></a><a href="#fn_3042"><font size="1">3043</font></a>
  needeth not to wash save his feet, whereas his whole [21] <i>body</i> is clean:  and ye also are clean, but not all of you.   <a name="fr_3043"></a><a href="#fn_3043"><font size="1">3044</font></a>
 For Jesus knew him that should betray him; therefore said he, Ye are not all clean.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.xliv-p34"></a>[22] [Arabic, p. 168]  <a name="fr_3044"></a><a href="#fn_3044"><font size="1">3045</font></a>
 So when he had washed their feet, he took his garments, and sat down, and [23] said unto them, Know ye what I have done unto you?   <a name="fr_3045"></a><a href="#fn_3045"><font size="1">3046</font></a>
 Ye call me, Master, [24] and, Lord:  and ye say well; so I am.   <a name="fr_3046"></a><a href="#fn_3046"><font size="1">3047</font></a>
 If then I, now, who am your Lord and Master, have washed for you your feet, how needful is it that ye should wash one anothers feet!  [25]  <a name="fr_3047"></a><a href="#fn_3047"><font size="1">3048</font></a>
 This have I given you as an example, that as I have done to you so ye should do [26] also.   <a name="fr_3048"></a><a href="#fn_3048"><font size="1">3049</font></a>
 Verily, verily, I say unto you, No servant is greater than his lord; nor an [27] apostle greater than he that sent him.   <a name="fr_3049"></a><a href="#fn_3049"><font size="1">3050</font></a>
 If ye know that, ye are happy if ye do it.  [28]  <a name="fr_3050"></a><a href="#fn_3050"><font size="1">3051</font></a>
 My saying this <a name="fr_3051"></a><a href="#fn_3051"><font size="1">3052</font></a>
  is not for all of you:  for I know whom I have chosen:  but that the scripture might be fulfilled, He that eateth with me bread lifted against me his <a name="page_112"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 112</font></a> <a name="iv.iii.xliv-Page_112"></a>[29] heel.   <a name="fr_3052"></a><a href="#fn_3052"><font size="1">3053</font></a>
 Henceforth I say unto you before it come to pass, that, when it cometh to [30] pass, ye may believe that I am <i>he</i>.   <a name="fr_3053"></a><a href="#fn_3053"><font size="1">3054</font></a>
 Verily, verily, I say unto you, Whosoever receiveth whomsoever I send receiveth me; and whosoever receiveth me receiveth him that sent me.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.xliv-p45"></a>[31]  <a name="fr_3054"></a><a href="#fn_3054"><font size="1">3055</font></a>
 Who is the great <i>one</i>, he that sitteth, or he that serveth? is it not he that sitteth?  [32]  <a name="fr_3055"></a><a href="#fn_3055"><font size="1">3056</font></a>
 I am among you as he that serveth.   <a name="fr_3056"></a><a href="#fn_3056"><font size="1">3057</font></a>
 But ye are they that have continued with me [33] in my temptations;  <a name="fr_3057"></a><a href="#fn_3057"><font size="1">3058</font></a>
 I promise <a name="fr_3058"></a><a href="#fn_3058"><font size="1">3059</font></a>
  you, as my Father promised <a name="fr_3059"></a><a href="#fn_3059"><font size="1">3060</font></a>
  me, the kingdom, that ye may eat and drink at the table of my kingdom.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.xliv-p52"></a>[34]  <a name="fr_3060"></a><a href="#fn_3060"><font size="1">3061</font></a>
 And the first day <a name="fr_3061"></a><a href="#fn_3061"><font size="1">3062</font></a>
  came, the feast of unleavened bread, on which the Jews were [35] wont <a name="fr_3062"></a><a href="#fn_3062"><font size="1">3063</font></a>
  to sacrifice <a name="fr_3063"></a><a href="#fn_3063"><font size="1">3064</font></a>
  the passover.   <a name="fr_3064"></a><a href="#fn_3064"><font size="1">3065</font></a>
 And Jesus sent two of his disciples, Cephas and John, and said unto them, Go and make ready for us the passover, that we may eat.  [36, 37]  <a name="fr_3065"></a><a href="#fn_3065"><font size="1">3066</font></a>
 And they said unto him, Where wilt thou that we make ready for thee?   <a name="fr_3066"></a><a href="#fn_3066"><font size="1">3067</font></a>
 He said unto them, Go, enter the city;  <a name="fr_3067"></a><a href="#fn_3067"><font size="1">3068</font></a>
 and at the time of your entering, there shall meet you a man bearing a pitcher of water;  <a name="fr_3068"></a><a href="#fn_3068"><font size="1">3069</font></a>
 follow him, and the place where he entereth, say [38] to such an one, the master of the house,  <a name="fr_3069"></a><a href="#fn_3069"><font size="1">3070</font></a>
 Our Master saith, My time is come, and [Arabic, p. 169] at thy <i>house</i> I keep the passover.   <a name="fr_3070"></a><a href="#fn_3070"><font size="1">3071</font></a>
 Where then is the lodging-place where [39] I shall eat with my disciples?   <a name="fr_3071"></a><a href="#fn_3071"><font size="1">3072</font></a>
 And he will shew you a large upper room [40] spread and made ready:   <a name="fr_3072"></a><a href="#fn_3072"><font size="1">3073</font></a>
 there then make ready for us.   <a name="fr_3073"></a><a href="#fn_3073"><font size="1">3074</font></a>
 And his two disciples went out, and came to the city, and found as he had said unto them:  and they made ready the passover as he had said unto them.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.xliv-p67"></a>[41]  <a name="fr_3074"></a><a href="#fn_3074"><font size="1">3075</font></a>
 And when the evening was come, and the time arrived, Jesus came and reclined, [42] and the twelve apostles with him.   <a name="fr_3075"></a><a href="#fn_3075"><font size="1">3076</font></a>
 And he said unto them, With desire I have [43] desired to eat this passover with you before I suffer:   <a name="fr_3076"></a><a href="#fn_3076"><font size="1">3077</font></a>
 I say unto you, that henceforth I shall not eat it, until it is fulfilled in the kingdom of God.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.xliv-p71"></a>[44]  <a name="fr_3077"></a><a href="#fn_3077"><font size="1">3078</font></a>
 Jesus said that, and was agitated <a name="fr_3078"></a><a href="#fn_3078"><font size="1">3079</font></a>
  in his spirit, and testified, and said, Verily, [45] verily, I say unto you, One of you, <i>he</i> that eateth with me, shall betray me.   <a name="fr_3079"></a><a href="#fn_3079"><font size="1">3080</font></a>
 And they were very sorrowful; and they began to say unto him, one after another of [46] them, Can it be I, Lord?   <a name="fr_3080"></a><a href="#fn_3080"><font size="1">3081</font></a>
 He answered and said unto them, One of the twelve, [47] he that dippeth his hand with me in the dish, will betray me.   <a name="fr_3081"></a><a href="#fn_3081"><font size="1">3082</font></a>
 And lo, the hand of [48] him that betrayeth me is on the table.   <a name="fr_3082"></a><a href="#fn_3082"><font size="1">3083</font></a>
 And the Son of man goeth, as it is written of him:  woe then to that man by whose hand the Son of man is betrayed! for it [49] would have been better for that man had he not been born.   <a name="fr_3083"></a><a href="#fn_3083"><font size="1">3084</font></a>
 And the disciples [50] looked one on another, for they knew not to whom he referred;  <a name="fr_3084"></a><a href="#fn_3084"><font size="1">3085</font></a>
 and they began to search among themselves, who that might be who was to do <i>this</i>.</p>
