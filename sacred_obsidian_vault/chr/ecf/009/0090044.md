 <p> <a name="iv.iii.xxxv"></a>
 </p><p class="c27"><a name="iv.iii.xxxv-p1"></a> <span class="c6"><a name="iv.iii.xxxv-p1.1"></a>Section XXXV.</span></p> 
 <p class="c12"><a name="iv.iii.xxxv-p2"></a>[1]  <a name="fr_2388"></a><a href="#fn_2388"><font size="1">2389</font></a>
 And on the great day, which is the last of the feast, Jesus stood, crying out and [2] saying, If <i>any</i> man is thirsty, let him come unto me, and drink.   <a name="fr_2389"></a><a href="#fn_2389"><font size="1">2390</font></a>
 Every one that believeth in me, as the scriptures said, there shall flow from his belly rivers of pure [3] water.   <a name="fr_2390"></a><a href="#fn_2390"><font size="1">2391</font></a>
 He said that referring to the Spirit, which those who believed in him were to receive:  for the Spirit was not yet granted; and because Jesus had not yet been [4] [Arabic, p. 133] glorified.   <a name="fr_2391"></a><a href="#fn_2391"><font size="1">2392</font></a>
 And many of the multitude that heard his words said, This is [5] in truth the prophet.   <a name="fr_2392"></a><a href="#fn_2392"><font size="1">2393</font></a>
 And others said, This is the Messiah.  But others [6] said, Can it be that the Messiah will come from Galilee?   <a name="fr_2393"></a><a href="#fn_2393"><font size="1">2394</font></a>
 Hath not the scripture said that from the seed of David, and from Bethlehem, the village of David, the [7] Messiah cometh?   <a name="fr_2394"></a><a href="#fn_2394"><font size="1">2395</font></a>
 And there occurred a dissension in the multitude because of him.  [8]  <a name="fr_2395"></a><a href="#fn_2395"><font size="1">2396</font></a>
 And some of them were wishing to seize him; but no man laid a hand upon him.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.xxxv-p11"></a>[9]  <a name="fr_2396"></a><a href="#fn_2396"><font size="1">2397</font></a>
 And those officers came to the chief priests and Pharisees:  and the priests said [10] unto them, Why did ye not bring him?   <a name="fr_2397"></a><a href="#fn_2397"><font size="1">2398</font></a>
 The officers said, Never spake man thus [11] as speaketh this man.   <a name="fr_2398"></a><a href="#fn_2398"><font size="1">2399</font></a>
 The Pharisees said unto them, Perhaps ye also have gone [12, 13] astray?   <a name="fr_2399"></a><a href="#fn_2399"><font size="1">2400</font></a>
 Hath any of the rulers or the Pharisees haply believed in him?  <a name="fr_2400"></a><a href="#fn_2400"><font size="1">2401</font></a>
 except [14] this people which knows not the law; they are accursed.   <a name="fr_2401"></a><a href="#fn_2401"><font size="1">2402</font></a>
 Nicodemus, one of them, [15] he that had come to Jesus by night,  <a name="fr_2402"></a><a href="#fn_2402"><font size="1">2403</font></a>
 said unto them, Doth our law haply condemn [16] a man, except it hear him first and know what he hath done?   <a name="fr_2403"></a><a href="#fn_2403"><font size="1">2404</font></a>
 They answered and said unto him, Art thou also haply from Galilee?  Search, and see that a prophet riseth not from Galilee.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.xxxv-p20"></a>[17, 18]  <a name="fr_2404"></a><a href="#fn_2404"><font size="1">2405</font></a>
 And when the Pharisees assembled, Jesus asked them, and said,  <a name="fr_2405"></a><a href="#fn_2405"><font size="1">2406</font></a>
 What say ye of [19] the Messiah? whose son is he?  They said unto him, The son of David.   <a name="fr_2406"></a><a href="#fn_2406"><font size="1">2407</font></a>
 He said unto them, And how doth David in the Holy Spirit call him Lord? for he said,</p> 
 <p class="c28"><a name="iv.iii.xxxv-p24"></a>[20]  <a name="fr_2407"></a><a href="#fn_2407"><font size="1">2408</font></a>
 The Lord said unto my Lord,</p> 
 <p class="c29"><a name="iv.iii.xxxv-p26"></a>Sit on my right hand,</p> 
 <p class="c21"><a name="iv.iii.xxxv-p27"></a>That I may put thine enemies under thy feet.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.xxxv-p28"></a>[21, 22]  <a name="fr_2408"></a><a href="#fn_2408"><font size="1">2409</font></a>
 If then David calleth him Lord, how is he his son?   <a name="fr_2409"></a><a href="#fn_2409"><font size="1">2410</font></a>
 And no one was able to answer him; and no man dared from that day again to ask him of anything.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.xxxv-p31"></a>[23]  <a name="fr_2410"></a><a href="#fn_2410"><font size="1">2411</font></a>
 And Jesus addressed them again, and said, I am the light of the world; and he that [24] followeth me shall not walk in darkness, but shall find the light of life.   <a name="fr_2411"></a><a href="#fn_2411"><font size="1">2412</font></a>
 The Pharisees [Arabic, p. 134] said unto him, Thou bearest witness to thyself; thy witness is not true.   <a name="fr_2412"></a><a href="#fn_2412"><font size="1">2413</font></a>
 Jesus [25] answered and said unto them, If I bear witness to myself, my witness is true; for I know whence I came, and whither I go; but ye know not whence I came, or [26, 27] whither I go.   <a name="fr_2413"></a><a href="#fn_2413"><font size="1">2414</font></a>
 And ye judge after the flesh; and I judge no man.   <a name="fr_2414"></a><a href="#fn_2414"><font size="1">2415</font></a>
 And even if I judge, my judgement is true; because I am not alone, but I and my Father which [28, 29] sent me.   <a name="fr_2415"></a><a href="#fn_2415"><font size="1">2416</font></a>
 And in your law it is written, that the witness of two men is true.   <a name="fr_2416"></a><a href="#fn_2416"><font size="1">2417</font></a>
 I am he that beareth witness to myself, and my Father which sent me beareth witness to [30] me.   <a name="fr_2417"></a><a href="#fn_2417"><font size="1">2418</font></a>
 They said unto him, Where is thy Father?  Jesus answered and said unto them, Ye know not me, nor my Father:  for did ye know me, ye would know my [31] Father.   <a name="fr_2418"></a><a href="#fn_2418"><font size="1">2419</font></a>
 He said these sayings in the treasury, where he was teaching in the [32] temple:   <a name="fr_2419"></a><a href="#fn_2419"><font size="1">2420</font></a>
 and no man seized him; because his hour had not yet come.  Jesus said unto them again, I go truly, and ye shall seek me and not find me, and ye shall die [33] in your sins:  and where I go, ye cannot come.   <a name="fr_2420"></a><a href="#fn_2420"><font size="1">2421</font></a>
 The Jews said, Will he haply kill [34] himself, that he saith, Where I go, ye cannot come?   <a name="fr_2421"></a><a href="#fn_2421"><font size="1">2422</font></a>
 He said unto them, Ye are from below; and I am from above:  ye are of this world; and I am not of this [35] world.   <a name="fr_2422"></a><a href="#fn_2422"><font size="1">2423</font></a>
 I said unto you, that ye shall die in your sins:  if ye believe not that I am [36] he, ye shall die in your sins.   <a name="fr_2423"></a><a href="#fn_2423"><font size="1">2424</font></a>
 The Jews said, And thou, who art thou?  Jesus said <a name="page_98"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 98</font></a> <a name="iv.iii.xxxv-Page_98"></a>[37] unto them, If I should begin to speak unto you,  <a name="fr_2424"></a><a href="#fn_2424"><font size="1">2425</font></a>
 I have concerning you many words and judgement:  but he that sent me is true; and I, what I heard from him is what [38, 39] I say in the world.   <a name="fr_2425"></a><a href="#fn_2425"><font size="1">2426</font></a>
 And they knew not that he meant by that the Father.   <a name="fr_2426"></a><a href="#fn_2426"><font size="1">2427</font></a>
 Jesus [Arabic, p. 135] said unto them again, When ye have lifted up the Son of man, then ye shall know that I am he:  and I do nothing of myself, but as my Father [40] taught me, so I speak.   <a name="fr_2427"></a><a href="#fn_2427"><font size="1">2428</font></a>
 And he that sent me is with me; and my Father hath not [41] left me alone; because I do what is pleasing to him at all times.   <a name="fr_2428"></a><a href="#fn_2428"><font size="1">2429</font></a>
 And while he was saying that, many believed in him.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.xxxv-p51"></a>[42]  <a name="fr_2429"></a><a href="#fn_2429"><font size="1">2430</font></a>
 And Jesus said to those Jews that believed in him, If ye abide in my words, truly [43] ye are my disciples;  <a name="fr_2430"></a><a href="#fn_2430"><font size="1">2431</font></a>
 and ye shall know the truth, and the truth shall make you free.  [44]  <a name="fr_2431"></a><a href="#fn_2431"><font size="1">2432</font></a>
 They said unto him, We are the seed of Abraham, and have never served any man [45] in the way of slavery:  how then sayest thou, Ye shall be free children?   <a name="fr_2432"></a><a href="#fn_2432"><font size="1">2433</font></a>
 Jesus said unto them, Verily, verily, I say unto you, Every one that doeth a sin is a slave of [46] sin.   <a name="fr_2433"></a><a href="#fn_2433"><font size="1">2434</font></a>
 And the slave doth not remain for ever in the house; but the son remaineth [47, 48] for ever.   <a name="fr_2434"></a><a href="#fn_2434"><font size="1">2435</font></a>
 And if the Son set you free, truly ye shall be free children.  I know that ye are the seed of Abraham; but ye seek to slay me, because ye are unable for my [49] word.   <a name="fr_2435"></a><a href="#fn_2435"><font size="1">2436</font></a>
 And what I saw with my Father, I say:  and what ye saw with your father, [50] ye do.   <a name="fr_2436"></a><a href="#fn_2436"><font size="1">2437</font></a>
 They answered and said unto him, Our father is Abraham.  Jesus said unto them, If ye were the children of Abraham, ye would do the deeds of Abraham.  [51]  <a name="fr_2437"></a><a href="#fn_2437"><font size="1">2438</font></a>
 Now, behold, ye seek to kill me, a man that speak <a name="fr_2438"></a><a href="#fn_2438"><font size="1">2439</font></a>
  with you <a name="fr_2439"></a><a href="#fn_2439"><font size="1">2440</font></a>
  the truth, that I [52] heard from God:  this did Abraham not do.   <a name="fr_2440"></a><a href="#fn_2440"><font size="1">2441</font></a>
 And ye do the deeds of your father.  They said unto him, We were not <i>born</i> of fornication; <a name="fr_2441"></a><a href="#fn_2441"><font size="1">2442</font></a>
  we have one Father, who is [53] God.   <a name="fr_2442"></a><a href="#fn_2442"><font size="1">2443</font></a>
 Jesus said unto them, If God were your Father, ye would love me:  I proceeded and came <a name="fr_2443"></a><a href="#fn_2443"><font size="1">2444</font></a>
  from God; and it was not of my own self that I came, <a name="fr_2444"></a><a href="#fn_2444"><font size="1">2445</font></a>
  but he sent [54] [Arabic, p. 136] me.   <a name="fr_2445"></a><a href="#fn_2445"><font size="1">2446</font></a>
 Why then do ye not know my word?  Because ye cannot hear my word.  [55]  <a name="fr_2446"></a><a href="#fn_2446"><font size="1">2447</font></a>
 Ye are from the father, the devil, <a name="fr_2447"></a><a href="#fn_2447"><font size="1">2448</font></a>
  and the lust of your father do ye desire to do, who from the beginning is a slayer of men, and in the truth standeth not, because the truth is not in him.  And when he speaketh untruth, he speaketh from [56] himself:  for he is a liar, and the father of untruth.   <a name="fr_2448"></a><a href="#fn_2448"><font size="1">2449</font></a>
 And I who speak the truth, ye [57] believe me not.   <a name="fr_2449"></a><a href="#fn_2449"><font size="1">2450</font></a>
 Who of you rebuketh me for a sin?  And if I speak the truth, ye [58] do not believe me. <a name="fr_2450"></a><a href="#fn_2450"><font size="1">2451</font></a>
    <a name="fr_2451"></a><a href="#fn_2451"><font size="1">2452</font></a>
 Whosoever is of God heareth the words of God:  therefore do [59] ye not hear, because ye are not of God.   <a name="fr_2452"></a><a href="#fn_2452"><font size="1">2453</font></a>
 The Jews answered and said unto him, [60] Did we not say well that thou art a Samaritan, and hast demons?   <a name="fr_2453"></a><a href="#fn_2453"><font size="1">2454</font></a>
 Jesus said unto them, As for me, I have not a devil; but my Father do I honour, and ye dishonour [61] me.   <a name="fr_2454"></a><a href="#fn_2454"><font size="1">2455</font></a>
 I seek not my glory:  here is one who seeketh and judgeth.</p>
