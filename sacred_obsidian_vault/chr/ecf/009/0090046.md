 <p> <a name="iv.iii.xxxvii"></a>
 </p><p class="c27"><a name="iv.iii.xxxvii-p1"></a> <a name="page_100"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 100</font></a> <a name="iv.iii.xxxvii-Page_100"></a><span class="c6"><a name="iv.iii.xxxvii-p1.1"></a>Section XXXVII.</span></p> 
 <p class="c12"><a name="iv.iii.xxxvii-p2"></a>[1]  <a name="fr_2514"></a><a href="#fn_2514"><font size="1">2515</font></a>
 And Jesus said, To judge the world am I come, so that they that see not may [2] see, and they that see may become blind.   <a name="fr_2515"></a><a href="#fn_2515"><font size="1">2516</font></a>
 And some of the Pharisees which were [3] with him heard that, and they said unto him, Can it be that we are blind?   <a name="fr_2516"></a><a href="#fn_2516"><font size="1">2517</font></a>
 Jesus said unto them, If ye were blind, ye should not have sin:  but now ye say, We see:  and because of this your sin remaineth. <a name="fr_2517"></a><a href="#fn_2517"><font size="1">2518</font></a>
 </p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.xxxvii-p7"></a>[4] [Arabic, p. 140]  <a name="fr_2518"></a><a href="#fn_2518"><font size="1">2519</font></a>
 Verily, verily, I say unto you, Whosoever entereth not into the fold of the sheep by the door, but goeth up from another place, that <i>man</i> is a thief and a [5, 6] stealer.   <a name="fr_2519"></a><a href="#fn_2519"><font size="1">2520</font></a>
 But he that entereth by the door is the shepherd of the sheep.   <a name="fr_2520"></a><a href="#fn_2520"><font size="1">2521</font></a>
 And therefore <a name="fr_2521"></a><a href="#fn_2521"><font size="1">2522</font></a>
  the keeper of the door openeth for him the door; and the sheep hear his voice:  and [7] he calleth his sheep <a name="fr_2522"></a><a href="#fn_2522"><font size="1">2523</font></a>
  by their names, and they go forth unto him.   <a name="fr_2523"></a><a href="#fn_2523"><font size="1">2524</font></a>
 And when he putteth forth his sheep, he goeth before them, and his sheep <a name="fr_2524"></a><a href="#fn_2524"><font size="1">2525</font></a>
  follow him:  because [8] they know his voice.   <a name="fr_2525"></a><a href="#fn_2525"><font size="1">2526</font></a>
 And after a stranger will the sheep not go, but they flee from [9] him:  because they hear not the voice of a stranger.   <a name="fr_2526"></a><a href="#fn_2526"><font size="1">2527</font></a>
 This parable spake Jesus unto them:  but they knew not what he was saying unto them.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.xxxvii-p17"></a>[10]  <a name="fr_2527"></a><a href="#fn_2527"><font size="1">2528</font></a>
 Jesus said unto them again, Verily, verily, I say unto you, I am the door of the [11] sheep.   <a name="fr_2528"></a><a href="#fn_2528"><font size="1">2529</font></a>
 And all that came are thieves and stealers:  but the sheep heard them not.  [12]  <a name="fr_2529"></a><a href="#fn_2529"><font size="1">2530</font></a>
 I am the door:  and if a man enter by me, he shall live, and shall go in and go out, [13] and shall find pasture.   <a name="fr_2530"></a><a href="#fn_2530"><font size="1">2531</font></a>
 And the stealer cometh not, save that he may steal, and kill, and destroy:  but I came that they might have life, and that they might have [14] the thing <i>that is</i> better. <a name="fr_2531"></a><a href="#fn_2531"><font size="1">2532</font></a>
    <a name="fr_2532"></a><a href="#fn_2532"><font size="1">2533</font></a>
 I am the good shepherd; and the good shepherd giveth [15] himself <a name="fr_2533"></a><a href="#fn_2533"><font size="1">2534</font></a>
  for his sheep.   <a name="fr_2534"></a><a href="#fn_2534"><font size="1">2535</font></a>
 But the hireling, who is not a shepherd, and whose the sheep <a name="fr_2535"></a><a href="#fn_2535"><font size="1">2536</font></a>
  are not, when he seeth the wolf as it cometh, leaveth the sheep, and fleeth, [16] and the wolf cometh, and snatcheth away the sheep, and scattereth <a name="fr_2536"></a><a href="#fn_2536"><font size="1">2537</font></a>
  them:   <a name="fr_2537"></a><a href="#fn_2537"><font size="1">2538</font></a>
 and the [17] hireling fleeth because he is an hireling, and hath no care for the sheep.   <a name="fr_2538"></a><a href="#fn_2538"><font size="1">2539</font></a>
 I am the [18] good shepherd;  <a name="fr_2539"></a><a href="#fn_2539"><font size="1">2540</font></a>
 and I know what is mine, and what is mine knoweth me, as my Father knoweth me, and I know my Father; and I give myself <a name="fr_2540"></a><a href="#fn_2540"><font size="1">2541</font></a>
  for the sheep.  [19]  <a name="fr_2541"></a><a href="#fn_2541"><font size="1">2542</font></a>
 And I have other sheep also, that are not of this flock:  them also I must invite, and they shall hear my voice; and all the sheep shall be one, and the shepherd one.  [20] [Arabic, p. 141]  <a name="fr_2542"></a><a href="#fn_2542"><font size="1">2543</font></a>
 And therefore doth my Father love me, because I give my life, that I may [21] take it again.   <a name="fr_2543"></a><a href="#fn_2543"><font size="1">2544</font></a>
 No man taketh it from me, but I leave it of my own choice.  And I have the right to leave it, and have the right also to take it.  And this commandment did I receive of my Father.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.xxxvii-p35"></a>[22]  <a name="fr_2544"></a><a href="#fn_2544"><font size="1">2545</font></a>
 And there occurred a disagreement among the Jews because of these sayings.  [23]  <a name="fr_2545"></a><a href="#fn_2545"><font size="1">2546</font></a>
 And many of them said, He hath a devil, and is afflicted with madness; <a name="fr_2546"></a><a href="#fn_2546"><font size="1">2547</font></a>
  why listen [24] ye to him?   <a name="fr_2547"></a><a href="#fn_2547"><font size="1">2548</font></a>
 And others said, These sayings are not those of <i>men</i> possessed with demons.  Can a demon haply open the eyes of a blind <i>man?</i></p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.xxxvii-p40"></a>[25, 26]  <a name="fr_2548"></a><a href="#fn_2548"><font size="1">2549</font></a>
 And the feast of the dedication came on at Jerusalem:  and it was winter.   <a name="fr_2549"></a><a href="#fn_2549"><font size="1">2550</font></a>
 And [27] Jesus was walking in the temple in the porch of Solomon.   <a name="fr_2550"></a><a href="#fn_2550"><font size="1">2551</font></a>
 The Jews therefore surrounded him, and said unto him, Until when dost thou make our hearts anxious?  [28]  <a name="fr_2551"></a><a href="#fn_2551"><font size="1">2552</font></a>
 If thou art the Messiah, tell us plainly.  He answered and said unto them, I told you, and ye believe not:  and the deeds that I do in my Fathers name bear witness [29, 30] to me.   <a name="fr_2552"></a><a href="#fn_2552"><font size="1">2553</font></a>
 But ye believe not, because ye are not of my sheep, <a name="fr_2553"></a><a href="#fn_2553"><font size="1">2554</font></a>
  as I said unto you.  [31]  <a name="fr_2554"></a><a href="#fn_2554"><font size="1">2555</font></a>
 And my sheep <a name="fr_2555"></a><a href="#fn_2555"><font size="1">2556</font></a>
  hear my voice, and I know them, and they come after me:   <a name="fr_2556"></a><a href="#fn_2556"><font size="1">2557</font></a>
 and I give them eternal life; and they shall not perish for ever, nor shall any man snatch [32] them out of my hands. <a name="fr_2557"></a><a href="#fn_2557"><font size="1">2558</font></a>
    <a name="fr_2558"></a><a href="#fn_2558"><font size="1">2559</font></a>
 For the Father, who hath given <i>them</i> unto me, is greater [33] than all; and no man is able to take <i>them</i> from the hand of my <a name="fr_2559"></a><a href="#fn_2559"><font size="1">2560</font></a>
  Father.   <a name="fr_2560"></a><a href="#fn_2560"><font size="1">2561</font></a>
 I and [34, 35] my Father are one.   <a name="fr_2561"></a><a href="#fn_2561"><font size="1">2562</font></a>
 And the Jews took stones to stone him.   <a name="fr_2562"></a><a href="#fn_2562"><font size="1">2563</font></a>
 Jesus said unto them, Many good deeds from my Father have I shewed you; because of which <a name="fr_2563"></a><a href="#fn_2563"><font size="1">2564</font></a>
  of them, [36] then, do ye stone me?   <a name="fr_2564"></a><a href="#fn_2564"><font size="1">2565</font></a>
 The Jews said unto him, Not for the good deeds do we stone thee, but because thou blasphemest; and, whilst thou art a man, makest thyself <a name="page_101"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 101</font></a> <a name="iv.iii.xxxvii-Page_101"></a> [37] God.   <a name="fr_2565"></a><a href="#fn_2565"><font size="1">2566</font></a>
 Jesus said unto them, Is it not thus written in your law, I said, Ye are gods?  [38] [Arabic, p. 142]  <a name="fr_2566"></a><a href="#fn_2566"><font size="1">2567</font></a>
 And if he called those gods—for <a name="fr_2567"></a><a href="#fn_2567"><font size="1">2568</font></a>
  to them came the word of God (and it is [39] not possible in <a name="fr_2568"></a><a href="#fn_2568"><font size="1">2569</font></a>
  the scripture that <i>anything</i> should be undone)— <a name="fr_2569"></a><a href="#fn_2569"><font size="1">2570</font></a>
 he then, whom the Father hath sanctified and sent into the world, do ye say that he blasphemeth; [40] because I said unto you, I am the Son of God?   <a name="fr_2570"></a><a href="#fn_2570"><font size="1">2571</font></a>
 If then I do not the deeds of my [41] Father, ye believe me not. <a name="fr_2571"></a><a href="#fn_2571"><font size="1">2572</font></a>
    <a name="fr_2572"></a><a href="#fn_2572"><font size="1">2573</font></a>
 But if I do, <i>even</i> if ye believe not me, believe the deeds:  that ye may know and believe that my Father is in me, and I in my Father.  [42]  <a name="fr_2573"></a><a href="#fn_2573"><font size="1">2574</font></a>
 And they sought again to take him:  and he went forth out of their hands.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.xxxvii-p67"></a>[43]  <a name="fr_2574"></a><a href="#fn_2574"><font size="1">2575</font></a>
 And he went beyond Jordan to the place where John was baptizing formerly; [44] and abode there.   <a name="fr_2575"></a><a href="#fn_2575"><font size="1">2576</font></a>
 And many people came unto him; and they said, John did not [45] work even one sign:  but all that John said of this man is truth.   <a name="fr_2576"></a><a href="#fn_2576"><font size="1">2577</font></a>
 And many believed in him.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.xxxvii-p71"></a>[46]  <a name="fr_2577"></a><a href="#fn_2577"><font size="1">2578</font></a>
 And there was a sick <i>man</i>, named Lazarus, of the village of Bethany, the brother [47] of Mary and Martha.   <a name="fr_2578"></a><a href="#fn_2578"><font size="1">2579</font></a>
 And Mary was she that anointed with sweet ointment the feet of Jesus, and wiped <i>them</i> with her hair; and Lazarus, who was sick, was the [48] brother of this <i>woman</i>. <a name="fr_2579"></a><a href="#fn_2579"><font size="1">2580</font></a>
    <a name="fr_2580"></a><a href="#fn_2580"><font size="1">2581</font></a>
 And his sisters sent unto Jesus, and said unto him, Our [49] Lord, behold, he whom thou lovest is sick.   <a name="fr_2581"></a><a href="#fn_2581"><font size="1">2582</font></a>
 But Jesus said, This sickness is not unto death, but for the glorifying of God, that the Son of God may be glorified [50, 51] because of it.   <a name="fr_2582"></a><a href="#fn_2582"><font size="1">2583</font></a>
 And Jesus loved Martha, and Mary, and Lazarus.   <a name="fr_2583"></a><a href="#fn_2583"><font size="1">2584</font></a>
 And when he [52] heard that he was sick, he abode in the place where he was two days.   <a name="fr_2584"></a><a href="#fn_2584"><font size="1">2585</font></a>
 And after that, [53] he said unto his disciples, Come, let us go into Judæa.   <a name="fr_2585"></a><a href="#fn_2585"><font size="1">2586</font></a>
 His disciples said unto him, Our [Arabic, p. 143] Master, now the Jews desire to stone thee; and goest thou again thither?  [54, 55]  <a name="fr_2586"></a><a href="#fn_2586"><font size="1">2587</font></a>
 Jesus said unto them, Is not the day of twelve hours?  If then a man walk in the day, he stumbleth not, because he seeth the light of the world.   <a name="fr_2587"></a><a href="#fn_2587"><font size="1">2588</font></a>
 But if [56] a man walk in the night, he stumbleth, because there is no lamp in him.   <a name="fr_2588"></a><a href="#fn_2588"><font size="1">2589</font></a>
 This said Jesus:  and after that, he said unto them, Lazarus our friend hath fallen asleep; but [57] I am going to awaken him.   <a name="fr_2589"></a><a href="#fn_2589"><font size="1">2590</font></a>
 His disciples said unto him, Our Lord, if he hath [58] fallen asleep, he will recover.   <a name="fr_2590"></a><a href="#fn_2590"><font size="1">2591</font></a>
 But Jesus said that concerning his death:  while they [59] supposed that he spake of lying down to sleep.   <a name="fr_2591"></a><a href="#fn_2591"><font size="1">2592</font></a>
 Then Jesus said unto them plainly, [60] Lazarus is dead.   <a name="fr_2592"></a><a href="#fn_2592"><font size="1">2593</font></a>
 And I am glad that I was not there for your sakes, that ye may [61] believe; but let us go thither.   <a name="fr_2593"></a><a href="#fn_2593"><font size="1">2594</font></a>
 Thomas, who is called Thama, <a name="fr_2594"></a><a href="#fn_2594"><font size="1">2595</font></a>
  said to the disciples, his companions, Let us also go, and die with him.</p>
