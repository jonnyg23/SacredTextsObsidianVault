 </p><p class="c35"><a name="xv.iii.i.xviii-p1"></a> <span class="c2"><a name="xv.iii.i.xviii-p1.1"></a>18.  (3) of Substance.</span></p> 
 <p class="c12"><a name="xv.iii.i.xviii-p2"></a>In the third place a beginning may be that out of which a thing comes, the underlying matter from which things are formed.  This, however, is the view of those who hold matter itself to be uncreated, a view which we believers cannot share, since we believe God to have made the things that are out of the things which are not, as the <a name="page_307"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 307</font></a> <a name="xv.iii.i.xviii-Page_307"></a>mother of the seven martyrs in the Maccabees teaches, <a name="fr_4537"></a><a href="#fn_4537"><font size="1">4538</font></a>
  and as the angel of repentance in the Shepherd inculcated. <a name="fr_4538"></a><a href="#fn_4538"><font size="1">4539</font></a>
 </p>
