 <p> <a name="iv.iii.iv"></a>
 </p><p class="c27"><a name="iv.iii.iv-p1"></a> <a name="page_49"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 49</font></a> <a name="iv.iii.iv-Page_49"></a><span class="c6"><a name="iv.iii.iv-p1.1"></a>Section IV.</span></p> 
 <p class="c12"><a name="iv.iii.iv-p2"></a>[1]  <a name="fr_302"></a><a href="#fn_302"><font size="1">303</font></a>
 No man hath seen God at any time; the only <i>Son</i>, God, <a name="fr_303"></a><a href="#fn_303"><font size="1">304</font></a>
  which is in the bosom of his Father, he hath told of <i>him</i>.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.iv-p5"></a>[2]  <a name="fr_304"></a><a href="#fn_304"><font size="1">305</font></a>
 And this is the witness of John when the Jews sent to him from Jerusalem priests [3] and Levites to ask him, Who art thou?   <a name="fr_305"></a><a href="#fn_305"><font size="1">306</font></a>
 And he acknowledged, and denied not; [4] and he confessed that he was not the Messiah.   <a name="fr_306"></a><a href="#fn_306"><font size="1">307</font></a>
 And they asked him again, What then?  Art thou Elijah?  And he said, I am not he.  Art thou a prophet?  He [5] said, No.   <a name="fr_307"></a><a href="#fn_307"><font size="1">308</font></a>
 They said unto him, Then who art thou? that we may answer them that [6] sent us.  What sayest thou of thyself?   <a name="fr_308"></a><a href="#fn_308"><font size="1">309</font></a>
 And he said, I am the voice that crieth in [7] the desert, Repair ye the way of the Lord, as said Isaiah the prophet.   <a name="fr_309"></a><a href="#fn_309"><font size="1">310</font></a>
 And they [8] that were sent were from <a name="fr_310"></a><a href="#fn_310"><font size="1">311</font></a>
  the Pharisees.   <a name="fr_311"></a><a href="#fn_311"><font size="1">312</font></a>
 And they asked him and said unto him, Why baptizest thou now, when thou art not the Messiah, nor Elijah, nor a prophet?  [9]  <a name="fr_312"></a><a href="#fn_312"><font size="1">313</font></a>
 John answered and said unto them, I baptize with <a name="fr_313"></a><a href="#fn_313"><font size="1">314</font></a>
  water:  among you is standing [10] one whom ye know not:   <a name="fr_314"></a><a href="#fn_314"><font size="1">315</font></a>
 this is he who I said cometh after me and was before [11] me, the latchets of whose shoes I am not worthy to unloose.   <a name="fr_315"></a><a href="#fn_315"><font size="1">316</font></a>
 And that was in Bethany beyond Jordan, where John was baptizing.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.iv-p18"></a>[12]  <a name="fr_316"></a><a href="#fn_316"><font size="1">317</font></a>
 Now Johns raiment was camels hair, and <i>he was</i> girded with skins, and his food [13] [Arabic, p. 15] was of locusts and honey of the wilderness. <a name="fr_317"></a><a href="#fn_317"><font size="1">318</font></a>
     <a name="fr_318"></a><a href="#fn_318"><font size="1">319</font></a>
 Then went out unto him the people of Jerusalem, and all Judæa, and all the region which is about the [14, 15] Jordan;  <a name="fr_319"></a><a href="#fn_319"><font size="1">320</font></a>
 and they were baptized of him in the river Jordan, confessing their sins.   <a name="fr_320"></a><a href="#fn_320"><font size="1">321</font></a>
 But when he saw many of the Pharisees <a name="fr_321"></a><a href="#fn_321"><font size="1">322</font></a>
  and Sadducees <a name="fr_322"></a><a href="#fn_322"><font size="1">323</font></a>
  coming to be baptized, he said unto them, Ye children of vipers, who hath led you to flee from the wrath to come?  [16, 17]  <a name="fr_323"></a><a href="#fn_323"><font size="1">324</font></a>
 Do now the fruits which are worthy of repentance;  <a name="fr_324"></a><a href="#fn_324"><font size="1">325</font></a>
 and think and say not within yourselves, We have a father, <i>even</i> Abraham; for I say unto you, that God is able to [18] raise up of these stones children unto Abraham.   <a name="fr_325"></a><a href="#fn_325"><font size="1">326</font></a>
 Behold, the axe hath been laid at the roots of the trees, and so every tree that beareth not good fruit shall be taken and [19] cast into the fire.   <a name="fr_326"></a><a href="#fn_326"><font size="1">327</font></a>
 And the multitudes were asking him and saying, What shall we do?  [20]  <a name="fr_327"></a><a href="#fn_327"><font size="1">328</font></a>
 He answered and said unto them, He that hath two tunics shall <a name="fr_328"></a><a href="#fn_328"><font size="1">329</font></a>
  give to him that [21] hath not; and he that hath food shall <a name="fr_329"></a><a href="#fn_329"><font size="1">330</font></a>
  do likewise.   <a name="fr_330"></a><a href="#fn_330"><font size="1">331</font></a>
 And the publicans also came [22] to be baptized, and they said unto him, Teacher, what shall we do?   <a name="fr_331"></a><a href="#fn_331"><font size="1">332</font></a>
 He said unto [23] them, Seek not more than what ye are commanded to seek.   <a name="fr_332"></a><a href="#fn_332"><font size="1">333</font></a>
 And the servants <a name="fr_333"></a><a href="#fn_333"><font size="1">334</font></a>
  of the guard asked him and said, And we also, what shall we do?  He said unto them, Do not violence to any man, nor wrong him; and let your allowances satisfy you.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.iv-p37"></a>[24]  <a name="fr_334"></a><a href="#fn_334"><font size="1">335</font></a>
 And when the people were conjecturing about John, and all of them thinking [25] in their hearts whether he were haply <a name="fr_335"></a><a href="#fn_335"><font size="1">336</font></a>
  the Messiah,  <a name="fr_336"></a><a href="#fn_336"><font size="1">337</font></a>
 John answered and said unto them, I baptize you with water; there cometh one after me who is stronger than I, the latchets of whose shoes I am not worthy to loosen; he will baptize you with the [26] Holy Spirit and fire:   <a name="fr_337"></a><a href="#fn_337"><font size="1">338</font></a>
 who taketh the fan in his hand to cleanse his threshing-floors, [Arabic, p. 16] and the wheat he gathereth into his garners, while the straw he shall burn in fire which can <a name="fr_338"></a><a href="#fn_338"><font size="1">339</font></a>
  not be put out.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.iv-p43"></a>[27]  <a name="fr_339"></a><a href="#fn_339"><font size="1">340</font></a>
 And other things he taught and preached among the people.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.iv-p45"></a>[28]  <a name="fr_340"></a><a href="#fn_340"><font size="1">341</font></a>
 Then came Jesus from Galilee to the Jordan to John, to be baptized of him.  [29]  <a name="fr_341"></a><a href="#fn_341"><font size="1">342</font></a>
 And Jesus was about thirty years old, and it was supposed that he was the son of [30] Joseph. <a name="fr_342"></a><a href="#fn_342"><font size="1">343</font></a>
    <a name="fr_343"></a><a href="#fn_343"><font size="1">344</font></a>
 And John saw Jesus coming unto him, and said, This is the Lamb of [31] God, that taketh on itself the burden of the sins of the world!   <a name="fr_344"></a><a href="#fn_344"><font size="1">345</font></a>
 This is he concerning whom I said, There cometh after me a man who was before me, because he was <a name="page_50"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 50</font></a> <a name="iv.iii.iv-Page_50"></a>[32] before me. <a name="fr_345"></a><a href="#fn_345"><font size="1">346</font></a>
    <a name="fr_346"></a><a href="#fn_346"><font size="1">347</font></a>
 And I knew him not; but that he should be made manifest to Israel, [33] for this cause came I to baptize with water.   <a name="fr_347"></a><a href="#fn_347"><font size="1">348</font></a>
 And John was hindering him and [34] saying, I have need of being baptized by thee, and comest thou to me?   <a name="fr_348"></a><a href="#fn_348"><font size="1">349</font></a>
 Jesus answered him and said, Suffer this now:  thus it is our duty to fulfill all righteousness.  [35] Then he suffered him.   <a name="fr_349"></a><a href="#fn_349"><font size="1">350</font></a>
 And when all the people were baptized, Jesus also [36] was baptized.   <a name="fr_350"></a><a href="#fn_350"><font size="1">351</font></a>
 And immediately he went up out of the water, and heaven opened [37] [Arabic, p. 17] to him, <a name="fr_351"></a><a href="#fn_351"><font size="1">352</font></a>
   <a name="fr_352"></a><a href="#fn_352"><font size="1">353</font></a>
 and the Holy Spirit descended upon him in the similitude of the [38] body of a dove;  <a name="fr_353"></a><a href="#fn_353"><font size="1">354</font></a>
 and lo, a voice from heaven, saying, This is my beloved [39] Son, in whom I am well pleased.   <a name="fr_354"></a><a href="#fn_354"><font size="1">355</font></a>
 And John bare witness and said, I beheld the [40] Spirit descend from heaven like a dove; and it abode upon him.   <a name="fr_355"></a><a href="#fn_355"><font size="1">356</font></a>
 But I knew him not; but he that sent me to baptize with water, he said unto me, Upon whomsoever thou shalt behold the Spirit descending and lighting upon him, the same is he that [41] baptizeth with the Holy Spirit.   <a name="fr_356"></a><a href="#fn_356"><font size="1">357</font></a>
 And I have seen and borne witness that this is the Son of God.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.iv-p63"></a>[42, 43]  <a name="fr_357"></a><a href="#fn_357"><font size="1">358</font></a>
 And Jesus returned from the Jordan, filled with the Holy Spirit.   <a name="fr_358"></a><a href="#fn_358"><font size="1">359</font></a>
 And immediately the Spirit took him out into the wilderness, to be tried of the devil; <a name="fr_359"></a><a href="#fn_359"><font size="1">360</font></a>
   <a name="fr_360"></a><a href="#fn_360"><font size="1">361</font></a>
 and he [44] was with the beasts.   <a name="fr_361"></a><a href="#fn_361"><font size="1">362</font></a>
 And he fasted forty days and forty nights.   <a name="fr_362"></a><a href="#fn_362"><font size="1">363</font></a>
 And he ate nothing [45] in those days, and at the end of them he hungered.   <a name="fr_363"></a><a href="#fn_363"><font size="1">364</font></a>
 And the tempter came and said unto him, If thou art the Son of God, speak, and these stones shall become [46] bread.   <a name="fr_364"></a><a href="#fn_364"><font size="1">365</font></a>
 He answered and said, It is written, Not by bread alone shall man live, but [47] by every word that proceedeth out of the mouth of God.   <a name="fr_365"></a><a href="#fn_365"><font size="1">366</font></a>
 Then the devil <a name="fr_366"></a><a href="#fn_366"><font size="1">367</font></a>
  brought [48] him to the holy city, and set him on the pinnacle of the temple,  <a name="fr_367"></a><a href="#fn_367"><font size="1">368</font></a>
 and said unto him, If thou art the Son of God, cast thyself down:  for it is written,</p> 
 <p class="c28"><a name="iv.iii.iv-p75"></a>He shall give his angels charge concerning thee:</p> 
 <p class="c29"><a name="iv.iii.iv-p76"></a>And they shall take thee on their arms,</p> 
 <p class="c30"><a name="iv.iii.iv-p77"></a>So that thy foot shall not stumble against a stone.</p> 
 <p><a name="iv.iii.iv-p78"></a>[49]  <a name="fr_368"></a><a href="#fn_368"><font size="1">369</font></a>
 Jesus said unto him, And <a name="fr_369"></a><a href="#fn_369"><font size="1">370</font></a>
  it is written also, Thou shalt not tempt the Lord thy [50] God.   <a name="fr_370"></a><a href="#fn_370"><font size="1">371</font></a>
 And the devil <a name="fr_371"></a><a href="#fn_371"><font size="1">372</font></a>
  took him up to a high mountain, and shewed him all the kingdoms [51] [Arabic, p. 18] of the earth, and their glory, in the least time;  <a name="fr_372"></a><a href="#fn_372"><font size="1">373</font></a>
 and the devil <a name="fr_373"></a><a href="#fn_373"><font size="1">374</font></a>
  said unto him, To thee will I give all this dominion, and its glory, which is delivered to [52] me that I may give it to whomsoever I will.   <a name="fr_374"></a><a href="#fn_374"><font size="1">375</font></a>
 If then thou wilt worship before me, all of it shall be thine.</p>
