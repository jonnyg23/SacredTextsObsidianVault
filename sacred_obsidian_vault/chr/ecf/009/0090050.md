 <p> <a name="iv.iii.xli"></a>
 </p><p class="c27"><a name="iv.iii.xli-p1"></a> <span class="c6"><a name="iv.iii.xli-p1.1"></a>Section XLI.</span></p> 
 <p class="c12"><a name="iv.iii.xli-p2"></a>[1]  <a name="fr_2800"></a><a href="#fn_2800"><font size="1">2801</font></a>
 Therefore, behold, I, the wisdom of God, am sending unto you prophets, and apostles, and wise men, and scribes:  and some of them ye shall slay and crucify; and some of them ye shall beat in your synagogues, and persecute <a name="fr_2801"></a><a href="#fn_2801"><font size="1">2802</font></a>
  from city to [2] city:   <a name="fr_2802"></a><a href="#fn_2802"><font size="1">2803</font></a>
 that there may come on you all the blood of the righteous that hath been poured upon the ground <a name="fr_2803"></a><a href="#fn_2803"><font size="1">2804</font></a>
  from the blood of Abel the pure to the blood of Zachariah the son of Barachiah, whom ye slew between the temple <a name="fr_2804"></a><a href="#fn_2804"><font size="1">2805</font></a>
  and the altar.  [3]  <a name="fr_2805"></a><a href="#fn_2805"><font size="1">2806</font></a>
 Verily I say unto you, All these <i>things</i> shall come upon this generation. <a name="fr_2806"></a><a href="#fn_2806"><font size="1">2807</font></a>
 </p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.xli-p10"></a>[4] [Arabic, p. 155]  <a name="fr_2807"></a><a href="#fn_2807"><font size="1">2808</font></a>
 O Jerusalem, Jerusalem, slayer of the prophets, and stoner of them that are sent unto her! how many times did I wish to gather thy children, as [5] a hen gathereth her chickens under her wings, and ye would not!   <a name="fr_2808"></a><a href="#fn_2808"><font size="1">2809</font></a>
 Your house shall [6] be left over you desolate.   <a name="fr_2809"></a><a href="#fn_2809"><font size="1">2810</font></a>
 Verily I say unto you, Ye shall not see me henceforth, till ye shall say Blessed is he that cometh in the name of the Lord.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.xli-p14"></a>[7]  <a name="fr_2810"></a><a href="#fn_2810"><font size="1">2811</font></a>
 And many of the rulers also believed on him; but because of the Pharisees they [8] were not confessing <i>him</i>, lest they be put <a name="fr_2811"></a><a href="#fn_2811"><font size="1">2812</font></a>
  out of the synagogue:   <a name="fr_2812"></a><a href="#fn_2812"><font size="1">2813</font></a>
 and they loved [9] the praise of men more than the praising of God.   <a name="fr_2813"></a><a href="#fn_2813"><font size="1">2814</font></a>
 And Jesus cried and said, [10] Whosoever believeth in me, believeth not in me, but in him that sent me.   <a name="fr_2814"></a><a href="#fn_2814"><font size="1">2815</font></a>
 And [11] whosoever seeth me hath seen him that sent me.   <a name="fr_2815"></a><a href="#fn_2815"><font size="1">2816</font></a>
 I am come a light <a name="fr_2816"></a><a href="#fn_2816"><font size="1">2817</font></a>
  into the [12] world, and so every one that believeth in me abideth not in the darkness.   <a name="fr_2817"></a><a href="#fn_2817"><font size="1">2818</font></a>
 And whosoever heareth my sayings, and keepeth them not, I judge him not:  for I came [13] not to judge the world, but to give the world life. <a name="fr_2818"></a><a href="#fn_2818"><font size="1">2819</font></a>
   <a name="fr_2819"></a><a href="#fn_2819"><font size="1">2820</font></a>
 Whosoever wrongeth <a name="fr_2820"></a><a href="#fn_2820"><font size="1">2821</font></a>
  me, and receiveth not my sayings, there is one that judgeth him:  the word that I spake, it [14] shall judge him at the last day.   <a name="fr_2821"></a><a href="#fn_2821"><font size="1">2822</font></a>
 I from myself did not speak:  but the Father which sent me, he hath given me commandment, <a name="fr_2822"></a><a href="#fn_2822"><font size="1">2823</font></a>
  what I should say, and what I [15] should speak; and I know that his commandment <a name="fr_2823"></a><a href="#fn_2823"><font size="1">2824</font></a>
  is eternal life.   <a name="fr_2824"></a><a href="#fn_2824"><font size="1">2825</font></a>
 The things that I say now, as my Father hath said unto me, <i>even</i> so I say.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.xli-p30"></a>[16]  <a name="fr_2825"></a><a href="#fn_2825"><font size="1">2826</font></a>
 And when he said that unto them, the scribes and Pharisees began their evil-doing, being angry with <i>him</i>, and finding fault with his sayings, and harassing <a name="fr_2826"></a><a href="#fn_2826"><font size="1">2827</font></a>
  him [17] in many things;  <a name="fr_2827"></a><a href="#fn_2827"><font size="1">2828</font></a>
 seeking to catch something from his mouth, that they might be able to calumniate him.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.xli-p34"></a>[18]  <a name="fr_2828"></a><a href="#fn_2828"><font size="1">2829</font></a>
 And when there gathered together myriads of great multitudes, which almost trode <a name="page_107"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 107</font></a> <a name="iv.iii.xli-Page_107"></a>[Arabic, p. 156] one upon another, Jesus began to say unto his disciples, Preserve yourselves [19] from the leaven of the Pharisees, which is hypocrisy.   <a name="fr_2829"></a><a href="#fn_2829"><font size="1">2830</font></a>
 For there is nothing [20] concealed, that shall not be revealed:  nor hid, that shall not be known.   <a name="fr_2830"></a><a href="#fn_2830"><font size="1">2831</font></a>
 Everything that ye have said in the darkness shall be heard in the light; and what ye have spoken secretly in the ears in the inner chambers shall be proclaimed on the roofs.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.xli-p38"></a>[21, 22]  <a name="fr_2831"></a><a href="#fn_2831"><font size="1">2832</font></a>
 This said Jesus, and he went and hid himself from them.   <a name="fr_2832"></a><a href="#fn_2832"><font size="1">2833</font></a>
 But notwithstanding [23] his having done all these signs before them, they believed not in him:   <a name="fr_2833"></a><a href="#fn_2833"><font size="1">2834</font></a>
 that the word of Isaiah the prophet might be fulfilled, who said,</p> 
 <p class="c28"><a name="iv.iii.xli-p42"></a>My Lord, who is he that hath believed to hear us?</p> 
 <p class="c30"><a name="iv.iii.xli-p43"></a>And the arm of the Lord, to whom hath it appeared?</p> 
 <p><a name="iv.iii.xli-p44"></a>[24]  <a name="fr_2834"></a><a href="#fn_2834"><font size="1">2835</font></a>
 And for this reason it is not possible for them to believe, because Isaiah also said,</p> 
 <p class="c28"><a name="iv.iii.xli-p46"></a>[25]  <a name="fr_2835"></a><a href="#fn_2835"><font size="1">2836</font></a>
 They have blinded their eyes, and made dark their heart;</p> 
 <p class="c29"><a name="iv.iii.xli-p48"></a>That they may not see with their eyes, and understand with their heart,</p> 
 <p class="c29"><a name="iv.iii.xli-p49"></a>And turn,</p> 
 <p class="c30"><a name="iv.iii.xli-p50"></a>So that I should heal them.</p> 
 <p><a name="iv.iii.xli-p51"></a>[26]  <a name="fr_2836"></a><a href="#fn_2836"><font size="1">2837</font></a>
 This said Isaiah when he saw his glory, and spake of him.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.xli-p53"></a>[27]  <a name="fr_2837"></a><a href="#fn_2837"><font size="1">2838</font></a>
 And when Jesus went out of the temple, certain of his disciples came forward [28] to shew <a name="fr_2838"></a><a href="#fn_2838"><font size="1">2839</font></a>
  him the buildings of the temple,  <a name="fr_2839"></a><a href="#fn_2839"><font size="1">2840</font></a>
 and its beauty and greatness, and the strength of the stones that were laid in it, and the elegance of its building, and that [29] it was adorned with noble stones and beautiful colours.   <a name="fr_2840"></a><a href="#fn_2840"><font size="1">2841</font></a>
 Jesus answered and said [30] unto them, See ye these great buildings?  <a name="fr_2841"></a><a href="#fn_2841"><font size="1">2842</font></a>
 verily I say unto you, Days will come, when there shall not be left here a stone upon another, that shall not be cast down.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.xli-p59"></a>[31]  <a name="fr_2842"></a><a href="#fn_2842"><font size="1">2843</font></a>
 And two days before <a name="fr_2843"></a><a href="#fn_2843"><font size="1">2844</font></a>
  the passover of unleavened bread, the chief priests and [32] the scribes sought how they might take him by deceit, <a name="fr_2844"></a><a href="#fn_2844"><font size="1">2845</font></a>
  and kill him:   <a name="fr_2845"></a><a href="#fn_2845"><font size="1">2846</font></a>
 and they said, It shall not be at the feast, lest the people be agitated.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.xli-p64"></a>[33]  <a name="fr_2846"></a><a href="#fn_2846"><font size="1">2847</font></a>
 And when Jesus sat on the mount of Olives opposite the temple, his disciples, Simon Cephas and James and John and Andrew, came forward unto him, and said unto him [34] between themselves and him,  <a name="fr_2847"></a><a href="#fn_2847"><font size="1">2848</font></a>
 Teacher, tell us when that shall be, and what is the sign [35] [Arabic, p. 157] of thy coming and the end of the world.   <a name="fr_2848"></a><a href="#fn_2848"><font size="1">2849</font></a>
 Jesus answered and said unto them, Days will come, when ye shall long to see one of the days of the Son of [36, 37]  <a name="fr_2849"></a><a href="#fn_2849"><font size="1">2850</font></a>
 man, and shall not behold.   <a name="fr_2850"></a><a href="#fn_2850"><font size="1">2851</font></a>
 Take heed lest any man lead you astray.  Many shall [38] come in my name, and say, I am the Messiah;  <a name="fr_2851"></a><a href="#fn_2851"><font size="1">2852</font></a>
 and they shall say, The time is come [39] near, and shall lead many astray:  go not therefore after them.   <a name="fr_2852"></a><a href="#fn_2852"><font size="1">2853</font></a>
 And when ye hear of wars and tidings of insurrections, see <i>to it</i>, be <a name="fr_2853"></a><a href="#fn_2853"><font size="1">2854</font></a>
  not agitated:  for these <i>things</i> must [40] first be; only the end is not yet come.   <a name="fr_2854"></a><a href="#fn_2854"><font size="1">2855</font></a>
 Nation shall rise against nation, and kingdom [41] against kingdom:   <a name="fr_2855"></a><a href="#fn_2855"><font size="1">2856</font></a>
 and great earthquakes shall be in one place and another, and there shall be famines and deaths and agitations:  and there shall be fear and terror and great signs that <a name="fr_2856"></a><a href="#fn_2856"><font size="1">2857</font></a>
  shall appear from heaven, and there shall be great [42, 43] storms    <a name="fr_2857"></a><a href="#fn_2857"><font size="1">2858</font></a>
 All these <i>things</i> are the beginning of travail.   <a name="fr_2858"></a><a href="#fn_2858"><font size="1">2859</font></a>
 But before all of that, they shall lay hands upon you, and persecute you, and deliver you unto the synagogues [44] and into prisons, and bring you before kings and judges for my names sake.   <a name="fr_2859"></a><a href="#fn_2859"><font size="1">2860</font></a>
 And [45] that shall be unto you for a witness.   <a name="fr_2860"></a><a href="#fn_2860"><font size="1">2861</font></a>
 But first must my gospel be preached unto all [46] nations.   <a name="fr_2861"></a><a href="#fn_2861"><font size="1">2862</font></a>
 And when they bring you into the synagogues before the rulers and the authorities, be not anxious beforehand how ye shall answer for yourselves, or what ye [47, 48] shall say:   <a name="fr_2862"></a><a href="#fn_2862"><font size="1">2863</font></a>
 because it is not ye that speak, but the Holy Spirit.   <a name="fr_2863"></a><a href="#fn_2863"><font size="1">2864</font></a>
 Lay it to your heart, not [49] [Arabic, p. 158] to be anxious before the time what ye shall say:   <a name="fr_2864"></a><a href="#fn_2864"><font size="1">2865</font></a>
 and I shall <a name="fr_2865"></a><a href="#fn_2865"><font size="1">2866</font></a>
  give you understanding and wisdom, <a name="fr_2866"></a><a href="#fn_2866"><font size="1">2867</font></a>
  which all your adversaries shall not be able to gainsay.  [50]  <a name="fr_2867"></a><a href="#fn_2867"><font size="1">2868</font></a>
 And then shall they deliver you unto constraint, and shall kill you:  and ye shall be [51] hated of all nations because of my name.   <a name="fr_2868"></a><a href="#fn_2868"><font size="1">2869</font></a>
 And then shall many go astray, <a name="fr_2869"></a><a href="#fn_2869"><font size="1">2870</font></a>
  and they [52] shall hate one another, and deliver one another unto death.   <a name="fr_2870"></a><a href="#fn_2870"><font size="1">2871</font></a>
 And your parents, and your brethren, and your kinsfolk, and your friends shall deliver you up, and shall [53, 54] slay some of you.   <a name="fr_2871"></a><a href="#fn_2871"><font size="1">2872</font></a>
 But a lock of hair from your heads shall not perish.   <a name="fr_2872"></a><a href="#fn_2872"><font size="1">2873</font></a>
 And by [55] your patience ye shall gain <a name="fr_2873"></a><a href="#fn_2873"><font size="1">2874</font></a>
  your souls.   <a name="fr_2874"></a><a href="#fn_2874"><font size="1">2875</font></a>
 And many <i>men</i>, <a name="fr_2875"></a><a href="#fn_2875"><font size="1">2876</font></a>
  false prophets, shall arise, [56] and lead many astray.   <a name="fr_2876"></a><a href="#fn_2876"><font size="1">2877</font></a>
 And because of the abounding of iniquity, the love of many <a name="page_108"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 108</font></a> <a name="iv.iii.xli-Page_108"></a>[57] shall wax cold.   <a name="fr_2877"></a><a href="#fn_2877"><font size="1">2878</font></a>
 But he that endureth to the end, the same shall be saved.   <a name="fr_2878"></a><a href="#fn_2878"><font size="1">2879</font></a>
 And [58] this, the <a name="fr_2879"></a><a href="#fn_2879"><font size="1">2880</font></a>
  gospel of the kingdom, shall be preached in all the world for a testimony to all nations; and then shall come the end of all.</p>
