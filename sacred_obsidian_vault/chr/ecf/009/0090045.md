 <p> <a name="iv.iii.xxxvi"></a>
 </p><p class="c27"><a name="iv.iii.xxxvi-p1"></a> <span class="c6"><a name="iv.iii.xxxvi-p1.1"></a>Section XXXVI.</span></p> 
 <p class="c12"><a name="iv.iii.xxxvi-p2"></a>[1]  <a name="fr_2455"></a><a href="#fn_2455"><font size="1">2456</font></a>
 Verily, verily, I say unto you, Whosoever keepeth my word shall not see death [2] for ever.   <a name="fr_2456"></a><a href="#fn_2456"><font size="1">2457</font></a>
 The Jews said unto him, Now we know that thou hast demons.  Abraham is dead, and the prophets; and thou sayest, Whosoever keepeth my word shall not [3] taste death for ever.   <a name="fr_2457"></a><a href="#fn_2457"><font size="1">2458</font></a>
 Art thou haply greater than our father Abraham, who is [4] dead, and than the prophets, which are dead? whom makest thou thyself?   <a name="fr_2458"></a><a href="#fn_2458"><font size="1">2459</font></a>
 Jesus said unto them, If I glorify myself, my glory is nothing:  my Father is he that [5] glorifieth me; <i>of</i> whom ye say, that he is our <a name="fr_2459"></a><a href="#fn_2459"><font size="1">2460</font></a>
  God;  <a name="fr_2460"></a><a href="#fn_2460"><font size="1">2461</font></a>
 and <i>yet</i> ye have not known him:  but I know him; and if I should say that I know him not, I should become [6] [Arabic, p. 137] a liar like you:  but I know him, and keep his word.   <a name="fr_2461"></a><a href="#fn_2461"><font size="1">2462</font></a>
 Abraham your father [7] longed to see my day; and he saw, and rejoiced.   <a name="fr_2462"></a><a href="#fn_2462"><font size="1">2463</font></a>
 The Jews said unto him, [8] Thou art now not fifty years old, and hast thou seen Abraham?   <a name="fr_2463"></a><a href="#fn_2463"><font size="1">2464</font></a>
 Jesus said unto [9] them, Verily, verily, I say unto you, Before Abraham was, I am.   <a name="fr_2464"></a><a href="#fn_2464"><font size="1">2465</font></a>
 And they take <a name="fr_2465"></a><a href="#fn_2465"><font size="1">2466</font></a>
  stones to stone him:   <a name="fr_2466"></a><a href="#fn_2466"><font size="1">2467</font></a>
 but Jesus concealed himself, and went out of the temple.  And he passed through them, and went <i>his way</i>.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.xxxvi-p15"></a><a name="page_99"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 99</font></a> <a name="iv.iii.xxxvi-Page_99"></a>[10]  <a name="fr_2467"></a><a href="#fn_2467"><font size="1">2468</font></a>
 And as he passed, he saw a man blind from his mothers womb.   <a name="fr_2468"></a><a href="#fn_2468"><font size="1">2469</font></a>
 And his [11] disciples asked him, and said, Our Master, who sinned, this <i>man</i>, or his parents, so [12] that he was born blind? <a name="fr_2469"></a><a href="#fn_2469"><font size="1">2470</font></a>
    <a name="fr_2470"></a><a href="#fn_2470"><font size="1">2471</font></a>
 Jesus said unto them, Neither did he sin, nor his parents:  [13] but that the works of God may be seen in him. <a name="fr_2471"></a><a href="#fn_2471"><font size="1">2472</font></a>
    <a name="fr_2472"></a><a href="#fn_2472"><font size="1">2473</font></a>
 It is incumbent on me to do the deeds of him that sent me, while it is day:  a night will come, and no man will be [14] able to busy himself.   <a name="fr_2473"></a><a href="#fn_2473"><font size="1">2474</font></a>
 As long as I am in the world, I am the light of the world.  [15]  <a name="fr_2474"></a><a href="#fn_2474"><font size="1">2475</font></a>
 And when he said that, he spat upon the ground, and made clay of his spittle, and [16] smeared <i>it</i> on the eyes of the blind man, and said unto him,  <a name="fr_2475"></a><a href="#fn_2475"><font size="1">2476</font></a>
 Go and wash thyself in [17] the pool <a name="fr_2476"></a><a href="#fn_2476"><font size="1">2477</font></a>
  of Siloam. <a name="fr_2477"></a><a href="#fn_2477"><font size="1">2478</font></a>
    <a name="fr_2478"></a><a href="#fn_2478"><font size="1">2479</font></a>
 And he went and washed, and came seeing.  And his neighbours, which saw him of old begging, said, Is not this he that was sitting begging?  [18]  <a name="fr_2479"></a><a href="#fn_2479"><font size="1">2480</font></a>
 And some said, It is he; and others said, Nay, but he resembles him much.  He [19, 20] said, I am he.   <a name="fr_2480"></a><a href="#fn_2480"><font size="1">2481</font></a>
 They said unto him, How then were thine eyes opened?   <a name="fr_2481"></a><a href="#fn_2481"><font size="1">2482</font></a>
 He answered and said unto them, A man named Jesus made clay, and smeared <i>it</i> on my eyes, and said unto me, Go and wash in the water of Siloam:  and I went and [21] washed, and received sight. <a name="fr_2482"></a><a href="#fn_2482"><font size="1">2483</font></a>
    <a name="fr_2483"></a><a href="#fn_2483"><font size="1">2484</font></a>
 They said unto him, Where is he?  He said, I know not.  [22, 23] [Arabic, p. 138]  <a name="fr_2484"></a><a href="#fn_2484"><font size="1">2485</font></a>
 And they brought him that was previously blind to the Pharisees.   <a name="fr_2485"></a><a href="#fn_2485"><font size="1">2486</font></a>
 And the day in which Jesus made clay and opened with it his eyes was a sabbath [24] day.   <a name="fr_2486"></a><a href="#fn_2486"><font size="1">2487</font></a>
 And again the Pharisees asked him, How didst thou receive sight?  And he said [25] unto them, He put clay on mine eyes, and I washed, and received sight.   <a name="fr_2487"></a><a href="#fn_2487"><font size="1">2488</font></a>
 The people <a name="fr_2488"></a><a href="#fn_2488"><font size="1">2489</font></a>
  of the Pharisees said, This man is not from God, for he keepeth not the sabbath.  And others said, How can a man <i>that is</i> a sinner do these signs?  And there came [26] to be a division amongst them.   <a name="fr_2489"></a><a href="#fn_2489"><font size="1">2490</font></a>
 And again they said to that blind man, Thou, then, what sayest thou of him that opened for thee thine eyes?  He said unto them, [27] I say that he is a prophet.   <a name="fr_2490"></a><a href="#fn_2490"><font size="1">2491</font></a>
 And the Jews did not believe concerning him, that he was blind, and received sight, until they summoned the parents of him who received [28] sight, and asked them,  <a name="fr_2491"></a><a href="#fn_2491"><font size="1">2492</font></a>
 Is this <a name="fr_2492"></a><a href="#fn_2492"><font size="1">2493</font></a>
  your son, <i>of</i> whom ye said that he was born blind? [29] how then, behold, doth he now see?   <a name="fr_2493"></a><a href="#fn_2493"><font size="1">2494</font></a>
 His parents answered and said, We know [30] that this is our son, and that he was born blind:   <a name="fr_2494"></a><a href="#fn_2494"><font size="1">2495</font></a>
 but how he has come to see now, or who it is that opened his eyes, we know not:  and he also has reached his prime; [31] ask him, and he will speak for himself.   <a name="fr_2495"></a><a href="#fn_2495"><font size="1">2496</font></a>
 This said his parents, because they were fearing the Jews:  and the Jews decided, that if any man should confess of him that [32] he was the Messiah, they would put him out of the synagogue.   <a name="fr_2496"></a><a href="#fn_2496"><font size="1">2497</font></a>
 For this reason [33] said his parents, He hath reached his prime; ask him.   <a name="fr_2497"></a><a href="#fn_2497"><font size="1">2498</font></a>
 And they called the man a second time, him that was blind, and said unto him, Praise God:  we know that this [34] man is a sinner.   <a name="fr_2498"></a><a href="#fn_2498"><font size="1">2499</font></a>
 He answered and said unto them, Whether he be a sinner, I know [35] not:  I know one thing, that I was blind, and I now see.   <a name="fr_2499"></a><a href="#fn_2499"><font size="1">2500</font></a>
 They said unto him again, [36] [Arabic, p. 139] What did he unto thee? how opened he for thee thine eyes?   <a name="fr_2500"></a><a href="#fn_2500"><font size="1">2501</font></a>
 He said unto them, I said unto you, and ye did not hear:  what <a name="fr_2501"></a><a href="#fn_2501"><font size="1">2502</font></a>
  wish ye further to hear? [37] ye also, do ye wish to become disciples to him?   <a name="fr_2502"></a><a href="#fn_2502"><font size="1">2503</font></a>
 And they reviled him, and said unto him, Thou art the disciple <a name="fr_2503"></a><a href="#fn_2503"><font size="1">2504</font></a>
  of that <i>man</i>; but as for us, we are the disciples of [38] Moses.   <a name="fr_2504"></a><a href="#fn_2504"><font size="1">2505</font></a>
 And we know that God spake unto Moses:  but this man, we know not [39] whence he is.   <a name="fr_2505"></a><a href="#fn_2505"><font size="1">2506</font></a>
 The man answered and said unto them, From this is the wonder, [40] because ye know not whence he is, and mine eyes hath he opened.   <a name="fr_2506"></a><a href="#fn_2506"><font size="1">2507</font></a>
 And we know that God heareth not the voice of sinners:  but whosoever feareth him, and doeth [41] his will, him he heareth.   <a name="fr_2507"></a><a href="#fn_2507"><font size="1">2508</font></a>
 From eternity hath it not been heard of, that a man [42] opened the eyes of a blind <i>man</i>, who had been born in blindness.   <a name="fr_2508"></a><a href="#fn_2508"><font size="1">2509</font></a>
 If then this <i>man</i> [43] were not from God, he could not do that.   <a name="fr_2509"></a><a href="#fn_2509"><font size="1">2510</font></a>
 They answered and said unto him, Thou wast all of thee born in sins, and dost thou teach us?  And they put him forth without.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.xxxvi-p59"></a>[44]  <a name="fr_2510"></a><a href="#fn_2510"><font size="1">2511</font></a>
 And Jesus heard of his being put forth without, and found him, and said unto [45] him, Dost thou believe in the Son of God?   <a name="fr_2511"></a><a href="#fn_2511"><font size="1">2512</font></a>
 He that was made whole answered [46] and said, Who is he, my Lord, that I may believe in him?   <a name="fr_2512"></a><a href="#fn_2512"><font size="1">2513</font></a>
 Jesus said unto him, [47] Thou hast seen him, and he that speaketh to thee is he.   <a name="fr_2513"></a><a href="#fn_2513"><font size="1">2514</font></a>
 And he said, I believe, my Lord.  And he fell down worshipping him.</p>
