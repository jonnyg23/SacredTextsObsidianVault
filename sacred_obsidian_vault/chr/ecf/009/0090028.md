 <p> <a name="iv.iii.xix"></a>
 </p><p class="c27"><a name="iv.iii.xix-p1"></a> <span class="c6"><a name="iv.iii.xix-p1.1"></a>Section XIX.</span></p> 
 <p class="c12"><a name="iv.iii.xix-p2"></a>[1]  <a name="fr_1331"></a><a href="#fn_1331"><font size="1">1332</font></a>
 And in the fourth watch of the night Jesus came unto them, walking upon the [2]  <a name="fr_1332"></a><a href="#fn_1332"><font size="1">1333</font></a>
 water, after they had rowed <a name="fr_1333"></a><a href="#fn_1333"><font size="1">1334</font></a>
  with difficulty about twenty-five or thirty furlongs.  <a name="page_73"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 73</font></a> <a name="iv.iii.xix-Page_73"></a>[3]  <a name="fr_1334"></a><a href="#fn_1334"><font size="1">1335</font></a>
 And when he drew near unto their boat, his disciples saw him walking on the water; and they were troubled, and supposed that it was a false appearance; and they cried [4] out from their fear.   <a name="fr_1335"></a><a href="#fn_1335"><font size="1">1336</font></a>
 But Jesus straightway spoke unto them, and said, Take courage, [5] for it is I; fear not.   <a name="fr_1336"></a><a href="#fn_1336"><font size="1">1337</font></a>
 Then Cephas answered and said unto him, My Lord, if it be thou, [6] bid me to come unto thee on the water.   <a name="fr_1337"></a><a href="#fn_1337"><font size="1">1338</font></a>
 And Jesus said unto him, Come.  And [7] Cephas went down out of the boat, and walked on the water to come unto Jesus.   <a name="fr_1338"></a><a href="#fn_1338"><font size="1">1339</font></a>
 But [Arabic, p. 74] when he saw the wind strong, he feared, and was on the point of sinking; [8] and he lifted up his voice, and said, My Lord, save me.   <a name="fr_1339"></a><a href="#fn_1339"><font size="1">1340</font></a>
 And immediately our Lord stretched out his hand and took <i>hold of</i> him, and said unto him, [9] Thou of little faith, why didst thou doubt?   <a name="fr_1340"></a><a href="#fn_1340"><font size="1">1341</font></a>
 And when Jesus came near, he went up [10] unto them into the boat, he and Simon, and immediately the wind ceased.   <a name="fr_1341"></a><a href="#fn_1341"><font size="1">1342</font></a>
 And those that were in the ship came and worshipped him, and said, Truly thou art the [11] Son of God.   <a name="fr_1342"></a><a href="#fn_1342"><font size="1">1343</font></a>
 And straightway that ship arrived at the land which they made for.  [12]  <a name="fr_1343"></a><a href="#fn_1343"><font size="1">1344</font></a>
 And when they came out of the ship to the land, they marvelled greatly and were [13] perplexed in themselves:   <a name="fr_1344"></a><a href="#fn_1344"><font size="1">1345</font></a>
 and they had not understood by means of <a name="fr_1345"></a><a href="#fn_1345"><font size="1">1346</font></a>
  that bread, because their heart was gross.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.xix-p18"></a>[14]  <a name="fr_1346"></a><a href="#fn_1346"><font size="1">1347</font></a>
 And when the people of that region knew of the arrival of Jesus, they made haste in all that land, and began to bring those that were diseased, <a name="fr_1347"></a><a href="#fn_1347"><font size="1">1348</font></a>
  borne in their [15] beds to the place where they heard that he was.   <a name="fr_1348"></a><a href="#fn_1348"><font size="1">1349</font></a>
 And wheresoever the place <i>might be</i> which he entered, of the villages or the cities, they laid the sick in the markets, and sought of him that they might touch <a name="fr_1349"></a><a href="#fn_1349"><font size="1">1350</font></a>
  were it only the edge of his garment:  and all that touched <a name="fr_1350"></a><a href="#fn_1350"><font size="1">1351</font></a>
  him were healed and lived. <a name="fr_1351"></a><a href="#fn_1351"><font size="1">1352</font></a>
 </p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.xix-p25"></a>[16]  <a name="fr_1352"></a><a href="#fn_1352"><font size="1">1353</font></a>
 And on the day after that, the multitude which was standing on the shore of the sea saw that there was there no other ship save that into which the disciples had [17] gone up,  <a name="fr_1353"></a><a href="#fn_1353"><font size="1">1354</font></a>
 and that Jesus went not up into the ship with his disciples (but there were other ships from Tiberias near <a name="fr_1354"></a><a href="#fn_1354"><font size="1">1355</font></a>
  the place where they ate the bread when Jesus blessed [18] <i>it</i>):   <a name="fr_1355"></a><a href="#fn_1355"><font size="1">1356</font></a>
 and when that multitude saw that Jesus was not there, nor yet his disciples, they [19] [Arabic, p. 75] went up into those ships, and came to Capernaum, and sought Jesus.   <a name="fr_1356"></a><a href="#fn_1356"><font size="1">1357</font></a>
 And when they found him on the other side of the sea, they said unto him, Our [20] Master, when camest thou hither?   <a name="fr_1357"></a><a href="#fn_1357"><font size="1">1358</font></a>
 Jesus answered and said unto them, Verily, verily, I say unto you, Ye have not sought me because of <a name="fr_1358"></a><a href="#fn_1358"><font size="1">1359</font></a>
  your seeing the signs, but because of [21] your eating the bread and being satisfied.   <a name="fr_1359"></a><a href="#fn_1359"><font size="1">1360</font></a>
 Serve not the food which perisheth, but the food which abideth in eternal life, <a name="fr_1360"></a><a href="#fn_1360"><font size="1">1361</font></a>
  which the Son of man will give unto you:  him <a name="fr_1361"></a><a href="#fn_1361"><font size="1">1362</font></a>
  [22] hath God the Father sealed.   <a name="fr_1362"></a><a href="#fn_1362"><font size="1">1363</font></a>
 They said unto him, What shall we do that we may [23] work the work of God?   <a name="fr_1363"></a><a href="#fn_1363"><font size="1">1364</font></a>
 Jesus answered and said unto them, This is the work of [24] God, that ye believe in him whom he hath sent.   <a name="fr_1364"></a><a href="#fn_1364"><font size="1">1365</font></a>
 They said unto him, What sign hast thou done, that we may see, and believe in thee? what hast thou wrought?  [25]  <a name="fr_1365"></a><a href="#fn_1365"><font size="1">1366</font></a>
 Our fathers ate the manna in the wilderness; as it was written, Bread from heaven [26] gave he them to eat.   <a name="fr_1366"></a><a href="#fn_1366"><font size="1">1367</font></a>
 Jesus said unto them, Verily, verily, I say unto you, Moses gave you not bread from [27] heaven; but my Father gave <a name="fr_1367"></a><a href="#fn_1367"><font size="1">1368</font></a>
  you the bread of truth <a name="fr_1368"></a><a href="#fn_1368"><font size="1">1369</font></a>
  from heaven.   <a name="fr_1369"></a><a href="#fn_1369"><font size="1">1370</font></a>
 The bread of God is that which came down from heaven and gave the [28, 29] world life.   <a name="fr_1370"></a><a href="#fn_1370"><font size="1">1371</font></a>
 They said unto him, Our Lord, give us at all times this bread.   <a name="fr_1371"></a><a href="#fn_1371"><font size="1">1372</font></a>
 Jesus said unto them, I am the bread of life:  whosoever cometh unto me shall not hunger, [30] and whosoever believeth in me shall not thirst for ever.   <a name="fr_1372"></a><a href="#fn_1372"><font size="1">1373</font></a>
 But I said unto you, [31] Ye have seen me, and have not believed.   <a name="fr_1373"></a><a href="#fn_1373"><font size="1">1374</font></a>
 And all that my Father hath given to me cometh unto me; and whosoever cometh unto me I shall not cast him forth without.  [32]  <a name="fr_1374"></a><a href="#fn_1374"><font size="1">1375</font></a>
 I came down from heaven, not to do my <i>own</i> will, but to do the will of him [33] that sent me;  <a name="fr_1375"></a><a href="#fn_1375"><font size="1">1376</font></a>
 and this is the will of him that sent me, that I should lose nothing of [34] [Arabic, p. 76] that which he gave me, but raise it up in the last day.   <a name="fr_1376"></a><a href="#fn_1376"><font size="1">1377</font></a>
 This is the will of my Father, that every one that seeth the Son, and believeth in him, should have eternal life; and I will raise him up in the last day.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.xix-p51"></a>[35]  <a name="fr_1377"></a><a href="#fn_1377"><font size="1">1378</font></a>
 The Jews therefore murmured against him because of his saying, I am the bread [36] which came down from heaven.   <a name="fr_1378"></a><a href="#fn_1378"><font size="1">1379</font></a>
 And they said, Is not this Jesus, the son of Joseph, whose father and mother we know? then how saith this <i>man</i>, I came down from [37, 38] heaven?   <a name="fr_1379"></a><a href="#fn_1379"><font size="1">1380</font></a>
 Jesus answered and said unto them, Murmur not one with another.   <a name="fr_1380"></a><a href="#fn_1380"><font size="1">1381</font></a>
 No <a name="page_74"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 74</font></a> <a name="iv.iii.xix-Page_74"></a>man is able to come unto me, except the Father which sent me draw him; and I will [39] raise him up in the last day.   <a name="fr_1381"></a><a href="#fn_1381"><font size="1">1382</font></a>
 It is written in the prophet, They shall all be the taught of God.  Every one who heareth from the Father now, <a name="fr_1382"></a><a href="#fn_1382"><font size="1">1383</font></a>
  and learneth of him, cometh [40] unto me.   <a name="fr_1383"></a><a href="#fn_1383"><font size="1">1384</font></a>
 No man now seeth the Father; but he that is from God, he it is that seeth [41] the Father.   <a name="fr_1384"></a><a href="#fn_1384"><font size="1">1385</font></a>
 Verily, verily, I say unto you, Whosoever believeth in me hath eternal [42, 43] life.   <a name="fr_1385"></a><a href="#fn_1385"><font size="1">1386</font></a>
 I am the bread of life.   <a name="fr_1386"></a><a href="#fn_1386"><font size="1">1387</font></a>
 Your fathers ate the manna in the wilderness, and [44] they died.   <a name="fr_1387"></a><a href="#fn_1387"><font size="1">1388</font></a>
 This is the bread which came down from heaven, that a man may eat [45] of it, and not die.   <a name="fr_1388"></a><a href="#fn_1388"><font size="1">1389</font></a>
 I am the bread of life which came down from heaven:  and if a man eat of this bread he shall live for ever:   <a name="fr_1389"></a><a href="#fn_1389"><font size="1">1390</font></a>
 and the bread which I shall give is my body, which I give for the life of the world.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.xix-p65"></a>[46]  <a name="fr_1390"></a><a href="#fn_1390"><font size="1">1391</font></a>
 The Jews therefore quarrelled one with another, and said, How can he give us [47] [Arabic, p. 77] his body that we may eat it?   <a name="fr_1391"></a><a href="#fn_1391"><font size="1">1392</font></a>
 Jesus said unto them, Verily, verily, I say unto you, If ye do not eat the body of the Son of man and drink his blood, ye shall [48] not have life in yourselves.   <a name="fr_1392"></a><a href="#fn_1392"><font size="1">1393</font></a>
 Whosoever eateth of my body and drinketh of my blood [49] hath eternal life; and I will raise him up in the last day.   <a name="fr_1393"></a><a href="#fn_1393"><font size="1">1394</font></a>
 My body truly is meat, <a name="fr_1394"></a><a href="#fn_1394"><font size="1">1395</font></a>
  and [50] my blood truly is drink. <a name="fr_1395"></a><a href="#fn_1395"><font size="1">1396</font></a>
    <a name="fr_1396"></a><a href="#fn_1396"><font size="1">1397</font></a>
 Whosoever eateth my body and drinketh my blood abideth [51] in me, and I in him— <a name="fr_1397"></a><a href="#fn_1397"><font size="1">1398</font></a>
 as the living Father sent me, and I am alive because of the [52] Father; and whosoever eateth me, he also shall live because of me.   <a name="fr_1398"></a><a href="#fn_1398"><font size="1">1399</font></a>
 This is the bread which came down from heaven:  and not according as your fathers ate the [53] manna, and died:  whosoever eateth of this bread shall live for ever.   <a name="fr_1399"></a><a href="#fn_1399"><font size="1">1400</font></a>
 This he said in [54] the synagogue, when he was teaching in Capernaum.   <a name="fr_1400"></a><a href="#fn_1400"><font size="1">1401</font></a>
 And many of his disciples, when they heard, said, This word is hard; who is he that can hear it?</p>
