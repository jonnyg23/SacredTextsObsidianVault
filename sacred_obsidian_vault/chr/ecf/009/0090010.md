 <p> <a name="iv.iii.i"></a>
 </p><p class="c11"><a name="iv.iii.i-p1"></a> <a name="page_43"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 43</font></a> <a name="iv.iii.i-Page_43"></a><span class="c10"><a name="iv.iii.i-p1.1"></a>The Text of the Diatessaron.</span></p> 
 <p class="Centered"><a name="iv.iii.i-p2"></a> ————————————</p> 
 <p class="c27"><a name="iv.iii.i-p3"></a><span class="c6"><a name="iv.iii.i-p3.1"></a>[Section I]</span></p> 
 <p class="c12"><a name="iv.iii.i-p4"></a>[1]  <a name="fr_74"></a><a href="#fn_74"><font size="1">75</font></a>
 In the beginning was the Word, and the Word was with God, and God is the [2,3] Word.   <a name="fr_75"></a><a href="#fn_75"><font size="1">76</font></a>
 This <i>was</i> in the beginning with God.   <a name="fr_76"></a><a href="#fn_76"><font size="1">77</font></a>
 Everything was by his hand, and [4] without him not even one existing thing was <i>made</i>.   <a name="fr_77"></a><a href="#fn_77"><font size="1">78</font></a>
 In him was life, and the life [5] is the light of men.   <a name="fr_78"></a><a href="#fn_78"><font size="1">79</font></a>
 And the light shineth in the darkness, and the darkness apprehended it not.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.i-p10"></a>[6]  <a name="fr_79"></a><a href="#fn_79"><font size="1">80</font></a>
   <a name="fr_80"></a><a href="#fn_80"><font size="1">81</font></a>
 There was in the days of Herod the king a priest whose name was Zacharias, of the family of Abijah; and his wife was of the daughters of Aaron, and her name [7] was Elizabeth.   <a name="fr_81"></a><a href="#fn_81"><font size="1">82</font></a>
 And they were both righteous before God, walking in all his commands, [8] and in the uprightness of God without reproach.   <a name="fr_82"></a><a href="#fn_82"><font size="1">83</font></a>
 And they had no son, for [9] Elizabeth was barren, and they had both advanced in age.   <a name="fr_83"></a><a href="#fn_83"><font size="1">84</font></a>
 And while he discharged [10] [Arabic, p. 2] the duties of priest in the order of his service before God,  <a name="fr_84"></a><a href="#fn_84"><font size="1">85</font></a>
 according to the custom of the priesthood it was his turn to burn incense; so he entered the [11] temple of the Lord.   <a name="fr_85"></a><a href="#fn_85"><font size="1">86</font></a>
 And the whole gathering of the people were praying without at the [12] time of the incense.   <a name="fr_86"></a><a href="#fn_86"><font size="1">87</font></a>
 And there appeared unto Zacharias the angel of the Lord, standing [13] at the right of the altar of incense;  <a name="fr_87"></a><a href="#fn_87"><font size="1">88</font></a>
 and Zacharias was troubled when he saw him, [14] and fear fell upon him.   <a name="fr_88"></a><a href="#fn_88"><font size="1">89</font></a>
 But the angel said unto him, Be not agitated, <a name="fr_89"></a><a href="#fn_89"><font size="1">90</font></a>
  Zacharias, for thy prayer is heard, and thy wife Elizabeth shall bear thee a son, and thou shalt [15] call his name John;  <a name="fr_90"></a><a href="#fn_90"><font size="1">91</font></a>
 and thou shalt have joy and gladness, and many shall rejoice [16] at his birth.   <a name="fr_91"></a><a href="#fn_91"><font size="1">92</font></a>
 And he shall be great before the Lord, and shall not drink wine nor strong drink, and he shall be filled with the Holy Spirit <a name="fr_92"></a><a href="#fn_92"><font size="1">93</font></a>
  while he is in his mothers [17] womb.   <a name="fr_93"></a><a href="#fn_93"><font size="1">94</font></a>
 And he shall turn back many of the children of Israel to the Lord their [18] God.   <a name="fr_94"></a><a href="#fn_94"><font size="1">95</font></a>
 And he shall go before him in the spirit, and in the power of Elijah the prophet, to turn back the heart of the fathers to the sons, and those that obey not to the knowledge <a name="fr_95"></a><a href="#fn_95"><font size="1">96</font></a>
  of the righteous; and to prepare for the Lord a perfect people.  [19]  <a name="fr_96"></a><a href="#fn_96"><font size="1">97</font></a>
 And Zacharias said unto the angel, How shall I know this, since I am an old man [20] and my wife is advanced in years?   <a name="fr_97"></a><a href="#fn_97"><font size="1">98</font></a>
 And the angel answered and said unto him, I am Gabriel, that standeth before God; and I was sent to speak unto thee, and give [21] thee tidings of this.   <a name="fr_98"></a><a href="#fn_98"><font size="1">99</font></a>
 Henceforth thou shalt be speechless, and shalt not be able to speak until the day in which this shall come to pass, because thou didst not trust [22] this my word, which shall be accomplished in its time.   <a name="fr_99"></a><a href="#fn_99"><font size="1">100</font></a>
 And the people were standing [Arabic, p. 3] awaiting Zacharias, and they were perplexed at his delaying in the temple.  [23] <a name="fr_100"></a><a href="#fn_100"><font size="1">101</font></a>
 And when Zacharias went out, he was not able to speak unto them:  so they knew that he had seen in the temple a vision; and he made signs unto them, and [24] continued dumb.   <a name="fr_101"></a><a href="#fn_101"><font size="1">102</font></a>
 And when the days of his service were completed, he departed to his dwelling.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.i-p34"></a>[25]  <a name="fr_102"></a><a href="#fn_102"><font size="1">103</font></a>
 And after those days Elizabeth his wife conceived; and she hid herself five [26] months, and said,  <a name="fr_103"></a><a href="#fn_103"><font size="1">104</font></a>
 This hath the Lord done unto me in the days when he looked upon me, to remove my reproach from among men.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.i-p37"></a>[27]  <a name="fr_104"></a><a href="#fn_104"><font size="1">105</font></a>
 And <a name="fr_105"></a><a href="#fn_105"><font size="1">106</font></a>
  in the sixth month Gabriel the angel was sent from God to Galilee <a name="fr_106"></a><a href="#fn_106"><font size="1">107</font></a>
  to a [28] city called Nazareth,  <a name="fr_107"></a><a href="#fn_107"><font size="1">108</font></a>
 to a virgin given in marriage to a man named Joseph, of the [29] house of David; and the virgins name was Mary.   <a name="fr_108"></a><a href="#fn_108"><font size="1">109</font></a>
 And the angel entered unto her and said unto her, Peace be unto thee, thou who art filled with grace.  Our Lord <a name="page_44"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 44</font></a> <a name="iv.iii.i-Page_44"></a>[30] is with thee, thou blessed amongst women.   <a name="fr_109"></a><a href="#fn_109"><font size="1">110</font></a>
 And she, when she beheld, was agitated [31] at his word, and pondered what this salutation could be.   <a name="fr_110"></a><a href="#fn_110"><font size="1">111</font></a>
 And the angel said unto [32] her, Fear not, Mary, for thou hast found favour with God.   <a name="fr_111"></a><a href="#fn_111"><font size="1">112</font></a>
 Thou shalt now conceive, [33] and bear a son, and call his name <span class="sc"><a name="iv.iii.i-p45.2"></a>Jesus</span>.   <a name="fr_112"></a><a href="#fn_112"><font size="1">113</font></a>
 This shall be great, and shall be called the Son of the Most High; and the Lord God will give him the throne of [34] David his father:  and he shall rule over the house of Jacob for ever;  <a name="fr_113"></a><a href="#fn_113"><font size="1">114</font></a>
 and to his [35] kingdom there shall be no end.   <a name="fr_114"></a><a href="#fn_114"><font size="1">115</font></a>
 Mary said unto the angel, How shall this be to [36] me when no man hath known me?   <a name="fr_115"></a><a href="#fn_115"><font size="1">116</font></a>
 The angel answered and said unto her, The [Arabic, p. 4] Holy Spirit will come, and the power of the Most High shall rest upon thee, and therefore shall <i>he</i> that is born of thee be pure, and shall be called the Son [37] of God.   <a name="fr_116"></a><a href="#fn_116"><font size="1">117</font></a>
 And lo, Elizabeth thy kinswoman, she also hath conceived a son in her old [38] age; and this is the sixth month with her, her that is called barren.   <a name="fr_117"></a><a href="#fn_117"><font size="1">118</font></a>
 For nothing is [39] difficult for God.   <a name="fr_118"></a><a href="#fn_118"><font size="1">119</font></a>
 Mary said, Lo, I am the handmaid of the Lord; let it be unto me according unto thy word.  And the angel departed from her.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.i-p53"></a>[40]  <a name="fr_119"></a><a href="#fn_119"><font size="1">120</font></a>
 And then Mary arose in those days and went in haste into the hill country, <a name="fr_120"></a><a href="#fn_120"><font size="1">121</font></a>
  to a <a name="fr_121"></a><a href="#fn_121"><font size="1">122</font></a>
  [41] city of Judah;  <a name="fr_122"></a><a href="#fn_122"><font size="1">123</font></a>
 and entered into the house of Zacharias, and asked for the health of [42] Elizabeth.   <a name="fr_123"></a><a href="#fn_123"><font size="1">124</font></a>
 And when Elizabeth heard the salutation of Mary, the babe leaped in [43] her womb.   <a name="fr_124"></a><a href="#fn_124"><font size="1">125</font></a>
 And Elizabeth was filled with the Holy Spirit; and cried with a loud voice and said unto Mary, Blessed art thou amongst women, and blessed is the [44] fruit that is in thy womb.   <a name="fr_125"></a><a href="#fn_125"><font size="1">126</font></a>
 Whence have I this <i>privilege</i>, that the mother of my [45] Lord should come unto me?   <a name="fr_126"></a><a href="#fn_126"><font size="1">127</font></a>
 When the sound of thy salutation reached my ears, [46] with great joy rejoiced the babe in my womb.   <a name="fr_127"></a><a href="#fn_127"><font size="1">128</font></a>
 And blessed is she who believed [47] that what was spoken <i>to her</i> from the Lord would be fulfilled.   <a name="fr_128"></a><a href="#fn_128"><font size="1">129</font></a>
 And Mary said,</p> 
 <p class="c28"><a name="iv.iii.i-p64"></a>My soul doth magnify the Lord,</p> 
 <p class="c29"><a name="iv.iii.i-p65"></a>[48]  <a name="fr_129"></a><a href="#fn_129"><font size="1">130</font></a>
 And my spirit hath rejoiced in God my Saviour,</p> 
 <p class="c29"><a name="iv.iii.i-p67"></a>[49]  <a name="fr_130"></a><a href="#fn_130"><font size="1">131</font></a>
 Who hath looked upon the low estate of his handmaiden:</p> 
 <p class="c29"><a name="iv.iii.i-p69"></a>Lo, henceforth, all generations <a name="fr_131"></a><a href="#fn_131"><font size="1">132</font></a>
  shall pronounce blessing on me.</p> 
 <p class="c29"><a name="iv.iii.i-p71"></a>[50]  <a name="fr_132"></a><a href="#fn_132"><font size="1">133</font></a>
 For <a name="fr_133"></a><a href="#fn_133"><font size="1">134</font></a>
  he hath done great things for me, who is mighty,</p> 
 <p class="c29"><a name="iv.iii.i-p74"></a>And holy is his name.</p> 
 <p class="c29"><a name="iv.iii.i-p75"></a>[51]  <a name="fr_134"></a><a href="#fn_134"><font size="1">135</font></a>
 And his mercy embraceth them who fear him,</p> 
 <p class="c29"><a name="iv.iii.i-p77"></a>Throughout the ages and the times.</p> 
 <p class="c29"><a name="iv.iii.i-p78"></a>[52] [Arabic, p. 5]  <a name="fr_135"></a><a href="#fn_135"><font size="1">136</font></a>
 He wrought the victory with his arm,</p> 
 <p class="c29"><a name="iv.iii.i-p80"></a>And scattered them that prided themselves in their opinions.</p> 
 <p class="c29"><a name="iv.iii.i-p81"></a>[53]  <a name="fr_136"></a><a href="#fn_136"><font size="1">137</font></a>
 He overthrew them that acted haughtily from their thrones,</p> 
 <p class="c29"><a name="iv.iii.i-p83"></a>And raised the lowly.</p> 
 <p class="c29"><a name="iv.iii.i-p84"></a>[54]  <a name="fr_137"></a><a href="#fn_137"><font size="1">138</font></a>
 He satisfied with good things the hungry,</p> 
 <p class="c29"><a name="iv.iii.i-p86"></a>And left the rich without anything.</p> 
 <p class="c29"><a name="iv.iii.i-p87"></a>[55]  <a name="fr_138"></a><a href="#fn_138"><font size="1">139</font></a>
 He helped Israel his servant,</p> 
 <p class="c29"><a name="iv.iii.i-p89"></a>And remembered his mercy</p> 
 <p class="c29"><a name="iv.iii.i-p90"></a>[56]  <a name="fr_139"></a><a href="#fn_139"><font size="1">140</font></a>
 (According as he spake with our fathers)</p> 
 <p class="c30"><a name="iv.iii.i-p92"></a>Unto Abraham and unto his seed for ever.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.i-p93"></a>[57]  <a name="fr_140"></a><a href="#fn_140"><font size="1">141</font></a>
 And Mary abode with Elizabeth about three months, and returned unto her house.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.i-p95"></a>[58, 59]  <a name="fr_141"></a><a href="#fn_141"><font size="1">142</font></a>
 And Elizabeths time of delivery was come; and she brought forth a son.   <a name="fr_142"></a><a href="#fn_142"><font size="1">143</font></a>
 And her neighbours and kinsfolk heard that God had multiplied his mercy towards her; [60] and they rejoiced with her.   <a name="fr_143"></a><a href="#fn_143"><font size="1">144</font></a>
 And when <a name="fr_144"></a><a href="#fn_144"><font size="1">145</font></a>
  it was the eighth day, they came to circumcise the child, and called him Zacharias, <i>calling him</i> by the name of his father.  [61]  <a name="fr_145"></a><a href="#fn_145"><font size="1">146</font></a>
 And his mother answered and said unto them, Not so; but he shall be called John.  [62]  <a name="fr_146"></a><a href="#fn_146"><font size="1">147</font></a>
 And they said unto her, There is no man of thy kindred that is called by this name.  [63, 64]  <a name="fr_147"></a><a href="#fn_147"><font size="1">148</font></a>
 And they made signs to his father, <i>saying</i>, How dost thou wish to name him?   <a name="fr_148"></a><a href="#fn_148"><font size="1">149</font></a>
 And he asked for a tablet, and wrote and said, His name is John.  And every one wondered.  [65]  <a name="fr_149"></a><a href="#fn_149"><font size="1">150</font></a>
 And immediately his mouth was opened, and his tongue, and he spake and [66] praised God.   <a name="fr_150"></a><a href="#fn_150"><font size="1">151</font></a>
 And fear fell on all their neighbours:  and this was spoken of <a name="fr_151"></a><a href="#fn_151"><font size="1">152</font></a>
  in all <a name="page_45"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 45</font></a> <a name="iv.iii.i-Page_45"></a>[67] the mountains of Judah.   <a name="fr_152"></a><a href="#fn_152"><font size="1">153</font></a>
 And all who heard pondered in their hearts and said, What shall this child be?  And the hand of the Lord was with him.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.i-p108"></a>[68]  <a name="fr_153"></a><a href="#fn_153"><font size="1">154</font></a>
 And Zacharias his father was filled with the Holy Spirit, and prophesied and said,</p> 
 <p class="c28"><a name="iv.iii.i-p110"></a>[69]  <a name="fr_154"></a><a href="#fn_154"><font size="1">155</font></a>
 Blessed is the Lord, the God of Israel,</p> 
 <p class="c29"><a name="iv.iii.i-p112"></a>Who hath cared for his people, and wrought for it salvation;</p> 
 <p class="c29"><a name="iv.iii.i-p113"></a>[70]  <a name="fr_155"></a><a href="#fn_155"><font size="1">156</font></a>
 And hath raised for us the horn of salvation</p> 
 <p class="c29"><a name="iv.iii.i-p115"></a>[Arabic, p. 6] In the house of David his servant</p> 
 <p class="c29"><a name="iv.iii.i-p116"></a>[71]  <a name="fr_156"></a><a href="#fn_156"><font size="1">157</font></a>
 (As he spake by the mouth of his holy prophets from eternity),</p> 
 <p class="c29"><a name="iv.iii.i-p118"></a>[72]  <a name="fr_157"></a><a href="#fn_157"><font size="1">158</font></a>
 That he might save us from our enemies,</p> 
 <p class="c29"><a name="iv.iii.i-p120"></a>And from the hand of all them that hate us.</p> 
 <p class="c29"><a name="iv.iii.i-p121"></a>[73]  <a name="fr_158"></a><a href="#fn_158"><font size="1">159</font></a>
 And he hath performed his mercy towards our fathers,</p> 
 <p class="c29"><a name="iv.iii.i-p123"></a>And remembered his holy covenants,</p> 
 <p class="c29"><a name="iv.iii.i-p124"></a>[74]  <a name="fr_159"></a><a href="#fn_159"><font size="1">160</font></a>
 And the oath which he sware unto Abraham our father,</p> 
 <p class="c29"><a name="iv.iii.i-p126"></a>[75]  <a name="fr_160"></a><a href="#fn_160"><font size="1">161</font></a>
 That he would give us deliverance from the hand of our enemies,</p> 
 <p class="c29"><a name="iv.iii.i-p128"></a>And without fear we shall <a name="fr_161"></a><a href="#fn_161"><font size="1">162</font></a>
  serve before him</p> 
 <p class="c29"><a name="iv.iii.i-p130"></a>[76]  <a name="fr_162"></a><a href="#fn_162"><font size="1">163</font></a>
 All our days with equity and righteousness.</p> 
 <p class="c29"><a name="iv.iii.i-p132"></a>[77]  <a name="fr_163"></a><a href="#fn_163"><font size="1">164</font></a>
 And as for thee, O child, prophet of the Most High shalt thou be called.</p> 
 <p class="c29"><a name="iv.iii.i-p134"></a>Thou shalt go forth before the face of the Lord to prepare his way,</p> 
 <p class="c29"><a name="iv.iii.i-p135"></a>[78]  <a name="fr_164"></a><a href="#fn_164"><font size="1">165</font></a>
 To give the knowledge of salvation <a name="fr_165"></a><a href="#fn_165"><font size="1">166</font></a>
  unto his people,</p> 
 <p class="c29"><a name="iv.iii.i-p138"></a>For the forgiveness of their sins,</p> 
 <p class="c29"><a name="iv.iii.i-p139"></a>[79]  <a name="fr_166"></a><a href="#fn_166"><font size="1">167</font></a>
 Through the mercy of <a name="fr_167"></a><a href="#fn_167"><font size="1">168</font></a>
  the compassion of our God,</p> 
 <p class="c29"><a name="iv.iii.i-p142"></a>With which he careth for <a name="fr_168"></a><a href="#fn_168"><font size="1">169</font></a>
  us, to appear <a name="fr_169"></a><a href="#fn_169"><font size="1">170</font></a>
  from on high</p> 
 <p class="c29"><a name="iv.iii.i-p145"></a>[80]  <a name="fr_170"></a><a href="#fn_170"><font size="1">171</font></a>
 To give light to them that sit in darkness and under the shadow of death,</p> 
 <p class="c30"><a name="iv.iii.i-p147"></a>And to set straight our feet in the way of peace.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.i-p148"></a>[81]  <a name="fr_171"></a><a href="#fn_171"><font size="1">172</font></a>
 And the child grew and became strong in the spirit, and abode in the desert until the time of his appearing unto the children of Israel.</p>
