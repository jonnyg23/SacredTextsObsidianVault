 <p> <a name="iv.iii.xlvii"></a>
 </p><p class="c27"><a name="iv.iii.xlvii-p1"></a> <span class="c6"><a name="iv.iii.xlvii-p1.1"></a>Section XLVII.</span></p> 
 <p class="c12"><a name="iv.iii.xlvii-p2"></a>[1]  <a name="fr_3247"></a><a href="#fn_3247"><font size="1">3248</font></a>
 A little <i>while</i>, and ye shall not behold me; and a little <i>while</i> again, and ye shall [2] behold me; because I go to the Father.   <a name="fr_3248"></a><a href="#fn_3248"><font size="1">3249</font></a>
 His disciples therefore said one to another, What is this that he hath said unto us, A little <i>while</i>, and ye shall not behold me; and a little <i>while</i> again, and ye shall behold me:  and, I go to my [3] Father?   <a name="fr_3249"></a><a href="#fn_3249"><font size="1">3250</font></a>
 And they said, What is this little <i>while</i> that he hath said?  We know not [4] what he speaketh.   <a name="fr_3250"></a><a href="#fn_3250"><font size="1">3251</font></a>
 And Jesus perceived that they were seeking to ask him, and said unto them, Do ye inquire among yourselves concerning this, that I said unto you, A little <i>while</i>, and ye behold me not, and a little while again, and ye shall [5] behold me?   <a name="fr_3251"></a><a href="#fn_3251"><font size="1">3252</font></a>
 Verily, verily, <a name="fr_3252"></a><a href="#fn_3252"><font size="1">3253</font></a>
  I say unto you, that ye shall weep and grieve, but the world shall rejoice:  and ye shall be sorrowful, but your grief shall turn <a name="fr_3253"></a><a href="#fn_3253"><font size="1">3254</font></a>
  to joy.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.xlvii-p10"></a><a name="page_116"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 116</font></a> <a name="iv.iii.xlvii-Page_116"></a>[6]  <a name="fr_3254"></a><a href="#fn_3254"><font size="1">3255</font></a>
 For, a woman when the time is come for her that she should bring forth, the arrival of the day of her bringing forth distresseth her:  but whenever she hath brought forth a son, she remembereth not her distress, for joy at the birth of a man into the [7] world.   <a name="fr_3255"></a><a href="#fn_3255"><font size="1">3256</font></a>
 And ye now also grieve:  but I shall see you, and your hearts shall rejoice, [8] [Arabic, p. 178] and your joy no man taketh from you.   <a name="fr_3256"></a><a href="#fn_3256"><font size="1">3257</font></a>
 And in that day ye shall ask me nothing.  And verily, verily, <a name="fr_3257"></a><a href="#fn_3257"><font size="1">3258</font></a>
  I say unto you, All that ye ask my Father in my name, he will give you.   <a name="fr_3258"></a><a href="#fn_3258"><font size="1">3259</font></a>
 Hitherto ye have asked nothing [9] in my name:  ask, and ye shall receive, that your joy may be complete.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.xlvii-p16"></a>[10]  <a name="fr_3259"></a><a href="#fn_3259"><font size="1">3260</font></a>
 I have spoken unto you now in ænigmas: <a name="fr_3260"></a><a href="#fn_3260"><font size="1">3261</font></a>
   but there will come an hour when <a name="fr_3261"></a><a href="#fn_3261"><font size="1">3262</font></a>
  I shall not speak to you in ænigmas, <a name="fr_3262"></a><a href="#fn_3262"><font size="1">3263</font></a>
  but shall reveal unto you the Father plainly, [11] in that day when <a name="fr_3263"></a><a href="#fn_3263"><font size="1">3264</font></a>
  ye shall ask in my name:   <a name="fr_3264"></a><a href="#fn_3264"><font size="1">3265</font></a>
 and I say not unto you, that I shall [12] entreat the Father for you;  <a name="fr_3265"></a><a href="#fn_3265"><font size="1">3266</font></a>
 for the Father loveth you, because ye have loved me, [13] and have believed that I came forth from my Father.   <a name="fr_3266"></a><a href="#fn_3266"><font size="1">3267</font></a>
 I came forth from my Father, and came into the world:  and I leave the world, and go unto my Father.  [14]  <a name="fr_3267"></a><a href="#fn_3267"><font size="1">3268</font></a>
 His disciples said unto him, Lo, thy speech is now plain, and thou hast not said one [15] thing in an ænigma.   <a name="fr_3268"></a><a href="#fn_3268"><font size="1">3269</font></a>
 Now, lo, we know that thou knowest everything, and needest not that any man should ask thee:  and by this we believe that thou camest forth [16, 17] from God.   <a name="fr_3269"></a><a href="#fn_3269"><font size="1">3270</font></a>
 Jesus said unto them, Believe that an hour cometh,  <a name="fr_3270"></a><a href="#fn_3270"><font size="1">3271</font></a>
 and lo, it hath come, and ye shall be scattered, every one of you to his place, and shall leave me [18] alone:  and yet I am not alone, because the Father is with me.   <a name="fr_3271"></a><a href="#fn_3271"><font size="1">3272</font></a>
 This have I said unto you, that in me ye may have peace.  And in the world trouble shall overtake you:  but be of good courage; for I have overcome the world.</p> 
 <p class="c13"><a name="iv.iii.xlvii-p30"></a>[19]  <a name="fr_3272"></a><a href="#fn_3272"><font size="1">3273</font></a>
 This said Jesus, and lifted up his eyes unto heaven, and said, My Father, the hour [20] is come; glorify thy Son, that thy Son may glorify thee:   <a name="fr_3273"></a><a href="#fn_3273"><font size="1">3274</font></a>
 as thou gavest him authority [21] over all flesh, that all that thou hast given him, he might give them <a name="fr_3274"></a><a href="#fn_3274"><font size="1">3275</font></a>
  eternal life.   <a name="fr_3275"></a><a href="#fn_3275"><font size="1">3276</font></a>
 And this is eternal life, that they should <a name="fr_3276"></a><a href="#fn_3276"><font size="1">3277</font></a>
  know that thou alone art true God, and <i>that he</i> [22] [Arabic, p. 179] whom thou didst send is Jesus the Messiah. <a name="fr_3277"></a><a href="#fn_3277"><font size="1">3278</font></a>
    <a name="fr_3278"></a><a href="#fn_3278"><font size="1">3279</font></a>
 I glorified thee in the earth, [23] and the work which thou gavest me to do I have accomplished.   <a name="fr_3279"></a><a href="#fn_3279"><font size="1">3280</font></a>
 And now glorify thou me, O Father, beside thee, with that glory which I had with thee [24] before the world was.   <a name="fr_3280"></a><a href="#fn_3280"><font size="1">3281</font></a>
 I made known thy name to the men whom thou gavest me out of the world:  thine they were, and thou gavest them to me; and they have kept [25, 26] thy word.   <a name="fr_3281"></a><a href="#fn_3281"><font size="1">3282</font></a>
 Now they <a name="fr_3282"></a><a href="#fn_3282"><font size="1">3283</font></a>
  know that all that thou hast given me is from thee:   <a name="fr_3283"></a><a href="#fn_3283"><font size="1">3284</font></a>
 and the sayings which thou gavest me I have given unto them; and they received <i>them</i>, and knew of a truth that I came forth from thee, and believed that thou didst send me.  [27]  <a name="fr_3284"></a><a href="#fn_3284"><font size="1">3285</font></a>
 And I ask for their sake:  and my asking is not for the world, but for those whom [28] thou hast given me; for they are thine:   <a name="fr_3285"></a><a href="#fn_3285"><font size="1">3286</font></a>
 and all that is mine is thine, and all that is [29] thine is mine:  and I am glorified in them.   <a name="fr_3286"></a><a href="#fn_3286"><font size="1">3287</font></a>
 And now I am not in the world, and they are in the world, and I come to thee.  My <a name="fr_3287"></a><a href="#fn_3287"><font size="1">3288</font></a>
  holy Father, keep them in thy [30] name which thou hast given unto me, that they may be one, as we are.   <a name="fr_3288"></a><a href="#fn_3288"><font size="1">3289</font></a>
 When I was with them in the world, I kept them in thy name:  and I kept those whom thou gavest unto me:  and no man of them hath perished, but the son of perdition; that [31] the scripture might be fulfilled.   <a name="fr_3289"></a><a href="#fn_3289"><font size="1">3290</font></a>
 Now I come to thee:  and this I say in the world, [32] that my joy may be complete in them.   <a name="fr_3290"></a><a href="#fn_3290"><font size="1">3291</font></a>
 I have given them thy word; and the world [33] hated them, because they were not of the world, as I was not of the world.   <a name="fr_3291"></a><a href="#fn_3291"><font size="1">3292</font></a>
 And I ask not this, that thou take them from the world, but that thou keep them from the [34, 35] evil one.   <a name="fr_3292"></a><a href="#fn_3292"><font size="1">3293</font></a>
 They were not of the world, as I was not of the world.   <a name="fr_3293"></a><a href="#fn_3293"><font size="1">3294</font></a>
 O Father, sanctify [36] them in thy truth:  for thy word is truth.   <a name="fr_3294"></a><a href="#fn_3294"><font size="1">3295</font></a>
 And as thou didst send me into the world, I [37] [Arabic, p. 180] also send them into the world.   <a name="fr_3295"></a><a href="#fn_3295"><font size="1">3296</font></a>
 And for their sake I sanctify myself, that they [38] also may be sanctified in the truth.   <a name="fr_3296"></a><a href="#fn_3296"><font size="1">3297</font></a>
 Neither for these alone do I ask, but for [39] the sake of them that believe in me through their word;  <a name="fr_3297"></a><a href="#fn_3297"><font size="1">3298</font></a>
 that they may be all one; <a name="page_117"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 117</font></a> <a name="iv.iii.xlvii-Page_117"></a>as thou art in me, and I in thee, and so they also shall be one in us:  that the world [40] may believe that thou didst send me.   <a name="fr_3298"></a><a href="#fn_3298"><font size="1">3299</font></a>
 And the glory which thou hast given unto [41] me I have given unto them;  <a name="fr_3299"></a><a href="#fn_3299"><font size="1">3300</font></a>
 that they may be one, as we are one; I in them, and thou in me, that they may be perfect into <a name="fr_3300"></a><a href="#fn_3300"><font size="1">3301</font></a>
  one; and <i>that</i> the world may know that [42] thou didst send me, and that I <a name="fr_3301"></a><a href="#fn_3301"><font size="1">3302</font></a>
  loved them, as thou lovedst me.   <a name="fr_3302"></a><a href="#fn_3302"><font size="1">3303</font></a>
 Father, and those whom thou hast given me, I wish that, where I am, they may be with me also; that they may behold my glory, which thou hast given me:  for thou lovedst me before [43] the foundation <a name="fr_3303"></a><a href="#fn_3303"><font size="1">3304</font></a>
  of the world.   <a name="fr_3304"></a><a href="#fn_3304"><font size="1">3305</font></a>
 My righteous Father, <a name="fr_3305"></a><a href="#fn_3305"><font size="1">3306</font></a>
  and the world knew thee not, [44] but I know thee; and they knew that thou didst send me; and I made known unto them thy name, and will make it known to them;  <a name="fr_3306"></a><a href="#fn_3306"><font size="1">3307</font></a>
 that the love <i>wherewith</i> thou lovedst me may be in them, and I shall <a name="fr_3307"></a><a href="#fn_3307"><font size="1">3308</font></a>
  be in them.</p>
