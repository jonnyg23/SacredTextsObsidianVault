 <p class="c11"><a name="xxv.iii.iii.xxiii-p3"></a><span class="c10"><a name="xxv.iii.iii.xxiii-p3.1"></a>And</span> whereas what is human comes to an end, what is divine does not. For which reason also when we are dead, and when our nature is tired out, he raises us up, and leads us up [though] born of earth to heaven.</p>
