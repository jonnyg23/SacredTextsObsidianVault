 </p><p class="c41"><a name="xxv.iii.iii.xxi-p1"></a>  <span class="c10"><a name="xxv.iii.iii.xxi-p1.1"></a>Another Fragment</span>.</p>  
 <p class="c11"><a name="xxv.iii.iii.xxi-p2"></a><span class="c10"><a name="xxv.iii.iii.xxi-p2.1"></a>But</span> let us, brethren, who have received the vineyard from the Saviour, and are invited to the heavenly banquet, inasmuch as the Feast is now drawing nigh, take the branches of the palm <a name="fr_4527"></a><a href="#fn_4527"><font size="1">4528</font></a>
  trees, and proving conquerors of sin, let us too like those, who on that occasion went to meet the Saviour, make ourselves ready by our conduct, both to meet Him when He comes, and to go in with Him and partake of the immortal food, and from thenceforth live eternally in the heavens.</p>
