 <p class="c19"><a name="v.iv.v.xlvi-p4"></a>Easy enough, by the LXX. See <a href="../../../bib/kjv/isa063.htm#003">Isaiah lxiii. 3</a><a name="v.iv.v.xlvi-p4.1"></a>. <span class="Greek"><a name="v.iv.v.xlvi-p4.2"></a>καὶ τῶν εθνῶν οὐκ ἔστιν ἀνὴρ μετ᾽ εμοῦ</span>.
