 <p> <a name="v.vi.xxxix"></a>
 </p><p class="c30"><a name="v.vi.xxxix-p1"></a> <span class="c1"><a name="v.vi.xxxix-p1.1"></a>Chapter XXXIX.—Their Diversity of Sentiment Affects the Very Central Doctrine of Christianity, Even the Person and Character of the Lord Jesus. This Diversity Vitiates Every Gnostic School.</span></p> 
 <p class="c19"><a name="v.vi.xxxix-p2"></a>Now, concerning even the Lord Jesus, into how great a diversity of opinion are they divided! One party form Him of the blossoms of all the Æons. <a name="fr_6931"></a><a href="#fn_6931"><font size="1">6932</font></a>
  Another party will have it that He is made up only of those ten whom <i>the Word</i> and <i>the Life</i> <a name="fr_6932"></a><a href="#fn_6932"><font size="1">6933</font></a>
  produced; <a name="fr_6933"></a><a href="#fn_6933"><font size="1">6934</font></a>
  from which circumstance the titles of the Word and the Life were suitably transferred to Him. Others, again, that He rather sprang from the twelve, the offspring of <i>Man</i> and <i>the Church</i>, <a name="fr_6934"></a><a href="#fn_6934"><font size="1">6935</font></a>
  and therefore, they say, He was designated “Son of man.” Others, moreover, maintain that He was formed by <i>Christ</i> and <i>the Holy Spirit</i>, who have to provide for the establishment of the universe, <a name="fr_6935"></a><a href="#fn_6935"><font size="1">6936</font></a>
  and that He inherits by right His Fathers appellation. Some there are who have imagined that another origin must be found for the title “Son of man;” for they have had the presumption to call the Father Himself <i>Man</i>, by reason of the profound mystery of this title: so that what can you hope for more ample concerning faith in that God, with whom you are now yourself on a par? Such conceits are constantly cropping out <a name="fr_6936"></a><a href="#fn_6936"><font size="1">6937</font></a>
  amongst them, from the redundance of their mothers seed. <a name="fr_6937"></a><a href="#fn_6937"><font size="1">6938</font></a>
  And so it happens that the doctrines which have grown up amongst the Valentinians have already extended their rank growth to the woods of the Gnostics.</p>
