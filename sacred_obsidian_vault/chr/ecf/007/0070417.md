 <p> <a name="viii.iii.ix"></a>  </p><h4 align="center"><a name="viii.iii.ix-p0.1"></a>Chapter IX. <a name="fr_2453"></a><a href="#fn_2453"><font size="1">2454</font></a>
 —The Thanksgiving (Eucharist).</h4>  
 <p class="BodyText"><a name="viii.iii.ix-p2"></a>1. Now concerning the Thanksgiving (Eucharist), thus give thanks. 2. First, concerning the <a name="page_380"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 380</font></a> <a name="viii.iii.ix-Page_380"></a>cup: <a name="fr_2454"></a><a href="#fn_2454"><font size="1">2455</font></a>
  We thank thee, our Father, for the holy vine of <a name="viii.iii.ix-p3.3"></a>David Thy servant, <a name="fr_2455"></a><a href="#fn_2455"><font size="1">2456</font></a>
  which Thou madest known to us through <a name="viii.iii.ix-p4.4"></a>Jesus Thy Servant; to Thee be the glory for ever. 3. And concerning the broken <span><a name="viii.iii.ix-p4.5"></a>bread</span>: <a name="fr_2456"></a><a href="#fn_2456"><font size="1">2457</font></a>
  We thank Thee, our Father, for the life and knowledge which Thou madest known to us through <a name="viii.iii.ix-p5.3"></a>Jesus Thy Servant; to Thee be the glory for ever. 4. Even as this broken <span><a name="viii.iii.ix-p5.4"></a>bread</span> was scattered over the hills, <a name="fr_2457"></a><a href="#fn_2457"><font size="1">2458</font></a>
  and was gathered together and became one, so let Thy Church be gathered together from the ends of the earth into Thy kingdom; <a name="fr_2458"></a><a href="#fn_2458"><font size="1">2459</font></a>
  for Thine is the glory and the power through <a name="viii.iii.ix-p7.2"></a>Jesus Christ for ever. 5. But let no one eat or drink of your Thanksgiving (Eucharist), but they who have been baptized into the name of the Lord; for concerning this also the Lord hath said, Give not that which is holy to the dogs. <a name="fr_2459"></a><a href="#fn_2459"><font size="1">2460</font></a>
    </p>
