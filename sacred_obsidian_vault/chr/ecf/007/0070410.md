 <p> <a name="viii.iii.ii"></a>  </p><h4 align="center"><a name="viii.iii.ii-p0.1"></a>Chapter II. <a name="fr_2388"></a><a href="#fn_2388"><font size="1">2389</font></a>
 —The Second Commandment: Gross Sin Forbidden.</h4>  
 <p class="BodyText"><a name="viii.iii.ii-p2"></a>1. And the second commandment of the Teaching; 2. Thou shalt not commit murder, thou shalt not commit adultery, <a name="fr_2389"></a><a href="#fn_2389"><font size="1">2390</font></a>
  thou shalt not commit pæderasty, <a name="fr_2390"></a><a href="#fn_2390"><font size="1">2391</font></a>
  thou shalt not commit fornication, thou shalt not steal, <a name="fr_2391"></a><a href="#fn_2391"><font size="1">2392</font></a>
  thou shalt not practice magic, thou shalt not practice witchcraft, thou shalt not murder a child by abortion nor kill that which is begotten. <a name="fr_2392"></a><a href="#fn_2392"><font size="1">2393</font></a>
  Thou shalt not covet the things of thy neighbour, <a name="fr_2393"></a><a href="#fn_2393"><font size="1">2394</font></a>
  3. thou shalt not forswear thyself, <a name="fr_2394"></a><a href="#fn_2394"><font size="1">2395</font></a>
  thou shalt not bear false witness, <a name="fr_2395"></a><a href="#fn_2395"><font size="1">2396</font></a>
  thou shalt not speak evil, thou shalt bear no grudge. <a name="fr_2396"></a><a href="#fn_2396"><font size="1">2397</font></a>
  4. Thou shalt not be double-minded nor double-tongued; for to be double-tongued is a <a name="page_378"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 378</font></a> <a name="viii.iii.ii-Page_378"></a> snare of death. <a name="fr_2397"></a><a href="#fn_2397"><font size="1">2398</font></a>
  5. Thy speech shall not be false, nor empty, but fulfilled by deed. <a name="fr_2398"></a><a href="#fn_2398"><font size="1">2399</font></a>
  6. Thou shalt not be covetous, nor rapacious, nor a hypocrite, nor evil disposed, nor haughty. Thou shalt not take evil counsel against thy neighbour. <a name="fr_2399"></a><a href="#fn_2399"><font size="1">2400</font></a>
  7. Thou shalt not hate any man; but some thou shalt reprove, <a name="fr_2400"></a><a href="#fn_2400"><font size="1">2401</font></a>
  and concerning some thou shalt pray, and some thou shalt love more than thy own life. <a name="fr_2401"></a><a href="#fn_2401"><font size="1">2402</font></a>
    </p>
