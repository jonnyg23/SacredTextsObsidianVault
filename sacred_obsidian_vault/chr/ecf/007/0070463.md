 <p> <a name="ix.viii.iv"></a>  </p><h4 align="center"><a name="ix.viii.iv-p0.1"></a>Sec. IV.—Enumeration Ordained by Apostles.</h4>  <h5 align="center"><a name="ix.viii.iv-p0.2"></a>Who Were They that the Holy Apostles Sent and Ordained? </h5>  
 <p class="BodyText"><a name="ix.viii.iv-p1"></a><span class="sc"><a name="ix.viii.iv-p1.1"></a>XLVI</span>. Now concerning those bishops which have been ordained in our lifetime, we let you know that they are these:—<a name="ix.viii.iv-p1.2"></a>James the bishop of Jerusalem, the brother of our Lord; <a name="fr_3534"></a><a href="#fn_3534"><font size="1">3535</font></a>
  upon whose death the second was <a name="ix.viii.iv-p2.4"></a>Simeon the son of <a name="ix.viii.iv-p2.5"></a>Cleopas; after whom the third was <a name="ix.viii.iv-p2.6"></a>Judas the son of <a name="ix.viii.iv-p2.7"></a>James. Of Cæsarea of Palestine, the first was <a name="page_478"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 478</font></a> <a name="ix.viii.iv-Page_478"></a><a name="ix.viii.iv-p2.8"></a>Zacchæus, who was once a publican; after whom was <a name="ix.viii.iv-p2.9"></a>Cornelius, and the third <a name="ix.viii.iv-p2.10"></a>Theophilus. Of Antioch, <a name="ix.viii.iv-p2.11"></a>Euodius, ordained by me <a name="ix.viii.iv-p2.12"></a>Peter; and <a name="ix.viii.iv-p2.13"></a>Ignatius by <a name="ix.viii.iv-p2.14"></a>Paul. Of Alexandria, <a name="ix.viii.iv-p2.15"></a>Annianus was the first, ordained by <a name="ix.viii.iv-p2.16"></a>Mark the evangelist; the second <a name="ix.viii.iv-p2.17"></a>Avilius by <a name="ix.viii.iv-p2.18"></a>Luke, who was also an evangelist. Of the church of Rome, <a name="ix.viii.iv-p2.19"></a>Linus the son of <a name="ix.viii.iv-p2.20"></a>Claudia was the first, ordained by <a name="ix.viii.iv-p2.21"></a>Paul; <a name="fr_3535"></a><a href="#fn_3535"><font size="1">3536</font></a>
  and <a name="ix.viii.iv-p3.2"></a>Clemens, after <a name="ix.viii.iv-p3.3"></a>Linus death, the second, ordained by me <a name="ix.viii.iv-p3.4"></a>Peter. <a name="fr_3536"></a><a href="#fn_3536"><font size="1">3537</font></a>
  Of Ephesus, <a name="ix.viii.iv-p4.2"></a>Timotheus, ordained by <a name="ix.viii.iv-p4.3"></a>Paul; and <a name="ix.viii.iv-p4.4"></a>John, by me <a name="ix.viii.iv-p4.5"></a>John. Of Smyrna, <a name="ix.viii.iv-p4.6"></a>Aristo the first; after whom <a name="ix.viii.iv-p4.7"></a>Stratæas the son of <a name="ix.viii.iv-p4.8"></a>Lois; <a name="fr_3537"></a><a href="#fn_3537"><font size="1">3538</font></a>
  and the third <a name="ix.viii.iv-p5.2"></a>Aristo. Of Pergamus, <a name="ix.viii.iv-p5.3"></a>Gaius. Of Philadelphia, <a name="ix.viii.iv-p5.4"></a>Demetrius, by me. Of Cenchrea, <a name="ix.viii.iv-p5.5"></a>Lucius, by <a name="ix.viii.iv-p5.6"></a>Paul. Of Crete, <a name="ix.viii.iv-p5.7"></a>Titus. Of Athens, <a name="ix.viii.iv-p5.8"></a>Dionysius. Of Tripoli in Phœnicia, <a name="ix.viii.iv-p5.9"></a>Marathones. Of Laodicea in Phrygia, <a name="ix.viii.iv-p5.10"></a>Archippus. <a name="fr_3538"></a><a href="#fn_3538"><font size="1">3539</font></a>
  Of Colossæ, <a name="ix.viii.iv-p6.2"></a>Philemon. <a name="fr_3539"></a><a href="#fn_3539"><font size="1">3540</font></a>
  Of Borea in Macedonia, <a name="ix.viii.iv-p7.2"></a>Onesimus, once the servant of <a name="ix.viii.iv-p7.3"></a>Philemon. <a name="fr_3540"></a><a href="#fn_3540"><font size="1">3541</font></a>
  Of the churches of Galatia, <a name="ix.viii.iv-p8.2"></a>Crescens. <a name="fr_3541"></a><a href="#fn_3541"><font size="1">3542</font></a>
  Of the parishes of Asia, <a name="ix.viii.iv-p9.2"></a>Aquila and <a name="ix.viii.iv-p9.3"></a>Nicetas. Of the church of Æginæ, <a name="ix.viii.iv-p9.4"></a>Crispus. These are the bishops who are entrusted by us with the parishes in the Lord; whose doctrine keep ye always in mind, and observe our words. And may the Lord be with you now, and to endless ages, as Himself said to us when He was about to be taken up to His own God and Father. For says He, “Lo, I am with you all the days, until the end of the world. Amen.” <a name="fr_3542"></a><a href="#fn_3542"><font size="1">3543</font></a>
   </p>
