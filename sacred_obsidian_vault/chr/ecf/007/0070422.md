 <p> <a name="viii.iii.xiv"></a>  </p><h4 align="center"><a name="viii.iii.xiv-p0.1"></a>Chapter XIV. <a name="fr_2489"></a><a href="#fn_2489"><font size="1">2490</font></a>
 —Christian Assembly on the Lords Day.</h4>  
 <p class="BodyText"><a name="viii.iii.xiv-p2"></a>1. But every Lords day <a name="fr_2490"></a><a href="#fn_2490"><font size="1">2491</font></a>
  do ye gather yourselves together, and break bread, and give thanksgiving after having confessed your transgressions, <a name="fr_2491"></a><a href="#fn_2491"><font size="1">2492</font></a>
  that your sacrifice may be pure. <a name="fr_2492"></a><a href="#fn_2492"><font size="1">2493</font></a>
  2. But let no one that is at variance <a name="fr_2493"></a><a href="#fn_2493"><font size="1">2494</font></a>
  with his fellow come together with you, until they be reconciled, that your sacrifice may not be profaned. 3. For this is that which was spoken by the Lord: In every place and time offer to me a pure sacrifice; <a name="fr_2494"></a><a href="#fn_2494"><font size="1">2495</font></a>
  for I am a great King, saith the Lord, and my name is wonderful among the nations. <a name="fr_2495"></a><a href="#fn_2495"><font size="1">2496</font></a>
    </p>
