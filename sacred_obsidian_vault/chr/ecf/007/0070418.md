 <p> <a name="viii.iii.x"></a>  </p><h4 align="center"><a name="viii.iii.x-p0.1"></a>Chapter X. <a name="fr_2460"></a><a href="#fn_2460"><font size="1">2461</font></a>
 —Prayer After Communion.</h4>  
 <p class="BodyText"><a name="viii.iii.x-p2"></a>1. But after ye are filled, <a name="fr_2461"></a><a href="#fn_2461"><font size="1">2462</font></a>
  thus give thanks: 2. We thank Thee, holy Father, for Thy holy name which Thou didst cause to tabernacle in our hearts, and for the knowledge and faith and immortality, which Thou madest known to us through <a name="viii.iii.x-p3.8"></a>Jesus Thy Servant; to Thee be the glory for ever. 3. Thou, Master almighty, didst create all things for Thy names sake; Thou gavest food and drink to men for enjoyment, that they might give thanks to Thee; but to us Thou didst freely give spiritual food and drink and life eternal through Thy Servant. <a name="fr_2462"></a><a href="#fn_2462"><font size="1">2463</font></a>
  4. Before all things we thank Thee that Thou art mighty; to Thee be the glory for ever. 5. Remember, Lord, Thy Church, to deliver it from all evil and to make it perfect in Thy love, and gather it from the four winds, sanctified for Thy kingdom which Thou hast prepared for it; <a name="fr_2463"></a><a href="#fn_2463"><font size="1">2464</font></a>
  for Thine is the power and the glory for ever. 6. Let grace come, and let this world pass away. <a name="fr_2464"></a><a href="#fn_2464"><font size="1">2465</font></a>
  Hosanna to the God (Son) <a name="fr_2465"></a><a href="#fn_2465"><font size="1">2466</font></a>
  of <a name="viii.iii.x-p7.5"></a>David! If any one is holy, let him come; if any one is not so, let him repent. <a name="fr_2466"></a><a href="#fn_2466"><font size="1">2467</font></a>
  Maran atha. <a name="fr_2467"></a><a href="#fn_2467"><font size="1">2468</font></a>
  Amen. 7. But permit the prophets to make Thanksgiving as much as they desire. <a name="fr_2468"></a><a href="#fn_2468"><font size="1">2469</font></a>
    </p>
