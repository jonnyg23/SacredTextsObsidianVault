 <p> <a name="viii.iii.viii"></a>  </p><h4 align="center"><a name="viii.iii.viii-p0.1"></a>Chapter VIII. <a name="fr_2446"></a><a href="#fn_2446"><font size="1">2447</font></a>
 —Concerning Fasting and Prayer (the Lords Prayer).</h4>  
 <p class="BodyText"><a name="viii.iii.viii-p2"></a>1. But let not your fasts be with the hypocrites; <a name="fr_2447"></a><a href="#fn_2447"><font size="1">2448</font></a>
  for they fast on the second and fifth day of the week; but do ye fast on the fourth <span><a name="viii.iii.viii-p3.2"></a>day</span> and the Preparation (Friday). <a name="fr_2448"></a><a href="#fn_2448"><font size="1">2449</font></a>
  2. Neither pray as the hypocrites; but as the Lord commanded in His Gospel, <a name="fr_2449"></a><a href="#fn_2449"><font size="1">2450</font></a>
  thus pray: Our Father who art in heaven, hallowed be Thy name. Thy kingdom come. Thy will be done, as in heaven, <span><a name="viii.iii.viii-p5.3"></a>so</span> on earth. Give us to-day our daily (needful) bread, <a name="fr_2450"></a><a href="#fn_2450"><font size="1">2451</font></a>
  and forgive us our debt as we also forgive our debtors. And bring us not into temptation, but deliver us from the evil <span><a name="viii.iii.viii-p6.3"></a>one</span> (or, evil); for Thine is the power and the glory for ever. <a name="fr_2451"></a><a href="#fn_2451"><font size="1">2452</font></a>
  3. Thrice in the day thus pray. <a name="fr_2452"></a><a href="#fn_2452"><font size="1">2453</font></a>
    </p>
