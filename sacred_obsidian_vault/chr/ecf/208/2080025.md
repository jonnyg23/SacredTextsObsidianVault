 </p><p class="c5"><a name="vi.ii.viii-p1"></a>  <span class="c1"><a name="vi.ii.viii-p1.1"></a>VII.—Writings Spurious and Dubious.</span></p>  
 <p class="c20"><a name="vi.ii.viii-p2"></a>Under this head will be ranked besides writings objections against which have been already noticed:</p>  
 <p class="c42"><a name="vi.ii.viii-p3"></a>1.  Constitutiones monasticæ (<span class="Greek"><a name="vi.ii.viii-p3.1"></a>᾽</span><span class="Greek"><a name="vi.ii.viii-p3.2"></a>Α</span><span class="Greek"><a name="vi.ii.viii-p3.3"></a>σκητικαὶ διατάξεις</span>), in number thirty-four.</p>  
 <p class="c42"><a name="vi.ii.viii-p4"></a>2.  Pœnæ in monachos delinquentes, and Pœnæ in Canonicas (<span class="Greek"><a name="vi.ii.viii-p4.1"></a>ἐ</span><span class="Greek"><a name="vi.ii.viii-p4.2"></a>πιτίμια</span>).</p>  
 <p class="c42"><a name="vi.ii.viii-p5"></a>3.  Libri duo de Baptismo.</p>  
 <p class="c42"><a name="vi.ii.viii-p6"></a>4.  Sermones duo ascetici.</p>  
 <p class="c43"><a name="vi.ii.viii-p7"></a>5.  Various Homilies:</p>  
 <p class="c44"><a name="vi.ii.viii-p8"></a>a.  Adversus Calumniatores SS. Trinitatis,</p>  
 <p class="c44"><a name="vi.ii.viii-p9"></a>b.  Altera de Sp. Scto.,</p>  
 <p class="c44"><a name="vi.ii.viii-p10"></a>c.  In Sanctam Christi Generationem,</p>  
 <p class="c44"><a name="vi.ii.viii-p11"></a>d.  De Libero Arbitrio,</p>  
 <p class="c44"><a name="vi.ii.viii-p12"></a>e.  In aliquot Scripturæ locis, dicta in Lacizis.</p>  
 <p class="c44"><a name="vi.ii.viii-p13"></a>f.  III.  De Jejunio.</p>  
 <p class="c45"><a name="vi.ii.viii-p14"></a>g.  De Pœnitentia.</p>  
 <p class="c42"><a name="vi.ii.viii-p15"></a>6.  A book On True Virginity.</p>  
 <p class="c42"><a name="vi.ii.viii-p16"></a>7.  A treatise On consolation in adversity.</p>  
 <p class="c42"><a name="vi.ii.viii-p17"></a><a name="page_lxxiii"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. lxxiii</font></a> <a name="vi.ii.viii-Page_lxxiii"></a>8.  A treatise De laude solitariæ vitæ.</p>  
 <p class="c42"><a name="vi.ii.viii-p18"></a>9.  Admonitio ad filum spiritualem (extant only in Latin).</p>  
 <p class="c42"><a name="vi.ii.viii-p19"></a>10.  Sermones de moribus XXIV. (<span class="Greek"><a name="vi.ii.viii-p19.1"></a>ἠ</span><span class="Greek"><a name="vi.ii.viii-p19.2"></a>θικοὶ λόγοι</span>), a cento of extracts made by Simeon Metaphrastes.</p>
