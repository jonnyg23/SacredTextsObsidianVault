 </p><p class="c26"><a name="ix.cccxlvii-p1"></a>  <span class="c18"><a name="ix.cccxlvii-p1.1"></a>Letter CCCLII.</span></p>  
 <p class="c54"><a name="ix.cccxlvii-p2"></a>Libanius to Basil.</p>  
 <p class="c20"><a name="ix.cccxlvii-p3"></a><span class="c14"><a name="ix.cccxlvii-p3.1"></a>Behold</span>!  I have sent you my speech, all streaming with sweat as I am!  How should I be otherwise, when sending my speech to one who by his skill in oratory is able to shew that the wisdom of Plato and the ability of Demosthenes were belauded in vain?  I feel like a gnat compared with an elephant.  How I shiver and shake, as I reckon up the day when you will inspect my performance I am almost out of my wits!</p>
