 </p><p class="c26"><a name="ix.xiii-p1"></a>  <span class="c18"><a name="ix.xiii-p1.1"></a>Letter XII. <a name="fr_1887"></a><a href="#fn_1887"><font size="1">1888</font></a>

 <p class="c55"><a name="ix.xiii-p3"></a><i>To Olympius</i>. <a name="fr_1888"></a><a href="#fn_1888"><font size="1">1889</font></a>
 </p>  
 <p class="c20"><a name="ix.xiii-p5"></a><span class="c14"><a name="ix.xiii-p5.1"></a>Before</span> you did write me a few words:  now not even a few.  Your brevity will soon become silence.  Return to your old ways, and do not let me have to scold you for your laconic behaviour.  But I shall be glad even of a little letter in token of your great love.  Only write to me.</p>
