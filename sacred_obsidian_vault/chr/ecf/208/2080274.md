 </span></p>  
 <p class="c55"><a name="ix.ccii-p3"></a><i>To Amphilochius, bishop of Iconium</i>.</p>  
 <p class="c20"><a name="ix.ccii-p4"></a>I <span class="c14"><a name="ix.ccii-p4.1"></a>long</span> to meet you for many reasons, that I may have the benefit of your advice in the matters I had in hand, and that on beholding you after a long interval I may have some comfort for your absence.  But since both of us are prevented by the same reasons, you by the illness which has befallen you, and I by the malady of longer standing which has not yet left me, let us, if you will, each forgive the other, that both may free ourselves from blame.</p>
