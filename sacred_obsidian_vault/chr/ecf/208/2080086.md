 <p class="c55"><a name="ix.xiv-p3"></a><i>To Olympius</i>.</p>  
 <p class="c20"><a name="ix.xiv-p4"></a><span class="c14"><a name="ix.xiv-p4.1"></a>As</span> all the fruits of the season come to us in their proper time, flowers in spring, corn in summer, and apples <a name="fr_1890"></a><a href="#fn_1890"><font size="1">1891</font></a>
  in autumn, so the fruit for winter is talk.</p>
