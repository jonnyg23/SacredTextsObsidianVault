 <p class="c55"><a name="ix.cxix-p3"></a><i>To Jovinus, Bishop of Perrha</i>. <a name="fr_2393"></a><a href="#fn_2393"><font size="1">2394</font></a>
 </p>  
 <p class="c20"><a name="ix.cxix-p5"></a><span class="c14"><a name="ix.cxix-p5.1"></a>You</span> owe me a good turn.  For I lent you a kindness, which I ought to get back with interest;—a kind of interest, this, which our Lord does not refuse.  Pay me, then, my friend, by paying me a visit.  So much for the capital; what of the increment?  It is the fact of the visit being paid by you, who are a man as much superior to me, as fathers are better than children.</p>
