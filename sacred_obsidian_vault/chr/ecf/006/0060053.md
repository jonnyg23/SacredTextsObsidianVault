    <h2 align="CENTER"><a></a> Note.</h2>  
 <p>The valuable works of Africanus are found in vol. ix. of the Edinburgh edition, mixed up with the spurious Decretals  and remnants of preceding volumes. I am unable to make out very clearly who is the translator, but infer that Drs. Roberts and Donaldson should be credited with this work.</p>
