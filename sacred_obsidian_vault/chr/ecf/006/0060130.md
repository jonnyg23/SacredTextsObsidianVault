    <h2 align="CENTER"><a></a> General Note.</h2>  
 <p> (Vexillas,-as they are called, p. 399.)</p>  
 <p>IT is very curious to note how certain ideas are inherited from the earliest Fathers, and travel down, as here, to find a new expression in a distant age. Here our author reflects Justin Martyr,<sup><a href="footnote/fn63.htm#P6311_1930437">16</a></sup> and the <i>Labarum</i><sup><a href="footnote/fn63.htm#P6312_1930491">17</a></sup> itself is the outcrop of what Justin wrote to Antoninus Pius. </p>
