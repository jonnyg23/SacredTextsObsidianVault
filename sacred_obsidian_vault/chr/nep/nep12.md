 <font size="4"><p align="CENTER">UNCERTAINTY OF LIFE</p></font>.<dir>
 <dir>
 
 <p>I <font size="2">IN</font> the burying place may see<br>
   Graves shorter there than I;<br>
 From death's arrest no age is free<br>
   Young children, too, may die.</p>
 <p>My God, may such an awful sight<br>
   Awakening be to me!<br>
 O that by early grace I might<br>
   For death prepared be!</p></dir>
 </dir>
