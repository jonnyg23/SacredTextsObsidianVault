 <p><a name="page_15"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 15</font></a></p>
 <font size="4"><p align="CENTER">AN ALPHABET OF LESSONS FOR CHILDREN</p></font>.
 <font size="5"><p>A</p></font> <font size="4">WISE</font> son makes a glad father, but a foolish son is the heaviness of his mother.
 <font size="5"><p>B</p></font><font size="4">ETTER</font> is a little, with the fear of the Lord, than great treasure, and trouble therewith.
 <font size="5"><p>C</p></font><font size="4">OME</font> unto Christ, all ye who labor and are heavy hiden, and he will give you rest.
 <font size="5"><p>D</p></font><font size="4">o</font> not the abominable thing which I hate, saith the Lord.
 <font size="5"><p>E</p></font><font size="4">XCEPT</font> a man be born again, he cannot see the kingdom of God.
 <font size="5"><p>F</p></font><font size="4">OOLISHNESS</font> is bound up in the heart of a child, but the rod of correction will drive it from him.
 <font size="5"><p>G</p></font><font size="4">RIEVE</font> not the Holy Spirit, lest it depart from thee.
 <font size="5"><p>I</p></font><font size="4">T</font> is good for me to draw near unto God.
 <font size="5"><p>K</p></font><font size="4">EEP</font> thy heart with all diligence, for out of it are the issues of life.
 <font size="5"><p>L</p></font><font size="4">IARS</font> will have their part in the lake which burns with fire and brinistone
 <p><a name="page_16"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 16</font></a></p>
 <font size="5"><p>M</p></font><font size="4">ANY</font> are the afflictions of the righteous but the Lord delivers them out of them all.
 <font size="5"><p>N</p></font><font size="4">OW</font> is the accepted time; now is the day of salvation.
 <font size="5"><p>O</p></font><font size="4">UT</font> of the abundance of the heart thy mouth speaketh.
 <font size="5"><p>P</p></font><font size="4">RAY</font> to thy Father who is in secret, and, thy Father who sees in secret will reward thee openly.
 <font size="5"><p>Q</p></font><font size="4">UIT</font> you like men; be strong; stand fast in the faith.
 <font size="5"><p>R</p></font><font size="4">EMEMBER</font> thy Creator in the days of thy youth.
 <font size="5"><p>S</p></font><font size="4">ALVATION</font> belongeth unto the Lord.
 <font size="5"><p>T</p></font><font size="4">RUST</font> in God at all times, ye people; pour out your hearts before him.
 <font size="5"><p>U</p></font><font size="4">PON</font> the wicked God will rain a horrible tempest.
 <font size="5"><p>W</p></font><font size="4">OE</font> to the wicked; it will be ill with him, for the reward of his hand will be given<br>
 him.
 <font size="4"><p>E</p></font><font size="5">X</font><font size="4">HORT</font> one another daily while it is called to-day, lest any of you be hardened through the deceitfulness of sin.
 <font size="5"><p>Y</p></font><font size="4">OUNG</font> men, you have overcome the wicked one.
 <p><a name="page_17"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 17</font></a></p>
 <font size="5"><p>Z</p></font><font size="4">EAL</font> hath consumed me, because my enemies have forgotten the word of God.
