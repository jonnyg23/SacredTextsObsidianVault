 <font size="4"><p align="CENTER">EVENING PRAYER FOR A CHILD</p><dir>
 <dir>
 
 </dir></dir></font><p>Now I lay me down to sleep;<br>
 I pray the Lord my soul to keep;<br>
 If I should die before I wake,<br>
 I pray the Lord my soul to take.</p>
