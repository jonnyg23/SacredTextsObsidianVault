 <font size="4"><p align="CENTER">OUR SAVIOR'S GOLDEN RULE</p><dir>
 <dir>
 
 </dir></dir></font><p>BE you to others kind and true,<br>
 As you'd have others be to you,<br>
 And neither do nor say to men<br>
 Whate'er you would not take again</p>
