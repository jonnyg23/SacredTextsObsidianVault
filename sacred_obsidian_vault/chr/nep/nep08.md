 <font size="4"><p align="CENTER">MORNING PRAYER FOR A CHILD</p></font>.<dir>
 <dir>
 
 <p>Now I wake and see the light:<br>
 'Tis God who kept me through the night.<br>
 To him I lift my voice, and pray<br>
 That he would keep me through the day.<br>
 If I shouId die before 'tis done,<br>
 O God, accept me through thy Son.</p></dir>
