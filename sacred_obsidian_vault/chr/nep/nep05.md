 <font size="4"><p align="CENTER">THE TEN COMMANDMENTS</p></font>,
 <font size="2"><p align="CENTER">PUT INTO SHORT AND EASY RHYMES FOR CHILDREN</p></font>.
 <p>1. THOU shalt have no more gods but me.<br>
 2. Before no idol bend thy knee.<br>
 3. Take not the name of God in vain.<br>
 4. Dare not the Sabbath day profane,<br>
 5. Give both thy parents honor due.<br>
 6. Take heed that thou no murder do.<br>
 7. Abstain from words and deeds unclean.<br>
 8. Steal not, though thou be poor and mean.<br>
 9. Make not a wilful lie, nor love it.<br>
 10. What is thy neignbor's dare not covet.</p>
