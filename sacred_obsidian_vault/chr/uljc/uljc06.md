 <p><a name="page_155"><font size="1" color="green">p. 155</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">THE LIFE OF SAINT ISSA.</h3>  <h5 align="center">"BEST OF THE SONS OF MEN."</h5>  <h3 align="center">I.</h3>  
 <p>1. The earth trembled and the heavens wept, because of the great crime committed in the land of Israel.</p>  
 <p>2. For there was tortured and murdered the great and just Issa, in whom was manifest the soul of the Universe;</p>  
 <p>3. Which had incarnated in a simple mortal, to benefit men and destroy the evil spirit in them;</p>  
 <p>4. To lead back to peace, love and happiness, man, degraded by his sins, and recall him to the one and indivisible Creator whose mercy is infinite.</p>
