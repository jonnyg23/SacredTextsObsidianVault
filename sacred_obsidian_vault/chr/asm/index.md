 <p>
 Jan van Ruysbroeck [b. 1293 or 1294, d. 1381] was a Flemish mystic.
 He led a life of asuterity, eventually founding a congregation
 in Groenendaal.
 His writings were widely circulated in his lifetime and
 he influenced an entire generation of Christian mystics.
 He was beatified on Decmber 1st, 1908, by Pope St. Pius X.
