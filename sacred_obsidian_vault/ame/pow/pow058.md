 <p align="CENTER">WORDS TO BE SPOKEN WHILE MAKING DIVINATORY WANDS.</p> 
 <p>In making divinatory wands, they must be broken as before directed, and while breaking and before using them, the following words must be spoken:</p><dir> <dir>  
 <p>Divining rod, do thou keep that power,<br> Which God gave unto thee at the very first hour.</p></dir> </dir>
