 <p align="CENTER">A GOOD REMEDY FOR THE TOOTHACHE.</p> 
 <p>Stir the sore tooth with a needle until it draws blood; then take a thread and soak it with this blood. Then take vinegar and flour, mix them well so as to form a paste and spread it on a rag, then wrap this rag around the root of an apple-tree, and tie it very close with the above thread, after which the root must be well covered with ground.</p>
