 <p align="CENTER">A WELL-TRIED PLASTER TO REMOVE MORTIFICATION</p> 
 <p>Take six hen's eggs and boil them in hot ashes until they are right hard; then take the yellow of the eggs and fry them in a gill of lard until they are quite black; then put a handful of rue with it, and afterward filter it through a cloth. When this is done add a gill of sweet</p> 
 <p><a name="page_33"><font size="1" color="green">p. 33</font></a></p> 
 <p>oil to it. It will take most effect where the plaster for a female is prepared by a male, and the plaster for a male prepared by a female.</p>
