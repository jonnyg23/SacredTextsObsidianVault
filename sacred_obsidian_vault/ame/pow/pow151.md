 <p align="CENTER">A RECIPE FOR DESTROYING SPRING-TAILS OR GROUND-FLEAS.</p> 
 <p>Take the chaff upon which children have been lying in their cradles, or take the dung of horses, and put that upon the field, and the spring-tails or ground-flees will no longer do you any injury.</p>
