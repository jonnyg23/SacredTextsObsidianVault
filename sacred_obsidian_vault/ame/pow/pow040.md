 <p align="CENTER">A REMEDY TO RELIEVE PAIN.</p> 
 <p>Take a rag which was tied over a wound for the first time. and put it in water together with some copperas;</p> 
 <p><a name="page_19"><font size="1" color="green">p. 19</font></a></p> 
 <p>but do not venture to stir the copperas until you are certain of the pain having left you.</p>
