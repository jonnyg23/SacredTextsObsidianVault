 <p align="CENTER">TO MAKE CHICKENS LAY MANY EGGS.</p> 
 <p>Take the dung of rabbits, pound it to powder, mix it with bran, wet the mixture till it forms lumps, and feed your chickens with it, and they will keep on laying a great many eggs.</p>
