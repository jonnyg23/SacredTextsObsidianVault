 <p align="CENTER">FOR THE HOLLOW HORN IN COWS.</p> 
 <p>Bore a small hole in the hollow horn, milk the same cow, and squirt her milk into the horn; this is the best cure. Use a syringe to squirt the milk into the horn.</p>
