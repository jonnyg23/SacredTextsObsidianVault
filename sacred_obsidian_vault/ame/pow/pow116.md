 <p align="CENTER">ANOTHER METHOD OF STOPPING FIRE.</p> 
 <p>Our dear Sarah journeyed through the land, having a fiery hot brand in her hand. The fiery brand heats; the fiery brand sweats. Fiery brand, stop your beat; fiery brand, stop your sweat.</p>
