 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>Pow-Wows, or Long Lost Friend</i>, by John George Hoffman, [1820], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> 
 <p align="CENTER">HOW TO FASTEN OR SPELL-BIND ANYTHING.</p> 
 <p>You say, "Christ's cross and Christ's crown, Christ Jesus' colored blood, be thou every hour good. God, the Father, is before me; God, the Son, is beside me; God, the Holy Ghost, is behind me. Whoever now is stronger than these three persons may come, by day or night, to attack me." + + +</p> 
 <p>Then say the Lord's prayer three times.</p> 
 <p align="CENTER">ANOTHER WAY OF FASTENING OR SPELL-BINDING.</p> 
 <p>After repeating the above, You speak, "At every step may Jesus walk with (<i>name</i>). He is my head; I am his limb; therefore, Jesus, be with (<i>name</i>)."</p>
