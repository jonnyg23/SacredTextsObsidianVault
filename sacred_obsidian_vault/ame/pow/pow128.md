 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>Pow-Wows, or Long Lost Friend</i>, by John George Hoffman, [1820], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> 
 <p align="CENTER">ANOTHER METHOD OF TREATING A SICK COW.</p><dir> <dir>  
 <p>J. The cross of Jesus Christ poured out milk;<br> J. The cross of Jesus Christ poured out water;<br> J. The cross of Jesus Christ has poured them out.</p></dir> </dir>  
 <p>These lines must be written on three pieces of white paper; then take the milk of the sick cow and these three pieces of paper, put them in a pot, and scrape a little of the skull of a criminal; close it well, and put it over a hot fire, and the witch will have to die. If you</p> 
 <p><a name="page_45"><font size="1" color="green">p. 45</font></a></p> 
 <p>take the three pieces of paper, with the writing on them, in your mouth and go out before your house, speak three times, and then give them to your cattle, you shall not only see all the witches, but your cattle will also get well again.</p>
