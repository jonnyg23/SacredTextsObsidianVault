 <p align="CENTER">A GOOD REMEDY FOR THE TOOTHACHE.</p> 
 <p>Cut out a piece of greensward (sod) in the morning before sunrise, quite unbeshrewdly from any place, breathe three times upon it, and put it down upon the same place from which it was taken.</p>
