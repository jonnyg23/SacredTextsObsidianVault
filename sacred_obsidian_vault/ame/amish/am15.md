 <p><b>Satisfaction</b>.—Here we see the contented expression of a successful bidder at a public sale. Possibly the woman in the foreground did the bidding for the one to the left, while the one in the center says: "Oh!" (<a href="#img_01700">Upper right</a>)</p>

 <p><b>All Together</b> . . . Broad-brimmed hats, full-grown whiskers, heavy sweaters, a great-coat or two, and a bonnet. We don't know what she might be saying, but her audience is giving her all possible attention. (<a href="#img_01602">Lower left</a>)</p>
