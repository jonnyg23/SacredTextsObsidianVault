 <h3 align="CENTER">VED.</h3>
 <p>1. SING to the Almighty, ye heirs of Zarathustra, whose flesh was the food of lions.</p>
 <p>2. Sing to the Creator, Zarathustra's Almighty, the Eolin that spake in the wind.</p>
 <p>3. Sing to the followers of the Great Spirit! The Zarathustrians who forfeited their flesh and blood for Him.</p>
 <p>4. Sound loud His name, the E-O-Ih of the Zarathustrians, that sang Him on high!</p>
