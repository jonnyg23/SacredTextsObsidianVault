 <h3 align="CENTER">HARS.</h3>
 <p>1. ALL men know Him; none deny Him, the Creator!</p>
 <p>2. Who quickened into life all the living, out of the Almighty!</p>
 <p>3. Whose knowledge knoweth all, the whence and thence, Jehovih!</p>
 <p>4. Before Whom even Gods cry out: All is in Thee, O Jehovih!</p>
