 <h3 align="CENTER">TAUG.</h3>
 <p>1. WHERE is a God like unto Thee, Jehovih? Whose kaleidoscope is millions of suns!</p>
 <p>2. Whose breath blew upon thee, O earth! And thy voice joined in the songs of the stars!</p>
 <p>3. Whose breath blew upon thee, O Earth! And thy fire was congealed, and the heat run low!</p>
 <p>4. Praise thou the All Person, Whose mighty hand stayed the fire! And cooled the flaming earth.</p>
