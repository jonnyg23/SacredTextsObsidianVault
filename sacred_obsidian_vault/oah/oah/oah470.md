 <h3 align="CENTER">TOO.</h3>
 <p>1. ABOUT what under the sun can not men differ? but Thy Mightiness, Jehovih!</p>
 <p>2. Where in the heavens have the angels found Thy boundaries? or come to the termination?</p>
 <p>3. About whose magnitude and existence can not they differ, but in Thee, Thou Almighty! <a name="page_659"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 659</font></a></p>
 <p>4. Thou alone art the Soul and the Substance, the Only All, the Key Note of Harmony, Jehovih!</p>
