 <h3 align="CENTER">ROE.</h3>
 <p>1. JEHOVIH, first and last: Forever, Jehovih, O my beloved!</p>
 <p>2. Write Him in stone and iron, copper, silver and gold.</p>
 <p>3. Whose Person is the All and Whole: Creator of the boundless universe!</p>
 <p>4. Teach ye Him to the child; magnify the soul of man to see Jehovih!</p>
 <p>5. Welcome, pains and afflictions: Behind all riseth a greater glory.</p>
 <p>6. He knoweth my wanderings; for the feet of the faithful Jehovih provideth a place.</p>
 <p>7. He understandeth beforehand; the Creator cometh upon me in a way I saw not.</p>
 <p>8. None shall stand before the Almighty in the songs and praise of the righteous.</p>
