 <h3 align="CENTER">OTE.</h3>
 <p>1. PROCLAIM Him, O ye Andes and Cordilleras, stretching avast and on high, ye monuments of the Almighty!</p>
 <p>2. Praise His majesty, O ye Himal Layas, and ye Akai Shine, ye foot-prints of Jehovih!</p>
 <p>3. Praise His greatness, O ye Thia Shan, and ye Tapa Ling, that feed the Pacific, the Daughter of Jehovih.</p>
 <p>4. Sing unto Him, O all ye mountains of the earth; proclaim His power, which stood you up by the touch of his hand.</p>
