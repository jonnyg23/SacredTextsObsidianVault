 <h3 align="CENTER">VEN.</h3>
 <p>1. LET us sing to the Maker, Jehovih, O my beloved!</p>
 <p>2. Him Who is Mighty in thought, Jehovih! O my beloved!</p>
 <p>3. He but conceiveth, and, lo, a creation is done, O my beloved!</p>
 <p>4. Jehovih, the Creator, Almighty, O my beloved, sing unto Him, forever!</p>
