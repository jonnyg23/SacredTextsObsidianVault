 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="../tnspin.jpg"></a><br><i>Correspondence</i>, by Benedict de Spinoza, [1883], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">LETTER XLVIII.</h3>  
 <p>Written by a physician, Lambert de Velthuysen, to Isaac Orobio, and forwarded by the latter to Spinoza. It contains a detailed attack on the Tractatus Theologico-Politicus. Its tenor may be sufficiently seen from Spinoza's reply. (Written at Utrecht, January 24th, 1671.) Velthuysen afterwards became more friendly to Spinoza, as appears from Letter LXXV.</p>
