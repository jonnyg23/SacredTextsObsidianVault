 <h3 align="CENTER">DISMISSION OF LEVIATHAN</h3>
 <p align="CENTER"><img src="img/06501.jpg"></p>
 <p>The inscriptions on the seal are to be read as follows:</p>
 <p align="CENTER">MALCOH, SADAIJ, CUBOR DAMABIAH MENKIE LEJABEL MANIAH IJEJAVAI</p>
 <p>That is, Strong, Might spirit of hell, go back into thine own Works, in the name of Jehova.</p>
