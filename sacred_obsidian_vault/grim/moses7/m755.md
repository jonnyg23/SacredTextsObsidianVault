 <h3 align="CENTER">CHAPTER VI. THREE SIGNS WITH GRASSHOPPERS AND DARKNESS</h3>
 <p align="CENTER"><img src="img/08301.jpg"></p>
 <p align="CENTER">Conjuration</p>
 <p align="CENTER">ARDUSI! DALUSI!--Grasshoppers, darkness, arise in our service.</p>
 <p>These are the plagues which the Cuthians often employed in their exorcisms for punishment.</p>
