 <h3 align="CENTER">THE FOURTH TABLE OF THE SPIRITS OF THE EARTH</h3>
 <p align="CENTER">Conjuration</p>
 <p>I, N.N., command Thee, Awijel, by Otheos as also by Elmez through Agios. I, N.N., a servant of God, conjure Thee, Ahenatos Elijon, as also Adon was cited and called Zebaoth.</p>
 <p align="CENTER"><img src="img/04700.jpg"></p>
 <p>This Fourth Table will give the treasures of the earth, if it be laid in the earth. Its spirits will give the treasures of the earth at all times.</p>
