 <h3 align="CENTER">CONJURATION OF THE LAWS OF MOSES</h3>
 <p align="CENTER"><img src="img/06700.jpg"></p>
 <p>The inscription on the seal must be read as follows;</p>
 <p align="CENTER">AIJCON DUNSANAS PETHANIR THRIJGNIR IJON CIJNA NATER LAVIS PISTOIN</p>
 <p>If you wish to pronounce these words you must fast for three days, and you can perform wonders therewith. They cannot be translated on account of the Hebrew characters.</p>
