 <h3 align="CENTER">CHAPTER I. THE SPIRIT APPEARS IN A PILLAR OF FIRE BY NIGHT</h3>
 <p align="CENTER"><img src="img/08600.jpg"></p>
 <p align="CENTER">Conjuration</p>
 <p align="CENTER">TALUBSI! LATUBUSI! KALUBUSI! ALUSI!--Arise and bring me the pillar of Fire that I may see.</p>
 <p>The name of each angel must be called out three times to the four quarters of the earth, first with the voice, then with the horn.</p>
