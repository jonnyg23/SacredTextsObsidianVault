 <h3 align="CENTER">CONJURED SPIRIT APPEARS ON A PILLAR OF FIRE</h3>
 <p align="CENTER"><img src="img/06902.jpg"></p>
 <p>The inscriptions on the seal are to be said as follows:</p>
 <p align="CENTER">AFFABIJ ZIEN, JERAMIJE LATABI DAMAJESANO NOIJ LIJOIJ LEAIJ GLIJ EIJLOIJ LIECLE LOATE ELI ELI MECHARAMETHIJ RIJIBISAS SA FU AZIRA REACHA</p>
 <p>The Citation names the twelve evil spirits of man, through the help of the Father, of the Hebrew Eli. It is terrible.</p>
