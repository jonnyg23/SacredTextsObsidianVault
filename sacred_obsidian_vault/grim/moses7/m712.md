 <h3 align="CENTER">THE ELEVENTH TABLE OF THE SPIRITS</h3>
 <p align="CENTER">Conjuration</p>
 <p>I, N.N., cite Thee, Spirit Yhaij, by El, Yvaij, by Elohim, Ileh, by Elho * Kijlij, by Zebaoth, Taijn Iseij, by Tetragrammaton, Jeha, by Zadaij * Ahel, by Agla that you will obey my orders, as truly in and through the name Schemesumatie, upon which Josua called, and the sun stood still in its course. Fiat, fiat, fiat.</p>
 <p align="CENTER"><img src="img/05400.jpg"></p>
 <p>The Eleventh Table gives good luck and fortune. Its spirits give the treasures of the sea.</p>
