 <h3 align="CENTER">BALAAM'S SORCERY</h3>
 <p align="CENTER"><img src="img/06600.jpg"></p>
 <p>The inscriptions on this seal are to be read as follows:</p>
 <p align="CENTER">MELOCH, HEL ALOKIM TIPHRET HOD JESATH</p>
 <p>This brings vengeance upon enemies, and must not be disregarded because it contains the names of the Seven Tables of the Covenant.</p>
