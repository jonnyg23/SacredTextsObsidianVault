 <h3 align="CENTER">GENERAL CITATION OF MOSES ON ALL SPIRITS</h3>
 <p align="CENTER"><img src="img/06701.jpg"></p>
 <p>The inscriptions on the seal are to be read as follows:</p>
 <p>ELION GOEUA ADONAIJ CADAS EBREEL, ELOIL ELA AGIEL, AIJONI SACHADON, ESSUSELAS ELOHIM, DELIION JAU ELIJULA, DELIA JARI ZAZAEL PALIEMAO UMIEL, ONALA DILATUM SADATJ, ALMA JOD JAEL THAMA</p>
 <p>This citation is great and mighty. They are the names of the Creator, and the names of the two Cherubim on the Mercy Seat, Zarall and Jael.</p>
