 <h3 align="CENTER">THE FIFTH TABLE OF SATURN</h3>
 <p align="CENTER">Conjuration</p>
 <p>I, N.N., order, command and conjure Thee Sazlij, by Agios, Sedul, by Sother, Veduij, by Sabaot, Sove, Amonzion * Adoij, by Helohim, Jaho, by the Veritas Jehovah * Kawa, Alha, natos that ye must appear before me in human form, so truly as Daniel overcame and conquered Baal. Fiat, Fiat, Fiat.</p>
 <p align="CENTER"><img src="img/04800.jpg"></p>
 <p>The Spirits of the Fifth Table of Saturn will serve in everything according to wish. Their Table will bring good luck in play(games of chance).</p>
