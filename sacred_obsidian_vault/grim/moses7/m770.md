 <h3 align="CENTER">CHAPTER XI. BREASTPLATE OF MOSES</h3>
 <p align="CENTER"><img src="img/09300.jpg"></p>
 <p align="CENTER">Conjuration</p>
 <p align="CENTER">SCHEDUSI, WEDUSE, TIWISI.--I have sinned, I shall sin.</p>
 <p align="CENTER">Prayer</p>
 <p>Eternal God of our All! Our god! Hear our voice, spare and have mercy upon us. Accept our prayer in mercy and with pleasure. I have sinned. I have committed transgressions. I have sinned before Thee. I have done that which is displeasing unto Thee here in the earth. For the sake of Thy great name pardon me all the sins and iniquities and transgressions which I have committed against Thee from my youth. Perfect again all the holy names which I have blemished, Great Champion, terrible, highest God, eternal Lord, God Sabaoth.</p>
