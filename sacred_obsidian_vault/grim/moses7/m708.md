 <h3 align="CENTER">THE SEVENTH TABLE OF MARS</h3>
 <p align="CENTER">Conjuration</p>
 <p>I, N.N., cite Thee, Spirit Emol, by Deus Sachnaton * Luil, by Acumea * Luiji, by Ambriel *, Tijlaij, by Ehos *, by Jeha, by Zora * Ageh, by Awoth, * that you appear before me in a beautiful, human form, and accomplish my desire, thus truly in and through the anepobeijaron, which Aaron heard and which was prepared for him. Fiat, fiat, fiat.</p>
 <p align="CENTER"><img src="img/05000.jpg"></p>
 <p>The Seventh Seal of Mars brings good fortune. In case of quarrels the Spirits of Mars will help you.</p>
