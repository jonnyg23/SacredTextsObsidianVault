 <h3 align="CENTER">CHAPTER X. HELMET OF MOSES AND AARON</h3>
 <p align="CENTER"><img src="img/09201.jpg"></p>
 <p align="CENTER">WOCHUTU, TUKAL, BESCHUFA, GUTAL--If I shall sin, I shall blow with the great horn.</p>
 <p>Here the horn must be blown three times towards the four quarters of the earth. For the ram's horn, in the old covenant, is the symbol of omnipotence and of purification, or of beauty, truth and holiness.</p>
