 <h3 align="CENTER">PRINCIPAL CITATION ON ALL MINISTERING SPIRITS OF THE AIR AND OF EARTH, THE LIKE OF WHICH MANASSES AND SOLOMON USED AS THE TRUE KEY SOLOMONIS REGIS ISRAEL</h3>
 <p>You must stand on a prominent rock, hold a palm twig in your right hand, and wear a wreath of laurel around the temples. Then turn toward the east and say:</p>
 <p align="CENTER">ALIJA LAIJA LAUMIN OTHEON!</p>
 <p>At this time, a halo of light will surround you, and when you become aware of this light, fall upon your knees and worship. Then say in an audible voice the words inscribed in the following seal. You must speak slowly and distinctly.</p>
 <p align="CENTER"><img src="img/07400.jpg"></p>
 <p align="CENTER">ELIAM YOENA ADONAI CADUS EBREEL ELOYELA AGIEL, AYOM SACHADON OSSUSELAS ELOYM DE LIOMAR ELYNLA LELIA YAZI ZAZALL UNNEL OVELA DILATAM SADAY ALMA PANAIM ALYM CANAL DENSY USAMI YASAS CALIPI CALFAS SASNA SAFFA SADOJA AGLATA PANTOMEL AMRIEL AZIEN PHANATON SARZE PENERION YA EMANUEL JOD JALAPH AMPHIA THAN DOMIRAEL ALOWIN.</p>
