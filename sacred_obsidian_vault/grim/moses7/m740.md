 <h3 align="CENTER">THE SPIRIT APPEARS IN THE BURNING BUSH</h3>
 <p align="CENTER"><img src="img/07101.jpg"></p>
 <p>The inscriptions on the seal are to be read as follows:</p>
 <p align="CENTER">BABA CUCI HIEBU ZIADHI ELENEHET NA VEAN VIE ACHYA SALNA</p>
 <p>The spirit which appears here is God himself.</p>
