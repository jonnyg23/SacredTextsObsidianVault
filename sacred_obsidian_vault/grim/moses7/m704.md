 <h3 align="CENTER">THE THIRD TABLE OF THE SPIRITS OF WATER</h3>
 <p align="CENTER">Conjuration</p>
 <p>I call upon and command thee Chananya by God Tetragrammaton Eloh. I conjure Thee Yeschaijah by Alpha and Omega, and Thou art compelled through Adonai.</p>
 <p align="CENTER"><img src="img/04600.jpg"></p>
 <p>The Third Table brings great fortune by water, and its spirits will amply supply the treasures of the deep.</p>
