 <h3 align="CENTER">BREASTPLATE OF MOSES</h3>
 <p align="CENTER"><img src="img/06100.jpg"></p>
 <p>The Hebrew inscriptions within the seal are pronounced as follows:</p>
 <p align="CENTER">JEHOVA, ASER EHEJE CETHER ELEION EHEJE</p>
 <p>Their meaning is as follows:</p>
 <p>The Most High, whom no eye hath seen, nor tongue spoke; the Spirit which did great acts and performed great wonders. The Words of the Breastplate and the Helmet pronounced mean Holiness.</p>
