 <h3 align="CENTER">CHAPTER VIII. CITATION OF QUERNITHAY OR LEVIATHAN</h3>
 <p align="CENTER"><img src="img/09100.jpg"></p>
 <p align="CENTER">Conjuration</p>
 <p align="CENTER">ELUBATEL, EBUHUEL, ATUESUEL!</p>
 <p>Each name must be repeated three times. These, as well as the following invocations, contain only the peculiar names of the angels of omnipotence who will permit the conjured spirits to appear, or will compel them to appear by force.</p>
 <p align="CENTER">DISMISSAL</p>
 <p align="CENTER"><img src="img/09101.jpg"></p>
 <p align="CENTER">Conjuration</p>
 <p align="CENTER">I beseech thee, angel Elubatel, conduct N.N. {name of spirit} from my presence.</p>
 <p>Each angel's name must be pronounced three times with the voice and three times the horn must be blown, each time towards the four quarters of the earth.</p>
