 <p><a name="page_246"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 246</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">NOTES TO CHAPTER XXIX.</h3>  
 <p>(<i>a</i>) The Symbols of this Chapter are manifested in part by the Angels, and in part also by the Evil Spirits.</p>  
 <p>(<i>b</i>) ORIENS, PAIMON, ARITON, and AMAIMON, execute the Operations hereof by the means of their common Ministers. PAIMON also performs this Operation alone.</p>  
 <p>(<i>c</i>) The Familiar Spirits cannot well execute the Operations of this Chapter.</p>  
 <p>(<i>d</i>) No especial instructions are given by Abraham regarding this Chapter, by which Abraham says (in the First Book) that he himself had caused armed men to appear.</p>  
 <p>(<i>e</i>) No. 1 is a Gnomon of 13 Squares from a Square of 49 Squares. MACANEH = "an encampment".</p>  
 <p>No. 2 consists of 19 Squares from a Square of 81. MAHARACAH perhaps means "an ambuscade".</p>  
 <p>No. 3 consists of 21 Squares from a Square of 81 Squares.</p>
