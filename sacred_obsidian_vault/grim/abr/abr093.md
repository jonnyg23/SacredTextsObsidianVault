   <h3 align="center">NOTES TO CHAPTER XXIV.</h3>  
 <p>(<i>a</i>) The Symbols of this Chapter are manifested in part by the Angels, and in part also by the Evil Spirits.</p>  
 <p>(<i>b</i>) ARITON performs the Operations hereof; and MAGOT also; but separately.</p>  
 <p>(<i>c</i>) The Familiar Spirits can to an extent perform the Operations of this Chapter.</p>  
 <p>(<i>d</i>) No especial instructions are given by Abraham regarding this Chapter.</p>  
 <p>(<i>e</i>) No. 1 consists of 22 Squares taken from a Square of 49 Squares. The meaning of KIXALIS is not apparent.</p>  
 <p>No. 2 consists of 16 Squares from a Square of 49. QENEBAH probably conveys the idea of gain or possession.</p>  
 <p>No. 3 consists of 35 Squares from a Square of 81 Squares. QEDESELAN may signify things of value set apart.</p>  
 <p>No. 6 (the succession of numbers here is irregular) consists of 14 Squares from a Square of 25 Squares. MOREH means "to rebel against, to disobey".</p>  
 <p>No. 5 consists of a Square of 25 Squares. CARAC means "to involve or wrap up," also "garments, etc.".</p>  
 <p>No. 4 is a Gnomon of 13 Squares taken from a Square of 25 Squares. TALAH means "a young lamb," or "kid," according to whether its root terminates with Aleph or He.</p>
