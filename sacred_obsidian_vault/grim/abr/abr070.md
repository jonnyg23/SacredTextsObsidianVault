   <h3 align="center">THE THIRTEENTH CHAPTER.</h3>  
 <p><a name="img_tinit"></a>
 </p><table align="LEFT"><tr><td align="CENTER" valign="CENTER"><img src="img/tinit.jpg" align="LEFT"></td></tr></table>O cause a Dead Body to revive, and perform all the functions which a living person would do, and this during a space of Seven Years, by means of the Spirits.  
 <p>(1) From the Rising of the Sun until Mid-Day.</p>  
 <p>(2) From Mid-Day until the Setting of the Sun.</p>  
 <p>(3) From the Setting of the Sun until Mid-Night.</p>  
 <p>(4) From Mid-Night until the Rising of the Sun.</p>
