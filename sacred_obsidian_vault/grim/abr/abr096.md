   <h3 align="center">THE TWENTY-SIXTH CHAPTER.</h3>  
 <p><a name="img_tinit"></a>
 </p><table align="LEFT"><tr><td align="CENTER" valign="CENTER"><img src="img/tinit.jpg" align="LEFT"></td></tr></table>O open every Kind of Lock, without a Key, and without making any noise.  
 <p>(1) To open Doors.</p>  
 <p>(2) To open Padlocks.</p>  
 <p>(3) To open Larders (or Charnel-Houses).</p>  
 <p>(4) To open Strong-boxes (or Caskets).</p>  
 <p>(5) To open Prisons.</p>
