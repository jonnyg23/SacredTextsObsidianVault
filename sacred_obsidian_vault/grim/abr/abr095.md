 <p><a name="page_232"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 232</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">NOTES TO CHAPTER XXV.</h3>  
 <p>(<i>a</i>) The Symbols of this Chapter are manifested only by the Angel, or by the Guardian Angel.</p>  
 <p>(<i>b</i>) Abraham does not state to what Prince this Operation is submitted.</p>  
 <p>(<i>c</i>) The Familiar Spirits cannot well execute the Operations of this Chapter.</p>  
 <p>(<i>d</i>) No especial instructions are given by Abraham regarding this Chapter.</p>  
 <p>(<i>e</i>) No. 1 consists of 23 Squares taken from a Square of 81 Squares. NAHARIAMA means "a river of waters".</p>  
 <p>No. 2 is a Square of 64 Squares.</p>  
 <p>No. 3 is a Gnomon of 9 Squares from a Square of 25 Squares. MAIAM = "Abundant waters such as the sea".</p>
