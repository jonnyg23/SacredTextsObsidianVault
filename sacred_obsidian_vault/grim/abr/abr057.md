 <p><a name="page_184"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 184</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">NOTES TO CHAPTER VI.</h3>  
 <p>(<i>a</i>) The Symbols of this Chapter are manifested only by the Angels or by the Guardian Angel.</p>  
 <p>(<i>b</i>) ASTAROTH and ASMODEUS execute together the Operations of this Chapter.</p>  
 <p>(<i>c</i>) The Familiar Spirits cannot well execute the Operations of this Chapter.</p>  
 <p>(<i>d</i>) No especial instructions are given regarding this Chapter in the Second Book.</p>  
 <p>(<i>e</i>) No. 1 is a border of 20 Squares taken from a Square of 36 Squares.--TELAAH = perhaps from ThVLOH, = a Worm which pierces holes in the ground.</p>  
 <p>No. 2 is a border of 32 Squares taken from a Square of 81 Squares.--ALCABRUSI may mean "supported by planks or props, or beams". If so, probably this Square should be numbered 1.</p>  
 <p>No. 3 is a border of 20 Squares taken from a Square of 36 Squares. CADSAR perhaps from QTzR = to shorten or abbreviate a matter or work.</p>  
 <p>No. 4 is a Square of 49 Squares. PELAGIM, Hebrew PLGIM Divisions, strata, etc.</p>  
 <p>No. 5 is a Square of 36 Squares. KILOIN = Hebrew QLOIM, Excavations.</p>  
 <p>No. 6 is a Gnomon of 9 Squares taken from a Square of 25 Squares. NAKAB = Hebrew, NQB = Piercing. Perforation.</p>  
 <p>No. 7 is a Square of 36 Squares. KITTIK may mean = "To arrange in a store-place". TINNAT recalls a name used on some of the Gnostic Magical Gems. TANNIT is the name of a Tyrian goddess.</p>  
 <p>No. 8 is a Gnomon of 9 Squares out of a Square of 25 Squares. MARAK, from Hebrew MRQ = To cleanse, purge, or refine.</p>
