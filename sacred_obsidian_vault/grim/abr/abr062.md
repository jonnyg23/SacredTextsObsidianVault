   <h3 align="center">THE NINTH CHAPTER.</h3>  
 <p><a name="img_tinit"></a>
 </p><table align="LEFT"><tr><td align="CENTER" valign="CENTER"><img src="img/tinit.jpg" align="LEFT"></td></tr></table>O transform Animals into Men, and Men into Animals; etc.  
 <p>(1) To transform Men into Asses.</p>  
 <p>(2) Into Stags or Deer.</p>  
 <p>(3) Into Elephants.</p>  
 <p>(4) Into Wild Boars.</p>  
 <p>(5) Into Dogs.</p>  
 <p>(6) Into Wolves.</p>  
 <p>(7) Animals into Stones.</p>
