   <h3 align="center">THE TENTH CHAPTER.</h3>  
 <p><a name="img_tinit"></a>
 </p><table align="LEFT"><tr><td align="CENTER" valign="CENTER"><img src="img/tinit.jpg" align="LEFT"></td></tr></table>O hinder any Necromantic or Magical Operations from taking effect, except those of the Qabalah and of this Sacred Magic.  
 <p>(1) To undo any Magic soever.</p>  
 <p>(2) To heal the bewitched.</p>  
 <p>(3) To make Magical Storms cease.</p>  
 <p>(4) To discover any Magic.</p>  
 <p>(5) To hinder Sorcerers from Operating.</p>
