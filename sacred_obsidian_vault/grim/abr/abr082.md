   <h3 align="center">THE NINETEENTH CHAPTER.</h3>  
 <p><a name="img_finit"></a>
 </p><table align="LEFT"><tr><td align="CENTER" valign="CENTER"><img src="img/finit.jpg" align="LEFT"></td></tr></table>OR every description of Affection and Love.  
 <p>(1) To be beloved by one's Wife (or Husband).</p>  
 <p>(2) For some especial Love.</p>  
 <p>(3) To be beloved by a Relation.</p>  
 <p>(4) For a Maiden in particular.</p>  
 <p>(5) To acquire the affection of a Judge.</p>  
 <p>(6) To make oneself beloved by a Married person.</p>  
 <p>(7) To make oneself beloved by a Widow.</p>  
 <p>(8) By a girl already promised in Marriage.</p>  
 <p>(9) By a Maiden in general.</p>  
 <p>(10) By some especial Prince.</p>  
 <p>(11) By some especial King.</p>  
 <p><a name="page_215"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 215</font></a></p>  
 <p>(12) To obtain the friendship of some particular person.</p>  
 <p>(13) To have that of a Great Man.</p>  
 <p>(14) To be beloved by a Woman.</p>  
 <p>(15) To make oneself beloved by Ecclesiastics.</p>  
 <p>(16) To make oneself beloved by a Master.</p>  
 <p>(17) To make oneself beloved by a Mistress.</p>  
 <p>(18) To make oneself beloved by Infidels.</p>  
 <p>(19) By the Pope, by an Emperor, <a name="fr_232"></a><a href="#fn_232"><font size="1">1</font></a> or by Kings.</p>  
 <p>(20) For Adulteries in general.</p>
