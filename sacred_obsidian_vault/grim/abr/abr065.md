   <h3 align="center">NOTES TO CHAPTER X.</h3>  
 <p>(<i>a</i>) The Symbols of this Chapter are manifested only by the Angels, or by the Guardian Angel.</p>  
 <p>(<i>b</i>) MAGOTH executes the Operations of this Chapter.</p>  
 <p>(c) The Familiar Spirits cannot well execute the Operations of this Chapter.</p>  
 <p>(<i>d</i>) No especial instructions are given concerning this Chapter.</p>  
 <p><a name="page_192"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 192</font></a></p>  
 <p>(<i>e</i>) No. 1 consists of 17 Squares containing 18 letters (LI of "Codselim" occupying 1 Square) taken from a Square of 49 Squares.--CODSELIM and COHABIM <i>may</i> be from KSILIM, the foolish ones, and KABIM, the mourning ones (Hebrew).</p>  
 <p>No. 2. A border of 20 Squares taken from a Square of 36 Squares.--LACHAT, perhaps from Hebrew LChSh = to enchant.</p>  
 <p>No. 3. A Square of 81 Squares.--PARADILON, probably from Greek PARA, against, and DEILON, perverse, or miserable, or unfortunate.</p>  
 <p>No. 4. A Square of 25 Squares.--HORAH, from Hebrew ChRH, To be enraged; or HRH, to conceive, or bring forth.</p>  
 <p>No. 5. A Square of 49 Squares.--MACANEH from Hebrew MChNH, a fortification, castle, or defence. MADASUL, from MATzL, about me, before me, at my side.</p>
