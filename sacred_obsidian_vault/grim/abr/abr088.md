   <h3 align="center">THE TWENTY-SECOND CHAPTER.</h3>  
 <p><a name="img_tinit"></a>
 </p><table align="LEFT"><tr><td align="CENTER" valign="CENTER"><img src="img/tinit.jpg" align="LEFT"></td></tr></table>HIS Chapter is only for Evil, for with the Symbols herein we can cast Spells, and work every kind of Evil; we should not avail ourselves hereof.  
 <p>(1) To cast Spells upon Men.</p>  
 <p>(2) To bewitch Beasts.</p>  
 <p>(3) To cast a Spell upon the Liver.</p>  
 <p>(4) This Symbol should never be made use of.</p>  
 <p>(5) To cast a Spell upon the Heart.</p>  
 <p>(6) Upon the Head and other parts of the Body.</p>
