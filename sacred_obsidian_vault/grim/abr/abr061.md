 <p><a name="page_187"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 187</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">NOTES TO CHAPTER VIII.</h3>  
 <p>(<i>a</i>) The Symbols of this Chapter are manifested partly by the Angels and partly by the Evil Spirits.</p>  
 <p>(<i>b</i>) ASHTAROTH executes the Operations of this Chapter.</p>  
 <p>(<i>c</i>) The Familiar Spirits cannot execute the Operations of this Chapter.</p>  
 <p>(<i>d</i>) To excite a Tempest, give the Signal above you, and touch the Symbol on the top. To make it cease, touch it underneath.</p>  
 <p>(<i>e</i>) No. 1 is a Square of 49 Squares. CANAMAL = Hebrew ChNML, <i>i.e.</i>, Hailstones of great size.</p>  
 <p>No. 2 is a border of 16 taken from a Square of 25 Squares. TAKAT, TKO, Hebrew, has the sense of "immersed in, overflowed by".</p>  
 <p>No. 3. A Gnomon of 11 Squares taken from a Square of 36 Squares. SAGRIR, Hebrew SGRIR a most vehement rain and tempest".</p>  
 <p>No. 4. A Square of 25 Squares. HAMAG, perhaps from Hebrew, MOK, "to compress, or crush, or press".</p>
