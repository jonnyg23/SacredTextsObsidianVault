   <h3 align="center">THE TWENTY-FIRST CHAPTER.</h3>  
 <p><a name="img_tinit"></a>
 </p><table align="LEFT"><tr><td align="CENTER" valign="CENTER"><img src="img/tinit.jpg" align="LEFT"></td></tr></table>O transform oneself, and take different Faces and Forms.  
 <p>(1) To appear old.</p>  
 <p>(2) To take on the appearance of an Old Woman.</p>  
 <p>(3) To appear young.</p>  
 <p>(4) To transform oneself into a Girl.</p>  
 <p>(5) To appear like a young Child.</p>
