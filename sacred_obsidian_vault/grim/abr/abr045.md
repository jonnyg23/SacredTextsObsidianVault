 <p><a name="page_165"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 165</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">THE FIRST CHAPTER.</h3>  
 <p><a name="img_tinit"></a>
 </p><table align="LEFT"><tr><td align="CENTER" valign="CENTER"><img src="img/tinit.jpg" align="LEFT"></td></tr></table>TO know all manner of things Past and Future, which be not however directly opposed to God, and to His Most Holy Will.  
 <p>(1) To know all things Past and Future in general.</p>  
 <p>(2, 3) To know things appertaining unto the Future.</p>  
 <p>(4) Things to happen in War.</p>  
 <p>(5) Things past and forgotten.</p>  
 <p>(6) Tribulations to come.</p>  
 <p>(7) Things propitious to come.</p>  
 <p>(8) Things past regarding Enemies.</p>  
 <p>(9) To know the Signs of Tempests.</p>  
 <p>(10) To know the Secrets of War.</p>  
 <p>(11) To know true and false Friends.</p>
