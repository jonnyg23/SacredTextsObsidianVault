   <h3 align="center">NOTES TO CHAPTER XXIII.</h3>  
 <p>(<i>a</i>) The Symbols of this Chapter are manifested by the Evil Spirits only.</p>  
 <p>(<i>b</i>) ASTAROT performs the Operations hereof.</p>  
 <p>(<i>c</i>) The Familiar Spirits can to an extent perform the Operations of this Chapter.</p>  
 <p>(<i>d</i>) No especial instructions are given regarding this Chapter by Abraham the Jew.</p>  
 <p>(<i>e</i>) No. 1 consists of 12 Squares taken from a Square of 25 Squares. NAVEH means "a house or habitation".</p>  
 <p>No. 2 consists of 12 Squares from a Square of 25. QAQAH = "to make void or empty".</p>  
 <p>No. 3 consists of 15 Squares from a Square of 49 Squares. COMAHON means "a stronghold".</p>  
 <p>No. 4 consists of a Gnomon of 16 Squares from a Square of 36 Squares. BINIAM means "in affliction".</p>
