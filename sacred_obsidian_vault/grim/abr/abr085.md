   <h3 align="center">NOTES TO CHAPTER XX.</h3>  
 <p>(<i>a</i>) The Symbols of this Chapter are manifested in part by the Angels, and in part also by the Evil Spirits.</p>  
 <p>(<i>b</i>) BELZEBUD performs the Operations hereof.</p>  
 <p>(<i>c</i>) The Familiar Spirits cannot well execute the Operations of this chapter.</p>  
 <p>(<i>d</i>) See instructions for Chapter XIX, which serve equally for that, and the present Operation.</p>  
 <p>(<i>e</i>) No. 1 consists of 19 Squares irregularly arranged, and taken from a Square of 25 Squares.--KANNA means "jealous".</p>  
 <p>No. 2 consists of 13 Squares from a Square of 25 Squares.--SELAK = "to cast down or prostrate".</p>  
 <p>No. 3 consists of 18 Squares from a Square of 25.--ROQEN implies "persons in power".</p>  
 <p><a name="page_223"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 223</font></a></p>  
 <p>No. 4 consists of 25 Squares from a Square of 49.--ATLITIS is a corruption of the Greek adjective ATLETOS = "insufferable, not to be borne".</p>  
 <p>No. 5 consists of 19 Squares from a Square of 49.--OTSAMAH = "bodily strength".</p>  
 <p>No. 6 consists of 21 from a Square of 25.--SINAH = "Hatred".</p>  
 <p>No. 7 is a Gnomon of 9 Squares from a Square of 25. SATAN is the Name of one of the Chief Evil Spirits and has been elsewhere explained.</p>  
 <p>No. 8 consists of 19 Squares in the form of a capital F, taken from a Square of 49 Squares.--LOFITOS is evidently from the Greek LOPHESIS, meaning "rest, cessation from action (<i>i.e.</i> in this case military action)".</p>  
 <p>No. 9 consists of 13 Squares arranged in the form of an F, and taken from a Square of 25 Squares.--GIBOR = "strength, might, severity".</p>  
 <p>No. 10 is a Square of 25 Squares.--NOKAM = "vengeance".</p>  
 <p>No. 11 is also a Square of 25 Squares.--KELIK = "for all kinds of things".</p>  
 <p>No. 12 consists of 25 Squares from a Square of 49.--KERABAH = "assault, attack".</p>
