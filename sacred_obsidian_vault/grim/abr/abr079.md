   <h3 align="center">NOTES TO CHAPTER XVII.</h3>  
 <p>(<i>a</i>) The Symbols of this Chapter are manifested in part by the Angels, and in part also by the Evil Spirits.</p>  
 <p>(<i>b</i>) ORIENS, PAIMON, ARITON, and AMAIMON execute the Operations hereof by means of their common Ministers.</p>  
 <p>(<i>c</i>) The Familiar Spirits cannot well execute the Operations of this Chapter.</p>  
 <p>(<i>d</i>) Name the place aloud to which you wish to travel, and place the Symbol upon your head under the bonnet or the hat; but take well heed that it does not fall from you, which would be very dangerous. Do not as a rule travel by night; and select a calm and serene day for the Operation.</p>  
 <p>(<i>e</i>) No. 1 is a Square of 25 Squares. TASMA implies protection. TRMS is the Hebrew word used in the verse, "Thou shalt go upon the Lion and the Adder".</p>  
 <p>No. 2 consists of 8 Squares taken from a Square of 16. ANAN means "great labour".</p>  
 <p>No. 3 is a Square of 25 Squares. HOLOP means "to travel".</p>  
 <p>No. 4 is a Square of 16 Squares. ODAC means "to pass on from one place to another".</p>  
 <p>No. 5 is a Square of 25 Squares. ROLOR is perhaps from ROL "to move hurriedly".</p>  
 <p>No. 6 is a Square of 25 Squares. NATSA means "to flee or fly quickly".</p>
