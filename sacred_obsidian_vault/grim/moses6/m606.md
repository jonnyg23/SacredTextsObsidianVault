 <h1 align="CENTER">THE MYSTERY OF THE SIXTH SEAL.</h1>
 <p align="CENTER">The Seal of the Power Angels are Potestatum over the Angels and Spirits of all the Elements.</p>
 <p align="CENTER">CONJURATION.</p>
 <p>I, N.N., a servant of God, desire, call upon and conjure thee, Spirit Alymon, by the most dreadful words, Sather, Ehomo, Geno, Poro, Jehovah, Elohim, Volnah, Denach, Alonlam, Ophiel, Zophiel, Sophiel, Habriel, Eloha, Alesimus, Dileth, Melohim, and by all the holiest words through which thou canst be conquered, that thou appear before me in a mild, beautiful human form, and fulfil what I command thee, so surely as God will come to judge the living and the dead. Fiat, Fiat, Fiat.</p>
 <p align="CENTER"><img src="04100.jpg"></p>
 <p>The most obedient Angels of Power, seu Potestates, are the following four elements: Schunmyel, Alymon, Mupiel, Symnay, Semanglaf, Taftyah, Melech, Seolam, Waed, Sezah, Safyn, Kyptip, Taftyarohel, Aeburatiel, Anyam, Bymnam. This is the mystery or Seal of the Might-Angels. The peculiar Arcanum of this Seal of the Mighty is that if a man wears this seal in bed, he will learn what he desires to know through dreams and visions.</p>
