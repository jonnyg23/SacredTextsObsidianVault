 <h3 align="CENTER">§ 7. Talismans of the Sage of the Pyramids</h3>
 <p>I. Serves for the conjuration of celestial and infernal powers. It should be embroidered in silver upon sky-blue</p>
 <p><a name="page_118"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 118</font></a></p>
 <p>satin. The evoking words are SIRAS, ETAR, BESANAR, at which multitudes of spirits will appear.</p>

 <p align="CENTER"> </p>
 <p>II. Gives the love and complaisance of the entire female sex. It should be embroidered in silver on black satin. The evoking words are NADES, SURADIS, MANINER, pronounced with the ring-which should be on the middle finger of the left hand-pressed against the lips. This conjuration ensures the manifestation of a genius with rose-coloured wings, who, if addressed with the words SADER, PROSTAS, SOLASTER, Will traverse all space to transport you the lady of your heart, though she were the queen of the Caliph's seraglio. At the words MAMMES, LAHER, she will be removed by four slaves. It will be observed that this is occult ribaldry.</p>

 <p>III. Discovers all treasures and ensures their possession. The figure of the talisman should be embroidered in gold upon green satin. The words ONAÏM, PERANTÈS, RASONASTOS, will cause the appearance of seven genii <i>au teint bazané</i>, each of whom will pour out golden ducats from great bags of bide at the feet of the sorcerer, the operations of this Grimoire being performed upon a huge scale. <i>Item</i>, a black hooded bird will be perched upon the shoulder of each spirit.</p>
