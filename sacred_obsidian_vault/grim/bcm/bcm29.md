 <font size="2"><p align="CENTER">TALISMAN VIII</p></font>.<br>
 <a name="img_12200"></a>
 <a href="img/12200.jpg"><img src="tn/generic.jpg"><br><font size="-3">Click to view</font></a><br>
 <i><font size="2">The characters should be graven on the outer side of the Ring</font></i>.
 <p> </p>
 <p>VIII. Gives invisibility, even to the eyes of genii, so that God alone shall witness the actions of the possessor. It is accompanied by the power of penetrating everywhere and passing through brick walls. The magical words are BENATIR, CARARKAU, DEDOS, ETINARMI. For each operation the ring must be placed upon a different finger of the right hand. The talisman is of yellow satin embroidered with black silk.</p>
