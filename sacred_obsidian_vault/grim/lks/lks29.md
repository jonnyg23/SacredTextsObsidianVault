 <h3 align="CENTER">AN EXPLANATION OF SOLOMON'S TRIANGLE</h3>
 <i></i><p>Anephezeton.--Thou great God of all the Heavenly Host:</p>
 <i></i><p>Primeumaton.--Thou Who art the First and Last, let all spirits be subject unto us, and let the Spirit be bound in this triangle, which disturbs this place:</p>
 <i></i><p>Michael.--By Thy Holy Angel Michael, until I shall discharge him.</p>
 <p align="CENTER">(HERE ENDETH THIS FIRST BOOK OF THE LEMEGETON, WHICH IS CALLED THE GOETIA.)</p>
