 <h3 align="CENTER">EXPLANATION OF CERTAIN NAMES USED IN THIS BOOK LEMEGETON.</h3>
 <i></i><p>Eheie. Kether.--Almighty God, whose dwelling is in the highest Heavens:</p>
 <i></i><p>Haioth.--The great King of Heaven, and of all the powers therein:</p>
 <i></i><p>Methratton.--And of all the holy hosts of Angels and Archangels:</p>
 <i></i><p>Reschith.--Hear the prayers of Thy servant who putteth his trust in Thee:</p>
 <i></i><p>Hagalgalim.--Let thy Holy Angels be commanded to assist me at this time and at all times.</p>
 <i></i><p>Iehovah.--God Almighty, God Omnipotent, hear my prayer:</p>
 <i></i><p>Hadonat.--Command Thy Holy Angels above the fixed stars:</p>
 <i></i><p>Ophanim.--To be assisting and aiding Thy servant:</p>
 <i></i><p>Iophiel.--That I may command all spirits of air, water, fire, earth, and hell:</p>
 <i></i><p>Masloth.--So that it may tend unto Thy glory and unto the good of man.</p>
 <i></i><p>Iehovah.--God Almighty, God Omnipotent, hear my prayer:</p>
 <i></i><p>Elohim.--God with us, God be always present with us.</p>
 <i></i><p>Binah.--Strengthen us and support us, both now and for ever:</p>
 <i></i><p>Aralim.--In these our undertakings, which we perform but as instruments in Thy hands:</p>
 <i></i><p>Zabbathi (should be Shabbathii).--In the hands of Thee, the great God of Sabäoth.</p>
 <p><a name="page_68"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 68</font></a></p>
 <i></i><p>Hesel (should be Chesed).--Thou great God, governor and creator of the planets, and of the Host of Heaven:</p>
 <i></i><p>Hasmalim (should be Chashmalim).--Command them by Thine almighty power:</p>
 <i></i><p>Zelez (should be Zedeq).--To be now present and assisting to us Thy poor servants, both now and for ever.</p>
 <i></i><p>Elohim Geber (should be Gibor).--Most Almighty and eternal and ever living Lord God:</p>
 <i></i><p>Seraphim.--Command Thy seraphim:</p>
 <i></i><p>Camael, Madim.--To attend on us now at this time, to assist us, and to defend us from all perils and dangers.</p>
 <i></i><p>Eloha.--O Almighty God! be present with us both now and for ever:</p>
 <p>Tetragrammaton.--And let thine Almighty power and presence ever guard and protect us now and for ever:</p>
 <i></i><p>Raphael.--Let thy holy angel Raphael wait upon us at this present and for ever:</p>
 <i></i><p>Schemes (or Shemesh).--To assist us in these our undertakings.</p>
 <i></i><p>Iehovah.--God Almighty, God Omnipotent, hear my prayer:</p>
 <i></i><p>Sabäoth.--Thou great God of Sabäoth:</p>
 <i></i><p>Netzah (or Netzach).--All-seeing God:</p>
 <i></i><p>Elohim.--God be present with us, and let thy presence be now and always present with us:</p>
 <i></i><p>Haniel.--Let thy holy angel Haniel come and minister unto us at this present.</p>
 <i></i><p>Sabäoth.--O thou great God of Sabäoth, be present with us at this time and for ever:</p>
 <p><a name="page_69"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 69</font></a></p>
 <i></i><p>Hodben (should be Hod simply).--Let Thine Almighty power defend us and protect us, both now and for ever:</p>
 <i></i><p>Michael.--Let Michael, who is, under Thee, general of thy heavenly host:</p>
 <i></i><p>Cochab.--Come and expel all evil and danger from us both now and for ever.</p>
 <i></i><p>Sadai.--Thou great God of all wisdom and knowledge:</p>
 <i></i><p>Jesal (should be Iesod).--Instruct Thy poor and most humble servant:</p>
 <i></i><p>Cherubim.--By Thy holy cherubim:</p>
 <i></i><p>Gabriel.--By Thy Holy Angel Gabriel, who is the Author and Messenger of good tidings:</p>
 <i></i><p>Levanah.--Direct and support us at this present and for ever.</p>
