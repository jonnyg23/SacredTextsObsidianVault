 <h2 align="CENTER">CHAPTER XX</h2>
 <h3 align="CENTER">CONCERNING THE SILKEN CLOTH</h3>
 <p>WHEN any Instrument of the Art is properly consecrated, it should be wrapped in silk and put away, as we have said.</p>
 <p>Take, then, silk of any colour except black or grey, whereon write the words and Characters in <i><a href="kos44.htm#img_kos14">Figure 90</a></i>.</p>
 <p>Perfume it with incense of good odour, sprinkle it, and recite Psalms lxxxii.; lxxii.; cxxxiv.; lxiv.</p>
 <p>After this thou shalt put it aside for seven days with sweet spices and thou shalt use this silk to wrap all the Instruments of the Art.</p>
