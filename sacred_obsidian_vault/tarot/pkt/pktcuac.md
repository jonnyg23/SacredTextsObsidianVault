 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_cuac"></a>
 <span align="CENTER">
 <a href="img/cuac.jpg"><img src="tn/cuac.jpg"><br><font size="-3">Click to enlarge</font></a></span></p> 
 <p>The waters are beneath, and thereon are water-lilies; the hand issues from the cloud, holding in its palm the cup, from which four streams are pouring; a dove, bearing in its bill a cross-marked Host, descends to place the Wafer in the Cup; the dew of water is falling on all sides. It is an intimation of that which may lie behind the Lesser Arcana. <i>Divinatory Meanings</i>: House of the true heart, joy, content, abode, nourishment, abundance, fertility; Holy Table, felicity hereof. <i>Reversed</i>: House of the false heart, mutation, instability, revolution.</p>
