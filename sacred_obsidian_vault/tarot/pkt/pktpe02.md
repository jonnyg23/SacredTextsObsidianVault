 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_pe02"></a>
 <span align="CENTER">
 <a href="img/pe02.jpg"><img src="tn/pe02.jpg"><br><font size="-3">Click to enlarge</font></a></span></p> 
 <p>A young man, in the act of dancing, has a pentacle in either hand, and they are joined by that endless cord which is like the number 8 reversed. <i>Divinatory Meanings</i>: On the one hand it is represented as a card of gaiety, recreation and its connexions, which is the subject of the design; but it is read also as news and messages in writing, as obstacles, agitation, trouble, embroilment. <i>Reversed</i>: Enforced gaiety, simulated enjoyment, literal sense, handwriting, composition, letters of exchange.</p>
