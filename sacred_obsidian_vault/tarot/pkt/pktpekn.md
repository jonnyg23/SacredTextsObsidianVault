 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_pekn"></a>
 <span align="CENTER">
 <a href="img/pekn.jpg"><img src="tn/pekn.jpg"><br><font size="-3">Click to enlarge</font></a></span></p> 
 <p>He rides a slow, enduring, heavy horse, to which his own aspect corresponds. He exhibits his symbol, but does not look therein. <i>Divinatory Meanings</i>: Utility, serviceableness, interest, responsibility, rectitude-all on the normal and external plane. <i>Reversed</i>: inertia, idleness, repose of that kind, stagnation; also placidity, discouragement, carelessness.</p>
