 <p>A youthful figure, looking intently at the pentacle which hovers over his raised hands. He moves slowly, insensible of that which is about him. <i>Divinatory Meanings</i>: Application, study, scholarship, reflection another reading says news, messages and the bringer thereof; also rule, management. <i>Reversed</i>: Prodigality, dissipation, liberality, luxury; unfavourable news.</p>
