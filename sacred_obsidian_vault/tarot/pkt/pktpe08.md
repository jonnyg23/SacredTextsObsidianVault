 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_pe08"></a>
 <span align="CENTER">
 <a href="img/pe08.jpg"><img src="tn/pe08.jpg"><br><font size="-3">Click to enlarge</font></a></span></p> 
 <p>An artist in stone at his work, which he exhibits in the form of trophies. <i>Divinatory Meanings</i>: Work, employment, commission, craftsmanship, skill in craft and business, perhaps in the preparatory stage. <i>Reversed</i>: Voided ambition, vanity, cupidity, exaction, usury. It may also signify the possession of skill, in the sense of the ingenious mind turned to cunning and intrigue.</p>
