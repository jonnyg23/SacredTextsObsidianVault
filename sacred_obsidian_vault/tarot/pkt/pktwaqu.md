 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_waqu"></a>
 <span align="CENTER">
 <a href="img/waqu.jpg"><img src="tn/waqu.jpg"><br><font size="-3">Click to enlarge</font></a></span></p> 
 <p>The Wands throughout this suit are always in leaf, as it is a suit of life and animation. Emotionally and otherwise, the Queen's personality corresponds to that of the King, but is more magnetic. <i>Divinatory Meanings</i>: A dark woman, countrywoman, friendly, chaste, loving, honourable. If the card beside her signifies a man, she is well disposed towards him; if a woman, she is interested in the Querent. Also, love of money, or a certain success in business. <i>Reversed</i>: Good, economical, obliging, serviceable. Signifies also--but in certain positions and in the neighbourhood of other cards tending in such directions--opposition, jealousy, even deceit and infidelity.</p>
