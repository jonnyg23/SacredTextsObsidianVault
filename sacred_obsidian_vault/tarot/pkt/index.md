 <p>
 Although there were many Tarot decks prior to the Rider-Waite-Smith
 deck, and many after, none has gripped the popular imagination
 as much as this set.
 Waite covers the significance and deeper implications of each card,
 and gives practical instructions as to how to conduct a reading.
 The symbolism of the Rider-Waite-Smith deck is based on profound
 occult studies by Waite, and his exposition in this book of its
 use and meaning is unexcelled. This is a must-read for anyone who
 wants to understand the Tarot.--J. B. Hare
 </p>
