 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_pe04"></a>
 <span align="CENTER">
 <a href="img/pe04.jpg"><img src="tn/pe04.jpg"><br><font size="-3">Click to enlarge</font></a></span></p> 
 <p>A crowned figure, having a pentacle over his crown, clasps another with hands and arms; two pentacles are under his feet. He holds to that which he has. <i>Divinatory Meanings</i>: The surety of possessions, cleaving to that which one has, gift, legacy, inheritance. <i>Reversed</i>: Suspense, delay, opposition.</p>
