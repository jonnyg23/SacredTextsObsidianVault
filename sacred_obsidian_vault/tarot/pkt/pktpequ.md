 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_pequ"></a>
 <span align="CENTER">
 <a href="img/pequ.jpg"><img src="tn/pequ.jpg"><br><font size="-3">Click to enlarge</font></a></span></p> 
 <p>The face suggests that of a dark woman, whose qualities might be summed up in the idea of greatness of soul; she has also the serious cast of intelligence; she contemplates her symbol and may see worlds therein. <i>Divinatory Meanings</i>: Opulence, generosity, magnificence, security, liberty. <i>Reversed</i>: Evil, suspicion, suspense, fear, mistrust.</p>
