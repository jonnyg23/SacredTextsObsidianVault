 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_sw08"></a>
 <span align="CENTER">
 <a href="img/sw08.jpg"><img src="tn/sw08.jpg"><br><font size="-3">Click to enlarge</font></a></span></p> 
 <p>A woman, bound and hoodwinked, with the swords of the card about her. Yet it is rather a card of temporary durance than of irretrievable bondage. <i>Divinatory Meanings</i>: Bad news, violent chagrin, crisis, censure, power in trammels, conflict, calumny; also sickness. <i>Reversed</i>: Disquiet, difficulty, opposition, accident, treachery; what is unforeseen; fatality.</p>
