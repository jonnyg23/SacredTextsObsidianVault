 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_wa09"></a>
 <span align="CENTER">
 <a href="img/wa09.jpg"><img src="tn/wa09.jpg"><br><font size="-3">Click to enlarge</font></a></span></p> 
 <p>The figure leans upon his staff and has an expectant look, as if awaiting an enemy. Behind are eight other staves--erect, in orderly disposition, like a palisade. <i>Divinatory Meanings</i>: The card signifies strength in opposition. If attacked, the person will meet an onslaught boldly; and his build shews, that he may prove a formidable antagonist. With this main significance there are all its possible adjuncts--delay, suspension, adjournment. <i>Reversed</i>: Obstacles, adversity, calamity.</p>
