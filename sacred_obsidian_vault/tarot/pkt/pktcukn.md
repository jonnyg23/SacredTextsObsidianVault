 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_cukn"></a>
 <span align="CENTER">
 <a href="img/cukn.jpg"><img src="tn/cukn.jpg"><br><font size="-3">Click to enlarge</font></a></span></p> 
 <p>Graceful, but not warlike; riding quietly, wearing a winged helmet, referring to those higher graces of the imagination which sometimes characterize this card. He too is a dreamer, but the images of the side of sense haunt him in his vision. <i>Divinatory Meanings</i>: Arrival, approach--sometimes that of a messenger; advances, proposition, demeanour, invitation, incitement. <i>Reversed</i>: Trickery, artifice, subtlety, swindling, duplicity, fraud.</p>
