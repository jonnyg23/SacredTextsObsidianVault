 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_cuqu"></a>
 <span align="CENTER">
 <a href="img/cuqu.jpg"><img src="tn/cuqu.jpg"><br><font size="-3">Click to enlarge</font></a></span></p> 
 <p>Beautiful, fair, dreamy--as one who sees visions in a cup. This is, however, only one of her aspects; she sees, but she also acts, and her activity feeds her dream. <i>Divinatory Meanings</i>: Good, fair woman; honest, devoted woman, who will do service to the Querent; loving intelligence, and hence the gift of vision; success, happiness, pleasure; also wisdom, virtue; a perfect spouse and a good mother. <i>Reversed</i>: The accounts vary; good woman; otherwise, distinguished woman but one not to be trusted; perverse woman; vice, dishonour, depravity.</p>
