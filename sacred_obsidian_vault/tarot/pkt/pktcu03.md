 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_cu03"></a>
 <span align="CENTER">
 <a href="img/cu03.jpg"><img src="tn/cu03.jpg"><br><font size="-3">Click to enlarge</font></a></span></p> 
 <p>Maidens in a garden-ground with cups uplifted, as if pledging one another. <i>Divinatory Meanings</i>: The conclusion of any matter in plenty, perfection and merriment; happy issue, victory, fulfilment, solace, healing, <i>Reversed</i>: Expedition, dispatch, achievement, end. It signifies also the side of excess in physical enjoyment, and the pleasures of the senses.</p>
