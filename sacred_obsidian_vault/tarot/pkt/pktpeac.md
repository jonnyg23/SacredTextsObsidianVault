 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_peac"></a>
 <span align="CENTER">
 <a href="img/peac.jpg"><img src="tn/peac.jpg"><br><font size="-3">Click to enlarge</font></a></span></p> 
 <p>A hand--issuing, as usual, from a cloud--holds up a pentacle. <i>Divinatory Meanings</i>: Perfect contentment, felicity, ecstasy; also speedy intelligence; gold. <i>Reversed</i>: The evil side of wealth, bad intelligence; also great riches. In any case it shews prosperity, comfortable material conditions, but whether these are of advantage to the possessor will depend on whether the card is reversed or not.</p>
