 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_cu02"></a>
 <span align="CENTER">
 <a href="img/cu02.jpg"><img src="tn/cu02.jpg"><br><font size="-3">Click to enlarge</font></a></span></p> 
 <p>A youth and maiden are pledging one another, and above their cups rises the Caduceus of Hermes, between the great wings of which there appears a lion's head. It is a variant of a sign which is found in a few old examples of this card. Some curious emblematical meanings are attached to it, but they do not concern us in this place. <i>Divinatory Meanings</i>: Love, passion, friendship, affinity, union, concord, sympathy, the interrelation of the sexes, and--as a suggestion apart from all offices of divination--that desire which is not in Nature, but by which Nature is sanctified.</p>
