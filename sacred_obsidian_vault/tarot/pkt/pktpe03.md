 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_pe03"></a>
 <span align="CENTER">
 <a href="img/pe03.jpg"><img src="tn/pe03.jpg"><br><font size="-3">Click to enlarge</font></a></span></p> 
 <p>A sculptor at his work in a monastery. Compare the design which illustrates the Eight of Pentacles. The apprentice or amateur therein has received his reward and is now at work in earnest. <i>Divinatory Meanings</i>: <i>Métier</i>, trade, skilled labour; usually, however, regarded as a card of nobility, aristocracy, renown, glory. <i>Reversed</i>: Mediocrity, in work and otherwise, puerility, pettiness, weakness.</p>
