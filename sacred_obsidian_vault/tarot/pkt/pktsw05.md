 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_sw05"></a>
 <span align="CENTER">
 <a href="img/sw05.jpg"><img src="tn/sw05.jpg"><br><font size="-3">Click to enlarge</font></a></span></p> 
 <p>A disdainful man looks after two retreating and dejected figures. Their swords lie upon the ground. He carries two others on his left shoulder, and a third sword is in his right hand, point to earth. He is the master in possession of the field. <i>Divinatory Meanings</i>: Degradation, destruction, revocation, infamy, dishonour, loss, with the variants and analogues of these. <i>Reversed</i>: The same; burial and obsequies.</p>
