 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_wa04"></a>
 <span align="CENTER">
 <a href="img/wa04.jpg"><img src="tn/wa04.jpg"><br><font size="-3">Click to enlarge</font></a></span></p> 
 <p>From the four great staves planted in the foreground there is a great garland suspended; two female figures uplift nosegays; at their side is a bridge over a moat, leading to an old manorial house. <i>Divinatory Meanings</i>: They are for once almost on the surface--country life, haven of refuge, a species of domestic harvest-home, repose, concord, harmony, prosperity, peace, and the perfected work of these. <i>Reversed</i>: The meaning remains unaltered; it is prosperity, increase, felicity, beauty, embellishment.</p>
