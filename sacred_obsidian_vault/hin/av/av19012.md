 <p><a name="page_223"><font size="1" color="green">p. 223</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">HYMN XII</h3>  <h4 align="center">A prayer to Ushas or Dawn for wealth and long life</h4>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1901201">1</a></font></span>Dawn drives away her sister's gloom, and through her excellence<br>    makes her retrace her path.<br>   Through her may we gain God-appointed treasure, and with<br>    brave sons be glad through hundred winters.</p>
