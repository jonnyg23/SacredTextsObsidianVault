 <p><a name="page_164"><font size="1" color="green">p. 164</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">HYMN II</h3>  <h4 align="center">A charm to secure various blessings</h4>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1600201">1</a></font></span>Away from distasteful food, strength and sweet speech,<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1600202">2</a></font></span>Are pleasant. May I obtain a pleasant voice.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1600203">3</a></font></span>I have invoked the Protector; I have invoked his protection.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1600204">4</a></font></span>Quick of hearing are mine ears; mine ears hear what is good-<br>   Fain would I hear a pleasant sound.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1600205">5</a></font></span>Let not good hearing and overhearing fail the Eagle's eye, the<br>    undecaying light.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1600206">6</a></font></span>Thou art the couch of the Rishis. Let worship be paid to the<br>    divine couch.</p>
