 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Hymns of the Atharva Veda</i>, by Ralph T.H. Griffith, [1895], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">HYMN XIV</h3>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1501401">1</a></font></span>He when he went away to the eastern region, went away having<br>    become the Marut host, and having made Mind an eater of<br>    food. He who hath this knowledge eats food with Mind as<br>    food-eater. <a name="page_159"><font size="1" color="green">p. 159</font></a><br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1501402">2</a></font></span>He, when he went away to the southern region, went away<br>    having become Indra, and having made Strength an eater of<br>    food. He who hath this knowledge eats food with strength as<br>    food-eater.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1501403">3</a></font></span>He, when he went away to the western region, went away having<br>    become King Varuna, and having made the Waters eaters of<br>    food. He who hath this knowledge eats food with the Waters<br>    as food-eaters.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1501404">4</a></font></span>He, when he went away to the northern region, went away<br>    having become King Soma and having made the Seven Rishis'<br>    oblation an eater of food. He who hath this knowledge eats<br>    food with oblation as food-eater.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1501405">5</a></font></span>He, when he went away to the stedfast region, went away having<br>    become Vishnu and having made Virāj an eater of food. He<br>    who hath this knowledge eats food with Virāj as food-eater.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1501406">6</a></font></span>He, when he went away to animals, went away having become<br>   Rudra and having made herbs eaters of food. He who hath<br>    this knowledge eats food with herbs as food-eaters.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1501407">7</a></font></span>He, when he went away to the Fathers, went away having be-<br>    come King Yama and having made the exclamation Svadhā<br>    an eater of food. He who hath this knowledge eats food with<br>    the exclamation Svadhā as food-eater.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1501408">8</a></font></span>He, when he went away to men, went away having become Agni<br>    and having made the exclamation Svāhā an eater of food. He<br>    who hath this knowledge eats food with the exclamation<br>   Svahā as food-eater.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1501409">9</a></font></span>He, when he went away to the upper region, went away having<br>    become Brihaspati and having made the exclamation Vashat<br>    an eater of food. He who hath this knowledge eats food with<br>    the exclamation Vashat as food-eater.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1501410">10</a></font></span>He, when he went away to the Gods, went away having become<br>   Isana and having made Passion an eater of food. He who hath<br>    this knowledge eats food with Passion as food-eater.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1501411">11</a></font></span>He, when he went away to creatures, went away having become<br>   Prajāpati and having made vital breath an eater of food. He who<br>    hath this knowledge eats food with vital breath as foodeater.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1501412">12</a></font></span>He, when he went away to all the intermediate spaces, went<br>    away having become Parameshthin and having made Devotion<br>    an eater of food. He who hath this knowledge eats food with<br>   Devotion as food-eater.</p>
