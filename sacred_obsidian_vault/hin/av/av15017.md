 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1501701">1</a></font></span>His first diffused breath is this Earth.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1501702">2</a></font></span>His second diffused breath is that Firmament.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1501703">3</a></font></span>His third diffused breath is that Heaven.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1501704">4</a></font></span>His fourth diffused breath are those Constellations.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1501705">5</a></font></span>His fifth diffused breath are the Seasons.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1501706">6</a></font></span>His sixth diffused breath are the Season-groups.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1501707">7</a></font></span>His seventh diffused breath is the year.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1501708">8</a></font></span>With one and the same object the Gods go round the Year and<br>    the Seasons follow round the Vrātya.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1501709">9</a></font></span>When they surround the Sun on the day of New Moon, and that<br>    time of Full Moon.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1501710">10</a></font></span>That one immortality of theirs is just an oblation.</p>
