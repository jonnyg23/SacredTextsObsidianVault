 <p><a name="page_305"><font size="1" color="green">p. 305</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">HYMN XLVIII</h3>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2004801">1</a></font></span>The swiftly-moving songs of praise pour on thee streams of vital<br>    strength<br>   As mother cows refresh the calf.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2004802">2</a></font></span>Swift move the bright ones while they blend the Milk with vital<br>    vigour, as<br>   A dame her infant with her heart.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2004803">3</a></font></span>Fair hymns bring glory to the Strong, and Indra-vigour; unto,<br>    me<br>   Fatness and milk and length of days.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2004804">4</a></font></span>This brindled Bull hath come and sat before the Mother in the<br>    east,<br>   Advancing to the Father Heaven.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2004805">5</a></font></span>As expiration from breath she moves along the lucid spheres:<br>   The Bull shines forth through all the sky.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2004806">6</a></font></span>Song is bestowed upon the Bird. It reigns supreme throughout<br>    thirty realms<br>   Throughout the days at break of morn.</p>
