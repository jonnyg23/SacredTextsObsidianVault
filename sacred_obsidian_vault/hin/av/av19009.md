 <p><a name="page_219"><font size="1" color="green">p. 219</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">HYMN IX</h3>  <h4 align="center">A prayer for general protection and prosperity</h4>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1900901">1</a></font></span>Gentle be heaven, gentle be earth, gentle this spacious,<br>    atmosphere.<br>   Gentle be waters as they flow, gentle to us be plants and herbs!<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1900902">2</a></font></span>Gentle be signs of coming change, and that which is and is not.<br>    done!<br>   Gentle be past and future, yea, let all be gracious unto us.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1900903">3</a></font></span>Quickened by Prayer, this Goddess Vāk who standeth in the<br>    highest place,<br>   By whom the awful spell was made, even through her to us be<br>    peace!<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1900904">4</a></font></span>Or, made more keen by Prayer, this mind that standeth in the<br>    highest place,<br>   Whereby the awful spell was made, even through this be peace<br>    to us!<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1900905">5</a></font></span>These five sense-organs with the mind as sixth, sharpened by<br>   Prayer, abiding in my heart,<br>   By which the awful spell was made, even by these be peace to<br>    us.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1900906">6</a></font></span>Favour us Mitra, Varuna, and Vishnu, and Prajāpati! Gracious•<br>    to us be Indra and Brihaspati and Aryaman.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1900907">7</a></font></span>Favour us Mitra, Varuna, Vivasvān, and the Finisher,<br>   Portents on earth and in the air, and planets wandering in<br>    heaven!<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1900908">8</a></font></span>Gracious to us be trembling earth, gracious the flaming meteor<br>    stroke!<br>   Gracious be kine who yield red milk, gracious be earth when<br>    sinking down!<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1900909">9</a></font></span>Gracious be meteor-stricken constellation, gracious to us be<br>    magic spells and witchcraft!<br>   Gracious to us be buried charms, and gracious the meteors and<br>    the portents of the region!<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1900910">10</a></font></span>Kind be the Powers who seize the Moon, with Rāhu be Ādityas<br>    kind!<br>   Favour us Death and Cornet, and Rudras with penetrating.<br>    might!<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1900911">11</a></font></span>Rudras and Vasus favour us, Ādityas, Agnis favour us!<br>   Favour us mighty Rishis, Gods, Goddesses, and Brihaspati!<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1900912">12</a></font></span>Brahma, Dhātar, Prajāpati, Worlds, Vedas, Agnis, Rishis Seven. <a name="page_220"><font size="1" color="green">p. 220</font></a><br>   All these have blessed my happy way. May Indra be my<br>    guardian, may Brahmā protect and shelter me.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1900913">13</a></font></span>May all the Gods protect me, may the Gods united shield me<br>    well.<br>   May all alleviations in the world which the Seven Rishis know.<br>   Be kind and gracious unto me. Bliss and security be mine!<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1900914">14</a></font></span>Earth alleviation, air alleviation, heaven alleviation, waters<br>    alleviation, plants alleviation, trees alleviation, all Gods my al-<br>    leviation, collective Gods my alleviation, alleviation by allevia-<br>    tions. By these alleviations, these universal alleviations, I allay<br>    all that is terrific here, all that is cruel, all that is wicked. This<br>    hath been calmed, this is now auspicious.<br>   Let all be favourable to us.</p>
