 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2011501">1</a></font></span>I from my Father have received deep knowledge of the holy<br>   Law:<br>   I was born like unto the Sun.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2011502">2</a></font></span>After the lore of ancient time I make, like Kanva, beauteous<br>    songs,<br>   And Indra's self gains strength thereby.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2011503">3</a></font></span>Whatever Rishis have not praised thee, Indra, or have lauded<br>    thee,<br>   By me exalted wax thou strong.</p>
