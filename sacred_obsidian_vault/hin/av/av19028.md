 <p><a name="page_234"><font size="1" color="green">p. 234</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">HYMN XXVIII</h3>  <h4 align="center">A charm for the destruction of enemies</h4>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1902801">1</a></font></span>On thee I bind this Amulet for lengthened life and brilliancy.<br>   The rival-quelling Darbha grass that burns the spirit of a foe.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1902802">2</a></font></span>Burning the spirit of the foe, vexing the heart of enemies.<br>   Darbha, on every side, like heat, inflame all evil-hearted men.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1902803">3</a></font></span>O Darbha, burning round like heat, consuming foes, O Amulet,.<br>   Like Indra rending Vala cleave mine adversaries' hearts in twain.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1902804">4</a></font></span>Cleave through. O Darbha, Amulet, my foes', mine adversaries'.<br>    heart.<br>   Rise thou and batter down their heads like growth that coverethl<br>    the earth.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1902805">5</a></font></span>Cleave thou my rivals, Darbha, cleave the men who fain would,<br>    fight with me.<br>   Cleave all who wish me evil, cleave the men who hate me,.<br>   Amulet!<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1902806">6</a></font></span>Wound thou, my rivals, Darbha, etc. (as in 5, substituting.<br>   'wound' for cleave.)<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1902807">7</a></font></span>Tear thou my rivals, Darbha, etc.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1902808">8</a></font></span>How thou my rivals, Darbha, etc.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1902809">9</a></font></span>Carve thou my rivals, Darbha, etc.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1902810">10</a></font></span>Pierce thou my rivals, Darbha, pierce the men who fain would<br>    fight with me.<br>   Pierce those who wish me evil, pierce the men who hate me,<br>    Amulet!</p>
