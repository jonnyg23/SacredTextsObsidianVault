 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Hymns of the Atharva Veda</i>, by Ralph T.H. Griffith, [1895], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">HYMN CXIII</h3>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2011301">1</a></font></span>Both boons—may Indra hitherward turned, listen to this, prayer<br>    of ours,<br>   And, mightiest Maghavan with thought inclined to us come nigh<br>    to drink the Soma juice.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2011302">2</a></font></span>For him, strong independent Ruler, Heaven and Earth have<br>    fashioned forth for power and might.<br>   Thou seatest thee as first among thy peers in place, for thy soul<br>    longs for Soma juice.</p>
