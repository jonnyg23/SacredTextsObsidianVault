 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Hymns of the Atharva Veda</i>, by Ralph T.H. Griffith, [1895], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">HYMN XIX</h3>  <h4 align="center">A hymn recommending the protection of various Gods</h4>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1901901">1</a></font></span>Mitra arose with Earth. To that fort I lead you: enter it,<br>    occupy it. Let it give you protection and defence.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1901902">2</a></font></span>Vāyu arose with Air. To that fort, etc.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1901903">3</a></font></span>Sūrya arose with Heaven. To that fort, etc.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1901904">4</a></font></span>Chandramās arose with the Constellation. To that fort, etc.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1901905">5</a></font></span>Sacrifice arose with Priestly Fees. To that fort, etc.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1901906">6</a></font></span>Ocean arose with the Rivers. To that fort, etc.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1901907">7</a></font></span>Brahma arose with the Brahmachārīs. To that fort, etc.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1901908">8</a></font></span>Indra arose with Might. To that fort, etc. <a name="page_228"><font size="1" color="green">p. 228</font></a><br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1901909">9</a></font></span>The Gods arose with Immortality. To that fort, etc.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1901910">10</a></font></span>Prajāpati arose with Creatures. To that fort I lead you: enter<br>    it, occupy it give you protection and defence.</p>
