 <p><a name="page_61"><font size="1" color="green">p. 61</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">HYMN VII</h3>  <h4 align="center">A glorification of the Uchchhishta or Residue of Sacrifice</h4>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1100701">1</a></font></span>The Residue of Sacrifice containeth name, and from, and world:<br>   Indra and Agni and the whole universe are comprised therein.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1100702">2</a></font></span>The Residue of Sacrifice holdeth Earth, Heaven, and all that is:<br>   The Residue of Sacrifice holdeth sea, waters, Moon, and Wind.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1100703">3</a></font></span>Real, non-real, both are there, Prajāpati, and Death, and<br>    strength:<br>   Thereon depend the worldly ones: in me are glory Dra and Vra.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1100704">4</a></font></span>The firm, the fast, the strong, the hard, Brahma, the All-creating<br>    Ten.<br>   Gods, as a wheel about the nave, are fixed all round the<br>    Residue.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1100705">5</a></font></span>Verse, Song, and Sacrificial Text, chanting, the prelude, and the<br>    laud,<br>   The hum is in the Residue, the tone, the murmur of the psalm.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1100706">6</a></font></span>Within the Residue, like babes unborn, the parts of sacrifice,<br>   Aindrāgne Pāvamāna lie. Mahānāmnī, Mahavrata.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1100707">7</a></font></span>The Vājapeya, Royal Rite, the Agnishoma and its forms,<br>   Hymns, joyfullest with living grass the Asvamedha, are therein,<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1100708">8</a></font></span>Dikshā and Agnyādheya rite that sates the wish, with magic-<br>    hymn,<br>   Suspended rites, long sessions, are contained within the Residue.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1100709">9</a></font></span>Faith fire-oblation, fervent zeal, service, and sacrificial cry,<br>   Guerdon, good works and their reward, are stored within the<br>   Residue.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1100710">10</a></font></span>Sacrifice of one night, or two, Sadya1 kri, Ukthya, and Prakri,<br>   Call, deep-toned summons are therein, fine parts, through lore,<br>    of sacrifice,<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1100711">11</a></font></span>Sacrifice of four nights, of five, of six nights, day and night<br>    conjoined,<br>   Shodai, seven-night sacrifice, all these sprang from the Residue,<br>    these which the Immortal One contains. <a name="page_62"><font size="1" color="green">p. 62</font></a><br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1100712">12</a></font></span>Pratihāra and Nidhanam, the Visvajit, the Abhijit,<br>   The two Sāhnātirātrās and Twelve-day rite are stored therein.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1100713">13</a></font></span>Pleasantness, reverence, peace, and power, strength, vigour,<br>    immortality<br>   All forward wishes are with love satisfied in the Residue.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1100714">14</a></font></span>Nine several oceans, earths, and skies are set within, the Residue,<br>   Bright shines the Sun therein, in me, the Residue, are Day and<br>   Night.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1100715">15</a></font></span>The Residue the Father's sire, who bears this universe, supports<br>   Vishūvān, Upahavya, and all worship offered secretly.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1100716">16</a></font></span>The Father's sire, the Residue, grandson of Spirit, primal Sire,<br>   Lord of the universe, the Bull, dwells on the earth victorious.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1100717">17</a></font></span>Right, truth, dominion, fervent zeal, toil, duty, action, future,<br>    past,<br>   Valour; prosperity, and strength dwell in the Residue in<br>    strength.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1100718">18</a></font></span>Welfare, resolve and energy, the six expanses, kingship, sway,<br>   Prayer, and direction, and the year, oblation, planets, are there-<br>    in;<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1100719">19</a></font></span>And the four Hotars, Apri hymns, the Nivids, and Four-<br>    monthly rites,<br>   Oblations, sacrifices, and animal offerings, and their forms.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1100720">20</a></font></span>Months, half-months, sections of the year, seasons are in the<br>   Residue,<br>   The waters resonant afar, the thunder, and the mighty noise.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1100721">21</a></font></span>Pebbles, sand, stones, and herbs, and plants, and grass are in<br>    the Residue,<br>   Closely embraced and laid therein are lightnings and the clouds<br>    and rain.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1100722">22</a></font></span>Gain, acquisition, and success, fulness, complete prosperity.<br>   Great gain and wealth, are laid, concealed and treasured, in the<br>   Residue.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1100723">23</a></font></span>All things that breathe the breath of life, all creatures that have<br>    eyes to see,<br>   All the celestial Gods whose home is heaven sprang from the<br>   Residue.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1100724">24</a></font></span>Verses, and Songs, and magic hymns, Purāna, sacrificial text.<br>   All the celestial Gods whose home is heaven sprang from the<br>   Residue. <a name="page_63"><font size="1" color="green">p. 63</font></a><br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1100725">25</a></font></span>Inbreath and outbreath, eye and ear, decay and freedom from<br>    decay,<br>   All the celestial Gods whose home is heaven sprang from the<br>    Residue.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1100726">26</a></font></span>All pleasures and enjoyments, all delights and rapturous ecsta-<br>    sies,<br>   All the celestial Gods whose home is heaven sprang from the<br>    Residue.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1100727">27</a></font></span>The Deities, the Fathers, men, Gandharvas, and Apsarases.<br>   6 All the celestial Gods whose home is heaven sprang from the<br>   Residue.</p>
