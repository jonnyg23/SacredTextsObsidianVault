 <p><a name="page_352"><font size="1" color="green">p. 352</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">HYMN CVIII</h3>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2010801">1</a></font></span>O Indra, bring great strength to us, bring valour, Satakratu, thou<br>    most active, bring.<br>   A hero conquering in war.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2010802">2</a></font></span>For, gracious Satakratu, thou hast ever been a mother and a<br>    sire to us,<br>   So now for bliss we pray to thee.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2010803">3</a></font></span>To thee, Strong, Much-invoked who showest forth thy strength,<br>   O Satakratu, do I speak:<br>   So grant thou us heroic might.</p>
