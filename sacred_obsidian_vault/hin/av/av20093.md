 <p><a name="page_340"><font size="1" color="green">p. 340</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">HYMN XCIII</h3>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2009301">1</a></font></span>May our hymns give thee great delight. Display thy bounty,<br>   Thunderer.<br>   Drive off the enemies of prayer.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2009302">2</a></font></span>Crush with thy foot the niggard churls who bring no gifts.<br>   Might art thou:<br>   There is not one to equal thee.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2009303">3</a></font></span>Thou art the Lord of Soma pressed, Soma unpressed is also<br>    thine,<br>   Thou art the Sovran of the folk.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2009304">4</a></font></span>Swaying about the active Ones came nigh to Indra at his birth,<br>   And shared his great heroic might.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2009305">5</a></font></span>Based upon strength and victory and power, O Indra is thy<br>    birth.<br>   Thou, Mighty One, art strong indeed.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2009306">6</a></font></span>Thou art the Vritra-slayer, thou, Indra, hast spread the<br>    firmament:<br>   Thou hast with might upheld the heavens.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2009307">7</a></font></span>Thou, Indra, bearest in thine arms the lightning that accords<br>    with thee,<br>   Whetting thy thunderbolt with might.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2009308">8</a></font></span>Thou, Indra, art pre-eminent over all creatures in thy strength:<br>   Thou hast pervaded every place.</p>
