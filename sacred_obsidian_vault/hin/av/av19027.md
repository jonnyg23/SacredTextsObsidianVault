 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Hymns of the Atharva Veda</i>, by Ralph T.H. Griffith, [1895], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">HYMN XXVII</h3>  <h4 align="center">A benedictory hymn</h4>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1902701">1</a></font></span>Let the Bull guard thee with the kine, the Stallien with the fleet-<br>    foot steeds. <a name="page_233"><font size="1" color="green">p. 233</font></a><br>   Let Vāyu keep thee safe with prayer, and Indra with his mighty<br>    power.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1902702">2</a></font></span>Let Soma guard thee with the plants, Sūrya protect thee with<br>    the stars;<br>   With breath let Wind protect thee, and the Moon, foe-slayer,<br>    with the months.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1902703">3</a></font></span>Three are the earths, they say, and three the heavens, three are<br>    the atmospheres, and four the oceans.<br>   Threefold the hymn of praise, threefold the Waters. Let these<br>    with triple song and triplets guard thee.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1902704">4</a></font></span>Three vaults of heaven, and three seas, three bright, three<br>    stationary ones,<br>   Three Mātarisvans, and three suns, protectors, I arrange for<br>    thee.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1902705">5</a></font></span>Increasing thee with butter I, Agni! with fatness sprinkle thee.<br>   Let not magicians harm the life of Agni or of Moon or Sun.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1902706">6</a></font></span> Let not magicians mar your heat, your vital or diffusive breath.<br>   Brilliant and all-possessing Gods, run ye your course with God-<br>    like power.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1902707">7</a></font></span>Fire they endow with vital breath, Wind is compact, with vital<br>    breath:<br>   With vital breath the Gods produced the Sun whose face turn<br>    every way.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1902708">8</a></font></span>Live with the Life-Creators' life. Die not, live on to lengthened<br>    age.<br>   Live with the breath of men with souls. Submit not to the power<br>    of Death.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1902709">9</a></font></span>The secret treasure of the Gods which Indra, by pathways<br>    travelled by the Gods, discovered,<br>   That gold the Waters with the triplets guarded. May they keep<br>    thee with threebold hymn and triplets.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1902710">10</a></font></span>With friendly thoughts the Deities, three-and thirty, and three<br>    great Powers preserved it in the Waters.<br>   He made heroic powers with the gold that lies upon this Moon.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1902711">11</a></font></span>O ye eleven Gods who are in heaven, accept this sacrifice.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1902712">12</a></font></span>O ye eleven Gods who are in air, accept this sacrifice.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1902713">13</a></font></span>O ye eleven Gods who are on earth, accept this sacrifice.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1902714">14</a></font></span>15 repeated from XIX. 16. 1, 2.</p>
