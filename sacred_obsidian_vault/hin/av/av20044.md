 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2004401">1</a></font></span>Praise Indra whom our songs must laud, great Sovran of man-<br>    kind, the Chief<br>   Most liberal who controlleth men.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2004402">2</a></font></span>In whom the hymns of praise delight, and all the glory-giving<br>    songs,<br>   Like the flood's longing for the sea. <a name="page_303"><font size="1" color="green">p. 303</font></a><br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2004403">3</a></font></span>Him I invite with eulogy, best King, effective in the fight,<br>   Strong for the gain of mighty spoil.</p>
