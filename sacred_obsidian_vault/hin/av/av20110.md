 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Hymns of the Atharva Veda</i>, by Ralph T.H. Griffith, [1895], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">HYMN CX</h3>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2011001">1</a></font></span>For Indra, lover of carouse, loud be our songs about the juice:<br>   Let poets sing the hymn of praise.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2011002">2</a></font></span>We summon Indra to the draught, in whom all glories rest, in<br>    whom<br>   The seven communities rejoice.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2011003">3</a></font></span>By the three Soma jars the Gods span sacrifice that stirs the<br>    mind:<br>   Let our songs aid and prosper it.</p>
