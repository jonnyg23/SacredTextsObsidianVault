 <p><a name="page_154"><font size="1" color="green">p. 154</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">HYMN VI</h3>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1500601">1</a></font></span>He went his way to the region of the nadir. Earth and Agni and<br>    herbs and trees and shrubs and plants followed him. He who<br>    possesses this knowledge becomes the dear home of Earth and<br>   Agni and herbs and trees and shrubs and plants.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1500602">2</a></font></span>He went his way to the region of the zenith. Right and Truth<br>    and Sun and Moon and Stars followed him. He who possesses<br>    this knowledge becomes, etc., as in verse 1. <i>mutatis mutandis</i>.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1500603">3</a></font></span>He went away to the last region. Richas, Sāmans Yajus formulas<br>    and Devotion followed him. He who, etc., as above.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1500604">4</a></font></span>He went away to the great region. Itihāsa and Purāna and<br>   Gāthās and Nārāsansis followed him. He who, etc.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1500605">5</a></font></span>He went away to the supreme region. The Ēhavaniya, Gārha-<br>    patya, and Southern Fires, and Sacrifice, and Sacrificer, and<br>    sacrificial victims followed him. He who, etc.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1500606">6</a></font></span>He went away to the unindicated region. The Seasons, groups of<br>    seasons, the worlds and their inhabitants, the months and<br>    half-months, and Day and Night followed him. He who, etc.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1500607">7</a></font></span>He went away to the unfrequented region. Thence he thought<br>    that he should not return. Diti and Aditi and Idā and Indrāni<br>    followed him. He who, etc.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1500608">8</a></font></span>He went away to the regions. Virāj and all the Gods and all the<br>   Deities followed him. He who, etc.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1500609">9</a></font></span>He went away to all the intermediate spaces. Prajāpati and<br>   Parameshthin and the Father and the Great Father followed<br>    him. He who possesses this knowledge becomes the beloved<br>    home of Prajāpati and Parameshthin and the Father and the<br>   Great Father.</p>
