 <p><a name="page_211"><font size="1" color="green">p. 211</font></a></p>  <h1 align="center">BOOK XIX</h1>  <h3 align="center">HYMN I</h3>  <h4 align="center">An accompaniment to the offering of a Mixt Oblation</h4>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1900101">1</a></font></span>Let the streams flow together, let the winds and birds assembled<br>    come.<br>   Strengthen this sacrifice of mine, ye singers. I offer up a duly<br>    mixt oblation.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1900102">2</a></font></span>O Burnt Oblations, aid, and ye, Blent Offerings, this my<br>    sacrifice.<br>   Strengthen this sacrifice of mine, ye singers. I offer up a duly<br>    mixt oblation.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1900103">3</a></font></span>Each several form, each several force I seize, and compass round<br>    this man.<br>   May the Four Quarters strengthen this my sacrifice. I offer up a<br>    duly mixt oblation.</p>
