 <p><a name="page_262"><font size="1" color="green">p. 262</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">HYMN LXV</h3>  <h4 align="center">A hymn to Agni identified with the Sun</h4>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1906501">1</a></font></span>A Golden Eagle thou hast soared with light to heaven. Those<br>    who would harm thee as thou fliest skyward.<br>   Beat down, O Jātavedas, with thy fury. The strong hath feared:<br>    to heaven mount up with light, O Sūrya.</p>
