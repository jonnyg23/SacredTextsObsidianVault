 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1600301">1</a></font></span>I am the head of riches. Fain would I be the head of mine<br>    equals.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1600302">2</a></font></span>Let not Ruja and Vena desert me. Let not the Head and the<br>   Preserver forsake me.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1600303">3</a></font></span>Let not the Boiler and the Cup fail tme: let not the Supporter<br>    and the Sustainer abandon me.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1600304">4</a></font></span>Let not Unyoking and the Moist-fellied car desert me: let not<br>    the Sender of Moisture and Matarisvan forsake me.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1600305">5</a></font></span>Brihaspati is my soul, he who is called the Friend of man, dear<br>    to my heart.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1600306">6</a></font></span>My heart is free from sorrow; spacious is my dwelling-place. I<br>    am the sea in capacity.</p>
