 <p><a name="page_156"><font size="1" color="green">p. 156</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">HYMN X</h3>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1501001">1</a></font></span>So let the King, to whose house the Vrātya who possesses this<br>    knowledge comes as a guest.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1501002">2</a></font></span>Honour him as superior to himself. So he Both not act against<br>    the interests of his princely rank or his kingdom.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1501003">3</a></font></span>From him, verily, sprang Priesthood and Royalty. They said,<br>   Into whom shall we enter?<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1501004">4</a></font></span>Let Priesthood enter into Brihaspati, and Royalty into Indra,<br>    was the answer.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1501005">5</a></font></span>Hence Priesthood entered into Brihaspati and Royalty into<br>   Indra.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1501006">6</a></font></span>Now this Earth is Brihaspati, and Heaven is Indra.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1501007">7</a></font></span>Now this Agni is Priesthood, and yonder Sun is Royalty.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1501008">8</a></font></span>Priesthood comes to him, and he becomes endowed with priestly<br>    lustre.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1501009">9</a></font></span>Who knows that Earth is Brihaspati and Agni Priesthood.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1501010">10</a></font></span>Great power comes to him and he becomes endowed with great<br>    power.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1501011">11</a></font></span>Who knows that Āditya is Royalty and that Heaven is Indra.</p>
