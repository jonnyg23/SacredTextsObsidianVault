 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1501801">1</a></font></span>Of that Vrātya.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1501802">2</a></font></span>The right eye is the Sun and the left eye is the Moon.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1501803">3</a></font></span>His right ear is Agni and his left ear is Pavamāna.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1501804">4</a></font></span>Day and Night are his nostrils. Diti and Aditi are his head and<br>    skull.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1501805">5</a></font></span>By day the Vrātya is turned westward, by night he is turned<br>    eastward. Worship to the Vrātya!</p>
