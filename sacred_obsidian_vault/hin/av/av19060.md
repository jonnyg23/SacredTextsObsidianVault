 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Hymns of the Atharva Veda</i>, by Ralph T.H. Griffith, [1895], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">HYMN LX</h3>  <h4 align="center">A prayer for perfect bodily and mental health and vigour</h4>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1906001">1</a></font></span>May I have voice in my mouth, breath in my nostrils, sight in<br>    mine eyes, hearing in mine ears, hair that hath not turned<br>    gray, teeth free from yellowness, and much strength in mine<br>    arms.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1906002">2</a></font></span>May I have power in my thighs, swiftness in my legs, stedfast-<br>    ness in my feet. May all my members be uninjured and my<br>    soul unimpaired.</p>
