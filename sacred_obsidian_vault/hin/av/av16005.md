 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1600501">1</a></font></span>We know thine origin, O Sleep. Thou art the son of Grahi, the<br>    minister of Yama. As such preserve us from<br>    the evil dream.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1600502">2</a></font></span>We know thine origin, O Sleep. Thou art the son of Misery,<br>    etc.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1600504">4</a></font></span>We know thine origin, O Sleep. Thou art the son of Defeat etc.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1600506">6</a></font></span>We know thine origin, O Sleep.
