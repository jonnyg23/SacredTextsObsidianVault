 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Hymns of the Atharva Veda</i>, by Ralph T.H. Griffith, [1895], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">HYMN CXI</h3>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2011101">1</a></font></span>If, Indra, thou drink Soma by Vishnu's or Trita Āptya's side,<br>   Or with the Maruts take delight in flowing drops;<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2011102">2</a></font></span>Or, Sakra, if thou gladden thee afar or in the sea of air,<br>   Rejoice thee in this juice of ours, in flowing drops.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2011103">3</a></font></span>Or, Lord of Heroes, if thou aid the worshipper who sheds the<br>    juice,<br>   Or him whose laud delights thee, and his flowing drops.</p>
