 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Hymns of the Atharva Veda</i>, by Ralph T.H. Griffith, [1895], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">HYMN VII</h3>  <h4 align="center">A Prayer to the Lunar Mansions and other Powers for protection and prosperity</h4>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1900701">1</a></font></span>The brilliant lights shining in heaven together, which through<br>    the world glide on with rapid motion.<br>   And Days, and Firmament with songs I worship, seeking the<br>   Twenty-eight-fold for its favour.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1900702">2</a></font></span>Krittikās, Rohinī be swift to hear me! Let Mrigasiras bless me,<br>    help me Ārdrā!<br>   Punarvasu and Sūnritā, fair Pushya, the Sun, Asleshās, Maghā<br>    lead me onward!<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1900703">3</a></font></span>My bliss be Svāti and benignant Chitrā, my right First Phalgunis <a name="page_217"><font size="1" color="green">p. 217</font></a><br>    and present Hasta.<br>   Rādhas, Visākhas, gracious Anurādhā, Jyeshthā and happy-<br>    starred uninjured Mūla.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1900704">4</a></font></span>Food shall be earlier Ashādhas grant me; let those that follow<br>    bring me strength and vigour;<br>   With virtuous merit Abhijit endow me! Sravana and Sravishthās<br>    make me prosper.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1900705">5</a></font></span>Satabhishak afford me ample freedom, and both the Proshtha-<br>    padas guard me safely.<br>   Revati and the Asvayujas bring me luck, and the Bharanis<br>    abundant riches!</p>
