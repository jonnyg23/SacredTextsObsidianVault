 <p><a name="page_308"><font size="1" color="green">p. 308</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">HYMN LII</h3>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2005201">1</a></font></span>We compass thee like waters, we whose grass is trimmed and<br>   Soma pressed.<br>   Here where the filter pours its stream thy worshipper round<br>    thee, O Vritra-slayer, sit.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2005202">2</a></font></span>Men, Vasu! by the Soma, with lauds call thee to the foremost<br>    place.<br>   When comest thou athirst unto the juice as home, O Indra, like<br>    a bellowing bull?</p>
