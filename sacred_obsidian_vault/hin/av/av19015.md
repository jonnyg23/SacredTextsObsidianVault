 <p><a name="page_225"><font size="1" color="green">p. 225</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">HYMN XV</h3>  <h4 align="center">A prayer for peace and security</h4>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1901501">1</a></font></span>Indra, give us security from that whereof we are afraid.<br>   Help us, O Maghavan, let thy succour grant us this: drive foes<br>    and enemies afar.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1901502">2</a></font></span>We call on Indra, on the liberal giver: we will be prosperous in<br>    men and cattle.<br>   Let not the hosts of cruel fiends approach us. Drive of the<br>   Druhs to every side, O Indra.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1901503">3</a></font></span>Best, making household wealth increase. Indra our saviour, kills<br>    the foe.<br>   May he from outmost point be our potector, and from the<br>    centre and from west and eastward.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1901504">4</a></font></span>Lead us to ample room. O thou who knowest, to happiness<br>    security, and sunlight.<br>   Strong, Indra, are the arms of thee the mighty: may we betake<br>    us to their lofty shelter.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1901505">5</a></font></span>May air's mid-region give us peace and safety, safety may both<br>    these, Heaven and Earth, afford us.<br>   Security be ours from west, from eastward, from north and<br>    south may we be free from danger.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1901506">6</a></font></span>Safety be ours from friend and from the unfriendly, safety from<br>    what we know and what we know not.<br>   Safety be ours by night and in the day-time! friendly to me be<br>    all my hopes and wishes!</p>
