 <p><a name="page_300"><font size="1" color="green">p. 300</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">HYMN XXXIX</h3>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2003901">1</a></font></span>For you, from every side, we call Indra away from other men:<br>   Ours, and none others,’ let him be.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2003902">2</a></font></span>In Soma's ecstasy Indra spread the firmament and realms of<br>    light.<br>   When he cleft Vala limb from limb.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2003903">3</a></font></span>Showing the hidden cows he draye them forth for the Angirases,<br>   And Vala he cast headlong down.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2003904">4</a></font></span>By Indra were the luminous realms of heaven established and<br>    secured,<br>   Firm and immovable from their place.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2003900">0</a></font></span>5. Indra, thy laud moves quickly like a joyous wave of waters:<br>    bright.<br>   Have shone the drops that gladden thee.</p>
