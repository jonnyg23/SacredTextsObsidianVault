 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Hymns of the Atharva Veda</i>, by Ralph T.H. Griffith, [1895], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">HYMN XXVIII</h3>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2002801">1</a></font></span>In Soma's ecstasy Indra spread the firmament and realms of<br>    light.<br>   When he cleft Vala limb from limb.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2002802">2</a></font></span>Showing the hidden he draye forth the cows for the Angirases,<br>   And Vala he cast headlong down.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2002803">3</a></font></span>By Indra were the luminous realms of heaven established and<br>    secured.<br>   Firm and immovable in their place.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2002804">4</a></font></span>Indra, thy laud moves quickly like a joyous wave of water-floods.<br>   Bright shine the drops that gladden thee.</p>
