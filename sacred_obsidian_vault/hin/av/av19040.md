 <p><a name="page_244"><font size="1" color="green">p. 244</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">HYMN XL</h3>  <h4 align="center">A prayer for pardon of error in sacrifice, and for wisdom, strength, and life</h4>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1904001">1</a></font></span>For each defect of mine in voice and spirit I have approached<br>   One vehement and ardent.<br>   With all the Deities, fully approving, Brihaspati supply the<br>    want!<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1904002">2</a></font></span>Disturb ye not our intellect, O Waters, nor the power of prayer_<br>   Glide on your way, strength-giving, invocated: may I be vigor-<br>    ous and wise.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1904003">3</a></font></span>Mar not our consecrating rite, our intellect, or fervent zeal.<br>   Gracious to us for lengthened life, propitious let the Mothers<br>    be.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1904004">4</a></font></span>Vouchsafe to us, ye Asvins twain, such strength as, with atten-<br>    dant light,<br>   May through the darkness carry us.</p>
