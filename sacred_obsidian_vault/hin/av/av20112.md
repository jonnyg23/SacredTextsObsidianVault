 <p><a name="page_354"><font size="1" color="green">p. 354</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">HYMN CXII</h3>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2011201">1</a></font></span>Whatever, Vritra-slayer! thou Sūrya, hast risen upon to-day,<br>   That, Indra, all is in thy power.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2011202">2</a></font></span>When, Mighty One, Lord of the Brave, thou thinkest, I shall<br>    never die,<br>   That thought of thine is true indeed.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2011203">3</a></font></span>Thou, Indra, goest unto all Soma libations shed for thee,<br>   Both far away and near at hand.</p>
