 <p><a name="page_167"><font size="1" color="green">p. 167</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">HYMN VII</h3>  <h4 align="center">An imprecation on an enemy</h4>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1600701">1</a></font></span>Herewith I pierce this man. With poverty I pierce him. With<br>    disappearance I pierce him. With defeat I pierce him. With<br>   Grāhi I pierce him. With darkness I pierce him.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1600702">2</a></font></span>I summon him with the awful cruel orders of the Gods.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1600703">3</a></font></span>I place him between Vaisvānara's jaws.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1600704">4</a></font></span>Thus or otherwise let her swallow him up.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1600705">5</a></font></span>Him who hates us may his soul hate, and may he whom we<br>    hate hate himself.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1600706">6</a></font></span>We scorch out of heaven and earth and firmament the man who<br>    hates us.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1600707">7</a></font></span>Suyāman son of Chakshus.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1600708">8</a></font></span>Here I wipe away the evil dream on the descendant of Such-an-<br>    one, son of Such-a-woman.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1600709">9</a></font></span>Whatsoever I have met with, whether at dusk or during early<br>    night,<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1600710">10</a></font></span>Whether waking or sleeping, whether by day or by night.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1600711">11</a></font></span>Whether I meet with it day by day, from that do I bribe him<br>    away.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1600712">12</a></font></span>Slay him; rejoice in this; crush his ribs.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1600713">13</a></font></span>Let him not live. Let the breath of life forsake him.</p>
