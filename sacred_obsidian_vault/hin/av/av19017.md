 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Hymns of the Atharva Veda</i>, by Ralph T.H. Griffith, [1895], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">HYMN XVII</h3>  <h4 align="center">A prayer for protection from dangers in all directions</h4>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1901701">1</a></font></span>Agni from eastward guard me with the Vasus! To him I go, in<br>    him I rest: this fort I seek for refuge.<br>   May he protect me, may he be my guardian. I give my soul—All<br>    hail! into his keeping.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1901702">2</a></font></span>Vāyu with Air protect me from this region. To him I go, etc.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1901703">3</a></font></span>May Soma from the south, with Rudras, guard me. To him, etc.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1901704">4</a></font></span>Varuna with Ādityas guard me from this region! To him etc.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1901705">5</a></font></span>Sūrya with Earth and Heaven from the western region guard me<br>    well. To him, etc.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1901706">6</a></font></span>May Waters joined with Plants protect me from this region. To<br>    them I go, in them I rest: this fort I seek for refuge.<br>   May they protect me, may they be my guardians. I give my<br>    soul—All hail! —into their keeping.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1901707">7</a></font></span>May Visvakarman with the Seven Rishis be my protector from<br>    the northern region. To him, etc.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1901708">8</a></font></span>May Indra, Marut-girt, protect me from this region. To him,<br>    etc.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1901709">9</a></font></span>Prajāpati, of generative power, with the Pratishlhā save me from<br>    the nadir! To him, etc.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1901710">10</a></font></span>Brihaspati, joined by the Visvedevas, protect me from the region<br>    of the zenith! To him, I go, in him I rest; this fort I seek<br>    for refuge.<br>   May he protect me, may he be my guardian. I give my soul—<br>   All hail!—into his keeping.</p>
