 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2004001">1</a></font></span>Mayest thou verily be seen coming by fearless Indra's side:<br>   Both joyous: equal in your sheen.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2004002">2</a></font></span>With Indra's well-beloved hosts, the blameless, hastening to<br>    heaven,<br>   The sacrificer cries aloud.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2004003">3</a></font></span>Thereafter they, as is their wont, threw off the state of babes<br>    unborn,<br>   Assuming sacrificial name.</p>
