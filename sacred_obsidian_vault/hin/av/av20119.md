 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Hymns of the Atharva Veda</i>, by Ralph T.H. Griffith, [1895], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">HYMN CXIX</h3>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2011901">1</a></font></span>An ancient praise-song hath been sung: to Indra have ye said<br>    the prayer.<br>   They have sung many a Brihatī of sacrifice, poured forth th,<br>    worshipper's many thoughts.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2011902">2</a></font></span>In zealous haste the singers have sung forth a song distilling oil<br>    and rich in sweets.<br>   Riches have spread among us, and heroic strength; with us are<br>    flowing Soma drops.</p>
