 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2006501">1</a></font></span>Come, sing we praise to Indra, friends! the Hero who deserves<br>    the laud,<br>   Him who with none to aid o'ercomes all tribes of men. <a name="page_316"><font size="1" color="green">p. 316</font></a><br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2006502">2</a></font></span>To him who wins the kine, who keeps no cattle back, celestial<br>   God,<br>   Speak wondrous speech more sweet than butter and than mead.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2006503">3</a></font></span>Whose hero powers are measureless, whose bounty ne'er may be<br>    surpassed,<br>   Whose liberality, like light, is over all.</p>
