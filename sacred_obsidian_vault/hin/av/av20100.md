 <p><a name="page_347"><font size="1" color="green">p. 347</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">HYMN C</h3>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2010001">1</a></font></span>Now have we, Indra, Friend of Song, sent our great wishes<br>    forth to thee.<br>   Coming like floods that follow floods.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2010002">2</a></font></span>As rivers swell the ocean, so, Hero, our prayers increase thy<br>    might,<br>   Though of thyself, O Thunderer, waxing day by day.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2010003">3</a></font></span>With holy song they bind to the broad wide-yoked car the bay<br>    steeds of the rapid God,<br>   Bearers of India, yoked by prayer.</p>
