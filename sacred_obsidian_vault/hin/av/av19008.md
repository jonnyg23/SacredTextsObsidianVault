 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1900801">1</a></font></span>Benign to me be all those Lunar Mansions to which the Moon<br>    as he moves on doth honour.<br>   All that are in the sky, the air, the waters, on earth, on moun-<br>    tains, in the heavenly regions.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1900802">2</a></font></span>Propitious, mighty, let the eight-and-twenty together deal me<br>    out my share of profit.<br>   Profit and wealth be mine, and wealth and profit! To Day and<br>   Night be adoration rendered!<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1900803">3</a></font></span>Fair be my sunset, fair my morn and evening and day with.<br>    lucky chase and happy omens;<br>   With blessing and success, immortal Agni, go to the mortal and.<br>    return rejoicing.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1900804">4</a></font></span>Excitement and invoking cry, ill-omened sneezing and reproof,<br>   All sounds of empty pitchers send into the distance, Savitar!<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1900805">5</a></font></span>May we escape an evil sneeze, enjoy the sneeze of happy luck,<br>   And may our nostrils smelling what is pleasant pour forth<br>    fragrant scent.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1900806">6</a></font></span>These flames of Brāhmanaspati borne to all quarters in the-<br>    wind,<br>   Turn them all hither, Indra, and make them most gracious unto,<br>    me.</p>
