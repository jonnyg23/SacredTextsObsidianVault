 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2010601">1</a></font></span>That lofty energy of thine, thy strength and thine intelligence,<br>   Thy thunderbolt for which we long, our wish makes keen.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2010602">2</a></font></span>O Indra, heaven and earth augment thy manly power and thy<br>    renown,<br>   The waters and the mountains stir and urge thee on. <a name="page_350"><font size="1" color="green">p. 350</font></a><br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2010603">3</a></font></span>Vishnu, the lofty Ruling Power, Varuna, Mitra sing thy praise:<br>   In thee the Maruts' company hath great delight.</p>
