 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Hymns of the Atharva Veda</i>, by Ralph T.H. Griffith, [1895], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">HYMN XLIII</h3>  <h4 align="center">In praise of Brahma, Prayer, or Devotion</h4>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1904301">1</a></font></span>Whither men versed in Brahma go, with fervour and the cleans-<br>    ing rite,<br>   Thither let Agni lead me, let Agni give me intelligence, All hail<br>    to Agni!<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1904302">2</a></font></span>Whither etc.<br>   Thither let Vāyu lead me, let Vāyu vouchsafe me vital breath.<br>   All hail to Vāyu!<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1904303">3</a></font></span>Whither, etc.<br>   Thither let Sūrya lead me, let Sūrya vouchsafe me power of<br>    sight. All hail to Sürya<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1904304">4</a></font></span>Whither, etc.<br>   Thither let Chandra lead me, let Chandra vouchsafe me intellect.<br>   All hail to Chandra!<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1904305">5</a></font></span>Whither, etc.<br>   Thither let Soma lead me, let Soma vouchsafe me vital sap. All<br>    hail to Soma!<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1904306">6</a></font></span>Whither, etc. <a name="page_246"><font size="1" color="green">p. 246</font></a><br>   Thither let Indra lead me, let Indra bestow upon me power. All<br>    hail to Indra!<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1904307">7</a></font></span>Whither, etc.<br>   Thither, let Waters lead me, let the Waters give me deathless<br>    life. All hail to Waters!<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1904308">8</a></font></span>Whither, etc.<br>   Thither let Brahma lead me, let Brahma give Brahma unto me.<br>   All hail to Brahma!</p>
