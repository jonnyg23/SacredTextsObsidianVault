 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Hymns of the Atharva Veda</i>, by Ralph T.H. Griffith, [1895], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">HYMN XXIV</h3>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2002401">1</a></font></span>Come to the juice that we have pressed, to Soma, Indra! blent<br>    with milk:<br>   Come, favouring us, thy bay-drawn car!<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2002402">2</a></font></span>Come, Indra, to this gladdening drink, placed on the grass,<br>    pressed out with stones:<br>   Wilt thou not drink thy fill thereof?<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2002403">3</a></font></span>To Indra have my songs of praise gone forth, thus rapidly sent<br>    hence,<br>   To turn him to the Soma-draught.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2002404">4</a></font></span>Hither with songs of praise we call Indra to drink the Soma.<br>    juice:<br>   Will he not come to us by lauds?<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2002405">5</a></font></span>Indra, these Somas are expressed, Take them within thy belly,<br>   Lord Of Hundred Powers, thou Prince of wealth.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2002406">6</a></font></span>We know thee winner of the spoil and resolute in battles, Sage!<br>   Therefore thy blessing we implore.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2002407">7</a></font></span>Borne hither by thy stallions, drink, Indra, this juice which we<br>    have pressed,<br>   Mingled with barley and with milk.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2002408">8</a></font></span>Indra, for thee in thine own place I urge the Soma for thy<br>    draught: <a name="page_284"><font size="1" color="green">p. 284</font></a><br>   Deep in thy heart let it remain.<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2002409">9</a></font></span>We call on thee, the Ancient One, Indra, to drink the Soma<br>    juice,<br>   We Kusikas who seek thine aid.</p>
