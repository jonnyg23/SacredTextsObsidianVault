 <h3 align="CENTER">Book 6<br>Chapter 116
 </h3><table width="100%">
 <td width="50%">
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">1</font>
  śirasy añjalim ādāya kaikeyīnandivardhanaḥ<br>
   babhāṣe bharato jyeṣṭhaṃ rāmaṃ satyaparākramam<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">2</font>
  pūjitā māmikā mātā dattaṃ rājyam idaṃ mama<br>
   tad dadāmi punas tubhyaṃ yathā tvam adadā mama<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">3</font>
  dhuram ekākinā nyastām ṛṣabheṇa balīyasā<br>
   kiśoravad guruṃ bhāraṃ na voḍhum aham utsahe<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">4</font>
  vārivegena mahatā bhinnaḥ setur iva kṣaran<br>
   durbandhanam idaṃ manye rājyacchidram asaṃvṛtam<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">5</font>
  gatiṃ khara ivāśvasya haṃsasyeva ca vāyasaḥ<br>
   nānvetum utsahe deva tava mārgam ariṃdama<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">6</font>
  yathā ca ropito vṛkṣo jātaś cāntarniveśane<br>
   mahāṃś ca sudurāroho mahāskandhaḥ praśākhavān<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">7</font>
  śīryeta puṣpito bhūtvā na phalāni pradarśayet<br>
   tasya nānubhaved arthaṃ yasya hetoḥ sa ropyate<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">8</font>
  eṣopamā mahābāho tvam arthaṃ vettum arhasi<br>
   yady asmān manujendra tvaṃ bhaktān bhṛtyān na śādhi hi<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">9</font>
  jagad adyābhiṣiktaṃ tvām anupaśyatu sarvataḥ<br>
   pratapantam ivādityaṃ madhyāhne dīptatejasaṃ<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">10</font>
  tūryasaṃghātanirghoṣaiḥ kāñcīnūpuranisvanaiḥ<br>
    madhurair gītaśabdaiś ca pratibudhyasva śeṣva ca<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">11</font>
  yāvad āvartate cakraṃ yāvatī ca vasuṃdharā<br>
    tāvat tvam iha sarvasya svāmitvam abhivartaya<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">12</font>
  bharatasya vacaḥ śrutvā rāmaḥ parapuraṃjayaḥ<br>
    tatheti pratijagrāha niṣasādāsane śubhe<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">13</font>
  tataḥ śatrughnavacanān nipuṇāḥ śmaśruvardhakāḥ<br>
    sukhahastāḥ suśīghrāś ca rāghavaṃ paryupāsata<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">14</font>
  pūrvaṃ tu bharate snāte lakṣmaṇe ca mahābale<br>
    sugrīve vānarendre ca rākṣasendre vibhīṣaṇe<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">15</font>
  viśodhitajaṭaḥ snātaś citramālyānulepanaḥ<br>
    mahārhavasanopetas tasthau tatra śriyā jvalan<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">16</font>
  pratikarma ca rāmasya kārayām āsa vīryavān<br>
    lakṣmaṇasya ca lakṣmīvān ikṣvākukulavardhanaḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">17</font>
  pratikarma ca sītāyāḥ sarvā daśarathastriyaḥ<br>
    ātmanaiva tadā cakrur manasvinyo manoharam<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">18</font>
  tato rāghavapatnīnāṃ sarvāsām eva śobhanam<br>
    cakāra yatnāt kausalyā prahṛṣṭā putravatsalā<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">19</font>
  tataḥ śatrughnavacanāt sumantro nāma sārathiḥ<br>
    yojayitvābhicakrāma rathaṃ sarvāṅgaśobhanam<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">20</font>
  arkamaṇḍalasaṃkāśaṃ divyaṃ dṛṣṭvā rathaṃ sthitam<br>
    āruroha mahābāhū rāmaḥ satyaparākramaḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">21</font>
  ayodhyāyāṃ tu sacivā rājño daśarathasya ye<br>
    purohitaṃ puraskṛtya mantrayām āsur arthavat<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">22</font>
  mantrayan rāmavṛddhyarthaṃ vṛttyarthaṃ nagarasya ca<br>
    sarvam evābhiṣekārthaṃ jayārhasya mahātmanaḥ<br>
    kartum arhatha rāmasya yad yan maṅgalapūrvakam<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">23</font>
  iti te mantriṇaḥ sarve saṃdiśya tu purohitam<br>
    nagarān niryayus tūrṇaṃ rāmadarśanabuddhayaḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">24</font>
  hariyuktaṃ sahasrākṣo ratham indra ivānaghaḥ<br>
    prayayau ratham āsthāya rāmo nagaram uttamam<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">25</font>
  jagrāha bharato raśmīñ śatrughnaś chatram ādade<br>
    lakṣmaṇo vyajanaṃ tasya mūrdhni saṃparyavījayat<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">26</font>
  śvetaṃ ca vālavyajanaṃ sugrīvo vānareśvaraḥ<br>
    aparaṃ candrasaṃkāśaṃ rākṣasendro vibhīṣaṇaḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">27</font>
  ṛṣisaṃghair tadākāśe devaiś ca samarudgaṇaiḥ<br>
    stūyamānasya rāmasya śuśruve madhuradhvaniḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">28</font>
  tataḥ śatruṃjayaṃ nāma kuñjaraṃ parvatopamam<br>
    āruroha mahātejāḥ sugrīvo vānareśvaraḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">29</font>
  navanāgasahasrāṇi yayur āsthāya vānarāḥ<br>
    mānuṣaṃ vigrahaṃ kṛtvā sarvābharaṇabhūṣitāḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">30</font>
  śaṅkhaśabdapraṇādaiś ca dundubhīnāṃ ca nisvanaiḥ<br>
    prayayū puruṣavyāghras tāṃ purīṃ harmyamālinīm<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">31</font>
  dadṛśus te samāyāntaṃ rāghavaṃ sapuraḥsaram<br>
    virājamānaṃ vapuṣā rathenātirathaṃ tadā<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">32</font>
  te vardhayitvā kākutsthaṃ rāmeṇa pratinanditāḥ<br>
    anujagmur mahātmānaṃ bhrātṛbhiḥ parivāritam<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">33</font>
  amātyair brāhmaṇaiś caiva tathā prakṛtibhir vṛtaḥ<br>
    śriyā viruruce rāmo nakṣatrair iva candramāḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">34</font>
  sa purogāmibhis tūryais tālasvastikapāṇibhiḥ<br>
    pravyāharadbhir muditair maṅgalāni yayau vṛtaḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">35</font>
  akṣataṃ jātarūpaṃ ca gāvaḥ kanyās tathā dvijāḥ<br>
    narā modakahastāś ca rāmasya purato yayuḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">36</font>
  sakhyaṃ ca rāmaḥ sugrīve prabhāvaṃ cānilātmaje<br>
    vānarāṇāṃ ca tat karma vyācacakṣe 'tha mantriṇām<br>
    śrutvā ca vismayaṃ jagmur ayodhyāpuravāsinaḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">37</font>
  dyutimān etad ākhyāya rāmo vānarasaṃvṛtaḥ<br>
    hṛṣṭapuṣṭajanākīrṇām ayodhyāṃ praviveśa ha<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">38</font>
  tato hy abhyucchrayan paurāḥ patākās te gṛhe gṛhe<br>
    aikṣvākādhyuṣitaṃ ramyam āsasāda pitur gṛham<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">39</font>
  pitur bhavanam āsādya praviśya ca mahātmanaḥ<br>
    kausalyāṃ ca sumitrāṃ ca kaikeyīṃ cābhyavādayat<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">40</font>
  athābravīd rājaputro bharataṃ dharmiṇāṃ varam<br>
    athopahitayā vācā madhuraṃ raghunandanaḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">41</font>
  yac ca madbhavanaṃ śreṣṭhaṃ sāśokavanikaṃ mahat<br>
    muktāvaidūryasaṃkīrṇaṃ sugrīvasya nivedaya<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">42</font>
  tasya tadvacanaṃ śrutvā bharataḥ satyavikramaḥ<br>
    pāṇau gṛhītvā sugrīvaṃ praviveśa tam ālayam<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">43</font>
  tatas tailapradīpāṃś ca paryaṅkāstaraṇāni ca<br>
    gṛhītvā viviśuḥ kṣipraṃ śatrughnena pracoditāḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">44</font>
  uvāca ca mahātejāḥ sugrīvaṃ rāghavānujaḥ<br>
    abhiṣekāya rāmasya dūtān ājñāpaya prabho<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">45</font>
  sauvarṇān vānarendrāṇāṃ caturṇāṃ caturo ghaṭān<br>
    dadau kṣipraṃ sa sugrīvaḥ sarvaratnavibhūṣitān<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">46</font>
  yathā pratyūṣasamaye caturṇāṃ sāgarāmbhasām<br>
    pūrṇair ghaṭaiḥ pratīkṣadhvaṃ tathā kuruta vānarāḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">47</font>
  evam uktā mahātmāno vānarā vāraṇopamāḥ<br>
    utpetur gaganaṃ śīghraṃ garuḍā iva śīghragāḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">48</font>
  jāmbavāṃś ca hanūmāṃś ca vegadarśī ca vānaraḥ<br>
    ṛṣabhaś caiva kalaśāñ jalapūrṇān athānayan<br>
    nadīśatānāṃ pañcānāṃ jale kumbhair upāharan<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">49</font>
  pūrvāt samudrāt kalaśaṃ jalapūrṇam athānayat<br>
    suṣeṇaḥ sattvasaṃpannaḥ sarvaratnavibhūṣitam<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">50</font>
  ṛṣabho dakṣiṇāt tūrṇaṃ samudrāj jalam āharat<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">51</font>
  raktacandanakarpūraiḥ saṃvṛtaṃ kāñcanaṃ ghaṭam<br>
    gavayaḥ paścimāt toyam ājahāra mahārṇavāt<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">52</font>
  ratnakumbhena mahatā śītaṃ mārutavikramaḥ<br>
    uttarāc ca jalaṃ śīghraṃ garuḍānilavikramaḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">53</font>
  abhiṣekāya rāmasya śatrughnaḥ sacivaiḥ saha<br>
    purohitāya śreṣṭhāya suhṛdbhyaś ca nyavedayat<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">54</font>
  tataḥ sa prayato vṛddho vasiṣṭho brāhmaṇaiḥ saha<br>
    rāmaṃ ratnamayo pīṭhe sahasītaṃ nyaveśayat<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">55</font>
  vasiṣṭho vāmadevaś ca jābālir atha kāśyapaḥ<br>
    kātyāyanaḥ suyajñaś ca gautamo vijayas tathā<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">56</font>
  abhyaṣiñcan naravyāghraṃ prasannena sugandhinā<br>
    salilena sahasrākṣaṃ vasavo vāsavaṃ yathā<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">57</font>
  ṛtvigbhir brāhmaṇaiḥ pūrvaṃ kanyābhir mantribhis tathā<br>
    yodhaiś caivābhyaṣiñcaṃs te saṃprahṛṣṭāḥ sanaigamaiḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">58</font>
  sarvauṣadhirasaiś cāpi daivatair nabhasi sthitaiḥ<br>
    caturhir lokapālaiś ca sarvair devaiś ca saṃgataiḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">59</font>
  chatraṃ tasya ca jagrāha śatrughnaḥ pāṇḍuraṃ śubham<br>
    śvetaṃ ca vālavyajanaṃ sugrīvo vānareśvaraḥ<br>
    aparaṃ candrasaṃkāśaṃ rākṣasendro vibhīṣaṇaḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">60</font>
  mālāṃ jvalantīṃ vapuṣā kāñcanīṃ śatapuṣkarām<br>
    rāghavāya dadau vāyur vāsavena pracoditaḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">61</font>
  sarvaratnasamāyuktaṃ maṇiratnavibhūṣitam<br>
    muktāhāraṃ narendrāya dadau śakrapracoditaḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">62</font>
  prajagur devagandharvā nanṛtuś cāpsaro gaṇāḥ<br>
    abhiṣeke tad arhasya tadā rāmasya dhīmataḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">63</font>
  bhūmiḥ sasyavatī caiva phalavantaś ca pādapāḥ<br>
    gandhavanti ca puṣpāṇi babhūvū rāghavotsave<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">64</font>
  sahasraśatam aśvānāṃ dhenūnāṃ ca gavāṃ tathā<br>
    dadau śataṃ vṛṣān pūrvaṃ dvijebhyo manujarṣabhaḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">65</font>
  triṃśatkoṭīr hiraṇyasya brāhmaṇebhyo dadau punaḥ<br>
    nānābharaṇavastrāṇi mahārhāṇi ca rāghavaḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">66</font>
  arkaraśmipratīkāśāṃ kāñcanīṃ maṇivigrahām<br>
    sugrīvāya srajaṃ divyāṃ prāyacchan manujarṣabhaḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">67</font>
  vaidūryamaṇicitre ca vajraratnavibhūṣite<br>
    vāliputrāya dhṛtimān aṅgadāyāṅgade dadau<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">68</font>
  maṇipravarajuṣṭaṃ ca muktāhāram anuttamam<br>
    sītāyai pradadau rāmaś candraraśmisamaprabham<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">69</font>
  araje vāsasī divye śubhāny ābharaṇāni ca<br>
    avekṣamāṇā vaidehī pradadau vāyusūnave<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">70</font>
  avamucyātmanaḥ kaṇṭhād dhāraṃ janakanandinī<br>
    avaikṣata harīn sarvān bhartāraṃ ca muhur muhuḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">71</font>
  tām iṅgitajñaḥ saṃprekṣya babhāṣe janakātmajām<br>
    pradehi subhage hāraṃ yasya tuṣṭāsi bhāmini<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">72</font>
  pauruṣaṃ vikramo buddhir yasminn etāni nityadā<br>
    dadau sā vāyuputrāya taṃ hāram asitekṣaṇā<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">73</font>
  hanūmāṃs tena hāreṇa śuśubhe vānararṣabhaḥ<br>
    candrāṃśucayagaureṇa śvetābhreṇa yathācalaḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">74</font>
  tato dvivida maindābhyāṃ nīlāya ca paraṃtapaḥ<br>
    sarvān kāmaguṇān vīkṣya pradadau vasudhādhipaḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">75</font>
  sarvavānaravṛddhāś ca ye cānye vānareśvarāḥ<br>
    vāsobhir bhūṣaṇaiś caiva yathārhaṃ pratipūjitāḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">76</font>
  yathārhaṃ pūjitāḥ sarve kāmai ratnaiś ca puṣkalair<br>
    prahṛṣṭamanasaḥ sarve jagmur eva yathāgatam<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">77</font>
  rāghavaḥ paramodāraḥ śaśāsa parayā mudā<br>
    uvāca lakṣmaṇaṃ rāmo dharmajñaṃ dharmavatsalaḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">78</font>
  ātiṣṭha dharmajña mayā sahemāṃ; gāṃ pūrvarājādhyuṣitāṃ balena<br>
    tulyaṃ mayā tvaṃ pitṛbhir dhṛtā yā; tāṃ yauvarājye dhuram udvahasva<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">79</font>
  sarvātmanā paryanunīyamāno; yadā na saumitrir upaiti yogam<br>
    niyujyamāno bhuvi yauvarājye; tato 'bhyaṣiñcad bharataṃ mahātmā<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">80</font>
  rāghavaś cāpi dharmātmā prāpya rājyam anuttamam<br>
    īje bahuvidhair yajñaiḥ sasuhṛdbhrātṛbāndhavaḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">81</font>
  pauṇḍarīkāśvamedhābhyāṃ vājapeyena cāsakṛt<br>
    anyaiś ca vividhair yajñair ayajat pārthivarṣabhaḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">82</font>
  rājyaṃ daśasahasrāṇi prāpya varṣāṇi rāghavaḥ<br>
    śatāśvamedhān ājahre sadaśvān bhūridakṣiṇān<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">83</font>
  ājānulambibāhuś ca mahāskandhaḥ pratāpavān<br>
    lakṣmaṇānucaro rāmaḥ pṛthivīm anvapālayat<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">84</font>
  na paryadevan vidhavā na ca vyālakṛtaṃ bhayam<br>
    na vyādhijaṃ bhayaṃ vāpi rāme rājyaṃ praśāsati<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">85</font>
  nirdasyur abhaval loko nānarthaḥ kaṃ cid aspṛśat<br>
    na ca sma vṛddhā bālānāṃ pretakāryāṇi kurvate<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">86</font>
  sarvaṃ muditam evāsīt sarvo dharmaparo 'bhavat<br>
    rāmam evānupaśyanto nābhyahiṃsan parasparam<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">87</font>
  āsan varṣasahasrāṇi tathā putrasahasriṇaḥ<br>
    nirāmayā viśokāś ca rāme rājyaṃ praśāsati<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">88</font>
  nityapuṣpā nityaphalās taravaḥ skandhavistṛtāḥ<br>
    kālavarṣī ca parjanyaḥ sukhasparśaś ca mārutaḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">89</font>
  svakarmasu pravartante tuṣṭhāḥ svair eva karmabhiḥ<br>
    āsan prajā dharmaparā rāme śāsati nānṛtāḥ<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">90</font>
  sarve lakṣaṇasaṃpannāḥ sarve dharmaparāyaṇāḥ<br>
    daśavarṣasahasrāṇi rāmo rājyam akārayat<br>

  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">1</font>
  शिरस्य अञ्जलिम आदाय कैकेयीनन्दिवर्धनः<br>
   बभाषे भरतॊ जयेष्ठं रामं सत्यपराक्रमम<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">2</font>
  पूजिता मामिका माता दत्तं राज्यम इदं मम<br>
   तद ददामि पुनस तुभ्यं यथा तवम अददा मम<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">3</font>
  धुरम एकाकिना नयस्ताम ऋषभेण बलीयसा<br>
   किशॊरवद गुरुं भारं न वॊढुम अहम उत्सहे<br>
  <font color="GREEN" size="-1">4</font>
  वारिवेगेन महता भिन्नः सेतुर इव कषरन<br>
   दुर्बन्धनम इदं मन्ये राज्यच्छिद्रम असंवृतम<br>

  ततॊ दविविद मैन्दाभ्यां नीलाय च परंतपः<br>
    सर्वान कामगुणान वीक्ष्य परददौ वसुधाधिपः<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">75</font>
  सर्ववानरवृद्धाश च ये चान्ये वानरेश्वराः<br>
    वासॊभिर भूषणैश चैव यथार्हं परतिपूजिताः<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">76</font>
  यथार्हं पूजिताः सर्वे कामै रत्नैश च पुष्कलैर<br>
    परहृष्टमनसः सर्वे जग्मुर एव यथागतम<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">77</font>
  राघवः परमॊदारः शशास परया मुदा<br>
    उवाच लक्ष्मणं रामॊ धर्मज्ञं धर्मवत्सलः<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">78</font>
  आतिष्ठ धर्मज्ञ मया सहेमां; गां पूर्वराजाध्युषितां बलेन<br>
    तुल्यं मया तवं पितृभिर धृता या; तां यौवराज्ये धुरम उद्वहस्व<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">79</font>
  सर्वात्मना पर्यनुनीयमानॊ; यदा न सौमित्रिर उपैति यॊगम<br>
    नियुज्यमानॊ भुवि यौवराज्ये; ततॊ ऽभयषिञ्चद भरतं महात्मा<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">80</font>
  राघवश चापि धर्मात्मा पराप्य राज्यम अनुत्तमम<br>
    ईजे बहुविधैर यज्ञैः ससुहृद्भ्रातृबान्धवः<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">81</font>
  पौण्डरीकाश्वमेधाभ्यां वाजपेयेन चासकृत<br>
    अन्यैश च विविधैर यज्ञैर अयजत पार्थिवर्षभः<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">82</font>
  राज्यं दशसहस्राणि पराप्य वर्षाणि राघवः<br>
    शताश्वमेधान आजह्रे सदश्वान भूरिदक्षिणान<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">83</font>
  आजानुलम्बिबाहुश च महास्कन्धः परतापवान<br>
    लक्ष्मणानुचरॊ रामः पृथिवीम अन्वपालयत<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">84</font>
  न पर्यदेवन विधवा न च वयालकृतं भयम<br>
    न वयाधिजं भयं वापि रामे राज्यं परशासति<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">85</font>
  निर्दस्युर अभवल लॊकॊ नानर्थः कं चिद अस्पृशत<br>
    न च सम वृद्धा बालानां परेतकार्याणि कुर्वते<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">86</font>
  सर्वं मुदितम एवासीत सर्वॊ धर्मपरॊ ऽभवत<br>
    रामम एवानुपश्यन्तॊ नाभ्यहिंसन परस्परम<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">87</font>
  आसन वर्षसहस्राणि तथा पुत्रसहस्रिणः<br>
    निरामया विशॊकाश च रामे राज्यं परशासति<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">88</font>
  नित्यपुष्पा नित्यफलास तरवः सकन्धविस्तृताः<br>
    कालवर्षी च पर्जन्यः सुखस्पर्शश च मारुतः<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">89</font>
  सवकर्मसु परवर्तन्ते तुष्ठाः सवैर एव कर्मभिः<br>
    आसन परजा धर्मपरा रामे शासति नानृताः<br>
 <font color="GREEN" size="-1">90</font>
  सर्वे लक्षणसंपन्नाः सर्वे धर्मपरायणाः<br>
    दशवर्षसहस्राणि रामॊ राज्यम अकारयत<br>
