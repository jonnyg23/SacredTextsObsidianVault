 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="../../img/tn_hin.jpg"></a><br><i>The Upanishads, Part 2 (SBE15)</i>, by Max Müller, [1879], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">SEVENTH BRÂHMANA.</h3>  
 <p>1. They say that lightning is Brahman, because lightning (vidyut) is called so from cutting off (vidânât) <a name="fr_562"></a><a href="#fn_562"><font size="1">3</font></a>. Whosoever knows this, that lightning is Brahman, him (that Brahman) cuts off from evil, for lightning indeed is Brahman.</p>
