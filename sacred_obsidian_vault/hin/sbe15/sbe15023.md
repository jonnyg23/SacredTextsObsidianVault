 <p><a name="page_46"><font size="1" color="green">p. 46</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">SECOND ANUVÂKA.</h3>  
 <p>1. Om <a name="fr_199"></a><a href="#fn_199"><font size="1">1</font></a>! Let us explain <i>S</i>îkshâ, the doctrine of pronunciation, viz. letter, accent, quantity, effort (in the formation of letters), modulation, and union of letters (sandhi). This is the lecture on <i>S</i>îkshâ.</p>
