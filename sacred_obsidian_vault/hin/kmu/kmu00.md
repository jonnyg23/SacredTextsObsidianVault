 <p align="center">Six Chakras of Yoga with Petals and Deity<br> ——Symbols——</p>  
 <p>1. The Earth Chakra is of Yellow color, has four Petals, a Beast, Two Deities and Kundalini.</p>  
 <p>2. The Water Chakra is White, has Six Petals, a Beast and Two Deities.</p>  
 <p>4. The Air Chakra is Black, has Twelve Petals, a Beast and Two Deities.</p>  
 <p>5. The Ether Chakra is like Sea Water, has Sixteen Petals, a Beast and Two Deities.</p>  
 <p>6. The Mental Chakra has Two Petals and a Deity.</p>  
 <p>Sahasrara is Above All and is called, the One Thousand Petalled Lotus.</p>
