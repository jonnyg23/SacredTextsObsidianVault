 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="12:1:2">12:1:2</a></font></span></p>  <h3 align="center">SECOND BRÂHMA<i>N</i>A.</h3>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="12:1:2:1">12:1:2:1</a></font></span>1. Verily, from out of faith the gods fashioned the initiation, from out of Aditi the opening (sacrifice <a name="fr_424"></a><a href="#fn_424"><font size="1">2</font></a>), from out of Soma the buying (of Soma-plants), from out of Vish<i>n</i>u the guest-offering, from out of the sun the Pravargya, from out of the Svadhâ (the food of departed ancestors) the Upasads,</p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p><a name="page_139"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 139</font></a></p>  
 <p>from out of Agni and Soma the day of fasting, and from out of this world the opening Atirâtra <a name="fr_425"></a><a href="#fn_425"><font size="1">1</font></a>.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="12:1:2:2">12:1:2:2</a></font></span>2. From out of the year (they fashioned) the <i>K</i>aturvi<i>m</i><i>s</i>a day, from out of the priesthood the Abhiplava (sha<i>d</i>aha), from out of the nobility the P<i>ri</i>sh<i>th</i>ya (sha<i>d</i>aha) <a name="fr_426"></a><a href="#fn_426"><font size="1">2</font></a>, from out of Agni the Abhi<i>g</i>it, from out of the waters the Svarasâman days, from out of the sun the Vishuvat,--the Svarasâman days have been told;--from out of Indra the Vi<i>s</i>va<i>g</i>it,--the P<i>ri</i>sh<i>th</i>ya and Abhiplava have been told;--from out of Mitra and Varu<i>n</i>a the Go and</p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p><a name="page_140"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 140</font></a></p>  
 <p><span class="contnote"><font size="-2" color="GREEN">[paragraph continues]</font></span> Âyus <a name="fr_427"></a><a href="#fn_427"><font size="1">1</font></a>, from out of the Vi<i>s</i>ve Devâ<i>h</i> the Da<i>s</i>arâtra <a name="fr_428"></a><a href="#fn_428"><font size="1">2</font></a>, from out of the regions the P<i>ri</i>sh<i>th</i>ya-sha<i>d</i>aha of the Da<i>s</i>arâtra, from out of these worlds the <i>Kh</i>andoma days.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="12:1:2:3">12:1:2:3</a></font></span>3. From out of the year (they fashioned) the tenth day, from out of Pra<i>g</i>âpati the Mahâvrata, and from out of the world of heaven the Udayanîya Atirâtra:--such was the birth of the Year; and, verily, whosoever thus knows that birth of the Year becomes more (and more) glorious to (the end of) it, he becomes possessed of a (new) body, he becomes the Year, and, as the Year <a name="fr_429"></a><a href="#fn_429"><font size="1">3</font></a>, he goes to the gods.</p>
