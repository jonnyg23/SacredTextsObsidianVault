 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="13:1:9">13:1:9</a></font></span></p>  <h3 align="center">NINTH BRÂHMA<i>N</i>A.</h3>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="13:1:9:1">13:1:9:1</a></font></span>1. [Vâ<i>g</i>. S. XXII, 22 <a name="fr_743"></a><a href="#fn_739"><font size="1">1</font></a>], 'In the priestly office (brahman) may the Brâhma<i>n</i>a be born, endowed with spiritual lustre (brahmavar<i>k</i>asa):' on the Brâhma<i>n</i>a he thereby bestows spiritual lustre, whence of old the Brâhma<i>n</i>a was born as one endowed with spiritual lustre <a name="fr_744"></a><a href="#fn_740"><font size="1">2</font></a>.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="13:1:9:2">13:1:9:2</a></font></span>2. 'In the royal order may the Râ<i>g</i>anya be born, heroic, skilled in archery, sure of his mark, and a mighty car-fighter:' on the Râ<i>g</i>anya he thereby bestows the grandeur of heroism <a name="fr_745"></a><a href="#fn_741"><font size="1">3</font></a>, whence of old the Râ<i>g</i>anya was born as one heroic, skilled in archery, certain of his mark, and a mighty car-fighter.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="13:1:9:3">13:1:9:3</a></font></span>3. 'The milch cow:' on the cow he thereby bestows milk; whence of old the cow was born as one yielding milk.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="13:1:9:4">13:1:9:4</a></font></span>4. 'The draught ox:' on the ox he thereby bestows strength, whence of old the ox was born as a draught (animal).</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="13:1:9:5">13:1:9:5</a></font></span>5. 'The swift racer:' on the horse he thereby bestows speed, whence of old the horse was born as a runner.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="13:1:9:6">13:1:9:6</a></font></span>6. 'The well-favoured woman:' on the woman</p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p><a name="page_295"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 295</font></a></p>  
 <p>he thereby bestows beautiful form, whence the beautiful maiden is apt to become dear (to men).</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="13:1:9:7">13:1:9:7</a></font></span>7. 'The victorious warrior:' on the Râ<i>g</i>anya he thereby bestows the grandeur of victoriousness <a name="fr_746"></a><a href="#fn_742"><font size="1">1</font></a>, whence of old the Râ<i>g</i>anya was born as one victorious.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="13:1:9:8">13:1:9:8</a></font></span>8. 'The blitheful youth:' he, indeed, is a blitheful (or, sociable) youth who is in his prime of life; whence one who is in his prime of life is apt to become dear to women.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="13:1:9:9">13:1:9:9</a></font></span>9. 'May a hero be born unto this Sacrificer!' on the Sacrificer's family he thereby bestows manly vigour, whence of old a hero was born to him who had performed the (A<i>s</i>vamedha) sacrifice.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="13:1:9:10">13:1:9:10</a></font></span>10. 'May Par<i>g</i>anya rain for us whensoever we list!'--where they perform this sacrifice, there Par<i>g</i>anya, indeed, rains whenever they list;--'may our fruit-bearing plants ripen!'--there the fruit-bearing plants indeed ripen where they perform this sacrifice;--'may security of possession be assured for us!'--where they perform this sacrifice there security of possession indeed is assured; whence wherever they perform this (A<i>s</i>vamedha) sacrifice, security of possession becomes assured to the people.</p>
