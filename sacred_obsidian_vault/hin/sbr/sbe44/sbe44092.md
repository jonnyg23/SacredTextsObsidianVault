 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="13:2:10">13:2:10</a></font></span></p>  <h3 align="center">TENTH BRÂHMA<i>N</i>A.</h3>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="13:2:10:1">13:2:10:1</a></font></span>1. When they prepare the knife-paths, the Sacrificer makes for himself that passage across, a bridge, for the attainment of the heavenly world.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="13:2:10:2">13:2:10:2</a></font></span>2. They prepare them by means of needles; the needles, doubtless, are the people <a name="fr_836"></a><a href="#fn_832"><font size="1">3</font></a> (clans), and the A<i>s</i>vamedha is the royal power: they thus supply him with people and royal power combined. They are made of gold: the meaning of this has been explained.</p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p><a name="page_327"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 327</font></a></p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="13:2:10:3">13:2:10:3</a></font></span>3. Three kinds of needles are (used), copper ones, silver ones, and gold ones;--those of copper, doubtless, are the (principal) regions (of the compass), those of silver the intermediate ones, and those of gold the upper ones: it is by means of these (regions) they render it fit and proper. By way of horizontal and vertical (stitches <a name="fr_837"></a><a href="#fn_833"><font size="1">1</font></a>) they are many-formed, whence the regions are many-formed; and they are of distinct form, whence the regions are of distinct form.</p>
