 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="11:1:7">11:1:7</a></font></span></p>  <h3 align="center">SEVENTH BRÂHMA<i>N</i>A.</h3>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="11:1:7:1">11:1:7:1</a></font></span>1. Now there, on the occasion of the entering on the fast, it is said <a name="fr_114"></a><a href="#fn_114"><font size="1">2</font></a>, 'If he does not eat, he becomes</p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p><a name="page_21"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 21</font></a></p>  
 <p>consecrated to the Fathers <a name="fr_115"></a><a href="#fn_115"><font size="1">1</font></a>; and if he does eat he eats whilst passing over the gods;' and, in this respect, they lay down the rule, 'Let him therefore eat what grows in the forest.'</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="11:1:7:2">11:1:7:2</a></font></span>2. If he eats cultivated plants he eats the sacrificial essence of the offering-cake; and if he eats forest plants he eats the essence of the barhis <a name="fr_116"></a><a href="#fn_116"><font size="1">2</font></a>; and if he eats aught of trees he eats the essence of the fuel (for the sacrificial fire); and if he drinks milk he consumes the essence of the Sânnâyya <a name="fr_117"></a><a href="#fn_117"><font size="1">3</font></a>; and if he drinks water he consumes the essence of the lustral waters <a name="fr_118"></a><a href="#fn_118"><font size="1">4</font></a>; and if he eats nothing he becomes consecrated to the Fathers.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="11:1:7:3">11:1:7:3</a></font></span>3. As to this they say, 'What course of procedure is there?' Well, let him, on those two nights (of full and new moon), himself offer the Agnihotra: inasmuch as, after offering, he takes food he does not become consecrated to the Fathers, for that (libation) is an offering; and inasmuch as he performs that offering in his own self he does not eat of those sacrificial essences.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="11:1:7:4">11:1:7:4</a></font></span>4. Now all the nights concentrate themselves in these two nights: all the nights of the waxing moon concentrate in the night of full moon, and all the nights of the waning moon concentrate in the night of new moon; and, verily, for him who, knowing this, offers (the Agnihotra) himself on the day of</p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p><a name="page_22"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 22</font></a></p>  
 <p>the entrance on the fast, offering is always made by himself <a name="fr_119"></a><a href="#fn_119"><font size="1">1</font></a>.</p>
