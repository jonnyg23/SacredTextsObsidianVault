 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="13:3:7">13:3:7</a></font></span></p>  <h3 align="center">SEVENTH BRÂHMA<i>N</i>A.</h3>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="13:3:7:1">13:3:7:1</a></font></span>1. Verily, this is the sacrifice called Strengthful: wherever they worship with this sacrifice, everything indeed becomes strong.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="13:3:7:2">13:3:7:2</a></font></span>2. Verily, this is the sacrifice called Plenteous: wherever they worship with this sacrifice, everything indeed becomes plentiful.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="13:3:7:3">13:3:7:3</a></font></span>3. Verily, this is the sacrifice called Obtainment: wherever they worship with this sacrifice, everything indeed becomes obtained.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="13:3:7:4">13:3:7:4</a></font></span>4. Verily, this is the sacrifice called Distinction: wherever they worship with this sacrifice, everything indeed becomes distinct <a name="fr_884"></a><a href="#fn_880"><font size="1">1</font></a>.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="13:3:7:5">13:3:7:5</a></font></span>5. Verily, this is the sacrifice called Severance: wherever they worship with this sacrifice, everything indeed becomes severed <a name="fr_885"></a><a href="#fn_881"><font size="1">2</font></a>.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="13:3:7:6">13:3:7:6</a></font></span>6. Verily, this is the sacrifice called Food-abounding: wherever they worship with this sacrifice, everything indeed becomes abounding in food.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="13:3:7:7">13:3:7:7</a></font></span>7. Verily, this is the sacrifice called Sapful:</p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p><a name="page_345"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 345</font></a></p>  
 <p>wherever they worship with this sacrifice, everything indeed becomes rich in sap (or drink).</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="13:3:7:8">13:3:7:8</a></font></span>8. Verily, this is the sacrifice called Abounding in holiness: wherever they worship with this sacrifice, the Brâhma<i>n</i>a is born as one rich in holiness.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="13:3:7:9">13:3:7:9</a></font></span>9. Verily, this is the sacrifice called Excelling in hitting: wherever they worship with this sacrifice, the Râ<i>g</i>anya is born as one excelling in hitting (the mark).</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="13:3:7:10">13:3:7:10</a></font></span>10. Verily, this is the sacrifice called the Long (wide) one: wherever they worship with this sacrifice, a wide tract of forest-land will be provided <a name="fr_886"></a><a href="#fn_882"><font size="1">1</font></a>.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="13:3:7:11">13:3:7:11</a></font></span>11. Verily, this is the sacrifice called Fitness: wherever they worship with this sacrifice, everything indeed becomes fit and proper.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="13:3:7:12">13:3:7:12</a></font></span>12. Verily, this is the sacrifice called Support (foundation): wherever they worship with this sacrifice, everything indeed becomes supported (firmly established).</p>
