 <p>
 This is a short review of the major schools of yoga, including Hindu,
 Buddhist and Sufi varieties.
 Wood, whose translation of the <a href="../gpu/index.htm">The Garuda Purana</a>
 is also at this site, was a founding Theosophist
 and wrote extensively on Hinduism, and Yoga in particular.
 His works on the subject are written for Western readers, and where he needs
 to use Sanskrit or other esoteric terms, he takes care to explain them.
 He was a practicing Yogi for most of his lifetime.
 </p>
