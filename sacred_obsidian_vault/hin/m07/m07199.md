   <h3 align="center">SECTION CCIII</h3>  
 <p>"Sanjaya said, 'Having battled fiercely for five days, O king, the Brahmana (Drona) endued with great strength, fell and repaired to the region of Brahma. The fruits that arise from a study of the <i>Vedas</i> arise from a study of this <i>Parva</i> also. The great achievements of brave Kshatriyas have been described here. He who readeth or listeneth to the recitation of this <i>Parva</i> every day is freed from heinous sins and the most atrocious acts of his life. Brahmanas may always obtain herefrom the fruits</p>  
 <p><a name="page_492"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 492</font></a></p>  
 <p>of sacrifices. From this, Kshatriyas may obtain victory in fierce battle. The other orders (Vaisyas and Sudras) may obtain desirable sons and grandsons and all objects of desire!'"</p>  
 <p align="center"><i>The end of Drona Parva</i>.</p>
