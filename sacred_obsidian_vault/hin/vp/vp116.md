 <p><a name="page_465"><font size="1" color="green">p. 465</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">CHAP. XXIII.</h3>  
 <p align="center"><font size="-1">Future kings of Magadhá: descendants of Vrihadratha.</font></p>  
 <p>I WILL now relate to you the descendants of Vrihadratha, who will be the kings of Magadhá. There have been several powerful princes of this dynasty, of whom the most celebrated was Jarásandha; his son was Sahadeva; his son is Somápi <a name="fr_1331"></a><a href="#fn_1331"><font size="1">1</font></a>; his son will be Śrutavat <a name="fr_1332"></a><a href="#fn_1332"><font size="1">2</font></a>; his son will be Ayutáyus <a name="fr_1333"></a><a href="#fn_1333"><font size="1">3</font></a>; his son will be Niramitra <a name="fr_1334"></a><a href="#fn_1334"><font size="1">4</font></a>; his son will be Sukshatra <a name="fr_1335"></a><a href="#fn_1335"><font size="1">5</font></a>; his son will be Vrihatkarman <a name="fr_1336"></a><a href="#fn_1336"><font size="1">6</font></a>; his son will be Senajit <a name="fr_1337"></a><a href="#fn_1337"><font size="1">7</font></a>; his son will be Śrutanjaya <a name="fr_1338"></a><a href="#fn_1338"><font size="1">8</font></a>; his son will be Vipra <a name="fr_1339"></a><a href="#fn_1339"><font size="1">9</font></a>; his son will be Śuchi <a name="fr_1340"></a><a href="#fn_1340"><font size="1">10</font></a>; his son will be Kshemya <a name="fr_1341"></a><a href="#fn_1341"><font size="1">11</font></a>; his son will be Suvrata <a name="fr_1342"></a><a href="#fn_1342"><font size="1">12</font></a>; his son will be Dharma <a name="fr_1343"></a><a href="#fn_1343"><font size="1">13</font></a>; his son will be Suśuma <a name="fr_1344"></a><a href="#fn_1344"><font size="1">14</font></a>; his son will be Drid́hasena <a name="fr_1345"></a><a href="#fn_1345"><font size="1">15</font></a>; his son will be Sumati <a name="fr_1346"></a><a href="#fn_1346"><font size="1">16</font></a>; his son will be Suvala <a name="fr_1347"></a><a href="#fn_1347"><font size="1">17</font></a>; his son will be Suníta <a name="fr_1348"></a><a href="#fn_1348"><font size="1">18</font></a>; his son will be Satyajit <a name="fr_1349"></a><a href="#fn_1349"><font size="1">19</font></a>; his son will be Viśwajit <a name="fr_1350"></a><a href="#fn_1350"><font size="1">20</font></a>; his son will be Ripunjaya <a name="fr_1351"></a><a href="#fn_1351"><font size="1">21</font></a>. These are the Várhadrathas, who will reign for a thousand years <a name="fr_1352"></a><a href="#fn_1352"><font size="1">22</font></a>.</p>

 <p><a name="fn_1352"></a><a href="vp116.htm#fr_1352">465:22</a> Our list and that of the Váyu specifies twenty-one kings after Sahadeva: the Bhágavata specifies twenty, and in another passage states that to be the number. My copy of the Matsya names but nineteen, and the Radcliffe but twelve; but both agree in making the total thirty-two. They all concur with the text also in stating that 1000 years had elapsed from the great war, at the death of the last Várhadratha prince; and this is more worthy of credit than the details, which are obviously imperfect.</p>
