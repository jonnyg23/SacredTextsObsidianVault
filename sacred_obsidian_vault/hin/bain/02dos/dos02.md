 <p><font size="-1">[<i>Note</i>.--As the story belongs, by its title, both to Sun and Moon, it should be observed, that the Night and the two Twilights, Dusk and Dawn, apply to both in opposite ways. The Moon rises when the Sun sets, reigns over the Night when he is buried in Darkness, and either sets or vanishes when he is risen in his light. For the Moon is the type of Night, or this lower world (<i>ihaloka</i>), but the Sun, of Day, that is, of the other.]</font></p>
