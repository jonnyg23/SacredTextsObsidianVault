 <a name="page_138"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 138</font></a><h1 align="CENTER">XXXVIII.</h1>
 <p>1. Causing (bodily) pain to a Brâhma<i>n</i>a;</p>
 <p>2. Smelling at things which ought not to be smelt (such as excrements), or at spirituous liquor;</p>
 <p>3. Dishonest dealing;</p>
 <p>4. Sexual connection with cattle;</p>
 <p>5. And (sexual connection) with a man (or unnatural intercourse with a woman):</p>
 <p>6. Such are the crimes effecting loss of caste.</p>
 <p>7. He who has knowingly committed one of the acts effecting loss of caste shall perform the Sântapana<sup>[1]</sup> penance; he who has done so unawares shall perform the Prâ<i>g</i>âpatya<sup>[1]</sup> penance.</p>
