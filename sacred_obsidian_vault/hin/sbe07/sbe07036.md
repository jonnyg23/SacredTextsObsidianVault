 <a name="page_132"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 132</font></a><h1 align="CENTER">XXXIV.</h1>
 <p>1. Sexual connection with one's mother, or daughter, or daughter-in-law are crimes in the highest degree.</p>
 <p>2. Such criminals in the highest degree should proceed into the flames; for there is not any other way to atone for their crime.</p>
