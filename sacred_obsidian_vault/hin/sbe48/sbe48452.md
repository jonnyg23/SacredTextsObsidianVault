 <p>5. On account of the taking hold together.</p>
 <p>The text 'then both knowledge and work take hold of him' (B<i>ri</i>. Up. IV, 4, 2) shows that knowledge and work go together, and this going together is possible only if, in the manner stated, knowledge is subordinate to work.</p>
