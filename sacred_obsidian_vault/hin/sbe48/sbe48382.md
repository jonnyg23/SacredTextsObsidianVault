 <p>38. And on account of scriptural declaration.</p>
 <p>That he bestows all rewards--whether in the form of enjoyment or Release--Scripture also declares 'This indeed is the great, the unborn Self, the eater of food, the giver of</p>
 <p><a name="page_626"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 626</font></a></p>
 <p>wealth' (B<i>ri</i>. Up. IV, 4, 24); and 'For he alone causes delight' (Taitt. Up. II, 7).--Next a primâ facie view is stated.</p>
