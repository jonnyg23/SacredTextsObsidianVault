 <p>31. And hence also a scriptural passage as to non-proceeding according to liking.</p>
 <p>The above conclusion is further confirmed by a scriptural passage prohibiting licence of conduct on the part of any one. The text meant is a passage in the Sa<i>m</i>hitâ of the Ka<i>th</i>as, 'Therefore a Brahmawa does not drink spirituous liquor, thinking "may I not be stained by sin."'--Here terminates the adhikara<i>n</i>a of 'the allowance of all food.'</p>
