 <p>27. From the yoni the body.</p>
 <p>Only after having reached a yoni the soul, affected with a remnant of its works, obtains a new body, and only in a body there can be the enjoyment of pleasure and pain. When, therefore, previous to that the soul is said to reach ether, wind, and so on, this can only mean that it enters into conjunction with them.--Here terminates the adhikara<i>n</i>a of 'that animated by another soul.'</p>
