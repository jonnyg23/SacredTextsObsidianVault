 <p>37. Sm<i>ri</i>ti also states this.</p>
 <p>Sm<i>ri</i>ti also declares that men not belonging to an â<i>s</i>rama grow in knowledge through prayer and the like. 'Through prayer also a Brâhma<i>n</i>a may become perfect. May he perform other works or not, one who befriends all creatures is called a Brâhma<i>n</i>a' (Manu Sm<i>ri</i>. II, 17).</p>
