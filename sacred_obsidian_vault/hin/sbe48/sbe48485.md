 <p>38. And there is the promotion (of knowledge) through special acts (of duty).</p>
 <p><a name="page_705"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 705</font></a></p>
 <p>The above conclusion is founded not only on Reasoning and Sm<i>ri</i>ti; but Scripture even directly states that knowledge is benefited by practices not exclusively prescribed for the â<i>s</i>ramas, 'By penance, abstinence, faith, and knowledge he is to seek the Self (Pr. Up. I, 10).</p>
