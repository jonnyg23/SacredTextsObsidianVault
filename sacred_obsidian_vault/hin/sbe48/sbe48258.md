 <p>12. Earth (from water).</p>
 <p>Earth originates from water; for so the texts declare 'From water earth' (Taitt Up. II. 1, 1). 'It (water) sent forth food' (<i>Kh</i>. Up. VI, 2, 3). But how can the word 'food' denote earth?--To this the next Sûtra replies.</p>
