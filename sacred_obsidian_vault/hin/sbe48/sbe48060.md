 <p>27. And thus also, because (thus only) the designation of the beings, and so on, being the (four) feet is possible.</p>
 <p>The text, moreover, designates the Gâyatrî as having four feet, after having referred to the beings, the earth, the body, and the heart; now this has a sense only if it is Brahman, which here is called Gâyatrî.</p>
