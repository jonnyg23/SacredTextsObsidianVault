 <p>8. And on account of meditation.</p>
 <p>Since, as intimated by the text,'the Self is to be meditated</p>
 <p><a name="page_721"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 721</font></a></p>
 <p>upon,' the mental activity in question is of the nature of meditation, it requires as its necessary condition concentration of mind. For by meditation is understood thought directed upon one object and not disturbed by the ideas of other things.</p>
