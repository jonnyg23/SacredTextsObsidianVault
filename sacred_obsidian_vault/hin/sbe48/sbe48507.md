 <p>10. And Sm<i>ri</i>ti texts say the same.</p>
 <p>Sm<i>ri</i>ti texts also declare that he only who sits can meditate, 'Having placed his steady seat upon a pure spot, there seated upon that seat, concentrating his mind he should practise Yoga' (Bha. Gî. VI, 11-12).</p>
