 <p>14. Sm<i>ri</i>ti texts also declare this.</p>
 <p>That all beings are under the sway of Yama, Parâ<i>s</i>ara also and other Sm<i>ri</i>ti writers declare, 'And all these pass under the sway of Yama.'</p>
