 <p>34. On account of taking and the declaration as to its moving about.</p>
 <p>The text beginning 'And as a great king,' &amp;c., declares that 'the Self taking the pranas moves about in its own body,</p>
 <p><a name="page_555"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 555</font></a></p>
 <p>according to its pleasure' (B<i>ri</i>. Up. II, 1, 18), i.e. it teaches that the Self is active in taking to itself the prâ<i>n</i>as and moving about in the body.</p>
