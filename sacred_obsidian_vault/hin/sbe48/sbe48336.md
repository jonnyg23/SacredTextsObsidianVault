 <p>19. It moreover is recorded, in the world.</p>
 <p>Sm<i>ri</i>ti, moreover, states that the bodies of some specially meritorious persons, such as Draupadî, Dh<i>ri</i>sh<i>t</i>adyumna and others, were formed independently of the fifth oblation' (i.e. sexual union).</p>
