 <p>20. And as the different vital airs.</p>
 <p>As the one air, according as it undergoes in the body</p>
 <p><a name="page_467"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 467</font></a></p>
 <p>different modifications, acquires a new name, new characteristics, and new functions, being then called prâ<i>n</i>a, apâna, and so on; thus the one Brahman becomes the world, with its manifold moving and non-moving beings.--The non-difference of the world from Brahman, the highest cause, is thus fully established.</p>
 <p>Here terminates the 'ârambha<i>n</i>a' adhikara<i>n</i>a.</p>
