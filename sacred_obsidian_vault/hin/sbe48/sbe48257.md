 <p>11. Water (from fire).</p>
 <p>Water also originates 'thence,' i. e from fire; for so the texts declare 'From fire water' (Taitt. Up. II, 1, 1); 'that sent forth water' (<i>Kh</i>. Up. VI, 2, 3).</p>
