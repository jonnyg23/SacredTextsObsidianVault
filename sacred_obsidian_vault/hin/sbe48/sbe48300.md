 <p>2. (The scriptural statement of the plural) is secondary, on account of impossibility; and since (the highest Self) is declared before that.</p>
 <p>The plural form exhibited by the text must be taken (not in its literal, but) in a secondary figurative sense, since there is no room there for a plurality of things. For Scripture</p>
 <p><a name="page_570"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 570</font></a></p>
 <p>declares that previous to creation the highest Self only exists.</p>
