 <p>3. But, on account of impossibility, not the embodied soul.</p>
 <p>Those who fully consider this infinite multitude of exalted qualities will recognise that not even a shadow of them can belong to the individual soul--whether in the state of bondage or that of release--which is a thing as insignificant as a glow-worm and, through its connexion</p>
 <p><a name="page_263"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 263</font></a></p>
 <p>with a body, liable to the attacks of endless suffering. It is not possible therefore to hold that the section under discussion should refer to the individual soul.</p>
