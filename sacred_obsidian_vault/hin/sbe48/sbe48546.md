 <p>10. And on account of Sm<i>ri</i>ti.</p>
 <p>This follows from Sm<i>ri</i>ti also, which declares 'when the pralaya has come and the end of the Highest, they all together with Brahman enter the highest place.'--For all these reasons Bâdari holds that the troop of the conducting deities, beginning with Light, leads the souls of those only who meditate on the effected Brahman, i e. Hira<i>n</i>yagarbha.</p>
