 <p>30. This is said in Sm<i>ri</i>ti also.</p>
 <p>That for those as well who know Brahman, as for others, the eating of food of any kind is lawful only in case of extreme need, Sm<i>ri</i>ti also declares, 'He who being in danger of his life eats food from anywhere is stained by sin no more than the lotus leaf by water.'</p>
