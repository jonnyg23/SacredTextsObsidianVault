 <p>2. And for the same reason all follow after.</p>
 <p>Because speech's becoming one with mind means only conjunction with the latter, not merging within it; there is also no objection to what Scripture says as to all other organs that follow speech being united with mind.--Here terminates the adhikara<i>n</i>a of 'speech.'</p>
