 <p>21. Or not so, on account of difference.</p>
 <p>This is not so, for as Brahman is to be meditated upon in two different abodes, the meditations are separate. In both the <i>S</i>â<i>nd</i>ilya-vidyâs, on the other hand, Brahman is to be meditated upon as abiding within the heart.</p>
