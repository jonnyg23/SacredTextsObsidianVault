 <p>13. And because (the world also) would thus be eternal.</p>
 <p><a name="page_499"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 499</font></a></p>
 <p>The samavâya is a relation, and if that relation is eternal that to which the relation belongs must also be eternal, so that we would arrive at the unacceptable conclusion that the world is eternal.</p>
