 <p>I. 121. <i>tohi mori lagan lagâye re phakîr wâ</i></p> 
 <p>To Thee Thou hast drawn my love, O Fakir!<br> I was sleeping in my own chamber, and Thou didst awaken me; striking me with Thy voice, O Fakir!<br> I was drowning in the deeps of the <a name="page_54"><font size="1" color="green">p. 54</font></a> ocean of this world, and Thou didst save me: upholding me with Thine arm, O Fakir!<br> Only one word and no second--and Thou hast made me tear off all my bonds, O Fakir!<br> Kabîr says, "Thou hast united Thy heart to my heart, O Fakir!"</p>
