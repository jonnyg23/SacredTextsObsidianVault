 <p>II. 100. <i>nis` din sâlai ghâw</i></p> 
 <p>A SORE pain troubles me day and night, and I cannot sleep;<br> I long for the meeting with my Beloved, and my father's house gives me pleasure no more. <a name="page_80"><font size="1" color="green">p. 80</font></a><br> The gates of the sky are opened, the temple is revealed:<br> I meet my husband, and leave at His feet the offering of my body and my mind.</p>
