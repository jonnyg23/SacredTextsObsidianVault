 <p>II. 111. <i>Nârad, pyâr so antar nâhî</i></p> 
 <p>OH Narad! I know that my Lover cannot be far:<br> When my Lover wakes, I wake; when He sleeps, I sleep.<br> He is destroyed at the root who gives pain to my Beloved.<br> Where they sing His praise, there I live;<br> When He moves, I walk before Him: my heart yearns for my Beloved.<br> The infinite pilgrimage lies at His feet, a million devotees are seated there.<br> Kabîr says: "The Lover Himself reveals the glory of true love."</p>
