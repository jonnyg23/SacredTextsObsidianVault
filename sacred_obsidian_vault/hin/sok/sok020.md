 <p>II. 20. <i>paramâtam guru nika<b>t</b> virâjat<u>n</u></i></p> 
 <p>O MY heart! the Supreme Spirit, the great Master, is near you: wake, oh wake! <br> Run to the feet of your Beloved: for <a name="page_69"><font size="1" color="green">p. 69</font></a><br> your Lord stands near to your head.<br> You have slept for unnumbered ages; this morning will you not wake?</p>
