 <p>I. 122. <i>kaum muralî s'abd s'un ânand bhayo</i></p> 
 <p>WHAT is that flute whose music thrills me with joy?<br> The flame burns without a lamp;<br> The lotus blossoms without a root; <a name="page_99"><font size="1" color="green">p. 99</font></a><br> Flowers bloom in clusters;<br> The moon-bird is devoted to the moon;<br> With all its heart the rain-bird longs for the shower of rain;<br> But upon whose love does the Lover concentrate His entire life?</p>
