 <p>I. 58. <i>bâgo nâ jâ re nâ jâ</i></p> 
 <p>Do not go to the garden of flowers!<br> O Friend! go not there;<br> In your body is the garden of flowers.<br> Take your seat on the thousand petals of the lotus, and there gaze on the Infinite Beauty.</p>
