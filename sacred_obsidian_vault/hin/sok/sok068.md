 <p>I. 9. <i>nâ jâne sâhab kaisâ hai</i></p> 
 <p>I DO not know what manner of God is mine.<br> The Mullah cries aloud to Him: and why? Is your Lord deaf? The subtle anklets that ring on the <a name="page_111"><font size="1" color="green">p. 111</font></a> feet of an insect when it moves are heard of Him.<br> Tell your beads, paint your forehead with the mark of your God, and wear matted locks long and showy: but a deadly weapon is in your heart, and how shall you have God?</p>
