 <p>III. 55. <i>ved kahe sargu<b>n</b> ke âge</i></p> 
 <p>THE Vedas say that the Unconditioned stands beyond the world of Conditions.<br> O woman, what does it avail thee to dispute whether He is beyond all or in all? <a name="page_118"><font size="1" color="green">p. 118</font></a><br> See thou everything as thine own dwelling place: the mist of pleasure and pain can never spread there.<br> There Brahma is revealed day and night: there light is His garment, light is His seat, light rests on thy head.<br> Kabîr says: "The Master, who is true, He is all light."</p>
