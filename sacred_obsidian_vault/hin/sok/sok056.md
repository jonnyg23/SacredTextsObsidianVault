 <p>I. 73. <i>bhakti kâ mârag jhînâ re</i></p> 
 <p>SUBTLE is the path of love!<br> Therein there is no asking and no not-asking,<br> There one loses one's self at His feet,<br> There one is immersed in the joy of the seeking: plunged in the deeps of love as the fish in the water. <a name="page_101"><font size="1" color="green">p. 101</font></a><br> The lover is never slow in offering his head for his Lord's service.<br> Kabîr declares the secret of this love.</p>
