 <p>I. 52. <i>khasm na cînhai bâwari</i></p> 
 <p>O MAN, if thou dost not know thine own Lord, whereof art thou so proud?<br> Put thy cleverness away: mere words shall never unite thee to Him.<br> Do not deceive thyself with the witness of the Scriptures:<br> Love is something other than this, and he who has sought it truly has found it.</p>
