 <p>II. 45. <i>Hari ne apnâ âp chipâyâ</i></p> 
 <p>MY Lord hides Himself, and my Lord wonderfully reveals Himself:<br> My Lord has encompassed me with hardness, and my Lord has cast down my limitations.<br> My Lord brings to me words of sorrow and words of joy, and He Himself heals their strife.<br> I will offer my body and mind to my Lord: I will give up my life, but never can I forget my Lord!</p>
