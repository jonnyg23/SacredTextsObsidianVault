 <p>I. 129. <i>sakhiyo, ham hû<u>n</u> bhâî vâlamâs'î</i></p> 
 <p>DEAR friend, I am eager to meet my Beloved! My youth has flowered, and the pain of separation from Him troubles my breast.<br> I am wandering yet in the alleys of knowledge without purpose, but I have received His news in these alleys of knowledge.<br> I have a letter from my Beloved: in this letter is an unutterable message, and now my fear of death is done away.<br> Kabîr says: "O my loving friend! I have got for my gift the Deathless One."</p>
