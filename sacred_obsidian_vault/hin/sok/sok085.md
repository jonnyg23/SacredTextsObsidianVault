 <p>III. 89. <i>mor phakîrwâ mâ<b>n</b>gi jây</i></p> 
 <p>THE Beggar goes a-begging, but<br> I could not even catch sight of Him:<br> And what shall I beg of the Beggar He gives without my asking.<br> Kabîr says: "I am His own: now let that befall which may befall!"</p>
