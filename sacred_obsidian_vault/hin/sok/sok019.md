 <p>II. 77. <i>maddh âkas' âp jahân bai<b>t</b>he</i></p> 
 <p>THE middle region of the sky, wherein the spirit dwelleth, is radiant with the music of light;<br> There, where the pure and white music blossoms, my Lord takes His delight.<br> In the wondrous effulgence of each hair of His body, the brightness of millions of suns and of moons is lost.<br> On that shore there is a city, where the rain of nectar pours and pours, and never ceases.<br> Kabîr says: "Come, O Dharmadas! and see my great Lord's Durbar."</p>
