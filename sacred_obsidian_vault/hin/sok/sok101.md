 <p>II. 122. <i>kôî prem kî peng jhulâo re</i></p> 
 <p>HANG up the swing of love to-day! Hang the body and the mind between the arms of the Beloved, in the ecstasy of love's joy:<br> Bring the tearful streams of the rainy clouds to your eyes, and cover your heart with the shadow of darkness:<br> Bring your face nearer to His ear, and speak of the deepest longings of your heart.<br> Kabîr says: "Listen to me, brother! bring the vision of the Beloved in your heart."</p>  </body>
