 <p><a name="page_114"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 114</font></a></p>  <h1 align="center">Kali the Mother.</h1>  
 <p></p><dir><font size="-1">The stars are blotted out,<br> Clouds are covering clouds,<br> It is darkness, vibrant, sonant.<br> In the roaring whirling wind<br> Are the souls of a million lunatics,--<br> But loosed from the prison house,--<br> Wrenching trees by the roots,<br> Sweeping all from the path.<br> The sea has joined the fray,<br> And swirls up mountain-waves,<br> To reach the pitchy sky.<br>      Scattering plagues and sorrows,<br>      Dancing mad with joy,<br>      Come, Mother, Come!  
 <p>For Terror is thy name,<br> Death is in Thy breath.<br> And every shaking step<br> Destroys a world for e’er.<br> Thou "Time" the All-Destroyer<br>      Then come, O Mother, Come!</p>  
 <p>Who can misery love,<br> Dance in destruction's dance,<br> And hug the form of Death,<br>      To him the Mother comes.<br>                                       V.</p></font></dir>
