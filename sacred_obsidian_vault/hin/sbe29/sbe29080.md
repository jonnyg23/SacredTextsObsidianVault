 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Grihya Sutras, Part 1 (SBE29)</i>, by Hermann Oldenberg, [1886], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">KHA<i>N</i><i>D</i>A 18.</h3>  
 <p>1 <a name="fr_295"></a><a href="#fn_294"><font size="1">1</font></a>. 'May summer, winter and spring, autumn and rainy season be well-ordered to us. May we be under the safe protection of these seasons, and may they last (to us) through a hundred years. Svâhâ!</p>  
 <p>Beat away, O white one, with thy foot, with the forefoot and with the hind-foot, these seven daughters of Varu<i>n</i>a and all that belong to the king's tribe. Svâhâ!</p>  
 <p>'To the white one, the son of Vidârva svâhâ! To Vidârva svâhâ! To Takshaka Vai<i>s</i>âleya svâhâ! To Vi<i>s</i>âla svâhâ!'with (these words) he sacrifices (oblations) of Â<i>g</i>ya.</p>  
 <p>2. 'May a good winter, a good spring, a good summer be bestowed (on us). May the rains be to us happy rains; may the autumns be blessed to us.'</p>  
 <p>3. With (the verse), 'Blessing on us, Mitra' (Rig-veda I, 90, 9), he sweeps (the floor) with a Palâ<i>s</i>a branch,</p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p><a name="page_132"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 132</font></a></p>  
 <p>4. Sprinkles (it with water) with (the verse), 'From the sea the wave' (Rig-veda IV, 58, 1),</p>  
 <p>5. And spreads out a layer (of straw) with (the verse), 'Be soft, O earth' (Rig-veda I, 22, 15).</p>  
 <p>6. They then lie down on their sides, the eldest one to the right hand</p>  
 <p>7. With (the words), 'In the Brahman I establish myself, in the Kshatra,' on (their) right (sides);</p>  
 <p>8. With (the words), 'Among the horses I establish myself, among the cows,' on (their) left (sides);</p>  
 <p>9. With (the words), 'Among the cattle I establish myself, in prosperity,' on (their) right (sides);</p>  
 <p>10. With (the words), 'Among offspring I establish myself, in food,' on (their) left (sides).</p>  
 <p>11. With (the verse), 'Arise, the living' (Rig-veda I, 113, 16), they arise.</p>  
 <p>12. During that night they lie on that layer.</p>  
 <p>13. Afterwards where they like.</p>
