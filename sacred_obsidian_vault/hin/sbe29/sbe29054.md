 <p><a name="page_97"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 97</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">KHA<i>N</i><i>D</i>A 6.</h3>  
 <p>1. One who has not set up the (sacred <i>S</i>rauta) fires, when setting out on a journey, looks at his house.</p>  
 <p>2. (He murmurs the text,) 'Do ye both, Mitra and Varu<i>n</i>a, protect this house for me; unscathed, undisturbed, may Pûshan guard it till our return;'</p>  
 <p>3. And murmurs (the verse), 'Upon the path we have entered' (Rig-veda VI, 51, 16).</p>
