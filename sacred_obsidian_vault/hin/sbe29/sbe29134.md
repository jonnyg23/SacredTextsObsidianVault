 <p><a name="page_217"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 217</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">ADHYÂYA III, KA<i>N</i><i>D</i>IKÂ 1.</h3>  
 <p>1. Now (follow) the five sacrifices:</p>  
 <p>2. The sacrifice to the Gods, the sacrifice to the Beings, the sacrifice to the Fathers, the sacrifice to Brahman, the sacrifice to men.</p>  
 <p>3. Here now, if he makes oblations over the (sacred) fire, this is the sacrifice to the Gods.</p>  
 <p>If he makes Bali offerings, this is the sacrifice to the Beings.</p>  
 <p>If he gives (Pi<i>n</i><i>d</i>a offerings) to the Fathers, this is the sacrifice to the Fathers.</p>  
 <p>If he studies (Vedic) texts, this is the sacrifice to Brahman.</p>  
 <p>If he gives to men, this is the sacrifice to men.</p>  
 <p>4. These (five kinds of) sacrifices he should perform every day.</p>
