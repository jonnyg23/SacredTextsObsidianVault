 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Grihya Sutras, Part 1 (SBE29)</i>, by Hermann Oldenberg, [1886], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">KHA<i>N</i><i>D</i>A 5.</h3>  
 <p>1 <a name="fr_32"></a><a href="#fn_31"><font size="1">1</font></a>. There are four kinds of Pâkaya<i>g</i><i>ñ</i>as, viz. the HUTA, the AHUTA, the PRAHUTA, and the PRÂ<i>S</i>ITA.</p>  
 <p>2 <a name="fr_33"></a><a href="#fn_32"><font size="1">2</font></a>. On the following five occasions, viz. the wedding, the tonsure (of the child's head), the initiation (of the Brahma<i>k</i>ârin), the cutting of the beard, and the parting of the hair, (on these occasions) in the outer hall</p>  
 <p>3 <a name="fr_34"></a><a href="#fn_33"><font size="1">3</font></a>. To a place that has been smeared (with cow-dung), which is elevated, and which has been sprinkled (with water), he carries forward the fire,</p>  
 <p>4. Having kindled it by rubbing, according to some teachers, at his marriage.</p>  
 <p>5. During the northern course of the sun, in the</p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p><a name="page_21"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 21</font></a></p>  
 <p>time of the increasing moon, on an auspicious day he shall seize the hand of a girl,</p>  
 <p>6. Who should possess (the auspicious) characteristics (required),</p>  
 <p>7. Whose limbs should be proportionate,</p>  
 <p>8. Whose hair should be smooth,</p>  
 <p>9 <a name="fr_35"></a><a href="#fn_34"><font size="1">9</font></a>. Who should also have at her neck two curls turned to the right.</p>  
 <p>10. (Of such a girl) he shall know that she will give birth to six men.</p>
