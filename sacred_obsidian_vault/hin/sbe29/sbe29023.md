 <p><a name="page_47"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 47</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">KHA<i>N</i><i>D</i>A 21.</h3>  
 <p>1. In the fourth month the Garbharaksha<i>n</i>a (i.e. the ceremony for the protection of the embryo),</p>  
 <p>2. Sacrificing six oblations from a mess of cooked food with (the six verses of the hymn), 'Agni, joined with the prayer' (Rig-veda X, 162),</p>  
 <p>3. With (the verses), 'From thy eyes, thy nose' (Rig-veda X, 163), verse by verse besmearing her limbs with Â<i>g</i>ya salve.</p>
