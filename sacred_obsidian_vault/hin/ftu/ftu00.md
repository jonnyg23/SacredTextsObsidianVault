 <p>From the Upanishads</p>  
 <p><a name="page_ii"><font size="1" color="green">p. ii</font></a></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Wilt thou not ope thy heart to know<br> What rainbows teach, and sunsets show?<br> Verdict which accumulates<br> From lengthening scroll of human fates,<br> Voice of earth to earth returned,<br> Prayers of saints that inly burned,—<br> Saying, <i>What is excellent;<br> As God lives, is permanent<br> Hearts are dust, Hearts’ loves remain;<br> Heart's love will meet thee again</i>.<br>       .      .      .      .<br> House and tenant go to ground,<br> Lost in God, in Godhead found.</div>
