 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="../../img/tn_hin.jpg"></a><br><i>The Upanishads, Part 1 (SBE01)</i>, by Max Müller, [1879], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">SIXTH KHA<i>N</i><i>D</i>A.</h3>  
 <p>This is an exact repetition of the third kha<i>n</i><i>d</i>a. According to the commentator, the third kha<i>n</i><i>d</i>a was intended for the glory of the first word tad, while the sixth is intended for the glory of the whole hymn.</p>
