 <p>
 This is the first volume of the Sacred Books of the East, and
 the first etext ever prepared for sacred-texts.com.
 SBE 1 contains the introduction to the entire series,
 and explains the methodology and conventions used in the
 rest of the SBE.
 The Upanishads translated here are the Chandogya, Talavakara, Aitreya-Aranyaka,
 the Kaushitaki-Brahmana, and the Vajasaneyi Samhita.
