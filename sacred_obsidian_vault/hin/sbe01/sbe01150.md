 <p>1. 'But in reality he is an ativâdin who declares the Highest Being to be the True (Satya, τὸ ὄντως ὄν).'</p>  
 <p>'Sir, may I become an ativâdin by the True?'</p>  
 <p>'But we must desire to know the True.'</p>  
 <p>'Sir, I desire to know the True.'</p>
