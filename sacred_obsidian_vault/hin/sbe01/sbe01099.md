 <p>1. 'The altar is Par<i>g</i>anya (the god of rain), O Gautama; its fuel is the air itself, the smoke the cloud, the light the lightning, the coals. the thunderbolt, the sparks the thunderings <a name="fr_248"></a><a href="#fn_247"><font size="1">3</font></a>.</p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p><a name="page_79"><font size="1" color="green">p. 79</font></a></p>  
 <p>2. 'On that altar the Devas offer Soma, the king (the moon). From that oblation rises rain <a name="fr_249"></a><a href="#fn_248"><font size="1">1</font></a>.</p>
