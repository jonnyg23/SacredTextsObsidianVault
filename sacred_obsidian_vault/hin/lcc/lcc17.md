 <p><a name="page_176"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 176</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">EPILOGUE</h3>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">MAY kine yield streaming milk, the earth her grain,<br> And may the heaven give never-failing rain,<br>   The winds waft happiness to all that breathes,<br> And all that lives, live free from every pain.  
 <p>In paths of righteousness may Brahmans tread,<br> And high esteem their high deserving wed;<br> May kings in justice ways be ever led,<br> And earth, submissive, bend her grateful head.<span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">60</font></span></p></div>  
 <p align="center">[<i>Exeunt omnes</i>.]</p>
