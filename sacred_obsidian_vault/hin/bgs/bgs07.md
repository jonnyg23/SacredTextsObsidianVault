 VII. atha saptamodhyāyaḥ.  (jñānavijñānayogaḥ)<br>
 <br>
 śrībhagavān uvāca<br>
 <br>
 mayy āsaktamanāḥ pārtha yogaṃ yuñjan madāśrayaḥ<br>
 asaṃśayaṃ samagraṃ māṃ yathā jñāsyasi tac chṛṇu<br>
 7.1<br>
 <br>
 jñānaṃ tehaṃ savijñānam idaṃ vakṣyāmy aśeṣataḥ<br>
 yaj jñātvā neha bhūyo.anyaj jñātavyam avaśiṣyate 7.2<br>
 <br>
 manuṣyāṇāṃ sahasreṣu kaścid yatati siddhaye<br>
 yatatām api siddhānāṃ kaścin māṃ vetti tattvataḥ<br>
 7.3<br>
 <br>
 bhūmir āponalo vāyuḥ khaṃ mano buddhir eva ca<br>
 ahaṃkāra itīyaṃ me bhinnā prakṛtir aṣṭadhā 7.4<br>
 <br>
 apareyam itas tvanyāṃ prakṛtiṃ viddhi me parām<br>
 jīvabhūtāṃ mahābāho yayedaṃ dhāryate jagat 7.5<br>
 <br>
 etadyonīni bhūtāni sarvāṇīty upadhāraya<br>
 ahaṃ kṛtsnasya jagataḥ prabhavaḥ pralayas tathā 7.6<br>
 <br>
 mattaḥ parataraṃ nānyat kiṃcid asti dhanaṃjaya<br>
 mayi sarvam idaṃ protaṃ sūtre maṇigaṇā iva 7.7<br>
 <br>
 raso.aham apsu kaunteya prabhāsmi śaśisūryayoḥ<br>
 praṇavaḥ sarvavedeṣu śabdaḥ khe pauruṣaṃ nṛṣu 7.8<br>
 <br>
 puṇyo gandhaḥ pṛthivyāṃ ca tejaś cāsmi vibhāvasau<br>
 jīvanaṃ sarvabhūteṣu tapaś cāsmi tapasviṣu 7.9<br>
 <br>
 bījaṃ māṃ sarvabhūtānāṃ viddhi pārtha sanātanam<br>
 buddhir buddhimatām asmi tejas tejasvinām aham<br>
 7.10<br>
 <br>
 balaṃ balavatāṃ cāhaṃ kāmarāgavivarjitam<br>
 dharmāviruddho bhūteṣu kāmo.asmi bharatarṣabha<br>
 7.11.<br>
 <br>
 ye caiva sātvikā bhāvā rājasās tāmasāś ca ye<br>
 matta eveti tān viddhi na tv ahaṃ teṣu te mayi 7.12<br>
 <br>
 tribhir guṇamayair bhāvair ebhiḥ sarvam idaṃ jagat<br>
 mohitaṃ nābhijānāti mām ebhyaḥ param avyayam<br>
 7.13<br>
 <br>
 daivī hy eṣā guṇamayī mama māyā duratyayā<br>
 mām eva ye prapadyante māyām etāṃ taranti te 7.14<br>
 <br>
 na māṃ duṣkṛtino mūḍhāḥ prapadyante narādhamāḥ<br>
 māyayāpahṛtajñānā āsuraṃ bhāvam āśritāḥ 7.15<br>
 <br>
 caturvidhā bhajante māṃ janāḥ sukṛtinorjuna<br>
 ārto jijñāsur arthārthī jñānī ca bharatarṣabha 7.16<br>
 <br>
 teṣāṃ jñānī nityayukta ekabhaktir viśiṣyate<br>
 priyo hi jñāninotyartham ahaṃ sa ca mama priyaḥ<br>
 7.17<br>
 <br>
 udārāḥ sarva evaite jñānī tv ātmaiva me matam<br>
 āsthitaḥ sa hi yuktātmā mām evānuttamāṃ gatim<br>
 7.18<br>
 <br>
 bahūnāṃ janmanām ante jñānavān māṃ prapadyate<br>
 vāsudevaḥ sarvam iti sa mahātmā sudurlabhaḥ 7.19<br>
 <br>
 kāmais tais tair hṛtajñānāḥ prapadyantenyadevatāḥ<br>
 taṃ taṃ niyamam āsthāya prakṛtyā niyatāḥ svayā<br>
 7.20<br>
 <br>
 yo yo yāṃ yāṃ tanuṃ bhaktaḥ śraddhayārcitum<br>
 icchati<br>
 tasya tasyācalāṃ śraddhāṃ tām eva vidadhāmy aham<br>
 7.21<br>
 <br>
 sa tayā śraddhayā yuktas tasyārādhanam īhate<br>
 labhate ca tataḥ kāmān mayaivaḥ vihitān hi tān 7.22<br>
 <br>
 antavat tu phalaṃ teṣāṃ tad bhavaty alpamedhasām<br>
 devān devayajo yānti madbhaktā yānti mām api 7.23 <br>
 <br>
 avyaktaṃ vyaktim āpannaṃ manyante mām<br>
 abuddhayaḥ<br>
 paraṃ bhāvam ajānanto mamāvyayam anuttamam<br>
 7.24<br>
 <br>
 nāhaṃ prakāśaḥ sarvasya yogamāyāsamāvṛtaḥ<br>
 mūḍhoyaṃ nābhijānāti loko mām ajam avyayam 7.25<br>
 <br>
 vedāhaṃ samatītāni vartamānāni cārjuna<br>
 bhaviṣyāṇi ca bhūtāni māṃ tu veda na kaścana 7.26<br>
 <br>
 icchādveṣasamutthena dvandvamohena bhārata<br>
 sarvabhūtāni saṃmohaṃ sarge yānti paraṃtapa 7.27<br>
 <br>
 yeṣāṃ tv antagataṃ pāpaṃ janānāṃ puṇyakarmaṇām<br>
 te dvandvamohanirmuktā bhajante māṃ dṛḍhavratāḥ<br>
 7.28<br>
 <br>
 jarāmaraṇamokṣāya mām āśritya yatanti ye<br>
 te brahma tad viduḥ kṛtsnam adhyātmaṃ karma<br>
 cākhilam 7.29<br>
 <br>
 sādhibhūtādhidaivaṃ māṃ sādhiyajñaṃ ca ye viduḥ<br>
 prayāṇakālepi ca māṃ te vidur yuktacetasaḥ 7.30<br>
 <br>
