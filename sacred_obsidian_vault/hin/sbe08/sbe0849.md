 <h1 align="CENTER">CHAPTER XXII.</h1>
 <p align="CENTER">Brahman said:</p>
 <p>O best (of men)! I will explain to you accurately the quality of passion. Learn, O noble ones! the action of the quality of passion. Injuring (others), beauty <a name="fr_1564"></a><a href="#fn_1558"><font size="1">2</font></a>, toil, pleasure and pain, cold and heat, power <a name="fr_1565"></a><a href="#fn_1558"><font size="1">2</font></a>, war, peace, argument, repining <a name="fr_1566"></a><a href="#fn_1559"><font size="1">3</font></a>, endurance, strength, valour, frenzy, wrath, exercise and quarrel too, vindictiveness, desire, backbiting, battle, the thought (that this or that is) mine, preservation <a name="fr_1567"></a><a href="#fn_1560"><font size="1">4</font></a>, slaughter, bonds, affliction, buying and selling, touching <a name="fr_1568"></a><a href="#fn_1561"><font size="1">5</font></a> other people's weak points, by cutting, breaking, piercing; fierceness and cruelty, vilifying, pointing out others' weaknesses, thinking of (this) world, harbouring evil thoughts, animosity, abuse</p>

 <p><a name="page_324"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 324</font></a></p>
 <p>uttering falsehoods, bad <a name="fr_1569"></a><a href="#fn_1562"><font size="1">1</font></a> gifts, doubt, boasting, censure, praise, laudation <a name="fr_1570"></a><a href="#fn_1563"><font size="1">2</font></a>, prowess, defiance, attendance. (on another), obedience <a name="fr_1571"></a><a href="#fn_1564"><font size="1">3</font></a>, service,, harbouring desire, management <a name="fr_1572"></a><a href="#fn_1565"><font size="1">4</font></a>, policy, heedlessness, contumely, belongings <a name="fr_1573"></a><a href="#fn_1566"><font size="1">5</font></a>, and the various decorations which prevail in this world, for men, for women, for living creatures., for articles, and for houses, vexation, and also want of faith, vows and regulation <a name="fr_1574"></a><a href="#fn_1567"><font size="1">6</font></a>, and actions with expectations, and the various acts of public charity <a name="fr_1575"></a><a href="#fn_1568"><font size="1">7</font></a> the ceremony of Svâhâ, the ceremony of Svadhâ, the ceremony of Vashat <a name="fr_1576"></a><a href="#fn_1569"><font size="1">8</font></a>, salutation, both officiating at sacrifices and imparting instruction, and also sacrificing and study, gifts and acceptance of gifts, expiations, auspicious rites, the wish 'this may be mine and that may be mine,' affection generated by the qualities <a name="fr_1577"></a><a href="#fn_1570"><font size="1">9</font></a>, treachery and likewise deception, disrespect and respect, theft, slaughter, disgust, vexing (oneself), wakefulness, ostentation, haughtiness, and attachment also, devotion, pleasure and delight, gambling, common scandal, association with women, devotion</p>

 <p><a name="page_325"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 325</font></a></p>
 <p>to dancing, and instrumental or vocal music, all these qualities, O Brâhma<i>n</i>as! are described as passionate. The men who meditate on past, present, and future entities in this world <a name="fr_1578"></a><a href="sbe0850.htm#fn_1571"><font size="1">1</font></a>, who are always devoted to the triad--piety, wealth, and lust also <a name="fr_1579"></a><a href="sbe0850.htm#fn_1572"><font size="1">2</font></a>--who acting under (the impulse of) desires exult or. the success of all their desires, these men, who are enveloped by passion, have (their) currents downwards <a name="fr_1580"></a><a href="sbe0850.htm#fn_1573"><font size="1">3</font></a>. Born again and again in this world, they rejoice <a name="fr_1581"></a><a href="sbe0850.htm#fn_1574"><font size="1">4</font></a>, and wish for the fruit appertaining to the life after death <a name="fr_1582"></a><a href="sbe0850.htm#fn_1575"><font size="1">5</font></a> and that appertaining to this world also. They give and receive, and make Tarpa<i>n</i>a <a name="fr_1583"></a><a href="sbe0850.htm#fn_1576"><font size="1">6</font></a>, and also sacrifice. The qualities of passion have been described to you in many ways, and the action of the quality has also been stated accurately. The man who always understands these qualities, gets rid of all passionate qualities.</p>
