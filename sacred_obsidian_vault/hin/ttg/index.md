 <a href="ttg05.htm">Fourth Goblin: King Shudraka and Hero's Family. Which of the five deserves the most honour?</a><br>
 <a href="ttg06.htm">Fifth Goblin: The Brave Man, the Wise Man, and the Clever Man. To which should the girl be given?</a><br>
 <a href="ttg07.htm">Sixth Goblin: The Girl who transposed the Heads of her Husband and Brother. Which combination of head and body is her husband?</a><br>
 <a href="ttg08.htm">Seventh Goblin: The Mutual Services of King Fierce-lion and Prince Good. Which is the more deserving?</a><br>
 <a href="ttg09.htm">Eighth Goblin: The Specialist in Food, the Specialist in Women, and the Specialist in Cotton. Which is the cleverest?</a><br>
 <a href="ttg10.htm">Ninth Goblin: The Four Scientific Suitors. To which should the girl be given?</a><br>
 <a href="ttg11.htm">Tenth Goblin: The Three Delicate Wives of King Virtue-banner. Which is the most delicate?</a><br>
 <a href="ttg12.htm">Eleventh Goblin: The King who won a Fairy as his Wife. Why did his counsellor's heart break?</a><br>
 <a href="ttg13.htm">Twelfth Goblin: The Brahman who died because Poison from a Snake in the Claws of a Hawk fell into a Dish of Food given him by a Charitable Woman. Who is to blame for his death?</a><br>
 <a href="ttg14.htm">Thirteenth Goblin: The Girl who showed Great Devotion to the Thief. Did he weep or laugh?</a><br>
 <a href="ttg15.htm">Fourteenth Goblin: The Man who changed into a Woman at Will. Was his wife his or the other man's?</a><br>
 <a href="ttg16.htm">Fifteenth Goblin: The Fairy Prince Cloud-chariot and the Serpent Shell-crest. Which is the more self-sacrificing?</a><br>
 <a href="ttg17.htm">Sixteenth Goblin: The King who died for Love of his General's Wife; the General follows him in Death. Which is the more worthy?</a><br>
 <a href="ttg18.htm">Seventeenth Goblin: The Youth who went through the Proper Ceremonies. Why did he fail to win the magic spell?</a><br>
 <a href="ttg19.htm">Eighteenth Goblin: The Boy whom his Parents, the King, and the Giant conspired to Kill. Why did he laugh at the moment of death?</a><br>
 <a href="ttg20.htm">Nineteenth Goblin: The Man, his Wife, and her Lover, who all died for Love. Which was the most foolish?</a><br>
 <a href="ttg21.htm">Twentieth Goblin: The Four Brothers who brought a Dead Lion to Life. Which is to blame when he kills them all?</a><br>
 <a href="ttg22.htm">Twenty-first Goblin: The Old Hermit who exchanged his Body for that of the Dead Boy. Why did he weep and dance?</a><br>
 <a href="ttg23.htm">Twenty-second Goblin: The Father and Son who married Daughter and Mother. What relation were their children?</a><br>
