 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>Dakshinamurti Stotra</i>, translated by Alladi Mahadeva Sastri, [1920], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">PREFACE TO THE SECOND EDITION</h3>  
 <p>In this edition the Sanskrit texts of <i>Manasollasa</i> and <i>Pranava-vartika</i> have been added, while in the first edition the texts of <i>Dakshinamurti-stotra</i> and <i>Dakshinamurti-Upanishad</i> were alone given in the form of an appendix, separately from their translations. All the four texts are here embodied along with their English translations, for convenient reference. The text of the <i>Manasollasa</i> followed in the first Edition has been revised with the aid of the Mss. of the Adyar Library; and this has rendered necessary some changes in the text and the translation here and there. In other respects the second edition remains the same as the first.</p>  
 <p align="right"><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">A. Mahadeva Sastri</span></p>  
 <p>  ADYAR,<br> <i>September 1919</i>.</p>
