   <h2 align="center">VI, 99. Battle-charm of a king on the eve of battle.</h2>  
 <p>1. I call -upon thee, O Indra, from afar, upon thee for protection against tribulation. I call the strong avenger that has many names, and is of unequalled birth. <br> 2. Where the hostile weapon now rises against us,threatening to slay, there do we place the two arms of Indra round about.<br> 3. The two arms of Indra, the protector, do we place round about us: let him protect us! O god Savitar, and king Soma, render me of confident mind, that I may prosper!</p>
