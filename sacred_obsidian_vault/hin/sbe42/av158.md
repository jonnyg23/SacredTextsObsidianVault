   <h2 align="center">VI, 42. Charm to appease anger.</h2>  
 <p>1. As the bowstring from the bow, thus do I take off thy anger from thy heart, so that, having become of the same mind, we shall associate like friends!<br> 2. Like friends we shall associate-I take off thy anger. Under a stone that is heavy do we cast thy anger.<br> 3. I step upon thy anger with my heel and my fore-foot, so that, bereft of will, thou shalt not speak, shalt come up to my wish!</p>
