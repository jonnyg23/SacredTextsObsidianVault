   <h2 align="center">VI, 9. Charm to secure the love of a woman.</h2>  
 <p>1. Hanker thou after my body, my feet, hanker after my eyes, my thighs! The eyes of thee, as thou lustest after me, and thy hair shall be parched with love?<br> 2. I make thee cling to my arm, cling to my heart, so that thou shalt be in my power, shalt come up to my wish!<br> 3. The cows, the mothers of the ghee, who lick their young, in whose heart love is planted, shall make yonder woman bestow love upon me!</p>
