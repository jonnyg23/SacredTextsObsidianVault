   <h2 align="center">VII, 13. Charm to deprive enemies of their strength.</h2>  
 <p>1. As the rising sun takes away the lustre of the stars, thus do I take away the strength of both the women and the men that hate me.<br> 2. As many enemies as ye are, lookina out auainst me, as I come on--of those that hate me do I take away the strenorth, as the sun takes away the strength of persons asleep (while it rises).</p>
