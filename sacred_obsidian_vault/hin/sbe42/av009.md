   <h2 align="center">I, 22. Charm against jaundice and related diseases.</h2>  
 <p>1. Up to the sun shall go thy heart-ache and thy jaundice: in the colour of the red bull do we eovelop thee!<br> 2. We envelop thee in red tints, unto long life. May this person go unscathed, and be free of vellow colour!<br> 3. The cows whose divinity is Rohini, they who, moreover, axe (themselves) red (róhinin)-(in their) every form and every strength we do envelop thee.<br> 4. Into the parrots, into the ropanâkâs (thrush) do we put thy jaundice, and, furthermore, into the hâridravas (yellow wagtail) do we put thy jaundice.</p>
