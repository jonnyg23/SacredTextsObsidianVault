   <h2 align="center">VI, 64. Charm to allay discord.</h2>  
 <p>1. Do ye agree, unite yourselves, may your minds be in harmony, just as the gods of old in harmony-. sat down to their share!<br> 2. Same be their counsel, same their assembly, same their aim, in common their thought! The 'same' oblation do I sacrifice for you: do ye enter upon the same plan!<br> Same be your intention, same your hearts! Same be your mind, so that it may be perfectly in common to you!</p>
