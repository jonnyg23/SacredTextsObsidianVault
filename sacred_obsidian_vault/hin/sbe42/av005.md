   <h2 align="center">VII, 116. Charm against takman (fever).</h2>  
 <p>1. Homage (be) to the deliriously hot, the shaking, exciting, impetuous (takman)! Homage to the cold (takman), to him that in the past fulfilled desires!<br> 2. May (the takman) that returns on the morrow, he that returns on two (successive) days, the impious one, pass into this frog!</p>
