   <h2 align="center">VII, 11. Charm to protect grain from lightning.</h2>  
 <p>1. With thy broad thunder,with the beacon, elevated by the gods that pervade this all, with the lightning do thou not destroy our grain, O god; nor do thou destroy it with the rays of the sun!</p>
