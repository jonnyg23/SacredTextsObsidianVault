   <h1 align="center">VIII.</h1>  <h1 align="center">CHARMS IN EXPIATION OF SIN AND DEFILEMENT.</h1>  <h2 align="center">VI, 45. Prayer against mental delinquency.</h2>  
 <p>1. Pass far away, O sin of the mind! Why dost thou utter things not to be uttered? Pass away, I love thee not! To the trees, the forests go on! With the house, the cattle, is my mind.<br> 2. What wrongs we have committed through imprecation, calumny, and false speech, either awake, or asleep--Agni shall put far away from us all offensive evil deeds!<br> 3. What, O Indra Brahmanaspati, we do falselymay Praketas ('care-taker') Ângirasa protect us from misfortune, and from evil!</p>
