   <h2 align="center">VI, 79. Charm for procuring increase of grain.</h2>  
 <p>1. May this bounteous Nabhasaspati (the lord of the cloud) preserve for us (possessions) without measure in our house!<br> 2. Do thou, O Nabhasaspati, keep strengthening food in our house, may prosperity and goods come hither!<br> 3. O bounteous god, thou dost command thousandfold prosperity: of that do thou bestow upon iis, of that do thou give us, in that may we share with thee!</p>
