 <p><a name="page_1"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 1</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">SHAKUNTALA AND OTHER WRITINGS</h3>  <h3 align="center">SHAKUNTALA</h3>  <h3 align="center">A PLAY IN SEVEN ACTS</h3>  
 <p><a name="page_2"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 2</font></a></p>  <h1 align="center">DRAMATIS PERSONÆ</h1>  
 <p></p><dir>KING DUSHYANTA.<br> BHARATA, <i>nicknamed</i> All-tamer, <i>his son</i>.<br> MADHAVYA, <i>a clown, his companion</i>.<br> His charioteer.<br> RAIVATAKA, <i>a door-keeper</i>.<br> BHADRASENA, <i>a general</i>.<br> KARABHAKA, <i>a servant</i>.<br> PARVATAYANA, <i>a chamberlain</i>.<br> SOMARATA, <i>a chaplain</i>.</dir>  
 <p></p><dir>KANVA, <i>hermit father</i>.<br>    <i>his pupils</i>:<br>      SHARNGARAVA<br>      SHARADVATA<br>      HARITA<br> DURVASAS, <i>an irascible sage</i>.</dir>  
 <p></p><dir>The chief of police.<br>    <i>policemen</i>:<br>      SUCHAKA<br>      JANUKA<br> A fisherman.</dir>  
 <p></p><dir>SHAKUNTALA, <i>foster-child of Kanva</i>.<br>    <i>her friends</i>:<br>      ANUSUYA<br>      PRIYAMVADA<br> GAUTAMI, <i>hermit-mother</i>.</dir>  
 <p></p><dir>KASHYAPA, <i>father of the gods</i>.<br> ADITI, <i>mother of the gods</i>.<br> MATALI, <i>charioteer of heaven's king</i>.<br> GALAVA, <i>a pupil in heaven</i>.<br> MISHRAKESHI, <i>a heavenly nymph</i>.</dir>  
 <p><i>Stage-director and actress (in the prologue), hermits and hermit-women, two court poets, palace attendants, invisible fairies</i>.</p>  
 <p>The first four acts pass in Kanva's forest hermitage; acts five and six in the king's palace; act seven on a heavenly mountain. The time is perhaps seven years.</p>
