 <p>ha<i>m</i>sá, swan, I, 65, 9; III, 8, 9.</p>

 <p>har: háryamâ<i>n</i>a, longed for, III, 6, 4;—práti harya, accept graciously, I, 144, 7; práti háryâ<i>h</i>, thou acceptest, V, 2, 11.</p>  
 <p>hári, golden, I, 95, 1;—bay horse, fallow steed: hári-bhyâm, I, 76, 3; IV, 15, 7:1; hárî íti, IV, 15, 8; V, 27, 2.</p>  
 <p>hári-ke<i>s</i>a, whose hair is golden, III, 2, 13.</p>

 <p>hót<i>ri</i>, the Hot<i>ri</i> priest, I, 1, 1; 5, &amp;c.; I, 94, 6; II, 9, 1; III, 17, 5; hótârâ daívyâ, I, 13, 8:1; 142, 8; 188, 7; II, 3, 7; III, 4, 7; V, 5, 7;—saptá hót<i>ri</i>-bhi<i>h</i>, III, 10, 4.</p>
