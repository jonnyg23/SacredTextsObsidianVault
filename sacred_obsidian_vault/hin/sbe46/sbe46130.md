 <p><a name="page_418"><font size="1" color="green">p. 418</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">MA<i>N</i><i>D</i>ALA V, HYMN 26.</h3>  <h4 align="center">ASH<i>T</i>AKA IV, ADHYÂYA 1, VARGA 19–20.</h4>  
 <p>1. Agni, purifier! With thy splendour, with thy delightful tongue, O god, bring hither the gods and perform the sacrifice.</p>  
 <p>2. Thee therefore we approach, who swimmest in gh<i>ri</i>ta <a href="sbe46130.htm#an_5:26:2:1">1</a>, O (god) with brilliant light, thee of sun-like aspect. Bring hither the gods that they may feast.</p>  
 <p>3. Let us kindle thee, O sage, the brilliant offerer of feasts (to the gods), O Agni, the mighty (god) at the sacrifice.</p>  
 <p> </p>  
 <p>4. Agni, come hither with all the gods to the gift of the offering. We choose thee as our Hot<i>ri</i>.</p>  
 <p>5. Bring to the sacrificer who presses (Soma), Agni, abundance of heroes. Sit down on the sacrificial grass together with the gods.</p>  
 <p>6. Being kindled, Agni, conqueror of thousandfold (wealth), thou makest the ordinances (of the world) thrive, the praiseworthy messenger of the gods.</p>  
 <p> </p>  
 <p>7 <a href="sbe46130.htm#an_5:26:7:1">1</a>. Lay down Agni <i>G</i>âtavedas, the carrier of offerings, the youngest, the god, the priest.</p>  
 <p>8. May the sacrifice which best encompasses the gods, proceed to-day in due order. Spread the sacrificial grass that (the gods) may sit down on it.</p>  
 <p>9. May the Maruts, the A<i>s</i>vins, Mitra and Varu<i>n</i>a sit down on this (sacrificial grass). the gods with all their folk.</p>  
 <p><a name="page_419"><font size="1" color="green">p. 419</font></a></p>  <h4 align="center">NOTES.</h4>  
 <p>The same <i>Ri</i>shis. Metre, Gâyatrî. Verses 1–3 = SV. II, 871–873. Verse 1= TS. I, 3, 14, 8; 5, 5, 3; IV, 6, 1, 2; MS. I, 5, 1; II, 10, 1; IV, 10, 1; VS. XVII, 8. Verse 3 = TS. I, 1, 11, 2; VS. II, 4; comp. MS. I, 1, 12. Verse 7 = MS. IV, 11, 1.</p>  
 <p>As V, 26, this Sûkta also consists of T<i>ri</i><i>k</i>a hymns.</p>  <h5 align="center">Verse 2.</h5>  
 <p>Note <a name="an_5:26:2:1">1</a>. Comp. above, IV, 2, 3, note 1.</p>  <h5 align="center">Verse 7.</h5>  
 <p>Note <a name="an_5:26:7:1">1</a>. With verses 7 and 8, compare above, V, 22, 2.</p>
