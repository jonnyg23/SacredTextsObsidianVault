 <p align="center"> <font size="-1" color="green">Î</font></p>  
 <p>î: prá îmahe va<i>h</i>, we entreat for you, I, 36, 1:1;—â´ îmahe, we implore, III, 26, 5.</p>  
 <p>î<i>d</i>, to magnify: î<i>l</i>e, I, 1, 1:2; 44, 4; III, 1, 15; 27, 2; 12; IV, 3, 3; 9:1; î´<i>l</i>ate, I, 36, 1; 128, 8; III, 6, 3; 10, 2; 13, 2; 27, 14; V, 1, 7; 8, 3; 9, 1; 14, 2; 3; 21, 3; î<i>l</i>ata, I, 96, 3:1; î<i>t</i><i>t</i>e, V, 12, 6; î<i>l</i>îta, V, 17, 1; 21, 4; î<i>l</i>âna, II, 6, 6; 28, 1; î<i>li</i>tá, I. 13, 4:1; 142, 4:1; II, 3, 3; V, 5, 3; 7.</p>  
 <p>î<i>l</i>énya, to be magnified, I, 79, 5; 146, 5; III, 27, 13; V, 1, 9; 14, 5.</p>  
 <p>î´<i>d</i>ya, worthy of being magnified: î´<i>d</i>ya<i>h</i>, I, 1, 2; 12, 3:2; 75, 4; 188, 3; II, 1, 4; III, 2, 2; 5, 6; 9; 9, 8; 17, 4; 27, 4; 29, 2; 7; IV, 7, 1; 2; V, 22, 1.</p>

 <p>î´vat, like this, IV, 4, 6; 25, 5.</p>  
 <p>î<i>s</i>, to rule: î<i>s</i>ata, I, 36, 16; II, 7, 2; vásva<i>h</i> î<i>s</i>e, I, 71, 9; yâ´vat î´<i>s</i>e, as far as I have power, III, 18, 3.</p>  
 <p>î<i>s</i>âná, master, lord, I, 73, 9; 79, 4; 141, 3.</p>  
 <p>îsh: îshate, they flee, I, 141, 8;—â´ îshate, he advances, I, 149, 1:2.</p>
