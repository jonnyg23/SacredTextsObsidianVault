 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Rig Veda</i>, tr. by Ralph T.H. Griffith, [1896], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">HYMN CXLI. Viśvedevas.</h3>  
 <p>1. TURN hither, Agni, speak to us: come to us with a gracious mind.<br> Enrich us, Master of the house: thou art the Giver of our wealth.<br> 2 Let Aryarnan vouchsafe us wealth, and Bhaga, and Bṛhaspati.<br> Let the Gods give their gifts, and let Sūnṛtā, Goddess, grant us wealth.<br> 3 We call King Soma to our aid, and Agni with our songs and hymns,<br> Ādityas, Viṣṇu, Sūrya, and the Brahman Priest Bṛhaspati.<br> 4 Indra, Vāyu, Bṛhaspati, Gods swift to listen, we invoke,<br> That in the synod all the folk may be benevolent to us.<br> 5 Urge Aryaman to send us gifts, and Indra, and Bṛhaspati,<br> Vāta, Viṣṇu, Sarasvatī and the Strong Courser Savitar.<br> 6 Do thou, O Agni, with thy fires strengthen our prayer and sacrifice:<br> Urge givers to bestow their wealth to aid our service of the Gods.</p>
