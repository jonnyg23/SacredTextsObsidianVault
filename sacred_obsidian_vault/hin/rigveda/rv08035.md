 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Rig Veda</i>, tr. by Ralph T.H. Griffith, [1896], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">HYMN XXXV. Aśvins.</h3>  
 <p>1. WITH Agni and with Indra, Viṣṇu. Varuṇa, with the Ādityas, Rudras, Vasus, closely leagued;<br> Accordant, of one mind with Sūrya and with Dawn, O Aśvins, drink the Soma juice.<br> 2 With all the Holy Thoughts, all being Mighty Ones! in close alliance wil the Mountains, Heaven, and Earth;<br> Accordant. of one mind with Sūrya and with Dawn, O Aśvins, drink the Soma juice.<br> 3 With all the Deities, three times eleven, here, in close alliance with the Maruts, Bhṛgus, Floods;<br> Accordant, of one mind with Sūrya and with Dawn, O Aśvins, drink the Soma juice.<br> 4 Accept the sacrifice, attend to this my call: come nigh, O ye Twain Gods, to all libations here.<br> Accordant, of one mind with Sūrya and with Dawn, O Aśvins, bring us strengthening food.<br> 5 Accept our praise-song as a youth accepts a maid. Come nigh, O ye Twain Gods, to all libations here.<br> Accordant, of one mind with Sūrya and with Dawn, O Aśvins, bring us strengthening food.<br> 6 Accept the songs we sing, accept the solemn rite. Come nigh, O ye Twain Gods, to all libations here.<br> Accordant, of one mind with Sūrya and with Dawn, O Aśvins, bring us strengthening food.<br> 7 Ye fly as starlings fly unto the forest trees; like buffaloes ye seek the Soma we have shed.<br> Accordant, of one mind with Sūrya and with Dawn, come thrice, O Aśvins, to our home.<br> 8 Ye fly like swans, like those who travel on their way; like buffaloes ye seek the Soma we have shed.<br> Accordant, of one mind with Sūrya and with Dawn, come thrice, O Aśvins, to our home.<br> 9 Ye fly to our oblation like a pair of hawks; like buffaloes ye seek the Soma we have shed.<br> Accordant, of one mind with Sūrya and with Dawn, come thrice, O Aśvins, to our home.<br> 10 Come hitherward and drink and satisfy yourselves, bestow upon us progeny and affluence.<br> Accordant, of one mind with Sūrya and with Dawn, O Aśvins, grant us vigorous strength.<br> 11 Conquer your foes, protect us, praise your worshippers; bestow upon us progeny and affluence.<br> Accordant, of one mind with Sūrya and with Dawn, O Aśvins, grant us vigorous strength.<br> 12 Slay enemies, animate men whom ye befriend; bestow upon us progeny and affluence.<br> Accordant, of one mind with Sūrya and with Dawn, O Aśvins, grant us vigorous strength.<br> 13 With Mitra, Varuṇa, Dharma, and the Maruts in your company approach unto your praiser's call.<br> Accordant, of one mind with Sūrya and with Dawn, and with the Ādityas, Aśvins! come.<br> 14 With Viṣṇu and the Aṅgirases attending you, and with the Maruts come unto your praiser's call.<br> Accordant, of one mind with Sūrya and with Dawn, and with the Ādityas, Aśvins! come.<br> 15 With Ṛbhus and With Vājas. O ye Mighty Ones, leagued with the Maruts come ye to your praiser's call.<br> Accordant, of one mind with Sūrya and with Dawn, and with the Ādityas, Aśvins! come.<br> 16 Give spirit to our prayer and animate our thoughts; slay ye the Rākṣasas and drive away disease.<br> Accordant, of One mind with Sūrya and with Dawn, -the presser's Soma, Aśvins drink.<br> 17 Strengthen the Ruling Power, strengthen the men of war; slay ye the Rākṣasas and drive away disease.<br> Accordant, of one mind with Sūrya and with Dawn, the presser's Soma, Aśvins drink.<br> 18 Give strength unto the milch-kine, give the people strength, slay ye the Rākṣasas and drive away disease.<br> Accordant, of one mind with Sūrya and with Dawn, the presser's Soma, Aśvins drink.<br> 19 As ye heard Atri's earliest eulogy, so hear Śyāvāśva, Soma-presser, ye who reel in joy.<br> Accordant, of one mind with Sūrya and with Dawn, drink juice, O Aśvins, three days old.<br> 20 Further like running streams Śyāvāśva's eulogies who presses out the Soma, ye who reel in joy.<br> Accordant, of one mind with Sūrya and with Dawn, drink juice, O Aśvins, three days old.<br> 21 Seize, as ye grasp the reins, Śyāvāśva's solemn rites who presses out the Soma, ye who reel in joy.<br> Accordant, of one mind with Sūrya and with Dawn, drink juice, O Aśvins, three days old.<br> 22 Drive down your chariot hitherward drink ye the Soma's savoury juice.<br> Approach, ye Aśvins, come to us: I call you, eager for your aid. Grant treasures to the worshipper.<br> 23 When sacrifice which tells our reverence hath begun. Heroes! to drink the gushing juice,<br> Approach, ye Aśvins, come to us: I call you, eager for your aid. Grant treasures to the worshipper.<br> 24 Sate you with consecrated drink, with juice effused, ye Deities.<br> Approach, ye Aśvins, come to us: I call you, eager for your aid. Grant treasures to the worshipper.</p>
