 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Rig Veda</i>, tr. by Ralph T.H. Griffith, [1896], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">HYMN XLIII. Soma Pavamana.</h3>  
 <p>1. WE will enrobe with sacred song the Lovely One who, as a Steed,<br> Is decked with milk for rapturous joy.<br> 2 All songs of ours desiring grace adorn him in the ancient way,<br> Indu for Indra, for his drink.<br> 3 Soma flows on when purified, beloved and adorned with songs,<br> Songs of the sage Medhyātithi.<br> 4 O Soma Pavamana, find exceeding glorious wealth for us,<br> Wealth, Indu, fraught with boundless might.<br> 5 Like courser racing to the prize Indu, the lover of the Gods,<br> Roars, as he passes, in the sieve.<br> 6 Flow on thy way to win us strength, to speed the sage who praises thee:<br> Soma, bestow heroic power.</p>
