 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Rig Veda</i>, tr. by Ralph T.H. Griffith, [1896], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">HYMN CLVII. Viśvedevas.</h3>  
 <p>1. WE will, with Indra and all Gods to aid us, bring these existing worlds into subjection.<br> 2 Our sacrifice, our bodies, and our offspr. let Indra form together with Ādityas.<br> 3 With the Ādityas, with the band of Maruts, may Indra be Protector of our bodies.<br> 4 As when the Gods came, after they had slaughtered the Asuras, keeping safe their Godlike nature,<br> 5 Brought the Sun hitherward with mighty powers, and looked about them on their vigorous Godhead.</p>
