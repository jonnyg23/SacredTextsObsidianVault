 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Rig Veda</i>, tr. by Ralph T.H. Griffith, [1896], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">HYMN LIII. Soma Pavamana.</h3>  
 <p>1. O THOU with stones for arms, thy powers, crushing the fiends, have raised themselves:<br> Chase thou the foes who compass us.<br> 2 Thou conquerest thus with might when car meets car, and when the prize is staked:<br> With fearless heart will I sing praise.<br> 3 No one with evil thought assails this Pavamana's holy laws:<br> Crush him who fain would fight with thee.<br> 4 For Indra to the streams they drive the tawny rapture-dropping Steed,<br> Indu the bringer of delight.</p>
