 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Rig Veda</i>, tr. by Ralph T.H. Griffith, [1896], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">HYMN CXC. Creation.</h3>  
 <p>1. FROM Fervour kindled to its height Eternal Law and Truth were born:<br> Thence was the Night produced, and thence the billowy flood of sea arose.<br> 2 From that same billowy flood of sea the Year was afterwards produced,<br> Ordainer of the days nights, Lord over all who close the eye.<br> 3 Dhātar, the great Creator, then formed in due order Sun and Moon.<br> He formed in order Heaven and Earth, the regions of the air, and light.</p>
