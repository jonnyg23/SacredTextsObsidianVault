 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Rig Veda</i>, tr. by Ralph T.H. Griffith, [1896], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">HYMN CLVIII. Sūrya.</h3>  
 <p>1. MAY Sūrya guard us out of heaven, and Vāta from the firmament,<br> And Agni from terrestrial spots.<br> 2 Thou Savitar whose flame deserves hundred libations, be thou pleased:<br> From failing lightning keep us safe.<br> 3 May Savitar the God, and may Parvata also give us sight;<br> May the Creator give us sight.<br> 4 Give sight unto our eye, give thou our bodies sight that they may see:<br> May we survey, discern this world.<br> 5 Thus, Sūrya, may we look on thee, on thee most lovely to behold,<br> See clearly with the eyes of men.</p>
