 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Rig Veda</i>, tr. by Ralph T.H. Griffith, [1896], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">HYMN CLXXXVIII. Agni.</h3>  
 <p>1. Now send ye Jātavedas forth, send hitherward the vigorous Steed<br> To seat him on our sacred grass.<br> 2. I raise the lofty eulogy of Jātavedas, raining boons,<br> With sages for his hero band.<br> 3 With flames of Jātavedas which carry oblation to the Gods,<br> May he promote our sacrifice.</p>
