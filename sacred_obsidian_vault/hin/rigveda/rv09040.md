 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Rig Veda</i>, tr. by Ralph T.H. Griffith, [1896], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">HYMN XL. Soma Pavamana.</h3>  
 <p>1. THE Very Active hath assailed, while purified, all enemies:<br> They deck the Sage with holy songs.<br> 2 The Red hath mounted to his place; to India, goes the mighty juice:<br> He settles in his firm abode.<br> 3 O Indu, Soma, send us now great opulence from every side, Pour on us treasures thousandfold.<br> 4 O Soma Pavamana, bring, Indu, all splcndours hitherward:<br> Find for us food in boundless store.<br> 5 As thou art cleansed, bring hero strength and riches to thy worshipper,<br> And prosper thou the singer's hymns.<br> 6 O Indu, Soma, being cleansed, bring hither riches doublypiled,<br> Wealth, mighty Indu, meet for lauds.</p>
