 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Rig Veda</i>, tr. by Ralph T.H. Griffith, [1896], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">HYMN XXXIV. Some Pavamana.</h3>  
 <p>1. THE drop of Soma juice effused flows onward with this stream impelled.<br> Rending strong places with its might.<br> 2 Poured forth to Indra, Varuṇa, to Vāyu and the Marut hosts,<br> To Viṣṇu, flows the Soma juice.<br> 3 With stones they press the Soma forth, the Strong conducted by the strong:<br> They milk the liquor out with skill.<br> 4 Tis he whom Trita must refine, tis he who shall make Indra glad:<br> The Tawny One is decked with tints.<br> 5 Him do the Sons of Pṛśni milk, the dwelling-place of sacrifice,<br> Oblation lovely and most dear.<br> 6 To him in one unitcd stream th-,se songs flow on straight forward. he,<br> Loud voiced, hath made the milch-kine low.</p>
