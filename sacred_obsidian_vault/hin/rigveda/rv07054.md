 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Rig Veda</i>, tr. by Ralph T.H. Griffith, [1896], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">HYMN LIV. Vastospati.</h3>  
 <p>1. ACKNOWLEDGE us, O Guardian of the Homestead: bring no disease, and give us happy entrance.<br> Whateer we ask of thee, be pleased to grant it, and prosper thou quadrupeds and bipeds.<br> 2 Protector of the Home, be our promoter: increase our wealth in kine and steeds, O Indu.<br> May we be ever-youthful in thy friendship: be pleased in us as in his sons a father.<br> 3 Through thy dear fellowship that bringeth welfare, may we be victors, Guardian of the Dwelling!<br> Protect our happiness in rest and labour. Preserve us evermore, ye Gods, with blessings.</p>
