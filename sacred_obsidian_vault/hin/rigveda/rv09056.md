 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Rig Veda</i>, tr. by Ralph T.H. Griffith, [1896], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">HYMN LVI. Soma Pavamana.</h3>  
 <p>1. SWIFT to the purifying sieve flows Soma as exalted Law,<br> Slaying the fiends, loving the Gods.<br> 2 When Soma pours the strengthening food a hundred ever-active streams<br> To Indra's friendship win theirway.<br> 3 Ten Dames have sung to welcome thee, even as a maiden greets her love:<br> O Soma, thou art decked to win.<br> 4 Flow hitherward, O Indu, sweet to Indra and to Viṣṇu: guard<br> The men, the singers, from distress.</p>
