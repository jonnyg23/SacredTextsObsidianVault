 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Rig Veda</i>, tr. by Ralph T.H. Griffith, [1896], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">HYMN LXXIV. Soma-Rudra.</h3>  
 <p>1. HOLD fast your Godlike sway, O Soma-Rudra: let these our sacrifices quickly reach you.<br> Placing in every house your seven great treasures, bring blessing to our quadrupeds and bipeds.<br> 2 Soma and Rudra, chase to every quarter the sickness that hath visited our dwelling.<br> Drive Nirrti away into the distance, and give us excellent and happy glories.<br> 3 Provide, O Soma-Rudra, for our bodies all needful medicines to heal and cure us.<br> Set free and draw away the sin committed which we have still inherent in our persons.<br> 4 Armed with keen shafts and weapons, kind and loving, be gracious unto us, Soma and Rudra.<br> Release us from the noose of Varuṇa; keep us from sorrow, in your tender loving-kindness.</p>
