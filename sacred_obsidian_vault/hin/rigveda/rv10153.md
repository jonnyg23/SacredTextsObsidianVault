 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Rig Veda</i>, tr. by Ralph T.H. Griffith, [1896], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">HYMN CLIII. Indra.</h3>  
 <p>1. SWAYING about, the Active Ones came nigh to Indra at his birth,<br> And shared his great heroic might.<br> 2 Based upon strength and victory and power, O Indra is thy birth:<br> Thou, Mighty One, art strong indeed.<br> 3 Thou art the Vṛtra-slayer, thou, Indra, hast spread the firmament:<br> Thou hast with might upheld the heavens.<br> 4 Thou, Indra, bearest in thine arms the lightning that accords with thee,<br> Whetting thy thunderbolt with might.<br> 5 Thou, Indra, art preeminent over all creatures in thy might:<br> Thou hast pervaded every place.</p>
