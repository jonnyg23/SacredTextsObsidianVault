 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Rig Veda</i>, tr. by Ralph T.H. Griffith, [1896], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">HYMN LX. Soma Pavamana.</h3>  
 <p>1. SING forth and laud with sacred song most active Pavamana, laud<br> Indu who sees with thousand eyes.<br> 2 Thee who hast thousand eyes to see, bearer of thousand burthens, they<br> Have filtered through the fleecy cloth.<br> 3 He, Pavamana, hath streamed through the fleece then: he runs into the jars,<br> Finding his way to Indra's heart.<br> 4 That Indra may be bounteous, flow, most active Soma, for our weal:<br> Bring genial seed with progeny.</p>
