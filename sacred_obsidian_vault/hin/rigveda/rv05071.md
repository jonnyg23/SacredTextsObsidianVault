 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Rig Veda</i>, tr. by Ralph T.H. Griffith, [1896], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">HYMN LXXI. Mitra-Varuṇa.</h3>  
 <p>1. O Varuṇa and Mitra, ye who slay the foemen, come with might<br> To this our goodly sacrifice.<br> 2 For, Varuṇa and Mitra, ye Sages are Rulers over all. Fill full our songs, for this ye can.<br> 3 Come to the juice that we have pressed. Varuṇa, Mitra, come to drink<br> This Soma of the worshipper.</p>
