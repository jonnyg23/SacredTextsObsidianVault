 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Rig Veda</i>, tr. by Ralph T.H. Griffith, [1896], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">HYMN XLVIII. Soma Pavamana.</h3>  
 <p>1. WITH sacrifice we seek to thee kind Cherisher of manly might<br> In mansions of the lofty heavens;<br> 2 Gladdening crusher of the bold, ruling with very mighty sway,<br> Destroyer of a hundred forts.<br> 3 Hence, Sapient One! the Falcon, strong of wing, unwearied, brought thee down,<br> Lord over riches, from the sky.<br> 4 That each may see the light, the Bird brought us the guard of Law, the Friend<br> Of all, the speeder through the air.<br> 5 And now, sent forth, it hath attained to mighty power and majesty,<br> Most active, ready to assist.</p>
