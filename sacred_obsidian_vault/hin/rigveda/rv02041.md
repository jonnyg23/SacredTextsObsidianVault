 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Rig Veda</i>, tr. by Ralph T.H. Griffith, [1896], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">HYMN XLI. Various Deities.</h3>  
 <p>1. O VĀYU, come to us with all the thousand chariots that are thine,<br> Team-borne, to drink the Soma juice.<br> 2 Drawn by thy team, O Vāyu, come; to thee is offered this, the pure.<br> Thou visitest the presser's house.<br> 3 Indra and Vāyu, drawn by teams, ye Heroes, come today and drink.<br> Of the bright juice when blent with milk.<br> 4 This Soma hath been shed for you, Law-strengtheners, Mitra-Varuṇa!<br> Listen ye here to this my call.<br> 5 Both Kings who never injure aught seat them in their supremest home,<br> The thousand-pillared, firmly-based.<br> 6 Fed with oblation, Sovran Kings, Ādityas, Lords of liberal gifts.<br> They wait on him whose life is true.<br> 7 With kine, Nāsatyas, and with steeds, come, Aśvins, Rudras, to the house<br> That will protect its heroes well;<br> 8 Such, wealthy Gods! as none afar nor standing nigh to us may harm,<br> Yea, no malicious mortal foe.<br> 9 As such, O longed-far Aśvins, lead us on to wealth of varied sort,<br> Wealth that shall bring us room and rest.<br> 10 Verily Indra, conquering all, driveth een mighty fear away,<br> For firm is he and swift to act.<br> 11 Indra be gracious unto us: sin shall not reach us afterward,<br> And good shall be before us still.<br> 12 From all the regions of the world let Indra send security,<br> The foe-subduer, swift to act.<br> 13 O all ye Gods, come hitherward: hear this mine invocation, seat<br> Yourselves upon this sacred grass.<br> 14 Among the Śunahotras strong for you is this sweet gladdening draught.<br> Drink ye of this delightsome juice.<br> 15 Ye <a href="errata.htm#13">Maruts</a> led by Indra, Gods with Pūṣan for your bounteousest,<br> Hear all of you this call of mine.<br> 16 Best Mother, best of Rivers, best of Goddesses, Sarasvatī, We are, as twere, of no repute and dear Mother, give thou us renown.<br> 17 In thee, Sarasvatī, divine, all generations have their stay.<br> Be, glad with Śunahotra's sons: O Goddess grant us progeny.<br> 18 Enriched with sacrifice, accept Sarasvatī, these prayers of ours,<br> Thoughts which Gṛtsamadas beloved of Gods bring, Holy One, to thee.<br> 19 Ye who bless sacrifice, go forth, for verily we choose you both,<br> And Agni who conveys our gifts.<br> 20 This our effectual sacrifice, reaching the sky, shall Heaven and Earth<br> Present unto the Gods to-day.<br> 21 In both your laps, ye guileless Ones, the Holy Gods shall sit them down<br> To-day to drink the Soma here.</p>
