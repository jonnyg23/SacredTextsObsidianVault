 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Rig Veda</i>, tr. by Ralph T.H. Griffith, [1896], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">HYMN CLXXXV. Aditi.</h3>  
 <p>1. GREAT, unassailable must he the heavenly favour of Three Gods,<br> Varuṇa, Mitra, Aryaman.<br> 2 O'er these, neither at home nor yet abroad or pathways that are Strange,<br> The evil-minded foe hath power<br> 3 Nor over him,. the man on whom the Sons of Aditi bestow Eternal light that he may live.</p>
