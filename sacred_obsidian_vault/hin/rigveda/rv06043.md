 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Rig Veda</i>, tr. by Ralph T.H. Griffith, [1896], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">HYMN XLIII. Indra</h3>  
 <p>1. IN whose wild joy thou madest once Śambara Divodāsa's prey,<br> This Soma is pressed out for thee, O Indra: drink!<br> 2 Whose gladdening draught, shed from the points, thou guardest in the midst and end,<br> This Soma is pressed out for thee, O Indra drink!<br> 3 In whose wild joy thou settest free the kine held fast within the rock,<br> This Soma is pressed out for thee, O Indra: drink!<br> 4 This, in whose juice delighting thou gainest the might of Maghavan,<br> This Soma is pressed out for thee, O Indra drink!</p>
