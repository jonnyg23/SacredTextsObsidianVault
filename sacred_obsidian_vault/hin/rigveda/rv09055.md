 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Rig Veda</i>, tr. by Ralph T.H. Griffith, [1896], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">HYMN LV. Soma Pavamana.</h3>  
 <p>1. POUR on us with thy juice all kinds of corn, each sort of nourishment,<br> And, Soma, all felicities.<br> 2 As thine, O Indu, is the praise, and thine what springeth from the juice,<br> Seat thee on the dear sacred grass.<br> 3 And, finding for us kine and steeds, O Soma, with thy juice flow on<br> Through days that fly most rapidly.<br> 4 As one who conquers, neer subdued, attacks and stays the enemy,<br> Thus, Vanquisher of thousands! flow.</p>
