 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Rig Veda</i>, tr. by Ralph T.H. Griffith, [1896], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">HYMN CLXXXVI. Vāyu.</h3>  
 <p>1. FILLING our hearts with health and joy, may Vāta breathe his balm on us<br> May he prolong our days of life.<br> 2 Thou art our Father, Vāta, yea, thou art a Brother and a friend,<br> So give us strength that we may live.<br> 3 The store of Amṛta laid away yonder, O Vāta, in thine home,<br> Give us thereof that we may live.</p>
