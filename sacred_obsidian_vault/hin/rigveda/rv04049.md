 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Rig Veda</i>, tr. by Ralph T.H. Griffith, [1896], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">HYMN XLIX. Indra-Bṛhaspati.</h3>  
 <p>1. DEAR is this offering in your mouth, O Indra and Bṛhaspati:<br> Famed is the laud, the gladdening draught.<br> 2 This lovely Soma is effused, O Indra and Bṛhaspati,<br> For you, to drink it and rejoice.<br> 3 As Soma-drinkers to our house come, Indra and Bṛhaspati-and Indra-to drink Soma juice.<br> 4 Vouchsafe us riches hundredfold, O Indra, and Bṛhaspati,<br> With store of horses, thousandfold.<br> 5 O Indra. and Bṛhaspati, we call you when the meath is shed,<br> With songs, to drink the Soma juice.<br> 6 Drink, Indra and Bṛhaspati, the Soma in the giver's house:<br> Delight yourselves abiding there.</p>
