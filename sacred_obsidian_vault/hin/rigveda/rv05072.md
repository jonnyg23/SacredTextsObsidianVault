 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Rig Veda</i>, tr. by Ralph T.H. Griffith, [1896], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">HYMN LXXII. Mitra-Varuṇa.</h3>  
 <p>1 To Varuṇa and Mitra we offer with songs, as Atri did. Sit on the sacred grass to drink the Soma juice.<br> 2 By Ordinance and Law ye dwell in peace secure, bestirring men.<br> Sit on the sacred grass to drink the Soma juice.<br> 3 May Varuṇa and Mitra, for our help, accept the sacrifice.<br> Sit on the sacred grass to drink the Soma juice.</p>
