 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Rig Veda</i>, tr. by Ralph T.H. Griffith, [1896], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">HYMN CLXXII. Maruts.</h3>  
 <p>1. WONDERFUL let your coming be, wondrous with help, ye Bounteous Ones,<br> Maruts, who gleam as serpents gleam.<br> 2 Far be from us, O Maruts, ye free givers, your impetuous shaft;<br> Far from us be the stone ye hurl.<br> 3 O Bounteous Givers, touch ye not, O Maruts, Tṛṇskanda's folk;<br> Lift ye us up that we may live.</p>
