 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Rig Veda</i>, tr. by Ralph T.H. Griffith, [1896], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">HYMN CL. Agni.</h3>  
 <p>1. AGNI, thy faithful servant I call upon thee with many a gift,<br> As in the keeping of the great inciting God;<br> 2 Thou who neer movest thee to aid the indolent, the godless man,<br> Him who though wealthy never brings an offering.<br> 3 Splendid, O Singer, is that man, mightiest of the great in heaven.<br> Agni, may we be foremost, we thy worshippers.</p>
