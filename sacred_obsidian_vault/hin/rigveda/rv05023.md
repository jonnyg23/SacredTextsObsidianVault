 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Rig Veda</i>, tr. by Ralph T.H. Griffith, [1896], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">HYMN XXIII. Agni.</h3>  
 <p>1. By thy fair splendour's mighty power, O Agni, bring victorious wealth,<br> Wealth that oercometh all mankind, and, near us, conquereth in fight.<br> 2 Victorious Agni, bring to us the wealth that vanquisheth in war;<br> For thou art wonderful and true, giver of strength in herds of kine.<br> 3 For all the folk with one accord, whose sacred grass is trimmed and strewn,<br> Invite thee to their worship-halls, as a dear Priest, for choicest wealth.<br> 4 For he, the God of all men, hath gotten him might that quelleth foes.<br> O Agni, in these homes shine forth, bright God! for our prosperity, shine, Purifier! splendidly.</p>
