 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Rig Veda</i>, tr. by Ralph T.H. Griffith, [1896], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">HYMN LVIL Viśvedevas.</h3>  
 <p>1. LET us not, Indra, leave the path, the Soma-presser's sacrifice:<br> Let no malignity dwell with us.<br> 2 May we obtain, completely wrought, the thread spun out to reach the Gods,<br> That perfecteth the sacrifice.<br> 3 We call the spirit hither with the Soma of our parted sires,<br> Yea, with the Fathers' holy hymns.<br> 4 Thy spirit come to thee again for wisdom, energy, and lire,<br> That thou mayst long behold the sun!<br> 5 O Fathers, may the Heavenly Folk give us our spirit once again,<br> That we may be with those who live.<br> 6 O Soma with the spirit still within us, blest with progeny,<br> May we be busied in the law.</p>
