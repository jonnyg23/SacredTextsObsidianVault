 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Rig Veda</i>, tr. by Ralph T.H. Griffith, [1896], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">HYMN LIV. Soma Pavamana.</h3>  
 <p>1. AFTER his ancient splendour, they, the bold, have drawn the bright milk from<br> The Sage who wins a thousand gifts.<br> 2 In aspect he is like the Sun; he runneth forward to the lakes,<br> Seven currents flowing through the sky.<br> 3 He, shining in his splendour, stands high over all things that exist-<br> Soma, a God as Sūrya is.<br> 4 Thou, Indu, in thy brilliancy, pourest on us, as Indra's Friend,<br> Wealth from the kine to feast the Gods.</p>
