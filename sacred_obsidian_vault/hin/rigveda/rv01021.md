 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Rig Veda</i>, tr. by Ralph T.H. Griffith, [1896], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">HYMN XXI. Indra-Agni.</h3>  
 <p>1 INDRA and Agni I invoke fain are we for their song of praise:<br> Chief Soma-drinkers are they both.<br> 2 Praise ye, O men, and glorify Indra-Agni in the holy rites:<br> Sing praise to them in sacred songs.<br> 3 Indra and Agni we invite, the Soma-drinkers, for the fame<br> Of Mitra, to the Soma-draught.<br> 4 Strong Gods, we bid them come to this libation that stands ready here:<br> Indra and Agni, come to us.<br> 5 Indra and Agni, mighty Lords of our assembly, crush the fiends:<br> Childless be the devouring ones.<br> 6 Watch ye, through this your truthfulness, there in the place of spacious view<br> Indra and Agni, send us bliss.</p>
