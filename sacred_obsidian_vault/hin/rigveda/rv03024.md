 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Rig Veda</i>, tr. by Ralph T.H. Griffith, [1896], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">HYMN XXIV. Agni.</h3>  
 <p>1. AGNI, subdue opposing bands, and drive our enemies away.<br> Invincible, slay godless foes: give splendour to the worshipper.<br> 2 Lit with libation, Agni, thou, deathless, who callest Gods to feast,<br> Accept our sacrifice with joy.<br> 3 With splendour, Agni, Son of Strength, thou who art worshipped, wakeful One.<br> Seat thee on this my sacred grass.<br> 4 With all thy fires, with all the Gods, Agni, exalt the songs we sing.<br> And living men in holy rites.<br> 5 Grant, Agni, to the worshipper wealth rich in heroes, plenteous store,<br> Make thou us rich with many sons.</p>
