 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Rig Veda</i>, tr. by Ralph T.H. Griffith, [1896], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">HYMN XXX. Viśvedevas.</h3>  
 <p>1. NOT one of you, ye Gods, is small, none of you is a feeble child:<br> All of you, verily, are great.<br> 2 Thus be ye lauded, ye destroyers of the foe, ye Three-and-Thirty Deities,<br> The Gods of man, the Holy Ones.<br> 3 As such defend and succour us, with benedictions speak to us:<br> Lead us not from our fathers' and from Manu's path into the distance far away.<br> 4 Ye Deities who stay with us, and all ye Gods of all mankind,<br> Give us your wide protection, give shelter for cattle and for steed.</p>
