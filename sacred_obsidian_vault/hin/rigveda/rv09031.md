 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Rig Veda</i>, tr. by Ralph T.H. Griffith, [1896], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">HYMN XXXI. Soma Pavamana.</h3>  
 <p>1. THE, Soma-drops, benevolent, come forth as they are purified,<br> Bestowing wealth which all may see.<br> 2 O Indu, high oer heaven and earth be thou, increaser of our might:<br> The Master of all strength be thou.<br> 3 The winds are gracious in their love to thee, the rivers flow to thee<br> Soma, they multiply thy power.<br> 4 Soma, wax great. From every side may vigorous powers unite in thee:<br> Be in the gathering-Place of strength.<br> 5 For thee, brown-hued! the kine have poured imperishable oil and milk.<br> Aloft on the sublimest height.<br> 6 Friendship, O Indu, we desire with thee who bearest noble arms,<br> With thee, O Lord of all that is.</p>
