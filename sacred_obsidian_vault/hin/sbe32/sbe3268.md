 <p>î, to ask for (with two Acc.): î´mahe, I, 6, 10; V, 53, 13; îmahe, I, 43, 4; II, 34, 11.—áva îmahe, we pray to forgive, VII, 58, 5.—See also i.</p>  
 <p>î, pronominal base, see ayâ´, and îm.</p>  
 <p>îksh: abhí aíkshetâm, they look up to (acc.), X, 121, 6.</p>  
 <p>îṅkh, to toss: îṅkháyanti, I, 19, 7.</p>  
 <p>î<i>d</i>, to implore, to ask: î´<i>l</i>e, V, 60, 1:1; î<i>t</i><i>t</i>e (with double Acc.), I, 134, 5.</p>  
 <p>îm: I, 38, 11; 85, 11; 134, 2; 167, 8 (bis); V, 54, 4; yát îm, I, 87, 5; 167, 5; 7; VII, 56, 21; yé îm, V, 61, 11; ké îm, VII, 56, 1; úpa îm, I, 171, 2.</p>  
 <p>îr: â-îriré, they produced, assumed, I, 6, 4:3.—ut-îráyanti (vâ´<i>k</i>am), they send out, I, 168, 8; út îrayatha, you raise, V, 55, 51; út îrayanta, they have risen, VIII, 7, 3; út îrate, VIII, 7, 7; 17.—prá îrate, they come forth, VII, 56, 14; prá airata, they expanded, VIII, 20, 4; prá îrayâmi, I send forth, II, 33, 8; sám prá îrate, they rise, X, 168, 2.</p>  
 <p>î´vat, so much: î´vata<i>h</i>, VII, 56, 18.</p>  
 <p>î<i>s</i>, to rule, to be lord (with Gen.): î´<i>s</i>e, X, 121, 3; î<i>s</i>e, I, 165, 10; î´<i>s</i>ishe, I, 170, 5; î´sh<i>t</i>e, V, 87, 3; î<i>s</i>ire, V, 58, 1.—î<i>s</i>âná<i>h</i>, I, 87, 4; î´<i>s</i>ânât, II, 33, 9.</p>  
 <p>î<i>s</i>âna-k<i>rí</i>t, conferring powers: î<i>s</i>âna-k<i>rí</i>ta<i>h</i>, I, 64, 5:1.</p>  
 <p>îsh, to shrink: î´shante, VI, 66, 4.</p>
