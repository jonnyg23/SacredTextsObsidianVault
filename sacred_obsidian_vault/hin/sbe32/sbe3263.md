 <p><a name="page_451"><font size="1" color="green">p. 451</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">MA<i>N</i><i>D</i>ALA X, HYMN 186.</h3>  <h4 align="center">ASH<i>T</i>AKA VIII, ADHYÂYA 8, VARGA 44.</h4>  <h4 align="center"><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">To Vâta</span>.</h4>  
 <p>1. May Vâta waft medicine, healthful, delightful to our heart; may he prolong our lives!</p>  
 <p>2. Thou, O Vâta, art our father, and our brother, and our friend; do thou grant us to live!</p>  
 <p>3. O Vâta, from that treasure of the immortal which is placed in thy house yonder, give us to live!</p>  <h4 align="center">NOTES.</h4>  
 <p>Ascribed to Ula Vâtâyana, and addressed to Vâyu, under the name of Vâta. The metre is Gâyatrî. Verse 1 occurs in SV. I, 184; II, 1190; Taitt. Br. II, 4, 1, 8; Taitt. Âr. IV, 42, 8. Verse 3 in SV. II, 1192; Taitt. Br. II, 4, 1, 8; Taitt. Âr. IV, 42, 7.</p>
