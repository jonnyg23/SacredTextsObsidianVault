 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="../../img/tn_hin.jpg"></a><br><i>Vedic Hymns, Part I (SBE32)</i>, by Max Müller, [1891], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  
 <p>ûtí, protection, help, favour: ûtí<i>h</i>, II, 34, 15; VII, 59, 4; ûtî´, instr., I, 64, 13; 172, 1:1; VII, 57, 7; 59, 9:1; 10; ûtáye, II, 34, 14; VIII, 7, 6; ûtáya<i>h</i>, I, 167, 1; V, 54, 7; I, 134, 2; ûtí-bhi<i>h</i>, favours, I, 39, 8; 9; VII, 58, 3; VIII, 20, 24; ûtíshu, VIII, 20, 15.</p>  
 <p>û´dhan and û´dhar, udder: û´dha<i>h</i> divyâ´ni, the heavenly udders (clouds), I, 64, 5.—û´dhani, II, 34, 2:3; 6.—û´dha<i>h</i>, II, 34, 10; VI, 66, 1; VII, 56, 4:1.</p>  
 <p>û´ma, guardian: û´mâsa<i>h</i>, I, 166, 3; û´mâ<i>h</i>, V, 52, 12; X, 77, 8.</p>  
 <p>û´r<i>n</i>â, wool: û´r<i>n</i>â<i>h</i> vasata, V, 52, 9:1.</p>  
 <p>ûr<i>n</i>u: ápa ûr<i>n</i>ute, she uncovers, II, 34, 12:1.</p>  
 <p>ûrdhvá, erect: ûrdhvâ´ k<i>ri</i><i>n</i>avante, they stir up, I, 88, 3:1; ûrdhvám nunudre, they pushed up, I, 85, 10; 88, 4; ûrdhvâ´ santu, may they stand erect, I, 171, 3:1; ûrdhvâ´n na<i>h</i> karta, lift us up, I, 172, 3; ûrdhvâ´ tish<i>th</i>atu, may (the dawn) stand erect, I, 134, 1:2.</p>  
 <p>ûrmí, wave: ûrmáya<i>h</i>, I, 168, 2.</p>  
 <p>û´rmyâ, night: ûrmye, V, 61, 17:1.</p>  
 <p>ûh, see vah.</p>  
 <p>ûh, to watch: ohate, V, 52, 20; 11; ní ohate, V, 52, 11.—ohate (sakhitvé), he is counted (in your friendship), VIII, 7, 31.</p>
