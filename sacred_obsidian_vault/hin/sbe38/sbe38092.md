 <p>16. On account of his activity there also no contradiction exists.</p>  
 <p>There is no contradiction, as the same Yama is admitted to act as chief ruler in those seven hells. Of Kitragupta and others Sm<i>ri</i>ti merely speaks as superintendents employed by Yama.</p>
