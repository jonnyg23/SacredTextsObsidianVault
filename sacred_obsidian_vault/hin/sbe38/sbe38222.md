 <p>13. There being no specification (the rule does) not (specially apply to him who knows).</p>  
 <p>In passages such as 'Performing works here let a man live' &amp;c., which state definite rules, there is no specification</p>  
 <p><a name="page_294"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 294</font></a></p>  
 <p>of him who knows, since the definite rule is enjoined without any such specification.</p>
