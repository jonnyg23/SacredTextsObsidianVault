 <p>29. And on account of the non-sublation.</p>  
 <p>And thus those scriptural passages which distinguish lawful and unlawful food, such as <i>Kh</i>. Up. VII, 26, 2, 'When the food is pure the whole nature becomes pure,'--are non-sublated.</p>
