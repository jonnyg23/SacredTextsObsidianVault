 <p>6. From thence (the souls are led) by him only who belongs to the lightning; the sacred text stating that.</p>  
 <p>From thence, i.e. after they have come to the lightning they go to the world of Brahman, being led through the worlds of Varu<i>n</i>a and the rest by the person, not a man, who follows immediately after the lightning. For that that person leads them is stated in the following passage, 'When they have reached the place of lightning a person, not a man, leads them to the world of Brahman' (B<i>ri</i>. Up. VI, 2, 15). Varu<i>n</i>a and the rest, we must understand, favour them either by not hindering or somehow assisting them.--Therefore it is well said that light and so on are the gods who act as conductors.</p>
