 <p>37. This is stated in Sm<i>ri</i>ti also.</p>  
 <p>It is recorded in itihâsas also how Sa<i>m</i>varta and others who paid no regard to the duties incumbent on the â<i>s</i>ramas, in going naked and so on, became great Yogins all the same.--But the instances quoted from scripture and Sm<i>ri</i>ti furnish merely indicatory marks; what then is</p>  
 <p><a name="page_316"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 316</font></a></p>  
 <p>the final conclusion?--That conclusion is stated in the next Sûtra.</p>
