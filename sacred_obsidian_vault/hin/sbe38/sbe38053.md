 <p>52. And this is also the case in resolutions, &amp;c.</p>  
 <p>The objection pointed out before applies also to resolutions, &amp;c., for they also are made through the non-particular conjunction of the internal organ and the Self, in proximity to all Selfs. Hence they also cannot furnish a reason for limitation.</p>
