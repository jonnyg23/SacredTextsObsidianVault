 <p>
 This short collection of texts featuring Chuang Tzu (Zhuangzi), the
 Taoist sage, is a good introduction to
 the core concepts of the Taoist worldview.
 The author, Lionel Giles, was an eminent Victorian scholar of Chinese society,
 who, most notably, also translated <a href="../../cfu/artwar.txt">Sun Tsu's Art of War</a>.
 </p>
