 <p><a name="43"></a></p>  <h3 align="center">FORTY-THREE</h3>  
 <p>The tenderest things of creation prevail over the hardest. Something immaterial enters into the most impenetrable to preserve its unity. I, therefore, recognise the advantages of the principle of <i>wu-wei</i> and teach non-interference. There is no other doctrine</p>  
 <p><a name="page_51"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 51</font></a></p>  
 <p>that is grander than the doctrine of TAO, and no other teaching that is more universally potent than the teaching of <i>wu-wei</i>.</p>
