 <h2 align="center">CHAPTER VI<br> <small>THE IDEAL LIFE</small></h2>
 
 <p>Y<small>ANG</small> C<small>HU</small> said:</p>
 
 <p>   "Yuan Hsie lived in mean circumstances in Lu, while Tse Kung<a name="page_42_fr_1"></a><a href="#page_42_note_1"><font size="1">1</font></a> amassed wealth in Wei.</p>
 
 <p>   "Poverty galled the one, and riches caused uneasiness to the other.</p>
 
 <p>   "So poverty will not do nor wealth either."</p>
 
 <p>   "But what then will do?"</p>
 
 <p>   "I answer enjoy life and take one's ease, for those who know how to enjoy life are not poor, and he that lives at ease requires no riches."</p>
