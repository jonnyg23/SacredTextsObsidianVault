 <h2 align="center">TITLES OF THE ESSAYS NOT TRANSLATED</h2>
 
 <table align="center" cellpadding="10">
 
 <tr><td align="right" valign="top">No.</td><td> in the Text.</td></tr>
 
 <tr><td align="right" valign="top">3.</td><td>The hosts of Heaven and Earth declare the wonder and the glory of the Tao.</td></tr>
 
 <tr><td align="right" valign="top">4.</td><td>The earth and all physical phenomena are indications of the operations of the Tao.</td></tr>
 
 <tr><td align="right" valign="top">5.</td><td>The seasons and the divisions of time are phenomena indicative of the directing power of the Tao.</td></tr>
 
 <tr><td align="right" valign="top">6.</td><td>A survey of the invisible and profound changes that happen will reveal original causes which are indicative of the Tao.</td></tr>
 
 <tr><td align="right" valign="top">9.</td><td>When the rule of the emperor conforms to the principles of the Tao, there will be effective results.</td></tr>
 
 <tr><td align="right" valign="top">10.</td><td>Names savour of locality, and each folklore has a nature of its own. To reduce all to a common denomination, for unification, must be done by a unification of the heart first.</td></tr>
 
 <tr><td align="right" valign="top">11.</td><td>The nomenclature of things throw some light on their origin. In investigating these we are seeking for the truth.</td></tr>
 
 <tr><td align="right" valign="top">14.</td><td>The whole creation is symbolic of the Tao.</td></tr>
 
 <tr><td align="right" valign="top">16.</td><td>The majesty of the mountain symbolizes the accumulation of the truth of the Tao.</td></tr>
 
 <tr><td align="right" valign="top">17.</td><td>The Tao is the origin of the myriad things; their unity is like to the congregation of the trees of the forest.</td></tr>
 
 <tr><td align="right" valign="top">18.</td><td>An analysis of human history reveals the losses and gains following on the neglect or pursuit of the Tao.</td></tr>
 
 <tr><td align="right" valign="top">20.</td><td>In the myriad forms of creation a pure unity is manifested everywhere.</td></tr>
 
 <tr><td align="right" valign="top">21.</td><td>A summary of the contents and a commentary on the 3rd essay.</td></tr>
 
 </table>
