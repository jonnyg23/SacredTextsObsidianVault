 <h3 align="CENTER">CHAPTER 79.</h3>
 <p>The original reads, "The holy man keeps the left (<i>tso</i>) of contract" and <i>tso</i>, "left," means the debit side. The right side of the contract table contained the claims, <i>cheh</i>, which in its original meaning denotes "to go through" and then "that which can be enacted."</p>
