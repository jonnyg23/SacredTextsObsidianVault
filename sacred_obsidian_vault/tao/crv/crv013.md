 <h3 align="CENTER">7. DIMMING RADIANCE.</h3>
 <p><a name="img_tao07"></a>
 </p><table align="LEFT"><tr><td align="CENTER" valign="CENTER"><img src="img/tao07.jpg" align="LEFT"></td></tr></table>1. Heaven endures and earth is lasting. And why can heaven and earth endure and be lasting? Because they do not live for themselves. On that account can they endure.
 <p>2. Therefore</p>
 <p>The holy man puts his person behind and his person comes to the front. He surrenders his person and his person is preserved. Is it not because he seeks not his own? For that reason he can accomplish his own.</p>
