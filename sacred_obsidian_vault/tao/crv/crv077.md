 <h3 align="CENTER">71. THE DISEASE OF KNOWLEDGE.</h3>
 <p><a name="img_tao71"></a>
 </p><table align="LEFT"><tr><td align="CENTER" valign="CENTER"><img src="img/tao71.jpg" align="LEFT"></td></tr></table>1. To know the unknowable, that is elevating. Not to know the knowable, that is sickness.
 <p>2. Only by becoming sick of sickness can we be without sickness.</p>
 <p>3. The holy man is not sick. Because he is sick of sickness, therefore he is not sick.</p>
