 <h3 align="CENTER">CHAPTER 64.</h3>
 <p>The last word here translated by "interfere" is in Chinese <i>wei</i>, "to do" or "to act."</p>
 <p>The terms "likely" and "unlikely" are literal translations of the Chinese. Likely apparently means what is common or usual, and the unlikely, what is unusual.</p>
