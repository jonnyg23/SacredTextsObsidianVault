 <h3 align="CENTER">53. GAINING INSIGHT.</h3>
 <p><a name="img_tao53"></a>
 </p><table align="LEFT"><tr><td align="CENTER" valign="CENTER"><img src="img/tao53.jpg" align="LEFT"></td></tr></table>1. If I have ever so little knowledge, I shall walk in the great Reason. It is but expansion that I must fear.
 <p>2. The great Reason is very plain, but people are fond of by-paths.</p>
 <p>3. When the palace is very splendid, the fields are very weedy and granaries very empty.</p>
 <p>4. To wear ornaments and gay clothes, to carry sharp swords, to be excessive in drinking and eating, to have a redundance of costly articles, this is the pride of robbers.</p>
 <p>5. Surely, this is un-Reason.</p>
