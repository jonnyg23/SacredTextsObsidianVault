 <h3 align="CENTER">18. THE PALLIATION OF VULGARITY.</h3>
 <p><a name="img_tao18"></a>
 </p><table align="LEFT"><tr><td align="CENTER" valign="CENTER"><img src="img/tao18.jpg" align="LEFT"></td></tr></table>1. When the great Reason is obliterated, we have benevolence and justice. Prudence and circumspection appear, and we have much hypocrisy.
 <p>2. When family relations no longer harmonize, we have filial piety and paternal <a name="page_85"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 85</font></a> devotion. When the country and the clans decay through disorder, we have loyalty and allegiance.</p>
