 <h3 align="CENTER">35. THE VIRTUE OF BENEVOLENCE.</h3>
 <p><a name="img_tao35"></a>
 </p><table align="LEFT"><tr><td align="CENTER" valign="CENTER"><img src="img/tao35.jpg" align="LEFT"></td></tr></table><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <p>1. "Who holdeth fast to the great Form,<br>
 Of him the world will come in quest:<br>
 For there we never meet with harm,<br>
 There we find shelter, comfort, rest."</p></dir>
 </dir>
 
 <p>2. Music with dainties makes the passing stranger stop. But Reason, when <a name="page_98"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 98</font></a> coming from the mouth, how tasteless is it! It has no flavor. When looked at, there is not enough to be seen; when listened to, there is not enough to be heard. However, when used, it is inexhaustible.</p>
