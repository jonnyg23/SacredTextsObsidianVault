 <h3 align="CENTER">45. GREATEST VIRTUE.</h3>
 <p><a name="img_tao45"></a>
 </p><table align="LEFT"><tr><td align="CENTER" valign="CENTER"><img src="img/tao45.jpg" align="LEFT"></td></tr></table><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <p>1. "Greatest perfection imperfect will be,<br>
 But its work ne'er waneth.<br>
 Greatest fulness is vacuity,<br>
 Its work unexhausted remaineth."</p>
 <p>2. "Straightest lines resemble curves;<br>
 Greatest skill like a tyro serves;<br>
 Greatest eloquence stammers and swerves."</p></dir>
 </dir>
 
 <p>3. Motion conquers cold. Quietude <a name="page_106"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 106</font></a> conquers heat. Purity and clearness are the world's standard.</p>
