 <h3 align="CENTER">78. TRUST IN FAITH.</h3>
 <p><a name="img_tao78"></a>
 </p><table align="LEFT"><tr><td align="CENTER" valign="CENTER"><img src="img/tao78.jpg" align="LEFT"></td></tr></table>1. In the world nothing is tenderer and more delicate than water. In attacking the hard and the strong nothing will surpass it. There is nothing that herein takes its place.
 <p>2. The weak conquer the strong, the tender conquer the rigid. In the world there is no one who does not know it, but no one will practise it.</p>
 <p>3. Therefore the holy man says:</p><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <p>"Him who the country's sin makes his,<br>
 We hail as priest at the great sacrifice.<br>
 Him who the curse bears of the country's failing.<br>
 As king of the empire we are hailing."</p></dir>
 </dir>
 
 <p>4. True words seem paradoxical.</p>
