 <h3 align="CENTER">22. HUMILITY'S INCREASE.</h3>
 <p><a name="img_tao22"></a>
 </p><table align="LEFT"><tr><td align="CENTER" valign="CENTER"><img src="img/tao22.jpg" align="LEFT"></td></tr></table><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <p>1. "The crooked shall be straight,<br>
 Crushed ones recuperate,<br>
 The empty find their fill.<br>
 The worn with strength shall thrill;<br>
 Who little have receive,<br>
 And who have much will grieve."</p></dir>
 </dir>
 
 <p>2. Therefore</p>
 <p>The holy man embraces unity and becomes for all the world a model.</p>
 <p>Not self-displaying he is enlightened;</p>
 <p>Not self -approving he is distinguished;</p>
 <p>Not self-asserting he acquires merit;</p>
 <p>Not self-seeking he gaineth life.</p>
 <p>Since he does not quarrel, therefore no one in the world can quarrel with him.</p>
 <p>3. The saying of the ancients: "The crooked shall be straight," is it in any way vainly spoken? Verily, they will be straightened and return home.</p>
