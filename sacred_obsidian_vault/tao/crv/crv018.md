 <h3 align="CENTER">12. ABSTAINING FROM DESIRE.</h3>
 <p><a name="img_tao12"></a>
 </p><table align="LEFT"><tr><td align="CENTER" valign="CENTER"><img src="img/tao12.jpg" align="LEFT"></td></tr></table><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <p>1. "The five colors [combined] the human eye will blind;<br>
 The five notes [in one sound] the human ear confound;<br>
 The five tastes [when they blend] the human mouth offend."</p>
 <p>2. "Racing and hunting will human hearts turn mad,<br>
 Treasures high-prized make human conduct bad."</p></dir>
 </dir>
 
 <p>3. Therefore</p>
 <p>The holy man attends to the inner and not to the outer. He abandons the latter and chooses the former.</p>
