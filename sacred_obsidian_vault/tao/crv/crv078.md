 <h3 align="CENTER">72. HOLDING ONESELF DEAR.</h3>
 <p><a name="img_tao72"></a>
 </p><table align="LEFT"><tr><td align="CENTER" valign="CENTER"><img src="img/tao72.jpg" align="LEFT"></td></tr></table>1. If the people do not fear the dreadful, the great dreadful will come, surely.
 <p>2. Let them not deem their lives narrow. Let them not deem their lot wearisome. When it is not deemed wearisome, then it will not be wearisome.</p>
 <p>3. Therefore the holy man knows himself but does not display himself. He <a name="page_125"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 125</font></a> holds himself dear but does not honor himself. Thus he discards the latter and chooses the former.</p>
