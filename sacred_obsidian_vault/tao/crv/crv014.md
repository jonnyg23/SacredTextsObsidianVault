 <h3 align="CENTER">8. EASY BY NATURE.</h3>
 <p><a name="img_tao08"></a>
 </p><table align="LEFT"><tr><td align="CENTER" valign="CENTER"><img src="img/tao08.jpg" align="LEFT"></td></tr></table>1. Superior goodness resembleth water. The water's goodness benefiteth the ten thousand things, yet it quarreleth not.
 <p><a name="page_78"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 78</font></a></p>
 <p>2. Water dwelleth in the places which the multitudes of men shun; therefore it is near unto the eternal Reason</p>
 <p>3. The dwelling of goodness is in lowliness. The heart of goodness is in commotion. When giving, goodness showeth benevolence. In words, goodness keepeth faith. In government goodness standeth for order. In business goodness exhibiteth ability. The movements of goodness keep time.</p>
 <p>4. It quarreleth not. Therefore it is not rebuked.</p>
