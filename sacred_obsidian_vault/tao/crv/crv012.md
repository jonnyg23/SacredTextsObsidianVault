 <h3 align="CENTER">6. THE COMPLETION OF FORM.</h3>
 <p><a name="img_tao06"></a>
 </p><table align="LEFT"><tr><td align="CENTER" valign="CENTER"><img src="img/tao06.jpg" align="LEFT"></td></tr></table><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <p>1. "The valley spirit not expires,<br>
 Mysterious woman tis called by the sires.<br>
 The mysterious woman's door, to boot,<br>
 Is called of heaven and earth the root.<br>
 Forever and aye it seems to endure<br>
 And its use is without effort sure."</p>
