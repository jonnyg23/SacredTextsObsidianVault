 <h3 align="CENTER">CHAPTER 45.</h3>
 <p>Literally the second quotation reads:</p><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <p>"Greatest straightness is like a curve,<br>
 Greatest skill is like awkwardness,<br>
 Greatest eloquence is like stammering."</p></dir>
 </dir>
 
 <p>The first line reminds us of modern geometry where the straight line may be regarded as a curve of an infinitely small curvature. Cf. note on Chapter 41.</p>
