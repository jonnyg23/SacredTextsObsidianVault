 <h3 align="CENTER">48. FORGETTING KNOWLEDGE.</h3>
 <p><a name="img_tao48"></a>
 </p><table align="LEFT"><tr><td align="CENTER" valign="CENTER"><img src="img/tao48.jpg" align="LEFT"></td></tr></table>1. He who seeks learnedness will daily increase. He who seeks Reason will daily diminish. He will diminish and continue to diminish until he arrives at non-assertion.
 <p>2. With non-assertion there is nothing that he cannot achieve. When he takes the empire, it is always because he uses no diplomacy. He who uses diplomacy is not fit to take the empire.</p>
