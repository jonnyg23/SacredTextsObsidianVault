 <h3 align="CENTER">29. NON-ASSERTION.</h3>
 <p><a name="img_tao29"></a>
 </p><table align="LEFT"><tr><td align="CENTER" valign="CENTER"><img src="img/tao29.jpg" align="LEFT"></td></tr></table>1. When one desires to take in hand the empire and make it, I see him not succeed. The empire is a divine vessel which cannot be made. One who makes it, mars it. One who takes it, loses it.
 <p><a name="page_94"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 94</font></a></p><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <p>2. And it is said of beings:<br>
 "Some are obsequious, others move boldly,<br>
 Some breathe warmly, others coldly,<br>
 Some are strong and others weak,<br>
 Some rise proudly, others sneak."</p></dir>
 </dir>
 
 <p>3. Therefore the holy man abandons excess, he abandons extravagance, he abandons indulgence.</p>
