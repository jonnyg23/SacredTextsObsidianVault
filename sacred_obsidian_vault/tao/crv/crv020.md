 <h3 align="CENTER">14. PRAISING THE MYSTERIOUS.</h3>
 <p><a name="img_tao14"></a>
 </p><table align="LEFT"><tr><td align="CENTER" valign="CENTER"><img src="img/tao14.jpg" align="LEFT"></td></tr></table>1. We look at Reason and do not see it; its name is Colorless. We listen to Reason and do not hear it; its name is Soundless. We grope for Reason and do not grasp it; its name is Bodiless.
 <p>2. These three things cannot further be analyzed. Thus they are combined and conceived as a unity which on its surface is not clear and in its depth not obscure.</p>
 <p>3. Forever and aye Reason remains unnamable, <a name="page_82"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 82</font></a> and again and again it returns home to non-existence.</p>
 <p>4. This is called the form of the formless, the image of the imageless. This is called the transcendentally abstruse.</p>
 <p>5. In front its beginning is not seen. In the rear its end is not seen.</p>
 <p>6. By holding fast to the Reason of the ancients, the present is mastered and the origin of the past understood. This is called Reason's clue.</p>
