 <h3 align="CENTER">CHAPTER 59.</h3>
 <p>The "mother of the commonwealth" is commonly interpreted to be thrift. It is not impossible that Lao-tze means the Tao or Reason, but in the same chapter he uses the term Tao in the more general sense as "way."</p>
