 <h3 align="CENTER">17. SIMPLICITY IN HABITS.</h3>
 <p><a name="img_tao17"></a>
 </p><table align="LEFT"><tr><td align="CENTER" valign="CENTER"><img src="img/tao17.jpg" align="LEFT"></td></tr></table>1. Of great rulers the subjects do not notice the existence. To lesser ones people are attached; they praise them. Still lesser ones people fear, and the meanest ones people despise.
 <p>2. For it is said:</p>
 <p>"If your faith be insufficient, verily, you will receive no faith."</p>
 <p>3. How reluctantly they [the great rulers] considered their words! Merit they accomplished; deeds they performed; and the hundred families thought: "We are independent."</p>
