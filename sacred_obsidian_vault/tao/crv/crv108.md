 <h3 align="CENTER">CHAPTER 26.</h3>
 <p>The word <i>tsz</i>, translated "gravity," is a peculiar phrase which literally means "baggage wagon." The intermediate idea seems to be "heaviness" or "gravity," the latter in the double sense (literal and figurative) as used in English.</p>
 <p>In our former edition it was translated "dignity."</p>
