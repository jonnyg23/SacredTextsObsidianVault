 <h3 align="CENTER">43. ITS UNIVERSAL APPLICATION.</h3>
 <p><a name="img_tao43"></a>
 </p><table align="LEFT"><tr><td align="CENTER" valign="CENTER"><img src="img/tao43.jpg" align="LEFT"></td></tr></table>1. The world's weakest overcomes the world's hardest.
 <p>2. Non-existence enters into the impenetrable.</p>
 <p>3. Thereby I comprehend of non-assertion the advantage. There are few in the world who obtain of non-assertion the advantage and of silence the lesson.</p>
