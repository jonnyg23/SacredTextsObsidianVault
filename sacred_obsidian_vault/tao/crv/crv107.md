 <h3 align="CENTER">CHAPTER 25.</h3>
 <p>The word <i>shi</i>, "departing," may very well be understood in the sense of dying.</p>
 <p>The word <i>fan</i> means literally "return," denoting "coming back," and in order</p>
 <p><a name="page_161"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 161</font></a></p>
 <p>to imitate the terse Chinese text, the best translation for "having come back" is "home." Lao-tze says: "Reason, the great distant beyond, is our home."</p>
 <p>Section 5 seems to be a gloss which slipped into the text. At any rate the bracketed portion is too trivial to come from the hand of Lao-tze.</p>
