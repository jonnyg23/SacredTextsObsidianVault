 <p>He who attends daily to learning increases in learning. He who practices Tao daily diminishes. Again and again he humbles himself. Thus he attains to non-doing (wu wei). He practices non-doing and yet there is nothing left undone.</p>  
 <p>To command the empire one must not employ craft. If one uses craft he is not fit to command the empire.</p>
