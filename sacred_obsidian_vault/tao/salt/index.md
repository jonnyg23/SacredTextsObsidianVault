 <hr>
 <p>This is Lionel Giles' translation of the Tao te Ching, from
 the <a href="../../woe/index.htm">Wisdom of the East</a> series.
 Giles (1875-1958) was Keeper of the Department
 of Oriental Books and Manuscripts at the British Museum.
 He also translated Sun Tzu's <a href="../aow/index.htm">Art of War</a>.
 </p>
 <p>
 This translation is long out of print, which is a shame, because it
 is a very clear English rendition of this very difficult and profund
 text.
 </p>
