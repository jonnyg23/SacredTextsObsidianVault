 <h1 align="CENTER">T'ai-Shang Kan-Ying P'ien</h1>
 <h4 align="CENTER">Treatise of the Exalted One on Response and Retribution</h4>
 <h6 align="CENTER">Translated from the Chinese by</h6>
 <h2 align="CENTER">Teitaro Suzuki and Dr. Paul Carus</h2>
 <p align="CENTER">La Salle, Ill. The Open Court Publishing Co.</p>
 <p align="CENTER">[1906]</p>
 <p>
 This work is a key text about Taoist morality and ethics.
 It is also translated in the
 <a href="../sbe40/sbe40.htm">Sacred Books of the East, Volume 40</a>
 as the Tai Shang Tractate, albeit without the wonderful
 fables included in this edition. 
 The translators, Paul Carus and Teitaro Suzuki, also have
 other works at sacred texts: Paul Carus'
 <a href="../../bud/btg/index.htm">Buddha, the Gospel</a>,
 and
 <a href="../../bud/buddha2.htm">Buddha, the Word</a>,
 and Suzukis'
 <a href="../../bud/mzb/index.htm">Manual of Zen Buddhism</a>.
