 <h3 align="CENTER">(Quotations.<sup>[64]</sup>)</h3>
 <p>Those who have hitherto done evil deeds should henceforth mend and repent. (1200-1209)</p>
 <p>If evil be no longer practiced and good deeds done, and if in this way a man continues and continues, he will surely obtain happiness</p>
 <p>{p. 66}</p>
 <p>and felicity. He will, indeed, so to speak, transform curses into blessings. (1210-1230)</p>
