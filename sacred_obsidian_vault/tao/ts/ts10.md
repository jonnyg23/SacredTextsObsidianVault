 <h3 align="CENTER">(Good and Evil Spirits.)</h3>
 <p>If a man's heart be awakened to the good, though the good be not yet accomplished, good spirits verily are already following him. (1170-1184)</p>
 <p>If a man's heart be awakened to evil, though evil be not yet accomplished, evil spirits verily are already following him. (1185-1199)</p>
