 <p><a name="page_214"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 214</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">Mr. Shaver's Lemurian Alphabet</h3>  
 <p>AAnimal (used AN for short)</p>  
 <p>BBe (to existoften command)</p>  
 <p>CSee</p>  
 <p>D(also used DE) Disintegrant energy; Detrimental (most important symbol in language)</p>  
 <p>EEnergy (an all concept, including motion)</p>  
 <p>FFecund (use FE as in femalefecund man)</p>  
 <p>GGenerate (used GEN)</p>  
 <p>HHuman (some doubt on this one)</p>  
 <p>ISelf; Ego (same as our I)</p>  
 <p>J(see G) (same as generate)</p>  
 <p>KKinetic (force of motion)</p>  
 <p>LLife</p>  
 <p>MMan</p>  
 <p>NChild; Spore; Seed (as ninny)</p>  
 <p>OOrifice (a source concept)</p>  
 <p>PPower</p>  
 <p>QQuest (as question)</p>  
 <p>R(used as AR) Horror (symbol of dangerous quantity of dis force in the object)</p>  
 <p>S(SIS) (an important symbol of the sun)</p>  
 <p>T(used as TE) (the most important symbol; origin of the cross symbol) Integration; Force of growth (the intake of T is cause of gravity; the force is T; tic meant science of growth; remains as credit word)</p>  
 <p>UYou</p>  
 <p>VVital (used as VI) (the stuff Messmer calls animal magnetism; sex appeal)</p>  
 <p>WWill</p>  
 <p>XConflict (crossed force lines)</p>  
 <p><a href="errata.htm#10">Y</a>Why</p>  
 <p>ZZero (a quantity of energy of T neutralized by an equal quantity of D)</p>  <h3 align="center">Some "English" Lemurian Words</h3>  
 <p>ABSENTAnimal be sent (one was sent, therefore is not here)</p>  
 <p>ADDERA der (the animal is a der. or deadly)</p>  
 <p>ARRESTAnimal stops to rest (the ar syllable means is dangerously stopped)</p>  
 <p>BEGETTo cause to exist (command to generate the energy of inteorance)</p>  
 <p>BADBe a de (to be a destructive force)</p>  
 <p>BARDBar de (one who allays depressing de force, who over-joys us, decreases depression)</p>  
 <p>BIGBe I generate (in the act of generation, as pregnant)</p>  
 <p>BILKBe ill kinetic (to run away from ill, to dodgeK for movement)</p>  
 <p>DARKDetrimental horrible movement (harrowing things we are apt to see "in the dark")</p>  
 <p>DECEASEStopped by de (disintegrated to the noint of ceasing to bedeath)</p>  
 <p>DEVIATEDe vital ate (de has eaten the vital force. implicatiot. being the thing goes astray he-cause of destructive force)</p>  
 <p><a name="page_215"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 215</font></a></p>  
 <p>DEVILDe vile (to be vile with de; completely destructive)</p>  
 <p>DROPDe ro power (disintegrance governs power, thus it becomes less, falls)</p>  
 <p>LADYLay de (allay depression; complimentary term)</p>  
 <p>MADMan a de (one who may de, be apt to destroy)</p>  
 <p>MEANMe animal (animal conscious only of self)</p>  
 <p>MORBIDMore be I de (I don't want to be any more, I want to die)</p>  
 <p>NEEChild energy (charm)</p>  
 <p>NEUTRALNe you to ral (attracted by the charm of both parties)</p>  
 <p>OBSCENEOrifice see charm (orifice meant source of life, thus the meaning is evident)</p>  
 <p>PACTPower act (an empowered act)</p>  
 <p>PEALPower all (power and all combine to give a loud sound)</p>  
 <p>PRISONPrice on (to hold for ransom)</p>  
 <p>QUITQuest you I to (get someone else to do good)</p>  
 <p>VANVital animal (the leader)</p>  
 <p>ZEALZero all (foolish ardor to zeal)</p>    </body>
