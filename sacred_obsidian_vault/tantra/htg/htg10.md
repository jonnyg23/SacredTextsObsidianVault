 <p><a name="page_54"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 54</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">LAKṢMĪ <a name="fr_308"></a><a href="#fn_308"><font size="1">1</font></a></h3>  <h3 align="center">(LAKSMĪSTOTRAM)</h3>  <h3 align="center">FROM THE TANTRASĀRA <a name="fr_309"></a><a href="#fn_309"><font size="1">2</font></a></h3>  
 <p>O DEVĪ KAMALĀ, <a name="fr_310"></a><a href="#fn_310"><font size="1">3</font></a> beloved of Viṣṇu,<br> Adored by the three worlds,<br> As Thou art constant to Viṣṇu, be Thou constant to me.<br> Whoever worshipping Lakṣmī, reads these twelve names of Her--<br> Iśvarī, Kamala, <a name="fr_311"></a><a href="#fn_311"><font size="1">4</font></a> Lakṣmī, Calā, <a name="fr_312"></a><a href="#fn_312"><font size="1">5</font></a><br> Bhūti, <a name="fr_313"></a><a href="#fn_313"><font size="1">6</font></a> Haripriyā, <a name="fr_314"></a><a href="#fn_314"><font size="1">7</font></a> Padmā, <a name="fr_315"></a><a href="#fn_315"><font size="1">8</font></a> Padmālayā, <a name="fr_316"></a><a href="#fn_316"><font size="1">9</font></a> Sampat, <a name="fr_317"></a><a href="#fn_317"><font size="1">10</font></a></p>

 <h3 align="CENTER">Footnotes</h3>
 <p><a name="fn_308"></a><a href="htg10.htm#fr_308">54:1</a> Lakṣmī, commonly called Śrī, Devī of prosperity and beauty: the Śakti, or Spouse of Viṣṇu, who rose resplendent from the sea at the churning of the ocean by the Devas and Asuras, and then reclining on the breast of Hari, gazed upon the enraptured Devas. As her Lord assumes various forms, so does She.</p>
