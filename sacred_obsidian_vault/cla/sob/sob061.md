 <h3 align="CENTER">MNASIDIKA'S THREE BEAUTIES</h3>
 <p>That Mnasidika may be protected by the gods, I sacrificed two doves and two male hares to laughter-loving Aphrodite.</p>
 <p>To Ares <a href="sob154.htm#xref_80"><font size="1">80</font></a> I have given two armed cocks, and sinister Hecate has received two dogs who howled beneath the knife.</p>
 <p>Nor have I wrongly prayed these three immortals, for Mnasidika bears upon her face the imprint of their trinal godhead:</p>
 <p>Her lips are red as copper, her hair shines blue as steel and her eyes are black as silver.</p>
