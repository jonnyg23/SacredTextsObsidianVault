 <h6 align="CENTER">XLVII.</h6>
 <h3 align="CENTER">TO SABASIUS.</h3>
 <h6 align="CENTER">The FUMIGATION from AROMATICS</h6>
 <p>HEAR me, illustrious father, dæmon fam'd.<br>
 Great Saturn's offspring, and Sabasius nam'd;</p>
 <p><a name="page_180"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 180</font></a></p>
 <p>Inserting Bacchus, bearer of the vine,<br>
 And founding God, within thy thigh divine,<br>
 That when mature, the Dionysian God 5<br>
 Might burst the bands of his conceal'd abode,<br>
 And come to sacred Tmolus, his delight,<br>
 Where Ippa dwells, all beautiful and bright.<br>
 Come blessed Phrygian God, the king of all,<br>
 And aid thy mystics, when on thee they call. 10</p>
