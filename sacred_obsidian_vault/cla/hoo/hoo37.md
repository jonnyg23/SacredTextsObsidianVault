 <h6 align="CENTER">XXXII.</h6>
 <h3 align="CENTER">TO VICTORY.</h3>
 <h6 align="CENTER">The FUMIGATION from MANNA.</h6>
 <p>O Powerful Victory, by men desir'd,<br>
 With adverse breasts to dreadful fury fir'd,<br>
 Thee I invoke, whose might alone can quell<br>
 Contending rage, and molestation fell:<br>
 'Tis thine in battle to confer the crown, 5<br>
 The victor's prize, the mark of sweet renown;<br>
 For thou rul'st all things, Victory divine!<br>
 And glorious strife, and joyful shouts are thine.<br>
 Come, mighty Goddess, and thy suppliant bless,<br>
 With sparkling eye, elated with success; 10<br>
 May deeds illustrious thy protection claim,<br>
 And find, led on by thee immortal Fame.</p>
