 <h6 align="CENTER">XLIII.</h6>
 <h3 align="CENTER">TO SEMELE.</h3>
 <h6 align="CENTER">The FUMIGATION from STORAX.</h6>
 <p>CADMEAN Goddess, universal queen,<br>
 Thee, Semele I call, of beauteous mien;<br>
 Deep-bosom'd, lovely flowing locks are thine,<br>
 Mother of Bacchus, joyful and divine,<br>
 The mighty offspring, whom love's thunder bright, 5<br>
 Forc'd immature, and fright'ned into light:<br>
 Born from the deathless counsels, secret, high,<br>
 Of Jove Saturnian, regent of the sky<br>
 Whom Proserpine permits to view the light,<br>
 And visit mortals from the realms of night: 10<br>
 Constant attending on the sacred rites,<br>
 And feast triennial, which thy soul delights;<br>
 When thy son's wond'rous birth mankind relate,<br>
 And secrets deep, and holy celebrate.</p>
 <p><a name="page_178"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 178</font></a></p>
 <p>Now I invoke thee, great Cadmean queen, 15<br>
 To bless these rites with countenance serene.</p>
