 <h6 align="CENTER">XXII.</h6>
 <h3 align="CENTER">TO NEREUS.</h3>
 <h6 align="CENTER">The FUMIGATION from MYRRH.</h6>
 <p>O Thou, who doff the roots of Ocean keep<br>
 In seats cærulean, dæmon of the deep,<br>
 With fifty nymphs (attending in thy train,<br>
 Fair virgin artists) glorying thro' the main:<br>
 The dark foundation of the rolling sea 5<br>
 And Earth's wide bounds, belong much-fam'd to thee;<br>
 Great dæmon, source of all, whose pow'r can make<br>
 The Earth's unmeasur'd, holy basis shake,<br>
 When blust'ring winds in secret caverns pent,<br>
 By thee excited, struggle hard for vent: 10<br>
 Come, blessed Nereus, listen to my pray'r,<br>
 And cease to shake the earth with wrath severe;</p>
 <p><a name="page_148"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 148</font></a></p>
 <p>Send on our sacred rites abundant health,<br>
 With peace divine and necessary wealth.</p>
