 <h6 align="CENTER">XVIII.</h6>
 <h3 align="CENTER">TO THUNDRING JOVE.</h3>
 <h6 align="CENTER">The FUMIGATION from STORAX.</h6>
 <p>O Father Jove, who shak'st with fiery light<br>
 The world deep-sounding from thy lofty height:<br>
 From thee, proceeds th' ætherial lightning's blaze,<br>
 Flashing around intolerable rays.<br>
 Thy sacred thunders shake the blest abodes, 5<br>
 The shining regions of th' immortal Gods:<br>
 Thy pow'r divine, the flaming lightning shrouds,<br>
 With dark investiture, in fluid clouds.<br>
 'Tis thine to brandish thunders strong and dire,<br>
 To scatter storms, and dreadful darts of fire; 10<br>
 With roaring flames involving all around,<br>
 And bolts of thunder of tremendous sound.<br>
 Thy rapid dart can raise the hair upright,<br>
 And shake the heart of man with wild afright.<br>
 Sudden, unconquer'd, holy, thund'ring God, 15<br>
 'With noise unbounded, flying all abroad;<br>
 With all-devouring force, entire and strong,<br>
 Horrid, untam'd, thou roll'st the flames along.<br>
 Rapid, ætherial bolt, descending fire,<br>
 The earth all-parent, trembles at thy ire; 20<br>
 The sea all-shining; and each beast that hears<br>
 The sound terrific, with dread horror fears:<br>
 When Nature's face is bright with flashing fire,<br>
 And in the heavens resound thy thunders dire.</p>
 <p><a name="page_145"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 145</font></a></p>
 <p>Thy thunders white, the azure garments tear,<br>
 And burst the veil of all surrounding air.<br>
 O Jove, all-blessed, may thy wrath severe,<br>
 Hurl'd in the bosom of the deep appear,<br>
 And on the tops of mountains be reveal'd,<br>
 For thy strong arm is not from us conceal'd. 30<br>
 Propitious to these sacred rites incline,<br>
 And crown my wishes with a life divine:<br>
 Add royal health, and gentle peace beside,<br>
 With equal reason, for my constant guide.</p>
