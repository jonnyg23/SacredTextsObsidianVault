 <h6 align="CENTER">LXXI.</h6>
 <h3 align="CENTER">TO FORTUNE.</h3>
 <h6 align="CENTER">The FUMIGATION from FRANKINCENSE.</h6>
 <p>Approach strong Fortune, with propitious mind<br>
 And rich abundance, to my pray'r inclin'd</p>
 <p><a name="page_203"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 203</font></a></p>
 <p>Placid, and gentle Trivia, mighty nam'd,  <a name="fr_121"></a><a href="#fn_121"><font size="1">3</font></a><br>
 Imperial Dian, born of Pluto fam'd;<br>
 Mankind's unconquer'd, endless praise is thine, 5<br>
 Sepulch'ral, widely-wand'ring pow'r divine!<br>
 In thee, our various mortal life is found,<br>
 And some from thee hi copious wealth abound;<br>
 While others mourn thy hand averse to bless,<br>
 In all the bitterness of deep distress. 10<br>
 Bc present, Goddess, to thy vot'ry kind,<br>
 And give abundance with benignant mind.</p>
