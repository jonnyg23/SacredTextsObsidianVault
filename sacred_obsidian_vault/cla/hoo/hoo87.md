 <h6 align="CENTER">LXXXII.</h6>
 <h3 align="CENTER">TO OCEAN.</h3>
 <h6 align="CENTER">The Fumigation from AROMATICS</h6>
 <p>OCEAN I call, whose nature ever flows,<br>
 From whom at first both Gods and men arose;<br>
 Sire incorruptible, whose waves surround,  <a name="fr_126"></a><a href="#fn_126"><font size="1">3</font></a><br>
 And earth's concluding mighty circle bound:</p>
 <p><a name="page_220"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 220</font></a></p>
 <p>Hence every river, hence the spreading sea, 5<br>
 And earth's pure bubbling fountains spring from thee:<br>
 Hear, mighty fire, for boundless bliss is thine,<br>
 Whose waters purify the pow'rs divine:<br>
 Earth's friendly limit, fountain of the pole,<br>
 Whose waves wide spreading and circumfluent roll.<br>
 Approach benevolent, with placid mind,<br>
 And be for ever to thy mystics kind.</p>
