 <h6 align="CENTER">XLII</h6>
 <h3 align="CENTER">TO THE SEASONS.</h3>
 <h6 align="CENTER">The FUMIGATION from AROMATICS.</h6>
 <p>DAUGHTERS of Jove and Themis, seasons bright,<br>
 Justice, and blessed peace, and lawful right,<br>
 Vernal and grassy, vivid, holy pow'rs,<br>
 Whose balmy breath exhales in lovely flow'rs<br>
 All-colour'd seasons, rich increase your care, 5<br>
 Circling, for ever flourishing and fair:<br>
 Invested with a veil of shining dew,<br>
 A flow'ry veil delightful to the view:</p>
 <p><a name="page_177"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 177</font></a></p>
 <p>Attending Proserpine, when back from night,<br>
 The Fates and Graces lead her up to light; 10<br>
 When in a band-harmonious they advance,<br>
 And joyful round her, form the solemn dance:<br>
 With Ceres triumphing, and Jove divine;<br>
 Propitious come, and on our incense shine;<br>
 Give earth a blameless store of fruits to bear, 15<br>
 And make a novel mystic's life your care.</p>
