 <h6 align="CENTER">LIX.</h6>
 <h3 align="CENTER">TO THE GRACES.</h3>
 <h6 align="CENTER">The FUMIGATION from STORAX.</h6>
 <p>HEAR me, illustrious Graces, mighty nam'd,<br>
 From Jove descended and Eunomia fam'd;<br>
 Thalia, and Aglaia fair and bright,<br>
 And blest Euphrosyne whom joys delight:<br>
 Mothers of mirth, all lovely to the view, 5<br>
 Pleasure abundant pure belongs to you:<br>
 Various, forever flourishing and fair,<br>
 Desir'd by mortals, much invok'd in pray'r:<br>
 Circling, dark-ey'd, delightful to mankind,<br>
 Come, and your mystics bless with bounteous mind.</p>
