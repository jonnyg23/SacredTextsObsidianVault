 <h6 align="CENTER">XX.</h6>
 <h3 align="CENTER">TO THE CLOUDS.</h3>
 <h6 align="CENTER">The FUMIGATION from MYRRH.</h6>
 <p>Ærial clouds, thro' heav'n's resplendent plains<br>
 Who wander, parents of prolific rains;<br>
 Who nourish fruits, whose water'y frames are hurl'd,<br>
 By winds impetuous, round the mighty world;<br>
 All-thund'ring, lion-roaring, flashing fire, 5<br>
 In Air's wide bosom, bearing thunders dire<br>
 Impell'd by ev'ry stormy, sounding gale,<br>
 With rapid course, along the skies ye fail.<br>
 With blowing winds your wat'ry frames I call,<br>
 On mother Earth with fruitful show'rs to fall.</p>
