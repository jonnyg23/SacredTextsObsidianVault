 <h6 align="CENTER">XLVI.</h6>
 <h3 align="CENTER">TO BACCHUS PERICIONIUS <a name="fr_108"></a><a href="#fn_108"><font size="1">*</font></a>.</h3>
 <h6 align="CENTER">The FUMIGATION from AROMATICS.</h6>
 <p>BACCHUS Pericionius, hear my pray'r,<br>
 Who mad'st the house of Cadmus once thy care,<br>
 With matchless force, his pillars twining round,<br>
 (When burning thunders shook the solid ground,<br>
 In flaming, founding torrents borne along), 5<br>
 Propt by thy grasp indissolubly strong.<br>
 Come mighty Bacchus to these rites inclin'd,<br>
 And bless thy suppliants with rejoicing mind.</p>
