 <h6 align="CENTER">LII.</h6>
 <h3 align="CENTER">To AMPHIETUS BACCHUS.</h3>
 <h6 align="CENTER">The FUMIGATION from every AROMATIC except FRANKINCENSE.</h6>
 <p>TERRESTRIAL Dionysius, hear my pray'r,<br>
 Awak'ned rise with nymphs of lovely hair:<br>
 Great Amphietus Bacchus, annual God,<br>
 Who laid asleep in Proserpine's abode,<br>
 Did'st lull to drowsy and oblivious rest,<br>
 The rites triennial, and the sacred feast;<br>
 Which rous'd again by thee, in graceful ring,<br>
 Thy nurses round thee mystic anthems sing;<br>
 When briskly dancing with rejoicing pow'rs,<br>
 Thou mov'st in concert with the circling hours. 10<br>
 Come, blessed, fruitful, horned, and divine,<br>
 And on these rites with joyful aspect shine;<br>
 Accept the general incense and the pray'r,<br>
 And make prolific holy fruits thy care.</p>
