 <h6 align="CENTER">XII.</h6>
 <h3 align="CENTER">TO SATURN.</h3>
 <h6 align="CENTER">The FUMIGATION from STORAX.</h6>
 <p>ETHERIAL father, mighty Titan, hear,  <a name="fr_70"></a><a href="#fn_70"><font size="1">1</font></a><br>
 Great fire of Gods and men, whom all revere:<br>
 Endu'd with various council, pure and strong,<br>
 To whom perfection and decrease belong.<br>
 Consum'd by thee all forms that hourly die, 5<br>
 By thee restor'd, their former place supply;<br>
 The world immense in everlasting chains,<br>
 Strong and ineffable thy pow'r contains<br>
 Father of vast eternity, divine,<br>
 O mighty Saturn, various speech is thine: 10<br>
 Blossom of earth and of the starry skies,<br>
 Husband of Rhea, and Prometheus wife.</p>
 <p><a name="page_137"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 137</font></a></p>
 <p>Obstetric Nature, venerable root,<br>
 From which the various forms of being shoot;<br>
 No parts peculiar can thy pow'r enclose, 15<br>
 Diffus'd thro' all, from which the world arose,<br>
 O, best of beings, of a subtle mind,<br>
 Propitious hear to holy pray'rs inclin'd;<br>
 The sacred rites benevolent attend,<br>
 And grant a blameless life, a blessed end.</p>
