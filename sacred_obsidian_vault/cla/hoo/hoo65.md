 <h6 align="CENTER">LX.</h6>
 <h3 align="CENTER">TO NEMESIS.</h3>
 <h6 align="CENTER">A HYMN.</h6>
 <p>THEE, Nemesis I call, almighty queen,<br>
 By whom the deeds of mortal life are seen:<br>
 Eternal, much rever'd, of boundless sight,<br>
 Alone rejoicing in the just and right:<br>
 Changing the counsels of the human breast 5<br>
 For ever various, rolling without rest.</p>
 <p><a name="page_193"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 193</font></a></p>
 <p>To every mortal is thy influence known,<br>
 And men beneath thy righteous bondage groan;<br>
 For ev'ry thought within the mind conceal'd<br>
 Is to thy fight perspicuously reveal'd. 10<br>
 The soul unwilling reason to obey<br>
 By lawless passion rul'd, thy eyes survey.<br>
 All to see, hear, and rule, O pow'r divine<br>
 Whose nature Equity contains, is thine.<br>
 Come, blessed, holy Goddess, hear my pray'r, 15<br>
 And make thy mystic's life, thy constant care:<br>
 Give aid benignant in the needful hour,<br>
 And strength abundant to the reas'ning pow'r;<br>
 And far avert the dire, unfriendly race<br>
 Of counsels impious, arrogant, and base. 20</p>
