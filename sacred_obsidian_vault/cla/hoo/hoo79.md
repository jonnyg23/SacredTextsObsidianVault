 <h6 align="CENTER">LXXIV.</h6>
 <h3 align="CENTER">TO PALÆMON.</h3>
 <h6 align="CENTER">The FUMIGATION from. MANNA.</h6>
 <p>O Nurs'd with Dionysius, doom'd to keep<br>
 Thy dwelling in the widely-spreading deep:<br>
 With joyful aspect to my pray'r incline,<br>
 Propitious come, and bless the rites divine:<br>
 Thro' earth and sea thy ministers attend, 5<br>
 And from old Ocean's stormy waves defend:<br>
 For ships their safety ever owe to thee,<br>
 Who wand'rest with them thro' the raging sea.<br>
 Come, guardian pow'r, whom mortal tribes desire,<br>
 And far avert the deep's destructive ire. 10</p>
