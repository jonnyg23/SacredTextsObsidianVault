 <h6 align="CENTER">LXXIX.</h6>
 <h3 align="CENTER">TO THE NORTH WIND.</h3>
 <h6 align="CENTER">The FUMIGATION from FRANKINCENSE.</h6>
 <p>BOREAS, whose wint'ry blasts, terrific, tear<br>
 The bosom of the deep surrounding air;<br>
 Cold icy pow'r, approach, and fav'ring blow,<br>
 And Thrace a while desert expos'd to snow:<br>
 The misty station of the air dissolve, 5<br>
 With pregnant clouds, whose frames in show'rs resolve:<br>
 Serenely temper all within the sky,<br>
 And wipe from moisture, Æther's beauteous eye.</p>
