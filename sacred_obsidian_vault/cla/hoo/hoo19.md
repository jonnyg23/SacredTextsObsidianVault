 <h6 align="CENTER">XIV.</h6>
 <h3 align="CENTER">TO JUPITER.</h3>
 <h6 align="CENTER">The FUMIGATION from STORAX.</h6>
 <p>O Jove much-honor'd, Jove supremely great,<br>
 To thee our holy rites we consecrate,</p>
 <p><a name="page_140"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 140</font></a></p>
 <p>Our pray'rs and expiations, king divine,<br>
 For all things round thy head exalted shine.<br>
 The earth is thine, and mountains swelling high, 5<br>
 The sea profound, and all within the sky.<br>
 Saturnian king, descending from above,<br>
 Magnanimous, commanding, sceptred Jove;<br>
 All-parent, principle and end of all,<br>
 Whose pow'r almighty, shakes this earthly ball; 10<br>
 Ev'n Nature trembles at thy mighty nod,<br>
 Loud-sounding, arm'd with light'ning, thund'ring God.<br>
 Source of abundance, purifying king,<br>
 O various-form'd from whom all natures spring;<br>
 Propitious hear my pray'r, give blameless health, 15<br>
 With peace divine, and necessary wealth.</p>
