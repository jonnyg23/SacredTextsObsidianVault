 <h6 align="CENTER">XXXVI.</h6>
 <h3 align="CENTER">TO THE TITANS  <a name="fr_98"></a><a href="#fn_98"><font size="1">*</font></a></h3>
 <h6 align="CENTER">The FUMIGATION from FRANKINCENSE.</h6>
 <p>O Mighty Titans, who from heav'n and earth<br>
 Derive your noble and illustrious birth,<br>
 Our fathers fires, in Tartarus profound<br>
 Who dwell, deep merg'd beneath the solid ground:<br>
 Fountains and principles, from whom began 5<br>
 Th' afflicted, miserable, race of man:<br>
 Who not alone in earth's retreats abide,<br>
 But in the ocean and the air reside;<br>
 Since ev'ry species from your nature flows,<br>
 Which all prolific, nothing barren knows: 10<br>
 Avert your rage, if from th' infernal seats<br>
 One of your tribe should visit our retreats.</p>
