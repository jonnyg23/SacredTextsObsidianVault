 <h6 align="CENTER">LIII.</h6>
 <h3 align="CENTER">To SILENUS, SATYRUS, and the PRIESTESSES of BACCHUS.</h3>
 <h6 align="CENTER">The FUMIGATION from MANNA.</h6>
 <p>GREAT nurse of Bacchus, to my pray'r incline.,<br>
 Silenus, honor'd by the pow'rs divine<br>
 And by mankind at the triennial feast<br>
 Illustrious dæmon, reverenc'd as the best:<br>
 Holy, august, the source of lawful rites, 5<br>
 Rejoicing pow'r, whom vigilance delights<br>
 With Sylvans dancing ever young and fair,<br>
 Head of the Bacchic Nymphs, who ivy bear.<br>
 With all thy Satyrs on our incense shine,<br>
 Dæmons wild form'd, and bless the rites divine; 10<br>
 Come, rouse to sacred Joy thy pupil kin,  <a name="fr_113"></a><a href="#fn_113"><font size="1">*</font></a><br>
 And Brumal Nymphs with rites Lenæan bring;<br>
 Our orgies shining thro' the night inspire,<br>
 And bless triumphant pow'r the sacred choir.</p>
