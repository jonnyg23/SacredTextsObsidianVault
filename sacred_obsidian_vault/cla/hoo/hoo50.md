 <h6 align="CENTER">XLV.</h6>
 <h3 align="CENTER">TO LIKNITUS <a name="fr_107"></a><a href="#fn_107"><font size="1">+</font></a> BACCHUS.</h3>
 <h6 align="CENTER">The FUMIGATION from MANNA.</h6>
 <p>LIKNITAN Bacchus, bearer of the vine,<br>
 Thee I invoke to bless these rites divine:</p>
 <p><a name="page_179"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 179</font></a></p>
 <p>Florid and gay, of nymphs the blossom bright,<br>
 And of fair Venus, Goddess of delight,<br>
 'Tis thine mad footsteps with mad nymphs to beat, 5<br>
 Dancing thro' groves with lightly leaping feet:<br>
 From Jove's high counsels nurst by Proserpine,<br>
 And born the dread of all the pow'rs divine:<br>
 Come, blessed pow'r, regard thy suppliant's voice,<br>
 Propitious come, and in these rites rejoice. 10</p>
