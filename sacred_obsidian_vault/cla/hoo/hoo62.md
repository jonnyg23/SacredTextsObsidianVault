 <h6 align="CENTER">LVII.</h6>
 <h3 align="CENTER">TO CUPID, OR LOVE.</h3>
 <h6 align="CENTER">The FUMIGATION from AROMATICS.</h6>
 <p>I Call great Cupid, source of sweet delight,<br>
 Holy and pure, and lovely to the sight;<br>
 Darting, and wing'd, impetuous fierce desire,<br>
 With Gods and mortals playing, wand'ring fire:<br>
 Cautious, and two-fold, keeper of the keys 5<br>
 Of heav'n and earth, the air, and spreading seas;<br>
 Of all that Ceres' fertile realms contains,<br>
 By which th' all-parent Goddess life sustains,<br>
 Or dismal Tartarus is doom'd to keep,<br>
 Widely extended, or the sounding, deep; 10</p>
 <p><a name="page_190"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 190</font></a></p>
 <p>For thee, all Nature's various realms obey,<br>
 Who rul'st alone, with universal sway.<br>
 Come, blessed pow'r, regard these mystic fires,<br>
 And far avert, unlawful mad desires.</p>
