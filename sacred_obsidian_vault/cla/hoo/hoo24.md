 <h6 align="CENTER">XIX.</h6>
 <h3 align="CENTER">To JOVE, as the AUTHOR of LIGHTNING.</h3>
 <h6 align="CENTER">The FUMIGATION from FRANKINCENSE and MANNA.</h6>
 <p>I Call the mighty, holy, splendid light,<br>
 Aerial, dreadful-sounding, fiery-bright;<br>
 Flaming, aerial-light, with angry voice,<br>
 Lightning thro' lucid clouds with horrid noise.<br>
 Untam'd, to whom resentments dire belong,<br>
 Pure, holy pow'r, all-parent, great and strong:<br>
 Come, and benevolent these rites attend,<br>
 And grant my days a peaceful, blessed end.</p>
