 <h6 align="CENTER">LXXXI.</h6>
 <h3 align="CENTER">TO THE SOUTH WIND.</h3>
 <h6 align="CENTER">The FUMIGATION from FRANKINCENSE.</h6>
 <p>WIDE coursing gales, whose lightly leaping feet<br>
 With rapid wings the air's wet bosom beat,<br>
 Approach benevolent, swift-whirling pow'rs,<br>
 With humid clouds the principles of flow'rs:<br>
 For flow'ry clouds are portion'd to your care, 5<br>
 To send on earth from all surrounding air.<br>
 Bear, blessed pow'rs, these holy rites attend,<br>
 And fruitful rains on earth all-parent send.</p>
