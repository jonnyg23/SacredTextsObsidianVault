 <h6 align="CENTER">XLIV.</h6>
 <h3 align="CENTER">TO DIONYSIUS BASSAREUS TRIENNALIS <a name="fr_106"></a><a href="#fn_106"><font size="1">*</font></a>.</h3>
 <h6 align="CENTER">A HYMN</h6>
 <p>COME, blessed Dionysius, various nam'd,<br>
 Bull-fac'd, begot from Thunder, Bacchus fam'd.<br>
 Bassarian God, of universal might,<br>
 Whom swords, and blood, and sacred rage delight:<br>
 In heav'n rejoicing, mad, loud-sounding God, 5<br>
 Furious inspirer, bearer of the rod:<br>
 By Gods rever'd, who dwell'st with human kind,<br>
 Propitious come, with much-rejoicing mind.</p>
