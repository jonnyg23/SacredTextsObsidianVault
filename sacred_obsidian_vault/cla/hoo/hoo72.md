 <h6 align="CENTER">LXVII.</h6>
 <h3 align="CENTER">TO HEALTH.</h3>
 <h6 align="CENTER">The FUMIGATION from MANNA.</h6>
 <p>O Much-desir'd, prolific, gen'ral queen,<br>
 Hear me, life-bearing, Health, of beauteous mien,<br>
 Mother of all; by thee diseases dire,<br>
 Of bliss destructive, from our life retire;<br>
 And ev'ry house is flourishing and fair, 5<br>
 If with rejoicing aspect thou art there:<br>
 Each dædal art, thy vig'rous force inspires,<br>
 And all the world thy helping hand desires;<br>
 Pluto life's bane alone resists thy will,<br>
 And ever hates thy all-preserving skill. 10<br>
 O fertile queen, from thee forever flows<br>
 To mortal life from agony repose;<br>
 And men without thy all-sustaining ease,<br>
 Find nothing useful, nothing form'd to please;<br>
 Without thy aid, not Plutus' self can thrive, 15<br>
 Nor man to much afflicted age arrive;<br>
 For thou alone of countenance serene,<br>
 Dost govern all things, universal queen.<br>
 Assist thy mystics with propitious mind,<br>
 And far avert disease of ev'ry kind.</p>
