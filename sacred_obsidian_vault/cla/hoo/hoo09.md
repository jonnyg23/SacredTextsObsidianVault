 <h6 align="CENTER">IV.</h6>
 <h3 align="CENTER">TO FIRE.</h3>
 <h6 align="CENTER">The FUMIGATION from SAFFRON.</h6>
 <p>O Ever untam'd Fire, who reign'st on high<br>
 In Jove's dominions ruler of the sky;<br>
 The glorious sun with dazzling lustre bright,<br>
 And moon and stars from thee derive their light;<br>
 All taming pow'r, ætherial shining fire, 5<br>
 Whose vivid blasts the heat of life inspire:</p>
 <p><a name="page_118"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 118</font></a></p>
 <p>The world's best element, light-bearing pow'r,<br>
 With starry radiance shining, splendid flow'r,<br>
 O hear my suppliant pray'r, and may thy frame<br>
 Be ever innocent, serene, and tame. 10</p>
