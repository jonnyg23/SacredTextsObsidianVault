 <h6 align="CENTER">LVI.</h6>
 <h3 align="CENTER">TO THE TERRESTRIAL HERMES.</h3>
 <h6 align="CENTER">The FUMIGATION from STORAX.</h6>
 <p>HERMES I call, whom Fate decrees to dwell<br>
 In the dire path which leads to deepest hell<br>
 O Bacchic Hermes, progeny divine<br>
 Of Dionysius, parent of the vine,</p>
 <p><a name="page_189"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 189</font></a></p>
 <p>And of celestial Venus Paphian queen, 5<br>
 Dark eye-lash'd Goddess of a lovely mien:<br>
 Who constant wand'rest thro' the sacred feats<br>
 Where hell's dread empress, Proserpine, retreats;<br>
 To wretched souls the leader of thc way<br>
 When Fate decrees, to regions void of day: 10<br>
 Thine is the wand which causes sleep to fly,<br>
 Or lulls to slumb'rous rest the weary eye;<br>
 For Proserpine thro' Tart'rus dark and wide<br>
 Gave thee forever flowing souls to guide.<br>
 Come, blessed pow'r the sacrifice attend, 15<br>
 And grant our mystic works a happy end.</p>
