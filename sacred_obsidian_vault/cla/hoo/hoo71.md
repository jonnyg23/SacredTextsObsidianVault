 <h6 align="CENTER">LXVI.</h6>
 <h3 align="CENTER">TO ESCULAPIUS.</h3>
 <h6 align="CENTER">The FUMIGATION from MANNA.</h6>
 <p>GREAT Esculapius, skill'd to heal mankind,,<br>
 All-ruling Pæan, and physician kind;<br>
 Whose arts medic'nal, can alone assuage<br>
 Diseases dire, and stop their dreadful rage:<br>
 Strong lenient God, regard my suppliant pray'r,<br>
 Bring gentle Health, adorn'd with lovely hair;<br>
 Convey the means of mitigating pain,<br>
 And raging, deadly pestilence restrain.<br>
 O pow'r all-flourishing, abundant, bright,<br>
 Apollo's honor'd offspring, God of light; 10<br>
 Husband of blameless Health, the constant foe<br>
 Of dread Disease the minister of woe:</p>
 <p><a name="page_199"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 199</font></a></p>
 <p>Come, blessed saviour, and my health defend,<br>
 And to my life afford a prosp'rous end.</p>
