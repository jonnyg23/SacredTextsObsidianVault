 <h1 align="CENTER">60</h1>
 <h3 align="CENTER">To Priapus</h3>
 <p>Si quot habes versus, tot haberes poma, Priape,<br>
    esses antiquo ditior Alcinoo.</p>
 <p>Hadst thou as many of apples as offers of verses (Priapus!),<br>
 Richer than Alcinous ancient of days were thy lot.</p>
 <p>If as many verses so many apples thou hast dedicated to<br>
 thee, O Priapus, thou wilt be richer than of yore Alcinous.</p>
 <font size="2"><p>[1. Steals anything without my being aware of it.</p>
 <p>2. Sodomised.]</p>
