 <h1 align="CENTER">7</h1>
 <h3 align="CENTER">Priapus</h3>
 <p>Matronae procul hinc abite castae:<br>
 turpe est vos legere impudica verba.-<br>
 non assis faciunt euntque recta:<br>
 nimirum sapiunt videntque magnam<br>
 matronae quoque mentulam libenter.</p>
 <p>Matrons avoid this site, for your chaste breed<br>
 'Twere vile these verses <i>impudique</i> to read.<br>
 They still come on and not a <i>doit</i> they heed!<br>
 O'ermuch these matrons know and they regard<br>
 With willing glances this my vasty yard.</p>
 <p>Go far hence, ye virtuous wives, 'tis unseemly for you to read lewd verses.[1] They care not an as [for my words],[2] and straightway approach. Verily these matrons are sensible, and look joyfully, too, on the well-grown mentule.</p>
 <font size="2"><p>[1. The obscene inscriptions scrawled on the base of his statue.</p>
 <p>2. A Roman copper coin of small value.]</p>
