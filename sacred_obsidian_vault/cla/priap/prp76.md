 <h1 align="CENTER">76</h1>
 <h3 align="CENTER">Priapus</h3>
 <p>Per medios ibit pueros mediasque puellas<br>
    mentula, barbatis non nisi summa petet.</p>
 <p>Right through the middle of lads and of lasses a passage shall pierce<br>
 This yard, yet shall it touch bearded ones only aloft.</p>
 <p>Through the middles of lads and girls will my mentule make its way; those bearded 'twill not attack save at the height.</p>
 <font size="2"><p>[1. The courtesan's coynte being jocosely termed an altar.]</p>
