 <h1 align="CENTER">10</h1>
 <h3 align="CENTER">Priapus</h3>
 <p>Ne prendare, cave, prenso nec fuste nocebo,<br>
    saeva nec incurva vulnera falce dabo:<br>
 traiectus conto sic extendere pedali,<br>
    ut culum rugam non habuisse putes.</p>
 <p>'Ware of my catching! If caught, with rod I never will harm thee<br>
 Nor to thee deal sore wound using my sickle that curves.<br>
 Pierced with a foot-long pole thy skin shall be stretched in such fashion<br>
 Thou shalt be fain to believe ne'er had a wrinkle thine arse.</p>
 <p>Take heed lest thou art caught. If I do seize thee, nor with my club will I belabour thee, nor cruel wounds with the curved sickle will inflict on thee. Thrust into by my twelve-inch I pole, thou shalt be so stretched that thou wilt drink thy anus never had any wrinkles.</p>
