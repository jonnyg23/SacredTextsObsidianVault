 <h1 align="CENTER">70</h1>
 <h3 align="CENTER">Priapus</h3>
 <p>Cum fici tibi suavitas subibit<br>
 et iam porrigere huc manum libebit,<br>
 ad me respice, fur, et aestimato,<br>
 quot pondo est tibi mentulam cacandum.</p>
 <p>When the fig's honied sweet thy taste shall catch<br>
 And hither tempt thee hand of thee to stretch;<br>
 Glance at my nature, Thief! and estimate<br>
 The mentule thou must cack and what's it weight.</p>
 <p>When the sweetness of the fig shall come into thy mind and thou shalt long to stretch forth thine hand hither, glance mindfully on me, O thief, and calculate what weight of mentule will be voided by thee.</p>
