 <h1 align="CENTER">3</h1>
 <h3 align="CENTER">To Priapus</h3>
 <p>Obscenas rigido deo tabellas<br>
 dicans ex Elephantidos libellis<br>
 dat donum Lalage rogatque, temptes,<br>
 si pictas opus edat ad figuras.</p>
 <p>These tablets, sacred to the Rigid God,<br>
 From Elephantis' obscene booklets drawn,<br>
 Lalage offers and she prays thee try<br>
 To ply the painted figures' every part.</p>
 <p>Lalage dedicates a votive offering to the God of the standing prickle, bringing pictures from the shameless books of Elephantis, and begs him to try and imitate with her all the different coitions of the painted figures.</p>
