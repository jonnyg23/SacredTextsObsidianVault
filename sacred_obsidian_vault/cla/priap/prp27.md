 <h1 align="CENTER">27</h1>
 <h3 align="CENTER">To Priapus</h3>
 <p>Tu, qui non bene cogitas et aegre<br>
 carpendo tibi temperas ab horto,<br>
 pedicabere fascino pedali.<br>
 quod si tam gravis et molesta poena<br>
 non profecerit, altiora tangam.</p>
 <p>Thou, of unrighteous thought, that hardly canst<br>
 Refrain from robbing this my garden-plot,<br>
 With foot-long fascinum shalt bulghar'd be:<br>
 Yet if so mighty grievous punishment<br>
 Profit thee naught, at higher stead I'll strike.</p>
 <p>Thou who wickedly designest, and scarce forbearest from robbing my garden, shall be sodomised with my twelve-inch fascinum [phallus]. But if so severe and unpleasant a punishment shall not avail., I will strike higher.[1]</p>
