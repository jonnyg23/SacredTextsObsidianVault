 <h1 align="CENTER">72</h1>
 <h3 align="CENTER">Priapus</h3>
 <p>Si commissa meae carpes pomaria curae,<br>
    dulcia quid doleam perdere, doctus eris.</p>
 <p>An thou pluck of this orchard fruit to my guarding committed,<br>
 How for losing the sweets grieve I thou quickly shalt learn.</p>
 <p>If thou pilferest the orchards entrusted to my care, that I grieve to lose pleasurable things thou wilt be taught.[1]</p>
