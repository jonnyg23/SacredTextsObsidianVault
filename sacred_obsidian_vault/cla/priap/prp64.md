 <h1 align="CENTER">64</h1>
 <h3 align="CENTER">Priapus</h3>
 <p>Quidam mollior anseris medulla<br>
 furatum venit huc amore poenae:<br>
 furetur licet usque, non videbo.</p>
 <p>One than a goose's marrow softer far,<br>
 Comes hither stealing for its penalty's sake:<br>
 Steal he as please him: I will see him not.</p>
 <p>A certain one, more tender than the marrow of a goose, comes hither thieving for love of the punishment.[1] He may steal when he fists, I shall not see him.</p>
