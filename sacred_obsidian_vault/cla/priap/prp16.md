 <h1 align="CENTER">16</h1>
 <h3 align="CENTER">Priapus</h3>
 <p>Quid mecum tibi, circitor moleste?<br>
 ad me quid prohibes venire furem?<br>
 accedat, sine: laxior redibit.</p>
 <p>What hast thou, meddling watch, with me to do?<br>
 Why baulk the robber who to me would come?<br>
 Let him draw nigh: the laxer shall he go.</p>
 <p>What hast thou to do with me, thou meddlesome watchman? why dost thou hinder the thief from coming to me? Let him approach: he will return more 'open'!</p>
