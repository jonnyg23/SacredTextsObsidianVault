 <i><font size="2"><p align="CENTER">To the</p>
 </font></i><font size="4"><p align="CENTER">ANCIENT PLATONIC PHILOSOPHERS</p><dir>
 <dir>
 
 </dir></dir></font><font face="Times Roman,Times New Roman"><p>HAIL souls triumphant! Truth is all your own,<br>
 Lov'd by the wise, to Folly's sons unknown.<br>
 Let Ign'rance proudly boast her tyrant reign,<br>
 Her num'rous vot'ries, and her wide domain,<br>
 Your wisdom scorn, and with barbaric hand<br>
 Spread dire delusion thro' a falling land.<br>
 By you inspir'd, the glorious talk be mine,<br>
 To rise from Sense, and seek a life divine;<br>
 From Phantasy, the soul's Calypso, free,<br>
 To fail secure on Life's tempestuous sea,<br>
 Led by your doctrines like the Pleiads' light,<br>
 With guiding radiance streaming thro' the night,<br>
 From mighty Neptune's overwhelming ire,<br>
 Back to the, palace of my lawful Sire.</p>
 
 
 </font><p align="CENTER">THE END</p>
