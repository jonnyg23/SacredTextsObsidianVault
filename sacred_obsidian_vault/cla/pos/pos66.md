 <p><a name="page_79"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 79</font></a></p>  <h1 align="center">PHAON</h1>  
 <p><a name="page_80"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 80</font></a> <a name="page_81"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 81</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">PHILOMEL</h3>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Philomel</span> in my garden,<br> Messenger sweet of springtide,<br>      From the bough of the olive tree utter<br>          Tidings ecstatic.</p>  
 <p>Linger long on thy olden<br> Note as in days remembered;<br>      Ere the Boatman that knew Aphrodite<br>          Ravished my vision.</p>  
 <p>Fatal glamor of beauty,<br> Beauty of Gods made mortal;<br>      Ah, before its delight I am ever<br>          Fearful of heaven.</p>  
 <p>Spring in breeze and the blossom,<br> Grasses and leaves and odors,<br>      On my heart with the breath of a vanished<br>          April is shaken;</p>  
 <p>Shaken with thrill and regret of<br> Lost caresses and kisses;<br>      Anactoria's memory, Atthis<br>          Never forgotten.</p>  
 <p>Philomel in my garden,<br> Messenger sweet of springtide,<br>      From the bough of the olive tree utter<br>          Tidings ecstatic.</p>
