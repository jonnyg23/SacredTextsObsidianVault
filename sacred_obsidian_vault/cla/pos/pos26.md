 <p><a name="page_32"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 32</font></a> <a name="page_33"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 33</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">MAIDENHOOD</h3>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Do</span> I long for maidenhood?<br>   Do I long for days<br> When upon the mountain slope<br>   I would stand and gaze<br> Over the Ægean's blue<br>   Melting into mist,<br> Ere with love my virgin lips<br>   Cercolas had kissed?</p>  
 <p>Maidenhood, O maidenhood,<br>   Whither hast thou flown?<br> <i>To a land beyond the sea<br>   Thou hast never known</i>.<br> Maidenhood, O maidenhood,<br>   Wilt return to me?<br> <i>Never will my bloom again<br>   Give its grace to thee</i>.</p>  
 <p>Now the autumn skies are low,<br>   Youth and summer sped;<br> Shepherd hills are far away,<br>   Cercolas is dead.<br> Mitylene's marble courts<br>   Echo with my name;<br> Maidenhood, we never dreamed,<br>   Long ago of fame.</p>
