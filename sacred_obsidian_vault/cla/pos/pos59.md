 <p><a name="page_72"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 72</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">TELESIPPA</h3>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Sleep</span> thou in the bosom<br> Of thy tender girl friend,<br> Telesippa, gentle<br> Maiden from Miletus.</p>  
 <p>Like twin petals shyly<br> Closing to the darkness,<br> Dewy on your drooping<br> Lids shall fall her kisses.</p>  
 <p>While her arms enfold you,<br> On your drowsy senses<br> Shall her soft caresses<br> Seal delicious languor.</p>  
 <p>Warm from her desireful<br> Heart the flush of passion<br> On your cheek unconscious,<br> With her sighs shall deepen.</p>  
 <p>All the long sweet night-time,<br> Sleepless while you slumber,<br> She shall lie and quiver<br> With her love's mad longing.</p>
