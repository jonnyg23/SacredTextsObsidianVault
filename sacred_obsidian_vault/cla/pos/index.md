 <p>
 This is an early 20th century attempt to weave the fragments of Sappho
 into a set of complete poems.
 The translator is building on the consensus view of Sappho at the time,
 very heavily influenced by such
 speculations as <a href="../sob/index.htm">Bilitis</a>.
 The poetry is serviceable, and O'Hara is careful not to overstep
 his mission.
 This book is not very well known, and because it was printed in a small
 run and never reprinted, is fairly scarce and hence used copies are expensive.
 <small>--J.B. Hare, June 11th, 2008.</small>
 </p>
