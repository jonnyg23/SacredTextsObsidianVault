 <p><a name="page_68"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 68</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">ANDROMEDA</h3>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">What</span> bucolic maiden<br> Now thy heart bewitches,<br> O my Andromeda<br> Of the strange amours?</p>  
 <p>Round her awkward ankles<br> She has not the faintest<br> Sense of art to draw her<br> Long ungraceful tunic.</p>  
 <p>Yet she surely makes thee,<br> O my Andromeda,<br> For thy sweet unlawful<br> Love a fair requital.</p>  
 <p>Joy and praise attend thee,<br> In thy keen perceptive<br> Taste for beauty, daughter<br> Of Polyanax!</p>
