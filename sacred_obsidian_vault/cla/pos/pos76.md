 <p><a name="page_94"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 94</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">THE PRIESTESS OF ARTEMIS</h3>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Maidens</span>, that pass my tomb with laughter sweet,<br> A voice unresting echoes at your feet;<br> Pause, and if any would my story seek,<br> Dumb as I am, these graven words will speak;<br> Once in the vanished years it chanced to please<br> Arista, daughter of Hermocleides,<br> To dedicate my life in virgin bliss<br> To thee, revered of women, Artemis!<br> O Goddess, deign to bless my grandsire's line,<br> For Saon was a temple priest of thine;<br> And grant, O Queen, in thy benefic grace,<br> Unending fame and fortune to his race.</p>
