 <p><a name="page_70"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 70</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">GORGO</h3>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Gorgo</span>, I am weary<br> Of thy love's insistence,<br> Thou to me appearest<br> An ill-favored child.</p>  
 <p>Though I am than Gello<br> Fonder still of virgins,<br> Toward thee I have never<br> Felt the least desire.</p>  
 <p>Yesternight I knew not<br> What to do, for pity<br> Moved my bosom deeply,<br> Seeing thee implore.</p>  
 <p>Harassed by alternate<br> Yielding and refusal,<br> I was half persuaded<br> Then to grant thy prayer.</p>  
 <p>At my door thy presence<br> Lingers like a shadow;<br> Vain wouldst thou reproach me<br> With appealing eyes.</p>  
 <p>Dost thou think by constant<br> Proofs of lasting passion,<br> Slowly my obdurate<br> Will to wear away?</p>  
 <p>Gorgo, I am weary<br> Of thy love's insistence,<br> And my strength exhausted<br> Grants thy wish at last.</p>
