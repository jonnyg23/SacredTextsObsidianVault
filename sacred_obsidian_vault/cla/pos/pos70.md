 <p><a name="page_85"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 85</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">HESPERUS</h3>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Hesperus</span> shines<br>   Low on the eastern wave,<br> Off toward the Asian shore;</p>  
 <p>Over faint lines<br>   Whose grays and purples pave<br> Where seas night-calmed adore.</p>  
 <p>Fair vesper fire,<br>   Fairest of stars, the light<br> Benign of secret bliss</p>  
 <p>Star of desire,<br>   Bringing to me with night<br> Dreams and my Phaon's kiss.</p>
