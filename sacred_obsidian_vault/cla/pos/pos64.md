 <p><a name="page_77"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 77</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">DAMOPHYLA</h3>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Cold</span> of heart and strangely<br> Uninclined to passion,<br> Wisdom's vigil leaves thee,<br> Proud Damophyla.</p>  
 <p>Sapphics thou hast written,<br> Verses in my metre,<br> With a skill surpassing<br> In the melic art.</p>  
 <p>Love's superb enchantment<br> Thou art fain to banish,<br> Like the virgin Huntress<br> Long by thee adored.</p>  
 <p>Molded by thy tunic,<br> Every arching contour<br> Of her chaste and noble<br> Form I dream to see;</p>  
 <p>Even view her stepping<br> From the leafy covert<br> Down the dawn-white valley,<br> Stately as a stag.</p>  
 <p>Long I sued but found thee<br> Deaf to all entreaty,<br> Till one summer twilight<br> Listless in the heat;</p>  
 <p>Soothed by slumber's languor,<br> And my low monodic<br> Voice that hymned a pæan<br> In the praise of love;</p>  
 <p>Loth to yield yet vanquished,<br> As I knelt beside thee,<br> All thy long resistance<br> To my kiss succumbed.</p>
