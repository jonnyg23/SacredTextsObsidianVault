 <p><a name="page_34"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 34</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">EVER MAIDEN</h3>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">I shall</span> be ever maiden,<br>   Ever the little child,<br> In my passionate quest for the lovely,<br>   By earth's glad wonder beguiled.</p>  
 <p>I shall be ever maiden,<br>   Standing in soul apart,<br> For the Gods give the secret of beauty<br>   Alone to the virgin heart.</p>
