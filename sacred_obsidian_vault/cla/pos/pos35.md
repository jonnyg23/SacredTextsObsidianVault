 <p><a name="page_42"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 42</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">PRIDE</h3>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Pride</span> not thyself upon a ring,<br>   Or any trinket thing<br> Of fleeting value, dross or gold.</p>  
 <p>Wealth, lacking worth, is no safe friend,<br>   Though both to life may lend,<br> In just proportion, joy untold.</p>
