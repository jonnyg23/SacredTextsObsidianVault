 <p><a name="page_36"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 36</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">ASPIRATION</h3>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">I do</span> not think with my two arms to touch the sky,<br>   I do not dream to do almighty things;<br> So small a singing bird may never soar so high,<br>   To beat the sapphire fire with baffled wings.</p>  
 <p>I do not think with my two arms to touch the sky,<br>   I do not dream by any chance to share<br> With deathless Gods the bliss of Paphos they deny<br>   To men behind the azure veil of air.</p>
