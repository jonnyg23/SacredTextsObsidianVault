 <p><a name="page_38"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 38</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">COURAGE</h3>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Faint</span> not in thy strong heart!<br>   Nor downcast stand apart;<br> Beyond the reach of daring will there lies<br>   No beauty's prize.</p>  
 <p>Faint not in thy strong heart!<br>   Through temple, field and mart,<br> Courage alone the guerdon from the fray<br>   May bear away.</p>
