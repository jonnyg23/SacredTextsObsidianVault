 <p><a name="page_4"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 4</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">MUSAGETES</h3>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Come</span> with Musagetes, ye Hours and Graces,<br> Dance around the team of swans that attend him<br> Up Parnassian heights, to his holy temple<br> High on the hill-top;</p>  
 <p>Come, ye Muses, too, from the shades of Pindus,<br> Let your songs, that echo on winds of rapture,<br> Wake the lyre he tunes to the sweet inspiring<br> Sound of your voices.</p>
