 <p><a name="page_41"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 41</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">GNOMICS</h3>  <h4 align="center">I</h4>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">My</span> ways are quiet, none may find<br> My temper of malignant kind;<br> For one should check the words that start<br> When anger spreads within the heart.</p>  <h4 align="center">II</h4>  
 <p>Who from my hands what I can spare<br> Of gifts accept the largest share,<br> Those are the very ones who boast<br> No gratitude and wrong me most.</p>  <h4 align="center">III</h4>  
 <p>He who in face and form is fair<br> Must needs be good, the Gods declare;<br> But he whose thought and act are right<br> Will soon be equal fair to sight.</p>  <h4 align="center">IV</h4>  
 <p>Beauty of youth is but the flower<br> Of spring, whose pleasure lasts an hour;<br> While worth that knows no mortal doom<br> Is like the amaranthine bloom.</p>
