 <p><a name="page_54"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 54</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">COMPARISON</h3>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Less</span> soft a Tyrian robe<br>   Of texture fine,<br> Less delicate a rose<br>   Than flesh of thine.</p>  
 <p>Whiter thy breast than snow<br>   That virgin lies,<br> And deeper than the blue<br>   Of seas thy eyes.</p>  
 <p>More golden than the fruit<br>   Of orange trees,<br> Thy locks that floating lure<br>   The satyr breeze.</p>  
 <p>Less fine of silver string<br>   An Orphic lyre,<br> Less sweet than thy low laugh<br>   That wakes desire.</p>
