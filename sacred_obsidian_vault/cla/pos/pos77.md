 <p><a name="page_95"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 95</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">PELAGON</h3>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Above</span> the lowly grave of Pelagon,<br> Ill-fated fisher lad, Meniscus son,<br> His father placed as sign of storm and strife<br> The weel and oar, memorial of his life.</p>
