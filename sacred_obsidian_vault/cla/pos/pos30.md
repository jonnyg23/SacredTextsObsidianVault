 <p><a name="page_37"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 37</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">HERO, OF GYARA</h3>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">I taught</span> Hero, of Gyara, the swift runner;<br>   Swifter far was she than Atalanta,<br> When through clinging fleece of her wind-rippled<br>   Garments blushed the glimmer of her limbs.</p>  
 <p>I taught Hero, of Gyara, the swift runner;<br>   Lovelier was she than Atalanta,<br> When the straining vision of the suitor<br>   Saw her beauty mock impending death.</p>  
 <p>I taught Hero, of Gyara, the swift runner,<br>   All the singing numbers of Terpander,<br> Metres of Archilochus and Alcman,<br>   And my melic verse that glows supreme.</p>  
 <p>I taught Hero, of Gyara, the swift runner,<br>   Sapphics with their triple surge of music<br> Melting in the final verse Adonic,<br>   Like the foam fall of a spended wave.</p>
