 <p><a name="page_40"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 40</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">GOLD</h3>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Gold</span> is the son of Zeus,<br>   Immortal, bright;<br> Nor moth nor worm may eat it,<br>   Nor rust tarnish.</p>  
 <p>So are the Muse's gifts<br>   The offspring fair,<br> That merit from high heaven<br>   Youth eternal.</p>
