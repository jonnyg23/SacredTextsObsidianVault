 <p><a name="page_2"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 2</font></a></p>  <h1 align="center">SAPPHICS</h1>  
 <p><a name="page_3"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 3</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">THE MUSES</h3>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Hither</span> now, O Muses, leaving the golden<br> House of God unseen in the azure spaces,<br> Come and breathe on bosom and brow and kindle<br>              Song like the sunglow;</p>  
 <p>Come and lift my shaken soul to the sacred<br> Shadow cast by Helicon's rustling forests;<br> Sweep on wings of flame from the middle ether,<br>              Seize and uplift me;</p>  
 <p>Thrill my heart that throbs with unwonted fervor,<br> Chasten mouth and throat with immortal kisses,<br> Till I yield on maddening heights the very<br>              Breath of my body.</p>
