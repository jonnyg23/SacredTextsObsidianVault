 <p><a name="page_92"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 92</font></a> <a name="page_93"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 93</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">THE DUST OF TIMAS</h3>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">This</span> is the dust of Timas! Here inurned<br> Rest the dear ashes where so late had burned<br> Her spirit's flame. She perished, gentle maid,<br> Before her bridal day and now a shade,<br> Silent and sad, she evermore must be<br> In the dark chamber of Persephone.<br> When life had faded with the flower and leaf,<br> Each girl friend sweet, in token of her grief,<br> Resigned her severed locks with bended head,<br> Beauty's fair tribute to the lovely dead.</p>
