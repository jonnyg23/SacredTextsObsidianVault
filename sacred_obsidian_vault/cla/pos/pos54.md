 <p><a name="page_65"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 65</font></a></p>  <h1 align="center">GIRL FRIENDS</h1>  
 <p><a name="page_66"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 66</font></a> <a name="page_67"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 67</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">PRELUDE</h3>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Deftly</span> on my little<br> Seven-stringed barbitos,<br> Now to please my girl friends<br> Songs I set to music.</p>  
 <p>Maidens fair, companions<br> Of the Muses, never<br> Toward you shall my feelings<br> Undergo a change.</p>  
 <p>Chanted in a plaintive<br> Old Ionic measure,<br> All the songs I give you<br> Are the songs of love.</p>
