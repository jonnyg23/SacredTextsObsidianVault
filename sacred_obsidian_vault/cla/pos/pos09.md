 <p><a name="page_10"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 10</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">SUMMER</h3>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Slumber</span> streams from quivering leaves that listless<br> Bask in heat and stillness of Lesbian summer;<br> Breathless swoons the air with the apple-blossoms<br>                 Delicate odor;</p>  
 <p>From the shade of branches that droop and cover<br> Shallow trenches winding about the orchard,<br> Restful comes, and cool to the sense, the flowing<br>                 Murmur of water.</p>
