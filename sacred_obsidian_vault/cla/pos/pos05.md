 <p><a name="page_6"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 6</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">MOON AND STARS</h3>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">When</span> the moon at full on the sill of heaven<br> Lights her beacon, flooding the earth with silver,<br> All the shining stars that about her cluster<br>                Hide their fair faces;</p>  
 <p>So when Anactoria's beauty dazzles<br> Sight of mine, grown dim with the joy it gives me,<br> Gorgo, Atthis, Gyrinno, all the others<br>                Fade from my vision.</p>
