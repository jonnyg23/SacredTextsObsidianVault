 <p><a name="page_76"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 76</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">GONGYLA</h3>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">It</span> was when the sunset<br> Burned with saffron fire,<br> And Apollo's coursers<br> Turned below the hills,</p>  
 <p>That on Mitylene's<br> Marble bridge we met,<br> Gongyla, thou golden<br> Maid of Colophon.</p>  
 <p>Like the breath of morning<br> Or a breeze from sea,<br> Fresh thy beauty smote me,<br> Virile of the north.</p>  
 <p>Startled by thy vision,<br> Transports half divine<br> Flooded veins and bosom,<br> Shook me with desire.</p>  
 <p>Soon the kinder sunglow<br> Of Æolic lands<br> Melted all the futile<br> Snows about thy heart.</p>
