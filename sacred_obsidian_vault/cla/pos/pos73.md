 <p><a name="page_88"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 88</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">DARK-EYED SLEEP</h3>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Dark-eyed</span> Sleep, child of Night,<br>   Come in thy shadow garment to my couch,<br> And with thy soothing touch,<br> Cool as the vesper breeze,<br>   Grant that I may forget;</p>  
 <p>Bestow condign release,<br>   A taste of rest that comes with endless sleep;<br> Lure off the haunting dreams,<br> The dire Eumenides<br>   That torture my repose.</p>  
 <p>For I would live a space<br>   Though Phaon has forsaken me, nor yet<br> Be found on shadow fields<br> Among the lilies tall<br>   Of pale Persephone.</p>
