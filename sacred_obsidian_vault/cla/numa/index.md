 <h1 align="CENTER">The Religion of Numa</h1>
 <h5 align="CENTER">And Other Essays on the Religion of Ancient Rome</h5>
 <h2 align="CENTER">by Jesse Benedict Carter</h2>
 <h4 align="CENTER">[1906]</h4>
 </td>
 </tr></table>
 <hr>
 <p>
 This is a short survey of the history of Roman religion, particularly the
 Roman pantheon, from its origins as abstract animistic deities,
 the infiltration of Greek gods and goddesses, the influence of the
 Sibyllines, the introduction of Near Eastern Goddess worship and other deities,
 and finally the rise of the God-Emperors, codified under Augustus.
 </p>
