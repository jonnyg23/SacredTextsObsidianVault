 <p>
 In this book the eminent classical scholar Gilbert Murray
 details the evolution of Greek religion, from a cycle of festivals
 devoted to the principal Olympian Gods, through the Homeric hymns,
 Hellenistic philosophy including Platonism and Gnosticism.
 Murray traces elements through centuries of Greek spirituality which
 culminated in Christianity.
 He includes a translation of the Treatise of Sallustius,
 which is a credo of later Greek pagan beliefs and philosophy.
 This book is essential reading for classicists and anyone interested in
 the evolution of ancient Greek religion.
 </p>
