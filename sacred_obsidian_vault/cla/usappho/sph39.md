 <h1 align="CENTER"><a name="BM38">38</a></h1>
 <p>Ἕροσ δαὖτέ μ᾽ ὀ λυσιμέλεσ δόνει,<br>
 γλυκύπικρον ἀμάχανον ὄρπετον. <font size="1"><a href="xlatall.htm#58">[transcription]</a></font>
 </p>
 <p>Now Love, the ineluctable, dominates and shakes my being, and fills me with bitter-sweetness.</p>
 <p> </p>
 <p>Now Love, the ineluctable, with bitter sweetness<br>
 Fills me, overwhelms me, and shakes my being.</p>
 <p>Quoted by Hephaestion.</p>
