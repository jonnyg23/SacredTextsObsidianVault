 <h1 align="CENTER"><a name="BM18">18</a></h1>
 <p>Αρτίωσ μ᾽ ἀ χρυσοπέδιλλοσ Ἀύωσ. <font size="1"><a href="xlatall.htm#31">[transcription]</a></font>
 </p>
 <p>Just now the golden-sandalled Dawn [has called].</p>
 <p>There could hardly be a more beautiful epithet than "golden-sandalled" to apply to the Dawn. It is fully equal in this respect to "rosy-fingered," and in Greek both words are beautiful in sound.</p>
 <p>This is quoted by Ammonius of Alexandria about A.D. 400 to show Sappho's use of Ἀρτίωσ <font size="1"><a href="xlatall.htm#32">[transcription]</a></font>
 .</p>
