 <h1 align="CENTER"><a name="BM122">122</a></h1>
 <p>Athenaeus mentions Βάρωμοσ <font size="1"><a href="xlatall.htm#153">[transcription]</a></font>
  and σάρβιτοσ <font size="1"><a href="xlatall.htm#154">[transcription]</a></font>
 , two stringed instruments in use in the time of Sappho. Their exact character is not known. He also gives the form Βάρμοσ <font size="1"><a href="xlatall.htm#155">[transcription]</a></font>
  for the name of the former instrument.</p>
