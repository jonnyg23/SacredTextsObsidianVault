 <p>Ἕγω δ᾽ ἐπὶ μαλθάκαν τύλαν σπολέω μέλεα. <font size="1"><a href="xlatall.htm#68">[transcription]</a></font>
 </p>
 <p>But upon a soft cushion I dispose my limbs</p>

 <p>This is a good example of the choice of words which combine meaning and sound poetically.</p>
