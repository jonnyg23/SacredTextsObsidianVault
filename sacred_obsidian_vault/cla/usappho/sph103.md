 <h1 align="CENTER"><a name="BM102">102</a></h1>
 <p>...Χαῖρε, νύμφα,<br>
 Χαῖρε, τίμιε γαμβε, πόλλα. <font size="1"><a href="xlatall.htm#130">[transcription]</a></font>
 </p>
 <p>Hail bride, and all hail! noble bridegroom.</p>
 <p>Quoted by Servius about A.D. 390, and referred to by Pollux and Julian.</p>
