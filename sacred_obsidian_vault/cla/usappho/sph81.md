 <h1 align="CENTER"><a name="BM80">80</a></h1>
 <p>Δαύοισ ἀπάλασ ἐτάρασ ἐν στήθεσιν. <font size="1"><a href="xlatall.htm#103">[transcription]</a></font>
 </p>
 <p>Sleep thou, in the bosom of thy sweetheart.</p>
 <p>From the "Etymologicum Magnum." This fragment probably belongs among the Epithalamia.</p>
