 <h1 align="CENTER"><a name="BM87">87</a></h1>
 <p>Γλύκεια μᾶτερ, οὔ τοι δύναμαι κρέκην τὸν ἴστον,<br>
 πόθῳ δάμεισα παῖδοσ βραδίναν δἰ Ἀφρόδιταν. <font size="1"><a href="xlatall.htm#111">[transcription]</a></font>
 </p>
 <p>My sweet mother, broken by soft Aphrodite's spell, longing for a youth, I can no more weave the cloth.</p>
 <p>My sweet mother! Fair Aphrodite's spell<br>
 Has from me sense and reason all bereft,<br>
 And, yearning for that dear beloved youth,<br>
 No longer can I see the warp or weft.</p>
 <p>Quoted by Hephaestion as an example of metre.</p>
