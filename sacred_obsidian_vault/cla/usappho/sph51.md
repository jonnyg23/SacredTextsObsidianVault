 <h1 align="CENTER"><a name="BM50">50</a></h1>
 <p>Κρήσσαι νύ ποτ᾽ ὦδ᾽ ἐμμελέωσ πόδεσσιν<br>
 ὠρχεῦντ᾽ ἀπάλοισ ἀμφ᾽ επόεντα Βῶμον<br>
 πόασ τέπεν ἄνθοσ μάλακον μάτεισαι. <font size="1"><a href="xlatall.htm#72">[transcription]</a></font>
 </p>
 <p>Thus sometimes, the Cretan women, tender footed, dance in measure round the fair altar, crushing the fine bloom of the grass.</p>
 <p>From Hephaestion as an example of metre. Blass things that this and the preceeding framgment belong together. The whole is another example of the delicate imagery of Sappho.</p>
