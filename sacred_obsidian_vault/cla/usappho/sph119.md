 <h1 align="CENTER"><a name="BM118">118</a></h1>
 <p>Ρ᾽οδοπτήχεισ και ἐλικώπιδεσ καὶ καλλιπάρῃοι<br>
 καὶ μειλιχοφωνοι. <font size="1"><a href="xlatall.htm#150">[transcription]</a></font>
 </p>
 <p>With rosy cheeks and glancing eyes and voices sweet as honey.</p>
 <p>Philostratus says that this indeed is Sappho's sweet salutation.</p>
 <p>Aristaenetus says that Sappho in a hymeneal song uses the epithet μειλιχόφωνοι <font size="1"><a href="xlatall.htm#151">[transcription]</a></font>
 , "soft voiced".</p>
