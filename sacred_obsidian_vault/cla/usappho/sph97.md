 <h1 align="CENTER"><a name="BM96">96</a></h1>
 <p>Ὄλβιε γάμβρε, σοὶ μὲν δὴ γάμοσ, ὠσ ἄραο<br>
 ἐκτετέλεστ᾽ ἔχεισ δὲ πάρθενον, ἄν ἄραο. <font size="1"><a href="xlatall.htm#122">[transcription]</a></font>
 </p>
 <p>Happy bridegroom! Now has come thy wedding as thou wished, and thou hast the maiden of thy desire.</p>
 <p>Thou happy bridegroom! Now has dawned<br>
   That day of days supreme,<br>
 When in thine arms thou'lt hold at last<br>
   The maiden of thy dream.</p>
 <p>From Hepaestion.</p>
