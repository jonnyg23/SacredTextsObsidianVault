 <h1 align="CENTER"><a name="BM89">89</a></h1>
 <p>Πέρροχοσ ὠσ ὄτ᾽ ἄοιδοσ ὀ Λέσβοισ ἀλλοδάποισιν. <font size="1"><a href="xlatall.htm#113">[transcription]</a></font>
 </p>
 <p>Towering like the singer of Lesbos among men of other lands.</p>
 <p>Quoted by Demetrius about A.D. 150. It is possible that Terpander is meant, but the line may be merely a reference to Lesbian poets in general.</p>
