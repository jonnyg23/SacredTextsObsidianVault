 <h1 align="CENTER"><a name="BM69">69</a></h1>
 <p>Ἄλλά τισ οὐκ ἔμμι παλιγκότων<br>
 ὄργαν, ἀλλ᾽ ἀβάκην τὰν φρέν᾽ ἔχω. <font size="1"><a href="xlatall.htm#91">[transcription]</a></font>
 </p>
 <p>I am not of a malign nature but have a calm temper.</p>
 <p>Quoted in the "Etymologicum Magnum" to show the meaning of ἀβάκησ <font size="1"><a href="xlatall.htm#92">[transcription]</a></font>
 , "innocent", "unsophisticated."</p>
