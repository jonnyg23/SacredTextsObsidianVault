 <h1 align="CENTER"><a name="BM61">61</a></h1>
 <p>Ἕλθοντ᾽ ἐξ ὀράνω πορφυρίαν [ἔχοντα]<br>
      περθέμενον χλάμυν. <font size="1"><a href="xlatall.htm#83">[transcription]</a></font>
 </p>
 <p>Coming from heaven, clad in a purple mantle.</p>
 <p>Quoted by Pollux about A.D. 180 to illustrate Sappho's use of he word <tt>xlamu's</tt>, which she is said to be the first to use.</p>
