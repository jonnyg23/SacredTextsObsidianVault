 <h1 align="CENTER"><a name="BM66">66</a></h1>
 <p>Οὐδ᾽ ἴαν δοκίμοιμι προσίδοισαν φάοσ ἀλίω<br>
 ἔσσεσθαι σοφίαν πάρθενον ἐισ οὐδένα πω χρόνον τοιαύταν. <font size="1"><a href="xlatall.htm#88">[transcription]</a></font>
 </p>
 <p>I think that no maiden shall ever see the sunlight, who shall have thy wisdom.</p>
 <p>No maiden, I think, more wise than thou<br>
 Shall ever see the sun.</p>
 <p>Quoted by Chrysippus, and may be part of the preceding poem.</p>
