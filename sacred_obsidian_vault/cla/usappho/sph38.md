 <h1 align="CENTER"><a name="BM37">37</a></h1>
 <p>Ἦροσ ἄγγελοσ ἰμερόφωνοσ ἄήδων. <font size="1"><a href="xlatall.htm#57">[transcription]</a></font>
 </p>
 <p>The messager of spring, the sweet voiced nighingale.</p>
 <p>Quoted by the Scholiast on the Electra of Sophocles, 149, "the nightingale is the messager of Zeus, because it is the sign of spring."</p>
 <p>Compare Ben Johnson's "The Sad Shepherd," Act II, Scene vi: "The dear good angel of the Spring, the nightingale."</p>
