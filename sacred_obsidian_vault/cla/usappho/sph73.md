 <h1 align="CENTER"><a name="BM72">72</a></h1>
 <p>᾽Αλλ᾽ ἔων φίλοσ ἄμμιν [ἄλλο]<br>
 λέχοσ ἄρνυσω νεώτερον<br>
 οὐ γὰρ τλάσομ᾽ ἔγω ξυνοίκην<br>
 νεῳ γ᾽ ἔσσα γεραιτερα. <font size="1"><a href="xlatall.htm#95">[transcription]</a></font>
 </p>
 <p>For if thou lovest us, choose another and a younger spouse, for I will not endure to live with thee, old woman with young man.</p>
 <p>From the anthology of Stobaeus.</p>
