 <h1 align="CENTER"><a name="BM35">35</a></h1>
 <p>Πσαύην δ᾽ οὐ δοκίμοιμ᾽ ὀράνω δύσι πάχεσιν. <font size="1"><a href="xlatall.htm#55">[transcription]</a></font>
 </p>
 <p>With my two arms, I do not aspire to to touch the sky.</p>
 <p>Quoted by Herodian.</p>
