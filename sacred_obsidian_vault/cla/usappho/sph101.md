 <h1 align="CENTER"><a name="BM100">100</a></h1>
 <p>Χαίροισα ηύμφα, χαιρέτω δ᾽ ὀ γάμβροσ. <font size="1"><a href="xlatall.htm#128">[transcription]</a></font>
 </p>
 <p>The bride [comes] rejoicing, let the bridegroom also rejoice.</p>
 <p>From Hephaestion as a specimen of catalectic iambic.</p>
