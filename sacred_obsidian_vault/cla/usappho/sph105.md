 <h1 align="CENTER"><a name="BM104">104</a></h1>
 <p>A. Παρθενία, παρθενία, ποῖ με λίποισ᾽ ἀποίχῃ; <font size="1"><a href="xlatall.htm#132">[transcription]</a></font>
 <br>
 B. Οὐκέτι ἤξω πρὸσ σέ, οὐκέτι ἤξω. <font size="1"><a href="xlatall.htm#133">[transcription]</a></font>
 </p>
 <p>Maidenhood, maidenhood, whither art thou gone from me?<br>
 Never, O, never again, shall I return to thee.</p>
 <p>Quoted by Demetrius, to show the beauty of Sappho's style, and her successful use of repetition.</p>
