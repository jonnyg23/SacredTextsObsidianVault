 <h1 align="CENTER"><a name="BM67">67</a></h1>
 <p>Τίσ δ᾽ ἀγροιῶτίσ τοι θέλγει νόον,<br>
 οὐκ ἐπισταμένα τὰ βράκἐ ἔλκην<br>
      ἐπί τῶν σφύρων; <font size="1"><a href="xlatall.htm#89">[transcription]</a></font>
 </p>
 <p>What rustic girl bewitches thee who knows not how to draw her dress about her ankles?</p>
 <p>What rustic girl bewitches thee,<br>
 Who cannot even draw<br>
 Her garments neat as they should be,<br>
 Her ankles roundabout?</p>
 <p>Athenaeus and others quote these lines.</p>
