 <h1 align="CENTER"><a name="BM39">39</a></h1>
 <p>Ἄτθι σοὶ δ ἔμεθέν μεν άπήχθετο<br>
 φροντίσδεν, ἔπι δ᾽ Ἀνδρομέδαν πότῃ. <font size="1"><a href="xlatall.htm#59">[transcription]</a></font>
 </p>
 <p>But to thee, Athis, the thought of me is hateful; thou fliest to Andromeda.</p>
 <p>Quoted by Hephaestion with the preceding, to which it does not appear really to belong.</p>
