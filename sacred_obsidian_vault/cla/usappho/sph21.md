 <h1 align="CENTER"><a name="BM20">20</a></h1>
 <p>... Παντοδάπαισ μεμιγμένα χροίαισιν. <font size="1"><a href="xlatall.htm#34">[transcription]</a></font>
 </p>
 <p>Shot with innumerable hues.</p>
 <p>Quoted by the Scholiast on Apollonius of Rhodes, i, 727. Sappho's reference may be to the rainbow.</p>
