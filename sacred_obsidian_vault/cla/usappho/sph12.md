 <h1 align="CENTER"><a name="BM11">11</a></h1>
 <p>... Τάδε νῦν ἐταίραισ<br>
 ταῖσ ἔμαισι τέρπνα κάλωσ ἀείσω. <font size="1"><a href="xlatall.htm#16">[transcription]</a></font>
 </p>
 <p>This will I now sing skilfully to please my friends.</p>
 <p>Athanaeus quotes this to show that there is not necessarily any reproach in the word ἐταίραι <font size="1"><a href="xlatall.htm#17">[transcription]</a></font>
 . Like many others, the fragment is unfortunately too short for anything but a literal translation. The breathing of the word in question in Attic Greek would of course be rough.</p>
