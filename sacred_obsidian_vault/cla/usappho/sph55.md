 <h1 align="CENTER"><a name="BM54">54</a></h1>
 <p>Χρυσοφάη θεράπαιναν Άφροδίτασ. <font size="1"><a href="xlatall.htm#76">[transcription]</a></font>
 </p>
 <p>The handmaiden of Aphrodite, shining like gold.</p>
 <p>In a manuscript of Philodemas about 60 B.C., found at Herculaneum, in which it is said that Sappho thus addresses <tt>Peiðw'</tt>. There is some doubt about this as the manuscript is defective.</p>
