 <h1 align="CENTER"><a name="BM13">13</a></h1>
 <p>Ἕγω δὲ κῆν᾽ ὄττω τισ ἔπαται. <font size="1"><a href="xlatall.htm#19">[transcription]</a></font>
 </p>
 <p>But that which one desires I.</p>
 <p>Quoted by Apollonius and in 1914 found to be part of the poem in the "Oxyrhynchus Papyrus," No. 1231.</p>
