 <h1 align="CENTER"><a name="BM108">108</a></h1>
 <p>Μὴ κίνη χέραδασ. <font size="1"><a href="xlatall.htm#137">[transcription]</a></font>
 </p>
 <p>Stir not the pebbles.</p>
 <p>Mary Barnard translates this: "If you're squeamish, don't prod the beach rubble."</p>
 <p>From the Scholiast on Apollonius Rhodius. Χεράδεσ <font size="1"><a href="xlatall.htm#138">[transcription]</a></font>
  were little heaps of stone.</p>
