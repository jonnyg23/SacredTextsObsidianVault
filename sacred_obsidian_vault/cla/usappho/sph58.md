 <h1 align="CENTER"><a name="BM57">57</a></h1>
 <p>Δεῦτέ νυν ἄβραι Χάριτεσ, καλλίκομοί τε Μοῖσαι. <font size="1"><a href="xlatall.htm#79">[transcription]</a></font>
 </p>
 <p>Come now gentle Graces, and fair-haired Muses.</p>
 <p>Quoted by Hepaestion, Attilius Fortunatianus, and Servius as an example of the choriambic tetrameter used by Sappho.</p>
