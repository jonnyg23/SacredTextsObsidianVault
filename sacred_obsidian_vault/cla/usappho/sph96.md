 <h1 align="CENTER"><a name="BM95">95</a></h1>
 <p>Θυρώρῳ πόδεσ ἐπτορόγυιοι<br>
 τὰ δὲ σάμβαλα πεμπεβόνα,<br>
 πίσυγγοι δὲ δέκ᾽ ἐξεπόνασαν. <font size="1"><a href="xlatall.htm#121">[transcription]</a></font>
 </p>
 <p>To the door-keeper, feet seven fathoms long, and sandals of five bulls' hides, work for ten cobblers.</p>
 <p>Quoted by Hephaestion as a specimen of metre.</p>
