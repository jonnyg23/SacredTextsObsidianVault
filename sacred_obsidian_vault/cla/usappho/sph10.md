 <h1 align="CENTER"><a name="BM9">9</a></h1>
 <p>Αἴθ᾽ ἔγο χρυσοστέφαν᾽ Ἀφρόδιτα,<br>
 τόνδε τὸν πάλον λαχόην. <font size="1"><a href="xlatall.htm#13">[transcription]</a></font>
 </p>
 <p>May I win this prize, O golden-crowned Aphrodite.</p>
 <p>From Apollonius. Sappho invented many beautiful epithets to apply to Aphrodite, and this fragment contains one of them.</p>
