 <h1 align="CENTER"><a name="BM82">82</a></h1>
 <p>Ἕστι μοι κάλα πάισ χρυσίοισιν ἀνθέμοισιν<br>
 ἐμφέρην ἔχοισα μόρφαν, Κλῆισ ἀγαπάτα,<br>
 ἀντι τᾶσ ἔγω οὐδὲ Λυδίαν παῖσαν οὐδ᾽ ἔρανναν. <font size="1"><a href="xlatall.htm#105">[transcription]</a></font>
 </p>
 <p>I have a fair daughter with a form like golden flowers, Cleis the belovedest whom I cherish more than all Lydia or lovely [Lesbos].</p>
 <p>A fair daughter have I, Cleis by name,<br>
 Like a golden flower she seems to me.<br>
 Far more than all Lydia, her do I love,<br>
 Or Lesbos shimmering in the sea.</p>
 <p>Quoted and commented upon by Hephaestion.</p>
