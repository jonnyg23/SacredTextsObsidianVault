 <p>Ἄβρα δηὖτε παχήᾳ σπόλᾳ ἀλλόμαν. <font size="1"><a href="xlatall.htm#73">[transcription]</a></font>
 </p>

 <p>From Herodian as a specimen of metre. It may not be by Sappho.</p>
