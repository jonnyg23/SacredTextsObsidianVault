 <h1 align="CENTER"><a name="BM23">23</a></h1>
 <p>Οὔ τι μοι ὔμμεσ. <font size="1"><a href="xlatall.htm#39">[transcription]</a></font>
 </p>
 <p>You are nought to me.</p>
 <p>Ασ θελετ᾽ ὔμμεσ. <font size="1"><a href="xlatall.htm#40">[transcription]</a></font>
 </p>
 <p>While you will.</p>
 <p>These are quoted by Apollonius to show the Aeolic form ὔμμεσ. <font size="1"><a href="xlatall.htm#41">[transcription]</a></font>
 </p>
