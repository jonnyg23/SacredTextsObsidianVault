 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Phôcus</span> of <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Samos</span>: date unknown.</p>  
 <p>He was credited with a verse work on <i>Nautical Astronomy</i> sometimes ascribed to Thales. Nothing is known of its contents.</p>
