 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Menestôr</span> of <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Sybaris</span> was in his prime about the middle of the fifth century. He wrote on botany, but none of his writings has survived. His opinions are quoted by Theophrastus.</p>
