 <p><a name="page_125"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 125</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">77. BIÔN OF ABDÊRA</h3>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Biôn</span> of <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Abdêra</span> probably lived at the end of the fourth century B.C.</p>  
 <p>A follower of Democritus. He wrote on meteorology, but none of his writings survive.</p>
