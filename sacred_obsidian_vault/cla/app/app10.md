 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Theagenes</span> of <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Rhêgium</span> lived in the time of Cambyses (529-522 B.C.).</p>  
 <p>He was the first scholar known to have applied the allegorical method of interpretation to Homer. No writings survive.</p>
