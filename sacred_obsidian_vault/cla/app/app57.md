 <p><a name="page_87"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 87</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">62. CLEIDÊMUS</h3>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Cleidêmus</span>: birthplace not known. Was active in the fifth century B.C.</p>  
 <p>No writings survive. He is credited by Aristotle and Theophrastus with various scientific speculations.</p>
