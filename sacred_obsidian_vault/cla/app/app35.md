 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Polycleitus</span> of <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Argos</span> was active during the latter half of the fifth century B.C.</p>  
 <p>He wrote a treatise called <i>Canon</i>, on aesthetics.</p>  
 <p>1. The most difficult stage of the work is when the artists clay is within a nail's breadth <a name="fr_65"></a><a href="#fn_60"><font size="1">1</font></a> (<i>of completion?</i>)</p>  
 <p>2. The right result comes gradually through many numbers.</p>
