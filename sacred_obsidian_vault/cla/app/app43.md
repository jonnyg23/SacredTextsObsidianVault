 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Occelus</span> (or <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Ocellus</span>) of <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Lucania</span>: date uncertain.</p>  
 <p>Titles of several works were ascribed to him; but passages from a treatise <i>On the Nature of the Whole</i> are thought to be a forgery.</p>
