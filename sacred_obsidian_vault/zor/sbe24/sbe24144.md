 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Pahlavi Texts, Part III (SBE24)</i>, E.W. West, tr. [1885], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">CHAPTER LVI.</h3>  
 <p>1. The fifty-sixth subject is this, that, when any one wishes to make an evacuation of water, it is necessary that he <i>should</i> not make the evacuation of water <i>while standing</i> on <i>his</i> feet. 2. Because, in the commentary of the Vendîdâ<i>d</i> <a name="fr_1370"></a><a href="#fn_1359"><font size="1">1</font></a>, it is said, concerning that, <i>that it is</i> a serious sin.</p>  
 <p>3. When they squat for evacuating water it is necessary that <i>it extend only</i> <a name="fr_1371"></a><a href="#fn_1360"><font size="1">2</font></a> from the heel as far as the end of the toes; for, if it be more, every drop is a Tanâvar sin. 4. And, when they wish to squat for the evacuation of water, <i>they are</i> to utter one Yathâ-ahû-vairyô <a name="fr_1372"></a><a href="#fn_1361"><font size="1">3</font></a>; <i>and</i>, when the action is over, <i>they are</i> to recite the Ashem-vohû <a name="fr_1373"></a><a href="#fn_1362"><font size="1">4</font></a> three times, the Humatanãm <a name="fr_1374"></a><a href="#fn_1363"><font size="1">5</font></a> twice, the Hukhshathrôtemâi <a name="fr_1375"></a><a href="#fn_1364"><font size="1">6</font></a> three times, the Yathâ-ahû-vairyô four <i>times</i>, and the Ahunem-vairîm <a name="fr_1376"></a><a href="#fn_1365"><font size="1">7</font></a> to the end. 5. Because, every time that they act like this, they are pleasant in the eyes and hearts of mankind, and their words are more approved <i>in</i> the vicinity of kings.</p>
