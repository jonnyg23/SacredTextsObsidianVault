 <p><a name="page_89"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 89</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">CHAPTER XLVII.</h3>  
 <p>1. The sage asked the spirit of wisdom (2) thus: 'What is that thing <i>which</i> is the most perfect of all wealth? 3. What is that which is predominant over everything whatever? 4. And what is that from which no one is able to escape?'</p>  
 <p>5. The spirit of wisdom answered (6) thus: 'It is wisdom which is better than the wealth of every kind which is in the world. 7. It is destiny which is predominant over every one and everything. 8. And <i>it is</i> Vâê the bad <a name="fr_475"></a><a href="#fn_470"><font size="1">1</font></a> from whom no one is able to escape.'</p>
