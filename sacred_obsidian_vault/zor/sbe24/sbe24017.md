 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Pahlavi Texts, Part III (SBE24)</i>, E.W. West, tr. [1885], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">CHAPTER XI.</h3>  
 <p>1. The sage asked the spirit of wisdom (2) thus: 'Is wisdom good, or skill, or goodness <a name="fr_209"></a><a href="#fn_206"><font size="1">1</font></a>?'</p>  
 <p>3. The spirit of wisdom answered (4) thus: 'Wisdom with which <i>there</i> is no goodness, is not to be considered as wisdom; (5) and skill with which <i>there</i> is no wisdom, is not to be considered as skill.'</p>
