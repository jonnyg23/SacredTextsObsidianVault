 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Pahlavi Texts, Part III (SBE24)</i>, E.W. West, tr. [1885], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">CHAPTER LXXIX.</h3>  
 <p>1. The seventy-ninth subject is this, that it is necessary to make an effort that they <i>may</i> exercise munificence and liberality towards the good and the worthy. 2. For the exercise of liberality is grand, <i>in</i> such manner as it is better and pleasanter, <i>in</i> like manner, for the ground on which a liberal man walks, better for the wind that blows upon a liberal man, better for the horse on which a liberal man sits, better, <i>in</i> like manner, for the cow and goat that <a name="fr_1497"></a><a href="#fn_1486"><font size="1">2</font></a> a liberal man eats, <i>and</i> <a name="fr_1498"></a><a href="#fn_1487"><font size="1">3</font></a> pleasanter for the sun and moon and stars that shine upon a liberal man.</p>  
 <p>3. To such an extent is a liberal and munificent <i>man</i> precious (hazîz), that Hôrmazd speaks <a name="fr_1499"></a><a href="#fn_1488"><font size="1">4</font></a> thus: 'I have wished that I <i>might</i> give a recompense <i>to</i> a munificent man, if it be suitable for him, <i>but</i> I have not found any recompense and happiness that are suitable for him, except a blessing.' 4. And virtuous men and the united archangels are perpetually uttering blessings on account of the liberal man who maintains no refusal of his own things to a stranger.</p>  
 <p>

 <p>
 </p>  
 <p><a name="page_343"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 343</font></a></p>  
 <p>5. For it is declared in revelation, that the creator Hôrmazd spoke to Zaratu<i>s</i>t, the Spitamân, thus: 'I have created the supreme heaven of heavens for the sake of any of the liberal who provide for the worthy and give them something; and gloomy hell is for all those persons who give anything to the unworthy.'</p>  
 <p>6. <i>In</i> like manner it is declared in revelation, that there are thirty-three ways to heaven, besides <i>that of</i> the souls of the liberal. 7. If the soul be of any one else, it is not able to arrive in heaven <a name="fr_1500"></a><a href="#fn_1489"><font size="1">1</font></a> by that way. 8. Besides <i>this</i> happy <a name="fr_1501"></a><a href="#fn_1490"><font size="1">2</font></a> way, a soul of the liberal is able to arrive in heaven by means of the thirty-three ways. 9. For no one is it easier to arrive in heaven than <i>for</i> the liberal.</p>
