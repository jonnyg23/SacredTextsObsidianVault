 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Pahlavi Texts, Part III (SBE24)</i>, E.W. West, tr. [1885], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">CHAPTER LVII.</h3>  
 <p>1. The sage asked the spirit of wisdom (2) thus: 'Wherefore <i>is it</i> when the knowledge and sagacity of the spiritual and worldly <i>existences</i>, both united, are connected with thee?'</p>  
 <p>3. The spirit of wisdom answered (4) thus: 'For this reason, because, from the first, I, who am the innate wisdom, <i>apart</i> from the spiritual and worldly existences, have been with Aûha<i>r</i>ma<i>z</i><i>d</i>. 5. And</p>  
 <p><a name="page_99"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 99</font></a></p>  
 <p>the creator Aûha<i>r</i>ma<i>z</i><i>d</i> created (âfrî<i>d</i>ŏ) the angels of the spiritual and worldly creations, and all the other creatures and creations through the power and mightiness, the wisdom and sagacity of innate wisdom; and I produce <a name="fr_525"></a><a href="#fn_518"><font size="1">1</font></a> and he maintains and stimulates <i>them</i>. 6. And at the end of the renovation <i>of the universe</i> it is possible to cause the annihilation and destruction of Aharman and his miscreations more fully by the power of wisdom; (7) and Sôshâns <a name="fr_526"></a><a href="#fn_519"><font size="1">2</font></a>, with Kaî-Khûsrôî <a name="fr_527"></a><a href="#fn_519"><font size="1">2</font></a>, and those who the resurrection and future existence are able to act more fully, by means of the power and help of wisdom.</p>  
 <p>8. 'The knowledge and sagacity of the worldly <i>existence</i>, the learning and teaching in <a name="fr_528"></a><a href="#fn_520"><font size="1">3</font></a> every profession, and all advancement of temporal <i>beings</i> <a name="fr_529"></a><a href="#fn_521"><font size="1">4</font></a> are through wisdom: 9. The souls of the righteous, in escaping from hell <a name="fr_530"></a><a href="#fn_522"><font size="1">5</font></a> and coming <a name="fr_531"></a><a href="#fn_523"><font size="1">6</font></a> to heaven and the supreme heaven (garô<i>d</i>mân), arrive much better by means of the power and protection of wisdom. 10. And it is possible to seek the good living, pleasure, good repute, and every happiness of people in the worldly <i>existence</i>, through the power of wisdom.</p>  
 <p>11. 'And the maintenance of the seeds of men</p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p>

 <p><a name="page_100"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 100</font></a></p>  
 <p>and beasts <i>of burden</i>, oxen and sheep, and also every other creature and creation of Aûha<i>r</i>ma<i>z</i><i>d</i>, the lord, the seating <a name="fr_532"></a><a href="#fn_524"><font size="1">1</font></a> <i>of them</i> in the womb, and making manifest what is their food in the womb, so that they shall not die from hunger and thirst, and the allotment and maturing of the limbs are effected <a name="fr_533"></a><a href="#fn_525"><font size="1">2</font></a> more fully by means of the durability (dôrângarîh) and great potency which are in the force <a name="fr_534"></a><a href="#fn_526"><font size="1">3</font></a> of wisdom.</p>  
 <p>12. 'The arrangement of the earth and the mingling of the water in the earth, the growth and increase of plants, colour of various kinds, and the scent, taste, and pleasantness of various things are allotted and produced more fully through wisdom. 13. And the arrangement of Albûr<i>z</i> <a name="fr_535"></a><a href="#fn_527"><font size="1">4</font></a> around the world, the manifestation of the earth of the seven regions <a name="fr_536"></a><a href="#fn_528"><font size="1">5</font></a> and the sky above the mountain of Albûr<i>z</i>, the motion of the sun and moon and twelve constellations <a name="fr_537"></a><a href="#fn_529"><font size="1">6</font></a>, the six times of the season festivals (gâsanbâr) <a name="fr_538"></a><a href="#fn_530"><font size="1">7</font></a>. the five times <i>devoted</i> to the guardian spirits (fravar<i>d</i>îkân) <a name="fr_539"></a><a href="#fn_531"><font size="1">8</font></a>, the heaven which is in the place of good thoughts, the place of good words, the place of good deeds, and the perfect supreme</p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p>
 </p>

 <p>
 </p>  
 <p><a name="page_101"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 101</font></a></p>  
 <p>heaven (garô<i>d</i>mân) of all gloriousness <a name="fr_540"></a><a href="#fn_532"><font size="1">1</font></a>, the path of the spirits and worldly <i>existences</i>, and the <i>K</i>indvar bridge <a name="fr_541"></a><a href="#fn_533"><font size="1">2</font></a> are produced and allotted through the power of wisdom.</p>  
 <p>14. 'The watery-looking <a name="fr_542"></a><a href="#fn_534"><font size="1">3</font></a> cloud's seizing water from the sea, advancing in the atmosphere, and gradually breaking away <a name="fr_543"></a><a href="#fn_535"><font size="1">4</font></a>, drop by drop, to the earth, and Aûha<i>r</i>ma<i>z</i><i>d</i>'s <a name="fr_544"></a><a href="#fn_536"><font size="1">5</font></a> creatures thoroughly understanding the nature of heaven and hell, the compassion of Aûha<i>r</i>ma<i>z</i><i>d</i>, the archangels, and other angels as regards their own creatures, and the devastation and destructiveness of Aharman and the demons as regards the creatures of Aûha<i>r</i>ma<i>z</i><i>d</i> it is possible to comprehend through the more complete power <a name="fr_545"></a><a href="#fn_537"><font size="1">6</font></a> of wisdom. 15. And the good religion of the Ma<i>z</i><i>d</i>a-worshippers, the sayings and teaching of the spirits <a name="fr_546"></a><a href="#fn_538"><font size="1">7</font></a>, and the demons demolishing the worldly body <i>and</i> making <i>it</i> imperceptible by the sight of men are apprehended <a name="fr_547"></a><a href="#fn_539"><font size="1">8</font></a> more fully by means of the most perfect means of wisdom. 16. And even the struggle and warfare of Irân with foreigners (an-aîrân), and the smiting of Aharman and the demons it is possible to effect through the power of wisdom.</p>

 <p>17. 'To occasion the sun's inspection of the hidden water also, below the earth, it is expedient to convey <i>it</i> for tillage and cultivation, and the advantage, comfort, and enjoyment of men and beasts of burden, oxen and sheep, through the power of wisdom. 18. The thorough understanding of the pain and sickness of men and beasts <i>of burden</i>, oxen, sheep, and other animals, and the bringing of medicine and remedies, health of body and comfort unto them are much more possible to effect <a name="fr_548"></a><a href="#fn_540"><font size="1">1</font></a> by means of the power of wisdom.</p>  
 <p>19. 'And <i>as to</i> every man whose participation in wisdom is. much, his share of heaven is then much more. 20. Even <i>as to</i> Vi<i>s</i>tâsp <a name="fr_549"></a><a href="#fn_541"><font size="1">2</font></a>, Zaratû<i>s</i>t <a name="fr_550"></a><a href="#fn_542"><font size="1">3</font></a>, Gâyômar<i>d</i> <a name="fr_551"></a><a href="#fn_543"><font size="1">4</font></a>, and those others whose share of heaven was much the more <a name="fr_552"></a><a href="#fn_544"><font size="1">5</font></a>, <i>it was</i> on account of the much coming of wisdom unto them. 21. And <i>as to</i> Yim, Frê<i>d</i>ûn, Kâî-Ûs <a name="fr_553"></a><a href="#fn_545"><font size="1">6</font></a>, and those other rulers who obtained splendour (var<i>g</i>ŏ) and mightiness (tagakîh) <a name="fr_554"></a><a href="#fn_546"><font size="1">7</font></a> from the sacred beings just as the participation of Vi<i>s</i>tâsp and other rulers in the religion occurred <a name="fr_555"></a><a href="#fn_547"><font size="1">8</font></a>and their not attaining to the religion, and also as to the <i>times</i> when they have become ungrateful unto their own lord <a name="fr_556"></a><a href="#fn_548"><font size="1">9</font></a>, <i>it was</i> on account of the little coming of wisdom unto them.</p>  
 <p>22. 'And Aharman, also, and the demons deceive that man more, and lead <i>him</i> to hell, who is poorer</p>

 <p><a name="page_103"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 103</font></a></p>  
 <p>of wisdom and unsteadier in disposition. 23. And <i>it</i> is manifest, that, unto <i>him</i> who is virtuous in disposition, habit, and demeanour <a name="fr_557"></a><a href="#fn_549"><font size="1">1</font></a>, praise is then <i>due</i>, owing to his maintenance of wisdom. 24. For <i>it</i> is declared, that Aharman shouted to Zaratû<i>s</i>t thus <a name="fr_558"></a><a href="#fn_550"><font size="1">2</font></a>: "If thou desist from this good religion of the Ma<i>z</i><i>d</i>a-worshippers, then I <i>will</i> give thee a thousand years dominion of the worldly <i>existence</i>, (25) as <i>was</i> given to the Va<i>d</i>akân <a name="fr_559"></a><a href="#fn_551"><font size="1">3</font></a> monarch Dahâk <a name="fr_560"></a><a href="#fn_552"><font size="1">4</font></a>." 26. On account of complete wisdom, the virtuous disposition and demeanour of Zaratû<i>s</i>t not <i>having</i> hearkened and not being deluded, he did not become deceived and longing through that temptation of the accursed evil <i>one</i>, the wicked <a name="fr_561"></a><a href="#fn_553"><font size="1">5</font></a>. 27. And he spoke to Aharman (28) thus: "I <i>will</i> shatter and cause to run (dûkânam) <a name="fr_562"></a><a href="#fn_554"><font size="1">6</font></a>, and <i>will</i> make downcast (nigûîsâr) for thee <a name="fr_563"></a><a href="#fn_555"><font size="1">7</font></a>, the bodies of your demons and fiends, wizards and witches, through the Hôm <a name="fr_564"></a><a href="#fn_556"><font size="1">8</font></a> <i>and</i> sacred twigs <a name="fr_565"></a><a href="#fn_557"><font size="1">9</font></a>, and the good, true</p>

 </p>  
 <p>
 </p>

 </p>  
 <p><a name="page_104"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 104</font></a></p>  
 <p>religion which the creator Aûha<i>r</i>ma<i>z</i><i>d</i> <i>has</i> taught to me." 29. Aharman, when those words <i>were</i> heard by him, became confounded and stupefied, and rushed to hell, and remained confounded a long time.</p>  
 <p>30. 'This, too, is declared, that Aûha<i>r</i>ma<i>z</i><i>d</i>, when Aharman, by agreement <a name="fr_566"></a><a href="#fn_558"><font size="1">1</font></a>, had further operated <a name="fr_567"></a><a href="#fn_559"><font size="1">2</font></a> with his (Aûha<i>r</i>ma<i>z</i><i>d</i>'s) creatures and creation of every kind, afterwards formed an assembly with the angels and archangels of every kind <a name="fr_568"></a><a href="#fn_560"><font size="1">3</font></a>, and the welfare (â<i>v</i>â<i>d</i>îh) due to his own wisdom <i>was</i> mentioned and recounted by him.</p>  
 <p>31. 'This, too, is declared, that <i>for</i> the nine thousand years of renovation <a name="fr_569"></a><a href="#fn_561"><font size="1">4</font></a>, until the resurrection and future existence, wisdom maintains and stimulates the creatures and creation of every kind.</p>  
 <p>32. 'And this, too, is declared, that, <i>as to</i> him who is an ignorant and bad-tempered man, when he attains even to much eminence, opulence, and authority, even then he is not fit to elevate into that welfare and authority.'</p>
