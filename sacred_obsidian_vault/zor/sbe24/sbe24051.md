 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Pahlavi Texts, Part III (SBE24)</i>, E.W. West, tr. [1885], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">CHAPTER XLV.</h3>  
 <p>1. The sage asked the spirit of wisdom (2) thus: 'By what does Aharman most deceive and lead people to hell? 3. And from what is his pleasure most? 4. Where is the place he has a foundation?</p>  
 <p><a name="page_88"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 88</font></a></p>  
 <p><span class="contnote"><font size="-2" color="GREEN">[paragraph continues]</font></span> 5. Where, also, is his coming, <i>together</i> with the demons, most? 6. And from what is his food?'</p>  
 <p>7. The spirit of wisdom answered (8) thus: Aharman deceives people most by prosperity and adversity <a name="fr_470"></a><a href="#fn_465"><font size="1">1</font></a>, the fiend of apostasy, scepticism, and covetousness. 9. His pleasure, also, is most from the discord of men. 10. And his food is from the impenitence <a name="fr_471"></a><a href="#fn_466"><font size="1">2</font></a> and reticence of men. 11. He has a foundation in the malicious <a name="fr_472"></a><a href="#fn_467"><font size="1">3</font></a>. 12. And his coming and going are most with the wrathful.'</p>
