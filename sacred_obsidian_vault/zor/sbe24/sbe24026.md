 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Pahlavi Texts, Part III (SBE24)</i>, E.W. West, tr. [1885], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">CHAPTER XX.</h3>  
 <p>1. The sage asked the spirit of wisdom (2) thus: '<i>For</i> kings which is the one thing more advantageous, and which the more injurious?'</p>  
 <p>3. The spirit of wisdom answered (4) thus: 'For kings conversation with the wise <i>and</i> good is the</p>  
 <p><a name="page_51"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 51</font></a></p>  
 <p>one thing more advantageous, (5) and speaking <i>and</i> conversation with slanderers <i>and</i> double-dealers are the more injurious for them.'</p>
