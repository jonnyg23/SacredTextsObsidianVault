 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Pahlavi Texts, Part III (SBE24)</i>, E.W. West, tr. [1885], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">CHAPTER XXXVII.</h3>  
 <p>1. The sage asked the spirit of wisdom (2) thus: 'Through how many ways and motives of good works do people arrive most at heaven?'</p>  
 <p>3. The spirit of wisdom answered (4) thus: 'The first good work is liberality <a name="fr_392"></a><a href="#fn_387"><font size="1">5</font></a>. 5. The second, truth. 6. The third, thankfulness. 7. The fourth, contentment. 8. The fifth, wanting to produce welfare for the good, and becoming a friend to every one. 9. The sixth, being without doubt as to this, that the</p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p><a name="page_74"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 74</font></a></p>  
 <p>sky and earth and every benefit of the worldly and spiritual <i>existences</i> are owing to the creator Aûha<i>r</i>ma<i>z</i><i>d</i>. 10. The seventh, <i>being so</i> as to the unquestionableness of this <a name="fr_393"></a><a href="#fn_388"><font size="1">1</font></a>, that all misery and affliction are owing to Aharman the wicked, who is accursed. 11. The eighth, freedom from doubt as to the resurrection and future existence. 12. The ninth, who for love of the soul effects <a name="fr_394"></a><a href="#fn_389"><font size="1">2</font></a> a next-of-kin marriage. 13. The tenth, who arranges adoption <a name="fr_395"></a><a href="#fn_390"><font size="1">3</font></a>. 14. The eleventh, who practises regular industry. 15. The twelfth, who is without doubt in this pure, good religion of the Ma<i>z</i><i>d</i>a-worshippers. 16. The thirteenth, who is kindly regardful as to the ability and means of every one. 17. The fourteenth, who perceives <a name="fr_396"></a><a href="#fn_391"><font size="1">4</font></a> the kind regard of the good, and becomes himself, also, kindly regardful as to the goodness which <i>one</i> wants among the good. 18. The fifteenth, who seeks the affection of the good. 19. The sixteenth, who keeps malice and uncharitableness far from <i>his</i> mind. 20. The seventeenth, who bears no improper envy. 21. The eighteenth, who forms no desire of lust. 22. The nineteenth, who produces no discord with any one. 23. The twentieth, who brings no distress into the affairs Of a departed and unassisted <i>one</i> (avi<i>g</i>î<i>d</i>) <a name="fr_397"></a><a href="#fn_392"><font size="1">5</font></a>. 24. The twenty-first, who</p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p><a name="page_75"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 75</font></a></p>  
 <p>lets <a name="fr_398"></a><a href="#fn_393"><font size="1">1</font></a> no wrath into <i>his</i> body. 25. The twenty-second, who commits no sin on account of disgrace <a name="fr_399"></a><a href="#fn_394"><font size="1">2</font></a>. 26. The twenty-third, who forms no desire of lethargy on account of laziness. 27. The twenty-fourth, who is without doubt as to the sacred beings. 28. The twenty-fifth, who is without doubt as to the existence of heaven and hell, and the account which is <i>to be rendered</i> by the soul, the glory which is in heaven, and the misery which is in hell. 29. The twenty-sixth, who abstains <a name="fr_400"></a><a href="#fn_395"><font size="1">3</font></a> from slander and envious looks. 30. The twenty-seventh, who causes the happiness of himself, and gives happy advancement also to others. 31. The twenty-eighth, who becomes the help <a name="fr_401"></a><a href="#fn_396"><font size="1">4</font></a> of the good, and accuser of the bad. 32. The twenty-ninth, who restrains himself from deceit and evil (dû<i>s</i>îh) <a name="fr_402"></a><a href="#fn_397"><font size="1">5</font></a>. 33. The thirtieth, who does not speak false and untrue <a name="fr_403"></a><a href="#fn_398"><font size="1">6</font></a>. 34. The thirty-first, who restrains himself firmly from promise-breaking. 35. The thirty-second, who, for the sake of seeking his own benefit and happiness, causes the abstinence of others from evil. 36. <i>And</i> the thirty-third, who provides lodging accommodation for the sick and secluded <a name="fr_404"></a><a href="#fn_399"><font size="1">7</font></a> and traders.'</p>
