 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Pahlavi Texts, Part III (SBE24)</i>, E.W. West, tr. [1885], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">CHAPTER XCI.</h3>  
 <p>1. The ninety-first subject is this, that is, <i>in</i> what mode is it necessary to wash everything that becomes polluted by dead matter?</p>  
 <p>2. Gold <i>one is</i> to wash over once with <i>ceremonial</i> ablution <a name="fr_1556"></a><a href="#fn_1545"><font size="1">3</font></a> to make <i>it</i> dry once with dust, and to wash <i>it</i> over once with water. 3. Silver (nuqrah) <i>one is to do</i> twice; copper, tin, lead, and brass <i>articles</i> three times; steel four times; stone <i>articles</i> six times; turquoise, ruby (yâqût), amber, carbuncle, cornelian (haqîq), and, like these, whatever is from a mine (mahdan) are all to be washed six times in the manner which I have stated. 4. Afterwards they are clean <i>in</i> that manner, when every single time <i>one</i> washes<i></i> them over with <i>ceremonial</i> ablution, makes</p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p><a name="page_355"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 355</font></a></p>  
 <p><i>them</i> dry with dust, and washes <i>it</i> off <a name="fr_1557"></a><a href="#fn_1546"><font size="1">1</font></a>; and just like this <i>on</i> the occasion of the other timesup to three times, or four, or sixas far as whatever is ordered.</p>  
 <p>5. For pearls two modes <a name="fr_1558"></a><a href="#fn_1547"><font size="1">2</font></a> are ordered, but the conclusion is this, that they <i>should</i> wash <i>them</i> six times, just like stone <i>articles</i>. 6. The whole of wooden and earthen <i>ware</i> it is requisite to throw <i>away</i>. 7. All clothing of the body it is requisite to wash six times <i>in</i> the manner that I have stated, <i>and</i>, after that, to put <i>them</i> in a place where the sun and moon must <i>shine</i> on them <i>for</i> six months <a name="fr_1559"></a><a href="#fn_1548"><font size="1">3</font></a>; after that they are fit for a menstruous woman.</p>
