 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Pahlavi Texts, Part III (SBE24)</i>, E.W. West, tr. [1885], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">CHAPTER XIII.</h3>  
 <p>1. The thirteenth subject is this, that it is necessary to maintain the souls of fathers, mothers, and relations properly. 2. And, when any day of theirs <a name="fr_1173"></a><a href="#fn_1164"><font size="1">4</font></a> occurs, it is necessary to make an endeavour, so that they <i>may</i> accomplish the ceremonial (yazi<i>s</i>n), the sacred feast (myazd), <i>the consecration of</i> the sacred cakes (drôn), and the benedictions (âfrîngân).</p>  
 <p>3. For it is declared in revelation, that, every time that any day of theirs occurs, they will bring with</p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p><a name="page_274"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 274</font></a></p>  
 <p>themselves 9999 guardian spirits of the righteous, like that <i>case</i> when any one goes home himself, and brings people (qaûm) in hospitality. 4. And, when they utter <a name="fr_1174"></a><a href="#fn_1165"><font size="1">1</font></a> <i>the consecration of</i> the sacred cakes and sacred feast and the benedictions, those people become joyful and utter blessings on that house and master of the house, and on the house-mistress and any persons who are in that house.</p>  
 <p>5. But if they do not celebrate the sacred feast, <i>the consecration of</i> the sacred cakes, the ceremonial, and the benedictions, <i>the spirits</i> will remain for them <i>in</i> that place from dawn as long as the period of a day, and are maintaining a hope that 'perhaps they <i>will</i> have us <i>in</i> remembrance.' 6. Then, if they do not bring them <a name="fr_1175"></a><a href="#fn_1166"><font size="1">2</font></a> <i>into</i> remembrance, the souls turn upwards from that place, go very quickly on high, and will say, 'O creator Hôrmazd! they do not know that we are such as we are <a name="fr_1176"></a><a href="#fn_1167"><font size="1">3</font></a>, <i>and that</i> it is necessary for them <a name="fr_1177"></a><a href="#fn_1168"><font size="1">4</font></a> to come into this world, and in this world they will not give any one acquittal. 7. For them there is need of the good works in <i>consecrating</i> the sacred cakes and <i>celebrating</i> the sacred feast and benedictions; there is no need of them for such as we. 8. Yet (va lîkin), if they would have maintained a place <i>for</i> <a name="fr_1178"></a><a href="#fn_1169"><font size="1">5</font></a> the duty of those days, we should have turned away from them misfortunes of various kinds; but, as they have not maintained <a name="fr_1179"></a><a href="#fn_1170"><font size="1">6</font></a> observance of us <i>in</i> the day's duty, we are not able to come in friendship <i>to</i> this house.'</p>
