 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Pahlavi Texts, Part III (SBE24)</i>, E.W. West, tr. [1885], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">CHAPTER LXXIV.</h3>  
 <p>1. The seventy-fourth subject is this, that <i>at</i> dawn, when they rise up from sleep, it is first necessary to throw something <a name="fr_1477"></a><a href="#fn_1466"><font size="1">5</font></a> on the hands, that is the</p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p><a name="page_338"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 338</font></a></p>  
 <p>hand-cleansing <a name="fr_1478"></a><a href="#fn_1467"><font size="1">1</font></a>. 2. Afterwards, <i>they are</i> to wash the hands quite clean with water, <i>in</i> such manner that <i>they are</i> to wash the hands three times from the forearm (sâhid) to the end of the hand; and the face is washed from behind the ears to below the chin <i>and</i> up to the crown of the head; and <i>one</i> washes the feet three times thoroughly, as far as the leg (sâq); then <i>one</i> recites the K<i>e</i>m-nâ-mazdâ <a name="fr_1479"></a><a href="#fn_1468"><font size="1">2</font></a>.</p>  
 <p>3. If it be a place where <i>there</i> is no water, and the risk be that <i>the time for</i> the Nyâyi<i>s</i> <a name="fr_1480"></a><a href="#fn_1469"><font size="1">3</font></a> <i>should</i> pass by, it is requisite to cleanse <a name="fr_1481"></a><a href="#fn_1470"><font size="1">4</font></a> the hands three times <i>with</i> dust, and to perform the Nyâyi<i>s</i>. 4. Afterwards, when <i>one</i> arrives at water, <i>he is</i> to wash the hands and face a second time, and to accomplish the Nyâyi<i>s</i> <a name="fr_1482"></a><a href="#fn_1471"><font size="1">5</font></a>.</p>  
 <p>5. Before <i>the time</i> when <i>one</i> throws something on the hands it is not proper to wash the hands and face, <i>and</i> it is a Tanâvar sin; it is also not possible to work at anything whatever <i>with</i> the hands and face not washed.</p>
