 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Pahlavi Texts, Part III (SBE24)</i>, E.W. West, tr. [1885], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">CHAPTER LXVII.</h3>  
 <p>1. The sixty-seventh subject is this, that it is necessary for women to practise great abstinence from committing adultery. 2. For it is declared in revelation, <i>as to</i> every woman who <i>has</i> lain with a strange man, thus: 'What is it necessary to call her, and why is the explanation <a name="fr_1434"></a><a href="#fn_1423"><font size="1">1</font></a> that she is of one nature with all wizards and sinners?'</p>  
 <p>3. And in the commentary of the Vendîdâ<i>d</i> <a name="fr_1435"></a><a href="#fn_1424"><font size="1">2</font></a> it says '"every woman who consorts with two strange men is the first <i>down</i> upon me, who am Hôrmazd. 4. For if she takes a look into a river of water it will make <i>it</i> diminish, if she takes a look <a name="fr_1436"></a><a href="#fn_1425"><font size="1">3</font></a> at a tree <i>or</i> shrub the fruit of the trees becomes scanty, and if she speaks a word with a righteous man it will make the glory <a name="fr_1437"></a><a href="#fn_1426"><font size="1">4</font></a> of the man diminish." 5. Zaratu<i>s</i>t enquired of Hôrmazd, "What occurs on <a name="fr_1438"></a><a href="#fn_1427"><font size="1">5</font></a> her account?" 6. The creator Hôrmazd spoke thus: "It is necessary to kill her sooner than a biting serpent and similar creatures and wild beasts, because she is more harmful to my creatures."'</p>  
 <p>7. Therefore, since she is like this, it is necessary for women to keep themselves <i>with</i> great effort, so that they <i>may</i> not become unlawful unto their own</p>
