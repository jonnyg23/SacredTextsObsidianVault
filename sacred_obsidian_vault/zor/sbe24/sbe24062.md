 <p><a name="page_98"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 98</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">CHAPTER LVI.</h3>  
 <p>1. The sage asked the spirit of wisdom (2) thus: 'Wherefore are these mountains and rivers <a name="fr_522"></a><a href="#fn_515"><font size="1">1</font></a> made, which are in the world?'</p>  
 <p>3. The spirit of wisdom answered (4) thus: 'Of these mountains, which are in the world, there are <i>some</i> which are moderators of the wind, and there are <i>some</i> which are <a name="fr_523"></a><a href="#fn_516"><font size="1">2</font></a> warders off; (5) there are <i>some</i> which are the place and vent, the resting-place and support of the rainy cloud; (6) and there are <i>some</i> which are smiters of Aharman and the demons, and maintainers and vivifiers of the creatures and creation of Aûha<i>r</i>ma<i>z</i><i>d</i>, the lord.</p>  
 <p>7. 'And these rivers, which are in the world, the creator Aûha<i>r</i>ma<i>z</i><i>d</i> has formed, from the borders of Albûr<i>z</i> <a name="fr_524"></a><a href="#fn_517"><font size="1">3</font></a>, for providing the protection and <i>for</i> the vivification of his own creatures and creation.'</p>
