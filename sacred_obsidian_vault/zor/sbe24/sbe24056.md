 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Pahlavi Texts, Part III (SBE24)</i>, E.W. West, tr. [1885], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">CHAPTER L.</h3>  
 <p>1. The sage asked the spirit of wisdom (2) thus: 'Which is that opulent <i>person</i> who is to be considered as fortunate, and which is that <i>one</i> who is <i>to be considered</i> as evil-conditioned?'</p>  
 <p>3. The spirit of wisdom answered (4) thus: 'That <i>one</i> who has produced opulence by proper exertion is to be considered as fortunate; and that <i>one</i> who has produced <i>it</i> by dishonesty, as evil-conditioned.'</p>
