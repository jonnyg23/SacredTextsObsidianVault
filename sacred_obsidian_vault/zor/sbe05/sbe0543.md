 <p><a name="page_149"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 149</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">CHAPTER XXXIV <a name="fr_882"></a><a href="#fn_876"><font size="1">1</font></a>.</h3>  
 <p>0. On the reckoning of the years <a name="fr_883"></a><a href="#fn_877"><font size="1">2</font></a>.</p>  
 <p>1. Time was <i>for</i> twelve thousand years; <i>and</i> it says in revelation, that three thousand years was the duration of the spiritual <i>state</i>, where the creatures were unthinking, unmoving, <i>and</i> intangible <a name="fr_884"></a><a href="#fn_878"><font size="1">3</font></a>; <i>and</i> three thousand years <a name="fr_885"></a><a href="#fn_879"><font size="1">4</font></a> was the duration of Gâyômar<i>d</i>, with the ox, in the world. 2. As <i>this</i> was six thousand years series of millennium reigns <a name="fr_886"></a><a href="#fn_880"><font size="1">5</font></a> of Cancer, Leo, and Virgo had elapsed, because it was six thousand years when the millennium reign came to Libra, the adversary rushed in, and Gâyômar<i>d</i> lived thirty years in tribulation <a name="fr_887"></a><a href="#fn_881"><font size="1">6</font></a>. After the thirty years <a name="fr_888"></a><a href="#fn_882"><font size="1">7</font></a> Mâshya <i>and</i> Mashyôî grew up; it was fifty years while they were not wife <i>and</i> husband <a name="fr_889"></a><a href="#fn_883"><font size="1">8</font></a>, <i>and</i> they were ninety-three years together <i>as</i> wife <i>and</i> husband till the <i>time</i> when Hôshyang <a name="fr_890"></a><a href="#fn_884"><font size="1">9</font></a> came.</p>  
 <p>4. Hôshyang <i>was</i> forty years <a name="fr_891"></a><a href="#fn_885"><font size="1">10</font></a>, Takhmôrup <a name="fr_892"></a><a href="#fn_886"><font size="1">11</font></a> thirty years, Yim till <i>his</i> glory <a name="fr_893"></a><a href="#fn_887"><font size="1">12</font></a> departed six hundred and</p>

 <p>sixteen years and six months, <i>and</i> after that he was a hundred years in concealment. 5. Then the millennium <a name="fr_894"></a><a href="#fn_888"><font size="1">1</font></a> reign came to Scorpio, and Dahâk <a name="fr_895"></a><a href="#fn_889"><font size="1">2</font></a> ruled a thousand years. 6. After the millennium reign came to Sagittarius, Frê<i>d</i>ûn <a name="fr_896"></a><a href="#fn_890"><font size="1">3</font></a> <i>reigned</i> five hundred years; in the same five hundred years of Frê<i>d</i>ûn <i>were</i> the twelve years of Aîrî<i>k</i>; Mânû<i>s</i><i>k</i>îhar <a name="fr_897"></a><a href="#fn_891"><font size="1">4</font></a> <i>was</i> a hundred and twenty years, <i>and</i> in the same reign of Mânû<i>s</i><i>k</i>îhar, when he was in the mountain fastness (dûshkhvâr-gar) <a name="fr_898"></a><a href="#fn_892"><font size="1">5</font></a>, <i>were</i> the twelve years of Frâsîyâ<i>v</i>; Zôb <a name="fr_899"></a><a href="#fn_893"><font size="1">6</font></a> the Tûhmâspian <i>was</i> five years.</p>  
 <p>7. Kaî-Kabâ<i>d</i> <a name="fr_900"></a><a href="#fn_894"><font size="1">7</font></a> <i>was</i> fifteen years; Kaî-Kâûs, till he went to the sky, seventy-five years, <i>and</i> seventy-five years after that, altogether a hundred and fifty years; Kai-Khûsrôv sixty years; Kaî-Lôrâsp <a name="fr_901"></a><a href="#fn_895"><font size="1">8</font></a> a hundred and twenty years; Kaî-Vi<i>s</i>tâsp, till the coming of the religion, thirty years <a name="fr_902"></a><a href="#fn_896"><font size="1">9</font></a>, altogether a hundred and twenty years.</p>  
 <p>8. Vohûman <a name="fr_903"></a><a href="#fn_897"><font size="1">10</font></a> <i>son</i> of Spend-dâ<i>d</i> a hundred and</p>

 <p>9. The A<i>s</i>kânians bore the title in an uninterrupted (a-arûbâk) sovereignty two hundred and eighty-four years <a name="fr_906"></a><a href="#fn_900"><font size="1">3</font></a>, Ardashîr son of Pâpak <i>and</i> the number of the Sâsânians four hundred and sixty years <a name="fr_907"></a><a href="#fn_901"><font size="1">4</font></a>, and then it went to the Arabs.</p>

 <p><a name="fn_897"></a><a href="sbe0543.htm#fr_903">150:10</a> See Chap.  <p><a name="fn_898"></a><a href="sbe0543.htm#fr_904">151:1</a> A surname of Hûmâî.</p>
 <p><a name="fn_899"></a><a href="sbe0543.htm#fr_905">151:2</a> Sikandar-i Arûmâk, that is, Alexander the Roman (of the eastern or Greek empire), as Pahlavi writers assume.</p>
 <p><a name="fn_900"></a><a href="sbe0543.htm#fr_906">151:3</a> This period is nearly two centuries too short.</p>
 <p><a name="fn_901"></a><a href="sbe0543.htm#fr_907">151:4</a> The actual period of Sasanian rule was 425 years (A.D. 226-651). According to the figures given in the text, the tenth millennium, ruled by Capricornus, must have terminated in the fourth year of the last king, Yazdakar<i>d</i>. This agrees substantially with the Bahman Ya<i>s</i>t, which makes the millennium of Zaratû<i>s</i>t expire some time after the reign of Khûsrô Nôshirvân; probably in the time of Khûsrô Parvîz, or some forty years earlier than the fourth year of Yazdakar<i>d</i>. According to the text we must now be near the end of the first quarter of the twelfth and last millennium.</p>
