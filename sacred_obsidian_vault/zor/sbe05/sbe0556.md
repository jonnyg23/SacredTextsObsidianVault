Haug's Essays, for Essays on the Sacred Language, Writings, and Religion of the Parsis, by Martin Haug, 2nd edition. Huz. for Huzvâri<i>s</i>. Pahl. for Pahlavi. Pâz. for Pâzand. Pers. for Persian.
