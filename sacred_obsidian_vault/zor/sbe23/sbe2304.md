 <p>To Ameretâ<i>t</i> <a name="fr_22"></a><a href="#fn_21"><font size="1">5</font></a>, the master; to fatness and flocks; to the plenty of corn; and to the powerful Gaokerena <a name="fr_23"></a><a href="#fn_22"><font size="1">6</font></a>, made by Mazda.</p>
