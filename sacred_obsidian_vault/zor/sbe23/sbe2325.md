 <p><a name="page_310"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 310</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">XX. VANA<i>N</i><i>T</i> YA<i>S</i>T.</h3>  
 <p><font size="-1">This Ya<i>s</i>t ought to follow immediately after the Tîr Ya<i>s</i>t, as it is derived from the same Sîrôzah formula; the one in which Ti<i>s</i>trya is invoked along with Vana<i>n</i><i>t</i> and Haptôiri<i>n</i>ga (Sîrôzah, § 13). It is a mere supplement to that Ya<i>s</i>t. On Vana<i>n</i><i>t</i>, see <a href="sbe2313.htm#page_97">p. 97</a>, note <a href="sbe2313.htm#fn_481">6</a>.</font></p>  
 <p align="center">______________________</p>  
 <p><font size="-1">0. May Ahura Mazda be rejoiced! . . . .</font></p>  
 <p>Ashem Vohû: Holiness is the best of all good . . .</p>  
 <p>I confess myself a worshipper of Mazda, a follower of Zarathu<i>s</i>tra, one who hates the Daêvas and obeys the laws of Ahura;</p>  
 <p>For sacrifice, prayer, propitiation, and glorification unto [Hâvani], the holy and master of holiness . . . .</p>  
 <p>Unto the star Vana<i>n</i><i>t</i>, made by Mazda,</p>  
 <p>Be propitiation, with sacrifice, prayer, propitiation, and glorification.</p>  
 <p><font size="-1">Yathâ ahû vairyô: The will of the Lord is the law of holiness . . . .</font></p>  
 <p>1. We sacrifice unto the star Vana<i>n</i><i>t</i>, made by Mazda, the holy and master of holiness.</p>  
 <p>I will sacrifice unto Vana<i>n</i><i>t</i>, strong, invoked by his own name <a name="fr_1388"></a><a href="#fn_1322"><font size="1">1</font></a>, healing, in order to withstand the accursed and most foul Khrafstras <a name="fr_1389"></a><a href="#fn_1323"><font size="1">2</font></a> of the most abominable Angra Mainyu.</p>  
 <p><font size="-1">2. Yathâ ahû vairyô: The will of the Lord is the law of holiness . . . .</font></p>  
 <p>I bless the sacrifice and prayer, and the strength and vigour of the star Vana<i>n</i><i>t</i>, made by Mazda.</p>  
 <p><font size="-1">Ashem Vohû: Holiness is the best of all good . . . .</font></p>  
 <p>[Give] unto that man <a name="fr_1390"></a><a href="#fn_1324"><font size="1">3</font></a> brightness and glory, . . . . give him the bright, all-happy, blissful abode of the holy Ones.</p>
