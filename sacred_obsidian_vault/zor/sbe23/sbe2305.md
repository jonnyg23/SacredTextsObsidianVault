 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Zend Avesta, Part II (SBE23)</i>, James Darmesteter, tr. [1882], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">SÎRÔZAH II.</h3>  <h4 align="center">1. Ormazd.</h4>  
 <p>We sacrifice unto the bright and glorious Ahura Mazda; we sacrifice unto the Amesha-Spe<i>n</i>tas, the all-ruling, the all-beneficent.</p>  <h4 align="center">2. Bahman.</h4>  
 <p>We sacrifice unto Vohu-Manô, the Amesha-Spe<i>n</i>ta; we sacrifice unto Peace, whose breath is friendly, and who is more powerful to destroy than all other creatures. We sacrifice unto the heavenly Wisdom, made by Mazda; we sacrifice unto the Wisdom acquired through the ear, made by Mazda.</p>  <h4 align="center">3. Ardibehe<i>s</i>t.</h4>  
 <p>We sacrifice unto Asha-Vahi<i>s</i>ta, the fairest, the Amesha-Spe<i>n</i>ta; we sacrifice unto the much-desired Airyaman; we sacrifice unto the instrument made by Mazda; we sacrifice unto the good Saoka, with eyes of love, made by Mazda and holy.</p>  
 <p><a name="page_14"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 14</font></a></p>  <h4 align="center">4. Shahrêvar.</h4>  
 <p>We sacrifice unto Khshathra-Vairya, the Amesha-Spe<i>n</i>ta; we sacrifice unto the metals; we sacrifice unto Mercy and Charity.</p>  <h4 align="center">5. Sapendârmad.</h4>  
 <p>We sacrifice unto the good Spe<i>n</i>ta Ârmaiti; we sacrifice unto the good Râta, with eyes of love, made by Mazda and holy.</p>  <h4 align="center">6. Khordâd.</h4>  
 <p>We sacrifice unto Haurvatâ<i>t</i>, the Amesha-Spe<i>n</i>ta; we sacrifice unto the prosperity of the seasons. We sacrifice unto the years, the holy and masters of holiness.</p>  <h4 align="center">7. Murdâd.</h4>  
 <p>We sacrifice unto Ameretâ<i>t</i>, the Amesha-Spe<i>n</i>ta; we sacrifice unto fatness and flocks; we sacrifice unto the plenty of corn; we sacrifice unto the powerful Gaokerena, made by Mazda.</p>  
 <p>(At the gâh Hâvan): We sacrifice unto Mithra, the lord of wide pastures; we sacrifice unto Râma <i>H</i><i>v</i>âstra.</p>  
 <p>(At the gâh Rapithwin): We sacrifice unto Asha-Vahi<i>s</i>ta and unto Âtar, the son of Ahura Mazda.</p>  
 <p>(At the gâh Uzîren): We sacrifice unto Apãm Napâ<i>t</i>, the swift-horsed, the tall and shining lord, the lord of the females; we sacrifice unto the water made by Mazda and holy.</p>  
 <p>(At the gâh Aiwisrûthrem): We sacrifice unto the good, powerful, beneficent Fravashis of the holy ones; we sacrifice unto the females that bring forth flocks of males; we sacrifice unto the thrift of the</p>  
 <p><a name="page_15"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 15</font></a></p>  
 <p>seasons; we sacrifice unto the well-shapen and tall-formed Strength; we sacrifice unto Verethraghna, made by Mazda; we sacrifice unto the crushing Ascendant.</p>  
 <p>(At the gâh Ushahin): We sacrifice unto the holy, tall-formed, fiend-smiting Sraosha, who makes the world grow, the holy and master of holiness; we sacrifice unto Rashnu Razi<i>s</i>ta; we sacrifice unto Ar<i>s</i>tâ<i>t</i>, who makes the world grow, who makes the world increase.</p>  <h4 align="center">8. Dai pa Adar.</h4>  
 <p>We sacrifice unto the Maker Ahura Mazda, the bright and glorious; we sacrifice unto the Amesha-Spe<i>n</i>tas, the all-ruling, the all-beneficent.</p>  <h4 align="center">9. Âdar.</h4>  
 <p>We sacrifice unto Âtar, the son of Ahura Mazda; we sacrifice unto the Glory, made by Mazda; we sacrifice unto the Weal, made by Mazda; we sacrifice unto the Glory of the Aryas, made by Mazda; we sacrifice unto the awful Glory of the Kavis, made by Mazda.</p>  
 <p>We sacrifice unto Âtar, the son of Ahura Mazda; we sacrifice unto king Husravah; we sacrifice unto the lake of Husravah; we sacrifice unto Mount Âsnava<i>n</i><i>t</i>, made by Mazda; we sacrifice unto Lake <i>K</i>aê<i>k</i>asta, made by Mazda; we sacrifice unto the awful Glory of the Kavis, made by Mazda.</p>  
 <p>We sacrifice unto Âtar, the son of Ahura Mazda; we sacrifice unto Mount Raêva<i>n</i><i>t</i>, made by Mazda; we sacrifice unto the awful Glory of the Kavis, made by Mazda.</p>  
 <p>We sacrifice unto Âtar, the son of Ahura Mazda; we sacrifice unto Âtar, the beneficent, the warrior.</p>  
 <p><a name="page_16"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 16</font></a></p>  
 <p><span class="contnote"><font size="-2" color="GREEN">[paragraph continues]</font></span> We sacrifice unto that God, who is a full source of glory. We sacrifice. unto that God, who is a full source of healing.</p>  
 <p>We sacrifice unto Âtar, the son of Ahura Mazda; we sacrifice unto all Fires; we sacrifice unto the God, Nairyô-Sangha, who dwells in the navel of kings.</p>  <h4 align="center">10. Âbân.</h4>  
 <p>We sacrifice unto the good Waters, made by Mazda and holy; we sacrifice unto the holy water-spring Ardvi Anâhita; we sacrifice unto all waters, made by Mazda and holy; we sacrifice unto all plants, made by Mazda and holy.</p>  <h4 align="center">11. Khorshêd.</h4>  
 <p>We sacrifice unto the bright, undying, shining, swift-horsed Sun.</p>  <h4 align="center">12. Mâh.</h4>  
 <p>We sacrifice unto the Moon that keeps in it the seed of the Bull. We sacrifice unto the Soul and Fravashi of the only-created Bull; we sacrifice unto the Soul and Fravashi of the Bull of many species.</p>  <h4 align="center">13. Tîr.</h4>  
 <p>We sacrifice unto Ti<i>s</i>trya, the bright and glorious star; we sacrifice unto the powerful Satavaêsa, made by Mazda, who pushes waters forward; we sacrifice unto all the Stars that have in them the seed of the waters; we sacrifice unto all the Stars that have in them the seed of the earth; we sacrifice unto all the Stars that have in them the seeds of the plants; we sacrifice unto the Star Vana<i>n</i><i>t</i>, made by Mazda; we sacrifice unto those stars that are seven in number, the Haptôiri<i>n</i>gas, made by Mazda, glorious and healing; in order to oppose the Yâtus and Pairikas.</p>  
 <p><a name="page_17"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 17</font></a></p>  <h4 align="center">14. Gô<i>s</i>.</h4>  
 <p>We sacrifice unto the soul of the bounteous Cow; we sacrifice unto the powerful Drvâspa, made by Mazda and holy.</p>  <h4 align="center">15. Dai pa Mihir.</h4>  
 <p>We sacrifice unto the Maker Ahura Mazda, the bright and glorious; we sacrifice unto the Amesha-Spe<i>n</i>tas, the all-ruling, the all-beneficent.</p>  <h4 align="center">16. Mihir.</h4>  
 <p>We sacrifice unto Mithra, the lord of wide pastures, who has a thousand ears and ten thousand eyes, a God invoked by his own name; we sacrifice unto Râma <i>H</i><i>v</i>âstra.</p>  <h4 align="center">17. Srôsh.</h4>  
 <p>We sacrifice unto the holy, tall-formed, fiend-smiting, world-increasing Sraosha, holy and master of holiness.</p>  <h4 align="center">18. Rashn.</h4>  
 <p>We sacrifice unto Rashnu Razi<i>s</i>ta; we sacrifice unto Ar<i>s</i>tâ<i>t</i>, who makes the world grow, who makes the world increase; we sacrifice unto the true-spoken speech that makes the world grow.</p>  <h4 align="center">19. Farvardîn.</h4>  
 <p>We sacrifice unto the good, strong, beneficent Fravashis of the holy ones.</p>  <h4 align="center">20. Bahrâm.</h4>  
 <p>We sacrifice unto the well-shapen, tall-formed Strength; we sacrifice unto Verethraghna, made by Ahura; we sacrifice unto the crushing Ascendant.</p>  
 <p><a name="page_18"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 18</font></a></p>  <h4 align="center">21. Râm.</h4>  
 <p>We sacrifice unto Râma <i>H</i><i>v</i>âstra; we sacrifice unto the holy Vayu; we sacrifice unto Vayu, who works highly and is more powerful to destroy than all other creatures. Unto that part of thee do we sacrifice, O Vayu, that belongs to Spe<i>n</i>ta-Mainyu. We sacrifice unto the sovereign Sky; we sacrifice unto the Boundless Time; we sacrifice unto the sovereign Time of the long Period.</p>  <h4 align="center">22. Bâd.</h4>  
 <p>We sacrifice unto the beneficent, bounteous Wind; we sacrifice unto the wind that blows below; we sacrifice unto the wind that blows above; we sacrifice unto the wind that blows before; we sacrifice unto the wind that blows behind. We sacrifice unto the manly Courage.</p>  <h4 align="center">23. Dai pa Dîn.</h4>  
 <p>We sacrifice unto the Maker Ahura Mazda, the bright and glorious; we sacrifice unto the Amesha-Spe<i>n</i>tas.</p>  <h4 align="center">24. Dîn.</h4>  
 <p>We sacrifice unto the most right <i>K</i>ista, made by Mazda and holy; we sacrifice unto the good Law of the worshippers of Mazda.</p>  <h4 align="center">25. Ard.</h4>  
 <p>We sacrifice unto Ashi Vanguhi, the bright, high, strong, tall-formed, and merciful; we sacrifice unto the Glory made by Mazda; we sacrifice unto the Weal made by Mazda. We sacrifice unto Pâre<i>n</i>di, of the light chariot; we sacrifice unto the Glory of the Aryas, made by Mazda; we sacrifice</p>  
 <p><a name="page_19"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 19</font></a></p>  
 <p>unto the awful kingly Glory, made by Mazda; we sacrifice unto that awful Glory, that cannot be forcibly seized, made by Mazda; we sacrifice unto the Glory of Zarathu<i>s</i>tra, made by Mazda.</p>  <h4 align="center">26. Â<i>s</i>tâd.</h4>  
 <p>We sacrifice unto Ar<i>s</i>tâ<i>t</i>, who makes the world grow; we sacrifice unto Mount Ushi-darena, made by Mazda, a God of holy happiness.</p>  <h4 align="center">27. Âsmân.</h4>  
 <p>We sacrifice unto the shining Heavens; we sacrifice unto the bright, all-happy, blissful abode of the holy ones.</p>  <h4 align="center">28. Zemyâd.</h4>  
 <p>We sacrifice unto the Earth, a beneficent God; we sacrifice unto these places, unto these fields; we sacrifice unto Mount Ushi-darena, made by Mazda, a God of holy happiness; we sacrifice unto all the mountains, that are seats of holy happiness, of full happiness, made by Mazda, the holy and masters of holiness; we sacrifice unto the awful kingly Glory, made by Mazda; we sacrifice unto the awful Glory that cannot be forcibly seized, made by Mazda.</p>  <h4 align="center">29. Mahraspand.</h4>  
 <p>We sacrifice unto the Mãthra Spe<i>n</i>ta, of high glory; we sacrifice unto the Law opposed to the Daêvas; we sacrifice unto the Law of Zarathu<i>s</i>tra; we sacrifice unto the long-traditional teaching; we sacrifice unto the good Law of the worshippers of Mazda; we sacrifice unto the Devotion to the Mãthra Spe<i>n</i>ta; we sacrifice unto the understanding that keeps the Law of the worshippers of Mazda; we sacrifice unto</p>  
 <p><a name="page_20"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 20</font></a></p>  
 <p>the knowledge of the Mãthra Spe<i>n</i>ta; we sacrifice unto the heavenly Wisdom, made by Mazda; we sacrifice unto the Wisdom acquired through the ear and made by Mazda.</p>  <h4 align="center">30. Anêrân.</h4>  
 <p>We sacrifice unto the eternal and sovereign luminous space; we sacrifice unto the bright Garô-nmâna; we sacrifice unto the sovereign place of eternal Weal; we sacrifice unto the <i>K</i>inva<i>t</i>-bridge, made by Mazda; we sacrifice unto Apãm Napâ<i>t</i>, the swift-horsed, the high and shining lord, who has many wives; and we sacrifice unto the water, made by Mazda and holy; we sacrifice unto the golden and tall Haoma; we sacrifice unto the enlivening Haoma, who makes the world grow; we sacrifice unto Haoma, who keeps death far away; we sacrifice unto the pious and good Blessing; we sacrifice unto the awful, powerful, cursing thought of the wise, a God; we sacrifice unto all the holy Gods of the heavenly world; we sacrifice unto all the holy Gods of the material world.</p>  
 <p>I praise, I invoke, I meditate upon, and we sacrifice unto the good, strong, beneficent Fravashis of the holy ones <a name="fr_102"></a><a href="#fn_97"><font size="1">1</font></a>.</p>
