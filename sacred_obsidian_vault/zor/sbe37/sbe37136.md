 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Pahlavi Texts, Part IV (SBE37)</i>, E.W. West, tr. [1892], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">VII. RA<i>D</i>Ö-DÂ<i>D</i>-AÎTAG NASK <a name="fr_1796"></a><a href="#fn_1747"><font size="1">1</font></a>.</h3>  
 <p>Dk. VIII, Chap. VIII, 4, probably refers to the passage containing the statement thus mentioned in Sls. X, 29:'In the Ra<i>d</i>ŏ-dâ<i>d</i>-aîtîh many harsh things are said about the severe punishment of the unhelpful <i>ones</i> (avi<i>g</i>î<i>d</i>âr-daî<i>s</i>nânŏ) <a name="fr_1797"></a><a href="#fn_1748"><font size="1">2</font></a> in the spiritual <i>existence</i>.'</p>
