 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Pahlavi Texts, Part IV (SBE37)</i>, E.W. West, tr. [1892], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">CHAPTER XXX.</h3>  
 <p align="center"><i>Var</i>s<i>tmânsar Nask</i>.</p>  
 <p>1. The seventh fargar<i>d</i>, A<i>d</i>-tâ-vakhshyâ <a name="fr_815"></a><a href="#fn_809"><font size="1">2</font></a>, is regarding the maintenance of the worship and obeisance of the religion and the spirit of the liturgy; and this, too, that the spirit of the ceremonial of him who is a right-thinking, intelligent, and wise <a name="fr_816"></a><a href="#fn_810"><font size="1">3</font></a> man is quickly mixed up with the light of the sun, and connected with the accomplishment of the wishes and the joy of the archangels.</p>  
 <p>2. About the choice of will <i>by</i> mankind, and the existence of a way to reward through <i>their</i> decision. 3. About advice to mankind as to seeking that position in which it is possible to remain long with fondness, and <i>as to</i> reciting and teaching <a name="fr_817"></a><a href="#fn_811"><font size="1">4</font></a> the revelation of the sacred beings.</p>  
 <p>4. And, from the statement of Zaratû<i>s</i>t, about the shouting of the demon Aresh <a name="fr_818"></a><a href="#fn_812"><font size="1">5</font></a> to mankind, <i>thus:</i></p>

 <p>

 </p>  
 <p><a name="page_242"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 242</font></a></p>  
 <p><span class="contnote"><font size="-2" color="GREEN">[paragraph continues]</font></span> 'Aûha<i>r</i>ma<i>z</i><i>d</i> and Aharman have been two brothers in one womb <a name="fr_819"></a><a href="#fn_813"><font size="1">1</font></a>, and out of them the archangel <a name="fr_820"></a><a href="#fn_814"><font size="1">2</font></a> liked that which is evil <a name="fr_821"></a><a href="#fn_815"><font size="1">3</font></a>, through what <i>occurs</i> when the understanders of it <i>have</i> mentioned the worship of the demons and this, that, after it, you <i>should</i> present cattle to the planetary <i>bodies and</i> the demons.' 5. About the falsity of the demon Aresh, the separate origin of light and darkness, the goodness of the material <i>existence</i> of light for determining <i>what</i> is done, and the evil of that of darkness.</p>  
 <p>6. The grumbling of the evil spirit thus: 'I am he whose thoughts <i>are</i> evil, O beneficent spirit! he whose words <i>are</i> evil, <i>and</i> he whose deeds <i>are</i> evil <a name="fr_822"></a><a href="#fn_816"><font size="1">4</font></a>; what is dark is my garment which is very thick, with lower corners where, so far as many go, <i>it is still</i> darker <a name="fr_823"></a><a href="#fn_817"><font size="1">5</font></a>; evil thoughts, evil words, and evil deeds are my food, and I love those <i>of</i> them who are in <i>that place</i> through evil thoughts, evil words, and evil deeds.' 7. And the speaking of Aûha<i>r</i>ma<i>z</i><i>d</i> thus: 'I am he whose thoughts <i>are</i> good, O evil spirit! he whose words <i>are</i> good, and he whose deeds <i>are</i> good <a name="fr_824"></a><a href="#fn_818"><font size="1">6</font></a>; the sky is my garment, which <i>was</i> first produced from that substance of the worldly <i>existences</i> which is created as the stone above all stones <a name="fr_825"></a><a href="#fn_819"><font size="1">7</font></a>, that is, every jewel is set in it; good thoughts, good words, and good deeds are my food, and I love those <i>of</i> them who are in that <i>place</i></p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p>

 </p>  
 <p><a name="page_243"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 243</font></a></p>  
 <p>through good thoughts, good words, <i>and</i> good deeds.'</p>  
 <p>8. This, too, that true discrimination is not for them, the demons <a name="fr_826"></a><a href="#fn_820"><font size="1">1</font></a> astute in evil; and they never truly discriminate whose will is that of Akôman <a name="fr_827"></a><a href="#fn_821"><font size="1">2</font></a>. 9. And about the sickening (vîmârînî<i>d</i>anŏ) of the patron spirits of mankind, <i>by</i> the demons <a name="fr_828"></a><a href="#fn_822"><font size="1">3</font></a>, through the deceit of man towards man owing to the deceit of the demons; and the approach of mankind to evil proceedings <i>on the part</i> of the spiritual lordship, through those patron spirits <a name="fr_829"></a><a href="#fn_823"><font size="1">4</font></a>.</p>  
 <p>Io. Also the sending of monarchy and the wisdom of religion, <i>by</i> Aûha<i>r</i>ma<i>z</i><i>d</i>, for the preservation of the creatures; the recurrence of the mission <a name="fr_830"></a><a href="#fn_824"><font size="1">5</font></a> whereby <i>there are</i> injury and affliction for the demons and sovereignty again for Aûha<i>r</i>ma<i>z</i><i>d</i>, and they possess the reward of Vohûman <a name="fr_831"></a><a href="#fn_825"><font size="1">6</font></a> and <i>what</i> is required <i>by</i> the sacred beings; and the predominance <i>of</i> man over demon, in the end, the good over the evil, and the righteous over the wicked; also about the nature of those <i>who</i> are producing the renovation <i>of the universe</i>. 11. This, too, that is a declaration: 'They are those, <i>O</i> Zaratû<i>s</i>t the Spîtâmân! who shall produce the renovation, they have escaped (girekhtŏ) among the existences, they are</p>

 <p>
 </p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p><a name="page_244"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 244</font></a></p>  
 <p>vigilant <a name="fr_832"></a><a href="#fn_826"><font size="1">1</font></a> in seeking righteousness, and gentle-voiced; and, as regards righteousness in thought, they convert into righteousness anything virtuous which <i>belongs</i> to them.' 12. About the statement of <i>those</i> praised it is recited that it is thus mentioned in the Gâthas: 'So we are with <i>those</i> who are thinethat is, we are thine ownby us this renovation is to be produced in the existences <a name="fr_833"></a><a href="#fn_827"><font size="1">2</font></a>.' 13. About the perpetual convocation <i>held by</i> the archangel regarding . the production of the future existence <a name="fr_834"></a><a href="#fn_828"><font size="1">3</font></a>.</p>  
 <p>14. This, too, that <i>he</i> is an extender of the days of those who defeat the army of the fiend <a name="fr_835"></a><a href="#fn_829"><font size="1">4</font></a> and clothe themselves <i>with</i> deeds of shining light, and also those of a virtuous body, who are these: the priest, the warrior, the husbandman, and the man who is a ruler; with whom are Aharî<i>s</i>vang <a name="fr_836"></a><a href="#fn_830"><font size="1">5</font></a> and the spirit of liberality (râ<i>d</i>îh) <a name="fr_837"></a><a href="#fn_831"><font size="1">6</font></a>; they meditate with good thoughts (hû-mîni<i>s</i>nîh) <a name="fr_838"></a><a href="#fn_832"><font size="1">7</font></a> and joy, and, with pleasure to themselves, they give the world into the guardianship of Aûha<i>r</i>ma<i>z</i><i>d</i>, and also <i>of</i> Ashavahi<i>s</i>t <a name="fr_839"></a><a href="#fn_833"><font size="1">8</font></a>, when they possess the religion of Aûha<i>r</i>ma<i>z</i><i>d</i> as a ruler. 15. This, too, that <i>he</i>, whose thoughts are through a high-priest who possesses a patron spirit, always thinks that which is virtuous, and his sagacity increases <a name="fr_840"></a><a href="#fn_834"><font size="1">9</font></a>.</p>  
 <p>16. And about advice to mankind as to three things, through which the renovation and happy progress of the creatures arise, <i>namely</i>, seeking the</p>

 <p>17. The excellence <i>of</i> righteousness is perfect.</p>
