 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Pahlavi Texts, Part IV (SBE37)</i>, E.W. West, tr. [1892], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">CHAPTER X.</h3>  
 <p>1. The Ka<i>s</i>kîsrôbô <a name="fr_82"></a><a href="#fn_81"><font size="1">1</font></a> <i>contains</i> particulars about the explanation of the ceremonial <i>and</i> ritual of the sacred beings, through what <i>arises its</i> conversion <i>into</i> demon-worship, <i>and</i> information <i>as to</i> cleanness and uncleanness. 2. The preparations and precautions for the Ya<i>s</i>ts <a name="fr_83"></a><a href="#fn_82"><font size="1">2</font></a>; the tokens and signs of the overflowing and evil owing to the demons at various times, and the cause of their exhaustion and the final victory of the sacred beings. 3. Then the exalting chants <i>of</i> every kind, which Aûha<i>r</i>ma<i>z</i><i>d</i> <i>taught</i> to Zaratû<i>s</i>t, are called the teaching (sâstô) of the spirits.</p>  
 <p>4. Excellence <i>that is</i> perfect is righteousness.</p>
