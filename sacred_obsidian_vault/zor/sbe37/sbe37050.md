 <p><a name="page_172"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 172</font></a></p>  <h2 align="center">DÎNKA<i>R</i><i>D</i>.BOOK IX.</h2>  <h3 align="center">CHAPTER I <a name="fr_492"></a><a href="#fn_489"><font size="1">1</font></a>.</h3>  
 <p>1. Satisfaction (shnôkhar) <i>to</i> the creator Aûha<i>r</i>ma<i>z</i><i>d</i>, <i>and</i> obeisance <i>to</i> the Ma<i>z</i><i>d</i>a-worshipping religion.</p>  
 <p>2. The ninth book (babâ) is about the Hâs <i>and</i> Fargar<i>d</i>s <a name="fr_493"></a><a href="#fn_490"><font size="1">2</font></a> of the various Nasks; the object of procuring the division of those portions which exist <i>being</i> owing to the quantity of what is in each one of the Nasks; <i>also</i> an explanation of a suitable selection <a name="fr_494"></a><a href="#fn_491"><font size="1">3</font></a> therefrom, such as is an epitome (nisangag-1) of the abundant detail therein.</p>
