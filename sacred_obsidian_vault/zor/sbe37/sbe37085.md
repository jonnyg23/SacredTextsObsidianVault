 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Pahlavi Texts, Part IV (SBE37)</i>, E.W. West, tr. [1892], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">CHAPTER XXXVI.</h3>  
 <p align="center"><i>Var</i>s<i>tmânsar Nask</i>.</p>  
 <p>1. The thirteenth fargar<i>d</i>, U<i>s</i>tavaiti <a name="fr_983"></a><a href="#fn_974"><font size="1">4</font></a>, is about the great reward of him who, through virtuous procedure, may occasion the benefit of a man <a name="fr_984"></a><a href="#fn_975"><font size="1">5</font></a> <i>and of</i> the religion of righteousness also. 2. This, too, that the maintenance of righteousness <a name="fr_985"></a><a href="#fn_976"><font size="1">6</font></a> is through the practice <i>of it</i>.</p>  
 <p>3. About the tokens of a righteous manthat is, the evidence of himand <i>his</i> reverence for duty <i>and</i> good works; also his imperceptible perversion (kastârîh)that is, not a single sin is manifest</p>
