 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Pahlavi Texts, Part IV (SBE37)</i>, E.W. West, tr. [1892], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">CHAPTER XLIX.</h3>  
 <p align="center"><i>Bakŏ Nask</i>.</p>  
 <p>1. The third fargar<i>d</i> is the Yê<font size="-1">N</font>hê-hâtãm <a name="fr_1232"></a><a href="#fn_1219"><font size="1">4</font></a>: there is here taught by it the worship of Aûha<i>r</i>ma<i>z</i><i>d</i>, which is the law of Aûha<i>r</i>ma<i>z</i><i>d</i>, that is, its law is virtuous <a name="fr_1233"></a><a href="#fn_1220"><font size="1">5</font></a>. 2. This, too, that the worship of Aûha<i>r</i>ma<i>z</i><i>d</i> is occasioned by it, which is the asking for life for beings <i>by</i> mankind <a name="fr_1234"></a><a href="#fn_1220"><font size="1">5</font></a>. 3. <i>And</i> this, too, that the ritual of the males and females of the righteous occurs <i>through</i> it, which is the obeisance for the archangels <a name="fr_1235"></a><a href="#fn_1221"><font size="1">6</font></a>. 4. And the atonement</p>

 <p><a name="page_310"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 310</font></a></p>  
 <p><i>for</i> crimes (va<i>g</i>agânŏ), because it is a gratification, is all for Aûha<i>r</i>ma<i>z</i><i>d</i> personally therefrom; and in connection therewith it amounts to a gratification for Aûha<i>r</i>ma<i>z</i><i>d</i>.</p>  
 <p>5. Here <i>one</i> mentions three particulars <a name="fr_1236"></a><a href="#fn_1222"><font size="1">1</font></a> which are in one's worship of Aûha<i>r</i>ma<i>z</i><i>d</i> of every description. 6. One is when the design (dâ<i>d</i>ŏ) of the person is virtuous, because it is restrained by some virtuousness of thought; this is that which amounts to worship and obeisance <i>for</i> Aûha<i>r</i>ma<i>z</i><i>d</i> personally. 7. One is when it teaches an asking for life for mankind, and its ordinance is the protection, nourishment, <i>and</i> other assistance <i>and</i> gratification <i>of</i> mankind; a friend of the primitive worldly creation of mankind produced it, <i>and</i> it comes into connection <i>with</i> the bridge <i>judgment</i> of mankind, for the worship <i>and</i> gratification of Aûha<i>r</i>ma<i>z</i><i>d</i>. 8. <i>And</i> one is when <i>one</i> would celebrate the obeisance <i>for</i> the archangels, which is for the sake of strengthening the archangels, each separately, in <i>their</i> control of the business of preparing <i>and</i> managing the world; because <i>it</i> is declared by revelation <i>that</i> to worship is this, that the ceremonial <i>may</i> reach this bridge <a name="fr_1237"></a><a href="#fn_1223"><font size="1">2</font></a> in company with <i>one</i> (pa<i>d</i>vand), for the worship <i>and</i> gratification of Aûha<i>r</i>ma<i>z</i><i>d</i>; the archangel who is to be strengthened by the ceremonial is one, <i>and</i> mankind are developed by the strength of the archangel.</p>  
 <p>9. Of righteousness perfect is the excellence.</p>
