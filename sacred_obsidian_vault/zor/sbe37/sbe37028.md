 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Pahlavi Texts, Part IV (SBE37)</i>, E.W. West, tr. [1892], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">CHAPTER XXV.</h3>  
 <p align="center"><i>Ganabâ-sar-ni</i>g<i>a</i>d<i> Nask.</i></p>  
 <p>1. The second section is the Ar<i>g</i>istân ('<i>value code</i>'), particulars about the value of small consumption of animate, and also that of inanimate, property; <i>with</i> the desirableness of information thereon, each separately. 2. The value of not destroying a righteous man even for a decree and justice, <i>and</i> of atonement for injuring the existence <a name="fr_235"></a><a href="#fn_234"><font size="1">2</font></a> of the fire of Varahrân <a name="fr_236"></a><a href="#fn_235"><font size="1">3</font></a>.</p>
