 <p><a name="page_347"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 347</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">VISPARAD VIII (SP. IX).</h3>  
 <p>1. With this word be Thou approached <a name="fr_1444"></a><a href="#fn_1431"><font size="1">1</font></a>, with the proper word be Thou present here, Thou who art Ahura Mazda, the holy, together with the good Yazads who are the Bountiful Immortals, who rule aright, and dispose (of all) aright, together with fifty, and a hundred, and a thousand, and ten thousand, and millions, and yet more.</p>  
 <p>2. And to Him who rules the best let the Kingdom be <a name="fr_1445"></a><a href="#fn_1432"><font size="1">2</font></a>!</p>
