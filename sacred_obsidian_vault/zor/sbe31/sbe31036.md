 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Zend Avesta, Part III (SBE31)</i>, L.H. Mills, tr. [1886], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">YASNA XIV (SP. XV).</h3>  <h4 align="center">DEDICATIONS.</h4>  
 <p>1. I will come to You, O Ye Bountiful Immortals! as a praiser and a priest, and an invoker and sacrificer, as a memorising reciter and a chanter, for Your sacrifice and homage, which are to be offered to You, the Bountiful Immortals, and for our dedication and sanctification; (yea, for ours) who are the holy prophets (destined to benefit the saints).</p>  
 <p>2. And to You, O Ye Bountiful Immortals <a name="fr_1070"></a><a href="#fn_1062"><font size="1">2a</font></a>! would I dedicate the flesh of my very body <a name="fr_1071"></a><a href="#fn_1063"><font size="1">3</font></a>, and all the blessings of a prospered life <a name="fr_1072"></a><a href="#fn_1064"><font size="1">4</font></a>.</p>  
 <p>3. In this Zaothra with this Baresman, I desire to</p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p><a name="page_254"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 254</font></a></p>  
 <p>approach the holy Yazads with my praise <a name="fr_1073"></a><a href="#fn_1065"><font size="1">1</font></a>, and all the holy lords of the ritual order at their times, Hâvani at his time, and Sâvanghi and Vîsya at their times. 4. I confess myself a Mazdayasnian, and of Zarathu<i>s</i>tra's order <a name="fr_1074"></a><a href="#fn_1066"><font size="1">2</font></a>.</p>  
 <p>5. The Zaotar speaks: As an Ahû (revered and) chosen, the Zaotar (?) speaks forth to me (?).</p>  
 <p>The Ratu speaks: As an Ahû (revered and) to be chosen, the Zaotar speaks forth to me.</p>  
 <p>The Zaotar: So let the Ratu from his Righteousness, holy and learned, speak forth!</p>
