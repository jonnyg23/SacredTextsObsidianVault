LVII).</h3>  <h4 align="center">THE FSHÛSHÔ-MÃTHRA <a name="fr_1282"></a><a href="#fn_1272"><font size="1">1</font></a>.</h4>  
 <p>1. (Introduction.) (To the increase of our homage and praise of God) we offer this service <a name="fr_1283"></a><a href="#fn_1273"><font size="1">2</font></a> which, as our defence <a name="fr_1284"></a><a href="#fn_1274"><font size="1">3</font></a>, may shield us, which is worship <a name="fr_1285"></a><a href="#fn_1275"><font size="1">4</font></a> with its beneficent results; and Blessedness is with it of a verity <a name="fr_1286"></a><a href="#fn_1276"><font size="1">5</font></a>, and Piety as well. [(Pâzand) and of this worship the results here mentioned are the well-thought thought, the word well spoken, and the deed well done]; and let this our worship shelter us from the Daêva and from the evil-minded man. 2. And to this worship do we confide <a name="fr_1287"></a><a href="#fn_1277"><font size="1">6</font></a> our settlements</p>

 <p>and persons for protection and care, for guarding, and for oversight; (3) and in this worship will we abide, O Ahura Mazda! and with joy.</p>  
 <p>In this worship do we exercise our choices; and to it will we approach, and to it will we belong; yea, to revering worship will we confide our settlements and persons for protection, and for care, for guarding, and for oversight, to such worship as is the praise of such as You <a name="fr_1288"></a><a href="#fn_1278"><font size="1">1</font></a>.</p>  
 <p align="center">MÃTHRA.</p>  
 <p>4. The owner of herds is the righteous (one), and he is victorious when he strikes, and thus he is the best; [(Pâzand) we therefore offer (this) service (for herd-owners <a name="fr_1289"></a><a href="#fn_1279"><font size="1">2</font></a>)] for the herd-owner is the father of the Kine by the help of him who follows the ritual order; and he is the father of the holy man as well, and of the sanctified creation <a name="fr_1290"></a><a href="#fn_1280"><font size="1">3</font></a>. He is in verity the bestower of blessings, and to him <a name="fr_1291"></a><a href="#fn_1281"><font size="1">4</font></a>, O Ye Bountiful Immortals <a name="fr_1292"></a><a href="#fn_1282"><font size="1">5</font></a>! we render, (and his do we make) Your greatness, Your goodness, and Your (spiritual) beauty, and let this man, the cattle-owner, approach to guard over us; and may he be our watchman together with the Righteous Order, and with store for our nourishment and full generous liberality, together with sharing of the goods <a name="fr_1293"></a><a href="#fn_1283"><font size="1">6</font></a>, with gentleness <a name="fr_1294"></a><a href="#fn_1284"><font size="1">7</font></a>, and with Ahura Mazda's sacred Fire!</p>

 <p>5. O Ye Bountiful Immortals! as Ye have made us, so do Ye save us, holy men, and saintly women (as we are, and steadfast in the faith) <a name="fr_1295"></a><a href="#fn_1285"><font size="1">1</font></a>. Save us, O Ye Bountiful Immortals! Ye who rule aright and who dispose (of all) aright, for none other do I know, save You; then with Your Righteousness <a name="fr_1296"></a><a href="#fn_1286"><font size="1">2</font></a> do Ye save us.</p>  
 <p>6. And we offer hereby our thoughts, and words, and actions, our herds, and men, to the Bountiful Spirit. And may the creative stars of Ahura Mazda, the Creator, shine down on us, and round about us <a name="fr_1297"></a><a href="#fn_1287"><font size="1">3</font></a> with full herds and healthy settlements, with healthy herds and healthy men, and with all in vigour, and endowed with the blessing of the Lord. 7. Praise to Thee, O Fire of Ahura Mazda! may’st thou come to (us in) the greatest one of the engrossing interests <a name="fr_1298"></a><a href="#fn_1288"><font size="1">4</font></a> for the help of the great (effort), for the joy-producing grace of the great (interest of our cause); grant us both Weal and Deathlessness!</p>  
 <p>8. We sacrifice to the entire collection of the Praises of the Yasna, with the careful structure of their language which has reached the most its object. And we offer (our homage) in our celebrations to Thy body, O Ahura Mazda! the most beautiful of forms, these stars, and to that one, the highest of the high [(Pâzand) such as the sun was called]. Yea, we worship the Praises of the Yasna which were the production of the world of old.</p>
