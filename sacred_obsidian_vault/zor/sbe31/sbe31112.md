 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Zend Avesta, Part III (SBE31)</i>, L.H. Mills, tr. [1886], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">IV.</h3>  
 <p>1. I proclaim the Airy<i>e</i>mâ-ishyô as the greatest of all authoritative prayers, O Spitama! as the most influential and helpful for progress; and may the Saoshya<i>n</i>ts (who would further us) use it and revere it.</p>  
 <p>2. I am speaking in accordance with it, O Spitama! and therefore I shall rule as sovereign over creatures which are mine, I who am Ahura Mazda. Let no one rule as Angra Mainyu <a name="fr_1595"></a><a href="#fn_1566"><font size="1">2</font></a> over realms that are his own, O Zarathu<i>s</i>tra Spitama! 3. Let Angra Mainyu be hid beneath the earth <a name="fr_1596"></a><a href="#fn_1567"><font size="1">3</font></a>. Let the Daêvas likewise</p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p><a name="page_391"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 391</font></a></p>  
 <p>disappear. Let the dead arise (unhindered by these foes), and let bodily life be sustained in these now lifeless bodies.</p>
