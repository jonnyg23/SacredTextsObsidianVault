 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Zend Avesta, Part III (SBE31)</i>, L.H. Mills, tr. [1886], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">III. GÂH UZIREN <a name="fr_1569"></a><a href="#fn_1541"><font size="1">1</font></a>.</h3>  
 <p>1. Propitiation to Ahura Mazda! A blessing is Asha Vahi<i>s</i>ta—. I confess myself a Mazdayasnian of the order of Zarathu<i>s</i>tra, a foe to the Daêvas, devoted to the lore of the Lord, for the Uzayêirina, the holy lord of the ritual order, for sacrifice, homage, propitiation, and praise, and for Frâda<i>t</i>-vîra and Da<i>h</i><i>v</i>yuma, the holy lord(s) of the ritual order, for their sacrifice, homage, propitiation, and praise. 2. And to that lofty Ahura, Apãm-napâ<i>t</i>, and to the waters which Mazda created be sacrifice, homage, propitiation, and praise <a name="fr_1570"></a><a href="#fn_1542"><font size="1">2</font></a>! 3, 4. (G. I, 3, 4.) 5. We sacrifice to the Uzayêirina, the holy lord of the ritual order. And we sacrifice to the Zaotar, the holy lord of the ritual order, and to the Hâvanan, and to the Âtarevakhsha, and to the Frabaretar, and to the Âbere<i>t</i>, and to the Âsnatar, and the Raêthwi<i>s</i>kar, and to the Sraoshâvareza, holy lords of the ritual order. 6. And we sacrifice to Frâda<i>t</i>-vîra and Da<i>h</i><i>v</i>yuma, the holy lord of the ritual order. And we sacrifice to the stars, the moon, and the sun, and to the constellations (?), and we sacrifice to the stars without beginning (to their course?), and to the glory of the doctrinal proclamations</p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p><a name="page_384"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 384</font></a></p>  
 <p>which are the evil man's distress <a name="fr_1571"></a><a href="#fn_1543"><font size="1">1</font></a>. 7. And we sacrifice to the manifest performer of the truth (the correct maintainer of the rites), the holy lord of the ritual order. And we sacrifice to the later lore; yea, we sacrifice to the manifest fulfiller of the truth, and to the (entire) creation of the holy (and the clean) by day and by night with Zaothras together with offered prayers, for the sacrifice and homage of Da<i>h</i><i>v</i>yuma, the lord. And we sacrifice to Da<i>h</i><i>v</i>yuma, the holy lord of the ritual order. 8. And we sacrifice to that lofty and royal lord, the brilliant Apãm-napâ<i>t</i> of the fleet horses; and we sacrifice to the water which is Mazda-made and holy. 9, 10. And we sacrifice to thee, the Fire, Ahura Mazda's son <a name="fr_1572"></a><a href="#fn_1544"><font size="1">2</font></a>. 11 <a name="fr_1573"></a><a href="#fn_1545"><font size="1">3</font></a>. And I bless the sacrifice, homage, strength, and swiftness of that lofty Ahura Napâ<i>t</i>-apãm, and of the water which Mazda created <a name="fr_1574"></a><a href="#fn_1546"><font size="1">4</font></a>.</p>
