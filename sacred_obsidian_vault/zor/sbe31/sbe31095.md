 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Zend Avesta, Part III (SBE31)</i>, L.H. Mills, tr. [1886], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">VISPARAD XVIII (SP. XXI) <a name="fr_1499"></a><a href="#fn_1482"><font size="1">4</font></a>.</h3>  
 <p>1. We worship Ahura Mazda with the u<i>s</i>ta <a name="fr_1500"></a><a href="#fn_1483"><font size="1">5</font></a>. And we worship the Amesha Spe<i>n</i>ta with the u<i>s</i>ta, and the holy man, the saint. And we worship the prior world of the holy (and of the clean) with an u<i>s</i>ta, and the state of weal and salvation for the holy man (the saint). 2. And we worship that life-long state of blessedness (for the holy) which is the evil man's calamity <a name="fr_1501"></a><a href="#fn_1484"><font size="1">6</font></a>; yea, we worship his eternal <a name="fr_1502"></a><a href="#fn_1485"><font size="1">7</font></a> salvation, and with the salvation prayer. And we sacrifice to every saint who</p>
