 <p>Propitiation be to the created body of the Kine of blessed endowment, and to the Kine's soul (so, if there is one cow presented <a name="fr_1599"></a><a href="#fn_1570"><font size="1">3</font></a>). Propitiation be to the body and soul of you two (so, if there are two <a name="fr_1600"></a><a href="#fn_1570"><font size="1">3</font></a>).—To your body and soul (if there are three, or the entire herd <a name="fr_1601"></a><a href="#fn_1570"><font size="1">3</font></a>). (The Ahuna follows.)</p>
