 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Zend Avesta, Part III (SBE31)</i>, L.H. Mills, tr. [1886], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">YASNA XX.</h3>  <h4 align="center">ZAND, OR COMMENTARY, ON THE ASHEM VOHÛ.</h4>  
 <p>1. A blessing is Righteousness (called) the best; there is weal, there is weal to this man when the Right (helps) the Righteousness best, (when the pious man serves it in truth <a name="fr_1144"></a><a href="#fn_1135"><font size="1">5</font></a>). Ahura Mazda spake forth: Ashem vohû vahi<i>s</i>tem asti. To this Asha, the holy ritual sanctity, one attributes the</p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p><a name="page_267"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 267</font></a></p>  
 <p>qualities of 'good' and 'best,' as one attributes property to an owner; thus this sentence vohû vahi<i>s</i>tem asti is substantiated (at once <a name="fr_1145"></a><a href="#fn_1136"><font size="1">1</font></a>).</p>  
 <p>2. U<i>s</i>tâ asti u<i>s</i>tâ ahmâi; by this attribution of blessedness (the praiser) assigns every person (or thing) of a sacred nature to every holy person, and as one usually (?) and regularly (?) <a name="fr_1146"></a><a href="#fn_1137"><font size="1">2</font></a> assigns every person or thing (?) <a name="fr_1147"></a><a href="#fn_1138"><font size="1">3</font></a> that is holy to every holy man.</p>  
 <p>3. Yya<i>t</i> ashâi vahi<i>s</i>tai <a name="fr_1148"></a><a href="#fn_1139"><font size="1">4</font></a>; by these words the worshipper ascribes the entire Mãthra (to Asha Vahi<i>s</i>ta), and ascribes all to the Mãthra, as one ascribes the kingdom to Righteousness, and as one ascribes righteousness to the invoking saint; yea, as one ascribes righteousness to us who are the prophets (who shall help and bless the people). The three maxims of the sentences (are thus fulfilled). And every word (in its detail), and the entire utterance in its proclamation, is the word of Ahura Mazda.</p>  
 <p align="center">CATECHETICAL ADDITION <a name="fr_1149"></a><a href="#fn_1140"><font size="1">5</font></a>.</p>  
 <p>4. Mazda has made a proclamation. (Question.) Whom did He announce? (Answer.) That holy one who is both heavenly and earthly. (Question.) Of what character is He who has thus announced Him? (Answer.) He is the best, and the one who is exercising sovereign power. (Question.) Of what character is the man whom He announced? (Answer.)</p>

 <p><span class="contnote"><font size="-2" color="GREEN">[paragraph continues]</font></span> The holy and the best, the one who rules with no capricious tyranny.</p>  
 <p>We sacrifice to the (several) part(s) of the Asha Vahi<i>s</i>ta (prayer). We sacrifice to the heard-recital of the Asha Vahi<i>s</i>ta, to its memorising, its chanting, and its sacrificial use <a name="fr_1150"></a><a href="#fn_1141"><font size="1">1</font></a>!</p>
