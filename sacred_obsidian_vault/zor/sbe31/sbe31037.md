 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Zend Avesta, Part III (SBE31)</i>, L.H. Mills, tr. [1886], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">YASNA XV (SP. XVI).</h3>  <h4 align="center">THE SACRIFICE CONTINUES.</h4>  
 <p>1. With precept, praise, and with delight produced by grace <a name="fr_1075"></a><a href="#fn_1067"><font size="1">3</font></a>, I call upon the Bountiful Immortals the good, and also therewith the beautiful by name <a name="fr_1076"></a><a href="#fn_1068"><font size="1">4</font></a>; and I sacrifice to them with the blessing of the good ritual, with the earnest blessings of the good Mazdayasnian Faith.</p>  
 <p>2. Whose best gift from his Righteousness is mine in the offering Ahura this knoweth; who have lived, and live ever, by their names these I worship, while I draw near with praises <a name="fr_1077"></a><a href="#fn_1069"><font size="1">5</font></a>. The Good Kingdom is to be chosen, that lot which most of all bears on (our blessings <a name="fr_1078"></a><a href="#fn_1070"><font size="1">6</font></a>).</p>  
 <p>3. Let Sraosha (Obedience) be here present for</p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p><a name="page_255"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 255</font></a></p>  
 <p>the sacrifice of Ahura Mazda, the most beneficent, the holy, who is so dear to us as at the first, so at the last; yea, let him be present here <a name="fr_1079"></a><a href="#fn_1071"><font size="1">1</font></a>.</p>  
 <p>4. As the Ahû (revered and) to be chosen, the Âtarevakhsha thus speaks forth to me.</p>  
 <p>(Response): So let the Ratu from his righteousness, holy and learned, speak forth!</p>
