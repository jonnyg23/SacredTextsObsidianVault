 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Zend Avesta, Part III (SBE31)</i>, L.H. Mills, tr. [1886], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">II. GÂH RAPITHVIN <a name="fr_1566"></a><a href="#fn_1538"><font size="1">1</font></a>.</h3>  
 <p>1. Propitiation to Ahura Mazda. A blessing is Asha Vahi<i>s</i>ta. I confess as a Mazda-worshipper, and of Zarathu<i>s</i>tra's order—for Rapithwina, the holy lord of the ritual order, for sacrifice, homage, propitiation, and for praise, and for Frâda<i>t</i>-fshu and Za<i>n</i>tuma, the holy lord(s) of the ritual order, for sacrifice, homage,</p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p><a name="page_382"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 382</font></a></p>  
 <p>propitiation, and for praise. 2. And propitiation be to Asha Vahi<i>s</i>ta, and to Ahura Mazda's Fire, for sacrifice, homage, propitiation, and praise <a name="fr_1567"></a><a href="#fn_1539"><font size="1">1</font></a>. 3, 4. (See Y. LX XI, 2, 3.)</p>  
 <p>5. And we sacrifice to the Rapithwina, the holy lord of the ritual order, and to the Gâtha Ahunavaiti, the holy, and ruling in the ritual order; and to the Gâtha U<i>s</i>tavaiti, and to the Gâtha Spe<i>n</i>tâ-mainyu, and to the Gâtha Vohu-khshathra, and to the Gâtha Vahi<i>s</i>tôi<i>s</i>ti, holy, and ruling in the ritual order. 6. And we sacrifice to Frâda<i>t</i>-fshu, and to Za<i>n</i>tuma, and to the Fshûshô-mãthra, even to the word correctly spoken, and we sacrifice to the (many) words correctly spoken, even to the victorious ones which slay the Demon-gods (the Daêvas <a name="fr_1568"></a><a href="#fn_1540"><font size="1">2</font></a>). And we sacrifice to the waters and the lands, and to the plants, and to the heavenly Yazads who are givers of the holy and the good. And we sacrifice to the Bountiful Immortals, (the guardians) of the saints.</p>  
 <p>7. And we sacrifice to the good, heroic, bountiful Fravashis of the saints, and to the heights of Asha (called) Vahi<i>s</i>ta, and to the greatest Mãthras as moving us to action, the greatest as teaching faithfulness to holy vows, the greatest as referring to actions which are evidently just, and the greatest for the acquisition of the Mazdayasnian Faith. 8. And we sacrifice to that assembly and reunion which the Bountiful Immortals hold when they gather (?) on the heights of Heaven, for the sacrifice and homage of Za<i>n</i>tuma, the lord.</p>  
 <p>And we (therefore) sacrifice to Za<i>n</i>tuma (as) the holy lord of the ritual order. 9. And we sacrifice</p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p><a name="page_383"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 383</font></a></p>  
 <p>to Asha Vahi<i>s</i>ta (who is Righteousness the Best), and to the Fire, Ahura Mazda's son—. 10. Yea, we sacrifice to Thee, the Fire, Ahura Mazda's son, the holy ritual lord—.</p>  
 <p>I bless the sacrifice, homage, strength, and swiftness of Asha Vahi<i>s</i>ta, and of the Fire, of Ahura Mazda—. And to this one be the glory!</p>
