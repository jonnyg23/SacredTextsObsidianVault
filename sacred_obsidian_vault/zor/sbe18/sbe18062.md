 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Pahlavi Texts, Part II (SBE18)</i>, E.W. West, tr. [1882], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">CHAPTER LV.</h3>  
 <p>1. <i>As to</i> the fifty-fourth question <i>and</i> reply, that <i>which</i> you ask is thus: What is the occupation and capacity (giriftârîh) of the person <i>that has</i> to preserve those who are in their three nights' <i>trials</i> <a name="fr_693"></a><a href="#fn_690"><font size="1">2</font></a>, <i>and</i> who is he?</p>  
 <p>2. The reply is this, that it is said a husband (gab<i>r</i>â) is indispensable for preservation through the three nights' <i>trials</i> which shall be <i>for</i> a privileged wife, a father <i>for</i> those of a child, <i>and</i> a master <i>for</i> those of a servant.</p>
