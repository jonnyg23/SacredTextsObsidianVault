 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Pahlavi Texts, Part II (SBE18)</i>, E.W. West, tr. [1882], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">CHAPTER XXIX.</h3>  
 <p>1. The twenty-eighth question is that you ask thus: For what reason is it not allowable to perform</p>  
 <p><a name="page_61"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 61</font></a></p>  
 <p>the ceremony of Srôsh, the living spirit (ahvô) <a name="fr_185"></a><a href="#fn_184"><font size="1">1</font></a>, <i>along</i> with other propitiations (shnûmanŏ) <a name="fr_186"></a><a href="#fn_185"><font size="1">2</font></a>, when they reverence him separately?</p>  
 <p>2. The reply is this, that the lord of all <i>things</i> is the creator who is persistent over his own creatures, and a precious work is his own true service <a name="fr_187"></a><a href="#fn_186"><font size="1">3</font></a> which is given by him to Srôsh the righteous whom, for this reason, <i>one</i> is to reverence separately when even his name is not frequently mentioned, and <i>one</i> is not even to reverence the names of the archangels with him.</p>
