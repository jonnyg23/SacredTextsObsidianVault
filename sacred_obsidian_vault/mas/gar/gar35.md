 <p><a name="page_103"><font size="1" color="green">p. 103</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">SECOND DEGREE.</h3>  <h3 align="center">FELLOW-CRAFT.</h3>  
 <p><a name="page_104"><font size="1" color="green">p. 104</font></a></p>  
 <p>"The Second, or Fellow-Craft's Degree is rendered interesting by those scientific instructions and philosophical lectures which characterize latter parts of the mysteries; though both of these Degrees were made to tend to the glory of that God who had given such wonderful faculties to them, and to the welfare of their fellow creatures."—ARCHDEACON MANT.</p>
