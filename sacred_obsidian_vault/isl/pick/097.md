 <h1 align="CENTER">97. al-qadr: The Majesty</h1>
 <a name="an_097_001">1</a> Lo! We revealed it on the Night of Predestination. 
 <a name="an_097_002">2</a> Ah, what will convey unto thee what the Night of Power is! 
 <a name="an_097_003">3</a> The Night of Power is better than a thousand months. 
 <a name="an_097_004">4</a> The angels and the Spirit descend therein, by the permission of their Lord,  with all decrees. 
 <a name="an_097_005">5</a> (The night is) Peace until the rising of the dawn.
