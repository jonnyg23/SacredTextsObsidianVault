 <h1 align="CENTER">107. al-Ma`un: The Daily Necessaries</h1>
 <a name="an_107_001">1</a> Hast thou observed him who belieth religion ? 
 <a name="an_107_002">2</a> That is he who repelleth the orphan, 
 <a name="an_107_003">3</a> And urgeth not the feeding of the needy. 
 <a name="an_107_004">4</a> Ah, woe unto worshippers 
 <a name="an_107_005">5</a> Who are heedless of their prayer; 
 <a name="an_107_006">6</a> Who would be seen (at worship) 
 <a name="an_107_007">7</a> Yet refuse small kindnesses!
