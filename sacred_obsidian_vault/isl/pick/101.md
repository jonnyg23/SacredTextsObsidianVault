 <h1 align="CENTER">101. al-Qari`ah: The Terrible Calamity</h1>
 <a name="an_101_001">1</a> The Calamity! 
 <a name="an_101_002">2</a> What is the Calamity ? 
 <a name="an_101_003">3</a> Ah, what will convey unto thee what the Calamity is! 
 <a name="an_101_004">4</a> A day wherein mankind will be as thickly-scattered moths 
 <a name="an_101_005">5</a> And the mountains will become as carded wool. 
 <a name="an_101_006">6</a> Then, as for him whose scales are heavy (with good works), 
 <a name="an_101_007">7</a> He will live a pleasant life. 
 <a name="an_101_008">8</a> But as for him whose scales are light, 
 <a name="an_101_009">9</a> A bereft and Hungry One will be his mother, 
 <a name="an_101_010">10</a> Ah, what will convey unto thee what she is! - 
 <a name="an_101_011">11</a> Raging Fire.
