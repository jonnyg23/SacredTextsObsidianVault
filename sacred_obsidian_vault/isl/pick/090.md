 <h1 align="CENTER">90. al-Balad: The City</h1>
 <a name="an_090_001">1</a> Nay, I swear by this city - 
 <a name="an_090_002">2</a> And thou art an indweller of this city - 
 <a name="an_090_003">3</a> And the begetter and that which he begat, 
 <a name="an_090_004">4</a> We verily have created man in an atmosphere: 
 <a name="an_090_005">5</a> Thinketh he that none hath power over him ? 
 <a name="an_090_006">6</a> And he saith: I have destroyed vast wealth: 
 <a name="an_090_007">7</a> Thinketh he that none beholdeth him ? 
 <a name="an_090_008">8</a> Did We not assign unto him two eyes 
 <a name="an_090_009">9</a> And a tongue and two lips, 
 <a name="an_090_010">10</a> And guide him to the parting of the mountain ways ? 
 <a name="an_090_011">11</a> But he hath not attempted the Ascent - 
 <a name="an_090_012">12</a> Ah, what will convey unto thee what the Ascent is! - 
 <a name="an_090_013">13</a> (It is) to free a slave, 
 <a name="an_090_014">14</a> And to feed in the day of hunger. 
 <a name="an_090_015">15</a> An orphan near of kin, 
 <a name="an_090_016">16</a> Or some poor wretch in misery, 
 <a name="an_090_017">17</a> And to be of those who believe and exhort one another to perseverance and  exhort one another to pity. 
 <a name="an_090_018">18</a> Their place will be on the right hand. 
 <a name="an_090_019">19</a> But those who disbelieve Our revelations, their place will be on the left hand. 
 <a name="an_090_020">20</a> Fire will be an awning over them.
