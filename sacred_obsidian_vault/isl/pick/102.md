 <h1 align="CENTER">102. at-Takathur: Worldly Gain</h1>
 <a name="an_102_001">1</a> Rivalry in worldly increase distracteth you 
 <a name="an_102_002">2</a> Until ye come to the graves. 
 <a name="an_102_003">3</a> Nay, but ye will come to know! 
 <a name="an_102_004">4</a> Nay, but ye will come to know! 
 <a name="an_102_005">5</a> Nay, would that ye knew (now) with a sure knowledge! 
 <a name="an_102_006">6</a> For ye will behold hell-fire. 
 <a name="an_102_007">7</a> Aye, ye will behold it with sure vision. 
 <a name="an_102_008">8</a> Then, on that day, ye will be asked concerning pleasure.
