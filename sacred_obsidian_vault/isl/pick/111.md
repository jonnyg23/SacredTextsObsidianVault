 <h1 align="CENTER">111. al-Lahab: The Flame</h1>
 <a name="an_111_001">1</a> The power of Abu Lahab will perish, and he will perish. 
 <a name="an_111_002">2</a> His wealth and gains will not exempt him. 
 <a name="an_111_003">3</a> He will be plunged in flaming Fire, 
 <a name="an_111_004">4</a> And his wife, the wood-carrier, 
 <a name="an_111_005">5</a> Will have upon her neck a halter of palm-fibre.
