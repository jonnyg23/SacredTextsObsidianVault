 <h1 align="CENTER">114. an-Nas: The Men</h1>
 <a name="an_114_001">1</a> Say: I seek refuge in the Lord of mankind, 
 <a name="an_114_002">2</a> The King of mankind, 
 <a name="an_114_003">3</a> The God of mankind, 
 <a name="an_114_004">4</a> From the evil of the sneaking whisperer, 
 <a name="an_114_005">5</a> Who whispereth in the hearts of mankind, 
 <a name="an_114_006">6</a> Of the jinn and of mankind
