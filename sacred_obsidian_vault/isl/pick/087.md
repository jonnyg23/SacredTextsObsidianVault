 <h1 align="CENTER">87. al-A`la: The Most High</h1>
 <a name="an_087_001">1</a> Praise the name of thy Lord the Most High, 
 <a name="an_087_002">2</a> Who createth, then disposeth; 
 <a name="an_087_003">3</a> Who measureth, then guideth; 
 <a name="an_087_004">4</a> Who bringeth forth the pasturage, 
 <a name="an_087_005">5</a> Then turneth it to russet stubble. 
 <a name="an_087_006">6</a> We shall make thee read (O Muhammad) so that thou shalt not forget 
 <a name="an_087_007">7</a> Save that which Allah willeth. Lo! He knoweth the disclosed and that which  still is hidden; 
 <a name="an_087_008">8</a> And We shall ease thy way unto the state of ease. 
 <a name="an_087_009">9</a> Therefor remind (men), for of use is the reminder. 
 <a name="an_087_010">10</a> He will heed who feareth, 
 <a name="an_087_011">11</a> But the most hapless will flout it, 
 <a name="an_087_012">12</a> He who will be flung to the great Fire 
 <a name="an_087_013">13</a> Wherein he will neither die nor live. 
 <a name="an_087_014">14</a> He is successful who groweth, 
 <a name="an_087_015">15</a> And remembereth the name of his Lord, so prayeth, 
 <a name="an_087_016">16</a> But ye prefer the life of the world 
 <a name="an_087_017">17</a> Although the Hereafter is better and more lasting. 
 <a name="an_087_018">18</a> Lo! This is in the former scrolls. 
 <a name="an_087_019">19</a> The Books of Abraham and Moses.
