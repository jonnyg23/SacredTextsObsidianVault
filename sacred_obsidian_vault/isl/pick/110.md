 <h1 align="CENTER">110. an-Nasr: The Help</h1>
 <a name="an_110_001">1</a> When Allah's succour and the triumph cometh 
 <a name="an_110_002">2</a> And thou seest mankind entering the religion of Allah in troops, 
 <a name="an_110_003">3</a> Then hymn the praises of thy Lord, and seek forgiveness of Him. Lo! He is  ever ready to show mercy.
