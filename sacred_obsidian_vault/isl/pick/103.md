 <h1 align="CENTER">103. al-`Asr: Time</h1>
 <a name="an_103_001">1</a> By the declining day, 
 <a name="an_103_002">2</a> Lo! man is a state of loss, 
 <a name="an_103_003">3</a> Save those who believe and do good works, and exhort one another to truth  and exhort one another to endurance.
