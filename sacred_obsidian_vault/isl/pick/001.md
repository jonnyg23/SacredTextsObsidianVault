 <h1 align="CENTER">1. al-Fatihah: The Opening</h1>
 <a name="an_001_001">1</a> In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful. 
 <a name="an_001_002">2</a> Praise be to Allah, Lord of the Worlds, 
 <a name="an_001_003">3</a> The Beneficent, the Merciful. 
 <a name="an_001_004">4</a> Master of the Day of Judgment, 
 <a name="an_001_005">5</a> Thee (alone) we worship; Thee (alone) we ask for help. 
 <a name="an_001_006">6</a> Show us the straight path, 
 <a name="an_001_007">7</a> The path of those whom Thou hast favoured; Not the (path) of those who  earn Thine anger nor of those who go astray.
