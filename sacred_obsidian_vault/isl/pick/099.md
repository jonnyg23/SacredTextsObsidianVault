 <h1 align="CENTER">99. al-Zilzal: The Shaking</h1>
 <a name="an_099_001">1</a> When Earth is shaken with her (final) earthquake 
 <a name="an_099_002">2</a> And Earth yieldeth up her burdens, 
 <a name="an_099_003">3</a> And man saith: What aileth her ? 
 <a name="an_099_004">4</a> That day she will relate her chronicles, 
 <a name="an_099_005">5</a> Because thy Lord inspireth her. 
 <a name="an_099_006">6</a> That day mankind will issue forth in scattered groups to be shown their  deeds. 
 <a name="an_099_007">7</a> And whoso doeth good an atom's weight will see it then, 
 <a name="an_099_008">8</a> And whoso doeth ill an atom's weight will see it then.
