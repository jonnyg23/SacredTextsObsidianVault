 <h1 align="CENTER">95. at-Tin: The Fig</h1>
 <a name="an_095_001">1</a> By the fig and the olive, 
 <a name="an_095_002">2</a> By Mount Sinai, 
 <a name="an_095_003">3</a> And by this land made safe; 
 <a name="an_095_004">4</a> Surely We created man of the best stature 
 <a name="an_095_005">5</a> Then we reduced him to the lowest of the low, 
 <a name="an_095_006">6</a> Save those who believe and do good works, and theirs is a reward unfailing. 
 <a name="an_095_007">7</a> So who henceforth will give the lie to thee about the judgment ? 
 <a name="an_095_008">8</a> Is not Allah the most conclusive of all judges ?
