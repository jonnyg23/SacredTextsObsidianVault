 <h1 align="CENTER">93. ad-Duha: The Early Hours</h1>
 <a name="an_093_001">1</a> By the morning hours 
 <a name="an_093_002">2</a> And by the night when it is stillest, 
 <a name="an_093_003">3</a> Thy Lord hath not forsaken thee nor doth He hate thee, 
 <a name="an_093_004">4</a> And verily the latter portion will be better for thee than the former, 
 <a name="an_093_005">5</a> And verily thy Lord will give unto thee so that thou wilt be content. 
 <a name="an_093_006">6</a> Did He not find thee an orphan and protect (thee) ? 
 <a name="an_093_007">7</a> Did He not find thee wandering and direct (thee) ? 
 <a name="an_093_008">8</a> Did He not find thee destitute and enrich (thee) ? 
 <a name="an_093_009">9</a> Therefor the orphan oppress not, 
 <a name="an_093_010">10</a> Therefor the beggar drive not away, 
 <a name="an_093_011">11</a> Therefor of the bounty of thy Lord be thy discourse.
