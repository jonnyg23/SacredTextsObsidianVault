 This is M.M. Pickthall's English rendering of the Qur'an.
 It is technically not in the public domain in the US, because it
 was published in India after 1922.
 However, Pickthall died in 1936, so it is now in the public
 domain in countries with the death+50 public domain rule
 (e.g. Australia, Pakistan, Canada), and it
 is also in the public domain in countries with the death+70 rule
 (e.g. UK and countries in the European Union), as of the end of 2006.
