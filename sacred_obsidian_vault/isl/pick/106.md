 <h1 align="CENTER">106. al-Quraysh: The Quraish</h1>
 <a name="an_106_001">1</a> For the taming of Qureysh. 
 <a name="an_106_002">2</a> For their taming (We cause) the caravans to set forth in winter and summer. 
 <a name="an_106_003">3</a> So let them worship the Lord of this House, 
 <a name="an_106_004">4</a> Who hath fed them against hunger and hath made them safe from fear.
