 <h1 align="CENTER">113. al-Falaq: The Daybreak</h1>
 <a name="an_113_001">1</a> Say: I seek refuge in the Lord of the Daybreak 
 <a name="an_113_002">2</a> From the evil of that which He created; 
 <a name="an_113_003">3</a> From the evil of the darkness when it is intense, 
 <a name="an_113_004">4</a> And from the evil of malignant witchcraft, 
 <a name="an_113_005">5</a> And from the evil of the envier when he envieth.
