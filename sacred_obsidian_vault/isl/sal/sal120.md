 <p>(119)</p>  
 <p>His Supplication as taught to Imam Husain</p>  
 <p>(a.s.)</p>  
 <p><i>In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful</i></p>  
 <p>O Allah! I praise You for every bounty, and thank You on every</p>  
 <p>goodness. And for every sin I seek forgiveness from You, I ask for every</p>  
 <p>goodness and I seek refuge from You after every evil and there is no</p>  
 <p>might or power except Allah, the mighty and the great.</p>
