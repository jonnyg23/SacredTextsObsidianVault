 <p>(9)</p>  
 <p>His Supplication in Praise of Allah</p>  
 <p><i>In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful</i></p>  
 <p>O One Who is nearer to me than the jugular vein. O One Who fulfills all</p>  
 <p>He wishes. O One Who is in between a man and his heart. O One Who</p>  
 <p>is at the highest place of manifestation. O One Who has no parallel.</p>
