 <p>(29)</p>  
 <p>His supplication on sighting the New Moon</p>  
 <p><i>In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful</i></p>  
 <p>O Allah! I seek from You goodness, victory, protection and munificence</p>  
 <p>in sustenance in this month and I wish for all the goodness in the next</p>  
 <p>month and grant me safety in this month and the next from all calamities.</p>  
 <p>O Allah make this month safe for me in which there is protection and</p>  
 <p>excessive piety and give me grace for those actions which makes You</p>  
 <p>pleased with me.</p>
