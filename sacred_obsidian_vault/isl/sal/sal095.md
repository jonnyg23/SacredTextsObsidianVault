 <p>(94)</p>  
 <p>His supplication while rinsing the nostrils</p>  
 <p><i>In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful</i></p>  
 <p>O Allah! Do not prohibit the fragrance of heaven for me. Count me</p>  
 <p>among the people who will enjoy the fragrance and bounties of heaven.</p>
