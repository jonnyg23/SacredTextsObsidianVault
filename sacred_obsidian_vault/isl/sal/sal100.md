 <p>(99)</p>  
 <p>His supplication while wiping his feet</p>  
 <p><i>In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful</i></p>  
 <p>O Allah! Set my feet firm on the bridge of Sirat on the day when other</p>  
 <p>feet will be moving unsteadily and restrict my efforts in those things</p>  
 <p>which pleases You.</p>
