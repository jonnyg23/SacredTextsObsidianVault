 <p>(63)</p>  
 <p>His supplication for repayment of debts</p>  
 <p><i>In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful</i></p>  
 <p>O Allah! O the averter of sorrows. O the dispeller of grief! O the one to</p>  
 <p>ward off the sorrow and O the one to accept the prayers of uneasy</p>  
 <p>people. O the Beneficent in this world and the Hereafter. You are my</p>  
 <p>only hope, and O the one to show mercy on everything, have mercy on</p>  
 <p>me. Shower such a mercy on me which excels every other mercy and</p>  
 <p>free me from all the debts.</p>
