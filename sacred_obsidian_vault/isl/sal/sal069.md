 <p>(68)</p>  
 <p>His supplication for averting the trap of the</p>  
 <p>enemies</p>  
 <p><i>In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful</i></p>  
 <p>O Allah I seek refuge from You that no one from Your kingdom</p>  
 <p>oppresses me. O Allah I seek refuge from the eventuality that in spite of</p>  
 <p>Your guidance I should deviate. O Allah I seek nearness with You that in</p>  
 <p>spite of Your safety I be ruined. O Allah I seek closeness to You that I be</p>  
 <p>firm and stable in Your domain when the law and order is Yours and it is</p>  
 <p>Your Power and strength.</p>
