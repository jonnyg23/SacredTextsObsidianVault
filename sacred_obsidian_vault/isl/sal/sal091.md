 <p>(90)</p>  
 <p>His supplication after four units of prayer</p>  
 <p>In every rakat recite Surah Hamd once and Surah</p>  
 <p>Tauheed five times.</p>  
 <p><i>In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful</i></p>  
 <p>Glory be to the One Whose signs can never be erased. Pure is He</p>  
 <p>Whose treasure never depletes. Glory be to Him Whose property cannot</p>  
 <p>be destroyed. Pure is He Whose help never ceases, pure is He who is</p>  
 <p>without a partner in His affair, glorified be He for whom there is no one</p>  
 <p>but Allah (worth worshipping).</p>
