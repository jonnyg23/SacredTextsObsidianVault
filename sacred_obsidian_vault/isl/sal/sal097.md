 <p>(96)</p>  
 <p>His supplication while washing his right</p>  
 <p>hand</p>  
 <p><i>In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful</i></p>  
 <p>O Allah! Give me the scroll of my deeds in my right hand and the</p>  
 <p>certificate of permanent abode in Paradise. Make my accounting easy.</p>
