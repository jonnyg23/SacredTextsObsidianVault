 <p>(40)</p>  
 <p>His supplication for fits</p>  
 <p><i>In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful</i></p>  
 <p>O the winds, I recite the supplication, which Ali son of Abi Talib upon</p>  
 <p>whom be peace and the Prophet of Allah, peace be on him and his</p>  
 <p>progeny recited on the jinns in the valley of Sufia. So those jinns obeyed</p>  
 <p>the commands. Then O wind why don't you obey and leave the body of</p>  
 <p>so and so, son of so and so?</p>
