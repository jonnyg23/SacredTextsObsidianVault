 <p>(38)</p>  
 <p>His supplication for every physical pain</p>  
 <p><i>In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful</i></p>  
 <p>I take refuge from the Honour of Allah, and His Power on all things. I</p>  
 <p>seek refuge by the Ruler of the heavens and the earth. I seek refuge for</p>  
 <p>myself by the name of Whom no moving creature of the earth and the</p>  
 <p>skies could harm. And I seek refuge from the One Whose name</p>  
 <p>increases bounties and bestows cure.</p>
