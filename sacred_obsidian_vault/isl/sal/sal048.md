 <p>(47)</p>  
 <p>His supplication for those having moles</p>  
 <p>It should be recited till the last seven days of the month.</p>  
 <p><i>In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful</i></p>  
 <p>And the parable of an evil word is as an evil tree pulled up from the</p>  
 <p>earths surface; it has no stability. And the mountains shall be made to</p>  
 <p>crumble with (an awful) crumbling. So that they shall be as scattered</p>  
 <p>dust.</p>
