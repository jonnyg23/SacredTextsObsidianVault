 <p>(92)</p>  
 <p>His supplication while urinating</p>  
 <p><i>In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful</i></p>  
 <p>O Allah! Protect my private parts and save them from prohibitions,</p>  
 <p>conceal my sins and prohibit hell for me.</p>
