 <p>(37)</p>  
 <p>His supplication for being cured from</p>  
 <p>disease</p>  
 <p><i>In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful</i></p>  
 <p>O Allah, I ask You for early cure of my disease or patience upon Your</p>  
 <p>calamities and when I leave this world I should be admitted into Your</p>  
 <p>mercy.</p>
