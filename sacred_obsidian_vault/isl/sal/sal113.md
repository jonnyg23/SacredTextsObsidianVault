 <p>(112)</p>  
 <p>His supplication when prostrating for</p>  
 <p>thanking Allah (s.w.t.)</p>  
 <p><i>In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful</i></p>  
 <p>O my Lord! You have refrained me from evil, but I did not stop. You had</p>  
 <p>restricted me from prohibited things but I did not pay any heed. You</p>  
 <p>covered me up in Your bounties but I never thanked You. I seek Your</p>  
 <p>Pardon! I seek Your Pardon! O the Merciful I seek ease at the time of</p>  
 <p>death and forgiveness at the time of accounting.</p>
