 <p>(114)</p>  
 <p>His supplication recited in prostration</p>  
 <p><i>In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful</i></p>  
 <p>O Allah! In Your presence have mercy on me upon my degradation, my</p>  
 <p>helplessness, and upon the fear of people and have mercy upon my</p>  
 <p>fellowship of generous one.</p>
