 <p>(115)</p>  
 <p>His supplication at the times of going to bed</p>  
 <p><i>In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful</i></p>  
 <p>In the name of Allah I put my side on the bed. I am on the religion of</p>  
 <p>Ibrahim and Muhammad peace be on him and his progeny whose</p>  
 <p>obedience is made obligatory upon us. Whatever Allah wills, happens</p>  
 <p>and whatever He doesnt will, never happens.</p>
