 <p>(57)</p>  
 <p>His supplication for seeking sustenance</p>  
 <p><i>In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful</i></p>  
 <p>O Allah! Grant me wealth and save my honour, do not make me</p>  
 <p>dependent and degrade me that I seek sustenance from those whom</p>  
 <p>You sustain. Or I divert my hopes from Your mischievous creatures and</p>  
 <p>praise them and I am tested by these things. Although You are far</p>  
 <p>greater and above such things. Surely You have power over everything.</p>
