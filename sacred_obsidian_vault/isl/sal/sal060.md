 <p>(59)</p>  
 <p>His supplication, which he used to recite</p>  
 <p>three times in the morning</p>  
 <p><i>In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful</i></p>  
 <p>Glory is to Allah, the Holy king (repeat it thrice). O Allah I seek refuge</p>  
 <p>from the declining of Your bounties, I seek refuge from Your mercy being</p>  
 <p>turned away, I seek protection from Your immediate displeasure, I seek</p>  
 <p>forgiveness from my wretched actions, I seek Your refuge from the</p>  
 <p>mischief of the day and night. O Allah! I request You for the sake of Your</p>  
 <p>Honour, Power, Holiness and Your Power on Creation and blessings on</p>  
 <p>Muhammad (s.a.w.a.) and the progeny of Muhammad (s.a.w.a.) (then</p>  
 <p>ask your wishes).</p>
