 <p>(44)</p>  
 <p>His supplication at the time of childbirth</p>  
 <p><i>In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful</i></p>  
 <p>O the creator of one life from another! And the one Who takes one life</p>  
 <p>from another! O the one to separate one life from another life release</p>  
 <p>this newborn from the prison of the womb.</p>
