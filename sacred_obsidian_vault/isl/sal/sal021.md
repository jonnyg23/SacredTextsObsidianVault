 <p>(20)</p>  
 <p>Dua seeking forgiveness</p>  
 <p><i>In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful</i></p>  
 <p>O Allah forgive my sins of which You are aware and if I repeat forgive</p>  
 <p>me again. O Allah! I couldn't get an opportunity to fulfill the covenants</p>  
 <p>against my evil deeds, forgive these sins also. O Allah, forgive the sins</p>  
 <p>committed by the action of eyes, mistake of the lips, negligence of the</p>  
 <p>heart and by the movement of the tongue.</p>
