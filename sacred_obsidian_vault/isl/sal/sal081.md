 <p>(80)</p>  
 <p>His supplication for the return of the one</p>  
 <p>who flees the battlefield</p>  
 <p><i>In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful</i></p>  
 <p>Or like utter darkness in the stormy seas. There is wave upon wave,</p>  
 <p>above which is a Cloud, (layers of) utter darkness one above another;</p>  
 <p>when a man holds out his hand, he is unable to see it; and for</p>  
 <p>whomsoever Allah does not create light, he has no light.</p>
