 <p>(42)</p>  
 <p>His supplication for stomachache to be</p>  
 <p>recited after drinking some hot water</p>  
 <p><i>In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful</i></p>  
 <p>O Allah! O Allah! O Allah! O the Beneficent! O the Merciful! O the Lord</p>  
 <p>of the Lords! O Deity of the deities! O the King of the Kings! O the chief</p>  
 <p>of the Chiefs! Cure me from all the diseases and grant me strength from</p>  
 <p>all the ailments by Your cure, that I am Your slave and son of Your slave</p>  
 <p>and I move about only within Your power.</p>
