 <p>(34)</p>  
 <p>His supplication while prostrating</p>  
 <p><i>In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful</i></p>  
 <p>On the eve of Eid-ul-Fitr recite two Rakats Namaz. In first unit after</p>  
 <p>Surah Hamd recite this supplication 1000 times and in second unit after</p>  
 <p>Surah Hamd recite this supplication in prostration once.</p>  
 <p>O the possessor of Honour and O the doer of obligations O the one to</p>  
 <p>appoint Muhammad Mustafa (s.a.w.a.). Bless Muhammad (s.a.w.a.) and</p>  
 <p>his progeny.</p>
