 <p>(43)</p>  
 <p>His supplication for piles</p>  
 <p><i>In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful</i></p>  
 <p>O the generous! O the possessor of greatness! O the Merciful! O the</p>  
 <p>one Who is near! O the one Who hears our supplication! O the Creator!</p>  
 <p>O the generous! Bless Muhammad (s.a.w.a.) and his progeny and send</p>  
 <p>Your bounties upon me and cure the pain in me.</p>
