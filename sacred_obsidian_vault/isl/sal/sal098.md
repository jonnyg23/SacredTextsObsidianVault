 <p>(97)</p>  
 <p>His supplication while washing his left hand</p>  
 <p><i>In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful</i></p>  
 <p>O Allah! Do not give the scroll of my deeds in this hand, nor tie it around</p>  
 <p>my neck. And by Your sake I seek refuge of the dangerous fire of hell.</p>
