 <p>(105)</p>  
 <p>His supplication after every prayer for</p>  
 <p>protection and safety</p>  
 <p><i>In the name of Allah, the Beneficent, the Merciful</i></p>  
 <p>Glory be to Him, Who does not treat the citizens of His kingdom unjustly</p>  
 <p>and harshly. Glory be to Him Who does not let torment and pain come</p>  
 <p>suddenly upon mankind. Glory be to the compassionate, the Merciful. O</p>  
 <p>Allah! Fill my heart and mind with true enlightenment, discerning</p>  
 <p>intelligence, quick understanding and knowledge. Verily You have power</p>  
 <p>over all things.</p>
