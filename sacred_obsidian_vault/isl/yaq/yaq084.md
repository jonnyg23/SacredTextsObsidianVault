 <p><a name="page_1709"><font size="1" color="green">p. 1709</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">Sūra LXXXIV.</h3>  <h3 align="center"><i>Inshiqāq,</i> or The Rending Asunder.</h3>  
 <p><i>In the name of God, Most Gracious,<br> Most Merciful.</i></p>  
 <p>1. When the Sky is<br> Rent asunder,</p>  
 <p>2. And hearkens to<br> (The Command of) its Lord,—<br> And it must needs<br> (Do so);—</p>  
 <p>3. And when the Earth<br> Is flattened out,</p>  
 <p>4. And casts forth<br> What is within it<br> And becomes (clean) empty,</p>  
 <p>5. And hearkens to<br> (The Command of) its Lord,—<br> And it must needs<br> (Do so);—(then will come<br> Home the full Reality)</p>  
 <p><a name="page_1710"><font size="1" color="green">p. 1710</font></a></p>  
 <p>6. O thou man!<br> Verily thou art ever<br> Toiling on towards thy Lord—<br> Painfully toiling,—but thou<br> Shalt meet Him.</p>  
 <p>7. Then he who is given<br> His Record in his<br> Right hand,</p>  
 <p>8. Soon will his account<br> Be taken by an easy reckoning,</p>  
 <p>9. And he will turn<br> To his people, rejoicing!</p>  
 <p>10. But he who is given<br> His Record behind his back,—</p>  
 <p>11. Soon will he cry<br> For Perdition,</p>  
 <p>12. And he will enter<br> A Blazing Fire.</p>  
 <p>13. Truly, did he go about<br> Among his people, rejoicing!</p>  
 <p><a name="page_1711"><font size="1" color="green">p. 1711</font></a></p>  
 <p>14. Truly, did he think<br> That he would not<br> Have to return (to Us)!</p>  
 <p>15. Nay, nay! for his Lord<br> Was (ever) watchful of him!</p>  
 <p>16. So I do call<br> To witness the ruddy glow<br> Of Sunset;</p>  
 <p>17. The Night and its Homing;</p>  
 <p>18. And the Moon<br> In her Fulness:</p>  
 <p>19. Ye shall surely travel<br> From stage to stage</p>  
 <p><a name="page_1712"><font size="1" color="green">p. 1712</font></a></p>  
 <p>20. What then is the matter<br> With them, that they<br> Believe not?—</p>  
 <p>21. And when the Qur-ān<br> Is read to them, they<br> Fall not prostrate,</p>  
 <p>22. But on the contrary<br> The Unbelievers reject (it).</p>  
 <p>23. But God has full Knowledge<br> Of what they secrete<br> (In their breasts).</p>  
 <p>24. So announce to them<br> A Penalty Grievous,</p>  
 <p>25. Except to those who believe<br> And work righteous deeds:<br> For them is a Reward<br> That will never fail.</p>
