 <p><a name="page_1765"><font size="1" color="green">p. 1765</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">Sūra XCVII.</h3>  <h3 align="center"><i>Qadr,</i> or The Night of Power (or Honour).</h3>  
 <p><i>In the name of God, Most Gracious,<br> Most Merciful.</i></p>  
 <p>1. We have indeed revealed<br> This (Message)<br> In the Night of Power:</p>  
 <p>2. And what will explain<br> To thee what the Night<br> Of Power is?</p>  
 <p>3. The Night of Power<br> Is better than<br> A thousand Months,</p>  
 <p>4. Therein come dawn<br> The angels and the Spirit<br> By God's permission,<br> On every errand:</p>  
 <p>5. Peace!…This<br> Until the rise of Morn!</p>
