 <p><a name="page_14"><font size="1" color="green">p. 14</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">Sūra I.</h3>  <h3 align="center">Fātiḥa, or the Opening Chapter.</h3>  
 <p>1. <i>In the name of God, Most Gracious,<br> Most Merciful</i>.</p>  
 <p>2. Praise be to God,<br> The Cherisher and Sustainer of the Worlds;</p>  
 <p>3. Most Gracious, Most Merciful;</p>  
 <p>4. Master of the Day of Judgment.</p>  
 <p>5. Thee do we worship,<br> And Thine aid we seek.</p>  
 <p><a name="page_15"><font size="1" color="green">p. 15</font></a></p>  
 <p>6. Show us the straight way,</p>  
 <p>7. The way of those on whom<br> Thou hast bestowed Thy Grace,<br> Those whose (portion)<br> Is not wrath,<br> And who go not astray.</p>
