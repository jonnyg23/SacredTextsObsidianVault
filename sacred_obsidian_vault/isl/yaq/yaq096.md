 <p><a name="page_1761"><font size="1" color="green">p. 1761</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">Sūra XCVI.</h3>  <h3 align="center"><i>Iqraa</i>, or Read! or Proclaim!</h3>  <h6 align="center">Or <i>’Alaq</i>, or The Clot of Congealed Blood.</h6>  
 <p><i>In the name of God, Most Gracious,<br> Most Merciful.</i></p>  
 <p>1. Proclaim! (or Read!)<br> In the name<br> Of thy Lord and Cherisher,<br> Who created—</p>  
 <p>2. Created man, out of<br> A (mere) clot<br> Of congealed blood:</p>  
 <p>3. Proclaim! And thy Lord<br> Is Most Bountiful,—</p>  
 <p>4. He Who taught<br> (The use of) the Pen,—</p>  
 <p><a name="page_1762"><font size="1" color="green">p. 1762</font></a></p>  
 <p>5. Taught man that<br> Which he knew not.</p>  
 <p>6. Nay, but man doth<br> Transgress all bounds,</p>  
 <p>7. In that he looketh<br> Upon himself as self-sufficient.</p>  
 <p>8. Verily, to thy Lord<br> Is the return (of all).</p>  
 <p>9. Seest thou one<br> Who forbids—</p>  
 <p>10. A votary when he<br> (Turns) to pray?</p>  
 <p>11. Seest thou if<br> He is on (the road<br> Of) Guidance?—</p>  
 <p>12. Or enjoins Righteousness?</p>  
 <p><a name="page_1763"><font size="1" color="green">p. 1763</font></a></p>  
 <p>13. Seest thou if he<br> Denies (Truth) and turns away?</p>  
 <p>14. Knoweth he not<br> That God doth see?</p>  
 <p>15. Let him beware! If he<br> Desist not, We will<br> Drag him by the forelock,—</p>  
 <p>16. A lying, sinful forelock!</p>  
 <p>17. Then, let him call<br> (For help) to his council<br> (Of comrades):</p>  
 <p>18. We will call<br> On the angels of punishment<br> (To deal with him)!</p>  
 <p>19. Nay, heed him not:<br> But bow down in adoration,<br> And bring thyself<br> The closer (to God)!</p>
