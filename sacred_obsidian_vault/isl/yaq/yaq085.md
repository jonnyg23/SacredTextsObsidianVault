 <p><a name="page_1714"><font size="1" color="green">p. 1714</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">Sūra LXXXV.</h3>  <h3 align="center"><i>Burūj,</i> or The Zodiacal Signs.</h3>  
 <p><i>In the name of God, Most Gracious,<br> Most Merciful.</i></p>  
 <p>1. By the Sky, (displaying)<br> The Zodiacal Signs;</p>  
 <p>2. By the promised Day<br> (Of Judgment);</p>  
 <p>3. By one that witnesses,<br> And the subject of the witness;—</p>  
 <p>4. Woe to the makers<br> Of the pit (of fire),</p>  
 <p><a name="page_1715"><font size="1" color="green">p. 1715</font></a></p>  
 <p>5. Fire supplied (abundantly)<br> With Fuel:</p>  
 <p>6. Behold! they sat<br> Over against the (fire),</p>  
 <p>7. And they witnessed<br> (All) that they were doing<br> Against the Believers.</p>  
 <p>8. And they ill-treated them<br> For no other reason than<br> That they believed in God,<br> Exalted in Power,<br> Worthy of all Praise!—</p>  
 <p>9 Him to Whom belongs<br> The dominion of the heavens<br> And the earth!<br> And God is Witness<br> To all things.</p>  
 <p>10. Those who persecute (or draw<br> into temptation)<br> The Believers, men and women,<br> And do not turn<br> In repentance, will have<br> The Penalty of Hell:<br> They will have the Penalty<br> Of the Burning Fire.</p>  
 <p>11. For those who believe<br> And do righteous deeds,<br> Will be Gardens,<br> Beneath which Rivers flow:<br> That is the great Salvation,<br> (The fulfilment of all desires),</p>  
 <p><a name="page_1716"><font size="1" color="green">p. 1716</font></a></p>  
 <p>12. Truly strong is the Grip<br> (And Power) of thy Lord.</p>  
 <p>13. It is He Who creates<br> From the very beginning,<br> And He can restore (life).</p>  
 <p>14. And He is the Oft-Forgiving,<br> Full of loving-kindness,</p>  
 <p>15. Lord of the Throne of Glory,</p>  
 <p>16. Doer (without let)<br> Of all that He intends.</p>  
 <p>17. Has the story<br> Reached thee,<br> Of the Forces—</p>  
 <p>18. Of Pharaoh<br> And the <u>Th</u>amūd?</p>  
 <p>19. And yet the Unbelievers<br> (Persist) in rejecting<br> (The Truth)!</p>  
 <p>20. But God doth<br> Encompass them<br> From behind!</p>  
 <p><a name="page_1717"><font size="1" color="green">p. 1717</font></a></p>  
 <p>21. Nay, this is<br> A Glorious Qur-ān,</p>  
 <p>22. (Inscribed) in<br> A Tablet Preserved!</p>
