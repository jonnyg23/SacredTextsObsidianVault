 <p><a name="page_1727"><font size="1" color="green">p. 1727</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">Sūra LXXXVIII.</h3>  <h3 align="center"><i><u>G</u>āshiya,</i> or The Overwhelming Event.</h3>  
 <p><i>In the name of God, Most Gracious,<br> Most Merciful.</i></p>  
 <p>1. Has the story<br> Reached thee, of<br> The Overwhelming (Event)?</p>  
 <p>2. Some faces, that Day,<br> Will be humiliated,</p>  
 <p>3. Labouring (hard), weary,—</p>  
 <p>4. The while they enter<br> The Blazing Fire,—</p>  
 <p>5. The while they are given,<br> To drink, of a bailing hot spring,</p>  
 <p>6. No food will there be<br> For them but a bitter <i><u>Dh</u>arī’</i></p>  
 <p>7. Which will neither nourish<br> Nor satisfy hunger.</p>  
 <p>8. (Other) faces that Day<br> Will be joyful,</p>  
 <p><a name="page_1728"><font size="1" color="green">p. 1728</font></a></p>  
 <p>9. Pleased with their Striving,—</p>  
 <p>10. In a Garden on high,</p>  
 <p>11. Where they shall hear<br> No (word) of vanity:</p>  
 <p>12. Therein will be<br> A bubbling spring:</p>  
 <p>13. Therein will be Thrones<br> (Of dignity), raised on high,</p>  
 <p>14. Goblets placed (ready),</p>  
 <p>15. And Cushions set in rows,</p>  
 <p>16. And rich carpets<br> (All) spread out.</p>  
 <p>17. Do they not look<br> At the Camels,<br> How they are made?—</p>  
 <p><a name="page_1729"><font size="1" color="green">p. 1729</font></a></p>  
 <p>18. And at the Sky,<br> How it is raised high?—</p>  
 <p>19. And at the Mountains,<br> How they are fixed firm?—</p>  
 <p>20. And at the Earth,<br> How it is spread out?</p>  
 <p>21. Wherefore do thou give<br> Admonition, for thou art<br> One to admonish.</p>  
 <p>22. Thou art not one<br> To manage (men's) affairs.</p>  
 <p>23. But if any turn away<br> And reject God,—</p>  
 <p>24. God will punish him<br> With a mighty Punishment,</p>  
 <p>25. For to Us will be<br> Their Return;</p>  
 <p>26. Then it will be for Us<br> To call them to account.</p>
