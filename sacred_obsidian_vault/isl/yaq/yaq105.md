 <p><a name="page_1792"><font size="1" color="green">p. 1792</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">Sūra CV.</h3>  <h3 align="center"><i>Fīl,</i> or The Elephant.</h3>  
 <p><i>In the name of God, Most Gracious,<br> Most Merciful.</i></p>  
 <p>1. Seest thou not<br> How thy Lord dealt<br> With the Companions<br> Of the Elephant?</p>  
 <p>2. Did He not make<br> Their treacherous plan<br> Go astray?</p>  
 <p>3. And He sent against them<br> Flights of Birds,</p>  
 <p>4. Striking them with stones<br> Of baked clay.</p>  
 <p>5. Then did He make them<br> Like an empty field<br> Of stalks and straw,<br> (Of which the corn)<br> Has been eaten up.</p>
