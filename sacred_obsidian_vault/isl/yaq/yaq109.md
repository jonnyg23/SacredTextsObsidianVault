 <p><a name="page_1800"><font size="1" color="green">p. 1800</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">Sūra CIX</h3>  <h3 align="center"><i>Kāfirūn</i>, or Those who reject Faith.</h3>  
 <p><i>In the name of God, Most Gracious,<br> Most Merciful</i></p>  
 <p>1. Say: O ye<br> That reject Faith!</p>  
 <p>2. I worship not that<br> Which ye worship,</p>  
 <p>3. Nor will ye worship<br> That which I worship.</p>  
 <p>4. And I will not worship<br> That which ye have been<br> Wont to worship,</p>  
 <p>5. Nor will ye worship<br> That which I worship.</p>  
 <p>6. To you be your Way,<br> And to me mine.</p>
