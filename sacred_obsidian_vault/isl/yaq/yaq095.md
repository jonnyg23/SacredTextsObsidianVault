 <p><a name="page_1758"><font size="1" color="green">p. 1758</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">Sūra XCV.</h3>  <h3 align="center"><i>Tīn,</i> or The Fig.</h3>  
 <p><i>In the name of God, Most Gracious,<br> Most Merciful.</i></p>  
 <p>1. By the Fig<br> And the Olive,</p>  
 <p>2. And the Mount<br> Of Sinai,</p>  
 <p>3. And this City<br> Of security,—</p>  
 <p><a name="page_1759"><font size="1" color="green">p. 1759</font></a></p>  
 <p>4. We have <a href="errata.htm#9">indeed</a> created man<br> In the best of moulds,</p>  
 <p>5. Then do We abase him<br> (To be) the lowest<br> Of the low,—</p>  
 <p>6. Except such as believe<br> And do righteous deeds:<br> For they shall have<br> A reward unfailing.</p>  
 <p>7. Then what can,<br> After this, contradict thee<br> As to the Judgment<br> (To come)?</p>  
 <p>8. Is not God<br> The wisest of Judges?</p>
