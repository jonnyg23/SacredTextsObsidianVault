 <p><a name="page_1802"><font size="1" color="green">p. 1802</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">Sūra CX.</h3>  <h3 align="center"><i>Naṣr,</i> or Help.</h3>  
 <p><i>In the name of God, Most Gracious,<br> Most Merciful.</i></p>  
 <p>1. When comes the Help<br> Of God, and Victory,</p>  
 <p>2. And thou dost see<br> The People enter God's Religion<br> In crowds,</p>  
 <p>3. Celebrate the Praises<br> Of thy Lord, and pray<br> For His Forgiveness:<br> For He is Oft-Returning<br> (In Grace and Mercy).</p>
