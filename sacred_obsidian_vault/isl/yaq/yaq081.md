 <p><a name="page_1693"><font size="1" color="green">p. 1693</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">Sūra LXXXI.</h3>  <h3 align="center"><i>Takwīr,</i> or the Folding Up.</h3>  
 <p><i>In the name of God, Most Gracious,<br> Most Merciful</i></p>  
 <p>1. When the sun<br> (With its spacious light)<br> Is folded up;</p>  
 <p>2. When the stars<br> Fall, losing their lustre;</p>  
 <p>3. When the mountains vanish<br> (Like a mirage);</p>  
 <p>4. When the she-camels,<br> Ten months with young,<br> Are left untended;</p>  
 <p>5. When the wild beasts<br> Are herded together</p>  
 <p><a name="page_1694"><font size="1" color="green">p. 1694</font></a></p>  
 <p>(In human habitations);</p>  
 <p>6. When the oceans<br> Boil over with a swell;</p>  
 <p>7. When the souls<br> Are sorted out,<br> (Being joined, like with like);</p>  
 <p>8. When the female (infant),<br> Buried alive, is questioned—</p>  
 <p>9. For what crime<br> She was killed;</p>  
 <p>10. When the Scrolls<br> Are laid open;</p>  
 <p><a name="page_1695"><font size="1" color="green">p. 1695</font></a></p>  
 <p>11. When the World on High<br> Is unveiled;</p>  
 <p>12. When the Blazing Fire<br> Is kindled to fierce heat;</p>  
 <p>13. And when the Garden<br> Is brought near;—</p>  
 <p>14. (Then) shall each soul know<br> What it has put forward.</p>  
 <p>15. So verily I call<br> To witness the Planets—<br> That recede,</p>  
 <p><a name="page_1696"><font size="1" color="green">p. 1696</font></a></p>  
 <p>16. Go straight, or hide;</p>  
 <p>17. And the Night<br> As it dissipates;</p>  
 <p>18. And the Dawn<br> As it breathes away<br> The darkness;—</p>  
 <p>19. Verily this is the word<br> Of a most honourable Messenger,</p>  
 <p>20. Endued with Power,<br> With rank before<br> The Lord of the Throne,</p>  
 <p>21. With authority there,<br> (And) faithful to his trust.</p>  
 <p>22. And (O people!)<br> Your Companion is not<br> One possessed;</p>  
 <p><a name="page_1697"><font size="1" color="green">p. 1697</font></a></p>  
 <p>23. And without doubt he saw him<br> In the clear horizon.</p>  
 <p>24. Neither doth he withhold<br> Grudgingly a knowledge<br> Of the Unseen.</p>  
 <p>25. Nor is it the word<br> Of an evil spirit accursed!</p>  
 <p>26. When whither go ye?</p>  
 <p>27. Verily this is no less<br> Than a Message<br> To (all) the Worlds:</p>  
 <p>28. (With profit) to whoever<br> Among you wills<br> To go straight:</p>  
 <p>29. But ye shall not will<br> Except as God wills,—<br> The Cherisher of the Worlds.</p>
