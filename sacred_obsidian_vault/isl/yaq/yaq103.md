 <p><a name="page_1783"><font size="1" color="green">p. 1783</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">Sūra CIII.</h3>  <h3 align="center"><i>’Aṣr,</i> or Time through the Ages.</h3>  
 <p><i>In the name of God, Most Gracious,<br> Most Merciful.</i></p>  
 <p>1. By (the Token of)<br> Time (through the Ages),</p>  
 <p>2. Verily Man<br> Is in loss,</p>  
 <p>3. Except such as have Faith,<br> And do righteous deeds,<br> And (join together)<br> In the mutual teaching<br> Of Truth, and of<br> Patience and Constancy.</p>
