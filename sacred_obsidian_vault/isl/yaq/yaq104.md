 <p><a name="page_1789"><font size="1" color="green">p. 1789</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">Sūra CIV.</h3>  <h3 align="center"><i>Humaza,</i> or the Scandal-monger.</h3>  
 <p><i>In the name of God, Most Gracious,<br> Most Merciful.</i></p>  
 <p>1. Woe to every<br> (Kind of) scandal-monger<br> And backbiter,</p>  
 <p>2. Who pileth up wealth<br> And layeth it by,</p>  
 <p>3. Thinking that his wealth<br> Would make him last<br> For ever!</p>  
 <p>4. By no means! He will<br> Be sure to be thrown into<br> That which Breaks to Pieces.</p>  
 <p>5. And what will explain<br> To thee That which Breaks<br> To Pieces?</p>  
 <p>6. (It is) the Fire<br> Of (the Wrath of) God<br> Kindled (to a blaze),</p>  
 <p>7. The which doth mount<br> (Right) to the Hearts:</p>  
 <p><a name="page_1790"><font size="1" color="green">p. 1790</font></a></p>  
 <p>8. It shall be made<br> Into a vault over them,</p>  
 <p>9. In columns outstretched.</p>
