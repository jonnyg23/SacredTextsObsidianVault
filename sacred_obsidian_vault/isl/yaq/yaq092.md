 <p><a name="page_1746"><font size="1" color="green">p. 1746</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">Sūra XCII.</h3>  <h3 align="center"><i>Lail,</i> or The Night.</h3>  
 <p><i>In the name of God, Most Gracious,<br> Most Merciful.</i></p>  
 <p>1. By the Night as it<br> Conceals (the light);</p>  
 <p>2. By the Day as it<br> Appears in glory;</p>  
 <p>3. By (the mystery of)<br> The creation of male<br> And female;—</p>  
 <p>4. Verily, (the ends) ye<br> Strive for are diverse.</p>  
 <p>5. Ṣo he who gives<br> (In charity) and fears (God),</p>  
 <p>6. And (in all sincerity)<br> Testifies to the Best,—</p>  
 <p><a name="page_1747"><font size="1" color="green">p. 1747</font></a></p>  
 <p>7. We will indeed<br> Make smooth for him<br> The path to Bliss,</p>  
 <p>8. But he who is<br> A greedy miser<br> And thinks himself<br> Self-sufficient,</p>  
 <p>9. And gives the lie<br> To the Best,—</p>  
 <p>10. We will indeed<br> Make smooth for him<br> The Path to Misery;</p>  
 <p>11. Nor will his wealth<br> Profit him when he<br> Falls headlong (into the Pit).</p>  
 <p>12. Verily We take<br> Upon Ourselves to guide,</p>  
 <p>13. And verily unto Us<br> (Belong) the End<br> And the Beginning,</p>  
 <p><a name="page_1748"><font size="1" color="green">p. 1748</font></a></p>  
 <p>14. Therefore do I warn you<br> Of a Fire blazing fiercely;</p>  
 <p>15. None shall reach it<br> But those most unfortunate ones</p>  
 <p>16. Who give the lie to Truth<br> And turn their backs.</p>  
 <p>17. But those most devoted<br> To God shall be<br> Removed far from it,—</p>  
 <p>18. Those who spend their wealth<br> For increase in self-purification,</p>  
 <p>19. And have in their minds<br> No favour from anyone<br> For which a reward<br> Is expected in return,</p>  
 <p><a name="page_1749"><font size="1" color="green">p. 1749</font></a></p>  
 <p>20. But only the desire<br> To seek for the Countenance<br> Of their Lord Most High;</p>  
 <p>21. And soon will they<br> Attain (complete) satisfaction.</p>
