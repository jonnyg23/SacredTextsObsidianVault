 <p><a name="page_1798"><font size="1" color="green">p. 1798</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">Sūra CVIII.</h3>  <h3 align="center"><i>Kau<u>th</u>ar</i>, or Abundance.</h3>  
 <p><i>In the name of God, Most Gracious,<br> Most Merciful.</i></p>  
 <p>1. To thee have We<br> Granted the Fount<br> (of Abundance).</p>  
 <p>2. Therefore to thy Lord<br> Turn in Prayer<br> And Sacrifice.</p>  
 <p>3. For he who hateth thee,—<br> <i>He</i> will be cut off<br> (From Future Hope).</p>
