 <p><a name="page_1777"><font size="1" color="green">p. 1777</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">Sūra CI.</h3>  <h3 align="center"><i>Al-Qāri’a,</i> or The Day of Noise and Clamour.</h3>  
 <p><i>In the name of God, Most Gracious,<br> Most Merciful.</i></p>  
 <p>1. The (Day) of<br> Noise and Clamour:</p>  
 <p>2. What is the (Day)<br> Of Noise and Clamour?</p>  
 <p>3. And what will explain<br> To thee what the (Day)<br> Of Noise and Clamour is?</p>  
 <p>4. (It is) a Day whereon<br> Men will be like moths<br> Scattered about,</p>  
 <p>5. And the mountains<br> Will be like carded wool.</p>  
 <p>6. Then, he whose<br> Balance (of good deeds)<br> Will be (found) heavy,</p>  
 <p><a name="page_1778"><font size="1" color="green">p. 1778</font></a></p>  
 <p>7. Will be in a Life<br> Of good pleasure and<br> satisfaction.</p>  
 <p>8. But he whose<br> Balance (of good deeds)<br> Will be (found) light,—</p>  
 <p>9. Will have his home<br> In a (bottomless) Pit.</p>  
 <p>10. And what will explain<br> To thee what this is?</p>  
 <p>11. (It is) a Fire<br> Blazing fiercely!</p>
