 <p><a name="page_1780"><font size="1" color="green">p. 1780</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">Sūra CII.</h3>  <h3 align="center"><i>Taka<u>th</u>ur</i> or Piling Up.</h3>  
 <p><i>In the name of God, Most Gracious,<br> Most Merciful.</i></p>  
 <p>1. The mutual rivalry<br> For piling up (the good things<br> Of this world) diverts you<br> (From the more serious things),</p>  
 <p>2. Until ye visit the graves.</p>  
 <p>3. But nay, ye soon shall<br> Know (the reality).</p>  
 <p>4. Again, ye soon shall know!</p>  
 <p>5. Nay, were ye to know<br> With certainty of mind,<br> (Ye would beware!)</p>  
 <p>6. Ye shall certainly see<br> Hell-fire!</p>  
 <p>7. Again, ye shall see it<br> With certainty of sight!</p>  
 <p><a name="page_1781"><font size="1" color="green">p. 1781</font></a></p>  
 <p>8. Then, shall ye be<br> Questioned that Day<br> About the joy<br> (Ye indulged in!)</p>
