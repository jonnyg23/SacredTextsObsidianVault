 <p><a name="page_1804"><font size="1" color="green">p. 1804</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">Sūra CXI.</h3>  <h3 align="center"><i>Lahab,</i> or (the Father of) Flame.</h3>  
 <p><i>In the name of God, Most Gracious,<br> Most Merciful.</i></p>  
 <p>1. Perish the hands<br> Of the Father of Flame!<br> Perish he!</p>  
 <p>2. No profit to him<br> From all his wealth,<br> And all his gains!</p>  
 <p>3. Burnt soon will he be<br> In a Fire<br> Of blazing Flame!</p>  
 <p>4. His wife shall carry<br> The (crackling) wood—<br> As fuel!—</p>  
 <p>5. A twisted rope<br> Of palm-leaf fibre<br> Round her (own) neck!</p>
