 <p><a name="page_1808"><font size="1" color="green">p. 1808</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">Sūra CXIII</h3>  <h3 align="center"><i>Falaq,</i> or The Dawn.</h3>  
 <p><i>In the name of God, Most Gracious,<br> Most Merciful.</i></p>  
 <p>1. Say: I seek refuge<br> With the Lord of the Dawn,</p>  
 <p>2. From the mischief<br> Of created things;</p>  
 <p>3. From the mischief<br> Of Darkness as it overspreads;</p>  
 <p>4. From the mischief<br> Of those who practise<br> Secret Arts:</p>  
 <p>5. And from the mischief<br> Of the envious one<br> As he practises envy.</p>
