 <p><a name="page_1719"><font size="1" color="green">p. 1719</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">Sūra LXXXVI.</h3>  <h3 align="center"><i>Ṭāriq,</i> or The Night-Visitant.</h3>  
 <p><i>In the name of God, Most Gracious,<br> Most Merciful.</i></p>  
 <p>1. By the Sky<br> And the Night-Visitant<br> (Therein);—</p>  
 <p>2. And what will explain to thee<br> What the Night-Visitant is?—</p>  
 <p>3. (It is) the Star<br> Of piercing brightness;—</p>  
 <p>4. There is no soul but has<br> A protector over it.</p>  
 <p>5. Now let man but think<br> From what he is created!</p>  
 <p>6. He is created from<br> A drop emitted—</p>  
 <p>7. Proceeding from between<br> The backbone and the ribs:</p>  
 <p><a name="page_1720"><font size="1" color="green">p. 1720</font></a></p>  
 <p>8. Surely (God) is able<br> To bring him back<br> (To life)!</p>  
 <p>9. The Day that<br> (All) things secret<br> Will be tested,</p>  
 <p>10. (Man) will have<br> No power,<br> And no helper.</p>  
 <p>11. By the Firmament<br> Which returns (in its round),</p>  
 <p>12. And by the Earth<br> Which opens out<br> (For the gushing of springs<br> Or the sprouting of vegetation),—</p>  
 <p>13. Behold this is the Word<br> That distinguishes (Good<br> From Evil):</p>  
 <p>14. It is not a thing<br> For amusement.</p>  
 <p><a name="page_1721"><font size="1" color="green">p. 1721</font></a></p>  
 <p>15. As for them, they<br> Are but plotting a scheme,</p>  
 <p>16. And I am planning<br> A scheme.</p>  
 <p>17. Therefore grant a delay<br> To the unbelievers:<br> Give respite to them<br> Gently (for awhile).</p>
