 <p><a name="page_1806"><font size="1" color="green">p. 1806</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">Sūra CXII.</h3>  <h3 align="center"><i>I<u>kh</u>lāṣ</i>, or Purity (of Faith).</h3>  
 <p><i>In the name of God, Most Gracious,<br> Most Merciful.</i></p>  
 <p>1. Say: He is God,<br> The One and Only;</p>  
 <p>2. God, the Eternal, Absolute;</p>  
 <p>3. He begetteth not,<br> Nor is He begotten;</p>  
 <p>4. And there is none<br> Like unto Him.</p>
