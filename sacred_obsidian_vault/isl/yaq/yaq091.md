 <p><a name="page_1742"><font size="1" color="green">p. 1742</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">Sūra XCI.</h3>  <h3 align="center"><i>Shams,</i> or The Sun.</h3>  
 <p><i>In the name of God, Most Gracious,<br> Most Merciful.</i></p>  
 <p>1. By the Sun<br> And his (glorious) splendour;</p>  
 <p>2. By the Moon<br> As she follows him;</p>  
 <p>3. By the Day as it<br> Shows up (the Sun's) glory;</p>  
 <p>4. By the Night as it<br> Conceals it;</p>  
 <p>5. By the Firmament<br> And its (wonderful) structure;</p>  
 <p><a name="page_1743"><font size="1" color="green">p. 1743</font></a></p>  
 <p>6. By the Earth<br> And its (wide) expanse;</p>  
 <p>7. By the Soul,<br> And the proportion and order<br> Given to it;</p>  
 <p>8. And its enlightenment<br> As to its wrong<br> And its right;—</p>  
 <p>9. Truly he succeeds<br> That purifies it,</p>  
 <p>10. And he fails<br> That corrupts it!</p>  
 <p>11. The <u>Th</u>amūd (people)<br> Rejected (their prophet)<br> Through their inordinate<br> Wrong-doing.</p>  
 <p>12. Behold, the most wicked<br> Man among them was<br> Deputed (for impiety).</p>  
 <p>13. But the apostle of God<br> Said to them: "It is</p>  
 <p><a name="page_1744"><font size="1" color="green">p. 1744</font></a></p>  
 <p>A She-camel of God!<br> And (bar her not<br> From) having her drink!"</p>  
 <p>14. Then they rejected him<br> (As a false prophet),<br> And they hamstrung her.<br> So their Lord, on account<br> Of their crime, obliterated<br> Their traces and made them<br> Equal (in destruction,<br> High and low)!</p>  
 <p>15. And for Him<br> Is no fear<br> Of its consequences.</p>
