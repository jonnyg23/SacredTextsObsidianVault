 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra LXV.: Ṭalāq, or Divorce.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 2</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/065/065_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  Wakaayyin min qaryatin AAatat AAan amri rabbih<u>a</u> warusulihi fa<u>ha</u>sabn<u>a</u>h<u>a</u> <u>h</u>is<u>a</u>ban shadeedan waAAa<u>thth</u>abn<u>a</u>h<u>a</u> AAa<u>tha</u>ban nukr<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>8.  How many populations<br> That insolently opposed<br> The command of their Lord<br> And of His apostles,<br> Did We not then<br> Call to account,<br> To severe account?<br> And We imposed on them<br> An exemplary Punishment.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/065/065_009.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>9.   Fa<u>tha</u>qat wab<u>a</u>la amrih<u>a</u> wak<u>a</u>na AA<u>a</u>qibatu amrih<u>a</u> khusr<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>9.  Then did they taste<br> The evil result of<br> Their conduct, and the End<br> Of their conduct<br> Was Perdition.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/065/065_010.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>10.  aAAadda All<u>a</u>hu lahum AAa<u>tha</u>ban shadeedan fa<b>i</b>ttaqoo All<u>a</u>ha y<u>a</u> olee al-alb<u>a</u>bi alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo qad anzala All<u>a</u>hu ilaykum <u>th</u>ikr<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>10.  God has prepared for them<br> A severe Punishment<br> (In the Hereafter).<br> Therefore fear God,<br> O ye men of understanding—<br> Who have believed!<br> For God hath indeed<br> Sent down to you<br> A Message,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/065/065_011.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>11.  Rasoolan yatloo AAalaykum <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>ti All<u>a</u>hi mubayyin<u>a</u>tin liyukhrija alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo waAAamiloo a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>li<u>ha</u>ti mina a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i>ulum<u>a</u>ti il<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nnoori waman yu/min bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi wayaAAmal <u>sa</u>li<u>h</u>an yudkhilhu jann<u>a</u>tin tajree min ta<u>h</u>tih<u>a</u> al-anh<u>a</u>ru kh<u>a</u>lideena feeh<u>a</u> abadan qad a<u>h</u>sana All<u>a</u>hu lahu rizq<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>11.  An Apostle, who rehearses<br> To you the Signs of God<br> Containing clear explanations,<br> That he may lead forth<br> Those who believe<br> And do righteous deeds<br>From the depths of Darkness<br> Into Light. And those who<br> Believe in God and work<br> Righteousness, He will admit<br> To Gardens beneath which rivers<br> Flow, to dwell therein<br> For ever: God has indeed<br> Granted for them<br> A most excellent provision.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/065/065_012.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>12.  All<u>a</u>hu alla<u>th</u>ee khalaqa sabAAa sam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>tin wamina al-ar<u>d</u>i mithlahunna yatanazzalu al-amru baynahunna litaAAlamoo anna All<u>a</u>ha AAal<u>a</u> kulli shay-in qadeerun waanna All<u>a</u>ha qad a<u>hat</u>a bikulli shay-in AAilm<u>a</u><b>n</b>    </p>
 <p>12.  God is He Who<br> Created seven Firmaments<br> And of the earth<br> A similar number.<br> Through the midst<br> Of them (all) descends<br> His Command: that ye may<br> Know that God has power<br> Over all things, and that<br> God comprehends all things<br> In (His) Knowledge.</p>  <div class="filenav">
