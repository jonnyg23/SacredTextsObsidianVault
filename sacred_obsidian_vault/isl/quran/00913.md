 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra IX.: Tauba (Repentance) or Barāat (Immunity).</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 13</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_100.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>100.  Wa<b>al</b>ss<u>a</u>biqoona al-awwaloona mina almuh<u>a</u>jireena wa<b>a</b>l-an<u>sa</u>ri wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena ittabaAAoohum bi-i<u>h</u>s<u>a</u>nin ra<u>d</u>iya All<u>a</u>hu AAanhum wara<u>d</u>oo AAanhu waaAAadda lahum jann<u>a</u>tin tajree ta<u>h</u>tah<u>a</u> al-anh<u>a</u>ru kh<u>a</u>lideena feeh<u>a</u> abadan <u>tha</u>lika alfawzu alAAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>100.  The vanguard (of Islam)—<br> The first of those who forsook<br> (Their homes) and of those<br> Who gave them aid, and (also)<br> Those who follow them<br> In (all) good deeds,—<br> Well-pleased is God with them,<br> As are they with Him:<br>For them hath He prepared<br> Gardens under which rivers flow,<br> To dwell therein for ever:<br> That is the supreme Felicity.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_101.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>101.  Wamimman <u>h</u>awlakum mina al-aAAr<u>a</u>bi mun<u>a</u>fiqoona wamin ahli almadeenati maradoo AAal<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nnif<u>a</u>qi l<u>a</u> taAAlamuhum na<u>h</u>nu naAAlamuhum sanuAAa<u>thth</u>ibuhum marratayni thumma yuraddoona il<u>a</u> AAa<u>tha</u>bin AAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>101.  Certain of the desert Arabs<br> Round about you are Hypocrites,<br> As well as (desert Arabs) among<br> The Medina folk: they are<br> Obstinate in hypocrisy: thou<br> Knowest them not: We know them:<br> Twice shall We punish them:<br> And in addition shall they be<br> Sent to a grievous Penalty.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_102.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>102.  Wa<u>a</u>kharoona iAAtarafoo bi<u>th</u>unoobihim khala<u>t</u>oo AAamalan <u>sa</u>li<u>h</u>an wa<u>a</u>khara sayyi-an AAas<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hu an yatooba AAalayhim inna All<u>a</u>ha ghafoorun ra<u>h</u>eem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>102.  Others (there are who) have<br> Acknowledged their wrong-doings:<br> They have mixed an act<br> That was good with another<br> That was evil. Perhaps God<br> Will turn unto them (in mercy):<br> For God is Oft-Forgiving,<br> Most Merciful.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_103.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>103.  Khu<u>th</u> min amw<u>a</u>lihim <u>s</u>adaqatan tu<u>t</u>ahhiruhum watuzakkeehim bih<u>a</u> wa<u>s</u>alli AAalayhim inna <u>s</u>al<u>a</u>taka sakanun lahum wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu sameeAAun AAaleem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>103.  Of their goods take alms,<br> That so thou mightest<br> Purify and sanctify them;<br> And pray on their behalf.<br> Verily thy prayers are a source<br> Of security for them:<br> And God is One<br> Who heareth and knoweth.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_104.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>104.  Alam yaAAlamoo anna All<u>a</u>ha huwa yaqbalu a<b>l</b>ttawbata AAan AAib<u>a</u>dihi waya/khu<u>th</u>u a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>adaq<u>a</u>ti waanna All<u>a</u>ha huwa a<b>l</b>ttaww<u>a</u>bu a<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>eem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>104.  Know they not that God<br> Doth accept repentance from<br> His votaries and receives<br> Their gifts of charity, and that<br> God is verily He,<br> The Oft-Returning, Most Merciful?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_105.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>105.  Waquli iAAmaloo fasayar<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hu AAamalakum warasooluhu wa<b>a</b>lmu/minoona wasaturaddoona il<u>a</u> AA<u>a</u>limi alghaybi wa<b>al</b>shshah<u>a</u>dati fayunabbi-okum bim<u>a</u> kuntum taAAmaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>105.  And say: "Work (righteousness):<br> Soon will God observe your work,<br> And His Apostle, and the Believers:<br> Soon will ye be brought back<br> To the Knower of what is<br> Hidden and what is open:<br> Then will He show you<br> The truth of all that ye did."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_106.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>106.  Wa<u>a</u>kharoona murjawna li-amri All<u>a</u>hi imm<u>a</u> yuAAa<u>thth</u>ibuhum wa-imm<u>a</u> yatoobu AAalayhim wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu AAaleemun <u>h</u>akeem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>106.  There are (yet) others,<br> Held in suspense for the command<br> Of God, whether He will<br> Punish them, or turn in mercy<br> To them: and God<br> Is All-Knowing, Wise.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_107.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>107.  Wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena ittakha<u>th</u>oo masjidan <u>d</u>ir<u>a</u>ran wakufran watafreeqan bayna almu/mineena wa-ir<u>sa</u>dan liman <u>ha</u>raba All<u>a</u>ha warasoolahu min qablu walaya<u>h</u>lifunna in aradn<u>a</u> ill<u>a</u> al<u>h</u>usn<u>a</u> wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu yashhadu innahum lak<u>ath</u>iboon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>107.  And there are those<br> Who put up a mosque<br>By way of mischief and infidelity—<br> To disunite the Believers<br> And in preparation for one<br> Who warred against God<br> And His Apostle aforetime.<br> They will indeed swear<br> That their intention is nothing<br> But good; but God doth declare<br> That they are certainly liars.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_108.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>108.  L<u>a</u> taqum feehi abadan lamasjidun ossisa AAal<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>ttaqw<u>a</u> min awwali yawmin a<u>h</u>aqqu an taqooma feehi feehi rij<u>a</u>lun yu<u>h</u>ibboona an yata<u>t</u>ahharoo wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu yu<u>h</u>ibbu almu<u>tt</u>ahhireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>108.  Never stand thou forth therein.<br> There is a mosque whose foundation<br> Was laid from the first day<br> On piety; it is more worthy<br> Of thy standing forth (for prayer)<br> Therein. In it are men who<br> Love to be purified; and God<br> Loveth those who make themselves pure.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_109.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>109.  Afaman assasa buny<u>a</u>nahu AAal<u>a</u> taqw<u>a</u> mina All<u>a</u>hi wari<u>d</u>w<u>a</u>nin khayrun am man assasa buny<u>a</u>nahu AAal<u>a</u> shaf<u>a</u> jurufin h<u>a</u>rin fa<b>i</b>nh<u>a</u>ra bihi fee n<u>a</u>ri jahannama wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu l<u>a</u> yahdee alqawma a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>109.  Which then is best?—he that<br> Layeth his foundation<br> On piety to God<br> And His Good Pleasure?—or he<br> That layeth his foundation<br> On an undermined sand-cliff<br> Ready to crumble to pieces?<br> And it doth crumble to pieces<br> With him, into the fire<br> Of Hell. And God guideth not<br> People that do wrong.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_110.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>110.  L<u>a</u> yaz<u>a</u>lu buny<u>a</u>nuhumu alla<u>th</u>ee banaw reebatan fee quloobihim ill<u>a</u> an taqa<u>tt</u>aAAa quloobuhum wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu AAaleemun <u>h</u>akeem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>110.  The foundation of those<br> Who so build is never free<br>From suspicion and shakiness<br> In their hearts, until<br> Their hearts are cut to pieces.<br> And God is All-Knowing, Wise.</p> <div class="filenav">
