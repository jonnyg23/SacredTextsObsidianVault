 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra V.: Māïda, or The Table Spread.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 10</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_067.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>67.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>rrasoolu balligh m<u>a</u> onzila ilayka min rabbika wa-in lam tafAAal fam<u>a</u> ballaghta ris<u>a</u>latahu wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu yaAA<u>s</u>imuka mina a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si inna All<u>a</u>ha l<u>a</u> yahdee alqawma alk<u>a</u>fireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>67.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">70</font></span> O Apostle! proclaim<br> The (Message) which hath been<br> Sent to thee from thy Lord.<br> If thou didst not, thou<br>Wouldst not have fulfilled<br> And proclaimed His Mission.<br> And God will defend thee<br> From men (who mean mischief).<br> For God guideth not<br> Those who reject Faith.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_068.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>68.  Qul y<u>a</u> ahla alkit<u>a</u>bi lastum AAal<u>a</u> shay-in <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> tuqeemoo a<b>l</b>ttawr<u>a</u>ta wa<b>a</b>l-injeela wam<u>a</u> onzila ilaykum min rabbikum walayazeedanna katheeran minhum m<u>a</u> onzila ilayka min rabbika <u>t</u>ughy<u>a</u>nan wakufran fal<u>a</u> ta/sa AAal<u>a</u> alqawmi alk<u>a</u>fireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>68.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">71</font></span> Say: "O People of the Book!<br> Ye have no ground<br> To stand upon unless<br> Ye stand fast by the Law,<br> The Gospel, and all the revelation<br> That has come to you from<br> Your Lord." It is the revelation<br> That cometh to thee from<br> Thy Lord, that increaseth in most<br> Of them their obstinate<br> Rebellion and blasphemy.<br> But sorrow thou not<br> Over (these) people without Faith.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_069.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>69.  Inna alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena h<u>a</u>doo wa<b>al</b><u>ssa</u>bi-oona wa<b>al</b>nna<u>sa</u>r<u>a</u> man <u>a</u>mana bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi wa<b>a</b>lyawmi al-<u>a</u>khiri waAAamila <u>sa</u>li<u>h</u>an fal<u>a</u> khawfun AAalayhim wal<u>a</u> hum ya<u>h</u>zanoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>69.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">72</font></span> Those who believe (in the Qur-ān),<br> Those who follow the Jewish (scriptures),<br> And the Sabians and the Christians,—<br> Any who believe in God<br> And the Last Day,<br> And work righteousness,—<br> On them shall be no fear,<br> Nor shall they grieve.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_070.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>70.  Laqad akha<u>th</u>n<u>a</u> meeth<u>a</u>qa banee isr<u>a</u>-eela waarsaln<u>a</u> ilayhim rusulan kullam<u>a</u> j<u>a</u>ahum rasoolun bim<u>a</u> l<u>a</u> tahw<u>a</u> anfusuhum fareeqan ka<u>thth</u>aboo wafareeqan yaqtuloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>70.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">73</font></span> We took the Covenant<br> Of the Children of Israel<br> And sent them apostles.<br> Every time there came<br> To them an apostle<br> With what they themselves<br> Desired not—some<br> (Of these) they called<br>Impostors, and some they<br> (Go so far as to) slay.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_071.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>71.  Wa<u>h</u>asiboo all<u>a</u> takoona fitnatun faAAamoo wa<u>s</u>ammoo thumma t<u>a</u>ba All<u>a</u>hu AAalayhim thumma AAamoo wa<u>s</u>ammoo katheerun minhum wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu ba<u>s</u>eerun bim<u>a</u> yaAAmaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>71.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">74</font></span> They thought there would be<br> No trial (or punishment);<br> So they became blind and deaf;<br> Yet God (in mercy) turned<br> To them; yet again many<br> Of them became blind and deaf.<br> But God sees well<br> All that they do.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_072.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>72.  Laqad kafara alla<u>th</u>eena q<u>a</u>loo inna All<u>a</u>ha huwa almasee<u>h</u>u ibnu maryama waq<u>a</u>la almasee<u>h</u>u y<u>a</u> banee isr<u>a</u>-eela oAAbudoo All<u>a</u>ha rabbee warabbakum innahu man yushrik bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi faqad <u>h</u>arrama All<u>a</u>hu AAalayhi aljannata wama/w<u>a</u>hu a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>ru wam<u>a</u> li<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limeena min an<u>sa</u>r<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>72.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">75</font></span> They do blaspheme who say:<br> "God is Christ the son<br> Of Mary" But said Christ:<br> "O Children of Israel!<br> Worship God, my Lord<br> And your Lord." Whoever<br> Joins other gods with God,—<br> God will forbid him<br> The Garden, and the Fire<br> Will be his abode. There will<br> For the wrong-doers<br> Be no one to help.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_073.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>73.  Laqad kafara alla<u>th</u>eena q<u>a</u>loo inna All<u>a</u>ha th<u>a</u>lithu thal<u>a</u>thatin wam<u>a</u> min il<u>a</u>hin ill<u>a</u> il<u>a</u>hun w<u>ah</u>idun wa-in lam yantahoo AAamm<u>a</u> yaqooloona layamassanna alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo minhum AAa<u>tha</u>bun aleem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>73.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">76</font></span> They do blaspheme who say:<br> God is one of three<br> In a Trinity: for there is<br> No god except One God.<br> If they desist not<br> From their word (of blasphemy),<br> Verily a grievous penalty<br> Will befall the blasphemers<br> Among them.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_074.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>74.  Afal<u>a</u> yatooboona il<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi wayastaghfiroonahu wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu ghafoorun ra<u>h</u>eem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>74.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">77</font></span> Why turn they not to God,<br> And seek His forgiveness?<br> For God is Oft-forgiving,<br> Most Merciful.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_075.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>75.  M<u>a</u> almasee<u>h</u>u ibnu maryama ill<u>a</u> rasoolun qad khalat min qablihi a<b>l</b>rrusulu waommuhu <u>s</u>iddeeqatun k<u>a</u>n<u>a</u> ya/kul<u>a</u>ni a<b>l</b><u>tt</u>aAA<u>a</u>ma on<i><u>th</u></i>ur kayfa nubayyinu lahumu al-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>ti thumma on<i><u>th</u></i>ur ann<u>a</u> yu/fakoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>75.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">78</font></span> Christ the son of Mary<br> Was no more than<br> An Apostle; many were<br> The apostles that passed away<br>Before him. His mother<br> Was a woman of truth.<br> They had both to eat<br> Their (daily) food.<br> See how God doth make<br> His Signs clear to them;<br> Yet see in what ways<br> They are deluded<br> Away from the truth!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_076.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>76.  Qul ataAAbudoona min dooni All<u>a</u>hi m<u>a</u> l<u>a</u> yamliku lakum <u>d</u>arran wal<u>a</u> nafAAan wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu huwa a<b>l</b>ssameeAAu alAAaleem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>76.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">79</font></span> Say: "Will ye worship,<br> Besides God, something<br> Which hath no power either<br> To harm or benefit you?<br> But God,—He it is<br> That heareth and knoweth<br> All things."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_077.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>77.  Qul y<u>a</u> ahla alkit<u>a</u>bi l<u>a</u> taghloo fee deenikum ghayra al<u>h</u>aqqi wal<u>a </u>tattabiAAoo ahw<u>a</u>a qawmin qad <u>d</u>alloo min qablu waa<u>d</u>alloo katheeran wa<u>d</u>alloo AAan saw<u>a</u>-i a<b>l</b>ssabeel<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>77.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">80</font></span> Say: "O People of the Book!<br> Exceed not in your religion<br> The bounds (of what is proper),<br> Trespassing beyond the truth,<br> Nor follow the vain desires<br> Of people who went wrong<br> In times gone by,—who misled<br> Many, and strayed (themselves)<br> From the even Way.</p> <div class="filenav">
