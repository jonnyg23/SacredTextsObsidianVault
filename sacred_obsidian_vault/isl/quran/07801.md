 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra LXXVIII.: Nabaa, or The (Great) News.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/078/078_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  AAamma yatas<u>a</u>aloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>1.  Concerning what<br> Are they disputing?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/078/078_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  AAani a<b>l</b>nnaba-i alAAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>2.  Concerning the Great News,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/078/078_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  Alla<u>th</u>ee hum feehi mukhtalifoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>3.  About which they<br> Cannot agree.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/078/078_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  Kall<u>a</u> sayaAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>4.  Verily, they shall soon<br> (Come to) know!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/078/078_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  Thumma kall<u>a</u> sayaAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>5.  Verily, verily they shall<br> Soon (come to) know!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/078/078_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  Alam najAAali al-ar<u>d</u>a mih<u>a</u>d<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>6.  Have We not made<br> The earth as a wide<br> Expanse,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/078/078_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  Wa<b>a</b>ljib<u>a</u>la awt<u>a</u>d<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>7.  And the mountains as pegs?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/078/078_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  Wakhalaqn<u>a</u>kum azw<u>a</u>j<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>8.  And (have We not) created<br> You in pairs,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/078/078_009.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>9.  WajaAAaln<u>a</u> nawmakum sub<u>a</u>t<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>9.  And made your sleep<br> For rest,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/078/078_010.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>10.  WajaAAaln<u>a</u> allayla lib<u>a</u>s<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>10.  And made the night<br> As a covering,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/078/078_011.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>11.  WajaAAaln<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nnah<u>a</u>ra maAA<u>a</u>sh<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>11.  And made the day<br> As a means of subsistence?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/078/078_012.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>12.  Wabanayn<u>a</u> fawqakum sabAAan shid<u>a</u>d<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>12.  And (have We not)<br> Built over you<br> The seven firmaments,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/078/078_013.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>13.  WajaAAaln<u>a</u> sir<u>a</u>jan wahh<u>a</u>j<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>13.  And placed (therein)<br> A Light of Splendour?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/078/078_014.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>14.  Waanzaln<u>a</u> mina almuAA<u>s</u>ir<u>a</u>ti m<u>a</u>an thajj<u>a</u>j<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>14.  And do We not send down<br> From the clouds water<br> In abundance,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/078/078_015.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>15.  Linukhrija bihi <u>h</u>abban wanab<u>a</u>t<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>15.  That We may produce<br> Therewith corn and vegetables,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/078/078_016.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>16.  Wajann<u>a</u>tin alf<u>a</u>f<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>16.  And gardens of luxurious growth?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/078/078_017.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>17.  Inna yawma alfa<u>s</u>li k<u>a</u>na meeq<u>a</u>t<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>17.  Verily the Day<br> Of Sorting Out<br> Is a thing appointed,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/078/078_018.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>18.  Yawma yunfakhu fee a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>oori fata/toona afw<u>a</u>j<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>18.  The Day that the Trumpet<br> Shall be sounded, and ye<br> Shall come forth in crowds;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/078/078_019.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>19.  Wafuti<u>h</u>ati a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>o fak<u>a</u>nat abw<u>a</u>b<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>19.  And the heavens<br> Shall be opened<br> As if there were doors,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/078/078_020.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>20.  Wasuyyirati aljib<u>a</u>lu fak<u>a</u>nat sar<u>a</u>b<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>20.  And the mountains<br> Shall vanish, as if<br> They were a mirage.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/078/078_021.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>21.  Inna jahannama k<u>a</u>nat mir<u>sa</u>d<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>21.  Truly Hell is<br> As a place of ambush,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/078/078_022.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>22.  Li<b>l</b><u>tta</u>gheena ma<u>a</u>b<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>22.  For the transgressors<br> A place of destination:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/078/078_023.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>23.  L<u>a</u>bitheena feeh<u>a</u> a<u>h</u>q<u>a</u>b<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>23.  They will dwell therein<br> For ages.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/078/078_024.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>24.  L<u>a</u> ya<u>th</u>ooqoona feeh<u>a</u> bardan wal<u>a</u> shar<u>a</u>b<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>24.  Nothing cool shall they taste<br> Therein, nor any drink,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/078/078_025.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>25.  Ill<u>a</u> <u>h</u>ameeman waghass<u>a</u>q<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>25.  Save a boiling fluid<br> And a fluid, dark, murky,<br> Intensely cold,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/078/078_026.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>26.  Jaz<u>a</u>an wif<u>a</u>q<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>26.  A fitting recompense<br> (For them).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/078/078_027.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>27.  Innahum k<u>a</u>noo l<u>a</u> yarjoona <u>h</u>is<u>a</u>b<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>27.  For that they used not<br> To fear any account<br> (For their deeds),</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/078/078_028.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>28.  Waka<u>thth</u>aboo bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin<u>a</u> ki<u>ththa</u>b<u>a</u><b>n </b>   </p>
 <p>28.  But they (impudently) treated<br> Our Signs as false.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/078/078_029.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>29.  Wakulla shay-in a<u>hs</u>ayn<u>a</u>hu kit<u>a</u>b<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>29.  And all things have We<br> Preserved on record.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/078/078_030.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>30.  Fa<u>th</u>ooqoo falan nazeedakum ill<u>a</u> AAa<u>tha</u>b<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>30.  "So taste ye (the fruits<br> Of your deeds);<br> For no increase<br> Shall We grant you,<br> Except in Punishment.</p> <div class="filenav">
