 <p><a></a>21.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo l<u>a</u> tattabiAAoo khu<u>t</u>uw<u>a</u>ti a<b>l</b>shshay<u>ta</u>ni waman yattabiAA khu<u>t</u>uw<u>a</u>ti a<b>l</b>shshay<u>ta</u>ni fa-innahu ya/muru bi<b>a</b>lfa<u>h</u>sh<u>a</u>-i wa<b>a</b>lmunkari walawl<u>a</u> fa<u>d</u>lu All<u>a</u>hi AAalaykum wara<u>h</u>matuhu m<u>a</u> zak<u>a</u> minkum min a<u>h</u>adin abadan wal<u>a</u>kinna All<u>a</u>ha yuzakkee man yash<u>a</u>o wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu sameeAAun AAaleem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>21.  O ye who believe!<br> Follow not Satan's footsteps:<br> If any will follow the footsteps<br> Of Satan, he will (but) command<br> What is shameful and wrong:<br> And were it not for the grace<br> And mercy of God on you.<br> Not one of you would ever<br> Have been pure: but God<br> Doth purify whom He pleases:<br> And God is One Who<br> Hears and knows (all things).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/024/024_022.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>22.  Wal<u>a</u> ya/tali oloo alfa<u>d</u>li minkum wa<b>al</b>ssaAAati an yu/too olee alqurb<u>a</u> wa<b>a</b>lmas<u>a</u>keena wa<b>a</b>lmuh<u>a</u>jireena fee sabeeli All<u>a</u>hi walyaAAfoo walya<u>s</u>fa<u>h</u>oo al<u>a</u> tu<u>h</u>ibboona an yaghfira All<u>a</u>hu lakum wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu ghafoorun ra<u>h</u>eem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>22.  Let not those among you<br> Who are endued with grace<br> And amplitude of means<br> Resolve by oath against helping<br> Their kinsmen, those in want,<br> And those who have left<br>Their homes in God's cause:<br> Let them forgive and overlook,<br> Do you not wish<br> That God should forgive you?<br> For God is Oft-Forgiving,<br> Most Merciful.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/024/024_023.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>23.  Inna alla<u>th</u>eena yarmoona almu<u>hs</u>an<u>a</u>ti algh<u>a</u>fil<u>a</u>ti almu/min<u>a</u>ti luAAinoo fee a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> wa<b>a</b>l-<u>a</u>khirati walahum AAa<u>tha</u>bun AAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>23.  Those who slander chaste women,<br> Indiscreet but believing,<br> Are cursed in this life<br> And in the Hereafter:<br> For them is a grievous Penalty,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/024/024_024.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>24.  Yawma tashhadu AAalayhim alsinatuhum waaydeehim waarjuluhum bim<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo yaAAmaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>24.  On the Day when their tongues,<br> Their hands, and their feet<br> Will bear witness against them<br> As to their actions.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/024/024_025.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>25.  Yawma-i<u>th</u>in yuwaffeehimu All<u>a</u>hu deenahumu al<u>h</u>aqqa wayaAAlamoona anna All<u>a</u>ha huwa al<u>h</u>aqqu almubeen<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>25.  On that Day God<br> Will pay them back<br> (All) their just dues,<br> And they will realise<br> That God is<br> The (very) Truth,<br> That makes all things manifest.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/024/024_026.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>26.  Alkhabeeth<u>a</u>tu lilkhabeetheena wa<b>a</b>lkhabeethoona lilkhabeeth<u>a</u>ti wa<b>al</b><u>tt</u>ayyib<u>a</u>tu li<b>l</b><u>tt</u>ayyibeena wa<b>al</b><u>tt</u>ayyiboona li<b>l</b><u>tt</u>ayyib<u>a</u>ti ol<u>a</u>-ika mubarraoona mimm<u>a</u> yaqooloona lahum maghfiratun warizqun kareem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>26.  Women impure are for men impure<br> And men impure for women impure<br> And women of purity<br> Are for men of purity,<br> And men of purity<br> Are for women of purity:<br> These are not affected<br>By what people say:<br> For them there is forgiveness,<br> And a provision honourable.</p> <div class="filenav">
