 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra LVIII.: Mujādila, or The Woman who Pleads.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 2</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/058/058_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  Alam tara anna All<u>a</u>ha yaAAlamu m<u>a</u> fee a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wam<u>a</u> fee al-ar<u>d</u>i m<u>a</u> yakoonu min najw<u>a</u> thal<u>a</u>thatin ill<u>a</u> huwa r<u>a</u>biAAuhum wal<u>a</u> khamsatin ill<u>a</u> huwa s<u>a</u>disuhum wal<u>a</u> adn<u>a</u> min <u>tha</u>lika wal<u>a</u> akthara ill<u>a</u> huwa maAAahum ayna m<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo thumma yunabbi-ohum bim<u>a</u> AAamiloo yawma alqiy<u>a</u>mati inna All<u>a</u>ha bikulli shay-in AAaleem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>7.  Seest thou not that<br> God doth know (all) that is<br> In the heavens and<br> On earth? There is not<br> A secret consultation<br> Between three, but He<br> Makes the fourth among them,—<br> Nor between five but<br>He makes the sixth,—<br> Nor between fewer nor more,<br> But He is in their midst,<br> Wheresoever they be:<br> In the end will He<br> Tell them the truth<br> Of their conduct, on the Day<br> Of Judgment. For God<br> Has full knowledge<br> Of all things.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/058/058_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  Alam tara il<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena nuhoo AAani a<b>l</b>nnajw<u>a</u> thumma yaAAoodoona lim<u>a</u> nuhoo AAanhu wayatan<u>a</u>jawna bi<b>a</b>l-ithmi wa<b>a</b>lAAudw<u>a</u>ni wamaAA<u>s</u>iyati a<b>l</b>rrasooli wa-i<u>tha</u> j<u>a</u>ooka <u>h</u>ayyawka bim<u>a</u> lam yu<u>h</u>ayyika bihi All<u>a</u>hu wayaqooloona fee anfusihim lawl<u>a</u> yuAAa<u>thth</u>ibun<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hu bim<u>a</u> naqoolu <u>h</u>asbuhum jahannamu ya<u>s</u>lawnah<u>a</u> fabi/sa alma<u>s</u>eer<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>8.  Turnest thou not thy sight<br> Towards those who were<br> Forbidden secret counsels<br> Yet revert to that which<br> They were forbidden (to do)?<br> And they hold secret counsels<br> Among themselves for iniquity<br> And hostility, and disobedience<br> To the Apostle. And when<br> They come to thee,<br> They salute thee,<br> Not as God salutes thee,<br> (But in crooked ways):<br> And they say to themselves,<br> "Why does not God<br> Punish us for our words?"<br> Enough for them is Hell:<br> In it will they burn,<br> And evil is that destination!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/058/058_009.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>9.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo i<u>tha</u> tan<u>a</u>jaytum fal<u>a</u> tatan<u>a</u>jaw bi<b>a</b>l-ithmi wa<b>a</b>lAAudw<u>a</u>ni wamaAA<u>s</u>iyati a<b>l</b>rrasooli watan<u>a</u>jaw bi<b>a</b>lbirri wa<b>al</b>ttaqw<u>a</u> wa<b>i</b>ttaqoo All<u>a</u>ha alla<u>th</u>ee ilayhi tu<u>h</u>sharoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>9.  O ye who believe!<br> When ye hold secret counsel,<br> Do it not for iniquity<br> And hostility, and disobedience<br>To the Prophet; but do it<br> For righteousness and self-restraint;<br> And fear God, to Whom<br> Ye shall be brought back.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/058/058_010.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>10.  Innam<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nnajw<u>a</u> mina a<b>l</b>shshay<u>ta</u>ni liya<u>h</u>zuna alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo walaysa bi<u>da</u>rrihim shay-an ill<u>a</u> bi-i<u>th</u>ni All<u>a</u>hi waAAal<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi falyatawakkali almu/minoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>10.  Secret counsels are only<br> (Inspired) by the Evil One,<br> In order that he may<br> Cause grief to the Believers;<br> But he cannot harm them<br> In the least, except as<br> God permits; and on God<br> Let the Believers<br> Put their trust.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/058/058_011.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>11.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo i<u>tha</u> qeela lakum tafassa<u>h</u>oo fee almaj<u>a</u>lisi fa<b>i</b>fsa<u>h</u>oo yafsa<u>h</u>i All<u>a</u>hu lakum wa-i<u>tha</u> qeela onshuzoo fa<b>o</b>nshuzoo yarfaAAi All<u>a</u>hu alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo minkum wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena ootoo alAAilma daraj<u>a</u>tin wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu bim<u>a</u> taAAmaloona khabeer<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>11.  O ye who believe!<br> When ye are told<br> To make room<br> In the assemblies,<br> (Spread out and) make room:<br> (Ample) room will God provide<br> For you. And when<br> Ye are told to rise up,<br> Rise up: God will<br> Raise up, to (suitable) ranks<br> (And degrees), those of you<br> Who believe and who have<br>Been granted (mystic) Knowledge.<br> And God is well-acquainted<br> With all ye do.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/058/058_012.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>12.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo i<u>tha</u> n<u>a</u>jaytumu a<b>l</b>rrasoola faqaddimoo bayna yaday najw<u>a</u>kum <u>s</u>adaqatan <u>tha</u>lika khayrun lakum waa<u>t</u>haru fa-in lam tajidoo fa-inna All<u>a</u>ha ghafoorun ra<u>h</u>eem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>12.  O ye who believe<br> When ye consult<br> The Apostle in private,<br> Spend something in charity<br> Before your private consultation.<br> That will be best for you,<br> And most conducive<br> To purity (of conduct).<br> But if ye find not<br> (The wherewithal), God is<br> Oft-Forgiving, Most Merciful.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/058/058_013.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>13.  Aashfaqtum an tuqaddimoo bayna yaday najw<u>a</u>kum <u>s</u>adaq<u>a</u>tin fa-i<u>th</u> lam tafAAaloo wat<u>a</u>ba All<u>a</u>hu AAalaykum faaqeemoo a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>al<u>a</u>ta wa<u>a</u>too a<b>l</b>zzak<u>a</u>ta waa<u>t</u>eeAAoo All<u>a</u>ha warasoolahu wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu khabeerun bim<u>a</u> taAAmaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>13.  Is it that ye are<br> Afraid of spending sums<br> In charity before your<br> Private consultation (with him)<br>If, then, ye do not so,<br> And God forgives you,<br> Then (at least) establish<br> Regular prayer; practise<br> Regular charity; and obey<br> God and His Apostle.<br> And God is well-acquainted<br> With all that ye do.</p> <div class="filenav">
