 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra V.: Māïda, or The Table Spread.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo awfoo bi<b>a</b>lAAuqoodi o<u>h</u>illat lakum baheematu al-anAA<u>a</u>mi ill<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> yutl<u>a</u> AAalaykum ghayra mu<u>h</u>illee a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>aydi waantum <u>h</u>urumun inna All<u>a</u>ha ya<u>h</u>kumu m<u>a</u> yureed<b>u</b>    </p>
 <p>1.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">1</font></span> O ye who believe!<br> Fulfil (all) obligations<br> <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">2</font></span> Lawful unto you (for food)<br> Are all four-footed animals,<br> With the exceptions named:<br> But animals of the chase<br> Are forbidden while ye<br> Are in the Sacred Precincts<br> Or in pilgrim garb:<br> For God doth command<br> According to His Will and Plan</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo l<u>a</u> tu<u>h</u>illoo shaAA<u>a</u>-ira All<u>a</u>hi wal<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>shshahra al<u>h</u>ar<u>a</u>ma wal<u>a</u> alhadya wal<u>a</u> alqal<u>a</u>-ida wal<u>a</u> <u>a</u>mmeena albayta al<u>h</u>ar<u>a</u>ma yabtaghoona fa<u>d</u>lan min rabbihim wari<u>d</u>w<u>a</u>nan wa-i<u>tha</u> <u>h</u>alaltum fa<b>i</b><u>sta</u>doo wal<u>a</u> yajrimannakum shana<u>a</u>nu qawmin an <u>s</u>addookum AAani almasjidi al<u>h</u>ar<u>a</u>mi an taAAtadoo wataAA<u>a</u>wanoo AAal<u>a</u> albirri wa<b>al</b>ttaqw<u>a</u> wal<u>a</u> taAA<u>a</u>wanoo AAal<u>a</u> al-ithmi wa<b>a</b>lAAudw<u>a</u>ni wa<b>i</b>ttaqoo All<u>a</u>ha inna All<u>a</u>ha shadeedu alAAiq<u>a</u>b<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>2.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">3</font></span> O ye who believe!<br> Violate not the sanctity<br>Of the Symbols of God,<br> Nor of the Sacred Month,<br> Nor of the animals brought<br> For sacrifice, nor the garlands<br> That mark out such animals,<br> Nor the people resorting<br> To the Sacred House,<br> Seeking of the bounty<br> And good pleasure<br> Of their Lord.<br> But when ye are clear<br> Of the Sacred Precincts<br> And of pilgrim garb,<br> Ye may hunt<br> And let not the hatred<br> Of some people<br> In (once) shutting you out<br> Of the Sacred Mosque<br> Lead you to transgression<br> (And hostility on your part).<br> Help ye one another<br> In righteousness and piety,<br> But help ye not one another<br> In sin and rancour:<br> Fear God: for God<br> Is strict in punishment.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  <u>H</u>urrimat AAalaykumu almaytatu wa<b>al</b>ddamu wala<u>h</u>mu alkhinzeeri wam<u>a</u> ohilla lighayri All<u>a</u>hi bihi wa<b>a</b>lmunkhaniqatu wa<b>a</b>lmawqoo<u>th</u>atu wa<b>a</b>lmutaraddiyatu wa<b>al</b>nna<u>t</u>ee<u>h</u>atu wam<u>a</u> akala a<b>l</b>ssabuAAu ill<u>a</u> m<u>a th</u>akkaytum wam<u>a</u> <u>th</u>ubi<u>h</u>a AAal<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nnu<u>s</u>ubi waan tastaqsimoo bi<b>a</b>l-azl<u>a</u>mi <u>tha</u>likum fisqun alyawma ya-isa alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo min deenikum fal<u>a</u> takhshawhum wa<b>i</b>khshawni alyawma akmaltu lakum deenakum waatmamtu AAalaykum niAAmatee wara<u>d</u>eetu lakumu al-isl<u>a</u>ma deenan famani i<u>dt</u>urra fee makhma<u>s</u>atin ghayra mutaj<u>a</u>nifin li-ithmin fa-inna All<u>a</u>ha ghafoorun ra<u>h</u>eem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>3.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">4</font></span> Forbidden to you (for food)<br> Are: dead meat, blood,<br>The flesh of swine, and that<br> On which hath been invoked<br> The name of other than God;<br> That which hath been<br> Killed by strangling,<br> Or by a violent blow,<br> Or by a headlong fall,<br> Or by being gored to death;<br> That which hath been (partly)<br> Eaten by a wild animal;<br> Unless ye are able<br> To slaughter it (in due form);<br> That which is sacrificed<br> On stone (altars);<br> (Forbidden) also is the division<br> (Of meat) by raffling<br> With arrows: that is impiety.<br> This day have those who<br> Reject Faith given up<br> All hope of your religion:<br> Yet fear them not<br> But fear Me.<br> This day have I<br> Perfected your religion<br> For you, completed<br> My favour upon you,<br> And have chosen for you<br> Islam as your religion.<br> But if any is forced<br> By hunger, with no inclination<br> To transgression, God is<br> Indeed Oft-forgiving,<br> Most Merciful.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  Yas-aloonaka m<u>atha</u> o<u>h</u>illa lahum qul o<u>h</u>illa lakumu a<b>l</b><u>tt</u>ayyib<u>a</u>tu wam<u>a</u> AAallamtum mina aljaw<u>a</u>ri<u>h</u>i mukallibeena tuAAallimoonahunna mimm<u>a</u> AAallamakumu All<u>a</u>hu fakuloo mimm<u>a</u> amsakna AAalaykum wa<b>o</b><u>th</u>kuroo isma All<u>a</u>hi AAalayhi wa<b>i</b>ttaqoo All<u>a</u>ha inna All<u>a</u>ha sareeAAu al<u>h</u>is<u>a</u>b<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>4.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">5</font></span> They ask thee what is<br> Lawful to them (as food)<br> Say: Lawful unto you<br> Are (all) things good and pure:<br> And what ye have taught<br> Your trained hunting animals<br> (To catch) in the manner<br> Directed to you by God:<br> Eat what they catch for you,<br> But pronounce the name<br> Of God over it: and fear<br> God; for God is swift<br> In taking account.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  Alyawma o<u>h</u>illa lakumu a<b>l</b><u>tt</u>ayyib<u>a</u>tu wa<u>t</u>aAA<u>a</u>mu alla<u>th</u>eena ootoo alkit<u>a</u>ba <u>h</u>illun lakum wa<u>t</u>aAA<u>a</u>mukum <u>h</u>illun lahum wa<b>a</b>lmu<u>hs</u>an<u>a</u>tu mina almu/min<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>lmu<u>hs</u>an<u>a</u>tu mina alla<u>th</u>eena ootoo alkit<u>a</u>ba min qablikum i<u>tha</u> <u>a</u>taytumoohunna ojoorahunna mu<u>hs</u>ineena ghayra mus<u>a</u>fi<u>h</u>eena wal<u>a</u> muttakhi<u>th</u>ee akhd<u>a</u>nin waman yakfur bi<b>a</b>l-eem<u>a</u>ni faqad <u>h</u>abi<u>t</u>a AAamaluhu wahuwa fee al-<u>a</u>khirati mina alkh<u>a</u>sireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>5.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">6</font></span> This day are (all) things<br> Good and pure made lawful<br> Unto you. The food<br> Of the People of the Book<br> Is lawful unto you<br> And yours is lawful<br> Unto them.<br> (Lawful unto you in marriage)<br> Are (not only) chaste women<br> Who are believers, but<br> Chaste women among<br> The People of the Book,<br>Revealed before your time,—<br> When ye give them<br> Their due dowers, and desire<br> Chastity, not lewdness,<br> Nor secret intrigues.<br> If any one rejects faith,<br> Fruitless is his work,<br> And in the Hereafter<br> He will be in the ranks<br> Of those who have lost<br> (All spiritual good).</p> <div class="filenav">
