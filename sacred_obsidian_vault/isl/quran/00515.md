 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra V.: Māïda, or The Table Spread.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 15</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_109.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>109.  Yawma yajmaAAu All<u>a</u>hu a<b>l</b>rrusula fayaqoolu m<u>atha</u> ojibtum q<u>a</u>loo l<u>a</u> AAilma lan<u>a</u> innaka anta AAall<u>a</u>mu alghuyoob<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>109.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">112</font></span> One day will God<br> Gather the apostles together,<br> And ask: "What was<br> The response ye received<br> (From men to your teaching)?"<br> They will say: "We<br> Have no knowledge: it is Thou<br> Who knowest in full<br> All that is hidden."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_110.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>110.  I<u>th</u> q<u>a</u>la All<u>a</u>hu y<u>a</u> AAees<u>a</u> ibna maryama o<u>th</u>kur niAAmatee AAalayka waAAal<u>a</u> w<u>a</u>lidatika i<u>th</u> ayyadtuka biroo<u>h</u>i alqudusi tukallimu a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>sa fee almahdi wakahlan wa-i<u>th</u> AAallamtuka alkit<u>a</u>ba wa<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>ikmata wa<b>al</b>ttawr<u>a</u>ta wa<b>a</b>l-injeela wa-i<u>th</u> takhluqu mina a<b>l</b><u>tt</u>eeni kahay-ati a<b>l</b><u>tt</u>ayri bi-i<u>th</u>nee fatanfukhu feeh<u>a</u> fatakoonu <u>t</u>ayran bi-i<u>th</u>nee watubri-o al-akmaha wa<b>a</b>l-abra<u>s</u>a bi-i<u>th</u>nee wa-i<u>th</u> tukhriju almawt<u>a</u> bi-i<u>th</u>nee wa-i<u>th</u> kafaftu banee isr<u>a</u>-eela AAanka i<u>th</u> ji/tahum bi<b>a</b>lbayyin<u>a</u>ti faq<u>a</u>la alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo minhum in h<u>atha</u> ill<u>a</u> si<u>h</u>run mubeen<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>110.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">113</font></span> When will God say:<br> "O Jesus the son of Mary!<br> Recount My favour"'<br> To thee and to thy mother.<br> Behold! I strengthened thee<br> With the holy spirit,<br> So that thou didst speak<br> To the people in childhood<br> And in maturity.<br> Behold! I taught thee<br> The Book and Wisdom,<br> The Law and the Gospel.<br> And behold! thou makest<br> Out of clay, as it were,<br>The figure of a bird,<br> By My leave,<br> And thou breathest into it,<br> And it becometh a bird<br> By My leave,<br> And thou healest those<br> Born blind, and the lepers,<br> By My leave.<br> And behold! thou<br> Bringest forth the dead<br> By My leave ego<br> And behold! I did<br> Restrain the Children of Israel<br> From (violence to) thee<br> When thou didst show them<br> The Clear Signs,<br> And the unbelievers among them<br> Said: 'This is nothing<br> But evident magic.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_111.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>111.  Wa-i<u>th</u> aw<u>h</u>aytu il<u>a</u> al<u>h</u>aw<u>a</u>riyyeena an <u>a</u>minoo bee wabirasoolee q<u>a</u>loo <u>a</u>mann<u>a</u> wa<b>i</b>shhad bi-annan<u>a</u> muslimoon<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>111.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">114</font></span> "And behold! I inspired<br> The Disciples to have faith<br> In Me and Mine Apostle:<br> They said, 'We have faith,<br> And do thou bear witness<br> That we bow to God<br> As Muslims'."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_112.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>112.  I<u>th</u> q<u>a</u>la al<u>h</u>aw<u>a</u>riyyoona y<u>a</u> AAees<u>a</u> ibna maryama hal yasta<u>t</u>eeAAu rabbuka an yunazzila AAalayn<u>a</u> m<u>a</u>-idatan mina a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>-i q<u>a</u>la ittaqoo All<u>a</u>ha in kuntum mu/mineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>112.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">115</font></span> Behold! the Disciples said:<br> "O Jesus the son of Mary!<br> Can thy Lord send down to us<br> A Table set (with viands)<br>From heaven?" Said Jesus:<br> "Fear God, if ye have faith."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_113.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>113.  Q<u>a</u>loo nureedu an na/kula minh<u>a</u> wata<u>t</u>ma-inna quloobun<u>a</u> wanaAAlama an qad <u>s</u>adaqtan<u>a</u> wanakoona AAalayh<u>a</u> mina a<b>l</b>shsh<u>a</u>hideen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>113.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">116</font></span> They said: "We only wish<br> To eat thereof and satisfy<br> Our hearts, and to know<br> That thou hast indeed<br> Told us the truth; and<br> That we ourselves may be<br> Witnesses to the miracle."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_114.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>114.  Q<u>a</u>la AAees<u>a</u> ibnu maryama all<u>a</u>humma rabban<u>a</u> anzil AAalayn<u>a</u> m<u>a</u>-idatan mina a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>-i takoonu lan<u>a</u> AAeedan li-awwalin<u>a</u> wa<u>a</u>khirin<u>a</u> wa<u>a</u>yatan minka wa<b>o</b>rzuqn<u>a</u> waanta khayru a<b>l</b>rr<u>a</u>ziqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>114.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">117</font></span> Said Jesus the son of Mary:<br> "O God our Lord!<br> Send us from heaven<br> A Table set (with viands),<br> That there may be for us<br> For the first and the last of us—<br> A solemn festival<br> And a. Sign from Thee;<br> And provide for our sustenance,<br> For Thou art the best<br> Sustainer (of our needs)."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_115.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>115.  Q<u>a</u>la All<u>a</u>hu innee munazziluh<u>a</u> AAalaykum faman yakfur baAAdu minkum fa-innee oAAa<u>thth</u>ibuhu AAa<u>tha</u>ban l<u>a</u> oAAa<u>thth</u>ibuhu a<u>h</u>adan mina alAA<u>a</u>lameen<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>115.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">118</font></span> God said: "I will<br> Send it down unto you:<br> But if any of you<br> After that resisteth faith,<br> I will punish him<br> With a penalty such<br> As I have not inflicted<br> On any one among<br>All the peoples."</p> <div class="filenav">
