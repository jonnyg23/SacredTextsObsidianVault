 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra LXVIII.: Qalam, or the Pen, or Nūn.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 2</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/068/068_034.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>34.  Inna lilmuttaqeena AAinda rabbihim jann<u>a</u>ti a<b>l</b>nnaAAeem<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>34.  Verily, for the Righteous,<br> Are Gardens of Delight,<br> In the Presence<br> Of their Lord.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/068/068_035.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>35.  AfanajAAalu almuslimeena ka<b>a</b>lmujrimeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>35.  Shall We then treat<br> The People of Faith<br> Like the People of Sin?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/068/068_036.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>36.  M<u>a</u> lakum kayfa ta<u>h</u>kumoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>36.  What is the matter<br> With you? How judge ye?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/068/068_037.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>37.  Am lakum kit<u>a</u>bun feehi tadrusoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>37.  Or have ye a Book<br> Through which ye learn—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/068/068_038.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>38.  Inna lakum feehi lam<u>a</u> takhayyaroon<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>38.  That ye shall have,<br> Through it whatever<br> Ye choose?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/068/068_039.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>39.  Am lakum aym<u>a</u>nun AAalayn<u>a</u> b<u>a</u>lighatun il<u>a</u> yawmi alqiy<u>a</u>mati inna lakum lam<u>a</u> ta<u>h</u>kumoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>39.  Or have ye Covenants<br> With Us on oath,<br> Reaching to the Day<br> Of Judgment, (providing)<br> That ye shall have<br> Whatever ye shall demand?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/068/068_040.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>40.  Salhum ayyuhum bi<u>tha</u>lika zaAAeem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>40.  Ask thou of them,<br> Which of them will stand<br> Surety for that!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/068/068_041.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>41.  Am lahum shurak<u>a</u>o falya/too bishurak<u>a</u>-ihim in k<u>a</u>noo <u>sa</u>diqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>41.  Or have they some<br> "Partners" (in Godhead)?<br> Then let them produce<br> Their "partners",<br> If they are truthful!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/068/068_042.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>42.  Yawma yukshafu AAan s<u>a</u>qin wayudAAawna il<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>ssujoodi fal<u>a</u> yasta<u>t</u>eeAAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>42.  The Day that the Shin<br> Shall be laid bare,<br> And they shall be summoned<br> To bow in adoration,<br> But they shall not be able,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/068/068_043.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>43.  Kh<u>a</u>shiAAatan ab<u>sa</u>ruhum tarhaquhum <u>th</u>illatun waqad k<u>a</u>noo yudAAawna il<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>ssujoodi wahum s<u>a</u>limoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>43.  Their eyes will be<br> Cast down,—ignominy will<br> Cover them; seeing that<br> They had been summoned<br> Aforetime to bow in adoration,<br> While they were whole,<br> (And had refused).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/068/068_044.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>44.  Fa<u>th</u>arnee waman yuka<u>thth</u>ibu bih<u>atha</u> al<u>h</u>adeethi sanastadrijuhum min <u>h</u>aythu l<u>a</u> yaAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>44.  Then leave Me alone<br> With such as reject<br> This Message: by degrees<br> Shall We punish them<br>From directions they perceive not.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/068/068_045.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>45.  Waomlee lahum inna kaydee mateen<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>45.  A (long) respite will I<br> Grant them: truly<br> Powerful is My Plan.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/068/068_046.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>46.  Am tas-aluhum ajran fahum min maghramin muthqaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>46.  Or is it that thou dost<br> Ask them for a reward,<br> So that they are burdened<br> With a load of debt?—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/068/068_047.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>47.  Am AAindahumu alghaybu fahum yaktuboon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>47.  Or that the Unseen<br> Is in their hands, so that<br> They can write it down?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/068/068_048.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>48.  Fa<b>i</b><u>s</u>bir li<u>h</u>ukmi rabbika wal<u>a</u> takun ka<u>sah</u>ibi al<u>h</u>ooti i<u>th</u> n<u>a</u>d<u>a</u> wahuwa mak<i><u>th</u></i>oom<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>48.  So wait with patience<br> For the Command<br> Of thy Lord, and be not<br> Like the Companion<br> Of the Fish,—when he<br> Cried out in agony.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/068/068_049.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>49.  Lawl<u>a</u> an tad<u>a</u>rakahu niAAmatun min rabbihi lanubi<u>th</u>a bi<b>a</b>lAAar<u>a</u>-i wahuwa ma<u>th</u>moom<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>49.  Had not Grace<br> From His Lord<br> Reached him, he<br> Would indeed have been<br>Cast off on the naked<br> Shore, in disgrace.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/068/068_050.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>50.  Fa<b>i</b>jtab<u>a</u>hu rabbuhu fajaAAalahu mina a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>li<u>h</u>een<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>50.  Thus did his Lord<br> Choose him and make him<br> Of the company<br> Of the Righteous.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/068/068_051.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>51.  Wa-in yak<u>a</u>du alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo layuzliqoonaka bi-ab<u>sa</u>rihim lamm<u>a</u> samiAAoo a<b>l</b><u>thth</u>ikra wayaqooloona innahu lamajnoon<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>51.  And the Unbelievers<br> Would almost trip thee up<br> With their eyes when they<br> Hear the Message; and they<br> Say: "Surely he is possessed!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/068/068_052.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>52.  Wam<u>a</u> huwa ill<u>a</u> <u>th</u>ikrun lilAA<u>a</u>lameen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>52.  But it is nothing less<br> Than a Message<br> To all the worlds.</p>  <div class="filenav">
