 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XII.: Yūsuf, or Joseph.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 6</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_043.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>43.  Waq<u>a</u>la almaliku innee ar<u>a</u> sabAAa baqar<u>a</u>tin sim<u>a</u>nin ya/kuluhunna sabAAun AAij<u>a</u>fun wasabAAa sunbul<u>a</u>tin khu<u>d</u>rin waokhara y<u>a</u>bis<u>a</u>tin y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> almalao aftoonee fee ru/y<u>a</u>ya in kuntum li<b>l</b>rru/y<u>a</u> taAAburoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>43.  The king (of Egypt) said:<br> "I do see (in a vision)<br> Seven fat kine, whom seven<br> Lean ones devour,—and seven<br> Green ears of corn, and seven (others)<br> Withered. O ye chiefs!<br> Expound to me my vision<br> If it be that ye can<br> Interpret visions."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_044.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>44.  Q<u>a</u>loo a<u>d</u>gh<u>a</u>thu a<u>h</u>l<u>a</u>min wam<u>a</u> na<u>h</u>nu bita/weeli al-a<u>h</u>l<u>a</u>mi biAA<u>a</u>limeen<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>44.  They said: "A confused medley<br> Of dreams: and we are not<br> Skilled in the interpretation<br> Of dreams."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_045.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>45.  Waq<u>a</u>la alla<u>th</u>ee naj<u>a</u> minhum<u>a</u> wa<b>i</b>ddakara baAAda ommatin an<u>a</u> onabbi-okum bita/weelihi faarsiloon<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>45.  But the man who had been<br> Released, one of the two<br> (Who had been in prison)<br> And who now bethought him<br> After (so long) a space of time,<br> Said: "I will tell you<br> The truth of its interpretation:<br> Send ye me (therefor)."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_046.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>46.  Yoosufu ayyuh<u>a</u> a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>iddeequ aftin<u>a</u> fee sabAAi baqar<u>a</u>tin sim<u>a</u>nin ya/kuluhunna sabAAun AAij<u>a</u>fun wasabAAi sunbul<u>a</u>tin khu<u>d</u>rin waokhara y<u>a</u>bis<u>a</u>tin laAAallee arjiAAu il<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si laAAallahum yaAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>46.  "O Joseph!" (he said).<br> "O man of truth! Expound<br> To us (the dream)<br> Of seven fat kine<br> Whom seven lean ones<br> Devour, and of seven<br> Green ears of corn<br> And (seven) others withered:<br> That I may return<br> To the people, and that<br> They may understand."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_047.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>47.  Q<u>a</u>la tazraAAoona sabAAa sineena daaban fam<u>a</u> <u>h</u>a<u>s</u>adtum fa<u>th</u>aroohu fee sunbulihi ill<u>a</u> qaleelan mimm<u>a</u> ta/kuloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>47.  (Joseph) said: "For seven years<br> Shall ye diligently sow<br>As is your wont:<br> And the harvests that ye reap,<br> Ye shall leave them in the ear,—<br> Except a little, of which<br> Ye shall eat.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_048.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>48.  Thumma ya/tee min baAAdi <u>tha</u>lika sabAAun shid<u>a</u>dun ya/kulna m<u>a</u> qaddamtum lahunna ill<u>a</u> qaleelan mimm<u>a</u> tu<u>hs</u>inoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>48.  "Then will come<br> After that (period)<br> Seven dreadful (years),<br> Which will devour<br> What ye shall have laid by<br> In advance for them,—<br> (All) except a little<br> Which ye shall have<br> (Specially) guarded.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_049.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>49.  Thumma ya/tee min baAAdi <u>tha</u>lika AA<u>a</u>mun feehi yugh<u>a</u>thu a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>su wafeehi yaAA<u>s</u>iroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>49.  "Then will come<br> After that (period) a year<br> In which the people will have<br> Abundant water, and in which<br> They will press (wine and oil)."</p> <div class="filenav">
