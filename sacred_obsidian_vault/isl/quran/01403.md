 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XIV.: Ibrāhīm, or Abraham.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 3</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/014/014_013.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>13.  Waq<u>a</u>la alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo lirusulihim lanukhrijannakum min ar<u>d</u>in<u>a</u> aw lataAAoodunna fee millatin<u>a</u> faaw<u>ha</u> ilayhim rabbuhum lanuhlikanna a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>13.  And the Unbelievers said<br> To their apostles: "Be sure<br> We shall drive you out<br> Of our land, or ye shall<br> Return to our religion."<br> But their Lord inspired<br> (This Message) to them:<br> "Verily We shall cause<br> The wrong-doers to perish!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/014/014_014.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>14.  Walanuskinannakumu al-ar<u>d</u>a min baAAdihim <u>tha</u>lika liman kh<u>a</u>fa maq<u>a</u>mee wakh<u>a</u>fa waAAeed<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>14.  "And verily We shall<br> Cause you to abide<br> In the land, and succeed them.<br> This for such as fear<br> The Time when they shall stand<br> Before My tribunal,—such<br> As fear the Punishment denounced."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/014/014_015.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>15.  Wa<b>i</b>stafta<u>h</u>oo wakh<u>a</u>ba kullu jabb<u>a</u>rin AAaneed<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>15.  But they sought victory and decision<br> (There and then), and frustration<br> Was the lot of every<br> Powerful obstinate transgressor.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/014/014_016.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>16.  Min war<u>a</u>-ihi jahannamu wayusq<u>a</u> min m<u>a</u>-in <u>s</u>adeed<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>16.  In front of such a one<br> Is Hell, and he is given,<br> For drink, boiling fetid water.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/014/014_017.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>17.  YatajarraAAuhu wal<u>a</u> yak<u>a</u>du yuseeghuhu waya/teehi almawtu min kulli mak<u>a</u>nin wam<u>a</u> huwa bimayyitin wamin war<u>a</u>-ihi AAa<u>tha</u>bun ghalee<i><u>th</u></i><b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>17.  In gulps will he sip it,<br> But never will he be near<br> Swallowing it down his throat:<br> Death will come to him<br> From every quarter, yet<br> Will he not die: and<br> In front of him will be<br> A chastisement unrelenting.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/014/014_018.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>18.  Mathalu alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo birabbihim aAAm<u>a</u>luhum karam<u>a</u>din ishtaddat bihi a<b>l</b>rree<u>h</u>u fee yawmin AA<u>as</u>ifin l<u>a</u> yaqdiroona mimm<u>a</u> kasaboo AAal<u>a</u> shay-in <u>tha</u>lika huwa a<b>l</b><u>dd</u>al<u>a</u>lu albaAAeed<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>18.  The parable of those who<br> Reject their Lord is that<br> Their works are as ashes,<br> On which the wind blows<br> Furiously on a tempestuous day:<br> No power have they over<br> Aught that they have earned:<br> That is the straying<br> Far, far (from the goal).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/014/014_019.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>19.  Alam tara anna All<u>a</u>ha khalaqa a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>a bi<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>aqqi in yasha/ yu<u>th</u>hibkum waya/ti bikhalqin jadeed<b>in </b>   </p>
 <p>19.  Seest thou not that God<br> Created the heavens and the earth<br> In Truth? If He so will,<br> He can remove you<br> And put (in your place)<br> A new Creation?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/014/014_020.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>20.  Wam<u>a</u> <u>tha</u>lika AAal<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi biAAazeez<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>20.  Nor is that for God<br> Any great matter.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/014/014_021.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>21.  Wabarazoo lill<u>a</u>hi jameeAAan faq<u>a</u>la a<b>l</b><u>dd</u>uAAaf<u>a</u>o lilla<u>th</u>eena istakbaroo inn<u>a</u> kunn<u>a</u> lakum tabaAAan fahal antum mughnoona AAann<u>a</u> min AAa<u>tha</u>bi All<u>a</u>hi min shay-in q<u>a</u>loo law had<u>a</u>n<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hu lahadayn<u>a</u>kum saw<u>a</u>on AAalayn<u>a</u> ajaziAAn<u>a</u> am <u>s</u>abarn<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> lan<u>a</u> min ma<u>h</u>ee<u>s</u><b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>21.  They will all be marshalled<br> Before God together: then<br> Will the weak say to those<br> Who were arrogant, "For us,<br> We but followed you; can ye<br> Then avail us at all<br> Against the Wrath of God?"<br> They will reply, "If we<br> Had received the guidance<br> Of God, we should have<br> Given it to you: to us<br> It makes no difference (now)<br> Whether we rage, or bear<br> (These torments) with patience:<br> For ourselves there is no way<br> Of escape."</p> <div class="filenav">
