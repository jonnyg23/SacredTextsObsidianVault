 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXXVIII.: Ṣād (being one of the Abbreviated Letters).</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 5</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/038/038_065.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>65.  Qul innam<u>a</u> an<u>a</u> mun<u>th</u>irun wam<u>a</u> min il<u>a</u>hin ill<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hu alw<u>ah</u>idu alqahh<u>a</u>r<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>65.  Say: "Truly am I<br> A Warner: no god<br> Is there but the One<br> God, Supreme and Irresistible,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/038/038_066.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>66.  Rabbu a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i wam<u>a</u> baynahum<u>a</u> alAAazeezu alghaff<u>a</u>r<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>66.  "The Lord of the heavens<br> And the earth, and all<br> Between,—Exalted in Might,<br> Able to enforce His Will,<br> Forgiving again and again."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/038/038_067.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>67.  Qul huwa nabaon AAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>67.  Say: "That is a Message<br> Supreme (above all),—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/038/038_068.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>68.  Antum AAanhu muAAri<u>d</u>oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>68.  "From which ye<br> Do turn away!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/038/038_069.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>69.  M<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>na liya min AAilmin bi<b>a</b>lmala-i al-aAAl<u>a</u> i<u>th</u> yakhta<u>s</u>imoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>69.  "No knowledge have I<br> Of the Chiefs on high,<br> When they discuss<br> (Matters) among themselves.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/038/038_070.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>70.  In yoo<u>ha</u> ilayya ill<u>a</u> annam<u>a</u> an<u>a</u> na<u>th</u>eerun mubeen<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>70.  "Only this has been revealed<br> To me: that I am<br> To give warning<br> Plainly and publicly."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/038/038_071.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>71.  I<u>th</u> q<u>a</u>la rabbuka lilmal<u>a</u>-ikati innee kh<u>a</u>liqun basharan min <u>t</u>een<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>71.  Behold, thy Lord said<br> To the angels: "I am<br> About to create man<br> From clay:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/038/038_072.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>72.  Fa-i<u>tha</u> sawwaytuhu wanafakhtu feehi min roo<u>h</u>ee faqaAAoo lahu s<u>a</u>jideen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>72.  "When I have fashioned him<br> (In due proportion) and breathed<br> Into him of My spirit,<br> Fall ye down in obeisance<br> Unto him."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/038/038_073.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>73.  Fasajada almal<u>a</u>-ikatu kulluhum ajmaAAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>73.  So the angels prostrated themselves,<br> All of them together:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/038/038_074.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>74.  Ill<u>a</u> ibleesa istakbara wak<u>a</u>na mina alk<u>a</u>fireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>74.  Not so Iblīs: he<br> Was haughty, and became<br> One of those who reject Faith.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/038/038_075.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>75.  Q<u>a</u>la y<u>a</u> ibleesu m<u>a</u> manaAAaka an tasjuda lim<u>a</u> khalaqtu biyadayya astakbarta am kunta mina alAA<u>a</u>leen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>75.  (God) said: "O Iblīs!<br> What prevents thee from<br> Prostrating thyself to one<br> Whom I have created<br> With My hands?<br> Art thou haughty?<br>Or art thou one<br> Of the high (and mighty) ones?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/038/038_076.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>76.  Q<u>a</u>la an<u>a</u> khayrun minhu khalaqtanee min n<u>a</u>rin wakhalaqtahu min <u>t</u>een<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>76.  (Iblīs) said: "I am better<br> Than he: Thou createdst<br> Me from fire, and him<br> Thou createdst from clay."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/038/038_077.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>77.  Q<u>a</u>la fa<b>o</b>khruj minh<u>a</u> fa-innaka rajeem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>77.  (God) said: "Then get thee<br> Out from here: for thou<br> Art rejected, accursed.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/038/038_078.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>78.  Wa-inna AAalayka laAAnatee il<u>a</u> yawmi a<b>l</b>ddeen<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>78.  "And My Curse shall be<br> On thee till the Day<br> Of Judgment."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/038/038_079.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>79.  Q<u>a</u>la rabbi faan<i><u>th</u></i>irnee il<u>a</u> yawmi yubAAathoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>79.  (Iblīs) said: "O my Lord!<br> Give me then respite<br> Till the Day<br> The (dead) are raised."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/038/038_080.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>80.  Q<u>a</u>la fa-innaka mina almun<i><u>th</u></i>areen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>80.  (God) said: "Respite then<br> Is granted thee—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/038/038_081.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>81.  Il<u>a</u> yawmi alwaqti almaAAloom<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>81.  "Till the Day<br> Of the Time Appointed."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/038/038_082.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>82.  Q<u>a</u>la fabiAAizzatika laoghwiyannahum ajmaAAeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>82.  (Iblīs) said: "Then,<br> By Thy Power, I will<br> Put them all in the wrong,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/038/038_083.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>83.  Ill<u>a</u> AAib<u>a</u>daka minhumu almukhla<u>s</u>een<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>83.  "Except Thy Servants<br> Amongst them, sincere<br> And purified (by Thy grace)."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/038/038_084.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>84.  Q<u>a</u>la fa<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>aqqu wa<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>aqqa aqool<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>84.  (God) said: "Then<br> It is just and fitting—<br> And I say what is<br> Just and fitting—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/038/038_085.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>85.  Laamlaanna jahannama minka wamimman tabiAAaka minhum ajmaAAeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>85.  "That I will certainly fill<br> Hell with thee<br> And those that follow thee,—<br> Every one."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/038/038_086.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>86.  Qul m<u>a</u> as-alukum AAalayhi min ajrin wam<u>a</u> an<u>a</u> mina almutakallifeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>86.  Say: "No reward do I ask<br> Of you for this (Qur-ān),<br> Nor am I a pretender.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/038/038_087.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>87.  In huwa ill<u>a</u> <u>th</u>ikrun lilAA<u>a</u>lameen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>87.  "This is no less than<br> A Message to (all)<br> The Worlds.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/038/038_088.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>88.  WalataAAlamunna nabaahu baAAda <u>h</u>een<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>88.  "And ye shall certainly<br> Know the truth of it (all)<br> After a while."</p>  <div class="filenav">
