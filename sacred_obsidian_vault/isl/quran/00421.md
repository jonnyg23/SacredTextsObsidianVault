 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra IV.: Nisāa, or The Women.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 21</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_142.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>142.  Inna almun<u>a</u>fiqeena yukh<u>a</u>diAAoona All<u>a</u>ha wahuwa kh<u>a</u>diAAuhum wa-i<u>tha</u> q<u>a</u>moo il<u>a</u> a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>al<u>a</u>ti q<u>a</u>moo kus<u>a</u>l<u>a</u> yur<u>a</u>oona a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>sa wal<u>a</u> ya<u>th</u>kuroona All<u>a</u>ha ill<u>a</u> qaleel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>142.  The Hypocrites—they think<br> They are over-reaching God,<br> But He will over-reach them:<br> When they stand up to prayer,<br> They stand without earnestness,<br> To be seen of men,<br> But little do they hold<br> God in remembrance;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_143.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>143.  Mu<u>th</u>ab<u>th</u>abeena bayna <u>tha</u>lika l<u>a</u> il<u>a</u> h<u>a</u>ol<u>a</u>-i wal<u>a</u> il<u>a</u> h<u>a</u>ol<u>a</u>-i waman yu<u>d</u>lili All<u>a</u>hu falan tajida lahu sabeel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>143.  (They are) distracted in mind<br> Even in the midst of it,—<br> Being (sincerely) for neither<br> One group nor for another.<br> Whom God leaves straying,—<br> Never wilt thou find<br>For him the Way.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_144.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>144.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo l<u>a</u> tattakhi<u>th</u>oo alk<u>a</u>fireena awliy<u>a</u>a min dooni almu/mineena atureedoona an tajAAaloo lill<u>a</u>hi AAalaykum sul<u>ta</u>nan mubeen<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>144.  O ye who believe!<br> Take not for friends<br> Unbelievers rather than<br> Believers: do ye wish<br> To offer God an open<br> Proof against yourselves?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_145.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>145.  Inna almun<u>a</u>fiqeena fee a<b>l</b>ddarki al-asfali mina a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>ri walan tajida lahum na<u>s</u>eer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>145.  The Hypocrites will be<br> In the lowest depths<br> Of the Fire: no helper<br> Wilt thou find for them;—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_146.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>146.  Ill<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena t<u>a</u>boo waa<u>s</u>la<u>h</u>oo wa<b>i</b>AAta<u>s</u>amoo bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi waakhla<u>s</u>oo deenahum lill<u>a</u>hi faol<u>a</u>-ika maAAa almu/mineena wasawfa yu/ti All<u>a</u>hu almu/mineena ajran AAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>146.  Except for those who repent,<br> Mend (their life), hold fast<br> To God, and purify their religion<br> As in God's sight: if so<br> They will be (numbered)<br> With the Believers.<br> And soon will God<br> Grant to the Believers<br> A reward of immense value.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_147.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>147.  M<u>a</u> yafAAalu All<u>a</u>hu biAAa<u>tha</u>bikum in shakartum wa<u>a</u>mantum wak<u>a</u>na All<u>a</u>hu sh<u>a</u>kiran AAaleem<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>147.  What can God gain<br> By your punishment,<br> If ye are grateful<br> And ye believe?<br> Nay, it is God<br> That recogniseth<br> (All good), and knoweth<br> All things.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_148.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>148.  L<u>a</u> yu<u>h</u>ibbu All<u>a</u>hu aljahra bi<b>al</b>ssoo-i mina alqawli ill<u>a</u> man <i><u>th</u></i>ulima wak<u>a</u>na All<u>a</u>hu sameeAAan AAaleem<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>148.  God loveth not that evil<br> Should be noised abroad<br> In public speech, except<br> Where injustice hath been<br> Done; for God<br> Is He who heareth<br> And knoweth all things.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_149.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>149.  In tubdoo khayran aw tukhfoohu aw taAAfoo AAan soo-in fa-inna All<u>a</u>ha k<u>a</u>na AAafuwwan qadeer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>149.  Whether ye publish<br> A good deed or conceal it<br> Or cover evil with pardon,<br> Verily God doth blot out<br> (Sins) and hath power<br> (In the judgment of values),</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_150.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>150.  Inna alla<u>th</u>eena yakfuroona bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi warusulihi wayureedoona an yufarriqoo bayna All<u>a</u>hi warusulihi wayaqooloona nu/minu bibaAA<u>d</u>in wanakfuru bibaAA<u>d</u>in wayureedoona an yattakhi<u>th</u>oo bayna <u>tha</u>lika sabeel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>150.  Those who deny God<br> And His apostles, and (those<br> Who) wish to separate<br> God from His apostles,<br> Saying: "We believe in some<br> But reject others":<br> And (those who) wish<br> To take a course midway,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_151.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>151.  Ol<u>a</u>-ika humu alk<u>a</u>firoona <u>h</u>aqqan waaAAtadn<u>a</u> lilk<u>a</u>fireena AAa<u>tha</u>ban muheen<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>151.  They are in truth<br> (Equally) Unbelievers;<br>And We have prepared<br> For Unbelievers a humiliating<br> Punishment.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_152.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>152.  Wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi warusulihi walam yufarriqoo bayna a<u>h</u>adin minhum ol<u>a</u>-ika sawfa yu/teehim ojoorahum wak<u>a</u>na All<u>a</u>hu ghafooran ra<u>h</u>eem<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>152.  To those who believe<br> In God and His apostles<br> And make no distinction<br> Between any of the apostles,<br> We shall soon give<br> Their (due) rewards:<br> For God is Oft-forgiving,<br> Most Merciful.</p> <div class="filenav">
