 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra VI.: An’ām, or Cattle.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 6</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_051.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>51.  Waan<u>th</u>ir bihi alla<u>th</u>eena yakh<u>a</u>foona an yu<u>h</u>sharoo il<u>a</u> rabbihim laysa lahum min doonihi waliyyun wal<u>a</u> shafeeAAun laAAallahum yattaqoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>51.  Give this warning to those<br> In whose (hearts) is the fear<br> That they will be brought<br> (To Judgment) before their Lord:<br> Except for Him<br> They will have no protector<br> Nor intercessor:<br> That they may guard<br> (Against evil).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_052.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>52.  Wal<u>a</u> ta<u>t</u>rudi alla<u>th</u>eena yadAAoona rabbahum bi<b>a</b>lghad<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>lAAashiyyi yureedoona wajhahu m<u>a</u> AAalayka min <u>h</u>is<u>a</u>bihim min shay-in wam<u>a</u> min <u>h</u>is<u>a</u>bika AAalayhim min shay-in fata<u>t</u>rudahum fatakoona mina a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>52.  Send not away those<br> Who call on their Lord<br> Morning and evening,<br> Seeking His Face.<br> In naught art thou accountable<br> For them, and in naught are they<br> Accountable for thee,<br> That thou shouldst turn<br> Them away, and thus be<br> (One) of the unjust.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_053.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>53.  Waka<u>tha</u>lika fatann<u>a</u> baAA<u>d</u>ahum bibaAA<u>d</u>in liyaqooloo ah<u>a</u>ol<u>a</u>-i manna All<u>a</u>hu AAalayhim min baynin<u>a</u> alaysa All<u>a</u>hu bi-aAAlama bi<b>al</b>shsh<u>a</u>kireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>53.  Thus did We try<br> Some of them by comparison<br> With others, that they<br> Should say:"Is it these<br> Then that God hath<br> Favoured from amongst us?"<br> Doth not God know best<br> Those who are grateful?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_054.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>54.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> j<u>a</u>aka alla<u>th</u>eena yu/minoona bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin<u>a</u> faqul sal<u>a</u>mun AAalaykum kataba rabbukum AAal<u>a</u> nafsihi a<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>mata annahu man AAamila minkum soo-an bijah<u>a</u>latin thumma t<u>a</u>ba min baAAdihi waa<u>s</u>la<u>h</u>a faannahu ghafoorun ra<u>h</u>eem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>54.  When those come to thee<br> Who believe in Our Signs,<br> Say: "Peace be on you:<br> Your Lord hath inscribed<br> For Himself (the rule<br> Of) Mercy: verily,<br> If any of you did evil<br> In ignorance, and thereafter<br>Repented, and amended<br> (His conduct), lo! He is<br> Oft-forgiving, Most Merciful.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_055.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>55.  Waka<u>tha</u>lika nufa<u>ss</u>ilu al-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>ti walitastabeena sabeelu almujrimeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>55.  Thus do We explain<br> The Signs in detail:<br> That the way of the sinners<br> May be shown up.</p> <div class="filenav">
