 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXII.: Ḥajj, or The Pilgrimage.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 7</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/022/022_049.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>49.  Qul y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>su innam<u>a</u> an<u>a</u> lakum na<u>th</u>eerun mubeen<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>49.  Say: "O men! I am<br> (Sent) to you only to give<br>A clear warning:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/022/022_050.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>50.  Fa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo waAAamiloo a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>li<u>ha</u>ti lahum maghfiratun warizqun kareem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>50.  "Those who believe and work<br> Righteousness, for them<br> Is forgiveness and a sustenance<br> Most generous.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/022/022_051.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>51.  Wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena saAAaw fee <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin<u>a</u> muAA<u>a</u>jizeena ol<u>a</u>-ika a<u>s</u>-<u>ha</u>bu alja<u>h</u>eem<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>51.  "But those who strive<br> Against Our Signs, to frustrate<br> Them,—they will be<br> Companions of the Fire."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/022/022_052.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>52.  Wam<u>a</u> arsaln<u>a</u> min qablika min rasoolin wal<u>a</u> nabiyyin ill<u>a</u> i<u>tha</u> tamann<u>a</u> alq<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>shshay<u>ta</u>nu fee omniyyatihi fayansakhu All<u>a</u>hu m<u>a</u> yulqee a<b>l</b>shshay<u>ta</u>nu thumma yu<u>h</u>kimu All<u>a</u>hu <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tihi wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu AAaleemun <u>h</u>akeem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>52.  Never did We send<br> An apostle or a prophet<br> Before thee, but, when he<br> Framed a desire, Satan<br> Threw some (vanity)<br> Into his desire: but God<br> Will cancel anything (vain)<br> That Satan throws in,<br> And God will confirm<br> (And establish) His Signs:<br> For God is full of knowledge<br> And wisdom:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/022/022_053.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>53.  LiyajAAala m<u>a</u> yulqee a<b>l</b>shshay<u>ta</u>nu fitnatan lilla<u>th</u>eena fee quloobihim mara<u>d</u>un wa<b>a</b>lq<u>a</u>siyati quloobuhum wa-inna a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limeena lafee shiq<u>a</u>qin baAAeed<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>53.  That He may make<br> The suggestions thrown in<br> By Satan, but a trial<br> For those in whose hearts</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/022/022_054.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>54.  WaliyaAAlama alla<u>th</u>eena ootoo alAAilma annahu al<u>h</u>aqqu min rabbika fayu/minoo bihi fatukhbita lahu quloobuhum wa-inna All<u>a</u>ha lah<u>a</u>di alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo il<u>a</u> <u>s</u>ir<u>at</u>in mustaqeem<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>54.  And that those on whom<br> Knowledge has been bestowed may learn<br> That the (Qur-ān) is the Truth<br> From thy Lord, and that they<br> May believe therein, and their hearts<br> May be made humbly (open)<br> To it: for verily God is<br> The Guide of those who believe,<br> To the Straight Way.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/022/022_055.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>55.  Wal<u>a</u> yaz<u>a</u>lu alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo fee miryatin minhu <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> ta/tiyahumu a<b>l</b>ss<u>a</u>AAatu baghtatan aw ya/tiyahum AAa<u>tha</u>bu yawmin AAaqeem<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>55.  Those who reject Faith<br> Will not cease to be<br> In doubt concerning (Revelation)<br> Until the Hour (of Judgment)<br> Comes suddenly upon them,<br> Or there comes to them<br> The Penalty of a Day of Disaster.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/022/022_056.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>56.  Almulku yawma-i<u>th</u>in lill<u>a</u>hi ya<u>h</u>kumu baynahum fa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo waAAamiloo a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>li<u>ha</u>ti fee jann<u>a</u>ti a<b>l</b>nnaAAeem<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>56.  On that Day the Dominion<br> Will be that of God:<br> He will judge between them:<br> So those who believe<br> And work righteous deeds will be<br> In Gardens of Delight.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/022/022_057.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>57.  Wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo waka<u>thth</u>aboo bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin<u>a</u> faol<u>a</u>-ika lahum AAa<u>tha</u>bun muheen<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>57.  And for those who reject Faith<br> And deny Our Signs,<br> There will be a humiliating<br> Punishment.</p> <div class="filenav">
