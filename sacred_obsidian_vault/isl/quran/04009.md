 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XL.: Mū-min, or The Believer.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 9</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/040/040_079.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>79.  All<u>a</u>hu alla<u>th</u>ee jaAAala lakumu al-anAA<u>a</u>ma litarkaboo minh<u>a</u> waminh<u>a</u> ta/kuloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>79.  It is God who made<br> Cattle for you, that ye<br>May use some for riding<br> And some for food;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/040/040_080.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>80.  Walakum feeh<u>a</u> man<u>a</u>fiAAu walitablughoo AAalayh<u>a</u> <u>ha</u>jatan fee <u>s</u>udoorikum waAAalayh<u>a</u> waAAal<u>a</u> alfulki tu<u>h</u>maloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>80.  And there are (other) advantages<br> In them for you (besides);<br> That ye may through them<br> Attain to any need<br> (There may be) in your hearts;<br> And on them and on ships<br> Ye are carried.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/040/040_081.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>81.  Wayureekum <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tihi faayya <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>ti All<u>a</u>hi tunkiroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>81.  And He shows you (always)<br> His Signs: then which<br> Of the Signs of God<br> Will ye deny?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/040/040_082.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>82.  Afalam yaseeroo fee al-ar<u>d</u>i fayan<i><u>th</u></i>uroo kayfa k<u>a</u>na AA<u>a</u>qibatu alla<u>th</u>eena min qablihim k<u>a</u>noo akthara minhum waashadda quwwatan wa<u>a</u>th<u>a</u>ran fee al-ar<u>d</u>i fam<u>a</u> aghn<u>a</u> AAanhum m<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo yaksiboon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>82.  Do they not travel through<br> The earth and see what<br> Was the End of those<br> Before them? They were<br> More numerous than these<br> And superior in strength<br> And in the traces<br> (They have left) in the land:<br> Yet all that they accomplished<br> Was of no profit to them.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/040/040_083.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>83.  Falamm<u>a</u> j<u>a</u>at-hum rusuluhum bi<b>a</b>lbayyin<u>a</u>ti fari<u>h</u>oo bim<u>a</u> AAindahum mina alAAilmi wa<u>ha</u>qa bihim m<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo bihi yastahzi-oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>83.  For when their apostles<br> Came to them<br> With Clear Signs, they exulted<br> In such knowledge (and skill)<br>As they had; but<br> That very (Wrath) at which<br> They were wont to scoff<br> Hemmed them in.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/040/040_084.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>84.  Falamm<u>a</u> raaw ba/san<u>a</u> q<u>a</u>loo <u>a</u>mann<u>a</u> bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi wa<u>h</u>dahu wakafarn<u>a</u> bim<u>a</u> kunn<u>a</u> bihi mushrikeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>84.  But when they saw<br> Our Punishment, they said:<br> "We believe in God,—<br> The One God—and we<br> Reject the partners we used<br> To join with Him."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/040/040_085.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>85.  Falam yaku yanfaAAuhum eem<u>a</u>nuhum lamm<u>a</u> raaw ba/san<u>a</u> sunnata All<u>a</u>hi allatee qad khalat fee AAib<u>a</u>dihi wakhasira hun<u>a</u>lika alk<u>a</u>firoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>85.  But their professing the Faith<br> When they (actually) saw<br> Our Punishment was not going<br> To profit them.<br> (Such has been) God's way<br> Of dealing with His servants<br> (From the most ancient times).<br> And even thus did<br> The rejecters of God<br> Perish (utterly)!</p>  <div class="filenav">
