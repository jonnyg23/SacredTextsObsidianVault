 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XII.: Yūsuf, or Joseph.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 4</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_030.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>30.  Waq<u>a</u>la niswatun fee almadeenati imraatu alAAazeezi tur<u>a</u>widu fat<u>a</u>h<u>a</u> AAan nafsihi qad shaghafah<u>a</u> <u>h</u>ubban inn<u>a</u> lanar<u>a</u>h<u>a</u> fee <u>d</u>al<u>a</u>lin mubeen<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>30.  Ladies said in the City:<br> "The wife of the (great) ’Aziz<br> Is seeking to seduce her slave<br> From his (true) self:<br> Truly hath he inspired her<br> With violent love: we see<br> She is evidently going astray."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_031.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>31.  Falamm<u>a</u> samiAAat bimakrihinna arsalat ilayhinna waaAAtadat lahunna muttakaan wa<u>a</u>tat kulla w<u>ah</u>idatin minhunna sikkeenan waq<u>a</u>lati okhruj AAalayhinna falamm<u>a</u> raaynahu akbarnahu waqa<u>tt</u>aAAna aydiyahunna waqulna <u>ha</u>sha lill<u>a</u>hi m<u>a</u> h<u>atha</u> basharan in h<u>atha</u> ill<u>a</u> malakun kareem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>31.  When she heard<br> Of their malicious talk,<br> She sent for them<br> And prepared a banquet<br> For them: she gave<br> Each of them a knife:<br> And she said (to Joseph),<br> "Come out before them."<br> When they saw him,<br> Thy did extol him,<br> And (in their amazement)<br> Cut their hands: they said,<br> "God preserve us! no mortal<br> Is this! This is none other<br> Than a noble angel!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_032.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>32.  Q<u>a</u>lat fa<u>tha</u>likunna alla<u>th</u>ee lumtunnanee feehi walaqad r<u>a</u>wadtuhu AAan nafsihi fa<b>i</b>stAA<u>s</u>ama wala-in lam yafAAal m<u>a</u> <u>a</u>muruhu layusjananna walayakoonan mina a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>ghireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>32.  She said: "There before you<br> Is the man about whom<br> Ye did blame me!<br> I did seek to seduce him from<br> His (true) self but he did<br> Firmly save himself guiltless!…<br> And now, if he doth not<br> My bidding, he shall certainly<br> Be cast into prison,<br> And (what is more)<br> Be of the company of the vilest!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_033.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>33.  Q<u>a</u>la rabbi a<b>l</b>ssijnu a<u>h</u>abbu ilayya mimm<u>a</u> yadAAoonanee ilayhi wa-ill<u>a</u> ta<u>s</u>rif AAannee kaydahunna a<u>s</u>bu ilayhinna waakun mina alj<u>a</u>hileen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>33.  He said: "O my Lord!<br> The prison is more<br> To my liking than that<br> To which they invite me:<br> Unless Thou turn away<br> Their snare from me,<br> I should (in my youthful folly)<br> Feel inclined towards them<br> And join the ranks of the ignorant."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_034.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>34.  Fa<b>i</b>staj<u>a</u>ba lahu rabbuhu fa<u>s</u>arafa AAanhu kaydahunna innahu huwa a<b>l</b>ssameeAAu alAAaleem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>34.  So his Lord hearkened to him<br> (In his prayer), and turned<br> Away from him their snare:<br> Verily He heareth and knoweth<br> (All things).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_035.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>35.  Thumma bad<u>a</u> lahum min baAAdi m<u>a</u> raawoo al-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>ti layasjununnahu <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> <u>h</u>een<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>35.  Then it occurred to the men,<br> After they had seen the Signs,<br> (That it was best)<br> To Imprison him<br> For a time.</p> <div class="filenav">
