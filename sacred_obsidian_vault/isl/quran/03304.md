 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXXIII.: Aḥzāb, or The Confederates.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 4</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/033/033_028.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>28.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nnabiyyu qul li-azw<u>a</u>jika in kuntunna turidna al<u>h</u>ay<u>a</u>ta a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> wazeenatah<u>a</u> fataAA<u>a</u>layna omattiAAkunna waosarri<u>h</u>kunna sar<u>ah</u>an jameel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>28.  O Prophet! say<br> To thy Consorts:<br> "If it be that ye desire<br> The life of this world,<br> And its glitter,—then come!<br> I will provide for your<br>Enjoyment and set you free<br> In a handsome manner.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/033/033_029.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>29.  Wa-in kuntunna turidna All<u>a</u>ha warasoolahu wa<b>al</b>dd<u>a</u>ra al-<u>a</u>khirata fa-inna All<u>a</u>ha aAAadda lilmu<u>h</u>sin<u>a</u>ti minkunna ajran AAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>29.  But if ye seek God<br> And His Apostle, and<br> The Home of the Hereafter,<br> Verily God has prepared<br> For the well-doers amongst you<br> A great reward.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/033/033_030.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>30.  Y<u>a</u> nis<u>a</u>a a<b>l</b>nnabiyyi man ya/ti minkunna bif<u>ah</u>ishatin mubayyinatin yu<u>da</u>AAaf lah<u>a</u> alAAa<u>tha</u>bu <u>d</u>iAAfayni wak<u>a</u>na <u>tha</u>lika AAal<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi yaseer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>30.  O Consorts of the Prophet<br> If any of you were guilty<br> Of evident unseemly conduct,<br> The Punishment would be<br> Doubled to her, and that<br> Is easy for God</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/033/033_031.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>31.  Waman yaqnut minkunna lill<u>a</u>hi warasoolihi wataAAmal <u>sa</u>li<u>h</u>an nu/tih<u>a</u> ajrah<u>a</u> marratayni waaAAtadn<u>a</u> lah<u>a</u> rizqan kareem<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>31.  But any of you that is<br> Devout in the service of<br> God and His Apostle,<br> And works righteousness,—<br> To her shall We grant<br> Her reward twice: and We<br> Have prepared for her<br> A generous Sustenance.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/033/033_032.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>32.  Y<u>a</u> nis<u>a</u>a a<b>l</b>nnabiyyi lastunna kaa<u>h</u>adin mina a<b>l</b>nnis<u>a</u>-i ini ittaqaytunna fal<u>a</u> takh<u>d</u>aAAna bi<b>a</b>lqawli faya<u>t</u>maAAa alla<u>th</u>ee fee qalbihi mara<u>d</u>un waqulna qawlan maAAroof<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>32.  O Consorts of the Prophet!<br> Ye are not like any<br> Of the (other) women:<br> If ye do fear (God),<br> Be not too complaisant<br> Of speech, lest one<br> In whose heart is<br> A disease should be moved<br> With desire: but speak ye<br> A speech (that is) just.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/033/033_033.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>33.  Waqarna fee buyootikunna wal<u>a</u> tabarrajna tabarruja alj<u>a</u>hiliyyati al-ool<u>a</u> waaqimna a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>al<u>a</u>ta wa<u>a</u>teena a<b>l</b>zzak<u>a</u>ta waa<u>t</u>iAAna All<u>a</u>ha warasoolahu innam<u>a</u> yureedu All<u>a</u>hu liyu<u>th</u>hiba AAankumu a<b>l</b>rrijsa ahla albayti wayu<u>t</u>ahhirakum ta<u>t</u>heer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>33.  And stay quietly in<br> Your houses, and make not<br> A dazzling display, like<br> That of the former Times<br> Of Ignorance; and establish<br> Regular Prayer, and give<br> Regular Charity; and obey<br> God and His Apostle.<br> And God only wishes<br> To remove all abomination<br>From you, ye Members<br> Of the Family, and to make<br> You pure and spotless.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/033/033_034.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>34.  Wa<b>o</b><u>th</u>kurna m<u>a</u> yutl<u>a</u> fee buyootikunna min <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>ti All<u>a</u>hi wa<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>ikmati inna All<u>a</u>ha k<u>a</u>na la<u>t</u>eefan khabeer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>34.  And recite what is<br> Rehearsed to you in your<br> Homes, of the Signs of God<br> And His Wisdom:<br> For God understands<br> The finest mysteries and<br> Is well-acquainted (with them).</p> <div class="filenav">
