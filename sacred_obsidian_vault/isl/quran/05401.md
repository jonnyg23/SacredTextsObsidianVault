 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra LIV.: Qamar, or the Moon.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/054/054_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  Iqtarabati a<b>l</b>ss<u>a</u>AAatu wa<b>i</b>nshaqqa alqamar<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>1.  The Hour (of Judgment)<br> Is nigh, and the moon<br> Is cleft asunder.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/054/054_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  Wa-in yaraw <u>a</u>yatan yuAAri<u>d</u>oo wayaqooloo si<u>h</u>run mustamirr<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>2.  But if they see<br> A Sign, they turn away,<br> And say, "This is<br> (But) transient magic."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/054/054_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  Waka<u>thth</u>aboo wa<b>i</b>ttabaAAoo ahw<u>a</u>ahum wakullu amrin mustaqirr<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>3.  They reject (the warning)<br> And follow their (own) lusts<br> But every matter has<br> Its appointed time.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/054/054_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  Walaqad j<u>a</u>ahum mina al-anb<u>a</u>-i m<u>a</u> feehi muzdajar<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>4.  There have already come<br> To them Recitals wherein<br> There is (enough) to check (them),</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/054/054_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  <u>H</u>ikmatun b<u>a</u>lighatun fam<u>a</u> tughnee a<b>l</b>nnu<u>th</u>ur<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>5.  Mature wisdom;—but<br> (The preaching of) Warners<br> Profits them not.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/054/054_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  Fatawalla AAanhum yawma yadAAu a<b>l</b>dd<u>a</u>AAi il<u>a</u> shay-in nukur<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>6.  Therefore, (O Prophet,)<br> Turn away from them.<br> The Day that the Caller<br> Will call (them)<br> To a terrible affair,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/054/054_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  KhushshaAAan ab<u>sa</u>ruhum yakhrujoona mina al-ajd<u>a</u>thi kaannahum jar<u>a</u>dun muntashir<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>7.  They will come forth,<br> Their eyes humbled—<br> From (their) graves, (torpid)<br> Like locusts scattered abroad,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/054/054_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  Muh<u>t</u>iAAeena il<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>dd<u>a</u>AAi yaqoolu alk<u>a</u>firoona h<u>atha</u> yawmun AAasir<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>8.  Hastening, with eyes transfixed,<br> Towards the Caller!—<br> "Hard is this Day!",<br> The Unbelievers will say.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/054/054_009.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>9.  Ka<u>thth</u>abat qablahum qawmu noo<u>h</u>in faka<u>thth</u>aboo AAabdan<u>a</u> waq<u>a</u>loo majnoonun wa<b>i</b>zdujir<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>9.  Before them the People<br> Of Noah rejected (their apostle):<br> They rejected Our servant,<br> And said, "Here is<br> One possessed!", and he<br> Was driven out.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/054/054_010.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>10.  FadaAA<u>a</u> rabbahu annee maghloobun fa<b>i</b>nta<u>s</u>ir   </p>
 <p>10.  Then he called on his Lord:<br> "I am one overcome:<br> Do Thou then help (me)!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/054/054_011.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>11.  Fafata<u>h</u>n<u>a</u> abw<u>a</u>ba a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>-i bim<u>a</u>-in munhamir<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>11.  So We opened the gates<br> Of heaven, with water<br> Pouring forth.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/054/054_012.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>12.  Wafajjarn<u>a</u> al-ar<u>d</u>a AAuyoonan fa<b>i</b>ltaq<u>a</u> alm<u>a</u>o AAal<u>a</u> amrin qad qudir<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>12.  And We caused the earth<br> To gush forth with springs.<br> So the waters met (and rose)<br> To the extent decreed.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/054/054_013.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>13.  Wa<u>h</u>amaln<u>a</u>hu AAal<u>a</u> <u>tha</u>ti alw<u>ah</u>in wadusur<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>13.  But We bore him<br> On an (Ark) made of<br> Broad planks and caulked<br> With palm-fibre:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/054/054_014.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>14.  Tajree bi-aAAyunin<u>a</u> jaz<u>a</u>an liman k<u>a</u>na kufir<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>14.  She floats under our eyes<br> (And care): a recompense<br> To one who had been<br> Rejected (with scorn)!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/054/054_015.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>15.  Walaqad tarakn<u>a</u>h<u>a</u> <u>a</u>yatan fahal min muddakir<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>15.  And We have left<br> This as a Sign<br> (For all time): then<br> Is there any that will<br> Receive admonition?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/054/054_016.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>16.  Fakayfa k<u>a</u>na AAa<u>tha</u>bee wanu<u>th</u>ur<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>16.  But how (terrible) was<br> My Penalty and My Warning?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/054/054_017.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>17.  Walaqad yassarn<u>a</u> alqur-<u>a</u>na li<b>l</b><u>thth</u>ikri fahal min muddakir<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>17.  And We have indeed<br> Made the Qur-ān easy<br>To understand and remember:<br> Then is there any that<br> Will receive admonition?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/054/054_018.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>18.  Ka<u>thth</u>abat AA<u>a</u>dun fakayfa k<u>a</u>na AAa<u>tha</u>bee wanu<u>th</u>ur<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>18.  The ‘Ād (people) (too)<br> Rejected (Truth): then<br> How terrible was<br> My Penalty and My Warning?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/054/054_019.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>19.  Inn<u>a</u> arsaln<u>a</u> AAalayhim ree<u>h</u>an <u>s</u>ar<u>s</u>aran fee yawmi na<u>h</u>sin mustamirr<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>19.  For We sent against them<br> A furious wind, on a Day<br> Of violent Disaster,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/054/054_020.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>20.  TanziAAu a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>sa kaannahum aAAj<u>a</u>zu nakhlin munqaAAir<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>20.  Plucking out men as if<br> They were roots of palm-trees<br> Torn up (from the ground).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/054/054_021.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>21.  Fakayfa k<u>a</u>na AAa<u>tha</u>bee wanu<u>th</u>ur<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>21.  Yea, how (terrible) was<br> My Penalty and My Warning!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/054/054_022.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>22.  Walaqad yassarn<u>a</u> alqur-<u>a</u>na li<b>l</b><u>thth</u>ikri fahal min muddakir<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>22.  But We have indeed<br> Made the Qur-ān easy<br> To understand and remember:<br> Then is there any that<br> Will receive admonition?</p> <div class="filenav">
