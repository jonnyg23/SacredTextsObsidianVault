 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra IV.: Nisāa, or The Women.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 23</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_163.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>163.  Inn<u>a</u> aw<u>h</u>ayn<u>a</u> ilayka kam<u>a</u> aw<u>h</u>ayn<u>a</u> il<u>a</u> noo<u>h</u>in wa<b>al</b>nnabiyyeena min baAAdihi waaw<u>h</u>ayn<u>a</u> il<u>a</u> ibr<u>a</u>heema wa-ism<u>a</u>AAeela wa-is<u>ha</u>qa wayaAAqooba wa<b>a</b>l-asb<u>at</u>i waAAees<u>a</u> waayyooba wayoonusa wah<u>a</u>roona wasulaym<u>a</u>na wa<u>a</u>tayn<u>a</u> d<u>a</u>wooda zaboor<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>163.  We have sent thee<br> Inspiration, as We sent it<br> To Noah and the Messengers<br> After him: We sent<br> Inspiration to Abraham,<br> Ismail, Isaac, Jacob<br> And the Tribes, to Jesus,<br> Job, Jonah, Aaron, and Solomon,<br> And to David We gave<br> The Psalms.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_164.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>164.  Warusulan qad qa<u>s</u>a<u>s</u>n<u>a</u>hum AAalayka min qablu warusulan lam naq<u>s</u>u<u>s</u>hum AAalayka wakallama All<u>a</u>hu moos<u>a</u> takleem<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>164.  Of some apostles We have<br> Already told thee the story;<br> Of others we have not;—<br> And to Moses God spoke direct;—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_165.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>165.  Rusulan mubashshireena wamun<u>th</u>ireena li-all<u>a</u> yakoona li<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si AAal<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi <u>h</u>ujjatun baAAda a<b>l</b>rrusuli wak<u>a</u>na All<u>a</u>hu AAazeezan <u>h</u>akeem<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>165.  Apostles who gave good news<br> As well as warning,<br> That mankind, after (the coming)<br> Of the apostles, should have<br> No plea against God:<br> For God is Exalted in Power,<br> Wise.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_166.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>166.  L<u>a</u>kini All<u>a</u>hu yashhadu bim<u>a</u> anzala ilayka anzalahu biAAilmihi wa<b>a</b>lmal<u>a</u>-ikatu yashhadoona wakaf<u>a</u> bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi shaheed<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>166.  But God beareth witness<br> That what He hath sent<br> Unto thee He hath sent<br> From His (own) knowledge,<br>And the angels bear witness:<br> But enough is God for a witness.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_167.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>167.  Inna alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo wa<u>s</u>addoo AAan sabeeli All<u>a</u>hi qad <u>d</u>alloo <u>d</u>al<u>a</u>lan baAAeed<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>167.  Those who reject Faith<br> And keep off (men)<br> From the Way of God,<br> Have verily strayed far,<br> Far away from the Path.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_168.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>168.  Inna alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo wa<i><u>th</u></i>alamoo lam yakuni All<u>a</u>hu liyaghfira lahum wal<u>a</u> liyahdiyahum <u>t</u>areeq<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>168.  Those who reject Faith<br> And do wrong,—God<br> Will not forgive them<br> Nor guide them<br> To any way—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_169.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>169.  Ill<u>a</u> <u>t</u>areeqa jahannama kh<u>a</u>lideena feeh<u>a</u> abadan wak<u>a</u>na <u>tha</u>lika AAal<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi yaseer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>169.  Except the way of Hell,<br> To dwell therein for ever.<br> And this to God is easy.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_170.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>170.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>su qad j<u>a</u>akumu a<b>l</b>rrasoolu bi<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>aqqi min rabbikum fa<u>a</u>minoo khayran lakum wa-in takfuroo fa-inna lill<u>a</u>hi m<u>a</u> fee a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i wak<u>a</u>na All<u>a</u>hu AAaleeman <u>h</u>akeem<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>170.  O mankind! the Apostle<br> Hath come to you in truth<br> From God: believe in him:<br> It is best for you. But if<br> Ye reject Faith, to God<br> Belong all things in the heavens<br> And on earth: and God<br> Is All-knowing, All-wise.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_171.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>171.  Y<u>a</u> ahla alkit<u>a</u>bi l<u>a</u> taghloo fee deenikum wal<u>a</u> taqooloo AAal<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi ill<u>a</u> al<u>h</u>aqqa innam<u>a</u> almasee<u>h</u>u AAees<u>a</u> ibnu maryama rasoolu All<u>a</u>hi wakalimatuhu alq<u>a</u>h<u>a</u> il<u>a</u> maryama waroo<u>h</u>un minhu fa<u>a</u>minoo bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi warusulihi wal<u>a</u> taqooloo thal<u>a</u>thatun intahoo khayran lakum innam<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hu il<u>a</u>hun w<u>ah</u>idun sub<u>ha</u>nahu an yakoona lahu waladun lahu m<u>a</u> fee a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wam<u>a</u> fee al-ar<u>d</u>i wakaf<u>a</u> bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi wakeel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>171.  O People of the Book!<br> Commit no excesses<br> In your religion: nor say<br> Of God aught but the truth.<br>Christ Jesus the son of Mary<br> Was (no more than)<br> An apostle of God,<br> And His Word,<br> Which He bestowed on Mary,<br> And a Spirit proceeding<br> From Him: so believe<br> In God and His apostles.<br> Say not "Trinity": desist:<br> It will be better for you:<br> For God is One God:<br> Glory be to Him:<br> (Far Exalted is He) above<br> Having a son, To Him<br> Belong all things in the heavens<br> And on earth. And enough<br> Is God as a Disposer of affairs.</p> <div class="filenav">
