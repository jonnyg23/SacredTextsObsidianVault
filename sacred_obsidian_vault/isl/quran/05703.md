 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/057/057_020.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>20.  IAAlamoo annam<u>a</u> al<u>h</u>ay<u>a</u>tu a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> laAAibun walahwun wazeenatun wataf<u>a</u>khurun baynakum watak<u>a</u>thurun fee al-amw<u>a</u>li wa<b>a</b>l-awl<u>a</u>di kamathali ghaythin aAAjaba alkuff<u>a</u>ra nab<u>a</u>tuhu thumma yaheeju fatar<u>a</u>hu mu<u>s</u>farran thumma yakoonu <u>h</u>u<u>ta</u>man wafee al-<u>a</u>khirati AAa<u>tha</u>bun shadeedun wamaghfiratun mina All<u>a</u>hi wari<u>d</u>w<u>a</u>nun wam<u>a</u> al<u>h</u>ay<u>a</u>tu a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> ill<u>a</u> mat<u>a</u>AAu alghuroor<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>20.  Know ye (all), that<br> The life of this world<br> Is but play and amusement,<br> Pomp and mutual boasting<br> And multiplying, (in rivalry)<br> Among yourselves, riches<br> And children.<br> Here is a similitude:<br> How rain and the growth<br> Which it brings forth, delight<br> (The hearts of) the tillers;<br> Soon it withers; thou<br> Wilt see it grow yellow;<br> Then it becomes dry<br> And crumbles away.<br> But in the Hereafter<br> Is a Penalty severe<br> (For the devotees of wrong).<br> And Forgiveness from God<br> And (His) Good Pleasure<br>(For the devotees of God).<br> And what is the life<br> Of this world, but<br> Goods and chattels<br> Of deception?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/057/057_021.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>21.  S<u>a</u>biqoo il<u>a</u> maghfiratin min rabbikum wajannatin AAar<u>d</u>uh<u>a</u> kaAAar<u>d</u>i a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>-i wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i oAAiddat lilla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi warusulihi <u>tha</u>lika fa<u>d</u>lu All<u>a</u>hi yu/teehi man yash<u>a</u>o wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu <u>th</u>oo alfa<u>d</u>li alAAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>21.  Be ye foremost (in seeking)<br> Forgiveness from your Lord,<br> And a Garden (of Bliss),<br> The width whereof is<br> As the width of<br> Heaven and earth,<br> Prepared for those who believe<br> In God and His apostles:<br> That is the Grace of God,<br> Which He bestows on whom<br> He pleases: and God is<br> The Lord of Grace abounding.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/057/057_022.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>22.  M<u>a</u> a<u>sa</u>ba min mu<u>s</u>eebatin fee al-ar<u>d</u>i wal<u>a</u> fee anfusikum ill<u>a</u> fee kit<u>a</u>bin min qabli an nabraah<u>a</u> inna <u>tha</u>lika AAal<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi yaseer<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>22.  No misfortune can happen<br> On earth or in your souls<br> But is recorded in<br> A decree before We bring<br> It into existence:<br> That is truly easy for God:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/057/057_023.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>23.  Likayl<u>a</u> ta/saw AAal<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> f<u>a</u>takum wal<u>a</u> tafra<u>h</u>oo bim<u>a</u> <u>a</u>t<u>a</u>kum wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu l<u>a</u> yu<u>h</u>ibbu kulla mukht<u>a</u>lin fakhoor<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>23.  In order that ye may<br> Not despair over matters<br> That pass you by,<br> Nor exult over favours<br> Bestowed upon you.<br> For God loveth not<br>Any vainglorious boaster,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/057/057_024.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>24.  Alla<u>th</u>eena yabkhaloona waya/muroona a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>sa bi<b>a</b>lbukhli waman yatawalla fa-inna All<u>a</u>ha huwa alghaniyyu al<u>h</u>ameed<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>24.  Such persons as are<br> Covetous and commend<br> Covetousness to men.<br> And if any turn back<br> (From God's Way), verily<br> God is free of all needs,<br> Worthy of all praise.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/057/057_025.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>25.  Laqad arsaln<u>a</u> rusulan<u>a</u> bi<b>a</b>lbayyin<u>a</u>ti waanzaln<u>a</u> maAAahumu alkit<u>a</u>ba wa<b>a</b>lmeez<u>a</u>na liyaqooma a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>su bi<b>a</b>lqis<u>t</u>i waanzaln<u>a</u> al<u>h</u>adeeda feehi ba/sun shadeedun waman<u>a</u>fiAAu li<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si waliyaAAlama All<u>a</u>hu man yan<u>s</u>uruhu warusulahu bi<b>a</b>lghaybi inna All<u>a</u>ha qawiyyun AAazeez<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>25.  We sent aforetime<br> Our apostles with Clear Signs<br> And sent down with them<br> The Book and the Balance<br> (Of Right and Wrong), that men<br> May stand forth in justice;<br> And We sent down Iron<br> In which is (material for)<br> Mighty war, as well as<br> Many benefits for mankind,<br> That God may test who<br> It is that will help,<br>Unseen, Him and His apostles:<br> For God is Full of Strength,<br> Exalted in Might<br> (And able to enforce His Will).</p> <div class="filenav">
