 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_101.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>101.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> <u>d</u>arabtum fee al-ar<u>d</u>i falaysa AAalaykum jun<u>ah</u>un an taq<u>s</u>uroo mina a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>al<u>a</u>ti in khiftum an yaftinakumu alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo inna alk<u>a</u>fireena k<u>a</u>noo lakum AAaduwwan mubeen<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>101.  When ye travel<br> Through the earth,<br> There is no blame on you<br> If ye shorten your prayers,<br> For fear the Unbelievers<br> May attack you:<br> For the Unbelievers are<br> Unto you open enemies.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_102.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>102.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> kunta feehim faaqamta lahumu a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>al<u>a</u>ta faltaqum <u>ta</u>-ifatun minhum maAAaka walya/khu<u>th</u>oo asli<u>h</u>atahum fa-i<u>tha</u> sajadoo falyakoonoo min war<u>a</u>-ikum walta/ti <u>ta</u>-ifatun okhr<u>a</u> lam yu<u>s</u>alloo falyu<u>s</u>alloo maAAaka walya/khu<u>th</u>oo <u>h</u>i<u>th</u>rahum waasli<u>h</u>atahum wadda alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo law taghfuloona AAan asli<u>h</u>atikum waamtiAAatikum fayameeloona AAalaykum maylatan w<u>ah</u>idatan wal<u>a</u> jun<u>ah</u>a AAalaykum in k<u>a</u>na bikum a<u>th</u>an min ma<u>t</u>arin aw kuntum mar<u>da</u> an ta<u>d</u>aAAoo asli<u>h</u>atakum wakhu<u>th</u>oo <u>h</u>i<u>th</u>rakum inna All<u>a</u>ha aAAadda lilk<u>a</u>fireena AAa<u>tha</u>ban muheen<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>102.  When thou (O Apostle)<br> Art with them, and standest<br> To lead them in prayer,<br> Let one party of them<br> Stand up (in prayer) with thee,<br> Taking their arms with them:<br> When they finish<br> Their prostrations, let them<br> Take their position in the rear.<br> And let the other party come up<br> Which hath not yet prayed—<br> And let them pray with thee,<br> Taking all precautions,<br> And bearing arms:<br> The Unbelievers wish,<br> If ye were negligent<br> Of your arms and your baggage,<br> To assault you in a single rush.<br> But there is no blame on you<br> If ye put away your arms<br> Because of the inconvenience<br> Of rain or because ye are ill;<br> But take (every) precaution<br> For yourselves. For the<br> Unbelievers<br> God hath prepared<br> A humiliating punishment.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_103.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>103.  Fa-i<u>tha</u> qa<u>d</u>aytumu a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>al<u>a</u>ta fa<b>o</b><u>th</u>kuroo All<u>a</u>ha qiy<u>a</u>man waquAAoodan waAAal<u>a</u> junoobikum fa-i<u>tha</u> i<u>t</u>ma/nantum faaqeemoo a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>al<u>a</u>ta inna a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>al<u>a</u>ta k<u>a</u>nat AAal<u>a</u> almu/mineena kit<u>a</u>ban mawqoot<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>103.  When ye pass<br> (Congregational) prayers,<br> Celebrate God's praises,<br> Standing, sitting down,<br> Or lying down on your sides;<br> But when ye are free<br> From danger, set up<br> Regular Prayers:<br> For such prayers<br> Are enjoined on Believers<br> At stated times.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_104.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>104.  Wal<u>a</u> tahinoo fee ibtigh<u>a</u>-i alqawmi in takoonoo ta/lamoona fa-innahum ya/lamoona kam<u>a</u> ta/lamoona watarjoona mina All<u>a</u>hi m<u>a</u> l<u>a</u> yarjoona wak<u>a</u>na All<u>a</u>hu AAaleeman <u>h</u>akeem<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>104.  And slacken not<br> In following up the enemy:<br> If ye are suffering hardships,<br> They are suffering similar<br> Hardships; but ye have<br> Hope from God, while they<br> Have none. And God<br> Is full of knowledge and wisdom.</p> <div class="filenav">
