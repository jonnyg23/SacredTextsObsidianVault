 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XC.: Balad, or The City.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/090/090_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  L<u>a</u> oqsimu bih<u>atha</u> albalad<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>1.  I do call to witness<br> This City;—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/090/090_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  Waanta <u>h</u>illun bih<u>atha</u> albalad<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>2.  And thou art a freeman<br> Of this City;—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/090/090_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  Waw<u>a</u>lidin wam<u>a</u> walad<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>3.  And (the mystic ties<br> of) Parent and Child;—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/090/090_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  Laqad khalaqn<u>a</u> al-ins<u>a</u>na fee kabad<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>4.  Verily We have created<br> Man into toil and struggle.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/090/090_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  Aya<u>h</u>sabu an lan yaqdira AAalayhi a<u>h</u>ad<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>5.  Thinketh he, that none<br> Hath power over him?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/090/090_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  Yaqoolu ahlaktu m<u>a</u>lan lubad<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>6.  He may say (boastfully):<br> "Wealth have I squandered<br> In abundance!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/090/090_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  Aya<u>h</u>sabu an lam yarahu a<u>h</u>ad<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>7.  Thinketh he that none<br> Beholdeth him?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/090/090_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  Alam najAAal lahu AAaynayn<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>8.  Have We not made<br> For him a pair of eyes?—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/090/090_009.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>9.  Walis<u>a</u>nan washafatayn<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>9.  And a tongue,<br> And a pair of lips?—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/090/090_010.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>10.  Wahadayn<u>a</u>hu a<b>l</b>nnajdayn<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>10.  And shown him<br> The two highways?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/090/090_011.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>11.  Fal<u>a</u> iqta<u>h</u>ama alAAaqaba<b>ta</b>   </p>
 <p>11.  But he hath made no haste<br> On the path that is steep.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/090/090_012.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>12.  Wam<u>a</u> adr<u>a</u>ka m<u>a</u> alAAaqaba<b>tu</b>   </p>
 <p>12.  And what will explain<br> To thee the path that is steep?—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/090/090_013.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>13.  Fakku raqaba<b>tin</b>    </p>
 <p>13.  (It is:) freeing the bondman;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/090/090_014.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>14.  Aw i<u>t</u>AA<u>a</u>mun fee yawmin <u>th</u>ee masghaba<b>tin</b>   </p>
 <p>14.  Or the giving of food<br> In a day of privation</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/090/090_015.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>15.  Yateeman <u>tha</u> maqraba<b>tin</b>   </p>
 <p>15.  To the orphan<br> With claims of relationship,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/090/090_016.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>16.  Aw miskeenan <u>tha</u> matraba<b>tin</b>   </p>
 <p>16.  Or to the indigent<br> (Down) in the dust.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/090/090_017.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>17.  Thumma k<u>a</u>na mina alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo wataw<u>as</u>aw bi<b>al</b><u>ss</u>abri wataw<u>as</u>aw bi<b>a</b>lmar<u>h</u>ama<b>ti</b>   </p>
 <p>17.  Then will he be<br> Of those who believe,<br> And enjoin patience, (constancy,<br> And self-restraint), and enjoin<br> Deeds of kindness and compassion.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/090/090_018.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>18.  Ol<u>a</u>-ika a<u>s</u>-<u>ha</u>bu almaymana<b>ti</b>   </p>
 <p>18.  Such are the Companions<br> Of the Right Hand.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/090/090_019.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>19.  Wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin<u>a</u> hum a<u>s</u>-<u>ha</u>bu almash-ama<b>ti</b>   </p>
 <p>19.  But those who reject<br> Our Signs, they are<br> The (unhappy) Companions<br> Of the Left Hand.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/090/090_020.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>20.  AAalayhim n<u>a</u>run mu/<u>s</u>ada<b>tun</b>   </p>
 <p>20.  On them will be Fire<br> Vaulted over (all round).</p>  <div class="filenav">
