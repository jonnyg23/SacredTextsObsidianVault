 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XVI.: Naḥl or The Bee.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 5</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_035.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>35.  Waq<u>a</u>la alla<u>th</u>eena ashrakoo law sh<u>a</u>a All<u>a</u>hu m<u>a</u> AAabadn<u>a</u> min doonihi min shay-in na<u>h</u>nu wal<u>a</u> <u>a</u>b<u>a</u>on<u>a</u> wal<u>a</u> <u>h</u>arramn<u>a</u> min doonihi min shay-in ka<u>tha</u>lika faAAala alla<u>th</u>eena min qablihim fahal AAal<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>rrusuli ill<u>a</u> albal<u>a</u>ghu almubeen<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>35.  The worshippers of false gods<br> Say: "If God had so willed,<br> We should not have worshipped<br> Aught but Him—neither we<br>Nor our fathers,—nor should<br> We have prescribed prohibitions<br> Other than His." So did those<br> Who went before them.<br> But what is the mission<br> Of apostles but to preach<br> The Clear Message?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_036.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>36.  Walaqad baAAathn<u>a</u> fee kulli ommatin rasoolan ani oAAbudoo All<u>a</u>ha wa<b>i</b>jtaniboo a<b>l</b><u>tta</u>ghoota faminhum man had<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hu waminhum man <u>h</u>aqqat AAalayhi a<b>l</b><u>dd</u>al<u>a</u>latu faseeroo fee al-ar<u>d</u>i fa<b>o</b>n<i><u>th</u></i>uroo kayfa k<u>a</u>na AA<u>a</u>qibatu almuka<u>thth</u>ibeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>36.  For We assuredly sent<br> Amongst every People an apostle,<br> (With the Command), "Serve<br> God, and eschew Evil":<br> Of the people were some whom<br> God guided, and some<br> On whom Error became<br> Inevitably (established). So travel<br> Through the earth, and see<br> What was the end of those<br> Who denied (the Truth).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_037.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>37.  In ta<u>h</u>ri<u>s</u> AAal<u>a</u> hud<u>a</u>hum fa-inna All<u>a</u>ha l<u>a</u> yahdee man yu<u>d</u>illu wam<u>a</u> lahum min n<u>as</u>ireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>37.  If thou art anxious<br> For their guidance, yet<br> God guideth not such<br> As He leaves to stray,<br> And there is none<br> To help them.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_038.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>38.  Waaqsamoo bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi jahda aym<u>a</u>nihim l<u>a</u> yabAAathu All<u>a</u>hu man yamootu bal<u>a</u> waAAdan AAalayhi <u>h</u>aqqan wal<u>a</u>kinna akthara a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si l<u>a</u> yaAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>38.  They swear their strongest oaths<br> By God, that God will not<br>Raise up those who die<br> Nay, but it is a promise<br> (Binding) on Him in truth:<br> But most among mankind<br> Realise it not.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_039.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>39.  Liyubayyina lahumu alla<u>th</u>ee yakhtalifoona feehi waliyaAAlama alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo annahum k<u>a</u>noo k<u>ath</u>ibeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>39.  (They must be raised up),<br> In order that He may manifest<br> To them the truth of that<br> Wherein they differ, and that<br> The rejecters of Truth<br> May realise that they had<br> Indeed (surrendered to)<br> Falsehood.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_040.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>40.  Innam<u>a</u> qawlun<u>a</u> lishay-in i<u>tha</u> aradn<u>a</u>hu an naqoola lahu kun fayakoon<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>40.  For to anything which We<br> Have willed, We but say<br> The Word, "Be ", and it is.</p> <div class="filenav">
