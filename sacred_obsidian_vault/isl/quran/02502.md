 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXV.: Furqān, or The Criterion.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 2</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/025/025_010.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>10.  Tab<u>a</u>raka alla<u>th</u>ee in sh<u>a</u>a jaAAala laka khayran min <u>tha</u>lika jann<u>a</u>tin tajree min ta<u>h</u>tih<u>a</u> al-anh<u>a</u>ru wayajAAal laka qu<u>s</u>oor<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>10.  Blessed is He Who,<br> If that were His Will,<br> Could give thee better (things)<br> Than those,—Gardens beneath which<br> Rivers flow; and He could<br> Give thee Palaces (secure<br> To dwell in).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/025/025_011.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>11.  Bal ka<u>thth</u>aboo bi<b>al</b>ss<u>a</u>AAati waaAAtadn<u>a</u> liman ka<u>thth</u>aba bi<b>al</b>ss<u>a</u>AAati saAAeer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>11.  Nay, they deny the Hour<br> (Of the Judgment to come):<br> But We have prepared<br> A Blazing Fire for such<br> As deny the Hour:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/025/025_012.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>12.  I<u>tha</u> raat-hum min mak<u>a</u>nin baAAeedin samiAAoo lah<u>a</u> taghayyu<i><u>th</u></i>an wazafeer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>12.  When it sees them<br> From a place far off,<br> They will hear its fury<br> And its raging sigh.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/025/025_013.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>13.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> olqoo minh<u>a</u> mak<u>a</u>nan <u>d</u>ayyiqan muqarraneena daAAaw hun<u>a</u>lika thuboor<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>13.  And when they are cast,<br> Bound together, into a<br> Constricted place therein, they<br> Will plead for destruction<br> There and then!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/025/025_014.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>14.  L<u>a</u> tadAAoo alyawma thubooran w<u>ah</u>idan wa<b>o</b>dAAoo thubooran katheer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>14.  "This day plead not<br> For a single destruction:<br> Plead for destruction oft-repeated!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/025/025_015.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>15.  Qul a<u>tha</u>lika khayrun am jannatu alkhuldi allatee wuAAida almuttaqoona k<u>a</u>nat lahum jaz<u>a</u>an wama<u>s</u>eer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>15.  Say: "Is that best, or<br> The eternal Garden, promised<br> To the righteous? For them,<br> That is a reward as well<br> As a goal (of attainment).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/025/025_016.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>16.  Lahum feeh<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> yash<u>a</u>oona kh<u>a</u>lideena k<u>a</u>na AAal<u>a</u> rabbika waAAdan mas-ool<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>16.  "For them there will be<br> Therein all that they wish for:<br> They will dwell (there) for aye:<br> A promise to be prayed for<br> From thy Lord."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/025/025_017.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>17.  Wayawma ya<u>h</u>shuruhum wam<u>a</u> yaAAbudoona min dooni All<u>a</u>hi fayaqoolu aantum a<u>d</u>laltum AAib<u>a</u>dee h<u>a</u>ol<u>a</u>-i am hum <u>d</u>alloo a<b>l</b>ssabeel<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>17.  The Day He will gather<br> Them together as well as<br> Those whom they worship<br> Besides God, He will ask;<br>"Was it ye who led<br> These My servants astray,<br> Or did they stray<br> From the Path themselves?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/025/025_018.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>18.  Q<u>a</u>loo sub<u>ha</u>naka m<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>na yanbaghee lan<u>a</u> an nattakhi<u>th</u>a min doonika min awliy<u>a</u>a wal<u>a</u>kin mattaAAtahum wa<u>a</u>b<u>a</u>ahum <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> nasoo a<b>l</b><u>thth</u>ikra wak<u>a</u>noo qawman boor<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>18.  They will say: "Glory to Thee!<br> Not meet was it for us<br> That we should take<br> For protectors others besides Thee:<br> But Thou didst bestow,<br> On them and their fathers,<br> Good things (in life), until<br> They forgot the Message:<br> For they were a people<br> (Worthless and) lost."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/025/025_019.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>19.  Faqad ka<u>thth</u>abookum bim<u>a</u> taqooloona fam<u>a</u> tasta<u>t</u>eeAAoona <u>s</u>arfan wal<u>a</u> na<u>s</u>ran waman ya<i><u>th</u></i>lim minkum nu<u>th</u>iqhu AAa<u>tha</u>ban kabeer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>19.  (God will say): "Now<br> Have they proved you liars<br> In what ye say: so<br> Ye cannot avert (your penalty)<br> Nor (get) help." And whoever<br> Among you does wrong,<br> Him shall We cause to taste<br> Of a grievous Penalty.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/025/025_020.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>20.  Wam<u>a</u> arsaln<u>a</u> qablaka mina almursaleena ill<u>a</u> innahum laya/kuloona a<b>l</b><u>tt</u>aAA<u>a</u>ma wayamshoona fee al-asw<u>a</u>qi wajaAAaln<u>a</u> baAA<u>d</u>akum libaAA<u>d</u>in fitnatan ata<u>s</u>biroona wak<u>a</u>na rabbuka ba<u>s</u>eer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>20.  And the apostles whom We<br> Sent before thee were all<br> (Men) who ate food<br> And walked through the streets:<br> We have made some of you<br> As a trial for others:<br> Will ye have patience?<br> For God is One Who<br> Sees (all things).</p> <div class="filenav">
