 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra LI.: Zāriyāt, or the Winds That Scatter.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 2</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/051/051_024.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>24.  Hal at<u>a</u>ka <u>h</u>adeethu <u>d</u>ayfi ibr<u>a</u>heema almukrameen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>24.  Has the story<br> Reached thee, of the honoured<br> Guests of Abraham?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/051/051_025.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>25.  I<u>th</u> dakhaloo AAalayhi faq<u>a</u>loo sal<u>a</u>man q<u>a</u>la sal<u>a</u>mun qawmun munkaroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>25.  Behold, they entered<br> His presence, and said:<br> "Peace!" He said, "Peace"<br> (And thought, "These seem)<br> Unusual people."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/051/051_026.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>26.  Far<u>a</u>gha il<u>a</u> ahlihi faj<u>a</u>a biAAijlin sameen<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>26.  Then he turned quickly<br> To his household, brought<br> Out a fatted calf,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/051/051_027.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>27.  Faqarrabahu ilayhim q<u>a</u>la al<u>a</u> ta/kuloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>27.  And placed it before them…<br> He said, "Will ye not<br> Eat?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/051/051_028.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>28.  Faawjasa minhum kheefatan q<u>a</u>loo l<u>a</u> takhaf wabashsharoohu bighul<u>a</u>min AAaleem<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>28.  (When they did not eat),<br> He conceived a fear of them.<br> They said, "Fear not,"<br> And they gave him<br> Glad tidings of a son<br> Endowed with knowledge.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/051/051_029.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>29.  Faaqbalati imraatuhu fee <u>s</u>arratin fa<u>s</u>akkat wajhah<u>a</u> waq<u>a</u>lat AAajoozun AAaqeem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>29.  But his wife came forward<br> (Laughing) aloud: she smote<br> Her forehead and said:<br> "A barren old woman!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/051/051_030.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>30.  Q<u>a</u>loo ka<u>tha</u>liki q<u>a</u>la rabbuki innahu huwa al<u>h</u>akeemu alAAaleem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>30.  They said, "Even so<br> Has thy Lord spoken:<br> And He is full<br> Of Wisdom and Knowledge."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/051/051_031.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>31.  Q<u>a</u>la fam<u>a</u> kha<u>t</u>bukum ayyuh<u>a</u> almursaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>31.  (Abraham) said: "And what,<br> O ye Messengers,<br> Is your errand (now)?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/051/051_032.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>32.  Q<u>a</u>loo inn<u>a</u> orsiln<u>a</u> il<u>a</u> qawmin mujrimeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>32.  They said, "We have<br> Been sent to a people<br> (Deep) in sin;—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/051/051_033.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>33.  Linursila AAalayhim <u>h</u>ij<u>a</u>ratan min <u>t</u>een<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>33.  "To bring on, on them,<br> (A shower of) stones<br> Of clay (brimstone),</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/051/051_034.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>34.  Musawwamatan AAinda rabbika lilmusrifeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>34.  "Marked as from thy Lord<br> For those who trespass<br> Beyond bounds."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/051/051_035.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>35.  Faakhrajn<u>a</u> man k<u>a</u>na feeh<u>a</u> mina almu/mineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>35.  Then We evacuated<br> Those of the Believers<br> Who were there,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/051/051_036.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>36.  Fam<u>a</u> wajadn<u>a</u> feeh<u>a</u> ghayra baytin mina almuslimeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>36.  But We found not there<br> Any just (Muslim) persons<br> Except in one house:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/051/051_037.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>37.  Watarakn<u>a</u> feeh<u>a</u> <u>a</u>yatan lilla<u>th</u>eena yakh<u>a</u>foona alAAa<u>tha</u>ba al-aleem<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>37.  And We left there<br> A Sign for such as<br> Fear the Grievous Penalty.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/051/051_038.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>38.  Wafee moos<u>a</u> i<u>th</u> arsa<b>l</b>n<u>a</u>hu il<u>a</u> firAAawna bisul<u>ta</u>nin mubeen<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>38.  And in Moses<br> (Was another Sign):<br> Behold, We sent him<br> To Pharaoh, with authority<br> Manifest.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/051/051_039.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>39.  Fatawall<u>a</u> biruknihi waq<u>a</u>la s<u>ah</u>irun aw majnoon<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>39.  But (Pharaoh) turned back<br> With his Chiefs, and said,<br> "A sorcerer, or<br> One possessed!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/051/051_040.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>40.  Faakha<u>th</u>n<u>a</u>hu wajunoodahu fanaba<u>th</u>n<u>a</u>hum fee alyammi wahuwa muleem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>40.  So We took him<br> And his forces, and<br> Threw them into the sea;<br> And his was the blame:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/051/051_041.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>41.  Wafee AA<u>a</u>din i<u>th</u> arsaln<u>a</u> AAalayhimu a<b>l</b>rree<u>h</u>a alAAaqeem<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>41.  And in the ‘Ād (people)<br> (Was another Sign):<br> Behold, We sent against them<br> The devastating Wind:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/051/051_042.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>42.  M<u>a</u> ta<u>th</u>aru min shay-in atat AAalayhi ill<u>a</u> jaAAalat-hu ka<b>al</b>rrameem<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>42.  It left nothing whatever<br> That it came up against,<br> But reduced it to ruin<br> And rottenness.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/051/051_043.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>43.  Wafee thamooda i<u>th</u> qeela lahum tamattaAAoo <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> <u>h</u>een<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>43.  And in the <u>Th</u>amūd<br> (Was another Sign):<br> Behold, they were told,<br> "Enjoy (your brief day)<br> For a little while!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/051/051_044.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>44.  FaAAataw AAan amri rabbihim faakha<u>th</u>at-humu a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>AAiqatu wahum yan<i><u>th</u></i>uroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>44.  But they insolently defied<br> The Command of their Lord:<br> So the stunning noise<br> (Of an earthquake) seized<br> Them, even while they<br> Were looking on.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/051/051_045.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>45.  Fam<u>a</u> ista<u>ta</u>AAoo min qiy<u>a</u>min wam<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo munta<u>s</u>ireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>45.  Then they could not<br> Even stand (on their feet),<br> Nor could they help themselves.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/051/051_046.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>46.  Waqawma noo<u>h</u>in min qablu innahum k<u>a</u>noo qawman f<u>a</u>siqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>46.  So were the People<br> Of Noah before them:<br> For they wickedly transgressed.</p> <div class="filenav">
