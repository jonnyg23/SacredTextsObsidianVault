 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXX.: Rūm, or The Roman Empire.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 6</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/030/030_054.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>54.  All<u>a</u>hu alla<u>th</u>ee khalaqakum min <u>d</u>aAAfin thumma jaAAala min baAAdi <u>d</u>aAAfin quwwatan thumma jaAAala min baAAdi quwwatin <u>d</u>aAAfan washaybatan yakhluqu m<u>a</u> yash<u>a</u>o wahuwa alAAaleemu alqadeer<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>54.  It is God Who<br> Created you in a state<br> Of (helpless) weakness, then<br> Gave (you) strength after weakness,<br>Then, after strength, gave (you)<br> Weakness and a hoary head:<br> He creates as He wills,<br> And it is He Who has<br> All knowledge and power.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/030/030_055.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>55.  Wayawma taqoomu a<b>l</b>ss<u>a</u>AAatu yuqsimu almujrimoona m<u>a</u> labithoo ghayra s<u>a</u>AAatin ka<u>tha</u>lika k<u>a</u>noo yu/fakoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>55.  On the Day that<br> The Hour (of reckoning)<br> Will be established,<br> The transgressors will swear<br> That they tarried not<br> But an hour: thus were<br> They used to being deluded!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/030/030_056.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>56.  Waq<u>a</u>la alla<u>th</u>eena ootoo alAAilma wa<b>a</b>l-eem<u>a</u>na laqad labithtum fee kit<u>a</u>bi All<u>a</u>hi il<u>a</u> yawmi albaAAthi fah<u>atha</u> yawmu albaAAthi wal<u>a</u>kinnakum kuntum l<u>a</u> taAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>56.  But those endued with knowledge<br> And faith will say:<br> "Indeed ye did tarry,<br> Within God's Decree,<br> To the Day of Resurrection,<br> And this is the Day<br> Of Resurrection: but ye—<br> Ye were not aware!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/030/030_057.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>57.  Fayawma-i<u>th</u>in l<u>a</u> yanfaAAu alla<u>th</u>eena <i><u>th</u></i>alamoo maAA<u>th</u>iratuhum wal<u>a</u> hum yustaAAtaboon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>57.  So on that Day no excuse<br> Of theirs will<br> Avail the Transgressors,<br> Nor will they be invited (then)<br> To seek grace (by repentance).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/030/030_058.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>58.  Walaqad <u>d</u>arabn<u>a</u> li<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si fee h<u>atha</u> alqur-<u>a</u>ni min kulli mathalin wala-in ji/tahum bi-<u>a</u>yatin layaqoolanna alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo in antum ill<u>a</u> mub<u>t</u>iloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>58.  Verily We have propounded<br> For men, in this Qur-ān.<br> Every kind of Parable:<br> But if thou bring to them<br> Any Sign, the Unbelievers<br> Are sure to say, "Ye<br> Do nothing but talk vanities."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/030/030_059.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>59.  Ka<u>tha</u>lika ya<u>t</u>baAAu All<u>a</u>hu AAal<u>a</u> quloobi alla<u>th</u>eena l<u>a</u> yaAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>59.  Thus does God seal up<br> The hearts of those<br> Who understand not.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/030/030_060.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>60.  Fa<b>i</b><u>s</u>bir inna waAAda All<u>a</u>hi <u>h</u>aqqun wal<u>a</u> yastakhiffannaka alla<u>th</u>eena l<u>a</u> yooqinoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>60.  So patiently persevere: for<br> Verily the promise of God<br> Is true: nor let those<br> Shake thy firmness, who have<br> (Themselves) no certainty of faith.</p>  <div class="filenav">
