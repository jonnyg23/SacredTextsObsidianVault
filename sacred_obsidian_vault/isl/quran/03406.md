 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXXIV.: Sabā, or the City of Sabā</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 6</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/034/034_046.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>46.  Qul innam<u>a</u> aAAi<i><u>th</u></i>ukum biw<u>ah</u>idatin an taqoomoo lill<u>a</u>hi mathn<u>a</u> wafur<u>a</u>d<u>a</u> thumma tatafakkaroo m<u>a</u> bi<u>sah</u>ibikum min jinnatin in huwa ill<u>a</u> na<u>th</u>eerun lakum bayna yaday AAa<u>tha</u>bin shadeed<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>46.  Say: "I do admonish you<br> On one point: that ye<br> Do stand up before God,—<br> (It may be) in pairs,<br> Or (it may be) singly,—<br> And reflect (within yourselves):<br> Your Companion is not<br> Possessed: he is no less<br> Than a Warner to you,<br> In face of a terrible<br> Penalty."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/034/034_047.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>47.  Qul m<u>a</u> saaltukum min ajrin fahuwa lakum in ajriya ill<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi wahuwa AAal<u>a</u> kulli shay-in shaheed<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>47.  Say: "No reward do I<br> Ask of you: it is (all)<br> In your interest: my reward<br> Is only due from God:<br> And He is Witness<br> To all things."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/034/034_048.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>48.  Qul inna rabbee yaq<u>th</u>ifu bi<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>aqqi AAall<u>a</u>mu alghuyoob<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>48.  Say: "Verily my Lord<br> Doth cast the (mantle<br>Of) Truth (over His servants),—<br> He that has full knowledge<br> Of (all) that is hidden."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/034/034_049.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>49.  Qul j<u>a</u>a al<u>h</u>aqqu wam<u>a</u> yubdi-o alb<u>at</u>ilu wam<u>a</u> yuAAeed<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>49.  Say: "The Truth has arrived,<br> And Falsehood neither creates<br> Anything new, nor restores<br> Anything."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/034/034_050.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>50.  Qul in <u>d</u>alaltu fa-innam<u>a</u> a<u>d</u>illu AAal<u>a</u> nafsee wa-ini ihtadaytu fabim<u>a</u> yoo<u>h</u>ee ilayya rabbee innahu sameeAAun qareeb<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>50.  Say: "If I am astray,<br> I only stray to the loss<br> Of my own soul: but if<br> I receive guidance, it is seen<br> Because of the inspiration<br> Of my Lord to me:<br> It is He Who hears<br> All things, and is (ever) near."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/034/034_051.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>51.  Walaw tar<u>a</u> i<u>th</u> faziAAoo fal<u>a</u> fawta waokhi<u>th</u>oo min mak<u>a</u>nin qareeb<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>51.  If thou couldst but see<br> When they will quake<br> With terror; but then<br> There will be no escape<br> (For them), and they will he<br> Seized from a position<br> (Quite) near.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/034/034_052.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>52.  Waq<u>a</u>loo <u>a</u>mann<u>a</u> bihi waann<u>a</u> lahumu a<b>l</b>ttan<u>a</u>wushu min mak<u>a</u>nin baAAeed<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>52.  And they will say,<br> "We do believe (now)<br> In the (Truth)"; but how<br> Could they receive (Faith)<br> From a position (so) far off,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/034/034_053.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>53.  Waqad kafaroo bihi min qablu wayaq<u>th</u>ifoona bi<b>a</b>lghaybi min mak<u>a</u>nin baAAeed<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>53.  Seeing that they did reject<br> Faith (entirely) before, and<br> That they (continually) cast<br> (Slanders) on the Unseen<br> From a position far off?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/034/034_054.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>54.  Wa<u>h</u>eela baynahum wabayna m<u>a</u> yashtahoona kam<u>a</u> fuAAila bi-ashy<u>a</u>AAihim min qablu innahum k<u>a</u>noo fee shakkin mureeb<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>54.  And between them<br> And their desires,<br> Is placed a barrier,<br> As was done in the past<br> With their partisans:<br> For they were indeed<br> In suspicious (disquieting) doubt.</p>  <div class="filenav">
