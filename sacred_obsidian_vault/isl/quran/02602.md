 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXVI.: Shu‘arāa, or The Poets.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 2</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_010.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>10.  Wa-i<u>th</u> n<u>a</u>d<u>a</u> rabbuka moos<u>a</u> ani i/ti alqawma a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>10.  Behold, thy Lord called<br> Moses: "Go to the people<br> Of iniquity,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_011.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>11.  Qawma firAAawna al<u>a</u> yattaqoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>11.  "The people of Pharaoh:<br> Will they not fear God?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_012.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>12.  Q<u>a</u>la rabbi innee akh<u>a</u>fu an yuka<u>thth</u>iboon<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>12.  He said: "O my Lord!<br> I do fear that they<br> Will charge me with falsehood:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_013.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>13.  Waya<u>d</u>eequ <u>s</u>adree wal<u>a</u> yan<u>t</u>aliqu lis<u>a</u>nee faarsil il<u>a</u> h<u>a</u>roon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>13.  "My breast will be straitened?'<br> And my speech may not go<br> (Smoothly): so send unto Aaron.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_014.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>14.  Walahum AAalayya <u>th</u>anbun faakh<u>a</u>fu an yaqtuloon<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>14.  "And (further), they have<br> A charge of crime against me;<br> And I fear they may<br> Slay me."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_015.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>15.  Q<u>a</u>la kall<u>a</u> fa<b>i</b><u>th</u>hab<u>a</u> bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin<u>a</u> inn<u>a</u> maAAakum mustamiAAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>15.  God said: "By no means!<br> Proceed then, both of you,<br> With Our Signs; We<br> Are with you, and will<br> Listen (to your call).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_016.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>16.  Fa/tiy<u>a</u> firAAawna faqool<u>a</u> inn<u>a</u> rasoolu rabbi alAA<u>a</u>lameen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>16.  "So go forth, both of you,<br> To Pharaoh, and say:<br> 'We have been sent<br> By the Lord and Cherisher<br> Of the Worlds;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_017.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>17.  An arsil maAAan<u>a</u> banee isr<u>a</u>-eel<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>17.  "'Send thou with us<br> The Children of Israel.'"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_018.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>18.  Q<u>a</u>la alam nurabbika feen<u>a</u> waleedan walabithta feen<u>a</u> min AAumurika sineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>18.  (Pharaoh) said: "Did we not<br> Cherish thee as a child<br> Among us, and didst thou not<br> Stay in our midst<br> Many years of thy life?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_019.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>19.  WafaAAalta faAAlataka allatee faAAalta waanta mina alk<u>a</u>fireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>19.  "And thou didst a deed<br> Of thine which (thou knowest)<br> Thou didst, and thou art<br> An ungrateful (wretch)!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_020.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>20.  Q<u>a</u>la faAAaltuh<u>a</u> i<u>th</u>an waan<u>a</u> mina a<b>l</b><u>dda</u>lleen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>20.  Moses said: "I did it<br> Then, when I was<br> In error.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_021.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>21.  Fafarartu minkum lamm<u>a</u> khiftukum fawahaba lee rabbee <u>h</u>ukman wajaAAalanee mina almursaleen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>21.  "So I fled from you (all)<br> When I feared you;<br> But my Lord has (since)<br> Invested me with judgment<br> (And wisdom) and appointed me<br> As one of the apostles.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_022.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>22.  Watilka niAAmatun tamunnuh<u>a</u> AAalayya an AAabbadta banee isr<u>a</u>-eel<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>22.  "And this is the favour<br> With which thou dost<br> Reproach me,—that thou<br> Hast enslaved the Children<br> Of Israel!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_023.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>23.  Q<u>a</u>la firAAawnu wam<u>a</u> rabbu alAA<u>a</u>lameen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>23.  Pharaoh said: "And what<br> Is the 'Lord and Cherisher<br> Of the Worlds'?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_024.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>24.  Q<u>a</u>la rabbu a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i wam<u>a</u> baynahum<u>a</u> in kuntum mooqineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>24.  (Moses) said: "The Lord<br> And Cherisher of the heavens<br> And the earth, and all between,<br> If ye want to be<br> Quite sure."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_025.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>25.  Q<u>a</u>la liman <u>h</u>awlahu al<u>a</u> tastamiAAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>25.  (Pharaoh) said to those<br> Around: "Do ye not listen<br> (To what he says)?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_026.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>26.  Q<u>a</u>la rabbukum warabbu <u>a</u>b<u>a</u>-ikumu al-awwaleen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>26.  (Moses) said: "Your Lord<br> And the Lord of your fathers<br> From the beginning!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_027.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>27.  Q<u>a</u>la inna rasoolakumu alla<u>th</u>ee orsila ilaykum lamajnoon<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>27.  (Pharaoh) said: "Truly<br> Your apostle who has been<br> Sent to you is<br> A veritable madman!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_028.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>28.  Q<u>a</u>la rabbu almashriqi wa<b>a</b>lmaghribi wam<u>a</u> baynahum<u>a</u> in kuntum taAAqiloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>28.  (Moses) said: "Lord of the East<br> And the West, and all between!<br> If ye only had sense!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_029.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>29.  Q<u>a</u>la la-ini ittakha<u>th</u>ta il<u>a</u>han ghayree laajAAalannaka mina almasjooneen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>29.  (Pharaoh) said: "If thou<br> Dost put forward any god<br> Other than me, I will<br> Certainly put thee in prison!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_030.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>30.  Q<u>a</u>la awa law ji/tuka bishay-in mubeen<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>30.  (Moses) said: "Even if I<br> Showed you something<br> Clear (and) convincing?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_031.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>31.  Q<u>a</u>la fa/ti bihi in kunta mina a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>diqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>31.  (Pharaoh) said: "Show it then,<br> If thou tellest the truth!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_032.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>32.  Faalq<u>a</u> AAa<u>sa</u>hu fa-i<u>tha</u> hiya thuAAb<u>a</u>nun mubeen<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>32.  So (Moses) threw his rod,<br> And behold, it was<br> A serpent, plain (for all to see)!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_033.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>33.  WanazaAAa yadahu fa-i<u>tha</u> hiya bay<u>da</u>o li<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u><i><u>th</u></i>ireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>33.  And he drew out his hand,<br> And behold, it was white<br> To all beholders!</p> <div class="filenav">
