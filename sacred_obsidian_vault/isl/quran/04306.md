 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XLIII.: Zu<u>kh</u>ruf, or Gold Adornments.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 6</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/043/043_057.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>57.  Walamm<u>a</u> <u>d</u>uriba ibnu maryama mathalan i<u>tha</u> qawmuka minhu ya<u>s</u>iddoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>57.  When (Jesus) the son<br> Of Mary is held up<br> As an example, behold,<br> Thy people raise a clamour<br> Thereat (in ridicule)!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/043/043_058.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>58.  Waq<u>a</u>loo a<u>a</u>lihatun<u>a</u> khayrun am huwa m<u>a</u> <u>d</u>araboohu laka ill<u>a</u> jadalan bal hum qawmun kha<u>s</u>imoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>58.  And they say, "Are<br> Our gods best, or he?"<br> This they set forth<br> To thee, only by way<br> Of disputation: yea, they<br> Are a contentious people.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/043/043_059.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>59.  In huwa ill<u>a</u> AAabdun anAAamn<u>a</u> AAalayhi wajaAAaln<u>a</u>hu mathalan libanee isr<u>a</u>-eel<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>59.  He was no more than<br> A servant: We granted<br> Our favour to him,<br> And We made him<br> An example to the Children<br> Of Israel.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/043/043_060.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>60.  Walaw nash<u>a</u>o lajaAAaln<u>a</u> minkum mal<u>a</u>-ikatan fee al-ar<u>d</u>i yakhlufoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>60.  And if it were Our Will,<br> We could make angels<br> From amongst you, succeeding<br> Each other on the earth.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/043/043_061.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>61.  Wa-innahu laAAilmun li<b>l</b>ss<u>a</u>AAati fal<u>a</u> tamtarunna bih<u>a</u> wa<b>i</b>ttabiAAooni h<u>atha</u> <u>s</u>ir<u>at</u>un mustaqeem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>61.  And (Jesus) shall be<br> A Sign (for the coming<br> Of) the Hour (of Judgment):<br> Therefore have no doubt<br> About the (Hour), but<br> Follow ye Me: this<br> Is a Straight Way.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/043/043_062.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>62.  Wal<u>a</u> ya<u>s</u>uddannakumu a<b>l</b>shshay<u>ta</u>nu innahu lakum AAaduwwun mubeen<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>62.  Let not the Evil One<br> Hinder you: for he is<br> To you an enemy avowed.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/043/043_063.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>63.  Walamm<u>a</u> j<u>a</u>a AAees<u>a</u> bi<b>a</b>lbayyin<u>a</u>ti q<u>a</u>la qad ji/tukum bi<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>ikmati wali-obayyina lakum baAA<u>d</u>a alla<u>th</u>ee takhtalifoona feehi fa<b>i</b>ttaqoo All<u>a</u>ha waa<u>t</u>eeAAoon<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>63.  When Jesus came<br> With Clear Signs, he said:<br> "Now have I come<br> To you with Wisdom,<br> And in order to make<br> Clear to you some<br> Of the (points) on which<br> Ye dispute: therefore fear God<br> And obey me.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/043/043_064.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>64.  Inna All<u>a</u>ha huwa rabbee warabbukum fa<b>o</b>AAbudoohu h<u>atha</u> <u>s</u>ir<u>at</u>un mustaqeem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>64.  "For God, He is my Lord<br> And your Lord: so worship<br> Ye Him: this is<br> A Straight Way."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/043/043_065.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>65.  Fa<b>i</b>khtalafa al-a<u>h</u>z<u>a</u>bu min baynihim fawaylun lilla<u>th</u>eena <i><u>th</u></i>alamoo min AAa<u>tha</u>bi yawmin aleem<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>65.  But sects from among<br> Themselves fell into disagreement:<br> Then woe to the wrong-doers,<br> From the Penalty<br> Of a Grievous Day!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/043/043_066.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>66.  Hal yan<i><u>th</u></i>uroona ill<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>ss<u>a</u>AAata an ta/tiyahum baghtatan wahum l<u>a</u> yashAAuroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>66.  Do they only wait<br> For the Hour—that it<br> Should come on them<br> All of a sudden,<br> While they perceive not?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/043/043_067.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>67.  Al-akhill<u>a</u>o yawma-i<u>th</u>in baAA<u>d</u>uhum libaAA<u>d</u>in AAaduwwun ill<u>a</u> almuttaqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>67.  Friends on that Day<br> Will be foes, one<br> To another,—except<br> The Righteous.</p> <div class="filenav">
