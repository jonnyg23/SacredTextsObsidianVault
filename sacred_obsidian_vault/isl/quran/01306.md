 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XIII.: Ra’d, or Thunder.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 6</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/013/013_038.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>38.  Walaqad arsaln<u>a</u> rusulan min qablika wajaAAaln<u>a</u> lahum azw<u>a</u>jan wa<u>th</u>urriyyatan wam<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>na lirasoolin an ya/tiya bi-<u>a</u>yatin ill<u>a</u> bi-i<u>th</u>ni All<u>a</u>hi likulli ajalin kit<u>a</u>b<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>38.  We did send apostles<br> Before thee, and appointed<br> For them wives and children:<br> And it was never the part<br> Of an apostle to bring a Sign<br> Except as God permitted<br> (Or commanded). For each period<br> Is a Book (revealed).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/013/013_039.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>39.  Yam<u>h</u>oo All<u>a</u>hu m<u>a</u> yash<u>a</u>o wayuthbitu waAAindahu ommu alkit<u>a</u>b<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>39.  God doth blot out<br> Or confirm what He pleaseth:<br> With Him is<br> The Mother of the Book.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/013/013_040.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>40.  Wa-in m<u>a</u> nuriyannaka baAA<u>d</u>a alla<u>th</u>ee naAAiduhum aw natawaffayannaka fa-innam<u>a</u> AAalayka albal<u>a</u>ghu waAAalayn<u>a</u> al<u>h</u>is<u>a</u>b<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>40.  Whether We shall show thee<br> (Within thy life-time)<br> Part of what We promised them<br> Or take to ourselves thy soul<br>(Before it is all accomplished),—<br> Thy duty is to make<br> (The Message) reach them:<br> It is Our part<br> To call them to account.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/013/013_041.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>41.  Awa lam yaraw ann<u>a</u> na/tee al-ar<u>d</u>a nanqu<u>s</u>uh<u>a</u> min a<u>t</u>r<u>a</u>fih<u>a</u> wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu ya<u>h</u>kumu l<u>a</u> muAAaqqiba li<u>h</u>ukmihi wahuwa sareeAAu al<u>h</u>is<u>a</u>b<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>41.  See they not that We<br> Gradually reduce the land<br> (In their control) from its<br> Outlying borders ? (Where) God<br> Commands, there is none<br> To put back His command:<br> And He is Swift<br> In calling to account.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/013/013_042.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>42.  Waqad makara alla<u>th</u>eena min qablihim falill<u>a</u>hi almakru jameeAAan yaAAlamu m<u>a</u> taksibu kullu nafsin wasayaAAlamu alkuff<u>a</u>ru liman AAuqb<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>dd<u>a</u>r<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>42.  Those before them did (also)<br> Devise plots; but in all things<br> The master-planning is God's.<br> He knoweth the doings<br> Of every soul: and soon<br> Will the Unbelievers know<br> Who gets home in the End.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/013/013_043.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>43.  Wayaqoolu alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo lasta mursalan qul kaf<u>a</u> bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi shaheedan baynee wabaynakum waman AAindahu AAilmu alkit<u>a</u>b<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>43.  The Unbelievers say: "No apostle<br> Art thou." Say: "Enough<br> For a witness between me<br> And you is God, and such<br> As have knowledge of the Book."</p>  <div class="filenav">
