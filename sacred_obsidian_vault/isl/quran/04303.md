 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XLIII.: Zu<u>kh</u>ruf, or Gold Adornments.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 3</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/043/043_026.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>26.  Wa-i<u>th</u> q<u>a</u>la ibr<u>a</u>heemu li-abeehi waqawmihi innanee bar<u>a</u>on mimm<u>a</u> taAAbudoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>26.  Behold! Abraham said<br> To his father and his people:<br> "I do indeed clear myself<br> Of what ye worship:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/043/043_027.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>27.  Ill<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>ee fa<u>t</u>aranee fa-innahu sayahdeen<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>27.  "(I worship) only Him<br> Who made me, and He<br> Will certainly guide me."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/043/043_028.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>28.  WajaAAalah<u>a</u> kalimatan b<u>a</u>qiyatan fee AAaqibihi laAAallahum yarjiAAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>28.  And he left it<br> As a Word<br> To endure among those<br> Who came after him,<br> That they may turn back<br> (To God).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/043/043_029.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>29.  Bal mattaAAtu h<u>a</u>ol<u>a</u>-i wa<u>a</u>b<u>a</u>ahum <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> j<u>a</u>ahumu al<u>h</u>aqqu warasoolun mubeen<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>29.  Yea, I have given<br> The good things of this life<br> To these (men) and<br> Their fathers, until the Truth<br> Has come to them,<br> And an Apostle<br> Making things clear.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/043/043_030.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>30.  Walamm<u>a</u> j<u>a</u>ahumu al<u>h</u>aqqu q<u>a</u>loo h<u>atha</u> si<u>h</u>run wa-inn<u>a</u> bihi k<u>a</u>firoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>30.  But when the Truth came<br> To them, they said:<br> "This is sorcery, and we<br> Do reject it."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/043/043_031.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>31.  Waq<u>a</u>loo lawl<u>a</u> nuzzila h<u>atha</u> alqur-<u>a</u>nu AAal<u>a</u> rajulin mina alqaryatayni AAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>31.  Also, they say: "Why<br> Is not this Qur-ān sent<br> Down to some leading man<br> In either of the two<br> (Chief) cities?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/043/043_032.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>32.  Ahum yaqsimoona ra<u>h</u>mata rabbika na<u>h</u>nu qasamn<u>a</u> baynahum maAAeeshatahum fee al<u>h</u>ay<u>a</u>ti a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> warafaAAn<u>a</u> baAA<u>d</u>ahum fawqa baAA<u>d</u>in daraj<u>a</u>tin liyattakhi<u>th</u>a baAA<u>d</u>uhum baAA<u>d</u>an sukhriyyan wara<u>h</u>matu rabbika khayrun mimm<u>a</u> yajmaAAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>32.  Is it they who would portion out<br> The Mercy of thy Lord?<br> It is We Who portion out<br> Between them their livelihood<br>In the life of this world:<br> And We raise some of them<br> Above others in ranks,<br> So that some may command<br> Work from others.<br> But the Mercy of thy Lord<br> Is better than the (wealth)<br> Which they amass.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/043/043_033.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>33.  Walawl<u>a</u> an yakoona a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>su ommatan w<u>ah</u>idatan lajaAAaln<u>a</u> liman yakfuru bi<b>al</b>rra<u>h</u>m<u>a</u>ni libuyootihim suqufan min fi<u>dd</u>atin wamaAA<u>a</u>rija AAalayh<u>a</u> ya<i><u>th</u></i>haroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>33.  And were it not that<br> (All) men might become<br> Of one (evil) way of life,<br> We would provide,<br> For everyone that blasphemes<br> Against (God) Most Gracious,<br> Silver roofs for their houses,<br> And (silver) stair-ways<br> On which to go up,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/043/043_034.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>34.  Walibuyootihim abw<u>a</u>ban wasururan AAalayh<u>a</u> yattaki-oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>34.  And (silver) doors<br> To their houses, and thrones<br> (Of silver) on which<br> They could recline,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/043/043_035.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>35.  Wazukhrufan wa-in kullu <u>tha</u>lika lamm<u>a</u> mat<u>a</u>AAu al<u>h</u>ay<u>a</u>ti a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a </u>wa<b>a</b>l-<u>a</u>khiratu AAinda rabbika lilmuttaqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>35.  And also adornments<br> Of gold. But all this<br> Were nothing but conveniences<br> Of the present life:<br> The Hereafter, in the sight<br> Of thy Lord, is<br> For the Righteous.</p> <div class="filenav">
