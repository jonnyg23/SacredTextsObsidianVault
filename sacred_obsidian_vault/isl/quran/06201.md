 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra LXII.: Jumu‘a, or the Assembly (Friday) Prayer.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/062/062_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  Yusabbi<u>h</u>u lill<u>a</u>hi m<u>a</u> fee a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wam<u>a</u> fee al-ar<u>d</u>i almaliki alquddoosi alAAazeezi al<u>h</u>akeem<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>1.  Whatever is<br> In the heavens and<br> On earth, doth declare<br> The Praises and Glory<br> Of God,—the Sovereign,<br> The Holy One, the Exalted<br> In Might, the Wise.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/062/062_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  Huwa alla<u>th</u>ee baAAatha fee al-ommiyyeena rasoolan minhum yatloo AAalayhim <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tihi wayuzakkeehim wayuAAallimuhumu alkit<u>a</u>ba wa<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>ikmata wa-in k<u>a</u>noo min qablu lafee <u>d</u>al<u>a</u>lin mubeen<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>2.  It is He Who has sent<br> Amongst the Unlettered<br> An apostle from among<br> Themselves, to rehearse<br> To them His Signs,<br> To sanctify them, and<br> To instruct them in Scripture<br> And Wisdom,—although<br>They had been, before,<br> In manifest error;—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/062/062_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  Wa<u>a</u>khareena minhum lamm<u>a</u> yal<u>h</u>aqoo bihim wahuwa alAAazeezu al<u>h</u>akeem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>3.  As well as (to confer<br> All these benefits upon)<br> Others of them, who<br> Have not already joined them:<br> And He is Exalted<br> In Might, Wise.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/062/062_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  <u>Tha</u>lika fa<u>d</u>lu All<u>a</u>hi yu/teehi man yash<u>a</u>o wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu <u>th</u>oo alfa<u>d</u>li alAAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>4.  Such is the Bounty of God,<br> Which He bestows<br> On whom He will:<br> And God is the Lord<br> Of the highest bounty.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/062/062_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  Mathalu alla<u>th</u>eena <u>h</u>ummiloo a<b>l</b>ttawr<u>a</u>ta thumma lam ya<u>h</u>milooh<u>a</u> kamathali al<u>h</u>im<u>a</u>ri ya<u>h</u>milu asf<u>a</u>ran bi/sa mathalu alqawmi alla<u>th</u>eena ka<u>thth</u>aboo bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>ti All<u>a</u>hi wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu l<u>a</u> yahdee alqawma a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>5.  The similitude of those<br> Who were charged<br> With the (obligations<br> Of the) Mosaic Law,<br> But who subsequently failed<br> In those (obligations), is<br> That of a donkey<br> Which carries huge tomes<br> (But understands them not).<br> Evil is the similitude<br> Of people who falsify<br> The Signs of God:<br> And God guides not<br> People who do wrong.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/062/062_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  Qul y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena h<u>a</u>doo in zaAAamtum annakum awliy<u>a</u>o lill<u>a</u>hi min dooni a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si fatamannawoo almawta in kuntum <u>sa</u>diqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>6.  Say: "O ye that<br> Stand on Judaism!<br>If ye think that ye<br> Are friends to God,<br> To the exclusion of<br> (Other) men, then express<br> Your desire for Death,<br> If ye are truthful!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/062/062_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  Wal<u>a</u> yatamannawnahu abadan bim<u>a</u> qaddamat aydeehim wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu AAaleemun bi<b>al</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>7.  But never will they<br> Express their desire<br> (For Death), because of<br> The (deeds) their hands<br> Have sent on before them!<br> And God knows well<br> Those that do wrong!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/062/062_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  Qul inna almawta alla<u>th</u>ee tafirroona minhu fa-innahu mul<u>a</u>qeekum thumma turaddoona il<u>a</u> AA<u>a</u>limi alghaybi wa<b>al</b>shshah<u>a</u>dati fayunabbi-okum bim<u>a</u> kuntum taAAmaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>8.  Say: "The Death from which<br> Ye flee will truly<br> Overtake you: then will<br> Ye be sent back<br> To the Knower of things<br> Secret and open: and He<br> Will tell you (the truth<br> Of) the things that ye did!"</p> <div class="filenav">
