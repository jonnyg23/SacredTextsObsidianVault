 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra VII.: A’rāf, or the Heights</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 14</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_109.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>109.  Q<u>a</u>la almalao min qawmi firAAawna inna h<u>atha</u> las<u>ah</u>irun AAaleem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>109.  Said the Chiefs of the people<br> Of Pharaoh: "This is indeed<br> A sorcerer well-versed.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_110.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>110.  Yureedu an yukhrijakum min ar<u>d</u>ikum fam<u>atha</u> ta/muroon<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>110.  "His plan is to get you out<br> Of your land: then<br> What is it ye counsel?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_111.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>111.  Q<u>a</u>loo arjih waakh<u>a</u>hu waarsil fee almad<u>a</u>-ini <u>ha</u>shireen<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>111.  They said: "Keep him<br> And his brother in suspense<br> (For a while); and send<br> To the cities men to collect—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_112.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>112.  Ya/tooka bikulli s<u>ah</u>irin AAaleem<b>in</b>    </p>
 <p>112.  And bring up to thee<br> All (our) sorcerers well-versed.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_113.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>113.  Waj<u>a</u>a a<b>l</b>ssa<u>h</u>aratu firAAawna q<u>a</u>loo inna lan<u>a</u> laajran in kunn<u>a</u> na<u>h</u>nu algh<u>a</u>libeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>113.  So there came<br> The sorcerers to Pharaoh:<br> They said, "Of course<br> We shall have a (suitable)<br> Reward if we win!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_114.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>114.  Q<u>a</u>la naAAam wa-innakum lamina almuqarrabeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>114.  He said: "Yea, (and more),—<br> For ye shall in that case<br> Be (raised to posts)<br> Nearest (to my person)."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_115.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>115.  Q<u>a</u>loo y<u>a</u> moos<u>a</u> imm<u>a</u> an tulqiya wa-imm<u>a</u> an nakoona na<u>h</u>nu almulqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>115.  They said: "O Moses!<br> Wilt thou throw (first),<br> Or shall we have<br> The (first) throw?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_116.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>116.  Q<u>a</u>la alqoo falamm<u>a</u> alqaw sa<u>h</u>aroo aAAyuna a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si wa<b>i</b>starhaboohum waj<u>a</u>oo bisi<u>h</u>rin AAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>116.  Said Moses: "Throw ye (first)."<br> So when they threw,<br> They bewitched the eyes<br> Of the people, and struck<br> Terror into them: for they<br> Showed a great (feat of) magic.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_117.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>117.  Waaw<u>h</u>ayn<u>a</u> il<u>a</u> moos<u>a</u> an alqi AAa<u>sa</u>ka fa-i<u>tha</u> hiya talqafu m<u>a</u> ya/fikoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>117.  We put it into Moses's mind<br> By inspiration: "Throw (now)<br> Thy rod": and behold!<br> It swallows up straightway<br> All the falsehoods<br> Which they fake!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_118.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>118.  FawaqaAAa al<u>h</u>aqqu waba<u>t</u>ala m<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo yaAAmaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>118.  Thus truth was confirmed.<br> And all that they did<br> Was made of no effect.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_119.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>119.  Faghuliboo hun<u>a</u>lika wa<b>i</b>nqalaboo <u>sa</u>ghireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>119.  So the (great ones) were vanquished<br> There and then, and were<br> Made to look small.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_120.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>120.  Waolqiya a<b>l</b>ssa<u>h</u>aratu s<u>a</u>jideen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>120.  But the sorcerers fell down<br> Prostrate in adoration,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_121.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>121.  Q<u>a</u>loo <u>a</u>mann<u>a</u> birabbi alAA<u>a</u>lameen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>121.  Saying: "We believe<br> In the Lord of the Worlds,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_122.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>122.  Rabbi moos<u>a</u> wah<u>a</u>roon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>122.  "The Lord of Moses and Aaron.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_123.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>123.  Q<u>a</u>la firAAawnu <u>a</u>mantum bihi qabla an <u>ath</u>ana lakum inna h<u>atha</u> lamakrun makartumoohu fee almadeenati litukhrijoo minh<u>a</u> ahlah<u>a</u> fasawfa taAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>123.  Said Pharaoh: "Believe ye<br> In Him before I give<br> You permission? Surely<br> This is a trick which ye<br> Have planned in the City<br> To drive out its people:<br> But soon shall ye know<br> (The consequences).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_124.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>124.  Laoqa<u>t</u>iAAanna aydiyakum waarjulakum min khil<u>a</u>fin thumma lao<u>s</u>allibannakum ajmaAAeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>124.  "Be sure I will cut off<br> Your hands and your feet<br> On opposite sides, and I<br> Will cause you all<br> To die on the cross."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_125.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>125.  Q<u>a</u>loo inn<u>a</u> il<u>a</u> rabbin<u>a</u> munqaliboon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>125.  They said: "For us,<br> We are but sent back<br> Unto our Lord:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_126.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>126.  Wam<u>a</u> tanqimu minn<u>a</u> ill<u>a</u> an <u>a</u>mann<u>a</u> bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>ti rabbin<u>a</u> lamm<u>a</u> j<u>a</u>atn<u>a</u> rabban<u>a</u> afrigh AAalayn<u>a</u> <u>s</u>abran watawaffan<u>a</u> muslimeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>126.  "But thou dost wreak<br> Thy vengeance on us<br> Simply because we believed<br> In the Signs of our Lord<br> When they reached us!<br> Our Lord! pour out on us<br> Patience and constancy, and take<br> Our souls unto Thee<br> As Muslims (who bow<br> To Thy Will)!</p> <div class="filenav">
