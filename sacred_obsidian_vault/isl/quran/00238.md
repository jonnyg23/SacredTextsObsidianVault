 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra II.: Baqara, or the Heifer.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 38</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_274.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>274.  Alla<u>th</u>eena yunfiqoona amw<u>a</u>lahum bi<b>a</b>llayli wa<b>al</b>nnah<u>a</u>ri sirran waAAal<u>a</u>niyatan falahum ajruhum AAinda rabbihim wal<u>a</u> khawfun AAalayhim wal<u>a</u> hum ya<u>h</u>zanoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>274.  Whose who (in charity)<br> Spend of their goods<br> By night and by day,<br> In secret and in public,<br> Have their reward<br> With their Lord:<br> On them shall be no fear,<br> Nor shall they grieve.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_275.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>275.  Alla<u>th</u>eena ya/kuloona a<b>l</b>rrib<u>a</u> l<u>a</u> yaqoomoona ill<u>a</u> kam<u>a</u> yaqoomu alla<u>th</u>ee yatakhabba<u>t</u>uhu a<b>l</b>shshay<u>ta</u>nu mina almassi <u>tha</u>lika bi-annahum q<u>a</u>loo innam<u>a</u> albayAAu mithlu a<b>l</b>rrib<u>a</u> waa<u>h</u>alla All<u>a</u>hu albayAAa wa<u>h</u>arrama a<b>l</b>rrib<u>a</u> faman j<u>a</u>ahu mawAAi<i><u>th</u></i>atun min rabbihi fa<b>i</b>ntah<u>a</u> falahu m<u>a</u> salafa waamruhu il<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi waman AA<u>a</u>da faol<u>a</u>-ika a<u>s</u>-<u>ha</u>bu a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>ri hum feeh<u>a</u> kh<u>a</u>lidoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>275.  Those who devour usury<br> Will not stand except<br> As stands one whom<br> The Evil One by his touch<br> Hath driven to madness.<br> That is because they say:<br> "Trade is like usury,"<br> But God hath permitted trade<br>And forbidden usury.<br> Those who after receiving<br> Direction from their Lord,<br> Desist, shall be pardoned<br> For the past; their case<br> Is for God (to judge);<br> But those who repeat<br> (The offence) are Companions<br> Of the Fire: they will<br> Abide therein (for ever).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_276.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>276.  Yam<u>h</u>aqu All<u>a</u>hu a<b>l</b>rrib<u>a</u> wayurbee a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>adaq<u>a</u>ti wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu l<u>a</u> yu<u>h</u>ibbu kulla kaff<u>a</u>rin atheem<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>276.  God will deprive<br> Usury of all blessing,<br> But will give increase<br> For deeds of charity:<br> For He loveth not<br> Creatures ungrateful<br> And wicked.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_277.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>277.  Inna alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo waAAamiloo a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>li<u>ha</u>ti waaq<u>a</u>moo a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>al<u>a</u>ta wa<u>a</u>tawoo a<b>l</b>zzak<u>a</u>ta lahum ajruhum AAinda rabbihim wal<u>a</u> khawfun AAalayhim wal<u>a</u> hum ya<u>h</u>zanoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>277.  Those who believe,<br> And do deeds of righteousness,<br> And establish regular prayers<br> And regular charity,<br> Will have their reward<br> With their Lord:<br> On them shall be no fear,<br> Nor shall they grieve.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_278.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>278.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo ittaqoo All<u>a</u>ha wa<u>th</u>aroo m<u>a</u> baqiya mina a<b>l</b>rrib<u>a</u> in kuntum mu/mineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>278.  O ye who believe!<br> Fear God, and give up<br> What remains of your demand<br> For usury, if ye are<br> Indeed believers.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_279.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>279.  Fa-in lam tafAAaloo fa/<u>th</u>anoo bi<u>h</u>arbin mina All<u>a</u>hi warasoolihi wa-in tubtum falakum ruoosu amw<u>a</u>likum l<u>a</u> ta<i><u>th</u></i>limoona wal<u>a</u> tu<i><u>th</u></i>lamoon<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>279.  If ye do it not,<br> Take notice of war<br> From God and His Apostle:<br> But if ye turn back,<br> Ye shall have<br> Your capital sums:<br> Deal not unjustly,<br> And ye shall not<br> Be dealt with unjustly.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_280.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>280.  Wa-in k<u>a</u>na <u>th</u>oo AAusratin fana<i><u>th</u></i>iratun il<u>a</u> maysaratin waan ta<u>s</u>addaqoo khayrun lakum in kuntum taAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>280.  If the debtor is<br> In a difficulty,<br> Grant him time<br> Till it is easy<br> For him to repay.<br> But if ye remit it<br> By way of charity,<br> That is best for you<br> If ye only knew.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_281.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>281.  Wa<b>i</b>ttaqoo yawman turjaAAoona feehi il<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi thumma tuwaff<u>a</u> kullu nafsin m<u>a</u> kasabat wahum l<u>a</u> yu<i><u>th</u></i>lamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>281.  And fear the Day<br> When ye shall be<br> Brought back to God.<br> Then shall every soul<br> Be paid what it earned,<br> And none shall be<br> Dealt with unjustly.</p> <div class="filenav">
