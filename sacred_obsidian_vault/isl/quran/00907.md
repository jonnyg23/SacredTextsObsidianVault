 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra IX.: Tauba (Repentance) or Barāat (Immunity).</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 7</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_043.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>43.  AAaf<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hu AAanka lima a<u>th</u>inta lahum <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> yatabayyana laka alla<u>th</u>eena <u>s</u>adaqoo wataAAlama alk<u>ath</u>ibeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>43.  God give thee grace! Why<br> Didst thou grant them exemption<br> Until those who told the truth<br> Were seen by thee in a clear light,<br> And thou hadst proved the liars?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_044.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>44.  L<u>a</u> yasta/<u>th</u>inuka alla<u>th</u>eena yu/minoona bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi wa<b>a</b>lyawmi al-<u>a</u>khiri an yuj<u>a</u>hidoo bi-amw<u>a</u>lihim waanfusihim wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu AAaleemun bi<b>a</b>lmuttaqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>44.  Those who believe in God<br> And the Last Day ask thee<br> For no exemption from fighting<br> With their goods and persons.<br> And God knoweth well<br> Those who do their duty.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_045.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>45.  Innam<u>a</u> yasta/<u>th</u>inuka alla<u>th</u>eena l<u>a</u> yu/minoona bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi wa<b>a</b>lyawmi al-<u>a</u>khiri wa<b>i</b>rt<u>a</u>bat quloobuhum fahum fee raybihim yataraddadoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>45.  Only those ask thee for exemption<br> Who believe not in God<br> And the Last Day, and<br> Whose hearts are in doubt,<br> So that they are tossed<br> In their doubts to and fro.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_046.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>46.  Walaw ar<u>a</u>doo alkhurooja laaAAaddoo lahu AAuddatan wal<u>a</u>kin kariha All<u>a</u>hu inbiAA<u>a</u>thahum fathabba<u>t</u>ahum waqeela oqAAudoo maAAa alq<u>a</u>AAideen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>46.  If they had intended<br> To come out, they would<br> Certainly have made<br> Some preparation therefor;<br> But God was averse<br> To their being sent forth;<br> So He made them lag behind,<br> And they were told,<br> "Sit ye among those<br> Who sit (inactive)."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_047.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>47.  Law kharajoo feekum m<u>a</u> z<u>a</u>dookum ill<u>a</u> khab<u>a</u>lan walaaw<u>d</u>aAAoo khil<u>a</u>lakum yabghoonakumu alfitnata wafeekum samm<u>a</u>AAoona lahum wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu AAaleemun bi<b>al</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>47.  If they had come out<br> With you, they would not<br>Have added to your (strength)<br> But only (made for) disorder,<br> Hurrying to and fro in your midst<br> And sowing sedition among you,<br> And there would have been<br> Some among you<br> Who would have listened to them.<br> But God knoweth well<br> Those who do wrong.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_048.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>48.  Laqadi ibtaghawoo alfitnata min qablu waqallaboo laka al-omoora <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> j<u>a</u>a al<u>h</u>aqqu wa<i><u>th</u></i>ahara amru All<u>a</u>hi wahum k<u>a</u>rihoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>48.  Indeed they had plotted<br> Sedition before, and upset<br> Matters for thee,—until<br> The Truth arrived, and the Decree<br> Of God became manifest,<br> Much to their disgust.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_049.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>49.  Waminhum man yaqoolu i/<u>th</u>an lee wal<u>a</u> taftinnee al<u>a</u> fee alfitnati saqa<u>t</u>oo wa-inna jahannama lamu<u>h</u>ee<u>t</u>atun bi<b>a</b>lk<u>a</u>fireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>49.  Among them is (many) a man<br> Who says: "Grant me exemption<br> And draw me not.<br> Into trial." Have they not<br> Fallen into trial already?<br> And indeed Hell surrounds<br> The Unbelievers (on all sides).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_050.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>50.  In tu<u>s</u>ibka <u>h</u>asanatun tasu/hum wa-in tu<u>s</u>ibka mu<u>s</u>eebatun yaqooloo qad akha<u>th</u>n<u>a</u> amran<u>a</u> min qablu wayatawallaw wahum fari<u>h</u>oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>50.  If good befalls thee,<br> It grieves them; but if<br> A misfortune befalls thee,<br> They say, "We took indeed<br> Our precautions beforehand,"<br> And they turn away rejoicing.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_051.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>51.  Qul lan yu<u>s</u>eeban<u>a</u> ill<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> kataba All<u>a</u>hu lan<u>a</u> huwa mawl<u>a</u>n<u>a</u> waAAal<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi falyatawakkali almu/minoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>51.  Say: "Nothing will happen to us<br> Except what God has decreed<br> For us: He is our Protector":<br> And on God let the Believers<br> Put their trust.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_052.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>52.  Qul hal tarabba<u>s</u>oona bin<u>a</u> ill<u>a</u> i<u>h</u>d<u>a</u> al<u>h</u>usnayayni wana<u>h</u>nu natarabba<u>s</u>u bikum an yu<u>s</u>eebakumu All<u>a</u>hu biAAa<u>tha</u>bin min AAindihi aw bi-aydeen<u>a</u> fatarabba<u>s</u>oo inn<u>a</u> maAAakum mutarabbi<u>s</u>oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>52.  Say: "Can you expect for us<br> (Any fate) other than one<br> Of two glorious things—<br> (Martyrdom or victory)?<br> But we can expect for you<br> Either that God will send<br> His punishment from Himself,<br> Or by our hands. So wait<br> (Expectant); we too<br> Will wait with you."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_053.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>53.  Qul anfiqoo <u>t</u>awAAan aw karhan lan yutaqabbala minkum innakum kuntum qawman f<u>a</u>siqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>53.  Say: "Spend (for the Cause)<br> Willingly or unwillingly:<br> Not from you will it be<br> Accepted: for ye are indeed<br> A people rebellious and wicked."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_054.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>54.  Wam<u>a</u> manaAAahum an tuqbala minhum nafaq<u>a</u>tuhum ill<u>a</u> annahum kafaroo bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi wabirasoolihi wal<u>a</u> ya/toona a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>al<u>a</u>ta ill<u>a</u> wahum kus<u>a</u>l<u>a</u> wal<u>a</u> yunfiqoona ill<u>a</u> wahum k<u>a</u>rihoon<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>54.  The only reasons why<br> Their contributions are not<br> Accepted are: that they reject<br> God and His Apostle;<br> That they come to prayer<br> Without earnestness; and that<br> They offer contributions unwillingly.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_055.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>55.  Fal<u>a</u> tuAAjibka amw<u>a</u>luhum wal<u>a</u> awl<u>a</u>duhum innam<u>a</u> yureedu All<u>a</u>hu liyuAAa<u>thth</u>ibahum bih<u>a</u> fee al<u>h</u>ay<u>a</u>ti a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> watazhaqa anfusuhum wahum k<u>a</u>firoon<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>55.  Let not their wealth rats<br> Nor their (following in) sons<br>Dazzle thee: in reality<br> God's Plan is to punish them<br> With these things in this life,<br> And that their souls may perish<br> In their (very) denial of God.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_056.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>56.  Waya<u>h</u>lifoona bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi innahum laminkum wam<u>a</u> hum minkum wal<u>a</u>kinnahum qawmun yafraqoon<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>56.  They swear by God<br> That they are indeed<br> Of you; but they are not<br> Of you: yet they are afraid<br> (To appear in their true colours).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_057.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>57.  Law yajidoona maljaan aw magh<u>a</u>r<u>a</u>tin aw muddakhalan lawallaw ilayhi wahum yajma<u>h</u>oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>57.  If they could find<br> A place to flee to,<br> Or caves, or a place<br> Of concealment, they would<br> Turn straightway thereto,<br> With an obstinate rush.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_058.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>58.  Waminhum man yalmizuka fee a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>adaq<u>a</u>ti fa-in oAA<u>t</u>oo minh<u>a</u> ra<u>d</u>oo wa-in lam yuAA<u>t</u>aw minh<u>a</u> i<u>tha</u> hum yaskha<u>t</u>oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>58.  And among them are men<br> Who slander thee in the matter<br> Of (the distribution of) the alms:<br> If they are given part thereof,<br> They are pleased, but if not,<br> Behold! they are indignant!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_059.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>59.  Walaw annahum ra<u>d</u>oo m<u>a</u> <u>a</u>t<u>a</u>humu All<u>a</u>hu warasooluhu waq<u>a</u>loo <u>h</u>asbun<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hu sayu/teen<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hu min fa<u>d</u>lihi warasooluhu inn<u>a</u> il<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi r<u>a</u>ghiboon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>59.  If only they had been content<br> With what God and His Apostle<br> Gave them, and had said,<br> "Sufficient unto us is God!<br> God and His Apostle will soon<br> Give us of His bounty:<br> To God do we turn our hopes!"<br> (That would have been the right course).</p> <div class="filenav">
