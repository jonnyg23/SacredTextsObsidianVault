 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXVI.: Shu‘arāa, or The Poets.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  <u>Ta</u>-seen-meem   </p>
 <p>1.  Tā. Sīn. Mīm.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  Tilka <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tu alkit<u>a</u>bi almubeen<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>2.  These are Verses of the Book<br> That makes (things) clear.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  LaAAallaka b<u>a</u>khiAAun nafsaka all<u>a</u> yakoonoo mu/mineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>3.  It may be thou frettest<br> Thy soul with grief, that they<br> Do not become Believers.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  In nasha/ nunazzil AAalayhim mina a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>-i <u>a</u>yatan fa<i><u>th</u></i>allat aAAn<u>a</u>quhum lah<u>a</u> kh<u>ad</u>iAAeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>4.  If (such) were Our Will,<br> We could send down to them<br> From the sky a Sign,<br> To which they would bend<br> Their necks in humility.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  Wam<u>a</u> ya/teehim min <u>th</u>ikrin mina a<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>m<u>a</u>ni mu<u>h</u>dathin ill<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo AAanhu muAAri<u>d</u>een<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>5.  But there comes not<br> To them a newly-revealed<br> Message from (God) Most Gracious,<br> But they turn away therefrom.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  Faqad ka<u>thth</u>aboo fasaya/teehim anb<u>a</u>o m<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo bihi yastahzi-oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>6.  They have indeed rejected<br> (The Message): so they will<br> Know soon (enough) the truth<br> Of what they mocked at!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  Awa lam yaraw il<u>a</u> al-ar<u>d</u>i kam anbatn<u>a</u> feeh<u>a</u> min kulli zawjin kareem<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>7.  Do they not look<br> At the earth,—how many<br> Noble things of all kinds<br> We have produced therein?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  Inna fee <u>tha</u>lika la<u>a</u>yatan wam<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>na aktharuhum mu/mineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>8.  Verily, in this is a Sign:<br> But most of them<br> Do not believe.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_009.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>9.  Wa-inna rabbaka lahuwa alAAazeezu a<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>eem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>9.  And verily, thy Lord<br> Is He, the Exalted in Might,<br> Most Merciful.</p> <div class="filenav">
