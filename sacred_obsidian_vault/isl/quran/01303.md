 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XIII.: Ra’d, or Thunder.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 3</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/013/013_019.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>19.  Afaman yaAAlamu annam<u>a</u> onzila ilayka min rabbika al<u>h</u>aqqu kaman huwa aAAm<u>a</u> innam<u>a</u> yata<u>th</u>akkaru oloo al-alb<u>a</u>b<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>19.  Is then one who doth know<br> That that which hath been<br>Revealed unto thee<br> From thy Lord is the Truth,<br> Like one who is blind?<br> It is those who are<br> Endued with understanding<br> That receive admonition;—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/013/013_020.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>20.  Alla<u>th</u>eena yoofoona biAAahdi All<u>a</u>hi wal<u>a</u> yanqu<u>d</u>oona almeeth<u>a</u>q<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>20.  Those who fulfil the Covenant<br> Of God and fail not<br> In their plighted word;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/013/013_021.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>21.  Wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena ya<u>s</u>iloona m<u>a</u> amara All<u>a</u>hu bihi an yoo<u>s</u>ala wayakhshawna rabbahum wayakh<u>a</u>foona soo-a al<u>h</u>is<u>a</u>b<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>21.  Those who join together<br> Those things which God<br> Hath commanded to be joined,<br> Hold their Lord in awe,<br> And fear the terrible reckoning;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/013/013_022.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>22.  Wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena <u>s</u>abaroo ibtigh<u>a</u>a wajhi rabbihim waaq<u>a</u>moo a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>al<u>a</u>ta waanfaqoo mimm<u>a</u> razaqn<u>a</u>hum sirran waAAal<u>a</u>niyatan wayadraoona bi<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>asanati a<b>l</b>ssayyi-ata ol<u>a</u>-ika lahum AAuqb<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>dd<u>a</u>r<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>22.  Those who patiently persevere,<br> Seeking the countenance of their Lord;<br> Establish regular prayers; spend,<br> Out of (the gifts) We have bestowed<br> For their sustenance, secretly<br> And openly; and turn off Evil<br> With good: for such there is<br> The final attainment<br> Of the (Eternal) Home,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/013/013_023.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>23.  Jann<u>a</u>tu AAadnin yadkhuloonah<u>a</u> waman <u>s</u>ala<u>h</u>a min <u>a</u>b<u>a</u>-ihim waazw<u>a</u>jihim wa<u>th</u>urriyy<u>a</u>tihim wa<b>a</b>lmal<u>a</u>-ikatu yadkhuloona AAalayhim min kulli b<u>a</u>b<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>23.  Gardens of perpetual bliss:<br> They shall enter there,<br> As well as the righteous<br> Among their fathers, their spouses,<br>And their offspring:<br> And angels shall enter unto them<br> From every gate (with the salutation):</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/013/013_024.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>24.  Sal<u>a</u>mun AAalaykum bim<u>a</u> <u>s</u>abartum faniAAma AAuqb<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>dd<u>a</u>r<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>24.  "Peace unto you for that ye<br> Persevered in patience! Now<br> How excellent is the final Home!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/013/013_025.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>25.  Wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena yanqu<u>d</u>oona AAahda All<u>a</u>hi min baAAdi meeth<u>a</u>qihi wayaq<u>t</u>aAAoona m<u>a</u> amara All<u>a</u>hu bihi an yoo<u>s</u>ala wayufsidoona fee al-ar<u>d</u>i ol<u>a</u>-ika lahumu allaAAnatu walahum soo-o a<b>l</b>dd<u>a</u>r<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>25.  But those who break<br> The Covenant of God, after<br> Having plighted their word thereto,<br> And cut asunder those things<br> Which God has commanded<br> To be joined, and work mischief<br> In the land;—on them<br> Is the Curse; for them<br> Is the terrible Home!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/013/013_026.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>26.  All<u>a</u>hu yabsu<u>t</u>u a<b>l</b>rrizqa liman yash<u>a</u>o wayaqdiru wafari<u>h</u>oo bi<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>ay<u>a</u>ti a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> wam<u>a</u> al<u>h</u>ay<u>a</u>tu a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> fee al-<u>a</u>khirati ill<u>a</u> mat<u>a</u>AA<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>26.  God doth enlarge, or grant<br> By (strict) measure, the Sustenance<br> (Which He giveth) to whomso<br> He pleaseth. (The worldly) rejoice<br> In the life of this world:<br> But the life of this world<br> Is but little comfort<br> In the Hereafter.</p> <div class="filenav">
