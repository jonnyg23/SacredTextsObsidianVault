 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_284.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>284.  Lill<u>a</u>hi m<u>a</u> fee a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wam<u>a</u> fee al-ar<u>d</u>i wa-in tubdoo m<u>a</u> fee anfusikum aw tukhfoohu yu<u>ha</u>sibkum bihi All<u>a</u>hu fayaghfiru liman yash<u>a</u>o wayuAAa<u>thth</u>ibu man yash<u>a</u>o wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu AAal<u>a</u> kulli shay-in qadeer<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>284.  To God belongeth all<br> That is in the heavens<br> And on earth. Whether<br> Ye show what is in your minds<br> Or conceal it, God<br> Calleth you to account for it.<br>He forgiveth whom He pleaseth,<br> And punishefh whom He pleaseth.<br> For God hath power<br> Over all things.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_285.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>285.  <u>A</u>mana a<b>l</b>rrasoolu bim<u>a</u> onzila ilayhi min rabbihi wa<b>a</b>lmu/minoona kullun <u>a</u>mana bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi wamal<u>a</u>-ikatihi wakutubihi warusulihi l<u>a</u> nufarriqu bayna a<u>h</u>adin min rusulihi waq<u>a</u>loo samiAAn<u>a</u> waa<u>t</u>aAAn<u>a</u> ghufr<u>a</u>naka rabban<u>a</u> wa-ilayka alma<u>s</u>eer<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>285.  The Apostle believeth<br> In what hath been revealed<br> To him from his Lord,<br> As do the men of faith.<br> Each one (of them) believeth<br> In God, His angels,<br> His books, and His apostles.<br> "We make no distinction (they say)<br> Between one and another<br> Of His apostles." And they say:<br> "We hear, and we obey:<br> (We seek) Thy forgiveness,<br> Our Lord, and to Thee<br> Is the end of all journeys."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_286.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>286.  L<u>a</u> yukallifu All<u>a</u>hu nafsan ill<u>a</u> wusAAah<u>a</u> lah<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> kasabat waAAalayh<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> iktasabat rabban<u>a</u> l<u>a</u> tu-<u>a</u>khi<u>th</u>n<u>a</u> in naseen<u>a</u> aw akh<u>t</u>a/n<u>a</u> rabban<u>a</u> wal<u>a</u> ta<u>h</u>mil AAalayn<u>a</u> i<u>s</u>ran kam<u>a</u> <u>h</u>amaltahu AAal<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena min qablin<u>a</u> rabban<u>a</u> wal<u>a</u> tu<u>h</u>ammiln<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> l<u>a</u> <u>ta</u>qata lan<u>a</u> bihi wa<b>o</b>AAfu AAann<u>a</u> wa<b>i</b>ghfir lan<u>a</u> wa<b>i</b>r<u>h</u>amn<u>a</u> anta mawl<u>a</u>n<u>a</u> fa<b>o</b>n<u>s</u>urn<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> alqawmi alk<u>a</u>fireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>286.  On no soul doth God<br> Place a burden greater<br> Than it can bear.<br> It gets every good that it earns,<br> And it suffers every ill that if earns.<br> (Pray:) "Our Lord!<br> Condemn us not<br> If we forget or fall<br> Into error; our Lord!<br> Lay not on us a burden<br> Like that which Thou<br> Didst lay on those before us;<br>Our Lord! lay not on us<br> A burden greater than we<br> Have strength to bear.<br> Blot out our sins,<br> And grant us forgiveness,<br> Have mercy on us.<br> Thou art our Protector;<br> Help us against those<br> Who stand against Faith"</p>  <div class="filenav">
