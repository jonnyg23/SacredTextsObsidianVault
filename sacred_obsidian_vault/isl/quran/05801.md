 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra LVIII.: Mujādila, or The Woman who Pleads.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/058/058_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  Qad samiAAa All<u>a</u>hu qawla allatee tuj<u>a</u>diluka fee zawjih<u>a</u> watashtakee il<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu yasmaAAu ta<u>ha</u>wurakum<u>a</u> inna All<u>a</u>ha sameeAAun ba<u>s</u>eer<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>1.  God has indeed<br> Heard (and accepted) the statement<br> Of the woman who pleads<br> With thee concerning her husband<br> And carries her complaint<br> (In prayer) to God:<br> And God (always) hears<br> The arguments between both<br> Sides among you: for God<br> Hears and sees (all things).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/058/058_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  Alla<u>th</u>eena yu<i><u>th</u></i><u>a</u>hiroona minkum min nis<u>a</u>-ihim m<u>a</u> hunna ommah<u>a</u>tihim in ommah<u>a</u>tuhum ill<u>a</u> all<u>a</u>-ee waladnahum wa-innahum layaqooloona munkaran mina alqawli wazooran wa-inna All<u>a</u>ha laAAafuwwun ghafoor<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>2.  If any men among you<br> Divorce their wives by<br> (Calling them mothers),<br> They cannot be their mothers:<br> None can be their mothers<br> Except those who gave them<br> Birth. And in fact<br> They use words (both) iniquitous<br> And false: but truly<br> God is One that blots out<br>(Sins), and forgives<br> (Again and again).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/058/058_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  Wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena yu<i><u>th</u></i><u>a</u>hiroona min nis<u>a</u>-ihim thumma yaAAoodoona lim<u>a</u> q<u>a</u>loo fata<u>h</u>reeru raqabatin min qabli an yatam<u>a</u>ss<u>a</u> <u>tha</u>likum tooAAa<i><u>th</u></i>oona bihi wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu bim<u>a</u> taAAmaloona khabeer<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>3.  But those who divorce<br> Their wives by Zihār,<br> Then wish to go back<br> On the words they uttered,—<br> (It is ordained that<br> Such a one)<br> Should free a slave<br> Before they touch each other:<br> This are ye admonished<br> To perform: and God is<br> Well-acquainted with (all)<br> That ye do.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/058/058_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  Faman lam yajid fa<u>s</u>iy<u>a</u>mu shahrayni mutat<u>a</u>biAAayni min qabli an yatam<u>a</u>ss<u>a</u> faman lam yasta<u>t</u>iAA fa-i<u>t</u>AA<u>a</u>mu sitteena miskeenan <u>tha</u>lika litu/minoo bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi warasoolihi watilka <u>h</u>udoodu All<u>a</u>hi walilk<u>a</u>fireena AAa<u>tha</u>bun aleem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>4.  And if any has not<br> (The wherewithal),<br> He should fast for<br> Two months consecutively<br> Before they touch each other.<br> But if any is unable<br> To do so, he should feed<br> Sixty indigent ones.<br> This, that ye may show<br> Your faith in God<br>And His Apostle.<br> Those are limits (set<br> By) God. For those who<br> Reject (Him), there is<br> A grievous Penalty.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/058/058_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  Inna alla<u>th</u>eena yu<u>ha</u>ddoona All<u>a</u>ha warasoolahu kubitoo kam<u>a</u> kubita alla<u>th</u>eena min qablihim waqad anzaln<u>a</u> <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin bayyin<u>a</u>tin walilk<u>a</u>fireena AAa<u>tha</u>bun muheen<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>5.  Those who resist God<br> And His Apostle will be<br> Humbled to dust, as were<br> Those before them: for We<br> Have already sent down<br> Clear Signs. And the Unbelievers<br> (Will have) a humiliating Penalty,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/058/058_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  Yawma yabAAathuhumu All<u>a</u>hu jameeAAan fayunabbi-ohum bim<u>a</u> AAamiloo a<u>hsa</u>hu All<u>a</u>hu wanasoohu wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu AAal<u>a</u> kulli shay-in shaheed<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>6.  On the Day that<br> God will raise them<br> All up (again) and show<br> Them the truth (and meaning)<br> Of their conduct. God has<br> Reckoned its (value), though<br> They may have forgotten it,<br> For God is Witness<br> To all things.</p> <div class="filenav">
