 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra VII.: A’rāf, or the Heights</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 6</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_048.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>48.  Wan<u>a</u>d<u>a</u> a<u>s</u>-<u>ha</u>bu al-aAAr<u>a</u>fi rij<u>a</u>lan yaAArifoonahum biseem<u>a</u>hum q<u>a</u>loo m<u>a</u> aghn<u>a</u> AAankum jamAAukum wam<u>a</u> kuntum tastakbiroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>48.  The men on the Heights<br> Will call to certain men<br> Whom they will know<br> From their m arks, saying:<br> "Of what profit to you<br> Were your hoards and your<br> Arrogant ways?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_049.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>49.  Ah<u>a</u>ol<u>a</u>-i alla<u>th</u>eena aqsamtum l<u>a</u> yan<u>a</u>luhumu All<u>a</u>hu bira<u>h</u>matin odkhuloo aljannata l<u>a</u> khawfun AAalaykum wal<u>a</u> antum ta<u>h</u>zanoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>49.  "Behold! are these not<br> The men whom you swore<br> That God with His Mercy<br> Would never bless?<br> Enter ye the Garden:<br> No fear shall be on you,<br> Nor shall ye grieve."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_050.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>50.  Wan<u>a</u>d<u>a</u> a<u>s</u>-<u>ha</u>bu a<b>l</b>n<u>a</u>ri a<u>s</u>-<u>ha</u>ba aljannati an afee<u>d</u>oo AAalayn<u>a</u> mina alm<u>a</u>-i aw mimm<u>a</u> razaqakumu All<u>a</u>hu q<u>a</u>loo inna All<u>a</u>ha <u>h</u>arramahum<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> alk<u>a</u>fireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>50.  The Companions of the Fire<br> Will call to the Companions<br> Of the Garden: "Pour down<br> To us water or anything<br>That God doth provide<br> For your sustenance."<br> They will say: "Both<br> These things hath God forbidden<br> To those who rejected Him;—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_051.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>51.  Alla<u>th</u>eena ittakha<u>th</u>oo deenahum lahwan walaAAiban wagharrat-humu al<u>h</u>ay<u>a</u>tu a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> fa<b>a</b>lyawma nans<u>a</u>hum kam<u>a</u> nasoo liq<u>a</u>a yawmihim h<u>atha</u> wam<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin<u>a</u> yaj<u>h</u>adoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>51.  "Such as took their religion<br> To be mere amusement<br> And play, and were deceived<br> By the life of the world."<br> That day shall We forget them<br> As they forgot the meeting<br> Of this day of theirs,<br> And as they were wont<br> To reject Our Signs.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_052.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>52.  Walaqad ji/n<u>a</u>hum bikit<u>a</u>bin fa<u>ss</u>aln<u>a</u>hu AAal<u>a</u> AAilmin hudan wara<u>h</u>matan liqawmin yu/minoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>52.  For We had certainly<br> Sent unto them a Book,<br> Based on knowledge,<br> Which We explained<br> In detail,—a guide<br> And a mercy<br> To all who believe.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_053.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>53.  Hal yan<i><u>th</u></i>uroona ill<u>a</u> ta/weelahu yawma ya/tee ta/weeluhu yaqoolu alla<u>th</u>eena nasoohu min qablu qad j<u>a</u>at rusulu rabbin<u>a</u> bi<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>aqqi fahal lan<u>a</u> min shufaAA<u>a</u>a fayashfaAAoo lan<u>a</u> aw nuraddu fanaAAmala ghayra alla<u>th</u>ee kunn<u>a</u> naAAmalu qad khasiroo anfusahum wa<u>d</u>alla AAanhum m<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo yaftaroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>53.  Do they just wait<br> For the final fulfilment<br> Of the event? On the day<br> The event is finally fulfilled,<br> Those who disregarded it<br> Before will say: "The apostles<br> Of our Lord did indeed<br> Bring true (tidings). Have we<br> No intercessors now to intercede<br> On our behalf? Or could we<br>Be sent back? Then should eve<br> Behave differently from our<br> Behaviour in the past."<br> In fact they will have lost<br> Their souls, and the things<br> They invented will leave<br> Them in the lurch.</p> <div class="filenav">
