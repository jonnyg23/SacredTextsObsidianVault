 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra LIX.: Ḥa<u>sh</u>r, or The Gathering</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 2</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/059/059_011.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>11.  Alam tara il<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena n<u>a</u>faqoo yaqooloona li-ikhw<u>a</u>nihimu alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo min ahli alkit<u>a</u>bi la-in okhrijtum lanakhrujanna maAAakum wal<u>a</u> nu<u>t</u>eeAAu feekum a<u>h</u>adan abadan wa-in qootiltum lanan<u>s</u>urannakum wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu yashhadu innahum lak<u>ath</u>iboon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>11.  Hast thou not observed<br> The Hypocrites say<br> To their misbelieving brethren<br> Among the People of the Book?—<br> "If ye are expelled,<br> We too will go out<br> With you, and we will<br> Never hearken, to any one<br> In your affair; and if<br> Ye are attacked (in fight)<br>We will help you".<br> But God is witness<br> That they are indeed liars.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/059/059_012.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>12.  La-in okhrijoo l<u>a</u> yakhrujoona maAAahum wala-in qootiloo l<u>a</u> yan<u>s</u>uroonahum wala-in na<u>s</u>aroohum layuwallunna al-adb<u>a</u>ra thumma l<u>a</u> yun<u>s</u>aroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>12.  If they are expelled,<br> Never will they go out<br> With them; and if they<br> Are attacked (in fight),<br> They will never help them;<br> And if they do help them,<br> They will turn their backs;<br> So they will receive no help.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/059/059_013.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>13.  Laantum ashaddu rahbatan fee <u>s</u>udoorihim mina All<u>a</u>hi <u>tha</u>lika bi-annahum qawmun l<u>a</u> yafqahoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>13.  Of a truth ye are<br> Stronger (than they)<br> Because of the terror<br> In their hearts,<br> (Sent) by God.<br> This is because they are<br> Men devoid of understanding.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/059/059_014.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>14.  L<u>a</u> yuq<u>a</u>tiloonakum jameeAAan ill<u>a</u> fee quran mu<u>h</u>a<u>ss</u>anatin aw min war<u>a</u>-i judurin ba/suhum baynahum shadeedun ta<u>h</u>sabuhum jameeAAan waquloobuhum shatt<u>a</u> <u>tha</u>lika bi-annahum qawmun l<u>a</u> yaAAqiloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>14.  They will not fight you<br> (Even) together, except<br> In fortified townships,<br> Or from behind walls.<br> Strong is their fighting (spirit)<br> Amongst themselves:<br> Thou wouldst think<br> They were united,<br> But their hearts are divided:<br> That is because they<br>Are a people devoid<br> Of wisdom.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/059/059_015.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>15.  Kamathali alla<u>th</u>eena min qablihim qareeban <u>tha</u>qoo wab<u>a</u>la amrihim walahum AAa<u>tha</u>bun aleem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>15.  Like those who lately<br> Preceded them, they have<br> Tasted the evil result<br> Of their conduct; and<br> (In the Hereafter there is)<br> For them a grievous Penalty;—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/059/059_016.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>16.  Kamathali a<b>l</b>shshay<u>ta</u>ni i<u>th</u> q<u>a</u>la lil-ins<u>a</u>ni okfur falamm<u>a</u> kafara q<u>a</u>la innee baree-on minka innee akh<u>a</u>fu All<u>a</u>ha rabba alAA<u>a</u>lameen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>16.  (Their allies deceived them),<br> Like the Evil One,<br> When he says to man,<br> "Deny God": but when<br> (Man) denies God,<br> (The Evil One) says,<br> "I am free of thee:<br> I do fear God,<br> The Lord of the Worlds!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/059/059_017.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>17.  Fak<u>a</u>na AA<u>a</u>qibatahum<u>a</u> annahum<u>a</u> fee a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>ri kh<u>a</u>lidayni feeh<u>a</u> wa<u>tha</u>lika jaz<u>a</u>o a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>17.  The end of both will be<br> That they will go<br> Into the Fire, dwelling<br> Therein for ever.<br> Such is the reward<br> Of the wrong-doers.</p> <div class="filenav">
