 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_258.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>258.  Alam tara il<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>ee <u>ha</u>jja ibr<u>a</u>heema fee rabbihi an <u>a</u>t<u>a</u>hu All<u>a</u>hu almulka i<u>th</u> q<u>a</u>la ibr<u>a</u>heemu rabbiya alla<u>th</u>ee yu<u>h</u>yee wayumeetu q<u>a</u>la an<u>a</u> o<u>h</u>yee waomeetu q<u>a</u>la ibr<u>a</u>heemu fa-inna All<u>a</u>ha ya/tee bi<b>al</b>shshamsi mina almashriqi fa/ti bih<u>a</u> mina almaghribi fabuhita alla<u>th</u>ee kafara wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu l<u>a</u> yahdee alqawma a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>258.  Hast thou not<br> Turned thy vision to one<br> Who disputed with Abraham<br> About his Lord, because<br> God had granted him<br> Power? Abraham said:<br> "My Lord is He Who<br> Giveth life and death."<br> He said: "I give life and death."<br> Said Abraham: "But it is God<br> That causeth the sun<br> To rise from the East:<br> Do thou then cause him<br> To rise from the West."<br> Thus was he confounded<br> Who (in arrogance) rejected<br> Faith. Nor doth God<br> Give guidance<br> To a people unjust.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_259.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>259.  Aw ka<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>ee marra AAal<u>a</u> qaryatin wahiya kh<u>a</u>wiyatun AAal<u>a</u> AAurooshih<u>a</u> q<u>a</u>la ann<u>a</u> yu<u>h</u>yee h<u>ath</u>ihi All<u>a</u>hu baAAda mawtih<u>a</u> faam<u>a</u>tahu All<u>a</u>hu mi-ata AA<u>a</u>min thumma baAAathahu q<u>a</u>la kam labithta q<u>a</u>la labithtu yawman aw baAA<u>d</u>a yawmin q<u>a</u>la bal labithta mi-ata AA<u>a</u>min fa<b>o</b>n<i><u>th</u></i>ur il<u>a</u> <u>t</u>aAA<u>a</u>mika washar<u>a</u>bika lam yatasannah wa<b>o</b>n<i><u>th</u></i>ur il<u>a</u> <u>h</u>im<u>a</u>rika walinajAAalaka <u>a</u>yatan li<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si wa<b>o</b>n<i><u>th</u></i>ur il<u>a</u> alAAi<i><u>th</u></i><u>a</u>mi kayfa nunshizuh<u>a</u> thumma naksooh<u>a</u> la<u>h</u>man falamm<u>a</u> tabayyana lahu q<u>a</u>la aAAlamu anna All<u>a</u>ha AAal<u>a</u> kulli shay-in qadeer<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>259.  Or (take) the similitude<br> Of one who passed<br>By a hamlet, all in ruins<br> To its roofs. He said:<br> "Oh! how shall God<br> Bring it (ever) to life,<br> After (this) its death?"<br> But God caused him<br> To die for a hundred years,<br> Then raised him up (again).<br> He said: "How long<br> Didst thou tarry (thus)?"<br> He said: "(Perhaps) a day<br> Or part of a day." He said:<br> "Nay, thou hast tarried<br> Thus a hundred years;<br> But look at thy food<br> And thy drink; they show<br> No signs of age; and look<br> At thy donkey: and that<br> We may make of thee<br> A Sign unto the people,<br> Look further at the bones,<br> How We bring them together<br> And clothe them with flesh."<br> When this was shown clearly<br> To him, he said: "I know<br> That God hath power<br> Over all things."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_260.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>260.  Wa-i<u>th</u> q<u>a</u>la ibr<u>a</u>heemu rabbi arinee kayfa tu<u>h</u>yee almawt<u>a</u> q<u>a</u>la awa lam tu/min q<u>a</u>la bal<u>a</u> wal<u>a</u>kin liya<u>t</u>ma-inna qalbee q<u>a</u>la fakhu<u>th</u> arbaAAatan mina a<b>l</b><u>tt</u>ayri fa<u>s</u>urhunna ilayka thumma ijAAal AAal<u>a</u> kulli jabalin minhunna juz-an thumma odAAuhunna ya/teenaka saAAyan wa<b>i</b>AAlam anna All<u>a</u>ha AAazeezun <u>h</u>akeem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>260.  Behold! Abraham said:<br> "My Lord! Show me how<br> Thou givest life to the dead."<br> He said: "Dost thou not<br> Then believe?" He said:<br> "Yea! but to satisfy<br>My own understanding."<br> He said: "Take four birds;<br> Tame them to turn to thee;<br> Put a portion of them<br> On every hill, and call to them:<br> They will come to thee<br> (Flying) with speed.<br> Then know that God<br> Is Exalted in Power, Wise."</p> <div class="filenav">
