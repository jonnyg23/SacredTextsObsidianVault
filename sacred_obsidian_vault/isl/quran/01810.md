 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XVIII.: Kahf, or the Cave.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 10</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_071.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>71.  Fa<b>i</b>n<u>t</u>alaq<u>a</u> <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> i<u>tha</u> rakib<u>a</u> fee a<b>l</b>ssafeenati kharaqah<u>a</u> q<u>a</u>la akharaqtah<u>a</u> litughriqa ahlah<u>a</u> laqad ji/ta shay-an imr<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>71.  So they both proceeded:<br> Until, when they were<br> In the boat, he scuttled it.<br> Said Moses: "Hast thou<br> Scuttled it in order<br> To drown those in it?<br> Truly a strange thing<br> Hast thou done!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_072.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>72.  Q<u>a</u>la alam aqul innaka lan tasta<u>t</u>eeAAa maAAiya <u>s</u>abr<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>72.  He answered: "Did I not<br> Tell thee that thou canst<br> Have no patience with me?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_073.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>73.  Q<u>a</u>la l<u>a</u> tu-<u>a</u>khi<u>th</u>nee bim<u>a</u> naseetu wal<u>a</u> turhiqnee min amree AAusr<u>a</u><b>n</b>    </p>
 <p>73.  Moses said: "Rebuke me not<br> For forgetting, nor grieve me<br> By raising difficulties<br> In my case."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_074.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>74.  Fa<b>i</b>n<u>t</u>alaq<u>a</u> <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> i<u>tha</u> laqiy<u>a</u> ghul<u>a</u>man faqatalahu q<u>a</u>la aqatalta nafsan zakiyyatan bighayri nafsin laqad ji/ta shay-an nukr<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>74.  Then they proceeded:<br> Until, when they met<br> A young man, he slew him.<br> Moses said: "Hast thou<br> Slain an innocent person<br> Who had slain none?<br> Truly a foul (unheard-of) thing<br> Hast thou done!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_075.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>75.  Q<u>a</u>la alam aqul laka innaka lan tasta<u>t</u>eeAAa maAAiya <u>s</u>abr<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>75.  He answered, "Did I not<br> Tell thee that thou canst<br> Have no patience with me?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_076.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>76.  Q<u>a</u>la in saaltuka AAan shay-in baAAdah<u>a</u> fal<u>a</u> tu<u>sah</u>ibnee qad balaghta min ladunnee AAu<u>th</u>r<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>76.  (Moses) said: "If ever I<br> Ask thee about anything<br> After this, keep me not<br> In thy company: then wouldst<br> Thou have received (full) excuse<br> From my side.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_077.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>77.  Fa<b>i</b>n<u>t</u>alaq<u>a</u> <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> i<u>tha</u> atay<u>a</u> ahla qaryatin ista<u>t</u>AAam<u>a</u> ahlah<u>a</u> faabaw an yu<u>d</u>ayyifoohum<u>a</u> fawajad<u>a</u> feeh<u>a</u> jid<u>a</u>ran yureedu an yanqa<u>dd</u>a faaq<u>a</u>mahu q<u>a</u>la law shi/ta la<b>i</b>ttakha<u>th</u>ta AAalayhi ajr<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>77.  Then they proceeded:<br> Until, when they came<br> To the inhabitants of a town,<br> They asked them for food,<br> But they refused them<br> Hospitality. They found there<br> A wall on the point of<br> Falling down, but he<br> Set it up straight.<br> (Moses) said: "If thou<br> Hadst wished, surely thou<br> Couldst have exacted some<br> Recompense for it!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_078.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>78.  Q<u>a</u>la h<u>atha</u> fir<u>a</u>qu baynee wabaynika saonabbi-oka bita/weeli m<u>a</u> lam tasta<u>t</u>iAA AAalayhi <u>s</u>abr<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>78.  He answered: "This is<br> The parting between me<br> And thee: now will I<br> Tell thee the interpretation<br> Of (those things) over which<br> Thou wast unable<br> To hold patience.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_079.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>79.  Amm<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>ssafeenatu fak<u>a</u>nat limas<u>a</u>keena yaAAmaloona fee alba<u>h</u>ri faaradtu an aAAeebah<u>a</u> wak<u>a</u>na war<u>a</u>ahum malikun ya/khu<u>th</u>u kulla safeenatin gha<u>s</u>b<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>79.  "As for the boat,<br> It belonged to certain<br> Men in dire want:<br> They plied on the water:<br>I but wished to render it<br> Unserviceable, for there was<br> After them a certain king<br> Who seized on every boat<br> By force.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_080.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>80.  Waamm<u>a</u> alghul<u>a</u>mu fak<u>a</u>na abaw<u>a</u>hu mu/minayni fakhasheen<u>a</u> an yurhiqahum<u>a</u> <u>t</u>ughy<u>a</u>nan wakufr<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>80.  "As for the youth,<br> His parents were people<br> Of Faith, and we feared<br> That he would grieve them<br> By obstinate rebellion<br> And ingratitude (to God and man).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_081.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>81.  Faaradn<u>a</u> an yubdilahum<u>a</u> rabbuhum<u>a</u> khayran minhu zak<u>a</u>tan waaqraba ru<u>h</u>m<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>81.  "So we desired that<br> Their Lord would give them<br> In exchange (a son)<br> Better in purity (of conduct)<br> And closer in affection.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_082.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>82.  Waamm<u>a</u> aljid<u>a</u>ru fak<u>a</u>na lighul<u>a</u>mayni yateemayni fee almadeenati wak<u>a</u>na ta<u>h</u>tahu kanzun lahum<u>a</u> wak<u>a</u>na aboohum<u>a</u> <u>sa</u>li<u>h</u>an faar<u>a</u>da rabbuka an yablugh<u>a</u> ashuddahum<u>a</u> wayastakhrij<u>a</u> kanzahum<u>a</u> ra<u>h</u>matan min rabbika wam<u>a</u> faAAaltuhu AAan amree <u>tha</u>lika ta/weelu m<u>a</u> lam tas<u>t</u>iAA AAalayhi <u>s</u>abr<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>82.  "As for the wall,<br> It belonged to two youths,<br> Orphans, in the Town;<br> There was, beneath it,<br> A buried treasure, to which<br> They were entitled; their father<br>Had been a righteous man:<br> So thy Lord desired that<br> They should attain their age<br> Of full strength and get out<br> Their treasure—a mercy<br> (And favour) from thy Lord.<br> I did it not of my own<br> Accord. Such is the interpretation<br> Of (those things) over which<br> Thou wast unable<br> To hold patience."</p> <div class="filenav">
