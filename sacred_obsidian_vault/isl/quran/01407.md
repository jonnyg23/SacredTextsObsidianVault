 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XIV.: Ibrāhīm, or Abraham.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 7</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/014/014_042.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>42.  Wal<u>a</u> ta<u>h</u>sabanna All<u>a</u>ha gh<u>a</u>filan AAamm<u>a</u> yaAAmalu a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limoona innam<u>a</u> yu-akhkhiruhum liyawmin tashkha<u>s</u>u feehi al-ab<u>sa</u>r<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>42.  Think not that God<br> Doth not heed the deeds<br> Of those who do wrong.<br> He but giveth them respite<br> Against a Day when<br> The eyes will fixedly stare<br> In horror,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/014/014_043.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>43.  Muh<u>t</u>iAAeena muqniAAee ruoosihim l<u>a</u> yartaddu ilayhim <u>t</u>arfuhum waaf-idatuhum haw<u>a</u>/<b>on</b>   </p>
 <p>43.  They running forward<br> With necks outstretched,<br> Their heads uplifted, their gaze<br> Returning not towards them,<br> And their hearts a (gaping) void!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/014/014_044.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>44.  Waan<u>th</u>iri a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>sa yawma ya/teehimu alAAa<u>tha</u>bu fayaqoolu alla<u>th</u>eena <i><u>th</u></i>alamoo rabban<u>a</u> akhkhirn<u>a</u> il<u>a</u> ajalin qareebin nujib daAAwataka wanattabiAAi a<b>l</b>rrusula awa lam takoonoo aqsamtum min qablu m<u>a</u> lakum min zaw<u>a</u>l<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>44.  So warn mankind<br> Of the Day when the Wrath<br> Will reach them: then will<br> The wrong-doers say: "Our Lord!<br> Respite us (if only)<br> For a short Term: we will<br> Answer Thy Call, and follow<br> The apostles!"<br> "What! were ye not wont<br> To swear aforetime that ye<br> Should suffer no decline?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/014/014_045.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>45.  Wasakantum fee mas<u>a</u>kini alla<u>th</u>eena <i><u>th</u></i>alamoo anfusahum watabayyana lakum kayfa faAAaln<u>a</u> bihim wa<u>d</u>arabn<u>a</u> lakumu al-amth<u>a</u>l<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>45.  "And ye dwelt in the dwellings<br> Of men who wronged their own<br> Souls; ye were clearly shown<br> How We dealt with them;<br> And We put forth (many) Parables<br> In your behoof!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/014/014_046.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>46.  Waqad makaroo makrahum waAAinda All<u>a</u>hi makruhum wa-in k<u>a</u>na makruhum litazoola minhu aljib<u>a</u>l<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>46.  Mighty indeed were the plots<br> Which they made, but their plots<br> Were (well) within the sight<br> Of God, even though they were<br> Such as to shake the hills!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/014/014_047.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>47.  Fal<u>a</u> ta<u>h</u>sabanna All<u>a</u>ha mukhlifa waAAdihi rusulahu inna All<u>a</u>ha AAazeezun <u>th</u>oo intiq<u>a</u>m<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>47.  Never think that God would fail<br> His apostles in His promise:<br> For God is Exalted in Power,—<br> The Lord of Retribution.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/014/014_048.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>48.  Yawma tubaddalu al-ar<u>d</u>u ghayra al-ar<u>d</u>i wa<b>al</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>tu wabarazoo lill<u>a</u>hi alw<u>ah</u>idi alqahh<u>a</u>r<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>48.  One day the Earth will be<br> Changed to a different Earth,<br> And so will be the Heavens,<br> And (men) will be marshalled<br> Forth, before God, the One,<br> The Irresistible;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/014/014_049.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>49.  Watar<u>a</u> almujrimeena yawma-i<u>th</u>in muqarraneena fee al-a<u>s</u>f<u>a</u>d<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>49.  And thou wilt see<br> The Sinners that day<br> Bound together in fetters;—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/014/014_050.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>50.  Sar<u>a</u>beeluhum min qa<u>t</u>r<u>a</u>nin wataghsh<u>a</u> wujoohahumu a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>r<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>50.  Their garments of liquid pitch,<br> And their faces covered with Fire;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/014/014_051.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>51.  Liyajziya All<u>a</u>hu kulla nafsin m<u>a</u> kasabat inna All<u>a</u>ha sareeAAu al<u>h</u>is<u>a</u>b<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>51.  That God may requite<br> Each soul according<br> To its deserts;<br> And verily God is Swift<br> In calling to account.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/014/014_052.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>52.  H<u>atha</u> bal<u>a</u>ghun li<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si waliyun<u>th</u>aroo bihi waliyaAAlamoo annam<u>a</u> huwa il<u>a</u>hun w<u>ah</u>idun waliya<u>thth</u>akkara oloo al-alb<u>a</u>b<b>i</b>    </p>
 <p>52.  Here is a Message for mankind:<br> Let them take warning therefrom,<br> And let them know that He<br> Is (no other than) One God<br> Let men of understanding<br> Take heed.</p>  <div class="filenav">
