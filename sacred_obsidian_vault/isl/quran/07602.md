 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sura LXXVI.: Dahr, or Time, or Insān, or Man.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 2</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/076/076_023.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>23.  Inn<u>a</u> na<u>h</u>nu nazzaln<u>a</u> AAalayka alqur-<u>a</u>na tanzeel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>23.  It is We Who<br> Have sent down the Qur-ān<br> To thee by stages.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/076/076_024.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>24.  Fa<b>i</b><u>s</u>bir li<u>h</u>ukmi rabbika wal<u>a</u> tu<u>t</u>iAA minhum <u>a</u>thiman aw kafoor<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>24.  Therefore be patient<br> With constancy to the Command<br> Of thy Lord, and hearken not<br> To the sinner or the ingrate<br> Among them.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/076/076_025.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>25.  Wa<b>o</b><u>th</u>kuri isma rabbika bukratan waa<u>s</u>eel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>25.  And celebrate the name<br>Of thy Lord morning<br> And evening,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/076/076_026.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>26.  Wamina allayli fa<b>o</b>sjud lahu wasabbi<u>h</u>hu laylan <u>t</u>aweel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>26.  And part of the night,<br> Prostrate thyself to Him;<br> And glorify Him<br> A long night through.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/076/076_027.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>27.  Inna h<u>a</u>ol<u>a</u>-i yu<u>h</u>ibboona alAA<u>a</u>jilata waya<u>th</u>aroona war<u>a</u>ahum yawman thaqeel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>27.  As to these, they love<br> The fleeting life,<br> And put away behind them<br> A Day (that will be) hard.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/076/076_028.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>28.  Na<u>h</u>nu khalaqn<u>a</u>hum washadadn<u>a</u> asrahum wa-i<u>tha</u> shi/n<u>a</u> baddaln<u>a</u> amth<u>a</u>lahum tabdeel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>28.  It is We Who created<br> Them, and We have made<br> Their joints strong;<br> But, when We will,<br> We can substitute<br> The like of them<br> By a complete change.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/076/076_029.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>29.  Inna h<u>ath</u>ihi ta<u>th</u>kiratun faman sh<u>a</u>a ittakha<u>th</u>a il<u>a</u> rabbihi sabeel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>29.  This is an admonition:<br> Whosoever will, let him<br> Take a (straight) Path<br> To his Lord.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/076/076_030.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>30.  Wam<u>a</u> tash<u>a</u>oona ill<u>a</u> an yash<u>a</u>a All<u>a</u>hu inna All<u>a</u>ha k<u>a</u>na AAaleeman <u>h</u>akeem<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>30.  But ye will not,<br> Except as God wills;<br>For God is full of<br> Knowledge and Wisdom.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/076/076_031.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>31.  Yudkhilu man yash<u>a</u>o fee ra<u>h</u>matihi wa<b>al</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limeena aAAadda lahum AAa<u>tha</u>ban aleem<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>31.  He will admit<br> To His Mercy Whom He will;<br> But the wrong-doers,—<br> For them has He prepared<br> A grievous Penalty.</p>  <div class="filenav">
