 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra IX.: Tauba (Repentance) or Barāat (Immunity).</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 4</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_025.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>25.  Laqad na<u>s</u>arakumu All<u>a</u>hu fee maw<u>at</u>ina katheeratin wayawma <u>h</u>unaynin i<u>th</u> aAAjabatkum kathratukum falam tughni AAankum shay-an wa<u>da</u>qat AAalaykumu al-ar<u>d</u>u bim<u>a</u> ra<u>h</u>ubat thumma wallaytum mudbireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>25.  Assuredly God did help you<br> In many battle-fields<br> And on the day of Ḥunain:<br> Behold! your great numbers<br> Elated you, but they availed<br> You naught: the land,<br> For all that it is wide,<br> Did. constrain you, and ye<br>Turned back in retreat.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_026.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>26.  Thumma anzala All<u>a</u>hu sakeenatahu AAal<u>a</u> rasoolihi waAAal<u>a</u> almu/mineena waanzala junoodan lam tarawh<u>a</u> waAAa<u>thth</u>aba alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo wa<u>tha</u>lika jaz<u>a</u>o alk<u>a</u>fireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>26.  But God did pour His calm<br> On the Apostle and on the Believers,<br> And sent down forces which ye<br> Saw not: He punished<br> The Unbelievers: thus doth He<br> Reward those without Faith.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_027.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>27.  Thumma yatoobu All<u>a</u>hu min baAAdi <u>tha</u>lika AAal<u>a</u> man yash<u>a</u>o wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu ghafoorun ra<u>h</u>eem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>27.  Again will God, after this,<br> Turn (in mercy) to whom<br> He will: for God<br> Is Oft-forgiving, Most Merciful.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_028.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>28.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo innam<u>a</u> almushrikoona najasun fal<u>a</u> yaqraboo almasjida al<u>h</u>ar<u>a</u>ma baAAda AA<u>a</u>mihim h<u>atha</u> wa-in khiftum AAaylatan fasawfa yughneekumu All<u>a</u>hu min fa<u>d</u>lihi in sh<u>a</u>a inna All<u>a</u>ha AAaleemun <u>h</u>akeem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>28.  O ye who believe! Truly<br> The Pagans are unclean;<br> So let them not,<br> After this year of theirs,<br>Approach the Sacred Mosque.<br> And if ye fear poverty,<br> Soon will God enrich you,<br> If He wills, out of His bounty,<br> For God is All-Knowing, All-Wise.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_029.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>29.  Q<u>a</u>tiloo alla<u>th</u>eena l<u>a</u> yu/minoona bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi wal<u>a</u> bi<b>a</b>lyawmi al-<u>a</u>khiri wal<u>a</u> yu<u>h</u>arrimoona m<u>a</u> <u>h</u>arrama All<u>a</u>hu warasooluhu wal<u>a</u> yadeenoona deena al<u>h</u>aqqi mina alla<u>th</u>eena ootoo alkit<u>a</u>ba <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> yuAA<u>t</u>oo aljizyata AAan yadin wahum <u>sa</u>ghiroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>29.  Fight those who believe not<br> In God nor the Last Day,<br> Nor hold that forbidden<br> Which hath been forbidden<br> By God and His Apostle,<br> Nor acknowledge the Religion<br> Of Truth, (even if they are)<br> Of the People of the Book,<br> Until they pay the Jizya<br> With willing submission,<br> And feel themselves subdued.</p> <div class="filenav">
