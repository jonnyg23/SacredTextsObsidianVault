 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXVIII.: Qaṣaṣ, or the Narration.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 7</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/028/028_061.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>61.  Afaman waAAadn<u>a</u>hu waAAdan <u>h</u>asanan fahuwa l<u>a</u>qeehi kaman mattaAAn<u>a</u>hu mat<u>a</u>AAa al<u>h</u>ay<u>a</u>ti a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> thumma huwa yawma alqiy<u>a</u>mati mina almu<u>hd</u>areen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>61.  Are (these two) alike?—<br> One to whom We have made<br> A goodly promise, and who<br> Is going to reach its (fulfilment),<br> And one to whom We have<br> Given the good things of this<br> Life, but who, on the Day<br> Of Judgment, is to be among<br> Those brought up (for punishment)?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/028/028_062.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>62.  Wayawma yun<u>a</u>deehim fayaqoolu ayna shurak<u>a</u>-iya alla<u>th</u>eena kuntum tazAAumoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>62.  That Day (God) will<br> Call to them, and say:<br> "Where are My 'partners'?—<br> Whom ye imagined (to be such)?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/028/028_063.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>63.  Q<u>a</u>la alla<u>th</u>eena <u>h</u>aqqa AAalayhimu alqawlu rabban<u>a</u> h<u>a</u>ol<u>a</u>-i alla<u>th</u>eena aghwayn<u>a</u> aghwayn<u>a</u>hum kam<u>a</u> ghawayn<u>a</u> tabarra/n<u>a</u> ilayka m<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo iyy<u>a</u>n<u>a</u> yaAAbudoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>63.  Those against whom the charge<br> Will be proved, will say:<br> "Our Lord! These are the ones<br> Whom we led astray:<br> We led them astray, as we<br> Were astray ourselves: we free<br> Ourselves (from them) in Thy presence:<br> It was not us they worshipped."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/028/028_064.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>64.  Waqeela odAAoo shurak<u>a</u>akum fadaAAawhum falam yastajeeboo lahum waraawoo alAAa<u>tha</u>ba law annahum k<u>a</u>noo yahtadoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>64.  It will be said (to them):<br> "Call upon your 'partners'<br> (For help)": they will call<br> Upon them, but they will not<br> Listen to them; and they<br> Will see the Penalty (before them);<br> (How they will wish)<br> 'If only they had been<br> Open to guidance!'</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/028/028_065.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>65.  Wayawma yun<u>a</u>deehim fayaqoolu m<u>atha</u> ajabtumu almursaleen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>65.  That Day (God) will<br> Call to them, and say:<br> "What was the answer<br> Ye gave to the apostles?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/028/028_066.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>66.  FaAAamiyat AAalayhimu al-anb<u>a</u>o yawma-i<u>th</u>in fahum l<u>a</u> yatas<u>a</u>aloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>66.  Then the (whole) story that day<br> Will seem obscure to them<br> (Like light to the blind)<br> And they will not be able<br> (Even) to question each other.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/028/028_067.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>67.  Faamm<u>a</u> man t<u>a</u>ba wa<u>a</u>mana waAAamila <u>sa</u>li<u>h</u>an faAAas<u>a</u> an yakoona mina almufli<u>h</u>een<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>67.  But any that (in this life)<br> Had repented, believed, and worked<br> Righteousness, will have hopes<br> To be among those who<br> Achieve salvation.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/028/028_068.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>68.  Warabbuka yakhluqu m<u>a</u> yash<u>a</u>o wayakht<u>a</u>ru m<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>na lahumu alkhiyaratu sub<u>ha</u>na All<u>a</u>hi wataAA<u>a</u>l<u>a</u> AAamm<u>a</u> yushrikoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>68.  Why by Lord does create and choose<br> As He pleases: no choice<br> Have they (in the matter):<br> Glory to God! and far<br> Is He above the partners<br> They ascribe (to Him)!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/028/028_069.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>69.  Warabbuka yaAAlamu m<u>a</u> tukinnu <u>s</u>udooruhum wam<u>a</u> yuAAlinoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>69.  And thy Lord knows all<br> That their hearts conceal<br> And all that they reveal.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/028/028_070.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>70.  Wahuwa All<u>a</u>hu l<u>a</u> il<u>a</u>ha ill<u>a</u> huwa lahu al<u>h</u>amdu fee al-ool<u>a</u> wa<b>a</b>l-<u>a</u>khirati walahu al<u>h</u>ukmu wa-ilayhi turjaAAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>70.  And He is God: there is<br> No god. but He. To Him<br> Be praise, at the first<br> And at the last:<br> For Him is the Command,<br> And to Him shall ye<br> (All) be brought back.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/028/028_071.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>71.  Qul araaytum in jaAAala All<u>a</u>hu AAalaykumu allayla sarmadan il<u>a</u> yawmi alqiy<u>a</u>mati man il<u>a</u>hun ghayru All<u>a</u>hi ya/teekum bi<u>d</u>iy<u>a</u>-in afal<u>a</u> tasmaAAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>71.  Say: See ye? If God<br> Were to make the Night<br>Perpetual over you to the Day<br> Of Judgment, what god<br> Is there other than God,<br> Who can give you enlightenment?<br> Will ye not then hearken?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/028/028_072.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>72.  Qul araaytum in jaAAala All<u>a</u>hu AAalaykumu a<b>l</b>nnah<u>a</u>ra sarmadan il<u>a</u> yawmi alqiy<u>a</u>mati man il<u>a</u>hun ghayru All<u>a</u>hi ya/teekum bilaylin taskunoona feehi afal<u>a</u> tub<u>s</u>iroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>72.  Say: See ye? If God<br> Were to make the Day<br> Perpetual over you to the Day<br> Of Judgment, what god<br> Is there other than God,<br> Who can give you a Night<br> In which ye can rest?<br> Will ye not then see?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/028/028_073.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>73.  Wamin ra<u>h</u>matihi jaAAala lakumu allayla wa<b>al</b>nnah<u>a</u>ra litaskunoo feehi walitabtaghoo min fa<u>d</u>lihi walaAAallakum tashkuroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>73.  It is out of His Mercy<br> That He has made for you<br> Night and Day,—that ye<br> May rest therein, and that<br> Ye may seek of His Grace;—<br> And in order that ye<br> May be grateful.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/028/028_074.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>74.  Wayawma yun<u>a</u>deehim fayaqoolu ayna shurak<u>a</u>-iya alla<u>th</u>eena kuntum tazAAumoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>74.  The Day that He will<br> Call on them, He will say:<br> "Where are My 'partners'?—<br> Whom ye imagined (to be such)?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/028/028_075.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>75.  WanazaAAn<u>a</u> min kulli ommatin shaheedan faquln<u>a</u> h<u>a</u>too burh<u>a</u>nakum faAAalimoo anna al<u>h</u>aqqa lill<u>a</u>hi wa<u>d</u>alla AAanhum m<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo yaftaroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>75.  And from each people<br> Shall We draw a witness,<br> And We shall say: "Produce<br> Your Proof": then shall they<br> Know that the Truth is in<br> God (alone), and the (lies)<br> Which they invented will<br> Leave them in the lurch.</p> <div class="filenav">
