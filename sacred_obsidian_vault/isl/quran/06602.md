 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/066/066_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo tooboo il<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi tawbatan na<u>s</u>oo<u>h</u>an AAas<u>a</u> rabbukum an yukaffira AAankum sayyi-<u>a</u>tikum wayudkhilakum jann<u>a</u>tin tajree min ta<u>h</u>tih<u>a</u> al-anh<u>a</u>ru yawma l<u>a</u> yukhzee All<u>a</u>hu a<b>l</b>nnabiyya wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo maAAahu nooruhum yasAA<u>a</u> bayna aydeehim wabi-aym<u>a</u>nihim yaqooloona rabban<u>a</u> atmim lan<u>a</u> nooran<u>a</u> wa<b>i</b>ghfir lan<u>a</u> innaka AAal<u>a</u> kulli shay-in qadeer<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>8.  O ye who believe!<br> Turn to God<br> With sincere repentance:<br> In the hope that<br> Your Lord will remove<br> From you your ills<br> And admit you to Gardens<br> Beneath which Rivers flow,—<br> The Day that God<br> Will not permit<br> To be humiliated<br> The Prophet and those<br> Who believe with him.<br> Their Light will run<br> Forward before them<br> And by their right hands,<br> While they say, "Our Lord!<br> Perfect our Light for us,<br> And grant us Forgiveness:<br> For Thou hast power<br> Over all things."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/066/066_009.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>9.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nnabiyyu j<u>a</u>hidi alkuff<u>a</u>ra wa<b>a</b>lmun<u>a</u>fiqeena wa<b>o</b>ghlu<i><u>th</u></i> AAalayhim wama/w<u>a</u>hum jahannamu wabi/sa alma<u>s</u>eer<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>9.  O Prophet! Strive hard<br> Against the Unbelievers<br> And the Hypocrites,<br> And be firm against them.<br>Their abode is Hell,—<br> An evil refuge (indeed).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/066/066_010.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>10.  <u>D</u>araba All<u>a</u>hu mathalan lilla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo imraata noo<u>h</u>in wa<b>i</b>mraata loo<u>t</u>in k<u>a</u>nat<u>a</u> ta<u>h</u>ta AAabdayni min AAib<u>a</u>din<u>a</u> <u>sa</u>li<u>h</u>ayni fakh<u>a</u>nat<u>a</u>hum<u>a</u> falam yughniy<u>a</u> AAanhum<u>a</u> mina All<u>a</u>hi shay-an waqeela odkhul<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>ra maAAa a<b>l</b>dd<u>a</u>khileen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>10.  God sets forth,<br> For an example<br> To the Unbelievers,<br> The wife of Noah<br> And the wife of Lūṭ:<br> They were (respectively)<br> Under two of our righteous<br> Servants, but they were<br> False to their (husbands),<br> And they profited nothing<br> Before God on their account,<br> But were told: "Enter ye<br> The Fire along with<br> (Others) that enter!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/066/066_011.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>11.  Wa<u>d</u>araba All<u>a</u>hu mathalan lilla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo imraata firAAawna i<u>th</u> q<u>a</u>lat rabbi ibni lee AAindaka baytan fee aljannati wanajjinee min firAAawna waAAamalihi wanajjinee mina alqawmi a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>11.  And God sets forth,<br> As an example<br> To those who believe,<br> The wife of Pharaoh:<br>Behold she said:<br> "O my Lord! build<br> For me, in nearness<br> To Thee, a mansion<br> In the Garden,<br> And save me from Pharaoh<br> And his doings,<br> And save me from<br> Those that do wrong";</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/066/066_012.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>12.  Wamaryama ibnata AAimr<u>a</u>na allatee a<u>hs</u>anat farjah<u>a</u> fanafakhn<u>a</u> feehi min roo<u>h</u>in<u>a</u> wa<u>s</u>addaqat bikalim<u>a</u>ti rabbih<u>a</u> wakutubihi wak<u>a</u>nat mina alq<u>a</u>niteen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>12.  And Mary the daughter<br> Of ‘Imrān, who guarded<br> Her chastity; and We<br> Breathed into (her body)<br> Of Our spirit; and she<br> Testified to the truth<br> Of the words of her Lord<br> And of his Revelations,<br> And was one of the<br> Devout (servants).</p>  <div class="filenav">
