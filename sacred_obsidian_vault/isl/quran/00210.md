 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_083.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>83.  Wa-i<u>th</u> akha<u>th</u>n<u>a</u> meeth<u>a</u>qa banee isr<u>a</u>-eela l<u>a</u> taAAbudoona ill<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>ha wabi<b>a</b>lw<u>a</u>lidayni i<u>h</u>s<u>a</u>nan wa<u>th</u>ee alqurb<u>a</u> wa<b>a</b>lyat<u>a</u>m<u>a</u> wa<b>a</b>lmas<u>a</u>keeni waqooloo li<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si <u>h</u>usnan waaqeemoo a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>al<u>a</u>ta wa<u>a</u>too a<b>l</b>zzak<u>a</u>ta thumma tawallaytum ill<u>a</u> qaleelan minkum waantum muAAri<u>d</u>oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>83.  And remember We took<br> A Covenant from the Children<br> Of Israel (to this effect):<br> Worship none but God;<br> Treat with kindness<br> Your parents and kindred,<br> And orphans and those in need;<br> Speak fair to the people;<br> Be steadfast in prayer;<br> And practise regular charity.<br> Then did ye turn back,<br> Except a few among you,<br> And ye backslide (even now).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_084.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>84.  Wa-i<u>th</u> akha<u>th</u>n<u>a</u> meeth<u>a</u>qakum l<u>a</u> tasfikoona dim<u>a</u>akum wal<u>a</u> tukhrijoona anfusakum min diy<u>a</u>rikum thumma aqrartum waantum tashhadoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>84.  And remember We took<br> Your Covenant (to this effect):<br> Shed no blood amongst you,<br> Nor turn out your own people<br> From your homes: and this<br> Ye solemnly ratified,<br> And to this ye can bear witness.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_085.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>85.  Thumma antum h<u>a</u>ol<u>a</u>-i taqtuloona anfusakum watukhrijoona fareeqan minkum min diy<u>a</u>rihim ta<i><u>th</u></i><u>a</u>haroona AAalayhim bi<b>a</b>l-ithmi wa<b>a</b>lAAudw<u>a</u>ni wa-in ya/tookum os<u>a</u>r<u>a</u> tuf<u>a</u>doohum wahuwa mu<u>h</u>arramun AAalaykum ikhr<u>a</u>juhum afatu/minoona bibaAA<u>d</u>i alkit<u>a</u>bi watakfuroona bibaAA<u>d</u>in fam<u>a</u> jaz<u>a</u>o man yafAAalu <u>tha</u>lika minkum ill<u>a</u> khizyun fee al<u>h</u>ay<u>a</u>ti a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> wayawma alqiy<u>a</u>mati yuraddoona il<u>a</u> ashaddi alAAa<u>tha</u>bi wam<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hu bigh<u>a</u>filin AAamm<u>a</u> taAAmaloon<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>85.  After this it is ye, the same people,<br> Who slay among yourselves,<br> And banish a party of you<br> From their homes; assist<br> (Their enemies) against them,<br> In guilt and rancour;<br> And if they come to you<br> As captives, ye ransom them,<br>Though it was not lawful<br> For you to banish them.<br> Then is it only a part of the Book<br> That ye believe in,<br> And do ye reject the rest?<br> But what is the reward for those<br> Among you who behave like this<br> But disgrace in this life?—<br> And on the Day of Judgment<br> They shall be consigned<br> To the most grievous penalty.<br> For God is not unmindful<br> Of what ye do.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_086.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>86.  Ol<u>a</u>-ika alla<u>th</u>eena ishtarawoo al<u>h</u>ay<u>a</u>ta a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> bi<b>a</b>l-<u>a</u>khirati fal<u>a</u> yukhaffafu AAanhumu alAAa<u>tha</u>bu wal<u>a</u> hum yun<u>s</u>aroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>86.  These are the people who buy<br> The life of this world at the price<br> Of the Hereafter: their penalty<br> Shall not be lightened<br> Nor shall they be helped.</p> <div class="filenav">
