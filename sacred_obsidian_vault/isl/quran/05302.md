 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra LIII.: Najm, or the Star.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 2</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/053/053_026.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>26.  Wakam min malakin fee a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti l<u>a</u> tughnee shaf<u>a</u>AAatuhum shay-an ill<u>a</u> min baAAdi an ya/<u>th</u>ana All<u>a</u>hu liman yash<u>a</u>o wayar<u>da</u>   </p>
 <p>26.  How many-so-ever be<br> The angels in the heavens,<br> Their intercession will avail nothing<br> Except after God has given<br> Leave for whom He pleases<br> And that he is acceptable<br> To Him.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/053/053_027.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>27.  Inna alla<u>th</u>eena l<u>a</u> yu/minoona bi<b>a</b>l-<u>a</u>khirati layusammoona almal<u>a</u>-ikata tasmiyata al-onth<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>27.  Those who believe not<br> In the Hereafter, name<br> The angels with female names.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/053/053_028.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>28.  Wam<u>a</u> lahum bihi min AAilmin in yattabiAAoona ill<u>a</u> a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i>anna wa-inna a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i>anna l<u>a</u> yughnee mina al<u>h</u>aqqi shay-<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>28.  But they have no knowledge<br> Therein. They follow nothing<br> But conjecture; and conjecture<br> Avails nothing against Truth.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/053/053_029.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>29.  FaaAAri<u>d</u> AAan man tawall<u>a</u> AAan <u>th</u>ikrin<u>a</u> walam yurid ill<u>a</u> al<u>h</u>ay<u>a</u>ta a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>29.  Therefore shun those who<br> Turn away from Our Message<br> And desire nothing but<br> The life of this world.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/053/053_030.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>30.  <u>Tha</u>lika mablaghuhum mina alAAilmi inna rabbaka huwa aAAlamu biman <u>d</u>alla AAan sabeelihi wahuwa aAAlamu bimani ihtad<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>30.  That is as far as<br> Knowledge will reach them.<br> Verily thy Lord knoweth best<br> Those who stray from<br> His Path, and He knoweth<br> Best those who receive guidance.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/053/053_031.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>31.  Walill<u>a</u>hi m<u>a</u> fee a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wam<u>a</u> fee al-ar<u>d</u>i liyajziya alla<u>th</u>eena as<u>a</u>oo bim<u>a</u> AAamiloo wayajziya alla<u>th</u>eena a<u>h</u>sanoo bi<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>usn<u>a</u>    </p>
 <p>31.  Yea, to God belongs all<br> That is in the heavens<br> And on earth: so that<br> He rewards those who do<br> Evil, according to their deeds,<br> And He rewards those who<br> Do good, with what is best.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/053/053_032.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>32.  Alla<u>th</u>eena yajtaniboona kab<u>a</u>-ira al-ithmi wa<b>a</b>lfaw<u>ah</u>isha ill<u>a</u> allamama inna rabbaka w<u>a</u>siAAu almaghfirati huwa aAAlamu bikum i<u>th</u> anshaakum mina al-ar<u>d</u>i wa-i<u>th</u> antum ajinnatun fee bu<u>t</u>ooni ommah<u>a</u>tikum fal<u>a</u> tuzakkoo anfusakum huwa aAAlamu bimani ittaq<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>32.  Those who avoid<br> Great sins and shameful deeds,<br>Only (falling into) small faults,<br> Verily thy Lord is ample<br> In forgiveness. He knows<br> You well when He brings<br> You out of the earth,<br> And when ye are hidden<br> In your mothers’ wombs.<br> Therefore justify not yourselves;<br> He knows best who it is<br> That guards against evil.</p> <div class="filenav">
