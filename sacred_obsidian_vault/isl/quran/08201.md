 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra LXXXII.: Infiṭār, or The Cleaving Asunder.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/082/082_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  I<u>tha</u> a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>o infa<u>t</u>arat   </p>
 <p>1.  When the Sky<br> Is cleft asunder;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/082/082_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> alkaw<u>a</u>kibu intatharat   </p>
 <p>2.  When the Stars<br> Are scattered;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/082/082_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> albi<u>ha</u>ru fujjirat   </p>
 <p>3.  When the Oceans<br> Are suffered to burst forth;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/082/082_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> alqubooru buAAthirat   </p>
 <p>4.  And when the Graves<br> Are turned upside down;—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/082/082_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  AAalimat nafsun m<u>a</u> qaddamat waakhkharat   </p>
 <p>5.  (Then) shall each soul know<br> What it hath sent forward<br> And (what it hath) kept back.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/082/082_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> al-ins<u>a</u>nu m<u>a</u> gharraka birabbika alkareem<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>6.  O man! what has<br> Seduced thee from<br> Thy Lord Most Beneficent?—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/082/082_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  Alla<u>th</u>ee khalaqaka fasaww<u>a</u>ka faAAadalak<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>7.  Him Who created thee.<br> Fashioned thee in due proportion<br> And gave thee a just bias;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/082/082_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  Fee ayyi <u>s</u>ooratin m<u>a</u> sh<u>a</u>a rakkabak<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>8.  In whatever Form He wills,<br> Does He put thee together.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/082/082_009.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>9.  Kall<u>a</u> bal tuka<u>thth</u>iboona bi<b>al</b>ddeen<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>9.  Nay! but ye do<br> Reject Right and Judgment!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/082/082_010.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>10.  Wa-inna AAalaykum la<u>ha</u>fi<i><u>th</u></i>een<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>10.  But verily over you<br> (Are appointed angels)<br> To protect you,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/082/082_011.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>11.  Kir<u>a</u>man k<u>a</u>tibeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>11.  Kind and honourable,—<br> Writing down (your deeds):</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/082/082_012.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>12.  YaAAlamoona m<u>a</u> tafAAaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>12.  They know (and understand)<br> All that ye do.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/082/082_013.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>13.  Inna al-abr<u>a</u>ra lafee naAAeem<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>13.  As for the Righteous,<br> They will be in Bliss;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/082/082_014.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>14.  Wa-inna alfujj<u>a</u>ra lafee ja<u>h</u>eem<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>14.  And the Wicked—<br> They will be in the Fire,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/082/082_015.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>15.  Ya<u>s</u>lawnah<u>a</u> yawma a<b>l</b>ddeen<b>i</b>    </p>
 <p>15.  Which they will enter<br> On the Day of Judgment,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/082/082_016.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>16.  Wam<u>a</u> hum AAanh<u>a</u> bigh<u>a</u>-ibeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>16.  And they will not be<br> Able to keep away therefrom.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/082/082_017.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>17.  Wam<u>a</u> adr<u>a</u>ka m<u>a</u> yawmu a<b>l</b>ddeeni   </p>
 <p>17.  And what will explain<br> To thee what the Day<br> Of Judgment is?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/082/082_018.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>18.  Thumma m<u>a</u> adr<u>a</u>ka m<u>a</u> yawmu a<b>l</b>ddeen<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>18.  Again, what will explain<br> To thee what the Day<br> Of Judgment is?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/082/082_019.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>19.  Yawma l<u>a</u> tamliku nafsun linafsin shay-an wa<b>a</b>l-amru yawma-i<u>th</u>in lill<u>a</u>h<b>i</b>    </p>
 <p>19.  (It will be) the Day<br> When no soul shall have<br> Power (to do) aught<br> For another<br> For the Command, that Day,<br> Will be (wholly) with God.</p>  <div class="filenav">
