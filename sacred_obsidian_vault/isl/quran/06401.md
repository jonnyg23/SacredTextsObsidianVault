 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra LXIV.: Tagābun, or Mutual Loss and Gain.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/064/064_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  Yusabbi<u>h</u>u lill<u>a</u>hi m<u>a</u> fee a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wam<u>a</u> fee al-ar<u>d</u>i lahu almulku walahu al<u>h</u>amdu wahuwa AAal<u>a</u> kulli shay-in qadeer<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>1.  Whatever is<br> In the heavens and<br> On earth, doth declare<br> The Praises and Glory<br> Of God: to Him belongs<br> Dominion, and to Him belongs<br> Praise: and He has power<br> Over all things.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/064/064_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  Huwa alla<u>th</u>ee khalaqakum faminkum k<u>a</u>firun waminkum mu/minun wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu bim<u>a</u> taAAmaloona ba<u>s</u>eer<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>2.  It is He Who has<br> Created you; and of you<br> Are some that are<br> Unbelievers, and some<br> That are Believers:<br> And God sees well<br> All that ye do.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/064/064_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  Khalaqa a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>a bi<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>aqqi wa<u>s</u>awwarakum faa<u>h</u>sana <u>s</u>uwarakum wa-ilayhi alma<u>s</u>eer<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>3.  He has created the heavens<br> And the earth<br> In just proportions,<br> And has given you shape,<br> And made your shapes<br> Beautiful: and to Him<br> Is the final Goal.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/064/064_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  YaAAlamu m<u>a</u> fee a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i wayaAAlamu m<u>a</u> tusirroona wam<u>a</u> tuAAlinoona wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu AAaleemun bi<u>tha</u>ti a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>udoor<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>4.  He knows what is<br> In the heavens<br> And on earth;<br> And He knows what<br> Ye conceal and what<br> Ye reveal: yea, God<br> Knows well the (secrets)<br> Of (all) hearts.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/064/064_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  Alam ya/tikum nabao alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo min qablu fa<u>tha</u>qoo wab<u>a</u>la amrihim walahum AAa<u>tha</u>bun aleem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>5.  Has not the story<br> Reached you, of those<br> Who rejected Faith aforetime?<br> So they tasted the evil<br> Result of their conduct;<br> And they had<br> A grievous Penalty.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/064/064_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  <u>Tha</u>lika bi-annahu k<u>a</u>nat ta/teehim rusuluhum bi<b>a</b>lbayyin<u>a</u>ti faq<u>a</u>loo abasharun yahdoonan<u>a</u> fakafaroo watawallaw wa<b>i</b>staghn<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hu wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu ghaniyyun <u>h</u>ameed<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>6.  That was because there<br> Came to them apostles<br> With Clear Signs,<br> But they said:<br> "Shall (mere) human beings<br> Direct us?" So they rejected<br> (The Message) and turned away.<br> But God can do without (them):<br> And God is<br> Free of all needs,<br> Worthy of all praise.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/064/064_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  ZaAAama alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo an lan yubAAathoo qul bal<u>a</u> warabbee latubAAathunna thumma latunabbaonna bim<u>a</u> AAamiltum wa<u>tha</u>lika AAal<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi yaseer<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>7.  The Unbelievers think<br> That they will not be<br>Raised up (for Judgment).<br> Say: "Yea, by my Lord,<br> Ye shall surely be<br> Raised up: then shall ye<br> Be told (the truth) of<br> All that ye did.<br> And that is easy for God."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/064/064_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  Fa<u>a</u>minoo bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi warasoolihi wa<b>al</b>nnoori alla<u>th</u>ee anzaln<u>a</u> wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu bim<u>a</u> taAAmaloona khabeer<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>8.  Believe, therefore, in God<br> And His Apostle, and<br> In the Light which We<br> Have sent down. And God<br> Is well acquainted<br> With all that ye do.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/064/064_009.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>9.  Yawma yajmaAAukum liyawmi aljamAAi <u>tha</u>lika yawmu a<b>l</b>ttagh<u>a</u>buni waman yu/min bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi wayaAAmal <u>sa</u>li<u>h</u>an yukaffir AAanhu sayyi-<u>a</u>tihi wayudkhilhu jann<u>a</u>tin tajree min ta<u>h</u>tih<u>a</u> al-anh<u>a</u>ru kh<u>a</u>lideena feeh<u>a</u> abadan <u>tha</u>lika alfawzu alAAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>9.  The Day that He assembles<br> You (all) for a Day<br> Of Assembly,—that will be<br> A day of mutual loss<br> And gain (among you).<br> And those who believe<br> In God and work righteousness,—<br> He will remove from them<br> Their ills, and He will admit<br> Them to gardens beneath which<br>Rivers flow, to dwell therein<br> For ever: that will be<br> The Supreme Achievement.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/064/064_010.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>10.  Wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo waka<u>thth</u>aboo bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin<u>a</u> ol<u>a</u>-ika a<u>s</u>-<u>ha</u>bu a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>ri kh<u>a</u>lideena feeh<u>a</u> wabi/sa alma<u>s</u>eer<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>10.  But those who reject Faith<br> And treat Our Signs<br> As falsehoods, they will be<br> Companions of the Fire,<br> To dwell therein for aye:<br> And evil is that Goal.</p> <div class="filenav">
