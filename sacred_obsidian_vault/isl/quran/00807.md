 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra VIII.: Anfāl, or the Spoils of War.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 7</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/008/008_049.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>49.  I<u>th</u> yaqoolu almun<u>a</u>fiqoona wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena fee quloobihim mara<u>d</u>un gharra h<u>a</u>ol<u>a</u>-i deenuhum waman yatawakkal AAal<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi fa-inna All<u>a</u>ha AAazeezun <u>h</u>akeem<b>un</b>    </p>
 <p>49.  Lo! the Hypocrites say, and those<br> In whose hearts is a disease:<br> "These people,—their religion<br> Has misled them." But<br> If any trust in God, behold!<br> God is Exalted in might, Wise.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/008/008_050.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>50.  Walaw tar<u>a</u> i<u>th</u> yatawaff<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo almal<u>a</u>-ikatu ya<u>d</u>riboona wujoohahum waadb<u>a</u>rahum wa<u>th</u>ooqoo AAa<u>tha</u>ba al<u>h</u>areeq<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>50.  If thou couldst see,<br> When the angels take the souls<br> Of the Unbelievers (at death),<br> (How) they smite their faces<br> And their backs, (saying):<br> "Taste the Penalty of the blazing Fire—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/008/008_051.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>51.  <u>Tha</u>lika bim<u>a</u> qaddamat aydeekum waanna All<u>a</u>ha laysa bi<i><u>th</u></i>all<u>a</u>min lilAAabeed<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>51.  "Because of (the deeds) which<br> Your (own) hands sent forth:<br> For God is never unjust<br> To His servants:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/008/008_052.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>52.  Kada/bi <u>a</u>li firAAawna wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena min qablihim kafaroo bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>ti All<u>a</u>hi faakha<u>th</u>ahumu All<u>a</u>hu bi<u>th</u>unoobihim inna All<u>a</u>ha qawiyyun shadeedu alAAiq<u>a</u>b<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>52.  "(Deeds) after the manner<br> Of the People of Pharaoh<br> And of those before them:<br> They rejected the Signs of God,<br> And God punished them<br> For their crimes: for God<br> Is Strong, and Strict in punishment:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/008/008_053.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>53.  <u>Tha</u>lika bi-anna All<u>a</u>ha lam yaku mughayyiran niAAmatan anAAamah<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> qawmin <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> yughayyiroo m<u>a</u> bi-anfusihim waanna All<u>a</u>ha sameeAAun AAaleem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>53.  "Because God will never change<br> The Grace which He hath bestowed<br> On a people until they change<br> What is in their (own) souls:<br> And verily God is He<br> Who heareth and knoweth (all things)."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/008/008_054.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>54.  Kada/bi <u>a</u>li firAAawna wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena min qablihim ka<u>thth</u>aboo bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>ti rabbihim faahlakn<u>a</u>hum bi<u>th</u>unoobihim waaghraqn<u>a</u> <u>a</u>la firAAawna wakullun k<u>a</u>noo <i><u>th</u></i><u>a</u>limeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>54.  "(Deeds) after the manner<br> Of the People of Pharaoh<br> And those before them":<br> They treated as false the Signs<br> Of their Lord: so We<br>Destroyed them for their crimes,<br> And We drowned the People<br> Of Pharaoh: for they were all<br> Oppressors and wrong-doers.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/008/008_055.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>55.  Inna sharra a<b>l</b>ddaw<u>a</u>bbi AAinda All<u>a</u>hi alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo fahum l<u>a</u> yu/minoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>55.  For the worst of beasts<br> In the sight of God<br> Are those who reject Him:<br> They will not believe.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/008/008_056.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>56.  Alla<u>th</u>eena AA<u>a</u>hadta minhum thumma yanqu<u>d</u>oona AAahdahum fee kulli marratin wahum l<u>a</u> yattaqoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>56.  They are those with whom<br> Thou didst make a covenant,<br> But they break their covenant<br> Every time, and they have not<br> The fear (of God).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/008/008_057.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>57.  Fa-imm<u>a</u> tathqafannahum fee al<u>h</u>arbi fasharrid bihim man khalfahum laAAallahum ya<u>thth</u>akkaroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>57.  If ye gain the mastery<br> Over them in war,<br> Disperse, with them, those<br> Who follow them,<br> That they may remember.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/008/008_058.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>58.  Wa-imm<u>a</u> takh<u>a</u>fanna min qawmin khiy<u>a</u>natan fa<b>i</b>nbi<u>th</u> ilayhim AAal<u>a</u> sawa-in inna All<u>a</u>ha l<u>a</u> yu<u>h</u>ibbu alkh<u>a</u>-ineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>58.  If thou fearest treachery<br> From any group, throw back<br> (Their Covenant) to them, (so as<br> To be) on equal terms:<br> For God loveth not the treacherous.</p> <div class="filenav">
