 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XLI.: Ḥā-mīm (Abbreviated Letters), or Ḥā-Mīm Sajda, or Fuṣṣilat</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/041/041_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  <u>Ha</u>-meem   </p>
 <p>1.  Hā-Mīm:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/041/041_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  Tanzeelun mina a<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>m<u>a</u>ni a<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>eem<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>2.  A revelation from (God).<br> Most Gracious, Most Merciful—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/041/041_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  Kit<u>a</u>bun fu<u>ss</u>ilat <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tuhu qur-<u>a</u>nan AAarabiyyan liqawmin yaAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>3.  A Book, whereof the verses<br> Are explained in detail;—<br> A Qur-ān in Arabic,<br> For people who understand;—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/041/041_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  Basheeran wana<u>th</u>eeran faaAAra<u>d</u>a aktharuhum fahum l<u>a</u> yasmaAAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>4.  Giving Good News<br> And Admonition: yet most<br> Of them turn away<br> And so they hear not.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/041/041_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  Waq<u>a</u>loo quloobun<u>a</u> fee akinnatin mimm<u>a</u> tadAAoon<u>a</u> ilayhi wafee <u>atha</u>nin<u>a</u> waqrun wamin baynin<u>a</u> wabaynika <u>h</u>ij<u>a</u>bun fa<b>i</b>AAmal innan<u>a</u> AA<u>a</u>miloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>5.  They say: "Our hearts are<br> Under veils, (concealed)<br> From that to which thou<br> Dost invite us, and<br> In our ears is a deafness,<br> And between us and thee<br> Is a screen: so do<br> Thou (what thou wilt);<br>For us, we shall do<br> (What we will!)"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/041/041_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  Qul innam<u>a</u> an<u>a</u> basharun mithlukum yoo<u>ha</u> ilayya annam<u>a</u> il<u>a</u>hukum il<u>a</u>hun w<u>ah</u>idun fa<b>i</b>staqeemoo ilayhi wa<b>i</b>staghfiroohu wawaylun lilmushrikeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>6.  Say thou: "I am<br> But a man like you:<br> It is revealed to me<br> By inspiration, that your God<br> Is One God: so stand<br> True to Him, and ask<br> For His forgiveness."<br> And woe to those who<br> Join gods with God,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/041/041_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  Alla<u>th</u>eena l<u>a</u> yu/toona a<b>l</b>zzak<u>a</u>ta wahum bi<b>a</b>l-<u>a</u>khirati hum k<u>a</u>firoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>7.  Those who practise not<br> Regular Charity, and who<br> Even deny the Hereafter.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/041/041_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  Inna alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo waAAamiloo a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>li<u>ha</u>ti lahum ajrun ghayru mamnoon<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>8.  For those who believe<br> And work deeds of righteousness<br> Is a reward that will<br> Never fail.</p> <div class="filenav">
