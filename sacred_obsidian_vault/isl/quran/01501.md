 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XV.: Al-Hijr, or The Rocky Tract.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  Alif-l<u>a</u>m-r<u>a</u> tilka <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tu alkit<u>a</u>bi waqur-<u>a</u>nin mubeen<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>1.  A. L. R. These are<br> The Āyats of Revelation,—<br> Of a Qur-ān<br> That makes things clear.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  Rubam<u>a</u> yawaddu alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo law k<u>a</u>noo muslimeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>2.  Again and again will those<br> Who disbelieve, wish that they<br> Had bowed (to God's Will)<br> In Islam.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  <u>Th</u>arhum ya/kuloo wayatamattaAAoo wayulhihimu al-amalu fasawfa yaAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>3.  Leave them alone, to enjoy<br> (The good things of this life)<br> And to please themselves:<br> Let (false) Hope amuse them: soon<br> Will knowledge (undeceive them).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  Wam<u>a</u> ahlakn<u>a</u> min qaryatin ill<u>a</u> walah<u>a</u> kit<u>a</u>bun maAAloom<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>4.  Never did We destroy<br> A population that had not<br> A term decreed and assigned<br> Beforehand.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  M<u>a</u> tasbiqu min ommatin ajalah<u>a</u> wam<u>a</u> yasta/khiroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>5.  Neither can a people anticipate<br> Its Term, nor delay it.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  Waq<u>a</u>loo y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>ee nuzzila AAalayhi a<b>l</b><u>thth</u>ikru innaka lamajnoon<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>6.  They say: "O thou to whom<br> The Message is being revealed!<br> Truly thou art mad (or possessed)!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  Law m<u>a</u> ta/teen<u>a</u> bi<b>a</b>lmal<u>a</u>-ikati in kunta mina a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>diqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>7.  "Why bringest thou not<br> Angels to us if it be<br> That thou hast the Truth?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  M<u>a</u> nunazzilu almal<u>a</u>-ikata ill<u>a</u> bi<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>aqqi wam<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo i<u>th</u>an mun<i><u>th</u></i>areen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>8.  We send not the angels<br> Down except for just cause:<br> If they came (to the ungodly),<br> Behold! no respite would they have!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_009.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>9.  Inn<u>a</u> na<u>h</u>nu nazzaln<u>a</u> a<b>l</b><u>thth</u>ikra wa-inn<u>a</u> lahu la<u>ha</u>fi<i><u>th</u></i>oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>9.  We have, without doubt,<br> Sent down the Message;<br> And We will assuredly<br> Guard it (from corruption).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_010.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>10.  Walaqad arsaln<u>a</u> min qablika fee shiyaAAi al-awwaleen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>10.  We did send apostles before thee<br> Amongst the religious sects<br> Of old:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_011.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>11.  Wam<u>a</u> ya/teehim min rasoolin ill<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo bihi yastahzi-oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>11.  But never came an apostle<br> To them but they mocked him.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_012.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>12.  Ka<u>tha</u>lika naslukuhu fee quloobi almujrimeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>12.  Even so do we let it creep<br> Into the hearts of the sinners—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_013.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>13.  L<u>a</u> yu/minoona bihi waqad khalat sunnatu al-awwaleen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>13.  That they should not believe<br> In the (Message); but the ways<br>Of the ancients have passed away.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_014.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>14.  Walaw fata<u>h</u>n<u>a</u> AAalayhim b<u>a</u>ban mina a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>-i fa<i><u>th</u></i>alloo feehi yaAArujoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>14.  Even if We opened out to them<br> A gate from heaven,<br> And they were to continue<br> (All day) ascending therein,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_015.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>15.  Laq<u>a</u>loo innam<u>a</u> sukkirat ab<u>sa</u>run<u>a</u> bal na<u>h</u>nu qawmun mas<u>h</u>ooroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>15.  They would only say:<br> "Our eyes have been intoxicated:<br> Nay, we have been bewitched<br> By sorcery."</p> <div class="filenav">
