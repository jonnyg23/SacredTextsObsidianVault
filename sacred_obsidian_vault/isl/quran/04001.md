 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XL.: Mū-min, or The Believer.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/040/040_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  <u>Ha</u>-meem   </p>
 <p>1.  Hā-Mīm.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/040/040_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  Tanzeelu alkit<u>a</u>bi mina All<u>a</u>hi alAAazeezi alAAaleem<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>2.  The revelation<br> Of this Book<br> Is from God,<br> Exalted in Power,<br> Full of Knowledge,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/040/040_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  Gh<u>a</u>firi a<b>l</b><u>thth</u>anbi waq<u>a</u>bili a<b>l</b>ttawbi shadeedi alAAiq<u>a</u>bi <u>th</u>ee a<b>l</b><u>tt</u>awli l<u>a</u> il<u>a</u>ha ill<u>a</u> huwa ilayhi alma<u>s</u>eer<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>3.  Who forgiveth Sin,<br> Accepteth Repentance,<br> Is Strict in Punishment,<br> And hath a Long Reach<br> (In all things).<br> There is no god<br> But He: to Him<br> Is the Final Goal.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/040/040_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  M<u>a</u> yuj<u>a</u>dilu fee <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>ti All<u>a</u>hi ill<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo fal<u>a</u> yaghrurka taqallubuhum fee albil<u>a</u>d<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>4.  None can dispute<br> About the Signs of God<br> But the Unbelievers.<br> Let not, then,<br> Their strutting about<br> Through the land<br> Deceive thee!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/040/040_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  Ka<u>thth</u>abat qablahum qawmu noo<u>h</u>in wa<b>a</b>l-a<u>h</u>z<u>a</u>bu min baAAdihim wahammat kullu ommatin birasoolihim liya/khu<u>th</u>oohu waj<u>a</u>daloo bi<b>a</b>lb<u>at</u>ili liyud<u>h</u>i<u>d</u>oo bihi al<u>h</u>aqqa faakha<u>th</u>tuhum fakayfa k<u>a</u>na AAiq<u>a</u>b<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>5.  But (there were people) before them,<br> Who denied (the Signs),—<br> The People of Noah,<br> And the Confederates<br> (Of Evil) after them;<br> And every People plotted<br> Against their prophet,<br> To seize him, and disputed<br> By means of vanities,<br> Therewith to condemn<br> The Truth: but it was I<br> That seized them!<br> And how (terrible)<br> Was My Requital!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/040/040_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  Waka<u>tha</u>lika <u>h</u>aqqat kalimatu rabbika AAal<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo annahum a<u>s</u>-<u>ha</u>bu a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>r<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>6.  Thus was the Decree<br> Of thy Lord proved true<br> Against the Unbelievers<br> That truly they are<br> Companions of the Fire!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/040/040_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  Alla<u>th</u>eena ya<u>h</u>miloona alAAarsha waman <u>h</u>awlahu yusabbi<u>h</u>oona bi<u>h</u>amdi rabbihim wayu/minoona bihi wayastaghfiroona lilla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo rabban<u>a</u> wasiAAta kulla shay-in ra<u>h</u>matan waAAilman fa<b>i</b>ghfir lilla<u>th</u>eena t<u>a</u>boo wa<b>i</b>ttabaAAoo sabeelaka waqihim AAa<u>tha</u>ba alja<u>h</u>eem<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>7.  Those who sustain<br> The Throne (of God)<br> And those around it<br> Sing Glory and Praise<br> To their Lord; believe<br> In Him; and implore Forgiveness<br> For those who believe:<br> "Our Lord! Thy Reach<br>Is over all things,<br> In Mercy and Knowledge.<br> Forgive, then, those who<br> Turn in Repentance, and follow<br> Thy Path; and preserve them<br> From the Penalty<br> Of the Blazing Fire!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/040/040_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  Rabban<u>a</u> waadkhilhum jann<u>a</u>ti AAadnin allatee waAAadtahum waman <u>s</u>ala<u>h</u>a min <u>a</u>b<u>a</u>-ihim waazw<u>a</u>jihim wa<u>th</u>urriyy<u>a</u>tihim innaka anta alAAazeezu al<u>h</u>akeem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>8.  "And grant, our Lord!<br> That they enter<br> The Gardens of Eternity,<br> Which Thou hast promised<br> To them, and to the righteous<br> Among their fathers,<br> Their wives, and their posterity!<br> For Thou art (He),<br> The Exalted in Might,<br> Full of Wisdom.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/040/040_009.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>9.  Waqihimu a<b>l</b>ssayyi-<u>a</u>ti waman taqi a<b>l</b>ssayyi-<u>a</u>ti yawma-i<u>th</u>in faqad ra<u>h</u>imtahu wa<u>tha</u>lika huwa alfawzu alAAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>9.  "And preserve them<br> From (all) ills;<br> And any whom Thou<br> Dost preserve from ills<br> That Day,—on them<br> Wilt Thou have bestowed<br> Mercy indeed: and that<br> Will be truly (for them)<br> The highest Achievement".</p> <div class="filenav">
