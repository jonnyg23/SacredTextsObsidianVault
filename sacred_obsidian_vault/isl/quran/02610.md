 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXVI.: Shu‘arāa, or The Poets.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 10</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_176.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>176.  Ka<u>thth</u>aba a<u>s</u>-<u>ha</u>bu al-aykati almursaleen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>176.  The Companions of the Wood<br> Rejected the apostles.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_177.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>177.  I<u>th</u> q<u>a</u>la lahum shuAAaybun al<u>a</u> tattaqoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>177.  Behold, Shu‘aib said to them:<br> Will ye not fear (God)?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_178.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>178.  Innee lakum rasoolun ameen<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>178.  "I am to you an apostle<br> Worthy of all trust.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_179.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>179.  Fa<b>i</b>ttaqoo All<u>a</u>ha waa<u>t</u>eeAAoon<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>179.  "So fear God and obey me.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_180.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>180.  Wam<u>a</u> as-alukum AAalayhi min ajrin in ajriya ill<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> rabbi alAA<u>a</u>lameen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>180.  "No reward do I ask<br> Of you for it: my, reward<br> Is only from the Lord<br> Of the Worlds.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_181.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>181.  Awfoo alkayla wal<u>a</u> takoonoo mina almukhsireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>181.  "Give just measure,''<br> And cause no loss<br> (To others by fraud).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_182.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>182.  Wazinoo bi<b>a</b>lqis<u>ta</u>si almustaqeem<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>182.  "And weigh with scales<br> True and upright.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_183.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>183.  Wal<u>a</u> tabkhasoo a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>sa ashy<u>a</u>ahum wal<u>a</u> taAAthaw fee al-ar<u>d</u>i mufsideen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>183.  "And withhold not things<br> Justly due to men,<br> Nor do evil in the land,<br> Working mischief.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_184.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>184.  Wa<b>i</b>ttaqoo alla<u>th</u>ee khalaqakum wa<b>a</b>ljibillata al-awwaleen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>184.  "And fear Him Who created<br> You and (Who created)<br> The generations before (you)"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_185.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>185.  Q<u>a</u>loo innam<u>a</u> anta mina almusa<u>hh</u>areen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>185.  Whey said: "Thou art only<br> One of those bewitched!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_186.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>186.  Wam<u>a</u> anta ill<u>a</u> basharun mithlun<u>a</u> wa-in na<i><u>th</u></i>unnuka lamina alk<u>ath</u>ibeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>186.  "Thou art no more than<br> A mortal like us,<br> And indeed we think<br> Thou art a liar!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_187.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>187.  Faasqi<u>t</u> AAalayn<u>a</u> kisafan mina a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>-i in kunta mina a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>diqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>187.  "Now cause a piece<br> Of the sky to fall on us,<br> If thou art truthful!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_188.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>188.  Q<u>a</u>la rabbee aAAlamu bim<u>a</u> taAAmaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>188.  He said: "My Lord<br> Knows best what ye do."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_189.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>189.  Faka<u>thth</u>aboohu faakha<u>th</u>ahum AAa<u>tha</u>bu yawmi a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i>ullati innahu k<u>a</u>na AAa<u>tha</u>ba yawmin AAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>189.  But they rejected him.<br> Then the punishment<br> Of a day of overshadowing gloom<br> Seized them, and that was<br> The Penalty of a Great Day.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_190.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>190.  Inna fee <u>tha</u>lika la<u>a</u>yatan wam<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>na aktharuhum mu/mineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>190.  Verily in that is a Sign:<br> But most of them<br> Do not believe.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_191.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>191.  Wa-inna rabbaka lahuwa alAAazeezu a<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>eem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>191.  And verily thy Lord<br> Is He, the Exalted in Might,<br> Most Merciful.</p> <div class="filenav">
