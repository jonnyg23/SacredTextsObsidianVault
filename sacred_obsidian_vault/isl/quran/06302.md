 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/063/063_009.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>9.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo l<u>a</u> tulhikum amw<u>a</u>lukum wal<u>a</u> awl<u>a</u>dukum AAan <u>th</u>ikri All<u>a</u>hi waman yafAAal <u>tha</u>lika faol<u>a</u>-ika humu alkh<u>a</u>siroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>9.  O ye who believe!<br> Let not your riches<br> Or your children divert you<br> From the remembrance of God.<br> If any act thus,<br> The loss is their own.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/063/063_010.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>10.  Waanfiqoo min m<u>a</u> razaqn<u>a</u>kum min qabli an ya/tiya a<u>h</u>adakumu almawtu fayaqoola rabbi lawl<u>a</u> akhkhartanee il<u>a</u> ajalin qareebin faa<u>ss</u>addaqa waakun mina a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>li<u>h</u>een<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>10.  And spend something (in charity)<br> Out of the substance<br> Which We have bestowed<br> On you, before Death<br> Should come to any of you<br> And he should say,<br> "O my Lord! Why didst<br> Thou not give me<br> Respite for a little while?<br> I should then have given<br>(Largely) in charity, and I<br> Should have been one<br> Of the doers of good".</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/063/063_011.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>11.  Walan yu-akhkhira All<u>a</u>hu nafsan i<u>tha</u> j<u>a</u>a ajaluh<u>a</u> wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu khabeerun bim<u>a</u> taAAmaloon<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>11.  But to no soul<br> Will God grant respite<br> When the time appointed<br> (For it) has come; and God<br> Is well acquainted<br> With (all) that ye do.</p>  <div class="filenav">
