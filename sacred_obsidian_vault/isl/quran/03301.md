 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXXIII.: Aḥzāb, or The Confederates.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/033/033_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nnabiyyu ittaqi All<u>a</u>ha wal<u>a</u> tu<u>t</u>iAAi alk<u>a</u>fireena wa<b>a</b>lmun<u>a</u>fiqeena inna All<u>a</u>ha k<u>a</u>na AAaleeman <u>h</u>akeem<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>1.  O Prophet! Fear God,<br> And hearken not<br> To the Unbelievers<br> And the Hypocrites:<br> Verily god is full<br> Of knowledge and wisdom.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/033/033_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  Wa<b>i</b>ttabiAA m<u>a</u> yoo<u>ha</u> ilayka min rabbika inna All<u>a</u>ha k<u>a</u>na bim<u>a</u> taAAmaloona khabeer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>2.  But follow that which<br> Comes to thee by inspiration<br> From thy Lord: for God<br> Is well acquainted<br> With (all) that ye do.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/033/033_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  Watawakkal AAal<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi wakaf<u>a</u> bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi wakeel<u>a</u><b>n </b>   </p>
 <p>3.  And put thy trust<br> In God, and enough is God<br> As a Disposer of affairs.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/033/033_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  M<u>a</u> jaAAala All<u>a</u>hu lirajulin min qalbayni fee jawfihi wam<u>a</u> jaAAala azw<u>a</u>jakumu all<u>a</u>-ee tu<i><u>th</u></i><u>a</u>hiroona minhunna ommah<u>a</u>tikum wam<u>a</u> jaAAala adAAiy<u>a</u>akum abn<u>a</u>akum <u>tha</u>likum qawlukum bi-afw<u>a</u>hikum wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu yaqoolu al<u>h</u>aqqa wahuwa yahdee a<b>l</b>ssabeel<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>4.  God has not made<br> For any man two hearts<br>In his (one) body: nor has<br> He made your wives whom<br> Ye divorce by Ẓihār<br> Your mothers: nor has He<br> Made your adopted sons<br> Your sons. Such is (only)<br> Your (manner of) speech<br> By your mouths. but God<br> Tells (you) the Truth, and He<br> Shows the (right) Way.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/033/033_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  OdAAoohum li-<u>a</u>b<u>a</u>-ihim huwa aqsa<u>t</u>u AAinda All<u>a</u>hi fa-in lam taAAlamoo <u>a</u>b<u>a</u>ahum fa-ikhw<u>a</u>nukum fee a<b>l</b>ddeeni wamaw<u>a</u>leekum walaysa AAalaykum jun<u>ah</u>un feem<u>a</u> akh<u>t</u>a/tum bihi wal<u>a</u>kin m<u>a</u> taAAammadat quloobukum wak<u>a</u>na All<u>a</u>hu ghafooran ra<u>h</u>eem<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>5.  Call them by (the names<br> Of) their fathers: that is<br> Juster in the sight of God.<br> But if ye know not<br> Their father's (names, call<br> Them) your Brothers in faith,<br> Or your Maulās.<br> But there is no blame<br> On you if ye make<br> A mistake therein:<br> (What counts is)<br> The intention of your hearts:<br> And god is Oft-Returning,<br> Most Merciful.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/033/033_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  A<b>l</b>nnabiyyu awl<u>a</u> bi<b>a</b>lmu/mineena min anfusihim waazw<u>a</u>juhu ommah<u>a</u>tuhum waoloo al-ar<u>ha</u>mi baAA<u>d</u>uhum awl<u>a</u> bibaAA<u>d</u>in fee kit<u>a</u>bi All<u>a</u>hi mina almu/mineena wa<b>a</b>lmuh<u>a</u>jireena ill<u>a</u> an tafAAaloo il<u>a</u> awliy<u>a</u>-ikum maAAroofan k<u>a</u>na <u>tha</u>lika fee alkit<u>a</u>bi mas<u>t</u>oor<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>6.  The Prophet is closer<br> To the Believers than<br> Their own selves,<br> And his wives are<br> Their mothers. Blood-relations<br> Among each other have<br> Closer personal ties,<br> In the Decree of God.<br> Than (the Brotherhood of)<br> Believers and Muhājirs:<br> Nevertheless do ye<br> What is just to your<br> Closest friends: such is<br> The writing in the Decree<br> (Of God).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/033/033_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  Wa-i<u>th</u> akha<u>th</u>n<u>a</u> mina a<b>l</b>nnabiyyeena meeth<u>a</u>qahum waminka wamin noo<u>h</u>in wa-ibr<u>a</u>heema wamoos<u>a</u> waAAees<u>a</u> ibni maryama waakha<u>th</u>n<u>a</u> minhum meeth<u>a</u>qan ghalee<i><u>th</u></i><u>a</u><b>n</b>    </p>
 <p>7.  And remember We took<br> From the Prophets their<br> Covenant:<br> As (We did) from thee:<br> From Noah, Abraham, Moses,<br> And Jesus the son of Mary:<br> We took from them<br> A solemn Covenant:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/033/033_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  Liyas-ala a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>diqeena AAan <u>s</u>idqihim waaAAadda lilk<u>a</u>fireena AAa<u>tha</u>ban aleem<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>8.  That (God) may question<br> The (Custodians) of Truth concerning<br> The Truth they (were charged with):<br> And He has prepared<br> For the Unbelievers<br> A grievous Penalty.</p> <div class="filenav">
