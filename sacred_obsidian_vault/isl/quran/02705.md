 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXVII.: Naml, or the Ants.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 5</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_059.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>59.  Quli al<u>h</u>amdu lill<u>a</u>hi wasal<u>a</u>mun AAal<u>a</u> AAib<u>a</u>dihi alla<u>th</u>eena i<u>st</u>af<u>a</u> <u>a</u>ll<u>a</u>hu khayrun amm<u>a</u> yushrikoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>59.  Say: Praise be to God,<br> And Peace on His servants<br> Whom He has chosen<br> (For His Message). (Who)<br> Is better?—God or<br> The false gods they associate<br> (With Him)?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_060.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>60.  Amman khalaqa a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>a waanzala lakum mina a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>-i m<u>a</u>an faanbatn<u>a</u> bihi <u>h</u>ad<u>a</u>-iqa <u>tha</u>ta bahjatin m<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>na lakum an tunbitoo shajarah<u>a</u> a-il<u>a</u>hun maAAa All<u>a</u>hi bal hum qawmun yaAAdiloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>60.  Or, who has created<br> The heavens and the earth,<br> And who sends you down<br> Rain from the sky?<br> Yea, with it We cause<br> To grow well-planted orchards<br> Full of beauty and delight:<br> It is not in your power<br> To cause the growth<br> Of the trees in them. (Can there be<br> Another) god besides God?<br> Nay, they are a people<br> Who swerve from justice.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_061.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>61.  Amman jaAAala al-ar<u>d</u>a qar<u>a</u>ran wajaAAala khil<u>a</u>lah<u>a</u> anh<u>a</u>ran wajaAAala lah<u>a</u> raw<u>a</u>siya wajaAAala bayna alba<u>h</u>rayni <u>ha</u>jizan a-il<u>a</u>hun maAAa All<u>a</u>hi bal aktharuhum l<u>a</u> yaAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>61.  Or, who has made the earth<br> Firm to live in; made<br> Rivers in its midst; set<br> Thereon mountains immovable;<br> And made a separating bar<br> Between the two bodies<br> Of flowing water?<br> (Can there be another) god<br> Besides God? Nay, most<br> Of them know not.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_062.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>62.  Amman yujeebu almu<u>dt</u>arra i<u>tha</u> daAA<u>a</u>hu wayakshifu a<b>l</b>ssoo-a wayajAAalukum khulaf<u>a</u>a al-ar<u>d</u>i a-il<u>a</u>hun maAAa All<u>a</u>hi qaleelan m<u>a</u> ta<u>th</u>akkaroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>62.  Or, who listens to the (soul)<br> Distressed when it calls<br> On Him, and who relieves<br> Its suffering, and makes you<br>(Mankind) inheritors of the earth?'<br> (Can there be another) god<br> Besides God? Little it is<br> That ye heed!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_063.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>63.  Amman yahdeekum fee <i><u>th</u></i>ulum<u>a</u>ti albarri wa<b>a</b>lba<u>h</u>ri waman yursilu a<b>l</b>rriy<u>ah</u>a bushran bayna yaday ra<u>h</u>matihi a-il<u>a</u>hun maAAa All<u>a</u>hi taAA<u>a</u>l<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hu AAamm<u>a</u> yushrikoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>63.  Or, who guides you<br> Through the depths of darkness<br> On land and sea, and who<br> Sends the winds as heralds<br> Of glad tidings, going before<br> His Mercy? (Can there be<br> Another) god besides God?—<br> High is God above what<br> They associate with Him!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_064.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>64.  Amman yabdao alkhalqa thumma yuAAeeduhu waman yarzuqukum mina a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>-i wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i a-il<u>a</u>hun maAAa All<u>a</u>hi qul h<u>a</u>too burh<u>a</u>nakum in kuntum <u>sa</u>diqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>64.  Or, who originates Creation,<br> Then repeats it,<br> And who gives you sustenance<br> From heaven and earth?<br> (Can there be another) god<br> Besides God? Say, "Bring forth<br> Your argument, if ye<br> Are telling the truth!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_065.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>65.  Qul l<u>a</u> yaAAlamu man fee a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i alghayba ill<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hu wam<u>a</u> yashAAuroona ayy<u>a</u>na yubAAathoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>65.  Say: None in the heavens<br> Or on earth, except God,<br> Knows what is hidden:<br> Nor can they perceive<br> When they shall be raised<br> Up (for Judgment).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_066.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>66.  Bali idd<u>a</u>raka AAilmuhum fee al-<u>a</u>khirati bal hum fee shakkin minh<u>a</u> bal hum minh<u>a</u> AAamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>66.  Still less can their knowledge<br> Comprehend the Hereafter: nay,<br>They are in doubt and uncertainty<br> Thereanent; nay, they are blind<br> Thereunto!</p> <div class="filenav">
