 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra VII.: A’rāf, or the Heights</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 13</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_100.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>100.  Awa lam yahdi lilla<u>th</u>eena yarithoona al-ar<u>d</u>a min baAAdi ahlih<u>a</u> an law nash<u>a</u>o a<u>s</u>abn<u>a</u>hum bi<u>th</u>unoobihim wana<u>t</u>baAAu AAal<u>a</u> quloobihim fahum l<u>a</u> yasmaAAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>100.  To those who inherit<br> The earth in succession<br> To its (previous) possessors,<br> Is it not a guiding (lesson)<br> That, if We so willed,<br> We could punish them (too)<br> For their sins, and seal up<br> Their hearts so that they<br> Could not hear?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_101.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>101.  Tilka alqur<u>a</u> naqu<u>ss</u>u AAalayka min anb<u>a</u>-ih<u>a</u> walaqad j<u>a</u>at-hum rusuluhum bi<b>a</b>lbayyin<u>a</u>ti fam<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo liyu/minoo bim<u>a</u> ka<u>thth</u>aboo min qablu ka<u>tha</u>lika ya<u>t</u>baAAu All<u>a</u>hu AAal<u>a</u> quloobi alk<u>a</u>fireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>101.  Such were the towns<br> Whose story We (thus)<br> Relate unto thee:<br> There came indeed to them<br> Their apostles with clear (Signs):<br> But they would not believe<br> What they had rejected before.<br> Thus doth God seal up<br> The hearts of those<br> Who reject Faith.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_102.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>102.  Wam<u>a</u> wajadn<u>a</u> li-aktharihim min AAahdin wa-in wajadn<u>a</u> aktharahum laf<u>a</u>siqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>102.  Most of them We found not<br> Men (true) to their covenant:<br> But most of them We found<br> Rebellious and disobedient.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_103.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>103.  Thumma baAAathn<u>a</u> min baAAdihim moos<u>a</u> bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin<u>a</u> il<u>a</u> firAAawna wamala-ihi fa<i><u>th</u></i>alamoo bih<u>a</u> fa<b>o</b>nu<i><u>th</u></i>ur kayfa k<u>a</u>na AA<u>a</u>qibatu almufsideen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>103.  Then after them We sent<br> Moses with Our Signs<br> To Pharaoh and his chiefs,<br> But they wrongfully rejected them:<br> So see what was the end<br> Of those who made mischief.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_104.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>104.  Waq<u>a</u>la moos<u>a</u> y<u>a</u> firAAawnu innee rasoolun min rabbi alAA<u>a</u>lameen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>104.  Moses said: "O Pharaoh!<br> I am an apostle from<br> The Lord of the Worlds,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_105.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>105.  <u>H</u>aqeequn AAal<u>a</u> an l<u>a</u> aqoola AAal<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi ill<u>a</u> al<u>h</u>aqqa qad ji/tukum bibayyinatin min rabbikum faarsil maAAiya banee isr<u>a</u>-eel<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>105.  One for whom it is right<br> To say nothing but truth<br> About God. Now have I<br> Come unto you (people), from<br> Your Lord, with a clear (Sign):<br> So let the Children of Israel<br> Depart along with me."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_106.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>106.  Q<u>a</u>la in kunta ji/ta bi-<u>a</u>yatin fa/ti bih<u>a</u> in kunta mina a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>diqeen<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>106.  (Pharaoh) said: "If indeed<br> Thou hast come with a Sign,<br>Show it forth,<br> If thou tellest the truth."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_107.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>107.  Faalq<u>a</u> AAa<u>sa</u>hu fa-i<u>tha</u> hiya thuAAb<u>a</u>nun mubeen<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>107.  Then (Moses) threw his rod,<br> And behold! it was<br> A serpent, plain (for all to see)!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_108.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>108.  WanazaAAa yadahu fa-i<u>tha</u> hiya bay<u>da</u>o li<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u><i><u>th</u></i>ireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>108.  And he drew out his hand,<br> And behold! it was white<br> To all beholders!</p> <div class="filenav">
