 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XVIII.: Kahf, or the Cave.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 2</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_013.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>13.  Na<u>h</u>nu naqu<u>ss</u>u AAalayka nabaahum bi<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>aqqi innahum fityatun <u>a</u>manoo birabbihim wazidn<u>a</u>hum hud<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>13.  We relate to thee their story<br> In truth: they were youths<br> Who believed in their Lord,<br> And We advanced them In guidance:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_014.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>14.  Waraba<u>t</u>n<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> quloobihim i<u>th</u> q<u>a</u>moo faq<u>a</u>loo rabbun<u>a</u> rabbu a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i lan nadAAuwa min doonihi il<u>a</u>han laqad quln<u>a</u> i<u>th</u>an sha<u>t</u>a<u>ta</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>14.  We gave strength to their hearts:<br>Behold, they stood up<br> And said: "Our Lord is<br> The Lord of the heavens<br> And of the earth: never<br> Shall we call upon any god<br> Other than Him: if we<br> Did, we should indeed<br> Have uttered an enormity!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_015.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>15.  H<u>a</u>ol<u>a</u>-i qawmun<u>a</u> ittakha<u>th</u>oo min doonihi <u>a</u>lihatan lawl<u>a</u> ya/toona AAalayhim bisul<u>ta</u>nin bayyinin faman a<i><u>th</u></i>lamu mimmani iftar<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi ka<u>th</u>ib<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>15.  "These our people have taken<br> For worship gods other<br> Than Him: why do they<br> Not bring forward an authority<br> Clear (and convincing)<br> For what they do?<br> Who doth more wrong<br> Than such as invent<br> A falsehood against God?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_016.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>16.  Wa-i<u>th</u>i iAAtazaltumoohum wam<u>a</u> yaAAbudoona ill<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>ha fa/woo il<u>a</u> alkahfi yanshur lakum rabbukum min ra<u>h</u>matihi wayuhayyi/ lakum min amrikum mirfaq<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>16.  "When ye turn away<br> From them and the things<br> They worship other than God,<br> Betake yourselves to the Cave:<br> Your Lord will shower<br> His mercies on you<br> And dispose of your affair<br> Towards comfort and ease."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_017.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>17.  Watar<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>shshamsa i<u>tha</u> <u>t</u>alaAAat taz<u>a</u>waru AAan kahfihim <u>tha</u>ta alyameeni wa-i<u>tha</u> gharabat taqri<u>d</u>uhum <u>tha</u>ta a<b>l</b>shshim<u>a</u>li wahum fee fajwatin minhu <u>tha</u>lika min <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>ti All<u>a</u>hi man yahdi All<u>a</u>hu fahuwa almuhtadi waman yu<u>d</u>lil falan tajida lahu waliyyan murshid<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>17.  Thou wouldst have seen<br> The sun, when it rose,<br> Declining to the right<br> From their Cave, and when<br>It set, turning away<br> From them to the left,<br> While they lay in the open<br> Space in the midst<br> Of the Cave. Such are<br> Among the Signs of God:<br> He whom God guides<br> Is rightly guided; but he<br> Whom God leaves to stray,—<br> For him wilt thou find<br> No protector to lead him<br> To the Right Way.</p> <div class="filenav">
