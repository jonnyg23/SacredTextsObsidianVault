 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXII.: Ḥajj, or The Pilgrimage.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 4</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/022/022_026.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>26.  Wa-i<u>th</u> bawwa/n<u>a</u> li-ibr<u>a</u>heema mak<u>a</u>na albayti an l<u>a</u> tushrik bee shay-an wa<u>t</u>ahhir baytiya li<b>l</b><u>tta</u>-ifeena wa<b>a</b>lq<u>a</u>-imeena wa<b>al</b>rrukkaAAi a<b>l</b>ssujood<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>26.  Behold! We gave the site,<br> To Abraham, of the (Sacred) House,<br> (Saying): "Associate not anything<br> (In worship) with Me;<br> And sanctify My House<br> For those who compass it round,<br> Or stand up,<br> Or bow, or prostrate themselves<br> (Therein in prayer).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/022/022_027.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>27.  Waa<u>thth</u>in fee a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si bi<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>ajji ya/tooka rij<u>a</u>lan waAAal<u>a</u> kulli <u>da</u>mirin ya/teena min kulli fajjin AAameeq<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>27.  "And proclaim the Pilgrimage<br> Among men: they will come<br> To thee on foot and (mounted)<br> On every kind of camel,<br> Lean on accout of journeys<br> Through deep and distant<br> Mountain highways;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/022/022_028.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>28.  Liyashhadoo man<u>a</u>fiAAa lahum waya<u>th</u>kuroo isma All<u>a</u>hi fee ayy<u>a</u>min maAAloom<u>a</u>tin AAal<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> razaqahum min baheemati al-anAA<u>a</u>mi fakuloo minh<u>a</u> waa<u>t</u>AAimoo alb<u>a</u>-isa alfaqeer<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>28.  "That they may witness<br> The benefits (provided) for them,<br>And celebrate the name<br> Of God, through the Days<br> Appointed, over the cattle<br> Which He has provided for them<br> (For sacrifice): then eat ye<br> Thereof and feed the distressed<br> Ones in want.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/022/022_029.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>29.  Thumma lyaq<u>d</u>oo tafathahum walyoofoo nu<u>th</u>oorahum walya<u>tt</u>awwafoo bi<b>a</b>lbayti alAAateeq<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>29.  "Then let them complete<br> The rites prescribed<br> For them, perform their vows,<br> And (again) circumambulate<br> The Ancient House."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/022/022_030.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>30.  <u>Tha</u>lika waman yuAAa<i><u>thth</u></i>im <u>h</u>urum<u>a</u>ti All<u>a</u>hi fahuwa khayrun lahu AAinda rabbihi wao<u>h</u>illat lakumu al-anAA<u>a</u>mu ill<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> yutl<u>a</u> AAalaykum fa<b>i</b>jtaniboo a<b>l</b>rrijsa mina al-awth<u>a</u>ni wa<b>i</b>jtaniboo qawla a<b>l</b>zzoor<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>30.  Such (is the Pilgrimage):<br> Whoever honours the sacred<br> Rites of God, for him<br> It is good in the sight<br> Of his Lord. Lawful to you<br> (For food in Pilgrimage) are cattle,<br> Except those mentioned to you<br> (As exceptions): but shun<br> The abomination of idols,<br> And shun the word<br> That is false,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/022/022_031.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>31.  <u>H</u>unaf<u>a</u>a lill<u>a</u>hi ghayra mushrikeena bihi waman yushrik bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi fakaannam<u>a</u> kharra mina a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>-i fatakh<u>t</u>afuhu a<b>l</b><u>tt</u>ayru aw tahwee bihi a<b>l</b>rree<u>h</u>u fee mak<u>a</u>nin sa<u>h</u>eeq<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>31.  Being true in faith to God,<br> And never assigning partners<br>To Him: if anyone assigns<br> Partners to God, he is<br> As if he had fallen<br> From heaven and been snatched up<br> By birds, or the wind<br> Had swooped (like a bird<br> On its prey) and thrown him<br> Into a far-distant place.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/022/022_032.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>32.  <u>Tha</u>lika waman yuAAa<i><u>thth</u></i>im shaAA<u>a</u>-ira All<u>a</u>hi fa-innah<u>a</u> min taqw<u>a</u> alquloob<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>32.  Such (is his state): and<br> Whoever holds in honour<br> The Symbols of God,<br> (In the sacrifice of animals),<br> Such (honour) should come truly<br> From piety of heart.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/022/022_033.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>33.  Lakum feeh<u>a</u> man<u>a</u>fiAAu il<u>a</u> ajalin musamman thumma ma<u>h</u>illuh<u>a</u> il<u>a</u> albayti alAAateeq<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>33.  In them ye have benefits<br> For a term appointed:<br> In the end their place<br> Of sacrifice is near<br> The Ancient House.</p> <div class="filenav">
