 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XLIX.: Ḥujurāt, or the Inner Apartments.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/049/049_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo l<u>a</u> tuqaddimoo bayna yadayi All<u>a</u>hi warasoolihi wa<b>i</b>ttaqoo All<u>a</u>ha inna All<u>a</u>ha sameeAAun AAaleem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>1.  O ye who believe!<br> Put not yourselves forward<br> Before God and His Apostle;<br> But fear God: for God<br> Is He Who hears<br> And knows all things.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/049/049_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo l<u>a</u> tarfaAAoo a<u>s</u>w<u>a</u>takum fawqa <u>s</u>awti a<b>l</b>nnabiyyi wal<u>a</u> tajharoo lahu bi<b>a</b>lqawli kajahri baAA<u>d</u>ikum libaAA<u>d</u>in an ta<u>h</u>ba<u>t</u>a aAAm<u>a</u>lukum waantum l<u>a</u> tashAAuroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>2.  O ye who believe!<br> Raise not your voices!<br> Above the voice of the Prophet,<br> Nor speak aloud to him<br> In talk, as ye may<br> Speak aloud to one another,<br> Lest your deeds become!<br> Vain and ye perceive not.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/049/049_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  Inna alla<u>th</u>eena yaghu<u>dd</u>oona a<u>s</u>w<u>a</u>tahum AAinda rasooli All<u>a</u>hi ol<u>a</u>-ika alla<u>th</u>eena imta<u>h</u>ana All<u>a</u>hu quloobahum li<b>l</b>ttaqw<u>a</u> lahum maghfiratun waajrun AAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>3.  Those that lower their voice<br> In the presence of<br> God's Apostle,—their hearts<br> Has God tested for piety:<br> For them is Forgiveness<br> And a great Reward.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/049/049_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  Inna alla<u>th</u>eena yun<u>a</u>doonaka min war<u>a</u>-i al<u>h</u>ujur<u>a</u>ti aktharuhum l<u>a</u> yaAAqiloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>4.  Those who shout out<br> To thee from without<br>The Inner Apartments—<br> Most of them lack understanding.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/049/049_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  Walaw annahum <u>s</u>abaroo <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> takhruja ilayhim lak<u>a</u>na khayran lahum wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu ghafoorun ra<u>h</u>eem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>5.  If only they had patience<br> Until thou couldst<br> Come out to them,<br> It would be best<br> For them: but God is<br> Oft-Forgiving, Most Merciful.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/049/049_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo in j<u>a</u>akum f<u>a</u>siqun binaba-in fatabayyanoo an tu<u>s</u>eeboo qawman bijah<u>a</u>latin fatu<u>s</u>bi<u>h</u>oo AAal<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> faAAaltum n<u>a</u>dimeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>6.  O ye who believe!<br> If a wicked person comes<br> To you with any news,<br> Ascertain the truth, lest<br> Ye harm people unwittingly,<br> And afterwards become<br> Full of repentance for<br> What ye have done.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/049/049_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  Wa<b>i</b>AAlamoo anna feekum rasoola All<u>a</u>hi law yu<u>t</u>eeAAukum fee katheerin mina al-amri laAAanittum wal<u>a</u>kinna All<u>a</u>ha <u>h</u>abbaba ilaykumu al-eem<u>a</u>na wazayyanahu fee quloobikum wakarraha ilaykumu alkufra wa<b>a</b>lfusooqa wa<b>a</b>lAAi<u>s</u>y<u>a</u>na ol<u>a</u>-ika humu a<b>l</b>rr<u>a</u>shidoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>7.  And know that among you<br> Is God's Apostle: were he,<br> In many matters, to follow<br> Your (wishes), ye would<br> Certainly fall into misfortune:<br> But God has endeared<br> The Faith to you, and<br> Has made it beautiful<br> In your hearts, and He<br> Has made hateful to you<br> Unbelief, wickedness, and<br> Rebellion: such indeed are<br> Those who walk in righteousness;—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/049/049_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  Fa<u>d</u>lan mina All<u>a</u>hi waniAAmatan wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu AAaleemun <u>h</u>akeem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>8.  A grace and favour<br> From God; and God<br> Is full of Knowledge<br> And Wisdom.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/049/049_009.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>9.  Wa-in <u>ta</u>-ifat<u>a</u>ni mina almu/mineena iqtataloo faa<u>s</u>li<u>h</u>oo baynahum<u>a</u> fa-in baghat i<u>h</u>d<u>a</u>hum<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> al-okhr<u>a</u> faq<u>a</u>tiloo allatee tabghee <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> tafee-a il<u>a</u> amri All<u>a</u>hi fa-in f<u>a</u>at faa<u>s</u>li<u>h</u>oo baynahum<u>a</u> bi<b>a</b>lAAadli waaqsi<u>t</u>oo inna All<u>a</u>ha yu<u>h</u>ibbu almuqsi<u>t</u>een<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>9.  If two parties among<br> The Believers fall into<br> A quarrel, make ye peace<br> Between them: but if<br> One of them transgresses<br> Beyond bounds against the other,<br> Then fight ye (all) against<br> The one that transgresses<br> Until it complies with<br> The command of God;<br> But if it complies, then<br> Make peace between them<br> With justice, and be fair:<br> For God loves those<br> Who are fair (and just).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/049/049_010.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>10.  Innam<u>a</u> almu/minoona ikhwatun faa<u>s</u>li<u>h</u>oo bayna akhawaykum wa<b>i</b>ttaqoo All<u>a</u>ha laAAallakum tur<u>h</u>amoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>10.  The Believers are but<br> A single Brotherhood:<br> So make peace and<br> Reconciliation between your<br> Two (contending) brothers;<br> And fear God, that ye<br> May receive Mercy.</p> <div class="filenav">
