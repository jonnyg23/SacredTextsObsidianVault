 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra LVII.: Ḥadīd, or Iron.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/057/057_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  Sabba<u>h</u>a lill<u>a</u>hi m<u>a</u> fee a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i wahuwa alAAazeezu al<u>h</u>akeem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>1.  Whatever is in<br> The heavens and on earth,—<br> Let it declare<br> The Praises and Glory of God:<br> For He is the Exalted<br> In Might, the Wise.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/057/057_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  Lahu mulku a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i yu<u>h</u>yee wayumeetu wahuwa AAal<u>a</u> kulli shay-in qadeer<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>2.  To Him belongs the dominion<br> Of the heavens and the earth:<br> It is He Who gives<br> Life and Death; and He<br> Has Power over all things.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/057/057_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  Huwa al-awwalu wa<b>a</b>l-<u>a</u>khiru wa<b>al</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>hiru wa<b>a</b>lb<u>at</u>inu wahuwa bikulli shay-in AAaleem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>3.  He is the First<br> And the Last,<br> The Evident<br> And the Immanent:<br> And He has full knowledge<br> Of all things.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/057/057_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  Huwa alla<u>th</u>ee khalaqa a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>a fee sittati ayy<u>a</u>min thumma istaw<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> alAAarshi yaAAlamu m<u>a</u> yaliju fee al-ar<u>d</u>i wam<u>a</u> yakhruju minh<u>a</u> wam<u>a</u> yanzilu mina a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>-i wam<u>a</u> yaAAruju feeh<u>a</u> wahuwa maAAakum ayna m<u>a</u> kuntum wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu bim<u>a</u> taAAmaloona ba<u>s</u>eer<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>4.  He it is Who created<br> The heavens and the earth<br> In six Days, and is moreover<br> Firmly established on the Throne<br> (Of authority). He knows<br> What enters within the earth<br>And what comes forth out<br> Of it, what comes down<br> From heaven and what mounts<br> Up to it. And He is<br> With you wheresoever ye<br> May be. And God sees<br> Well all that ye do.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/057/057_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  Lahu mulku a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i wa-il<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi turjaAAu al-omoor<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>5.  To Him belongs the dominion<br> Of the heavens and the earth:<br> And all affairs are<br> Referred back to God.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/057/057_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  Yooliju allayla fee a<b>l</b>nnah<u>a</u>ri wayooliju a<b>l</b>nnah<u>a</u>ra fee allayi wahuwa AAaleemun bi<u>tha</u>ti a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>udoor<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>6.  He merges Night into Day,<br> And He merges Day into Night;<br> And He has full knowledge<br> Of the secrets of (all) hearts.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/057/057_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  <u>A</u>minoo bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi warasoolihi waanfiqoo mimm<u>a</u> jaAAalakum mustakhlafeena feehi fa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo minkum waanfaqoo lahum ajrun kabeer<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>7.  Believe in God<br> And His Apostle,<br> And spend (in charity)<br> Out of the (substance)<br> Whereof He has made you<br> Heirs. For, those of you<br> Who believe and spend<br> (In charity),—for them<br> Is a great Reward.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/057/057_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  Wam<u>a</u> lakum l<u>a</u> tu/minoona bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi wa<b>al</b>rrasoolu yadAAookum litu/minoo birabbikum waqad akha<u>th</u>a meeth<u>a</u>qakum in kuntum mu/mineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>8.  What cause have ye<br> Why ye should not believe<br> In God?—And the Apostle<br> Invites you to believe<br>In your Lord, and has<br> Indeed taken your Covenant,<br> If ye are men of faith.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/057/057_009.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>9.  Huwa alla<u>th</u>ee yunazzilu AAal<u>a</u> AAabdihi <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin bayyin<u>a</u>tin liyukhrijakum mina a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i>ulum<u>a</u>ti il<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nnoori wa-inna All<u>a</u>ha bikum laraoofun ra<u>h</u>eem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>9.  He is the One Who<br> Sends to His Servant<br> Manifest Signs, that He<br> May lead you from<br> The depths of Darkness<br> Into the Light And verily,<br> God is to you<br> Most kind and Merciful.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/057/057_010.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>10.  Wam<u>a</u> lakum all<u>a</u> tunfiqoo fee sabeeli All<u>a</u>hi walill<u>a</u>hi meer<u>a</u>thu a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i l<u>a</u> yastawee minkum man anfaqa min qabli alfat<u>h</u>i waq<u>a</u>tala ol<u>a</u>-ika aAA<i><u>th</u></i>amu darajatan mina alla<u>th</u>eena anfaqoo min baAAdu waq<u>a</u>taloo wakullan waAAada All<u>a</u>hu al<u>h</u>usn<u>a</u> wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu bim<u>a</u> taAAmaloona khabeer<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>10.  And what cause have ye<br> Why ye should not spend<br> In the cause of God?—<br> For to God belongs<br> The heritage of the heavens<br> And the earth.<br> Not equal among you<br> Are those who spent (freely)<br> And fought, before the Victory,<br> (With those who did so later).<br> Those are higher in rank<br> Than those who spent (freely)<br> And fought afterwards.<br> But to all has God promised<br> A goodly (reward). And God<br> Is well acquainted<br> With all that ye do.</p> <div class="filenav">
