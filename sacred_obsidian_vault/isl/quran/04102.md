 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XLI.: Ḥā-mīm (Abbreviated Letters), or Ḥā-Mīm Sajda, or Fuṣṣilat</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 2</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/041/041_009.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>9.  Qul a-innakum latakfuroona bi<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>ee khalaqa al-ar<u>d</u>a fee yawmayni watajAAaloona lahu and<u>a</u>dan <u>tha</u>lika rabbu alAA<u>a</u>lameen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>9.  Say: Is it that ye<br> Deny Him Who created<br> The earth in two Days?<br> And do ye join equals<br> With Him? He is<br> The Lord of (all)<br> The Worlds.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/041/041_010.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>10.  WajaAAala feeh<u>a</u> raw<u>a</u>siya min fawqih<u>a</u> wab<u>a</u>raka feeh<u>a</u> waqaddara feeh<u>a</u> aqw<u>a</u>tah<u>a</u> fee arbaAAati ayy<u>a</u>min saw<u>a</u>an li<b>l</b>ss<u>a</u>-ileen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>10.  He set on the (earth),<br> Mountains standing firm,<br> High above it,<br> And bestowed blessings on<br> The earth, and measured therein<br> All things to give them<br> Nourishment in due proportion,<br> In four Days, in accordance<br> With (the needs of)<br> Those who seek (sustenance).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/041/041_011.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>11.  Thumma istaw<u>a</u> il<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>-i wahiya dukh<u>a</u>nun faq<u>a</u>la lah<u>a</u> walil-ar<u>d</u>i i/tiy<u>a</u> <u>t</u>awAAan aw karhan q<u>a</u>lat<u>a</u> atayn<u>a</u> <u>ta</u>-iAAeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>11.  Moreover He comprehended<br> In His design the sky,<br> And it had been (as) smoke:<br> He said to it<br> And to the earth:<br> "Come ye together,<br> Willingly or unwillingly."<br> They said: "We do come<br> (Together), in willing obedience."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/041/041_012.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>12.  Faqa<u>da</u>hunna sabAAa sam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>tin fee yawmayni waaw<u>ha</u> fee kulli sam<u>a</u>-in amrah<u>a</u> wazayyann<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>a a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> bima<u>sa</u>bee<u>h</u>a wa<u>h</u>if<i><u>th</u></i>an <u>tha</u>lika taqdeeru alAAazeezi alAAaleem<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>12.  So he completed them<br> As seven firmaments<br>In two Days, and He<br> Assigned to each heaven<br> Its duty and command.<br> And We adorned<br> The lower heaven<br> With lights, and (provided it)<br> With guard. Such<br> Is the Decree of (Him)<br> The Exalted in Might,<br> Full of knowledge.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/041/041_013.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>13.  Fa-in aAAra<u>d</u>oo fuqul an<u>th</u>artukum <u>sa</u>AAiqatan mithla <u>sa</u>AAiqati AA<u>a</u>din wathamood<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>13.  But if they turn away,<br> Say thou: "I have warned<br> You of a stunning Punishment<br> (As of thunder and lightning)<br> Like that which (overtook)<br> The ‘Ād and the <u>Th</u>amūd!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/041/041_014.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>14.  I<u>th</u> j<u>a</u>at-humu a<b>l</b>rrusulu min bayni aydeehim wamin khalfihim all<u>a</u> taAAbudoo ill<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>ha q<u>a</u>loo law sh<u>a</u>a rabbun<u>a</u> laanzala mal<u>a</u>-ikatan fa-inn<u>a</u> bim<u>a</u> orsiltum bihi k<u>a</u>firoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>14.  Behold, the apostles came<br> To them, from before them<br> And behind them, (preaching):<br> "Serve none but God."<br> They said, "If our Lord<br> Had so pleased, He would<br>Certainly have sent down angels<br> (To preach): now we reject<br> Your mission (altogether)."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/041/041_015.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>15.  Faamm<u>a</u> AA<u>a</u>dun fa<b>i</b>stakbaroo fee al-ar<u>d</u>i bighayri al<u>h</u>aqqi waq<u>a</u>loo man ashaddu minn<u>a</u> quwwatan awa lam yaraw anna All<u>a</u>ha alla<u>th</u>ee khalaqahum huwa ashaddu minhum quwwatan wak<u>a</u>noo bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin<u>a</u> yaj<u>h</u>adoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>15.  Now the ‘Ād behaved<br> Arrogantly through the land,<br> Against (all) truth and reason,<br> And said: "Who is superior<br> To us in strength?" What!<br> Did they not see that<br> God, Who created them,<br> Was superior to them<br> In strength? But they<br> Continued to reject Our Signs!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/041/041_016.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>16.  Faarsaln<u>a</u> AAalayhim ree<u>h</u>an <u>s</u>ar<u>s</u>aran fee ayy<u>a</u>min na<u>h</u>is<u>a</u>tin linu<u>th</u>eeqahum AAa<u>tha</u>ba alkhizyi fee al<u>h</u>ay<u>a</u>ti a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> walaAAa<u>tha</u>bu al-<u>a</u>khirati akhz<u>a</u> wahum l<u>a</u> yun<u>s</u>aroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>16.  So We sent against them<br> A furious Wind through days<br> Of disaster, that We might<br> Give them a taste<br> Of a Penalty of humiliation<br> In this Life; but the Penalty<br> Of a Hereafter will be<br> More humiliating still:<br> And they will find<br> No help.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/041/041_017.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>17.  Waamm<u>a</u> thamoodu fahadayn<u>a</u>hum fa<b>i</b>sta<u>h</u>abboo alAAam<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> alhud<u>a</u> faakha<u>th</u>at-hum <u>sa</u>AAiqatu alAAa<u>tha</u>bi alhooni bim<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo yaksiboon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>17.  As to the <u>Th</u>amūd,<br> We gave them guidance,<br> But they preferred blindness<br> (Of heart) to Guidance:<br> So the stunning Punishment<br> Of humiliation seized them,<br> Because of what they had earned.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/041/041_018.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>18.  Wanajjayn<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo wak<u>a</u>noo yattaqoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>18.  But We delivered those<br> Who believed and practised righteousness.</p> <div class="filenav">
