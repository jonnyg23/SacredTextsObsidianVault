 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra VII.: A’rāf, or the Heights</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  Alif-l<u>a</u>m-meem-<u>sa</u>d   </p>
 <p>1.  Alif, Lām, Mīm, Ṣād.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  Kit<u>a</u>bun onzila ilayka fal<u>a</u> yakun fee <u>s</u>adrika <u>h</u>arajun minhu litun<u>th</u>ira bihi wa<u>th</u>ikr<u>a</u> lilmu/mineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>2.  A Book revealed unto thee,—<br> So let thy heart be oppressed'<br> No more by any difficulty<br> On that account,<br> That with it thou mightest<br> Warn (the erring) and teach<br> The Believers.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  IttabiAAoo m<u>a</u> onzila ilaykum min rabbikum wal<u>a</u> tattabiAAoo min doonihi awliy<u>a</u>a qaleelan m<u>a</u> ta<u>th</u>akkaroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>3.  Follow (O men!) the revelation<br> Given unto you from your Lord,<br> And follow not, as friends<br> Or protectors, other than Him.<br> Little it is ye remember<br> Of admonition.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  Wakam min qaryatin ahlakn<u>a</u>h<u>a</u> faj<u>a</u>ah<u>a</u> ba/sun<u>a</u> bay<u>a</u>tan aw hum q<u>a</u>-iloon<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>4.  How many towns have We<br> Destroyed (for their sins)?<br> Our punishment took them<br> On a sudden by night<br> Or while they slept<br> For their afternoon rest.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  Fam<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>na daAAw<u>a</u>hum i<u>th</u> j<u>a</u>ahum ba/sun<u>a</u> ill<u>a</u> an q<u>a</u>loo inn<u>a</u> kunn<u>a</u> <i><u>th</u></i><u>a</u>limeen<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>5.  When (thus) Our punishment<br> Took them, no cry<br> Did they utter but this:<br> "Indeed we did wrong."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  Falanas-alanna alla<u>th</u>eena orsila ilayhim walanas-alanna almursaleen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>6.  When shall we question<br> Those to whom Our Message<br> Was sent and those by whom<br> We sent it.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  Falanaqu<u>ss</u>anna AAalayhim biAAilmin wam<u>a</u> kunn<u>a</u> gh<u>a</u>-ibeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>7.  And verily We shall recount<br> Their whole story<br> With knowledge, for We<br> Were never absent<br> (At any time or place).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  Wa<b>a</b>lwaznu yawma-i<u>th</u>ini al<u>h</u>aqqu faman thaqulat maw<u>a</u>zeenuhu faol<u>a</u>-ika humu almufli<u>h</u>oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>8.  The balance that day<br> Will be true (to a nicety):<br> Those whose scale (of good)<br> Will be heavy, will prosper:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_009.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>9.  Waman khaffat maw<u>a</u>zeenuhu faol<u>a</u>-ika alla<u>th</u>eena khasiroo anfusahum bim<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin<u>a</u> ya<i><u>th</u></i>limoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>9.  Those whose scale will be light,<br> Will find their souls<br> In perdition, for that they<br> Wrongfully treated Our Signs.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_010.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>10.  Walaqad makkann<u>a</u>kum fee al-ar<u>d</u>i wajaAAaln<u>a</u> lakum feeh<u>a</u> maAA<u>a</u>yisha qaleelan m<u>a</u> tashkuroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>10.  It is We Who have<br> Placed you with authority<br> On earth, and provided<br> You therein with means<br> For the fulfilment of your life:<br> Small are the thanks<br> That ye give!</p> <div class="filenav">
