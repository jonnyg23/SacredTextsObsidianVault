 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra I.: Fātiḥa, or the Opening Chapter.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/001/001_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  Bismi All<u>a</u>hi a<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>m<u>a</u>ni a<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>eem<b>i</b>  </p>
 <p>1.  In the name of God, Most Gracious,<br> Most Merciful.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/001/001_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  Al<u>h</u>amdu lill<u>a</u>hi rabbi alAA<u>a</u>lameen<b>a</b>  </p>
 <p>2.  Praise be to God,<br> The Cherisher and Sustainer of the Worlds;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/001/001_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  A<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>m<u>a</u>ni a<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>eem<b>i</b>  </p>
 <p>3.  Most Gracious, Most Merciful;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/001/001_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  M<u>a</u>liki yawmi a<b>l</b>ddeen<b>i</b>  </p>
 <p>4.  Master of the Day of Judgment.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/001/001_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  Iyy<u>a</u>ka naAAbudu wa-iyy<u>a</u>ka nastaAAeen<b>u</b>  </p>
 <p>5.  Thee do we worship,<br> And Thine aid we seek.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/001/001_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  Ihdin<u>a</u> a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>ir<u>at</u>a almustaqeem<b>a</b>  </p>
 <p>6.  Show us the straight way,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/001/001_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  <u>S</u>ir<u>at</u>a alla<u>th</u>eena anAAamta AAalayhim ghayri almagh<u>d</u>oobi AAalayhim wal<u>a</u> a<b>l</b><u>dda</u>lleen<b>a</b>  </p>
 <p>7.  The way of those on whom<br> Thou hast bestowed Thy Grace,<br> Those whose (portion)<br> Is not wrath,<br> And who go not astray.</p>  <div class="filenav">
