 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XV.: Al-Hijr, or The Rocky Tract.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 4</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_045.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>45.  Inna almuttaqeena fee jann<u>a</u>tin waAAuyoon<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>45.  The righteous (will be)<br> Amid Gardens<br> And fountains<br> (Of clear-flowing water).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_046.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>46.  Odkhulooh<u>a</u> bisal<u>a</u>min <u>a</u>mineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>46.  (Their greeting will be):<br> "Enter ye here<br> In Peace and Security."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_047.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>47.  WanazaAAn<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> fee <u>s</u>udoorihim min ghillin ikhw<u>a</u>nan AAal<u>a</u> sururin mutaq<u>a</u>bileen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>47.  And We shall remove<br> From their hearts any<br> Lurking sense of injury:<br> (They will be) brothers<br> (Joyfully) facing each other<br> On thrones (of dignity).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_048.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>48.  L<u>a</u> yamassuhum feeh<u>a</u> na<u>s</u>abun wam<u>a</u> hum minh<u>a</u> bimukhrajeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>48.  There no sense of fatigue<br> Shall touch them,<br> Nor shall they (ever)<br> Be asked to leave.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_049.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>49.  Nabbi/ AAib<u>a</u>dee annee an<u>a</u> alghafooru a<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>eem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>49.  Tell My servants<br> That I am indeed<br> The Oft-Forgiving,<br> Most Merciful;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_050.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>50.  Waanna AAa<u>tha</u>bee huwa alAAa<u>tha</u>bu al-aleem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>50.  And that My Penalty<br> Will be indeed<br> The most grievous Penalty.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_051.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>51.  Wanabbi/hum AAan <u>d</u>ayfi ibr<u>a</u>heem<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>51.  Tell them about<br> The guests of Abraham.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_052.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>52.  I<u>th</u> dakhaloo AAalayhi faq<u>a</u>loo sal<u>a</u>man q<u>a</u>la inn<u>a</u> minkum wajiloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>52.  When they entered his presence<br> And said, "Peace!"<br> He said, "We feel<br> Afraid of you!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_053.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>53.  Q<u>a</u>loo l<u>a</u> tawjal inn<u>a</u> nubashshiruka bighul<u>a</u>min AAaleem<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>53.  They said: "Fear not!<br> We give thee glad tidings<br>Of a son endowed<br> With wisdom."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_054.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>54.  Q<u>a</u>la abashshartumoonee AAal<u>a</u> an massaniya alkibaru fabima tubashshiroon<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>54.  He said: "Do ye give me<br> Glad tidings that old age<br> Has seized me? Of what,<br> Then, is your good news?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_055.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>55.  Q<u>a</u>loo bashsharn<u>a</u>ka bi<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>aqqi fal<u>a</u> takun mina alq<u>a</u>ni<u>t</u>een<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>55.  They said: "We give thee<br> Glad tidings in truth:<br> Be not then in despair!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_056.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>56.  Q<u>a</u>la waman yaqna<u>t</u>u min ra<u>h</u>mati rabbihi ill<u>a</u> a<b>l</b><u>dda</u>lloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>56.  He said: "And who<br> Despairs of the mercy<br> Of his Lord, but such<br> As go astray?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_057.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>57.  Q<u>a</u>la fam<u>a</u> kha<u>t</u>bukum ayyuh<u>a</u> almursaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>57.  Abraham said: "What then<br> Is the business on which<br> Ye (have come), O ye<br> Messengers (of God)?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_058.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>58.  Q<u>a</u>loo inn<u>a</u> orsiln<u>a</u> il<u>a</u> qawmin mujrimeen<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>58.  They said: "We have been<br> Sent to a people<br> (Deep) in sin,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_059.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>59.  Ill<u>a</u> <u>a</u>la loo<u>t</u>in inn<u>a</u> lamunajjoohum ajmaAAeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>59.  "Excepting the adherents<br> Of Lūt: them we are certainly<br>(Charged) to save (from harm),—<br> All—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_060.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>60.  Ill<u>a</u> imraatahu qaddarn<u>a</u> innah<u>a</u> lamina algh<u>a</u>bireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>60.  "Except his wife, who,<br> We have ascertained,<br> Will be among those<br> Who will lag behind."</p> <div class="filenav">
