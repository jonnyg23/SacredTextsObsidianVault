 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_141.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>141.  Wahuwa alla<u>th</u>ee anshaa jann<u>a</u>tin maAAroosh<u>a</u>tin waghayra maAAroosh<u>a</u>tin wa<b>al</b>nnakhla wa<b>al</b>zzarAAa mukhtalifan okuluhu wa<b>al</b>zzaytoona wa<b>al</b>rrumm<u>a</u>na mutash<u>a</u>bihan waghayra mutash<u>a</u>bihin kuloo min thamarihi i<u>tha</u> athmara wa<u>a</u>too <u>h</u>aqqahu yawma <u>h</u>a<u>sa</u>dihi wal<u>a</u> tusrifoo innahu l<u>a</u> yu<u>h</u>ibbu almusrifeen<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>141.  It is He who produceth<br> Gardens, with trellises<br> And without, and dates,<br> And tilth with produce<br> Of all kinds, and olives<br> And pomegranates,<br> Similar (in kind)<br> And different (in variety):<br> Eat of their fruit<br> In their season, but render<br> The dues that are proper<br> On the day that the harvest<br> Is gathered. But waste not<br> By excess: for God<br> Loveth not the wasters.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_142.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>142.  Wamina al-anAA<u>a</u>mi <u>h</u>amoolatan wafarshan kuloo mimm<u>a</u> razaqakumu All<u>a</u>hu wal<u>a</u> tattabiAAoo khu<u>t</u>uw<u>a</u>ti a<b>l</b>shshay<u>ta</u>ni innahu lakum AAaduwwun mubeen<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>142.  Of the cattle are some<br> For burden and some for meat:<br>Eat what God hath provided<br> For you, and follow not<br> The footsteps of Satan:<br> For he is to you<br> An avowed enemy.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_143.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>143.  Tham<u>a</u>niyata azw<u>a</u>jin mina a<b>l</b><u>dd</u>a/ni ithnayni wamina almaAAzi ithnayni qul <u>a</u><b>l</b><u>thth</u>akarayni <u>h</u>arrama ami alonthayayni amm<u>a</u> ishtamalat AAalayhi ar<u>ha</u>mu alonthayayni nabbi-oonee biAAilmin in kuntum <u>sa</u>diqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>143.  (Take) eight (head of cattle)<br> In (four) pairs:<br> Of sheep a pair,<br> And of goats a pair;<br> Say, hath He forbidden<br> The two males,<br> Or the two females,<br> Or (the young) which the wombs<br> Of the two females enclose?<br> Tell me with knowledge<br> If ye are truthful:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_144.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>144.  Wamina al-ibili ithnayni wamina albaqari ithnayni qul <u>a</u><b>l</b><u>thth</u>akarayni <u>h</u>arrama ami alonthayayni amm<u>a</u> ishtamalat AAalayhi ar<u>ha</u>mu alonthayayni am kuntum shuhad<u>a</u>a i<u>th</u> wa<u>ssa</u>kumu All<u>a</u>hu bih<u>atha</u> faman a<i><u>th</u></i>lamu mimmani iftar<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi ka<u>th</u>iban liyu<u>d</u>illa a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>sa bighayri AAilmin inna All<u>a</u>ha l<u>a</u> yahdee alqawma a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>144.  Of camels a pair,<br> And of oxen a pair;<br> Say, hath He forbidden<br> The two males,<br> Or the two females,<br> Or (the young) which the wombs<br> Of the two females enclose?—<br> Were ye present when God<br> Ordered you such a thing?<br> But who doth more wrong<br> Than one who invents<br> A lie against God,<br> To lead astray men<br> Without knowledge?<br> For God guideth not<br> People who do wrong</p> <div class="filenav">
