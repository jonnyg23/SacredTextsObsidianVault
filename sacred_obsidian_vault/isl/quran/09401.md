 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XCIV.: Inshirāḥ, or The Expansion.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/094/094_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  Alam nashra<u>h</u> laka <u>s</u>adrak<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>1.  Have We not<br> Expanded thee thy breast?—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/094/094_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  Wawa<u>d</u>aAAn<u>a</u> AAanka wizrak<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>2.  And removed from thee<br> Thy burden</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/094/094_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  Alla<u>th</u>ee anqa<u>d</u>a <i><u>th</u></i>ahrak<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>3.  The which did gall<br> Thy back?—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/094/094_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  WarafaAAn<u>a</u> laka <u>th</u>ikrak<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>4.  And raised high the esteem<br> (In which) thou (art held)?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/094/094_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  Fa-inna maAAa alAAusri yusr<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>5.  So, verily,<br> With every difficulty,<br> There is relief:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/094/094_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  Inna maAAa alAAusri yusr<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>6.  Verily, with every difficulty<br> There is relief.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/094/094_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  Fa-i<u>tha</u> faraghta fa<b>i</b>n<u>s</u>ab   </p>
 <p>7.  Therefore, when thou art<br> Free (from thine immediate task),<br> Still labour hard,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/094/094_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  Wa-il<u>a</u> rabbika fa<b>i</b>rghab      </p>
 <p>8.  And to thy Lord<br> Turn (all) thy attention.</p>  <div class="filenav">
