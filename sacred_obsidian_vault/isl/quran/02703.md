 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXVII.: Naml, or the Ants.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 3</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_032.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>32.  Q<u>a</u>lat y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> almalao aftoonee fee amree m<u>a</u> kuntu q<u>at</u>iAAatan amran <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> tashhadoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>32.  the said: "Ye chiefs!<br> Advise me in (this)<br> My affair: no affair<br> Have I decided<br> Except in your presence."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_033.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>33.  Q<u>a</u>loo na<u>h</u>nu oloo quwwatin waoloo ba/sin shadeedin wa<b>a</b>l-amru ilayki fa<b>o</b>n<i><u>th</u></i>uree m<u>atha</u> ta/mureen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>33.  They said: "We are endued<br> With strength, and given<br> To vehement war:<br> But the command is<br> With thee; so consider<br> What thou wilt command."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_034.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>34.  Q<u>a</u>lat inna almulooka i<u>tha</u> dakhaloo qaryatan afsadooh<u>a</u> wajaAAaloo aAAizzata ahlih<u>a</u> a<u>th</u>illatan waka<u>tha</u>lika yafAAaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>34.  She said: "Kings, when they<br> Enter a country, despoil it,<br>And make the noblest<br> Of its people its meanest<br> Thus do they behave.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_035.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>35.  Wa-innee mursilatun ilayhim bihadiyyatin fan<u>a</u><i><u>th</u></i>iratun bima yarjiAAu almursaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>35.  "But I am going to send<br> Him a present, and (wait)<br> To see with what (answer)<br> Return (my) ambassadors."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_036.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>36.  Falamm<u>a</u> j<u>a</u>a sulaym<u>a</u>na q<u>a</u>la atumiddoonani bim<u>a</u>lin fam<u>a</u> <u>a</u>t<u>a</u>niya All<u>a</u>hu khayrun mimm<u>a</u> <u>a</u>t<u>a</u>kum bal antum bihadiyyatikum tafra<u>h</u>oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>36.  Now when (the embassy) came<br> To Solomon, he said:<br> "Will ye give me abundance<br> In wealth? But that which<br> God has given me is better<br> Than that which He has<br> Given you! Nay it is ye<br> Who rejoice in your gift!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_037.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>37.  IrjiAA ilayhim falana/tiyannahum bijunoodin l<u>a</u> qibala lahum bih<u>a</u> walanukhrijannahum minh<u>a</u> a<u>th</u>illatan wahum <u>sa</u>ghiroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>37.  "Go back to them, and be sure<br> We shall come to them<br> With such hosts as they<br> Will never be able to meet:<br> We shall expel them<br> From there in disgrace,<br> And they will feel<br> Humbled (indeed)."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_038.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>38.  Q<u>a</u>la y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> almalao ayyukum ya/teenee biAAarshih<u>a</u> qabla an ya/toonee muslimeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>38.  He said (to his own men):<br> "Ye Chiefs! which of you<br> Can bring me her throne<br> Before they come to me<br> In submission?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_039.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>39.  Q<u>a</u>la AAifreetun mina aljinni an<u>a</u> <u>a</u>teeka bihi qabla an taqooma min maq<u>a</u>mika wa-inne AAalayhi laqawiyyun ameen<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>39.  Said an ‘Ifrit, of the Jinns:<br> "I will bring it to thee<br>Before thou rise from thy<br> Council: indeed I have<br> Full strength for the purpose,<br> And may he trusted."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_040.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>40.  Q<u>a</u>la alla<u>th</u>ee AAindahu AAilmun mina alkit<u>a</u>bi an<u>a</u> <u>a</u>teeka bihi qabla an yartadda ilayka <u>t</u>arfuka falamm<u>a</u> ra<u>a</u>hu mustaqirran AAindahu q<u>a</u>la h<u>atha</u> min fa<u>d</u>li rabbee liyabluwanee aashkuru am akfuru waman shakara fa-innam<u>a</u> yashkuru linafsihi waman kafara fa-inna rabbee ghaniyyun kareem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>40.  Said one who had knowledge<br> Of the Book: "I will<br> Bring it to thee within<br> The twinkling of an eye!"<br> Then when (Solomon) saw it<br> Placed firmly before him,<br> He said: "This is<br> By the grace of my Lord!<br> To test me whether I am<br> Grateful or ungrateful!<br> And if any is grateful,<br> Truly his gratitude is (a gain)<br> For his own soul; hut if<br> Any is ungrateful, truly<br> My Lord is Free of all Needs,<br> Supreme in Honour!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_041.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>41.  Q<u>a</u>la nakkiroo lah<u>a</u> AAarshah<u>a</u> nan<i><u>th</u></i>ur atahtadee am takoonu mina alla<u>th</u>eena l<u>a</u> yahtadoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>41.  He said: "Transform her throne<br> Out of all recognition by her:<br> Let us see whether she<br> Is guided (to the truth)<br>Or is one of those who<br> Receive no guidance."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_042.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>42.  Falamm<u>a</u> j<u>a</u>at qeela ah<u>a</u>ka<u>tha</u> AAarshuki q<u>a</u>lat kaannahu huwa waooteen<u>a</u> alAAilma min qablih<u>a</u> wakunn<u>a</u> muslimeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>42.  So when she arrived,<br> She was asked, "Is this<br> Thy throne?" She said,<br> "It was just like this;<br> And knowledge was bestowed<br> On us in advance of this,<br> And we have submitted<br> To God (in Islam)."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_043.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>43.  Wa<u>s</u>addah<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>nat taAAbudu min dooni All<u>a</u>hi innah<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>nat min qawmin k<u>a</u>fireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>43.  And he diverted her<br> From the worship of others<br> Besides God: for she was<br> (Sprung) of a people<br> That had no faith.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_044.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>44.  Qeela lah<u>a</u> odkhulee a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>ar<u>h</u>a falamm<u>a</u> raat-hu <u>h</u>asibat-hu lujjatan wakashafat AAan s<u>a</u>qayh<u>a</u> q<u>a</u>la innahu <u>s</u>ar<u>h</u>un mumarradun min qaw<u>a</u>reera q<u>a</u>lat rabbi innee <i><u>th</u></i>alamtu nafsee waaslamtu maAAa sulaym<u>a</u>na lill<u>a</u>hi rabbi alAA<u>a</u>lameen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>44.  She was asked to enter<br> The lofty Palace: but<br> When she saw it, she<br> Thought it was a lake<br> Of water, and she (tucked up<br> Her skirts), uncovering her legs.<br> He said: "This is<br> But a palace paved<br>Smooth with slabs of glass."<br> She said: "O my Lord!<br> I have indeed wronged"<br> My soul: I do (now)<br> Submit (in Islam), with Solomon,<br> To the Lord of the Worlds."</p> <div class="filenav">
