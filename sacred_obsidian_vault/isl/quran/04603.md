 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XLVI.: Aḥqāf, or Winding Sand-tracts.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 3</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/046/046_021.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>21.  Wa<b>o</b><u>th</u>kur akh<u>a</u> AA<u>a</u>din i<u>th</u> an<u>th</u>ara qawmahu bi<b>a</b>l-a<u>h</u>q<u>a</u>fi waqad khalati a<b>l</b>nnu<u>th</u>uru min bayni yadayhi wamin khalfihi all<u>a</u> taAAbudoo ill<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>ha innee akh<u>a</u>fu AAalaykum AAa<u>tha</u>ba yawmin AAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>21.  Mention (Hūd)<br> One of ‘Ād's (own) brethren:<br> Behold, he warned his people<br> About the winding Sand-tracts:<br> But there have been Warners<br> Before him and after him:<br> "Worship ye none other<br> Than God: truly I fear<br> For you the Penalty<br> Of a Mighty Day."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/046/046_022.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>22.  Q<u>a</u>loo aji/tan<u>a</u> lita/fikan<u>a</u> AAan <u>a</u>lihatin<u>a</u> fa/tin<u>a</u> bim<u>a</u> taAAidun<u>a</u> in kunta mina a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>diqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>22.  They said: "Hast thou come<br> In order to turn us aside<br>From our gods? Then bring<br> Upon us the (calamity)<br> With which thou dost<br> Threaten us, if thou<br> Art telling the truth!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/046/046_023.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>23.  Q<u>a</u>la innam<u>a</u> alAAilmu AAinda All<u>a</u>hi waoballighukum m<u>a</u> orsiltu bihi wal<u>a</u>kinnee ar<u>a</u>kum qawman tajhaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>23.  He said: "The Knowledge<br> (Of when it will come)<br> Is only with God: I<br> Proclaim to you the mission<br> On which I have been sent:<br> But I see that ye<br> Are a people in ignorance!"…</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/046/046_024.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>24.  Falamm<u>a</u> raawhu AA<u>a</u>ri<u>d</u>an mustaqbila awdiyatihim q<u>a</u>loo h<u>atha</u> AA<u>a</u>ri<u>d</u>un mum<u>t</u>irun<u>a</u> bal huwa m<u>a</u> istaAAjaltum bihi ree<u>h</u>un feeh<u>a</u> AAa<u>tha</u>bun aleem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>24.  Then, when they saw<br> The (Penalty in the shape of)<br> A cloud traversing the sky,<br> Corning to meet their valleys,<br> They said, "This cloud<br> Will give us rain!"<br> "Nay, it is the (calamity)<br> Ye were asking to be<br> Hastened!—a wind<br> Wherein is a Grievous Penalty!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/046/046_025.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>25.  Tudammiru kulla shay-in bi-amri rabbih<u>a</u> faa<u>s</u>ba<u>h</u>oo l<u>a</u> yur<u>a</u> ill<u>a</u> mas<u>a</u>kinuhum ka<u>tha</u>lika najzee alqawma almujrimeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>25.  "Everything will it destroy<br> By the command of its Lord!"<br> Then by the morning they—<br> Nothing was to be seen<br> But (the ruins of) their houses!<br> Thus do We recompense<br> Those given to sin!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/046/046_026.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>26.  Walaqad makkann<u>a</u>hum feem<u>a</u> in makkann<u>a</u>kum feehi wajaAAaln<u>a</u> lahum samAAan waab<u>sa</u>ran waaf-idatan fam<u>a</u> aghn<u>a</u> AAanhum samAAuhum wal<u>a</u> ab<u>sa</u>ruhum wal<u>a</u> af-idatuhum min shay-in i<u>th</u> k<u>a</u>noo yaj<u>h</u>adoona bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>ti All<u>a</u>hi wa<u>ha</u>qa bihim m<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo bihi yastahzi-oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>26.  And We had firmly established<br> Them in a (prosperity and) power<br> Which We have not given<br> To you (ye Quraish!)<br> And We had endowed them<br> With (faculties of)<br> Hearing, seeing, heart and intellect<br> But of no profit to them<br> Were their (faculties of)<br> Hearing, sight, and heart<br> And intellect, when they<br> Went on rejecting the Signs<br> Of God; and they were<br> (Completely) encircled<br> By that which they<br> Used to mock at!</p> <div class="filenav">
