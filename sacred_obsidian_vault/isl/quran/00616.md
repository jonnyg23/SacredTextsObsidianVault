 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra VI.: An’ām, or Cattle.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 16</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_130.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>130.  Y<u>a</u> maAAshara aljinni wa<b>a</b>l-insi alam ya/tikum rusulun minkum yaqu<u>ss</u>oona AAalaykum <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tee wayun<u>th</u>iroonakum liq<u>a</u>a yawmikum h<u>atha</u> q<u>a</u>loo shahidn<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> anfusin<u>a</u> wagharrat-humu al<u>h</u>ay<u>a</u>tu a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> washahidoo AAal<u>a</u> anfusihim annahum k<u>a</u>noo k<u>a</u>fireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>130.  "O ye assembly of Jinns<br> And men! came there not<br> Unto you apostles from amongst you<br> Setting forth unto you<br> My Signs, and warning you<br> Of the meeting of this Day<br> Of yours?" They will say:<br> "We bear witness against<br> Ourselves." It was<br> The life of this world<br> That deceived them. So<br> Against themselves will they<br> Bear witness that they<br> Rejected Faith.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_131.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>131.  <u>Tha</u>lika an lam yakun rabbuka muhlika alqur<u>a</u> bi<i><u>th</u></i>ulmin waahluh<u>a</u> gh<u>a</u>filoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>131.  (The apostles were sent) thus,<br> For thy Lord would not<br> Destroy for their wrong-doing<br> Men's habitations whilst<br> Their occupants were unwarned.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_132.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>132.  Walikullin daraj<u>a</u>tun mimm<u>a</u> AAamiloo wam<u>a</u> rabbuka bigh<u>a</u>filin AAamm<u>a</u> yaAAmaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>132.  To all are degrees (or ranks)<br> According to their deeds:<br> For thy Lord<br> Is not unmindful<br> Of anything that they do.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_133.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>133.  Warabbuka alghaniyyu <u>th</u>oo a<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>mati in yasha/ yu<u>th</u>hibkum wayastakhlif min baAAdikum m<u>a</u> yash<u>a</u>o kam<u>a</u> anshaakum min <u>th</u>urriyyati qawmin <u>a</u>khareen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>133.  Thy Lord is Self-sufficient,<br> Full of Mercy: if it were<br> His Will, He could destroy<br> You, and in your place<br> Appoint whom He will<br> As your successors, even as<br> He raised you up<br> From the posterity<br> Of other people.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_134.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>134.  Inna m<u>a</u> tooAAadoona la<u>a</u>tin wam<u>a</u> antum bimuAAjizeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>134.  All that hath been<br> Promised unto you<br> Will come to pass:<br> Nor can ye frustrate it<br> (In the least bit).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_135.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>135.  Qul y<u>a</u> qawmi iAAmaloo AAal<u>a</u> mak<u>a</u>natikum innee AA<u>a</u>milun fasawfa taAAlamoona man takoonu lahu AA<u>a</u>qibatu a<b>l</b>dd<u>a</u>ri innahu l<u>a</u> yufli<u>h</u>u a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>135.  Say: "O my people!<br> Do whatever ye can:<br> I will do (my part):<br> Soon will ye know<br> Who it is whose end<br> Will be (best) in the Hereafter:<br> Certain it is that<br> The wrong-doers will not prosper."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_136.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>136.  WajaAAaloo lill<u>a</u>hi mimm<u>a</u> <u>th</u>araa mina al<u>h</u>arthi wa<b>a</b>l-anAA<u>a</u>mi na<u>s</u>eeban faq<u>a</u>loo h<u>atha</u> lill<u>a</u>hi bizaAAmihim wah<u>atha</u> lishurak<u>a</u>-in<u>a</u> fam<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>na lishurak<u>a</u>-ihim fal<u>a</u> ya<u>s</u>ilu il<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi wam<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>na lill<u>a</u>hi fahuwa ya<u>s</u>ilu il<u>a</u> shurak<u>a</u>-ihim s<u>a</u>a m<u>a</u> ya<u>h</u>kumoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>136.  Out of what God<br> Hath produced in abundance<br> In tilth and in cattle,<br> They assigned Him a share:<br> They say, according to their fancies:<br> "This is for God, and this"—<br> For Our "partners"!<br> But the share of their "partners"<br> Reacheth not God, whilst<br> The share of God reacheth<br> Their "partners"! Evil<br> (And unjust) is their assignment!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_137.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>137.  Waka<u>tha</u>lika zayyana likatheerin mina almushrikeena qatla awl<u>a</u>dihim shurak<u>a</u>ohum liyurdoohum waliyalbisoo AAalayhim deenahum walaw sh<u>a</u>a All<u>a</u>hu m<u>a</u> faAAaloohu fa<u>th</u>arhum wam<u>a</u> yaftaroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>137.  Even so, in the eyes<br> Of most of the Pagans,<br> Their "partners" made alluring<br> The slaughter of their children,<br> In order to lead them<br> To their own destruction,<br> And cause confusion<br> In their religion.<br> If God had willed,<br> They would not have done so:<br> But leave alone<br> Them and their inventions.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_138.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>138.  Waq<u>a</u>loo h<u>ath</u>ihi anAA<u>a</u>mun wa<u>h</u>arthun <u>h</u>ijrun l<u>a</u> ya<u>t</u>AAamuh<u>a</u> ill<u>a</u> man nash<u>a</u>o bizaAAmihim waanAA<u>a</u>mun <u>h</u>urrimat <i><u>th</u></i>uhooruh<u>a</u> waanAA<u>a</u>mun l<u>a</u> ya<u>th</u>kuroona isma All<u>a</u>hi AAalayh<u>a</u> iftir<u>a</u>an AAalayhi sayajzeehim bim<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo yaftaroon<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>138.  And they say that<br> Such and such cattle and crops<br> Are taboo, and none should<br> Eat of them except those<br> Whom—so they say—We<br> Wish; further, there are<br> Cattle forbidden to yoke<br> Or burden, and cattle<br> On which, (at slaughter),<br> The name of God is not<br> Pronounced;—inventions<br> Against God's name: soon<br> Will He requite them<br> For their inventions.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_139.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>139.  Waq<u>a</u>loo m<u>a</u> fee bu<u>t</u>ooni h<u>ath</u>ihi al-anAA<u>a</u>mi kh<u>a</u>li<u>s</u>atun li<u>th</u>ukoorin<u>a</u> wamu<u>h</u>arramun AAal<u>a</u> azw<u>a</u>jin<u>a</u> wa-in yakun maytatan fahum feehi shurak<u>a</u>o sayajzeehim wa<u>s</u>fahum innahu <u>h</u>akeemun AAaleem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>139.  They say: "What is<br> In the wombs of<br> Such and such cattle<br> Is specially reserved<br> (For food) for our men,<br> And forbidden to our women;<br> But if it is still-born,<br> Then all have shares therein.<br>For their (false) attribution<br> (Of superstitions to God),<br> He will soon punish them:<br> For He is full<br> Of wisdom and knowledge.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_140.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>140.  Qad khasira alla<u>th</u>eena qataloo awl<u>a</u>dahum safahan bighayri AAilmin wa<u>h</u>arramoo m<u>a</u> razaqahumu All<u>a</u>hu iftir<u>a</u>an AAal<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi qad <u>d</u>alloo wam<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo muhtadeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>140.  Lost are those who slay<br> Their children, from folly,<br> Without knowledge, and forbid<br> Food which God hath provided<br> For them, inventing (lies)<br> Against God. They have<br> Indeed gone astray<br> And heeded no guidance.</p> <div class="filenav">
