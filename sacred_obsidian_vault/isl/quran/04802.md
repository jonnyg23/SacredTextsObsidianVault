 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XLVIII.: Fat-ḥ or Victory.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 2</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/048/048_011.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>11.  Sayaqoolu laka almukhallafoona mina al-aAAr<u>a</u>bi shaghalatn<u>a</u> amw<u>a</u>lun<u>a</u> waahloon<u>a</u> fa<b>i</b>staghfir lan<u>a</u> yaqooloona bi-alsinatihim m<u>a</u> laysa fee quloobihim qul faman yamliku lakum mina All<u>a</u>hi shay-an in ar<u>a</u>da bikum <u>d</u>arran aw ar<u>a</u>da bikum nafAAan bal k<u>a</u>na All<u>a</u>hu bim<u>a</u> taAAmaloona khabeer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>11.  The desert Arabs who<br> Lagged behind will<br>Say to thee:<br> "We were engaged in<br> (Looking after) our flocks<br> And herds, and our families:<br> Do thou then ask<br> Forgiveness for us.<br> They say with their tongues<br> What is not in their hearts.<br> Say: "Who then has<br> Any power at all<br> (To intervene) on your behalf<br> With God, if His Will<br> Is to give you some loss<br> Or to give you some profit?<br> But God is well acquainted<br> With all that ye do.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/048/048_012.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>12.  Bal <i><u>th</u></i>anantum an lan yanqaliba a<b>l</b>rrasoolu wa<b>a</b>lmu/minoona il<u>a</u> ahleehim abadan wazuyyina <u>tha</u>lika fee quloobikum wa<i><u>th</u></i>anantum <i><u>th</u></i>anna a<b>l</b>ssaw-i wakuntum qawman boor<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>12.  "Nay, ye thought that<br> The Apostle and the Believers<br> Would never return to<br> Their families; this seemed<br> Pleasing in your hearts, and<br> Ye conceived an evil thought,<br> For ye are a people<br> Lost (in wickedness)."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/048/048_013.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>13.  Waman lam yu/min bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi warasoolihi fa-inn<u>a</u> aAAtadn<u>a</u> lilk<u>a</u>fireena saAAeer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>13.  And if any believe not<br> In God and His Apostle,<br> We have prepared,<br> For those who reject God,<br> A Blazing Fire!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/048/048_014.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>14.  Walill<u>a</u>hi mulku a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i yaghfiru liman yash<u>a</u>o wayuAAa<u>thth</u>ibu man yash<u>a</u>o wak<u>a</u>na All<u>a</u>hu ghafooran ra<u>h</u>eem<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>14.  To God belongs the dominion<br> Of the heavens and the earth:<br> He forgives whom He wills,<br> And He punishes whom He<br>Wills: but God is<br> Oft-Forgiving, Most Merciful.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/048/048_015.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>15.  Sayaqoolu almukhallafoona i<u>tha</u> in<u>t</u>alaqtum il<u>a</u> magh<u>a</u>nima lita/khu<u>th</u>ooh<u>a</u> <u>th</u>aroon<u>a</u> nattabiAAkum yureedoona an yubaddiloo kal<u>a</u>ma All<u>a</u>hi qul lan tattabiAAoon<u>a</u> ka<u>tha</u>likum q<u>a</u>la All<u>a</u>hu min qablu fasayaqooloona bal ta<u>h</u>sudoonan<u>a</u> bal k<u>a</u>noo l<u>a</u> yafqahoona ill<u>a</u> qaleel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>15.  Those who lagged behind<br> (Will say), when ye (are<br> Free to) march and take<br> Booty (in war): "Permit us<br> To follow you." They wish<br> To change God's decree:<br> Say: "Not thus<br> Will ye follow us:<br> God has already declared<br> (This) beforehand": then they<br> Will say, "But ye are<br> Jealous of us." Nay,<br> But little do they understand<br> (Such things).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/048/048_016.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>16.  Qul lilmukhallafeena mina al-aAAr<u>a</u>bi satudAAawna il<u>a</u> qawmin olee ba/sin shadeedin tuq<u>a</u>tiloonahum aw yuslimoona fa-in tu<u>t</u>eeAAoo yu/tikumu All<u>a</u>hu ajran <u>h</u>asanan wa-in tatawallaw kam<u>a</u> tawallaytum min qablu yuAAa<u>thth</u>ibkum AAa<u>tha</u>ban aleem<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>16.  Say to the desert Arabs<br> Who lagged behind: "Ye<br> Shall be summoned (to fight)<br> Against a people given to<br> Vehement war: then shall ye<br> Fight, or they shall submit.<br> Then if ye show obedience,<br> God will grant you<br> A goodly reward, but if<br> Ye turn back as ye<br>Did before, He will punish<br> You with a grievous Penalty."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/048/048_017.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>17.  Laysa AAal<u>a</u> al-aAAm<u>a</u> <u>h</u>arajun wal<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> al-aAAraji <u>h</u>arajun wal<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> almaree<u>d</u>i <u>h</u>arajun waman yu<u>t</u>iAAi All<u>a</u>ha warasoolahu yudkhilhu jann<u>a</u>tin tajree min ta<u>h</u>tih<u>a</u> al-anh<u>a</u>ru waman yatawalla yuAAa<u>thth</u>ibhu AAa<u>tha</u>ban aleem<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>17.  No blame is there<br> On the blind, nor is<br> There blame on the lame,<br> Nor on one ill (if he<br> Joins not the war):<br> But he that obeys God<br> And His Apostle,—(God)<br> Will admit him to Gardens<br> Beneath which rivers flow;<br> And he who turns back,<br> (God) will punish him<br> With a grievous Penalty.</p> <div class="filenav">
