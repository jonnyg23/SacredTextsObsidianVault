 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XII.: Yūsuf, or Joseph.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 9</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_069.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>69.  Walamm<u>a</u> dakhaloo AAal<u>a</u> yoosufa <u>a</u>w<u>a</u> ilayhi akh<u>a</u>hu q<u>a</u>la innee an<u>a</u> akhooka fal<u>a</u> tabta-is bim<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo yaAAmaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>69.  Now when they came<br> Into Joseph's presence,<br> He received his (full) brother<br> To stay with him. He said<br> (To him): "Behold! I am thy (own)<br> Brother; so grieve not<br> At aught of their doings."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_070.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>70.  Falamm<u>a</u> jahhazahum bijah<u>a</u>zihim jaAAala a<b>l</b>ssiq<u>a</u>yata fee ra<u>h</u>li akheehi thumma a<u>thth</u>ana mu-a<u>thth</u>inun ayyatuh<u>a</u> alAAeeru innakum las<u>a</u>riqoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>70.  At length when he had furnished<br> Them forth with provisions<br> (Suitable) for them, he put<br> The drinking cup into<br> His brother's saddle-bag.<br> Then shouted out a Crier:<br> "O ye (in) the Caravan!<br> Behold! ye are thieves,<br> Without doubt!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_071.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>71.  Q<u>a</u>loo waaqbaloo AAalayhim m<u>atha</u> tafqidoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>71.  They said, turning towards them:<br> "What is it that ye miss?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_072.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>72.  Q<u>a</u>loo nafqidu <u>s</u>uw<u>a</u>AAa almaliki waliman j<u>a</u>a bihi <u>h</u>imlu baAAeerin waan<u>a</u> bihi zaAAeem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>72.  They said: "We miss<br> The great beaker of the king;<br>For him who produces it,—<br> Is (the reward of)<br> A camel load; I<br> Will be bound by it."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_073.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>73.  Q<u>a</u>loo ta<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi laqad AAalimtum m<u>a</u> ji/n<u>a</u> linufsida fee al-ar<u>d</u>i wam<u>a</u> kunn<u>a</u> s<u>a</u>riqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>73.  (The brothers) said: "By God!<br> Well ye know that we<br> Came not to make mischief<br> In the land, and we are<br> No thieves!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_074.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>74.  Q<u>a</u>loo fam<u>a</u> jaz<u>a</u>ohu in kuntum k<u>ath</u>ibeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>74.  (The Egyptians) said: "What then<br> Shall be the penalty of this,<br> If ye are (proved) to have lied?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_075.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>75.  Q<u>a</u>loo jaz<u>a</u>ohu man wujida fee ra<u>h</u>lihi fahuwa jaz<u>a</u>ohu ka<u>tha</u>lika najzee a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>75.  They said: "The penalty<br> Should be that he<br> In whose saddle-bag<br> It is found, should be held<br> (As bondman) to atone<br> For the (crime). Thus it is<br> We punish the wrong-doers!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_076.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>76.  Fabadaa bi-awAAiyatihim qabla wiAA<u>a</u>-i akheehi thumma istakhrajah<u>a</u> min wiAA<u>a</u>-i akheehi ka<u>tha</u>lika kidn<u>a</u> liyoosufa m<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>na liya/khu<u>th</u>a akh<u>a</u>hu fee deeni almaliki ill<u>a</u> an yash<u>a</u>a All<u>a</u>hu narfaAAu daraj<u>a</u>tin man nash<u>a</u>o wafawqa kulli <u>th</u>ee AAilmin AAaleem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>76.  So he began (the search)<br> With their baggage,<br>Before (he came to) the baggage<br> Of his brother: at length<br> He brought it out of his<br> Brother's baggage. Thus did We<br> Plan for Joseph. He could not<br> Take his brother by the law<br> Of the king except that God<br> Willed it (so). We raise<br> To degrees (of wisdom) whom<br> We please: but over all<br> Endued with knowledge is One,<br> The All-Knowing.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_077.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>77.  Q<u>a</u>loo in yasriq faqad saraqa akhun lahu min qablu faasarrah<u>a</u> yoosufu fee nafsihi walam yubdih<u>a</u> lahum q<u>a</u>la antum sharrun mak<u>a</u>nan wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu aAAlamu bim<u>a</u> ta<u>s</u>ifoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>77.  They said: "If he steals,<br> There was a brother of his<br> Who did steal before (him)."<br> But these things did Joseph<br> Keep locked in his heart,<br> Revealing not the secrets to them.<br>He (simply) said (to himself):<br> "Ye are the worse situated;<br> And God knoweth best<br> The truth of what ye assert!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_078.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>78.  Q<u>a</u>loo y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alAAazeezu inna lahu aban shaykhan kabeeran fakhu<u>th</u> a<u>h</u>adan<u>a</u> mak<u>a</u>nahu inn<u>a</u> nar<u>a</u>ka mina almu<u>h</u>sineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>78.  They said: "O exalted one!<br> Behold! he has a father,<br> Aged and venerable, (who will<br> Grieve for him); so take<br> One of us in his place;<br> For we see that thou art<br> (Gracious) in doing good."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_079.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>79.  Q<u>a</u>la maAA<u>ath</u>a All<u>a</u>hi an na/khu<u>th</u>a ill<u>a</u> man wajadn<u>a</u> mat<u>a</u>AAan<u>a</u> AAindahu inn<u>a</u> i<u>th</u>an la<i><u>th</u></i><u>a</u>limoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>79.  He said: "God forbid<br> That we take other than him<br> With whom we found<br> Our property: indeed<br> (If we did so), we should<br> Be acting wrongfully.</p> <div class="filenav">
