 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra L.: Qāf.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 3</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/050/050_030.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>30.  Yawma naqoolu lijahannama hali imtala/ti wataqoolu hal min mazeed<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>30.  One Day We will<br> Ask Hell, "Art thou<br> Filled to the full?"<br> It will say, "Are there<br> Any more (to come)?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/050/050_031.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>31.  Waozlifati aljannatu lilmuttaqeena ghayra baAAeed<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>31.  And the Garden<br> Will be brought nigh<br>To the Righteous,—no more<br> A thing distant.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/050/050_032.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>32.  H<u>atha</u> m<u>a</u> tooAAadoona likulli aww<u>a</u>bin <u>h</u>afee<i><u>th</u></i><b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>32.  (A voice will say:)<br> "This is what was<br> Promised for you,<br> For every one who turned<br> (To God) in sincere repentance,<br> Who kept (His Law),</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/050/050_033.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>33.  Man khashiya a<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>m<u>a</u>na bi<b>a</b>lghaybi waj<u>a</u>a biqalbin muneeb<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>33.  "Who feared (God)<br> Most Gracious unseen,<br> And brought a heart<br> Turned in devotion (to Him):</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/050/050_034.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>34.  Odkhulooh<u>a</u> bisal<u>a</u>min <u>tha</u>lika yawmu alkhulood<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>34.  "Enter ye therein<br> In Peace and Security;<br> This is a Day<br> Of Eternal Life!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/050/050_035.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>35.  Lahum m<u>a</u> yash<u>a</u>oona feeh<u>a</u> waladayn<u>a</u> mazeed<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>35.  There will be for them<br> Therein all that they wish,—<br> And more besides<br> In Our Presence,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/050/050_036.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>36.  Wakam ahlakn<u>a</u> qablahum min qarnin hum ashaddu minhum ba<u>t</u>shan fanaqqaboo fee albil<u>a</u>di hal min ma<u>h</u>ee<u>s</u><b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>36.  But how many<br> Generations before them<br> Did We destroy (for their<br> Sins),—stronger in power<br> Than they? Then did they<br> Wander through the land:<br> Was there any place<br> Of escape (for them)?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/050/050_037.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>37.  Inna fee <u>tha</u>lika la<u>th</u>ikr<u>a</u> liman k<u>a</u>na lahu qalbun aw alq<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>ssamAAa wahuwa shaheed<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>37.  Verily in this<br> Is a Message<br> For any that has<br> A heart and understanding<br> Or who gives ear and<br> Earnestly witnesses (the truth).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/050/050_038.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>38.  Walaqad khalaqn<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>a wam<u>a</u> baynahum<u>a</u> fee sittati ayy<u>a</u>min wam<u>a</u> massan<u>a</u> min lughoob<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>38.  We created the heavens<br> And the earth and all<br> Between them in Six Days,<br> Nor did any sense<br> Of weariness touch Us.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/050/050_039.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>39.  Fa<b>i</b><u>s</u>bir AAal<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> yaqooloona wasabbi<u>h</u> bi<u>h</u>amdi rabbika qabla <u>t</u>ulooAAi a<b>l</b>shshamsi waqabla alghuroob<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>39.  Bear, then, with patience,<br> All that they say,<br> And celebrate the praises<br> Of thy Lord, before<br> The rising of the sun<br> And before (its) setting,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/050/050_040.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>40.  Wamina allayli fasabbi<u>h</u>hu waadb<u>a</u>ra a<b>l</b>ssujood<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>40.  And during part<br> Of the night, (also,)<br> Celebrate His praises,<br> And (so likewise)<br> After the postures<br> Of adoration.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/050/050_041.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>41.  Wa<b>i</b>stamiAA yawma yun<u>a</u>di almun<u>a</u>di min mak<u>a</u>nin qareeb<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>41.  And listen for the Day<br> When the Caller will call<br> Out from a place<br> Quite near,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/050/050_042.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>42.  Yawma yasmaAAoona a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>ay<u>h</u>ata bi<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>aqqi <u>tha</u>lika yawmu alkhurooj<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>42.  The Day when they will<br> Hear a (mighty) Blast<br> In (very) truth: that<br> Will be the Day<br> Of Resurrection.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/050/050_043.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>43.  Inn<u>a</u> na<u>h</u>nu nu<u>h</u>yee wanumeetu wa-ilayn<u>a</u> alma<u>s</u>eer<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>43.  Verily it is We Who<br> Give Life and Death;<br> And to Us is<br> The Final Goal—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/050/050_044.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>44.  Yawma tashaqqaqu al-ar<u>d</u>u AAanhum sir<u>a</u>AAan <u>tha</u>lika <u>h</u>ashrun AAalayn<u>a</u> yaseer<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>44.  The Day when<br> The Earth will be<br> Rent asunder, from (men)<br> Hurrying out: that will be<br> A gathering together,—<br> Quite easy for Us.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/050/050_045.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>45.  Na<u>h</u>nu aAAlamu bim<u>a</u> yaqooloona wam<u>a</u> anta AAalayhim bijabb<u>a</u>rin fa<u>th</u>akkir bi<b>a</b>lqur-<u>a</u>ni man yakh<u>a</u>fu waAAeed<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>45.  We know best what they<br> Say; and thou art not<br> One to overawe them<br> By force. So admonish<br> With the Qur-ān such<br> As fear My Warning!</p>  <div class="filenav">
