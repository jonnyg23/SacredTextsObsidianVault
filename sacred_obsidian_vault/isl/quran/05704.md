 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/057/057_026.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>26.  Walaqad arsaln<u>a</u> noo<u>h</u>an wa-ibr<u>a</u>heema wajaAAaln<u>a</u> fee <u>th</u>urriyyatihim<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nnubuwwata wa<b>a</b>lkit<u>a</u>ba faminhum muhtadin wakatheerun minhum f<u>a</u>siqoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>26.  And We sent Noah<br> And Abraham, and established<br> In their line Prophethood<br> And Revelation: and some of them!<br> Were on right guidance,<br> But many of them<br> Became rebellious transgressors.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/057/057_027.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>27.  Thumma qaffayn<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> <u>a</u>th<u>a</u>rihim birusulin<u>a</u> waqaffayn<u>a</u> biAAees<u>a</u> ibni maryama wa<u>a</u>tayn<u>a</u>hu al-injeela wajaAAaln<u>a</u> fee quloobi alla<u>th</u>eena ittabaAAoohu ra/fatan wara<u>h</u>matan warahb<u>a</u>niyyatan ibtadaAAooh<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> katabn<u>a</u>h<u>a</u> AAalayhim ill<u>a</u> ibtigh<u>a</u>a ri<u>d</u>w<u>a</u>ni All<u>a</u>hi fam<u>a</u> raAAawh<u>a</u> <u>h</u>aqqa riAA<u>a</u>yatih<u>a</u> fa<u>a</u>tayn<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo minhum ajrahum wakatheerun minhum f<u>a</u>siqoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>27.  Then, in their wake,<br> We followed them up<br> With (others of) Our apostles:<br> We sent after them<br> Jesus the son of Mary,<br> And bestowed on him<br> The Gospel; and We ordained<br> In the hearts of those<br> Who followed him<br> Compassion and Mercy.<br>But the Monasticism<br> Which they invented<br> For themselves, We did not<br> Prescribe for them:<br> (We commanded) only<br> The seeking for the Good<br> Pleasure of God; but that<br> They did not foster<br> As they should have done.<br> Yet We bestowed, on those<br> Among them who believed,<br> Their (due) reward, but<br> Many of them are<br> Rebellious transgressors.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/057/057_028.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>28.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo ittaqoo All<u>a</u>ha wa<u>a</u>minoo birasoolihi yu/tikum kiflayni min ra<u>h</u>matihi wayajAAal lakum nooran tamshoona bihi wayaghfir lakum wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu ghafoorun ra<u>h</u>eem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>28.  O ye that believe!<br> Fear God, and believe<br> In His Apostle, and He will<br> Bestow on you a double<br> Portion of His Mercy:<br> He will provide for you<br>A Light by which ye<br> Shall walk (straight<br> In your path), and He<br> Will forgive you (your past):<br> For God is Oft-Forgiving,<br> Most Merciful:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/057/057_029.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>29.  Li-all<u>a</u> yaAAlama ahlu alkit<u>a</u>bi all<u>a</u> yaqdiroona AAal<u>a</u> shay-in min fa<u>d</u>li All<u>a</u>hi waanna alfa<u>d</u>la biyadi All<u>a</u>hi yu/teehi man yash<u>a</u>o wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu <u>th</u>oo alfa<u>d</u>li alAAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>29.  That the People of<br> The Book may know<br> That they have no power<br> Whatever over the Grace<br> Of God, that (His) Grace<br> Is (entirely) in his hand,<br> To bestow it on<br> Whomsoever He wills.<br> For God is the Lord.<br> Of Grace abounding.</p>  <div class="filenav">
