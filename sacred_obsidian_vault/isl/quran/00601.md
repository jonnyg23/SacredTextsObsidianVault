 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra VI.: An’ām, or Cattle.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  Al<u>h</u>amdu lill<u>a</u>hi alla<u>th</u>ee khalaqa a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>a wajaAAala a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i>ulum<u>a</u>ti wa<b>al</b>nnoora thumma alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo birabbihim yaAAdiloon<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>1.  Praise be to God,<br> Who created the heavens<br> And the earth,<br> And made the Darkness<br> And the Light.<br> Yet those who reject Faith<br> Hold (others) as equal.<br> With their Guardian-Lord.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  Huwa alla<u>th</u>ee khalaqakum min <u>t</u>eenin thumma qa<u>da</u> ajalan waajalun musamman AAindahu thumma antum tamtaroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>2.  He it is Who created<br> You from clay, and then<br> Decreed a stated term<br> (For you). And there is<br> In His Presence another<br> Determined term; yet<br> Ye doubt within yourselves!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  Wahuwa All<u>a</u>hu fee a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wafee al-ar<u>d</u>i yaAAlamu sirrakum wajahrakum wayaAAlamu m<u>a</u> taksiboon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>3.  And He is God<br> In the heavens<br> And on earth.<br> He knoweth what ye<br> Hide, and what ye reveal,<br> And He knoweth<br> The (recompense) which<br> Ye earn (by your deeds)</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  Wam<u>a</u> ta/teehim min <u>a</u>yatin min <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>ti rabbihim ill<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo AAanh<u>a</u> muAAri<u>d</u>een<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>4.  But never did a single<br> One of the Signs<br> Of their Lord reach them,<br> But they turned<br> Away therefrom.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  Faqad ka<u>thth</u>aboo bi<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>aqqi lamm<u>a</u> j<u>a</u>ahum fasawfa ya/teehim anb<u>a</u>o m<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo bihi yastahzi-oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>5.  And now they reject<br> The truth when it reaches<br> Them: but soon shall they<br> Learn the reality of what<br> They used to mock at.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  Alam yaraw kam ahlakn<u>a</u> min qablihim min qarnin makkann<u>a</u>hum fee al-ar<u>d</u>i m<u>a</u> lam numakkin lakum waarsaln<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>a AAalayhim midr<u>a</u>ran wajaAAaln<u>a</u> al-anh<u>a</u>ra tajree min ta<u>h</u>tihim faahlakn<u>a</u>hum bi<u>th</u>unoobihim waansha/n<u>a</u> min baAAdihim qarnan <u>a</u>khareen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>6.  See they not how many<br> Of those before them<br> We did destroy?—<br> Generations We had established<br> On the earth, in strength<br> Such as We have not given<br> To you—for whom<br> We poured out rain<br> From the skies in abundance,<br> And gave (fertile) streams<br> Flowing beneath their (feet):<br> Yet for their sins<br> We destroyed them,<br> And raised in their wake<br> Fresh generations<br> (To succeed them).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  Walaw nazzaln<u>a</u> AAalayka kit<u>a</u>ban fee qir<u>ta</u>sin falamasoohu bi-aydeehim laq<u>a</u>la alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo in h<u>atha</u> ill<u>a</u> si<u>h</u>run mubeen<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>7.  If We had sent<br> Unto thee a written<br> (Message) on parchment,<br> So that they could<br> Touch it with their hands,<br> The Unbelievers would<br> Have been sure to say:<br> "This is nothing but<br>Obvious magic!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  Waq<u>a</u>loo lawl<u>a</u> onzila AAalayhi malakun walaw anzaln<u>a</u> malakan laqu<u>d</u>iya al-amru thumma l<u>a</u> yun<i><u>th</u></i>aroon<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>8.  They say: "Why is not<br> An angel sent down to him?"<br> If We did send down<br> An angel, the matter<br> Would be settled at once,<br> And no respite<br> Would be granted them.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_009.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>9.  Walaw jaAAaln<u>a</u>hu malakan lajaAAaln<u>a</u>hu rajulan walalabasn<u>a</u> AAalayhim m<u>a</u> yalbisoon<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>9.  If We had made it<br> An angel, We should<br> Have sent him as a man,<br> And We should certainly<br> Have caused them confusion<br> In a matter which they have<br> Already covered with confusion.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_010.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>10.  Walaqadi istuhzi-a birusulin min qablika fa<u>ha</u>qa bi<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena sakhiroo minhum m<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo bihi yastahzi-oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>10.  Mocked were (many)<br> Apostles before thee;<br> But their scoffers<br> Were hemmed in<br> By the thing that they mocked.</p> <div class="filenav">
