 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra LIV.: Qamar, or the Moon.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 2</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/054/054_023.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>23.  Ka<u>thth</u>abat thamoodu bi<b>al</b>nnu<u>th</u>ur<b>i</b>    </p>
 <p>23.  The Thamūd (also)<br> Rejected (their) Warners.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/054/054_024.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>24.  Faq<u>a</u>loo abasharan minn<u>a</u> w<u>ah</u>idan nattabiAAuhu inn<u>a</u> i<u>th</u>an lafee <u>d</u>al<u>a</u>lin wasuAAur<b>in</b>    </p>
 <p>24.  For they said: "What!<br> A man! a solitary one<br>From among ourselves!<br> Shall we follow such a one?<br> Truly should we then be<br> Straying in mind, and mad!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/054/054_025.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>25.  Aolqiya a<b>l</b><u>thth</u>ikru AAalayhi min baynin<u>a</u> bal huwa ka<u>ththa</u>bun ashir<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>25.  "Is it that the Message<br> Is sent to him,<br> Of all people amongst us?<br> Nay, he is a liar,<br> An insolent one!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/054/054_026.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>26.  SayaAAlamoona ghadan mani alka<u>ththa</u>bu al-ashir<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>26.  Ah! they will know<br> On the morrow, which is<br> The liar, the insolent one!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/054/054_027.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>27.  Inn<u>a</u> mursiloo a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>qati fitnatan lahum fa<b>i</b>rtaqibhum wa<b>i</b><u>st</u>abir   </p>
 <p>27.  For We will send<br> The she-camel<br> By way of trial for them.<br> So watch them, (O Ṣāliḥ),<br> And possess thyself in patience!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/054/054_028.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>28.  Wanabbi/hum anna alm<u>a</u>a qismatun baynahum kullu shirbin mu<u>h</u>ta<u>d</u>ar<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>28.  And tell them that<br> The water is to be<br> Divided between them:<br> Each one's right to drink<br> Being brought forward<br> (By suitable turns).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/054/054_029.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>29.  Fan<u>a</u>daw <u>sah</u>ibahum fataAA<u>ata</u> faAAaqar<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>29.  But they called<br> To their companion,<br> And he took a sword<br> In hand, and hamstrung (her).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/054/054_030.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>30.  Fakayfa k<u>a</u>na AAa<u>tha</u>bee wanu<u>th</u>ur<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>30.  Ah! how (terrible) was<br> My Penalty and My Warning!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/054/054_031.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>31.  Inn<u>a</u> arsaln<u>a</u> AAalayhim <u>s</u>ay<u>h</u>atan w<u>ah</u>idatan fak<u>a</u>noo kahasheemi almu<u>h</u>ta<i><u>th</u></i>ir<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>31.  For We sent against them<br> A single Mighty Blast,<br> And they became<br> Like the dry stubble used<br> By one who pens cattle.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/054/054_032.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>32.  Walaqad yassarn<u>a</u> alqur-<u>a</u>na li<b>l</b><u>thth</u>ikri fahal min muddakir<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>32.  And we have indeed<br> Made the Qur-ān easy<br> To understand and remember:<br> Then is there any that<br> Will receive admonition?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/054/054_033.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>33.  Ka<u>thth</u>abat qawmu loo<u>t</u>in bi<b>al</b>nnu<u>th</u>ur<b>i</b>    </p>
 <p>33.  The People of Lūṭ<br> Rejected (his) Warning.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/054/054_034.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>34.  Inn<u>a</u> arsaln<u>a</u> AAalayhim <u>has</u>iban ill<u>a</u> <u>a</u>la loo<u>t</u>in najjayn<u>a</u>hum bisa<u>h</u>ar<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>34.  We sent against them<br> A violent tornado<br> With showers of stones,<br> (Which destroyed them), except<br> Lūṭ's household: them We<br> Delivered by early Dawn,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/054/054_035.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>35.  NiAAmatan min AAindin<u>a</u> ka<u>tha</u>lika najzee man shakar<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>35.  As a Grace from Us:<br> Thus do We reward<br> Those who give thanks.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/054/054_036.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>36.  Walaqad an<u>th</u>arahum ba<u>t</u>shatan<u>a</u> fatam<u>a</u>raw bi<b>al</b>nnu<u>th</u>ur<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>36.  And (Lūṭ) did warn them<br> Of Our Punishment, but<br> They disputed about the Warning.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/054/054_037.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>37.  Walaqad r<u>a</u>wadoohu AAan <u>d</u>ayfihi fa<u>t</u>amasn<u>a</u> aAAyunahum fa<u>th</u>ooqoo AAa<u>tha</u>bee wanu<u>th</u>ur<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>37.  And they even sought<br> To snatch away his guests<br> From him, but We blinded<br> Their eyes. (They heard:)<br> "Now taste ye My Wrath<br> And My Warning."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/054/054_038.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>38.  Walaqad <u>s</u>abba<u>h</u>ahum bukratan AAa<u>tha</u>bun mustaqirr<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>38.  Early on the morrow<br> An abiding Punishment<br> Seized them:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/054/054_039.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>39.  Fa<u>th</u>ooqoo AAa<u>tha</u>bee wanu<u>th</u>ur<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>39.  "So taste ye My Wrath<br> And My Warning."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/054/054_040.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>40.  Walaqad yassarn<u>a</u> alqur-<u>a</u>na li<b>l</b><u>thth</u>ikri fahal min muddakir<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>40.  And We have indeed<br> Made the Qur-ān easy<br> To understand and remember:<br> Then is there any that<br> Will receive admonition?</p> <div class="filenav">
