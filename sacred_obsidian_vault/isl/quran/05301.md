 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra LIII.: Najm, or the Star.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/053/053_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  Wa<b>al</b>nnajmi i<u>tha</u> haw<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>1.  By the Star<br> When it goes down,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/053/053_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  M<u>a</u> <u>d</u>alla <u>sah</u>ibukum wam<u>a</u> ghaw<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>2.  Your Companion is neither<br> Astray nor being misled,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/053/053_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  Wam<u>a</u> yan<u>t</u>iqu AAani alhaw<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>3.  Nor does he say (aught)<br> Of (his own) Desire.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/053/053_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  In huwa ill<u>a</u> wa<u>h</u>yun yoo<u>ha</u>   </p>
 <p>4.  It is no less than<br> Inspiration sent down to him:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/053/053_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  AAallamahu shadeedu alquw<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>5.  He was taught by one<br> Mighty in Power,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/053/053_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  <u>Th</u>oo mirratin fa<b>i</b>staw<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>6.  Endued with Wisdom:<br> For he appeared<br> (In stately form)</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/053/053_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  Wahuwa bi<b>a</b>l-ofuqi al-aAAl<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>7.  While he was in<br> The highest part<br> Of the horizon:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/053/053_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  Thumma dan<u>a</u> fatadall<u>a</u>    </p>
 <p>8.  Then he approached<br> And came closer,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/053/053_009.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>9.  Fak<u>a</u>na q<u>a</u>ba qawsayni aw adn<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>9.  And was at a distance<br> Of but two bow-lengths<br> Or (even) nearer;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/053/053_010.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>10.  Faaw<u>ha</u> il<u>a</u> AAabdihi m<u>a</u> aw<u>ha</u>   </p>
 <p>10.  So did (God) convey<br> The inspiration to His Servant—<br> (Conveyed) what He (meant)<br> To convey.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/053/053_011.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>11.  M<u>a</u> ka<u>th</u>aba alfu-<u>a</u>du m<u>a</u> ra<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>11.  The (Prophet's) (mind and) heart<br> In no way falsified<br> That which he saw.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/053/053_012.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>12.  Afatum<u>a</u>roonahu AAal<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> yar<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>12.  Will ye then dispute<br> With him concerning<br> What he saw?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/053/053_013.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>13.  Walaqad ra<u>a</u>hu nazlatan okhr<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>13.  For indeed he saw him<br> At a second descent,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/053/053_014.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>14.  AAinda sidrati almuntah<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>14.  Near the Lote-tree<br> Beyond which none may pass:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/053/053_015.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>15.  AAindah<u>a</u> jannatu alma/w<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>15.  Near it is the Garden<br> Of Abode.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/053/053_016.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>16.  I<u>th</u> yaghsh<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>ssidrata m<u>a</u> yaghsh<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>16.  Behold, the Lote-tree<br> Was shrouded<br> (In mystery unspeakable!)</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/053/053_017.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>17.  M<u>a</u> z<u>a</u>gha alba<u>s</u>aru wam<u>a</u> <u>t</u>agh<u>a</u>    </p>
 <p>17.  (His) sight never swerved,<br> Nor did it go wrong!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/053/053_018.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>18.  Laqad ra<u>a</u> min <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>ti rabbihi alkubr<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>18.  For truly did he see,<br> Of the Signs of his Lord,<br> The Greatest!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/053/053_019.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>19.  Afaraaytumu all<u>a</u>ta wa<b>a</b>lAAuzz<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>19.  Have ye seen<br> Lāt, and ‘Uzzā,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/053/053_020.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>20.  Waman<u>a</u>ta a<b>l</b>thth<u>a</u>lithata al-okhr<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>20.  And another,<br> The third (goddess), Manāt?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/053/053_021.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>21.  Alakumu a<b>l</b><u>thth</u>akaru walahu al-onth<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>21.  What! For you<br> The male sex,<br> And for Him, the female?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/053/053_022.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>22.  Tilka i<u>th</u>an qismatun <u>d</u>eez<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>22.  Behold, such would be<br> Indeed a division<br> Most unfair!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/053/053_023.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>23.  In hiya ill<u>a</u> asm<u>a</u>on sammaytumooh<u>a</u> antum wa<u>a</u>b<u>a</u>okum m<u>a</u> anzala All<u>a</u>hu bih<u>a</u> min sul<u>ta</u>nin in yattabiAAoona ill<u>a</u> a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i>anna wam<u>a</u> tahw<u>a</u> al-anfusu walaqad j<u>a</u>ahum min rabbihimu alhud<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>23.  These are nothing but names<br> Which ye have devised,—<br> Ye and your fathers,<br> For which God has sent<br> Down no authority (whatever).<br> They follow nothing but<br> Conjecture and what<br> Their own souls desire!—<br> Even though there has already<br> Come to them Guidance<br> From their Lord!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/053/053_024.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>24.  Am lil-ins<u>a</u>ni m<u>a</u> tamann<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>24.  Nay, shall man have (just)<br> Anything he hankers after?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/053/053_025.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>25.  Falill<u>a</u>hi al-<u>a</u>khiratu wa<b>a</b>l-ool<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>25.  But it is to God<br> That the End and<br> The Beginning (of all things)<br> Belong.</p> <div class="filenav">
