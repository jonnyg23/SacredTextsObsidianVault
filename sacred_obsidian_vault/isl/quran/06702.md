 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra LXVII.: Mulk, or Dominion.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 2</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/067/067_015.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>15.  Huwa alla<u>th</u>ee jaAAala lakumu al-ar<u>d</u>a <u>th</u>aloolan fa<b>i</b>mshoo fee man<u>a</u>kibih<u>a</u> wakuloo min rizqihi wa-ilayhi a<b>l</b>nnushoor<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>15.  It is He Who has<br> Made the earth manageable<br>For you, so traverse<br> Ye through its tracts<br> And enjoy of the Sustenance<br> Which He furnishes: but<br> Unto Him is the Resurrection.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/067/067_016.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>16.  Aamintum man fee a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>-i an yakhsifa bikumu al-ar<u>d</u>a fa-i<u>tha</u> hiya tamoor<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>16.  Do ye feel secure that<br> He Who is in Heaven<br> Will not cause you<br> To be swallowed up<br> By the earth when it<br> Shakes (as in an earthquake)?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/067/067_017.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>17.  Am amintum man fee a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>-i an yursila AAalaykum <u>has</u>iban fasataAAlamoona kayfa na<u>th</u>eer<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>17.  Or do ye feel secure<br> That He Who is in Heaven<br> Will not send against you<br> A violent tornado<br> (With showers of stones),<br> So that ye shall<br> Know how (terrible)<br> Was My warning?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/067/067_018.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>18.  Walaqad ka<u>thth</u>aba alla<u>th</u>eena min qablihim fakayfa k<u>a</u>na nakeer<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>18.  But indeed men before them<br> Rejected (My warning):<br> Then how (terrible) was<br> My rejection (of them)?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/067/067_019.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>19.  Awa lam yaraw il<u>a</u> a<b>l</b><u>tt</u>ayri fawqahum <u>sa</u>ff<u>a</u>tin wayaqbi<u>d</u>na m<u>a</u> yumsikuhunna ill<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>m<u>a</u>nu innahu bikulli shay-in ba<u>s</u>eer<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>19.  Do they not observe<br> The birds above them,<br>Spreading their wings<br> And folding them in?<br> None can uphold them<br> Except (God) Most Gracious:<br> Truly it is He<br> That watches over all things.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/067/067_020.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>20.  Amman h<u>atha</u> alla<u>th</u>ee huwa jundun lakum yan<u>s</u>urukum min dooni a<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>m<u>a</u>ni ini alk<u>a</u>firoona ill<u>a</u> fee ghuroor<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>20.  Nay, who is there<br> That can help you,<br> (Even as) an army,<br> Besides (God) Most Merciful?<br> In nothing but delusion<br> Are the Unbelievers.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/067/067_021.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>21.  Amman h<u>atha</u> alla<u>th</u>ee yarzuqukum in amsaka rizqahu bal lajjoo fee AAutuwwin wanufoor<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>21.  Or who is there<br> That can provide you<br> With Sustenance if He<br> Were to withhold His provision?<br> Nay, they obstinately persist<br> In insolent impiety<br> And flight (from the Truth).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/067/067_022.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>22.  Afaman yamshee mukibban AAal<u>a</u> wajhihi ahd<u>a</u> amman yamshee sawiyyan AAal<u>a</u> <u>s</u>ir<u>at</u>in mustaqeem<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>22.  Is then one who<br> Walks headlong, with his face<br> Grovelling, better guided,—<br> Or one who walks<br> Evenly on a Straight Way?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/067/067_023.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>23.  Qul huwa alla<u>th</u>ee anshaakum wajaAAala lakumu a<b>l</b>ssamAAa wa<b>a</b>l-ab<u>sa</u>ra wa<b>a</b>l-af-idata qaleelan m<u>a</u> tashkuroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>23.  Say: "It is He Who<br> Has created you (and made<br> You grow), and made<br> For you the faculties<br> Of hearing, seeing,<br> Feeling and understanding:<br> Little thanks it is ye give.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/067/067_024.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>24.  Qul huwa alla<u>th</u>ee <u>th</u>araakum fee al-ar<u>d</u>i wa-ilayhi tu<u>h</u>sharoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>24.  Say: "It is He Who<br> Has multiplied you<br> Through the earth,<br> And to Him shall ye<br> Be gathered together."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/067/067_025.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>25.  Wayaqooloona mat<u>a</u> h<u>atha</u> alwaAAdu in kuntum <u>sa</u>diqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>25.  They ask: When will<br> This promise be (fulfilled)?—<br> If ye are telling<br> The truth.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/067/067_026.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>26.  Qul innam<u>a</u> alAAilmu AAinda All<u>a</u>hi wa-innam<u>a</u> an<u>a</u> na<u>th</u>eerun mubeen<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>26.  Say: "As to the knowledge<br> Of the time, it is<br> With God alone:<br> I am (sent) only<br> To warn plainly in public."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/067/067_027.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>27.  Falamm<u>a</u> raawhu zulfatan see-at wujoohu alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo waqeela h<u>atha</u> alla<u>th</u>ee kuntum bihi taddaAAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>27.  At length, when they<br> See it close at hand,<br> Grieved will be the faces<br> Of the Unbelievers,<br>And it will be said<br> (To them): "This is<br> (The promise fulfilled),<br> Which ye were calling for!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/067/067_028.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>28.  Qul araaytum in ahlakaniya All<u>a</u>hu waman maAAiya aw ra<u>h</u>iman<u>a</u> faman yujeeru alk<u>a</u>fireena min AAa<u>tha</u>bin aleem<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>28.  Say: "See ye?—<br> If God were<br> To destroy me,<br> And those with me,<br> Or if He bestows<br> His Mercy on us,—<br> Yet who can deliver<br> The Unbelievers from<br> A grievous Penalty?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/067/067_029.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>29.  Qul huwa a<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>m<u>a</u>nu <u>a</u>mann<u>a</u> bihi waAAalayhi tawakkaln<u>a</u> fasataAAlamoona man huwa fee <u>d</u>al<u>a</u>lin mubeen<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>29.  Say: "He is (God)<br> Most Gracious: we have<br> Believed in Him,<br> And on Him have we<br> Put our trust:<br> So, soon will ye know<br> Which (of us) it is<br> That is in manifest error."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/067/067_030.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>30.  Qul araaytum in a<u>s</u>ba<u>h</u>a m<u>a</u>okum ghawran faman ya/teekum bim<u>a</u>-in maAAeen<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>30.  Say: "See ye?—<br> If your stream be<br> Some morning lost<br> (In the underground earth),<br> Who then can supply you<br> With clear-flowing water?"</p>  <div class="filenav">
