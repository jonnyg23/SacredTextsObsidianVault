 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XIX.: Maryam, or Mary.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 6</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_083.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>83.  Alam tara ann<u>a</u> arsaln<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>shshay<u>at</u>eena AAal<u>a</u> alk<u>a</u>fireena taozzuhum azz<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>83.  Seest thou not that We<br> Have set the Evil Ones on<br> Against the Unbelievers,<br> To incite them with fury?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_084.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>84.  Fal<u>a</u> taAAjal AAalayhim innam<u>a</u> naAAuddu lahum AAadd<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>84.  So make no haste<br> Against them, for We<br> But count out to them<br> A (limited) number (of days).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_085.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>85.  Yawma na<u>h</u>shuru almuttaqeena il<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>m<u>a</u>ni wafd<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>85.  The day We shall gather<br> The righteous to (God)<br> Most Gracious, like a hand<br> Presented before a king for honours,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_086.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>86.  Wanasooqu almujrimeena il<u>a</u> jahannama wird<u>a</u><b>n</b>    </p>
 <p>86.  And We shall drive<br> The sinners to hell,<br> Like thirsty cattle<br> Driven down to water,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_087.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>87.  L<u>a</u> yamlikoona a<b>l</b>shshaf<u>a</u>AAata ill<u>a</u> mani ittakha<u>th</u>a AAinda a<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>m<u>a</u>ni AAahd<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>87.  None shall have the power<br> Of intercession, but such a one<br> As has received permission (or promise)<br> From (God) Most Gracious.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_088.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>88.  Waq<u>a</u>loo ittakha<u>th</u>a a<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>m<u>a</u>nu walad<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>88.  They say: "(God) Most Gracious<br> Has begotten a son!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_089.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>89.  Laqad ji/tum shay-an idd<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>89.  Indeed ye have put forth<br> A thing most monstrous!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_090.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>90.  Tak<u>a</u>du a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>tu yatafa<u>tt</u>arna minhu watanshaqqu al-ar<u>d</u>u watakhirru aljib<u>a</u>lu hadd<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>90.  At it the skies are ready<br> To burst, the earth<br> To split asunder, and<br> The mountains to fall down<br> In utter ruin,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_091.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>91.  An daAAaw li<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>m<u>a</u>ni walad<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>91.  That they should invoke<br> A son for (God) Most Gracious.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_092.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>92.  Wam<u>a</u> yanbaghee li<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>m<u>a</u>ni an yattakhi<u>th</u>a walad<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>92.  For it is not consonant<br> With the majesty of (God)<br> Most Gracious that He<br> Should beget a son.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_093.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>93.  In kullu man fee a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i ill<u>a</u> <u>a</u>tee a<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>m<u>a</u>ni AAabd<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>93.  Not one of the beings<br> In the heavens and the earth<br> But must come to (God)<br> Most Gracious as a servant.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_094.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>94.  Laqad a<u>hsa</u>hum waAAaddahum AAadd<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>94.  He does take an account<br> Of them (all), and hath<br> Numbered them (all) exactly.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_095.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>95.  Wakulluhum <u>a</u>teehi yawma alqiy<u>a</u>mati fard<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>95.  And everyone of them<br> Will come to Him singly<br> On the Day of Judgment.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_096.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>96.  Inna alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo waAAamiloo a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>li<u>ha</u>ti sayajAAalu lahumu a<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>m<u>a</u>nu wudd<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>96.  On those who believe<br> And work deeds of righteousness,<br> Will (God) Most Gracious<br> Bestow Love.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_097.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>97.  Fa-innam<u>a</u> yassarn<u>a</u>hu bilis<u>a</u>nika litubashshira bihi almuttaqeena watun<u>th</u>ira bihi qawman ludd<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>97.  So have We made<br> The (Qur-ān) easy<br> In thine own tongue,<br> That with it thou mayest give<br>Glad tidings to the righteous,<br> And warnings to people<br> Given to contention.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_098.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>98.  Wakam ahlakn<u>a</u> qablahum min qarnin hal tu<u>h</u>issu minhum min a<u>h</u>adin aw tasmaAAu lahum rikz<u>a</u><b>n</b>    </p>
 <p>98.  But how many (countless)<br> Generations before them<br> Have We destroyed? Canst thou<br> Find a single one of them<br> (Now) or hear. (so much<br> As) a whisper of them?</p>  <div class="filenav">
