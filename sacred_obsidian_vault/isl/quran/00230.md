 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_232.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>232.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> <u>t</u>allaqtumu a<b>l</b>nnis<u>a</u>a fabalaghna ajalahunna fal<u>a</u> taAA<u>d</u>uloohunna an yanki<u>h</u>na azw<u>a</u>jahunna i<u>tha</u> tar<u>ad</u>aw baynahum bi<b>a</b>lmaAAroofi <u>tha</u>lika yooAAa<i><u>th</u></i>u bihi man k<u>a</u>na minkum yu/minu bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi wa<b>a</b>lyawmi al-<u>a</u>khiri <u>tha</u>likum azk<u>a</u> lakum waa<u>t</u>haru wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu yaAAlamu waantum l<u>a</u> taAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>232.  When ye divorce<br> Women, and they fulfil<br> The term of their (’Iddat),<br> Do not prevent them<br> From marrying<br> Their (former) husbands,<br> If they mutually agree<br> On equitable terms.<br> This instruction<br> Is for all amongst you,<br> Who believe in God<br> And the Last Day.<br> That is (the course<br>Making for) most virtue<br> And purity amongst you,<br> And God knows,<br> And ye know not.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_233.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>233.  Wa<b>a</b>lw<u>a</u>lid<u>a</u>tu yur<u>d</u>iAAna awl<u>a</u>dahunna <u>h</u>awlayni k<u>a</u>milayni liman ar<u>a</u>da an yutimma a<b>l</b>rra<u>da</u>AAata waAAal<u>a</u> almawloodi lahu rizquhunna wakiswatuhunna bi<b>a</b>lmaAAroofi l<u>a</u> tukallafu nafsun ill<u>a</u> wusAAah<u>a</u> l<u>a</u> tu<u>da</u>rra w<u>a</u>lidatun biwaladih<u>a</u> wal<u>a</u> mawloodun lahu biwaladihi waAAal<u>a</u> alw<u>a</u>rithi mithlu <u>tha</u>lika fa-in ar<u>a</u>d<u>a</u> fi<u>sa</u>lan AAan tar<u>ad</u>in minhum<u>a</u> watash<u>a</u>wurin fal<u>a</u> jun<u>ah</u>a AAalayhim<u>a</u> wa-in aradtum an tastar<u>d</u>iAAoo awl<u>a</u>dakum fal<u>a</u> jun<u>ah</u>a AAalaykum i<u>tha</u> sallamtum m<u>a</u> <u>a</u>taytum bi<b>a</b>lmaAAroofi wa<b>i</b>ttaqoo All<u>a</u>ha wa<b>i</b>AAlamoo anna All<u>a</u>ha bim<u>a</u> taAAmaloona ba<u>s</u>eer<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>233.  The mothers shall give suck<br> To their offspring<br> For two whole years,<br> If the father desires<br> To complete the term.<br> But he shall bear the cost<br> Of their food and clothing<br> On equitable terms.<br> No soul shall have<br> A burden laid on it<br> Greater than it can bear.<br> No mother shall be<br> Treated unfairly<br> On account of her child.<br> Nor father<br> On account of his child,<br> An heir shall be chargeable<br> In the same way.<br> If they both decide<br> On weaning,<br> By mutual consent,<br> And after due consultation,<br> There is no blame on them.<br> If ye decide<br> On a foster-mother<br> For your offspring,<br> There is no blame on you,<br> Provided ye pay (the mother)<br> What ye offered,<br> On equitable terms.<br> But fear God and know<br> That God sees well<br> What ye do.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_234.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>234.  Wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena yutawaffawna minkum waya<u>th</u>aroona azw<u>a</u>jan yatarabba<u>s</u>na bi-anfusihinna arbaAAata ashhurin waAAashran fa-i<u>tha</u> balaghna ajalahunna fal<u>a</u> jun<u>ah</u>a AAalaykum feem<u>a</u> faAAalna fee anfusihinna bi<b>a</b>lmaAAroofi wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu bim<u>a</u> taAAmaloona khabeer<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>234.  If any of you die<br> And leave widows behind,<br>They shall wait concerning themselves<br> Four months and ten days:<br> When they have fulfilled<br> Their term, there is no blame<br> On you if they dispose<br> Of themselves in a just<br> And reasonable manner.<br> And God is well acquainted<br> With what ye do.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_235.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>235.  Wal<u>a</u> jun<u>ah</u>a AAalaykum feem<u>a</u> AAarra<u>d</u>tum bihi min khi<u>t</u>bati a<b>l</b>nnis<u>a</u>-i aw aknantum fee anfusikum AAalima All<u>a</u>hu annakum sata<u>th</u>kuroonahunna wal<u>a</u>kin l<u>a</u> tuw<u>a</u>AAidoohunna sirran ill<u>a</u> an taqooloo qawlan maAAroofan wal<u>a</u> taAAzimoo AAuqdata a<b>l</b>nnik<u>ah</u>i <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> yablugha alkit<u>a</u>bu ajalahu wa<b>i</b>AAlamoo anna All<u>a</u>ha yaAAlamu m<u>a</u> fee anfusikum fa<b>i</b><u>hth</u>aroohu wa<b>i</b>AAlamoo anna All<u>a</u>ha ghafoorun <u>h</u>aleem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>235.  There is no blame<br> On you if ye make<br> An offer of betrothal<br> Or hold it in your hearts.<br> God knows that ye<br> Cherish them in your hearts:<br> But do not make a secret contract<br> With them except in terms<br> Honourable, nor resolve on the tie<br> Of marriage till the term<br> Prescribed is fulfilled.<br> And know that God<br> Knoweth what is in your hearts,<br> And take heed of Him;<br> And know that God is<br> Oft-forgiving, Most Forbearing.</p> <div class="filenav">
