 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra VI.: An’ām, or Cattle.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 18</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_145.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>145.  Qul l<u>a</u> ajidu feem<u>a</u> oo<u>h</u>iya ilayya mu<u>h</u>arraman AAal<u>a</u> <u>ta</u>AAimin ya<u>t</u>AAamuhu ill<u>a</u> an yakoona maytatan aw daman masfoo<u>h</u>an aw la<u>h</u>ma khinzeerin fa-innahu rijsun aw fisqan ohilla lighayri All<u>a</u>hi bihi famani i<u>dt</u>urra ghayra b<u>a</u>ghin wal<u>a</u> AA<u>a</u>din fa-inna rabbaka ghafoorun ra<u>h</u>eem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>145.  Say: "I find not<br> In the Message received<br> By me by inspiration<br> Any (meat) forbidden<br> To be eaten by one<br> Who wishes to eat it,<br> Unless it be dead meat,<br> Or blood poured forth,<br>Or the flesh of swine,—<br> For it is an abomination—<br> Or, what is impious, (meat)<br> On which a name has been<br> Invoked, other than God's."<br> But (even so), if a person<br> Is forced by necessity,<br> Without wilful disobedience,<br> Nor transgressing due limits,—<br> Thy Lord is Oft-forgiving,<br> Most Merciful.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_146.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>146.  WaAAal<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena h<u>a</u>doo <u>h</u>arramn<u>a</u> kulla <u>th</u>ee <i><u>th</u></i>ufurin wamina albaqari wa<b>a</b>lghanami <u>h</u>arramn<u>a</u> AAalayhim shu<u>h</u>oomahum<u>a</u> ill<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> <u>h</u>amalat <i><u>th</u></i>uhooruhum<u>a</u> awi al<u>h</u>aw<u>a</u>y<u>a</u> aw m<u>a</u> ikhtala<u>t</u>a biAAa<i><u>th</u></i>min <u>tha</u>lika jazayn<u>a</u>hum bibaghyihim wa-inn<u>a</u> la<u>sa</u>diqoon<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>146.  For those who followed<br> The Jewish Law, We forbade<br> Every (animal) with<br> Undivided hoof,<br> And We forbade them<br> The fat of the ox<br> And the sheep, except<br> What adheres to their backs<br> Or their entrails,<br> Or is mixed up<br> With a bone:<br> This in recompense<br> For their wilful disobedience:<br> For We are True<br> (In Our ordinances).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_147.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>147.  Fa-in ka<u>thth</u>abooka faqul rabbukum <u>th</u>oo ra<u>h</u>matin w<u>a</u>siAAatin wal<u>a</u> yuraddu ba/suhu AAani alqawmi almujrimeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>147.  If they accuse thee<br> Of falsehood, say:<br> "Your Lord is full<br> Of mercy all-embracing;<br> But from people in guilt<br> Never will His wrath<br> Be turned back.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_148.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>148.  Sayaqoolu alla<u>th</u>eena ashrakoo law sh<u>a</u>a All<u>a</u>hu m<u>a</u> ashrakn<u>a</u> wal<u>a</u> <u>a</u>b<u>a</u>on<u>a</u> wal<u>a</u> <u>h</u>arramn<u>a</u> min shay-in ka<u>tha</u>lika ka<u>thth</u>aba alla<u>th</u>eena min qablihim <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> <u>tha</u>qoo ba/san<u>a</u> qul hal AAindakum min AAilmin fatukhrijoohu lan<u>a</u> in tattabiAAoona ill<u>a</u> a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i>anna wa-in antum ill<u>a</u> takhru<u>s</u>oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>148.  Those who give partners<br> (To God) will say:<br>"If God had wished,<br> We should not have<br> Given partners to Him,<br> Nor would our fathers;<br> Nor should we have had<br> Any taboos." So did<br> Their ancestors argue<br> Falsely, until they tasted<br> Of Our wrath. Say:<br> "Have ye any (certain)<br> Knowledge? If so, produce<br> It before us. Ye follow<br> Nothing but conjecture:<br> Ye do nothing but lie."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_149.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>149.  Qul falill<u>a</u>hi al<u>h</u>ujjatu alb<u>a</u>lighatu falaw sh<u>a</u>a lahad<u>a</u>kum ajmaAAeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>149.  Say: "With God is the argument<br> That reaches home: if it had<br> Been His Will, He could<br> Indeed have guided you all."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_150.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>150.  Qul halumma shuhad<u>a</u>akumu alla<u>th</u>eena yashhadoona anna All<u>a</u>ha <u>h</u>arrama h<u>atha</u> fa-in shahidoo fal<u>a</u> tashhad maAAahum wal<u>a</u> tattabiAA ahw<u>a</u>a alla<u>th</u>eena ka<u>thth</u>aboo bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin<u>a</u> wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena l<u>a</u> yu/minoona bi<b>a</b>l-<u>a</u>khirati wahum birabbihim yaAAdiloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>150.  Say: "Bring forward your witnesses<br> To prove that God did<br> Forbid so and so." If they<br> Bring such witnesses,<br> Be not thou amongst them:<br> Nor follow thou the vain<br> Desires of such as treat<br> Our Signs as falsehoods,<br> And such as believe not<br> In the Hereafter: for they<br> Hold others as equal<br> With their Guardian-Lord.</p> <div class="filenav">
