 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra V.: Māïda, or The Table Spread.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 12</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_087.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>87.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo l<u>a</u> tu<u>h</u>arrimoo <u>t</u>ayyib<u>a</u>ti m<u>a</u> a<u>h</u>alla All<u>a</u>hu lakum wal<u>a</u> taAAtadoo inna All<u>a</u>ha l<u>a</u> yu<u>h</u>ibbu almuAAtadeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>87.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">90</font></span> O ye who believe!<br> Make not unlawful<br>The good things which God<br> Hath made lawful for you,<br> But commit no excess:<br> For God loveth not<br> Those given to excess.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_088.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>88.  Wakuloo mimm<u>a</u> razaqakumu All<u>a</u>hu <u>h</u>al<u>a</u>lan <u>t</u>ayyiban wa<b>i</b>ttaqoo All<u>a</u>ha alla<u>th</u>ee antum bihi mu/minoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>88.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">91</font></span> Eat of the things which<br> God hath provided for you,<br> Lawful and good; but fear<br> God, in Whom ye believe.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_089.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>89.  L<u>a</u> yu-<u>a</u>khi<u>th</u>ukumu All<u>a</u>hu bi<b>a</b>llaghwi fee aym<u>a</u>nikum wal<u>a</u>kin yu-<u>a</u>khi<u>th</u>ukum bim<u>a</u> AAaqqadtumu al-aym<u>a</u>na fakaff<u>a</u>ratuhu i<u>t</u>AA<u>a</u>mu AAasharati mas<u>a</u>keena min awsa<u>t</u>i m<u>a</u> tu<u>t</u>AAimoona ahleekum aw kiswatuhum aw ta<u>h</u>reeru raqabatin faman lam yajid fa<u>s</u>iy<u>a</u>mu thal<u>a</u>thati ayy<u>a</u>min <u>tha</u>lika kaff<u>a</u>ratu aym<u>a</u>nikum i<u>tha</u> <u>h</u>alaftum wa<b>i</b><u>h</u>fa<i><u>th</u></i>oo aym<u>a</u>nakum ka<u>tha</u>lika yubayyinu All<u>a</u>hu lakum <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tihi laAAallakum tashkuroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>89.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">92</font></span> God will not call you<br> To account for what is<br> Futile in your oaths,<br> But He will call you<br> To account for your deliberate<br> Oaths: for expiation, feed<br> Ten indigent persons,<br> On a scale of the average<br> For the food of your families;<br> Or clothe them; or give<br> A slave his freedom.<br> If that is beyond your means,<br> Fast for three days.<br> That is the expiation<br> For the oaths ye have sworn.<br> But keep to your oaths.<br> Thus doth God make clear<br> To you His Signs, that ye<br> May be grateful.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_090.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>90.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo innam<u>a</u> alkhamru wa<b>a</b>lmaysiru wa<b>a</b>l-an<u>sa</u>bu wa<b>a</b>l-azl<u>a</u>mu rijsun min AAamali a<b>l</b>shshay<u>ta</u>ni fa<b>i</b>jtaniboohu laAAallakum tufli<u>h</u>oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>90.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">93</font></span> O ye who believe!<br> Intoxicants and gambling,<br>(Dedication of) stones,<br> And (divination by) arrows,<br> Are an abomination,—<br> Of Satan's handiwork:<br> Eschew such (abomination),<br> That ye may prosper.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_091.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>91.  Innam<u>a</u> yureedu a<b>l</b>shshay<u>ta</u>nu an yooqiAAa baynakumu alAAad<u>a</u>wata wa<b>a</b>lbagh<u>da</u>a fee alkhamri wa<b>a</b>lmaysiri waya<u>s</u>uddakum AAan <u>th</u>ikri All<u>a</u>hi waAAani a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>al<u>a</u>ti fahal antum muntahoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>91.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">94</font></span> Satan's plan is (but)<br> To excite enmity and hatred<br> Between you, with intoxicants<br> And gambling, and hinder you<br> From the remembrance<br> Of God, and from prayer:<br> Will ye not then abstain?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_092.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>92.  Waa<u>t</u>eeAAoo All<u>a</u>ha waa<u>t</u>eeAAoo a<b>l</b>rrasoola wa<b>i</b><u>hth</u>aroo fa-in tawallaytum fa<b>i</b>AAlamoo annam<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> rasoolin<u>a</u> albal<u>a</u>ghu almubeen<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>92.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">95</font></span> Obey God, and obey the Apostle,<br> And beware (of evil):<br> If ye do turn back,<br> Know ye that it is<br> Our Apostle's duty<br> To proclaim (the Message)<br> In the clearest manner.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_093.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>93.  Laysa AAal<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo waAAamiloo a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>li<u>ha</u>ti jun<u>ah</u>un feem<u>a</u> <u>t</u>aAAimoo i<u>tha</u> m<u>a</u> ittaqaw wa<u>a</u>manoo waAAamiloo a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>li<u>ha</u>ti thumma ittaqaw wa<u>a</u>manoo thumma ittaqaw waa<u>h</u>sanoo wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu yu<u>h</u>ibbu almu<u>h</u>sineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>93.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">96</font></span> On those who believe<br> And do deeds of righteousness<br> There is no blame<br> For what they ate (in the past),<br> When they guard themselves<br> From evil, and believe,<br> And do deeds of righteousness,—<br> (Or) again, guard themselves<br> From evil and believe,—<br> (Or) again, guard themselves<br> From evil and do good.<br>For God loveth those<br> Who do good.</p> <div class="filenav">
