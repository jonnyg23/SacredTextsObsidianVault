 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XI.: Hūd (The Prophet Hūd).</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 5</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_050.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>50.  Wa-il<u>a</u> AA<u>a</u>din akh<u>a</u>hum hoodan q<u>a</u>la y<u>a</u> qawmi oAAbudoo All<u>a</u>ha m<u>a</u> lakum min il<u>a</u>hin ghayruhu in antum ill<u>a</u> muftaroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>50.  To the ’Ād People<br> (We sent) Hūd, one<br> Of their own brethren.<br> He said: "O my people!<br> Worship God! ye have<br> No other god but Him.<br> (Your other gods) ye do nothing<br> But invent!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_051.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>51.  Y<u>a</u> qawmi l<u>a</u> as-alukum AAalayhi ajran in ajriya ill<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>ee fa<u>t</u>aranee afal<u>a</u> taAAqiloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>51.  "O my people! I ask of you<br> No reward for this (Message).<br> My reward is from none<br> But Him who created me:<br> Will ye not then understand?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_052.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>52.  Way<u>a</u> qawmi istaghfiroo rabbakum thumma tooboo ilayhi yursili a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>a AAalaykum midr<u>a</u>ran wayazidkum quwwatan il<u>a</u> quwwatikum wal<u>a</u> tatawallaw mujrimeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>52.  "And O my people! Ask<br> Forgiveness of your Lord,<br>And turn to Him (in repentance):<br> He will send you the skies<br> Pouring abundant rain,<br> And add strength<br> To your strength:<br> So turn ye not back<br> In sin!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_053.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>53.  Q<u>a</u>loo y<u>a</u> hoodu m<u>a</u> ji/tan<u>a</u> bibayyinatin wam<u>a</u> na<u>h</u>nu bit<u>a</u>rikee <u>a</u>lihatin<u>a</u> AAan qawlika wam<u>a</u> na<u>h</u>nu laka bimu/mineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>53.  They said: "O Hūd!<br> No Clear (Sign) hast thou<br> Brought us, and we are not<br> The ones to desert our gods<br> On thy word! Nor shall we<br> Believe in thee!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_054.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>54.  In naqoolu ill<u>a</u> iAAtar<u>a</u>ka baAA<u>d</u>u <u>a</u>lihatin<u>a</u> bisoo-in q<u>a</u>la innee oshhidu All<u>a</u>ha wa<b>i</b>shhadoo annee baree-on mimm<u>a</u> tushrikoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>54.  "We say nothing but that<br> (Perhaps) some of our gods<br> May have seized thee<br> With imbecility." He said:<br> "I call God to witness,<br> And do ye bear witness,<br> That I am free from the sin<br> Of ascribing, to Him,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_055.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>55.  Min doonihi fakeedoonee jameeAAan thumma l<u>a</u> tun<i><u>th</u></i>iroon<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>55.  "Other gods as partners!<br> So scheme (your worst) against me,<br> All of you, and give me<br> No respite.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_056.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>56.  Innee tawakkaltu AAal<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi rabbee warabbikum m<u>a</u> min d<u>a</u>bbatin ill<u>a</u> huwa <u>a</u>khi<u>th</u>un bin<u>as</u>iyatih<u>a</u> inna rabbee AAal<u>a</u> <u>s</u>ir<u>at</u>in mustaqeem<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>56.  "I put my trust in God,<br> My Lord and your Lord!<br> There is not a moving<br> Creature, but He hath<br> Grasp of its fore-lock.<br> Verily, it is my Lord<br> That is on a straight Path.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_057.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>57.  Fa-in tawallaw faqad ablaghtukum m<u>a</u> orsiltu bihi ilaykum wayastakhlifu rabbee qawman ghayrakum wal<u>a</u> ta<u>d</u>urroonahu shay-an inna rabbee AAal<u>a</u> kulli shay-in <u>h</u>afee<i><u>th</u></i><b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>57.  "If ye turn away,—<br> I (at least) have conveyed<br> The Message with which I<br> Was sent to you. My Lord<br> Will make another People<br> To succeed you, and you<br> Will not harm Him<br> In the least. For my Lord<br> Hath care and watch<br> Over all things."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_058.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>58.  Walamm<u>a</u> j<u>a</u>a amrun<u>a</u> najjayn<u>a</u> hoodan wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo maAAahu bira<u>h</u>matin minn<u>a</u> wanajjayn<u>a</u>hum min AAa<u>tha</u>bin ghalee<i><u>th</u></i><b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>58.  So when Our decree<br> Issued, We saved Hūd<br> And those who believed<br> With him, by (special) Grace<br> From Ourselves: We saved them<br> From a severe Penalty.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_059.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>59.  Watilka AA<u>a</u>dun ja<u>h</u>adoo bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>ti rabbihim waAAa<u>s</u>aw rusulahu wa<b>i</b>ttabaAAoo amra kulli jabb<u>a</u>rin AAaneed<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>59.  Such were the ’Ād People:<br> They rejected the Signs<br>Of their Lord and Cherisher;<br> Disobeyed His Apostles;<br> And followed the command<br> Of every powerful, obstinate<br> Transgressor.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_060.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>60.  WaotbiAAoo fee h<u>ath</u>ihi a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> laAAnatan wayawma alqiy<u>a</u>mati al<u>a</u> inna AA<u>a</u>dan kafaroo rabbahum al<u>a</u> buAAdan liAA<u>a</u>din qawmi hood<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>60.  And they were pursued<br> By a Curse in this Life,<br> And on the Day of Judgment.<br> Ah! Behold! For the ’Ād<br> Rejected their Lord and Cherisher<br> Ah! Behold! Removed (from sight)<br> Were ’Ād the People of Hūd!</p> <div class="filenav">
