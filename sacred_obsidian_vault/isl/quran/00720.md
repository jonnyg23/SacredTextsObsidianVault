 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_158.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>158.  Qul y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>su innee rasoolu All<u>a</u>hi ilaykum jameeAAan alla<u>th</u>ee lahu mulku a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i l<u>a</u> il<u>a</u>ha ill<u>a</u> huwa yu<u>h</u>yee wayumeetu fa<u>a</u>minoo bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi warasoolihi a<b>l</b>nnabiyyi al-ommiyyi alla<u>th</u>ee yu/minu bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi wakalim<u>a</u>tihi wa<b>i</b>ttabiAAoohu laAAallakum tahtadoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>158.  Say: "O men! I am sent<br> Unto you all, as the Apostle<br> Of God, to Whom belongeth<br> The dominion of the heavens<br> And the earth: there is no god<br> But He: it is He that giveth<br> Both life and death. So believe<br> In God and His Apostle,<br> The unlettered Prophet,<br> Who believeth in God<br> And His Words: follow him<br> That (so) ye may be guided."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_159.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>159.  Wamin qawmi moos<u>a</u> ommatun yahdoona bi<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>aqqi wabihi yaAAdiloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>159.  Of the people of Moses<br> There is a section<br> Who guide and do justice<br> In the light of truth.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_160.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>160.  Waqa<u>tt</u>aAAn<u>a</u>humu ithnatay AAashrata asb<u>at</u>an omaman waaw<u>h</u>ayn<u>a</u> il<u>a</u> moos<u>a</u> i<u>th</u>i istasq<u>a</u>hu qawmuhu ani i<u>d</u>rib biAAa<u>sa</u>ka al<u>h</u>ajara fa<b>i</b>nbajasat minhu ithnat<u>a</u> AAashrata AAaynan qad AAalima kullu on<u>a</u>sin mashrabahum wa<i><u>th</u></i>allaln<u>a</u> AAalayhimu algham<u>a</u>ma waanzaln<u>a</u> AAalayhimu almanna wa<b>al</b>ssalw<u>a</u> kuloo min <u>t</u>ayyib<u>a</u>ti m<u>a</u> razaqn<u>a</u>kum wam<u>a</u> <i><u>th</u></i>alamoon<u>a</u> wal<u>a</u>kin k<u>a</u>noo anfusahum ya<i><u>th</u></i>limoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>160.  We divided them into twelve Tribes<br> Or nations. We directed<br> Moses by inspiration,<br> When his (thirsty) people asked<br> Him for water: "Strike the rock<br> With thy staff": out of it<br> There gushed forth twelve springs:<br> Each group knew its own place<br> For water. We gave them<br> The shade of clouds, and sent<br> Down to them manna and quails,<br> (Saying): "Eat of the good things<br> We have provided for you":<br> (But they rebelled); to Us<br> They did no harm, but<br> They harmed their own souls.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_161.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>161.  Wa-i<u>th</u> qeela lahumu oskunoo h<u>ath</u>ihi alqaryata wakuloo minh<u>a</u> <u>h</u>aythu shi/tum waqooloo <u>h</u>i<u>tt</u>atun wa<b>o</b>dkhuloo alb<u>a</u>ba sujjadan naghfir lakum kha<u>t</u>ee-<u>a</u>tikum sanazeedu almu<u>h</u>sineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>161.  And remember it was<br> Said to them:<br> "Dwell in this town<br> And eat therein as ye wish,<br> But say the word of humility<br> And enter the gate<br> In a posture of humility:<br> We shall forgive you<br> Your faults; We shall increase<br> (The portion of) those who do good."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_162.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>162.  Fabaddala alla<u>th</u>eena <i><u>th</u></i>alamoo minhum qawlan ghayra alla<u>th</u>ee qeela lahum faarsaln<u>a</u> AAalayhim rijzan mina a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>-i bim<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo ya<i><u>th</u></i>limoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>162.  But the transgressors among them<br> Changed the word from that<br> Which had been given them<br> So we sent on them<br> A plague from heaven.<br> For that they repeatedly transgressed.</p> <div class="filenav">
