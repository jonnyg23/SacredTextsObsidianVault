 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra IV.: Nisāa, or The Women.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 12</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_088.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>88.  Fam<u>a</u> lakum fee almun<u>a</u>fiqeena fi-atayni wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu arkasahum bim<u>a</u> kasaboo atureedoona an tahdoo man a<u>d</u>alla All<u>a</u>hu waman yu<u>d</u>lili All<u>a</u>hu falan tajida lahu sabeel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>88.  Why should ye be<br> Divided into two parties<br>About the Hypocrites?<br> God hath upset them<br> For their (evil) deeds.<br> Would ye guide those<br> Whom God hath thrown<br> Out of the Way? For those<br> Whom God hath thrown<br> Out of the Way, never<br> Shalt thou find the Way.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_089.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>89.  Waddoo law takfuroona kam<u>a</u> kafaroo fatakoonoona saw<u>a</u>an fal<u>a</u> tattakhi<u>th</u>oo minhum awliy<u>a</u>a <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> yuh<u>a</u>jiroo fee sabeeli All<u>a</u>hi fa-in tawallaw fakhu<u>th</u>oohum wa<b>o</b>qtuloohum <u>h</u>aythu wajadtumoohum wal<u>a</u> tattakhi<u>th</u>oo minhum waliyyan wal<u>a</u> na<u>s</u>eer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>89.  They but wish that ye<br> Should reject Faith,<br> As they do, and thus be<br> On the same footing (as they):<br> But take not friends<br> From their ranks<br> Until they flee<br> In the way of God<br> (From what is forbidden).<br> But if they turn renegades,<br> Seize them and slay them<br> Wherever ye find them;<br> And (in any case) take<br> No friends or helpers<br> From their ranks:—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_090.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>90.  Ill<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena ya<u>s</u>iloona il<u>a</u> qawmin baynakum wabaynahum meeth<u>a</u>qun aw j<u>a</u>ookum <u>h</u>a<u>s</u>irat <u>s</u>udooruhum an yuq<u>a</u>tilookum aw yuq<u>a</u>tiloo qawmahum walaw sh<u>a</u>a All<u>a</u>hu lasalla<u>t</u>ahum AAalaykum falaq<u>a</u>talookum fa-ini iAAtazalookum falam yuq<u>a</u>tilookum waalqaw ilaykumu a<b>l</b>ssalama fam<u>a</u> jaAAala All<u>a</u>hu lakum AAalayhim sabeel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>90.  Except those who join<br> A group between whom<br> And you there is a treaty<br>(Of peace), or those who approach<br> You with hearts restraining<br> Them from fighting you<br> As well as fighting their own<br> People. If God had pleased,<br> He could have given them<br> Power over you, and they<br> Would have fought you:<br> Therefore if they withdraw<br> From you but fight you not,<br> And (instead) send you<br> (Guarantees of) peace, then God<br> Hath opened no way<br> For you (to war against them).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_091.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>91.  Satajidoona <u>a</u>khareena yureedoona an ya/manookum waya/manoo qawmahum kulla m<u>a</u> ruddoo il<u>a</u> alfitnati orkisoo feeh<u>a</u> fa-in lam yaAAtazilookum wayulqoo ilaykumu a<b>l</b>ssalama wayakuffoo aydiyahum fakhu<u>th</u>oohum wa<b>o</b>qtuloohum <u>h</u>aythu thaqiftumoohum waol<u>a</u>-ikum jaAAaln<u>a</u> lakum AAalayhim sul<u>ta</u>nan mubeen<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>91.  Others you will find<br> That wish to gain<br> Your confidence as well<br> As that of their people:<br> Every time they are sent back<br> To temptation, they succumb<br> Thereto: if they withdraw not<br> From you nor give you (guarantees)<br> Of peace besides<br> Restraining their hands,<br> Seize them and slay them<br> Wherever ye get them:<br> In their case<br> We have provided you<br> With a clear argument<br> Against them.</p> <div class="filenav">
