 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XCII.: Lail, or The Night.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/092/092_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  Wa<b>a</b>llayli i<u>tha</u> yaghsh<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>1.  By the Night as it<br> Conceals (the light);</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/092/092_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  Wa<b>al</b>nnah<u>a</u>ri i<u>tha</u> tajall<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>2.  By the Day as it<br> Appears in glory;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/092/092_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  Wam<u>a</u> khalaqa a<b>l</b><u>thth</u>akara wa<b>a</b>l-onth<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>3.  By (the mystery of)<br> The creation of male<br> And female;—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/092/092_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  Inna saAAyakum lashatt<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>4.  Verily, (the ends) ye<br> Strive for are diverse.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/092/092_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  Faamm<u>a</u> man aAA<u>ta</u> wa<b>i</b>ttaq<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>5.  So he who gives<br> (In charity) and fears (God),</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/092/092_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  Wa<u>s</u>addaqa bi<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>usn<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>6.  And (in all sincerity)<br> Testifies to the Best,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/092/092_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  Fasanuyassiruhu lilyusr<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>7.  We will indeed<br> Make smooth for him<br> The path to Bliss,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/092/092_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  Waamm<u>a</u> man bakhila wa<b>i</b>staghn<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>8.  But he who is<br> A greedy miser<br> And thinks himself<br> Self-sufficient,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/092/092_009.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>9.  Waka<u>thth</u>aba bi<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>usn<u>a</u>    </p>
 <p>9.  And gives the lie<br> To the Best,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/092/092_010.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>10.  Fasanuyassiruhu lilAAusr<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>10.  We will indeed<br> Make smooth for him<br> The Path to Misery;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/092/092_011.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>11.  Wam<u>a</u> yughnee AAanhu m<u>a</u>luhu i<u>tha</u> taradd<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>11.  Nor will his wealth<br> Profit him when he<br> Falls headlong (into the Pit).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/092/092_012.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>12.  Inna AAalayn<u>a</u> lalhud<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>12.  Verily We take<br> Upon Ourselves to guide,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/092/092_013.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>13.  Wa-inna lan<u>a</u> lal-<u>a</u>khirata wa<b>a</b>l-ool<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>13.  And verily unto Us<br> (Belong) the End<br> And the Beginning,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/092/092_014.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>14.  Faan<u>th</u>artukum n<u>a</u>ran tala<i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>14.  Therefore do I warn you<br> Of a Fire blazing fiercely;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/092/092_015.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>15.  L<u>a</u> ya<u>s</u>l<u>a</u>h<u>a</u> ill<u>a</u> al-ashq<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>15.  None shall reach it<br> But those most unfortunate ones</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/092/092_016.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>16.  Alla<u>th</u>ee ka<u>thth</u>aba watawall<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>16.  Who give the lie to Truth<br> And turn their backs.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/092/092_017.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>17.  Wasayujannabuh<u>a</u> al-atq<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>17.  But those most devoted<br> To God shall be<br> Removed far from it,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/092/092_018.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>18.  Alla<u>th</u>ee yu/tee m<u>a</u>lahu yatazakk<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>18.  Those who spend their wealth<br> For increase in self-purification,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/092/092_019.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>19.  Wam<u>a</u> li-a<u>h</u>adin AAindahu min niAAmatin tujz<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>19.  And have in their minds<br> No favour from anyone<br> For which a reward<br> Is expected in return,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/092/092_020.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>20.  Ill<u>a</u> ibtigh<u>a</u>a wajhi rabbihi al-aAAl<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>20.  But only the desire<br> To seek for the Countenance<br> Of their Lord Most High;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/092/092_021.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>21.  Walasawfa yar<u>da</u>      </p>
 <p>21.  And soon will they<br> Attain (complete) satisfaction.</p>  <div class="filenav">
