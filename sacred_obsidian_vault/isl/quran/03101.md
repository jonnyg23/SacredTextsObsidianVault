 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXXI.: Luqmān (the Wise).</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/031/031_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  Alif-l<u>a</u>m-meem   </p>
 <p>1.  A. L. M.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/031/031_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  Tilka <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tu alkit<u>a</u>bi al<u>h</u>akeem<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>2.  These are Verses<br> Of the Wise Book,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/031/031_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  Hudan wara<u>h</u>matan lilmu<u>h</u>sineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>3.  A Guide and a Mercy<br> To the Doers of Good,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/031/031_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  Alla<u>th</u>eena yuqeemoona a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>al<u>a</u>ta wayu/toona a<b>l</b>zzak<u>a</u>ta wahum bi<b>a</b>l-<u>a</u>khirati hum yooqinoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>4.  Those who establish regular Prayer,<br> And give regular Charity,<br> And have (in their hearts)<br> The assurance of the Hereafter.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/031/031_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  Ol<u>a</u>-ika AAal<u>a</u> hudan min rabbihim waol<u>a</u>-ika humu almufli<u>h</u>oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>5.  These are on (true) guidance<br> From their Lord; and these<br> Are the ones who will prosper.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/031/031_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  Wamina a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si man yashtaree lahwa al<u>h</u>adeethi liyu<u>d</u>illa AAan sabeeli All<u>a</u>hi bighayri AAilmin wayattakhi<u>th</u>ah<u>a</u> huzuwan ol<u>a</u>-ika lahum AAa<u>tha</u>bun muheen<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>6.  But there are, among men,<br> Those who purchase idle tales,'<br>Without knowledge (or meaning),<br> To mislead (men) from the Path<br> Of God and throw ridicule<br> (On the Path): for such<br> There will be a humiliating<br> Penalty.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/031/031_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> tutl<u>a</u> AAalayhi <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tun<u>a</u> wall<u>a</u> mustakbiran kaan lam yasmaAAh<u>a</u> kaanna fee o<u>th</u>unayhi waqran fabashshirhu biAAa<u>tha</u>bin aleem<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>7.  When Our Signs are rehearsed<br> To such a one, he turns<br> Away in arrogance, as if<br> He heard them not, as if<br> There were deafness in both<br> His ears: announce to him<br> A grievous Penalty.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/031/031_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  Inna alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo waAAamiloo a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>li<u>ha</u>ti lahum jann<u>a</u>tu a<b>l</b>nnaAAeem<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>8.  For those who believe<br> And work righteous deeds,<br> There will be Gardens<br> Of Bliss,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/031/031_009.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>9.  Kh<u>a</u>lideena feeh<u>a</u> waAAda All<u>a</u>hi <u>h</u>aqqan wahuwa alAAazeezu al<u>h</u>akeem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>9.  To dwell therein. The promise<br> Of God is true: and He<br> Is Exalted in power, Wise.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/031/031_010.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>10.  Khalaqa a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti bighayri AAamadin tarawnah<u>a</u> waalq<u>a</u> fee al-ar<u>d</u>i raw<u>a</u>siya an tameeda bikum wabaththa feeh<u>a</u> min kulli d<u>a</u>bbatin waanzaln<u>a</u> mina a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>-i m<u>a</u>an faanbatn<u>a</u> feeh<u>a</u> min kulli zawjin kareem<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>10.  He created the heavens<br> Without any pillars that ye<br> Can see; He set<br> On the earth mountains<br> Standing firm, lest it<br> Should shake with you;<br> And He scattered through it<br> Beasts of all kinds.<br> We send down rain<br> From the sky, and produce<br>On the earth every kind.<br> Of noble creature, in pairs.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/031/031_011.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>11.  H<u>atha</u> khalqu All<u>a</u>hi faaroonee m<u>atha</u> khalaqa alla<u>th</u>eena min doonihi bali a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limoona fee <u>d</u>al<u>a</u>lin mubeen<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>11.  Such is the Creation of God:<br> Now show Me what is there<br> That others besides Him<br> Have created: nay, but<br> The Transgressors are<br> In manifest error.</p> <div class="filenav">
