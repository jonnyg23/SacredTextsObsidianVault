 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra VII.: A’rāf, or the Heights</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 12</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_094.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>94.  Wam<u>a</u> arsaln<u>a</u> fee qaryatin min nabiyyin ill<u>a</u> akha<u>th</u>n<u>a</u> ahlah<u>a</u> bi<b>a</b>lba/s<u>a</u>-i wa<b>al</b><u>dd</u>arr<u>a</u>-i laAAallahum ya<u>dd</u>arraAAoon<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>94.  Whenever We sent a prophet<br> To a town, We took up<br> Its people in suffering<br> And adversity, in order<br> That they might learn humility.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_095.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>95.  Thumma baddaln<u>a</u> mak<u>a</u>na a<b>l</b>ssayyi-ati al<u>h</u>asanata <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> AAafaw waq<u>a</u>loo qad massa <u>a</u>b<u>a</u>an<u>a</u> a<b>l</b><u>dd</u>arr<u>a</u>o wa<b>al</b>ssarr<u>a</u>o faakha<u>th</u>n<u>a</u>hum baghtatan wahum l<u>a</u> yashAAuroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>95.  Then We changed their suffering<br> Into prosperity, until they grew<br> And multiplied, and began<br> To say: "Our fathers (too)<br> Were touched by suffering<br> And affluence"… Behold!<br> We called them to account<br> Of a sudden, while they<br> Realised not (their peril).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_096.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>96.  Walaw anna ahla alqur<u>a</u> <u>a</u>manoo wa<b>i</b>ttaqaw lafata<u>h</u>n<u>a</u> AAalayhim barak<u>a</u>tin mina a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>-i wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i wal<u>a</u>kin ka<u>thth</u>aboo faakha<u>th</u>n<u>a</u>hum bim<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo yaksiboon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>96.  If the people of the towns<br> Had but believed and feared<br> God, We should indeed<br> Have opened out to them<br>(All kinds of) blessings<br> From heaven and earth;<br> But they rejected (the truth),<br> And We brought them<br> To book for their misdeeds.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_097.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>97.  Afaamina ahlu alqur<u>a</u> an ya/tiyahum ba/sun<u>a</u> bay<u>a</u>tan wahum n<u>a</u>-imoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>97.  Did the people of the towns<br> Feel secure against the coming<br> Of Our wrath by night<br> While they were asleep?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_098.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>98.  Awa amina ahlu alqur<u>a</u> an ya/tiyahum ba/sun<u>a</u> <u>d</u>u<u>h</u>an wahum yalAAaboon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>98.  Or else did they feel<br> Secure against its coming<br> In broad daylight while they<br> Played about (care-free)?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_099.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>99.  Afaaminoo makra All<u>a</u>hi fal<u>a</u> ya/manu makra All<u>a</u>hi ill<u>a</u> alqawmu alkh<u>a</u>siroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>99.  Did they then feel secure<br> Against the Plan of God?—<br> But no one can feel<br> Secure from the Plan<br> Of God, except those<br> (Doomed) to ruin!</p> <div class="filenav">
