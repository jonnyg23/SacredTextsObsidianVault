 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra CXIII: Falaq, or The Dawn.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/113/113_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  Qul aAAoo<u>th</u>u birabbi alfalaq<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>1.  Say: I seek refuge<br> With the Lord of the Dawn,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/113/113_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  Min sharri m<u>a</u> khalaq<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>2.  From the mischief<br> Of created things;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/113/113_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  Wamin sharri gh<u>a</u>siqin i<u>tha</u> waqab<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>3.  From the mischief<br> Of Darkness as it overspreads;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/113/113_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  Wamin sharri a<b>l</b>nnaff<u>a</u>th<u>a</u>ti fee alAAuqad<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>4.  From the mischief<br> Of those who practise<br> Secret Arts:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/113/113_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  Wamin sharri <u>ha</u>sidin i<u>tha</u> <u>h</u>asad<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>5.  And from the mischief<br> Of the envious one<br> As he practises envy.</p>  <div class="filenav">
