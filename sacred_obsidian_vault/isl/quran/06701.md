 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra LXVII.: Mulk, or Dominion.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/067/067_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  Tab<u>a</u>raka alla<u>th</u>ee biyadihi almulku wahuwa AAal<u>a</u> kulli shay-in qadeer<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>1.  Blessed be He<br> In Whose hands<br> Is Dominion;<br> And He over all things<br> Hath Power;—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/067/067_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  Alla<u>th</u>ee khalaqa almawta wa<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>ay<u>a</u>ta liyabluwakum ayyukum a<u>h</u>sanu AAamalan wahuwa alAAazeezu alghafoor<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>2.  He Who created Death<br> And Life, that He<br> May try which of you<br> Is best in deed:<br> And He is the Exalted<br> In Might, Oft-Forgiving;—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/067/067_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  Alla<u>th</u>ee khalaqa sabAAa sam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>tin <u>t</u>ib<u>a</u>qan m<u>a</u> tar<u>a</u> fee khalqi a<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>m<u>a</u>ni min taf<u>a</u>wutin fa<b>i</b>rjiAAi alba<u>s</u>ara hal tar<u>a</u> min fu<u>t</u>oor<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>3.  He Who created<br> The seven heavens<br> One above another:<br> No want of proportion<br> Wilt thou see<br> In the Creation<br> Of (God) Most Gracious.<br> So turn thy vision again:<br> Seest thou any flaw?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/067/067_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  Thumma irjiAAi alba<u>s</u>ara karratayni yanqalib ilayka alba<u>s</u>aru kh<u>a</u>si-an wahuwa <u>h</u>aseer<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>4.  Again turn thy vision<br> A second time: (thy) vision<br> Will come back to thee<br> Dull and discomfited,<br> In a state worn out.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/067/067_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  Walaqad zayyann<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>a a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> bima<u>sa</u>bee<u>h</u>a wajaAAaln<u>a</u>h<u>a</u> rujooman li<b>l</b>shshay<u>at</u>eeni waaAAtadn<u>a</u> lahum AAa<u>tha</u>ba a<b>l</b>ssaAAeer<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>5.  And We have,<br> (From of old),<br> Adorned the lowest heaven<br> With Lamps, and We<br> Have made such (Lamps)<br> (As) missiles to drive<br> Away the Evil Ones,<br> And have prepared for them<br> The Penalty<br> Of the Blazing Fire.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/067/067_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  Walilla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo birabbihim AAa<u>tha</u>bu jahannama wabi/sa alma<u>s</u>eer<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>6.  For those who reject<br> Their Lord (and Cherisher)<br>Is the Penalty of Hell:<br> And evil is (such) destination.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/067/067_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  I<u>tha</u> olqoo feeh<u>a</u> samiAAoo lah<u>a</u> shaheeqan wahiya tafoor<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>7.  When they are cast therein,<br> They will hear<br> The (terrible) drawing in<br> Of its breath<br> Even as it blazes forth,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/067/067_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  Tak<u>a</u>du tamayyazu mina alghay<i><u>th</u></i>i kullam<u>a</u> olqiya feeh<u>a</u> fawjun saalahum khazanatuh<u>a</u> alam ya/tikum na<u>th</u>eer<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>8.  Almost bursting with fury:<br> Every time a Group<br> Is cast therein, its Keepers<br> Will ask, "Did no Warner<br> Come to you?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/067/067_009.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>9.  Q<u>a</u>loo bal<u>a</u> qad j<u>a</u>an<u>a</u> na<u>th</u>eerun faka<u>thth</u>abn<u>a</u> waquln<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> nazzala All<u>a</u>hu min shay-in in antum ill<u>a</u> fee <u>d</u>al<u>a</u>lin kabeer<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>9.  They will say: "Yes indeed;<br> A Warner did come to us,<br> But we rejected him<br> And said, "God never<br> Sent down any (Message):<br> Ye are innothing but<br> An egregious delusion!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/067/067_010.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>10.  Waq<u>a</u>loo law kunn<u>a</u> nasmaAAu aw naAAqilu m<u>a</u> kunn<u>a</u> fee a<u>s</u>-<u>ha</u>bi a<b>l</b>ssaAAeer<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>10.  They will further say:<br> "Had we but listened<br> Or used our intelligence,<br> We should not (now)<br>Be among the Companions<br> Of the Blazing Fire!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/067/067_011.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>11.  Fa<b>i</b>AAtarafoo bi<u>th</u>anbihim fasu<u>h</u>qan li-a<u>s</u>-<u>ha</u>bi a<b>l</b>ssaAAeer<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>11.  They will then confess<br> Their sins: but far<br> Will be (Forgiveness)<br> From the Companions<br> Of the Blazing Fire!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/067/067_012.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>12.  Inna alla<u>th</u>eena yakhshawna rabbahum bi<b>a</b>lghaybi lahum maghfiratun waajrun kabeer<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>12.  As for those who<br> Fear their Lord unseen,<br> For them is Forgiveness<br> And a great Reward.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/067/067_013.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>13.  Waasirroo qawlakum awi ijharoo bihi innahu AAaleemun bi<u>tha</u>ti a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>udoor<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>13.  And whether ye hide<br> Your word or publish it,<br> He certainly has (full) knowledge,<br> Of the secrets of (all) hearts.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/067/067_014.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>14.  Al<u>a</u> yaAAlamu man khalaqa wahuwa alla<u>t</u>eefu alkhabeer<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>14.  Should He not know,—<br> He that created?<br> And He is the One<br> That understands the finest<br> Mysteries (and) is<br> Well-acquainted (with them).</p> <div class="filenav">
