 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_060.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>60.  Innam<u>a</u> a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>adaq<u>a</u>tu lilfuqar<u>a</u>-i wa<b>a</b>lmas<u>a</u>keeni wa<b>a</b>lAA<u>a</u>mileena AAalayh<u>a</u> wa<b>a</b>lmu-allafati quloobuhum wafee a<b>l</b>rriq<u>a</u>bi wa<b>a</b>lgh<u>a</u>rimeena wafee sabeeli All<u>a</u>hi wa<b>i</b>bni a<b>l</b>ssabeeli faree<u>d</u>atan mina All<u>a</u>hi wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu AAaleemun <u>h</u>akeemun   </p>
 <p>60.  Alms are for the poor<br> And the needy, and those<br> Employed to administer the (funds);<br> For those whose hearts<br> Have been (recently) reconciled<br> (To Truth); for those in bondage<br> And in debt; in the cause<br> Of God; and for the wayfarer:<br> (Thus is it) ordained by God,<br> And God is full of knowledge<br> And wisdom.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_061.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>61.  Waminhumu alla<u>th</u>eena yu/<u>th</u>oona a<b>l</b>nnabiyya wayaqooloona huwa o<u>th</u>unun qul o<u>th</u>unu khayrin lakum yu/minu bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi wayu/minu lilmu/mineena wara<u>h</u>matun lilla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo minkum wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena yu/<u>th</u>oona rasoola All<u>a</u>hi lahum AAa<u>tha</u>bun aleem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>61.  Among them are men<br> Who molest the Prophet<br> And say, "He is (all) ear."<br> Say, "He listens to what is<br> Best for you: he believes<br> In God, has faith<br> In the Believers, and is a Mercy<br> To those of you who believe."<br> But those who molest the Apostle<br> Will have a grievous penalty.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_062.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>62.  Ya<u>h</u>lifoona bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi lakum liyur<u>d</u>ookum wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu warasooluhu a<u>h</u>aqqu an yur<u>d</u>oohu in k<u>a</u>noo mu/mineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>62.  To you they swear by God.<br> In order to please you:<br> But it is more fitting<br> That they should please<br> God and His Apostle,<br> If they are Believers.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_063.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>63.  Alam yaAAlamoo annahu man yu<u>ha</u>didi All<u>a</u>ha warasoolahu faanna lahu n<u>a</u>ra jahannama kh<u>a</u>lidan feeh<u>a</u> <u>tha</u>lika alkhizyu alAAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>63.  Know they not that for those<br> Who oppose God and His Apostle,<br> Is the Fire of Hell?—<br> Wherein they shall dwell.<br> That is the supreme disgrace.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_064.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>64.  Ya<u>hth</u>aru almun<u>a</u>fiqoona an tunazzala AAalayhim sooratun tunabbi-ohum bim<u>a</u> fee quloobihim quli istahzi-oo inna All<u>a</u>ha mukhrijun m<u>a</u> ta<u>hth</u>aroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>64.  The Hypocrites are afraid<br> Lest a Sūra should be sent down<br> About them, showing them what<br> Is (really passing) in their hearts.<br> Say: "Mock ye! But verily<br> God will bring to light all<br> That ye fear (should be revealed).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_065.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>65.  Wala-in saaltahum layaqoolunna innam<u>a</u> kunn<u>a</u> nakhoo<u>d</u>u wanalAAabu qul abi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi wa<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tihi warasoolihi kuntum tastahzi-oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>65.  If thou dost question them,<br> They declare (with emphasis):<br> "We were only talking idly<br> And in play." Say: "Was it<br> At God, and His Signs,<br> And His Apostle, that ye<br> Were mocking?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_066.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>66.  L<u>a</u> taAAta<u>th</u>iroo qad kafartum baAAda eem<u>a</u>nikum in naAAfu AAan <u>ta</u>-ifatin minkum nuAAa<u>thth</u>ib <u>ta</u>-ifatan bi-annahum k<u>a</u>noo mujrimeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>66.  Make ye no excuses:<br> Ye have rejected Faith<br> After ye had accepted it.<br> If We pardon some of you,<br> We will punish others amongst you,<br> For that they are in sin.</p> <div class="filenav">
