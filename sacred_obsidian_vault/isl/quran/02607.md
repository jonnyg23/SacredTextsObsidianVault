 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXVI.: Shu‘arāa, or The Poets.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 7</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_123.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>123.  Ka<u>thth</u>abat AA<u>a</u>dun almursaleen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>123.  The ’Ad (people) rejected'<br> The apostles.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_124.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>124.  I<u>th</u> q<u>a</u>la lahum akhoohum hoodun al<u>a</u> tattaqoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>124.  Behold, their brother Hūd<br> Said to them: "Will ye not<br> Fear (God)?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_125.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>125.  Innee lakum rasoolun ameen<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>125.  "I am to you an apostle<br> Worthy of all trust:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_126.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>126.  Fa<b>i</b>ttaqoo All<u>a</u>ha waa<u>t</u>eeAAoon<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>126.  "So fear God and obey me.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_127.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>127.  Wam<u>a</u> as-alukum AAalayhi min ajrin in ajriya ill<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> rabbi alAA<u>a</u>lameen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>127.  "No reward do I ask<br> Of you for it my reward<br> Is only from the Lord<br> Of the Worlds.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_128.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>128.  Atabnoona bikulli reeAAin <u>a</u>yatan taAAbathoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>128.  "Do ye build a landmark<br> On every high place<br> To amuse yourselves?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_129.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>129.  Watattakhi<u>th</u>oona ma<u>sa</u>niAAa laAAallakum takhludoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>129.  "And do ye get for yourselves<br> Fine buildings in the hope<br> Of living therein (for ever)?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_130.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>130.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> ba<u>t</u>ashtum ba<u>t</u>ashtum jabb<u>a</u>reen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>130.  "And when ye exert<br> Your strong hand,<br> Do ye do it like men<br> Of absolute power?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_131.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>131.  Fa<b>i</b>ttaqoo All<u>a</u>ha waa<u>t</u>eeAAoon<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>131.  "Now fear God, and obey me.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_132.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>132.  Wa<b>i</b>ttaqoo alla<u>th</u>ee amaddakum bim<u>a</u> taAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>132.  "Yea, fear Him Who<br> Has bestowed on you<br> Freely all that ye know.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_133.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>133.  Amaddakum bi-anAA<u>a</u>min wabaneen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>133.  "Freely has He bestowed<br> On you cattle and sons,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_134.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>134.  Wajann<u>a</u>tin waAAuyoon<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>134.  "And Gardens and Springs.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_135.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>135.  Innee akh<u>a</u>fu AAalaykum AAa<u>tha</u>ba yawmin AAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>135.  "Truly I fear for you<br> The Penalty of a Great Day."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_136.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>136.  Q<u>a</u>loo saw<u>a</u>on AAalayn<u>a</u> awaAAa<i><u>th</u></i>ta am lam takun mina alw<u>a</u>AAi<i><u>th</u></i>een<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>136.  They said: "It is the same<br> To us whether thou<br> Admonish us or be not<br> Among (our) admonishers!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_137.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>137.  In h<u>atha</u> ill<u>a</u> khuluqu al-awwaleen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>137.  "This is no other than<br> A customary device<br> Of the ancients,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_138.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>138.  Wam<u>a</u> na<u>h</u>nu bimuAAa<u>thth</u>abeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>138.  "And we are not the ones<br> To receive Pains and Penalties!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_139.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>139.  Faka<u>thth</u>aboohu faahlakn<u>a</u>hum inna fee <u>tha</u>lika la<u>a</u>yatan wam<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>na aktharuhum mu/mineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>139.  So they rejected him,<br> And We destroyed them.<br> Verily in this is a Sign:<br> But most of them<br> Do not believe.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_140.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>140.  Wa-inna rabbaka lahuwa alAAazeezu a<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>eem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>140.  And verily thy Lord<br> Is He, the Exalted in Might,<br> Most Merciful.</p> <div class="filenav">
