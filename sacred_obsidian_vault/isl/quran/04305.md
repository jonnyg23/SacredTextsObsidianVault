 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XLIII.: Zu<u>kh</u>ruf, or Gold Adornments.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 5</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/043/043_046.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>46.  Walaqad arsaln<u>a</u> moos<u>a</u> bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin<u>a</u> il<u>a</u> firAAawna wamala-ihi faq<u>a</u>la innee rasoolu rabbi alAA<u>a</u>lameen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>46.  We did send Moses<br> Aforetime, with Our Signs,<br>To Pharaoh and his Chiefs:<br> He said, "I am an apostle<br> Of the Lord of the Worlds."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/043/043_047.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>47.  Falamm<u>a</u> j<u>a</u>ahum bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin<u>a</u> i<u>tha</u> hum minh<u>a</u> ya<u>dh</u>akoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>47.  But when he came to them<br> With Our Signs, behold,<br> They ridiculed them.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/043/043_048.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>48.  Wam<u>a</u> nureehim min <u>a</u>yatin ill<u>a</u> hiya akbaru min okhtih<u>a</u> waakha<u>th</u>n<u>a</u>hum bi<b>a</b>lAAa<u>tha</u>bi laAAallahum yarjiAAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>48.  We showed them Sign<br> After Sign, each greater<br> Than, its fellow, and We<br> Seized them with Punishment,<br> In order that they<br> Might turn (to Us).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/043/043_049.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>49.  Waq<u>a</u>loo y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>s<u>ah</u>iru odAAu lan<u>a</u> rabbaka bim<u>a</u> AAahida AAindaka innan<u>a</u> lamuhtadoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>49.  And they said, "O thou<br> Sorcerer! Invoke thy Lord<br> For us according to<br> His covenant with thee;<br> For we shall truly<br> Accept guidance."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/043/043_050.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>50.  Falamm<u>a</u> kashafn<u>a</u> AAanhumu alAAa<u>tha</u>ba i<u>tha</u> hum yankuthoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>50.  But when We removed<br> The Penalty from them,<br> Behold, they broke their word.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/043/043_051.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>51.  Wan<u>a</u>d<u>a</u> firAAawnu fee qawmihi q<u>a</u>la y<u>a</u> qawmi alaysa lee mulku mi<u>s</u>ra wah<u>ath</u>ihi al-anh<u>a</u>ru tajree min ta<u>h</u>tee afal<u>a</u> tub<u>s</u>iroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>51.  And Pharaoh proclaimed<br> Among his people, saying:<br> "O my people! Does not<br> The dominion of Egypt<br> Belong to me, (witness)<br> These streams flowing<br> Underneath my (palace)? What!<br> See ye not then?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/043/043_052.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>52.  Am an<u>a</u> khayrun min h<u>atha</u> alla<u>th</u>ee huwa maheenun wal<u>a</u> yak<u>a</u>du yubeen<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>52.  "Am I not better<br> Than this (Moses), who<br> Is a contemptible wretch<br> And can scarcely<br> Express himself clearly?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/043/043_053.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>53.  Falawl<u>a</u> olqiya AAalayhi aswiratun min <u>th</u>ahabin aw j<u>a</u>a maAAahu almal<u>a</u>-ikatu muqtarineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>53.  "Then why are not<br> Gold bracelets bestowed<br> On him, or (why)<br> Come (not) with him<br> Angels accompanying him<br> In procession?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/043/043_054.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>54.  Fa<b>i</b>stakhaffa qawmahu faa<u>ta</u>AAoohu innahum k<u>a</u>noo qawman f<u>a</u>siqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>54.  Thus did he make<br> Fools of his people,<br> And they obeyed him:<br> Truly were they a people<br> Rebellious (against God).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/043/043_055.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>55.  Falamm<u>a</u> <u>a</u>safoon<u>a</u> intaqamn<u>a</u> minhum faaghraqn<u>a</u>hum ajmaAAeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>55.  When at length they<br> Provoked Us, We exacted<br> Retribution from them, and<br> We drowned them all.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/043/043_056.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>56.  FajaAAaln<u>a</u>hum salafan wamathalan lil-<u>a</u>khireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>56.  And We made them<br> (A people) of the Past<br> And an Example<br> To later ages.</p> <div class="filenav">
