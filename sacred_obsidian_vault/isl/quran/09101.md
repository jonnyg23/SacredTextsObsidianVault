 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XCI.: Shams, or The Sun.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/091/091_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  Wa<b>al</b>shshamsi wa<u>d</u>u<u>ha</u>h<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>1.  By the Sun<br> And his (glorious) splendour;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/091/091_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  Wa<b>a</b>lqamari i<u>tha</u> tal<u>a</u>h<u>a</u>    </p>
 <p>2.  By the Moon<br> As she follows him;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/091/091_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  Wa<b>al</b>nnah<u>a</u>ri i<u>tha</u> jall<u>a</u>h<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>3.  By the Day as it<br> Shows up (the Sun's) glory;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/091/091_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  Wa<b>a</b>llayli i<u>tha</u> yaghsh<u>a</u>h<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>4.  By the Night as it<br> Conceals it;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/091/091_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  Wa<b>al</b>ssam<u>a</u>-i wam<u>a</u> ban<u>a</u>h<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>5.  By the Firmament<br> And its (wonderful) structure;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/091/091_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  Wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i wam<u>a</u> <u>t</u>a<u>ha</u>h<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>6.  By the Earth<br> And its (wide) expanse;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/091/091_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  Wanafsin wam<u>a</u> saww<u>a</u>h<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>7.  By the Soul,<br> And the proportion and order<br> Given to it;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/091/091_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  Faalhamah<u>a</u> fujoorah<u>a</u> wataqw<u>a</u>h<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>8.  And its enlightenment<br> As to its wrong<br> And its right;—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/091/091_009.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>9.  Qad afla<u>h</u>a man zakk<u>a</u>h<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>9.  Truly he succeeds<br> That purifies it,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/091/091_010.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>10.  Waqad kh<u>a</u>ba man dass<u>a</u>h<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>10.  And he fails<br> That corrupts it!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/091/091_011.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>11.  Ka<u>thth</u>abat thamoodu bi<u>t</u>aghw<u>a</u>h<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>11.  The <u>Th</u>amūd (people)<br> Rejected (their prophet)<br> Through their inordinate<br> Wrong-doing.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/091/091_012.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>12.  I<u>th</u>i inbaAAatha ashq<u>a</u>h<u>a</u>    </p>
 <p>12.  Behold, the most wicked<br> Man among them was<br> Deputed (for impiety).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/091/091_013.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>13.  Faq<u>a</u>la lahum rasoolu All<u>a</u>hi n<u>a</u>qata All<u>a</u>hi wasuqy<u>a</u>h<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>13.  But the apostle of God<br> Said to them: "It is<br>A She-camel of God!<br> And (bar her not<br> From) having her drink!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/091/091_014.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>14.  Faka<u>thth</u>aboohu faAAaqarooh<u>a</u> fadamdama AAalayhim rabbuhum bi<u>th</u>anbihim fasaww<u>a</u>h<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>14.  Then they rejected him<br> (As a false prophet),<br> And they hamstrung her.<br> So their Lord, on account<br> Of their crime, obliterated<br> Their traces and made them<br> Equal (in destruction,<br> High and low)!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/091/091_015.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>15.  Wal<u>a</u> yakh<u>a</u>fu AAuqb<u>a</u>h<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>15.  And for Him<br> Is no fear<br> Of its consequences.</p>  <div class="filenav">
