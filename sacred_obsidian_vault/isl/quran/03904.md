 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXXIX.: Zumar, or the Crowds.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 4</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/039/039_032.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>32.  Faman a<i><u>th</u></i>lamu mimman ka<u>th</u>aba AAal<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi waka<u>thth</u>aba bi<b>al</b><u>ss</u>idqi i<u>th</u> j<u>a</u>ahu alaysa fee jahannama mathwan lilk<u>a</u>fireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>32.  Who, then, doth more wrong<br> Than one who utters<br> A lie concerning God,<br> And rejects the Truth<br> When it comes to him<br> Is there not in Hell<br> An abode for blasphemers?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/039/039_033.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>33.  Wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>ee j<u>a</u>a bi<b>al</b><u>ss</u>idqi wa<u>s</u>addaqa bihi ol<u>a</u>-ika humu almuttaqoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>33.  And he who brings the Truth<br> And he who confirms<br> (And supports) it—such are<br> The men who do right.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/039/039_034.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>34.  Lahum m<u>a</u> yash<u>a</u>oona AAinda rabbihim <u>tha</u>lika jaz<u>a</u>o almu<u>h</u>sineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>34.  They shall have all<br> That they wish for,<br> In the presence of their Lord:<br> Such is the reward<br> Of those who do good:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/039/039_035.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>35.  Liyukaffira All<u>a</u>hu AAanhum aswaa alla<u>th</u>ee AAamiloo wayajziyahum ajrahum bi-a<u>h</u>sani alla<u>th</u>ee k<u>a</u>noo yaAAmaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>35.  So that God will<br> Turn off from them<br> (Even) the worst in their deeds<br> And give them their reward<br> According to the best<br> Of what they have done.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/039/039_036.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>36.  Alaysa All<u>a</u>hu bik<u>a</u>fin AAabdahu wayukhawwifoonaka bi<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena min doonihi waman yu<u>d</u>lili All<u>a</u>hu fam<u>a</u> lahu min h<u>a</u>d<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>36.  Is not God enough<br> For His servant? But<br> They try to frighten thee<br> With other (gods) besides Him!<br> For such as God leaves<br> To stray, there can be<br> No guide.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/039/039_037.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>37.  Waman yahdi All<u>a</u>hu fam<u>a</u> lahu min mu<u>d</u>illin alaysa All<u>a</u>hu biAAazeezin <u>th</u>ee intiq<u>a</u>m<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>37.  And such as God doth<br> Guide there can be<br> None to lead astray.<br> Is not God Exalted<br> In Power, (Able to enforce<br> His Will), Lord of Retribution?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/039/039_038.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>38.  Wala-in saaltahum man khalaqa a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>a layaqoolunna All<u>a</u>hu qul afaraaytum m<u>a</u> tadAAoona min dooni All<u>a</u>hi in ar<u>a</u>daniya All<u>a</u>hu bi<u>d</u>urrin hal hunna k<u>a</u>shif<u>a</u>tu <u>d</u>urrihi aw ar<u>a</u>danee bira<u>h</u>matin hal hunna mumsik<u>a</u>tu ra<u>h</u>matihi qul <u>h</u>asbiya All<u>a</u>hu AAalayhi yatawakkalu almutawakkiloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>38.  If indeed thou ask them<br> Who it is that created<br> The heavens and the earth,<br> They would be sure to say.<br> "God ". Say: "See ye then?<br> The things that ye invoke<br> Besides God,—can they,<br> If God wills some Penalty<br> For me, remove His Penalty?—<br> Or if He wills some Grace<br> For me, can they keep back<br>His Grace?" Say: "Sufficient<br> Is God for me!<br> In Him trust those<br> Who put their trust."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/039/039_039.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>39.  Qul y<u>a</u> qawmi iAAmaloo AAal<u>a</u> mak<u>a</u>natikum innee AA<u>a</u>milun fasawfa taAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>39.  Say: "O my people!<br> Do whatever ye can:<br> I will do (my part):<br> But soon will ye know—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/039/039_040.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>40.  Man ya/teehi AAa<u>tha</u>bun yukhzeehi waya<u>h</u>illu AAalayhi AAa<u>tha</u>bun muqeem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>40.  "Who it is to whom<br> Comes a Penalty<br> Of ignominy, and on whom<br> Descends a Penalty that abides."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/039/039_041.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>41.  Inn<u>a</u> anzaln<u>a</u> AAalayka alkit<u>a</u>ba li<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si bi<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>aqqi famani ihtad<u>a</u> falinafsihi waman <u>d</u>alla fa-innam<u>a</u> ya<u>d</u>illu AAalayh<u>a</u> wam<u>a</u> anta AAalayhim biwakeel<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>41.  Verily We have revealed<br> The Book to thee<br> In Truth, for (instructing)<br> mankind.<br> He, then, that receives guidance<br> Benefits his own soul:<br> But he that strays<br> Injures his own soul.<br> Nor art thou set<br> Over them to dispose<br> Of their affairs.</p> <div class="filenav">
