 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra II.: Baqara, or the Heifer.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 17</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_142.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>142.  Sayaqoolu a<b>l</b>ssufah<u>a</u>o mina a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si m<u>a</u> wall<u>a</u>hum AAan qiblatihimu allatee k<u>a</u>noo AAalayh<u>a</u> qul lill<u>a</u>hi almashriqu wa<b>a</b>lmaghribu yahdee man yash<u>a</u>o il<u>a</u> <u>s</u>ir<u>at</u>in mustaqeem<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>142.  The Fools among the people<br> Will say: "What hath turned<br> Them from the Qibla to which<br> They were used?" Say:<br> To God belong both East and West:<br> He guideth whom He will<br> To a Way that is straight.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_143.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>143.  Waka<u>tha</u>lika jaAAaln<u>a</u>kum ommatan wasa<u>t</u>an litakoonoo shuhad<u>a</u>a AAal<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si wayakoona a<b>l</b>rrasoolu AAalaykum shaheedan wam<u>a</u> jaAAaln<u>a</u> alqiblata allatee kunta AAalayh<u>a</u> ill<u>a</u> linaAAlama man yattabiAAu a<b>l</b>rrasoola mimman yanqalibu AAal<u>a</u> AAaqibayhi wa-in k<u>a</u>nat lakabeeratan ill<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena had<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hu wam<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>na All<u>a</u>hu liyu<u>d</u>eeAAa eem<u>a</u>nakum inna All<u>a</u>ha bi<b>al</b>nn<u>a</u>si laraoofun ra<u>h</u>eem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>143.  Thus have We made of you<br> An Ummat justly balanced,<br> That ye might be witnesses<br> Over the nations,<br>And the Apostle a witness<br> Over yourselves;<br> And We appointed the Qibla<br> To which thou wast used,<br> Only to test those who followed<br> The Apostle from those<br> Who would turn on their heels<br> (From the Faith). Indeed it was<br> (A change) momentous, except<br> To those guided by God.<br> And never would God<br> Make your faith of no effect.<br> For God is to all people<br> Most surely full of kindness,<br> Most Merciful.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_144.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>144.  Qad nar<u>a</u> taqalluba wajhika fee a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>-i falanuwalliyannaka qiblatan tar<u>da</u>h<u>a</u> fawalli wajhaka sha<u>t</u>ra almasjidi al<u>h</u>ar<u>a</u>mi wa<u>h</u>aythu m<u>a</u> kuntum fawalloo wujoohakum sha<u>t</u>rahu wa-inna alla<u>th</u>eena ootoo alkit<u>a</u>ba layaAAlamoona annahu al<u>h</u>aqqu min rabbihim wam<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hu bigh<u>a</u>filin AAamm<u>a</u> yaAAmaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>144.  We see the turning<br> Of thy face (for guidance)<br> To the heavens: now<br> Shall We turn thee<br> To a Qibla that shall<br> Please thee. Turn then<br> Thy face in the direction<br> Of the sacred Mosque:<br> Wherever ye are, turn<br> Your faces in that direction.<br>The people of the Book<br> Know well that that is<br> The truth from their Lord.<br> Nor is God unmindful<br> Of what they do.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_145.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>145.  Wala-in atayta alla<u>th</u>eena ootoo alkit<u>a</u>ba bikulli <u>a</u>yatin m<u>a</u> tabiAAoo qiblataka wam<u>a</u> anta bit<u>a</u>biAAin qiblatahum wam<u>a</u> baAA<u>d</u>uhum bit<u>a</u>biAAin qiblata baAA<u>d</u>in wala-ini ittabaAAta ahw<u>a</u>ahum min baAAdi m<u>a</u> j<u>a</u>aka mina alAAilmi innaka i<u>th</u>an lamina a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>145.  Even if thou wert to bring<br> To the people of the Book<br> All the Signs (together),<br> They would not follow<br> Thy Qibla; nor art thou<br> Going to follow their Qibla;<br> Nor indeed will they follow<br> Each other's Qibla. If thou<br> After the knowledge hath reached thee,<br> Wert to follow their (vain)<br> Desires,—then wert thou<br> Indeed (clearly) in the wrong.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_146.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>146.  Alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>tayn<u>a</u>humu alkit<u>a</u>ba yaAArifoonahu kam<u>a</u> yaAArifoona abn<u>a</u>ahum wa-inna fareeqan minhum layaktumoona al<u>h</u>aqqa wahum yaAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>146.  The people of the Book<br> Know this as they know<br> Their own sons; but some<br> Of them conceal the truth<br> Which they themselves know.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_147.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>147.  Al<u>h</u>aqqu min rabbika fal<u>a</u> takoonanna mina almumtareen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>147.  The Truth is from thy Lord;<br> So be not at all in doubt.</p> <div class="filenav">
