 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXIX.: ‘Ankabūt, or the Spider</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 3</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/029/029_023.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>23.  Wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>ti All<u>a</u>hi waliq<u>a</u>-ihi ol<u>a</u>-ika ya-isoo min ra<u>h</u>matee waol<u>a</u>-ika lahum AAa<u>tha</u>bun aleem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>23.  Those who reject the Signs<br> Of God and the Meeting<br> With Him (in the Hereafter),<br> It is they who shall despair<br> Of My mercy: it is they<br> Who will (suffer)<br> A most grievous Penalty.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/029/029_024.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>24.  Fam<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>na jaw<u>a</u>ba qawmihi ill<u>a</u> an q<u>a</u>loo oqtuloohu aw <u>h</u>arriqoohu faanj<u>a</u>hu All<u>a</u>hu mina a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>ri inna fee <u>tha</u>lika la<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin liqawmin yu/minoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>24.  So naught was the answer<br> Of (Abraham's) people except<br> That they said: "Slay him<br> Or burn him." But God<br> Did save him from the Fire<br> Verily in this are Signs<br> For people who believe.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/029/029_025.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>25.  Waq<u>a</u>la innam<u>a</u> ittakha<u>th</u>tum min dooni All<u>a</u>hi awth<u>a</u>nan mawaddata baynikum fee al<u>h</u>ay<u>a</u>ti a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> thumma yawma alqiy<u>a</u>mati yakfuru baAA<u>d</u>ukum bibaAA<u>d</u>in wayalAAanu baAA<u>d</u>ukum baAA<u>d</u>an wama-w<u>a</u>kumu a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>ru wam<u>a</u> lakum min n<u>as</u>ireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>25.  And he said: "For you,<br> Ye have taken (for worship)<br> Idols besides God, out of<br> Mutual love and regard<br>Between yourselves in this life;<br> But on the Day of Judgment<br> Ye shall disown each other<br> And curse each other:<br> And your abode will be<br> The Fire, and ye shall have<br> None to help."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/029/029_026.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>26.  Fa<u>a</u>mana lahu loo<u>t</u>un waq<u>a</u>la innee muh<u>a</u>jirun il<u>a</u> rabbee innahu huwa alAAazeezu al<u>h</u>akeem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>26.  But Lūṭ had faith in Him:<br> He said: "I will leave<br> Home for the sake of<br> My Lord: for He is<br> Exalted in Might, and Wise."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/029/029_027.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>27.  Wawahabn<u>a</u> lahu is<u>ha</u>qa wayaAAqooba wajaAAaln<u>a</u> fee <u>th</u>urriyyatihi a<b>l</b>nnubuwwata wa<b>a</b>lkit<u>a</u>ba wa<u>a</u>tayn<u>a</u>hu ajrahu fee a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> wa-innahu fee al-<u>a</u>khirati lamina a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>li<u>h</u>een<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>27.  And We gave (Abraham)<br> Isaac and Jacob, and ordained<br> Among his progeny Prophethood<br> And Revelation, and We<br> Granted him his reward<br> In this life; and he was<br> In the Hereafter (of the company)<br> Of the Righteous.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/029/029_028.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>28.  Waloo<u>t</u>an i<u>th</u> q<u>a</u>la liqawmihi innakum lata/toona alf<u>ah</u>ishata m<u>a</u> sabaqakum bih<u>a</u> min a<u>h</u>adin mina alAA<u>a</u>lameen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>28.  And (remember) Lūṭ: behold,<br> He said to his people:<br>"Ye do commit lewdness,<br> Such as no people in Creation<br> (Ever) committed before you.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/029/029_029.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>29.  A-innakum lata/toona a<b>l</b>rrij<u>a</u>la wataq<u>t</u>aAAoona a<b>l</b>ssabeela wata/toona fee n<u>a</u>deekumu almunkara fam<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>na jaw<u>a</u>ba qawmihi ill<u>a</u> an q<u>a</u>loo i/tin<u>a</u> biAAa<u>tha</u>bi All<u>a</u>hi in kunta mina a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>diqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>29.  "Do ye indeed approach men,<br> And cut off the highway?<br> And practise wickedness<br> (Even) in your councils?"<br> But his people gave no answer<br> But this: they said:<br> "Bring us the Wrath of God<br> If thou tellest the truth."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/029/029_030.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>30.  Q<u>a</u>la rabbi on<u>s</u>urnee AAal<u>a</u> alqawmi almufsideen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>30.  He said: "O my Lord!<br> Help Thou me against people<br> Who do mischief!"</p> <div class="filenav">
