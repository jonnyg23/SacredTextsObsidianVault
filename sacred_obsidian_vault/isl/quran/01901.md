 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XIX.: Maryam, or Mary.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  K<u>a</u>f-h<u>a</u>-y<u>a</u>-AAayn-<u>sa</u>d   </p>
 <p>1.  Kāf. Hā. Yā. ‘Ain Ṣād.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  <u>Th</u>ikru ra<u>h</u>mati rabbika AAabdahu zakariyy<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>2.  (This is) a recital<br> Of the Mercy of thy Lord<br> To His servant Zakarīya.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  I<u>th</u> n<u>a</u>d<u>a</u> rabbahu nid<u>a</u>an khafiyy<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>3.  Behold! he cried<br> To his Lord in secret,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  Q<u>a</u>la rabbi innee wahana alAAa<i><u>th</u></i>mu minnee wa<b>i</b>shtaAAala a<b>l</b>rra/su shayban walam akun biduAA<u>a</u>-ika rabbi shaqiyy<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>4.  Praying: "O my Lord!<br> Infirm indeed are my bones,<br> And the hair of my head<br> Doth glisten with grey:<br> But never am I unblest,<br> O my Lord, in my prayer<br> To Thee!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  Wa-innee khiftu almaw<u>a</u>liya min war<u>a</u>-ee wak<u>a</u>nati imraatee AA<u>a</u>qiran fahab lee min ladunka waliyy<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>5.  "Now I fear (what)<br> My relatives (and colleagues)<br> (Will do) after me:<br> But my wife is barren:<br> So give me an heir<br> As from Thyself,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  Yarithunee wayarithu min <u>a</u>li yaAAqooba wa<b>i</b>jAAalhu rabbi ra<u>d</u>iyy<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>6.  "(One that) will (truly)<br> "Represent me, and represent<br> The posterity of Jacob;<br> And make him, O my Lord!<br> One with whom Thou art<br> Well-pleased!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  Y<u>a</u> zakariyy<u>a</u> inn<u>a</u> nubashshiruka bighul<u>a</u>min ismuhu ya<u>h</u>y<u>a</u> lam najAAal lahu min qablu samiyy<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>7.  (His prayer was answered):<br>"O Zakarīya! We give thee<br> Good news of a son:<br> His name shall be Yaḥyā:<br> On none by that name<br> Have We conferred distinction before."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  Q<u>a</u>la rabbi ann<u>a</u> yakoonu lee ghul<u>a</u>mun wak<u>a</u>nati imraatee AA<u>a</u>qiran waqad balaghtu mina alkibari AAitiyy<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>8.  He said: "O my Lord!<br> How shall I have a son,<br> When my wife is barren<br> And I have grown quite decrepit<br> From old age?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_009.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>9.  Q<u>a</u>la ka<u>tha</u>lika q<u>a</u>la rabbuka huwa AAalayya hayyinun waqad khalaqtuka min qablu walam taku shay-<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>9.  He said: "So (it will be):<br> Thy Lord saith, "That is<br> Easy for Me: I did<br> Indeed create thee before,<br> When thou hadst been nothing!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_010.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>10.  Q<u>a</u>la rabbi ijAAal lee <u>a</u>yatan q<u>a</u>la <u>a</u>yatuka all<u>a</u> tukallima a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>sa thal<u>a</u>tha lay<u>a</u>lin sawiyy<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>10.  (Zakarīya) said: "O my Lord!<br> Give me a Sign."<br> "Thy Sign," was the answer,<br> "Shall be that thou<br> Shalt speak to no man<br> For three nights,<br> Although thou art not dumb."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_011.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>11.  Fakharaja AAal<u>a</u> qawmihi mina almi<u>h</u>r<u>a</u>bi faaw<u>ha</u> ilayhim an sabbi<u>h</u>oo bukratan waAAashiyy<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>11.  So Zakarīya came out<br> To his people<br> From his chamber:<br> He told them by signs<br> To celebrate God's praises<br> In the morning<br> And in the evening.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_012.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>12.  Y<u>a</u> ya<u>h</u>y<u>a</u> khu<u>th</u>i alkit<u>a</u>ba biquwwatin wa<u>a</u>tayn<u>a</u>hu al<u>h</u>ukma <u>s</u>abiyy<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>12.  (To his son came the command):<br> "O Yaḥyā! take hold<br>Of the Book with might":<br> And We gave him Wisdom<br> Even as a youth,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_013.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>13.  Wa<u>h</u>an<u>a</u>nan min ladunn<u>a</u> wazak<u>a</u>tan wak<u>a</u>na taqiyy<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>13.  And pity (for all creatures)<br> As from us, and purity:<br> He was devout,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_014.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>14.  Wabarran biw<u>a</u>lidayhi walam yakun jabb<u>a</u>ran AAa<u>s</u>iyy<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>14.  And kind to his parents,<br> And he was not overbearing<br> Or rebellious.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_015.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>15.  Wasal<u>a</u>mun AAalayhi yawma wulida wayawma yamootu wayawma yubAAathu <u>h</u>ayy<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>15.  So Peace on him<br> The day he was born,<br> The day that he dies,<br> And the day that he<br> Will be raised up<br> To life (again)</p> <div class="filenav">
