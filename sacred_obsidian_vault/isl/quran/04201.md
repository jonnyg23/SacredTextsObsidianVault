 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XLII.: Shūrā, or Consultation.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/042/042_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  <u>Ha</u>-meem   </p>
 <p>1.  Hā-Mīm;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/042/042_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  AAayn-seen-q<u>a</u>f   </p>
 <p>2.  ‘Ain. Sīn. Kāf.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/042/042_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  Ka<u>tha</u>lika yoo<u>h</u>ee ilayka wa-il<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena min qablika All<u>a</u>hu alAAazeezu al<u>h</u>akeem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>3.  Thus doth (He) send<br> Inspiration to thee<br> As (He did) to those before thee,—<br> God, Exalted in Power,<br> Full of Wisdom.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/042/042_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  Lahu m<u>a</u> fee a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wam<u>a</u> fee al-ar<u>d</u>i wahuwa alAAaliyyu alAAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>4.  To Him belongs all<br> That is in the heavens<br> And on earth: and He<br> Is Most High, Most Great.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/042/042_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  Tak<u>a</u>du a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>tu yatafa<u>tt</u>arna min fawqihinna wa<b>a</b>lmal<u>a</u>-ikatu yusabbi<u>h</u>oona bi<u>h</u>amdi rabbihim wayastaghfiroona liman fee al-ar<u>d</u>i al<u>a</u> inna All<u>a</u>ha huwa alghafooru a<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>eem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>5.  The heavens are almost<br> Rent asunder from above them<br> (By His Glory):<br> And the angels celebrate<br> The Praises of their Lord,<br> And pray for forgiveness<br> For (all) beings on earth:<br> Behold! Verily God is He,<br>The Oft-Forgiving,<br> Most Merciful.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/042/042_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  Wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena ittakha<u>th</u>oo min doonihi awliy<u>a</u>a All<u>a</u>hu <u>h</u>afee<i><u>th</u></i>un AAalayhim wam<u>a</u> anta AAalayhim biwakeel<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>6.  And those who take<br> As protectors others besides<br> Him,—<br> God doth watch over them;<br> And thou art not<br> The disposer of their affairs.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/042/042_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  Waka<u>tha</u>lika aw<u>h</u>ayn<u>a</u> ilayka qur-<u>a</u>nan AAarabiyyan litun<u>th</u>ira omma alqur<u>a</u> waman <u>h</u>awlah<u>a</u> watun<u>th</u>ira yawma aljamAAi l<u>a</u> rayba feehi fareequn fee aljannati wafareequn fee a<b>l</b>ssaAAeer<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>7.  Thus have We sent<br> By inspiration to thee<br> An Arabic Qur-ān:<br> That thou mayest warn<br> The Mother of Cities<br> And all around her,—<br> And warn (them) of<br> The Day of Assembly,<br> Of which there is no doubt:<br> (When) some will be<br> In the Garden, and some<br> In the Blazing Fire.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/042/042_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  Walaw sh<u>a</u>a All<u>a</u>hu lajaAAalahum ommatan w<u>ah</u>idatan wal<u>a</u>kin yudkhilu man yash<u>a</u>o fee ra<u>h</u>matihi wa<b>al</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limoona m<u>a</u> lahum min waliyyin wal<u>a</u> na<u>s</u>eer<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>8.  If God had so willed,<br> He could have made them<br> A single people; but He<br> Admits whom He will<br> To His Mercy;<br> And the wrong-doers<br> Will have no protector<br> Nor helper.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/042/042_009.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>9.  Ami ittakha<u>th</u>oo min doonihi awliy<u>a</u>a fa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu huwa alwaliyyu wahuwa yu<u>h</u>yee almawt<u>a</u> wahuwa AAal<u>a</u> kulli shay-in qadeer<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>9.  What! Have they taken<br> (For worship) protectors<br>Besides Him? But it is<br> God,—He is the Protector,<br> And it is He Who<br> Gives life to the dead:<br> It is He Who has power<br> Over all things.</p> <div class="filenav">
