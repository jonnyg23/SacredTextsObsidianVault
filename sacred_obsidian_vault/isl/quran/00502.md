 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra V.: Māïda, or The Table Spread.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 2</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo i<u>tha</u> qumtum il<u>a</u> a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>al<u>a</u>ti fa<b>i</b>ghsiloo wujoohakum waaydiyakum il<u>a</u> almar<u>a</u>fiqi wa<b>i</b>msa<u>h</u>oo biruoosikum waarjulakum il<u>a</u> alkaAAbayni wa-in kuntum junuban fa<b>i</b><u>tt</u>ahharoo wa-in kuntum mar<u>da</u> aw AAal<u>a</u> safarin aw j<u>a</u>a a<u>h</u>adun minkum mina algh<u>a</u>-i<u>t</u>i aw l<u>a</u>mastumu a<b>l</b>nnis<u>a</u>a falam tajidoo m<u>a</u>an fatayammamoo <u>s</u>aAAeedan <u>t</u>ayyiban fa<b>i</b>msa<u>h</u>oo biwujoohikum waaydeekum minhu m<u>a</u> yureedu All<u>a</u>hu liyajAAala AAalaykum min <u>h</u>arajin wal<u>a</u>kin yureedu liyu<u>t</u>ahhirakum waliyutimma niAAmatahu AAalaykum laAAallakum tashkuroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>6.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">7</font></span> O ye who believe!<br> When ye prepare<br> For prayer, wash<br> Your faces, and your hands<br> (And arms) to the elbows;<br> Rub your heads (with water);<br> And (wash) your feet<br> To the ankles.<br> If ye are in a state<br> Of ceremonial impurity,<br> Bathe your whole body.<br> But if ye are ill,<br> Or on a journey,<br> Or one of you cometh<br> From offices of nature,<br> Or ye have been<br> In contact with women,<br> And ye find no water,<br> Then take for yourselves<br> Clean sand or earth,<br>And rub therewith<br> Your faces and hands.<br> God doth not wish<br> To place you in a difficulty,<br> But to make you clean,<br> And to complete<br> His favour to you,<br> That ye may be grateful.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  Wa<b>o</b><u>th</u>kuroo niAAmata All<u>a</u>hi AAalaykum wameeth<u>a</u>qahu alla<u>th</u>ee w<u>a</u>thaqakum bihi i<u>th</u> qultum samiAAn<u>a</u> waa<u>t</u>aAAn<u>a</u> wa<b>i</b>ttaqoo All<u>a</u>ha inna All<u>a</u>ha AAaleemun bi<u>tha</u>ti a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>udoor<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>7.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">8</font></span> And call in remembrance<br> The favour of God<br> Unto you, and His Covenant,<br> Which He ratified<br> With you, when ye said:<br> "We hear and we obey":<br> And fear God, for God<br> Knoweth well<br> The secrets of your hearts.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo koonoo qaww<u>a</u>meena lill<u>a</u>hi shuhad<u>a</u>a bi<b>a</b>lqis<u>t</u>i wal<u>a</u> yajrimannakum shana<u>a</u>nu qawmin AAal<u>a</u> all<u>a</u> taAAdiloo iAAdiloo huwa aqrabu li<b>l</b>ttaqw<u>a</u> wa<b>i</b>ttaqoo All<u>a</u>ha inna All<u>a</u>ha khabeerun bim<u>a</u> taAAmaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>8.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">9</font></span> O ye who believe!<br> Stand out firmly<br> For God, as witnesses<br> To fair dealing, and let not<br> The hatred of others<br> To you make you swerve<br> To wrong and depart from<br> Justice. Be just: that is<br> Next to Piety: and fear God.<br> For God is well-acquainted<br> With all that ye do.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_009.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>9.  WaAAada All<u>a</u>hu alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo waAAamiloo a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>li<u>ha</u>ti lahum maghfiratun waajrun AAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>9.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">10</font></span> To those who believe<br> And do deeds of righteousness<br> Hath God promised forgiveness<br> And a great reward.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_010.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>10.  Wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo waka<u>thth</u>aboo bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin<u>a</u> ol<u>a</u>-ika a<u>s</u>-<u>ha</u>bu alja<u>h</u>eem<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>10.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">11</font></span> Those who reject faith<br> And deny Our Signs<br> Will be Companions<br> Of Hell-fire.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_011.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>11.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo o<u>th</u>kuroo niAAmata All<u>a</u>hi AAalaykum i<u>th</u> hamma qawmun an yabsu<u>t</u>oo ilaykum aydiyahum fakaffa aydiyahum AAankum wa<b>i</b>ttaqoo All<u>a</u>ha waAAal<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi falyatawakkali almu/minoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>11.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">12</font></span> O ye who believe<br> Call in remembrance<br> The favour of God<br> Unto you when<br> Certain men formed the design<br> To stretch out<br> Their hands against you,<br> But (God) held back<br> Their hands from you:<br> So fear God. And on God<br> Let Believers put<br> (All) their trust.</p> <div class="filenav">
