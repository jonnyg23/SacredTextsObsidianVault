 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra II.: Baqara, or the Heifer.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 24</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_189.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>189.  Yas-aloonaka AAani al-ahillati qul hiya maw<u>a</u>qeetu li<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si wa<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>ajji walaysa albirru bi-an ta/too albuyoota min <i><u>th</u></i>uhoorih<u>a</u> wal<u>a</u>kinna albirra mani ittaq<u>a</u> wa/too albuyoota min abw<u>a</u>bih<u>a</u> wa<b>i</b>ttaqoo All<u>a</u>ha laAAallakum tufli<u>h</u>oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>189.  They ask thee<br> Concerning the New Moons.<br> Say: They are but signs<br> To mark fixed periods of time<br> In (the affairs of) men,<br> And for Pilgrimage.<br> It is no virtue if ye enter<br> Your houses from the back:<br> It is virtue if ye fear God.<br> Enter houses<br> Through the proper doors:<br> And fear God:<br> That ye may prosper.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_190.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>190.  Waq<u>a</u>tiloo fee sabeeli All<u>a</u>hi alla<u>th</u>eena yuq<u>a</u>tiloonakum wal<u>a</u> taAAtadoo inna All<u>a</u>ha l<u>a</u> yu<u>h</u>ibbu almuAAtadeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>190.  Fight in the cause of God<br> Those who fight you,<br> But do not transgress limits;<br> For God loveth not transgressors.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_191.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>191.  Wa<b>o</b>qtuloohum <u>h</u>aythu thaqiftumoohum waakhrijoohum min <u>h</u>aythu akhrajookum wa<b>a</b>lfitnatu ashaddu mina alqatli wal<u>a</u> tuq<u>a</u>tiloohum AAinda almasjidi al<u>h</u>ar<u>a</u>mi <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> yuq<u>a</u>tilookum feehi fa-in q<u>a</u>talookum fa<b>o</b>qtuloohum ka<u>tha</u>lika jaz<u>a</u>o alk<u>a</u>fireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>191.  And slay them<br> Wherever ye catch them,<br> And turn them out<br> From where they have<br>Turned you out;<br> For tumult and oppression<br> Are worse than slaughter;<br> But fight them not<br> At the Sacred Mosque,<br> Unless they (first)<br> Fight you there;<br> But if they fight you,<br> Slay them.<br> Such is the reward<br> Of those who suppress faith.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_192.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>192.  Fa-ini intahaw fa-inna All<u>a</u>ha ghafoorun ra<u>h</u>eem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>192.  But if they cease,<br> God is Oft-forgiving,<br> Most Merciful.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_193.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>193.  Waq<u>a</u>tiloohum <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> l<u>a</u> takoona fitnatun wayakoona a<b>l</b>ddeenu lill<u>a</u>hi fa-ini intahaw fal<u>a</u> AAudw<u>a</u>na ill<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>193.  And fight them on<br> Until there is no more<br> Tumult or oppression,<br> And there prevail<br> Justice and faith in God;<br> But if they cease,<br> Let there be no hostility<br> Except to those<br> Who practise oppression.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_194.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>194.  A<b>l</b>shshahru al<u>h</u>ar<u>a</u>mu bi<b>al</b>shshahri al<u>h</u>ar<u>a</u>mi wa<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>urum<u>a</u>tu qi<u>sas</u>un famani iAAtad<u>a</u> AAalaykum fa<b>i</b>AAtadoo AAalayhi bimithli m<u>a</u> iAAtad<u>a</u> AAalaykum wa<b>i</b>ttaqoo All<u>a</u>ha wa<b>i</b>AAlamoo anna All<u>a</u>ha maAAa almuttaqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>194.  The prohibited month<br> For the prohibited month,—<br> And so for all things prohibited,—<br> There is the law of equality.<br> If then any one transgresses<br> The prohibition against you,<br> Transgress ye likewise<br> Against him.<br> But fear God, and know<br> That God is with those<br> Who restrain themselves.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_195.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>195.  Waanfiqoo fee sabeeli All<u>a</u>hi wal<u>a</u> tulqoo bi-aydeekum il<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>ttahlukati waa<u>h</u>sinoo inna All<u>a</u>ha yu<u>h</u>ibbu almu<u>h</u>sineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>195.  And spend of your substance<br> In the cause of God,<br> And make not your own hands<br> Contribute to (your) destruction;<br> But do good;<br> For God loveth those<br> Who do good.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_196.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>196.  Waatimmoo al<u>h</u>ajja wa<b>a</b>lAAumrata lill<u>a</u>hi fa-in o<u>hs</u>irtum fam<u>a</u> istaysara mina alhadyi wal<u>a</u> ta<u>h</u>liqoo ruoosakum <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> yablugha alhadyu ma<u>h</u>illahu faman k<u>a</u>na minkum maree<u>d</u>an aw bihi a<u>th</u>an min ra/sihi fafidyatun min <u>s</u>iy<u>a</u>min aw <u>s</u>adaqatin aw nusukin fa-i<u>tha</u> amintum faman tamattaAAa bi<b>a</b>lAAumrati il<u>a</u> al<u>h</u>ajji fam<u>a</u> istaysara mina alhadyi faman lam yajid fa<u>s</u>iy<u>a</u>mu thal<u>a</u>thati ayy<u>a</u>min fee al<u>h</u>ajji wasabAAatin i<u>tha</u> rajaAAtum tilka AAasharatun k<u>a</u>milatun <u>tha</u>lika liman lam yakun ahluhu <u>had</u>iree almasjidi al<u>h</u>ar<u>a</u>mi wa<b>i</b>ttaqoo All<u>a</u>ha wa<b>i</b>AAlamoo anna All<u>a</u>ha shadeedu alAAiq<u>a</u>b<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>196.  And complete<br> The Ḥajj or ‘umra <br>In the service of God.<br> But if ye are prevented<br> (From completing it),<br> Send an offering<br> For sacrifice,<br> Such as ye may find,<br> And do not shave your heads<br> Until the offering reaches<br> The place of sacrifice.<br> And if any of you is ill,<br> Or has an ailment in his scalp,<br> (Necessitating shaving),<br> (He should) in compensation<br> Either fast, or feed the poor,<br> Or offer sacrifice;<br> And when ye are<br> In peaceful conditions (again),<br> If any one wishes<br> To continue the ‘umra <br> On to the ḥajj,<br> He must make an offering,<br> Such as he can afford,<br> But if he cannot afford it,<br> He should fast<br> Three days during the ḥajj<br> And seven days on his return,<br> Making ten days in all.<br> This is for those<br> Whose household<br> Is not in (the precincts<br> Of) the Sacred Mosque.<br> And fear God,<br> And know that God.<br> Is strict in punishment?</p> <div class="filenav">
