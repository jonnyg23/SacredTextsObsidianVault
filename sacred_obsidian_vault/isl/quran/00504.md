 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra V.: Māïda, or The Table Spread.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 4</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_020.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>20.  Wa-i<u>th</u> q<u>a</u>la moos<u>a</u> liqawmihi y<u>a</u> qawmi o<u>th</u>kuroo niAAmata All<u>a</u>hi AAalaykum i<u>th</u> jaAAala feekum anbiy<u>a</u>a wajaAAalakum mulookan wa<u>a</u>t<u>a</u>kum m<u>a</u> lam yu/ti a<u>h</u>adan mina alAA<u>a</u>lameen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>20.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">22</font></span> Remember Moses said<br> To his people: "O my People!<br> Call in remembrance the favour<br> Of God unto you, when He<br> Produced prophets among you,<br> Made you kings, and gave<br> You what He had not given<br> To any other among the peoples.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_021.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>21.  Y<u>a</u> qawmi odkhuloo al-ar<u>d</u>a almuqaddasata allatee kataba All<u>a</u>hu lakum wal<u>a</u> tartaddoo AAal<u>a</u> adb<u>a</u>rikum fatanqaliboo kh<u>a</u>sireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>21.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">23</font></span> "O my people! enter<br> The holy land which<br> God hath assigned unto you,<br> And turn not back<br> Ignominiously, for then<br> Will ye be overthrown,<br> To your own ruin."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_022.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>22.  Q<u>a</u>loo y<u>a</u> moos<u>a</u> inna feeh<u>a</u> qawman jabb<u>a</u>reena wa-inn<u>a</u> lan nadkhulah<u>a</u> <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> yakhrujoo minh<u>a</u> fa-in yakhrujoo minh<u>a</u> fa-inn<u>a</u> d<u>a</u>khiloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>22.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">24</font></span> They said: "O Moses!<br> In this land are a people<br> Of exceeding strength:<br> Never shall we enter it<br> Until they leave it:<br> If (once) they leave,<br> Then shall we enter."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_023.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>23.  Q<u>a</u>la rajul<u>a</u>ni mina alla<u>th</u>eena yakh<u>a</u>foona anAAama All<u>a</u>hu AAalayhim<u>a</u> odkhuloo AAalayhimu alb<u>a</u>ba fa-i<u>tha</u> dakhaltumoohu fa-innakum gh<u>a</u>liboona waAAal<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi fatawakkaloo in kuntum mu/mineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>23.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">25</font></span> (But) among (their) God-fearing men<br> Were two on whom<br> God had bestowed His grace:<br> They said: "Assault them<br> At the (proper) Gate:<br> When once ye are in,<br> Victory will be yours;<br> <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">26</font></span> But on God put your trust<br> If ye have faith."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_024.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>24.  Q<u>a</u>loo y<u>a</u> moos<u>a</u> inn<u>a</u> lan nadkhulah<u>a</u> abadan m<u>a</u> d<u>a</u>moo feeh<u>a</u> fa-i<u>th</u>hab anta warabbuka faq<u>a</u>til<u>a</u> inn<u>a</u> h<u>a</u>hun<u>a</u> q<u>a</u>AAidoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>24.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">27</font></span> They said: "O Moses!<br> While they remain there,<br> Never shall we be able<br> To enter, to the end of time.<br> Go thou, and thy Lord,<br> And fight ye two,<br> While we sit here"<br> (And watch)."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_025.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>25.  Q<u>a</u>la rabbi innee l<u>a</u> amliku ill<u>a</u> nafsee waakhee fa<b>o</b>fruq baynan<u>a</u> wabayna alqawmi alf<u>a</u>siqeen<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>25.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">28</font></span> He said: "O my Lord!<br> I have power only<br>Over myself and my brother:<br> So separate us from this<br> Rebellious people!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_026.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>26.  Q<u>a</u>la fa-innah<u>a</u> mu<u>h</u>arramatun AAalayhim arbaAAeena sanatan yateehoona fee al-ar<u>d</u>i fal<u>a</u> ta/sa AAal<u>a</u> alqawmi alf<u>a</u>siqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>26.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">29</font></span> God said: "Therefore<br> Will the land be out<br> Of their reach for forty years:<br> In distraction will they<br> Wander through the land:<br> But sorrow thou not<br> Over these rebellious people.</p> <div class="filenav">
