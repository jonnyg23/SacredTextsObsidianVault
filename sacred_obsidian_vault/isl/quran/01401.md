 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XIV.: Ibrāhīm, or Abraham.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/014/014_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  Alif-l<u>a</u>m-r<u>a</u> kit<u>a</u>bun anzaln<u>a</u>hu ilayka litukhrija a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>sa mina a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i>ulum<u>a</u>ti il<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nnoori bi-i<u>th</u>ni rabbihim il<u>a</u> <u>s</u>ir<u>at</u>i alAAazeezi al<u>h</u>ameed<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>1.  A. L. R. A Book<br> Which We have revealed<br> Unto thee, in order that<br> Thou mightest lead mankind<br> Out of the depths of darkness<br> Into light—by the leave<br> Of their Lord—to the Way<br> Of (Him) the Exalted in Power,<br> Worthy of all Praise!—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/014/014_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  All<u>a</u>hi alla<u>th</u>ee lahu m<u>a</u> fee a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wam<u>a</u> fee al-ar<u>d</u>i wawaylun lilk<u>a</u>fireena min AAa<u>tha</u>bin shadeed<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>2.  Of God, to Whom do belong<br> All things in the heavens<br> And on earth!<br> But alas for the Unbelievers<br> For a terrible Penalty<br> (Their Unfaith will bring them)!—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/014/014_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  Alla<u>th</u>eena yasta<u>h</u>ibboona al<u>h</u>ay<u>a</u>ta a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> al-<u>a</u>khirati waya<u>s</u>uddoona AAan sabeeli All<u>a</u>hi wayabghoonah<u>a</u> AAiwajan ol<u>a</u>-ika fee <u>d</u>al<u>a</u>lin baAAeed<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>3.  Those who love the life<br> Of this world more than<br> The Hereafter, who hinder (men)<br> From the Path of God<br> And seek therein something crooked:<br> They are astray<br> By a long distance.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/014/014_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  Wam<u>a</u> arsaln<u>a</u> min rasoolin ill<u>a</u> bilis<u>a</u>ni qawmihi liyubayyina lahum fayu<u>d</u>illu All<u>a</u>hu man yash<u>a</u>o wayahdee man yash<u>a</u>o wahuwa alAAazeezu al<u>h</u>akeem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>4.  We sent not an apostle<br> Except (to teach) in the language<br> Of his (own) people, in order<br> To make (things) clear to them.<br> Now God leaves straying<br> Those whom He pleases<br> And guides whom He pleases:<br> And He is Exalted in Power,<br> Full of Wisdom.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/014/014_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  Walaqad arsaln<u>a</u> moos<u>a</u> bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin<u>a</u> an akhrij qawmaka mina a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i>ulum<u>a</u>ti il<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nnoori wa<u>th</u>akkirhum bi-ayy<u>a</u>mi All<u>a</u>hi inna fee <u>tha</u>lika la<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin likulli <u>s</u>abb<u>a</u>rin shakoor<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>5.  We sent Moses with Our Signs<br> (And the command). "Bring out<br> Thy people from the depths<br> Of darkness into light,<br> And teach them to remember<br> The Days of God." Verily<br> In this there are Signs<br> For such as are firmly patient<br> And constant,—grateful and appreciative.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/014/014_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  Wa-i<u>th</u> q<u>a</u>la moos<u>a</u> liqawmihi o<u>th</u>kuroo niAAmata All<u>a</u>hi AAalaykum i<u>th</u> anj<u>a</u>kum min <u>a</u>li firAAawna yasoomoonakum soo-a alAAa<u>tha</u>bi wayu<u>th</u>abbi<u>h</u>oona abn<u>a</u>akum wayasta<u>h</u>yoona nis<u>a</u>akum wafee <u>tha</u>likum bal<u>a</u>on min rabbikum AAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>6.  Remember! Moses said<br> To his people: "Call to mind<br> The favour of God to you<br> When He delivered you<br>From the people of Pharaoh:<br> They set you hard tasks<br> And punishments, slaughtered<br> Your sons, and let your women-folk<br> Live: therein was<br> A tremendous trial from your Lord."</p> <div class="filenav">
