 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra VIII.: Anfāl, or the Spoils of War.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 6</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/008/008_045.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>45.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo i<u>tha</u> laqeetum fi-atan fa<b>o</b>thbutoo wa<b>o</b><u>th</u>kuroo All<u>a</u>ha katheeran laAAallakum tufli<u>h</u>oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>45.  O ye who believe!<br> When ye meet a force,<br> Be firm, and call God<br> In remembrance much (and often);<br> That ye may prosper:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/008/008_046.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>46.  Waa<u>t</u>eeAAoo All<u>a</u>ha warasoolahu wal<u>a</u> tan<u>a</u>zaAAoo fatafshaloo wata<u>th</u>haba ree<u>h</u>ukum wa<b>i</b><u>s</u>biroo inna All<u>a</u>ha maAAa a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>bireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>46.  And obey God and His Apostle;<br> And fall into no disputes,<br>Lest ye lose heart<br> And your power depart;<br> And be patient and persevering:<br> For God is with those<br> Who patiently persevere:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/008/008_047.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>47.  Wal<u>a</u> takoonoo ka<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena kharajoo min diy<u>a</u>rihim ba<u>t</u>aran wari-<u>a</u>a a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si waya<u>s</u>uddoona AAan sabeeli All<u>a</u>hi wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu bim<u>a</u> yaAAmaloona mu<u>h</u>ee<u>t</u><b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>47.  And be not like those<br> Who started from their homes<br> Insolently and to be seen of men,<br> And to hinder (men)<br> From the path of God:<br> For God compasseth round about<br> All that they do.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/008/008_048.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>48.  Wa-i<u>th</u> zayyana lahumu a<b>l</b>shshay<u>ta</u>nu aAAm<u>a</u>lahum waq<u>a</u>la l<u>a</u> gh<u>a</u>liba lakumu alyawma mina a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si wa-innee j<u>a</u>run lakum falamm<u>a</u> tar<u>a</u>ati alfi-at<u>a</u>ni naka<u>s</u>a AAal<u>a</u> AAaqibayhi waq<u>a</u>la innee baree-on minkum innee ar<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> l<u>a</u> tarawna innee akh<u>a</u>fu All<u>a</u>ha wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu shadeedu alAAiq<u>a</u>b<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>48.  Remember Satan made<br> Their (sinful) acts seem<br> Alluring to them, and said:<br> "No one among men<br> Can overcome you this day,<br> While I am near to you":<br> But when the two forces<br> Came in sight of each other,<br> He turned on his heels,<br> And said: "Lo! I am clear<br> Of you; to! I see<br> What ye see not;<br> Lo! I fear God; for God<br> Is strict in punishment.</p> <div class="filenav">
