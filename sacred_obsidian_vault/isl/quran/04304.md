 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XLIII.: Zu<u>kh</u>ruf, or Gold Adornments.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 4</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/043/043_036.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>36.  Waman yaAAshu AAan <u>th</u>ikri a<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>m<u>a</u>ni nuqayyi<u>d</u> lahu shay<u>ta</u>nan fahuwa lahu qareen<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>36.  If anyone withdraws himself<br> From remembrance<br>Of (God) Most Gracious,<br> We appoint for him<br> An evil one, to be<br> An intimate companion to him.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/043/043_037.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>37.  Wa-innahum laya<u>s</u>uddoonahum AAani a<b>l</b>ssabeeli waya<u>h</u>saboona annahum muhtadoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>37.  Such (evil ones) really<br> Hinder them from the Path,<br> But they think that they<br> Are being guided aright!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/043/043_038.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>38.  <u>H</u>att<u>a</u> i<u>tha</u> j<u>a</u>an<u>a</u> q<u>a</u>la y<u>a</u> layta baynee wabaynaka buAAda almashriqayni fabi/sa alqareen<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>38.  At length, when (such a one)<br> Comes to Us, he says<br> (To his evil companion):<br> "Would that between me<br> And thee were the distance<br> Of East and West!" Ah!<br> Evil is the companion (indeed)!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/043/043_039.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>39.  Walan yanfaAAakumu alyawma i<u>th</u> <i><u>th</u></i>alamtum annakum fee alAAa<u>tha</u>bi mushtarikoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>39.  When ye have done wrong,<br> It will avail you nothing,<br> That day, that ye shall be<br> Partners in punishment!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/043/043_040.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>40.  Afaanta tusmiAAu a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>umma aw tahdee alAAumya waman k<u>a</u>na fee <u>d</u>al<u>a</u>lin mubeen<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>40.  Canst thou then make<br> The deaf to hear, or give<br> Direction to the blind<br> Or to such as (wander)<br> In manifest error?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/043/043_041.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>41.  Fa-imm<u>a</u> na<u>th</u>habanna bika fa-inn<u>a</u> minhum muntaqimoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>41.  Even if We take thee<br> Away, We shall be sure<br> To exact retribution from them,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/043/043_042.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>42.  Aw nuriyannaka alla<u>th</u>ee waAAadn<u>a</u>hum fa-inn<u>a</u> AAalayhim muqtadiroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>42.  Or We shall show thee<br> That (accomplished) which We<br> Have promised them:<br> For verily We shall<br> Prevail over them.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/043/043_043.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>43.  Fa<b>i</b>stamsik bi<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>ee oo<u>h</u>iya ilayka innaka AAal<u>a</u> <u>s</u>ir<u>at</u>in mustaqeem<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>43.  So hold thou fast<br> To the Revelation sent down<br> To thee: verily thou<br> Art on a Straight Way.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/043/043_044.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>44.  Wa-innahu la<u>th</u>ikrun laka waliqawmika wasawfa tus-aloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>44.  The (Qur-ān) is indeed<br> The Message, for thee<br> And for thy people;<br> And soon shall ye<br> (All) be brought to account.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/043/043_045.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>45.  Wa<b>i</b>s-al man arsaln<u>a</u> min qablika min rusulin<u>a</u> ajaAAaln<u>a</u> min dooni a<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>m<u>a</u>ni <u>a</u>lihatan yuAAbadoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>45.  And question thou our apostles<br> Whom We sent before thee;<br> Did We appoint any deities<br> Other than (God) Most Gracious,<br> To be worshipped?</p> <div class="filenav">
