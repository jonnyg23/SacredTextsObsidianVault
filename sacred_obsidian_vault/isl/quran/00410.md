 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra IV.: Nisāa, or The Women.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 10</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_071.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>71.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo khu<u>th</u>oo <u>h</u>i<u>th</u>rakum fa<b>i</b>nfiroo thub<u>a</u>tin awi infiroo jameeAA<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>71.  O ye who believe!<br> Take your precautions,<br> And either go forth in parties<br> Or go forth all together.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_072.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>72.  Wa-inna minkum laman layuba<u>tt</u>i-anna fa-in a<u>sa</u>batkum mu<u>s</u>eebatun q<u>a</u>la qad anAAama All<u>a</u>hu AAalayya i<u>th</u> lam akun maAAahum shaheed<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>72.  There are certainly among you<br> Men who would tarry behind:<br> If a misfortune befalls you,<br> They say: "God did favour us<br> In that we were not<br> Present among them."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_073.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>73.  Wala-in a<u>sa</u>bakum fa<u>d</u>lun mina All<u>a</u>hi layaqoolanna kaan lam takun baynakum wabaynahu mawaddatun y<u>a</u> laytanee kuntu maAAahum faafooza fawzan AAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>73.  But if good fortune comes to you<br> From God, they would be sure<br> To say—as if there had never been<br> Ties of affection between you and them—<br>"Oh! I wish I had been with them;<br> A fine thing should I then<br> Have made of it!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_074.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>74.  Falyuq<u>a</u>til fee sabeeli All<u>a</u>hi alla<u>th</u>eena yashroona al<u>h</u>ay<u>a</u>ta a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> bi<b>a</b>l-<u>a</u>khirati waman yuq<u>a</u>til fee sabeeli All<u>a</u>hi fayuqtal aw yaghlib fasawfa nu/teehi ajran AAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>74.  Let those fight<br> In the cause of God<br> Who sell the life of this world<br> For the Hereafter.<br> To him who fighteth<br> In the cause of God,—<br> Whether he is slain<br> Or gets victory<br> Soon shall We give him<br> A reward of great (value).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_075.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>75.  Wam<u>a</u> lakum l<u>a</u> tuq<u>a</u>tiloona fee sabeeli All<u>a</u>hi wa<b>a</b>lmusta<u>d</u>AAafeena mina a<b>l</b>rrij<u>a</u>li wa<b>al</b>nnis<u>a</u>-i wa<b>a</b>lwild<u>a</u>ni alla<u>th</u>eena yaqooloona rabban<u>a</u> akhrijn<u>a</u> min h<u>ath</u>ihi alqaryati a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limi ahluh<u>a</u> wa<b>i</b>jAAal lan<u>a</u> min ladunka waliyyan wa<b>i</b>jAAal lan<u>a</u> min ladunka na<u>s</u>eer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>75.  And why should ye not<br> Fight in the cause of God<br> And of those who, being weak,<br> Are ill-treated (and oppressed)?—<br> Men, women, and children,<br> Whose cry is: "Our Lord!<br> Rescue us from this town,<br> Whose people are oppressors;<br> And raise for us from Thee<br> One who will protect;<br> And raise for us from Thee<br> One who will help!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_076.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>76.  Alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo yuq<u>a</u>tiloona fee sabeeli All<u>a</u>hi wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo yuq<u>a</u>tiloona fee sabeeli a<b>l</b><u>tta</u>ghooti faq<u>a</u>tiloo awliy<u>a</u>a a<b>l</b>shshay<u>ta</u>ni inna kayda a<b>l</b>shshay<u>ta</u>ni k<u>a</u>na <u>d</u>aAAeef<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>76.  Those who believe<br> Fight in the cause of God,<br> And those who reject Faith<br> Fight in the cause of Evil:<br> So fight ye against the<br> Friends of Satan: feeble indeed<br> Is the cunning of Satan.</p> <div class="filenav">
