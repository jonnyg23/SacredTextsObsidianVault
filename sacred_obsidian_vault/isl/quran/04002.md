 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XL.: Mū-min, or The Believer.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 2</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/040/040_010.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>10.  Inna alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo yun<u>a</u>dawna lamaqtu All<u>a</u>hi akbaru min maqtikum anfusakum i<u>th</u> tudAAawna il<u>a</u> al-eem<u>a</u>ni fatakfuroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>10.  The Unbelievers will be<br> Addressed: "Greater was<br> The aversion of God to you<br> Than (is), your aversion<br>To yourselves, seeing that ye<br> Were called to the Faith<br> And ye used to refuse."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/040/040_011.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>11.  Q<u>a</u>loo rabban<u>a</u> amattan<u>a</u> ithnatayni waa<u>h</u>yaytan<u>a</u> ithnatayni fa<b>i</b>AAtarafn<u>a</u> bi<u>th</u>unoobin<u>a</u> fahal il<u>a</u> khuroojin min sabeel<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>11.  They will say: "Our Lord!<br> Twice hast Thou made us<br> Without life, and twice<br> Hast Thou given us Life!<br> Now have we recognised<br> Our sins: is there<br> Any way out (of this)?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/040/040_012.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>12.  <u>Tha</u>likum bi-annahu i<u>tha</u> duAAiya All<u>a</u>hu wa<u>h</u>dahu kafartum wa-in yushrak bihi tu/minoo fa<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>ukmu lill<u>a</u>hi alAAaliyyi alkabeer<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>12.  (The answer will be:)<br> "This is because, when<br> God was invoked as<br> The Only (object of worship),<br> Ye did reject Faith,<br> But when partners were<br> Joined to Him, ye believed!<br> The Command is with God,<br> Most High, Most Great!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/040/040_013.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>13.  Huwa alla<u>th</u>ee yureekum <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tihi wayunazzilu lakum mina a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>-i rizqan wam<u>a</u> yata<u>th</u>akkaru ill<u>a</u> man yuneeb<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>13.  He it is Who showeth<br> You His Signs, and sendeth<br> Down Sustenance for you<br> From the sky: but only<br> Those receive admonition<br> Who turn (to God).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/040/040_014.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>14.  Fa<b>o</b>dAAoo All<u>a</u>ha mukhli<u>s</u>eena lahu a<b>l</b>ddeena walaw kariha alk<u>a</u>firoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>14.  Call ye, then, upon God<br> With sincere devotion to Him,<br>Even though the Unbelievers<br> May detest it.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/040/040_015.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>15.  RafeeAAu a<b>l</b>ddaraj<u>a</u>ti <u>th</u>oo alAAarshi yulqee a<b>l</b>rroo<u>h</u>a min amrihi AAal<u>a</u> man yash<u>a</u>o min AAib<u>a</u>dihi liyun<u>th</u>ira yawma a<b>l</b>ttal<u>a</u>q<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>15.  Raised high above ranks<br> (Or degrees),<br> (He is) the Lord<br> Of the Throne (of authority):<br> By His Command doth He<br> Send the spirit (of inspiration)<br> To any of His servants<br> He pleases, that it may<br> Warn (men) of the Day<br> Of Mutual Meeting,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/040/040_016.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>16.  Yawma hum b<u>a</u>rizoona l<u>a</u> yakhf<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi minhum shay-on limani almulku alyawma lill<u>a</u>hi alw<u>ah</u>idi alqahh<u>a</u>r<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>16.  The Day whereon<br> They will (all) come forth:<br> Not a single thing<br> Concerning them is hidden<br> From God. Whose will be<br> The Dominion that Day?<br> That of God, the One,<br> The Irresistible!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/040/040_017.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>17.  Alyawma tujz<u>a</u> kullu nafsin bim<u>a</u> kasabat l<u>a</u> <i><u>th</u></i>ulma alyawma inna All<u>a</u>ha sareeAAu al<u>h</u>is<u>a</u>b<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>17.  That Day will every soul<br> Be requited for what<br> It earned; no injustice<br> Will there be that Day,<br> For God is Swift<br> In taking account.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/040/040_018.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>18.  Waan<u>th</u>irhum yawma al-<u>a</u>zifati i<u>th</u>i alquloobu lad<u>a</u> al<u>h</u>an<u>a</u>jiri k<u>a</u><i><u>th</u></i>imeena m<u>a</u> li<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limeena min <u>h</u>ameemin wal<u>a</u> shafeeAAin yu<u>ta</u>AA<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>18.  Warn them of the Day<br> That is (ever) drawing near,<br> When the Hearts will<br> (Come) right up to the Throats<br> To choke (them);<br> No intimate friend<br> Nor intercessor will the<br> wrong-doers<br> Have, who could be<br> Listened to.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/040/040_019.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>19.  YaAAlamu kh<u>a</u>-inata al-aAAyuni wam<u>a</u> tukhfee a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>udoor<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>19.  (God) knows of (the tricks)<br> That deceive with the eyes,<br> And all that the hearts<br> (Of men) conceal.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/040/040_020.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>20.  Wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu yaq<u>d</u>ee bi<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>aqqi wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena yadAAoona min doonihi l<u>a</u> yaq<u>d</u>oona bishay-in inna All<u>a</u>ha huwa a<b>l</b>ssameeAAu alba<u>s</u>eer<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>20.  And God will judge<br> With (Justice and) Truth:<br> But those whom (men)<br> Invoke besides Him, will<br> Not (be in a position)<br> To judge at all.<br> Verily it is God (alone)<br> Who hears and sees<br> (All things).</p> <div class="filenav">
