 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XLIII.: Zu<u>kh</u>ruf, or Gold Adornments.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 2</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/043/043_016.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>16.  Ami ittakha<u>th</u>a mimm<u>a</u> yakhluqu ban<u>a</u>tin waa<u>s</u>f<u>a</u>kum bi<b>a</b>lbaneen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>16.  What! Has He taken<br> Daughters out of what He<br> Himself creates, and granted<br> To you sons for choice?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/043/043_017.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>17.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> bushshira a<u>h</u>aduhum bim<u>a</u> <u>d</u>araba li<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>m<u>a</u>ni mathalan <i><u>th</u></i>alla wajhuhu muswaddan wahuwa ka<i><u>th</u></i>eem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>17.  When news is brought<br> To one of them of (the birth<br> Of) what he sets up<br> As a likeness to (God)<br> Most Gracious, his face<br> Darkens, and he is filled<br> With inward grief!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/043/043_018.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>18.  Awaman yunashshao fee al<u>h</u>ilyati wahuwa fee alkhi<u>sa</u>mi ghayru mubeen<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>18.  Is then one brought up<br> Among trinkets, and unable<br> To give a clear account<br> In a dispute (to be<br> Associated with God)?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/043/043_019.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>19.  WajaAAaloo almal<u>a</u>-ikata alla<u>th</u>eena hum AAib<u>a</u>du a<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>m<u>a</u>ni in<u>a</u>than ashahidoo khalqahum satuktabu shah<u>a</u>datuhum wayus-aloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>19.  And they make into females<br> Angels who themselves serve<br>God. Did they witness<br> Their creation? Their evidence<br> Will be recorded, and they<br> Will be called to account!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/043/043_020.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>20.  Waq<u>a</u>loo law sh<u>a</u>a a<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>m<u>a</u>nu m<u>a</u> AAabadn<u>a</u>hum m<u>a</u> lahum bi<u>tha</u>lika min AAilmin in hum ill<u>a</u> yakhru<u>s</u>oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>20.  ("Ah!") they say, "If<br> It had been the Will<br> Of (God) Most Gracious,<br> We should not have<br> Worshipped such (deities)!"<br> Of that they have<br> No knowledge! They<br> Do nothing but lie!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/043/043_021.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>21.  Am <u>a</u>tayn<u>a</u>hum kit<u>a</u>ban min qablihi fahum bihi mustamsikoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>21.  What! have We given them<br> A Book before this,<br> To which they are<br> Holding fast?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/043/043_022.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>22.  Bal q<u>a</u>loo inn<u>a</u> wajadn<u>a</u> <u>a</u>b<u>a</u>an<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> ommatin wa-inn<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> <u>a</u>th<u>a</u>rihim muhtadoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>22.  Nay! they say: "We found<br> Our fathers following<br> A certain religion,<br> And we do guide ourselves<br> By their footsteps."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/043/043_023.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>23.  Waka<u>tha</u>lika m<u>a</u> arsaln<u>a</u> min qablika fee qaryatin min na<u>th</u>eerin ill<u>a</u> q<u>a</u>la mutrafooh<u>a</u> inn<u>a</u> wajadn<u>a</u> <u>a</u>b<u>a</u>an<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> ommatin wa-inn<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> <u>a</u>th<u>a</u>rihim muqtadoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>23.  Just in the same way,<br> Whenever We sent a Warner<br> Before thee to any people,<br> The wealthy ones among them<br> Said: "We found our fathers<br> Following a certain religion,<br> And we will certainly<br> Follow in their footsteps."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/043/043_024.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>24.  Q<u>a</u>la awa law ji/tukum bi-ahd<u>a</u> mimm<u>a</u> wajadtum AAalayhi <u>a</u>b<u>a</u>akum q<u>a</u>loo inn<u>a</u> bim<u>a</u> orsiltum bihi k<u>a</u>firoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>24.  He said: "What!<br> Even if I brought you<br> Better guidance than that<br> Which ye found<br> Your fathers following?"<br> They said: "For us,<br> We deny that ye (prophets)<br> Are sent (on a mission<br> At all)."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/043/043_025.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>25.  Fa<b>i</b>ntaqamn<u>a</u> minhum fa<b>o</b>n<i><u>th</u></i>ur kayfa k<u>a</u>na AA<u>a</u>qibatu almuka<u>thth</u>ibeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>25.  So We exacted retribution<br> From them: now see<br> What was the end<br> Of those who rejected (Truth)!</p> <div class="filenav">
