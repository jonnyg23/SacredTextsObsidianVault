 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXXVII.: Ṣāffāt, or Those Ranged in Ranks.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 4</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_114.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>114.  Walaqad manann<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> moos<u>a</u> wah<u>a</u>roon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>114.  Again, (of old,)<br> We bestowed Our favour<br>On Moses and Aaron,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_115.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>115.  Wanajjayn<u>a</u>hum<u>a</u> waqawmahum<u>a</u> mina alkarbi alAAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>115.  And We delivered them<br> And their people from<br> (Their) Great Calamity;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_116.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>116.  Wana<u>s</u>arn<u>a</u>hum fak<u>a</u>noo humu algh<u>a</u>libeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>116.  And We helped them,<br> So they overcame<br> (their troubles);</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_117.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>117.  Wa<u>a</u>tayn<u>a</u>hum<u>a</u> alkit<u>a</u>ba almustabeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>117.  And We gave them<br> The Book which helps<br> To make things clear;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_118.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>118.  Wahadayn<u>a</u>hum<u>a</u> a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>ir<u>at</u>a almustaqeem<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>118.  And We guided them<br> To the Straight Way.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_119.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>119.  Watarakn<u>a</u> AAalayhim<u>a</u> fee al-<u>a</u>khireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>119.  And We left (this blessing)<br> For them among generations<br> (To come) in later times:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_120.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>120.  Sal<u>a</u>mun AAal<u>a</u> moos<u>a</u> wah<u>a</u>roon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>120.  "Peace and salutation<br> To Moses and Aaron!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_121.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>121.  Inn<u>a</u> ka<u>tha</u>lika najzee almu<u>h</u>sineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>121.  Thus indeed do We reward<br> Those who do right.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_122.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>122.  Innahum<u>a</u> min AAib<u>a</u>din<u>a</u> almu/mineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>122.  For they were two<br> Of Our believing Servants.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_123.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>123.  Wa-inna ily<u>a</u>sa lamina almursaleen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>123.  So also was Elias<br> Among those sent (by Us).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_124.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>124.  I<u>th</u> q<u>a</u>la liqawmihi al<u>a</u> tattaqoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>124.  Behold, he said<br> To his people,<br> "Will ye not fear (God)?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_125.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>125.  AtadAAoona baAAlan wata<u>th</u>aroona a<u>h</u>sana alkh<u>a</u>liqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>125.  "Will ye call upon Baal<br> And forsake the Best<br> Of Creators,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_126.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>126.  All<u>a</u>ha rabbakum warabba <u>a</u>b<u>a</u>-ikumu al-awwaleen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>126.  "God, your Lord and Cherisher<br> And the Lord and Cherisher<br> Of your fathers of old?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_127.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>127.  Faka<u>thth</u>aboohu fa-innahum lamu<u>hd</u>aroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>127.  But they rejected him,<br> And they will certainly<br> Be called up (for punishment),—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_128.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>128.  Ill<u>a</u> AAib<u>a</u>da All<u>a</u>hi almukhla<u>s</u>een<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>128.  Except the sincere and devoted<br> Servants of God (among them).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_129.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>129.  Watarakn<u>a</u> AAalayhi fee al-<u>a</u>khireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>129.  And We left (this blessing)<br> For him among generations<br> (To come) in later times:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_130.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>130.  Sal<u>a</u>mun AAal<u>a</u> il y<u>a</u>seen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>130.  "Peace and salutation<br> To such as Elias!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_131.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>131.  Inn<u>a</u> ka<u>tha</u>lika najzee almu<u>h</u>sineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>131.  Thus indeed do We reward<br> Those who do right.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_132.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>132.  Innahu min AAib<u>a</u>din<u>a</u> almu/mineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>132.  For he was one<br> Of Our believing Servants.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_133.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>133.  Wa-inna loo<u>t</u>an lamina almursaleen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>133.  So also was Lot<br> Among those sent (by Us).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_134.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>134.  I<u>th</u> najjayn<u>a</u>hu waahlahu ajmaAAeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>134.  Behold, We delivered him<br> And his adherents, all</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_135.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>135.  Ill<u>a</u> AAajoozan fee algh<u>a</u>bireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>135.  Except an old woman<br> Who was among those<br> Who lagged behind:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_136.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>136.  Thumma dammarn<u>a</u> al-<u>a</u>khareen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>136.  Then We destroyed<br> The rest.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_137.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>137.  Wa-innakum latamurroona AAalayhim mu<u>s</u>bi<u>h</u>een<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>137.  Verily, ye pass<br> By their (sites),<br> By day—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_138.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>138.  Wabi<b>a</b>llayli afal<u>a</u> taAAqiloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>138.  And by night:<br> Will ye not understand?</p> <div class="filenav">
