 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXI.: Anbiyāa, or The Prophets</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 5</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_076.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>76.  Wanoo<u>h</u>an i<u>th</u> n<u>a</u>d<u>a</u> min qablu fa<b>i</b>stajabn<u>a</u> lahu fanajjayn<u>a</u>hu waahlahu mina alkarbi alAAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>76.  (Remember) Noah, when<br> He cried (to Us) aforetime:<br> We listened to his (prayer)<br> And delivered him and his<br> Family from great distress.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_077.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>77.  Wana<u>s</u>arn<u>a</u>hu mina alqawmi alla<u>th</u>eena ka<u>thth</u>aboo bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin<u>a</u> innahum k<u>a</u>noo qawma saw-in faaghraqn<u>a</u>hum ajmaAAeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>77.  We helped him against<br> People who rejected Our Signs:<br> Truly they were a people<br> Given to Evil: so We<br> Drowned them (in the Flood)<br> All together.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_078.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>78.  Wad<u>a</u>wooda wasulaym<u>a</u>na i<u>th</u> ya<u>h</u>kum<u>a</u>ni fee al<u>h</u>arthi i<u>th</u> nafashat feehi ghanamu alqawmi wakunn<u>a</u> li<u>h</u>ukmihim sh<u>a</u>hideen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>78.  And remember David<br> And Solomon, when they<br> Gave judgment in the matter<br> Of the field into which<br> The sheep of certain people<br> Had strayed by night:<br> We did witness their judgment.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_079.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>79.  Fafahhamn<u>a</u>h<u>a</u> sulaym<u>a</u>na wakullan <u>a</u>tayn<u>a</u> <u>h</u>ukman waAAilman wasakhkharn<u>a</u> maAAa d<u>a</u>wooda aljib<u>a</u>la yusabbi<u>h</u>na wa<b>al</b><u>tt</u>ayra wakunn<u>a</u> f<u>a</u>AAileen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>79.  To Solomon We inspired<br> The (right) understanding<br> Of the matter: to each<br> (Of them) We gave Judgment<br> And Knowledge; it was<br> Our power that made<br> The hills and the birds<br> Celebrate Our praises,<br> With David: it was We<br> Who did (all these things).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_080.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>80.  WaAAallamn<u>a</u>hu <u>s</u>anAAata laboosin lakum litu<u>hs</u>inakum min ba/sikum fahal antum sh<u>a</u>kiroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>80.  It was We Who taught him<br> The making of coats of mail<br>For your benefit, to guard<br> You from each other's violence:<br> Will ye then be grateful?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_081.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>81.  Walisulaym<u>a</u>na a<b>l</b>rree<u>h</u>a AA<u>as</u>ifatan tajree bi-amrihi il<u>a</u> al-ar<u>d</u>i allatee b<u>a</u>rakn<u>a</u> feeh<u>a</u> wakunn<u>a</u> bikulli shay-in AA<u>a</u>limeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>81.  (It was Our power that<br> Made) the violent (unruly)<br> Wind flow (tamely) for Solomon,<br> To his order, to the land<br> Which We had blessed:<br> For We do know all things.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_082.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>82.  Wamina a<b>l</b>shshay<u>at</u>eeni man yaghoo<u>s</u>oona lahu wayaAAmaloona AAamalan doona <u>tha</u>lika wakunn<u>a</u> lahum <u>ha</u>fi<i><u>th</u></i>een<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>82.  And of the evil ones,<br> Were some who dived<br> For him, and did other work<br> Besides; and it was We<br> Who guarded them.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_083.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>83.  Waayyooba i<u>th</u> n<u>a</u>d<u>a</u> rabbahu annee massaniya a<b>l</b><u>dd</u>urru waanta ar<u>h</u>amu a<b>l</b>rr<u>ah</u>imeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>83.  And (remember) Job, when<br> He cried to his Lord,<br> "Truly distress has seized me,<br> But Thou art the Most<br>Merciful of those that are<br> Merciful."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_084.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>84.  Fa<b>i</b>stajabn<u>a</u> lahu fakashafn<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> bihi min <u>d</u>urrin wa<u>a</u>tayn<u>a</u>hu ahlahu wamithlahum maAAahum ra<u>h</u>matan min AAindin<u>a</u> wa<u>th</u>ikr<u>a</u> lilAA<u>a</u>bideen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>84.  So We listened to him:<br> We removed the distress<br> That was on him,<br> And We restored his people<br> To him, and doubled<br> Their number,—as a Grace<br> From Ourselves, and a thing<br> For commemoration, for all<br> Who serve Us.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_085.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>85.  Wa-ism<u>a</u>AAeela wa-idreesa wa<u>tha</u> alkifli kullun mina a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>bireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>85.  And (remember) Ismā‘īl,<br> Idrīs, and Ẓul-kifl, all<br> (Men) of constancy and patience;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_086.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>86.  Waadkhaln<u>a</u>hum fee ra<u>h</u>matin<u>a</u> innahum mina a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>li<u>h</u>een<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>86.  We admitted them to<br> Our Mercy: for they<br> Were of the Righteous ones.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_087.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>87.  Wa<u>tha</u> a<b>l</b>nnooni i<u>th</u> <u>th</u>ahaba mugh<u>ad</u>iban fa<i><u>th</u></i>anna an lan naqdira AAalayhi fan<u>a</u>d<u>a</u> fee a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i>ulum<u>a</u>ti an l<u>a</u> il<u>a</u>ha ill<u>a</u> anta sub<u>ha</u>naka innee kuntu mina a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>87.  And remember Ẓun-nūn,<br> When he departed in wrath:<br> He imagined that We<br> Had no power over him!<br> But he cried through the depths<br> Of darkness, "There is<br> No god but Thou:<br> Glory to Thee: I was<br> Indeed wrong!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_088.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>88.  Fa<b>i</b>stajabn<u>a</u> lahu wanajjayn<u>a</u>hu mina alghammi waka<u>tha</u>lika nunjee almu/mineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>88.  So We listened to him:<br> And delivered him from<br> Distress: and thus do We<br> Deliver those who have faith.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_089.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>89.  Wazakariyy<u>a</u> i<u>th</u> n<u>a</u>d<u>a</u> rabbahu rabbi l<u>a</u> ta<u>th</u>arnee fardan waanta khayru alw<u>a</u>ritheen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>89.  And (remember) Zakarīyā,<br> When he cried to his Lord:<br> "O my Lord! leave me not<br> Without offspring, though Thou<br> Art the best of inheritors."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_090.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>90.  Fa<b>i</b>stajabn<u>a</u> lahu wawahabn<u>a</u> lahu ya<u>h</u>y<u>a</u> waa<u>s</u>la<u>h</u>n<u>a</u> lahu zawjahu innahum k<u>a</u>noo yus<u>a</u>riAAoona fee alkhayr<u>a</u>ti wayadAAoonan<u>a</u> raghaban warahaban wak<u>a</u>noo lan<u>a</u> kh<u>a</u>shiAAeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>90.  So We listened to him:<br> And We granted him<br>Yabya: We cured his wife's<br> (Barrenness) for him. These (three)<br> Were ever quick in emulation<br> In good works: they used<br> To call on Us with love<br> And reverence, and humble themselves<br> Before Us.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_091.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>91.  Wa<b>a</b>llatee a<u>hs</u>anat farjah<u>a</u> fanafakhn<u>a</u> feeh<u>a</u> min roo<u>h</u>in<u>a</u> wajaAAaln<u>a</u>h<u>a</u> wa<b>i</b>bnah<u>a</u> <u>a</u>yatan lilAA<u>a</u>lameen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>91.  And (remember) her who<br> Guarded her chastity:<br> We breathed into her<br> Of Our Spirit, and We<br> Made her and her son<br> A Sign for all peoples.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_092.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>92.  Inna h<u>ath</u>ihi ommatukum ommatan w<u>ah</u>idatan waan<u>a</u> rabbukum faoAAbudoon<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>92.  Verily, this Brotherhood<br> Of yours is a single Brotherhood,<br> And I am your Lord<br> And Cherisher: therefore<br> Serve Me (and no other).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/021/021_093.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>93.  Wataqa<u>tt</u>aAAoo amrahum baynahum kullun ilayn<u>a</u> r<u>a</u>jiAAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>93.  But (later generations) cut off<br> Their affair (of unity),<br> One from another: (yet)<br> Will they all return to Us.</p> <div class="filenav">
