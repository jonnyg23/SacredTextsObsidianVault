 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/047/047_012.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>12.  Inna All<u>a</u>ha yudkhilu alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo waAAamiloo a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>li<u>ha</u>ti jann<u>a</u>tin tajree min ta<u>h</u>tih<u>a</u> al-anh<u>a</u>ru wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo yatamattaAAoona waya/kuloona kam<u>a</u> ta/kulu al-anAA<u>a</u>mu wa<b>al</b>nn<u>a</u>ru mathwan lahum    </p>
 <p>12.  Verily God will admit<br> Those who believe and do<br> Righteous deeds, to Gardens<br> Beneath which rivers flow;<br>While those who reject God<br> Will enjoy (this world)<br> And eat as cattle eat;<br> And the Fire will<br> Be their abode.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/047/047_013.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>13.  Wakaayyin min qaryatin hiya ashaddu quwwatan min qaryatika allatee akhrajatka ahlakn<u>a</u>hum fal<u>a</u> n<u>as</u>ira lahum    </p>
 <p>13.  And how many cities,<br> With more power than<br> Thy city which has<br> Driven thee out,<br> Have We destroyed<br> (For their sins)?<br> And there was none<br> To aid them.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/047/047_014.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>14.  Afaman k<u>a</u>na AAal<u>a</u> bayyinatin min rabbihi kaman zuyyina lahu soo-o AAamalihi wa<b>i</b>ttabaAAoo ahw<u>a</u>ahum    </p>
 <p>14.  Is then one who is<br> On a clear (Path)<br> From his Lord,<br> No better than one<br> To whom the evil<br> Of his conduct seems pleasing,<br> And such as follow<br> Their own lusts?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/047/047_015.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>15.  Mathalu aljannati allatee wuAAida almuttaqoona feeh<u>a</u> anh<u>a</u>run min m<u>a</u>-in ghayri <u>a</u>sinin waanh<u>a</u>run min labanin lam yataghayyar <u>t</u>aAAmuhu waanh<u>a</u>run min khamrin la<u>thth</u>atin li<b>l</b>shsh<u>a</u>ribeena waanh<u>a</u>run min AAasalin mu<u>s</u>affan walahum feeh<u>a</u> min kulli a<b>l</b>ththamar<u>a</u>ti wamaghfiratun min rabbihim kaman huwa kh<u>a</u>lidun fee a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>ri wasuqoo m<u>a</u>an <u>h</u>ameeman faqa<u>tt</u>aAAa amAA<u>a</u>ahum   </p>
 <p>15.  (Here is) a Parable<br> Of the Garden which<br> The righteous are promised:<br> In it are rivers<br> Of water incorruptible;<br> Rivers of milk<br> Of which the taste<br> Never changes; rivers<br>Of wine, a joy<br> To those who drink;<br> And rivers of honey<br> Pure and clear. In it<br> There are for them<br> All kinds of fruits;<br> And Grace from their Lord.<br> (Can those in such Bliss)<br> Be compared to such as<br> Shall dwell for ever<br> In the Fire, and be given,<br> To drink, boiling water,<br> So that it cuts up<br> Their bowels (to pieces)?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/047/047_016.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>16.  Waminhum man yastamiAAu ilayka <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> i<u>tha</u> kharajoo min AAindika q<u>a</u>loo lilla<u>th</u>eena ootoo alAAilma m<u>atha</u> q<u>a</u>la <u>a</u>nifan ol<u>a</u>-ika alla<u>th</u>eena <u>t</u>abaAAa All<u>a</u>hu AAal<u>a</u> quloobihim wa<b>i</b>ttabaAAoo ahw<u>a</u>ahum    </p>
 <p>16.  And among them are men<br> Who listen to thee,<br> But in the end, when they<br> Go out from thee,<br> They say to those who<br> Have received Knowledge,<br> "What is it he said<br> Just then?" Such are<br> Men whose hearts God<br> Has sealed, and who<br> Follow their own lusts.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/047/047_017.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>17.  Wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena ihtadaw z<u>a</u>dahum hudan wa<u>a</u>t<u>a</u>hum taqw<u>a</u>hum   </p>
 <p>17.  But to those who receive<br> Guidance, He increases<br> The (light of) Guidance,<br> And bestows on them<br> Their Piety and Restraint<br> (From evil).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/047/047_018.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>18.  Fahal yan<i><u>th</u></i>uroona ill<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>ss<u>a</u>AAata an ta/tiyahum baghtatan faqad j<u>a</u>a ashr<u>at</u>uh<u>a</u> faann<u>a</u> lahum i<u>tha</u> j<u>a</u>at-hum <u>th</u>ikr<u>a</u>hum   </p>
 <p>18.  Do they then only wait<br> For the Hour,—that it<br> Should come on them<br> Of a sudden? But already<br> Have come some tokens<br> Thereof, and when it<br> (Actually) is on them,<br> How can they benefit<br> Then by their admonition?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/047/047_019.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>19.  Fa<b>i</b>AAlam annahu l<u>a</u> il<u>a</u>ha ill<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hu wa<b>i</b>staghfir li<u>th</u>anbika walilmu/mineena wa<b>a</b>lmu/min<u>a</u>ti wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu yaAAlamu mutaqallabakum wamathw<u>a</u>kum   </p>
 <p>19.  Know, therefore, that<br> There is no god<br> But God, and ask<br> Forgiveness for thy fault,<br> And for the men<br> And women who believe:<br> For God knows how ye<br> Move about and how<br> Ye dwell in your homes.</p> <div class="filenav">
