 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XL.: Mū-min, or The Believer.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 7</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/040/040_061.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>61.  All<u>a</u>hu alla<u>th</u>ee jaAAala lakumu allayla litaskunoo feehi wa<b>al</b>nnah<u>a</u>ra mub<u>s</u>iran inna All<u>a</u>ha la<u>th</u>oo fa<u>d</u>lin AAal<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si wal<u>a</u>kinna akthara a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si l<u>a</u> yashkuroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>61.  It is God Who has<br> Made the Night for you,<br> That ye may rest therein,<br> And the Day, as that<br> Which helps (you) to see.<br> Verily God is Full of<br> Grace and Bounty to men:<br> Yet most men give<br> No thanks.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/040/040_062.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>62.  <u>Tha</u>likumu All<u>a</u>hu rabbukum kh<u>a</u>liqu kulli shay-in l<u>a</u> il<u>a</u>ha ill<u>a</u> huwa faann<u>a</u> tu/fakoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>62.  Such is God, your Lord,<br> The Creator of all things.<br> There is no god but He:<br> Then how ye are delude<br> Away from the Truth!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/040/040_063.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>63.  Ka<u>tha</u>lika yu/faku alla<u>th</u>eena k<u>a</u>noo bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>ti All<u>a</u>hi yaj<u>h</u>adoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>63.  Thus are deluded those<br> Who are wont to reject<br> The Signs of God.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/040/040_064.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>64.  All<u>a</u>hu alla<u>th</u>ee jaAAala lakumu al-ar<u>d</u>a qar<u>a</u>ran wa<b>al</b>ssam<u>a</u>a bin<u>a</u>an wa<u>s</u>awwarakum faa<u>h</u>sana <u>s</u>uwarakum warazaqakum mina a<b>l</b><u>tt</u>ayyib<u>a</u>ti <u>tha</u>likumu All<u>a</u>hu rabbukum fatab<u>a</u>raka All<u>a</u>hu rabbu alAA<u>a</u>lameen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>64.  It is God Who has<br> Made for you the earth<br>As a resting place,<br> And the sky as a canopy,<br> And has given you shape—<br> And made your shapes<br> Beautiful,—and has provided<br> For you Sustenance,<br> Of things pure and good;—<br> Such is God your Lord.<br> So Glory to God,<br> The Lord of the Worlds!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/040/040_065.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>65.  Huwa al<u>h</u>ayyu l<u>a</u> il<u>a</u>ha ill<u>a</u> huwa fa<b>o</b>dAAoohu mukhli<u>s</u>eena lahu a<b>l</b>ddeena al<u>h</u>amdu lill<u>a</u>hi rabbi alAA<u>a</u>lameen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>65.  He is the Living (One):<br> There is no god but He:<br> Call upon Him, giving Him<br> Sincere devotion. Praise he<br> To God, Lord of the Worlds!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/040/040_066.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>66.  Qul innee nuheetu an aAAbuda alla<u>th</u>eena tadAAoona min dooni All<u>a</u>hi lamm<u>a</u> j<u>a</u>aniya albayyin<u>a</u>tu min rabbee waomirtu an oslima lirabbi alAA<u>a</u>lameen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>66.  Say: "I have been forbidden<br> To invoke those whom ye<br> Invoke besides God,—seeing that<br> The Clear Signs have come<br> To me from my Lord;<br> And I have been commanded<br> To bow (in Islām)<br> To the Lord of the Worlds."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/040/040_067.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>67.  Huwa alla<u>th</u>ee khalaqakum min tur<u>a</u>bin thumma min nu<u>t</u>fatin thumma min AAalaqatin thumma yukhrijukum <u>t</u>iflan thumma litablughoo ashuddakum thumma litakoonoo shuyookhan waminkum man yutawaff<u>a</u> min qablu walitablughoo ajalan musamman walaAAallakum taAAqiloon<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>67.  It is He Who has<br> Created you from dust,<br>Then from a sperm-drop,<br> Then from a leech-like clot;<br> Then does He get you<br> Out (into the light)<br> As a child: then lets you<br> (Grow and) reach your age<br> Of full strength; then<br> Lets you become old,—<br> Though of you there are<br> Some who die before;—<br> And lets you reach<br> A Term appointed;<br> In order that ye<br> May learn wisdom.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/040/040_068.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>68.  Huwa alla<u>th</u>ee yu<u>h</u>yee wayumeetu fa-i<u>tha</u> qa<u>da</u> amran fa-innam<u>a</u> yaqoolu lahu kun fayakoon<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>68.  It is He Who gives Life<br> And Death; and when He<br> Decides upon an affair,<br> He says to it, "Be",<br> And it is.</p> <div class="filenav">
