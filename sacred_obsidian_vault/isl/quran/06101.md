 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra LXI.: Ṣaff, or Battle Array.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/061/061_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  Sabba<u>h</u>a lill<u>a</u>hi m<u>a</u> fee a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wam<u>a</u> fee al-ar<u>d</u>i wahuwa alAAazeezu al<u>h</u>akeem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>1.  Whatever is<br> In the heavens and<br> On earth, let it declare<br> The Praises and Glory<br> Of God: for He is<br> The Exalted in Might,<br> The Wise.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/061/061_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo lima taqooloona m<u>a</u> l<u>a</u> tafAAaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>2.  O ye who believe!<br> Why say ye that<br> Which ye do not?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/061/061_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  Kabura maqtan AAinda All<u>a</u>hi an taqooloo m<u>a</u> l<u>a</u> tafAAaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>3.  Grievously odious is it<br> In the sight of God<br> That ye say that<br> Which ye do not.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/061/061_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  Inna All<u>a</u>ha yu<u>h</u>ibbu alla<u>th</u>eena yuq<u>a</u>tiloona fee sabeelihi <u>s</u>affan kaannahum buny<u>a</u>nun mar<u>s</u>oo<u>s</u><b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>4.  Truly God loves those<br> Who fight in His Cause<br> In battle array, as if<br> They were a solid<br> Cemented structure.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/061/061_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  Wa-i<u>th</u> q<u>a</u>la moos<u>a</u> liqawmihi y<u>a</u> qawmi lima tu/<u>th</u>oonanee waqad taAAlamoona annee rasoolu All<u>a</u>hi ilaykum falamm<u>a</u> z<u>a</u>ghoo az<u>a</u>gha All<u>a</u>hu quloobahum wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu l<u>a</u> yahdee alqawma alf<u>a</u>siqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>5.  And remember, Moses said<br> To his people: "O my people!<br>Why do ye vex and insult<br> Me, though ye know<br> That I am the apostle<br> Of God (sent) to you?"<br> Then when they went wrong,<br> God let their hearts go wrong.<br> For God guides not those<br> Who are rebellious transgressors.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/061/061_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  Wa-i<u>th</u> q<u>a</u>la AAees<u>a</u> ibnu maryama y<u>a</u> banee isr<u>a</u>-eela innee rasoolu All<u>a</u>hi ilaykum mu<u>s</u>addiqan lim<u>a</u> bayna yadayya mina a<b>l</b>ttawr<u>a</u>ti wamubashshiran birasoolin ya/tee min baAAdee ismuhu a<u>h</u>madu falamm<u>a</u> j<u>a</u>ahum bi<b>a</b>lbayyin<u>a</u>ti q<u>a</u>loo h<u>atha</u> si<u>h</u>run mubeen<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>6.  And remember, Jesus,<br> The son of Mary, said:<br> "O Children of Israel!<br> I am the apostle of God<br> (Sent) to you, confirming<br> The Law (which came)<br> Before me, and giving<br> Glad Tidings of an Apostle<br> To come after me,<br> Whose name shall be Aḥmad."<br> But when he came to them<br> With Clear Signs,<br> They said, "This is<br> Evident sorcery!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/061/061_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  Waman a<i><u>th</u></i>lamu mimmani iftar<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi alka<u>th</u>iba wahuwa yudAA<u>a</u> il<u>a</u> al-isl<u>a</u>mi wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu l<u>a</u> yahdee alqawma a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>7.  Who doth greater wrong<br> Than one who invents<br>Falsehood against God,<br> Even as he is being invited<br> To Islām? And God<br> Guides not those<br> Who do wrong.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/061/061_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  Yureedoona liyu<u>t</u>fi-oo noora All<u>a</u>hi bi-afw<u>a</u>hihim wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu mutimmu noorihi walaw kariha alk<u>a</u>firoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>8.  Their intention is<br> To extinguish God's Light<br> (By blowing) with their mouths:<br> But God will complete<br> (The revelation of) His Light,<br> Even though the Unbelievers<br> May detest (it).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/061/061_009.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>9.  Huwa alla<u>th</u>ee arsala rasoolahu bi<b>a</b>lhud<u>a</u> wadeeni al<u>h</u>aqqi liyu<i><u>th</u></i>hirahu AAal<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>ddeeni kullihi walaw kariha almushrikoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>9.  It is He Who has sent<br> His Apostle with Guidance<br> And the Religion of Truth,<br> That he may proclaim it<br> Over all religion,<br> Even though the Pagans<br> May detest (it).</p> <div class="filenav">
