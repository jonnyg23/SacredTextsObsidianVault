 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XLI.: Ḥā-mīm (Abbreviated Letters), or Ḥā-Mīm Sajda, or Fuṣṣilat</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 3</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/041/041_019.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>19.  Wayawma yu<u>h</u>sharu aAAd<u>a</u>o All<u>a</u>hi il<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>ri fahum yoozaAAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>19.  On the Day that<br> The enemies of God<br> Will be gathered together<br> To the Fire, they will<br> Be marched in ranks.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/041/041_020.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>20.  <u>H</u>att<u>a</u> i<u>tha</u> m<u>a</u> j<u>a</u>ooh<u>a</u> shahida AAalayhim samAAuhum waab<u>sa</u>ruhum wajulooduhum bim<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo yaAAmaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>20.  At length, when they reach<br> The (Fire), their hearing,<br> Their sight, and their skins<br> Will bear witness against them,<br> As to (all) their deeds.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/041/041_021.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>21.  Waq<u>a</u>loo lijuloodihim lima shahidtum AAalayn<u>a</u> q<u>a</u>loo an<u>t</u>aqan<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hu alla<u>th</u>ee an<u>t</u>aqa kulla shay-in wahuwa khalaqakum awwala marratin wa-ilayhi turjaAAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>21.  They will say to their skins:<br> "Why bear ye witness<br> Against us?" They will say:<br> "God hath given us speech,—<br> (He) Who giveth speech<br> To everything: He created<br> You for the first time,<br> And unto Him were ye<br> To return.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/041/041_022.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>22.  Wam<u>a</u> kuntum tastatiroona an yashhada AAalaykum samAAukum wal<u>a</u> ab<u>sa</u>rukum wal<u>a</u> juloodukum wal<u>a</u>kin <i><u>th</u></i>anantum anna All<u>a</u>ha l<u>a</u> yaAAlamu katheeran mimm<u>a</u> taAAmaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>22.  "Ye did not seek<br> To hide yourselves, lest<br>Your hearing, your sight,<br> And your skins should bear<br> Witness against you! But<br> Ye did think that God<br> Knew not many of <a href="errata.htm#6">the</a> things<br> That ye used to do!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/041/041_023.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>23.  Wa<u>tha</u>likum <i><u>th</u></i>annukumu alla<u>th</u>ee <i><u>th</u></i>anantum birabbikum ard<u>a</u>kum faa<u>s</u>ba<u>h</u>tum mina alkh<u>a</u>sireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>23.  "But this thought of yours<br> Which ye did entertain<br> Concerning your Lord, hath<br> Brought you to destruction,<br> And (now) have ye become<br> Of those utterly lost!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/041/041_024.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>24.  Fa-in ya<u>s</u>biroo fa<b>al</b>nn<u>a</u>ru mathwan lahum wa-in yastaAAtiboo fam<u>a</u> hum mina almuAAtabeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>24.  If, then, they have patience,<br> The Fire will be<br> A Home for them!<br> And if they beg<br> To be received into favour,<br> Into favour will they not<br> (Then) be received.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/041/041_025.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>25.  Waqayya<u>d</u>n<u>a</u> lahum quran<u>a</u>a fazayyanoo lahum m<u>a</u> bayna aydeehim wam<u>a</u> khalfahum wa<u>h</u>aqqa AAalayhimu alqawlu fee omamin qad khalat min qablihim mina aljinni wa<b>a</b>l-insi innahum k<u>a</u>noo kh<u>a</u>sireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>25.  And We have destined<br> For them intimate companions<br> (Of like nature), who made<br> Alluring to them what was<br> Before them and behind them;<br> And the sentence among<br> The previous generations of Jinns<br> And men; who have passed away,<br>Is proved against them;<br> For they are utterly lost.</p> <div class="filenav">
