 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_177.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>177.  Laysa albirra an tuwalloo wujoohakum qibala almashriqi wa<b>a</b>lmaghribi wal<u>a</u>kinna albirra man <u>a</u>mana bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi wa<b>a</b>lyawmi al-<u>a</u>khiri wa<b>a</b>lmal<u>a</u>-ikati wa<b>a</b>lkit<u>a</u>bi wa<b>al</b>nnabiyyeena wa<u>a</u>t<u>a</u> alm<u>a</u>la AAal<u>a</u> <u>h</u>ubbihi <u>th</u>awee alqurb<u>a</u> wa<b>a</b>lyat<u>a</u>m<u>a</u> wa<b>a</b>lmas<u>a</u>keena wa<b>i</b>bna alssabeeli wa<b>al</b>ss<u>a</u>-ileena wafee a<b>l</b>rriq<u>a</u>bi waaq<u>a</u>ma a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>al<u>a</u>ta wa<u>a</u>t<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>zzak<u>a</u>ta wa<b>a</b>lmoofoona biAAahdihim i<u>tha</u> AA<u>a</u>hadoo wa<b>al</b><u>ssa</u>bireena fee alba/s<u>a</u>-i wa<b>al</b><u>dd</u>arr<u>a</u>-i wa<u>h</u>eena alba/si ol<u>a</u>-ika alla<u>th</u>eena <u>s</u>adaqoo waol<u>a</u>-ika humu almuttaqoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>177.  It is not righteousness<br> That ye turn your faces<br> Towards East or West;<br> But it is righteousness—<br> To believe in God<br> And the Last Day,<br> And the Angels,<br> And the Book,<br> And the Messengers;<br> To spend of your substance,<br> Out of love for Him,<br> For your kin,<br> For orphans,<br> For the needy,<br> For the wayfarer,<br> For those who ask,<br> And for the ransom of slaves;<br> To be steadfast in prayer,<br> And practice regular charity;<br> To fulfil the contracts<br> Which ye have made;<br>And to be firm and patient,<br> In pain (or suffering)<br> And adversity,<br> And throughout<br> All periods of panic.<br> Such are the people<br> Of truth, the God-fearing.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_178.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>178.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo kutiba AAalaykumu alqi<u>sas</u>u fee alqatl<u>a</u> al<u>h</u>urru bi<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>urri wa<b>a</b>lAAabdu bi<b>a</b>lAAabdi wa<b>a</b>lonth<u>a</u> bi<b>a</b>lonth<u>a</u> faman AAufiya lahu min akheehi shay-on fa<b>i</b>ttib<u>a</u>AAun bi<b>a</b>lmaAAroofi waad<u>a</u>on ilayhi bi-i<u>h</u>s<u>a</u>nin <u>tha</u>lika takhfeefun min rabbikum wara<u>h</u>matun famani iAAtad<u>a</u> baAAda <u>tha</u>lika falahu AAa<u>tha</u>bun aleem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>178.  O ye who believe!<br> The law of equality<br> Is prescribed to you<br> In cases of murder:<br> The free for the free,<br> The slave for the slave,<br> The woman for the woman.<br> But if any remission<br> Is made by the brother<br> Of the slain, then grant<br> Any reasonable demand,<br> And compensate him<br>With handsome gratitude.<br> This is a concession<br> And a Mercy<br> From your Lord.<br> After this whoever<br> Exceeds the limits<br> Shall be in grave penalty.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_179.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>179.  Walakum fee alqi<u>sas</u>i <u>h</u>ay<u>a</u>tun y<u>a</u> olee al-alb<u>a</u>bi laAAallakum tattaqoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>179.  In the Law of Equality<br> There is (saving of) Life<br> To you, O ye men of understanding;<br> That ye may<br> Restrain yourselves.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_180.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>180.  Kutiba AAalaykum i<u>tha</u> <u>h</u>a<u>d</u>ara a<u>h</u>adakumu almawtu in taraka khayran alwa<u>s</u>iyyatu lilw<u>a</u>lidayni wa<b>a</b>l-aqrabeena bi<b>a</b>lmaAAroofi <u>h</u>aqqan AAal<u>a</u> almuttaqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>180.  It is prescribed,<br> When death approaches<br> Any of you, if he leave<br> Any goods, that he make a bequest<br> To parents and next of kin,<br> According to reasonable usage;<br> This is due<br> From the God-fearing.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_181.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>181.  Faman baddalahu baAAda m<u>a</u> samiAAahu fa-innam<u>a</u> ithmuhu AAal<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena yubaddiloonahu inna All<u>a</u>ha sameeAAun AAaleem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>181.  If anyone changes the bequest<br> After hearing it,<br> The guilt shall be on those<br> Who make the change.<br> For God hears and knows<br> (All things).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_182.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>182.  Faman kh<u>a</u>fa min moo<u>s</u>in janafan aw ithman faa<u>s</u>la<u>h</u>a baynahum fal<u>a</u> ithma AAalayhi inna All<u>a</u>ha ghafoorun ra<u>h</u>eem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>182.  But if anyone fears<br> Partiality or wrong-doing<br> On the part of the testator,<br> And makes peace between<br>(The parties concerned),<br> There is no wrong in him:<br> For God is Oft-forgiving,<br> Most Merciful.</p> <div class="filenav">
