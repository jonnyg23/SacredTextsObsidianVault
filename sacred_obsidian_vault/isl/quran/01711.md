 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XVII.: Banī Isrā-īl, or the Children of Israel,</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 11</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_094.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>94.  Wam<u>a</u> manaAAa a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>sa an yu/minoo i<u>th</u> j<u>a</u>ahumu alhud<u>a</u> ill<u>a</u> an q<u>a</u>loo abaAAatha All<u>a</u>hu basharan rasool<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>94.  What kept men back<br> From Belief when Guidance<br> Came to them, was nothing<br> But this: they said,<br> "Has God sent a man<br> (Like us) to be (His) Apostle?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_095.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>95.  Qul law k<u>a</u>na fee al-ar<u>d</u>i mal<u>a</u>-ikatun yamshoona mu<u>t</u>ma-inneena lanazzaln<u>a</u> AAalayhim mina a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>-i malakan rasool<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>95.  Say, "If there were settled,<br> On earth, angels walking about<br>In peace and quiet, We should<br> Certainly have sent them<br> Down from the heavens<br> An angel for an apostle."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_096.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>96.  Qul kaf<u>a</u> bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi shaheedan baynee wabaynakum innahu k<u>a</u>na biAAib<u>a</u>dihi khabeeran ba<u>s</u>eer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>96.  Say: "Enough is God<br> For a witness between me<br> And you: for He is<br> Well acquainted with His servants,<br> And He sees (all things).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_097.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>97.  Waman yahdi All<u>a</u>hu fahuwa almuhtadi waman yu<u>d</u>lil falan tajida lahum awliy<u>a</u>a min doonihi wana<u>h</u>shuruhum yawma alqiy<u>a</u>mati AAal<u>a</u> wujoohihim AAumyan wabukman wa<u>s</u>umman ma/w<u>a</u>hum jahannamu kullam<u>a</u> khabat zidn<u>a</u>hum saAAeer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>97.  It is he whom God guides,<br> That is on true guidance;<br> But he whom He leaves<br> Astray—for such wilt thou<br> Find no protector besides Him.<br> On the Day of Judgment<br> We shall gather them together,<br> Prone on their faces,<br> Blind, dumb, and deaf:<br> Their abode will be Hell:<br> Every time it shows abatement,<br> We shall increase for them<br> The fierceness of the Fire.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_098.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>98.  <u>Tha</u>lika jaz<u>a</u>ohum bi-annahum kafaroo bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin<u>a</u> waq<u>a</u>loo a-i<u>tha</u> kunn<u>a</u> AAi<i><u>th</u></i><u>a</u>man waruf<u>a</u>tan a-inn<u>a</u> lamabAAoothoona khalqan jadeed<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>98.  That is their recompense,<br> Because they rejected Our Signs,<br> And said, "When we are reduced<br> To bones and broken dust,<br> Should we really be raised up<br> (To be) a new Creation?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_099.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>99.  Awa lam yaraw anna All<u>a</u>ha alla<u>th</u>ee khalaqa a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>a q<u>a</u>dirun AAal<u>a</u> an yakhluqa mithlahum wajaAAala lahum ajalan l<u>a</u> rayba feehi faab<u>a</u> a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limoona ill<u>a</u> kufoor<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>99.  See they not that God,<br> Who created the heavens<br> And the earth, has power<br> To create the like of them<br> (Anew)? Only He has<br> Decreed a term appointed,<br> Of which there is no doubt.<br> But the unjust refuse<br> (To receive it) except<br> With ingratitude.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_100.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>100.  Qul law antum tamlikoona khaz<u>a</u>-ina ra<u>h</u>mati rabbee i<u>th</u>an laamsaktum khashyata al-inf<u>a</u>qi wak<u>a</u>na al-ins<u>a</u>nu qatoor<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>100.  Say: "If ye had<br> Control of the Treasures<br> Of the Mercy of my Lord,<br> Behold, ye would keep them<br> Back, for fear of spending<br> Them: for man<br> Is (ever) niggardly!"</p> <div class="filenav">
