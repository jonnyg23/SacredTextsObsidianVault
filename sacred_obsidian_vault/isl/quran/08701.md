 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra LXXXVII.: A’lā, or The Most High.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/087/087_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  Sabbi<u>h</u>i isma rabbika al-aAAl<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>1.  Glorify the name<br> Of' thy Guardian-Lord<br> Most High,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/087/087_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  Alla<u>th</u>ee khalaqa fasaww<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>2.  Who hath created,<br> And further, given<br> Order and proportion;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/087/087_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  Wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>ee qaddara fahad<u>a</u>    </p>
 <p>3.  Who hath ordained laws.<br> And granted guidance;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/087/087_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  Wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>ee akhraja almarAA<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>4.  And Who bringeth out<br> The (green and luscious) pasture,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/087/087_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  FajaAAalahu ghuth<u>a</u>an a<u>h</u>w<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>5.  And then doth make it<br> (But) swarthy stubble.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/087/087_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  Sanuqri-oka fal<u>a</u> tans<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>6.  By degrees shall We<br> Teach thee to declare<br>(The Message), so thou<br> Shalt not forget,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/087/087_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  Ill<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> sh<u>a</u>a All<u>a</u>hu innahu yaAAlamu aljahra wam<u>a</u> yakhf<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>7.  Except as God wills:<br> For He knoweth<br> What is manifest<br> And what is hidden.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/087/087_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  Wanuyassiruka lilyusr<u>a</u>    </p>
 <p>8.  And We will make it<br> Easy for thee (to follow)<br> The simple (Path).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/087/087_009.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>9.  Fa<u>th</u>akkir in nafaAAati a<b>l</b><u>thth</u>ikr<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>9.  Therefore give admonition<br> In case the admonition<br> Profits (the hearer).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/087/087_010.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>10.  Saya<u>thth</u>akkaru man yakhsh<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>10.  The admonition will be received<br> By those who fear (God):</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/087/087_011.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>11.  Wayatajannabuh<u>a</u> al-ashq<u>a</u>    </p>
 <p>11.  But it will be avoided<br> By those most unfortunate ones,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/087/087_012.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>12.  Alla<u>th</u>ee ya<u>s</u>l<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>ra alkubr<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>12.  Who will enter<br> The Great Fire,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/087/087_013.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>13.  Thumma l<u>a</u> yamootu feeh<u>a</u> wal<u>a</u> ya<u>h</u>y<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>13.  In which they will then<br> Neither die nor live.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/087/087_014.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>14.  Qad afla<u>h</u>a man tazakk<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>14.  But those will prosper<br> Who purify themselves,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/087/087_015.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>15.  Wa<u>th</u>akara isma rabbihi fa<u>s</u>all<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>15.  And glorify the name<br> Of their Guardian-Lord,<br> And (lift their hearts)<br> In Prayer.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/087/087_016.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>16.  Bal tu/thiroona al<u>h</u>ay<u>a</u>ta a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>16.  Nay (behold), ye prefer<br> The life of this world;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/087/087_017.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>17.  Wa<b>a</b>l-<u>a</u>khiratu khayrun waabq<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>17.  But the Hereafter<br> Is better and more enduring.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/087/087_018.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>18.  Inna h<u>atha</u> lafee a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>u<u>h</u>ufi al-ool<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>18.  And this is<br> In the Books<br> Of the earliest (Revelations),—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/087/087_019.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>19.  <u>S</u>u<u>h</u>ufi ibr<u>a</u>heema wamoos<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>19.  The Books of<br> Abraham and Moses.</p>  <div class="filenav">
