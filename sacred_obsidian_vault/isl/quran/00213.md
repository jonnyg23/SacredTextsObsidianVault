 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra II.: Baqara, or the Heifer.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 13</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_104.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>104.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo l<u>a</u> taqooloo r<u>a</u>AAin<u>a</u> waqooloo on<i><u>th</u></i>urn<u>a</u> wa<b>i</b>smaAAoo walilk<u>a</u>fireena AAa<u>tha</u>bun aleem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>104.  ye of Faith!<br> Say not (to the Apostle)<br> Words of ambiguous import,<br> But words of respect;<br> And hearken (to him):<br> To those without Faith<br> Is a grievous punishment.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_105.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>105.  M<u>a</u> yawaddu alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo min ahli alkit<u>a</u>bi wal<u>a</u> almushrikeena an yunazzala AAalaykum min khayrin min rabbikum wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu yakhta<u>ss</u>u bira<u>h</u>matihi man yash<u>a</u>o wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu <u>th</u>oo alfa<u>d</u>li alAAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>105.  It is never the wish<br> Of those without Faith<br> Among the People of the Book,<br> Nor of the Pagans,<br> That anything good<br> Should come down to you<br> From your Lord.<br> But God will choose<br> For His special Mercy<br> Whom He will—for God is<br> Lord of grace abounding.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_106.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>106.  M<u>a</u> nansakh min <u>a</u>yatin aw nunsih<u>a</u> na/ti bikhayrin minh<u>a</u> aw mithlih<u>a</u> alam taAAlam anna All<u>a</u>ha AAal<u>a</u> kulli shay-in qadeer<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>106.  None of Our revelations<br> Do We abrogate<br> Or cause to be forgotten,<br> But We substitute<br>Something better or similar:<br> Knowest thou not that God<br> Hath power over all things?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_107.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>107.  Alam taAAlam anna All<u>a</u>ha lahu mulku a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i wam<u>a</u> lakum min dooni All<u>a</u>hi min waliyyin wal<u>a</u> na<u>s</u>eer<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>107.  Knowest thou not<br> That to God belongeth<br> The dominion of the heavens<br> And the earth?<br> And besides Him ye have<br> Neither patron nor helper.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_108.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>108.  Am tureedoona an tas-aloo rasoolakum kam<u>a</u> su-ila moos<u>a</u> min qablu waman yatabaddali alkufra bi<b>a</b>l-eem<u>a</u>ni faqad <u>d</u>alla saw<u>a</u>a a<b>l</b>ssabeel<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>108.  Would ye question<br> Your Apostle as Moses's<br> Was questioned of old?<br> But whoever changeth<br> From Faith to Unbelief,<br> Hath strayed without doubt<br> From the even way.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_109.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>109.  Wadda katheerun min ahli alkit<u>a</u>bi law yaruddoonakum min baAAdi eem<u>a</u>nikum kuff<u>a</u>ran <u>h</u>asadan min AAindi anfusihim min baAAdi m<u>a</u> tabayyana lahumu al<u>h</u>aqqu fa<b>o</b>AAfoo wa<b>i</b><u>s</u>fa<u>h</u>oo <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> ya/tiya All<u>a</u>hu bi-amrihi inna All<u>a</u>ha AAal<u>a</u> kulli shay-in qadeer<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>109.  Quite a number of the People<br> Of the Book wish they could<br> Turn you (people) back<br> To infidelity after ye have believed,<br> From selfish envy,<br> After the Truth hath become<br> Manifest unto them:<br> But forgive and overlook,<br>Till God accomplish<br> His purpose; for God<br> Hath power over all things.'</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_110.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>110.  Waaqeemoo a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>al<u>a</u>ta wa<u>a</u>too a<b>l</b>zzak<u>a</u>ta wam<u>a</u> tuqaddimoo li-anfusikum min khayrin tajidoohu AAinda All<u>a</u>hi inna All<u>a</u>ha bim<u>a</u> taAAmaloona ba<u>s</u>eer<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>110.  And be steadfast in prayer<br> And regular in charity:<br> And whatever good<br> Ye send forth for your souls<br> Before you, ye shall find it<br> With God: for God sees<br> Well all that ye do.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_111.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>111.  Waq<u>a</u>loo lan yadkhula aljannata ill<u>a</u> man k<u>a</u>na hoodan aw na<u>sa</u>r<u>a</u> tilka am<u>a</u>niyyuhum qul h<u>a</u>too burh<u>a</u>nakum in kuntum <u>sa</u>diqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>111.  And they say: "None<br> Shall enter Paradise unless<br> He be a Jew or a Christian."<br> Those are their (vain) desires.<br> Say: "Produce your proof<br> If ye are truthful."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_112.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>112.  Bal<u>a</u> man aslama wajhahu lill<u>a</u>hi wahuwa mu<u>h</u>sinun falahu ajruhu AAinda rabbihi wal<u>a</u> khawfun AAalayhim wal<u>a</u> hum ya<u>h</u>zanoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>112.  Nay,—whoever submits<br> His whole self to God<br> And is a doer of good,<br> He will get his reward<br> With his Lord;<br> On such shall be no fear,<br> Nor shall they grieve.</p> <div class="filenav">
