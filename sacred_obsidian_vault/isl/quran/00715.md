 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra VII.: A’rāf, or the Heights</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 15</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_127.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>127.  Waq<u>a</u>la almalao min qawmi firAAawna ata<u>th</u>aru moos<u>a</u> waqawmahu liyufsidoo fee al-ar<u>d</u>i waya<u>th</u>araka wa<u>a</u>lihataka q<u>a</u>la sanuqattilu abn<u>a</u>ahum wanasta<u>h</u>yee nis<u>a</u>ahum wa-inn<u>a</u> fawqahum q<u>a</u>hiroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>127.  Said the chiefs of Pharaoh's<br> People: "Wilt thou leave<br> Moses and his people,<br> To spread mischief in the land,<br> And to abandon thee<br> And thy gods?" He said:<br> "Their male children will we<br> Slay; (only) their females<br> Will we save alive;<br> And we have over them<br>(Power) irresistible."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_128.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>128.  Q<u>a</u>la moos<u>a</u> liqawmihi istaAAeenoo bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi wa<b>i</b><u>s</u>biroo inna al-ar<u>d</u>a lill<u>a</u>hi yoorithuh<u>a</u> man yash<u>a</u>o min AAib<u>a</u>dihi wa<b>a</b>lAA<u>a</u>qibatu lilmuttaqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>128.  Said Moses to his people:<br> "Pray for help from God,.<br> And (wait) in patience and<br> constancy:<br> For the earth is God's,<br> To give as a heritage<br> To such of His servants<br> As He pleaseth; and the end<br> Is (best) for the righteous.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_129.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>129.  Q<u>a</u>loo oo<u>th</u>een<u>a</u> min qabli an ta/tiyan<u>a</u> wamin baAAdi m<u>a</u> ji/tan<u>a</u> q<u>a</u>la AAas<u>a</u> rabbukum an yuhlika AAaduwwakum wayastakhlifakum fee al-ar<u>d</u>i fayan<i><u>th</u></i>ura kayfa taAAmaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>129.  They said: "We have had<br> (Nothing but) trouble, both before<br> And after thou camest<br> To us." He said:<br> "It may be that your Lord<br> Will destroy your enemy<br> And make you inheritors<br> In the earth; that so<br> He may try you<br> By your deeds."</p> <div class="filenav">
