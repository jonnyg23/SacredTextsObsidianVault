 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXXII: Sajda, or Adoration.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 2</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/032/032_012.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>12.  Walaw tar<u>a</u> i<u>th</u>i almujrimoona n<u>a</u>kisoo ruoosihim AAinda rabbihim rabban<u>a</u> ab<u>s</u>arn<u>a</u> wasamiAAn<u>a</u> faarjiAAn<u>a</u> naAAmal <u>sa</u>li<u>h</u>an inn<u>a</u> mooqinoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>12.  If only thou couldst see<br> When the guilty ones<br> Will bend low their heads<br> Before their Lord, (saying:)<br> "Our Lord! We have seen<br> And we have heard:<br> Now then send us back<br> (To the world): we will<br> Work righteousness: for we<br> Do indeed (now) believe."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/032/032_013.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>13.  Walaw shi-n<u>a</u> la<u>a</u>tayn<u>a</u> kulla nafsin hud<u>a</u>h<u>a</u> wal<u>a</u>kin <u>h</u>aqqa alqawlu minnee laamlaanna jahannama mina aljinnati wa<b>al</b>nn<u>a</u>si ajmaAAeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>13.  If We had so willed,<br> We could certainly have brought<br> Every soul its true guidance:<br> But the Word from Me<br> Will come true, "I will<br> Fill Hell with Jinns<br> And men all together."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/032/032_014.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>14.  Fa<u>th</u>ooqoo bim<u>a</u> naseetum liq<u>a</u>a yawmikum h<u>atha</u> inn<u>a</u> naseen<u>a</u>kum wa<u>th</u>ooqoo AAa<u>tha</u>ba alkhuldi bim<u>a</u> kuntum taAAmaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>14.  "Taste ye then—for ye<br> Forgot the Meeting<br> Of this Day of yours,<br> And We too will<br> Forget you—taste ye<br> The Penalty of Eternity<br> For your (evil) deeds!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/032/032_015.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>15.  Innam<u>a</u> yu/minu bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena i<u>tha</u> <u>th</u>ukkiroo bih<u>a</u> kharroo sujjadan wasabba<u>h</u>oo bi<u>h</u>amdi rabbihim wahum l<u>a</u> yastakbiroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>15.  Only those believe<br> In Our Signs, who, when<br>They are recited to them,<br> Fall down in adoration,<br> And celebrate the praises<br> Of their Lord, nor are they<br> (Ever) puffed up with pride.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/032/032_016.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>16.  Tataj<u>a</u>f<u>a</u> junoobuhum AAani alma<u>da</u>jiAAi yadAAoona rabbahum khawfan wa<u>t</u>amaAAan wamimm<u>a</u> razaqn<u>a</u>hum yunfiqoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>16.  Their limbs do forsake<br> Their beds of sleep, the while<br> They call on their Lord,<br> In Fear and Hope:<br> And they spend (in charity)<br> Out of the sustenance which<br> We have bestowed on them.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/032/032_017.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>17.  Fal<u>a</u> taAAlamu nafsun m<u>a</u> okhfiya lahum min qurrati aAAyunin jaz<u>a</u>an bim<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo yaAAmaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>17.  Now no person knows<br> What delights of the eye<br> Are kept hidden (in reserve)<br> For them—as a reward<br> For their (good) Deeds.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/032/032_018.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>18.  Afaman k<u>a</u>na mu/minan kaman k<u>a</u>na f<u>a</u>siqan l<u>a</u> yastawoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>18.  Is then the man<br> Who believes no better<br> Than the man who is<br> Rebellious and wicked?<br> Not equal are they.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/032/032_019.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>19.  Amm<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo waAAamiloo a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>li<u>ha</u>ti falahum jann<u>a</u>tu alma/w<u>a</u> nuzulan bim<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo yaAAmaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>19.  For those who believe<br> And do righteous deeds,<br> Are Gardens as hospitable<br> Homes, for their (good) deeds.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/032/032_020.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>20.  Waamm<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena fasaqoo fama/w<u>a</u>humu a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>ru kullam<u>a</u> ar<u>a</u>doo an yakhrujoo minh<u>a</u> oAAeedoo feeh<u>a</u> waqeela lahum <u>th</u>ooqoo AAa<u>tha</u>ba a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>ri alla<u>th</u>ee kuntum bihi tuka<u>thth</u>iboon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>20.  As to those who are<br> Rebellious and wicked, their abode<br> Will be the Fire: every time<br> They wish to get away<br> Therefrom, they will be forced<br> Thereinto, and it will be said<br> To them: "Taste ye<br> The Penalty of the Fire,<br> The which ye were wont<br> To reject as false."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/032/032_021.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>21.  Walanu<u>th</u>eeqannahum mina alAAa<u>tha</u>bi al-adn<u>a</u> doona alAAa<u>tha</u>bi al-akbari laAAallahum yarjiAAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>21.  And indeed We will make<br> Them taste of the Penalty<br> Of this (life) prior to<br> The supreme Penalty, in order<br> That they may (repent and) return.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/032/032_022.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>22.  Waman a<i><u>th</u></i>lamu mimman <u>th</u>ukkira bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>ti rabbihi thumma aAAra<u>d</u>a AAanh<u>a</u> inn<u>a</u> mina almujrimeena muntaqimoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>22.  And who does more wrong<br> Than one to whom are recited<br> The Signs of his Lord,<br> And who then turns away<br> Therefrom? Verily from those<br> Who transgress We shall exact<br> (Due) Retribution.</p> <div class="filenav">
