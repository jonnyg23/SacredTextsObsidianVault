 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXIX.: ‘Ankabūt, or the Spider</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 2</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/029/029_014.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>14.  Walaqad arsaln<u>a</u> noo<u>h</u>an il<u>a</u> qawmihi falabitha feehim alfa sanatin ill<u>a</u> khamseena AA<u>a</u>man faakha<u>th</u>ahumu a<b>l</b><u>tt</u>oof<u>a</u>nu wahum <i><u>th</u></i><u>a</u>limoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>14.  We (once) sent Noah<br> To his people, and he tarried<br> Among them a thousand years<br> Less fifty: but the Deluge<br> Overwhelmed them while they<br> (Persisted in) sin.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/029/029_015.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>15.  Faanjayn<u>a</u>hu waa<u>s</u>-<u>ha</u>ba a<b>l</b>ssafeenati wajaAAaln<u>a</u>h<u>a</u> <u>a</u>yatan lilAA<u>a</u>lameen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>15.  But We saved him<br> And the Companions<br> Of the Ark, and We made<br> The (Ark) a Sign<br> For all Peoples!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/029/029_016.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>16.  Wa-ibr<u>a</u>heema i<u>th</u> q<u>a</u>la liqawmihi oAAbudoo All<u>a</u>ha wa<b>i</b>ttaqoohu <u>tha</u>likum khayrun lakum in kuntum taAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>16.  And (We also saved)<br> Abraham: behold, he said<br> To his people, "Serve God<br> And fear Him: that<br> Will be best for you<br> If ye understand!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/029/029_017.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>17.  Innam<u>a</u> taAAbudoona min dooni All<u>a</u>hi awth<u>a</u>nan watakhluqoona ifkan inna alla<u>th</u>eena taAAbudoona min dooni All<u>a</u>hi l<u>a</u> yamlikoona lakum rizqan fa<b>i</b>btaghoo AAinda All<u>a</u>hi a<b>l</b>rrizqa wa<b>o</b>AAbudoohu wa<b>o</b>shkuroo lahu ilayhi turjaAAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>17.  "For ye do worship idols<br> Besides God, and ye invent<br> Falsehood. The things that ye<br> Worship besides God have<br> No power to give you sustenance:<br>Then seek ye sustenance<br> From God, serve Him,<br> And be grateful to Him:<br> To Him will be your return.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/029/029_018.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>18.  Wa-in tuka<u>thth</u>iboo faqad ka<u>thth</u>aba omamun min qablikum wam<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>rrasooli ill<u>a</u> albal<u>a</u>ghu almubeen<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>18.  "And if ye reject (the Message),<br> So did generations before you:<br> And the duty of the apostle<br> Is only to preach publicly<br> (And clearly)."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/029/029_019.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>19.  Awa lam yaraw kayfa yubdi-o All<u>a</u>hu alkhalqa thumma yuAAeeduhu inna <u>tha</u>lika AAal<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi yaseer<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>19.  See they not how God<br> Originates creation, then<br> Repeats it: truly that<br> Is easy for God.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/029/029_020.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>20.  Qul seeroo fee al-ar<u>d</u>i fa<b>o</b>n<i><u>th</u></i>uroo kayfa badaa alkhalqa thumma All<u>a</u>hu yunshi-o a<b>l</b>nnash-ata al-<u>a</u>khirata inna All<u>a</u>ha AAal<u>a</u> kulli shay-in qadeer<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>20.  Say: "Travel through the earth<br> And see how God did<br> Originate creation; so will<br> God produce a later creation:<br> For God has power<br> Over all things.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/029/029_021.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>21.  YuAAa<u>thth</u>ibu man yash<u>a</u>o wayar<u>h</u>amu man yash<u>a</u>o wa-ilayhi tuqlaboon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>21.  "He punishes whom He pleases,<br> And He grants mercy to whom<br>He pleases, and towards Him<br> Are ye turned.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/029/029_022.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>22.  Wam<u>a</u> antum bimuAAjizeena fee al-ar<u>d</u>i wal<u>a</u> fee a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>-i wam<u>a</u> lakum min dooni All<u>a</u>hi min waliyyin wal<u>a</u> na<u>s</u>eer<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>22.  "Not on earth nor in heaven<br> Will ye be able (fleeing)<br> To frustrate (His Plan),<br> Nor have ye, besides God,<br> Any protector or helper."</p> <div class="filenav">
