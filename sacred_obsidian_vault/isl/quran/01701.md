 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XVII.: Banī Isrā-īl, or the Children of Israel,</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  Sub<u>ha</u>na alla<u>th</u>ee asr<u>a</u> biAAabdihi laylan mina almasjidi al<u>h</u>ar<u>a</u>mi il<u>a</u> almasjidi al-aq<u>sa</u> alla<u>th</u>ee b<u>a</u>rakn<u>a</u> <u>h</u>awlahu linuriyahu min <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin<u>a</u> innahu huwa a<b>l</b>ssameeAAu alba<u>s</u>eer<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>1.  Glory to (God)<br> Who did take His Servant<br> For a Journey by night<br> From the Sacred Mosque<br> To the Farthest Mosque,<br> Whose precincts We did<br> Bless,—in order that We<br> Might show him some<br> Of Our Signs: for He<br> Is the One Who heareth<br> And seeth (all things).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  Wa<u>a</u>tayn<u>a</u> moos<u>a</u> alkit<u>a</u>ba wajaAAaln<u>a</u>hu hudan libanee isr<u>a</u>-eela all<u>a</u> tattakhi<u>th</u>oo min doonee wakeel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>2.  We gave Moses the Book,<br> And made it a Guide<br>To the Children of Israel,<br> (Commanding): "Take not<br> Other than Me<br> As Disposer of (your) affairs."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  <u>Th</u>urriyyata man <u>h</u>amaln<u>a</u> maAAa noo<u>h</u>in innahu k<u>a</u>na AAabdan shakoor<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>3.  O ye that are sprung<br> From those whom We carried<br> (In the Ark) with Noah!<br> Verily he was a devotee<br> Most grateful.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  Waqa<u>d</u>ayn<u>a</u> il<u>a</u> banee isr<u>a</u>-eela fee alkit<u>a</u>bi latufsidunna fee al-ar<u>d</u>i marratayni walataAAlunna AAuluwwan kabeer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>4.  And We gave (clear) warning<br> To the Children of Israel<br> In the Book, that twice<br> Would they do mischief<br> On the earth and be elated<br> With mighty arrogance<br> (And twice would they be punished)!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  Fa-i<u>tha</u> j<u>a</u>a waAAdu ool<u>a</u>hum<u>a</u> baAAathn<u>a</u> AAalaykum AAib<u>a</u>dan lan<u>a</u> olee ba/sin shadeedin faj<u>a</u>soo khil<u>a</u>la a<b>l</b>ddiy<u>a</u>ri wak<u>a</u>na waAAdan mafAAool<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>5.  When the first of the warnings<br> Came to pass, We sent<br> Against you Our servants<br> Given to terrible warfare:<br> They entered the very inmost<br> Parts of your homes;<br> And it was a warning<br> (Completely) fulfilled.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  Thumma radadn<u>a</u> lakumu alkarrata AAalayhim waamdadn<u>a</u>kum bi-amw<u>a</u>lin wabaneena wajaAAaln<u>a</u>kum akthara nafeer<u>a</u><b>n</b>    </p>
 <p>6.  Then did We grant you<br> The Return as against them:<br> We gave you increase<br> In resources and sons,<br> And made you<br> The more numerous<br> In man-power.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  In a<u>h</u>santum a<u>h</u>santum li-anfusikum wa-in asa/tum falah<u>a</u> fa-i<u>tha</u> j<u>a</u>a waAAdu al-<u>a</u>khirati liyasoo-oo wujoohakum waliyadkhuloo almasjida kam<u>a</u> dakhaloohu awwala marratin waliyutabbiroo m<u>a</u> AAalaw tatbeer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>7.  If ye did well,<br> Ye did well for yourselves;<br> If ye did evil,<br> (Ye did it) against yourselves?<br> So when the second<br> Of the warnings came to pass,<br> (We permitted your enemies)<br> To disfigure your faces,<br> And to enter your Temple<br> As they had entered it before,<br> And to visit with destruction<br> All that fell into their power.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  AAas<u>a</u> rabbukum an yar<u>h</u>amakum wa-in AAudtum AAudn<u>a</u> wajaAAaln<u>a</u> jahannama lilk<u>a</u>fireena <u>h</u>a<u>s</u>eer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>8.  It may be that your Lord<br> May (yet) show Mercy<br>Unto you; but if ye<br> Revert (to your sins),<br> We shall revert<br> (To Our punishments):<br> And We have made Hell<br> A prison for those who<br> Reject (all Faith),</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_009.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>9.  Inna h<u>atha</u> alqur-<u>a</u>na yahdee lillatee hiya aqwamu wayubashshiru almu/mineena alla<u>th</u>eena yaAAmaloona a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>li<u>ha</u>ti anna lahum ajran kabeer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>9.  Verily this Qur-ān<br> Doth guide to that<br> Which is most right (or stable),<br> And giveth the glad tidings<br> To the Believers who work<br> Deeds of righteousness,<br> That they shall have<br> A magnificent reward;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_010.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>10.  Waanna alla<u>th</u>eena l<u>a</u> yu/minoona bi<b>a</b>l-<u>a</u>khirati aAAtadn<u>a</u> lahum AAa<u>tha</u>ban aleem<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>10.  And to those who believe not<br> In the Hereafter, (it announceth)<br> That We have prepared<br> For them a Penalty<br> Grievous (indeed).</p> <div class="filenav">
