 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra LXXXVIII.: <u>G</u>āshiya, or The Overwhelming Event.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/088/088_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  Hal at<u>a</u>ka <u>h</u>adeethu algh<u>a</u>shiyat<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>1.  Has the story<br> Reached thee, of<br> The Overwhelming (Event)?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/088/088_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  Wujoohun yawma-i<u>th</u>in kh<u>a</u>shiAAa<b>tun</b>   </p>
 <p>2.  Some faces, that Day,<br> Will be humiliated,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/088/088_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  AA<u>a</u>milatun n<u>as</u>iba<b>tun</b>   </p>
 <p>3.  Labouring (hard), weary,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/088/088_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  Ta<u>s</u>l<u>a</u> n<u>a</u>ran <u>ha</u>miya<b>tan</b>   </p>
 <p>4.  The while they enter<br> The Blazing Fire,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/088/088_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  Tusq<u>a</u> min AAaynin <u>a</u>niya<b>tin</b>   </p>
 <p>5.  The while they are given,<br> To drink, of a bailing hot spring,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/088/088_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  Laysa lahum <u>t</u>aAA<u>a</u>mun ill<u>a</u> min <u>d</u>areeAA<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>6.  No food will there be<br> For them but a bitter <u>Dh</u>arī’</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/088/088_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  L<u>a</u> yusminu wal<u>a</u> yughnee min jooAA<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>7.  Which will neither nourish<br> Nor satisfy hunger.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/088/088_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  Wujoohun yawma-i<u>th</u>in n<u>a</u>AAima<b>tun</b>   </p>
 <p>8.  (Other) faces that Day<br> Will be joyful,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/088/088_009.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>9.  LisaAAyih<u>a</u> r<u>ad</u>iya<b>tun</b>   </p>
 <p>9.  Pleased with their Striving,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/088/088_010.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>10.  Fee jannatin AA<u>a</u>liya<b>tin</b>    </p>
 <p>10.  In a Garden on high,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/088/088_011.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>11.  L<u>a</u> tasmaAAu feeh<u>a</u> l<u>a</u>ghiya<b>tan</b>   </p>
 <p>11.  Where they shall hear<br> No (word) of vanity:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/088/088_012.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>12.  Feeh<u>a</u> AAaynun j<u>a</u>riya<b>tun</b>   </p>
 <p>12.  Therein will be<br> A bubbling spring:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/088/088_013.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>13.  Feeh<u>a</u> sururun marfooAAa<b>tun</b>   </p>
 <p>13.  Therein will be Thrones<br> (Of dignity), raised on high,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/088/088_014.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>14.  Waakw<u>a</u>bun maw<u>d</u>ooAAa<b>tun</b>   </p>
 <p>14.  Goblets placed (ready),</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/088/088_015.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>15.  Wanam<u>a</u>riqu ma<u>s</u>foofa<b>tun</b>    </p>
 <p>15.  And Cushions set in rows,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/088/088_016.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>16.  Wazar<u>a</u>biyyu mabthootha<b>tun</b>   </p>
 <p>16.  And rich carpets<br> (All) spread out.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/088/088_017.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>17.  Afal<u>a</u> yan<i><u>th</u></i>uroona il<u>a</u> al-ibili kayfa khuliqat   </p>
 <p>17.  Do they not look<br> At the Camels,<br> How they are made?—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/088/088_018.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>18.  Wa-il<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>-i kayfa rufiAAat   </p>
 <p>18.  And at the Sky,<br> How it is raised high?—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/088/088_019.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>19.  Wa-il<u>a</u> aljib<u>a</u>li kayfa nu<u>s</u>ibat   </p>
 <p>19.  And at the Mountains,<br> How they are fixed firm?—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/088/088_020.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>20.  Wa-il<u>a</u> al-ar<u>d</u>i kayfa su<u>t</u>i<u>h</u>at   </p>
 <p>20.  And at the Earth,<br> How it is spread out?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/088/088_021.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>21.  Fa<u>th</u>akkir innam<u>a</u> anta mu<u>th</u>akkir<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>21.  Wherefore do thou give<br> Admonition, for thou art<br> One to admonish.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/088/088_022.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>22.  Lasta AAalayhim bimu<u>s</u>ay<u>t</u>ir<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>22.  Thou art not one<br> To manage (men's) affairs.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/088/088_023.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>23.  Ill<u>a</u> man tawall<u>a</u> wakafar<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>23.  But if any turn away<br> And reject God,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/088/088_024.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>24.  FayuAAa<u>thth</u>ibuhu All<u>a</u>hu alAAa<u>tha</u>ba al-akbar<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>24.  God will punish him<br> With a mighty Punishment,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/088/088_025.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>25.  Inna ilayn<u>a</u> iy<u>a</u>bahum   </p>
 <p>25.  For to Us will be<br> Their Return;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/088/088_026.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>26.  Thumma inna AAalayn<u>a</u> <u>h</u>is<u>a</u>bahum   </p>
 <p>26.  Then it will be for Us<br> To call them to account.</p>  <div class="filenav">
