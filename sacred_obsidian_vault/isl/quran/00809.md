 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra VIII.: Anfāl, or the Spoils of War.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 9</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/008/008_065.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>65.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nnabiyyu <u>h</u>arri<u>d</u>i almu/mineena AAal<u>a</u> alqit<u>a</u>li in yakun minkum AAishroona <u>sa</u>biroona yaghliboo mi-atayni wa-in yakun minkum mi-atun yaghliboo alfan mina alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo bi-annahum qawmun l<u>a</u> yafqahoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>65.  O Apostle! rouse the Believers<br> To the fight. If there are<br> Twenty amongst you, patient<br> And persevering, they will<br> Vanquish two hundred: if a hundred,<br> They will vanquish a thousand<br> Of the Unbelievers: for these<br> Are a people without understanding.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/008/008_066.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>66.  Al-<u>a</u>na khaffafa All<u>a</u>hu AAankum waAAalima anna feekum <u>d</u>aAAfan fa-in yakun minkum mi-atun <u>sa</u>biratun yaghliboo mi-atayni wa-in yakun minkum alfun yaghliboo alfayni bi-i<u>th</u>ni All<u>a</u>hi wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu maAAa a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>bireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>66.  For the present, God<br> Hath lightened your (task),<br> For He knoweth that there is<br> A weak spot in you:<br> But (even so), if there are<br> A hundred of you, patient<br> And persevering, they will<br> Vanquish two hundred, and if<br> A thousand, they will vanquish<br> Two thousand, with the leave<br> Of God: for God is with those<br> Who patiently persevere.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/008/008_067.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>67.  M<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>na linabiyyin an yakoona lahu asr<u>a</u> <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> yuthkhina fee al-ar<u>d</u>i tureedoona AAara<u>d</u>a a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu yureedu al-<u>a</u>khirata wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu AAazeezun <u>h</u>akeem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>67.  It is not fitting<br> For an Apostle<br> That he should have<br> Prisoners of war until<br> He hath thoroughly subdued<br> The land. Ye look<br> For the temporal goods<br> Of this world; but God<br> Looketh to the Hereafter:<br> And God is Exalted in might, Wise.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/008/008_068.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>68.  Lawl<u>a</u> kit<u>a</u>bun mina All<u>a</u>hi sabaqa lamassakum feem<u>a</u> akha<u>th</u>tum AAa<u>tha</u>bun AAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>68.  Had it not been for<br> A previous ordainment<br>From God, a severe penalty<br> Would have reached you<br> For the (ransom) that ye took.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/008/008_069.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>69.  Fakuloo mimm<u>a</u> ghanimtum <u>h</u>al<u>a</u>lan <u>t</u>ayyiban wa<b>i</b>ttaqoo All<u>a</u>ha inna All<u>a</u>ha ghafoorun ra<u>h</u>eem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>69.  But (now) enjoy what ye took<br> In war, lawful and good:<br> But fear God: for God<br> Is Oft-forgiving, Most Merciful.</p> <div class="filenav">
