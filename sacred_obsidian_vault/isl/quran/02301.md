 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXIII.: Mu-minūn, or The Believers.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  Qad afla<u>h</u>a almu/minoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>1.  The Believers must<br> (Eventually) win through,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  Alla<u>th</u>eena hum fee <u>s</u>al<u>a</u>tihim kh<u>a</u>shiAAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>2.  Those who humble themselves<br> In their prayers;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  Wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena hum AAani allaghwi muAAri<u>d</u>oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>3.  Who avoid vain talk;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  Wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena hum li<b>l</b>zzak<u>a</u>ti f<u>a</u>AAiloon<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>4.  Who are active in deeds<br> Of charity;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  Wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena hum lifuroojihim <u>ha</u>fi<i><u>th</u></i>oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>5.  Who abstain from sex,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  Ill<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> azw<u>a</u>jihim aw m<u>a</u> malakat aym<u>a</u>nuhum fa-innahum ghayru maloomeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>6.  Except with those joined<br> To them in the marriage bond,<br> Or (the captives) whom<br> Their right hands possess,—<br> For (in their case) they are<br> Free from blame,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  Famani ibtagh<u>a</u> war<u>a</u>a <u>tha</u>lika faol<u>a</u>-ika humu alAA<u>a</u>doon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>7.  But those whose desires exceed<br> Those limits are transgressors;—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  Wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena hum li-am<u>a</u>n<u>a</u>tihim waAAahdihim r<u>a</u>AAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>8.  Those who faithfully observe<br> Their trusts and their covenants;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_009.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>9.  Wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena hum AAal<u>a</u> <u>s</u>alaw<u>a</u>tihim yu<u>ha</u>fi<i><u>th</u></i>oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>9.  And who (strictly) guard<br> Their prayers;—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_010.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>10.  Ol<u>a</u>-ika humu alw<u>a</u>rithoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>10.  These will be the heirs,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_011.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>11.  Alla<u>th</u>eena yarithoona alfirdawsa hum feeh<u>a</u> kh<u>a</u>lidoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>11.  Who will inherit Paradise:<br> They will dwell therein<br> (For ever).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_012.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>12.  Walaqad khalaqn<u>a</u> al-ins<u>a</u>na min sul<u>a</u>latin min <u>t</u>een<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>12.  Man We did create<br> From a quintessence (of clay);</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_013.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>13.  Thumma jaAAaln<u>a</u>hu nu<u>t</u>fatan fee qar<u>a</u>rin makeen<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>13.  Then We placed him<br> As (a drop of) sperm<br>In a place of rest,<br> Firmly fixed;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_014.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>14.  Thumma khalaqn<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nnu<u>t</u>fata AAalaqatan fakhalaqn<u>a</u> alAAalaqata mu<u>d</u>ghatan fakhalaqn<u>a</u> almu<u>d</u>ghata AAi<i><u>th</u></i><u>a</u>man fakasawn<u>a</u> alAAi<i><u>th</u></i><u>a</u>ma la<u>h</u>man thumma ansha/n<u>a</u>hu khalqan <u>a</u>khara fatab<u>a</u>raka All<u>a</u>hu a<u>h</u>sanu alkh<u>a</u>liqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>14.  Then We made the sperm<br> Into a clot of congealed blood;<br> Then of that clot We made<br> A (fœtus) lump; then We<br> Made out of that lump<br> Bones and clothed the bones<br> With flesh; then We developed<br> Out of it another creature.<br> So blessed be God,<br> The Best to create!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_015.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>15.  Thumma innakum baAAda <u>tha</u>lika lamayyitoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>15.  After that, at length<br> Ye will die.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_016.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>16.  Thumma innakum yawma alqiy<u>a</u>mati tubAAathoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>16.  Again, on the Day<br> Of Judgment, will ye be<br> Raised up.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_017.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>17.  Walaqad khalaqn<u>a</u> fawqakum sabAAa <u>t</u>ar<u>a</u>-iqa wam<u>a</u> kunn<u>a</u> AAani alkhalqi gh<u>a</u>fileen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>17.  And We have made, above you,<br> Seven tracts; and We<br> Are never unmindful<br> Of (Our) Creation,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_018.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>18.  Waanzaln<u>a</u> mina a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>-i m<u>a</u>an biqadarin faaskann<u>a</u>hu fee al-ar<u>d</u>i wa-inn<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> <u>th</u>ah<u>a</u>bin bihi laq<u>a</u>diroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>18.  And We send down water<br> From the sky according to<br> (Due) measure, and We cause it<br> To soak in the soil;<br> And We certainly are able<br> To drain it off (with ease).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_019.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>19.  Faansha/n<u>a</u> lakum bihi jann<u>a</u>tin min nakheelin waaAAn<u>a</u>bin lakum feeh<u>a</u> faw<u>a</u>kihu katheeratun waminh<u>a</u> ta/kuloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>19.  With it We grow for you<br> Gardens of date-palms<br> And vines: in them have ye<br> Abundant fruits: and of them<br> Ye eat (and have enjoyment),—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_020.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>20.  Washajaratan takhruju min <u>t</u>oori sayn<u>a</u>a tanbutu bi<b>al</b>dduhni wa<u>s</u>ibghin lil<u>a</u>kileen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>20.  Also a tree springing<br> Out of Mount Sinai,<br> Which produces oil,<br> And relish for those<br> Who use it for food.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_021.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>21.  Wa-inna lakum fee al-anAA<u>a</u>mi laAAibratan nusqeekum mimm<u>a</u> fee bu<u>t</u>oonih<u>a</u> walakum feeh<u>a</u> man<u>a</u>fiAAu katheeratun waminh<u>a</u> ta/kuloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>21.  And in cattle (too) ye<br> Have an instructive example:<br> From within their bodies<br> We produce (milk) for you<br>To drink; there art, in them,<br> (Besides), numerous (other)<br> Benefits for you;<br> And of their (meat) ye eat;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/023/023_022.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>22.  WaAAalayh<u>a</u> waAAal<u>a</u> alfulki tu<u>h</u>maloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>22.  And on them, as well as<br> In ships, ye ride.</p> <div class="filenav">
