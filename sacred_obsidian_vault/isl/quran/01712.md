 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XVII.: Banī Isrā-īl, or the Children of Israel,</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 12</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_101.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>101.  Walaqad <u>a</u>tayn<u>a</u> moos<u>a</u> tisAAa <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin bayyin<u>a</u>tin fa<b>i</b>s-al banee isr<u>a</u>-eela i<u>th</u> j<u>a</u>ahum faq<u>a</u>la lahu firAAawnu innee laa<i><u>th</u></i>unnuka y<u>a</u> moos<u>a</u> mas<u>h</u>oor<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>101.  To Moses We did give<br> Nine Clear Signs:<br> Ask the Children of Israel:<br> When he came to them,<br> Pharaoh said to him:<br> "O Moses! I consider thee,<br>Indeed, to have been<br> Worked upon by sorcery!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_102.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>102.  Q<u>a</u>la laqad AAalimta m<u>a</u> anzala h<u>a</u>ol<u>a</u>-i ill<u>a</u> rabbu a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i ba<u>sa</u>-ira wa-innee laa<i><u>th</u></i>unnuka y<u>a</u> firAAawnu mathboor<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>102.  Moses said, "Thou knowest<br> Well that these things<br> Have been sent down by none<br> But the Lord of the heavens<br> And the earth as eye-opening<br> Evidence: and I consider thee<br> Indeed, O Pharaoh, to be<br> One doomed to destruction!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_103.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>103.  Faar<u>a</u>da an yastafizzahum mina al-ar<u>d</u>i faaghraqn<u>a</u>hu waman maAAahu jameeAA<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>103.  So he resolved to remove them<br> From the face of the earth:<br> But We did drown him<br> And all who were with him.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_104.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>104.  Waquln<u>a</u> min baAAdihi libanee isr<u>a</u>-eela oskunoo al-ar<u>d</u>a fa-i<u>tha</u> j<u>a</u>a waAAdu al-<u>a</u>khirati ji/n<u>a</u> bikum lafeef<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>104.  And We said thereafter<br> To the Children of Israel,<br> "Dwell securely in the land<br> (Of promise)": but when<br> The second of the warnings came<br> To pass, We gathered you<br> Together in a mingled crowd</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_105.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>105.  Wabi<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>aqqi anzaln<u>a</u>hu wabi<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>aqqi nazala wam<u>a</u> arsaln<u>a</u>ka ill<u>a</u> mubashshiran wana<u>th</u>eer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>105.  We sent down the (Qur-ān)<br> In Truth, and in Truth<br> Has it descended: and We sent<br> Thee but to give Glad<br> Tidings and to warn (sinners).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_106.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>106.  Waqur-<u>a</u>nan faraqn<u>a</u>hu litaqraahu AAal<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si AAal<u>a</u> mukthin wanazzaln<u>a</u>hu tanzeel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>106.  (It is) a Qur-ān<br> Which We have divided<br> (Into parts from time to time),<br> In order that thou mightest<br> Recite it to men<br> At intervals: We have<br> Revealed it by stages.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_107.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>107.  Qul <u>a</u>minoo bihi aw l<u>a</u> tu/minoo inna alla<u>th</u>eena ootoo alAAilma min qablihi i<u>tha</u> yutl<u>a</u> AAalayhim yakhirroona lil-a<u>th</u>q<u>a</u>ni sujjad<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>107.  Say: "Whether ye believe<br> In it or not, it is true<br> That those who were given<br> Knowledge beforehand, when<br> It it recited to them,<br> Fall down on their faces<br> In humble prostration,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_108.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>108.  Wayaqooloona sub<u>ha</u>na rabbin<u>a</u> in k<u>a</u>na waAAdu rabbin<u>a</u> lamafAAool<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>108.  "And they say, "Glory<br> To our Lord! Truly<br> Has the promise of our Lord<br> Been fulfilled!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_109.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>109.  Wayakhirroona lil-a<u>th</u>q<u>a</u>ni yabkoona wayazeeduhum khushooAA<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>109.  They fall down on their faces<br> In tears, and it increases<br> Their (earnest) humility!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_110.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>110.  Quli odAAoo All<u>a</u>ha awi odAAoo a<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>m<u>a</u>na ayyan m<u>a</u> tadAAoo falahu al-asm<u>a</u>o al<u>h</u>usn<u>a</u> wal<u>a</u> tajhar bi<u>s</u>al<u>a</u>tika wal<u>a</u> tukh<u>a</u>fit bih<u>a</u> wa<b>i</b>btaghi bayna <u>tha</u>lika sabeel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>110.  Say: "Call upon God, or<br> Call upon Raḥmān:<br> By whatever name ye call<br> Upon Him, (it is well):<br> For to Him belong<br> The Most Beautiful Names.<br> Neither speak thy Prayer aloud,<br> Nor speak it in a low tone,<br> But seek a middle course<br> Between."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_111.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>111.  Waquli al<u>h</u>amdu lill<u>a</u>hi alla<u>th</u>ee lam yattakhi<u>th</u> waladan walam yakun lahu shareekun fee almulki walam yakun lahu waliyyun mina a<b>l</b><u>thth</u>ulli wakabbirhu takbeer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>111.  Say: "Praise be to God,<br> Who begets no son,<br> And has no partner<br> In (His) dominion:<br> Nor (needs) He any<br> To protect Him from<br> humiliation:<br> Yea, magnify Him<br> For His greatness and glory!"</p>  <div class="filenav">
