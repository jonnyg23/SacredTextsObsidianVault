 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra IX.: Tauba (Repentance) or Barāat (Immunity).</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 12</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_090.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>90.  Waj<u>a</u>a almuAAa<u>thth</u>iroona mina al-aAAr<u>a</u>bi liyu/<u>th</u>ana lahum waqaAAada alla<u>th</u>eena ka<u>th</u>aboo All<u>a</u>ha warasoolahu sayu<u>s</u>eebu alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo minhum AAa<u>tha</u>bun aleem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>90.  And there were, among<br> The desert Arabs (also),<br> Men who made excuses<br> And came to claim exemption;<br> And those who were false<br> To God and His Apostle<br> (Merely) sat inactive.<br> Soon will a grievous penalty<br> Seize the Unbelievers<br> Among them.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_091.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>91.  Laysa AAal<u>a</u> a<b>l</b><u>dd</u>uAAaf<u>a</u>-i wal<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> almar<u>da</u> wal<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena l<u>a</u> yajidoona m<u>a</u> yunfiqoona <u>h</u>arajun i<u>tha</u> na<u>s</u>a<u>h</u>oo lill<u>a</u>hi warasoolihi m<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> almu<u>h</u>sineena min sabeelin wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu ghafoorun ra<u>h</u>eem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>91.  There is no blame<br> On those who are infirm,<br>Or ill, or who find<br> No resources to spend<br> (On the Cause), if they<br> Are sincere (in duty) to God<br> And His Apostle:<br> No ground (of complaint)<br> Can there be against such<br> As do right: and God<br> Is Oft-Forgiving, Most Merciful.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_092.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>92.  Wal<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena i<u>tha</u> m<u>a</u> atawka lita<u>h</u>milahum qulta l<u>a</u> ajidu m<u>a</u> a<u>h</u>milukum AAalayhi tawallaw waaAAyunuhum tafee<u>d</u>u mina a<b>l</b>ddamAAi <u>h</u>azanan all<u>a</u> yajidoo m<u>a</u> yunfiqoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>92.  Nor (is there blame)<br> On those who came to thee<br> To be provided with mounts,<br> And when thou saidst,<br> "I can find no mounts<br> For you, "they turned back,<br> Their eyes streaming with tears<br> Of grief that they had<br> No resources wherewith<br> To provide the expenses.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_093.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>93.  Innam<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>ssabeelu AAal<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena yasta/<u>th</u>inoonaka wahum aghniy<u>a</u>o ra<u>d</u>oo bi-an yakoonoo maAAa alkhaw<u>a</u>lifi wa<u>t</u>abaAAa All<u>a</u>hu AAal<u>a</u> quloobihim fahum l<u>a</u> yaAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>93.  The ground (of complaint)<br> Is against such as claim<br> Exemption while they are rich.<br> They prefer to stay<br> With the (women) who remain<br> Behind: God hath sealed<br> Their hearts; so they know not<br> (What they miss).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_094.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>94.  YaAAta<u>th</u>iroona ilaykum i<u>tha</u> rajaAAtum ilayhim qul l<u>a</u> taAAta<u>th</u>iroo lan nu/mina lakum qad nabbaan<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hu min akhb<u>a</u>rikum wasayar<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hu AAamalakum warasooluhu thumma turaddoona il<u>a</u> AA<u>a</u>limi alghaybi wa<b>al</b>shshah<u>a</u>dati fayunabbi-okum bim<u>a</u> kuntum taAAmaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>94.  They will present their excuses<br> To you when ye return<br> To them. Say thou: "Present<br> No excuses: we shall not<br> Believe you: God hath already<br> Informed us of the true state<br> Of matters concerning you:<br> It is your actions that God<br> And His Apostle will observe:<br> In the end will ye<br> Be brought back to Him<br> Who knoweth what is hidden<br> And what is open:<br> Then will He show you<br> The truth of all<br> That ye did."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_095.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>95.  Saya<u>h</u>lifoona bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi lakum i<u>tha</u> inqalabtum ilayhim lituAAri<u>d</u>oo AAanhum faaAAri<u>d</u>oo AAanhum innahum rijsun wama/w<u>a</u>hum jahannamu jaz<u>a</u>an bim<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo yaksiboon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>95.  They will swear to you by God,<br> When ye return to them,<br> That ye may leave them alone.<br> So leave them alone:<br> For they are an abomination,<br> And Hell is their dwelling-place,—<br> A fitting recompense<br> For the (evil) that they did.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_096.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>96.  Ya<u>h</u>lifoona lakum litar<u>d</u>aw AAanhum fa-in tar<u>d</u>aw AAanhum fa-inna All<u>a</u>ha l<u>a</u> yar<u>da</u> AAani alqawmi alf<u>a</u>siqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>96.  They will swear unto you,<br> That ye may be pleased with them,<br> But if ye are pleased with them,<br> God is not pleased<br> With those who disobey.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_097.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>97.  Al-aAAr<u>a</u>bu ashaddu kufran wanif<u>a</u>qan waajdaru all<u>a</u> yaAAlamoo <u>h</u>udooda m<u>a</u> anzala All<u>a</u>hu AAal<u>a</u> rasoolihi wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu AAaleemun <u>h</u>akeem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>97.  The Arabs of the desert<br> Are the worst in unbelief<br> And hypocrisy, and most fitted<br> To be in ignorance<br> Of the command which God<br> Hath sent down to His Apostle:<br> But God is All-Knowing,<br> All-Wise.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_098.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>98.  Wamina al-aAAr<u>a</u>bi man yattakhi<u>th</u>u m<u>a</u> yunfiqu maghraman wayatarabba<u>s</u>u bikumu a<b>l</b>ddaw<u>a</u>-ira AAalayhim d<u>a</u>-iratu a<b>l</b>ssaw-i wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu sameeAAun AAaleem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>98.  Some of the desert Arabs<br> Look upon their payments<br>As a fine, and watch<br> For disasters for you: on them<br> Be the disaster of Evil:<br> For God is He that heareth<br> And knoweth (all things).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_099.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>99.  Wamina al-aAAr<u>a</u>bi man yu/minu bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi wa<b>a</b>lyawmi al-<u>a</u>khiri wayattakhi<u>th</u>u m<u>a</u> yunfiqu qurub<u>a</u>tin AAinda All<u>a</u>hi wa<u>s</u>alaw<u>a</u>ti a<b>l</b>rrasooli al<u>a</u> innah<u>a</u> qurbatun lahum sayudkhiluhumu All<u>a</u>hu fee ra<u>h</u>matihi inna All<u>a</u>ha ghafoorun ra<u>h</u>eem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>99.  But some of the desert Arabs<br> Believe in God and the Last Day,<br> And look on their payments<br> As pious gifts bringing them<br> Nearer to God and obtaining<br> The prayers of the Apostle.<br> Aye, indeed they bring them<br> Nearer (to Him): soon will God<br> Admit them to His Mercy:<br> For God is Oft-Forgiving,<br> Most Merciful.</p> <div class="filenav">
