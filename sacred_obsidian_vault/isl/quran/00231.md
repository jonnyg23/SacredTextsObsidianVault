 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra II.: Baqara, or the Heifer.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 31</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_236.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>236.  L<u>a</u> jun<u>ah</u>a AAalaykum in <u>t</u>allaqtumu a<b>l</b>nnis<u>a</u>a m<u>a</u> lam tamassoohunna aw tafri<u>d</u>oo lahunna faree<u>d</u>atan wamattiAAoohunna AAal<u>a</u> almoosiAAi qadaruhu waAAal<u>a</u> almuqtiri qadaruhu mat<u>a</u>AAan bi<b>a</b>lmaAAroofi <u>h</u>aqqan AAal<u>a</u> almu<u>h</u>sineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>236.  There is no blame on you<br> If ye divorce women<br> Before consummation<br> Or the fixation of their dower;<br> But bestow on them<br> (A suitable gift),<br> The wealthy<br> According to his means,<br> And the poor<br> According to his means;—<br>A gift of a reasonable amount<br> Is due from those<br> Who wish to do the right thing.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_237.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>237.  Wa-in <u>t</u>allaqtumoohunna min qabli an tamassoohunna waqad fara<u>d</u>tum lahunna faree<u>d</u>atan fani<u>s</u>fu m<u>a</u> fara<u>d</u>tum ill<u>a</u> an yaAAfoona aw yaAAfuwa alla<u>th</u>ee biyadihi AAuqdatu a<b>l</b>nnik<u>ah</u>i waan taAAfoo aqrabu li<b>l</b>ttaqw<u>a</u> wal<u>a</u> tansawoo alfa<u>d</u>la baynakum inna All<u>a</u>ha bim<u>a</u> taAAmaloona ba<u>s</u>eer<b>un </b>   </p>
 <p>237.  And if ye divorce them<br> Before consummation,<br> But after the fixation<br> Of a dower for them,<br> Then the half of the dower<br> (Is due to them), unless<br> They remit it<br> Or (the man's half) is remitted<br> By him in whose hands<br> Is the marriage tie;<br> And the remission<br> (Of the man's half)<br> Is the nearest to righteousness.<br> And do not forget<br> Liberality between yourselves.<br> For God sees well<br> All that ye do.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_238.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>238.  <u>Ha</u>fi<i><u>th</u></i>oo AAal<u>a</u> a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>alaw<u>a</u>ti wa<b>al</b><u>ss</u>al<u>a</u>ti alwus<u>ta</u> waqoomoo lill<u>a</u>hi q<u>a</u>niteen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>238.  Guard strictly<br> Your (habit of) prayers,<br> Especially the Middle Prayer;<br> And stand before God<br> In a devout (frame of mind).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_239.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>239.  Fa-in khiftum farij<u>a</u>lan aw rukb<u>a</u>nan fa-i<u>tha</u> amintum fa<b>o</b><u>th</u>kuroo All<u>a</u>ha kam<u>a</u> AAallamakum m<u>a</u> lam takoonoo taAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>239.  If ye fear (an enemy),<br> Pray on foot, or riding,<br> (As may be most convenient),<br> But when ye are<br> In security, celebrate<br> God's praises in the manner<br> He has taught you,<br> Which ye knew not (before).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_240.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>240.  Wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena yutawaffawna minkum waya<u>th</u>aroona azw<u>a</u>jan wa<u>s</u>iyyatan li-azw<u>a</u>jihim mat<u>a</u>AAan il<u>a</u> al<u>h</u>awli ghayra ikhr<u>a</u>jin fa-in kharajna fal<u>a</u> jun<u>ah</u>a AAalaykum fee m<u>a</u> faAAalna fee anfusihinna min maAAroofin wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu AAazeezun <u>h</u>akeem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>240.  Those of you<br> Who die and leave widows<br> Should bequeath<br> For their widows<br> A year's maintenance<br> And residence;<br> But if they leave<br> (The residence),<br> There is no blame on you<br> For what they do<br> With themselves,<br> Provided it is reasonable.<br> And God is Exalted in Power, Wise.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_241.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>241.  Walilmu<u>t</u>allaq<u>a</u>ti mat<u>a</u>AAun bi<b>a</b>lmaAAroofi <u>h</u>aqqan AAal<u>a</u> almuttaqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>241.  For divorced women<br> Maintenance (should be provided)<br> On a reasonable (scale).<br> This is a duty<br> On the righteous.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_242.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>242.  Ka<u>tha</u>lika yubayyinu All<u>a</u>hu lakum <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tihi laAAallakum taAAqiloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>242.  Thus doth God<br> Make clear His Signs<br> To you: in order that<br> Ye may understand.</p> <div class="filenav">
