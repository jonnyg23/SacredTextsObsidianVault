 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XVII.: Banī Isrā-īl, or the Children of Israel,</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 6</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_053.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>53.  Waqul liAAib<u>a</u>dee yaqooloo allatee hiya a<u>h</u>sanu inna a<b>l</b>shshay<u>ta</u>na yanzaghu baynahum inna a<b>l</b>shshay<u>ta</u>na k<u>a</u>na lil-ins<u>a</u>ni AAaduwwan mubeen<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>53.  Say to My servants<br> That they should (only) say<br> Those things that are best:<br> For Satan doth sow<br> Dissensions among them:<br> For Satan is to man<br> An avowed enemy.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_054.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>54.  Rabbukum aAAlamu bikum in yasha/ yar<u>h</u>amkum aw in yasha/ yuAAa<u>thth</u>ibkum wam<u>a</u> arsaln<u>a</u>ka AAalayhim wakeel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>54.  It is your Lord<br> That knoweth you best:<br> If He please, He granteth<br> You mercy, or if He please,<br> Punishment: We have not sent<br> Thee to be a disposer<br> Of their affairs for them.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_055.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>55.  Warabbuka aAAlamu biman fee a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i walaqad fa<u>dd</u>aln<u>a</u> baAA<u>d</u>a a<b>l</b>nnabiyyeena AAal<u>a</u> baAA<u>d</u>in wa<u>a</u>tayn<u>a</u> d<u>a</u>wooda zaboor<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>55.  And it is your Lord<br> That knoweth best all beings<br> That are in the heavens<br> And on earth: We<br> Did bestow on some Prophets<br> More (and other) gifts<br> Than on others: and We gave<br> To David (the gift<br> Of) the Psalms.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_056.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>56.  Quli odAAu alla<u>th</u>eena zaAAamtum min doonihi fal<u>a</u> yamlikoona kashfa a<b>l</b><u>dd</u>urri AAankum wal<u>a</u> ta<u>h</u>weel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>56.  Say: "Call on those—<br> Besides Him—whom ye fancy:<br> They have neither the power<br> To remove your troubles<br> From you nor to change them."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_057.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>57.  Ol<u>a</u>-ika alla<u>th</u>eena yadAAoona yabtaghoona il<u>a</u> rabbihimu alwaseelata ayyuhum aqrabu wayarjoona ra<u>h</u>matahu wayakh<u>a</u>foona AAa<u>tha</u>bahu inna AAa<u>tha</u>ba rabbika k<u>a</u>na ma<u>hth</u>oor<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>57.  Those whom they call upon<br> Do desire (for themselves) means<br> Of access to their Lord,—<br> Even those who are nearest:<br> They hope for His Mercy<br> And fear His Wrath:<br> For the Wrath of thy Lord<br> Is something to take heed of</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_058.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>58.  Wa-in min qaryatin ill<u>a</u> na<u>h</u>nu muhlikooh<u>a</u> qabla yawmi alqiy<u>a</u>mati aw muAAa<u>thth</u>ibooh<u>a</u> AAa<u>tha</u>ban shadeedan k<u>a</u>na <u>tha</u>lika fee alkit<u>a</u>bi mas<u>t</u>oor<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>58.  There is not a population<br> But We shall destroy it<br> Before the Day of Judgment<br> Or punish it with<br> A dreadful Penalty:<br> That is written<br> In the (eternal) Record.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_059.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>59.  Wam<u>a</u> manaAAan<u>a</u> an nursila bi<b>a</b>l-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>ti ill<u>a</u> an ka<u>thth</u>aba bih<u>a</u> al-awwaloona wa<u>a</u>tayn<u>a</u> thamooda a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>qata mub<u>s</u>iratan fa<i><u>th</u></i>alamoo bih<u>a</u> wam<u>a</u> nursilu bi<b>a</b>l-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>ti ill<u>a</u> takhweef<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>59.  And We refrain from sending<br> The Signs, only because<br> The men of former generations<br> Treated them as false:<br>We sent the She-camel<br> To the <u>Th</u>amūd to open<br> Their eyes, but they<br> Treated her wrongfully:<br> We only send the Signs<br> By way of terror<br> (And warning from evil).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_060.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>60.  Wa-i<u>th</u> quln<u>a</u> laka inna rabbaka a<u>hat</u>a bi<b>al</b>nn<u>a</u>si wam<u>a</u> jaAAaln<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>rru/y<u>a</u> allatee arayn<u>a</u>ka ill<u>a</u> fitnatan li<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si wa<b>al</b>shshajarata almalAAoonata fee alqur-<u>a</u>ni wanukhawwifuhum fam<u>a</u> yazeeduhum ill<u>a</u> <u>t</u>ughy<u>a</u>nan kabeer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>60.  Behold! We told thee<br> That thy Lord doth encompass<br> Mankind round about:<br> We granted the Vision<br> Which We showed thee,<br> But as a trial for men,—<br> As also the Cursed Tree<br> (Mentioned) in the Qur-ān:<br> We put terror (and warning)<br> Into them, but it only<br> Increases their inordinate<br> transgression!</p> <div class="filenav">
