 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXIX.: ‘Ankabūt, or the Spider</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 6</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/029/029_052.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>52.  Qul kaf<u>a</u> bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi baynee wabaynakum shaheedan yaAAlamu m<u>a</u> fee a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo bi<b>a</b>lb<u>at</u>ili wakafaroo bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi ol<u>a</u>-ika humu alkh<u>a</u>siroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>52.  Say: "Enough is God<br> For a Witness between me<br>And you: He knows<br> What is in the heavens<br> And on earth. And it is<br> Those who believe in vanities<br> And reject God, that<br> Will perish (in the end).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/029/029_053.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>53.  WayastaAAjiloonaka bi<b>a</b>lAAa<u>tha</u>bi walawl<u>a</u> ajalun musamman laj<u>a</u>ahumu alAAa<u>tha</u>bu walaya/tiyannahum baghtatan wahum l<u>a</u> yashAAuroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>53.  They ask thee<br> To hasten on the Punishment<br> (For them): had it not been<br> For a term (of respite)<br> Appointed, the Punishment<br> Would certainly have come<br> To them: and it will<br> Certainly reach them,—<br> Of a sudden, while they<br> Perceive not!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/029/029_054.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>54.  YastaAAjiloonaka bi<b>a</b>lAAa<u>tha</u>bi wa-inna jahannama lamu<u>h</u>ee<u>t</u>atun bi<b>a</b>lk<u>a</u>fireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>54.  They ask thee<br> To hasten on the Punishment:<br> But, of a surety,<br> Hell will encompass<br> The rejecters of Faith!—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/029/029_055.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>55.  Yawma yaghsh<u>a</u>humu alAAa<u>tha</u>bu min fawqihim wamin ta<u>h</u>ti arjulihim wayaqoolu <u>th</u>ooqoo m<u>a</u> kuntum taAAmaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>55.  On the Day that<br> The Punishment shall cover them<br> From above them and<br> From below them,<br> And (a Voice) shall say:<br> "Taste ye (the fruits)<br> Of your deeds!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/029/029_056.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>56.  Y<u>a</u> AAib<u>a</u>diya alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo inna ar<u>d</u>ee w<u>a</u>siAAatun fa-iyy<u>a</u>ya fa<b>o</b>AAbudoon<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>56.  O My servants who believe!<br> Truly, spacious is My Earth:<br> Therefore serve ye Me—<br> (And Me alone)!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/029/029_057.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>57.  Kullu nafsin <u>tha</u>-iqatu almawti thumma ilayn<u>a</u> turjaAAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>57.  Every soul shall have<br> A taste of death:<br> In the end to Us<br> Shall ye be brought back.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/029/029_058.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>58.  Wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo waAAamiloo a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>li<u>ha</u>ti lanubawwi-annahum mina aljannati ghurafan tajree min ta<u>h</u>tih<u>a</u> al-anh<u>a</u>ru kh<u>a</u>lideena feeh<u>a</u> niAAma ajru alAA<u>a</u>mileen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>58.  But those who believe<br> And work deeds of righteousness—<br> To them shall We give<br> A Home in Heaven,—<br> Lofty mansions beneath which<br> Flow rivers,—to dwell therein<br> For aye;—an excellent reward<br> For those who do (good)!—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/029/029_059.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>59.  Alla<u>th</u>eena <u>s</u>abaroo waAAal<u>a</u> rabbihim yatawakkaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>59.  Those who persevere in patience,<br> And put their trust<br> In their Lord and Cherisher.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/029/029_060.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>60.  Wakaayyin min d<u>a</u>bbatin l<u>a</u> ta<u>h</u>milu rizqah<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hu yarzuquh<u>a</u> wa-iyy<u>a</u>kum wahuwa a<b>l</b>ssameeAAu alAAaleem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>60.  How many are the creatures<br> That carry not their own<br>Sustenance? It is God<br> Who feeds (both) them and you:<br> For He hears and knows<br> (All things).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/029/029_061.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>61.  Wala-in saaltahum man khalaqa a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>a wasakhkhara a<b>l</b>shshamsa wa<b>a</b>lqamara layaqoolunna All<u>a</u>hu faann<u>a</u> yu/fakoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>61.  If indeed thou ask them<br> Who has created the heavens<br> And the earth and subjected<br> The sun and the moon<br> (To His Law), they will<br> Certainly reply, "God".<br> How are they then deluded<br> Away (from the truth)?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/029/029_062.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>62.  All<u>a</u>hu yabsu<u>t</u>u a<b>l</b>rrizqa liman yash<u>a</u>o min AAib<u>a</u>dihi wayaqdiru lahu inna All<u>a</u>ha bikulli shay-in AAaleem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>62.  God enlarges the sustenance<br> (Which He gives) to whichever<br> Of His servants He pleases;<br> And He (similarly) grants<br> By (strict) measure, (as He pleases):<br> For God has full knowledge<br> Of all things.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/029/029_063.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>63.  Wala-in saaltahum man nazzala mina a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>-i m<u>a</u>an faa<u>h</u>y<u>a</u> bihi al-ar<u>d</u>a min baAAdi mawtih<u>a</u> layaqoolunna All<u>a</u>hu quli al<u>h</u>amdu lill<u>a</u>hi bal aktharuhum l<u>a</u> yaAAqiloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>63.  And if indeed thou ask them<br> Who it is that sends down<br> Rain from the sky,<br> And gives life therewith<br> To the earth after its death,<br> They will certainly reply,<br> "God!" Say, "Praise be<br> To God!" But most<br> Of them understand not.</p> <div class="filenav">
