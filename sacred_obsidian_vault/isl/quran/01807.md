 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XVIII.: Kahf, or the Cave.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 7</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_050.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>50.  Wa-i<u>th</u> quln<u>a</u> lilmal<u>a</u>-ikati osjudoo li-<u>a</u>dama fasajadoo ill<u>a</u> ibleesa k<u>a</u>na mina aljinni fafasaqa AAan amri rabbihi afatattakhi<u>th</u>oonahu wa<u>th</u>urriyyatahu awliy<u>a</u>a min doonee wahum lakum AAaduwwun bi/sa li<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limeena badal<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>50.  Behold! We said<br> To the angels, "Bow down<br> To Adam": they bowed down<br> Except Iblīs. He was<br> One of the Jinns, and he<br> Broke the Command<br> Of his Lord.<br> Will ye then take him<br> And his progeny as protectors<br> Rather than Me? And they<br> Are enemies to you!<br> Evil would be the exchange<br> For the wrong-doers!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_051.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>51.  M<u>a</u> ashhadtuhum khalqa a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i wal<u>a</u> khalqa anfusihim wam<u>a</u> kuntu muttakhi<u>th</u>a almu<u>d</u>illeena AAa<u>d</u>ud<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>51.  I called them not<br> To witness the creation<br> Of the heavens and the earth,<br> Nor (even) their own creation:<br> Nor is it for Me<br> To take as helpers<br> Such as lead (men) astray!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_052.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>52.  Wayawma yaqoolu n<u>a</u>doo shurak<u>a</u>-iya alla<u>th</u>eena zaAAamtum fadaAAawhum falam yastajeeboo lahum wajaAAaln<u>a</u> baynahum mawbiq<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>52.  One Day He will say,<br> "Call on those whom ye<br> Thought to be My partners,"<br> And they will call on them,<br> But they will not listen<br> To them; and We shall<br> Make for them a place<br> Of common perdition</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_053.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>53.  Wara<u>a</u> almujrimoona a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>ra fa<i><u>th</u></i>annoo annahum muw<u>a</u>qiAAooh<u>a</u> walam yajidoo AAanh<u>a</u> ma<u>s</u>rif<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>53.  And the Sinful shall see<br> The Fire and apprehend<br> That they have to fall<br> Therein no means will they<br> Find to turn away therefrom.</p> <div class="filenav">
