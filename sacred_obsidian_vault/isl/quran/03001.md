 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXX.: Rūm, or The Roman Empire.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/030/030_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  Alif-l<u>a</u>m-meem   </p>
 <p>1.  A. L. M.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/030/030_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  Ghulibati a<b>l</b>rroom<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>2.  The Roman Empire<br> Has been defeated—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/030/030_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  Fee adn<u>a</u> al-ar<u>d</u>i wahum min baAAdi ghalabihim sayaghliboon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>3.  In a land close by;<br> But they, (even) after<br> (This) defeat of theirs,<br> Will soon be victorious—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/030/030_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  Fee bi<u>d</u>AAi sineena lill<u>a</u>hi al-amru min qablu wamin baAAdu wayawma-i<u>th</u>in yafra<u>h</u>u almu/minoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>4.  Within a few years.<br> With God is the Decision,<br> In the Past<br> And in the Future:<br> On that Day shall<br> The Believers rejoice—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/030/030_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  Bina<u>s</u>ri All<u>a</u>hi yan<u>s</u>uru man yash<u>a</u>o wahuwa alAAazeezu a<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>eem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>5.  With the help of God.<br> He helps whom He will,<br> And He is Exalted in Might,<br> Most Merciful.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/030/030_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  WaAAda All<u>a</u>hi l<u>a</u> yukhlifu All<u>a</u>hu waAAdahu wal<u>a</u>kinna akthara a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si l<u>a</u> yaAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>6.  (It is) the promise of God.<br> Never does God depart<br> From His promise:<br> But most men understand not.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/030/030_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  YaAAlamoona <i><u>th</u></i><u>a</u>hiran mina al<u>h</u>ay<u>a</u>ti a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> wahum AAani al-<u>a</u>khirati hum gh<u>a</u>filoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>7.  They know but the outer<br> (Things) in the life<br> Of this world: but<br> Of the End of things<br> They are heedless.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/030/030_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  Awa lam yatafakkaroo fee anfusihim m<u>a</u> khalaqa All<u>a</u>hu a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>a wam<u>a</u> baynahum<u>a</u> ill<u>a</u> bi<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>aqqi waajalin musamman wa-inna katheeran mina a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si biliq<u>a</u>-i rabbihim lak<u>a</u>firoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>8.  Do they not reflect<br> In their own minds?<br> Not but for just ends<br> And for a term appointed,<br> Did God create the heavens<br> And the earth, and all<br> Between them: yet are there<br> Truly many among men<br> Who deny the meeting<br> With their Lord<br> (At the Resurrection)!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/030/030_009.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>9.  Awa lam yaseeroo fee al-ar<u>d</u>i fayan<i><u>th</u></i>uroo kayfa k<u>a</u>na AA<u>a</u>qibatu alla<u>th</u>eena min qablihim k<u>a</u>noo ashadda minhum quwwatan waath<u>a</u>roo al-ar<u>d</u>a waAAamarooh<u>a</u> akthara mimm<u>a</u> AAamarooh<u>a</u> waj<u>a</u>at-hum rusuluhum bi<b>a</b>lbayyin<u>a</u>ti fam<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>na All<u>a</u>hu liya<i><u>th</u></i>limahum wal<u>a</u>kin k<u>a</u>noo anfusahum ya<i><u>th</u></i>limoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>9.  Do they not travel<br> Through the earth, and see<br> What was the End<br> Of those before them?<br> They were superior to them<br> In strength: they tilled<br> The soil and populated it<br> In greater numbers than these<br> Have done: there came to them<br> Their apostles with Clear (Signs),<br> (Which they rejected, to their<br> Own destruction): it was not<br> God who wronged them, but<br> They wronged their own souls.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/030/030_010.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>10.  Thumma k<u>a</u>na AA<u>a</u>qibata alla<u>th</u>eena as<u>a</u>oo a<b>l</b>ssoo-<u>a</u> an ka<u>thth</u>aboo bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>ti All<u>a</u>hi wak<u>a</u>noo bih<u>a</u> yastahzi-oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>10.  In the long run<br> Evil in the extreme<br> Will be the End of those<br> Who do evil; for that<br> They rejected the Signs<br> Of God, and held them up<br> To ridicule.</p> <div class="filenav">
