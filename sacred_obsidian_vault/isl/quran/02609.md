 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXVI.: Shu‘arāa, or The Poets.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 9</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_160.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>160.  Ka<u>thth</u>abat qawmu loo<u>t</u>in almursaleen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>160.  The people of Lūt rejected'<br> The apostles.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_161.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>161.  I<u>th</u> q<u>a</u>la lahum akhoohum loo<u>t</u>un al<u>a</u> tattaqoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>161.  Behold, their brother Lūt<br> Said to them: "Will ye not<br> Fear (God)?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_162.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>162.  Innee lakum rasoolun ameen<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>162.  "I am to you an apostle<br> Worthy of all trust.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_163.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>163.  Fa<b>i</b>ttaqoo All<u>a</u>ha waa<u>t</u>eeAAoon<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>163.  "So fear God and obey me.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_164.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>164.  Wam<u>a</u> as-alukum AAalayhi min ajrin in ajriya ill<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> rabbi alAA<u>a</u>lameen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>164.  "No reward do I ask<br> Of you for it my reward<br> Is only from the Lord<br> Of the Worlds.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_165.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>165.  Ata/toona a<b>l</b><u>thth</u>ukr<u>a</u>na mina alAA<u>a</u>lameen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>165.  "Of all the creatures<br> In the world, will ye<br> Approach males,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_166.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>166.  Wata<u>th</u>aroona m<u>a</u> khalaqa lakum rabbukum min azw<u>a</u>jikum bal antum qawmun AA<u>a</u>doon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>166.  "And leave those whom God<br> Has created for you<br> To be your mates?<br> Nay, ye are a people<br> Transgressing (all limits)!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_167.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>167.  Q<u>a</u>loo la-in lam tantahi y<u>a</u> loo<u>t</u>u latakoonanna mina almukhrajeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>167.  Whey said: "If thou desist not,<br> O Lūt! thou wilt assuredly<br> Be cast out!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_168.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>168.  Q<u>a</u>la innee liAAamalikum mina alq<u>a</u>leen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>168.  He said: "I do detest<br> Your doings:"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_169.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>169.  Rabbi najjinee waahlee mimm<u>a</u> yaAAmaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>169.  "O my Lord! deliver me<br> And my family from<br> Such things as they do!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_170.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>170.  Fanajjayn<u>a</u>hu waahlahu ajmaAAeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>170.  So We delivered him<br> And his family,—all</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_171.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>171.  Ill<u>a</u> AAajoozan fee algh<u>a</u>bireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>171.  Except an old woman<br> Who lingered behind.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_172.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>172.  Thumma dammarn<u>a</u> al-<u>a</u>khareen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>172.  But the rest We destroyed Utterly.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_173.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>173.  Waam<u>t</u>arn<u>a</u> AAalayhim ma<u>t</u>aran fas<u>a</u>a ma<u>t</u>aru almun<u>th</u>areen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>173.  We rained down on them<br> A shower (of brimstone):<br> And evil was the shower<br> On those who were admonished<br> (But heeded not)!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_174.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>174.  Inna fee <u>tha</u>lika la<u>a</u>yatan wam<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>na aktharuhum mu/mineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>174.  Verily in this is a Sign<br> But most of them<br> Do not believe.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/026/026_175.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>175.  Wa-inna rabbaka lahuwa alAAazeezu a<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>eem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>175.  And verily thy Lord<br> Is He, the Exalted in Might<br> Most Merciful.</p> <div class="filenav">
