 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra IV.: Nisāa, or The Women.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>su ittaqoo rabbakumu alla<u>th</u>ee khalaqakum min nafsin w<u>ah</u>idatin wakhalaqa minh<u>a</u> zawjah<u>a</u> wabaththa minhum<u>a</u> rij<u>a</u>lan katheeran wanis<u>a</u>an wa<b>i</b>ttaqoo All<u>a</u>ha alla<u>th</u>ee tas<u>a</u>aloona bihi wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>ha</u>ma inna All<u>a</u>ha k<u>a</u>na AAalaykum raqeeb<b>an</b>   </p>
 <p>1.  O mankind! reverence<br> Your Guardian-Lord,<br> Who created you<br> From a single Person,<br> Created, of like nature,<br> His mate, and from them twain<br> Scattered (like seeds)<br> Countless men and women;—<br> Reverence God, through Whom<br> Ye demand your mutual (rights),<br> And (reverence) the wombs<br> (That bore you): for God<br> Ever watches over you.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  Wa<u>a</u>too alyat<u>a</u>m<u>a</u> amw<u>a</u>lahum wal<u>a</u> tatabaddaloo alkhabeetha bi<b>al</b><u>tt</u>ayyibi wal<u>a</u> ta/kuloo amw<u>a</u>lahum il<u>a</u> amw<u>a</u>likum innahu k<u>a</u>na <u>h</u>ooban kabeer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>2.  To orphans restore their property<br> (When they reach their age),<br> Nor substitute (your) worthless things<br> For (their) good ones; and devour not<br> Their substance (by mixing it up)<br>With your own. For this is<br> I ndeed a great sin.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  Wa-in khiftum all<u>a</u> tuqsi<u>t</u>oo fee alyat<u>a</u>m<u>a</u> fa<b>i</b>nki<u>h</u>oo m<u>a</u> <u>ta</u>ba lakum mina a<b>l</b>nnis<u>a</u>-i mathn<u>a</u> wathul<u>a</u>tha warub<u>a</u>AAa fa-in khiftum all<u>a</u> taAAdiloo faw<u>ah</u>idatan aw m<u>a</u> malakat aym<u>a</u>nukum <u>tha</u>lika adn<u>a</u> all<u>a</u> taAAooloo   </p>
 <p>3.  If ye fear that ye shall not<br> Be able to deal justly<br> With the orphans,<br> Marry women of your choice,<br> Two, or three, or four;<br> But if ye fear that ye shall not<br> Be able to deal justly (with them),<br> Then only one, or (a captive)<br> That your right hands possess.<br> That will be more suitable,<br> To prevent you<br> From doing injustice.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  Wa<u>a</u>too a<b>l</b>nnis<u>a</u>a <u>s</u>aduq<u>a</u>tihinna ni<u>h</u>latan fa-in <u>t</u>ibna lakum AAan shay-in minhu nafsan fakuloohu hanee-an maree-<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>4.  And give the women<br> (On marriage) their dower<br> As a free gift; but if they,<br> Of their own good pleasure,<br> Remit any part of it to you,<br> Take it and enjoy it<br> With right good cheer.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  Wal<u>a</u> tu/too a<b>l</b>ssufah<u>a</u>a amw<u>a</u>lakumu allatee jaAAala All<u>a</u>hu lakum qiy<u>a</u>man wa<b>o</b>rzuqoohum feeh<u>a</u> wa<b>o</b>ksoohum waqooloo lahum qawlan maAAroof<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>5.  To those weak of understanding<br> Make not over your property,<br> Which God hath made<br> A means of support for you,<br> But feed and clothe them<br> Therewith, and speak to them<br> Words of kindness and justice.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  Wa<b>i</b>btaloo alyat<u>a</u>m<u>a</u> <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> i<u>tha</u> balaghoo a<b>l</b>nnik<u>ah</u>a fa-in <u>a</u>nastum minhum rushdan fa<b>i</b>dfaAAoo ilayhim amw<u>a</u>lahum wal<u>a</u> ta/kulooh<u>a</u> isr<u>a</u>fan wabid<u>a</u>ran an yakbaroo waman k<u>a</u>na ghaniyyan falyastaAAfif waman k<u>a</u>na faqeeran falya/kul bi<b>a</b>lmaAAroofi fa-i<u>tha</u> dafaAAtum ilayhim amw<u>a</u>lahum faashhidoo AAalayhim wakaf<u>a</u> bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi <u>h</u>aseeb<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>6.  Make trial of orphans<br> Until they reach the age<br> Of marriage; if then ye find<br> Sound judgment in them,<br> Release their property to them;<br> But consume it not wastefully,<br> Nor in haste against their growing up.<br> If the guardian is well-off,<br> Let him claim no remuneration,<br> But if he is poor, let him<br> Have for himself what is<br> Just and reasonable.<br> When ye release their property<br> To them, take witnesses<br> In their presence:<br> But all-sufficient<br> Is God in taking account.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  Li<b>l</b>rrij<u>a</u>li na<u>s</u>eebun mimm<u>a</u> taraka alw<u>a</u>lid<u>a</u>ni wa<b>a</b>l-aqraboona wali<b>l</b>nnis<u>a</u>-i na<u>s</u>eebun mimm<u>a</u> taraka alw<u>a</u>lid<u>a</u>ni wa<b>a</b>l-aqraboona mimm<u>a</u> qalla minhu aw kathura na<u>s</u>eeban mafroo<u>da</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>7.  From what is left by parents<br> And those nearest relateds,<br> There is a share for men<br> And a share for women,<br> Whether the property be small<br> Or large,—a determinate share.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> <u>h</u>a<u>d</u>ara alqismata oloo alqurb<u>a</u> wa<b>a</b>lyat<u>a</u>m<u>a</u> wa<b>a</b>lmas<u>a</u>keenu fa<b>o</b>rzuqoohum minhu waqooloo lahum qawlan maAAroof<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>8.  But if at the time of division<br> Other relatives, or orphans,<br> Or poor, are present,<br> Feed them out of the (property),<br> And speak to them<br> Words of kindness and justice.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_009.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>9.  Walyakhsha alla<u>th</u>eena law tarakoo min khalfihim <u>th</u>urriyyatan <u>d</u>iAA<u>a</u>fan kh<u>a</u>foo AAalayhim falyattaqoo All<u>a</u>ha walyaqooloo qawlan sadeed<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>9.  Let those (disposing of an estate)<br> Have the same fear in their minds<br> As they would have for their own<br> If they had left a helpless family behind:<br> Let them fear God, and speak<br> Words of appropriate (comfort),</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_010.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>10.  Inna alla<u>th</u>eena ya/kuloona amw<u>a</u>la alyat<u>a</u>m<u>a</u> <i><u>th</u></i>ulman innam<u>a</u> ya/kuloona fee bu<u>t</u>oonihim n<u>a</u>ran wasaya<u>s</u>lawna saAAeer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>10.  Those who unjustly<br> Eat up the property<br> Of orphans, eat up<br> A Fire into their own<br> Bodies: they will soon<br> Be enduring a blazing Fire!</p> <div class="filenav">
