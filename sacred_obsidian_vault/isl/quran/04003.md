 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XL.: Mū-min, or The Believer.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 3</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/040/040_021.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>21.  Awa lam yaseeroo fee al-ar<u>d</u>i fayan<i><u>th</u></i>uroo kayfa k<u>a</u>na AA<u>a</u>qibatu alla<u>th</u>eena k<u>a</u>noo min qablihim k<u>a</u>noo hum ashadda minhum quwwatan wa<u>a</u>th<u>a</u>ran fee al-ar<u>d</u>i faakha<u>th</u>ahumu All<u>a</u>hu bi<u>th</u>unoobihim wam<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>na lahum mina All<u>a</u>hi min w<u>a</u>q<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>21.  Do they not travel<br> Through the earth and see<br> What was the End<br> Of those before them<br> They were even superior<br> To them in strength,<br> And in the traces (they<br> Have left) in the land:<br> But God did call them<br> To account for their sins,<br> And none had they<br> To defend them against God.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/040/040_022.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>22.  <u>Tha</u>lika bi-annahum k<u>a</u>nat ta/teehim rusuluhum bi<b>a</b>lbayyin<u>a</u>ti fakafaroo faakha<u>th</u>ahumu All<u>a</u>hu innahu qawiyyun shadeedu alAAiq<u>a</u>b<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>22.  That was because there came<br> To them their apostles<br> With Clear (Signs),<br> But they rejected them:<br> So God called them<br> To account: for He is<br> Full of Strength,<br> Strict in Punishment.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/040/040_023.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>23.  Walaqad arsaln<u>a</u> moos<u>a</u> bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin<u>a</u> wasul<u>ta</u>nin mubeen<b>in</b>    </p>
 <p>23.  Of old We sent Moses,<br> With Our Signs<br> And an Authority manifest,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/040/040_024.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>24.  Il<u>a</u> firAAawna wah<u>a</u>m<u>a</u>na waq<u>a</u>roona faq<u>a</u>loo s<u>ah</u>irun ka<u>ththa</u>b<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>24.  To Pharaoh, Hāmān,<br> And Qārūn; but they<br> Called (him) "a sorcerer<br> Telling lies!"…</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/040/040_025.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>25.  Falamm<u>a</u> j<u>a</u>ahum bi<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>aqqi min AAindin<u>a</u> q<u>a</u>loo oqtuloo abn<u>a</u>a alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo maAAahu wa<b>i</b>sta<u>h</u>yoo nis<u>a</u>ahum wam<u>a</u> kaydu alk<u>a</u>fireena ill<u>a</u> fee <u>d</u>al<u>a</u>l<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>25.  Now, when he came to them<br> In Truth, from Us,<br> They said, "Slay the sons<br> Of those who believe a<br> With him, and keep alive<br> Their females," but the plots<br> Of Unbelievers (end) in nothing<br> But errors (and delusions)!…</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/040/040_026.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>26.  Waq<u>a</u>la firAAawnu <u>th</u>aroonee aqtul moos<u>a</u> walyadAAu rabbahu innee akh<u>a</u>fu an yubaddila deenakum aw an yu<i><u>th</u></i>hira fee al-ar<u>d</u>i alfas<u>a</u>d<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>26.  Said Pharaoh: "Leave me<br> To slay Moses; and let him<br> Call on his Lord!<br> What I fear is lest<br> He should change your religion,<br> Or lest he should cause<br> Mischief to appear<br> In the land!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/040/040_027.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>27.  Waq<u>a</u>la moos<u>a</u> innee AAu<u>th</u>tu birabbee warabbikum min kulli mutakabbirin l<u>a</u> yu/minu biyawmi al<u>h</u>is<u>a</u>b<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>27.  Moses said: "I have indeed<br> Called upon my Lord<br> And your Lord<br> (For protection) from every<br> Arrogant one who believes not<br> In the Day of Account!"</p> <div class="filenav">
