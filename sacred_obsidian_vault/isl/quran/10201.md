 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra CII.: Taka<u>th</u>ur or Piling Up.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/102/102_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  Alh<u>a</u>kumu a<b>l</b>ttak<u>a</u>thur<b>u</b>    </p>
 <p>1.  The mutual rivalry<br> For piling up (the good things<br> Of this world) diverts you<br> (From the more serious things),</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/102/102_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  <u>H</u>att<u>a</u> zurtumu almaq<u>a</u>bir<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>2.  Until ye visit the graves.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/102/102_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  Kall<u>a</u> sawfa taAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>3.  But nay, ye soon shall<br> Know (the reality).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/102/102_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  Thumma kall<u>a</u> sawfa taAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>4.  Again, ye soon shall know!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/102/102_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  Kall<u>a</u> law taAAlamoona AAilma alyaqeen<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>5.  Nay, were ye to know<br> With certainty of mind,<br> (Ye would beware!)</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/102/102_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  Latarawunna alja<u>h</u>eem<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>6.  Ye shall certainly see<br> Hell-fire!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/102/102_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  Thumma latarawunnah<u>a</u> AAayna alyaqeen<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>7.  Again, ye shall see it<br> With certainty of sight!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/102/102_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  Thumma latus-alunna yawma-i<u>th</u>in AAani a<b>l</b>nnaAAeem<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>8.  Then, shall ye be<br> Questioned that Day<br> About the joy<br> (Ye indulged in!)</p>  <div class="filenav">
