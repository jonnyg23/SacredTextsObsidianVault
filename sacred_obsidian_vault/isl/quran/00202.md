 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra II.: Baqara, or the Heifer.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 2</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  Wamina a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si man yaqoolu <u>a</u>mann<u>a</u> biAll<u>a</u>hi wabi<b>a</b>lyawmi al-<u>a</u>khiri wam<u>a</u> hum bimu/mineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>8.  Of the people there are some who say:<br> "We believe in God and the Last Day;"<br> But they do not (really) believe.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_009.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>9.  Yukh<u>a</u>diAAoona All<u>a</u>ha wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo wam<u>a</u> yakhdaAAoona ill<u>a</u> anfusahum wam<u>a</u> yashAAuroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>9.  Fain would they deceive<br> God and those who believe,<br> But they only deceive themselves,<br> And realize (it) not!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_010.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>10.  Fee quloobihim mara<u>d</u>un faz<u>a</u>dahumu All<u>a</u>hu mara<u>d</u>an walahum AAa<u>tha</u>bun aleemun bim<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo yak<u>th</u>iboon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>10.  In their hearts is a disease;<br>And God has increased their disease:<br> And grievous is the penalty they (incur),<br> Because they are false (to themselves).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_011.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>11.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> qeela lahum l<u>a</u> tufsidoo fee al-ar<u>d</u>i q<u>a</u>loo innam<u>a</u> na<u>h</u>nu mu<u>s</u>li<u>h</u>oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>11.  When it is said to them:<br> "Make not mischief on the earth,"<br> They say: "Why, we only<br> Want to make peace!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_012.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>12.  Al<u>a</u> innahum humu almufsidoona wal<u>a</u>kin l<u>a</u> yashAAuroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>12.  Of a surety, they are the ones<br> Who make mischief,<br> But they realize (it) not.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_013.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>13.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> qeela lahum <u>a</u>minoo kam<u>a</u> <u>a</u>mana a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>su q<u>a</u>loo anu/minu kam<u>a</u> <u>a</u>mana a<b>l</b>ssufah<u>a</u>o al<u>a</u> innahum humu a<b>l</b>ssufah<u>a</u>o wal<u>a</u>kin l<u>a</u> yaAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>13.  When it is said to them:<br> "Believe as the others believe:"<br> They say: "Shall we believe<br> As the fools believe?"<br> Nay, of a surety they are the fools,<br> But they do not know."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_014.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>14.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> laqoo alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo q<u>a</u>loo <u>a</u>mann<u>a</u> wa-i<u>tha</u> khalaw il<u>a</u> shay<u>at</u>eenihim q<u>a</u>loo inn<u>a</u> maAAakum innam<u>a</u> na<u>h</u>nu mustahzi-oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>14.  When they meet those who believe,<br> They say: "We believe;"<br> But when they are alone<br> With their evil ones,<br> They say: "We are really with you:<br> We (were) only jesting."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_015.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>15.  All<u>a</u>hu yastahzi-o bihim wayamudduhum fee <u>t</u>ughy<u>a</u>nihim yaAAmahoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>15.  God will throw back<br> Their mockery on them,<br> And give them rope in their trespasses;<br> So they will wander like blind ones<br> (To and fro).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_016.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>16.  Ol<u>a</u>-ika alla<u>th</u>eena ishtarawoo a<b>l</b><u>dd</u>al<u>a</u>lata bi<b>a</b>lhud<u>a</u> fam<u>a</u> rabi<u>h</u>at tij<u>a</u>ratuhum wam<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo muhtadeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>16.  These are they who have bartered<br> Guidance for error:<br>But their traffic is profitless,<br> And they have lost true direction,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_017.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>17.  Mathaluhum kamathali alla<u>th</u>ee istawqada n<u>a</u>ran falamm<u>a</u> a<u>da</u>at m<u>a</u> <u>h</u>awlahu <u>th</u>ahaba All<u>a</u>hu binoorihim watarakahum fee <i><u>th</u></i>ulum<u>a</u>tin l<u>a</u> yub<u>s</u>iroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>17.  Their similitude is that of a man<br> Who kindled a fire;<br> When it lighted all around him,<br> God took away their light<br> And left them in utter darkness.<br> So they could not see.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_018.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>18.  <u>S</u>ummun bukmun AAumyun fahum l<u>a</u> yarjiAAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>18.  Deaf, dumb, and blind,<br> They will not return (to the path).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_019.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>19.  Aw ka<u>s</u>ayyibin mina a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>-i feehi <i><u>th</u></i>ulum<u>a</u>tun waraAAdun wabarqun yajAAaloona a<u>sa</u>biAAahum fee <u>atha</u>nihim mina a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>aw<u>a</u>AAiqi <u>h</u>a<u>th</u>ara almawti wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu mu<u>h</u>ee<u>t</u>un bi<b>a</b>lk<u>a</u>fireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>19.  Or (another similitude)<br> Is that of a rain-laden cloud<br> From the sky: in it are zones<br> Of darkness, and thunder and lightning:<br> They press their fingers in their ears<br> To keep out the stunning thunder-clap,<br> The while they are in terror of death.<br> But God is ever round<br> The rejecters of Faith!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_020.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>20.  Yak<u>a</u>du albarqu yakh<u>t</u>afu ab<u>sa</u>rahum kullam<u>a</u> a<u>da</u>a lahum mashaw feehi wa-i<u>tha</u> a<i><u>th</u></i>lama AAalayhim q<u>a</u>moo walaw sh<u>a</u>a All<u>a</u>hu la<u>th</u>ahaba bisamAAihim waab<u>sa</u>rihim inna All<u>a</u>ha AAal<u>a</u> kulli shay-in qadeer<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>20.  The lightning all but snatches away<br> Their sight; every time the light<br> (Helps) them, they walk therein,<br> And when the darkness grows on them,<br> They stand still.<br> And if God willed, He could take away<br> Their faculty of hearing and seeing;<br> For God hath power over all things</p> <div class="filenav">
