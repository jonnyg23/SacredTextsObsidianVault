 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra LXX.: Ma‘ārij, or the Ways of Ascent.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/070/070_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  Saala s<u>a</u>-ilun biAAa<u>tha</u>bin w<u>a</u>qiAA<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>1.  A questioner asked<br> About a Penalty<br> To befall—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/070/070_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  Lilk<u>a</u>fireena laysa lahu d<u>a</u>fiAA<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>2.  The Unbelievers,<br> The which there is none<br> To ward off,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/070/070_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  Mina All<u>a</u>hi <u>th</u>ee almaAA<u>a</u>rij<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>3.  (A Penalty) from God,<br> Lord of the Ways<br> Of Ascent.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/070/070_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  TaAAruju almal<u>a</u>-ikatu wa<b>al</b>rroo<u>h</u>u ilayhi fee yawmin k<u>a</u>na miqd<u>a</u>ruhu khamseena alfa sana<b>tin</b>   </p>
 <p>4.  The angels and<br> The Spirit ascend<br> Unto Him in a Day<br> The measure whereof<br> Is (as) fifty thousand years:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/070/070_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  Fa<b>i</b><u>s</u>bir <u>s</u>abran jameel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>5.  Therefore do thou hold<br> Patience,—a Patience<br> Of beautiful (contentment).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/070/070_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  Innahum yarawnahu baAAeed<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>6.  They see the (Day) indeed<br> As a far-off (event):</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/070/070_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  Wanar<u>a</u>hu qareeb<u>a</u><b>n</b>    </p>
 <p>7.  But We see it<br> (Quite) near.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/070/070_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  Yawma takoonu a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>o ka<b>a</b>lmuhl<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>8.  The Day that<br> The sky will be like<br> Molten brass,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/070/070_009.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>9.  Watakoonu aljib<u>a</u>lu ka<b>a</b>lAAihn<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>9.  And the mountains will be<br> Like wool,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/070/070_010.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>10.  Wal<u>a</u> yas-alu <u>h</u>ameemun <u>h</u>ameem<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>10.  And no friend will ask<br> After a friend,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/070/070_011.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>11.  Yuba<u>ss</u>aroonahum yawaddu almujrimu law yaftadee min AAa<u>tha</u>bi yawmi-i<u>th</u>in bibaneeh<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>11.  Though they will be put<br> In sight of each other,—<br> The sinner's desire will be:<br> Would that he could<br>Redeem himself from<br> The Penalty of that Day<br> By (sacrificing) his children,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/070/070_012.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>12.  Wa<u>sah</u>ibatihi waakheeh<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>12.  His wife and his brother,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/070/070_013.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>13.  Wafa<u>s</u>eelatihi allatee tu/weeh<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>13.  His kindred who sheltered him,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/070/070_014.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>14.  Waman fee al-ar<u>d</u>i jameeAAan thumma yunjeeh<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>14.  And all, all that is<br> On earth,—so it could<br> Deliver him:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/070/070_015.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>15.  Kall<u>a</u> innah<u>a</u> la<i><u>th</u></i><u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>15.  By no means!<br> For it would be<br> The Fire of Hell!—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/070/070_016.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>16.  Nazz<u>a</u>AAatan li<b>l</b>shshaw<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>16.  Plucking out (his being)<br> Right to the skull!—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/070/070_017.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>17.  TadAAoo man adbara watawall<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>17.  Inviting (all) such<br> As turn their backs<br> And turn away their faces<br> (From the Right),</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/070/070_018.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>18.  WajamaAAa faawAA<u>a</u>    </p>
 <p>18.  And collect (wealth)<br> And hide it (from use)!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/070/070_019.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>19.  Inna al-ins<u>a</u>na khuliqa halooAA<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>19.  Truly man was created<br> Very impatient;—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/070/070_020.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>20.  I<u>tha</u> massahu a<b>l</b>shsharru jazooAA<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>20.  Fretful when evil<br> Touches him;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/070/070_021.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>21.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> massahu alkhayru manooAA<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>21.  And niggardly when<br> Good reaches him;—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/070/070_022.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>22.  Ill<u>a</u> almu<u>s</u>alleen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>22.  Not so those devoted<br> To Prayer;—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/070/070_023.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>23.  Alla<u>th</u>eena hum AAal<u>a</u> <u>s</u>al<u>a</u>tihim d<u>a</u>-imoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>23.  Those who remain steadfast<br> To their prayer;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/070/070_024.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>24.  Wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena fee amw<u>a</u>lihim <u>h</u>aqqun maAAloom<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>24.  And those in whose wealth<br> Is a recognised right</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/070/070_025.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>25.  Li<b>l</b>ss<u>a</u>-ili wa<b>a</b>lma<u>h</u>room<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>25.  For the (needy) who asks<br> And him who is prevented<br> (For some reason from asking);</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/070/070_026.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>26.  Wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena yu<u>s</u>addiqoona biyawmi a<b>l</b>ddeen<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>26.  And those who hold<br> To the truth of the Day<br> Of Judgment;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/070/070_027.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>27.  Wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena hum min AAa<u>tha</u>bi rabbihim mushfiqoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>27.  And those who fear<br> The displeasure of their Lord,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/070/070_028.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>28.  Inna AAa<u>tha</u>ba rabbihim ghayru ma/moon<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>28.  For their Lord's displeasure<br> Is the opposite of Peace<br> And Tranquillity;—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/070/070_029.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>29.  Wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena hum lifuroojihim <u>ha</u>fi<i><u>th</u></i>oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>29.  And those who guard<br> Their chastity,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/070/070_030.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>30.  Ill<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> azw<u>a</u>jihim aw m<u>a</u> malakat aym<u>a</u>nuhum fa-innahum ghayru maloomeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>30.  Except with their wives<br> And the (captives) whom<br> Their right hands possess,—<br> For (then) they are not<br> To be blamed,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/070/070_031.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>31.  Famani ibtagh<u>a</u> war<u>a</u>a <u>tha</u>lika faol<u>a</u>-ika humu alAA<u>a</u>doon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>31.  But those who trespass<br> Beyond this are transgressors;—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/070/070_032.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>32.  Wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena hum li-am<u>a</u>n<u>a</u>tihim waAAahdihim r<u>a</u>AAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>32.  And those who respect<br> Their trusts and covenants;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/070/070_033.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>33.  Wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena hum bishah<u>a</u>d<u>a</u>tihim q<u>a</u>-imoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>33.  And those who stand firm<br> In their testimonies;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/070/070_034.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>34.  Wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena hum AAal<u>a</u> <u>s</u>al<u>a</u>tihim yu<u>ha</u>fi<i><u>th</u></i>oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>34.  And those who guard<br> (The sacredness) of their<br> worship;—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/070/070_035.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>35.  Ol<u>a</u>-ika fee jann<u>a</u>tin mukramoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>35.  Such will be<br> The honoured ones<br> In the Gardens (of Bliss).</p> <div class="filenav">
