 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra LXXI.: Nūḥ, or Noah.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 2</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/071/071_021.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>21.  Q<u>a</u>la noo<u>h</u>un rabbi innahum AAa<u>s</u>awnee wa<b>i</b>ttabaAAoo man lam yazidhu m<u>a</u>luhu wawaladuhu ill<u>a</u> khas<u>a</u>r<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>21.  Noah said: "O my Lord!<br> They have disobeyed me,<br> But they follow (men)<br> Whose wealth and children<br> Give them no Increase<br> But only Loss.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/071/071_022.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>22.  Wamakaroo makran kubb<u>a</u>r<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>22.  "And they have devised<br> A tremendous Plot.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/071/071_023.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>23.  Waq<u>a</u>loo l<u>a</u> ta<u>th</u>arunna <u>a</u>lihatakum wal<u>a</u> ta<u>th</u>arunna waddan wal<u>a</u> suw<u>a</u>AAan wal<u>a</u> yaghootha wayaAAooqa wanasr<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>23.  "And they have said<br> (To each other),<br> "Abandon not your gods:<br> Abandon neither Wadd<br> Nor Suwā‘, neither<br> Yagūth nor Ya‘ūq,<br> Nor Nasr’;—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/071/071_024.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>24.  Waqad a<u>d</u>alloo katheeran wal<u>a</u> tazidi a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limeena ill<u>a</u> <u>d</u>al<u>a</u>l<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>24.  "They have already<br> Misled many; and<br> Grant Thou no increase<br> To the wrong-doers but in<br> Straying (from their mark)."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/071/071_025.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>25.  Mimm<u>a</u> kha<u>t</u>ee-<u>a</u>tihim oghriqoo faodkhiloo n<u>a</u>ran falam yajidoo lahum min dooni All<u>a</u>hi an<u>sa</u>r<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>25.  Because of their sins<br> They were drowned<br> (In the flood),<br> And were made to enter<br> The Fire (of Punishment):<br> And they found<br> In lieu of God<br> None to help them.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/071/071_026.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>26.  Waq<u>a</u>la noo<u>h</u>un rabbi l<u>a</u> ta<u>th</u>ar AAal<u>a</u> al-ar<u>d</u>i mina alk<u>a</u>fireena dayy<u>a</u>r<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>26.  And Noah said:<br> "O my Lord! Leave not<br> Of the Unbelievers,<br> A single one on earth!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/071/071_027.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>27.  Innaka in ta<u>th</u>arhum yu<u>d</u>illoo AAib<u>a</u>daka wal<u>a</u> yalidoo ill<u>a</u> f<u>a</u>jiran kaff<u>a</u>r<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>27.  "For, if Thou dost leave<br> (Any of) them, they will<br> But mislead Thy devotees,<br> And they will breed none<br> But wicked ungrateful ones.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/071/071_028.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>28.  Rabbi ighfir lee waliw<u>a</u>lidayya waliman dakhala baytiya mu/minan walilmu/mineena wa<b>a</b>lmu/min<u>a</u>ti wal<u>a</u> tazidi a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limeena ill<u>a</u> tab<u>a</u>ran   </p>
 <p>28.  "O my Lord! Forgive me,<br> My parents, all who<br> Enter my house in Faith,<br> And (all) believing men<br>And believing women:<br> And to the wrong-doers<br> Grant Thou no increase<br> But in Perdition!"</p>  <div class="filenav">
