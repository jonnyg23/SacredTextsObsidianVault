 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXVII.: Naml, or the Ants.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  <u>Ta</u>-seen tilka <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tu alqur-<u>a</u>ni wakit<u>a</u>bin mubeen<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>1.  Tā. Sīn.<br> These are verses<br> Of the Qur-ān,—a Book<br> That makes (things) clear;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  Hudan wabushr<u>a</u> lilmu/mineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>2.  A Guide; and Glad Tidings<br> For the Relievers,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  Alla<u>th</u>eena yuqeemoona a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>al<u>a</u>ta wayu/toona a<b>l</b>zzak<u>a</u>ta wahum bi<b>a</b>l-<u>a</u>khirati hum yooqinoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>3.  Those who establish regular prayers<br> And give in regular charity,<br> And also have (full) assurance<br> Of the Hereafter.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  Inna alla<u>th</u>eena l<u>a</u> yu/minoona bi<b>a</b>l-<u>a</u>khirati zayyann<u>a</u> lahum aAAm<u>a</u>lahum fahum yaAAmahoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>4.  As to those who believe not<br> In the Hereafter, We have<br> Made their deeds pleasing<br> In their eyes; and so they<br> Wander about in distraction.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  Ol<u>a</u>-ika alla<u>th</u>eena lahum soo-o alAAa<u>tha</u>bi wahum fee al-<u>a</u>khirati humu al-akhsaroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>5.  Such are they for whom<br> A grievous Penalty is (waiting):<br> And in the Hereafter theirs<br> Will be the greatest loss.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  Wa-innaka latulaqq<u>a</u> alqur-<u>a</u>na min ladun <u>h</u>akeemin AAaleem<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>6.  As to thee, the Qur-ān<br> Is bestowed upon thee<br> From the presence of One<br> Who is Wise and All-Knowing.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  I<u>th</u> q<u>a</u>la moos<u>a</u> li-ahlihi innee <u>a</u>nastu n<u>a</u>ran sa<u>a</u>teekum minh<u>a</u> bikhabarin aw <u>a</u>teekum bishih<u>a</u>bin qabasin laAAallakum ta<u>st</u>aloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>7.  Behold! Moses said<br> To his family "I perceive<br> A fire; soon will I bring you<br> From there some information,<br> Or I will bring you<br> A burning brand to light<br> Our fuel, that ye may<br> Warm yourselves.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  Falamm<u>a</u> j<u>a</u>ah<u>a</u> noodiya an boorika man fee a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>ri waman <u>h</u>awlah<u>a</u> wasub<u>ha</u>na All<u>a</u>hi rabbi alAA<u>a</u>lameen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>8.  But when he came<br> To the (Fire), a voice<br> Was heard: "Blessed are those<br> In the Fire and those around:<br> And Glory to God,<br> The Lord of the Worlds.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_009.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>9.  Y<u>a</u> moos<u>a</u> innahu an<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hu alAAazeezu al<u>h</u>akeem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>9.  "O Moses! Verily,<br> I am God, the Exalted<br> In Might, the Wise!...</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_010.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>10.  Waalqi AAa<u>sa</u>ka falamm<u>a</u> ra<u>a</u>h<u>a</u> tahtazzu kaannah<u>a</u> j<u>a</u>nnun wall<u>a</u> mudbiran walam yuAAaqqib y<u>a</u> moos<u>a</u> l<u>a</u> takhaf innee l<u>a</u> yakh<u>a</u>fu ladayya almursaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>10.  "Now do thou throw thy rod!"<br> But when he saw it<br> Moving (of its own accord)<br> As if it had been a snake,<br> He turned back in retreat,<br> And retraced not his steps:<br> "O Moses!" (it was said),<br> "Fear not: truly, in My presence,<br> Those called as apostles<br> Have no fear,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_011.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>11.  Ill<u>a</u> man <i><u>th</u></i>alama thumma baddala <u>h</u>usnan baAAda soo-in fa-innee ghafoorun ra<u>h</u>eem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>11.  "But if any have done wrong<br> And have thereafter substituted<br> Good to take the place of evil,<br> Truly, I am Oft-Forgiving,<br> Most Merciful.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_012.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>12.  Waadkhil yadaka fee jaybika takhruj bay<u>da</u>a min ghayri soo-in fee tisAAi <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin il<u>a</u> firAAawna waqawmihi innahum k<u>a</u>noo qawman f<u>a</u>siqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>12.  "Now put thy hand into<br> Thy bosom, and it will<br> Come forth white without stain<br> (Or harm): (these are) among<br> The nine Signs (thou wilt take)<br> To Pharaoh and his people:<br> For they are a people<br> Rebellious in transgression."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_013.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>13.  Falamm<u>a</u> j<u>a</u>at-hum <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tun<u>a</u> mub<u>s</u>iratan q<u>a</u>loo h<u>atha</u> si<u>h</u>run mubeen<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>13.  But when Our Signs came<br> To them, that should have""<br> Opened their eyes, they said:<br> "This is sorcery manifest!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/027/027_014.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>14.  Waja<u>h</u>adoo bih<u>a</u> wa<b>i</b>stayqanat-h<u>a</u> anfusuhum <i><u>th</u></i>ulman waAAuluwwan fa<b>o</b>n<i><u>th</u></i>ur kayfa k<u>a</u>na AA<u>a</u>qibatu almufsideen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>14.  And they rejected those Signs<br> In iniquity and arrogance,<br> Though their souls were convinced<br> Thereof: so see what was<br> The end of those<br> Who acted corruptly!</p> <div class="filenav">
