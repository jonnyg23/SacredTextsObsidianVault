 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXXIII.: Aḥzāb, or The Confederates.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 2</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/033/033_009.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>9.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo o<u>th</u>kuroo niAAmata All<u>a</u>hi AAalaykum i<u>th</u> j<u>a</u>atkum junoodun faarsaln<u>a</u> AAalayhim ree<u>h</u>an wajunoodan lam tarawh<u>a</u> wak<u>a</u>na All<u>a</u>hu bim<u>a</u> taAAmaloona ba<u>s</u>eer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>9.  O ye who believe!<br> Remember the Grace of God,<br> (Bestowed) on you, when<br> There came down on you<br> Hosts (to overwhelm you):<br> But We sent against them<br>A hurricane and forces<br> That ye saw not:<br> But God sees (clearly)<br> All that ye do.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/033/033_010.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>10.  I<u>th</u> j<u>a</u>ookum min fawqikum wamin asfala minkum wa-i<u>th</u> z<u>a</u>ghati al-ab<u>sa</u>ru wabalaghati alquloobu al<u>h</u>an<u>a</u>jira wata<i><u>th</u></i>unnoona bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i>unoon<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>10.  Behold! they came on you<br> From above you and from<br> Below you, and behold,<br> The eyes became dim<br> And the hearts gaped<br> Up to the throats,<br> And ye imagined various<br> (Vain) thoughts about God!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/033/033_011.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>11.  Hun<u>a</u>lika ibtuliya almu-minoona wazulziloo zilz<u>a</u>lan shadeed<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>11.  In that situation<br> Were the Believers tried:<br> They were shaken as by<br> A tremendous shaking.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/033/033_012.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>12.  Wa-i<u>th</u> yaqoolu almun<u>a</u>fiqoona wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena fee quloobihim mara<u>d</u>un m<u>a</u> waAAadan<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hu warasooluhu ill<u>a</u> ghuroor<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>12.  Find behold! The Hypocrites<br> And those in whose hearts<br> Is a disease (even) say: "God<br> And His Apostle promised us<br> Nothing but delusions!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/033/033_013.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>13.  Wa-i<u>th</u> q<u>a</u>lat <u>ta</u>-ifatun minhum y<u>a</u> ahla yathriba l<u>a</u> muq<u>a</u>ma lakum fa<b>i</b>rjiAAoo wayasta/<u>th</u>inu fareequn minhumu a<b>l</b>nnabiyya yaqooloona inna buyootan<u>a</u> AAawratun wam<u>a</u> hiya biAAawratin in yureedoona ill<u>a</u> fir<u>a</u>r<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>13.  Behold! A party among them<br> Said: "Ye men of Ya<u>th</u>rib!<br> Ye cannot stand (the attack)!<br> Therefore go back!"<br> And a band of them<br> Ask for leave of the Prophet,<br>Saying, "Truly our houses<br> Are bare and exposed," though<br> They were not exposed:<br> They intended nothing but<br> To run away.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/033/033_014.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>14.  Walaw dukhilat AAalayhim min aq<u>ta</u>rih<u>a</u> thumma su-iloo alfitnata la<u>a</u>tawh<u>a</u> wam<u>a</u> talabbathoo bih<u>a</u> ill<u>a</u> yaseer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>14.  And if an entry had<br> Been effected to them<br> From the sides of the (City),<br> And they had been<br> Incited to sedition.<br> They would certainly have<br> Brought it to pass, with<br> None but a brief delay!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/033/033_015.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>15.  Walaqad k<u>a</u>noo AA<u>a</u>hadoo All<u>a</u>ha min qablu l<u>a</u> yuwalloona al-adb<u>a</u>ra wak<u>a</u>na AAahdu All<u>a</u>hi mas-ool<u>a</u><b>n</b>    </p>
 <p>15.  And yet they had already<br> Covenanted with God not to turn<br> Their backs, and a covenant<br> With God must (surely)<br> Be answered for.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/033/033_016.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>16.  Qul lan yanfaAAakumu alfir<u>a</u>ru in farartum mina almawti awi alqatli wa-i<u>th</u>an l<u>a</u> tumattaAAoona ill<u>a</u> qaleel<u>a</u><b>n</b>    </p>
 <p>16.  Say: "Running away will not<br> Profit you if ye are<br> Running away from death<br> Or slaughter; and even if<br> (Ye do escape), no more<br> Than a brief (respite)<br> Will ye be allowed to enjoy!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/033/033_017.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>17.  Qul man <u>tha</u> alla<u>th</u>ee yaAA<u>s</u>imukum mina All<u>a</u>hi in ar<u>a</u>da bikum soo-an aw ar<u>a</u>da bikum ra<u>h</u>matan wal<u>a</u> yajidoona lahum min dooni All<u>a</u>hi waliyyan wal<u>a</u> na<u>s</u>eer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>17.  Say: "Who is it that can<br> Screen you from God<br> If it be His wish<br> To give you Punishment<br> Or to give you Mercy?"<br> Nor will they find for themselves,<br> Besides God, any protector<br> Or helper.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/033/033_018.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>18.  Qad yaAAlamu All<u>a</u>hu almuAAawwiqeena minkum wa<b>a</b>lq<u>a</u>-ileena li-ikhw<u>a</u>nihim halumma ilayn<u>a</u> wal<u>a</u> ya/toona alba/sa ill<u>a</u> qaleel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>18.  Verily God knows those<br> Among you who keep back<br> (Men) and those who say<br> To their brethren, "Come along<br> To us", but come not<br> To the fight except<br> For just a little while,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/033/033_019.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>19.  Ashi<u>hh</u>atan AAalaykum fa-i<u>tha</u> j<u>a</u>a alkhawfu raaytahum yan<i><u>th</u></i>uroona ilayka tadooru aAAyunuhum ka<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>ee yughsh<u>a</u> AAalayhi mina almawti fa-i<u>tha</u> <u>th</u>ahaba alkhawfu salaqookum bi-alsinatin <u>h</u>id<u>a</u>din ashi<u>hh</u>atan AAal<u>a</u> alkhayri ol<u>a</u>-ika lam yu/minoo faa<u>h</u>ba<u>t</u>a All<u>a</u>hu aAAm<u>a</u>lahum wak<u>a</u>na <u>tha</u>lika AAal<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi yaseer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>19.  Covetous over you<br> Then when fear comes,<br> Thou wilt see them looking<br> To thee, their eyes revolving,<br> Like (those of) one over whom<br> Hovers death: but when<br> The fear is past,<br> They will smite you<br> With sharp tongues, covetous<br> Of goods. Such men have<br> No faith, and so God<br> Has made their deeds<br> Of none effect: and that<br> Is easy for God.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/033/033_020.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>20.  Ya<u>h</u>saboona al-a<u>h</u>z<u>a</u>ba lam ya<u>th</u>haboo wa-in ya/ti al-a<u>h</u>z<u>a</u>bu yawaddoo law annahum b<u>a</u>doona fee al-aAAr<u>a</u>bi yas-aloona AAan anb<u>a</u>-ikum walaw k<u>a</u>noo feekum m<u>a</u> q<u>a</u>taloo ill<u>a</u> qaleel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>20.  They think that the Confederates<br> Have not withdrawn; and if<br> The Confederates should come (again),<br> They would wish they were<br> In the deserts (wandering)<br> Among the Bedouins, and<br> Seeking news about you<br> (From a safe distance);<br> And if they were<br> In your midst, they<br> Would fight but little.</p> <div class="filenav">
