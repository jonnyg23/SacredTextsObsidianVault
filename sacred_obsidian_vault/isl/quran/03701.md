 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXXVII.: Ṣāffāt, or Those Ranged in Ranks.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  Wa<b>al</b><u>ssa</u>ff<u>a</u>ti <u>s</u>aff<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>1.  By those who range<br> Themselves in ranks,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  Fa<b>al</b>zz<u>a</u>jir<u>a</u>ti zajr<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>2.  And so are strong<br> In repelling (evil),</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  Fa<b>al</b>tt<u>a</u>liy<u>a</u>ti <u>th</u>ikr<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>3.  And thus proclaim<br> The Message (of God)!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  Inna il<u>a</u>hakum law<u>ah</u>id<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>4.  Verily, verily, your God<br> Is One!—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  Rabbu a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i wam<u>a</u> baynahum<u>a</u> warabbu almash<u>a</u>riq<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>5.  Lord of the heavens<br> And of the earth,<br>And all between them,<br> And Lord of every point<br> At the rising of the sun!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  Inn<u>a</u> zayyann<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>a a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> bizeenatin alkaw<u>a</u>kib<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>6.  We have indeed decked<br> The lower heaven with beauty<br> (In) the stars,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  Wa<u>h</u>if<i><u>th</u></i>an min kulli shay<u>ta</u>nin m<u>a</u>rid<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>7.  (For beauty) and for guard<br> Against all obstinate<br> Rebellious evil spirits,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  L<u>a</u> yassammaAAoona il<u>a</u> almala-i al-aAAl<u>a</u> wayuq<u>th</u>afoona min kulli j<u>a</u>nib<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>8.  (So) they should not strain<br> Their ears in the direction<br> Of the Exalted Assembly<br>But be cast away<br> From every side,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_009.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>9.  Du<u>h</u>ooran walahum AAa<u>tha</u>bun w<u>as</u>ib<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>9.  Repulsed, for they are<br> Under a perpetual penalty,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_010.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>10.  Ill<u>a</u> man kha<u>t</u>ifa alkha<u>t</u>fata faatbaAAahu shih<u>a</u>bun th<u>a</u>qib<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>10.  Except such as snatch away<br> Something by stealth, and they<br> Are pursued by a flaming<br> Fire, of piercing brightness.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_011.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>11.  Fa<b>i</b>staftihim ahum ashaddu khalqan am man khalaqn<u>a</u> inn<u>a </u>khalaqn<u>a</u>hum min <u>t</u>eenin l<u>a</u>zib<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>11.  Just ask their opinion:<br> Are they the more difficult<br> To create, or the (other) beings<br> We have created?<br> Them have We created<br> Out of a sticky clay!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_012.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>12.  Bal AAajibta wayaskharoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>12.  Truly dost thou marvel,<br> While they ridicule,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_013.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>13.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> <u>th</u>ukkiroo l<u>a</u> ya<u>th</u>kuroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>13.  And, when they are<br> Admonished, pay no heed,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_014.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>14.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> raaw <u>a</u>yatan yastaskhiroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>14.  And, when they see<br> A Sign, turn it<br> To mockery,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_015.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>15.  Waq<u>a</u>loo in h<u>atha</u> ill<u>a</u> si<u>h</u>run mubeen<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>15.  And say, "This is nothing<br> But evident sorcery!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_016.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>16.  A-i<u>tha</u> mitn<u>a</u> wakunn<u>a</u> tur<u>a</u>ban waAAi<i><u>th</u></i><u>a</u>man a-inn<u>a</u> lamabAAoothoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>16.  "What! when we die,<br> And become dust and bones,<br>Shall we (then) be<br> Raised up (again)?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_017.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>17.  Awa <u>a</u>b<u>a</u>on<u>a</u> al-awwaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>17.  "And also our fathers<br> Of old?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_018.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>18.  Qul naAAam waantum d<u>a</u>khiroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>18.  Say thou: "Yea, and ye shall<br> Then be humiliated<br> (On account of your evil).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_019.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>19.  Fa-innam<u>a</u> hiya zajratun w<u>ah</u>idatun fa-i<u>tha</u> hum yan<i><u>th</u></i>uroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>19.  Then it will be a single<br> (Compelling) cry;<br> And behold, they will<br> Begin to see!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_020.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>20.  Waq<u>a</u>loo y<u>a</u> waylan<u>a</u> h<u>atha</u> yawmu a<b>l</b>ddeen<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>20.  They will say, "Ah!<br> Woe to us! this is<br> The Day of Judgment!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/037/037_021.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>21.  H<u>atha</u> yawmu alfa<u>s</u>li alla<u>th</u>ee kuntum bihi tuka<u>thth</u>iboon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>21.  (A voice will say,)<br> "This is the Day<br> Of Sorting Out, whose<br> Truth ye (once) denied!"</p> <div class="filenav">
