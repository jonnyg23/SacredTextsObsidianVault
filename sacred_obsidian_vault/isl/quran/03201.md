 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXXII: Sajda, or Adoration.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/032/032_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  Alif-l<u>a</u>m-meem   </p>
 <p>1.  A. L. M.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/032/032_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  Tanzeelu alkit<u>a</u>bi l<u>a</u> rayba feehi min rabbi alAA<u>a</u>lameen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>2.  (This is) the revelation<br> Of the Book in which<br> There is no doubt,—<br> From the Lord of the Worlds.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/032/032_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  Am yaqooloona iftar<u>a</u>hu bal huwa al<u>h</u>aqqu min rabbika litun<u>th</u>ira qawman m<u>a</u> at<u>a</u>hum min na<u>th</u>eerin min qablika laAAallahum yahtadoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>3.  Or do they say,<br> "He has forged it"?<br> Nay, it is the Truth<br> From thy Lord, that thou<br> Mayest admonish a people<br> To whom no warner<br> Has come before thee:<br> In order that they<br> May receive guidance.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/032/032_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  All<u>a</u>hu alla<u>th</u>ee khalaqa a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>a wam<u>a</u> baynahum<u>a</u> fee sittati ayy<u>a</u>min thumma istaw<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> alAAarshi m<u>a</u> lakum min doonihi min waliyyin wal<u>a</u> shafeeAAin afal<u>a</u> tata<u>th</u>akkaroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>4.  It is God Who has<br> Created the heavens<br> And the earth, and all<br> Between them, in six Days,<br>And is firmly established<br> On the Throne (of authority):<br> Ye have none, besides Him,<br> To protect or intercede (for you):<br> Will ye not then<br> Receive admonition?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/032/032_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  Yudabbiru al-amra mina a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>-i il<u>a</u> al-ar<u>d</u>i thumma yaAAruju ilayhi fee yawmin k<u>a</u>na miqd<u>a</u>ruhu alfa sanatin mimm<u>a</u> taAAuddoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>5.  He rules (all) affairs<br> From the heavens<br> To the earth: in the end<br> Will (all affairs) go up<br> To Him, on a Day,<br> The space whereof will be<br> (As) a thousand years<br> Of your reckoning.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/032/032_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  <u>Tha</u>lika AA<u>a</u>limu alghaybi wa<b>al</b>shshah<u>a</u>dati alAAazeezu a<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>eem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>6.  Such is He, the Knower<br> Of all things, hidden<br> And open, the Exalted<br> (In power), the Merciful;—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/032/032_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  Alla<u>th</u>ee a<u>h</u>sana kulla shay-in khalaqahu wabadaa khalqa al-ins<u>a</u>ni min <u>t</u>een<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>7.  He Who has made<br> Everything which He has created<br> Most Good: He began<br> The creation of man<br> With (nothing more than) clay,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/032/032_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  Thumma jaAAala naslahu min sul<u>a</u>latin min m<u>a</u>-in maheen<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>8.  And made his progeny<br> From a quintessence<br> Of the nature of<br> A fluid despised:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/032/032_009.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>9.  Thumma saww<u>a</u>hu wanafakha feehi min roo<u>h</u>ihi wajaAAala lakumu a<b>l</b>ssamAAa wa<b>a</b>l-ab<u>sa</u>ra wa<b>a</b>l-af-idata qaleelan m<u>a</u> tashkuroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>9.  But He fashioned him<br> In due proportion, and breathed<br> Into him something of<br> His spirit. And He gave<br> You (the faculties of) hearing<br> And sight and feeling<br> (And understanding):<br> Little thanks do ye give!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/032/032_010.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>10.  Waq<u>a</u>loo a-i<u>tha</u> <u>d</u>al<u>a</u>ln<u>a</u> fee al-ar<u>d</u>i a-inn<u>a</u> lafee khalqin jadeedin bal hum biliq<u>a</u>-i rabbihim k<u>a</u>firoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>10.  And they say: "What!<br> When we lie, hidden<br> And lost, in the earth,<br> Shall we indeed be<br> In a Creation renewed?<br> Nay, they deny the Meeting<br> With their Lord!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/032/032_011.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>11.  Qul yatawaff<u>a</u>kum malaku almawti alla<u>th</u>ee wukkila bikum thumma il<u>a</u> rabbikum turjaAAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>11.  Say: "The Angel of Death,<br> Put in charge of you,<br> Will (duly) take your souls:<br> Then shall ye be brought<br> Back to your Lord."</p> <div class="filenav">
