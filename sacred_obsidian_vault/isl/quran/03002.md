 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXX.: Rūm, or The Roman Empire.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 2</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/030/030_011.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>11.  All<u>a</u>hu yabdao alkhalqa thumma yuAAeeduhu thumma ilayhi turjaAAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>11.  It is God Who begins<br> (The process of) creation;<br> Then repeats it; then<br> Shall ye be brought back<br> To Him.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/030/030_012.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>12.  Wayawma taqoomu a<b>l</b>ss<u>a</u>AAatu yublisu almujrimoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>12.  On the Day that<br> The Hour will be established,<br> The guilty will be<br> Struck dumb with despair.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/030/030_013.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>13.  Walam yakun lahum min shurak<u>a</u>-ihim shufaAA<u>a</u>o wak<u>a</u>noo bishurak<u>a</u>-ihim k<u>a</u>fireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>13.  No intercessor will they have<br> Among their "Partners",<br> And they will (themselves)<br> Reject their "Partners".</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/030/030_014.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>14.  Wayawma taqoomu a<b>l</b>ss<u>a</u>AAatu yawma-i<u>th</u>in yatafarraqoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>14.  On the Day that<br> The Hour will he established,—<br> That Day shall (all men)<br> Be sorted out.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/030/030_015.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>15.  Faamm<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo waAAamiloo a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>li<u>ha</u>ti fahum fee raw<u>d</u>atin yu<u>h</u>baroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>15.  Then those who have believed<br> And worked righteous deeds,<br> Shall be made happy<br> In a Mead of Delight.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/030/030_016.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>16.  Waamm<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo waka<u>thth</u>aboo bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin<u>a</u> waliq<u>a</u>-i al-<u>a</u>khirati faol<u>a</u>-ika fee alAAa<u>tha</u>bi mu<u>hd</u>aroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>16.  And those who have rejected<br> Faith and falsely denied<br> Our Signs and the meeting<br> Of the Hereafter,—such<br> Shall be brought forth to<br> Punishment.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/030/030_017.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>17.  Fasub<u>ha</u>na All<u>a</u>hi <u>h</u>eena tumsoona wa<u>h</u>eena tu<u>s</u>bi<u>h</u>oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>17.  So (give) glory to God,<br> When ye reach eventide<br>And when ye rise<br> In the morning;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/030/030_018.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>18.  Walahu al<u>h</u>amdu fee a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i waAAashiyyan wa<u>h</u>eena tu<i><u>th</u></i>hiroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>18.  Yea, To Him be praise,<br> In the heavens and on earth;<br> And in the late afternoon<br> And when the day<br> Begins to decline.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/030/030_019.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>19.  Yukhriju al<u>h</u>ayya mina almayyiti wayukhriju almayyita mina al<u>h</u>ayyi wayu<u>h</u>yee al-ar<u>d</u>a baAAda mawtih<u>a</u> waka<u>tha</u>lika tukhrajoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>19.  It is He Who brings out<br> The living from the dead,<br> And brings out the dead<br> From the living, and Who<br> Gives life to the earth<br> After it is dead:<br> And thus shall ye be<br> Brought out (from the dead).</p> <div class="filenav">
