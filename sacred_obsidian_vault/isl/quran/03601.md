 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXXVI.: Yā-Sīn (being Abbreviated Letters).</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/036/036_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  Y<u>a</u>-seen   </p>
 <p>1.  Yā Sīn.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/036/036_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  Wa<b>a</b>lqur-<u>a</u>ni al<u>h</u>akeem<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>2.  By the Qur-ān<br> Full of Wisdom,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/036/036_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  Innaka lamina almursaleen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>3.  Thou art indeed<br> One of the apostles,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/036/036_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  AAal<u>a</u> <u>s</u>ir<u>at</u>in mustaqeem<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>4.  On a Straight Way.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/036/036_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  Tanzeela alAAazeezi a<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>eem<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>5.  It is a Revelation<br> Sent down by (Him),<br> The Exalted in Might,<br> Most Merciful,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/036/036_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  Litun<u>th</u>ira qawman m<u>a</u> on<u>th</u>ira <u>a</u>b<u>a</u>ohum fahum gh<u>a</u>filoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>6.  In order that thou mayest<br> Admonish a people,<br> Whose fathers had received<br> No admonition, and who<br> Therefore remain heedless<br> (Of the Signs of God).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/036/036_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  Laqad <u>h</u>aqqa alqawlu AAal<u>a</u> aktharihim fahum l<u>a</u> yu/minoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>7.  The Word is proved true<br> Against the greater part of them:<br> For they do not believe.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/036/036_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  Inn<u>a</u> jaAAaln<u>a</u> fee aAAn<u>a</u>qihim aghl<u>a</u>lan fahiya il<u>a</u> al-a<u>th</u>q<u>a</u>ni fahum muqma<u>h</u>oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>8.  We have put yokes<br> Round their necks<br> Right up to their chins,<br> So that their heads are<br> Forced up (and they cannot see).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/036/036_009.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>9.  WajaAAaln<u>a</u> min bayni aydeehim saddan wamin khalfihim saddan faaghshayn<u>a</u>hum fahum l<u>a</u> yub<u>s</u>iroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>9.  And We have put<br> A bar in front of them<br> And a bar behind them,<br> And further, We have<br> Covered them up; so that<br> They cannot see.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/036/036_010.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>10.  Wasaw<u>a</u>on AAalayhim aan<u>th</u>artahum am lam tun<u>th</u>irhum l<u>a</u> yu/minoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>10.  The same is it to them<br> Whether thou admonish them<br> Or thou do not admonish<br> Them: they will not believe.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/036/036_011.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>11.  Innam<u>a</u> tun<u>th</u>iru mani ittabaAAa a<b>l</b><u>thth</u>ikra wakhashiya a<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>m<u>a</u>na bi<b>a</b>lghaybi fabashshirhu bimaghfiratin waajrin kareem<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>11.  Thou canst but admonish<br> Such a one as follows<br> The Message and fears<br> The (Lord) Most Gracious, unseen:<br> Give such a one, therefore,<br> Good tidings, of Forgiveness<br> And a reward most generous.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/036/036_012.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>12.  Inn<u>a</u> na<u>h</u>nu nu<u>h</u>yee almawt<u>a</u> wanaktubu m<u>a</u> qaddamoo wa<u>a</u>th<u>a</u>rahum wakulla shay-in a<u>hs</u>ayn<u>a</u>hu fee im<u>a</u>min mubeen<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>12.  Verily We shall give life<br> To the dead, and We record<br> That which they send before<br> And that which they leave<br> Behind, and of all things<br> Have We taken account<br>In a clear Book<br> (Of evidence).</p> <div class="filenav">
