 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XIX.: Maryam, or Mary.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 3</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_041.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>41.  Wa<b>o</b><u>th</u>kur fee alkit<u>a</u>bi ibr<u>a</u>heema innahu k<u>a</u>na <u>s</u>iddeeqan nabiyy<u>a</u><b>n</b>    </p>
 <p>41.  (Also) mention in the Book<br> (The story of) Abraham:<br> He was a man of Truth,<br> A prophet.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_042.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>42.  I<u>th</u> q<u>a</u>la li-abeehi y<u>a</u> abati lima taAAbudu m<u>a</u> l<u>a</u> yasmaAAu wal<u>a</u> yub<u>s</u>iru wal<u>a</u> yughnee AAanka shay-<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>42.  Behold, he said to his father:<br> "O my father! why<br> Worship that which heareth not<br> And seeth not, and can<br> Profit thee nothing?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_043.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>43.  Y<u>a</u> abati innee qad j<u>a</u>anee mina alAAilmi m<u>a</u> lam ya/tika fa<b>i</b>ttabiAAnee ahdika <u>s</u>ir<u>at</u>an sawiyy<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>43.  "O my father! to me<br> Hath come knowledge which<br> Hath not reached thee:<br> So follow me: I will guide<br> Thee to a Way that<br> Is even and straight.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_044.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>44.  Y<u>a</u> abati l<u>a</u> taAAbudi a<b>l</b>shshay<u>ta</u>na inna a<b>l</b>shshay<u>ta</u>na k<u>a</u>na li<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>m<u>a</u>ni AAa<u>s</u>iyy<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>44.  "O my father! serve not<br> Satan: for Satan is<br> A rebel against (God)<br> Most Gracious</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_045.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>45.  Y<u>a</u> abati innee akh<u>a</u>fu an yamassaka AAa<u>tha</u>bun mina a<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>m<u>a</u>ni fatakoona li<b>l</b>shshya<u>ta</u>ni waliyy<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>45.  "O my father! I fear<br> Lest a Penalty afflict thee<br> From (God) Most Gracious,<br> So that thou become<br> To Satan a friend."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_046.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>46.  Q<u>a</u>la ar<u>a</u>ghibun anta AAan <u>a</u>lihatee y<u>a</u> ibr<u>a</u>heemu la-in lam tantahi laarjumannaka wa<b>o</b>hjurnee maliyy<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>46.  (The father) replied: "Dost thou<br> Hate my gods, O Abraham?<br> If thou forbear not, I will<br> Indeed stone thee:<br> Now get away from me<br> For a good long while!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_047.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>47.  Q<u>a</u>la sal<u>a</u>mun AAalayka saastaghfiru laka rabbee innahu k<u>a</u>na bee <u>h</u>afiyy<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>47.  Abraham said: "Peace be<br> On thee: I will pray<br> To my Lord for thy<br> forgiveness:<br> For He is to me<br> Most Gracious.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_048.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>48.  WaaAAtazilukum wam<u>a</u> tadAAoona min dooni All<u>a</u>hi waadAAoo rabbee AAas<u>a</u> all<u>a</u> akoona biduAA<u>a</u>-i rabbee shaqiyy<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>48.  "And I will turn away<br> From you (all) and from those<br> Whom ye invoke besides God:<br> I will call on my Lord:<br> Perhaps, by my prayer to my Lord,<br> I shall be not unblest."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_049.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>49.  Falamm<u>a</u> iAAtazalahum wam<u>a</u> yaAAbudoona min dooni All<u>a</u>hi wahabn<u>a</u> lahu is<u>ha</u>qa wayaAAqooba wakullan jaAAaln<u>a</u> nabiyy<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>49.  When he had turned away<br> From them and from those<br> Whom they worshipped besides<br> God, We bestowed on him<br> Isaac and Jacob, and each one<br> Of them We made a prophet.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/019/019_050.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>50.  Wawahabn<u>a</u> lahum min ra<u>h</u>matin<u>a</u> wajaAAaln<u>a</u> lahum lis<u>a</u>na <u>s</u>idqin AAaliyy<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>50.  And We bestowed<br> Of Our Mercy on them,<br> And We granted them<br> Lofty honour on the tongue<br> Of truth.</p> <div class="filenav">
