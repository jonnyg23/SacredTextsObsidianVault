 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XCIX.: Zilzāl, or The Convulsion.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/099/099_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  I<u>tha</u> zulzilati al-ar<u>d</u>u zilz<u>a</u>lah<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>1.  When the Earth is<br> Shaken to her (utmost)<br> convulsion,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/099/099_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  Waakhrajati al-ar<u>d</u>u athq<u>a</u>lah<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>2.  And the Earth throws up<br> Her burdens (from within),</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/099/099_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  Waq<u>a</u>la al-ins<u>a</u>nu m<u>a</u> lah<u>a</u>    </p>
 <p>3.  And man cries (distressed):<br> "What is the matter with her?"—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/099/099_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  Yawma-i<u>th</u>in tu<u>h</u>addithu akhb<u>a</u>rah<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>4.  On that Day will she<br> Declare her tidings:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/099/099_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  Bi-anna rabbaka aw<u>ha</u> lah<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>5.  For that thy Lord will<br> Have given her inspiration,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/099/099_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  Yawma-i<u>th</u>in ya<u>s</u>duru a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>su asht<u>a</u>tan liyuraw aAAm<u>a</u>lahum   </p>
 <p>6.  On that Day will men<br> Proceed in companies sorted out,<br>To be shown the Deeds<br> That they (had done).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/099/099_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  Faman yaAAmal mithq<u>a</u>la <u>th</u>arratin khayran yarah<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>7.  Then shall anyone who<br> Has done an atom's weight<br> Of good, see it!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/099/099_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  Waman yaAAmal mithq<u>a</u>la <u>th</u>arratin sharran yarah<b>u</b>      </p>
 <p>8.  And anyone who<br> Has done an atom's weight<br> Of evil, shall see it.</p>  <div class="filenav">
