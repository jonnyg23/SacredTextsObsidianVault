 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XI.: Hūd (The Prophet Hūd).</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 8</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_084.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>84.  Wa-il<u>a</u> madyana akh<u>a</u>hum shuAAayban q<u>a</u>la y<u>a</u> qawmi oAAbudoo All<u>a</u>ha m<u>a</u> lakum min il<u>a</u>hin ghayruhu wal<u>a</u> tanqu<u>s</u>oo almiky<u>a</u>la wa<b>a</b>lmeez<u>a</u>na innee ar<u>a</u>kum bikhayrin wa-innee akh<u>a</u>fu AAalaykum AAa<u>tha</u>ba yawmin mu<u>h</u>ee<u>t</u><b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>84.  To the Madyan people<br> (We sent) Shu’aib, one<br> Of their own brethren: he said:<br> "O my people! worship God:<br> Ye have no other god<br> But Him. And give not<br> Short measure or weight:<br> I see you in prosperity,<br> But I fear for you<br> The Penalty of a Day<br> That will compass (you) all round.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_085.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>85.  Way<u>a</u> qawmi awfoo almiky<u>a</u>la wa<b>a</b>lmeez<u>a</u>na bi<b>a</b>lqis<u>t</u>i wal<u>a</u> tabkhasoo a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>sa ashy<u>a</u>ahum wal<u>a</u> taAAthaw fee al-ar<u>d</u>i mufsideen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>85.  "And O my people! give<br> Just measure and weight,<br> Nor withhold from the people<br> The things that are their due:<br> Commit not evil in the land<br> With intent to do mischief.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_086.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>86.  Baqiyyatu All<u>a</u>hi khayrun lakum in kuntum mu/mineena wam<u>a</u> an<u>a</u> AAalaykum bi<u>h</u>afee<i><u>th</u></i><b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>86.  "That which is left you<br> By God is best for you,<br>If ye (but) believed!<br> But I am not set<br> Over you to keep watch!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_087.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>87.  Q<u>a</u>loo y<u>a</u> shuAAaybu a<u>s</u>al<u>a</u>tuka ta/muruka an natruka m<u>a</u> yaAAbudu <u>a</u>b<u>a</u>on<u>a</u> aw an nafAAala fee amw<u>a</u>lin<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> nash<u>a</u>o innaka laanta al<u>h</u>aleemu a<b>l</b>rrasheed<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>87.  They said: "O Shu’aib!<br> Does thy (religion of) prayer<br> Command thee that we<br> Leave off the worship which<br> Our fathers practised, or<br> That we leave off doing<br> What we like with our property?<br> Truly, thou art the one<br> That forbeareth with faults<br> And is right-minded!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_088.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>88.  Q<u>a</u>la y<u>a</u> qawmi araaytum in kuntu AAal<u>a</u> bayyinatin min rabbee warazaqanee minhu rizqan <u>h</u>asanan wam<u>a</u> oreedu an okh<u>a</u>lifakum il<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> anh<u>a</u>kum AAanhu in oreedu ill<u>a</u> al-i<u>s</u>l<u>ah</u>a m<u>a</u> ista<u>t</u>aAAtu wam<u>a</u> tawfeeqee ill<u>a</u> bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi AAalayhi tawakkaltu wa-ilayhi oneeb<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>88.  He said: "O my people!<br> See ye whether I hate<br> A Clear (Sign) from my Lord,<br> And He hath given me<br> Sustenance (pure and) good<br> As from Himself? I wish not,<br> In opposition to you, to do<br> That which I forbid you to do.<br> I only desire (your) betterment<br> To the best of my power;<br> And my success (in my task)<br> Can only come from God.<br> In Him I trust,<br> And unto Him I look.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_089.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>89.  Way<u>a</u> qawmi l<u>a</u> yajrimannakum shiq<u>a</u>qee an yu<u>s</u>eebakum mithlu m<u>a</u> a<u>sa</u>ba qawma noo<u>h</u>in aw qawma hoodin aw qawma <u>sa</u>li<u>h</u>in wam<u>a</u> qawmu loo<u>t</u>in minkum bibaAAeed<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>89.  "And O my people!<br> Let not my dissent (from you)<br>Cause you to sin,<br> Lest ye suffer<br> A fate similar to that<br> Of the people of Noah<br> Or of Hūd or of Sālib,<br> Nor are the people of Lūt<br> Far off from you!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_090.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>90.  Wa<b>i</b>staghfiroo rabbakum thumma tooboo ilayhi inna rabbee ra<u>h</u>eemun wadood<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>90.  "But ask forgiveness<br> Of your Lord, and turn<br> Unto Him (in repentance):<br> For my Lord is indeed<br> Full of mercy and loving-kindness."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_091.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>91.  Q<u>a</u>loo y<u>a</u> shuAAaybu m<u>a</u> nafqahu katheeran mimm<u>a</u> taqoolu wa-inn<u>a</u> lanar<u>a</u>ka feen<u>a</u> <u>d</u>aAAeefan walawl<u>a</u> rah<u>t</u>uka larajamn<u>a</u>ka wam<u>a</u> anta AAalayn<u>a</u> biAAazeez<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>91.  They said: "O Shu’aib!<br> Much of what thou sayest<br> We do not understand!<br> In fact among us we see<br> That thou hast no strength!<br> Were it not for thy family,<br> We should certainly<br> Have stoned thee!<br> For thou hast among us<br> No great position!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_092.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>92.  Q<u>a</u>la y<u>a</u> qawmi arah<u>t</u>ee aAAazzu AAalaykum mina All<u>a</u>hi wa<b>i</b>ttakha<u>th</u>tumoohu war<u>a</u>akum <i><u>th</u></i>ihriyyan inna rabbee bim<u>a</u> taAAmaloona mu<u>h</u>ee<u>t</u><b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>92.  He said: "O my people!<br> Is then my family<br> Of more consideration with you<br> Than God? For ye cast Him<br> Away behind your backs<br> (With contempt). But verily<br> My Lord encompasseth<br> On all sides<br> All that ye do!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_093.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>93.  Way<u>a</u> qawmi iAAmaloo AAal<u>a</u> mak<u>a</u>natikum innee AA<u>a</u>milun sawfa taAAlamoona man ya/teehi AAa<u>tha</u>bun yukhzeehi waman huwa k<u>ath</u>ibun wa<b>i</b>rtaqiboo inne maAAakum raqeeb<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>93.  "And O my people!<br> Do whatever ye can:<br>I will do (my part):<br> Soon will ye know<br> Who it is on whom<br> Descends the Penalty<br> Of ignominy, and who<br> Is a liar!<br> And watch ye!<br> For I too am watching<br> With you!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_094.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>94.  Walamm<u>a</u> j<u>a</u>a amrun<u>a</u> najjayn<u>a</u> shuAAayban wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo maAAahu bira<u>h</u>matin minn<u>a</u> waakha<u>th</u>ati alla<u>th</u>eena <i><u>th</u></i>alamoo a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>ay<u>h</u>atu faa<u>s</u>ba<u>h</u>oo fee diy<u>a</u>rihim j<u>a</u>thimeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>94.  When Our decree issued,<br> We saved Shu’aib and those<br> Who believed with him,<br> By (special) Mercy from Ourselves<br> But the (mighty) Blast did seize<br> The wrong-doers, and they<br> Lay prostrate in their homes<br> By the morning,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_095.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>95.  Kaan lam yaghnaw feeh<u>a</u> al<u>a</u> buAAdan limadyana kam<u>a</u> baAAidat thamood<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>95.  As if they had never<br> Dwelt and flourished there!<br> Ah! Behold! How the Madyan<br> Were removed (from sight)<br> As were removed the <u>Th</u>amūd!</p> <div class="filenav">
