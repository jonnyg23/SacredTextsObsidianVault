 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra II.: Baqara, or the Heifer.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 25</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_197.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>197.  Al<u>h</u>ajju ashhurun maAAloom<u>a</u>tun faman fara<u>d</u>a feehinna al<u>h</u>ajja fal<u>a</u> rafatha wal<u>a</u> fusooqa wal<u>a</u> jid<u>a</u>la fee al<u>h</u>ajji wam<u>a</u> tafAAaloo min khayrin yaAAlamhu All<u>a</u>hu watazawwadoo fa-inna khayra a<b>l</b>zz<u>a</u>di a<b>l</b>ttaqw<u>a</u> wa<b>i</b>ttaqooni y<u>a</u> olee al-alb<u>a</u>b<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>197.  For Ḥajj <br> Are the months well known.<br> If any one undertakes<br> That duty therein,<br> Let there be no obscenity,<br> Nor wickedness,<br> Nor wrangling<br> In the Ḥajj.<br> And whatever good<br> Ye do, (be sure)<br> God knoweth it.<br> And take a provision<br> (With you) for the journey,<br> But the best of provisions<br> Is right conduct.<br> So fear Me,<br> O ye that are wise.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_198.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>198.  Laysa AAalaykum jun<u>ah</u>un an tabtaghoo fa<u>d</u>lan min rabbikum fa-i<u>tha</u> afa<u>d</u>tum min AAaraf<u>a</u>tin fa<b>o</b><u>th</u>kuroo All<u>a</u>ha AAinda almashAAari al<u>h</u>ar<u>a</u>mi wa<b>o</b><u>th</u>kuroohu kam<u>a</u> had<u>a</u>kum wa-in kuntum min qablihi lamina a<b>l</b><u>dda</u>lleen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>198.  It is no crime in you<br> If ye seek of the bounty<br> Of your Lord (during pilgrimage).<br> Then when ye pour down<br> From (Mount) ’Arafāt,<br> Celebrate the praises of God<br> At the Sacred Monument,<br>And celebrate His praises<br> As He has directed you,<br> Even though, before this,<br> Ye went astray.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_199.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>199.  Thumma afee<u>d</u>oo min <u>h</u>aythu af<u>ad</u>a a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>su wa<b>i</b>staghfiroo All<u>a</u>ha inna All<u>a</u>ha ghafoorun ra<u>h</u>eem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>199.  Then pass on<br> At a quick pace from the place<br> Whence it is usual<br> For the multitude<br> So to do, and ask<br> For God's forgiveness.<br> For God is Oft-forgiving,<br> Most Merciful.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_200.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>200.  Fa-i<u>tha</u> qa<u>d</u>aytum man<u>a</u>sikakum fa<b>o</b><u>th</u>kuroo All<u>a</u>ha ka<u>th</u>ikrikum <u>a</u>b<u>a</u>akum aw ashadda <u>th</u>ikran famina a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si man yaqoolu rabban<u>a</u> <u>a</u>tin<u>a</u> fee a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> wam<u>a</u> lahu fee al-<u>a</u>khirati min khal<u>a</u>q<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>200.  So when ye have<br> Accomplished your holy rites,<br> Celebrate the praises of God,<br> As ye used to celebrate<br> The praises of your fathers,—<br> Yea, with far more<br> Heart and soul.<br> There are men who say:<br> "Our <a href="errata.htm#0">Lord</a>! Give us<br> (Thy bounties) in this world!"<br> But they will have<br> No portion in the Hereafter.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_201.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>201.  Waminhum man yaqoolu rabban<u>a</u> <u>a</u>tin<u>a</u> fee a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> <u>h</u>asanatan wafee al-<u>a</u>khirati <u>h</u>asanatan waqin<u>a</u> AAa<u>tha</u>ba a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>r<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>201.  And there are men who say:<br> "Our Lord! Give us<br> Good in this world<br> And good in the Hereafter,<br> And defend us<br> From the torment<br> Of the Fire!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_202.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>202.  Ol<u>a</u>-ika lahum na<u>s</u>eebun mimm<u>a</u> kasaboo wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu sareeAAu al<u>h</u>is<u>a</u>b<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>202.  To these will be allotted<br> What they have earned;<br> And God is quick in account.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_203.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>203.  Wa<b>o</b><u>th</u>kuroo All<u>a</u>ha fee ayy<u>a</u>min maAAdood<u>a</u>tin faman taAAajjala fee yawmayni fal<u>a</u> ithma AAalayhi waman taakhkhara fal<u>a</u> ithma AAalayhi limani ittaq<u>a</u> wa<b>i</b>ttaqoo All<u>a</u>ha wa<b>i</b>AAlamoo annakum ilayhi tu<u>h</u>sharoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>203.  Celebrate the praises of God<br> During the Appointed Days.<br> But if any one hastens<br> To leave in two days,<br> There is no blame on him,<br> And if any one stays on,<br> There is no blame on him,<br> If his aim is to do right.<br> Then fear God, and know<br> That ye will surely<br> Be gathered unto Him.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_204.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>204.  Wamina a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si man yuAAjibuka qawluhu fee al<u>h</u>ay<u>a</u>ti a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> wayushhidu All<u>a</u>ha AAal<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> fee qalbihi wahuwa aladdu alkhi<u>sa</u>m<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>204.  There is the type of man<br> Whose speech<br> About this world's life<br> May dazzle thee,<br> And he calls God to witness<br> About what is in his heart;<br> Yet is he the most contentious<br> Of enemies.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_205.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>205.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> tawall<u>a</u> saAA<u>a</u> fee al-ar<u>d</u>i liyufsida feeh<u>a</u> wayuhlika al<u>h</u>artha wa<b>al</b>nnasla wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu l<u>a</u> yu<u>h</u>ibbu alfas<u>a</u>d<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>205.  When he turns his back,<br> His aim everywhere<br> Is to spread mischief<br> Through the earth and destroy<br> Crops and cattle.<br> But God loveth not mischief.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_206.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>206.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> qeela lahu ittaqi All<u>a</u>ha akha<u>th</u>at-hu alAAizzatu bi<b>a</b>l-ithmi fa<u>h</u>asbuhu jahannamu walabi/sa almih<u>a</u>d<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>206.  When it is said to him,<br> "Fear God,"<br> He is led by arrogance<br> To (more) crime.<br>Enough for him is Hell;—<br> An evil bed indeed<br> (To lie on)!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_207.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>207.  Wamina a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si man yashree nafsahu ibtigh<u>a</u>a mar<u>da</u>ti All<u>a</u>hi wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu raoofun bi<b>a</b>lAAib<u>a</u>d<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>207.  And there is the type of man<br> Who gives his life<br> To earn the pleasure of God;<br> And God is full of kindness<br> To (His) devotees.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_208.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>208.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo odkhuloo fee a<b>l</b>ssilmi k<u>a</u>ffatan wal<u>a</u> tattabiAAoo khu<u>t</u>uw<u>a</u>ti a<b>l</b>shshay<u>ta</u>ni innahu lakum AAaduwwun mubeen<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>208.  O ye who believe!<br> Enter into Islam<br> Whole-heartedly;<br> And follow not<br> The footsteps<br> Of the Evil One;<br> For he is to you<br> An avowed enemy.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_209.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>209.  Fa-in zalaltum min baAAdi m<u>a</u> j<u>a</u>atkumu albayyin<u>a</u>tu fa<b>i</b>AAlamoo anna All<u>a</u>ha AAazeezun <u>h</u>akeem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>209.  If ye backslide<br> After the clear (Signs)<br> Have come to you,<br> Then know that God<br> Is Exalted in Power, Wise.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_210.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>210.  Hal yan<i><u>th</u></i>uroona ill<u>a</u> an ya/tiyahumu All<u>a</u>hu fee <i><u>th</u></i>ulalin mina algham<u>a</u>mi wa<b>a</b>lmal<u>a</u>-ikatu waqu<u>d</u>iya al-amru wa-il<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi turjaAAu al-omoor<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>210.  Will they wait<br> Until God comes to them<br> In canopies of clouds,<br> With angels (in His train)<br> And the question<br> Is (thus) settled?<br> But to God<br> Do all questions<br> Go back (for decision).</p> <div class="filenav">
