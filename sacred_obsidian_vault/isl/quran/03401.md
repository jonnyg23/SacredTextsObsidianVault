 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXXIV.: Sabā, or the City of Sabā</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/034/034_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  Al<u>h</u>amdu lill<u>a</u>hi alla<u>th</u>ee lahu m<u>a</u> fee a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wam<u>a</u> fee al-ar<u>d</u>i walahu al<u>h</u>amdu fee al-<u>a</u>khirati wahuwa al<u>h</u>akeemu alkhabeer<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>1.  Praise be to God,<br> To Whom belong all things<br> In the heavens and on earth:<br> To Him be Praise<br> In the Hereafter:<br> And He is Full of Wisdom,<br> Acquainted with all things.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/034/034_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  YaAAlamu m<u>a</u> yaliju fee al-ar<u>d</u>i wam<u>a</u> yakhruju minh<u>a</u> wam<u>a</u> yanzilu mina a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>-i wam<u>a</u> yaAAruju feeh<u>a</u> wahuwa a<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>eemu alghafoor<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>2.  He knows all that goes<br> Into the earth, and all that<br> Comes out thereof; all that<br> Comes down from the sky<br> And all that ascends thereto<br> And He is the Most Merciful,<br> The Oft-Forgiving.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/034/034_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  Waq<u>a</u>la alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo l<u>a</u> ta/teen<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>ss<u>a</u>AAatu qul bal<u>a</u> warabbee lata/tiyannakum AA<u>a</u>limi alghaybi l<u>a</u> yaAAzubu AAanhu mithq<u>a</u>lu <u>th</u>arratin fee a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wal<u>a</u> fee al-ar<u>d</u>i wal<u>a</u> a<u>s</u>gharu min <u>tha</u>lika wal<u>a</u> akbaru ill<u>a</u> fee kit<u>a</u>bin mubeen<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>3.  The Unbelievers say,<br> "Never to us will come<br>The Hour": say, "Nay!<br> But most surely,<br> By my Lord, it will come<br> Upon you;—by Him<br> Who knows the unseen,—<br> From Whom is not hidden<br> The least little atom<br> In the Heavens or on earth:<br> Nor is there anything less<br> Than that, or greater, but<br> Is in the Record Perspicuous:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/034/034_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  Liyajziya alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo waAAamiloo a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>li<u>ha</u>ti ol<u>a</u>-ika lahum maghfiratun warizqun kareem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>4.  That He may reward<br> Those who believe and work<br> Deeds of righteousness: for such<br> Is Forgiveness and a Sustenance<br> Most Generous."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/034/034_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  Wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena saAAaw fee <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin<u>a</u> muAA<u>a</u>jizeena ol<u>a</u>-ika lahum AAa<u>tha</u>bun min rijzin aleem<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>5.  But those who strive<br> Against Our Signs, to frustrate<br> Them,—for such will be<br> A Penalty,—a Punishment<br> Most humiliating.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/034/034_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  Wayar<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena ootoo alAAilma alla<u>th</u>ee onzila ilayka min rabbika huwa al<u>h</u>aqqa wayahdee il<u>a</u> <u>s</u>ir<u>at</u>i alAAazeezi al<u>h</u>ameed<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>6.  And those to whom<br> Knowledge has come see<br> That the (Revelation) sent down<br> To thee from thy Lord—<br> That is the Truth,<br> And that it guides<br>To the Path of the Exalted<br> (In Might), Worthy<br> Of all praise.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/034/034_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  Waq<u>a</u>la alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo hal nadullukum AAal<u>a</u> rajulin yunabbi-okum i<u>tha</u> muzziqtum kulla mumazzaqin innakum lafee khalqin jadeed<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>7.  The Unbelievers say<br> (In ridicule): "Shall we<br> Point out to you a man<br> That will tell you,<br> When ye are all scattered<br> To pieces in disintegration,<br> That ye shall (then be<br> Raised) in a New Creation?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/034/034_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  Aftar<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi ka<u>th</u>iban am bihi jinnatun bali alla<u>th</u>eena l<u>a</u> yu/minoona bi<b>a</b>l-<u>a</u>khirati fee alAAa<u>tha</u>bi wa<b>al</b><u>dd</u>al<u>a</u>li albaAAeed<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>8.  "Has he invented a falsehood<br> Against God, or has<br> A spirit (seized) him?"—<br> Nay, it is those who<br> Believe not in the Hereafter,<br> That are in (real) Penalty,<br> And in farthest Error.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/034/034_009.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>9.  Afalam yaraw il<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> bayna aydeehim wam<u>a</u> khalfahum mina a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>-i wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i in nasha/ nakhsif bihimu al-ar<u>d</u>a aw nusqi<u>t</u> AAalayhim kisafan mina a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>-i inna fee <u>tha</u>lika la<u>a</u>yatan likulli AAabdin muneeb<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>9.  See they not what is<br> Before them and behind them,<br> Of the sky and the earth<br> If We wished, We could<br> Cause the earth to swallow<br> Them up, or cause a piece<br> Of the sky to fall upon them.<br> Verily in this is a Sign<br> For every devotee that<br> Turns to God (in repentance).</p> <div class="filenav">
