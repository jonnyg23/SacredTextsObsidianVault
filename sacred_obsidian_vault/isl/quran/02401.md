 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXIV.: Nūr, or Light.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/024/024_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  Sooratun anzaln<u>a</u>h<u>a</u> wafara<u>d</u>n<u>a</u>h<u>a</u> waanzaln<u>a</u> feeh<u>a</u> <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin bayyin<u>a</u>tin laAAallakum ta<u>th</u>akkaroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>1.  A Sūra which We<br> Have sent down and<br> Which We have ordained:<br> In it have We sent down<br> Clear Signs, in order that<br> Ye may receive admonition.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/024/024_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  A<b>l</b>zz<u>a</u>niyatu wa<b>al</b>zz<u>a</u>nee fa<b>i</b>jlidoo kulla w<u>ah</u>idin minhum<u>a</u> mi-ata jaldatin wal<u>a</u> ta/khu<u>th</u>kum bihim<u>a</u> ra/fatun fee deeni All<u>a</u>hi in kuntum tu/minoona bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi wa<b>a</b>lyawmi al-<u>a</u>khiri walyashhad AAa<u>tha</u>bahum<u>a</u> <u>ta</u>-ifatun mina almu/mineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>2.  The woman and the man<br> Guilty of adultery or fornication,—<br> Flog each of them<br> With a hundred stripes:<br> Let not compassion move you<br> In their case, in a matter<br> Prescribed by God, if ye believe<br> In God and the Last Day:<br> And let a party<br> Of the Believers<br> Witness their punishment.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/024/024_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  A<b>l</b>zz<u>a</u>nee l<u>a</u> yanki<u>h</u>u ill<u>a</u> z<u>a</u>niyatan aw mushrikatan wa<b>al</b>zz<u>a</u>niyatu l<u>a</u> yanki<u>h</u>uh<u>a</u> ill<u>a</u> z<u>a</u>nin aw mushrikun wa<u>h</u>urrima <u>tha</u>lika AAal<u>a</u> almu/mineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>3.  Let no man guilty of<br> Adultery or fornication marry<br> Any but a woman<br> Similarly guilty, or an Unbeliever:<br> Nor let any but such a man<br> Or an Unbeliever<br> Marry such a woman:<br> To the Believers such a thing<br> Is forbidden.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/024/024_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  Wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena yarmoona almu<u>hs</u>an<u>a</u>ti thumma lam ya/too bi-arbaAAati shuhad<u>a</u>a fa<b>i</b>jlidoohum tham<u>a</u>neena jaldatan wal<u>a</u> taqbaloo lahum shah<u>a</u>datan abadan waol<u>a</u>-ika humu alf<u>a</u>siqoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>4.  And those who launch<br> A charge against chaste women,<br> And produce not four witnesses<br> (To support their allegations),—<br> Flog them with eighty stripes;<br> And reject their evidence<br> Ever after: for such men<br> Are wicked transgressors;—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/024/024_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  Ill<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena t<u>a</u>boo min baAAdi <u>tha</u>lika waa<u>s</u>la<u>h</u>oo fa-inna All<u>a</u>ha ghafoorun ra<u>h</u>eem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>5.  Unless they repent thereafter<br> And mend (their conduct);<br> For God is Oft-Forgiving,<br> Most Merciful.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/024/024_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  Wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena yarmoona azw<u>a</u>jahum walam yakun lahum shuhad<u>a</u>o ill<u>a</u> anfusuhum fashah<u>a</u>datu a<u>h</u>adihim arbaAAu shah<u>a</u>d<u>a</u>tin bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi innahu lamina a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>diqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>6.  And for those who launch<br> A charge against their spouses,<br> And have (in support)<br> No evidence but their own,—<br>Their solitary evidence<br> (Can be received) if they<br> Bear witness four times<br> (With an oath) by God<br> That they are solemnly<br> Telling the truth;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/024/024_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  Wa<b>a</b>lkh<u>a</u>misatu anna laAAnata All<u>a</u>hi AAalayhi in k<u>a</u>na mina alk<u>ath</u>ibeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>7.  And the fifth (oath)<br> (Should be) that they solemnly<br> Invoke the curse of God<br> On themselves if they<br> Tell a lie.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/024/024_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  Wayadrao AAanh<u>a</u> alAAa<u>tha</u>ba an tashhada arbaAAa shah<u>a</u>d<u>a</u>tin bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi innahu lamina alk<u>ath</u>ibeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>8.  But it would avert<br> The punishment from the wife,<br> If she bears witness<br> Four times (with an oath)<br> By God, that (her husband)<br> Is telling a lie;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/024/024_009.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>9.  Wa<b>a</b>lkh<u>a</u>misata anna gha<u>d</u>aba All<u>a</u>hi AAalayh<u>a</u> in k<u>a</u>na mina a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>diqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>9.  And the fifth (oath)<br> Should be that she solemnly<br> Invokes the wrath of God<br> On herself if (her accuser)<br> Is telling the truth.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/024/024_010.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>10.  Walawl<u>a</u> fa<u>d</u>lu All<u>a</u>hi AAalaykum wara<u>h</u>matuhu waanna All<u>a</u>ha taww<u>a</u>bun <u>h</u>akeem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>10.  If it were not<br> For God's grace and mercy<br> On you, and that God<br> Is Oft-Returning,<br> Full of wisdom,<br> (Ye would be ruined indeed).</p> <div class="filenav">
