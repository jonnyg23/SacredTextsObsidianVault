 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra L.: Qāf.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/050/050_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  Q<u>a</u>f wa<b>a</b>lqur-<u>a</u>ni almajeed<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>1.  Qāf.<br>By the Glorious Qur-ān<br> (Thou art God's Apostle).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/050/050_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  Bal AAajiboo an j<u>a</u>ahum mun<u>th</u>irun minhum faq<u>a</u>la alk<u>a</u>firoona h<u>atha</u> shay-on AAajeeb<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>2.  But they wonder that<br> There has come to them<br> A Warner from among<br> Themselves.<br> So the Unbelievers say:<br> "This is a wonderful thing!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/050/050_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  A-i<u>tha</u> mitn<u>a</u> wakunn<u>a</u> tur<u>a</u>ban <u>tha</u>lika rajAAun baAAeed<b>un</b>    </p>
 <p>3.  "What! When we die<br> And become dust, (shall we<br> Live again?) That is<br> A (sort of) Return<br> Far (from our understanding)."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/050/050_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  Qad AAalimn<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> tanqu<u>s</u>u al-ar<u>d</u>u minhum waAAindan<u>a</u> kit<u>a</u>bun <u>h</u>afee<i><u>th</u></i><b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>4.  We already know<br> How much of them<br> The earth takes away:<br> With Us is a Record<br> Guarding (the full account).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/050/050_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  Bal ka<u>thth</u>aboo bi<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>aqqi lamm<u>a</u> j<u>a</u>ahum fahum fee amrin mareej<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>5.  But they deny the truth<br> When it comes to them:<br> So they are in<br> A confused state.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/050/050_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  Afalam yan<i><u>th</u></i>uroo il<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>-i fawqahum kayfa banayn<u>a</u>h<u>a</u> wazayyann<u>a</u>h<u>a</u> wam<u>a</u> lah<u>a</u> min furooj<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>6.  Do they not look<br> At the sky above them?—<br> How We have made it<br> And adorned it,<br> And there are no<br> Flaws in it?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/050/050_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  Wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>a madadn<u>a</u>h<u>a</u> waalqayn<u>a</u> feeh<u>a</u> raw<u>a</u>siya waanbatn<u>a</u> feeh<u>a</u> min kulli zawjin baheej<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>7.  And the earth—<br> We have spread it out,<br> And set thereon mountains<br> Standing firm, and produced<br> Therein every kind of<br> Beautiful growth (in pairs)—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/050/050_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  Tab<u>s</u>iratan wa<u>th</u>ikr<u>a</u> likulli AAabdin muneeb<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>8.  To be observed<br> And commemorated<br> By every devotee<br> Turning (to God).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/050/050_009.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>9.  Wanazzaln<u>a</u> mina a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>-i m<u>a</u>an mub<u>a</u>rakan faanbatn<u>a</u> bihi jann<u>a</u>tin wa<u>h</u>abba al<u>h</u>a<u>s</u>eed<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>9.  And We send down<br> From the sky Rain<br> Charged with blessing,<br> And We produce therewith<br> Gardens and Grain for harvests;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/050/050_010.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>10.  Wa<b>al</b>nnakhla b<u>a</u>siq<u>a</u>tin lah<u>a</u> <u>t</u>alAAun na<u>d</u>eed<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>10.  And tall (and stately)<br> Palm-trees, with shoots<br> Of fruit-stalks, piled<br> One over another;—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/050/050_011.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>11.  Rizqan lilAAib<u>a</u>di waa<u>h</u>yayn<u>a</u> bihi baldatan maytan ka<u>tha</u>lika alkhurooj<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>11.  </p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/050/050_012.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>12.  Ka<u>thth</u>abat qablahum qawmu noo<u>h</u>in waa<u>s</u>-<u>ha</u>bu a<b>l</b>rrassi wathamood<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>12.  Before them was denied<br> (The Hereafter) by the People<br> Of Noah, the Companions<br> Of the Rass, the <u>Th</u>amūd,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/050/050_013.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>13.  WaAA<u>a</u>dun wafirAAawnu wa-ikhw<u>a</u>nu loo<u>t</u><b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>13.  The ‘Ād, Pharaoh,<br> The Brethren of Lūṭ,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/050/050_014.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>14.  Waa<u>s</u>-<u>ha</u>bu al-aykati waqawmu tubbaAAin kullun ka<u>thth</u>aba a<b>l</b>rrusula fa<u>h</u>aqqa waAAeed<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>14.  The Companions of the Wood,<br> And the People of Tubba‘;<br> Each one (of them) rejected<br> The apostles, and My warning<br> Was duly fulfilled (in them).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/050/050_015.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>15.  AfaAAayeen<u>a</u> bi<b>a</b>lkhalqi al-awwali bal hum fee labsin min khalqin jadeed<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>15.  Were We then weary<br> With the first Creation,<br> That they should be<br> In confused doubt<br> About a new Creation?</p> <div class="filenav">
