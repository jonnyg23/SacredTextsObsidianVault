 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXXI.: Luqmān (the Wise).</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 2</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/031/031_012.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>12.  Walaqad <u>a</u>tayn<u>a</u> luqm<u>a</u>na al<u>h</u>ikmata ani oshkur lill<u>a</u>hi waman yashkur fa-innam<u>a</u> yashkuru linafsihi waman kafara fa-inna All<u>a</u>ha ghaniyyun <u>h</u>ameed<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>12.  We bestowed (in the past)<br> Wisdom on Luqmān:<br> "Show (thy) gratitude to God."<br> Any who is (so) grateful<br> Does so to the profit<br> Of his own soul: but if<br> Any is ungrateful, verily<br> God is free of all wants,<br> Worthy of all praise.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/031/031_013.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>13.  Wa-i<u>th</u> q<u>a</u>la luqm<u>a</u>nu li<b>i</b>bnihi wahuwa yaAAi<i><u>th</u></i>uhu y<u>a</u> bunayya l<u>a</u> tushrik bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi inna a<b>l</b>shshirka la<i><u>th</u></i>ulmun AAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>13.  Behold, Luqmān said<br> To his son by way of<br>Instruction: "O my son!<br> Join not in worship<br> (Others) with God: for<br> False worship is indeed<br> The highest wrong-doing."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/031/031_014.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>14.  Wawa<u>ss</u>ayn<u>a</u> al-ins<u>a</u>na biw<u>a</u>lidayhi <u>h</u>amalat-hu ommuhu wahnan AAal<u>a</u> wahnin wafi<u>sa</u>luhu fee AA<u>a</u>mayni ani oshkur lee waliw<u>a</u>lidayka ilayya alma<u>s</u>eer<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>14.  And We have enjoined on man<br> (To be good) to his parents:<br> In travail upon travail<br> Did his mother bear him,<br> And in years twain<br> Was his weaning: (hear<br> The command), "Show gratitude<br> To Me and to thy parents:<br> To Me is (thy final) Goal.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/031/031_015.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>15.  Wa-in j<u>a</u>had<u>a</u>ka AAal<u>a</u> an tushrika bee m<u>a</u> laysa laka bihi AAilmun fal<u>a</u> tu<u>t</u>iAAhum<u>a</u> wa<u>sah</u>ibhum<u>a</u> fee a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> maAAroofan wa<b>i</b>ttabiAA sabeela man an<u>a</u>ba ilayya thumma ilayya marjiAAukum faonabbi-okum bim<u>a</u> kuntum taAAmaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>15.  "But if they strive<br> To make thee join<br> In worship with Me<br> Things of which thou hast<br> No knowledge, obey them not;<br> Yet bear them company<br> In this life with justice<br> (And consideration), and follow<br> The way of those who<br> Turn to Me (in love):<br> In the End the return<br> Of you all is to Me,<br> And I will tell you<br> The truth (and meaning)<br> Of all that ye did."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/031/031_016.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>16.  Y<u>a</u> bunayya innah<u>a</u> in taku mithq<u>a</u>la <u>h</u>abbatin min khardalin fatakun fee <u>s</u>akhratin aw fee a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti aw fee al-ar<u>d</u>i ya/ti bih<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hu inna All<u>a</u>ha la<u>t</u>eefun khabeer<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>16.  "O my son!" (said Luqmān),<br> "If there be (but) the weight<br> Of a mustard-seed and<br> It were (hidden) in a rock,'<br> Or (anywhere) in the heavens or<br> On earth, God will bring it<br> Forth: for God understands'<br> The finest mysteries, (and)<br> Is well-acquainted (with them).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/031/031_017.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>17.  Y<u>a</u> bunayya aqimi a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>al<u>a</u>ta wa/mur bi<b>a</b>lmaAAroofi wa<b>i</b>nha AAani almunkari wa<b>i</b><u>s</u>bir AAal<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> a<u>sa</u>baka inna <u>tha</u>lika min AAazmi al-omoor<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>17.  "O my son! establish<br> Regular prayer, enjoin what is<br> Just, and forbid what is wrong:<br> And bear with patient constancy<br> Whate’er betide thee; for this<br> Is firmness (of purpose)<br> In (the conduct of) affairs.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/031/031_018.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>18.  Wal<u>a</u> tu<u>s</u>aAAAAir khaddaka li<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si wal<u>a</u> tamshi fee al-ar<u>d</u>i mara<u>h</u>an inna All<u>a</u>ha l<u>a</u> yu<u>h</u>ibbu kulla mukht<u>a</u>lin fakhoor<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>18.  "And swell not thy cheek<br> (For pride) at men,<br> Nor walk in insolence<br> Through the earth;<br> For God loveth not<br> Any arrogant boaster.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/031/031_019.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>19.  Wa<b>i</b>q<u>s</u>id fee mashyika wa<b>o</b>gh<u>d</u>u<u>d</u> min <u>s</u>awtika inna ankara al-a<u>s</u>w<u>a</u>ti la<u>s</u>awtu al<u>h</u>ameer<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>19.  "And be moderate<br> In thy pace, and lower<br>Thy voice; for the harshest<br> Of sounds without doubt<br> Is the braying of the ass."</p> <div class="filenav">
