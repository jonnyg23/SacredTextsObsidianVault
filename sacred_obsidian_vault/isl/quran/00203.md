 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra II.: Baqara, or the Heifer.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 3</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_021.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>21.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>su oAAbudoo rabbakumu alla<u>th</u>ee khalaqakum wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena min qablikum laAAallakum tattaqoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>21.  O ye people!<br> Adore your Guardian-Lord,<br> Who created you<br> And those who came before you,<br> That ye may have the chance<br> To learn righteousness;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_022.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>22.  Alla<u>th</u>ee jaAAala lakumu al-ar<u>d</u>a fir<u>a</u>shan wa<b>al</b>ssam<u>a</u>a bin<u>a</u>an waanzala mina a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>-i m<u>a</u>an faakhraja bihi mina a<b>l</b>ththamar<u>a</u>ti rizqan lakum fal<u>a</u> tajAAaloo lill<u>a</u>hi and<u>a</u>dan waantum taAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>22.  Who has made the earth your couch,<br> And the heavens your canopy;<br> And sent down rain from the heavens;<br> And brought forth therewith<br> Fruits for your sustenance;<br> Then set not up rivals unto God<br> When ye know (the truth).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_023.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>23.  Wa-in kuntum fee raybin mimm<u>a</u> nazzaln<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> AAabdin<u>a</u> fa/too bisooratin min mithlihi wa<b>o</b>dAAoo shuhad<u>a</u>akum min dooni All<u>a</u>hi in kuntum <u>sa</u>diqeen<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>23.  And if ye are in doubt<br> As to what We have revealed<br> From time to time to Our servant,<br> Then produce a Sūra<br> Like thereunto;<br> And call your witnesses or helpers<br> (If there are any) besides God,<br> If your (doubts) are true.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_024.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>24.  Fa-in lam tafAAaloo walan tafAAaloo fa<b>i</b>ttaqoo a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>ra allatee waqooduh<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>su wa<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>ij<u>a</u>ratu oAAiddat lilk<u>a</u>fireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>24.  But if ye cannot<br> And of a surety ye cannot—<br> Then fear the Fire<br> Whose fuel is Men and Stones,—<br> Which is prepared for those<br>Who reject Faith.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_025.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>25.  Wabashshiri alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo waAAamiloo a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>li<u>ha</u>ti anna lahum jann<u>a</u>tin tajree min ta<u>h</u>tih<u>a</u> al-anh<u>a</u>ru kullam<u>a</u> ruziqoo minh<u>a</u> min thamaratin rizqan q<u>a</u>loo h<u>atha</u> alla<u>th</u>ee ruziqn<u>a</u> min qablu waotoo bihi mutash<u>a</u>bihan walahum feeh<u>a</u> azw<u>a</u>jun mu<u>t</u>ahharatun wahum feeh<u>a</u> kh<u>a</u>lidoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>25.  But give glad tidings<br> To those who believe<br> And work righteousness,<br> That their portion is Gardens,<br> Beneath which rivers flow.<br> Every time they are fed<br> With fruits therefrom,<br> They say: "Why, this is<br> What we were fed with before,"<br> For they are given things in similitude;<br> And they have therein<br> Companions pure (and holy);<br> And they abide therein (for ever).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_026.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>26.  Inna All<u>a</u>ha l<u>a</u> yasta<u>h</u>yee an ya<u>d</u>riba mathalan m<u>a</u> baAAoo<u>d</u>atan fam<u>a</u> fawqah<u>a</u> faamm<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo fayaAAlamoona annahu al<u>h</u>aqqu min rabbihim waamm<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo fayaqooloona m<u>atha</u> ar<u>a</u>da All<u>a</u>hu bih<u>atha</u> mathalan yu<u>d</u>illu bihi katheeran wayahdee bihi katheeran wam<u>a</u> yu<u>d</u>illu bihi ill<u>a</u> alf<u>a</u>siqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>26.  God disdains not to use<br> The similitude of things,<br> Lowest as well as highest.<br>Those who believe know<br> That it is truth from their Lord;<br> But those who reject Faith say:<br> "What means God by this similitude?"<br> By it He causes many to stray,<br> And many He leads into the right path;<br> But He causes not to stray,<br> Except those who forsake (the path),—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_027.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>27.  Alla<u>th</u>eena yanqu<u>d</u>oona AAahda All<u>a</u>hi min baAAdi meeth<u>a</u>qihi wayaq<u>t</u>aAAoona m<u>a</u> amara All<u>a</u>hu bihi an yoo<u>s</u>ala wayufsidoona fee al-ar<u>d</u>i ol<u>a</u>-ika humu alkh<u>a</u>siroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>27.  Those who break God's Covenant<br> After it is ratified,<br> And who sunder what God<br> Has ordered to be joined,<br> And do mischief on earth:<br> These cause loss (only) to themselves.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_028.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>28.  Kayfa takfuroona biAll<u>a</u>hi wakuntum amw<u>a</u>tan faa<u>h</u>y<u>a</u>kum thumma yumeetukum thumma yu<u>h</u>yeekum thumma ilayhi turjaAAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>28.  How can ye reject<br> The faith in God?—<br> Seeing that ye were without life,<br> And He gave you life<br> Then will He cause you to die,<br> And will again bring you to life;<br> And again to Him will ye return.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_029.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>29.  Huwa alla<u>th</u>ee khalaqa lakum m<u>a</u> fee al-ar<u>d</u>i jameeAAan thumma istaw<u>a</u> il<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>-i fasaww<u>a</u>hunna sabAAa sam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>tin wahuwa bikulli shay-in AAaleem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>29.  It is He Who hath created for you<br> All things that are on earth;<br> Moreover His design comprehended the heavens,<br> For He gave order and perfection<br> To the seven firmaments;<br> And of all things<br> He hath perfect knowledge.</p> <div class="filenav">
