 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra CVII.: Mā’ūn, or Neighbourly Needs.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/107/107_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  Araayta alla<u>th</u>ee yuka<u>thth</u>ibu bi<b>al</b>ddeen<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>1.  Seest thou one<br> Who denies the Judgment<br> (To come)?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/107/107_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  Fa<u>tha</u>lika alla<u>th</u>ee yaduAAAAu alyateem<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>2.  Then such is the (man)<br> Who repulses the orphan<br> (With harshness),</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/107/107_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  Wal<u>a</u> ya<u>h</u>u<u>dd</u>u AAal<u>a</u> <u>t</u>aAA<u>a</u>mi almiskeen<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>3.  And encourages not<br> The feeding of the indigent.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/107/107_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  Fawaylun lilmu<u>s</u>alleen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>4.  So woe to the worshippers</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/107/107_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  Alla<u>th</u>eena hum AAan <u>s</u>al<u>a</u>tihim s<u>a</u>hoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>5.  Who are neglectful<br> Of their Prayers,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/107/107_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  Alla<u>th</u>eena hum yur<u>a</u>oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>6.  Those who (want but)<br> To be seen (of men),</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/107/107_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  WayamnaAAoona alm<u>a</u>AAoon<b>a</b>      </p>
 <p>7.  But refuse (to supply)<br> (Even) neighbourly needs.</p>  <div class="filenav">
