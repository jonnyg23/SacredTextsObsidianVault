 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra VI.: An’ām, or Cattle.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 5</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_042.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>42.  Walaqad arsaln<u>a</u> il<u>a</u> omamin min qablika faakha<u>th</u>n<u>a</u>hum bi<b>a</b>lba/s<u>a</u>-i wa<b>al</b><u>dd</u>arr<u>a</u>-i laAAallahum yata<u>d</u>arraAAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>42.  Before thee We sent<br> (Apostles) to many nations,<br> And We afflicted the nations<br> With suffering and adversity,<br> That they might learn humility.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_043.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>43.  Falawl<u>a</u> i<u>th</u> j<u>a</u>ahum ba/sun<u>a</u> ta<u>d</u>arraAAoo wal<u>a</u>kin qasat quloobuhum wazayyana lahumu a<b>l</b>shshay<u>ta</u>nu m<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo yaAAmaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>43.  When the suffering reached<br> Them from Us, why then<br> Did they not learn humility?<br> On the contrary their hearts<br> Became hardened, and Satan<br> Made their (sinful) acts<br> Seem alluring to them.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_044.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>44.  Falamm<u>a</u> nasoo m<u>a</u> <u>th</u>ukkiroo bihi fata<u>h</u>n<u>a</u> AAalayhim abw<u>a</u>ba kulli shay-in <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> i<u>tha</u> fari<u>h</u>oo bim<u>a</u> ootoo akha<u>th</u>n<u>a</u>hum baghtatan fa-i<u>tha</u> hum mublisoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>44.  But when they forgot<br> The warning they had received,<br>We opened to them the gates<br> Of all (good) things,<br> Until, in the midst<br> Of their enjoyment<br> Of our gifts,<br> On a sudden, We called<br> Them to account, when lo!<br> They were plunged in despair!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_045.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>45.  Faqu<u>t</u>iAAa d<u>a</u>biru alqawmi alla<u>th</u>eena <i><u>th</u></i>alamoo wa<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>amdu lill<u>a</u>hi rabbi alAA<u>a</u>lameen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>45.  Of the wrong-doers the last<br> Remnant was cut off.<br> Praise be to God,<br> The Cherisher of the Worlds.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_046.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>46.  Qul araaytum in akha<u>th</u>a All<u>a</u>hu samAAakum waab<u>sa</u>rakum wakhatama AAal<u>a</u> quloobikum man il<u>a</u>hun ghayru All<u>a</u>hi ya/teekum bihi on<i><u>th</u></i>ur kayfa nu<u>s</u>arrifu al-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>ti thumma hum ya<u>s</u>difoon<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>46.  Say:"Think ye, if God<br> Took away your hearing<br> And your sight, and sealed up<br> Your hearts, who—a god<br> Other than God—could<br> Restore them to you?"<br> See how We explain<br> The Signs by various (symbols);<br> Yet they turn aside.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_047.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>47.  Qul araaytakum in at<u>a</u>kum AAa<u>tha</u>bu All<u>a</u>hi baghtatan aw jahratan hal yuhlaku ill<u>a</u> alqawmu a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limoon<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>47.  Say:"Think ye, if<br> The Punishment of God<br> Comes to you,<br> Whether suddenly or openly,<br> Will any be destroyed<br> Except those who do wrong?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_048.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>48.  Wam<u>a</u> nursilu almursaleena ill<u>a</u> mubashshireena wamun<u>th</u>ireena faman <u>a</u>mana waa<u>s</u>la<u>h</u>a fal<u>a</u> khawfun AAalayhim wal<u>a</u> hum ya<u>h</u>zanoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>48.  We send the apostles<br> Only to give good news<br>And to warn: so those<br> Who believe and mend<br> (Their lives),—upon them<br> Shall be no fear,<br> Nor shall they grieve.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_049.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>49.  Wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena ka<u>thth</u>aboo bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin<u>a</u> yamassuhumu alAAa<u>tha</u>bu bim<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo yafsuqoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>49.  But those who reject<br> Our Signs,—them<br> Shall punishment touch,<br> For that they ceased not<br> From transgressing.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_050.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>50.  Qul l<u>a</u> aqoolu lakum AAindee khaz<u>a</u>-inu All<u>a</u>hi wal<u>a</u> aAAlamu alghayba wal<u>a</u> aqoolu lakum innee malakun in attabiAAu ill<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> yoo<u>ha</u> ilayya qul hal yastawee al-aAAm<u>a</u> wa<b>a</b>lba<u>s</u>eeru afal<u>a</u> tatafakkaroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>50.  Say:"I tell you not<br> That with me<br> Are the Treasures of God,<br> Nor do I know<br> What is hidden,<br> Nor do I tell you I am<br> An angel. I but follow<br> What is revealed to me."<br> Say:"Can the blind<br> Be held equal to the seeing?"<br> Will ye then consider not?</p> <div class="filenav">
