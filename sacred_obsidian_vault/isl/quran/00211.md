 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra II.: Baqara, or the Heifer.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 11</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_087.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>87.  Walaqad <u>a</u>tayn<u>a</u> moos<u>a</u> alkit<u>a</u>ba waqaffayn<u>a</u> min baAAdihi bi<b>al</b>rrusuli wa<u>a</u>tayn<u>a</u> AAees<u>a</u> ibna maryama albayyin<u>a</u>ti waayyadn<u>a</u>hu biroo<u>h</u>i alqudusi afakullam<u>a</u> j<u>a</u>akum rasoolun bim<u>a</u> l<u>a</u> tahw<u>a</u> anfusukumu istakbartum fafareeqan ka<u>thth</u>abtum wafareeqan taqtuloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>87.  We gave Moses the Book<br> And followed him up<br> With a succession of Apostles;<br> We gave Jesus the son of Mary<br> Clear (Signs) and strengthened him<br> With the holy spirit. Is it<br> That whenever there comes to you<br> An Apostle with what ye<br> Yourselves desire not, ye are<br> Puffed up with pride?—<br> Some ye called impostors,<br> And others ye slay!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_088.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>88.  Waq<u>a</u>loo quloobun<u>a</u> ghulfun bal laAAanahumu All<u>a</u>hu bikufrihim faqaleelan m<u>a</u> yu/minoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>88.  They say, "Our hearts<br> Are the wrappings (which preserve<br> God's Word: we need no more)."<br> Nay, God's curse is on them<br> For their blasphemy:<br> Little is it they believe.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_089.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>89.  Walamm<u>a</u> j<u>a</u>ahum kit<u>a</u>bun min AAindi All<u>a</u>hi mu<u>s</u>addiqun lim<u>a</u> maAAahum wak<u>a</u>noo min qablu yastafti<u>h</u>oona AAal<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo falamm<u>a</u> j<u>a</u>ahum m<u>a</u> AAarafoo kafaroo bihi falaAAnatu All<u>a</u>hi AAal<u>a</u> alk<u>a</u>fireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>89.  And when there comes to them<br> A Book from God, confirming<br> What is with them,—although<br> From of old they had prayed<br> For victory against those<br> Without Faith,—when there comes.<br> To them that which they<br> (Should) have recognized,<br> They refuse to believe in it<br> But the curse of God<br> Is on those without Faith.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_090.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>90.  Bi/sam<u>a</u> ishtaraw bihi anfusahum an yakfuroo bim<u>a</u> anzala All<u>a</u>hu baghyan an yunazzila All<u>a</u>hu min fa<u>d</u>lihi AAal<u>a</u> man yash<u>a</u>o min AAib<u>a</u>dihi fab<u>a</u>oo bigha<u>d</u>abin AAal<u>a</u> gha<u>d</u>abin walilk<u>a</u>fireena AAa<u>tha</u>bun muheen<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>90.  Miserable is the price<br> For which they have sold<br> Their souls, in that they<br> Deny (the revelation)<br> Which God has sent down,<br> In insolent envy that God<br> Of His Grace should send it<br> To any of His servants He pleases:<br>Thus have they drawn<br> On themselves Wrath upon Wrath.<br> And humiliating is the punishment<br> Of those who reject Faith.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_091.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>91.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> qeela lahum <u>a</u>minoo bim<u>a</u> anzala All<u>a</u>hu q<u>a</u>loo nu/minu bim<u>a</u> onzila AAalayn<u>a</u> wayakfuroona bim<u>a</u> war<u>a</u>ahu wahuwa al<u>h</u>aqqu mu<u>s</u>addiqan lim<u>a</u> maAAahum qul falima taqtuloona anbiy<u>a</u>a All<u>a</u>hi min qablu in kuntum mu/mineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>91.  When it is said to them,<br> "Believe in what God<br> Hath sent down," they say,<br> "We believe in what was sent down<br> To us": yet they reject<br> All besides, even if it be Truth<br> Confirming what is with them.<br> Say: "Why then have ye slain<br> The prophets of God in times<br> Gone by, if ye did indeed<br> Believe?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_092.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>92.  Walaqad j<u>a</u>akum moos<u>a</u> bi<b>a</b>lbayyin<u>a</u>ti thumma ittakha<u>th</u>tumu alAAijla min baAAdihi waantum <i><u>th</u></i><u>a</u>limoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>92.  There came to you Moses<br> With clear (Signs); yet<br> Ye worshipped the Calf<br> (Even) after that, and ye<br> Did behave wrongfully.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_093.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>93.  Wa-i<u>th</u> akha<u>th</u>n<u>a</u> meeth<u>a</u>qakum warafaAAn<u>a</u> fawqakumu a<b>l</b><u>tt</u>oora khu<u>th</u>oo m<u>a</u> <u>a</u>tayn<u>a</u>kum biquwwatin wa<b>i</b>smaAAoo q<u>a</u>loo samiAAn<u>a</u> waAAa<u>s</u>ayn<u>a</u> waoshriboo fee quloobihimu alAAijla bikufrihim qul bi/sam<u>a</u> ya/murukum bihi eem<u>a</u>nukum in kuntum mu/mineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>93.  And remember We took<br> Your Covenant and We raised<br> Above you (the towering height)<br> Of Mount (Sinai):<br> (Saying): "Hold firmly<br> To what We have given you,<br> And hearken (to the Law)"<br> They said: "We hear,<br> And we disobey"<br> And they had to drink<br>Into their hearts<br> (Of the taint) of the Calf<br> Because of their Faithlessness.<br> Say: "Vile indeed<br> Are the behests of your Faith<br> If ye have any faith!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_094.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>94.  Qul in k<u>a</u>nat lakumu a<b>l</b>dd<u>a</u>ru al-<u>a</u>khiratu AAinda All<u>a</u>hi kh<u>a</u>li<u>s</u>atan min dooni a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si fatamannawoo almawta in kuntum <u>sa</u>diqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>94.  Say: "If the last Home,<br> With God, be for you specially,<br> And not for anyone else,<br> Then seek ye for death,<br> If ye are sincere."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_095.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>95.  Walan yatamannawhu abadan bim<u>a</u> qaddamat aydeehim wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu AAaleemun bia<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>95.  But they will never seek<br> For death, on account of the (sins)<br> Which their hands have sent<br> On before them.<br> And God is well-acquainted<br> With the wrong-doers.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_096.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>96.  Walatajidannahum a<u>h</u>ra<u>s</u>a a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si AAal<u>a</u> <u>h</u>ay<u>a</u>tin wamina alla<u>th</u>eena ashrakoo yawaddu a<u>h</u>aduhum law yuAAammaru alfa sanatin wam<u>a</u> huwa bimuza<u>h</u>zi<u>h</u>ihi mina alAAa<u>tha</u>bi an yuAAammara wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu ba<u>s</u>eerun bim<u>a</u> yaAAmaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>96.  Thou wilt indeed find them,<br> Of all people, most greedy<br> Of life,—even more<br> Than the idolaters:<br> Each one of them wishes<br> He could be given a life<br> Of a thousand years:<br> But the grant of such life<br> Will not save him<br> From (due) punishment.<br> For God sees well<br> All that they do.</p> <div class="filenav">
