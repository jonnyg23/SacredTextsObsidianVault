 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra V.: Māïda, or The Table Spread.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 11</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_078.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>78.  LuAAina alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo min banee isr<u>a</u>-eela AAal<u>a</u> lis<u>a</u>ni d<u>a</u>wooda waAAees<u>a</u> ibni maryama <u>tha</u>lika bim<u>a</u> AAa<u>s</u>aw wak<u>a</u>noo yaAAtadoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>78.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">81</font></span> Curses were pronounced<br> On those among the Children<br> Of Israel who rejected Faith,<br> By the tongue of David<br> And of Jesus the son of Mary:<br> Because they disobeyed<br> And persisted in Excesses.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_079.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>79.  K<u>a</u>noo l<u>a</u> yatan<u>a</u>hawna AAan munkarin faAAaloohu labi/sa m<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo yafAAaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>79.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">82</font></span> Nor did they (usually)<br> Forbid one another<br> The iniquities which they<br> Committed: evil indeed<br> Were the deeds which they did.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_080.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>80.  Tar<u>a</u> katheeran minhum yatawallawna alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo labi/sa m<u>a </u>qaddamat lahum anfusuhum an sakhi<u>t</u>a All<u>a</u>hu AAalayhim wafee alAAa<u>tha</u>bi hum kh<u>a</u>lidoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>80.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">83</font></span> Thou seest many of them<br> Turning in friendship<br> To the Unbelievers.<br> Evil indeed are (the works) which<br> Their souls have sent forward<br> Before them (with the result),<br> That God's wrath<br> Is on them,<br> And in torment<br> Will they abide.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_081.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>81.  Walaw k<u>a</u>noo yu/minoona bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi wa<b>al</b>nnabiyyi wam<u>a</u> onzila ilayhi m<u>a</u> ittakha<u>th</u>oohum awliy<u>a</u>a wal<u>a</u>kinna katheeran minhum f<u>a</u>siqoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>81.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">84</font></span> If only they had believed<br> In God, in the Apostle,<br> And in what hath been<br> Revealed to him, never<br> Would they have taken<br> Them for friends and protectors,<br> But most of them are<br> Rebellious wrong-doers.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_082.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>82.  Latajidanna ashadda a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si AAad<u>a</u>watan lilla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo alyahooda wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena ashrakoo walatajidanna aqrabahum mawaddatan lilla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo alla<u>th</u>eena q<u>a</u>loo inn<u>a</u> na<u>sa</u>r<u>a</u> <u>tha</u>lika bi-anna minhum qisseeseena waruhb<u>a</u>nan waannahum l<u>a</u> yastakbiroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>82.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">85</font></span> Strongest among men in enmity<br> To the Believers wilt thou<br> Find the Jews and Pagans;<br> And nearest among them in love<br> To the Believers wilt thou<br> Find those who say,<br> "We are Christians":<br> Because amongst these are<br> Men devoted to learning<br> And men who have renounced<br> The world, and they<br> Are not arrogant.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_083.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>83.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> samiAAoo m<u>a</u> onzila il<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>rrasooli tar<u>a</u> aAAyunahum tafee<u>d</u>u mina a<b>l</b>ddamAAi mimm<u>a</u> AAarafoo mina al<u>h</u>aqqi yaqooloona rabban<u>a</u> <u>a</u>mann<u>a</u> fa<b>o</b>ktubn<u>a</u> maAAa a<b>l</b>shsh<u>a</u>hideen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>83.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">86</font></span> And when they listen<br> To the revelation received<br> By the Apostle, thou wilt<br> See their eyes overflowing<br> With tears, for they<br> Recognise the truth:<br> They pray: "Our Lord!<br> We believe; write us<br> Down among the witnesses.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_084.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>84.  Wam<u>a</u> lan<u>a</u> l<u>a</u> nu/minu bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi wam<u>a</u> j<u>a</u>an<u>a</u> mina al<u>h</u>aqqi wana<u>t</u>maAAu an yudkhilan<u>a</u> rabbun<u>a</u> maAAa alqawmi a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>li<u>h</u>een<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>84.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">87</font></span> "What cause can we have<br> Not to believe in God<br> And the truth which has<br> Come to us, seeing that<br> We long for our Lord<br> To admit us to the company<br> Of the righteous?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_085.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>85.  Faath<u>a</u>bahumu All<u>a</u>hu bim<u>a</u> q<u>a</u>loo jann<u>a</u>tin tajree min ta<u>h</u>tih<u>a</u> al-anh<u>a</u>ru kh<u>a</u>lideena feeh<u>a</u> wa<u>tha</u>lika jaz<u>a</u>o almu<u>h</u>sineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>85.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">88</font></span> And for this their prayer<br> Hath God rewarded them<br> With Gardens, with rivers<br> Flowing underneath,—their eternal<br> Home. Such is the recompense<br> Of those who do good.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_086.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>86.  Wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo waka<u>thth</u>aboo bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin<u>a</u> ol<u>a</u>-ika a<u>s</u>-<u>ha</u>bu alja<u>h</u>eem<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>86.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">89</font></span> But those who reject Faith<br> And belie Our Signs,—<br> They shall be Companions<br> Of Hell-fire.</p> <div class="filenav">
