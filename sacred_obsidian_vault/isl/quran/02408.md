 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/024/024_058.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>58.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo liyasta/<u>th</u>inkumu alla<u>th</u>eena malakat aym<u>a</u>nukum wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena lam yablughoo al<u>h</u>uluma minkum thal<u>a</u>tha marr<u>a</u>tin min qabli <u>s</u>al<u>a</u>ti alfajri wa<u>h</u>eena ta<u>d</u>aAAoona thiy<u>a</u>bakum mina a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i>aheerati wamin baAAdi <u>s</u>al<u>a</u>ti alAAish<u>a</u>-i thal<u>a</u>thu AAawr<u>a</u>tin lakum laysa AAalaykum wal<u>a</u> AAalayhim jun<u>ah</u>un baAAdahunna <u>t</u>aww<u>a</u>foona AAalaykum baAA<u>d</u>ukum AAal<u>a</u> baAA<u>d</u>in ka<u>tha</u>lika yubayyinu All<u>a</u>hu lakumu al-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>ti wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu AAaleemun <u>h</u>akeem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>58.  O ye who believe!<br> Let those whom your right hands<br> Possess, and the (children) among you<br>Who have not come of age<br> Ask your permission (before<br> They come to your presence),<br> On three occasions: before<br> Morning prayer; the while<br> Ye doff your clothes<br> For the noonday heat;<br> And after the late-night prayer:<br> These are your three times<br> Of undress: outside those times<br> It is not wrong for you<br> Or for them to move about<br> Attending to each other:<br> Thus does God make clear<br> The Signs to you: for God<br> Is full of knowledge and wisdom.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/024/024_059.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>59.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> balagha al-a<u>t</u>f<u>a</u>lu minkumu al<u>h</u>uluma falyasta/<u>th</u>inoo kam<u>a</u> ista/<u>th</u>ana alla<u>th</u>eena min qablihim ka<u>tha</u>lika yubayyinu All<u>a</u>hu lakum <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tihi wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu AAaleemun <u>h</u>akeem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>59.  But when the children among you<br> Come of age, let them (also)<br> Ask for permission, as do those<br> Senior to them (in age):<br> Thus does God make clear<br> His Signs to you: for God<br> Is full of knowledge and wisdom.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/024/024_060.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>60.  Wa<b>a</b>lqaw<u>a</u>AAidu mina a<b>l</b>nnis<u>a</u>-i all<u>a</u>tee l<u>a</u> yarjoona nik<u>ah</u>an falaysa AAalayhinna jun<u>ah</u>un an ya<u>d</u>aAAna thiy<u>a</u>bahunna ghayra mutabarrij<u>a</u>tin bizeenatin waan yastaAAfifna khayrun lahunna wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu sameeAAun AAaleem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>60.  Such elderly women as are<br> Past the prospect of marriage,—<br> There is no blame on them<br> If they lay aside<br>Their (outer) garments, provided<br> They make not a wanton display<br> Of their beauty: but<br> It is best for them<br> To be modest: and God<br> Is One Who sees and knows<br> All things.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/024/024_061.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>61.  Laysa AAal<u>a</u> al-aAAm<u>a</u> <u>h</u>arajun wal<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> al-aAAraji <u>h</u>arajun wal<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> almaree<u>d</u>i <u>h</u>arajun wal<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> anfusikum an ta/kuloo min buyootikum aw buyooti <u>a</u>b<u>a</u>-ikum aw buyooti ommah<u>a</u>tikum aw buyooti ikhw<u>a</u>nikum aw buyooti akhaw<u>a</u>tikum aw buyooti aAAm<u>a</u>mikum aw buyooti AAamm<u>a</u>tikum aw buyooti akhw<u>a</u>likum aw buyooti kh<u>a</u>l<u>a</u>tikum aw m<u>a</u> malaktum maf<u>a</u>ti<u>h</u>ahu aw <u>s</u>adeeqikum laysa AAalaykum jun<u>ah</u>un an ta/kuloo jameeAAan aw asht<u>a</u>tan fa-i<u>tha</u> dakhaltum buyootan fasallimoo AAal<u>a</u> anfusikum ta<u>h</u>iyyatan min AAindi All<u>a</u>hi mub<u>a</u>rakatan <u>t</u>ayyibatan ka<u>tha</u>lika yubayyinu All<u>a</u>hu lakumu al-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>ti laAAallakum taAAqiloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>61.  It is no fault in the blind<br> Nor in one born lame, nor<br> In one afflicted with illness,<br> Nor in yourselves, that ye<br> Should eat in your own houses,<br> Or those of your fathers,<br> Or your mothers, or your brothers,<br> Or your sisters, or your father's brothers<br> Or your father's sisters,<br> Or your mother's brothers,<br> Or your mother's sisters,<br> Or in houses of which<br> The keys are in your possession,<br> Or in the house of a sincere<br> Friend of yours: there is<br> No blame on you, whether<br> Ye eat in company or<br> Separately. But if ye<br> Enter houses, salute each other—<br> A greeting of blessing<br> And purity as from God.<br>Thus does God make clear<br> The Signs to you: that ye<br> May understand.</p> <div class="filenav">
