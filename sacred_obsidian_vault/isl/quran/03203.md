 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXXII: Sajda, or Adoration.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 3</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/032/032_023.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>23.  Walaqad <u>a</u>tayn<u>a</u> moos<u>a</u> alkit<u>a</u>ba fal<u>a</u> takun fee miryatin min liq<u>a</u>-ihi wajaAAaln<u>a</u>hu hudan libanee isr<u>a</u>-eel<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>23.  We did indeed aforetime<br> Give the Book to Moses:<br>Be not then in doubt<br> Of its reaching (thee):<br> And We made it<br> A guide to the Children<br> Of Israel.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/032/032_024.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>24.  WajaAAaln<u>a</u> minhum a-immatan yahdoona bi-amrin<u>a</u> lamm<u>a</u> <u>s</u>abaroo wak<u>a</u>noo bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin<u>a</u> yooqinoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>24.  And We appointed, from among<br> Them, Leaders, giving guidance<br> Under Our command, so long<br> As they persevered with patience<br> And continued to have faith<br> In Our Signs.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/032/032_025.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>25.  Inna rabbaka huwa yaf<u>s</u>ilu baynahum yawma alqiy<u>a</u>mati feem<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo feehi yakhtalifoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>25.  Verily thy Lord will judge<br> Between them on the Day<br> Of Judgment, in the matters<br> Wherein they differ<br> (among themselves)</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/032/032_026.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>26.  Awa lam yahdi lahum kam ahlakn<u>a</u> min qablihim mina alqurooni yamshoona fee mas<u>a</u>kinihim inna fee <u>tha</u>lika la<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin afal<u>a</u> yasmaAAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>26.  Does it not teach them<br> A lesson, how many generations<br> We destroyed before them,<br> In whose dwellings they<br> (Now) go to and fro?<br> Verily in that are Signs:<br> Do they not then listen?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/032/032_027.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>27.  Awa lam yaraw ann<u>a</u> nasooqu alm<u>a</u>a il<u>a</u> al-ar<u>d</u>i aljuruzi fanukhriju bihi zarAAan ta-kulu minhu anAA<u>a</u>muhum waanfusuhum afal<u>a</u> yub<u>s</u>iroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>27.  And do they not see<br> That We do drive Rain<br> To parched soil (bare<br> Of herbage), and produce therewith<br> Crops, providing food<br> For their cattle and themselves?<br> Have they not the vision?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/032/032_028.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>28.  Wayaqooloona mat<u>a</u> h<u>atha</u> alfat<u>h</u>u in kuntum <u>sa</u>diqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>28.  They say: "When will<br> This Decision be, if ye<br> Are telling the truth?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/032/032_029.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>29.  Qul yawma alfat<u>h</u>i l<u>a</u> yanfaAAu alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo eem<u>a</u>nuhum wal<u>a</u> hum yun<i><u>th</u></i>aroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>29.  Say: "On the Day<br> Of Decision, no profit<br> Will it he to Unbelievers<br> If they (then) believe!<br> Nor Will they he granted<br> A respite."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/032/032_030.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>30.  FaaAAri<u>d</u> AAanhum wa<b>i</b>nta<i><u>th</u></i>ir innahum munta<i><u>th</u></i>iroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>30.  So turn away from them,<br> And wait: they too<br> Are waiting.</p>  <div class="filenav">
