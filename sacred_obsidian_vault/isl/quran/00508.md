 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra V.: Māïda, or The Table Spread.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 8</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_051.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>51.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo l<u>a</u> tattakhi<u>th</u>oo alyahooda wa<b>al</b>nna<u>sa</u>r<u>a</u> awliy<u>a</u>a baAA<u>d</u>uhum awliy<u>a</u>o baAA<u>d</u>in waman yatawallahum minkum fa-innahu minhum inna All<u>a</u>ha l<u>a</u> yahdee alqawma a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>51.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">54</font></span> O ye who believe!<br> Take not the Jews<br> And the Christians<br> For your friends and protectors:<br> They are but friends and protectors)<br> To each other. And he<br>Amongst you that turns to them<br> (For friendship) is of them.<br> Verily God guideth not<br> A people unjust.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_052.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>52.  Fatar<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena fee quloobihim mara<u>d</u>un yus<u>a</u>riAAoona feehim yaqooloona nakhsh<u>a</u> an tu<u>s</u>eeban<u>a</u> d<u>a</u>-iratun faAAas<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hu an ya/tiya bi<b>a</b>lfat<u>h</u>i aw amrin min AAindihi fayu<u>s</u>bi<u>h</u>oo AAal<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> asarroo fee anfusihim n<u>a</u>dimeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>52.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">55</font></span> Those in whose hearts<br> Is a disease—thou seest<br> How eagerly they run about<br> Amongst them, saying:<br> "We do fear lest a change<br> Of fortune bring us disaster."<br> Ah! perhaps God will give<br> (Thee) victory, or a decision<br> According to His Will.<br> Then will they repent<br> Of the thoughts which they secretly<br> Harboured in their hearts.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_053.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>53.  Wayaqoolu alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo ah<u>a</u>ol<u>a</u>-i alla<u>th</u>eena aqsamoo bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi jahda aym<u>a</u>nihim innahum lamaAAakum <u>h</u>abi<u>t</u>at aAAm<u>a</u>luhum faa<u>s</u>ba<u>h</u>oo kh<u>a</u>sireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>53.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">56</font></span> And those who believe<br> Will say: "Are these<br> The men who swore<br> Their strongest oaths by God,<br> That they were with you?"<br> All that they do<br> Will be in vain,<br> And they will fall<br> Into (nothing but) ruin.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_054.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>54.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo man yartadda minkum AAan deenihi fasawfa ya/tee All<u>a</u>hu biqawmin yu<u>h</u>ibbuhum wayu<u>h</u>ibboonahu a<u>th</u>illatin AAal<u>a</u> almu/mineena aAAizzatin AAal<u>a</u> alk<u>a</u>fireena yuj<u>a</u>hidoona fee sabeeli All<u>a</u>hi wal<u>a</u> yakh<u>a</u>foona lawmata l<u>a</u>-imin <u>tha</u>lika fa<u>d</u>lu All<u>a</u>hi yu/teehi man yash<u>a</u>o wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu w<u>a</u>siAAun AAaleem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>54.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">57</font></span> O ye who believe!<br> If any from among you<br> Turn back from his Faith,<br> Soon will God produce<br> A people whom He will love<br> As they will love Him,—<br> Lowly with the Believers,<br> Mighty against the Rejecters,<br> Fighting in the Way of God,<br> And never afraid<br> Of the reproaches<br>Of such as find fault.<br> That is the Grace of God,<br> Which He will bestow<br> On whom He pleaseth.<br> And God encompasseth all,<br> And He knoweth all things.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_055.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>55.  Innam<u>a</u> waliyyukumu All<u>a</u>hu warasooluhu wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo alla<u>th</u>eena yuqeemoona a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>al<u>a</u>ta wayu/toona a<b>l</b>zzak<u>a</u>ta wahum r<u>a</u>kiAAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>55.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">58</font></span> Your (real) friends are<br> (No less than) God,<br> His Apostle, and the (Fellowship<br> Of) Believers,—those who<br> Establish regular prayers<br> And regular charity,<br> And they bow<br> Down humbly (in worship).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_056.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>56.  Waman yatawalla All<u>a</u>ha warasoolahu wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo fa-inna <u>h</u>izba All<u>a</u>hi humu algh<u>a</u>liboon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>56.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">59</font></span> As to those who turn<br> (For friendship) to God,<br> His Apostle, and the (Fellowship<br> Of) Believers,—it is<br> The Fellowship of God<br> That must certainly triumph.</p> <div class="filenav">
