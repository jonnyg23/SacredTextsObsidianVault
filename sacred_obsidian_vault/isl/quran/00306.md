 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_055.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>55.  I<u>th</u> q<u>a</u>la All<u>a</u>hu y<u>a</u> AAees<u>a</u> innee mutawaffeeka war<u>a</u>fiAAuka ilayya wamu<u>t</u>ahhiruka mina alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo waj<u>a</u>AAilu alla<u>th</u>eena ittabaAAooka fawqa alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo il<u>a</u> yawmi alqiy<u>a</u>mati thumma ilayya marjiAAukum faa<u>h</u>kumu baynakum feem<u>a</u> kuntum feehi takhtalifoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>55.  Behold! God said:<br> "O Jesus! I will take thee<br> And raise thee to Myself<br> And clear thee (of the falsehoods)<br> Of those who blaspheme;<br> I will make those<br> Who follow thee superior<br> To those who reject faith,<br> To the Day of Resurrection:<br> Then shall ye all<br> Return unto me,<br> And I will judge<br> Between you of the matters<br> Wherein ye dispute.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_056.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>56.  Faamm<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo faoAAa<u>thth</u>ibuhum AAa<u>tha</u>ban shadeedan fee a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> wa<b>a</b>l-<u>a</u>khirati wam<u>a</u> lahum min n<u>as</u>ireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>56.  "As to those who reject faith,<br> I will punish them<br> With terrible agony<br> In this world and in the Hereafter,<br> Nor will they have<br> Anyone to help.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_057.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>57.  Waamm<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo waAAamiloo a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>li<u>ha</u>ti fayuwaffeehim ojoorahum wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu l<u>a</u> yu<u>h</u>ibbu a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>57.  "As to those who believe<br> And work righteousness,<br> God will pay them (in full)<br> Their reward;<br> But God loveth not<br> Those who do wrong.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_058.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>58.  <u>Tha</u>lika natloohu AAalayka mina al-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>ti wa<b>al</b><u>thth</u>ikri al<u>h</u>akeem<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>58.  "This is what we rehearse<br> Unto thee of the Signs<br> And the Message<br> Of Wisdom."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_059.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>59.  Inna mathala AAees<u>a</u> AAinda All<u>a</u>hi kamathali <u>a</u>dama khalaqahu min tur<u>a</u>bin thumma q<u>a</u>la lahu kun fayakoon<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>59.  The similitude of Jesus<br> Before God is as that of Adam;<br> He created him from dust,<br> Then said to him, "Be":<br> And he was.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_060.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>60.  Al<u>h</u>aqqu min rabbika fal<u>a</u> takun mina almumtareen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>60.  The Truth (comes)<br> From God alone;<br> So be not of those<br> Who doubt.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_061.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>61.  Faman <u>ha</u>jjaka feehi min baAAdi m<u>a</u> j<u>a</u>aka mina alAAilmi faqul taAA<u>a</u>law nadAAu abn<u>a</u>an<u>a</u> waabn<u>a</u>akum wanis<u>a</u>an<u>a</u> wanis<u>a</u>akum waanfusan<u>a</u> waanfusakum thumma nabtahil fanajAAal laAAnata All<u>a</u>hi AAal<u>a</u> alk<u>ath</u>ibeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>61.  If any one disputes<br> In this matter with thee,<br> Now after (full) knowledge<br> Hath come to thee,<br> Say: "Come! let us<br> Gather together,<br> Our sons and your sons,<br> Our women and your women,<br> Ourselves and yourselves:<br> Then let us earnestly pray,<br> And invoke the curse<br> Of God on those who lie!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_062.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>62.  Inna h<u>atha</u> lahuwa alqa<u>s</u>a<u>s</u>u al<u>h</u>aqqu wam<u>a</u> min il<u>a</u>hin ill<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hu wa-inna All<u>a</u>ha lahuwa alAAazeezu al<u>h</u>akeem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>62.  This is the true account:<br> There is no god<br> Except God;<br> And God—He is indeed<br>The Exalted in Power,<br> The Wise.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_063.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>63.  Fa-in tawallaw fa-inna All<u>a</u>ha AAaleemun bi<b>a</b>lmufsideen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>63.  But if they turn back,<br> God hath full knowledge<br> Of those who do mischief.</p> <div class="filenav">
