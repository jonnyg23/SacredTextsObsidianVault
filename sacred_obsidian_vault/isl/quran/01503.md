 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XV.: Al-Hijr, or The Rocky Tract.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 3</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_026.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>26.  Walaqad khalaqn<u>a</u> al-ins<u>a</u>na min <u>s</u>al<u>sa</u>lin min <u>h</u>ama-in masnoon<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>26.  We created man from sounding clay,<br> From mud moulded into shape;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_027.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>27.  Wa<b>a</b>lj<u>a</u>nna khalaqn<u>a</u>hu min qablu min n<u>a</u>ri a<b>l</b>ssamoom<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>27.  And the Jinn race, We had<br> Created before, from the fire<br> Of a scorching wind.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_028.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>28.  Wa-i<u>th</u> q<u>a</u>la rabbuka lilmal<u>a</u>-ikati innee kh<u>a</u>liqun basharan min <u>s</u>al<u>sa</u>lin min <u>h</u>ama-in masnoon<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>28.  Behold! thy Lord said<br> To the angels: "I am about<br> To create man, from sounding clay<br> From mud moulded into shape;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_029.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>29.  Fa-i<u>tha</u> sawwaytuhu wanafakhtu feehi min roo<u>h</u>ee faqaAAoo lahu s<u>a</u>jideen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>29.  "When I have fashioned him<br> (In due proportion) and breathed<br> Into him of My spirit,<br> Fall ye down in obeisance<br> Unto him.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_030.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>30.  Fasajada almal<u>a</u>-ikatu kulluhum ajmaAAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>30.  So the angels prostrated themselves,<br> All of them together:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_031.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>31.  Ill<u>a</u> ibleesa ab<u>a</u> an yakoona maAAa a<b>l</b>ss<u>a</u>jideen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>31.  Not so Iblīs: he refused to be<br> Among those who prostrated themselves.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_032.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>32.  Q<u>a</u>la y<u>a</u> ibleesu m<u>a</u> laka all<u>a</u> takoona maAAa a<b>l</b>ss<u>a</u>jideen<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>32.  (God) said: "O Iblīs!<br> What is your reason<br> For not being among those<br> Who prostrated themselves?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_033.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>33.  Q<u>a</u>la lam akun li-asjuda libasharin khalaqtahu min <u>s</u>al<u>sa</u>lin min <u>h</u>ama-in masnoon<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>33.  (Iblīs) said: "I am not one<br> To prostrate myself to man,<br> Whom Thou didst create<br> From sounding clay, from mud<br> Moulded into shape."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_034.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>34.  Q<u>a</u>la fa<b>o</b>khruj minh<u>a</u> fa-innaka rajeem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>34.  (God) said: "Then get thee out<br> From here; for thou art<br> Rejected, accursed.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_035.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>35.  Wa-inna AAalayka allaAAnata il<u>a</u> yawmi a<b>l</b>ddeen<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>35.  "And the Curse shall be<br> On thee till the Day of Judgment."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_036.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>36.  Q<u>a</u>la rabbi faan<i><u>th</u></i>irnee il<u>a</u> yawmi yubAAathoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>36.  (Iblīs) said: "O my Lord!<br> Give me then respite<br> Till the Day<br> The (dead) are raised."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_037.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>37.  Q<u>a</u>la fa-innaka mina almun<i><u>th</u></i>areen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>37.  (God) said: "Respite<br> Is granted thee—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_038.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>38.  Il<u>a</u> yawmi alwaqti almaAAloom<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>38.  "Till the Day<br> Of the Time Appointed."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_039.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>39.  Q<u>a</u>la rabbi bim<u>a</u> aghwaytanee laozayyinanna lahum fee al-ar<u>d</u>i walaoghwiyannahum ajmaAAeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>39.  (Iblīs) said: "O my Lord!<br> Because Thou hast put me<br> In the wrong, I will<br> Make (wrong) fair-seeming<br> To them on the earth,<br> And I will put them<br> All in the wrong,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_040.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>40.  Ill<u>a</u> AAib<u>a</u>daka minhumu almukhla<u>s</u>een<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>40.  "Except Thy servants among them,<br> Sincere and purified<br> (By Thy grace)."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_041.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>41.  Q<u>a</u>la h<u>atha</u> <u>s</u>ir<u>at</u>un AAalayya mustaqeem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>41.  (God) said: "This (Way<br> Of My sincere servants) is<br> Indeed a Way that leads<br> Straight to Me.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_042.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>42.  Inna AAib<u>a</u>dee laysa laka AAalayhim sul<u>ta</u>nun ill<u>a</u> mani ittabaAAaka mina algh<u>a</u>ween<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>42.  "For over My servants<br> No authority shalt thou<br> Have, except such as<br> Put themselves in the wrong<br> And follow thee."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_043.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>43.  Wa-inna jahannama lamawAAiduhum ajmaAAeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>43.  And verily, Hell<br> Is the promised abode<br> For them all!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/015/015_044.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>44.  Lah<u>a</u> sabAAatu abw<u>a</u>bin likulli b<u>a</u>bin minhum juz-on maqsoom<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>44.  To it are seven Gates:<br> For each of those Gates<br> Is a (special) class<br> (Of sinners) assigned.</p> <div class="filenav">
