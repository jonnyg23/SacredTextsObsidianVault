 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_027.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>27.  Wa<b>o</b>tlu AAalayhim nabaa ibnay <u>a</u>dama bi<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>aqqi i<u>th</u> qarrab<u>a</u> qurb<u>a</u>nan fatuqubbila min a<u>h</u>adihim<u>a</u> walam yutaqabbal mina al-<u>a</u>khari q<u>a</u>la laaqtulannaka q<u>a</u>la innam<u>a</u> yataqabbalu All<u>a</u>hu mina almuttaqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>27.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">30</font></span> Recite to them the truth<br> Of the story of the two sons<br>Of Adam. Behold! they each<br> Presented a sacrifice (to God):<br> It was accepted from one,<br> But not from the other.<br> Said the latter: "Be sure<br> I will slay thee." "Surely,"<br> Said the former, "God<br> Doth accept of the sacrifice<br> Of those who are righteous.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_028.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>28.  La-in basa<u>t</u>ta ilayya yadaka litaqtulanee m<u>a</u> an<u>a</u> bib<u>a</u>si<u>t</u>in yadiya ilayka li-aqtulaka innee akh<u>a</u>fu All<u>a</u>ha rabba alAA<u>a</u>lameen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>28.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">31</font></span>"If thou dost stretch thy hand<br> Against me, to slay me,<br> It is not for me to stretch<br> My hand against thee<br> To slay thee: for I do fear<br> God, the Cherisher of the Worlds.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_029.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>29.  Innee oreedu an taboo-a bi-ithmee wa-ithmika fatakoona min a<u>s</u>-<u>ha</u>bi a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>ri wa<u>tha</u>lika jaz<u>a</u>o a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>29.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">32</font></span> "For me, I intend to let<br> Thee draw on thyself<br> My sin as well as thine,<br> For thou wilt be among<br> The Companions of the Fire,<br> And that is the reward<br> Of those who do wrong."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_030.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>30.  Fa<u>t</u>awwaAAat lahu nafsuhu qatla akheehi faqatalahu faa<u>s</u>ba<u>h</u>a mina alkh<u>a</u>sireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>30.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">33</font></span> The (selfish) soul of the other<br> Led him to the murder<br> Of his brother: he murdered<br> Him, and became (himself)<br> One of the lost ones.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_031.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>31.  FabaAAatha All<u>a</u>hu ghur<u>a</u>ban yab<u>h</u>athu fee al-ar<u>d</u>i liyuriyahu kayfa yuw<u>a</u>ree saw-ata akheehi q<u>a</u>la y<u>a</u> waylat<u>a</u> aAAajaztu an akoona mithla h<u>atha</u> alghur<u>a</u>bi faow<u>a</u>riya saw-ata akhee faa<u>s</u>ba<u>h</u>a mina a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>dimeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>31.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">34</font></span> Then God sent a raven,<br> Who scratched the ground,<br>To show him how to hide<br> The shame of his brother.<br> "Woe is me!" said he;<br> "Was I not even able<br> To be as this raven,<br> And to hide the shame<br> Of my brother?" Then he became<br> Full of regrets—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_032.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>32.  Min ajli <u>tha</u>lika katabn<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> banee isr<u>a</u>-eela annahu man qatala nafsan bighayri nafsin aw fas<u>a</u>din fee al-ar<u>d</u>i fakaannam<u>a</u> qatala a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>sa jameeAAan waman a<u>h</u>y<u>a</u>h<u>a</u> fakaannam<u>a</u> a<u>h</u>y<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>sa jameeAAan walaqad j<u>a</u>at-hum rusulun<u>a</u> bi<b>a</b>lbayyin<u>a</u>ti thumma inna katheeran minhum baAAda <u>tha</u>lika fee al-ar<u>d</u>i lamusrifoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>32.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">35</font></span> On that account: We ordained<br> For the Children of Israel<br> That if any one slew<br> A person—unless it be<br> For murder or for spreading<br> Mischief in the land—<br> It would be as if<br> He slew the whole people:<br> And if any one saved a life,<br> It would be as if he saved<br> The life of the whole people.<br> Then although there came<br> To them Our Apostles<br> With Clear Signs, yet,<br> Even after that, many<br> Of them continued to commit<br> Excesses in the land.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_033.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>33.  Innam<u>a</u> jaz<u>a</u>o alla<u>th</u>eena yu<u>ha</u>riboona All<u>a</u>ha warasoolahu wayasAAawna fee al-ar<u>d</u>i fas<u>a</u>dan an yuqattaloo aw yu<u>s</u>allaboo aw tuqa<u>tt</u>aAAa aydeehim waarjuluhum min khil<u>a</u>fin aw yunfaw mina al-ar<u>d</u>i <u>tha</u>lika lahum khizyun fee a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> walahum fee al-<u>a</u>khirati AAa<u>tha</u>bun AAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>33.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">36</font></span> The punishment of those<br> Who wage war against God<br> And His Apostle, and strive<br> With might and main<br> For mischief through the land<br> Is: execution, or crucifixion,<br>Or the cutting off of hands<br> And feet from opposite sides,<br> Or exile from the land:<br> That is their disgrace<br> In this world, and<br> A heavy punishment is theirs<br> In the Hereafter;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_034.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>34.  Ill<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena t<u>a</u>boo min qabli an taqdiroo AAalayhim fa<b>i</b>AAlamoo anna All<u>a</u>ha ghafoorun ra<u>h</u>eem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>34.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">37</font></span> Except for those who repent<br> Before they fall<br> Into your power:<br> In that case, know<br> That God is Oft-forgiving,<br> Most Merciful.</p> <div class="filenav">
