 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XVI.: Naḥl or The Bee.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 16</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_120.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>120.  Inna ibr<u>a</u>heema k<u>a</u>na ommatan q<u>a</u>nitan lill<u>a</u>hi <u>h</u>aneefan walam yaku mina almushrikeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>120.  Abraham was indeed a model,<br> Devoutly obedient to God,<br> (And) true in faith, and he<br> Joined not gods with God:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_121.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>121.  Sh<u>a</u>kiran li-anAAumihi ijtab<u>a</u>hu wahad<u>a</u>hu il<u>a</u> <u>s</u>ir<u>at</u>in mustaqeem<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>121.  He showed his gratitude<br> For the favours of God,<br> Who chose him, and guided him<br> To a Straight Way.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_122.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>122.  Wa<u>a</u>tayn<u>a</u>hu fee a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> <u>h</u>asanatan wa-innahu fee al-<u>a</u>khirati lamina a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>li<u>h</u>een<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>122.  And We gave him Good<br> In this world, and he will be,<br>In the Hereafter, in the ranks<br> Of the Righteous,''</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_123.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>123.  Thumma aw<u>h</u>ayn<u>a</u> ilayka ani ittabiAA millata ibr<u>a</u>heema <u>h</u>aneefan wam<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>na mina almushrikeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>123.  So We have taught thee<br> The inspired (message),<br> "Follow the ways of Abraham<br> The True in Faith, and he<br> Joined not gods with God."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_124.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>124.  Innam<u>a</u> juAAila a<b>l</b>ssabtu AAal<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena ikhtalafoo feehi wa-inna rabbaka laya<u>h</u>kumu baynahum yawma alqiy<u>a</u>mati feem<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo feehi yakhtalifoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>124.  The Sabbath was only made<br> (Strict) for those who disagreed<br> (As to its observance);<br> But God will judge between them<br> On the Day of Judgment,<br> As to their differences,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_125.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>125.  OdAAu il<u>a</u> sabeeli rabbika bi<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>ikmati wa<b>a</b>lmawAAi<i><u>th</u></i>ati al<u>h</u>asanati waj<u>a</u>dilhum bi<b>a</b>llatee hiya a<u>h</u>sanu inna rabbaka huwa aAAlamu biman <u>d</u>alla AAan sabeelihi wahuwa aAAlamu bi<b>a</b>lmuhtadeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>125.  Invite (all) to the Way<br> Of thy Lord with wisdom<br> And beautiful preaching;<br> And argue with them<br> In ways that are best<br> And most gracious:<br> For thy Lord knoweth best,<br> Who have strayed from His Path,<br>And who receive guidance.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_126.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>126.  Wa-in AA<u>a</u>qabtum faAA<u>a</u>qiboo bimithli m<u>a</u> AAooqibtum bihi wala-in <u>s</u>abartum lahuwa khayrun li<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>bireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>126.  And if ye do catch them out,<br> Catch them out no worse<br> Than they catch you out:<br> But if ye show patience,<br> That is indeed the best (course)<br> For those who are patient.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_127.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>127.  Wa<b>i</b><u>s</u>bir wam<u>a</u> <u>s</u>abruka ill<u>a</u> bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi wal<u>a</u> ta<u>h</u>zan AAalayhim wal<u>a</u> taku fee <u>d</u>ayqin mimm<u>a</u> yamkuroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>127.  And do thou be patient,<br> For thy patience is but<br> From God; nor grieve over them:<br> And distress not thyself<br> Because of their plots.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_128.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>128.  Inna All<u>a</u>ha maAAa alla<u>th</u>eena ittaqaw wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena hum mu<u>h</u>sinoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>128.  For God is with those<br> Who restrain themselves,<br> And those who do good.</p>  <div class="filenav">
