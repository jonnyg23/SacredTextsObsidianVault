 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XVI.: Naḥl or The Bee.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 7</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_051.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>51.  Waq<u>a</u>la All<u>a</u>hu l<u>a</u> tattakhi<u>th</u>oo il<u>a</u>hayni ithnayni innam<u>a</u> huwa il<u>a</u>hun w<u>ah</u>idun fa-iyy<u>a</u>ya fa<b>i</b>rhaboon<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>51.  God has said: "Take not<br> (For worship) two gods:<br> For He is just One God:<br> Then fear Me (and Me alone)."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_052.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>52.  Walahu m<u>a</u> fee a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i walahu a<b>l</b>ddeenu w<u>as</u>iban afaghayra All<u>a</u>hi tattaqoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>52.  To Him belongs whatever<br> Is in the heavens and on earth,<br> And to Him is duty due always:<br> Then will ye fear other<br> Than God?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_053.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>53.  Wam<u>a</u> bikum min niAAmatin famina All<u>a</u>hi thumma i<u>tha</u> massakumu a<b>l</b><u>dd</u>urru fa-ilayhi taj-aroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>53.  And ye have no good thing<br> But is from God: and moreover,<br> When ye are touched by distress,<br> Unto Him ye cry with groans;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_054.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>54.  Thumma i<u>tha</u> kashafa a<b>l</b><u>dd</u>urra AAankum i<u>tha</u> fareequn minkum birabbihim yushrikoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>54.  Yet, when He removes<br> The distress from you, behold!<br> Some of you turn to other gods<br> To join with their Lord—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_055.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>55.  Liyakfuroo bim<u>a</u> <u>a</u>tayn<u>a</u>hum fatamattaAAoo fasawfa taAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>55.  (As if) to show their ingratitude<br> For the favours We have<br> Bestowed on them! Then enjoy<br> (Your brief day); but soon<br> Will ye know (your folly)!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_056.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>56.  WayajAAaloona lim<u>a</u> l<u>a</u> yaAAlamoona na<u>s</u>eeban mimm<u>a</u> razaqn<u>a</u>hum ta<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi latus-alunna AAamm<u>a</u> kuntum taftaroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>56.  And they (even) assign,<br> To things they do not know,<br> A portion out of that<br> Which We have bestowed<br> For their sustenance!<br> By God, ye shall certainly<br> Be called to account<br> For your false inventions.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_057.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>57.  WayajAAaloona lill<u>a</u>hi alban<u>a</u>ti sub<u>ha</u>nahu walahum m<u>a</u> yashtahoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>57.  And they assign daughters<br> For God!—Glory be to Him!—<br> And for themselves (sons,<br> The issue) they desire!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_058.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>58.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> bushshira a<u>h</u>aduhum bi<b>a</b>lonth<u>a</u> <i><u>th</u></i>alla wajhuhu muswaddan wahuwa ka<i><u>th</u></i>eem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>58.  When news is brought<br> To one of them, of (the birth<br> Of) a female (child), his face<br> Darkens, and he is filled<br> With inward grief!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_059.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>59.  Yataw<u>a</u>r<u>a</u> mina alqawmi min soo-i m<u>a</u> bushshira bihi ayumsikuhu AAal<u>a</u> hoonin am yadussuhu fee a<b>l</b>ttur<u>a</u>bi al<u>a</u> s<u>a</u>a m<u>a</u> ya<u>h</u>kumoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>59.  With shame does he hide<br> Himself from his people,<br>Because of the bad news<br> He has had!<br> Shall he retain it<br> On (sufferance and) contempt,<br> Or bury it in the dust?<br> Ah! what an evil (choice)<br> They decide on?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_060.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>60.  Lilla<u>th</u>eena l<u>a</u> yu/minoona bi<b>a</b>l-<u>a</u>khirati mathalu a<b>l</b>ssaw-i walill<u>a</u>hi almathalu al-aAAl<u>a</u> wahuwa alAAazeezu al<u>h</u>akeem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>60.  To those who believe not<br> In the Hereafter, applies<br> The similitude of evil:<br> To God applies the highest<br> Similitude: for He is<br> The Exalted in Power,<br> Full of Wisdom.</p> <div class="filenav">
