 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sara LXXIX.: Nāzi’āt, or Those Who Tear Out.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/079/079_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  Wa<b>al</b>nn<u>a</u>ziAA<u>a</u>ti gharq<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>1.  By the (angels)<br> Who tear out<br> (The souls of the wicked)<br> With violence;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/079/079_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  Wa<b>al</b>nn<u>a</u>shi<u>ta</u>ti nash<u>ta</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>2.  By those who gently<br> Draw out (the souls<br> Of the blessed);</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/079/079_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  Wa<b>al</b>ss<u>a</u>bi<u>ha</u>ti sab<u>ha</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>3.  And by those who glide<br> Along (on errands of mercy),</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/079/079_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  Fa<b>al</b>ss<u>a</u>biq<u>a</u>ti sabq<u>a</u><b>n</b>    </p>
 <p>4.  Then press forward<br> As in a race,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/079/079_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  Fa<b>a</b>lmudabbir<u>a</u>ti amr<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>5.  Then arrange to do<br> (The Commands of their Lord),—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/079/079_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  Yawma tarjufu a<b>l</b>rr<u>a</u>jifa<b>tu</b>   </p>
 <p>6.  One Day everything that<br> Can be in commotion will<br> Be in violent commotion,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/079/079_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  TatbaAAuh<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>rr<u>a</u>difa<b>tu</b>   </p>
 <p>7.  Followed by oft-repeated<br> (Commotions):</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/079/079_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  Quloobun yawma-i<u>th</u>in w<u>a</u>jifa<b>tun</b>   </p>
 <p>8.  Hearts that Day<br> Will be in agitation;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/079/079_009.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>9.  Ab<u>sa</u>ruh<u>a</u> kh<u>a</u>shiAAa<b>tun</b>   </p>
 <p>9.  Cast down will be<br> (Their owners’) eyes.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/079/079_010.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>10.  Yaqooloona a-inn<u>a</u> lamardoodoona fee al<u>ha</u>fira<b>ti</b>   </p>
 <p>10.  They say (now): "What!<br> Shall we indeed be<br> Returned to (our) former state?—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/079/079_011.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>11.  A-i<u>tha</u> kunn<u>a</u> AAi<i><u>th</u></i><u>a</u>man nakhira<b>tan</b>   </p>
 <p>11.  "What!—when we shall<br> Have become rotten bones?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/079/079_012.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>12.  Q<u>a</u>loo tilka i<u>th</u>an karratun kh<u>a</u>sira<b>tun</b>   </p>
 <p>12.  They say: "It would<br> In that case, be<br> A return with loss!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/079/079_013.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>13.  Fa-innam<u>a</u> hiya zajratun w<u>ah</u>ida<b>tun</b>   </p>
 <p>13.  But verily, it will<br> Be but a single<br>(Compelling) Cry,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/079/079_014.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>14.  Fa-i<u>tha</u> hum bi<b>al</b>ss<u>a</u>hira<b>ti</b>   </p>
 <p>14.  When, behold, they<br> Will be in the (full)<br> Awakening (to Judgment).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/079/079_015.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>15.  Hal at<u>a</u>ka <u>h</u>adeethu moos<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>15.  Has the story<br> Of Moses reached thee?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/079/079_016.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>16.  I<u>th</u> n<u>a</u>d<u>a</u>hu rabbuhu bi<b>a</b>lw<u>a</u>di almuqaddasi <u>t</u>uw<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>16.  Behold, thy Lord did call<br> To him in the sacred valley<br> Of Ṭuwā:—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/079/079_017.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>17.  I<u>th</u>hab il<u>a</u> firAAawna innahu <u>t</u>agh<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>17.  "Go thou to Pharaoh,<br> For he has indeed<br> Transgressed all bounds:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/079/079_018.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>18.  Faqul hal laka il<u>a</u> an tazakk<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>18.  "And say to him,<br> "Wouldst thou that thou<br> Shouldst he purified<br> (From sin)?—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/079/079_019.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>19.  Waahdiyaka il<u>a</u> rabbika fatakhsh<u>a</u>    </p>
 <p>19.  "And that I guide thee<br> To thy Lord, so thou<br> Shouldst fear Him?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/079/079_020.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>20.  Faar<u>a</u>hu al-<u>a</u>yata alkubr<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>20.  Then did (Moses) show him<br> The Great Sign.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/079/079_021.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>21.  Faka<u>thth</u>aba waAAa<u>sa</u>   </p>
 <p>21.  But (Pharaoh) rejected it<br> And disobeyed (guidance);</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/079/079_022.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>22.  Thumma adbara yasAA<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>22.  Further, he turned his back,<br> Striving hard (against God).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/079/079_023.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>23.  Fa<u>h</u>ashara fan<u>a</u>d<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>23.  Then he collected (his men)<br> And made a proclamation,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/079/079_024.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>24.  Faq<u>a</u>la an<u>a</u> rabbukumu al-aAAl<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>24.  Saying, "I am your Lord,<br> Most High".</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/079/079_025.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>25.  Faakha<u>th</u>ahu All<u>a</u>hu nak<u>a</u>la al-<u>a</u>khirati wa<b>a</b>l-ool<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>25.  But God did punish him,<br> (And made an) example<br> Of him,—in the Hereafter,<br> As in this life.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/079/079_026.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>26.  Inna fee <u>tha</u>lika laAAibratan liman yakhsh<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>26.  Verily in this is<br> An instructive warning<br> For whosoever feareth (God).</p> <div class="filenav">
