 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra LXIV.: Tagābun, or Mutual Loss and Gain.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 2</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/064/064_011.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>11.  M<u>a</u> a<u>sa</u>ba min mu<u>s</u>eebatin ill<u>a</u> bi-i<u>th</u>ni All<u>a</u>hi waman yu/min bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi yahdi qalbahu wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu bikulli shay-in AAaleem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>11.  No kind of calamity<br> Can occur, except<br> By the leave of God:<br> And if any one believes<br> In God, (God) guides his<br> Heart (aright): for God<br> Knows all things.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/064/064_012.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>12.  Waa<u>t</u>eeAAoo All<u>a</u>ha waa<u>t</u>eeAAoo a<b>l</b>rrasoola fa-in tawallaytum fa-innam<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> rasoolin<u>a</u> albal<u>a</u>ghu almubeen<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>12.  So obey God, and obey<br> His Apostle: but if<br> Ye turn back, the duty<br> Of Our Apostle is but<br> To proclaim (the Message)<br> Clearly and openly.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/064/064_013.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>13.  All<u>a</u>hu l<u>a</u> il<u>a</u>ha ill<u>a</u> huwa waAAal<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi falyatawakkali almu/minoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>13.  God! There is no god<br> But He: and on God,<br> Therefore, let the Believers<br> Put their trust.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/064/064_014.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>14.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo inna min azw<u>a</u>jikum waawl<u>a</u>dikum AAaduwwan lakum fa<b>i</b><u>hth</u>aroohum wa-in taAAfoo wata<u>s</u>fa<u>h</u>oo wataghfiroo fa-inna All<u>a</u>ha ghafoorun ra<u>h</u>eem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>14.  O ye who believe!<br> Truly, among your wives<br>And your children are (some<br> That are) enemies to<br> Yourselves: so beware<br> Of them! But if ye<br> Forgive and overlook,<br> And cover up (their faults),<br> Verily God is<br> Oft-Forgiving, Most Merciful.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/064/064_015.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>15.  Innam<u>a</u> amw<u>a</u>lukum waawl<u>a</u>dukum fitnatun wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu AAindahu ajrun AAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>15.  Your riches and your children<br> May be but a trial:<br> But in the Presence of God,<br> Is the highest Reward.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/064/064_016.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>16.  Fa<b>i</b>ttaqoo All<u>a</u>ha m<u>a</u> ista<u>t</u>aAAtum wa<b>i</b>smaAAoo waa<u>t</u>eeAAoo waanfiqoo khayran li-anfusikum waman yooqa shu<u>hh</u>a nafsihi faol<u>a</u>-ika humu almufli<u>h</u>oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>16.  So fear God<br> As much as ye can;<br> Listen and obey;<br> And spend in charity<br> For the benefit of<br> Your own souls.<br> And those saved from<br> The covetousness of their own<br>Souls,—they are the ones<br> That achieve prosperity.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/064/064_017.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>17.  In tuqri<u>d</u>oo All<u>a</u>ha qar<u>d</u>an <u>h</u>asanan yu<u>da</u>AAifhu lakum wayaghfir lakum wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu shakoorun <u>h</u>aleem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>17.  If ye loan to God<br> A beautiful loan, He<br> Will double it to<br> Your (credit), and He<br> Will grant you Forgiveness:<br> For God is most Ready<br> To appreciate (service)<br> Most Forbearing,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/064/064_018.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>18.  AA<u>a</u>limu alghaybi wa<b>al</b>shshah<u>a</u>dati alAAazeezu al<u>h</u>akeem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>18.  Knower of what is hidden<br> And what is open,<br> Exalted in Might,<br> Full of Wisdom.</p>  <div class="filenav">
