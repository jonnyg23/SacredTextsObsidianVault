 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/033/033_053.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>53.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo l<u>a</u> tadkhuloo buyoota a<b>l</b>nnabiyyi ill<u>a</u> an yu/<u>th</u>ana lakum il<u>a</u> <u>t</u>aAA<u>a</u>min ghayra n<u>a</u><i><u>th</u></i>ireena in<u>a</u>hu wal<u>a</u>kin i<u>tha</u> duAAeetum fa<b>o</b>dkhuloo fa-i<u>tha</u> <u>t</u>aAAimtum fa<b>i</b>ntashiroo wal<u>a</u> musta/niseena li<u>h</u>adeethin inna <u>tha</u>likum k<u>a</u>na yu/<u>th</u>ee a<b>l</b>nnabiyya fayasta<u>h</u>yee minkum wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu l<u>a</u> yasta<u>h</u>yee mina al<u>h</u>aqqi wa-i<u>tha</u> saaltumoohunna mat<u>a</u>AAan fa<b>i</b>s-aloohunna min war<u>a</u>-i <u>h</u>ij<u>a</u>bin <u>tha</u>likum a<u>t</u>haru liquloobikum waquloobihinna wam<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>na lakum an tu/<u>th</u>oo rasoola All<u>a</u>hi wal<u>a</u> an tanki<u>h</u>oo azw<u>a</u>jahu min baAAdihi abadan inna <u>tha</u>likum k<u>a</u>na AAinda All<u>a</u>hi AAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>53.  O ye who believe!<br> Enter not the Prophet's houses,—<br> Until leave is given you,<br> For a meal, (and then)<br> Not (so early as) to wait<br> For its preparation: but when<br> Ye are invited, enter;<br> And when ye have taken<br> Your meal, disperse,<br> Without seeking familiar talk.<br> Such (behaviour) annoys<br> The Prophet: he is ashamed<br> To dismiss you, but<br> God is not ashamed<br> (To tell you) the truth.<br> And when ye<br> Ask (his ladies)<br> For anything ye want,<br> Ask them from before<br> A screen: that makes<br> For greater purity for<br> Your hearts and for theirs.<br> Nor is it right for you<br>That ye should annoy<br> God's Apostle, or that<br> Ye should marry his widows<br> After him at any time.<br> Truly such a thing is<br> In God's sight an enormity.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/033/033_054.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>54.  In tubdoo shay-an aw tukhfoohu fa-inna All<u>a</u>ha k<u>a</u>na bikulli shay-in AAaleem<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>54.  Whether ye reveal anything<br> Or conceal it, verily<br> God has full knowledge<br> Of all things.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/033/033_055.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>55.  L<u>a</u> jun<u>ah</u>a AAalayhinna fee <u>a</u>b<u>a</u>-ihinna wal<u>a</u> abn<u>a</u>-ihinna wal<u>a</u> ikhw<u>a</u>nihinna wal<u>a</u> abn<u>a</u>-i ikhw<u>a</u>nihinna wal<u>a</u> abn<u>a</u>-i akhaw<u>a</u>tihinna wal<u>a</u> nis<u>a</u>-ihinna wal<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> malakat aym<u>a</u>nuhunna wa<b>i</b>ttaqeena All<u>a</u>ha inna All<u>a</u>ha k<u>a</u>na AAal<u>a</u> kulli shay-in shaheed<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>55.  There is no blame<br> (On these ladies if they<br> Appear) before their fathers<br> Or their sons, their brothers,<br> Or their brothers’ sons,<br> Or their sisters’ sons,<br> Or their women,<br> Or the (slaves) whom<br> Their right hands possess.<br> And, (ladies), fear God;<br> For God is Witness<br> To all things.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/033/033_056.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>56.  Inna All<u>a</u>ha wamal<u>a</u>-ikatahu yu<u>s</u>alloona AAal<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nnabiyyi y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo <u>s</u>alloo AAalayhi wasallimoo tasleem<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>56.  God and His Angels<br> Send blessings on the Prophet:<br>O ye that believe!<br> Send ye blessings on him,<br> And salute him<br> With all respect.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/033/033_057.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>57.  Inna alla<u>th</u>eena yu/<u>th</u>oona All<u>a</u>ha warasoolahu laAAanahumu All<u>a</u>hu fee a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> wa<b>a</b>l-<u>a</u>khirati waaAAadda lahum AAa<u>tha</u>ban muheen<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>57.  Those who annoy<br> God and His Apostle—<br> God has cursed them<br> In this world and<br> In the Hereafter,<br> And has prepared for them<br> A humiliating Punishment.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/033/033_058.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>58.  Wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena yu/<u>th</u>oona almu/mineena wa<b>a</b>lmu/min<u>a</u>ti bighayri m<u>a</u> iktasaboo faqadi i<u>h</u>tamaloo buht<u>a</u>nan wa-ithman mubeen<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>58.  And those who annoy<br> Believing men and women<br> Undeservedly, bear<br> (on themselves)<br> A calumny and a glaring sin.</p> <div class="filenav">
