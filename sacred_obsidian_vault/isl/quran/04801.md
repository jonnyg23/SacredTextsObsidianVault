 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XLVIII.: Fat-ḥ or Victory.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/048/048_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  Inn<u>a</u> fata<u>h</u>n<u>a</u> laka fat<u>h</u>an mubeen<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>1.  Verily We have granted<br> Thee a manifest Victory:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/048/048_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  Liyaghfira laka All<u>a</u>hu m<u>a</u> taqaddama min <u>th</u>anbika wam<u>a</u> taakhkhara wayutimma niAAmatahu AAalayka wayahdiyaka <u>s</u>ir<u>at</u>an mustaqeem<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>2.  That God may forgive thee<br> Thy faults of the past<br> And those to follow;<br> Fulfil His favour to thee;<br> And guide thee<br> On the Straight Way;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/048/048_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  Wayan<u>s</u>uraka All<u>a</u>hu na<u>s</u>ran AAazeez<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>3.  And that God may help<br> Thee with powerful help.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/048/048_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  Huwa alla<u>th</u>ee anzala a<b>l</b>ssakeenata fee quloobi almu/mineena liyazd<u>a</u>doo eem<u>a</u>nan maAAa eem<u>a</u>nihim walill<u>a</u>hi junoodu a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i wak<u>a</u>na All<u>a</u>hu AAaleeman <u>h</u>akeem<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>4.  It is He Who sent<br> Down Tranquillity<br> Into the hearts of<br> The Believers, that they may<br> Add Faith to their Faith;—<br> For to God belong<br>The Forces of the heavens<br> And the earth; and God is<br> Full of Knowledge and Wisdom;—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/048/048_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  Liyudkhila almu/mineena wa<b>a</b>lmu/min<u>a</u>ti jann<u>a</u>tin tajree min ta<u>h</u>tih<u>a</u> al-anh<u>a</u>ru kh<u>a</u>lideena feeh<u>a</u> wayukaffira AAanhum sayyi-<u>a</u>tihim wak<u>a</u>na <u>tha</u>lika AAinda All<u>a</u>hi fawzan AAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>5.  That He may admit<br> The men and women<br> Who believe, to Gardens<br> Beneath which rivers flow,<br> To dwell therein for aye,<br> And remove their ills<br> From them;—and that is,<br> In the sight of God,<br> The highest achievement<br> (For man),—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/048/048_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  WayuAAa<u>thth</u>iba almun<u>a</u>fiqeena wa<b>a</b>lmun<u>a</u>fiq<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>lmushrikeena wa<b>a</b>lmushrik<u>a</u>ti a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>nneena bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi <i><u>th</u></i>anna a<b>l</b>ssaw-i AAalayhim d<u>a</u>-iratu a<b>l</b>ssaw-i wagha<u>d</u>iba All<u>a</u>hu AAalayhim walaAAanahum waaAAadda lahum jahannama was<u>a</u>at ma<u>s</u>eer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>6.  And that He may punish<br> The Hypocrites, men and<br> Women, and the Polytheists,<br> Men and women, who imagine<br> An evil opinion of God.<br> On them is a round<br> Of Evil: the Wrath of God<br> Is on them: He has cursed<br> Them and got Hell ready<br> For them: and evil<br> Is it for a destination.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/048/048_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  Walill<u>a</u>hi junoodu a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i wak<u>a</u>na All<u>a</u>hu AAazeezan <u>h</u>akeem<u>a</u><b>n</b>    </p>
 <p>7.  For to God belong<br> The Forces of the heavens<br> And the earth; and God is<br> Exalted in Power,<br> Full of Wisdom.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/048/048_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  Inn<u>a</u> arsaln<u>a</u>ka sh<u>a</u>hidan wamubashshiran wana<u>th</u>eer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>8.  We have truly sent thee<br> As a witness, as a<br> Bringer of Glad Tidings,<br> And as a Warner:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/048/048_009.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>9.  Litu/minoo bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi warasoolihi watuAAazziroohu watuwaqqiroohu watusabbi<u>h</u>oohu bukratan waa<u>s</u>eel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>9.  In order that ye<br> (O men) may believe<br> In God and His Apostle,<br> That ye may assist<br> And honour Him,<br> And celebrate His praises<br> Morning and evening.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/048/048_010.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>10.  Inna alla<u>th</u>eena yub<u>a</u>yiAAoonaka innam<u>a</u> yub<u>a</u>yiAAoona All<u>a</u>ha yadu All<u>a</u>hi fawqa aydeehim faman nakatha fa-innam<u>a</u> yankuthu AAal<u>a</u> nafsihi waman awf<u>a</u> bim<u>a</u> AA<u>a</u>hada AAalayhu All<u>a</u>ha fasayu/teehi ajran AAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>10.  Verily those who plight<br> Their fealty to thee<br> Do no less than plight<br> Their fealty to God:<br> The Hand of God is<br> Over their hands:<br> Then any one who violates<br> His oath, does so<br> To the harm of his own<br> Soul, and any one who<br> Fulfils what he has<br> Covenanted with God,—<br> God will soon grant him<br> A great Reward.</p> <div class="filenav">
