 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XLIV.: Du<u>kh</u>ān, or Smoke (or Mist.)</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 2</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/044/044_030.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>30.  Walaqad najjayn<u>a</u> banee isr<u>a</u>-eela mina alAAa<u>tha</u>bi almuheen<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>30.  We did deliver aforetime<br> The Children of Israel<br> From humiliating Punishment,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/044/044_031.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>31.  Min firAAawna innahu k<u>a</u>na AA<u>a</u>liyan mina almusrifeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>31.  Inflicted by Pharaoh, for he<br> Was arrogant (even) among<br> Inordinate transgressors.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/044/044_032.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>32.  Walaqadi ikhtarn<u>a</u>hum AAal<u>a</u> AAilmin AAal<u>a</u> alAA<u>a</u>lameen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>32.  And We chose them aforetime<br> Above the nations, knowingly,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/044/044_033.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>33.  Wa<u>a</u>tayn<u>a</u>hum mina al-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>ti m<u>a</u> feehi bal<u>a</u>on mubeen<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>33.  And granted them Signs<br> In which there was<br> A manifest trial.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/044/044_034.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>34.  Inna h<u>a</u>ol<u>a</u>-i layaqooloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>34.  As to these (Quraish),<br> They say forsooth:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/044/044_035.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>35.  In hiya ill<u>a</u> mawtatun<u>a</u> al-ool<u>a</u> wam<u>a</u> na<u>h</u>nu bimunshareen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>35.  "There is nothing beyond<br> Our first death,<br> And we shall not<br> Be raised again.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/044/044_036.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>36.  Fa/too bi-<u>a</u>b<u>a</u>-in<u>a</u> in kuntum <u>sa</u>diqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>36.  "Then bring (back)<br> Our forefathers, if what<br> Ye say is true!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/044/044_037.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>37.  Ahum khayrun am qawmu tubbaAAin wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena min qablihim ahlakn<u>a</u>hum innahum k<u>a</u>noo mujrimeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>37.  What! are they better<br> Than the people of Tubba‘<br> And those who were<br> Before them? We destroyed<br> Them because they were<br> Guilty of sin.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/044/044_038.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>38.  Wam<u>a</u> khalaqn<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>a wam<u>a</u> baynahum<u>a</u> l<u>a</u>AAibeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>38.  We created not<br> The heavens, the earth,<br> And all between them,<br> Merely in (idle) sport:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/044/044_039.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>39.  M<u>a</u> khalaqn<u>a</u>hum<u>a</u> ill<u>a</u> bi<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>aqqi wal<u>a</u>kinna aktharahum l<u>a</u> yaAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>39.  We created them not<br> Except for just ends:<br> But most of them<br> Do not understand.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/044/044_040.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>40.  Inna yawma alfa<u>s</u>li meeq<u>a</u>tuhum ajmaAAeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>40.  Verily the Day of<br> Sorting Out is the time<br> Appointed for all of them,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/044/044_041.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>41.  Yawma l<u>a</u> yughnee mawlan AAan mawlan shay-an wal<u>a</u> hum yun<u>s</u>aroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>41.  The Day when no protector<br> Can avail his client<br> In aught, and no help<br> Can they receive,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/044/044_042.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>42.  Ill<u>a</u> man ra<u>h</u>ima All<u>a</u>hu innahu huwa alAAazeezu a<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>eem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>42.  Except such as receive<br> God's Mercy: for He is<br> Exalted in Might, Most Merciful.</p> <div class="filenav">
