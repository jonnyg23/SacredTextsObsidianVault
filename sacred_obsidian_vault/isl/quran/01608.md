 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_061.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>61.  Walaw yu-<u>a</u>khi<u>th</u>u All<u>a</u>hu a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>sa bi<i><u>th</u></i>ulmihim m<u>a</u> taraka AAalayh<u>a</u> min d<u>a</u>bbatin wal<u>a</u>kin yu-akhkhiruhum il<u>a</u> ajalin musamman fa-i<u>tha</u> j<u>a</u>a ajaluhum l<u>a</u> yasta/khiroona s<u>a</u>AAatan wal<u>a</u> yastaqdimoon<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>61.  If God were to punish<br> Men for their wrong-doing,<br> He would not leave, on the (earth);<br> A single living creature:<br> But He gives them respite<br> For a stated Term:<br> When their Term expires,<br> They would not be able<br> To delay (the punishment)<br> For a single hour, just as<br> They would not be able<br> To anticipate it (for a single hour).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_062.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>62.  WayajAAaloona lill<u>a</u>hi m<u>a</u> yakrahoona wata<u>s</u>ifu alsinatuhumu alka<u>th</u>iba anna lahumu al<u>h</u>usn<u>a</u> l<u>a</u> jarama anna lahumu a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>ra waannahum mufra<u>t</u>oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>62.  They attribute to God<br> What they hate (for themselves),<br> And their tongues assert<br> The falsehood that all good things<br> Are for themselves: without doubt<br> For them is the Fire, and they<br> Will be the first to be<br> Hastened on into it!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_063.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>63.  Ta<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi laqad arsaln<u>a</u> il<u>a</u> omamin min qablika fazayyana lahumu a<b>l</b>shshay<u>ta</u>nu aAAm<u>a</u>lahum fahuwa waliyyuhumu alyawma walahum AAa<u>tha</u>bun aleem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>63.  By God, We (also) sent<br> (Our apostles) to Peoples<br> Before thee; but Satan<br> Made, (to the wicked),<br> Their own acts seem alluring:<br> He is also their patron to-day,<br> But they shall have<br> A most grievous penalty.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_064.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>64.  Wam<u>a</u> anzaln<u>a</u> AAalayka alkit<u>a</u>ba ill<u>a</u> litubayyina lahumu alla<u>th</u>ee ikhtalafoo feehi wahudan wara<u>h</u>matan liqawmin yu/minoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>64.  And We sent down the Book<br> To thee for the express purpose,<br> That thou shouldst make clear<br> To them those things in which<br> They differ, and that it should he<br> A guide and a mercy<br> To those who believe.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_065.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>65.  Wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu anzala mina a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>-i m<u>a</u>an faa<u>h</u>y<u>a</u> bihi al-ar<u>d</u>a baAAda mawtih<u>a</u> inna fee <u>tha</u>lika la<u>a</u>yatan liqawmin yasmaAAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>65.  And God sends down rain<br> From the skies, and gives therewith<br> Life to the earth after its death:<br> Verily in this is a Sign<br>For those who listen.</p> <div class="filenav">
