 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra LXXV.: Qiyāmat, or the Resurrection.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/075/075_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  L<u>a</u> oqsimu biyawmi alqiy<u>a</u>mat<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>1.  I do call to witness<br> The Resurrection Day;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/075/075_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  Wal<u>a</u> oqsimu bi<b>al</b>nnafsi allaww<u>a</u>mat<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>2.  And I do call to witness<br> The self-reproaching spirit:<br> (Eschew Evil).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/075/075_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  Aya<u>h</u>sabu al-ins<u>a</u>nu allan najmaAAa AAi<i><u>th</u></i><u>a</u>mah<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>3.  Does man think that We<br> Cannot assemble his bones?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/075/075_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  Bal<u>a</u> q<u>a</u>direena AAal<u>a</u> an nusawwiya ban<u>a</u>nah<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>4.  Nay, We are able to put<br> Together in perfect order<br> The very tips of his fingers.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/075/075_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  Bal yureedu al-ins<u>a</u>nu liyafjura am<u>a</u>mah<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>5.  But man wishes to do<br> Wrong (even) in the time<br> In front of him.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/075/075_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  Yas-alu ayy<u>a</u>na yawmu alqiy<u>a</u>ma<b>ti</b>   </p>
 <p>6.  He questions: "When<br> Is the Day of Resurrection?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/075/075_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  Fa-i<u>tha</u> bariqa alba<u>s</u>ar<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>7.  At length, when<br> The Sight is dazed,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/075/075_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  Wakhasafa alqamar<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>8.  And the moon is<br> Buried in darkness.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/075/075_009.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>9.  WajumiAAa a<b>l</b>shshamsu wa<b>a</b>lqamar<b>u</b>    </p>
 <p>9.  And the sun and moon<br> Are joined together,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/075/075_010.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>10.  Yaqoolu al-ins<u>a</u>nu yawma-i<u>th</u>in ayna almafar<b>ru</b>   </p>
 <p>10.  That Day will Man say:<br> "Where is the refuge?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/075/075_011.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>11.  Kall<u>a</u> l<u>a</u> wazar<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>11.  By no means!<br> No place of safety!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/075/075_012.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>12.  Il<u>a</u> rabbika yawma-i<u>th</u>in almustaqar<b>ru</b>   </p>
 <p>12.  Before thy Lord (alone),<br> That Day will be<br> The place of rest.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/075/075_013.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>13.  Yunabbao al-ins<u>a</u>nu yawma-i<u>th</u>in bim<u>a</u> qaddama waakhkhar<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>13.  That Day will Man<br> Be told (all) that he<br> Put forward, and all<br> That he put back.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/075/075_014.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>14.  Bali al-ins<u>a</u>nu AAal<u>a</u> nafsihi ba<u>s</u>eera<b>tun</b>   </p>
 <p>14.  Nay, man will be<br> Evidence against himself,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/075/075_015.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>15.  Walaw alq<u>a</u> maAA<u>ath</u>eerah<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>15.  Even though he were<br> To put up his excuses.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/075/075_016.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>16.  L<u>a</u> tu<u>h</u>arrik bihi lis<u>a</u>naka litaAAjala bih<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>16.  Move not thy tongue<br> Concerning the (Qur-ān)<br> To make haste therewith,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/075/075_017.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>17.  Inna AAalayn<u>a</u> jamAAahu waqur-<u>a</u>nah<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>17.  It is for Us to collect it<br> And to promulgate it:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/075/075_018.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>18.  Fa-i<u>tha</u> qara/n<u>a</u>hu fa<b>i</b>ttabiAA qur-<u>a</u>nah<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>18.  But when We have<br> Promulgated it, follow thou<br> Its recital (as promulgated):</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/075/075_019.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>19.  Thumma inna AAalayn<u>a</u> bay<u>a</u>nah<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>19.  Nay more, it is<br> For us to explain it<br> (And make it clear):</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/075/075_020.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>20.  Kall<u>a</u> bal tu<u>h</u>ibboona alAA<u>a</u>jila<b>ta</b>   </p>
 <p>20.  Nay, (ye men!)<br> But ye love<br> The fleeting life,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/075/075_021.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>21.  Wata<u>th</u>aroona al-<u>a</u>khira<b>ta</b>   </p>
 <p>21.  And leave alone<br> The Hereafter.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/075/075_022.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>22.  Wujoohun yawma-i<u>th</u>in n<u>ad</u>ira<b>tun</b>   </p>
 <p>22.  Some faces, that Day,<br> Will beam (in brightness<br> And beauty);—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/075/075_023.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>23.  Il<u>a</u> rabbih<u>a</u> n<u>a</u><i><u>th</u></i>ira<b>tun</b>   </p>
 <p>23.  Looking towards their Lord;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/075/075_024.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>24.  Wawujoohun yawma-i<u>th</u>in b<u>a</u>sira<b>tun</b>   </p>
 <p>24.  And some faces, that Day,<br> Will be sad and dismal,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/075/075_025.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>25.  Ta<i><u>th</u></i>unnu an yufAAala bih<u>a</u> f<u>a</u>qira<b>tun</b>   </p>
 <p>25.  In the thought that some<br> Back-breaking calamity was about<br> To be indicted on them;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/075/075_026.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>26.  Kall<u>a</u> i<u>tha</u> balaghati a<b>l</b>ttar<u>a</u>qiy<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>26.  Yea, when (the soul)<br> Reaches to the collar-bone<br> (In its exit),</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/075/075_027.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>27.  Waqeela man r<u>a</u>q<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>27.  And there will be a cry,<br> "Who is a magician<br> (To restore him)?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/075/075_028.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>28.  Wa<i><u>th</u></i>anna annahu alfir<u>a</u>q<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>28.  And he will conclude<br> That it was (the Time)<br> Of Parting;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/075/075_029.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>29.  Wa<b>i</b>ltaffati a<b>l</b>ss<u>a</u>qu bi<b>a</b>lss<u>a</u>q<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>29.  And one leg will be<br> Joined with another:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/075/075_030.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>30.  Il<u>a</u> rabbika yawma-i<u>th</u>in almas<u>a</u>q<b>u</b>    </p>
 <p>30.  That Day the Drive<br> Will be (all) to thy Lord!</p> <div class="filenav">
