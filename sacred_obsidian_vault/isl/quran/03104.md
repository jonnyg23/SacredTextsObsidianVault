 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/031/031_031.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>31.  Alam tara anna alfulka tajree fee alba<u>h</u>ri biniAAmati All<u>a</u>hi liyuriyakum min <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tihi inna fee <u>tha</u>lika la<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin likulli <u>s</u>abb<u>a</u>rin shakoor<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>31.  Seest thou not that<br> The ships sail through<br> The Ocean by the grace<br> Of God?—that He may<br> Show you of His Signs?<br> Verily in this are Signs<br> For all who constantly persevere<br> And give thanks.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/031/031_032.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>32.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> ghashiyahum mawjun ka<b>al</b><i><u>thth</u></i>ulali daAAawoo All<u>a</u>ha mukhli<u>s</u>eena lahu a<b>l</b>ddeena falamm<u>a</u> najj<u>a</u>hum il<u>a</u> albarri faminhum muqta<u>s</u>idun wam<u>a</u> yaj<u>h</u>adu bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin<u>a</u> ill<u>a</u> kullu khatt<u>a</u>rin kafoor<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>32.  When a wave covers them<br> Like the canopy (of clouds),<br> They call to God<br> Offering Him sincere devotion.<br>But when He has delivered them<br> Safely to land, there are<br> Among them those that halt<br> Between (right and wrong).<br> But none reject Our Signs<br> Except only a perfidious<br> Ungrateful (wretch)!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/031/031_033.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>33.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>su ittaqoo rabbakum wa<b>i</b>khshaw yawman l<u>a</u> yajzee w<u>a</u>lidun AAan waladihi wal<u>a</u> mawloodun huwa j<u>a</u>zin AAan w<u>a</u>lidihi shay-an inna waAAda All<u>a</u>hi <u>h</u>aqqun fal<u>a</u> taghurrannakumu al<u>h</u>ay<u>a</u>tu a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> wal<u>a</u> yaghurrannakum bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi algharoor<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>33.  O mankind! do your duty<br> To your Lord, and fear<br> (The coming of) a Day<br> When no father can avail<br> Aught for his son, nor<br> A son avail aught<br> For his father.<br> Verily, the promise of God<br> Is true: let not then<br> This present life deceive you,<br> Nor let the Chief Deceiver<br> Deceive you about God.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/031/031_034.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>34.  Inna All<u>a</u>ha AAindahu AAilmu a<b>l</b>ss<u>a</u>AAati wayunazzilu alghaytha wayaAAlamu m<u>a</u> fee al-ar<u>ha</u>mi wam<u>a</u> tadree nafsun m<u>atha</u> taksibu ghadan wam<u>a</u> tadree nafsun bi-ayyi ar<u>d</u>in tamootu inna All<u>a</u>ha AAaleemun khabeer<b>un</b>    </p>
 <p>34.  <br> It is He Who sends down<br> Rain, and He Who knows<br> What is in the wombs.<br>Nor does any one know<br> What it is that he will<br> Earn on the morrow:<br> Nor does any one know<br> In what land he is<br> To die. Verily with God<br> Is full knowledge and He<br> Is acquainted (with all things)</p>  <div class="filenav">
