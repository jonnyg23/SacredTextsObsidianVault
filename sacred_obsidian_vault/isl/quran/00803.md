 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra VIII.: Anfāl, or the Spoils of War.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 3</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/008/008_020.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>20.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo a<u>t</u>eeAAoo All<u>a</u>ha warasoolahu wal<u>a</u> tawallaw AAanhu waantum tasmaAAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>20.  O ye who believe!<br> Obey God and His Apostle,<br> And turn not away from him<br> When ye hear (him speak).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/008/008_021.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>21.  Wal<u>a</u> takoonoo ka<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena q<u>a</u>loo samiAAn<u>a</u> wahum l<u>a</u> yasmaAAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>21.  Nor be like those who say,<br> "We hear," but listen not:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/008/008_022.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>22.  Inna sharra a<b>l</b>ddaw<u>a</u>bbi AAinda All<u>a</u>hi a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>ummu albukmu alla<u>th</u>eena l<u>a</u> yaAAqiloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>22.  For the worst of beasts<br> In the sight of God<br> Are the deaf and the dumb,—<br> Those who understand not.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/008/008_023.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>23.  Walaw AAalima All<u>a</u>hu feehim khayran laasmaAAahum walaw asmaAAahum latawallaw wahum muAAri<u>d</u>oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>23.  If God had found in them<br> Any good, He would indeed<br> Have made them listen:<br> (As it is), if He had made them<br> Listen, they would but have<br> Turned back and declined (faith).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/008/008_024.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>24.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo istajeeboo lill<u>a</u>hi wali<b>l</b>rrasooli i<u>tha</u> daAA<u>a</u>kum lim<u>a</u> yu<u>h</u>yeekum wa<b>i</b>AAlamoo anna All<u>a</u>ha ya<u>h</u>oolu bayna almar-i waqalbihi waannahu ilayhi tu<u>h</u>sharoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>24.  O ye who believe!<br> Give your response to God<br> And His Apostle, when He<br> Calleth you to that which<br> Will give you life;<br> And know that God<br> Cometh in between a man<br> And his heart, and that<br> It is He to Whom<br> Ye shall (all) be gathered.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/008/008_025.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>25.  Wa<b>i</b>ttaqoo fitnatan l<u>a</u> tu<u>s</u>eebanna alla<u>th</u>eena <i><u>th</u></i>alamoo minkum kh<u>ass</u>atan wa<b>i</b>AAlamoo anna All<u>a</u>ha shadeedu alAAiq<u>a</u>b<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>25.  And fear tumult or oppression,<br> Which affecteth not in particular<br> (Only) those of you who do wrong:<br> And know that God<br> Is strict in punishment.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/008/008_026.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>26.  Wa<b>o</b><u>th</u>kuroo i<u>th</u> antum qaleelun musta<u>d</u>AAafoona fee al-ar<u>d</u>i takh<u>a</u>foona an yatakha<u>tt</u>afakumu a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>su fa<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>kum waayyadakum bina<u>s</u>rihi warazaqakum mina a<b>l</b><u>tt</u>ayyib<u>a</u>ti laAAallakum tashkuroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>26.  Call to mind when ye<br> Were a small (band),<br> Despised through the land,<br> And afraid that men might<br> Despoil and kidnap you;<br> But He provided a safe asylum<br> For you, strengthened you<br> With His aid, and gave you<br> Good things for sustenance:<br> That ye might be grateful.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/008/008_027.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>27.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo l<u>a</u> takhoonoo All<u>a</u>ha wa<b>al</b>rrasoola watakhoonoo am<u>a</u>n<u>a</u>tikum waantum taAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>27.  O ye that believe!<br> Betray not the trust<br> Of God and the Apostle,<br> Nor misappropriate knowingly<br> Things entrusted to you.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/008/008_028.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>28.  Wa<b>i</b>AAlamoo annam<u>a</u> amw<u>a</u>lukum waawl<u>a</u>dukum fitnatun waanna All<u>a</u>ha AAindahu ajrun AAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>28.  And know ye<br> That your possessions<br>And your progeny<br> Are but a trial;<br> And that it is God<br> With whom lies<br> Your highest reward.</p> <div class="filenav">
