 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra LVI.: Wāqi‘a, or The Inevitable Event.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 2</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_039.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>39.  Thullatun mina al-awwaleen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>39.  A (goodly) number<br> From those of old,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_040.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>40.  Wathullatun mina al-<u>a</u>khireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>40.  And a (goodly) number<br> From those of later times.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_041.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>41.  Waa<u>s</u>-<u>ha</u>bu a<b>l</b>shshim<u>a</u>li m<u>a</u> a<u>s</u>-<u>ha</u>bu a<b>l</b>shshim<u>a</u>l<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>41.  The Companions of<br> The Left Hand,—<br> What will be<br> The Companions of<br> The Left Hand?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_042.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>42.  Fee samoomin wa<u>h</u>ameem<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>42.  (They will be) in the midst<br> Of a fierce Blast of Fire<br> And in Boiling Water,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_043.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>43.  Wa<i><u>th</u></i>illin min ya<u>h</u>moom<b>in</b>    </p>
 <p>43.  And in the shades<br> Of Black Smoke:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_044.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>44.  L<u>a</u> b<u>a</u>ridin wal<u>a</u> kareem<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>44.  Nothing (will there be)<br> To refresh, nor to please:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_045.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>45.  Innahum k<u>a</u>noo qabla <u>tha</u>lika mutrafeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>45.  For that they were wont<br> To be indulged, before that,<br>In wealth (and luxury),</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_046.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>46.  Wak<u>a</u>noo yu<u>s</u>irroona AAal<u>a</u> al<u>h</u>inthi alAAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>46.  And persisted obstinately<br> In wickedness supreme!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_047.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>47.  Wak<u>a</u>noo yaqooloona a-i<u>tha</u> mitn<u>a</u> wakunn<u>a</u> tur<u>a</u>ban waAAi<i><u>th</u></i><u>a</u>man a-inn<u>a</u> lamabAAoothoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>47.  And they used to say,<br> "What! when we die<br> And become dust and bones,<br> Shall we then indeed<br> Be raised up again?—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_048.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>48.  Awa <u>a</u>b<u>a</u>on<u>a</u> al-awwaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>48.  "(We) and our fathers of old?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_049.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>49.  Qul inna al-awwaleena wa<b>a</b>l-<u>a</u>khireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>49.  Say: "Yea, those of old<br> And those of later times,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_050.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>50.  LamajmooAAoona il<u>a</u> meeq<u>a</u>ti yawmin maAAloom<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>50.  "All will certainly be<br> Gathered together for the meeting<br> Appointed for a Day<br> Well-known.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_051.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>51.  Thumma innakum ayyuh<u>a</u> a<b>l</b><u>dda</u>lloona almuka<u>thth</u>iboon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>51.  "Then will ye truly,—<br> O ye that go wrong,<br> And treat (Truth) as Falsehood!—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_052.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>52.  La<u>a</u>kiloona min shajarin min zaqqoom<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>52.  "Ye will surely taste<br> Of the Tree of Zaqqūm.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_053.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>53.  Fam<u>a</u>li-oona minh<u>a</u> albu<u>t</u>oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>53.  "Then will ye fill<br> Your insides therewith,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_054.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>54.  Fash<u>a</u>riboona AAalayhi mina al<u>h</u>ameem<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>54.  "And drink Boiling Water<br> On top of it:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_055.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>55.  Fash<u>a</u>riboona shurba alheem<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>55.  "Indeed ye shall drink<br> Like diseased camels<br> Raging with thirst!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_056.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>56.  H<u>atha</u> nuzuluhum yawma a<b>l</b>ddeen<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>56.  Such will be their entertainment<br> On the Day of Requital!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_057.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>57.  Na<u>h</u>nu khalaqn<u>a</u>kum falawl<u>a</u> tu<u>s</u>addiqoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>57.  It is We Who have<br> Created you: why will ye<br> Not witness the Truth?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_058.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>58.  Afaraaytum m<u>a</u> tumnoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>58.  Do ye then see?—<br> The (human Seed) that<br> Ye throw out,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_059.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>59.  Aantum takhluqoonahu am na<u>h</u>nu alkh<u>a</u>liqoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>59.  Is it ye who create it,<br> Or are We the Creators?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_060.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>60.  Na<u>h</u>nu qaddarn<u>a</u> baynakumu almawta wam<u>a</u> na<u>h</u>nu bimasbooqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>60.  We have decreed Death<br> To be your common lot,<br> And We are not<br> To be frustrated</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_061.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>61.  AAal<u>a</u> an nubaddila amth<u>a</u>lakum wanunshi-akum fee m<u>a</u> l<u>a</u> taAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>61.  From changing your Forms<br> And creating you (again)<br> In (Forms) that ye know not.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_062.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>62.  Walaqad AAalimtumu a<b>l</b>nnash-ata al-ool<u>a</u> falawl<u>a</u> ta<u>th</u>akkaroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>62.  And ye certainly know already<br> The first form of creation:<br> Why then do ye not<br> Celebrate His praises?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_063.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>63.  Afaraaytum m<u>a</u> ta<u>h</u>ruthoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>63.  See ye the seed that<br> Ye sow in the ground?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_064.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>64.  Aantum tazraAAoonahu am na<u>h</u>nu a<b>l</b>zz<u>a</u>riAAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>64.  Is it ye that cause it<br> To grow, or are We<br> The Cause?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_065.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>65.  Law nash<u>a</u>o lajaAAaln<u>a</u>hu <u>h</u>u<u>ta</u>man fa<i><u>th</u></i>altum tafakkahoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>65.  Were it Our Will,<br> We could crumble it<br> To dry powder, and ye would<br> Be left in wonderment,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_066.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>66.  Inn<u>a</u> lamughramoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>66.  (Saying), "We are indeed<br> Left with debts (for nothing):</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_067.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>67.  Bal na<u>h</u>nu ma<u>h</u>roomoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>67.  "Indeed are we shut out<br> (Of the fruits of our labour)",</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_068.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>68.  Afaraaytumu alm<u>a</u>a alla<u>th</u>ee tashraboon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>68.  See ye the water<br> Which ye drink?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_069.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>69.  Aantum anzaltumoohu mina almuzni am na<u>h</u>nu almunziloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>69.  Do ye bring it Down<br> (In rain) from the Cloud<br> Or do We?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_070.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>70.  Law nash<u>a</u>o jaAAaln<u>a</u>hu oj<u>a</u>jan falawl<u>a</u> tashkuroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>70.  Were it Our Will,<br> We could make it<br>Salt (and unpalatable):<br> Then why do ye not<br> Give thanks?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_071.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>71.  Afaraaytumu a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>ra allatee tooroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>71.  See ye the Fire<br> Which ye kindle?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_072.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>72.  Aantum ansha/tum shajaratah<u>a</u> am na<u>h</u>nu almunshi-oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>72.  Is it ye who grow<br> The tree which feeds<br> The fire, or do We<br> Grow it?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_073.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>73.  Na<u>h</u>nu jaAAaln<u>a</u>h<u>a</u> ta<u>th</u>kiratan wamat<u>a</u>AAan lilmuqween<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>73.  We have made it<br> A memorial (of Our handiwork),<br> And an article of comfort<br> And convenience for<br> The denizens of deserts.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/056/056_074.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>74.  Fasabbi<u>h</u> bi<b>i</b>smi rabbika alAAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>74.  Then celebrate with praises,<br> The name of thy Lord,<br> The Supreme!</p> <div class="filenav">
