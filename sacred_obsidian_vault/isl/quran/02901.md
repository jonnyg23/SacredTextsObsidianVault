 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXIX.: ‘Ankabūt, or the Spider</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/029/029_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  Alif-l<u>a</u>m-meem   </p>
 <p>1.  A. L. M.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/029/029_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  A<u>h</u>asiba a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>su an yutrakoo an yaqooloo <u>a</u>mann<u>a</u> wahum l<u>a</u> yuftanoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>2.  Do men think that<br> They will he left alone<br> On saying, "We believe",<br> And that they will not<br> Be tested?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/029/029_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  Walaqad fatann<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena min qablihim falayaAAlamanna All<u>a</u>hu alla<u>th</u>eena <u>s</u>adaqoo walayaAAlamanna alk<u>ath</u>ibeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>3.  We did test those<br> Before them, and God will<br> Certainly know those who are<br> True from those who are false.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/029/029_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  Am <u>h</u>asiba alla<u>th</u>eena yaAAmaloona a<b>l</b>ssayyi-<u>a</u>ti an yasbiqoon<u>a</u> s<u>a</u>a m<u>a</u> ya<u>h</u>kumoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>4.  Do those who practise<br> Evil think that they<br> Will get the better of us?<br> Evil is their judgment!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/029/029_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  Man k<u>a</u>na yarjoo liq<u>a</u>a All<u>a</u>hi fa-inna ajala All<u>a</u>hi la<u>a</u>tin wahuwa a<b>l</b>ssameeAAu alAAaleem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>5.  For those whose hopes are<br> in the meeting with God<br> (In the Hereafter, let them strive);<br> For the Term (appointed)<br>By God is surely coming:<br> And He hears and knows<br> (All things).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/029/029_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  Waman j<u>a</u>hada fa-innam<u>a</u> yuj<u>a</u>hidu linafsihi inna All<u>a</u>ha laghaniyyun AAani alAA<u>a</u>lameen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>6.  And if any strive (with might<br> And main), they do so<br> For their own souls:<br> For God is free of all<br> Needs from all creation.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/029/029_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  Wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo waAAamiloo a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>li<u>ha</u>ti lanukaffiranna AAanhum sayyi-<u>a</u>tihim walanajziyannahum a<u>h</u>sana alla<u>th</u>ee k<u>a</u>noo yaAAmaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>7.  Whose who believe and work<br> Righteous deeds,—from them<br> Shall We blot out all evil<br> (That may be) in them,<br> And We shall reward<br> Them according to<br> The best of their deeds.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/029/029_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  Wawa<u>ss</u>ayn<u>a</u> al-ins<u>a</u>na biw<u>a</u>lidayhi <u>h</u>usnan wa-in j<u>a</u>had<u>a</u>ka litushrika bee m<u>a</u> laysa laka bihi AAilmun fal<u>a</u> tu<u>t</u>iAAhum<u>a</u> ilayya marjiAAukum faonabbi-okum bim<u>a</u> kuntum taAAmaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>8.  We have enjoined on man<br> Kindness to parents: but if<br> They (either of them) strive<br> (To force) thee to join<br> With Me (in worship)<br> Anything of which thou hast<br> No knowledge, obey them not.<br> Ye have (all) to return<br> To Me, and I will<br> Tell you (the truth)<br> Of all that ye did.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/029/029_009.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>9.  Wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo waAAamiloo a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>li<u>ha</u>ti lanudkhilannahum fee a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>li<u>h</u>een<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>9.  And those who believe<br> And work righteous deeds,—<br> Them shall We admit<br> To the company of the Righteous.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/029/029_010.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>10.  Wamina a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si man yaqoolu <u>a</u>mann<u>a</u> bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi fa-i<u>tha</u> oo<u>th</u>iya fee All<u>a</u>hi jaAAala fitnata a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si kaAAa<u>tha</u>bi All<u>a</u>hi wala-in j<u>a</u>a na<u>s</u>run min rabbika layaqoolunna inn<u>a</u> kunn<u>a</u> maAAakum awa laysa All<u>a</u>hu bi-aAAlama bim<u>a</u> fee <u>s</u>udoori alAA<u>a</u>lameen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>10.  Then there are among men<br> Such as say, "We believe<br> In God"; but when they suffer<br> Affliction in (the cause of) God,<br> They treat men's oppression<br> As if it were the Wrath<br> Of God! And if help<br> Comes (to thee) from thy Lord,<br> They are sure to say,'<br> "We have (always) been<br> With you!" Does not God<br> Know best all that is<br> In the hearts of all Creation?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/029/029_011.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>11.  WalayaAAlamanna All<u>a</u>hu alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo walayaAAlamanna almun<u>a</u>fiqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>11.  And God most certainly knows<br> Those who believe, and as certainly<br> Those who are Hypocrites.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/029/029_012.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>12.  Waq<u>a</u>la alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo lilla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo ittabiAAoo sabeelan<u>a</u> walna<u>h</u>mil kha<u>ta</u>y<u>a</u>kum wam<u>a</u> hum bi<u>ha</u>mileena min kha<u>ta</u>y<u>a</u>hum min shay-in innahum lak<u>ath</u>iboon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>12.  And the Unbelievers say<br> To those who believe:<br> "Follow our path, and we<br> Will bear (the consequences)"<br> Of your faults." Never<br> In the least will they<br> Bear their faults: in fact<br> They are liars!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/029/029_013.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>13.  Walaya<u>h</u>milunna athq<u>a</u>lahum waathq<u>a</u>lan maAAa athq<u>a</u>lihim walayus-alunna yawma alqiy<u>a</u>mati AAamm<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo yaftaroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>13.  They will bear their own<br> Burdens, and (other) burdens<br> Along with their own,<br> And on the Day of Judgment.<br> They will be called to account<br> For their falsehoods.</p> <div class="filenav">
