 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra LXXXI.: Takwīr, or the Folding Up.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/081/081_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  I<u>tha</u> a<b>l</b>shshamsu kuwwirat   </p>
 <p>1.  When the sun<br> (With its spacious light)<br> Is folded up;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/081/081_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> a<b>l</b>nnujoomu inkadarat   </p>
 <p>2.  When the stars<br> Fall, losing their lustre;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/081/081_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> aljib<u>a</u>lu suyyirat   </p>
 <p>3.  When the mountains vanish<br> (Like a mirage);</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/081/081_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> alAAish<u>a</u>ru AAu<u>tt</u>ilat   </p>
 <p>4.  When the she-camels,<br> Ten months with young,<br> Are left untended;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/081/081_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> alwu<u>h</u>ooshu <u>h</u>ushirat   </p>
 <p>5.  When the wild beasts<br> Are herded together<br>(In human habitations);</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/081/081_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> albi<u>ha</u>ru sujjirat   </p>
 <p>6.  When the oceans<br> Boil over with a swell;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/081/081_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> a<b>l</b>nnufoosu zuwwijat   </p>
 <p>7.  When the souls<br> Are sorted out,<br> (Being joined, like with like);</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/081/081_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> almawoodatu su-ilat   </p>
 <p>8.  When the female (infant),<br> Buried alive, is questioned—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/081/081_009.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>9.  Bi-ayyi <u>th</u>anbin qutilat   </p>
 <p>9.  For what crime<br> She was killed;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/081/081_010.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>10.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>u<u>h</u>ufu nushirat   </p>
 <p>10.  When the Scrolls<br> Are laid open;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/081/081_011.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>11.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>o kushi<u>t</u>at   </p>
 <p>11.  When the World on High<br> Is unveiled;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/081/081_012.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>12.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> alja<u>h</u>eemu suAAAAirat   </p>
 <p>12.  When the Blazing Fire<br> Is kindled to fierce heat;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/081/081_013.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>13.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> aljannatu ozlifat   </p>
 <p>13.  And when the Garden<br> Is brought near;—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/081/081_014.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>14.  AAalimat nafsun m<u>a</u> a<u>hd</u>arat   </p>
 <p>14.  (Then) shall each soul know<br> What it has put forward.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/081/081_015.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>15.  Fal<u>a</u> oqsimu bi<b>a</b>lkhunnas<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>15.  So verily I call<br> To witness the Planets—<br> That recede,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/081/081_016.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>16.  Aljaw<u>a</u>ri alkunnas<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>16.  Go straight, or hide;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/081/081_017.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>17.  Wa<b>a</b>llayli i<u>tha</u> AAasAAas<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>17.  And the Night<br> As it dissipates;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/081/081_018.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>18.  Wa<b>al</b><u>ss</u>ub<u>h</u>i i<u>tha</u> tanaffas<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>18.  And the Dawn<br> As it breathes away<br> The darkness;—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/081/081_019.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>19.  Innahu laqawlu rasoolin kareem<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>19.  Verily this is the word<br> Of a most honourable Messenger,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/081/081_020.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>20.  <u>Th</u>ee quwwatin AAinda <u>th</u>ee alAAarshi makeen<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>20.  Endued with Power,<br> With rank before<br> The Lord of the Throne,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/081/081_021.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>21.  Mu<u>ta</u>AAin thamma ameen<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>21.  With authority there,<br> (And) faithful to his trust.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/081/081_022.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>22.  Wam<u>a</u> <u>sah</u>ibukum bimajnoon<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>22.  And (O people!)<br> Your Companion is not<br> One possessed;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/081/081_023.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>23.  Walaqad ra<u>a</u>hu bi<b>a</b>lofuqi almubeen<b>i</b>    </p>
 <p>23.  And without doubt he saw him<br> In the clear horizon.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/081/081_024.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>24.  Wam<u>a</u> huwa AAal<u>a</u> alghaybi bi<u>d</u>aneen<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>24.  Neither doth he withhold<br> Grudgingly a knowledge<br> Of the Unseen.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/081/081_025.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>25.  Wam<u>a</u> huwa biqawli shay<u>ta</u>nin rajeem<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>25.  Nor is it the word<br> Of an evil spirit accursed!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/081/081_026.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>26.  Faayna ta<u>th</u>haboon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>26.  When whither go ye?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/081/081_027.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>27.  In huwa ill<u>a</u> <u>th</u>ikrun lilAA<u>a</u>lameen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>27.  Verily this is no less<br> Than a Message<br> To (all) the Worlds:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/081/081_028.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>28.  Liman sh<u>a</u>a minkum an yastaqeem<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>28.  (With profit) to whoever<br> Among you wills<br> To go straight:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/081/081_029.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>29.  Wam<u>a</u> tash<u>a</u>oona ill<u>a</u> an yash<u>a</u>a All<u>a</u>hu rabbu alAA<u>a</u>lameen<b>a</b>      </p>
 <p>29.  But ye shall not will<br> Except as God wills,—<br> The Cherisher of the Worlds.</p>  <div class="filenav">
