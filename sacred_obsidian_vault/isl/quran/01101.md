 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XI.: Hūd (The Prophet Hūd).</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  Alif-l<u>a</u>m-r<u>a</u> kit<u>a</u>bun o<u>h</u>kimat <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tuhu thumma fu<u>ss</u>ilat min ladun <u>h</u>akeemin khabeer<b>in</b>    </p>
 <p>1.  A. L. R.<br> (This is) a Book,<br> With verses basic or fundamental<br> (Of established meaning),<br> Further explained in detail,—<br> From One Who is Wise<br> And Well-Acquainted (with al] things):</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  All<u>a</u> taAAbudoo ill<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>ha innanee lakum minhu na<u>th</u>eerun wabasheer<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>2.  (It teacheth) that ye should<br> Worship none but God.<br> (Say:) "Verily I am<br> (Sent) unto you from Him<br> To warn and to bring<br> Glad tidings:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  Waani istaghfiroo rabbakum thumma tooboo ilayhi yumattiAAkum mat<u>a</u>AAan <u>h</u>asanan il<u>a</u> ajalin musamman wayu/ti kulla <u>th</u>ee fa<u>d</u>lin fa<u>d</u>lahu wa-in tawallaw fa-inee akh<u>a</u>fu AAalaykum AAa<u>tha</u>ba yawmin kabeer<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>3.  "(And to preach thus), "Seek ye<br> The forgiveness of your Lord,<br> And turn to Him in repentance;<br> That He may grant you<br> Enjoyment, good (and true),<br> For a term appointed,<br> And bestow His abounding grace<br> On all who abound in merit!<br> But if ye turn away,<br> Then I fear for you<br> The Penalty of a Great Day:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  Il<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi marjiAAukum wahuwa AAal<u>a</u> kulli shay-in qadeer<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>4.  "'To God is your return,<br> And He hath power<br> Over all things.'"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  Al<u>a</u> innahum yathnoona <u>s</u>udoorahum liyastakhfoo minhu al<u>a</u> <u>h</u>eena yastaghshoona thiy<u>a</u>bahum yaAAlamu m<u>a</u> yusirroona wam<u>a</u> yuAAlinoona innahu AAaleemun bi<u>tha</u>ti a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>udoor<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>5.  Behold! they fold up<br> Their hearts, that they may lie<br> Hid from Him! Ah! even<br> When they cover themselves<br> With their garments, He knoweth<br> What they conceal, and what<br> They reveal: for He knoweth<br> Well the (inmost secrets)<br> Of the hearts</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  Wam<u>a</u> min d<u>a</u>bbatin fee al-ar<u>d</u>i ill<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi rizquh<u>a</u> wayaAAlamu mustaqarrah<u>a</u> wamustawdaAAah<u>a</u> kullun fee kit<u>a</u>bin mubeen<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>6.  Where is no moving creature<br> On earth but its sustenance<br> Dependeth on God: He knoweth<br> The time and place of its<br> Definite abode and its<br> Temporary deposit:<br> All is in a clear Record.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  Wahuwa alla<u>th</u>ee khalaqa a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>a fee sittati ayy<u>a</u>min wak<u>a</u>na AAarshuhu AAal<u>a</u> alm<u>a</u>-i liyabluwakum ayyukum a<u>h</u>sanu AAamalan wala-in qulta innakum mabAAoothoona min baAAdi almawti layaqoolanna alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo in h<u>atha</u> ill<u>a</u> si<u>h</u>run mubeen<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>7.  He it is Who created<br> The heavens and the earth<br> In six Days—and His Throne<br> Was over the Waters—<br> That He might try you,<br> Which of you is best<br> In conduct. But if<br> Thou wert to say to them,<br> "Ye shall indeed be raised up<br> After death", the Unbelievers<br> Would be sure to say,<br> "This is nothing but<br> Obvious sorcery!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/011/011_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  Wala-in akhkharn<u>a</u> AAanhumu alAAa<u>tha</u>ba il<u>a</u> ommatin maAAdoodatin layaqoolunna m<u>a</u> ya<u>h</u>bisuhu al<u>a</u> yawma ya/teehim laysa ma<u>s</u>roofan AAanhum wa<u>ha</u>qa bihim m<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo bihi yastahzi-oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>8.  If We delay the penalty<br> For them for a definite term,<br> They are sure to say,<br> "What keeps it back?"<br>Ah! On the day it (actually)<br> Reaches them, nothing will.<br> Turn it away from them,<br> And they will be completely<br> Encircled by that which<br> They used to mock at!</p> <div class="filenav">
