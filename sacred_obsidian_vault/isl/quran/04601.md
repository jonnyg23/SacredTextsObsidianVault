 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XLVI.: Aḥqāf, or Winding Sand-tracts.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/046/046_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  <u>Ha</u>-meem   </p>
 <p>1.  Hā-Mīm.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/046/046_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  Tanzeelu alkit<u>a</u>bi mina All<u>a</u>hi alAAazeezi al<u>h</u>akeem<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>2.  The revelation<br> Of the Book<br> Is from God<br> The Exalted in Power,<br> Full of Wisdom.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/046/046_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  M<u>a</u> khalaqn<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>a wam<u>a</u> baynahum<u>a</u> ill<u>a</u> bi<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>aqqi waajalin musamman wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo AAamm<u>a</u> on<u>th</u>iroo muAAri<u>d</u>oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>3.  We created not<br> The heavens and the earth<br> And all between them<br> But for just ends, and<br> For a term appointed:<br> But those who reject Faith<br> Turn away from that<br> Whereof they are warned.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/046/046_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  Qul araaytum m<u>a</u> tadAAoona min dooni All<u>a</u>hi aroonee m<u>atha</u> khalaqoo mina al-ar<u>d</u>i am lahum shirkun fee a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti eetoonee bikit<u>a</u>bin min qabli h<u>atha</u> aw ath<u>a</u>ratin min AAilmin in kuntum <u>sa</u>diqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>4.  Say: "Do ye see<br> What it is ye invoke<br> Besides God? Show me<br> What it is they<br> Have created on earth,<br> Or have they a share<br> In the heavens?<br> Bring me a Book<br>(Revealed) before this,<br> Or any remnant of knowledge<br> (Ye may have), if ye<br> Are telling the truth!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/046/046_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  Waman a<u>d</u>allu mimman yadAAoo min dooni All<u>a</u>hi man l<u>a</u> yastajeebu lahu il<u>a</u> yawmi alqiy<u>a</u>mati wahum AAan duAA<u>a</u>-ihim gh<u>a</u>filoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>5.  And who is more astray<br> Than one who invokes,<br> Besides God, such as will<br> Not answer him to the Day<br> Of Judgment, and who<br> (In fact) are unconscious<br> Of their call (to them)?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/046/046_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> <u>h</u>ushira a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>su k<u>a</u>noo lahum aAAd<u>a</u>an wak<u>a</u>noo biAAib<u>a</u>datihim k<u>a</u>fireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>6.  And when mankind<br> Are gathered together<br> (At the Resurrection),<br> They will be hostile<br> To them and reject<br> Their worship (altogether)!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/046/046_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> tutl<u>a</u> AAalayhim <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tun<u>a</u> bayyin<u>a</u>tin q<u>a</u>la alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo lil<u>h</u>aqqi lamm<u>a</u> j<u>a</u>ahum h<u>atha</u> si<u>h</u>run mubeen<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>7.  When Our Clear Signs<br> Are rehearsed to them,<br> The Unbelievers say,<br> Of the Truth<br> When it comes to them:<br> "This is evident sorcery!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/046/046_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  Am yaqooloona iftar<u>a</u>hu qul ini iftaraytuhu fal<u>a</u> tamlikoona lee mina All<u>a</u>hi shay-an huwa aAAlamu bim<u>a</u> tufee<u>d</u>oona feehi kaf<u>a</u> bihi shaheedan baynee wabaynakum wahuwa alghafooru a<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>eem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>8.  Or do they say,<br> "He has forged it"?<br> Say: "Had I forged it,<br> Then can ye obtain<br> No single (blessing) for me<br> From God. He knows best<br> Of that whereof ye talk<br> (So glibly)! Enough is He<br>For a witness between me<br> And you! And He is<br> Oft-Forgiving, Most Merciful."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/046/046_009.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>9.  Qul m<u>a</u> kuntu bidAAan mina a<b>l</b>rrusuli wam<u>a</u> adree m<u>a</u> yufAAalu bee wal<u>a</u> bikum in attabiAAu ill<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> yoo<u>ha</u> ilayya wam<u>a</u> an<u>a</u> ill<u>a</u> na<u>th</u>eerun mubeen<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>9.  Say: "I am no bringer<br> Of new-fangled doctrine<br> Among the apostles, nor<br> Do I know what will<br> Be done with me or<br> With you. I follow<br> But that which is revealed<br> To me by inspiration;<br> I am but a Warner<br> Open and clear."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/046/046_010.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>10.  Qul araaytum in k<u>a</u>na min AAindi All<u>a</u>hi wakafartum bihi washahida sh<u>a</u>hidun min banee isr<u>a</u>-eela AAal<u>a</u> mithlihi fa<u>a</u>mana wa<b>i</b>stakbartum inna All<u>a</u>ha l<u>a</u> yahdee alqawma a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>10.  Say: "See ye?<br> If (this teaching) be<br> From God, and ye reject it,<br> And a witness from among<br> The Children of Israel testifies<br> To its similarity<br> (With earlier scripture),<br> And has believed<br> While ye are arrogant,<br> (How unjust ye are!)<br> Truly, God guides not<br> A people unjust."</p> <div class="filenav">
