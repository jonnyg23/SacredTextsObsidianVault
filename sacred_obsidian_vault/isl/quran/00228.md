 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra II.: Baqara, or the Heifer.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 28</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_222.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>222.  Wayas-aloonaka AAani alma<u>h</u>ee<u>d</u>i qul huwa a<u>th</u>an fa<b>i</b>AAtaziloo a<b>l</b>nnis<u>a</u>a fee alma<u>h</u>ee<u>d</u>i wal<u>a</u> taqraboohunna <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> ya<u>t</u>hurna fa-i<u>tha</u> ta<u>t</u>ahharna fa/toohunna min <u>h</u>aythu amarakumu All<u>a</u>hu inna All<u>a</u>ha yu<u>h</u>ibbu a<b>l</b>ttaww<u>a</u>beena wayu<u>h</u>ibbu almuta<u>t</u>ahhireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>222.  They ask thee<br> Concerning women's courses.<br> Say: They are<br> A hurt and a pollution:<br>So keep away from women<br> In their courses, and do not<br> Approach them until<br> They are clean.<br> But when they have<br> Purified themselves,<br> Ye may approach them<br> In any manner, time, or place<br> Ordained for you by God.<br> For God loves those<br> Who turn to Him constantly<br> And He loves those<br> Who keep themselves pure and clean,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_223.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>223.  Nis<u>a</u>okum <u>h</u>arthun lakum fa/too <u>h</u>arthakum ann<u>a</u> shi/tum waqaddimoo li-anfusikum wa<b>i</b>ttaqoo All<u>a</u>ha wa<b>i</b>AAlamoo annakum mul<u>a</u>qoohu wabashshiri almu/mineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>223.  Your wives are<br> As a tilth unto you;<br> So approach your tilth<br> When or how ye will;<br> But do some good act<br> For your souls beforehand;<br> And fear God,<br> And know that ye are<br> To meet Him (in the Hereafter),<br> And give (these) good tidings<br> To those who believe.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_224.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>224.  Wal<u>a</u> tajAAaloo All<u>a</u>ha AAur<u>d</u>atan li-aym<u>a</u>nikum an tabarroo watattaqoo watu<u>s</u>li<u>h</u>oo bayna a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu sameeAAun AAaleem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>224.  And make not<br> God's (name) an excuse<br> In your oaths against<br> Doing good, or acting rightly,<br> Or making peace<br> Between persons;<br>For God is One<br> Who heareth and knoweth<br> All things.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_225.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>225.  L<u>a</u> yu-<u>a</u>khi<u>th</u>ukumu All<u>a</u>hu bi<b>a</b>llaghwi fee aym<u>a</u>nikum wal<u>a</u>kin yu-<u>a</u>khi<u>th</u>ukum bim<u>a</u> kasabat quloobukum wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu ghafoorun <u>h</u>aleem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>225.  God will not<br> Call you to account<br> For thoughtlessness<br> In your oaths,<br> But for the intention<br> In your hearts;<br> And He is<br> Oft-forgiving<br> Most Forbearing.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_226.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>226.  Lilla<u>th</u>eena yu/loona min nis<u>a</u>-ihim tarabbu<u>s</u>u arbaAAati ashhurin fa-in f<u>a</u>oo fa-inna All<u>a</u>ha ghafoorun ra<u>h</u>eem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>226.  For those who take<br> An oath for abstention<br> From their wives,<br> A waiting for four months<br> Is ordained;<br> If then they return,<br> God is Oft-forgiving,<br> Most Merciful.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_227.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>227.  Wa-in AAazamoo a<b>l</b><u>tt</u>al<u>a</u>qa fa-inna All<u>a</u>ha sameeAAun AAaleem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>227.  But if their intention<br> Is firm for divorce,<br> God heareth<br> And knoweth all things.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/002/002_228.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>228.  Wa<b>a</b>lmu<u>t</u>allaq<u>a</u>tu yatarabba<u>s</u>na bi-anfusihinna thal<u>a</u>thata quroo-in wal<u>a</u> ya<u>h</u>illu lahunna an yaktumna m<u>a</u> khalaqa All<u>a</u>hu fee ar<u>ha</u>mihinna in kunna yu/minna bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi wa<b>a</b>lyawmi al-<u>a</u>khiri wabuAAoolatuhunna a<u>h</u>aqqu biraddihinna fee <u>tha</u>lika in ar<u>a</u>doo i<u>s</u>l<u>ah</u>an walahunna mithlu alla<u>th</u>ee AAalayhinna bi<b>a</b>lmaAAroofi wali<b>l</b>rrij<u>a</u>li AAalayhinna darajatun wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu AAazeezun <u>h</u>akeem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>228.  Divorced women<br> Shall wait concerning themselves<br>For three monthly periods.<br> Nor is it lawful for them<br> To hide what God<br> Hath created in their wombs,<br> If they have faith<br> In God and the Last Day.<br> And their husbands<br> Have the better right<br> To take them back<br> In that period, if<br> They wish for reconciliation.<br> And women shall have rights<br> Similar to the rights<br> Against them, according<br> To what is equitable;<br> But men have a degree<br> (Of advantage) over them.<br> And God is Exalted in Power, Wise.</p> <div class="filenav">
