 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XLVII.: Muḥammad (the Prophet).</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 4</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/047/047_029.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>29.  Am <u>h</u>asiba alla<u>th</u>eena fee quloobihim mara<u>d</u>un an lan yukhrija All<u>a</u>hu a<u>d</u>gh<u>a</u>nahum    </p>
 <p>29.  Or do those in whose<br> Hearts is a disease, think<br> That God will not bring<br> To light all their rancour?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/047/047_030.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>30.  Walaw nash<u>a</u>o laarayn<u>a</u>kahum falaAAaraftahum biseem<u>a</u>hum walataAArifannahum fee la<u>h</u>ni alqawli wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu yaAAlamu aAAm<u>a</u>lakum    </p>
 <p>30.  Had We so willed,<br> We could have shown them<br> Up to thee, and thou<br> Shouldst have known them<br> By their marks: but surely<br> Thou wilt know them<br> By the tone of their speech!<br> And God knows<br> All that ye do.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/047/047_031.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>31.  Walanabluwannakum <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> naAAlama almuj<u>a</u>hideena minkum wa<b>al</b><u>ssa</u>bireena wanabluwa akhb<u>a</u>rakum   </p>
 <p>31.  And We shall try you<br> Until We test those<br> Among you who strive<br> Their utmost and persevere<br> In patience; and We shall<br> Try your reported (mettle).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/047/047_032.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>32.  Inna alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo wa<u>s</u>addoo AAan sabeeli All<u>a</u>hi wash<u>a</u>qqoo a<b>l</b>rrasoola min baAAdi m<u>a</u> tabayyana lahumu alhud<u>a</u> lan ya<u>d</u>uroo All<u>a</u>ha shay-an wasayu<u>h</u>bi<u>t</u>u aAAm<u>a</u>lahum   </p>
 <p>32.  Those who reject God,<br> Hinder (men) from<br> The Path of God, and resist<br> The Apostle, after Guidance<br>Has been clearly shown to them,<br> Will not injure God<br> In the least, but He<br> Will make their deeds<br> Of no effect.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/047/047_033.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>33.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo a<u>t</u>eeAAoo All<u>a</u>ha waa<u>t</u>eeAAoo a<b>l</b>rrasoola wal<u>a</u> tub<u>t</u>iloo aAAm<u>a</u>lakum   </p>
 <p>33.  O ye who believe!<br> Obey God, and obey<br> The Apostle, and make<br> Not vain your deeds!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/047/047_034.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>34.  Inna alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo wa<u>s</u>addoo AAan sabeeli All<u>a</u>hi thumma m<u>a</u>too wahum kuff<u>a</u>run falan yaghfira All<u>a</u>hu lahum   </p>
 <p>34.  Those who reject God,<br> And hinder (men) from the Path<br> Of God, then die rejecting God,—<br> God will not forgive them.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/047/047_035.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>35.  Fal<u>a</u> tahinoo watadAAoo il<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>ssalmi waantumu al-aAAlawna wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu maAAakum walan yatirakum aAAm<u>a</u>lakum   </p>
 <p>35.  Be not weary and<br> Faint-hearted, crying for peace,<br> When ye should be<br> Uppermost: for God is<br> With you, and will never<br> Put you in loss<br> For your (good) deeds.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/047/047_036.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>36.  Innam<u>a</u> al<u>h</u>ay<u>a</u>tu a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> laAAibun walahwun wa-in tu/minoo watattaqoo yu/tikum ojoorakum wal<u>a</u> yas-alkum amw<u>a</u>lakum   </p>
 <p>36.  The life of this world<br> Is but play and amusement:<br> And if ye believe<br> And guard against evil,<br> He will grant you<br> Your recompense, and will not<br>Ask you (to give up)<br> Your possessions.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/047/047_037.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>37.  In yas-alkumooh<u>a</u> fayu<u>h</u>fikum tabkhaloo wayukhrij a<u>d</u>gh<u>a</u>nakum   </p>
 <p>37.  If He were to ask you<br> For all of them, and<br> Press you, ye would<br> Covetously withhold, and He would<br> Bring out all your ill-feeling.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/047/047_038.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>38.  H<u>a</u> antum h<u>a</u>ol<u>a</u>-i tudAAawna litunfiqoo fee sabeeli All<u>a</u>hi faminkum man yabkhalu waman yabkhal fa-innam<u>a</u> yabkhalu AAan nafsihi wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu alghaniyyu waantumu alfuqar<u>a</u>o wa-in tatawallaw yastabdil qawman ghayrakum thumma l<u>a</u> yakoonoo amth<u>a</u>lakum   </p>
 <p>38.  Behold, ye are those<br> Invited to spend<br> (Of your substance)<br> In the Way of God:<br> But among you are some<br> That are niggardly. But any<br> Who are niggardly are so<br> At the expense of<br> Their own souls.<br> But God is free<br> Of all wants,<br> And it is ye that are needy.<br> If ye turn back<br> (From the Path), He will<br> Substitute in your stead<br> Another people; then they<br> Would not be like you!</p>  <div class="filenav">
