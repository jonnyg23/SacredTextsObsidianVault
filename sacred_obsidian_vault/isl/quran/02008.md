 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_129.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>129.  Walawl<u>a</u> kalimatun sabaqat min rabbika lak<u>a</u>na liz<u>a</u>man waajalun musamm<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>129.  Had it not been<br> For a Word that went forth<br> Before from thy Lord,<br> (Their punishment) must necessarily<br> Have come; but there is<br> A term appointed (for respite).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_130.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>130.  Fa<b>i</b><u>s</u>bir AAal<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> yaqooloona wasabbi<u>h</u> bi<u>h</u>amdi rabbika qabla <u>t</u>ulooAAi a<b>l</b>shshamsi waqabla ghuroobih<u>a</u> wamin <u>a</u>n<u>a</u>-i allayli fasabbi<u>h</u> waa<u>t</u>r<u>a</u>fa a<b>l</b>nnah<u>a</u>ri laAAallaka tar<u>da</u>   </p>
 <p>130.  Therefore be patient with what<br> They say, and celebrate (constantly)<br> The praises of thy Lord,<br> Before the rising of the sun,<br> And before its setting;<br> Yea, celebrate them<br> For part of the hours<br> Of the night, and at the sides<br> Of the day: that thou<br> Mayest have (spiritual) joy.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_131.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>131.  Wal<u>a</u> tamuddanna AAaynayka il<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> mattaAAn<u>a</u> bihi azw<u>a</u>jan minhum zahrata al<u>h</u>ay<u>a</u>ti a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> linaftinahum feehi warizqu rabbika khayrun waabq<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>131.  Nor strain thine eyes in longing<br> For the things We have given<br> For enjoyment to parties ,<br> Of them, the splendour<br>Of the life of this world,<br> Through which We test them:<br> But the provision of thy Lord<br> Is better and more enduring.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_132.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>132.  Wa/mur ahlaka bi<b>al</b><u>ss</u>al<u>a</u>ti wa<b>i</b><u>st</u>abir AAalayh<u>a</u> l<u>a</u> nas-aluka rizqan na<u>h</u>nu narzuquka wa<b>a</b>lAA<u>a</u>qibatu li<b>l</b>ttaqw<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>132.  Enjoin prayer on thy people,<br> And be constant therein.<br> We ask thee not to provide<br> Sustenance: We provide it<br> For thee. But the (fruit of)<br> The Hereafter is for Righteousness.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_133.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>133.  Waq<u>a</u>loo lawl<u>a</u> ya/teen<u>a</u> bi-<u>a</u>yatin min rabbihi awa lam ta/tihim bayyinatu m<u>a</u> fee a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>u<u>h</u>ufi al-ool<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>133.  ''They say: "Why does he not<br> Bring us a Sign from<br> His Lord?" Has not<br> A Clear Sign come to them<br> Of all that was<br> In the former Books<br> Of revelation?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_134.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>134.  Walaw ann<u>a</u> ahlakn<u>a</u>hum biAAa<u>tha</u>bin min qablihi laq<u>a</u>loo rabban<u>a</u> lawl<u>a</u> arsalta ilayn<u>a</u> rasoolan fanattabiAAa <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tika min qabli an na<u>th</u>illa wanakhz<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>134.  And if We had inflicted<br> On them a penalty before this,<br> They would have said:<br> "Our Lord! If only Thou<br> Hadst sent us an apostle,<br> We should certainly have followed<br>Thy Signs before we were<br> Humbled and put to shame."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_135.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>135.  Qul kullun mutarabbi<u>s</u>un fatarabba<u>s</u>oo fasataAAlamoona man a<u>s</u>-<u>ha</u>bu a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>ir<u>at</u>i a<b>l</b>ssawiyyi wamani ihtad<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>135.  Say: "Each one (of us)<br> Is waiting: wait ye, therefore,<br> And soon shall ye know<br> Who it is that is<br> On the straight and even<br> Way, and who it is<br> That has received guidance."</p>  <div class="filenav">
