 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXIX.: ‘Ankabūt, or the Spider</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 7</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/029/029_064.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>64.  Wam<u>a</u> h<u>ath</u>ihi al<u>h</u>ay<u>a</u>tu a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> ill<u>a</u> lahwun walaAAibun wa-inna a<b>l</b>dd<u>a</u>ra al-<u>a</u>khirata lahiya al<u>h</u>ayaw<u>a</u>nu law k<u>a</u>noo yaAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>64.  What is the life of this world<br> But amusement and play?<br> But verily the Home<br> In the Hereafter,—that is<br> Life indeed, if they but knew.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/029/029_065.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>65.  Fa-i<u>tha</u> rakiboo fee alfulki daAAawoo All<u>a</u>ha mukhli<u>s</u>eena lahu a<b>l</b>ddeena falamm<u>a</u> najj<u>a</u>hum il<u>a</u> albarri i<u>tha</u> hum yushrikoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>65.  Now, if they embark<br> On a boat, they call<br> On God, making their devotion<br> Sincerely (and exclusively) to Him;<br> But when He has delivered<br> Them safely to (dry) land,<br> Behold, they give a share<br> (Of their worship to others)!—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/029/029_066.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>66.  Liyakfuroo bim<u>a</u> <u>a</u>tayn<u>a</u>hum waliyatamattaAAoo fasawfa yaAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>66.  Disdaining ungratefully Our gifts,<br> And giving themselves up<br> To (worldly) enjoyment! But soon<br> Will they know.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/029/029_067.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>67.  Awa lam yaraw ann<u>a</u> jaAAaln<u>a</u> <u>h</u>araman <u>a</u>minan wayutakha<u>tt</u>afu a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>su min <u>h</u>awlihim afabi<b>a</b>lb<u>at</u>ili yu/minoona wabiniAAmati All<u>a</u>hi yakfuroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>67.  Do they not then see<br> That We have made<br> A Sanctuary secure, and that<br> Men are being snatched away<br> From all around them?<br> Then, do they believe in that<br>Which is vain, and reject<br> The Grace of God?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/029/029_068.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>68.  Waman a<i><u>th</u></i>lamu mimmani iftar<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi ka<u>th</u>iban aw ka<u>thth</u>aba bi<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>aqqi lamm<u>a</u> j<u>a</u>ahu alaysa fee jahannama mathwan lilk<u>a</u>fireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>68.  And who does more wrong<br> Than he who invents<br> A lie against God<br> Or rejects the Truth<br> When it reaches him?<br> Is there not a home<br> In Hell for those who<br> Reject Faith?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/029/029_069.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>69.  Wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena j<u>a</u>hadoo feen<u>a</u> lanahdiyannahum subulan<u>a</u> wa-inna All<u>a</u>ha lamaAAa almu<u>h</u>sineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>69.  And those who strive<br> In Our (Cause),—We will<br> Certainly guide them<br> To Our Paths:<br> For verily God<br> Is with those<br> Who do right.</p>  <div class="filenav">
