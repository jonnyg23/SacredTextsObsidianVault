 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra IV.: Nisāa, or The Women.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 18</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_116.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>116.  Inna All<u>a</u>ha l<u>a</u> yaghfiru an yushraka bihi wayaghfiru m<u>a</u> doona <u>tha</u>lika liman yash<u>a</u>o waman yushrik bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi faqad <u>d</u>alla <u>d</u>al<u>a</u>lan baAAeed<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>116.  God forgiveth not<br> (The sin of) joining other gods<br> With Him; but He forgiveth<br> Whom He pleaseth other sins<br> Than this: one who joins<br> Other gods with God,<br> Hath strayed far, far away<br> (From the Right).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_117.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>117.  In yadAAoona min doonihi ill<u>a</u> in<u>a</u>than wa-in yadAAoona ill<u>a</u> shay<u>ta</u>nan mareed<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>117.  (The Pagans), leaving Him,<br> Call but upon female deities:<br> They call but upon Satan<br> The persistent rebel!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_118.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>118.  LaAAanahu All<u>a</u>hu waq<u>a</u>la laattakhi<u>th</u>anna min AAib<u>a</u>dika na<u>s</u>eeban mafroo<u>da</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>118.  God did curse him,<br> But he said: "I will take<br> Of Thy servants a portion<br> Marked off;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_119.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>119.  Walao<u>d</u>illannahum walaomanniyannahum wala<u>a</u>murannahum falayubattikunna <u>atha</u>na al-anAA<u>a</u>mi wala<u>a</u>murannahum falayughayyirunna khalqa All<u>a</u>hi waman yattakhi<u>th</u>i a<b>l</b>shshay<u>ta</u>na waliyyan min dooni All<u>a</u>hi faqad khasira khusr<u>a</u>nan mubeen<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>119.  "I will mislead them,<br> And I will create<br>In them false desires; I will<br> Order them to slit the ears<br> Of cattle, and to deface<br> The (fair) nature created<br> By God." Whoever,<br> Forsaking God, takes Satan<br> For a friend, hath<br> Of a surety suffered<br> A loss that is manifest.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_120.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>120.  YaAAiduhum wayumanneehim wam<u>a</u> yaAAiduhumu a<b>l</b>shshay<u>ta</u>nu ill<u>a</u> ghuroor<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>120.  Satan makes them promises,<br> And creates in them false desires;<br> But Satan's promises<br> Are nothing but deception.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_121.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>121.  Ol<u>a</u>-ika ma/w<u>a</u>hum jahannamu wal<u>a</u> yajidoona AAanh<u>a</u> ma<u>h</u>ee<u>sa</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>121.  They (his dupes)<br> Will have their dwelling<br> In Hell, and from it<br> They will find no way<br> Of escape.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_122.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>122.  Wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo waAAamiloo a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>li<u>ha</u>ti sanudkhiluhum jann<u>a</u>tin tajree min ta<u>h</u>tih<u>a</u> al-anh<u>a</u>ru kh<u>a</u>lideena feeh<u>a</u> abadan waAAda All<u>a</u>hi <u>h</u>aqqan waman a<u>s</u>daqu mina All<u>a</u>hi qeel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>122.  But those who believe<br> And do deeds of righteousness,—<br> We shall soon admit them<br> To Gardens, with rivers<br> Flowing beneath,—to dwell<br> Therein for ever.<br> God's promise is the truth,<br> And whose word can be<br> Truer than God's?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_123.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>123.  Laysa bi-am<u>a</u>niyyikum wal<u>a</u> am<u>a</u>niyyi ahli alkit<u>a</u>bi man yaAAmal soo-an yujza bihi wal<u>a</u> yajid lahu min dooni All<u>a</u>hi waliyyan wal<u>a</u> na<u>s</u>eer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>123.  Not your desires, nor those<br> Of the People of the Book<br>(Can prevail): whoever<br> Works evil, will be<br> Requited accordingly.<br> Nor will he find, besides God,<br> Any protector or helper.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_124.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>124.  Waman yaAAmal mina a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>li<u>ha</u>ti min <u>th</u>akarin aw onth<u>a</u> wahuwa mu/minun faol<u>a</u>-ika yadkhuloona aljannata wal<u>a</u> yu<i><u>th</u></i>lamoona naqeer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>124.  If any do deeds<br> Of righteousness,<br> Be they male or female—<br> And have faith,<br> They will enter Heaven,<br> And not the least injustice<br> Will be done to them.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_125.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>125.  Waman a<u>h</u>sanu deenan mimman aslama wajhahu lill<u>a</u>hi wahuwa mu<u>h</u>sinun wa<b>i</b>ttabaAAa millata ibr<u>a</u>heema <u>h</u>aneefan wa<b>i</b>ttakha<u>th</u>a All<u>a</u>hu ibr<u>a</u>heema khaleel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>125.  Who can be better<br> In religion than one<br> Who submits his whole self<br> To God, does good,<br> And follows the way<br> Of Abraham the true in faith?<br> For God did take<br> Abraham for a friend.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_126.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>126.  Walill<u>a</u>hi m<u>a</u> fee a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wam<u>a</u> fee al-ar<u>d</u>i wak<u>a</u>na All<u>a</u>hu bikulli shay-in mu<u>h</u>ee<u>ta</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>126.  But to God belong all things<br> In the heavens and on earth:<br> And He it is that<br> Encompasseth all things.</p> <div class="filenav">
