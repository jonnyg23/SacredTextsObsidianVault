 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XCVII.: Qadr, or The Night of Power (or Honour).</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/097/097_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  Inn<u>a</u> anzaln<u>a</u>hu fee laylati alqadr<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>1.  We have indeed revealed<br> This (Message)<br> In the Night of Power:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/097/097_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  Wam<u>a</u> adr<u>a</u>ka m<u>a</u> laylatu alqadr<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>2.  And what will explain<br> To thee what the Night<br> Of Power is?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/097/097_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  Laylatu alqadri khayrun min alfi shahr<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>3.  The Night of Power<br> Is better than<br> A thousand Months,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/097/097_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  Tanazzalu almal<u>a</u>-ikatu wa<b>al</b>rroo<u>h</u>u feeh<u>a</u> bi-i<u>th</u>ni rabbihim min kulli amr<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>4.  Therein come dawn<br> The angels and the Spirit<br> By God's permission,<br> On every errand:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/097/097_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  Sal<u>a</u>mun hiya <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> ma<u>t</u>laAAi alfajr<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>5.  Peace!…This<br> Until the rise of Morn!</p>  <div class="filenav">
