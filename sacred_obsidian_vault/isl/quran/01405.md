 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XIV.: Ibrāhīm, or Abraham.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 5</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/014/014_028.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>28.  Alam tara il<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena baddaloo niAAmata All<u>a</u>hi kufran waa<u>h</u>alloo qawmahum d<u>a</u>ra albaw<u>a</u>r<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>28.  <br> Into blasphemy and caused<br> Their people to descend<br> To the House of Perdition?—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/014/014_029.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>29.  Jahannama ya<u>s</u>lawnah<u>a</u> wabi/sa alqar<u>a</u>r<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>29.  Into Hell? They will burn<br> Therein,—an evil place<br> To stay in!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/014/014_030.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>30.  WajaAAaloo lill<u>a</u>hi and<u>a</u>dan liyu<u>d</u>illoo AAan sabeelihi qul tamattaAAoo fa-inna ma<u>s</u>eerakum il<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>r<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>30.  And they set up (idols)<br> As equal to God, to mislead<br> (Men) from the Path! Say:<br> "Enjoy (your brief power)!<br> But verily ye are making<br> Straightway for Hell!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/014/014_031.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>31.  Qul liAAib<u>a</u>diya alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo yuqeemoo a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>al<u>a</u>ta wayunfiqoo mimm<u>a</u> razaqn<u>a</u>hum sirran waAAal<u>a</u>niyatan min qabli an ya/tiya yawmun l<u>a</u> bayAAun feehi wal<u>a</u> khil<u>a</u>l<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>31.  Speak to my servants<br> Who have believed,<br> That they may establish<br> Regular prayers, and spend<br> (In charity) out of the Sustenance<br>We have given them,<br> Secretly and openly, before<br> The coming of a Day<br> In which there will be<br> Neither mutual bargaining<br> Nor befriending.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/014/014_032.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>32.  All<u>a</u>hu alla<u>th</u>ee khalaqa a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>a waanzala mina a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>-i m<u>a</u>an faakhraja bihi mina a<b>l</b>ththamar<u>a</u>ti rizqan lakum wasakhkhara lakumu alfulka litajriya fee alba<u>h</u>ri bi-amrihi wasakhkhara lakumu al-anh<u>a</u>r<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>32.  It is God Who hath created<br> The heavens and the earth<br> And sendeth down rain<br> From the skies, and with it<br> Bringeth out fruits wherewith<br> To feed you; it is He<br> Who hath made the ships subject<br> To you, that they may sail<br> Through the sea by His Command;<br> And the rivers (also)<br> Hath He made subject to you.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/014/014_033.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>33.  Wasakhkhara lakumu a<b>l</b>shshamsa wa<b>a</b>lqamara d<u>a</u>-ibayni wasakhkhara lakumu allayla wa<b>al</b>nnah<u>a</u>r<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>33.  And He hath made subject<br> To you the sun and the moon,<br> Both diligently pursuing<br> Their courses; and the Night<br> And the Day hath He (also)<br> Made subject to you.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/014/014_034.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>34.  Wa<u>a</u>t<u>a</u>kum min kulli m<u>a</u> saaltumoohu wa-in taAAuddoo niAAmata All<u>a</u>hi l<u>a</u> tu<u>hs</u>ooh<u>a</u> inna al-ins<u>a</u>na la<i><u>th</u></i>aloomun kaff<u>a</u>r<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>34.  And He giveth you<br> Of all that ye ask for.<br> But if ye count the favours<br> Of God, never will ye<br> Be able to number them.<br> Verily, man is given up<br> To injustice and ingratitude.</p> <div class="filenav">
