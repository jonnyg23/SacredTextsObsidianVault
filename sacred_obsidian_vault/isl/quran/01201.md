 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XII.: Yūsuf, or Joseph.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  Alif-l<u>a</u>m-r<u>a</u> tilka <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tu alkit<u>a</u>bi almubeen<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>1.  A. L. R. These are<br> The Symbols (or Verses)<br> Of the Perspicuous Book.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  Inn<u>a</u> anzaln<u>a</u>hu qur-<u>a</u>nan AAarabiyyan laAAallakum taAAqiloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>2.  We have sent it down<br> As an Arabic Qur-ān,<br> In order that ye may<br> Learn wisdom.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  Na<u>h</u>nu naqu<u>ss</u>u AAalayka a<u>h</u>sana alqa<u>s</u>a<u>s</u>i bim<u>a</u> aw<u>h</u>ayn<u>a</u> ilayka h<u>atha</u> alqur-<u>a</u>na wa-in kunta min qablihi lamina algh<u>a</u>fileen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>3.  We do relate unto thee<br> The most beautiful of stories,<br> In that We reveal to thee<br> This (portion of the) Qur-ān:<br> Before this, thou too<br> Was among those<br> Who knew it not.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  I<u>th</u> q<u>a</u>la yoosufu li-abeehi y<u>a</u> abati innee raaytu a<u>h</u>ada AAashara kawkaban wa<b>al</b>shshamsa wa<b>a</b>lqamara raaytuhum lee s<u>a</u>jideen<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>4.  Behold, Joseph said<br> To his father: "O my father!<br>I did see eleven stars<br> And the sun and the moon:<br> I saw them prostrate themselves<br> To me!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  Q<u>a</u>la y<u>a</u> bunayya l<u>a</u> taq<u>s</u>u<u>s</u> ru/y<u>a</u>ka AAal<u>a</u> ikhwatika fayakeedoo laka kaydan inna a<b>l</b>shshay<u>ta</u>na lil-ins<u>a</u>ni AAaduwwun mubeen<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>5.  Said (the father):<br> "My (dear) little son!<br> Relate not thy vision<br> To thy brothers, lest they<br> Concoct a plot against thee:<br> For Satan is to man<br> An avowed enemy!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/012/012_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  Waka<u>tha</u>lika yajtabeeka rabbuka wayuAAallimuka min ta/weeli al-a<u>ha</u>deethi wayutimmu niAAmatahu AAalayka waAAal<u>a</u> <u>a</u>li yaAAqooba kam<u>a</u> atammah<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> abawayka min qablu ibr<u>a</u>heema wa-is<u>ha</u>qa inna rabbaka AAaleemun <u>h</u>akeem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>6.  "Thus will thy Lord<br> Choose thee and teach thee<br> The interpretation of stories (and events)<br> And perfect His favour<br> To thee and to the posterity<br> Of Jacob—even as He<br> Perfected it to thy fathers<br> Abraham and Isaac aforetime!<br> For God is full of knowledge<br> And wisdom."</p> <div class="filenav">
