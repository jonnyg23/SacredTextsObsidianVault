 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XL.: Mū-min, or The Believer.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 6</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/040/040_051.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>51.  Inn<u>a</u> lanan<u>s</u>uru rusulan<u>a</u> wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo fee al<u>h</u>ay<u>a</u>ti a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> wayawma yaqoomu al-ashh<u>a</u>d<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>51.  We will, without doubt,<br> Help Our apostles and those<br> Who believe, (both)<br> In this world's life<br> And on the Day<br> When the Witnesses<br> Will stand forth,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/040/040_052.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>52.  Yawma l<u>a</u> yanfaAAu a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limeena maAA<u>th</u>iratuhum walahumu allaAAnatu walahum soo-o a<b>l</b>dd<u>a</u>r<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>52.  The Day when no profit<br> Will it be to Wrong-doers<br> To present their excuses,<br> But they will (only) have<br> The Curse and the Home<br> Of Misery.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/040/040_053.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>53.  Walaqad <u>a</u>tayn<u>a</u> moos<u>a</u> alhud<u>a</u> waawrathn<u>a</u> banee isr<u>a</u>-eela alkit<u>a</u>b<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>53.  We did aforetime give Moses<br> The (Book of) Guidance,<br> And We gave the Book<br> In inheritance to the Children<br> Of Israel,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/040/040_054.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>54.  Hudan wa<u>th</u>ikr<u>a</u> li-olee al-alb<u>a</u>b<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>54.  A Guide and a Message<br> To men of understanding.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/040/040_055.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>55.  Fa<b>i</b><u>s</u>bir inna waAAda All<u>a</u>hi <u>h</u>aqqun wa<b>i</b>staghfir li<u>th</u>anbika wasabbi<u>h</u> bi<u>h</u>amdi rabbika bi<b>a</b>lAAashiyyi wa<b>a</b>l-ibk<u>a</u>r<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>55.  Patiently, then, persevere:<br> For the Promise of God<br> Is true: and ask forgiveness<br> For thy fault, and celebrate<br>The Praises of thy Lord<br> In the evening<br> And in the morning.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/040/040_056.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>56.  Inna alla<u>th</u>eena yuj<u>a</u>diloona fee <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>ti All<u>a</u>hi bighayri sul<u>ta</u>nin at<u>a</u>hum in fee <u>s</u>udoorihim ill<u>a</u> kibrun m<u>a</u> hum bib<u>a</u>ligheehi fa<b>i</b>staAAi<u>th</u> bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi innahu huwa a<b>l</b>ssameeAAu alba<u>s</u>eer<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>56.  Those who dispute<br> About the Signs of God<br> Without any authority<br> Bestowed on them,—there is<br> Nothing in their breasts<br> But (the quest of) greatness,<br> Which they shall never<br> Attain to: seek refuge,<br> Then, in God: it is He<br> Who hears and sees (all things).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/040/040_057.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>57.  Lakhalqu a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i akbaru min khalqi a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si wal<u>a</u>kinna akthara a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si l<u>a</u> yaAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>57.  Assuredly the creation<br> Of the heavens<br> And the earth<br> Is a greater (matter)<br> Than the creation of men:<br> Yet most men understand not.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/040/040_058.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>58.  Wam<u>a</u> yastawee al-aAAm<u>a</u> wa<b>a</b>lba<u>s</u>eeru wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo waAAamiloo a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>li<u>ha</u>ti wal<u>a</u> almusee-o qaleelan m<u>a</u> tata<u>th</u>akkaroon<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>58.  Not equal are the blind<br> And those who (clearly) see:<br> Nor are (equal) those<br> Who believe and work<br> Deeds of righteousness, and<br> Those who do evil.<br> Little do ye learn<br> By admonition!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/040/040_059.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>59.  Inna a<b>l</b>ss<u>a</u>AAata la<u>a</u>tiyatun l<u>a</u> rayba feeh<u>a</u> wal<u>a</u>kinna akthara a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si l<u>a</u> yu/minoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>59.  The Hour will certainly come:<br> Therein is no doubt:<br> Yet most men believe not.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/040/040_060.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>60.  Waq<u>a</u>la rabbukumu odAAoonee astajib lakum inna alla<u>th</u>eena yastakbiroona AAan AAib<u>a</u>datee sayadkhuloona jahannama d<u>a</u>khireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>60.  And your Lord says:<br> "Call on Me; I<br> Will answer your (Prayer):<br> But those who are<br> Too arrogant to serve Me<br> Will surely find themselves<br> In Hell—in humiliation!"</p> <div class="filenav">
