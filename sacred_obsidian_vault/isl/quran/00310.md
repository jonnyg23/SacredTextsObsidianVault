 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra III.: Āl-i-’Imrān, or The Family of ’Imrān.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 10</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_092.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>92.  Lan tan<u>a</u>loo albirra <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> tunfiqoo mimm<u>a</u> tu<u>h</u>ibboona wam<u>a</u> tunfiqoo min shay-in fa-inna All<u>a</u>ha bihi AAaleem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>92.  By no means shall ye<br> Attain righteousness unless<br> Ye give (freely) of that<br> Which ye love; and whatever<br> Ye give, of a truth<br> God knoweth it well.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_093.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>93.  Kullu a<b>l</b><u>tt</u>aAA<u>a</u>mi k<u>a</u>na <u>h</u>illan libanee isr<u>a</u>-eela ill<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> <u>h</u>arrama isr<u>a</u>-eelu AAal<u>a</u> nafsihi min qabli an tunazzala a<b>l</b>ttawr<u>a</u>tu qul fa/too bi<b>al</b>ttawr<u>a</u>ti fa<b>o</b>tlooh<u>a</u> in kuntum <u>sa</u>diqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>93.  All food was lawful<br> To the Children of Israel,<br> Except what Israel<br> Made unlawful for itself,<br> Before the Law (of Moses)<br> Was revealed. Say:<br> "Bring ye the Law<br> And study it,<br> If ye be men of truth."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_094.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>94.  Famani iftar<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi alka<u>th</u>iba min baAAdi <u>tha</u>lika faol<u>a</u>-ika humu a<b>l</b><i><u>th</u></i><u>a</u>limoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>94.  If any, after this, invent<br> A lie and attribute it<br> To God, they are indeed<br> Unjust wrong-doers.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_095.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>95.  Qul <u>s</u>adaqa All<u>a</u>hu fa<b>i</b>ttabiAAoo millata ibr<u>a</u>heema <u>h</u>aneefan wam<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>na mina almushrikeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>95.  Say: "God speaketh<br> The Truth: follow<br> The religion of Abraham,<br> The sane in faith; he<br> Was not of the Pagans."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_096.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>96.  Inna awwala baytin wu<u>d</u>iAAa li<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si lalla<u>th</u>ee bibakkata mub<u>a</u>rakan wahudan lilAA<u>a</u>lameen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>96.  The first House (of worship)<br> Appointed for men<br> Was that at Bakka:<br> Full of blessing<br>And of guidance<br> For all kinds of beings:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_097.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>97.  Feehi <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tun bayyin<u>a</u>tun maq<u>a</u>mu ibr<u>a</u>heema waman dakhalahu k<u>a</u>na <u>a</u>minan walill<u>a</u>hi AAal<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si <u>h</u>ijju albayti mani ista<u>ta</u>AAa ilayhi sabeelan waman kafara fa-inna All<u>a</u>ha ghaniyyun AAani alAA<u>a</u>lameen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>97.  In it are Signs<br> Manifest; (for example),<br> The Station of Abraham;<br> Whoever enters it<br> Attains security;<br> Pilgrimage thereto is a duty<br> Men owe to God,<br> Those who can afford<br> The journey; but if any<br> Deny faith, God stands not<br> In need of any of His creatures.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_098.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>98.  Qul y<u>a</u> ahla alkit<u>a</u>bi lima takfuroona bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>ti All<u>a</u>hi wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu shaheedun AAal<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> taAAmaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>98.  Say: "O People of the Book!<br> Why reject ye the Signs<br> Of God, when God<br> Is Himself witness<br> To all ye do?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_099.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>99.  Qul y<u>a</u> ahla alkit<u>a</u>bi lima ta<u>s</u>uddoona AAan sabeeli All<u>a</u>hi man <u>a</u>mana tabghoonah<u>a</u> AAiwajan waantum shuhad<u>a</u>o wam<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hu bigh<u>a</u>filin AAamm<u>a</u> taAAmaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>99.  Say: "O ye People of the Book!<br> Why obstruct ye<br> Those who believe,<br> From the Path of God,<br> Seeking to make it crooked,<br> While ye were yourselves<br> Witnesses (to God's Covenant)?'<br> But God is not unmindful<br> Of all that ye do."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_100.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>100.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo in tu<u>t</u>eeAAoo fareeqan mina alla<u>th</u>eena ootoo alkit<u>a</u>ba yaruddookum baAAda eem<u>a</u>nikum k<u>a</u>fireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>100.  O ye who believe!<br> If ye listen<br> To a faction<br> Among the People of the Book,<br> They would (indeed)<br> Render you apostates<br> After ye have believed!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_101.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>101.  Wakayfa takfuroona waantum tutl<u>a</u> AAalaykum <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tu All<u>a</u>hi wafeekum rasooluhu waman yaAAta<u>s</u>im bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi faqad hudiya il<u>a</u> <u>s</u>ir<u>at</u>in mustaqeem<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>101.  And how would ye<br> Deny Faith while unto you<br> Are rehearsed the Signs<br> Of God, and among you<br>Lives the Apostle?<br> Whoever holds<br> Firmly to God<br> Will be shown<br> A Way that is straight.</p> <div class="filenav">
