 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XVI.: Naḥl or The Bee.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 9</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_066.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>66.  Wa-inna lakum fee al-anAA<u>a</u>mi laAAibratan nusqeekum mimm<u>a</u> fee bu<u>t</u>oonihi min bayni farthin wadamin labanan kh<u>a</u>li<u>s</u>an s<u>a</u>-ighan li<b>l</b>shsh<u>a</u>ribeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>66.  And verily in cattle (too)<br> Will ye find an instructive Sign.<br> From what is within their bodies,<br> Between excretions and blood,<br> We produce, for your drink,<br> Milk, pure and agreeable<br> To those who drink it.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_067.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>67.  Wamin thamar<u>a</u>ti a<b>l</b>nnakheeli wa<b>a</b>l-aAAn<u>a</u>bi tattakhi<u>th</u>oona minhu sakaran warizqan <u>h</u>asanan inna fee <u>tha</u>lika la<u>a</u>yatan liqawmin yaAAqiloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>67.  And from the fruit<br> Of the date-palm and the vine,<br> Ye get out wholesome drink<br> And food: behold, in this<br> Also is a Sign<br> For those who are wise.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_068.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>68.  Waaw<u>ha</u> rabbuka il<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nna<u>h</u>li ani ittakhi<u>th</u>ee mina aljib<u>a</u>li buyootan wamina a<b>l</b>shshajari wamimm<u>a</u> yaAArishoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>68.  And thy Lord taught the Bee<br> To build its cells in hills,<br> On trees, and in (men's) habitations;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_069.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>69.  Thumma kulee min kulli a<b>l</b>ththamar<u>a</u>ti fa<b>o</b>slukee subula rabbiki <u>th</u>ululan yakhruju min bu<u>t</u>oonih<u>a</u> shar<u>a</u>bun mukhtalifun alw<u>a</u>nuhu feehi shif<u>a</u>on li<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si inna fee <u>tha</u>lika la<u>a</u>yatan liqawmin yatafakkaroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>69.  Then to eat of all<br> The produce (of the earth),<br> And find with skill the spacious<br> Paths of its Lord: there issues<br> From within their bodies<br> A drink of varying colours,<br> Wherein is healing for men:<br> Verily in this is a Sign<br> For those who give thought.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/016/016_070.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>70.  Wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu khalaqakum thumma yatawaff<u>a</u>kum waminkum man yuraddu il<u>a</u> ar<u>th</u>ali alAAumuri likay l<u>a</u> yaAAlama baAAda AAilmin shay-an inna All<u>a</u>ha AAaleemun qadeer<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>70.  It is God Who creates you<br> And takes your souls at death;<br> And of you there are<br> Some who are sent back<br> To a feeble age, so that<br> They know nothing after<br> Having known (much):<br> For God is All-Knowing,<br> All-Powerful.</p> <div class="filenav">
