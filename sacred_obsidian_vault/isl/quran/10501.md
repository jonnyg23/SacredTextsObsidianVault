 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra CV.: Fīl, or The Elephant.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/105/105_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  Alam tara kayfa faAAala rabbuka bi-a<u>s</u>-<u>ha</u>bi alfeel<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>1.  Seest thou not<br> How thy Lord dealt<br> With the Companions<br> Of the Elephant?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/105/105_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  Alam yajAAal kaydahum fee ta<u>d</u>leel<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>2.  Did He not make<br> Their treacherous plan<br> Go astray?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/105/105_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  Waarsala AAalayhim <u>t</u>ayran ab<u>a</u>beel<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>3.  And He sent against them<br> Flights of Birds,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/105/105_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  Tarmeehim bi<u>h</u>ij<u>a</u>ratin min sijjeel<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>4.  Striking them with stones<br> Of baked clay.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/105/105_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  FajaAAalahum kaAAa<u>s</u>fin ma/kool<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>5.  Then did He make them<br> Like an empty field<br> Of stalks and straw,<br> (Of which the corn)<br> Has been eaten up.</p>  <div class="filenav">
