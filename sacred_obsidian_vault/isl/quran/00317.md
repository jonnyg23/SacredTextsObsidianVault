 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra III.: Āl-i-’Imrān, or The Family of ’Imrān.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 17</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_156.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>156.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo l<u>a</u> takoonoo ka<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo waq<u>a</u>loo li-ikhw<u>a</u>nihim i<u>tha</u> <u>d</u>araboo fee al-ar<u>d</u>i aw k<u>a</u>noo ghuzzan law k<u>a</u>noo AAindan<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> m<u>a</u>too wam<u>a</u> qutiloo liyajAAala All<u>a</u>hu <u>tha</u>lika <u>h</u>asratan fee quloobihim wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu yu<u>h</u>yee wayumeetu wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu bim<u>a</u> taAAmaloona ba<u>s</u>eer<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>156.  O ye who believe!<br> Be not like the Unbelievers,<br> Who say of their brethren,<br> When they are travelling<br> Through the earth or engaged<br> In fighting: "If they had stayed<br> With us, they would not<br> Have died, or been slain."<br>This that God may make it<br> A cause of sighs and regrets<br> In their hearts. It is God<br> That gives Life and Death,<br> And God sees well<br> All that ye do.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_157.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>157.  Wala-in qutiltum fee sabeeli All<u>a</u>hi aw muttum lamaghfiratun mina All<u>a</u>hi wara<u>h</u>matun khayrun mimm<u>a</u> yajmaAAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>157.  And if ye are slain, or die,<br> In the way of God,<br> Forgiveness and mercy<br> From God are far better<br> Than all they could amass.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_158.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>158.  Wala-in muttum aw qutiltum la-il<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi tu<u>h</u>sharoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>158.  And if ye die, or are slain,<br> Lo! it is-unto God<br> That ye are brought together.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_159.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>159.  Fabim<u>a</u> ra<u>h</u>matin mina All<u>a</u>hi linta lahum walaw kunta fa<i><u>thth</u></i>an ghalee<i><u>th</u></i>a alqalbi la<b>i</b>nfa<u>dd</u>oo min <u>h</u>awlika fa<b>o</b>AAfu AAanhum wa<b>i</b>staghfir lahum wash<u>a</u>wirhum fee al-amri fa-i<u>tha</u> AAazamta fatawakkal AAal<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi inna All<u>a</u>ha yu<u>h</u>ibbu almutawakkileen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>159.  It is part of the Mercy<br> Of God that thou dost deal<br> Gently with them.<br> Wert thou severe<br> Or harsh-hearted,<br> They would have broken away<br> From about thee: so pass over<br> (Their faults), and ask<br> For (God's) forgiveness<br> For them; and consult<br> Them in affairs (of moment).<br> Then, when thou hast<br> Taken a decision,<br> Put thy trust in God.<br>For God loves those<br> Who put their trust (in Him).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_160.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>160.  In yan<u>s</u>urkumu All<u>a</u>hu fal<u>a</u> gh<u>a</u>liba lakum wa-in yakh<u>th</u>ulkum faman <u>tha</u> alla<u>th</u>ee yan<u>s</u>urukum min baAAdihi waAAal<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi falyatawakkali almu/minoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>160.  If God helps you,<br> Non can overcome you:<br> If He forsakes you,<br> Who is there, after that,<br> That can help you?<br> In God, then,<br> Let Believers put their trust.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_161.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>161.  Wam<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>na linabiyyin an yaghulla waman yaghlul ya/ti bim<u>a</u> ghalla yawma alqiy<u>a</u>mati thumma tuwaff<u>a</u> kullu nafsin m<u>a</u> kasabat wahum l<u>a</u> yu<i><u>th</u></i>lamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>161.  No prophet could (ever)<br> Be false to his trust.<br> If any person is so false,<br> He shall, on the Day<br> Of Judgment, restore<br> What he misappropriated;<br> Then shall every soul<br> Receive its due,—<br> Whatever it earned,—<br> And none shall be<br> Dealt with unjustly.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_162.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>162.  Afamani ittabaAAa ri<u>d</u>w<u>a</u>na All<u>a</u>hi kaman b<u>a</u>a bisakha<u>t</u>in mina All<u>a</u>hi wama/w<u>a</u>hu jahannamu wabi/sa alma<u>s</u>eer<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>162.  Is the man who follows<br> The good pleasure of God<br> Like the man who draws<br> On himself the wrath<br> Of God, and whose abode<br> Is in Hell?—<br> A woeful refuge!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_163.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>163.  Hum daraj<u>a</u>tun AAinda All<u>a</u>hi wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu ba<u>s</u>eerun bim<u>a</u> yaAAmaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>163.  They are in varying grades<br> In the sight of God,<br> And God sees well<br> All that they do.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_164.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>164.  Laqad manna All<u>a</u>hu AAal<u>a</u> almu/mineena i<u>th</u> baAAatha feehim rasoolan min anfusihim yatloo AAalayhim <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tihi wayuzakkeehim wayuAAallimuhumu alkit<u>a</u>ba wa<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>ikmata wa-in k<u>a</u>noo min qablu lafee <u>d</u>al<u>a</u>lin mubeen<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>164.  God did confer<br> A great favour<br>On the Believers<br> When He sent among them<br> An Apostle from among<br> Themselves, rehearsing<br> Unto them the Signs<br> Of God, sanctifying them,<br> And instructing them<br> In Scripture and Wisdom,<br> While, before that,<br> They had been<br> In manifest error.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_165.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>165.  Awa lamm<u>a</u> a<u>sa</u>batkum mu<u>s</u>eebatun qad a<u>s</u>abtum mithlayh<u>a</u> qultum ann<u>a</u> h<u>atha</u> qul huwa min AAindi anfusikum inna All<u>a</u>ha AAal<u>a</u> kulli shay-in qadeer<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>165.  What! When a single<br> Disaster smites you,<br> Although ye smote (your enemies)<br> With one twice as great,<br> Do ye say?—<br> "Whence is this?"<br> Say (to them):<br> "It is from yourselves:<br> For God hath power<br> Over all things."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_166.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>166.  Wam<u>a</u> a<u>sa</u>bakum yawma iltaq<u>a</u> aljamAA<u>a</u>ni fabi-i<u>th</u>ni All<u>a</u>hi waliyaAAlama almu/mineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>166.  What ye suffered<br> On the day the two armies<br> Met, was with the leave<br> Of God, in order that<br> He might test the Believers,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_167.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>167.  WaliyaAAlama alla<u>th</u>eena n<u>a</u>faqoo waqeela lahum taAA<u>a</u>law q<u>a</u>tiloo fee sabeeli All<u>a</u>hi awi idfaAAoo q<u>a</u>loo law naAAlamu qit<u>a</u>lan la<b>i</b>ttabaAAn<u>a</u>kum hum lilkufri yawma-i<u>th</u>in aqrabu minhum lil-eem<u>a</u>ni yaqooloona bi-afw<u>a</u>hihim m<u>a</u> laysa fee quloobihim wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu aAAlamu bim<u>a</u> yaktumoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>167.  And the Hypocrites also.<br> These were told: "Come,<br> Fight in the way of God,<br> Or (at least) drive<br> (The foe from Your city)."<br> They said: "Had we known<br> How to fight, we should<br> Certainly have followed you."<br>They were that day<br> Nearer to Unbelief<br> Than to Faith,<br> Saying with their lips<br> What was not in their hearts.<br> But God hath full knowledge<br> Of all they conceal.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_168.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>168.  Alla<u>th</u>eena q<u>a</u>loo li-ikhw<u>a</u>nihim waqaAAadoo law a<u>ta</u>AAoon<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> qutiloo qul fa<b>i</b>draoo AAan anfusikumu almawta in kuntum <u>sa</u>diqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>168.  (They are) the ones that say,<br> (Of their brethren slain),<br> While they themselves<br> Sit (at ease): "If only<br> They had listened to us,<br> They would not have been slain."<br> Say: "Avert death<br> From your own selves,<br> If ye speak the truth."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_169.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>169.  Wal<u>a</u> ta<u>h</u>sabanna alla<u>th</u>eena qutiloo fee sabeeli All<u>a</u>hi amw<u>a</u>tan bal a<u>h</u>y<u>a</u>on AAinda rabbihim yurzaqoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>169.  Think not of those<br> Who are slain in God's way<br> As dead. Nay, they live,<br> Finding their sustenance<br> In the Presence of their Lord;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_170.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>170.  Fari<u>h</u>eena bim<u>a</u> <u>a</u>t<u>a</u>humu All<u>a</u>hu min fa<u>d</u>lihi wayastabshiroona bi<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena lam yal<u>h</u>aqoo bihim min khalfihim all<u>a</u> khawfun AAalayhim wal<u>a</u> hum ya<u>h</u>zanoon<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>170.  They rejoice in the Bounty<br> Provided by God:<br> And with regard to those<br> Left behind, who have not<br> Yet joined them (in their bliss),<br> The (Martyrs) glory in the fact<br> That on them is no fear,<br> Nor have they (cause to) grieve.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_171.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>171.  Yastabshiroona biniAAmatin mina All<u>a</u>hi wafa<u>d</u>lin waanna All<u>a</u>ha l<u>a</u> yu<u>d</u>eeAAu ajra almu/mineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>171.  They glory in the Grace<br> And the Bounty from God,<br> And in the fact that<br> God suffereth not<br>The reward of the Faithful<br> To be lost (in the least).</p> <div class="filenav">
