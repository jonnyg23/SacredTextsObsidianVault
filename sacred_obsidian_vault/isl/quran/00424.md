 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra IV.: Nisāa, or The Women.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 24</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_172.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>172.  Lan yastankifa almasee<u>h</u>u an yakoona AAabdan lill<u>a</u>hi wal<u>a</u> almal<u>a</u>-ikatu almuqarraboona waman yastankif AAan AAib<u>a</u>datihi wayastakbir fasaya<u>h</u>shuruhum ilayhi jameeAA<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>172.  Christ disdaineth not<br> To serve and worship God,<br> Nor do the angels, those<br> Nearest (to God):<br> Those who disdain<br> His worship and are arrogant,—<br> He will gather them all<br> Together unto Himself<br> To (answer).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_173.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>173.  Faamm<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo waAAamiloo a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>li<u>ha</u>ti fayuwaffeehim ojoorahum wayazeeduhum min fa<u>d</u>lihi waamm<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena istankafoo wa<b>i</b>stakbaroo fayuAAa<u>thth</u>ibuhum AAa<u>tha</u>ban aleeman wal<u>a</u> yajidoona lahum min dooni All<u>a</u>hi waliyyan wal<u>a</u> na<u>s</u>eer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>173.  But to those who believe<br> And do deeds of righteousness,<br> He will give their (due)<br> Rewards,—and more,<br> Out of His bounty:<br> But those who are<br> Disdainful and arrogant,<br> He will punish<br>With a grievous penalty;<br> Nor will they find,<br> Besides God, any<br> To protect or help them.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_174.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>174.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>su qad j<u>a</u>akum burh<u>a</u>nun min rabbikum waanzaln<u>a</u> ilaykum nooran mubeen<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>174.  O mankind! Verily<br> There hath come to you<br> A convincing proof<br> From your Lord:<br> For We have sent unto you<br> A light (that is) manifest.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_175.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>175.  Faamm<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi wa<b>i</b>AAta<u>s</u>amoo bihi fasayudkhiluhum fee ra<u>h</u>matin minhu wafa<u>d</u>lin wayahdeehim ilayhi <u>s</u>ir<u>at</u>an mustaqeem<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>175.  Then those who believe<br> In God, and hold fast<br> To Him,—soon will He<br> Admit them to Mercy<br> And Grace from Himself,<br> And guide them to Himself<br> By a straight Way.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_176.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>176.  Yastaftoonaka quli All<u>a</u>hu yufteekum fee alkal<u>a</u>lati ini imruon halaka laysa lahu waladun walahu okhtun falah<u>a</u> ni<u>s</u>fu m<u>a</u> taraka wahuwa yarithuh<u>a</u> in lam yakun lah<u>a</u> waladun fa-in k<u>a</u>nat<u>a</u> ithnatayni falahum<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>ththuluth<u>a</u>ni mimm<u>a</u> taraka wa-in k<u>a</u>noo ikhwatan rij<u>a</u>lan wanis<u>a</u>an fali<b>l</b><u>thth</u>akari mithlu <u>h</u>a<i><u>thth</u></i>i alonthayayni yubayyinu All<u>a</u>hu lakum an ta<u>d</u>illoo wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu bikulli shay-in AAaleem<b>un</b>    </p>
 <p>176.  They ask thee<br> For a legal decision.<br> Say: God directs (thus)<br> About those who leave<br> No descendants or ascendants<br> As heirs. If it is a man<br> That dies, leaving a sister<br> But no child, she shall<br>Have half the inheritance:<br> If (such a deceased was)<br> A woman, who left no child,<br> Her brother takes her inheritance:<br> If there are two sisters,<br> They shall have two-thirds<br> Of the inheritance<br> (Between them): if there are<br> Brothers and sisters, (they share),<br> The male having twice<br> The share of the female.<br> Thus doth God make clear<br> To you (His law), lest<br> Ye err. And God<br> Hath knowledge of all things.</p>  <div class="filenav">
