 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra III.: Āl-i-’Imrān, or The Family of ’Imrān.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 11</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_102.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>102.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo ittaqoo All<u>a</u>ha <u>h</u>aqqa tuq<u>a</u>tihi wal<u>a</u> tamootunna ill<u>a</u> waantum muslimoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>102.  O ye who believe!<br> Fear God as He should be<br> Feared, and die not<br> Except in a state<br> Of Islam.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_103.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>103.  Wa<b>i</b>AAta<u>s</u>imoo bi<u>h</u>abli All<u>a</u>hi jameeAAan wal<u>a</u> tafarraqoo wa<b>o</b><u>th</u>kuroo niAAmata All<u>a</u>hi AAalaykum i<u>th</u> kuntum aAAd<u>a</u>an faallafa bayna quloobikum faa<u>s</u>ba<u>h</u>tum biniAAmatihi ikhw<u>a</u>nan wakuntum AAal<u>a</u> shaf<u>a</u> <u>h</u>ufratin mina a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>ri faanqa<u>th</u>akum minh<u>a</u> ka<u>tha</u>lika yubayyinu All<u>a</u>hu lakum <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tihi laAAallakum tahtadoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>103.  And hold fast,<br> All together, by the Rope<br> Which God (stretches out<br> For you), and be not divided<br> Among yourselves;<br> And remember with gratitude<br> God's favour on you;<br> For ye were enemies<br> And He joined your hearts<br> In love, so that by His Grace,<br> Ye became brethren;<br> And ye were on the brink<br> Of the Pit of Fire,<br> And He saved you from it.<br> Thus doth God make<br> His Signs clear to you:<br> That ye may be guided.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_104.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>104.  Waltakun minkum ommatun yadAAoona il<u>a</u> alkhayri waya/muroona bi<b>a</b>lmaAAroofi wayanhawna AAani almunkari waol<u>a</u>-ika humu almufli<u>h</u>oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>104.  Let there arise out of you<br> A band of people<br> Inviting to all that is good,<br> Enjoining what is right,<br>And forbidding what is wrong:<br> They are the ones<br> To attain felicity.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_105.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>105.  Wal<u>a</u> takoonoo ka<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena tafarraqoo wa<b>i</b>khtalafoo min baAAdi m<u>a</u> j<u>a</u>ahumu albayyin<u>a</u>tu waol<u>a</u>-ika lahum AAa<u>tha</u>bun AAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>105.  Be not like those<br> Who are divided<br> Amongst themselves<br> And fall into disputations<br> After receiving<br> Clear Signs:<br> For them<br> Is a dreadful Penalty,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_106.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>106.  Yawma tabya<u>dd</u>u wujoohun wataswaddu wujoohun faamm<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena iswaddat wujoohuhum akafartum baAAda eem<u>a</u>nikum fa<u>th</u>ooqoo alAAa<u>tha</u>ba bim<u>a</u> kuntum takfuroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>106.  On the Day when<br> Some faces will be (lit up<br> With) white, and some faces<br> Will be (in the gloom of) black:<br> To those whose faces<br> Will be black, (will be said):<br> "Did ye reject Faith<br> After accepting it?<br> Taste then the Penalty<br> For rejecting Faith."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_107.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>107.  Waamm<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena ibya<u>dd</u>at wujoohuhum fafee ra<u>h</u>mati All<u>a</u>hi hum feeh<u>a</u> kh<u>a</u>lidoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>107.  But those whose faces<br> Will be (lit with) white,—<br> They will be in (the light<br> Of) God's mercy: therein<br> To dwell (for ever).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_108.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>108.  Tilka <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tu All<u>a</u>hi natlooh<u>a</u> AAalayka bi<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>aqqi wam<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hu yureedu <i><u>th</u></i>ulman lilAA<u>a</u>lameen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>108.  These are the Signs<br> Of God: We rehearse them<br> To thee in Truth:<br> And God means<br> No injustice to any<br> Of His creatures.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_109.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>109.  Walill<u>a</u>hi m<u>a</u> fee a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wam<u>a</u> fee al-ar<u>d</u>i wa-il<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi turjaAAu al-omoor<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>109.  To God belongs all<br> That is in the heavens<br>And on earth: to Him<br> Do all questions<br> Go back (for decision).</p> <div class="filenav">
