 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra CXI.: Lahab, or (the Father of) Flame.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/111/111_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  Tabbat yad<u>a</u> abee lahabin watab<b>ba</b>   </p>
 <p>1.  Perish the hands<br> Of the Father of Flame!<br> Perish he!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/111/111_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  M<u>a</u> aghn<u>a</u> AAanhu m<u>a</u>luhu wam<u>a</u> kasab<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>2.  No profit to him<br> From all his wealth,<br> And all his gains!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/111/111_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  Saya<u>s</u>l<u>a</u> n<u>a</u>ran <u>tha</u>ta lahab<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>3.  Burnt soon will he be<br> In a Fire<br> Of blazing Flame!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/111/111_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  Wa<b>i</b>mraatuhu <u>h</u>amm<u>a</u>lata al<u>h</u>a<u>t</u>ab<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>4.  His wife shall carry<br> The (crackling) wood—<br> As fuel!—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/111/111_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  Fee jeedih<u>a</u> <u>h</u>ablun min masad<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>5.  A twisted rope<br> Of palm-leaf fibre<br> Round her (own) neck!</p>  <div class="filenav">
