 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_144.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>144.  Wam<u>a</u> mu<u>h</u>ammadun ill<u>a</u> rasoolun qad khalat min qablihi a<b>l</b>rrusulu afa-in m<u>a</u>ta aw qutila inqalabtum AAal<u>a</u> aAAq<u>a</u>bikum waman yanqalib AAal<u>a</u> AAaqibayhi falan ya<u>d</u>urra All<u>a</u>ha shay-an wasayajzee All<u>a</u>hu a<b>l</b>shsh<u>a</u>kireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>144.  Muhammad is no more<br> Than an Apostle: many<br> Were the Apostles that passed away<br> Before him. If he died<br> Or were slain, will ye then<br> Turn back on your heels?<br> If any did turn back<br> On his heels, not the least<br> Harm will he do to God;<br> But God (on the other hand)<br> Will swiftly reward those<br> Who (serve him) with gratitude.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_145.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>145.  Wam<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>na linafsin an tamoota ill<u>a</u> bi-i<u>th</u>ni All<u>a</u>hi kit<u>a</u>ban mu-ajjalan waman yurid thaw<u>a</u>ba a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> nu/tihi minh<u>a</u> waman yurid thaw<u>a</u>ba al-<u>a</u>khirati nu/tihi minh<u>a</u> wasanajzee a<b>l</b>shsh<u>a</u>kireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>145.  Nor can a soul die<br> Except by God's leave,<br>The term being fixed<br> As by writing. If any<br> Do desire a reward<br> In this life, We shall give it<br> To him; and if any<br> Do desire a reward<br> In the Hereafter, We shall<br> Give it to him.<br> And swiftly shall We reward<br> Those that (serve us with) gratitude.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_146.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>146.  Wakaayyin min nabiyyin q<u>a</u>tala maAAahu ribbiyyoona katheerun fam<u>a</u> wahanoo lim<u>a</u> a<u>sa</u>bahum fee sabeeli All<u>a</u>hi wam<u>a</u> <u>d</u>aAAufoo wam<u>a</u> istak<u>a</u>noo wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu yu<u>h</u>ibbu a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>bireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>146.  How many of the Prophets<br> Fought (in God's way),<br> And with them (fought)<br> Large bands of godly men?<br> But they never lost heart<br> If they met with disaster<br> In God's way, nor did<br> They weaken (in will)<br> Nor give in. And God<br> Loves those who are<br> Firm and steadfast.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_147.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>147.  Wam<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>na qawlahum ill<u>a</u> an q<u>a</u>loo rabban<u>a</u> ighfir lan<u>a</u> <u>th</u>unooban<u>a</u> wa-isr<u>a</u>fan<u>a</u> fee amrin<u>a</u> wathabbit aqd<u>a</u>man<u>a</u> wa<b>o</b>n<u>s</u>urn<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> alqawmi alk<u>a</u>fireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>147.  All that they said was:<br> "Our Lord! forgive us<br> Our sins and anything<br> We may have done<br> That transgressed our duty:<br> Establish our feet firmly,<br> And help us against<br> Those that resist<br> Faith."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_148.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>148.  Fa<u>a</u>t<u>a</u>humu All<u>a</u>hu thaw<u>a</u>ba a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> wa<u>h</u>usna thaw<u>a</u>bi al-<u>a</u>khirati wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu yu<u>h</u>ibbu almu<u>h</u>sineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>148.  And God gave them<br> A reward in this world,<br> And the excellent reward<br> Of the Hereafter. For God<br> Loveth those who do good.</p> <div class="filenav">
