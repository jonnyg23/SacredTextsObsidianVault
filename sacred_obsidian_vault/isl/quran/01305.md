 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XIII.: Ra’d, or Thunder.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 5</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/013/013_032.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>32.  Walaqadi istuhzi-a birusulin min qablika faamlaytu lilla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo thumma akha<u>th</u>tuhum fakayfa k<u>a</u>na AAiq<u>a</u>b<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>32.  Mocked were (many) apostles<br> Before thee: but I granted<br> Respite to the Unbelievers,<br> And finally I punished them:<br> Then how (terrible) was My requital!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/013/013_033.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>33.  Afaman huwa q<u>a</u>-imun AAal<u>a</u> kulli nafsin bim<u>a</u> kasabat wajaAAaloo lill<u>a</u>hi shurak<u>a</u>a qul sammoohum am tunabbi-oonahu bim<u>a</u> l<u>a</u> yaAAlamu fee al-ar<u>d</u>i am bi<i><u>th</u></i><u>a</u>hirin mina alqawli bal zuyyina lilla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo makruhum wa<u>s</u>uddoo AAani a<b>l</b>ssabeeli waman yu<u>d</u>lili All<u>a</u>hu fam<u>a</u> lahu min h<u>a</u>d<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>33.  Is then He Who standeth<br> Over every soul (and knoweth)<br> All that it doth,<br> (Like any others)? And yet<br> They ascribe partners to God.<br> Say: "But name them!<br> Is it that ye will<br> Inform Him of something<br> He knoweth not on earth,<br> Or is it (just) a show<br> Of words?" Nay! to those<br> Who believe not, their pretence<br> seems pleasing, but they are<br> Kept back (thereby) from the Path.<br> And those whom God leaves<br> To stray, no one can guide.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/013/013_034.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>34.  Lahum AAa<u>tha</u>bun fee al<u>h</u>ay<u>a</u>ti a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> walaAAa<u>tha</u>bu al-<u>a</u>khirati ashaqqu wam<u>a</u> lahum mina All<u>a</u>hi min w<u>a</u>q<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>34.  For them is a Penalty<br> In the life of this world,<br> But harder, truly, is the Penalty<br> Of the Hereafter: and defender<br> Have they none against God.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/013/013_035.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>35.  Mathalu aljannati allatee wuAAida almuttaqoona tajree min ta<u>h</u>tih<u>a</u> al-anh<u>a</u>ru okuluh<u>a</u> d<u>a</u>-imun wa<i><u>th</u></i>illuh<u>a</u> tilka AAuqb<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena ittaqaw waAAuqb<u>a</u> alk<u>a</u>fireena a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>r<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>35.  The parable of the Garden<br> Which the righteous are promised!—<br>Beneath it flow rivers:<br> Perpetual is the enjoyment thereof<br> And the shade therein:<br> Such is the End<br> Of the Righteous; and the End<br> Of Unbelievers is the Fire.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/013/013_036.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>36.  Wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>tayn<u>a</u>humu alkit<u>a</u>ba yafra<u>h</u>oona bim<u>a</u> onzila ilayka wamina al-a<u>h</u>z<u>a</u>bi man yunkiru baAA<u>d</u>ahu qul innam<u>a</u> omirtu an aAAbuda All<u>a</u>ha wal<u>a</u> oshrika bihi ilayhi adAAoo wa-ilayhi ma<u>a</u>b<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>36.  Those to whom We have<br> Given the Book rejoice<br> At what hath been revealed<br> Unto thee: but there are<br> Among the clans those who reject<br> A part thereof. Say:<br> "I am commanded to worship<br> God, and not to join partners<br> With Him. Unto him<br> Do I call, and<br> Unto Him is my return."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/013/013_037.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>37.  Waka<u>tha</u>lika anzaln<u>a</u>hu <u>h</u>ukman AAarabiyyan wala-ini ittabaAAta ahw<u>a</u>ahum baAAda m<u>a</u> j<u>a</u>aka mina alAAilmi m<u>a</u> laka mina All<u>a</u>hi min waliyyin wal<u>a</u> w<u>a</u>q<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>37.  Thus have We revealed it<br> To be a judgment of authority<br>In Arabic. Wert thou to follow<br> Their (vain) desires after the knowledge<br> Which hath reached thee,<br> Then wouldst thou find<br> Neither protector nor defender<br> Against God.</p> <div class="filenav">
