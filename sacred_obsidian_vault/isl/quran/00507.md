 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra V.: Māïda, or The Table Spread.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 7</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_044.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>44.  Inn<u>a</u> anzaln<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>ttawr<u>a</u>ta feeh<u>a</u> hudan wanoorun ya<u>h</u>kumu bih<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nnabiyyoona alla<u>th</u>eena aslamoo lilla<u>th</u>eena h<u>a</u>doo wa<b>al</b>rrabb<u>a</u>niyyoona wa<b>a</b>l-a<u>h</u>b<u>a</u>ru bim<u>a</u> istu<u>h</u>fi<i><u>th</u></i>oo min kit<u>a</u>bi All<u>a</u>hi wak<u>a</u>noo AAalayhi shuhad<u>a</u>a fal<u>a</u> takhshawoo a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>sa wa<b>i</b>khshawni wal<u>a</u> tashtaroo bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tee thamanan qaleelan waman lam ya<u>h</u>kum bim<u>a</u> anzala All<u>a</u>hu faol<u>a</u>-ika humu alk<u>a</u>firoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>44.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">47</font></span> It was We who revealed<br> The Law (to Moses): therein<br> Was guidance and light.'<br> By its standard have been judged<br> The Jews, by the Prophets<br> Who bowed (as in Islam)<br> To God's Will, by the Rabbis<br> And the Doctors of Law:<br>For to them was entrusted<br> The protection of God's Book,<br> And they were witnesses thereto:<br> Therefore fear not men,<br> But fear Me, and sell not<br> My Signs for a miserable price.<br> If any do fail to judge<br> By (the light of) what God<br> Hath revealed, they are<br> (No better than) Unbelievers.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_045.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>45.  Wakatabn<u>a</u> AAalayhim feeh<u>a</u> anna a<b>l</b>nnafsa bi<b>al</b>nnafsi wa<b>a</b>lAAayna bi<b>a</b>lAAayni wa<b>a</b>l-anfa bi<b>a</b>l-anfi wa<b>a</b>lo<u>th</u>una bi<b>a</b>lo<u>th</u>uni wa<b>al</b>ssinna bi<b>al</b>ssinni wa<b>a</b>ljuroo<u>h</u>a qi<u>sas</u>un faman ta<u>s</u>addaqa bihi fahuwa kaff<u>a</u>ratun lahu waman lam ya<u>h</u>kum bim<u>a</u> anzala All<u>a</u>hu faol<u>a</u>-ika humu a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>45.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">48</font></span> We ordained therein for them:<br> "Life for life, eye for eye,<br> Nose for nose, ear for ear,<br> Tooth for tooth, and wounds<br> Equal for equal." But if<br> Any one remits the retaliation<br> Byway of charity, it is<br> An act of atonement for himself.<br> And if any fail to judge<br> By (the light of) what God<br> Hath revealed, they are<br> (No better than) wrong-doers.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_046.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>46.  Waqaffayn<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> <u>a</u>th<u>a</u>rihim biAAees<u>a</u> ibni maryama mu<u>s</u>addiqan lim<u>a</u> bayna yadayhi mina a<b>l</b>ttawr<u>a</u>ti wa<u>a</u>tayn<u>a</u>hu al-injeela feehi hudan wanoorun wamu<u>s</u>addiqan lim<u>a</u> bayna yadayhi mina a<b>l</b>ttawr<u>a</u>ti wahudan wamawAAi<i><u>th</u></i>atan lilmuttaqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>46.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">49</font></span> And in their footsteps<br> We sent Jesus the son<br>Of Mary, confirming<br> The Law that had come<br> Before him: We sent him<br> The Gospel: therein<br> Was guidance and light,<br> And confirmation of the Law<br> That had come before him:<br> A guidance and an admonition<br> To those who fear God.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_047.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>47.  Walya<u>h</u>kum ahlu al-injeeli bim<u>a</u> anzala All<u>a</u>hu feehi waman lam ya<u>h</u>kum bim<u>a</u> anzala All<u>a</u>hu faol<u>a</u>-ika humu alf<u>a</u>siqoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>47.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">50</font></span> Let the People of the Gospel<br> Judge by what God hath revealed<br> Therein. If any do fail<br> To judge by (the light of)<br> What God hath revealed,<br> They are (no better than)<br> Those who rebel.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_048.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>48.  Waanzaln<u>a</u> ilayka alkit<u>a</u>ba bi<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>aqqi mu<u>s</u>addiqan lim<u>a</u> bayna yadayhi mina alkit<u>a</u>bi wamuhayminan AAalayhi fa<b>o</b><u>h</u>kum baynahum bim<u>a</u> anzala All<u>a</u>hu wal<u>a</u> tattabiAA ahw<u>a</u>ahum AAamm<u>a</u> j<u>a</u>aka mina al<u>h</u>aqqi likullin jaAAaln<u>a</u> minkum shirAAatan waminh<u>a</u>jan walaw sh<u>a</u>a All<u>a</u>hu lajaAAalakum ommatan w<u>ah</u>idatan wal<u>a</u>kin liyabluwakum feem<u>a</u> <u>a</u>t<u>a</u>kum fa<b>i</b>stabiqoo alkhayr<u>a</u>ti il<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi marjiAAukum jameeAAan fayunabbi-okum bim<u>a</u> kuntum feehi takhtalifoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>48.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">51</font></span> To thee We sent the Scripture<br> In truth, confirming<br> The scripture that came<br> Before it, and guarding it's<br> In safety: so judge<br> Between them by what<br> God hath revealed,<br> And follow not their vain<br> Desires, diverging<br> From the Truth that hath come<br> To thee. To each among you<br> Have We prescribed a Law<br> And an Open Way.<br> If God had so willed.<br> He would have made you<br> A single People, but (His<br>Plan is) to test you in what<br> He hath given you: so strive<br> As in a race in all virtues.<br> The goal of you all is to God;<br> It is He that will show you<br> The truth of the matters<br> In which ye dispute;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_049.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>49.  Waani o<u>h</u>kum baynahum bim<u>a</u> anzala All<u>a</u>hu wal<u>a</u> tattabiAA ahw<u>a</u>ahum wa<b>i</b><u>hth</u>arhum an yaftinooka AAan baAA<u>d</u>i m<u>a</u> anzala All<u>a</u>hu ilayka fa-in tawallaw fa<b>i</b>AAlam annam<u>a</u> yureedu All<u>a</u>hu an yu<u>s</u>eebahum bibaAA<u>d</u>i <u>th</u>unoobihim wa-inna katheeran mina a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si laf<u>a</u>siqoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>49.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">52</font></span> And this (He commands):<br> Judge thou between them<br> By what God hath revealed,<br> And follow not their vain<br> Desires, but beware of them<br> Lest they beguile thee<br> From any of that (teaching)<br> Which God hath sent down<br> To thee. And if they turn<br> Away, be assured that<br> For some of their crimes<br> It is God's purpose to punish<br> Them. And truly most men<br> Are rebellious.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_050.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>50.  Afa<u>h</u>ukma alj<u>a</u>hiliyyati yabghoona waman a<u>h</u>sanu mina All<u>a</u>hu <u>h</u>ukman liqawmin yooqinoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>50.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">53</font></span> Do they then seek after<br> A judgment of (the Days<br> Of) Ignorance? But who,<br> For a people whose faith<br> Is assured, can give<br> Better judgment than God?</p> <div class="filenav">
