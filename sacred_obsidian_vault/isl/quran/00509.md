 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra V.: Māïda, or The Table Spread.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 9</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_057.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>57.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo l<u>a</u> tattakhi<u>th</u>oo alla<u>th</u>eena ittakha<u>th</u>oo deenakum huzuwan walaAAiban mina alla<u>th</u>eena ootoo alkit<u>a</u>ba min qablikum wa<b>a</b>lkuff<u>a</u>ra awliy<u>a</u>a wa<b>i</b>ttaqoo All<u>a</u>ha in kuntum mu/mineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>57.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">60</font></span> O ye who believe!<br> Take not for friends<br> And protectors those<br> Who take your religion<br> For a mockery or sport,—<br> Whether among those<br> Who received the Scripture<br> Before you, or among those<br>Who reject Faith;<br> But fear ye God,<br> If ye have Faith (indeed).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_058.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>58.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> n<u>a</u>daytum il<u>a</u> a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>al<u>a</u>ti ittakha<u>th</u>ooh<u>a</u> huzuwan walaAAiban <u>tha</u>lika bi-annahum qawmun l<u>a</u> yaAAqiloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>58.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">61</font></span> When ye proclaim<br> Your call to prayer,<br> They take it (but)<br> As mockery and sport;<br> That is because they are<br> A people without understanding.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_059.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>59.  Qul y<u>a</u> ahla alkit<u>a</u>bi hal tanqimoona minn<u>a</u> ill<u>a</u> an <u>a</u>mann<u>a</u> bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi wam<u>a</u> onzila ilayn<u>a</u> wam<u>a</u> onzila min qablu waanna aktharakum f<u>a</u>siqoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>59.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">62</font></span> Say: "O People of the Book!<br> Do ye disapprove of us<br> For no other reason than<br> That we believe in God,<br> And the revelation<br> That hath come to us<br> And that which came<br> Before (us), and (perhaps)<br> That most of you<br> Are rebellious and disobedient?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_060.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>60.  Qul hal onabbi-okum bisharrin min <u>tha</u>lika mathoobatan AAinda All<u>a</u>hi man laAAanahu All<u>a</u>hu wagha<u>d</u>iba AAalayhi wajaAAala minhumu alqiradata wa<b>a</b>lkhan<u>a</u>zeera waAAabada a<b>l</b><u>tta</u>ghooti ol<u>a</u>-ika sharrun mak<u>a</u>nan waa<u>d</u>allu AAan saw<u>a</u>-i a<b>l</b>ssabeel<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>60.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">63</font></span> Say: "Shall I point out<br> To you something much worse<br> Than this, (as judged)<br> By the treatment it received<br> From God? Those who<br> Incurred the curse of God<br> And His wrath, those of whom some<br> He transformed into apes and swine,<br> Those who worshipped Evil;—<br> These are (many times) worse<br> In rank, and far more astray<br> From the even Path!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_061.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>61.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> j<u>a</u>ookum q<u>a</u>loo <u>a</u>mann<u>a</u> waqad dakhaloo bi<b>a</b>lkufri wahum qad kharajoo bihi wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu aAAlamu bim<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo yaktumoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>61.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">64</font></span> When they come to thee,<br> They say: "We believe":<br>But in fact they enter<br> With a mind against Faith,<br> And they go out<br> With the same.<br> But God knoweth fully<br> All that they hide.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_062.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>62.  Watar<u>a</u> katheeran minhum yus<u>a</u>riAAoona fee al-ithmi wa<b>a</b>lAAudw<u>a</u>ni waaklihimu a<b>l</b>ssu<u>h</u>ta labi/sa m<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo yaAAmaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>62.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">65</font></span> Many of them dost thou<br> See, racing each other<br> In sin and rancour,<br> And their eating of things<br> Forbidden. Evil indeed<br> Are the things that they do.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_063.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>63.  Lawl<u>a</u> yanh<u>a</u>humu a<b>l</b>rrabb<u>a</u>niyyoona wa<b>a</b>l-a<u>h</u>b<u>a</u>ru AAan qawlihimu al-ithma waaklihimu a<b>l</b>ssu<u>h</u>ta labi/sa m<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo ya<u>s</u>naAAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>63.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">66</font></span> Why do not the Rabbis<br> And the doctors of law forbid<br> Them from their (habit<br> Of) uttering sinful words<br> And eating things forbidden?<br> Evil indeed are their works.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_064.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>64.  Waq<u>a</u>lati alyahoodu yadu All<u>a</u>hi maghloolatun ghullat aydeehim waluAAinoo bim<u>a</u> q<u>a</u>loo bal yad<u>a</u>hu mabsoo<u>t</u>at<u>a</u>ni yunfiqu kayfa yash<u>a</u>o walayazeedanna katheeran minhum m<u>a</u> onzila ilayka min rabbika <u>t</u>ughy<u>a</u>nan wakufran waalqayn<u>a</u> baynahumu alAAad<u>a</u>wata wa<b>a</b>lbagh<u>da</u>a il<u>a</u> yawmi alqiy<u>a</u>mati kullam<u>a</u> awqadoo n<u>a</u>ran lil<u>h</u>arbi a<u>t</u>faah<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hu wayasAAawna fee al-ar<u>d</u>i fas<u>a</u>dan wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu l<u>a</u> yu<u>h</u>ibbu almufsideen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>64.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">67</font></span> The Jews say: "God's hand<br> Is tied up." Be their hands<br> Tied up and be they accursed<br> For the (blasphemy) they utter.<br> Nay, both His hands<br> Are widely outstretched:<br> He giveth and spendeth<br> (Of His bounty) as He pleaseth.<br> But the revelation that<br> Cometh to thee from God<br> Increaseth in most of them<br> Their obstinate rebellion<br> And blasphemy. Amongst them<br> We have placed enmity<br>And hatred till the Day<br> Of Judgment. Every time<br> They kindle the fire of war,<br> God doth extinguish it;<br> But they (ever) strive<br> To do mischief on earth.<br> And God loveth not<br> Those who do mischief.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_065.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>65.  Walaw anna ahla alkit<u>a</u>bi <u>a</u>manoo wa<b>i</b>ttaqaw lakaffarn<u>a</u> AAanhum sayyi-<u>a</u>tihim walaadkhaln<u>a</u>hum jann<u>a</u>ti a<b>l</b>nnaAAeem<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>65.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">68</font></span> If only the People of the Book<br> Had believed and been righteous,<br> We should indeed have<br> Blotted out their iniquities<br> And admitted them<br> To Gardens of Bliss.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/005/005_066.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>66.  Walaw annahum aq<u>a</u>moo a<b>l</b>ttawr<u>a</u>ta wa<b>a</b>l-injeela wam<u>a</u> onzila ilayhim min rabbihim laakaloo min fawqihim wamin ta<u>h</u>ti arjulihim minhum ommatun muqta<u>s</u>idatun wakatheerun minhum s<u>a</u>a m<u>a</u> yaAAmaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>66.  <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">69</font></span> If only they had stood fast<br> By the Law, the Gospel,<br> And all the revelation that was sent<br> To them from their Lord,<br> They would have enjoyed<br> Happiness from every side.'<br> There is from among them<br> A party on the right course:<br> But many of them<br> Follow a course that is evil.</p> <div class="filenav">
