 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra III.: Āl-i-’Imrān, or The Family of ’Imrān.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 4</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_031.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>31.  Qul in kuntum tu<u>h</u>ibboona All<u>a</u>ha fa<b>i</b>ttabiAAoonee yu<u>h</u>bibkumu All<u>a</u>hu wayaghfir lakum <u>th</u>unoobakum wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu ghafoorun ra<u>h</u>eem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>31.  Say: "If ye do love God,<br> Follow me: God will love you<br> And forgive you your sins:<br> For God is Oft-Forgiving,<br> Most Merciful."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_032.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>32.  Qul a<u>t</u>eeAAoo All<u>a</u>ha wa<b>al</b>rrasoola fa-in tawallaw fa-inna All<u>a</u>ha l<u>a</u> yu<u>h</u>ibbu alk<u>a</u>fireen<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>32.  Say: "Obey God<br> And His Apostle":<br> But if they turn back,<br> God loveth not those<br> Who reject Faith.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_033.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>33.  Inna All<u>a</u>ha i<u>st</u>af<u>a</u> <u>a</u>dama wanoo<u>h</u>an wa<u>a</u>la ibr<u>a</u>heema wa<u>a</u>la AAimr<u>a</u>na AAal<u>a</u> alAA<u>a</u>lameen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>33.  God did choose<br> Adam and Noah, the family<br> Of Abraham, and the family<br> Of ’Imrān above all people,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_034.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>34.  <u>Th</u>urriyyatan baAA<u>d</u>uh<u>a</u> min baAA<u>d</u>in wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu sameeAAun AAaleem<b>un</b>    </p>
 <p>34.  Offspring, one of the other:<br> And God heareth<br> And knoweth all things.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_035.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>35.  I<u>th</u> q<u>a</u>lati imraatu AAimr<u>a</u>na rabbi innee na<u>th</u>artu laka m<u>a</u> fee ba<u>t</u>nee mu<u>h</u>arraran fataqabbal minnee innaka anta a<b>l</b>ssameeAAu alAAaleem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>35.  Behold! a woman of ’Imrān<br> Said: "O my Lord! I do<br>Dedicate unto Thee<br> What is in my womb<br> For Thy special service:<br> So accept this of me:<br> For Thou hearest<br> And knowest all things."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_036.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>36.  Falamm<u>a</u> wa<u>d</u>aAAat-h<u>a</u> q<u>a</u>lat rabbi innee wa<u>d</u>aAAtuh<u>a</u> onth<u>a</u> wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu aAAlamu bim<u>a</u> wa<u>d</u>aAAat walaysa a<b>l</b><u>thth</u>akaru ka<b>a</b>lonth<u>a</u> wa-innee sammaytuh<u>a</u> maryama wa-innee oAAee<u>th</u>uh<u>a</u> bika wa<u>th</u>urriyyatah<u>a</u> mina a<b>l</b>shshay<u>ta</u>ni a<b>l</b>rrajeem<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>36.  When she was delivered,<br> She said: "O my Lord!<br> Behold! I am delivered<br> Of a female child!"—<br> And God knew best<br> What she brought forth—<br> "And nowise is the male<br> Like the female.<br> I have named her Mary,<br> And I commend her<br> And her offspring<br> To Thy protection<br> From the Evil One,<br> The Rejected."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_037.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>37.  Fataqabbalah<u>a</u> rabbuh<u>a</u> biqaboolin <u>h</u>asanin waanbatah<u>a</u> nab<u>a</u>tan <u>h</u>asanan wakaffalah<u>a</u> zakariyy<u>a</u> kullam<u>a</u> dakhala AAalayh<u>a</u> zakariyy<u>a</u> almi<u>h</u>r<u>a</u>ba wajada AAindah<u>a</u> rizqan q<u>a</u>la y<u>a</u> maryamu ann<u>a</u> laki h<u>atha</u> q<u>a</u>lat huwa min AAindi All<u>a</u>hi inna All<u>a</u>ha yarzuqu man yash<u>a</u>o bighayri <u>h</u>is<u>a</u>b<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>37.  Right graciously<br> Did her Lord accept her:<br> He made her grow<br> In purity and beauty:<br> To the care of Zakarīya<br> Was she assigned.<br> Every time that he entered<br> (Her) chamber to see her,<br> He found her supplied<br> With sustenance. He said:<br> "O Mary! Whence (comes) this<br> To you?" She said:<br> "From God: for God<br> Provides sustenance<br> To whom He pleases,<br> Without measure."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_038.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>38.  Hun<u>a</u>lika daAA<u>a</u> zakariyy<u>a</u> rabbahu q<u>a</u>la rabbi hab lee min ladunka <u>th</u>urriyyatan <u>t</u>ayyibatan innaka sameeAAu a<b>l</b>dduAA<u>a</u>/-<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>38.  There here did Zakarīya<br> Pray to his Lord, saying:<br> "O my Lord! Grant unto me<br> From Thee a progeny<br> That is pure: for Thou<br> Art He that heareth prayer!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_039.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>39.  Fan<u>a</u>dat-hu almal<u>a</u>-ikatu wahuwa q<u>a</u>-imun yu<u>s</u>allee fee almi<u>h</u>r<u>a</u>bi anna All<u>a</u>ha yubashshiruka biya<u>h</u>y<u>a</u> mu<u>s</u>addiqan bikalimatin mina All<u>a</u>hi wasayyidan wa<u>h</u>a<u>s</u>ooran wanabiyyan mina a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>li<u>h</u>een<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>39.  While he was standing<br> In prayer in the chamber,<br> The angels called unto him:<br> "God doth give thee<br> Glad tidings of Yaḥyā,<br> Witnessing the truth<br> Of a Word from God, and (be<br> Besides) noble, chaste,<br> And a Prophet,<br> Of the (goodly) company<br> Of the righteous."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_040.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>40.  Q<u>a</u>la rabbi ann<u>a</u> yakoonu lee ghul<u>a</u>mun waqad balaghaniya alkibaru wa<b>i</b>mraatee AA<u>a</u>qirun q<u>a</u>la ka<u>tha</u>lika All<u>a</u>hu yafAAalu m<u>a</u> yash<u>a</u>/<b>o</b>   </p>
 <p>40.  He said: "O my Lord!<br> How shall I have a son,<br> Seeing I am very old,<br> And my wife is barren?"<br> "Thus, "was the answer,<br> "Doth God accomplish<br> What He willeth,"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_041.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>41.  Q<u>a</u>la rabbi ijAAal lee <u>a</u>yatan q<u>a</u>la <u>a</u>yatuka all<u>a</u> tukallima a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>sa thal<u>a</u>thata ayy<u>a</u>min ill<u>a</u> ramzan wa<b>o</b><u>th</u>kur rabbaka katheeran wasabbi<u>h</u> bi<b>a</b>lAAashiyyi wa<b>a</b>l-ibk<u>a</u>r<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>41.  He said: "O my Lord!<br> Give me a Sign!"<br> "Thy Sign, "was the answer,<br> "Shall be that thou<br> Shalt speak to no man<br> For three days<br> But with signals.<br> Then celebrate<br> The praises of thy Lord<br> Again and again,<br> And glorify Him<br> In the evening<br> And in the morning."</p> <div class="filenav">
