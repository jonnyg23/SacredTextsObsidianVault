 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra VII.: A’rāf, or the Heights</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 17</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_142.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>142.  Waw<u>a</u>AAadn<u>a</u> moos<u>a</u> thal<u>a</u>theena laylatan waatmamn<u>a</u>h<u>a</u> biAAashrin fatamma meeq<u>a</u>tu rabbihi arbaAAeena laylatan waq<u>a</u>la moos<u>a</u> li-akheehi h<u>a</u>roona okhlufnee fee qawmee waa<u>s</u>li<u>h</u> wal<u>a</u> tattabiAA sabeela almufsideen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>142.  We appointed for Moses<br> Thirty nights, and completed<br> (The period) with ten (more):<br> Thus was completed the term<br> (Of communion) with his Lord,<br> Forty nights. And Moses<br> Had charged his brother Aaron<br> (Before he went up):<br>"Act for me amongst my people:<br> Do right, and follow not<br> The way of those<br> Who do mischief."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_143.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>143.  Walamm<u>a</u> j<u>a</u>a moos<u>a</u> limeeq<u>a</u>tin<u>a</u> wakallamahu rabbuhu q<u>a</u>la rabbi arinee an<i><u>th</u></i>ur ilayka q<u>a</u>la lan tar<u>a</u>nee wal<u>a</u>kini on<i><u>th</u></i>ur il<u>a</u> aljabali fa-ini istaqarra mak<u>a</u>nahu fasawfa tar<u>a</u>nee falamm<u>a</u> tajall<u>a</u> rabbuhu liljabali jaAAalahu dakkan wakharra moos<u>a</u> <u>s</u>aAAiqan falamm<u>a</u> af<u>a</u>qa q<u>a</u>la sub<u>ha</u>naka tubtu ilayka waan<u>a</u> awwalu almu/mineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>143.  When Moses came<br> To the place appointed by Us,<br> And his Lord addressed him,<br> He said: "O my Lord!<br> Show (Thyself) to me,<br> That I may look upon Thee."<br> God said, "By no means<br> Canst thou see Me (direct);<br> But look upon the mount;<br> If it abide<br> In its place, then<br> Shalt thou see Me."<br> When his Lord manifested<br> His glory on the Mount,<br> He made it as dust,<br> And Moses fell down<br> In a swoon. When he<br> Recovered his senses he said:<br> "Glory be to Thee! To Thee<br> I turn in repentance, and I<br> Am the first to believe."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_144.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>144.  Q<u>a</u>la y<u>a</u> moos<u>a</u> innee i<u>st</u>afaytuka AAal<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si biris<u>a</u>l<u>a</u>tee wabikal<u>a</u>mee fakhu<u>th</u> m<u>a</u> <u>a</u>taytuka wakun mina a<b>l</b>shsh<u>a</u>kireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>144.  (God) said: "O Moses!<br> I have chosen thee<br> Above (other) men,<br> By the mission I (have<br> Given thee) and the words<br> I (have spoken to thee):<br> Take then the (revelation)<br> Which I give thee,<br> And be of those<br> Who give thanks."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_145.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>145.  Wakatabn<u>a</u> lahu fee al-alw<u>ah</u>i min kulli shay-in mawAAi<i><u>th</u></i>atan wataf<u>s</u>eelan likulli shay-in fakhu<u>th</u>h<u>a</u> biquwwatin wa/mur qawmaka ya/khu<u>th</u>oo bi-a<u>h</u>sanih<u>a</u> saoreekum d<u>a</u>ra alf<u>a</u>siqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>145.  And We ordained laws<br> For him in the Tablets<br> In all matters, both<br> Commanding and explaining<br> All things, (and said):<br> "Take and hold these<br> With firmness, and enjoin<br> Thy people to hold fast<br> By the best in the precepts:<br> Soon shall I show you<br> The homes of the wicked,—<br> (How they lie desolate)."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_146.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>146.  Saa<u>s</u>rifu AAan <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tiya alla<u>th</u>eena yatakabbaroona fee al-ar<u>d</u>i bighayri al<u>h</u>aqqi wa-in yaraw kulla <u>a</u>yatin l<u>a</u> yu/minoo bih<u>a</u> wa-in yaraw sabeela a<b>l</b>rrushdi l<u>a</u> yattakhi<u>th</u>oohu sabeelan wa-in yaraw sabeela alghayyi yattakhi<u>th</u>oohu sabeelan <u>tha</u>lika bi-annahum ka<u>thth</u>aboo bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin<u>a</u> wak<u>a</u>noo AAanh<u>a</u> gh<u>a</u>fileen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>146.  Those who behave arrogantly<br> On the earth in defiance<br>Of right—them will I<br> Turn away from My Signs:<br> Even if they see all the Signs,<br> They will not believe in them;<br> And if they see the way<br> Of right conduct, they will<br> Not adopt it as the Way;<br> But if they see the way<br> Of error, that is<br> The Way they will adopt.<br> For they have rejected<br> Our Signs, and failed<br> To take warning from them.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_147.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>147.  Wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena ka<u>thth</u>aboo bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin<u>a</u> waliq<u>a</u>-i al-<u>a</u>khirati <u>h</u>abi<u>t</u>at aAAm<u>a</u>luhum hal yujzawna ill<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo yaAAmaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>147.  Those who reject Our Signs<br> And the Meeting in the Hereafter,—<br> Vain are their deeds:<br> Can they expect to be rewarded<br> Except as they have wrought?</p> <div class="filenav">
