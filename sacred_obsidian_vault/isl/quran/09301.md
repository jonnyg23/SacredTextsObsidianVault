 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XCIII.: <u>Dh</u>uhā, or The Glorious Morning Light.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/093/093_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  Wa<b>al</b><u>dd</u>u<u>ha</u>   </p>
 <p>1.  By the Glorious<br> Morning Light,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/093/093_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  Wa<b>a</b>llayli i<u>tha</u> saj<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>2.  And by the Night<br> When it is still,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/093/093_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  M<u>a</u> waddaAAaka rabbuka wam<u>a</u> qal<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>3.  Thy Guardian-Lord<br> Hath not forsaken thee,<br> Nor is He displeased,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/093/093_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  Walal-<u>a</u>khiratu khayrun laka mina al-ool<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>4.  And verily the hereafter<br> Will be better for thee<br> Than the present.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/093/093_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  Walasawfa yuAA<u>t</u>eeka rabbuka fatar<u>da</u>   </p>
 <p>5.  And soon will thy<br> Guardian-Lord give thee<br> (That wherewith) thou<br> Shalt be well-pleased.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/093/093_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  Alam yajidka yateeman fa<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>6.  Did He not find thee<br> An orphan and give thee<br> Shelter (and care)?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/093/093_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  Wawajadaka <u>da</u>llan fahad<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>7.  And He found thee<br> Wandering, and He gave<br> Thee guidance.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/093/093_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  Wawajadaka AA<u>a</u>-ilan faaghn<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>8.  And He found thee<br> In need, and made<br> Thee independent.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/093/093_009.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>9.  Faamm<u>a</u> alyateema fal<u>a</u> taqhar   </p>
 <p>9.  Therefore, treat not<br> The orphan with harshness,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/093/093_010.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>10.  Waamm<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>ss<u>a</u>-ila fal<u>a</u> tanhar   </p>
 <p>10.  Nor repulse the petitioner<br> (Unheard);</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/093/093_011.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>11.  Waamm<u>a</u> biniAAmati rabbika fa<u>h</u>addith      </p>
 <p>11.  But the Bounty<br> Of thy Lord—<br> Rehearse and proclaim!</p>  <div class="filenav">
