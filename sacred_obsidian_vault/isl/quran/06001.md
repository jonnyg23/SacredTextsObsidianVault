 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra LX.: Mumtaḥana, or the Woman to be Examined.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/060/060_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo l<u>a</u> tattakhi<u>th</u>oo AAaduwwee waAAaduwwakum awliy<u>a</u>a tulqoona ilayhim bi<b>a</b>lmawaddati waqad kafaroo bim<u>a</u> j<u>a</u>akum mina al<u>h</u>aqqi yukhrijoona a<b>l</b>rrasoola wa-iyy<u>a</u>kum an tu/minoo bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi rabbikum in kuntum kharajtum jih<u>a</u>dan fee sabeelee wa<b>i</b>btigh<u>a</u>a mar<u>da</u>tee tusirroona ilayhim bi<b>a</b>lmawaddati waan<u>a</u> aAAlamu bim<u>a</u> akhfaytum wam<u>a</u> aAAlantum waman yafAAalhu minkum faqad <u>d</u>alla saw<u>a</u>a a<b>l</b>ssabeel<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>1.  O ye who believe!<br> Take not My enemies<br> And yours as friends<br> (Or protectors),—offering them<br> (Your) love, even though<br> They have rejected the Truth<br> That has come to you,<br> And have (on the contrary)<br> Driven out the Prophet<br> And yourselves (from your homes),<br> (Simply) because ye believe<br> In God your Lord!<br> If ye have come out<br> To strive in My Way<br> And to seek My Good Pleasure,<br> (Take them not as friends),<br> Holding secret converse<br> Of love (and friendship)<br> With them: for I know<br> Full well all that ye<br> Conceal and all that ye<br> Reveal. And any of you<br> That does this has strayed<br> From the Straight Path.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/060/060_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  In yathqafookum yakoonoo lakam aAAd<u>a</u>an wayabsu<u>t</u>oo ilaykum aydiyahum waalsinatahum bi<b>al</b>ssoo-i wawaddoo law takfuroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>2.  If they were to get<br> The better of you,<br>They would behave to you<br> As enemies, and stretch forth<br> Their hands and their tongues<br> Against you for evil;<br> And they desire that ye<br> Should reject the Truth.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/060/060_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  Lan tanfaAAakum ar<u>ha</u>mukum wal<u>a</u> awl<u>a</u>dukum yawma alqiy<u>a</u>mati yaf<u>s</u>ilu baynakum wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu bim<u>a</u> taAAmaloona ba<u>s</u>eer<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>3.  Of no profit to you<br> Will be your relatives<br> And your children<br> On the Day of Judgment:<br> He will judge between you:<br> For God sees well<br> All that ye do.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/060/060_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  Qad k<u>a</u>nat lakum oswatun <u>h</u>asanatun fee ibr<u>a</u>heema wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena maAAahu i<u>th</u> q<u>a</u>loo liqawmihim inn<u>a</u> bura<u>a</u>o minkum wamimm<u>a</u> taAAbudoona min dooni All<u>a</u>hi kafarn<u>a</u> bikum wabad<u>a</u> baynan<u>a</u> wabaynakumu alAAad<u>a</u>watu wa<b>a</b>lbagh<u>da</u>o abadan <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> tu/minoo bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi wa<u>h</u>dahu ill<u>a</u> qawla ibr<u>a</u>heema li-abeehi laastaghfiranna laka wam<u>a</u> amliku laka mina All<u>a</u>hi min shay-in rabban<u>a</u> AAalayka tawakkaln<u>a</u> wa-ilayka anabn<u>a</u> wa-ilayka alma<u>s</u>eer<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>4.  There is for you<br> An excellent example (to follow)<br> In Abraham and those with him,<br> When they said<br> To their people:<br> "We are clear of you<br> And of whatever ye worship<br> Besides God: we have rejected<br> You, and there has arisen,<br> Between us and you, enmity<br> And hatred for ever,—unless<br> Ye believe in God<br> And Him alone":<br> But not when Abraham<br> Said to his father:<br> "I will pray for forgiveness<br>For thee, though I have<br> No power (to get) aught<br> On thy behalf from God."<br> (They prayed): "Our Lord!<br> In Thee do we trust,<br> And to Thee do we turn<br> In repentance: to Thee<br> Is (our) final Goal.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/060/060_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  Rabban<u>a</u> l<u>a</u> tajAAaln<u>a</u> fitnatan lilla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo wa<b>i</b>ghfir lan<u>a</u> rabban<u>a</u> innaka anta alAAazeezu al<u>h</u>akeem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>5.  "Our Lord! Make us not<br> A (test and) trial<br> For the Unbelievers,<br> But forgive us, our Lord!<br> For Thou art the Exalted<br> In Might, the Wise."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/060/060_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  Laqad k<u>a</u>na lakum feehim oswatun <u>h</u>asanatun liman k<u>a</u>na yarjoo All<u>a</u>ha wa<b>a</b>lyawma al-<u>a</u>khira waman yatawalla fa-inna All<u>a</u>ha huwa alghanniyyu al<u>h</u>ameed<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>6.  There was indeed in them<br> An excellent example for you<br> To follow,—for those<br> Whose hope is in God<br> And in the Last Day.<br> But if any turn away,<br> Truly God is Free of all<br> Wants, Worthy of all Praise.</p> <div class="filenav">
