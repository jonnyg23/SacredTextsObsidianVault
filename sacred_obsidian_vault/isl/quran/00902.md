 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra IX.: Tauba (Repentance) or Barāat (Immunity).</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 2</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  Kayfa yakoonu lilmushrikeena AAahdun AAinda All<u>a</u>hi waAAinda rasoolihi ill<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena AA<u>a</u>hadtum AAinda almasjidi al<u>h</u>ar<u>a</u>mi fam<u>a</u> istaq<u>a</u>moo lakum fa<b>i</b>staqeemoo lahum inna All<u>a</u>ha yu<u>h</u>ibbu almuttaqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>7.  How can there be a league,<br> Before God and His Apostle,<br> With the Pagans, except those<br> With whom ye made a treaty<br> Near the Sacred Mosque?<br> As long as these stand true<br> To you, stand ye true to them:<br> For God doth love the righteous.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  Kayfa wa-in ya<i><u>th</u></i>haroo AAalaykum l<u>a</u> yarquboo feekum illan wal<u>a</u> <u>th</u>immatan yur<u>d</u>oonakum bi-afw<u>a</u>hihim wata/b<u>a</u> quloobuhum waaktharuhum f<u>a</u>siqoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>8.  How (can there be such a league),<br> Seeing that if they get an advantage<br> Over you, they respect not<br> In you the ties either of kinship<br>Or of covenant? With (fair words<br> From) their mouths they entice you,<br> But their hearts are averse<br> From you; and most of them<br> Are rebellious and wicked.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_009.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>9.  Ishtaraw bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>ti All<u>a</u>hi thamanan qaleelan fa<u>s</u>addoo AAan sabeelihi innahum s<u>a</u>a m<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo yaAAmaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>9.  The Signs of God have they sold<br> For a miserable price,<br> And (many) have they hindered<br> From His Way: evil indeed<br> Are the deeds they have done.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_010.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>10.  L<u>a</u> yarquboona fee mu/minin illan wal<u>a</u> <u>th</u>immatan waol<u>a</u>-ika humu almuAAtadoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>10.  In a Believer they respect not<br> The ties either of kinship<br> Or of covenant! It is they<br> Who have transgressed all bounds.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_011.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>11.  Fa-in t<u>a</u>boo waaq<u>a</u>moo a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>al<u>a</u>ta wa<u>a</u>tawoo a<b>l</b>zzak<u>a</u>ta fa-ikhw<u>a</u>nukum fee a<b>l</b>ddeeni wanufa<u>ss</u>ilu al-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>ti liqawmin yaAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>11.  But (even so), if they repent,<br> Establish regular prayers,<br> And practise regular charity,—<br> They are your brethren in Faith:<br> (Thus) do We explain the Signs<br> in detail, for those who understand.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_012.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>12.  Wa-in nakathoo aym<u>a</u>nahum min baAAdi AAahdihim wa<u>t</u>aAAanoo fee deenikum faq<u>a</u>tiloo a-immata alkufri innahum l<u>a</u> aym<u>a</u>na lahum laAAallahum yantahoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>12.  But if they violate their oaths<br> After their covenant,<br> And taunt you for your Faith,—<br> Fight ye the chiefs of Unfaith:<br> For their oaths are nothing to them:<br> That thus they may be restrained.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_013.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>13.  Al<u>a</u> tuq<u>a</u>tiloona qawman nakathoo aym<u>a</u>nahum wahammoo bi-ikhr<u>a</u>ji a<b>l</b>rrasooli wahum badaookum awwala marratin atakhshawnahum fa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu a<u>h</u>aqqu an takhshawhu in kuntum mu/mineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>13.  Will ye not fight people<br> Who violated their oaths,<br>Plotted to expel the Apostle,<br> And took the aggressive<br> By being the first (to assault) you?<br> Do ye fear them? Nay,<br> It is God Whom ye should<br> More justly fear, if ye believe!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_014.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>14.  Q<u>a</u>tiloohum yuAAa<u>thth</u>ibhumu All<u>a</u>hu bi-aydeekum wayukhzihim wayan<u>s</u>urkum AAalayhim wayashfi <u>s</u>udoora qawmin mu/mineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>14.  Fight them, and God will<br> Punish them by your hands,<br> Cover them with shame,<br> Help you (to victory) over them,<br> Heal the breasts of Believers,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_015.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>15.  Wayu<u>th</u>hib ghay<i><u>th</u></i>a quloobihim wayatoobu All<u>a</u>hu AAal<u>a</u> man yash<u>a</u>o wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu AAaleemun <u>h</u>akeem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>15.  And still the indignation of their hearts!<br> For God will turn (in mercy)<br> To whom He will; and God<br> Is All-Knowing, All-Wise.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/009/009_016.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>16.  Am <u>h</u>asibtum an tutrakoo walamm<u>a</u> yaAAlami All<u>a</u>hu alla<u>th</u>eena j<u>a</u>hadoo minkum walam yattakhi<u>th</u>oo min dooni All<u>a</u>hi wal<u>a</u> rasoolihi wal<u>a</u> almu/mineena waleejatan wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu khabeerun bim<u>a</u> taAAmaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>16.  Or think ye that ye<br> Shall be abandoned,<br> As though God did not know'<br> Those among you who strive<br> With might and main, and take<br> None for friends and protectors<br> Except God, His Apostle,<br> And the (community of) Believers?<br> But God is well-acquainted<br> With (all) that ye do.</p> <div class="filenav">
