 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra VI.: An’ām, or Cattle.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 20</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_155.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>155.  Wah<u>atha</u> kit<u>a</u>bun anzaln<u>a</u>hu mub<u>a</u>rakun fa<b>i</b>ttabiAAoohu wa<b>i</b>ttaqoo laAAallakum tur<u>h</u>amoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>155.  And this is a Book<br> Which We have revealed<br> As a blessing: so follow it<br> And be righteous, that ye<br> May receive mercy:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_156.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>156.  An taqooloo innam<u>a</u> onzila alkit<u>a</u>bu AAal<u>a</u> <u>ta</u>-ifatayni min qablin<u>a</u> wa-in kunn<u>a</u> AAan dir<u>a</u>satihim lagh<u>a</u>fileen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>156.  Lest ye should say:<br> "The Book was sent down<br> To two Peoples before us,<br> And for our part, we<br> Remained unacquainted<br>With all that they learned<br> By assiduous study;"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_157.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>157.  Aw taqooloo law ann<u>a</u> onzila AAalayn<u>a</u> alkit<u>a</u>bu lakunn<u>a</u> ahd<u>a</u> minhum faqad j<u>a</u>akum bayyinatun min rabbikum wahudan wara<u>h</u>matun faman a<i><u>th</u></i>lamu mimman ka<u>thth</u>aba bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>ti All<u>a</u>hi wa<u>s</u>adafa AAanh<u>a</u> sanajzee alla<u>th</u>eena ya<u>s</u>difoona AAan <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin<u>a</u> soo-a alAAa<u>tha</u>bi bim<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo ya<u>s</u>difoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>157.  Or lest ye should say:<br> "If the Book had only<br> Been sent down to us,<br> We should have followed<br> Its guidance better than they."<br> Now then hath come<br> Unto you a Clear (Sign)<br> From your Lord,—and a guide<br> And a mercy: then who<br> Could do more wrong<br> Than one who rejecteth<br> God's Signs, and turneth<br> Away therefrom? In good time<br> Shall We requite those<br> Who turn away from Our Signs,<br> With a dreadful penalty,<br> For their turning away.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_158.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>158.  Hal yan<i><u>th</u></i>uroona ill<u>a</u> an ta/tiyahumu almal<u>a</u>-ikatu aw ya/tiya rabbuka aw ya/tiya baAA<u>d</u>u <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>ti rabbika yawma ya/tee baAA<u>d</u>u <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>ti rabbika l<u>a</u> yanfaAAu nafsan eem<u>a</u>nuh<u>a</u> lam takun <u>a</u>manat min qablu aw kasabat fee eem<u>a</u>nih<u>a</u> khayran quli inta<i><u>th</u></i>iroo inn<u>a</u> munta<i><u>th</u></i>iroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>158.  Are they waiting to see<br> If the angels come to them,<br> Or thy Lord (Himself),<br> Or certain of the Signs<br> Of thy Lord!<br> The day that certain<br> Of the Signs of thy Lord<br> Do come, no good<br> Will it do to a soul<br> To believe in them then,<br> If it believed not before<br> Nor earned righteousness<br> Through its Faith. Say:<br> "Wait ye: we too<br> Are waiting."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_159.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>159.  Inna alla<u>th</u>eena farraqoo deenahum wak<u>a</u>noo shiyaAAan lasta minhum fee shay-in innam<u>a</u> amruhum il<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi thumma yunabbi-ohum bim<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo yafAAaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>159.  As for those who divide<br> Their religion and break up<br> Into sects, thou hast<br> No part in them in the least:<br> Their affair is with God:<br> He will in the end<br> Tell them the truth<br> Of all that they did.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_160.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>160.  Man j<u>a</u>a bi<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>asanati falahu AAashru amth<u>a</u>lih<u>a</u> waman j<u>a</u>a bi<b>al</b>ssayyi-<u>a</u>ti fal<u>a</u> yujz<u>a</u> ill<u>a</u> mithlah<u>a</u> wahum l<u>a</u> yu<i><u>th</u></i>lamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>160.  He that doeth good<br> Shall have ten times<br> As much to his credit:<br> He that doeth evil<br> Shall only be recompensed<br> According to his evil:<br> No wrong shall be done<br> Unto (any of) them.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_161.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>161.  Qul innanee had<u>a</u>nee rabbee il<u>a</u> <u>s</u>ir<u>at</u>in mustaqeemin deenan qiyaman millata ibr<u>a</u>heema <u>h</u>aneefan wam<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>na mina almushrikeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>161.  Say: "Verily, my Lord<br> Hath guided me to<br> A Way that is straight,—<br> A religion of right,<br> The Path (trod) by Abraham<br> The true in faith,<br> And he (certainly)<br> Joined not gods with God."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_162.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>162.  Qul inna <u>s</u>al<u>a</u>tee wanusukee wama<u>h</u>y<u>a</u>ya wamam<u>a</u>tee lill<u>a</u>hi rabbi alAA<u>a</u>lameen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>162.  Say: "Truly, my prayer<br> And my service of sacrifice,<br> My life and my death,<br> Are (all) for God,<br> The Cherisher of the Worlds:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_163.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>163.  L<u>a</u> shareeka lahu wabi<u>tha</u>lika omirtu waan<u>a</u> awwalu almuslimeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>163.  No partner hath He:<br> This am I commanded,<br> And I am the first<br> Of those who bow<br> To His Will.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_164.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>164.  Qul aghayra All<u>a</u>hi abghee rabban wahuwa rabbu kulli shay-in wal<u>a</u> taksibu kullu nafsin ill<u>a</u> AAalayh<u>a</u> wal<u>a</u> taziru w<u>a</u>ziratun wizra okhr<u>a</u> thumma il<u>a</u> rabbikum marjiAAukum fayunabbi-okum bim<u>a</u> kuntum feehi takhtalifoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>164.  Say: "Shall I seek<br> For (my) Cherisher<br>Other than God,<br> When He is the Cherisher<br> Of all things (that exist)?<br> Every soul draws the meed<br> Of its acts on none<br> But itself: no bearer<br> Of burdens can bear<br> The burden of another.<br> Your goal in the end<br> Is towards God: He will tell<br> You the truth of the things<br> Wherein ye disputed."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_165.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>165.  Wahuwa alla<u>th</u>ee jaAAalakum khal<u>a</u>-ifa al-ar<u>d</u>i warafaAAa baAA<u>d</u>akum fawqa baAA<u>d</u>in daraj<u>a</u>tin liyabluwakum fee m<u>a</u> <u>a</u>t<u>a</u>kum inna rabbaka sareeAAu alAAiq<u>a</u>bi wa-innahu laghafoorun ra<u>h</u>eem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>165.  It is He Who hath made<br> You (His) agents, inheritors<br> Of the earth: He hath raised<br> You in ranks, some above<br> Others: that He may try you<br> In the gifts He hath given you:<br> For thy Lord is quick<br> In punishment: yet He<br> Is indeed Oft-forgiving,<br> Most Merciful.</p>  <div class="filenav">
