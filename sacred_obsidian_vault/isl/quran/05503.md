 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/055/055_046.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>46.  Waliman kh<u>a</u>fa maq<u>a</u>ma rabbihi jannat<u>a</u>n<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>46.  But for such as fear<br> The time when they will<br> Stand before (the Judgment Seat<br> Of) their Lord,<br> There will be two Gardens—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/055/055_047.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>47.  Fabi-ayyi <u>a</u>l<u>a</u>-i rabbikum<u>a</u> tuka<u>thth</u>ib<u>a</u>n<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>47.  Then which of the favours<br> Of your Lord will ye deny?—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/055/055_048.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>48.  <u>Th</u>aw<u>a</u>t<u>a</u> afn<u>a</u>n<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>48.  Containing all kinds<br> (Of trees and delights);—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/055/055_049.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>49.  Fabi-ayyi <u>a</u>l<u>a</u>-i rabbikum<u>a</u> tuka<u>thth</u>ib<u>a</u>n<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>49.  Then which of the favours<br> Of your Lord will ye deny?—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/055/055_050.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>50.  Feehim<u>a</u> AAayn<u>a</u>ni tajriy<u>a</u>n<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>50.  In them (each) will be<br> Two Springs flowing (free);</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/055/055_051.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>51.  Fabi-ayyi <u>a</u>l<u>a</u>-i rabbikum<u>a</u> tuka<u>thth</u>ib<u>a</u>n<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>51.  Then which of the favours<br> Of your Lord will ye deny?—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/055/055_052.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>52.  Feehim<u>a</u> min kulli f<u>a</u>kihatin zawj<u>a</u>n<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>52.  In them will be Fruits<br> Of every kind, two and two.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/055/055_053.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>53.  Fabi-ayyi <u>a</u>l<u>a</u>-i rabbikum<u>a</u> tuka<u>thth</u>ib<u>a</u>n<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>53.  Then which of the favours<br> Of your Lord will ye deny?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/055/055_054.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>54.  Muttaki-eena AAal<u>a</u> furushin ba<u>ta</u>-inuh<u>a</u> min istabraqin wajan<u>a</u> aljannatayni d<u>a</u>n<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>54.  They will recline on Carpets,<br> Whose inner linings will be<br> Of rich brocade: the Fruit<br> Of the Gardens will be<br> Near (and easy of reach).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/055/055_055.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>55.  Fabi-ayyi <u>a</u>l<u>a</u>-i rabbikum<u>a</u> tuka<u>thth</u>ib<u>a</u>n<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>55.  Then which of the favours<br> Of your Lord will ye deny?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/055/055_056.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>56.  Feehinna q<u>as</u>ir<u>a</u>tu a<b>l</b><u>tt</u>arfi lam ya<u>t</u>mithhunna insun qablahum wal<u>a</u> j<u>a</u>n<b>nun</b>   </p>
 <p>56.  In them will be (Maidens),<br> Chaste, restraining their glances,<br> Whom no man or Jinn<br> Before them has touched;—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/055/055_057.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>57.  Fabi-ayyi <u>a</u>l<u>a</u>-i rabbikum<u>a</u> tuka<u>thth</u>ib<u>a</u>n<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>57.  Then which of the favours<br> Of your Lord will ye deny?—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/055/055_058.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>58.  Kaannahunna aly<u>a</u>qootu wa<b>a</b>lmarj<u>a</u>n<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>58.  Like unto rubies and coral.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/055/055_059.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>59.  Fabi-ayyi <u>a</u>l<u>a</u>-i rabbikum<u>a</u> tuka<u>thth</u>ib<u>a</u>n<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>59.  Then which of the favours<br> Of your Lord will ye deny?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/055/055_060.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>60.  Hal jaz<u>a</u>o al-i<u>h</u>s<u>a</u>ni ill<u>a</u> al-i<u>h</u>s<u>a</u>n<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>60.  Is there any Reward<br> For Good-other than Good?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/055/055_061.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>61.  Fabi-ayyi <u>a</u>l<u>a</u>-i rabbikum<u>a</u> tuka<u>thth</u>ib<u>a</u>n<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>61.  Then which of the favours<br> Of your Lord will ye deny?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/055/055_062.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>62.  Wamin doonihim<u>a</u> jannat<u>a</u>n<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>62.  And besides these two,<br> There are two other Gardens,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/055/055_063.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>63.  Fabi-ayyi <u>a</u>l<u>a</u>-i rabbikum<u>a</u> tuka<u>thth</u>ib<u>a</u>n<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>63.  Then which of the favours<br> Of your Lord will ye deny?—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/055/055_064.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>64.  Mudh<u>a</u>mmat<u>a</u>n<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>64.  Dark-green in colour<br> (From plentiful watering).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/055/055_065.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>65.  Fabi-ayyi <u>a</u>l<u>a</u>-i rabbikum<u>a</u> tuka<u>thth</u>ib<u>a</u>n<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>65.  Then which of the favours<br> Of your Lord will ye deny?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/055/055_066.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>66.  Feehim<u>a</u> AAayn<u>a</u>ni na<u>dda</u>khat<u>a</u>n<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>66.  In them (each) will be<br> Two Springs pouring forth water<br> In continuous abundance:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/055/055_067.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>67.  Fabi-ayyi <u>a</u>l<u>a</u>-i rabbikum<u>a</u> tuka<u>thth</u>ib<u>a</u>n<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>67.  Then which of the favours<br> Of your Lord will ye deny?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/055/055_068.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>68.  Feehim<u>a</u> f<u>a</u>kihatun wanakhlun warumm<u>a</u>n<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>68.  In them will be Fruits,<br> And dates and pomegranates:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/055/055_069.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>69.  Fabi-ayyi <u>a</u>l<u>a</u>-i rabbikum<u>a</u> tuka<u>thth</u>ib<u>a</u>n<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>69.  Then which of the favours<br> Of your Lord will ye deny?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/055/055_070.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>70.  Feehinna khayr<u>a</u>tun <u>h</u>is<u>a</u>n<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>70.  In them will be<br> Fair (Companions), good,<br> beautiful;—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/055/055_071.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>71.  Fabi-ayyi <u>a</u>l<u>a</u>-i rabbikum<u>a</u> tuka<u>thth</u>ib<u>a</u>n<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>71.  Then which of the favours<br> Of your Lord will ye deny?—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/055/055_072.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>72.  <u>H</u>oorun maq<u>s</u>oor<u>a</u>tun fee alkhiy<u>a</u>m<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>72.  Companions restrained (as to<br> Their glances), in (goodly)<br> pavilions;—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/055/055_073.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>73.  Fabi-ayyi <u>a</u>l<u>a</u>-i rabbikum<u>a</u> tuka<u>thth</u>ib<u>a</u>n<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>73.  Then which of the favours<br> Of your Lord will ye deny?—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/055/055_074.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>74.  Lam ya<u>t</u>mithhunna insun qablahum wal<u>a</u> j<u>a</u>n<b>nun</b>   </p>
 <p>74.  Whom no man or Jinn<br> Before them has touched;—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/055/055_075.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>75.  Fabi-ayyi <u>a</u>l<u>a</u>-i rabbikum<u>a</u> tuka<u>thth</u>ib<u>a</u>n<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>75.  Then which of the favours<br> Of your Lord will ye deny?—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/055/055_076.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>76.  Muttaki-eena AAal<u>a</u> rafrafin khu<u>d</u>rin waAAabqariyyin <u>h</u>is<u>a</u>n<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>76.  Reclining on green Cushions<br> And rich Carpets of beauty.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/055/055_077.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>77.  Fabi-ayyi <u>a</u>l<u>a</u>-i rabbikum<u>a</u> tuka<u>thth</u>ib<u>a</u>n<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>77.  Then which of the favours<br> Of your Lord will ye deny?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/055/055_078.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>78.  Tab<u>a</u>raka ismu rabbika <u>th</u>ee aljal<u>a</u>li wa<b>a</b>l-ikr<u>a</u>m<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>78.  Blessed be the name<br> Of thy Lord,<br> Full of Majesty,<br> Bounty and Honour.</p>  <div class="filenav">
