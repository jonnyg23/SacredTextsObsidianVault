 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XCVIII.: Baiyina, or The Clear Evidence.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/098/098_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  Lam yakuni alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo min ahli alkit<u>a</u>bi wa<b>a</b>lmushrikeena munfakkeena <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> ta/tiyahumu albayyina<b>tu</b>   </p>
 <p>1.  Those who reject (Truth),<br> Among the People of the Book<br> And among the Polytheists,<br> Were not going to depart<br> (From their ways) until<br> There should come to them<br> Clear Evidence,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/098/098_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  Rasoolun mina All<u>a</u>hi yatloo <u>s</u>u<u>h</u>ufan mu<u>t</u>ahhara<b>tan</b>   </p>
 <p>2.  An apostle from God,<br> Rehearsing scriptures<br> Kept pure and holy:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/098/098_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  Feeh<u>a</u> kutubun qayyima<b>tun</b>   </p>
 <p>3.  Wherein are laws (or decrees)<br> Right and straight.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/098/098_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  Wam<u>a</u> tafarraqa alla<u>th</u>eena ootoo alkit<u>a</u>ba ill<u>a</u> min baAAdi m<u>a</u> j<u>a</u>at-humu albayyina<b>tu</b>    </p>
 <p>4.  Nor did the People<br> Of the Book<br>Make schisms,<br> Until after there came<br> To them Clear Evidence.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/098/098_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  Wam<u>a</u> omiroo ill<u>a</u> liyaAAbudoo All<u>a</u>ha mukhli<u>s</u>eena lahu a<b>l</b>ddeena <u>h</u>unaf<u>a</u>a wayuqeemoo a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>al<u>a</u>ta wayu/too a<b>l</b>zzak<u>a</u>ta wa<u>tha</u>lika deenu alqayyima<b>ti</b>   </p>
 <p>5.  And they have been commanded<br> No more than this:<br> To worship God,<br> Offering Him sincere devotion,<br> Being True (in faith);<br> To establish regular Prayer;<br> And to practise regular Charity;<br> And that is the Religion<br> Right and Straight.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/098/098_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  Inna alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo min ahli alkit<u>a</u>bi wa<b>a</b>lmushrikeena fee n<u>a</u>ri jahannama kh<u>a</u>lideena feeh<u>a</u> ol<u>a</u>-ika hum sharru albariyya<b>ti</b>   </p>
 <p>6.  Those who reject (Truth),<br> Among the People of the Book<br> And among the Polytheists,<br> Will be in hell-fire,<br> To dwell therein (for aye).<br> They are the worst<br> Of creatures.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/098/098_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  Inna alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo waAAamiloo a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>li<u>ha</u>ti ol<u>a</u>-ika hum khayru albariyya<b>ti</b>   </p>
 <p>7.  Those who have faith<br> And do righteous deeds,—<br> They are the best<br> Of creatures.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/098/098_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  Jaz<u>a</u>ohum AAinda rabbihim jann<u>a</u>tu AAadnin tajree min ta<u>h</u>tih<u>a</u> al-anh<u>a</u>ru kh<u>a</u>lideena feeh<u>a</u> abadan ra<u>d</u>iya All<u>a</u>hu AAanhum wara<u>d</u>oo AAanhu <u>tha</u>lika liman khashiya rabbah<b>u</b>    </p>
 <p>8.  Their reward is with God:<br> Gardens of Eternity,<br> Beneath which rivers flow;<br> They will dwell therein<br> For ever; God well pleased<br> With them, and they with Him;<br> All this for such as<br> Fear their Lord and Cherisher.</p>  <div class="filenav">
