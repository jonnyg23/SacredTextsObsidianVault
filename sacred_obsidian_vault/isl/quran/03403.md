 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXXIV.: Sabā, or the City of Sabā</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 3</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/034/034_022.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>22.  Quli odAAoo alla<u>th</u>eena zaAAamtum min dooni All<u>a</u>hi l<u>a</u> yamlikoona mithq<u>a</u>la <u>th</u>arratin fee a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wal<u>a</u> fee al-ar<u>d</u>i wam<u>a</u> lahum feehim<u>a</u> min shirkin wam<u>a</u> lahu minhum min <i><u>th</u></i>aheer<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>22.  Say: "Call upon other (gods)<br> Whom ye fancy, besides God:<br> They have no power,<br> Not the weight of an atom,—<br> In the heavens or on earth:<br> No (sort of) share have they<br> Therein, nor is any of them<br> A helper to God.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/034/034_023.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>23.  Wal<u>a</u> tanfaAAu a<b>l</b>shshaf<u>a</u>AAatu AAindahu ill<u>a</u> liman a<u>th</u>ina lahu <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> i<u>tha</u> fuzziAAa AAan quloobihim q<u>a</u>loo m<u>atha</u> q<u>a</u>la rabbukum q<u>a</u>loo al<u>h</u>aqqa wahuwa alAAaliyyu alkabeer<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>23.  "No intercession can avail<br> In His Presence, except for those<br> For whom He has granted<br> Permission. So far (is this<br> The case) that, when terror<br> Is removed from their hearts<br> (At the Day of Judgment, then)<br> Will they say, "What is it<br> That your Lord commanded?"<br> They will say, "That which is<br> True and just; and He is<br> The Most High, Most Great."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/034/034_024.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>24.  Qul man yarzuqukum mina a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i quli All<u>a</u>hu wa-inn<u>a</u> aw iyy<u>a</u>kum laAAal<u>a</u> hudan aw fee <u>d</u>al<u>a</u>lin mubeen<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>24.  Say: "Who gives you<br> Sustenance, from the heavens<br>And the earth?" Say:<br> "It is God; and certain it is<br> That either we or ye<br> Are on right guidance<br> Or in manifest error!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/034/034_025.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>25.  Qul l<u>a</u> tus-aloona AAamm<u>a</u> ajramn<u>a</u> wal<u>a</u> nus-alu AAamm<u>a</u> taAAmaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>25.  Say: "Ye shall not be<br> Questioned as to our sins,<br> Nor shall we be questioned<br> As to what ye do."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/034/034_026.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>26.  Qul yajmaAAu baynan<u>a</u> rabbun<u>a</u> thumma yafta<u>h</u>u baynan<u>a</u> bi<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>aqqi wahuwa alfatt<u>ah</u>u alAAaleem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>26.  Say: "Our Lord will gather us<br> Together and will in the end<br> Decide the matter between us<br> (And you) in truth and justice:<br> And He is the One to decide,<br> The One Who knows all."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/034/034_027.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>27.  Qul arooniya alla<u>th</u>eena al<u>h</u>aqtum bihi shurak<u>a</u>a kall<u>a</u> bal huwa All<u>a</u>hu alAAazeezu al<u>h</u>akeem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>27.  Say: "Show me those whom<br> Ye have joined with Him<br> As partners: by no means<br> (Can ye). Nay, He is God,<br> The Exalted in Power,<br> The Wise."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/034/034_028.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>28.  Wam<u>a</u> arsaln<u>a</u>ka ill<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>ffatan li<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si basheeran wana<u>th</u>eeran wal<u>a</u>kinna akthara a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si l<u>a</u> yaAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>28.  We have not sent thee<br> But as a universal (Messenger)<br>To men, giving them<br> Glad tidings, and warning them<br> (Against sin), but most men<br> Understand not.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/034/034_029.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>29.  Wayaqooloona mat<u>a</u> h<u>atha</u> alwaAAdu in kuntum <u>sa</u>diqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>29.  They say: "When will this<br> Promise (come to pass)<br> If ye are telling the truth?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/034/034_030.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>30.  Qul lakum meeAA<u>a</u>du yawmin l<u>a</u> tasta/khiroona AAanhu s<u>a</u>AAatan wal<u>a</u> tastaqdimoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>30.  Say: "The appointment to you<br> Is for a Day, which ye<br> Cannot put back for an hour<br> Nor put forward."</p> <div class="filenav">
