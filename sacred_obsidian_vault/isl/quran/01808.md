 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XVIII.: Kahf, or the Cave.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 8</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_054.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>54.  Walaqad <u>s</u>arrafn<u>a</u> fee h<u>atha</u> alqur-<u>a</u>ni li<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si min kulli mathalin wak<u>a</u>na al-ins<u>a</u>nu akthara shay-in jadal<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>54.  We have explained<br> In detail in this Qur-ān,<br> For the benefit of mankind,<br> Every kind of similitude:<br> But man is, in most things,<br> Contentious.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_055.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>55.  Wam<u>a</u> manaAAa a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>sa an yu/minoo i<u>th</u> j<u>a</u>ahumu alhud<u>a</u> wayastaghfiroo rabbahum ill<u>a</u> an ta/tiyahum sunnatu al-awwaleena aw ya/tiyahumu alAAa<u>tha</u>bu qubul<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>55.  And what is there<br> To keep back men<br> From believing, now that<br> Guidance has come to them,<br> Nor from praying for forgiveness<br> From their Lord, but that<br> (They ask that) the ways<br> Of the ancients be repeated<br> With them, or the Wrath<br> Be brought to them<br> Face to face?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_056.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>56.  Wam<u>a</u> nursilu almursaleena ill<u>a</u> mubashshireena wamun<u>th</u>ireena wayuj<u>a</u>dilu alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo bi<b>a</b>lb<u>at</u>ili liyud<u>h</u>i<u>d</u>oo bihi al<u>h</u>aqqa wa<b>i</b>ttakha<u>th</u>oo <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tee wam<u>a</u> on<u>th</u>iroo huzuw<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>56.  We only send the apostles<br> To give glad tidings<br> And to give warnings<br> But the Unbelievers dispute<br> With vain argument, in order<br> Therewith to weaken the truth,<br> And they treat My Signs<br> As a jest, as also the fact<br> That they are warned!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_057.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>57.  Waman a<i><u>th</u></i>lamu mimman <u>th</u>ukkira bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>ti rabbihi faaAAra<u>d</u>a AAanh<u>a</u> wanasiya m<u>a</u> qaddamat yad<u>a</u>hu inn<u>a</u> jaAAaln<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> quloobihim akinnatan an yafqahoohu wafee <u>atha</u>nihim waqran wa-in tadAAuhum il<u>a</u> alhud<u>a</u> falan yahtadoo i<u>th</u>an abad<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>57.  And who doth more wrong<br> Than one who is reminded<br> Of the Signs of his Lord,<br> But turns away from them,<br> Forgetting the (deeds) which his hands<br> Have sent forth? Verily We<br> Have set veils over their hearts<br> Lest they should understand this,<br> and over their ears, deafness.<br> If thou callest them<br> To guidance, even then<br> Will they never accept guidance.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_058.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>58.  Warabbuka alghafooru <u>th</u>oo a<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>mati law yu-<u>a</u>khi<u>th</u>uhum bim<u>a</u> kasaboo laAAajjala lahumu alAAa<u>tha</u>ba bal lahum mawAAidun lan yajidoo min doonihi maw-il<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>58.  But your Lord is most forgiving,<br> Full of Mercy. If he were<br> To call them (at once) to account<br> For what they have earned,<br> then surely He would<br> Have hastened their Punishment:<br> But they have their appointed<br> Time, beyond which they<br> Will find no refuge.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_059.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>59.  Watilka alqur<u>a</u> ahlakn<u>a</u>hum lamm<u>a</u> <i><u>th</u></i>alamoo wajaAAaln<u>a</u> limahlikihim mawAAid<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>59.  Such were the populations<br> We destroyed when they<br> Committed iniquities; but<br> We fixed an appointed time<br> For their destruction.</p> <div class="filenav">
