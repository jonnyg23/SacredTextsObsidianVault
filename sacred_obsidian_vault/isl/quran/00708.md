 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra VII.: A’rāf, or the Heights</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 8</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_059.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>59.  Laqad arsaln<u>a</u> noo<u>h</u>an il<u>a</u> qawmihi faq<u>a</u>la y<u>a</u> qawmi oAAbudoo All<u>a</u>ha m<u>a</u> lakum min il<u>a</u>hin ghayruhu innee akh<u>a</u>fu AAalaykum AAa<u>tha</u>ba yawmin AAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>59.  We sent Noah to his people.<br> He said: "O my people!<br> Worship God! ye have<br> No other god but Him.<br> I fear for you the Punishment<br> Of a dreadful Day!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_060.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>60.  Q<u>a</u>la almalao min qawmihi inn<u>a</u> lanar<u>a</u>ka fee <u>d</u>al<u>a</u>lin mubeen<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>60.  The leaders of his people<br> Said: "Ah! we see thee<br> Evidently wandering (in mind)."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_061.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>61.  Q<u>a</u>la y<u>a</u> qawmi laysa bee <u>d</u>al<u>a</u>latun wal<u>a</u>kinnee rasoolun min rabbi alAA<u>a</u>lameen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>61.  He said: "O my people!<br> No wandering is there<br> In my (mind): on the contrary<br> I am an apostle from<br> The Lord and Cherisher<br> Of the Worlds!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_062.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>62.  Oballighukum ris<u>a</u>l<u>a</u>ti rabbee waan<u>s</u>a<u>h</u>u lakum waaAAlamu mina All<u>a</u>hi m<u>a</u> l<u>a</u> taAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>62.  "I but fulfil towards you<br> The duties of my Lord's mission:<br> Sincere is my advice to you,<br> And I know from God<br> Something that ye know not.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_063.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>63.  Awa AAajibtum an j<u>a</u>akum <u>th</u>ikrun min rabbikum AAal<u>a</u> rajulin minkum liyun<u>th</u>irakum walitattaqoo walaAAallakum tur<u>h</u>amoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>63.  "Do ye wonder that<br> There hath come to you<br> A message from your Lord,<br> Through a man of your own<br> People, to warn you,—<br> So that ye may fear God<br> And haply receive His Mercy?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/007/007_064.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>64.  Faka<u>thth</u>aboohu faanjayn<u>a</u>hu wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena maAAahu fee alfulki waaghraqn<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena ka<u>thth</u>aboo bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin<u>a</u> innahum k<u>a</u>noo qawman AAameen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>64.  But they rejected him,<br> And We delivered him,<br> And those with him,<br> In the Ark:<br> But We overwhelmed<br> In the Flood those<br> Who rejected Our Signs.<br> They were indeed<br> A blind people!</p> <div class="filenav">
