 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XLVII.: Muḥammad (the Prophet).</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/047/047_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  Alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo wa<u>s</u>addoo AAan sabeeli All<u>a</u>hi a<u>d</u>alla aAAm<u>a</u>lahum    </p>
 <p>1.  Those who reject God<br> And hinder (men) from<br> The Path of God,<br> Their deeds will God<br> Render astray<br> (From their mark).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/047/047_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  Wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo waAAamiloo a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>li<u>ha</u>ti wa<u>a</u>manoo bim<u>a</u> nuzzila AAal<u>a</u> mu<u>h</u>ammadin wahuwa al<u>h</u>aqqu min rabbihim kaffara AAanhum sayyi-<u>a</u>tihim waa<u>s</u>la<u>h</u>a b<u>a</u>lahum    </p>
 <p>2.  But those who believe<br> And work deeds of<br> Righteousness, and believe<br> In the (Revelation) sent down<br> To Muhammad—for it is<br> The Truth from their Lord,<br> He will remove from them<br> Their ills and improve<br> Their condition.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/047/047_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  <u>Tha</u>lika bi-anna alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo ittabaAAoo alb<u>at</u>ila waanna alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo ittabaAAoo al<u>h</u>aqqa min rabbihim ka<u>tha</u>lika ya<u>d</u>ribu All<u>a</u>hu li<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si amth<u>a</u>lahum   </p>
 <p>3.  This because those who<br> Reject God follow vanities,<br> While those who believe follow<br> The Truth from their Lord:<br> Thus does God set forth<br> For men their lessons<br> By similitudes.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/047/047_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  Fa-i<u>tha</u> laqeetumu alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo fa<u>d</u>arba a<b>l</b>rriq<u>a</u>bi <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> i<u>tha</u> athkhantumoohum fashuddoo alwath<u>a</u>qa fa-imm<u>a</u> mannan baAAdu wa-imm<u>a</u> fid<u>a</u>an <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> ta<u>d</u>aAAa al<u>h</u>arbu awz<u>a</u>rah<u>a</u> <u>tha</u>lika walaw yash<u>a</u>o All<u>a</u>hu la<b>i</b>nta<u>s</u>ara minhum wal<u>a</u>kin liyabluwa baAA<u>d</u>akum bibaAA<u>d</u>in wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena qutiloo fee sabeeli All<u>a</u>hi falan yu<u>d</u>illa aAAm<u>a</u>lahum   </p>
 <p>4.  Therefore, when ye meet<br> The Unbelievers (in fight),<br>Smite at their necks;<br> At length, when ye have<br> Thoroughly subdued them,<br> Bind a bond<br> Firmly (on them): thereafter<br> (Is the time for) either<br> Generosity or ransom:<br> Until the war lays down<br> Its burdens. Thus (are ye<br> Commanded): but if it<br> Had been God's Will,<br> He could certainly have exacted<br> Retribution from them (Himself);<br> But (He lets you fight)<br> In order to test you,<br> Some with others.<br> But those who are slain<br> In the way of God,<br> He will never let<br> Their deeds be lost.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/047/047_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  Sayahdeehim wayu<u>s</u>li<u>h</u>u b<u>a</u>lahum    </p>
 <p>5.  Soon will He guide them<br> And improve their condition,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/047/047_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  Wayudkhiluhumu aljannata AAarrafah<u>a</u> lahum   </p>
 <p>6.  And admit them to<br> The Garden which He<br> Has announced for them.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/047/047_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo in tan<u>s</u>uroo All<u>a</u>ha yan<u>s</u>urkum wayuthabbit aqd<u>a</u>makum   </p>
 <p>7.  O ye who believe!<br> If ye will aid<br> (The cause of) God,<br> He will aid you,<br> And plant your feet firmly.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/047/047_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  Wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo fataAAsan lahum waa<u>d</u>alla aAAm<u>a</u>lahum   </p>
 <p>8.  But those who reject (God),<br> For them is destruction,<br> And (God) will render<br> Their deeds astray<br> (From their mark).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/047/047_009.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>9.  <u>Tha</u>lika bi-annahum karihoo m<u>a</u> anzala All<u>a</u>hu faa<u>h</u>ba<u>t</u>a aAAm<u>a</u>lahum   </p>
 <p>9.  That is because they<br> Hate the Revelation of God;<br> So He has made<br> Their deeds fruitless.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/047/047_010.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>10.  Afalam yaseeroo fee al-ar<u>d</u>i fayan<i><u>th</u></i>uroo kayfa k<u>a</u>na AA<u>a</u>qibatu alla<u>th</u>eena min qablihim dammara All<u>a</u>hu AAalayhim walilk<u>a</u>fireena amth<u>a</u>luh<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>10.  Do they not travel<br> Through the earth, and see<br> What was the End<br> Of those before them<br> (Who did evil)?<br> God brought utter destruction<br> On them, and similar<br> (Fates await) those who<br> Reject God.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/047/047_011.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>11.  <u>Tha</u>lika bi-anna All<u>a</u>ha mawl<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo waanna alk<u>a</u>fireena l<u>a</u> mawl<u>a</u> lahum    </p>
 <p>11.  That is because God<br> Is the Protector of those<br> Who believe, but<br> Those who reject God<br> Have no protector.</p> <div class="filenav">
