 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XVIII.: Kahf, or the Cave.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 12</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_102.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>102.  Afa<u>h</u>asiba alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo an yattakhi<u>th</u>oo AAib<u>a</u>dee min doonee awliy<u>a</u>a inn<u>a</u> aAAtadn<u>a</u> jahannama lilk<u>a</u>fireena nuzul<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>102.  Do the Unbelievers think<br> That they can take<br> My servants as protectors<br> Besides Me? Verily We<br> Have prepared Hell<br> For the Unbelievers<br> For (their) entertainment.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_103.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>103.  Qul hal nunabbi-okum bi<b>a</b>l-akhsareena aAAm<u>a</u>l<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>103.  Say: "Shall we tell you<br> Of those who lose most<br> In respect of their deeds?—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_104.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>104.  Alla<u>th</u>eena <u>d</u>alla saAAyuhum fee al<u>h</u>ay<u>a</u>ti a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> wahum ya<u>h</u>saboona annahum yu<u>h</u>sinoona <u>s</u>unAA<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>104.  "Those whose efforts have<br> Been wasted in this life,<br> While they thought that<br> They were acquiring good<br> By their works?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_105.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>105.  Ol<u>a</u>-ika alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>ti rabbihim waliq<u>a</u>-ihi fa<u>h</u>abi<u>t</u>at aAAm<u>a</u>luhum fal<u>a</u> nuqeemu lahum yawma alqiy<u>a</u>mati wazn<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>105.  They are those who deny<br> The Signs of their Lord<br> And the fact of their<br> Having to meet Him<br> (In the Hereafter): vain<br> Will be their works,<br> Nor shall We, on the Day<br> Of Judgment, give them<br> Any weight.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_106.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>106.  <u>Tha</u>lika jaz<u>a</u>ohum jahannamu bim<u>a</u> kafaroo wa<b>i</b>ttakha<u>th</u>oo <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tee warusulee huzuw<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>106.  That is their reward,<br> Hell; because they rejected<br> Faith, and took My Signs<br> And My Messengers<br> By way of jest.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_107.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>107.  Inna alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo waAAamiloo a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>li<u>ha</u>ti k<u>a</u>nat lahum jann<u>a</u>tu alfirdawsi nuzul<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>107.  As to those who believe<br> And work righteous deeds,<br> They have, for their entertainment,<br> The Gardens of Paradise,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_108.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>108.  Kh<u>a</u>lideena feeh<u>a</u> l<u>a</u> yabghoona AAanh<u>a</u> <u>h</u>iwal<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>108.  Wherein they shall dwell<br> (For aye): no change<br> Will they wish for from them.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_109.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>109.  Qul law k<u>a</u>na alba<u>h</u>ru mid<u>a</u>dan likalim<u>a</u>ti rabbee lanafida alba<u>h</u>ru qabla an tanfada kalim<u>a</u>tu rabbee walaw ji/n<u>a</u> bimithlihi madad<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>109.  Say: "If the ocean were<br> Ink (wherewith to write out)<br> The words of my Lord,<br> Sooner would the ocean be<br> Exhausted than would the words<br> Of my Lord, even if we<br> Added another ocean<br> Like it, for its aid."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_110.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>110.  Qul innam<u>a</u> an<u>a</u> basharun mithlukum yoo<u>ha</u> ilayya annam<u>a</u> il<u>a</u>hukum il<u>a</u>hun w<u>ah</u>idun faman k<u>a</u>na yarjoo liq<u>a</u>a rabbihi falyaAAmal AAamalan <u>sa</u>li<u>h</u>an wal<u>a</u> yushrik biAAib<u>a</u>dati rabbihi a<u>h</u>ad<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>110.  Say: "I am but a man<br> Like yourselves, (but)<br> The inspiration has come<br> To me, that your God is<br> One God: whoever expects<br> To meet his Lord, let him<br> Work righteousness, and,<br> In the worship of his Lord,<br> Admit no one as partner.</p>  <div class="filenav">
