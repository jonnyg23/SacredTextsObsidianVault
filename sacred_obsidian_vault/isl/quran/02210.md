 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXII.: Ḥajj, or The Pilgrimage.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 10</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/022/022_073.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>73.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>su <u>d</u>uriba mathalun fa<b>i</b>stamiAAoo lahu inna alla<u>th</u>eena tadAAoona min dooni All<u>a</u>hi lan yakhluqoo <u>th</u>ub<u>a</u>ban walawi ijtamaAAoo lahu wa-in yaslubuhumu a<b>l</b><u>thth</u>ub<u>a</u>bu shay-an l<u>a</u> yastanqi<u>th</u>oohu minhu <u>d</u>aAAufa a<b>l</b><u>tta</u>libu wa<b>a</b>lma<u>t</u>loob<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>73.  O men! Here is<br> A parable set forth!<br> Listen to it! Those<br> On whom, besides God,<br> Ye call, cannot create<br> (Even) a fly, if they all<br> Met together for the purpose!<br> And if the fly should snatch<br> Away anything from them,<br> They would have no power<br> To release it from the fly.<br> Feeble are those who petition<br> And those whom they petition!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/022/022_074.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>74.  M<u>a</u> qadaroo All<u>a</u>ha <u>h</u>aqqa qadrihi inna All<u>a</u>ha laqawiyyun AAazeez<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>74.  No just estimate have they<br> Made of God: for God<br> Is He Who is strong<br> And able to carry out<br> His Will.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/022/022_075.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>75.  All<u>a</u>hu ya<u>st</u>afee mina almal<u>a</u>-ikati rusulan wamina a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si inna All<u>a</u>ha sameeAAun ba<u>s</u>eer<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>75.  God chooses Messengers<br> From angels and from men<br> For God is He Who hears<br> And sees (all things).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/022/022_076.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>76.  YaAAlamu m<u>a</u> bayna aydeehim wam<u>a</u> khalfahum wa-il<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi turjaAAu al-omoor<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>76.  He knows what is before them<br> And what is behind them:<br>And to God go back<br> All questions (for decision).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/022/022_077.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>77.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo irkaAAoo wa<b>o</b>sjudoo wa<b>o</b>AAbudoo rabbakum wa<b>i</b>fAAaloo alkhayra laAAallakum tufli<u>h</u>oon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>77.  O ye who believe!<br> Bow down, prostrate yourselves,<br> And adore your Lord;<br> And do good;<br> That ye may prosper.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/022/022_078.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>78.  Waj<u>a</u>hidoo fee All<u>a</u>hi <u>h</u>aqqa jih<u>a</u>dihi huwa ijtab<u>a</u>kum wam<u>a</u> jaAAala AAalaykum fee a<b>l</b>ddeeni min <u>h</u>arajin millata abeekum ibr<u>a</u>heema huwa samm<u>a</u>kumu almuslimeena min qablu wafee h<u>atha</u> liyakoona a<b>l</b>rrasoolu shaheedan AAalaykum watakoonoo shuhad<u>a</u>a AAal<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si faaqeemoo a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>al<u>a</u>ta wa<u>a</u>too a<b>l</b>zzak<u>a</u>ta wa<b>i</b>AAta<u>s</u>imoo bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi huwa mawl<u>a</u>kum faniAAma almawl<u>a</u> waniAAma a<b>l</b>nna<u>s</u>eer<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>78.  And strive in His cause<br> As ye ought to strive,<br> (With sincerity and under discipline).<br> He has chosen you, and has<br> Imposed no difficulties on you<br> In religion; it is the cult<br> Of your father Abraham.<br> It is He Who has named<br> You Muslims, both before<br> And in this (Revelation);<br> That the Apostle may be<br> A witness for you, and ye<br> Be witnesses for mankind!<br> So establish regular Prayer,<br> Give regular Charity,<br> And hold fast to God!<br> He is your Protector—<br> The best to protect<br> And the Best to help!</p>  <div class="filenav">
