 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXXIV.: Sabā, or the City of Sabā</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 4</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/034/034_031.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>31.  Waq<u>a</u>la alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo lan nu/mina bih<u>atha</u> alqur-<u>a</u>ni wal<u>a</u> bi<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>ee bayna yadayhi walaw tar<u>a</u> i<u>th</u>i a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limoona mawqoofoona AAinda rabbihim yarjiAAu baAA<u>d</u>uhum il<u>a</u> baAA<u>d</u>in alqawla yaqoolu alla<u>th</u>eena istu<u>d</u>AAifoo lilla<u>th</u>eena istakbaroo lawl<u>a</u> antum lakunn<u>a</u> mu/mineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>31.  The Unbelievers say:<br> "We shall neither believe<br> In this scripture nor in (any)<br> That (came) before it."<br> Couldst thou but see when<br> The wrong-doers will be made<br> To stand before their Lord,<br> Throwing back the word (of blame)<br>On one another! Those who<br> Had been despised will say<br> To the arrogant ones:<br> "Had it not been for you,<br> We should certainly<br> Have been believers!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/034/034_032.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>32.  Q<u>a</u>la alla<u>th</u>eena istakbaroo lilla<u>th</u>eena istu<u>d</u>AAifoo ana<u>h</u>nu <u>s</u>adadn<u>a</u>kum AAani alhud<u>a</u> baAAda i<u>th</u> j<u>a</u>akum bal kuntum mujrimeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>32.  The arrogant ones will say<br> To those who had been despised:<br> "Was it we who kept you<br> Back from Guidance after<br> It reached you? Nay, rather,<br> It was ye who transgressed.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/034/034_033.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>33.  Waq<u>a</u>la alla<u>th</u>eena istu<u>d</u>AAifoo lilla<u>th</u>eena istakbaroo bal makru allayli wa<b>al</b>nnah<u>a</u>ri i<u>th</u> ta/muroonan<u>a</u> an nakfura bi<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hi wanajAAala lahu and<u>a</u>dan waasarroo a<b>l</b>nnad<u>a</u>mata lamm<u>a</u> raawoo alAAa<u>tha</u>ba wajaAAaln<u>a</u> al-aghl<u>a</u>la fee aAAn<u>a</u>qi alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo hal yujzawna ill<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo yaAAmaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>33.  Those who had been despised<br> Will say to the arrogant ones:<br> "Nay! it was a plot<br> (Of yours) by day and by night:<br> Behold! ye (constantly) ordered us<br> To be ungrateful to God<br> And to attribute equals to Him!<br> They will declare (their) repentance<br> When they see the Penalty:<br> We shall put yokes<br>On the necks of the Unbelievers:<br> It would only be a requital<br> For their (ill) Deeds.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/034/034_034.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>34.  Wam<u>a</u> arsaln<u>a</u> fee qaryatin min na<u>th</u>eerin ill<u>a</u> q<u>a</u>la mutrafooh<u>a</u> inn<u>a</u> bim<u>a</u> orsiltum bihi k<u>a</u>firoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>34.  Never did We send<br> A Warner to a population,<br> But the wealthy ones among them<br> Said: "We believe not<br> In the (Message) with which<br> Ye have been sent."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/034/034_035.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>35.  Waq<u>a</u>loo na<u>h</u>nu aktharu amw<u>a</u>lan waawl<u>a</u>dan wam<u>a</u> na<u>h</u>nu bimuAAa<u>thth</u>abeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>35.  They said: "We have more<br> In wealth and in sons<br> And we cannot be punished."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/034/034_036.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>36.  Qul inna rabbee yabsu<u>t</u>u a<b>l</b>rrizqa liman yash<u>a</u>o wayaqdiru wal<u>a</u>kinna akthara a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si l<u>a</u> yaAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>36.  Say: "Verily my Lord enlarges<br> And restricts the Provision<br> To whom He pleases, but<br> Most men understand not."</p> <div class="filenav">
