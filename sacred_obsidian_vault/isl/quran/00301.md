 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra III.: Āl-i-’Imrān, or The Family of ’Imrān.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  Alif-l<u>a</u>m-meem   </p>
 <p>1.  A. L. M.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  All<u>a</u>hu l<u>a</u> il<u>a</u>ha ill<u>a</u> huwa al<u>h</u>ayyu alqayyoom<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>2.  God! There is no god<br> But He,—the Living,<br> The Self-Subsisting, Eternal.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  Nazzala AAalayka alkit<u>a</u>ba bi<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>aqqi mu<u>s</u>addiqan lim<u>a</u> bayna yadayhi waanzala a<b>l</b>ttawr<u>a</u>ta wa<b>a</b>l-injeel<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>3.  It is He Who sent down<br> To thee (step by step),<br> In truth, the Book,<br> Confirming what went before it;<br> And He sent down the Law<br> (Of Moses) and the Gospel<br> (Of Jesus) before this,<br> As a guide to mankind,<br> And He sent down the Criterion<br> (Of judgment between right and wrong).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  Min qablu hudan li<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si waanzala alfurq<u>a</u>na inna alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>ti All<u>a</u>hi lahum AAa<u>tha</u>bun shadeedun wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu AAazeezun <u>th</u>oo intiq<u>a</u>m<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>4.  Then those who reject<br> Faith in the Signs of God<br> Will suffer the severest<br> Penalty, and God<br> Is Exalted in Might,<br> Lord of Retribution.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  Inna All<u>a</u>ha l<u>a</u> yakhf<u>a</u> AAalayhi shay-on fee al-ar<u>d</u>i wal<u>a</u> fee a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>/-<b>i </b>   </p>
 <p>5.  From God, verily<br> Nothing is hidden<br> On earth or in the heavens.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  Huwa alla<u>th</u>ee yu<u>s</u>awwirukum fee al-ar<u>ha</u>mi kayfa yash<u>a</u>o l<u>a</u> il<u>a</u>ha ill<u>a</u> huwa alAAazeezu al<u>h</u>akeem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>6.  He it is Who shapes you<br> In the wombs as He pleases.<br>There is no god but He,<br> The Exalted in Might,<br> The Wise.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  Huwa alla<u>th</u>ee anzala AAalayka alkit<u>a</u>ba minhu <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tun mu<u>h</u>kam<u>a</u>tun hunna ommu alkit<u>a</u>bi waokharu mutash<u>a</u>bih<u>a</u>tun faamm<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>eena fee quloobihim zayghun fayattabiAAoona m<u>a</u> tash<u>a</u>baha minhu ibtigh<u>a</u>a alfitnati wa<b>i</b>btigh<u>a</u>a ta/weelihi wam<u>a</u> yaAAlamu ta/weelahu ill<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hu wa<b>al</b>rr<u>a</u>sikhoona fee alAAilmi yaqooloona <u>a</u>mann<u>a</u> bihi kullun min AAindi rabbin<u>a</u> wam<u>a</u> ya<u>thth</u>akkaru ill<u>a</u> oloo al-alb<u>a</u>b<b>i</b>    </p>
 <p>7.  He it is Who has sent down<br> To thee the Book:<br> In it are verses<br> Basic or fundamental<br> (Of established meaning);<br> They are the foundation<br> Of the Book: others<br> Are allegorical. But those<br> In whose hearts is perversity follow<br> The part thereof that is allegorical,<br> Seeking discord, and searching<br> For its hidden meanings,<br> But ṭo one knows<br> Its hidden meanings except God.<br> And those who are firmly grounded<br> In knowledge say: "We believe<br> In the Book; the whole of it<br> Is from our Lord:" and none<br> Will grasp the Message<br> Except men of understanding.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  Rabban<u>a</u> l<u>a</u> tuzigh qulooban<u>a</u> baAAda i<u>th</u> hadaytan<u>a</u> wahab lan<u>a</u> min ladunka ra<u>h</u>matan innaka anta alwahh<u>a</u>b<b>u</b>    </p>
 <p>8.  "Our Lord!" (they say),<br> "Let not our hearts deviate<br> Now after Thou hast guided us,<br> But grant us mercy<br> From Thine own Presence;<br> For Thou art the Grantor<br> Of bounties without measure.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_009.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>9.  Rabban<u>a</u> innaka j<u>a</u>miAAu a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si liyawmin l<u>a</u> rayba feehi inna All<u>a</u>ha l<u>a</u> yukhlifu almeeAA<u>a</u>d<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>9.  "Our Lord! Thou art He<br> That will gather mankind<br> Together against a Day about which<br> There is no doubt; for God<br> Never fails in His promise."</p> <div class="filenav">
