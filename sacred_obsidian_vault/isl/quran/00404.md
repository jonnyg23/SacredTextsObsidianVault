 <p><a></a>23.  <u>H</u>urrimat AAalaykum ommah<u>a</u>tukum waban<u>a</u>tukum waakhaw<u>a</u>tukum waAAamm<u>a</u>tukum wakh<u>a</u>l<u>a</u>tukum waban<u>a</u>tu al-akhi waban<u>a</u>tu al-okhti waommah<u>a</u>tukumu all<u>a</u>tee ar<u>d</u>aAAnakum waakhaw<u>a</u>tukum mina a<b>l</b>rra<u>da</u>AAati waommah<u>a</u>tu nis<u>a</u>-ikum warab<u>a</u>-ibukumu all<u>a</u>tee fee <u>h</u>ujoorikum min nis<u>a</u>-ikumu all<u>a</u>tee dakhaltum bihinna fa-in lam takoonoo dakhaltum bihinna fal<u>a</u> jun<u>ah</u>a AAalaykum wa<u>h</u>al<u>a</u>-ilu abn<u>a</u>-ikumu alla<u>th</u>eena min a<u>s</u>l<u>a</u>bikum waan tajmaAAoo bayna al-okhtayni ill<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> qad salafa inna All<u>a</u>ha k<u>a</u>na ghafooran ra<u>h</u>eem<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>23.  Prohibited to you<br> (For marriage) are:—<br> Your mothers, daughters,<br> Sisters; father's sisters,<br> Mother's sisters; brother's daughters,<br> Sister's daugters; foster-mothers<br> (Who gave you suck), foster-sisters;<br> Your wives’ mothers;<br> Your step-daughters under your<br> Guardianship, born of your wives<br> To whom ye have gone in,<br> No prohibition if ye have not gone in;—<br> (Those who have been)<br> Wives of your sons proceeding<br> From your loins;<br> And two sisters in wedlock<br> At one and the same time,<br> Except for what is past;<br> For God is Oft-forgiving,<br> Most Merciful;—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_024.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>24.  Wa<b>a</b>lmu<u>hs</u>an<u>a</u>tu mina a<b>l</b>nnis<u>a</u>-i ill<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> malakat aym<u>a</u>nukum kit<u>a</u>ba All<u>a</u>hi AAalaykum wao<u>h</u>illa lakum m<u>a</u> war<u>a</u>a <u>tha</u>likum an tabtaghoo bi-amw<u>a</u>likum mu<u>hs</u>ineena ghayra mus<u>a</u>fi<u>h</u>eena fam<u>a</u> istamtaAAtum bihi minhunna fa<u>a</u>toohunna ojoorahunna faree<u>d</u>atan wal<u>a</u> jun<u>ah</u>a AAalaykum feem<u>a</u> tar<u>ad</u>aytum bihi min baAAdi alfaree<u>d</u>ati inna All<u>a</u>ha k<u>a</u>na AAaleeman <u>h</u>akeem<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>24.  Also (prohibited are)<br> Women already married,<br> Except those<br> Whom your right hands possess:<br> Thus hath God ordained<br> (Prohibitions) against you:<br> Except for these, all others<br> Are lawful, provided<br> Ye seek (them in marriage)<br> With gifts from your property,—<br> Desiring chastity, not lust.<br> Seeing that ye derive<br> Benefit from them, give them<br> Their dowers (at least)<br> As prescribed; but if,<br> After a dower is prescribed, ye agree<br> Mutually (to vary it),<br> There is no blame on you,<br> And God is All-knowing<br> All-wise.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/004/004_025.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>25.  Waman lam yasta<u>t</u>iAA minkum <u>t</u>awlan an yanki<u>h</u>a almu<u>hs</u>an<u>a</u>ti almu/min<u>a</u>ti famin m<u>a</u> malakat aym<u>a</u>nukum min fatay<u>a</u>tikumu almu/min<u>a</u>ti wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu aAAlamu bi-eem<u>a</u>nikum baAA<u>d</u>ukum min baAA<u>d</u>in fa<b>i</b>nki<u>h</u>oohunna bi-i<u>th</u>ni ahlihinna wa<u>a</u>toohunna ojoorahunna bi<b>a</b>lmaAAroofi mu<u>hs</u>an<u>a</u>tin ghayra mas<u>a</u>fi<u>ha</u>tin wal<u>a</u> muttakhi<u>tha</u>ti akhd<u>a</u>nin fa-i<u>tha</u> o<u>hs</u>inna fa-in atayna bif<u>ah</u>ishatin faAAalayhinna ni<u>s</u>fu m<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> almu<u>hs</u>an<u>a</u>ti mina alAAa<u>tha</u>bi <u>tha</u>lika liman khashiya alAAanata minkum waan ta<u>s</u>biroo khayrun lakum wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu ghafoorun ra<u>h</u>eem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>25.  If any of you have not<br> The means wherewith<br> To wed free believing women,<br> They may wed believing<br> Girls from among those<br> Whom your right hands possess:<br> God hath full knowledge<br> About your Faith.<br> Ye are one from another:<br> Wed them with the leave<br> Of their owners, and give them<br> Their dowers, according to what<br>Is reasonable: they should be<br> Chaste, not lustful, nor taking<br> Paramours: when they<br> Are taken in wedlock,<br> If they fall into shame,<br> Their punishment is half<br> That for free women.<br> This (permission) is for those<br> Among you who fear sin;<br> But it is better for you<br> That ye practise self-restraint.<br> And God is Oft-forgiving,<br> Most Merciful.</p> <div class="filenav">
