 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XX.: Ṭā-Hā. (Mystic Letters, Ṭ. H.)</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  <u>Ta</u>-h<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>1.  Tā-Hā.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  M<u>a</u> anzaln<u>a</u> AAalayka alqur-<u>a</u>na litashq<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>2.  We have not sent down<br> The Qur-ān to thee to be<br> (An occasion) for thy distress,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  Ill<u>a</u> ta<u>th</u>kiratan liman yakhsh<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>3.  But only as an admonition<br> To those who fear (God),—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  Tanzeelan mimman khalaqa al-ar<u>d</u>a wa<b>al</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti alAAul<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>4.  A revelation from Him<br> Who created the earth<br> And the heavens on high.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  A<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>m<u>a</u>nu AAal<u>a</u> alAAarshi istaw<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>5.  (God) Most Gracious<br> Is firmly established<br> On the throne (of authority).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  Lahu m<u>a</u> fee a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wam<u>a</u> fee al-ar<u>d</u>i wam<u>a</u> baynahum<u>a</u> wam<u>a</u> ta<u>h</u>ta a<b>l</b>ththar<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>6.  To Him belongs what is<br> In the heavens and on earth,<br> And all between them,<br> And all beneath the soil.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  Wa-in tajhar bi<b>a</b>lqawli fa-innahu yaAAlamu a<b>l</b>ssirra waakhf<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>7.  If thou pronounce the word<br> Aloud, (it is no matter):<br>For verily He knoweth<br> What is secret and what<br> Is yet more hidden.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  All<u>a</u>hu l<u>a</u> il<u>a</u>ha ill<u>a</u> huwa lahu al-asm<u>a</u>o al<u>h</u>usn<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>8.  God! there is no god<br> But He! To Him belong<br> The Most Beautiful Names.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_009.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>9.  Wahal at<u>a</u>ka <u>h</u>adeethu moos<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>9.  Has the story of Moses<br> Reached thee?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_010.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>10.  I<u>th</u> ra<u>a</u> n<u>a</u>ran faq<u>a</u>la li-ahlihi omkuthoo innee <u>a</u>nastu n<u>a</u>ran laAAallee <u>a</u>teekum minh<u>a</u> biqabasin aw ajidu AAal<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>ri hud<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>10.  Behold, he saw a fire:<br> So he said to his family,<br>"Tarry ye; I perceive<br> A fire; perhaps I can<br> Bring you some burning brand<br> Therefrom, or find some guidance<br> At the fire."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_011.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>11.  Falamm<u>a</u> at<u>a</u>h<u>a</u> noodiya y<u>a</u> moos<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>11.  But when he came<br> To the fire, a voice<br> Was heard: "O Moses!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_012.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>12.  Innee an<u>a</u> rabbuka fa<b>i</b>khlaAA naAAlayka innaka bi<b>a</b>lw<u>a</u>di almuqaddasi <u>t</u>uw<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>12.  "Verily I am thy Lord!<br> Therefore (in My presence)<br> Put off thy shoes: thou art<br> In the sacred valley Ṭuwā</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_013.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>13.  Waan<u>a</u> ikhtartuka fa<b>i</b>stamiAA lim<u>a</u> yoo<u>ha</u>   </p>
 <p>13.  "I have chosen thee:<br> Listen, then, to the inspiration<br> (Sent to thee).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_014.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>14.  Innanee an<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hu l<u>a</u> il<u>a</u>ha ill<u>a</u> an<u>a</u> fa<b>o</b>AAbudnee waaqimi a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>al<u>a</u>ta li<u>th</u>ikree   </p>
 <p>14.  "Verily, I am God:<br> There is no god but I:<br> So serve thou Me (only),<br> And establish regular prayer<br> For celebrating My praise.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_015.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>15.  Inna a<b>l</b>ss<u>a</u>AAata <u>a</u>tiyatun ak<u>a</u>du okhfeeh<u>a</u> litujz<u>a</u> kullu nafsin bim<u>a</u> tasAA<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>15.  "Verily the Hour is coming—<br> My design is to keep it<br>Hidden—for every soul<br> To receive its reward<br> By the measure of<br> Its Endeavour.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_016.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>16.  Fal<u>a</u> ya<u>s</u>uddannaka AAanh<u>a</u> man l<u>a</u> yu/minu bih<u>a</u> wa<b>i</b>ttabaAAa haw<u>a</u>hu fatard<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>16.  "Therefore let not such as<br> Believe not therein<br> But follow their own<br> Lusts, divert thee therefrom,<br> Lest thou perish!"…</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_017.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>17.  Wam<u>a</u> tilka biyameenika y<u>a</u> moos<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>17.  "And what is that<br> In thy right hand,<br> O Moses?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_018.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>18.  Q<u>a</u>la hiya AAa<u>sa</u>ya atawakkao AAalayh<u>a</u> waahushshu bih<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> ghanamee waliya feeh<u>a</u> ma<u>a</u>ribu okhr<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>18.  He said, "It is<br> My rod: on it<br> I lean; with it<br> I beat down fodder<br> For my flocks; and<br> In it I find<br> Other uses."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_019.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>19.  Q<u>a</u>la alqih<u>a</u> y<u>a</u> moos<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>19.  (God) said, "Throw it,<br> O Moses!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_020.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>20.  Faalq<u>a</u>h<u>a</u> fa-i<u>tha</u> hiya <u>h</u>ayyatun tasAA<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>20.  He threw it, and behold!<br> It was a snake,<br> Active in motion.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_021.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>21.  Q<u>a</u>la khu<u>th</u>h<u>a</u> wal<u>a</u> takhaf sanuAAeeduh<u>a</u> seeratah<u>a</u> al-ool<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>21.  (God) said, "Seize it,<br> And fear not: We<br> Shall return it at once<br> To its former condition"…</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_022.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>22.  Wa<b>o</b><u>d</u>mum yadaka il<u>a</u> jan<u>ah</u>ika takhruj bay<u>da</u>a min ghayri soo-in <u>a</u>yatan okhr<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>22.  "Now draw thy hand<br> Close to thy side:<br> It shall come forth white<br> (And shining), without harm<br> (Or stain),—as another Sign,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_023.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>23.  Linuriyaka min <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin<u>a</u> alkubr<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>23.  "In order that We<br> May show thee<br> (Two) of our Greater Signs.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_024.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>24.  I<u>th</u>hab il<u>a</u> firAAawna innahu <u>t</u>agh<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>24.  "Go thou to Pharaoh,<br> For he has indeed<br> Transgressed all bounds."</p> <div class="filenav">
