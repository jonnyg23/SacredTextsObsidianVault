 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XLV.: Ja<u>th</u>iya, or Bowing the Knee.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 2</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/045/045_012.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>12.  All<u>a</u>hu alla<u>th</u>ee sakhkhara lakumu alba<u>h</u>ra litajriya alfulku feehi bi-amrihi walitabtaghoo min fa<u>d</u>lihi walaAAallakum tashkuroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>12.  It is God Who has<br> Subjected the sea to you,<br> That ships may sail<br> Through it by His command,<br> That ye may seek<br> Of His Bounty, and that<br> Ye may be grateful.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/045/045_013.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>13.  Wasakhkhara lakum m<u>a</u> fee a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wam<u>a</u> fee al-ar<u>d</u>i jameeAAan minhu inna fee <u>tha</u>lika la<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin liqawmin yatafakkaroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>13.  And He has subjected<br> To you, as from Him,<br> All that is in the heavens<br> And on earth: behold,<br> In that are Signs indeed<br> For those who reflect.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/045/045_014.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>14.  Qul lilla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo yaghfiroo lilla<u>th</u>eena l<u>a</u> yarjoona ayy<u>a</u>ma All<u>a</u>hi liyajziya qawman bim<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo yaksiboon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>14.  Tell those who believe,<br> To forgive those who<br> Do not look forward<br> To the Days of God:<br>It is for Him to recompense<br> (For good or ill) each People<br> According to what<br> They have earned.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/045/045_015.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>15.  Man AAamila <u>sa</u>li<u>h</u>an falinafsihi waman as<u>a</u>a faAAalayh<u>a</u> thumma il<u>a</u> rabbikum turjaAAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>15.  If any one does<br> A righteous deed,<br> It enures to the benefit<br> Of his own soul;<br> If he does evil,<br> It works against<br> (His own soul).<br> In the end will ye<br> (All) be brought back<br> To your Lord.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/045/045_016.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>16.  Walaqad <u>a</u>tayn<u>a</u> banee isr<u>a</u>-eela alkit<u>a</u>ba wa<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>ukma wa<b>al</b>nnubuwwata warazaqn<u>a</u>hum mina a<b>l</b><u>tt</u>ayyib<u>a</u>ti wafa<u>dd</u>aln<u>a</u>hum AAal<u>a</u> alAA<u>a</u>lameen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>16.  We did aforetime<br> Grant to the Children<br> Of Israel the Book,<br> The Power of Command,<br> And Prophethood; We gave<br> Them, for Sustenance, things<br> Good and pure; and We<br> Favoured them above the nations.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/045/045_017.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>17.  Wa<u>a</u>tayn<u>a</u>hum bayyin<u>a</u>tin mina al-amri fam<u>a</u> ikhtalafoo ill<u>a</u> min baAAdi m<u>a</u> j<u>a</u>ahumu alAAilmu baghyan baynahum inna rabbaka yaq<u>d</u>ee baynahum yawma alqiy<u>a</u>mati feem<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo feehi yakhtalifoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>17.  And We granted them<br> Clear Signs in affairs<br>(Of Religion): it was only<br> After knowledge had been<br> Granted to them that they<br> Fell into schisms, through<br> Insolent envy among themselves<br> Verily thy Lord will judge<br> Between them on the Day<br> Of Judgment as to those<br> Matters in which they<br> Set up differences.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/045/045_018.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>18.  Thumma jaAAaln<u>a</u>ka AAal<u>a</u> shareeAAatin mina al-amri fa<b>i</b>ttabiAAh<u>a</u> wal<u>a</u> tattabiAA ahw<u>a</u>a alla<u>th</u>eena l<u>a</u> yaAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>18.  Then we put thee<br> On the (right) Way<br> Of Religion: so follow<br> Thou that (Way),<br> And follow not the desires<br> Of those who know not.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/045/045_019.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>19.  Innahum lan yughnoo AAanka mina All<u>a</u>hi shay-an wa-inna a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limeena baAA<u>d</u>uhum awliy<u>a</u>o baAA<u>d</u>in wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu waliyyu almuttaqeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>19.  They will be of no<br> Use to thee in the sight<br> Of God: it is only<br> Wrong-doers (that stand as)<br> Protectors, one to another:<br> But God is the Protector<br> Of the Righteous.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/045/045_020.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>20.  H<u>atha</u> ba<u>sa</u>-iru li<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si wahudan wara<u>h</u>matun liqawmin yooqinoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>20.  These are clear evidences<br> To men, and a Guidance<br> And Mercy to those<br> Of assured Faith.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/045/045_021.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>21.  Am <u>h</u>asiba alla<u>th</u>eena ijtara<u>h</u>oo a<b>l</b>ssayyi-<u>a</u>ti an najAAalahum ka<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo waAAamiloo a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>li<u>ha</u>ti saw<u>a</u>an ma<u>h</u>y<u>a</u>hum wamam<u>a</u>tuhum s<u>a</u>a m<u>a</u> ya<u>h</u>kumoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>21.  What! do those who<br> Seek after evil ways<br> Think that We shall<br> Hold them equal with<br> Those who believe and<br> Do righteous deeds,—that<br> Equal will be their<br> Life and their death?<br> Ill is the judgment<br> That they make.</p> <div class="filenav">
