 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra VI.: An’ām, or Cattle.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 10</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_083.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>83.  Watilka <u>h</u>ujjatun<u>a</u> <u>a</u>tayn<u>a</u>h<u>a</u> ibr<u>a</u>heema AAal<u>a</u> qawmihi narfaAAu daraj<u>a</u>tin man nash<u>a</u>o inna rabbaka <u>h</u>akeemun AAaleem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>83.  That was the reasoning<br> About Us, which<br> We gave to Abraham<br> (To use) against his people:<br>We raise whom We will,<br> Degree after degree:<br> For thy Lord is full<br> Of wisdom and knowledge.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_084.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>84.  Wawahabn<u>a</u> lahu is<u>ha</u>qa wayaAAqooba kullan hadayn<u>a</u> wanoo<u>h</u>an hadayn<u>a</u> min qablu wamin <u>th</u>urriyyatihi d<u>a</u>wooda wasulaym<u>a</u>na waayyooba wayoosufa wamoos<u>a</u> wah<u>a</u>roona waka<u>tha</u>lika najzee almu<u>h</u>sineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>84.  We gave him Isaac<br> And Jacob: all (three)<br> We guided:<br> And before him,<br> We guided Noah,<br> And among his progeny,<br> David, Solomon, Job,<br> Joseph, Moses, and Aaron:<br> Thus do We reward<br> Those who do good:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_085.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>85.  Wazakariyy<u>a</u> waya<u>h</u>y<u>a</u> waAAees<u>a</u> wa<b>i</b>ly<u>a</u>sa kullun mina a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>li<u>h</u>een<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>85.  And Zakarīya and John,<br> And Jesus and Elias:<br> All in the ranks<br> Of the Righteous:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_086.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>86.  Wa-ism<u>a</u>AAeela wa<b>i</b>lyasaAAa wayoonusa waloo<u>t</u>an wakullan fa<u>dd</u>aln<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> alAA<u>a</u>lameen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>86.  And Ismā’īl and Elisha,<br> And Jonas, and Lot:<br>And to all We gave<br> Favour above the nations:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_087.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>87.  Wamin <u>a</u>b<u>a</u>-ihim wa<u>th</u>urriyy<u>a</u>tihim wa-ikhw<u>a</u>nihim wa<b>i</b>jtabayn<u>a</u>hum wahadayn<u>a</u>hum il<u>a</u> <u>s</u>ir<u>at</u>in mustaqeem<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>87.  (To them) and to their fathers,<br> And progeny and brethren:<br> We chose them,<br> And We guided them<br> To a straight Way.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_088.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>88.  <u>Tha</u>lika hud<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi yahdee bihi man yash<u>a</u>o min AAib<u>a</u>dihi walaw ashrakoo la<u>h</u>abi<u>t</u>a AAanhum m<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>noo yaAAmaloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>88.  This is the Guidance<br> Of God: He giveth<br> That guidance to whom<br> He pleaseth, of His worshippers.<br> If they were to join<br> Other gods with Him,<br> All that they did<br> Would be vain for them,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_089.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>89.  Ol<u>a</u>-ika alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>tayn<u>a</u>humu alkit<u>a</u>ba wa<b>a</b>l<u>h</u>ukma wa<b>al</b>nnubuwwata fa-in yakfur bih<u>a</u> h<u>a</u>ol<u>a</u>-i faqad wakkaln<u>a</u> bih<u>a</u> qawman laysoo bih<u>a</u> bik<u>a</u>fireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>89.  These were the men<br> To whom We gave<br> The Book, and Authority,<br> And Prophethood: if these<br> (Their descendants) reject them,<br> Behold! We shall entrust<br> Their charge to a new People<br> Who reject them not.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/006/006_090.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>90.  Ol<u>a</u>-ika alla<u>th</u>eena had<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hu fabihud<u>a</u>humu iqtadih qul l<u>a</u> as-alukum AAalayhi ajran in huwa ill<u>a</u> <u>th</u>ikr<u>a</u> lilAA<u>a</u>lameen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>90.  Those were the (prophets)<br> Who received God's guidance:<br> Copy the guidance they received;<br> Say: "No reward for this<br> Do I ask of you:<br> This is no less than<br> A Message for the nations."</p> <div class="filenav">
