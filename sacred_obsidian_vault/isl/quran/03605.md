 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXXVI.: Yā-Sīn (being Abbreviated Letters).</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 5</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/036/036_068.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>68.  Waman nuAAammirhu nunakkis-hu fee alkhalqi afal<u>a</u> yaAAqiloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>68.  If We grant long life<br> To any, We cause him<br> To be reversed in nature:<br> Will they not then understand?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/036/036_069.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>69.  Wam<u>a</u> AAallamn<u>a</u>hu a<b>l</b>shshiAAra wam<u>a</u> yanbaghee lahu in huwa ill<u>a</u> <u>th</u>ikrun waqur-<u>a</u>nun mubeen<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>69.  We have not instructed<br> The (Prophet) in Poetry,<br> Nor is it meet for him:<br> This is no less than<br>A Message and a Qur-ān<br> Making things clear:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/036/036_070.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>70.  Liyun<u>th</u>ira man k<u>a</u>na <u>h</u>ayyan waya<u>h</u>iqqa alqawlu AAal<u>a</u> alk<u>a</u>fireen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>70.  That it may give admonition<br> To any (who are) alive,<br> And that the charge<br> May be proved against those<br> Who reject (Truth).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/036/036_071.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>71.  Awa lam yaraw ann<u>a</u> khalaqn<u>a</u> lahum mimm<u>a</u> AAamilat aydeen<u>a</u> anAA<u>a</u>man fahum lah<u>a</u> m<u>a</u>likoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>71.  See they not that it is<br> We Who have created<br> For them—among the things<br> Which our hands have fashioned—<br> Cattle, which are under<br> Their dominion?—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/036/036_072.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>72.  Wa<u>th</u>allaln<u>a</u>h<u>a</u> lahum faminh<u>a</u> rakoobuhum waminh<u>a</u> ya/kuloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>72.  And that We have<br> Subjected them to their (use)?<br> Of them some do carry them<br> And some they eat:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/036/036_073.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>73.  Walahum feeh<u>a</u> man<u>a</u>fiAAu wamash<u>a</u>ribu afal<u>a</u> yashkuroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>73.  And they have (other) profits<br> From them (besides), and they<br> Get (milk) to drink.<br> Will they not then<br> Be grateful?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/036/036_074.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>74.  Wa<b>i</b>ttakha<u>th</u>oo min dooni All<u>a</u>hi <u>a</u>lihatan laAAallahum yun<u>s</u>aroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>74.  Yet they take (for worship)<br> Gods other than God,<br>(Hoping) that they might<br> Be helped!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/036/036_075.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>75.  L<u>a</u> yasta<u>t</u>eeAAoona na<u>s</u>rahum wahum lahum jundun mu<u>hd</u>aroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>75.  They have not the power<br> To help them: but they<br> Will be brought up<br> (Before Our Judgment-seat)<br> As a troop (to be condemned).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/036/036_076.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>76.  Fal<u>a</u> ya<u>h</u>zunka qawluhum inn<u>a</u> naAAlamu m<u>a</u> yusirroona wam<u>a</u> yuAAlinoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>76.  Let not their speech, then,<br> Grieve thee. Verily We know<br> What they hide as well as<br> What they disclose.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/036/036_077.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>77.  Awa lam yar<u>a</u> al-ins<u>a</u>nu ann<u>a</u> khalaqn<u>a</u>hu min nu<u>t</u>fatin fa-i<u>tha</u> huwa kha<u>s</u>eemun mubeen<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>77.  Doth not man see<br> That it is We Who<br> Created him from sperm?<br> Yet behold! he (stands forth)<br> As an open adversary!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/036/036_078.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>78.  Wa<u>d</u>araba lan<u>a</u> mathalan wanasiya khalqahu q<u>a</u>la man yu<u>h</u>yee alAAi<i><u>th</u></i><u>a</u>ma wahiya rameem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>78.  And he makes comparisons<br> For Us, and forgets his own<br> (Origin and) Creation:<br> He says, "Who can give<br> Life to (dry) bones<br> And decomposed ones (at that)?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/036/036_079.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>79.  Qul yu<u>h</u>yeeh<u>a</u> alla<u>th</u>ee anshaah<u>a</u> awwala marratin wahuwa bikulli khalqin AAaleem<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>79.  Say, "He will give them<br> Life Who created them<br> For the first time!<br> For He is well-versed<br> In every kind of creation!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/036/036_080.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>80.  Alla<u>th</u>ee jaAAala lakum mina a<b>l</b>shshajari al-akh<u>d</u>ari n<u>a</u>ran fa-i<u>tha</u> antum minhu tooqidoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>80.  "The same Who produces<br> For you fire out of<br> The green tree, when behold!<br> Ye kindle therewith<br> (Your own fires)!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/036/036_081.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>81.  Awa laysa alla<u>th</u>ee khalaqa a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>a biq<u>a</u>dirin AAal<u>a</u> an yakhluqa mithlahum bal<u>a</u> wahuwa alkhall<u>a</u>qu alAAaleem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>81.  "Is not He Who created<br> The heavens and the earth<br> Able to create the like<br> Thereof?"—Yea, indeed!<br> For he is the Creator Supreme,<br> Of skill and knowledge (infinite)!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/036/036_082.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>82.  Innam<u>a</u> amruhu i<u>tha</u> ar<u>a</u>da shay-an an yaqoola lahu kun fayakoon<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>82.  Verily, when He intends<br> A thing, His Command is,<br> "Be", and it is!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/036/036_083.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>83.  Fasub<u>ha</u>na alla<u>th</u>ee biyadihi malakootu kulli shay-in wa-ilayhi turjaAAoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>83.  So glory to Him<br> In Whose hands is<br> The dominion of all things:<br> And to Him will<br> Be all brought back.</p>  <div class="filenav">
