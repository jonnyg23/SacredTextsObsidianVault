 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XVIII.: Kahf, or the Cave.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 9</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_060.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>60.  Wa-i<u>th</u> q<u>a</u>la moos<u>a</u> lifat<u>a</u>hu l<u>a</u> abra<u>h</u>u <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> ablugha majmaAAa alba<u>h</u>rayni aw am<u>d</u>iya <u>h</u>uqub<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>60.  Behold, Moses said<br> To his attendant, "I will not<br> Give up until I reach<br> The junction of the two<br> Seas or (until) I spend<br> Years and years in travel."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_061.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>61.  Falamm<u>a</u> balagh<u>a</u> majmaAAa baynihim<u>a</u> nasiy<u>a</u> <u>h</u>ootahum<u>a</u> fa<b>i</b>ttakha<u>th</u>a sabeelahu fee alba<u>h</u>ri sarab<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>61.  But when they reached<br> The Junction, they forgot<br> (About) their Fish, which took<br> Its course through the sea<br> (Straight) as in a tunnel.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_062.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>62.  Falamm<u>a</u> j<u>a</u>waz<u>a</u> q<u>a</u>la lifat<u>a</u>hu <u>a</u>tin<u>a</u> ghad<u>a</u>an<u>a</u> laqad laqeen<u>a</u> min safarin<u>a</u> h<u>atha</u> na<u>s</u>ab<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>62.  When they had passed on<br> (Some distance), Moses said<br> To his attendant: "Bring us<br> Our early meal; truly<br> We have suffered much fatigue<br> At this (stage of) our journey."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_063.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>63.  Q<u>a</u>la araayta i<u>th</u> awayn<u>a</u> il<u>a</u> a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>akhrati fa-innee naseetu al<u>h</u>oota wam<u>a</u> ans<u>a</u>neehu ill<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>shshay<u>ta</u>nu an a<u>th</u>kurahu wa<b>i</b>ttakha<u>th</u>a sabeelahu fee alba<u>h</u>ri AAajab<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>63.  He replied: "Sawest thou<br> (What happened) when we<br> Betook ourselves to the rock?<br> I did indeed forget<br> (About) the Fish: none but<br> Satan made me forget<br> To tell (you) about it:<br> It took its course through<br> The sea in a marvellous way!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_064.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>64.  Q<u>a</u>la <u>tha</u>lika m<u>a</u> kunn<u>a</u> nabghi fa<b>i</b>rtadd<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> <u>a</u>th<u>a</u>rihim<u>a</u> qa<u>s</u>a<u>sa</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>64.  Moses said: "That was what<br> We were seeking after:"<br> So they went back<br> On their footsteps, following<br> (The path they had come).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_065.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>65.  Fawajad<u>a</u> AAabdan min AAib<u>a</u>din<u>a</u> <u>a</u>tayn<u>a</u>hu ra<u>h</u>matan min AAindin<u>a</u> waAAallamn<u>a</u>hu min ladunn<u>a</u> AAilm<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>65.  So they found one<br> Of Our servants,<br>On whom We had bestowed<br> Mercy from Ourselves<br> And whom We had taught<br> Knowledge from Our own<br> Presence.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_066.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>66.  Q<u>a</u>la lahu moos<u>a</u> hal attabiAAuka AAal<u>a</u> an tuAAallimani mimm<u>a</u> AAullimta rushd<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>66.  Moses said to him:<br> "May I follow thee,<br> On the footing that<br> Thou teach me something<br> Of the (Higher) Truth<br> Which thou hast been taught?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_067.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>67.  Q<u>a</u>la innaka lan tasta<u>t</u>eeAAa maAAiya <u>s</u>abr<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>67.  (The other) said: "Verily<br> Thou wilt not be able<br> To have patience with me!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_068.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>68.  Wakayfa ta<u>s</u>biru AAal<u>a</u> m<u>a</u> lam tu<u>h</u>i<u>t</u> bihi khubr<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>68.  "And how canst thou<br> Have patience about things<br> About which thy understanding<br> Is not complete?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_069.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>69.  Q<u>a</u>la satajidunee in sh<u>a</u>a All<u>a</u>hu <u>sa</u>biran wal<u>a</u> aAA<u>s</u>ee laka amr<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>69.  Moses said: "Thou wilt<br> Find me, if God so will,<br> (Truly) patient: nor shall I<br> Disobey thee in aught."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/018/018_070.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>70.  Q<u>a</u>la fa-ini ittabaAAtanee fal<u>a</u> tas-alnee AAan shay-in <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> o<u>h</u>ditha laka minhu <u>th</u>ikr<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>70.  The other said: "If then<br> Thou wouldst follow me,<br> Ask me no questions<br> About anything until I<br> Myself speak to thee<br> Concerning it."</p> <div class="filenav">
