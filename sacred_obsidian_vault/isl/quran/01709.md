 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XVII.: Banī Isrā-īl, or the Children of Israel,</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 9</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_078.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>78.  Aqimi a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>al<u>a</u>ta lidulooki a<b>l</b>shshamsi il<u>a</u> ghasaqi allayli waqur-<u>a</u>na alfajri inna qur-<u>a</u>na alfajri k<u>a</u>na mashhood<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>78.  Establish regular prayers—<br> At the sun's decline<br>Till the darkness of the night,<br> And the morning prayer<br> And reading: for the prayer<br> And reading in the morning<br> Carry their testimony.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_079.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>79.  Wamina allayli fatahajjad bihi n<u>a</u>filatan laka AAas<u>a</u> an yabAAathaka rabbuka maq<u>a</u>man ma<u>h</u>mood<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>79.  And pray in the small watches<br> Of the morning: (it would be)<br> An additional prayer<br> (Or spiritual profit)<br> For thee: soon will thy Lord<br> Raise thee to a Station<br> Of Praise and Glory!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_080.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>80.  Waqul rabbi adkhilnee mudkhala <u>s</u>idqin waakhrijnee mukhraja <u>s</u>idqin wa<b>i</b>jAAal lee min ladunka sul<u>ta</u>nan na<u>s</u>eer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>80.  Say: "O my Lord!<br> Let my entry be<br> By the Gate of Truth<br> And Honour, and likewise<br> My exit by the Gate<br> Of Truth and Honour;<br> And grant me<br> From Thy Presence<br> An authority to aid (me).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_081.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>81.  Waqul j<u>a</u>a al<u>h</u>aqqu wazahaqa alb<u>at</u>ilu inna alb<u>at</u>ila k<u>a</u>na zahooq<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>81.  And say: "Truth has (now)<br> Arrived, and Falsehood perished:<br> For Falsehood is (by its nature)<br> Bound to perish."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_082.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>82.  Wanunazzilu mina alqur-<u>a</u>ni m<u>a</u> huwa shif<u>a</u>on wara<u>h</u>matun lilmu/mineena wal<u>a</u> yazeedu a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limeena ill<u>a</u> khas<u>a</u>r<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>82.  We send down (stage by stage)<br> In the Qur-ān that which<br> Is a healing and a mercy<br> To those who believe:<br> To the unjust it causes<br> Nothing but loss after loss.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_083.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>83.  Wa-i<u>tha</u> anAAamn<u>a</u> AAal<u>a</u> al-ins<u>a</u>ni aAAra<u>d</u>a wana<u>a</u> bij<u>a</u>nibihi wa-i<u>tha</u> massahu a<b>l</b>shsharru k<u>a</u>na yaoos<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>83.  Yet when We bestow<br> Our favours on man,<br> He turns away and becomes<br> Remote on his side (instead<br> Of coming to Us), and when<br> Evil seizes him he<br> Gives himself up to despair!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/017/017_084.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>84.  Qul kullun yaAAmalu AAal<u>a</u> sh<u>a</u>kilatihi farabbukum aAAlamu biman huwa ahd<u>a</u> sabeel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>84.  Say: "Everyone acts<br> According to his own disposition:<br> But your Lord knows best<br> Who it is that is<br> Best guided on the Way."</p> <div class="filenav">
