 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XLIV.: Du<u>kh</u>ān, or Smoke (or Mist.)</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 3</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/044/044_043.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>43.  Inna shajarata a<b>l</b>zzaqqoom<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>43.  Verily the tree<br> Of  Zaqqūm</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/044/044_044.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>44.  <u>T</u>aAA<u>a</u>mu al-atheem<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>44.  Will be the food<br> Of the Sinful,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/044/044_045.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>45.  Ka<b>a</b>lmuhli yaghlee fee albu<u>t</u>oon<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>45.  Like molten brass;<br> It will boil<br> In their insides,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/044/044_046.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>46.  Kaghalyi al<u>h</u>ameem<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>46.  Like the boiling<br> Of scalding water.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/044/044_047.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>47.  Khu<u>th</u>oohu fa<b>i</b>AAtiloohu il<u>a</u> saw<u>a</u>-i alja<u>h</u>eem<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>47.  (A voice will cry:<br> "Seize ye him<br> And drag him<br> Into the midst<br> Of the Blazing Fire!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/044/044_048.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>48.  Thumma <u>s</u>ubboo fawqa ra/sihi min AAa<u>tha</u>bi al<u>h</u>ameem<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>48.  "Then pour over his head<br> The Penalty of Boiling Water</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/044/044_049.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>49.  <u>Th</u>uq innaka anta alAAazeezu alkareem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>49.  "Taste thou (this)!<br> Truly wast thou<br> Mighty, full of honour!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/044/044_050.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>50.  Inna h<u>atha</u> m<u>a</u> kuntum bihi tamtaroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>50.  "Truly this is what<br> Ye used to doubt!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/044/044_051.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>51.  Inna almuttaqeena fee maq<u>a</u>min ameen<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>51.  As to the Righteous<br> (They will be) in<br> A position of Security,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/044/044_052.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>52.  Fee jann<u>a</u>tin waAAuyoon<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>52.  Among Gardens and Springs;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/044/044_053.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>53.  Yalbasoona min sundusin wa-istabraqin mutaq<u>a</u>bileen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>53.  Dressed in fine silk<br> And in rich brocade,<br> They will face each other;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/044/044_054.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>54.  Ka<u>tha</u>lika wazawwajn<u>a</u>hum bi<u>h</u>oorin AAeen<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>54.  So; and We shall<br> Join them to Companions<br> With beautiful, big,<br> And lustrous eyes.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/044/044_055.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>55.  YadAAoona feeh<u>a</u> bikulli f<u>a</u>kihatin <u>a</u>mineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>55.  There can they call<br> For every kind of fruit<br> In peace and security;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/044/044_056.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>56.  L<u>a</u> ya<u>th</u>ooqoona feeh<u>a</u> almawta ill<u>a</u> almawtata al-ool<u>a</u> wawaq<u>a</u>hum AAa<u>tha</u>ba alja<u>h</u>eem<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>56.  Nor will they there<br> Taste Death, except the first<br> Death; and He will preserve<br> Them from the Penalty<br> Of the Blazing Fire,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/044/044_057.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>57.  Fa<u>d</u>lan min rabbika <u>tha</u>lika huwa alfawzu alAAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>57.  As a Bounty from thy Lord!<br> That will be<br> The supreme achievement!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/044/044_058.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>58.  Fa-innam<u>a</u> yassarn<u>a</u>hu bilis<u>a</u>nika laAAallahum yata<u>th</u>akkaroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>58.  Verily, We have made<br> This (Qur-ān) easy,<br> In thy tongue,<br> In order that they<br> May give heed.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/044/044_059.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>59.  Fa<b>i</b>rtaqib innahum murtaqiboon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>59.  So wait thou and watch;<br> For they (too) are waiting.</p>  <div class="filenav">
