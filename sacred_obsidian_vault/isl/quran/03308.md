 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXXIII.: Aḥzāb, or The Confederates.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 8</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/033/033_059.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>59.  Y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nnabiyyu qul li-azw<u>a</u>jika waban<u>a</u>tika wanis<u>a</u>-i almu/mineena yudneena AAalayhinna min jal<u>a</u>beebihinna <u>tha</u>lika adn<u>a</u> an yuAArafna fal<u>a</u> yu/<u>th</u>ayna wak<u>a</u>na All<u>a</u>hu ghafooran ra<u>h</u>eem<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>59.  O Prophet! Tell<br> Thy wives and daughters,<br> And the believing women,<br> That they should cast<br> Their outer garments over<br> Their persons (when abroad):<br> That is most convenient,<br> That they should be known<br>(As such) and not molested.<br> And God is Oft-Forgiving,<br> Most Merciful.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/033/033_060.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>60.  La-in lam yantahi almun<u>a</u>fiqoona wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena fee quloobihim mara<u>d</u>un wa<b>a</b>lmurjifoona fee almadeenati lanughriyannaka bihim thumma l<u>a</u> yuj<u>a</u>wiroonaka feeh<u>a</u> ill<u>a</u> qaleel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>60.  Truly, if the Hypocrites,<br> And those in whose hearts<br> Is a disease, and those who<br> Stir up sedition in the City,<br> Desist not, We shall certainly<br> Stir thee up against them:<br> Then will they not be<br> Able to stay in it<br> As thy neighbours<br> For any length of time:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/033/033_061.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>61.  MalAAooneena ayna m<u>a</u> thuqifoo okhi<u>th</u>oo waquttiloo taqteel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>61.  They shall have a curse<br> On them: wherever they<br> Are found, they shall be<br> Seized and slain<br> (Without mercy).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/033/033_062.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>62.  Sunnata All<u>a</u>hi fee alla<u>th</u>eena khalaw min qablu walan tajida lisunnati All<u>a</u>hi tabdeel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>62.  (Such was) the practice<br> (Approved) of God among those<br> Who lived aforetime:<br> No change wilt thou find<br> In the practice (approved)<br> Of God.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/033/033_063.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>63.  Yas-aluka a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>su AAani a<b>l</b>ss<u>a</u>AAati qul innam<u>a</u> AAilmuh<u>a</u> AAinda All<u>a</u>hi wam<u>a</u> yudreeka laAAalla a<b>l</b>ss<u>a</u>AAata takoonu qareeb<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>63.  Men ask thee concerning<br> The Hour: say, "The knowledge<br> Thereof is with God (alone)":<br> And what will make thee<br> Understand?—perchance<br> The Hour is nigh!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/033/033_064.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>64.  Inna All<u>a</u>ha laAAana alk<u>a</u>fireena waaAAadda lahum saAAeer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>64.  Verily God has cursed<br> The Unbelievers and prepared<br> For them a Blazing Fire,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/033/033_065.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>65.  Kh<u>a</u>lideena feeh<u>a</u> abadan l<u>a</u> yajidoona waliyyan wal<u>a</u> na<u>s</u>eer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>65.  To dwell therein for ever:<br> No protector will they find,<br> Nor helper.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/033/033_066.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>66.  Yawma tuqallabu wujoohuhum fee a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>ri yaqooloona y<u>a</u> laytan<u>a</u> a<u>t</u>aAAn<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>ha waa<u>t</u>aAAn<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>rrasool<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>66.  The Day that their faces<br> Will be turned upside down<br> In the Fire, they will say:<br> "Woe to us! would that<br> We had obeyed God<br> And obeyed the Apostle!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/033/033_067.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>67.  Waq<u>a</u>loo rabban<u>a</u> inn<u>a</u> a<u>t</u>aAAn<u>a</u> s<u>a</u>datan<u>a</u> wakubar<u>a</u>an<u>a</u> faa<u>d</u>alloon<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>ssabeel<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>67.  And they would say:<br> "Our Lord! We obeyed<br> Our chiefs and our great ones,<br> And they misled us<br> As to the (right) path.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/033/033_068.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>68.  Rabban<u>a</u> <u>a</u>tihim <u>d</u>iAAfayni mina alAAa<u>tha</u>bi wa<b>i</b>lAAanhum laAAnan kabeer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>68.  "Our Lord! Give them<br> Double Penalty<br> And curse them<br> With a very great Curse!"</p> <div class="filenav">
