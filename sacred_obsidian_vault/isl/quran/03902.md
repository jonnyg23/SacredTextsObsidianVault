 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXXIX.: Zumar, or the Crowds.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 2</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/039/039_010.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>10.  Qul y<u>a</u> AAib<u>a</u>di alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo ittaqoo rabbakum lilla<u>th</u>eena a<u>h</u>sanoo fee h<u>ath</u>ihi a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> <u>h</u>asanatun waar<u>d</u>u All<u>a</u>hi w<u>a</u>siAAatun innam<u>a</u> yuwaff<u>a</u> a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>biroona ajrahum bighayri <u>h</u>is<u>a</u>b<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>10.  Say: "O ye<br> My servants who believe!<br> Fear your Lord.<br> Good is (the reward)<br> For those who do good<br> In this world.<br>Spacious is God's earth!<br> Those who patiently persevere<br> Will truly receive<br> A reward without measure!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/039/039_011.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>11.  Qul innee omirtu an aAAbuda All<u>a</u>ha mukhli<u>s</u>an lahu a<b>l</b>ddeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>11.  Say: "Verily, I am commanded<br> To serve God<br> With sincere devotion;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/039/039_012.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>12.  Waomirtu li-an akoona awwala almuslimeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>12.  "And I am commanded<br> To be the first<br> Of those who bow<br> To God in Islam."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/039/039_013.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>13.  Qul innee akh<u>a</u>fu in AAa<u>s</u>aytu rabbee AAa<u>tha</u>ba yawmin AAa<i><u>th</u></i>eem<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>13.  Say: "I would, if I<br> Disobeyed my Lord,<br> Indeed have fear<br> Of the Penalty<br> Of a Mighty Day."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/039/039_014.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>14.  Quli All<u>a</u>ha aAAbudu mukhli<u>s</u>an lahu deenee   </p>
 <p>14.  Say: "It is God I serve,<br> With my sincere<br> (And exclusive) devotion:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/039/039_015.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>15.  Fa<b>o</b>AAbudoo m<u>a</u> shi/tum min doonihi qul inna alkh<u>a</u>sireena alla<u>th</u>eena khasiroo anfusahum waahleehim yawma alqiy<u>a</u>mati al<u>a</u> <u>tha</u>lika huwa alkhusr<u>a</u>nu almubeen<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>15.  "Serve ye what ye will<br> Besides Him." Say:<br> "Truly, those in loss<br> Are those who lose<br> Their own souls<br> And their People<br> On the Day of Judgment:<br> Ah! that is indeed<br> The (real and) evident Loss!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/039/039_016.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>16.  Lahum min fawqihim <i><u>th</u></i>ulalun mina a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>ri wamin ta<u>h</u>tihim <i><u>th</u></i>ulalun <u>tha</u>lika yukhawwifu All<u>a</u>hu bihi AAib<u>a</u>dahu y<u>a</u> AAib<u>a</u>di fa<b>i</b>ttaqoon<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>16.  They shall have Layers<br> Of Fire above them,<br> And Layers (of Fire)<br> Below them: with this<br> Doth God warn off<br> His Servants: "O My Servants!<br> Then fear ye Me!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/039/039_017.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>17.  Wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena ijtanaboo a<b>l</b><u>tta</u>ghoota an yaAAbudooh<u>a</u> waan<u>a</u>boo il<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>hi lahumu albushr<u>a</u> fabashshir AAib<u>a</u>d<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>17.  Those who eschew Evil,<br> And fall not into<br> Its worship,—and turn<br> To God (in repentance),—<br> For them is Good News:<br> So announce the Good News<br> To My Servants,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/039/039_018.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>18.  Alla<u>th</u>eena yastamiAAoona alqawla fayattabiAAoona a<u>h</u>sanahu ol<u>a</u>-ika alla<u>th</u>eena had<u>a</u>humu All<u>a</u>hu waol<u>a</u>-ika hum oloo al-alb<u>a</u>b<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>18.  Those who listen<br> To the Word,<br> And follow<br> The best (meaning) in it:<br> Those are the ones<br> Whom God has guided, and those<br> Are the ones endued<br> With understanding.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/039/039_019.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>19.  Afaman <u>h</u>aqqa AAalayhi kalimatu alAAa<u>tha</u>bi afaanta tunqi<u>th</u>u man fee a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>r<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>19.  Is, then, one against whom<br> The decree of Punishment<br>Is justly due (equal<br> To one who eschews evil)?<br> Wouldst thou, then, deliver<br> One (who is) in the Fire?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/039/039_020.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>20.  L<u>a</u>kini alla<u>th</u>eena ittaqaw rabbahum lahum ghurafun min fawqih<u>a</u> ghurafun mabniyyatun tajree min ta<u>h</u>tih<u>a</u> al-anh<u>a</u>ru waAAda All<u>a</u>hi l<u>a</u> yukhlifu All<u>a</u>hu almeeAA<u>a</u>d<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>20.  But it is for those<br> Who fear their Lord,<br> That lofty mansions,<br> One above another,<br> Have been built:<br> Beneath them flow<br> Rivers (of delight): (such is)<br> The Promise of God:<br> Never doth God fail in<br> (His) promise.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/039/039_021.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>21.  Alam tara anna All<u>a</u>ha anzala mina a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>-i m<u>a</u>an fasalakahu yan<u>a</u>beeAAa fee al-ar<u>d</u>i thumma yukhriju bihi zarAAan mukhtalifan alw<u>a</u>nuhu thumma yaheeju fatar<u>a</u>hu mu<u>s</u>farran thumma yajAAaluhu <u>h</u>u<u>ta</u>man inna fee <u>tha</u>lika la<u>th</u>ikr<u>a</u> li-olee al-alb<u>a</u>b<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>21.  Seest thou not that God<br> Sends down rain from<br> The sky, and leads it<br> Through springs in the earth.?<br> Then He causes to grow,<br> Therewith, produce of various<br> Colours: then it withers;<br> Thou wilt see it grow yellow;<br> Then He makes it<br> Dry up and crumble away.<br> Truly, in this, is<br> A Message of remembrance to<br> Men of understanding.</p> <div class="filenav">
