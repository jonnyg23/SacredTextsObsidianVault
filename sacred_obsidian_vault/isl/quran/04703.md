 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XLVII.: Muḥammad (the Prophet).</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 3</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/047/047_020.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>20.  Wayaqoolu alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo lawl<u>a</u> nuzzilat sooratun fa-i<u>tha</u> onzilat sooratun mu<u>h</u>kamatun wa<u>th</u>ukira feeh<u>a</u> alqit<u>a</u>lu raayta alla<u>th</u>eena fee quloobihim mara<u>d</u>un yan<i><u>th</u></i>uroona ilayka na<i><u>th</u></i>ara almaghshiyyi AAalayhi mina almawti faawl<u>a</u> lahum   </p>
 <p>20.  Those who believe say,<br> "Why is not a Sūra<br> Sent down (for us)?"<br> But when a Sūra<br> Of basic or categorical<br> Meaning is revealed,<br> And fighting is mentioned<br> Therein, thou wilt see those<br> In whose hearts is a disease<br> Looking at thee with a look<br> Of one in swoon at<br> The approach of death.<br> But more fitting for them—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/047/047_021.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>21.  <u>Ta</u>AAatun waqawlun maAAroofun fa-i<u>tha</u> AAazama al-amru falaw <u>s</u>adaqoo All<u>a</u>ha lak<u>a</u>na khayran lahum    </p>
 <p>21.  Were it to obey<br> And say what is just,<br> And when a matter<br> Is resolved on, it were<br> Best for them if they<br> Were true to God.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/047/047_022.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>22.  Fahal AAasaytum in tawallaytum an tufsidoo fee al-ar<u>d</u>i watuqa<u>tt</u>iAAoo ar<u>ha</u>makum   </p>
 <p>22.  Then, is it<br> To be expected of you,<br> If ye were put in authority,<br> That ye will do mischief<br> In the land, and break<br> Your ties of kith and kin?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/047/047_023.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>23.  Ol<u>a</u>-ika alla<u>th</u>eena laAAanahumu All<u>a</u>hu faa<u>s</u>ammahum waaAAm<u>a</u> ab<u>sa</u>rahum   </p>
 <p>23.  </p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/047/047_024.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>24.  Afal<u>a</u> yatadabbaroona alqur-<u>a</u>na am AAal<u>a</u> quloobin aqf<u>a</u>luh<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>24.  Do they not then<br> Earnestly seek to understand<br> The Qur-ān, or are<br> Their hearts locked up<br> By them?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/047/047_025.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>25.  Inna alla<u>th</u>eena irtaddoo AAal<u>a</u> adb<u>a</u>rihim min baAAdi m<u>a</u> tabayyana lahumu alhud<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>shshay<u>ta</u>nu sawwala lahum waaml<u>a</u> lahum   </p>
 <p>25.  Those who turn back<br> As apostates after Guidance<br> Was clearly shown to them,—<br> The Evil One has instigated<br> Them and buoyed them up<br> With false hopes.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/047/047_026.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>26.  <u>Tha</u>lika bi-annahum q<u>a</u>loo lilla<u>th</u>eena karihoo m<u>a</u> nazzala All<u>a</u>hu sanu<u>t</u>eeAAukum fee baAA<u>d</u>i al-amri wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu yaAAlamu isr<u>a</u>rahum   </p>
 <p>26.  This, because they said<br> To those who hate what<br> God has revealed, "We<br> Will obey you in part<br> Of (this) matter"; but God<br> Knows their (inner) secrets.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/047/047_027.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>27.  Fakayfa i<u>tha</u> tawaffat-humu almal<u>a</u>-ikatu ya<u>d</u>riboona wujoohahum waadb<u>a</u>rahum    </p>
 <p>27.  But how (will it be)<br> When the angels take<br> Their souls at death,<br> And smite their faces<br> And their backs?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/047/047_028.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>28.  <u>Tha</u>lika bi-annahumu ittabaAAoo m<u>a</u> askha<u>t</u>a All<u>a</u>ha wakarihoo ri<u>d</u>w<u>a</u>nahu faa<u>h</u>ba<u>t</u>a aAAm<u>a</u>lahum   </p>
 <p>28.  This because they followed<br> That which called forth<br> The Wrath of God, and<br> They hated God's good pleasure;<br> So He made their deeds<br> Of no effect.</p> <div class="filenav">
