 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra CIX: Kāfirūn, or Those who reject Faith.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/109/109_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  Qul y<u>a</u> ayyuh<u>a</u> alk<u>a</u>firoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>1.  Say: O ye<br> That reject Faith!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/109/109_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  L<u>a</u> aAAbudu m<u>a</u> taAAbudoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>2.  I worship not that<br> Which ye worship,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/109/109_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  Wal<u>a</u> antum AA<u>a</u>bidoona m<u>a</u> aAAbud<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>3.  Nor will ye worship<br> That which I worship.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/109/109_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  Wal<u>a</u> an<u>a</u> AA<u>a</u>bidun m<u>a</u> AAabadtum   </p>
 <p>4.  And I will not worship<br> That which ye have been<br> Wont to worship,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/109/109_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  Wal<u>a</u> antum AA<u>a</u>bidoona m<u>a</u> aAAbud<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>5.  Nor will ye worship<br> That which I worship.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/109/109_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  Lakum deenukum waliya deen<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>6.  To you be your Way,<br> And to me mine.</p>  <div class="filenav">
