 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXII.: Ḥajj, or The Pilgrimage.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 2</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/022/022_011.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>11.  Wamina a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si man yaAAbudu All<u>a</u>ha AAal<u>a</u> <u>h</u>arfin fa-in a<u>sa</u>bahu khayrun i<u>t</u>maanna bihi wa-in a<u>sa</u>bat-hu fitnatun inqalaba AAal<u>a</u> wajhihi khasira a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> wa<b>a</b>l-<u>a</u>khirata <u>tha</u>lika huwa alkhusr<u>a</u>nu almubeen<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>11.  There are among men<br> Some who serve God,<br> As it were, on the verge:<br> If good befalls them, they are,<br> Therewith, well content; but<br> If a trial comes to them,<br> They turn on their faces:<br> They lose both this world<br> And the Hereafter: that<br> Is loss for all to see!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/022/022_012.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>12.  YadAAoo min dooni All<u>a</u>hi m<u>a</u> l<u>a</u> ya<u>d</u>urruhu wam<u>a</u> l<u>a</u> yanfaAAuhu <u>tha</u>lika huwa a<b>l</b><u>dd</u>al<u>a</u>lu albaAAeed<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>12.  They call on such deities:<br> Besides God, as can neither<br> Hurt nor profit them:<br> That is straying far indeed<br> (From the Way)!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/022/022_013.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>13.  YadAAoo laman <u>d</u>arruhu aqrabu min nafAAihi labi/sa almawl<u>a</u> walabi/sa alAAasheer<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>13.  (Perhaps) they call on one<br> Whose hurt is nearer<br> Than his profit: evil, indeed,<br> Is the patron, and evil<br> The companion (for help)!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/022/022_014.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>14.  Inna All<u>a</u>ha yudkhilu alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo waAAamiloo a<b>l</b><u>ssa</u>li<u>ha</u>ti jann<u>a</u>tin tajree min ta<u>h</u>tih<u>a</u> al-anh<u>a</u>ru inna All<u>a</u>ha yafAAalu m<u>a</u> yureed<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>14.  Verily God will admit<br> Those who believe and work<br> Righteous deeds, to Gardens,<br> Beneath which rivers flow:<br> For God carries out<br> All that He plans,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/022/022_015.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>15.  Man k<u>a</u>na ya<i><u>th</u></i>unnu an lan yan<u>s</u>urahu All<u>a</u>hu fee a<b>l</b>dduny<u>a</u> wa<b>a</b>l-<u>a</u>khirati falyamdud bisababin il<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>-i thumma liyaq<u>t</u>aAA falyan<i><u>th</u></i>ur hal yu<u>th</u>hibanna kayduhu m<u>a</u> yaghee<i><u>th</u></i><b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>15.  If any think that God<br> Will not help him<br> (His Apostle) in this world<br> And the Hereafter, let him<br> Stretch out a rope<br> To the ceiling and cut (himself)<br> Off: then let him see<br> Whether his plan will remove<br> That which enrages (him)!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/022/022_016.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>16.  Waka<u>tha</u>lika anzaln<u>a</u>hu <u>a</u>y<u>a</u>tin bayyin<u>a</u>tin waanna All<u>a</u>ha yahdee man yureed<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>16.  Thus have We sent down<br> Clear Signs; and verily<br> God Both guide whom<br> He will!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/022/022_017.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>17.  Inna alla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena h<u>a</u>doo wa<b>al</b><u>ssa</u>bi-eena wa<b>al</b>nna<u>sa</u>r<u>a</u> wa<b>a</b>lmajoosa wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena ashrakoo inna All<u>a</u>ha yaf<u>s</u>ilu baynahum yawma alqiy<u>a</u>mati inna All<u>a</u>ha AAal<u>a</u> kulli shay-in shaheed<b>un</b>   </p>
 <p>17.  Those who believe (in the Qur-ān),<br> Those who follow the Jewish (scriptures),<br> And the Sabians, Christians,<br> Magians, and Polytheists,—<br> God will judge between them<br>On the Day of Judgment:<br> For God is witness<br> Of all things.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/022/022_018.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>18.  Alam tara anna All<u>a</u>ha yasjudu lahu man fee a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti waman fee al-ar<u>d</u>i wa<b>al</b>shshamsu wa<b>a</b>lqamaru wa<b>al</b>nnujoomu wa<b>a</b>ljib<u>a</u>lu wa<b>al</b>shshajaru wa<b>al</b>ddaw<u>a</u>bbu wakatheerun mina a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si wakatheerun <u>h</u>aqqa AAalayhi alAAa<u>tha</u>bu waman yuhini All<u>a</u>hu fam<u>a</u> lahu min mukrimin inna All<u>a</u>ha yafAAalu m<u>a</u> yash<u>a</u>/<b>o</b>   </p>
 <p>18.  Seest thou not that<br> To God bow down in worship<br> All things that are<br> In the heavens and on earth,—<br> The sun, the moon, the stars;<br> The hills, the trees, the animals;<br> And a great number among<br> Mankind? But a great number<br> Are (also) such as are<br> Fit for Punishment: and such<br> As God shall disgrace,<br> None can raise to honour:<br> For God carries out<br> All that He wills.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/022/022_019.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>19.  H<u>atha</u>ni kha<u>s</u>m<u>a</u>ni ikhta<u>s</u>amoo fee rabbihim fa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo qu<u>tt</u>iAAat lahum thiy<u>a</u>bun min n<u>a</u>rin yu<u>s</u>abbu min fawqi ruoosihimu al<u>h</u>ameem<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>19.  These two antagonists dispute<br> With each other about their Lord:<br> But those who deny (their Lord),—<br> For them will be cut out<br> A garment of Fire:<br> Over their heads will be<br> Poured out boiling water.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/022/022_020.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>20.  Yu<u>s</u>haru bihi m<u>a</u> fee bu<u>t</u>oonihim wa<b>a</b>ljulood<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>20.  With it will be scalded<br> What is within their bodies,<br> As well as (their) skins.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/022/022_021.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>21.  Walahum maq<u>a</u>miAAu min <u>h</u>adeed<b>in</b>   </p>
 <p>21.  In addition there will be<br> Maces of iron (to punish) them.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/022/022_022.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>22.  Kullam<u>a</u> ar<u>a</u>doo an yakhrujoo minh<u>a</u> min ghammin oAAeedoo feeh<u>a</u> wa<u>th</u>ooqoo AAa<u>tha</u>ba al<u>h</u>areeq<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>22.  Every time they wish<br> To get away therefrom,<br> From anguish, they will be<br> Forced back therein, and<br> (It will be said), "Taste ye<br> The Penalty of Burning!"</p> <div class="filenav">
