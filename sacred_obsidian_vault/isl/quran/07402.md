 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra LXXIV.: Mudda<u>th</u><u>th</u>ir, or One Wrapped Up.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 2</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/074/074_032.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>32.  Kall<u>a</u> wa<b>a</b>lqamar<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>32.  Nay, verily:<br> By the Moon,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/074/074_033.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>33.  Wa<b>a</b>llayli i<u>th</u> adbar<b>a</b>    </p>
 <p>33.  And by the Night<br> As it retreateth,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/074/074_034.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>34.  Wa<b>al</b><u>ss</u>ub<u>h</u>i i<u>tha</u> asfar<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>34.  And by the Dawn<br> As it shineth forth,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/074/074_035.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>35.  Innah<u>a</u> la-i<u>h</u>d<u>a</u> alkubar<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>35.  This is but one<br> Of the mighty (portents),</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/074/074_036.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>36.  Na<u>th</u>eeran lilbashar<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>36.  A warning to mankind,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/074/074_037.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>37.  Liman sh<u>a</u>a minkum an yataqaddama aw yataakhkhar<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>37.  To any of you that<br> Chooses to press forward,<br> Or to follow behind;—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/074/074_038.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>38.  Kullu nafsin bim<u>a</u> kasabat raheena<b>tun</b>   </p>
 <p>38.  Every soul will be (held)<br> In pledge for its deeds.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/074/074_039.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>39.  Ill<u>a</u> a<u>s</u>-<u>ha</u>ba alyameen<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>39.  Except the Companions<br> Of the Right Hand.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/074/074_040.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>40.  Fee jann<u>a</u>tin yatas<u>a</u>aloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>40.  (They will be) in Gardens<br> (Of Delight): they will<br> Question each other,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/074/074_041.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>41.  AAani almujrimeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>41.  And (ask) of the Sinners:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/074/074_042.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>42.  M<u>a</u> salakakum fee saqar<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>42.  "What led you<br> Into Hell-Fire?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/074/074_043.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>43.  Q<u>a</u>loo lam naku mina almu<u>s</u>alleen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>43.  They will say:<br> "We were not of those<br> Who prayed;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/074/074_044.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>44.  Walam naku nu<u>t</u>AAimu almiskeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>44.  "Nor were we of those<br> Who fed the indigent;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/074/074_045.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>45.  Wakunn<u>a</u> nakhoo<u>d</u>u maAAa alkh<u>a</u>-i<u>d</u>een<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>45.  "But we used to talk<br> Vanities with vain talkers;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/074/074_046.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>46.  Wakunn<u>a</u> nuka<u>thth</u>ibu biyawmi a<b>l</b>ddeen<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>46.  "And we used to deny<br> The Day of Judgment,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/074/074_047.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>47.  <u>H</u>att<u>a</u> at<u>a</u>n<u>a</u> alyaqeen<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>47.  "Until there came to us<br> (The Hour) that is certain."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/074/074_048.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>48.  Fam<u>a</u> tanfaAAuhum shaf<u>a</u>AAatu a<b>l</b>shsh<u>a</u>fiAAeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>48.  Then will no intercession<br> Of (any) intercessors<br> Profit them.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/074/074_049.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>49.  Fam<u>a</u> lahum AAani a<b>l</b>tta<u>th</u>kirati muAAri<u>d</u>een<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>49.  When what is<br> The matter with them<br> That they turn away<br> From admonition?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/074/074_050.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>50.  Kaannahum <u>h</u>umurun mustanfira<b>tun</b>   </p>
 <p>50.  As if they were<br> Affrighted asses,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/074/074_051.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>51.  Farrat min qaswara<b>tin</b>   </p>
 <p>51.  Fleeing from a lion!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/074/074_052.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>52.  Bal yureedu kullu imri-in minhum an yu/t<u>a</u> <u>s</u>u<u>h</u>ufan munashshara<b>tan</b>   </p>
 <p>52.  Forsooth, each one of them<br> Wants to be given<br> Scrolls (of revelation) spread out!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/074/074_053.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>53.  Kall<u>a</u> bal l<u>a</u> yakh<u>a</u>foona al-<u>a</u>khira<b>ta</b>   </p>
 <p>53.  By no means! But<br> They fear not the Hereafter.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/074/074_054.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>54.  Kall<u>a</u> innahu ta<u>th</u>kira<b>tun</b>   </p>
 <p>54.  Nay, this surely<br> Is an admonition:</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/074/074_055.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>55.  Faman sh<u>a</u>a <u>th</u>akarah<b>u</b>   </p>
 <p>55.  Let any who will,<br> Keep it in remembrance!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/074/074_056.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>56.  Wam<u>a</u> ya<u>th</u>kuroona ill<u>a</u> an yash<u>a</u>a All<u>a</u>hu huwa ahlu a<b>l</b>ttaqw<u>a</u> waahlu almaghfirat<b>i</b>   </p>
 <p>56.  But none will keep it<br> In remembrance except<br> As God wills: He<br> Is the Lord of Righteousness,<br> And the Lord of Forgiveness.</p>  <div class="filenav">
