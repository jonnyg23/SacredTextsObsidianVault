 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XXV.: Furqān, or The Criterion.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 1</h3> 
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/025/025_001.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>1.  Tab<u>a</u>raka alla<u>th</u>ee nazzala alfurq<u>a</u>na AAal<u>a</u> AAabdihi liyakoona lilAA<u>a</u>lameena na<u>th</u>eer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>1.  Blessed is He Who<br> Sent down the Criterion<br> To His Servant, that<br> May be an admonition<br> To all creatures;—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/025/025_002.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>2.  Alla<u>th</u>ee lahu mulku a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i walam yattakhi<u>th</u> waladan walam yakun lahu shareekun fee almulki wakhalaqa kulla shay-in faqaddarahu taqdeer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>2.  He to Whom belongs<br> The dominion of the heavens<br> And the earth: no son<br> Has He begotten, nor has He<br> A partner in His dominion:<br> It is He Who created<br> All things, and ordered them<br> In due proportions.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/025/025_003.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>3.  Wa<b>i</b>ttakha<u>th</u>oo min doonihi <u>a</u>lihatan l<u>a</u> yakhluqoona shay-an wahum yukhlaqoona wal<u>a</u> yamlikoona li-anfusihim <u>d</u>arran wal<u>a</u> nafAAan wal<u>a</u> yamlikoona mawtan wal<u>a</u> <u>h</u>ay<u>a</u>tan wal<u>a</u> nushoor<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>3.  Yet have they taken,<br> Besides Him, gods that can<br> Create nothing but are themselves<br> Created; that have no control<br> Of hurt or good to themselves;<br> Nor can they control Death<br> Nor Life nor Resurrection.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/025/025_004.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>4.  Waq<u>a</u>la alla<u>th</u>eena kafaroo in h<u>atha</u> ill<u>a</u> ifkun iftar<u>a</u>hu waaAA<u>a</u>nahu AAalayhi qawmun <u>a</u>kharoona faqad j<u>a</u>oo <i><u>th</u></i>ulman wazoor<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>4.  But the Misbelievers say:<br> "Naught is this but a lie<br> Which he has forged,<br> And others have helped him<br> At it." In truth it is they<br> Who have put forward<br> An iniquity and a falsehood.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/025/025_005.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>5.  Waq<u>a</u>loo as<u>at</u>eeru al-awwaleena iktatabah<u>a</u> fahiya tuml<u>a</u> AAalayhi bukratan waa<u>s</u>eel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>5.  And they say: "Tales of<br> The ancients, which he has caused<br> To be written: and they<br> Are dictated before him<br> Morning and evening."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/025/025_006.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>6.  Qul anzalahu alla<u>th</u>ee yaAAlamu a<b>l</b>ssirra fee a<b>l</b>ssam<u>a</u>w<u>a</u>ti wa<b>a</b>l-ar<u>d</u>i innahu k<u>a</u>na ghafooran ra<u>h</u>eem<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>6.  Say: "The (Qur-ān) was sent down<br> By Him Who knows<br> The Mystery (that is) in the heavens<br> And the earth: verily He<br> Is Oft-Forgiving, Most Merciful."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/025/025_007.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>7.  Waq<u>a</u>loo m<u>a</u>li h<u>atha</u> a<b>l</b>rrasooli ya/kulu a<b>l</b><u>tt</u>aAA<u>a</u>ma wayamshee fee al-asw<u>a</u>qi lawl<u>a</u> onzila ilayhi malakun fayakoona maAAahu na<u>th</u>eer<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>7.  find they say: "What sort<br> Of an apostle is this,<br> Who eats food, and walks<br> Through the streets? Why<br> Has not an angel<br> Been sent down to him<br> To give admonition with him?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/025/025_008.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>8.  Aw yulq<u>a</u> ilayhi kanzun aw takoonu lahu jannatun ya/kulu minh<u>a</u> waq<u>a</u>la a<b>l</b><i><u>thth</u></i><u>a</u>limoona in tattabiAAoona ill<u>a</u> rajulan mas<u>h</u>oor<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>8.  "Or (why) has not a treasure<br> Been bestowed on him, or<br> Why has he (not) a garden<br> For enjoyment?" The wicked<br> Say: "Ye follow none other<br> Than a man bewitched."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/025/025_009.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>9.  On<i><u>th</u></i>ur kayfa <u>d</u>araboo laka al-amth<u>a</u>la fa<u>d</u>alloo fal<u>a</u> yasta<u>t</u>eeAAoona sabeel<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>9.  See what kinds of comparisons<br> They make for thee!<br> But they have gone astray,<br> And never a way will they<br> Be able to find!</p> <div class="filenav">
