 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra XX.: Ṭā-Hā. (Mystic Letters, Ṭ. H.)</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 5</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_090.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>90.  Walaqad q<u>a</u>la lahum h<u>a</u>roonu min qablu y<u>a</u> qawmi innam<u>a</u> futintum bihi wa-inna rabbakumu a<b>l</b>rra<u>h</u>m<u>a</u>nu fa<b>i</b>ttabiAAoonee waa<u>t</u>eeAAoo amree   </p>
 <p>90.  Aaron had already, before this<br> Said to them: ."O my people!<br> Ye are being tested in this:<br> For verily your Lord is (God)<br> Most Gracious: so follow me<br> And obey my command."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_091.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>91.  Q<u>a</u>loo lan nabra<u>h</u>a AAalayhi AA<u>a</u>kifeena <u>h</u>att<u>a</u> yarjiAAa ilayn<u>a</u> moos<u>a</u>   </p>
 <p>91.  They had said: "We will not<br> Abandon this cult, but we<br> Will devote ourselves to it<br> Until Moses returns to us.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_092.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>92.  Q<u>a</u>la y<u>a</u> h<u>a</u>roonu m<u>a</u> manaAAaka i<u>th</u> raaytahum <u>d</u>alloo    </p>
 <p>92.  (Moses) said: "O Aaron!<br> What kept thee back, when<br> Thou sawest them going wrong,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_093.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>93.  All<u>a</u> tattabiAAani afaAAa<u>s</u>ayta amree   </p>
 <p>93.  "From following me? Didst thou<br> Then disobey my order?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_094.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>94.  Q<u>a</u>la yabnaomma l<u>a</u> ta/khu<u>th</u> bili<u>h</u>yatee wal<u>a</u> bira/see innee khasheetu an taqoola farraqta bayna banee isr<u>a</u>-eela walam tarqub qawlee   </p>
 <p>94.  (Aaron) replied: "O son<br> Of my mother! Seize (me) not<br> By my beard nor by<br> (The hair of) my head!<br> Truly I feared lest thou<br> Shouldst say, "Thou hast caused<br> A division among the Children<br> Of Israel, and thou didst not<br> Respect my word!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_095.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>95.  Q<u>a</u>la fam<u>a</u> kha<u>t</u>buka y<u>a</u> s<u>a</u>miriy<b>yu</b>   </p>
 <p>95.  (Moses) said: "What then<br> Is thy case, O Sāmarī?"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_096.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>96.  Q<u>a</u>la ba<u>s</u>urtu bim<u>a</u> lam yab<u>s</u>uroo bihi faqaba<u>d</u>tu qab<u>d</u>atan min athari a<b>l</b>rrasooli fanaba<u>th</u>tuh<u>a</u> waka<u>tha</u>lika sawwalat lee nafsee   </p>
 <p>96.  He replied: "I saw what<br> They saw not: so I took<br>A handful (of dust) from<br> The footprint of the Apostle,<br> And threw it (into the calf):<br> Thus did my soul suggest<br> To me."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_097.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>97.  Q<u>a</u>la fa<b>i</b><u>th</u>hab fa-inna laka fee al<u>h</u>ay<u>a</u>ti an taqoola l<u>a</u> mis<u>a</u>sa wa-inna laka mawAAidan lan tukhlafahu wa<b>o</b>n<i><u>th</u></i>ur il<u>a</u> il<u>a</u>hika alla<u>th</u>ee <i><u>th</u></i>alta AAalayhi AA<u>a</u>kifan lanu<u>h</u>arriqannahu thumma lanansifannahu fee alyammi nasf<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>97.  (Moses) said: "Get thee gone!<br> But thy (punishment) in this life<br> Will be that thou wilt say,<br> 'Touch me not'; and moreover<br> (For a future penalty) thou hast<br> A promise that will not fail:<br> Now look at thy god,<br> Of whom thou hast become<br> A devoted worshipper:<br> We will certainly (melt) it<br> In a blazing fire and scatter<br> It broadcast in the sea!"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_098.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>98.  Innam<u>a</u> il<u>a</u>hukumu All<u>a</u>hu alla<u>th</u>ee l<u>a</u> il<u>a</u>ha ill<u>a</u> huwa wasiAAa kulla shay-in AAilm<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>98.  But the God of you all<br> Is the One God: there is<br> No god but He: all things<br> He comprehends in His knowledge.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_099.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>99.  Ka<u>tha</u>lika naqu<u>ss</u>u AAalayka min anb<u>a</u>-i m<u>a</u> qad sabaqa waqad <u>a</u>tayn<u>a</u>ka min ladunn<u>a</u> thikra<b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>99.  Thus do We relate to thee<br> Some stories of what happened<br> Before: for We have sent<br> Thee a Message from<br> Our own Presence,</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_100.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>100.  Man aAAra<u>d</u>a AAanhu fa-innahu ya<u>h</u>milu yawma alqiy<u>a</u>mati wizr<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>100.  If any do turn away<br> Therefrom, verily they will<br> Bear a burden<br> On the Day of Judgment;</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_101.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>101.  Kh<u>a</u>lideena feehi was<u>a</u>a lahum yawma alqiy<u>a</u>mati <u>h</u>iml<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>101.  They will abide in this (state):<br> And grievous will the burden<br> Be to them on that Day,—</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_102.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>102.  Yawma yunfakhu fee a<b>l</b><u>ss</u>oori wana<u>h</u>shuru almujrimeena yawma-i<u>th</u>in zurq<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>102.  The Day when the Trumpet<br> Will be sounded: that Day,<br> We shall gather the sinful,<br> Blear-eyed (with terror).</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_103.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>103.  Yatakh<u>a</u>fatoona baynahum in labithtum ill<u>a</u> AAashr<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>103.  In whispers will they consult<br> Each other: "Ye tarried not<br> Longer than ten (Days);"</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/020/020_104.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>104.  Na<u>h</u>nu aAAlamu bim<u>a</u> yaqooloona i<u>th</u> yaqoolu amthaluhum <u>t</u>areeqatan in labithtum ill<u>a</u> yawm<u>a</u><b>n</b>   </p>
 <p>104.  We know best what they<br> Will say, when their leader<br>Most eminent in Conduct<br> Will say: "Ye tarried not<br> Longer than a day!"</p> <div class="filenav">
