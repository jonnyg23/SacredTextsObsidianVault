 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Holy Quran</i>, tr. by Yusuf Ali, [1934], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h1 align="CENTER">Sūra III.: Āl-i-’Imrān, or The Family of ’Imrān.</h1> <h3 align="CENTER">Section 7</h3> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_064.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>64.  Qul y<u>a</u> ahla alkit<u>a</u>bi taAA<u>a</u>law il<u>a</u> kalimatin saw<u>a</u>-in baynan<u>a</u> wabaynakum all<u>a</u> naAAbuda ill<u>a</u> All<u>a</u>ha wal<u>a</u> nushrika bihi shay-an wal<u>a</u> yattakhi<u>th</u>a baAA<u>d</u>un<u>a</u> baAA<u>d</u>an arb<u>a</u>ban min dooni All<u>a</u>hi fa-in tawallaw faqooloo ishhadoo bi-ann<u>a</u> muslimoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>64.  Say: "O People<br> Of the Book! come<br> To common terms<br> As between us and you:<br> That we worship<br> None but God;<br> That we associate<br> No partners with Him;<br> That we erect not,<br> From among ourselves,<br> Lords and patrons<br> Other than God."<br> If then they turn back,<br> Say ye: "Bear witness<br> That we (at least)<br> Are Muslims (bowing<br> To God's Will)."</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_065.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>65.  Y<u>a</u> ahla alkit<u>a</u>bi lima tu<u>ha</u>jjoona fee ibr<u>a</u>heema wam<u>a</u> onzilati a<b>l</b>ttawr<u>a</u>tu wa<b>a</b>l-injeelu ill<u>a</u> min baAAdihi afal<u>a</u> taAAqiloon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>65.  Ye People of the Book!<br> Why dispute ye<br> About Abraham,<br> When the Law and the Gospel<br> Were not revealed<br> Till after him?<br> Have ye no understanding?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_066.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>66.  H<u>a</u> antum h<u>a</u>ol<u>a</u>-i <u>ha</u>jajtum feem<u>a</u> lakum bihi AAilmun falima tu<u>ha</u>jjoona feem<u>a</u> laysa lakum bihi AAilmun wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu yaAAlamu waantum l<u>a</u> taAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>66.  Ah! Ye are those<br> Who fell to disputing<br> (Even) in matters of which<br> Ye had some knowledge!<br> But why dispute ye<br> In matters of which<br> Ye have no knowledge?<br> It is God Who knows,<br> And ye who know not!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_067.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>67.  M<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>na ibr<u>a</u>heemu yahoodiyyan wal<u>a</u> na<u>s</u>r<u>a</u>niyyan wal<u>a</u>kin k<u>a</u>na <u>h</u>aneefan musliman wam<u>a</u> k<u>a</u>na mina almushrikeen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>67.  Abraham was not a Jew<br> Nor yet a Christian;<br> But he was true in Faith,<br> And bowed his will to God's,<br> (Which is Islam),<br> And he joined not gods with God.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_068.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>68.  Inna awl<u>a</u> a<b>l</b>nn<u>a</u>si bi-ibr<u>a</u>heema lalla<u>th</u>eena ittabaAAoohu wah<u>atha</u> a<b>l</b>nnabiyyu wa<b>a</b>lla<u>th</u>eena <u>a</u>manoo wa<b>A</b>ll<u>a</u>hu waliyyu almu/mineen<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>68.  Without doubt, among men,<br> The nearest of kin to Abraham,<br> Are those who follow him,<br> As are also this Apostle<br> And those who believe:<br> And God is the Protector<br> Of those who have faith.</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_069.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>69.  Waddat <u>ta</u>-ifatun min ahli alkit<u>a</u>bi law yu<u>d</u>illoonakum wam<u>a</u> yu<u>d</u>illoona ill<u>a</u> anfusahum wam<u>a</u> yashAAuroon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>69.  It is the wish of a section<br> Of the People of the Book<br> To lead you astray.<br> But they shall lead astray<br> (Not you), but themselves,<br> And they do not perceive!</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_070.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>70.  Y<u>a</u> ahla alkit<u>a</u>bi lima takfuroona bi-<u>a</u>y<u>a</u>ti All<u>a</u>hi waantum tashhadoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>70.  Ye People of the Book!<br> Why reject ye<br>The Signs of God,<br> Of which ye are<br> (Yourselves) witnesses?</p> <hr>
 <p align="RIGHT"><img src="../gif/003/003_071.gif"></p> 
 <p><a></a>71.  Y<u>a</u> ahla alkit<u>a</u>bi lima talbisoona al<u>h</u>aqqa bi<b>a</b>lb<u>at</u>ili wataktumoona al<u>h</u>aqqa waantum taAAlamoon<b>a</b>   </p>
 <p>71.  Ye People of the Book!<br> Why do ye clothe<br> Truth with falsehood,<br> And conceal the Truth,<br> While ye have knowledge?</p> <div class="filenav">
