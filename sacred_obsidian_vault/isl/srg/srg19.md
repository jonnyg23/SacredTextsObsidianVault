 <p><a name="page_92"><font size="1" color="green">p. 92</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">EPILOGUE</h3>  
 <p>THIS bouquet of scented blossoms<br> I have plucked from that garden,<br> And have called it "The Secret Rose Garden."<br> In it are blooming<br> Roses of the mysteries of the heart<br> Untold before;<br> In it the tongues of the lilies are all singing,<br> And the eyes of the narcissus behold all, far and near.<br> Gaze on each one of these with your heart's eyes<br> Till your doubts melt away.<br> You will see tradition, earthly and mystical truths,<br> All arranged clearly in knowledge of detail.<br> Do not seek with cold eyes to find blemishes,<br> Or the roses will turn to thorns as you gaze.<br> Ingratitude is a sign of ignorance,<br> For those who know the truth are thankful.<br> When you remember me, breathe "Mercy be upon him."<br> I am ending with my own name,<br> "O Allah, grant me a 'Lauded' end." <a name="fr_6"></a><a href="#fn_6"><font size="1">1</font></a></p>
