 <p>
 Sa'd ud Din Mahmud Shabistari was born in Persia, in Shabistar, near Tabriz,
 about 1250 CE.
 His best known work, <cite>The Secret Rose Garden</cite> was written as a reply
 to questions by a Sufi doctor of Herat.
 This set of verses uses the rich Sufi allegorical language to explore
 the path to God.
 </p>
