 <p><a name="page_76"><font size="1" color="green">p. 76</font></a></p>  <h1 align="center">PART XI</h1>  <h2 align="center">THE SELF</h2>  <h3 align="center">THE GAMBLE OF THE SELF</h3>  
 <p>REAL prayer can only be yours<br> When you have staked and gambled yourself away<br> And your essence is pure.<br> Then "a joy of the eyes" are your prayers<br> And no separation remains,<br> For knower and known are one and the same.</p>  <h3 align="center">TRANSCEND SELF</h3>  
 <p>RISE above time and space,<br> Pass by the world, and be to yourself your own world.</p>  
 <p><a name="page_77"><font size="1" color="green">p. 77</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">SELFLESSNESS</h3>  
 <p>IN the empty heart, void of self<br> Can be heard the echoing cry,<br> "I am the Truth."<br> Thus is man one with the Eternal,<br> Travelling, travel and traveller have become one.</p>
