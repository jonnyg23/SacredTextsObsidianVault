 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 4:564</h3><p><i>Narrated Ibn 'Umar:</i></p>
 Allah's Apostle said, "Do not enter the ruined dwellings of those who 
 were unjust to themselves unless (you enter) weeping, lest you should 
 suffer the same punishment as was inflicted upon them."
