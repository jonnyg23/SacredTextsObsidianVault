 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 4:330</h3><p><i>Narrated 'Amr bin Al-Harith:</i></p>
 The Prophet did not leave anything (after his death) except his arms, 
 a white mule, and a (piece of) land which he had given as Sadaqa.
