 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 4:530</h3><p><i>Narrated Nafi:</i></p>
 Ibn 'Umar used to kill snakes but when Abu Lubaba informed him that 
 the Prophet had forbidden the killing of snakes living in houses, he 
 gave up killing them.
