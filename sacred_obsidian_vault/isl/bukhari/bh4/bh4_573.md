 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 4:570</h3><p><i>Narrated Ibn Abbas:</i></p>
 The Prophet entered the Ka'ba and found in it the pictures of 
 (Prophet) Abraham and Mary. On that he said' "What is the matter with 
 them ( i.e. Quraish)? They have already heard that angels do not enter
 a house in which there are pictures; yet this is the picture of 
 Abraham. And why is he depicted as practicing divination by arrows?"
