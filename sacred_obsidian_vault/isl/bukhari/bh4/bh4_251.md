 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 4:249</h3><p><i>Narrated Abu Bashir Al-Ansari:</i></p>
 That he was in the company of Allah's Apostle on some of his journeys.
 (The sub-narrator 'Abdullah adds, "I think that Abu Bashir also said, 
 'And the people were at their sleeping places.") Allah's Apostle sent 
 a messenger ordering: "There shall not remain any necklace of string 
 or any other kind of necklace round the necks of camels except it is 
 cut off."
