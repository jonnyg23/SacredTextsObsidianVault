 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 4:542</h3><p><i>Narrated Sufyan bin Abi Zuhair Ash-Shani:</i></p>
 That he heard Allah's Apostle saying, "If somebody keeps a dog that is
 neither used for farm work nor for guarding the livestock, he will 
 lose one Qirat (of the reward) of his good deeds everyday."
