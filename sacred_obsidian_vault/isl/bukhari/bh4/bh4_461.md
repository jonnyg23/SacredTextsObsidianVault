 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 4:457</h3><p><i>Narrated Aisha:</i></p>
 Whoever claimed that (the Prophet) Muhammad saw his Lord, is 
 committing a great fault, for he only saw Gabriel in his genuine shape
 in which he was created covering the whole horizon.
