 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 4:482</h3><p><i>Narrated Abu Huraira:</i></p>
 Allah's Apostle said, "The (Hell) Fire complained to its Lord saying, 
 'O my Lord! My different parts eat up each other.' So, He allowed it 
 to take two breaths, one in the winter and the other in summer, and 
 this is the reason for the severe heat and the bitter cold you find 
 (in weather)."
