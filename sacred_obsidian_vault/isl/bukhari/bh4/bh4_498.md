 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 4:494</h3><p><i>Narrated Ibn Umar:</i></p>
 Allah's Apostle said, "When the (upper) edge of the sun appears (in 
 the morning), don't perform a prayer till the sun appears in full, and
 when the lower edge of the sun sets, don't perform a prayer till it 
 sets completely. And you should not seek to pray at sunrise or sunset 
 for the sun rises between two sides of the head of the devil (or 
 Satan)."
