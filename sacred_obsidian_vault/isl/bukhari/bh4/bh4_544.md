 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 4:541</h3><p><i>Narrated Abu Huraira:</i></p>
 Allah's Apostle said, "If somebody keeps a dog, he loses one Qirat (of
 the reward) of his good deeds everyday, except if he keeps it for the 
 purpose of agriculture or for the protection of livestock. "
