 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 4:625</h3><p><i>Narrated Ibn 'Abbas:</i></p>
 The Prophet said, "No slave (of Allah) should say that I am better 
 than Yunus bin Matta." So the Prophet mentioned his father's name with
 his name.
