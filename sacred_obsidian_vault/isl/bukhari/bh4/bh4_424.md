 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 4:420</h3><p><i>Narrated Said bin Zaid bin Amr bin Nufail:</i></p>
 That Arwa sued him before Marwan for a right, which she claimed, he 
 had deprived her of. On that Said said, "How should I deprive her of 
 her right? I testify that I heard Allah's Apostle saying, 'If anyone 
 takes a span of land unjustly, his neck will be encircled with it down
 seven earths on the Day of Resurrection."
