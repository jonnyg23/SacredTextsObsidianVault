 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 4:431</h3><p><i>Narrated Abu Huraira:</i></p>
 The Prophet said, "If Allah loves a person, He calls Gabriel saying, 
 'Allah loves so and-so; O Gabriel! Love him.' Gabriel would love him 
 and make an announcement amongst the inhabitants of the Heaven. 'Allah
 loves so-and-so, therefore you should love him also,' and so all the 
 inhabitants of the Heaven would love him, and then he is granted the 
 pleasure of the people on the earth."
