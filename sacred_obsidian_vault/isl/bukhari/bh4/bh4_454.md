 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 4:450</h3><p><i>Narrated Salim's father:</i></p>
 Once Gabriel promised the Prophet (that he would visit him, but 
 Gabriel did not come) and later on he said, "We, angels, do not enter 
 a house which contains a picture or a dog."
