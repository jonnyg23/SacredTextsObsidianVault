 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 4:605</h3><p><i>Narrated 'Aisha:</i></p>
 The Prophet returned to Khadija while his heart was beating rapidly. 
 She took him to Waraqa bin Naufal who was a Christian convert and used
 to read the Gospels in Arabic Waraqa asked (the Prophet), "What do you
 see?" When he told him, Waraqa said, "That is the same angel whom 
 Allah sent to the Prophet) Moses. Should I live till you receive the 
 Divine Message, I will support you strongly."
