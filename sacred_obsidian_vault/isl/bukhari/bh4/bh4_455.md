 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 4:451</h3><p><i>Narrated Abu Huraira:</i></p>
 Allah's Apostle said, "When the Imam, during the prayer, says, "Allah 
 hears him who praises Him', say: 'O Allah! Our Lord! All the praises 
 are for You/, for if the saying of anyone of you coincides with the 
 saying of the angels, his past sins will be forgiven."
