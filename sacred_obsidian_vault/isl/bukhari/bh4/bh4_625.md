 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 4:622</h3><p><i>Narrated Ibn Abbas:</i></p>
 The Prophet once came to us and said, "All the nations were displayed 
 in front of me, and I saw a large multitude of people covering the 
 horizon. Somebody said, 'This is Moses and his followers.' "
