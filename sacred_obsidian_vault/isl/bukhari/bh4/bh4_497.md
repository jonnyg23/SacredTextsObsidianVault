 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 4:493</h3><p><i>Narrated Ibn 'Abbas:</i></p>
 The Prophet said, "If anyone of you, when having sexual relation with 
 his wife, say: 'In the name of Allah. O Allah! Protect us from Satan 
 and prevent Satan from approaching our offspring you are going to give
 us,' and if he begets a child (as a result of that relation) Satan 
 will not harm it."
