 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 4:654</h3><p><i>Narrated 'Umar:</i></p>
 I heard the Prophet saying, "Do not exaggerate in praising me as the 
 Christians praised the son of Mary, for I am only a Slave. So, call me
 the Slave of Allah and His Apostle."
