 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 4:653</h3><p><i>Narrated Abu Huraira:</i></p>
 The Prophet said, "Jesus, seeing a man stealing, asked him, 'Did you 
 steal?, He said, 'No, by Allah, except Whom there is None who has the 
 right to be worshipped' Jesus said, 'I believe in Allah and suspect my
 eyes."
