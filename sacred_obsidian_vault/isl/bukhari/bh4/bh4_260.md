 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 4:258</h3><p><i>Narrated Ibn 'Umar:</i></p>
 During some of the Ghazawat of Allah's Apostle a woman was found 
 killed, so Allah's Apostle forbade the killing of women and children.
