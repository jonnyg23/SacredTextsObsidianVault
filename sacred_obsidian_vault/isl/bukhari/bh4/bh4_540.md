 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 4:537</h3><p><i>Narrated Abu Huraira:</i></p>
 The Prophet said "If a house fly falls in the drink of anyone of you, 
 he should dip it (in the drink), for one of its wings has a disease 
 and the other has the cure for the disease."
