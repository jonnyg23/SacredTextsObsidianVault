 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 4:492</h3><p><i>Narrated 'Abdullah:</i></p>
 It was mentioned before the Prophet that there was a man who slept the
 night till morning (after sunrise). The Prophet said, "He is a man in 
 whose ears (or ear) Satan had urinated."
