 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 4:341</h3><p><i>Narrated Anas bin Malik:</i></p>
 When the cup of Allah's Apostle got broken, he fixed it with a silver 
 wire at the crack. (The sub-narrator, 'Asim said, "I saw the cup and 
 drank (water) in it.")
