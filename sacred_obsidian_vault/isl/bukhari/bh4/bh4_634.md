 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 4:631</h3><p><i>Narrated Abdullah bin Amr:</i></p>
 Allah's Apostle said to me, "The most beloved fasting to Allah was the
 fasting of (the Prophet) David who used to fast on alternate days. And
 the most beloved prayer to Allah was the prayer of David who used to 
 sleep for (the first) half of the night and pray for 1/3 of it and 
 (again) sleep for a sixth of it."
