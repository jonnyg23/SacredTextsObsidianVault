 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 4:58</h3><p><i>Narrated Jundab bin Sufyan:</i></p>
 In one of the holy Battles a finger of Allah's Apostle (got wounded 
 and) bled. He said, "You are just a finger that bled, and what you got
 is in Allah's Cause."
