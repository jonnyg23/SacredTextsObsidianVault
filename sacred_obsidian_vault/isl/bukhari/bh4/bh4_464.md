 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 4:460</h3><p><i>Narrated Abu Huraira:</i></p>
 Allah's Apostle said, "If a husband calls his wife to his bed (i.e. to
 have sexual relation) and she refuses and causes him to sleep in 
 anger, the angels will curse her till morning."
