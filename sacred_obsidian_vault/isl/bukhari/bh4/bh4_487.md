 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 4:483</h3><p><i>Narrated Abu Jamra Ad-Dabi:</i></p>
 I used to sit with Ibn 'Abbas in Mecca. Once I had a fever and he said
 (to me), "Cool your fever with Zam-zam water, for Allah's Apostle 
 said: 'It, (the Fever) is from the heat of the (Hell) Fire; so, cool 
 it with water (or Zam-zam water)."
