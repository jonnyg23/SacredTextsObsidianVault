 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 4:470</h3><p><i>Narrated Sahl bin Sad:</i></p>
 The Prophet said, "Verily! 70,000 or 700,000 of my followers will 
 enter Paradise altogether; so that the first and the last amongst them
 will enter at the same time, and their faces will be glittering like 
 the bright full moon."
