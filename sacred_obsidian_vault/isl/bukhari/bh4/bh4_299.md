 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 4:295</h3><p><i>Narrated Ibn 'Abbas:</i></p>
 A man came to the Prophet and said, "O Allah's Apostle! I have 
 enlisted in the army for such-and-such Ghazwa, and my wife is leaving 
 for Hajj." Allah's Apostle said, "Go back and perform Hajj with your 
 wife."
