 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 4:443</h3><p><i>Narrated Ibn 'Abbas:</i></p>
 Allah's Apostle was the most generous of all the people, and he used 
 to be more generous in the month of Ramadan when Gabriel used to meet 
 him. Gabriel used to meet him every night in Ramadan to study the Holy
 Quran carefully together. Allah's Apostle used to become more generous
 than the fast wind when he met Gabriel
