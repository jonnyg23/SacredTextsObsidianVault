 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 4:648</h3><p><i>Narrated Ibn Umar:</i></p>
 The Prophet said, "I saw Moses, Jesus and Abraham (on the night of my 
 Ascension to the heavens). Jesus was of red complexion, curly hair and
 a broad chest. Moses was of brown complexion, straight hair and tall 
 stature as if he was from the people of Az-Zutt."
