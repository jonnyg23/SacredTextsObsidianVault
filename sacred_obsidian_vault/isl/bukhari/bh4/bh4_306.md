 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 4:302</h3><p><i>Narrated Nafi:</i></p>
 Once a slave of Ibn 'Umar fled and joined the Byzantine. Khalid bin 
 Al-Walid got him back and returned him to 'Abdullah (bin 'Umar). Once 
 a horse of Ibn 'Umar also ran away and followed the Byzantines, and he
 (i.e. Khalid) got it back and returned it to 'Abdullah.
