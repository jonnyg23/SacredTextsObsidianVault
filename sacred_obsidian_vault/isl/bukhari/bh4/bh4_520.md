 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 4:516</h3><p><i>Narrated Abu Huraira:</i></p>
 The Prophet said, "If anyone of you rouses from sleep and performs the
 ablution, he should wash his nose by putting water in it and then 
 blowing it out thrice, because Satan has stayed in the upper part of 
 his nose all the night."
