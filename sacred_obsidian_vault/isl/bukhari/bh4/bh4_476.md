 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 4:472</h3><p><i>Narrated Al-Bara bin Azib:</i></p>
 Allah's Apostle was given a silken garment, and its beauty and 
 delicacy astonished the people. On that, Allah's Apostle said, "No 
 doubt, the handkerchiefs of Sad bin Muadh in Paradise are better than 
 this."
