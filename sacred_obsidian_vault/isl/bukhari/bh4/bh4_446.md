 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 4:442</h3><p><i>Narrated Ibn Abbas:</i></p>
 Allah's Apostle said, "Gabriel read the Qur'an to me in one way (i.e. 
 dialect) and I continued asking him to read it in different ways till 
 he read it in seven different ways."
