 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 1:775</h3><p><i>Narrated Al-Bara' bin 'Azib:</i></p>
 (and he was not a liar) We used to pray behind the Prophet and when he
 said, "Sami' a-l-lahu Liman hamida", none of us would bend his back 
 (to go for prostration) till the Prophet had placed his, forehead on 
 the ground.
