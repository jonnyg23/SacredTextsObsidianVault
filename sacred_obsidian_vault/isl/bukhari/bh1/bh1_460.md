 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 1:458</h3><p><i>Narrated Abu Huraira:</i></p>
 Allah's Apostle sent some horse men to Najd and they brought a man 
 called Thumama bin Uthal from Bani Hanifa. They fastened him to one of
 the pillars of the mosque.
