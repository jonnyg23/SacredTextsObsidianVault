 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 1:237</h3><p><i>Narrated Maimuna:</i></p>
 The Prophet was asked regarding ghee in which a mouse had fallen. He 
 said, "Take out the mouse and throw away the ghee around it (and use 
 the rest.)"
