 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 1:523</h3><p><i>Narrated Anas bin Malik:</i></p>
 We used to pray the Asr prayer and after that if someone happened to 
 go to the tribe of Bani Amr bin Auf, he would find them still praying 
 the Asr (prayer).
