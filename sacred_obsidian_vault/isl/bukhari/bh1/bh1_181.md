 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 1:181</h3><p><i>Narrated Usama bin Zaid:</i></p>
 "When Allah's Apostle departed from 'Arafat, he turned towards a 
 mountain pass where he answered the call of nature. (After he had 
 finished) I poured water and he performed ablution and then I said to 
 him, "O Allah's Apostle! Will you offer the prayer?" He replied, "The 
 Musalla (place of the prayer) is ahead of you (in Al-Muzdalifa)."
