 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 1:23</h3><p><i>Narrated 'Abdullah (bin 'Umar):</i></p>
 Once Allah's Apostle passed by an Ansari (man) who was admonishing to 
 his brother regarding Haya'. On that Allah's Apostle said, "Leave him 
 as Haya' is a part of faith." (See Hadith No. 8)
