 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 1:627</h3><p><i>Narrated Malik bin Huwairith:</i></p>
 Prophet said (to two persons), "Whenever the prayer time becomes due, 
 you should pronounce Adhan and then Iqama and the older of you should 
 lead the prayer."
