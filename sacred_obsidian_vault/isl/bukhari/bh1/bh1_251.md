 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 1:251</h3><p><i>Narrated Abu Salama:</i></p>
 'Aisha's brother and I went to 'Aisha and he asked her about the bath 
 of the Prophet. She brought a pot containing about a Sa' of water and 
 took a bath and poured it over her head and at what time there was a 
 screen between her and us.
