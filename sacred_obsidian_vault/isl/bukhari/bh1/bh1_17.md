 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 1:18</h3><p><i>Narrated Abu Said Al-Khudri:</i></p>
 Allah's Apostle said, "A time will come that the best property of a 
 Muslim will be sheep which he will take on the top of mountains and 
 the places of rainfall (valleys) so as to flee with his religion from 
 afflictions."
