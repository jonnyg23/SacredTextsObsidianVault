 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 1:675</h3><p><i>Narrated 'Abdullah bin 'Abi Qatada:</i></p>
 My father said, "The Prophet said, 'When I stand for prayer, I intend 
 to prolong it but on hearing the cries of a child, I cut it short, as 
 I dislike to trouble the child's mother.' "
