 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 1:284</h3><p><i>Narrated Abu Salama :</i></p>
 I asked 'Aisha "Did the Prophet use to sleep while he was Junub?" She 
 replied, "Yes, but he used to perform ablution (before going to bed).
