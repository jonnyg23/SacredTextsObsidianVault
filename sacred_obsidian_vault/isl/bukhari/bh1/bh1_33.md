 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 1:34</h3><p><i>Narrated Abu Huraira:</i></p>
 Allah's Apostle said, "Whoever establishes the prayers on the night of
 Qadr out of sincere faith and hoping to attain Allah's rewards (not to
 show off) then all his past sins will be forgiven."
