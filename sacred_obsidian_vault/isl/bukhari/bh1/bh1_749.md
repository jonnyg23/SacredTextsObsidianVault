 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 1:747</h3><p><i>Narrated Abu Huraira:</i></p>
 The Prophet said, "Say Amin" when the Imam says it and if the Amin of 
 any one of you coincides with that of the angels then all his past 
 sins will be forgiven." Ibn Shihab said, "Allah's Apostle used to Say 
 "Amin."
