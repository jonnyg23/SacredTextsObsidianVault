 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 1:274</h3><p><i>Narrated Abu Huraira:</i></p>
 Once the call (Iqama) for the prayer was announced and the rows were 
 straightened. Allah's Apostle came out; and when he stood up at his 
 Musalla, he remembered that he was Junub. Then he ordered us to stay 
 at our places and went to take a bath and then returned with water 
 dropping from his head. He said, "Allahu-Akbar", and we all offered 
 the prayer with him.
