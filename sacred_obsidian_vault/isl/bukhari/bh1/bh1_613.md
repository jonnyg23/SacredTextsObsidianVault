 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 1:611</h3><p><i>Narrated 'Abdullah bin Abi:</i></p>
 Qatada, My father said, "Allah's Apostle said, 'If the Iqama is 
 pronounced, then do not stand for the prayer till you see me (in front
 of you) and do it calmly.' "
