 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 1:495</h3><p><i>Narrated Abu Qatada Al-Ansari:</i></p>
 Allah's Apostle was praying and he was carrying Umama the daughters of
 Zainab, the daughter of Allah's Apostle and she was the daughter of 
 'As bin Rabi'a bin 'AbduShams. When he prostrated, he put her down and
 when he stood, he carried her (on his neck).
