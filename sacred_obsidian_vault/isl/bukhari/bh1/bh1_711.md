 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 1:709</h3><p><i>Narrated Anas bin Malik:</i></p>
 The Prophet said, "Perform the bowing and the prostrations properly. 
 By Allah, I see you from behind me (or from behind my back) when you 
 bow or prostrate."
