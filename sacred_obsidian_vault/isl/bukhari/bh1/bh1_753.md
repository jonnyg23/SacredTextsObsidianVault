 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 1:751</h3><p><i>Narrated Imran bin Husain:</i></p>
 I offered the prayer with 'Ali in Basra and he made us remember the 
 prayer which we used to pray with Allah's Apostle. 'Ali said Takbir on
 each rising and bowing.
