 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 1:812</h3><p><i>Narrated Ibn 'Umar:</i></p>
 During the holy battle of Khaibar the Prophet said, "Whoever ate from 
 this plant (i.e. garlic) should not enter our mosque."
