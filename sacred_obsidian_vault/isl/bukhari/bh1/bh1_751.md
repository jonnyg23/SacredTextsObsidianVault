 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 1:749</h3><p><i>Narrated Abu Huraira:</i></p>
 Allah's Apostle said, "Say Amen' when the Imam says "Ghair-il-maghdubi
 'alaihim wala-ddal-lin; not the path of those who earn Your Anger 
 (such as Jews) nor of those who go astray (such as Christians); all 
 the past sins of the person whose saying (of Amin) coincides with that
 of the angels, will be forgiven.
