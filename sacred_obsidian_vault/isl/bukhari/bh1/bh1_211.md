 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 1:211</h3><p><i>Narrated 'Aisha:</i></p>
 Allah's Apostle said, "If anyone of you feels drowsy while praying he 
 should go to bed (sleep) till his slumber is over because in praying 
 while drowsy one does not know whether one is asking for forgiveness 
 or for a bad thing for oneself."
