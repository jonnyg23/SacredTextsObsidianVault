 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 1:592</h3><p><i>Narrated Hafsa:</i></p>
 When the Muadh-dhin pronounced the Adhan for Fajr prayer and the dawn 
 became evident the Prophet ordered a two Rakat light prayer (Sunna) 
 before the Iqama of the compulsory (congregational) prayer.
