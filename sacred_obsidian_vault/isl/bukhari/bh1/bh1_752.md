 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 1:750</h3><p><i>Narrated Abu Bakra:</i></p>
 I reached the Prophet in the mosque while he was bowing in prayer and 
 I too bowed before joining the row mentioned it to the Prophet and he 
 said to me, "May Allah increase your love for the good. But do not 
 repeat it again (bowing in that way)."
