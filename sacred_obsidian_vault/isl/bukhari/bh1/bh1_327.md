 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 1:327</h3><p><i>Narrated 'Aisha:</i></p>
 The Prophet said to me, "Give up the prayer when your menses begin and
 when it has finished, wash the blood off your body (take a bath) and 
 start praying."
