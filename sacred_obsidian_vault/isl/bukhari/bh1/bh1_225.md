 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 1:225</h3><p><i>Narrated Hudhaifa':</i></p>
 The Prophet and I walked till we reached the dumps of some people. He 
 stood, as any one of you stands, behind a wall and urinated. I went 
 away, but he beckoned me to come. So I approached him and stood near 
 his back till he finished.
