 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 1:663</h3><p><i>Narrated Abu Huraira:</i></p>
 Allah's Apostle said, "If the Imam leads the prayer correctly then he 
 and you will receive the rewards but if he makes a mistake (in the 
 prayer) then you will receive the reward for the prayer and the sin 
 will be his."
