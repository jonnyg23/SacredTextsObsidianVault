 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 1:623</h3><p><i>Narrated Abu Musa:</i></p>
 The Prophet said, "The people who get tremendous reward for the prayer
 are those who are farthest away (from the mosque) and then those who 
 are next farthest and so on. Similarly one who waits to pray with the 
 Imam has greater reward than one who prays and goes to bed. "
