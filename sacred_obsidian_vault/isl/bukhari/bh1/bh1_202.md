 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 1:202</h3><p><i>Narrated Al-Mughlra bin Shu'ba:</i></p>
 Once Allah's Apostle went out to answer the call of nature and I 
 followed him with a tumbler containing water, and when he finished, I 
 poured water and he performed ablution and passed wet hands over his 
 Khuffs.
