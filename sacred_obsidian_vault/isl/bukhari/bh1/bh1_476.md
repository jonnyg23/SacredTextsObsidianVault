 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 1:474</h3><p><i>Narrated 'Aun bin Abi Juhaifa:</i></p>
 I heard my father saying, "The Prophet led us, and prayed a two-Rak'at
 Zuhr prayer and then a two-Rak'at 'Asr prayer at Al-Batha' with an 
 'Anza (planted) in front of him (as a Sutra) while women and donkeys 
 were passing in front of him (beyond that 'Anza)."
