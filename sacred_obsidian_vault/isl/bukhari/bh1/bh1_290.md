 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 1:290</h3><p><i>Narrated Abu Huraira:</i></p>
 The Prophet said, "When a man sits in between the four parts of a 
 woman and did the sexual intercourse with her, bath becomes 
 compulsory."
