 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 1:796</h3><p><i>Narrated Abu Bakr As-Siddiq:</i></p>
 I asked Allah's Apostle to teach me an invocation so that I may invoke
 Allah with it in my prayer. He told me to say, "Allahumma inni zalumtu
 nafsi zulman kathiran, Wala yaghfirudhdhunuba illa anta faghfirli 
 maghfiratan min 'Indika, war-hamni innaka antal-ghafururrahim (O 
 Allah! I have done great injustice to myself and none except You 
 forgives sins, so please forgive me and be Merciful to me as You are 
 the Forgiver, the Merciful)."
