 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 1:654</h3><p><i>Narrated Itban bin Malik Al-Ansari:</i></p>
 The Prophet (came to my house and) asked permission for entering and I
 allowed him. He asked, "Where do you like me to pray in your house?" I
 pointed to a place which I liked. He stood up for prayer and we 
 aligned behind him and he finished the prayer with Taslim and we did 
 the same.
