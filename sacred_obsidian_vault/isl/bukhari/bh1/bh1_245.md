 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 1:245</h3><p><i>Narrated Abu Burda:</i></p>
 My father said, "I came to the Prophet and saw him carrying a Siwak in
 his hand and cleansing his teeth, saying, 'U' U'," as if he was 
 retching while the Siwak was in his mouth."
