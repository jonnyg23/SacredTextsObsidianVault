 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 1:615</h3><p><i>Narrated Anas:</i></p>
 Once the Iqama was pronounced and the Prophet was talking to a man (in
 a low voice) in a corner of the mosque and he did not lead the prayer 
 till (some of) the people had slept (dozed in a sitting posture) .
