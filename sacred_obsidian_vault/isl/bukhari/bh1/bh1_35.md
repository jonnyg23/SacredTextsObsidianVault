 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 1:36</h3><p><i>Narrated Abu Huraira:</i></p>
 Allah's Apostle said: "Whoever establishes prayers during the nights 
 of Ramadan faithfully out of sincere faith and hoping to attain 
 Allah's rewards (not for showing off), all his past sins will be 
 forgiven."
