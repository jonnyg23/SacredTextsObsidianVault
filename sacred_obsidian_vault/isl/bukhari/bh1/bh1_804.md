 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 1:802</h3><p><i>Narrated Abu Ma'bad:</i></p>
 (the freed slave of Ibn 'Abbas) Ibn 'Abbas told me, "In the lifetime 
 of the Prophet it was the custom to celebrate Allah's praises aloud 
 after the compulsory congregational prayers." Ibn 'Abbas further said,
 "When I heard the Dhikr, I would learn that the compulsory 
 congregational prayer had ended."
