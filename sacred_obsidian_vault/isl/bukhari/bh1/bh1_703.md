 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 1:701</h3><p><i>Narrated Abu Huraira:</i></p>
 The Prophet said, "The Imam is to be followed. Say the Takbir when he 
 says it; bow if he bows; if he says 'Sami a-l-lahu Liman hamida', say,
 ' Rabbana wa-laka-l-hamd', prostrate if he prostrates and pray sitting
 altogether if he prays sitting."
