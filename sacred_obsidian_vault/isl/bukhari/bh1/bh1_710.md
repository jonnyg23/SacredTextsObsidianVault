 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 1:708</h3><p><i>Narrated Abu Huraira:</i></p>
 Allah's Apostle said, "You see me facing the Qibla; but, by Allah, 
 nothing is hidden from me regarding your bowing and submissiveness and
 I see you from behind my back."
