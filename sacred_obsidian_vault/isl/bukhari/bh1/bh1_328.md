 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 1:328</h3><p><i>Narrated Samura bin Jundab:</i></p>
 The Prophet offered the funeral prayer for the dead body of a woman 
 who died of (during) delivery (i.e. child birth) and he stood by the 
 middle of her body.
