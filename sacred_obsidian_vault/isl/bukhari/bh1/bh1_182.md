 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 1:182</h3><p><i>Narrated Al-Mughira bin Shu'ba:</i></p>
 I was in the company of Allah's Apostle on one of the journeys and he 
 went out to answer the call of nature (and after he finished) I poured
 water and he performed ablution; he washed his face, forearms and 
 passed his wet hand over his head and over the two Khuff, (leather 
 socks).
