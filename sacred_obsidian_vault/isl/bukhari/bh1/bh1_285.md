 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 1:285</h3><p><i>Narrated 'Umar bin Al-Khattab:</i></p>
 I asked Allah's Apostle "Can any one of us sleep while he is Junub?" 
 He replied, "Yes, if he performs ablution, he can sleep while he is 
 Junub."
