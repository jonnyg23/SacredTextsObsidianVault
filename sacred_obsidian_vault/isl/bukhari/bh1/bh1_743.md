 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 1:741</h3><p><i>Narrated Ibn 'Abbas:</i></p>
 The Prophet recited aloud in the prayers in which he was ordered to do
 so and quietly in the prayers in which he was ordered to do so. "And 
 your Lord is not forgetful." "Verily there was a good example for you 
 in the ways of the Prophet."
