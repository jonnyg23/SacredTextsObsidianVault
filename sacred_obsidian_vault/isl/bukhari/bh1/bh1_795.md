 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 1:793</h3><p><i>Narrated 'Abdullah bin Malik bin Buhaina:</i></p>
 Once Allah's Apostle led us in the Zuhr prayer and got up (after the 
 prostrations of the second Rak'a) although he should have sat (for the
 Tashah-hud). So at the end of  the prayer, he prostrated twice while 
 sitting (prostrations of Sahu).
