 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 1:491</h3><p><i>Narrated 'Aisha:</i></p>
 The Prophet used to pray while I was sleeping across in his bed in 
 front of him. Whenever he wanted to pray Witr, he would wake me up and
 I would pray Witr.
