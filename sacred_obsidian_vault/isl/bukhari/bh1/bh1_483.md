 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 1:481</h3><p><i>Narrated Yazid bin Al 'Ubaid:</i></p>
 I used to accompany Salama bin Al-Akwa' and he used to pray behind the
 pillar which was near the place where the Quran's were kept I said, "O
 Abu Muslim! I see you always seeking to pray behind this pillar." He 
 replied, "I saw Allah's Apostle always seeking to pray near that 
 pillar."
