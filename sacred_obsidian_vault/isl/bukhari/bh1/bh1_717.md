 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 1:715</h3><p><i>Narrated 'Abdullah bin 'Abbas:</i></p>
 Once solar eclipse occurred during the lifetime of Allah's Apostle. He
 offered the eclipse prayer. His companions asked, "O Allah's Apostle! 
 We saw you trying to take something while standing at your place and 
 then we saw you retreating." The Prophet said, "I was shown Paradise 
 and wanted to have a bunch of fruit from it. Had I taken it, you would
 have eaten from it as long as the world remains."
