 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 1:773</h3><p><i>Narrated Ibn 'Abbas:</i></p>
 The Prophet was ordered (by Allah) to prostrate on seven parts and not
 to tuck up the clothes or hair (while praying). Those parts are: the 
 forehead (along with the tip of nose), both hands, both knees, and 
 (toes of) both feet.
