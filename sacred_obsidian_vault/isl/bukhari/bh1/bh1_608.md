 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 1:606</h3><p><i>Narrated 'Aun bin Abi Juhaifa:</i></p>

 Bilal came and informed him about the prayer and then came out with an
 Anza and planted it in front of Allah's Apostle at Al-Abtah and 
 pronounced the Iqama."
