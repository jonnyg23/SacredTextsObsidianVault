 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 1:354</h3><p><i>Narrated Abu Huraira:</i></p>
 A person asked Allah's Apostle about the offering of the prayer in a 
 single garment. Allah's Apostle replied, "Has every one of you got two
 garments?"
