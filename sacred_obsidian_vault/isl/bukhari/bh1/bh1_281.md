 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 1:281</h3><p><i>Narrated Abu Huraira:</i></p>
 The Prophet came across me in one of the streets of Medina and at that
 time I was Junub. So I slipped away from him and went to take a bath. 
 On my return the Prophet said, "O Abu Huraira! Where have you been?" I
 replied, "I was Junub, so I disliked to sit in your company." The 
 Prophet said, "Subhan Allah! A believer never becomes impure."
