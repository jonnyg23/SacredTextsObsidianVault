 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 1:11</h3><p><i>Narrated 'Abdullah bin 'Amr:</i></p>
 A man asked the Prophet , "What sort of deeds or (what qualities of) 
 Islam are good?" The Prophet replied, 'To feed (the poor) and greet 
 those whom you know and those whom you do not Know (See 
 Hadith No. 27).
