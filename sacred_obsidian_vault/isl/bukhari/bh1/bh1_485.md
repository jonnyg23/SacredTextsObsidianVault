 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 1:483</h3><p><i>Narrated Ibn 'Umar:</i></p>
 The Prophet entered the Ka'ba along with Usama bin Zaid, 'Uthman bin 
 Talha and Bilal and remained there for a long time. When they came 
 out, I was the first man to enter the Ka'ba. I asked Bilal "Where did 
 the Prophet pray?" Bilal replied, "Between the two front Pillars."
