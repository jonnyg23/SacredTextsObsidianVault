 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 1:717</h3><p><i>Narrated Anas bin Malik:</i></p>
 The Prophet said, "What is wrong with those people who look towards 
 the sky during the prayer?" His talk grew stern while delivering this 
 speech and he said, "They should stop (looking towards the sky during 
 the prayer); otherwise their eye-sight would be taken away."
