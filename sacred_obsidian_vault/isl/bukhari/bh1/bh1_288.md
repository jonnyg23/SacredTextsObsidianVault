 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 1:288</h3><p><i>Narrated 'Abdullah bin 'Umar:</i></p>
 Umar bin Al-Khattab told Allah's Apostle, "I became Junub at night." 
 Allah's Apostle replied, "Perform ablution after washing your private 
 parts and then sleep."
