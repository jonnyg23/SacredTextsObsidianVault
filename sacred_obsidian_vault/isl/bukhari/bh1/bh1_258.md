 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 1:258</h3><p><i>Narrated 'Aisha:</i></p>
 Whenever the Prophet took the bath of Janaba (sexual relation or wet 
 dream) he asked for the Hilab or some other scent. He used to take it 
 in his hand, rub it first over the right side of his head and then 
 over the left and then rub the middle of his head with both hands.
