 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 1:196</h3><p><i>Narrated 'Abdullah bin Zaid:</i></p>
 Once Allah's Apostle came to us and we brought out water for him in a 
 brass pot. He performed ablution thus: He washed his face thrice, and 
 his forearms to the elbows twice, then passed his wet hands lightly 
 over the head from front to rear and brought them to front again and 
 washed his feet (up to the ankles).
