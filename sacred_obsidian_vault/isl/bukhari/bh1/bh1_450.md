 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 1:449</h3><p><i>Narrated 'Aisha:</i></p>
 When the verses of Surat "Al-Baqara"' about the usury Riba were 
 revealed, the Prophet went to the mosque and recited them in front of 
 the people and then banned the trade of alcohol.
