 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 2:65</h3><p><i>Narrated Nafi':</i></p>
 Ibn  Umar said something similar to Mujahid's saying: Whenever 
 (Muslims and non-Muslims) stand face to face in battle, the Muslims 
 can pray while standing. Ibn  Umar added, "The Prophet said, 'If the 
 number of the enemy is greater than the Muslims, they can pray while 
 standing or riding (individually).' "
