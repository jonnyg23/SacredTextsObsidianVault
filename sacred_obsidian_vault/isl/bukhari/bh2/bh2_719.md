 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 2:717</h3><p><i>Narrated 'Abdullah bin 'Umar :</i></p>
 Allah's Apostle offered a two-Rakat prayer at Mina. Abu Bakr, 'Umar 
 and 'Uthman, (during the early years of his caliphate) followed the 
 same practice.
