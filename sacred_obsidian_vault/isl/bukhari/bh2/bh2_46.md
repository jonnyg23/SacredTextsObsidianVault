 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 2:47</h3><p><i>Narrated Abu Hummaid As-Sa'idi</i></p>
 One night Allah's Apostle (p.b.u.h) stood up after the prayer and 
 recited "Tashah-hud" and then praised Allah as He deserved and said, 
 "Amma ba'du."
