 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 2:185</h3><p><i>Narrated Ibn 'Umar.</i></p>
 Whenever the Prophet recited the Sura which contained the prostration 
 of recitation he used to prostrate and then, we, too, would prostrate 
 and some of us did not find a place for prostration.
