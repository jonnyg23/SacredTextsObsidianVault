 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 2:31</h3><p><i>Narrated Abu Huraira:</i></p>
 heard Allah's Apostles (p.b.u.h) saying, "If the prayer is started do 
 not run for it but just walk for it calmly and pray whatever you get, 
 and complete whatever is missed. "
