 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 2:282</h3><p><i>Narrated Abu Huraira</i></p>
 Allah's Apostle said, "One prayer in my Mosque is better than one 
 thousand prayers in any other mosque excepting Al-Masjid-AI-Haram."
