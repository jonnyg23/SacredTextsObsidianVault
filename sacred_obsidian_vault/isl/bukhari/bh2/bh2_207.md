 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 2:208</h3><p><i>Narrated Salim bin Abdullah:</i></p>
 Ibn 'Umar said, "Allah's Apostle used to pray the Nawafil on the back 
 of his Mount (carriage) by signs facing any direction." Ibn 'Umar used
 to do the same.
