 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 2:53</h3><p><i>Narrated Jabir:</i></p>
 A man entered the Mosque while the Prophet was delivering the Khutba. 
 The Prophet said to him, "Have you prayed?" The man replied in the 
 negative. The Prophet said, "Pray two Rakat."
