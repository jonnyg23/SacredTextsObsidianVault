 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 2:115</h3><p><i>Narrated Muhammad bin Sirin:</i></p>
 Anas was asked, "Did the Prophet recite Qunut in the Fajr prayer?" 
 Anas replied in the affirmative. He was further asked, "Did he recite 
 Qunut before bowing?" Anas replied, "He recited Qunut after bowing for
 some time (for one month)."
