 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 2:194</h3><p><i>Narrated Abu Huraira:</i></p>
 The Prophet (p.b.u.h) said, "It is not permissible for a woman who 
 believes in Allah and the Last Day to travel for one day and night 
 except with a Mahram."
