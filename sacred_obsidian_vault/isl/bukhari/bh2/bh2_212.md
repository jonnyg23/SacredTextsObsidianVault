 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 2:213</h3><p><i>Narrated Anas bin Malik:</i></p>
 Whenever the Prophet started the journey before noon, he used to delay
 the Zuhr prayer till the time for the 'Asr prayer and then he would 
 dismount and pray them together; and whenever the sun declined before 
 he started the journey he used to offer the Zuhr prayer and then ride 
 (for the journey).
