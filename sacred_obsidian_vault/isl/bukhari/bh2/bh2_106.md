 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 2:107</h3><p><i>Narrated 'Abdullah bin 'Umar :</i></p>
 The Prophet said, "Night prayer is offered as two Rakat followed by 
 two Rakat and so on, and if you want to finish it, pray only one Raka 
 which will be Witr for all the previous Rakat." Al-Qasim said, "Since 
 we attained the age of puberty we have seen some people offering a 
 three-Rakat prayer as Witr and all that is permissible. I hope there 
 will be no harm in it."
