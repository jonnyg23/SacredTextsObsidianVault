 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 2:264</h3><p><i>Narrated Abu Qatada bin Rabi Al-Ansari;</i></p>
 The Prophet said, "If anyone of you enters a Mosque, he should not sit
 until he has offered a two-Rakat prayer."
