 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 2:580</h3><p><i>Narrated Ibn 'Umar:</i></p>
 Allah's Apostle made it incumbent on all the slave or free Muslims, 
 male or female, to pay one Sa' of dates or barley as Zakat-ul-Fitr.
