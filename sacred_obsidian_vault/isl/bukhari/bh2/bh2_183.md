 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 2:184</h3><p><i>Narrated Abu Rafi:</i></p>
 I offered the 'Isha' prayer behind Abu Huraira and he recited 
 Idhas-Sama' Un-Shaqqat, and prostrated. I said, "What is this?" Abu 
 Huraira said, "I prostrated behind Abu-l-Qasim and I will do the same 
 till I meet him."
