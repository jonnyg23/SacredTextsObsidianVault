 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 2:583</h3><p><i>Narrated 'Abdullah bin 'Umar</i></p>
 The Prophet ordered (Muslims) to give one Sa' of dates or one Sa' of 
 barley as Zakat-ul-Fitr. The people rewarded two Mudds of wheat as 
 equal to that.
