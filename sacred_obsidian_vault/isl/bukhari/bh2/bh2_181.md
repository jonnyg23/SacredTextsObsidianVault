 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 2:182</h3><p><i>Narrated Ibn 'Umar,</i></p>
 When the Prophet recited Surat As-Sajda and we were with him, he would
 prostrate and we also would prostrate with him and some of us (because
 of the heavy rush) would not find a place (for our foreheads) to 
 prostrate on.
