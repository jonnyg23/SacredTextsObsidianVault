 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 2:287</h3><p><i>Narrated Abu Huraira:</i></p>
 The Prophet said, "Between my house and my pulpit there is a garden of
 the gardens of Paradise, and my pulpit is on my fountain tank (i.e. 
 Al-Kauthar)."
