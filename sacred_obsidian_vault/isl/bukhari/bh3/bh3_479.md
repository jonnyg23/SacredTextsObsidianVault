 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 3:482</h3><p><i>Narrated Abu Masud Al-Ansari:</i></p>
 Allah's Apostle regarded illegal the price of a dog, the earnings of a
 prostitute, and the charges taken by a soothsayer.
