 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 3:676</h3><p><i>Narrated Jabir bin 'Abdullah:</i></p>
 The Prophet said, "The right of pre-emption is valid in every joint 
 property, but when the land is divided and the way is demarcated, then
 there is no right of pre-emption."
