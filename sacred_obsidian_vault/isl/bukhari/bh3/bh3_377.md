 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 3:380</h3><p><i>Narrated Ibn 'Umar:</i></p>
 Allah's Apostle forbade Muzabana; and Muzabana is the selling of fresh
 dates for dried old dates by measure, and the selling of fresh grapes 
 for dried grapes by measure.
