 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 3:685</h3><p><i>Narrated Anas:</i></p>
 No doubt, the Prophet mortgaged his armor for barley grams. Once I 
 took barley bread with some dissolved fat on it to the Prophet and I 
 heard him saying, "The household of Muhammad did not possess except a 
 Sa (of food grain, barley, etc.) for both the morning and the evening 
 meals although they were nine houses."
