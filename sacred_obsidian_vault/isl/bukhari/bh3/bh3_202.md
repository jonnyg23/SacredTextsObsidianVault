 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 3:205</h3><p><i>Narrated Muhammad bin 'Abbas:</i></p>
 I asked Jabir "Did the Prophet forbid fasting on Fridays?" He replied,
 "Yes." (Other narrators added, "If he intends to fast only that 
 day.")
