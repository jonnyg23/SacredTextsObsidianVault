 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 3:341</h3><p><i>Narrated Salim:</i></p>
 that his father said. "I saw those, who used to buy foodstuff without 
 measuring or weighing in the life time of the Prophet being punished 
 if they sold it before carrying it to their own houses."
