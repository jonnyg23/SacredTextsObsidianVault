 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 3:399</h3><p><i>Narrated 'Abdullah bin 'Umar:</i></p>
 Allah's Apostle forbade the sale of fruits till their benefit is 
 evident. He forbade both the seller and the buyer (such sale).
