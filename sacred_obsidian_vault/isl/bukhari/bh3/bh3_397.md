 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 3:400</h3><p><i>Narrated Anas:</i></p>
 Allah's Apostle forbade the sale of date fruits till they were ripe. 
 Abu 'Abdullah (Al-Bukhari) said, "That means till they were red (can 
 be eaten)."
