 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 3:628</h3><p><i>Narrated Ibn 'Abbas:</i></p>
 The Prophet sent Mu'adh to Yemen and said, "Be afraid, from the curse 
 of the oppressed as there is no screen between his invocation and 
 Allah."
