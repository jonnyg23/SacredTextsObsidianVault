 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 3:583</h3><p><i>Narrated Abu Huraira:</i></p>
 The Prophet said, "If someone leaves some property, it will be for the
 inheritors, and if he leaves some weak offspring, it will be for us to
 support them."
