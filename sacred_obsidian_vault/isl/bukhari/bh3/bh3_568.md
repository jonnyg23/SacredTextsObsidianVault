 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 3:569</h3><p><i>Narrated Rafi 'bin Khadij and Sahl bin Al Hathma:</i></p>
 Allah's Apostle forbade the sale of Muzabana, i.e. selling of fruits 
 for fruits, except in the case of 'Araya; he allowed the owners of 
 'Araya such kind of sale.
