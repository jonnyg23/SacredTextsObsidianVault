 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 3:416</h3><p><i>Narrated Jabir bin Abdullah:</i></p>
 Allah's Apostle decided the validity of pre-emption in every joint 
 undivided property, but if the boundaries were well marked or the ways
 and streets were fixed, then there was no pre-emption.
