 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 3:767</h3><p><i>Narrated Aisha:</i></p>
 I said, "O Allah's Apostle! I have two neighbors; which of them should
 I give a gift to?" The Prophet said, "(Give) to the one whose door is 
 nearer to you."
