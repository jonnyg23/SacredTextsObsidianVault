 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 3:407</h3><p><i>Narrated Ibn 'Umar:</i></p>
 Allah's Apostle forbade Al-Muzabana, i.e. to sell ungathered dates of 
 one's garden for measured dried dates or fresh ungathered grapes for 
 measured dried grapes; or standing crops for measured quantity of 
 foodstuff. He forbade all such bargains.
