 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 3:161</h3><p><i>Narrated Thabit Al-Bunani:</i></p>
 Anas bin Malik was asked whether they disliked the cupping for a 
 fasting person. He replied in the negative and said, "Only if it 
 causes weakness."
