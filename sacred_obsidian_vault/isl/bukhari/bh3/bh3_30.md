 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 3:31</h3><p><i>Narrated Abu Huraira:</i></p>
 The Prophet said, "Traveling is a kind of torture as it prevents one 
 from eating, drinking and sleeping properly. So, when one's needs are 
 fulfilled, one should return quickly to one's family."
