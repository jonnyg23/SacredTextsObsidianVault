 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 3:55</h3><p><i>Narrated Aisha:</i></p>
 Allah's Apostle said, "Five kinds of animals are harmful and could be 
 killed in the Haram (Sanctuary). These are: the crow, the kite, the 
 scorpion, the mouse and the rabid dog."
