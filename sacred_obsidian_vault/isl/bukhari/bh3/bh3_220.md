 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 3:223</h3><p><i>Narrated Abu Musa:</i></p>
 The day of 'Ashura' was considered as 'Id day by the Jews. So the 
 Prophet ordered, "I recommend you (Muslims) to fast on this day."
