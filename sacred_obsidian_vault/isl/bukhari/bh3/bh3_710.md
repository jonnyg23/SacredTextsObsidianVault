 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 3:711</h3><p><i>Narrated Jabir bin 'Abdullah:</i></p>
 A man amongst us declared that his slave would be freed after his 
 death. The Prophet called for that slave and sold him. The slave died 
 the same year.
