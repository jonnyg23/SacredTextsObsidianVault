 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 3:183</h3><p><i>Narrated Abdullah bin Umar:</i></p>
 Allah's Apostle forbade Al-Wisal. The people said (to him), "But you 
 practice it?" He said, "I am not like you, for I am given food and 
 drink by Allah."
