 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 3:461</h3><p><i>Narrated Abu Musa Al-Ashari:</i></p>
 The Prophet said, "The honest treasurer who gives willingly what he is
 ordered to give, is one of the two charitable persons, (the second 
 being the owner)."
