 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 3:630</h3><p><i>Narrated Aisha:</i></p>
 Regarding the explanation of the following verse:-- "If a wife fears 
 Cruelty or desertion On her husband's part." (4.128) A man may dislike
 his wife and intend to divorce her, so she says to him, "I give up my 
 rights, so do not divorce me." The above verse was revealed concerning
 such a case.
