 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 3:376</h3><p><i>Narrated 'Abdullah:</i></p>
 Some people used to buy foodstuff at the head of the market and used 
 to sell it on the spot. Allah's Apostle forbade them to sell it till 
 they brought it to (their) places.
