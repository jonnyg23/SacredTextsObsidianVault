 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 3:722</h3><p><i>Narrated Ibn 'Umar:</i></p>
 Allah's Apostle said, "If a slave is honest and faithful to his master
 and worships his Lord (Allah) in a perfect manner, he will get a 
 double reward."
