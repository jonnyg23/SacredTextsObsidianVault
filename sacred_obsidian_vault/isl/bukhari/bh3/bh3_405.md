 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 3:408</h3><p><i>Narrated Ibn 'Umar:</i></p>
 The Prophet said, "Whoever pollinates date palms and then sells them, 
 the fruits will belong to him unless the buyer stipulates that the 
 fruits should belong to him (and the seller agrees)."
