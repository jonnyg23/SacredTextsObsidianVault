 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 3:429</h3><p><i>Narrated 'Aisha:</i></p>
 When the last verses of Surat-al-Baqara were revealed, the Prophet 
 went out (of his house to the Mosque) and said, "The trade of alcohol 
 has become illegal."
