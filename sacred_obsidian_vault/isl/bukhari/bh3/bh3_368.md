 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 3:371</h3><p><i>Narrated Abu Huraira:</i></p>
 The Prophet forbade the meeting (of caravans) on the way and the 
 selling of goods by an inhabitant of the town on behalf of a desert 
 dweller.
