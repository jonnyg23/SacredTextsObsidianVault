 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 3:566</h3><p><i>Narrated Zaid bin Thabit:</i></p>
 The Prophet permitted selling the dates of the 'Araya for ready dates 
 by estimating the amount of the former (as they are still on the 
 trees).
