 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 3:224</h3><p><i>Narrated Ibn 'Abbas:</i></p>
 I never saw the Prophet seeking to fast on a day more (preferable to 
 him) than this day, the day of 'Ashura', or this month, i.e. the month
 of Ramadan.
