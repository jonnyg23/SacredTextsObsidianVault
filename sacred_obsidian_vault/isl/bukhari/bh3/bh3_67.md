 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 3:68</h3><p><i>Narrated Abdullah:</i></p>
 Allah's Apostle was asked what sort of clothes a Muhrim should wear. 
 He replied, "He should not wear a shirt, turbans, trousers, a hooded 
 cloak, or a dress perfumed with saffron or Wars; and if slippers are 
 not available he can wear Khuffs but he should cut them so that they 
 reach below the ankles.
