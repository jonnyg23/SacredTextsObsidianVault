 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 3:877</h3><p><i>Narrated Abdullah bin Umar:</i></p>
 Allah's Apostle said, "If someone sells pollinated date-palms, their 
 fruits will be for the seller, unless the buyer stipulates the 
 contrary."
