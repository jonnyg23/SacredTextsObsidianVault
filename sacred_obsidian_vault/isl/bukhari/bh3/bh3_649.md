 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 3:650</h3><p><i>Narrated Jabir:</i></p>
 The Prophet entered the Mosque, and I too went there after tying the 
 camel at the pavement of the Mosque. I said (to the Prophet ), "This 
 is your camel." He came out and started examining the camel and said, 
 "Both the camel and its price are for you."
