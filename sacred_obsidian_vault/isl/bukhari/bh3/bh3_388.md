 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 3:391</h3><p><i>Narrated Abu Sa'id Al-Khudri:</i></p>
 Allah's Apostle forbade Muzabana and Muhaqala; and Muzabana means the 
 selling of ripe dates for dates still on the trees.
