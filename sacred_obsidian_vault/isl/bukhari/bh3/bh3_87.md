 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 3:89</h3><p><i>Narrated 'Uqba bin 'Amir:</i></p>
 My sister vowed to go on foot to the Ka'ba, and she asked me to take 
 the verdict of the Prophet about it. So, I did and the Prophet said, 
 "She should walk and also should ride."
