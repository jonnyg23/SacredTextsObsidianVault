 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 3:726</h3><p><i>Narrated 'Abdullah:</i></p>
 The Prophet said, "If a slave serves his Saiyid (i.e. master) 
 sincerely and worships his Lord (Allah) perfectly, he will get a 
 double reward."
