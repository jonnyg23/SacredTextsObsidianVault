 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 3:297</h3><p><i>Narrated Aisha:</i></p>
 When the last Verses of Surat al- Baqara were revealed, the Prophet 
 recited them in the mosque and proclaimed the trade of alcohol as 
 illegal.
