 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 3:395</h3><p><i>Narrated Abu Huraira:</i></p>
 The Prophet allowed the sale of the dates of 'Araya provided they were
 about five Awsuq (singular: Wasaq which means sixty Sa's) or less (in 
 amount).
