 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 3:487</h3><p><i>Narrated Abu Huraira:</i></p>
 The Prophet said, "Procrastination (delay) in paying debts by a 
 wealthy person is injustice. So, if your debt is transferred from your
 debtor to a rich debtor, you should agree."
