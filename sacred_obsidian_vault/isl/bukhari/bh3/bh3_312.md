 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 3:315</h3><p><i>Narrated Anas bin Malik:</i></p>
 Abu Taiba cupped Allah's Apostle so he ordered that he be paid one Sa 
 of dates and ordered his masters to reduce his tax (as he was a slave 
 and had to pay a tax to them).
