 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 3:751</h3><p><i>Narrated Anas bin Malik:</i></p>
 Some meat was brought to the Prophet and it was said that the meat had
 been given in charity to Buraira. He said, "It was Sadaqa for Buraira 
 but a gift for us."
