 <h3 align="CENTER">Hadith 3:597</h3><p><i>Narrated Ibn 'Umar:</i></p>
 A man was often cheated in buying. The Prophet said to him, "When you 
 buy something, say (to the seller), No cheating." The man used to say 
 so thenceforward .
