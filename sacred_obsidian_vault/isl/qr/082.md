 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">The Qur'ân, Rodwell edition [1876]; at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">SURA LXXXII.--THE CLEAVING [XXXI.]</h3>  
 <p align="center">MECCA.--19 Verses</p>  
 <p>In the Name of God, the Compassionate, the Merciful<br> WHEN the Heaven shall CLEAVE asunder,<br> And when the stars shall disperse,<br> And when the seas <a name="fr_77"></a><a href="#fn_77"><font size="1">1</font></a> shall be commingled,<br> And when the graves shall be turned upside down,<br> Each soul shall recognise its earliest and its latest actions.<br> O man! what hath misled thee against thy generous Lord,<br> Who hath created thee and moulded thee and shaped thee aright?<br> In the form which pleased Him hath He fashioned thee.<br> Even so; but ye treat the Judgment as a lie.<br> Yet truly there are guardians over you--<br> Illustrious recorders--<br> Cognisant of your actions.<br> Surely amid delights shall the righteous dwell,<br> But verily the impure in Hell-fire:<br> They shall be burned at it on the day of doom,<br> And they shall not be able to hide themselves from it.<br> Who shall teach thee what the day of doom is?<br> Once more. Who shall teach thee what the day of doom is?<br> It is a day when one soul shall be powerless for another soul: all sovereignty on that day shall be with God.</p>
