 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">The Qur'ân, Rodwell edition [1876]; at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">SURA CI.--THE BLOW [XXIX.]</h3>  
 <p align="center">MECCA.--8 Verses</p>  
 <p>In the Name of God, the Compassionate, the Merciful<br> THE BLOW! what is the Blow?<br> Who shall teach thee what the Blow is?<br> The Day when men shall be like scattered moths,<br> And the mountains shall be like flocks of carded wool,<br> Then as to him whose balances are heavy--his shall be a life that shall please him well:<br> And as to him whose balances are light--his dwelling-place <a name="fr_76"></a><a href="#fn_76"><font size="1">1</font></a> shall be the pit.<br> And who shall teach thee what the pit (El-Hawiya) is?<br> A raging fire!</p>
