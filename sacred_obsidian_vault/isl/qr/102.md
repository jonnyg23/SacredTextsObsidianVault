 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">The Qur'ân, Rodwell edition [1876]; at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">SURA CII.--DESIRE [XV.]</h3>  
 <p align="center">MECCA.--8 Verses</p>  
 <p>In the name of God, the Compassionate, the Merciful<br> THE DESIRE of increasing riches occupieth you,<br> Till ye come to the grave.<br> Nay! but in the end ye shall know<br> Nay! once more,in the end ye shall know your folly.<br> Nay! would that ye knew it with knowledge of certainty!<br> Surely ye shall see hell-fire.<br> Then shall ye surely see it with the eye of certainty;<br> Then shall ye on that day be taken to task concerning pleasures.</p>
