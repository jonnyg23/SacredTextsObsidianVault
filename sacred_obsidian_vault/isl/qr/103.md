 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">The Qur'ân, Rodwell edition [1876]; at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">SURA CIII.--THE AFTERNOON [XXVII.]</h3>  
 <p align="center">MECCA.--3 Verses</p>  
 <p>In the Name of God, the Compassionate, the Merciful<br> I SWEAR by the declining day!<br> Verily, man's lot is cast amid destruction, <a name="fr_71"></a><a href="#fn_71"><font size="1">1</font></a><br> Save those who believe and do the things which be right, and enjoin truth and enjoin stedfastness on each other.</p>
