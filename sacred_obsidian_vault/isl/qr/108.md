 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">The Qur'ân, Rodwell edition [1876]; at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">SURA CVIII.--THE ABUNDANCE [XII.]</h3>  
 <p align="center">MECCA.--3 Verses</p>  
 <p>In the Name of God, the Compassionate, the Merciful<br> TRULY we have given thee an ABUNDANCE;<br> Pray therefore to the Lord, and slay the victims.<br> Verily whose hateth thee shall be childless. <a name="fr_47"></a><a href="#fn_47"><font size="1">1</font></a></p>
