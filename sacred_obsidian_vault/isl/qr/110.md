 <p align="center">MEDINA.--3 Verses</p>  
 <p>In the Name of God, the Compassionate, the Merciful<br> WHEN the HELP of God and the victory arrive,<br> And thou seest men entering the religion of God by troops;<br> Then utter the praise of thy Lord, implore His pardon; for He loveth to turn in mercy.</p>
