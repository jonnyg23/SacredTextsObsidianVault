 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">The Qur'ân, Rodwell edition [1876]; at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">SURA XCIV.--THE OPENING [V.]</h3>  
 <p align="center">MECCA.--8 Verses</p>  
 <p>In the Name of God, the Compassionate, the Merciful<br> HAVE we not OPENED thine heart for thee?<br> And taken off from thee thy burden,<br> Which galled thy back?<br> And have we not raised thy name for thee?<br> Then verily along with trouble cometh ease.<br> Verily along with trouble cometh ease.<br> But when thou art set at liberty, then prosecute thy toil.<br> And seek thy Lord with fervour.</p>
