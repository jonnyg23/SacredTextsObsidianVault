 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>Arabian Wisdom</i>, by John Wortabet, [1913], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center"><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Murder</span></h3>  
 <p>The first thing which shall be taken up in the Day of Judgment is murder.</p>  
 <p>Man is a building made by God, and he who destroys the building of God shall be demolished.</p>  
 <p>Put no man to undeserved death, for God forbids murder.</p>  
 <p>Announce violent death to the murderer, and poverty to the adulterer, though after a season.</p>
