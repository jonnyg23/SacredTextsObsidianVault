 <p>
 This short collection of Islamic wisdom literature was
 originally published in the
 <a href="../../woe/index.htm">Wisdom of the East</a> Series.
 It features gems from the Quran, the Hadith, and traditional
 proverbs and aphorisms.
 This is principally meant as an enjoyable read; unfortunately
 the sayings are unattributed, which makes it less useful as a scholarly
 work.
 </p>
