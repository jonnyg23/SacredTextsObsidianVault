 <p>In travelling you will find health and profit.</p>  
 <p>If water stagnates long it becomes foul.</p>  
 <p>A roaming dog is better than a couching lion.</p>  
 <p>During a journey a man's character is weighed and revealed.</p>  
 <p>The day on which a journey is begun is half the journey done.</p>
