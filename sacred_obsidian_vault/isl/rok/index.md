 <p>
 This short entry in the <cite>Wisdom of the East</cite> series
 includes a short introduction to Islam, and a set of
 topical quotations from the <a href="../sbe06/index.htm">Palmer</a>
 translation of the Qur'an.
 The author was an English academic sympathetic to Islam who
 was writing at the turn of the 20th century, and this should
 be kept in mind while reading this.
 </p>
