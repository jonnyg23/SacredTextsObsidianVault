 <p><a name="page_vii"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. vii</font></a></p>  <h1 align="center">ABBREVIATIONS</h1>  
 <p><i>All references given without an indication of the name are to the Holy Qur'ān, the first figure representing the number of the chapter and the second the number of the verse.</i></p>  
 <p><i>Words uttered by the Holy Prophet are throughout given within inverted commas.</i></p>
