 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">The Qur'ân, part II (Sacred Books of the East volume 9), Palmer edition [1880]; at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">THE CHAPTER OF 'NECESSARIES.'</h3>  <h3 align="center">(CVII. Place of origin doubtful.)</h3>  
 <p>IN the name of the merciful and compassionate God.<br> Hast thou considered him who calls the judgment a lie? He it is who pushes the orphan away; and urges not (others) to feed the poor.<br> But we to those who pray [5] and who are careless in their prayers,<br> Who pretend and withhold necessaries <a name="fr_503"></a><a href="#fn_503"><font size="1">2</font></a>.</p>
