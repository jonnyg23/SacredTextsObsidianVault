 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">The Qur'ân, part II (Sacred Books of the East volume 9), Palmer edition [1880]; at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">THE CHAPTER OF HELP.</h3>  <h3 align="center">(CX. Mecca.)</h3>  
 <p>IN the name of the merciful and compassionate God.<br> When there comes God's help and victory,<br> And thou shalt see men enter into God's religion by troops,<br> Then celebrate the praises of thy Lord, and ask forgiveness of Him, verily, He is relentant!</p>
