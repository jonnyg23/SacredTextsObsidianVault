 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">The Qur'ân, part II (Sacred Books of the East volume 9), Palmer edition [1880]; at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">THE CHAPTER OF POWER <a name="fr_495"></a><a href="#fn_495"><font size="1">1</font></a>.</h3>  <h3 align="center">(XCVII. Place of origin doubtful.)</h3>  
 <p>IN the name of the merciful and compassionate God.<br> Verily, we sent it down on the Night of Power!<br> And what shall make thee know what the Night of Power is?--the Night of Power is better than a thousand months!<br> The angels and the Spirit descend therein, by the permission of their Lord with every bidding.<br> [5] Peace it is until rising of the dawn!</p>
