 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">The Qur'ân, part II (Sacred Books of the East volume 9), Palmer edition [1880]; at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">THE CHAPTER OF THE EARTHQUAKE.</h3>  <h3 align="center">(XCIX. Place of origin doubtful.)</h3>  
 <p>IN the name of the merciful and compassionate God.<br> When the earth shall quake with its quaking!<br> And the earth shall bring forth her burdens, and man shall say, 'What ails her!'<br> On that day she shall tell her tidings, [5] because thy Lord inspires her.<br> On the day when men shall come up in separate</p>  
 <p><a name="page_339"><font size="1" color="green">p. 339</font></a></p>  
 <p>bands to show their works: and he who does the weight of an atom of good shall see it! and he who does the weight of an atom of evil shall see it!</p>
