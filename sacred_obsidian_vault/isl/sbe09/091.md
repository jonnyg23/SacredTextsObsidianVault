 <p><a name="page_333"><font size="1" color="green">p. 333</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">THE CHAPTER OF THE SUN.</h3>  <h3 align="center">(XCI. Mecca.)</h3>  
 <p>IN the name of the merciful and compassionate God.<br> By the sun and its noonday brightness!<br> And the moon when it follows him!<br> And the day when it displays him!<br> And the night when it covers him!<br> [5] And the heaven and what built it!<br> And the earth and what spread it!<br> And the soul and what fashioned it, and taught it<br>   its sin and its piety!<br> Prosperous is he who purifies it!</p>  
 <p>[10] And disappointed is he who corrupts it! Thamûd called the apostle a liar <a name="fr_489"></a><a href="092.htm#fn_489"><font size="1">1</font></a> in their outrage, when their wretch rose up and the apostle of God said to them, 'God's she-camel! so give her to drink.'</p>  
 <p>But they called him a liar, and they ham-strung her; but their Lord destroyed them in their sins, and served them all alike; [15] and He fears not the result thereof!</p>
