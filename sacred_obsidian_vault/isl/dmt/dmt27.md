 <p><a name="page_361"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 361</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">II</h3>  <h3 align="center">ON MUSLIM HISTORY AND ON PRESENT CONDITION OF MUSLIM WORLD</h3>  
 <p>AUGUST MÜLLER: <i>Die Beherrscher der Gläubigen</i>. Berlin, 1882. A very brightly written sketch based on thorough knowledge.</p>  
 <p>GUSTAV WEIL: <i>Geschichte der Chalifen</i>. 3 vols. Mannheim, 1846-1851.</p>  
 <p>SIR WILLIAM MUIR: <i>The Caliphate, its Rise, Decline and Fall</i>. London, 1891.</p>  
 <p>THEODOR NÖLDEKE: <i>Zur tendentiösen Gestaltung der Urgeschichte des Islâms</i>. ZDMG, lii, pp. 16 ff. All Nöldeke's papers on the early history of Islam are worthy of the most careful study.</p>  
 <p>G. VON VLOTEN: <i>Zur Abbasiden Geschichte</i>. ZDMG, lii, pp. 213 ff. On the early Abbasids.</p>  
 <p>R. E. BRÜNNOW: <i>De Charidschiten unter den ersten Omayyaden</i>. Leyden, 1884.</p>  
 <p>EDUARD SACHAU: <i>Über eine Arabische Chronik aus Zanzibar</i>. Mitth. a.d. Sem. f. Orient. Sprachen. Berlin, 1898. On Ibadites.</p>  
 <p>GEORGE PERCY BADGER: <i>History of the Imams and Seyyids of Oman</i>, by Salîl-ibn-Razîk. London: Hakluyt Society, 1871. Valuable for Ibadite history, law and theology.</p>  
 <p>M. J. DE GOEJE: <i>Mémoire sur les Carmathes du Bahraïn et les Fatimides</i>. Leyden, 1886.</p>  
 <p>JOHN NICHOLSON: <i>An Account of the Establishment of the Fatemite Dynasty in Africa</i>. Tübingen and Bristol, 1840.</p>  
 <p>QUATREMÈRE: <i>Mémoires historiques sur la dynastie des Khalifes Fatimites</i>. JA, 3, ii.</p>  
 <p>SYLVESTRE DE SACY: <i>Exposé de la religion des Druzes et la vie du Khalife Hakem-biamr-allah</i>. 2 vols. Paris, 1838.</p>  
 <p>F. WÜSTENFELD: <i>Geschichte der Fatimiden-Khalifen</i>. Göttingen, 1881.</p>  
 <p>STANLEY LANE-POOLE: <i>A History of Egypt in the Middle Ages</i>. New York, 1901. For the origin and founding of the Fatimid Dynasty, the Khalifa al-Hakim, etc.</p>  
 <p>H. L. FLEISCHER: <i>Briefwechsel zwischen den Anführern der Wahhabiten und dem Pasha von Damaskus</i>. <i>Kleinere Schriften</i>, iii, pp. 341 ff. First published in ZDMG for year 1857.</p>  
 <p><a name="page_362"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 362</font></a></p>  
 <p>E. REHATSEK; <i>The History of the Wahhabys in Arabia and In India</i>. Journal of Asiatic Society of Bengal. No. xxxviii (read January, 1880).</p>  
 <p><i>Turkey in Europe</i>, by "Odysseus." London, 1900. The present situation, with its historical antecedents in European Turkey and the Balkans generally.</p>  
 <p>H. O. DWIGHT: <i>Constantinople and its Problems</i>. New York, 1901.</p>  
 <p>A. S. WHITE: <i>The Expansion of Egypt</i>. London, 1899. The present situation in Egypt and its historical antecedents.</p>  
 <p>W. W. HUNTER: <i>Our Indian Mussulmans</i>. London, 1871.</p>  
 <p>SIR LEWIS PELLY: <i>The Miracle Play of Hasan and Husain</i>. London, 1879.</p>  
 <p>W. S. BLUNT: <i>The Future of Islam</i>. London, 1880.</p>
