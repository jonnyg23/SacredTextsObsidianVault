 <p><a name="page_52"><font size="1" color="green">p. 52</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">AS TO THE NATURE OF FIDELITY</h3>  
 <p>O soul! be firm footed as regards fidelity,<br> For money hath no currency without its stamp.<br> If thou turnest not the rein from the path of fidelity,<br> Thou wilt be a friend in the hearts of thine enemies.<br> Turn not thy soul's face from the abode of fidelity,<br> That thou be not ashamed before the face of thy friends.<br> Place not thy foot outside the street of fidelity,<br> Since tyranny is not fitting in the case of friends.<br> It is wrong to separate thyself from beloved ones;<br> It is contrary to fidelity to sever thyself from thy comrades. <a name="page_53"><font size="1" color="green">p. 53</font></a><br> Want of fidelity is the attribute of women.<br> Do not learn the unseemly conduct of that sex.</p>
