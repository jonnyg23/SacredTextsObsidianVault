 <p>
 The Persian poet Sadi, best known for his poetic collections the
 <a href="../bus/index.htm">Bustan</a> and
 <a href="../gulistan.txt">Gulistan</a>,
 also wrote this work, the <cite>Pand Namah</cite>, or <cite>Scroll of Wisdom</cite>.
 This is a small collection of poems on moral themes.
 Translated by Arthur N. Wollaston, this entry in the
 <cite>Wisdom of the East</cite> series is one of the best.
 </p>
