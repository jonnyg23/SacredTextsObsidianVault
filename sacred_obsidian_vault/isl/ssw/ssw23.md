 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>Sadi's Scroll of Wisdom</i>, by Arthur N. Wollaston, [1906], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">IN EXPLANATION OF PATIENCE</h3>  
 <p>If patience is thy helper<br> Thou wilt attain everlasting happiness.<br> Patience is the attribute of prophets;<br> Those who practise religion turn not aside from this direction.<br> Patience openeth the door of the desires of friends,<br> For save patience there is no key for them.<br> Patience giveth thee the desire of thine heart,<br> For at the hands of mankind thy difficulties are solved.<br> Patience is best in every case,<br> For in this sentence is much meaning. <a name="page_55"><font size="1" color="green">p. 55</font></a><br> Patience giveth thee thy desire,<br> It relieveth thee from pain and misfortune.<br> Patience is the key of the door of thine aspirations,<br> The enlarger of the kingdom of desire.<br> Exercise patience if thou art religious,<br> For haste is the attribute of devils.</p>
