 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>Sadi's Scroll of Wisdom</i>, by Arthur N. Wollaston, [1906], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">IN EXPLANATION OF THE WINE OF AFFECTION AND LOVE</h3>  
 <p>O cupbearer! bring wine as it were a garment of fire,<br> For a man with a soul desires this (religious) intoxication.<br> The ruby wine in the golden goblet<br> Is soul-inspiring, as it were a beautiful pearl.<br> Welcome is the fire of desire to those inspired with love!<br> Welcome are the delightful pains of the lords of love! <a name="page_51"><font size="1" color="green">p. 51</font></a><br> Bring this wine as it were the water of immortality, <a name="fr_10"></a><a href="#fn_10"><font size="1">1</font></a><br> For from its fragrance the soul findeth deliverance from grief!<br> Happy that soul that desires a Friend! <a name="fr_11"></a><a href="#fn_11"><font size="1">2</font></a><br> Happy that person who is ensnared in the bonds of affection for Him!<br> Happy that person who is enamoured of the face of the Friend!<br> Happy that person whose abode is the Nook of the Friend!<br> A Friend as it were wine like soul-refreshing Peace!<br> Wine—the purest like a beautiful face!<br> Happy men of soul who adore wine!<br> Happy the flavour of wine to men of spirit!</p>
