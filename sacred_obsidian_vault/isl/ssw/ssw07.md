 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>Sadi's Scroll of Wisdom</i>, by Arthur N. Wollaston, [1906], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">IN PRAISE OF GENEROSITY</h3>  
 <p>O soul! whoever spreadeth the table of benevolence<br> Is famous in the world of liberality.<br> Generosity will make thee renowned throughout the universe;<br> Generosity will secure thee happiness.<br> Beside generosity there is nothing in the world,<br> Nor is aught more current in any market!<br> Generosity will be the source of delight; <a name="page_32"><font size="1" color="green">p. 32</font></a><br> Generosity will be the harvest of life.<br> Freshen the heart of the world by generosity;<br> Fill the globe with the renown of thy generosity.<br> For ever be steadfast in generosity;<br> Since the Creator of the soul is beneficent.</p>
