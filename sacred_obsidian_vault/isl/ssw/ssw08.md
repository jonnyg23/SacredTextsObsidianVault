 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>Sadi's Scroll of Wisdom</i>, by Arthur N. Wollaston, [1906], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">DESCRIPTION OF BENEVOLENCE</h3>  
 <p>The man of good parts maketh choice of benevolence;<br> For mankind becometh prosperous from benevolence.<br> Be conqueror of the world through kindness and benevolence;<br> Be a chief in the kingdom of kindness and generosity.<br> Benevolence is the attribute of men of piety,<br> Benevolence is the duty of the prosperous.<br> Benevolence is the touchstone of the alloy of sin, <a name="page_33"><font size="1" color="green">p. 33</font></a><br> Benevolence is the medicine for all ills.<br> Be not, if you can, void of benevolence,<br> That you may snatch the ball of excellence owing to benevolence.</p>
