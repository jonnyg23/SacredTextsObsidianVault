 <p><a name="page_107"><font size="1" color="green">p. 107</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">VERSES</h3>  <h4 align="center">OF YEZID TO HIS FATHER, MOWIAH,</h4>  <h5 align="center">WHO REPROACHED HIM FOR DRUNKENNESS.</h5>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">MUST then my failings from the shaft<br>   Of anger ne’er escape?<br> And dost thou storm because I've quaffed<br>   The water of the grape?  
 <p>That I can thus from wine be driven,<br>   Thou surely ne’er canst think—<br> Another reason thou hast given<br>   Why I resolve to drink:</p>  
 <p>’Twas sweet the flowing cup to seize,<br>   ’Tis sweet thy rage to see;<br> And, first, I drink myself to please,<br>   And, next—to anger thee!</p></div>
