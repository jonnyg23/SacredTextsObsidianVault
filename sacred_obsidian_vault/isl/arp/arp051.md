 <p><a name="page_123"><font size="1" color="green">p. 123</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">TO A FRIEND,</h3>  <h4 align="center">ON HIS BIRTHDAY.</h4>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">WHEN born, in tears we saw thee drowned,<br>   While thine assembled friends around<br>   With smiles their joy confessed:<br> So live, that at thy parting hour,<br> They may the flood of sorrow pour,<br>   And thou in smiles be dressed.</div>
