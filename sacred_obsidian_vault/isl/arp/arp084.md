 <p><a name="page_166"><font size="1" color="green">p. 166</font></a></p>  <h4 align="center">ON PROCRASTINATION.</h4>  <h5 align="center">BY HEBAT ALLAH IBN ALTALMITH.</h5>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">YOUTH is a drunken, noisy hour,<br>   With every folly fraught;<br> But man, by Age's chastening power,<br>   Is sobered into thought.  
 <p>Then we resolve our faults to shun,<br>   And shape our course anew;<br> But ere the wise reform's begun,<br>   Life closes on our view.</p>  
 <p>The travellers thus, who wildly roam,<br>   Or heedlessly delay,<br> Are left, when they should reach their home,<br>   Benighted on the way.</p></div>
