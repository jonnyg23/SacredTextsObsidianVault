 <p><a name="page_103"><font size="1" color="green">p. 103</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">ON HIS FRIENDS.</h3>  <h4 align="center">BY MESKIN ALDARAMY.</h4>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">WITH conscious pride I view the band<br> Of faithful friends that round me stand;<br> With pride exult, that I alone<br> Can join these scattered gems in one:<br>     For they're a wreath of pearls, and I<br>     The silken cord on which they lie.  
 <p>’Tis mine their inmost souls to see;<br> Unlocked is every heart to me;<br> To me they cling, on me they rest,<br> And I've a place in every breast:<br>     For they're a wreath of pearls, and I<br>     The silken cord on which they lie.</p></div>
