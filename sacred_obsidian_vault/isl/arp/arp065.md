 <p><a name="page_137"><font size="1" color="green">p. 137</font></a></p>  <h5 align="center">ON THE</h5>  <h4 align="center">DEATH OF A SON.</h4>  <h5 align="center">BY ALI BEN MOHAMMED ALTAHMANY.</h5>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">TYRANT of Man! Imperious Fate!<br>   I bow before thy dread decree;<br> Nor hope in this uncertain state<br>   To find a seat secure from thee.  
 <p>Life is a dark, tumultuous stream,<br>   With many a care and sorrow foul;<br> Yet thoughtless mortals vainly deem<br>   That it can yield a limpid bowl.</p>  
 <p>Think not that stream will backward flow,<br>   Or cease its destined course to keep;<br> As soon the blazing spark shall glow<br>   Beneath the surface of the deep.</p>  
 <p>Believe not Fate, at thy command,<br>   Will grant a meed she never gave;<br> As soon the airy tower shall stand<br>   That's built upon a passing wave.</p>  
 <p>Life is a sleep of threescore years;<br>   Death bids us wake and hail the light;<br> And man, with all his hopes and fears,<br>   Is but a phantom of the night.</p></div>
