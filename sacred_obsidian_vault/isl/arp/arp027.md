 <p><a name="page_98"><font size="1" color="green">p. 98</font></a></p>  <h4 align="center">ON THE</h4>  <h3 align="center">DEATH OF HIS MISTRESS.</h3>  <h4 align="center">BY ABU SAHER ALHEDILY.</h4>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">DOST thou wonder that I flew<br> Charmed to meet my Leila's view?<br> Dost thou wonder that I hung<br> Raptured on my Leila's tongue?—<br> If her ghost's funereal screech<br> Through the earth my grave should reach,<br> On that voice I loved so well<br> My transported ghost would dwell:<br> If in death I can descry<br> Where my Leila's relics lie,<br> Saher's dust will flit away,<br> There to join his Leila's clay.</div>
