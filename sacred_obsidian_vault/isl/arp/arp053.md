 <p><a name="page_126"><font size="1" color="green">p. 126</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">EPIGRAM</h3>  <h6 align="center">ON</h6>  <h4 align="center">EBN NAPHTA-WAH.</h4>  <h5 align="center">BY MOHAMMED BEN ZEID ALMOTAKALAM.</h5>  
 <p><small>[IN order to understand Ben Zeid's Charade. we must remark that, in Arabic, <i>Naphta</i> signifies a combustible not very much unlike our gunpowder, and that <i>Wah</i> is an exclamation of sorrow.]</small></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">BY the former with ruin and death we are curst;<br>   In the latter we grieve for the ills of the first;<br> And as for the whole where together they meet,<br>   It's a drunkard, a liar, a thief, and a cheat.</div>
