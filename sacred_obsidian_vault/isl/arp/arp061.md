 <p><a name="page_133"><font size="1" color="green">p. 133</font></a></p>  <h4 align="center">ON THE</h4>  <h3 align="center">CAPRICES OF FORTUNE.</h3>  <h5 align="center">BY SHEMS ALMAALI CABUS,</h5>  <h5 align="center">THE DETHRONED SULTAN OF GEORGIA.</h5>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">WHY should I blush that Fortune's frown<br>   Dooms me life's humble paths to tread?<br> To live unheeded and unknown!<br>   To sink forgotten to the dead!  
 <p>’Tis not the good, the wise, the brave,<br>   That surest shine, or highest rise:<br> The feather sports upon the wave,<br>   The pearl in ocean's cavern lies.</p>  
 <p>Each lesser star that studs the sphere<br>   Sparkles with undiminished light:<br> Dark and eclipsed alone appear<br>   The Lord of Day, the Queen of Night.</p></div>
