 <p><a name="page_109"><font size="1" color="green">p. 109</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">TO THE KHALIF</h3>  <h2 align="center">HAROUN ALRASHID,</h2>  <h5 align="center">ON HIS UNDERTAKING A PILGRIMAGE TO MECCA.</h5>  <h4 align="center">By IBRAHIM BEN ADHEM.</h4>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">RELIGION'S gems can ne’er adorn<br>   The flimsy robe by Pleasure worn:<br> Its feeble texture soon would tear,<br>   And give those jewels to the air.  
 <p>Thrice happy they who seek th’ abode<br>   Of peace and pleasure, in their God!<br> Who spurn the world, its joys despise,<br>   And grasp at bliss beyond the skies.</p></div>
