 <p><a name="page_112"><font size="1" color="green">p. 112</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">EPIGRAM</h3>  <h6 align="center">ON</h6>  <h5 align="center">TAHER BEN HOSEIN,</h5>  <h6 align="center">WHO WAS AMBIDEXTER AND ONE-EYED.</h6>  
 <p>A PAIR of right hands and a single dim eye<br> Must form not a man, but a monster, they cry:<br> Change a hand to an eye, good Taher, if you can,<br> And a monster perhaps may be changed to man.</p>
