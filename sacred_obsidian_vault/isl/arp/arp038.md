 <p><a name="page_111"><font size="1" color="green">p. 111</font></a></p>  <h5 align="center">ON THE</h5>  <h3 align="center">RUIN OF THE BARMECIDES.</h3>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">NO, Barmec! time hath never shown<br>   So sad a change of wayward fate;<br> Nor sorrowing mortals ever known<br>   A grief so true, a loss so great.  
 <p>Spouse of the world! thy soothing breast<br>   Did balm to every woe afford;<br> And now, no more by thee caressed,<br>   The widowed world bewails her lord.</p></div>
