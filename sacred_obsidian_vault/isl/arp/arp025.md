 <img src="img/09500.jpg" alt="Decorative Page Header"></p>  <h6 align="center">ON THE</h6>  <h3 align="center">TOMB OF MANO.</h3>  <h4 align="center">BY HASSAN ALASADY.</h4>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">FRIENDS of my heart, who share my sighs!<br> Go seek the turf where Mano lies,<br> And woo the dewy clouds of Spring<br> To sweep it with prolific wing.  
 <p>Within that cell, beneath that heap,<br> Friendship and Truth and Honour sleep.<br> Beneficence, that used to clasp<br> The world within her ample grasp,<br> There rests entombed—of thought bereft;<br> For were one conscious atom left,<br> New bliss, new kindness to display,<br> ’T would burst the grave, and seek the day.</p>  
 <p>But though in dust thy relics lie,<br> Thy virtues, Mano, ne’er shall die:<br> Though Nile's full stream be seen no more,<br> That spread his waves from shore to shore,<br> Still in the verdure of the plain<br> His vivifying smiles remain.</p></div>
