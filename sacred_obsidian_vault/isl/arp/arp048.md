 <p><a name="page_121"><font size="1" color="green">p. 121</font></a></p>  <h4 align="center">ON A VALETUDINARIAN.</h4>  <h5 align="center">BY THE SAME.</h5>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">SO careful is Isa, and anxious to last,<br>   So afraid of himself is he grown,<br> He swears through two nostrils the breath goes too fast,<br>   And he's trying to breathe through but one.</div>
