 <p><a name="page_141"><font size="1" color="green">p. 141</font></a></p>  <h5 align="center">ON</h5>  <h4 align="center">THE INCOMPATIBILITY OF PRIDE</h4>  <h4 align="center">AND TRUE GLORY.</h4>  <h5 align="center">BY ABU ALOLA.</h5>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">THINK not, Abdallah, Pride and Fame<br>   Can ever travel hand in hand;<br> With breast opposed, and adverse aim,<br>   On the same narrow path they stand.  
 <p>Thus Youth and Age together meet,<br>   And Life's divided moments share:<br> This can't advance till that retreat;<br>   What's here increased, is lessened there.</p>  
 <p>And thus the falling shades of Night<br>   Still struggle with the lucid ray,<br> And ere they stretch their gloomy flight,<br>   Must win the lengthened space from Day.</p></div>
