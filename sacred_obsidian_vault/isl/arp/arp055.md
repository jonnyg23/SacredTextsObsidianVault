 <p><a name="page_127"><font size="1" color="green">p. 127</font></a></p>  <h4 align="center">TO A LADY,</h4>  <h5 align="center">ON SEEING HER BLUSH.</h5>  <h5 align="center">BY THE KHALIF RADHI BILLAH.</h5>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">LEILA, whene’er I gaze on thee<br>   My altered cheek turns pale;<br> While upon thine, sweet maid, I see<br>   A deep’ning blush prevail.  
 <p>Leila, shall I the cause impart<br>   Why such a change takes place?—<br> The crimson stream deserts my heart<br>   To mantle on thy face.</p></div>
