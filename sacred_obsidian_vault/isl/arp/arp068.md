 <p><a name="page_140"><font size="1" color="green">p. 140</font></a></p>  <h4 align="center">TO ADVERSITY.</h4>  <h5 align="center">BY ABU MENBAA CARAWASH, SULTAN OF MOUSEL.</h5>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">HAIL, chastening friend, Adversity! ’tis thine<br> The mental ore to temper and refine;<br> To cast in Virtue's mould the yielding heart,<br> And Honour's polish to the mind impart.  
 <p>Without thy wakening touch, thy plastic aid,<br> I'd lain the shapeless mass that Nature made;<br> But formed, great artist, by thy magic hand,<br> I gleam a sword, to conquer and command.</p></div>
