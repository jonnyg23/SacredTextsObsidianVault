 <p><a name="page_142"><font size="1" color="green">p. 142</font></a></p>  <h5 align="center">ON THE</h5>  <h4 align="center">DEATH OF NEDHAM ALMOLK,</h4>  <h6 align="center">VIZIER TO THE THREE FIRST SELJUK</h6>  <h6 align="center">SULTANS OF PERSIA.</h6>  <h5 align="center">BY SHEBAL ADDAULET.</h5>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">THY virtues, famed through every land,<br>   Thy spotless life in age and youth,<br> Prove thee a pearl, <a name="fr_22"></a><a href="#fn_22"><font size="1">*</font></a> by Nature's hand<br>   Formed out of purity and truth.  
 <p>Too long its beams of orient light<br>   Upon a thankless world were shed:<br> <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Allah</span> has now revenged the slight,<br>   And called it to its native bed.</p></div>
