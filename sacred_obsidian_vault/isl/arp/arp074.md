 <p><a name="page_147"><font size="1" color="green">p. 147</font></a></p>  <h4 align="center">THE INCONSISTENT.</h4>  <h5 align="center">TO A LADY, UPON HER REFUSAL OF A PRESENT OF</h5>  <h5 align="center">MELONS, AND HER REJECTION OF THE</h5>  <h5 align="center">ADDRESSES OF AN ADMIRER.</h5>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">WHEN I sent you my melons, you cried out with scorn,<br>   "They ought to be <i>heavy</i>, and <i>wrinkled</i>, and <i>yellow:</i>"<br> When I offered myself, whom those graces adorn,<br>   You flouted, and called me an <i>ugly old</i> fellow!</div>
