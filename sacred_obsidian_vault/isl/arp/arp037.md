 <p><a name="page_110"><font size="1" color="green">p. 110</font></a></p>  <h6 align="center">ON THE</h6>  <h3 align="center">INAUGURATION OF HAROUN ALRASHID,</h3>  <h6 align="center">AND THE</h6>  <h4 align="center">APPOINTMENT OF YAHIA</h4>  <h5 align="center">TO BE HIS VIZIER.</h5>  <h4 align="center">BY ISAAC ALMOUSELY.</h4>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">TH’ affrighted sun erewhile had fled,<br>   And hid his radiant face in night;<br> A cheerless gloom the world o’erspread—<br>   But Haroun came, and all was bright.  
 <p>Again the sun shoots forth his rays;<br>   Nature is decked in beauty's robe:<br>   For mighty Haroun's sceptre sways,<br> And Yahia's arm sustains the globe.</p></div>
