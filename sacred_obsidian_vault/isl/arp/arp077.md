 <p><a name="page_151"><font size="1" color="green">p. 151</font></a></p>  <h4 align="center">EPIGRAM</h4>  <h6 align="center">ON</h6>  <h4 align="center">ABU ALCHAIR SELAMU,</h4>  <h6 align="center">AN EGYPTIAN PHYSICIAN.</h6>  <h5 align="center">BY GEORGE, A PHYSICIAN OF ANTIOCH.</h5>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">WHOEVER has recourse to thee<br>   Can hope for health no more:<br> He's launched into perdition's sea,<br>   A sea without a shore.  
 <p>Where’er admission thou canst gain,<br>   Where’er thy phiz can pierce,<br> At once the Doctor they retain,<br>   The mourners and the hearse.</p></div>
