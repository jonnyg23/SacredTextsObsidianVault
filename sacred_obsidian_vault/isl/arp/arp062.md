 <p><a name="page_134"><font size="1" color="green">p. 134</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">LIFE.</h3>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">LIKE sheep, we're doomed to travel o’er<br>   The fated track to all assigned;<br> These follow those that went before,<br>   And leave the world to those behind.  
 <p>As the flock seeks the pasturing shade,<br>   Man presses to the future day;<br> While Death, amidst the tufted glade,<br>   Like the dun robber, <a name="fr_21"></a><a href="#fn_21"><font size="1">*</font></a> waits his prey.</p></div>
