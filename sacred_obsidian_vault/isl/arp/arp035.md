 <p><a name="page_108"><font size="1" color="green">p. 108</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">ON FATALISM.</h3>  <h4 align="center">BY THE IMAM SHAFAY MOHAMMED BEN IDRIS.</h4>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">NOT always wealth, not always force,<br>   A splendid destiny commands;<br> The lordly vulture gnaws the corse<br>   That rots upon yon barren sands.  
 <p>Nor want nor weakness still conspires<br>   To bind us to a sordid state;<br> The fly, that with a touch expires,<br>   Sips honey from the royal plate.</p></div>
