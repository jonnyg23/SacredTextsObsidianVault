 <p><a name="page_1"><font size="1" color="green">p. 1</font></a></p>  <h1 align="center">THE QUR'ÂN.</h1>  <h3 align="center">THE OPENING CHAPTER.</h3>  <h3 align="center">(I. Mecca.)</h3>  
 <p>IN the name of the merciful and compassionate God.</p>  
 <p>Praise belongs to God, the Lord of the worlds, the merciful, the compassionate, the ruler of the day of judgment! Thee we serve and Thee we ask for aid. [5] Guide us in the right path, the path of those Thou art gracious to <a name="fr_39"></a><a href="#fn_39"><font size="1">1</font></a>; not of those Thou art wroth with; nor of those who err.</p>
