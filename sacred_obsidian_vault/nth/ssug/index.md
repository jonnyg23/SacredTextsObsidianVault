 <p>
 This is one of Max Freedom Long's latter books.
 In this we can see the evolution of his ideas, consistent with
 the views expressed in
 <a href="../ssbm/index.htm">The Secret Science Behind Miracles</a>.
 One of the highlights of this book is the review of the self-help scene
 during the mid-50s, including a mention of Dianetics, as well as
 General Semantics and other belief systems.
 Mostly though, this is a practical manual of self-suggestion using
 the Huna techniques, including detailed instructions as to how the
 operation works.
 An appendix discusses the larger context of this in the Huna world.--J.B. Hare
