 <dir><i>When we are able to do away with all sense of separateness by living continually in the realization of this oneness, not only will our bodily ills and weaknesses vanish, but all limitations along all lines.</i>--<a href="twi07.htm#page_86">p. 86</a>

 <p>
 This is a clearly written explanation of the New Thought philosophy.
 Trine discusses how to tap into the power of the cosmos to impact
 personal health, wealth, and growth.
 This book is essential reading for anyone interested in this belief system.
 </p>
