 <p><a name="page_iv"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. iv</font></a> <a name="page_v"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. v</font></a></p>  <div align="center">  <table border="0">  <tr>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>LOOK TO THIS   DAY</p>   
 <p>Look to this   day!<br>   For it is life, the very life of life.<br>   For yesterday is already a dream, and tomorrow is only a vision;<br>   But today, well lived, makes every yesterday<br>   A dream of happiness, and every tomorrow a vision of hope.<br>   Look well, therefore, to this day!<br>   Such is the salutation of the dawn!</p>
