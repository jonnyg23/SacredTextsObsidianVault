 <p></p>  <h3 align="center">EARS</h3>  
 <p>(<i>DeafnessCorrespondenceStrong personal will, stubbornness and a desire not to hear certain things</i>.)</p>  
 <p align="center"></p>  <h4 align="center">AFFIRMATION</h4>  
 <p>My ears are the ears of Spirit. The Light of the Christ now streams through my ears dissolving all hardness or malformation.</p>  
 <p>I hear clearly the voice of intuition and give instant obedience.</p>  
 <p>I hear clearly glad tidings of great joy.</p>
