 <p></p>  <h4 align="center">ANAEMIA</h4>  
 <p>(<i>CorrespondenceUnfed desireslack of happiness</i>.)</p>  
 <p align="center"></p>  <h4 align="center">AFFIRMATION</h4>  
 <p>I am nourished by the Spirit within. Every cell in my body is filled with light. I give thanks for radiant health and endless happiness. (<i>This statement may be used in the healing of any disease</i>.)</p>
