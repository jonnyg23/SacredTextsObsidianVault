 <p></p>  <h3 align="center">FORGIVENESS</h3>  
 <p align="center"></p>  <h4 align="center">AFFIRMATIONS</h4>  
 <p>I forgive everyone and everyone forgives me. The gates swing open for my good.</p>  
 <p align="center"></p>  
 <p>I call on the law of forgiveness. I am free from mistakes and the consequences of mistakes. I am under grace and not under karmic law.</p>  
 <p align="center"></p>  
 <p>Though my mistakes be as scarlet, I shall be washed whiter than snow.</p>  
 <p align="center"></p>  
 <p>What didn't happen in the Kingdom never happened anywhere.</p>
