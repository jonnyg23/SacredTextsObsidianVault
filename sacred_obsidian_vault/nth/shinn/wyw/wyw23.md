 <p></p>  <h3 align="center">FALSE GROWTHS</h3>  
 <p>(<i>CorrespondenceJealousy, hatred, resentment, fear, etc., etc</i>.)</p>  
 <p align="center"></p>  <h4 align="center">AFFIRMATION</h4>  
 <p>Every plant my Father in Heaven has not planted shall be rooted up. All false ideas in my consciousness are now obliterated. The Light of the Christ streams through every cell and I give thanks for my radiant health and happiness now and forevermore.</p>
