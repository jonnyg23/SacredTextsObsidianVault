 <p></p>  <h3 align="center">INTERVIEWS</h3>  
 <p align="center"></p>  <h4 align="center">AFFIRMATIONS</h4>  
 <p>There is no competition on the Spiritual plane. What is mine is given me, under grace.</p>  
 <p align="center"></p>  
 <p>I am identified in love with the Spirit of this person (or persons). God protects my interests and the Divine Idea now comes out of this situation.</p>
