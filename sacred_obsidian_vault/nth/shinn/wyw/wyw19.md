 <p></p>  <h3 align="center">EYES</h3>  
 <p>(<i>Imperfect vision. CorrespondencesFear, suspicion, seeing obstacles. Watching for unhappy events to come to passliving in the past or futurenot living in the NOW</i>.)</p>  
 <p align="center"></p>  <h4 align="center">AFFIRMATIONS</h4>  
 <p>The Light of the Christ now floods my eyeballs. I have the crystal clear vision of the Spirit. I see clearly and distinctly there are no obstacles on my pathway. I see clearly the fulfillment of my heart's desire.</p>  
 <p align="center"></p>  
 <p>I have the X-ray eye of the Spirit. I see through apparent obstacles. I see clearly the miracle come to pass.</p>  
 <p align="center"></p>  
 <p>I have the crystal clear vision of the Spirit, I see clearly the open road. There are no obstacles on my pathway. I now see miracles and wonders come to pass.</p>  
 <p align="center"></p>  
 <p>I give thanks for my perfect sight. I see God in every face, I see good in every situation.</p>  
 <p align="center"></p>  
 <p></p>  
 <p>I have the crystal clear vision of the Spirit. I look up and down and all around, for my good comes from North, South, East and West.</p>  
 <p align="center"></p>  
 <p>My eyes are God's eyes, perfect and flawless. The Light of the Christ floods my eyeballs and streams on my pathway. I see clearly there are no lions on my way, only angels and endless blessings.</p>
