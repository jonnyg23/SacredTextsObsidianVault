 <p></p>  <h3 align="center">HEART DISEASE</h3>  
 <p>(<i>CorrespondenceFear, Anger, etc</i>.)</p>  
 <p align="center"></p>  <h4 align="center">AFFIRMATION</h4>  
 <p>My heart is a perfect idea in Divine Mind and is now in its right place, doing its right work.</p>  
 <p>It is a happy heart, a fearless heart and a loving heart.</p>  
 <p>The Light of the Christ streams through every cell and I give thanks for my radiant health.</p>
