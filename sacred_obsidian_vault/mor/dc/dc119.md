 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="../../img/tn_mor.jpg"></a><br><i>Doctrine and Covenants of the Church of Jesus Christ of the Latter-Day Saints</i>, at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">119. Revelation through Joseph Smith, Far West, Missouri, July 8, 1838. <i><br> History of the Church</i> 3:44.</h3>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_119:1">119:1</a></font></span> Verily, thus saith the Lord, I require all their surplus property to be put into the hands of the bishop of my church in Zion,</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_119:2">119:2</a></font></span> For the building of mine house, and for the laying of the foundation of Zion and for the priesthood, and for the debts of the Presidency of my Church.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_119:3">119:3</a></font></span> And this shall be the beginning of the tithing of my people.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_119:4">119:4</a></font></span> And after that, those who have thus been tithed shall pay one-tenth of all their interest annually; and this shall be a standing law unto them forever, for my holy priesthood, saith the Lord.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_119:5">119:5</a></font></span> Verily I say unto you, it shall come to pass that all those who gather unto the land of Zion shall be tithed of their surplus properties, and shall observe this law, or they shall not be found worthy to abide among you.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_119:6">119:6</a></font></span> And I say unto you, if my people observe not this law, to keep it holy, and by this law sanctify the land of Zion unto me, that my statutes and my judgments may be kept thereon, that it may be most holy, behold, verily I say unto you, it shall not be a land of Zion unto you.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_119:7">119:7</a></font></span> And this shall be an ensample unto all the stakes of Zion. Even so. Amen. </p>
