 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="../../img/tn_mor.jpg"></a><br><i>Doctrine and Covenants of the Church of Jesus Christ of the Latter-Day Saints</i>, at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">47. Revelation through Joseph Smith, Kirtland, Ohio, March 8, 1831. <i><br> History of the Church</i> 1:166.</h3>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_47:1">47:1</a></font></span> Behold, it is expedient in me that my servant John should write and keep a regular history, and assist you, my servant Joseph, in transcribing all things which shall be given you, until he is called to further duties.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_47:2">47:2</a></font></span> Again, verily I say unto you that he can also lift up his voice in meetings, whenever it shall be expedient.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_47:3">47:3</a></font></span> And again, I say unto you that it shall be appointed unto him to keep the church record and history continually; for Oliver Cowdery I have appointed to another office.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_47:4">47:4</a></font></span> Wherefore, it shall be given him, inasmuch as he is faithful, by the Comforter, to write these things. Even so. Amen. </p>
