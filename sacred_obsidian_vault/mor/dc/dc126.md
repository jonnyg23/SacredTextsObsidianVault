Revelation through Joseph Smith, Nauvoo, Illinois, July 9, 1841. <i><br> History of the Church</i> 4:382.</h3>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_126:1">126:1</a></font></span> Dear and well-beloved brother, Brigham Young, verily thus saith the Lord unto you: My servant Brigham, it is no more required at your hand to leave your family as in times past, for your offering is acceptable to me.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_126:2">126:2</a></font></span> I have seen your labor and toil in journeyings for my name.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_126:3">126:3</a></font></span> I therefore command you to send my word abroad, and take especial care of your family from this time, henceforth and forever. Amen. </p>
