 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="../../img/tn_mor.jpg"></a><br><i>Doctrine and Covenants of the Church of Jesus Christ of the Latter-Day Saints</i>, at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">91. Revelation through Joseph Smith, Kirtland, Ohio, March 9, 1833. <i><br> History of the Church</i> 1:331-32.</h3>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_91:1">91:1</a></font></span> Verily, thus saith the Lord unto you concerning the Apocrypha—There are many things contained therein that are true, and it is mostly translated correctly;</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_91:2">91:2</a></font></span> There are many things contained therein that are not true, which are interpolations by the hands of men.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_91:3">91:3</a></font></span> Verily, I say unto you, that it is not needful that the Apocrypha should be translated.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_91:4">91:4</a></font></span> Therefore, whoso readeth it, let him understand, for the Spirit manifesteth truth;</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_91:5">91:5</a></font></span> And whoso is enlightened by the Spirit shall obtain benefit therefrom;</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_91:6">91:6</a></font></span> And whoso receiveth not by the Spirit, cannot be benefited. Therefore it is not needful that it should be translated. Amen. </p>
