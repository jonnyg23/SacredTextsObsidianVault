Revelation to Joseph Smith and Sidney Rigdon, Fayette, New York, January 1831. <i><br> History of the Church</i> 1:145.</h3>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_40:1">40:1</a></font></span> Behold, verily I say unto you, that the heart of my servant James Covill was right before me, for he covenanted with me that he would obey my word.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_40:2">40:2</a></font></span> And he received the word with gladness, but straightway Satan tempted him; and the fear of persecution and the cares of the world caused him to reject the word.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_40:3">40:3</a></font></span> Wherefore he broke my covenant, and it remaineth with me to do with him as seemeth me good. Amen. </p>
