 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="../../img/tn_mor.jpg"></a><br><i>Doctrine and Covenants of the Church of Jesus Christ of the Latter-Day Saints</i>, at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">23. Revelation through Joseph Smith, Manchester, New York, April 1830, to Oliver Cowdery, Hyrum Smith, Samuel H. Smith, Joseph Smith, Sr., and Joseph Knight, Sr. <i><br> History of the Church</i> 1:80.</h3>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_23:1">23:1</a></font></span> Behold, I speak unto you, Oliver, a few words. Behold, thou art blessed, and art under no condemnation. But beware of pride, lest thou shouldst enter into temptation.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_23:2">23:2</a></font></span> Make known thy calling unto the church, and also before the world, and thy heart shall be opened to preach the truth from henceforth and forever. Amen.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_23:3">23:3</a></font></span> Behold, I speak unto you, Hyrum, a few words; for thou also art under no condemnation, and thy heart is opened, and thy tongue loosed; and thy calling is to exhortation, and to strengthen the church continually. Wherefore thy duty is unto the church forever, and this because of thy family. Amen.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_23:4">23:4</a></font></span> Behold, I speak a few words unto you, Samuel; for thou also art under no condemnation, and thy calling is to exhortation, and to strengthen the church; and thou art not as yet called to preach before the world. Amen.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_23:5">23:5</a></font></span> Behold, I speak a few words unto you, Joseph; for thou also art under no condemnation, and thy calling also is to exhortation, and to strengthen the church; and this is thy duty from henceforth and forever. Amen.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_23:6">23:6</a></font></span> Behold, I manifest unto you, Joseph Knight, by these words, that you must take up your cross, in the which you must pray vocally before the world as well as in secret, and in your family, and among your friends, and in all places.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_23:7">23:7</a></font></span> And, behold, it is your duty to unite with the true church, and give your language to exhortation continually, that you may receive the reward of the laborer. Amen. </p>
