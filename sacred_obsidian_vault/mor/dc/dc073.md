 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="../../img/tn_mor.jpg"></a><br><i>Doctrine and Covenants of the Church of Jesus Christ of the Latter-Day Saints</i>, at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">73. Revelation to Joseph Smith and Sidney Rigdon, Hiram, Ohio, January 10, 1832. <i><br> History of the Church</i> 1:241-42.</h3>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_73:1">73:1</a></font></span> For verily, thus saith the Lord, it is expedient in me that they should continue preaching the gospel, and in exhortation to the churches in the regions round about, until conference;</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_73:2">73:2</a></font></span> And then, behold, it shall be made known unto them, by the voice of the conference, their several missions.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_73:3">73:3</a></font></span> Now, verily I say unto you my servants, Joseph Smith, Jun., and Sidney Rigdon, saith the Lord, it is expedient to translate again;</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_73:4">73:4</a></font></span> And, inasmuch as it is practicable, to preach in the regions round about until conference; and after that it is expedient to continue the work of translation until it be finished.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_73:5">73:5</a></font></span> And let this be a pattern unto the elders until further knowledge, even as it is written.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_73:6">73:6</a></font></span> Now I give no more unto you at this time. Gird up your loins and be sober. Even so. Amen. </p>
