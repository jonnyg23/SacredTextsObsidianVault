 <p><a name="page_211"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 211</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">TLASKAN WORDS.</h3>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Akasa</span>. The great ocean of ether sweeping in and through all things.</p>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Lomkatos</span>. Omn taken; those who have passed through the change called death.</p>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Neontu</span>. One of the disciples of Zertoulem most deeply loved by the Master.</p>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Omn</span>. The Eternal; God.</p>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Otmar</span>. Under clouds; not yet awakened to the light of the spirit.</p>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Sebas-thā-ontu</span>. Sebas, mountains; thā, the sun; ontu, setting. "The Mountains of the Setting Sun."</p>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Thā</span>. The sun.</p>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Tlaskan</span>. The sacred race that peopled a portion of Central America many thousands of years ago.</p>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Tlaskanata</span>. The Land of the Sacred Record; so called because the people were led to this land by a prophecy in their sacred writings.</p>
