 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>Wisdom of the Ages</i>, by George A. Fuller, [1916], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  
 <p><a name="page_33"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 33</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">IX.</h3>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Rise</span>, oh, my soul, to still loftier heights</p>  
 <p>Unfettered be all thy wings!</p>  
 <p>Let earth's empurpled mountains fade upon my vision.</p>  
 <p>Rise, oh, my soul, to still loftier heights</p>  
 <p>Unfettered be all thy wings!</p>  
 <p>Let the great sun sink and fade from out the heavens.</p>  
 <p>Rise, oh, my soul, to still loftier heights</p>  
 <p>Unfettered be all thy wings!</p>  
 <p>Fade from my vision, oh, ye mighty worlds and suns, flaming with amber, and gold and purple light.</p>  
 <p>Rise, oh, my soul, to still loftier heights</p>  
 <p>Unfettered be all thy wings!</p>  
 <p>Past the great abysses of the formless and</p>  
 <p><a name="page_34"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 34</font></a></p>  
 <p>the void where suns are born and worlds and suns sink in their graves.</p>  
 <p>Rise, oh, my soul, to still loftier heights</p>  
 <p>Unfettered be all thy wings!</p>  
 <p>Through spaces limitless and heavens immeasurable I fain would wing my way.</p>  
 <p>Rise, oh, my soul, to still loftier heights</p>  
 <p>Unfettered be all thy wings!</p>  
 <p>Through circling spheres of light where spirits and angels dwell I still would cleave my way.</p>  
 <p>Rise, oh, my soul, to still loftier heights</p>  
 <p>Unfettered be all thy wings!</p>  
 <p>Past globes of dazzling brightness where earth's Messiahs live still would I wend my way.</p>  
 <p>Rise, oh, my soul, to still loftier heights</p>  
 <p>Unfettered be all thy wings!</p>  
 <p>On, on, with maddening rush through trackless azure fields, thick sown with sparkling</p>  
 <p><a name="page_35"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 35</font></a></p>  
 <p>gems, on wings of love, oh, soul, pursue thy way.</p>  
 <p>Rise, oh, my soul, to still loftier heights</p>  
 <p>Unfettered be all thy wings!</p>  
 <p>Swift, oh, soul, be thy onward flight beyond those white-lighted spheres where archangels dwell.</p>  
 <p>Rise, oh, my soul, to still loftier heights</p>  
 <p>Unfettered be all thy wings!</p>  
 <p>Behold where dwell the sons of God in Infinite light and splendor.</p>  
 <p>Rise, oh, my soul, to still loftier heights</p>  
 <p>Unfettered be all thy wings!</p>  
 <p>With bowed and reverent head approach the centre where dwells the Infinite Omn, whose splendor, beauty and glory no artist soul can depict.</p>  
 <p>Yet rise, oh, my soul, to loftier heights</p>  
 <p>Unfettered be all thy wings!</p>  
 <p>Within thyself unfold all that thine enraptured</p>  
 <p><a name="page_36"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 36</font></a></p>  
 <p>spirit has beheld. Put from thee all that is unclean and impure!</p>  
 <p>Awake, oh, my soul, to loftier ideals!</p>  
 <p>Aroused be all thy latent powers!</p>  
 <p>Let thy destiny as revealed spur thee on to greater efforts, to nobler sacrifices!</p>  
 <p>Be clean, oh, soul; be pure, oh, soul!</p>  
 <p>Around thee shines the halo of immortal light!</p>  
 <p>Rise, then, oh, my soul, to loftier heights</p>  
 <p>Unfettered be all thy wings!</p>
