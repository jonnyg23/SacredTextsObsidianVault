 <p><a name="page_iii"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. iii</font></a> <a name="page_iv"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. iv</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">FOREWORD</h3>  
 <p>This is the first attempt ever made to present the teachings of the great Tibetan sage, Tilak.</p>  
 <p>The teachings of Tilak find their source in the Ancient Wisdomteachings that are ever passed from mentor to pupil.</p>  
 <p>On parchment leaves Tilak inscribed his occult experiences, and relates his remarkable association with the Invisible Hierarchy.</p>  
 <p>Tilak's great writings will be a revelation to all seeking light on life's enigma during these turbulent times.</p>
