 <p>
 This long-forgotten esoteric novella, written in the waning days of
 World War II, is a series of parables held together by a fictional
 Tibetan character, Tilak.
 The author expounds on a diverse set of
 esoteric themes, including reincarnation, soulmates,
 auras, karma, lost continents,
 and ascended masters.
 Although plainly written, fans of the genre will enjoy this short book,
 which appears on the Internet for the first time at sacred-texts.
 </p>
