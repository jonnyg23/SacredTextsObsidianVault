 <p>I stood at eventime. The never-ending plain<br>   All empty looked and void. Yet, as I gazed again<br>   An army bivouacked. Unnumbered points of light<br>   Bespoke a force Supreme—invincible for Right.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>
