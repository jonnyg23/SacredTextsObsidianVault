 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Comte de Gabalis</i> [1913], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">BOOK OF THE WARS OF THE LORD.</h3>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_II">II</a></font></span>"Wherefore it is said in the book of the Wars of the Lord, What he did in the Red Sea, and in the brooks of Arnon." <small>NUMBERS xxi, 14</small>.</p>  
 <p>This is the only known reference to this work extant. From its title, Hebrew scholars conclude that the Book of the Wars of the Lord contained songs celebrating the victories of the Israelites under the leadership of J HWH. Modern critics regard Numbers xxi, verses 17-18, 27, and following, as extracts from this book.</p>
