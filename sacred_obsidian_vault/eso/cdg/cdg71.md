 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Comte de Gabalis</i> [1913], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">779 A.D. AGOBARD, 840 A.D.</h3>  <h3 align="center">BISHOP OF LYONS.</h3>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_HHH">HHH</a></font></span>The record of the date of the birth inscribed by his own hand is extant in Bede's Martyrology, which is preserved at Rome. This book states that Agobard was born in Spain and came to France when three years of age. At thirty-seven he became Archbishop of Lyons, and was one of the most celebrated and learned prelates of the ninth century.</p>
