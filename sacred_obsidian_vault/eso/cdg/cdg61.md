 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Comte de Gabalis</i> [1913], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">TYRESIAS,</h3>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_XX">XX</a></font></span>A native of Thebes, was the most celebrated prophet of all antiquity, and during his lifetime honoured as a God. His daughter Manto instituted the famous Oracle of the Clarian Apollo which foretold the untimely death of Germanicus.</p>
