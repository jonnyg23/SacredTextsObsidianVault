 <h3 align="center" style="text-align:center">THE SECRET TEACHINGS OF ALL AGES:
 COPYRIGHT STATUS.</h3>
 
 <p class="MsoNormal"><p> </p></p>
 
 <p class="MsoNormal">There is more than sufficient evidence online to validate
 the claim that the work "The Secret Teachings of All Ages," published
 in 1928, is currently in the public domain. This is based on the legal
 requirement that a work published during the period 1923-1962 (inclusive) had
 to be renewed at the US Copyright Office in the 28th year. In practice, the
 renewals for books published in 1928 were published in 1955 through 1957.</p>
 
 <p class="MsoNormal"><p> </p></p>
 
 <p class="MsoNormal">If no renewal is found, the book's copyright has lapsed. We
 believe that this can be more than adequately demonstrated using online
 resources.</p>
