 <h3><a name="eris">Erisian</a></h3>
 <p>
 This loosely affiliated set of anarchistic belief systems
 can best be described as a dadaist cult
 of the Goddess of Chaos, Eris.
 It is closely associated with the contemporary counter-culture,
 borrowing heavily from (or attributing its beliefs to)
 Paganism, Shamanism, Ritual Magic, and Cybernetics.
 </p>
 
 <p>
 <a href="#" onclick="window.open('../cdshop/index.htm','cartwin','');"><img src="../img/buycd.jpg" height="20" border="0"></a>
 
 <span class="c_e">
 <span class="c_t"><a href="pridisc.htm">Principia Discordia</a></span><br>
 <i>133,998 bytes</i>.<br>
 <span class="c_b">A starting point for Erisianism, one of the first 'copyleft' documents.</span><br>
 <span class="c_a">Robert Anton Wilson</span> had a hand in this text (going beyond just writing the introduction),
 </span>
 </p>
 
 <p>
 <a href="#" onclick="window.open('../cdshop/index.htm','cartwin','');"><img src="../img/buycd.jpg" height="20" border="0"></a>
 
 <span class="c_e">
 <span class="c_t"><a href="taz.htm">The Temporary Autonomous Zone, Ontological Anarchy, Poetic Terrorism</a></span><br>
 by <span class="c_a">Hakim Bey</span> <i>287,797 bytes</i><br>
 <span class="c_b">Hakim Bey's anarchistic-shamanistic-neotribal views greatly influenced rave culture and the tatooing/body piercing craze of the nineties.</span>
 </span>
 </p>
 
 
 <span class="c_e">
 <p>
 <span class="c_t"><a href="bob/subman.htm">The SubGenius Manifesto</a></span>
 <br>
 <span class="c_a">Bob Dobbs</span>,<br>
 <span class="c_b">The ultimate Dada religion, the Church of the SubGenius, the cult of 'Bob Dobbs', not for the irony-deficient.</span>
 </p></span>
 
 <span class="c_e">
 The entire body of SubGenius literature is now
