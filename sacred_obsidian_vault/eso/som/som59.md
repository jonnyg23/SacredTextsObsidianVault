 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">The Science of Mind, by Ernest Shurtleff Holmes [1926], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">THE POWER OF THE WORD</h3>  
 <p align="center"><b>I CONTROL MY MENTAL HOUSEHOLD</b> <b>AND</b> <b>CONQUER ALL FEAR AND DOUBT</b></p>  
 <p>I conquer my mental household and cast out all fear and doubt.<br> Let my Word cast out all sense of fear and doubt and let my thoughts be lifted unto Him Who lives Within.<br> My Word has dissolved all fear within me, and has cast out all doubt.</p>  
 <p><a name="page_366"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 366</font></a></p>  
 <p>My Word shall guard my thought and make me receive only that which is Good and Perfect.</p>  
 <p align="center"><i>I control my life</i>.</p>  
 <p> </p>  
 <p align="center"><b>MY WORD COMES BACK TO ME</b></p>  
 <p>My word comes back to me laden with the fruits of its own speech.<br> My Word is the Law unto my Life, and the Law unto everything that I speak.<br> O Word, go forth and heal and bless all humanity.<br> Tell them of their Divine Birthright.<br> Tell the stranger that he is not alone, but that One goes with him Who knows and cares.<br> Tell the sick that they are healed and the poor that they cannot want.<br> Tell the unhappy of the joy of the Soul, and break the bonds of those who are in prison.</p>  
 <p align="center">My Word shall come back to me blessed of God and man<i>.</i></p>  
 <p> </p>  
 <p align="center"><b>MY WORD SHALL BEAR FRUIT</b></p>  
 <p>The Word of my mouth shall bear fruit.<br> It shall accomplish and prosper, and shall not return unto me void.<br> My Word is the law unto the thing whereunto it is sent, and it cannot come back empty-handed.<br> I send out my Word, and it is law unto my life.</p>  
 <p align="center"><i>My Word is Power</i>.</p>  
 <p> </p>  
 <p align="center"><b>NOTHING CAN HINDER</b></p>  
 <p>Nothing can hinder my Word from working;<br> It will work, and nothing can stop it.</p>  
 <p><a name="page_367"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 367</font></a></p>  
 <p>My word is the Law unto that thing whereunto it is spoken, and will become fulfilled in the right way and at the right time.<br> My Word is complete and perfect, and is the presence and the<br> Power of the One Mind that is in and through all.<br> I speak that Word and know that it will accomplish.<br> I wait in perfect confidence for the Word to fulfill itself in my life.</p>  
 <p align="center"><i>My Word is law</i>.</p>  
 <p> </p>  
 <p align="center"><b>O MAN, SPEAK FORTH THY WORD</b></p>  
 <p>O man, speak forth thy word and be not afraid.<br> Did you not know; have you not heard?<br> His Divinity is planted within thee, and thy word is one with all power.<br> The Spirit of the Most High is thy Spirit, and the word of God is thy word.<br> Thy freedom is hid within thee, and thy inner light shall illumine they way.<br> Speak, man, and be free! Announce and proclaim thy works!<br> Let thy word go forth with power, and thy Spirit shall conquer all.</p>  
 <p align="center"><i>Spirit within me, speak</i>.</p>  
 <p> </p>  
 <p align="center"><b>THE POWER OF THE WORD</b></p>  
 <p>The Word is a might Power, and that Word is in me and through me now.<br> My Word is one with the All Good and cannot fail to accomplish the desired ends.<br> My Word goes forth with Power unto everything that I do, say or think.<br> The Word is my Power by day and by night.</p>  
 <p><a name="page_368"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 368</font></a></p>  
 <p>I will speak the Word and trust in the great Law of Life to fulfill it.</p>  
 <p align="center"><i>I speak the word in full confidence</i>.</p>  
 <p> </p>  
 <p align="center"><b>THE WORD OF POWER</b></p>  
 <p>My Word is a Word of Power, for I know that it is the Word of the Great God within me.<br> My Word shall accomplish and prosper, and shall do good unto all who call upon my name.<br> My Word is a tower of strength and cannot be denied.<br> It is complete and perfect here and now.<br> My Word is the Word of God.</p>  
 <p align="center"><i>My word is the word of God</i>.</p>  
 <p> </p>  
 <p align="center"><b>THE UNASSAILABLE TRUTH</b> <b>AND</b> <b>THE IRRESISTIBLE WORD</b></p>  
 <p>The Truth within me is unassailable, and the Power of the Word is irresistible.<br> I can even now feel that my Word has gone forth with Power and Reality, and that it will accomplish that purpose for which it was created.<br> Limitless is its Power and wonderful are its works.<br> It can be nothing less than the Almighty working in and through me.<br> I will let this Word of the Spirit go forth from my mouth, and heal and bless the world.<br> It shall be as a strong tower unto all who call upon it.<br> The Truth is Complete and Perfect, and is within me now.</p>  
 <p align="center"><i>My Word is complete and perfect, now</i>.</p>  
 <p> </p>  
 <p><a name="page_369"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 369</font></a></p>  
 <p align="center"><b>I BEHOLD IN THEE HIS IMAGE</b></p>  
 <p>I behold in thee His Image.<br> In thee, my friend, I see God and through you I feel His presence.<br> I see in the hand that gives, His hand;<br> And in the voice that speaks of Love, I hear Him speak.<br> For His lines have gone out into all places,<br> And from the highest to the lowest, all, all partake of His nature.<br> "For He is all in all, over all and through all."</p>  
 <p align="center"><i>I perceive that God is in all people</i>.</p>
