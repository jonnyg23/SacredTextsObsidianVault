 <p align="center"><b>PEACE BE UNTO THEE, STRANGER</b></p>  
 <p></p><dir>eace be unto thee, stranger, enter and be not afraid.<br> I have left the gate open and thou art welcome to my home.<br> There is room in my house for all.<br> I have swept the hearth and lighted the fire.<br> The room is warm and cheerful and you will find comfort and rest within.<br> The table is laid and the fruits of Life are spread before thee.<br> The wine is here also, it sparkles in the light.<br> I have set a chair for you where the sunbeams dance through the shade.<br> Sit and rest and refresh your soul.<br> Eat of the fruit and drink the wine.<br> All, all is yours, and you are welcome.</dir>
