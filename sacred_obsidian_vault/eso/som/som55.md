 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">The Science of Mind, by Ernest Shurtleff Holmes [1926], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">IMMANENT POWER</h3>  
 <p align="center"><b>AS LOVE ENTERS, FEAR DEPARTS</b></p>  
 <p>As Love enters, fear vanishes.<br> I am so filled with Love that no fear can enter my thought.<br> I am not afraid, for I know that a Perfect Intelligence guards and governs my every act.<br> Perfect Love casteth out all fear.</p>  
 <p><a name="page_345"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 345</font></a></p>  
 <p>I am unafraid and strong in my faith in Him Who keeps me from all harm.</p>  
 <p align="center"><i>Perfect Love casteth out all fear</i>.</p>  
 <p> </p>  
 <p align="center"><b>HE WILL KEEP THEE</b></p>  
 <p>The Inner Spirit of man is God, and my Inner Spirit is the Realization of my Life.<br> I know that my Inner Spirit will keep me from all harm, and will not let destruction come near me.<br> I am unafraid in the midst of confusion, and unmoved in the face of calamity.<br> I am confident in the presence of seeming danger, and fearless before any sense of trouble.</p>  
 <p align="center"><i>He will keep me</i>.</p>  
 <p> </p>  
 <p align="center"><b>INFINITE LIFE WITHIN</b></p>  
 <p>Infinite Life within me, which is God, guard Thou my feet and keep Thou my way.<br> Let me not stray from Thee, but compel me to do Thy will.<br> I am guarded and governed by an Infinite Intelligence and an Omnipotent Power.<br> No mistakes can be made and none ever have been made.<br> An unerring judgement operates through me and I am lead by the Spirit of Truth into all Good and into all Peace and Happiness.</p>  
 <p align="center"><i>Infinite Life within me</i>.</p>  
 <p> </p>  
 <p align="center"><b>MY FEET SHALL NOT FALTER</b></p>  
 <p>My feet shall not falter, for they are kept upon the path of Life through the Power of the Eternal Spirit.</p>  
 <p><a name="page_346"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 346</font></a></p>  
 <p>Guide Thou my feet; compel my way; direct my paths and keep me in Thy Presence.<br> My feet are guarded, and I am guided into the All Good.</p>  
 <p align="center"><i>He guides my feet</i>.</p>  
 <p> </p>  
 <p align="center"><b>NO HARM SHALL BEFALL THEE</b></p>  
 <p>No harm shall befall you, my friend, for a Divine Presence attends your way and guards you into The All Good.<br> Loving kindness awaits you at every turn of Life's road.<br> Guidance is yours along the pathway of experience,<br> And an Infallible Power protects you.<br> God, Himself, and no other is your Keeper.</p>  
 <p align="center"><i>I proclaim this for you</i>.</p>  
 <p> </p>  
 <p align="center"><b>POWER TO LIVE</b></p>  
 <p>I have the power to live the life of good.<br> My power is from On High; it cannot be taken from me.<br> It will not leave me desolate.<br> Power flows through me and is in me, and<br> I can now feel and sense it.<br> The Power to live is in me and I cannot desert me.<br> It is my power and is continually present.</p>  
 <p align="center"><i>I am the power to live</i>.</p>  
 <p> </p>  
 <p align="center"><b>THE CIRCLE OF LOVE</b></p>  
 <p>A circle of love is drawn around me and mine, and all.<br> No harm can enter that Sacred Circle, for it is the Love of God.</p>  
 <p><a name="page_347"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 347</font></a></p>  
 <p>It is a complete protection from all evil.<br> "I will fear no evil, for Thou art with me."<br> There is no evil and no harm.<br> I am free from all sense of fear.</p>  
 <p align="center"><i>Love surrounds and protects me</i>.</p>  
 <p> </p>  
 <p align="center"><b>THE CIRCLE OF PROTECTION</b></p>  
 <p>I draw around me a circle of love and protection.<br> No harm can enter nor find place within that charmed circle of life and love, for it represents God's Loving Care and Eternal Watchfulness.<br> I will rest within me now, and I will speak comfort to my Soul and tell It of all the wonders of its life, safe from the din of strife and fear.</p>  
 <p align="center"><i>I am protected from On High</i>.</p>  
 <p> </p>  
 <p align="center"><b>THE POWER WITHIN BLESSES ALL</b></p>  
 <p>The Power within me is blessing all mankind, and is forever healing all with whom I come in contact.<br> The Power within me is God, and It must bless and help and heal all who come near It.<br> Silently the work goes on, and silently all are being helped by this Inner Power which is operating through me.<br> I will give thanks that my Power within is silently blessing and helping everyone to whom my thought reaches.</p>  
 <p align="center"><i>The Life within me blesses all mankind</i>.</p>  
 <p> </p>  
 <p align="center"><b>THE QUICK ANSWER</b></p>  
 <p>My answer comes quickly and surely back to me from On High.</p>  
 <p><a name="page_348"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 348</font></a></p>  
 <p>My answer will not fail me, for the Law of the Universe is the Power through which it comes.<br> I shall not doubt nor fear, for the answer is swift and certain.</p>  
 <p align="center"><i>My answer comes</i>.</p>
