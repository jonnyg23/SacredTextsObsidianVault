 <p><a name="page_iii"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. iii</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">Logical Structure of this Work.</h3>  
 <p align="center"><a name="img_struct"></a>
 <img src="img/struct.jpg"></p>  
 <p>The facts in nature are the foundation out of which all consciousness and knowledge grow. Science is the outgrowth of these facts through careful investigation and study. Revelation is facts derived from the inner-consciousnessintuitionwhich is allied to the cause-world and is cognizant of the mind from which it originated, or from messengers (angels) from God.</p>  
 <p>Revelation united with science gives a substantial foundation for philosophy and theology, which are the proper outgrowth of <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">science and revelation</span>.</p>  
 <p align="center">Philosophy is the mathematics of reason applied.</p>
