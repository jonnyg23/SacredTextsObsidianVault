 <p><a name="page_110"><font size="1" color="green">p. 110</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">SOME HINDOO USES OF NUMBERS.</h3>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">In</span> ancient India, in the Sanscrit language, certain words were used as equivalent to the low numbers: for One they said Moon or Earth: for Two they used many words of things in pairs such as eye, wing, arm: for Three they used Rama, fire or guna, for they knew of 3 Ramas, 3 kinds of fire and 3 gunas or qualities: for 4 they used Veda, age or ocean: for 6 they used Seasons: for 7 they used Sage or vowel: for 12 they said Sun or Zodiac; and for 20 they used nails, meaning those of hands and feet. Edward B. Tylor.</p>
