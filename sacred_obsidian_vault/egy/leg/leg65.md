 <p>[§ XLIII. The height of the Nile in flood at Elephantine is twenty-eight cubits, at Mendes and Xoïs low Nile is seven cubits, and at Memphis middle Nile is fourteen cubits; these figures are to be compared with the twenty-eight days of the Moon's revolution, the seven-day phase of the Moon, and the fourteen days' Moon, or full moon. Apis was begotten by a ray of light from the Moon, and on the fourteenth day of the month Phamenoth <a name="fr_344"></a><a href="leg66.htm#fn_342"><font size="1">1</font></a> Osiris entered the Moon. Osiris is the power of the Moon, Isis the productive faculty in it.]</p>
