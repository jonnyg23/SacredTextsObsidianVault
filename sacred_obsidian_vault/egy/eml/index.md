 <p>
 This highly readable book covers
 Egyptian religion, history,
 and culture through its entire civilization.
 We are accustomed to history measured in decades or centuries.
 Egypt requires thinking in terms of millenia.
 There was not one monolithic Egyptian belief system; it went through
 profound changes over time; this book describes this evolution in
 great detail.
 Mackenzie includes many extracts from religious texts, folk tales,
 and historical documents.
