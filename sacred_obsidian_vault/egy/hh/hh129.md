 <p><a name="page_120"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 120</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">LVI. HOW A KING WHO KEEPS HIMSELF APART, AND SHEWS NO MERCY TO DELINQUENCIES.</h3>  
 <p align="center"><a name="img_12000"></a>
 <img src="img/12000.jpg"> <a name="fr_123"></a><a href="#fn_118"><font size="1">1</font></a></p>  
 <p>When they would symbolise <i>a king who keeps himself apart</i>, <a name="fr_124"></a><a href="#fn_119"><font size="1">2</font></a> <i>and shews no mercy to delinquencies</i>, <a name="fr_125"></a><a href="#fn_120"><font size="1">3</font></a> they depict an EAGLE; for he builds his nest in desert places, and flies higher than all birds.</p>
