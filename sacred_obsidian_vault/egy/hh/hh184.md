 <p align="center"><a name="img_15301"></a>
 <img src="img/15301.jpg"></p>  
 <p>When they would symbolise <i>a man that has commerce with persons of another tribe</i>,</p>  
 <p><a name="page_154"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 154</font></a></p>  
 <p>they depict the LAMPREY; because it ascends out of the sea, and has commerce with the vipers, and straightway returns to the sea.</p>
