 <p><a name="page_147"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 147</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">C. HOW A MAN WHO IS TARDY IN MOVING WITH HIS FEET.</h3>  
 <p align="center"><a name="img_14700"></a>
 <img src="img/14700.jpg"></p>  
 <p>When they would symbolise <i>a man who is tardy in moving with his feet</i>, they delineate A CAMEL <a name="fr_144"></a><a href="#fn_139"><font size="1">1</font></a>; for this is the only animal which bends the thigh, whence it is called κάμηλος <a name="fr_145"></a><a href="#fn_140"><font size="1">2</font></a>, a camel.</p>
