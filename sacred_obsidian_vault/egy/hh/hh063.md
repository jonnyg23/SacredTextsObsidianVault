 <p align="center"><a name="img_08101"></a>
 <img src="img/08101.jpg"> <a name="fr_89"></a><a href="#fn_87"><font size="1">1</font></a></p>  
 <p>Again when they would</p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p><a name="page_82"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 82</font></a></p>  
 <p>signify and designate a <i>king who rules the world</i>, they delineate the same SERPENT, and in the middle thereof they represent a LARGE HOUSE, and with reason for the royal abode from him - - - - - - in the world.</p>
