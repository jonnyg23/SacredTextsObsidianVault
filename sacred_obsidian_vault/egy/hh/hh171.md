 <p align="center"><a name="img_14500"></a>
 <img src="img/14500.jpg"></p>  
 <p>When they would symbolise <i>a man skilled in celestial matters</i>, they depict A CRANE FLYING;  <a name="fr_142"></a><a href="#fn_137"><font size="1">2</font></a> for she always flies very high, to inspect the clouds lest they send forth a storm, that she may remain in quiet. <a name="fr_143"></a><a href="#fn_138"><font size="1">3</font></a></p>
