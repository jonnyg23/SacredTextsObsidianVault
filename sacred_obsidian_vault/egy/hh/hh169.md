 <p align="center"><a name="img_14401"></a>
 <img src="img/14401.jpg"></p>  
 <p>When they would symbolise <i>an old man dying of hunger</i>, they delineate AN EAGLE WITH HIS BEAK EXTREMELY HOOKED; for as he grows old his beak becomes extremely hooked, and he dies of hunger.</p>
