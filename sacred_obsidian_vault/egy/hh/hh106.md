 <p><a name="page_108"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 108</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">XXXIII. WHAT BY AN ICHNEUMON.</h3>  
 <p align="center"><a name="img_10800"></a>
 <img src="img/10800.jpg"></p>  
 <p>When they would represent <i>a man that is feeble, and unable of himself to help himself, but who does so by the aid of others</i>, they delineate an ICHNEUMON. For this animal, when it spies a serpent, does not at once attack it, but by its noise calls others to its assistance, and then attacks the serpent.</p>
