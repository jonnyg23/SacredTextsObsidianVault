 <p align="center"><a name="img_08401"></a>
 <img src="img/08401.jpg"> <a name="fr_96"></a><a href="#fn_91"><font size="1">2</font></a></p>  
 <p>To represent a <i>month</i>, they depict as before explained the figure of the MOON when it has attained the age of eight and twenty days of equal lengths, each day containing twenty-four</p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p><a name="page_85"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 85</font></a></p>  
 <p>hours, for during these it is apparent, and in the remaining two it is in a state of evanescence.</p>
