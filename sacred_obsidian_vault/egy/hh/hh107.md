 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Hieroglyphics of Horapollo</i>, tr. Alexander Turner Cory, [1840], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">XXXIV. WHAT THEY DENOTE BY ENGRAVING ORIGANUM (WILD MARJORAM) FOR A HIEROGLYPHIC.</h3>  
 <p align="center"><a name="img_10801"></a>
 <img src="img/10801.jpg"></p>  
 <p>When they would symbolise the <i>departure of ants</i>, they engrave ORIGANUM. For if this plant be laid down over the spot from whence the ants issue forth, it causes them to desert it.</p>
