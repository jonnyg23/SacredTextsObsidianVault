 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Hieroglyphics of Horapollo</i>, tr. Alexander Turner Cory, [1840], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">XLIII. HOW THEY DENOTE A WOMAN WHO HAS BROUGHT FORTH FEMALE INFANTS.</h3>  
 <p align="center"><a name="img_11301"></a>
 <img src="img/11301.jpg"></p>  
 <p>When they would symbolise a woman who has brought forth female infants first, they delineate a BULL INCLINING TOWARDS THE LEFT: and again if male infants, then they delineate a BULL inclining to the right. Is enim ex congressu discedens, si ad lævam</p>  
 <p><a name="page_114"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 114</font></a></p>  
 <p>conversus fuerit, genitam esse fœminam indicat, si ad dexteram marem.</p>
