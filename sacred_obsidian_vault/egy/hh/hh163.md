 <p><a name="page_141"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 141</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">XC. HOW A MAN WHO CONCEALS HIS DEPRAVITY WITHIN HIMSELF.</h3>  
 <p align="center"><a name="img_14100"></a>
 <img src="img/14100.jpg"></p>  
 <p>When they would denote <i>a man who conceals his depravity within himself</i>, and hides himself so as not to be known by his own friends and family, they depict a PANTHER; for it secretly pursues the beasts, not suffering its scent to escape, which is [a hindrance in the pursuit fl of other animals.</p>
