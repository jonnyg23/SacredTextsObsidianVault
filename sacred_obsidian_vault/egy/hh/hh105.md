 <p align="center"><a name="img_10701"></a>
 <img src="img/10701.jpg"></p>  
 <p>When they would symbolise <i>a woman who remains a widow till death</i>, they depict a BLACK DOVE; for this bird has no connexion with another mate from the time that it is widowed.</p>
