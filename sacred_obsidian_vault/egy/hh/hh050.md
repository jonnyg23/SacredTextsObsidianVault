 <img src="img/06800.jpg"> <a name="fr_80"></a><a href="#fn_78"><font size="1">1</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">XLVIII. HOW THE MEMBER OF A PROLIFIC MAN.</h3>  
 <p>To denote the <i>member of a prolific man</i>, they depict a</p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p><a name="page_69"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 69</font></a></p>  
 <p>GOAT, and not a bull: ille enim antequam annum attigerit, coire non solet: hic septem statim post ortum diebus congreditur, infœcundum et genituræ minime accommodum semen excernens. Prius tamen ac celerius cæteris animantibus coit.</p>
