 <p><a name="page_102"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 102</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">XXI. How Anything of Long Duration.</h3>  
 <p align="center"><a name="img_10200"></a>
 <img src="img/10200.jpg"> <a name="fr_109"></a><a href="#fn_104"><font size="1">1</font></a> <a name="fr_110"></a><a href="#fn_105"><font size="1">2</font></a></p>  
 <p>A STAG shoots its horns every year, and when depicted, signifies anything of <i>long duration</i>.</p>
