 <p><a name="page_112"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 112</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">XL. HOW THEY DENOTE A MAN WHO LIVES WITH HIS WIFE.</h3>  
 <p align="center"><a name="img_11200"></a>
 <img src="img/11200.jpg"></p>  
 <p>When they would symbolise <i>a man living in intercourse with his own wife</i>, they depict TWO CROWS; for these birds cohabit with one another in the same manner as does a man by nature.</p>
