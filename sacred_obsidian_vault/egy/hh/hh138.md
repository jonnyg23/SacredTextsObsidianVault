 <p align="center"><a name="img_12600"></a>
 <img src="img/12600.jpg"></p>  
 <p>When they would symbolise <i>a man injured by self inflictions</i>, they delineate a BEAVER; for when pursued he tears out his own testicles, and casts them as spoil to his pursuers.</p>
