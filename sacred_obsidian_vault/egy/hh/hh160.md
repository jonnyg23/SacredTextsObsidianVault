 <p align="center"><a name="img_13900"></a>
 <img src="img/13900.jpg"></p>  
 <p>When they would symbolise <i>a man that is quick in his movements, but who moves without prudence and consideration</i>, they portray A STAG AND A VIPER; for she flees at the sight of the viper.</p>
