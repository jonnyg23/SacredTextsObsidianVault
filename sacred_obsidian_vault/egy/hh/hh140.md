 <p align="center"><a name="img_12700"></a>
 <img src="img/12700.jpg"></p>  
 <p>When they would symbolise <i>a man that conceals his own defects</i>, they depict AN APE <a name="fr_128"></a><a href="#fn_123"><font size="1">1</font></a> MAKING WATER; for when he makes water he conceals his urine.</p>
