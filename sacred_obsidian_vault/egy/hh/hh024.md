 <p><a name="page_45"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 45</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">XXII. HOW THEY DESIGNATE EGYPT.</h3>  
 <p align="center"><a name="img_04500"></a>
 <img src="img/04500.jpg"> <a name="fr_49"></a><a href="#fn_47"><font size="1">1</font></a></p>  
 <p>To designate <i>Egypt</i>, <a name="fr_50"></a><a href="#fn_48"><font size="1">2</font></a> they depict a BURNING CENSER AND A HEART. ABOVE IT, implying, that as the heart of a jealous person is constantly inflamed, so Egypt from its heat perpetually vivifies the things which are in or near it.</p>
