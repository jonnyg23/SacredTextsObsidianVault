 <p align="center"><a name="img_15400"></a>
 <img src="img/15400.jpg"></p>  
 <p>When they would symbolise <i>a man punished for murder and repenting</i>, they depict a PARTINACA (a fish with a sting in its tail,) CAUGHT ON A HOOK; for when caught it casts away the sting in its tail.</p>
