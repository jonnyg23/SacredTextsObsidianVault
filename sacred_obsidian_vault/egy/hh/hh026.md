 <p align="center"><a name="img_04601"></a>
 <img src="img/04601.jpg"> <a name="fr_52"></a><a href="#fn_49"><font size="1">2</font></a></p>  
 <p>When they would denote an <i>amulet</i>, they pourtray TWO</p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p><a name="page_47"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 47</font></a></p>  
 <p><span class="contnote"><font size="-2" color="GREEN">[paragraph continues]</font></span> HUMAN HEADS, one of a male looking inwards, the other of a female looking outwards, (for they say that no demon will interfere with any person thus guarded); for without inscriptions they protect themselves with the two heads.</p>
