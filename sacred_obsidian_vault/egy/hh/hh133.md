 <p align="center"><a name="img_12301"></a>
 <img src="img/12301.jpg"></p>  
 <p>When they would denote <i>children plotting against their mothers</i>, they delineate a VIPER; for the viper is not</p>  
 <p><a name="page_124"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 124</font></a></p>  
 <p>brought forth in the [usual manner?], but disengages itself by gnawing through the belly of its mother.</p>
