 <p><a name="page_113"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 113</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">XLII. WHAT THEY DESIGN BY DEPICTING A MULE.</h3>  
 <p align="center"><a name="img_11300"></a>
 <img src="img/11300.jpg"></p>  
 <p>When they would symbolise <i>a barren woman</i>, they delineate a MULE; for this animal is barren, quod matricem rectam non habet.</p>
