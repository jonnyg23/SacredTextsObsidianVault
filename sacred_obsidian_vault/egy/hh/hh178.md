 <p><a name="page_150"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 150</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">CV. HOW A MAN THAT WASTEFULLY CONSUMES BOTH THINGS THAT ARE REQUISITE AND THINGS THAT ARE NOT.</h3>  
 <p align="center"><a name="img_15000"></a>
 <img src="img/15000.jpg"></p>  
 <p>When they would symbolise <i>a man that wastefully consumes both things that are requisite and useless</i>, they delineate a POLYPUS; for after eating much and extravagantly, it lays by food in its holes, and when it has consumed that which is useful, it then throws away that which is useless.</p>
