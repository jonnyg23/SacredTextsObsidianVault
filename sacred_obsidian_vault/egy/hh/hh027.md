 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Hieroglyphics of Horapollo</i>, tr. Alexander Turner Cory, [1840], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">XXV. HOW THEY DENOTE AN IMPERFECT MAN.</h3>  
 <p align="center"><a name="img_04700"></a>
 <img src="img/04700.jpg"> <a name="fr_53"></a><a href="#fn_51"><font size="1">1</font></a></p>  
 <p>To denote an imperfect man, they delineate a FROG, because it is generated from the slime of the river, whence</p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p><a name="page_48"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 48</font></a></p>  
 <p>it occasionally happens that it is seen with one part of a frog, and the remainder formed of slime, so that should the river fall, the animal would be left imperfect.</p>
