 <p><a name="page_157"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 157</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">CXVII. HOW A MAN PREVIOUSLY DERANGED IN HIS INTELLECTS, BUT AFTERWARDS BECOMING SANE.</h3>  
 <p align="center"><a name="img_15700"></a>
 <img src="img/15700.jpg"></p>  
 <p>When they would symbolise <i>a man previously deranged in his intellects, but afterwards becoming sane, and bringing a degree of regularity into his life</i>, they depict a SYRINX; for it is soothing, and calls to remembrance things that have been pleasurably done; and it produces a very regulated sound.</p>
