 <p align="center"><a name="img_14101"></a>
 <img src="img/14101.jpg"></p>  
 <p>When they would symbolise <i>a man deceived by flattery</i>, they represent A STAG AND A MAN PLAYING ON A PIPE; for she is caught while</p>  
 <p><a name="page_142"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 142</font></a></p>  
 <p>listening to the sweet breathings of the singers as she stands entranced in pleasure.</p>
