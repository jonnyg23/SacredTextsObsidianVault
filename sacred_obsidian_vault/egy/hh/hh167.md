 <p align="center"><a name="img_14300"></a>
 <img src="img/14300.jpg"> <a name="fr_139"></a><a href="#fn_134"><font size="1">1</font></a></p>  
 <p>When they would symbolise <i>a man that guards himself from the plots of his enemies</i>, they depict A CRANE ON THE WATCH; for these birds guard themselves by watching in turns during the whole night.</p>
