 <p align="center"><a name="img_13800"></a>
 <img src="img/13800.jpg"></p>  
 <p>When they would symbolise <i>a king that flees from folly and intemperance</i>, they delineate AN ELEPHANT <a name="fr_137"></a><a href="#fn_132"><font size="1">1</font></a> AND A RAM; for he flees at the sight of a ram.</p>
