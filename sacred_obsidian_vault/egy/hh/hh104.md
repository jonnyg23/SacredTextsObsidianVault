 <p><a name="page_107"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 107</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">XXXI. WHAT THEY DENOTE BY DEPICTING A SWALLOW.</h3>  
 <p align="center"><a name="img_10700"></a>
 <img src="img/10700.jpg"> <a name="fr_120"></a><a href="#fn_115"><font size="1">1</font></a></p>  
 <p>When they would signify that <i>the whole of a parent's substance has been left to the sons</i>, they depict a SWALLOW. For she rolls herself in the mud, and builds a nest for her young, when she is herself about to die.</p>
