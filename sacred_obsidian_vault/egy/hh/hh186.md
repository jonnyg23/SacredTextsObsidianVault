 <p align="center"><a name="img_15401"></a>
 <img src="img/15401.jpg"></p>  
 <p>When they would symbolise <i>a man that eats unsparingly </i></p>  
 <p><a name="page_155"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 155</font></a></p>  
 <p><i>of another's substance, and afterwards consumes his own</i>, they depict a POLYPUS; for, if he be in want of food from other things, he eats his own feelers.</p>
