 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Hieroglyphics of Horapollo</i>, tr. Alexander Turner Cory, [1840], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">XLI. WHAT THEY INTIMATE BY POURTRAYING A BLIND BEETLE.</h3>  
 <p align="center"><a name="img_11201"></a>
 <img src="img/11201.jpg"></p>  
 <p>When they would symbolise <i>a man who has caught a fever from a stroke of the sun, and died in consequence</i>, they portray a BLIND BEETLE; for this creature dies after it has been blinded by the sun.</p>
