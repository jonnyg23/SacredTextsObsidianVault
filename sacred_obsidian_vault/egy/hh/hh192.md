 <p><a name="page_159"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 159</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">CXIX. HOW A MAN THAT IS FOND OF BUILDING.</h3>  
 <p align="center"><a name="img_15900"></a>
 <img src="img/15900.jpg"> <a name="fr_147"></a><a href="#fn_142"><font size="1">1</font></a></p>  
 <p>When they would symbolise <i>a man that is fond of building</i>, they delineate A MAN'S HAND; for it performs all works.</p>  
 <p> </p>  
 <p> </p>
