 <p align="center"><a name="img_12801"></a>
 <img src="img/12801.jpg"></p>  
 <p>When they would symbolise <i>one that is unsettled</i>, and that does not remain in the same state, but is sometimes strong, and at other times weak, they depict an HYÆNA; for this creature is at times male, and at times female. <a name="fr_129"></a><a href="#fn_124"><font size="1">1</font></a></p>
