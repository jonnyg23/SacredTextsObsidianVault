 <p><a name="page_46"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 46</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">XXIII. HOW A MAN THAT HAS NOT TRAVELLED ABROAD.</h3>  
 <p align="center"><a name="img_04600"></a>
 <img src="img/04600.jpg"></p>  
 <p>To symbolize <i>a man that has not travelled out of his own country</i>, they delineate an ONOCEPHALUS [creature with an ass's head], because he is neither acquainted with history, nor conversant with foreign affairs.</p>
