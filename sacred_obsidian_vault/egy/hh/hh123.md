 <p><a name="page_117"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 117</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">L. HOW A MAN WHO IS WEAK AND PERSECUTED BY ANOTHER.</h3>  
 <p align="center"><a name="img_11700"></a>
 <img src="img/11700.jpg"></p>  
 <p>When they would symbolise <i>a man that is weak and persecuted by a stronger</i>, they delineate a BUSTARD AND A HORSE; for this bird flies away whenever it sees a horse.</p>
