 <p align="center"><a name="img_09300"></a>
 <img src="img/09300.jpg"></p>  
 <p>When a STAR is depicted by the Egyptians, it sometimes symbolizes <i>God</i>, sometimes evening, sometimes <i>night</i>, sometimes <i>time</i>, and sometimes the <i>soul</i> of a male man.</p>
