 <p align="center"><a name="img_14901"></a>
 <img src="img/14901.jpg"></p>  
 <p>When they would symbolise <i>a man who saves many in the sea</i>, they depict the TORPEDO FISH; for this, when it perceives a number of fishes unable to swim, draws them to itself and preserves them.</p>
