 <p><a name="page_51"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 51</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">XXIX. HOW A VOICE FROM A DISTANCE.</h3>  
 <p align="center"><a name="img_05100"></a>
 <img src="img/05100.jpg"></p>  
 <p>When they would symbolise a voice <i>from a distance</i>, which is called by the Egyptians Ouaie, they portray the VOICE OF THE AIR, i. e. THUNDER, than which nothing utters a greater or more powerful voice.</p>
