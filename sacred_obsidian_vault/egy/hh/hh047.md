 <p><a name="page_66"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 66</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">XLV. HOW THEY REPRESENT THE MOUTH.</h3>  
 <p align="center"><a name="img_06600"></a>
 <img src="img/06600.jpg"></p>  
 <p>To represent the <i>mouth</i> they depict a SERPENT, because the serpent is powerful in no other of its members except the mouth alone.</p>
