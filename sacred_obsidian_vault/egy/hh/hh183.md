 <p align="center"><a name="img_15300"></a>
 <img src="img/15300.jpg"></p>  
 <p>When they would symbolise <i>a man that vomits up his food, and again eats insatiably</i>, they depict a SEAWEASEL; for it brings forth through its mouth, and drinks in the seed whilst swimming.</p>
