 <h1 align="CENTER">The Egyptian Heaven and Hell</h1>
 <h2 align="CENTER">by E. A. Wallis Budge</h2>
 <h4 align="CENTER">[1905]</h4>
 <h4 align="CENTER">(original title) The Contents of the Books of the Other World
 Described and Compared</h4>
 <p>
 This is the third volume in Budges' treatment of the Ancient
 Egyptian Books of the underworld, the Tuat.
 Budge, who among dozens of other books wrote the
 <cite>Cooks' Tour Guide to Egypt</cite>,
 collates the information from the Book of the Am-Tuat
 and the Book of Gates as a travel guide.
 </p>
