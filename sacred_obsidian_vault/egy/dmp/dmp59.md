   <h3 align="center">VERSO COL. XXVII.</h3>  
 <p>(1) According to that which is above within, saying, 'I am this Sit-ta-ko, Setem is my name, (2) Setem is my correct name. I am Gantha, Ginteu, Giriteu, (3) Hrinoute, Arinoute, Labtatha, Laptutha, (4) Laksantha, Sarisa, Markharahuteu, (5) Arsinga-khla; another volume (says) Arsinga-label, Bolboel, (6) Boel (<i>bis</i>), Loteri, Klogasantra, Iaho, (7) is my name, Iaho is my correct name,</p>  
 <p><a name="page_200"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 200</font></a> <a name="page_201"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 201</font></a></p>  
 <p><font size="-2" color="GREEN">[paragraph continues]</font> Balkham, the mighty (?) one of heaven, (8) Ablanathanalba, gryphon of the shrine of the god which stands to-day (?).'</p>
