   <h3 align="center">VERSO COL. V.</h3>  
 <p>(1) A medicament to stop blood: juice of 'Great Nile(?)' plant (2) together with beer; you make the woman drink it in the morning (3) before she has eaten; then it stops.</p>  
 <p>(4) The way to know it of a woman whether she is enceinte: you make the woman (5) pass her water on this herb as above again (6) in the evening; when the morning comes and if you find the plant (7) scorched (?), she will not conceive; if you find it (8) flourishing, she will conceive.</p>  
 <p>(9) A medicament to stop blood: Leaf of <i>sheisha</i>, (10) leaf of 'fly-bronze,' fresh; pound, put (it) (11) on you, you lie with the woman. Another: myrrh, (12) garlic, gall of a gazelle; pound with (13) old scented wine; put (it) on you, you lie with her.</p>  
 <p>(14) Asphodelos, (15) otherwise called 'wild onion.'</p>  
 <p><a name="page_178"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 178</font></a> <a name="page_179"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 179</font></a></p>  
 <p>(16) <i>Khelkebe</i>, (17) otherwise called 'wild garlic.'</p>
