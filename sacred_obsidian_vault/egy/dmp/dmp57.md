 <p>(1) ...... (2) hawk's dung, salt, <i>asi</i> plant, (3) <i>bel</i>, pound together, anoint (4) your phallus with it and lie with (5) the woman. If it is dry, you (6) pound a little of it with wine, and you (7) anoint your phallus with it (8) and you lie with the woman. Excellent (<i>bis</i>).</p>
