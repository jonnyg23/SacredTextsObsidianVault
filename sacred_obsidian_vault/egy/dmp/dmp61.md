   <h3 align="center">VERSO COL. XXIX.</h3>  
 <p>(1) [Spell to] make mad any man or any woman.</p>  
 <p>(2) You take the hair of the man whom you wish, together with the hair (3) of a dead (murdered?) man; and you tie them to each other, (4) and tie them to the body of a hawk, and you release (?) it (5) alive. If you wish to do it for some days, (6) you put the hawk in a place and you feed it in your house.</p>
