   <h3 align="center">COL. XXII.</h3>  
 <p>(1) Behold! (spell?) of the name of the Great-of-Five which they pronounce to every spirit. There is none</p>  
 <p><a name="page_144"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 144</font></a> <a name="page_145"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 145</font></a></p>  
 <p>that is (2) stronger than it in the books. If you pronounce these charms to any vessel, (3) then the gods depart not before you have questioned them concerning every word and they have told you (4) the answer about heaven, earth, and the underworld, a distant inquiry (?), water, (5) (and) the fields. A charm which is in the power (?) of a man to pronounce.</p>
