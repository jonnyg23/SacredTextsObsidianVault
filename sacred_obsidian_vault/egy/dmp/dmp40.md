   <h3 align="center">VERSO COL. VIII.</h3>  
 <p>(1) Gout. (2) You make the man sit down; you place clay under the feet of the man; (3) you put ..... to it(?), his feet resting on it; you ask (4) the man, saying, 'Has it hearkened?' for three days. Thereafter you take an ant (?), (5) you cook it in oil of henna; you anoint his feet (6) with it. When you have finished, you take Alexandrian figs and dried grapes (7) and potentilla; you pound them with wine; you anoint him besides (?) (8) these; and you blow on him with your mouth.</p>
