   <h3 align="center">VERSO COL. IV.</h3>  
 <p>(1) Medicament for an ear that is watery.</p>  
 <p>(2) Salt, heat with good wine; (3) you apply to it after cleansing (?) it first. (4) You scrape salt, heat with wine; (5) you apply to it for four days.</p>  
 <p>(6) σαλαμάνδρα, (7) a small lizard (8) which is of the colour of chrysolite. (9). It has no feet.</p>  
 <p>(10) 'Ram's horn,' κεφαλική is its name, (11) a herb which is like a wild fennel bush; (12) its leaf and its stem</p>  
 <p><a name="page_176"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 176</font></a> <a name="page_177"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 177</font></a></p>  
 <p>are incised like (13) the 'love-man' plant: you pound it when it is dry, you gather (?) it, (14) you make it into a dry powder; you apply it to any wound; then it is cured.</p>  
 <p>Styrax, (16) it grows like <i>slom</i> (?) (17) as to its leaf; its seed is twisted (18) like the 'ram's horn' plant, it bearing (19) a small spine at its end.</p>
