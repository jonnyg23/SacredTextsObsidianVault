   <h3 align="center">VERSO COL. XXX.</h3>  
 <p>(1) If you ........ dung of a <i>smoune</i>-goose, (2) then her body falls.</p>  
 <p>(3) Another: You anoint your phallus with dung of (4) a <i>kel</i>, and you lie with (the) woman, then she feels thy love (i.e. for thee). (5) You pound dung of ...... with honey, (6) and you anoint your phallus with it as above again.</p>  
 <p>(7) Another: dung of hyaena (?) with ointment of (8) roses as above again.</p>  
 <p>(9) Another: You fumigate a woman with ichneumon's dung (10) when the menstruation is on her; then she is cured.</p>  
 <p>(11) Ass's dung also--this method (of treatment).</p>
