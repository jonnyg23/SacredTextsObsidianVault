 <p><a name="page_168"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 168</font></a> <a name="page_169"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 169</font></a></p>  <h1 align="center">VERSO</h1>  <h3 align="center">VERSO COL. I.</h3>  
 <p>(1) Eyebrow of Ra: ὀφρὺς ἡλίου.(3) Eyebrow of the moon. ὀφρὺς σελήνης.(3) These are some herbs.</p>  
 <p>(4) <i>Heliogonon</i>. (5) <i>Selenegonon</i>. (6) These are some herbs.</p>  
 <p>(7) Spurge, (8) which is that small herb that is in the gardens (9) and which exudes milk.(10) If you put its milk on a man's skin, (11) it causes a blister.</p>
