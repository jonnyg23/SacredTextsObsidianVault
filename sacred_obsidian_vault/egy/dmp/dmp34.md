   <h3 align="center">VERSO COL. II.</h3>  
 <p>(1) <i>Chamaemelon</i>. 'Clean-straw' is its name.</p>  
 <p>(2) <i>Leucanthemon</i>. 'Prick-horse' (?) is its name.</p>  
 <p>(3) <i>Crinanthemon</i>. 'None is better than I' is its name.</p>  
 <p>(4) <i>Chrysanthemon</i>, 'Fine-face' is its name, otherwise</p>  
 <p><a name="page_170"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 170</font></a> <a name="page_171"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 171</font></a></p>  
 <p>said 'the gold flower' (5) of the wreath-seller; its leaf is strong, its stem is cold (?), (6) its flower is golden; its leaf is like <i>crinanthemon</i>.</p>  
 <p>(7) Magnesia, (8) <i>manesia</i>.(9) A stone of ..... black like (10) stibium; when you grind it, it is black.</p>  
 <p>(11) <i>Magnes</i>. Magnesia viva; it is brought (i.e. imported?).</p>  
 <p>(12) <i>Maknes</i>. When you scrape it, it is black.</p>  
 <p>(13) <i>Maknes</i> of man. It is brought (14) from India (?); when you scrape it (15) it exudes blood.</p>  
 <p>(16) To drug (?) your enemy; (17) an <i>apshe</i>-beetle (?); you burn it with styrax (?), (18) you pound it together with one drachma of apple (19) and a ..... and you .... (20) and you put a ........</p>
