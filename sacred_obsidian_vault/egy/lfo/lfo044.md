 <h3 align="CENTER">THE FORTY-FIFTH CEREMONY.</h3>
 <font face="Times Roman,Times New Roman"><p>A Rethu cake was next offered, and the Kher heb said:--</p>
 <p>"Osiris Unas, the Eye of Horus, which was chained up [by Set], hath been presented unto thee."</p>
 <p>The Rethu cake was round, and on it was probably</p>
