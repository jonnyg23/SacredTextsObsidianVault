 <h3 align="CENTER">THE FORTY-THIRD CEREMONY.</h3>
 <font face="Times Roman,Times New Roman"><p>In the next ceremony the priest presented for the <i>ush</i>(?) offering a Tua cake and a Shens cake, whilst the Kher heb said:--</p>
 <p>"Osiris Unas, the Eye of Horus hath been presented</p>
 <p><a name="page_105"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 105</font></a></p>
 <p>unto thee, and it hath been made to approach thy mouth for thee."</p>
