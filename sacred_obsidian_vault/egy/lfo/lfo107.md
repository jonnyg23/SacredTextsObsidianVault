 <h3 align="CENTER">THE ONE HUNDRED AND EIGHTH CEREMONY.</h3>
 <font face="Times Roman,Times New Roman"><p>Two vessels of Babat fruit, with the formula:--</p>
 <p>"Osiris Unas, the Eye of Horus hath been presented unto thee, and behold, it is from Baba (?)."</p>
 <p> </p>
 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_14101"></a>
 <img src="img/14101.jpg"><br>
 </p></font><font face="Times Roman,Times New Roman" size="1">The Sem priest presenting two vessels of Babat fruit</font><font face="Times Roman,Times New Roman">.
 <p> </p>
 <p><a name="page_142"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 142</font></a></p>
 <p>Dümichen thought that Baba was a name of Set, but it seems more probable that the Baba mentioned here is the first-born son of Osiris.</p>
