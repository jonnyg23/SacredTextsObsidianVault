 <h3 align="CENTER">THE SIXTY-FOURTH CEREMONY.</h3>
 <font face="Times Roman,Times New Roman"><p>Four cakes made of fine, white flour (<i>qemh</i>), with the formula:--</p>
 <p>"Osiris Unas, the Eye of Horus, which was fettered by him [i.e., by Set], hath been presented unto thee."</p>
