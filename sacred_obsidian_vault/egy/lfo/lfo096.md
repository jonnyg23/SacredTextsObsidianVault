 <h3 align="CENTER">THE NINETY-SEVENTH CEREMONY.</h3>
 <font face="Times Roman,Times New Roman"><p>Two vessels of white wine, with the formula:--</p>
 <p>"Osiris Unas, the Eye of Horus, the glorious one,</p>
 <p><a name="page_135"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 135</font></a></p>
 <p>which he [i.e., Set] devoured and afterwards vomited, <a name="fr_79"></a><a href="#fn_79"><font size="1">1</font></a> hath been presented unto thee."</p>
