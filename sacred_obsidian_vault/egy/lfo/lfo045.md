 <h3 align="CENTER">THE FORTY-SIXTH CEREMONY.</h3>
 <font face="Times Roman,Times New Roman"><p>A vessel of Tchesert drink was next presented, and the Kher heb said:--</p>
 <p>"Osiris Unas, the Eye of Horus hath been presented unto thee, the little one, which became the food of Set (or, which entered into Set)."</p>
 <p>The "little" Eye of Horus here referred to seems to be the moon, which, as already mentioned, was devoured piecemeal by Set after the fourteenth day each month.</p>
 <p><a name="page_107"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 107</font></a></p>
