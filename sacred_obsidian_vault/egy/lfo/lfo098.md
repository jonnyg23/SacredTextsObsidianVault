 <h3 align="CENTER">THE NINETY-NINTH CEREMONY.</h3>
 <font face="Times Roman,Times New Roman"><p>Two vessels of Qem wine, or wine of Mareotis, with the formula:--</p>
 <p>"Osiris Unas, the Eye of Horus which was snared in a net hath been presented unto thee, and thy mouth hath been opened thereby."</p>
