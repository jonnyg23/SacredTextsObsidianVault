 <h3 align="CENTER">THE TWENTY-FOURTH CEREMONY.</h3>
 <font face="Times Roman,Times New Roman"><p>The next four ceremonies deal with the offering of wine and three kinds of beer. The SEM priest presented</p>
 <p><a name="page_85"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 85</font></a></p>
 <p>a white vessel of wine to the deceased, and as he did so the Kher heb said:--</p>
 <p>"Unas, the Eye of Horus hath been presented unto thee, which was snatched from the hand of Set, and was rescued for thee, and thou dost open thy mouth with it."</p>
