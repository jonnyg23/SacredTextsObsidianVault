 <h3 align="CENTER">THE NINETEENTH CEREMONY.</h3>
 <font face="Times Roman,Times New Roman"><p>The process of restoring to the deceased the fluid of his body is continued in the next ceremony, wherein the SEM priest presents to him a black stone vase, containing <i>hent</i> beer. Whilst he is doing this the Kher heb says:--</p>
 <p>"Osiris Unas, there hath been presented unto thee</p>
