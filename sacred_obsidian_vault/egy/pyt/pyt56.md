 <h3 align="center">53. RESURRECTION, TRANSFIGURATION, AND LIFE OF THE KING IN HEAVEN,
 UTTERANCE 676.</h3>
 
 <p align="center"><i>Utterance
 676</i>.</p>
 
 <p>2007a. To say: Thy water belongs to thee, thine abundance belongs to thee,
 thine efflux belongs to thee,</p>
 
 <p>2007b. which issues from Osiris.</p>
 
 <p>2008a. Collect thy bones; arrange thy limbs;</p>
 
 <p>2008b. shake off thy dust; untie thy bandages.</p>
 
 <p>2009a. The tomb is open for thee; the double doors of the coffin are undone
 for thee;</p>
 
 <p>2009b. the double doors of heaven are open for thee.</p>
 
 <p>2009c. "Hail," says Isis;
 "(come) in peace," says Nephthys,</p>
 
 <p>2009d. when they see their brother at the feast of Atum.</p>
 
 <p>2010a. These thy libations, Osiris, are in Busiris, in <i>Grg.w-bȝ</i>(.<i>f</i> );</p>
 
 <p>2010b. thy soul is in thy body; thy might is behind thee; remain chief of
 (or, master of) thy powers.</p>
 
 <p>2011a. Raise thyself up, N.,</p>
 
 <p>2011b. travel over the southern regions; travel over the northern regions;</p>
 
 <p>2011c. be thou powerful over the powers that are in thee.</p>
 
 <p>2011d. Thy spirits, the jackals, are given thee which Horus of Hierakonpolis
 has given to thee.</p>
 
 <p>2012a. Raise thyself up, N., be seated on thy firm (or, copper) throne.</p>
 
 <p>2012b. Anubis, who is chief of the divine pavillion (<i>sḥ-n<u>t</u>r</i>), has commanded</p>
 
 <p>2012c. thy purification with thy eight <i>nmś.t</i>-jars
 and (thy) eight <i>ȝb.t</i>-jars, which
 come from the <i>sḥ-n<u>t</u>r</i>.</p>
 
 <p>2013a. Thou art a god who supports the sky, who beautifies the earth.</p>
 
 <p>2013b. The <i>śmnt.t</i>-woman laments
 for thee; the great <i>min.t</i> mourns for
 thee;</p>
 
 <p>2014a. arms agitate for thee; feet tremble for thee,</p>
 
 <p>2014b. when thou ascendest as a star, as the morning star.</p>
 
 <p>2014c. He comes to thee, his father; he comes to thee, Geb;</p>
 
 <p><a name="page_299"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 299</font></a></p>
 
 <p>2015a. take his hand, let him sit upon the great seat;</p>
 
 <p>2015b. let him unite with the two <i>mt</i>-lakes
 of <i>ḳbḥ.w</i>;</p>
 
 <p>2015c. purify his mouth with natron on the lap of <i>Mḫnti-irti</i>;</p>
 
 <p>2015d. purify his nails, upper and lower.</p>
 
 <p>2016a. Let one do for him what thou didst do for his brother, Osiris, . on
 the day of counting the bones,</p>
 
 <p>2016b. of making firm the sandals, of ferrying over the lake <i>Rd-wr</i>.</p>
 
 <p>2017a. To thee come the wise and the understanding;</p>
 
 <p>2017b. thou art called to the southern <i>itr.t</i>-palace;</p>
 
 <p>2017c. to thee come (the gods of) the full northern <i>itr.t</i>-palace, with a salutation.</p>
