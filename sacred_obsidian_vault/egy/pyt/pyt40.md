 <h3 align="center">37. THE RESURRECTION OF OSIRIS WITH WHOM THE GODS ARE SATISFIED, UTTERANCE
 577.</h3>
 
 <p align="center"><i>Utterance
 577</i>.</p>
 
 <p>1520a. To say: Osiris dawns, pure, mighty; high, lord of truth</p>
 
 <p>1520b. on the first of the year; lord of the year.</p>
 
 <p>1521a. Atum father of the gods is satisfied; Shu and Tefnut are satisfied;
 Geb and Nut are satisfied;</p>
 
 <p>1521b. Osiris and [Isis] are satisfied; Set and [Neit] (Nephthys?) are
 satisfied;</p>
 
 <p>1522a. all the gods who are in heaven are satisfied; all the gods who are on
 earth and in the lands are satisfied;</p>
 
 <p>1522b. all the southern and northern gods are satisfied; all the western and
 eastern gods are satisfied;</p>
 
 <p>1522c. all the nome gods are satisfied; all the city gods are satisfied</p>
 
 <p>1523a. with the great and mighty word, which comes forth from the mouth of
 Thot, concerning Osiris,</p>
 
 <p>1523b. the seal of life, the seal of the gods.</p>
 
 <p>1523c. Anubis, the counter of hearts, deducts Osiris N. from the gods who
 belong to the earth, (and assigns him) to the gods who are in heaven,</p>
 
 <p>1524a. lord of wine at the inundation.</p>
 
 <p>1524b. His year is calculated for him; his hour knows him.</p>
 
 <p>1524c. N. is known by his year which is with him;</p>
 
 <p>1524d. his hour which is with him knows him.</p>
 
 <p><a name="page_240"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 240</font></a></p>
 
 <p>1525. "Come, my child," says Atum, "come to us," say
 they, say the gods to thee, Osiris.</p>
 
 <p>1526a. ("Our) brother is come to us, the eldest, the first (begotten)
 of his father, the first (born) of his mother,"</p>
 
 <p>152 6b. say they, say the gods.</p>
 
 <p>1527a. Heaven conceived him: <i>Dwȝ.t</i>
 gave him birth;</p>
 
 <p>152 7b. N. was conceived with him by heaven;</p>
 
 <p>1527c. N. was given birth with him by <i>Dȝ.t</i>.</p>
 
 <p>152 8a. Thou supported the sky on thy right side, having life;</p>
 
 <p>1528b. thou livest, because the gods ordained that thou live.</p>
 
 <p>1528c. N. supports the sky on his right side, having life;</p>
 
 <p>1528d. he lives, his life, because the gods have ordained that he live.</p>
 
 <p>1529a. Thou leanest on the earth on thy left side, having joy;</p>
 
 <p>1529b. thou livest thy life, because the gods have ordained that thou live.</p>
 
 <p>1529c. N. leans on the [earth] on his left side, having life (or joy?);</p>
 
 <p>1529d. he lives his life, because (the gods) have ordained that he live.</p>
 
 <p>1530a. N. ascended on the eastern side of the sky;</p>
 
 <p>1530b. he descends as a green bird;</p>
 
 <p>1530c. he descends ---- lord of the <i>Dȝ.t</i>-lakes.</p>
 
 <p>1530d. N. is purified in the lakes of the <i>śmn</i>-goose.</p>
