 <h3 align="center">55. THE DECEASED KING ASCENDS TO HEAVEN, UTTERANCE 684.</h3>
 
 <p align="center"><i>Utterance
 684</i>.</p>
 
 <p>2051a. To say: N. ascended at thy ascension, Osiris;</p>
 
 <p>2051b. N. has spoken (with) his <i>ka</i>
 in heaven.</p>
 
 <p>2051c. The bones of N. are firm (or, copper), and the limbs of N.</p>
 
 <p>2051d. are like the stars, the imperishable stars.</p>
 
 <p>2052a. Given that N. be encompassed, then a great one falls into the hands
 of N.</p>
 
 <p>2052b. The mother of N. is Nut;</p>
 
 <p>2053a. the father of N. is Shu; the mother of N. is Tefnut.</p>
 
 <p>2053b. They take N. to heaven, to heaven-on the smoke of incense.</p>
 
 <p>2054. N. is purified; N. lives; N. makes his seat like Osiris;</p>
 
 <p>2055a. N. sits at thy side (lit. shoulder), Osiris; N. spits on thy hair,
 Osiris;</p>
 
 <p>2055b. he will not let him become diseased; N. will not permit him to be
 bald,</p>
 
 <p><a name="page_303"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 303</font></a></p>
 
 <p>2055c. at the mouth of N. daily, at the beginnings (of the feast) of the
 half months, at the beginnings (of the feast) of the months.</p>
 
 <p>2056a. N. sits at thy side (lit. shoulder), Horus; N. spits on thy hair,
 Horus;</p>
 
 <p>2056b. he will not let it become diseased; N. will not permit himself to be
 bald,</p>
 
 <p>2056c. at the mouth of N. daily, at the beginnings (of the feast) of the
 half months, at the beginnings (or the feast) of the months.</p>
 
 <p>2057. N. is one of these four beings, sons of Atum, sons of Nut,</p>
 
 <p>2058a. who do not rot; N. does not rot;</p>
 
 <p>2058b. who do not decay; N. does not decay;</p>
 
 <p>2058c. who do not fall upon earth from heaven;</p>
 
 <p>2058d. N. does not fall upon the earth from heaven.</p>
 
 <p>2059a. N. was sought; N. is found with them;</p>
 
 <p>2059b. N. is one of them, praised by the bull of heaven.</p>
 
 <p>2060. N. makes his <i>ka</i> arise; N.
 returns (?); N. strides--</p>
 
 <p>2061a. the good companion makes his <i>ka</i>
 arise, returns (?), strides.</p>
 
 <p>2061b. N. rests at home, on the under (side) of the body of the sky, like a <i>nfr.t</i>-star,</p>
 
 <p>2061c. at the meanderings of the Winding Watercourse.</p>
 
 <p>2062a. When N. ascends to heaven, give him this formula: "Rē is good
 each day."</p>
 
 <p>2062b. N. put himself on thy way, Horus of <i>Šsm.t</i>, on which thou leadest the gods</p>
 
 <p>2062c. to the beautiful ways of heaven and of the Marsh of Offerings.</p>
