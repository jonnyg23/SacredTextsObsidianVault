 <h3 align="center">28. A LITANY OF ASCENSION, UTTERANCE 539.</h3>
 
 <p align="center"><i>Utterance
 539</i>.</p>
 
 <p>1303a. To say: The head of N. is like that of the vulture,</p>
 
 <p>1303b. when he ascends and lifts himself to the sky.</p>
 
 <p>1303c. The skull of N. is like that of divine stars,</p>
 
 <p>1303d. when it ascends and [lifts itself to the sky].</p>
 
 <p>1304a. [The forehead of N. is like that of] ---- and Nu,</p>
 
 <p>1304b. when it ascends and lifts itself to the sky.</p>
 
 <p>1304c. The face of N. is like that of Wepwawet,</p>
 
 <p>1304d. when he ascends and lifts himself to the sky.</p>
 
 <p>1305a. The eyes of N. (are like those of) the Great One who is chief of the
 Souls of Heliopolis,</p>
 
 <p>1305b. when he ascends and lifts himself to the sky.</p>
 
 <p>1305c. The nose of N. is like that of Thot,</p>
 
 <p>1305d. when he ascends [and lifts himself to the sky].</p>
 
 <p><a name="page_214"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 214</font></a></p>
 
 <p>1306a. [The mouth of] N. is like that of him who traverses the great lake,</p>
 
 <p>1306b. when he ascends and lifts himself to the sky.</p>
 
 <p>1306c. The tongue of N. is like that of truth in the boat of truth,</p>
 
 <p>1306d. when he ascends and lifts himself to the sky.</p>
 
 <p>1307a. The teeth of N. are (like those of) spirits,</p>
 
 <p>1307b. when he ascends and lifts, himself to the sky.</p>
 
 <p>1307c. The lips of N. are like those of -----)</p>
 
 <p>1307d. [when he ascends and lifts] himself to the sky.</p>
 
 <p>1308a. The chin of N. is like that of <i><u>H</u>rti-ḫnti-Ḫm</i>,</p>
 
 <p>1308b. when he ascends and lifts himself to the sky.</p>
 
 <p>1308c. The back of N. is like that of the wild-bull,</p>
 
 <p>1308d. when he ascends, and lifts himself to the sky.</p>
 
 <p>1309a. The arms of N. are like those of Set,</p>
 
 <p>1309b. when he ascends and lifts himself [to the sky].</p>
 
 <p>1309c. ---------------------</p>
 
 <p>1309d. [when he ascends and lifts himself to the sky].</p>
 
 <p>1310a. ----- like -- <i>Bȝibw</i>,</p>
 
 <p>1310b. when he ascends and lifts himself to the sky.</p>
 
 <p>1310c. The heart of N. is like that of Bastet,</p>
 
 <p>1310d. when he ascends and lifts himself to the sky.</p>
 
 <p>1311a. The belly of N. is like that of Nut,</p>
 
 <p>1311b. when he ascends and lifts himself [to the sky].</p>
 
 <p>1311c. -----------------</p>
 
 <p>1311d. [when he ascends and lifts himself to the sky].</p>
 
 <p>1312a. ----- of N. -- like --- of the Two Enneads,</p>
 
 <p>1312b. when he ascends and lifts himself to the sky.</p>
 
 <p>1312 C. The seat of N. is like that of <i>Ḥeḳet</i>,</p>
 
 <p>1312d. when he ascends and lifts himself to the sky.</p>
 
 <p>1313a. The buttocks of N. are like those of the boat of the evening, and the
 boat of the morning,</p>
 
 <p>1313b. when he ascends and lifts, himself to the sky.</p>
 
 <p>1313c. The phallus of N. is like that of <i>Ḥapi</i>,</p>
 
 <p>1313d. when he ascends and lifts himself to the sky.</p>
 
 <p>1314a. The thighs of N. are like those of Neit and <i>Śerḳet</i>,</p>
 
 <p>1314b. when he ascends and lifts himself to the sky.</p>
 
 <p>1314c. The legs of N. are like those of the two souls who are before the
 field <i><u>d</u>r</i>,</p>
 
 <p>1314d. when he ascends and lifts himself to the sky.</p>
 
 <p><a name="page_215"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 215</font></a></p>
 
 <p>1315a. The feet of N. are like those of the two morning boats of the sun,</p>
 
 <p>1315b. when he ascends and lifts himself to the sky.</p>
 
 <p>1315c. The toes of N. are like those of the Souls of Heliopolis,</p>
 
 <p>1315d. when he ascends and lifts himself to the sky.</p>
 
 <p>1316a. N. is he who belongs to a god, the son of a god,</p>
 
 <p>1316b. when he ascends and lifts himself to the sky.</p>
 
 <p>1316c. N. is the son of Rē, his beloved,</p>
 
 <p>1316d. when he ascends and lifts himself to the sky.</p>
 
 <p>1317a. N. is begotten of Rē,</p>
 
 <p>1317b. when he ascends and lifts himself to the sky.</p>
 
 <p>1317c. N. is conceived of Rē,</p>
 
 <p>1317d. when he ascends and lifts himself to the sky.</p>
 
 <p>131 8a. N. is born of Rē,</p>
 
 <p>1318b. when he ascends, and lifts himself to the sky.</p>
 
 <p>1318c. This magic is in the body of N.,</p>
 
 <p>1318d. when he ascends and lifts himself to the sky.</p>
 
 <p>1319a. N. is the great sceptre in the great court in Heliopolis,</p>
 
 <p>1319b. when he ascends and lifts himself to the sky.</p>
 
 <p>1320a. (He is) <i><u>H</u>nnw</i>,</p>
 
 <p>1320b. when he ascends and lifts himself to the sky.</p>
 
 <p>1320c. (He is) Horus, the child, the youth,</p>
 
 <p>1320d. when this N. ascends and lifts himself to the sky.</p>
 
 <p>1321a. Nut (is) she who cannot be fertilized without putting (down) her
 arms,</p>
 
 <p>1321b. when he ascends and lifts himself to the sky.</p>
 
 <p>1321c. Geb is not diverted from his way,</p>
 
 <p>1321d. when N. ascends and lifts himself to the sky.</p>
 
 <p>1322a. Each god who constructs not a stairway for N.,</p>
 
 <p>1322b. when he ascends and lifts himself to the sky,</p>
 
 <p>1322c. he shall have no <i>pḳ</i>-cake,
 he shall have no shade,</p>
 
 <p>1323a. he shall not wash himself in the <i>ḫȝw</i>-bowl,</p>
 
 <p>1323b. he shall not smell (taste) a leg (of meat); he shall not pass a
 cutlet (over the mouth) (i.e., he shall not taste a cutlet),</p>
 
 <p>1323c. the earth shall not be hoed for him; the <i>wdn.t</i>-offerings shall not be made for him,</p>
 
 <p>1323d. when this N. ascends and lifts himself to the sky.</p>
 
 <p>1324a. It is certainly not N. who says that against you, O gods;</p>
 
 <p>1324b. it is magic which says that against you, O gods.</p>
 
 <p><a name="page_216"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 216</font></a></p>
 
 <p>1324c. N. belongs to a region under magic.</p>
 
 <p>1325a. Each god who constructs stairs (or, stairway) for N.,</p>
 
 <p>1325b. when N. ascends and lifts himself to the sky;</p>
 
 <p>1325c. each god who vacates his throne in his boat, 1325d. when this N.
 ascends, and lifts himself to the sky,</p>
 
 <p>1326a. the earth shall be hoed for him, the <i>wdn.t</i>-offering shall be made for him,</p>
 
 <p>1326b. a <i>nmt.t</i>-bowl shall be made
 for him,</p>
 
 <p>1326c. he shall smell a leg (of meat), he shall pass a cutlet (over the
 mouth),</p>
 
 <p>1326d. when this N. ascends and lifts himself to the sky.</p>
 
 <p>1327a. Each god who takes the arm of N. to the sky, 1327b. when he comes to
 the house of Horus which is in <i>ḳbḥ.w</i>,</p>
 
 <p>1327c. his <i>ka</i> shall be justified
 before Geb.</p>
