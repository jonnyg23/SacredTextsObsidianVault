 <h3>VIII 1</h3>
 PAv, nay, loron plus feu qu'à sang sera,<br>
 Laude nager, fuir grand aux surrez.<br>
 Les agassas entrée refusera,<br>
 Pampon, Durance les tiendra enserrez.<br>
 <p>
 Pau, Nay, Loron will be more of fire than blood,<br>
 to swim in praise, the great one to flee to the confluence (of rivers).<br>
 He will refuse entry to the magpies<br>
 Pampon and the Durance will keep them confined.<br>
 </p><p>
 </p><h3>VIII 1 *</h3>
 Several will be confused in their waiting,<br>
 Pardon will not be given the inhabitants:<br>
 Those who thought well of persisting in the waiting,<br>
 But not much spare time will be given them.<br>
 <p>
 </p><h3>VIII 2</h3>
 Condon &amp; Aux &amp; autour de Mirande<br>
 Ie voy du ciel feu qui les enuironne.<br>
 Sol Mars conioint au Lyon, puis Marmande<br>
 Foudre, grand gresle, mur tombe dans Garonne.<br>
 <p>
 Condom and Auch and around Mirande,<br>
 I see fire from the sky which encompasses them.<br>
 Sun and Mars conjoined in Leo, then at Marmande,<br>
 lightning, great hail, a wall falls into the Garonne.<br>
 </p><p>
 </p><h3>VIII 2 *</h3>
 Several will come, and speak of peace,<br>
 Between Monarchs and very powerful lords:<br>
 But it will not be accorded so soon,<br>
 Unless they become more obedient than the others.<br>
 <p>
 </p><h3>VIII 3</h3>
 Au fort chasteau de Vigilanue &amp; Resuiers<br>
 Sera serré le puisnay de Nancy :<br>
 Dedans Turin seront ards les premiers,<br>
 Lors que de dueil Luon sera transy.<br>
 <p>
 Within the strong castle of Vigilance and Resviers<br>
 the younger born of Nancy will be shut up.<br>
 In Turin the first ones will be burned,<br>
 when Lyons will be transported with grief.<br>
 </p><p>
 </p><h3>VIII 3 *</h3>
 Alas what a fury ! Alas what a pity<br>
 Will there be between people:<br>
 never did one see such a friendship<br>
 As the wolves will have diligent in running.<br>
 <p>
 </p><h3>VIII 4</h3>

 Le Cardinal de France apparoistra<br>
 Par Logarion Romain sera deceu,<br>
 Foiblesse à l'Aigle, &amp; force au Coq naistra.<br>
 <p>
 The cock will be received into Monace, <br>
 the Cardinal of France will appear;<br>
 He will be deceived by the Roman legation;<br>
 weakness to the eagle, strength will be born to the cock.<br>
 </p><p>
 </p><h3>VIII 4 *</h3>
 Many people will want to come to terms<br>
 With the great lords who will bring war upon them:<br>
 They will not want to hear anything of it from them,<br>
 Alas! if Gos does not send peace to the earth.<br>
 <p>
 </p><h3>VIII 5</h3>

 Pour la Lucerne le canton destorné,<br>
 Quand on verra le grand Coq au cercueil.<br>
 <p>
 There will appear a shining ornate temple,<br>

 For the canton of Lucerne turned aside,<br>
 when one will see the great cock in his shroud.<br>
 </p><p>
 </p><h3>VIII 5 *</h3>
 Varieties of aid will come from all sides,<br>

 Suddenly they will be much urged on,<br>
 But they will be unable to assist at that hour.<br>

 <p>
