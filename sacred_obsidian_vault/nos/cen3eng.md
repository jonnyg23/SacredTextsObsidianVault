 <center><h1>Century III</h1></center>
 <p>
 1<br>
 After combat and naval battle,<br>
 The great Neptune in his highest belfry:<br>
 Red adversary will become pale with fear,<br>
 Putting the great Ocean in dread.<br>
 </p><p>
 2<br>
 The divine word will give to the sustenance,<br>
 Including heaven, earth, gold hidden in the mystic milk:<br>
 Body, soul, spirit having all power,<br>
 As much under its feet as the Heavenly see.<br>
 </p><p>
 3<br>
 Mars and Mercury, and the silver joined together,<br>
 Towards the south extreme drought:<br>
 In the depths of Asia one will say the earth trembles,<br>
 Corinth, Ephesus then in perplexity.<br>
 </p><p>
 4<br>
 When they will be close the lunar ones will fail,<br>
 From one another not greatly distant,<br>
 Cold, dryness, danger towards the frontiers, <br>
 Even where the oracle has had its beginning.<br>
 </p><p>
 5<br>
 Near, far the failure of the two great luminaries<br>
 Which will occur between April and March.<br>
 Oh, what a loss! but two great good-natured ones<br>
 By land and sea will relieve all parts.<br>
 </p><p>
 6<br>
 Within the closed temple the lightning will enter,<br>
 The citizens within their fort injured:<br>
 Horses, cattle, men, the wave will touch the wall,<br>
 Through famine, drought, under the weakest armed.<br>
 </p><p>
 7<br>
 The fugitives, fire from the sky on the pikes:<br>
 Conflict near the ravens frolicking,<br>
 From land they cry for aid and heavenly relief,<br>
 When the combatants will be near the walls.<br>
 </p><p>
 8<br>
 The Cimbri joined with their neighbors<br>
 Will come to ravage almost Spain:<br>
 Peoples gathered in Guienne and Limousin<br>
 Will be in league, and will bear them company.<br>
 </p><p>
 9<br>
 Bordeaux, Rouen and La Rochelle joined<br>
 Will hold around the great Ocean sea,<br>
 English, Bretons and the Flemings allied<br>
 Will chase them as far as Roanne.<br>
 </p><p>
 10<br>
 Greater calamity of blood and famine,<br>
 Seven times it approaches the marine shore:<br>
 Monaco from hunger, place captured, captivity,<br>
 The great one led crunching in a metaled cage.<br>
 </p><p>
 11<br>
 The arms to fight in the sky a long time,<br>
 The tree in the middle of the city fallen:<br>
 Sacred bough clipped, steel, in the face of the firebrand,<br>
 Then the monarch of Adria fallen.<br>
 </p><p>
 12<br>
 Because of the swelling of the Ebro, Po, Tagus, Tiber and Rhône<br>
 And because of the pond of Geneva and Arezzo,<br>
 The two great chiefs and cities of the Garonne,<br>
 Taken, dead, drowned: human booty divided.<br>
 </p><p>
 </p><p>
 13.<br>
 Through lightning in the arch gold and silver melted,<br>
 Of two captives one will eat the other:<br>
 The greatest one of the city stretched out,<br>
 When submerged the fleet will swim.<br>
 </p><p>
 14<br>
 Through the branch of the valiant personage<br>
 Of lowest France: because of the unhappy father<br>
 Honors, riches, travail in his old age,<br>
 For having believed the advice of a simple man.<br>
 </p><p>
 15<br>
 The realm, will change in heart, vigor and glory,<br>
 In all points having its adversary opposed:<br>
 Then through death France an infancy will subjugate,<br>
 A great Regent will then be more contrary.<br>
 </p><p>
 16<br>
 An English prince Marc in his heavenly heart<br>
 Will want to pursue his prosperous fortune,<br>
 Of the two duels one will pierce his gall:<br>
 Hated by him well loved by his mother.<br>
 </p><p>
 17<br>
 Mount Aventine will be seen to burn at night:<br>
 The sky very suddenly dark in Flanders:<br>
 When the monarch will chase his nephew,<br>
 Then Church people will commit scandals.<br>
 </p><p>
 18<br>
 After the rather long rain milk,<br>
 In several places in Reims the sky touched:<br>
 Alas, what a bloody murder is prepared near them,<br>
 Fathers and sons Kings will not dare approach.<br>
 </p><p>
 19<br>
 In Lucca it will come to rain blood and milk,<br>
 Shortly before a change of praetor:<br>
 Great plague and war, famine and drought will be made visible<br>
 Far away where their prince and rector will die.<br>
 </p><p>
 20<br>
 Through the regions of the great river Guadalquivir<br>
 Deep in Iberia to the Kingdom of Grenada<br>
 Crosses beaten back by the Mahometan peoples<br>
 One of Cordova will betray his country<br>
 </p><p>
 21<br>
 In the Conca by the Adriatic Sea<br>
 There will appear a horrible fish,<br>
 With face human and its end aquatic,<br>
 Which will be taken without the hook.<br>
 </p><p>
 22<br>
 Six days the attack made before the city:<br>
 Battle will be given strong and harsh:<br>
 Three will surrender it, and to them pardon:<br>
 The rest to fire and to bloody slicing and cutting.<br>
 </p><p>
 23<br>
 If, France, you pass beyond the Ligurian Sea,<br>
 You will see yourself shut up in islands and seas:<br>
 Mahomet contrary, more so the Adriatic Sea:<br>
 You will gnaw the bones of horses and asses.<br>
 </p><p>
 24<br>
 Great confusion in the enterprise,<br>
 Loss of people, countless treasure:<br>
 You ought not to extend further there.<br>
 France, let what I say be remembered.<br>
 </p><p>
 25<br>
 He who will attain to the kingdom of Navarre<br>
 When Sicily and Naples will be joined:<br>
 He will hold Bigorre and Landes through Foix and Oloron<br>
 From one who will be too closely allied with Spain.<br>
 </p><p>
 26<br>
 They will prepare idols of Kings and Princes,<br>
 Soothsayers and empty prophets elevated:<br>
 Horn, victim of gold, and azure, dazzling,<br>
 The soothsayers will be interpreted.<br>
 </p><p>
 27<br>
 Libyan Prince powerful in the West<br>
 Will come to inflame very much French with Arabian.<br>
 Learned in letters condescending he will<br>
 Translate the Arabian language into French.<br>
 </p><p>
 28<br>
 Of land weak and parentage poor,<br>
 Through piece and peace he will attain to the empire.<br>
 For a long time a young female to reign,<br>
 Never has one so bad come upon the kingdom.<br>
 </p><p>
 29<br>
 The two nephews brought up in diverse places:<br>
 Naval battle, land, fathers fallen:<br>
 They will come to be elevated very high in making war<br>
 To avenge the injury, enemies succumbed.<br>
 </p><p>
 30<br>
 He who during the struggle with steel in the deed of war<br>
 Will have carried off the prize from on greater than he:<br>
 By night six will carry the grudge to his bed,<br>
 Without armor he will surprised suddenly.<br>
 </p><p>
 31<br>
 On the field of Media, of Arabia and of Armenia<br>
 Two great armies will assemble thrice:<br>
 The host near the bank of the Araxes,<br>
 They will fall in the land of the great Suleiman.<br>
 </p><p>
 32<br>
 The great tomb of the people of Aquitaine <br>
 Will approach near to Tuscany,<br>
 When Mars will be in the corner of Germany<br>
 And in the land of the Mantuan people.<br>
 </p><p>
 33<br>
 In the city where the wolf will enter,<br>
 Very near there will the enemies be:<br>
 Foreign army will spoil a great country.<br>
 The friends will pass at the wall and Alps.<br>
 </p><p>
 34<br>
 When the eclipse of the Sun will then be,<br>
 The monster will be seen in full day:<br>
 Quite otherwise will one interpret it,<br>
 High price unguarded: none will have foreseen it.<br>
 </p><p>
 35<br>
 From the very depths of the West of Europe,<br>
 A young child will be born of poor people,<br>
 He who by his tongue will seduce a great troop:<br>
 His fame will increase towards the realm of the East.<br>
 </p><p>
 36<br>
 Buried apoplectic not dead,<br>
 He will be found to have his hands eaten:<br>
 When the city will condemn the heretic,<br>
 He who it seemed to them had changed their laws.<br>
 </p><p>
 37<br>
 The speech delivered before the attack,<br>
 Milan taken by the Eagle through deceptive ambushes:<br>
 Ancient wall driven in by cannons,<br>
 Through fire and blood few given quarter.<br>
 </p><p>
 38<br>
 The Gallic people and a foreign nation<br>
 Beyond the mountains, dead, captured and killed:<br>
 In the contrary month and near vintage time,<br>
 Through the Lords drawn up in accord.<br>
 </p><p>
 39<br>
 The seven in three months in agreement<br>
 To subjugate the Apennine Alps:<br>
 But the tempest and cowardly Ligurian,<br>
 Destroys them in sudden ruins.<br>
 </p><p>
 40<br>
 The great theater will come to be set up again:<br>
 The dice cast and the snares already laid.<br>
 Too much the first one will come to tire in the death knell,<br>
 Prostrated by arches already a long time split.<br>
 </p><p>
 41<br>
 Hunchback will be elected by the council,<br>
 A more hideous monster not seen on earth,<br>
 The willing blow will put out his eye:<br>
 The traitor to the King received as faithful.<br>
 </p><p>
 42<br>
 The child will be born with two teeth in his mouth,<br>
 Stones will fall during the rain in Tuscany:<br>
 A few years after there will be neither wheat nor barley,<br>
 To satiate those who will faint from hunger.<br>
 </p><p>
 43<br>
 People from around the Tarn, Lot and Garonne<br>
 Beware of passing the Apennine mountains:<br>
 Your tomb near Rome and Ancona,<br>
 The black frizzled beard will have a trophy set up.<br>
 </p><p>
 44<br>
 When the animal domesticated by man<br>
 After great pains and leaps will come to speak:<br>
 The lightning to the virgin will be very harmful,<br>
 Taken from earth and suspended in the air.<br>
 </p><p>
 45<br>
 The five strangers entered in the temple,<br>
 Their blood will come to pollute the land:<br>
 To the Toulousans it will be a very hard example<br>
 Of one who will come to exterminate their laws.<br>
 </p><p>
 46<br>
 The sky ( of Plancus' city ) forebodes to us<br>
 Through clear signs and fixed stars,<br>
 That the time of its sudden change is approaching,<br>
 Neither for its good, nor for its evils.<br>
 </p><p>
 47<br>
 The old monarch chased out of his realm<br>
 Will go to the East asking for its help:<br>
 For fear of the crosses he will fold his banner:<br>
 To Mitylene he will go through port and by land.<br>
 </p><p>
 48<br>
 Seven hundred captives bound roughly.<br>
 Lots drawn for the half to be murdered:<br>
 The hope at hand will come very promptly<br>
 But not as soon as the fifteenth death.<br>
 </p><p>
 49<br>
 Gallic realm, you will be much changed:<br>
 To a foreign place is the empire transferred:<br>
 You will be set up amidst other customs and laws:<br>
 Rouen and Chartres will do much of the worst to you.<br>
 </p><p>
 50<br>
 The republic of the great city<br>
 Will not want to consent to the great severity:<br>
 King summoned by trumpet to go out,<br>
 The ladder at the wall, the city will repent.<br>
 </p><p>
 51<br>
 Paris conspires to commit a great murder<br>
 Blois will cause it to be fully carried out:<br>
 Those of Orléans will want to replace their chief,<br>
 Angers, Troyes, Langres will commit a misdeed against them.<br>
 </p><p>
 52<br>
 In Campania there will be a very long rain,<br>
 In Apulia very great drought.<br>
 The Cock will see the Eagle, its wing poorly finished,<br>
 By the Lion will it be put into extremity.<br>
 </p><p>
 53<br>
 When the greatest one will carry off the prize<br>
 Of Nuremberg, of Augsburg, and those of Bâle<br>
 Through Cologne the chief Frankfort retaken<br>
 They will cross through Flanders right into Gaul.<br>
 </p><p>
 54<br>
 One of the greatest ones will flee to Spain<br>
 Which will thereafter come to bleed in a long wound:<br>
 Armies passing over the high mountains,<br>
 Devastating all, and then to reign in peace.<br>
 </p><p>
 55<br>
 In the year that one eye will reign in France,<br>
 The court will be in very unpleasant trouble:<br>
 The great one of Blois will kill his friend:<br>
 The realm placed in harm and double doubt.<br>
 </p><p>
 56<br>
 Montauban, Nîmes, Avignon and Béziers,<br>
 Plague, thunder and hail in the wake of Mars:<br>
 Of Paris bridge, Lyons wall, Montpellier,<br>
 After six hundreds and seven score three pairs.<br>
 </p><p>
 57<br>
 Seven times will you see the British nation change,<br>
 Steeped in blood in 290 years:<br>
 Free not at all its support Germanic.<br>
 Aries doubt his Bastarnian pole.<br>
 </p><p>
 58<br>
 Near the Rhine from the Noric mountains<br>
 Will be born a great one of people come too late,<br>
 One who will defend Sarmatia and the Pannonians,<br>
 One will not know what will have become of him.<br>
 </p><p>
 59<br>
 Barbarian empire usurped by the third,<br>
 The greater part of his blood he will put to death:<br>
 Through senile death the fourth struck by him,<br>
 For fear that the blood through the blood be not dead.<br>
 </p><p>
 60<br>
 Throughout all Asia (Minor) great proscription,<br>
 Even in Mysia, Lycia and Pamphilia.<br>
 Blood will be shed because of the absolution<br>
 Of a young black one filled with felony.<br>
 </p><p>
 61<br>
 The great band and sect of crusaders<br>
 Will be arrayed in Mesopotamia:<br>
 Light company of the nearby river,<br>
 That such law will hold for an enemy.<br>
 </p><p>
 62<br>
 Near the Douro by the closed Tyrian sea,<br>
 He will come to pierce the great Pyrenees mountains.<br>
 One hand shorter his opening glosses,<br>
 He will lead his traces to Carcassone.<br>
 </p><p>
 63<br>
 The Roman power will be thoroughly abased,<br>
 Following in the footsteps of its great neighbor:<br>
 Hidden civil hatreds and debates<br>
 Will delay their follies for the buffoons.<br>
 </p><p>
 64<br>
 The chief of Persia will occupy great Olchades,<br>
 The trireme fleet against the Mahometan people<br>
 From Parthia, and Media: and the Cyclades pillaged:<br>
 Long rest at the great Ionian port.<br>
 </p><p>
 65<br>
 When the sepulcher of the great Roman is found,<br>
 The day after a Pontiff will be elected:<br>
 Scarcely will he be approved by the Senate<br>
 Poisoned, his blood in the sacred chalice.<br>
 </p><p>
 66<br>
 The great Bailiff of Orléans put to death<br>
 Will be by one of blood revengeful:<br>
 Of death deserved he will not die, nor by chance:<br>
 He made captive poorly by his feet and hands.<br>
 </p><p>
 67<br>
 A new sect of Philosophers<br>
 Despising death, gold, honors and riches<br>
 Will not be bordering upon the German mountains:<br>
 To follow them they will have power and crowds.<br>
 </p><p>
 68<br>
 Leaderless people of Spain and Italy<br>
 Dead, overcome within the Peninsula:<br>
 Their dictator betrayed by irresponsible folly,<br>
 Swimming in blood everywhere in the latitude.<br>
 </p><p>
 69<br>
 The great army led by a young man,<br>
 It will come to surrender itself into the hands of the enemies:<br>
 But the old one born to the half-pig,<br>
 He will cause Châlon and Mâcon to be friends.<br>
 </p><p>
 70<br>
 The great Britain including England<br>
 Will come to be flooded very high by waters<br>
 The new League of Ausonia will make war,<br>
 So that they will come to strive against them.<br>
 </p><p>
 71<br>
 Those in the isles long besieged<br>
 Will take vigor and force against their enemies:<br>
 Those outside dead overcome by hunger,<br>
 They will be put in greater hunger than ever before.<br>
 </p><p>
 72<br>
 The good old man buried quite alive,<br>
 Near the great river through false suspicion:<br>
 The new old man ennobled by riches,<br>
 Captured on the road all his gold for ransom.<br>
 </p><p>
 73<br>
 When the cripple will attain to the realm,<br>
 For his competitor he will have a near bastard:<br>
 He and the realm will become so very mangy<br>
 That before he recovers, it will be too late.<br>
 </p><p>
 74<br>
 Naples, Florence, Faenza and Imola,<br>
 They will be on terms of such disagreement<br>
 As to delight in the wretches of Nola<br>
 Complaining of having mocked its chief.<br>
 </p><p>
 75<br>
 Pau, Verona, Vicenza, Saragossa,<br>
 From distant swords lands wet with blood:<br>
 Very great plague will come with the great shell,<br>
 Relief near, and the remedies very far.<br>
 </p><p>
 76<br>
 In Germany will be born diverse sects,<br>
 Coming very near happy paganism,<br>
 The heart captive and returns small,<br>
 They will return to paying the true tithe.<br>
 </p><p>
 77<br>
 The third climate included under Aries<br>
 The year 1727 in October,<br>
 The King of Persia captured by those of Egypt:<br>
 Conflict, death, loss: to the cross great shame.<br>
 </p><p>
 78<br>
 The chief of Scotland, with six of Germany,<br>
 Captive of the Eastern seamen:<br>
 They will pass Gibraltar and Spain,<br>
 Present in Persia for the fearful new King.<br>
 </p><p>
 79<br>
 The fatal everlasting order through the chain<br>
 Will come to turn through consistent order:<br>
 The chain of Marseilles will be broken:<br>
 The city taken, the enemy at the same time.<br>
 </p><p>
 80<br>
 The worthy one chased out of the English realm,<br>
 The adviser through anger put to the fire:<br>
 His adherents will go so low to efface themselves<br>
 That the bastard will be half received.<br>
 </p><p>
 81<br>
 The great shameless, audacious bawler,<br>
 He will be elected governor of the army:<br>
 The boldness of his contention,<br>
 The bridge broken, the city faint from fear.<br>
 </p><p>
 82<br>
 Fréjus, Antibes, towns around Nice,<br>
 They will be thoroughly devastated by sea and by land:<br>
 The locusts by land and by sea the wind propitious,<br>
 Captured, dead, bound, pillaged without law of war.<br>
 </p><p>
 83<br>
 The long hairs of Celtic Gaul<br>
 Accompanied by foreign nations,<br>
 They will make captive the people of Aquitaine,<br>
 For succumbing to their designs.<br>
 </p><p>
 84<br>
 The great city will be thoroughly desolated,<br>
 Of the inhabitants not a single one will remain there:<br>
 Wall, sex, temple and virgin violated,<br>
 Through sword, fire, plague, cannon people will die.<br>
 </p><p>
 85<br>
 The city taken through deceit and guile,<br>
 Taken in by means of a handsome youth:<br>
 Assault given by the Robine near the Aude,<br>
 He and all dead for having thoroughly deceived.<br>
 </p><p>
 86<br>
 A chief of Ausonia will go to Spain<br>
 By sea, he will make a stop in Marseilles:<br>
 Before his death he will linger a long time:<br>
 After his death one will see a great marvel.<br>
 </p><p>
 87<br>
 Gallic fleet, do not approach Corsica,<br>
 Less Sardinia, you will rue it:<br>
 Every one of you will die frustrated of the help of the cape:<br>
 You will swim in blood, captive you will not believe me.<br>
 </p><p>
 88<br>
 From Barcelona a very great army by sea,<br>
 All Marseilles will tremble with terror:<br>
 Isles seized help shut off by sea,<br>
 Your traitor will swim on land.<br>
 </p><p>
 89<br>
 At that time Cyprus will be frustrated<br>
 Of its relief by those of the Aegean Sea:<br>
 Old ones slaughtered: but by speeches and supplications<br>
 Their King seduced, Queen outraged more.<br>
 </p><p>
 90<br>
 The great Satyr and Tiger of Hyrcania,<br>
 Gift presented to those of the Ocean:<br>
 A fleet's chief will set out from Carmania,<br>
 One who will take land at the Tyrren Phocaean.<br>
 </p><p>
 91<br>
 The tree which had long been dead and withered,<br>
 In one night it will come to grow green again:<br>
 The Cronian King sick, Prince with club foot,<br>
 Feared by his enemies he will make his sail bound.<br>
 </p><p>
 92<br>
 The world near the last period,<br>
 Saturn will come back again late:<br>
 Empire transferred towards the Dusky nation,<br>
 The eye plucked out by the Goshawk at Narbonne.<br>
 </p><p>
 93<br>
 In Avignon the chief of the whole empire<br>
 Will make a stop on the way to desolated Paris:<br>
 Tricast will hold the anger of Hannibal:<br>
 Lyons will be poorly consoled for the change.<br>
 </p><p>
 94<br>
 For five hundred years more one will keep count of him<br>
 Who was the ornament of his time:<br>
 Then suddenly great light will he give,<br>
 He who for this century will render them very satisfied.<br>
 </p><p>
 95<br>
 The law of More will be seen to decline:<br>
 After another much more seductive:<br>
 Dnieper first will come to give way:<br>
 Through gifts and tongue another more attractive.<br>
 </p><p>
 96<br>
 The Chief of Fossano will have his throat cut<br>
 By the leader of the bloodhound and greyhound:<br>
 The deed executed by those of the Tarpeian Rock,<br>
 Saturn in Leo February 13.<br>
 </p><p>
 97<br>
 New law to occupy the new land<br>
 Towards Syria, Judea and Palestine:<br>
 The great barbarian empire to decay,<br>
 Before the Moon completes it cycle.<br>
 </p><p>
 98<br>
 Two royal brothers will wage war so fiercely<br>
 That between them the war will be so mortal<br>
 That both will occupy the strong places:<br>
 Their great quarrel will fill realm and life.<br>
 </p><p>
 99<br>
 In the grassy fields of Alleins and Vernègues<br>
 Of the Lubéron range near the Durance,<br>
 The conflict will be very sharp for both armies,<br>
 Mesopotamia will fail in France.<br>
 </p><p>
 100<br>
 The last one honored amongst the Gauls,<br>
 Over the enemy man will he be victorious:<br>
 Force and land in a moment explored,<br>
 When the envious one will die from an arrow shot.<br>
 </p><p>
