 <p>11. Khumma-khaldasu the king of <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Elam</span> without being sick died in his palace.</p>  
 <p>12. For 5 years Khumma-khaldasu reigned over <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Elam</span>.</p>  
 <p>13. Urtagu his brother sat upon the throne in <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Elam</span>.</p>  
 <p>14. In a month I do not know Nadin-Suma the <i>Gu-enna</i></p>  
 <p>15. and Kudur the son of Dakuri went to <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Assyria</span>.</p>  
 <p align="center">_________</p>  
 <p>16. In the 7th year on the 5th day of the month Adar the soldiers of <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Assyria</span> marched into <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Egypt</span>.</p>  
 <p>17. In the month Adar Istar of the city of <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Accad</span> and the gods of the city of <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Accad</span></p>  
 <p>18. had departed from the country of <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Elam</span> and on the 10th day of the month Adar entered the city of <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Accad</span>.</p>  
 <p align="center">_________</p>  
 <p>19. In the 8th year of Esar-haddon in the month Tebet on a day of which the date has been lost <a name="fr_82"></a><a href="#fn_82"><font size="1">1</font></a></p>  
 <p>20. the country of the <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Rurizâ</span> was occupied; its spoil was carried away.</p>  
 <p>21. In the month Kisleu its spoil was brought into the city of <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Ur</span>.</p>  
 <p>22. On the 5th day of the month Adar the wife of the king died.</p>  
 <p align="center">_________</p>  
 <p>23. In the tenth year in the month Nisan the soldiers of <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Assyria</span> marched into Egypt. <a name="fr_83"></a><a href="#fn_83"><font size="1">2</font></a></p>  
 <p>24. On the 3d day of the month Tammuz and also on the 16th and 18th days</p>  
 <p>25. three times the Egyptians were defeated with heavy loss. <a name="fr_84"></a><a href="#fn_84"><font size="1">3</font></a></p>  
 <p>26. On the 22d day Memphis, <a name="fr_85"></a><a href="#fn_85"><font size="1">4</font></a> the royal city, was captured.</p>  
 <p>27. Its king fled; his son descended into the country of [<span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Ethiopia</span>].</p>

 <p>29. In the 11th year the king [remained] in <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Assyria</span>; his officers ……</p>  
 <p align="center">_________</p>  
 <p>30. In the 12th year the king of <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Assyria</span> ……</p>  
 <p>31. On the march he fell ill, and died on the 10th day of the month Marchesvan. <a name="fr_86"></a><a href="#fn_86"><font size="1">1</font></a></p>  
 <p>32. For 12 years Esar-haddon reigned over <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Assyria</span>.</p>  
 <p>33. Saul-suma-yukina in <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Babylon</span>, Assur-bani-pal in <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Assyria</span>, his two sons, sat on the throne.</p>  
 <p align="center">_________</p>  
 <p>34. In the accession year of Saul-suma-yukina in the month Iyyar, <a name="fr_87"></a><a href="#fn_87"><font size="1">2</font></a></p>  
 <p>35. Bel and the gods of <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Accad</span> from the city of <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Assur</span></p>  
 <p>36. had gone forth and on the 11th day of the month Iyyar had entered into <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Babylon</span>.</p>  
 <p>37. In that year [against] the city of <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Kirbitum</span> <a name="fr_88"></a><a href="#fn_88"><font size="1">3</font></a> [there was war]; its king is conquered.</p>  
 <p>38. On the 10th day of the month Tebet Bel-edir-<i>nisi</i> (?) in <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Babylon</span> is seized and put to death.</p>  
 <p align="center">_________</p>  
 <p>39. The first part (of the chronicle) has been written like its original and has been made public.</p>  
 <p>40. The tablet of Ana-Bel-<span style="font-variant:small-caps;">kan</span> the son of Libludhu</p>  
 <p>41. the son of Nis-Sin, by the hand of Ea-iddin the son of</p>  
 <p>42. Ana-Bel-<span style="font-variant:small-caps;">kan</span> the son of Libludhu of Babylon,</p>  
 <p>43. the 5th day of the month … the 22d year of Darius king of <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Babylon</span>,</p>  
 <p>44. the king of the world.</p>
