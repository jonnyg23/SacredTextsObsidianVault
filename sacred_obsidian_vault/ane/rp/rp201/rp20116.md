 <p><a name="page_73"><font size="1" color="green">p. 73</font></a></p>  <h4 align="center">IV. <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Inscription of Entena on a Buttress</span></h4>  
 <p>1. To the goddess <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Gatumdug</span>,<br> 2. the mother of <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Shirpurla-ki</span>,<br> 3. Entena,<br> 4. the patesi<br> 5. of <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Shirpurla-ki</span>,<br> 6. who has built the temple of the goddess <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Gatumdug</span>.<br> 7. His god<br> 8. is the god <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Dun-sir(?)-anna</span>.</p>
