 <p><a name="page_133"><font size="1" color="green">p. 133</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">THE ASSYRIAN EPIC OF THE CREATION</h3>  <h4 align="center">FIRST TABLET OF THE STORY OF THE CREATION</h4>  
 <p align="center"><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Obverse</span></p>  
 <p>1. At that time the heaven above had not yet announced,<br> 2. or the earth beneath recorded, a name;<br> 3. the unopened <a name="fr_259"></a><a href="#fn_258"><font size="1">1</font></a> deep was their generator,<br> 4. <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Mummu-Tiamat</span> (the chaos of the sea) was the mother of them all.<br> 5. Their waters were embosomed as one, <a name="fr_260"></a><a href="#fn_259"><font size="1">2</font></a> and<br> 6. the corn-field <a name="fr_261"></a><a href="#fn_260"><font size="1">3</font></a> was unharvested, the pasture was un-grown.<br> 7. At that time the gods had not appeared, any of them;<br> 8. by no name were they recorded, no destiny (had they fixed).<br> 9. Then the (great) gods were created,<br> 10. <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Lakhmu</span> and <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Lakhamu</span> issued forth (the first), <br> 11. until they grew up (when)<br> 12. <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">An-sar</span> and <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Ki-sar</span> were created.<br> 13. Long were the days, extended (was the time, until)<br> 14. the gods <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Anu</span>, (<span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Bel</span> and <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Ea</span> were born),<br> 15. <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">An-sar</span> and <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Ki-sar</span> (gave them birth).</p>  
 <p align="center"><i>The rest of the tablet is lost</i>.</p>
