 <p><a name="page_20"><font size="1" color="green">p. 20</font></a></p>  <h4 align="center">No. 3.—TRANSLATION OF THE THIRD DYNASTIC TABLET</h4>  
 <p align="center"><i>Obv</i>.—<small>COLUMN I</small></p>  
 <p>Only the ends of two lines in the middle have been preserved.</p>  
 <p>… 600 (years) he reigned.</p>  
 <p>[The kings] … (were) in all.</p>  
 <p align="center"><i>Obv</i>.—<small>COLUMN II</small></p>  
 <p>… ili ……</p>  
 <p>(<span style="font-variant:small-caps;">an</span>) Illadu <a name="fr_39"></a><a href="#fn_39"><font size="1">1</font></a> the son of the same for … (years).</p>  
 <p>Mul-men-nunna …</p>  
 <p><i>Abil</i> (?)-Kis the son of …</p>  
 <p align="center"><i>Obv</i>.—<small>COLUMN III</small></p>  
 <p align="center"><i>Is entirely lost. It contained about seventy line</i>s.</p>  
 <p align="center"><i>Rev</i>.—<small>COLUMN IV</small></p>  
 <p>[The dynasty] of <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Babylon</span>, [11 kings for 294 years].</p>  
 <p>’Sumu-[abi for 15 years].</p>  
 <p>Zabû [for 14 years.]</p>  
 <p>Abil-Sin [for 18 years].</p>  
 <p>Sin-[muballidh for 30 years].</p>  
 <p align="center"><i>The next six lines are destroyed</i>.</p>  
 <p>The 1[1 kings of the dynasty of <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Uru-azagga</span>].</p>  
 <p>For 3[68 years].</p>  
 <p align="center">_________</p>  
 <p>An[man] …</p>  
 <p>Ki[-<span style="font-variant:small-caps;">An</span>-nigas] …</p>  
 <p align="center"><i>The rest of the column is destroyed</i>.</p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p><a name="page_21"><font size="1" color="green">p. 21</font></a></p>  
 <p align="center"><i>Rev</i>.—<small>COLUMN V</small></p>  
 <p>The <i>marshmen</i> (?) of the country of the sea (were) in all:</p>  
 <p align="center">_________</p>  
 <p>The leader of the <i>marshmen</i> (?) of the land of the sea (was) Siminas-sipak the son of Erba-Sin;</p>  
 <p>whose reign was prosperous: his god brought him aid; for 17 years he reigned.</p>  
 <p>In the palace of Sargon (his corpse) was burned.</p>  
 <p>Ea-mukin-zira established himself as king, the son of Kha’smar; <a name="fr_40"></a><a href="#fn_40"><font size="1">1</font></a> for 3 months he reigned.</p>  
 <p>In the vestments of <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Bit-Kha’smar</span> he was burned.</p>  
 <p>Kassû-nadin-akhi the son of Sappâ <a name="fr_41"></a><a href="#fn_41"><font size="1">2</font></a> reigned for 6 years. [He was burned] in the palace.</p>  
 <p>The 3 kings of the dynasty of the country of the Sea reigned for 23 years.</p>  
 <p align="center">_________</p>  
 <p>[E]-ulbar-sakin-sumi the son of Bazi reigned for 15 years: in the palace of <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Kar-Merodach</span> [he was burned].</p>  
 <p>[Uras]-kudurri-utsur the son of Bazi reigned for 2 years.</p>  
 <p>[Silanim]-Suqamuna the son of Bazi reigned for 3 months: in the palace of <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Lu</span> … <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Sa</span> [he was burned].</p>  
 <p align="center">_________</p>  
 <p>[The 3] kings of the dynasty of the house of Bazi reigned for 20 years (and) 3 months.</p>  
 <p>…… a descendant of the race of <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Elam</span> reigned for 6 years.</p>  
 <p>In the palace of Sargon he was burned.</p>  
 <p align="center">_________</p>  
 <p>[One king] of the dynasty of <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Elam</span> reigned for 6 years.</p>  
 <p align="center"><i>The rest of the tablet is lost</i>.</p>
