 <p><a name="page_125"><font size="1" color="green">p. 125</font></a></p>  <h4 align="center">CONTRACT-TABLETS RELATING TO BELSHAZZAR</h4>  
 <p align="center">No. I</p>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">A house</span> belonging to Nebo-akhi-iddin, the son of Sulâ, the son of Egibi, which adjoins the house of Bel-nadin, the son of Rimut, the son of the <i>soldier</i> (?) <a name="fr_426"></a><a href="#fn_422"><font size="1">1</font></a> has been handed over (by Nebo-akhi-iddin) for 3 years to Nebo-yukin-akhi the secretary <a name="fr_427"></a><a href="#fn_423"><font size="1">2</font></a> of Belshazzar, the son of the king, for 1½ manehs of silver, sub-letting of the house being forbidden, as well as interest on the money. (Nebo-yukin-akhi) undertakes to plant trees and repair the house. <a name="fr_428"></a><a href="#fn_424"><font size="1">3</font></a> At the expiration of the 3 years Nebo-akhi-iddin shall repay the money, namely 1½ manehs, to Nebo-yukin-akhi, and Nebo-yukin-akhi shall quit the house in the presence of Nebo-akhi-iddin. The witnesses (are) Kabtiya, the son of Tabnêa, the son of Egibi; Tabik-zira, the son of Nergal-yusallim, the son of Sin-karabi-isime; Nebo-zira-ibni, the son of Ardia; and the priest Bel-akhi-basa, the son of Nebo-baladhsu-iqbi. (Dated) Babylon, the 21st day of Nisan, the 5th year of Nabonidos king of Babylon. <a name="fr_429"></a><a href="#fn_425"><font size="1">4</font></a></p>

 <p align="center">No. III</p>

The money, namely 1 maneh 16 shekels, Nebo-tsabit-idâ, by the agency of Itti-Merodach-baladhu, the son of Nebo-akhi-iddin, the son of Egibi, has presented to Bel-iddina.
