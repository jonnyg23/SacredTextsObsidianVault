 <p><a name="page_107"><font size="1" color="green">p. 107</font></a></p>  <h4 align="center">IX. <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Inscription of Nam-maghâni</span></h4>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">On a Stone from the Threshold of a Door</span> <a name="fr_254"></a><a href="#fn_254"><font size="1">1</font></a></p>  
 <p>1. For the goddess <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Bau</span>,<br> 2. the good lady,<br> 3. the daughter of <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Anna</span>,<br> 4. the mistress of <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Uru-azagga</span>,<br> 5. his mistress,<br> 6. Nam-maghâni,<br> 7. the patesi<br> 8. of <span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Shirpurla</span>,<br> 9. her powerful minister,<br> 10. as the stone of a threshold <a name="fr_255"></a><a href="#fn_255"><font size="1">2</font></a><br> 11. has made this.</p>
