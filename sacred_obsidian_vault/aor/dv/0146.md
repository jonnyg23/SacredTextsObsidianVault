 <h3 align="CENTER">146.</h3>
 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_v108501"></a>
 <img src="img/v108501.jpg"></p>
 <p>
 <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"> The law of the incidence of light.</font></span>The edges of a window which are illuminated by 2 lights of equal
 degrees of brightness will not reflect light of equal brightness
 into the chamber within.
 </p><p>
 If <i>b</i> is a candle and <i>a c</i> our hemisphere both will illuminate the
 edges of the window <i>m</i> <i>n</i>, but light <i>b</i> will only illuminate <i>f
 g</i> and the hemisphere <i>a</i> will light all of <i>d e</i>.
 </p><p>
