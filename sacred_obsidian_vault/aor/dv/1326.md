 <h3 align="CENTER">1326.</h3>
 <p>
 (Of Peasants who work in shirts)
 </p><p>
 Shadows will come from the East which will blacken with great colour
 darkness the sky that covers Italy.
 </p><p>
 (Of the Barbers.)
 </p><p>
 All men will take refuge in Africa.
