 <h3 align="CENTER">1475.</h3>
 <p>
 Archimedes gave the quadrature of a polygonal figure, but not of the circle. Hence
 Archimedes never squared any figure with
 curved sides. He squared the circle minus
 the smallest portion that the intellect can
 conceive, that is the smallest point visible. <a name="fr_518"></a><a href="#fn_518"><font size="1">818</font></a>
