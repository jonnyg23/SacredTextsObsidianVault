 <h3 align="CENTER">955.</h3>
 <p>
 <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"> The ebb and flow of the tide (955-960).</font></span>Where there is a larger quantity of water, there is a greater flow
 and ebb, but the contrary in narrow waters.
 </p><p>
 Look whether the sea is at its greatest flow when the moon is half
 way over our hemisphere [on the meridian].
