 <p>
 </p><h3 align="CENTER">ZOOLOGY AND COMPARATIVE ANATOMY.</h3>
 <p>
 <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"> The divisions of the animal kingdom (816. 817).</font></span><i>Man</i>. The description of man, which includes that of such creatures
 as are of almost the same species, as Apes, Monkeys and the like,
 which are many,
 </p><p>
 <i>The Lion</i> and its kindred, as Panthers.  <a name="fr_90"></a><a href="#fn_90"><font size="1">424</font></a>
  Wildcats (?) Tigers, Leopards, Wolfs, Lynxes, Spanish cats,
 common cats and the like.
 </p><p>
 <i>The Horse</i> and its kindred, as Mule, Ass and the like, with incisor
 teeth above and below.
 </p><p>
 <i>The Bull</i> and its allies with horns and without upper incisors as
 the Buffalo, Stag Fallow Deer, Wild Goat, Swine, Goat, wild Goats
 Muskdeers, Chamois, Giraffe.
