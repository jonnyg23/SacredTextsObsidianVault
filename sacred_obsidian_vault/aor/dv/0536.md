 <h3 align="CENTER">536.</h3>
 <p>
 </p><h3 align="CENTER">HOW HIGH THE POINT OF SIGHT SHOULD BE PLACED.</h3>
 <p>
 <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"> On the choice of a position (536-537)</font></span>The point of sight must be at the level of the eye of an ordinary
 man, and the farthest limit of the plain where it touches the sky
 must be placed at the level of that line where the earth and sky
 meet; excepting mountains, which are independent of it.
