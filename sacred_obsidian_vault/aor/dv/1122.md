 <h3 align="CENTER">1122.</h3>
 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_v227800"></a>
 <img src="img/v227800.jpg"></p>
 <p>
 <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"> On Flying machines (1122-1126).</font></span>Man when flying must stand free from the waist upwards so as to be
 able to balance himself as he does in a boat so that the centre of
 gravity in himself and in the machine may counterbalance each other,
 and be shifted as necessity demands for the changes of its centre of
 resistance.
