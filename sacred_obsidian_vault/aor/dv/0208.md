 <h3 align="CENTER">208.</h3>
 <h3 align="CENTER">THE MIRROR.</h3>
 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_v111500"></a>
 <img src="img/v111500.jpg"></p>
 <p>
 <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"> Experiments with the mirror (208-210).</font></span>If the illuminated object is of the same size as the luminous body
 and as that in which the light is reflected, the amount of the
 reflected light will bear the same proportion to the intermediate
 light as this second light will bear to the first, if both bodies
 are smooth and white.
