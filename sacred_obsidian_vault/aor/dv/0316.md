 <h3 align="CENTER">316.</h3>
 <p>
 From <i>a</i> to <i>b</i>--that is to say from the roots of the hair in front
 to the top of the head--ought to be equal to <i>c</i> <i>d</i>;--that is from
 the bottom of the nose to the meeting of the lips in the middle of
 the mouth. From the inner corner of the eye <i>m</i> to the top of the
 head <i>a</i> is as far as from <i>m</i> down to the chin <i>s</i>. <i>s</i> <i>c</i> <i>f</i> <i>b</i>
 are all at equal distances from each other.
 </p><p>
