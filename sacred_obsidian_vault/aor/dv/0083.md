 <h3 align="CENTER">83.</h3>
 <p>
 </p><h3 align="CENTER">OF THE PLANE OF GLASS.</h3>
 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_v105301"></a>
 <img src="img/v105301.jpg"></p>
 <p>
 <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"> Demostration of perspective by means of a vertical glass plane (83-85).</font></span>Perspective is nothing else than seeing place [or objects] behind a
 plane of glass, quite transparent, on the surface of which the
 objects behind that glass are to be drawn. These can be traced in
 pyramids to the point in the eye, and these pyramids are intersected
 on the glass plane.
