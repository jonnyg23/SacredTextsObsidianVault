 <p></p><p>
 </p><h3 align="CENTER">OF THE SHADOW CAST BY A BODY PLACED BETWEEN 2 EQUAL LIGHTS.</h3>
 <p>
 <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"> The effect of light at different distances.</font></span>A body placed between 2 equal lights will cast 2 shadows of itself
 in the direction of the lines of the 2 lights; and if you move this
 body placing it nearer to one of the lights the shadow cast towards
 the nearer light will be less deep than that which falls towards the
 more distant one.
