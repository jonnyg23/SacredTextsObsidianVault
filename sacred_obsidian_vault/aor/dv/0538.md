 <h3 align="CENTER">538.</h3>
 <p>
 </p><h3 align="CENTER">OF PLACING A FIGURE IN THE FOREGROUND OF A HISTORICAL PICTURE.</h3>
 <p>
 <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"> The apparent size of figures in a picture (538-539)</font></span>You must make the foremost figure in the picture less than the size
 of nature in proportion to the number of braccia at which you place
 it from the front line, and make the others in proportion by the
 above rule.
