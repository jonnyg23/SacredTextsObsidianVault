 <h3 align="CENTER">101.</h3>
 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_v106001"></a>
 <img src="img/v106001.jpg"></p>
 <p>
 Begin from the line <i>m f</i> with the eye below; then go up and do the
 same with the line <i>n f</i>, then with the eye above and close to the 2
 gauges on the ground look at <i>m n</i>; then as <i>c m</i> is to <i>m n</i> so
 will <i>n m</i> be to <i>n s</i>.
 </p><p>
 If <i>a n</i> goes 3 times into <i>f b, m p</i> will do the same into <i>p g</i>.
 Then go backwards so far as that <i>c d</i> goes twice into <i>a n</i> and <i>p
 g</i> will be equal to <i>g h</i>. And <i>m p</i> will go into <i>h p</i> as often as
 <i>d c</i> into <i>o p</i>.
 </p><p>
