 <h3 align="CENTER">1087.</h3>
 <p><a name="page_260"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 260</font></a></p>
 <p></p><p>
 <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"> Libya.</font></span>Describe the mountains of shifting deserts; that is to say the
 formation of waves of sand borne by the wind, and of its mountains
 and hills, such as occur in Libya. Examples may be seen on the wide
 sands of the Po and the Ticino, and other large rivers.
