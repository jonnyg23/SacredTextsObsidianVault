 <h3 align="CENTER">342.</h3>
 <p>
 <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"> The torso from the front and back.</font></span>The width of a man under the arms is the same as at the hips.
 </p><p>
 A man's width across the hips is equal to the distance from the top
 of the hip to the bottom of the buttock, when a man stands
 </p><p><a name="page_182"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 182</font></a></p>
 <p></p><p align="CENTER"><a name="img_v118200"></a>
 <img src="img/v118200.jpg"></p>
 equally balanced on both feet; and there is the same distance from the top
 of the hip to the armpit. The waist, or narrower part above the hips
 will be half way between the arm pits and the bottom of the buttock.
 <p>
