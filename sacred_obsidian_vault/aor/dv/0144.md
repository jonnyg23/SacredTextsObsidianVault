 <h3 align="CENTER">144.</h3>
 <p>
 The eye which looks (at a spot) half way between the shadow and the
 light which
 </p><p><a name="page_85"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 85</font></a></p>
 <p>surrounds the body in shadow will see that the deepest
 shadows on that body will meet
 </p><p align="CENTER"><a name="img_v108500"></a>
 <img src="img/v108500.jpg"></p>
 <p>the eye at equal angles, that is at
 the same angle as that of sight.
 </p><p>
