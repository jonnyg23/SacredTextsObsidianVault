 <h3 align="CENTER">791.</h3>
 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_v209601"></a>
 <img src="img/v209601.jpg"></p>
 <p>
 The window <i>a</i> is well placed under the window <i>c</i>, and the window
 <i>b</i> is badly placed under the pier <i>d</i>, because this latter is
 without support and foundation; mind therefore never to make a break
 under the piers between the windows.
