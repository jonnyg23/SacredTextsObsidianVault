 <h3 align="CENTER">291.</h3>
 <p>
 </p><h3 align="CENTER">OF THE VISIBILITY OF COLOURS.</h3>
 <p>
 Which colour strikes most? An object at a distance is most
 conspicuous, when it is lightest, and the darkest is least visible.
