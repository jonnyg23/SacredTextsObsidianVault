 <p>
 </p><h3 align="CENTER">OF THE SHADOWS OF A LEAF.</h3>
 <p>
 <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"> The proportions of light and shade in a leaf (423-426).</font></span>Sometimes a leaf has three accidents [of light] that is: shade,
 lustre [reflected light] and transparency [transmitted light]. Thus,
 if the light were at <i>n</i> as regards the leaf <i>s</i>, and the eye at
 <i>m</i>, it would see <i>a</i> in full light, <i>b</i> in shadow and <i>c</i>
 transparent.
