 <h3 align="CENTER">163.</h3>
 <p><a name="page_94"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 94</font></a></p>
 <p></p><p>
 <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"> On the relation of derived and primary shadow (163-165).</font></span>The derived shadow can never resemble the body from which it
 proceeds unless the light is of the same form and size as the body
 causing the shadow.
 </p><p>
 The derived shadow cannot be of the same form as the primary shadow
 unless it is intercepted by a plane parallel to it.
