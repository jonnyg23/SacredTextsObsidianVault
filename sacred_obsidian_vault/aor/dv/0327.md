 <h3 align="CENTER">327.</h3>
 <p>
 The whole length of the foot will lie between the elbow and the
 wrist and between the elbow and the inner angle of the arm
 </p><p><a name="page_177"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 177</font></a></p>
 <p>towards the breast when the arm is folded. The foot is as long as the whole
 head of a man, that is from under the chin to the topmost part of
 the head <a name="fr_145"></a><a href="#fn_145"><font size="1">143</font></a>
  in the way here figured.
 </p><p>
