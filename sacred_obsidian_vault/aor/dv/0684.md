 <h3 align="CENTER">684.</h3>
 <p>
   Truth      the sun.<br>
   falsehood   a mask.<br>
   innocence,<br>
   malignity.<br>
 </p><p>
   Fire destroys falsehood,<br>
   that is sophistry, and<br>
   restores truth, driving out<br>
   darkness.<br>
 </p><p>
   Fire may be represented as the destroy of<br>
   all sophistry, and as the<br>
   image and demonstration of truth;<br>
   because it is light and drives<br>
   out darkness which conceals<br>
   all essences [or subtle things].<br>
 </p><p>
  <a name="fr_324"></a><a href="#fn_324"><font size="1">322</font></a>
 
 </p><p><a name="page_357"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 357</font></a></p>
 <p></p><p>
 </p><h3 align="CENTER">TRUTH.</h3>
 <p>
   Fire destroys all sophistry, that is deceit;<br>
   and maintains truth alone, that is gold.<br>
 </p><p>
   Truth at last cannot be hidden.<br>
   Dissimulation  is of no avail. Dissimulation is<br>
   to no purpose before<br>
   so great a judge.<br>
   Falsehood puts on a mask.<br>
   Nothing is hidden under the sun.<br>
 </p><p>
   Fire is to represent truth because it<br>
   destroys all sophistry and lies; and the<br>
   mask is for lying and falsehood<br>
   which conceal truth.<br>
 </p><p>
