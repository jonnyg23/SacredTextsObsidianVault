 <h3 align="CENTER">337.</h3>
 <p>
 <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"> The relative proportions of the torso and of the foot.</font></span>The torso <i>a b</i> in its thinnest part measures a foot; and from <i>a</i>
 to <i>b</i> is 2 feet, which makes two squares to the seat--its thinnest
 part goes 3 times into the length, thus making 3 squares.
