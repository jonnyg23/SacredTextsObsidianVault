 <p></p><h3 align="CENTER">VII.</h3>
 <p>
 </p><h3 align="CENTER">ON THE ATMOSPHERE.</h3>
 <p>
 <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"> Constituents of the atmosphere.</font></span>That the brightness of the air is occasioned by the water which has
 dissolved itself in it into imperceptible molecules. These, being
 lighted by the sun from the opposite side, reflect the brightness
 which is visible in the air; and the azure which is seen in it is
 caused by the darkness that is hidden beyond the air.  <a name="fr_196"></a><a href="#fn_196"><font size="1">530</font></a>
