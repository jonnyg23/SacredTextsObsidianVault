 <h3 align="CENTER">319.</h3>
 <p>
 <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"> Proportions of the head seen in front (319-321).</font></span><i>a n o f</i> are equal to the mouth.
 </p><p>
 <i>a c</i> and <i>a f</i> are equal to the space between one eye and the
 other.
 </p><p>
 <i>n m o f q r</i> are equal to half the width of the eye lids, that is
 from the inner [lachrymatory] corner of the eye to its outer corner;
 and in like manner the division between the chin and the mouth; and
 in the same way the narrowest part of the nose between the eyes. And
 these spaces, each in itself, is the 19th part of the head, <i>n o</i> is
 equal to the length of the eye or of the space between the eyes.
 </p><p>
 <i>m c</i> is 1/3 of <i>n m</i> measuring from the outer corner of the eyelids
 to the letter <i>c</i>. <i>b s</i> will be equal to the width of the nostril.
 </p><p>
