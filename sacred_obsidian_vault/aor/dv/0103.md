 <p>
 </p><h3 align="CENTER">PERSPECTIVE.</h3>
 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_v106100"></a>
 <img src="img/v106100.jpg"></p>
 <p>
 Let <i>f</i> be the level and distance of the eye; and <i>a</i> the vertical
 plane, as high as a man; let <i>e</i> be a man, then I say that on the
 plane this will be the distance from the plane to the 2nd man.
