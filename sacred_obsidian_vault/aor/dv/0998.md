 <h3 align="CENTER">998.</h3>
 <p>
 </p><h3 align="CENTER">OF MOTION.</h3>
 <p>
 I ask whether the true motion of the clouds can be known by the
 motion of their shadows; and in like manner of the motion of the
 sun.
