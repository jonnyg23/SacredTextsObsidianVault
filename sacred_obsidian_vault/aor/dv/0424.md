 <h3 align="CENTER">424.</h3>
 <p><a name="page_216"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 216</font></a></p>
 <p></p><p>
 A leaf with a concave surface seen from the under side and
 up-side-down will sometimes show itself as half in shade, and half
 transparent. Thus, if <i>o p</i> is the leaf and the light <i>m</i> and the
 eye <i>n</i>, this will see <i>o</i> in shadow because the light does not fall
 upon it between equal angles, neither on the upper nor the under
 side, and <i>p</i> is lighted on the upper side and the light is
 transmitted to its under side.  <a name="fr_218"></a><a href="#fn_218"><font size="1">216</font></a>
