 <p>
 <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"> Relative proportions of the foot and of the face (325--327).</font></span>The foot, from where it is attached to the leg, to the tip of the
 great toe is as long as the space between the upper part of the chin
 and the roots of the hair <i>a b</i>; and equal to five sixths of the
 face.
