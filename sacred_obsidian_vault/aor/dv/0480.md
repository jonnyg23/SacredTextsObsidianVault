 <h3 align="CENTER">480.</h3>
 <p>
 When the air is condensed into rain it would produce a vacuum if the
 rest of the air did not prevent this by filling its place, as
 </p><p><a name="page_240"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 240</font></a></p>
 <p>it does with a violent rush; and this is the wind which rises in the
 summer time, accompanied by heavy rain.
