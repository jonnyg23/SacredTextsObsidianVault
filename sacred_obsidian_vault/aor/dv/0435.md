 <h3 align="CENTER">435.</h3>
 <p>
 <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"> A classification of trees according to their colours.</font></span>The trees in a landscape are of various kinds of green, inasmuch as
 some verge
 </p><p><a name="page_220"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 220</font></a></p>
 <p>towards blackness, as firs, pines, cypresses, laurels,
 box and the like. Some tend to yellow such as walnuts, and pears,
 vines and verdure. Some are both yellowish and dark as chesnuts,
 holm-oak. Some turn red in autumn as the service-tree, pomegranate,
 vine, and cherry; and some are whitish as the willow, olive, reeds
 and the like. Trees are of various forms ...
