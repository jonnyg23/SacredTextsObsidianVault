 <h3 align="CENTER">1055.</h3>
 <p>
 <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"> Alessandria in Piedmont (1055. 1056).</font></span>At Candia in Lombardy, near Alessandria della Paglia, in making a
 well for Messer Gualtieri  <a name="fr_240"></a><a href="#fn_240"><font size="1">574</font></a>
  of Candia, the
 skeleton of a very large boat was found about 10 braccia
 underground; and as the timber was black and fine, it seemed good to
 the said Messer Gualtieri to have the mouth of the well lengthened
 in such a way as that the ends of the boat should be uncovered.
 </p><p>
