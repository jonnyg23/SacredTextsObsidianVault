 <h3 align="CENTER">760.</h3>
 <p>
 <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"> On the proportions of a courtyard.</font></span>In the courtyard the walls must be half the height of its width,
 that is if the court be 40 braccia, the house must be 20 high as
 regards the walls of the said courtyard; and this courtyard must be
 half as wide as the whole front.
