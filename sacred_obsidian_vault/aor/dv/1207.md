 <h3 align="CENTER">1207.</h3>
 <p>
 <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"> Against alchemists (1207. 1208).</font></span>The false interpreters of nature declare that quicksilver is the
 common seed of every metal, not remembering that nature varies the
 seed according to the variety of the things she desires to produce
 in the world.
