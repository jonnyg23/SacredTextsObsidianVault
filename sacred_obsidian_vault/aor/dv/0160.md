 <h3 align="CENTER">160.</h3>
 <p>
 </p><h3 align="CENTER">SHADOW AND LIGHT.</h3>
 <p>
 <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"> Different sorts of derived shadows (160-162).</font></span>The forms of shadows are three: inasmuch as if the solid body which
 casts the shadow is equal (in size) to the light, the shadow
 resembles
 </p><p align="CENTER"><a name="img_v109200"></a>
 <img src="img/v109200.jpg"></p>
 <p>a column without any termination (in length). If the body
 is larger than the light the shadow resembles a truncated and
 inverted pyramid, and its length has also no defined termination.
 But if the body is smaller than the light, the shadow will resemble
 a pyramid and come to an end, as is seen in eclipses of the moon.
 </p><p>
