 <p></p><p>
 </p><h3 align="CENTER">MEMORARDUM.</h3>
 <p>
 To make some provisions for my garden,
 --Giordano, <i>De Ponderibus</i> <a name="fr_447"></a><a href="#fn_447"><font size="1">753</font></a>
 ,--the peacemaker,
 the flow and ebb of the sea,--have two
 baggage trunks made, look to Beltraffio's  <a name="fr_448"></a><a href="#fn_448"><font size="1">754</font></a>
  lathe
 and have taken the stone,--out leave the books
 belonging to Messer Andrea the German,--
 make scales of a long reed and weigh the
 substance when hot and again when cold.
 The mirror of Master Luigi; <i>A b</i> the flow
 and ebb of the water is shown at the mill
 of Vaprio,--a cap.
 </p><p>
