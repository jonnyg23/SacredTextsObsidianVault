 <h3 align="CENTER">333.</h3>
 <p>
 <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"> The relative proportions of the torso and of the whole figure.</font></span>The cubit is one fourth of the height of a man and is equal to the
 greatest width of the shoulders. From the joint of one shoulder to
 the other is two faces and is equal to the distance from the top of
 the breast to the navel.  <a name="fr_157"></a><a href="#fn_157"><font size="1">155</font></a>
  From this
 point to the genitals is a face's length.
 </p><p>
