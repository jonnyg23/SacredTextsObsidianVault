 <h3 align="CENTER">1529.</h3>
 <p>
 Memorandum of the money I have had
 from the King as my salary from July 1508
 till April next 1509. First 100 scudi, then
 70, then 50, then 20 and then 200 florins at
 48 soldi the florin.
