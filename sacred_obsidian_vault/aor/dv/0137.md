 <h3 align="CENTER">137.</h3>
 <p>
 </p><h3 align="CENTER">SHOWS HOW LIGHT FROM ANY SIDE CONVERGES TO ONE POINT.</h3>
 <p>
 <span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"> Experiments on the relation of light and shadow within a room (137--140).</font></span>Although the balls <i>a b c</i> are lighted from one window,
 nevertheless, if you follow the lines of their shadows you will see
 they intersect at a point forming the angle <i>n</i>.
