 </p><h3 align="CENTER">TO REPRESENT INGRATITUDE.</h3>
 <p>
   When the sun appears<br>
   which dispels darkness in<br>
   general, you put out the<br>
   light which dispelled it<br>
   for you in particular<br>
   for your need and convenience.<br>
