 <p>
 </p><h3 align="CENTER">OF PAINTING.</h3>
 <p>
 Of the nature of movements in man. Do not repeat the same gestures
 in the limbs of men unless you are compelled by the necessity of
 their action, as is shown in <i>a b</i>.
