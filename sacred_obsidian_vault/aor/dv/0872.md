 <h3 align="CENTER">872.</h3>
 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_v214301"></a>
 <img src="img/v214301.jpg"></p>
 <p>
 <i>a</i>, the side of the body in light and shade <i>b</i>, faces the whole
 portion of the hemisphere bed <i>e f</i>, and does not face any part of
 the darkness of the earth. And the same occurs at the point <i>o</i>;
 therefore the space a <i>o</i> is throughout of one and the same
 brightness, and s faces only four degrees of the hemisphere <i>d e f g
 h</i>, and also the whole of the earth <i>s h</i>, which will render it
 darker; and how much must be demonstrated by calculation.  <a name="fr_116"></a><a href="#fn_116"><font size="1">450</font></a>
