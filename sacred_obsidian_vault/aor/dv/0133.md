 <p>
 </p><h3 align="CENTER">OF THE HIGHEST LIGHTS WHICH TURN AND MOVE AS THE EYE MOVES WHICH SEES THE OBJECT.</h3>
 <p>
 Suppose the body to be the round object figured here and let the
 light be at the point <i>a</i>, and let the illuminated side of the
 object be <i>b c</i> and the eye at the point <i>d</i>: I say that, as lustre
 is every where and complete in each part, if you stand at the point
 <i>d</i> the lustre will appear at <i>c</i>, and in proportion as the eye
 moves from <i>d</i> to <i>a</i>, the lustre will move from <i>c</i> to <i>n</i>.
