 <h1 align="CENTER">The Wandering Host</h1>
 <h2 align="CENTER">by David Starr Jordan</h2>
 <h4 align="CENTER">[1904]</h4>
 <hr>
 <p>This is an allegorical tale about the search for spiritual meaning.
 Jordan was the first President of Stanford University.
 This etext was scanned from an copy published in 1904, in an edition
 with artwork reminiscent of the "Arts and Crafts" style originated
 by William Morris.
 </p>
 <p>
 There were no internal page numbers, so the page breaks are indicated by
 a 'vertical bar' character with the note 'page' in the margin in green type.
 </p>
