 This is a comprehensive study of the mythology of the dragon on a world-wide
 scale.
 Ingersoll deals with this archetypal beast in the lore of India, Korea,
 Japan, China, Wales, as well as the English legend of St. George.
 Anyone wishing to study dragons will find this a treasure-trove of
 information on the subject.
