 <p>
 This page indexes etexts which don't currently
 have a category at this site,
 are just plain uncategorizable, or deal with a wide
 range of religions, mythologies or folklore.</p>
 <p>
 Several of these have been contributed by Eliza at
 at <a href="https://www.sacredspiral.com/">https://www.sacredspiral.com/</a> [External Site]. These are marked [EY].
 </p>

 by <span class="c_a">W.H. Matthews</span> [<span class="c_d">1922</span>]<br>
 <span class="c_b">A classic study of mazes and their symbolism, with dozens of illustrations.</span>

 <p>

 <span class="c_b">A comprehensive reference to Elizabethan folklore and cultural references in Shakespeare and his contemporaries.</span>

 by <span class="c_a">Robert Brown</span> [<span class="c_d">1881</span>]<br>
 <span class="c_b">Explore the deep mythological significance of the unicorn.</span>
 </span>
 </p>
 
 
 <p>

 by <span class="c_a">Padraic Colum</span> [<span class="c_d">1930</span>]<br>

 by <span class="c_a">John Bathurst Deane</span> [<span class="c_d">1833</span>]<br>
 <span class="c_b">A very detailed but dated study of serpent-worship, particularly relating it to the Biblical story of the Garden of Eden.</span>
 </span>
