 <p><a name="page_378"><font size="1" color="green">p. 378</font></a></p>  <h2 align="center">[SECT. CXLIV.—EMPEROR AN-KŌ (PART I.—HE SLAYS KING OHO-KUSAKA).]</h2>  
 <p>The august child <a name="fr_2247"></a><a href="#fn_2242"><font size="1">1</font></a> Anaho dwelt at the palace of Anaho at Isonokami, <a name="fr_2248"></a><a href="#fn_2243"><font size="1">2</font></a> and ruled the Empire. The Heavenly Sovereign sent the Grandee of Ne, <a name="fr_2249"></a><a href="#fn_2244"><font size="1">3</font></a> ancestor of the Grandees of Sakamoto, to the residence of King Oho-kusaka, on behalf of his younger brother Prince Oho-hatsuse to command thus: "I wish Thine Augustness's younger sister</p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p><a name="page_379"><font size="1" color="green">p. 379</font></a></p>  
 <p><span class="contnote"><font size="-2" color="GREEN">[paragraph continues]</font></span> Queen Waka-kusaka to wed Prince Oho-hatsuse. So do thou present her." <a name="fr_2250"></a><a href="#fn_2245"><font size="1">4</font></a> Then King Oho-kusaka did obeisance four times, and said: "Owing to a supposition that there might be some such Great Commands, I have kept her always indoor. <a name="fr_2251"></a><a href="#fn_2246"><font size="1">5</font></a> With reverence <a name="fr_2252"></a><a href="#fn_2247"><font size="1">6</font></a> will I respectfully offer her according to the Great Commands." Nevertheless, thinking it disrespectful [merely] to send a message, <a name="fr_2253"></a><a href="#fn_2248"><font size="1">7</font></a> he forthwith, as a ceremonial gift <a name="fr_2254"></a><a href="#fn_2249"><font size="1">8</font></a> from his younger sister, made [the Grandee of Ne] take a pushwood jewel head-dress <a name="fr_2255"></a><a href="#fn_2250"><font size="1">9</font></a> to present [to the Heavenly Sovereign]. The Grandee of Ne forthwith stole the jewel headdress meant as a ceremonial gift, and slandered King Oho-kusaka, saying: "King Oho-kusaka would not receive the Imperial Commands, but said: "An soror mea fiet ejusdem stirpis [viri] inferior storea?" <a name="fr_2256"></a><a href="#fn_2251"><font size="1">10</font></a> and, grasping the hilt of his cross-sword, <a name="fr_2257"></a><a href="#fn_2252"><font size="1">11</font></a> was angry." So the Heavenly Sovereign, having in his great anger slain King Oho-kusaka, took that King's chief wife Nagata-no-oho-iratsume, <a name="fr_2258"></a><a href="#fn_2253"><font size="1">12</font></a> and made her Empress.</p>
