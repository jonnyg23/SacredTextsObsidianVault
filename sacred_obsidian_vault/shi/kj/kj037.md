 <p><a name="page_112"><font size="1" color="green">p. 112</font></a> [93]</p>  <h2 align="center">[SECT. XXX.—THE AUGUST DELIBERATION FOR PACIFYING THE LAND.]</h2>  
 <p>The Heaven-Shining-Great-August-Deity commanded, saying: "The Luxuriant-Reed-Plains-the-Land-of-Fresh-Rice-ears-of-a-Thousand-Autumns,—Of Long-Five-Hundred-Autumns <a name="fr_633"></a><a href="#fn_629"><font size="1">1</font></a> is the land which my august child His Augustness Truly-Conqueror-I-Conquer-Conquering-Swift-Heavenly-Great-Great-Ears <a name="fr_634"></a><a href="#fn_630"><font size="1">2</font></a> shall govern." Having [thus] deigned to charge him, she sent him down from Heaven. <a name="fr_635"></a><a href="#fn_631"><font size="1">3</font></a> Hereupon His Augustness Heavenly-Great-Great-Ears, standing on the Floating Bridge of Heaven, <a name="fr_636"></a><a href="#fn_632"><font size="1">4</font></a> said: "The Luxuriant-Reed-Plains-the-Land-of-Fresh-Rice-ears-of-a-Thousand-Autumns,—of Long-Five-Hundred-Autumns is painfully uproarious,—it is." <a name="fr_637"></a><a href="#fn_633"><font size="1">5</font></a> With this announcement, he immediately re-ascended, and informed the Heaven-Shining-Great-August-Deity. Then the High-August-Producing-Wondrous-Deity <a name="fr_638"></a><a href="#fn_634"><font size="1">6</font></a> and the Heaven-Shining-Great-August-Deity commanded the eight hundred myriad Deities to assemble in a divine assembly in the bed of the Tranquil River of Heaven, <a name="fr_639"></a><a href="#fn_635"><font size="1">7</font></a> and caused the Deity Thought-Includer <a name="fr_640"></a><a href="#fn_636"><font size="1">8</font></a> to think [of a plan], and said: <a name="fr_641"></a><a href="#fn_637"><font size="1">9</font></a> "This Central Land of Reed-Plains is the land with which we</p>

 <p><a name="page_113"><font size="1" color="green">p. 113</font></a></p>  
 <p>have deigned to charge our august child as the land which he shall govern. So as he deems that violent and savage Earthly Deities <a name="fr_642"></a><a href="#fn_638"><font size="1">10</font></a> are numerous in this land, [69] which Deity shall we send to subdue them?" Then the Deity Thought-Includer and likewise the eight hundred myriad Deities took counsel and said: "The Deity Ame-no-ho-hi <a name="fr_643"></a><a href="#fn_639"><font size="1">11</font></a> is the one that should be sent." So they sent the Deity Ame-no-ho-hi; but he at once curried favour with the Deity Master-of-the-Great-Land, and for three years brought back no report.</p>

 <p><a name="fn_630"></a><a href="kj037.htm#fr_634">112:2</a> See Sect. XIII, Note 18. Henceforward this tremendous name is mostly abbreviated to <i>Ame-no-oshi-ho-mimi</i> (probably signifying "Heavenly-Great-Great-Ears.")</p>
