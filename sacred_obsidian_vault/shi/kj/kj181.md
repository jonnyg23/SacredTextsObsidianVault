 <p>His Augustness Hiro-kuni-oshi-take-<i>kana</i>-hi dwelt in the Palace of Kanahashi at Magari, and ruled the Empire. This Heavenly Monarch had no august children. His august grave is at the village of Takaya in Furuchi in Kafuchi.</p>
