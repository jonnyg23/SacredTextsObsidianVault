 <p><a name="page_332"><font size="1" color="green">p. 332</font></a></p>  <h2 align="center">[SECT. CXVIII.—EMPEROR Ō-JIN (PART XV.—HIS AGE AND PLACE OF BURIAL).]</h2>  
 <p>The august years of this Heavenly Sovereign Homuda were altogether one hundred and thirty. His august mausoleum is on the mound of Mofusu <a name="fr_2022"></a><a href="#fn_2017"><font size="1">1</font></a> at Wega in Kafuchi.</p>

 <h3 align="CENTER">Footnotes</h3>
 <p><a name="fn_2017"></a><a href="kj125.htm#fr_2022">332:1</a> <a name="page_332"><font size="1" color="green">p. 332</font></a> Or, as Motowori reads it, <i>Mofushi</i>. The etymology is uncertain. <i>Wega</i> has already appeared in Sect. CIII (Note 3). The Old Printed Edition and some Manuscripts have at the conclusion of this volume the following note: <a name="img_33200"></a>
 <img src="img/33200.jpg">, "It is the mausoleum of Mozu." But Mozu is in the province of Idzumi, and all the later editions discard this note as an interpolation.</p>
