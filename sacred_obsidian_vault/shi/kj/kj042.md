 <p><a name="page_138"><font size="1" color="green">p. 138</font></a></p>  <h2 align="center">[SECT. XXXV.—THE DUCHESS OF SARU.]</h2>  
 <p>So then he charged Her Augustness the Heavenly-Alarming-Female [saying]: "Do thou, who wast the one to make known this Great Deity Prince of Saruta who respectfully served as my august vanguard, <a name="fr_768"></a><a href="#fn_764"><font size="1">1</font></a> respectfully escort him [back]; and do thou likewise bear the august name of that Deity, and respectfully serve me." Wherefore the Duchesses of Saru bear the name of the Male Deity the Prince of Saruta, and the women are Duchesses of Saru. <a name="fr_769"></a><a href="#fn_765"><font size="1">2</font></a></p>
