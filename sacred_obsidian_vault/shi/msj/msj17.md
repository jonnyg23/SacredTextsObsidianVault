 <p><a name="page_92"><font size="1" color="green">p. 92</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">AUTUMN THOUGHTS</h3>  
 <p align="center"><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">By Tachibane no Masato</span></p>  
 <p>  In the chill Autumn breeze<br> A lonely insect weeping<br>   Heavy dew-tears forlorn.<br> Tell me, what feelings sweeping<br>   Through its heart, passion-torn,<br> Shake its frail frame with weeping?</p>  <h3 align="center">THE BARRIER AT NAKOSO</h3>  
 <p>Gone is the barrier that of old<br>      Had crossed my way,<br> But in its place my eyes behold,<br>      As on I stray—<br> The wild winds sweep a scented cloud<br> Of fluttering petals from the cherry-trees—<br> Into the narrow pathway, where they crowd<br>      Whirled by the breeze<br>      And bid my footsteps stay,</p>  
 <p><a name="page_93"><font size="1" color="green">p. 93</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">THE DRAGON-FLIES</h3>  
 <p>The gold sun shimmering in noontide skies<br> Shines down, where the red-burnished dragonflies<br> Flit to and fro in the translucent haze<br> Over the village of eventless days!</p>  <h3 align="center">CHERRY-BLOSSOM</h3>  
 <p align="center"><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">By Tomonori—Early 10th Century</span></p>  
 <p>The cherry-blossom gleams, a pearly haze,<br> Across the landscape, far as eye can see,<br> Like mist-wreaths veiling in their shifting maze<br> Yoshino's mountain-gorges’ mystery.</p>  <h3 align="center">GLORY OF YOSHINO</h3>  
 <p align="center"><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">By Teitoku</span></p>  
 <p>Would that some stranger hitherward would stray,<br> Unto whose wondering eyes I might display<br> The blossoms' glory in spring sunrise glowing,<br> Rose-pink of dawn upon a mist of grey,<br> Yoshino's cherries on her mountains blowing.</p>
