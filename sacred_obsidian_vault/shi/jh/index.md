 <p>
 This is a short collection of a couple of hundred Japanese Haiku.
 This was printed by the art book small press, Peter Pauper.
 In the original each haiku is accompanied by one of 59 Japanese seals.
 These seals are reproduced here.
 </p>
 <p>
 The divisions in the body of the text are arbitrary and were not
 in the original book.
 </p>
