 <h1 align="CENTER">NOH THEATER</h1>
 <p align="CENTER">Mystery Science Theater 3000, sketch during <i>Invasion of the Neptune Men</i> (MSTK #819).</p>
 <p align="CENTER">DRAMATIS PERSONÆ</p>
 <p align="CENTER">MIKE NELSON</p>
 <p align="CENTER">CROW T. ROBOT, A 'BOT</p>
 <p align="CENTER">TOM SERVO, A 'BOT</p>
 <p align="CENTER">GYPSY, A 'BOT</p>
 <p align="CENTER">Location: Bridge of the Satellite of Love.</p>
 <p>CROW</p><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <p>Hello and....</p></dir>
 </dir>
 
 <p>BOTS</p><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <p>Konnichi wa!</p></dir>
 </dir>
 
 <p>SERVO</p><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <p>Tonight we present a traditional Japanese Kabuki play. Translated to English, this ancient work is entitled <i>Neil Simon's The Sunshine Boys</i>.</p>
 <p>Now, how many of you are familiar with Japanese theater?</p></dir>
 </dir>
 
 <p>(NELSON <i>looks around. He's the only one in an audience of folding chairs</i>).</p><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <p>Anybody field this one... Anyone at all...(NELSON <i>tentatively raises his hand</i>) Ah yes, you sir; and you enjoy Kabuki theater?</p></dir>
 </dir>
 
 <p>NELSON</p><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <p>Uh, actually I prefer Noh theater.</p></dir>
 </dir>
 
 <p>CROW</p><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <p>Well, then, why did you raise your hand?</p></dir>
 </dir>
 
 <p>NELSON</p><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <p>Because I like Noh theater. Noh plays are my favorite.</p></dir>
 </dir>
 
 <p>SERVO</p><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <p>So you don't like any theater at all?</p></dir>
 </dir>
 
 <p>NELSON</p><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <p>No, let me explain. Noh theater is classic Japanese drama. Noh plays have been produced since the 13th century, and Noh actors are revered even today.</p></dir>
 </dir>
 
 <p>CROW</p><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <p>Why are you dissing Japanese theater? What's your deal, man?</p></dir>
 </dir>
 
 <p>NELSON</p><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <p>No, wait a minute! Noh theater started in Japan...</p></dir>
 </dir>
 
 <p>SERVO</p><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <p>Oh, so now you tell us Japan doesn't have any theater whatsoever?</p></dir>
 </dir>
 
 <p>NELSON</p><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <p>They have lots of theater, including Noh theater!</p></dir>
 </dir>
 
 <p>CROW</p><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <p>So they have lots of theater and they have no theater?</p></dir>
 </dir>
 
 <p>NELSON</p><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <p>Exactly!</p></dir>
 </dir>
 
 <p>SERVO</p><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <p>No theater?</p></dir>
 </dir>
 
 <p>CROW</p><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <p>What?</p></dir>
 </dir>
 
 <p>NELSON</p><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <p>Yes!</p></dir>
 </dir>
 
 <p>BOTS</p><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <p>WHAT?!</p></dir>
 </dir>
 
 <p>NELSON</p><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <p>Noh theater!!!! N...O....H!!!!</p></dir>
 </dir>
 
 <p>SERVO</p><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <p>N-O-H, huh? There you just go and show what an idiot you are!</p></dir>
 </dir>
 
 <p>CROW</p><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <p>I'll handle this, Tom, calm down! Mike: I'm going to ask you a series of simple questions which even a cretin like you could answer yes or no.</p>
 <p>Now, is there theater in Japan?</p></dir>
 </dir>
 
 <p>NELSON</p><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <p>Yes.</p></dir>
 </dir>
 
 <p>CROW</p><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <p>Good, and do you have a particular favorite type of Japanese theater?</p></dir>
 </dir>
 
 <p>NELSON</p><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <p>Yes.</p></dir>
 </dir>
 
 <p>CROW</p><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <p>Well, good, now we're getting somewhere! Mike: will you tell me the name of your favorite form of Japanese theater?</p></dir>
 </dir>
 
 <p>NELSON</p><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <p>Noh!</p></dir>
 </dir>
 
 <p>CROW</p><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <p>Why not?</p></dir>
 </dir>
 
 <p>NELSON</p><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <p>Why not what?!</p></dir>
 </dir>
 
 <p>CROW</p><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <p>Why won't you tell me your favorite Japanese theater?</p></dir>
 </dir>
 
 <p>NELSON</p><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <p>I just did.</p></dir>
 </dir>
 
 <p>CROW</p><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <p>Did what?!</p></dir>
 </dir>
 
 <p>NELSON (<i>spits out each word</i>)</p><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <p>Told you my favorite form of Japanese theater!</p></dir>
 </dir>
 
 <p>CROW</p><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <p>You did?</p></dir>
 </dir>
 
 <p>NELSON</p><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <p>Yes!!!</p></dir>
 </dir>
 
 <p>CROW</p><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <p>Well, will you tell me again?</p></dir>
 </dir>
 
 <p>NELSON</p><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <p>Yes! NOH!</p></dir>
 </dir>
 
 <p>SERVO</p><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <p>Oh, come on!</p></dir>
 </dir>
 
 <p>CROW</p><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <p>Mike, I'm going to grab a step ladder so you can jump up my butt!</p></dir>
 </dir>
 
 <p>SERVO</p><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <p>May we do our little Kabuki play now?</p></dir>
 </dir>
 
 <p>NELSON</p><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <p>Yes, but just remember I like Noh theater better than I like Kabuki theater.</p></dir>
 </dir>
 
 <p>CROW</p><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <p>I thought you didn't like Kabuki!</p></dir>
 </dir>
 
 <p>NELSON</p><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <p>I do!!!</p></dir>
 </dir>
 
 <p>BOTS (<i>Sobbing</i>)</p>
 <p>NELSON (<i>Aside to fourth wall</i>)</p><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <p>You got to mess with them every now and then...(<i>Commercial sign flashes</i>) We'll be right back.</p></dir>
 </dir>
 
 <p>GYPSY</p><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <p>I get it, Mike...</p></dir>
 </dir>
