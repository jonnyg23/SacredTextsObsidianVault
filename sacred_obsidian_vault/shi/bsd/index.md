 <p>
 This is Inazo Nitobe's account of the Samurai code, Bushido.
 This was a far-reaching philosophy of life which governed every action
 of the warrior caste.
 An understanding of Bushido is crucial if you want to understand
 Japanese culture at a deep level.
 </p>
