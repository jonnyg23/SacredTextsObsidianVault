 <img src="img/03200.jpg"></p>  <h3 align="center">32</h3>  <h3 align="center">TSURAKI HARUMICHI</h3>  <h3 align="center">HARUMICHI NO TSURAKI</h3>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">  Yama gawa ni<br> Kaze no kaketaru<br>   Shigarami wa<br> Nagare mo aenu<br> Momiji nari keri.  
 <p>THE stormy winds of yesterday<br>   The maple branches shook;<br> And see! a mass of crimson leaves<br>   Has lodged within that nook,<br>   And choked the mountain brook.</p></div>  
 <p><small>The writer of this verse died in the year 864.</small></p>
