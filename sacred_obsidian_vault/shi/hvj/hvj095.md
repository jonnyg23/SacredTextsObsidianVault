 <img src="img/09400.jpg"></p>  <h3 align="center">94</h3>  <h3 align="center">THE PRIVY COUNCILLOR MASATSUNE</h3>  <h3 align="center">SANGI MASATSUNE</h3>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">  Miyoshino no<br> Yama no aki kaze<br>   Sayo fukete<br> Furu sato samuku<br> Koromo utsu nari.  
 <p>AROUND Mount Miyoshino's crest<br>   The autumn winds blow drear;<br> The villagers are beating cloth,<br>   Their merry din I hear,<br>   This night so cold and clear.</p></div>  
 <p><small>Masatsune was a son of the writer of verse No. <a href="hvj084.htm#page_83">83</a>; he died in the year 1221. He appears in the illustration sitting alone in his house, listening to the sound of the villagers beating the cloth to make it supple.</small></p>
