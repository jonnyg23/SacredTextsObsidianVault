 <img src="img/06800.jpg"></p>  <h3 align="center">68</h3>  <h3 align="center">THE RETIRED EMPEROR SANJŌ</h3>  <h3 align="center">SANJŌ IN</h3>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">  Kokoro ni mo<br> Arade uki yo ni<br>   Nagaraeba<br> Koishikarubeki<br> Yowa no tsuki kana.  
 <p>IF in this troubled world of ours<br>   I still must linger on,<br> My only friend shall be the moon,<br>   Which on my sadness shone,<br>   When other friends were gone.</p></div>  
 <p><small>The Emperor Sanjō, who reigned A.D. 1012-1015) was the son of the Emperor Reisei; he fell into ill health, his palace was burnt down twice, and he was forced to abdicate by Michinaga Fujiwara (see verse No. <a href="hvj060.htm#page_59">59</a>)</small></p>
