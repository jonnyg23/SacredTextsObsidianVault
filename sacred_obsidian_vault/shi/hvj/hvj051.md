 <img src="img/05000.jpg"></p>  <h3 align="center">50</h3>  <h3 align="center">YOSHITAKA FUJIWARA</h3>  <h3 align="center">FUJIWARA NO YOSHITAKA</h3>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">  Kimi ga tame<br> Oshikarazarishi<br>   Inochi sae<br> Nagaku mogana to<br> Omoi keru kana.  
 <p>DEATH had no terrors, Life no joys,<br>   Before I met with thee;<br> But now I fear, however long<br>   My life may chance to be,<br>   ’Twill be too short for me!</p></div>  
 <p><small>Yoshitaka died in the year 974. See verse No. <a href="hvj019.htm#page_18">18</a> for a note of the Fujiwara family.</small></p>
