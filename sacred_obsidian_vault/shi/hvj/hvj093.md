 <img src="img/09200.jpg"></p>  <h3 align="center">92</h3>  <h3 align="center">SANUKI, IN ATTENDANCE ON THE RETIRED EMPEROR NIJŌ</h3>  <h3 align="center">NIJŌ IN SANUKI</h3>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">  Waga sode wa<br> Shiohi ni mienu<br>   Oki no ishi no<br> Hito koso shirane<br> Kawaku ma mo nashi.  
 <p>MY sleeve is wet with floods of tears<br>   As here I sit and cry;<br> ’Tis wetter than a low-tide rock,—<br>   No one, howe’er he try,<br>   Can find a spot that 's dry!</p></div>  
 <p><small>The Lady Sanuki was one of the Minamoto family, and lived at the Court of the Emperor Nijō, who reigned A.D. 1159-1165. She was the daughter of the retired Emperor Goshirakawa, and died A.D. 1165.</small></p>
