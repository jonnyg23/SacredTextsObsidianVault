 <img src="img/08400.jpg"></p>  <h3 align="center">84</h3>  <h3 align="center">THE MINISTER KIYO-SUKE FUJIWARA</h3>  <h3 align="center">FUJIWARA NO KIYO-SUKE ASON</h3>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">  Nagaraeba<br> Mata konogoro ya<br>   Shinobaremu<br> Ushi to mishi yo zo<br> Ima wa koishiki.  
 <p>TIME was when I despised my youth,<br>   As boyhood only can;<br> What would I give for boyhood now,<br>   When finishing life's span<br>   An old decrepid man!</p></div>  
 <p><small>Kiyo-suke was the son of the writer of verse No. <a href="hvj080.htm#page_79">79</a>, and lived in the latter part of the twelfth century.</small></p>
