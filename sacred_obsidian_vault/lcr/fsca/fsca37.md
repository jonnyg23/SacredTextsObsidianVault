 <img src="img/16200.jpg" alt="Opinicus statant."></p>  <h3 align="center">The Opinicus, or Epimacus</h3>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">This</span> creature appears to be a variety of the griffin family. Authorities blazon it as having its body and four legs like those of a lion; the head and neck and wings like an eagle, and the short tail of a camel, sometimes borne sans wings.</p>  
 <p>Such a monster with wings endorsed or, was the crest of the <i>Barber Surgeons of London</i>.</p>  
 <p><i>Two opinici vert, purfled or, beaked sable, wings gules</i>, support the insignia of the <i>Plasterers’ Company</i>.</p>
