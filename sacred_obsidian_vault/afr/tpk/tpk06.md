   <h1 align="center">BALM YARD</h1>  
 <p>First and last every soul for admission must be believers in the power of King Ras Tafari the living God.</p>  
 <p>An admission fee must be paid in advance from four shillings up according to the power and duration of the miserable infirmities whereof one is afflicted (Special Notice).</p>  
 <p>Sometimes King Alpha has to perform special medical attention.</p>
