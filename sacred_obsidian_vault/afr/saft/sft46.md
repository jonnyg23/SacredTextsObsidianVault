 <p><a name="an_fn_46"></a></p>   <h1 align="CENTER">A FOURTH VERSION OF THE SAME FABLE</h1> 
 <p>THE Moon, they say, wished to send a message to Men, and the Hare said that he would take it. "Run, then," said the Moon, "and tell Men that as I die and am renewed, so shall they also be renewed." But the Hare deceived Men, and said, "As I die and perish, so shall you also."</p>
