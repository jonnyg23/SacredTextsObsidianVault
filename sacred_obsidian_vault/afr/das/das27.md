 <p><a name="page_233"><font size="1" color="green">p. 233</font></a></p> <h1 align="CENTER">GLOSSARY</h1> 
 <p>ahmeen--spoken in a prayer</p> 
 <p>ameela--spoken in a prayer</p> 
 <p>anansi, An Nancy-spider</p> 
 <p>balonga--watermelon</p> 
 <p>belambi, hakabara, mahamadu--spoken in a prayer</p> 
 <p>boo-boo-no--circular African house of sticks and straw, plastered with mud</p> 
 <p>cop--to cut, to bleed</p> 
 <p>cunjuh--magical practices, the casting of spells</p> 
 <p>de big raid--War Between the States</p> 
 <p>deloe--water</p> 
 <p>diffy--fire</p> 
 <p>dose--conjure mixture</p> 
 <p>figlin water--wine</p> 
 <p>flim--pancake</p> 
 <p>gran--grandfather, grandmother</p> 
 <p>ground house--African dwelling</p> 
 <p>gumbo--okra</p> 
 <p>hakabara--spoken in a prayer</p> 
 <p>han--a magic charm carried to insure good luck</p> 
 <p>heaby root--a powerful spell</p> 
 <p>hoodoo--magical practices, the casting of spells</p> 
 <p>juba haltuh--water bucket</p> 
 <p>line scahs--tribal marks</p> 
 <p>maduba--mistress</p> 
 <p>ma-foo-bey--ejaculation of surprise</p> 
 <p>mahaba--master</p> 
 <p>maulin a bumba--thunder</p> 
 <p>mojo--charm.</p> 
 <p>mosojo--sojo--pot</p>

 <p>nyana--seafood</p> 
 <p>planted--place an evil charm</p> 
 <p>plat-eye--especially dreaded ghost</p> 
 <p>put down--place an evil charm</p> 
 <p>rootin--casting an evil charm</p> 
 <p>saraka--flat rice cakes</p> 
 <p>settin-ups--wakes</p> 
 <p>shadduh--ghost</p> 
 <p>shoutin--rhythmic beating of hands and feet</p> 
 <p>skinskon--expression of anger</p> 
 <p>strikin roun--working conjure</p> 
 <p>tabby--a mixture of lime and oyster shell, used for building material</p> 
 <p>totem, taboo--forbidden article of food</p> 
 <p>tuh fix--cast a spell</p> 
 <p>voodoo--magical practices, the casting of spells</p> 
 <p>wukin roots--curing by magic</p>
