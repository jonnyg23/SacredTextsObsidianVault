 <h3 align="CENTER">61. The Fasting Trial (fragment).</h3>
 <i></i><p align="CENTER">Julia Gentle, Santa Cruz Mountains.</p>
 <p>Jumpin' Dick say he would bear longer hunger than White Belly. So White Belly up a tree where a grape a drop, an' Jumpin' Dick pick up on de ground.</p>
 <p>White Belly say,</p><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <font size="2"><p>"Coo coo, me lovin'! coo coo, me lovin'!<br>
 Everybody goin' to look for dem dandy."</p></font></dir>
 </dir>
 
 <p>Jumpin' Dick dance an' sing,</p><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <font size="2"><p>"Every Monday morning,<br>
     Zum zick a zum zum!<br>
 Every Monday morning,<br>
     Zum zick a zum zum!"</p></font></dir>
 </dir>
