 <center><i>NOTE:</i> </center><h3 align="CENTER">90. Bird Arinto.</h3>
 <p>Jekyll, 54-57, Man-Crow, tells the same story. See also numbers 70 and 89.</p>
 <p>The story occurs as an episode in Zeltner's Kama, 54-61 and Tremearne's How Auta Killed Dodo, 408-412.</p>
 <p>For the golden tongue and teeth see numbers 90, 95, and Jekyll, 56; and compare Zeltner, 5.</p>
