 <center><i>NOTE:</i> </center><h3 align="CENTER">48. Why Dog is always Looking.</h3>
 <p>A Jamaica negro proverb runs, "Darg say befo' him plant yam fe look like masquita' foot, him satisfy fe tun beggar." See Cundall's collection (Kingston, 1910), 211.</p>
