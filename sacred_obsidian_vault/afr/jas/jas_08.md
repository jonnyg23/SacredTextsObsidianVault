 <p>1. My father have a thing in his yard; nobody can ride him but little Johnny.</p>
 <p align="RIGHT">--Grass-quit riding a grass-stalk.</p>
 <p>2. My father have a thing in his yard and never ride him till him back break.</p>
 <p align="RIGHT">--House-roof; a man sits astride it to mend the thatch.</p>
 <p>3. My father have a little pony in him yard and there's only one man, little Johnny, can ride it. Johnny ride with a pair of white reins and he go over a bridge.[1]</p>
 <p align="RIGHT">--Needle is the pony, thread the reins, the crooked finger is the bridge, and the thimble is Johnny.</p>
 <p>4. My father has a horse in his yard; it jump an' jump, an' de rein get shorter an' shorter.</p>
 <p align="RIGHT">--Needle and thread.</p>
 <p>5. My father have a grey horse in him yard, ride him nowhere but on him tail.[2]</p>
 <p align="RIGHT">--Pipe.</p><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <p>a) My father saddle his horse at his head an ride him at his tail.</p></dir>

 <p align="RIGHT">--Gun.</p><dir>
