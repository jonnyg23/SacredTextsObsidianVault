 <center><i>NOTE:</i> </center><h3 align="CENTER">149. Animal Talk.</h3>
 <p>Again there are, in this medley which imitates animal sounds, reminiscences of consecutive Nansi stories--Crab's words, in number 54; Ground Dove's in 50. For the fasting contest, see number 61.</p>
 <p>Tremearne, 28, says that the imitation of animal cries is a favorite device in African story-telling. Compare Hollis, Nandi, 109-111, where a great many examples are given of this kind of entertainment.</p>
