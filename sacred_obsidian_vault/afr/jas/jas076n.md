 <center><i>NOTE:</i> </center><h3 align="CENTER">76. The Greedy Child.</h3>
 <p>The idea of a water spirit who allows no one to cross a river without an offering of food, seems to be common in West Africa; e. g. Dayrell, 107-114. Jekyll, 100-101, Dry River, has a Jamaica version of this story. Compare also Tremearne, 209-210, 307-314.</p>
