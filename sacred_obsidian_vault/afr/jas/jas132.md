 <h3 align="CENTER">132. Anansi claims the dinner.</h3>
 <i></i><p align="CENTER">Edward Daley, Mandeville,</p>
 <p>Anansi an' certain number of men was going to a certain place. Certain men give dey own names; Anansi start to give his name now, said, "Mine is 'Dem-men-came-here-las'-night'." When deh get where deh go to, deh bring out dinner. Deh say it is for 'de-men-came-here-las'-night'. Anansi claim de dinner, an' nobody else get any.</p>
