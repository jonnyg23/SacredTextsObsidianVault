 <center><i>NOTE:</i> </center><h3 align="CENTER">46. Why Tumble-bug Rolls in the Dung.</h3>
 <p>In Seidel's story of the "Miracle of the Sidi" (Geschichten und Lieder der Afrikaner, 105), the devil dares the Sidi to marry a slave to a princess. The father of the princess has set to her wooers the supposedly impossible task of filling a bag with hyacinths out</p>
 <p>{p. 290}</p>
 <p>of hyacinth season. The Sidi fills the bag with stones and bids the slave empty it out before the king, when the stones are by miracle turned into hyacinths.</p>
