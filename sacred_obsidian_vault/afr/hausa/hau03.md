 <p>PREFACE</p> 
 <p>AUTHOR'S NOTE<br> ALPHABET</p> 
 <p align="CENTER">PART I</p> 
 <p>A SHORT HISTORY, PURPORTING TO GIVE THE ORIGIN OF THE HAUSA NATION AND THE STORY OF THEIR CONVERSION TO THE MOHAMMEDAN RELIGION.</p> 
 <p align="CENTER">PART II</p> 
 <p>STORIES IN WHICH PEOPLE ARE THE HEROES AND HEROINES</p> 
 <p>1. The story of the slave by name 'The World'<br> 2. How brothers and sisters first came to quarrel and hate each other<br> 3. The story of the boy and the old woman, and how the wasp got his small waist<br> 4. The story about a beautiful maiden, and how the hartebeest got the marks under its eyes like teardrops<br> 5. How the whip and the 'maara' spoon (a broken bit of calabash) came to the haunts of men<br> 6. A story about a chief, and how his sons observed his funeral, and the origin of the spider<br> 7. A story about an orphan, showing that 'he who sows evil, it comes forth in his own garden'<br> 8. A story about a witch, and how the baby of the family outwitted her, and invented the first walled town<br> 9. The doctor who went a pilgrimage to Mecca on a hyena<br> 10. A story about a chief and his cook<br> 11. A story about three youths all skilled in certain things, and how they used that skill to circumvent a difficulty<br> 12. A story about a giant, and the cause of thunder<br> 13. A story about an orphan which was the origin of the saying 'The orphan with a coat of skin is hated, but when it is a metal one he is honoured'<br> 14. A story of a jealous man and what befell him<br> 15. A story of a great friendship and how it was put to the test<br> 16. A story about a test of skill<br> 17. A story about Miss Salt, Miss Pepper, &amp;c.<br> 18. The story of Muusa (Moses) and how it came about that brothers and sisters do not marry each other<br> 19. A story about a hunter and his son<br> 20. A story about a maiden and the pumpkin<br> 21. The Gaawoo-tree and the maiden, and the first person who ever went mad</p>
