 <p><a name="page_26"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 26</font></a></p>  <h1 align="center">Chapter 13</h1>  <h3 align="center">CEREMONY OF THE MORTALS</h3>  
 <p>MORTALS, you follow me, I will show you where the Marriage Office is Perfect Ceremony, is Black Supremacy.<br> "The Monarch Finger" on your (Right Limb is, Holy Union's finger).<br> Both (male and Female), will stand facing Each other at the Matrimonial Tribunal, and The Marriage officer will (Read) to them My (Perfect Crown Document).</p>  
 <p>And say to my Guest, According to The Perfect Copy, of His and Her Arch Majesty King Alpha.<br> You are now equal, HEAD and Pillow-Heart and SOUL Life-HOLD COMPANION.</p>  
 <p>She cannot hurt you, and you cannot hurt her, she cannot leave you on Earth, and you cannot get to Heaven without her.<br> Your ring is an emblem of Loyalty, To The Perfect Tree of Life.</p>  
 <p>Thus saith Our Creator The Living God.<br> By Perfect Ceremony, The Church Triumphant and Black Supremacy.</p>  
 <p>A Royal Child Christmas Gift. (9.O.C.NIGHT) FROM THE CIRCLE THRONE THE KING OF KINGS.</p>
