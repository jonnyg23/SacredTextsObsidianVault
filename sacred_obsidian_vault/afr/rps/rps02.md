 <p><a name="page_7"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 7</font></a></p>  <h1 align="center">ETHIOPIA'S PREFACE</h1>  
 <p>The preface of this Rule Book, is the Finger Post, into the Kingdom of Ethiopia.<br> Ethiopia is the Succeeding Kingdom of the Angle Saxon kingdom.<br> Our philosopher is the Angle Saxon Philosopher's (Successor), a wide awake Universal Master Mind.</p>  
 <p>A man of greater learning is not found on the face of the Globe. A swifter thinker is God Almighty. A better Christian Soul and King Alpha and Omega. Women must be proud of good men, when they are right on the job.<br> Men must be proud of us Women when we can deliver able Sons and Daughters to the Four Poles of the Globe.</p>  
 <p>And make the Nations Hearts rejoice with raging joy. We give God the Glory.</p>  
 <p>THE CROWN MISTRESS THE THRONE LAW-GIVER. His &amp; Her Majesty King Alpha and Queen Omega, COPYRIGHT.<br> His &amp; Her Dynasty Queen Lula may Fitz Balintine Pettersburgh, S.J.W., A B.C. Ph.D., L.L. ,K.O.K.</p>  
 <p><a name="page_8"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 8</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">ETHIOPIA'S FLY LEAF</h3>  
 <p>ROYAL AIR MOUNT. I am going to teach the Princess to fly around the poles.</p>  
 <p>PHILOSOPHERS.--Brother Pettersburgh and Sister Lula may Butler.</p>  
 <p>CLERGYMEN.--Bible Owner, Lexium, and Money Mint. My Perfect Air Mount is Black Supremacy, The Church Triumphant. My Dynasty is the Triumphant Dynasty.</p>  
 <p>We are King Alpha and Queen Omega, THE PAYMASTERS of the World.<br> Do not forget we are Black Supremacy.<br> A.B.C. Post Graduates<br> .J.W.K.A.Q.O.K.O.K.</p>
