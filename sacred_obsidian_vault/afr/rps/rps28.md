 <p><a name="page_44"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 44</font></a></p>  <h1 align="center">Chapter 26</h1>  <h3 align="center">WORLD'S BUILDING</h3>  
 <p>The Root and Foundation of World's Buildings is by Communication.<br> Heaven is Runned by Communication.<br> The Wisdom of GOD DEPENDS on Communication.<br> January 10, 1926, A.D. 2000, His Monarch, Victor, Communication is Lady Pettersburgh's First born.<br> And Lady Triumph the Second.<br> Must not be seen before, The 12. Tri-Virgin Equinoctial Equinox.<br> CELESTIAL LAW A.B.C.S.J.W.K.O.K.K.A.Q.O. January 12 (9.O.C.A.M.). WORLD'S CAPITAL COPYRIGHT OF HOLY TIME.</p>  
 <p>TO HIS MONARCH, VICTOR, COMMUNICATION AND HIS SISTER LADY TRIUMPH.<br> I am The Monarch of Creation, Your Perfect Father, I am writing to you today before you are being CONCEIVED in The WORLD in your Mother's Belly.</p>  
 <p>Lady Pettersburgh the Perfect Mrs. of BLACK SUPREMACY is my Wife, Your Mother, is a perfect lady.<br> I married Her, through Communication in the copyright Department at The World's Capital, and could not TELL what part of the Globe, she was at the time.<br> And Seal Her Married Diploma behind her Back, because she is Queen Omega and I am His Majesty King Alpha the King of Kings.</p>
