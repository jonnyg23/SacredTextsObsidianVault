 <p><a name="page_45"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 45</font></a></p>  <h1 align="center">Chapter 27</h1>  <h3 align="center">WORLD'S BUILDING</h3>  
 <p>And up to the Hour, January 12, your Birth Day, I have not had a line from her, for the Anarchy, The Angle-Militant Supremacy, at Nationality will not allow her to Communicate with me.<br> For they was ruling the Port of Communication, and they will not allow her to CROSS The Angle-Militant Gulf.</p>  
 <p>Plant that as you go, from Generation to Generation, as Long as God Lives.<br> And She and I, are The Keepers of The Tree of Life and Creation's Wisdom and Power House, and Money Mint is ours, for life.<br> And children must INHERIT their PARENTS WEALTH.</p>  
 <p>That is why they tries to Kill you in your Mother's Belly. Have nothing to do with the Anarchy: Do not allow Black Supremacy to Marry any one from white supremacy. for there is a Rock BOTTOM OFFENCE, CALLED SLAVERY in the Heart of White Folks that will come up in one NIGHT.</p>  
 <p>He will put you and his own WIFE to sleep in his bed, and let you cohabit with her all you want just to get you in.<br> He will worship you, just to get on top of your Belly.</p>  
 <p><a name="page_46"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 46</font></a></p>  
 <p>Emily MCGHIE (said), white Peoples Mind is the Snake's Mind. Before I trust a White Person, I trust a Snake.</p>  
 <p>They take your LIFE with their PRIVATE INTO YOUR PRIVATE. (Last Warning)</p>
