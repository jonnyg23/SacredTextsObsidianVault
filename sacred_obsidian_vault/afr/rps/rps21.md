 <p><a name="page_33"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 33</font></a></p>  <h1 align="center">Chapter 19</h1>  <h3 align="center">ETHIOPIA'S TRIUMPHANT PROCLAMATION</h3>  
 <p>The Bible Owner, of Holy Time, DENOUNCED The Bible Militant.<br> Also The Militant Dictionary.<br> And take off the Black Man and his Posterities from off The Angle-Militant SLAVE-TRAIN at Nationality.<br> And Planted the Church Triumphant, The Black Supremacy on The Triumphant Soil of the World's Capital, The New Bible Land, The Isles of Spring.<br> The same Country, That The Anarchy called, Jamaica, British West Indies.<br> Black Supremacy's Greatest Men and Women are Sub-Ways and AIR-MASTERS of every SHADE.</p>  
 <p>They Sleep in BED and Eat WITH you, and you do not KNOW what TRIGGER your LIFE and destiny is ON.<br> The Gods of Laws are my Students.</p>  
 <p>The Air you are breathing This Minute is Mine.</p>  
 <p>The BABWIRE Eternity is Mine.<br> The Brim-stone and Fire, Vulcannon is Mine.</p>  
 <p>Every Thunder and Lightening is Mine.</p>  
 <p>I want you to know that the (indomitable firmament is Mine.)</p>  
 <p><a name="page_34"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 34</font></a></p>  
 <p>When I speak to her she OBEY MY ROYAL</p>  
 <p>VOICE.</p>  
 <p>His and Her Majesty King Alpha and Queen</p>  
 <p>Omega and Lady Pettersburgh, King of Kings.</p>
