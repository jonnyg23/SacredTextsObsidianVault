 <p><a name="page_18"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 18</font></a></p>  <h1 align="center">Chapter 6</h1>  <h3 align="center">OBEAH</h3>  
 <p>A Balm yard is not a Hospital<br> Neither is it an Obeah Shop<br> Peoples that is guilty of Obeah must not visit a Balm Yard Nor in the Assembly of Black Supremacy.</p>  
 <p>No admittance for Obeah dogs.<br> No admittance for FORTUNE-TELLERS and witch and Old Hige.</p>  
 <p>None whatever, no admittance for GHOST, WITCH, Lizards.</p>  
 <p>No admittance for Alligators, Snakes, PUSS, Crabs, Flies, Ants, Rats, and Mice, and LODESTONES, and Pins and Needles.<br> Jan-Crows, The Ravens, and Candles, and Fast Cups, and Rum Bottles, and Grave Yards are not REQUIRED.<br> People's Clothes, a beast HAIR, and FOWLS and Grave Dirt not wanted.</p>  
 <p>Eleven, the Woman's Baby will strive in HER BELLY, AND YOUR snake and Lizard will not be able to hurt HER.<br> For your Ghost will come right back to yourself.</p>  
 <p>For this is Ethiopia Balm Yard, and we do not have no leprosy.<br> For Ghost only visit the Lepers Home.<br> This poison is for ALL Bad Spirits, it is No. 666 it is good for the Pope of Rome and The Monarch of Hell's Bottom.</p>
