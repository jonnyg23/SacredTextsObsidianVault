 <p><a name="page_59"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 59</font></a></p>  <h1 align="center">Chapter 37</h1>  <h3 align="center">NONE MATRIMONIAL PROSECUTION</h3>  
 <p>CRIME OF NONE, MATRIMONIAL LEZE MAJESTY (MATRIMONIAL PROSECUTION.)<br> (Act.) 7377. SPANISH TOWN, English Jamaica (Record office)<br> Adam-Abraham-Angle-Saxon, the Indomitable Lepor and his Harlot, Eve and Sarai, and Keturah was driven out of my (Virtuous) Dynasty by myself, since B.C. 4004, for fornication and THEFT.<br> My SACRED Chronicle, dated B.C. 4004 and my Great Chronological Fifty Horse Power-Printing Press. I am that said Man that Adam-Abraham and his boy CAIN &amp; ABLE CUT to pieces and took My Royal Printing Fifty Horse Rower Press, and pattern it and call it The Chronicle Printing Press.<br> His "Whoredom" is Recorded in First Chronicle, 1st Chap 32, etc.</p>  
 <p><a name="page_60"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 60</font></a></p>  
 <p>And more than that, Adam-Abraham-Angle-Saxon, was never MARRIED,<br> nor any That followed that Lepor.<br> For up to October 17, 1925, I requested the General Register Office in Spanish Town, Kingston, Jamaica, for Adam-Abraham-Angle-Saxon "Certified Marriage Officer's Diploma" (This World's Strongest Document).</p>  
 <p>Reply, October 21st, 1925 (Letter Number 7477). Sir you requested me to send you (Certified Marriage Officer's Diploma.)<br> In reply to say, Adam-Abraham-Angle-Saxon-Office does not supply such documents.<br> To The Rev. Fitz Balintine Pettersburgh, Ph.D,.L.L.D., October 21, 1925. A.D., Post Script.<br> The Slave Owners Children, they has a little SLIP OF PAPER into the Office of Slave City Official TROUSERS Pockets in the FARMS.<br> That is all we think of their Official Names and Denominations, O.K. Notice.<br> That is called Non-Matrimonial Leze Majesty.<br> Supreme Action. His and Her Triumphant Dynasty, His and Her Monarch Black Supremacy, Crown Marriage Dynasty, of Lady Creation, King Alpha and Queen Omega.</p>
