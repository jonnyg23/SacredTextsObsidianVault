 <p><a name="page_9"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 9</font></a></p>  <h1 align="center">Chapter 1.</h1>  <h3 align="center">THE ETHIOPIAN WESTERN PHILOSOPHY</h3>  
 <p>Now, this is the morning of our resurrection therefore, we the Royal Tree, are very busy, cleaning up our Ancient and Modern Royal right away from Pole to Pole.<br> And the preparing of Guests for the coronation.</p>  
 <p>THE CORONATION.--The Coronation of Ethiopia's Postarities are as sure as the purity of pure gold.</p>  
 <p>THE CROWN MISTRESS.--I Mrs. Indiana Coombs, being the Crown Mrs. of our Ethiopian Repository, for the Tropic of Cancer, I move the Crownship of the world right at this yard limit of time.</p>  
 <p>And present this generation of the 20th century my supreme Book of Royal Rules from the Ethiopian Western Repository.</p>  <h3 align="center">ILLUSTRATION</h3>  
 <p>Owing to the Universal Rend of our Ancient and Modern Kingdoms, we are at this junction of our history scattered over the Globe into little Sectional groups commonly called Bands.</p>  
 <p><a name="page_10"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 10</font></a></p>  
 <p>Ethiopian's Western Repository, is a strictly Christian Museum.</p>  
 <p>All our local bands throughout the globe, are bent towards this Royal Repository.</p>  
 <p>THE ROYAL AUTHORITY.--This Official Bill of Royal Authority, is to admit all Bands, Missions, Camps, Denominations, into this Supreme Royal Repository.</p>  
 <p>THE BALMING MISTRESS.--I being the Balming Mistress of many worlds I charge the Power-House right now.</p>  <h3 align="center">THE RULE BOOK</h3>  
 <p>The Rule Book leads you into different department of the Kingdom.<br> The Records of the Kingdom are with us, unto this day.<br> The regulations, points you to the baseses of the Kingdom.</p>
