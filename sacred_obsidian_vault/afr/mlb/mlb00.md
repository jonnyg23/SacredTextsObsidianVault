 <h1 align="CENTER">MYTHS AND LEGENDS</h1>
 <h6 align="CENTER">OF</h6>
 <h2 align="CENTER">THE BANTU</h2>
 <h3 align="CENTER">ALICE WERNER</h3>
 <p align="CENTER">[1933]</p>
 <h6 align="CENTER">{Scanned at sacred-texts.com 2/2000}</h6>
 <p align="CENTER">In Memoriam<br>
 HARRIETTE EMILY COLENSO<br>
 June 2, 1932<br>
 AGNES MARY COLENSO<br>
 JUIY 26, 1932</p>
 <i></i><p align="CENTER">Closed the kind eyes; nevermore the clasp of the faithful hand.<br>
 But the clamour and wrath of men are still, where they sweetly rest,<br>
 And the loved dust is one with the dust of the well-loved land.<br>
 Earth has taken the wronged and the wronger both to her breast.<br>
 Cetshwayo sleeps in Inkandhla, Rhodes on Matopo height,<br>
 Escombe and Osborn alike in the dear Natalian soil.<br>
 Do they dream? And what dreams are theirs in the hush of the kindly night?<br>
 Never, since time began, has any come back to tell....<br>
 O brave, true, loving hearts, at rest from long strife and toil.<br>
 Mandiza, Sineke, Mamonga, Kebeni, Magema,<br>
 Hail and farewell!</p>
