 <h1 align="CENTER">SIRIUS AND CANOPUS.</h1>
 <p>My (step)grandmother, <i>Ttuai-ang</i>, was the one who used to rejoice about Canopus. She said--</p>
 <p align="CENTER">Sirius!<br>
 Sirius!<br>
 Winks like<br>
 Canopus!</p>
 <p align="CENTER">Canopus<br>
 Winks like<br>
 Sirius!</p>
 <p align="CENTER">Canopus<br>
 Winks like<br>
 Sirius!</p>
 <p align="CENTER">Sirius<br>
 Winks like<br>
 Canopus!</p>
 <p> </p>
 <p>While my grandmother felt that food was abundant.<small>[1]</small></p>
 <p><small>[1. We are wont to say <i>!Xu</i>, when food is abundant.]</small></p>
