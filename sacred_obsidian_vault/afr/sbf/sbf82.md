 <h1 align="CENTER">2. FURTHER CHANGES OF FORM.</h1>
 <p>The sun rose, and <i>|Xue</i> was a <i>dui</i>;<small>[1]</small> and the sun set, and <i>|Xue</i> was an Omuherero and lay down; and the sun rose, and <i>|Xue</i> was <i>|Xue</i>, and went into another country and was a <i>sha'o</i><small>[2]</small>; and the sun</p>
 <p><small>[1. The flower of the <i>dui</i> is light-coloured; its fruit is green; another day, (when) its fruit has ripened, its fruit is red.</small></p>
 <p>2. The <i>shao</i> is a tall tree, like the <i>!kuni</i> (palm?).]</p>
 <p>set, and <i>|Xue</i> was a Makoba, and lay down; and the sun rose and <i>|Xue</i> was a <i>|naXane</i>.<small>[1]</small></p>
 <p><small>[1. (One kind of) <i>!naXane</i> lies upon the earth; another (kind of) <i>!naXane</i> is a tree. The <i>!naXane</i> are numerous. The fruit of the tree <i>!naXane</i> is yellowish. The fruit of the tree <i>!naXane</i> is large; and the ground <i>!naXane</i> fruit is small, and resembles the <i>|kui</i> fruit, is red, is small, and abundant.]</small></p>
