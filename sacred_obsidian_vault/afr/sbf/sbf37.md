 <h1 align="CENTER">THE SONG OF THE SPRINGBOK MOTHERS.</h1>
 <p>The Springbok mothers sang (soothing their children)--</p><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <p>"A-a' hng<br>
 O Springbok Child!<br>
 sleep for me.<br>
 A-a' hng<br>
 O Springbok Child!<br>
 Sleep for me."</p></dir>
