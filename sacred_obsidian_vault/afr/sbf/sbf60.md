 <h1 align="CENTER">CUTTING OFF THE TOP OF THE LITTLE FINGER.</h1>
 <h2 align="CENTER">SECOND ACCOUNT.</h2>
 <p>Her father, <i>|Ukwaiyau</i>, was the one who cut off the upper joint of his daughter <i>Kaueten-ang</i>'s little finger.<small>[1]</small></p>
 <p>My husband was the one who cut off (the upper joint of) <i>!kabbe-tu</i>'s ("Willem Streep's") finger.</p>
 <p><small>[1. <i>|Xaken-ang</i> further explained that the joint is cut off with reed. It is thought to make children live to grow up. It is done before they suck at all.]</small></p>
