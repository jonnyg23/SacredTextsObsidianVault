 <h1 align="CENTER">APPENDIX.</h1>
 <h2 align="CENTER">A. FEW !KUNG TEXTS.</h2>
 <h3 align="CENTER">THE DOINGS OF |XUE ARE MANY.</h3>
 <p>The works of <i>|Xue</i> are many, and were not one, but many; and my father's father, <i>Karu</i>, told me about <i>|Xue</i>'s doings, for <i>|Xue</i>'s works are numerous.</p>
 <h3 align="CENTER">VARIOUS TRANSFORMATIONS OF |XUE</h3>
 <h2 align="CENTER">1. |XUE AS !NAXANE.</h2>
 <p>(When) the sun rose, <i>|Xue</i> was <i>!naXane</i>; the birds ate <i>|Xue</i>; <i>|Xue</i> was <i>!naXane</i>. The sun set, (and) <i>|Xue</i>, was <i>|Xue</i>; and lay down and slept. The night fell, and <i>|Xue</i> lay down, (he) slept; the place was dark; and the sun rose, and <i>|Xue</i> was another (kind of) <i>!naXane</i>, a large (kind of) <i>!naXane</i>, which is a tree. And the night fell, (and) <i>|Xue</i> was not a tree, and was <i>|Xue</i>, and lay down.</p>
