 <p class="indent">92. T<small>HE</small> W<small>OMAN WHO WANTED TO BE A</small> M<small>AN</small>.—A woman named Arnarkuak would not give up scolding
 
 <!------------------------page 443------------------------>
 
 <a name="page_443"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 443</font></a>
 
 her son on account of his want of skill in hunting and other manly pursuits. Once in his absence, when he had gone out kayaking, she forced her daughter-in-law, by threatening her with death, to flee with her to the interior of the country, where she disguised herself like a man, and had her daghter-in-law, Ukuamak, for a wife. But the son found out their place of refuge, and killed his despicable mother.</p>
