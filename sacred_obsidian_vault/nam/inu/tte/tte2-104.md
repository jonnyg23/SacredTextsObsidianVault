 <p class="indent">104. T<small>HE</small> G<small>IANT OF</small> K<small>ANGERSUAK OR</small> C<small>APE</small> F<small>AREWELL</small>.—The people from the south (or east) and those from the north (or west) were at war with each other. The latter had a powerful champion, who was sitting on the top of Kangersuak to watch the Southlanders passing by. A man who had been killed by him left a son, who practised angakok science, and
 
 <!------------------------page 450------------------------>
 
 <a name="page_450"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 450</font></a>
 
 revenged his father by inducing the giant to walk with him over a marshy plain, where he went down, and from beneath pierced the feet of the giant, and afterwards killed him.</p>
