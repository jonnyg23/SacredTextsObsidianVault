 <h3 align="center">NASAL SOUNDS</h3>
 
 <p>anh, anse like French <i>an</i> in <i>tante</i></p>
 
 <p>onh, onse as in French <i>ton</i></p>
 
 <p>inh like French <i>in</i> in <i>intime</i></p>
 
 <p> Hyphens in middle of words to denote a separation of tone between two vowels or separating syllables, and to help in pronouncing long compound words. Proper accenting is very important in pronunciation.</p>
 
 <p> In the spelling and pronunciation of Navajo words, an attempt has been made to approximate as closely as possible to an English or Latin equivalent.</p>
