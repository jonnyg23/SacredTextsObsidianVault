 <a name="page_47"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 47</font></a><h1 align="CENTER">A PRAYER OF THE SECOND DAY OF THE NIGHT CHANT.</h1>
 <p align="CENTER">(See The Night Chant, p. 81, par. 355.)</p>
 <h3 align="CENTER">I.</h3>
 <p>1. From the base of the east.<br>
 2. From the base of the Pelado Peak.<br>
 3. From the house made of mirage,<br>
 4. From the story made of mirage,<br>
 5. From the doorway of rainbow,<br>
 6. The path out of which is the rainbow,<br>
 7. The rainbow passed out with me.<br>
 S. The rainbow raised up with me.<br>
 9. Through the middle of broad fields,<br>
 10. The rainbow returned with me.<br>
 11. To where my house is visible,<br>
 12. The rainbow returned with me.<br>
 13. To the roof of my house,<br>
 14. The rainbow returned with me.<br>
 15. To the entrance of my house,<br>
 16. The rainbow returned with me.<br>
 17. To just within my house,<br>
 18. The rainbow returned with me.<br>
 19. To my fireside,<br>
 20. The rainbow returned with me.<br>
 21. To the center of my house,<br>
 22. The rainbow returned with me.<br>
 23. At the fore part of my house with the dawn,<br>
 24. The Talking God sits with me.<br>
 25. The House God sits with me.<br>
 26. Pollen Boy sits with me.<br>
 27. Grasshopper Girl sits with me.<br>
 28. In beauty Estsánatlehi, my mother, for her I return.<br>
 29. Beautifully my fire to me is restored.</p>
 <p><a name="page_48"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 48</font></a></p>
 <p>30. Beautifully my possessions are to me restored.<br>
 31. Beautifully my soft goods to me are restored.<br>
 32. Beautifully my hard goods to me are restored.<br>
 34. Beautifully my horses to me are restored.<br>
 34. Beautifully my sheep to me are restored.<br>
 35. Beautifully my old men to me are restored.<br>
 36. Beautifully my old women to me are restored.<br>
 37. Beautifully my young men to me are restored.<br>
 38. Beautifully my women to me are restored.<br>
 39. Beautifully my children to me are restored.<br>
 40. Beautifully my wife to me is restored.<br>
 41. Beautifully my chiefs to me are restored.<br>
 42. Beautifully my country to me is restored.<br>
 43. Beautifully my fields to me are restored.<br>
 44. Beautifully my house to me is restored.<br>
 45. Talking God sits with me.<br>
 46. House God sits with me.<br>
 47. Pollen Boy sits with me.<br>
 48. Grasshopper Girl sits with me.<br>
 49. Beautifully white corn to me is restored.<br>
 50. Beautifully yellow corn to me is restored.<br>
 51. Beautifully blue corn to me is restored.<br>
 52. Beautifully corn of all kinds to me is restored.<br>
 53. In beauty may I walk.<br>
 54. All day long may I walk.<br>
 55. Through the returning seasons may I walk.<br>
 56. (Translation uncertain.)<br>
 57. Beautifully will I possess again.<br>
 58. (Translation uncertain.)<br>
 59. Beautifully birds . . .<br>
 60. Beautifully joyful birds<br>
 61. On the trail marked with pollen may I walk.<br>
 62. With grasshoppers about my feet may I walk.<br>
 63. With dew about my feet may I walk.<br>
 64. With beauty may I walk.<br>
 65. With beauty before me, may I walk.<br>
 66. With beauty behind me, may I walk.<br>
 67. With beauty above me, may I walk.</p>
 <p><a name="page_49"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 49</font></a></p>
 <p>68. With beauty below me, may, I walk.<br>
 69. With beauty all around me, may I walk.<br>
 70. In old age wandering on a trail of beauty, lively, may I walk.<br>
 71. In old age wandering on a trail of beauty, living again, may I walk.<br>
 72. It is finished in beauty.<br>
 73. It is finished in beauty.</p>
 <h3 align="CENTER">II.</h3>
 <p>1. From the base of the south.<br>
 2. From the base of the San Mateo mountain.</p>
 <p>(The rest as in Part I, except that 65 and 66 and also 67 and 68 are transposed.)</p>
 <h3 align="CENTER">III.</h3>
 <p>1. From the base of the west.<br>
 2. From the base of the San Francisco mountain.</p>
 <p>(The rest as in Part 1.)</p>
 <h3 align="CENTER">IV.</h3>
 <p>1. From the base of the north.<br>
 2. From the base of the San Juan mountains.</p>
 <p>(The rest as in Part II; but "It is finished in beauty" is repeated four times.)</p>
 <h3 align="CENTER">TEXT AND INTERLINEAR TRANSLATION.</h3>
 <p>1. <i>H</i>aá` biyáde<i>n</i><br>

 <p>17. <i>S</i>ó<i>h</i>o<i>g</i>an bahastláde<br>

 <p>18. Nat<i>s</i>ílït <i>s</i>ï<i>l</i>náhindel<br>
 <font size="2">Rainbow | with me it returned</font></p>
 <p>19. <i>S</i>ó<i>h</i>o<i>g</i>an <i>h</i>onïs<i>h</i>á`de<br>
 <font size="2">My house | the hearth</font></p>
 <p>20. Nat<i>s</i>ílït <i>s</i>ï<i>l</i>náhindel<br>
 <font size="2">Rainbow | with me it returned.</font></p>
 <p>21. <i>S</i>ó<i>h</i>o<i>g</i>an yaha<i>l</i>nígë<br>

 <p>23. <i>H</i>ayo<i>l</i>ká<i>l</i> bë<i>s</i>ó<i>h</i>o<i>g</i>an ntsit<i>l</i>ági<br>
