 <h1 align="CENTER">XIX. Hymn to the God of Flowers.</h1>
 <h3 align="CENTER">XIX. Macuilxochitl Icuic.</h3>
 <p align="CENTER"><a href="#english">English</a></p>
 <p>1. Ayya, yao, xochitlycaca umpan iuitza tlamacazecatla tlamocoyoalca.</p>
 <p>2. Ayya, yao, ayo intinotzicaya teumechaue oya, yao, tlauizcalac yacallea tlamacazecatlo tlamocoyoualca.</p>
 <p>3. Tetzauhteutla notecuyo tezcatlipuca quinanquilican çinteutla, oay.</p>
 <p>4. Tezcatzonco moyolca ayyaquetl yya tochin quiyocuxquia noteuh, niquiyatlacaz, niquiyamamaliz, mixcoatepetl colhoacan.</p>
 <p>5. Tozquixaya, nictzotzoniyao, yn tezcatzintli tezcatzintli tezcaxocoyeua, tzoniztapaliati tlaoc xoconoctlia ho, a.</p>
 <i></i><p>Var. 1. Tlamocoioaleua. 5. Tozquiuaia. Tzoniztapalatiati.</p>
 <h3 align="CENTER">Gloss.</h3>
 <p>1. <i>Q. n.</i>, ompa nochan in xochitlicacan in itlamacazqui ni macuilxochitl.</p>
 <p>2. <i>Q. n.</i>, motilinia in tinoçi in ompa titlaecoltilozque umpa tochan ez.</p>
 <p>3. <i>Q. n.</i>, yn tetzauitl in tezcatlipoca ca oyaque auh ynic tiui umpa titlananquilizque in centeotl.</p>
 <p>4. Tezcatzonco moyolcan, <i>q. n.</i>, tezcatzonco oyol in tochtli ynic yaz, oquiyocux, oquipic, y noteuh oquito nittlaçaz, nicmamaliz, in mixcoatepetl colhoacan, <i>id est</i>, nictepeuaz.</p>
 <p>5. Tozquixaya nictzotzomiao, <i>q. n.</i>, nictzotzona, in tezcatzintli oncan nexa in tezcatzonco, oncan oyol tzoniztapalatiati ocxoni ni octli.</p>
 <p>{p. 66}</p>
 <h3 align="CENTER"><a name="english">Hymn to Macuilxochitl.</a></h3>
 <p>1. Yes, I shall go there to-night, to the house of flowers I shall exercise the priestly office to-night.</p>
 <p>2. We labor in thy house, our mother, from dawn unto night, fulfilling the priestly office, laboring in the night.</p>
 <p>3. A dreadful god is our god Tezcatlipoca, he is the only god, he will answer us.</p>
 <p>4. His heart is in the Tezcatzontli; my god is not timid like a hare nor is he peaceable; I shall overturn, I shall penetrate the Mixcoatepec in Colhuacan.</p>
 <p>5. I sing, I play on an instrument, I am the noble instrument, the mirror; I am he who lifts the mirror; I cry aloud, intoxicated with the wine of the tuna.</p>
 <h3 align="CENTER">Notes.</h3>
 <p>As before stated (Notes to Hymn VIII), Macuilxochitl is another title of the flower-god Xochipilli.</p></body>
