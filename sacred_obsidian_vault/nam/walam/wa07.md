 <p>1. All were peaceful, long ago, there at the Talega land.</p>
 <p>1. Wemilangundo wulamo talli talegaking.</p>
 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_wa5_2"></a>
 <img src="img/wa5_2.jpg"></p>
 <p>2. The Pipe-Bearer was chief at the White river.</p>
 <p>2. Tamaganend sakimanep wapalaneng.</p>
 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_wa5_3"></a>
 <img src="img/wa5_3.jpg"></p>
 <p>3. White-Lynx was chief, much corn was planted.</p>
 <p>3. Wapushuwi sakimanep kelitgeman.</p>
 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_wa5_4"></a>
 <img src="img/wa5_4.jpg"></p>
 <p>4. Good-and-Strong was chief; the people were many.</p>
 <p>4. Wulitshinik sakimanep makdopannik.</p>
 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_wa5_5"></a>
 <img src="img/wa5_5.jpg"></p>
 <p>5. The Recorder was chief; he painted the records.</p>
 <p>5. Lekhihitin sakimanep wallamolumin.</p>
 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_wa5_6"></a>
 <img src="img/wa5_6.jpg"></p>
 <p>6. Pretty-Blue-Bird was chief; there was much fruit.</p>
 <p>6. Kolachuisen sakimanep makeliming.</p>
 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_wa5_7"></a>
 <img src="img/wa5_7.jpg"></p>
 <p>7. Always-There was chief; the towns were many.</p>
 <p>7. Pematalli sakimanep makelinik.</p>
 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_wa5_8"></a>
 <img src="img/wa5_8.jpg"></p>
 <p>8. Paddler-up-Stream was chief; he was much on the rivers,</p>
 <p>8. Pepomahenem sakimanep makelaning.</p>
 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_wa5_9"></a>
 <img src="img/wa5_9.jpg"></p>
 <p>9. Little-Cloud was chief; many departed,</p>
 <p>9. Tankawon sakimanep makeleyachik,</p>
 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_wa5_10"></a>
 <img src="img/wa5_10.jpg"></p>
 <p>10. The Nanticokes and the Shawnees going to the south.</p>
 <p>10. Nentegowi shawanowi shawanaking.</p>
 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_wa5_11"></a>
 <img src="img/wa5_11.jpg"></p>
 <p>11. Big-Beaver was chief, at the White Salt Lick.</p>
 <p>11. Kichitamak sakimanep wapahoning.</p>
 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_wa5_12"></a>
 <img src="img/wa5_12.jpg"></p>
 <p>12. The Seer, the praised one, went to the west.</p>
 <p>12. Onowutok awolagan wunkenahep.</p>
 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_wa5_13"></a>
 <img src="img/wa5_13.jpg"></p>
 <p>13. He went to the west, to the southwest, to the western villages.</p>
 <p>13. Wtinpakitonis wunshawononis wunkiwikwotank.</p>
 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_wa5_14"></a>
 <img src="img/wa5_14.jpg"></p>
 <p>14. The Rich-Down-River-Man was chief, at Talega river.</p>
 <p>14. Pawanami sakimanep taleganah.</p>
 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_wa5_15"></a>
 <img src="img/wa5_15.jpg"></p>
 <p>15. The Walker was chief; there was much war.</p>
 <p>15. Lokwelend sakimanep makpalliton.</p>
 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_wa5_16"></a>
 <img src="img/wa5_16.jpg"></p>
 <p>16. Again with the Tawa people, again with the Stone people, again with the northern people.</p>
 <p>16. Lappi towako lappi sinako lappi lowako.</p>
 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_wa5_17"></a>
 <img src="img/wa5_17.jpg"></p>
 <p>17. Grandfather-of-Boats was chief; he went to lands in boats.</p>
 <p>17. Mokolmokom sakimanep mokolakolin.</p>
 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_wa5_18"></a>
 <img src="img/wa5_18.jpg"></p>
 <p>18. Snow-Hunter was chief; he went to the north land.</p>
 <p>18. Winelowich sakimanep lowushkakiang.</p>
 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_wa5_19"></a>
 <img src="img/wa5_19.jpg"></p>
 <p>19. Look-About was chief, he went to the Talega mountains.</p>
 <p>19. Linkwekinuk sakimanep talegachukang.</p>
