 <p>20. East-Villager was chief; he was east of Talega.</p>
 <p>20. Wapalawikwan sakimanep waptalegawing.</p>
 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_wa5_21"></a>
 <img src="img/wa5_21.jpg"></p>
 <p>21. A great land and a wide land was the east land,</p>
 <p>21. Amangaki amigaki wapakisinep.</p>
 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_wa5_22"></a>
 <img src="img/wa5_22.jpg"></p>
 <p>22. A land without snakes, a rich land, a pleasant land.</p>
 <p>22. Mattakohaki mapawaki mawulitenol.</p>
 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_wa5_23"></a>
 <img src="img/wa5_23.jpg"></p>
 <p>23. Great Fighter was chief, toward the north.</p>
 <p>23. Gikenopalat sakimanep pekochilowan.</p>
 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_wa5_24"></a>
 <img src="img/wa5_24.jpg"></p>
 <p>24. At the Straight river, River-Loving was chief.</p>
 <p>24. Saskwihanang hanaholend sakimanep.</p>
 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_wa5_25"></a>
 <img src="img/wa5_25.jpg"></p>
 <p>25. Becoming-Fat was chief at Sassafras land.</p>
 <p>25. Gattawisi sakimanep winakaking.</p>
 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_wa5_26"></a>
 <img src="img/wa5_26.jpg"></p>
 <p>26. All the hunters made wampum again at the great sea.</p>
 <p>26. Wemi lowichik gishikshawipek lappi kichipek.</p>
 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_wa5_27"></a>
 <img src="img/wa5_27.jpg"></p>
 <p>27. Red-Arrow was chief at the stream again.</p>
 <p>27. Makhiawip sakimanep lapihaneng.</p>
 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_wa5_28"></a>
 <img src="img/wa5_28.jpg"></p>
 <p>28. The Painted-Man was chief at the Mighty Water.</p>
 <p>28. Wolomenap sakimanep maskekitong.</p>
 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_wa5_29"></a>
 <img src="img/wa5_29.jpg"></p>
 <p>29. The Easterners and the Wolves go northeast.</p>
 <p>29. Wapanand tumewand waplowaan.</p>
 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_wa5_30"></a>
 <img src="img/wa5_30.jpg"></p>
 <p>30. Good-Fighter was chief, and went to the north.</p>
 <p>30. Wulitpallat sakimanep piskwilowan.</p>
 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_wa5_31"></a>
 <img src="img/wa5_31.jpg"></p>
 <p>31. The Mengwe, the Lynxes, all trembled.</p>
 <p>31. Mahongwi pungelika wemi nungwi.</p>
 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_wa5_32"></a>
 <img src="img/wa5_32.jpg"></p>
 <p>32. Again an Affable was chief, and made peace with all,</p>
 <p>32. Lappi tamenend sakimanepit wemi langundit.</p>
 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_wa5_33"></a>
 <img src="img/wa5_33.jpg"></p>
 <p>33. All were friends, all were United, under this great chief</p>
 <p>33. Wemi nitis wemi takwicken sakima kichwon.</p>
 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_wa5_36"></a>
 <img src="img/wa5_36.jpg"></p>
 <p>36. Great-Beaver was chief, remaining in Sassafras land.</p>
 <p>36. Kichitamak sakimanep winakununda.</p>
 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_wa5_37"></a>
 <img src="img/wa5_37.jpg"></p>
 <p>37. White-Body was chief on the sea shore.</p>
 <p>37. Wapahakey sakimanep sheyabian.</p>
 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_wa5_38"></a>
 <img src="img/wa5_38.jpg"></p>
 <p>38. Peace-Maker was chief, friendly to all.</p>
 <p>38. Elangomel sakimanep makeliwulit.</p>
 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_wa5_39"></a>
 <img src="img/wa5_39.jpg"></p>
 <p>39. He-Makes-Mistakes was chief, hurriedly coming.</p>
 <p>39. Pitenumen sakimanep unchihillen.</p>
