 <img src="img/wa2_1.jpg"></p>
 <p>1. Long ago there was a mighty snake and beings evil to men.</p>
 <p>1. Wtilamo maskanako anup lennowak makowini essopak.</p>
 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_wa2_2"></a>
 <img src="img/wa2_2.jpg"></p>
 <p>2. This mighty snake hated those who were there (and) greatly disquieted those whom he hated.</p>
 <p>2. Maskanako shingalusit nijini essopak shawelendamep eken shingalan.</p>
 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_wa2_3"></a>
 <img src="img/wa2_3.jpg"></p>
 <p>3. They both did harm, they both injured each other, both were not in peace.</p>
 <p>3. Nisliawi` palliton, nishawi machiton, nishawi matta lungtindowin.</p>
 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_wa2_4"></a>
 <img src="img/wa2_4.jpg"></p>
 <p>4. Driven from their homes they fought with this murderer.</p>
 <p>4. Mattapewi wiki nihanlowit mekwazoan.</p>
 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_wa2_5"></a>
 <img src="img/wa2_5.jpg"></p>
 <p>5. The mighty snake firmly resolved to harm the men.</p>
 <p>5. Maskanako gishi penatiwelendamep lennowak owini palliton.</p>
 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_wa2_6"></a>
 <img src="img/wa2_6.jpg"></p>
 <p>6. He brought three persons, he brought a monster, he brought a rushing water.</p>
 <p>6. Nakowa petonep, amangam petonep, akopehella petonep.</p>
 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_wa2_7"></a>
 <img src="img/wa2_7.jpg"></p>
 <p>7. Between the hills the water rushed and rushed, dashing through and through, destroying much.</p>
 <p>7. Pehella pehella, pohoka pohoka, eshohok eshohok, palliton palliton.</p>
 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_wa2_8"></a>
 <img src="img/wa2_8.jpg"></p>
 <p>8. Nanabush, the Strong White One, grandfather of beings, grandfather of men, was on the Turtle Island.</p>
 <p>8. Tulapit menapit Nanabotish maskaboush owinimokom linowimokom.</p>
 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_wa2_9"></a>
 <img src="img/wa2_9.jpg"></p>
 <p>9. There he was walking and creating, as he passed by and created the turtle.</p>
 <p>9. Gishikin-pommixin tulagishattenlohxin.</p>
 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_wa2_10"></a>
 <img src="img/wa2_10.jpg"></p>
 <p>10. Beings and men all go forth, they walk in the floods and shallow waters, down stream thither to the Turtle Island.</p>
 <p>10. Owini linowi wemoltin, Pehella gahani pommixin, Nahiwi tatalli tulapin.</p>
 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_wa2_11"></a>
 <img src="img/wa2_11.jpg"></p>
 <p>11. There were many monster fishes, which ate some of them.</p>
 <p>11. Amanganek makdopannek alendyuwek metzipannek.</p>
 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_wa2_12"></a>
 <img src="img/wa2_12.jpg"></p>
 <p>12. The Manito daughter, coming, helped with her canoe, helped all, as they came and came.</p>
 <p>12. Manito-dasin mokol-wichemap, Palpal payat payat wemichemap.</p>
 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_wa2_13"></a>
 <img src="img/wa2_13.jpg"></p>
 <p>13. [And also] Nanabush, Nanabush, the grandfather of all, the grandfather of beings, the grandfather of men, the grandfather of the turtle.</p>
 <p>13. Nanaboush Nanaboush wemimokom, Wimimokom linnimokom tulamokom.</p>
 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_wa2_14"></a>
 <img src="img/wa2_14.jpg"></p>
 <p>14. The men then were together on the turtle, like to turtles.</p>
 <p>14. Linapi-ma tulapi-ma tulapewi tapitawi.</p>
 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_wa2_15"></a>
 <img src="img/wa2_15.jpg"></p>
 <p>15. Frightened on the turtle, they prayed on the turtle that what was spoiled should be restored.</p>
 <p>15. Wishanem tulpewi pataman tulpewi poniton wuliton.</p>
 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_wa2_16"></a>
 <img src="img/wa2_16.jpg"></p>
 <p>16. The water ran off, the earth dried, the lakes were at rest, all was silent, and the mighty snake departed.</p>
 <p>16. Kshipehelen penkwihilen, Kwamipokho sitwalikho, Maskan wagan palliwi palliwi.</p>
