 <h3 align="CENTER">SONG BEFORE THE FIGHT WITH CHEOF-HAHVO SEEVEN <a name="fr_5"></a><a href="#fn_5"><font size="1">1</font></a></h3>
 <p>In the land where there are a great many galley-worms--<br>
 I will get the doctor out,<br>
 It will lighten his heart.</p>
 <h3 align="CENTER">A SONG OF THE DOCTOR WHOSE SNAKE THREW DOWN THE VAHAHKKEE</h3>
 <p>I made the black snake;<br>
 And he went across and wounded the vahahkkee,</p>
