 <h1 align="CENTER">STORIES OF THE FIRST NIGHT</h1>
 <p><a name="page_27"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 27</font></a></p>
 <h3 align="CENTER">THE TRADITIONS OF THE PIMAS</h3>
 <p>THE old man, Comalk Hawk-Kih, (Thin Buckskin) began by saying that these were stories which he used to hear his father tell, they being handed down from father to son, and that when he was little he did not pay much attention, but when he grew older he determined to learn them, and asked his father to teach him, which his father did, and now he knew them all.</p>
