 <h3 align="CENTER">THE FIRST SONG OF EE-EE-TOY'S ARMY</h3>
 <p>The White Earth I come to and sing;<br>
 Where many war-bonnets are shaking with the wind;<br>
 There we come together to dance and to sing.</p>
 <h3 align="CENTER">THE DOCTOR'S SONG TO THE HUNTERS</h3>
 <i></i><p>Sahn-a-mahl! <a name="fr_4"></a><a href="#fn_4"><font size="1">1</font></a><br>
 Haymohl give me the necklace!<br>
 Sooadack Ceeavawt give me the turquoise ear-rings!</p>
