 <h3 align="CENTER">JUHWERTA MAHKAI'S SONG BEFORE THE FLOOD</h3>
 <p>       My poor people,<br>
        Who will see,<br>
        Who will see<br>
 This water which will moisten the earth!</p>
 <h3 align="CENTER">THE SONG OF SUPERSTITION MOUNTAINS</h3>
 <p>We are destroyed!<br>
 By my stone we are destroyed!<br>
 We are rightly turned into stone.</p>
 <h3 align="CENTER">EE-EE-TOY'S SONG WHEN HE MADE THE WORLD SERPENTS</h3>
 <p>I know what to do;<br>
 I am going to move the water both ways.</p>
