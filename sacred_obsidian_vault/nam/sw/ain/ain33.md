 <h3 align="CENTER">NOTES ON THE STORY OF SOHAHNEE MAHKAI</h3>
 <p>In this we are given a most graphic and pathetic glimpse of Indian warfare.</p>
 <p>Notice the bushes are "cut down" (broken off more likely) by a stick. A glimpse of the rude old tools.</p>
 <p>Very poetic is the conception of Veeipschool, "the being above who is always sad, and makes people sad when anything bad has happened." A personification of premonition.</p>
