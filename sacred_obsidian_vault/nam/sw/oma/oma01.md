 <p>LETTER OF TRANSMITTAL</p>
 <p align="RIGHT">SMITHSONIAN INSTITUTION,          <br>
 BUREAU OF AMERICAN ETHNOLOGY,    <br>
 <i>Washington, D. C., November 1, 1941</i>.</p>
 <p>Sir: I have the honor to transmit herewith a manuscript, entitled "Origin Myth of Acoma and Other Records," by Matthew W. Stirling, and to recommend that it be published as a bulletin of the Bureau of American Ethnology.</p>
 <p>            Very respectfully yours,</p>
 <p align="RIGHT">M. W. STIRLING, <i>Chief</i>.</p>
 <p>DR. C. G. ABBOT,</p>
 <i></i><p>Secretary of the Smithsonian Institution.</p>
