 <h3 align="CENTER">PLATE 17</h3>
 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_pl17"></a>
 </p><p align="CENTER">
 <a href="img/pl17.jpg"><img src="tn/pl17.jpg" alt="Plate 17"><br><font size="-3">Click to enlarge</font></a><br>Plate 17<br></p>
 <p>Prayer sticks for the Mountains of the four directions.</p>
 <i></i><p>N: Spruce wood (brown; face yellow) and twigs; headdress (center black, outlined in green, sides red, top edged with green), symbol of snow for winter. <i>W</i>: Pine (brown; face blue); okai reeds (green) from early spring water plant; headdress (center lobe black, side lobes green), symbol of early leaves for spring. <i>S</i>: Fir(?) (brown, face red); cattail leaves (green); headdress, symbol of jar, clouds (face, white) and water (green) for summer. <i>E</i>: Aspen (gray, face white); corn leaves (green) ("the clothes"); headdress, symbol of field (red terrace outlined in black; lightning symbol in black) for fall. Turkey feathers at ears of all four prayer sticks; eagle down at top.</p>
