 <h3 align="CENTER">PLATE 10</h3>
 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_pl10a"></a>
 </p><p align="CENTER">
 <a href="img/pl10a.jpg"><img src="tn/pl10a.jpg" alt="Plate 10, Figure 1"><br><font size="-3">Click to enlarge</font></a><br>Plate 10, Figure 1<br></p>
 <p>FIGURE 1. Kiva floor and murals. Central panel shows floor with medicine men (X X X) sitting behind altar, with shipapu kuwatsaishuma (O), medicine bowl (<a name="img_12100"></a>
 <img src="img/12100.jpg">), tsiwaimitiima (<a name="img_12101"></a>
 <img src="img/12101.jpg">), fireplace (<a name="img_12102"></a>
 <img src="img/12102.jpg">), ladders (<a name="img_12103"></a>
 <img src="img/12103.jpg">). <i>North wall</i>: Flint bird (white with black and brown dots, legs and tail brown), Clouds (upper three, white; lower three, orange, blue, red) and lightning (red) over field (orange). <i>West wall</i>: Water snake (brown) (killed by the Twins), Tsitsanits katsina (half blue, half black; forehead black divided by red triangle, horns green), Oshach katsina (blue face, green forehead, feather top piece); star (brown). <i>South wall</i> always left blank. <i>East wall</i>: Bears (left, black; right, brown) (note hearts, also two shamanistic eagle feathers in left paw); Kopishtaiya, Sun (red, with black "rays"); Kopishtaiya katsina (see pl. 5, fig. 2); Tsiukiri katsina (see pl. 3, fig. 2). People sit in an are around south, southeast, and southwest walls.</p>
 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_pl10b"></a>
 </p><p align="CENTER">
 <a href="img/pl10b.jpg"><img src="tn/pl10b.jpg" alt="Plate 10, Figure 2"><br><font size="-3">Click to enlarge</font></a><br>Plate 10, Figure 2<br></p>
 <p>FIGURE 2. Altar sand paintings. <i>a</i>, Fire society altar: The rim (blue) is the sky; the upper crescent contains the symbols for the sun (red), moon (yellow), and stars; the arc (pale gray) is the Milky Way; the face (yellow with black hair) below, Iatiku; and the spots (yellow) represent the earth; the triangular object (red) on breast is the heart, the center of the earth and the center of the picture. b, Ant society altar: Circle (pale gray) and spots (yellow) are of corn meal; star, blue; sun, red; moon, yellow. The lizard (green with 4 bands--yellow, blue, red, white--around throat) can eat ants and gives power to cure a person afflicted with itching caused by ants.</p>
