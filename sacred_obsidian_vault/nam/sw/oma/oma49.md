 <h3 align="CENTER">PLATE 16</h3>
 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_pl16a"></a>
 </p><p align="CENTER">
 <a href="img/pl16a.jpg"><img src="tn/pl16a.jpg" alt="Plate 16, Figure 1"><br><font size="-3">Click to enlarge</font></a><br>Plate 16, Figure 1<br></p>
 <p>FIGURE 1. Weapons (waiishi) given by Sun to the Twins. <i>a</i>, Dart used before any other weapon; corn cob (brown), large and burned and smoothed with stone; wood foreshaft (black) with arrowpoint (gray); feathers of any kind. <i>b</i>, Throwing stick (chaipichama), hard wood (brown) with lightning design (gray). <i>c</i>, Club of hard wood (brown), chaitsi (rabbit-club root). <i>d</i>, Otiawani of hard wood (brown). <i>e</i>, Arrow of achĭti wood (brown); eagle feathers (black) and arrowhead (gray) tied with sinew. <i>f</i>, Bow of oak (brown) with sinew back; string of sinew which is laid out flat, wet, then twisted up.</p>
 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_pl16b"></a>
 </p><p align="CENTER">
 <a href="img/pl16b.jpg"><img src="tn/pl16b.jpg" alt="Plate 16, Figure 2"><br><font size="-3">Click to enlarge</font></a><br>Plate 16, Figure 2<br></p>
 <p>FIGURE 2. Shield and pouch of the Twins. <i>a</i>, Shield of buffalo hide; the two-headed water snake (brown) that the Twins killed at the time of the flood; stone point (tip white, butt yellow, with 2 lines, blue and red above) that Lightning (red) tried to kill them with, the colors representing the different kinds of stone points that were used; bottom, water waves (blue); eagle feathers. <i>b</i>, Pouch (light tan).</p>
