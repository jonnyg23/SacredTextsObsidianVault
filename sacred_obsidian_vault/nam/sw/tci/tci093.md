 <h3 align="CENTER">WOODRATS <a name="fr_176"></a><a href="#fn_175"><font size="1">1</font></a></h3>
 <p>The woodrats lived in the mountains. There were a mother and father and lots of children. He sang for the children to dance,</p><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <p><font size="-1">I, i, i,<br>
 Cactus, <a href="errata.htm#1">cactus</a><br>
 It's useful to us (for nest)<br>
 It's useful to us!<br>
 Ha a yot si!</font></p></dir>
 </dir>
 
 <p><font size="-2" color="GREEN">[paragraph continues]</font> The little woodrats jumped and jumped and rolled over on the ground. He said,</p><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <p><font size="-1">I'm singing for you,<br>
 Try your best!<br>
 Try your best!<br>
 So we can kill a deer.</font></p></dir>
 </dir>
 
 <p><font size="-2" color="GREEN">[paragraph continues]</font> They tried hard and their father sang the song.</p>
