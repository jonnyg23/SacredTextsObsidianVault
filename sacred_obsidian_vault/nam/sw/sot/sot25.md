 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>Songs of the Tewa</i>, by Herbert Joseph Spinden, [1933], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h5 align="center">XXII</h5>  <h5 align="center">SLEEPY BIRD LULLABY</h5>  
 <p>There are many sleepy little birds,<br> Sleepy little birds, sleepy little birds,<br> So go to sleep, my little girl,<br> My little Frosted-Cockle-Burr,<br> O, come you sleepy little birds<br> And slumber on her hollow eyes<br> That she may sleep the livelong day,<br> That she may sleep the livelong night.</p>
