 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>Songs of the Tewa</i>, by Herbert Joseph Spinden, [1933], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h5 align="center">L</h5>  <h5 align="center">DEAD ON THE WAR PATH</h5>  
 <p>This very day, a little while ago, you lived<br> But now you are neither man nor woman,<br> Breathless you are, for the Navahos killed you!<br> Then remember us not, for here and now<br> We bring you your food. Then take and keep<br> Your earth-walled place: once! twice!<br> Three times! four times! Then leave us now!</p>
