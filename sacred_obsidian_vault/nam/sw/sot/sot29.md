 <p><a name="page_88"><font size="1" color="green">p. 88</font></a></p>  <h5 align="center">XXV</h5>  <h5 align="center">INITIATION SONG OF THE TEWA K’OSA</h5>  
 <p><i>The K’osa make pantomime</i><br>           In Pine Tree Glade<br>           It rained this much<br>           In a water-hole amid the rocks.</p>  
 <p><i>The K’osa address Kâkanyu Sendo</i><br>           Old Man Kâkanyu!<br>           The big yellow eye<br>           Runs off in the water!</p>  
 <p><i>The K' osa make pantomime</i><br>           In Pine Tree Glade<br>           It rained this much<br>           In a water hole amid the rocks.</p>  
 <p><i>The K’osa address Uhu Sendo</i><br>           Old man Uhu!<br>           The big yellow eye<br>           Runs off in the water.</p>  
 <p><i>The Cloud People pursue the K’osa</i><br>           No, my man! No, my man.<br>           The yellow biscuit with a hole—<br>           That is all I mean!</p>
