 <p><a name="page_73"><font size="1" color="green">p. 73</font></a></p>  <h5 align="center">III</h5>  <h5 align="center">I WONDER HOW MY HOME IS</h5>  
 <p>In San Juan I wonder how my home is.<br> Surrounded by green cottonwoods my home is,<br> Now I remember all and now I sing!<br> Now I remember how I used to live<br> And how I used to walk amid my corn<br> And through my fields. Alas! what can I do?</p>
