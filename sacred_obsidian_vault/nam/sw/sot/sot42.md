 <p><a name="page_102"><font size="1" color="green">p. 102</font></a></p>  <h5 align="center">XXXVII</h5>  <h5 align="center">THAT BUFFALO MAY COME</h5>  
 <p>From far away frozen Buffalo Country<br> Hither now they come with their little ones,<br> Rapidly now they walk, rapidly they walk.<br> Even now they reach the Red Bird Cap.</p>  
 <p>Oh Buffalo Old Man! Oh Buffalo Old Woman!<br> Come hither rapidly with your little ones.<br> To Y’o pha k’ewe come with your little ones.</p>  
 <p>They bring to us long life together<br> And even now they reach Tesuque!</p>
