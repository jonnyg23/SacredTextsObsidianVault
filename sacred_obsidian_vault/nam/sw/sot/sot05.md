 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>Songs of the Tewa</i>, by Herbert Joseph Spinden, [1933], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  
 <p><a name="page_72"><font size="1" color="green">p. 72</font></a></p>  <h5 align="center">I</h5>  <h5 align="center">THE ROAD OF MAGIC</h5>  
 <p>Yonder on White Mountain Plain<br> It was good in the long ago!<br> San Juan girls and San Juan boys,<br> Together they used to walk<br> Where lies the Road of Magic.</p>  
 <p>Yonder on Cactus Stalk Plain<br> It was good in the long ago!<br> Together we used to walk,<br> San Juan girls and San Juan boys,<br> Where lies the Road of Magic.</p>  
 <p>Yonder on Painted Mountain Plain<br> It was good in the long ago!<br> San Juan girls and San Juan boys,<br> With them I used to walk<br> Where lies the Road of Magic.</p>  
 <p>Here on Medicine Hill Plain<br> Again we walk together!<br> San Juan girls and San Juan boys,<br> Again we walk together<br> Where lies the Road of Magic!</p>
