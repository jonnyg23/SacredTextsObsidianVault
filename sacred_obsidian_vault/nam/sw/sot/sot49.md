 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>Songs of the Tewa</i>, by Herbert Joseph Spinden, [1933], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h5 align="center">XLIV</h5>  <h5 align="center">WHEN THE CORN MOTHERS ARE FED</h5>  
 <p><i>The Bear Priest sings and throws meal on the altar</i><br>     Now and here<br>     My gods I give you<br>     This corn meal<br>     And thank you.</p>  
 <p><i>The People join and throw meal on the altar</i><br>     Now and here,<br>     Our old women Gods,<br>     Take this!<br>     Long life give us<br>     And our children!</p>
