 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>Songs of the Tewa</i>, by Herbert Joseph Spinden, [1933], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  
 <p><a name="page_106"><font size="1" color="green">p. 106</font></a></p>  <h5 align="center">XLV</h5>  <h5 align="center">PRAYER FOR LONG LIFE</h5>  
 <p>Our old women gods, we ask you!<br> Our old women gods, we ask you!<br> Then give to us long life together,<br> May we live until our frosted hair<br> Is white; may we live till then<br> This life that now we know!</p>  
 <p>Our old women gods,<br> Come hither,<br> Take this!<br> Long life give us<br> And our people!</p>  
 <p>We bring you meal<br> For arm-leg nourishment<br> Then grant to us<br> Our span of life<br> And to our children also!</p>
