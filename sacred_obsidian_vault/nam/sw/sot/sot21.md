 <p><a name="page_80"><font size="1" color="green">p. 80</font></a></p>  <h5 align="center">XVIII</h5>  <h5 align="center">RAINS FOR THE HARVEST</h5>  
 <p>Over there in your fields you have<br> Musk-melon flowers in the morning.<br> Over there in your fields you have<br> Corn-tassle flowers in the morning.<br> In your fields now the water bird sings<br> And here in your village the fogs<br> And the black clouds come massing.<br> They come here to see! They come here to see!<br> <i>Mbe’e a-ha we-o-’e</i></p>
