 <p><a name="page_107"><font size="1" color="green">p. 107</font></a></p>  <h5 align="center">XLVII</h5>  <h5 align="center">WHEN THE MAN TAKES OFFICE</h5>  
 <p><i>The summer cacique speaks:</i><br>         Here and now my arm,<br>         Painted Sand-burrs Bringing,<br>         Of the Sun People, chosen<br>         Old-woman, old-man to be:<br>         Accept this governor's staff,<br>         Agree now to help it<br>         Protect it, obey it, love it.</p>  
 <p><i>The new governor replies:</i><br>         Here and now, my fathers,<br>         Summer priest, Winter priest,<br>         I hear your words and obey them.</p>
