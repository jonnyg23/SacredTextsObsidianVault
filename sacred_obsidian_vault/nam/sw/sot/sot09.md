 <p><a name="page_74"><font size="1" color="green">p. 74</font></a></p>  <h5 align="center">V</h5>  <h5 align="center">BANTER</h5>  
 <p>Oh, somewhere yonder in the west<br> You go away to gather wood.<br> And now you shout and now you sing.<br> Oh yes, I remember! Abruptly you left me!<br> Laughing was I, nevertheless, you left me!</p>
