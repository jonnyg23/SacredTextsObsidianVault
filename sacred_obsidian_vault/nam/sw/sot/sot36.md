 <p><a name="page_96"><font size="1" color="green">p. 96</font></a></p>  <h5 align="center">XXXII</h5>  <h5 align="center">SONGS IN THE TURTLE DANCE OF NAMBE</h5>  
 <p><i>First song</i></p>  
 <p>Yellow Flower Girl!<br> Blue Flower Girl!<br> Mottled Corn Girl!<br> Blue Corn Girl!<br> Thus on the plain,<br> Thus on the plain,<br> Everything they revive,<br> And hither return!</p>  
 <p><i>Second song</i></p>  
 <p>Out over Great Dawn Canyon Lake<br> The various fog plains be scattered<br> And now from there Cloud Boys come walking<br> And now from there Cloud Girls come walking.<br> With little ones they come to Nambe town<br> To make the children live and grow<br> That, one and all, we may be happy.</p>  
 <p><i>Third song</i></p>  
 <p>Rain waters now are falling<br> Afar the thunders crash,<br> Afar the lightnings gleam,<br> Afar the lightnings flash<br> And quickly now return.</p>  
 <p><i>Fourth song</i></p>  
 <p>The red round fruits<br> We gather in!<br> The red round fruits<br> We gather in!</p>
