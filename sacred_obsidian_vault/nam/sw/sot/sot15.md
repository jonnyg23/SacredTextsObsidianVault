 <p>"That dirty one, that ragged one."<br> Thus do they deride us two.</p>  
 <p>But you, you have good fortune;<br> Yellow clothes, pink clothes, blue clothes.<br> These are the only kinds you wear!</p>  
 <p>But you not mind to be a mule!</p>
