 <img src="img/03000.jpg"></p>  <h3 align="center">Mission Santa Clara de Assisi</h3>  
 <p><a name="img_einit"></a>
 </p><table align="LEFT"><tr><td align="CENTER" valign="CENTER"><img src="img/einit.jpg" alt="E"></td></tr></table>IGHTH in line was founded in 1777. It is located in the lovely little city of Santa Clara adjacent to the better known metropolis of San Jose. By reason of fires and one or two rather destructive temblors, this once beautiful structure finally reached almost complete destruction. Lately, however, the Jesuit Fathers having had for many years the seat of their university at Santa Clara have rebuilt the old church exactly as it originally stood.
