 <img src="img/05200.jpg"></p>  <h3 align="center">Mission Santa Ines</h3>  
 <p><a name="img_finit"></a>
 </p><table align="LEFT"><tr><td align="CENTER" valign="CENTER"><img src="img/finit.jpg" alt="F"></td></tr></table>OUNDED in 1804, nineteenth in the chain, located near the town of Los Olivos. This is one of the most beautiful of all Missions, and a great deal of restoration work has been done upon it mainly through the financial assistance of the Society of the Native Sons of the Golden West. Surrounding the old church is an Indian Reservation still maintained by the Government upon which a few Indian families continue to live. Much of the credit for the restoration of this beautiful edifice is due to the zeal of Father Alexander Butler who was for many years the resident priest.
