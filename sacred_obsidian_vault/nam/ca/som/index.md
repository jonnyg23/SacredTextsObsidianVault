 <h1 align="CENTER">A SABOBA ORIGIN-MYTH.</h1>
 <h2 align="CENTER">By George Wharton James</h2>
 <h4 align="CENTER">Journal of the American Folk-Lore Society, Vol. XV., No. 61, pp. 36-9.</h4>
 <h4 align="CENTER">[1902]</h4>
 <hr>
 <p>This is a short article on the origin myth of the native Californians
 of the Tehachapi mountain region, written over a hundred years ago.
 This area traditionally marks the bounday between
 northern and southern California.
 Although this article reflects some backward attitudes of the time,
 it is the one of the few (if any) records
 we have of the mythology of this region.
 </p>
