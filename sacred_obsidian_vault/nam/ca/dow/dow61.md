 <p><a name="page_223"><font size="1" color="green">p. 223</font></a></p>  <h1 align="center">Beliefs Concerning Natural Phenomena</h1>  <h3 align="center">THUNDER</h3>  
 <p><i>The Northern Mewuk near South Fork Cosumnes River say</i>:</p>  
 <p><i>Tim'-mel-le</i> the Thunder is, or is like, <i>Ti'-e-te</i> the Valley Bluejay. He lives down below [west] in the San Joaquin Valley, where the clouds are. Sometimes he becomes angry and makes a great rumbling noise; this noise is <i>Tim'-mel-le</i> the Thunder. <a name="fr_26"></a><a href="#fn_26"><font size="1">28</font></a></p>
