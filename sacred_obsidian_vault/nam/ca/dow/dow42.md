 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Dawn of the World</i>, by C. Hart Merriam, [1910], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">HOW HE-LE'-JAH THE COUGAR HUNTS DEER</h3>  
 <p><i>The Northern Mewuk say</i>:</p>  
 <p><i>He-le'-jah</i> the Cougar is a hunter. He hunts <i>O-woo'-yah</i> the Deer. He crawls toward it like a cat, without making any noise; and when near enough makes a big leap and catches it, or knocks it down with his long tail. When he has killed the</p>  
 <p><a name="page_210"><font size="1" color="green">p. 210</font></a></p>  
 <p><span class="contnote"><font size="-2" color="GREEN">[paragraph continues]</font></span> Deer he throws his long tail around it, and packs it off on his back.</p>
