 <p><i>The Middle Mewuk of Stanislaus River region say</i>:</p>  
 <p><i>Ki'-ki'-ah</i>, the Crested Bluejay of the mountains, plants acorns so that oak trees come up almost everywhere.</p>  
 <p>[Several other tribes mention the same habit which, by the way, is hardly a myth.]</p>
