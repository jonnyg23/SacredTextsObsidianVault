 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Dawn of the World</i>, by C. Hart Merriam, [1910], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">THE RAINBOW</h3>  
 <p><i>The Northern Mewuk say</i>:</p>  
 <p><i>Ku-yet'-tah</i> the Rainbow comes to tell the people a baby is born. When anyone sees <i>Ku-yet'-tah</i> he knows that somewhere a new baby has come. Everybody knows that.</p>  
 <p><i>The Hookooeko of Nicasio and Tomales Bay say</i>:</p>  
 <p><i>Kah-chah</i> the Rainbow is the bow of <i>O'-ye</i> the Coyote-man.</p>
