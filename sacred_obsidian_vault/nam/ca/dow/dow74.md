 <p><a name="page_236"><font size="1" color="green">p. 236</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">KA'-LUM-ME THE ROCK GIANT OF WENNOK VALLEY</h3>  
 <p><i>The Olayome of Putah Creek say</i>:</p>  
 <p>In a cave under the cliff on the east face of <i>Oo'-tel-tal-lah pow'-we</i>, a small mountain southwest of the south end of Wennok Lake in Lake County, dwells <i>Kā'-lum-me</i> the Rock Giant. He used to roam about nights, catching Indians and carrying them off to his cave to eat. He has not done this for some time.</p>
