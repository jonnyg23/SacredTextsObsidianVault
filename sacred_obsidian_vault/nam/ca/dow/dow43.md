 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Dawn of the World</i>, by C. Hart Merriam, [1910], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">HOW TOO-LE'-ZE, THE TIMBER-WOLF HUNTS DEER</h3>  
 <p><i>The Northern Mewuk say</i>:</p>  
 <p><i>Too-le'-ze</i> the Big Wolf is a hunter. Like <i>He-le'-jah</i> the Cougar or Mountain Lion he hunts Deer, but he hunts in a different way. He chases them like <i>Choo'-koo</i> the Dog but catches them by the throat with his claws, which he sinks deep into the sides of the throat. In the early morning he howls long howls. He used to be common here but now is rarely seen.</p>
