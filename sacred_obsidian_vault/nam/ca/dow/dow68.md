 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Dawn of the World</i>, by C. Hart Merriam, [1910], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">PIGMIES AND WATER PEOPLE</h3>  
 <p><i>The Hookooeko of Nicasio and Tomales Bay say</i>:</p>  
 <p><i>Se'-kah</i> the Little Folk dwell in thick places in the dark redwood forest, where no people live. They are very small. Sometimes they make people crazy.</p>  
 <p><i>Le'-wah ke'-lak</i> the Water People live in the ocean, in a roundhouse under the water; sometimes they come up and show themselves.</p>
