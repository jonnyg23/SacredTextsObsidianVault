 <p><i>The Hookooeko of San Rafael say</i>:</p>  
 <p><i>Yu'-ten me'-chah</i> the Evil One lives in the hills just north of San Rafael; he travels about at night and sometimes comes and touches people when they are asleep, to frighten them.</p>
