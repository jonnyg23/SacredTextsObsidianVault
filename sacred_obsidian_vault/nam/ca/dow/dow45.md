 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Dawn of the World</i>, by C. Hart Merriam, [1910], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">THE FIRST TEETH GO TO SOO-WAH-TAH, THE GOPHER</h3>  
 <p><i>The Middle Mewuk of Tuolumne River say</i>:</p>  
 <p>When a child sheds its first teeth they should be</p>  
 <p><a name="page_211"><font size="1" color="green">p. 211</font></a></p>  
 <p>saved and taken to <i>Soo-wah-tah</i> the Pocket Gopher, and carefully put into his hole. Then the second teeth will come quickly and grow to be strong and good.</p>
