 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Dawn of the World</i>, by C. Hart Merriam, [1910], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">WHERE KOO-TAH THE MONEY-CLAM CAME FROM</h3>  
 <p><i>The Olamentko of Bodega Bay say</i>:</p>  
 <p>Coyote-man brought <i>Koo'-tah</i> the big clam, from which <i>pis'-pe</i> the shell money is made, and planted it here at Bodega Bay. <a name="fr_21"></a><a href="#fn_21"><font size="1">23</font></a> This is the place and the only place where the big clam was in the beginning. Wherever else you find it now, the seed came from here. The Tomales Bay people got their seed here.</p>
