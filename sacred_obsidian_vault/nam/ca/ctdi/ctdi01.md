 <h1 align="CENTER">CEREMONIES AND TRADITIONS OF THE DIEGUEÑO INDIANS. <a name="fr_0"></a><a href="#fn_0"><font size="1">1</font></a></h1>
 <h2 align="CENTER">BY CONSTANCE GODDARD DU BOIS.</h2>
 <p>THE following pages, discussing mainly the relations existing between the religious practices and religious beliefs--the ceremonies and the traditions--of the Diegueño Indians of Southern California, may be regarded as supplementary to a parallel but fuller treatment of the neighboring Luiseños. <a name="fr_1"></a><a href="#fn_1"><font size="1">2</font></a> The Diegueños are of Yuman stock, the Luiseños Shoshonean; but the two tribes are very similar in general culture, and, as has been pointed out, one has evidently greatly influenced the religious life of the other.</p>
