 <h1 align="CENTER">XXIII.</h1>
 <h3 align="CENTER">Formula used at the Tcexōltcwe Rocks. <a name="fr_60"></a><a href="#fn_60"><font size="1">2</font></a></h3>
 <p>na-xōL-tûñ na-xōL-tûñ dō-wes-yō nûñ-xōs-tiñ<br>
 Let it get soft | Let it get soft | I don't like | frost.</p>
