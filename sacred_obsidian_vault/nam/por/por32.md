 <p><a name="page_121"><font size="1" color="green">p. 121</font></a></p>  <h4 align="center">SONG OF THE RAIN CHANT</h4>  
 <p align="center"><b>NAVAJO</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Far as man can see,<br>     Comes the rain,<br>     Comes the rain with me.</div>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">From the Rain-Mount,<br> Rain-Mount far away,<br>     Comes the rain,<br>     Comes the rain with me.</div>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">O’er the corn,<br> O’er the corn, tall corn,<br>     Comes the rain,<br>     Comes the rain with me.</div>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">’Mid the lightnings,<br> ’Mid the lightnings zigzag,<br> ’Mid the lightnings flashing,<br>     Comes the rain,<br>     Comes the rain with me.</div>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">’Mid the swallows,<br> ’Mid the swallows blue,<br> Chirping glad together,<br>     Comes the rain,<br>     Comes the rain with me.</div>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Through the pollen,<br> Through the pollen blest,<br> All in pollen hidden,<br>     Comes the rain,<br>     Comes the rain with me.</div>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Far as man can see,<br>     Comes the rain,<br>     Comes the rain with me.</div>
