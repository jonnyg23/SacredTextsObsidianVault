 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Path on the Rainbow</i>, edited by George W. Cronyn, [1918], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">WAR SONGS</h3>  <h4 align="center">ZUNI</h4>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">To you of the six regions<br> Whose homes cover the earth<br> I give shells..<br> Yellow ant, blue ant, red ant, all color ant<br> U—hu—we—u—hu—we-<br> U—hu—we—u—hu—we.<br> To you whose homes<br> Are covered with mountain tops</div>  
 <p><a name="page_62"><font size="1" color="green">p. 62</font></a></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">I give shells<br> Yellow, blue, red, white, all color, black.<br> When we meet the enemy<br> A little arrow storm will arise.<br> When we meet him on the road nearby<br> He will never more inhale the sacred breath of day.<br> I inhale the sacred breath of day.<br> All come quickly,<br> The enemy comes from ambush.<br> A Navajo is killed,<br> We inhale the sacred breath of day.</div>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">You have met the enemy.<br> He will never more inhale the sacred breath of day.<br> The enemy is destroyed.<br> Who will be our great Bow priest?<br> Who is to become a Bow priest?<br> Well! who will kick the scalp?<br> His younger mother, his elder sister.<br> Who will wash the scalp?<br> His younger father, his elder brother.<br> Well!<br> Good!</div>
