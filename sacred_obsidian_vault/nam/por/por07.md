 <p><a name="page_10"><font size="1" color="green">p. 10</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">FIRE-FLY SONG</h3>  
 <p align="center"><b>OJIBWA</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Flitting white-fire insects!<br> Wandering small-fire beasts!<br> Wave little stars about my bed!<br> Weave little stars into my sleep!<br> Come, little dancing white-fire bug,<br> Come, little flitting white-fire beast!<br> Light me with your white-flame magic,<br> Your little star-torch.</div>
