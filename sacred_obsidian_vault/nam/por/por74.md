 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">    Strike ye our land<br> With curved horns!<br> Now with cries<br> Bending our bodies,</div>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p><a name="page_201"><font size="1" color="green">p. 201</font></a></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Breathe fire upon us;<br> Now with feet<br> Trampling the earth,<br> Let your hoofs<br> Thunder over us!<br> Strike ye our land<br> With curved horns!</div>
