 <p><a name="page_12"><font size="1" color="green">p. 12</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">LOVE-SONG</h3>  
 <p align="center"><b>WINNEBAGO</b></p>  
 <p>Whomsoe’er look I upon<br>     He becomes love-crazed;<br> Whomsoe’er speak I unto,<br>     He becomes love-crazed;<br> Whomsoe’er whisper I to,<br>     He becomes love-crazed;<br> All men who love women,<br> Them I rule, them I rule,<br>     My friend;<br> Whom I touch, whom I touch,<br>     He becomes love-crazed.</p>  <h3 align="center">HOLY SONG</h3>  
 <p align="center"><b>WINNEBAGO</b></p>  
 <p>    </p><div style="margin-left: 32px">(Saith the Spirit,<br> "Dream, oh, dream again,<br> And tell of me,<br>         Dream thou!")</div>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Into solitude went I<br> And wisdom was revealed to me.<br>     (Saith the Spirit,<br> "Dream, oh, dream again,<br> And tell of me,<br>         Dream thou!")</div>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Let the whole world hear me,<br> Wise am I!<br>     (Now saith the Spirit,<br> "Tell of me,<br>         Dream thou!")</div>  
 <p><a name="page_13"><font size="1" color="green">p. 13</font></a></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">All was revealed to me;<br> From the beginning<br> Know I all, hear me!<br> All was revealed to me!<br>     (Now saith the Spirit,<br> "Tell of me,<br>         Dream thou!")</div>
