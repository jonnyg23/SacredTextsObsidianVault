 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Path on the Rainbow</i>, edited by George W. Cronyn, [1918], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">CHINOOK SONGS</h3>  <h4 align="center">SONGS OF THE MAN</h4>  
 <p align="center"><b>I</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Ya, that is good!<br> Ya, that is good!<br> That worthless woman<br>     does not like me.</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>II</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Very unhappy I was<br>     with my wife,<br>     in Victoria.<br> Nobody<br>     said good-day to us<br>     in Victoria.</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>III</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Aya, aya!<br>     I have seen<br>     Sitka your country.<br> Never mind, if I die<br>     now soon.</div>  
 <p><a name="page_155"><font size="1" color="green">p. 155</font></a></p>  <h4 align="center">SONGS OF THE WOMAN</h4>  
 <p align="center"><b>I</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">I don't care<br>     if you desert me.<br> Many pretty boys are in the town.<br> Soon I shall take another one.<br> That is not hard for me!</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>II</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Nothing shall bother my mind now.<br> Don't speak to me. I wish I were dead with my sister.</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>III</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Ya  ya,<br> When you take a wife,<br> Ya  ya,<br> Don't become angry with me.<br> I do not care.</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>IV</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">I am very glad<br>     when the steamboat comes here.<br> I think I shall cry<br>     when the steamboat leaves.</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>V</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">I broke down! my dear!<br> Say good-bye!<br>     to me now.<br> Always I cry<br>     for I live far away.</div>
