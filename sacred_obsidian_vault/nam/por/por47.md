Cronyn, [1918], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">SONGS FROM THE FAR NORTH</h3>  <h3 align="center">SONGS FOR THE GREAT FEAST TO THE DEAD</h3>  
 <p align="center"><b>I</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">For our children are gone,<br>     While those of our friends remain.<br> <i>      Ai-ya-ya-yai.<br> </i>Come back, nephew, come back, we miss you;<br> <i>      Ai-ya-ya-yai.<br> </i>Come back to us, our lost ones,<br>     We have presents for you.<br> <i>      Ai-ya-ya-yai.<br> </i>Oh, my brother, come back to me,<br> <i>      Ai-ya-ya-yai.<br> </i>Come back, my brother, I am lonely,<br> <i>      Ai-ya-ya-yai.<br> </i>My brother come back and we<br>     Will give you a small present,<br> <i>      Ai-ya-ya-yai-yae-yai,</i> etc.<br> My children, where are you?<br> <i>      Ai-ya-ya-yai</i>.<br> Come back to us, our children.<br>     We are lonely and sad.<br> <i>      Ai-ya-ya-yai.</i></div>  
 <p align="center"><b>II</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Come, my brother,<br> Return to us again;<br> We wait for you;</div>  
 <p><a name="page_160"><font size="1" color="green">p. 160</font></a></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Come, brother, come.<br> <i>      Ai-ya-ya-yai.</i><br> Return once more.<br> Our mother, come back to us.<br> <i>      Ai-ya-ya-yai.</i><br> Return, our father;<br> We wait for you;<br> Come back to us,<br> And we, who are lonely,<br> Will give you food.<br> <i>      Ai-ya-ya-yai.</i></div>  
 <p align="center"><b>III</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Dead ones, come here;<br> <i>      A-la’-ai-ya’<br> </i>Come here, do.<br>     sealskins for a tent you will get,<br> <i>      A-la’-ai-ya’<br> </i>Come here, do.<br>     reindeer skins for a bed you will get,<br> <i>      A-la’-ai-ya’</i></div>  
 <p align="center"><b>IV</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">We will sing a song.<br> We will go down the current.<br> The waves will rise;<br> The waves will fall.<br> The dogs will growl at us.</div>
