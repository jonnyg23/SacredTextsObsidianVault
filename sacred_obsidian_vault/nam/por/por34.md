 <p><a name="page_123"><font size="1" color="green">p. 123</font></a></p>  <h4 align="center">CORN-GRINDING SONG</h4>  
 <p align="center"><b>ZUÑI</b></p>  
 <p align="center"><b>I</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">O, my lovely mountain,<br>     To’ yallanne!<br> O, my lovely mountain,<br>     To’ yallanne!<br>     To’ yallanne!<br> High up in the sky,<br> See Rain-makers seated,<br> Hither come the rain-clouds now,<br>      He-ya, ha-ya, he-ya!</div>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Behold, yonder<br> All will soon be abloom<br> Where the flowers spring<br> Tall shall grow the youthful corn-plants.</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>II</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Lovely! See the cloud, the cloud appear!<br> Lovely! See the rain, the rain draw near!<br>     Who spoke?<br> ’Twas the little corn-ear<br> High on the tip of the stalk<br> Singing while it looked at me<br>     Talking aloft there<br> "Ah, perchance the floods<br>     Hither moving<br> Ah, may the floods come this way!"</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>III</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Yonder, yonder the fair rainbow,<br> See the rainbow brightly decked and painted!<br> Now the swallow bringeth glad news to your corn,</div>  
 <p><a name="page_124"><font size="1" color="green">p. 124</font></a></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Singing, "Hitherward, hitherward, hitherward, rain,<br>     "Hither come!<br>     "Hither come!"<br> Now hear the corn-plants murmur,<br> "We are growing, everywhere!<br> "Hi, yai! The world, how fair!"</div>
