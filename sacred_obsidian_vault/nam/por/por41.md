 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Path on the Rainbow</i>, edited by George W. Cronyn, [1918], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h4 align="center">BEAR SONG</h4>  
 <p align="center"><b>HAIDA</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Chief, chief, that I am,<br> Be careful how you pull your grandfather around.<br> Be careful how you pull around your grandfather<br> As you sit beside him.<br> I am too much of a boy for you.<br> Chief, chief that I am,  
 <p>Chief, chief that I am,<br> I am already far away.<br> At the cliff, coming from my passage through the mountains,<br> I, hold up my head grandly.<br> Chief, chief that I am,<br> I am already far away from it.<br> From my blue mountain I am now far away.<br> On the Island I travel, led about proudly.<br> From it I am far away.<br> Chief, chief that I am.</p>  
 <p>Chief, chief that I am,<br> They say that I have green mountains.<br> They say that I went into the creek I own which stretches its length afar.<br> Chief, chief that I am.</p></div>  
 <p><a name="page_138"><font size="1" color="green">p. 138</font></a></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Chief, chief that I am,<br> When the sun rises I start traveling about.<br> Now I am lying under a deadfall.<br> Chief, chief that I am.  
 <p>Chief, chief that I am,<br> My power is all taken away,<br> My power is all taken away.<br> Chief, chief that I am,<br> My power is all taken away,<br> Chief, chief that I am.</p>  
 <p>Chief, chief, whither did my great brother wander proudly?<br> My mind shakes as I go about.<br> Chief, chief.</p>  
 <p>Chief, chief,<br> Tell me where he fell.<br> I do not know the place.<br> Chief, chief, chief.</p></div>
