 <p><a name="page_82"><font size="1" color="green">p. 82</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">SONG OF THE HORSE</h3>  <h4 align="center">NAVAJO</h4>  
 <p>    How joyous his neigh!<br> Lo, the Turquoise Horse of Johano-ai,<br>     How joyous his neigh,<br> There on precious hides outspread standeth he;<br>     How joyous his neigh,<br> There on tips of fair fresh flowers feedeth he;<br>     How joyous his neigh,<br> There of mingled waters holy drinketh he;<br>     How joyous his neigh,<br> There he spurneth dust of glittering grains;<br>     How joyous his neigh,<br> There in mist of sacred pollen hidden, all hidden he;<br>     How joyous his neigh,<br> There his offspring many grow and thrive for evermore;<br>     How joyous his neigh!</p>
