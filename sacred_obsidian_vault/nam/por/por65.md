 <p><a name="page_188"><font size="1" color="green">p. 188</font></a></p>  <h4 align="center">SONG OF A PASSIONATE LOVER</h4>  
 <p align="center"><b>FROM THE YOKUT</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Come not near my songs,<br> You who are not my lover,<br> Lest from out that ambush<br> Leaps my heart upon you!  
 <p>When my songs are glowing<br> As an almond thicket<br> With the bloom upon it,<br> Lies my heart in ambush<br> All amid my singing;<br> Come not near my songs,<br> You who are not my lover!</p>  
 <p>Do not hear my songs,<br> You who are not my lover,<br> Over-sweet the heart is<br> Where my love has bruised it,<br> Breathe you not that fragrance,<br> You who are not my lover!<br> Do not stoop above my heart<br> With its languor on you,<br> Lest I should not know you<br> From my own belovèd,<br> Lest from out my singing<br> Leaps my heart upon you!</p></div>
