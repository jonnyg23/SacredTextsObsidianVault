Cronyn, [1918], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">THE TOWN CRIER CALLS AT DAWN TO ANNOUNCE THE FEAST</h3>  <h4 align="center">PUEBLO</h4>  
 <p>All people awake, open your eyes, arise,<br> Become children of light, vigorous, active, sprightly.<br> Hasten clouds from the four world quarters;<br> Come snow in plenty, that water may be abundant when summer comes;<br> Come ice, cover the fields, that the planting may yield abundance,<br> Let all hearts be glad!<br> The knowing ones will assemble in four days;<br> They will encircle the village dancing and singing songs…<br> That moisture may come in abundance.</p>
