 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Path on the Rainbow</i>, edited by George W. Cronyn, [1918], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">WHERE THE FIGHT WAS</h3>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">    In the place where the fight was<br> Across the river,<br> In the place where the fight was<br> Across the river:<br> A heavy load for a woman<br> To lift in her blanket,<br> A heavy load for a woman<br> To carry on her shoulder.<br> In the place where the fight was<br> Across the river,<br> In the place where the fight was<br> Across the river:<br> The women go wailing<br> To gather the wounded,<br> The women go wailing<br> To pick up the dead.</div>
