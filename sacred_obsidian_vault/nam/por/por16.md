 <p align="center"><b>DAKOTA</b></p>  
 <p align="center"><b>I</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Something I've killed, and I lift up my voice;<br> Something I've killed, and I lift up my voice;<br> The northern buffalo I've killed, and I lift up my voice;<br> Something I've killed, and I lift up my voice.</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>II</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">The saddle bind:<br> Children dear.<br> For half a day,<br> I will kill.</div>
