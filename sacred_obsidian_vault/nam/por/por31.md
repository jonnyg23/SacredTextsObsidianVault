 <p><a name="page_120"><font size="1" color="green">p. 120</font></a></p>  <h4 align="center">HUNTING SONG</h4>  
 <p align="center"><b>NAVAJO</b></p>  
 <p>      </p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Comes the deer to my singing,<br>       Comes the deer to my song,<br>       Comes the deer to my singing.</div>  
 <p>    </p><div style="margin-left: 32px">He, the blackbird, he am I,<br>     Bird beloved of the wild deer,<br>       Comes the deer to my singing.</div>  
 <p>    </p><div style="margin-left: 32px">From the Mountain Black,<br>     From the summit,<br>     Down the trail, coming, coming now,<br>       Comes the deer to my singing.</div>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Through the blossoms,<br>     Through the flowers, coming, coming now,<br>       Comes the deer to my singing.</div>  
 <p>    </p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Through the flower dew-drops,.<br>       Coming, coming now,<br>       Comes the deer to my singing.</div>  
 <p>    </p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Through the pollen, flower pollen,<br>       Coming, coming now,<br>       Comes the deer to my singing.</div>  
 <p>    </p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Starting with his left fore-foot,<br> Stamping, turns the frightened deer,<br>       Comes the deer to my singing.</div>  
 <p>    </p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Quarry mine, blessed am I<br>     In the luck of the chase.<br>       Comes the deer to my singing.</div>  
 <p>      </p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Comes the deer to my singing,<br>       Comes the deer to my song,<br>       Comes the deer to my singing.</div>
