 <p><a name="page_122"><font size="1" color="green">p. 122</font></a></p>  <h4 align="center">SONG FROM THE MOUNTAIN CHANT</h4>  
 <p align="center"><b>NAVAJO</b></p>  
 <p>    </p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Thereof he telleth.</div>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Now of the Holy Youth,<br>     Thereof he telleth.</div>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Moccasins decked with black,<br>     Thereof he telleth.</div>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">And richly broidered dress,<br>     Thereof he telleth.</div>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Arm-bands of eagle feathers,<br>     Thereof he telleth.</div>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">And now the rain-plumes,<br>     Thereof he telleth.</div>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Now of the Male-Rain,<br>     Thereof he telleth.</div>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Now of the rain-drops fallen,<br>     Thereof he telleth.</div>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Now of Unending Life,<br>     Thereof he telleth.</div>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Now of Unchanging Joy,<br>     Thereof he telleth.</div>  
 <p>    </p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Thereof he telleth.</div>
