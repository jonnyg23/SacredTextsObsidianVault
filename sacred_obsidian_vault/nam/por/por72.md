 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Path on the Rainbow</i>, edited by George W. Cronyn, [1918], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">LONE-DOG UNTO THE DELAWARES</h3>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Brothers—Lennapi,<br> Hear ye, come ye, all ye—<br> By south-way, east-way, shore-land place,<br> Men come,<br> Boats come,<br> Float fast,<br> Handsome.<br> Man-who-Paints, much-talker, he much-walked<br> Easterly, south also,<br> All-time stalked—<br> Friends they, he says,<br> Sun-rise men, sun-born men, east-coming;<br> Great things have, wonderful, thundering<br> Yea, great things, hear we, from clam-clam-sea—<br> Hear ye—<br> White-bird boat, great eagle, floats up streams,<br> Man-carrying, house-bearing, much-fire gleams.<br> Friends they,<br> Say they,<br> Come ye, hear ye, all ye,<br> Brothers there, cousins there, Lennapi,<br> Turtle men, turkey men, wolf clan,</div>  
 <p><a name="page_200"><font size="1" color="green">p. 200</font></a></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Let us running-friendly be,<br> Let us brother-hearted be,<br> Giver-brothers standing we,<br> Men with meat, men with hides, everyman,<br> Come ye, run ye, all ye.<br> From Branchy-river-place,<br> From Straightened-river-place,<br> From Great-falls-at,<br> From Shore-land-at,<br> Sassafras-land.<br> Many caves men,<br> Riding waves men,<br> All ye Wolves, Turtles, Lennapi—<br> Bring your gifts, laying them<br> At Clam-clam-sea.</div>
