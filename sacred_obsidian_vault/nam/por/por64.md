 <p align="center"><b>FROM THE PAIUTE</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Go thy way in comeliness!<br> Strong sun across the sod doth make<br> Such quickening as thy countenance.  
 <p>Pursue thy unguessed errand and pass by;<br> I am more worth for what thy passing wakes,<br> Great races in my loins to thee that cry!<br> My blood is redder for thy loveliness.<br> Prosper; be fair; pass by!</p></div>
