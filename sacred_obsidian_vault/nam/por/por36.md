 <p><a name="page_125"><font size="1" color="green">p. 125</font></a></p>  <h4 align="center">HE-HEA KATZINA SONG</h4>  
 <p align="center"><b>HOPI</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Corn-blossom maidens<br> Here in the fields,<br> Patches of beans in flower,<br> Fields all abloom,<br> Water shining after rain,<br> Blue clouds looming above.</div>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Now behold!<br> Through bright clusters of flowers<br> Yellow butterflies<br> Are chasing at play,<br> And through the blossoming beans<br> Blue butterflies<br> Are chasing at play.</div>
