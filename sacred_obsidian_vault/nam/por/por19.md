 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Path on the Rainbow</i>, edited by George W. Cronyn, [1918], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">SONGS OF THE GHOST-DANCE RELIGION</h3>  <h4 align="center">ARAPAHO</h4>  
 <p align="center"><b>DISILLUSION</b></p>  
 <p align="center"><b>I</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">My children, when at first I liked the Whites,<br> My children, when at first I liked the Whites,<br> I gave them fruits,<br> I gave them fruits.</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>ECSTASY</b></p>  
 <p align="center"><b>II</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">My children, my children,<br> The wind makes the head-feathers sing—<br> The wind makes the head-feathers sing.<br> My children, my children.</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>LABORS OF THE SPIRIT</b></p>  
 <p align="center"><b>III</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">My children, my children,<br> I take pity on those who have been taught,<br> Because they push on hard,<br> Says our Father.</div>  
 <p><a name="page_52"><font size="1" color="green">p. 52</font></a></p>  
 <p align="center"><b>WONDER</b></p>  
 <p align="center"><b>IV</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">How bright is the moonlight!<br> Tonight as I ride with my load of buffalo beef.</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>THE WHIRLWIND (POWER OF CHANGE) SPEAKS</b></p>  
 <p align="center"><b>V</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">I circle around<br> The boundaries of earth,<br> Wearing the long wing feathers as I fly.</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>VISION</b></p>  
 <p align="center"><b>VI</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">My children, my children,<br> Look! the earth is about to move.<br> My Father tells me so.</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>SPIRIT-JOY</b></p>  
 <p align="center"><b>VII</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">I fly around yellow,<br> I fly with the wild rose on my head,<br> On high—He’e’e’!</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>REVELATION</b></p>  
 <p align="center"><b>VIII</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">My children, my children,<br> It is I who wear the morning star on my head;<br> I show it to my children,<br> Says the Father.</div>  
 <p><a name="page_53"><font size="1" color="green">p. 53</font></a></p>  
 <p align="center"><b>TRAVAIL, OF SPIRIT</b></p>  
 <p align="center"><b>IX</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Father, have pity on me,<br> Father, have pity on me;<br> I am crying for thirst;<br> All is gone—I have nothing to eat.</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>INVOCATION</b></p>  
 <p align="center"><b>X</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Father, the Morning Star!<br> Father, the Morning Star!<br> Look on us, we have danced until daylight,<br> Take pity on us—Hi’i’i!</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>THE MESSIAH SPEAKS</b></p>  
 <p align="center"><b>CHEYENNE</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">I am coming in sight—<br> I bring the Whirlwind with me—<br> That you may know one another.</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>REINCARNATION</b></p>  
 <p align="center"><b>COMANCHE</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">We shall live again,<br> We shall live again.<br> The sun's beams are spreading out—He’e’yo’!<br> The sun's yellow rays are spreading out—Ahi’ni’yo’!</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>SONGS OF LIFE RETURNING</b></p>  
 <p align="center"><b>PAIUTE</b></p>  
 <p align="center"><b>I</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">The wind stirs the willows.<br> The wind stirs the grasses.</div>  
 <p><a name="page_54"><font size="1" color="green">p. 54</font></a></p>  
 <p align="center"><b>II</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">The cottonwoods are growing tall,<br> They are growing tall and verdant.</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>III</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">A slender antelope,<br> A slender antelope<br> He is wallowing upon the ground.</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>IV</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Fog! Fog!<br> Lightning! Lightning!<br> Whirlwind! Whirlwind!</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>V</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Whirlwind! Whirlwind!<br> The snowy earth comes gliding, the snowy earth comes<br>     gliding.</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>VI</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">There is dust from the whirlwind,<br> There is dust from the whirlwind,<br> The whirlwind on the mountain.</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>VII</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">The rocks are ringing.<br> The rocks are ringing,<br> They are ringing in the mountains.</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>SONG OF THE PATH OF SOULS</b></p>  
 <p align="center"><b>VIII</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">The snow lies there—ro’rani!<br> The snow lies there—ro’rani!<br> The milky way lies there!</div>  
 <p><a name="page_55"><font size="1" color="green">p. 55</font></a></p>  
 <p align="center"><b>CREATION</b></p>  
 <p align="center"><b>SIOUX</b></p>  
 <p align="center"><b>I</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">This is my work—Yo’yoyo’!<br> All that grows upon the earth is mine—Yo’yoyo’!<br> Says the Father—Yo’yoyo’!<br> E'ya Yo’yoyo’!</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>LAMENT</b></p>  
 <p align="center"><b>II</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Mother, come home; mother, come home.<br> My little brother goes about always crying,<br> My little brother goes about always crying.<br> Mother, come home; mother come home.</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>COURIER CHANT</b></p>  
 <p align="center"><b>III</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">He! They have come back racing,<br> Why, they say there is to be a buffalo hunt over here,<br> Why, they say there is to be a buffalo hunt over here,<br> Make arrows! Make arrows!<br> Says the Father, says the Father.</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>THE MILLENIUM</b></p>  
 <p align="center"><b>IV</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">The whole world is coming,<br> A nation is coming, a nation is coming,<br> The Eagle has brought the message to the tribe.<br> Over the whole earth they are coming;<br> The buffalo are coming, the buffalo are coming,<br> The Crow has brought the message to the tribe.</div>  
 <p><a name="page_56"><font size="1" color="green">p. 56</font></a></p>  
 <p align="center"><b>JUDGMENT</b></p>  
 <p align="center"><b>KIOWA</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">The Father will descend.<br> Everybody will arise.<br> Stretch out your hands.<br> The earth will tremble.</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>VISITATION</b></p>  
 <p align="center"><b>II</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">The spirit army is approaching,<br> The whole world is moving onward.<br> See! Everybody is standing watching.<br> Let us all pray.</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>THE SECRET OF HUMANITY</b></p>  
 <p align="center"><b>III</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Because I am poor,<br> I pray for every living creature.</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>THE GOD-MAN</b></p>  
 <p align="center"><b>IV</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">My Father has had pity on me.<br> I have eyes like my Father's,<br> I have hands like my Father's,<br> I have legs like my Father's,<br> I have a form like my Father's.</div>  
 <p><a name="page_57"><font size="1" color="green">p. 57</font></a></p>  
 <p align="center"><b>THE SPIRIT HUNTERS</b></p>  
 <p align="center"><b>V</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">The spirit host is advancing, they say.<br> They are coming with the buffalo, they say.<br> They are coming with the new earth, they say.</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>MYSTIC</b></p>  
 <p align="center"><b>VI</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">That wind, that wind<br> Shakes my tipi, shakes my tipi,<br> And sings a song for me,<br> And sings a song for me.</div>  
 <p align="center"><b>EXHORTATION</b></p>  
 <p align="center"><b>CADDO</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Come on, Caddo, we are all going up,<br> Come on, Caddo, we are all going up<br> To the great village—He’e’ye’!<br> To the great village—He’e’ye’!<br> With our Father above,<br> With our Father above when he dwells on high—He’e’ye’!<br> Where our Mother dwells—Hhe’e’ye’!<br> Where our Mother dwells—Hhe’e’ye’!</div>
