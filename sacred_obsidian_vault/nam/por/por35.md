 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Path on the Rainbow</i>, edited by George W. Cronyn, [1918], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">KOROSTA KATZINA SONG</h3>  
 <p align="center"><b>HOPI</b></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">    Yellow butterflies,<br> Over the blossoming virgin corn,<br>     With pollen-painted faces<br> Chase one another in brilliant throng.  
 <p>    Blue butterflies,<br> Over the blossoming virgin beans,<br>     With pollen-painted faces<br> Chase one another in brilliant streams.</p>  
 <p>    Over the blossoming corn,<br>     Over the virgin corn<br> Wild bees hum!</p>  
 <p>    Over the blossoming beans,<br>     Over the virgin beans<br> Wild bees hum!</p>  
 <p>Over your field of growing-corn<br>     All day shall hang the thunder-cloud;<br> Over your field of growing corn<br>     All day shall come the rushing rain.</p></div>
