 <h3 align="CENTER">SECTION 57</h3>
 <p>"'Now another message to tell your people.</p>
 <p>"'You may ask three questions concerning three privileges when you go among your relatives at the ceremony of Nîsko'wûknî <a name="fr_64"></a><a href="#fn_64"><font size="1">1</font></a> and ask what one is fitted for them.</p>
 <p>"'Who among you likes best to call upon the afflicted? Who among you loves to commune alone in the forests? Who among you is most anxious concerning religious conditions?''</p>
 <p>So they asked him. Eniaiehuk.</p>
