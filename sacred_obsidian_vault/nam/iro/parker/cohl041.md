 <h3 align="CENTER">SECTION 30</h3>
 <p>"'Now another message to tell your people.</p>
 <p>Four words the Creator has given for bringing happiness. They</p>
 <p><a name="page_41"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 41</font></a></p>
 <p>are amusements devised in the heaven world, the Osto'wägo'wa, <a name="fr_38"></a><a href="#fn_38"><font size="1">1</font></a> Gone:'owo<sup>n</sup>?, Ado<sup>n</sup>'wê<sup>n</sup> and Ganäwê<sup>n</sup>'gowa.'"</p>
 <p>So they said and he said. Eniaiehuk.</p>
