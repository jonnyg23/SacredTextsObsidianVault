 <h3 align="CENTER">SECTION 97</h3>
 <p>So now another.</p>
 <p>"'Verily you have seen the breast of a man hanging here by the road and in the center of that breast you saw a bullet hole. <a name="fr_84"></a><a href="#fn_84"><font size="1">1</font></a> Now we have caused this thing to be placed there. All will see it and he will see it who did the wrong when he comes upon the great road and know that he must turn aside and enter upon a journey over the wide and rough road.'"</p>
 <p>So they said and he said. Eniaiehuk.</p>
