 <h3 align="CENTER">SECTION 109</h3>
 <p>"Now they revealed another.</p>
 <p>"Now the punisher called out in a loud voice and commanded two persons to appear before him. Now when they stood before</p>
 <p><a name="page_74"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 74</font></a></p>
 <p>him he handed them what seemed a pack of red hot iron cards. Then he forced the two to sit down facing each other and compelled them to shuffle the cards and as they did flames spurted out from between them. So they cried out in great agony, sucked their fingers in their mouths, handled the cards again until their flesh was eaten away and the meat fell off. So this is what he saw.</p>
 <p>"Then the messengers said, 'This is the punishment meted out to those who handle cards and repent not.'"</p>
 <p>So they said. Eniaiehuk.</p>
