 <h3 align="CENTER">SECTION 26</h3>
 <p>"'Now another message to tell your relatives.</p>
 <p>"'This concerns education. It is concerning studying in English schools.</p>
 <p>"'Now let the Council appoint twelve people to study, two from each nation of the six. So many white people are about you that you must study to know their ways.'"</p>
 <p>So they said and he said. Eniaiehuk.</p>
