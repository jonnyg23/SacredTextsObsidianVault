 <h3 align="CENTER">SECTION 61.</h3>
 <p>"'So now another.</p>
 <p>"'Now it is understood that Dio`he?'ko<sup>n</sup> (the corn, bean and squash spirits), have a secret medicine, o`sagan'dâ? and o`sdîs'dani. So soak your seed corn in these two medicines before you plant your fields. The medicines grow on the flat lands near streams.'"</p>
 <p>So they said and he said. Eniaiehuk.</p>
