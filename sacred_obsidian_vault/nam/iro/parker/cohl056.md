 <h3 align="CENTER">SECTION 45</h3>
 <p>"'Now another message for your people.</p>
 <p>"'Some of your relatives and descendants will say, "We lack an understanding of this religion," and this will be the cry of the</p>
 <p><a name="page_46"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 46</font></a></p>
 <p>people. But even we, the servants of the Creator, do not understand all things. Now some when they are turned to the right way will say, "I will continue so for all of my days," but this will not be so for they surely will fall short in some things. This is why even we can not understand all things.'"</p>
 <p>So they said. Eniaiehuk.</p>
