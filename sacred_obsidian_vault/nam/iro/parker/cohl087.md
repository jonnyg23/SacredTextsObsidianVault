 <h3 align="CENTER">SECTION 76</h3>
 <p>"Now another message.</p>
 <p>"Now we think that the Creator will stop the earth and heavens. All the powers of nature will he suspend. Now they will see this who refuse to believe in Gai'wiio`."</p>
 <p>So they said and he said. Eniaiehuk.</p>
