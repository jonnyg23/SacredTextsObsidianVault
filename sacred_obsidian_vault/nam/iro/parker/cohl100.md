 <h3 align="CENTER">SECTION 89</h3>
 <p>"So they proceeded a little ways farther and soon they said, 'We will stop here.' Then they pointed out a certain spot and said, 'Watch! Look upon the eastern heavens and observe!'</p>
 <p><a name="page_65"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 65</font></a></p>
 <p>So he looked and saw two immense drops (or balls of liquid) hanging, one red and one yellow. It seemed that they were suspended only for an instant and would momentarily fall.</p>
 <p>"Then the messengers asked, 'What did you see there?'</p>
 <p>"He answered, 'I saw two drops, one red and one yellow, suspended as if about to fall.'</p>
 <p>"Then the messengers replied, 'Truly you have spoken. It is so. Should one of those drops fall it would bring great calamity upon the earth. Many people would leave the earth should one drop but we are doing our utmost to prevent such an event.'"</p>
 <p>So they said and he said. Eniaiehuk.</p>
