 <h3 align="CENTER">SECTION 40</h3>
 <p>"'Now another message to tell your people.</p>
 <p>"'The religious leaders and the chiefs must enforce obedience to the teachings of Gai'wiio`.'"</p>
 <p>So they said and he said. Eniaiehuk.</p>
