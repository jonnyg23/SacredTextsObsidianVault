 <h2 align="CENTER">THE JOURNEY OVER THE GREAT SKY-ROAD</h2>
 <h3 align="CENTER">SECTION 82</h3>
 <p>"'Now another message.</p>
 <p>"'Now it is the time for our departure. We shall now go on a journey and then you shall see the coming of the fourth messenger, the journey of our friends and the works of the living of earth. More, you will see the house of the punisher and the lands of our Creator.'"</p>
 <p>So they said. Eniaiehuk.</p>
