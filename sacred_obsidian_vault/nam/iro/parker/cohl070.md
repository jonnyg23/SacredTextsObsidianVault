 <h3 align="CENTER">SECTION 59</h3>
 <p>"'Now another message.</p>
 <p>"'It is this: We, the messengers of our Creator, see strong drink used during the season when corn is planted. Now let those</p>
 <p><a name="page_54"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 54</font></a></p>
 <p>who use this evil drink know that it consumes the elements of life They must repent.'"</p>
 <p>So they said and he said. Eniaiehuk.</p>
