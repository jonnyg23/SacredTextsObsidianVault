 <h3 align="CENTER">SECTION 58</h3>
 <p>"'Now another message.</p>
 <p>"'Now this matter will devolve upon you.</p>
 <p>"'The people will assemble in council and ask, "Who among us is able to say, 'I compel you to assemble?'"</p>
 <p>"'Now when the question is set forth each person must make reply. The chiefs must demand it.'</p>
 <p>"'Now it happened that he fulfilled the requirements and all the people assembled and with one accord acclaimed that Ganio`dai'io` should lead them and that they should never murmur.</p>
 <p>"'Now that the people had done, he was patient to learn the result.</p>
 <p>"'The council adjourned and the messengers came and questioned him saying, 'How did you understand your people? '</p>
 <p>"'He answered, 'The majority consented that I should lead them.' <a name="fr_65"></a><a href="#fn_65"><font size="1">2</font></a></p>
 <p>"'Then the messengers replied, 'Truly the greater number will follow you."</p>
 <p>So they said and he said. Eniaiehuk.</p>
