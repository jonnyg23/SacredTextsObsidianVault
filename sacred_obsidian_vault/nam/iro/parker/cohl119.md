 <h3 align="CENTER">SECTION 108</h3>
 <p>"Now they showed him another.</p>
 <p>"Now the punisher called out in a loud voice saying, 'My nephew, come hither,' and the man stood before him. 'Now, nephew, play your violin as was once your delight.' The punisher handed the man a bar of hot iron and forced him to rub it upon his arm. So he played and the cords of his arm were the strings of the instrument and made the music. So in great agony he cried and screamed until he fell. <a name="fr_88"></a><a href="#fn_88"><font size="1">1</font></a></p>
 <p>"Then said the four messengers, 'You have seen the punishment of the man who failed to repent.'"</p>
 <p>So they said. Eniaiehuk.</p>
