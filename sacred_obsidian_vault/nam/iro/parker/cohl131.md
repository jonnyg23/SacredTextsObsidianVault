 <h3 align="CENTER">SECTION 120</h3>
 <p>"Now he was at Cornplanter ten years, at Cold Spring two years and at Tonawanda four years. From there he went to Ganonktiyuk'gegäo, Onondaga, and there fell our head man."</p>
