 <h3 align="CENTER">SECTION 111</h3>
 <p>"Then said the messengers, 'We will proceed on our journey. It would be a hard thing should we tarry too long and meet the Creator on the road before we reach his pleasant lands. If we should meet him you should be compelled to stay here forever.'</p>
 <p>So they said. Eniaiehuk.</p>
