 <h3 align="CENTER">SECTION 74</h3>
 <p>"Now another message.</p>
 <p>"In that time every poisonous creature will appear. These creatures the Creator has imprisoned in the underworld and they are the creations of the evil-minded spirit. Now it is our opinion that when they are released many people will be captured and poisoned by them. Men will see these hardships when they fail to believe in Gai'wiio`."</p>
 <p>So they said and he said. Eniaiehuk.</p>
