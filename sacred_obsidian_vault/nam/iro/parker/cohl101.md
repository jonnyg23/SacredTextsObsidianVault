 <h3 align="CENTER">SECTION 90</h3>
 <p>"So they proceeded but had not gone a long distance before they said, 'We will stop and watch a certain place. Now listen to the earth.'</p>
 <p>"So he listened and as well as he could understand he thought that he heard wailing and mourning. The sounds seemed to be the crying of children.</p>
 <p>"Then the messengers asked, 'What did you observe?'</p>
 <p>"He answered, 'I thought that I heard the wailing of the aged and the crying of children.'</p>
 <p>"Then the messengers replied, 'It is true. What you have heard is the substance of life going back to the Creator. When this time comes there will be great misery upon the earth.'</p>
 <p>So they said and he said. Eniaiehuk.</p>
