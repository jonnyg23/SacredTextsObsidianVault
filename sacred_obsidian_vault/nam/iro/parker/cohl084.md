 <h3 align="CENTER">SECTION 73</h3>
 <p>"Now another message.</p>
 <p>"In that time you will hear many rumors of men who say, 'I have spoken with the Creator.' So also will you see many wonders</p>
 <p><a name="page_59"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 59</font></a></p>
 <p>but they will not endure for they will be the work of the evil spirit.</p>
 <p>"Verily we say that there will be none other than you who will receive a message from the Creator through us. This truth will be proclaimed when the end comes."</p>
 <p>So they said and he said. Eniaiehuk.</p>
