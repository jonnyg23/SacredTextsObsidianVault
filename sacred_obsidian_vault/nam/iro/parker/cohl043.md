 <h3 align="CENTER">SECTION 32</h3>
 <p>Now another message for your people.</p>
 <p>He who created us appointed that there should be chiefs, (hodi'ion?), and that they should do good for the people.'"</p>
 <p>So they said and he said. Eniaiehuk.</p>
