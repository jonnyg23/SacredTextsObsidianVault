 <h3 align="CENTER">SECTION 42</h3>
 <p>"'Now another message to tell your people.</p>
 <p><a name="page_45"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 45</font></a></p>
 <p>"'Chiefs and high officers have spoken derisively of each other and quarreled. <a name="fr_46"></a><a href="#fn_46"><font size="1">1</font></a> What they have done must not be done again.'"</p>
 <p>So they said and he said. Eniaiehuk.</p>
