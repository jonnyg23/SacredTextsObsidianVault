 <h3 align="CENTER">SECTION 53</h3>
 <p>"'Now another message.</p>
 <p>"The four messengers arose from a sitting of the prophecy.</p>
 <p>"Now he said that certain songs and parts of songs are not known and some societies are new and their powers untried. So</p>
 <p><a name="page_51"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 51</font></a></p>
 <p>make a feast and throw tobacco instead of singing. But the chiefs said that that plan should be laid aside and notwithstanding, the songs should be sung as far as possible.</p>
 <p>"Now the messengers said that they should secure provisions enough for the feast and be sure. Some have planned to have strong drink used at the feast but this must not be tolerated. Only food must be used.'" <a name="fr_56"></a><a href="#fn_56"><font size="1">1</font></a></p>
 <p>So they said and he said. Eniaiehuk.</p>
