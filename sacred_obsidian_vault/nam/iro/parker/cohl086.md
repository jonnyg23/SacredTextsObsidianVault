 <h3 align="CENTER">SECTION 75</h3>
 <p>"Now another message.</p>
 <p>"Now there will be some who will enter into a sleep. When they lie down they will be in health and as they sleep the Creator will withdraw their lives for they are true. To the faithful this will happen."'</p>
 <p>So they said and he said. Eniaiehuk.</p>
