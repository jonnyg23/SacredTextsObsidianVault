 <h3 align="CENTER">SECTION 69</h3>
 <p>"Now another message.</p>
 <p>"Now it is said that you must relate what the messengers say about the coming end of the earth. Relate how all those who refuse to believe in Gai'wiio` will suffer hardships. <a name="fr_73"></a><a href="#fn_73"><font size="1">1</font></a> Now when the earth is about to end the chiefs and head-men will disagree and that will be a sign. So also, the Honon'diont will disagree. Then will the relations know the truth."</p>
 <p>So they said and he said. Eniaiehuk.</p>
