 <h3 align="CENTER">SECTION 123</h3>
 <p>"Now there will be another and his name will be the New Voice, Hawênose?'.</p>
 <p>"So now it was that Ganio`dai'io` was bidden the third time to sing his song and this the messengers said would be the last.</p>
 <p>"Now then he said, 'There is nothing to incumber me from fulfilling my call."'</p>
 <p>So said our head man. Eniaiehuk.</p>
