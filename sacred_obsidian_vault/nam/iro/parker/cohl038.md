 <h3 align="CENTER">SECTION 27</h3>
 <p>"'Now another message to tell your people.</p>
 <p>"'Now some men have much work and invite all their friends to come and aid them and they do so. Now this is a good plan and the Creator designed it. He ordained that men should help one another <a name="fr_32"></a><a href="#fn_32"><font size="1">1</font></a> (a:danidä'oshä`).'"</p>
 <p>So they said and he said. Eniaiehuk.</p>
