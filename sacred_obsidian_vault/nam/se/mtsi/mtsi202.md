 <h3 align="CENTER">2. THE PLEIADES (18)</h3>
 <p>The Cluster-stars liked to travel about and dance. They were lazy people who wanted to dance and travel about all of the time. When the planting season arrived they planted and cultivated only pole beans. They ate them, but when it began to get hot they disappeared. They are the Cluster-stars.</p>
