 <h3 align="CENTER">50. THE CONCEIT OF TCIKTCINIGÅSI</h3>
 <p>The Tciktcinigåsi (wren?) stood under a log close to the ground and said, "Would not you say that I am very tall? When I rise on my toes I strike my head against the sky."</p>
