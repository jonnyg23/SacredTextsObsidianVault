 <h3 align="CENTER">84. NOTE ON KILLING EAGLES.</h3>
 <font face="Times Roman,Times New Roman"><p>If one does not know how he does not touch them. He will get sick. His arms and legs will draw up. He can not walk and it causes his bones to ache.</p>
