 <h3 align="CENTER">THE WOLVES AND THE DEER</h3>
 <p>The Wolves and the Deer have a feast. They play laughing at each other. The Wolves laugh first. The Deer fear the large teeth of the Wolves. The Deer are told to laugh aloud. When the Wolves see that the Deer have no teeth, they devour them.</p>
