 <h3 align="CENTER">103. THE WAR IN THE SPRUCE CANOE <a name="fr_156"></a><a href="#fn_156"><font size="1">a</font></a></h3>
 <p>People went to war from Chilkat in a spruce canoe. They drifted down on the people below, and they came to Dî'<u>g</u>âtiyA. Then they defeated the Stikine people completely, and afterward they made peace together.</p>
