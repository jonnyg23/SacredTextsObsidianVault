 <h3 align="CENTER">Transformation of the Man with many Mouths. <a name="fr_19"></a><a href="#fn_19"><font size="1">2</font></a></h3>
 <p>Then Q!â'nêqi?la<u>x</u><sup>u</sup> started. He saw people laughing. Then he became afraid. Behold! he discovered Mouth-Body, who was this way. Behold! he had many mouths. Q!â'nêqi?la<u>x</u><sup>u</sup> started. Is that the way you are?" Then he said to him, "That is the way we are."--"Don't be that way in later generations." Then he said, "Come to me, that I may set you right, else you might continue this way in later generations." Then (Mouth-Body) came</p>
 <p>
 </p>
 <p><a name="page_207"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 207</font></a></p>
 <p>to him, and he stroked with his hand over his body, over his past mouths, and he had only one mouth. Then (Mouth-Body) spoke. "You have taken pity on me."</p>
