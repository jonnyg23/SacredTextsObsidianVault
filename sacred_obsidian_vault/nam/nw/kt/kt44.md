 <h3 align="CENTER">Q!â'nêqi?la<u>x</u><sup>u</sup> meets Ô'?mâ<strike>l</strike>. <a name="fr_21"></a><a href="#fn_21"><font size="1">2</font></a></h3>
 <p>Q!â'nêqi?la<u>x</u><sup>u</sup> started and saw Ô'?mâ<strike>l</strike>. Then Ô'?mâ<strike>l</strike> pointed at Q!â'nêqi?la<u>x</u><sup>u</sup>. Then he pointed at the forehead of Q!â'nêqi?la<u>x</u><sup>u</sup>, and there was a hole in the forehead of Q!â'nêqi?la<u>x</u><sup>u</sup>. Then he took revenge, and pointed from a long ways off. Truly, they were supernatural beings. Q!â'nêqi?la<u>x</u><sup>u</sup> and Ô'?mâ<strike>l</strike> were equal.</p>
