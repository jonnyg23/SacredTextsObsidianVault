 <h3 align="CENTER">Origin of the Mink. <a name="fr_15"></a><a href="#fn_15"><font size="1">1</font></a></h3>
 <p>Q!â'nêqi?la<u>x</u><sup>u</sup> started, and saw the Mink sharpening (something). "What is that?"--"This will be for taking revenge." "Give it to me, that I may see it!"--"This is nice. Turn your back to me." Then he put it on his back. "Go start! You shall be the mink of later man."</p>
