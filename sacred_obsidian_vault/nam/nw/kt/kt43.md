 <h3 align="CENTER">Q!â'nêqi?la<u>x</u><sup>u</sup> meets Oldest-One-in-the-World. <a name="fr_20"></a><a href="#fn_20"><font size="1">1</font></a></h3>
 <p>Q!â'nêqi?la<u>x</u><sup>u</sup> started and came to Oldest-One-in-the-World. Oldest-One-in -the- World went down to the beach and stood on the beach in the sea. "I [you] am going to die here," said Oldest-One-in-the-World. Then he was turned into stone by Q!â'nêqi?la<u>x</u><sup>u</sup>, who was afraid of <strike>L</strike>Exyâ'lik*ilayô.</p>
