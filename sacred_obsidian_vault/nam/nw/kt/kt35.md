 <h3 align="CENTER">Q!â'nêqi?la<u>x</u><sup>u</sup> meets Shaman. <a name="fr_12"></a><a href="#fn_12"><font size="1">1</font></a></h3>
 <p>Q!â'nêqi?la<u>x</u><sup>u</sup> started. He saw Shaman, and he saw his fire. The fire was in the woods. He saw his ring of red cedar-bark, and he saw his wood-worms. Q!â'nêqi?la<u>x</u><sup>u</sup> was afraid, and he went past behind him.</p>
