 <font face="Times Roman,Times New Roman"><p>Êya hâ hiê', etc.<br>
 Qâ'gai<u>g</u>aña nâ'<u>g</u>a L!ao Sk<u>î</u>'ls<u>î</u>s xê'gAndigi wa'di kuñg<u>î</u>ña'ñ uga'-i.<br>
 Uncles | houses in | but | Sk<u>î</u>'ls<u>î</u>s | makes a noise (potlatch) | for it | (he or she) is crying.<br>
 Wa'di kû'djiwa ê'ya hâ hîê!, etc.<br>
 For it | sits greatly.</p>
 <p>Êya hâ hiê', etc.<br>
 But he (or she) is crying for the noise Sk<u>î</u>'ls<u>î</u>s (now reborn) makes in his uncles' houses (at the potlatch).<br>
 For it he sits greatly.</p>
