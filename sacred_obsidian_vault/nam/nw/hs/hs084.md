 <h3 align="CENTER">82.</h3>
 <font face="Times Roman,Times New Roman"><p>A LAga-i yuku'ndjudasi î'ñgut s<u>g</u>â'nas û dalA'ñ y<u>ê</u>'dada-dîgînî.<br>
 This | land is a point (Rose Spit) | on | supernatural beings | those | you | left.</p>
 <p>Supernatural beings used to leave you on this point of land (i. e., Rose Spit).</p>
