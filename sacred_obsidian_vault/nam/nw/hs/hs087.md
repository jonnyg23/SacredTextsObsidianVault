 <h3 align="CENTER">85.</h3>
 <font face="Times Roman,Times New Roman"><p>Gû'gus t!ao dAñ sû'kudjiwañ gia'ga t!a'gwa?<br>
 What | for | you | are crying | things (clothing) | for?<br>
 WAsk!iên a'ña dAñ giA'nda kudjû'asañ.<br>
 But | yours | you | shall wear | chief (or "dear").</p>
 <p>For what do you cry, chief (or dear)? For clothing?<br>
 You shall wear it, chief (or dear).</p>
