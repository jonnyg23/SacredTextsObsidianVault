 <h3 align="CENTER">94. <a name="fr_92"></a><a href="#fn_86"><font size="1">2</font></a></h3>
 <font face="Times Roman,Times New Roman"><p>K!iwa'-i L! nAñ qä'ñAs <u>L</u>û gwî nAñ qa'-idañ.<br>
 The trail (of the dead) | but | one | could see | if | upon | one (= I) | could go,<br>
 Gwai'yê dAñ â'<strike>l</strike>djiwa-i.<br>
 Elder brother, | your | whole body.</p>
 <p>If I could see the trail (of the dead), I would enter upon it.<br>
 Elder brother, (I want to see) your whole body.</p>
