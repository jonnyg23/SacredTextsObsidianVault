 <h3 align="CENTER">84.</h3>
 <font face="Times Roman,Times New Roman"><p>Y<u>ê</u>n dAñ <u>î</u>'skudals <u>L</u>û gAm <strike>l</strike> dî'gu ku'ñgîñañAñ hAñ.<br>
 Truly | you are chief (or "dear") if not you on my (imp.) (knees) cry!<br>
 Dî gwa ga lgaiqendigwA'ñus.<br>
 I | am not rich.</p>
 <p>If you are truly a chief (reborn), do not cry upon my knees!<br>
 I am not rich. <a name="fr_85"></a><a href="#fn_79"><font size="1">1</font></a></p>
