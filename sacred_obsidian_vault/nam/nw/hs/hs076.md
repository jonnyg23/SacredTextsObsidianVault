 <h3 align="CENTER">74.</h3>
 <font face="Times Roman,Times New Roman"><p>Dja'+djâts L!aha'o lâ, dja'+djâts L!aha'o lâ.<br>
 Women are better (than men), women are better (than men).<br>
 Dja'+djâts L!a kîñgê'diasLa.<br>
 Women have more property.</p>
 <p><a name="page_50"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 50</font></a></p>
 <p>KîlsLa-i'<u>g</u>an k!îsLa'-i dAñ <u>g</u>ê'tgwañ axAn?<br>
 My chiefs men of one's own family) | chiefs | you | are | where?</p>
 <p>Women are better (than men), women are better (than men).<br>
 Women have more property.<br>
 Chiefs of my family, where are you?</p>
