 <h3 align="CENTER">Battle-Song of Women.</h3>
 <h3 align="CENTER">103.</h3>
 <font face="Times Roman,Times New Roman"><p align="CENTER">[Song by women during the absence of their husbands with a war-party.]</p>
 <p>Sk!A'<u>g</u>a-o t!a'êt qô'nagAñ.<br>
 Sk!A'<u>g</u>a, | this one | killed | many people.</p>
 <p>Sk!A'<u>g</u>ao killed (and enslaved) many people.</p>
