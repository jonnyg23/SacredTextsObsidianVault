 <h3 align="CENTER">21. <a name="fr_26"></a><a href="#fn_22"><font size="1">1</font></a></h3>
 <font face="Times Roman,Times New Roman"><p>Ha LA ha La+ ha Lê'+, ha LA ha La+ ha Lê+.<br>
 (Laughter)<br>
 Â'ga <strike>l</strike><u>g</u><strike>l</strike>ga'odî<u>g</u>An s<u>g</u>oa'na gwê+,<br>
 Here is where | black ground used to be | one | [of them],<br>
 Ha LA ha La+ ha Lê'+, ha LA ha La+ ha Lê+.<br>
 (Laughter)<br>
 A gâ'ldjidAs <a name="fr_27"></a><a href="#fn_23"><font size="1">2</font></a> gâ'ñañ. A gâ'ldjidAs gâ'ñañ.<br>
 Crow | like a, | crow | like a.</p>
 <p>Ha LA ha La+ ha Lê'+, ha LA ha La+ ha Lê+.<br>
 Here is where one of the black tattoo-marks used to be, <a name="fr_28"></a><a href="#fn_24"><font size="1">3</font></a><br>
 Ha LA ha La+ ha Lê'+, ha LA ha La+ ha Lê+.<br>
 (Black) just like a crow, (black) just like a crow.</p>
