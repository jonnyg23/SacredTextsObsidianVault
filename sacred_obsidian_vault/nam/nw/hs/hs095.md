 <h3 align="CENTER">93. <a name="fr_91"></a><a href="#fn_85"><font size="1">1</font></a></h3>
 <font face="Times Roman,Times New Roman"><p>Qo-ês LL<u>î</u>'ñalAñ <sup>?</sup>a-i <strike>l</strike> kusî't!A<strike>l</strike>.<br>
 Clouds | open with your hands | through, | look down.<br>
 DAñ <strike>l</strike>û'y<u>î</u>ña-i dAñ xA'ñ<u>î</u>ña-i.<br>
 Your | body all | your | face all (we wish to see).</p>
 <p>Parting the clouds with your hands, look down (from Tâ'xet's house).<br>
 We wish to see your body and your face.</p>
