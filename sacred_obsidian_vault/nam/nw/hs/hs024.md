 <h3 align="CENTER">Q!ô'na qê'<u>g</u>awa-i.</h3>
 <h3 align="CENTER">22.</h3>
 <font face="Times Roman,Times New Roman"><p>Hao tcî'nAñ lanâ'<u>g</u>a gua dâ dâ'<u>g</u>a.<br>
 Your grandfather's | town | ? | you | own.<br>
 <u>G</u>a'<u>g</u>odiya-i gua s<u>g</u>â'naga, <u>G</u>a'<u>g</u>odiya-i gua s<u>g</u>â'naga.<br>
 Lies | large | ? | is it powerful, Lies | large | ? | is it powerful.<br>
 Hao tcî'nAñ lanâ'<u>g</u>a gua dâ dâ'<u>g</u>a.<br>
 Your grandfather's | town | ? | you | own.<br>
 Qî'ngodiya-i gua s<u>g</u>â'naga, Qî'ngodiya-i gua s<u>g</u>â'naga.<br>
 Lies down greatly | ? | is it powerful, Lies down greatly | ? | is it powerful.<br>
 Hao tcî'nAñ <u>x</u>a<strike>l</strike> tc!â'anu<u>g</u>a dâ dâ'<u>g</u>a uyâ'te gâ'djuyañ.<br>
 Your grand-father's | copper | fire | you | own | you only are fit | to sit | greatly.</p>
 <p>Do you own your grandfather's town ?<br>
 Lying large, has it supernatural power? Lying large, has it supernatural power'<br>
 Do you own your grandfather's town?<br>
 Lying greatly, has it supernatural power? Lying greatly, has it supernatural power?<br>
 You only are fit, sitting greatly, to own your grandfather's copper-fire.</p>
