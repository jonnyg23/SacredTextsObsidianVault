 <h3 align="CENTER">Stasa'os qê'<u>g</u>awa-i.</h3>
 <h3 align="CENTER">43.</h3>
 <font face="Times Roman,Times New Roman"><p>Wa'<u>L</u>u dî'nAñ gîda' kû<u>x</u>iâ'ñgwansî' <u>L</u>u g<u>î</u>ñxAn sî'<strike>l</strike><u>g</u>et a'ñ<u>g</u>a<br>
 At that time | my child | youth | goes around as a | when | for nothing | alone | my own<br>
     <strike>l</strike>a q!â'-ugwañ.<br>
     I sit -around.</p>
 <p>When my child becomes a youth, I shall vainly sit around alone (for he will go to live with his uncle).</p>
