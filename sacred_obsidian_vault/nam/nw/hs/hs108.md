 <h3 align="CENTER">Songs of Derision.</h3>
 <font face="Times Roman,Times New Roman"><p>[Songs of this kind were largely in Tsimshian. The following is a translation of one of them, used in making fun of a poor man who pretended that he was a chief.]</p>
 </font><h3 align="CENTER">106.</h3>
 <font face="Times Roman,Times New Roman"><p>Laugh at the chief! for, although he is a chief, he has no rattle in his hand.</p></font>
