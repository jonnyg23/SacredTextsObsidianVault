 <h1 align="CENTER">I.--CRADLE-SONGS.</h1>
 <font face="Times Roman,Times New Roman"><p align="CENTER">(Skidegate Dialect.)</p>
 </font><h3 align="CENTER">Xâ'gi lâ'nas.</h3>
 <h3 align="CENTER">1.</h3>
 <font face="Times Roman,Times New Roman"><p>L'djâ'ada kûdju's <u>L</u>û Q!ô'na l'nagâ'-i ya'kA<strike>l</strike>sî'<u>g</u>a L <u>g</u>aya'o<u>g</u>a<br>
 A woman | it was | when | Skedans | the town of | middle in | its | smoke<br>
 s<u>g</u>aqô'n<u>g</u>a-<strike>l</strike><u>î</u>ña's <u>L</u>û'hao L djâ'ada<u>g</u>a-i!<br>
 large | may be | when | what (kind of a) woman (are you)!</p>
 <p>L'djâ'ada kûdjû'sgu <strike>L</strike><u>g</u>a-i <u>g</u>A'n<u>L</u>a-i ge'istA L! sî'qîgAñ[ga]<br>
 A woman | was there | Skedans | Creek | out of | they | make the noise<br>
 qa-i<u>x</u>una'ñ-<strike>l</strike><u>î</u>ña's <u>L</u>û'hao L djâadâ'ga!<br>
 of singing continually | may (not) be | when | what | (kind of a) woman are (you)!</p>
 <p>You need not think that the smoke of your house in the middle of Skedans will be as great as when you were a woman (in your previous life upon earth <a name="fr_0"></a><a href="#fn_0"><font size="1">1</font></a>).</p>
 <p>You need not think that they will make such a continual noise of singing in Skedans Creek as they used to when you were a woman (in your previous existence).</p>
