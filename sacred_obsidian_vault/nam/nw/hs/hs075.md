 <h3 align="CENTER">Nâ'-ikun qê'<u>g</u>awa-i.</h3>
 <h3 align="CENTER">73.</h3>
 <font face="Times Roman,Times New Roman"><p>Î'dj<u>î</u>sigwA'ns gî dî'nAñ gwa'wañ sû'wasi.<br>
 Not a common woman at all | my child | he refuses | says.<br>
 Wa'gî dî'+nAñ gwa'wañ nAñ sû'wasi.<br>
 To it | my child | refuses, | some one | says.<br>
 A'ñ<u>g</u>a xA'nhao înasû'wa g<u>î</u>t<u>î</u>'n-djats xAn A'ñ<u>g</u>a ina'suwê+,<br>
 His own | just there | wants that one | Eagle-woman | yet | his | says he wants that one,<br>
 A'ñ<u>g</u>a xA'nhao înasî'ñgwañ.<br>
 His own | just there | he will marry.</p>
 <p>Even a noble woman my child says he refuses.<br>
 One says he refuses her.<br>
 He wants just that Eagle woman for himself (indicating a particular one),<br>
 Just that one he will marry.</p>
