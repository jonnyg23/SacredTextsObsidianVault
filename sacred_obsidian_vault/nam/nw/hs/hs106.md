 <h3 align="CENTER">Song used by All Families in making Peace</h3>
 <h3 align="CENTER">(Ga lâ' s?a'lAña-i).</h3>
 <h3 align="CENTER">104.</h3>
 <font face="Times Roman,Times New Roman"><p>Yê<strike>l</strike> dî tâda's <u>L</u>û q!e'nañ dî u'nsAtsgaiya'ndô.<br>
 Raven | me | ate | if | myself | I | would not know.<br>
 Ao L<u>î</u>sînôt dAga'ñ <strike>l</strike> sû'g.<br>
 Now | first time | for myself | I | am singing.</p>
 <p>If Raven had eaten me, <a name="fr_101"></a><a href="#fn_95"><font size="1">1</font></a> I would not know myself.<br>
 Now for the first time I am singing to myself.</p>
