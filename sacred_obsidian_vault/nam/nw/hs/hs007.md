 <h3 align="CENTER">5.</h3>
 <font face="Times Roman,Times New Roman"><p>Hao gî'na gA+n dAñ îdjagâ'djî'was ê'dji.<br>
 This | thing | for | you | sitting as a boy | are.<br>
 Hao gî'na gA+n dAñ îdjagâ'djî'was ê'dji.<br>
 This | thing | for | you | sitting as a boy | are.<br>
 NAñk<u>î</u>'lSLas agA'ñ <u>î</u>'ndaLxagâ'<u>g</u>Anî.<br>
 NAñk<u>î</u>'lSLas | himself | made a human being.<br>
 Skîls na<u>g</u>â'<u>g</u>a kûskî'ndias ê'dji, wA'stA Q!aku'ngwi<br>
 Property in the house | was, | from it | Rose Spit towards<br>
     <u>g</u>a-i<strike>l</strike>gaga'ñ dA'ña<strike>l</strike> Ldjûdal.<br>
     his flood | with | tidal wave went.<br>
 Gwa-isku'n xâ'-idA<u>g</u>a-i xA'nhao dAñ nâ'<u>g</u>a <strike>l</strike>kiä'si<u>g</u>ei<br>
 North Island | people | even | your | house | towards the door<br>
     gut gunL!gA'ndias ê'djî.<br>
     are as many as when waves meet each other and are packed close together.<br>
 Hao gî'na <u>g</u>A+n dAñ îdjagâ'djî'was ê'dji.<br>
 This | thing | for | you | sitting as a boy | are.</p>
 <p>This is why you are a boy<br>
 This is why you are a boy<br>
 NAñk<u>î</u>'lSLas has become a human being.<br>
 From the property in his house a flood went towards Rose Spit.<br>
 Even from North Island the people are crowded into your house, as when waves meet and are packed together.<br>
 That is why you are a boy. <a name="fr_5"></a><a href="#fn_5"><font size="1">1</font></a></p>
