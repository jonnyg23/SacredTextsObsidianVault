 <h3 align="CENTER">T!ô'<strike>l</strike>k!a g<u>î</u>tAnâ'-i.</h3>
 <h3 align="CENTER">98. <a name="fr_95"></a><a href="#fn_89"><font size="1">2</font></a></h3>
 <font face="Times Roman,Times New Roman"><p>LA'gas dAñ qä'ñ kû'gits <u>L</u>û agA'ñ <strike>l</strike> kû stAñ g<u>î</u>'ndagiâ'ñaxAñ.<br>
 Land | you | see, | beloved one | when | yourself, | dear (or chief), | two | if you made in canoe.</p>
 <p>If you had seen land, beloved, you would have saved yourself, dear. <a name="fr_96"></a><a href="#fn_90"><font size="1">3</font></a></p>
