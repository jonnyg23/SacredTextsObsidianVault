 <h3 align="CENTER">59.</h3>
 <font face="Times Roman,Times New Roman"><p>L<u>î</u>'ñgua xAldâ'ñgAñ q!ô'<strike>l</strike><u>g</u>et [ahîyîa]<br>
 Why is it | your slave | close by,<br>
 Hao dâ <u>î</u>lgîyâ'gAñAñ dâ sûkû'djiwañ?<br>
      You | want something you cannot get, | you | say?<br>
 DAñ qô'naga kûdjû'<u>g</u>eda qâñ <strike>l</strike> qê'<u>g</u>An.<br>
 You | are too foolish, | my uncle I | bore.</p>
 <p>Why do you cry for something that you cannot get,<br>
 Sitting close by your slave? <a name="fr_66"></a><a href="#fn_61"><font size="1">2</font></a><br>
 You are very foolish, uncle I bore.</p>
