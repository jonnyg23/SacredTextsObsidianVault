 <h3 align="CENTER">67.</h3>

 Yâ'ña, yâ'ña, k<u>î</u>lsLa'-i<u>g</u>an. Yâ'ña, yâ'ña, k<u>î</u>'ñ<u>g</u>et<u>g</u>an.<br>
 Be careful, | be careful, | my chief! | Be careful, | be careful, | my master!</p>
 <p>My child is a boy because he is going to do as his grandfather did when one went to his place long ago.<br>
 After he had been there, his canoe was so deeply laden (with gifts), that they had to put the weather-boards on it (to increase its capacity);<br>
 For it my child is going to be a leader.<br>
 Be careful, be careful, my chief! Be careful, be careful, my master!</p>
