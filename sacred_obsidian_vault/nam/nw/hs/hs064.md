 <h3 align="CENTER">62.</h3>
 <font face="Times Roman,Times New Roman"><p>Ayî'hiya, ayî'hiya (many times).<br>
 Dâ <u>î</u>s<u>î</u>'ñ gut lâ'<u>g</u>aasañ <strike>L</strike><u>g</u>ai-û' l'nagâ'-i qâñ <strike>l</strike> qê'gAn.<br>
 You | too | will be happy there | Skidegate | town, | my uncle I | bore.<br>
 Ayî'hiya, ayî'hiya (many times).</p>
 <p>Ayî'hiya, ayî'hiya, etc.<br>
 You will again be happy there in Skidegate town, <a name="fr_70"></a><a href="#fn_65"><font size="1">3</font></a> uncle I bore.<br>
 Ayî'hiya, ayî'hiya, etc.</p>
