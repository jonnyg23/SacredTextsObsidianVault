 <h3 align="CENTER">51.</h3>
 <font face="Times Roman,Times New Roman"><p>Ayâ'ña î'ldjao yaña <u>x</u>î'lsîs gañâ'ñ gu't<u>g</u>ei dalA'ñ <u>xî</u>'ldj<u>î</u><br>
 Be careful, | noble men | mine, | leaves | like to one another | you are going<br>
     q<u>ê</u>'ndaldiasga.<br>
     to grow.<br>
 Ayâ'ña kî'lsLa-i <u>g</u>â'ña, ayâ'ña kî'lsLa-i <u>g</u>â'ña.<br>
 Be careful, | chief | mine! | Be careful, | chief | mine!</p>
