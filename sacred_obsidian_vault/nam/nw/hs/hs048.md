 <h3 align="CENTER">Sqoâ'<strike>l</strike>adas.</h3>
 <h3 align="CENTER">46.</h3>
 <font face="Times Roman,Times New Roman"><p>WA'<u>g</u>a xê'gañ qeñg<u>î</u>'ndala-i (four times)<br>
 His (son or daughter) making a great noise went by on the water.<br>
 Agua ga'-idjî<u>x</u>uihao.<br>
 I wonder which way he is going (i. e., the child)!<br>
 WA'<u>g</u>a xê'gañ qeñg<u>î</u>'ndala-i<br>
 His (son or daughter) making a great noise went by on the water.<br>
 A'gua gwaisku'ngwi.<br>
 It must be towards the north point of the islands.</p>
 <p>His great son (the child) went by upon the water with a great noise.<br>
 I wonder whither he is going!<br>
 His great son went by upon the water with a great noise.<br>
 Perhaps to North Island (to invite the people to a potlatch).</p>
