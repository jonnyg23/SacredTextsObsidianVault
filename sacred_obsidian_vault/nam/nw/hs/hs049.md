 <h3 align="CENTER">47.</h3>
 <font face="Times Roman,Times New Roman"><p>Hî hîyaihîya gwa-i kî'lsLa-i hao ahai'ya aya q!ai'g<u>î</u>ndalAñ<br>
               Island | chief | this |              was coming<br>
     <strike>l</strike> gûdA'ñ hao îhî ahaiya<br>
     I thought, but<br>
 K<u>î</u>lsLa'-is Lua'-i L!a q<u>ê</u>ng<u>î</u>ndala-i, hî hîyai hîya.<br>
 Chief's | canoe, | however, | comes greatly.</p>
 <p>I thought the island chief <a name="fr_55"></a><a href="#fn_50"><font size="1">1</font></a> was coming,<br>
