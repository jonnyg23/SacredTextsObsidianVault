 <h3 align="CENTER">63.</h3>
 <font face="Times Roman,Times New Roman"><p>I hî'hîa.<br>
 Gû'sgihao dâ kungiñâ'ñwañ?<br>
 What | you | are crying for?<br>
 Xâ'na-qa'<strike>l</strike>i gî gua dâ ku'ñgîñañ?<br>
 Skidegate Inlet | for | ? | you | cry?<br>
 <u>Î</u>'s<u>î</u>ñ sta dAñ kî'iñawasañ.<br>
 Again | from | you | will have news sent around about you.</p>
 <p>I hî'hîa.<br>
 What are you crying for?<br>
 Do you cry for Skidegate Inlet?<br>
 Again from there you will have news spread around about you (when your husband puts up a house-pole).</p>
