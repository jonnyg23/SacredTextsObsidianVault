 <h3 align="CENTER">76.</h3>
 <font face="Times Roman,Times New Roman"><p>Gîsta L!ao da<u>g</u>a'sado sî'liya dâ skA'ndAñ kudjû'gi[gê+]<br>
 Who | but | will own it | after it | you | are crying | are sitting<br>
 dô'nê a<strike>l</strike>djî'wai? <a name="fr_82"></a><a href="#fn_76"><font size="1">2</font></a><br>
 younger brother | are sitting?</p>
 <p>But do you sit crying over who will afterwards own it, younger brother of good family?</p>
