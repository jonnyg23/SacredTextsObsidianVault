 <p>14.</p>
 <p>Hao dalA'ñ s<u>g</u>A'nxAnhao ya'tê gadA'ldi<u>g</u>anê q!o'ldjat, hao<br>
 You | only ones | were brought up well | chief women,<br>
 dalA'ñ s<u>g</u>A'nxAnhao ya'tê gadA'ldi<u>g</u>anê q!o'ldjat,<br>
 You | only ones | were brought up well | chief women,<br>
 Q!ô'na-kun <u>g</u>adô' ga <u>L</u>nda'lL!xaga-iyu.<br>
 Skedans point | around | sit in his canoe and come with him.<br>
 Hao dalA'ñ s<u>g</u>A'nxAnhao, etc. (four times).<br>
 You | only ones , etc.<br>
 Tcî'nañ qô'naiya-i gwai'<u>g</u>a+gut a<strike>l</strike> dalA'ñ dAñq!â'-isgidan sû.<br>
 Your grand-father | powerful | his islands together | you | pulled | it is said.<br>
 A'ñga gî A'ñ<u>g</u>axawâ'yu.<br>
 Theirs | to | how they let with it.</p>
 <p><a name="page_14"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 14</font></a></p>
 <p>Hao dalA'ñ s<u>g</u>A'nxAnhao, etc. (four times)<br>
 You | only ones, | etc.<br>
 Q!êdAs k!ia'o<u>g</u>a gi <u>g</u>axa-ûxansLiya'-i yu. <a name="fr_19"></a><a href="#fn_15"><font size="1">1</font></a><br>
 Tattoo | for | sit down (or take the position).</p>
 <p>You, chief women, are the only ones brought up well enough; you, chief women, are the only ones brought up well enough,<br>
 To sit in (the chief's) canoe and come around Skedans point with him.<br>
 You, chief women, are the only ones, etc. (four times),<br>
 To pull your powerful grandfather's islands <a name="fr_20"></a><a href="#fn_16"><font size="1">2</font></a> together, they say.<br>
 You, chief women, are the only ones, etc. (four times),<br>
 To sit down to receive tattoo-marks.</p>
