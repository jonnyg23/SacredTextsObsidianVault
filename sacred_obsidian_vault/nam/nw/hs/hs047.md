 <h3 align="CENTER">45.</h3>
 <font face="Times Roman,Times New Roman"><p>DAñ s<u>g</u>u'nxA'n gua djâ'ada kudjû'diawîs,<br>
 You | are not the only one | woman | we who belong to a low family,<br>
 DAñ s<u>g</u>u'nxA'n gua djâ'ada kudjû'diawîs,<br>
 You | are not the only one | woman | we who belong to a low family,<br>
 Djia'djats hao qoa'nga,<br>
 Women | are plenty,<br>
 Djia'djats hao qoan kuha'oga.<br>
 Women | plenty | belonging to a low class.</p>
 <p>You are not the only woman of our low-class family,<br>
 You are not the only woman of our low-class family.<br>
 There are plenty of women,<br>
 There are plenty of low-class women. <a name="fr_54"></a><a href="#fn_49"><font size="1">1</font></a></p>
