 <h3 align="CENTER">31.</h3>
 <font face="Times Roman,Times New Roman"><p>Â'gua k<u>î</u>lsLa'-i agA'ñ inâ'sLda qîn<strike>l</strike>gAlûgA'n hao hao.<br>

 DAñ k<u>î</u>'ñ<u>g</u>at ugua', dAñ k<u>î</u>'ñ<u>g</u>at ugua'.<br>

 DAñ sLû'<strike>l</strike>gA<strike>l</strike>wAñ gua'. DAñ+ qî'ñ<strike>l</strike>gA<strike>l</strike>wAñ gua'.<br>

 <p>Here the chief causes himself to grow up greatly as he sits.<br>
 You are becoming great, you are becoming great.<br>
 You are moving so greatly as you sit. You are moving highly as you sit.<br>
 You are becoming great, you are becoming great.<br>
 You are moving so greatly as you sit. You are moving highly as you sit.<br>
 You are moving so greatly as you sit.</p>
