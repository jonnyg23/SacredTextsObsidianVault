 <h3 align="CENTER">Qâ'-ia<strike>l</strike> lâ'nas.</h3>
 <font face="Times Roman,Times New Roman"><p align="CENTER">(Songs of Qâ-i l'nagâ'-i.)</p>
 </font><h3 align="CENTER">65.</h3>
 <font face="Times Roman,Times New Roman"><p>Qâ-i dî'txa+ <strike>l</strike> qî'ñgwañ qa'odî+ kî'lsLa-i <strike>l</strike>îñ <strike>l</strike> qê'xa<br>
 Sea-Lion (Town) | behind | I | was looking around | a while, | chief | is going to be | I | found,<br>
     kA'n<u>x</u>ida-i+.<br>
     boy just big enough to walk.<br>
 Aiyâ'ña, aiyâ'ña, kî'lsLa-igan.<br>
 Take care, | take care, | my own chief!<br>
 Aiyâ'ña qî'ñgêt<u>g</u>añ.<br>
 Take care, | my own master (or chief)!</p>
 <p>While I was looking around behind Sea-Lion Town, I found the future chief, a boy just big enough to walk.<br>
 Take care, take care, my own chief!<br>
 Take care, my own master!</p>
