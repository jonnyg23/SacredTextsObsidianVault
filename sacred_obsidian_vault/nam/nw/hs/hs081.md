 <h3 align="CENTER">79.</h3>
 <font face="Times Roman,Times New Roman"><p>Tcî'nAñ lû'<u>g</u>a giû'gulaga, tcî'nAñ lû'<u>g</u>a giû'gulaga.<br>
 Grand-father's | wave | listens for, | grand-father's | wave | listens for.<br>
 StA L!ao tcînA'ñ sî'<u>g</u>a gut kû'djûgia<u>g</u>andalanê.<br>
 After it | but | grand-father's | sea | upon | goes along stopping often on the way (upon the water).</p>
 <p><a name="page_52"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 52</font></a></p>
 <p>(He) listens for grandfather's <a name="fr_83"></a><a href="#fn_77"><font size="1">1</font></a> wave, he listens for grandfather's wave;<br>
 But afterwards he goes along upon grandfather's sea, stopping every now and then on the way.</p>
