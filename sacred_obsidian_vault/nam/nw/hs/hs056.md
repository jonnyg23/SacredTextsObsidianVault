 <h3 align="CENTER">54.</h3>
 <font face="Times Roman,Times New Roman"><p>Gûgu's gi la g<u>î</u>tg<u>î</u>ñâ'+ñ?<br>
 What | for | he (or she) | is crying like a noble's son [g<u>î</u>t]?<br>
 Gûgu's gi la ga<strike>l</strike>ga<strike>l</strike>wa'ñ?<br>
 What | for | he | moves around?<br>
 Atcî'na-i nâ'ga-i gî la kung<u>î</u>ña'ñ <a name="fr_60"></a><a href="#fn_55"><font size="1">1</font></a> <u>g</u>a<strike>l</strike>ga<strike>l</strike>wa's ahî'gua, etc.<br>
 Grandfather | the house | for | he | is crying | moves about as he is seated.</p>
 <p>Why does he cry as a noble cries (i.e., softly)?<br>
 Why does he move around as he sits?<br>
 He moves around and cries for grandfather's house.</p>
