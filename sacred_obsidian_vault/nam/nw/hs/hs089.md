 <h3 align="CENTER">Skî'daoqao.</h3>
 <h3 align="CENTER">87. <a name="fr_86"></a><a href="#fn_80"><font size="1">1</font></a></h3>
 <font face="Times Roman,Times New Roman"><p>DAñ tcîn <strike>l</strike>k!iä'nao dAn dA qä'tcû kû'udAñ.<br>
 Your | grand-father | by stick (cedar trunk used for canoe) | you | for | is looking | chief's son (or "dear").<br>
 <u>G</u>A'i<strike>l</strike><sup>?</sup>Añ kû's<u>Lî</u>gA<strike>l</strike>.<br>
 To yourself | go straight up.</p>
 <p>Your grandfather's canoe is looking for you, dear.<br>
 Go straight up to it.</p>
