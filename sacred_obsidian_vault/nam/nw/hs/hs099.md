 <h3 align="CENTER">Tâs lâ'nas.</h3>
 <h3 align="CENTER">97.</h3>
 <font face="Times Roman,Times New Roman"><p>[This was composed by Qadjiqô'k<sup>u</sup> when his niece was drowned in Q!a'nAn River, and her body could not be found.]</p>
 <p>Gû'stas, gî <strike>l</strike> da'-indAgwAñgAñ?<br>
 What | for | I | poor one | searching?<br>
 Hâ'djadia nâ'da-i.<br>
 Alas! | my niece.</p>
 <p>For what am I, unfortunate one, looking?<br>
 Alas! my niece.</p>
