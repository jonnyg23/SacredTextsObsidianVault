 <h3 align="CENTER">Na yû'Ans xâ'-idAga-i.</h3>
 <h3 align="CENTER">30.</h3>
 <font face="Times Roman,Times New Roman"><p>NAñk<u>î</u>'lSLas <u>g</u>ôñ<u>g</u>â'+ qîndjî'wayû hao hao qîn<u>x</u>iê'ndalAñ.<br>
 NAñk<u>î</u>'lSLas's | father | a great one, | such a great one coming along.<br>
 Gua+ q<u>î</u>n<u>g</u>ê'dao î+dja'n sû qî'n<strike>l</strike>ga<strike>l</strike>wañ.<br>
 Halloo! | great chief | is | he says | great one moving about.<br>
 Gua'+ qwî<u>g</u>a (gî) gî'na <u>g</u>ag<u>î</u>t<strike>l</strike>ûg<u>î</u>n û'hao hao îs<br>
 halloo! | sky to | something | stretched up (like a rope) | he is there, | it is he<br>
     q<u>î</u>n<u>x</u>i<u>ê</u>'ndAl-Añgua'.<br>
     great one moving along.<br>
 Q<u>î</u>ñ<u>g</u>ê'do î+dja'n sû q<u>î</u>'ñ<strike>l</strike>gA<strike>l</strike>wAñ gua'.<br>
 Great chief | is | he says | great moving about, | halloo!</p>
 <p>He says it is NAñk<u>î</u>'lSLas's great father moving along so greatly.<br>
 Halloo, great chief moving about!<br>
 Halloo! he moves along greatly like something extending to the sky.<br>
 Halloo great chief moving about! <a name="fr_41"></a><a href="#fn_37"><font size="1">2</font></a></p>
