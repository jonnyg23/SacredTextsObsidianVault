 <h3 align="CENTER">64.</h3>
 <font face="Times Roman,Times New Roman"><p>GadA'ldiañ, gadA'ldiañ (many times),<br>
 How great you are, | how great you are!<br>
 Sû'digwañ gî'dadiañ, gî'dadian (last word repeated several times)<br>
 They said | belonging to a high family, | belonging to a high family<br>
 A da'lañ siñq!a'oda<u>g</u>a a<u>g</u>ê'gî at t!â'<u>g</u>wus k!î'tayû'-<u>g</u>adas<br>
 You | gambling-stick bag | into | with | copper | throw a great one (in exchange for services)<br>
     wa'at s<u>g</u>un gîdâ'diañ sûdî'gwañ. <a name="fr_71"></a><a href="#fn_66"><font size="1">1</font></a><br>
     with it | only | belong to a high family, | they say.</p>
 <p>How great you are, how great you are! etc.,<br>
 They say belonging to a high family, belonging to a high family, etc.<br>
 In exchange you throw a great copper into the gambling stick bag. <a name="fr_72"></a><a href="#fn_66"><font size="1">1</font></a> They say those with this are the only ones who belong to a high family.</p>
