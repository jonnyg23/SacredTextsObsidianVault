 <font face="Times Roman,Times New Roman"><p>Îhîyîyaha, etc.<br>

 <p>Îhîyîyaha, etc.<br>
 You cry because you want to dance as you used to when guests came through the strait <a name="fr_62"></a><a href="#fn_57"><font size="1">1</font></a> to you.<br>
 It is not now as it was in olden times.<br>
 Even slaves (that is, members of other families besides the Yak<sup>u</sup> g<u>î</u>'t<u>î</u>na-i) are beginning to own good abalone shells.<br>
 Îhiya, etc.</p>
