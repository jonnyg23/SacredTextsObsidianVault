 <h3 align="CENTER">16.</h3>
 <font face="Times Roman,Times New Roman"><p>Dî L!naxAn <u>g</u>ê'i<strike>l</strike>dañ hao a gûdâ'ñ<u>g</u>ani[hê].<br>
 I | like that | became | they used to wish.<br>
 WAgaña'ñ ô la gêi<strike>l</strike>sgia'ñ hao ô.<br>
 Like that | she became | soon.<br>
 Â'haô dalA'ñ s<u>g</u>A'nxAn k<u>î'</u>lsLa-i <a name="fr_22"></a><a href="#fn_18"><font size="1">1</font></a> ya'ta dî gadAldjî'gañ<br>
 You | only | chief | only (or fit) | you | are (said of a high family)<br>
 hao ô+.</p>
 <p>They used to wish that I should be like that.<br>
 Like it she soon became.<br>
 You are the only ones fit to be chiefs' daughters.</p>
