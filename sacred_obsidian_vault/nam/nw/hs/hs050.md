 <h3 align="CENTER">48.</h3>
 <font face="Times Roman,Times New Roman"><p>Ayâ'ña kî'lsLa-i ga'ña (four times).<br>
 Be careful of | chief | my own.<br>
 Dja Lan <strike>l</strike>a q!ô'gu<u>g</u>a<u>g</u>o.<br>
 Say, | stop | biting.<br>
 DAñ si-û' djigu'ldA<u>x</u>wañ gîda'-i.<br>
 Your month might become crooked | mosquitoes | common things.</p>
 <p>Be careful of my chief.<br>
 Stop biting!<br>
 Your mouth might become crooked, common mosquitoes. <a name="fr_57"></a><a href="#fn_52"><font size="1">1</font></a></p>
