 <h3 align="CENTER">60.</h3>
 <font face="Times Roman,Times New Roman"><p>Îhîhîa, îhîhîa (many times).<br>
 Tâ'-idAldans dî'txa xA'nhao dî'nAñ kû'sqetgwansi <u>L</u>û sq!ao-<br>
 Waves behind even my child looks around when salmon-<br>
     <u>g</u>âns q!ana's at dî'nAñ dî g<u>î</u>ñqô'nañgasañ.<br>
     berries unripe with my child one will fool.<br>
 Îhîhîa, îhîhîa.</p>
 <p>Îhîhîa, îhîhîa, etc.<br>

 will fool me with unripe salmon-berries. <a name="fr_67"></a><a href="#fn_62"><font size="1">3</font></a><br>
