 <h3 align="CENTER">40.</h3>
 <font face="Times Roman,Times New Roman"><p>AdAñ <u>g</u>ô'+ñ<u>g</u>a nagâ'ga, adAñ <u>g</u>ô'+ñ<u>g</u>a na<u>g</u>â'<u>g</u>a, Q!ô'na<br>
 Your | father's | house in, | your | father's | house in, | Cape Q!ô'na<br>
 kun sq!ê'na-i hao[a] ga ta <u>g</u>oña<strike>l</strike>xa'ndies.<br>
 sea-gulls | eating things are making cries.<br>
 Ga dAñ gîda<u>g</u>a<strike>l</strike>gâ'<strike>l</strike> uga'ñ.<br>
 All these things | you | are going to move | proudly as you sit.</p>
 <p>AdAñ <u>g</u>ô'+ñ<u>g</u>a nagâ'ga, adAñ <u>g</u>ô'+ñ<u>g</u>a na<u>g</u>â'<u>g</u>a, Gîlû'sAms<br>
 Your | father's | house in, | your | father's | house in, | Nass Inlet<br>
 kun sq!ê'na-i hao[a] ga tâ <u>g</u>oña<strike>l</strike>xa'ndies.<br>
 sea-gulls | eating things are making cries.<br>
 Ga dAñ gîda<u>g</u>a<strike>l</strike>gâ'<strike>l</strike> uga'ñ.<br>
 All these things | you | are going to move | proudly as you sit.</p>
 <p><a name="page_31"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 31</font></a></p>
 <p>In your father's house, in your father's house, Cape Q!ô'na sea-gulls <a name="fr_52"></a><a href="#fn_47"><font size="1">1</font></a> make cries as they eat.<br>
 You are going to bear yourself proudly in the midst of these things.</p>
