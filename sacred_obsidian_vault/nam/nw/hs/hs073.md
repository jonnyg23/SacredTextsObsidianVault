 <h3 align="CENTER">71.</h3>
 <font face="Times Roman,Times New Roman"><p>Û+hua', <a name="fr_79"></a><a href="#fn_73"><font size="1">1</font></a> û+hua', ûhua'-a-a,<br>
 Loftiest one, | loftiest one, | loftiest one,<br>
 DAñ nâ'<u>g</u>a ga L!a qoa'ndîgînî gua kî'lsLa-i.<br>
 Your | house | in there was formerly plenty, | ? | chief.<br>
 Gî'na uhua', uhua', qâ<strike>l</strike>i sku'na yûdA'lgAñAs a,<br>
 Something, | greatest one, | greatest one, | inside (some thing) big | smells strongly,<br>
 U'hua, û'hua, qî'ndal?<br>
 Loftiest one, | loftiest one, | mightiest one?</p>
 <p>Loftiest one, loftiest one, loftiest one,<br>
 There used to be plenty in your house, chief.<br>
 Does not something big (i. e., a whale) smell strongly in your house,<br>
 Loftiest, loftiest, mightiest chief?</p>
