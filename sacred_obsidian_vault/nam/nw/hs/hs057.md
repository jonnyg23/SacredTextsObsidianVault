 <h3 align="CENTER">Yâ'k<sup>u</sup> gîtîna'-i.</h3>
 <h3 align="CENTER">55.</h3>
 <font face="Times Roman,Times New Roman"><p align="CENTER">(Words in Tlingit)</p>
 <p>Ya'naxê, ya'naxê; â'hao; ya'naxê, ya'naxê. <a name="fr_61"></a><a href="#fn_56"><font size="1">2</font></a><br>
 Gadjî'djûs dûqa<strike>l</strike> dAsgî <u>x</u>ûk<sup>u</sup> yana.<br>
             his dog<br>
 WAgakîda kAda'ostêdja.</p>
 <p>
 </p>
 <p><a name="page_39"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 39</font></a></p>
 <p align="CENTER">Haida Equivalent.</p>
 <p>Xa'as xA'nhao qê'gas wa'<u>g</u>a A'ñga k!û'<u>g</u>agA'ñga:<br>
 Dogs | even | when they have pups | to them | theirs | love:<br>
 Wa'a<strike>l</strike>hao A'ñ<u>g</u>a ga dî k!ûga'gAñ.<br>
 That is why | mine | I | love.</p>
 <p>Even dogs love their offspring:<br>
 So I love mine.</p>
