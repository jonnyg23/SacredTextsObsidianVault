 <h3 align="CENTER">86.</h3>
 <font face="Times Roman,Times New Roman"><p>Nañ <u>g</u>êst' dî'na qoga'-iwas <u>L</u>û naas <u>g</u>adô' dî'na kû'sqedA-<br>
 Out of his house | mine | goes out | when, | house | around | mine | will | walk<br>
 gwañasañ.<br>
 and look.<br>
 Î'ldjao dA'ñA<strike>l</strike> kuda'ltc!aasañ.<br>
 Chief (or rich man)| with | my dear will enter.</p>
 <p>When my child goes out of his house, he will walk around among the houses and look about.<br>
 With chiefs (only) my dear will enter.</p>
