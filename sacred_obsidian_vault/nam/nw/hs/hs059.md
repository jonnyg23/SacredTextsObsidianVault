 <h3 align="CENTER">57.</h3>
 <font face="Times Roman,Times New Roman"><p>Hî hî ha hî, etc.<br>
 Lan L!a ku'ng<u>î</u>ñañ.<br>
 Stop, | however, | crying.<br>
 Lan L!a kû'djiû.<br>
 Stop, | how-ever, | and sit down.<br>
 <strike>L</strike>ima'n sqâ'lAña-i dA'ñ<u>g</u>a gu't<u>g</u>An kûdA'sdiga,<br>
 (Kind of skin) | stowed away | yours | lies in many | caches one after the other.<br>
 <u>Î</u>'s<u>î</u>ñ qâ'<strike>l</strike>i gut dâ kû<u>x</u>iâ'ñgwa'ñgasañ<br>
 Again | inside (of house) | you | will go round.<br>
 Lan a <a name="fr_63"></a><a href="#fn_58"><font size="1">1</font></a> s<u>g</u>a'-i<strike>l</strike> k<u>î</u>lsLa'-i I qê'gAn. <a name="fr_64"></a><a href="#fn_59"><font size="1">2</font></a><br>
 Stop | your | crying, | chief | I | bore.<br>
 Hî hî ha hî, etc.</p>
 <p>Hî hî ha hî, etc.<br>
 But stop crying!<br>
 Stop and sit down!<br>
 Your <strike>l</strike>imA'n blankets lie stowed away in many storehouses.<br>
 Again you will go round inside of the house.<br>
 Stop crying, chief I bore!<br>
 Hî hî ha hî, etc.</p>
