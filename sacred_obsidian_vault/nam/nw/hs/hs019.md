 <h3 align="CENTER">17.</h3>
 <font face="Times Roman,Times New Roman"><p>Hao dalA'ñ s<u>g</u>A'nxAn k<u>î</u>lsLa'is <a name="fr_23"></a><a href="#fn_19"><font size="1">2</font></a> ya'ta <u>g</u>adA'ldî<u>g</u>Ankwê;<br>
 You | only | chief | are of a high family<br>
 Hao dalA'ñ s<u>g</u>A'nxAn k<u>î</u>lsLa'is ya'ta <u>g</u>adA'ldî<u>g</u>Ankwê;<br>
 You | only | chief | are of a high family<br>
 Gî L! (aya+) q!otgâ'ñdixan djâ'<u>g</u>adA'ñ<u>g</u>an<u>î</u>.<br>
 For they | used to be soliciting | (they) tried to get the woman a long time.<br>
 Hao dalA'ñ s<u>g</u>A'nxAn k<u>î</u>lsLa'is ya'ta <u>g</u>adA'ldî<u>g</u>Ankwê;<br>
 You | only | chief | are of a high family<br>
 Hao dalA'ñ s<u>g</u>A'nxAn k<u>î</u>lsLa'is ya'ta <u>g</u>adA'ldî<u>g</u>Ankwê;<br>
 You | only | chief | are of a high family</p>
 <p>You alone are fit to be a chief woman,<br>
 You alone are fit to be a chief woman.<br>
 For (you) they begged a long time (to obtain you in marriage).<br>
 You alone are fit to be a chief woman,<br>
 You alone are fit to be a chief woman.</p>
