 <h3 align="CENTER">50.</h3>
 <font face="Times Roman,Times New Roman"><p>Â'dAñ gô'ñ<u>g</u>a nâ'<u>g</u>a<u>g</u>a adA'ñ tc<u>î</u>'n<u>g</u>a nâ'<u>g</u>a adA'ñ tc<u>î</u>'n<u>g</u>a<br>
 Your | father's | house in, | your | grand-father's | house, your | grand-father's<br>
     nâ'<u>g</u>a,<br>

 Gilû'sAms <a name="fr_58"></a><a href="#fn_53"><font size="1">2</font></a> sq!ê'na-i ga ta<u>g</u>ô'na<strike>l</strike>xandles ga dAñ qoya'<br>

 <p>Â'dAñ gô'ñ<u>g</u>a nâ'<u>g</u>a<u>g</u>a <strike>L</strike><u>g</u>ai-û'kun sq!ê'na-i ga ta<u>g</u>ô'ñal-<br>
 Your | father's | house in | Skidegate Point | sea-gulls | make a noise while<br>
 xandies <u>g</u>a dAñ gî'da<u>g</u>a<strike>l</strike>gA<strike>l</strike>.<br>
 eating | in, | you | get higher all the time (receiving more tattoo-marks, etc.)</p>
 <p>In your father's house, in your (grandfather's house, in your grandfather's house,<br>
 Where Nass Inlet sea-gulls (i. e., the Nass people) make noises as they eat, you, dear, move highly.<br>
 In your father's house, where Skidegate Point sea-gulls (i. e., Skidegate people) make a noise as they eat, you get higher (i. e., become a greater chief) all the time.</p>
