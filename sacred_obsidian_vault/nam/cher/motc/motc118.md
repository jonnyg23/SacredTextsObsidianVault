 <center><h1>118. Baby Song, To Please The Children</h1></center><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <i><font size="2"><p>Ha'wiye'-hyuwe', Ha'wiye'-hyuwe',<br>
 Yu'wê-yuwëhe', Ha'wiyëhyu'-uwe'--<br>
 Yâ'nû une'guhi' tsana'sehâ';<br>
 E`tï une'guhi' tsana'sehâ;<br>
 Yâ'nû nudûñnelû' tsa'nadiskâ</p>
 </font></i><p>Ha'wiye'-hyuwe', Ha'wiye'-hyuwe',<br>
 Yu'wê-yuwëhe', Ha'wiyëhyu'-uwe'--<br>
 The Bear is very bad, so they say;<br>
 Long time ago he was very bad, so they say;<br>
 The Bear did so and so, they say.</p></dir>
