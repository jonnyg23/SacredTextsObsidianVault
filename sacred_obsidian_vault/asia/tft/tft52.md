 <p><a name="page_191"><font size="1" color="green">p. 191</font></a></p>  <h4 align="center">FORTY-NINE</h4>  <h4 align="center">Tibetan Song</h4>  <h2 align="center">TRANSLATED FROM TIBETAN BY</h2>  <h2 align="center">A. L. SHELTON, M.D.</h2>  <h3 align="center">I.</h3>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">In the middle of the sea is a high mountain.<br> The sun is shining on the mountain, on a great plain<br> The flowers are blooming.<br> When the sun is shining on the yellow flowers<br> All men are pleased.<br> On the mountain are grass and water.<br> The cows are resting in the grass, water and sun.<br> On this mountain the evergreens grow always.<br> The cuckoos are resting in the trees.<br> The trees are blue, cuckoos are blue, all men are happy.</div>  <h3 align="center">2.</h3>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">The snows are everlasting.<br> There are small and large black tents.<br> All the lions are tied.<br> Milk is as the waters of the sea.<br> The tents are like cliffs.<br> All the eagles are tied.<br> Milk is like the sea.<br> On the plain are the tents great and small.<br> The deer are all tied.<br> Their milk is as the sea.</div>  
 <p><a name="page_192"><font size="1" color="green">p. 192</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">3.</h3>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">At the head of this great plain<br> Are ninety-nine hundred fine horses.<br> Their saddles are all of gold.<br> The name of this is beautiful. (All immortals live here.)<br> In the middle of this plain<br> Are many herds of cattle.<br> They all eat from golden stalls.<br> They are immortal.<br> At the lower end of this plain, the sheep are herded.<br> They are all happy and immortal.</div>
