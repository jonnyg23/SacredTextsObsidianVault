 <p>This page
 links together resources at sacred-texts
 pertaining to traditional people of Asia, including Siberia and
 Southeast Asia.
 </p>
 <p>
 Asia is also the origin of many of the most important and ancient
 world religions, including
 <a href="../hin/index.htm">Hinduism</a>,
 <a href="../bud/index.htm">Buddhism</a>,
 <a href="../cfu/index.htm">Confucianism</a>,
 and <a href="../tao/index.htm">Taoism</a>.
 
 
 <a name="siberia"></a>
 </p><h3>Siberia</h3>
 
 <p>
 It is worth noting that many of the Siberian traditions bear more than a passing
 resemblance to those of the <a href="../nam/inu/index.htm">Inuit</a>
 and the <a href="../nam/nw/index.htm">Northwestern</a> Native Americans.
 </p>
 
 <p>
 <a href="#" onclick="window.open('../cdshop/index.htm','cartwin','');"><img src="../img/buycd.jpg" height="20" border="0"></a>
 
 <span class="c_e">
 <span class="c_t"><a href="jss/index.htm">A Journey in Southern Siberia</a></span><br>
 by <span class="c_a">Jeremiah Curtin</span> [<span class="c_d">1909</span>]<br>
 <span class="c_b">The mythology and folklore of the Buriat Mongols of Siberia.</span>
 </span>
 </p>
 
 
 <p>
 <a href="#" onclick="window.open('../cdshop/index.htm','cartwin','');"><img src="../img/buycd.jpg" height="20" border="0"></a>
 
 <span class="c_e">
 <span class="c_t"><a href="../nam/inu/eos/index.htm">The Eskimo of Siberia</a></span><br>
 by <span class="c_a">Waldemar Bogoras</span> [<span class="c_d">1913</span>]
 </span>
 </p>
 
 <p>
 <a href="#" onclick="window.open('../cdshop/index.htm','cartwin','');"><img src="../img/buycd.jpg" height="20" border="0"></a>
 
 <span class="c_e">
 <span class="c_t"><a href="cm/index.htm">Chukchee Mythology</a></span><br>
 By <span class="c_a">Waldemar Bogoras</span> [<span class="c_d">1910</span>]<br>
 <span class="c_b">The Chukchee occupy the extreme north-eastern tip of Asia, adjacent to the Arctic Ocean and the Behring Strait.</span>
 </span>
 </p>
 
 <p>
 <a href="#" onclick="window.open('../cdshop/index.htm','cartwin','');"><img src="../img/buycd.jpg" height="20" border="0"></a>
 
 <span class="c_e">
 <span class="c_t"><a href="kort/index.htm">Koryak Texts</a></span><br>
 by <span class="c_a">Waldemar Bogoras</span> [<span class="c_d">1917</span>]<br>
 <span class="c_b">A book of mythological texts from the Koryak people of Kamchatka.</span>
 </span>
 </p>
 
 <p>
 <a href="#" onclick="window.open('../cdshop/index.htm','cartwin','');"><img src="../img/buycd.jpg" height="20" border="0"></a>
 
 <span class="c_e">
 <span class="c_t"><a href="tes/index.htm">Tales of Yukaghir, Lamut, and Russianized Natives of Eastern Siberia</a></span><br>
 by <span class="c_a">Waldemar Bogoras</span> [<span class="c_d">1918</span>]<br>
 <span class="c_b">A collection of folklore from Eastern Siberia.</span>
 </span>
 </p>
 
 <p>
 <a href="#" onclick="window.open('../cdshop/index.htm','cartwin','');"><img src="../img/buycd.jpg" height="20" border="0"></a>
 
 <span class="c_e">
 <span class="c_t"><a href="../sha/sis/index.htm">Shamanism in Siberia</a></span><br>
 from <i>Aboriginal Siberia</i>,
 by <span class="c_a">M. A. Czaplicka</span> [<span class="c_d">1914</span>]<br>
 <span class="c_b">A comprehensive review of the ethographic literature concerning Shamanism in Sibera.</span>
 </span>
 <br>
 
 
 </p><h3>Central Asia</h3>
 <p>
 <a href="#" onclick="window.open('../cdshop/index.htm','cartwin','');"><img src="../img/buycd.jpg" height="20" border="0"></a>

 <a href="#" onclick="window.open('../cdshop/index.htm','cartwin','');"><img src="../img/buycd.jpg" height="20" border="0"></a>

 by <span class="c_a">H. G. Raverty</span> [<span class="c_d">1867</span>]<br>
 <span class="c_b">Anthology of later Sufi poets from Afghanistan, many translated directly from rare manuscripts.</span>
 </span>
 <br>
 
 <p>
 <a href="#" onclick="window.open('../cdshop/index.htm','cartwin','');"><img src="../img/buycd.jpg" height="20" border="0"></a>

 <span class="c_t"><a href="flo/index.htm">Folklore and Legends: Oriental</a></span><br>
 by <span class="c_a">Charles John Tibbitts</span> [<span class="c_d">1889</span>]<br>
 <span class="c_b">A collection of Arabian, Persian, Indian and Kalmyk tales, including the seldom-seen 'Relations of Ssidi Kur.'</span>
 </span>

 <p>

 </span>
 </p>
 
 <p>
 <a href="#" onclick="window.open('../cdshop/index.htm','cartwin','');"><img src="../img/buycd.jpg" height="20" border="0"></a>

 <span class="c_b">A short account of the Yezidis by a Christian Missionary.</span>
 </span>
 <br>
 
 </p><p>

 <span class="c_b">The mysterious religion of the Druze of Lebanon.</span>
 </span>
 <br>
 
 <a href="#" onclick="window.open('../cdshop/index.htm','cartwin','');"><img src="../img/buycd.jpg" height="20" border="0"></a>

 <span class="c_t"><a href="../isl/arp/index.htm">Arabian Poetry</a></span><br>
 by <span class="c_a">W. A. Clouston</span> [<span class="c_d">1881</span>]<br>
