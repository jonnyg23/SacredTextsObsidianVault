 <p><a name="page_5"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 5</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">O NIGHT, BE LONG</h3>  <h5 align="center">BY N. KOUCHAK</h5>  
 <p></p><dir>O NIGHT, be long--long as an endless year!<br> Descend, thick darkness, black and full of fear!<br>      To-night my heart's desire has been fulfilled--<br>      My love is here at last--a guest concealed!  
 <p>Dawn, stand behind seven mountains--out of sight,<br> Lest thou my loved one banish with thy light;<br>      I would for ever thus in darkness rest<br>      So I might ever clasp him to my breast.</p></dir>
