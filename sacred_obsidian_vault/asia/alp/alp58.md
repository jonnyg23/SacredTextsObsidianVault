 <p><a name="page_81"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 81</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">O’ER THE MOUNTAINS HIGH HE WENT</h3>  <h5 align="center">FOLK SONG</h5>  
 <p>O'ER the mountains high he went,<br>      Love, love!<br>      In the meadows above<br>      I seek for my love.<br> With a weary sigh he went.<br>      Love, love!<br>      To fight thou art gone<br>      While I stay here alone.</p>  
 <p>Neither quail nor partridge stirred.<br>      Love, love!<br>      In the meadows above<br>      I look for my love<br> And he left without a word.<br>      Love, love!<br>      To fight thou art gone<br>      While I stay here alone.</p>  
 <p>Like a flower's fragrance sweet<br> He came past, and vanished fleet.<br>      I beheld him and I loved<br>      But we never more shall meet.</p>  
 <p>When my lover rode away<br> Not a farewell did I say;<br>      None there is to help him now--<br>      Sourb Carapet, <a name="fr_9"></a><a href="#fn_9"><font size="1">1</font></a> bless his way!</p>
