 <p><a name="page_3"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 3</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">THE APPLE TREE</h3>  <h5 align="center">FOLK SONG</h5>  
 <p></p><dir>     THE door of Heaven open seemed<br>      And in thy house the sunlight gleamed.  
 <p>As through the garden's willow’d walks I hied<br> Full many a tree and blossom I espied.<br> But of all trees, the Apple Tree most fair<br> And beautiful did unto me appear.<br> It sobbed and wept. Its leaves said murmuringly:<br> "I would that God had ne’er created me!<br> The badge of sin and wickedness I am<br> E’en at thy feast, O Father Abraham. <a name="fr_0"></a><a href="#fn_0"><font size="1">1</font></a><br>      The apple growing on me first<br>      From Eden came ere it was cursed,<br>      Alas, alas, I am undone!<br>      Why fell I to that evil one?"</p></dir>
