 <p><a name="page_21"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 21</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">CRADLE SONG</h3>  <h5 align="center">BY RAPHAEL PATKANIAN</h5>  
 <p align="center">(1830-1892)</p>  
 <p align="center"><i>Mother</i></p>  
 <p></p><dir>SWEET slumber now creeps o’er thee slow,<br> Sweet breezes rock thee to and fro:<br> My baby sleeps, so soft and low<br> With sweetest songs I'll sing <i>oror</i>. <a name="fr_4"></a><a href="#fn_4"><font size="1">1</font></a></dir>  
 <p align="center"><i>Baby</i></p>  
 <p></p><dir>O Mother dear, thou art unkind<br> My sleepless eyes so long to bind. <a name="fr_5"></a><a href="#fn_5"><font size="1">2</font></a><br> Anon I'll rest, and sleep resigned;--<br> Release me now, sing not <i>oror</i>.</dir>  
 <p align="center"><i>Mother</i></p>  
 <p></p><dir>Why dost thou shed those tears that flow<br> Down thy sad cheeks with pearly glow '<br> Thou’lt break thy heart with sobbing so,--<br> Whom wilt thou have to sing <i>oror?</i> </dir>  
 <p align="center"><i>Baby</i></p>  
 <p></p><dir>At least my hands and feet unbind--<br> My tender limbs are all confined;<br> That gentle sleep my eyes may find,<br> Then tie me in, and sing <i>oror</i>.</dir>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p><a name="page_22"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 22</font></a></p>  
 <p align="center"><i>Mother</i></p>  
 <p></p><dir>That tongue of thine is passing sweet,<br> Yet with thy yards I cannot mete.<br> Thou wilt not sleep, but at thy feet<br> Wouldst have me sit, and sing <i>oror</i>.</dir>  
 <p align="center"><i>Baby</i></p>  
 <p></p><dir>All piteously I raise my prayer,<br> I sob and cry, thou dost not hear.<br> Thy sweet voice seems to charm thine ear--<br> I weep, thou singest still <i>oror</i>.</dir>  
 <p align="center"><i>Mother</i></p>  
 <p></p><dir>Hush, hush, and sleep, my baby dear.<br> My love shall guard thee, year by year,<br> Until my rose-tree blossoms fair,<br> Then ’neath his shade I'll sing <i>oror</i>.</dir>  
 <p align="center"><i>Baby</i></p>  
 <p></p><dir>Thy heart is made of stone, I see.<br> I wept and wept, all uselessly.<br> Now I shall sleep, I can't be free,<br> All night, all night sing me <i>oror!</i> </dir>  
 <p> </p>
