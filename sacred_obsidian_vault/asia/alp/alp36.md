 <p><a name="page_43"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 43</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">THE ARMENIAN POET'S PRAYER</h3>  <h5 align="center">BY ALEXANDER DZADOURIAN</h5>  
 <p align="center">(<i>Born</i> 1870)</p>  
 <p></p><dir>O GOD, ’tis not for laurel wreaths I pray,<br> For pompous funeral or jubilee;<br> Nor yet for fame beyond my life's decay--<br> All these my country will accord to me.  
 <p>One favour, Lord of Heaven, I implore--<br> One that my land to me will never give:<br> Grant me a crust of bread, or else such store<br> Of grace that I on air may learn to live!</p></dir>
