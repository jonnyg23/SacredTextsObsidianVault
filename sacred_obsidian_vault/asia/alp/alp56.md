 <p><a name="page_78"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 78</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">THE PARROT'S SONG</h3>  <h5 align="center">BY RAFFI</h5>  
 <p align="center">(1837-1888)</p>  
 <p></p><dir>WITH nuts and sweets and dainty fare,<br>      My lady feeds me oft.<br> She decks my cage with tender care,<br>      And hands so white and soft.  
 <p>But not a moment's joy can give<br>      This pampering care to me,<br> Since as a prisoner here I live,<br>      In gilded misery.</p></dir>
