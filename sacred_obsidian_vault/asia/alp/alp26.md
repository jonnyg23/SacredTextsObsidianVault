 <p><a name="page_29"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 29</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">YE MOUNTAIN BLUEBELLS</h3>  <h5 align="center">BY AVETIS ISAHAKIAN</h5>  
 <p></p><dir>YE mountain bluebells, weep with me,<br>      And flowers in coloured crowds;<br> Weep, nightingale, on yonder tree,<br>      Cool winds dropped from the clouds.  
 <p>All dark around the earth and sky,<br>      All lonely here I mourn.<br> My love is gone,--light of my eye;<br>      I sob and weep forlorn.</p>  
 <p>Alas, no more he cares for me--<br>      He left me unconsoled;<br> He pierced my heart, then cruelly<br>      Left me in pain untold.</p>  
 <p>Ye mountain bluebells, weep with me,<br>      And flowers in coloured crowds;<br> Weep, nightingale, on yonder tree,--<br>      Cool winds dropped from the clouds.</p></dir>
