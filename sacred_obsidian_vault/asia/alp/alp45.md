 <p><a name="page_59"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 59</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">LIKE AN OCEAN IS THIS WORLD</h3>  <h5 align="center">BY HOVHANNES ERZINGATZI</h5>  
 <p align="center">(<i>Born</i> 1260)</p>  
 <p></p><dir>     LIKE an ocean is this world;<br> None undrenched may cross that ocean.<br>      My ship too its sails unfurled,<br> Ere I knew it was in motion.  
 <p>     Now we draw towards the land,<br> And I fear the sea-board yonder:--<br>      Lest the rocks upon the strand<br> Break and tear our planks asunder.</p>  
 <p>     I will pray God that He raise<br> From the shore a breeze to meet us,<br>      To disperse this gloomy haze,<br> That a happy land may greet us.</p></dir>
