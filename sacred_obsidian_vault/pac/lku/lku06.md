 <p><a name="page_19"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 19</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">THE FIFTH ERA.</h3>  <h4 align="center">FIRST VERSE.</h4>  
 <p>The advance of age when Kapokanokano (night of strength)<br> Established heaps in the Polalouli (depth of night),<br> And the dark fresh color of the earth thrown up<br> Was the darkness of the famous Polalouli (night in the deep),<br> Who married for wife Kapokanokano.<br> His snout was of great size and with it dug the earth;<br> He dug until he raised a great mound,<br> He raised a hill for his gods,<br> A hill, a precipice in front,<br> For the offspring of a pig which was born;<br> Built a house and paid the forest<br> And rested by the patches of Loiloa,<br>              For Umi who is to possess the land,<br>              For Umi who is to reign anon;<br> The land where Kapokanokano dwelt,<br> To which place laid a path of frailest trail,<br> A trail as fine as the choicest hair of this pig,<br> A being was born half pig, half god,<br> At the time of life of Kapokanokano,<br> Who became the wife of Polalouli.<br>              Night was born.</p>  <h4 align="center">SECOND VERSE.</h4>  
 <p>The Poowaawaa was born, his head was uneven.<br> The Poopahapaha was born, his head was flat and spread.<br> The Poohiwahiwa was born, he appeared noble.<br> The Poohaole was born, he became a haole (foreigner).<br> The Poomahakea was born, his skin was fair.<br> The Pooapahu was born, was a hairy man.<br> The Poomeumeu was born, is a short man.<br> The Pooauli was born, is dark complexioned.<br> The Hewahewa was born, and he remained so (light-headed).<br> The Lawalawa was born, becomes a lawalawa. <a name="page_20"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 20</font></a><br> The Hooipo was born, and became hooipoipo (loving).<br> The Hulu was born, and became a-aia (demented).<br> The Hulupii was born, and became piipii (curly-headed).<br> The Meleuli was born, and became melemele (yellow-haired).<br> The Haupo was born, and became hauponuinui (noble-chested).<br> The Hilahila was born, and became hilahila (very bashful).<br> The Kenakena was born, and became kenakena (bitter).<br> The Luheluhe was born, and became luheluhe (limber).<br> The Awaawa was born, and became awaawa (sour disposed).<br> The Aliilii was born, and became liilii (puny).<br> The Makuakua was born, and became kuakua (great).<br> The Halahala was born, decorated with lei Hala.<br> The Eweewe was born, who was proud of his pedigree.<br> The Huelo Maewa was born, with very long tail.<br> The Hulu liha was born, and became lihelihe (hairy eggs).<br> The Pukaua was born, and became a warrior.<br> The Meheula was born, and became red.<br> The Puuwelu was born, and became weluwelu (ragged).<br> That is his, this is in shreds.<br> Then came the children of Loiloa,<br> And the land grew and spread,<br> And the goblet of wish was lowered<br> Of affections for the tribe of relations,<br> Of songs that grasp of Oma's friends<br> Till relations are enrolled from Kapokanokano<br>                           At yester eve.<br>                             ’Tis night.</p>
