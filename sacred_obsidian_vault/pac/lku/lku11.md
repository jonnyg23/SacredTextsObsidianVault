 <p><a name="page_25"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 25</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">THE TENTH ERA.</h3>  
 <p>Maila, with Lailai for protection,<br> And Kane of Kapokinikini was support, Kii was helpless.<br> Laioloolo was born and lived at Kapapa.<br> Kamahaina was born a man,<br> Kamamale was born a man,<br> Kamakalua a woman,<br> Poeleieholo a child,<br> Poeleaaholo a child,<br> Then Wehiwelawehi loa.<br> Lailai went back to Kane,<br> Hai was born a woman,<br> Halia was born a woman,<br> Hakea was born a man,<br> The Muki, Muka, and Mukekeke were born (kisses, smacks, chirruping),<br> Smacks, boils, and other weaknesses,<br> Moku, Monu, mumuleana (strife, broils, and huffiness),<br> The men became speechless from sulkiness,<br> Became cross from envy of ours,<br> Of the woman who is brave and fearless,<br> Then hid and dared not claim kin,<br> To claim kin with her child.<br> The heavens deny the right of kin (being of younger branch),<br> Yield the sacred right to Kii;<br> For to be with Kii, ’tis his to claim.<br> Kane then taunts her eldest for this,<br> Kii retaliates through Lailai for his being the younger branch;<br> He flings a stone and hits Kane;<br> Then is heard the sound of the drum,<br> The sign of life for the younger.<br> Kane, furious with jealous anger, struck her for faithlessness<br> For the younger child of the younger branch.<br> That is why first-born are always hakus (superiors, lords), <a name="page_26"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 26</font></a><br> First through Lailai, next by Kii,<br> Their first-born with sacred birthright<br>                             Is born.</p>
