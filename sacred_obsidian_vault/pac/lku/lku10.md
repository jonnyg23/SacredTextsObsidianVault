   <h3 align="center">THE NINTH ERA.</h3>  
 <p>Lailai, of the quaking earth,<br> Of great heat and noise, and opening heavens,<br> This woman ascending to heaven,<br> Climbed to heaven by the forest.<br> Onehenehe flew where the earth rose.<br> Children of Kii that were born from the brain,<br> Were born and flew, both flew to heaven.<br> Then the signs appeared and cast their shadow<br> On their forehead, a bread fruit was impressed,<br> On their chins shot roots of fire.<br> This woman was from a race of delusions (myth),<br> A woman with dark skin, from the land of Iipakalani,<br> Where numbers of men lived in the heat.<br> This woman lived in Nuumealani,<br> Land where the Aoa thrived,<br> Who stripped with great ease the leaves of the Koa;<br> A woman whose person was never seen,<br> From her to Kii, from him to Kane,<br> From her to Kane of Kapokinikini.<br> The times of those people came to naught.<br> A tribe, a generation of great strength,<br> She alone flew to her abode,<br> And on the boughs of the Aoa tree, in Nuumealani stayed,<br> Became pregnant, and the earth was born.<br> Haha Poele was born a woman,<br> Hapopo was born,<br> Lohelohe was born last of all.<br> These were the children of this woman.<br>                             ’Tis day.</p>
