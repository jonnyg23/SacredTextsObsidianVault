 <p><a name="page_vii"><font size="1" color="green">p. vii</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">NOTE</h3>  
 <p>IN response to repeated requests, the compiler now presents in book form the series of legends that have been made a feature of "The Hawaiian Annual" for a number of years past. The series has been enriched by the addition of several tales, the famous shark legend having been furnished for this purpose from the papers of the Hawaiian Historical Society.</p>  
 <p>The collection embraces contributions by the Rev. A. O. Forbes, Dr. N. B. Emerson, J. S. Emerson, Mrs. E. M. Nakuina, W. M. Gibson, Dr. C. M. Hyde, and others, all of whom are recognized authorities.</p>  
 <p align="right">T. G. T.</p>  
 <p>HONOLULU, January 1, 1907.</p>
