 <h1 align="CENTER">THE KUMULIPO</h1>
 <h4 align="CENTER">A Hawaiian Creation Chant</h4>
 <h5 align="CENTER">translated with commentary by</h5>
 <h3 align="CENTER">Martha Warren Beckwith</h3>
 <p align="CENTER">[Published 1951, copyright not renewed]</p>
 <p>
 This book, which appears for the first time on the Internet at Sacred
 Texts, contains the traditional Creation and Geneological
 Chant of the Hawaiian royal family, and Beckwith's
 extensive textual, anthroplogical, and historical commentary.
 This is one of the few source documents available in the realm of Polynesian
 religion, and it is stunning.
 The full <a href="ku31.htm">Hawaiian version of the chant</a> is included.</p>
 <p>
 Also available at this site is
 <a href="../lku/index.htm">Queen Liliuokalani's translation of the Kumulipo</a>.
 </p>
 <p>
 Very few works this recent are yet in the public domain.
 This book is in the public domain because it was not
 renewed in a timely fashion as the US copyright
 law required at the time.</p>
