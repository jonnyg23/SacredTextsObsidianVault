 <p><a name="page_526"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 526</font></a></p> <h3 align="CENTER">TRANSLATION OF EASTER ISLAND TABLET.</h3> <i><h4 align="CENTER">"Ate-a-renga-hokan iti poheraa."</h4> </i><h4 align="CENTER">LOVE SONG.</h4> 
 <p align="CENTER">(Plates XLIV and XLV.)</p> 
 <p>Ka tagi, Renga-a-manu ---- hakaopa;<br>   Chiu runarame a ita metua.<br> Ka ketu te nairo hihi ---- O to hoa!<br>   Eaha ton tiena ---- e te hoa ---- e!</p> 
 <p>Ita haga ta poapatu ---- O te hoa!<br>   Kahii te riva forani ---- O te hoa e!<br> Auwe ka tagi ati ---- u ---- a ---- iti iti.<br>   Eha ton tiena ---- e ta hoa ---- e.</p> 
 <p>Ta hi tiena ita have.<br>    Horoa ita have.<br>    Horoa moni e fahiti;<br>           Ita ori miro;<br>           Ana piri atu;<br>           Ana piri atu;<br>           Ana taga atu.</p> <h4 align="CENTER">ENGLISH TRANSLATION.</h4> <h4 align="CENTER">NATIVE LOVE SONG,</h4> 
 <p>Who is sorrowing? It is Renga-a-manu Hakopa?<br> A red branch descended from her father.<br> Open thine eyelids, my true love.<br> Where is your brother, my love?<br> At the feast in the Bay of Salutation<br> We will meet under the feathers of your clan.<br> She has long been yearning after you.<br> Send your brother as a mediator of love between us,<br> Your brother who is now at the house of my father.<br> O, where is the messenger of love between us?<br> When the feast of drift-wood is commemorated<br> There we will meet in loving embrace.</p>
