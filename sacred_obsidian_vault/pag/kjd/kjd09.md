 <h1 align="CENTER">Newes from Scotland,</h1>
 <p align="CENTER">Declaring the Damnable</p>
 <i></i><p align="CENTER">life and death of Doctor Fian, a</p>
 <p align="CENTER">notable Sorcerer, who was burned at</p>
 <p align="CENTER">Edenbrough in Ianuary laft.</p>
 <p align="CENTER">1591.</p>
 <i></i><p align="CENTER">Which Doctor was regefter to the Diuell</p>
 <p align="CENTER">that fundry times preached at North Barrick Kirke, to a number of notorious Witches.</p>
 <p align="CENTER">With the true examinations of the faide Doctor and Witches as they vttered them in the <i>prefence of the Scottifh King</i>.</p>
 <p align="CENTER">Difcouering how they pretended</p>
 <i></i><p align="CENTER">to bewitch and drowne his Maieftie in the Sea</p>
 <p align="CENTER">comming from Denmarke with fuch</p>
 <i></i><p align="CENTER">or her wonderfull matters as the like</p>
 <p align="CENTER">hath not been heard of at</p>
 <p align="CENTER">any time.</p>
 <p align="CENTER">Publifhed according to the Scottifh Coppie.</p>
 <p align="CENTER">AT LONDON</p>
 <p align="CENTER">Printed for William</p>
 <i></i><p align="CENTER">Wright.</p>
