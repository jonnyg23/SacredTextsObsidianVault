   <h3 align="center">HYMN TO THE UNKNOWN GOD</h3>  
 <p align="center">(<i>Peruvian</i>)</p>  
 <p></p><dir>O RULER! Lord of the universe,<br> Whether thou art male,<br> Whether thou art female,<br> Lord of reproduction<br> Wherever thou mayest be!<br> O Lord of divination<br> Where art thou?<br> Thou mayest be above,<br> Thou mayest be below,<br> Or perhaps around<br> Thy splendid throne and sceptre.<br> O hear me!<br> From the sky above,<br> In which thou mayest be,<br> From the sea beneath<br> In which thou mayest be.<br> Creator of the world,<br> Maker of all men;<br> Lord of all Lords<br> My eyes fail me for longing to see thee<br> For the sole desire to know thee.<br> O look down upon me<br> For thou knowest me.<br> The sun--the moon--<br> The day--the night--<br> Spring--winter,<br> Are not ordained in vain<br> By thee, O Deity!<br> They all travel<br> To the assigned place;<br> They all arrive<br> At their destined ends<br> Whithersoever thou pleasest.<br> Thy royal sceptre<br> Thou holdest.<br> O hear me!<br> O choose me!<br> Let it not be<br> That I should tire,<br> That I should die!</dir>
