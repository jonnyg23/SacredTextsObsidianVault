   <h3 align="center">PRAYER OF THE SOWER</h3>  
 <p align="center">(<i>Finnish</i>)</p>  
 <p></p><dir>BLESSING to the seed I scatter,<br> Where it falls upon the meadow,<br> By the grace of Ukko mighty,<br> Through the open finger spaces<br> Of the hand that all things fashioned.<br> Queen of meadow-land and pasture!<br> Bid the earth unlock her treasures.<br> Bid the soil the young seed nourish,<br> Never shall their teeming forces<br> Never shall their strength prolific<br> Fail to nourish and sustain us<br> If the Daughters of Creation,<br> They, the free and bounteous givers<br> Still extend their, gracious favor<br> Offer still their strong protection.<br> Rise, O Earth! from out thy slumbers<br> Bid the soil unlock her treasures!</dir>
