   <h3 align="center">CHINESE LITURGY</h3>  
 <p></p><dir>ONE in spirit,<br> We invoke thee!<br> Hail, Amit-abha of the world!<br> O would that our merciful teacher, Sakya-muni,<br> And our great Father Amit-abha Would now descend and be present with us.<br> Would that the perfect compassion-ate heart would now draw near And receive our offerings.<br> May the omnipotent and omniscient Holy Spirit<br> Come to us while we recite these divine sentences.</dir>
