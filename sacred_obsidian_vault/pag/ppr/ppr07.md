   <h3 align="center">ADDRESS TO SUPREME DEITY</h3>  
 <p align="center">(<i>Assyrian</i>)</p>  
 <p></p><dir>IN HEAVENS who is great? Thou alone art great!<br> On earth who is great? Thou alone art great!<br> When thy voice resounds in heaven, the gods fall prostrate!<br> When thy voice resounds on earth, the genii kiss the dust!</dir>
