   <h3 align="center">APACHE PRAYER</h3>  
 <p align="center">(<i>American</i>)</p>  
 <p></p><dir>STENATLIHAN, You are good!<br> I pray for a long life.<br> I pray for your good looks.<br> I pray for good breath.<br> I pray for good speech.<br> I pray for feet like yours to carry me through a long life.<br> I pray for a life like yours.<br> I walk with people, ahead of me all is well.<br> I pray for people to smile as long as I live.<br> I pray to live long.<br> I pray, I say, for a long life to live with you where the good people are.<br> I live in poverty.<br> I wish the people there to speak of goodness and to talk to me.<br> I wish you to divide your good things with me, as a brother.<br> Ahead of me is goodness, lead me on.</dir>  
 <p align="center"><font size="-1">(Stenatlihan is the supreme sky goddess)</font></p>
