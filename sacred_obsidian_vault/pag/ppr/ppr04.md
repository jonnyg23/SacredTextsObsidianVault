   <h3 align="center">TO THE CREATIVE GOD</h3>  
 <p align="center">(<i>Accadian</i>)</p>  
 <p><font size="-1">(The Accadian was already a dead language in the Seventeenth Century, B. C.)</font></p>  
 <p></p><dir>O LORD of Charms, Illustrious! who gives<br> Life to the Dead, the Merciful who lives,<br> And grants to hostile gods of Heaven return,<br> To homage render, worship thee, and learn<br> Obedience!<br> Thou who didst create mankind<br> In tenderness, thy love round us, oh wind!<br> The Merciful, the God with whom is Life<br> Establish us, O Lord, in darkest strife O never may thy truth forgotten be.<br> May Accad's race forever worship thee!</dir>
