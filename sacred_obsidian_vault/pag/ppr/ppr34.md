   <h3 align="center">AN INCA'S DEATH PRAYER</h3>  
 <p align="center">(<i>Peruvian</i>)</p>  
 <p></p><dir>O CREATOR of men<br> Thy servant speaks.<br> Then look on him<br> The king of Cusco.  
 <p>Do not forget me<br> O thou noble creator.<br> O thou of my dreams.<br> Dost thou forget<br> And I on the point of death?<br> Wilt thou ignore my prayer<br> Or wilt thou make known<br> Who thou art?<br> Thou mayest be what I thought,<br> Yet perchance thou art a phantom,<br> A thing that causes fear.<br> Oh, if I might know!<br> Oh, if it could be revealed!<br> Thou who made me out of earth,<br> And of clay formed me.<br> Oh look upon me!<br> Who art thou, O Creator?<br> Now I am very old.</p></dir>
