   <h3 align="center">PRAYER TO THE MEXICAN GOD OF FIRE</h3>  
 <p align="center">(<i>Mexican</i>)</p>  
 <p><font size="-1">(A victim is sacrificed for rain in the temple of the Earth Fire--a crater of a volcano)</font></p>  
 <p align="center"><i>Victim</i></p>  
 <p>"TN THE Hall of Flame let me not put to shame my ancestors; <a href="errata.htm#2">descending</a> there let me not put you to shame!</p>  
 <p>"I fasten a rope to the sacred tree. I twist it in eight folds, that by it, I, a magician, may descend to the magical house."</p>  
 <p align="center"><i>Priests</i></p>  
 <p>"Begin your song in the Hall of Flames! Begin your song in the Hall of Flames!"</p>  
 <p align="center"><i>Devotees</i></p>  
 <p>"Why does not the magician come forth?</p>  
 <p>Why does he not rise up?"</p>  
 <p align="center"><i>Priests</i></p>  
 <p>"Let his subjects assist in the Hall of Flames!</p>  
 <p>He appears! He appears! Let his subjects assist!"</p>  
 <p>"Let his servants never cease the song of the Hall of Flames--let them rejoice greatly--let them dance wonderfully!</p>  
 <p align="center">(To the victim)</p>  
 <p>Call ye for the Woman with Abundant Hair, whose care is the Mist and the Rain; call ye for Her!"</p>
