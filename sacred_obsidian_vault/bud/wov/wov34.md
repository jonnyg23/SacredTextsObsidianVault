 <p align="center"><b>Edited by L. CRANMER-BYNG and Dr. S. A. KAPADIA</b></p>  <h3 align="center">THE SERIES AND ITS PURPOSE</h3>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">This</span> Series has a definite object. It is, by means of the best Oriental literature—its wisdom, philosophy, poetry, and ideals—to bring together West and East in a spirit of mutual sympathy, goodwill, and understanding. From India, China, Japan, Persia, Arabia, Palestine, and Egypt these words of wisdom have been gathered.</p>
