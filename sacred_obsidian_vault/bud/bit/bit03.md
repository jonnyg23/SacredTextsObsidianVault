 <p align="CENTER">Group I.</p>
 <p align="CENTER">Representations of Deities.</p>
 <p>Plate I. The thirty-five. Buddhas of Confession, in Tibetan Tungshakchi sangye songs.</p>
 <p>II. Vajrasattva, in Tibetan Dozjesempa, the God above all.</p>
 <p>III. The Dhyâni Buddha Amitâbha, in Tibetan Odpagmed.</p>
 <p>IV. Padmapâni, in Tibetan Chenresi, the particular Protector of Tibet.</p>
 <p>V. Maitreya, in Tibetan Jampa, the Buddha to come,</p>
 <p>VI. Manjusrî, in Tibetan Jamjang, the God of wisdom.</p>
 <p>VII. The goddess Doljang, the deified consort of king Srongtsan Gampo, (A.D. 617-98).</p>
