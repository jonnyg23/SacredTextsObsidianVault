 <p>
 This is a translation of an important treatise of Mahayana
 Buddhism, the Bodhicharyavatara of Santideva,
 part of the Wisdom of the East Series.
 It includes a long introduction by the translator, Barnett,
 who discusses the rise of Mahayana Buddhism, going into some
 depth about the significance of these doctrines.
 </p>
