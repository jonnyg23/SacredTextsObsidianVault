 <p><a name="page_400"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 400</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">3.</h3>  
 <p>1. Now at that time the venerable Udâyi had committed an offence--the first Sa<i>m</i>ghâdisesa--which he had for one day concealed.</p>  
 <p>He told the Bhikkhus (&amp;c., as before).</p>  
 <p>They told this thing to the Blessed One.</p>  
 <p>'Then, O Bhikkhus, let the Sa<i>m</i>gha impose a probation of one day on Udâyi the Bhikkhu for an offence (&amp;c., as in the first paragraph of this section down to) concealed.</p>  
 <p>2. 'Now thus, O Bhikkhus, ought it to be imposed--.'</p>  
 <p>[Here follows the kammavâ<i>k</i>â precisely as in chapter 1, §§ 2 and 3, with the necessary changes in the wording, a. of the offence, b. of the penalty.]</p>
