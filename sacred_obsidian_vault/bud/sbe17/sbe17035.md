   <h3 align="center">21.</h3>  
 <p>1. Now at that time edible fruit was very plentiful in Sâvatthi, but there was no one to make it allowable <a name="fr_178"></a><a href="#fn_176"><font size="1">2</font></a>. And the Bhikkhus, fearing to offend, would not eat of it.</p>  
 <p>They told that thing to the Blessed One.</p>  
 <p>'I allow you, O Bhikkhus, to eat fruit which has not yet had any seed in it, or which has no more seed in it, even without any one being there to make it allowable <a name="fr_179"></a><a href="#fn_177"><font size="1">3</font></a>.'</p>
