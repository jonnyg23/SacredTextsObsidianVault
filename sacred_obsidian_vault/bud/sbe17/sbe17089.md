   <h3 align="center">22.</h3>  
 <p>1. Now at that time a quantity of robes had come into the possession of a certain Bhikkhu, and he was desirous of giving those robes to his father and mother.</p>  
 <p>They told this matter to the Blessed One.</p>  
 <p>'Since they are his father and mother, what can we say, O Bhikkhus, though he give them to them. I allow you, O Bhikkhus, to give (robes, in such a case,) to your parents. And a gift of faith is not to be made of no avail. Whosoever shall make it of no avail, he is guilty of a dukka<i>t</i>a <a name="fr_441"></a><a href="#fn_433"><font size="1">2</font></a>.'</p>
