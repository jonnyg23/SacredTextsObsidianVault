   <h3 align="center">16.</h3>  
 <p>1. When he had undergone that probation he told the Bhikkhus, &amp;c. [all that had happened since chapter 13]</p>  
 <p>They told, &amp;c.</p>  
 <p>'Let then the Sa<i>m</i>gha, O Bhikkhus, impose upon Udâyi the Bhikkhu a Mânatta of six days' duration for these offences <a name="fr_734"></a><a href="#fn_713"><font size="1">1</font></a>.</p>  
 <p>'Now thus, O Bhikkhus, [here follows one kammavâ<i>k</i>â in the form given in chapter 1.]'</p>
