   <h3 align="center">4.</h3>  
 <p>1. Now at that time Bhikkhus, who had rendered themselves liable to be thrown back to the commencement (of their probationary course) <a name="fr_723"></a><a href="#fn_705"><font size="1">2</font></a>, used to</p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p><a name="page_394"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 394</font></a></p>  
 <p>accept reverence [&amp;c., as before in chapter 1, reading throughout 'Bhikkhus liable to be thrown back to the beginning of their probationary course' for 'Bhikkhus who had been placed on probation <a name="fr_724"></a><a href="#fn_706"><font size="1">1</font></a>;' and omitting in chapter 2 the announcements referred to there in our note].</p>
