   <h3 align="center">8.</h3>  
 <p>1. At that time the cloth in the Sa<i>m</i>gha's store-room was not protected (from rain, mice, &amp;c.) They told this thing to the Blessed One.</p>  
 <p>'I prescribe, O Bhikkhus, that you appoint a Bhikkhu possessed of the following five qualities, to take charge of the store-room: (a person) who does not go in the evil course of lust (&amp;c., as in chap. 5, § 1), and who knows what is protected and what is not.</p>  
 <p>'And you ought, O Bhikkhus, to appoint (&amp;c., see chap. 5, § 2):</p>  
 <p>2. At that time the <i>Kh</i>abbaggiya Bhikkhus expelled a Bhikkhu, who had charge of a store-room, from his place.</p>  
 <p>They told this thing to the Blessed One.</p>  
 <p>'Let no one, O Bhikkhus, expel a Bhikkhu, who has charge of a store-room, from his place. He who does so, commits a dukka<i>t</i>a offence.'</p>
