   <h3 align="center">8.</h3>  
 <p>1. When he had undergone that probation, and was liable to the Mânatta, he committed an offence--the first Sa<i>m</i>ghâdisesa--which he did not conceal.</p>  
 <p>He told the Bhikkhus, saying, &amp;c. . . .</p>  
 <p>They told this matter to the Blessed One.</p>  
 <p>'Let then the Sa<i>m</i>gha, O Bhikkhus, throw back Udâyi the Bhikkhu to the commencement (of his probationary course).'</p>  
 <p>2, 3. [Here follows the kammavâ<i>k</i>â in the same form as is given in chapter 1.]</p>
