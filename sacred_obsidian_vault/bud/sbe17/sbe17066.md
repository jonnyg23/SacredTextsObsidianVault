   <h3 align="center">12.</h3>  
 <p>'A Bhikkhu intent on finding a comfortable place (to live in), after the Ka<i>th</i>ina ceremony has been held, takes a robe, and goes away, thinking, "I will go to such and such a residence; if it is comfortable there, I will remain there if it is not, I will go to such and such a residence; if it is comfortable there, I will remain there; if it is not, I will go to such and such a residence; if it is comfortable there, I will remain there; if it is not, I will go back."</p>  
 <p>'When he has got beyond the boundary, he thinks, "I will have the robe made up here, and will never go back," &amp;c. <a name="fr_334"></a><a href="#fn_329"><font size="1">3</font></a>'</p>  
 <p align="center">__________________</p>  
 <p align="center">End of the five cases of the Bhikkhu intent on comfort.</p>
