   <h3 align="center">10.</h3>  
 <p>1. Now at that time the Bhikkhus who were sick had need of sifted <a name="fr_118"></a><a href="#fn_116"><font size="1">4</font></a> chunam as medicine.</p>  
 <p>They told this thing to the Blessed One.</p>  
 <p>'I allow, O Bhikkhus, the use of a chunam sieve.'</p>  
 <p>They had need of the chunam very fine.</p>  
 <p>'I allow, O Bhikkhus, the use of a cloth sieve.'</p>  
 <p>2. Now at that time a certain Bhikkhu had a disease not human. Though his teacher and his superior nursed him, they were not able to make him well. He went to a place where swine were slaughtered, and ate the raw flesh, and drank the blood. Thereby his sickness abated.</p>  
 <p>They told this thing to the Blessed One.</p>  
 <p>'I allow, O Bhikkhus, in the case of a disease not human, the use of raw flesh and of blood.'</p>
