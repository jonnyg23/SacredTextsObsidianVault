   <h3 align="center">22.</h3>  
 <p>1. Now at that time a certain Bhikkhu had committed two Sa<i>m</i>ghâdisesa offences which he had concealed for two months. And it occurred to him, 'Lo! I have committed two Sa<i>m</i>ghâdisesa offences which I have concealed for two months. Let me now ask the Sa<i>m</i>gha for a probation of two months for one offence concealed for two months.' And he asked the Sa<i>m</i>gha for a probation of two months for</p>  
 <p><a name="page_411"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 411</font></a></p>  
 <p>one offence concealed for two months. And the Sa<i>m</i>gha imposed upon him a probation (&amp;c., as before, down to) for two months. Whilst he was undergoing that probation, shame overcame him in that he thought, 'I have committed, &amp;c. . . . And it occurred to me, &amp;c. . . . And I asked, &amp;c. . . . And the Sa<i>m</i>gha imposed . . . . And whilst I was undergoing . . . . (&amp;c., as before, down to) for two months.'</p>  
 <p>'Let me now ask the Sa<i>m</i>gha for a probation of two months for the other offence concealed for two months.'</p>  
 <p>2. He told the Bhikkhus [all that had happened]. They told this thing to the Blessed One.</p>  
 <p>3. 'Let then the Sa<i>m</i>gha, O Bhikkhus, impose upon that Bhikkhu a probation of two months for that other offence concealed for two months.'</p>  
 <p>[Here follows the kammavâ<i>k</i>â as before.]</p>  
 <p>'Then that Bhikkhu, O Bhikkhus, should undergo probation for two months from that date <a name="fr_737"></a><a href="#fn_716"><font size="1">1</font></a>.'</p>
