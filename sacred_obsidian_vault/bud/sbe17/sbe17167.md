   <h3 align="center">13.</h3>  
 <p>1. Now at that time the venerable Udâyi committed an offence--the first Sa<i>m</i>ghâdisesa--and for half a month he concealed it.</p>  
 <p>[The rest of this chapter is precisely the same as chapter 3, reading 'for half a month' instead of 'for one day.']</p>
