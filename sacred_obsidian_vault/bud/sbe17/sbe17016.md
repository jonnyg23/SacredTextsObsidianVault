   <h3 align="center">2.</h3>  
 <p>1. Now at that time the Bhikkhus who were sick had need of fatty substances as medicine.</p>  
 <p>They told this thing to the Blessed One.</p>  
 <p>'I allow you, O Bhikkhus, the use of the fat of bears, of fish, of alligators, of swine, and of asses, if received at the right time, cooked at the right time, mixed at the right time, to be partaken of with oil.'</p>  
 <p>2. 'If the fat be received, O Bhikkhus, at the wrong time, cooked at the wrong time, and mixed at the wrong time, and then taken, the Bhikkhu is guilty of three dukka<i>t</i>a offences.'</p>  
 <p>'If it be received, O Bhikkhus, at the right time, cooked at the wrong time, and mixed at the wrong</p>  
 <p><a name="page_44"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 44</font></a></p>  
 <p>time, and then taken, the Bhikkhu is guilty of two dukka<i>t</i>a offences.'</p>  
 <p>'If it be received, O Bhikkhus, at the right time, cooked at the right time, and mixed at the wrong time, and then taken, the Bhikkhu is guilty of a dukka<i>t</i>a offence.'</p>  
 <p>'If it be received, O Bhikkhus, at the right time, cooked at the right time, and mixed at the right time, and then taken, the Bhikkhu is not guilty.'</p>
