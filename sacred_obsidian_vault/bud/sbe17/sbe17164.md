   <h3 align="center">10.</h3>  
 <p>1. While he was undergoing that Mânatta he</p>  
 <p><a name="page_403"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 403</font></a></p>  
 <p>committed an offence--the first Sa<i>m</i>ghâdisesa which he did not conceal.</p>  
 <p>He told the Bhikkhus, saying, 'I committed, &amp;c. . . . I asked the Sa<i>m</i>gha. . . . The Sa<i>m</i>gha imposed [&amp;c., going through all that had happened, down to the end of the first paragraph in this chapter].</p>  
 <p>They told, &amp;c.</p>  
 <p>'Let then the Sa<i>m</i>gha, O Bhikkhus, impose upon Udâyi the Bhikkhu a Mânatta of six days' duration, throwing him back to the commencement (of his Mânatta).</p>  
 <p>[The kammavâ<i>k</i>â as before.]</p>
