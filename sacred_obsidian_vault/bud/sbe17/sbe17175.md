   <h3 align="center">21.</h3>  
 <p>1. Now at that time a certain Bhikkhu had committed numerous Sa<i>m</i>ghâdisesa offences, one of which he had concealed for one day, two for two days, three for three days, [and so on down to] and ten for ten days.</p>  
 <p>He told, &amp;c.</p>  
 <p>They told, &amp;c.</p>  
 <p>'Let then the Sa<i>m</i>gha, O Bhikkhus, impose upon that Bhikkhu an inclusive probation equal in duration to the longest time during which he has concealed any one or more of those offences <a name="fr_736"></a><a href="#fn_715"><font size="1">1</font></a>.'</p>  
 <p>[Then follows the kammavâ<i>k</i>â as before.]</p>
