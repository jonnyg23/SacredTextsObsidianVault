   <h3 align="center">15.</h3>  
 <p>1. When he had undergone that probation, and while he was liable to the Mânatta, he committed an offence--the first Sa<i>m</i>ghâdisesa--which for five days he concealed.</p>  
 <p>He told, &amp;c. . . . [all that happened, from chapter 1 3 downwards].</p>  
 <p>They told, &amp;c.</p>  
 <p>'Let then the Sa<i>m</i>gha, O Bhikkhus, for that he when he had (&amp;c., as in the first paragraph) throw Udâyi the Bhikkhu back to the commencement of</p>  
 <p><a name="page_406"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 406</font></a></p>  
 <p>his probationary term, and impose upon him an inclusive probation (for this and) for the former offence.</p>
