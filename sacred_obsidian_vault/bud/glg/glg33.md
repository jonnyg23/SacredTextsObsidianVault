 <p></p>  <h3 align="center">33. This Mind Is Not Buddha</h3>  
 <p>A monk asked Baso: "What is Buddha?"</p>  
 <p>Baso said: "This mind is not Buddha."</p>  
 <p> </p>  
 <p><i>Mumon's comment:</i> If anyone understands this, he is a graduate of Zen.</p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px"><i>If you meet a fencing-master on the road, you may give him your sword,<br> If you meet a poet, you may offer him your poem.<br> When you meet others, say only a part of what you intend.<br> Never give the whole thing at once</i>.</div>
