 <p align="center">THE SERIES AND ITS PURPOSE</p>  
 <p>This Series has a definite object. It is, by means of the best Oriental literature—its wisdom, philosophy, poetry, and ideals —to bring together West and East in a spirit of mutual sympathy, goodwill, and understanding. From India, China, Japan, Persia, Arabia, Palestine, and Egypt these words of wisdom have been gathered.</p>
