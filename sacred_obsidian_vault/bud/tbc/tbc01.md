 <p><a name="page_iii"><font size="1" color="green">p. iii</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">DEDICATION.</h3>  
 <p>In token of respect and affection I dedicate to my counsellor and friend of many years, Hikkaduwe Sumangala, Pradhâna Nâyaka Sthavira and High Priest of Adam's Peak (Sripada) and the Western Province, the "Buddhist Catechism," in its revised form.</p>  
 <p align="right">H. S. OLCOTT.</p>  
 <p>ADYAR, 1903.</p>
