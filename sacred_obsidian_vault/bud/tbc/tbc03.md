 <p><a name="page_vi"><font size="1" color="green">p. vi</font></a> <a name="page_vii"><font size="1" color="green">p. vii</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">CERTIFICATE</h3>  <h3 align="center">TO THE FIRST EDITION.</h3>  
 <p align="right"><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Vidyodaya College</span>,    <br> <i>Colombo</i>, <i>7th July</i>, 1881.</p>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">I hereby</span> certify that I have carefully examined the Sinhalese version of the catechism prepared by Colonel H. S. Olcott, and that the same is in agreement with the Canon of the Southern Buddhist Church. I recommend the work to teachers in Buddhist schools, and to all others who may wish to impart information to beginners about the essential features of our religion.</p>  
 <p align="right"><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">H. Sumangala</span>,<br> <i>High Priest of the Sripada &amp; Galle, and Principal of the Vidyodaya Parivena</i>.</p>  
 <p align="right"> </p>  
 <p align="center">_____________</p>  
 <p align="right"><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Vidyodaya College</span>,    <br> <i>April 7th</i>, 1897.</p>  
 <p>I have gone over the thirty-third (English) edition of the catechism, with the help of interpreters, and. confirm my recommendation for its use in Buddhist schools.</p>  
 <p align="right">H. SUMANGALA.</p>
