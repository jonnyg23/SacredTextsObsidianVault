 <h1 align="CENTER">THE AWAKENING OF FAITH</h1>
 <h2 align="CENTER">OPENING HYMN</h2><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <p>I yield my life to the All,<br>
 To the All, SOUL, full of good,<br>
 In wisdom all complete,<br>
 In power all divine,<br>
 In pity--would save all.</p>
 <p>To LAW which does embody<br>
 The Archetype of all.</p>
 <p>To CHURCH which does contain<br>
 The Archetype in Seed,</p>
 <p>That men may be delivered<br>
 From doubt and evil ways;<br>
 Get Faith in the Great School<br>
 Perpetuating God!</p></dir>
 </dir>
