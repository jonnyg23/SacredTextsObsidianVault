 <p><a name="page_122"><font size="1" color="green">p. 122</font></a> <a name="page_123"><font size="1" color="green">p. 123</font></a></p>  <h1 align="center">PART II</h1>  <h1 align="center">THE SIX YOGAS OF NAROPA</h1>  <h3 align="center">[IN TSONG-KHA-PA'S COMMENTARY] <a name="fr_6"></a><a href="#fn_6"><font size="1">*</font></a></h3>  <h3 align="center">ENTERING THE PROFOUND PATH THROUGH THE SUCCESSIVE TEACHING OF The Six Yogas of Naropa, WHICH IS NAMED The Book of the Three Faiths<a href="ettt22.htm#an_n_nar:1">1</a><a name="an_r_nar:1"></a>.</h3>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p><a name="page_124"><font size="1" color="green">p. 124</font></a> <a name="page_125"><font size="1" color="green">p. 125</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center"><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Prologue</span></h3>  
 <p>With great respect I bow down to the Lotus-Feet of the revered gurus who are identical with the great Vajra Dhara<a href="ettt22.htm#an_n_nar:2">2</a><a name="an_r_nar:2"></a>.</p>  
 <p>I prostrate myself in front of His supreme Body, Mouth and Mind!</p>  
 <p>I bow down to my guru and his omnipresent miraculous powers!</p>  
 <p>He who grants all wishes (of ours),</p>  
 <p>He who possesses all the merits and virtues,</p>  
 <p>He who is the mighty master, the embodiment of Vajra Dhara!</p>  
 <p>To him, the mighty lord of eight merits, I render my obeisance,</p>  
 <p>Gathering all the quintessence of the profound Tantras.</p>  
 <p>The accomplished masters Deropa and Naropa, through their lineage, handed down this teaching!</p>  
 <p>It is known as the Six Yogas of Naropa,</p>  
 <p>It is the teaching heard in all directions!</p>  
 <p>Following its path, the hard-working and well-gifted disciples</p>  
 <p>Are led to the plane of the saviours!</p>  
 <p>Through a careful and continuous study</p>  
 <p>Of the teachings by the successive gurus in the lineage,</p>  
 <p>I now elucidate for you the way to liberation!</p>
