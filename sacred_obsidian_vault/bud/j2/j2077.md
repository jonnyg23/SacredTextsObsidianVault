 <p><a name="page_145"><font size="1" color="green">p. 145</font></a> <a name="an_j224"></a></p>  <h3 align="center">No. 224.</h3>  <h3 align="center">KUMBHĪLA-JĀTAKA.</h3>  
 <p>"<i>O Ape</i>," <i>etc</i>.--This story the Master told at the Bamboo Grove, about Devadatta.</p>  
 <p align="center">_____________________________</p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px"><small>"O Ape, these virtues four bring victory:<br> Truth, Wisdom, Self-control, and Piety.  
 </small><p>"Without these blessings is no victory--<br> Truth, Wisdom, Self-control, and Piety."</p></div>
