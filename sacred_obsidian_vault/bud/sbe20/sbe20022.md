   <h3 align="center">7.</h3>  
 <p>1. Now at that time a certain Bhikkhu, tormented by distaste (for meditation, &amp;c.), castrated himself <a name="fr_159"></a><a href="#fn_154"><font size="1">3</font></a>. They told this matter to the Blessed One.</p>  
 <p>'When one thing wanted cutting off, O Bhikkhus, that foolish fellow has cut off another! You are</p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p><a name="page_78"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 78</font></a></p>  
 <p>not, O Bhikkhus, to castrate yourselves. Whosoever does so, shall be guilty of a thulla<i>k</i><i>k</i>aya.'</p>
