   <h3 align="center">7.</h3>  
 <p>1. Now at that time Bhikkhunîs in Sa<i>m</i>gha assembled, having fallen into quarrel, strife, and dispute, got to blows, and were unable to settle the point at issue. They told this matter to the Blessed One.</p>  
 <p>'I allow Bhikkhus, O Bhikkhus, to settle for Bhikkhunîs a point at issue.'</p>  
 <p>Now at that time Bhikkhus were settling for Bhikkhunîs a point at issue, and as the point at issue was being enquired into, it was found that both Bhikkhunîs competent to take part in an official act <a name="fr_880"></a><a href="#fn_866"><font size="1">1</font></a>, and Bhikkhunîs who had been guilty of an offence (had taken part in the Sa<i>m</i>gha during the official act (the Kamma) at which the point in issue arose).</p>  
 <p>The Bhikkhunîs said: 'It would be well if you, Sirs, would carry out disciplinary proceedings against the guilty Bhikkhunîs, or absolve their offence: for</p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p><a name="page_334"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 334</font></a></p>  
 <p>thus has it been laid down by the Blessed One: "Bhikkhus are to settle for the Bhikkhunîs a point at issue."'</p>  
 <p>They told this matter to the Blessed One.</p>  
 <p>'I allow Bhikkhus, O Bhikkhus, to set on foot <a name="fr_881"></a><a href="sbe20099.htm#fn_867"><font size="1">1</font></a> an official act for Bhikkhunîs, and then to give it in charge for Bhikkhunîs to carry out the official act against Bhikkhunîs. And I allow Bhikkhus to set on foot the (dealing with an) offence for Bhikkhunîs, and then to give it in charge for Bhikkhunîs to accept the confession thereof.'</p>
