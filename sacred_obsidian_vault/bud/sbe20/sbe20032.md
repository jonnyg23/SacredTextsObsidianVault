 <p><a name="page_114"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 114</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">17.</h3>  
 <p>1. Now at that time the Bhikkhus used to bathe anywhere all over the Ârâma, and the Ârâma became muddy.</p>  
 <p>They told this matter to the Blessed One.</p>  
 <p>'I allow you, O Bhikkhus, a pool (at the entrance to the Ârâma).'</p>  
 <p>The pool was public, and the Bhikkhus were ashamed to bathe in it.</p>  
 <p>'I allow you, O Bhikkhus, to surround it with enclosures of three kinds--brick walls, stone walls, and wooden fences <a name="fr_300"></a><a href="#fn_294"><font size="1">1</font></a>.'</p>  
 <p>The pool became muddy.</p>  
 <p>'I allow you, O Bhikkhus, to flag it with three kinds of flooring--brick flooring, stone flooring, or wooden flooring.'</p>  
 <p>The water settled.</p>  
 <p>'I allow you, O Bhikkhus, a drain.'</p>  
 <p>Now at that time the Bhikkhus' limbs became cold.</p>  
 <p>They told this matter to the Blessed One.</p>  
 <p>'I allow you, O Bhikkhus, to make use of a towel <a name="fr_301"></a><a href="#fn_295"><font size="1">2</font></a>, and to wipe the water off with a cloth.'</p>  
 <p>2. Now at that time a certain Upâsaka was desirous of making a tank for the use of the Sa<i>m</i>gha.</p>  
 <p>They told this matter to the Blessed One.</p>  
 <p>'I allow you, O Bhikkhus, a tank.'</p>  
 <p>The sides of the tank fell in <a name="fr_302"></a><a href="#fn_296"><font size="1">3</font></a>.</p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p><a name="page_115"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 115</font></a></p>  
 <p>'I allow you, O Bhikkhus, to line the tank with facing of three kinds--brick facing, stone facing, and wooden facing.'</p>  
 <p>They found difficulty <a name="fr_303"></a><a href="#fn_297"><font size="1">1</font></a> in getting into it.</p>  
 <p>'I allow you, O Bhikkhus, flights of stairs of three kinds--brick steps, stone steps, and wooden steps.'</p>  
 <p>While going up them, they fell down.</p>  
 <p>'I allow you, O Bhikkhus, a balustrade.'</p>  
 <p>The water in the tank became stale.</p>  
 <p>'I allow you, O Bhikkhus, the use of pipes to lay on the water <a name="fr_304"></a><a href="#fn_298"><font size="1">2</font></a>, and to drain the water off <a name="fr_305"></a><a href="#fn_299"><font size="1">3</font></a>.'</p>  
 <p>Now at that time a certain Bhikkhu was desirous of erecting a bath-room with a nillekha <a name="fr_306"></a><a href="#fn_300"><font size="1">4</font></a> roof for the use of the Sa<i>m</i>gha.</p>  
 <p>They told this matter to the Blessed One.</p>  
 <p>'I allow you, O Bhikkhus, a bath-room with such a roof to it.'</p>
