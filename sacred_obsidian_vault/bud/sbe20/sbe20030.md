   <h3 align="center">15.</h3>  
 <p>1. Now at that time the Bhikkhus, when naked <a name="fr_283"></a><a href="#fn_277"><font size="1">2</font></a>, saluted one another, and received salutes; did service to one another, and received services; gave to one another, and accepted; ate, both hard food and soft; tasted; and drank.</p>  
 <p>They told this matter to the Blessed One.</p>  
 <p>'A man, O Bhikkhus, when naked, is not to give salutations, nor receive them; is not to do services, nor to accept them <a name="fr_284"></a><a href="#fn_278"><font size="1">3</font></a>; is not to give, nor to receive; is not to eat either hard or soft; is not to taste; is not to drink. Whosoever does so, shall be guilty of a dukka<i>t</i>a.'</p>
