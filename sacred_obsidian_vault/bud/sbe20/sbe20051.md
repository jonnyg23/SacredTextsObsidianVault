   <h3 align="center">36.</h3>  
 <p>Now at that time the <i>Kh</i>abbaggiya Bhikkhus followed evil practices such as these--they used to plant [&amp;c., word for word as in the long list at I, 13, I, 2, down to the end].</p>  
 <p>They told this matter to the Blessed One.</p>  
 <p>'You are not, O Bhikkhus, to follow manifold evil practices. Whosoever does so, shall be dealt with according to the law.'</p>
