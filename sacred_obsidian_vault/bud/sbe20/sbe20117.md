   <h3 align="center">26.</h3>  
 <p>1. Now at that time a certain Bhikkhunî first abandoned the precepts, and then threw off the robes <a name="fr_956"></a><a href="#fn_940"><font size="1">1</font></a>. Afterwards she came back, and asked the Bhikkhunîs to admit her into the Order again.</p>  
 <p>They told this matter to the Blessed One.</p>  
 <p>'A Bhikkhunî ought not, O Bhikkhus, to abandon the precepts. But by having thrown off the robes she is ipso facto incapable of being a Bhikkhunî.'</p>  
 <p>2. Now at that time a certain Bhikkhunî, though still wearing the orange-coloured robe, joined a sect of the Titthiyas. Afterwards she came back, and asked the Bhikkhunîs to receive her into the Order again.</p>  
 <p>They told this matter to the Blessed One. .</p>  
 <p>'A Bhikkhunî, O Bhikkhus, who, when still wearing the orange-coloured robe, has gone over to the Titthiyas, is not to be received again <a name="fr_957"></a><a href="#fn_941"><font size="1">2</font></a>.'</p>
