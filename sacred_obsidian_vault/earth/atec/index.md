 <p>
 Burroughs set this classic Scifi/Fantasy novel in a version of the Hollow
 Earth which can only be reached by an advanced tunneling machine.
 Breaking through into the inner core, the adventurers find
 a world (Pellucidar) which
 physically resmbles Cyrus Teed's
 <a href="../cc/index.htm">Cellular Cosmology</a>,
 except that we are on the <i>outside</i>.
 <i>Inside</i> is a primitive world where dinosaurs still exist, and humans
 are not at the top of the food chain...
