 <p><a name="page_11"><font size="1" color="green">p. 11</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">INDICATIONS ON THE DIAGRAM.</h3>  
 <p>A, A, A, A, Corner sockets of the Pyramid's base.</p>  
 <p>B, B, B, Pyramid cut in half, viewed from the east.</p>  
 <p>C, C, C, Entrance passage.</p>  
 <p>D, D, First ascending passage.</p>  
 <p>E, E, E, The well.</p>  
 <p>F, The subterranean chamber.</p>  
 <p>G, G, G, Native rock, left standing.</p>  
 <p>H, Horizontal passage to Queen's Chamber.</p>  
 <p>I, Sabbatic or Queen's Chamber.</p>  
 <p>J, Grand niche in Queen's Chamber.</p>  
 <p>K, K, Ventilating tubes to Queen's Chamber.</p>  
 <p>L, Grand Gallery.</p>  
 <p>M, M, M, Rampstones, incisions, and vertical settings along the sides of Grand Gallery's base.</p>  
 <p>N, Great step at south end of Grand Gallery.</p>  
 <p>O, Granite leaf in anteroom to King's Chamber.</p>  
 <p>P, P, Anteroom to King's Chamber.</p>  
 <p>Q, King's Chamber.</p>  
 <p>R. Grand Coffer in King's Chamber.</p>  
 <p>S, S, S, S, S, Chambers of construction.</p>  
 <p>T, T, Ventilating tubes to King's Chamber.</p>  
 <p>U, Supposed undiscovered Chamber.</p>  
 <p>V, V, Cartouches of the two Kings, Shufu and Nem-Shufu, otherwise called Cheops or Suphis, and Sen-Suphis or Noh-Suphis, under whose co-regency the Great Pyramid was built.</p>  
 <p>W, W, Sections of next two pyramids, showing their interior openings.</p>  
 <p>X, X, Al Mamoun's forced passage.</p>  
 <p>Y, Time-marks of the building of the pyramid.</p>  
 <p>Z, Z, Z, Z, Casing-stones, now gone.</p>
