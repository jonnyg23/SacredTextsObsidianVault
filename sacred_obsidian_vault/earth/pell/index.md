 <p>
 In this, the first (of many) sequels to
 <a href="../atec/index.htm">At the Earth's Core</a>,
 David Innes returns to Pellucidar, the world inside the Earth.
 Burroughs would revisit Pellucidar in later yarns
 with his consumate hero, Tarzan.
 But for now, this wraps up the first two Burroughs Hollow Earth novels
 nicely, as (spoiler alert) Innes saves the <i>inner</i> world and
 reclaims his lost love, Dian, from his arch-enemy.
 </p>
