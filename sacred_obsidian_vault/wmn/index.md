 <p>
 All of the major world religions deprecate women to some degree.
 This page archives texts which relate specifically to women and religion
 from a female perspective.
 This includes <a href="#feminist">historic feminist texts</a> on the topic,
 texts about <a href="#goddess">Goddess-oriented sprituality</a> and
 <a href="#womenwarriors">Amazons</a>, as well as
 <a href="#other">texts from otherwise male-dominated religions</a>
 in the same vein.
 As might be expected, there are far fewer of these than might be desired.
 </p>
