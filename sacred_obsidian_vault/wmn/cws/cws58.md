 <h3 align="CENTER">No. II</h3>
 <h4 align="CENTER">THE "LORD'S PRAYER"</h4>
 <i></i><p align="CENTER">being a prayer of the Elect for interior Perfectionment</p>
 <p>OUR Father-Mother who art in the upper and the within: Hallowed be Thy name:</p>
 <p>Thy kingdom come:</p>
 <p>Thy will be done, in the body as in the spirit:</p>
 <p>Give us every day the communion of the mystical bread:</p>
 <p>And perfect us in the power of Thy Sons, according as we give ourselves to perfect others:</p>
 <p>And in the hour of temptation deliver us from the hand of Satan.</p>
 <p>For Thine are the kingdom, the power, and the glory.</p>
 <p>In the life eternal, and in the Amen.[A]</p>
