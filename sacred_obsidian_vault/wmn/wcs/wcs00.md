 <p>    This Book is <b>Inscribed</b> to the Memory of my Mother, who was at once mother, sister, friend:</p>
 <b></b><p>    Dedicated to all Christian women and men, of whatever creed or name who, bound by Church or State, have not dared to Think for Themselves:</p>
 <b></b><p>    Addressed to all Persons, who, breaking away from custom and the usage of ages, dare seek Truth for the sake of Truth. To all such it will be welcome; to all others, aggressive and educational.</p>
