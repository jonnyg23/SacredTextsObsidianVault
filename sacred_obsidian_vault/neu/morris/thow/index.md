 <p>
 This is one of William Morris' fantasy books, set in the world of the
 ancient Germanic Gothic tribes.
 J.R.R. Tolkien acknowledged the literary influence of this book,
 as well as <a href="../rotm/index.htm">The Roots of the Mountains</a>,
 published in the same year.
 In this book, Morris' Goths live in a land called the Mark on a river
 in the forest of Mirkwood, in ancent Germany.
 They worship the Germanic gods Tyr and Odin by horse-sacrifice.
 Morris' Goths have shamans who can see the future.
 They repel an invasion by the Romans, led by a war leader with a mysterious
 piece of cursed dwarf-forged armor.
