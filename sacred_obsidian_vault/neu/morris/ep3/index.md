 <p>This is the third part of a three part etext of William Morris' epic-length
 poem, <cite>The Earthly Paradise</cite>.
 In this section Morris weaves more tales from Northern and Classical mythology;
 including the tale of Hercules and the Golden Apples;
 Aslaug, the daughter of Sigurd and Brynhild;
 the myth of Bellerophon; and two tales of the Goddess Venus.
 </p><p>
 Confusingly, this is actually part IV of the original edition (the first volume was conidered part I and II).
 </p>
