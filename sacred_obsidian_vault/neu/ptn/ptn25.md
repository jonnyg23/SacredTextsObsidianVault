 <p><a name="page_105"><font size="1" color="green">p. 105</font></a></p>  <h1 align="center">The Cock and Hen</h1>  
 <p>[In this tale the notes of the Cock and Hen must be imitated.]</p>  
 <p><i>Hen</i>—"You promise me shoes year after year, year after year, and yet I get no shoes!"</p>  
 <p><i>Cock</i>—"You shall have them, never fear! Henny penny!"</p>  
 <p><i>Hen</i>—"I lay egg after egg, egg after egg, and yet I go about barefoot!"</p>  
 <p><i>Cock</i>—Well, take your eggs, and be off to the tryst, and buy yourself shoes, and don't go any longer barefoot!"</p>
