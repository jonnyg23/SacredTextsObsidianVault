 <p><a name="page_9"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 9</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">THE FISHERMAN</h3>  
 <p>ALTHOUGH you hide in the ebb and flow<br> Of the pale tide when the moon has set,<br> The people of coming days will know<br> About the casting out of my net,<br> And how you have leaped times out of mind<br> Over the little silver cords,<br> And think that you were hard and unkind,<br> And blame you with many bitter words.</p>
