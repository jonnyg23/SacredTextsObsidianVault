 <p><a name="page_45"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 45</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">HE WISHES FOR THE CLOTHS OF HEAVEN</h3>  
 <p>HAD I the heavens' embroidered cloths,<br> Enwrought with golden and silver light,<br> The blue and the dim and the dark cloths<br> Of night and light and the half light,<br> I would spread the cloths under your feet:<br> But I, being poor, have only my dreams;<br> I have spread my dreams under your feet;<br> Tread softly because you tread on my dreams.</p>
