 <p><a name="page_352"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 352</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">A MEDITATION IN TIME OF WAR</h3>  
 <p>FOR one throb of the Artery,<br> While on that old grey stone I sat<br> Under the old wind-broken tree,<br> I knew that One is animate<br> Mankind inanimate phantasy.</p>
