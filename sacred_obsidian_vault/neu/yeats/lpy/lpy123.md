 <p><a name="page_255"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 255</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">THE SCHOLARS</h3>  
 <p>BALD heads forgetful of their sins,<br> Old, learned, respectable bald heads<br> Edit and annotate the lines<br> That young men, tossing on their beds,<br> Rhymed out in love's despair<br> To flatter beauty's ignorant ear.</p>  
 <p>They'll cough in the ink to the world's end;<br> Wear out the carpet with their shoes<br> Earning respect; have no strange friend;<br> If they have sinned nobody knows.<br> Lord, what would they say<br> Should their Catullus walk that way?</p>
