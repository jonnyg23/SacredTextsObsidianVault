 <p><a name="page_38"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 38</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">MAID QUIET</h3>  
 <p>WHERE has Maid Quiet gone to,<br> Nodding her russet hood?<br> The winds that awakened the stars<br> Are blowing through my blood.<br> O how could I be so calm<br> When she rose up to depart?<br> Now words that called up the lightning<br> Are hurtling through my heart.</p>
