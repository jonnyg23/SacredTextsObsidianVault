 <p><a name="page_20"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 20</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">HE REPROVES THE CURLEW</h3>  
 <p>O, CURLEW, cry no more in the air,<br> Or only to the water in the West;<br> Because your crying brings to my mind<br> Passion-dimmed eyes and long heavy hair<br> That was shaken out over my breast:<br> There is enough evil in the crying of wind.</p>
