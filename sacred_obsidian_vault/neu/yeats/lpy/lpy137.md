 <p><a name="page_283"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 283</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">A DEEP-SWORN VOW</h3>  
 <p>OTHERS because you did not keep<br> That deep-sworn vow have been friends of mine;<br> Yet always when I look death in the face,<br> When I clamber to the heights of sleep,<br> Or when I grow excited with wine,<br> Suddenly I meet your face.</p>
