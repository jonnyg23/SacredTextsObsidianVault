 <p><a name="page_229"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 229</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">THAT THE NIGHT COME</h3>  
 <p>SHE lived in storm and strife,<br> Her soul had such desire<br> For what proud death may bring<br> That it could not endure<br> The common good of life,<br> But lived as ’twere a king<br> That packed his marriage day<br> With banneret and pennon,<br> Trumpet and kettledrum,<br> And the outrageous cannon,<br> To bundle time away<br> That the night come.</p>
