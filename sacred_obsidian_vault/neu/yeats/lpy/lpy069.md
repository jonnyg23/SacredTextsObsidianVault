 <p><a name="page_159"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 159</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">THE COMING OF WISDOM WITH TIME</h3>  
 <p>THOUGH leaves are many, the root is one;<br> Through all the lying days of my youth<br> I swayed my leaves and flowers in the sun;<br> Now I may wither into the truth.</p>
