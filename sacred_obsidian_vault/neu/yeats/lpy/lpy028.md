 <p><a name="page_33"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 33</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">HE THINKS OF THOSE WHO HAVE SPOKEN EVIL OF HIS BELOVED</h3>  
 <p>HALF close your eyelids, loosen your hair,<br> And dream about the great and their pride;<br> They have spoken against you everywhere,<br> But weigh this song with the great and their pride;<br> I made it out of a mouthful of air,<br> Their children's children shall say they have lied.</p>
