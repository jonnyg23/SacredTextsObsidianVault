 <p><a name="page_218"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 218</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">THE REALISTS</h3>  
 <p>HOPE that you may understand!<br> What can books of men that wive<br> In a dragon-guarded land,<br> Paintings of the dolphin-drawn<br> Sea-nymphs in their pearly waggons<br> Do, but awake a hope to live<br> That had gone<br> With the dragons?</p>
