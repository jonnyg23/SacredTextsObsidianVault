 <p><a name="page_82"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 82</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">THE OLD MEN ADMIRING THEMSELVES IN THE WATER</h3>  
 <p>I HEARD the old, old men say,<br> "Everything alters,<br> And one by one we drop away."<br> They had hands like claws, and their knees<br> Were twisted like the old thorn trees<br> By the waters.<br> I heard the old, old men say,<br> "All that's beautiful drifts away<br> Like the waters."</p>
