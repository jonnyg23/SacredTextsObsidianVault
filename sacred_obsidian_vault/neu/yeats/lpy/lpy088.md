 <p><a name="page_201"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 201</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">WHEN HELEN LIVED</h3>  
 <p>WE have cried in our despair<br> That men desert,<br> For some trivial affair<br> Or noisy, insolent, sport,<br> Beauty that we have won<br> From bitterest hours;<br> Yet we, had we walked within<br> Those topless towers<br> Where Helen walked with her boy,<br> Had given but as the rest<br> Of the men and women of Troy,<br> A word and a jest.</p>
