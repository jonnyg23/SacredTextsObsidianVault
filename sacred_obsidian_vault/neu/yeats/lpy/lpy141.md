 <p><a name="page_287"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 287</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">ON BEING ASKED FOR A WAR POEM</h3>  
 <p>I THINK it better that in times like these<br> A poet keep his mouth shut, for in truth<br> We have no gift to set a statesman right;<br> He has had enough of meddling who can please<br> A young girl in the indolence of her youth,<br> Or an old man upon a winter's night.</p>
