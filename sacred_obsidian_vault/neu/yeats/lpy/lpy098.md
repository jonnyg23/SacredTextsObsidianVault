 <p><a name="page_220"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 220</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">II</h3>  <h3 align="center">THE PEACOCK</h3>  
 <p>WHAT'S riches to him<br> That has made a great peacock<br> With the pride of his eye?<br> The wind-beaten, stone-grey,<br> And desolate Three-rock<br> Would nourish his whim.<br> Live he or die<br> Amid wet rocks and heather,<br> His ghost will be gay<br> Adding feather to feather<br> For the pride of his eye.</p>
