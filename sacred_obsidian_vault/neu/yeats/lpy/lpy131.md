 <p><a name="page_272"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 272</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">MEMORY</h3>  
 <p>ONE had a lovely face,<br> And two or three had charm,<br> But charm and face were in vain<br> Because the mountain grass<br> Cannot but keep the form<br> Where the mountain hare has lain.</p>
