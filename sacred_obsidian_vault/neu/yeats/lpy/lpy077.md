 <p><a name="page_167"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 167</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">A FRIEND'S ILLNESS</h3>  
 <p>SICKNESS brought me this<br> Thought, in that scale of his:<br> Why should I be dismayed<br> Though flame had burned the whole<br> World, as it were a coal,<br> Now I have seen it weighed<br> Against a soul?</p>
