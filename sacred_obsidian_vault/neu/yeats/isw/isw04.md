 <h3 align="CENTER">THE ARROW.</h3>
 <p>I thought of your beauty and this arrow<br>
 Made out of a wild thought is in my marrow.<br>
 There's no man may look upon her, no man,<br>
 As when newly grown to be a woman,<br>
 Blossom pale, she pulled down the pale blossom<br>
 At the moth hour and hid it in her bosom.<br>
 This beauty's kinder yet for a reason<br>
 I could weep that the old is out of season.</p>
