 <h3 align="CENTER">THE OLD MEN ADMIRING THEMSELVES IN THE WATER.</h3>
 <p>I heard the old, old men say<br>
 'Everything alters,<br>
 And one by one we drop away.' <a name="page_27"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 27</font></a><br>
 They had hands like claws, and their knees<br>
 Were twisted like the old thorn trees<br>
 By the waters.<br>
 I heard the old, old men say<br>
 'All that's beautiful drifts away<br>
 Like the waters.'</p>
