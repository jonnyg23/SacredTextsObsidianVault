 <h1>Riddle 35</h1>
 <p><cite>Verse Indeterminate Saxon</cite></p>
 <dl compact>
 <dt></dt>
 <dd>
 Mec se wæta wong,      wundrum freorig, <br>
 of his innaþe      ærist cende. <br>
 Ne wat ic mec beworhtne      wulle flysum, <br>
 hærum þurh heahcræft,      hygeþoncum min. <br>
 </dd>

 <dd>
 Wundene me ne beoð wefle,      ne ic wearp hafu, <br>
 ne þurh þreata geþræcu      þræd me ne hlimmeð, <br>
 ne æt me hrutende      hrisil scriþeð, <br>
 ne mec ohwonan      sceal <strong>am</strong> cnyssan. <br>
 Wyrmas mec ne awæfan      wyrda cræftum, <br>
 </dd>
 <dt>10</dt>
 <dd>
 þa þe geolo godwebb      geatwum frætwað. <br>
 Wile mec mon hwæþre seþeah      wide ofer eorþan <br>
 hatan for hæleþum      hyhtlic gewæde. <br>
 Saga soðcwidum,      searoþoncum gleaw, <br>
 wordum wisfæst,      hwæt þis <strong>gewæde</strong> sy. <br>
