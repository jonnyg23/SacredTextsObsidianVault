 <h1>The Paris Psalter: Psalm 96</h1>
 <p><cite>Verse Indeterminate Saxon</cite></p>
 <dl compact>
 <dt></dt>
 <dd>
 Rixað nu mid rihte      rice drihten; <br>
 is eorðe nu      eac on blisse, <br>
 and þæs fægerne      gefean habbað <br>
 ealanda mænig      ut on garsæcge. <br>
