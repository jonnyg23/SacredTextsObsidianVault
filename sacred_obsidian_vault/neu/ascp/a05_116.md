 <h1>The Paris Psalter: Psalm 116</h1>
 <p><cite>Verse Indeterminate Saxon</cite></p>
 <dl compact>
 <dt></dt>
 <dd>
 Ealle þeode      ecne drihten <br>
 mid hygecræfte      herigan wordum, <br>
 and hine eall folc on efn      æðelne herigan. <br>
 Forþon his mildheortnyss      is mycel ofer us <br>
 </dd>
 <dt>5</dt>
 <dd>
 torhtlice getrymed,      til mancynne, <br>
 and soðfæstnys      swylce dryhtnes <br>
 wunað <strong>ece</strong>      awa to feore. <br>
 </dd>
