 <h1>Fragments of Psalms: Psalm 34</h1>
 <p><cite>Verse Indeterminate Saxon</cite></p>
 <dl compact>
 <dt></dt>
 <dd>
 <em>Iudica, domine, nocentes me; expugna inpugnantes me.</em><br>
 Dem, drihten, nu           þa me deredon ær,  <br>
 afeoht swylce           þa me fuhtan to.  <br>
 <em>Adprehende arma et scutum, et exsurge in adiutorium michi.</em><br>
 Gegrip gar and scyld,           and me georne gestand  <br>
 on fultume           wið feonda gryre.  <br>
 </dd>
 <dt>5</dt>
 <dd>
 <em>Effunde <b>frameam</b> et conclude aduersus eos qui me persecuntur; dic anime m<em>æ</em><em>æ</em>, Salus tua ego sum.</em><br>
 Heald me herewæpnum           wið unholdum  <br>
 and wige beluc           wraðum feondum  <br>
 þe min ehtend           ealle syndon;  <br>
 sæge þonne syððan           sawle minre  <br>
 þæt ðu hire on hæle           hold gestode.  <br>
 </dd>
