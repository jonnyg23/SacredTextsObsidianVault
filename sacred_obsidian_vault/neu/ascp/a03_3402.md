 <h1>Riddle 62</h1>
 <p><cite>Verse Indeterminate Saxon</cite></p>
 <dl compact>
 <dt></dt>
 <dd>
 Ic eom heard ond scearp,      <strong>hingonges</strong> strong, <br>
 forðsiþes from,      frean unforcuð, <br>
 wade under wambe      ond me weg sylfa <br>
 ryhtne geryme.      Rinc bið on ofeste, <br>
 </dd>
 <dt>5</dt>
 <dd>
 se mec on þyð      æftanweardne, <br>
 hæleð mid hrægle;      hwilum ut tyhð <br>
 of hole hatne,      hwilum eft fareð <br>
 on nearo nathwær,      nydeþ swiþe <br>
 suþerne secg.      Saga hwæt ic hatte. <br>
 </dd>
