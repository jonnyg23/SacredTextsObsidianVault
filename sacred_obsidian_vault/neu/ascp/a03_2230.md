 <h1>Riddle 30</h1>
 <p><cite>Verse Indeterminate Saxon</cite></p>
 <dl compact>
 <dt></dt>
 <dd>
 Ic eom legbysig,      lace mid winde, <br>
 bewunden mid wuldre,      wedre gesomnad, <br>
 fus forðweges,      fyre gebysgad, <br>
 bearu blowende,      byrnende gled. <br>
 </dd>
 <dt>5</dt>
 <dd>
 Ful oft mec gesiþas      sendað æfter hondum, <br>
 þæt mec weras ond wif      wlonce cyssað. <br>
 þonne ic mec onhæbbe,      ond hi <strong>onhnigaþ</strong> to me <br>
 monige mid miltse,      þær ic monnum sceal <br>
 ycan upcyme      eadignesse. <br>
 </dd>
