 <h1>Riddle 61</h1>
 <p><cite>Verse Indeterminate Saxon</cite></p>
 <dl compact>
 <dt></dt>
 <dd>
 Oft mec fæste bileac      freolicu meowle, <br>
 ides on earce,      hwilum up ateah <br>
 folmum sinum      ond frean sealde, <br>
 holdum þeodne,      swa hio haten wæs. <br>
 </dd>
 <dt>5</dt>
 <dd>
 Siðþan me on hreþre      heafod sticade, <br>
 nioþan upweardne,      on nearo fegde. <br>
 Gif þæs ondfengan      ellen dohte, <br>
 <strong>mec</strong> frætwedne      fyllan sceolde <br>
 ruwes nathwæt.      Ræd hwæt ic mæne. <br>
 </dd>
