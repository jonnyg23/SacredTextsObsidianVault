 <h1>Riddle 51</h1>
 <p><cite>Verse Indeterminate Saxon</cite></p>
 <dl compact>
 <dt></dt>
 <dd>
 Ic seah wrætlice      wuhte feower <br>
 samed siþian;      swearte wæran lastas, <br>
 swaþu swiþe blacu.      Swift wæs on fore, <br>
 fuglum framra;      <strong>fleag</strong> <strong>on</strong> lyfte, <br>
 </dd>
 <dt>5</dt>
 <dd>
 deaf under yþe.      Dreag unstille <br>
 winnende wiga      se him <strong>wegas</strong> tæcneþ <br>
 ofer fæted gold      feower eallum. <br>
 </dd>
