 <h1>Aldhelm</h1>
 <p><cite>Verse Indeterminate Saxon</cite></p>
 <dl compact>
 <dt></dt>
 <dd>
 þus me gesette      <em>sanctus et iustus</em> <br>
 beorn boca gleaw,      <em>bonus auctor</em>, <br>
 Ealdelm, æþele sceop,      <em>etiam fuit <br>
 ipselos</em> on <strong>æðele</strong>      Angolsexna, <br>
 </dd>
 <dt>5</dt>
 <dd>
 byscop on Bretene.      Biblos ic nu sceal, <br>
 <em>ponus et pondus      pleno cum sensu</em>, <br>
 geonges geanoðe      geomres <em>iamiamque</em>, <br>
 secgan soð, nalles leas,      þæt him symle wæs <br>
 <em>euthenia</em>      oftor on fylste, <br>
 </dd>
 <dt>10</dt>
 <dd>
 æne on eðle      ec ðon ðe se is <br>
 yfel on gesæd.      <em>Etiam nusquam</em> <br>
 ne <strong>sceal</strong> ladigan      <em>labor quem tenet</em> <br>
 encratea,      ac he ealneg sceal <br>
 boethia      biddan georne <br>
 </dd>
 <dt>15</dt>
 <dd>
 þurh his modes gemind      <em>micro in cosmo</em>, <br>
 þæt him drihten gyfe      dinams on eorðan, <br>
 <em>fortis factor</em>,      þæt he forð simle <br>
 </dd>
