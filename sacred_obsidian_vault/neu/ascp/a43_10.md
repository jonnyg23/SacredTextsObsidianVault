 <h1>Metrical Charm 10: For Loss of Cattle</h1>
 <p><cite>Verse Indeterminate Saxon</cite></p>
 <dl compact>
 <dt></dt>
 <dd>
 ðis man sceal cweðan ðonne his ceapa hwilcne man for- <br>
 stolenne. <strong>Cwyð</strong> ær he ænyg oþer word cweðe: <br>
 Bethlem hattæ seo burh      ðe Crist on geboren wes, <br>
 seo is gemærsod      ofer ealne middangeard; <br>
 </dd>
 <dt>5</dt>
 <dd>
 swa ðeos dæd wyrþe      for monnum mære, <br>
 <em>per crucem Christi</em>! And gebide þe ðonne þriwa east and <br>
 cweð þriwa: <em>Crux Christi ab oriente reducat</em>. And <strong><em>III</em></strong> <br>
 west and cweð: <em>Crux Christi ab occidente reducat</em>. And <br>
 <strong><em>III</em></strong> suð and cweð: <em>Crux Christi a meridie reducant</em>. And <br>
 </dd>
 <dt>10</dt>
 <dd>
 <strong><em>III</em></strong> norð and cweð: <em>Crux Christi abscondita sunt et inuenta <br>
 est</em>. Iudeas Crist ahengon, gedidon him dæda þa wyrstan; <br>
 hælon þæt hi forhelan ne mihton. Swa næfre ðeos dæd <br>
 forholen ne wyrðe <em>per crucem Christi</em>. <br>
 </dd>
 </dl>
