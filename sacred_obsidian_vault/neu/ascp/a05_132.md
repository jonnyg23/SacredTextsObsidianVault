 <h1>The Paris Psalter: Psalm 132</h1>
 <p><cite>Verse Indeterminate Saxon</cite></p>
 <dl compact>
 <dt></dt>
 <dd>
 Efne hu glædlic bið      and god swylce, <br>
 þætte broður on an      begen hicgen, <br>
 þær hig ænne sculan      eard weardian. <br>
 Swa unguentum mæg      æðele wyrtcynn <br>
 </dd>
 <dt>5</dt>
 <dd>
 heafde healdan      hrore stence, <br>
 mid þy Aaron his beard      oftast smyrede. <br>
 Seo niðer astah      on his reafæs fnæd <br>
 swa æþele deaw      on Hermone, <br>
 se ofer Sionbeorge      sneome astigeð. <br>
 </dd>
 <dt>10</dt>
 <dd>
 Forþon her bebead      halig drihten <br>
 lifes bletsunga      lange to feore <br>
 of þisson nu      awa to worulde. <br>
 </dd>
