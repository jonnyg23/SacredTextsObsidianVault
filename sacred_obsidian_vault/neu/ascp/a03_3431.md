 <h1>Riddle 91</h1>
 <p><cite>Verse Indeterminate Saxon</cite></p>
 <dl compact>

 <dd>
 Min heafod is           homere geþuren,  <br>
 searopila wund,           sworfen feole.  <br>
 Oft ic begine           þæt me ongean sticað,  <br>
 þonne ic hnitan sceal,           hringum gyrded,  <br>
 </dd>
 <dt>5</dt>
 <dd>
 hearde wið heardum,           hindan þyrel,  <br>
 forð ascufan           þæt mines frean  <br>
 mod ᚹ freoþað           middelnihtum.  <br>
 Hwilum ic under bæc           bregde nebbe,  <br>
 hyrde þæs hordes,           þonne min hlaford wile  <br>
 </dd>
 <dt>10</dt>
 <dd>
 lafe þicgan           þara þe he of life het  <br>
 wælcræfte awrecan           willum sinum.  <br>
