 <h1>Riddle 74</h1>
 <p><cite>Verse Indeterminate Saxon</cite></p>
 <dl compact>
 <dt></dt>
 <dd>
 Ic wæs fæmne geong,      feaxhar cwene, <br>
 ond ænlic rinc      on ane tid; <br>
 fleah mid fuglum      ond on flode swom, <br>
 deaf under yþe      dead mid fiscum, <br>
 </dd>

 ond on foldan stop,      hæfde <strong>ferð</strong> cwicu. <br>
