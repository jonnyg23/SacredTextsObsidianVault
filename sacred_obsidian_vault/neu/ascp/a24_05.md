 <h1>Fragments of Psalms: Psalm 5</h1>
 <p><cite>Verse Indeterminate Saxon</cite></p>
 <dl compact>
 <dt></dt>
 <dd>
 <em>Verba mea auribus percipe, domine; intellege clamorem meum.</em><br>
 Word þu min onfoh,           wuldres ealdor,  <br>
 and mid earum gehyr,           ece drihten.  <br>
 <b><em>Intende</em></b> uoci orationis m<em>æ</em><em>æ</em>, rex meus et deus meus.<br>
 Ongyt mine clypunga           cuðum gereorde,  <br>
 beheald min gebed           holdum mode;  <br>
 </dd>
 <dt>5</dt>
 <dd>
 þu eart min cyning           and eac ece god.  <br>
 <em>Quoniam ad te orabo: Domine, mane exaudies uocem meam.</em><br>
 Forðon ic to ðe,           ece drihten,  <br>
 soðum gebidde,           and ðu symble gehyr  <br>
 morgena gehwylce           mine stefne.  <br>
 <em>Mane adstabo tibi et uidebo, quoniam nos deus uolens iniquitatem tu es.</em><br>
 Ic þe æt stande           ær on morgen  <br>
 </dd>
 <dt>10</dt>
 <dd>
 and ðe sylfne geseo;           forðon ic to soðe wat  <br>
 þæt ðu unriht ne wilt           ænig, drihten.  <br>
 </dd>
