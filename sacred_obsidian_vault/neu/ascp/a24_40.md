 <h1>Fragments of Psalms: Psalm 40</h1>
 <p><cite>Verse Indeterminate Saxon</cite></p>
 <dl compact>
 <dt></dt>
 <dd>
 <em>Ego dixi, Domine, miserere mei; sana animam meam, quia peccaui tibi.</em><br>
 Ic nu mægene cweðe:           "Miltsa me, drihten,  <br>
 hæl mine sawle,           forðon me hreoweð nu  <br>
 þæt ic firene on ðe           fremede geneahhige."  <br>
