 <h1>Riddle 50</h1>
 <p><cite>Verse Indeterminate Saxon</cite></p>
 <dl compact>
 <dt></dt>
 <dd>
 Wiga is on eorþan      wundrum acenned <br>
 dryhtum to nytte,      of dumbum twam <br>
 torht atyhted,      þone on teon wigeð <br>
 feond his feonde.      <strong>Forstrangne</strong> oft <br>
 </dd>
 <dt>5</dt>
 <dd>
 wif hine wrið;      he him wel hereð, <br>
 þeowaþ him geþwære,      gif him þegniað <br>
 mægeð ond mæcgas      mid gemete ryhte, <br>
 fedað hine fægre;      he him fremum stepeð <br>
 life on lissum.      Leanað grimme <br>
 </dd>
 <dt>10</dt>
 <dd>
 <strong>þam</strong> þe hine wloncne      weorþan læteð. <br>
 </dd>
