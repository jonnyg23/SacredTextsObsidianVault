 <h1>The Partridge</h1>
 <p><cite>Verse Indeterminate Saxon</cite></p>
 <dl compact>
 <dt></dt>
 <dd>
 Hyrde ic secgan gen      bi sumum fugle <br>
 wundorlicne      <br>
      fæger, <br>
 þæt word þe gecwæð      wuldres ealdor: <br>
 </dd>
 <dt>5</dt>
 <dd>
 "In swa hwylce tiid      swa ge mid treowe to me <br>
 on hyge hweorfað,      ond ge hellfirena <br>
 sweartra geswicað,      swa ic symle to eow <br>
 mid siblufan      sona gecyrre <br>
 þurh milde mod.      Ge beoð me siþþan <br>
 </dd>
 <dt>10</dt>
 <dd>
 torhte tireadge      talade ond rimde, <br>
 beorhte gebroþor      on bearna stæl." <br>
 Uton we þy geornor      gode oliccan, <br>
 firene feogan,      friþes earnian, <br>
 duguðe to dryhtne,      þenden us dæg scine, <br>
 </dd>
 <dt>15</dt>
 <dd>
 þæt swa æþelne      eardwica cyst <br>
 in wuldres wlite      wunian motan. <br>
 <em>Finit</em>. <br>
 </dd>
