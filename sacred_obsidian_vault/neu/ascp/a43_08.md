 <h1>Metrical Charm 8: For a Swarm of Bees</h1>
 <p><cite>Verse Indeterminate Saxon</cite></p>
 <dl compact>
 <dt></dt>
 <dd>
 Wið ymbe nim eorþan, oferweorp mid þinre swiþran <br>
 handa under þinum swiþran fet, and cwet: <br>
 Fo ic under fot,      funde ic hit. <br>
 Hwæt, eorðe mæg      wið ealra wihta gehwilce <br>
 </dd>
 <dt>5</dt>
 <dd>
 and wið andan      and wið æminde <br>
 and wið þa micelan      mannes tungan. <br>
 And <strong>wiððon</strong> forweorp ofer greot, þonne hi swirman, and cweð: <br>
 Sitte ge, sigewif,      sigað to eorþan! <br>
 Næfre ge wilde      to wuda fleogan. <br>
 </dd>
 <dt>10</dt>
 <dd>
 Beo ge swa gemindige      mines godes, <br>
 swa bið manna gehwilc      metes and eþeles. <br>
 </dd>
