 <h1>Caedmon's Hymn: Northumbrian Version</h1>
 <p><cite>Verse Early Anglian</cite></p>
 <dl compact>
 <dt></dt>
 <dd>
 Nu scylun hergan      hefaenricaes uard, <br>
 metudæs maecti      end his modgidanc, <br>
 uerc uuldurfadur,      sue he uundra gihuaes, <br>
 eci dryctin,      or astelidæ. <br>
 </dd>
 <dt>5</dt>
 <dd>
 He aerist scop      aelda barnum <br>
 heben til hrofe,      haleg scepen; <br>
 tha middungeard      moncynnæs uard, <br>
 eci dryctin,      æfter tiadæ <br>
 firum foldu,      frea allmectig. <br>
 </dd>
