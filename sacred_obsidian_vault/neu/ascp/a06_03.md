 <h1>The Meters of Boethius: Meter 3</h1>
 <p><cite>Verse Indeterminate Saxon</cite></p>
 <dl compact>
 <dt></dt>
 <dd>
 æala, on hu grimmum      and hu grundleasum <br>
 seaðe swinceð      þæt sweorcende mod, <br>
 þonne hit þa strongan      stormas beatað <br>
 weoruldbisgunga,      þonne hit winnende <br>
 </dd>
 <dt>5</dt>
 <dd>
 his agen leoht      an forlæteð, <br>
 and mid uua forgit      þone ecan gefean, <br>
 ðringð on þa ðiostro      ðisse worulde, <br>
 sorgum geswenced.      Swa is þissum nu <br>
 mode gelumpen,      nu hit mare ne wat <br>
 </dd>
 <dt>10</dt>
 <dd>
 for gode godes      buton gnornunge <br>
 fremdre worulde.      Him is frofre ðearf. <br>
 </dd>
