 <h1>Riddle 4</h1>
 <p><cite>Verse Indeterminate Saxon</cite></p>
 <dl compact>
 <dt></dt>
 <dd>
 Ic sceal þragbysig      þegne minum, <br>
 <strong>hringum</strong> hæfted,      hyran georne, <br>
 min bed brecan,      breahtme cyþan <br>
 þæt me halswriþan      hlaford sealde. <br>
 </dd>
 <dt>5</dt>
 <dd>
 Oft mec slæpwerigne      secg oðþe meowle <br>
 gretan eode;      ic him gromheortum <br>
 winterceald oncweþe.      Wearm lim <br>
 gebundenne bæg      hwilum bersteð; <br>
 se þeah biþ on þonce      þegne minum, <br>
 </dd>
 <dt>10</dt>
 <dd>
 medwisum men,      me þæt sylfe, <br>
 þær wiht wite,      ond wordum min <br>
 on sped mæge      spel gesecgan. <br>
 </dd>
