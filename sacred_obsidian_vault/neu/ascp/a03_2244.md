 <h1>Riddle 44</h1>
 <p><cite>Verse Indeterminate Saxon</cite></p>
 <dl compact>
 <dt></dt>
 <dd>
 Wrætlic hongað      bi weres þeo, <br>
 frean under sceate.      Foran is þyrel. <br>
 Bið stiþ ond heard,      stede hafað godne; <br>
 þonne se esne      his agen hrægl <br>
 </dd>
 <dt>5</dt>
 <dd>
 ofer cneo hefeð,      wile þæt cuþe hol <br>
 mid his hangellan      heafde gretan <br>
 þæt he <strong>efenlang</strong> ær      oft gefylde. <br>
 </dd>
