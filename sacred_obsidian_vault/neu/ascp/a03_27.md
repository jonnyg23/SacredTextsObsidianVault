 <h1>Alms-Giving</h1>
 <p><cite>Verse Indeterminate Saxon</cite></p>
 <dl compact>
 <dt></dt>
 <dd>
 Wel bið þam eorle      þe him on innan hafað, <br>
 reþehygdig wer,      rume heortan; <br>
 þæt him biþ for worulde      weorðmynda mæst, <br>
 ond for ussum dryhtne      doma selast. <br>
 </dd>
 <dt>5</dt>
 <dd>
 Efne swa he mid wætre      þone weallendan <br>
 leg adwæsce,      þæt he leng ne mæg <br>
 blac byrnende      burgum sceððan, <br>
 swa he mid ælmessan      ealle toscufeð <br>
 synna wunde,      sawla lacnað. <br>
 </dd>
