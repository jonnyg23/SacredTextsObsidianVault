 <h1>Riddle 86</h1>
 <p><cite>Verse Indeterminate Saxon</cite></p>
 <dl compact>
 <dt></dt>
 <dd>
 Wiht cwom gongan      þær weras sæton <br>
 monige on mæðle,      mode snottre; <br>
 hæfde an eage      ond earan twa, <br>
 ond <em>II</em> fet,      <em>XII</em> hund heafda, <br>
 </dd>
 <dt>5</dt>
 <dd>
 <strong>hrycg</strong> ond wombe      ond honda twa, <br>
 earmas ond eaxle,      anne sweoran <br>
 ond sidan twa.      Saga hwæt ic hatte. <br>
