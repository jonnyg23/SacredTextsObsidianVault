 <h1>Riddle 95</h1>
 <p><cite>Verse Indeterminate Saxon</cite></p>
 <dl compact>
 <dt></dt>
 <dd>
 Ic eom indryhten      ond eorlum cuð, <br>
 ond reste oft;      ricum ond heanum, <br>
 folcum gefræge      <strong>fere</strong> wide, <br>
 ond me fremdes ær      freondum stondeð <br>
 </dd>
 <dt>5</dt>
 <dd>
 hiþendra hyht,      gif ic habban sceal <br>
 blæd in burgum      oþþe beorhtne god. <br>
 Nu snottre men      swiþast lufiaþ <br>
 midwist mine;      ic monigum sceal <br>
 wisdom cyþan;      no þær word sprecað <br>
 </dd>
 <dt>10</dt>
 <dd>
 ænig ofer eorðan.      þeah nu ælda bearn <br>
 londbuendra      lastas mine <br>
 swiþe secað,      ic swaþe hwilum <br>
 mine bemiþe      monna gehwylcum. <br>
 </dd>
