 <h1>Fragments of Psalms: Psalm 43</h1>
 <p><cite>Verse Indeterminate Saxon</cite></p>
 <dl compact>
 <dt></dt>
 <dd>
 <em>Exsurge, domine, adiuua nos, et libera nos propter nomen tuum.</em><br>
 Aris, drihten, nu           and us ricene do  <br>
 fælne fultum,           and us æt feondum ahrede,  <br>
 forðon we naman þinne           nyde lufiað.  <br>
