 <h1>Riddle 46</h1>
 <p><cite>Verse Indeterminate Saxon</cite></p>
 <dl compact>
 <dt></dt>
 <dd>
 <strong>Wer</strong> sæt æt wine      mid his wifum twam <br>
 ond his twegen suno      ond his twa dohtor, <br>
 swase gesweostor,      ond <strong>hyra</strong> suno twegen, <br>
 freolico frumbearn;      fæder wæs þær inne <br>
 </dd>
 <dt>5</dt>
 <dd>
 þara æþelinga      æghwæðres mid, <br>
 eam ond nefa.      Ealra wæron fife <br>
 eorla ond idesa      insittendra. <br>
 </dd>
