 <h1>The Paris Psalter: Psalm 133</h1>
 <p><cite>Verse Indeterminate Saxon</cite></p>
 <dl compact>
 <dt></dt>
 <dd>
 Efne bletsien nu      bliðe drihten <br>
 ealle his agene      onbyhtscealcas. <br>
 Ge þe on godes huse      gearwe standað, <br>
 and on cafertunum      Cristes huses <br>
 </dd>
 <dt>5</dt>
 <dd>
 ures þæs halgan godes      held <strong>begangað.</strong> <br>
 Hebbað neodlice      nihta gehwylcere <br>
 eowre handa      on halig lof <br>
 and bletsiað      balde drihten. <br>
 Ge bletsige      bliðe drihten <br>
 </dd>
 <dt>10</dt>
 <dd>
 of Sionbeorge      symble æt þearfe, <br>
 se þe heofon worhte,      hrusan swylce. <br>
