 <h1>Riddle 37</h1>
 <p><cite>Verse Indeterminate Saxon</cite></p>
 <dl compact>
 <dt></dt>
 <dd>
 Ic þa wihte geseah;      womb wæs on hindan <br>
 þriþum aþrunten.      þegn folgade, <br>
 mægenrofa man,      ond micel hæfde <br>
 gefered <strong>þæt</strong> hit felde,      fleah þurh his eage. <br>
 </dd>
 <dt>5</dt>
 <dd>
 Ne swylteð he symle,      þonne syllan sceal <br>
 innað þam oþrum,      ac him eft cymeð <br>
 bot in bosme,      blæd biþ aræred; <br>
 he sunu wyrceð,      bið him sylfa fæder. <br>
 </dd>
