 <h1>Riddle 54</h1>
 <p><cite>Verse Indeterminate Saxon</cite></p>
 <dl compact>
 <dt></dt>
 <dd>
 Hyse cwom gangan,      þær he hie wisse <br>
 stondan in wincsele,      stop feorran to, <br>
 hror hægstealdmon,      hof his agen <br>
 hrægl hondum up,      <strong>hrand</strong> under gyrdels <br>
 </dd>
 <dt>5</dt>
 <dd>
 hyre stondendre      stiþes nathwæt, <br>
 worhte his willan;      wagedan buta. <br>
 þegn onnette,      wæs þragum nyt <br>
 tillic esne,      teorode hwæþre <br>
 æt stunda gehwam      strong ær þon <strong>hio,</strong> <br>
 </dd>
 <dt>10</dt>
 <dd>
 werig þæs weorces.      Hyre weaxan ongon <br>
 under gyrdelse      þæt oft gode men <br>
 ferðþum freogað      ond mid feo bicgað. <br>
 </dd>
