 <h1>Caedmon's Hymn: West Saxon Version</h1>
 <p><cite>Verse Early Saxon</cite></p>
 <dl compact>
 <dt></dt>
 <dd>
 Nu sculon herigean      heofonrices weard, <br>
 meotodes meahte      and his modgeþanc, <br>
 weorc wuldorfæder,      swa he wundra gehwæs, <br>
 ece drihten,      or onstealde. <br>
 </dd>
 <dt>5</dt>
 <dd>
 He ærest sceop      eorðan bearnum <br>
 heofon to hrofe,      halig scyppend; <br>
 þa middangeard      moncynnes weard, <br>
 ece drihten,      æfter teode <br>
 firum foldan,      frea ælmihtig. <br>
 </dd>
