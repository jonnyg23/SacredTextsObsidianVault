 <h1>Fragments of Psalms: Psalm 50</h1>
 <p><cite>Verse Indeterminate Saxon</cite></p>
 <dl compact>
 <dt></dt>
 <dd>
 <em>Miserere mei, deus, secundam magnam misericordiam tuam.</em><br>
 Mildsa me, mihtig drihten,           swa ðu manegum dydest,  <br>
 æfter ðinre þære mycelan           mildheortnysse.  <br>
 <em>Auerte faciem tuam a peccatis meis, et omnes iniquitates meas dele.</em><br>
 Awend þine ansyne           a fram minum  <br>
 fræcnum fyrenum,           and nu forð heonon  <br>
 </dd>
 <dt>5</dt>
 <dd>
 eall min unriht adwæsc           æghwær symle.  <br>
 <em>Cor mundum crea in me, deus, et spiritum rectum innoua in uisceribus meis.</em><br>
 Syle me, halig god,           heortan clæne,  <br>
 and rihtne gast,           god, geniwa  <br>
 on minre gehigde           huru, <b>min</b> drihten.  <br>
 <em>Ne proicias me a facie tua, et spiritum sanctum tuum ne auferas a me.</em><br>
 Ne awyrp þu me,           wuldres ealdor,  <br>
 </dd>
 <dt>10</dt>
 <dd>
 fram ðinre ansyne           æfre to feore,  <br>
 ne huru on weg aber           þone halgan gast,  <br>
 þæt he me færinga           fremde wyrðe.  <br>
 <em>Redde michi l<em>æ</em>titiam salutaris tui, et spiritu principali confirma me.</em><br>
 Syle me þinre hælu           holde blisse,  <br>
 and me ealdorlice           æþele gaste  <br>
 </dd>
 <dt>15</dt>
 <dd>
 on <b>ðinne</b> willan getryme,           weroda drihten.  <br>
 </dd>
