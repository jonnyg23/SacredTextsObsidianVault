 <h1>Riddle 83</h1>
 <p><cite>Verse Indeterminate Saxon</cite></p>
 <dl compact>
 <dt></dt>
 <dd>
 Frod wæs min <strong>fromcynn</strong>      <br>
 biden in burgum,      siþþan bæles weard <br>
 ...... wera      life bewunden, <br>
 fyre gefælsad.      Nu me fah warað <br>
 </dd>
 <dt>5</dt>
 <dd>
 eorþan broþor,      se me ærest wearð <br>
 gumena to gyrne.      Ic ful gearwe gemon <br>
 hwa min fromcynn      fruman agette <br>
 eall of earde;      ic him yfle ne mot, <br>
 ac ic <strong>hæftnyd</strong>      hwilum arære <br>
 </dd>
 <dt>10</dt>
 <dd>
 wide geond wongas.      Hæbbe ic <strong>wundra</strong> fela, <br>
 middangeardes      mægen unlytel, <br>
 ac ic miþan sceal      monna gehwylcum <br>
 degolfulne dom      dyran cræftes, <br>
 siðfæt minne.      Saga hwæt ic hatte. <br>
 </dd>
