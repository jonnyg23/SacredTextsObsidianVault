 <h1>The Metrical Epilogue to MS 41, Corpus Christi College, Cambridge</h1>
 <p><cite>Verse Indeterminate Saxon</cite></p>
 <dl compact>
 <dt></dt>
 <dd>
 <strong>Bidde</strong> ic eac      æghwylcne mann, <br>
 brego, rices weard,      þe þas boc ræde <br>
 and þa bredu befo,      fira aldor, <br>
 þæt gefyrðrige þone writre      wynsum cræfte <br>
 </dd>
 <dt>5</dt>
 <dd>
 þe ðas boc awrat      bam handum twam, <br>
 þæt he mote manega gyt      mundum synum <br>
 geendigan,      his aldre to willan, <br>
 and him þæs geunne      se ðe ah ealles geweald, <br>
 rodera waldend,      þæt he on riht mote <br>
 </dd>
 <dt>10</dt>
 <dd>
 oð his daga ende      drihten herigan. <br>
 Amen.      Geweorþe þæt. <br>
 </dd>
