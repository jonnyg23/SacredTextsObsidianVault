 <h1>The Paris Psalter: Psalm 81</h1>
 <p><cite>Verse Indeterminate Saxon</cite></p>
 <dl compact>
 <dt></dt>
 <dd>
 God mihtig stod      godum on gemange, <br>
 and he hi on midle      mægene tosceadeð. <br>
 Hu lange deme ge      domas unrihte, <br>
 and ge onfoð ansyna      synnwyrcendra? <br>
 </dd>
 <dt>5</dt>
 <dd>
 Demað steopcildum      domas soðe <br>
 and eadmedum      eac þæt sylfe, <br>
 wædlum and ðearfum      wel soðfæstum. <br>
 <strong>Ge</strong> of firenfulra      fæcnum handum <br>
 þearfan and wædlan      þriste alysað. <br>
 </dd>
 <dt>10</dt>
 <dd>
 Ne ongeatan hi      ne geara wistan, <br>
 ac hi on ðystrum      þrage eodan; <br>
 ealle abeofedan      eorðan staþelas. <br>
 Ic ærest cwæð:      "Ge synd uppe godu, <br>
 ealle uphea,      and æðele bearn. <br>
 </dd>
 <dt>15</dt>
 <dd>
 Ge þonne sweltað      samod mid mannum, <br>
 swa ealdormann      an gefealleð." <br>
 Aris drihten, nu!      Dem eorðware, <br>
 forþan þu eart erfeweard      ealra ðeoda. <br>
 </dd>
