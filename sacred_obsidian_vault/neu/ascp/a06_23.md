 <h1>The Meters of Boethius: Meter 23</h1>
 <p><cite>Verse Indeterminate Saxon</cite></p>
 <dl compact>
 <dt></dt>
 <dd>
 Sie ðæt la on eorðan      ælces ðinges <br>
 gesælig mon,      gif he gesion mæge <br>
 þone hlutrestan      heofontorhtan stream, <br>
 æðelne æwelm      ælces goodes, <br>
 </dd>
 <dt>5</dt>
 <dd>
 and of him selfum      ðone sweartan mist, <br>
 modes þiostro,      mæg aweorpan. <br>
 We sculon ðeah gita      mid godes fylste <br>
 ealdum and leasum      ðinne ingeðonc <br>
 betan bispellum,      þæt ðu ðe bet mæge <br>
 </dd>
 <dt>10</dt>
 <dd>
 aredian to rodorum      rihte stige <br>
 on ðone ecan eard      ussa saula. <br>
 </dd>
