 <h1>Riddle 32</h1>
 <p><cite>Verse Indeterminate Saxon</cite></p>
 <dl compact>
 <dt></dt>
 <dd>
 Is þes middangeard      missenlicum <br>
 wisum gewlitegad,      wrættum gefrætwad. <br>
 Siþum sellic      ic seah searo hweorfan, <br>
 grindan wið greote,      giellende faran. <br>
 </dd>
 <dt>5</dt>
 <dd>
 Næfde sellicu wiht      syne ne folme, <br>
 exle ne earmas;      sceal on anum fet <br>
 searoceap swifan,      swiþe feran, <br>
 faran ofer feldas.      Hæfde <strong>fela</strong> ribba; <br>
 muð wæs on middan.      Moncynne nyt, <br>
 </dd>
 <dt>10</dt>
 <dd>
 <strong>fereð</strong> foddurwelan,      folcscipe dreogeð, <br>
 wist in wigeð,      ond werum gieldeð <br>
 gaful geara gehwam      þæs þe guman brucað, <br>
 rice ond heane.      Rece, gif þu cunne, <br>
 wis worda gleaw,      hwæt sio wiht sie. <br>
 </dd>
