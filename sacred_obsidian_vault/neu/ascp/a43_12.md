 <h1>Metrical Charm 12: Against a Wen</h1>
 <p><cite>Verse Indeterminate Saxon</cite></p>
 <dl compact>
 <dt></dt>
 <dd>
 Wenne, wenne,      wenchichenne, <br>
 her ne scealt þu timbrien,      ne nenne tun habben, <br>
 ac þu scealt north eonene      to þan nihgan berhge, <br>
 þer þu hauest, ermig,      enne broþer. <br>
 </dd>
 <dt>5</dt>
 <dd>
 He þe sceal legge      leaf et heafde. <br>
 Under fot <strong>wolues,</strong>      under ueþer earnes, <br>
 under earnes clea,      a þu geweornie. <br>
 Clinge þu      alswa col on heorþe, <br>
 scring þu      alswa <strong>scerne</strong> awage, <br>
 </dd>
 <dt>10</dt>
 <dd>
 and weorne      alswa weter on anbre. <br>
 Swa litel þu gewurþe      alswa linsetcorn, <br>
 and miccli lesse      alswa anes handwurmes hupeban, <br>
 and alswa litel þu gewurþe      þet þu nawiht gewurþe. <br>
 </dd></dl></body>
