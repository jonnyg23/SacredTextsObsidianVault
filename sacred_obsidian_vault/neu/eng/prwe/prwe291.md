 <p align="CENTER">MEETING ON THE STAIRS</p>
 <p>
  IT is considered unlucky to meet on the stairs, and often
 one will retire to his or her room rather than run the risk of -giving or
 receiving ill luck.</p>
 <p>I find this superstition prevails also in the Midland
 counties.</p>
