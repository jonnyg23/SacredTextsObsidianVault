 <p align="CENTER">CHAPELL UNY.</p>
 <p>ON the first three Wednesdays in May, children suffering
 from mesenteric diseases are dipped three times in this well, <i>against the
 sun, </i>and <i>dragged </i>three times around the well on the grass, in the
 same direction.</p>
