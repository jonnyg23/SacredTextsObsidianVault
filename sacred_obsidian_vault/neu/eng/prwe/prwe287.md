 <p align="CENTER">THE SPARK ON THE CANDLE.</p>
 <p>
  A BRIGHT spark on the candle-wick indicates a letter coming
 to the house. The person towards whom it shines will receive it. The time
 of its arrival is determined by striking the bottom of the candlestick on
 the table. If the spark comes off on the first blow, it will be received
 to-morrow; if two blows are required, on the second day,--and so on.</p>
