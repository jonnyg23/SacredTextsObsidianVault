 <p align="CENTER">BELLERIAN</p>
 <p>SAMUEL BUTLER of Shrewsbury, in his "Ancient and Modern
 Geography," p. 112,<i> </i>says, "Ocrinum was the Lizard, and B<i>o</i>lerium
 the Land's End." Ainsworth, Latin Dictionary, 4th edition, has "B<i>a</i>lerium
 --Burien in Cornwall." It is really in the parish of Sennen.</p>
