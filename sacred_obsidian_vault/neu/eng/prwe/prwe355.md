 <p align="CENTER">THE TINNER OF CHYANNOR</p>
 <p>IN Trengothal stood a low hut called the Ram's House.
 This was said to have been built by the tinners, and called <i>
 Chyan-nance</i> or House in the Valley. <i>Nor</i>, in Williams's Welsh
 Dictionary, is earth, This makes <i>Chyan-nor</i>, or the House of
 Earth.</p>
