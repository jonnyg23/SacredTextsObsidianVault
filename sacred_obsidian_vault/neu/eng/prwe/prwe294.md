 <p align="CENTER">TO CURE THE HICCOUGH.</p>
 <p>
  </p>
 <p>WET the forefinger of the right hand with spittle, and cross
 the front of the left shoe or boot three times, repeating the Lord's Prayer
 backwards.</p>
