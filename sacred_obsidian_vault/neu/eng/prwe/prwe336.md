 <p align="CENTER">WAYLAND SMITH.</p>
 <p>"'WAYLAND SMITH:' a Dissertation on a Tradition of
 the Middle Ages, from the French of G. B. Depping and Francisque Michel,
 with Additions by S. W. Singer, and the Amplified Legend, by
 Oehlenschlager." Pickering, 1847.</p>
 <p>To this very interesting little volume I would refer
 those of my readers who feel desirous of tracing.the resemblance of our
 humble "Jack the Tinkeard," with the Icelandic <i>Valund</i>, the
 English <i>Velond</i>, or Sir Walter Scott's <i>Wayland</i> in
 "Kenilworth."</p>
