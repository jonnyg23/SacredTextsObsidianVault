 <p align="CENTER">THE CORNISH CHOUGH.</p>
 <p>THE tradition relative to King Arthur and his transformation
 into a raven, is fixed very decidedly on the Cornish Chough, from the colour
 of its beak and talons. The --</p>
 <p>"Talons and beak all red with blood"</p>
 <p>are said to mark the violent end to which this celebrated
 chieftain came.</p>
