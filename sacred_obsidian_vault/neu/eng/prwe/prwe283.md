 <p align="CENTER">THE MONTH OF MAY UNLUCKY</p>
 <p>
  MAY is regarded by many as an unhealthy and unlucky month.</p>
 <p>Children born in the month of May are called "May chets,"
 and kittens cast in May are invariably destroyed, for--<br>
 <br>
 " May chets<br>
 Bad luck begets."<br>
 </p>
 <p>Another rhyme is--</p>
 <p>"A hot May,<br>
 Fat church hay,"</p>
 <p>meaning that funerals will be plenty.</p>
