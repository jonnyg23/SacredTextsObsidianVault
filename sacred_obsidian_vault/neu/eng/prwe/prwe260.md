 <p align="CENTER">RHYME ON THE EVEN ASH.</p>
 <p>
  </p>
 <p>"EVEN ash, I thee do pluck;<br>
 Hoping thus to meet good luck.<br>
 If no luck I get from thee,<br>
 I shall wish thee on the tree."</p>
