 <p align="CENTER">WEATHER AT LISKEARD.</p>
 <p>
  </p>
 <p>"THE south wind always brings wet weather;<br>
 The north wind, wet and cold together.<br>
 The west wind always brings us rain;<br>
 The east wind blows it back again.</p>
 <p>If the sun in red should set,<br>
 The next day surely will be wet<br>
 If the sun should set in gray,<br>
 The next will be a rainy day."</p>
 <p><i>Bond's Looe.</i></p>
