 <p>"Have you any stories like this guidwife?'</p>
 <p>Ah, she said; 'there were plenty of people could tell these stories once. I used to hear them telling them over the fire at night; but people is so changed with pride now, that tey crave for nothing.'"</p>
 <p align="RIGHT">--Campbell</p>
