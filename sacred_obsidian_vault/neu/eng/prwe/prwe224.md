 <p align="CENTER">SHROVE TUESDAY AT ST IVES.</p>
 <p>FORMERLY it was customary for the boys to tie stones to
 cords, and with these parade the town, slinging these stones against the
 doors, shouting aloud,--</p>
 <p>"Give me a pancake, now--now--now,<br>
 Or I 'II souse in your door with a row--tow--tow."</p>
 <p>A genteel correspondent assures me "this is observed now
 in the lower parts of the town only."</p>
