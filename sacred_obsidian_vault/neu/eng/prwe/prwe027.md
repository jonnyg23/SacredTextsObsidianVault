 <p align="CENTER">A NATIVE PIGSEY STORY.</p>
 <p><br>
 " 'YE see that 'ere hoss there?" said a Liskeard farmer to a West-Country
 miner.</p>
 <p>"What ov it?" asked the miner.</p>
 <p>"Well, that 'ere hoss he 'n been ridden to death a'most by
 the pigsies again."</p>
 <p>"Pigsies!" said the miner; "thee don't b'leve in they, do
 'ee?"</p>
 <p>"Ees I do; but I specks you 're a West-Country bucca, ain't
 'ee? It you 'd a had yourn hosses wrode to death every riite, you 'd tell
 another tayl, I reckon. But as sure as I 'se living the pigsies do ride on 'em
 whenever they 'ye a mind to."</p>
