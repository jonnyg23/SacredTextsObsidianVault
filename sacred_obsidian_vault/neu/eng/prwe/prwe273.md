 <p align="CENTER">AN OLD CORNISH RHYME.</p>
 <p>
  "WHEN the corn is in the shock,<br>
 Then the fish are on the rock."</p>
 <p>The pilchard visits this coast in the early autumn. These
 are the "fish " <i>par</i> <i>excellence</i> of the Cornish, and they are
 thus distinguished.</p>
