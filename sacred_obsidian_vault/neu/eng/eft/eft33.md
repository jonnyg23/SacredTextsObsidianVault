 <h1 align="CENTER">The Strange Visitor</h1>
 <p>A WOMAN was sitting at her reel one night;<br>
 And still she sat, and still she reeled, and still she wished for company.</p>
 <p>In came a pair of broad broad soles, and sat down at the fireside;<br>
 And still she sat, and still she reeled, and still she wished for company.</p>
 <p align="CENTER"><a name="img_050"></a>
 <a href="img/050.jpg"><img src="tn/050.jpg"><br><font size="-3">Click to enlarge</font></a></p>
 <p>In came a pair of small small legs, and sat down on the broad broad soles;<br>
 And still she sat, and still she reeled, and still she wished for company.</p>
 <p>In came a pair of thick thick knees, and sat down on the small small legs;<br>
 And still she sat, and still she reeled, and still she wished for company.</p>
 <p>In came a pair of thin thin thighs, and sat down on the thick knees;<br>
 And still she sat, and still she reeled, and still she wished for company.</p>
 <p>In came a pair of huge huge hips, and sat down on the thin thin thighs;<br>
 And still she sat, and still she reeled, and still she wished for company.</p>
 <p>In came a wee wee waist, and sat down on the huge huge hips;<br>
 And still she sat, and still she reeled, and still she wished for company.</p>
 <p>In came a pair of broad broad shoulders, and sat down on the wee wee waist;<br>
 And still she sat, and still she reeled, and still she wished for company.</p>
 <p>In came a pair of small small arms, and sat down on the broad broad shoulders;<br>
 And still she sat, and still she reeled, and still she wished for company.</p>
 <p>In came a pair of huge huge hands, and sat down on the small small arms;<br>
 And still she sat, and still she reeled, and still she wished for company.</p>
 <p>In came a small small neck, and sat down on the broad broad shoulders;<br>
 And still she sat, and still she reeled, and still she wished for company.</p>
 <p>In came a huge huge head, and sat down on the small small neck.</p>
 <p>'How did you get such broad broad feet?' quoth the woman.<br>
 'Much tramping, much tramping' <i>(gruffly).</i></p>
 <p>'How did you get such small small legs?'<br>
 <i>'Aih-h-h!--</i>late--and <i>wee-e-e--</i>moul' <i>(whiningly).</i></p>
 <p>'How did you get such thick thick knees?'<br>
 'Much praying, much praying' <i>(piously);</i></p>
 <p>'How did you get such thin thin thighs?'<br>
 'Aih-h-h!--late--and wee-e-e--moul' <i>(whiningly).</i></p>
 <p>'How did you get such big big hips?'<br>
 'Much sitting, much sitting' (<i>gruffly</i>).</p>
 <p>'How did you get such a wee wee waist?'<br>
 'Aih-h-h!--late--and wee-e-e--moul' <i>(whiningly).</i></p>
 <p>'How did you get such broad broad shoulders?'<br>
 'With carrying broom, with carrying broom' (<i>gruffly</i>).</p>
 <p>'How did you get such small small arms?'<br>
 'Aih-h-h!--late--and we-e-e--moul' <i>(whiningly).</i></p>
 <p>'How did you get such huge huge hands?'<br>
 'Threshing with an iron flail, threshing with an iron flail' <i>(gruffly).</i></p>
 <p>'How did you get such a small small neck?'<br>
 'Aih-h-h!--late--wee-e-e--moul' <i>(pitifully).</i></p>
 <p>'How did you get such a huge huge head?'<br>
 'Much knowledge, much knowledge' <i>(keenly).</i></p>
 <p>'What do you come for?'<br>
 'For you !' <i>(At the top of the voice, with a wave of the arm, and a stamp of the feet.)</i></p>
