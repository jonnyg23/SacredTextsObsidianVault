At<br> <a name="an_7"></a> the leeste, no drede ne myghte overcomen<br> <a name="an_8"></a> tho muses, that thei ne were felawes, and folwyden<br> <a name="an_9"></a> my wey (that is to seyn, whan<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_10">10</a></font></span> I was exiled). They that weren glorie of<br> <a name="an_11"></a> my youthe, whilom weleful and grene,<br> <a name="an_12"></a> conforten nowe the sorwful wyerdes of me, olde<br> <a name="an_13"></a> man. For eelde is comyn unwarly uppon me,<br> <a name="an_14"></a> hasted by the harmes that Y have, and sorwe<br> <a name="an_15"></a> hath comandid his age to ben in me. Heeris hore<br> <a name="an_16"></a> arn schad overtymeliche upon myn heved, and<br> <a name="an_17"></a> the slakke skyn trembleth of myn emptid body.<br> <a name="an_18"></a> Thilke deth of men is weleful that ne comyth<br> <a name="an_19"></a> noght in yeeris that ben swete, but<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_20">20</a></font></span> cometh to wrecches often yclepid. But now, for Fortune<br> <a name="an_27"></a> cloudy hath chaunged hir deceyvable<br> <a name="an_28"></a> chere to meward, myn unpietous lif draweth<br> <a name="an_29"></a> along unagreable duellynges in me. For he that hath fallen stood<br> <a name="an_32"></a> noght in stedefast degre.<br> <a name="an_1"></a> In the mene while that I, stille, recordede<br> <a name="an_2"></a> these thynges with myself and merkid my weply<br> <a name="an_3"></a> compleynte with office of poyntel, I saw,<br> <a name="an_4"></a> stondynge aboven the heghte of myn heved, a<br> <a name="an_5"></a> womman of ful greet reverence by semblaunt,<br> <a name="an_6"></a> hir eien brennynge and cleer-seynge over the<br> <a name="an_7"></a> comune myghte of men; with a lifly colour<br> <a name="an_8"></a> and with swich vigour and strengthe that it ne<br> <a name="an_9"></a> myghte nat ben emptid, al were it so<br> <span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_10">10</a></font></span> that sche was ful of so greet age that men<br> <a name="an_11"></a> ne wolden nat trowen in no manere that<br> <a name="an_12"></a> sche were of our elde.
