 <img src="img/15.jpg"></p>  <h3 align="center">Laughing Song,</h3>  
 <p>When the green woods laugh with the voice of joy<br> And the dimpling stream runs laughing by,<br> When the air does laugh with our merry wit,<br> And the green hill laughs with the noise of it.</p>  
 <p>When the meadows laugh with lively green<br> And the grasshopper laughs in the merry scene,<br> When Mary and Susan and Emily,<br> With their sweet round mouths sing Ha, Ha, He.</p>  
 <p>When the painted birds laugh in the shade<br> Where our table with cherries and nuts is spread<br> Come live &amp; be merry and join with me,<br> To sing the sweet chorus of Ha, Ha, He.</p>
