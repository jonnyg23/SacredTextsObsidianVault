 <img src="img/05.jpg"></p>  <h3 align="center">The Shepherd.</h3>  
 <p>How sweet is the Shepherds sweet lot,<br> From the morn to the evening he strays:<br> He shall follow his sheep all the day<br> And his tongue shall be filled with praise.</p>  
 <p>For he hears the lambs innocent call.<br> And he hears the ewes tender reply.<br> He is watchful while they are in peace,<br> For they know when their Shepherd is nigh.</p>
