 <img src="img/11.jpg"></p>  <h3 align="center">The Blossom.</h3>  
 <p>Merry Merry Sparrow<br> Under leaves so green<br> A happy Blossom<br> Sees you swift as arrow<br> Seek your cradle narrow<br> Near my Bosom.</p>  
 <p>Pretty Pretty Robin<br> Under leaves so green<br> A happy Blossom<br> Hears you sobbing sobbing<br> Pretty Pretty Robin<br> Near my Bosom.</p>
