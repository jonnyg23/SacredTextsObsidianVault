 <img src="img/39.jpg"></p>  <h3 align="center">The SICK ROSE</h3>  
 <p>O Rose thou art sick.<br> The invisible worm,<br> That flies in the night<br> In the howling storm:</p>  
 <p>Has found out thy bed<br> Of crimson joy:<br> And his dark secret love<br> Does thy life destroy.</p>
