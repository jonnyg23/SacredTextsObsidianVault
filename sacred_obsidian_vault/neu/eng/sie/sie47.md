 <img src="img/55.jpg"></p>  <h3 align="center">A DIVINE IMAGE</h3>  
 <p>Cruelty has a Human Heart<br> And Jealousy a Human Face<br> Terror, the Human Form Divine<br> And Secrecy, the Human Dress</p>  
 <p>The Human Dress, is forged Iron<br> The Human Form, a fiery Forge.<br> The Human Face, a Furnace seal’d<br> The Human Heart, its hungry Gorge.</p>
