 <img src="img/41.jpg"></p>  <h3 align="center">The Angel</h3>  
 <p>I Dreamt a Dream! what can it mean?<br> And that I was a maiden Queen:<br> Guarded by an Angel mild:<br> Witless woe, was neer beguil’d!</p>  
 <p>And I wept both night and day<br> And he wip’d my tears away<br> And I wept both day and night<br> And hid from him my hearts delight</p>  
 <p>So he took his wings and fled:<br> Then the morn blush’d rosy red:<br> I dried my tears &amp; armd my fears,<br> With ten thousand shields and spears.</p>  
 <p>Soon my Angel came again:<br> I was arm’d, he came in vain:<br> For the time of youth was fled<br> And grey hairs were on my head</p>
