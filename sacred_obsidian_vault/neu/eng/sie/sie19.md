 <img src="img/25.jpg"></p>  <h3 align="center">Infant Joy</h3>  
 <p>I have no name<br> I am but two days old.—<br> What shall I call thee?<br> I happy am<br> Joy is my name,<br> Sweet joy befall thee!</p>  
 <p>Pretty joy!<br> Sweet joy but two days old.<br> Sweet joy I call thee:<br> Thou dost smile.<br> I sing the while<br> Sweet joy befall thee.</p>
