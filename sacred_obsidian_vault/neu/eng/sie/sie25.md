 <img src="img/31.jpg"></p>  <h3 align="center">EARTH'S Answer.</h3>  
 <p>Earth rais’d up her head,<br> From the darkness dread &amp; drear,<br> Her light fled:<br> Stony dread!<br> And her locks cover’d with grey despair.</p>  
 <p>Prison’d on watry shore<br> Starry Jealousy does keep my den<br> Cold and hoar<br> Weeping o’er<br> I hear the Father of the ancient men</p>  
 <p>Selfish father of men<br> Cruel jealous selfish fear<br> Can delight<br> Chain’d in night<br> The virgins of youth and morning bear.</p>  
 <p>Does spring hide its joy<br> When buds and blossoms grow?<br> Does the sower?<br> Sow by night?<br> Or the plowman in darkness plow?</p>  
 <p>Break this heavy chain,<br> That does freeze my bones around<br> Selfish! vain!<br> Eternal bane!<br> That free Love with bondage bound.</p>
