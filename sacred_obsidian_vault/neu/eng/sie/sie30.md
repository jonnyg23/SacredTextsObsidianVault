 <img src="img/38.jpg"></p>  <h3 align="center">NURSES Song</h3>  
 <p>When the voices of children, are heard on the green<br> And whisprings are in the dale:<br> The days of my youth rise fresh in my mind,<br> My face turns green and pale.</p>  
 <p>Then come home my children, the sun is gone down<br> And the dews of night arise<br> Your spring &amp; your day, are wasted in play<br> And your winter and night in disguise.</p>
