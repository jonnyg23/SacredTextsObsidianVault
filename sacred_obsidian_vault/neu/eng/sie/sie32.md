 <img src="img/40.jpg"></p>  <h3 align="center">THE FLY.</h3>  
 <p>Little Fly<br> Thy summers play,<br> My thoughtless hand<br> Has brush’d away.</p>  
 <p>Am not I<br> A fly like thee?<br> Or art not thou<br> A man like me?</p>  
 <p>For I dance<br> And drink &amp; sing:<br> Till some blind hand<br> Shall brush my wing.</p>  
 <p>If thought is life<br> And strength &amp; breath<br> And the want<br> Of thought is death;</p>  
 <p>Then am I<br> A happy fly,<br> If I live,<br> Or if I die.</p>
