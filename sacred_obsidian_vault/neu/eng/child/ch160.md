 <h1 align="CENTER">160A: The Knight of Liddesdale</h1>
 <pre>
 
 160A.1	 The Countesse of Douglas out of her boure she came,
 	 And loudly there that she did call:
 	 It is for the Lord of Liddesdale
 	 That I let all these teares downe fall.
