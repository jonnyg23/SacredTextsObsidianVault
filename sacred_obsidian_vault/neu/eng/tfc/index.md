 <p>
 This is a modern retelling of the highlights of
 Chaucer's <cite>Canterbury Tales</cite>.
 While it is written for a younger audience, readers of any
 age can enjoy these timeless stories, illustrated by witty
 fairy-tale style color drawings by Arthur Szyk.
 </p>
