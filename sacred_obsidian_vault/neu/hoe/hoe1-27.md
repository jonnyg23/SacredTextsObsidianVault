 <h1 align="center">SECTION I</h1>
 
 <h3 align="center"><i>TALES ILLUSTRATIVE OF THE “KALEVIPOEG”</i></h3>
 
 <p>T<small>HE</small> following stories are thoroughly Esthonian in character, and, with the exception of the first, mostly exhibit variants of the Kalevide’s journeys to Pōrgu.</p>
 
 <p> That of “Slyboots” is also interesting from the resemblance of a portion of it to “Jack and the Beanstalk.”</p>
