 <h2 align="center">THE OFFICIOUS FLIES.</h2>
 
 <p>A <small>FEW</small> dozen flies once attacked a cart-horse who was feeding quietly in a thicket, and lamented that they were not more numerous, that they might make him lie down. Presently his skin began to itch, when he lay down, rolled first on once side and then on the other, and crushed them all.</p>
