 <h2 align="center">CHARM AGAINST SNAKE-BITE.<a name="page_298_fr_1"></a><a href="#page_298_note_1"><font size="1">1</font></a></h2>
 
 <table align="center" cellpadding="10">
 
 <tr><td><small>T<small>HOU</small> beneath the bridge, the smooth wood<br> Under juniper the rough wood,<br> Thou the arrow in the willows,<br> O thou challenged gold-adorned one,<br> Earthy-coloured, liver-coloured,<br> Rainy-hued and hazel-coloured,<br> Firebrand hued and cherry-coloured,<br> Do not thou in secret bite me,<br> Nor attack me unsuspecting,<br> Do not bite me when I heed not.<br></small></td></tr>
 
 </table>
