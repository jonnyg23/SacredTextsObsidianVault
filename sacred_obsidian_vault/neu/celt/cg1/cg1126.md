 <p> </p>  
 <p><small>THIS hymn was sung by a pilgrim in setting out on his pilgrimage. The family and friends joined the traveller <a name="page_317"><font size="1" color="green">p. 317</font></a> in singing the hymn and starting the journey, from which too frequently, for various causes, he never returned.</small></p>

 <p>BITH a bhi na   m’ bhial,<br>   Bladh a bhi na m’ chainn,<br>   Blath na siri na mo bhile,<br>   Gun an tig mi nail.</p>   
 <p>An gaol thug   Iosa Criosda<br>   Bhi lionadh gach cridhe domh,<br>   An gaol thug Iosa Criosda<br>   Da m’ lionadh air an son.</p>   
 <p>Siubhal choire,   siubhal choille,<br>   Siubhal fraoine fada, fas,<br>   Moire mhin-gheal sior dha m’ chobhair,<br>   Am Buachaill Iosa m’ dhion ’s a char.<br>   Moire mhin-gheal sior dha m’ chobhair,<br>   Am Buachaill Iosa m’ dhion ’s a chas.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>LIFE be in my   speech,<br>   Sense in what I say,<br>   The bloom of cherries on my lips,<br>   Till I come back again.</p>   
 <p>The love Christ   Jesus gave<br>   Be filling every heart for me,<br>   The love Christ Jesus gave<br>   Filling me for every one.</p>   
 <p>Traversing   corries, traversing forests,<br>   Traversing valleys long and wild.<br>   The fair white Mary still uphold me,<br>   The Shepherd Jesu be my shield,<br>   The fair white Mary still uphold me,<br>   The Shepherd Jesu be my shield.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>
