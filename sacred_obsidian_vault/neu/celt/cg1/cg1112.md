 <p>BLARAGAN reidh,   fada, farsuinn,<br>   Faileagan feile fo ’r casan,<br>   Cairdeas Mhic De dh’ ar toir dhachaidh<br>   Gu faiche nam fuaran,<br>        Faiche nam fuaran.</p>   
 <p>Gum bu duinte   duibh gach slochd,<br>   Gum bu sumhail duibh gach cnoc,<br>   Gum bu clumhaidh duibh gach nochd,<br>   Am fochar nam fuar-bheann,<br>        Fochar ham fuar-bheann.</p>   
 <p>Comraig   Pheadail agus Phoil,<br>   Comraig Sheumais agus Eoin,<br>   Comraig Bhride mhin ’s Mhuir Oigh,<br>   Dh’ ar comhlach ’s dh’ ar cuallach,<br>   O! comraig gach aon dh’ an chomhl<br>   Dh’ ar comhnadh ’s dh’ ar cuanadh.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>PASTURES smooth,   long, and spreading,<br>   Grassy meads aneath your feet,<br>   The friendship of God the Son to bring you home<br>   To the field of the fountains,<br>        Field of the fountains.</p>   
 <p>Closed be every   pit to you,<br>   Smoothed be every knoll to you,<br>   Cosy every exposure to you,<br>   Beside the cold mountains,<br>        Beside the cold mountains.</p>   
 <p>The care of   Peter and of Paul,<br>   The care of James and of John,<br>   The care of Bride fair and of Mary Virgin,<br>   To meet you and to tend you,<br>        Oh! the care of all the band<br>        To protect you and to strengthen you.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>
