 <p>FUIDHEAGAN no corr   do shnath<br>   Cha do chum ’s cha chum mo lamh.</p>   
 <p>Gach dath a ta   ’s a bhogha-fhrois<br>   Chaidh troimh mo mheoirean fo na chrois,</p>   
 <p>Geal is dubh,   dearg is madar,<br>   Uaine, ciar-ghlas, agus sgarlaid,</p>   
 <p>Gorm, is   grisionn ’s dath na caorach,<br>   ’S caoibean cha robh dhith air aodach.</p>   
 <p>Guidhim Bride   bith na faolachd,<br>   Guidhim Muire min na gaolachd,<br>   Guidhim Iosa Criosd na daonnachd,<br>   Gun mi fein dhol eug a ’n aonais,<br>        Gun mi fein dhol eug a ’n aonais.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>THRUMS nor odds   of thread<br>   My hand never kept, nor shall keep,</p>   
 <p>Every colour in   the bow of the shower<br>   Has gone through my fingers beneath the cross,</p>   
 <p>White and   black, red and madder,<br>   Green, dark grey, and scarlet,</p>   
 <p>Blue, and roan,   and colour of the sheep,<br>   And never a particle of cloth was wanting.</p>   
 <p>I beseech calm   Bride the generous,<br>   I beseech mild Mary the loving,<br>   I beseech Christ Jesu the humane,<br>   That I may not die without them,<br>        That I may not die without them.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>
