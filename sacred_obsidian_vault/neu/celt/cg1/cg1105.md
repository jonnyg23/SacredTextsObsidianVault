 <p>EUDAIL thu   ’s thu dh’an chrodh mhara,<br>   Chra chluasach, bheum chluasach, bheannach;<br>   Chrathadh fual air cruach do sheanar,<br>   ’S cha tar thu uam-s’ a Luan no Sha’urn.<br>         Ho hoiligean, ho m’ aighean!<br>         Ho hoiligean, ho m’ aighean!<br>         Ho hoiligean, ho m’ aighean!<br>         Mo lochruidh chaomh gach taobh an   abhuinn.</p>   
 <p>Eudail thu   ’s thu chrodh na tire.<br>   Bheir thu marrum, bheir thu mis dhomh;<br>   Bheir thu bainne barr na ciob dhomh,<br>   ’S cha b’ e glaisle ghlas an t-siobain.<br>         Ho hoiligean, ho m’ aighean!</p>   
 <p>Eudail thu   ’s thu chrodh an t-saoghail,<br>   Bheir thu bainne barr an fhraoich dhomh;<br>   Cha bhainne glas air bhlas a chaorain,<br>   Ach bainne meal ’s e air gheal na faoileig.<br>         Ho hoiligean, ho m’ aighean!</p>   
 <p>Bheir Bride   bhinn dhut linn is ograidh,<br>   Bheir Moire mhin dhut li dha d’ chomhdach,<br>   Bheir Michael liobha dhut ri dha d’ sheoladh,<br>   ’S bheir Iosda Criosda dhut sith is solas.<br>         Ho hoiligean, ho m’ aighean!</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>MY treasure   thou, and thou art of the sea kine,<br>   Red eared, notch eared, high horned;<br>   Urine was sprinkled on the rump of thy grandsire,<br>   And thou shalt not win from me on Monday nor Saturday.<br>         Ho hoiligean, ho my heifers!<br>         Ho hoiligean, ho my heifers!<br>         Ho hoiligean, ho my heifers!<br>         My kindly kine on each side of the   stream.</p>   
 <p>My treasure   thou, and thou art of the land trine,<br>   Thou wilt give me milk produce, thou wilt give me dainty;<br>   Thou wilt give me milk from the top of the club-moss,<br>   And not the grey water of the sand-drift.<br>         Ho hoiligean, ho my heifers!</p>   
 <p>My treasure   thou, and thou art of the world's kine,<br>   Thou wilt give me milk from the heather tops;<br>   Not grey milk of the taste of the rowan berries,.<br>   But honey milk and white as the sea-gull.<br>         Ho hoiligean, ho my heifers!</p>   
 <p>The melodious   Bride will give thee offspring and young,<br>   The lovely Mary will give thee colour to cover thee,<br>   The lustrous Michael will give thee a star to guide thee,<br>   And Christ Jesu will give thee peace and joy.<br>         Ho hoiligean, ho my heifers!</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>
