 <p>IS math a   ghabhas mi mo rann,<br>   A teurnadh le gleann;<br>       Aon rann,<br>       Da rann,<br>       Tri rann,<br>       Ceithir rann,<br>       Coig rann,<br>       Sia rann,<br>       Seachd rann,<br>       Seachd gu lath rann<br>       Seachd gu lath rann.</p>   
 <p>Nar a gonar   fear an eididh,<br>   Nar a reubar e gu brath,<br>   Cian theid e ’n cath no ’n comhrag,<br>   Sgiath chomarach an Domhnach da,<br>   Can theid e ’n cath no ’n comhrag,<br>   Sgiath chomarach an Domhnach da.</p>   
 <p>Chan ath-aodach   seo, ’s chan fhaoigh e,<br>   ’S cha chuid cleir no sagairt e.</p>   
 <p>Biolair uaine   ga buain fo<br>   ’S air a toir do mhnai gun fhiosd;<br>   Lurg an fheidh an ceann an sgadain,<br>   ’S an caol chalp a bhradain bhric.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>WELL can I say   my rune,<br>   Descending with the glen;<br>       One rune,<br>       Two runes,<br>       Three runes,<br>       Four runes,<br>       Five runes,<br>       Six runes.<br>       Seven runes,<br>       Seven and a half runes,<br>       Seven and a half runes.</p>   
 <p>May the man of   this clothing never be wounded,<br>   May torn he never be;<br>   What time he goes into battle or combat,<br>   May the sanctuary shield of the Lord be his.<br>   What time he goes into battle or combat,<br>   May the sanctuary shield of the Lord be his.</p>   
 <p>This is not   second clothing and it is not thigged,<br>   Nor is it the right of sacristan or of priest.</p>   
 <p>Cresses green   culled beneath a stone,<br>   And given to a woman in secret.<br>   The shank of the deer in the head of the herring,<br>   And in the slender tail of the speckled salmon.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>
