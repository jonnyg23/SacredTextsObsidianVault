 <p>THAINIG thugam   cobhair,<br>   Moire gheal is Bride;<br>   Mar a rug Anna Moire,<br>   Mar a rug Moire Criosda,<br>   Mar a rug Eile Eoin Baistidh<br>   Gun mhar-bhith dha dhi,<br>   Cuidich thusa mise ’m asaid,<br>        Cuidich mi a Bhride!</p>   
 <p>Mar a gheineadh   Criosd am Moire<br>   Comhliont air gach laimh,<br>   Cobhair thusa mise, mhoime,<br>   An gein a thoir bho ’n chnaimh;<br>   ’S mar a chomhn thu Oigh an t-solais,<br>   Gun or, gun odh, gun ni,<br>   Comhn orm-sa, ’s mor m’ othrais,<br>        Comhn orm a Bhride!</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>THERE came to   me assistance,<br>   Mary fair and Bride;<br>   As Anna bore Mary,<br>   As Mary bore Christ,<br>   As Eile bore John the Baptist<br>   Without flaw in him,<br>   Aid thou me in mine unbearing,<br>        Aid me, O Bride!</p>   
 <p>As Christ was   conceived of Mary<br>   Full perfect on every hand,<br>   Assist thou me, foster-mother,<br>   The conception to bring from the bone;<br>   And as thou didst aid the Virgin of joy,<br>   Without gold, without corn, without kine,<br>   Aid thou me, great is my sickness,<br>        Aid me, O Bride!</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>
