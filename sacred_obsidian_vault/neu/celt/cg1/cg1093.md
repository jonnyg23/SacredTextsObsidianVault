 <p>CAIRIDH mi an   tula,<br>   Mar a chaireadh Muire,<br>   Claim Bhride ’s Mhuire,<br>   Car an tula ’s car an lair,<br>   ’S car an ardraich uile.</p>   
 <p>Co iad air lian   a muigh?<br>   Micheal grian-gheal mo luin.<br>   Co iad air meadhon lair?<br>   Eoin, Peadail, agus Pail.<br>   Co iad ri bial mo stoc?<br>   Moire ghrian-gheal ’s a Mac.</p>   
 <p>Bial Dia   dh’ orduich,<br>   Aingheal Dia bhoinich,<br>   Aingheal geal an car an tealla,<br>   Gon tig la geal gu beola.<br>   Aingheal geal an car an tealla,<br>   Gon tig la geal gu beola.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>I WILL build   the hearth,<br>   As Mary would build it.<br>   The encompassment of Bride and of Mary,<br>   Guarding the hearth, guarding the floor,<br>   Guarding the household all.</p>   
 <p>Who are they on   the lawn without?<br>   Michael the sun-radiant of my trust.<br>   Who are they on the middle of the floor?<br>   John and Peter and Paul.<br>   Who are they by the front of my bed?<br>   Sun-bright Mary and her Son.</p>   
 <p>The mouth of   God ordained,<br>   The angel of God proclaimed,<br>   An angel white in charge of the hearth<br>   Till white day shall come to the embers.<br>   An angel white in charge of the hearth<br>   Till white day shall come to the embers.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>
