 <img src="img/03400.jpg" alt="T"></p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>TA mi lubadh mo   ghlun<br>   An suil an Athar a chruthaich mi,<br>   An suil a Mhic a cheannaich mi,<br>   An suil a Spioraid a ghlanaich mi,<br>        Le gradh agus run.</p>   
 <p>Doirt a nuas   oirnn a flathas<br>   Trocair shuairce do mhathas;<br>   Fhir tha ’n uachdar na Cathair,<br>        Dean-sa fathamas ruinn.</p>   
 <p>Tabhair duinn,   a Shlan’ear Aigh,<br>   Eagal De, gaol De, agus gradh,<br>   Is toil De dheanamh air talamh gach re,<br>   Mar ni ainghlich is naoimhich air neamh;<br>   Gach la agus oidhche thoir duinn do sheimh,<br>        Gach la agus oidhche thoir duinn do sheimh.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>I AM bending my   knee<br>   In the eye of the Father who created me,<br>   In the eye of the Son who died for me,<br>   In the eye of the Spirit who cleansed me,<br>        In love and desire.</p>   
 <p>Pour down upon   us from heaven<br>   The rich blessing of Thy forgiveness;<br>   Thou who art uppermost in the City,<br>        Be Thou patient with us.</p>   
 <p>Grant to us,   Thou Saviour of Glory,<br>   The fear of God, the love of God, and His affection,<br>   And the will of God to do on earth at all times<br>   As angels and saints do in heaven;<br>   Each day and night give us Thy peace.<br>        Each day and night give us Thy peace.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>
