 <p align="center"><a name="page_159"><font size="1" color="green">p. 159</font></a></p>   </td>  </tr>  <tr>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="327">   <h3 align="center">BEANNACHADH BLIADHNA UIR [67]</h3>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="327">   <h3 align="center">THE BLESSING OF THE NEW YEAR</h3>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>  
 <p> </p>  
 <p><small>THIS poem was repeated the first thing on the first day of the year. It was <a name="page_159"><font size="1" color="green">p. 159</font></a> common throughout the Highlands and Islands. The writer has heard versions of it in many places.</small></p>  
 <p> </p>  <div align="center">  <table border="0">  <tr>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p><a name="page_158"><font size="1" color="green">p. 158</font></a></p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p><a name="page_159"><font size="1" color="green">p. 159</font></a></p>   </td>  </tr>  <tr>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p><a name="img_15800"></a>
 <img src="img/15800.jpg" alt="D"></p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>DHE, beannaich dhomh   an la ur,<br>   Nach do thuradh dhomh roimhe riamh;<br>   Is ann gu beannachadh do ghnuis,<br>   Thug thu ’n uine seo dhomh, a Dhia.</p>   
 <p>Beannaich thusa   dhomh mo shuil,<br>   Beannaicheadh mo shuil na chi;<br>   Beannaichidh mise mo nabaidh,<br>   Beannaicheadh mo nabaidh mi.</p>   
 <p>Dhe tabhair dhomh-sa   cridhe glan,<br>   Na leig a seall do shula mi;<br>   Beannaich dhomh mo ghin ’s mo bhean,<br>   ’S beannaich domh mo nearc ’s mo ni.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>GOD, bless to   me the new day,<br>   Never vouchsafed to me before;<br>   It is to bless Thine own presence<br>   Thou hast given me this time, O God.</p>   
 <p>Bless Thou to   me mine eye,<br>   May mine eye bless all it sees;<br>   I will bless my neighbour,<br>   May my neighbour bless me.</p>   
 <p>God, give me a   clean heart,<br>   Let me not from sight of Thine eye;<br>   Bless to me my children and my wife,<br>   And bless to me my means and my cattle.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>
