 <p>OIDHCHE sin bha   ’m Buachaill a muigh<br>   Cha deacha buarach air boin,<br>   Cha deacha geum a beul laoigh,<br>   Caoineadh Buachaill a chruidh,<br>        Caoineadh Buachaill a chruidh.</p>   
 <p>     Ho   m’ aghan! ho m’ aghan!<br>        Ho m’ aghan! m’ aghan gaoil<br>        Chridheag chridh, choir, ghradhaich,<br>        Air sgath an Ard Righ gabh ri d’ laogh.</p>   
 <p>Oidhche sin bha   ’m Buachaill air chall,<br>   Fhuaradh anns an Teampull e.<br>   Righ na gile thighinn a nall!<br>   Righ na greine nuas a neamh!<br>        Righ na greine nuas a neamh!</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>THE night the   Herdsman was out<br>   No shackle went on a cow,<br>   Lowing ceased not from the mouth of calf<br>   Wailing the Herdsman of the flock,<br>        Wailing the herdsman of the flock.</p>   
 <p>     Ho   my heifer! ho my heifer!<br>        Ho my heifer! my heifer beloved!<br>        My heartling heart, kind, fond,<br>        For the sake of the High King take to thy calf.</p>   
 <p>The night the   Herdsman was missing,<br>   In the Temple He was found.<br>   The King of the moon to come hither!<br>   The King of the sun down from heaven!<br>        King of the sun down from heaven!</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>
