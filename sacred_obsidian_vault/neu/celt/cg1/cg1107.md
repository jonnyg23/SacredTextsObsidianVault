 <p>THOIR am   bainne, bho dhonn,<br>   Ce ’n conn ma ’n ceillinn?<br>   Laogh na ba ud braigh na beinge,<br>   ’S laogh mo ghraidh-sa air graisich eile.<br>        O! ho! graisich eile.</p>   
 <p>     Thoir   am bainne, bho dhonn,<br>        Moir am bainne, bho dhonn,<br>        Thoir am bainne, bho dhonn,<br>             Trom steilleach.</p>   
 <p>Ach gheobh mo   ghaol-sa laoighean cais-fhionn,<br>   Is buarach caon a theid caomh ma casan;<br>   Cha bhuarach gaoisid, fraoich, no asgairt,<br>   Ach buarach dhaor a bheir daoin a Sasgunn.<br>        O! ho! a Sasgunn.</p>   
 <p>’S gheobh   mo righinn-sa finn na maise<br>   Buarach min a theid sliom ma casan;<br>   Cha bhuarach cioba, lioin, no asgairt,<br>   Ach buarach shiod thig a nios a Sasgunn.<br>        O! ho! a Sasgunn.</p>   
 <p>’S gheobh   mo chiall-sa fiar is fasga,<br>   ’S gheobh i aonach, fraoch, is machair,<br>   ’S gheobh i mislean, ciob, is fasbhuain,<br>   ’S gheobh i am fion thig ’o shian nan cas-bheann.<br>        O! ho! nan cas-bheann.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>GIVE thy milk,   brown cow,<br>   For what reason should I conceal?<br>   The [skin of the] calf of yonder cow on the partition,<br>   While the calf of my love is on another grange.<br>        Oh! ho! another grange.</p>   
 <p>     Give   thy milk, brown cow,<br>        Give thy milk, brown cow,<br>        Give thy milk, brown cow,<br>             Heavily flowing.</p>   
 <p>My beloved   shall get white-bellied calves,<br>   And a fetter fine that shall go kindly round her legs;<br>   No fetter of hair, nor of heather, nor of lint refuse,<br>   But a dear fetter that men bring from Saxon land.<br>        Oh! ho! from Saxon land.</p>   
 <p>And my queen   maiden of beauty shall get<br>   A fetter smooth to go softly round her legs;<br>   No fetter of cord, nor of lint, nor lint refuse,<br>   But a fetter of silk up from Saxon land.<br>        Oh! ho! from Saxon land.</p>   
 <p>My beloved   shall get grass and shelter,<br>   She shall get hill, heath, and plain,<br>   She shall get meadow-grass, club-rush, and stubble,<br>   And she shall get the wine that comes from the elements of the steep bens.<br>        Oh! ho! the steep bens.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>
