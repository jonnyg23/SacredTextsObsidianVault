 <p>DHE stiuir mi   le d’ ghliocas,<br>   Dhe smachd mi le d’ cheartas,<br>   Dhe foir mi le d’ throcair,<br>   Dhe comh’n mi le d’ chumhachd.</p>   
 <p>Dhe lion mi le   d’ lanachd,<br>   Dhe dion mi le d’ sgaileachd,<br>   Dhe lion mi le d’ ghrasachd,<br>   Air sgath do Mhic Unga.</p>   
 <p>Iosa Criosda a   shiol Dhaibhidh,<br>   Fear-tathaich an teampuill,<br>   Uan-iobairt a gharaidh,<br>   A bhasaich air mo shon.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>GOD guide me   with Thy wisdom,<br>   God chastise me with Thy justice,<br>   God help me with Thy mercy,<br>   God protect me with Thy strength.</p>   
 <p>God fill me   with Thy fullness,<br>   God shield me with Thy shade,<br>   God fill me with Thy grace,<br>   For the sake of Thine Anointed Son.</p>   
 <p>Jesu Christ of   the seed of David,<br>   Visiting One of the Temple,<br>   Sacrificial Lamb of the Garden,<br>   Who died for me.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>
