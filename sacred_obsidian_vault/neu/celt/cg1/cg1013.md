 <p>BEANNAICH, a Thriath   nam flath fial,<br>   Mi fein ’s gach sion a ta na m’ choir,<br>   Beannaich mi ’n am uile ghniomh,<br>   Dean mi tearuinte ri m’ bheo,<br>          Dean mi tearuinte ri m’ bheo.</p>   
 <p>Bho gach   gruagach is ban-sith,<br>   Bho gach mi-run agus bron,<br>   Bho gach glaistig is ban-nigh,<br>   Gach luch-sith agus luch-feoir,<br>          Gach luch-sith agus luch-feoir.</p>   
 <p>Bho gach fuath   bhiodh feadh nam beann<br>   Bho gach greann bhiodh teann d’ am thoir,<br>   Bho gach uruisg measg nan gleann,<br>   Teasruig mi gu ceann mo lo,<br>          Teasruig mi gu ceann mo lo.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>BLESS, O Chief   of generous chiefs,<br>   Myself and everything anear me,<br>   Bless me in all my actions,<br>   Make Thou me safe for ever,<br>          Make Thou me safe for ever.</p>   
 <p>From every   brownie and ban-shee,<br>   From every evil wish and sorrow,<br>   From every nymph and water-wraith,<br>   From every fairy-mouse and grass-mouse,<br>          From every fairy-mouse and   grass-mouse.</p>   
 <p>From every   troll among the hills,<br>   From every siren hard pressing me,<br>   From every ghoul within the glens,<br>   Oh! save me till the end of my day.<br>          Oh! save me till the end of my day.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>
