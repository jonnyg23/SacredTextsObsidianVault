 <p>NIS tha mis air   tighinn dh’ ur duthaich<br>   A dh’ urachadh dhuibh na Callaig;<br>   Cha leig mi leas a dhol ga innse,<br>   Bha i ann ri linn ar seanar.</p>   
 <p>Dirim ris an   ardorus,<br>   Teurnam ris an starsach,<br>   Mo dhuan a ghabhail doigheil,<br>   Modhail, moineil, maineil.</p>   
 <p>Caisean Callaig   ’na mo phoca,<br>   Is mor an ceo thig as an ealachd.<br>   *           *           *           *</p>   
 <p>Gheibh fear an   taighe ’na dhorn e,<br>   Cuiridh e shron anns an teallach;<br>   Theid e deiseil air na paisdean,<br>   Seachd ar air bean an taighe.</p>   
 <p>Bean an taighe   is i is fhiach e,<br>   Lamh a riarach oirnn na Callaig,<br>   Sochair bheag a bhlath an t-samhraidh,<br>   Tha mi ’n geall air leis an arain.</p>   
 <p>Tabhair duinn   ma dh’ fhaodas,<br>   Mar a faod na cum maill oirnn,<br>   Mise gille Mhic De ’s an dorus,<br>   Eirich fein is fosgail domh e.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>I AM now come   to your country,<br>   To renew to you the Hogmanay,<br>   I need not tell you of it,<br>   It was in the time of our forefathers.</p>   
 <p>I ascend by the   door lintel,<br>   I descend by the doorstep,<br>   I will sing my song becomingly,<br>   Mannerly, slowly, mindfully.</p>   
 <p>The Hogmanay   skin is in my pocket,<br>   Great will be the smoke from it presently.<br>   *           *           *           *</p>   
 <p>The house-man   will get it in his hand,<br>   He will place its nose in the fire;<br>   He will go sunwards round the babes,<br>   And for seven verities round the housewife.</p>   
 <p>The housewife   it is she who deserves it,<br>   The hand to dispense to us the Hogmanay,<br>   A small gift of the bloom of summer,<br>   Much I wish it with the bread.</p>   
 <p>Give it to us   if it be possible,<br>   If you may not, do not detain us;<br>   I am the servant of God's Son at the door,<br>   Arise thyself and open to me.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>
