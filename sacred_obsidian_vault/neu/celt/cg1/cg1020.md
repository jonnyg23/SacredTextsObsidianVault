 <p>CROIS nan naomh   agus nan aingeal liom<br>   Bho fhrois m’ aodain gu faobhar mo bhonn.</p>   
 <p>   *       *       *       *       *       *</p>   
 <p>A Mhicheil   mhil, a Mhoire ghlorach,<br>   A Bhride mhin nan dualan orach,<br>   Dionaibh mi ’s a cholunn bhronach,<br>   Dionadh tri mi air sligh via corach.<br>        O! tri mi air sligh na corach.</p>   
 <p>Dionaibh mi   ’s a choich-anama bhochd,<br>   Dionaibh mi ’s mi cho diblidh nochd,<br>   Dionaibh mi air sligh gun lochd,<br>   Dionadh tri air mo thi a nochd.<br>        O! tri air mo thi a nochd.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>THE cross of   the saints and of the angels with me<br>   From the top of my face to the edge of my soles.</p>   
 <p>   *       *       *       *       *       *</p>   
 <p>O Michael mild,   O Mary of glory,<br>   O gentle Bride of the locks of gold,<br>   Preserve ye me in the weakly body,<br>   The three preserve me on the just path.<br>        Oh! three preserve me on the just path.</p>   
 <p>Preserve ye me   in the soul-shrine poor,<br>   Preserve ye me, and I so weak and naked,<br>   Preserve ye me without offence on the way,<br>   The preservation of the three upon me to-night.<br>        Oh! the three to shield me to-night.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>
