 <p>HEIRE Bannag,   hoire Bannag,<br>           Heire Bannag, air a bheo.</p>   
 <p>Mac na niula,   Mac na neula,<br>   Mac na runna, Mac na reula,<br>           Heire Bannag, etc.</p>   
 <p>Mac na dile,   Mac na deire,<br>   Mac na spire, Mac na speura,<br>           Heire Bannag, etc.</p>

 <p>HEY the Gift,   ho the Gift,<br>           Hey the Gift, on the living.</p>   
 <p>Son of the   dawn, Son of the clouds,<br>   Son of the planet, Son of the star,<br>           Hey the Gift, etc.</p>   
 <p>Son of the   rain, Son of the dew,<br>   Son of the welkin, Son of the sky,<br>           Hey the Gift, etc.</p>   
 <p>Son of the   flame, Son of the light,<br>   Son of the sphere, Son of the globe,<br>           Hey the Gift, etc.</p>   
 <p>Son of the   elements, Son of the heavens,<br>   Son of the moon, Son of the sun,<br>           Hey the Gift, etc.</p>
