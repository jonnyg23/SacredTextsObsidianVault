 <p>DHE, tha mi   liuthail m’ aodainn,<br>   Anns na naodh gatha greine,<br>   Mar a liuthail Moire a Mac,<br>        Am bainne brac breine.</p>   
 <p>Meilc a bhi   ’na m’ aodann,<br>   Maon a bhi ’na m’ ghnuis,<br>   Mire meala ’na mo theanga,<br>        M’ anail mar an tuis.</p>   
 <p>Is dubh an   taigh ud thall,<br>   Is duibhe daoine a th’ ann;<br>   Is mis an eala bhan,<br>        Banruinn os an ceann.</p>   
 <p>Falbhaidh mi an   ainme Dhia,<br>   An riochd fiadh, an riochd each,<br>   An riochd nathar, an riochd righ,<br>        Is cathar mi na gach neach.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>GOD, I am   bathing my face<br>   In the nine rays of the sun,<br>   As Mary bathed her Son<br>        In generous milk fermented.</p>   
 <p>Sweetness be in   my face,<br>   Riches be in my countenance,<br>   Comb-honey be in my tongue,<br>        My breath as the incense.</p>   
 <p>Black is yonder   house,<br>   Blacker men therein;<br>   I am the white swan,<br>        Queen over them.</p>   
 <p>I will go in   the name of God,<br>   In likeness of deer, in likeness of horse,<br>   In likeness of serpent, in likeness of king,<br>        More victorious am I than all persons.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>
