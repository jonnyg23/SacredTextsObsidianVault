 <p>THIG, a Mhuire,   ’s bligh a bho,<br>   Thig, a Bhride, ’s comraig i,<br>     Thig, a Chaluim-chille chaoimh,<br>       ’S iadh do dha laimh mu m’ bhoin.<br>         Ho m’ aghan, ho m’ agh gaoil,<br>         Ho m’ aghan, ho m’ agh gaoil,<br>         Ho m’ aghan, ho m’ agh gaoil,<br>         M’ aghan cri, coir, gradhach,<br>           An sgath an Ard Righ gabh ri   d’ laogh.</p>   
 <p>Thig, a Mhuire,   dh’ fhios mo bho,<br>   Thig, a Bhride mhor na loin,<br>   Thig, a bhanachaig Iosda Criosda,<br>     ’S cur do lamh a nios fo m’ bhoin.<br>       Ho m’ aghan, ho m’ agh gaoil.</p>   
 <p>Bo lurach   dhubh, bo na h-airidh,<br>   Bo a bha-theach, mathair laogh,<br>   Luban siomain air crodh na tire,<br>     Buarach shiod air m’ aighean gaoil.<br>       Ho m’ aghan, ho m’ agh gaoil.</p>   
 <p>Mo bho dhubh,   mo bho dhubh,<br>   Is ionann galar dhomh-s’ is dhuit,<br>   Thus a caoidh do luran laoigh,<br>     Mise mo mhac gaoil fo ’n mhuir,<br>       M’aon mhac gaoil fo ’n mhuir.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>COME, Mary, and   milk my cow,<br>   Come, Bride, and encompass her,<br>   Come, Columba the benign,<br>     And twine thine arms around my. cow.<br>       Ho my heifer, ho my gentle heifer,<br>       Ho my heifer, ho my gentle heifer,<br>       Ho my heifer, ho my gentle heifer,<br>       My heifer dear, generous and kind,<br>         For the sake of the High King take to thy   calf.</p>   
 <p>Come, Mary   Virgin, to my cow,<br>   Come, great Bride, the beauteous,<br>   Come, thou milkmaid of Jesus Christ,<br>     And place thine arms beneath my cow.<br>       Ho my heifer, ho my gentle heifer.</p>   
 <p>Lovely black   cow, pride of the <a href="errata.htm#3">sheiling</a>,<br>   First cow of the byre, choice mother of calves,<br>   Wisps of straw round the cows of the townland,<br>     A shackle of silk on my heifer beloved.<br>       Ho my heifer, ho my gentle heifer.</p>   
 <p>My black cow,   my black cow,<br>   A like sorrow afflicts me and thee,<br>   Thou grieving for thy lovely calf,<br>     I for my beloved son under the sea,<br>       My beloved only son under the sea.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>
