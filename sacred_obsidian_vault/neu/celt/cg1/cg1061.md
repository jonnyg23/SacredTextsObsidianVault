 <p>HOIRE! hoire!   beannaicht e! beannaicht e!<br>   Hoire! hoire! beannaicht e! beannaicht e!<br>         Ho! hi! beannaicht an Righ!<br>         Ho! hi! biodh aoibh.</p>   
 <p>Buaidh biodh   air an tulaich seo,<br>   Na chualas leibh ’s na chunnas leibh,<br>   Air na leaca loma loinnear lair,<br>         ’S air na clacha corrach cuimir   clair,<br>         Hoire! hoire! beannaicht e! beannaicht e!</p>   
 <p>Beannaich an   taigh ’s na bheil ann,<br>   Eadar chuaill is chlach is chrann<br>   Imir do Dhia eadar bhrat is aodach,<br>         Slainte dhaoine gun robh ann,<br>         Hoire! hoire! beannaicht e! beannaicht e!</p>   
 <p>Gu mu buan   mu’n tulach sibh,<br>   Gu mu slan mu’n teallach sibh,<br>   Gu mu liuth dul ’s ceann sguilb ’s an aros,<br>         Daoine tamh ’s a bhunntair,<br>         Hoire! hoire! beannaicht e! beannaicht e!</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>HAIL King! hail   King! blessed is He! blessed is He!<br>   Hail King! hail King! blessed is He! blessed is He!<br>         Ho, hail! blessed the King!<br>         Ho, hi! let there be joy!</p>   
 <p>Prosperity be   upon this dwelling,<br>   On all that ye have heard and seen,<br>   On the bare bright floor flags,<br>         On the shapely standing stone staves,<br>         Hail King! hail King! blessed is He!   blessed is He!</p>   
 <p>Bless this   house and all that it contains,<br>   From rafter and stone and beam;<br>   Deliver it to God from pall to cover,<br>         Be the healing of men therein,<br>         Hail King! hail King! blessed is He!   blessed is He!</p>   
 <p>Be ye in   lasting possession of the house,<br>   Be ye healthy about the hearth,<br>   Many be the ties and stakes in the homestead,<br>         People dwelling on this foundation,<br>         Hail King! hail King! blessed is He!   blessed is He!</p>   </td>  </tr>  <tr>   <td valign="top" align="top">

 <p>Iobair dh   ’an Ti eadar bhonn agus bhrat,<br>   Eadar chuaill agus chlach agus chrann;<br>   Iobair a ris eadar shlat agus aodach,<br>   Slanadh shaoghal a dhaoine th’ ann,<br>         Hoire! hoire! beannaicht e! beannaicht e!<br>         Hoire! hoire! beannaicht e! beannaicht e!<br>              Ho, hi,   beannaicht an Righ,<br>                  Ho,   hi, biodh aoibh!</p>   
 <p>         Beannaicht   an Righ,<br>            Gun tus gun chrich,<br>            Gu suth, gu sior,<br>            Gach linn gu brath,<br>                  Ho!   hi! biodh aoibh!</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>Offer to the   Being from found to cover,<br>   Include stave and stone and beam;<br>   Offer again both rods and cloth,<br>   Be health to the people therein,<br>         Hail King! hail King! blessed is He!   blessed is He!<br>         Hail King! hail King! blessed is He!   blessed is He!<br>              Ho, hail!   blessed the King!<br>                  Let   there be joy!</p>   
 <p>         Blessed   the King,<br>            Without beginning,   without ending,<br>            To everlasting, to   eternity,<br>                  Every   generation for aye,<br>                  Ho!   hi! let there be joy!</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>
