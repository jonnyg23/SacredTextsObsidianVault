 <p>LABHRAM gach la   a reir do cheartais,<br>   Gach la taisbim do smachd, a Dhe;<br>   Labhram gach la a reir do reachd-sa,<br>   Gach la is oidhche bithim toigh riut fein.</p>   
 <p>Gach la cunntam   fath do throcair,<br>   Toirim gach la dha do nosda speis;<br>   Gach la tionnsgam fein dhut oran,<br>   Teillim gach la do ghloir, a Dhe.</p>   
 <p>Beirim gach la   gaol dhut, Iosa,<br>   Gach oidhche nithim da reir;<br>   Gach la ’s oidhche, duar is soillse,<br>   Luaidhim do chaoibhneas dhomh, a Dhe.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>MAY I speak   each day according to Thy justice,<br>   Each day may I show Thy chastening, O God;<br>   May I speak each day according to Thy wisdom,<br>   Each day and night may I be at peace with Thee.</p>   
 <p>Each day may I   count the causes of Thy mercy,<br>   May I each day give heed to Thy laws;<br>   Each day may I compose to Thee a song,<br>   May I harp each day Thy praise, O God.</p>   
 <p>May I each day   give love to Thee, Jesu,<br>   Each night may I do the same;<br>   Each day and night, dark and light,<br>   May I laud Thy goodness to me, O God.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>
