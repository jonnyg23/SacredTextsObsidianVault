 <p>HOIRE! hoire!   beannaicht e! beannaicht e!<br>   Hoire! hoire! beannaicht e! beannaicht e!<br>   Hoire! hoire! beannaicht e’n Righ dh’ am bi sinn a’ seinn,<br>           Ho! ro! biodh aoibh!</p>   
 <p>Nochd oidhche   Nollaige moire,<br>   Rugadh Mac na Moir Oighe,<br>   Rainig a bhonnaibh an lar,<br>   Mac nam buadh a nuas o’n ard,<br>   Dhealraich neamh is cruinne dha,<br>           Ho! ro! biodh aoibh!</p>   
 <p>Seimh saoghal   dha, sona neamh dha,<br>   Feuch rainig a bhonn an lar,<br>   Fodhail Righ dha, failt Uain dha,<br>   Righ nam buadh, Uan nan agh,<br>   Shoillsich cluan agus cuanta dha,<br>           Ho! ro! biodh aoibh!</p>   
 <p>Shoillsich   frith dha, shoillsich fonn dha,<br>   Nuall nan tonn le fonn nan tragh,<br>   Ag innse dhuinne gun d’ rugadh Criosda<br>   Mac Righ nan righ a tir na slaint;<br>   Shoillsich grian nam beannaibh ard dha,<br>           Ho! ro! biodh aoibh!</p>   
 <p>Shoillsich ce   dha is cruinne comhla,<br>   Dh’ fhosgail De an Domhnaich Dorus;<br>   A Mhic Mhuir Oighe greas ga’m chomhnadh,<br>   A Chriosd an dochais, a Chomhla ’n t-sonais,<br>   Oradh Ghreine shleibh is mhonaidh,<br>           Ho! ro! biodh aoibh!</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>HAIL King! hail   King! blessed is He! blessed is He!<br>   Hail King! hail King! blessed is He! blessed is He!<br>   Hail King! hail King! blessed is He, the King of whom we sing,<br>           All hail! let there be joy!</p>   
 <p>This night is   the eve of the great Nativity,<br>   Born is the Son of Mary the Virgin,<br>   The soles of His feet have reached the earth,<br>   The Son of glory down from on high,<br>   Heaven and earth glowed to Him,<br>           All hail! let there be joy!</p>   
 <p>The peace of   earth to Him, the joy of heaven to Him,<br>   Behold His feet have reached the world;<br>   The homage of a King be His, the welcome of a Lamb be His,<br>   King all victorious, Lamb all glorious,<br>   Earth and ocean illumed to Him,<br>           All hail! let there be joy!</p>   
 <p>The mountains   glowed to Him, the plains glowed to Him,<br>   The voice of the waves with the song of the strand,<br>   Announcing to us that Christ is born,<br>   Son of the King of kings from the land of salvation;<br>   Shone the sun on the mountains high to Him,<br>   All hail! let there be joy!</p>   
 <p>Shone to Him   the earth and sphere together,<br>   God the Lord has opened a Door;<br>   Son of Mary Virgin, hasten Thou to help me,<br>   Thou Christ of hope, Thou Door of joy,<br>   Golden Sun of hill and mountain,<br>           All hail! let there be joy!</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>
