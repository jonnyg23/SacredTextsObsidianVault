 <p>AN Domhnach   naomha do Dhe<br>   Tabhair do chre dh’ an chinne-daon,<br>   Do t’ athair is do d’ mhathair chaomh,<br>   Thar gach aon ’s gach ni ’s an t-saoghal.</p>   
 <p>Na dean sainn   air mhor no bheag,<br>   Na dean tair air tais no truaigh,<br>   Fiamh an uilc a d’ choir na leig,<br>   Na tabhair ’s na toill masladh uair.</p>   
 <p>Na deich fana   thug Dia duit,<br>   Tuig gun dail iad agus dearbh,<br>   Creid direach an Righ nan dul,<br>   Cuir air chul uidh thoir a dhealbh.</p>   
 <p>Bi dileas da   d’ thighearna-cinn,<br>   Bi dileas da d’ righ ’s gach eang,<br>   Bi dileas duit fein a ris,<br>   Dileas da d’ Ard Righ thar gach dreang.</p>   
 <p>Na tabhair   toi’eum do neach air bith,<br>   An earail toi’eum a thoir ort fein,<br>   ’S ged shiubhladh tu cuan is cith,<br>   Lean cas-cheum Aon-unga Dhe.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>ON the holy   Sunday of thy God<br>   Give thou thine heart to all mankind,<br>   To thy father and thy mother loving,<br>   Beyond any person or thing in the world.</p>   
 <p>Do not covet   large or small,<br>   Do not despise weakling or poor,<br>   Semblance of evil allow not near thee,<br>   Never give nor earn thou shame.</p>   
 <p>The ten   commands God gave thee,<br>   Understand them early and prove,<br>   Believe direct in the King of the elements,<br>   Put behind thee ikon-worship.</p>   
 <p>Be faithful to   thine over-lord,<br>   Be true to thy king in every need,<br>   Be true to thine own self besides,<br>   True to thy High-King above all obstacles.</p>   
 <p>Do not thou   malign any man,<br>   Lest thou thyself maligned shouldst be,<br>   And shouldst thou travel ocean and earth,<br>   Follow the very step of God's Anointed.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>
