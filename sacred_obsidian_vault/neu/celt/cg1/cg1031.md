 <p>O IOS, a nochd,<br>   Aghair nam bochd,<br>   Cholann gun lochd,<br>   Dh’ fhuilinn gu gort,<br>   Fo bhinn nan olc,<br>   ’S a cheusadh.</p>   
 <p>Saor mi bho   olc,<br>   Saor mi bho lochd,<br>   Caomhain mo chorp,<br>   Naomhaich mi nochd,<br>   O Ios, a nochd,<br>   ’S na treig mi.</p>   
 <p>Bairig domh   neart,<br>   Aghair nam feart,<br>   Stiuir mi ’na d’ cheart,<br>   Stiuir mi ’na d’ neart,<br>   O Ios, ’na d’ neart<br>   Gleidh mi.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>O JESU!   to-night,<br>   Thou Shepherd of the poor,<br>   Thou sinless person<br>   Who didst suffer full sore,<br>   By ban of the wicked,<br>   And wast crucified.</p>   
 <p>Save me from   evil,<br>   Save me from harm,<br>   Save Thou my body,<br>   Sanctify me to-night,<br>   O Jesu! to-night,<br>   Nor leave me.</p>   
 <p>Endow me with   strength,<br>   Thou Herdsman of might.<br>   Guide me aright,<br>   Guide me in Thy strength,<br>   O Jesu! in Thy strength<br>   Preserve me.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>
