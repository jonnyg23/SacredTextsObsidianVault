 <p>O IOS gun   lochd,<br>   A Righ nam bochd,<br>   A chiosadh gort<br>   Fo bhinn nan olc,<br>   Dion-s, an nochd,<br>        Bho Iudas mi.</p>   
 <p>M’ anam   air do laimh, a Chriosda,<br>   A Righ na Cathrach Neomh,<br>   Is tu cheannaich m’ anam, Iosa,<br>        Is tu dh’ iobair beatha dhomh.</p>   
 <p>Teasruig mi air   sgath mo sprochd,<br>   Air sgath do phais, do lot is t’ fhala fein,<br>   Is tabhair tearuint mi an nochd<br>        Am fochar Cathair De.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>O JESU without   sin,<br>   King of the poor,<br>   Who wert sorely subdued<br>   Under ban of the wicked,<br>   Shield Thou me this night<br>        From Judas.</p>   
 <p>My soul on   Thine own arm, O Christ,<br>   Thou the King of the City of Heaven,<br>   Thou it was who bought’st my soul, O Jesu,<br>        Thou it was who didst sacrifice Thy life for   me.</p>   
 <p>Protect Thou me   because of my sorrow,<br>   For the sake of Thy passion, Thy wounds, and Thine own blood,<br>   And take me in safety to-night<br>        Near to the City of God.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>
