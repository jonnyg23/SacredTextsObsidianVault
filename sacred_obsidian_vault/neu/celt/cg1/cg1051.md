 <p>FAILTE dhuit, a   Mhoire Mhathair!<br>   Tha thu lan dhe na grasan caomh,<br>   Tha ’n Tighearna Dia maille riut a ghnath.<br>   Beannaicht thu, Mhairi, am measg nam mnai,<br>   Beannaicht toradh do bhronn, Iosa,<br>   Beannaicht thu, Righinn an ais;<br>   A Naomh Mhoire, a Mhathair Iosa,<br>   Guidh air mo shon-sa, peacach truagh,<br>   Nis agus aig uair mo bhais,<br>        Nis agus aig uair mo bhais!</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>HAIL to thee,   Mary, Mother!<br>   Thou art full of loving grace,<br>   The Lord God is always with thee,<br>   Blessed art thou Mary among women,<br>   Blessed is the fruit of thy womb, Jesus,<br>   Blessed art thou, Queen of grace;<br>   Thou holy Mary, thou Mother of Jesus,<br>   Plead for me a miserable sinner,<br>   Now and at the hour of death,<br>        Now and at the hour of death!</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>
