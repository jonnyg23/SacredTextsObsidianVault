 <p> </p>  
 <p><small>THE kindling of the fire is a work full of interest to the housewife. When 'lifting' the fire in the morning the woman prays, in an undertone, that the fire may be blessed to her and to her household, and to the glory <a name="page_231"><font size="1" color="green">p. 231</font></a> of God who gave it. The people look upon fire as a miracle of Divine power provided for their good--to warm their bodies when they are cold, to cook their food when they are hungry, and to remind them that they too, like the fire, need constant renewal mentally and physically.</small></p>

 <p>TOGAIDH mi mo   theine an diugh,<br>   An lathair ainghlean naomha neimh,<br>   An lathair Airil is ailde cruth,<br>   An lathair Uiril nan uile sgeimh,<br>   Gun ghnu, gun tnu, gun fharmad.<br>   Gun ghiomh, gun gheimh roimh neach fo’n ghrein,<br>   Ach Naomh Mhac De da m’ thearmad.<br>        Gun ghnu, gun tnu, gun fharmad,<br>        Gun ghiomh, gun gheimh, roimh neach fo’n   ghrein,<br>        Ach Naomh Mhac De da m’ thearmad.</p>   
 <p>Dhe fadaidh   fein na m’ chridhe steach,<br>   Aingheal ghraidh do m’ choimhearsnach,<br>   Do m’ namh, do m’ dhamh, do m’ chairde,<br>   Do ’n t-saoidh, do ’n daoidh, do ’n traille.<br>   A Mhic na Moire min-ghile,<br>   Bho ’n ni is isde crannchaire,<br>   Gu ruig an t-Ainm is airde.<br>        A Mhic na Moire min-ghile,<br>        Bho ’n ni is isde crannchaire,<br>        Gu ruig an t-Ainm is airde.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">

 <p>I WILL kindle   my fire this morning<br>   In presence of the holy angels of heaven,<br>   In presence of Ariel of the loveliest form,<br>   In presence of Uriel of the myriad charms,<br>   Without malice, without jealousy, without envy,<br>   Without fear, without terror of any one under the sun,<br>   But the Holy Son of God to shield me.<br>        Without malice, without jealousy, without envy,<br>        Without fear, without terror of any one under   the sun,<br>        But the Holy Son of God to shield me.</p>   
 <p>God, kindle   Thou in my heart within<br>   A flame of love to my neighbour,<br>   To my foe, to my friend, to my kindred all,<br>   To the brave, to the knave, to the thrall,<br>   O Son of the loveliest Mary,<br>   From the lowliest thing that liveth,<br>   To the Name that is highest of all.<br>        O Son of the loveliest Mary,<br>        From the lowliest thing that liveth,<br>        To the Name that is highest of all.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>
