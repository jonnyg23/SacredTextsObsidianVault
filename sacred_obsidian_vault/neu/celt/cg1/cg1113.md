 <p>GUN gleidheadh   Moire min an ciob,<br>   Gun gleidheadh Bride bith an ciob,<br>   Gun gleidheadh Calum-cille an ciob,<br>   Gun gleidheadh Maol-ribhe an ciob,<br>   Gun gleidheadh Carmag an ciob,<br>   O'n mhi-chu ’s o’n mharhh-chu.</p>   
 <p>Gun gleidheadh   Odhran an crodh,<br>   Gun gleidheadh Maodhan an crodh,<br>   Gun gleidheadh Donnan an crodh,<br>   Gun gleidheadh Moluag an crodh,<br>   Gun gleidheadh Maolruan an crodh,<br>   Am boglach ’s an crualach.</p>   
 <p>Gun gleidheadh   Spiorad foir an treud,<br>   Gun gleidheadh Mac Moir Oigh an treud,<br>   Gun gleidheadh Ti na gloir an treud,<br>   Gun gleidheadh an Teoir an treud,<br>   Bho reubain ’s bho mhearchall,<br>       Bho reubain ’s bho mhearchall.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>MAY Mary the   mild keep the sheep,<br>   May Bride the calm keep the sheep,<br>   May Columba keep the sheep,<br>   May Maolruba keep the sheep,<br>   May Carmac keep the sheep,<br>   From the fox and the wolf.</p>   
 <p>May Oran keep   the kine,<br>   May Modan keep the kine,<br>   May Dorman keep the kine,<br>   May Moluag keep the kine,<br>   May Maolruan keep the kine,<br>   On soft land and hard land.</p>   
 <p>May the Spirit   of peace preserve the flocks,<br>   May the Son of Mary Virgin preserve the flocks,<br>   May the God of glory preserve the flocks,<br>   May the Three preserve the flocks,<br>   From wounding and from death-loss,<br>       From wounding and from death-loss.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>
