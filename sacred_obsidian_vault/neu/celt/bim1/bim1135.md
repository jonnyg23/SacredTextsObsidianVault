 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Barddas of Iolo Morganwg, Vol. I.</i>, ed. by J. Williams Ab Ithel, [1862], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">THE MATERIALS.</h3>  
 <p>The five materials of every existence and life: calas; fluidity; air; <a name="fr_349"></a><a href="#fn_347"><font size="1">2</font></a> sun; <a name="fr_350"></a><a href="#fn_348"><font size="1">3</font></a> and nwyvre. <a name="fr_351"></a><a href="#fn_349"><font size="1">4</font></a></p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p><a name="page_384"><font size="1" color="green">p. 384</font></a> <a name="page_385"><font size="1" color="green">p. 385</font></a></p>  
 <p>They are thus variously arranged:--</p>  
 <p>Calas, fluidity, and nwyvre; <a name="fr_352"></a><a href="#fn_350"><font size="1">1</font></a> and the uvel unites with the nwyvre, the air unites with the uvel, the water, or fluidity, unites with the air, and the calas unites with fluidity.</p>
