 <p><a name="page_114"><font size="1" color="green">p. 114</font></a> <a name="page_115"><font size="1" color="green">p. 115</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">THE THREE SYMBOLS.</h3>  
 <p>There are three kinds of symbol: the symbol of letter, which the ancient authors called the symbol of cyrven; the symbol of number; and the symbol of blazonry, as a coat of arms, which belongs to the art of a herald-bard.</p>
