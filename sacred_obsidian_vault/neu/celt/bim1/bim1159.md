 <p><a name="page_418"><font size="1" color="green">p. 418</font></a> <a name="page_419"><font size="1" color="green">p. 419</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">THE ALBANS.</h3>  
 <p>They are as follows in the Book of Sion Howel Gwyn--the Book of Tre’rbryn:--</p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Alban Elved is the calend of October;<br> Alban Arthan is the calend of January;<br> Alban Eilir is the calend of spring;<br> Alban Hevin is the calend of summer.</div>
