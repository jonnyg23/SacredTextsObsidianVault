Williams Ab Ithel, [1862], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">THE PARTS OF THE HUMAN BODY IN WHICH ARE THE FACULTIES.</h3>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">1. In the forehead are the sense and intellect;<br> 2. In the nape is the memory;</div>  
 <p><a name="page_392"><font size="1" color="green">p. 392</font></a> <a name="page_393"><font size="1" color="green">p. 393</font></a></p>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">3. In the pate are discretion and reason;<br> 4. In the breast is lust;<br> 5. In the heart is love;<br> 6. In the bile are anger and wrath;<br> 7. In the lungs is the breath;<br> 8. In the spleen is joyousness;<br> 9. In the body is the blood;<br> 10. In the liver is the heat;<br> 11. In the spirit is the mind;<br> 12. In the soul is faith.</div>
