Williams Ab Ithel, [1862], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">TRIADS OF BARDISM. <a name="fr_327"></a><a href="#fn_325"><font size="1">2</font></a></h3>  
 <p>39. The three privileges of Gwynvyd: the complete predominance of love over hatred; complete power, under</p>  
 <p>
 </p>  
 <p><a name="page_358"><font size="1" color="green">p. 358</font></a> <a name="page_359"><font size="1" color="green">p. 359</font></a></p>  
 <p>the privilege of right and bliss, derived from universal knowledge; and complete satisfaction with every thing, since every opposition and Cythraul have ceased.</p>  
 <p>40. Three things that are endless: fire, that is, light; life, that is, God; and understanding, that is, truth.</p>  
 <p>41. The three necessities of all animations: vocation; awen; and privilege; and there is nothing else in the primary nature of these that is thoroughly one with it.</p>  
 <p>42. The three necessities prior to perfect knowledge: to see; to suffer; and to remember every thing, in every state of life.</p>  
 <p>43. Three things which man knows not what they are: God; nonentity; and infinitude.</p>  
 <p>44. The three sources of knowledge: reason; nature; and impulse.</p>
