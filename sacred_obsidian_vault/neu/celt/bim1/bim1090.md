 <p><a name="page_214"><font size="1" color="green">p. 214</font></a> <a name="page_215"><font size="1" color="green">p. 215</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">THE THREE IMPERCEPTIBILITIES OF GOD.</h3>  
 <p>There are three things that are imperceptible in God: His origin, because there can have been no time in which He did not exist; the greatness of His love, for how much so ever He does, He will see no end to what He can in justice accomplish; and His power, because there is no end, size, or measure to what He can do after the utmost intention.</p>
