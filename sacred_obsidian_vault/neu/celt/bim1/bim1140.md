 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Barddas of Iolo Morganwg, Vol. I.</i>, ed. by J. Williams Ab Ithel, [1862], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">THE SEVEN MATERIALS OF MAN.</h3>  
 <p>1. Earth, from which is the body;</p>  
 <p>2. Water, from which are the blood and humour;</p>  
 <p>3. Sun, from which are the heat and light;</p>  
 <p>4. Air, from which are the breath and motion;</p>  
 <p>5. Nwyvre, from which are the feeling and affection;</p>  
 <p>6. The Holy Ghost, from Whom are the reason and understanding;</p>  
 <p>7. God, from Whom is life everlasting.</p>  
 <p align="right">The Blue Bard of the Chair has said it.</p>
