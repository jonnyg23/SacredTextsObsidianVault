 <center><h1>Dedication to Dowager Lady Chatterton</h1></center>
   <p>
 <br>
 
   </p>
 
   
 
 
 <p align="center">
 TO THE</p>
 
 
 <p align="center">
 DOWAGER LADY CHATTERTON,</p>
 
 <p align="center">
 CASTLE MAHON.</p>
 
 <p>
  </p>
 
 <p>
  </p>
 
   <p>
 THEE, Lady, would I lead through Fairy-land<br>
 
   (Whence cold and doubting reasoners are exiled),<br>
 
   A land of dreams, with air-built castles piled;<br>
 
   The moonlight SHEFROS there, in merry band<br>
 
   With arful CLURICAUNE, should ready stand<br>
 
   To welcome thee - Imagination's child!<br>
 
   Till on thy ear would burst so sadly wild<br>
 
   The BANSHEE'S shriek, who points with wither'd hand<br>
 
   In the dim twilight should the PHOOKA come,<br>
 
   Whose dusky form fades in the sunny light,<br>
 
   That opens clear calm LAKES upon thy sight,<br>
 
   Where blessed spirts dwell in endless bloom.<br>
 
   I know thee, Lady - thou wilt not deride<br>
 
   Such Fairy Scenes. - Then onward with thy Guide.</p>
 
   <p>
  </p>
 
   <p>
 T. Crofton Croker</p>
