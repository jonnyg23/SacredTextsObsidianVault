 <p>
 This is the first of four volumes of Campbell's collection of Scottish
 folklore.
 This volume has many stories which have been anthologized elsewhere, as well
 as the extensive introduction which not only serves to introduce Scottish
 folklore, but as a prolegomena to the study of oral folklore in general.
 </p>
 <p align="RIGHT">
 <font size="-1">
 --John Bruno Hare, March 26th, 2004
