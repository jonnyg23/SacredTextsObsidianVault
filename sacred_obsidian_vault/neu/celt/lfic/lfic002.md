 <p align="CENTER">Dedication</p>
 <p> </p>
 <p align="CENTER">TO</p>
 <p> </p>
 <p align="CENTER">JOSEPH SHERIDAN LE FANU, ESQ.</p>
 <p align="CENTER">AUTHOR OF "UNCLE SILAS," ETC.</p>
 <p>DEAR SIR,</p>
 <p>I beg to offer this collection of Irish Legends to your acceptance, from respect for your high position among English writers, in consideration of your being a truly good man, and in gratitude for your kind encouragement of my own literary attempts. Without that encouragement I should probably never have an opportunity of penning a dedication to any one.</p>
 <p>I am, dear Sir,</p>
 <p>Your faithful Servant</p>
 <p>PATRICK KENNEDY.</p>
 <p>LOUGH NA PIASTHA COTTAGE.</p>
