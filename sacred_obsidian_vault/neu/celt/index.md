 <p>
 Some of these books and texts are translations of Celtic legends
 and sagas; others are retellings of the material, folklore, or
 literary works based on Celtic themes.
 During the 19th and early 20th Century there was a resurgence of interest in
 the folklore of Ireland, Wales and Scotland.  This was reflected in
 a huge amount of literature based on the rich Celtic mythopoetic heritage,
 most of it very faithful to the original material,
 some in a more modern voice.</p>
 
 <p>
 A good number of these files were originally scanned by Phillip
 Brown for his (now defunct) Celtic Folklore website.
 These are indicated by [PB].
