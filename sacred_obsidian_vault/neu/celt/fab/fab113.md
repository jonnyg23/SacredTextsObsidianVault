 <h3 align="CENTER">XCVIII.</h3>
 <h3 align="CENTER">THE PLEASANT THINGS OF TALIESSIN.</h3>
 <h3 align="CENTER">BOOK OF TALIESSIN IV.</h3>
 <p align="CENTER"></p>
 <p>A PLEASANT virtue, extreme, penance to an extreme course;<br>
 Also pleasant, when God is delivering me.<br>
 Pleasant, the carousal that hinders not mental exertion;<br>
 Also pleasant, to drink together about horns.<br>
 Pleasant is Nud, the superior wolf-lord;<br>
 Also pleasant, a generous one at Candlemas tide.<br>
 Pleasant, berries in the time of harvest;<br>
 Also pleasant, wheat upon the stalk.<br>
 Pleasant, the sun moving in the firmament;<br>
 10 Also pleasant, the retaliators of outcries.<br>
 Pleasant,, a steed with a thick mane in a tangle; <a name="page_551"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 551</font></a><br>
 Also pleasant, crackling fuel.<br>
 Pleasant, desire, and silver fringes;<br>
 Also pleasant, the conjugal ring.<br>
 Pleasant, the eagle on the shore of the sea when it flows;<br>
 Also pleasant, sea-gulls playing.<br>
 Pleasant, a horse with gold-enamelled trappings;<br>
 Also pleasant to be holiest in a breach.<br>
 Pleasant, liquors of the mead-brewer to the multitude;<br>
 20 Also pleasant, a songster generous, amiable.<br>
 Pleasant, the open field to cuckoos and the nightingale;<br>
 Also pleasant when the weather is serene.<br>
 Pleasant, right, and a perfect wedding;<br>
 Also pleasant, a present that is loved.<br>
 Pleasant, a meal from the penance of a priest;<br>
 Also pleasant to bring to the altar.<br>
 Pleasant, mead in a court to a minstrel,<br>
 Also pleasant, the limiting a great crowd.<br>
 Pleasant, the catholic clergy in the church,<br>
 30 Also pleasant, a minstrel in the hall.<br>
 Pleasant to bring back the divisions of a parish;<br>
 Also pleasant to us the time of paradise.<br>
 Pleasant, the moon, a luminary in the heavens;<br>
 Also pleasant where there is a good rememberer.<br>
 Pleasant, summer, and slow long day;<br>
 Also pleasant to pass out of chastisement.<br>
 Pleasant, the blossoms on the tops of the pear-trees;<br>
 Also pleasant, friendship with the Creator.<br>
 Pleasant, the solitary doe and the fawn;<br>
 40 Also pleasant, the foamy horseblock.<br>
 Pleas-ant, the camp when the leek flourishes;<br>
 Also pleasant, the charlock in the springing corn.<br>
 Pleasant, a steed in a leather halter;<br>
 Also pleasant, alliance with a king.<br>
 Pleasant, the hero that destroys not the yielding; <a name="page_552"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 552</font></a><br>
 Also pleasant, the splendid Cymraec language.<br>
 Pleasant, the heath when it is green;<br>
 Also pleasant, the salt marsh for cattle.<br>
 Pleasant, the time when calves draw milk;<br>
 50 Also pleasant, foamy horsemanship.<br>
 And what is pleasant to me is no worse.<br>
 And. the paternal horn by mead-nourished payment.<br>
 Pleasant, the directing of fish in the pond;<br>
 Also pleasant, calling about to play.<br>
 Pleasant, the word that utters the Trinity;<br>
 Also pleasant, extreme penance for sin.<br>
 Pleasant, the summer of pleasantness;<br>
 Communion with the Lord, in the day of judgment.</p>
