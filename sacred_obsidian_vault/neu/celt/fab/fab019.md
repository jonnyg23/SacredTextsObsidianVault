 <h3 align="CENTER">III.</h3>
 <h3 align="CENTER">THE DEATH-SONG OF EROF.</h3>
 <h3 align="CENTER">BOOK OF TALIESSIN XL.</h3>
 <p align="CENTER"></p>
 <p>WERE changed the elements<br>
 Like night into day,<br>
 When came the gloriously-free,<br>
 Erewlf chief of baptism.<br>
 Erewlf said,<br>
 That he valued not death.<br>
 Shield of the Mordei<br>
 Upon him it broke.<br>
 Erewlf the arranger,<br>
 10 Determined, frantic.<br>
 Four columns of equal length;<br>
 Ruddy, gold along them.<br>
 The columns of Erewlf<br>
 Will not dare a threatening,<br>
 A threatening will not dare.<br>
 The heat of the sun did not leave him.<br>
 No one went to heaven<br>
 Until went he,<br>
 Erewlf the wall-piercer.<br>
 20 May the sand be my covering,<br>
 May the Trinity grant me<br>
 Mercy on the day of judgment,<br>
 In unity without want.</p>
