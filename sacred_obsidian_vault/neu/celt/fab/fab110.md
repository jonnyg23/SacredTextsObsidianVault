 <h3 align="CENTER">XCV.</h3>
 <h3 align="CENTER">SONG TO THE LITTLE WORLD.</h3>
 <h3 align="CENTER">BOOK OF TALIESSIN LVI.</h3>
 <p align="CENTER"></p>
 <p>THE beautiful I sang of, I will sing.<br>
 The world one day more.<br>
 Much I reason,<br>
 And I meditate.<br>
 I will address the bards of the world,<br>
 Since it is not told me<br>
 What supports the world,<br>
 That it falls not into vacancy.<br>
 Or if the world should fall,<br>
 10 On what would it fall? <a name="page_542"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 542</font></a><br>
 Who would uphold it?<br>
 The world, how it comes again,<br>
 When it falls in decay,<br>
 Again in the enclosing circle.<br>
 The world, how wonderful it is,<br>
 That it falls not at once.<br>
 The world, how peculiar it is,<br>
 So great was it trampled on.<br>
 Johannes, Mattheus,<br>
 20 Lucas, and Marcus,<br>
 They sustain the word<br>
 Through the grace of the Spirit.</p>
