 <p>
 This anthology of Celtic folklore tales by Joseph Jacobs is
 one of the best of the crop.
 It includes some great tales such as "Munachar and Manachar",
 the "Brewery of Eggshells", and "Fair, Brown and Trembling".
 Jacobs also includes an extensive Notes and References section
 if you want to follow up on each tale.
 </p>
