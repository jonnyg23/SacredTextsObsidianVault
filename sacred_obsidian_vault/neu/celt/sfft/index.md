 <p>
 This is a collection of Scottish folklore which will appeal to all ages.
 There are animal tales, stories of the fairies of Scotland including
 Brownies, Bogles, Kelpies, Mermaids and others,
 and tales of Witches and of Giants.
 While many of the themes are similar to other European folk-tales,
 this collection emphasizes specifically Scottish aspects of the stories.
 </p>
