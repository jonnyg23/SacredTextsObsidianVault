 <p align="center"><a name="img__01000"></a>
 <img src="img/_01000.jpg"></p>  <div align="center">  <table border="0">  <tr>   <td valign="top" align="top" width="362">   
 <p><i>Ah, Mona's isle, fair Mona's isle,<br>   No land so dear as thou to me;<br>   Thy gorse and heather covered hills,<br>   With waterfalls and sparkling rills,<br>   Which join the bright green sea</i>.</p>   
 <p><i>I love to wander in solitude<br>   By the banks of thy gurgling streams,<br>   Or sit and muse on a mossy stone<br>   Of fairy-lore, buggane, and gnome,<br>   Screen’d from the sungod's beams</i>.</p>   
 <p><i>’Tis sweet to ramble alone,<br>   At eve o’er the silvery sand,<br>   Watching the waves in the moonlight gleam,<br>   Now here, now there, in frolic they seem<br>   To coyley kiss the land.</i></p>   
 <p><i>Each valley, mountain, and glen,<br>   Waterfall, streamlet, and sea,<br>   Cavern, rock, harbour, and bay,<br>   Last home of the Elfin and Fay,<br>   Fair Mona, are all dear to me</i>.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>
