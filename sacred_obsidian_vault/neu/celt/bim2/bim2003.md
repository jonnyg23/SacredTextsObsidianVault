 <p align="center">(From the Book of Llywelyn Sion.)</p>  
 <p>The following are the Triads of the Bards of the Isle of Britain, and the memorials and records thereof, as preserved by the voice of Gorsedd of the Bards of the Isle of Britain--together with their essence and nature.</p>  
 <p>When the Cymry first came into the island of Britain from the Country of Summer, where they had been previously</p>
