 <p align="CENTER">Love Dreams</p>
 <blockquote>The girl who wishes to see her future husband must go out and gather certain herbs in the light of the full moon of the new year, repeating this charm--</blockquote><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <blockquote>"Moon, moon, tell unto me <br>
 When my true love I shall see?<br>
 What fine clothes am I to wear?<br>
 How many children shall I bear?<br>
 For if my love comes not to me<br>
 Dark and dismal my life will be."</blockquote>
 <blockquote>Then the girl, cutting three pieces of clay from the sod with a black-hafted knife, carries them home, ties them up in the left stocking with the right garter, places the parcel under her pillow, and dreams a true dream of the man she is to marry and of all her future fate.</blockquote></dir>
