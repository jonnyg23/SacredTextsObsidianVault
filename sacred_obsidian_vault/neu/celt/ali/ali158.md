 <p align="CENTER">A Charm for Safety</p>
 <blockquote>Pluck ten blades of yarrow, keep nine, and cast the tenth away for tithe to the spirits. Put the nine in your stocking, under the heel of the right foot<i>, </i>when going a journey, and the Evil One will have no power over you.</blockquote>
