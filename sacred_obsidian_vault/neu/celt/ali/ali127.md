 <p align="CENTER">For a Sore Breast</p>
 <blockquote>To be said in Irish, while a piece of butter is rubbed over the breast--</blockquote>
 <blockquote>"O Son, see how swelled is the breast of the woman! O, you that bore a Son, look at it yourself! O Mary! O King of Heaven, let this woman be healed! AMEN."</blockquote>
