 <p align="CENTER">For Rheumatism</p>
 <blockquote>The operator makes passes, like the mesmerist, over the member affected by the rheumatic pain, never touching the part, but moving his hand slowly over it at some distance, while he mutters a form of words in a low voice.</blockquote>
