 <p align="CENTER">For Love</p>
 <blockquote>This is a charm I set for love; a woman's charm of love and desire; a charm of God that none can break--</blockquote>
 <blockquote>"You for me, and I for thee and for none else; your face to mine, and your head turned away from all others."</blockquote>
 <blockquote>This is to be repeated three times secretly, over a drink given to the one beloved.</blockquote>
