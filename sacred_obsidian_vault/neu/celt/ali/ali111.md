 <p align="CENTER">For a Pain in the Side</p>
 <blockquote>"God save you, my three brothers, God save you! And how far have ye to go, my three brothers?"</blockquote>
 <blockquote>"To the Mount of Olivet, to bring back gold for a cup to hold the tears of Christ."</blockquote>
 <blockquote>"Go, then. Gather the gold; and may the tears of Christ fall on it, and thou wilt be cured, both body and soul."</blockquote>
 <blockquote>These words must be said while a drink is given to the patient.</blockquote>
