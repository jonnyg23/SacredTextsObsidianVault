 <p align="CENTER">Against Enemies</p>
 <blockquote>Three things are of the Evil One--</blockquote><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <blockquote>An evil eye;<br>
 An evil tongue;<br>
 An evil mind.</blockquote>
 <blockquote>Three things are of God; and these three are what Mary told to her Son, for she heard them in heaven--</blockquote>
 <blockquote>The merciful word;<br>
 The singing word;<br>
 And the good word.</blockquote>
 <blockquote>May the power of these three holy things be on all the men and women of Erin for evermore.</blockquote></dir>
