 <p align="CENTER">To Cause Hatred Between Lovers</p>
 <blockquote>Take a handful of clay from a new-made grave, and shake it between them, saying--</blockquote>
 <blockquote>"Hate ye one another! May ye be as hateful to each other as sin to Christ, as bread eaten without blessing is to God."</blockquote>
