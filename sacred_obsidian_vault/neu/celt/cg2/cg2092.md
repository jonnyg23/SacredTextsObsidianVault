 <p>RI la do   shlainte,<br>   Cha dean thu crabhadh,<br>   Cha tabhair thu taine,<br>        ’S cha tar thu tuis;</p>   
 <p>Ceann an   ardain,<br>   Cridhe na gabhachd,<br>   Beul gun fhaigheam,<br>        ’S cha nar leat cuis.</p>   
 <p>Ach thig do   gheamhradh,<br>   Is cruas do theanndachd,<br>   Is bidh do cheann mar<br>        Am meall ’s an uir;</p>   
 <p>Do luth air   failing,<br>   Do chruth air fhagail,<br>   Is tu na do thraill,<br>        Air do dha ghlun.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>IN the day of   thy health,<br>   Thou wilt not give devotion,<br>   Thou wilt not give kine,<br>        Nor wilt thou offer incense;</p>   
 <p>Head of   haughtiness,<br>   Heart of greediness,<br>   Mouth unhemmed,<br>        Nor ashamed art thou.</p>   
 <p>But thy winter   will come,<br>   And the hardness of thy distress,<br>   And thy head shall be as<br>        The clod in the earth</p>   
 <p>Thy strength   having failed,<br>   Thine aspect having gone,<br>   And thou a thrall,<br>        On thy two knees.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>
