 <p>OBA mho-ghil,<br>   A chuir Moir Oighe,<br>   Chon ighinn Dorail,<br>   Nan or-bhi cuach,<br>   A nall air mor-thir,<br>   A nall air oir-thir,<br>   A nall air log-thir,<br>   A nall air cuan,<br>   Chon casga sula,<br>   Chon casga dula,<br>   Chon casga tnutha,<br>   Chon casga fuatha,<br>   Chon tilleadh breotaich,<br>   Chon tilleadh greotaich,<br>   Chon tilleadh sreotaich,<br>   Chon tilleadh ruaidh.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>THE spell   fair-white,<br>   Sent of Mary Virgin,<br>   To the daughter of Dorail,<br>   Of the golden-yellow hair,<br>   Hither on main-land,<br>   Hither on coast-land,<br>   Hither on lake-land,<br>   Hither on ocean,<br>   To thwart eye,<br>   To thwart net,<br>   To thwart envy,<br>   To thwart hate.<br>   To repel 'breotaich,'<br>   To repel 'greotaich,'<br>   To repel 'sreotaich,'<br>   To repel rose.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>
