 <p>BUAINIDH mi a chathair   aigh<br>   A bhuain Criosd le leth-laimh.</p>   
 <p>Thainig Ard   Righ nan aingeal<br>   Le ghradh ’s le fhath os mo chionn.</p>   
 <p>Thainig Iosa   Criosda steach<br>   Le bliochd, le blachd, le barr,<br>   Le laoigh bhoirionn, le ais.</p>   
 <p>Air suil bhig,   air suil mhoir,<br>   Air uachdar cuid Chriosd.</p>   
 <p>An ainm Ti nan   dul<br>   Cum rium do ghras,<br>   Crun Righ nan aingeal,<br>   Bainne chur an uth ’s an ar,<br>   Le laoigh bhoirionn, le al.</p>   
 <p>Gun robh agaibh   fad nan seachd bliadhna<br>   Gun chall laogh, gun chall bainne,<br>   Gun chall maona no caomh charaid.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>I WILL pluck the   gracious yarrow<br>   That Christ plucked with His one hand.</p>   
 <p>The High King   of the angels<br>   Came with His love and His countenance above me.</p>   
 <p>Jesus Christ   came hitherward<br>   With milk, with substance, with produce,<br>   With female calves, with milk product.</p>   
 <p>On small eye,   on large eye,<br>   Over Christ's property.</p>   
 <p>In name of the   Being of life<br>   Supply me with Thy grace,<br>   The crown of the King of the angels<br>   To put milk in udder and gland,<br>   With female calves, with progeny.</p>   
 <p>May you have   the length of seven years<br>   Without loss of calf, without loss of milk,<br>   Without loss of means or of dear friends.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>
