 <p>BUAINIDH mi an   earnaid,<br>   Le earlaid a bruth,<br>   Chur barrlait air gach ainreit,<br>   Fad ’s is earnaid i.</p>   
 <p>Earnaid shith,   earnaid shith,<br>   Mo niarach an neach dh’ am bi,<br>   Ni bheil ni mu iadhadh grein,<br>   Nach bheil di-se le buaidh reidh.</p>   
 <p>Buainidh mi a   chraobh urramach<br>   Bhuain Moire mhor, Mathair chobhair an t-sluaigh,<br>   Chur dhiom gach sgeula sguana, sgulanach,<br>   Dim-bith, dim-baigh, dim-buaidh,<br>   Fuailisg, guailisg, duailisg, doilisg,<br>   Gun teid mi dh’ an fhuar lic fo’n talamh.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>PLUCK will I   the fairy wort,<br>   With expectation from the fairy bower,<br>   To overcome every oppression,<br>   As long as it be fairy wort.</p>   
 <p>Fairy wort,   fairy wort,<br>   I envy the one who has thee,<br>   There is nothing the sun encircles,<br>   But is to her a sure victory.</p>   
 <p>Pluck will I   mine honoured plant<br>   Plucked by the great Mary, helpful Mother of the people,<br>   To cast off' me every tale of scandal and flippancy,<br>   Ill-life, ill-love, ill-luck,<br>   Hatred, falsity, fraud and vexation,<br>   Till I go in the cold grave beneath the sod.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>
