 <p>DIA na gile,   Dia na greine,<br>   Dh’ orduich dhuinne Mac na meine.<br>   Muire min gheal air a glun,<br>   Criosda nigh nan dul ’n a h-uchd.<br>   Is mise an cleireach stucanach,<br>   Dol timcheall nan clach stacanach,<br>   Is leir dhomh tulach, is leir dhomh traigh,<br>   Is leir dhomh ainghlean air an t-snamh,<br>   Is leir dhomh calpa cuimir, cruinn,<br>   A tighinn air tir le cairdeas duinn.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>GOD of the   moon, God of the sun,<br>   Who ordained to us the Son of mercy.<br>   The fair Mary upon her knee,<br>   Christ the King of life in her lap.<br>   I am the cleric established,<br>   Going round the founded stones,<br>   I behold mansions, I behold shores,<br>   I behold angels floating,<br>   I behold the shapely rounded column<br>   Coming landwards in friendship to us.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>
