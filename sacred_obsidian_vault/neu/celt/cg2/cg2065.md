 <p>EOLAS ta agam   air a ghalar fhuail,<br>        Air a ghalar a ta buan;<br>   Eolas ta agam air a ghalar dhearg,<br>        Air a ghalar a ta garg.</p>   
 <p>Mar a ruitheas   abhuinn fhuar,<br>        Mar a mheileas muileann luath,<br>   Fhir a dh’orduich tir is muir,<br>        Casg air fhuil, ruith air fhual.</p>   
 <p>An ainm Athar,   agus Mic,<br>   An ainm Spioraid Naoimh.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>I HAVE a charm   for the gravel disease,<br>        For the disease that is perverse<br>   I have a charm for the red disease,<br>        For the disease that is irritating.</p>   
 <p>As runs a river   cold,<br>        As grinds a rapid mill,<br>   Thou who didst ordain land and sea,<br>        Cease the blood and let flow the urine.</p>   
 <p>In name of   Father, and of Son,<br>        In name of Holy Spirit.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>
