 <p>BUAINIDH mis an   eidheann-mu-chrann,<br>   Mar a bhuain Moire le a leth-laimh,<br>   Mar a dh’ orduich Righ nan dul,<br>   Bainne chur an uth ’s an ar,<br>   Le laoigh bhreaca, bhoirionn, bhailgneach,<br>   Mar a thubhradh anns an dailgneachd,<br>   Air an laraich seo gu ceann la ’s bliadhna,<br>   A uchd Dia nan dul ’s nan cursa comhla.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>I WILL pluck   the tree-entwining ivy,<br>   As Mary plucked with her one hand,<br>   As the King of life has ordained,<br>   To put milk in udder and gland,<br>   With speckled fair female calves,<br>   As was spoken in the prophecy,<br>   On this foundation for a year and a day,<br>   Through the bosom of the God of life, and of all the powers.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>
