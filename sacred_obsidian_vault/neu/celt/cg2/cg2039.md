 <p>BUAINIDH mi an   dearg-chasachan aic,<br>   An lion a bhuain Bride mhin tromh glaic,<br>   Air buaidh shlainte, air buaidh chairdeas<br>       Air buaidh thoileachais,<br>   Air buaidh droch run, air buaidh droch shul,<br>       Air buaidh chronachais.<br>   Air buaidh droch bheud, air buaidh droch bheus,<br>       Air buaidh ghonachais,<br>   Air buaidh droch sgeul, air buaidh droch bheul,<br>       Air buaidh shonachais--<br>       Air buaidh shonachais.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>PLUCK will I   the little red-stalk of surety,<br>   The lint the lovely Bride drew through her palm,<br>   For success of health, for success of friendship,<br>       For success of joyousness,<br>   For overcoming of evil mind, for overcoming of evil eye,<br>       For overcoming of bewitchment,<br>   For overcoming of evil deed, for overcoming of evil conduct,<br>       For overcoming of malediction,<br>   For overcoming of evil news, for overcoming of evil words,<br>       For success of blissfulness--<br>       For success of blissfulness.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>
