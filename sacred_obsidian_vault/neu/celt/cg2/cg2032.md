 <p>CUIRIM an oba   seo ri m’ shuil,<br>   Mar a dh’ orduich Righ nan dul,<br>   Oba Pheadail, oba Phoil,<br>   Oba Sheumais, oba Eoin,<br>   Oba Chaluim-chille chaoimh,<br>   Oba Phadra sar gach naoimh,<br>   Oba Bhride bhith nam ba,<br>   Oba Mhoire mhin nan agh,<br>   Oba tromla, oba treuid,<br>   Oba lomra, oba spreidh,<br>   Oba nolla, oba ni,<br>   Oba sona, oba sith,<br>   Oba troga, oba treuin,<br>   An treas oba is fearr fo’n ghrein,<br>   Oba bhuadha nan Tri Bhuadh,<br>        Athar, Mic, Spioraid buan.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>I PLACE this   spell to mine eye,<br>   As the Ring of life ordained,<br>   Spell of Peter, spell of Paul,<br>   Spell of James, spell of John,<br>   Spell of Columba benign,<br>   Spell of Patrick, chief of saints,<br>   Spell of Bride, tranquil of the kine,<br>   Spell of Mary, lovely of the joys,<br>   Spell of cows, spell of herds,<br>   Spell of sheep, spell of flocks,<br>   Spell of greatness, spell of means,<br>   Spell of joy, spell of peace,<br>   Spell of war, spell of the brave,<br>   The third best spell under the sun,<br>   The powerful spell of the Three Powers,<br>        Father, Son, Spirit everlasting.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>
