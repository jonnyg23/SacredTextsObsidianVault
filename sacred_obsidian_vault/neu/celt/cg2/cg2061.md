 <p>BUAINIDH mi a   chloimh chat,<br>   Mar a bhuain Mathair Chriosda tromh glac,<br>   Air bhuaidh, air bhuar, air bhleoghann,<br>   Air chual, ’s air thoradh na tana,<br>   Gun chall uan, gun chall caora,<br>   Gun chall maosa, gun chall lara,<br>   Gun chall bo, gun chall laogha,<br>   Gun chall maona, gun chall carda,<br>        A uchd Ti nan dui,<br>        ’S nan cursa comhla.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>I WILL pluck   the catkin wool,<br>   As plucked the Mother of Christ through her palm,<br>   For luck, for kine, for milking,<br>   For herds, for increase, for cattle,<br>   Without loss of lamb, without loss of sheep,<br>   Without loss of goat, without loss of mare,<br>   Without loss of cow, without loss of calf,<br>   Without loss of means, without loss of friends,<br>        From the bosom of the God of life,<br>        And the courses together.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>
