 <p>BUAINIDH mi mo   choinneachan,<br>   Mar choinneamh ri mo naomh,<br>   Chasga fuath nam fear foille,<br>        Agus boile nam ban baoth.</p>   
 <p>Buainidh mi   m’ achlasan,<br>   Mar achainidh ri m’ Righ,<br>   Gur liom-sa buaidh an achlasain,<br>        Thar gach neach a chi.</p>   
 <p>Buainim an   duille gu h-ard,<br>   Mar a dh’ orduich an t-Ard Righ,<br>   An ainm Tri Naomh nan agh,<br>        Agus Moire, Mathair Chriosd.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>I WILL pluck   what I meet,<br>   As in communion with my saint,<br>   To stop the wiles of wily men,<br>        And the arts of foolish women.</p>   
 <p>I will pluck my   Columba plant,<br>   As a prayer to my King,<br>   That mine be the power of Columba's plant,<br>        Over every one I see.</p>   
 <p>I will pluck   the leaf above,<br>   As ordained of the High King,<br>   In name of the Three of glory,<br>        And of Mary, Mother of Christ.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>
