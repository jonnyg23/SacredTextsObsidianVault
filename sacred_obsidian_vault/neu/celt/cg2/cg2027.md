 <img src="img/05600.jpg" alt="G"></p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>GE be co rinn   duit an t-suil,<br>   Gun curn i air fein,<br>   Gun curn i air a thur,<br>   Gun curn i air a spreidh,<br>   Air a chaillich mhungaich,<br>   Air a chaillaich mhiongaich,<br>   Air a chaillaich mhangaich,<br>   ’S air a chaillich gheur-luirg,<br>   A dh’ eirich ’s a mhaduinn,<br>   ’S a suil ’n a seilbh,<br>   ’S a seilbh ’n a seoin,<br>   Nar a leatha a buaile fein,<br>   Nar a leatha leth a deoin,<br>   A chuid nach ith na fithich di,<br>   Gun ith na h-eoin.</p>   
 <p>Ceathrar a rinn   duit an t-suil,<br>   Fear agus bean, mac agus mum;<br>   Triuir a thilgeas diot an tnu,<br>   Athair agus Mac, agus Spiorad Numh.</p>   
 <p>Mar a thog   Criosd am meas,<br>   Thar bharra nam preas,<br>   Gun ann a thogas e dhiot-s’ a nis<br>   Gach cnid, gach tnu, gach farmad,<br>   O’n la’n diugh gu la deireannach do shaoghail.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>WHOSO laid on   thee the eye,<br>   May it lie upon himself,<br>   May it lie upon his house,<br>   May it lie upon his flocks,<br>   On the shuffling carlin,<br>   On the sour-faced carlin,<br>   On the bounding carlin,<br>   On the sharp-shanked carlin,<br>   Who arose in the morning,<br>   With her eye on her flocks,<br>   With her flocks in her 'seoin,'<br>   May she never own a fold,<br>   May she never have half her desires,<br>   The part of her which the ravens do not eat,<br>   May the birds devour.</p>   
 <p>Four made to   thee the eye,<br>   Man and dame, youth and maid;<br>   Three who will cast off thee the envy,<br>   The Father, the Son, and the Holy Spirit.</p>   
 <p>As Christ   lifted the fruit,<br>   From the branches of the bushes,<br>   May He now lift off thee<br>   Every ailment, every envy, every jealousy,<br>   From this day forth till the last day of thy life.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>
