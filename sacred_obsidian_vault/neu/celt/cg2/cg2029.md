 <p>THAINIG dithis   a mach<br>   A Cathrach Neobh,<br>   Fear agus bean,<br>   A dheanadh nan ōisnean.</p>   
 <p>Mallaich dha na   beana bur-shuileach,<br>   Mallaich dha na feara fur-shuileach,<br>   Mallaich dha na ceithir saighde, guineach, guid,<br>   Dh’ fhaodadh a bhi ’n aorabh duine ’s bruid.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>THERE came two   out<br>   From the City of Heaven,<br>   A man and a woman,<br>   To make the 'ōisnean.'</p>   
 <p>Curses on the   blear-eyed women,<br>   Curses on the sharp-eyed men,<br>   Curses on the four venomous arrows of disease,<br>   That may be in the constitution of man and beast.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>
