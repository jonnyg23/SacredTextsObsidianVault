 <p>CHAIDH Criosda   ri croich,<br>   Sgiuch cas eich;<br>   Thainig Criosda ri lar,<br>   Shlanaich a chas.</p>   
 <p>Mar a shlanaich   sin<br>   Gun slanaich seo,<br>   Ma ’s e thoil a dheanamh,<br>   A uchd Ti nan dul,<br>   Agus Triuir na Trianaid,<br>        Ti nan dul,<br>        Triuir na Trianaid.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>CHRIST went on   the cross,<br>   Sprained the leg of a horse;<br>   Christ came to the ground,<br>   Whole became the leg.</p>   
 <p>As that was   made whole<br>   May this become whole,<br>   If His will be so to do,<br>   Through the bosom of the God of life,<br>   And of the Three of the Trinity,<br>        The God of life,<br>        The Three of Trinity.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>
