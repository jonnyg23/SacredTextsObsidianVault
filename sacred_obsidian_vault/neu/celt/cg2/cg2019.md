 <p>            SIAN   a chuir Moir air a Mac,<br>               Sian   romh mharbhadh, sian romh lot,<br>               Sian   eadar cioch agus glun,<br>               Sian   eadar glun agus lorc,<br>               Sian   nan tri sian,<br>               Sian   nan coig sian,<br>               Sian   nan seachd sian,<br>               Eadar   barr do chinn<br>               Agus   bonn do chos.<br>               Sian   nan seachd paidir, a h-aon,<br>               Sian   nan seachd paidir, a dha,<br>               Sian   nan seachd paidir, a tri,<br>               Sian   nan seachd paidir, a ceithir,<br>               Sian   nan seachd paidir, a coig,<br>               Sian   nan seachd paidir, a sia,<br>               Sian   nan seachd paidir, a seachd<br>                    Ort   a nis.<br>               Bho   chlaban do bhathas,<br>               Gu   dathas do bhonn,<br>               Ga   d’ chumail o d’ chul,<br>               Ga   d’ chumhn o t’ aghaidh.</p>   
 <p>Clogad slainne   mu d’ cheann,<br>   Cearcul comhnant mu d’ bhraigh,<br>   Uchd-eididh an t-sagairt mu d’ bhrollach,<br>   Ga d’ dhion an cogadh ’s an comhrag nan namh.</p>   
 <p>Ma's ruaig   dhuit, oig, o thaobh do chuil,<br>   Buaidh na h-Oigh ga do chomhnadh dluth,<br>   Sear no siar, siar no sear,<br>   Tuath no deas, deas no tuath.</p>   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>            THE   sain put by Mary on her Son,<br>               Sain   from death, sain from wound,<br>               Sain   from breast to knee,<br>               Sain   from knee to foot,<br>               Sain   of the three sains,<br>               Sain   of the five sains,<br>               Sain   of the seven sains,<br>               From   the crown of thy head<br>               To   the soles of thy feet.<br>               Sain   of the seven paters, one,<br>               Sain   of the seven paters, two,<br>               Sain   of the seven paters, three,<br>               Sain   of the seven paters, four,<br>               Sain   of the seven paters, five,<br>               Sain   of the seven paters, six,<br>               Sain   of the seven paters, seven<br>                    Upon   thee now.<br>               From   the edge of thy brow,<br>               To   thy coloured soles,<br>               To   preserve thee from behind,<br>               To   sustain thee in front.</p>   
 <p>Be the helmet   of salvation about thine head,<br>   Be the corslet of the covenant about thy throat,<br>   Be the breastplate of the priest upon thy breast,<br>   To shield thee in the battle and combat of thine enemies.</p>   
 <p>If pursued, oh   youth, from behind thy back,<br>   The power of the Virgin be close to succour thee,<br>   East or west, west or east,<br>   North or south, south or north.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>
