 <p>CUIRIM an obi   seo ri m’ shuil,<br>   Mar a dh’ orduich Ti nan dul,<br>   A uchd Pheadail, a uchd Phoil,<br>   An treas ob is fearr fo’n ghrein.</p>   
 <p>Sil, a Mhoire,   sil, a Bhride,<br>   Sil, a Phadra, righ nan reachd,<br>   Sil, a Chalum-chille chaoimh,<br>   Sil, a Chiarain naoimh nam feart.</p>   
 <p>Air bhuadh   larach, air chruadh lamha,<br>   An cath tearmaid, an cath farmaid,<br>   Air gach mac da math d’ an teid,<br>   Bidh Mac De leis an treuin armachd.</p>   
 <p>A uchd Athar,<br>   A uchd Mic,<br>   A uchd Spioraid Naoimh.<br>                                    Amen.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>I PLACE this   charm to mine eye,<br>   As the King of life ordained,<br>   From the bosom of Peter and Paul,<br>   The third best amulet under the sun.</p>   
 <p>Pour Mary, pour   Bride,<br>   Pour Patrick, king of laws,<br>   Pour Columba the kindly,<br>   Pour Ciaran, saint of power.</p>   
 <p>For victory in   battle, for hardness of hand,<br>   In battle of defence, in battle of offence,<br>   On every son with whom it shall go well,<br>   The Son of God will be with him in full armour.</p>   
 <p>From the bosom   of Father,<br>   From the bosom of Son,<br>   From the bosom of Holy Spirit.<br>                                         Amen.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>
