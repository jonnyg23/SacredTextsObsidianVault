 <p>BUAINIDH mi am   mothan,<br>   Luibh nan naodh alt,<br>   Buainidh agus boinichidh,<br>        Do Bhride bhorr ’s dh’ a Dalt.</p>   
 <p>Buainidh mi am   mothan,<br>   A dh’ orduich Righ nam feart,<br>   Buainidh agus boinichidh,<br>        Do Mhoire mhor ’s dh’ a Mac.</p>   
 <p>Buainidh mi am   mothan,<br>   A dh’ orduich Righ nan dul,<br>   Bheir buaidh air gach foirneart,<br>        Is ob air obi shul.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>PLUCK will I   the 'mothan,'<br>   Plant of the nine joints,<br>   Pluck will I and vow me,<br>        To noble Bride and her Fosterling.</p>   
 <p>Pluck will I   the 'mothan,'<br>   As ordained of the King of power,<br>   Pluck will I and vow me,<br>        To great Mary and her Son.</p>   
 <p>Pluck will I   the 'mothan,'<br>   As ordained of the King of life,<br>   To overcome all oppression,<br>        And the spell of evil eye.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>
