 <p>EOLAS a rinn   Calum,<br>   Dh’ aona bho caillich,<br>   Air a chraillich, air a ghaillich,<br>   Air a bholg, air a cholg,<br>   Air a mheirbhein;</p>   
 <p>Air a ghalar   ghir,<br>   Air a ghalar chir,<br>   Air a ghalar mhir,<br>   Air a ghalar tolg,<br>   Air an tairbhein;</p>   
 <p>Air a ghalar   chil,<br>   Air a ghalar mhil,<br>   Air a ghalar lioil,<br>   Air a ghalar dhearg,<br>   Air a mhearchann;</p>   
 <p>Sgoiltidh mi an   crailleach,<br>   Sgoiltidh mi an gailleach,<br>   Sgoiltidh mi am bolg,<br>   Sgoiltidh mi an colg,<br>   Agus marbhaidh mi am meirbhein;</p>   
 <p>Sgoiltidh mi an   gir,<br>   Sgoiltidh mi an cir,<br>   Sgoiltidh mi am mir,<br>   Sgoiltidh mi an tolg,<br>   Agus falbhaidh an tairbhein;</p>   
 <p>Sgoiltidh mi an   cil,<br>   Sgoiltidh mi am mil,<br>   Sgoiltidh mi an lioil,<br>   Sgoiltidh mi an dearg,<br>   Is seargaidh am mearchann.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>THE spell made   of Columba,<br>   To the one cow of the woman,<br>   For the 'crailleach,' for the gum disease,<br>   For the bag, for the 'colg,'<br>   For the indigestion (?);</p>   
 <p>For the flux   disease,<br>   For the cud disease,<br>   For the 'mir' disease,<br>   For the 'tolg' disease,<br>   For the surfeit (?);</p>   
 <p>For the 'cil'   disease,<br>   For the 'mil' disease,<br>   For the water disease,<br>   For the red disease,<br>   For the madness (?);</p>   
 <p>I will cleave   the 'crailleach,'<br>   I will cleave the gum disease,<br>   I will cleave the bag,<br>   I will cleave the 'colg,'<br>   And I will kill the indigestion (?);</p>   
 <p>I will cleave   the flux,<br>   I will cleave the cud,<br>   I will cleave the 'mir,'<br>   I will cleave the 'tolg,'<br>   And drive away the surfeit (?);</p>   
 <p>I will cleave   the 'cil,'<br>   I will cleave the 'mil,'<br>   I will cleave the water,<br>   I will cleave the red,<br>   And wither will the madness (?).</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>
