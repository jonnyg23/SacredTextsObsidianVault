 <p>CHURNAICH suil   thu,<br>   Thurmaich bial thu,<br>   Runaich cridh thu,<br>   Smunaich miann thu.</p>   
 <p>Ceathrar a rinn   du-sa trasd,<br>   Fear agus bean,<br>   Mac agus murn;<br>   Triuir cuiream riu ’g an casg,<br>          Athair,<br>          Mac,<br>          Spiorad Numh.</p>   
 <p>Cuiream fianuis   chon Moire,<br>   Mathair-chobhair an t-sluaigh,<br>   Cuiream fianuis chon Bride,<br>   Muime Chriosda nam buadh,<br>   Cuiream fianuis chon Chaluim,<br>   Ostal oirthir is chuain,<br>   ’S cuiream fianuis chon flathas,<br>   Chon gach naoimh is gach aingil tha shuas.</p>   
 <p>Ma's e fear a   rinn do lochd,<br>   Le droch shuil,<br>   Le droch run,<br>   Le droch ruam,</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>AN eye covered   thee,<br>   A mouth spoke thee,<br>   A heart envied thee,<br>   A mind desired thee.</p>   
 <p>Four made thee   thy cross,    [? have done thee harm<br>   Man and wife,<br>   Youth and maid;<br>   Three will I send to thwart them,<br>          Father,<br>          Son,<br>          Spirit Holy.</p>   
 <p>I appeal to   Mary,<br>   Aidful mother of men,<br>   I appeal to Bride,<br>   Foster-mother of Christ omnipotent,<br>   I appeal to Columba,<br>   Apostle of shore and sea,<br>   And I appeal to heaven,<br>   To all saints and angels that be above.</p>   
 <p>If it be a man   that has done thee harm,<br>   With evil eye,<br>   With evil wish,<br>   With evil passion,</p>   </td>  </tr>  <tr>   <td valign="top" align="top">

 <p>Gun tilg thu   dhiot gach olc,<br>   Gach mug,<br>   Gach gnug,<br>   Gach gruam,<br>   ’S gum bi thu gu math gu brath,<br>   Ri linn an snathle seo<br>   Dhol a d’ dhail mu’n cuart,<br>   An onair De agus Ios,<br>   Agus Spioraid ioic bhi-bhuain<font size="-1" color="green">.</font></p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>Mayest thou   cast off each ill,<br>   Every malignity,<br>   Every malice,<br>   Every harassment,<br>   And mayest thou be well for ever,<br>   While this thread<br>   Goes round thee,<br>   In honour of God and of Jesus,<br>   And of the Spirit of balm everlasting.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>
