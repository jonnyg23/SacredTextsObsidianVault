 <p>BUAINIDH mis am   mothan suairce,<br>   An luibh is luachmhoire ’s an tom,<br>   Dulagan nan seachd sagart,<br>   ’S an agallaich a ta n’ an com.</p>   
 <p>  *       *       *       *<br>     *       *       *       *<br>   Gur liom an ciall ’s an codhail,<br>   Fad ’s a bhios am mothan liom.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>I WILL pluck   the gracious 'mothan,'<br>   Plant most precious in the field,<br>   That mine be the holiness of the seven priests,<br>   And the eloquence that is within them.</p>   
 <p>  *       *       *       *<br>     *       *       *       *<br>   That mine be their wisdom and their counsel,<br>   While the 'mothan' is mine.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>
