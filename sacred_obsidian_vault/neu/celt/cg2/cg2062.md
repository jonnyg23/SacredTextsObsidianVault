 <p>BUAINIDH mi   fhin a chloimh chat,<br>   An lion a bhuain Bride mhin tromh glac,<br>   Air bhuaidh, air bhuar, air thoradh,<br>   Air dhair, air chairr, air bhleoghann,<br>   Air laoigh bhoineann bhailgionn,<br>       Mar a thubhradh anns an deailgne.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">
