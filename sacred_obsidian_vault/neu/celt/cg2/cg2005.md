 <p>A RU eugail,   aogail, atail,<br>   Fag uth na ba caisne,<br>   Fag uth na ba cait-cinn,<br>   Fag, fag a phait sin,<br>        Agus tar pait eil ort.</p>   
 <p>A ru rag,   rudaidh,<br>   Our an uth a mhairt,<br>   Fag an t-at ’s an t-utha,<br>   Teich gu grunn na claiche.</p>   
 <p>Cuirim ru ri   clach,<br>   Cuirim clach ri lar,<br>   Cuirim bainne an uth,<br>   Cuirim sugh an ar.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>THOU rose   deathly, deadly, swollen,<br>   Leave the udder of the white-footed cow,<br>   Leave the udder of the spotted cow,<br>   Leave, leave that swelling,<br>        And betake thyself to other swelling.</p>   
 <p>Thou rose   thrawn, obstinate,<br>   Surly in the udder of the cow,<br>   Leave thou the swelling and the udder,<br>   Flee to the bottom of the stone.</p>   
 <p>I place the   rose to the stone,<br>   I place the stone to the earth,<br>   I place milk in the udder,<br>   I place substance in the kidney.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>
