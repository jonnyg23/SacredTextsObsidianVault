 <p>DIA na gile,   Dia na greine,<br>   Dia na cruinne, Dia nan reula,<br>   Dia nan dile, tir, is neamha,<br>   Dh’ orduich dhuinne Righ na feile.</p>   
 <p>’S i   Moire mhin chaidh air a glun,<br>   ’S e Ti nan dul a chaidh na h-uchd,<br>   Chaidh durch is diuir a chur air chul,<br>   ’S chaidh reul an iuil an aird gu much.</p>   
 <p>Dh’   fhoillsich fearann, dh’ fhoillsich fonn,<br>   Dh’ fhoillsich doltrom agus struth,<br>   Leagadh bron is thogadh fonn,<br>   Chaidh ceol air bonn le clar is cruth.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>GOD of the   moon, God of the sun,<br>   God of the globe, God of the stars,<br>   God of the waters, the land, and the skies,<br>   Who ordained to us the King of promise.</p>   
 <p>It was Mary   fair who went upon her knee,<br>   It was the King of life who went upon her lap,<br>   Darkness and tears were set behind,<br>   And the star of guidance went up early.</p>   
 <p>Illumed the   land, illumed the world,<br>   Illumed doldrum and current,<br>   Grief was laid and joy was raised,<br>   Music was set up with harp and pedal-harp.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>
