 <p>PAIDIR Moire a   h-aon,<br>   Paidir Moire a dha,<br>   Paidir Moire a tri,<br>   Paidir Moire a ceithir,<br>   Paidir Moire a coig,<br>   Paidir Moire a sia,<br>   Paidir Moire a seachd,<br>   Seachd paidriche Moire gu brath<br>   Eadar cradh agus ceart,<br>   Eadar bonn agus braigh,<br>   Eadar slan agus feart.</p>   
 <p>Chaidh Criosd   air as,<br>   Sgiuch a cas,<br>   Thainig e bhan<br>   Shlanuich e cas;<br>   Mar a shlanuich e sin<br>   Gun slanuich e seo,<br>   Agus na ’s mo na seo<br>   Ma ’s e thoil a dheanamh.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>ROSARY of Mary,   one,<br>   Rosary of Mary, two,<br>   Rosary of Mary, three,<br>   Rosary of Mary, four,<br>   Rosary of Mary, five,<br>   Rosary of Mary, six,<br>   Rosary of Mary, seven,<br>   Seven Rosaries of Mary ever<br>   Between pain and ease,<br>   Between sole and summit,<br>   Between health and grave</p>   
 <p>Christ went on   an ass,<br>   She sprained her foot,<br>   He came down<br>   And healed her foot;<br>   As He healed that<br>   May He heal this,<br>   And greater than this,<br>   If it be His will to do.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>
