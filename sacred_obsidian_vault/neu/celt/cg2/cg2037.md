 <p>CUIREAM   fionn-faoilidh umam,<br>   A thraoghadh feirge falamh,<br>   A chumail rium mo chliu,<br>   Fad ’s a bhios mi biu air talamh.</p>   
 <p>O Mhicheil! glac   mo lamh,<br>   Liobh rium cairdeas De,<br>   Ma tha mi-run no di-run air mo namh,<br>   Criosd a bhi eadar mis is e,<br>        O, Criosd eadar mis is e!</p>   
 <p>Ma tha mi-run   no di-run air mo sgath,<br>   Criosd a bhi eadar mis is e,<br>        O, Criosd eadar mis is e!</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>I PLACE the   'fionn-faoilidh' on me,<br>   To drain wrath empty,<br>   To preserve to me my fame,<br>   While I shall live on earth.</p>   
 <p>O Michael!   grasp my hand,<br>   Vouchsafe to me the love of God,<br>   If there be ill-will or ill-wish in mine enemy,<br>   Christ be between me and him,<br>        Oh, Christ between me and him!</p>   
 <p>If there be   ill-will or ill-wish concerning me,<br>   Christ be between me and it,<br>        Oh, Christ between me and it!</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>
