 <p>Thig, a   chuinneag, thig;<br>   Thig, a chuinneag, thig;</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">

 <p>The mild will   come,<br>   The kind will come,<br>   The loving will come,<br>   The devious will come,<br>   The brim-full of the globe will come,<br>   To set the churn a-running;<br>   The kindly Columba will come in his array;<br>   And the golden-haired Bride of the kine.</p>   
 <p>A splash is   here,<br>   A plash is here,<br>   A plash is here,<br>   A splash is here,<br>   A crash is here,<br>   A squash is here,<br>   A squash is here,<br>   A crash is here,<br>   A big soft snail is here,<br>   The sap of each of the cows is here,<br>   A thing better than honey and spruce,<br>   A bogle yellow and fresh is here.</p>   
 <p>A thing better   than right is here,<br>   The fist of the big priest is here,<br>   A thing better than the carcase is here,<br>   The head of the dead man is here,<br>   A thing better than wine is here,<br>   The full of the cog of Caristine<br>   Of live things soft and fair are here,<br>         Of live things soft and fair are here.</p>
