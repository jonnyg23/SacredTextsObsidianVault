 <p>MILE failte   dhut fo d’ bhreid,<br>   Ri do re gu robh thu slan,<br>   Luth is laithean dhut le sith,<br>        Do pharas le do ni bhi fas.</p>   
 <p>An tus do   chomh-ruith is tu og,<br>   An tus do lo iarr Ti nan dul,<br>   Cha churam dha nach toir e ceart<br>        Gach foil is feart a bhios ’nad run.</p>   
 <p>An coron-ceile   a chuir thu suas,<br>   Is tric a fhuair e buaidh do mhnai;<br>   Bi-sa subhailc ach bi suairc,<br>        Bi-sa stuam an lid ’s an laimh.</p>   
 <p>Bi-sa fialaidh   ach bi glic,<br>   Bi-sa misneachail ach stold,<br>   Bi-sa bruithneach ach bi balbh,<br>        Bi-sa caimeineach ach coir.</p>   
 <p>Na dean   criontaireachd an toirt,<br>   Na dean brosg ach na bi fuar,<br>   Na labhair fos air neach ge h-olc,<br>        Ma labhrar ort na toir-sa fuath.</p>   
 <p>Bi-sa   gleidhteach air h-ainm,<br>   Bi-sa sgeimineach ach suairc,<br>   Lamh Dhe biodh air h-eilm,<br>        An deilbh, an gniamh ’s an smuain.</p>   
 <p>Na bi gearanach   fo d’ chrois,<br>   Siubhail socair fo chopan lan,<br>   A chaoidh dh’an olc na toir-sa speis,<br>        ’S le do bhreid dhut ceud mile failt!</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>A THOUSAND   hails to thee beneath thy kertch,<br>   During thy course mayest thou be whole,<br>   Strength and days be thine in peace,<br>        Thy paradise with thy means increase.</p>   
 <p>In beginning   thy dual race, and thou young,<br>   In beginning thy course, seek thou the God of life,<br>   Fear not but He will rightly rule<br>        Thine every secret need and prayer.</p>   
 <p>This spousal   crown thou now hast donned,<br>   Full oft has gotten grace to woman,<br>   Be thou virtuous, but be gracious,<br>        Be thou pure in word and hand.</p>   
 <p>Be thou   hospitable, yet be wise,<br>   Be thou courageous, but be calm,<br>   Be thou frank, but be reserved,<br>        Be thou exact, yet generous.</p>   
 <p>Be not miserly   in giving,<br>   Do not flatter, yet be not cold,<br>   Speak not ill of man, though ill he be,<br>        If spoken of, show not resentment.</p>   
 <p>Be thou careful   of thy name,<br>   Be thou dignified yet kind,<br>   The hand of God be on thine helm,<br>        In inception, in act, and in thought.</p>   
 <p>Be not   querulous beneath thy cross,<br>   Walk thou warily when thy cup is full,<br>   Never to evil give thou countenance,<br>        And with thy kertch, to thee a hundred thousand   hails!</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>
