 <p>CHAIDH Criosd a   mach<br>   Maduinn moch,<br>   Fhuair e cas nan each<br>   ’Nan spruilleach bog;<br>   Chuir e smior ri smior,<br>   Chuir e smuais ri smuais,<br>   Chuir e cnaimh ri cnaimh,<br>   Chuir e streabhon ri streabhon,<br>   Chuir e feith ri feith,<br>   Chuir e fuil ri fuil,<br>   Chuir e creais ri creais,<br>   Chuir e feoil ri feoil,<br>   Chuir e saill ri saill,<br>   Chuir e craicionn ri craicionn,<br>   Chuir e flonn ri flonn,<br>   Chuir e blath ri blath,<br>   Chuir e fuar ri fuar;<br>   Mar a leighis Righ nam buadh sin<br>   Is dual gun leighis e seo,<br>   Ma ’s e thoil fein a dheanamh.<br>         A uchd Ti nan dul,<br>         Agus Tiur na Trianaid.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>CHRIST went out<br>   In the morning early,<br>   He found the legs of the horses<br>   In fragments soft;<br>   He put marrow to marrow,<br>   He put pith to pith,<br>   He put bone to bone,<br>   He put membrane to membrane,<br>   He put tendon to tendon,<br>   He put blood to blood,<br>   He put tallow to tallow,<br>   He put flesh to flesh,<br>   He put fat to fat,<br>   He put skin to skin,<br>   He put hair to hair,<br>   He put warm to warm,<br>   He put cool to cool,<br>   As the King of power healed that<br>   It is in His nature to heal this,<br>   If it be His own will to do it.<br>         Through the bosom of the Being of life,<br>         And of the Three of the Trinity.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>
