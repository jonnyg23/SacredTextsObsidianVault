 <p>D UAIR bha   Criosda fo choill,<br>        Agus naimhdean air a dheigh,<br>   Is e thuirt faochaire na foill,<br>        Ris an daol ’s an dealan-de--</p>   
 <p>'Am facas seach   an diugh no ’n raoir,<br>        Mac mo ghaol-sa, Mac De?'<br>   'Chunna, chunna,' ors an daol,<br>        'Mac na saorsa seach an de.'</p>   
 <p>'Breug! breug!   breug!'<br>        Orsa cearran cre nan each,<br>   'A bhliadhna mhor chon an de,<br>        Chaidh Mac De seach.'</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>WHEN Christ was   under the wood,<br>        And enemies were pursuing Him,<br>   The crooked one of deception,<br>        Said to the black beetle and the butterfly</p>   
 <p>'Saw ye pass   to-day or yesterday,<br>        The Son of my love, the Son of God?'<br>   'We saw! we saw!' said the black beetle,<br>        'The Son of redemption pass yesterday.'</p>   
 <p>'False! false!   false!'<br>        Said the little clay beetle of horses,<br>   'A full year yesterday,<br>        The Son of God went by.'</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>
