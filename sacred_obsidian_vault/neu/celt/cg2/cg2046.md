 <p>BUAINIDH mi an   earr reidh,<br>   Gum bu cheinide mo chruth,<br>   Gum bu bhlathaide mo bheuil,<br>   Gum bu gheinide mo ghuth.<br>   Biodh mo ghuth mar ghath na grein,<br>   Biodh mo bheuil mar ein nan subh.</p>   
 <p>Gum bu h-eilean   mi air muir,<br>   Gum bu tulach mi air tir,<br>   Gum bu reuil mi ri ra dorcha,<br>   Gum bu lorg mi dhuine cli,<br>   Leonaidh mi a h-uile duine,<br>   Cha leoin duine mi.</p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p> </p>   </td>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>I WILL pluck   the yarrow fair,<br>   That more benign shall be my face,<br>   That more warm shall be my lips,<br>   That more chaste shall be my speech,<br>   Be my speech the beams of the sun,<br>   Be my lips the sap of the strawberry.</p>   
 <p>May I be an   isle in the sea,<br>   May I be a hill on the shore,<br>   May I be a star in waning of the moon,<br>   May I be a staff to the weak,<br>   Wound can I every man,<br>   Wound can no man me.</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>
