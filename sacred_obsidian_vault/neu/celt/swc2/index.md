 <p>
 This is the second of three volumes of William Bottrells' collection of
 Cornish folklore, legend and local tales.
 This volume includes several episodic stories, particularly the generational
 saga of the I'ans.
 While many of the folk-tales have thematic correlations in other
 story-telling traditions, all of these stories are localized by Bottrell
 in the Cornish landscape.
 This land is a tricky one, abounding with giants, ancient castles,
 and strange standing stones.
 The Devil is a real presence in these stories, always ready to take a comic
 turn if necessary.--J.B. Hare, Jan. 24 2010.
 </p>
