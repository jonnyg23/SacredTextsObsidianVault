 <b></b><p align="CENTER">23b. Here Followeth the Accoutrement of the Charioteers</p>
 <p>Then came the charioteers of the Ulstermen to them. Thrice fifty was their number. They offered three battles to the hosts. Thrice their number fell at their hands, and the charioteers themselves fell on the field whereon they stood. Hence this here is called the 'Accoutrement of the Charioteers [with stones.]' </p>
