 <p align="center">WHAT WE HAVE COME TO</p>  
 <p>When the advertiser saw the cathedral spires over the downs in the distance, he looked at them and wept.</p>  
 <p>"If only," he said, "this were an advertisement of Beefo, so nice, so nutritious, try it in your soup, ladies like it."</p>
