 <b><font size="5"><p align="CENTER"><a name="_Toc29619146">Dedication</a></p>
 </font></b><font size="4"><p align="CENTER"> </p>
 <p align="CENTER">To Iceland</p>
 <p align="CENTER"> </p>
 <p><br>
 Hail, Isle! with mist and snowstorms girt around,<br>
 Where fire and earthquake rend the shatterd ground--<br>
 Here once oer furthest oceans icy path<br>
 The Northmen fled a tyrant monarchs wrath:<br>
 Here, cheerd by song and story, dwelt they free,<br>
 And held unscathd their laws and liberty.</p>
