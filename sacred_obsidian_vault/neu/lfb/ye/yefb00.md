 <p align="center">THE YELLOW FAIRY BOOK</p>  
 <p align="center">Edited by ANDREW LANG</p>  
 <p align="center">London, New York: Longmans, Green, and Co.</p>  
 <p align="center">[1894]</p>  
 <p align="center">Dedication</p>  
 <p align="center">TO</p>  
 <p align="center">JOAN, TODDLES, AND TINY</p>  
 <p> </p>  
 <p>Books Yellow, Red, and Green and Blue,<br> All true, or just as good as true,<br> And here's the Yellow Book for YOU!</p>  
 <p>Hard is the path from A to Z,<br> And puzzling to a curly head,<br> Yet leads to Books—Green, Blue, and Red.</p>  
 <p>For every child should understand<br> That letters from the first were planned<br> To guide us into Fairy Land</p>  
 <p>So labour at your Alphabet,<br> For by that learning shall you get<br> To lands where Fairies may be met.</p>  
 <p>And going where this pathway goes,<br> You too, at last, may find, who knows?<br> The Garden of the Singing Rose.</p>
