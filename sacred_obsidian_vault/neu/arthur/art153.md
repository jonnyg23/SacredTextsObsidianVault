 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">An Arthurian Miscellany at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <center> <h2 align="CENTER">GOD'S GRAAL</h2>  <h3 align="CENTER">by</h3>  <h2 align="CENTER">DANTE GABRIEL ROSSETTI</h2>  </center>  <br>       The ark of the Lord of Hosts<br>  Whose name is called by the name of Him<br>  Who dwelleth between the Cherubim.<br>  <br>  O Thou that in no house dost dwell,<br>  But walk'st in tent and tabernacle.<br>  <br>  For God of all strokes will have one<br>  In every battle that is done.<br>  <br>  Lancelot lay beside the well:<br>       (God's Graal is good)<br>  Oh my soul is sad to tell<br>  The weary quest and the bitter quell;<br>  For he was the lord of lordlihood<br>  And sleep on his eyelids fell.<br>  <br>  Lancelot lay before the shrine;<br>       (The apple tree's in the wood)<br>  There was set Christ's very sign,<br>  The bread unknown and the unknown wine<br>  That the soul's life for a livelihood<br>       Craves from his wheat and vine.<br>
