 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN">An Arthurian Miscellany at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <center> <h2 align="CENTER">NEAR AVALON</h2>  <h3 align="CENTER">by</h3>  <h2 align="CENTER">WILLIAM MORRIS</h2>  </center>  <br>   A ship with shields before the sun,<br>  Six maidens round the mast,<br>  A red-gold crown on every one,<br>  A green gown on the last.<br>  <br>  The fluttering green banners there<br>  Are wrought with ladies' heads most fair,<br>  And a portraiture of Guenevere<br>  The middle of each sail doth bear.<br>  <br>  A ship with sails before the wind,<br>  And round the helm six knights,<br>  Their heaumes are on, whereby, half blind,<br>  They pass by many sights.<br>  <br>  The tatter'd scarlet banners there<br>  Right soon will leave the spear-heads bare.<br>  Those six knights sorrowfully bear<br>  In all their heaumes some yellow hair.<br>
