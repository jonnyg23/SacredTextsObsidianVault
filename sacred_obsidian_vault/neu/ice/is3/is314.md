 <p align="center">V.</p>
 
 <p align="center"> </p>
 
 <p align="center">AD MISSAM.</p>
 
 <p align="center"> </p>
 
 <p align="center">IN FESTO TRANSLATIONIS MAGNI
 MARTYRIS.</p>
 
 <p align="center"> </p>
 
 <p align="center">Officium.</p>
 
 <p align="center">Gloria et honore coronasti eum et
 constituisti eum super opera manuum tuarum. [Ps. 8. v. 5, 6.]</p>
 
 <p align="center"> </p>
 
 <p align="center">Versus.</p>
 
 <p align="center">Domine Deus noster, quam admirabile
 est nomen tuum in omni terra. [Ps. 8. v. 9.]</p>
 
 <p align="center"> </p>
 
 <p align="center">Collecta.</p>
 
 <p align="center">Adesto Domine supplicationibus
 nostris, et intercedente beato Magno martyre tuo ab hostium nos defende
 propiciatus incursu.  Per Dominum.</p>
 
 <p align="center"> </p>
 
 <p align="center">Lectio.</p>
 
 <p align="center">Justus si morte ........... in
 electos illius.  [Libri. Sap. 4. v. 7-15.]</p>
 
 <p align="center"> </p>
 
 <p align="center">Gradale.</p>
 
 <p align="center">Posuisti Domine super caput ejus
 coronam de lapide precioso.  [Ps. 20. v. 3.]</p>
 
 <p align="center"> </p>
 
 <p align="center">Versus.</p>
 
 <p align="center">Desiderium animae ejus tribuisti ei,
 et voluntate labiorum ejus non fraudasti eum.  [Ps. 20. v. 2.]</p>
 
 <p align="center"> </p>
 
 <p align="center">Versus.</p>
 
 <p align="center">Alleluia.  Beatus vir qui timet
 Dominum, in mandatis ejus cupit (1) nimis.  [Ps. 111. v. 1.]</p>
 
 <p align="center"> </p>
 
 <p align="center">Evangelium.</p>
 
 <p align="center">Secundum Matheum [x. 34-42.]  In
 illo tempore dixit Jesus, Nolite .......... non perdet mercedem suam.</p>
 
 <p align="center"> </p>
 
 <p align="center">Offertorium.</p>
 
 <p align="center">Posuisti Domine in capite ejus
 coronam de lapide precioso.</p>
 
 <p align="center"> </p>
 
 <p align="center">Secreta.</p>
 
 <p align="center">Presentia munera quaesumus Domine
 serena pietate intuere, ut Sancti Spiritus perfundantur benedictione et in
 nostris cordibus eam dilectionem validam infundant, per quam sanctus martyr
 Magnus omnia corporis tormenta devicit.  Per Dominum eundem.</p>
 
 <p align="center"> </p>
 
 <p align="center">Communio.</p>
 
 <p align="center">Posuisti Domine in capite ejus
 coronam de lapide precioso.</p>
 
 <p align="center"> </p>
 
 <p align="center">Complenda. (2)</p>
 
 <p align="center">Sumpsimus Domine sancti martiris tui
 Magni solempnitate celestia sacramenta cujus suffragiis quaesumus largiaris, ut
 quod temporaliter gerimus eternis gaudiis consequamur.  Per Dominum.</p>
 
 <p align="center"> </p>
 
 <p align="center"> </p>
 
 <p>********************</p>
 
 <p>1.                 
 "Cupit." The Vulgate reads volet for cupit.</p>
 
 <p>2.                 
 "Complenda."  In the Drontheim Missal this term is synonymous with
 the Postcommunio of other Missals.</p>
