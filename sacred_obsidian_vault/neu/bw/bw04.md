 <p align="CENTER">FROM<br>
 LAPLAND<br>
 OF<br>
 AURORA COLORS<br>
 WHITE SILENCE<br>
 MOSSY TUNDRAS<br>
 AND HILLS WHERE<br>
 REINDEER<br>
 FEED</p><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <p>Lapland's Children, they are singing,<br>
 Chant the Children of the Northland!<br>
 While the winter nights grow longer,<br>
 And the days have no returnings;<br>
 And the Long Nights shimmer whitely<br>
 O'er the icy wastes and tundras;<br>
 Where the Reindeer nibble mosses,<br>
 Silver lichens 'neath the snowfall.</p>
 <p>Lapland's Children still are singing,<br>
 Chant the Children of the Northland!<br>
 "See! across the sky are sweeping<br>
 Amber lights that shift and flutter,<br>
 Rosy arc and violet flashings,<br>
 Flames that dart and veils that tremble,<br>
 O'er the sky they move and glimmer!"</p>
 <p>Lapland's Children still are singing,<br>
 Chant the Children of the Northland! <br>
 "See! O see! The Merry Dancers,<br>
 Towards the zenith streaming, leaping!<br>
 In the heart of the Aurora, <br>
 Dance the Merry Baltic Wizards, <br>
 From the Lands of Baltic Wonders, <br>
 From the Lands of Baltic Magic!"</p>
