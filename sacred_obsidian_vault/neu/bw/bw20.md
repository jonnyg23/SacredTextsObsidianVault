 <p align="CENTER">FROM<br>
 ESTONIA<br>
 THE WISE AND THE FREE<br>
 LAND OF ENCHANTMENT<br>
 AND OF<br>
 WONDER LEGENDS</p><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <p>Where the mystic cliffs are rising,<br>
 Where the billows beat and clammer,<br>
 Where they leap, and boil, and bubble,<br>
 In a Cavern, Crystal-Lighted,<br>
 Sings Estonia's Happy Wizard--<br>
 Sings a song of weird enchantments,<br>
 Sings of gnomes and magic serpents,<br>
 Sings of treasure and of mermaids,<br>
 Sings of fairies and of witches,<br>
 Sings of moons, and suns, and starlets,<br>
 Sings the mighty deeds of wonder<br>
 Wrought by strong and handsome Heroes,<br>
 In Estonia, Land of Magic,<br>
 In Estonia, Land of Wizards.</p>
