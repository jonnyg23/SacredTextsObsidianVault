 <p>
 This is one of the numerous Yogi Publication Society (YPS)
 books which have been attributed to William Walker Atkinson under pseudonym.
 It bears strong similarities
 to <a href="../../eso/kyb/index.htm">The Kybalion</a>,
 which is also known to have been authored by Atkinson.
 The material was later re-worked as one of the volumes in his series
 <cite>The Arcane Teachings</cite>.
 </p>
 <p>
 This etext was scanned from an original 1918 printing of this work.
 The pagination and emphasis differ slightly from modern YPS
 printings of this book.
 </p>
