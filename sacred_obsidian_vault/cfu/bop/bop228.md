 <p><a name="page_302"><font size="1" color="green">p. 302</font></a></p>  <h4 align="center">V</h4>  
 <p></p><div style="font-size: 90%">The <i>Ch‘ing Ying;</i> metaphorical and allusive. Against listening to slanderers.</div>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 20%; margin-right: 20%; font-size: 110%;"><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1">1</a></font></span>Like the blue flies buzzing round,<br>   And on the fences lighting,<br> Are the sons of slander found,<br>   Who never cease their biting.<br> O thou happy, courteous king,<br> To the winds their slanders fling.  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2">2</a></font></span>Buzzing round the blue flies hear,<br>   About the jujubes flocking!<br> So the slanderers appear,<br>   Whose calumnies are shocking.<br> By no law or order bound,<br> All the kingdom they confound.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_3">3</a></font></span>How they buzz, those odious flies,<br>   Upon the hazels clust’ring!<br> And as odious are the lies<br>   Of those slanderers blust’ring.<br> Hatred stirred between us two<br> Shows the evil they can do.</p></div>
