 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Book of Poetry</i>, tr. by James Legge, [1876], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h4 align="center">VIII</h4>  
 <p></p><div style="font-size: 90%">The <i>Po Hsi;</i> narrative and metaphorical. A wife mourns over the protracted absence of her husband on the king's service.</div>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 20%; margin-right: 20%; font-size: 110%;"><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1">1</a></font></span>How martial looks my noble man,<br>   The hero of the land!<br> See him in chariot lead the van,<br>   His halberd in his hand!  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2">2</a></font></span>Since eastward on his course he sped,<br>   My hair neglected flies.<br> I might anoint and wash my head,<br>   But not to meet his eyes.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_3">3</a></font></span>For rain, for rain, the people cry,<br>   But brightly shines the sun;<br> So for my absent lord long I,<br>   Head pained, and heart undone. <a name="page_72"><font size="1" color="green">p. 72</font></a></p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_4">4</a></font></span>Where shall I Lethe's lily find,<br>   Behind my house to set?<br> I think of him with aching mind,<br>   For how can I forget?</p></div>
