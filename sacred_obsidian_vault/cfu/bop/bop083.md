 <p><a name="page_92"><font size="1" color="green">p. 92</font></a></p>  <h4 align="center">VI</h4>  
 <p></p><div style="font-size: 90%">The <i>Kao Ch‘iu;</i> narrative. Celebrating some officer of Chêng for his elegant appearance and integrity.</div>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 20%; margin-right: 20%; font-size: 110%;"><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1">1</a></font></span>How glossy is the lambkin's fur,<br>   Smooth to the touch, and fair to view!<br> In it arrayed, that officer<br>   Rests in his lot, to virtue true.  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2">2</a></font></span>With leopard cuffs, the lambkin's fur<br>   Seems made for wearer strong in fight.<br> It well becomes that officer,<br>   Whom none will see swerve from the right.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_3">3</a></font></span>Splendid his robe of lambkin's fur,<br>   With its three decorations grand!<br> It well beseems that officer,<br>   The pride and glory of our land.</p></div>
