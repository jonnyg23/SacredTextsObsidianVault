 <p></p><div style="font-size: 90%">The <i>Ts‘ai Ko;</i> narrative. A lady longs for the society of the object of her affection.</div>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 20%; margin-right: 20%; font-size: 110%;"><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1">1</a></font></span>He's there, the dolichos among!<br> Only one day from sight away,—<br> To me it seems as three months long!  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2">2</a></font></span>He's there, among the southernwood!<br> Only one day from sight away,—<br> It seems three seasons' solitude! <a name="page_83"><font size="1" color="green">p. 83</font></a></p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_3">3</a></font></span>Among the mugwort he appears!<br> Only one day from sight away,—<br> To me it seems as three fully years!</p></div>
