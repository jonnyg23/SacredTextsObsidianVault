 <p><a name="page_8"><font size="1" color="green">p. 8</font></a></p>  <h4 align="center">VIII</h4>  
 <p></p><div style="font-size: 90%">The <i>Fou I;</i> narrative. The song of the plantain gatherers.</div>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 20%; margin-right: 20%; font-size: 110%;"><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1">1</a></font></span>We gather and gather the plantains;<br>   Come gather them anyhow.<br> Yes, gather and gather the plantains,<br>   And here we have got them now.  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2">2</a></font></span>We gather and gather the plantains;<br>   Now off the ears we must tear.<br> Yes, gather and gather the plantains,<br>   And now the seeds are laid bare.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_3">3</a></font></span>We gather and gather the plantains,<br>   The seeds in our skirts are placed.<br> Yes, gather and gather the plantains,<br>   Ho! safe in the girdled waist.</p></div>
