 <p></p><div style="font-size: 90%">The <i>Chu Lin;</i> narrative. The intrigue of Duke Ling with the lady of Chu Lin.</div>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 20%; margin-right: 20%; font-size: 110%;"><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1">1</a></font></span>What to Chu Lin takes his car?<br> Hsia Nan is the leading star.<br> Not for Chu Lin does he go;<br> Hsia Nan ‘tis who draws him so.  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2">2</a></font></span>Oft his purpose to go there<br> At the court he will declare:—<br> "Yoke for me my goodly team;<br> I to-night in Chu will dream.<br> With those colts my way I'll make,<br> Morning meal at Chu to take."</p></div>
