 <p><a name="page_89"><font size="1" color="green">p. 89</font></a></p>  <h4 align="center">IV</h4>  
 <p></p><div style="font-size: 90%">The <i>Ta Shu Yü T‘ien;</i> narrative. Celebrating the charioteering, daring, and archery of <i>Shu Tuan</i>.</div>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 20%; margin-right: 20%; font-size: 110%;"><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1">1</a></font></span>Our Shu a-hunting forth has gone;<br> In four-horsed chariot grand he shone.<br> As ribbon in his grasp each rein;<br> With measured steps, like dancers twain,<br>   The outside horses flew.<br> They now have reached the marshy ground;<br> At once the flames break out around.<br> With naked arm and chest Shu stands;<br> A tiger fierce his nervous hands<br>   Grapple and soon subdue.<br> He then presents it to the duke,<br> While all with wonder on him look.<br> But, Shu, try not such sport again.<br> What grief were ours if you were slain!<br>   Your daring we should rue.  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2">2</a></font></span>Our Shu a-hunting drove away,<br> His four steeds all of color bay.<br> The outsides followed close behind <a name="page_90"><font size="1" color="green">p. 90</font></a><br> The insides, finest of their kind,<br>   Like wild geese on the wing.<br> They now have gained the marshy ground;<br> At once the flames blaze all around.<br> Few archers can with Shu compare;<br> A charioteer of cunning rare,<br>   The steeds before him spring.<br> Now they dash on in course direct;<br> Now they're brought up and quickly checkt.<br> Forth flies the arrow, fleet and stark,<br> Nor fails to hit its proper mark,<br>   His left hand following.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_3">3</a></font></span>Our Shu a-hunting drove away,<br> His four steeds all of color gray.<br> With heads in line the insides sped;<br> The outsides followed like the head<br>   Succeeded by the arms.<br> They now have reached the marshy ground;<br> One blaze of flame wraps all around;<br>   Soon cease the hunt's alarms.<br> Shu's steeds before him slowly move;<br> His skill the arrows cease to prove.<br> Straightway the quiver's lid is closed,<br> And in its case the bow reposed.<br>   How his fine bearing charms!</p></div>
