 <p><a name="page_143"><font size="1" color="green">p. 143</font></a></p>  <h4 align="center">IV</h4>  
 <p></p><div style="font-size: 90%">The <i>Chien Chia;</i> narrative. Some one tells how he sought another, whom it seemed easy to find, and yet could not find him.</div>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 20%; margin-right: 20%; font-size: 110%;"><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1">1</a></font></span>Reed and rush are dark and green;<br> As hoarfrost the white dew is seen.<br> Him, the man I have in mind,<br> By this water I should find.<br> Searching, up the stream I haste,<br> On a long and toilsome quest.<br> Downwards then I turn, and see!<br> In the midstream standeth he.<br> He is there but far removed;<br> Vain has all my searching proved.  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2">2</a></font></span>Reed and rush luxuriant rise;<br> Still undried the white dew lies.<br> Him, the man I have in mind,<br> On the stream's edge I should find.<br> Upwards first my course I keep,<br> Though the way is rough and steep. <a name="page_144"><font size="1" color="green">p. 144</font></a><br> Downwards then, and what to see?<br> In the midstream standeth he,<br> On the islet, far removed;—<br> Vain has all my searching proved.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_3">3</a></font></span>Reed and rush grow thick and tall;<br> Ceases not the dew to fall.<br> Him, the man I have in mind,<br> On the stream's bank I should find.<br> Upwards first I go along,<br> But the hard path leads me wrong.<br> Downwards then my steps I turn,<br> And in midstream him discern,<br> On the island, far removed;—<br> Vain has all my searching proved.</p></div>
