 <p></p><div style="font-size: 90%">The <i>Huan;</i> narrative. Celebrating the merit and success of King Wu.</div>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 20%; margin-right: 20%; font-size: 110%;">Throughout our myriad regions there is peace;<br>   Year after year the plenteous harvest brings.<br> Great Heaven in showing favors does not cease,<br>   And will with blessing crown Chou's line of kings.<br> The martial sovereign, Wu, the confidence<br>   He won from all his officers retained.<br> Their service would he through the realm dispense,<br>   And for his house security thus gained.<br> Oh! glorious was he in the sight of Heaven,<br> By which to him the throne of Shang was given.</div>
