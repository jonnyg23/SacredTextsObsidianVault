 <p></p><div style="font-size: 90%">The <i>Pan;</i> narrative. The greatness of Chou, and its firm possession of the kingdom, as seen in the progresses of its reigning sovereign.</div>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 20%; margin-right: 20%; font-size: 110%;">Oh! great is now the house of Chou.<br>   The lofty hills we climb,—<br> Both those whose ridges far extend,<br>   And those with peaks sublime. <a name="page_462"><font size="1" color="green">p. 462</font></a>  
 <p>Along the Ho we watch the flow<br>   Of its embankèd stream.<br> Where’er o’erhead the sky is spread,<br>   Me truly king men deem.</p>  
 <p>The princes all, both great and small,<br>   My summons promptly own.<br> So by this progress do I know<br>   Chou stablished on the throne.</p></div>
