 <p><a name="page_10"><font size="1" color="green">p. 10</font></a></p>  <h4 align="center">X</h4>  
 <p></p><div style="font-size: 90%">The Ju Fên; mainly narrative. The affection of the wives on the Ju, and their solicitude about their husbands’ honor.</div>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 20%; margin-right: 20%; font-size: 110%;"><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1">1</a></font></span>Along the raised banks of the Ju,<br>   To hew slim stein and branch I wrought,<br> My lord away, my husband true,<br>   Like hunger pang my troubled thought!  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2">2</a></font></span>Along the raised banks of the Ju,<br>   Branch and fresh shoot confessed my art.<br> I've seen my lord, my husband true,<br>   And still he folds me in his heart.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_3">3</a></font></span>As the toiled bream makes red its tail,<br>   Toil you, sir, for the royal house,<br> Amidst its blazing fires, nor quail:—<br>   Your parents see you pay your vows.</p></div>
