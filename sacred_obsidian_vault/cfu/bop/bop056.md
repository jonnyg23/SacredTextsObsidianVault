 <p><a name="page_57"><font size="1" color="green">p. 57</font></a></p>  <h4 align="center">IX</h4>  
 <p></p><div style="font-size: 90%">The <i>Kan Mao;</i> narrative. The zeal of the officers of Wei to welcome men of worth.</div>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 20%; margin-right: 20%; font-size: 110%;"><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1">1</a></font></span>Where Chün's suburbs lie remote,<br> From the staffs the oxtails float.<br> High the staffs, and each one bright<br> With its silken bandlets white!<br> Four cars drawn by steeds of fire<br> Welcome guest whom. all desire.<br> Admirable, what will he<br> Give to meet such courtesy?  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2">2</a></font></span>In Chün's suburbs near the town,<br> Fly the falcon banners, blown<br> From the staffs that rise around,<br> All with bands of white silk bound.<br> Five cars drawn by horses strong<br> Wait the guest who comes along.<br> Courteous, worthy,—what shall he<br> Pay for all this courtesy?</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_3">3</a></font></span>Now the walls of Chün we see;<br> Feather’d streamers flutter free<br> From the flagstaffs strong and stout,<br> "Girt by silken bands about. <a name="page_58"><font size="1" color="green">p. 58</font></a><br> Six cars drawn by steeds of fame<br> Well attest the guest's high name.<br> Sage profound, what can he say<br> That such welcome will repay?</p></div>
