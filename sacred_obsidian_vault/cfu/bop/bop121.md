 <p><a name="page_131"><font size="1" color="green">p. 131</font></a></p>  <h4 align="center">V</h4>  
 <p></p><div style="font-size: 90%">The <i>Ch‘ou Mou;</i> allusive. Husband and wife express their delight at their unexpected union.</div>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 20%; margin-right: 20%; font-size: 110%;"><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1">1</a></font></span>  Round and round the fagots I've bound,<br>     And the Heart in heaven shines clear.<br>   Oh! that I such an evening have found!<br>     That this good man should be here!<br>       O me! O me!<br> With a husband like this I have nothing to fear.  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2">2</a></font></span>  Round and round the grass we have bound;<br>     And the corner shines the Heart.<br>   Strange that we such an evening have found!<br>     Thus to meet and never to part!<br>       O joy! O joy!<br> That this meeting should come without scheming or art!</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_3">3</a></font></span>  Round and round the thorns I have bound;<br>     From the door the Heart I see.<br>   Oh! that I such an evening have found!<br>     That this Beauty is come to me!<br>       O me! O me!<br> That this lady so lovely mine only should be!</p></div>
