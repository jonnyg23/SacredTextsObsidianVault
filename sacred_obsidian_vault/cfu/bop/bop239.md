 <p><a name="page_325"><font size="1" color="green">p. 325</font></a></p>  <h4 align="center">VI</h4>  
 <p></p><div style="font-size: 90%">The <i>Mien Man;</i> allusive. Some inferior complains of his toil in an expedition, and of the neglect with which he was treated by his superiors.</div>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 20%; margin-right: 20%; font-size: 110%;"><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1">1</a></font></span>  Twitters fast the oriole,<br>     Where yonder bends the mound.<br>   The happy little creature<br>     Its resting place has found.<br> So have not I. The journey's length<br> And weary toil o’ertask my strength.<br> Give me to eat; give me to drink;<br> And teach my mind the way to think.<br> Then bid a baggage cart prepare<br> Along the route myself to bear.  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2">2</a></font></span>  Twitters fast the oriole,<br>     Where shows its edge the mound:<br>   The happy little creature<br>     Its resting place has found.<br> So have not I. I dare not shrink<br> From the long way, but trembling think,<br> Unable to hold on, I'll sink. <a name="page_326"><font size="1" color="green">p. 326</font></a><br> Give me to drink; and give me food;<br> And teach my mind the thing that's good.<br> Then bid a baggage cart prepare<br> Along the route myself to bear.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_3">3</a></font></span>  Twitters fast the oriole,<br>     Where spreads its side the mound.<br>   The happy little creature<br>     Its resting place has found.<br> So have not I. I dare not shrink<br> From the long way, but trembling think,<br> Before we reach the end, I'll sink.<br> Give me to drink; to food invite;<br> And tell my mind the thing that's right.<br> Then bid a baggage cart prepare<br> Along the route myself to hear.</p></div>
