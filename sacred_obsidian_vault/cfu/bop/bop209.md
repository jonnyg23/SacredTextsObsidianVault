 <p><a name="page_263"><font size="1" color="green">p. 263</font></a></p>  <h4 align="center">VI</h4>  
 <p></p><div style="font-size: 90%">The <i>Hsiang Pai;</i> metaphorical, narrative, and allusive. A eunuch, suffering from slander, complains of his fate, and warns and denounces his enemies.</div>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 20%; margin-right: 20%; font-size: 110%;"><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1">1</a></font></span>A few fine lines, at random drawn,<br> Like the shell pattern wrought in lawn<br>   To hasty glance will seem.<br> My trivial faults base slander's slime<br> Distorted into foulest crime,<br>   And men me worthless deem.  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2">2</a></font></span>A few small points, pricked down on wood,<br> May be made out a picture good<br>   Of the bright southern Sieve.<br> Who planned, and helped those slanderers vile,<br> My name with base lies to defile?<br>   Unpitied, here I grieve.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_3">3</a></font></span>With babbling tongues you go about,<br> And only scheme how to make out<br>   The lies you scatter round.<br> Hear me.—Be careful what you say;<br> People erelong your words will weigh,<br>   And liars you'll be found.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_4">4</a></font></span>Clever you are, with changeful schemes!<br> How else could all your evil dreams <a name="page_264"><font size="1" color="green">p. 264</font></a><br>   And slanders work their way?<br> Men now believe you; by and by,<br> The truth found out, each vicious lie<br>   Will ill for ill repay.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_5">5</a></font></span>The proud rejoice; the sufferer weeps.<br> O azure Heaven, from out thy deeps<br>   Why look in silence down?<br> Behold those proud men and rebuke;<br> With pity on the sufferers look,<br>   And on the evil frown.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_6">6</a></font></span>Those slanderers I would gladly take,<br> With all who help their schemes to make,<br>   And to the tigers throw.<br> If wolves and tigers such should spare,<br> I'd hurl them ’midst the freezing air,<br>   Where the keen north winds blow.<br> And should the north compassion feel<br> I'd fling them to great Heaven, to deal<br>   On them its direst woe.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_7">7</a></font></span>As on the acred heights <i>you</i> dwell,<br> My place is in the willow dell,<br>   One is the other near.<br> Before you, officers, I spread<br> These lines by me, poor eunuch, made.<br>   Think not Mêng-tzŭ severe.</p></div>
