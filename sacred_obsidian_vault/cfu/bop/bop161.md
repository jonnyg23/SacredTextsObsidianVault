 <p></p><div style="font-size: 90%">The <i>Fa Ko;</i> metaphorical. While there is a proper and necessary way for everything, man need not go far to find what it is.</div>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 20%; margin-right: 20%; font-size: 110%;"><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1">1</a></font></span>In hewing an ax shaft, how must you act?<br>   Another ax take, or you'll never succeed.<br> In taking a wife, be sure ‘tis a fact,<br>   That with no go-between you never can speed.  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2">2</a></font></span>In hewing an ax shaft, hewing a shaft,<br>   For a copy you have the ax in your hand.<br> In choosing a wife, you follow the craft,<br>   And forthwith on the mats the feast vessels stand.</p></div>
