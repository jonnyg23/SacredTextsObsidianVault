 <p><a name="page_120"><font size="1" color="green">p. 120</font></a></p>  <h4 align="center">IV</h4>  
 <p></p><div style="font-size: 90%">The <i>Chih Hu;</i> narrative. A young soldier on service solaces himself with the thought of home.</div>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 20%; margin-right: 20%; font-size: 110%;"><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1">1</a></font></span>To the top of that tree-clad hill I go,<br>   And towards my father I gaze,<br> Till with my mind's eye his form I espy,<br>   And my mind's ear hears how he says:<br> "Alas for my son on service abroad!<br>   He rests not from morning till eve.<br> May he careful be, and come back to me!<br>   While he is away, how I grieve!"  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2">2</a></font></span>To the top of that barren hill. I climb,<br>   And towards my mother I gaze,<br> Till with my mind's eye her form I espy,<br>   And my mind's ear hears how she says:<br> "Alas for my child on service abroad!<br>   He never in sleep shuts an eye.<br> May he careful be, and come back to me!<br>   In the wild may his body not lie!" <a name="page_121"><font size="1" color="green">p. 121</font></a></p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_3">3</a></font></span>Up the lofty ridge I, toiling, ascend,<br>   And towards my brother I gaze,<br> Till with my mind's eye his form I espy,<br>   And my mind's ear hears how he says:<br> "Alas! my young brother, serving abroad,<br>   All day with his comrades must roam.<br> May he careful be, and come back to me,<br>   And die not away from his home!"</p></div>
