 <p><a name="page_74"><font size="1" color="green">p. 74</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center"><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Book VI. The Odes of the Royal Domain</span></h3>  <h4 align="center">I</h4>  
 <p></p><div style="font-size: 90%">The <i>Shu Li;</i> narrative. An officer describes his melancholy and reflections on seeing the desolation of the old capital of Chou.</div>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 20%; margin-right: 20%; font-size: 110%;"><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1">1</a></font></span>Where the palaces rose grand,<br> When Chou nobly ruled the land,<br> Millets, some with drooping head,<br> Some, just coming into blade,<br> All around abundant grew.<br> Slow the fields I wandered through,<br> Moved in heart such sight to view.<br> Friends who knew me understood.<br> What induced my saddened mood.<br> Those who did not know me said,<br> There I search for something made.<br> O thou azure Heaven, remote,<br> Who this desolation wrought?  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2">2</a></font></span>Where the palaces rose grand,<br> When Chou nobly ruled the land,<br> Millets, drooping, heavy here,<br> There just coming into ear,<br> All around abundant grew. <a name="page_75"><font size="1" color="green">p. 75</font></a><br> Slow the fields I wandered through,<br> Drunk with grief such sights to view.<br> Friends who knew me understood<br> What induced my saddened mood.<br> Those who did not know me said,<br> There I search for something made.<br> O thou azure Heaven, remote,<br> Who this desolation wrought?</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_3">3</a></font></span>Where the palaces rose grand,<br> When Chou nobly ruled the land,<br> Millets, heavy, drooping low,<br> Some the bursting grain that show,<br> All around abundant grew.<br> Slow the fields I wandered through,<br> Breath nigh stopt such scene to view.<br> Friends who knew me understood<br> What induced my saddened mood.<br> Those who did not know me said,<br> There I search for something made.<br> O thou azure Heaven, remote,<br> Who this desolation wrought?</p></div>
