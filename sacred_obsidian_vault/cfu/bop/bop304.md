 <p><a name="page_461"><font size="1" color="green">p. 461</font></a></p>  <h4 align="center">X</h4>  
 <p></p><div style="font-size: 90%">The <i>Lai;</i> narrative. The praise of King Wên.</div>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 20%; margin-right: 20%; font-size: 110%;">King Wên with earnest purpose toiled,<br>   And nothing left undone.<br> ’Tis right his sons should now be styled<br>   Lords of the realm he won.  
 <p>Abroad his virtue we will spread;<br>   Him in our hearts we'll shrine;<br> Our aim that peace establishèd<br>   May last while lasts our line.</p>  
 <p>Through him it was there came to Chou<br>   Heaven's favor and decree.<br> Him in our breasts we cherish now;<br>   Our pattern shall he be.</p></div>
