 <p><a name="page_6"><font size="1" color="green">p. 6</font></a></p>  <h4 align="center">V</h4>  
 <p></p><div style="font-size: 90%">The Chung Ssŭ; metaphorical. The fruitfulness of the locust; supposed to celebrate Tai Ssŭ’s freedom from jealousy.</div>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 20%; margin-right: 20%; font-size: 110%;"><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1">1</a></font></span>Ye locusts, wingèd tribes,<br>   Gather in concord fine;<br> Well your descendants may<br>   In numerous bright hosts shine!  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2">2</a></font></span>Ye locusts, wingèd tribes,<br>   Your wings in flight resound;<br> Well your descendants may<br>   In endless lines be found!</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_3">3</a></font></span>Ye locusts, wingèd tribes,<br>   Together cluster strong;<br> Well your descendants may<br>   In swarms forever throng!</p></div>
