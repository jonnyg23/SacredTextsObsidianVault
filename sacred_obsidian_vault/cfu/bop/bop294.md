 <p></p><div style="font-size: 90%">The <i>Wu;</i> narrative. Sung in the ancestral temple to the music regulating the dance in honor of the achievements of King Wu.</div>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 20%; margin-right: 20%; font-size: 110%;">You were august and great, O royal Wu,<br>   And showed your matchless strength in every plan.<br> Your father Wên, accomplished he and true,<br>   Had on the road to triumph led the van. <a name="page_450"><font size="1" color="green">p. 450</font></a>  
 <p>The heritage you from your sire obtained,<br>   And soon the oppressive rule of Yin was staid.<br> O’er all its hosts the victory you gained,<br>   And, firmly ’stablished, all the land you swayed.</p></div>
