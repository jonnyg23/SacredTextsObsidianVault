 <p><a name="page_266"><font size="1" color="green">p. 266</font></a></p>  <h4 align="center">VIII</h4>  
 <p></p><div style="font-size: 90%">The <i>Liao O;</i> metaphorical, narrative, and allusive. A son deplores his hard fate in being prevented from rendering the last services to his parents, and enlarges on the parental claim.</div>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 20%; margin-right: 20%; font-size: 110%;"><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1">1</a></font></span>Long and large the <i>O</i> plants grow.<br> <i>Hao</i> plants surely I should know!<br> How can I confound them so?<br> Grief has robbed my eyes of sight,<br> Almost plunging me in night.<br> Others' hands laid in the grave,<br> Those whose pain my being gave.  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2">2</a></font></span>Long and large the <i>O</i> plants grow.<br> <i>Wei</i> plants surely I should know!<br> How can I confound them so?<br> Grief has robbed my eyes of sight,<br> Almost plunging me in night.<br> Others’ hands laid in the earth,<br> Those whose suffering gave me birth.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_3">3</a></font></span>Pitcher should be filled from vase;<br> Where this fails, ‘tis reckoned base.<br> Than to live as orphan left,<br> Better be of life bereft! <a name="page_267"><font size="1" color="green">p. 267</font></a><br> Father dead, on whom depend?<br> Mother dead, where find a friend?<br> I, abroad, this sad case know,<br> And, at home, can nowhere go.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_4">4</a></font></span>Father, from whose loins I sprung,<br> Mother, on whose breast I hung,<br> Tender were ye, and ye fed,<br> Now upheld, now gently led.<br> Eyes untiring watched my way;<br> Often in your arms I lay.<br> How could I repay your love,<br> Vast as arch of heaven above?</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_5">5</a></font></span>Cold and bleak that southern hill!<br> Tempest fierce with terror thrill.<br> All around is dark, but more<br> Dark the lot which I deplore!<br> Others all can happy be;—<br> Why from grief am I not free?</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_6">6</a></font></span>Hill so steep what foot can brave?<br> Blustering winds around it rave.<br> Fierce the winds! As fierce the fate,<br> Which pursues me desolate!<br> Happy all save me alone,<br> Thinking aye of dues undone!</p></div>
