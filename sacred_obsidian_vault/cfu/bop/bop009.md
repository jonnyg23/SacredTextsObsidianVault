 <p></p><div style="font-size: 90%">The<i> T‘ao Yao;</i> allusive. Praise of a bride going to her future home.</div>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 20%; margin-right: 20%; font-size: 110%;"><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1">1</a></font></span>Graceful and young the peach tree stands;<br>   How rich its flowers, all gleaming bright!<br> This bride to her new home repairs;<br>   Chamber and house she'll order right. <a name="page_7"><font size="1" color="green">p. 7</font></a>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2">2</a></font></span>Graceful and young the peach tree stands;<br>   Large crops of fruit it soon will show.<br> This bride to her new home repairs;<br>   Chamber and house her sway shall know.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_3">3</a></font></span>Graceful and young the peach tree stands,<br>   Its foliage clustering green and full.<br> This bride to her new home repairs;<br>   Her household will attest her rule.</p>
