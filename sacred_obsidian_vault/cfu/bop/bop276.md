 <p><a name="page_434"><font size="1" color="green">p. 434</font></a></p>  <h4 align="center">II</h4>  
 <p></p><div style="font-size: 90%">The <i>Wei T‘ien Chih Ming;</i> narrative. Celebrating the virtue of King Wên as comparable to that of Heaven; and looking to him for blessing in the future.</div>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 20%; margin-right: 20%; font-size: 110%;">Heaven by a deep and ceaseless law<br>   Orders its ways with man.<br> Pure shone, without a single flaw,<br>   The virtue of King Wên.<br> To us he shows his kindness still.<br>   As all our powers we strain<br> To be in concord with his will,<br>   His favor we shall gain.<br> So may the last his throne to fill<br>   His love and grace retain!</div>
