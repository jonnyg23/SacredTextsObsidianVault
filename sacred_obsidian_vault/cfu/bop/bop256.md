 <p><a name="page_365"><font size="1" color="green">p. 365</font></a></p>  <h4 align="center">III</h4>  
 <p></p><div style="font-size: 90%">The <i>Chi Tsui;</i> narrative. Responsive to the last;—the uncles and brothers of the king express their sense of his kindness, and their wishes for his happiness, mostly in the words in which the personators of the dead had conveyed the satisfaction of his ancestors with the sacrifice offered to them and promised to him their blessing.</div>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 20%; margin-right: 20%; font-size: 110%;"><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1">1</a></font></span>You gave us of the brimming cup,<br>   And crowned us with your grace.<br> Great king, forever may you live<br>   With brightening happiness!  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2">2</a></font></span>You gave us of the brimming cup,<br>   And dainty viands spread.<br> May you, great king, forever live!<br>   Your splendor never fade!</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_3">3</a></font></span>Yea, perfect may your wisdom be,<br>   Achieving its high aim!<br> "First ends in last; last springs from first;—<br>   Revealed, that message came. <a name="page_366"><font size="1" color="green">p. 366</font></a></p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_4">4</a></font></span>What said the message from your sires?<br>   "Vessels and gifts are clean;<br> And all your friends, assisting you,<br>   Behave with reverent mien.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_5">5</a></font></span>"Most reverently you did your part,<br>   And reverent by your side<br> Your son appeared. On you henceforth<br>   Shall ceaseless blessings bide.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_6">6</a></font></span>"What shall the ceaseless blessing be?<br>   That in your palace high<br> For myriad years you dwell in peace,<br>   Rich in posterity.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_7">7</a></font></span>"What of the sons shall from you flow?<br>   Through you, thus blessed of Heaven,<br> Those myriad years of royal sway<br>   Shall to your sons be given.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_8">8</a></font></span>"And whence shall come that lustrous race?<br>   From your heroic wife.<br> From her shall come the line of sons<br>   To live again your life."</p></div>
