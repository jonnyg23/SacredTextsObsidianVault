 <p></p><div style="font-size: 90%">The <i>Fang Yu Ch‘iao Ch‘ao;</i> allusive. A lady laments the alienation of her lover by means of evil tongues.</div>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 20%; margin-right: 20%; font-size: 110%;"><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1">1</a></font></span>The magpies’ nests are on the bank;<br> On heights the lovely pea grows rank; <a name="page_156"><font size="1" color="green">p. 156</font></a><br> While withered my heart is and blank.<br>   Who wiled my love away?  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2">2</a></font></span>The temple path its fine tiles shows;<br> On heights the ribbon plant bright grows;<br> While my breast heaves with sorrow's throes.<br>   Who led his heart astray?</p></div>
