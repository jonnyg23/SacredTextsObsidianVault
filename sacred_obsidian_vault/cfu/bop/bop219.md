 <p><a name="page_287"><font size="1" color="green">p. 287</font></a></p>  <h4 align="center">VI</h4>  
 <p></p><div style="font-size: 90%">The <i>Hsin Nan Shan;</i> narrative. Husbandry traced to its first author; details about it, going on to the subject of sacrifices to ancestors.</div>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 20%; margin-right: 20%; font-size: 110%;"><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1">1</a></font></span>Yes, all about that southern hill,<br>   Great Yü pursued his wondrous toil.<br> He drained the plain, the marsh he dried;<br>   Our lord in fields laid out the soil.<br> Their boundaries we now define,<br> As south, or east, the fields incline.  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2">2</a></font></span>The wintry heavens, one arch of clouds,<br>   Send down the flakes that fill the sky.<br> Then come the drizzling rains of spring,<br>   That moisture, with the snow, supply,<br> To soak and fit the ground for use,<br> And in its season grain produce.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_3">3</a></font></span>The plots, arranged in order fit,<br>   The millets in abundance bear. <a name="page_288"><font size="1" color="green">p. 288</font></a><br> So shall our lord the harvest view;<br>   While food and spirits we prepare,<br> For those in whom our sires descend,<br> And guests who at the feast attend.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_4">4</a></font></span>The central plot the huts contains,<br>   While gourds each path and boundary line.<br> Their fruits preserved, aside we put,<br>   Till ’mong the offerings they shall shine.<br> So through his sires our lord shall gain<br> Long life, and gifts from Heaven obtain.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_5">5</a></font></span>The fragrant spirits first are poured;<br>   Then near the gate the bull is led;—<br> So we invite our sires to come.<br>   To show the victim pure and red,<br> The knife with bells slides through the hair.<br> Its blood and fat away we bear.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_6">6</a></font></span>'Then all our offerings we present,<br>   Diffusing round a fragrance great.<br> How brilliant is the sacrifice!<br>   Our ancestors in kingly state<br> Are there, unseen; but they shall send<br> Blessing and life,—years without end!</p></div>
