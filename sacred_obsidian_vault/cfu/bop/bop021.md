 <p><a name="page_17"><font size="1" color="green">p. 17</font></a></p>  <h4 align="center">VII</h4>  
 <p></p><div style="font-size: 90%">The <i>Kao Yang;</i> narrative. The easy dignity of the officers at some court.</div>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 20%; margin-right: 20%; font-size: 110%;"><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1">1</a></font></span>Arrayed in skins of lamb or sheep,<br>   With five silk braidings all of white,<br> From court they go, to take their meal,<br>   All self-possessed, with spirits light.  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2">2</a></font></span>How on their skins of lamb or sheep<br>   The five seams wrought with white silk show!<br> With easy steps, and self-possessed,<br>   From court, to take their meal, they go.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_3">3</a></font></span>Upon their skins of lamb or sheep<br>   Shines the white silk the seams to link.<br> With easy steps and self-possessed,<br>   They go from court to eat and drink.</p></div>
