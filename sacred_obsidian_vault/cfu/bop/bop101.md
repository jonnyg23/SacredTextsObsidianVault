 <p><a name="page_107"><font size="1" color="green">p. 107</font></a></p>  <h4 align="center">III</h4>  
 <p></p><div style="font-size: 90%">The <i>Chu;</i> narrative. A bride describes her first meeting with her bridegroom.</div>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 20%; margin-right: 20%; font-size: 110%;"><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1">1</a></font></span>He waited ’tween the gate and screen,<br> With ear-plug strings of white silk seen.<br> The plugs themselves had <i>hua</i> stones’ sheen.  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2">2</a></font></span>He waited in the court and stood,<br> With ear-plug strings of silk green-hued.<br> The plugs themselves were <i>yung</i> stones good.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_3">3</a></font></span>There in the hall he stood and stayed.<br> Of yellow silk his plug strings made.<br> The plugs of fine <i>ying</i> gems displayed.</p></div>
