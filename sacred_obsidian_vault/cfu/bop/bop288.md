 <p><a name="page_444"><font size="1" color="green">p. 444</font></a></p>  <h4 align="center">IV</h4>  
 <p></p><div style="font-size: 90%">The <i>Fêng Nien;</i> narrative. An ode of thanksgiving for a plentiful year.</div>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 20%; margin-right: 20%; font-size: 110%;">The plenteous year has given us large supply<br> Of rice and millet, and our granaries high<br> Hardly suffice the produce to contain,—<br> Millions of measures of the garnered grain!<br> From this distilled, shall spirits, strong and sweet,<br> Our sires and mothers with their fragrance greet,<br> When to their shrines each season we repair;<br> And in all other rites their part shall bear.<br> Blessings of every kind our land shall crown;—<br> And all the spirits our devotion own!</div>
