 <p><a name="page_73"><font size="1" color="green">p. 73</font></a></p>  <h4 align="center">X</h4>  
 <p></p><div style="font-size: 90%">The <i>Mu Kua;</i> metaphorical. Small gifts of kindness should be responded to with greater; while friendship is more than any gift.</div>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 20%; margin-right: 20%; font-size: 110%;"><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1">1</a></font></span>A tree gourd they gave me in compliment,<br>   And I in return gave a lovely <i>chü</i> gem,<br> ’Twas not in return for the compliment;—<br>   I wished to make lasting my friendship with them.  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2">2</a></font></span>A peach they presented in compliment,<br>   And I in return gave a lovely <i>yao</i> gem.<br> ’Twas not in return for the compliment;—<br>   I wished to make lasting my friendship with them.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_3">3</a></font></span>A plum they presented in compliment,<br>   And I in return gave a lovely <i>ch‘i</i> stone.<br> ’Twas not in return for the compliment;—<br>   Our friendship to knit was my motive alone.</p></div>
