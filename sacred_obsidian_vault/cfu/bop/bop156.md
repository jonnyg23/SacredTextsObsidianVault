 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Book of Poetry</i>, tr. by James Legge, [1876], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h4 align="center">IV</h4>  
 <p></p><div style="font-size: 90%">The <i>Hsia Ch‘üan;</i> metaphorical and allusive. The misery and misgovernment of Ts‘ao makes the writer think of Chou and of its former vigor and prosperity.</div>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 20%; margin-right: 20%; font-size: 110%;"><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_1">1</a></font></span>Down from the spring the chilling waters pass,<br> And overflow the bushy wolf-tail grass;—<br>   Fit emblem of our state unblest. <a name="page_167"><font size="1" color="green">p. 167</font></a><br> In the dark night, restless, I wake and sigh,<br> And to my thoughts Chou's capital comes nigh,<br>   When through its kings the land had rest.  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_2">2</a></font></span>The bushy southernwood is flooded o’er,<br> By the cold waters from that spring which pour;—<br>   Fit emblem of our state unblest.<br> In the dark night, restless, I wake and sigh,<br> And to my thoughts Chou's capital comes nigh,<br>   When through its kings the land had rest.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_3">3</a></font></span>The bushy plants, whose stalks serve to divine<br> Beneath the waters of that cold spring pine;—<br>   Fit emblem of our state unblest.<br> In the dark night, restless, I wake and sigh,<br> And to my thoughts Chou's capital comes nigh,<br>   When through its kings the land had rest.</p>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_4">4</a></font></span>Of old there fell the fertilizing rains,<br> And brightly shone the millet on our plains;—<br>   The land knew no oppression hard.<br> The states the king's authority obeyed,<br> And to each lord, for loyal service paid,<br>   The chief of Hsün dispensed reward.</p></div>
