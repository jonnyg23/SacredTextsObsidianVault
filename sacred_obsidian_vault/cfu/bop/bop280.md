 <p></p><div style="font-size: 90%">The <i>Hao T‘ien Yu Ch‘êng Ming;</i> narrative. Appropriate at a sacrifice to King Ch‘êng.</div>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 20%; margin-right: 20%; font-size: 110%;">The fixed decree of mighty Heaven<br>   Had long to Chou assigned the throne,<br> Which first to Wên and Wu was given.<br>   Ch‘êng next to them as ruler shone. <a name="page_437"><font size="1" color="green">p. 437</font></a><br> Then the foundations of his power<br>   Were made by Ch‘êng more deep and sure.<br> Through night and day no idle hour<br>   He knew,—the reticent and pure.<br> His virtue wrought, and glorified<br>   The heritage he had obtained;<br> Nor were his heart's best powers unplied,<br>   Till o’er the land good order reigned.</div>
