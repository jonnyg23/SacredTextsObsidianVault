 <p></p><div style="font-size: 90%">The <i>Cho;</i> narrative. An ode in praise of King Wu.</div>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 20%; margin-right: 20%; font-size: 110%;">Oh! powerful was the mighty host,<br>   By which the throne to Chou was given.<br> Wu nursed it, while the time was dark,<br>   Submissive to the will of Heaven.<br> But when the day propitious came,<br> He armed himself, and marched to fame. <a name="page_460"><font size="1" color="green">p. 460</font></a><br> And what the martial king achieved,<br>   We have been favored to obtain.<br> That we may rightly use the lot,<br>   Which thou for us didst nobly gain,<br> Thy course, O king, we on our part<br>   Will follow with sincerest heart.</div>
