 <p>In the Pavilion of Green Purity<br> The green of the circling lawns is mirrored anew,<br> Across the crystal frontiers <a name="fr_4"></a><a href="#fn_4"><font size="1">2</font></a> that divide<br> Are smiling flowers and raindrops glancing through.<br> And lo, the strange blue void wells clear of the clouds<br> Like a sky beyond a sky, with a blue beyond the blue.</p>
