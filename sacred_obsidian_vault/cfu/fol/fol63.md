 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>A Feast of Lanterns</i>, by L. Cranmer-Byng, [1916], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">A MEDLEY OF PERFUME</h3>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">Prone beside the western stream,<br> In the lilied dusk I dream;<br> And mocking me the wind of spring<br> Such medley of perfume doth bring,<br> I cannot tell what fragrance blows,<br> Nor guess the lotus from the rose.</div>
