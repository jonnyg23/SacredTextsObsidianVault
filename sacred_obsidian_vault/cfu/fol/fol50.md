 <p><a name="page_79"><font size="1" color="green">p. 79</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">WEN T‘UNG</h3>  <h4 align="center">CIRCA A.D. 1100</h4>  <h3 align="center">MORNING</h3>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px"><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Sunbeams</span> through twinkling pinewoods cast<br> Their shadows on my window screen.<br> A night of clouds and rain is past<br> And, newly blue and freshly green,<br> The Dawn rebuilds my world at last.<br> Pear-tree and plum-tree shed their burden sweet,<br> And children's happy voices rouse the street.</div>
