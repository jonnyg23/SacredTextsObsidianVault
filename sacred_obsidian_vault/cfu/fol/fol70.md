 <p><a name="page_95"><font size="1" color="green">p. 95</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">HOME</h3>  
 <p>Surely there sings no lighter heart than mine beneath the sky!<br> And now, companion of my nights of long moon-dreams, good-bye!<br> For mine is the silver dragon car<br> That hovers beyond the Rainbow Dome, <a name="fr_5"></a><a href="#fn_5"><font size="1">1</font></a><br> And it's oh to be galloping, galloping home <br> Where my dream-born children are!</p>
