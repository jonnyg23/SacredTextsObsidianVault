 <p><a name="page_69"><font size="1" color="green">p. 69</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">AT FORTY-ONE</h3>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">The waters from the pool are vanishing;<br> A mellow sunlight steeps the window-panes,<br> And autumn winds ply many a pleasant fan.  
 <p>O gold and green, half-ripe, the acacias glow,<br> While o’er the threshold of his summer falls<br> The shadow of a solitary man.</p></div>  <h4><hr>
