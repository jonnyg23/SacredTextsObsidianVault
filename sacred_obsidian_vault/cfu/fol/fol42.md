 <p><a name="page_72"><font size="1" color="green">p. 72</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">SONGS OF THE NIGHT</h3>  <h4 align="center">I</h4>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">In flowing crowds<br> The moon-born clouds<br> Cast their light shade<br> O’er stairs of jade;<br> And all the moonlit ways are one,<br> Shining in silver unison.<br> Yet who can read aright<br> The mystery of night?</div>  <h4 align="center">II</h4>  
 <p>Spring-time, and sounds of the streaming water-fall;<br> Deep night, on shrunken hill-tops spreads her pall.<br> The moon steers through a maze of pines, and lo,<br> A thousand thrusting peaks are set aglow.</p>  <h4 align="center">III</h4>  
 <p>In the cold water the collected snow<br> Melts, and the frozen stream begins to flow.<br> The laughing girls slip homeward through the dark,<br> While sand-birds wheel around the fisher's barque.</p>
