 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>A Feast of Lanterns</i>, by L. Cranmer-Byng, [1916], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">WILLOW FLOWERS</h3>  
 <p>O willow flowers like flakes of snow,<br> Where do your wandering legions go?<br> Little we care, and less we know!<br> Our ways are the ways of the wind;<br> Our life in the whirl, and death in the drifts below.</p>
