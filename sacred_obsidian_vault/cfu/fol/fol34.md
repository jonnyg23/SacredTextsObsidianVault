 <p><a name="page_68"><font size="1" color="green">p. 68</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">MORNING STUDIES</h3>  
 <p>Smooth and white the walls that ring the pool,<br> Carefully swept the rose-walk's mossy green,<br> Across the water dimpling winds blow cool<br> Where lotus-leaves as large as fans are seen.<br> What does yon flower-bright pavilion hold?<br> Simply a lute and there a song enscrolled.<br> To the sound of dropping pearls I turn the leaves,<br> Playing, swaying beneath the spell the soul of Autumn weaves.<br> Thus quietly the morning studies end,<br> And so I wait my friend.</p>
