 <p><a name="page_80"><font size="1" color="green">p. 80</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">LU YU</h3>  <h4 align="center">A.D. 1125-1210</h4>  
 <p><small><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">A distinguished</span> official who also made a name for himself as poet and historian.</small></p>  <h3 align="center">SONG OF THREE GORGES</h3>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">From the twelve Hills of the Witches I see the Nine Peaks rise;<br> Beyond my prows a myriad tints flush autumn's empty skies.<br> Untrue the legend, "Morning clouds, and evening rain,"<br> The howling of gibbons in bright moonlight fills the plain.<br> When long June days begin<br> I wander to Nan-pin,<br> And moor my boat to a little quay<br> Where monkeys swing from tree to tree.<br> Now shadows gloom Ch‘u Yüan's grey memorial;<br> And by the tomb of Yü red roses fall.</div>
