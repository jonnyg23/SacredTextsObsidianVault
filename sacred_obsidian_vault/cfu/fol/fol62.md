 <p><a name="page_91"><font size="1" color="green">p. 91</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">A FEAST OF LANTERNS</h3>  
 <p></p><div style="margin-left: 32px">   In spring for sheer delight<br> I set the lanterns swinging through the trees,<br>    Bright as the myriad argosies of night,<br>    That ride the clouded billows of the sky.<br> Red dragons leap and plunge in gold and silver seas,<br>    And, O my garden gleaming cold and white,<br>    Thou hast outshone the far faint moon on high.</div>
