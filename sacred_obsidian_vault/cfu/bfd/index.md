 <p>
 This is a translation of the <cite>Hsio Ching</cite>, or the
 <cite>Book of Filial Duty</cite>.
 It was written about 400 BCE, about a century following the death
 of Confucius.
 The source edition of this etext 
 was published in the <a href="../../woe/index.htm">Wisdom of the East</a>
 series.
 </p>
