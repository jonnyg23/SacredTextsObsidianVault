   <h5 align="center">ODE 3. THE WEI <i>KH</i>ING.</h5>  <h5 align="center">APPROPRIATE AT SOME SACRIFICE TO KING WĂN, AND CELEBRATING HIS STATUTES.</h5>  
 <p><font size="-1">Nothing more can, with any likelihood of truth, be said of this short piece, which moreover has the appearance of being a fragment.</font></p>  
 <p>Clear and to be preserved bright, Are the statutes of king Wăn. From the first sacrifice (to him), Till now when they have issued in our complete state, They have been the happy omen of (the fortunes of) <i>K</i>âu.</p>
