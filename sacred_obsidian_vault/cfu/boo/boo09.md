 <p><a name="page_22"><font size="1" color="green">p. 22</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center"><i>THE SLANDERERS</i></h3>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">The</span> blue flies buzz upon the wing,<br> From fence to fence they wander;<br> O happy King! O courteous King!<br> Give heed to no man's slander.</p>  
 <p>The noisy blue flies rumble round,<br> Upon the gum-trees lighting;<br> A tongue of evil hath no bound,<br> And sets the realm a-fighting.</p>  
 <p>The clumsy blue flies buzzing round<br> Upon the hazels blunder;<br> O cursèd tongue that knows no bound,<br> And sets us two asunder.</p>
