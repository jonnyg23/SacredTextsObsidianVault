 <p><a name="page_49"><font size="1" color="green">p. 49</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center"><i>BEFORE THE FORD</i></h3>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">If</span> your heart be kind and true,<br> I will ford the stream with you.<br> If your fickle thoughts go straying,<br> Come with me no more a-maying.<br> Oh, you silly, silly swain!<br> Better men than you remain.</p>  
 <p>If you love me, dear my lord,<br> Bid me and I'll cross the ford.<br> Should your roving thoughts forsake me,<br> Thoughts more kind will captive make me.<br> Oh, you silly, silly swain!<br> Better men than you remain.</p>
