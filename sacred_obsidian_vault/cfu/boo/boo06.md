 <p><a name="page_19"><font size="1" color="green">p. 19</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center"><i>FLIGHT</i></h3>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Cold</span> and keen the north wind blows,<br>   Silent falls the shroud of snows.<br>     You who gave me your heart<br>     Let us join hands and depart!<br>         Is this a time for delay?<br>             Now, while we may,<br>                   Let us away.</p>  
 <p>Wailingly the north wind goes,<br> Wailing through a whirl of snows.<br>     You who gave me your heart<br>     Let us join hands and depart!<br>         Is this a time for delay?<br>            Now, while we may,<br>              Let us away.</p>  
 <p>Only the lonely fox is red,<br> Black but the crow-flight overhead.<br>     You who gave me your heart,<br>     The chariot creaks to depart,<br>         Is this a time for delay?<br>            Now, while we may,<br>              Let us away.</p>
