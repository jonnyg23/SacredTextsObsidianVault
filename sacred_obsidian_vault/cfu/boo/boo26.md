 <p><a name="page_41"><font size="1" color="green">p. 41</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center"><i>THE EPHEMERAE</i></h3>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">In</span> black and yellow are clad<br> The wings of the ephemerae;<br> But my heart is sad, is sad,<br> Because they will not stay with me.</p>  
 <p>Many colours adorn<br> The robes of the ephemerae;<br> But my heart's forlorn, forlorn,<br> Because they will not rest with me.</p>  
 <p>In robes of hempen snow<br> Rise the ephemerae;<br> But my heart is full of woe<br> Because they will not bide with me.</p>
