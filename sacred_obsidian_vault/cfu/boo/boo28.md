 <p><a name="page_43"><font size="1" color="green">p. 43</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center"><i>THREE GIFTS</i></h3>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">A</span> royal gourd was given me,<br> And in exchange an emerald I gave,<br> No mere return for courtesy,<br> But that our friendship might outlast the grave.</p>  
 <p>A princely peach was given me,<br> And in exchange a ruby gem I gave,<br> No mere exchange for courtesy,<br> But that our friendship might outlast the grave.</p>  
 <p>A yellow plum was given me,<br> And in exchange a sardonyx I gave,<br> No mere return for courtesy,<br> But that our friendship might outlast the grave.</p>
