 <p><a name="page_44"><font size="1" color="green">p. 44</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center"><i>BRAVE THOUGHTS</i></h3>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Green</span> is the upper robe,<br> Green with a yellow lining;<br> My sorrow none may probe,<br> Nor can I cease repining.</p>  
 <p>Green is the upper robe,<br> The lower garb is yellow;<br> My sorrow none may probe,<br> Nor any season mellow.</p>  
 <p>The silk was of emerald dye,<br> Ah! this was all your doing;<br> But I dream of an age gone by<br> To keep my heart from rueing.</p>  
 <p>Fine linen or coarse, ’tis cold,<br> But all I have to dress me;<br> So I think of the men of old,<br> And find brave thoughts possess me.</p>
