 <p><a name="page_24"><font size="1" color="green">p. 24</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center"><i>CITY OF CHOW</i></h3>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Cold</span> from the spring the waters pass<br> Over the waving pampas grass.<br>         All night long in dream I lie,<br>         Ah me! ah me! to awake and sigh—<br>         Sigh for the City of Chow.</p>  
 <p>Cold from the spring the rising flood<br> Covers the tangled southernwood.<br>         All night long in dream I lie,<br>         Ah me! ah me! to awake and sigh—<br>         Sigh for the City of Chow.</p>  
 <p>Cold from its source the stream meanders,<br> Darkly down through the oleanders.<br>         All night long in dream I lie,<br>         Ah me! ah me! to awake and sigh—<br>         Sigh for the City of Chow.</p>
