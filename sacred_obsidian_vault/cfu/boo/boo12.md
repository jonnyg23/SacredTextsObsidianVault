 <p><a name="page_25"><font size="1" color="green">p. 25</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center"><i>I</i></h3>  <h3 align="center"><i>THE PRAYER OF THE EMPEROR CHING</i></h3>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Let</span> me be reverent, be reverent,<br> Even as the way of Heaven is evident,<br> And its appointment easy is to mar.</p>  
 <p>Let me not say, "It is too high above,"<br> Above us and below us doth it move,<br> And daily watches wheresoe’er we are.</p>  
 <p>It is but as a little child I ask,<br> Without intelligence to do my task,<br> Yet learning, month by month, and day by day.</p>  
 <p>I will hold fast some gleams of knowledge bright.<br> Help me to bear my heavy burden right,<br> And show me how to walk in wisdom's way.</p>
