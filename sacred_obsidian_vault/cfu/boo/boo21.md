 <p><a name="page_36"><font size="1" color="green">p. 36</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center"><i>THE NIGHTLONG TRYST</i></h3>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Down</span> by the eastern gate<br> The willow wood's astir;<br> From dusk to dawn I wait<br> Through the soundless hours for her,<br> Till the morning star is shining.</p>  
 <p>Down by the eastern gate<br> The willow-thicket pales;<br> From dusk to dawn I wait,<br> Till the last red lantern fails,<br> And the morning star is shining.</p>
