 <p><a name="page_40"><font size="1" color="green">p. 40</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center"><i>A FRIEND FORGOTTEN</i></h3>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">The</span> winds blow soft from the East,<br> But the storm welters by.<br> In the day of disaster and fear,<br> It was all you and I.<br> In the hour of your pride<br> You have cast me aside.</p>  
 <p>The bland winds blown from the East<br> Tornadoes pursue.<br> In the hour of disaster and fear<br> More than brother were you.<br> In the hour of delight<br> I am cast from your sight.</p>  
 <p>The winds come fair from the East<br> On the hills overhead<br> There is never a blade that is green,<br> Not a leaf but is dead.<br> My worth you forget,<br> But my faults linger yet.</p>
