 <p><a name="page_52"><font size="1" color="green">p. 52</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center"><i>WITHOUT HER</i></h3>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">The</span> moon comes forth in her brightness;<br> Fair as the moon was she,<br> That bright and beautiful lady<br> Who lit the night for me.<br> Would that I saw her now,<br> With the stars around her brow.</p>  
 <p>The moon comes forth in her splendour;<br> Fair through the void she burns,<br> That pale and beautiful lady,<br> My moon, no more returns;<br> But under the alien skies<br> She shines in a stranger's eyes.</p>  
 <p>The moon comes forth in her glory;<br> Kind to the world is she.<br> That kind and beautiful lady,<br> Doth charm no night for me.<br> Oh, when the dawn-star wanes<br> For the sun to rend my chains!</p>
