 <p><a name="page_42"><font size="1" color="green">p. 42</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center"><i>HAPPY IN HAOU</i></h3>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Fishes</span> are there, by the score, I trow,<br> Their large heads sleepily showing;<br> The King is here, in the city of Haou,<br> At ease while the wine-cup's flowing.</p>  
 <p>Fishes are there in the weeds enow,<br> Their long tails lazily swaying;<br> The King is here, in the city of Haou,<br> Drinking, dreaming, delaying.</p>  
 <p>The fish lie under the willow bough<br> That leans and shadows the rushes;<br> The King is here, in the city of Haou,<br> At peace, and the wine-cup blushes.</p>
