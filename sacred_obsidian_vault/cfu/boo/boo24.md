 <p><a name="page_39"><font size="1" color="green">p. 39</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center"><i>BLUE COLLAR</i></h3>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">O You</span> with the collar of blue,<br> My heart is longing for you.<br> Though to call you I am not free,<br> Wherefore not send to me?</p>  
 <p>O you with the girdle of blue,<br> Long, long do I think of you.<br> Though to seek you I am not free,<br> Wherefore not come to me?</p>  
 <p>Ah, random and pleasure-drawn,<br> To the View Tower you are gone;<br> And a day without your sight<br> Is like three months in its flight.</p>
