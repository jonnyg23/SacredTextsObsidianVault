 <p><a name="page_35"><font size="1" color="green">p. 35</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center"><i>PRINCELY VISITORS</i></h3>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">White</span> birds went over the West—<br> Young egrets, over the marshlands flying,<br> My Lords came visiting, ermine-dressed,<br> With the birds in their elegant beauty vieing.</p>  
 <p>In their States they have high renown,<br> Of the city of Chow they are never tiring,<br> And the rivers of night wind darkly down<br> Past the towers of their fame still aspiring.</p>
