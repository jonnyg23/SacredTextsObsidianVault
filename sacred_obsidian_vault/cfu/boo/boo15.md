 <p><a name="page_29"><font size="1" color="green">p. 29</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center"><i>LADY OF THE LAGOON</i></h3>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">By</span> the shores of that lagoon,<br> Where the water-lily lies,<br> Where the tall valerians rise<br> Slender as the crescent moon,<br> Goes Hëa Nan…Ah, Hëa Nan,<br>       Sleep brings me no relief:<br>       My heart is full of grief.</p>  
 <p>By the shores of that lagoon,<br> Where the drowsy lotus lies,<br> Where the tall valerians rise<br> Brighter than the orbèd moon,<br> Shines Hëa Nan…Ah, Hëa Nan,<br>       I turn and turn all night,<br>       And dawn brings no respite.</p>
