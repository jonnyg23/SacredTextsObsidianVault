 <p>
 This selection of poems from the Chinese Book of Odes appeared
 in the <a href="../../woe/index.htm">Wisdom of the East</a>
 series.
 The Book of Odes, or Shih Ching, is one of the Five Classics
 from ancient China.
 A complete <a href="../bop/index.htm">translation by Legge</a>
 is also available at this site.
