 <p><a name="page_45"><font size="1" color="green">p. 45</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center"><i>ON THE BANKS OF HO</i></h3>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">The</span> little boat of cypress rocks,<br> Rocks in the midst of Ho;<br> He was my lord, whose long dark locks<br> Divided in their downward flow.<br> Till death betide,<br> His bride,<br> I'll wed no other.<br> O Heaven! O mother!<br> Will you not understand your child?</p>  
 <p>The little boat of cypress rocks<br> There by the side of Ho;<br> He was my only one, whose locks<br> Divided in their downward flow.<br> Till death betide,<br> His bride,<br> I'll wed no other.<br> O Heaven! O mother!<br> Far from me be the thing defiled!<br> Will you not understand your child?</p>
