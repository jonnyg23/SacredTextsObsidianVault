 <p><a name="page_56"><font size="1" color="green">p. 56</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center"><i>UNAVAILING</i></h3>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">He</span> stabs me with a scornful smile.<br> <i>Winds are wailing at the door.<br> </i>Scornful words and whispers vile,<br> Ye have thrust me to the core.</p>  
 <p><i>Whirling dust the northwind blows.<br> </i>Surely he will seek his mate!<br> But he neither comes nor goes,<br> Through the long dumb hours that wait.</p>  
 <p>Blew the wind and veiled the sky;<br> One hour's gleam, then clouds again.<br> Sleep went trailing softly by,<br> Left me to the old dull pain.</p>  
 <p>Clouds across the darkness sweep,<br> Thunder rolls its monotone.<br> Who shall put my heart to sleep?<br> Heart that aches, and aches alone.</p>
