 <p><a name="page_93"><font size="1" color="green">p. 93</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">[59] SINCE I LAY ILL</h3>  <h4 align="center">(A.D. <i>840</i>)</h4>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Since</span> I lay ill, how long has passed?<br> Almost a hundred heavy-hanging days.<br> The maids have learnt to gather my medicine-herbs;<br> The dog no longer barks when the doctor comes.<br> The jars in my cellar are plastered deep with mould;<br> My singer's carpets are half crumbled to dust.<br> How can I bear, when the Earth renews her light,<br> To watch from a pillow the beauty of Spring unfold?</p>
