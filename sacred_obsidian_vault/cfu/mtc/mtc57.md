 <p><a name="page_90"><font size="1" color="green">p. 90</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">[56] TO A TALKATIVE GUEST</h3>  <h4 align="center">(A.D. <i>836</i>)</h4>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">The</span> town visitor's easy talk flows in an endless stream;<br> The country host's quiet thoughts ramble timidly on.<br> "I beg you, Sir, do not tell me about things at Ch‘ang-an;<br> For you entered just when my harp was tuned and lying<br>     balanced on my knees."</p>
