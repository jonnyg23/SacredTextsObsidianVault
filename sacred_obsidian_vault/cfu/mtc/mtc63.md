 <p><a name="page_99"><font size="1" color="green">p. 99</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">[62] RESIGNATION</h3>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Keep</span> off your thoughts from things that are past and done;<br> For thinking of the past wakes regret and pain.<br> Keep off your thoughts from thinking what will happen;<br> To think of the future fills one with dismay.<br> Better by day to sit like a sack in your chair;<br> Better by night to lie a stone in your bed.<br> When food comes, then open your mouth;<br> When sleep comes, then close your eyes.</p>
