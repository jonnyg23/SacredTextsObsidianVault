 <p><a name="page_34"><font size="1" color="green">p. 34</font></a> <a name="page_35"><font size="1" color="green">p. 35</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">PO CHÜ-I</h3>  <h3 align="center">LIFE OF PO CHÜ-I</h3>  
 <p>772 Born on 20th of 1st month.</p>  
 <p>800 Passes his examinations.</p>  
 <p>806 Receives a minor post at Chou-chih, near the capital.</p>  
 <p>807 Made Scholar of the Han Lin Academy.</p>  
 <p>811 Retires to Wei River, being in mourning for his mother.</p>  
 <p>814 Returns to Court.</p>  
 <p>815 Banished to Hsün-yang.</p>  
 <p>818 Removed to Chung-chou.</p>  
 <p>820 Reprieved and returns to Court.</p>  
 <p>822 Governor of Hangchow.</p>  
 <p>825 Governor of Soochow.</p>  
 <p>826 Retires owing to illness.</p>  
 <p>827 Returns to Ch‘ang-an.</p>  
 <p>829 Settles permanently at Lo-yang.</p>  
 <p>831 Governor of Ho-nan, the province of which Lo-yang was capital.</p>  
 <p>833 Retires owing to illness.</p>  
 <p>839 Has paralytic stroke in tenth month.</p>  
 <p>846 Dies in the eighth month.</p>
