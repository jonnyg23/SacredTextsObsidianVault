 <p><a name="page_92"><font size="1" color="green">p. 92</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">[58] MY SERVANT WAKES ME</h3>  <h4 align="center">(A.D. <i>839</i>)</h4>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">My</span> servant wakes me: "Master, it is broad day.<br> Rise from bed; I bring you bowl and comb.<br> Winter comes and the morning air is chill;<br> To-day your Honour must not venture abroad."<br> When I stay at home, no one comes to call;<br> What must I do with the long, idle hours?<br> Setting my chair where a faint sunshine falls<br> I have warmed wine and opened my poetry-books.</p>
