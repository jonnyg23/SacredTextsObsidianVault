 <p><a name="page_42"><font size="1" color="green">p. 42</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">[16] GOING ALONE TO SPEND A NIGHT AT THE HSIEN–YU TEMPLE</h3>  <h4 align="center">(A.D. <i>806</i>)</h4>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">The</span> crane from the shore standing at the top of the steps;<br> The moon on the pool seen at the open door;<br> Where these are, I made my lodging-place<br> And for two nights could not turn away.<br> I am glad I chanced on a place so lonely and still<br> With no companion to drag me early home.<br> Now that I have tasted the joy of being alone<br> I will never again come with a friend at my side.</p>
