 <p><a name="page_98"><font size="1" color="green">p. 98</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">[61] ILLNESS</h3>  <h4 align="center"><i>(Written circa 842, when he was paralyzed)</i></h4>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Dear</span> friends, there is no cause for so much sympathy.<br> I shall certainly manage from time to time to take my walks abroad.<br> All that matters is an active mind, what is the use of feet?<br> By land one can ride in a carrying-chair; by water, be rowed in a boat.</p>
