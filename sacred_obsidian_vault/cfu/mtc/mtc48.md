 <p><a name="page_79"><font size="1" color="green">p. 79</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">[47] WRITTEN WHEN GOVERNOR OF SOOCHOW</h3>  <h4 align="center">(A.D. <i>825</i>)</h4>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">A government</span> building, not my own home.<br> A Government garden, not my own trees.<br> But at Lo-yang I have a small house<br> And on Wei River I have built a thatched hut.<br> I am free from the ties of marrying and giving in marriage;<br> If I choose to retire, I have somewhere to end my days.<br> And though I have lingered long beyond my time,<br> To retire now would be better than not at all!</p>
