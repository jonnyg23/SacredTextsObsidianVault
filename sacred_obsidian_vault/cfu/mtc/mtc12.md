 <p><a name="page_36"><font size="1" color="green">p. 36</font></a> <a name="page_37"><font size="1" color="green">p. 37</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">[11] AFTER PASSING THE EXAMINATION</h3>  <h4 align="center">(A.D. <i>800</i>)</h4>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">For</span> ten years I never left my books;<br> I went up… and won unmerited praise.<br> My high place I do not much prize;<br> The joy of my parents will first make me proud.<br> Fellow students, six or seven men,<br> See me off as I leave the City gate.<br> My covered couch is ready to drive away;<br> Flutes and strings blend their parting tune.<br> Hopes achieved dull the pains of parting;<br> Fumes of wine shorten the long road…<br> Shod with wings is the horse of him who rides<br> On a Spring day the road that leads to home.</p>
