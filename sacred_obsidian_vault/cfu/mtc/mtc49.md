 <p><a name="page_80"><font size="1" color="green">p. 80</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">[48] GETTING UP EARLY ON A SPRING MORNING</h3>  <h4 align="center"><i>(Part of a poem written when Governor of Soochow in 825)</i></h4>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">The</span> early light of the rising sun shines on the beams of my house;<br> The first banging of opened doors echoes like the roll of a drum.<br> The dog lies curled on the stone step, for the earth is wet with dew;<br> The birds come near to the window and chatter, telling that the day is fine.<br> With the lingering fumes of yesterday's wine my head is still heavy;<br> With new doffing of winter clothes my body has grown light.</p>
