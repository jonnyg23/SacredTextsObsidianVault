                                             LYRA 
                  
               Lyra 
               In her bubble 
               Dances near me now 
                  
               Tiny seer 
               You never wobble 
               As you dance the Tao 
                  
               Rainbow Fire 
               Like the Maple 
               And the Oak Tree, too 
                 
               Flumes your Air 
               Suppley 
               Beauty!  Wow! 
                 
               (Us little people gotta stick together even at a distance...maybe better at a
             distance ) 
                        Hugh Read 
                        The Terraboard, Minneapolis, MN (Opus 1:282/341) 
               
                                                                                            1860
