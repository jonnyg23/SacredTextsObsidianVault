 <p><a name="page_xvi"><font size="1" color="green">p. xvi</font></a></p>
 
 <p></p><blockquote><span style="font-size: small">Was he one or many, merging<br>    Name and fame in one, <br>Like a stream, to which, converging, <br>    Many streamlets run?</span>
 <p align="center"> . . . . . . </p>
 
 <p><span style="font-size: small">Who shall call his dreams fallacious? <br>    Who has searched or sought <br>All the unexplored and spacious <br>    Universe of thought? <br><br>Who in his own skill confiding, <br>    Shall with rule and line <br>Mark the border-land dividing <br>    Human and divine? <br><br>Trismegistus! Three times greatest! <br>    How thy name sublime <br>Has descended to this latest <br>    Progeny of time! </span></p></blockquote>
 
 <p align="right"><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Longfellow</span>, <i>Hermes Trismegistus.</i> <a name="fr_0"></a><a href="#fn_0"><font size="1">1</font></a></p>
