 <p></p><h3 align="center">[AN APOPHTHEGM]</h3>
 <p><small>(Text: W., i. 34, 5.)</small></p>
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Hermes</span> on being asked, What is God?—replied: The Demiurge of wholes,—the Mind most wise and everlasting.</p>
