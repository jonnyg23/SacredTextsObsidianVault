 <p></p><h3 align="center">OF THE WHOLE ECONOMY</h3>
 <p><small>(Patrizzi (p. 38) gives no title, but only the heading: “To the Same Ammon (Αμμωνα).”</small></p>
 <p><small>Text: Stob., <i>Phys</i>., v. 16, under sub-heading: “Of the Whole Economy,” followed by: “Of Hermes from the [Sermons] to Ammon (Ἀμοῦν <a name="fr_201"></a><a href="#fn_201"><font size="1">1</font></a>)”; G. p. 68; M. i. 47; W. i. 79, 80.</small></p>
 <p><small>Ménard, Livre IV., No. i. of “Fragments of the Books of Hermes to Ammon”).</small></p>
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Now</span> what supporteth the whole World, <a name="fr_202"></a><a href="#fn_202"><font size="1">2</font></a> is Providence; what holdeth it together and encircleth it about, is [called] Necessity; what drives all on and drives them round, <a name="fr_203"></a><a href="#fn_203"><font size="1">3</font></a> is Fate, bringing Necessity to bear on them (for that its nature is the bringing into play of [this] Necessity); [it <a name="fr_204"></a><a href="#fn_204"><font size="1">4</font></a> is] the cause of birth and death <a name="fr_205"></a><a href="#fn_205"><font size="1">5</font></a> of life.</p>
 <p>So, then, the Cosmos is beneath the sway of</p>
 <p>
 </p>
 <p>
 </p>
 <p>
 </p>
 <p>
 </p>
 <p>
 </p>
 <p><a name="page_62"><font size="1" color="green">p. 62</font></a></p>
 <p><span class="contnote"><font size="-2" color="GREEN">[paragraph continues]</font></span> Providence <a name="fr_206"></a><a href="#fn_206"><font size="1">1</font></a> (for tis the first to meet with it); but Providence [itself] <a name="fr_207"></a><a href="#fn_207"><font size="1">2</font></a> extends itself to Heaven.</p>
 <p>For which cause, <a name="fr_208"></a><a href="#fn_208"><font size="1">3</font></a> too, the Gods revolve, and speed round [Heaven], <a name="fr_209"></a><a href="#fn_209"><font size="1">4</font></a> possessed of tireless, never-ceasing motion.</p>
 <p>But Fate [extends itself in Cosmos]; for which cause, too, Necessity [encompasses the Cosmos]. <a name="fr_210"></a><a href="#fn_210"><font size="1">5</font></a></p>
 <p>And Providence foreknows; but Fates the reason of the disposition of the Stars. <a name="fr_211"></a><a href="#fn_211"><font size="1">6</font></a></p>
 <p>Such is the law that no one can escape, by which all things are ordered. <a name="fr_212"></a><a href="#fn_212"><font size="1">7</font></a></p>
