 <p><a name="page_2"><font size="1" color="green">p. 2</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">PRAYER ON ENTERING THE SYNAGOGUE.</h3>  
 <p align="center"><i>On entering the Synagogue say the following:—</i></p>  
 <p>As for me, in the abundance of thy lovingkindness will I come into thy house: I will worship toward thy holy temple in the fear of thee.</p>  
 <p><small>Into the house of God we will walk with the throng.</small></p>  
 <p>How goodly are thy tents, O Jacob, thy dwelling places, O Israel! As for me, in the abundance of thy lovingkindness will I come into thy house: I will worship toward thy holy temple in the fear of thee. Lord, I love the habitation of thy house, and the place where thy glory dwelleth. As for me, I will worship and bow down: I will bend the knee before the Lord, my Maker. And as for me, may my prayer unto thee, O Lord, be in an acceptable time: O God, in the abundance of thy lovingkindness, answer me in the truth of thy salvation.</p>
