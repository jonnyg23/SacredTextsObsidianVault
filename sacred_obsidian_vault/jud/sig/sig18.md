 <p><a name="page_18"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 18</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">15</h3>  <h3 align="center">FOR A MARRIAGE</h3>  
 <p>Send to the princes daughter<br>   Her ruddy, fair-eyed king,<br> Like a fruitful branch he blossoms,<br>   Transplanted to a spring.</p>  
 <p>Thy Torah has his worship,<br>   He runs, to taste its charms,<br> Before Thee like a warrior,<br>   Accoutred in his arms.</p>  
 <p>I day by day am waiting<br>   Salvations promised day,<br> Enquiring how and whence it<br>   Will come to be my stay.</p>  
 <p>Restore the tortured People<br>   To the friend of her youth divine,<br> And bring the two together<br>   To the house of joy and wine.</p>
