 <p><a name="page_8"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 8</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">5</h3>  <h3 align="center">THREE THINGS CONSPIRE</h3>  
 <p>Three things conspire together in mine eyes<br> To bring the remembrance of Thee ever before me,<br> And I possess them as faithful witnesses:<br> Thy heavens, for whose sake I recall Thy name,<br> The earth I live on, that rouseth my thought<br> With its expanse which recalleth the expander of my pedestal,<br> And the musing of my heart when I look within the depths of myself.<br> Bless the Lord, O my soul, for ever and aye!</p>
