 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Selected Religious Poems of Solomon ibn Gabirol</i>, tr. by Israel Zangwill, [1923], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h4 align="center">VI.</h4>  
 <p>Thou art mighty and there is none among all Thou hast formed and created who can emulate Thy deeds and Thy power.<br> Thou art mighty, and Thine is the completed power beyond change or alteration.<br> Thou art mighty, and from the abundance of Thy might dost Thou pardon in the time of Thy wrath<br> And forbearest long with sinners. <a name="page_86"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 86</font></a><br> Thou art mighty, and Thy mercies are upon all Thy creatures, yea upon all of them.<br> "These are the mighty deeds which are from eternity."</p>
