 <p><a name="page_70"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 70</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">42</h3>  <h3 align="center">LOOK UP TO THY MAKER</h3>  
 <p>Look up to thy Maker, O soul of mine,<br>   Thy Creator remember whilst thou art young;<br> Cry morning and night to His grace divine,<br>   And in all thy songs let His name be sung.</p>  
 <p>On earth the Lord is thy portion and cup,<br>   And when from thy body thou goest lone,<br> A place for thy rest He hath builded up<br>   And made thee a nest underneath His throne.</p>  
 <p>Wherefore morning and night I will bless my Lord,<br> And from all that hath breath let His praise be poured.</p>
