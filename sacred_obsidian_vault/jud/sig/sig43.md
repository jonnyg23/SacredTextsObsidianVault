 <p><a name="page_68"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 68</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">40</h3>  <h3 align="center">FORGET THY AFFLICTION</h3>  
 <p>Forget thy affliction, and cease supplication,<br>   Recall thy release from Egyptian rod,<br> The hand is not short that hath laid earths foundation,<br>   Who stretched out the heavens remaineth thy God.</p>  
 <p>And at thy due season the glory that dwelleth<br>   In Zion shall rest on thy head that great day,<br> When moonlight as sunlight in radiance welleth<br>   And sunlight shall glow with a sevenfold ray.</p>
