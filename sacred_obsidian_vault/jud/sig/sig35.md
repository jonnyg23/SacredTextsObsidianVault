 <p><a name="page_51"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 51</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">32</h3>  <h3 align="center">LORD OF THE WORLD</h3>  
 <p>Lord of the world, O hear my psalm,<br>   And as sweet incense take my plea.<br>   My heart hath set its love on Thee<br> And finds in speech its only balm.</p>  
 <p>This thought forever haunts my mind,<br>   Some day to Thee I must return,<br>   From Thee I came and backward yearn<br> My very fount and source to find.</p>  
 <p>Not mine the merit that I stand<br>   Before Thee thus, since all is Thine,<br>   The glorious work of force divine,<br> No product of my heart or hand.</p>  
 <p>My soul to Thee was humbly bent<br>   Even before she had her birth,<br>   Before upon the sphere of earth<br> Her heavnly greatness made descent.</p>
