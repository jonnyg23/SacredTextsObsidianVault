 <p><a name="page_74"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 74</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">45</h3>  <h3 align="center">MY HEART CLAMOURS</h3>  
 <p>My heart craves to praise Thee,<br> But I am unable.<br> Would my understanding<br> Were as spacious as Solomons.<br> Without it my wisdom<br> As yet ill suffices<br> For expounding Thy wonders<br> And Thy deeds of beneficence<br> Wrought for me and all mankind.<br> Without Thee alls hopeless,<br> And where is the rock<br> Sustaining, suspending<br> The weight of the world?<br> I am as one orphaned;<br> Nay, on Thee I am cast.<br> What then can I do<br> But look to Thee, wait on Thee,<br> In whose hand is the spirit<br> Of all that is living,<br> In whose hand is the breath<br> Of all the creation?</p>
