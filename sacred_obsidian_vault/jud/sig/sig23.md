 <p><a name="page_28"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 28</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">20</h3>  <h3 align="center">DUOLOGUE</h3>  
 <p><i>God:</i></p>  
 <p>"Daughter of Zion, tried in Sorrows furnace,<br>   Een as I swore thy fathers, be at rest.<br> I swore it for My sake, and now thy crying<br>   Hath mounted to My habitation blest,<br>   And I have heard, for gracious is My breast."</p>  
 <p><i>Israel:</i></p>  
 <p>"Obeisance low I made, for I am feeble,<br>   Thy kindliness responds to all who yearn.<br> Come back, dear Lord, whose name is linked with pardon,<br>   No other saviour Israel can discern,<br>   Unto his myriad families return!"</p>  
 <p><i>God:</i></p>  
 <p>"Whereer thy origin, whosoeer thy master,<br>   A man shall comenay, Ithy cause to plead,<br> Whoever holds the bill of thy divorcement.<br>   Like wall or tower of fire I guard thy seed,<br>   Then wherefore weep or heart affrighted heed?"</p>  
 <p><a name="page_29"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 29</font></a></p>  
 <p><i>Israel:</i></p>  
 <p>"Why do I weep? Because Thou keepest silence,<br>   Though violence rages and, all uncontrolled,<br> The mob destroys, and we as slaves to strangers,<br>   Master and man together, have been sold,<br>   And no Redeemer do our eyes behold."</p>  
 <p><i>God:</i></p>  
 <p>"Who art thou thus to shrink from man in terror<br>   And be dismayed because of mankinds scorn?<br> My angel I will send, as wrote the prophet,<br>   And gather Israel winnowed and new-born:<br>   This miracle shall be to-morrow morn."</p>  
 <p><i>Israel:</i></p>  
 <p>"To gather me my chieftains Thou didst promise,<br>   The day comes not and miracle is none,<br> Nor see I Temple built nor any herald<br>   Of Peace arrive to be my Holy One<br>   Ah, wherefore lingers Jesses promised son?"</p>  
 <p><i>God:</i></p>  
 <p>"Behold, I keep the oath I swore to gather<br>   My captiveskings shall bring their gifts to thee;<br> Created for a witness to the nations,<br>   My holy ones shall testify to Me<br>   Yea, Jesses son Mine eyes already see."</p>
