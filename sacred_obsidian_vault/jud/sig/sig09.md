 <p><a name="page_9"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 9</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">6</h3>  <h3 align="center">BEFORE MY KING</h3>  
 <p>In prayer prone before my King,<br>   I bend to Him my face and knee,<br>   My heart His sacrifice shall be,<br> My tear His liquid offering.</p>  
 <p>In waiting for the suns caress,<br>   In watching for the morning light<br>   To scatter all my godless night,<br> My soul consumes in weariness.</p>  
 <p>Though He delays, shall I not start<br>   To seek His face? Nay, of a sooth<br>   I yet shall find his word of ruth<br> Bring comfort to my bitter heart.</p>  
 <p>The promise Zechariah gave,<br>   How sweet it tastes in this our woe!<br>   My soul shall bid my heart to know<br> I trust the living God to save.</p>
