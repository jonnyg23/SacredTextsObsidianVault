 <p><a name="page_69"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 69</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">41</h3>  <h3 align="center">TO MY SOUL</h3>  
 <p>Be wise, my precious soul, and haste<br>   To bow to God in reverence.<br> Let vanities no more be chased,<br> Bethink thee ere this world lies waste,<br>   The world that waits thee going hence.</p>  
 <p>Thy life to Gods life is akin,<br>   Concealed like His beneath a veil,<br> Since He is free of flaw or sin,<br> Like purity thou too canst win,<br>   To reach perfection wherefore fail?</p>  
 <p>And as His arm upholds the sky,<br>   Do thou thy dumb brute body lift,<br> Thou, soul, to which we can descry<br> No like on earthO magnify<br>   The God of whom thou art the gift.</p>  
 <p>Greet then, my soul, thy Rock with praise,<br>   Hail him, my inmost heart, with song<br> Unceasingly throughout my days,<br> And let all souls their voices raise<br>   My benediction to prolong.</p>
