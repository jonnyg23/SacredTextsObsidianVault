 <p><a name="page_46"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 46</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">29</h3>  <h3 align="center">FOR THE DAY OF MEMORIAL</h3>  
 <p>The seeker of good shall acceptance find<br>   From the God whose glory is boundless,<br> If he turn unto Him with repentant mind,<br> And sackcloth on both of his shoulders bind<br>         By way of memorial.</p>  
 <p>So come and return to our God on high<br>   Who fashioned the uttermost heavens,<br> Let your songs of praise to His footstool fly<br> And thank Him to-night in a choral cry<br>         By way of memorial.</p>  
 <p>O King of the Kingdom that hails Thy name<br>   Since first to the void Thou spakest,<br> Evoking the light that from darkness came,<br> Accept this plea to expunge my shame<br>         As rite of memorial.</p>  
 <p>Prepare, O Israel, to meet Thy God,<br>   Let every man seek to find ransom,<br> Remove the evil at which ye nod,<br> Cleanse ye and wash ye or dread His rod<br>         This day of memorial.</p>
