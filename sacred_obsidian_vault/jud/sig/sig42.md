 <p><a name="page_67"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 67</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">39</h3>  <h3 align="center">ASK OF ME</h3>  
 <p>"Ask of Me, beautiful mouth,<br>   What dost thou ask of Me?<br> For thy suppliant cry<br> Hath ascended on high<br>   Inclining My ear to thy plea."</p>  
 <p>"First with the lion we met,<br>   Next came the leopards leap,<br> We were fain to take flight<br> From our gardens delight<br>   And into a hiding-place creep.</p>  
 <p>"Hardly these creatures had passed,<br>   Sated with Judahs spoil,<br> Than the wild ass we feared<br> Out of midnight appeared<br>   To trample and dwell on our soil.</p>  
 <p>"Ishmaels offspring command<br>   Back to his Arab land,<br> As his mother of old<br> To her mistress was told<br>   To return and submit to her hand."</p>
