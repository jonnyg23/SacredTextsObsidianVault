 <p><a name="page_14"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 14</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">11</h3>  <h3 align="center">MY REFUGE</h3>  
 <p>I have made Thee my refuge, my terror and trembling,<br>   And when straitly besieged I have made Thee my tower,<br> When to left and to right I have sought for a helper,<br>   I could look for dear life to no aid but Thy power.<br> More than all earthly treasure I have made Thee my portion,<br>   Through all cares the delight and desire of my days,<br> In the flood of Thy love I have rapture eternal<br>   And prayer is but an occasion for praise.</p>
