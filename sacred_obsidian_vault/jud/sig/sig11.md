 <p><a name="page_11"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 11</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">8</h3>  <h3 align="center">POUR OUT THY HEART</h3>  
 <p>Pour out thy heart to the Rock,<br>   Pour out thy inmost soul<br> To the stronghold naught can shock,<br>   As the mornings and evenings roll.</p>  
 <p>To Him who around and before<br>   Is, whether thou rest or roam,<br> To Him let thy thoughts upsoar,<br>   Be thou on the road or at home.</p>  
 <p>Thus tested by praise and belief,<br>   Thou favour divine shalt gain,<br> He will turn His ear to thy grief,<br>   He will bend His eye on thy pain.</p>  
 <p>Behold, He will pay thy reward,<br>   Thou shalt share the abode of the blest,<br> For the day thou return to the Lord,<br>   He will draw thee close to His breast.</p>
