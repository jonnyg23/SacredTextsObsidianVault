 <p>Thou art great, and compared with Thy greatness all greatness is humbled and all excess diminished.<br> Incalculably great is Thy being,<br> Superber than the starry heaven,<br> Beyond and above all grandeur,<br> "And exalted beyond all blessing and praise."</p>
