 <p><a name="page_13"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 13</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">10</h3>  <h3 align="center">TIS JOY TO ME</h3>  
 <p>Tis joy to me to dwell in Thee,<br>   At thought of Thee all grief retreats,<br> Thy mercies call for thanks, but all<br>   I have to pay are tongues conceits.</p>  
 <p>Not heavens height can bound Thy might,<br>   How then shall thought due praise assign?<br> Teach me, and bless with righteousness,<br>   And let my will but further Thine.</p>  
 <p>The praise I bring as offering<br>   Accept in lieu of sacrifice,<br> My service call memorial,<br>   Pleased with Thy worshippers device.</p>  
 <p>Let Thy clear eye, O Lord, descry<br>   How wretched are my fears and hopes,<br> And send Thy light to chase the night<br>   In which my blinded spirit gropes.</p>  
 <p>In kindness great, compassionate,<br>   O guard for me Thy tenderness,<br> Within its wideness let me hide<br>   The vastness of my trespasses.</p>  
 <p>And as Thy name to me became<br>   A treasure in my heart to stand,<br> So let to Thee my spirit be<br>   A treasure held within Thy hand.</p>
