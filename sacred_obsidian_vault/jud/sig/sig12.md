 <p><a name="page_12"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 12</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">9</h3>  <h3 align="center">SIX YEARS WERE DECREED</h3>  
 <p>Six years were decreed for a slave to wait<br>   When his freedom he sought at his masters hand,<br> But the years of my bondage lack term or date,<br>   It is hard, O my Master, to understand.</p>  
 <p>Why, Sire, should a hand-maids son bear sway,<br>   And me with affliction and anguish task?<br> There cometh no answer, howeer I pray,<br>   In despite that each day for reply I ask.</p>  
 <p>What word at the last wilt Thou say, my King?<br>   An Thou findest no ransom, O Lord, take me!<br> Take me for Thy people as offering,<br>   I will serve Thee for ever and neer go free.</p>
