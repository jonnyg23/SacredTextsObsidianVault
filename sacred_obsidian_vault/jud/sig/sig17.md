 <p><a name="page_17"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 17</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">14</h3>  <h3 align="center">HUMBLE OF SPIRIT</h3>  
 <p>Humble of spirit, lowly of knee and stature,<br> But in fear and awe abounding,<br> I come before Thee.<br> And in Thy presence to myself appear<br> As a little earth-worm.<br> O Thou, who fillest the earth and whose greatness is endless,<br> Shall one like me laud Thee,<br> And how shall he honour Thee?<br> The angels of heaven do not suffice,<br> How then one like me?<br> Thou hast wrought good and hast magnified mercies,<br> Wherefore the soul shall magnify praise of Thee.</p>
