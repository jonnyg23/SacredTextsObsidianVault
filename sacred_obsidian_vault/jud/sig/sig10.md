 <p><a name="page_10"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 10</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">7</h3>  <h3 align="center">OPEN THE GATE</h3>  
 <p>Open the gate, my love,<br>   Arise and open the gate,<br> For my soul is dismayed<br> And sorely afraid<br>   And Hagars brood mocks my estate.</p>  
 <p>The heart of the hand-maids sons<br>   Is hateful and haughty grown,<br> And all because of the cry<br> Of Ishmael piercing the sky,<br>   Ascending and reaching the Throne.</p>  
 <p>I stumble twixt beast and beast,<br>   The wild ass swift to slay<br> Has followed my flight<br> From the courts of Night<br>   Where crushed of the boar I lay.</p>  
 <p>Alas! for my thick-sealed fate,<br>   Ah woe for the days to come!<br> It helps but to pain me<br> That none can explain me,<br>   And I, myself, I am dumb.</p>
