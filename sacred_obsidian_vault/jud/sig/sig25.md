 <p><a name="page_35"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 35</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">22</h3>  <h3 align="center">JUDGMENT</h3>  
 <p>My breast I am smiting,<br> My own sins indicting.<br>   How then canst Thou draw me<br>   To strife and thus awe me,<br> And bring Me to judgment?</p>  
 <p>My branch hangeth ailing,<br> My eyelid is failing,<br>   My aims to derision<br>   Are turned by the vision<br> Of Thee bringing judgment.</p>  
 <p>The creditor calleth,<br> The dread decree falleth,<br>   The awful day breaking<br>   Gods creatures sets quaking<br> In fear of His judgment.</p>  
 <p>Through Thy attributes preaching,<br> Almighty, and teaching,<br>   O weigh aberration<br>   In the scale of salvation,<br> Nor bring us to judgment.</p>  
 <p>In Thy merciful fashion<br> Award us compassion,<br>   That man who but dust is<br>   May handle with justice<br> The haters of judgment.</p>  
 <p><a name="page_36"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 36</font></a></p>  
 <p>Like a vapour evanished,<br> Man is melted and banished,<br>   His birth is coëval<br>   With a harvest of evil,<br> Tis Thou must bring judgment.</p>  
 <p>We awaitO behold us<br> Thy love to enfold us.<br>   Did Thy warning not hasten<br>   Our impulse to chasten?<br> For the Lord loveth judgment.</p>
