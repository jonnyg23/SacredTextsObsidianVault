 <p>
 Jewish folklore is full of vivid stories that both entertain and teach.
 This book is based on tales from the <a href="../talmud.htm">Talmud</a> and
 <a href="../index.htm#midrash">Midrash</a>.
 While some of the themes are familiar from fairy-tales and folklore
 from other traditions, the stories in this collection are infused with
 the perennial Jewish
 struggle for survival and dignity, as well as a large helping of gentle humor.
 </p>
