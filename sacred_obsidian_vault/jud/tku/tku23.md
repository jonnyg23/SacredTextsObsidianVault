 <h1 align="CENTER">CHAPTER XV.</h1>
 <h3 align="CENTER">CONCERNING THE FOURTH PART OF THE BEARD OF MACROPROSOPUS.</h3>
 <p>378. THE hair is disposed in the fourth conformation, and it descendeth beneath the mouth from the one side even unto the other side.</p>
 <p>379. That is intimated (in the saying of Micah) in these words: "Of the remnant of his heritage:" LShAIRITh NChLThV, <i>Lishairith Nachalatho</i>.</p>
 <p>380. Like as it is said in 2 Kings xix. 4: "And thou shalt lift up thy prayer for the remnant that is left." Where every part that is found truly remaining is called the remnant.</p>
 <p>381. For it is written, Zeph. iii. 13: "The remnant of Israel, ShARITh IShRAL, <i>Sharith Israel</i>, shall not do iniquity."</p>
