 <h1 align="CENTER">CHAPTER XX.</h1>
 <h3 align="CENTER">CONCERNING THE BODY OF MICROPROSOPUS.</h3>
 <p>709. THE masculine power is extended through Däath; and the Assemblies and Conclaves are filled.</p>
 <p>710. It commenceth from the beginning of the skull, and it is extended throughout the whole body, through the breast, and through the arms, and through all the other parts.</p>
