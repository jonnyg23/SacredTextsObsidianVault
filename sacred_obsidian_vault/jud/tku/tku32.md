 <h1 align="CENTER">CHAPTER XXIV.</h1>
 <h3 align="CENTER">CONCLUSION OF THE MATTER CONCERNING MACROPROSOPUS.</h3>
 <p>494. RABBI SCHIMEON spake unto his companions, and said: "When that veil is expanded (<i>by which is to he understood the representation of the beard of Macroprosopus</i>) which ye behold above us, <a name="fr_78"></a><a href="#fn_77"><font size="1">1</font></a> I see that all the conformations have descended therein, and that they shine forth in that place. (<i>Now like as if he intended to say, AMN, Amen, the discourse concerning Macroprosopus being finished, he describeth this particular symbolism, which is contained in the ensuing symbols</i>.)</p>
 <p>495. "And a certain covering, even the splendour of the most holy and blessed God (<i>otherwise the opening of holiness; but by this is understood the Tetragrammaton, which, together with the name, ADNI, Adonai, maketh the number of the word AMN, Amen, that is 91</i>): is expanded through four columns on four sides (<i>which are the four letters of the holy name, by which he saith that space is surrounded</i>).</p>
 <p>496. "One column is so placed that it reacheth from the lowest unto the highest. (<i>This is the Kingdom of the emanations</i>, <a name="fr_79"></a><a href="#fn_78"><font size="1">2</font></a> <i>the base and lowest Part of the whole system</i> <a name="fr_80"></a><a href="#fn_79"><font size="1">3</font></a> <i>of emanation, because it is said to ascend from the lowest part of the middle column</i> <a name="fr_81"></a><a href="#fn_80"><font size="1">4</font></a> <i>even unto the summit of the Crown</i>. <a name="fr_82"></a><a href="#fn_81"><font size="1">5</font></a>)</p>
 <p>497. "And therein is a certain MGRVPIA, <a name="fr_83"></a><a href="#fn_82"><font size="1">6</font></a> <i>Megerophia</i>, vessel containing fire (<i>for like as the fire an the altar could not be touched with bare hands, so that</i></p>

But the four letters of the name ADNI, Adonai, are hereby to be understood, which are inserted into and united with the four letters IHVH, in this manner, IAHDVNHI</i>); which seize upon that. veil, and withdraw it from the superiors.</p>
 <p>498. "And thus in the second column <a name="fr_85"></a><a href="#fn_84"><font size="1">2</font></a> and the third column and the fourth column (<i>that is, the four letters are applied to the other four letters, as hath just been said</i>).</p>
