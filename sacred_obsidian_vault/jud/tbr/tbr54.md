 <p><a name="page_72"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 72</font></a></p>  <h4 align="center">If the Benediction after a Meal has been forgotten.</h4>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><b>M</b>.</font></span>VIII. 8 (7). In the case of him who has eaten, and has forgotten, and not said the Benediction—the School of Shammai say: Let him return to his place <a name="fr_435"></a><a href="#fn_434"><font size="1">1</font></a> and say it. The School of Hillel say: Let him say it in the place where he remembers it. <a name="fr_436"></a><a href="#fn_435"><font size="1">2</font></a> How long after may he say the Benediction? Until the food in his bowels is consumed. <a name="fr_437"></a><a href="#fn_436"><font size="1">3</font></a></p>
