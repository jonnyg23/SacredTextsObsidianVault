 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Tractate Sanhedrin</i>, Herbert Danby tr. [1919], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">13. Who curses His Parents.</h3>  
 <p>8<i>b</i>. He who curses his father or mother <a name="fr_324"></a><a href="#fn_321"><font size="1">2</font></a> is not guilty until he curse them by the Name; if he curse them under a pseudonym <a name="fr_325"></a><a href="#fn_322"><font size="1">3</font></a> R. Meir would hold him guilty, but the majority innocent.</p>
