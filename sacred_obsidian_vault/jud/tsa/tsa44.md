 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Tractate Sanhedrin</i>, Herbert Danby tr. [1919], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">(5) He who prophesies in the Name of a False God.</h3>  
 <p><span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><b>M</b>.</font></span>XI. 6<i>a</i>. He who prophesies in the name of a false god, <a name="fr_504"></a><a href="#fn_497"><font size="1">1</font></a> is he who says: "Thus saith the god." Such a one is guilty even though his message confirm the <i>halaka</i> concerning unclean and clean things.</p>
