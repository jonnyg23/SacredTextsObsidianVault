 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Tractate Sanhedrin</i>, Herbert Danby tr. [1919], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">14. The Seducer of a Betrothed Damsel.</h3>  
 <p>9. He who has criminal connexion with a betrothed damsel <a name="fr_326"></a><a href="#fn_323"><font size="1">4</font></a> is guilty only if she be a virgin, betrothed, and in her father's house. If two have criminal connexion with her, the first is to be stoned and the second strangled.</p>  
 <p></p><dir><b>T</b>. X. 8. He who has criminal connexion with a betrothed damsel is guilty only if she be a damsel, a virgin, betrothed, and in her father's house. If she be a betrothed damsel and in her husband's house, or a betrothed woman of marriageable age <a name="fr_327"></a><a href="#fn_324"><font size="1">5</font></a> in her father's house, (9) or a married woman in</dir>

 </p>

 <p><span class="margnote"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><b>T</b>.</font></span></p><dir>either her father's or her husband's house, whoever has criminal connexion with her is to be strangled. <a name="fr_328"></a><a href="#fn_325"><font size="1">1</font></a> If ten have had criminal connexion with her at a time when she is still a virgin, all are to be stoned; if she be not a virgin the first is to be stoned and the others strangled.  
 <p>10. If, however, she have received her husband in her father's house, then, although she be still a virgin, one who has criminal connexion with her is to be strangled. <a name="fr_329"></a><a href="#fn_325"><font size="1">1</font></a></p>  
 <p>A betrothed damsel who has committed adultery is to be stoned at the door of her father's house. If there is no door to her father's house, she is to be stoned where she committed adultery. And if it be in a heathen city, she is to be stoned at the door of the court.</p></dir>
