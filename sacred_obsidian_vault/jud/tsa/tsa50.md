There were no page numbers in this section; the page numbers have been supplied for technical reasons.--JBH.)</font></p>  <h4 align="center">TRANSLATIONS OF EARLY DOCUMENTS</h4>  
 <p align="center">A<i> Series of texts important for the study of Christian origins, by various authors</i></p>  
 <p align="center">UNDER THE JOINT EDITORSHIP OF</p>  
 <p align="center">The Rev. W. O. E. OESTERLEY, D.D.</p>  
 <p align="center">AND</p>  
 <p align="center">The Rev. CANON G. H. BOX, M.A.</p>  
 <p>THE object of the Series is to provide short, cheap, and handy text-books for students, either working by themselves or in classes. The aim is to furnish in translations important texts unencumbered by commentary or elaborate notes, which can be had in larger works.</p>
