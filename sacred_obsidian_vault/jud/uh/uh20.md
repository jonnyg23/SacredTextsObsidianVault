 <img src="img/school.jpg"></p>  <h3 align="center">A Madrigal of Numbers</h3>  
 <p><small>The leader asks the questions. The whole company responds, each reading as fast as possible, in the effort to finish the answer first.</small></p>  
 <p>Who knows One?</p>  
 <p>I know One: One is the God of the World.</p>  
 <p>Who knows Two?</p>  
 <p>I know Two: Two Tables of the Covenant. One God of the World.</p>  
 <p>Who knows Three?</p>  
 <p>I know Three: Three Patriarchs; Two Tables of the Covenant; One God of the World.</p>  
 <p>Who knows Four?</p>  
 <p>I know Four: Four Mothers of Israel; Three Patriarchs; Two Tables of the Covenant; One God of the World.</p>

 <p>Who knows Five?</p>  
 <p>I know Five: Five Books of Moses; Four Mothers of Israel; Three Patriarchs; Two Tables of the Covenant; One God of the World.</p>  
 <p>Who knows Six?</p>  
 <p>I know Six: Six Days of Creation; Five Books of Moses; Four Mothers of Israel; Three Patriarchs; Two Tables of the Covenant; One God of the World.</p>  
 <p>Who knows Seven?</p>  
 <p>I know Seven: Seven Days of the Week; Six Days of Creation; Five Books of Moses; Four Mothers of Israel; Three Patriarchs; Two Tables of the Covenant; One God of the World.</p>  
 <p>Who knows Eight?</p>  
 <p>I know Eight: Eight Lights of Ḥanukkah; Seven Days of the Week; Six Days of Creation; Five Books of Moses; Four Mothers of Israel; Three Patriarchs; Two Tables of the Covenant; One God of the World.</p>

 <p>Who knows Ten?</p>  
 <p>I know Ten: Ten Commandments; Nine Festivals; Eight Lights of Ḥanukkah; Seven Days of the Week; Six Days of Creation; Five Books of Moses; Four Mothers of Israel; Three Patriarchs; Two Tables of the Covenant; One God of the World.</p>  
 <p>Who knows Eleven?</p>  
 <p>I know Eleven: Eleven Stars in Joseph's Dream; Ten Commandments; Nine Festivals; Eight Lights of Ḥanukkah; Seven Days of the Week; Six Days of Creation; Five Books of Moses; Four Mothers of Israel; Three Patriarchs; Two Tables of the Covenant; One God of the World.</p>  
 <p>Who knows Twelve?</p>  
 <p>I know Twelve: Twelve Tribes; Eleven Stars; Ten Commandments; Nine Festivals; Eight Lights of Ḥanukkah; Seven Days of the Week; Six Days of Creation; Five Books of Moses; Four Mothers of Israel; Three Patriarchs; Two Tables of the Covenant; One God of the World.</p>
