 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Union Haggadah</i>, ed. by The Central Council of American Rabbis [1923], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">Order of the Service</h3>  
 <p><a name="page_xvi"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. xvi</font></a></p>  <div align="center">  <table border="0">  <tr>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>1. Recite the Kiddush (Sanctification of the festival).</p>   </td>   <td valign="bottom" align="bottom">   
 <p align="right">‏קַדֵּשׁ‎</p>   </td>  </tr>  <tr>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>2. Partake of parsley dipped in salt water.</p>   </td>   <td valign="bottom" align="bottom">   
 <p align="right">‏כַּרְפַּס‎</p>   </td>  </tr>  <tr>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>3. Break the middle Matzo, and hide one part to be eaten at the end of the   meal as the Aphikomon.</p>   </td>   <td valign="bottom" align="bottom">   
 <p align="right">‏יַֽחַץ‎</p>   </td>  </tr>  <tr>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>4. Tell the story of Israel's deliverance from Egyptian bondage.</p>   </td>   <td valign="bottom" align="bottom">   
 <p align="right">‏מַגִּיר‎</p>   </td>  </tr>  <tr>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>5. Recite the blessing before the meal, including the special blessing   over Matzo.</p>   </td>   <td valign="bottom" align="bottom">   
 <p align="right">‏מוֹצִיא, מַצָּה‎</p>   </td>  </tr>  <tr>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>6. Combine Matzo, Moror and Ḥaroses and eat them together.</p>   </td>   <td valign="bottom" align="bottom">   
 <p align="right">‏כּוֹרֵךְ, מָרוֹר‎</p>   </td>  </tr>  <tr>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>7. Partake of the festival meal.</p>   </td>   <td valign="bottom" align="bottom">   
 <p align="right">‏שֻׁלְחָן עוֹרֵךְ‎</p>   </td>  </tr>  <tr>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>8. Conclude the meal by eating the Aphikomon.</p>   </td>   <td valign="bottom" align="bottom">   
 <p align="right">‏צָפוּן‎</p>   </td>  </tr>  <tr>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>9. Say grace after the meal.</p>   </td>   <td valign="bottom" align="bottom">   
 <p align="right">‏בָּרֵךְ‎</p>   </td>  </tr>  <tr>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>10. Recite the remainder of the Hallel.</p>   </td>   <td valign="bottom" align="bottom">   
 <p align="right">‏הַלֵּל‎</p>   </td>  </tr>  <tr>   <td valign="top" align="top">   
 <p>11. End with a prayer for the acceptance of the service.</p>   </td>   <td valign="bottom" align="bottom">   
 <p align="right">‏נִרְצָה‎</p>   </td>  </tr> </table>  </div>
