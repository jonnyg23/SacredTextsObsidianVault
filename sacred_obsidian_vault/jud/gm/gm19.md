 <p><a name="page_114"><font size="1" color="green">p. 114</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">PRAYER</h3>  
 <p><span style="font-variant:small-caps;">Once</span> Rabbi Israel passed through a house of prayer. An old Jew sat there huddled over a book, reading in a hasty mumble, reading faster and faster, hour after hour.</p>  
 <p>Rabbi Israel said, "He is so absorbed in his learning that he has forgotten there is a God over the world."</p>
