 <p align="center">_________</p>  <h1 align="center">XLIII. TREATISE BECHOROTH.</h1>  
 <p>[Relates to the firstborn of human beings, and of animals. Laws founded on Exod. xiii. 12, 13; and Num. xviii. 15–18.]</p>  
 <p align="center">_________</p>  <h1 align="center">XLIV. TREATISE ERACHIN,</h1>  <h6 align="center">VALUATIONS.</h6>  
 <p>[Contains laws relating to objects consecrated to Divine worship, founded on Lev. xxvii. 2; and also, to vows, founded on Lev. xxvii. 28.]</p>  
 <p align="center">_________</p>  <h1 align="center">XLV. TREATISE TAMURAH,</h1>  <h6 align="center">SUBSTITUTION.</h6>  
 <p>[Contains laws relating to consecrated animals that have had others substituted in their stead, founded on Lev. xxiv. 10 and 33.]</p>  
 <p align="center">_________</p>  <h1 align="center">XLVI. TREATISE KERITOTH,</h1>  <h6 align="center">EXCISIONS.</h6>  
 <p>[Relates to offences which, if wantonly committed, are punished by excision, but which, if inadvertently committed, entail the obligation to bring sin or trespass offerings.]</p>  
 <p><a name="page_355"><font size="1" color="green">p. 355</font></a></p>  <h1 align="center">XLVII. TREATISE MEHILAH,</h1>  <h6 align="center">TRESPASS.</h6>  
 <p>[Contains laws relating to objects that have been consecrated and converted to profane uses, founded on Num. v. 6–8.]</p>  
 <p align="center">_________</p>  <h1 align="center">XLVIII. TREATISE TAMID,</h1>  <h6 align="center">CONTINUAL OFFERINGS.</h6>  
 <p>[Contains laws relating to the daily sacrifices, founded on Exod. xxxiv. 38–42; and on Num. xxviii. 3.]</p>  
 <p align="center">_________</p>  <h1 align="center">XLIX. TREATISE MIDDOTH,</h1>  <h6 align="center">MEASUREMENTS.</h6>  
 <p>[Alludes to the size and dimensions of the Temple, and its various parts; related by an eye-witness, R. Eleazar ben Jacob, assisted by Abbah Saul.]</p>  
 <p align="center">_________</p>  <h1 align="center">L. TREATISE KANIM.</h1>  
 <p align="center">[Relates to birds for sacrifices.]</p>  
 <p>Which closes Seder Kedoshim.</p>  
 <p align="center">=========================</p>  <h1 align="center">‏סדר טהרות‎</h1>  <h6 align="center">LEGAL PURIFICATIONS; CONTAINS</h6>  <h1 align="center">LI. TREATISE KELIM.</h1>  
 <p>[Laws relating to things liable to contract and to communicate uncleanness, founded on Lev. xi. 11–32; and Num. xix. 14, and xxxi. 20.]</p>  
 <p align="center">_________</p>  <h1 align="center">LXII. TREATISE OHOLOTH.</h1>  
 <p>[Contains laws relating to uncleanness arising from a corpse, founded on Num. xix. 14.]</p>  
 <p><a name="page_356"><font size="1" color="green">p. 356</font></a></p>  
 <p align="center">_________</p>  <h1 align="center">LIII. TREATISE NEGAIM.</h1>  
 <p>[Contains laws relating to uncleanness arising from leprosy, and other contagious diseases, founded on Lev. xiii. and xiv.]</p>  
 <p align="center">_________</p>  <h1 align="center">LIV. TREATISE PARAH.</h1>  
 <p align="center">[Has laws relating to the red heifer, founded on Num. xix.]</p>  
 <p align="center">_________</p>  <h1 align="center">LV. TREATISE TAHAROTH.</h1>  
 <p align="center">[Relates to minor impurities, according to their various degrees.]</p>  
 <p align="center">_________</p>  <h1 align="center">LVI. TREATISE MIKVAOTH.</h1>  
 <p>[Contains laws that relate to diving baths for the cleansing of persons and utensils, founded on Num. xxxi. 23.]</p>  
 <p align="center">_________</p>  <h1 align="center">LVII. TREATISE NIDDAH.</h1>  
 <p>[Contains precepts regulating the conduct of, and intercourse with, women, during their menses, after child-birth, and generally at their periods of separation [‏נדה‎] and uncleanness, founded on Lev. xii. 1–6; xxv. 19–31.]</p>  
 <p align="center">_________</p>  <h1 align="center">LVIII. TREATISE MAKSHEERIN.</h1>  
 <p>[Relates to the laws and rules of purification, founded on Lev. xi. 36–38.]</p>  
 <p align="center">_________</p>  <h1 align="center">LIX. TREATISE ZABIM.</h1>  
 <p align="center">[Has laws relating to fluxes, founded on Lev. xv.]</p>  
 <p align="center">_________</p>  <h1 align="center">LX. TREATISE TEBUL YOM.</h1>  
 <p>[Relates to purifications by ablution on the day the uncleanness has been contracted. Laws founded on Lev. xvii. 15, and xxii. 6, 7.]</p>
