 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>Eighteen Treatises from the Mishna</i>, by D. A. Sola and M. J. Raphall, [1843], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h1 align="center">V. TREATISE SHEBIITH.</h1>  
 <p>[Treats of laws relating to the Seventh Sabbatical year, founded on Exod. xxiii. 10, Lev. xxv. 1, and Deut. xv. 1.]</p>  <h1 align="center">VI. TREATISE TEROOMOTH.</h1>  
 <p>[Contains laws founded on Num. xviii. 8, relating to the heave-offering, consecrated to the use of the priesthood].</p>  <h1 align="center">VII. TREATISE MAASEROTH.</h1>  
 <p>[Contains laws relating to the first tithes, founded on Lev. xxvii. 30, and Num. xviii. 28].</p>  <h1 align="center">VIII. TREATISE MAASAR SHENI.</h1>  
 <p>[Contains laws relating to the second tithes, founded on Deut. xiv. 22, and xxvi. 14].</p>  <h1 align="center">IX. TREATISE CHALAH.</h1>  
 <p>[Contains laws relating to the first dough, founded on Num. xv. 20.]</p>  
 <p><a name="page_33"><font size="1" color="green">p. 33</font></a></p>  <h1 align="center">X. TREATISE ORLAH.</h1>  
 <p>[Relates to uncircumcised trees, i.e., trees newly planted, the fruits of which must not be eaten during the first three years, and which are consecrated during the fourth. The rules and regulations on this subject are founded on Lev. xix. 23.]</p>  <h1 align="center">XI. TREATISE BIKOORIM.</h1>  
 <p>[Contains laws relating to first-fruits, founded on Exod. xxiii. 19, and Deut. xxvi. 1.]</p>  
 <p>Which closes the first Seder Zeraim [of seeds] containing Agrarian Laws.</p>
