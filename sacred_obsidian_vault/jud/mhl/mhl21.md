 <h1 align="CENTER">BIBLIOGRAPHY</h1>
 <p>For the general history of the Hebrew race during the Middle Ages, the fullest and most satisfactory work is the six-volume work:</p><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <p>HEINRICH GRAETZ, "History of the Jews" (Jewish Publication Society, Philadelphia, 1891-1898).</p>
 <p>H. H. MILMAN, "History of the Jews" (new edition, New York, 1909).</p></dir>
 </dir>
 
 <p>Shorter treatises of value include:</p><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <p>I. ABRAHAMS, "Chapters on Jewish Literature" (Jewish Publication Society, Philadelphia, 1899).</p>
 <p>1. ABRAHAMS, "Short History of Jewish Literature" (London, 1906).</p>
 <p>J. ABELSON, "Jewish Mysticism" (London, 1913).</p>
 <p>W. OESTERLEY and G. BOX, "Religion and Worship of the Synagogue" (London, 1907).</p>
 <p>LADY MAGNUS "Outlines of Jewish History" (Philadelphia, 1890).</p>
 <p>I. ABRAHAMS, "Jewish Life in the Middle Ages" (London, 1896).</p></dir>
 </dir>
 
 <p>For the Kabbalah, the reader is recommended to:</p><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <p>A. E. WAITE, "Doctrine and Literature of the Kabalah" (London, 1902).</p>
 <p>B. PICK, "The Cabala, Its Influence on Judaism and Christianity" (Chicago, 1913).</p>
 <p>F. HARTMANN, "Life of Paracelsus" (London).</p>
 <p>A. E. WAITE, "History of the Rosicrucians" (London).</p></dir>
 </dir>
 
 <p>For the texts themselves, the most interesting and valuable translations we have are:</p><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <p>S. RAPAPORT, "Tales and Maxims from the Midrash " (New York 1907).</p>
 <p>T. WRIGHT, "Early Travels in Palestine" (London, 1848).</p></dir>
 </dir>
 
 <p><a name="page_430"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 430</font></a></p><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <p>S. S. Wise (translator), IBN GABIROL'S "Improvement of the Moral Qualities" (Columbia University, 1901).</p>
 <p>H. GOLLANCZ, "The Ethical Treatises of Berachya" (London, 1902).</p>
 <p>S. L. MATHERS, "The Kabbalah Unveiled" (Boston, 1887).</p>
 <p>W. W. WESTCOTT, "The Sepher Yetzirah" (Bath, 1887).</p></dir>
 </dir>
 
 <p>See also:</p><dir>
 <dir>
 
 <p>The Publications of the Society of Hebrew Literature.</p>
 <p>The Jewish Quarterly Review.</p>
 <p>The Publications of the American Jewish Historical Society.</p>
