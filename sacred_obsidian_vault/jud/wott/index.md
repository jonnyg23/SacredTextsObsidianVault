 <p>
 This is a fairly up-to-date and highly readable introduction to the
 Talmud, the age-old storehouse of Jewish wisdom.
 Bokser covers the long history of the Talmud, from its origin
 in the Babylonian exile, its growth through the five centuries after the
 Roman destruction of the Temple, and the later persecution of the Talmud.
 The book covers a number of high-level topics, including social
 ethics and personal morality, with numerous examples from the Talmud.
 <small>--J.B. Hare, 1/3/2009.</small>
 </p>
