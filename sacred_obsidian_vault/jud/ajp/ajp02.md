 <p>D<sup>s</sup> = <i>Zur rabbinischen Spruchkunde.</i></p>  
 <p>The number refers to the number of the proverb in these collections.</p>  
 <p>The asterisk denotes that the proverb is specifically cited as such in <i>one</i> or <i>more</i> of the references that are given.</p>
