 <p><a name="page_57"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 57</font></a></p>  <h1 align="center">GLOSSARY OF RABBINICAL WORDS.</h1>  
 <p align="center"><b>‏א‎</b></p>  
 <p>‏אָדַק‎ <i>v</i>. To adhere, cohere. VI, 8.</p>  
 <p>‏אַדֵּר‎ <i>n</i>. [Syriac ‏אאר,‎ Greek ἀήρ] Air. II.</p>  
 <p>‏אוֹת‎ <i>n</i>. Sign, letter; ‏אוֹתִיּוֹת, יסוֹד‎ fundamental letters. I, 1.</p>  
 <p>‏אֵילָךְ‎ <i>adv</i>. ‏מִבַּאן וְאֵילֵךְ‎ hinc et ulterius; from now further. IV 16</p>  
 <p>‏אֵלוּ‎. These. Equals the biblical ‏אֵלֶה.‎VI. 1.</p>  
 <p>‏אֲלַכְסוֹן‎ <i>adj</i>. [Greek λόξον] Oblique, diagonal direction. V, 2.</p>  
 <p>‏אֶמְעָע‎ <i>n</i>. Middle. centre. I, 2.</p>  
 <p align="center"><b>‏בּ‎</b></p>  
 <p>‏בּורֵי‎ <i>n</i>. Clearness, perspicuity. I, 3. ‏וְהַעֲמְד דָבָר על בּוּרְיוֹ‎ and put the subject in a clear point of view.</p>  
 <p>‏בָּבָא‎ <i>n</i>. Division. V, 6.</p>  
 <p>‏בִּנְתַּיִם‎ or ‏בְּינוֹתַוִם‎ Composed of ‏בֵּין שְׁתְּוִם,‎ omitting ‏שׁ‎ between them. I, 1. [See Duke's Sprache der Mischnah, p. 68.]</p>  
 <p align="center"><b>‏ג‎</b></p>  
 <p>‏גְּב‎ <i>n</i>. Back. ‏עַג גַּבֵּי‎ upon the back id est, upon or above. VI, 8.</p>  
 <p>‏גּוּף‎ <i>n</i>. Body, substance II, 6.</p>  
 <p>‏גַּלְגַּל‎ <i>n</i>. Circle, celestial orb, or sphere. II, 4.</p>  
 <p><a name="page_58"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 58</font></a></p>  
 <p align="center"><b>‏ד‎</b></p>  
 <p>‏דִּבּוּר‎ n. Word. I, 8.</p>  
 <p align="center"><b>‏ה‎</b></p>  
 <p>‏הִרְהֵר‎ <i>v</i>. Think, muse. meditate, reflect. I, 7.</p>  
 <p>‏הִרְהוּר‎ <i>n</i>. Reflection, meditation. V, 1.</p>  
 <p align="center"><b>‏ז‎</b></p>  
 <p>‏זְכוּת‎ <i>n</i>. Innocence, purity, godliness, merit. II, 1.</p>  
 <p align="center"><b>‏ח‎</b></p>  
 <p>‏חוֹכָה‎ <i>n</i>. Misdeed, trespass. II, 1.</p>  
 <p>‏חָזַר‎ <i>v</i>. To return, to turn one's self round. II, 5.</p>  
 <p>‏חֲלִילָה‎ <i>n</i>. Rotation; from ‏חָלֹל‎ to dance round. II, 5.</p>  
 <p align="center"><b>‏ט‎</b></p>  
 <p>‏טָחוֹל‎ <i>n</i>. Milt, spleen. V, 4.</p>  
 <p align="center"><b>‏כּ‎</b></p>  
 <p>‏כָּאן‎ <i>or</i> ‏בַּאנ‎ <i>adv</i>. Here, there; ‏מִכַּאן‎ thence, from thence. IV, 16. </p>  
 <p>‏כּוֹבָב‎ <i>n</i>. Star; especially the planet Mercury. IV, 7.</p>  
 <p>‏כּיּן‎ <i>piel</i> ‏כֵיִן‎ <i>v</i>. Direct; ‏מְכֻיָּן‎directed, situated. I, 2.</p>  
 <p>‏כִּיעוּר‎ <i>n</i>. Ugliness. IV, 3.</p>  
 <p>‏כַּךְ‎ or ‏לְכָּךְ‎ <i>adv</i>. So, thus. I, 7.</p>  
 <p>‏כָּרַע‎ <i>Hiph</i>. ‏הִכְריעַ‎ <i>v</i>. To intervene in any thing, to mediate the peace, accomodate a quarrel. II, 1.</p>  
 <p align="center"><b>‏ל‎</b></p>  
 <p>‏לְעִימָה‎ <i>n</i>. Eating, swallowing. V, 1.</p>  
 <p>‏לְפִיכָךְ‎ <i>adv</i>. Composed of the words ‏לְפִי‎ and ‏כָף‎. According to that, therefore. IV, 15.</p>  
 <p><a name="page_59"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 59</font></a></p>  
 <p align="center"><b>‏מ‎</b></p>  
 <p>‏מַאֲדִים‎ <i>n</i>. The planet Mars. IV, 7.</p>  
 <p>‏מִדָּה‎ <i>n</i>. Measure, quality, divine attribute. I, 4.</p>  
 <p>‏מִשְנָה‎ n. Doctrine, lesson, paragraph.</p>  
 <p>‏מַוָלוֹת‎ <i>n</i>. Constellations; especially the twelve signs of the Zodiac. V, 3. ‏מַוָּל מוֹב‎ a happy constellation.</p>  
 <p>‏כְּמִין‎ <i>adv</i>. It is a particle like ‏כּ,‎ as; it is added the word ‏מִין‎ kind, denoting: as a kind of, like, as. II, 4.</p>  
 <p>‏מֵימַר‎ <i>v</i>. Chald. inf. ‏מְאמַר‎ or ‏מֵמַר‎. To speak, command. II, 5.</p>  
 <p>‏מַמָּשׁ‎ <i>n</i>. Substance, reality. II, 5.</p>  
 <p>‏מַעֲוִיבָה‎ <i>n</i>. Rampart, a floor, pavement. I, 8.</p>  
 <p>‏מָרָה‎ <i>n</i>. Gall. V, 4</p>  
 <p>‏מִתְנַהֵנ‎ See ‏נָהַנ.‎ IV, 1.</p>  
 <p align="center"><b>‏נ‎</b></p>  
 <p>‏נָהַנ‎ <i>v</i>. With a ‏בּ‎ following after it, signifies: to make use of any thing. IV, 1.</p>  
 <p>‏נוֹנַהּ‎ <i>n</i>. The planet Venus. IV, 7.</p>  
 <p>‏נָעַץ‎ <i>v</i>. Chald. Stick in, fasten, conjoin, connect. I, 6.</p>  
 <p>‏נָקָב‎ <i>n</i>. Opening. IV, 8.</p>  
 <p align="center"><b>‏ס‎</b></p>  
 <p>‏סָדַר‎ <i>v</i>. Arrange. V, 16.</p>  
 <p>‏סִימָן‎ <i>n</i>. σημεϊον Sign, illustration. II, 4. [See Geiger's Lesestücke der Mishnah, p. 121.]</p>  
 <p>‏סְלִיק‎ <i>n</i>. Finished; the end (of a book or chapter.)</p>  
 <p align="center"><b>‏ע‎</b></p>  
 <p>‏עֲרֵיבָה‎ <i>n</i>. Contention, rivalry. V, 5.</p>  
 <p>‏עָתִיד‎ <i>n</i>. Future. II, 2.</p>  
 <p><a name="page_60"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 60</font></a></p>  
 <p align="center"><b>‏פ‎</b></p>  
 <p>‏פֶרֶק‎ <i>n</i>. Chapter, section.</p>  
 <p>‏פָשׁוּם‎ <i>adj</i>. Divested of clothes, undressed, simple. I, 8.</p>  
 <p align="center"><b>‏צ‎</b></p>  
 <p>‏צֶרֶק‎ <i>n</i>. The planet Jupiter. IV, 7.</p>  
 <p>‏צְפִייָח‎ <i>n</i>. Appearance. I, 5.</p>  
 <p>‏צָרַף‎ <i>v</i>. Refine, melt together, connect, combine. II, 2.</p>  
 <p align="center"><b>‏ק‎</b></p>  
 <p>‏קָכַע‎ <i>v</i>. To fix, to fasten. I, 8. II, 3. </p>  
 <p>‏קֵיבָח‎ or ‏קֵבָה‎ <i>n</i>. Stomach. V, 4.</p>  
 <p>‏קַרְקְבָן‎ or ‏קוּרְקְבָן‎ <i>n</i>. Stomach. V, 4.</p>  
 <p align="center"><b>‏ר‎</b></p>  
 <p>‏רַאֲיָה‎ <i>n</i>. Argument, evidence. VI, 2.</p>  
 <p>‏רְאִיָה‎ <i>n</i>. Sight. V, 1.</p>  
 <p>‏רְוִיָה‎ <i>n</i>. Redundancy of water, moistness. III. 4.</p>  
 <p>‏רֵיחַ‎ <i>v</i>. Smell. V, 1.</p>  
 <p align="center"><b>‏ש‎</b></p>  
 <p>‏שַׁבְחָאִי‎ <i>n</i>. The planet Saturn. IV, 7.</p>  
 <p>‏שִׂיחָה‎ <i>n</i>. Speaking. V, 1.</p>  
 <p>‏שְׁמִיעָה‎ <i>n</i>. Hearing. V, 1.</p>  
 <p>‏שֵׁרֵת‎ <i>v</i>. To serve. I, 8.</p>  
 <p align="center"><b>‏תּ‎</b></p>  
 <p>‏תְּלִי‎ <i>n</i>. The constellation Draco or Dragon. VI, 2.</p>  
 <p>‏תַּשְׁמִיש‎ <i>n</i>. Coition. V, 1.</p>  
 <p>‏תָּפַם‎ <i>or</i> ‏תָּפַשׂ‎ <i>v</i>. To seize, to take hold of.</p>    </body>
