 <p>
 What does the future hold?
 This is one of the big questions we'd all like answered.
 This page has links to resources at sacred-texts that relate to
 prophecy and divination.
 </p>
 
 
 <h3>Prophecy</h3>
 <p>
 <a href="#" onclick="window.open('../cdshop/index.htm','cartwin','');"><img src="../img/buycd.jpg" height="20" border="0"></a>
 
 <span class="c_e">
 <span class="c_t"><a href="../nos/index.htm">The Prophecies of Nostradamus</a></span><br>
 by <span class="c_a">Nostradamus</span>.
 <span class="c_b">The complete predictions of Nostradamus both in English and Old French</span>,
 with a biography of Nostradamus and some notes about the events of 9/11.
 </span>
 </p>
 
 
 <p>
 <a href="#" onclick="window.open('../cdshop/index.htm','cartwin','');"><img src="../img/buycd.jpg" height="20" border="0"></a>
 
 <span class="c_e">
 <span class="c_t"><a href="../nos/mst/index.htm">Nostradamus: The Man Who Saw Through Time</a></span><br>
 by <span class="c_a">Lee McCann</span> [<span class="c_d">1941</span>].<br>
 <span class="c_b">A biography and historical novel of Nostradamus</span>, with many interpreted quatrains, written during World War II.
 </span>
 </p>
 
 
 <p>
 <a href="#" onclick="window.open('../cdshop/index.htm','cartwin','');"><img src="../img/buycd.jpg" height="20" border="0"></a>
 
 <span class="c_e">
 <span class="c_t"><a href="../nos/oon/index.htm">The Oracles of Nostradamus</a></span><br>
 by <span class="c_a">Charles A. Ward</span> [<span class="c_d">1891</span>].<br>
 <span class="c_b">The complete text of one of the best books about Nostradamus ever written.</span>
 </span>
 </p>
 
 <p>
 <a href="#" onclick="window.open('../cdshop/index.htm','cartwin','');"><img src="../img/buycd.jpg" height="20" border="0"></a>
 
 <span class="c_e">
 <span class="c_t"><a href="../cla/sib/index.htm">The Sibylline Oracles</a></span><br>
 Tr. by <span class="c_a">Milton S. Terry</span> [<span class="c_d">1899</span>].<br>
 <span class="c_b">Read the mysterious (purported) predictions of the Roman Sibylline Oracle.</span>
 </span>
 </p>
 
 <p>
 <a href="#" onclick="window.open('../cdshop/index.htm','cartwin','');"><img src="../img/buycd.jpg" height="20" border="0"></a>
 
 <span class="c_e">
 <span class="c_t"><a href="../bib/kjv/rev.htm">The Book of Revelation</a></span><br>
 <span class="c_b">The most famous Christian prophecy.</span>
 </span>
 </p>
 
 <p>
 <a href="#" onclick="window.open('../cdshop/index.htm','cartwin','');"><img src="../img/buycd.jpg" height="20" border="0"></a>
 
 <span class="c_e">
 <span class="c_t"><a href="pop/index.htm">The Prophecies of Paracelsus</a></span><br>
 by <span class="c_a">Paracelsus</span>, tr. by <span class="c_a">J.K</span> [<span class="c_d">1915</span>].<br>
 <span class="c_b">The alchemist and esoteric philosopher issued a set of 32 predictions</span>, each illustrated by an enigmatic woodcut.
 </span>
 </p>
 
 <p>
 <a href="#" onclick="window.open('../cdshop/index.htm','cartwin','');"><img src="../img/buycd.jpg" height="20" border="0"></a>
 
 <span class="c_e">
 <span class="c_t"><a href="msi/index.htm">Mother Shipton Investigated</a></span><br>
 by <span class="c_a">William H. Harrison</span> [<span class="c_d">1881</span>]<br>
 <span class="c_b">Prophecies attributed to Mother Shipton</span>, a legendary English figure
 who lived over 500 years ago are startlingly accurate.
 Were these true predictions or just simply a self-perpetuating hoax?
 </span>
 </p>
 
 <a href="#" onclick="window.open('../cdshop/index.htm','cartwin','');"><img src="../img/buycd.jpg" height="20" border="0"></a>
 
 <span class="c_e">
 <span class="c_t"><a href="../neu/celt/pbs/index.htm">The Prophecies of the Brahan Seer</a></span><br>
 by <span class="c_a">Alexander Mackenzie</span>, intro. by <span class="c_a">Andrew Lang</span> [<span class="c_d">1899</span>]<br>
 <span class="c_b">The tale of the famed Scottish clarivoyant and his amazing, and very specific, predictions.</span>
 </span>
 <br>
 
 <h3>Divination</h3>
 
 <p>If you'd like to get your own view of the future, here 
 are some systems for doing so.</p>
 
 <p>
 <a href="#" onclick="window.open('../cdshop/index.htm','cartwin','');"><img src="../img/buycd.jpg" height="20" border="0"></a>
 
 <span class="c_e">
 <span class="c_t"><a href="../time/cal/astro.htm">Planetary Positions</a></span><br>
 <span class="c_b">Get the current astrological chart</span>, cast one for your birthdate,
 or for any historical date.
 </span>
 </p>
 
 <a href="#" onclick="window.open('../cdshop/index.htm','cartwin','');"><img src="../img/buycd.jpg" height="20" border="0"></a>
 
 <span class="c_e">
 <span class="c_t"><a href="../ich/index.htm">Sacred Books of the East, Volume 16</a></span>; the <span class="c_t">I Ching</span><br>
 by <span class="c_a">James Legge</span> [<span class="c_d">1899</span>]<br>
 <span class="c_b">The famous Chinese oracle book, one of the oldest surviving sacred texts.</span>
 </span>
 <br>
 
 <p>
 <a href="#" onclick="window.open('../cdshop/index.htm','cartwin','');"><img src="../img/buycd.jpg" height="20" border="0"></a>
 
 <span class="c_e">
 <span class="c_t"><a href="../tarot/pkt/index.htm">The Pictorial Key to the Tarot</a></span><br>
 by <span class="c_a">A.E. Waite</span> [<span class="c_d">1911</span>]<br>
 <span class="c_b">Read the classic by A.E. Waite on the Tarot.</span>
 Includes the Waite-Rider Tarot deck.
 Also: <a href="../tarot/pkt/tarot0.htm">get a Tarot reading</a>!
 </span>
 </p>
