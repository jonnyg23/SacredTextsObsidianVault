 <p align="center"><a href="../kjv/hab003.htm">KJV</a></p><p>1  A prayer<sup><a href="h8605.htm">8605</a></sup> of Habakkuk<sup><a href="h2265.htm">2265</a></sup> the prophet<sup><a href="h5030.htm">5030</a></sup> upon<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> Shigionoth.<sup><a href="h7692.htm">7692</a></sup> </p>
 <p>2  O LORD,<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> I have heard<sup><a href="h8085.htm">8085</a></sup> thy speech,<sup><a href="h8088.htm">8088</a></sup> and was afraid:<sup><a href="h3372.htm">3372</a></sup> O LORD,<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> revive<sup><a href="h2421.htm">2421</a></sup> thy work<sup><a href="h6467.htm">6467</a></sup> in the midst<sup><a href="h7130.htm">7130</a></sup> of the years,<sup><a href="h8141.htm">8141</a></sup> in the midst<sup><a href="h7130.htm">7130</a></sup> of the years<sup><a href="h8141.htm">8141</a></sup> make known;<sup><a href="h3045.htm">3045</a></sup> in wrath<sup><a href="h7267.htm">7267</a></sup> remember<sup><a href="h2142.htm">2142</a></sup> mercy.<sup><a href="h7355.htm">7355</a></sup> </p>
 <p>3  God<sup><a href="h0433.htm">433</a></sup> came<sup><a href="h0935.htm">935</a></sup> from Teman,<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h8487.htm">8487</a></sup> and the Holy One<sup><a href="h6918.htm">6918</a></sup> from mount<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h2022.htm">2022</a></sup> Paran.<sup><a href="h6290.htm">6290</a></sup> Selah.<sup><a href="h5542.htm">5542</a></sup> His glory<sup><a href="h1935.htm">1935</a></sup> covered<sup><a href="h3680.htm">3680</a></sup> the heavens,<sup><a href="h8064.htm">8064</a></sup> and the earth<sup><a href="h0776.htm">776</a></sup> was full<sup><a href="h4390.htm">4390</a></sup> of his praise.<sup><a href="h8416.htm">8416</a></sup> </p>
 <p>4  And his brightness<sup><a href="h5051.htm">5051</a></sup> was<sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> as the light;<sup><a href="h0216.htm">216</a></sup> he had horns<sup><a href="h7161.htm">7161</a></sup> coming out of his hand:<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3027.htm">3027</a></sup> and there<sup><a href="h8033.htm">8033</a></sup> was the hiding<sup><a href="h2253.htm">2253</a></sup> of his power.<sup><a href="h5797.htm">5797</a></sup> </p>
 <p>5  Before<sup><a href="h6440.htm">6440</a></sup> him went<sup><a href="h1980.htm">1980</a></sup> the pestilence,<sup><a href="h1698.htm">1698</a></sup> and burning coals<sup><a href="h7565.htm">7565</a></sup> went forth<sup><a href="h3318.htm">3318</a></sup> at his feet.<sup><a href="h7272.htm">7272</a></sup> </p>
 <p>6  He stood,<sup><a href="h5975.htm">5975</a></sup> and measured<sup><a href="h4128.htm">4128</a></sup> the earth:<sup><a href="h0776.htm">776</a></sup> he beheld,<sup><a href="h7200.htm">7200</a></sup> and drove asunder<sup><a href="h5425.htm">5425</a></sup> the nations;<sup><a href="h1471.htm">1471</a></sup> and the everlasting<sup><a href="h5703.htm">5703</a></sup> mountains<sup><a href="h2042.htm">2042</a></sup> were scattered,<sup><a href="h6327.htm">6327</a></sup> the perpetual<sup><a href="h5769.htm">5769</a></sup> hills<sup><a href="h1389.htm">1389</a></sup> did bow:<sup><a href="h7817.htm">7817</a></sup> his ways<sup><a href="h1979.htm">1979</a></sup> are everlasting.<sup><a href="h5769.htm">5769</a></sup> </p>
 <p>7  I saw<sup><a href="h7200.htm">7200</a></sup> the tents<sup><a href="h0168.htm">168</a></sup> of Cushan<sup><a href="h3572.htm">3572</a></sup> in<sup><a href="h8478.htm">8478</a></sup> affliction:<sup><a href="h0205.htm">205</a></sup> and the curtains<sup><a href="h3407.htm">3407</a></sup> of the land<sup><a href="h0776.htm">776</a></sup> of Midian<sup><a href="h4080.htm">4080</a></sup> did tremble.<sup><a href="h7264.htm">7264</a></sup> </p>
 <p>8  Was the LORD<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> displeased<sup><a href="h2734.htm">2734</a></sup> against the rivers?<sup><a href="h5104.htm">5104</a></sup> was thine anger<sup><a href="h0639.htm">639</a></sup> against the rivers?<sup><a href="h5104.htm">5104</a></sup> was thy wrath<sup><a href="h5678.htm">5678</a></sup> against the sea,<sup><a href="h3220.htm">3220</a></sup> that<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> thou didst ride<sup><a href="h7392.htm">7392</a></sup> upon<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> thine horses<sup><a href="h5483.htm">5483</a></sup> and thy chariots<sup><a href="h4818.htm">4818</a></sup> of salvation?<sup><a href="h3444.htm">3444</a></sup> </p>
 <p>9  Thy bow<sup><a href="h7198.htm">7198</a></sup> was made quite naked,<sup><a href="h5783.htm">5783</a></sup> <sup><a href="h6181.htm">6181</a></sup> according to the oaths<sup><a href="h7621.htm">7621</a></sup> of the tribes,<sup><a href="h4294.htm">4294</a></sup> even thy word.<sup><a href="h0562.htm">562</a></sup> Selah.<sup><a href="h5542.htm">5542</a></sup> Thou didst cleave<sup><a href="h1234.htm">1234</a></sup> the earth<sup><a href="h0776.htm">776</a></sup> with rivers.<sup><a href="h5104.htm">5104</a></sup> </p>
 <p>10  The mountains<sup><a href="h2022.htm">2022</a></sup> saw<sup><a href="h7200.htm">7200</a></sup> thee, and they trembled:<sup><a href="h2342.htm">2342</a></sup> the overflowing<sup><a href="h2230.htm">2230</a></sup> of the water<sup><a href="h4325.htm">4325</a></sup> passed by:<sup><a href="h5674.htm">5674</a></sup> the deep<sup><a href="h8415.htm">8415</a></sup> uttered<sup><a href="h5414.htm">5414</a></sup> his voice,<sup><a href="h6963.htm">6963</a></sup> and lifted up<sup><a href="h5375.htm">5375</a></sup> his hands<sup><a href="h3027.htm">3027</a></sup> on high.<sup><a href="h7315.htm">7315</a></sup> </p>
 <p>11  The sun<sup><a href="h8121.htm">8121</a></sup> and moon<sup><a href="h3394.htm">3394</a></sup> stood still<sup><a href="h5975.htm">5975</a></sup> in their habitation:<sup><a href="h2073.htm">2073</a></sup> at the light<sup><a href="h0216.htm">216</a></sup> of thine arrows<sup><a href="h2671.htm">2671</a></sup> they went,<sup><a href="h1980.htm">1980</a></sup> and at the shining<sup><a href="h5051.htm">5051</a></sup> of thy glittering<sup><a href="h1300.htm">1300</a></sup> spear.<sup><a href="h2595.htm">2595</a></sup> </p>
 <p>12  Thou didst march through<sup><a href="h6805.htm">6805</a></sup> the land<sup><a href="h0776.htm">776</a></sup> in indignation,<sup><a href="h2195.htm">2195</a></sup> thou didst thresh<sup><a href="h1758.htm">1758</a></sup> the heathen<sup><a href="h1471.htm">1471</a></sup> in anger.<sup><a href="h0639.htm">639</a></sup> </p>
 <p>13  Thou wentest forth<sup><a href="h3318.htm">3318</a></sup> for the salvation<sup><a href="h3468.htm">3468</a></sup> of thy people,<sup><a href="h5971.htm">5971</a></sup> even for salvation<sup><a href="h3468.htm">3468</a></sup> with<sup><a href="h0854.htm">854</a></sup> thine anointed;<sup><a href="h4899.htm">4899</a></sup> thou woundedst<sup><a href="h4272.htm">4272</a></sup> the head<sup><a href="h7218.htm">7218</a></sup> out of the house<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h1004.htm">1004</a></sup> of the wicked,<sup><a href="h7563.htm">7563</a></sup> by discovering<sup><a href="h6168.htm">6168</a></sup> the foundation<sup><a href="h3247.htm">3247</a></sup> unto<sup><a href="h5704.htm">5704</a></sup> the neck.<sup><a href="h6677.htm">6677</a></sup> Selah.<sup><a href="h5542.htm">5542</a></sup> </p>
 <p>14  Thou didst strike through<sup><a href="h5344.htm">5344</a></sup> with his staves<sup><a href="h4294.htm">4294</a></sup> the head<sup><a href="h7218.htm">7218</a></sup> of his villages:<sup><a href="h6518.htm">6518</a></sup> they came out as a whirlwind<sup><a href="h5590.htm">5590</a></sup> to scatter<sup><a href="h6327.htm">6327</a></sup> me: their rejoicing<sup><a href="h5951.htm">5951</a></sup> was as<sup><a href="h3644.htm">3644</a></sup> to devour<sup><a href="h0398.htm">398</a></sup> the poor<sup><a href="h6041.htm">6041</a></sup> secretly.<sup><a href="h4565.htm">4565</a></sup> </p>
 <p>15  Thou didst walk<sup><a href="h1869.htm">1869</a></sup> through the sea<sup><a href="h3220.htm">3220</a></sup> with thine horses,<sup><a href="h5483.htm">5483</a></sup> through the heap<sup><a href="h2563.htm">2563</a></sup> of great<sup><a href="h7227.htm">7227</a></sup> waters.<sup><a href="h4325.htm">4325</a></sup> </p>
 <p>16  When I heard,<sup><a href="h8085.htm">8085</a></sup> my belly<sup><a href="h0990.htm">990</a></sup> trembled;<sup><a href="h7264.htm">7264</a></sup> my lips<sup><a href="h8193.htm">8193</a></sup> quivered<sup><a href="h6750.htm">6750</a></sup> at the voice:<sup><a href="h6963.htm">6963</a></sup> rottenness<sup><a href="h7538.htm">7538</a></sup> entered<sup><a href="h0935.htm">935</a></sup> into my bones,<sup><a href="h6106.htm">6106</a></sup> and I trembled<sup><a href="h7264.htm">7264</a></sup> in<sup><a href="h8478.htm">8478</a></sup> myself, that<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> I might rest<sup><a href="h5117.htm">5117</a></sup> in the day<sup><a href="h3117.htm">3117</a></sup> of trouble:<sup><a href="h6869.htm">6869</a></sup> when he cometh up<sup><a href="h5927.htm">5927</a></sup> unto the people,<sup><a href="h5971.htm">5971</a></sup> he will invade<sup><a href="h1464.htm">1464</a></sup> them with his troops. </p>
 <p>17  Although<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> the fig tree<sup><a href="h8384.htm">8384</a></sup> shall not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> blossom,<sup><a href="h6524.htm">6524</a></sup> neither<sup><a href="h0369.htm">369</a></sup> shall fruit<sup><a href="h2981.htm">2981</a></sup> be in the vines;<sup><a href="h1612.htm">1612</a></sup> the labor<sup><a href="h4639.htm">4639</a></sup> of the olive<sup><a href="h2132.htm">2132</a></sup> shall fail,<sup><a href="h3584.htm">3584</a></sup> and the fields<sup><a href="h7709.htm">7709</a></sup> shall yield<sup><a href="h6213.htm">6213</a></sup> no<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> meat;<sup><a href="h0400.htm">400</a></sup> the flock<sup><a href="h6629.htm">6629</a></sup> shall be cut off<sup><a href="h1504.htm">1504</a></sup> from the fold,<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h4356.htm">4356</a></sup> and there shall be no<sup><a href="h0369.htm">369</a></sup> herd<sup><a href="h1241.htm">1241</a></sup> in the stalls:<sup><a href="h7517.htm">7517</a></sup> </p>
 <p>18  Yet I<sup><a href="h0589.htm">589</a></sup> will rejoice<sup><a href="h5937.htm">5937</a></sup> in the LORD,<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> I will joy<sup><a href="h1523.htm">1523</a></sup> in the God<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> of my salvation.<sup><a href="h3468.htm">3468</a></sup> </p>
 <p>19  The LORD<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> God<sup><a href="h0136.htm">136</a></sup> is my strength,<sup><a href="h2428.htm">2428</a></sup> and he will make<sup><a href="h7760.htm">7760</a></sup> my feet<sup><a href="h7272.htm">7272</a></sup> like hinds'<sup><a href="h0355.htm">355</a></sup> feet, and he will make me to walk<sup><a href="h1869.htm">1869</a></sup> upon<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> mine high places.<sup><a href="h1116.htm">1116</a></sup> To the chief singer<sup><a href="h5329.htm">5329</a></sup> on my stringed instruments.<sup><a href="h5058.htm">5058</a></sup> </p>

 <p align="center"><a href="../sep/hab003.htm">Sep</a></p><p>1 προσευχὴ Αμβακουμ τοῦ προφήτου μετὰ ᾠδῆς </p>
 <p>2 κύριε εἰσακήκοα τὴν ἀκοήν σου καὶ ἐφοβήθην κατενόησα τὰ ἔργα σου καὶ ἐξέστην ἐν μέσῳ δύο ζῴων γνωσθήσῃ ἐν τῷ ἐγγίζειν τὰ ἔτη ἐπιγνωσθήσῃ ἐν τῷ παρεῖναι τὸν καιρὸν ἀναδειχθήσῃ ἐν τῷ ταραχθῆναι τὴν ψυχήν μου ἐν ὀργῇ ἐλέους μνησθήσῃ </p>
 <p>3 ὁ θεὸς ἐκ Θαιμαν ἥξει καὶ ὁ ἅγιος ἐξ ὄρους κατασκίου δασέος διάψαλμα ἐκάλυψεν οὐρανοὺς ἡ ἀρετὴ αὐτοῦ καὶ αἰνέσεως αὐτοῦ πλήρης ἡ γῆ </p>
 <p>4 καὶ φέγγος αὐτοῦ ὡς φῶς ἔσται κέρατα ἐν χερσὶν αὐτοῦ καὶ ἔθετο ἀγάπησιν κραταιὰν ἰσχύος αὐτοῦ </p>
 <p>5 πρὸ προσώπου αὐτοῦ πορεύσεται λόγος καὶ ἐξελεύσεται ἐν πεδίλοις οἱ πόδες αὐτοῦ </p>
 <p>6 ἔστη καὶ ἐσαλεύθη ἡ γῆ ἐπέβλεψεν καὶ διετάκη ἔθνη διεθρύβη τὰ ὄρη βίᾳ ἐτάκησαν βουνοὶ αἰώνιοι </p>
 <p>7 πορείας αἰωνίας αὐτοῦ ἀντὶ κόπων εἶδον σκηνώματα Αἰθιόπων πτοηθήσονται καὶ αἱ σκηναὶ γῆς Μαδιαμ </p>
 <p>8 μὴ ἐν ποταμοῖς ὠργίσθης κύριε ἢ ἐν ποταμοῖς ὁ θυμός σου ἢ ἐν θαλάσσῃ τὸ ὅρμημά σου ὅτι ἐπιβήσῃ ἐπὶ τοὺς ἵππους σου καὶ ἡ ἱππασία σου σωτηρία </p>
 <p>9 ἐντείνων ἐντενεῖς τὸ τόξον σου ἐπὶ τὰ σκῆπτρα λέγει κύριος διάψαλμα ποταμῶν ῥαγήσεται γῆ </p>
 <p>10 ὄψονταί σε καὶ ὠδινήσουσιν λαοί σκορπίζων ὕδατα πορείας αὐτοῦ ἔδωκεν ἡ ἄβυσσος φωνὴν αὐτῆς ὕψος φαντασίας αὐτῆς </p>
 <p>11 ἐπήρθη ὁ ἥλιος καὶ ἡ σελήνη ἔστη ἐν τῇ τάξει αὐτῆς εἰς φῶς βολίδες σου πορεύσονται εἰς φέγγος ἀστραπῆς ὅπλων σου </p>
 <p>12 ἐν ἀπειλῇ ὀλιγώσεις γῆν καὶ ἐν θυμῷ κατάξεις ἔθνη </p>
 <p>13 ἐξῆλθες εἰς σωτηρίαν λαοῦ σου τοῦ σῶσαι τοὺς χριστούς σου ἔβαλες εἰς κεφαλὰς ἀνόμων θάνατον ἐξήγειρας δεσμοὺς ἕως τραχήλου διάψαλμα </p>
 <p>14 διέκοψας ἐν ἐκστάσει κεφαλὰς δυναστῶν σεισθήσονται ἐν αὐτῇ διανοίξουσιν χαλινοὺς αὐτῶν ὡς ἔσθων πτωχὸς λάθρᾳ </p>
 <p>15 καὶ ἐπεβίβασας εἰς θάλασσαν τοὺς ἵππους σου ταράσσοντας ὕδωρ πολύ </p>
 <p>16 ἐφυλαξάμην καὶ ἐπτοήθη ἡ κοιλία μου ἀπὸ φωνῆς προσευχῆς χειλέων μου καὶ εἰσῆλθεν τρόμος εἰς τὰ ὀστᾶ μου καὶ ὑποκάτωθέν μου ἐταράχθη ἡ ἕξις μου ἀναπαύσομαι ἐν ἡμέρᾳ θλίψεως τοῦ ἀναβῆναι εἰς λαὸν παροικίας μου </p>
 <p>17 διότι συκῆ οὐ καρποφορήσει καὶ οὐκ ἔσται γενήματα ἐν ταῖς ἀμπέλοις ψεύσεται ἔργον ἐλαίας καὶ τὰ πεδία οὐ ποιήσει βρῶσιν ἐξέλιπον ἀπὸ βρώσεως πρόβατα καὶ οὐχ ὑπάρχουσιν βόες ἐπὶ φάτναις </p>
 <p>18 ἐγὼ δὲ ἐν τῷ κυρίῳ ἀγαλλιάσομαι χαρήσομαι ἐπὶ τῷ θεῷ τῷ σωτῆρί μου </p>
 <p>19 κύριος ὁ θεὸς δύναμίς μου καὶ τάξει τοὺς πόδας μου εἰς συντέλειαν ἐπὶ τὰ ὑψηλὰ ἐπιβιβᾷ με τοῦ νικῆσαι ἐν τῇ ᾠδῇ αὐτοῦ </p>

 <p align="center"><a href="../vul/hab003.htm">Vul</a></p><p>1 Oratio Habacuc prophetæ, pro ignorantiis.<br>
 </p>
 <p>2 [Domine, audivi auditionem tuam, et timui.<br> Domine, opus tuum, in medio annorum vivifica illud;<br> in medio annorum notum facies:<br> cum iratus fueris, misericordiæ recordaberis.<br></p>
 <p>3 Deus ab austro veniet,<br> et Sanctus de monte Pharan:<br> operuit cælos gloria ejus,<br> et laudis ejus plena est terra.<br></p>
 <p>4 Splendor ejus ut lux erit,<br> cornua in manibus ejus:<br> ibi abscondita est fortitudo ejus.<br></p>
 <p>5 Ante faciem ejus ibit mors,<br> et egredietur diabolus ante pedes ejus.<br></p>
 <p>6 Stetit, et mensus est terram;<br> aspexit, et dissolvit gentes,<br> et contriti sunt montes sæculi:<br> incurvati sunt colles mundi ab itineribus æternitatis ejus.<br></p>
 <p>7 Pro iniquitate vidi tentoria Æthiopiæ;<br> turbabuntur pelles terræ Madian.<br></p>
 <p>8 Numquid in fluminibus iratus es, Domine?<br> aut in fluminibus furor tuus?<br> vel in mari indignatio tua?<br> Qui ascendes super equos tuos, et quadrigæ tuæ salvatio.<br></p>
 <p>9 Suscitans suscitabis arcum tuum,<br> juramenta tribubus quæ locutus es;<br> fluvios scindes terræ.<br></p>
 <p>10 Viderunt te, et doluerunt montes;<br> gurges aquarum transiit:<br> dedit abyssus vocem suam;<br> altitudo manus suas levavit.<br></p>
 <p>11 Sol et luna steterunt in habitaculo suo:<br> in luce sagittarum tuarum ibunt,<br> in splendore fulgurantis hastæ tuæ.<br></p>
 <p>12 In fremitu conculcabis terram;<br> in furore obstupefacies gentes.<br></p>
 <p>13 Egressus es in salutem populi tui,<br> in salutem cum christo tuo:<br> percussisti caput de domo impii,<br> denudasti fundamentum ejus usque ad collum.<br></p>
 <p>14 Maledixisti sceptris ejus,<br> capiti bellatorum ejus,<br> venientibus ut turbo ad dispergendum me:<br> exsultatio eorum, sicut ejus qui devorat pauperem in abscondito.<br></p>
 <p>15 Viam fecisti in mari equis tuis,<br> in luto aquarum multarum.<br></p>
 <p>16 Audivi, et conturbatus est venter meus;<br> a voce contremuerunt labia mea.<br> Ingrediatur putredo in ossibus meis,<br> et subter me scateat:<br> ut requiescam in die tribulationis,<br> ut ascendam ad populum accinctum nostrum.<br></p>
 <p>17 Ficus enim non florebit,<br> et non erit germen in vineis;<br> mentietur opus olivæ,<br> et arva non afferent cibum:<br> abscindetur de ovili pecus,<br> et non erit armentum in præsepibus.<br></p>
 <p>18 Ego autem in Domino gaudebo;<br> et exsultabo in Deo Jesu meo.<br></p>
 <p>19 Deus Dominus fortitudo mea,<br> et ponet pedes meos quasi cervorum:<br> et super excelsa mea deducet me<br> victor in psalmis canentem.]</p>
