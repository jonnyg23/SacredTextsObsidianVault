 <p>3  Thy nakedness<sup><a href="h6172.htm">6172</a></sup> shall be uncovered,<sup><a href="h1540.htm">1540</a></sup> yea,<sup><a href="h1571.htm">1571</a></sup> thy shame<sup><a href="h2781.htm">2781</a></sup> shall be seen:<sup><a href="h7200.htm">7200</a></sup> I will take<sup><a href="h3947.htm">3947</a></sup> vengeance,<sup><a href="h5359.htm">5359</a></sup> and I will not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> meet<sup><a href="h6293.htm">6293</a></sup> thee as a man.<sup><a href="h0120.htm">120</a></sup> </p>

 <p>5  Sit<sup><a href="h3427.htm">3427</a></sup> thou silent,<sup><a href="h1748.htm">1748</a></sup> and get<sup><a href="h0935.htm">935</a></sup> thee into darkness,<sup><a href="h2822.htm">2822</a></sup> O daughter<sup><a href="h1323.htm">1323</a></sup> of the Chaldeans:<sup><a href="h3778.htm">3778</a></sup> for<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> thou shalt no<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> more<sup><a href="h3254.htm">3254</a></sup> be called,<sup><a href="h7121.htm">7121</a></sup> The lady<sup><a href="h1404.htm">1404</a></sup> of kingdoms.<sup><a href="h4467.htm">4467</a></sup> </p>

 <p>7  And thou saidst,<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> I shall be<sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> a lady<sup><a href="h1404.htm">1404</a></sup> forever:<sup><a href="h5769.htm">5769</a></sup> so that<sup><a href="h5704.htm">5704</a></sup> thou didst not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> lay<sup><a href="h7760.htm">7760</a></sup> these<sup><a href="h0428.htm">428</a></sup> things to<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> thy heart,<sup><a href="h3820.htm">3820</a></sup> neither<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> didst remember<sup><a href="h2142.htm">2142</a></sup> the latter end<sup><a href="h0319.htm">319</a></sup> of it. </p>
 <p>8  Therefore hear<sup><a href="h8085.htm">8085</a></sup> now<sup><a href="h6258.htm">6258</a></sup> this,<sup><a href="h2063.htm">2063</a></sup> thou that art given to pleasures,<sup><a href="h5719.htm">5719</a></sup> that dwellest<sup><a href="h3427.htm">3427</a></sup> carelessly,<sup><a href="h0983.htm">983</a></sup> that sayest<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> in thine heart,<sup><a href="h3824.htm">3824</a></sup> I<sup><a href="h0589.htm">589</a></sup> am, and none else beside<sup><a href="h0657.htm">657</a></sup> <sup><a href="h5750.htm">5750</a></sup> me; I shall not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> sit<sup><a href="h3427.htm">3427</a></sup> as a widow,<sup><a href="h0490.htm">490</a></sup> neither<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> shall I know<sup><a href="h3045.htm">3045</a></sup> the loss of children:<sup><a href="h7908.htm">7908</a></sup> </p>

 <p>10  For thou hast trusted<sup><a href="h0982.htm">982</a></sup> in thy wickedness:<sup><a href="h7451.htm">7451</a></sup> thou hast said,<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> None<sup><a href="h0369.htm">369</a></sup> seeth<sup><a href="h7200.htm">7200</a></sup> me. Thy wisdom<sup><a href="h2451.htm">2451</a></sup> and thy knowledge,<sup><a href="h1847.htm">1847</a></sup> it<sup><a href="h1931.htm">1931</a></sup> hath perverted<sup><a href="h7725.htm">7725</a></sup> thee; and thou hast said<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> in thine heart,<sup><a href="h3820.htm">3820</a></sup> I<sup><a href="h0589.htm">589</a></sup> am, and none else beside<sup><a href="h0657.htm">657</a></sup> <sup><a href="h5750.htm">5750</a></sup> me. </p>
 <p>11  Therefore shall evil<sup><a href="h7451.htm">7451</a></sup> come<sup><a href="h0935.htm">935</a></sup> upon<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> thee; thou shalt not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> know<sup><a href="h3045.htm">3045</a></sup> from whence it riseth:<sup><a href="h7837.htm">7837</a></sup> and mischief<sup><a href="h1943.htm">1943</a></sup> shall fall<sup><a href="h5307.htm">5307</a></sup> upon<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> thee; thou shalt not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> be able<sup><a href="h3201.htm">3201</a></sup> to put it off:<sup><a href="h3722.htm">3722</a></sup> and desolation<sup><a href="h7722.htm">7722</a></sup> shall come<sup><a href="h0935.htm">935</a></sup> upon<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> thee suddenly,<sup><a href="h6597.htm">6597</a></sup> which thou shalt not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> know.<sup><a href="h3045.htm">3045</a></sup> </p>
 <p>12  Stand<sup><a href="h5975.htm">5975</a></sup> now<sup><a href="h4994.htm">4994</a></sup> with thine enchantments,<sup><a href="h2267.htm">2267</a></sup> and with the multitude<sup><a href="h7230.htm">7230</a></sup> of thy sorceries,<sup><a href="h3785.htm">3785</a></sup> wherein<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> thou hast labored<sup><a href="h3021.htm">3021</a></sup> from thy youth;<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h5271.htm">5271</a></sup> if so be<sup><a href="h0194.htm">194</a></sup> thou shalt be able<sup><a href="h3201.htm">3201</a></sup> to profit,<sup><a href="h3276.htm">3276</a></sup> if so be<sup><a href="h0194.htm">194</a></sup> thou mayest prevail.<sup><a href="h6206.htm">6206</a></sup> </p>

 <p>15  Thus<sup><a href="h3651.htm">3651</a></sup> shall they be<sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> unto thee with whom<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> thou hast labored,<sup><a href="h3021.htm">3021</a></sup> even thy merchants,<sup><a href="h5503.htm">5503</a></sup> from thy youth:<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h5271.htm">5271</a></sup> they shall wander<sup><a href="h8582.htm">8582</a></sup> every one<sup><a href="h0376.htm">376</a></sup> to his quarter;<sup><a href="h5676.htm">5676</a></sup> none<sup><a href="h0369.htm">369</a></sup> shall save<sup><a href="h3467.htm">3467</a></sup> thee. </p>

 <p align="center"><a href="../vul/isa047.htm">Vul</a></p><p>1 [Descende, sede in pulvere,<br> virgo filia Babylon:<br> sede in terra; non est solium<br> filiæ Chaldæorum,<br> quia ultra non vocaberis<br> mollis et tenera.<br></p>
 <p>2 Tolle molam, et mole farinam;<br> denuda turpitudinem tuam;<br> discooperi humerum, revela crura,<br> transi flumina.<br></p>
 <p>3 Revelabitur ignominia tua,<br> et videbitur opprobrium tuum;<br> ultionem capiam, et non resistet mihi homo.<br></p>
 <p>4 Redemptor noster, Dominus exercituum nomen illius,<br> Sanctus Israël.<br></p>
 <p>5 Sede tacens, et intra in tenebras,<br> filia Chaldæorum,<br> quia non vocaberis ultra<br> domina regnorum.<br></p>
 <p>6 Iratus sum super populum meum:<br> contaminavi hæreditatem meam,<br> et dedi eos in manu tua:<br> non posuisti eis misericordias;<br> super senem aggravasti jugum tuum valde.<br></p>
 <p>7 Et dixisti: In sempiternum ero domina.<br> Non posuisti hæc super cor tuum,<br> neque recordata es novissimi tui.<br></p>
 <p>8 Et nunc audi hæc delicata,<br> et habitans confidenter,<br> quæ dicis in corde tuo:<br> Ego sum, et non est præter me amplius;<br> non sedebo vidua,<br> et ignorabo sterilitatem.<br></p>
 <p>9 Venient tibi duo hæc<br> subito in die una,<br> sterilitas et viduitas:<br> universa venerunt super te,<br> propter multitudinem maleficiorum tuorum,<br> et propter duritiam incantatorum tuorum vehementem.<br></p>
 <p>10 Et fiduciam habuisti in malitia tua,<br> et dixisti: Non est qui videat me.<br> Sapientia tua et scientia tua,<br> hæc decepit te.<br> Et dixisti in corde tuo:<br> Ego sum, et præter me non est altera.<br></p>
 <p>11 Veniet super te malum,<br> et nescies ortum ejus;<br> et irruet super te calamitas<br> quam non poteris expiare;<br> veniet super te repente miseria<br> quam nescies.<br></p>
 <p>12 Sta cum incantatoribus tuis<br> et cum multitudine maleficiorum tuorum,<br> in quibus laborasti ab adolescentia tua,<br> si forte quod prosit tibi,<br> aut si possis fieri fortior.<br></p>
 <p>13 Defecisti in multitudine consiliorum tuorum.<br> Stent, et salvent te<br> augures cæli,<br> qui contemplabantur sidera,<br> et supputabant menses,<br> ut ex eis annuntiarent ventura tibi.<br></p>
 <p>14 Ecce facti sunt quasi stipula,<br> ignis combussit eos;<br> non liberabunt animam suam<br> de manu flammæ;<br> non sunt prunæ quibus calefiant,<br> nec focus ut sedeant ad eum.<br></p>
 <p>15 Sic facta sunt tibi in quibuscumque laboraveras:<br> negotiatores tui ab adolescentia tua,<br> unusquisque in via sua erraverunt;<br> non est qui salvet te.]</p>
