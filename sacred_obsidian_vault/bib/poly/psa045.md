 <p>5  Thine arrows<sup><a href="h2671.htm">2671</a></sup> are sharp<sup><a href="h8150.htm">8150</a></sup> in the heart<sup><a href="h3820.htm">3820</a></sup> of the king's<sup><a href="h4428.htm">4428</a></sup> enemies;<sup><a href="h0341.htm">341</a></sup> whereby the people<sup><a href="h5971.htm">5971</a></sup> fall<sup><a href="h5307.htm">5307</a></sup> under<sup><a href="h8478.htm">8478</a></sup> thee. </p>

 <p>9  Kings'<sup><a href="h4428.htm">4428</a></sup> daughters<sup><a href="h1323.htm">1323</a></sup> were among thy honorable<sup><a href="h3368.htm">3368</a></sup> women: upon thy right hand<sup><a href="h3225.htm">3225</a></sup> did stand<sup><a href="h5324.htm">5324</a></sup> the queen<sup><a href="h7694.htm">7694</a></sup> in gold<sup><a href="h3800.htm">3800</a></sup> of Ophir.<sup><a href="h0211.htm">211</a></sup> </p>
 <p>10  Hearken,<sup><a href="h8085.htm">8085</a></sup> O daughter,<sup><a href="h1323.htm">1323</a></sup> and consider,<sup><a href="h7200.htm">7200</a></sup> and incline<sup><a href="h5186.htm">5186</a></sup> thine ear;<sup><a href="h0241.htm">241</a></sup> forget<sup><a href="h7911.htm">7911</a></sup> also thine own people,<sup><a href="h5971.htm">5971</a></sup> and thy father's<sup><a href="h0001.htm">1</a></sup> house;<sup><a href="h1004.htm">1004</a></sup> </p>
