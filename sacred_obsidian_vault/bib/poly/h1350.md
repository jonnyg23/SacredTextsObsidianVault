  <p>H1350<br>  גּאל  <br> גָּאַל  ‎  gâ‘al  <br><i>gaw-al‘ </i><br>A primitive root, to <i>redeem</i> (according to the Oriental law of kinship), that is, to <i>be</i> <i>the</i> <i>next</i> <i>of</i> <i>kin</i> (and as such to <i>buy</i> <i>back</i> a relative’s property, <i>marry</i> his widow, etc.): -  X in any wise, X at all, avenger, deliver, (do, perform the part of near, next) kinsfolk (-man), purchase, ransom, redeem (-er), revenger.<br></p>
