 <p align="center"><a href="../kjv/psa147.htm">KJV</a></p><p>1  Praise<sup><a href="h1984.htm">1984</a></sup> ye the LORD:<sup><a href="h3050.htm">3050</a></sup> for<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> it is good<sup><a href="h2896.htm">2896</a></sup> to sing praises<sup><a href="h2167.htm">2167</a></sup> unto our God;<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> for<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> it is pleasant;<sup><a href="h5273.htm">5273</a></sup> and praise<sup><a href="h8416.htm">8416</a></sup> is comely.<sup><a href="h5000.htm">5000</a></sup> </p>

 <p>4  He telleth<sup><a href="h4487.htm">4487</a></sup> the number<sup><a href="h4557.htm">4557</a></sup> of the stars;<sup><a href="h3556.htm">3556</a></sup> he calleth<sup><a href="h7121.htm">7121</a></sup> them all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> by their names.<sup><a href="h8034.htm">8034</a></sup> </p>
 <p>5  Great<sup><a href="h1419.htm">1419</a></sup> is our Lord,<sup><a href="h0113.htm">113</a></sup> and of great<sup><a href="h7227.htm">7227</a></sup> power:<sup><a href="h3581.htm">3581</a></sup> his understanding<sup><a href="h8394.htm">8394</a></sup> is infinite.<sup><a href="h0369.htm">369</a></sup> <sup><a href="h4557.htm">4557</a></sup> </p>

 <p>7  Sing<sup><a href="h6030.htm">6030</a></sup> unto the LORD<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> with thanksgiving;<sup><a href="h8426.htm">8426</a></sup> sing praise<sup><a href="h2167.htm">2167</a></sup> upon the harp<sup><a href="h3658.htm">3658</a></sup> unto our God:<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> </p>
 <p>8  Who covereth<sup><a href="h3680.htm">3680</a></sup> the heaven<sup><a href="h8064.htm">8064</a></sup> with clouds,<sup><a href="h5645.htm">5645</a></sup> who prepareth<sup><a href="h3559.htm">3559</a></sup> rain<sup><a href="h4306.htm">4306</a></sup> for the earth,<sup><a href="h0776.htm">776</a></sup> who maketh grass<sup><a href="h2682.htm">2682</a></sup> to grow<sup><a href="h6779.htm">6779</a></sup> upon the mountains.<sup><a href="h2022.htm">2022</a></sup> </p>
 <p>9  He giveth<sup><a href="h5414.htm">5414</a></sup> to the beast<sup><a href="h0929.htm">929</a></sup> his food,<sup><a href="h3899.htm">3899</a></sup> and to the young<sup><a href="h1121.htm">1121</a></sup> ravens<sup><a href="h6158.htm">6158</a></sup> which<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> cry.<sup><a href="h7121.htm">7121</a></sup> </p>
 <p>10  He delighteth<sup><a href="h2654.htm">2654</a></sup> not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> in the strength<sup><a href="h1369.htm">1369</a></sup> of the horse:<sup><a href="h5483.htm">5483</a></sup> he taketh not pleasure<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> <sup><a href="h7521.htm">7521</a></sup> in the legs<sup><a href="h7785.htm">7785</a></sup> of a man.<sup><a href="h0376.htm">376</a></sup> </p>
 <p>11  The LORD<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> taketh pleasure in<sup><a href="h7521.htm">7521</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> them that fear<sup><a href="h3373.htm">3373</a></sup> him, in <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> those that hope<sup><a href="h3176.htm">3176</a></sup> in his mercy.<sup><a href="h2617.htm">2617</a></sup> </p>
 <p>12  Praise<sup><a href="h7623.htm">7623</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> the LORD,<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> O Jerusalem;<sup><a href="h3389.htm">3389</a></sup> praise<sup><a href="h1984.htm">1984</a></sup> thy God,<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> O Zion.<sup><a href="h6726.htm">6726</a></sup> </p>
 <p>13  For<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> he hath strengthened<sup><a href="h2388.htm">2388</a></sup> the bars<sup><a href="h1280.htm">1280</a></sup> of thy gates;<sup><a href="h8179.htm">8179</a></sup> he hath blessed<sup><a href="h1288.htm">1288</a></sup> thy children<sup><a href="h1121.htm">1121</a></sup> within<sup><a href="h7130.htm">7130</a></sup> thee. </p>
 <p>14  He maketh<sup><a href="h7760.htm">7760</a></sup> peace<sup><a href="h7965.htm">7965</a></sup> in thy borders,<sup><a href="h1366.htm">1366</a></sup> and filleth<sup><a href="h7646.htm">7646</a></sup> thee with the finest<sup><a href="h2459.htm">2459</a></sup> of the wheat.<sup><a href="h2406.htm">2406</a></sup> </p>
 <p>15  He sendeth forth<sup><a href="h7971.htm">7971</a></sup> his commandment<sup><a href="h0565.htm">565</a></sup> upon earth:<sup><a href="h0776.htm">776</a></sup> his word<sup><a href="h1697.htm">1697</a></sup> runneth<sup><a href="h7323.htm">7323</a></sup> very<sup><a href="h5704.htm">5704</a></sup> swiftly.<sup><a href="h4120.htm">4120</a></sup> </p>
 <p>16  He giveth<sup><a href="h5414.htm">5414</a></sup> snow<sup><a href="h7950.htm">7950</a></sup> like wool:<sup><a href="h6785.htm">6785</a></sup> he scattereth<sup><a href="h6340.htm">6340</a></sup> the hoarfrost<sup><a href="h3713.htm">3713</a></sup> like ashes.<sup><a href="h0665.htm">665</a></sup> </p>
 <p>17  He casteth forth<sup><a href="h7993.htm">7993</a></sup> his ice<sup><a href="h7140.htm">7140</a></sup> like morsels:<sup><a href="h6595.htm">6595</a></sup> who<sup><a href="h4310.htm">4310</a></sup> can stand<sup><a href="h5975.htm">5975</a></sup> before<sup><a href="h6440.htm">6440</a></sup> his cold?<sup><a href="h7135.htm">7135</a></sup> </p>

 <p>19  He showeth<sup><a href="h5046.htm">5046</a></sup> his word<sup><a href="h1697.htm">1697</a></sup> unto Jacob,<sup><a href="h3290.htm">3290</a></sup> his statutes<sup><a href="h2706.htm">2706</a></sup> and his judgments<sup><a href="h4941.htm">4941</a></sup> unto Israel.<sup><a href="h3478.htm">3478</a></sup> </p>

Praise<sup><a href="h1984.htm">1984</a></sup> ye the LORD.<sup><a href="h3050.htm">3050</a></sup> </p>
