 <p align="center"><a href="../kjv/pro004.htm">KJV</a></p><p>1  Hear,<sup><a href="h8085.htm">8085</a></sup> ye children,<sup><a href="h1121.htm">1121</a></sup> the instruction<sup><a href="h4148.htm">4148</a></sup> of a father,<sup><a href="h0001.htm">1</a></sup> and attend<sup><a href="h7181.htm">7181</a></sup> to know<sup><a href="h3045.htm">3045</a></sup> understanding.<sup><a href="h0998.htm">998</a></sup> </p>
 <p>2  For<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> I give<sup><a href="h5414.htm">5414</a></sup> you good<sup><a href="h2896.htm">2896</a></sup> doctrine,<sup><a href="h3948.htm">3948</a></sup> forsake<sup><a href="h5800.htm">5800</a></sup> ye not<sup><a href="h0408.htm">408</a></sup> my law.<sup><a href="h8451.htm">8451</a></sup> </p>
 <p>3  For<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> I was<sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> my father's<sup><a href="h0001.htm">1</a></sup> son,<sup><a href="h1121.htm">1121</a></sup> tender<sup><a href="h7390.htm">7390</a></sup> and only<sup><a href="h3173.htm">3173</a></sup> beloved in the sight<sup><a href="h6440.htm">6440</a></sup> of my mother.<sup><a href="h0517.htm">517</a></sup> </p>
 <p>4  He taught<sup><a href="h3384.htm">3384</a></sup> me also, and said<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> unto me, Let thine heart<sup><a href="h3820.htm">3820</a></sup> retain<sup><a href="h8551.htm">8551</a></sup> my words:<sup><a href="h1697.htm">1697</a></sup> keep<sup><a href="h8104.htm">8104</a></sup> my commandments,<sup><a href="h4687.htm">4687</a></sup> and live.<sup><a href="h2421.htm">2421</a></sup> </p>
 <p>5  Get<sup><a href="h7069.htm">7069</a></sup> wisdom,<sup><a href="h2451.htm">2451</a></sup> get<sup><a href="h7069.htm">7069</a></sup> understanding:<sup><a href="h0998.htm">998</a></sup> forget<sup><a href="h7911.htm">7911</a></sup> it not;<sup><a href="h0408.htm">408</a></sup> neither<sup><a href="h0408.htm">408</a></sup> decline<sup><a href="h5186.htm">5186</a></sup> from the words<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0561.htm">561</a></sup> of my mouth.<sup><a href="h6310.htm">6310</a></sup> </p>
 <p>6  Forsake<sup><a href="h5800.htm">5800</a></sup> her not,<sup><a href="h0408.htm">408</a></sup> and she shall preserve<sup><a href="h8104.htm">8104</a></sup> thee: love<sup><a href="h0157.htm">157</a></sup> her, and she shall keep<sup><a href="h5341.htm">5341</a></sup> thee. </p>
 <p>7  Wisdom<sup><a href="h2451.htm">2451</a></sup> is the principal thing;<sup><a href="h7225.htm">7225</a></sup> therefore get<sup><a href="h7069.htm">7069</a></sup> wisdom:<sup><a href="h2451.htm">2451</a></sup> and with all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> thy getting<sup><a href="h7075.htm">7075</a></sup> get<sup><a href="h7069.htm">7069</a></sup> understanding.<sup><a href="h0998.htm">998</a></sup> </p>
 <p>8  Exalt<sup><a href="h5549.htm">5549</a></sup> her, and she shall promote<sup><a href="h7311.htm">7311</a></sup> thee: she shall bring thee to honor,<sup><a href="h3513.htm">3513</a></sup> when<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> thou dost embrace<sup><a href="h2263.htm">2263</a></sup> her. </p>
 <p>9  She shall give<sup><a href="h5414.htm">5414</a></sup> to thine head<sup><a href="h7218.htm">7218</a></sup> an ornament<sup><a href="h3880.htm">3880</a></sup> of grace:<sup><a href="h2580.htm">2580</a></sup> a crown<sup><a href="h5850.htm">5850</a></sup> of glory<sup><a href="h8597.htm">8597</a></sup> shall she deliver<sup><a href="h4042.htm">4042</a></sup> to thee. </p>
 <p>10  Hear,<sup><a href="h8085.htm">8085</a></sup> O my son,<sup><a href="h1121.htm">1121</a></sup> and receive<sup><a href="h3947.htm">3947</a></sup> my sayings;<sup><a href="h0561.htm">561</a></sup> and the years<sup><a href="h8141.htm">8141</a></sup> of thy life<sup><a href="h2416.htm">2416</a></sup> shall be many.<sup><a href="h7235.htm">7235</a></sup> </p>
 <p>11  I have taught<sup><a href="h3384.htm">3384</a></sup> thee in the way<sup><a href="h1870.htm">1870</a></sup> of wisdom;<sup><a href="h2451.htm">2451</a></sup> I have led<sup><a href="h1869.htm">1869</a></sup> thee in right<sup><a href="h3476.htm">3476</a></sup> paths.<sup><a href="h4570.htm">4570</a></sup> </p>
 <p>12  When thou goest,<sup><a href="h1980.htm">1980</a></sup> thy steps<sup><a href="h6806.htm">6806</a></sup> shall not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> be straitened;<sup><a href="h3334.htm">3334</a></sup> and when<sup><a href="h0518.htm">518</a></sup> thou runnest,<sup><a href="h7323.htm">7323</a></sup> thou shalt not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> stumble.<sup><a href="h3782.htm">3782</a></sup> </p>
 <p>13  Take fast hold<sup><a href="h2388.htm">2388</a></sup> of instruction;<sup><a href="h4148.htm">4148</a></sup> let her not<sup><a href="h0408.htm">408</a></sup> go:<sup><a href="h7503.htm">7503</a></sup> keep<sup><a href="h5341.htm">5341</a></sup> her; for<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> she<sup><a href="h1931.htm">1931</a></sup> is thy life.<sup><a href="h2416.htm">2416</a></sup> </p>
 <p>14  Enter<sup><a href="h0935.htm">935</a></sup> not<sup><a href="h0408.htm">408</a></sup> into the path<sup><a href="h0734.htm">734</a></sup> of the wicked,<sup><a href="h7563.htm">7563</a></sup> and go<sup><a href="h0833.htm">833</a></sup> not<sup><a href="h0408.htm">408</a></sup> in the way<sup><a href="h1870.htm">1870</a></sup> of evil<sup><a href="h7451.htm">7451</a></sup> men. </p>
 <p>15  Avoid<sup><a href="h6544.htm">6544</a></sup> it, pass<sup><a href="h5674.htm">5674</a></sup> not<sup><a href="h0408.htm">408</a></sup> by it, turn<sup><a href="h7847.htm">7847</a></sup> from<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> it, and pass away.<sup><a href="h5674.htm">5674</a></sup> </p>
 <p>16  For<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> they sleep<sup><a href="h3462.htm">3462</a></sup> not,<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> except<sup><a href="h0518.htm">518</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> they have done mischief;<sup><a href="h7489.htm">7489</a></sup> and their sleep<sup><a href="h8142.htm">8142</a></sup> is taken away,<sup><a href="h1497.htm">1497</a></sup> unless<sup><a href="h0518.htm">518</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> they cause some to fall.<sup><a href="h3782.htm">3782</a></sup> </p>
 <p>17  For<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> they eat<sup><a href="h3898.htm">3898</a></sup> the bread<sup><a href="h3899.htm">3899</a></sup> of wickedness,<sup><a href="h7562.htm">7562</a></sup> and drink<sup><a href="h8354.htm">8354</a></sup> the wine<sup><a href="h3196.htm">3196</a></sup> of violence.<sup><a href="h2555.htm">2555</a></sup> </p>
 <p>18  But the path<sup><a href="h0734.htm">734</a></sup> of the just<sup><a href="h6662.htm">6662</a></sup> is as the shining<sup><a href="h5051.htm">5051</a></sup> light,<sup><a href="h0216.htm">216</a></sup> that shineth<sup><a href="h0215.htm">215</a></sup> more and more<sup><a href="h1980.htm">1980</a></sup> unto<sup><a href="h5704.htm">5704</a></sup> the perfect<sup><a href="h3559.htm">3559</a></sup> day.<sup><a href="h3117.htm">3117</a></sup> </p>
 <p>19  The way<sup><a href="h1870.htm">1870</a></sup> of the wicked<sup><a href="h7563.htm">7563</a></sup> is as darkness:<sup><a href="h0653.htm">653</a></sup> they know<sup><a href="h3045.htm">3045</a></sup> not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> at what<sup><a href="h4100.htm">4100</a></sup> they stumble.<sup><a href="h3782.htm">3782</a></sup> </p>
 <p>20  My son,<sup><a href="h1121.htm">1121</a></sup> attend<sup><a href="h7181.htm">7181</a></sup> to my words;<sup><a href="h1697.htm">1697</a></sup> incline<sup><a href="h5186.htm">5186</a></sup> thine ear<sup><a href="h0241.htm">241</a></sup> unto my sayings.<sup><a href="h0561.htm">561</a></sup> </p>
 <p>21  Let them not<sup><a href="h0408.htm">408</a></sup> depart<sup><a href="h3868.htm">3868</a></sup> from thine eyes;<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h5869.htm">5869</a></sup> keep<sup><a href="h8104.htm">8104</a></sup> them in the midst<sup><a href="h8432.htm">8432</a></sup> of thine heart.<sup><a href="h3824.htm">3824</a></sup> </p>
 <p>22  For<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> they<sup><a href="h1992.htm">1992</a></sup> are life<sup><a href="h2416.htm">2416</a></sup> unto those that find<sup><a href="h4672.htm">4672</a></sup> them, and health<sup><a href="h4832.htm">4832</a></sup> to all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> their flesh.<sup><a href="h1320.htm">1320</a></sup> </p>
 <p>23  Keep<sup><a href="h5341.htm">5341</a></sup> thy heart<sup><a href="h3820.htm">3820</a></sup> with all<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> diligence;<sup><a href="h4929.htm">4929</a></sup> for<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> out of<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> it are the issues<sup><a href="h8444.htm">8444</a></sup> of life.<sup><a href="h2416.htm">2416</a></sup> </p>
 <p>24  Put away<sup><a href="h5493.htm">5493</a></sup> from<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> thee a froward<sup><a href="h6143.htm">6143</a></sup> mouth,<sup><a href="h6310.htm">6310</a></sup> and perverse<sup><a href="h3891.htm">3891</a></sup> lips<sup><a href="h8193.htm">8193</a></sup> put far<sup><a href="h7368.htm">7368</a></sup> from<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> thee. </p>
 <p>25  Let thine eyes<sup><a href="h5869.htm">5869</a></sup> look<sup><a href="h5027.htm">5027</a></sup> right on,<sup><a href="h5227.htm">5227</a></sup> and let thine eyelids<sup><a href="h6079.htm">6079</a></sup> look straight<sup><a href="h3474.htm">3474</a></sup> before<sup><a href="h5048.htm">5048</a></sup> thee. </p>
 <p>26  Ponder<sup><a href="h6424.htm">6424</a></sup> the path<sup><a href="h4570.htm">4570</a></sup> of thy feet,<sup><a href="h7272.htm">7272</a></sup> and let all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> thy ways<sup><a href="h1870.htm">1870</a></sup> be established.<sup><a href="h3559.htm">3559</a></sup> </p>
 <p>27  Turn<sup><a href="h5186.htm">5186</a></sup> not<sup><a href="h0408.htm">408</a></sup> to the right hand<sup><a href="h3225.htm">3225</a></sup> nor to the left:<sup><a href="h8040.htm">8040</a></sup> remove<sup><a href="h5493.htm">5493</a></sup> thy foot<sup><a href="h7272.htm">7272</a></sup> from evil.<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h7451.htm">7451</a></sup> </p>

 <p align="center"><a href="../sep/pro004.htm">Sep</a></p><p>1 ἀκούσατε παῖδες παιδείαν πατρὸς καὶ προσέχετε γνῶναι ἔννοιαν </p>

 <p>6 μηδὲ ἐγκαταλίπῃς αὐτήν καὶ ἀνθέξεταί σου ἐράσθητι αὐτῆς καὶ τηρήσει σε </p>
 <p>8 περιχαράκωσον αὐτήν καὶ ὑψώσει σε τίμησον αὐτήν ἵνα σε περιλάβῃ </p>
 <p>9 ἵνα δῷ τῇ σῇ κεφαλῇ στέφανον χαρίτων στεφάνῳ δὲ τρυφῆς ὑπερασπίσῃ σου </p>
 <p>10 ἄκουε υἱέ καὶ δέξαι ἐμοὺς λόγους καὶ πληθυνθήσεται ἔτη ζωῆς σου ἵνα σοι γένωνται πολλαὶ ὁδοὶ βίου </p>
 <p>11 ὁδοὺς γὰρ σοφίας διδάσκω σε ἐμβιβάζω δέ σε τροχιαῖς ὀρθαῖς </p>
 <p>12 ἐὰν γὰρ πορεύῃ οὐ συγκλεισθήσεταί σου τὰ διαβήματα ἐὰν δὲ τρέχῃς οὐ κοπιάσεις </p>
 <p>13 ἐπιλαβοῦ ἐμῆς παιδείας μὴ ἀφῇς ἀλλὰ φύλαξον αὐτὴν σεαυτῷ εἰς ζωήν σου </p>
 <p>14 ὁδοὺς ἀσεβῶν μὴ ἐπέλθῃς μηδὲ ζηλώσῃς ὁδοὺς παρανόμων </p>
 <p>15 ἐν ᾧ ἂν τόπῳ στρατοπεδεύσωσιν μὴ ἐπέλθῃς ἐκεῖ ἔκκλινον δὲ ἀπ᾽ αὐτῶν καὶ παράλλαξον </p>
 <p>16 οὐ γὰρ μὴ ὑπνώσωσιν ἐὰν μὴ κακοποιήσωσιν ἀφῄρηται ὁ ὕπνος αὐτῶν καὶ οὐ κοιμῶνται </p>
 <p>17 οἵδε γὰρ σιτοῦνται σῖτα ἀσεβείας οἴνῳ δὲ παρανόμῳ μεθύσκονται </p>
 <p>18 αἱ δὲ ὁδοὶ τῶν δικαίων ὁμοίως φωτὶ λάμπουσιν προπορεύονται καὶ φωτίζουσιν ἕως κατορθώσῃ ἡ ἡμέρα </p>
 <p>19 αἱ δὲ ὁδοὶ τῶν ἀσεβῶν σκοτειναί οὐκ οἴδασιν πῶς προσκόπτουσιν </p>
 <p>20 υἱέ ἐμῇ ῥήσει πρόσεχε τοῖς δὲ ἐμοῖς λόγοις παράβαλε σὸν οὖς </p>
 <p>21 ὅπως μὴ ἐκλίπωσίν σε αἱ πηγαί σου φύλασσε αὐτὰς ἐν σῇ καρδίᾳ </p>
 <p>22 ζωὴ γάρ ἐστιν τοῖς εὑρίσκουσιν αὐτὰς καὶ πάσῃ σαρκὶ ἴασις </p>
 <p>23 πάσῃ φυλακῇ τήρει σὴν καρδίαν ἐκ γὰρ τούτων ἔξοδοι ζωῆς </p>
 <p>24 περίελε σεαυτοῦ σκολιὸν στόμα καὶ ἄδικα χείλη μακρὰν ἀπὸ σοῦ ἄπωσαι </p>
 <p>25 οἱ ὀφθαλμοί σου ὀρθὰ βλεπέτωσαν τὰ δὲ βλέφαρά σου νευέτω δίκαια </p>
 <p>26 ὀρθὰς τροχιὰς ποίει σοῖς ποσὶν καὶ τὰς ὁδούς σου κατεύθυνε </p>
 <p>27 μὴ ἐκκλίνῃς εἰς τὰ δεξιὰ μηδὲ εἰς τὰ ἀριστερά ἀπόστρεψον δὲ σὸν πόδα ἀπὸ ὁδοῦ κακῆς  <br>

  αὐτὸς δὲ ὀρθὰς ποιήσει τὰς τροχιάς σου τὰς δὲ πορείας σου ἐν εἰρήνῃ προάξει </p>

 <p align="center"><a href="../vul/pro004.htm">Vul</a></p><p>1 [Audite, filii, disciplinam patris,<br> et attendite ut sciatis prudentiam.<br></p>
 <p>2 Donum bonum tribuam vobis:<br> legem meam ne derelinquatis.<br></p>
 <p>3 Nam et ego filius fui patris mei,<br> tenellus et unigenitus coram matre mea.<br></p>
 <p>4 Et docebat me, atque dicebat:<br> Suscipiat verba mea cor tuum;<br> custodi præcepta mea, et vives.<br></p>
 <p>5 Posside sapientiam, posside prudentiam:<br> ne obliviscaris, neque declines a verbis oris mei.<br></p>
 <p>6 Ne dimittas eam, et custodiet te:<br> dilige eam, et conservabit te.<br></p>
 <p>7 Principium sapientiæ: posside sapientiam,<br> et in omni possessione tua acquire prudentiam.<br></p>
 <p>8 Arripe illam, et exaltabit te;<br> glorificaberis ab ea cum eam fueris amplexatus.<br></p>
 <p>9 Dabit capiti tuo augmenta gratiarum,<br> et corona inclyta proteget te.]<br>
 </p>
 <p>10 [Audi, fili mi, et suscipe verba mea,<br> ut multiplicentur tibi anni vitæ.<br></p>
 <p>11 Viam sapientiæ monstrabo tibi;<br> ducam te per semitas æquitatis:<br></p>
 <p>12 quas cum ingressus fueris, non arctabuntur gressus tui,<br> et currens non habebis offendiculum.<br></p>
 <p>13 Tene disciplinam, ne dimittas eam;<br> custodi illam, quia ipsa est vita tua.<br></p>
 <p>14 Ne delecteris in semitis impiorum,<br> nec tibi placeat malorum via.<br></p>
 <p>15 Fuge ab ea, nec transeas per illam;<br> declina, et desere eam.<br></p>
 <p>16 Non enim dormiunt nisi malefecerint,<br> et rapitur somnus ab eis nisi supplantaverint.<br></p>
 <p>17 Comedunt panem impietatis,<br> et vinum iniquitatis bibunt.<br></p>
 <p>18 Justorum autem semita quasi lux splendens procedit,<br> et crescit usque ad perfectam diem.<br></p>
 <p>19 Via impiorum tenebrosa;<br> nesciunt ubi corruant.<br></p>
 <p>20 Fili mi, ausculta sermones meos,<br> et ad eloquia mea inclina aurem tuam.<br></p>
 <p>21 Ne recedant ab oculis tuis:<br> custodi ea in medio cordis tui:<br></p>
 <p>22 vita enim sunt invenientibus ea,<br> et universæ carni sanitas.<br></p>
 <p>23 Omni custodia serva cor tuum,<br> quia ex ipso vita procedit.<br></p>
 <p>24 Remove a te os pravum,<br> et detrahentia labia sint procul a te.<br></p>
 <p>25 Oculi tui recta videant,<br> et palpebræ tuæ præcedant gressus tuos.<br></p>
 <p>26 Dirige semitam pedibus tuis,<br> et omnes viæ tuæ stabilientur.<br></p>
 <p>27 Ne declines ad dexteram neque ad sinistram;<br> averte pedem tuum a malo:<br> vias enim quæ a dextris sunt novit Dominus:<br> perversæ vero sunt quæ a sinistris sunt.<br> Ipse autem rectos faciet cursus tuos,<br> itinera autem tua in pace producet.]</p>
