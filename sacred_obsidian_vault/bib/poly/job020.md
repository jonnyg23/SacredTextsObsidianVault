 <p align="center"><a href="../kjv/job020.htm">KJV</a></p><p>1  Then answered<sup><a href="h6030.htm">6030</a></sup> Zophar<sup><a href="h6691.htm">6691</a></sup> the Naamathite,<sup><a href="h5284.htm">5284</a></sup> and said,<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> </p>
 <p>2  Therefore<sup><a href="h3651.htm">3651</a></sup> do my thoughts<sup><a href="h5587.htm">5587</a></sup> cause me to answer,<sup><a href="h7725.htm">7725</a></sup> and for<sup><a href="h5668.htm">5668</a></sup> this I make haste.<sup><a href="h2363.htm">2363</a></sup> </p>

 <p>5  That<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> the triumphing<sup><a href="h7445.htm">7445</a></sup> of the wicked<sup><a href="h7563.htm">7563</a></sup> is short,<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h7138.htm">7138</a></sup> and the joy<sup><a href="h8057.htm">8057</a></sup> of the hypocrite<sup><a href="h2611.htm">2611</a></sup> but for<sup><a href="h5704.htm">5704</a></sup> a moment?<sup><a href="h7281.htm">7281</a></sup> </p>
 <p>6  Though<sup><a href="h0518.htm">518</a></sup> his excellency<sup><a href="h7863.htm">7863</a></sup> mount up<sup><a href="h5927.htm">5927</a></sup> to the heavens,<sup><a href="h8064.htm">8064</a></sup> and his head<sup><a href="h7218.htm">7218</a></sup> reach<sup><a href="h5060.htm">5060</a></sup> unto the clouds;<sup><a href="h5645.htm">5645</a></sup> </p>
 <p>7  Yet he shall perish<sup><a href="h0006.htm">6</a></sup> forever<sup><a href="h5331.htm">5331</a></sup> like his own dung:<sup><a href="h1561.htm">1561</a></sup> they which have seen<sup><a href="h7200.htm">7200</a></sup> him shall say,<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> Where<sup><a href="h0335.htm">335</a></sup> is he? </p>
 <p>8  He shall fly away<sup><a href="h5774.htm">5774</a></sup> as a dream,<sup><a href="h2472.htm">2472</a></sup> and shall not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> be found:<sup><a href="h4672.htm">4672</a></sup> yea, he shall be chased away<sup><a href="h5074.htm">5074</a></sup> as a vision<sup><a href="h2384.htm">2384</a></sup> of the night.<sup><a href="h3915.htm">3915</a></sup> </p>
 <p>9  The eye<sup><a href="h5869.htm">5869</a></sup> also which saw<sup><a href="h7805.htm">7805</a></sup> him shall see him no<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> more;<sup><a href="h3254.htm">3254</a></sup> neither<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> shall his place<sup><a href="h4725.htm">4725</a></sup> any more<sup><a href="h5750.htm">5750</a></sup> behold<sup><a href="h7789.htm">7789</a></sup> him. </p>
 <p>10  His children<sup><a href="h1121.htm">1121</a></sup> shall seek to please<sup><a href="h7521.htm">7521</a></sup> the poor,<sup><a href="h1800.htm">1800</a></sup> and his hands<sup><a href="h3027.htm">3027</a></sup> shall restore<sup><a href="h7725.htm">7725</a></sup> their goods.<sup><a href="h0202.htm">202</a></sup> </p>
 <p>11  His bones<sup><a href="h6106.htm">6106</a></sup> are full<sup><a href="h4390.htm">4390</a></sup> of the sin of his youth,<sup><a href="h5934.htm">5934</a></sup> which shall lie down<sup><a href="h7901.htm">7901</a></sup> with<sup><a href="h5973.htm">5973</a></sup> him in<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> the dust.<sup><a href="h6083.htm">6083</a></sup> </p>
 <p>12  Though<sup><a href="h0518.htm">518</a></sup> wickedness<sup><a href="h7451.htm">7451</a></sup> be sweet<sup><a href="h4985.htm">4985</a></sup> in his mouth,<sup><a href="h6310.htm">6310</a></sup> though he hide<sup><a href="h3582.htm">3582</a></sup> it under<sup><a href="h8478.htm">8478</a></sup> his tongue;<sup><a href="h3956.htm">3956</a></sup> </p>
 <p>13  Though he spare<sup><a href="h2550.htm">2550</a></sup> <sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> it, and forsake<sup><a href="h5800.htm">5800</a></sup> it not;<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> but keep it still<sup><a href="h4513.htm">4513</a></sup> within<sup><a href="h8432.htm">8432</a></sup> his mouth:<sup><a href="h2441.htm">2441</a></sup> </p>
 <p>14  Yet his meat<sup><a href="h3899.htm">3899</a></sup> in his bowels<sup><a href="h4578.htm">4578</a></sup> is turned,<sup><a href="h2015.htm">2015</a></sup> it is the gall<sup><a href="h4846.htm">4846</a></sup> of asps<sup><a href="h6620.htm">6620</a></sup> within<sup><a href="h7130.htm">7130</a></sup> him. </p>
 <p>15  He hath swallowed down<sup><a href="h1104.htm">1104</a></sup> riches,<sup><a href="h2428.htm">2428</a></sup> and he shall vomit them up again:<sup><a href="h6958.htm">6958</a></sup> God<sup><a href="h0410.htm">410</a></sup> shall cast them out<sup><a href="h3423.htm">3423</a></sup> of<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> his belly.<sup><a href="h0990.htm">990</a></sup> </p>
 <p>16  He shall suck<sup><a href="h3243.htm">3243</a></sup> the poison<sup><a href="h7219.htm">7219</a></sup> of asps:<sup><a href="h6620.htm">6620</a></sup> the viper's<sup><a href="h0660.htm">660</a></sup> tongue<sup><a href="h3956.htm">3956</a></sup> shall slay<sup><a href="h2026.htm">2026</a></sup> him. </p>
 <p>17  He shall not<sup><a href="h0408.htm">408</a></sup> see<sup><a href="h7200.htm">7200</a></sup> the rivers,<sup><a href="h6390.htm">6390</a></sup> the floods,<sup><a href="h5104.htm">5104</a></sup> the brooks<sup><a href="h5158.htm">5158</a></sup> of honey<sup><a href="h1706.htm">1706</a></sup> and butter.<sup><a href="h2529.htm">2529</a></sup> </p>
 <p>18  That which he labored for<sup><a href="h3022.htm">3022</a></sup> shall he restore,<sup><a href="h7725.htm">7725</a></sup> and shall not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> swallow it down:<sup><a href="h1104.htm">1104</a></sup> according to his substance<sup><a href="h2428.htm">2428</a></sup> shall the restitution<sup><a href="h8545.htm">8545</a></sup> be, and he shall not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> rejoice<sup><a href="h5965.htm">5965</a></sup> therein. </p>
 <p>19  Because<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> he hath oppressed<sup><a href="h7533.htm">7533</a></sup> and hath forsaken<sup><a href="h5800.htm">5800</a></sup> the poor;<sup><a href="h1800.htm">1800</a></sup> because he hath violently taken away<sup><a href="h1497.htm">1497</a></sup> a house<sup><a href="h1004.htm">1004</a></sup> which he built<sup><a href="h1129.htm">1129</a></sup> not;<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> </p>
 <p>20  Surely<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> he shall not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> feel<sup><a href="h3045.htm">3045</a></sup> quietness<sup><a href="h7961.htm">7961</a></sup> in his belly,<sup><a href="h0990.htm">990</a></sup> he shall not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> save<sup><a href="h4422.htm">4422</a></sup> of that which he desired.<sup><a href="h2530.htm">2530</a></sup> </p>
 <p>21  There shall none<sup><a href="h0369.htm">369</a></sup> of his meat<sup><a href="h0400.htm">400</a></sup> be left;<sup><a href="h8300.htm">8300</a></sup> therefore<sup><a href="h3651.htm">3651</a></sup> <sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> shall no<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> man look<sup><a href="h2342.htm">2342</a></sup> for his goods.<sup><a href="h2898.htm">2898</a></sup> </p>
 <p>22  In the fullness<sup><a href="h4390.htm">4390</a></sup> of his sufficiency<sup><a href="h5607.htm">5607</a></sup> he shall be in straits:<sup><a href="h3334.htm">3334</a></sup> every<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> hand<sup><a href="h3027.htm">3027</a></sup> of the wicked<sup><a href="h6001.htm">6001</a></sup> shall come<sup><a href="h0935.htm">935</a></sup> upon him. </p>
 <p>23  When he<sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> is about to fill<sup><a href="h4390.htm">4390</a></sup> his belly,<sup><a href="h0990.htm">990</a></sup> God shall cast<sup><a href="h7971.htm">7971</a></sup> the fury<sup><a href="h2740.htm">2740</a></sup> of his wrath<sup><a href="h0639.htm">639</a></sup> upon him, and shall rain<sup><a href="h4305.htm">4305</a></sup> it upon<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> him while he is eating.<sup><a href="h3894.htm">3894</a></sup> </p>
 <p>24  He shall flee<sup><a href="h1272.htm">1272</a></sup> from the iron<sup><a href="h1270.htm">1270</a></sup> weapon,<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h5402.htm">5402</a></sup> and the bow<sup><a href="h7198.htm">7198</a></sup> of steel<sup><a href="h5154.htm">5154</a></sup> shall strike him through.<sup><a href="h2498.htm">2498</a></sup> </p>
 <p>25  It is drawn,<sup><a href="h8025.htm">8025</a></sup> and cometh out<sup><a href="h3318.htm">3318</a></sup> of the body;<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h1465.htm">1465</a></sup> yea, the glittering sword<sup><a href="h1300.htm">1300</a></sup> cometh<sup><a href="h1980.htm">1980</a></sup> out of his gall:<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h4846.htm">4846</a></sup> terrors<sup><a href="h0367.htm">367</a></sup> are upon<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> him. </p>
 <p>26  All<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> darkness<sup><a href="h2822.htm">2822</a></sup> shall be hid<sup><a href="h2934.htm">2934</a></sup> in his secret places:<sup><a href="h6845.htm">6845</a></sup> a fire<sup><a href="h0784.htm">784</a></sup> not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> blown<sup><a href="h5301.htm">5301</a></sup> shall consume<sup><a href="h0398.htm">398</a></sup> him; it shall go ill<sup><a href="h7489.htm">7489</a></sup> with him that is left<sup><a href="h8300.htm">8300</a></sup> in his tabernacle.<sup><a href="h0168.htm">168</a></sup> </p>
 <p>27  The heaven<sup><a href="h8064.htm">8064</a></sup> shall reveal<sup><a href="h1540.htm">1540</a></sup> his iniquity;<sup><a href="h5771.htm">5771</a></sup> and the earth<sup><a href="h0776.htm">776</a></sup> shall rise up<sup><a href="h6965.htm">6965</a></sup> against him. </p>
 <p>28  The increase<sup><a href="h2981.htm">2981</a></sup> of his house<sup><a href="h1004.htm">1004</a></sup> shall depart,<sup><a href="h1540.htm">1540</a></sup> and his goods shall flow away<sup><a href="h5064.htm">5064</a></sup> in the day<sup><a href="h3117.htm">3117</a></sup> of his wrath.<sup><a href="h0639.htm">639</a></sup> </p>
 <p>29  This<sup><a href="h2088.htm">2088</a></sup> is the portion<sup><a href="h2506.htm">2506</a></sup> of a wicked<sup><a href="h7563.htm">7563</a></sup> man<sup><a href="h0120.htm">120</a></sup> from God,<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> and the heritage<sup><a href="h5159.htm">5159</a></sup> appointed<sup><a href="h0561.htm">561</a></sup> unto him by God.<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0410.htm">410</a></sup> </p>

 <p>7 ὅταν γὰρ δοκῇ ἤδη κατεστηρίχθαι τότε εἰς τέλος ἀπολεῖται οἱ δὲ ἰδόντες αὐτὸν ἐροῦσιν ποῦ ἐστιν </p>
 <p>8 ὥσπερ ἐνύπνιον ἐκπετασθὲν οὐ μὴ εὑρεθῇ ἔπτη δὲ ὥσπερ φάσμα νυκτερινόν </p>
 <p>9 ὀφθαλμὸς παρέβλεψεν καὶ οὐ προσθήσει καὶ οὐκέτι προσνοήσει αὐτὸν ὁ τόπος αὐτοῦ </p>

 <p>11 ὀστᾶ αὐτοῦ ἐνεπλήσθησαν νεότητος αὐτοῦ καὶ μετ᾽ αὐτοῦ ἐπὶ χώματος κοιμηθήσεται </p>
 <p>12 ἐὰν γλυκανθῇ ἐν στόματι αὐτοῦ κακία κρύψει αὐτὴν ὑπὸ τὴν γλῶσσαν αὐτοῦ </p>
 <p>13 οὐ φείσεται αὐτῆς καὶ οὐκ ἐγκαταλείψει αὐτὴν καὶ συνέξει αὐτὴν ἐν μέσῳ τοῦ λάρυγγος αὐτοῦ </p>
 <p>14 καὶ οὐ μὴ δυνηθῇ βοηθῆσαι ἑαυτῷ χολὴ ἀσπίδος ἐν γαστρὶ αὐτοῦ </p>
 <p>15 πλοῦτος ἀδίκως συναγόμενος ἐξεμεσθήσεται ἐξ οἰκίας αὐτοῦ ἐξελκύσει αὐτὸν ἄγγελος </p>
 <p>16 θυμὸν δὲ δρακόντων θηλάσειεν ἀνέλοι δὲ αὐτὸν γλῶσσα ὄφεως </p>
 <p>17 μὴ ἴδοι ἄμελξιν νομάδων μηδὲ νομὰς μέλιτος καὶ βουτύρου </p>
 <p>18 εἰς κενὰ καὶ μάταια ἐκοπίασεν πλοῦτον ἐξ οὗ οὐ γεύσεται ὥσπερ στρίφνος ἀμάσητος ἀκατάποτος </p>
 <p>19 πολλῶν γὰρ ἀδυνάτων οἴκους ἔθλασεν δίαιταν δὲ ἥρπασεν καὶ οὐκ ἔστησεν </p>
 <p>20 οὐκ ἔστιν αὐτοῦ σωτηρία τοῖς ὑπάρχουσιν ἐν ἐπιθυμίᾳ αὐτοῦ οὐ σωθήσεται </p>
 <p>21 οὐκ ἔστιν ὑπόλειμμα τοῖς βρώμασιν αὐτοῦ διὰ τοῦτο οὐκ ἀνθήσει αὐτοῦ τὰ ἀγαθά </p>
 <p>22 ὅταν δὲ δοκῇ ἤδη πεπληρῶσθαι θλιβήσεται πᾶσα δὲ ἀνάγκη ἐπ᾽ αὐτὸν ἐπελεύσεται </p>
 <p>23 εἴ πως πληρώσαι γαστέρα αὐτοῦ ἐπαποστείλαι ἐπ᾽ αὐτὸν θυμὸν ὀργῆς νίψαι ἐπ᾽ αὐτὸν ὀδύνας </p>
 <p>24 καὶ οὐ μὴ σωθῇ ἐκ χειρὸς σιδήρου τρώσαι αὐτὸν τόξον χάλκειον </p>
 <p>25 διεξέλθοι δὲ διὰ σώματος αὐτοῦ βέλος ἀστραπαὶ δὲ ἐν διαίταις αὐτοῦ περιπατήσαισαν ἐπ᾽ αὐτῷ φόβοι </p>
 <p>26 πᾶν δὲ σκότος αὐτῷ ὑπομείναι κατέδεται αὐτὸν πῦρ ἄκαυστον κακώσαι δὲ αὐτοῦ ἐπήλυτος τὸν οἶκον </p>
 <p>27 ἀνακαλύψαι δὲ αὐτοῦ ὁ οὐρανὸς τὰς ἀνομίας γῆ δὲ ἐπανασταίη αὐτῷ </p>
 <p>28 ἑλκύσαι τὸν οἶκον αὐτοῦ ἀπώλεια εἰς τέλος ἡμέρα ὀργῆς ἐπέλθοι αὐτῷ </p>
 <p>29 αὕτη ἡ μερὶς ἀνθρώπου ἀσεβοῦς παρὰ κυρίου καὶ κτῆμα ὑπαρχόντων αὐτῷ παρὰ τοῦ ἐπισκόπου </p>

 <p align="center"><a href="../vul/job020.htm">Vul</a></p><p>1 Respondens autem Sophar Naamathites, dixit:</p>
 <p>2 [Idcirco cogitationes meæ variæ succedunt sibi,<br> et mens in diversa rapitur.<br></p>
 <p>3 Doctrinam qua me arguis audiam,<br> et spiritus intelligentiæ meæ respondebit mihi.<br></p>
 <p>4 Hoc scio a principio,<br> ex quo positus est homo super terram,<br></p>
 <p>5 quod laus impiorum brevis sit,<br> et gaudium hypocritæ ad instar puncti.<br></p>
 <p>6 Si ascenderit usque ad cælum superbia ejus,<br> et caput ejus nubes tetigerit,<br></p>
 <p>7 quasi sterquilinium in fine perdetur,<br> et qui eum viderant, dicent: Ubi est?<br></p>
 <p>8 Velut somnium avolans non invenietur:<br> transiet sicut visio nocturna.<br></p>
 <p>9 Oculus qui eum viderat non videbit,<br> neque ultra intuebitur eum locus suus.<br></p>
 <p>10 Filii ejus atterentur egestate,<br> et manus illius reddent ei dolorem suum.<br></p>
 <p>11 Ossa ejus implebuntur vitiis adolescentiæ ejus,<br> et cum eo in pulvere dormient.<br></p>
 <p>12 Cum enim dulce fuerit in ore ejus malum,<br> abscondet illud sub lingua sua.<br></p>
 <p>13 Parcet illi, et non derelinquet illud,<br> et celabit in gutture suo.<br></p>
 <p>14 Panis ejus in utero illius<br> vertetur in fel aspidum intrinsecus.<br></p>
 <p>15 Divitias quas devoravit evomet,<br> et de ventre illius extrahet eas Deus.<br></p>
 <p>16 Caput aspidum suget,<br> et occidet eum lingua viperæ.<br></p>
 <p>17 (Non videat rivulos fluminis,<br> torrentes mellis et butyri.)<br></p>
 <p>18 Luet quæ fecit omnia, nec tamen consumetur:<br> juxta multitudinem adinventionum suarum, sic et sustinebit.<br></p>
 <p>19 Quoniam confringens nudavit pauperes:<br> domum rapuit, et non ædificavit eam.<br></p>
 <p>20 Nec est satiatus venter ejus:<br> et cum habuerit quæ concupierat, possidere non poterit.<br></p>
 <p>21 Non remansit de cibo ejus,<br> et propterea nihil permanebit de bonis ejus.<br></p>
 <p>22 Cum satiatus fuerit, arctabitur:<br> æstuabit, et omnis dolor irruet super eum.<br></p>
 <p>23 Utinam impleatur venter ejus,<br> ut emittat in eum iram furoris sui,<br> et pluat super illum bellum suum.<br></p>
 <p>24 Fugiet arma ferrea,<br> et irruet in arcum æreum.<br></p>
 <p>25 Eductus, et egrediens de vagina sua,<br> et fulgurans in amaritudine sua:<br> vadent et venient super eum horribiles.<br></p>
 <p>26 Omnes tenebræ absconditæ sunt in occultis ejus;<br> devorabit eum ignis qui non succenditur:<br> affligetur relictus in tabernaculo suo.<br></p>
 <p>27 Revelabunt cæli iniquitatem ejus,<br> et terra consurget adversus eum.<br></p>
 <p>28 Apertum erit germen domus illius:<br> detrahetur in die furoris Dei.<br></p>
 <p>29 Hæc est pars hominis impii a Deo,<br> et hæreditas verborum ejus a Domino.]</p>
