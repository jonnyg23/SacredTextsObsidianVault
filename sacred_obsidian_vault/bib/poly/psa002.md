 <p align="center"><a href="../kjv/psa002.htm">KJV</a></p><p>1  Why<sup><a href="h4100.htm">4100</a></sup> do the heathen<sup><a href="h1471.htm">1471</a></sup> rage,<sup><a href="h7283.htm">7283</a></sup> and the people<sup><a href="h3816.htm">3816</a></sup> imagine<sup><a href="h1897.htm">1897</a></sup> a vain thing?<sup><a href="h7385.htm">7385</a></sup> </p>
 <p>2  The kings<sup><a href="h4428.htm">4428</a></sup> of the earth<sup><a href="h0776.htm">776</a></sup> set themselves,<sup><a href="h3320.htm">3320</a></sup> and the rulers<sup><a href="h7336.htm">7336</a></sup> take counsel<sup><a href="h3245.htm">3245</a></sup> together,<sup><a href="h3162.htm">3162</a></sup> against<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> the LORD,<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> and against<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> his anointed,<sup><a href="h4899.htm">4899</a></sup> saying, </p>
 <p>3  Let us break their bands asunder,<sup><a href="h5423.htm">5423</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> <sup><a href="h4147.htm">4147</a></sup> and cast away<sup><a href="h7993.htm">7993</a></sup> their cords<sup><a href="h5688.htm">5688</a></sup> from<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> us. </p>
 <p>4  He that sitteth<sup><a href="h3427.htm">3427</a></sup> in the heavens<sup><a href="h8064.htm">8064</a></sup> shall laugh:<sup><a href="h7832.htm">7832</a></sup> the Lord<sup><a href="h0136.htm">136</a></sup> shall have them in derision.<sup><a href="h3932.htm">3932</a></sup> </p>
 <p>5  Then<sup><a href="h0227.htm">227</a></sup> shall he speak<sup><a href="h1696.htm">1696</a></sup> unto<sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> them in his wrath,<sup><a href="h0639.htm">639</a></sup> and vex<sup><a href="h0926.htm">926</a></sup> them in his sore displeasure.<sup><a href="h2740.htm">2740</a></sup> </p>
 <p>6  Yet have I<sup><a href="h0589.htm">589</a></sup> set<sup><a href="h5258.htm">5258</a></sup> my king<sup><a href="h4428.htm">4428</a></sup> upon<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> my holy<sup><a href="h6944.htm">6944</a></sup> hill<sup><a href="h2022.htm">2022</a></sup> of Zion.<sup><a href="h6726.htm">6726</a></sup> </p>
 <p>7  I will declare<sup><a href="h5608.htm">5608</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> the decree:<sup><a href="h2706.htm">2706</a></sup> the LORD<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> hath said<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> unto<sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> me, Thou<sup><a href="h0859.htm">859</a></sup> art my Son;<sup><a href="h1121.htm">1121</a></sup> this day<sup><a href="h3117.htm">3117</a></sup> have I<sup><a href="h0589.htm">589</a></sup> begotten<sup><a href="h3205.htm">3205</a></sup> thee. </p>
 <p>8  Ask<sup><a href="h7592.htm">7592</a></sup> of<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> me, and I shall give<sup><a href="h5414.htm">5414</a></sup> thee the heathen<sup><a href="h1471.htm">1471</a></sup> for thine inheritance,<sup><a href="h5159.htm">5159</a></sup> and the uttermost parts<sup><a href="h0657.htm">657</a></sup> of the earth<sup><a href="h0776.htm">776</a></sup> for thy possession.<sup><a href="h0272.htm">272</a></sup> </p>
 <p>9  Thou shalt break<sup><a href="h7489.htm">7489</a></sup> them with a rod<sup><a href="h7626.htm">7626</a></sup> of iron;<sup><a href="h1270.htm">1270</a></sup> thou shalt dash them in pieces<sup><a href="h5310.htm">5310</a></sup> like a potter's<sup><a href="h3335.htm">3335</a></sup> vessel.<sup><a href="h3627.htm">3627</a></sup> </p>
 <p>10  Be wise<sup><a href="h7919.htm">7919</a></sup> now<sup><a href="h6258.htm">6258</a></sup> therefore, O ye kings:<sup><a href="h4428.htm">4428</a></sup> be instructed,<sup><a href="h3256.htm">3256</a></sup> ye judges<sup><a href="h8199.htm">8199</a></sup> of the earth.<sup><a href="h0776.htm">776</a></sup> </p>
 <p>11  Serve<sup><a href="h5647.htm">5647</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> the LORD<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> with fear,<sup><a href="h3374.htm">3374</a></sup> and rejoice<sup><a href="h1523.htm">1523</a></sup> with trembling.<sup><a href="h7461.htm">7461</a></sup> </p>
 <p>12  Kiss<sup><a href="h5401.htm">5401</a></sup> the Son,<sup><a href="h1248.htm">1248</a></sup> lest<sup><a href="h6435.htm">6435</a></sup> he be angry,<sup><a href="h0599.htm">599</a></sup> and ye perish<sup><a href="h0006.htm">6</a></sup> from the way,<sup><a href="h1870.htm">1870</a></sup> when<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> his wrath<sup><a href="h0639.htm">639</a></sup> is kindled<sup><a href="h1197.htm">1197</a></sup> but a little.<sup><a href="h4592.htm">4592</a></sup> Blessed<sup><a href="h0835.htm">835</a></sup> are all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> they that put their trust<sup><a href="h2620.htm">2620</a></sup> in him. </p>

 <p>8 αἴτησαι παρ᾽ ἐμοῦ καὶ δώσω σοι ἔθνη τὴν κληρονομίαν σου καὶ τὴν κατάσχεσίν σου τὰ πέρατα τῆς γῆς </p>
 <p>9 ποιμανεῖς αὐτοὺς ἐν ῥάβδῳ σιδηρᾷ ὡς σκεῦος κεραμέως συντρίψεις αὐτούς </p>

 <p>12 δράξασθε παιδείας μήποτε ὀργισθῇ κύριος καὶ ἀπολεῖσθε ἐξ ὁδοῦ δικαίας ὅταν ἐκκαυθῇ ἐν τάχει ὁ θυμὸς αὐτοῦ μακάριοι πάντες οἱ πεποιθότες ἐπ᾽ αὐτῷ </p>
