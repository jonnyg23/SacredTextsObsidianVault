  <p>H6999<br>  קטר  <br> קָטַר  ‎  qâṭar  <br><i>kaw-tar‘ </i><br>A primitive root (rather identical with <a href="h7000.htm">7000</a> through the idea of fumigation in a <i>close</i> place and perhaps thus <i>driving</i> out the occupants); to <i>smoke</i>, that is, turn into fragrance by fire (especially as an act of worship): - burn (incense, sacrifice) (upon), (altar for) incense, kindle, offer (incense, a sacrifice).<br></p>
