 <p>5  Then<sup><a href="h0227.htm">227</a></sup> the eyes<sup><a href="h5869.htm">5869</a></sup> of the blind<sup><a href="h5787.htm">5787</a></sup> shall be opened,<sup><a href="h6491.htm">6491</a></sup> and the ears<sup><a href="h0241.htm">241</a></sup> of the deaf<sup><a href="h2795.htm">2795</a></sup> shall be unstopped.<sup><a href="h6605.htm">6605</a></sup> </p>

 <p>7  And the parched ground<sup><a href="h8273.htm">8273</a></sup> shall become<sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> a pool,<sup><a href="h0098.htm">98</a></sup> and the thirsty land<sup><a href="h6774.htm">6774</a></sup> springs<sup><a href="h4002.htm">4002</a></sup> of water:<sup><a href="h4325.htm">4325</a></sup> in the habitation<sup><a href="h5116.htm">5116</a></sup> of dragons,<sup><a href="h8565.htm">8565</a></sup> where each lay,<sup><a href="h7258.htm">7258</a></sup> shall be grass<sup><a href="h2682.htm">2682</a></sup> with reeds<sup><a href="h7070.htm">7070</a></sup> and rushes.<sup><a href="h1573.htm">1573</a></sup> </p>
 <p>8  And a highway<sup><a href="h4547.htm">4547</a></sup> shall be<sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> there,<sup><a href="h8033.htm">8033</a></sup> and a way,<sup><a href="h1870.htm">1870</a></sup> and it shall be called<sup><a href="h7121.htm">7121</a></sup> The way<sup><a href="h1870.htm">1870</a></sup> of holiness;<sup><a href="h6944.htm">6944</a></sup> the unclean<sup><a href="h2931.htm">2931</a></sup> shall not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> pass over<sup><a href="h5674.htm">5674</a></sup> it; but it<sup><a href="h1931.htm">1931</a></sup> shall be for those: the wayfaring men,<sup><a href="h1980.htm">1980</a></sup> <sup><a href="h1870.htm">1870</a></sup> though fools,<sup><a href="h0191.htm">191</a></sup> shall not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> err<sup><a href="h8582.htm">8582</a></sup> therein. </p>
 <p>9  No<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> lion<sup><a href="h0738.htm">738</a></sup> shall be<sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> there,<sup><a href="h8033.htm">8033</a></sup> nor<sup><a href="h1077.htm">1077</a></sup> any ravenous<sup><a href="h6530.htm">6530</a></sup> beast<sup><a href="h2416.htm">2416</a></sup> shall go up<sup><a href="h5927.htm">5927</a></sup> thereon, it shall not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> be found<sup><a href="h4672.htm">4672</a></sup> there;<sup><a href="h8033.htm">8033</a></sup> but the redeemed<sup><a href="h1350.htm">1350</a></sup> shall walk<sup><a href="h1980.htm">1980</a></sup> there: </p>

 <p>2 καὶ ἐξανθήσει καὶ ἀγαλλιάσεται τὰ ἔρημα τοῦ Ιορδάνου καὶ ἡ δόξα τοῦ Λιβάνου ἐδόθη αὐτῇ καὶ ἡ τιμὴ τοῦ Καρμήλου καὶ ὁ λαός μου ὄψεται τὴν δόξαν κυρίου καὶ τὸ ὕψος τοῦ θεοῦ </p>
 <p>3 ἰσχύσατε χεῖρες ἀνειμέναι καὶ γόνατα παραλελυμένα </p>
 <p>4 παρακαλέσατε οἱ ὀλιγόψυχοι τῇ διανοίᾳ ἰσχύσατε μὴ φοβεῖσθε ἰδοὺ ὁ θεὸς ἡμῶν κρίσιν ἀνταποδίδωσιν καὶ ἀνταποδώσει αὐτὸς ἥξει καὶ σώσει ἡμᾶς </p>
 <p>5 τότε ἀνοιχθήσονται ὀφθαλμοὶ τυφλῶν καὶ ὦτα κωφῶν ἀκούσονται </p>
 <p>6 τότε ἁλεῖται ὡς ἔλαφος ὁ χωλός καὶ τρανὴ ἔσται γλῶσσα μογιλάλων ὅτι ἐρράγη ἐν τῇ ἐρήμῳ ὕδωρ καὶ φάραγξ ἐν γῇ διψώσῃ </p>
 <p>7 καὶ ἡ ἄνυδρος ἔσται εἰς ἕλη καὶ εἰς τὴν διψῶσαν γῆν πηγὴ ὕδατος ἔσται ἐκεῖ εὐφροσύνη ὀρνέων ἔπαυλις καλάμου καὶ ἕλη </p>
 <p>8 ἐκεῖ ἔσται ὁδὸς καθαρὰ καὶ ὁδὸς ἁγία κληθήσεται καὶ οὐ μὴ παρέλθῃ ἐκεῖ ἀκάθαρτος οὐδὲ ἔσται ἐκεῖ ὁδὸς ἀκάθαρτος οἱ δὲ διεσπαρμένοι πορεύσονται ἐπ᾽ αὐτῆς καὶ οὐ μὴ πλανηθῶσιν </p>
 <p>9 καὶ οὐκ ἔσται ἐκεῖ λέων οὐδὲ τῶν θηρίων τῶν πονηρῶν οὐ μὴ ἀναβῇ ἐπ᾽ αὐτὴν οὐδὲ μὴ εὑρεθῇ ἐκεῖ ἀλλὰ πορεύσονται ἐν αὐτῇ λελυτρωμένοι </p>
 <p>10 καὶ συνηγμένοι διὰ κύριον ἀποστραφήσονται καὶ ἥξουσιν εἰς Σιων μετ᾽ εὐφροσύνης καὶ εὐφροσύνη αἰώνιος ὑπὲρ κεφαλῆς αὐτῶν ἐπὶ γὰρ κεφαλῆς αὐτῶν αἴνεσις καὶ ἀγαλλίαμα καὶ εὐφροσύνη καταλήμψεται αὐτούς ἀπέδρα ὀδύνη καὶ λύπη καὶ στεναγμός </p>

 <p align="center"><a href="../vul/isa035.htm">Vul</a></p><p>1 [Lætabitur deserta et invia,<br> et exsultabit solitudo,<br> et florebit quasi lilium.<br></p>
 <p>2 Germinans germinabit, et exsultabit<br> lætabunda et laudans:<br> gloria Libani data est ei,<br> decor Carmeli et Saron;<br> ipsi videbunt gloriam Domini,<br> et decorem Dei nostri.<br></p>
 <p>3 Confortate manus dissolutas,<br> et genua debilia roborate.<br></p>
 <p>4 Dicite pusillanimis:<br> Confortamini, et nolite timere:<br> ecce Deus vester ultionem adducet retributionis;<br> Deus ipse veniet, et salvabit vos.<br></p>
 <p>5 Tunc aperientur oculi cæcorum,<br> et aures surdorum patebunt;<br></p>
 <p>6 tunc saliet sicut cervus claudus,<br> et aperta erit lingua mutorum:<br> quia scissæ sunt in deserto aquæ,<br> et torrentes in solitudine;<br></p>
 <p>7 et quæ erat arida, erit in stagnum,<br> et sitiens in fontes aquarum.<br> In cubilibus, in quibus prius dracones habitabant,<br> orietur viror calami et junci.<br></p>
 <p>8 Et erit ibi semita et via,<br> et via sancta vocabitur:<br> non transibit per eam pollutus,<br> et hæc erit vobis directa via,<br> ita ut stulti non errent per eam.<br></p>
 <p>9 Non erit ibi leo,<br> et mala bestia non ascendet per eam,<br> nec invenietur ibi;<br> et ambulabunt qui liberati fuerint.<br></p>
 <p>10 Et redempti a Domino convertentur,<br> et venient in Sion cum laude,<br> et lætitia sempiterna super caput eorum:<br> gaudium et lætitiam obtinebunt,<br> et fugiet dolor et gemitus.]</p>
