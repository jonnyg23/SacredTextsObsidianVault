  <p>H4994<br>  נא  <br> נָא  ‎  nâ‘  <br><i>naw </i><br>A primitive particle of incitement and entreaty, which may usually be rendered I <i>pray</i>, <i>now</i> or <i>then</i>; added mostly to verbs (in the imperative or future), or to interjections, occasionally to an adverb or conjugation: - I beseech (pray) thee (you), go to, now, oh.<br></p>
