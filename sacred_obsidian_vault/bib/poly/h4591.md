  <p>H4591<br>  מעט  <br> מָעַט  ‎  mâ‛aṭ  <br><i>maw-at‘ </i><br>A primitive root; properly to <i>pare</i> off, that is, <i>lessen</i>; intransitively to <i>be</i> (or causatively to <i>make</i>) <i>small</i> or <i>few</i> (or figuratively <i>ineffective</i>): - suffer to decrease, diminish, (be, X borrow a, give, make) few (in number, -ness), gather least (little), be (seem) little, (X give the) less, be minished, bring to nothing.<br></p>
