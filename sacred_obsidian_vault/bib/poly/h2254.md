  <p>H2254<br>  חבל  <br> חָבַל  ‎  châbal  <br><i>khaw-bal‘ </i><br>A primitive root; to <i>wind</i> tightly (as a rope), that is, to <i>bind</i>; specifically by a <i>pledge</i>; figuratively to <i>pervert</i>, <i>destroy</i>; also to <i>writhe</i> in pain (especially of parturition): -  X at all, band, bring forth, (deal) corrupt (-ly) destroy, offend, lay to (take a) pledge, spoil, travail, X very, withhold.<br></p>
