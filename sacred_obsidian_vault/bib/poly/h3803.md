  <p>H3803<br>  כּתר  <br> כָּתַר  ‎  kâthar  <br><i>kaw-thar‘ </i><br>A primitive root; to <i>enclose</i>; hence (in a friendly sense) to <i>crown</i>, (in a hostile one) to <i>besiege</i>; also to <i>wait</i> (as restraining oneself): - beset round, compass about, be crowned inclose round, suffer.<br></p>
