 <h3 align="CENTER">Proverbs 11</h3><table border="1">
 <tr>
 <td valign="top">
 <p align="center"><a href="../kjv/pro011.htm">KJV</a></p><p>1  A false<sup><a href="h4820.htm">4820</a></sup> balance<sup><a href="h3976.htm">3976</a></sup> is abomination<sup><a href="h8441.htm">8441</a></sup> to the LORD:<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> but a just<sup><a href="h8003.htm">8003</a></sup> weight<sup><a href="h0068.htm">68</a></sup> is his delight.<sup><a href="h7522.htm">7522</a></sup> </p>
 <p>2  When pride<sup><a href="h2087.htm">2087</a></sup> cometh,<sup><a href="h0935.htm">935</a></sup> then cometh<sup><a href="h0935.htm">935</a></sup> shame:<sup><a href="h7036.htm">7036</a></sup> but with<sup><a href="h0854.htm">854</a></sup> the lowly<sup><a href="h6800.htm">6800</a></sup> is wisdom.<sup><a href="h2451.htm">2451</a></sup> </p>
 <p>3  The integrity<sup><a href="h8538.htm">8538</a></sup> of the upright<sup><a href="h3477.htm">3477</a></sup> shall guide<sup><a href="h5148.htm">5148</a></sup> them: but the perverseness<sup><a href="h5558.htm">5558</a></sup> of transgressors<sup><a href="h0898.htm">898</a></sup> shall destroy<sup><a href="h7703.htm">7703</a></sup> them. </p>
 <p>4  Riches<sup><a href="h1952.htm">1952</a></sup> profit<sup><a href="h3276.htm">3276</a></sup> not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> in the day<sup><a href="h3117.htm">3117</a></sup> of wrath:<sup><a href="h5678.htm">5678</a></sup> but righteousness<sup><a href="h6666.htm">6666</a></sup> delivereth<sup><a href="h5337.htm">5337</a></sup> from death.<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h4194.htm">4194</a></sup> </p>
 <p>5  The righteousness<sup><a href="h6666.htm">6666</a></sup> of the perfect<sup><a href="h8549.htm">8549</a></sup> shall direct<sup><a href="h3474.htm">3474</a></sup> his way:<sup><a href="h1870.htm">1870</a></sup> but the wicked<sup><a href="h7563.htm">7563</a></sup> shall fall<sup><a href="h5307.htm">5307</a></sup> by his own wickedness.<sup><a href="h7564.htm">7564</a></sup> </p>
 <p>6  The righteousness<sup><a href="h6666.htm">6666</a></sup> of the upright<sup><a href="h3477.htm">3477</a></sup> shall deliver<sup><a href="h5337.htm">5337</a></sup> them: but transgressors<sup><a href="h0898.htm">898</a></sup> shall be taken<sup><a href="h3920.htm">3920</a></sup> in their own naughtiness.<sup><a href="h1942.htm">1942</a></sup> </p>
 <p>7  When a wicked<sup><a href="h7563.htm">7563</a></sup> man<sup><a href="h0120.htm">120</a></sup> dieth,<sup><a href="h4194.htm">4194</a></sup> his expectation<sup><a href="h8615.htm">8615</a></sup> shall perish:<sup><a href="h0006.htm">6</a></sup> and the hope<sup><a href="h8431.htm">8431</a></sup> of unjust<sup><a href="h0205.htm">205</a></sup> men perisheth.<sup><a href="h0006.htm">6</a></sup> </p>
 <p>8  The righteous<sup><a href="h6662.htm">6662</a></sup> is delivered<sup><a href="h2502.htm">2502</a></sup> out of trouble,<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h6869.htm">6869</a></sup> and the wicked<sup><a href="h7563.htm">7563</a></sup> cometh<sup><a href="h0935.htm">935</a></sup> in his stead.<sup><a href="h8478.htm">8478</a></sup> </p>
 <p>9  A hypocrite<sup><a href="h2611.htm">2611</a></sup> with his mouth<sup><a href="h6310.htm">6310</a></sup> destroyeth<sup><a href="h7843.htm">7843</a></sup> his neighbor:<sup><a href="h7453.htm">7453</a></sup> but through knowledge<sup><a href="h1847.htm">1847</a></sup> shall the just<sup><a href="h6662.htm">6662</a></sup> be delivered.<sup><a href="h2502.htm">2502</a></sup> </p>
 <p>10  When it goeth well<sup><a href="h2898.htm">2898</a></sup> with the righteous,<sup><a href="h6662.htm">6662</a></sup> the city<sup><a href="h7151.htm">7151</a></sup> rejoiceth:<sup><a href="h5970.htm">5970</a></sup> and when the wicked<sup><a href="h7563.htm">7563</a></sup> perish,<sup><a href="h0006.htm">6</a></sup> there is shouting.<sup><a href="h7440.htm">7440</a></sup> </p>
 <p>11  By the blessing<sup><a href="h1293.htm">1293</a></sup> of the upright<sup><a href="h3477.htm">3477</a></sup> the city<sup><a href="h7176.htm">7176</a></sup> is exalted:<sup><a href="h7311.htm">7311</a></sup> but it is overthrown<sup><a href="h2040.htm">2040</a></sup> by the mouth<sup><a href="h6310.htm">6310</a></sup> of the wicked.<sup><a href="h7563.htm">7563</a></sup> </p>
 <p>12  He that is void<sup><a href="h2638.htm">2638</a></sup> of wisdom<sup><a href="h3820.htm">3820</a></sup> despiseth<sup><a href="h0936.htm">936</a></sup> his neighbor:<sup><a href="h7453.htm">7453</a></sup> but a man<sup><a href="h0376.htm">376</a></sup> of understanding<sup><a href="h8394.htm">8394</a></sup> holdeth his peace.<sup><a href="h2790.htm">2790</a></sup> </p>
 <p>13  A talebearer<sup><a href="h1980.htm">1980</a></sup> <sup><a href="h7400.htm">7400</a></sup> revealeth<sup><a href="h1540.htm">1540</a></sup> secrets:<sup><a href="h5475.htm">5475</a></sup> but he that is of a faithful<sup><a href="h0539.htm">539</a></sup> spirit<sup><a href="h7307.htm">7307</a></sup> concealeth<sup><a href="h3680.htm">3680</a></sup> the matter.<sup><a href="h1697.htm">1697</a></sup> </p>
 <p>14  Where no<sup><a href="h0369.htm">369</a></sup> counsel<sup><a href="h8458.htm">8458</a></sup> is, the people<sup><a href="h5971.htm">5971</a></sup> fall:<sup><a href="h5307.htm">5307</a></sup> but in the multitude<sup><a href="h7230.htm">7230</a></sup> of counselors<sup><a href="h3289.htm">3289</a></sup> there is safety.<sup><a href="h8668.htm">8668</a></sup> </p>
 <p>15  He that is surety<sup><a href="h6148.htm">6148</a></sup> for a stranger<sup><a href="h2114.htm">2114</a></sup> shall smart<sup><a href="h7451.htm">7451</a></sup> <sup><a href="h7489.htm">7489</a></sup> for it: and he that hateth<sup><a href="h8130.htm">8130</a></sup> suretiship<sup><a href="h8628.htm">8628</a></sup> is sure.<sup><a href="h0982.htm">982</a></sup> </p>
 <p>16  A gracious<sup><a href="h2580.htm">2580</a></sup> woman<sup><a href="h0802.htm">802</a></sup> retaineth<sup><a href="h8551.htm">8551</a></sup> honor:<sup><a href="h3519.htm">3519</a></sup> and strong<sup><a href="h6184.htm">6184</a></sup> men retain<sup><a href="h8551.htm">8551</a></sup> riches.<sup><a href="h6239.htm">6239</a></sup> </p>
 <p>17  The merciful<sup><a href="h2617.htm">2617</a></sup> man<sup><a href="h0376.htm">376</a></sup> doeth good<sup><a href="h1580.htm">1580</a></sup> to his own soul:<sup><a href="h5315.htm">5315</a></sup> but he that is cruel<sup><a href="h0394.htm">394</a></sup> troubleth<sup><a href="h5916.htm">5916</a></sup> his own flesh.<sup><a href="h7607.htm">7607</a></sup> </p>
 <p>18  The wicked<sup><a href="h7563.htm">7563</a></sup> worketh<sup><a href="h6213.htm">6213</a></sup> a deceitful<sup><a href="h8267.htm">8267</a></sup> work:<sup><a href="h6468.htm">6468</a></sup> but to him that soweth<sup><a href="h2232.htm">2232</a></sup> righteousness<sup><a href="h6666.htm">6666</a></sup> shall be a sure<sup><a href="h0571.htm">571</a></sup> reward.<sup><a href="h7938.htm">7938</a></sup> </p>
 <p>19  As<sup><a href="h3651.htm">3651</a></sup> righteousness<sup><a href="h6666.htm">6666</a></sup> tendeth to life:<sup><a href="h2416.htm">2416</a></sup> so he that pursueth<sup><a href="h7291.htm">7291</a></sup> evil<sup><a href="h7451.htm">7451</a></sup> pursueth it to his own death.<sup><a href="h4194.htm">4194</a></sup> </p>
 <p>20  They that are of a froward<sup><a href="h6141.htm">6141</a></sup> heart<sup><a href="h3820.htm">3820</a></sup> are abomination<sup><a href="h8441.htm">8441</a></sup> to the LORD:<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> but such as are upright<sup><a href="h8549.htm">8549</a></sup> in their way<sup><a href="h1870.htm">1870</a></sup> are his delight.<sup><a href="h7522.htm">7522</a></sup> </p>
 <p>21  Though hand<sup><a href="h3027.htm">3027</a></sup> join in hand,<sup><a href="h3027.htm">3027</a></sup> the wicked<sup><a href="h7451.htm">7451</a></sup> shall not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> be unpunished:<sup><a href="h5352.htm">5352</a></sup> but the seed<sup><a href="h2233.htm">2233</a></sup> of the righteous<sup><a href="h6662.htm">6662</a></sup> shall be delivered.<sup><a href="h4422.htm">4422</a></sup> </p>
 <p>22  As a jewel<sup><a href="h5141.htm">5141</a></sup> of gold<sup><a href="h2091.htm">2091</a></sup> in a swine's<sup><a href="h2386.htm">2386</a></sup> snout,<sup><a href="h0639.htm">639</a></sup> so is a fair<sup><a href="h3303.htm">3303</a></sup> woman<sup><a href="h0802.htm">802</a></sup> which is without<sup><a href="h5493.htm">5493</a></sup> discretion.<sup><a href="h2940.htm">2940</a></sup> </p>
 <p>23  The desire<sup><a href="h8378.htm">8378</a></sup> of the righteous<sup><a href="h6662.htm">6662</a></sup> is only<sup><a href="h0389.htm">389</a></sup> good:<sup><a href="h2896.htm">2896</a></sup> but the expectation<sup><a href="h8615.htm">8615</a></sup> of the wicked<sup><a href="h7563.htm">7563</a></sup> is wrath.<sup><a href="h5678.htm">5678</a></sup> </p>
 <p>24  There is<sup><a href="h3426.htm">3426</a></sup> that scattereth,<sup><a href="h6340.htm">6340</a></sup> and yet<sup><a href="h5750.htm">5750</a></sup> increaseth;<sup><a href="h3254.htm">3254</a></sup> and there is that withholdeth<sup><a href="h2820.htm">2820</a></sup> more than is meet,<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3476.htm">3476</a></sup> but it tendeth to poverty.<sup><a href="h4270.htm">4270</a></sup> </p>
 <p>25  The liberal<sup><a href="h1293.htm">1293</a></sup> soul<sup><a href="h5315.htm">5315</a></sup> shall be made fat:<sup><a href="h1878.htm">1878</a></sup> and he that watereth<sup><a href="h7301.htm">7301</a></sup> shall be watered<sup><a href="h3384.htm">3384</a></sup> also<sup><a href="h1571.htm">1571</a></sup> himself.<sup><a href="h1931.htm">1931</a></sup> </p>
 <p>26  He that withholdeth<sup><a href="h4513.htm">4513</a></sup> corn,<sup><a href="h1250.htm">1250</a></sup> the people<sup><a href="h3816.htm">3816</a></sup> shall curse<sup><a href="h6895.htm">6895</a></sup> him: but blessing<sup><a href="h1293.htm">1293</a></sup> shall be upon the head<sup><a href="h7218.htm">7218</a></sup> of him that selleth<sup><a href="h7666.htm">7666</a></sup> it. </p>
 <p>27  He that diligently seeketh<sup><a href="h7836.htm">7836</a></sup> good<sup><a href="h2896.htm">2896</a></sup> procureth<sup><a href="h1245.htm">1245</a></sup> favor:<sup><a href="h7522.htm">7522</a></sup> but he that seeketh<sup><a href="h1875.htm">1875</a></sup> mischief,<sup><a href="h7451.htm">7451</a></sup> it shall come<sup><a href="h0935.htm">935</a></sup> unto him. </p>
 <p>28  He<sup><a href="h1931.htm">1931</a></sup> that trusteth<sup><a href="h0982.htm">982</a></sup> in his riches<sup><a href="h6239.htm">6239</a></sup> shall fall:<sup><a href="h5307.htm">5307</a></sup> but the righteous<sup><a href="h6662.htm">6662</a></sup> shall flourish<sup><a href="h6524.htm">6524</a></sup> as a branch.<sup><a href="h5929.htm">5929</a></sup> </p>
 <p>29  He that troubleth<sup><a href="h5916.htm">5916</a></sup> his own house<sup><a href="h1004.htm">1004</a></sup> shall inherit<sup><a href="h5157.htm">5157</a></sup> the wind:<sup><a href="h7307.htm">7307</a></sup> and the fool<sup><a href="h0191.htm">191</a></sup> shall be servant<sup><a href="h5650.htm">5650</a></sup> to the wise<sup><a href="h2450.htm">2450</a></sup> of heart.<sup><a href="h3820.htm">3820</a></sup> </p>
 <p>30  The fruit<sup><a href="h6529.htm">6529</a></sup> of the righteous<sup><a href="h6662.htm">6662</a></sup> is a tree<sup><a href="h6086.htm">6086</a></sup> of life;<sup><a href="h2416.htm">2416</a></sup> and he that winneth<sup><a href="h3947.htm">3947</a></sup> souls<sup><a href="h5315.htm">5315</a></sup> is wise.<sup><a href="h2450.htm">2450</a></sup> </p>
 <p>31  Behold,<sup><a href="h2005.htm">2005</a></sup> the righteous<sup><a href="h6662.htm">6662</a></sup> shall be recompensed<sup><a href="h7999.htm">7999</a></sup> in the earth:<sup><a href="h0776.htm">776</a></sup> much more<sup><a href="h0637.htm">637</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> the wicked<sup><a href="h7563.htm">7563</a></sup> and the sinner.<sup><a href="h2398.htm">2398</a></sup> </p>

 <p align="center"><a href="../sep/pro011.htm">Sep</a></p><p>1 ζυγοὶ δόλιοι βδέλυγμα ἐνώπιον κυρίου στάθμιον δὲ δίκαιον δεκτὸν αὐτῷ </p>
 <p>2 οὗ ἐὰν εἰσέλθῃ ὕβρις ἐκεῖ καὶ ἀτιμία στόμα δὲ ταπεινῶν μελετᾷ σοφίαν </p>
 <p>3 ἀποθανὼν δίκαιος ἔλιπεν μετάμελον πρόχειρος δὲ γίνεται καὶ ἐπίχαρτος ἀσεβῶν ἀπώλεια </p>
 <p>5 δικαιοσύνη ἀμώμους ὀρθοτομεῖ ὁδούς ἀσέβεια δὲ περιπίπτει ἀδικίᾳ </p>
 <p>6 δικαιοσύνη ἀνδρῶν ὀρθῶν ῥύεται αὐτούς τῇ δὲ ἀπωλείᾳ αὐτῶν ἁλίσκονται παράνομοι </p>
 <p>7 τελευτήσαντος ἀνδρὸς δικαίου οὐκ ὄλλυται ἐλπίς τὸ δὲ καύχημα τῶν ἀσεβῶν ὄλλυται </p>
 <p>8 δίκαιος ἐκ θήρας ἐκδύνει ἀντ᾽ αὐτοῦ δὲ παραδίδοται ὁ ἀσεβής </p>
 <p>9 ἐν στόματι ἀσεβῶν παγὶς πολίταις αἴσθησις δὲ δικαίων εὔοδος </p>
 <p>10 ἐν ἀγαθοῖς δικαίων κατώρθωσεν πόλις </p>
 <p>11 στόμασιν δὲ ἀσεβῶν κατεσκάφη </p>
 <p>12 μυκτηρίζει πολίτας ἐνδεὴς φρενῶν ἀνὴρ δὲ φρόνιμος ἡσυχίαν ἄγει </p>
 <p>13 ἀνὴρ δίγλωσσος ἀποκαλύπτει βουλὰς ἐν συνεδρίῳ πιστὸς δὲ πνοῇ κρύπτει πράγματα </p>
 <p>14 οἷς μὴ ὑπάρχει κυβέρνησις πίπτουσιν ὥσπερ φύλλα σωτηρία δὲ ὑπάρχει ἐν πολλῇ βουλῇ </p>
 <p>15 πονηρὸς κακοποιεῖ ὅταν συμμείξῃ δικαίῳ μισεῖ δὲ ἦχον ἀσφαλείας </p>
 <p>16 γυνὴ εὐχάριστος ἐγείρει ἀνδρὶ δόξαν θρόνος δὲ ἀτιμίας γυνὴ μισοῦσα δίκαια πλούτου ὀκνηροὶ ἐνδεεῖς γίνονται οἱ δὲ ἀνδρεῖοι ἐρείδονται πλούτῳ </p>
 <p>17 τῇ ψυχῇ αὐτοῦ ἀγαθὸν ποιεῖ ἀνὴρ ἐλεήμων ἐξολλύει δὲ αὐτοῦ σῶμα ὁ ἀνελεήμων </p>
 <p>18 ἀσεβὴς ποιεῖ ἔργα ἄδικα σπέρμα δὲ δικαίων μισθὸς ἀληθείας </p>
 <p>19 υἱὸς δίκαιος γεννᾶται εἰς ζωήν διωγμὸς δὲ ἀσεβοῦς εἰς θάνατον </p>
 <p>20 βδέλυγμα κυρίῳ διεστραμμέναι ὁδοί προσδεκτοὶ δὲ αὐτῷ πάντες ἄμωμοι ἐν ταῖς ὁδοῖς αὐτῶν </p>
 <p>21 χειρὶ χεῖρας ἐμβαλὼν ἀδίκως οὐκ ἀτιμώρητος ἔσται ὁ δὲ σπείρων δικαιοσύνην λήμψεται μισθὸν πιστόν </p>
 <p>22 ὥσπερ ἐνώτιον ἐν ῥινὶ ὑός οὕτως γυναικὶ κακόφρονι κάλλος </p>
 <p>23 ἐπιθυμία δικαίων πᾶσα ἀγαθή ἐλπὶς δὲ ἀσεβῶν ἀπολεῖται </p>
 <p>24 εἰσὶν οἳ τὰ ἴδια σπείροντες πλείονα ποιοῦσιν εἰσὶν καὶ οἳ συνάγοντες ἐλαττονοῦνται </p>
 <p>25 ψυχὴ εὐλογουμένη πᾶσα ἁπλῆ ἀνὴρ δὲ θυμώδης οὐκ εὐσχήμων </p>
 <p>26 ὁ συνέχων σῖτον ὑπολίποιτο αὐτὸν τοῖς ἔθνεσιν εὐλογία δὲ εἰς κεφαλὴν τοῦ μεταδιδόντος </p>
 <p>27 τεκταινόμενος ἀγαθὰ ζητεῖ χάριν ἀγαθήν ἐκζητοῦντα δὲ κακά καταλήμψεται αὐτόν </p>
 <p>28 ὁ πεποιθὼς ἐπὶ πλούτῳ οὗτος πεσεῖται ὁ δὲ ἀντιλαμβανόμενος δικαίων οὗτος ἀνατελεῖ </p>
 <p>29 ὁ μὴ συμπεριφερόμενος τῷ ἑαυτοῦ οἴκῳ κληρονομήσει ἄνεμον δουλεύσει δὲ ἄφρων φρονίμῳ </p>
 <p>30 ἐκ καρποῦ δικαιοσύνης φύεται δένδρον ζωῆς ἀφαιροῦνται δὲ ἄωροι ψυχαὶ παρανόμων </p>
 <p>31 εἰ ὁ μὲν δίκαιος μόλις σῴζεται ὁ ἀσεβὴς καὶ ἁμαρτωλὸς ποῦ φανεῖται </p>

 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎7 ‏בְּמ֤וֹת אָדָ֣ם רָ֭שָׁע תֹּאבַ֣ד תִּקְוָ֑ה וְתוֹחֶ֖לֶת אוֹנִ֣ים אָבָֽדָה׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎8 ‏צַ֭דִּיק מִצָּרָ֣ה נֶחֱלָ֑ץ וַיָּבֹ֖א רָשָׁ֣ע תַּחְתָּֽיו׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎9 ‏בְּפֶ֗ה חָ֭נֵף יַשְׁחִ֣ת רֵעֵ֑הוּ וּ֝בְדַ֗עַת צַדִּיקִ֥ים יֵחָלֵֽצוּ׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎10 ‏בְּט֣וּב צַ֭דִּיקִים תַּעֲלֹ֣ץ קִרְיָ֑ה וּבַאֲבֹ֖ד רְשָׁעִ֣ים רִנָּֽה׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎11 ‏בְּבִרְכַּ֣ת יְ֭שָׁרִים תָּר֣וּם קָ֑רֶת וּבְפִ֥י רְ֝שָׁעִ֗ים תֵּהָרֵֽס׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎12 ‏בָּז־לְרֵעֵ֥הוּ חֲסַר־לֵ֑ב וְאִ֖ישׁ תְּבוּנ֣וֹת יַחֲרִֽישׁ׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎13 ‏הוֹלֵ֣ךְ רָ֭כִיל מְגַלֶּה־סּ֑וֹד וְנֶאֱמַן־ר֝֗וּחַ מְכַסֶּ֥ה דָבָֽר׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎14 ‏בְּאֵ֣ין תַּ֭חְבֻּלוֹת יִפָּל־עָ֑ם וּ֝תְשׁוּעָ֗ה בְּרֹ֣ב יוֹעֵֽץ׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎15 ‏רַע־יֵ֭רוֹעַ כִּי־עָ֣רַב זָ֑ר וְשֹׂנֵ֖א תֹקְעִ֣ים בּוֹטֵֽחַ׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎16 ‏אֵֽשֶׁת־חֵ֭ן תִּתְמֹ֣ךְ כָּב֑וֹד וְ֝עָרִיצִ֗ים יִתְמְכוּ־עֹֽשֶׁר׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎17 ‏גֹּמֵ֣ל נַ֭פְשׁוֹ אִ֣ישׁ חָ֑סֶד וְעֹכֵ֥ר שְׁ֝אֵר֗וֹ אַכְזָרִֽי׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎18 ‏רָשָׁ֗ע עֹשֶׂ֥ה פְעֻלַּת־שָׁ֑קֶר וְזֹרֵ֥עַ צְ֝דָקָ֗ה שֶׂ֣כֶר אֱמֶֽת׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎19 ‏כֵּן־צְדָקָ֥ה לְחַיִּ֑ים וּמְרַדֵּ֖ף רָעָ֣ה לְמוֹתֽוֹ׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎20 ‏תּוֹעֲבַ֣ת יְ֭הוָה עִקְּשֵׁי־לֵ֑ב וּ֝רְצוֹנ֗וֹ תְּמִ֣ימֵי דָֽרֶךְ׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎21 ‏יָ֣ד לְ֭יָד לֹא־יִנָּ֣קֶה רָּ֑ע וְזֶ֖רַע צַדִּיקִ֣ים נִמְלָֽט׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎22 ‏נֶ֣זֶם זָ֭הָב בְּאַ֣ף חֲזִ֑יר אִשָּׁ֥ה יָ֝פָ֗ה וְסָ֣רַת טָֽעַם׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎23 ‏תַּאֲוַ֣ת צַדִּיקִ֣ים אַךְ־ט֑וֹב תִּקְוַ֖ת רְשָׁעִ֣ים עֶבְרָֽה׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎24 ‏יֵ֣שׁ מְ֭פַזֵּר וְנוֹסָ֥ף ע֑וֹד וְחוֹשֵׂ֥ךְ מִ֝יֹּ֗שֶׁר אַךְ־לְמַחְסֽוֹר׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎25 ‏נֶֽפֶשׁ־בְּרָכָ֥ה תְדֻשָּׁ֑ן וּ֝מַרְוֶ֗ה גַּם־ה֥וּא יוֹרֶֽא׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎26 ‏מֹ֣נֵֽעַ בָּ֭ר יִקְּבֻ֣הוּ לְא֑וֹם וּ֝בְרָכָ֗ה לְרֹ֣אשׁ מַשְׁבִּֽיר׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎27 ‏שֹׁ֣חֵֽר ט֭וֹב<font color="red"><sup>3</sup></font> יְבַקֵּ֣שׁ רָצ֑וֹן וְדֹרֵ֖שׁ רָעָ֣ה תְבוֹאֶֽנּוּ׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎28 ‏בּוֹטֵ֣חַ בְּ֭עָשְׁרוֹ ה֣וּא יִפֹּ֑ל וְ֝כֶעָלֶ֗ה צַדִּיקִ֥ים יִפְרָֽחוּ׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎29 ‏עוֹכֵ֣ר בֵּ֭יתוֹ יִנְחַל־ר֑וּחַ וְעֶ֥בֶד אֱ֝וִ֗יל לַחֲכַם־לֵֽב׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎30 ‏פְּֽרִי־צַ֭דִּיק עֵ֣ץ חַיִּ֑ים וְלֹקֵ֖חַ נְפָשׂ֣וֹת<font color="red"><sup>1</sup></font> חָכָֽם׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎31 ‏הֵ֣ן צַ֭דִּיק בָּאָ֣רֶץ יְשֻׁלָּ֑ם אַ֝֗ף כִּֽי־רָשָׁ֥ע וְחוֹטֵֽא׃ </span></p>
 </td>

 <p align="center"><a href="../vul/pro011.htm">Vul</a></p><p>1 [Statera dolosa abominatio est apud Dominum,<br> et pondus æquum voluntas ejus.<br></p>
 <p>2 Ubi fuerit superbia, ibi erit et contumelia;<br> ubi autem est humilitas, ibi et sapientia.<br></p>
 <p>3 Simplicitas justorum diriget eos,<br> et supplantatio perversorum vastabit illos.<br></p>
 <p>4 Non proderunt divitiæ in die ultionis;<br> justitia autem liberabit a morte.<br></p>
 <p>5 Justitia simplicis diriget viam ejus,<br> et in impietate sua corruet impius.<br></p>
 <p>6 Justitia rectorum liberabit eos,<br> et in insidiis suis capientur iniqui.<br></p>
 <p>7 Mortuo homine impio, nulla erit ultra spes,<br> et exspectatio sollicitorum peribit.<br></p>
 <p>8 Justus de angustia liberatus est,<br> et tradetur impius pro eo.]<br>
 </p>
 <p>9 [Simulator ore decipit amicum suum;<br> justi autem liberabuntur scientia.<br></p>
 <p>10 In bonis justorum exsultabit civitas,<br> et in perditione impiorum erit laudatio.<br></p>
 <p>11 Benedictione justorum exaltabitur civitas,<br> et ore impiorum subvertetur.<br></p>
 <p>12 Qui despicit amicum suum indigens corde est;<br> vir autem prudens tacebit.<br></p>
 <p>13 Qui ambulat fraudulenter, revelat arcana;<br> qui autem fidelis est animi, celat amici commissum.<br></p>
 <p>14 Ubi non est gubernator, populus corruet;<br> salus autem, ubi multa consilia.<br></p>
 <p>15 Affligetur malo qui fidem facit pro extraneo;<br> qui autem cavet laqueos securus erit.<br></p>
 <p>16 Mulier gratiosa inveniet gloriam,<br> et robusti habebunt divitias.]<br>
 </p>
 <p>17 [Benefacit animæ suæ vir misericors;<br> qui autem crudelis est, etiam propinquos abjicit.<br></p>
 <p>18 Impius facit opus instabile,<br> seminanti autem justitiam merces fidelis.<br></p>
 <p>19 Clementia præparat vitam,<br> et sectatio malorum mortem.<br></p>
 <p>20 Abominabile Domino cor pravum,<br> et voluntas ejus in iis qui simpliciter ambulant.<br></p>
 <p>21 Manus in manu non erit innocens malus;<br> semen autem justorum salvabitur.<br></p>
 <p>22 Circulus aureus in naribus suis,<br> mulier pulchra et fatua.<br></p>
 <p>23 Desiderium justorum omne bonum est;<br> præstolatio impiorum furor.<br></p>
 <p>24 Alii dividunt propria, et ditiores fiunt;<br> alii rapiunt non sua, et semper in egestate sunt.<br></p>
 <p>25 Anima quæ benedicit impinguabitur,<br> et qui inebriat, ipse quoque inebriabitur.<br></p>
 <p>26 Qui abscondit frumenta maledicetur in populis;<br> benedictio autem super caput vendentium.<br></p>
 <p>27 Bene consurgit diluculo qui quærit bona;<br> qui autem investigator malorum est, opprimetur ab eis.<br></p>
 <p>28 Qui confidit in divitiis suis corruet:<br> justi autem quasi virens folium germinabunt.<br></p>
 <p>29 Qui conturbat domum suam possidebit ventos,<br> et qui stultus est serviet sapienti.<br></p>
 <p>30 Fructus justi lignum vitæ,<br> et qui suscipit animas sapiens est.<br></p>
 <p>31 Si justus in terra recipit,<br> quanto magis impius et peccator!]</p>
