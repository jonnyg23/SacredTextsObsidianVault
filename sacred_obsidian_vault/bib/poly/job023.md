 <p align="center"><a href="../kjv/job023.htm">KJV</a></p><p>1  Then Job<sup><a href="h0347.htm">347</a></sup> answered<sup><a href="h6030.htm">6030</a></sup> and said,<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> </p>
 <p>2  Even<sup><a href="h1571.htm">1571</a></sup> today<sup><a href="h3117.htm">3117</a></sup> is my complaint<sup><a href="h7879.htm">7879</a></sup> bitter:<sup><a href="h4805.htm">4805</a></sup> my stroke<sup><a href="h3027.htm">3027</a></sup> is heavier<sup><a href="h3513.htm">3513</a></sup> than<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> my groaning.<sup><a href="h0585.htm">585</a></sup> </p>
 <p>3  Oh that<sup><a href="h4310.htm">4310</a></sup> <sup><a href="h5414.htm">5414</a></sup> I knew<sup><a href="h3045.htm">3045</a></sup> where I might find<sup><a href="h4672.htm">4672</a></sup> him! that I might come<sup><a href="h0935.htm">935</a></sup> even to<sup><a href="h5704.htm">5704</a></sup> his seat!<sup><a href="h8499.htm">8499</a></sup> </p>
 <p>4  I would order<sup><a href="h6186.htm">6186</a></sup> my cause<sup><a href="h4941.htm">4941</a></sup> before<sup><a href="h6440.htm">6440</a></sup> him, and fill<sup><a href="h4390.htm">4390</a></sup> my mouth<sup><a href="h6310.htm">6310</a></sup> with arguments.<sup><a href="h8433.htm">8433</a></sup> </p>
 <p>5  I would know<sup><a href="h3045.htm">3045</a></sup> the words<sup><a href="h4405.htm">4405</a></sup> which he would answer<sup><a href="h6030.htm">6030</a></sup> me, and understand<sup><a href="h0995.htm">995</a></sup> what<sup><a href="h4100.htm">4100</a></sup> he would say<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> unto me. </p>
 <p>6  Will he plead<sup><a href="h7378.htm">7378</a></sup> against<sup><a href="h5978.htm">5978</a></sup> me with his great<sup><a href="h7230.htm">7230</a></sup> power?<sup><a href="h3581.htm">3581</a></sup> No;<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> but<sup><a href="h0389.htm">389</a></sup> he<sup><a href="h1931.htm">1931</a></sup> would put<sup><a href="h7760.htm">7760</a></sup> strength in me. </p>
 <p>7  There<sup><a href="h8033.htm">8033</a></sup> the righteous<sup><a href="h3477.htm">3477</a></sup> might dispute<sup><a href="h3198.htm">3198</a></sup> with<sup><a href="h5973.htm">5973</a></sup> him; so should I be delivered<sup><a href="h6403.htm">6403</a></sup> forever<sup><a href="h5331.htm">5331</a></sup> from my judge.<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h8199.htm">8199</a></sup> </p>
 <p>8  Behold,<sup><a href="h2005.htm">2005</a></sup> I go<sup><a href="h1980.htm">1980</a></sup> forward,<sup><a href="h6924.htm">6924</a></sup> but he is not<sup><a href="h0369.htm">369</a></sup> there; and backward,<sup><a href="h0268.htm">268</a></sup> but I cannot<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> perceive<sup><a href="h0995.htm">995</a></sup> him: </p>
 <p>9  On the left hand,<sup><a href="h8040.htm">8040</a></sup> where he doth work,<sup><a href="h6213.htm">6213</a></sup> but I cannot<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> behold<sup><a href="h2372.htm">2372</a></sup> him: he hideth<sup><a href="h5848.htm">5848</a></sup> himself on the right hand,<sup><a href="h3225.htm">3225</a></sup> that I cannot<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> see<sup><a href="h7200.htm">7200</a></sup> him: </p>
 <p>10  But<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> he knoweth<sup><a href="h3045.htm">3045</a></sup> the way<sup><a href="h1870.htm">1870</a></sup> that I take:<sup><a href="h5978.htm">5978</a></sup> when he hath tried<sup><a href="h0974.htm">974</a></sup> me, I shall come forth<sup><a href="h3318.htm">3318</a></sup> as gold.<sup><a href="h2091.htm">2091</a></sup> </p>
 <p>11  My foot<sup><a href="h7272.htm">7272</a></sup> hath held<sup><a href="h0270.htm">270</a></sup> his steps,<sup><a href="h0838.htm">838</a></sup> his way<sup><a href="h1870.htm">1870</a></sup> have I kept,<sup><a href="h8104.htm">8104</a></sup> and not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> declined.<sup><a href="h5186.htm">5186</a></sup> </p>
 <p>12  Neither<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> have I gone back<sup><a href="h4185.htm">4185</a></sup> from the commandment<sup><a href="h4687.htm">4687</a></sup> of his lips;<sup><a href="h8193.htm">8193</a></sup> I have esteemed<sup><a href="h6845.htm">6845</a></sup> the words<sup><a href="h0561.htm">561</a></sup> of his mouth<sup><a href="h6310.htm">6310</a></sup> more than my necessary<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h2706.htm">2706</a></sup> food. </p>
 <p>13  But he<sup><a href="h1931.htm">1931</a></sup> is in one<sup><a href="h0259.htm">259</a></sup> mind, and who<sup><a href="h4310.htm">4310</a></sup> can turn<sup><a href="h7725.htm">7725</a></sup> him? and what his soul<sup><a href="h5315.htm">5315</a></sup> desireth,<sup><a href="h0183.htm">183</a></sup> even that he doeth.<sup><a href="h6213.htm">6213</a></sup> </p>
 <p>14  For<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> he performeth<sup><a href="h7999.htm">7999</a></sup> the thing that is appointed<sup><a href="h2706.htm">2706</a></sup> for me: and many<sup><a href="h7227.htm">7227</a></sup> such<sup><a href="h2007.htm">2007</a></sup> things are with<sup><a href="h5973.htm">5973</a></sup> him. </p>
 <p>15  Therefore<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3651.htm">3651</a></sup> am I troubled<sup><a href="h0926.htm">926</a></sup> at his presence:<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h6440.htm">6440</a></sup> when I consider,<sup><a href="h0995.htm">995</a></sup> I am afraid<sup><a href="h6342.htm">6342</a></sup> of<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> him. </p>
 <p>16  For God<sup><a href="h0410.htm">410</a></sup> maketh my heart soft<sup><a href="h7401.htm">7401</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3820.htm">3820</a></sup> and the Almighty<sup><a href="h7706.htm">7706</a></sup> troubleth<sup><a href="h0926.htm">926</a></sup> me: </p>
 <p>17  Because<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> I was not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> cut off<sup><a href="h6789.htm">6789</a></sup> before<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h6440.htm">6440</a></sup> the darkness,<sup><a href="h2822.htm">2822</a></sup> neither hath he covered<sup><a href="h3680.htm">3680</a></sup> the darkness<sup><a href="h0652.htm">652</a></sup> from my face.<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h6440.htm">6440</a></sup> </p>

 <p align="center"><a href="../sep/job023.htm">Sep</a></p><p>1 ὑπολαβὼν δὲ Ιωβ λέγει </p>
 <p>2 καὶ δὴ οἶδα ὅτι ἐκ χειρός μου ἡ ἔλεγξίς ἐστιν καὶ ἡ χεὶρ αὐτοῦ βαρεῖα γέγονεν ἐπ᾽ ἐμῷ στεναγμῷ </p>
 <p>3 τίς δ᾽ ἄρα γνοίη ὅτι εὕροιμι αὐτὸν καὶ ἔλθοιμι εἰς τέλος </p>
 <p>4 εἴποιμι δὲ ἐμαυτοῦ κρίμα τὸ δὲ στόμα μου ἐμπλήσαιμι ἐλέγχων </p>
 <p>5 γνῴην δὲ ῥήματα ἅ μοι ἐρεῖ αἰσθοίμην δὲ τίνα μοι ἀπαγγελεῖ </p>
 <p>6 καὶ εἰ ἐν πολλῇ ἰσχύι ἐπελεύσεταί μοι εἶτα ἐν ἀπειλῇ μοι οὐ χρήσεται </p>
 <p>7 ἀλήθεια γὰρ καὶ ἔλεγχος παρ᾽ αὐτοῦ ἐξαγάγοι δὲ εἰς τέλος τὸ κρίμα μου </p>
 <p>8 εἰς γὰρ πρῶτα πορεύσομαι καὶ οὐκέτι εἰμί τὰ δὲ ἐπ᾽ ἐσχάτοις τί οἶδα </p>
 <p>9 ἀριστερὰ ποιήσαντος αὐτοῦ καὶ οὐ κατέσχον περιβαλεῖ δεξιά καὶ οὐκ ὄψομαι </p>
 <p>10 οἶδεν γὰρ ἤδη ὁδόν μου διέκρινεν δέ με ὥσπερ τὸ χρυσίον </p>
 <p>11 ἐξελεύσομαι δὲ ἐν ἐντάλμασιν αὐτοῦ ὁδοὺς γὰρ αὐτοῦ ἐφύλαξα καὶ οὐ μὴ ἐκκλίνω </p>
 <p>12 ἀπὸ ἐνταλμάτων αὐτοῦ καὶ οὐ μὴ παρέλθω ἐν δὲ κόλπῳ μου ἔκρυψα ῥήματα αὐτοῦ </p>
 <p>13 εἰ δὲ καὶ αὐτὸς ἔκρινεν οὕτως τίς ἐστιν ὁ ἀντειπὼν αὐτῷ ὃ γὰρ αὐτὸς ἠθέλησεν καὶ ἐποίησεν </p>
 <p>15 διὰ τοῦτο ἐπ᾽ αὐτῷ ἐσπούδακα νουθετούμενος δὲ ἐφρόντισα αὐτοῦ  <br>
  ἐπὶ τούτῳ ἀπὸ προσώπου αὐτοῦ κατασπουδασθῶ κατανοήσω καὶ πτοηθήσομαι ἐξ αὐτοῦ </p>
 <p>16 κύριος δὲ ἐμαλάκυνεν τὴν καρδίαν μου ὁ δὲ παντοκράτωρ ἐσπούδασέν με </p>
 <p>17 οὐ γὰρ ᾔδειν ὅτι ἐπελεύσεταί μοι σκότος πρὸ προσώπου δέ μου ἐκάλυψεν γνόφος </p>

 <p align="center"><a href="../vul/job023.htm">Vul</a></p><p>1 Respondens autem Job, ait:</p>
 <p>2 [Nunc quoque in amaritudine est sermo meus,<br> et manus plagæ meæ aggravata est super gemitum meum.<br></p>
 <p>3 Quis mihi tribuat ut cognoscam et inveniam illum,<br> et veniam usque ad solium ejus?<br></p>
 <p>4 Ponam coram eo judicium,<br> et os meum replebo increpationibus:<br></p>
 <p>5 ut sciam verba quæ mihi respondeat,<br> et intelligam quid loquatur mihi.<br></p>
 <p>6 Nolo multa fortitudine contendat mecum,<br> nec magnitudinis suæ mole me premat.<br></p>
 <p>7 Proponat æquitatem contra me,<br> et perveniat ad victoriam judicium meum.<br></p>
 <p>8 Si ad orientem iero, non apparet;<br> si ad occidentem, non intelligam eum.<br></p>
 <p>9 Si ad sinistram, quid agam? non apprehendam eum;<br> si me vertam ad dexteram, non videbo illum.<br></p>
 <p>10 Ipse vero scit viam meam,<br> et probavit me quasi aurum quod per ignem transit.<br></p>
 <p>11 Vestigia ejus secutus est pes meus:<br> viam ejus custodivi, et non declinavi ex ea.<br></p>
 <p>12 A mandatis labiorum ejus non recessi,<br> et in sinu meo abscondi verba oris ejus.<br></p>
 <p>13 Ipse enim solus est, et nemo avertere potest cogitationem ejus:<br> et anima ejus quodcumque voluit, hoc fecit.<br></p>
 <p>14 Cum expleverit in me voluntatem suam,<br> et alia multa similia præsto sunt ei.<br></p>
 <p>15 Et idcirco a facie ejus turbatus sum,<br> et considerans eum, timore sollicitor.<br></p>
 <p>16 Deus mollivit cor meum,<br> et Omnipotens conturbavit me.<br></p>
 <p>17 Non enim perii propter imminentes tenebras,<br> nec faciem meam operuit caligo.]</p>
