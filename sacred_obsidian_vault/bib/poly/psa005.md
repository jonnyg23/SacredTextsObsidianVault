 <p>2  Hearken<sup><a href="h7181.htm">7181</a></sup> unto the voice<sup><a href="h6963.htm">6963</a></sup> of my cry,<sup><a href="h7773.htm">7773</a></sup> my King,<sup><a href="h4428.htm">4428</a></sup> and my God:<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> for<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> unto<sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> thee will I pray.<sup><a href="h6419.htm">6419</a></sup> </p>
 <p>3  My voice<sup><a href="h6963.htm">6963</a></sup> shalt thou hear<sup><a href="h8085.htm">8085</a></sup> in the morning,<sup><a href="h1242.htm">1242</a></sup> O LORD;<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> in the morning<sup><a href="h1242.htm">1242</a></sup> will I direct<sup><a href="h6186.htm">6186</a></sup> my prayer unto thee, and will look up.<sup><a href="h6822.htm">6822</a></sup> </p>
 <p>4  For<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> thou<sup><a href="h0859.htm">859</a></sup> art not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> a God<sup><a href="h0410.htm">410</a></sup> that hath pleasure<sup><a href="h2655.htm">2655</a></sup> in wickedness:<sup><a href="h7562.htm">7562</a></sup> neither<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> shall evil<sup><a href="h7451.htm">7451</a></sup> dwell<sup><a href="h1481.htm">1481</a></sup> with thee. </p>
 <p>5  The foolish<sup><a href="h1984.htm">1984</a></sup> shall not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> stand<sup><a href="h3320.htm">3320</a></sup> in<sup><a href="h5048.htm">5048</a></sup> thy sight:<sup><a href="h5869.htm">5869</a></sup> thou hatest<sup><a href="h8130.htm">8130</a></sup> all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> workers<sup><a href="h6466.htm">6466</a></sup> of iniquity.<sup><a href="h0205.htm">205</a></sup> </p>
 <p>6  Thou shalt destroy<sup><a href="h0006.htm">6</a></sup> them that speak<sup><a href="h1696.htm">1696</a></sup> leasing:<sup><a href="h3577.htm">3577</a></sup> the LORD<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> will abhor<sup><a href="h8581.htm">8581</a></sup> the bloody<sup><a href="h1818.htm">1818</a></sup> and deceitful<sup><a href="h4820.htm">4820</a></sup> man.<sup><a href="h0376.htm">376</a></sup> </p>
 <p>7  But as for me,<sup><a href="h0589.htm">589</a></sup> I will come<sup><a href="h0935.htm">935</a></sup> into thy house<sup><a href="h1004.htm">1004</a></sup> in the multitude<sup><a href="h7230.htm">7230</a></sup> of thy mercy:<sup><a href="h2617.htm">2617</a></sup> and in thy fear<sup><a href="h3374.htm">3374</a></sup> will I worship<sup><a href="h7812.htm">7812</a></sup> toward<sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> thy holy<sup><a href="h6944.htm">6944</a></sup> temple.<sup><a href="h1964.htm">1964</a></sup> </p>
 <p>8  Lead<sup><a href="h5148.htm">5148</a></sup> me, O LORD,<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> in thy righteousness<sup><a href="h6666.htm">6666</a></sup> because of<sup><a href="h4616.htm">4616</a></sup> mine enemies;<sup><a href="h8324.htm">8324</a></sup> make thy way<sup><a href="h1870.htm">1870</a></sup> straight<sup><a href="h3474.htm">3474</a></sup> before my face.<sup><a href="h6440.htm">6440</a></sup> </p>
 <p>9  For<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> there is no<sup><a href="h0369.htm">369</a></sup> faithfulness<sup><a href="h3559.htm">3559</a></sup> in their mouth;<sup><a href="h6310.htm">6310</a></sup> their inward part<sup><a href="h7130.htm">7130</a></sup> is very wickedness;<sup><a href="h1942.htm">1942</a></sup> their throat<sup><a href="h1627.htm">1627</a></sup> is an open<sup><a href="h6605.htm">6605</a></sup> sepulcher;<sup><a href="h6913.htm">6913</a></sup> they flatter<sup><a href="h2505.htm">2505</a></sup> with their tongue.<sup><a href="h3956.htm">3956</a></sup> </p>
 <p>10  Destroy<sup><a href="h0816.htm">816</a></sup> thou them, O God;<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> let them fall<sup><a href="h5307.htm">5307</a></sup> by their own counsels;<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h4156.htm">4156</a></sup> cast them out<sup><a href="h5080.htm">5080</a></sup> in the multitude<sup><a href="h7230.htm">7230</a></sup> of their transgressions;<sup><a href="h6588.htm">6588</a></sup> for<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> they have rebelled<sup><a href="h4784.htm">4784</a></sup> against thee. </p>
 <p>11  But let all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> those that put their trust<sup><a href="h2620.htm">2620</a></sup> in thee rejoice:<sup><a href="h8055.htm">8055</a></sup> let them ever<sup><a href="h5769.htm">5769</a></sup> shout for joy,<sup><a href="h7442.htm">7442</a></sup> because thou defendest<sup><a href="h5526.htm">5526</a></sup> them:<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> let them also that love<sup><a href="h0157.htm">157</a></sup> thy name<sup><a href="h8034.htm">8034</a></sup> be joyful<sup><a href="h5970.htm">5970</a></sup> in thee. </p>
 <p>12  For<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> thou,<sup><a href="h0859.htm">859</a></sup> LORD,<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> wilt bless<sup><a href="h1288.htm">1288</a></sup> the righteous;<sup><a href="h6662.htm">6662</a></sup> with favor<sup><a href="h7522.htm">7522</a></sup> wilt thou compass<sup><a href="h5849.htm">5849</a></sup> him as with a shield.<sup><a href="h6793.htm">6793</a></sup> </p>

 <p align="center"><a href="../sep/psa005.htm">Sep</a></p><p>1 εἰς τὸ τέλος ὑπὲρ τῆς κληρονομούσης ψαλμὸς τῷ Δαυιδ </p>
 <p>2 τὰ ῥήματά μου ἐνώτισαι κύριε σύνες τῆς κραυγῆς μου </p>
 <p>3 πρόσχες τῇ φωνῇ τῆς δεήσεώς μου ὁ βασιλεύς μου καὶ ὁ θεός μου ὅτι πρὸς σὲ προσεύξομαι κύριε </p>
 <p>4 τὸ πρωὶ εἰσακούσῃ τῆς φωνῆς μου τὸ πρωὶ παραστήσομαί σοι καὶ ἐπόψομαι </p>
 <p>5 ὅτι οὐχὶ θεὸς θέλων ἀνομίαν σὺ εἶ οὐδὲ παροικήσει σοι πονηρευόμενος </p>
 <p>6 οὐ διαμενοῦσιν παράνομοι κατέναντι τῶν ὀφθαλμῶν σου ἐμίσησας πάντας τοὺς ἐργαζομένους τὴν ἀνομίαν </p>
 <p>7 ἀπολεῖς πάντας τοὺς λαλοῦντας τὸ ψεῦδος ἄνδρα αἱμάτων καὶ δόλιον βδελύσσεται κύριος </p>
 <p>8 ἐγὼ δὲ ἐν τῷ πλήθει τοῦ ἐλέους σου εἰσελεύσομαι εἰς τὸν οἶκόν σου προσκυνήσω πρὸς ναὸν ἅγιόν σου ἐν φόβῳ σου </p>
 <p>9 κύριε ὁδήγησόν με ἐν τῇ δικαιοσύνῃ σου ἕνεκα τῶν ἐχθρῶν μου κατεύθυνον ἐνώπιόν μου τὴν ὁδόν σου </p>
 <p>10 ὅτι οὐκ ἔστιν ἐν τῷ στόματι αὐτῶν ἀλήθεια ἡ καρδία αὐτῶν ματαία τάφος ἀνεῳγμένος ὁ λάρυγξ αὐτῶν ταῖς γλώσσαις αὐτῶν ἐδολιοῦσαν </p>
 <p>11 κρῖνον αὐτούς ὁ θεός ἀποπεσάτωσαν ἀπὸ τῶν διαβουλίων αὐτῶν κατὰ τὸ πλῆθος τῶν ἀσεβειῶν αὐτῶν ἔξωσον αὐτούς ὅτι παρεπίκρανάν σε κύριε </p>
 <p>12 καὶ εὐφρανθήτωσαν πάντες οἱ ἐλπίζοντες ἐπὶ σέ εἰς αἰῶνα ἀγαλλιάσονται καὶ κατασκηνώσεις ἐν αὐτοῖς καὶ καυχήσονται ἐν σοὶ πάντες οἱ ἀγαπῶντες τὸ ὄνομά σου </p>
 <p>13 ὅτι σὺ εὐλογήσεις δίκαιον κύριε ὡς ὅπλῳ εὐδοκίας ἐστεφάνωσας ἡμᾶς </p>

 <p>4 Quoniam ad te orabo, Domine:<br> mane exaudies vocem meam.<br></p>

 <p>8 Ego autem in multitudine misericordiæ tuæ<br> introibo in domum tuam;<br> adorabo ad templum sanctum tuum in timore tuo.<br></p>
 <p>9 Domine, deduc me in justitia tua:<br> propter inimicos meos dirige in conspectu tuo viam meam.<br></p>
 <p>10 Quoniam non est in ore eorum veritas;<br> cor eorum vanum est.<br></p>

 <p>12 Et lætentur omnes qui sperant in te;<br> in æternum exsultabunt, et habitabis in eis.<br> Et gloriabuntur in te omnes qui diligunt nomen tuum,<br></p>
