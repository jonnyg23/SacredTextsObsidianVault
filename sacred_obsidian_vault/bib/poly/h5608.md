  <p>H5608<br>  ספר  <br> סָפַר  ‎  sâphar  <br><i>saw-far‘ </i><br>A primitive root; properly to <i>score</i> with a mark as a tally or record, that is, (by implication) to <i>inscribe</i>, and also to <i>enumerate</i>; intensively to <i>recount</i>, that is, <i>celebrate: - </i>commune, (ac-) count, declare, number, + penknife, reckon, scribe, shew forth, speak, talk, tell (out), writer.<br></p>
