  <p>H3034<br>  ידה  <br> יָדָה  ‎  yâdâh  <br><i>yaw-daw‘ </i><br>A primitive root; used only as denominative from <a href="h3027.htm">3027</a>  literally to <i>use</i> (that is, hold out) <i>the</i> <i>hand</i>; physically to <i>throw</i> (a stone, an arrow) at or away; especially to <i>revere</i> or <i>worship</i> (with extended hands); intensively to <i>bemoan</i> (by wringing the hands): - cast (out), (make) confess (-ion), praise, shoot, (give) thank (-ful, -s, -sgiving).<br></p>
