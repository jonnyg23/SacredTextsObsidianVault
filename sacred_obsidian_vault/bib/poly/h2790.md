  <p>H2790<br>  חרשׁ  <br> חָרַשׁ  ‎  chârash  <br><i>khaw-rash‘ </i><br>A primitive root; to <i>scratch</i>, that is, (by implication) to <i>engrave</i>, <i>plough</i>; hence (from the use of tools) to <i>fabricate</i> (of any material); figuratively to <i>devise</i> (in a bad sense); hence (from the idea of secrecy) to be <i>silent</i>, to <i>let</i> <i>alone</i>; hence (by implication) to <i>be</i> <i>deaf</i> (as an accompaniment of dumbness): -  X altogether, cease, conceal, be deaf, devise, ear, graven, imagine, leave off speaking, hold peace, plow (-er, -man), be quiet, rest, practise secretly, keep silence, be silent, speak not a word, be still, hold tongue, worker.<br></p>
