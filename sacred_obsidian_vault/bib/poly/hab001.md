 <p>3  Why<sup><a href="h4100.htm">4100</a></sup> dost thou show<sup><a href="h7200.htm">7200</a></sup> me iniquity,<sup><a href="h0205.htm">205</a></sup> and cause me to behold<sup><a href="h5027.htm">5027</a></sup> grievance?<sup><a href="h5999.htm">5999</a></sup> for spoiling<sup><a href="h7701.htm">7701</a></sup> and violence<sup><a href="h2555.htm">2555</a></sup> are before<sup><a href="h5048.htm">5048</a></sup> me: and there are<sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> that raise up<sup><a href="h5375.htm">5375</a></sup> strife<sup><a href="h7379.htm">7379</a></sup> and contention.<sup><a href="h4066.htm">4066</a></sup> </p>
 <p>4  Therefore<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3651.htm">3651</a></sup> the law<sup><a href="h8451.htm">8451</a></sup> is slacked,<sup><a href="h6313.htm">6313</a></sup> and judgment<sup><a href="h4941.htm">4941</a></sup> doth never<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> <sup><a href="h5331.htm">5331</a></sup> go forth:<sup><a href="h3318.htm">3318</a></sup> for<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> the wicked<sup><a href="h7563.htm">7563</a></sup> doth compass about<sup><a href="h3803.htm">3803</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> the righteous;<sup><a href="h6662.htm">6662</a></sup> therefore<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3651.htm">3651</a></sup> wrong<sup><a href="h6127.htm">6127</a></sup> judgment<sup><a href="h4941.htm">4941</a></sup> proceedeth.<sup><a href="h3318.htm">3318</a></sup> </p>
 <p>5  Behold<sup><a href="h7200.htm">7200</a></sup> ye among the heathen,<sup><a href="h1471.htm">1471</a></sup> and regard,<sup><a href="h5027.htm">5027</a></sup> and wonder marvelously:<sup><a href="h8539.htm">8539</a></sup> <sup><a href="h8539.htm">8539</a></sup> for<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> I will work<sup><a href="h6466.htm">6466</a></sup> a work<sup><a href="h6467.htm">6467</a></sup> in your days,<sup><a href="h3117.htm">3117</a></sup> which ye will not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> believe,<sup><a href="h0539.htm">539</a></sup> though<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> it be told<sup><a href="h5608.htm">5608</a></sup> you. </p>
 <p>6  For,<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> lo,<sup><a href="h2009.htm">2009</a></sup> I raise up<sup><a href="h6965.htm">6965</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> the Chaldeans,<sup><a href="h3778.htm">3778</a></sup> that bitter<sup><a href="h4751.htm">4751</a></sup> and hasty<sup><a href="h4116.htm">4116</a></sup> nation,<sup><a href="h1471.htm">1471</a></sup> which shall march<sup><a href="h1980.htm">1980</a></sup> through the breadth<sup><a href="h4800.htm">4800</a></sup> of the land,<sup><a href="h0776.htm">776</a></sup> to possess<sup><a href="h3423.htm">3423</a></sup> the dwelling places<sup><a href="h4908.htm">4908</a></sup> that are not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> theirs. </p>
 <p>7  They<sup><a href="h1931.htm">1931</a></sup> are terrible<sup><a href="h0366.htm">366</a></sup> and dreadful:<sup><a href="h3372.htm">3372</a></sup> their judgment<sup><a href="h4941.htm">4941</a></sup> and their dignity<sup><a href="h7613.htm">7613</a></sup> shall proceed<sup><a href="h3318.htm">3318</a></sup> of<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> themselves. </p>
 <p>8  Their horses<sup><a href="h5483.htm">5483</a></sup> also are swifter<sup><a href="h7043.htm">7043</a></sup> than the leopards,<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h5246.htm">5246</a></sup> and are more fierce<sup><a href="h2300.htm">2300</a></sup> than the evening<sup><a href="h6153.htm">6153</a></sup> wolves:<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h2061.htm">2061</a></sup> and their horsemen<sup><a href="h6571.htm">6571</a></sup> shall spread<sup><a href="h6335.htm">6335</a></sup> themselves, and their horsemen<sup><a href="h6571.htm">6571</a></sup> shall come<sup><a href="h0935.htm">935</a></sup> from far;<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h7350.htm">7350</a></sup> they shall fly<sup><a href="h5774.htm">5774</a></sup> as the eagle<sup><a href="h5404.htm">5404</a></sup> that hasteth<sup><a href="h2363.htm">2363</a></sup> to eat.<sup><a href="h0398.htm">398</a></sup> </p>
 <p>9  They shall come<sup><a href="h0935.htm">935</a></sup> all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> for violence:<sup><a href="h2555.htm">2555</a></sup> their faces<sup><a href="h6440.htm">6440</a></sup> shall sup up<sup><a href="h4041.htm">4041</a></sup> as the east wind,<sup><a href="h6921.htm">6921</a></sup> and they shall gather<sup><a href="h0622.htm">622</a></sup> the captivity<sup><a href="h7628.htm">7628</a></sup> as the sand.<sup><a href="h2344.htm">2344</a></sup> </p>
 <p>10  And they<sup><a href="h1931.htm">1931</a></sup> shall scoff<sup><a href="h7046.htm">7046</a></sup> at the kings,<sup><a href="h4428.htm">4428</a></sup> and the princes<sup><a href="h7336.htm">7336</a></sup> shall be a scorn<sup><a href="h4890.htm">4890</a></sup> unto them: they<sup><a href="h1931.htm">1931</a></sup> shall deride<sup><a href="h7832.htm">7832</a></sup> every<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> stronghold;<sup><a href="h4013.htm">4013</a></sup> for they shall heap<sup><a href="h6651.htm">6651</a></sup> dust,<sup><a href="h6083.htm">6083</a></sup> and take<sup><a href="h3920.htm">3920</a></sup> it. </p>
 <p>11  Then<sup><a href="h0227.htm">227</a></sup> shall his mind<sup><a href="h7307.htm">7307</a></sup> change,<sup><a href="h2498.htm">2498</a></sup> and he shall pass over,<sup><a href="h5674.htm">5674</a></sup> and offend,<sup><a href="h0816.htm">816</a></sup> imputing this<sup><a href="h2098.htm">2098</a></sup> his power<sup><a href="h3581.htm">3581</a></sup> unto his god.<sup><a href="h0433.htm">433</a></sup> </p>
 <p>12  Art thou<sup><a href="h0859.htm">859</a></sup> not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> from everlasting,<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h6924.htm">6924</a></sup> O LORD<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> my God,<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> mine Holy One?<sup><a href="h6918.htm">6918</a></sup> we shall not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> die.<sup><a href="h4191.htm">4191</a></sup> O LORD,<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> thou hast ordained<sup><a href="h7760.htm">7760</a></sup> them for judgment;<sup><a href="h4941.htm">4941</a></sup> and, O mighty God,<sup><a href="h6697.htm">6697</a></sup> thou hast established<sup><a href="h3245.htm">3245</a></sup> them for correction.<sup><a href="h3198.htm">3198</a></sup> </p>
 <p>13  Thou art of purer<sup><a href="h2889.htm">2889</a></sup> eyes<sup><a href="h5869.htm">5869</a></sup> than to behold<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h7200.htm">7200</a></sup> evil,<sup><a href="h7451.htm">7451</a></sup> and canst<sup><a href="h3201.htm">3201</a></sup> not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> look<sup><a href="h5027.htm">5027</a></sup> on<sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> iniquity:<sup><a href="h5999.htm">5999</a></sup> wherefore<sup><a href="h4100.htm">4100</a></sup> lookest<sup><a href="h5027.htm">5027</a></sup> thou upon them that deal treacherously,<sup><a href="h0898.htm">898</a></sup> and holdest thy tongue<sup><a href="h2790.htm">2790</a></sup> when the wicked<sup><a href="h7563.htm">7563</a></sup> devoureth<sup><a href="h1104.htm">1104</a></sup> the man that is more righteous<sup><a href="h6662.htm">6662</a></sup> than<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> he? </p>
 <p>14  And makest<sup><a href="h6213.htm">6213</a></sup> men<sup><a href="h0120.htm">120</a></sup> as the fishes<sup><a href="h1709.htm">1709</a></sup> of the sea,<sup><a href="h3220.htm">3220</a></sup> as the creeping things,<sup><a href="h7431.htm">7431</a></sup> that have no<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> ruler<sup><a href="h4910.htm">4910</a></sup> over them? </p>
 <p>15  They take up<sup><a href="h5927.htm">5927</a></sup> all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> of them with the angle,<sup><a href="h2443.htm">2443</a></sup> they catch<sup><a href="h1641.htm">1641</a></sup> them in their net,<sup><a href="h2764.htm">2764</a></sup> and gather<sup><a href="h0622.htm">622</a></sup> them in their drag:<sup><a href="h4365.htm">4365</a></sup> therefore<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3651.htm">3651</a></sup> they rejoice<sup><a href="h8055.htm">8055</a></sup> and are glad.<sup><a href="h1523.htm">1523</a></sup> </p>
 <p>16  Therefore<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3651.htm">3651</a></sup> they sacrifice<sup><a href="h2076.htm">2076</a></sup> unto their net,<sup><a href="h2764.htm">2764</a></sup> and burn incense<sup><a href="h6999.htm">6999</a></sup> unto their drag;<sup><a href="h4365.htm">4365</a></sup> because<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> by them<sup><a href="h1992.htm">1992</a></sup> their portion<sup><a href="h2506.htm">2506</a></sup> is fat,<sup><a href="h8082.htm">8082</a></sup> and their meat<sup><a href="h3978.htm">3978</a></sup> plenteous.<sup><a href="h1277.htm">1277</a></sup> </p>
 <p>17  Shall they therefore<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3651.htm">3651</a></sup> empty<sup><a href="h7324.htm">7324</a></sup> their net,<sup><a href="h2764.htm">2764</a></sup> and not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> spare<sup><a href="h2550.htm">2550</a></sup> continually<sup><a href="h8548.htm">8548</a></sup> to slay<sup><a href="h2026.htm">2026</a></sup> the nations?<sup><a href="h1471.htm">1471</a></sup> </p>

 <p align="center"><a href="../sep/hab001.htm">Sep</a></p><p>1 τὸ λῆμμα ὃ εἶδεν Αμβακουμ ὁ προφήτης </p>
 <p>2 ἕως τίνος κύριε κεκράξομαι καὶ οὐ μὴ εἰσακούσῃς βοήσομαι πρὸς σὲ ἀδικούμενος καὶ οὐ σώσεις </p>
 <p>3 ἵνα τί μοι ἔδειξας κόπους καὶ πόνους ἐπιβλέπειν ταλαιπωρίαν καὶ ἀσέβειαν ἐξ ἐναντίας μου γέγονεν κρίσις καὶ ὁ κριτὴς λαμβάνει </p>
 <p>4 διὰ τοῦτο διεσκέδασται νόμος καὶ οὐ διεξάγεται εἰς τέλος κρίμα ὅτι ὁ ἀσεβὴς καταδυναστεύει τὸν δίκαιον ἕνεκεν τούτου ἐξελεύσεται τὸ κρίμα διεστραμμένον </p>
 <p>5 ἴδετε οἱ καταφρονηταί καὶ ἐπιβλέψατε καὶ θαυμάσατε θαυμάσια καὶ ἀφανίσθητε διότι ἔργον ἐγὼ ἐργάζομαι ἐν ταῖς ἡμέραις ὑμῶν ὃ οὐ μὴ πιστεύσητε ἐάν τις ἐκδιηγῆται </p>
 <p>6 διότι ἰδοὺ ἐγὼ ἐξεγείρω ἐφ᾽ ὑμᾶς τοὺς Χαλδαίους τοὺς μαχητάς τὸ ἔθνος τὸ πικρὸν καὶ τὸ ταχινὸν τὸ πορευόμενον ἐπὶ τὰ πλάτη τῆς γῆς τοῦ κατακληρονομῆσαι σκηνώματα οὐκ αὐτοῦ </p>
 <p>7 φοβερὸς καὶ ἐπιφανής ἐστιν ἐξ αὐτοῦ τὸ κρίμα αὐτοῦ ἔσται καὶ τὸ λῆμμα αὐτοῦ ἐξ αὐτοῦ ἐξελεύσεται </p>
 <p>8 καὶ ἐξαλοῦνται ὑπὲρ παρδάλεις οἱ ἵπποι αὐτοῦ καὶ ὀξύτεροι ὑπὲρ τοὺς λύκους τῆς Ἀραβίας καὶ ἐξιππάσονται οἱ ἱππεῖς αὐτοῦ καὶ ὁρμήσουσιν μακρόθεν καὶ πετασθήσονται ὡς ἀετὸς πρόθυμος εἰς τὸ φαγεῖν </p>
 <p>9 συντέλεια εἰς ἀσεβεῖς ἥξει ἀνθεστηκότας προσώποις αὐτῶν ἐξ ἐναντίας καὶ συνάξει ὡς ἄμμον αἰχμαλωσίαν </p>
 <p>10 καὶ αὐτὸς ἐν βασιλεῦσιν ἐντρυφήσει καὶ τύραννοι παίγνια αὐτοῦ καὶ αὐτὸς εἰς πᾶν ὀχύρωμα ἐμπαίξεται καὶ βαλεῖ χῶμα καὶ κρατήσει αὐτοῦ </p>
 <p>11 τότε μεταβαλεῖ τὸ πνεῦμα καὶ διελεύσεται καὶ ἐξιλάσεται αὕτη ἡ ἰσχὺς τῷ θεῷ μου </p>
 <p>12 οὐχὶ σὺ ἀπ᾽ ἀρχῆς κύριε ὁ θεὸς ὁ ἅγιός μου καὶ οὐ μὴ ἀποθάνωμεν κύριε εἰς κρίμα τέταχας αὐτόν καὶ ἔπλασέν με τοῦ ἐλέγχειν παιδείαν αὐτοῦ </p>
 <p>13 καθαρὸς ὀφθαλμὸς τοῦ μὴ ὁρᾶν πονηρά καὶ ἐπιβλέπειν ἐπὶ πόνους οὐ δυνήσῃ ἵνα τί ἐπιβλέπεις ἐπὶ καταφρονοῦντας παρασιωπήσῃ ἐν τῷ καταπίνειν ἀσεβῆ τὸν δίκαιον </p>
 <p>14 καὶ ποιήσεις τοὺς ἀνθρώπους ὡς τοὺς ἰχθύας τῆς θαλάσσης καὶ ὡς τὰ ἑρπετὰ τὰ οὐκ ἔχοντα ἡγούμενον </p>
 <p>15 συντέλειαν ἐν ἀγκίστρῳ ἀνέσπασεν καὶ εἵλκυσεν αὐτὸν ἐν ἀμφιβλήστρῳ καὶ συνήγαγεν αὐτὸν ἐν ταῖς σαγήναις αὐτοῦ ἕνεκεν τούτου εὐφρανθήσεται καὶ χαρήσεται ἡ καρδία αὐτοῦ </p>
 <p>16 ἕνεκεν τούτου θύσει τῇ σαγήνῃ αὐτοῦ καὶ θυμιάσει τῷ ἀμφιβλήστρῳ αὐτοῦ ὅτι ἐν αὐτοῖς ἐλίπανεν μερίδα αὐτοῦ καὶ τὰ βρώματα αὐτοῦ ἐκλεκτά </p>
 <p>17 διὰ τοῦτο ἀμφιβαλεῖ τὸ ἀμφίβληστρον αὐτοῦ καὶ διὰ παντὸς ἀποκτέννειν ἔθνη οὐ φείσεται </p>

 <p align="center"><a href="../vul/hab001.htm">Vul</a></p><p>1 Onus quod vidit Habacuc propheta.<br>
 </p>
 <p>2 [Usquequo, Domine, clamabo,<br> et non exaudies?<br> vociferabor ad te, vim patiens,<br> et non salvabis?<br></p>
 <p>3 Quare ostendisti mihi iniquitatem et laborem,<br> videre prædam et injustitiam contra me?<br> Et factum est judicium, et contradictio potentior.<br></p>
 <p>4 Propter hoc lacerata est lex,<br> et non pervenit usque ad finem judicium;<br> quia impius prævalet adversus justum,<br> propterea egreditur judicium perversum.<br></p>
 <p>5 Aspicite in gentibus, et videte;<br> admiramini, et obstupescite:<br> quia opus factum est in diebus vestris,<br> quod nemo credet cum narrabitur.<br></p>
 <p>6 Quia ecce ego suscitabo Chaldæos,<br> gentem amaram et velocem,<br> ambulantem super latitudinem terræ,<br> ut possideat tabernacula non sua.<br></p>
 <p>7 Horribilis et terribilis est:<br> ex semetipsa judicium et onus ejus egredietur.<br></p>
 <p>8 Leviores pardis equi ejus,<br> et velociores lupis vespertinis:<br> et diffundentur equites ejus:<br> equites namque ejus de longe venient;<br> volabunt quasi aquila festinans ad comedendum.<br></p>
 <p>9 Omnes ad prædam venient,<br> facies eorum ventus urens;<br> et congregabit quasi arenam captivitatem.<br></p>
 <p>10 Et ipse de regibus triumphabit,<br> et tyranni ridiculi ejus erunt;<br> ipse super omnem munitionem ridebit,<br> et comportabit aggerem, et capiet eam.<br></p>
 <p>11 Tunc mutabitur spiritus, et pertransibit, et corruet:<br> hæc est fortitudo ejus dei sui.]<br>
 </p>
 <p>12 [Numquid non tu a principio,<br> Domine, Deus meus, sancte meus,<br> et non moriemur?<br> Domine, in judicium posuisti eum,<br> et fortem, ut corriperes, fundasti eum.<br></p>
 <p>13 Mundi sunt oculi tui, ne videas malum,<br> et respicere ad iniquitatem non poteris.<br> Quare respicis super iniqua agentes,<br> et taces devorante impio justiorem se?<br></p>
 <p>14 Et facies homines quasi pisces maris,<br> et quasi reptile non habens principem.<br></p>
 <p>15 Totum in hamo sublevavit,<br> traxit illud in sagena sua,<br> et congregavit in rete suum.<br> Super hoc lætabitur, et exsultabit.<br></p>
 <p>16 Propterea immolabit sagenæ suæ,<br> et sacrificabit reti suo,<br> quia in ipsis incrassata est pars ejus,<br> et cibus ejus electus.<br></p>
 <p>17 Propter hoc ergo expandit sagenam suam,<br> et semper interficere gentes non parcet.]</p>
