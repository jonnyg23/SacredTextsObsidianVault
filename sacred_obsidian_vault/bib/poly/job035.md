 <p>3  For<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> thou saidst,<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> What<sup><a href="h4100.htm">4100</a></sup> advantage<sup><a href="h5532.htm">5532</a></sup> will it be unto thee? and, What<sup><a href="h4100.htm">4100</a></sup> profit<sup><a href="h3276.htm">3276</a></sup> shall I have, if I be cleansed from my sin?<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h2403.htm">2403</a></sup> </p>
 <p>4  I<sup><a href="h0589.htm">589</a></sup> will answer<sup><a href="h7725.htm">7725</a></sup> <sup><a href="h4405.htm">4405</a></sup> thee, and thy companions<sup><a href="h7453.htm">7453</a></sup> with<sup><a href="h5973.htm">5973</a></sup> thee. </p>
 <p>5  Look<sup><a href="h5027.htm">5027</a></sup> unto the heavens,<sup><a href="h8064.htm">8064</a></sup> and see;<sup><a href="h7200.htm">7200</a></sup> and behold<sup><a href="h7789.htm">7789</a></sup> the clouds<sup><a href="h7834.htm">7834</a></sup> which are higher<sup><a href="h1361.htm">1361</a></sup> than<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> thou. </p>
 <p>6  If<sup><a href="h0518.htm">518</a></sup> thou sinnest,<sup><a href="h2398.htm">2398</a></sup> what<sup><a href="h4100.htm">4100</a></sup> doest<sup><a href="h6466.htm">6466</a></sup> thou against him? or if thy transgressions<sup><a href="h6588.htm">6588</a></sup> be multiplied,<sup><a href="h7231.htm">7231</a></sup> what<sup><a href="h4100.htm">4100</a></sup> doest<sup><a href="h6213.htm">6213</a></sup> thou unto him? </p>
 <p>7  If<sup><a href="h0518.htm">518</a></sup> thou be righteous,<sup><a href="h6663.htm">6663</a></sup> what<sup><a href="h4100.htm">4100</a></sup> givest<sup><a href="h5414.htm">5414</a></sup> thou him? or<sup><a href="h0176.htm">176</a></sup> what<sup><a href="h4100.htm">4100</a></sup> receiveth<sup><a href="h3947.htm">3947</a></sup> he of thine hand?<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3027.htm">3027</a></sup> </p>
 <p>8  Thy wickedness<sup><a href="h7562.htm">7562</a></sup> may hurt a man<sup><a href="h0376.htm">376</a></sup> as thou<sup><a href="h3644.htm">3644</a></sup> art; and thy righteousness<sup><a href="h6666.htm">6666</a></sup> may profit the son<sup><a href="h1121.htm">1121</a></sup> of man.<sup><a href="h0120.htm">120</a></sup> </p>
 <p>9  By reason of the multitude<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h7230.htm">7230</a></sup> of oppressions<sup><a href="h6217.htm">6217</a></sup> they make the oppressed to cry:<sup><a href="h2199.htm">2199</a></sup> they cry out<sup><a href="h7768.htm">7768</a></sup> by reason of the arm<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h2220.htm">2220</a></sup> of the mighty.<sup><a href="h7227.htm">7227</a></sup> </p>
 <p>10  But none<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> saith,<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> Where<sup><a href="h0346.htm">346</a></sup> is God<sup><a href="h0433.htm">433</a></sup> my maker,<sup><a href="h6213.htm">6213</a></sup> who giveth<sup><a href="h5414.htm">5414</a></sup> songs<sup><a href="h2158.htm">2158</a></sup> in the night;<sup><a href="h3915.htm">3915</a></sup> </p>
 <p>11  Who teacheth<sup><a href="h0502.htm">502</a></sup> us more than the beasts<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0929.htm">929</a></sup> of the earth,<sup><a href="h0776.htm">776</a></sup> and maketh us wiser<sup><a href="h2449.htm">2449</a></sup> than the fowls<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h5775.htm">5775</a></sup> of heaven?<sup><a href="h8064.htm">8064</a></sup> </p>
 <p>12  There<sup><a href="h8033.htm">8033</a></sup> they cry,<sup><a href="h6817.htm">6817</a></sup> but none<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> giveth answer,<sup><a href="h6030.htm">6030</a></sup> because<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h6440.htm">6440</a></sup> of the pride<sup><a href="h1347.htm">1347</a></sup> of evil men.<sup><a href="h7451.htm">7451</a></sup> </p>
 <p>13  Surely<sup><a href="h0389.htm">389</a></sup> God<sup><a href="h0410.htm">410</a></sup> will not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> hear<sup><a href="h8085.htm">8085</a></sup> vanity,<sup><a href="h7723.htm">7723</a></sup> neither<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> will the Almighty<sup><a href="h7706.htm">7706</a></sup> regard<sup><a href="h7789.htm">7789</a></sup> it. </p>
 <p>14  Although<sup><a href="h0637.htm">637</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> thou sayest<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> thou shalt not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> see<sup><a href="h7789.htm">7789</a></sup> him, yet judgment<sup><a href="h1779.htm">1779</a></sup> is before<sup><a href="h6440.htm">6440</a></sup> him; therefore trust<sup><a href="h2342.htm">2342</a></sup> thou in him. </p>
 <p>15  But now,<sup><a href="h6258.htm">6258</a></sup> because<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> it is not<sup><a href="h0369.htm">369</a></sup> so, he hath visited<sup><a href="h6485.htm">6485</a></sup> in his anger;<sup><a href="h0639.htm">639</a></sup> yet he knoweth<sup><a href="h3045.htm">3045</a></sup> it not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> in great<sup><a href="h3966.htm">3966</a></sup> extremity:<sup><a href="h6580.htm">6580</a></sup> </p>
 <p>16  Therefore doth Job<sup><a href="h0347.htm">347</a></sup> open<sup><a href="h6475.htm">6475</a></sup> his mouth<sup><a href="h6310.htm">6310</a></sup> in vain;<sup><a href="h1892.htm">1892</a></sup> he multiplieth<sup><a href="h3527.htm">3527</a></sup> words<sup><a href="h4405.htm">4405</a></sup> without<sup><a href="h1097.htm">1097</a></sup> knowledge.<sup><a href="h1847.htm">1847</a></sup> </p>

 <p>3 ἢ ἐρεῖς τί ποιήσω ἁμαρτών </p>
 <p>4 ἐγὼ σοὶ δώσω ἀπόκρισιν καὶ τοῖς τρισὶν φίλοις σου </p>
 <p>5 ἀνάβλεψον εἰς τὸν οὐρανὸν καὶ ἰδέ κατάμαθε δὲ νέφη ὡς ὑψηλὰ ἀπὸ σοῦ </p>
 <p>6 εἰ ἥμαρτες τί πράξεις εἰ δὲ καὶ πολλὰ ἠνόμησας τί δύνασαι ποιῆσαι </p>
 <p>7 ἐπεὶ δὲ οὖν δίκαιος εἶ τί δώσεις αὐτῷ ἢ τί ἐκ χειρός σου λήμψεται </p>
 <p>8 ἀνδρὶ τῷ ὁμοίῳ σου ἡ ἀσέβειά σου καὶ υἱῷ ἀνθρώπου ἡ δικαιοσύνη σου </p>
 <p>9 ἀπὸ πλήθους συκοφαντούμενοι κεκράξονται βοήσονται ἀπὸ βραχίονος πολλῶν </p>
 <p>10 καὶ οὐκ εἶπεν ποῦ ἐστιν ὁ θεὸς ὁ ποιήσας με ὁ κατατάσσων φυλακὰς νυκτερινάς </p>
 <p>11 ὁ διορίζων με ἀπὸ τετραπόδων γῆς ἀπὸ δὲ πετεινῶν οὐρανοῦ </p>
 <p>12 ἐκεῖ κεκράξονται καὶ οὐ μὴ εἰσακούσῃ καὶ ἀπὸ ὕβρεως πονηρῶν </p>
 <p>13 ἄτοπα γὰρ οὐ βούλεται ὁ κύριος ἰδεῖν αὐτὸς γὰρ ὁ παντοκράτωρ ὁρατής ἐστιν </p>
 <p>14 τῶν συντελούντων τὰ ἄνομα καὶ σώσει με κρίθητι δὲ ἐναντίον αὐτοῦ εἰ δύνασαι αἰνέσαι αὐτόν ὡς ἔστιν </p>
 <p>15 καὶ νῦν ὅτι οὐκ ἔστιν ἐπισκεπτόμενος ὀργὴν αὐτοῦ καὶ οὐκ ἔγνω παραπτώματι σφόδρα </p>
 <p>16 καὶ Ιωβ ματαίως ἀνοίγει τὸ στόμα αὐτοῦ ἐν ἀγνωσίᾳ ῥήματα βαρύνει </p>
