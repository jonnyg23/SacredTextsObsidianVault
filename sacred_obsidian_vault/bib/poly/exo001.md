 <p>7  And the children<sup><a href="h1121.htm">1121</a></sup> of Israel<sup><a href="h3478.htm">3478</a></sup> were fruitful,<sup><a href="h6509.htm">6509</a></sup> and increased abundantly,<sup><a href="h8317.htm">8317</a></sup> and multiplied,<sup><a href="h7235.htm">7235</a></sup> and waxed exceeding mighty;<sup><a href="h6105.htm">6105</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3966.htm">3966</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3966.htm">3966</a></sup> and the land<sup><a href="h0776.htm">776</a></sup> was filled<sup><a href="h4390.htm">4390</a></sup> with<sup><a href="h0854.htm">854</a></sup> them. </p>

 <p>9  And he said<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> unto<sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> his people,<sup><a href="h5971.htm">5971</a></sup> Behold,<sup><a href="h2009.htm">2009</a></sup> the people<sup><a href="h5971.htm">5971</a></sup> of the children<sup><a href="h1121.htm">1121</a></sup> of Israel<sup><a href="h3478.htm">3478</a></sup> are more<sup><a href="h7227.htm">7227</a></sup> and mightier<sup><a href="h6099.htm">6099</a></sup> than<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> we: </p>
 <p>10  Come<sup><a href="h3051.htm">3051</a></sup> on, let us deal wisely<sup><a href="h2449.htm">2449</a></sup> with them; lest<sup><a href="h6435.htm">6435</a></sup> they multiply,<sup><a href="h7235.htm">7235</a></sup> and it come to pass,<sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> that, when<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> there falleth out<sup><a href="h7122.htm">7122</a></sup> any war,<sup><a href="h4421.htm">4421</a></sup> they<sup><a href="h1931.htm">1931</a></sup> join<sup><a href="h3254.htm">3254</a></sup> also<sup><a href="h1571.htm">1571</a></sup> unto<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> our enemies,<sup><a href="h8130.htm">8130</a></sup> and fight<sup><a href="h3898.htm">3898</a></sup> against us, and so get them up<sup><a href="h5927.htm">5927</a></sup> out of<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> the land.<sup><a href="h0776.htm">776</a></sup> </p>
 <p>11  Therefore they did set<sup><a href="h7760.htm">7760</a></sup> over<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> them taskmasters<sup><a href="h8269.htm">8269</a></sup> <sup><a href="h4522.htm">4522</a></sup> to<sup><a href="h4616.htm">4616</a></sup> afflict<sup><a href="h6031.htm">6031</a></sup> them with their burdens.<sup><a href="h5450.htm">5450</a></sup> And they built<sup><a href="h1129.htm">1129</a></sup> for Pharaoh<sup><a href="h6547.htm">6547</a></sup> treasure<sup><a href="h4543.htm">4543</a></sup> cities,<sup><a href="h5892.htm">5892</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> Pithom<sup><a href="h6619.htm">6619</a></sup> and Rameses.<sup><a href="h7486.htm">7486</a></sup> </p>
 <p>12  But the more<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> they afflicted<sup><a href="h6031.htm">6031</a></sup> them, the more<sup><a href="h3651.htm">3651</a></sup> they multiplied<sup><a href="h7235.htm">7235</a></sup> and grew.<sup><a href="h3651.htm">3651</a></sup> <sup><a href="h6555.htm">6555</a></sup> And they were grieved<sup><a href="h6973.htm">6973</a></sup> because<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h6440.htm">6440</a></sup> of the children<sup><a href="h1121.htm">1121</a></sup> of Israel.<sup><a href="h3478.htm">3478</a></sup> </p>
 <p>13  And the Egyptians<sup><a href="h4714.htm">4714</a></sup> made <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> the children<sup><a href="h1121.htm">1121</a></sup> of Israel<sup><a href="h3478.htm">3478</a></sup> to serve<sup><a href="h5647.htm">5647</a></sup> with rigor:<sup><a href="h6531.htm">6531</a></sup> </p>
 <p>14  And they made <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> their lives<sup><a href="h2416.htm">2416</a></sup> bitter<sup><a href="h4843.htm">4843</a></sup> with hard<sup><a href="h7186.htm">7186</a></sup> bondage,<sup><a href="h5656.htm">5656</a></sup> in mortar,<sup><a href="h2563.htm">2563</a></sup> and in brick,<sup><a href="h3843.htm">3843</a></sup> and in all manner<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> of service<sup><a href="h5656.htm">5656</a></sup> in the field:<sup><a href="h7704.htm">7704</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> their service,<sup><a href="h5656.htm">5656</a></sup> wherein<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> they made them serve,<sup><a href="h5647.htm">5647</a></sup> was with rigor.<sup><a href="h6531.htm">6531</a></sup> </p>
 <p>15  And the king<sup><a href="h4428.htm">4428</a></sup> of Egypt<sup><a href="h4714.htm">4714</a></sup> spoke<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> to the Hebrew<sup><a href="h5680.htm">5680</a></sup> midwives,<sup><a href="h3205.htm">3205</a></sup> of which<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> the name<sup><a href="h8034.htm">8034</a></sup> of the one<sup><a href="h0259.htm">259</a></sup> was Shiphrah,<sup><a href="h8236.htm">8236</a></sup> and the name<sup><a href="h8034.htm">8034</a></sup> of the other<sup><a href="h8145.htm">8145</a></sup> Puah:<sup><a href="h6326.htm">6326</a></sup> </p>
 <p>16  And he said,<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> When ye do the office of a midwife<sup><a href="h3205.htm">3205</a></sup> to <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> the Hebrew women,<sup><a href="h5680.htm">5680</a></sup> and see<sup><a href="h7200.htm">7200</a></sup> them upon<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> the stools;<sup><a href="h0070.htm">70</a></sup> if<sup><a href="h0518.htm">518</a></sup> it<sup><a href="h1931.htm">1931</a></sup> be a son,<sup><a href="h1121.htm">1121</a></sup> then ye shall kill<sup><a href="h4191.htm">4191</a></sup> him: but if<sup><a href="h0518.htm">518</a></sup> it<sup><a href="h1931.htm">1931</a></sup> be a daughter,<sup><a href="h1323.htm">1323</a></sup> then she shall live.<sup><a href="h2425.htm">2425</a></sup> </p>

 <p>18  And the king<sup><a href="h4428.htm">4428</a></sup> of Egypt<sup><a href="h4714.htm">4714</a></sup> called for<sup><a href="h7121.htm">7121</a></sup> the midwives,<sup><a href="h3205.htm">3205</a></sup> and said<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> unto them, Why<sup><a href="h4069.htm">4069</a></sup> have ye done<sup><a href="h6213.htm">6213</a></sup> this<sup><a href="h2088.htm">2088</a></sup> thing,<sup><a href="h1697.htm">1697</a></sup> and have saved<sup><a href="h2421.htm">2421</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> the men children<sup><a href="h3206.htm">3206</a></sup> alive? </p>

 <p>20  Therefore God<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> dealt well<sup><a href="h3190.htm">3190</a></sup> with the midwives:<sup><a href="h3205.htm">3205</a></sup> and the people<sup><a href="h5971.htm">5971</a></sup> multiplied,<sup><a href="h7235.htm">7235</a></sup> and waxed very mighty.<sup><a href="h6105.htm">6105</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3966.htm">3966</a></sup> </p>
 <p>21  And it came to pass,<sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> because<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> the midwives<sup><a href="h3205.htm">3205</a></sup> feared<sup><a href="h3372.htm">3372</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> God,<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> that he made<sup><a href="h6213.htm">6213</a></sup> them houses.<sup><a href="h1004.htm">1004</a></sup> </p>
 <p>22  And Pharaoh<sup><a href="h6547.htm">6547</a></sup> charged<sup><a href="h6680.htm">6680</a></sup> all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> his people,<sup><a href="h5971.htm">5971</a></sup> saying,<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> Every<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> son<sup><a href="h1121.htm">1121</a></sup> that is born<sup><a href="h3209.htm">3209</a></sup> ye shall cast<sup><a href="h7993.htm">7993</a></sup> into the river,<sup><a href="h2975.htm">2975</a></sup> and every<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> daughter<sup><a href="h1323.htm">1323</a></sup> ye shall save alive.<sup><a href="h2421.htm">2421</a></sup> </p>

 <p align="center"><a href="../vul/exo001.htm">Vul</a></p><p>1 Hæc sunt nomina filiorum Israël qui ingressi sunt in Ægyptum cum Jacob: singuli cum domibus suis introierunt:</p>
 <p>2 Ruben, Simeon, Levi, Judas,</p>
 <p>3 Issachar, Zabulon et Benjamin,</p>
 <p>4 Dan et Nephthali, Gad et Aser.</p>
 <p>5 Erant igitur omnes animæ eorum qui egressi sunt de femore Jacob, septuaginta: Joseph autem in Ægypto erat.</p>
 <p>6 Quo mortuo, et universis fratribus ejus, omnique cognatione illa,</p>
 <p>7 filii Israël creverunt, et quasi germinantes multiplicati sunt: ac roborati nimis, impleverunt terram.<br>
 </p>
 <p>8 Surrexit interea rex novus super Ægyptum, qui ignorabat Joseph.</p>
 <p>9 Et ait ad populum suum: Ecce, populus filiorum Israël multus, et fortior nobis est.</p>
 <p>10 Venite, sapienter opprimamus eum, ne forte multiplicetur: et si ingruerit contra nos bellum, addatur inimicis nostris, expugnatisque nobis egrediatur de terra.</p>
 <p>11 Præposuit itaque eis magistros operum, ut affligerent eos oneribus: ædificaveruntque urbes tabernaculorum Pharaoni, Phithom et Ramesses.</p>
 <p>12 Quantoque opprimebant eos, tanto magis multiplicabantur, et crescebant:</p>
 <p>13 oderantque filios Israël Ægyptii, et affligebant illudentes eis,</p>
 <p>14 atque ad amaritudinem perducebant vitam eorum operibus duris luti et lateris, omnique famulatu, quo in terræ operibus premebantur.</p>
 <p>15 Dixit autem rex Ægypti obstetricibus Hebræorum, quarum una vocabatur Sephora, altera Phua,</p>
 <p>16 præcipiens eis: Quando obstetricabitis Hebræas, et partus tempus advenerit: si masculus fuerit, interficite eum: si femina, reservate.</p>
 <p>17 Timuerunt autem obstetrices Deum, et non fecerunt juxta præceptum regis Ægypti, sed conservabant mares.</p>
 <p>18 Quibus ad se accersitis, rex ait: Quidnam est hoc quod facere voluistis, ut pueros servaretis?</p>
 <p>19 Quæ responderunt: Non sunt Hebreæ sicut ægyptiæ mulieres: ipsæ enim obstetricandi habent scientiam, et priusquam veniamus ad eas, pariunt.</p>
 <p>20 Bene ergo fecit Deus obstetricibus: et crevit populus, confortatusque est nimis.</p>
 <p>21 Et quia timuerunt obstetrices Deum, ædificavit eis domos.</p>
 <p>22 Præcepit ergo Pharao omni populo suo, dicens: Quidquid masculini sexus natum fuerit, in flumen projicite: quidquid feminini, reservate.</p>
