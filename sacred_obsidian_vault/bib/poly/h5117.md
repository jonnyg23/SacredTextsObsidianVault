  <p>H5117<br>  נוּח  <br> נוַּח  ‎  nûach  <br><i>noo‘-akh </i><br>A primitive root; to <i>rest</i>, that is, <i>settle</i> down; used in a great variety of applications, literally and figuratively, intransitively, transitively and causatively (to <i>dwell</i>, <i>stay</i>, <i>let</i> <i>fall</i>, <i>place</i>, <i>let</i> <i>alone</i>, <i>withdraw</i>, <i>give</i> <i>comfort</i>, etc.): - cease, be confederate, lay, let down, (be) quiet, remain, (cause to, be at, give, have, make to) rest, set down.
