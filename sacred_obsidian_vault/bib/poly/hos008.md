 <p align="center"><a href="../kjv/hos008.htm">KJV</a></p><p>1  Set the trumpet<sup><a href="h7782.htm">7782</a></sup> to<sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> thy mouth.<sup><a href="h2441.htm">2441</a></sup> He shall come as an eagle<sup><a href="h5404.htm">5404</a></sup> against<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> the house<sup><a href="h1004.htm">1004</a></sup> of the LORD,<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> because<sup><a href="h3282.htm">3282</a></sup> they have transgressed<sup><a href="h5674.htm">5674</a></sup> my covenant,<sup><a href="h1285.htm">1285</a></sup> and trespassed<sup><a href="h6586.htm">6586</a></sup> against<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> my law.<sup><a href="h8451.htm">8451</a></sup> </p>
 <p>2  Israel<sup><a href="h3478.htm">3478</a></sup> shall cry<sup><a href="h2199.htm">2199</a></sup> unto me, My God,<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> we know<sup><a href="h3045.htm">3045</a></sup> thee. </p>
 <p>3  Israel<sup><a href="h3478.htm">3478</a></sup> hath cast off<sup><a href="h2186.htm">2186</a></sup> the thing that is good:<sup><a href="h2896.htm">2896</a></sup> the enemy<sup><a href="h0341.htm">341</a></sup> shall pursue<sup><a href="h7291.htm">7291</a></sup> him. </p>
 <p>4  They<sup><a href="h1992.htm">1992</a></sup> have set up kings,<sup><a href="h4427.htm">4427</a></sup> but not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> by<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> me: they have made princes,<sup><a href="h8323.htm">8323</a></sup> and I knew<sup><a href="h3045.htm">3045</a></sup> it not:<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> of their silver<sup><a href="h3701.htm">3701</a></sup> and their gold<sup><a href="h2091.htm">2091</a></sup> have they made<sup><a href="h6213.htm">6213</a></sup> them idols,<sup><a href="h6091.htm">6091</a></sup> that<sup><a href="h4616.htm">4616</a></sup> they may be cut off.<sup><a href="h3772.htm">3772</a></sup> </p>
 <p>5  Thy calf,<sup><a href="h5695.htm">5695</a></sup> O Samaria,<sup><a href="h8111.htm">8111</a></sup> hath cast thee off;<sup><a href="h2186.htm">2186</a></sup> mine anger<sup><a href="h0639.htm">639</a></sup> is kindled<sup><a href="h2734.htm">2734</a></sup> against them: how long<sup><a href="h5704.htm">5704</a></sup> <sup><a href="h4970.htm">4970</a></sup> will it be ere<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> they attain<sup><a href="h3201.htm">3201</a></sup> to innocency?<sup><a href="h5356.htm">5356</a></sup> </p>
 <p>6  For<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> from Israel<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3478.htm">3478</a></sup> was it<sup><a href="h1931.htm">1931</a></sup> also: the workman<sup><a href="h2796.htm">2796</a></sup> made<sup><a href="h6213.htm">6213</a></sup> it;<sup><a href="h1931.htm">1931</a></sup> therefore it is not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> God:<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> but<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> the calf<sup><a href="h5695.htm">5695</a></sup> of Samaria<sup><a href="h8111.htm">8111</a></sup> shall be<sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> broken in pieces.<sup><a href="h7616.htm">7616</a></sup> </p>
 <p>7  For<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> they have sown<sup><a href="h2232.htm">2232</a></sup> the wind,<sup><a href="h7307.htm">7307</a></sup> and they shall reap<sup><a href="h7114.htm">7114</a></sup> the whirlwind:<sup><a href="h5492.htm">5492</a></sup> it hath no<sup><a href="h0369.htm">369</a></sup> stalk:<sup><a href="h7054.htm">7054</a></sup> the bud<sup><a href="h6780.htm">6780</a></sup> shall yield<sup><a href="h6213.htm">6213</a></sup> no<sup><a href="h1097.htm">1097</a></sup> meal:<sup><a href="h7058.htm">7058</a></sup> if so be<sup><a href="h0194.htm">194</a></sup> it yield,<sup><a href="h6213.htm">6213</a></sup> the strangers<sup><a href="h2114.htm">2114</a></sup> shall swallow it up.<sup><a href="h1104.htm">1104</a></sup> </p>
 <p>8  Israel<sup><a href="h3478.htm">3478</a></sup> is swallowed up:<sup><a href="h1104.htm">1104</a></sup> now<sup><a href="h6258.htm">6258</a></sup> shall they be<sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> among the Gentiles<sup><a href="h1471.htm">1471</a></sup> as a vessel<sup><a href="h3627.htm">3627</a></sup> wherein is no<sup><a href="h0369.htm">369</a></sup> pleasure.<sup><a href="h2656.htm">2656</a></sup> </p>
 <p>9  For<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> they<sup><a href="h1992.htm">1992</a></sup> are gone up<sup><a href="h5927.htm">5927</a></sup> to Assyria,<sup><a href="h0804.htm">804</a></sup> a wild ass<sup><a href="h6501.htm">6501</a></sup> alone<sup><a href="h0909.htm">909</a></sup> by himself: Ephraim<sup><a href="h0669.htm">669</a></sup> hath hired<sup><a href="h8566.htm">8566</a></sup> lovers.<sup><a href="h0158.htm">158</a></sup> </p>
 <p>10  Yea,<sup><a href="h1571.htm">1571</a></sup> though<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> they have hired<sup><a href="h8566.htm">8566</a></sup> among the nations,<sup><a href="h1471.htm">1471</a></sup> now<sup><a href="h6258.htm">6258</a></sup> will I gather<sup><a href="h6908.htm">6908</a></sup> them, and they shall sorrow<sup><a href="h2490.htm">2490</a></sup> a little<sup><a href="h4592.htm">4592</a></sup> for the burden<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h4853.htm">4853</a></sup> of the king<sup><a href="h4428.htm">4428</a></sup> of princes.<sup><a href="h8269.htm">8269</a></sup> </p>
 <p>11  Because<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> Ephraim<sup><a href="h0669.htm">669</a></sup> hath made many<sup><a href="h7235.htm">7235</a></sup> altars<sup><a href="h4196.htm">4196</a></sup> to sin,<sup><a href="h2398.htm">2398</a></sup> altars<sup><a href="h4196.htm">4196</a></sup> shall be<sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> unto him to sin.<sup><a href="h2398.htm">2398</a></sup> </p>
 <p>12  I have written<sup><a href="h3789.htm">3789</a></sup> to him the great things<sup><a href="h7230.htm">7230</a></sup> of my law,<sup><a href="h8451.htm">8451</a></sup> but they were counted<sup><a href="h2803.htm">2803</a></sup> as<sup><a href="h3644.htm">3644</a></sup> a strange thing.<sup><a href="h2114.htm">2114</a></sup> </p>
 <p>13  They sacrifice<sup><a href="h2076.htm">2076</a></sup> flesh<sup><a href="h1320.htm">1320</a></sup> for the sacrifices<sup><a href="h2077.htm">2077</a></sup> of mine offerings,<sup><a href="h1890.htm">1890</a></sup> and eat<sup><a href="h0398.htm">398</a></sup> it; but the LORD<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> accepteth<sup><a href="h7521.htm">7521</a></sup> them not;<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> now<sup><a href="h6258.htm">6258</a></sup> will he remember<sup><a href="h2142.htm">2142</a></sup> their iniquity,<sup><a href="h5771.htm">5771</a></sup> and visit<sup><a href="h6485.htm">6485</a></sup> their sins:<sup><a href="h2403.htm">2403</a></sup> they<sup><a href="h1992.htm">1992</a></sup> shall return<sup><a href="h7725.htm">7725</a></sup> to Egypt.<sup><a href="h4714.htm">4714</a></sup> </p>
 <p>14  For Israel<sup><a href="h3478.htm">3478</a></sup> hath forgotten<sup><a href="h7911.htm">7911</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> his Maker,<sup><a href="h6213.htm">6213</a></sup> and buildeth<sup><a href="h1129.htm">1129</a></sup> temples;<sup><a href="h1964.htm">1964</a></sup> and Judah<sup><a href="h3063.htm">3063</a></sup> hath multiplied<sup><a href="h7235.htm">7235</a></sup> fenced<sup><a href="h1219.htm">1219</a></sup> cities:<sup><a href="h5892.htm">5892</a></sup> but I will send<sup><a href="h7971.htm">7971</a></sup> a fire<sup><a href="h0784.htm">784</a></sup> upon his cities,<sup><a href="h5892.htm">5892</a></sup> and it shall devour<sup><a href="h0398.htm">398</a></sup> the palaces<sup><a href="h0759.htm">759</a></sup> thereof. </p>

 <p>6 ἐν τῷ Ισραηλ καὶ αὐτὸ τέκτων ἐποίησεν καὶ οὐ θεός ἐστιν διότι πλανῶν ἦν ὁ μόσχος σου Σαμάρεια </p>
 <p>7 ὅτι ἀνεμόφθορα ἔσπειραν καὶ ἡ καταστροφὴ αὐτῶν ἐκδέξεται αὐτά δράγμα οὐκ ἔχον ἰσχὺν τοῦ ποιῆσαι ἄλευρον ἐὰν δὲ καὶ ποιήσῃ ἀλλότριοι καταφάγονται αὐτό </p>
 <p>8 κατεπόθη Ισραηλ νῦν ἐγένετο ἐν τοῖς ἔθνεσιν ὡς σκεῦος ἄχρηστον </p>

 <p>10 διὰ τοῦτο παραδοθήσονται ἐν τοῖς ἔθνεσιν νῦν εἰσδέξομαι αὐτούς καὶ κοπάσουσιν μικρὸν τοῦ χρίειν βασιλέα καὶ ἄρχοντας </p>
 <p>11 ὅτι ἐπλήθυνεν Εφραιμ θυσιαστήρια εἰς ἁμαρτίας ἐγένοντο αὐτῷ θυσιαστήρια ἠγαπημένα </p>
 <p>12 καταγράψω αὐτῷ πλῆθος καὶ τὰ νόμιμα αὐτοῦ εἰς ἀλλότρια ἐλογίσθησαν θυσιαστήρια τὰ ἠγαπημένα </p>
 <p>13 διότι ἐὰν θύσωσιν θυσίαν καὶ φάγωσιν κρέα κύριος οὐ προσδέξεται αὐτά νῦν μνησθήσεται τὰς ἀδικίας αὐτῶν καὶ ἐκδικήσει τὰς ἁμαρτίας αὐτῶν αὐτοὶ εἰς Αἴγυπτον ἀπέστρεψαν καὶ ἐν Ἀσσυρίοις ἀκάθαρτα φάγονται </p>
 <p>14 καὶ ἐπελάθετο Ισραηλ τοῦ ποιήσαντος αὐτὸν καὶ ᾠκοδόμησαν τεμένη καὶ Ιουδας ἐπλήθυνεν πόλεις τετειχισμένας καὶ ἐξαποστελῶ πῦρ εἰς τὰς πόλεις αὐτοῦ καὶ καταφάγεται τὰ θεμέλια αὐτῶν </p>
