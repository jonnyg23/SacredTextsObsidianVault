 <p>11  Wickedness<sup><a href="h1942.htm">1942</a></sup> is in the midst<sup><a href="h7130.htm">7130</a></sup> thereof: deceit<sup><a href="h8496.htm">8496</a></sup> and guile<sup><a href="h4820.htm">4820</a></sup> depart<sup><a href="h4185.htm">4185</a></sup> not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> from<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> her streets.<sup><a href="h7339.htm">7339</a></sup> </p>

 <p>13  But it was thou,<sup><a href="h0859.htm">859</a></sup> a man<sup><a href="h0582.htm">582</a></sup> mine equal,<sup><a href="h6187.htm">6187</a></sup> my guide,<sup><a href="h0441.htm">441</a></sup> and mine acquaintance.<sup><a href="h3045.htm">3045</a></sup> </p>
 <p>14  We took sweet<sup><a href="h4985.htm">4985</a></sup> counsel<sup><a href="h5475.htm">5475</a></sup> together,<sup><a href="h3162.htm">3162</a></sup> and walked<sup><a href="h1980.htm">1980</a></sup> unto the house<sup><a href="h1004.htm">1004</a></sup> of God<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> in company.<sup><a href="h7285.htm">7285</a></sup> </p>

 <p>16  As for me, I<sup><a href="h0589.htm">589</a></sup> will call<sup><a href="h7121.htm">7121</a></sup> upon<sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> God;<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> and the LORD<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> shall save<sup><a href="h3467.htm">3467</a></sup> me. </p>
 <p>17  Evening,<sup><a href="h6153.htm">6153</a></sup> and morning,<sup><a href="h1242.htm">1242</a></sup> and at noon,<sup><a href="h6672.htm">6672</a></sup> will I pray,<sup><a href="h7878.htm">7878</a></sup> and cry aloud:<sup><a href="h1993.htm">1993</a></sup> and he shall hear<sup><a href="h8085.htm">8085</a></sup> my voice.<sup><a href="h6963.htm">6963</a></sup> </p>

 <p align="center"><a href="../vul/psa055.htm">Vul</a></p><p>1 In finem, pro populo qui a sanctis longe factus est. David in tituli inscriptionem, cum tenuerunt eum Allophyli in Geth.</p>
 <p>2 [Miserere mei, Deus, quoniam conculcavit me homo;<br> tota die impugnans, tribulavit me.<br></p>
 <p>3 Conculcaverunt me inimici mei tota die,<br> quoniam multi bellantes adversum me.<br></p>
 <p>4 Ab altitudine diei timebo:<br> ego vero in te sperabo.<br></p>
 <p>5 In Deo laudabo sermones meos;<br> in Deo speravi:<br> non timebo quid faciat mihi caro.<br></p>
 <p>6 Tota die verba mea execrabantur;<br> adversum me omnes cogitationes eorum in malum.<br></p>
 <p>7 Inhabitabunt, et abscondent;<br> ipsi calcaneum meum observabunt.<br> Sicut sustinuerunt animam meam,<br></p>
 <p>8 pro nihilo salvos facies illos;<br> in ira populos confringes.<br></p>
 <p>9 Deus, vitam meam annuntiavi tibi;<br> posuisti lacrimas meas in conspectu tuo,<br> sicut et in promissione tua:<br></p>
 <p>10 tunc convertentur inimici mei retrorsum.<br> In quacumque die invocavero te,<br> ecce cognovi quoniam Deus meus es.<br></p>
 <p>11 In Deo laudabo verbum;<br> in Domino laudabo sermonem.<br> In Deo speravi:<br> non timebo quid faciat mihi homo.<br></p>
 <p>12 In me sunt, Deus, vota tua,<br> quæ reddam, laudationes tibi:<br></p>
 <p>13 quoniam eripuisti animam meam de morte,<br> et pedes meos de lapsu,<br> ut placeam coram Deo in lumine viventium.]</p>
