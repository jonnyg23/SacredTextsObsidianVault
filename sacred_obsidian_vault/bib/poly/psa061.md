 <p align="center"><a href="../kjv/psa061.htm">KJV</a></p><p>1  To the chief Musician<sup><a href="h5329.htm">5329</a></sup> upon<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> Neginah,<sup><a href="h5058.htm">5058</a></sup> A Psalm of David.<sup><a href="h1732.htm">1732</a></sup> Hear<sup><a href="h8085.htm">8085</a></sup> my cry,<sup><a href="h7440.htm">7440</a></sup> O God;<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> attend unto<sup><a href="h7181.htm">7181</a></sup> my prayer.<sup><a href="h8605.htm">8605</a></sup> </p>
 <p>2  From the end<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h7097.htm">7097</a></sup> of the earth<sup><a href="h0776.htm">776</a></sup> will I cry<sup><a href="h7121.htm">7121</a></sup> unto<sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> thee, when my heart<sup><a href="h3820.htm">3820</a></sup> is overwhelmed:<sup><a href="h5848.htm">5848</a></sup> lead<sup><a href="h5148.htm">5148</a></sup> me to the rock<sup><a href="h6697.htm">6697</a></sup> that is higher<sup><a href="h7311.htm">7311</a></sup> than<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> I. </p>

 <p>4  I will abide<sup><a href="h1481.htm">1481</a></sup> in thy tabernacle<sup><a href="h0168.htm">168</a></sup> forever:<sup><a href="h5769.htm">5769</a></sup> I will trust<sup><a href="h2620.htm">2620</a></sup> in the covert<sup><a href="h5643.htm">5643</a></sup> of thy wings.<sup><a href="h3671.htm">3671</a></sup> Selah.<sup><a href="h5542.htm">5542</a></sup> </p>
 <p>5  For<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> thou,<sup><a href="h0859.htm">859</a></sup> O God,<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> hast heard<sup><a href="h8085.htm">8085</a></sup> my vows:<sup><a href="h5088.htm">5088</a></sup> thou hast given<sup><a href="h5414.htm">5414</a></sup> me the heritage<sup><a href="h3425.htm">3425</a></sup> of those that fear<sup><a href="h3373.htm">3373</a></sup> thy name.<sup><a href="h8034.htm">8034</a></sup> </p>
 <p>6  Thou wilt prolong<sup><a href="h3254.htm">3254</a></sup> the king's<sup><a href="h4428.htm">4428</a></sup> life:<sup><a href="h3117.htm">3117</a></sup> <sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3117.htm">3117</a></sup> and his years<sup><a href="h8141.htm">8141</a></sup> as<sup><a href="h3644.htm">3644</a></sup> many generations.<sup><a href="h1755.htm">1755</a></sup> <sup><a href="h1755.htm">1755</a></sup> </p>
 <p>7  He shall abide<sup><a href="h3427.htm">3427</a></sup> before<sup><a href="h6440.htm">6440</a></sup> God<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> forever:<sup><a href="h5769.htm">5769</a></sup> O prepare<sup><a href="h4487.htm">4487</a></sup> mercy<sup><a href="h2617.htm">2617</a></sup> and truth,<sup><a href="h0571.htm">571</a></sup> which may preserve<sup><a href="h5341.htm">5341</a></sup> him. </p>
 <p>8  So<sup><a href="h3651.htm">3651</a></sup> will I sing praise<sup><a href="h2167.htm">2167</a></sup> unto thy name<sup><a href="h8034.htm">8034</a></sup> forever,<sup><a href="h5703.htm">5703</a></sup> that I may daily<sup><a href="h3117.htm">3117</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3117.htm">3117</a></sup> perform<sup><a href="h7999.htm">7999</a></sup> my vows.<sup><a href="h5088.htm">5088</a></sup> </p>

 <p align="center"><a href="../sep/psa061.htm">Sep</a></p><p>1 εἰς τὸ τέλος ὑπὲρ Ιδιθουν ψαλμὸς τῷ Δαυιδ </p>
 <p>2 οὐχὶ τῷ θεῷ ὑποταγήσεται ἡ ψυχή μου παρ᾽ αὐτοῦ γὰρ τὸ σωτήριόν μου </p>
 <p>3 καὶ γὰρ αὐτὸς θεός μου καὶ σωτήρ μου ἀντιλήμπτωρ μου οὐ μὴ σαλευθῶ ἐπὶ πλεῖον </p>
 <p>4 ἕως πότε ἐπιτίθεσθε ἐπ᾽ ἄνθρωπον φονεύετε πάντες ὡς τοίχῳ κεκλιμένῳ καὶ φραγμῷ ὠσμένῳ </p>
 <p>5 πλὴν τὴν τιμήν μου ἐβουλεύσαντο ἀπώσασθαι ἔδραμον ἐν ψεύδει τῷ στόματι αὐτῶν εὐλογοῦσαν καὶ τῇ καρδίᾳ αὐτῶν κατηρῶντο διάψαλμα </p>
 <p>6 πλὴν τῷ θεῷ ὑποτάγηθι ἡ ψυχή μου ὅτι παρ᾽ αὐτοῦ ἡ ὑπομονή μου </p>
 <p>7 ὅτι αὐτὸς θεός μου καὶ σωτήρ μου ἀντιλήμπτωρ μου οὐ μὴ μεταναστεύσω </p>
 <p>8 ἐπὶ τῷ θεῷ τὸ σωτήριόν μου καὶ ἡ δόξα μου ὁ θεὸς τῆς βοηθείας μου καὶ ἡ ἐλπίς μου ἐπὶ τῷ θεῷ </p>
 <p>9 ἐλπίσατε ἐπ᾽ αὐτόν πᾶσα συναγωγὴ λαοῦ ἐκχέετε ἐνώπιον αὐτοῦ τὰς καρδίας ὑμῶν ὁ θεὸς βοηθὸς ἡμῶν διάψαλμα </p>
 <p>10 πλὴν μάταιοι οἱ υἱοὶ τῶν ἀνθρώπων ψευδεῖς οἱ υἱοὶ τῶν ἀνθρώπων ἐν ζυγοῖς τοῦ ἀδικῆσαι αὐτοὶ ἐκ ματαιότητος ἐπὶ τὸ αὐτό </p>
 <p>11 μὴ ἐλπίζετε ἐπὶ ἀδικίαν καὶ ἐπὶ ἅρπαγμα μὴ ἐπιποθεῖτε πλοῦτος ἐὰν ῥέῃ μὴ προστίθεσθε καρδίαν </p>
 <p>12 ἅπαξ ἐλάλησεν ὁ θεός δύο ταῦτα ἤκουσα </p>
 <p>13 ὅτι τὸ κράτος τοῦ θεοῦ καὶ σοί κύριε τὸ ἔλεος ὅτι σὺ ἀποδώσεις ἑκάστῳ κατὰ τὰ ἔργα αὐτοῦ </p>

 <p align="center"><a href="../vul/psa061.htm">Vul</a></p><p>1 In finem, pro Idithun. Psalmus David.</p>
 <p>2 [Nonne Deo subjecta erit anima mea?<br> ab ipso enim salutare meum.<br></p>
 <p>3 Nam et ipse Deus meus et salutaris meus;<br> susceptor meus, non movebor amplius.<br></p>
 <p>4 Quousque irruitis in hominem?<br> interficitis universi vos,<br> tamquam parieti inclinato et maceriæ depulsæ.<br></p>
 <p>5 Verumtamen pretium meum cogitaverunt repellere;<br> cucurri in siti:<br> ore suo benedicebant,<br> et corde suo maledicebant.<br></p>
 <p>6 Verumtamen Deo subjecta esto, anima mea,<br> quoniam ab ipso patientia mea:<br></p>
 <p>7 quia ipse Deus meus et salvator meus,<br> adjutor meus, non emigrabo.<br></p>
 <p>8 In Deo salutare meum et gloria mea;<br> Deus auxilii mei, et spes mea in Deo est.<br></p>
 <p>9 Sperate in eo, omnis congregatio populi;<br> effundite coram illo corda vestra:<br> Deus adjutor noster in æternum.<br></p>
 <p>10 Verumtamen vani filii hominum,<br> mendaces filii hominum in stateris,<br> ut decipiant ipsi de vanitate in idipsum.<br></p>
 <p>11 Nolite sperare in iniquitate,<br> et rapinas nolite concupiscere;<br> divitiæ si affluant, nolite cor apponere.<br></p>
 <p>12 Semel locutus est Deus;<br> duo hæc audivi:<br> quia potestas Dei est,</p>
 <p>13 et tibi, Domine, misericordia:<br> quia tu reddes unicuique juxta opera sua.]</p>
