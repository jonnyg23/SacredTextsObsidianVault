 <p>18  I will pay<sup><a href="h7999.htm">7999</a></sup> my vows<sup><a href="h5088.htm">5088</a></sup> unto the LORD<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> now<sup><a href="h4994.htm">4994</a></sup> in the presence<sup><a href="h5048.htm">5048</a></sup> of all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> his people,<sup><a href="h5971.htm">5971</a></sup> </p>
