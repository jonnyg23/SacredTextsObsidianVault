  <p>H2763<br>  חרם  <br> חָרַם  ‎  châram  <br><i>khaw-ram‘ </i><br>A primitive root; to <i>seclude</i>; specifically (by a ban) to <i>devote</i> to religious uses (especially destruction); physically and reflexively to be <i>blunt</i> as to the nose: - make accursed, consecrate, (utterly) destroy, devote, forfeit, have a flat nose, utterly (slay, make away).<br></p>
