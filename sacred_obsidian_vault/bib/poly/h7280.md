  <p>H7280<br>  רגע  <br> רָגַע  ‎  râga‛  <br><i>raw-gah‘ </i><br>A primitive root; properly to <i>toss</i> violently and suddenly (the sea with waves, the skin with boils); figuratively (in a favorable manner) to <i>settle</i>, that is, quiet; specifically to <i>wink</i> (from the motion of the eye lids): - break, divide, find ease, be a moment, (cause, give, make to) rest, make suddenly.<br></p>
