 <p align="center"><a href="../kjv/psa115.htm">KJV</a></p><p>1  Not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> unto us, O LORD,<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> unto us, but<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> unto thy name<sup><a href="h8034.htm">8034</a></sup> give<sup><a href="h5414.htm">5414</a></sup> glory,<sup><a href="h3519.htm">3519</a></sup> for<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> thy mercy,<sup><a href="h2617.htm">2617</a></sup> and for<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> thy truth's<sup><a href="h0571.htm">571</a></sup> sake. </p>
 <p>2  Wherefore<sup><a href="h4100.htm">4100</a></sup> should the heathen<sup><a href="h1471.htm">1471</a></sup> say,<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> Where<sup><a href="h0346.htm">346</a></sup> is now<sup><a href="h4994.htm">4994</a></sup> their God?<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> </p>

 <p>5  They have mouths,<sup><a href="h6310.htm">6310</a></sup> but they speak<sup><a href="h1696.htm">1696</a></sup> not:<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> eyes<sup><a href="h5869.htm">5869</a></sup> have they, but they see<sup><a href="h7200.htm">7200</a></sup> not:<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> </p>

 <p>7  They have hands,<sup><a href="h3027.htm">3027</a></sup> but they handle<sup><a href="h4184.htm">4184</a></sup> not:<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> feet<sup><a href="h7272.htm">7272</a></sup> have they, but they walk<sup><a href="h1980.htm">1980</a></sup> not:<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> neither<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> speak<sup><a href="h1897.htm">1897</a></sup> they through their throat.<sup><a href="h1627.htm">1627</a></sup> </p>

 <p>14  The LORD<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> shall increase you more and more,<sup><a href="h3254.htm">3254</a></sup> <sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> you and your children.<sup><a href="h1121.htm">1121</a></sup> </p>
 <p>15  Ye<sup><a href="h0859.htm">859</a></sup> are blessed<sup><a href="h1288.htm">1288</a></sup> of the LORD<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> which made<sup><a href="h6213.htm">6213</a></sup> heaven<sup><a href="h8064.htm">8064</a></sup> and earth.<sup><a href="h0776.htm">776</a></sup> </p>

 <p>17  The dead<sup><a href="h4191.htm">4191</a></sup> praise<sup><a href="h1984.htm">1984</a></sup> not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> the LORD,<sup><a href="h3050.htm">3050</a></sup> neither<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> any<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> that go down<sup><a href="h3381.htm">3381</a></sup> into silence.<sup><a href="h1745.htm">1745</a></sup> </p>
