  <p>H2224<br>  זרח  <br> זָרַח  ‎  zârach  <br><i>zaw-rakh‘ </i><br>A primitive root; properly to <i>irradiate</i> (or shoot forth beams), that is, to <i>rise</i> (as the sun); specifically to <i>appear</i> (as a symptom of leprosy): - arise, rise (up), as soon as it is up.<br></p>
