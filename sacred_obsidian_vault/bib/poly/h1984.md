  <p>H1984<br>  הלל  <br> הָלַל  ‎  hâlal  <br><i>haw-lal‘ </i><br>A primitive root; to <i>be</i> <i>clear</i> (originally of sound, but usually of color); to <i>shine</i>; hence to <i>make</i> <i>a</i> <i>show</i>; to <i>boast</i>; and thus to <i>be</i> (clamorously) <i>foolish</i>; to <i>rave</i>; causatively to <i>celebrate</i>; also to <i>stultify: - </i>(make) boast (self), celebrate, commend, (deal, make), fool (-ish, -ly), glory, give [light], be (make, feign self) mad (against), give in marriage, [sing, be worthy of] praise, rage, renowned, shine.<br></p>
