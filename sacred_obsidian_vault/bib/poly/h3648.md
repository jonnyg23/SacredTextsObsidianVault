  <p>H3648<br>  כּמר  <br> כָּמַר  ‎  kâmar  <br><i>kaw-mar‘ </i><br>A primitive root; properly to <i>intertwine</i> or <i>contract</i>, that is, (by implication) to <i>shrivel</i> (as with heat); figuratively to <i>be</i> deeply <i>affected</i> with passion (love or pity): - be black, be kindled, yearn.<br></p>
