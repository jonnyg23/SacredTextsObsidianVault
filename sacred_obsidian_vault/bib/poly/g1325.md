  <p>G1325<br>  δίδωμι  <br> didōmi  <br><i>did‘-o-mee </i><br>A prolonged form of a primary verb (which is used as an alternate in most of the tenses); to <i>give</i> (used in a very wide application, properly or by implication, literally or figuratively; greatly modified by the connection): - adventure, bestow, bring forth, commit, deliver (up), give, grant, hinder, make, minister, number, offer, have power, put, receive, set, shew, smite (+ with the hand), strike (+ with the palm of the hand), suffer, take, utter, yield.<br></p>
