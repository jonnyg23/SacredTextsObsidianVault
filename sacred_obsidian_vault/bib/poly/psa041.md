 <p align="center"><a href="../kjv/psa041.htm">KJV</a></p><p>1  To the chief Musician,<sup><a href="h5329.htm">5329</a></sup> A Psalm<sup><a href="h4210.htm">4210</a></sup> of David.<sup><a href="h1732.htm">1732</a></sup> Blessed<sup><a href="h0835.htm">835</a></sup> is he that considereth<sup><a href="h7919.htm">7919</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> the poor:<sup><a href="h1800.htm">1800</a></sup> the LORD<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> will deliver<sup><a href="h4422.htm">4422</a></sup> him in time<sup><a href="h3117.htm">3117</a></sup> of trouble.<sup><a href="h7451.htm">7451</a></sup> </p>
 <p>2  The LORD<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> will preserve<sup><a href="h8104.htm">8104</a></sup> him, and keep him alive;<sup><a href="h2421.htm">2421</a></sup> and he shall be blessed<sup><a href="h0833.htm">833</a></sup> upon the earth:<sup><a href="h0776.htm">776</a></sup> and thou wilt not<sup><a href="h0408.htm">408</a></sup> deliver<sup><a href="h5414.htm">5414</a></sup> him unto the will<sup><a href="h5315.htm">5315</a></sup> of his enemies.<sup><a href="h0341.htm">341</a></sup> </p>
 <p>3  The LORD<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> will strengthen<sup><a href="h5582.htm">5582</a></sup> him upon<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> the bed<sup><a href="h6210.htm">6210</a></sup> of languishing:<sup><a href="h1741.htm">1741</a></sup> thou wilt make<sup><a href="h2015.htm">2015</a></sup> all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> his bed<sup><a href="h4904.htm">4904</a></sup> in his sickness.<sup><a href="h2483.htm">2483</a></sup> </p>
 <p>4  I<sup><a href="h0589.htm">589</a></sup> said,<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> LORD,<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> be merciful<sup><a href="h2603.htm">2603</a></sup> unto me: heal<sup><a href="h7495.htm">7495</a></sup> my soul;<sup><a href="h5315.htm">5315</a></sup> for<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> I have sinned<sup><a href="h2398.htm">2398</a></sup> against thee. </p>
 <p>5  Mine enemies<sup><a href="h0341.htm">341</a></sup> speak<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> evil<sup><a href="h7451.htm">7451</a></sup> of me, When<sup><a href="h4970.htm">4970</a></sup> shall he die,<sup><a href="h4191.htm">4191</a></sup> and his name<sup><a href="h8034.htm">8034</a></sup> perish?<sup><a href="h0006.htm">6</a></sup> </p>
 <p>6  And if<sup><a href="h0518.htm">518</a></sup> he come<sup><a href="h0935.htm">935</a></sup> to see<sup><a href="h7200.htm">7200</a></sup> me, he speaketh<sup><a href="h1696.htm">1696</a></sup> vanity:<sup><a href="h7723.htm">7723</a></sup> his heart<sup><a href="h3820.htm">3820</a></sup> gathereth<sup><a href="h6908.htm">6908</a></sup> iniquity<sup><a href="h0205.htm">205</a></sup> to itself; when he goeth<sup><a href="h3318.htm">3318</a></sup> abroad,<sup><a href="h2351.htm">2351</a></sup> he telleth<sup><a href="h1696.htm">1696</a></sup> it. </p>
 <p>7  All<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> that hate<sup><a href="h8130.htm">8130</a></sup> me whisper<sup><a href="h3907.htm">3907</a></sup> together<sup><a href="h3162.htm">3162</a></sup> against<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> me: against<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> me do they devise<sup><a href="h2803.htm">2803</a></sup> my hurt.<sup><a href="h7451.htm">7451</a></sup> </p>
 <p>8  An evil<sup><a href="h1100.htm">1100</a></sup> disease,<sup><a href="h1697.htm">1697</a></sup> say they, cleaveth fast<sup><a href="h3332.htm">3332</a></sup> unto him: and now that<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> he lieth<sup><a href="h7901.htm">7901</a></sup> he shall rise up<sup><a href="h6965.htm">6965</a></sup> no<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> more.<sup><a href="h3254.htm">3254</a></sup> </p>
 <p>9  Yea,<sup><a href="h1571.htm">1571</a></sup> mine own familiar friend,<sup><a href="h7965.htm">7965</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0376.htm">376</a></sup> in whom<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> I trusted,<sup><a href="h0982.htm">982</a></sup> which did eat<sup><a href="h0398.htm">398</a></sup> of my bread,<sup><a href="h3899.htm">3899</a></sup> hath lifted up<sup><a href="h1431.htm">1431</a></sup> his heel<sup><a href="h6119.htm">6119</a></sup> against<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> me. </p>
 <p>10  But thou,<sup><a href="h0859.htm">859</a></sup> O LORD,<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> be merciful<sup><a href="h2603.htm">2603</a></sup> unto me, and raise me up,<sup><a href="h6965.htm">6965</a></sup> that I may requite<sup><a href="h7999.htm">7999</a></sup> them. </p>
 <p>11  By this<sup><a href="h2063.htm">2063</a></sup> I know<sup><a href="h3045.htm">3045</a></sup> that<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> thou favorest<sup><a href="h2654.htm">2654</a></sup> me, because<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> mine enemy<sup><a href="h0341.htm">341</a></sup> doth not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> triumph<sup><a href="h7321.htm">7321</a></sup> over<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> me. </p>
 <p>12  And as for me,<sup><a href="h0589.htm">589</a></sup> thou upholdest<sup><a href="h8551.htm">8551</a></sup> me in mine integrity,<sup><a href="h8537.htm">8537</a></sup> and settest<sup><a href="h5324.htm">5324</a></sup> me before thy face<sup><a href="h6440.htm">6440</a></sup> forever.<sup><a href="h5769.htm">5769</a></sup> </p>
 <p>13  Blessed<sup><a href="h1288.htm">1288</a></sup> be the LORD<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> God<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> of Israel<sup><a href="h3478.htm">3478</a></sup> from everlasting,<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h5769.htm">5769</a></sup> and to<sup><a href="h5704.htm">5704</a></sup> everlasting.<sup><a href="h5769.htm">5769</a></sup> Amen,<sup><a href="h0543.htm">543</a></sup> and Amen.<sup><a href="h0543.htm">543</a></sup> </p>

 <p align="center"><a href="../sep/psa041.htm">Sep</a></p><p>1 εἰς τὸ τέλος εἰς σύνεσιν τοῖς υἱοῖς Κορε </p>
 <p>2 ὃν τρόπον ἐπιποθεῖ ἡ ἔλαφος ἐπὶ τὰς πηγὰς τῶν ὑδάτων οὕτως ἐπιποθεῖ ἡ ψυχή μου πρὸς σέ ὁ θεός </p>
 <p>3 ἐδίψησεν ἡ ψυχή μου πρὸς τὸν θεὸν τὸν ζῶντα πότε ἥξω καὶ ὀφθήσομαι τῷ προσώπῳ τοῦ θεοῦ </p>
 <p>4 ἐγενήθη μοι τὰ δάκρυά μου ἄρτος ἡμέρας καὶ νυκτὸς ἐν τῷ λέγεσθαί μοι καθ᾽ ἑκάστην ἡμέραν ποῦ ἐστιν ὁ θεός σου </p>
 <p>5 ταῦτα ἐμνήσθην καὶ ἐξέχεα ἐπ᾽ ἐμὲ τὴν ψυχήν μου ὅτι διελεύσομαι ἐν τόπῳ σκηνῆς θαυμαστῆς ἕως τοῦ οἴκου τοῦ θεοῦ ἐν φωνῇ ἀγαλλιάσεως καὶ ἐξομολογήσεως ἤχου ἑορτάζοντος </p>
 <p>6 ἵνα τί περίλυπος εἶ ψυχή καὶ ἵνα τί συνταράσσεις με ἔλπισον ἐπὶ τὸν θεόν ὅτι ἐξομολογήσομαι αὐτῷ σωτήριον τοῦ προσώπου μου ὁ θεός μου </p>
 <p>7 πρὸς ἐμαυτὸν ἡ ψυχή μου ἐταράχθη διὰ τοῦτο μνησθήσομαί σου ἐκ γῆς Ιορδάνου καὶ Ερμωνιιμ ἀπὸ ὄρους μικροῦ </p>
 <p>8 ἄβυσσος ἄβυσσον ἐπικαλεῖται εἰς φωνὴν τῶν καταρρακτῶν σου πάντες οἱ μετεωρισμοί σου καὶ τὰ κύματά σου ἐπ᾽ ἐμὲ διῆλθον </p>
 <p>9 ἡμέρας ἐντελεῖται κύριος τὸ ἔλεος αὐτοῦ καὶ νυκτὸς ᾠδὴ παρ᾽ ἐμοί προσευχὴ τῷ θεῷ τῆς ζωῆς μου </p>
 <p>10 ἐρῶ τῷ θεῷ ἀντιλήμπτωρ μου εἶ διὰ τί μου ἐπελάθου ἵνα τί σκυθρωπάζων πορεύομαι ἐν τῷ ἐκθλίβειν τὸν ἐχθρόν μου </p>
 <p>11 ἐν τῷ καταθλάσαι τὰ ὀστᾶ μου ὠνείδισάν με οἱ θλίβοντές με ἐν τῷ λέγειν αὐτούς μοι καθ᾽ ἑκάστην ἡμέραν ποῦ ἐστιν ὁ θεός σου </p>
 <p>12 ἵνα τί περίλυπος εἶ ψυχή καὶ ἵνα τί συνταράσσεις με ἔλπισον ἐπὶ τὸν θεόν ὅτι ἐξομολογήσομαι αὐτῷ ἡ σωτηρία τοῦ προσώπου μου ὁ θεός μου </p>

 <p align="center"><a href="../vul/psa041.htm">Vul</a></p><p>1 In finem. Intellectus filiis Core.</p>
 <p>2 [Quemadmodum desiderat cervus ad fontes aquarum,<br> ita desiderat anima mea ad te, Deus.<br></p>
 <p>3 Sitivit anima mea ad Deum fortem, vivum;<br> quando veniam, et apparebo ante faciem Dei?<br></p>
 <p>4 Fuerunt mihi lacrimæ meæ panes die ac nocte,<br> dum dicitur mihi quotidie: Ubi est Deus tuus?<br></p>
 <p>5 Hæc recordatus sum, et effudi in me animam meam,<br> quoniam transibo in locum tabernaculi admirabilis, usque ad domum Dei,<br> in voce exsultationis et confessionis, sonus epulantis.<br></p>
 <p>6 Quare tristis es, anima mea?<br> et quare conturbas me?<br> Spera in Deo, quoniam adhuc confitebor illi,<br> salutare vultus mei,<br></p>
 <p>7 et Deus meus.<br> Ad meipsum anima mea conturbata est:<br> propterea memor ero tui de terra Jordanis et Hermoniim a monte modico.<br></p>
 <p>8 Abyssus abyssum invocat, in voce cataractarum tuarum;<br> omnia excelsa tua, et fluctus tui super me transierunt.<br></p>
 <p>9 In die mandavit Dominus misericordiam suam,<br> et nocte canticum ejus;<br> apud me oratio Deo vitæ meæ.<br></p>
 <p>10 Dicam Deo: Susceptor meus es;<br> quare oblitus es mei?<br> et quare contristatus incedo, dum affligit me inimicus?<br></p>
 <p>11 Dum confringuntur ossa mea,<br> exprobraverunt mihi qui tribulant me inimici mei,<br> dum dicunt mihi per singulos dies: Ubi est Deus tuus?<br></p>
 <p>12 Quare tristis es, anima mea?<br> et quare conturbas me?<br> Spera in Deo, quoniam adhuc confitebor illi,<br> salutare vultus mei, et Deus meus.]</p>
