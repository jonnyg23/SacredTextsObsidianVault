 <p>2  But as for me,<sup><a href="h0589.htm">589</a></sup> my feet<sup><a href="h7272.htm">7272</a></sup> were almost<sup><a href="h4592.htm">4592</a></sup> gone;<sup><a href="h5186.htm">5186</a></sup> my steps<sup><a href="h0838.htm">838</a></sup> had well nigh<sup><a href="h0369.htm">369</a></sup> slipped.<sup><a href="h8210.htm">8210</a></sup> </p>

 <p>7  Their eyes<sup><a href="h5869.htm">5869</a></sup> stand out<sup><a href="h3318.htm">3318</a></sup> with fatness:<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h2459.htm">2459</a></sup> they have more<sup><a href="h5674.htm">5674</a></sup> than heart<sup><a href="h3824.htm">3824</a></sup> could wish.<sup><a href="h4906.htm">4906</a></sup> </p>
 <p>8  They are corrupt,<sup><a href="h4167.htm">4167</a></sup> and speak<sup><a href="h1696.htm">1696</a></sup> wickedly<sup><a href="h7451.htm">7451</a></sup> concerning oppression:<sup><a href="h6233.htm">6233</a></sup> they speak<sup><a href="h1696.htm">1696</a></sup> loftily.<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h4791.htm">4791</a></sup> </p>

 <p>16  When I thought<sup><a href="h2803.htm">2803</a></sup> to know<sup><a href="h3045.htm">3045</a></sup> this,<sup><a href="h2063.htm">2063</a></sup> it<sup><a href="h1931.htm">1931</a></sup> was too painful<sup><a href="h5999.htm">5999</a></sup> for<sup><a href="h5869.htm">5869</a></sup> me; </p>

 <p align="center"><a href="../vul/psa073.htm">Vul</a></p><p>1 Intellectus Asaph. [Ut quid, Deus, repulisti in finem,<br> iratus est furor tuus super oves pascuæ tuæ?<br></p>
 <p>2 Memor esto congregationis tuæ,<br> quam possedisti ab initio.<br> Redemisti virgam hæreditatis tuæ,<br> mons Sion, in quo habitasti in eo.<br></p>
 <p>3 Leva manus tuas in superbias eorum in finem:<br> quanta malignatus est inimicus in sancto!<br></p>
 <p>4 Et gloriati sunt qui oderunt te in medio solemnitatis tuæ;<br> posuerunt signa sua, signa:<br></p>
 <p>5 et non cognoverunt sicut in exitu super summum.<br> Quasi in silva lignorum securibus<br></p>
 <p>6 exciderunt januas ejus in idipsum;<br> in securi et ascia dejecerunt eam.<br></p>
 <p>7 Incenderunt igni sanctuarium tuum;<br> in terra polluerunt tabernaculum nominis tui.<br></p>
 <p>8 Dixerunt in corde suo cognatio eorum simul:<br> Quiescere faciamus omnes dies festos Dei a terra.<br></p>
 <p>9 Signa nostra non vidimus;<br> jam non est propheta;<br> et nos non cognoscet amplius.<br></p>
 <p>10 Usquequo, Deus, improperabit inimicus?<br> irritat adversarius nomen tuum in finem?<br></p>
 <p>11 Ut quid avertis manum tuam,<br> et dexteram tuam de medio sinu tuo in finem?<br></p>
 <p>12 Deus autem rex noster ante sæcula:<br> operatus est salutem in medio terræ.<br></p>
 <p>13 Tu confirmasti in virtute tua mare;<br> contribulasti capita draconum in aquis.<br></p>
 <p>14 Tu confregisti capita draconis;<br> dedisti eum escam populis Æthiopum.<br></p>
 <p>15 Tu dirupisti fontes et torrentes;<br> tu siccasti fluvios Ethan.<br></p>
 <p>16 Tuus est dies, et tua est nox;<br> tu fabricatus es auroram et solem.<br></p>
 <p>17 Tu fecisti omnes terminos terræ;<br> æstatem et ver tu plasmasti ea.<br></p>
 <p>18 Memor esto hujus: inimicus improperavit Domino,<br> et populus insipiens incitavit nomen tuum.<br></p>
 <p>19 Ne tradas bestiis animas confitentes tibi,<br> et animas pauperum tuorum ne obliviscaris in finem.<br></p>
 <p>20 Respice in testamentum tuum,<br> quia repleti sunt qui obscurati sunt terræ domibus iniquitatum.<br></p>
 <p>21 Ne avertatur humilis factus confusus;<br> pauper et inops laudabunt nomen tuum.<br></p>
 <p>22 Exsurge, Deus, judica causam tuam;<br> memor esto improperiorum tuorum,<br> eorum quæ ab insipiente sunt tota die.<br></p>
 <p>23 Ne obliviscaris voces inimicorum tuorum:<br> superbia eorum qui te oderunt ascendit semper.]</p>
