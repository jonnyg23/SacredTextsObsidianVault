  <p>H8199<br>  שׁפט  <br> שָׁפַט  ‎  shâphaṭ  <br><i>shaw-fat‘ </i><br>A primitive root; to <i>judge</i>, that is, pronounce <i>sentence</i> (for or against); by implication to <i>vindicate</i> or <i>punish</i>; by extension to <i>govern</i>; passively to <i>litigate</i> (literally or figuratively): -  + avenge, X that condemn, contend, defend, execute (judgment), (be a) judge (-ment), X needs, plead, reason, rule.<br></p>
