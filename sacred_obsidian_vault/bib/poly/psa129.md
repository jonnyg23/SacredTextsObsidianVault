 <p>7  Wherewith the mower<sup><a href="h7114.htm">7114</a></sup> filleth<sup><a href="h4390.htm">4390</a></sup> not<sup><a href="h7945.htm">7945</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> his hand;<sup><a href="h3709.htm">3709</a></sup> nor he that bindeth<sup><a href="h6014.htm">6014</a></sup> sheaves his bosom.<sup><a href="h2683.htm">2683</a></sup> </p>
