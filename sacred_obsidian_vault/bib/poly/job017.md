 <h3 align="CENTER">Job 17</h3><table border="1">

 <td valign="top">
 <p align="center"><a href="../kjv/job017.htm">KJV</a></p><p>1  My breath<sup><a href="h7307.htm">7307</a></sup> is corrupt,<sup><a href="h2254.htm">2254</a></sup> my days<sup><a href="h3117.htm">3117</a></sup> are extinct,<sup><a href="h2193.htm">2193</a></sup> the graves<sup><a href="h6913.htm">6913</a></sup> are ready for me. </p>
 <p>2  Are there not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> mockers<sup><a href="h2049.htm">2049</a></sup> with<sup><a href="h5978.htm">5978</a></sup> me? and doth not mine eye<sup><a href="h5869.htm">5869</a></sup> continue<sup><a href="h3885.htm">3885</a></sup> in their provocation?<sup><a href="h4784.htm">4784</a></sup> </p>
 <p>3  Lay down<sup><a href="h7760.htm">7760</a></sup> now,<sup><a href="h4994.htm">4994</a></sup> put me in a surety<sup><a href="h6148.htm">6148</a></sup> with<sup><a href="h5973.htm">5973</a></sup> thee; who<sup><a href="h4310.htm">4310</a></sup> is he<sup><a href="h1931.htm">1931</a></sup> that will strike<sup><a href="h8628.htm">8628</a></sup> hands<sup><a href="h3027.htm">3027</a></sup> with me? </p>
 <p>4  For<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> thou hast hid<sup><a href="h6845.htm">6845</a></sup> their heart<sup><a href="h3820.htm">3820</a></sup> from understanding:<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h7922.htm">7922</a></sup> therefore<sup><a href="h3651.htm">3651</a></sup> <sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> shalt thou not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> exalt<sup><a href="h7311.htm">7311</a></sup> them. </p>
 <p>5  He that speaketh<sup><a href="h5046.htm">5046</a></sup> flattery<sup><a href="h2506.htm">2506</a></sup> to his friends,<sup><a href="h7453.htm">7453</a></sup> even the eyes<sup><a href="h5869.htm">5869</a></sup> of his children<sup><a href="h1121.htm">1121</a></sup> shall fail.<sup><a href="h3615.htm">3615</a></sup> </p>
 <p>6  He hath made<sup><a href="h3322.htm">3322</a></sup> me also a byword<sup><a href="h4914.htm">4914</a></sup> of the people;<sup><a href="h5971.htm">5971</a></sup> and formerly<sup><a href="h6440.htm">6440</a></sup> I was<sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> as a tabret.<sup><a href="h8611.htm">8611</a></sup> </p>
 <p>7  Mine eye<sup><a href="h5869.htm">5869</a></sup> also is dim<sup><a href="h3543.htm">3543</a></sup> by reason of sorrow,<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3708.htm">3708</a></sup> and all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> my members<sup><a href="h3338.htm">3338</a></sup> are as a shadow.<sup><a href="h6738.htm">6738</a></sup> </p>
 <p>8  Upright<sup><a href="h3477.htm">3477</a></sup> men shall be astonished<sup><a href="h8074.htm">8074</a></sup> at<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> this,<sup><a href="h2063.htm">2063</a></sup> and the innocent<sup><a href="h5355.htm">5355</a></sup> shall stir up himself<sup><a href="h5782.htm">5782</a></sup> against<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> the hypocrite.<sup><a href="h2611.htm">2611</a></sup> </p>
 <p>9  The righteous<sup><a href="h6662.htm">6662</a></sup> also shall hold<sup><a href="h0270.htm">270</a></sup> on his way,<sup><a href="h1870.htm">1870</a></sup> and he that hath clean<sup><a href="h2889.htm">2889</a></sup> hands<sup><a href="h3027.htm">3027</a></sup> shall be stronger and stronger.<sup><a href="h3254.htm">3254</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0555.htm">555</a></sup> </p>
 <p>10  But<sup><a href="h0199.htm">199</a></sup> as for you all,<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> do ye return,<sup><a href="h7725.htm">7725</a></sup> and come<sup><a href="h0935.htm">935</a></sup> now:<sup><a href="h4994.htm">4994</a></sup> for I cannot<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> find<sup><a href="h4672.htm">4672</a></sup> one wise<sup><a href="h2450.htm">2450</a></sup> man among you. </p>
 <p>11  My days<sup><a href="h3117.htm">3117</a></sup> are past,<sup><a href="h5674.htm">5674</a></sup> my purposes<sup><a href="h2154.htm">2154</a></sup> are broken off,<sup><a href="h5423.htm">5423</a></sup> even the thoughts<sup><a href="h4180.htm">4180</a></sup> of my heart.<sup><a href="h3824.htm">3824</a></sup> </p>
 <p>12  They change<sup><a href="h7760.htm">7760</a></sup> the night<sup><a href="h3915.htm">3915</a></sup> into day:<sup><a href="h3117.htm">3117</a></sup> the light<sup><a href="h0216.htm">216</a></sup> is short<sup><a href="h7138.htm">7138</a></sup> because<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h6440.htm">6440</a></sup> of darkness.<sup><a href="h2822.htm">2822</a></sup> </p>
 <p>13  If<sup><a href="h0518.htm">518</a></sup> I wait,<sup><a href="h6960.htm">6960</a></sup> the grave<sup><a href="h7585.htm">7585</a></sup> is mine house:<sup><a href="h1004.htm">1004</a></sup> I have made<sup><a href="h7502.htm">7502</a></sup> my bed<sup><a href="h3326.htm">3326</a></sup> in the darkness.<sup><a href="h2822.htm">2822</a></sup> </p>
 <p>14  I have said<sup><a href="h7121.htm">7121</a></sup> to corruption,<sup><a href="h7845.htm">7845</a></sup> Thou<sup><a href="h0859.htm">859</a></sup> art my father:<sup><a href="h0001.htm">1</a></sup> to the worm,<sup><a href="h7415.htm">7415</a></sup> Thou art my mother,<sup><a href="h0517.htm">517</a></sup> and my sister.<sup><a href="h0269.htm">269</a></sup> </p>
 <p>15  And where<sup><a href="h0346.htm">346</a></sup> is now<sup><a href="h0645.htm">645</a></sup> my hope?<sup><a href="h8615.htm">8615</a></sup> as for my hope,<sup><a href="h8615.htm">8615</a></sup> who<sup><a href="h4310.htm">4310</a></sup> shall see<sup><a href="h7789.htm">7789</a></sup> it? </p>
 <p>16  They shall go down<sup><a href="h3381.htm">3381</a></sup> to the bars<sup><a href="h0905.htm">905</a></sup> of the pit,<sup><a href="h7585.htm">7585</a></sup> when<sup><a href="h0518.htm">518</a></sup> our rest<sup><a href="h5183.htm">5183</a></sup> together<sup><a href="h3162.htm">3162</a></sup> is in<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> the dust.<sup><a href="h6083.htm">6083</a></sup> </p>

 <p>2 λίσσομαι κάμνων καὶ τί ποιήσας </p>
 <p>3 ἔκλεψαν δέ μου τὰ ὑπάρχοντα ἀλλότριοι τίς ἐστιν οὗτος τῇ χειρί μου συνδεθήτω </p>
 <p>4 ὅτι καρδίαν αὐτῶν ἔκρυψας ἀπὸ φρονήσεως διὰ τοῦτο οὐ μὴ ὑψώσῃς αὐτούς </p>
 <p>5 τῇ μερίδι ἀναγγελεῖ κακίας ὀφθαλμοὶ δέ μου ἐφ᾽ υἱοῖς ἐτάκησαν </p>
 <p>6 ἔθου δέ με θρύλημα ἐν ἔθνεσιν γέλως δὲ αὐτοῖς ἀπέβην </p>
 <p>7 πεπώρωνται γὰρ ἀπὸ ὀργῆς οἱ ὀφθαλμοί μου πεπολιόρκημαι μεγάλως ὑπὸ πάντων </p>
 <p>8 θαῦμα ἔσχεν ἀληθινοὺς ἐπὶ τούτῳ δίκαιος δὲ ἐπὶ παρανόμῳ ἐπανασταίη </p>
 <p>9 σχοίη δὲ πιστὸς τὴν ἑαυτοῦ ὁδόν καθαρὸς δὲ χεῖρας ἀναλάβοι θάρσος </p>
 <p>10 οὐ μὴν δὲ ἀλλὰ πάντες ἐρείδετε καὶ δεῦτε δή οὐ γὰρ εὑρίσκω ἐν ὑμῖν ἀληθές </p>
 <p>11 αἱ ἡμέραι μου παρῆλθον ἐν βρόμῳ ἐρράγη δὲ τὰ ἄρθρα τῆς καρδίας μου </p>
 <p>12 νύκτα εἰς ἡμέραν ἔθηκαν φῶς ἐγγὺς ἀπὸ προσώπου σκότους </p>
 <p>13 ἐὰν γὰρ ὑπομείνω ᾅδης μου ὁ οἶκος ἐν δὲ γνόφῳ ἔστρωταί μου ἡ στρωμνή </p>
 <p>14 θάνατον ἐπεκαλεσάμην πατέρα μου εἶναι μητέρα δέ μου καὶ ἀδελφὴν σαπρίαν </p>
 <p>15 ποῦ οὖν μου ἔτι ἐστὶν ἡ ἐλπίς ἦ τὰ ἀγαθά μου ὄψομαι </p>
 <p>16 ἦ μετ᾽ ἐμοῦ εἰς ᾅδην καταβήσονται ἢ ὁμοθυμαδὸν ἐπὶ χώματος καταβησόμεθα </p>

 <p align="center"><a href="../vul/job017.htm">Vul</a></p><p>1 [Spiritus meus attenuabitur;<br> dies mei breviabuntur:<br> et solum mihi superest sepulchrum.<br></p>
 <p>2 Non peccavi,<br> et in amaritudinibus moratur oculus meus.<br></p>
 <p>3 Libera me, Domine, et pone me juxta te,<br> et cujusvis manus pugnet contra me.<br></p>
 <p>4 Cor eorum longe fecisti a disciplina:<br> propterea non exaltabuntur.<br></p>
 <p>5 Prædam pollicetur sociis,<br> et oculi filiorum ejus deficient.<br></p>
 <p>6 Posuit me quasi in proverbium vulgi,<br> et exemplum sum coram eis.<br></p>
 <p>7 Caligavit ab indignatione oculus meus,<br> et membra mea quasi in nihilum redacta sunt.<br></p>
 <p>8 Stupebunt justi super hoc,<br> et innocens contra hypocritam suscitabitur.<br></p>
 <p>9 Et tenebit justus viam suam,<br> et mundis manibus addet fortitudinem.<br></p>
 <p>10 Igitur omnes vos convertimini, et venite,<br> et non inveniam in vobis ullum sapientem.<br></p>
 <p>11 Dies mei transierunt;<br> cogitationes meæ dissipatæ sunt,<br> torquentes cor meum.<br></p>
 <p>12 Noctem verterunt in diem,<br> et rursum post tenebras spero lucem.<br></p>
 <p>13 Si sustinuero, infernus domus mea est,<br> et in tenebris stravi lectulum meum.<br></p>
 <p>14 Putredini dixi: Pater meus es;<br> Mater mea, et soror mea, vermibus.<br></p>
 <p>15 Ubi est ergo nunc præstolatio mea?<br> et patientiam meam quis considerat?<br></p>
 <p>16 In profundissimum infernum descendent omnia mea:<br> putasne saltem ibi erit requies mihi?]</p>
