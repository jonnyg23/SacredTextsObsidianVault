  <p>H3259<br>  יעד  <br> יָעַד  ‎  yâ‛ad  <br><i>yaw-ad‘ </i><br>A primitive root; to <i>fix</i> upon (by agreement or appointment); by implication to <i>meet</i> (at a stated time), to <i>summon</i> (to trial), to <i>direct</i> (in a certain quarter or position), to <i>engage</i> (for marriage): - agree, (make an) appoint (-ment, a time), assemble (selves), betroth, gather (selves, together), meet (together), set (a time).<br></p>
