 <h3 align="CENTER">Sirach 5</h3><table border="1">
 <tr>
 <td valign="top">
 <p align="center"><a href="../apo/sir005.htm">Apo</a></p><p>1 Set thy heart upon thy goods; and say not, I have enough for my life. </p>
 <p>2 Follow not thine own mind and thy strength, to walk in the ways of thy heart: </p>
 <p>3 And say not, Who shall controul me for my works? for the Lord will surely revenge thy pride. </p>
 <p>4 Say not, I have sinned, and what harm hath happened unto me? for the Lord is longsuffering, he will in no wise let thee go. </p>
 <p>5 Concerning propitiation, be not without fear to add sin unto sin: </p>
 <p>6 And say not His mercy is great; he will be pacified for the multitude of my sins: for mercy and wrath come from him, and his indignation resteth upon sinners. </p>
 <p>7 Make no tarrying to turn to the Lord, and put not off from day to day: for suddenly shall the wrath of the Lord come forth, and in thy security thou shalt be destroyed, and perish in the day of vengeance. </p>
 <p>8 Set not thine heart upon goods unjustly gotten, for they shall not profit thee in the day of calamity. </p>
 <p>9 Winnow not with every wind, and go not into every way: for so doth the sinner that hath a double tongue. </p>
 <p>10 Be stedfast in thy understanding; and let thy word be the same. </p>
 <p>11 Be swift to hear; and let thy life be sincere; and with patience give answer. </p>
 <p>12 If thou hast understanding, answer thy neighbour; if not, lay thy hand upon thy mouth. </p>
 <p>13 Honour and shame is in talk: and the tongue of man is his fall. </p>
 <p>14 Be not called a whisperer, and lie not in wait with thy tongue: for a foul shame is upon the thief, and an evil condemnation upon the double tongue. </p>
 <p>15 Be not ignorant of any thing in a great matter or a small. </p>
 </td>
 <td valign="top">
 <p align="center"><a href="../sep/sir005.htm">Sep</a></p><p>1 μὴ ἔπεχε ἐπὶ τοῖς χρήμασίν σου καὶ μὴ εἴπῃς αὐτάρκη μοί ἐστιν </p>
 <p>2 μὴ ἐξακολούθει τῇ ψυχῇ σου καὶ τῇ ἰσχύι σου πορεύεσθαι ἐν ἐπιθυμίαις καρδίας σου </p>
 <p>3 καὶ μὴ εἴπῃς τίς με δυναστεύσει ὁ γὰρ κύριος ἐκδικῶν ἐκδικήσει </p>
 <p>4 μὴ εἴπῃς ἥμαρτον καὶ τί μοι ἐγένετο ὁ γὰρ κύριός ἐστιν μακρόθυμος </p>
 <p>5 περὶ ἐξιλασμοῦ μὴ ἄφοβος γίνου προσθεῖναι ἁμαρτίαν ἐφ᾽ ἁμαρτίαις </p>
 <p>6 καὶ μὴ εἴπῃς ὁ οἰκτιρμὸς αὐτοῦ πολύς τὸ πλῆθος τῶν ἁμαρτιῶν μου ἐξιλάσεται ἔλεος γὰρ καὶ ὀργὴ παρ᾽ αὐτῷ καὶ ἐπὶ ἁμαρτωλοὺς καταπαύσει ὁ θυμὸς αὐτοῦ </p>
 <p>7 μὴ ἀνάμενε ἐπιστρέψαι πρὸς κύριον καὶ μὴ ὑπερβάλλου ἡμέραν ἐξ ἡμέρας ἐξάπινα γὰρ ἐξελεύσεται ὀργὴ κυρίου καὶ ἐν καιρῷ ἐκδικήσεως ἐξολῇ </p>
 <p>8 μὴ ἔπεχε ἐπὶ χρήμασιν ἀδίκοις οὐδὲν γὰρ ὠφελήσει σε ἐν ἡμέρᾳ ἐπαγωγῆς </p>
 <p>9 μὴ λίκμα ἐν παντὶ ἀνέμῳ καὶ μὴ πορεύου ἐν πάσῃ ἀτραπῷ οὕτως ὁ ἁμαρτωλὸς ὁ δίγλωσσος </p>
 <p>10 ἴσθι ἐστηριγμένος ἐν συνέσει σου καὶ εἷς ἔστω σου ὁ λόγος </p>
 <p>11 γίνου ταχὺς ἐν ἀκροάσει σου καὶ ἐν μακροθυμίᾳ φθέγγου ἀπόκρισιν </p>
 <p>12 εἰ ἔστιν σοι σύνεσις ἀποκρίθητι τῷ πλησίον εἰ δὲ μή ἡ χείρ σου ἔστω ἐπὶ τῷ στόματί σου </p>
 <p>13 δόξα καὶ ἀτιμία ἐν λαλιᾷ καὶ γλῶσσα ἀνθρώπου πτῶσις αὐτῷ </p>
 <p>14 μὴ κληθῇς ψίθυρος καὶ τῇ γλώσσῃ σου μὴ ἐνέδρευε ἐπὶ γὰρ τῷ κλέπτῃ ἐστὶν αἰσχύνη καὶ κατάγνωσις πονηρὰ ἐπὶ διγλώσσου </p>
 <p>15 ἐν μεγάλῳ καὶ ἐν μικρῷ μὴ ἀγνόει καὶ ἀντὶ φίλου μὴ γίνου ἐχθρός ὄνομα γὰρ πονηρὸν αἰσχύνην καὶ ὄνειδος κληρονομήσει οὕτως ὁ ἁμαρτωλὸς ὁ δίγλωσσος </p>
 </td>
 <td valign="top">
 <p align="center"><a href="../vul/sir005.htm">Vul</a></p><p>1 [Noli attendere ad possessiones iniquas,<br> et ne dixeris: Est mihi sufficiens vita:<br> nihil enim proderit in tempore vindictæ et obductionis.<br></p>
 <p>2 Ne sequaris in fortitudine tua concupiscentiam cordis tui,<br></p>
 <p>3 et ne dixeris: Quomodo potui?<br> aut, Quis me subjiciet propter facta mea?<br> Deus enim vindicans vindicabit.<br></p>
 <p>4 Ne dixeris: Peccavi: et quid mihi accidit triste?<br> Altissimus enim est patiens redditor.<br></p>
 <p>5 De propitiatio peccato noli esse sine metu,<br> neque adjicias peccatum super peccatum.<br></p>
 <p>6 Et ne dicas: Miseratio Domini magna est,<br> multitudinis peccatorum meorum miserebitur:<br></p>
 <p>7 misericordia enim et ira ab illo cito proximant,<br> et in peccatores respicit ira illius.<br></p>
 <p>8 Non tardes converti ad Dominum,<br> et ne differas de die in diem:<br></p>
 <p>9 subito enim veniet ira illius,<br> et in tempore vindictæ disperdet te.<br></p>
 <p>10 Noli anxius esse in divitiis injustis:<br> non enim proderunt tibi in die obductionis et vindictæ.]<br>
 </p>
 <p>11 [Non ventiles te in omnem ventum,<br> et non eas in omnem viam:<br> sic enim omnis peccator probatur in duplici lingua.<br></p>
 <p>12 Esto firmus in via Domini,<br> et in veritate sensus tui et scientia:<br> et prosequatur te verbum pacis et justitiæ.<br></p>
 <p>13 Esto mansuetus ad audiendum verbum, ut intelligas,<br> et cum sapientia proferas responsum verum.<br></p>
 <p>14 Si est tibi intellectus, responde proximo:<br> sin autem, sit manus tua super os tuum,<br> ne capiaris in verbo indisciplinato, et confundaris.<br></p>
 <p>15 Honor et gloria in sermone sensati:<br> lingua vero imprudentis subversio est ipsius.<br></p>
 <p>16 Non appelleris susurro,<br> et lingua tua ne capiaris et confundaris:<br></p>
 <p>17 super furem enim est confusio et pnitentia,<br> et denotatio pessima super bilinguem:<br> susurratori autem odium, et inimicitia, et contumelia.<br></p>
 <p>18 Justifica pusillum<br> et magnum similiter.]</p>
