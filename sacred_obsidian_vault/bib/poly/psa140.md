 <p>2  Which<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> imagine<sup><a href="h2803.htm">2803</a></sup> mischiefs<sup><a href="h7451.htm">7451</a></sup> in their heart;<sup><a href="h3820.htm">3820</a></sup> continually<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3117.htm">3117</a></sup> are they gathered together<sup><a href="h1481.htm">1481</a></sup> for war.<sup><a href="h4421.htm">4421</a></sup> </p>
 <p>3  They have sharpened<sup><a href="h8150.htm">8150</a></sup> their tongues<sup><a href="h3956.htm">3956</a></sup> like<sup><a href="h3644.htm">3644</a></sup> a serpent;<sup><a href="h5175.htm">5175</a></sup> adders'<sup><a href="h5919.htm">5919</a></sup> poison<sup><a href="h2534.htm">2534</a></sup> is under<sup><a href="h8478.htm">8478</a></sup> their lips.<sup><a href="h8193.htm">8193</a></sup> Selah.<sup><a href="h5542.htm">5542</a></sup> </p>
 <p>4  Keep<sup><a href="h8104.htm">8104</a></sup> me, O LORD,<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> from the hands<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3027.htm">3027</a></sup> of the wicked;<sup><a href="h7563.htm">7563</a></sup> preserve<sup><a href="h5341.htm">5341</a></sup> me from the violent man;<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0376.htm">376</a></sup> <sup><a href="h2555.htm">2555</a></sup> who<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> have purposed<sup><a href="h2803.htm">2803</a></sup> to overthrow<sup><a href="h1760.htm">1760</a></sup> my goings.<sup><a href="h6471.htm">6471</a></sup> </p>
 <p>5  The proud<sup><a href="h1343.htm">1343</a></sup> have hid<sup><a href="h2934.htm">2934</a></sup> a snare<sup><a href="h6341.htm">6341</a></sup> for me, and cords;<sup><a href="h2256.htm">2256</a></sup> they have spread<sup><a href="h6566.htm">6566</a></sup> a net<sup><a href="h7568.htm">7568</a></sup> by the wayside;<sup><a href="h3027.htm">3027</a></sup> <sup><a href="h4570.htm">4570</a></sup> they have set<sup><a href="h7896.htm">7896</a></sup> gins<sup><a href="h4170.htm">4170</a></sup> for me.  <p>6  I said<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> unto the LORD,<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> Thou<sup><a href="h0859.htm">859</a></sup> art my God:<sup><a href="h0410.htm">410</a></sup> hear<sup><a href="h0238.htm">238</a></sup> the voice<sup><a href="h6963.htm">6963</a></sup> of my supplications,<sup><a href="h8469.htm">8469</a></sup> O LORD.<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> </p>
 <p>7  O GOD<sup><a href="h3069.htm">3069</a></sup> the Lord,<sup><a href="h0136.htm">136</a></sup> the strength<sup><a href="h5797.htm">5797</a></sup> of my salvation,<sup><a href="h3444.htm">3444</a></sup> thou hast covered<sup><a href="h5526.htm">5526</a></sup> my head<sup><a href="h7218.htm">7218</a></sup> in the day<sup><a href="h3117.htm">3117</a></sup> of battle.<sup><a href="h5402.htm">5402</a></sup> </p>
 <p>8  Grant<sup><a href="h5414.htm">5414</a></sup> not,<sup><a href="h0408.htm">408</a></sup> O LORD,<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> the desires<sup><a href="h3970.htm">3970</a></sup> of the wicked:<sup><a href="h7563.htm">7563</a></sup> further<sup><a href="h6329.htm">6329</a></sup> not<sup><a href="h0408.htm">408</a></sup> his wicked device;<sup><a href="h2162.htm">2162</a></sup> lest they exalt<sup><a href="h7311.htm">7311</a></sup> themselves.  <p>9  As for the head<sup><a href="h7218.htm">7218</a></sup> of those that compass me about,<sup><a href="h4524.htm">4524</a></sup> let the mischief<sup><a href="h5999.htm">5999</a></sup> of their own lips<sup><a href="h8193.htm">8193</a></sup> cover<sup><a href="h3680.htm">3680</a></sup> them. </p>
 <p>10  Let burning coals<sup><a href="h1513.htm">1513</a></sup> fall<sup><a href="h4131.htm">4131</a></sup> upon<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> them: let them be cast<sup><a href="h5307.htm">5307</a></sup> into the fire;<sup><a href="h0784.htm">784</a></sup> into deep pits,<sup><a href="h4113.htm">4113</a></sup> that they rise<sup><a href="h6965.htm">6965</a></sup> not<sup><a href="h1077.htm">1077</a></sup> up again. </p>
 <p>11  Let not<sup><a href="h1077.htm">1077</a></sup> an evil speaker<sup><a href="h0376.htm">376</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3956.htm">3956</a></sup> be established<sup><a href="h3559.htm">3559</a></sup> in the earth:<sup><a href="h0776.htm">776</a></sup> evil<sup><a href="h7451.htm">7451</a></sup> shall hunt<sup><a href="h6679.htm">6679</a></sup> the violent<sup><a href="h2555.htm">2555</a></sup> man<sup><a href="h0376.htm">376</a></sup> to overthrow<sup><a href="h4073.htm">4073</a></sup> him. </p>
 <p>12  I know<sup><a href="h3045.htm">3045</a></sup> that<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> the LORD<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> will maintain<sup><a href="h6213.htm">6213</a></sup> the cause<sup><a href="h1779.htm">1779</a></sup> of the afflicted,<sup><a href="h6041.htm">6041</a></sup> and the right<sup><a href="h4941.htm">4941</a></sup> of the poor.<sup><a href="h0034.htm">34</a></sup> </p>
 <p>13  Surely<sup><a href="h0389.htm">389</a></sup> the righteous<sup><a href="h6662.htm">6662</a></sup> shall give thanks<sup><a href="h3034.htm">3034</a></sup> unto thy name:<sup><a href="h8034.htm">8034</a></sup> the upright<sup><a href="h3477.htm">3477</a></sup> shall dwell<sup><a href="h3427.htm">3427</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> in thy presence.<sup><a href="h6440.htm">6440</a></sup> </p>

 <p>5 παιδεύσει με δίκαιος ἐν ἐλέει καὶ ἐλέγξει με ἔλαιον δὲ ἁμαρτωλοῦ μὴ λιπανάτω τὴν κεφαλήν μου ὅτι ἔτι καὶ ἡ προσευχή μου ἐν ταῖς εὐδοκίαις αὐτῶν </p>

 <p>8 ὅτι πρὸς σέ κύριε κύριε οἱ ὀφθαλμοί μου ἐπὶ σὲ ἤλπισα μὴ ἀντανέλῃς τὴν ψυχήν μου </p>
 <p>9 φύλαξόν με ἀπὸ παγίδος ἧς συνεστήσαντό μοι καὶ ἀπὸ σκανδάλων τῶν ἐργαζομένων τὴν ἀνομίαν </p>
 <p>10 πεσοῦνται ἐν ἀμφιβλήστρῳ αὐτοῦ ἁμαρτωλοί κατὰ μόνας εἰμὶ ἐγὼ ἕως οὗ ἂν παρέλθω </p>

 <p>3 Pone, Domine, custodiam ori meo,<br> et ostium circumstantiæ labiis meis.<br></p>
 <p>4 Non declines cor meum in verba malitiæ,<br> ad excusandas excusationes in peccatis;<br> cum hominibus operantibus iniquitatem,<br> et non communicabo cum electis eorum.<br></p>
 <p>5 Corripiet me justus in misericordia, et increpabit me:<br> oleum autem peccatoris non impinguet caput meum.<br> Quoniam adhuc et oratio mea in beneplacitis eorum:<br></p>
 <p>6 absorpti sunt juncti petræ judices eorum.<br> Audient verba mea, quoniam potuerunt.<br></p>
 <p>7 Sicut crassitudo terræ erupta est super terram,<br> dissipata sunt ossa nostra secus infernum.<br></p>
 <p>8 Quia ad te, Domine, Domine, oculi mei;<br> in te speravi, non auferas animam meam.<br></p>
 <p>9 Custodi me a laqueo quem statuerunt mihi,<br> et a scandalis operantium iniquitatem.<br></p>
 <p>10 Cadent in retiaculo ejus peccatores:<br> singulariter sum ego, donec transeam.]</p>
