 <p align="center"><a href="../kjv/pro029.htm">KJV</a></p><p>1  He,<sup><a href="h0376.htm">376</a></sup> that being often reproved<sup><a href="h8433.htm">8433</a></sup> hardeneth<sup><a href="h7185.htm">7185</a></sup> his neck,<sup><a href="h6203.htm">6203</a></sup> shall suddenly<sup><a href="h6621.htm">6621</a></sup> be destroyed,<sup><a href="h7665.htm">7665</a></sup> and that without<sup><a href="h0369.htm">369</a></sup> remedy.<sup><a href="h4832.htm">4832</a></sup> </p>
 <p>2  When the righteous<sup><a href="h6662.htm">6662</a></sup> are in authority,<sup><a href="h7235.htm">7235</a></sup> the people<sup><a href="h5971.htm">5971</a></sup> rejoice:<sup><a href="h8055.htm">8055</a></sup> but when the wicked<sup><a href="h7563.htm">7563</a></sup> beareth rule,<sup><a href="h4910.htm">4910</a></sup> the people<sup><a href="h5971.htm">5971</a></sup> mourn.<sup><a href="h0584.htm">584</a></sup> </p>
 <p>3  Whoso<sup><a href="h0376.htm">376</a></sup> loveth<sup><a href="h0157.htm">157</a></sup> wisdom<sup><a href="h2451.htm">2451</a></sup> rejoiceth<sup><a href="h8055.htm">8055</a></sup> his father:<sup><a href="h0001.htm">1</a></sup> but he that keepeth company<sup><a href="h7462.htm">7462</a></sup> with harlots<sup><a href="h2181.htm">2181</a></sup> spendeth<sup><a href="h0006.htm">6</a></sup> his substance.<sup><a href="h1952.htm">1952</a></sup> </p>
 <p>4  The king<sup><a href="h4428.htm">4428</a></sup> by judgment<sup><a href="h4941.htm">4941</a></sup> establisheth<sup><a href="h5975.htm">5975</a></sup> the land:<sup><a href="h0776.htm">776</a></sup> but he<sup><a href="h0376.htm">376</a></sup> that receiveth gifts<sup><a href="h8641.htm">8641</a></sup> overthroweth<sup><a href="h2040.htm">2040</a></sup> it. </p>
 <p>5  A man<sup><a href="h1397.htm">1397</a></sup> that flattereth<sup><a href="h2505.htm">2505</a></sup> <sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> his neighbor<sup><a href="h7453.htm">7453</a></sup> spreadeth<sup><a href="h6566.htm">6566</a></sup> a net<sup><a href="h7568.htm">7568</a></sup> for<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> his feet.<sup><a href="h6471.htm">6471</a></sup> </p>
 <p>6  In the transgression<sup><a href="h6588.htm">6588</a></sup> of an evil<sup><a href="h7451.htm">7451</a></sup> man<sup><a href="h0376.htm">376</a></sup> there is a snare:<sup><a href="h4170.htm">4170</a></sup> but the righteous<sup><a href="h6662.htm">6662</a></sup> doth sing<sup><a href="h7442.htm">7442</a></sup> and rejoice.<sup><a href="h8055.htm">8055</a></sup> </p>
 <p>7  The righteous<sup><a href="h6662.htm">6662</a></sup> considereth<sup><a href="h3045.htm">3045</a></sup> the cause<sup><a href="h1779.htm">1779</a></sup> of the poor:<sup><a href="h1800.htm">1800</a></sup> but the wicked<sup><a href="h7563.htm">7563</a></sup> regardeth<sup><a href="h0995.htm">995</a></sup> not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> to know<sup><a href="h1847.htm">1847</a></sup> it. </p>
 <p>8  Scornful<sup><a href="h3944.htm">3944</a></sup> men<sup><a href="h0376.htm">376</a></sup> bring a city into a snare:<sup><a href="h6315.htm">6315</a></sup> <sup><a href="h7151.htm">7151</a></sup> but wise<sup><a href="h2450.htm">2450</a></sup> men turn away<sup><a href="h7725.htm">7725</a></sup> wrath.<sup><a href="h0639.htm">639</a></sup> </p>
 <p>9  If a wise<sup><a href="h2450.htm">2450</a></sup> man<sup><a href="h0376.htm">376</a></sup> contendeth<sup><a href="h8199.htm">8199</a></sup> with<sup><a href="h0854.htm">854</a></sup> a foolish<sup><a href="h0191.htm">191</a></sup> man,<sup><a href="h0376.htm">376</a></sup> whether he rage<sup><a href="h7264.htm">7264</a></sup> or laugh,<sup><a href="h7832.htm">7832</a></sup> there is no<sup><a href="h0369.htm">369</a></sup> rest.<sup><a href="h5183.htm">5183</a></sup> </p>
 <p>10  The bloodthirsty<sup><a href="h0376.htm">376</a></sup> <sup><a href="h1818.htm">1818</a></sup> hate<sup><a href="h8130.htm">8130</a></sup> the upright:<sup><a href="h8535.htm">8535</a></sup> but the just<sup><a href="h3477.htm">3477</a></sup> seek<sup><a href="h1245.htm">1245</a></sup> his soul.<sup><a href="h5315.htm">5315</a></sup> </p>
 <p>11  A fool<sup><a href="h3684.htm">3684</a></sup> uttereth<sup><a href="h3318.htm">3318</a></sup> all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> his mind:<sup><a href="h7307.htm">7307</a></sup> but a wise<sup><a href="h2450.htm">2450</a></sup> man keepeth<sup><a href="h7623.htm">7623</a></sup> it in till afterwards.<sup><a href="h0268.htm">268</a></sup> </p>
 <p>12  If a ruler<sup><a href="h4910.htm">4910</a></sup> hearken<sup><a href="h7181.htm">7181</a></sup> to<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> lies,<sup><a href="h1697.htm">1697</a></sup> <sup><a href="h8267.htm">8267</a></sup> all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> his servants<sup><a href="h8334.htm">8334</a></sup> are wicked.<sup><a href="h7563.htm">7563</a></sup> </p>
 <p>13  The poor<sup><a href="h7326.htm">7326</a></sup> and the deceitful<sup><a href="h8501.htm">8501</a></sup> man<sup><a href="h0376.htm">376</a></sup> meet together:<sup><a href="h6298.htm">6298</a></sup> the LORD<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> lighteneth<sup><a href="h0215.htm">215</a></sup> both<sup><a href="h8147.htm">8147</a></sup> their eyes.<sup><a href="h5869.htm">5869</a></sup> </p>
 <p>14  The king<sup><a href="h4428.htm">4428</a></sup> that faithfully<sup><a href="h0571.htm">571</a></sup> judgeth<sup><a href="h8199.htm">8199</a></sup> the poor,<sup><a href="h1800.htm">1800</a></sup> his throne<sup><a href="h3678.htm">3678</a></sup> shall be established<sup><a href="h3559.htm">3559</a></sup> forever.<sup><a href="h5703.htm">5703</a></sup> </p>
 <p>15  The rod<sup><a href="h7626.htm">7626</a></sup> and reproof<sup><a href="h8433.htm">8433</a></sup> give<sup><a href="h5414.htm">5414</a></sup> wisdom:<sup><a href="h2451.htm">2451</a></sup> but a child<sup><a href="h5288.htm">5288</a></sup> left<sup><a href="h7971.htm">7971</a></sup> to himself bringeth his mother<sup><a href="h0517.htm">517</a></sup> to shame.<sup><a href="h0954.htm">954</a></sup> </p>
 <p>16  When the wicked<sup><a href="h7563.htm">7563</a></sup> are multiplied,<sup><a href="h7235.htm">7235</a></sup> transgression<sup><a href="h6588.htm">6588</a></sup> increaseth:<sup><a href="h7235.htm">7235</a></sup> but the righteous<sup><a href="h6662.htm">6662</a></sup> shall see<sup><a href="h7200.htm">7200</a></sup> their fall.<sup><a href="h4658.htm">4658</a></sup> </p>
 <p>17  Correct<sup><a href="h3256.htm">3256</a></sup> thy son,<sup><a href="h1121.htm">1121</a></sup> and he shall give thee rest;<sup><a href="h5117.htm">5117</a></sup> yea, he shall give<sup><a href="h5414.htm">5414</a></sup> delight<sup><a href="h4574.htm">4574</a></sup> unto thy soul.<sup><a href="h5315.htm">5315</a></sup> </p>
 <p>18  Where there is no<sup><a href="h0369.htm">369</a></sup> vision,<sup><a href="h2377.htm">2377</a></sup> the people<sup><a href="h5971.htm">5971</a></sup> perish:<sup><a href="h6544.htm">6544</a></sup> but he that keepeth<sup><a href="h8104.htm">8104</a></sup> the law,<sup><a href="h8451.htm">8451</a></sup> happy<sup><a href="h0835.htm">835</a></sup> is he. </p>
 <p>19  A servant<sup><a href="h5650.htm">5650</a></sup> will not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> be corrected<sup><a href="h3256.htm">3256</a></sup> by words:<sup><a href="h1697.htm">1697</a></sup> for though<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> he understand<sup><a href="h0995.htm">995</a></sup> he will not<sup><a href="h0369.htm">369</a></sup> answer.<sup><a href="h4617.htm">4617</a></sup> </p>
 <p>20  Seest<sup><a href="h2372.htm">2372</a></sup> thou a man<sup><a href="h0376.htm">376</a></sup> that is hasty<sup><a href="h0213.htm">213</a></sup> in his words?<sup><a href="h1697.htm">1697</a></sup> there is more hope<sup><a href="h8615.htm">8615</a></sup> of a fool<sup><a href="h3684.htm">3684</a></sup> than of<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> him. </p>
 <p>21  He that delicately bringeth up<sup><a href="h6445.htm">6445</a></sup> his servant<sup><a href="h5650.htm">5650</a></sup> from a child<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h5290.htm">5290</a></sup> shall have him become<sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> his son<sup><a href="h4497.htm">4497</a></sup> at the length.<sup><a href="h0319.htm">319</a></sup> </p>
 <p>22  An angry<sup><a href="h0639.htm">639</a></sup> man<sup><a href="h0376.htm">376</a></sup> stirreth up<sup><a href="h1624.htm">1624</a></sup> strife,<sup><a href="h4066.htm">4066</a></sup> and a furious<sup><a href="h2534.htm">2534</a></sup> man<sup><a href="h1167.htm">1167</a></sup> aboundeth<sup><a href="h7227.htm">7227</a></sup> in transgression.<sup><a href="h6588.htm">6588</a></sup> </p>
 <p>23  A man's<sup><a href="h0120.htm">120</a></sup> pride<sup><a href="h1346.htm">1346</a></sup> shall bring him low:<sup><a href="h8213.htm">8213</a></sup> but honor<sup><a href="h3519.htm">3519</a></sup> shall uphold<sup><a href="h8551.htm">8551</a></sup> the humble<sup><a href="h8217.htm">8217</a></sup> in spirit.<sup><a href="h7307.htm">7307</a></sup> </p>
 <p>24  Whoso is partner<sup><a href="h2505.htm">2505</a></sup> with<sup><a href="h5973.htm">5973</a></sup> a thief<sup><a href="h1590.htm">1590</a></sup> hateth<sup><a href="h8130.htm">8130</a></sup> his own soul:<sup><a href="h5315.htm">5315</a></sup> he heareth<sup><a href="h8085.htm">8085</a></sup> cursing,<sup><a href="h0423.htm">423</a></sup> and betrayeth<sup><a href="h5046.htm">5046</a></sup> it not.<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> </p>
 <p>25  The fear<sup><a href="h2731.htm">2731</a></sup> of man<sup><a href="h0120.htm">120</a></sup> bringeth<sup><a href="h5414.htm">5414</a></sup> a snare:<sup><a href="h4170.htm">4170</a></sup> but whoso putteth his trust<sup><a href="h0982.htm">982</a></sup> in the LORD<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> shall be safe.<sup><a href="h7682.htm">7682</a></sup> </p>
 <p>26  Many<sup><a href="h7227.htm">7227</a></sup> seek<sup><a href="h1245.htm">1245</a></sup> the ruler's<sup><a href="h4910.htm">4910</a></sup> favor;<sup><a href="h6440.htm">6440</a></sup> but every man's<sup><a href="h0376.htm">376</a></sup> judgment<sup><a href="h4941.htm">4941</a></sup> cometh from the LORD.<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> </p>
 <p>27  An unjust<sup><a href="h5766.htm">5766</a></sup> man<sup><a href="h0376.htm">376</a></sup> is an abomination<sup><a href="h8441.htm">8441</a></sup> to the just:<sup><a href="h6662.htm">6662</a></sup> and he that is upright<sup><a href="h3477.htm">3477</a></sup> in the way<sup><a href="h1870.htm">1870</a></sup> is abomination<sup><a href="h8441.htm">8441</a></sup> to the wicked.<sup><a href="h7563.htm">7563</a></sup> </p>

 <p align="center"><a href="../vul/pro029.htm">Vul</a></p><p>1 [Viro qui corripientem dura cervice contemnit,<br> repentinus ei superveniet interitus,<br> et eum sanitas non sequetur.<br></p>
 <p>2 In multiplicatione justorum lætabitur vulgus;<br> cum impii sumpserint principatum, gemet populus.<br></p>
 <p>3 Vir qui amat sapientiam lætificat patrem suum;<br> qui autem nutrit scorta perdet substantiam.<br></p>
 <p>4 Rex justus erigit terram;<br> vir avarus destruet eam.<br></p>
 <p>5 Homo qui blandis fictisque sermonibus loquitur amico suo<br> rete expandit gressibus ejus.<br></p>
 <p>6 Peccantem virum iniquum involvet laqueus,<br> et justus laudabit atque gaudebit.<br></p>
 <p>7 Novit justus causam pauperum;<br> impius ignorat scientiam.<br></p>
 <p>8 Homines pestilentes dissipant civitatem;<br> sapientes vero avertunt furorem.<br></p>
 <p>9 Vir sapiens si cum stulto contenderit,<br> sive irascatur, sive rideat, non inveniet requiem.<br></p>
 <p>10 Viri sanguinum oderunt simplicem;<br> justi autem quærunt animam ejus.<br></p>
 <p>11 Totum spiritum suum profert stultus;<br> sapiens differt, et reservat in posterum.<br></p>
 <p>12 Princeps qui libenter audit verba mendacii,<br> omnes ministros habet impios.<br></p>
 <p>13 Pauper et creditor obviaverunt sibi:<br> utriusque illuminator est Dominus.<br></p>
 <p>14 Rex qui judicat in veritate pauperes,<br> thronus ejus in æternum firmabitur.]<br>
 </p>
 <p>15 [Virga atque correptio tribuit sapientiam;<br> puer autem qui dimittitur voluntati suæ confundit matrem suam.<br></p>
 <p>16 In multiplicatione impiorum multiplicabuntur scelera,<br> et justi ruinas eorum videbunt.<br></p>
 <p>17 Erudi filium tuum, et refrigerabit te,<br> et dabit delicias animæ tuæ.<br></p>
 <p>18 Cum prophetia defecerit, dissipabitur populus;<br> qui vero custodit legem beatus est.<br></p>
 <p>19 Servus verbis non potest erudiri,<br> quia quod dicis intelligit, et respondere contemnit.<br></p>
 <p>20 Vidisti hominem velocem ad loquendum?<br> stultitia magis speranda est quam illius correptio.<br></p>
 <p>21 Qui delicate a pueritia nutrit servum suum<br> postea sentiet eum contumacem.]<br>
 </p>
 <p>22 [Vir iracundus provocat rixas,<br> et qui ad indignandum facilis est erit ad peccandum proclivior.<br></p>
 <p>23 Superbum sequitur humilitas,<br> et humilem spiritu suscipiet gloria.<br></p>
 <p>24 Qui cum fure participat odit animam suam;<br> adjurantem audit, et non indicat.<br></p>
 <p>25 Qui timet hominem cito corruet;<br> qui sperat in Domino sublevabitur.<br></p>
 <p>26 Multi requirunt faciem principis,<br> et judicium a Domino egreditur singulorum.<br></p>
 <p>27 Abominantur justi virum impium,<br> et abominantur impii eos qui in recta sunt via.<br> Verbum custodiens filius<br> extra perditionem erit.]</p>
