  <p>H5046<br>  נגד  <br> נָגַד  ‎  nâgad  <br><i>naw-gad‘ </i><br>A primitive root; properly to <i>front</i>, that is, stand boldly out opposite; by implication (causatively), to <i>manifest</i>; figuratively to <i>announce</i> (always by word of mouth to one present); specifically to <i>expose</i>, <i>predict</i>, <i>explain</i>, <i>praise: - </i>bewray, X certainly, certify, declare (-ing), denounce, expound, X fully, messenger, plainly, profess, rehearse, report, shew (forth), speak, X surely, tell, utter.<br></p>
