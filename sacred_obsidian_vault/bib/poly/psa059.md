 <p>6  They return<sup><a href="h7725.htm">7725</a></sup> at evening:<sup><a href="h6153.htm">6153</a></sup> they make a noise<sup><a href="h1993.htm">1993</a></sup> like a dog,<sup><a href="h3611.htm">3611</a></sup> and go round about<sup><a href="h5437.htm">5437</a></sup> the city.<sup><a href="h5892.htm">5892</a></sup> </p>

 <p>8  But thou,<sup><a href="h0859.htm">859</a></sup> O LORD,<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> shalt laugh<sup><a href="h7832.htm">7832</a></sup> at them; thou shalt have all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> the heathen<sup><a href="h1471.htm">1471</a></sup> in derision.<sup><a href="h3932.htm">3932</a></sup> </p>
 <p>9  Because of his strength<sup><a href="h5797.htm">5797</a></sup> will I wait<sup><a href="h8104.htm">8104</a></sup> upon<sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> thee: for<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> God<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> is my defense.<sup><a href="h4869.htm">4869</a></sup> </p>
 <p>10  The God<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> of my mercy<sup><a href="h2617.htm">2617</a></sup> shall prevent<sup><a href="h6923.htm">6923</a></sup> me: God<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> shall let me see<sup><a href="h7200.htm">7200</a></sup> my desire upon mine enemies.<sup><a href="h8324.htm">8324</a></sup> </p>
 <p>11  Slay<sup><a href="h2026.htm">2026</a></sup> them not,<sup><a href="h0408.htm">408</a></sup> lest<sup><a href="h6435.htm">6435</a></sup> my people<sup><a href="h5971.htm">5971</a></sup> forget:<sup><a href="h7911.htm">7911</a></sup> scatter<sup><a href="h5128.htm">5128</a></sup> them by thy power;<sup><a href="h2428.htm">2428</a></sup> and bring them down,<sup><a href="h3381.htm">3381</a></sup> O Lord<sup><a href="h0136.htm">136</a></sup> our shield.<sup><a href="h4043.htm">4043</a></sup> </p>
 <p>12  For the sin<sup><a href="h2403.htm">2403</a></sup> of their mouth<sup><a href="h6310.htm">6310</a></sup> and the words<sup><a href="h1697.htm">1697</a></sup> of their lips<sup><a href="h8193.htm">8193</a></sup> let them even be taken<sup><a href="h3920.htm">3920</a></sup> in their pride:<sup><a href="h1347.htm">1347</a></sup> and for cursing<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0423.htm">423</a></sup> and lying<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3585.htm">3585</a></sup> which they speak.<sup><a href="h5608.htm">5608</a></sup> </p>
