 <p>2  O my God,<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> I trust<sup><a href="h0982.htm">982</a></sup> in thee: let me not<sup><a href="h0408.htm">408</a></sup> be ashamed,<sup><a href="h0954.htm">954</a></sup> let not<sup><a href="h0408.htm">408</a></sup> mine enemies<sup><a href="h0341.htm">341</a></sup> triumph<sup><a href="h5970.htm">5970</a></sup> over me. </p>

 <p>5  Lead<sup><a href="h1869.htm">1869</a></sup> me in thy truth,<sup><a href="h0571.htm">571</a></sup> and teach<sup><a href="h3925.htm">3925</a></sup> me: for<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> thou<sup><a href="h0859.htm">859</a></sup> art the God<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> of my salvation;<sup><a href="h3468.htm">3468</a></sup> on thee do I wait<sup><a href="h6960.htm">6960</a></sup> all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> the day.<sup><a href="h3117.htm">3117</a></sup> </p>

 <p>11  For thy name's sake,<sup><a href="h4616.htm">4616</a></sup> <sup><a href="h8034.htm">8034</a></sup> O LORD,<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> pardon<sup><a href="h5545.htm">5545</a></sup> mine iniquity;<sup><a href="h5771.htm">5771</a></sup> for<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> it<sup><a href="h1931.htm">1931</a></sup> is great.<sup><a href="h7227.htm">7227</a></sup> </p>
 <p>12  What<sup><a href="h4310.htm">4310</a></sup> man<sup><a href="h0376.htm">376</a></sup> is he that<sup><a href="h2088.htm">2088</a></sup> feareth<sup><a href="h3373.htm">3373</a></sup> the LORD?<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> him shall he teach<sup><a href="h3384.htm">3384</a></sup> in the way<sup><a href="h1870.htm">1870</a></sup> that he shall choose.<sup><a href="h0977.htm">977</a></sup> </p>
 <p>13  His soul<sup><a href="h5315.htm">5315</a></sup> shall dwell<sup><a href="h3885.htm">3885</a></sup> at ease;<sup><a href="h2896.htm">2896</a></sup> and his seed<sup><a href="h2233.htm">2233</a></sup> shall inherit<sup><a href="h3423.htm">3423</a></sup> the earth.<sup><a href="h0776.htm">776</a></sup> </p>
 <p>14  The secret<sup><a href="h5475.htm">5475</a></sup> of the LORD<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> is with them that fear<sup><a href="h3373.htm">3373</a></sup> him; and he will show<sup><a href="h3045.htm">3045</a></sup> them his covenant.<sup><a href="h1285.htm">1285</a></sup> </p>
 <p>15  Mine eyes<sup><a href="h5869.htm">5869</a></sup> are ever<sup><a href="h8548.htm">8548</a></sup> toward<sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> the LORD;<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> for<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> he<sup><a href="h1931.htm">1931</a></sup> shall pluck my feet out<sup><a href="h3318.htm">3318</a></sup> <sup><a href="h7272.htm">7272</a></sup> of the net.<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h7568.htm">7568</a></sup> </p>

 <p>20  O keep<sup><a href="h8104.htm">8104</a></sup> my soul,<sup><a href="h5315.htm">5315</a></sup> and deliver<sup><a href="h5337.htm">5337</a></sup> me: let me not<sup><a href="h0408.htm">408</a></sup> be ashamed;<sup><a href="h0954.htm">954</a></sup> for<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> I put my trust<sup><a href="h2620.htm">2620</a></sup> in thee. </p>
 <p>21  Let integrity<sup><a href="h8537.htm">8537</a></sup> and uprightness<sup><a href="h3476.htm">3476</a></sup> preserve<sup><a href="h5341.htm">5341</a></sup> me; for<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> I wait on<sup><a href="h6960.htm">6960</a></sup> thee. </p>
