 <p>2  Thus<sup><a href="h3541.htm">3541</a></sup> saith<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> Cyrus<sup><a href="h3566.htm">3566</a></sup> king<sup><a href="h4428.htm">4428</a></sup> of Persia,<sup><a href="h6539.htm">6539</a></sup> The LORD<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> God<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> of heaven<sup><a href="h8064.htm">8064</a></sup> hath given<sup><a href="h5414.htm">5414</a></sup> me all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> the kingdoms<sup><a href="h4467.htm">4467</a></sup> of the earth;<sup><a href="h0776.htm">776</a></sup> and he<sup><a href="h1931.htm">1931</a></sup> hath charged<sup><a href="h6485.htm">6485</a></sup> <sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> me to build<sup><a href="h1129.htm">1129</a></sup> him a house<sup><a href="h1004.htm">1004</a></sup> at Jerusalem,<sup><a href="h3389.htm">3389</a></sup> which<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> is in Judah.<sup><a href="h3063.htm">3063</a></sup> </p>
 <p>3  Who<sup><a href="h4310.htm">4310</a></sup> is there among you of all<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> his people?<sup><a href="h5971.htm">5971</a></sup> his God<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> be<sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> with<sup><a href="h5973.htm">5973</a></sup> him, and let him go up<sup><a href="h5927.htm">5927</a></sup> to Jerusalem,<sup><a href="h3389.htm">3389</a></sup> which<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> is in Judah,<sup><a href="h3063.htm">3063</a></sup> and build<sup><a href="h1129.htm">1129</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> the house<sup><a href="h1004.htm">1004</a></sup> of the LORD<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> God<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> of Israel,<sup><a href="h3478.htm">3478</a></sup> (he<sup><a href="h1931.htm">1931</a></sup> is the God,)<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> which<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> is in Jerusalem.<sup><a href="h3389.htm">3389</a></sup> </p>
 <p>4  And whosoever<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> remaineth<sup><a href="h7604.htm">7604</a></sup> in any<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> place<sup><a href="h4725.htm">4725</a></sup> where<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> he<sup><a href="h1931.htm">1931</a></sup> sojourneth,<sup><a href="h1481.htm">1481</a></sup> let the men<sup><a href="h0376.htm">376</a></sup> of his place<sup><a href="h4725.htm">4725</a></sup> help<sup><a href="h5375.htm">5375</a></sup> him with silver,<sup><a href="h3701.htm">3701</a></sup> and with gold,<sup><a href="h2091.htm">2091</a></sup> and with goods,<sup><a href="h7399.htm">7399</a></sup> and with beasts,<sup><a href="h0929.htm">929</a></sup> beside<sup><a href="h5973.htm">5973</a></sup> the freewill offering<sup><a href="h5071.htm">5071</a></sup> for the house<sup><a href="h1004.htm">1004</a></sup> of God<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> that<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> is in Jerusalem.<sup><a href="h3389.htm">3389</a></sup> </p>
 <p>5  Then rose up<sup><a href="h6965.htm">6965</a></sup> the chief<sup><a href="h7218.htm">7218</a></sup> of the fathers<sup><a href="h0001.htm">1</a></sup> of Judah<sup><a href="h3063.htm">3063</a></sup> and Benjamin,<sup><a href="h1144.htm">1144</a></sup> and the priests,<sup><a href="h3548.htm">3548</a></sup> and the Levites,<sup><a href="h3881.htm">3881</a></sup> with all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> them <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> whose spirit<sup><a href="h7307.htm">7307</a></sup> God<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> had raised,<sup><a href="h5782.htm">5782</a></sup> to go up<sup><a href="h5927.htm">5927</a></sup> to build<sup><a href="h1129.htm">1129</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> the house<sup><a href="h1004.htm">1004</a></sup> of the LORD<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> which<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> is in Jerusalem.<sup><a href="h3389.htm">3389</a></sup> </p>
 <p>6  And all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> they that were about<sup><a href="h5439.htm">5439</a></sup> them strengthened<sup><a href="h2388.htm">2388</a></sup> their hands<sup><a href="h3027.htm">3027</a></sup> with vessels<sup><a href="h3627.htm">3627</a></sup> of silver,<sup><a href="h3701.htm">3701</a></sup> with gold,<sup><a href="h2091.htm">2091</a></sup> with goods,<sup><a href="h7399.htm">7399</a></sup> and with beasts,<sup><a href="h0929.htm">929</a></sup> and with precious things,<sup><a href="h4030.htm">4030</a></sup> beside all<sup><a href="h0905.htm">905</a></sup> <sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> that was willingly offered.<sup><a href="h5068.htm">5068</a></sup> </p>

 <p>9  And this<sup><a href="h0428.htm">428</a></sup> is the number<sup><a href="h4557.htm">4557</a></sup> of them: thirty<sup><a href="h7970.htm">7970</a></sup> chargers<sup><a href="h0105.htm">105</a></sup> of gold,<sup><a href="h2091.htm">2091</a></sup> a thousand<sup><a href="h0505.htm">505</a></sup> chargers<sup><a href="h0105.htm">105</a></sup> of silver,<sup><a href="h3701.htm">3701</a></sup> nine<sup><a href="h8672.htm">8672</a></sup> and twenty<sup><a href="h6242.htm">6242</a></sup> knives,<sup><a href="h4252.htm">4252</a></sup> </p>
 <p>10  Thirty<sup><a href="h7970.htm">7970</a></sup> basins<sup><a href="h3713.htm">3713</a></sup> of gold,<sup><a href="h2091.htm">2091</a></sup> silver<sup><a href="h3701.htm">3701</a></sup> basins<sup><a href="h3713.htm">3713</a></sup> of a second<sup><a href="h4932.htm">4932</a></sup> sort four<sup><a href="h0702.htm">702</a></sup> hundred<sup><a href="h3967.htm">3967</a></sup> and ten,<sup><a href="h6235.htm">6235</a></sup> and other<sup><a href="h0312.htm">312</a></sup> vessels<sup><a href="h3627.htm">3627</a></sup> a thousand.<sup><a href="h0505.htm">505</a></sup> </p>

 <p align="center"><a href="../vul/ezr001.htm">Vul</a></p><p>1 In anno primo Cyri regis Persarum, ut compleretur verbum Domini ex ore Jeremiæ, suscitavit Dominus spiritum Cyri regis Persarum: et traduxit vocem in omni regno suo, etiam per scripturam, dicens:</p>
 <p>2 Hæc dicit Cyrus rex Persarum: Omnia regna terræ dedit mihi Dominus Deus cæli, et ipse præcepit mihi ut ædificarem ei domum in Jerusalem, quæ est in Judæa.</p>
 <p>3 Quis est in vobis de universo populo ejus? Sit Deus illius cum ipso. Ascendat in Jerusalem, quæ est in Judæa, et ædificet domum Domini Dei Israël: ipse est Deus qui est in Jerusalem.</p>
 <p>4 Et omnes reliqui in cunctis locis ubicumque habitant, adjuvent eum viri de loco suo argento et auro, et substantia, et pecoribus, excepto quod voluntarie offerunt templo Dei, quod est in Jerusalem.<br>
 </p>
 <p>5 Et surrexerunt principes patrum de Juda et Benjamin, et sacerdotes, et Levitæ, et omnis cujus Deus suscitavit spiritum, ut ascenderent ad ædificandum templum Domini, quod erat in Jerusalem.</p>
 <p>6 Universique qui erant in circuitu, adjuverunt manus eorum in vasis argenteis et aureis, in substantia et jumentis, in supellectili, exceptis his quæ sponte obtulerant.</p>
 <p>7 Rex quoque Cyrus protulit vasa templi Domini, quæ tulerat Nabuchodonosor de Jerusalem, et posuerat ea in templo dei sui.</p>
 <p>8 Protulit autem ea Cyrus rex Persarum per manum Mithridatis filii Gazabar, et annumeravit ea Sassabasar principi Juda.</p>
 <p>9 Et hic est numerus eorum: phialæ aureæ triginta, phialæ argenteæ mille, cultri viginti novem, scyphi aurei triginta,</p>
 <p>10 scyphi argentei secundi quadringenti decem, vasa alia mille.</p>
 <p>11 Omnia vasa aurea et argentea quinque millia quadringenta: universa tulit Sassabasar cum his qui ascendebant de transmigratione Babylonis in Jerusalem.</p>
