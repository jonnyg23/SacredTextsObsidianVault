  <p>H7554<br>  רקע  <br> רָקַע  ‎  râqa‛  <br><i>raw-kah‘ </i><br>A primitive root; to <i>pound</i> the earth (as a sign of passion); by analogy to <i>expand</i> (by hammering); by implication to <i>overlay</i> (with thin sheets of metal): - beat, make broad, spread abroad (forth, over, out, into plates), stamp, stretch.<br></p>
