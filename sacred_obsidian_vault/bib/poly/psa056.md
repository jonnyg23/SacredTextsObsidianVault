 <p align="center"><a href="../kjv/psa056.htm">KJV</a></p><p>1  To the chief Musician<sup><a href="h5329.htm">5329</a></sup> upon<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> Jonath-elem-rechokim,<sup><a href="h3128.htm">3128</a></sup> Michtam<sup><a href="h4387.htm">4387</a></sup> of David,<sup><a href="h1732.htm">1732</a></sup> when the Philistines<sup><a href="h6430.htm">6430</a></sup> took<sup><a href="h0270.htm">270</a></sup> him in Gath.<sup><a href="h1661.htm">1661</a></sup> Be merciful<sup><a href="h2603.htm">2603</a></sup> unto me, O God:<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> for<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> man<sup><a href="h0582.htm">582</a></sup> would swallow me up;<sup><a href="h7602.htm">7602</a></sup> he fighting<sup><a href="h3898.htm">3898</a></sup> daily<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3117.htm">3117</a></sup> oppresseth<sup><a href="h3905.htm">3905</a></sup> me. </p>
 <p>2  Mine enemies<sup><a href="h8324.htm">8324</a></sup> would daily<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3117.htm">3117</a></sup> swallow me up:<sup><a href="h7602.htm">7602</a></sup> for<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> they be many<sup><a href="h7227.htm">7227</a></sup> that fight<sup><a href="h3898.htm">3898</a></sup> against me, O thou most High.<sup><a href="h4791.htm">4791</a></sup> </p>
 <p>3  What time<sup><a href="h3117.htm">3117</a></sup> I am afraid,<sup><a href="h3372.htm">3372</a></sup> I<sup><a href="h0589.htm">589</a></sup> will trust<sup><a href="h0982.htm">982</a></sup> in<sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> thee. </p>
 <p>4  In God<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> I will praise<sup><a href="h1984.htm">1984</a></sup> his word,<sup><a href="h1697.htm">1697</a></sup> in God<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> I have put my trust;<sup><a href="h0982.htm">982</a></sup> I will not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> fear<sup><a href="h3372.htm">3372</a></sup> what<sup><a href="h4100.htm">4100</a></sup> flesh<sup><a href="h1320.htm">1320</a></sup> can do<sup><a href="h6213.htm">6213</a></sup> unto me. </p>
 <p>5  Every<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> day<sup><a href="h3117.htm">3117</a></sup> they wrest<sup><a href="h6087.htm">6087</a></sup> my words:<sup><a href="h1697.htm">1697</a></sup> all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> their thoughts<sup><a href="h4284.htm">4284</a></sup> are against<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> me for evil.<sup><a href="h7451.htm">7451</a></sup> </p>
 <p>6  They gather themselves together,<sup><a href="h1481.htm">1481</a></sup> they hide<sup><a href="h6845.htm">6845</a></sup> themselves, they<sup><a href="h1992.htm">1992</a></sup> mark<sup><a href="h8104.htm">8104</a></sup> my steps,<sup><a href="h6119.htm">6119</a></sup> when<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> they wait for<sup><a href="h6960.htm">6960</a></sup> my soul.<sup><a href="h5315.htm">5315</a></sup> </p>
 <p>7  Shall they<sup><a href="h3926.htm">3926</a></sup> escape<sup><a href="h6403.htm">6403</a></sup> by<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> iniquity?<sup><a href="h0205.htm">205</a></sup> in thine anger<sup><a href="h0639.htm">639</a></sup> cast down<sup><a href="h3381.htm">3381</a></sup> the people,<sup><a href="h5971.htm">5971</a></sup> O God.<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> </p>
 <p>8  Thou tellest<sup><a href="h5608.htm">5608</a></sup> my wanderings:<sup><a href="h5112.htm">5112</a></sup> put<sup><a href="h7760.htm">7760</a></sup> thou<sup><a href="h0859.htm">859</a></sup> my tears<sup><a href="h1832.htm">1832</a></sup> into thy bottle:<sup><a href="h4997.htm">4997</a></sup> are they not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> in thy book?<sup><a href="h5612.htm">5612</a></sup> </p>
 <p>9  When<sup><a href="h3117.htm">3117</a></sup> I cry<sup><a href="h7121.htm">7121</a></sup> unto thee, then<sup><a href="h0227.htm">227</a></sup> shall mine enemies<sup><a href="h0341.htm">341</a></sup> turn<sup><a href="h7725.htm">7725</a></sup> back:<sup><a href="h0268.htm">268</a></sup> this<sup><a href="h2088.htm">2088</a></sup> I know;<sup><a href="h3045.htm">3045</a></sup> for<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> God<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> is for me. </p>
 <p>10  In God<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> will I praise<sup><a href="h1984.htm">1984</a></sup> his word:<sup><a href="h1697.htm">1697</a></sup> in the LORD<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> will I praise<sup><a href="h1984.htm">1984</a></sup> his word.<sup><a href="h1697.htm">1697</a></sup> </p>
 <p>11  In God<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> have I put my trust:<sup><a href="h0982.htm">982</a></sup> I will not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> be afraid<sup><a href="h3372.htm">3372</a></sup> what<sup><a href="h4100.htm">4100</a></sup> man<sup><a href="h0120.htm">120</a></sup> can do<sup><a href="h6213.htm">6213</a></sup> unto me. </p>
 <p>12  Thy vows<sup><a href="h5088.htm">5088</a></sup> are upon<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> me, O God:<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> I will render<sup><a href="h7999.htm">7999</a></sup> praises<sup><a href="h8426.htm">8426</a></sup> unto thee. </p>
 <p>13  For<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> thou hast delivered<sup><a href="h5337.htm">5337</a></sup> my soul<sup><a href="h5315.htm">5315</a></sup> from death:<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h4194.htm">4194</a></sup> wilt not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> thou deliver my feet<sup><a href="h7272.htm">7272</a></sup> from falling,<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h1762.htm">1762</a></sup> that I may walk<sup><a href="h1980.htm">1980</a></sup> before<sup><a href="h6440.htm">6440</a></sup> God<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> in the light<sup><a href="h0216.htm">216</a></sup> of the living.<sup><a href="h2416.htm">2416</a></sup> </p>

 <p align="center"><a href="../sep/psa056.htm">Sep</a></p><p>1 εἰς τὸ τέλος μὴ διαφθείρῃς τῷ Δαυιδ εἰς στηλογραφίαν ἐν τῷ αὐτὸν ἀποδιδράσκειν ἀπὸ προσώπου Σαουλ εἰς τὸ σπήλαιον </p>
 <p>2 ἐλέησόν με ὁ θεός ἐλέησόν με ὅτι ἐπὶ σοὶ πέποιθεν ἡ ψυχή μου καὶ ἐν τῇ σκιᾷ τῶν πτερύγων σου ἐλπιῶ ἕως οὗ παρέλθῃ ἡ ἀνομία </p>
 <p>3 κεκράξομαι πρὸς τὸν θεὸν τὸν ὕψιστον τὸν θεὸν τὸν εὐεργετήσαντά με </p>
 <p>4 ἐξαπέστειλεν ἐξ οὐρανοῦ καὶ ἔσωσέν με ἔδωκεν εἰς ὄνειδος τοὺς καταπατοῦντάς με διάψαλμα ἐξαπέστειλεν ὁ θεὸς τὸ ἔλεος αὐτοῦ καὶ τὴν ἀλήθειαν αὐτοῦ </p>
 <p>5 καὶ ἐρρύσατο τὴν ψυχήν μου ἐκ μέσου σκύμνων ἐκοιμήθην τεταραγμένος υἱοὶ ἀνθρώπων οἱ ὀδόντες αὐτῶν ὅπλον καὶ βέλη καὶ ἡ γλῶσσα αὐτῶν μάχαιρα ὀξεῖα </p>
 <p>6 ὑψώθητι ἐπὶ τοὺς οὐρανούς ὁ θεός καὶ ἐπὶ πᾶσαν τὴν γῆν ἡ δόξα σου </p>
 <p>7 παγίδα ἡτοίμασαν τοῖς ποσίν μου καὶ κατέκαμψαν τὴν ψυχήν μου ὤρυξαν πρὸ προσώπου μου βόθρον καὶ ἐνέπεσαν εἰς αὐτόν διάψαλμα </p>
 <p>8 ἑτοίμη ἡ καρδία μου ὁ θεός ἑτοίμη ἡ καρδία μου ᾄσομαι καὶ ψαλῶ </p>
 <p>9 ἐξεγέρθητι ἡ δόξα μου ἐξεγέρθητι ψαλτήριον καὶ κιθάρα ἐξεγερθήσομαι ὄρθρου </p>
 <p>10 ἐξομολογήσομαί σοι ἐν λαοῖς κύριε ψαλῶ σοι ἐν ἔθνεσιν </p>
 <p>11 ὅτι ἐμεγαλύνθη ἕως τῶν οὐρανῶν τὸ ἔλεός σου καὶ ἕως τῶν νεφελῶν ἡ ἀλήθειά σου </p>
 <p>12 ὑψώθητι ἐπὶ τοὺς οὐρανούς ὁ θεός καὶ ἐπὶ πᾶσαν τὴν γῆν ἡ δόξα σου </p>

 <p align="center"><a href="../vul/psa056.htm">Vul</a></p><p>1 In finem, ne disperdas. David in tituli inscriptionem, cum fugeret a facie Saul in speluncam.</p>
 <p>2 [Miserere mei, Deus, miserere mei,<br> quoniam in te confidit anima mea.<br> Et in umbra alarum tuarum sperabo,<br> donec transeat iniquitas.<br></p>
 <p>3 Clamabo ad Deum altissimum,<br> Deum qui benefecit mihi.<br></p>
 <p>4 Misit de cælo, et liberavit me;<br> dedit in opprobrium conculcantes me.<br> Misit Deus misericordiam suam et veritatem suam,<br></p>
 <p>5 et eripuit animam meam de medio catulorum leonum.<br> Dormivi conturbatus.<br> Filii hominum dentes eorum arma et sagittæ,<br> et lingua eorum gladius acutus.<br></p>
 <p>6 Exaltare super cælos, Deus,<br> et in omnem terram gloria tua.<br></p>
 <p>7 Laqueum paraverunt pedibus meis,<br> et incurvaverunt animam meam.<br> Foderunt ante faciem meam foveam,<br> et inciderunt in eam.</p>
 <p>8 Paratum cor meum, Deus, paratum cor meum;<br> cantabo, et psalmum dicam.<br></p>
 <p>9 Exsurge, gloria mea;<br> exsurge, psalterium et cithara:<br> exsurgam diluculo.<br></p>
 <p>10 Confitebor tibi in populis, Domine,<br> et psalmum dicam tibi in gentibus:<br></p>
 <p>11 quoniam magnificata est usque ad cælos misericordia tua,<br> et usque ad nubes veritas tua.<br></p>
 <p>12 Exaltare super cælos, Deus,<br> et super omnem terram gloria tua.]</p>
