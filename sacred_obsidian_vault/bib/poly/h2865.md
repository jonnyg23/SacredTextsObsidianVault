  <p>H2865<br>  חתת  <br> חָתַת  ‎  châthath  <br><i>khaw-thath‘ </i><br>A primitive root; properly to <i>prostrate</i>; hence to <i>break</i> down, either (literally) by violence, or (figuratively) by confusion and fear: - abolish, affright, be (make) afraid, amaze, beat down, discourage, (cause to) dismay, go down, scare, terrify.<br></p>
