 <p align="center"><a href="../kjv/pro010.htm">KJV</a></p><p>1  The proverbs<sup><a href="h4912.htm">4912</a></sup> of Solomon.<sup><a href="h8010.htm">8010</a></sup> A wise<sup><a href="h2450.htm">2450</a></sup> son<sup><a href="h1121.htm">1121</a></sup> maketh a glad<sup><a href="h8055.htm">8055</a></sup> father:<sup><a href="h0001.htm">1</a></sup> but a foolish<sup><a href="h3684.htm">3684</a></sup> son<sup><a href="h1121.htm">1121</a></sup> is the heaviness<sup><a href="h8424.htm">8424</a></sup> of his mother.<sup><a href="h0517.htm">517</a></sup> </p>
 <p>2  Treasures<sup><a href="h0214.htm">214</a></sup> of wickedness<sup><a href="h7562.htm">7562</a></sup> profit<sup><a href="h3276.htm">3276</a></sup> nothing:<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> but righteousness<sup><a href="h6666.htm">6666</a></sup> delivereth<sup><a href="h5337.htm">5337</a></sup> from death.<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h4194.htm">4194</a></sup> </p>

 <p>4  He becometh poor<sup><a href="h7326.htm">7326</a></sup> that dealeth<sup><a href="h6213.htm">6213</a></sup> with a slack<sup><a href="h7423.htm">7423</a></sup> hand:<sup><a href="h3709.htm">3709</a></sup> but the hand<sup><a href="h3027.htm">3027</a></sup> of the diligent<sup><a href="h2742.htm">2742</a></sup> maketh rich.<sup><a href="h6238.htm">6238</a></sup> </p>
 <p>5  He that gathereth<sup><a href="h0103.htm">103</a></sup> in summer<sup><a href="h7019.htm">7019</a></sup> is a wise<sup><a href="h7919.htm">7919</a></sup> son:<sup><a href="h1121.htm">1121</a></sup> but he that sleepeth<sup><a href="h7290.htm">7290</a></sup> in harvest<sup><a href="h7105.htm">7105</a></sup> is a son<sup><a href="h1121.htm">1121</a></sup> that causeth shame.<sup><a href="h0954.htm">954</a></sup> </p>
 <p>6  Blessings<sup><a href="h1293.htm">1293</a></sup> are upon the head<sup><a href="h7218.htm">7218</a></sup> of the just:<sup><a href="h6662.htm">6662</a></sup> but violence<sup><a href="h2555.htm">2555</a></sup> covereth<sup><a href="h3680.htm">3680</a></sup> the mouth<sup><a href="h6310.htm">6310</a></sup> of the wicked.<sup><a href="h7563.htm">7563</a></sup> </p>
 <p>7  The memory<sup><a href="h2143.htm">2143</a></sup> of the just<sup><a href="h6662.htm">6662</a></sup> is blessed:<sup><a href="h1293.htm">1293</a></sup> but the name<sup><a href="h8034.htm">8034</a></sup> of the wicked<sup><a href="h7563.htm">7563</a></sup> shall rot.<sup><a href="h7537.htm">7537</a></sup> </p>
 <p>8  The wise<sup><a href="h2450.htm">2450</a></sup> in heart<sup><a href="h3820.htm">3820</a></sup> will receive<sup><a href="h3947.htm">3947</a></sup> commandments:<sup><a href="h4687.htm">4687</a></sup> but a prating<sup><a href="h8193.htm">8193</a></sup> fool<sup><a href="h0191.htm">191</a></sup> shall fall.<sup><a href="h3832.htm">3832</a></sup> </p>
 <p>9  He that walketh<sup><a href="h1980.htm">1980</a></sup> uprightly<sup><a href="h8537.htm">8537</a></sup> walketh<sup><a href="h1980.htm">1980</a></sup> surely:<sup><a href="h0983.htm">983</a></sup> but he that perverteth<sup><a href="h6140.htm">6140</a></sup> his ways<sup><a href="h1870.htm">1870</a></sup> shall be known.<sup><a href="h3045.htm">3045</a></sup> </p>
 <p>10  He that winketh<sup><a href="h7169.htm">7169</a></sup> with the eye<sup><a href="h5869.htm">5869</a></sup> causeth<sup><a href="h5414.htm">5414</a></sup> sorrow:<sup><a href="h6094.htm">6094</a></sup> but a prating<sup><a href="h8193.htm">8193</a></sup> fool<sup><a href="h0191.htm">191</a></sup> shall fall.<sup><a href="h3832.htm">3832</a></sup> </p>
 <p>11  The mouth<sup><a href="h6310.htm">6310</a></sup> of a righteous<sup><a href="h6662.htm">6662</a></sup> man is a well<sup><a href="h4726.htm">4726</a></sup> of life:<sup><a href="h2416.htm">2416</a></sup> but violence<sup><a href="h2555.htm">2555</a></sup> covereth<sup><a href="h3680.htm">3680</a></sup> the mouth<sup><a href="h6310.htm">6310</a></sup> of the wicked.<sup><a href="h7563.htm">7563</a></sup> </p>
 <p>12  Hatred<sup><a href="h8135.htm">8135</a></sup> stirreth up<sup><a href="h5782.htm">5782</a></sup> strifes:<sup><a href="h4090.htm">4090</a></sup> but love<sup><a href="h0160.htm">160</a></sup> covereth<sup><a href="h3680.htm">3680</a></sup> <sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> sins.<sup><a href="h6588.htm">6588</a></sup> </p>
 <p>13  In the lips<sup><a href="h8193.htm">8193</a></sup> of him that hath understanding<sup><a href="h0995.htm">995</a></sup> wisdom<sup><a href="h2451.htm">2451</a></sup> is found:<sup><a href="h4672.htm">4672</a></sup> but a rod<sup><a href="h7626.htm">7626</a></sup> is for the back<sup><a href="h1460.htm">1460</a></sup> of him that is void<sup><a href="h2638.htm">2638</a></sup> of understanding.<sup><a href="h3820.htm">3820</a></sup> </p>
 <p>14  Wise<sup><a href="h2450.htm">2450</a></sup> men lay up<sup><a href="h6845.htm">6845</a></sup> knowledge:<sup><a href="h1847.htm">1847</a></sup> but the mouth<sup><a href="h6310.htm">6310</a></sup> of the foolish<sup><a href="h0191.htm">191</a></sup> is near<sup><a href="h7138.htm">7138</a></sup> destruction.<sup><a href="h4288.htm">4288</a></sup> </p>
 <p>15  The rich man's<sup><a href="h6223.htm">6223</a></sup> wealth<sup><a href="h1952.htm">1952</a></sup> is his strong<sup><a href="h5797.htm">5797</a></sup> city:<sup><a href="h7151.htm">7151</a></sup> the destruction<sup><a href="h4288.htm">4288</a></sup> of the poor<sup><a href="h1800.htm">1800</a></sup> is their poverty.<sup><a href="h7389.htm">7389</a></sup> </p>
 <p>16  The labor<sup><a href="h6468.htm">6468</a></sup> of the righteous<sup><a href="h6662.htm">6662</a></sup> tendeth to life:<sup><a href="h2416.htm">2416</a></sup> the fruit<sup><a href="h8393.htm">8393</a></sup> of the wicked<sup><a href="h7563.htm">7563</a></sup> to sin.<sup><a href="h2403.htm">2403</a></sup> </p>
 <p>17  He is in the way<sup><a href="h0734.htm">734</a></sup> of life<sup><a href="h2416.htm">2416</a></sup> that keepeth<sup><a href="h8104.htm">8104</a></sup> instruction:<sup><a href="h4148.htm">4148</a></sup> but he that refuseth<sup><a href="h5800.htm">5800</a></sup> reproof<sup><a href="h8433.htm">8433</a></sup> erreth.<sup><a href="h8582.htm">8582</a></sup> </p>
 <p>18  He that hideth<sup><a href="h3680.htm">3680</a></sup> hatred<sup><a href="h8135.htm">8135</a></sup> with lying<sup><a href="h8267.htm">8267</a></sup> lips,<sup><a href="h8193.htm">8193</a></sup> and he that uttereth<sup><a href="h3318.htm">3318</a></sup> a slander,<sup><a href="h1681.htm">1681</a></sup> is a fool.<sup><a href="h3684.htm">3684</a></sup> </p>
 <p>19  In the multitude<sup><a href="h7230.htm">7230</a></sup> of words<sup><a href="h1697.htm">1697</a></sup> there wanteth<sup><a href="h2308.htm">2308</a></sup> not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> sin:<sup><a href="h6588.htm">6588</a></sup> but he that refraineth<sup><a href="h2820.htm">2820</a></sup> his lips<sup><a href="h8193.htm">8193</a></sup> is wise.<sup><a href="h7919.htm">7919</a></sup> </p>
 <p>20  The tongue<sup><a href="h3956.htm">3956</a></sup> of the just<sup><a href="h6662.htm">6662</a></sup> is as choice<sup><a href="h0977.htm">977</a></sup> silver:<sup><a href="h3701.htm">3701</a></sup> the heart<sup><a href="h3820.htm">3820</a></sup> of the wicked<sup><a href="h7563.htm">7563</a></sup> is little worth.<sup><a href="h4592.htm">4592</a></sup> </p>
 <p>21  The lips<sup><a href="h8193.htm">8193</a></sup> of the righteous<sup><a href="h6662.htm">6662</a></sup> feed<sup><a href="h7462.htm">7462</a></sup> many:<sup><a href="h7227.htm">7227</a></sup> but fools<sup><a href="h0191.htm">191</a></sup> die<sup><a href="h4191.htm">4191</a></sup> for want<sup><a href="h2638.htm">2638</a></sup> of wisdom.<sup><a href="h3820.htm">3820</a></sup> </p>
 <p>22  The blessing<sup><a href="h1293.htm">1293</a></sup> of the LORD,<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> it<sup><a href="h1931.htm">1931</a></sup> maketh rich,<sup><a href="h6238.htm">6238</a></sup> and he addeth<sup><a href="h3254.htm">3254</a></sup> no<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> sorrow<sup><a href="h6089.htm">6089</a></sup> with<sup><a href="h5973.htm">5973</a></sup> it. </p>
 <p>23  It is as sport<sup><a href="h7814.htm">7814</a></sup> to a fool<sup><a href="h3684.htm">3684</a></sup> to do<sup><a href="h6213.htm">6213</a></sup> mischief:<sup><a href="h2154.htm">2154</a></sup> but a man<sup><a href="h0376.htm">376</a></sup> of understanding<sup><a href="h8394.htm">8394</a></sup> hath wisdom.<sup><a href="h2451.htm">2451</a></sup> </p>
 <p>24  The fear<sup><a href="h4034.htm">4034</a></sup> of the wicked,<sup><a href="h7563.htm">7563</a></sup> it<sup><a href="h1931.htm">1931</a></sup> shall come upon<sup><a href="h0935.htm">935</a></sup> him: but the desire<sup><a href="h8378.htm">8378</a></sup> of the righteous<sup><a href="h6662.htm">6662</a></sup> shall be granted.<sup><a href="h5414.htm">5414</a></sup> </p>
 <p>25  As the whirlwind<sup><a href="h5492.htm">5492</a></sup> passeth,<sup><a href="h5674.htm">5674</a></sup> so is the wicked<sup><a href="h7563.htm">7563</a></sup> no<sup><a href="h0369.htm">369</a></sup> more: but the righteous<sup><a href="h6662.htm">6662</a></sup> is an everlasting<sup><a href="h5769.htm">5769</a></sup> foundation.<sup><a href="h3247.htm">3247</a></sup> </p>
 <p>26  As vinegar<sup><a href="h2558.htm">2558</a></sup> to the teeth,<sup><a href="h8127.htm">8127</a></sup> and as smoke<sup><a href="h6227.htm">6227</a></sup> to the eyes,<sup><a href="h5869.htm">5869</a></sup> so<sup><a href="h3651.htm">3651</a></sup> is the sluggard<sup><a href="h6102.htm">6102</a></sup> to them that send<sup><a href="h7971.htm">7971</a></sup> him. </p>
 <p>27  The fear<sup><a href="h3374.htm">3374</a></sup> of the LORD<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> prolongeth<sup><a href="h3254.htm">3254</a></sup> days:<sup><a href="h3117.htm">3117</a></sup> but the years<sup><a href="h8141.htm">8141</a></sup> of the wicked<sup><a href="h7563.htm">7563</a></sup> shall be shortened.<sup><a href="h7114.htm">7114</a></sup> </p>
 <p>28  The hope<sup><a href="h8431.htm">8431</a></sup> of the righteous<sup><a href="h6662.htm">6662</a></sup> shall be gladness:<sup><a href="h8057.htm">8057</a></sup> but the expectation<sup><a href="h8615.htm">8615</a></sup> of the wicked<sup><a href="h7563.htm">7563</a></sup> shall perish.<sup><a href="h0006.htm">6</a></sup> </p>
 <p>29  The way<sup><a href="h1870.htm">1870</a></sup> of the LORD<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> is strength<sup><a href="h4581.htm">4581</a></sup> to the upright:<sup><a href="h8537.htm">8537</a></sup> but destruction<sup><a href="h4288.htm">4288</a></sup> shall be to the workers<sup><a href="h6466.htm">6466</a></sup> of iniquity.<sup><a href="h0205.htm">205</a></sup> </p>
 <p>30  The righteous<sup><a href="h6662.htm">6662</a></sup> shall never<sup><a href="h5769.htm">5769</a></sup> <sup><a href="h1077.htm">1077</a></sup> be removed:<sup><a href="h4131.htm">4131</a></sup> but the wicked<sup><a href="h7563.htm">7563</a></sup> shall not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> inhabit<sup><a href="h7931.htm">7931</a></sup> the earth.<sup><a href="h0776.htm">776</a></sup> </p>
 <p>31  The mouth<sup><a href="h6310.htm">6310</a></sup> of the just<sup><a href="h6662.htm">6662</a></sup> bringeth forth<sup><a href="h5107.htm">5107</a></sup> wisdom:<sup><a href="h2451.htm">2451</a></sup> but the froward<sup><a href="h8419.htm">8419</a></sup> tongue<sup><a href="h3956.htm">3956</a></sup> shall be cut out.<sup><a href="h3772.htm">3772</a></sup> </p>
 <p>32  The lips<sup><a href="h8193.htm">8193</a></sup> of the righteous<sup><a href="h6662.htm">6662</a></sup> know<sup><a href="h3045.htm">3045</a></sup> what is acceptable:<sup><a href="h7522.htm">7522</a></sup> but the mouth<sup><a href="h6310.htm">6310</a></sup> of the wicked<sup><a href="h7563.htm">7563</a></sup> speaketh frowardness.<sup><a href="h8419.htm">8419</a></sup> </p>

 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎6 ‏בְּ֭רָכוֹת לְרֹ֣אשׁ צַדִּ֑יק וּפִ֥י רְ֝שָׁעִ֗ים יְכַסֶּ֥ה חָמָֽס׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎7 ‏זֵ֣כֶר צַ֭דִּיק לִבְרָכָ֑ה וְשֵׁ֖ם רְשָׁעִ֣ים יִרְקָֽב׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎8 ‏חֲכַם־לֵ֭ב יִקַּ֣ח מִצְוֹ֑ת וֶאֱוִ֥יל שְׂ֝פָתַ֗יִם יִלָּבֵֽט׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎9 ‏הוֹלֵ֣ךְ בַּ֭תֹּם יֵ֣לֶךְ בֶּ֑טַח וּמְעַקֵּ֥שׁ דְּ֝רָכָ֗יו יִוָּדֵֽעַ׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎10 ‏קֹ֣רֵֽץ<font color="red"><sup>3</sup></font> עַ֭יִן יִתֵּ֣ן עַצָּ֑בֶת וֶאֱוִ֥יל שְׂ֝פָתַ֗יִם יִלָּבֵֽט׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎11 ‏מְק֣וֹר חַ֭יִּים פִּ֣י צַדִּ֑יק וּפִ֥י רְ֝שָׁעִ֗ים יְכַסֶּ֥ה חָמָֽס׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎12 ‏שִׂ֭נְאָה תְּעוֹרֵ֣ר מְדָנִ֑ים וְעַ֥ל כָּל־פְּ֝שָׁעִ֗ים תְּכַסֶּ֥ה אַהֲבָֽה׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎13 ‏בְּשִׂפְתֵ֣י נָ֭בוֹן תִּמָּצֵ֣א חָכְמָ֑ה וְ֝שֵׁ֗בֶט לְגֵ֣ו חֲסַר־לֵֽב׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎14 ‏חֲכָמִ֥ים יִצְפְּנוּ־דָ֑עַת וּפִֽי־אֱ֝וִיל מְחִתָּ֥ה קְרֹבָֽה׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎15 ‏ה֣וֹן עָ֭שִׁיר קִרְיַ֣ת עֻזּ֑וֹ מְחִתַּ֖ת דַּלִּ֣ים רֵישָֽׁם׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎16 ‏פְּעֻלַּ֣ת צַדִּ֣יק לְחַיִּ֑ים תְּבוּאַ֖ת רָשָׁ֣ע לְחַטָּֽאת׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎17 ‏אֹ֣רַח לְ֭חַיִּים שׁוֹמֵ֣ר מוּסָ֑ר וְעוֹזֵ֖ב תּוֹכַ֣חַת מַתְעֶֽה׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎18 ‏מְכַסֶּ֣ה שִׂ֭נְאָה שִׂפְתֵי־שָׁ֑קֶר וּמוֹצִ֥א דִ֝בָּ֗ה ה֣וּא כְסִֽיל׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎19 ‏בְּרֹ֣ב דְּ֭בָרִים לֹ֣א יֶחְדַּל־פָּ֑שַׁע וְחֹשֵׂ֖ךְ שְׂפָתָ֣יו מַשְׂכִּֽיל׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎20 ‏כֶּ֣סֶף נִ֭בְחָר לְשׁ֣וֹן צַדִּ֑יק לֵ֖ב רְשָׁעִ֣ים כִּמְעָֽט׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎21 ‏שִׂפְתֵ֣י צַ֭דִּיק יִרְע֣וּ רַבִּ֑ים וֶֽ֝אֱוִילִ֗ים בַּחֲסַר־לֵ֥ב יָמֽוּתוּ׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎22 ‏בִּרְכַּ֣ת יְ֭הוָה הִ֣יא תַעֲשִׁ֑יר וְלֹֽא־יוֹסִ֖ף עֶ֣צֶב עִמָּֽהּ׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎23 ‏כִּשְׂח֣וֹק לִ֭כְסִיל עֲשׂ֣וֹת זִמָּ֑ה וְ֝חָכְמָ֗ה לְאִ֣ישׁ תְּבוּנָֽה׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎24 ‏מְגוֹרַ֣ת רָ֭שָׁע הִ֣יא תְבוֹאֶ֑נּוּ וְתַאֲוַ֖ת צַדִּיקִ֣ים יִתֵּֽן׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎25 ‏כַּעֲב֣וֹר ס֭וּפָה<font color="red"><sup>3</sup></font> וְאֵ֣ין רָשָׁ֑ע וְ֝צַדִּ֗יק יְס֣וֹד עוֹלָֽם׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎26 ‏כַּחֹ֤מֶץ׀ לַשִּׁנַּ֗יִם וְכֶעָשָׁ֥ן לָעֵינָ֑יִם כֵּ֥ן הֶ֝עָצֵ֗ל לְשֹׁלְחָֽיו׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎27 ‏יִרְאַ֣ת יְ֭הוָה תּוֹסִ֣יף יָמִ֑ים וּשְׁנ֖וֹת רְשָׁעִ֣ים תִּקְצֹֽרְנָה׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎28 ‏תּוֹחֶ֣לֶת צַדִּיקִ֣ים שִׂמְחָ֑ה וְתִקְוַ֖ת רְשָׁעִ֣ים תֹּאבֵֽד׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎29 ‏מָע֣וֹז לַ֭תֹּם דֶּ֣רֶךְ יְהוָ֑ה וּ֝מְחִתָּ֗ה לְפֹ֣עֲלֵי אָֽוֶן׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎30 ‏צַדִּ֣יק לְעוֹלָ֣ם בַּל־יִמּ֑וֹט וּ֝רְשָׁעִ֗ים לֹ֣א יִשְׁכְּנוּ־אָֽרֶץ׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎31 ‏פִּֽי־צַ֭דִּיק יָנ֣וּב חָכְמָ֑ה וּלְשׁ֥וֹן תַּ֝הְפֻּכ֗וֹת תִּכָּרֵֽת׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎32 ‏שִׂפְתֵ֣י צַ֭דִּיק יֵדְע֣וּן רָצ֑וֹן וּפִ֥י רְ֝שָׁעִ֗ים תַּהְפֻּכֽוֹת׃ </span></p>

 <p align="center"><a href="../vul/pro010.htm">Vul</a></p><p>1 [Filius sapiens lætificat patrem,<br> filius vero stultus mstitia est matris suæ.<br></p>
 <p>2 Nil proderunt thesauri impietatis,<br> justitia vero liberabit a morte.<br></p>
 <p>3 Non affliget Dominus fame animam justi,<br> et insidias impiorum subvertet.<br></p>
 <p>4 Egestatem operata est manus remissa;<br> manus autem fortium divitias parat.<br> Qui nititur mendaciis, hic pascit ventos;<br> idem autem ipse sequitur aves volantes.<br></p>
 <p>5 Qui congregat in messe, filius sapiens est;<br> qui autem stertit æstate, filius confusionis.<br></p>
 <p>6 Benedictio Domini super caput justi;<br> os autem impiorum operit iniquitas.<br></p>
 <p>7 Memoria justi cum laudibus,<br> et nomen impiorum putrescet.<br></p>
 <p>8 Sapiens corde præcepta suscipit;<br> stultus cæditur labiis.<br></p>
 <p>9 Qui ambulat simpliciter ambulat confidenter;<br> qui autem depravat vias suas manifestus erit.<br></p>
 <p>10 Qui annuit oculo dabit dolorem;<br> et stultus labiis verberabitur.]<br>
 </p>
 <p>11 [Vena vitæ os justi,<br> et os impiorum operit iniquitatem.<br></p>
 <p>12 Odium suscitat rixas,<br> et universa delicta operit caritas.<br></p>
 <p>13 In labiis sapientis invenitur sapientia,<br> et virga in dorso ejus qui indiget corde.<br></p>
 <p>14 Sapientes abscondunt scientiam;<br> os autem stulti confusioni proximum est.<br></p>
 <p>15 Substantia divitis, urbs fortitudinis ejus;<br> pavor pauperum egestas eorum.<br></p>
 <p>16 Opus justi ad vitam,<br> fructus autem impii ad peccatum.<br></p>
 <p>17 Via vitæ custodienti disciplinam;<br> qui autem increpationes relinquit, errat.<br></p>
 <p>18 Abscondunt odium labia mendacia;<br> qui profert contumeliam, insipiens est.<br></p>
 <p>19 In multiloquio non deerit peccatum,<br> qui autem moderatur labia sua prudentissimus est.<br></p>
 <p>20 Argentum electum lingua justi;<br> cor autem impiorum pro nihilo.<br></p>
 <p>21 Labia justi erudiunt plurimos;<br> qui autem indocti sunt in cordis egestate morientur.]<br>
 </p>
 <p>22 [Benedictio Domini divites facit,<br> nec sociabitur eis afflictio.<br></p>
 <p>23 Quasi per risum stultus operatur scelus,<br> sapientia autem est viro prudentia.<br></p>
 <p>24 Quod timet impius veniet super eum;<br> desiderium suum justus dabitur.<br></p>
 <p>25 Quasi tempestas transiens non erit impius;<br> justus autem quasi fundamentum sempiternum.<br></p>
 <p>26 Sicut acetum dentibus, et fumus oculis,<br> sic piger his qui miserunt eum.<br></p>
 <p>27 Timor Domini apponet dies,<br> et anni impiorum breviabuntur.<br></p>
 <p>28 Exspectatio justorum lætitia,<br> spes autem impiorum peribit.<br></p>
 <p>29 Fortitudo simplicis via Domini,<br> et pavor his qui operantur malum.<br></p>
 <p>30 Justus in æternum non commovebitur,<br> impii autem non habitabunt super terram.<br></p>
 <p>31 Os justi parturiet sapientiam;<br> lingua pravorum peribit.<br></p>
 <p>32 Labia justi considerant placita,<br> et os impiorum perversa.]</p>
