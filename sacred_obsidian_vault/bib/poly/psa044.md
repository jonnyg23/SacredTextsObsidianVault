 <p>2  How thou<sup><a href="h0859.htm">859</a></sup> didst drive out<sup><a href="h3423.htm">3423</a></sup> the heathen<sup><a href="h1471.htm">1471</a></sup> with thy hand,<sup><a href="h3027.htm">3027</a></sup> and plantedst<sup><a href="h5193.htm">5193</a></sup> them; how thou didst afflict<sup><a href="h7489.htm">7489</a></sup> the people,<sup><a href="h3816.htm">3816</a></sup> and cast them out.<sup><a href="h7971.htm">7971</a></sup> </p>
 <p>3  For<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> they got not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> the land<sup><a href="h0776.htm">776</a></sup> in possession<sup><a href="h3423.htm">3423</a></sup> by their own sword,<sup><a href="h2719.htm">2719</a></sup> neither<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> did their own arm<sup><a href="h2220.htm">2220</a></sup> save<sup><a href="h3467.htm">3467</a></sup> them: but<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> thy right hand,<sup><a href="h3225.htm">3225</a></sup> and thine arm,<sup><a href="h2220.htm">2220</a></sup> and the light<sup><a href="h0216.htm">216</a></sup> of thy countenance,<sup><a href="h6440.htm">6440</a></sup> because<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> thou hadst a favor<sup><a href="h7521.htm">7521</a></sup> unto them. </p>
 <p>4  Thou<sup><a href="h0859.htm">859</a></sup> art my King,<sup><a href="h4428.htm">4428</a></sup> O God:<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> command<sup><a href="h6680.htm">6680</a></sup> deliverances<sup><a href="h3444.htm">3444</a></sup> for Jacob.<sup><a href="h3290.htm">3290</a></sup> </p>
 <p>5  Through thee will we push down<sup><a href="h5055.htm">5055</a></sup> our enemies:<sup><a href="h6862.htm">6862</a></sup> through thy name<sup><a href="h8034.htm">8034</a></sup> will we tread them under<sup><a href="h0947.htm">947</a></sup> that rise up against<sup><a href="h6965.htm">6965</a></sup> us. </p>
 <p>6  For<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> I will not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> trust<sup><a href="h0982.htm">982</a></sup> in my bow,<sup><a href="h7198.htm">7198</a></sup> neither<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> shall my sword<sup><a href="h2719.htm">2719</a></sup> save<sup><a href="h3467.htm">3467</a></sup> me. </p>
 <p>7  But<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> thou hast saved<sup><a href="h3467.htm">3467</a></sup> us from our enemies,<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h6862.htm">6862</a></sup> and hast put them to shame<sup><a href="h0954.htm">954</a></sup> that hated<sup><a href="h8130.htm">8130</a></sup> us. </p>
 <p>8  In God<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> we boast<sup><a href="h1984.htm">1984</a></sup> all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> the day<sup><a href="h3117.htm">3117</a></sup> long, and praise<sup><a href="h3034.htm">3034</a></sup> thy name<sup><a href="h8034.htm">8034</a></sup> forever.<sup><a href="h5769.htm">5769</a></sup> Selah.<sup><a href="h5542.htm">5542</a></sup> </p>

 <p>10  Thou makest us to turn<sup><a href="h7725.htm">7725</a></sup> back<sup><a href="h0268.htm">268</a></sup> from<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> the enemy:<sup><a href="h6862.htm">6862</a></sup> and they which hate<sup><a href="h8130.htm">8130</a></sup> us spoil<sup><a href="h8154.htm">8154</a></sup> for themselves. </p>
 <p>11  Thou hast given<sup><a href="h5414.htm">5414</a></sup> us like sheep<sup><a href="h6629.htm">6629</a></sup> appointed for meat;<sup><a href="h3978.htm">3978</a></sup> and hast scattered<sup><a href="h2219.htm">2219</a></sup> us among the heathen.<sup><a href="h1471.htm">1471</a></sup> </p>
 <p>12  Thou sellest<sup><a href="h4376.htm">4376</a></sup> thy people<sup><a href="h5971.htm">5971</a></sup> for naught,<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> <sup><a href="h1952.htm">1952</a></sup> and dost not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> increase<sup><a href="h7235.htm">7235</a></sup> thy wealth by their price.<sup><a href="h4242.htm">4242</a></sup> </p>
 <p>13  Thou makest<sup><a href="h7760.htm">7760</a></sup> us a reproach<sup><a href="h2781.htm">2781</a></sup> to our neighbors,<sup><a href="h7934.htm">7934</a></sup> a scorn<sup><a href="h3933.htm">3933</a></sup> and a derision<sup><a href="h7047.htm">7047</a></sup> to them that are round about<sup><a href="h5439.htm">5439</a></sup> us. </p>
 <p>14  Thou makest<sup><a href="h7760.htm">7760</a></sup> us a byword<sup><a href="h4912.htm">4912</a></sup> among the heathen,<sup><a href="h1471.htm">1471</a></sup> a shaking<sup><a href="h4493.htm">4493</a></sup> of the head<sup><a href="h7218.htm">7218</a></sup> among the people.<sup><a href="h3816.htm">3816</a></sup> </p>
 <p>15  My confusion<sup><a href="h3639.htm">3639</a></sup> is continually<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3117.htm">3117</a></sup> before<sup><a href="h5048.htm">5048</a></sup> me, and the shame<sup><a href="h1322.htm">1322</a></sup> of my face<sup><a href="h6440.htm">6440</a></sup> hath covered<sup><a href="h3680.htm">3680</a></sup> me, </p>
 <p>16  For the voice<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h6963.htm">6963</a></sup> of him that reproacheth<sup><a href="h2778.htm">2778</a></sup> and blasphemeth;<sup><a href="h1442.htm">1442</a></sup> by reason<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h6440.htm">6440</a></sup> of the enemy<sup><a href="h0341.htm">341</a></sup> and avenger.<sup><a href="h5358.htm">5358</a></sup> </p>
 <p>17  All<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> this<sup><a href="h2063.htm">2063</a></sup> is come upon<sup><a href="h0935.htm">935</a></sup> us; yet have we not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> forgotten<sup><a href="h7911.htm">7911</a></sup> thee, neither<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> have we dealt falsely<sup><a href="h8266.htm">8266</a></sup> in thy covenant.<sup><a href="h1285.htm">1285</a></sup> </p>
 <p>18  Our heart<sup><a href="h3820.htm">3820</a></sup> is not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> turned<sup><a href="h5472.htm">5472</a></sup> back,<sup><a href="h0268.htm">268</a></sup> neither have our steps<sup><a href="h0838.htm">838</a></sup> declined<sup><a href="h5186.htm">5186</a></sup> from<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> thy way;<sup><a href="h0734.htm">734</a></sup> </p>
 <p>19  Though<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> thou hast sore broken<sup><a href="h1794.htm">1794</a></sup> us in the place<sup><a href="h4725.htm">4725</a></sup> of dragons,<sup><a href="h8577.htm">8577</a></sup> and covered<sup><a href="h3680.htm">3680</a></sup> <sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> us with the shadow of death.<sup><a href="h6757.htm">6757</a></sup> </p>
 <p>20  If<sup><a href="h0518.htm">518</a></sup> we have forgotten<sup><a href="h7911.htm">7911</a></sup> the name<sup><a href="h8034.htm">8034</a></sup> of our God,<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> or stretched out<sup><a href="h6566.htm">6566</a></sup> our hands<sup><a href="h3709.htm">3709</a></sup> to a strange<sup><a href="h2114.htm">2114</a></sup> god;<sup><a href="h0410.htm">410</a></sup> </p>

 <p>7 ὁ θρόνος σου ὁ θεός εἰς τὸν αἰῶνα τοῦ αἰῶνος ῥάβδος εὐθύτητος ἡ ῥάβδος τῆς βασιλείας σου </p>

 <p>9 σμύρνα καὶ στακτὴ καὶ κασία ἀπὸ τῶν ἱματίων σου ἀπὸ βάρεων ἐλεφαντίνων ἐξ ὧν ηὔφρανάν σε </p>
 <p>10 θυγατέρες βασιλέων ἐν τῇ τιμῇ σου παρέστη ἡ βασίλισσα ἐκ δεξιῶν σου ἐν ἱματισμῷ διαχρύσῳ περιβεβλημένη πεποικιλμένη </p>
 <p>11 ἄκουσον θύγατερ καὶ ἰδὲ καὶ κλῖνον τὸ οὖς σου καὶ ἐπιλάθου τοῦ λαοῦ σου καὶ τοῦ οἴκου τοῦ πατρός σου </p>
 <p>12 ὅτι ἐπεθύμησεν ὁ βασιλεὺς τοῦ κάλλους σου ὅτι αὐτός ἐστιν ὁ κύριός σου </p>
 <p>13 καὶ προσκυνήσουσιν αὐτῷ θυγατέρες Τύρου ἐν δώροις τὸ πρόσωπόν σου λιτανεύσουσιν οἱ πλούσιοι τοῦ λαοῦ </p>
 <p>14 πᾶσα ἡ δόξα αὐτῆς θυγατρὸς βασιλέως ἔσωθεν ἐν κροσσωτοῖς χρυσοῖς περιβεβλημένη πεποικιλμένη </p>
 <p>15 ἀπενεχθήσονται τῷ βασιλεῖ παρθένοι ὀπίσω αὐτῆς αἱ πλησίον αὐτῆς ἀπενεχθήσονταί σοι </p>
 <p>16 ἀπενεχθήσονται ἐν εὐφροσύνῃ καὶ ἀγαλλιάσει ἀχθήσονται εἰς ναὸν βασιλέως </p>
 <p>17 ἀντὶ τῶν πατέρων σου ἐγενήθησάν σοι υἱοί καταστήσεις αὐτοὺς ἄρχοντας ἐπὶ πᾶσαν τὴν γῆν </p>
 <p>18 μνησθήσονται τοῦ ὀνόματός σου ἐν πάσῃ γενεᾷ καὶ γενεᾷ διὰ τοῦτο λαοὶ ἐξομολογήσονταί σοι εἰς τὸν αἰῶνα καὶ εἰς τὸν αἰῶνα τοῦ αἰῶνος </p>

 <p align="center"><a href="../vul/psa044.htm">Vul</a></p><p>1 In finem, pro iis qui commutabuntur. Filiis Core, ad intellectum. Canticum pro dilecto.</p>
 <p>2 [Eructavit cor meum verbum bonum:<br> dico ego opera mea regi.<br> Lingua mea calamus scribæ<br> velociter scribentis.<br></p>
 <p>3 Speciosus forma præ filiis hominum,<br> diffusa est gratia in labiis tuis:<br> propterea benedixit te Deus in æternum.<br></p>
 <p>4 Accingere gladio tuo super femur tuum, potentissime.<br></p>
 <p>5 Specie tua et pulchritudine tua<br> intende, prospere procede, et regna,<br> propter veritatem, et mansuetudinem, et justitiam;<br> et deducet te mirabiliter dextera tua.<br></p>
 <p>6 Sagittæ tuæ acutæ:<br> populi sub te cadent,<br> in corda inimicorum regis.<br></p>
 <p>7 Sedes tua, Deus, in sæculum sæculi;<br> virga directionis virga regni tui.<br></p>
 <p>8 Dilexisti justitiam, et odisti iniquitatem;<br> propterea unxit te Deus, Deus tuus,<br> oleo lætitiæ, præ consortibus tuis.<br></p>
 <p>9 Myrrha, et gutta, et casia a vestimentis tuis,<br> a domibus eburneis; ex quibus delectaverunt te<br></p>
 <p>10 filiæ regum in honore tuo.<br> Astitit regina a dextris tuis<br> in vestitu deaurato, circumdata varietate.<br></p>
 <p>11 Audi, filia, et vide, et inclina aurem tuam;<br> et obliviscere populum tuum, et domum patris tui.<br></p>
 <p>12 Et concupiscet rex decorem tuum,<br> quoniam ipse est Dominus Deus tuus, et adorabunt eum.<br></p>
 <p>13 Et filiæ Tyri in muneribus vultum tuum deprecabuntur;<br> omnes divites plebis.<br></p>
 <p>14 Omnis gloria ejus filiæ regis ab intus,<br> in fimbriis aureis,<br></p>
 <p>15 circumamicta varietatibus.<br> Adducentur regi virgines post eam;<br> proximæ ejus afferentur tibi.<br></p>
 <p>16 Afferentur in lætitia et exsultatione;<br> adducentur in templum regis.<br></p>
 <p>17 Pro patribus tuis nati sunt tibi filii;<br> constitues eos principes super omnem terram.<br></p>
 <p>18 Memores erunt nominis tui in omni generatione et generationem:<br> propterea populi confitebuntur tibi in æternum, et in sæculum sæculi.]</p>
