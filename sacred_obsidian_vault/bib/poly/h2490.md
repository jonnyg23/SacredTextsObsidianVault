  <p>H2490<br>  חלל  <br> חָלַל  ‎  châlal  <br><i>khaw-lal‘ </i><br>A primitive root (compare <a href="h2470.htm">2470</a> ; properly to <i>bore</i>, that is, (by implication) to <i>wound</i>, to <i>dissolve</i>; figuratively to <i>profane</i> (a person, place or thing), to <i>break</i> (one’s word), to <i>begin</i> (as if by an opening-wedge); denominatively (from <a href="h2485.htm">2485</a>  to <i>play</i> (the flute): - begin (X men began), defile, X break, defile, X eat (as common things), X first, X gather the grape thereof, X take inheritance, pipe, player on instruments, pollute, (cast as) profane (self), prostitute, slay (slain), sorrow, stain, wound.<br></p>
