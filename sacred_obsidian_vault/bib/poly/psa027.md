 <p>2  When the wicked,<sup><a href="h7489.htm">7489</a></sup> even mine enemies<sup><a href="h6862.htm">6862</a></sup> and my foes,<sup><a href="h0341.htm">341</a></sup> came<sup><a href="h7126.htm">7126</a></sup> upon<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> me to eat up<sup><a href="h0398.htm">398</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> my flesh,<sup><a href="h1320.htm">1320</a></sup> they<sup><a href="h1992.htm">1992</a></sup> stumbled<sup><a href="h3782.htm">3782</a></sup> and fell.<sup><a href="h5307.htm">5307</a></sup> </p>
 <p>3  Though<sup><a href="h0518.htm">518</a></sup> a host<sup><a href="h4264.htm">4264</a></sup> should encamp<sup><a href="h2583.htm">2583</a></sup> against<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> me, my heart<sup><a href="h3820.htm">3820</a></sup> shall not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> fear:<sup><a href="h3372.htm">3372</a></sup> though<sup><a href="h0518.htm">518</a></sup> war<sup><a href="h4421.htm">4421</a></sup> should rise<sup><a href="h6965.htm">6965</a></sup> against<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> me, in this<sup><a href="h2063.htm">2063</a></sup> will I<sup><a href="h0589.htm">589</a></sup> be confident.<sup><a href="h0982.htm">982</a></sup> </p>

 <p>5  For<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> in the time<sup><a href="h3117.htm">3117</a></sup> of trouble<sup><a href="h7451.htm">7451</a></sup> he shall hide<sup><a href="h6845.htm">6845</a></sup> me in his pavilion:<sup><a href="h5520.htm">5520</a></sup> in the secret<sup><a href="h5643.htm">5643</a></sup> of his tabernacle<sup><a href="h0168.htm">168</a></sup> shall he hide<sup><a href="h5641.htm">5641</a></sup> me; he shall set me up<sup><a href="h7311.htm">7311</a></sup> upon a rock.<sup><a href="h6697.htm">6697</a></sup> </p>
 <p>6  And now<sup><a href="h6258.htm">6258</a></sup> shall mine head<sup><a href="h7218.htm">7218</a></sup> be lifted up<sup><a href="h7311.htm">7311</a></sup> above<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> mine enemies<sup><a href="h0341.htm">341</a></sup> round about<sup><a href="h5439.htm">5439</a></sup> me: therefore will I offer<sup><a href="h2076.htm">2076</a></sup> in his tabernacle<sup><a href="h0168.htm">168</a></sup> sacrifices<sup><a href="h2077.htm">2077</a></sup> of joy;<sup><a href="h8643.htm">8643</a></sup> I will sing,<sup><a href="h7891.htm">7891</a></sup> yea, I will sing praises<sup><a href="h2167.htm">2167</a></sup> unto the LORD.<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> </p>
 <p>7  Hear,<sup><a href="h8085.htm">8085</a></sup> O LORD,<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> when I cry<sup><a href="h7121.htm">7121</a></sup> with my voice:<sup><a href="h6963.htm">6963</a></sup> have mercy<sup><a href="h2603.htm">2603</a></sup> also upon me, and answer<sup><a href="h6030.htm">6030</a></sup> me. </p>
 <p>8  When thou saidst, Seek<sup><a href="h1245.htm">1245</a></sup> ye my face;<sup><a href="h6440.htm">6440</a></sup> my heart<sup><a href="h3820.htm">3820</a></sup> said<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> unto thee, <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> Thy face,<sup><a href="h6440.htm">6440</a></sup> LORD,<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> will I seek.<sup><a href="h1245.htm">1245</a></sup> </p>
 <p>9  Hide<sup><a href="h5641.htm">5641</a></sup> not<sup><a href="h0408.htm">408</a></sup> thy face<sup><a href="h6440.htm">6440</a></sup> far from<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> me; put not thy servant away<sup><a href="h5186.htm">5186</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0408.htm">408</a></sup> <sup><a href="h5650.htm">5650</a></sup> in anger:<sup><a href="h0639.htm">639</a></sup> thou hast been<sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> my help;<sup><a href="h5833.htm">5833</a></sup> leave<sup><a href="h5203.htm">5203</a></sup> me not,<sup><a href="h0408.htm">408</a></sup> neither<sup><a href="h0408.htm">408</a></sup> forsake<sup><a href="h5800.htm">5800</a></sup> me, O God<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> of my salvation.<sup><a href="h3468.htm">3468</a></sup> </p>
 <p>10  When<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> my father<sup><a href="h0001.htm">1</a></sup> and my mother<sup><a href="h0517.htm">517</a></sup> forsake<sup><a href="h5800.htm">5800</a></sup> me, then the LORD<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> will take me up.<sup><a href="h0622.htm">622</a></sup> </p>
 <p>11  Teach<sup><a href="h3384.htm">3384</a></sup> me thy way,<sup><a href="h1870.htm">1870</a></sup> O LORD,<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> and lead<sup><a href="h5148.htm">5148</a></sup> me in a plain<sup><a href="h4334.htm">4334</a></sup> path,<sup><a href="h0734.htm">734</a></sup> because of<sup><a href="h4616.htm">4616</a></sup> mine enemies.<sup><a href="h8324.htm">8324</a></sup> </p>
 <p>12  Deliver<sup><a href="h5414.htm">5414</a></sup> me not<sup><a href="h0408.htm">408</a></sup> over unto the will<sup><a href="h5315.htm">5315</a></sup> of mine enemies:<sup><a href="h6862.htm">6862</a></sup> for<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> false<sup><a href="h8267.htm">8267</a></sup> witnesses<sup><a href="h5707.htm">5707</a></sup> are risen up<sup><a href="h6965.htm">6965</a></sup> against me, and such as breathe out<sup><a href="h3307.htm">3307</a></sup> cruelty.<sup><a href="h2555.htm">2555</a></sup> </p>
 <p>13  I had fainted, unless<sup><a href="h3884.htm">3884</a></sup> I had believed<sup><a href="h0539.htm">539</a></sup> to see<sup><a href="h7200.htm">7200</a></sup> the goodness<sup><a href="h2898.htm">2898</a></sup> of the LORD<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> in the land<sup><a href="h0776.htm">776</a></sup> of the living.<sup><a href="h2416.htm">2416</a></sup> </p>
 <p>14  Wait<sup><a href="h6960.htm">6960</a></sup> on<sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> the LORD:<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> be of good courage,<sup><a href="h2388.htm">2388</a></sup> and he shall strengthen<sup><a href="h0553.htm">553</a></sup> thine heart:<sup><a href="h3820.htm">3820</a></sup> wait,<sup><a href="h6960.htm">6960</a></sup> I say, on<sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> the LORD.<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> </p>
