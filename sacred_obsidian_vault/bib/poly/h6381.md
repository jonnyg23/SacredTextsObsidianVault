  <p>H6381<br>  פּלא  <br> פָּלָא  ‎  pâlâ‘  <br><i>paw-law‘ </i><br>A primitive root; properly perhaps to <i>separate</i>, that is, <i>distinguish</i> (literally or figuratively); by implication to <i>be</i> (causatively <i>make</i>) <i>great</i>, <i>difficult</i>, <i>wonderful: - </i>accomplish, (arise . . . too, be too) hard, hidden, things too high, (be, do, do a, shew) marvelous (-ly, -els, things, work), miracles, perform, separate, make singular, (be, great, make) wonderful (-ers, -ly, things, works), wondrous (things, works, -ly).<br></p>
