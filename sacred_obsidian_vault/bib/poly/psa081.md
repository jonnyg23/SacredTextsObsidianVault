 <h3 align="CENTER">Psalms 81</h3><table border="1">
 <tr>
 <td valign="top">
 <p align="center"><a href="../kjv/psa081.htm">KJV</a></p><p>1  To the chief Musician<sup><a href="h5329.htm">5329</a></sup> upon<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> Gittith,<sup><a href="h1665.htm">1665</a></sup> A Psalm of Asaph.<sup><a href="h0623.htm">623</a></sup> Sing aloud<sup><a href="h7442.htm">7442</a></sup> unto God<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> our strength:<sup><a href="h5797.htm">5797</a></sup> make a joyful noise<sup><a href="h7321.htm">7321</a></sup> unto the God<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> of Jacob.<sup><a href="h3290.htm">3290</a></sup> </p>
 <p>2  Take<sup><a href="h5375.htm">5375</a></sup> a psalm,<sup><a href="h2172.htm">2172</a></sup> and bring<sup><a href="h5414.htm">5414</a></sup> hither the timbrel,<sup><a href="h8596.htm">8596</a></sup> the pleasant<sup><a href="h5273.htm">5273</a></sup> harp<sup><a href="h3658.htm">3658</a></sup> with<sup><a href="h5973.htm">5973</a></sup> the psaltery.<sup><a href="h5035.htm">5035</a></sup> </p>
 <p>3  Blow up<sup><a href="h8628.htm">8628</a></sup> the trumpet<sup><a href="h7782.htm">7782</a></sup> in the new moon,<sup><a href="h2320.htm">2320</a></sup> in the time appointed,<sup><a href="h3677.htm">3677</a></sup> on our solemn feast<sup><a href="h2282.htm">2282</a></sup> day.<sup><a href="h3117.htm">3117</a></sup> </p>
 <p>4  For<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> this<sup><a href="h1931.htm">1931</a></sup> was a statute<sup><a href="h2706.htm">2706</a></sup> for Israel,<sup><a href="h3478.htm">3478</a></sup> and a law<sup><a href="h4941.htm">4941</a></sup> of the God<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> of Jacob.<sup><a href="h3290.htm">3290</a></sup> </p>
 <p>5  This he ordained<sup><a href="h7760.htm">7760</a></sup> in Joseph<sup><a href="h3084.htm">3084</a></sup> for a testimony,<sup><a href="h5715.htm">5715</a></sup> when he went out<sup><a href="h3318.htm">3318</a></sup> through<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> the land<sup><a href="h0776.htm">776</a></sup> of Egypt:<sup><a href="h4714.htm">4714</a></sup> where I heard<sup><a href="h8085.htm">8085</a></sup> a language<sup><a href="h8193.htm">8193</a></sup> that I understood<sup><a href="h3045.htm">3045</a></sup> not.<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> </p>
 <p>6  I removed<sup><a href="h5493.htm">5493</a></sup> his shoulder<sup><a href="h7926.htm">7926</a></sup> from the burden:<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h5447.htm">5447</a></sup> his hands<sup><a href="h3709.htm">3709</a></sup> were delivered<sup><a href="h5674.htm">5674</a></sup> from the pots.<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h1731.htm">1731</a></sup> </p>
 <p>7  Thou calledst<sup><a href="h7121.htm">7121</a></sup> in trouble,<sup><a href="h6869.htm">6869</a></sup> and I delivered<sup><a href="h2502.htm">2502</a></sup> thee; I answered<sup><a href="h6030.htm">6030</a></sup> thee in the secret place<sup><a href="h5643.htm">5643</a></sup> of thunder:<sup><a href="h7482.htm">7482</a></sup> I proved<sup><a href="h0974.htm">974</a></sup> thee at<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> the waters<sup><a href="h4325.htm">4325</a></sup> of Meribah.<sup><a href="h4809.htm">4809</a></sup> Selah.<sup><a href="h5542.htm">5542</a></sup> </p>
 <p>8  Hear,<sup><a href="h8085.htm">8085</a></sup> O my people,<sup><a href="h5971.htm">5971</a></sup> and I will testify<sup><a href="h5749.htm">5749</a></sup> unto thee: O Israel,<sup><a href="h3478.htm">3478</a></sup> if<sup><a href="h0518.htm">518</a></sup> thou wilt hearken<sup><a href="h8085.htm">8085</a></sup> unto me; </p>
 <p>9  There shall no<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> strange<sup><a href="h2114.htm">2114</a></sup> god<sup><a href="h0410.htm">410</a></sup> be<sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> in thee; neither<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> shalt thou worship<sup><a href="h7812.htm">7812</a></sup> any strange<sup><a href="h5236.htm">5236</a></sup> god.<sup><a href="h0410.htm">410</a></sup> </p>
 <p>10  I<sup><a href="h0595.htm">595</a></sup> am the LORD<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> thy God,<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> which brought<sup><a href="h5927.htm">5927</a></sup> thee out of the land<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0776.htm">776</a></sup> of Egypt:<sup><a href="h4714.htm">4714</a></sup> open thy mouth<sup><a href="h6310.htm">6310</a></sup> wide,<sup><a href="h7337.htm">7337</a></sup> and I will fill<sup><a href="h4390.htm">4390</a></sup> it. </p>
 <p>11  But my people<sup><a href="h5971.htm">5971</a></sup> would not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> hearken<sup><a href="h8085.htm">8085</a></sup> to my voice;<sup><a href="h6963.htm">6963</a></sup> and Israel<sup><a href="h3478.htm">3478</a></sup> would<sup><a href="h0014.htm">14</a></sup> none<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> of me. </p>
 <p>12  So I gave them up<sup><a href="h7971.htm">7971</a></sup> unto their own hearts'<sup><a href="h3820.htm">3820</a></sup> lust:<sup><a href="h8307.htm">8307</a></sup> and they walked<sup><a href="h1980.htm">1980</a></sup> in their own counsels.<sup><a href="h4156.htm">4156</a></sup> </p>
 <p>13  Oh that<sup><a href="h3863.htm">3863</a></sup> my people<sup><a href="h5971.htm">5971</a></sup> had hearkened<sup><a href="h8085.htm">8085</a></sup> unto me, and Israel<sup><a href="h3478.htm">3478</a></sup> had walked<sup><a href="h1980.htm">1980</a></sup> in my ways!<sup><a href="h1870.htm">1870</a></sup> </p>
 <p>14  I should soon<sup><a href="h4592.htm">4592</a></sup> have subdued<sup><a href="h3665.htm">3665</a></sup> their enemies,<sup><a href="h0341.htm">341</a></sup> and turned<sup><a href="h7725.htm">7725</a></sup> my hand<sup><a href="h3027.htm">3027</a></sup> against<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> their adversaries.<sup><a href="h6862.htm">6862</a></sup> </p>
 <p>15  The haters<sup><a href="h8130.htm">8130</a></sup> of the LORD<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> should have submitted<sup><a href="h3584.htm">3584</a></sup> themselves unto him: but their time<sup><a href="h6256.htm">6256</a></sup> should have endured<sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> forever.<sup><a href="h5769.htm">5769</a></sup> </p>
 <p>16  He should have fed<sup><a href="h0398.htm">398</a></sup> them also with the finest<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h2459.htm">2459</a></sup> of the wheat:<sup><a href="h2406.htm">2406</a></sup> and with honey<sup><a href="h1706.htm">1706</a></sup> out of the rock<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h6697.htm">6697</a></sup> should I have satisfied<sup><a href="h7646.htm">7646</a></sup> thee. </p>
