 <p>2  Lift up thyself,<sup><a href="h5375.htm">5375</a></sup> thou judge<sup><a href="h8199.htm">8199</a></sup> of the earth:<sup><a href="h0776.htm">776</a></sup> render<sup><a href="h7725.htm">7725</a></sup> a reward<sup><a href="h1576.htm">1576</a></sup> to<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> the proud.<sup><a href="h1343.htm">1343</a></sup> </p>

 <p>13  That thou mayest give him rest<sup><a href="h8252.htm">8252</a></sup> from the days<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3117.htm">3117</a></sup> of adversity,<sup><a href="h7451.htm">7451</a></sup> until<sup><a href="h5704.htm">5704</a></sup> the pit<sup><a href="h7845.htm">7845</a></sup> be digged<sup><a href="h3738.htm">3738</a></sup> for the wicked.<sup><a href="h7563.htm">7563</a></sup> </p>
 <p>14  For<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> the LORD<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> will not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> cast off<sup><a href="h5203.htm">5203</a></sup> his people,<sup><a href="h5971.htm">5971</a></sup> neither<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> will he forsake<sup><a href="h5800.htm">5800</a></sup> his inheritance.<sup><a href="h5159.htm">5159</a></sup> </p>
 <p>15  But<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> judgment<sup><a href="h4941.htm">4941</a></sup> shall return<sup><a href="h7725.htm">7725</a></sup> unto<sup><a href="h5704.htm">5704</a></sup> righteousness:<sup><a href="h6664.htm">6664</a></sup> and all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> the upright<sup><a href="h3477.htm">3477</a></sup> in heart<sup><a href="h3820.htm">3820</a></sup> shall follow<sup><a href="h0310.htm">310</a></sup> it. </p>

 <p>23  And he shall bring<sup><a href="h7725.htm">7725</a></sup> upon<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> them <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> their own iniquity,<sup><a href="h0205.htm">205</a></sup> and shall cut them off<sup><a href="h6789.htm">6789</a></sup> in their own wickedness;<sup><a href="h7451.htm">7451</a></sup> yea, the LORD<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> our God<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> shall cut them off.<sup><a href="h6789.htm">6789</a></sup> </p>
