 <p align="center"><a href="../kjv/psa076.htm">KJV</a></p><p>1  To the chief Musician<sup><a href="h5329.htm">5329</a></sup> on Neginoth,<sup><a href="h5058.htm">5058</a></sup> A Psalm<sup><a href="h4210.htm">4210</a></sup> or Song<sup><a href="h7892.htm">7892</a></sup> of Asaph.<sup><a href="h0623.htm">623</a></sup> In Judah<sup><a href="h3063.htm">3063</a></sup> is God<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> known:<sup><a href="h3045.htm">3045</a></sup> his name<sup><a href="h8034.htm">8034</a></sup> is great<sup><a href="h1419.htm">1419</a></sup> in Israel.<sup><a href="h3478.htm">3478</a></sup> </p>
 <p>2  In Salem<sup><a href="h8004.htm">8004</a></sup> also is<sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> his tabernacle,<sup><a href="h5520.htm">5520</a></sup> and his dwelling place<sup><a href="h4585.htm">4585</a></sup> in Zion.<sup><a href="h6726.htm">6726</a></sup> </p>
 <p>3  There<sup><a href="h8033.htm">8033</a></sup> broke<sup><a href="h7665.htm">7665</a></sup> he the arrows<sup><a href="h7565.htm">7565</a></sup> of the bow,<sup><a href="h7198.htm">7198</a></sup> the shield,<sup><a href="h4043.htm">4043</a></sup> and the sword,<sup><a href="h2719.htm">2719</a></sup> and the battle.<sup><a href="h4421.htm">4421</a></sup> Selah.<sup><a href="h5542.htm">5542</a></sup> </p>
 <p>4  Thou<sup><a href="h0859.htm">859</a></sup> art more glorious<sup><a href="h0215.htm">215</a></sup> and excellent<sup><a href="h0117.htm">117</a></sup> than the mountains<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h2042.htm">2042</a></sup> of prey.<sup><a href="h2964.htm">2964</a></sup> </p>
 <p>5  The stouthearted<sup><a href="h0047.htm">47</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3820.htm">3820</a></sup> are spoiled,<sup><a href="h7997.htm">7997</a></sup> they have slept<sup><a href="h5123.htm">5123</a></sup> their sleep:<sup><a href="h8142.htm">8142</a></sup> and none<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> of the men<sup><a href="h0376.htm">376</a></sup> of might<sup><a href="h2428.htm">2428</a></sup> have found<sup><a href="h4672.htm">4672</a></sup> their hands.<sup><a href="h3027.htm">3027</a></sup> </p>
 <p>6  At thy rebuke,<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h1606.htm">1606</a></sup> O God<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> of Jacob,<sup><a href="h3290.htm">3290</a></sup> both the chariot<sup><a href="h7393.htm">7393</a></sup> and horse<sup><a href="h5483.htm">5483</a></sup> are cast into a dead sleep.<sup><a href="h7290.htm">7290</a></sup> </p>
 <p>7  Thou,<sup><a href="h0859.htm">859</a></sup> even thou,<sup><a href="h0859.htm">859</a></sup> art to be feared:<sup><a href="h3372.htm">3372</a></sup> and who<sup><a href="h4310.htm">4310</a></sup> may stand<sup><a href="h5975.htm">5975</a></sup> in thy sight<sup><a href="h6440.htm">6440</a></sup> when once<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0227.htm">227</a></sup> thou art angry?<sup><a href="h0639.htm">639</a></sup> </p>
 <p>8  Thou didst cause judgment<sup><a href="h1779.htm">1779</a></sup> to be heard<sup><a href="h8085.htm">8085</a></sup> from<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> heaven;<sup><a href="h8064.htm">8064</a></sup> the earth<sup><a href="h0776.htm">776</a></sup> feared,<sup><a href="h3372.htm">3372</a></sup> and was still,<sup><a href="h8252.htm">8252</a></sup> </p>
 <p>9  When God<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> arose<sup><a href="h6965.htm">6965</a></sup> to judgment,<sup><a href="h4941.htm">4941</a></sup> to save<sup><a href="h3467.htm">3467</a></sup> all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> the meek<sup><a href="h6035.htm">6035</a></sup> of the earth.<sup><a href="h0776.htm">776</a></sup> Selah.<sup><a href="h5542.htm">5542</a></sup> </p>
 <p>10  Surely<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> the wrath<sup><a href="h2534.htm">2534</a></sup> of man<sup><a href="h0120.htm">120</a></sup> shall praise<sup><a href="h3034.htm">3034</a></sup> thee: the remainder<sup><a href="h7611.htm">7611</a></sup> of wrath<sup><a href="h2534.htm">2534</a></sup> shalt thou restrain.<sup><a href="h2296.htm">2296</a></sup> </p>
 <p>11  Vow,<sup><a href="h5087.htm">5087</a></sup> and pay<sup><a href="h7999.htm">7999</a></sup> unto the LORD<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> your God:<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> let all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> that be round about<sup><a href="h5439.htm">5439</a></sup> him bring<sup><a href="h2986.htm">2986</a></sup> presents<sup><a href="h7862.htm">7862</a></sup> unto him that ought to be feared.<sup><a href="h4172.htm">4172</a></sup> </p>
 <p>12  He shall cut off<sup><a href="h1219.htm">1219</a></sup> the spirit<sup><a href="h7307.htm">7307</a></sup> of princes:<sup><a href="h5057.htm">5057</a></sup> he is terrible<sup><a href="h3372.htm">3372</a></sup> to the kings<sup><a href="h4428.htm">4428</a></sup> of the earth.<sup><a href="h0776.htm">776</a></sup> </p>

 <p align="center"><a href="../sep/psa076.htm">Sep</a></p><p>1 εἰς τὸ τέλος ὑπὲρ Ιδιθουν τῷ Ασαφ ψαλμός </p>
 <p>2 φωνῇ μου πρὸς κύριον ἐκέκραξα φωνῇ μου πρὸς τὸν θεόν καὶ προσέσχεν μοι </p>
 <p>3 ἐν ἡμέρᾳ θλίψεώς μου τὸν θεὸν ἐξεζήτησα ταῖς χερσίν μου νυκτὸς ἐναντίον αὐτοῦ καὶ οὐκ ἠπατήθην ἀπηνήνατο παρακληθῆναι ἡ ψυχή μου </p>
 <p>4 ἐμνήσθην τοῦ θεοῦ καὶ εὐφράνθην ἠδολέσχησα καὶ ὠλιγοψύχησεν τὸ πνεῦμά μου διάψαλμα </p>
 <p>5 προκατελάβοντο φυλακὰς οἱ ὀφθαλμοί μου ἐταράχθην καὶ οὐκ ἐλάλησα </p>
 <p>6 διελογισάμην ἡμέρας ἀρχαίας καὶ ἔτη αἰώνια ἐμνήσθην καὶ ἐμελέτησα </p>
 <p>7 νυκτὸς μετὰ τῆς καρδίας μου ἠδολέσχουν καὶ ἔσκαλλεν τὸ πνεῦμά μου </p>
 <p>8 μὴ εἰς τοὺς αἰῶνας ἀπώσεται κύριος καὶ οὐ προσθήσει τοῦ εὐδοκῆσαι ἔτι </p>
 <p>9 ἢ εἰς τέλος τὸ ἔλεος αὐτοῦ ἀποκόψει ἀπὸ γενεᾶς εἰς γενεάν </p>
 <p>10 ἢ ἐπιλήσεται τοῦ οἰκτιρῆσαι ὁ θεὸς ἢ συνέξει ἐν τῇ ὀργῇ αὐτοῦ τοὺς οἰκτιρμοὺς αὐτοῦ διάψαλμα </p>
 <p>11 καὶ εἶπα νῦν ἠρξάμην αὕτη ἡ ἀλλοίωσις τῆς δεξιᾶς τοῦ ὑψίστου </p>
 <p>12 ἐμνήσθην τῶν ἔργων κυρίου ὅτι μνησθήσομαι ἀπὸ τῆς ἀρχῆς τῶν θαυμασίων σου </p>
 <p>13 καὶ μελετήσω ἐν πᾶσιν τοῖς ἔργοις σου καὶ ἐν τοῖς ἐπιτηδεύμασίν σου ἀδολεσχήσω </p>
 <p>14 ὁ θεός ἐν τῷ ἁγίῳ ἡ ὁδός σου τίς θεὸς μέγας ὡς ὁ θεὸς ἡμῶν </p>
 <p>15 σὺ εἶ ὁ θεὸς ὁ ποιῶν θαυμάσια ἐγνώρισας ἐν τοῖς λαοῖς τὴν δύναμίν σου </p>
 <p>16 ἐλυτρώσω ἐν τῷ βραχίονί σου τὸν λαόν σου τοὺς υἱοὺς Ιακωβ καὶ Ιωσηφ διάψαλμα </p>
 <p>17 εἴδοσάν σε ὕδατα ὁ θεός εἴδοσάν σε ὕδατα καὶ ἐφοβήθησαν καὶ ἐταράχθησαν ἄβυσσοι πλῆθος ἤχους ὑδάτων </p>
 <p>18 φωνὴν ἔδωκαν αἱ νεφέλαι καὶ γὰρ τὰ βέλη σου διαπορεύονται </p>
 <p>19 φωνὴ τῆς βροντῆς σου ἐν τῷ τροχῷ ἔφαναν αἱ ἀστραπαί σου τῇ οἰκουμένῃ ἐσαλεύθη καὶ ἔντρομος ἐγενήθη ἡ γῆ </p>
 <p>20 ἐν τῇ θαλάσσῃ ἡ ὁδός σου καὶ αἱ τρίβοι σου ἐν ὕδασι πολλοῖς καὶ τὰ ἴχνη σου οὐ γνωσθήσονται </p>
 <p>21 ὡδήγησας ὡς πρόβατα τὸν λαόν σου ἐν χειρὶ Μωυσῆ καὶ Ααρων </p>

 <p align="center"><a href="../vul/psa076.htm">Vul</a></p><p>1 In finem, pro Idithun. Psalmus Asaph.</p>
 <p>2 [Voce mea ad Dominum clamavi;<br> voce mea ad Deum, et intendit mihi.<br></p>
 <p>3 In die tribulationis meæ Deum exquisivi;<br> manibus meis nocte contra eum, et non sum deceptus.<br> Renuit consolari anima mea;<br></p>
 <p>4 memor fui Dei, et delectatus sum,<br> et exercitatus sum, et defecit spiritus meus.<br></p>
 <p>5 Anticipaverunt vigilias oculi mei;<br> turbatus sum, et non sum locutus.<br></p>
 <p>6 Cogitavi dies antiquos,<br> et annos æternos in mente habui.<br></p>
 <p>7 Et meditatus sum nocte cum corde meo,<br> et exercitabar, et scopebam spiritum meum.<br></p>
 <p>8 Numquid in æternum projiciet Deus?<br> aut non apponet ut complacitior sit adhuc?<br></p>
 <p>9 aut in finem misericordiam suam abscindet,<br> a generatione in generationem?<br></p>
 <p>10 aut obliviscetur misereri Deus?<br> aut continebit in ira sua misericordias suas?<br></p>
 <p>11 Et dixi: Nunc cpi;<br> hæc mutatio dexteræ Excelsi.<br></p>
 <p>12 Memor fui operum Domini,<br> quia memor ero ab initio mirabilium tuorum:<br></p>
 <p>13 et meditabor in omnibus operibus tuis,<br> et in adinventionibus tuis exercebor.<br></p>
 <p>14 Deus, in sancto via tua:<br> quis deus magnus sicut Deus noster?<br></p>
 <p>15 Tu es Deus qui facis mirabilia:<br> notam fecisti in populis virtutem tuam.<br></p>
 <p>16 Redemisti in brachio tuo populum tuum,<br> filios Jacob et Joseph.<br></p>
 <p>17 Viderunt te aquæ, Deus;<br> viderunt te aquæ, et timuerunt:<br> et turbatæ sunt abyssi.<br></p>
 <p>18 Multitudo sonitus aquarum;<br> vocem dederunt nubes.<br> Etenim sagittæ tuæ transeunt;<br></p>
 <p>19 vox tonitrui tui in rota.<br> Illuxerunt coruscationes tuæ orbi terræ;<br> commota est, et contremuit terra.<br></p>
 <p>20 In mari via tua, et semitæ tuæ in aquis multis,<br> et vestigia tua non cognoscentur.<br></p>
 <p>21 Deduxisti sicut oves populum tuum,<br> in manu Moysi et Aaron.]</p>
