  <p>H1481<br>  גּוּר  <br> גּוּר  ‎  gûr  <br><i>goor </i><br>A primitive root; properly to <i>turn</i> aside from the road (for a lodging or any other purpose), that is, <i>sojourn</i> (as a guest); also to <i>shrink</i>, <i>fear</i> (as in a <i>strange</i> place); also to <i>gather</i> for hostility (as <i>afraid</i>): - abide, assemble, be afraid, dwell, fear, gather (together), inhabitant, remain, sojourn, stand in awe, (be) stranger, X surely.<br></p>
