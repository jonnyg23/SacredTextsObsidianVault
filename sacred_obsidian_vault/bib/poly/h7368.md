  <p>H7368<br>  רחק  <br> רָחַק  ‎  râchaq  <br><i>raw-khak‘ </i><br>A primitive root; to <i>widen</i> (in any direction), that is, (intransitively) <i>recede</i> or (transitively) <i>remove</i> (literally or figuratively, of place or relation): - (a, be, cast, drive, get, go, keep [self], put, remove, be too, [wander], withdraw) far (away, off), loose, X refrain, very, (be) a good way (off).<br></p>
