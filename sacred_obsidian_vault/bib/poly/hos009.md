 <p>5  What<sup><a href="h4100.htm">4100</a></sup> will ye do<sup><a href="h6213.htm">6213</a></sup> in the solemn<sup><a href="h4150.htm">4150</a></sup> day,<sup><a href="h3117.htm">3117</a></sup> and in the day<sup><a href="h3117.htm">3117</a></sup> of the feast<sup><a href="h2282.htm">2282</a></sup> of the LORD?<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> </p>
 <p>6  For,<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> lo,<sup><a href="h2009.htm">2009</a></sup> they are gone<sup><a href="h1980.htm">1980</a></sup> because of destruction:<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h7701.htm">7701</a></sup> Egypt<sup><a href="h4714.htm">4714</a></sup> shall gather them up,<sup><a href="h6908.htm">6908</a></sup> Memphis<sup><a href="h4644.htm">4644</a></sup> shall bury<sup><a href="h6912.htm">6912</a></sup> them: the pleasant<sup><a href="h4261.htm">4261</a></sup> places for their silver,<sup><a href="h3701.htm">3701</a></sup> nettles<sup><a href="h7057.htm">7057</a></sup> shall possess<sup><a href="h3423.htm">3423</a></sup> them: thorns<sup><a href="h2336.htm">2336</a></sup> shall be in their tabernacles.<sup><a href="h0168.htm">168</a></sup> </p>

 <p>8  The watchman<sup><a href="h6822.htm">6822</a></sup> of Ephraim<sup><a href="h0669.htm">669</a></sup> was with<sup><a href="h5973.htm">5973</a></sup> my God:<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> but the prophet<sup><a href="h5030.htm">5030</a></sup> is a snare<sup><a href="h6341.htm">6341</a></sup> of a fowler<sup><a href="h3352.htm">3352</a></sup> in<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> his ways,<sup><a href="h1870.htm">1870</a></sup> and hatred<sup><a href="h4895.htm">4895</a></sup> in the house<sup><a href="h1004.htm">1004</a></sup> of his God.<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> </p>
 <p>9  They have deeply<sup><a href="h6009.htm">6009</a></sup> corrupted<sup><a href="h7843.htm">7843</a></sup> themselves, as in the days<sup><a href="h3117.htm">3117</a></sup> of Gibeah:<sup><a href="h1390.htm">1390</a></sup> therefore he will remember<sup><a href="h2142.htm">2142</a></sup> their iniquity,<sup><a href="h5771.htm">5771</a></sup> he will visit<sup><a href="h6485.htm">6485</a></sup> their sins.<sup><a href="h2403.htm">2403</a></sup> </p>

 <p>12  Though<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0518.htm">518</a></sup> they bring up<sup><a href="h1431.htm">1431</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> their children,<sup><a href="h1121.htm">1121</a></sup> yet will I bereave<sup><a href="h7921.htm">7921</a></sup> them, that there shall not be a man<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0120.htm">120</a></sup> left: yea,<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> woe<sup><a href="h0188.htm">188</a></sup> also<sup><a href="h1571.htm">1571</a></sup> to them when I depart<sup><a href="h5493.htm">5493</a></sup> from<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> them! </p>
 <p>13  Ephraim,<sup><a href="h0669.htm">669</a></sup> as<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> I saw<sup><a href="h7200.htm">7200</a></sup> Tyrus,<sup><a href="h6865.htm">6865</a></sup> is planted<sup><a href="h8362.htm">8362</a></sup> in a pleasant place:<sup><a href="h5116.htm">5116</a></sup> but Ephraim<sup><a href="h0669.htm">669</a></sup> shall bring forth<sup><a href="h3318.htm">3318</a></sup> his children<sup><a href="h1121.htm">1121</a></sup> to<sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> the murderer.<sup><a href="h2026.htm">2026</a></sup> </p>

 <p>16  Ephraim<sup><a href="h0669.htm">669</a></sup> is smitten,<sup><a href="h5221.htm">5221</a></sup> their root<sup><a href="h8328.htm">8328</a></sup> is dried up,<sup><a href="h3001.htm">3001</a></sup> they shall bear<sup><a href="h6213.htm">6213</a></sup> no<sup><a href="h1077.htm">1077</a></sup> fruit:<sup><a href="h6529.htm">6529</a></sup> yea,<sup><a href="h1571.htm">1571</a></sup> though<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> they bring forth,<sup><a href="h3205.htm">3205</a></sup> yet will I slay<sup><a href="h4191.htm">4191</a></sup> even the beloved<sup><a href="h4261.htm">4261</a></sup> fruit of their womb.<sup><a href="h0990.htm">990</a></sup> </p>

 <p>8 σκοπὸς Εφραιμ μετὰ θεοῦ προφήτης παγὶς σκολιὰ ἐπὶ πάσας τὰς ὁδοὺς αὐτοῦ μανίαν ἐν οἴκῳ κυρίου κατέπηξαν </p>
 <p>9 ἐφθάρησαν κατὰ τὰς ἡμέρας τοῦ βουνοῦ μνησθήσεται ἀδικίας αὐτοῦ ἐκδικήσει ἁμαρτίας αὐτοῦ </p>
 <p>10 ὡς σταφυλὴν ἐν ἐρήμῳ εὗρον τὸν Ισραηλ καὶ ὡς σκοπὸν ἐν συκῇ πρόιμον εἶδον πατέρας αὐτῶν αὐτοὶ εἰσῆλθον πρὸς τὸν Βεελφεγωρ καὶ ἀπηλλοτριώθησαν εἰς αἰσχύνην καὶ ἐγένοντο οἱ ἠγαπημένοι ὡς οἱ ἐβδελυγμένοι </p>
 <p>11 Εφραιμ ὡς ὄρνεον ἐξεπετάσθη αἱ δόξαι αὐτῶν ἐκ τόκων καὶ ὠδίνων καὶ συλλήμψεων </p>
 <p>12 διότι καὶ ἐὰν ἐκθρέψωσιν τὰ τέκνα αὐτῶν ἀτεκνωθήσονται ἐξ ἀνθρώπων διότι καὶ οὐαὶ αὐτοῖς ἐστιν σάρξ μου ἐξ αὐτῶν </p>
 <p>13 Εφραιμ ὃν τρόπον εἶδον εἰς θήραν παρέστησαν τὰ τέκνα αὐτῶν καὶ Εφραιμ τοῦ ἐξαγαγεῖν εἰς ἀποκέντησιν τὰ τέκνα αὐτοῦ </p>
 <p>14 δὸς αὐτοῖς κύριε τί δώσεις αὐτοῖς δὸς αὐτοῖς μήτραν ἀτεκνοῦσαν καὶ μαστοὺς ξηρούς </p>
 <p>15 πᾶσαι αἱ κακίαι αὐτῶν εἰς Γαλγαλ ὅτι ἐκεῖ αὐτοὺς ἐμίσησα διὰ τὰς κακίας τῶν ἐπιτηδευμάτων αὐτῶν ἐκ τοῦ οἴκου μου ἐκβαλῶ αὐτούς οὐ μὴ προσθήσω τοῦ ἀγαπῆσαι αὐτούς πάντες οἱ ἄρχοντες αὐτῶν ἀπειθοῦντες </p>
 <p>16 ἐπόνεσεν Εφραιμ τὰς ῥίζας αὐτοῦ ἐξηράνθη καρπὸν οὐκέτι μὴ ἐνέγκῃ διότι καὶ ἐὰν γεννήσωσιν ἀποκτενῶ τὰ ἐπιθυμήματα κοιλίας αὐτῶν </p>
 <p>17 ἀπώσεται αὐτοὺς ὁ θεός ὅτι οὐκ εἰσήκουσαν αὐτοῦ καὶ ἔσονται πλανῆται ἐν τοῖς ἔθνεσιν </p>

 <p align="center"><a href="../vul/hos009.htm">Vul</a></p><p>1 [Noli lætari, Israël;<br> noli exsultare sicut populi:<br> quia fornicatus es a Deo tuo;<br> dilexisti mercedem super omnes areas tritici.<br></p>
 <p>2 Area et torcular non pascet eos,<br> et vinum mentietur eis:<br></p>
 <p>3 non habitabunt in terra Domini.<br> Reversus est Ephraim in Ægyptum,<br> et in Assyriis pollutum comedit.<br></p>
 <p>4 Non libabunt Domino vinum,<br> et non placebunt ei.<br> Sacrificia eorum quasi panis lugentium;<br> omnes qui comedent eum, contaminabuntur:<br> quia panis eorum animæ ipsorum:<br> non intrabit in domum Domini.<br></p>
 <p>5 Quid facietis in die solemni,<br> in die festivitatis Domini?<br></p>
 <p>6 Ecce enim profecti sunt a vastitate:<br> Ægyptus congregabit eos;<br> Memphis sepeliet eos:<br> desiderabile argentum eorum urtica hæreditabit,<br> lappa in tabernaculis eorum.<br></p>
 <p>7 Venerunt dies visitationis,<br> venerunt dies retributionis.<br> Scitote, Israël,<br> stultum prophetam,<br> insanum virum spiritualem,<br> propter multitudinem iniquitatis tuæ,<br> et multitudinem amentiæ.<br></p>
 <p>8 Speculator Ephraim cum Deo meo,<br> propheta laqueus ruinæ factus est super omnes vias ejus;<br> insania in domo Dei ejus.<br></p>
 <p>9 Profunde peccaverunt, sicut in diebus Gabaa.<br> Recordabitur iniquitatis eorum,<br> et visitabit peccata eorum.<br></p>
 <p>10 Quasi uvas in deserto inveni Israël,<br> quasi prima poma ficulneæ in cacumine ejus vidi patres eorum:<br> ipsi autem intraverunt ad Beelphegor,<br> et abalienati sunt in confusionem,<br> et facti sunt abominabiles sicut ea quæ dilexerunt.<br></p>
 <p>11 Ephraim quasi avis avolavit;<br> gloria eorum a partu, et ab utero, et a conceptu.<br></p>
 <p>12 Quod etsi enutrierint filios suos,<br> absque liberis eos faciam in hominibus;<br> sed et væ eis cum recessero ab eis!<br></p>
 <p>13 Ephraim, ut vidi, Tyrus erat fundata in pulchritudine;<br> et Ephraim educet ad interfectorem filios suos.<br></p>
 <p>14 Da eis, Domine. Quid dabis eis?<br> da eis vulvam sine liberis, et ubera arentia.<br></p>
 <p>15 Omnes nequitiæ eorum in Galgal,<br> quia ibi exosos habui eos.<br> Propter malitiam adinventionum eorum,<br> de domo mea ejiciam eos;<br> non addam ut diligam eos:<br> omnes principes eorum recedentes.<br></p>
 <p>16 Percussus est Ephraim;<br> radix eorum exsiccata est:<br> fructum nequaquam facient,<br> quod etsi genuerint,<br> interficiam amantissima uteri eorum.<br></p>
 <p>17 Abjiciet eos Deus meus, quia non audierunt eum,<br> et erunt vagi in nationibus.]</p>
