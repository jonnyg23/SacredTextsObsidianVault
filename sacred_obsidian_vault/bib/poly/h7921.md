  <p>H7921<br>  שׁכל  <br> שָׁכוֹל  ‎  shâkôl  <br><i>shaw-kole‘ </i><br>A primitive root; properly to <i>miscarry</i>, that is, <i>suffer</i> <i>abortion</i>; by analogy to <i>bereave</i> (literally or figuratively): - bereave (of children), barren, cast calf (fruit, young), be (make) childless, deprive, destroy, X expect, lose children, miscarry, rob of children, spoil.<br></p>
