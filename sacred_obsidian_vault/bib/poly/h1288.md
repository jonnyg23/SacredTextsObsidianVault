  <p>H1288<br>  בּרך  <br> בָּרַך  ‎  bârak  <br><i>baw-rak‘ </i><br>A primitive root; to <i>kneel</i>; by implication to <i>bless</i> God (as an act of adoration), and (vice-versa) man (as a benefit); also (by euphemism) to <i>curse</i> (God or the king, as treason): -  X abundantly, X altogether, X at all, blaspheme, bless, congratulate, curse, X greatly, X indeed, kneel (down), praise, salute, X still, thank.<br></p>
