  <p>H8104<br>  שׁמר  <br> שָׁמַר  ‎  shâmar  <br><i>shaw-mar‘ </i><br>A primitive root; properly to <i>hedge</i> about (as with thorns), that is, <i>guard</i>; generally to <i>protect</i>, <i>attend</i> <i>to</i>, etc.: - beware, be circumspect, take heed (to self), keep (-er, self), mark, look narrowly, observe, preserve, regard, reserve, save (self), sure, (that lay) wait (for), watch (-man).<br></p>
