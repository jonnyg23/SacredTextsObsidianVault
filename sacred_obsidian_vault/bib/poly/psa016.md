 <p align="center"><a href="../kjv/psa016.htm">KJV</a></p><p>1  Michtam<sup><a href="h4387.htm">4387</a></sup> of David.<sup><a href="h1732.htm">1732</a></sup> Preserve<sup><a href="h8104.htm">8104</a></sup> me, O God:<sup><a href="h0410.htm">410</a></sup> for<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> in thee do I put my trust.<sup><a href="h2620.htm">2620</a></sup> </p>
 <p>2  O my soul, thou hast said<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> unto the LORD,<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> Thou<sup><a href="h0859.htm">859</a></sup> art my Lord:<sup><a href="h0136.htm">136</a></sup> my goodness<sup><a href="h2896.htm">2896</a></sup> extendeth not<sup><a href="h1077.htm">1077</a></sup> to<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> thee; </p>
 <p>3  But to the saints<sup><a href="h6918.htm">6918</a></sup> that<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> are in the earth,<sup><a href="h0776.htm">776</a></sup> and to the excellent,<sup><a href="h0117.htm">117</a></sup> in whom is all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> my delight.<sup><a href="h2656.htm">2656</a></sup> </p>
 <p>4  Their sorrows<sup><a href="h6094.htm">6094</a></sup> shall be multiplied<sup><a href="h7235.htm">7235</a></sup> that hasten<sup><a href="h4116.htm">4116</a></sup> after another<sup><a href="h0312.htm">312</a></sup> god: their drink offerings<sup><a href="h5262.htm">5262</a></sup> of blood<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h1818.htm">1818</a></sup> will I not<sup><a href="h1077.htm">1077</a></sup> offer,<sup><a href="h5258.htm">5258</a></sup> nor<sup><a href="h1077.htm">1077</a></sup> take up<sup><a href="h5375.htm">5375</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> their names<sup><a href="h8034.htm">8034</a></sup> into<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> my lips.<sup><a href="h8193.htm">8193</a></sup> </p>
 <p>5  The LORD<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> is the portion<sup><a href="h4521.htm">4521</a></sup> of mine inheritance<sup><a href="h2506.htm">2506</a></sup> and of my cup:<sup><a href="h3563.htm">3563</a></sup> thou<sup><a href="h0859.htm">859</a></sup> maintainest<sup><a href="h8551.htm">8551</a></sup> my lot.<sup><a href="h1486.htm">1486</a></sup> </p>
 <p>6  The lines<sup><a href="h2256.htm">2256</a></sup> are fallen<sup><a href="h5307.htm">5307</a></sup> unto me in pleasant<sup><a href="h5273.htm">5273</a></sup> places; yea,<sup><a href="h0637.htm">637</a></sup> I have<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> a goodly<sup><a href="h8231.htm">8231</a></sup> heritage.<sup><a href="h5159.htm">5159</a></sup> </p>
 <p>7  I will bless<sup><a href="h1288.htm">1288</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> the LORD,<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> who<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> hath given me counsel:<sup><a href="h3289.htm">3289</a></sup> my reins<sup><a href="h3629.htm">3629</a></sup> also<sup><a href="h0637.htm">637</a></sup> instruct<sup><a href="h3256.htm">3256</a></sup> me in the night seasons.<sup><a href="h3915.htm">3915</a></sup> </p>
 <p>8  I have set<sup><a href="h7737.htm">7737</a></sup> the LORD<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> always<sup><a href="h8548.htm">8548</a></sup> before<sup><a href="h5048.htm">5048</a></sup> me: because<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> he is at my right hand,<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3225.htm">3225</a></sup> I shall not<sup><a href="h1077.htm">1077</a></sup> be moved.<sup><a href="h4131.htm">4131</a></sup> </p>
 <p>9  Therefore<sup><a href="h3651.htm">3651</a></sup> my heart<sup><a href="h3820.htm">3820</a></sup> is glad,<sup><a href="h8055.htm">8055</a></sup> and my glory<sup><a href="h3519.htm">3519</a></sup> rejoiceth:<sup><a href="h1523.htm">1523</a></sup> my flesh<sup><a href="h1320.htm">1320</a></sup> also<sup><a href="h0637.htm">637</a></sup> shall rest<sup><a href="h7931.htm">7931</a></sup> in hope.<sup><a href="h0983.htm">983</a></sup> </p>
 <p>10  For<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> thou wilt not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> leave<sup><a href="h5800.htm">5800</a></sup> my soul<sup><a href="h5315.htm">5315</a></sup> in hell;<sup><a href="h7585.htm">7585</a></sup> neither<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> wilt thou suffer<sup><a href="h5414.htm">5414</a></sup> thine Holy One<sup><a href="h2623.htm">2623</a></sup> to see<sup><a href="h7200.htm">7200</a></sup> corruption.<sup><a href="h7845.htm">7845</a></sup> </p>
 <p>11  Thou wilt show<sup><a href="h3045.htm">3045</a></sup> me the path<sup><a href="h0734.htm">734</a></sup> of life:<sup><a href="h2416.htm">2416</a></sup> in<sup><a href="h0854.htm">854</a></sup> thy presence<sup><a href="h6440.htm">6440</a></sup> is fullness<sup><a href="h7648.htm">7648</a></sup> of joy;<sup><a href="h8057.htm">8057</a></sup> at thy right hand<sup><a href="h3225.htm">3225</a></sup> there are pleasures<sup><a href="h5273.htm">5273</a></sup> forevermore.<sup><a href="h5331.htm">5331</a></sup> </p>

 <p align="center"><a href="../sep/psa016.htm">Sep</a></p><p>1 προσευχὴ τοῦ Δαυιδ εἰσάκουσον κύριε τῆς δικαιοσύνης μου πρόσχες τῇ δεήσει μου ἐνώτισαι τῆς προσευχῆς μου οὐκ ἐν χείλεσιν δολίοις </p>
 <p>2 ἐκ προσώπου σου τὸ κρίμα μου ἐξέλθοι οἱ ὀφθαλμοί μου ἰδέτωσαν εὐθύτητας </p>
 <p>3 ἐδοκίμασας τὴν καρδίαν μου ἐπεσκέψω νυκτός ἐπύρωσάς με καὶ οὐχ εὑρέθη ἐν ἐμοὶ ἀδικία </p>
 <p>4 ὅπως ἂν μὴ λαλήσῃ τὸ στόμα μου τὰ ἔργα τῶν ἀνθρώπων διὰ τοὺς λόγους τῶν χειλέων σου ἐγὼ ἐφύλαξα ὁδοὺς σκληράς </p>
 <p>5 κατάρτισαι τὰ διαβήματά μου ἐν ταῖς τρίβοις σου ἵνα μὴ σαλευθῶσιν τὰ διαβήματά μου </p>
 <p>6 ἐγὼ ἐκέκραξα ὅτι ἐπήκουσάς μου ὁ θεός κλῖνον τὸ οὖς σου ἐμοὶ καὶ εἰσάκουσον τῶν ῥημάτων μου </p>
 <p>7 θαυμάστωσον τὰ ἐλέη σου ὁ σῴζων τοὺς ἐλπίζοντας ἐπὶ σὲ ἐκ τῶν ἀνθεστηκότων τῇ δεξιᾷ σου </p>
 <p>8 φύλαξόν με ὡς κόραν ὀφθαλμοῦ ἐν σκέπῃ τῶν πτερύγων σου σκεπάσεις με </p>
 <p>9 ἀπὸ προσώπου ἀσεβῶν τῶν ταλαιπωρησάντων με οἱ ἐχθροί μου τὴν ψυχήν μου περιέσχον </p>
 <p>10 τὸ στέαρ αὐτῶν συνέκλεισαν τὸ στόμα αὐτῶν ἐλάλησεν ὑπερηφανίαν </p>
 <p>11 ἐκβάλλοντές με νυνὶ περιεκύκλωσάν με τοὺς ὀφθαλμοὺς αὐτῶν ἔθεντο ἐκκλῖναι ἐν τῇ γῇ </p>
 <p>12 ὑπέλαβόν με ὡσεὶ λέων ἕτοιμος εἰς θήραν καὶ ὡσεὶ σκύμνος οἰκῶν ἐν ἀποκρύφοις </p>
 <p>13 ἀνάστηθι κύριε πρόφθασον αὐτοὺς καὶ ὑποσκέλισον αὐτούς ῥῦσαι τὴν ψυχήν μου ἀπὸ ἀσεβοῦς ῥομφαίαν σου ἀπὸ ἐχθρῶν τῆς χειρός σου </p>
 <p>14 κύριε ἀπὸ ὀλίγων ἀπὸ γῆς διαμέρισον αὐτοὺς ἐν τῇ ζωῇ αὐτῶν καὶ τῶν κεκρυμμένων σου ἐπλήσθη ἡ γαστὴρ αὐτῶν ἐχορτάσθησαν υἱῶν καὶ ἀφῆκαν τὰ κατάλοιπα τοῖς νηπίοις αὐτῶν </p>
 <p>15 ἐγὼ δὲ ἐν δικαιοσύνῃ ὀφθήσομαι τῷ προσώπῳ σου χορτασθήσομαι ἐν τῷ ὀφθῆναι τὴν δόξαν σου </p>

 <p align="center"><a href="../vul/psa016.htm">Vul</a></p><p>1 Oratio David. [Exaudi, Domine, justitiam meam;<br> intende deprecationem meam.<br> Auribus percipe orationem meam,<br> non in labiis dolosis.<br></p>
 <p>2 De vultu tuo judicium meum prodeat;<br> oculi tui videant æquitates.<br></p>
 <p>3 Probasti cor meum, et visitasti nocte;<br> igne me examinasti, et non est inventa in me iniquitas.<br></p>
 <p>4 Ut non loquatur os meum opera hominum:<br> propter verba labiorum tuorum, ego custodivi vias duras.<br></p>
 <p>5 Perfice gressus meos in semitis tuis,<br> ut non moveantur vestigia mea.<br></p>
 <p>6 Ego clamavi, quoniam exaudisti me, Deus;<br> inclina aurem tuam mihi, et exaudi verba mea.<br></p>
 <p>7 Mirifica misericordias tuas,<br> qui salvos facis sperantes in te.<br></p>
 <p>8 A resistentibus dexteræ tuæ custodi me<br> ut pupillam oculi.<br> Sub umbra alarum tuarum protege me<br></p>
 <p>9 a facie impiorum qui me afflixerunt.<br> Inimici mei animam meam circumdederunt;<br></p>
 <p>10 adipem suum concluserunt:<br> os eorum locutum est superbiam.<br></p>
 <p>11 Projicientes me nunc circumdederunt me;<br> oculos suos statuerunt declinare in terram.<br></p>
 <p>12 Susceperunt me sicut leo paratus ad prædam,<br> et sicut catulus leonis habitans in abditis.<br></p>
 <p>13 Exsurge, Domine: præveni eum, et supplanta eum:<br> eripe animam meam ab impio;<br> frameam tuam</p>
 <p>14 ab inimicis manus tuæ.<br> Domine, a paucis de terra divide eos in vita eorum;<br> de absconditis tuis adimpletus est venter eorum.<br> Saturati sunt filiis,<br> et dimiserunt reliquias suas parvulis suis.<br></p>
 <p>15 Ego autem in justitia apparebo conspectui tuo;<br> satiabor cum apparuerit gloria tua.]</p>
