 <h3 align="CENTER">Sirach 3</h3><table border="1">
 <tr>
 <td valign="top">
 <p align="center"><a href="../apo/sir003.htm">Apo</a></p><p>1 Hear me your father, O children, and do thereafter, that ye may be safe. </p>
 <p>2 For the Lord hath given the father honour over the children, and hath confirmed the authority of the mother over the sons. </p>
 <p>3 Whoso honoureth his father maketh an atonement for his sins: </p>
 <p>4 And he that honoureth his mother is as one that layeth up treasure. </p>
 <p>5 Whoso honoureth his father shall have joy of his own children; and when he maketh his prayer, he shall be heard. </p>
 <p>6 He that honoureth his father shall have a long life; and he that is obedient unto the Lord shall be a comfort to his mother. </p>
 <p>7 He that feareth the Lord will honour his father, and will do service unto his parents, as to his masters. </p>
 <p>8 Honour thy father and mother both in word and deed, that a blessing may come upon thee from them. </p>
 <p>9 For the blessing of the father establisheth the houses of children; but the curse of the mother rooteth out foundations. </p>
 <p>10 Glory not in the dishonour of thy father; for thy father's dishonour is no glory unto thee. </p>
 <p>11 For the glory of a man is from the honour of his father; and a mother in dishonour is a reproach to the children. </p>
 <p>12 My son, help thy father in his age, and grieve him not as long as he liveth. </p>
 <p>13 And if his understanding fail, have patience with him; and despise him not when thou art in thy full strength. </p>
 <p>14 For the relieving of thy father shall not be forgotten: and instead of sins it shall be added to build thee up. </p>
 <p>15 In the day of thine affliction it shall be remembered; thy sins also shall melt away, as the ice in the fair warm weather. </p>
 <p>16 He that forsaketh his father is as a blasphemer; and he that angereth his mother is cursed: of God. </p>
 <p>17 My son, go on with thy business in meekness; so shalt thou be beloved of him that is approved. </p>
 <p>18 The greater thou art, the more humble thyself, and thou shalt find favour before the Lord. </p>
 <p>19 Many are in high place, and of renown: but mysteries are revealed unto the meek. </p>
 <p>20 For the power of the Lord is great, and he is honoured of the lowly. </p>
 <p>21 Seek not out things that are too hard for thee, neither search the things that are above thy strength. </p>
 <p>22 But what is commanded thee, think thereupon with reverence, for it is not needful for thee to see with thine eyes the things that are in secret. </p>
 <p>23 Be not curious in unnecessary matters: for more things are shewed unto thee than men understand. </p>
 <p>24 For many are deceived by their own vain opinion; and an evil suspicion hath overthrown their judgment. </p>
 <p>25 Without eyes thou shalt want light: profess not the knowledge therefore that thou hast not. </p>
 <p>26 A stubborn heart shall fare evil at the last; and he that loveth danger shall perish therein. </p>
 <p>27 An obstinate heart shall be laden with sorrows; and the wicked man shall heap sin upon sin. </p>
 <p>28 In the punishment of the proud there is no remedy; for the plant of wickedness hath taken root in him. </p>
 <p>29 The heart of the prudent will understand a parable; and an attentive ear is the desire of a wise man. </p>
 <p>30 Water will quench a flaming fire; and alms maketh an atonement for sins. </p>
 <p>31 And he that requiteth good turns is mindful of that which may come hereafter; and when he falleth, he shall find a stay. </p>

 <p align="center"><a href="../sep/sir003.htm">Sep</a></p><p>1 ἐμοῦ τοῦ πατρὸς ἀκούσατε τέκνα καὶ οὕτως ποιήσατε ἵνα σωθῆτε </p>
 <p>2 ὁ γὰρ κύριος ἐδόξασεν πατέρα ἐπὶ τέκνοις καὶ κρίσιν μητρὸς ἐστερέωσεν ἐφ᾽ υἱοῖς </p>
 <p>3 ὁ τιμῶν πατέρα ἐξιλάσκεται ἁμαρτίας </p>
 <p>4 καὶ ὡς ὁ ἀποθησαυρίζων ὁ δοξάζων μητέρα αὐτοῦ </p>
 <p>5 ὁ τιμῶν πατέρα εὐφρανθήσεται ὑπὸ τέκνων καὶ ἐν ἡμέρᾳ προσευχῆς αὐτοῦ εἰσακουσθήσεται </p>
 <p>6 ὁ δοξάζων πατέρα μακροημερεύσει καὶ ὁ εἰσακούων κυρίου ἀναπαύσει μητέρα αὐτοῦ </p>
 <p>7 καὶ ὡς δεσπόταις δουλεύσει ἐν τοῖς γεννήσασιν αὐτόν </p>
 <p>8 ἐν ἔργῳ καὶ λόγῳ τίμα τὸν πατέρα σου ἵνα ἐπέλθῃ σοι εὐλογία παρ᾽ αὐτοῦ </p>
 <p>9 εὐλογία γὰρ πατρὸς στηρίζει οἴκους τέκνων κατάρα δὲ μητρὸς ἐκριζοῖ θεμέλια </p>
 <p>10 μὴ δοξάζου ἐν ἀτιμίᾳ πατρός σου οὐ γάρ ἐστίν σοι δόξα πατρὸς ἀτιμία </p>
 <p>11 ἡ γὰρ δόξα ἀνθρώπου ἐκ τιμῆς πατρὸς αὐτοῦ καὶ ὄνειδος τέκνοις μήτηρ ἐν ἀδοξίᾳ </p>
 <p>12 τέκνον ἀντιλαβοῦ ἐν γήρᾳ πατρός σου καὶ μὴ λυπήσῃς αὐτὸν ἐν τῇ ζωῇ αὐτοῦ </p>
 <p>13 κἂν ἀπολείπῃ σύνεσιν συγγνώμην ἔχε καὶ μὴ ἀτιμάσῃς αὐτὸν ἐν πάσῃ ἰσχύι σου </p>
 <p>14 ἐλεημοσύνη γὰρ πατρὸς οὐκ ἐπιλησθήσεται καὶ ἀντὶ ἁμαρτιῶν προσανοικοδομηθήσεταί σοι </p>
 <p>15 ἐν ἡμέρᾳ θλίψεώς σου ἀναμνησθήσεταί σου ὡς εὐδία ἐπὶ παγετῷ οὕτως ἀναλυθήσονταί σου αἱ ἁμαρτίαι </p>
 <p>16 ὡς βλάσφημος ὁ ἐγκαταλιπὼν πατέρα καὶ κεκατηραμένος ὑπὸ κυρίου ὁ παροργίζων μητέρα αὐτοῦ </p>
 <p>17 τέκνον ἐν πραΰτητι τὰ ἔργα σου διέξαγε καὶ ὑπὸ ἀνθρώπου δεκτοῦ ἀγαπηθήσῃ </p>
 <p>18 ὅσῳ μέγας εἶ τοσούτῳ ταπείνου σεαυτόν καὶ ἔναντι κυρίου εὑρήσεις χάριν </p>
 <p>20 ὅτι μεγάλη ἡ δυναστεία τοῦ κυρίου καὶ ὑπὸ τῶν ταπεινῶν δοξάζεται </p>
 <p>21 χαλεπώτερά σου μὴ ζήτει καὶ ἰσχυρότερά σου μὴ ἐξέταζε </p>
 <p>22 ἃ προσετάγη σοι ταῦτα διανοοῦ οὐ γάρ ἐστίν σοι χρεία τῶν κρυπτῶν </p>
 <p>23 ἐν τοῖς περισσοῖς τῶν ἔργων σου μὴ περιεργάζου πλείονα γὰρ συνέσεως ἀνθρώπων ὑπεδείχθη σοι </p>
 <p>24 πολλοὺς γὰρ ἐπλάνησεν ἡ ὑπόλημψις αὐτῶν καὶ ὑπόνοια πονηρὰ ὠλίσθησεν διανοίας αὐτῶν </p>
 <p>26 καρδία σκληρὰ κακωθήσεται ἐπ᾽ ἐσχάτων καὶ ὁ ἀγαπῶν κίνδυνον ἐν αὐτῷ ἀπολεῖται </p>
 <p>27 καρδία σκληρὰ βαρυνθήσεται πόνοις καὶ ὁ ἁμαρτωλὸς προσθήσει ἁμαρτίαν ἐφ᾽ ἁμαρτίαις </p>
 <p>28 ἐπαγωγῇ ὑπερηφάνου οὐκ ἔστιν ἴασις φυτὸν γὰρ πονηρίας ἐρρίζωκεν ἐν αὐτῷ </p>
 <p>29 καρδία συνετοῦ διανοηθήσεται παραβολήν καὶ οὖς ἀκροατοῦ ἐπιθυμία σοφοῦ </p>
 <p>30 πῦρ φλογιζόμενον ἀποσβέσει ὕδωρ καὶ ἐλεημοσύνη ἐξιλάσεται ἁμαρτίας </p>
 <p>31 ὁ ἀνταποδιδοὺς χάριτας μέμνηται εἰς τὰ μετὰ ταῦτα καὶ ἐν καιρῷ πτώσεως αὐτοῦ εὑρήσει στήριγμα </p>

 <p align="center"><a href="../vul/sir003.htm">Vul</a></p><p>1 [Filii sapientiæ ecclesia justorum,<br> et natio illorum obedientia et dilectio.<br></p>
 <p>2 Judicium patris audite, filii,<br> et sic facite, ut salvi sitis.<br></p>
 <p>3 Deus enim honoravit patrem in filiis:<br> et judicium matris exquirens, firmavit in filios.<br></p>
 <p>4 Qui diligit Deum exorabit pro peccatis,<br> et continebit se ab illis,<br> et in oratione dierum exaudietur.<br></p>
 <p>5 Et sicut qui thesaurizat,<br> ita et qui honorificat matrem suam.<br></p>
 <p>6 Qui honorat patrem suum jucundabitur in filiis,<br> et in die orationis suæ exaudietur.<br></p>
 <p>7 Qui honorat patrem suum vita vivet longiore,<br> et qui obedit patri refrigerabit matrem.<br></p>
 <p>8 Qui timet Dominum honorat parentes,<br> et quasi dominis serviet his qui se genuerunt.<br></p>
 <p>9 In opere, et sermone, et omni patientia, honora patrem tuum,<br></p>
 <p>10 ut superveniat tibi benedictio ab eo,<br> et benedictio illius in novissimo maneat.<br></p>
 <p>11 Benedictio patris firmat domos filiorum:<br> maledictio autem matris eradicat fundamenta.<br></p>
 <p>12 Ne glorieris in contumelia patris tui:<br> non enim est tibi gloria ejus confusio.<br></p>
 <p>13 Gloria enim hominis ex honore patris sui,<br> et dedecus filii pater sine honore.<br></p>
 <p>14 Fili, suscipe senectam patris tui,<br> et non contristes eum in vita illius:<br></p>
 <p>15 et si defecerit sensu, veniam da,<br> et ne spernas eum in virtute tua:<br> eleemosyna enim patris non erit in oblivione.<br></p>
 <p>16 Nam pro peccato matris restituetur tibi bonum:<br></p>
 <p>17 et in justitia ædificabitur tibi,<br> et in die tribulationis commemorabitur tui,<br> et sicut in sereno glacies, solventur peccata tua.<br></p>
 <p>18 Quam malæ famæ est qui derelinquit patrem,<br> et est maledictus a Deo qui exasperat matrem!]<br>
 </p>
 <p>19 [Fili, in mansuetudine opera tua perfice,<br> et super hominum gloriam diligeris.<br></p>
 <p>20 Quanto magnus es, humilia te in omnibus,<br> et coram Deo invenies gratiam:<br></p>
 <p>21 quoniam magna potentia Dei solius,<br> et ab humilibus honoratur.<br></p>
 <p>22 Altiora te ne quæsieris,<br> et fortiora te ne scrutatus fueris:<br> sed quæ præcepit tibi Deus, illa cogita semper,<br> et in pluribus operibus ejus ne fueris curiosus.<br></p>
 <p>23 Non est enim tibi necessarium<br> ea, quæ abscondita sunt, videre oculis tuis.<br></p>
 <p>24 In supervacuis rebus noli scrutari multipliciter,<br> et in pluribus operibus ejus non eris curiosus.<br></p>
 <p>25 Plurima enim super sensum hominum ostensa sunt tibi:<br></p>
 <p>26 multos quoque supplantavit suspicio illorum,<br> et in vanitate detinuit sensus illorum.<br></p>
 <p>27 Cor durum habebit male in novissimo,<br> et qui amat periculum in illo peribit.<br></p>
 <p>28 Cor ingrediens duas vias non habebit successus,<br> et pravus corde in illis scandalizabitur.<br></p>
 <p>29 Cor nequam gravabitur in doloribus,<br> et peccator adjiciet ad peccandum.<br></p>
 <p>30 Synagogæ superborum non erit sanitas,<br> frutex enim peccati radicabitur in illis, et non intelligetur.<br></p>
 <p>31 Cor sapientis intelligitur in sapientia,<br> et auris bona audiet cum omni concupiscentia sapientiam.<br></p>
 <p>32 Sapiens cor et intelligibile abstinebit se a peccatis,<br> et in operibus justitiæ successus habebit.]<br>
 </p>
 <p>33 [Ignem ardentem exstinguit aqua,<br> et eleemosyna resistit peccatis:<br></p>
 <p>34 et Deus prospector est ejus qui reddit gratiam:<br> meminit ejus in posterum,<br> et in tempore casus sui inveniet firmamentum.]</p>
