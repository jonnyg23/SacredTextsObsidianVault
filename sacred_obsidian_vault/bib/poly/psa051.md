 <p>2  Wash<sup><a href="h3526.htm">3526</a></sup> me throughly<sup><a href="h7235.htm">7235</a></sup> from mine iniquity,<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h5771.htm">5771</a></sup> and cleanse<sup><a href="h2891.htm">2891</a></sup> me from my sin.<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h2403.htm">2403</a></sup> </p>
 <p>3  For<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> I<sup><a href="h0589.htm">589</a></sup> acknowledge<sup><a href="h3045.htm">3045</a></sup> my transgressions:<sup><a href="h6588.htm">6588</a></sup> and my sin<sup><a href="h2403.htm">2403</a></sup> is ever<sup><a href="h8548.htm">8548</a></sup> before<sup><a href="h5048.htm">5048</a></sup> me. </p>
 <p>4  Against thee, thee only,<sup><a href="h0905.htm">905</a></sup> have I sinned,<sup><a href="h2398.htm">2398</a></sup> and done<sup><a href="h6213.htm">6213</a></sup> this evil<sup><a href="h7451.htm">7451</a></sup> in thy sight:<sup><a href="h5869.htm">5869</a></sup> that<sup><a href="h4616.htm">4616</a></sup> thou mightest be justified<sup><a href="h6663.htm">6663</a></sup> when thou speakest,<sup><a href="h1696.htm">1696</a></sup> and be clear<sup><a href="h2135.htm">2135</a></sup> when thou judgest.<sup><a href="h8199.htm">8199</a></sup> </p>
 <p>5  Behold,<sup><a href="h2005.htm">2005</a></sup> I was shapen<sup><a href="h2342.htm">2342</a></sup> in iniquity;<sup><a href="h5771.htm">5771</a></sup> and in sin<sup><a href="h2399.htm">2399</a></sup> did my mother<sup><a href="h0517.htm">517</a></sup> conceive<sup><a href="h3179.htm">3179</a></sup> me. </p>
 <p>6  Behold,<sup><a href="h2005.htm">2005</a></sup> thou desirest<sup><a href="h2654.htm">2654</a></sup> truth<sup><a href="h0571.htm">571</a></sup> in the inward parts:<sup><a href="h2910.htm">2910</a></sup> and in the hidden<sup><a href="h5640.htm">5640</a></sup> part thou shalt make me to know<sup><a href="h3045.htm">3045</a></sup> wisdom.<sup><a href="h2451.htm">2451</a></sup> </p>
 <p>7  Purge<sup><a href="h2398.htm">2398</a></sup> me with hyssop,<sup><a href="h0231.htm">231</a></sup> and I shall be clean:<sup><a href="h2891.htm">2891</a></sup> wash<sup><a href="h3526.htm">3526</a></sup> me, and I shall be whiter<sup><a href="h3835.htm">3835</a></sup> than snow.<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h7950.htm">7950</a></sup> </p>
 <p>8  Make me to hear<sup><a href="h8085.htm">8085</a></sup> joy<sup><a href="h8342.htm">8342</a></sup> and gladness;<sup><a href="h8057.htm">8057</a></sup> that the bones<sup><a href="h6106.htm">6106</a></sup> which thou hast broken<sup><a href="h1794.htm">1794</a></sup> may rejoice.<sup><a href="h1523.htm">1523</a></sup> </p>
 <p>9  Hide<sup><a href="h5641.htm">5641</a></sup> thy face<sup><a href="h6440.htm">6440</a></sup> from my sins,<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h2399.htm">2399</a></sup> and blot out<sup><a href="h4229.htm">4229</a></sup> all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> mine iniquities.<sup><a href="h5771.htm">5771</a></sup> </p>
 <p>10  Create<sup><a href="h1254.htm">1254</a></sup> in me a clean<sup><a href="h2889.htm">2889</a></sup> heart,<sup><a href="h3820.htm">3820</a></sup> O God;<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> and renew<sup><a href="h2318.htm">2318</a></sup> a right<sup><a href="h3559.htm">3559</a></sup> spirit<sup><a href="h7307.htm">7307</a></sup> within<sup><a href="h7130.htm">7130</a></sup> me. </p>
 <p>11  Cast<sup><a href="h7993.htm">7993</a></sup> me not<sup><a href="h0408.htm">408</a></sup> away from thy presence;<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h6440.htm">6440</a></sup> and take<sup><a href="h3947.htm">3947</a></sup> not<sup><a href="h0408.htm">408</a></sup> thy holy<sup><a href="h6944.htm">6944</a></sup> spirit<sup><a href="h7307.htm">7307</a></sup> from<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> me. </p>
 <p>12  Restore<sup><a href="h7725.htm">7725</a></sup> unto me the joy<sup><a href="h8342.htm">8342</a></sup> of thy salvation;<sup><a href="h3468.htm">3468</a></sup> and uphold<sup><a href="h5564.htm">5564</a></sup> me with thy free<sup><a href="h5082.htm">5082</a></sup> spirit.<sup><a href="h7307.htm">7307</a></sup> </p>
 <p>13  Then will I teach<sup><a href="h3925.htm">3925</a></sup> transgressors<sup><a href="h6586.htm">6586</a></sup> thy ways;<sup><a href="h1870.htm">1870</a></sup> and sinners<sup><a href="h2400.htm">2400</a></sup> shall be converted<sup><a href="h7725.htm">7725</a></sup> unto<sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> thee. </p>
 <p>14  Deliver<sup><a href="h5337.htm">5337</a></sup> me from bloodguiltiness,<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h1818.htm">1818</a></sup> O God,<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> thou God<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> of my salvation:<sup><a href="h8668.htm">8668</a></sup> and my tongue<sup><a href="h3956.htm">3956</a></sup> shall sing aloud<sup><a href="h7442.htm">7442</a></sup> of thy righteousness.<sup><a href="h6666.htm">6666</a></sup> </p>
 <p>15  O Lord,<sup><a href="h0136.htm">136</a></sup> open<sup><a href="h6605.htm">6605</a></sup> thou my lips;<sup><a href="h8193.htm">8193</a></sup> and my mouth<sup><a href="h6310.htm">6310</a></sup> shall show forth<sup><a href="h5046.htm">5046</a></sup> thy praise.<sup><a href="h8416.htm">8416</a></sup> </p>
 <p>16  For<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> thou desirest<sup><a href="h2654.htm">2654</a></sup> not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> sacrifice;<sup><a href="h2077.htm">2077</a></sup> else would I give<sup><a href="h5414.htm">5414</a></sup> it: thou delightest<sup><a href="h7521.htm">7521</a></sup> not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> in burnt offering.<sup><a href="h5930.htm">5930</a></sup> </p>
 <p>17  The sacrifices<sup><a href="h2077.htm">2077</a></sup> of God<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> are a broken<sup><a href="h7665.htm">7665</a></sup> spirit:<sup><a href="h7307.htm">7307</a></sup> a broken<sup><a href="h7665.htm">7665</a></sup> and a contrite<sup><a href="h1794.htm">1794</a></sup> heart,<sup><a href="h3820.htm">3820</a></sup> O God,<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> thou wilt not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> despise.<sup><a href="h0959.htm">959</a></sup> </p>
 <p>18  Do good<sup><a href="h3190.htm">3190</a></sup> in thy good pleasure<sup><a href="h7522.htm">7522</a></sup> unto <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> Zion:<sup><a href="h6726.htm">6726</a></sup> build<sup><a href="h1129.htm">1129</a></sup> thou the walls<sup><a href="h2346.htm">2346</a></sup> of Jerusalem.<sup><a href="h3389.htm">3389</a></sup> </p>
 <p>19  Then<sup><a href="h0227.htm">227</a></sup> shalt thou be pleased<sup><a href="h2654.htm">2654</a></sup> with the sacrifices<sup><a href="h2077.htm">2077</a></sup> of righteousness,<sup><a href="h6664.htm">6664</a></sup> with burnt offering<sup><a href="h5930.htm">5930</a></sup> and whole burnt offering:<sup><a href="h3632.htm">3632</a></sup> then<sup><a href="h0227.htm">227</a></sup> shall they offer<sup><a href="h5927.htm">5927</a></sup> bullocks<sup><a href="h6499.htm">6499</a></sup> upon<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> thine altar.<sup><a href="h4196.htm">4196</a></sup> </p>
