 <h3 align="CENTER">Proverbs 22</h3><table border="1">

 <td valign="top">
 <p align="center"><a href="../kjv/pro022.htm">KJV</a></p><p>1  A good name<sup><a href="h8034.htm">8034</a></sup> is rather to be chosen<sup><a href="h0977.htm">977</a></sup> than great<sup><a href="h7227.htm">7227</a></sup> riches,<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h6239.htm">6239</a></sup> and loving<sup><a href="h2896.htm">2896</a></sup> favor<sup><a href="h2580.htm">2580</a></sup> rather than silver<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3701.htm">3701</a></sup> and gold.<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h2091.htm">2091</a></sup> </p>
 <p>2  The rich<sup><a href="h6223.htm">6223</a></sup> and poor<sup><a href="h7326.htm">7326</a></sup> meet together:<sup><a href="h6298.htm">6298</a></sup> the LORD<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> is the maker<sup><a href="h6213.htm">6213</a></sup> of them all.<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> </p>
 <p>3  A prudent<sup><a href="h6175.htm">6175</a></sup> man foreseeth<sup><a href="h7200.htm">7200</a></sup> the evil,<sup><a href="h7451.htm">7451</a></sup> and hideth himself:<sup><a href="h5641.htm">5641</a></sup> but the simple<sup><a href="h6612.htm">6612</a></sup> pass on,<sup><a href="h5674.htm">5674</a></sup> and are punished.<sup><a href="h6064.htm">6064</a></sup> </p>
 <p>4  By<sup><a href="h6118.htm">6118</a></sup> humility<sup><a href="h6038.htm">6038</a></sup> and the fear<sup><a href="h3374.htm">3374</a></sup> of the LORD<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> are riches,<sup><a href="h6239.htm">6239</a></sup> and honor,<sup><a href="h3519.htm">3519</a></sup> and life.<sup><a href="h2416.htm">2416</a></sup> </p>
 <p>5  Thorns<sup><a href="h6791.htm">6791</a></sup> and snares<sup><a href="h6341.htm">6341</a></sup> are in the way<sup><a href="h1870.htm">1870</a></sup> of the froward:<sup><a href="h6141.htm">6141</a></sup> he that doth keep<sup><a href="h8104.htm">8104</a></sup> his soul<sup><a href="h5315.htm">5315</a></sup> shall be far<sup><a href="h7368.htm">7368</a></sup> from<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> them. </p>
 <p>6  Train up<sup><a href="h2596.htm">2596</a></sup> a child<sup><a href="h5288.htm">5288</a></sup> in<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> the way<sup><a href="h1870.htm">1870</a></sup> he should go:<sup><a href="h6310.htm">6310</a></sup> and<sup><a href="h1571.htm">1571</a></sup> when<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> he is old,<sup><a href="h2204.htm">2204</a></sup> he will not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> depart<sup><a href="h5493.htm">5493</a></sup> from<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> it. </p>
 <p>7  The rich<sup><a href="h6223.htm">6223</a></sup> ruleth<sup><a href="h4910.htm">4910</a></sup> over the poor,<sup><a href="h7326.htm">7326</a></sup> and the borrower<sup><a href="h3867.htm">3867</a></sup> is servant<sup><a href="h5650.htm">5650</a></sup> to the lender.<sup><a href="h0376.htm">376</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3867.htm">3867</a></sup> </p>
 <p>8  He that soweth<sup><a href="h2232.htm">2232</a></sup> iniquity<sup><a href="h5766.htm">5766</a></sup> shall reap<sup><a href="h7114.htm">7114</a></sup> vanity:<sup><a href="h0205.htm">205</a></sup> and the rod<sup><a href="h7626.htm">7626</a></sup> of his anger<sup><a href="h5678.htm">5678</a></sup> shall fail.<sup><a href="h3615.htm">3615</a></sup> </p>
 <p>9  He<sup><a href="h1931.htm">1931</a></sup> that hath a bountiful<sup><a href="h2896.htm">2896</a></sup> eye<sup><a href="h5869.htm">5869</a></sup> shall be blessed;<sup><a href="h1288.htm">1288</a></sup> for<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> he giveth<sup><a href="h5414.htm">5414</a></sup> of his bread<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3899.htm">3899</a></sup> to the poor.<sup><a href="h1800.htm">1800</a></sup> </p>
 <p>10  Cast out<sup><a href="h1644.htm">1644</a></sup> the scorner,<sup><a href="h3887.htm">3887</a></sup> and contention<sup><a href="h4066.htm">4066</a></sup> shall go out;<sup><a href="h3318.htm">3318</a></sup> yea, strife<sup><a href="h1779.htm">1779</a></sup> and reproach<sup><a href="h7036.htm">7036</a></sup> shall cease.<sup><a href="h7673.htm">7673</a></sup> </p>
 <p>11  He that loveth<sup><a href="h0157.htm">157</a></sup> pureness<sup><a href="h2890.htm">2890</a></sup> of heart,<sup><a href="h3820.htm">3820</a></sup> for the grace<sup><a href="h2580.htm">2580</a></sup> of his lips<sup><a href="h8193.htm">8193</a></sup> the king<sup><a href="h4428.htm">4428</a></sup> shall be his friend.<sup><a href="h7453.htm">7453</a></sup> </p>
 <p>12  The eyes<sup><a href="h5869.htm">5869</a></sup> of the LORD<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> preserve<sup><a href="h5341.htm">5341</a></sup> knowledge,<sup><a href="h1847.htm">1847</a></sup> and he overthroweth<sup><a href="h5557.htm">5557</a></sup> the words<sup><a href="h1697.htm">1697</a></sup> of the transgressor.<sup><a href="h0898.htm">898</a></sup> </p>
 <p>13  The slothful<sup><a href="h6102.htm">6102</a></sup> man saith,<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> There is a lion<sup><a href="h0738.htm">738</a></sup> without,<sup><a href="h2351.htm">2351</a></sup> I shall be slain<sup><a href="h7523.htm">7523</a></sup> in<sup><a href="h8432.htm">8432</a></sup> the streets.<sup><a href="h7339.htm">7339</a></sup> </p>
 <p>14  The mouth<sup><a href="h6310.htm">6310</a></sup> of strange women<sup><a href="h2114.htm">2114</a></sup> is a deep<sup><a href="h6013.htm">6013</a></sup> pit:<sup><a href="h7745.htm">7745</a></sup> he that is abhorred<sup><a href="h2194.htm">2194</a></sup> of the LORD<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> shall fall<sup><a href="h5307.htm">5307</a></sup> therein.<sup><a href="h8033.htm">8033</a></sup> </p>
 <p>15  Foolishness<sup><a href="h0200.htm">200</a></sup> is bound<sup><a href="h7194.htm">7194</a></sup> in the heart<sup><a href="h3820.htm">3820</a></sup> of a child;<sup><a href="h5288.htm">5288</a></sup> but the rod<sup><a href="h7626.htm">7626</a></sup> of correction<sup><a href="h4148.htm">4148</a></sup> shall drive it far<sup><a href="h7368.htm">7368</a></sup> from<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> him. </p>
 <p>16  He that oppresseth<sup><a href="h6231.htm">6231</a></sup> the poor<sup><a href="h1800.htm">1800</a></sup> to increase<sup><a href="h7235.htm">7235</a></sup> his riches, and he that giveth<sup><a href="h5414.htm">5414</a></sup> to the rich,<sup><a href="h6223.htm">6223</a></sup> shall surely<sup><a href="h0389.htm">389</a></sup> come to want.<sup><a href="h4270.htm">4270</a></sup> </p>
 <p>17  Bow down<sup><a href="h5186.htm">5186</a></sup> thine ear,<sup><a href="h0241.htm">241</a></sup> and hear<sup><a href="h8085.htm">8085</a></sup> the words<sup><a href="h1697.htm">1697</a></sup> of the wise,<sup><a href="h2450.htm">2450</a></sup> and apply<sup><a href="h7896.htm">7896</a></sup> thine heart<sup><a href="h3820.htm">3820</a></sup> unto my knowledge.<sup><a href="h1847.htm">1847</a></sup> </p>
 <p>18  For<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> it is a pleasant<sup><a href="h5273.htm">5273</a></sup> thing if<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> thou keep<sup><a href="h8104.htm">8104</a></sup> them within<sup><a href="h0990.htm">990</a></sup> thee; they shall withal<sup><a href="h3162.htm">3162</a></sup> be fitted<sup><a href="h3559.htm">3559</a></sup> in<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> thy lips.<sup><a href="h8193.htm">8193</a></sup> </p>
 <p>19  That thy trust<sup><a href="h4009.htm">4009</a></sup> may be<sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> in the LORD,<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> I have made known<sup><a href="h3045.htm">3045</a></sup> to thee this day,<sup><a href="h3117.htm">3117</a></sup> even<sup><a href="h0637.htm">637</a></sup> to thee.<sup><a href="h0859.htm">859</a></sup> </p>
 <p>20  Have not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> I written<sup><a href="h3789.htm">3789</a></sup> to thee excellent things<sup><a href="h7991.htm">7991</a></sup> in counsels<sup><a href="h4156.htm">4156</a></sup> and knowledge,<sup><a href="h1847.htm">1847</a></sup> </p>
 <p>21  That I might make thee know<sup><a href="h3045.htm">3045</a></sup> the certainty<sup><a href="h7189.htm">7189</a></sup> of the words<sup><a href="h0561.htm">561</a></sup> of truth;<sup><a href="h0571.htm">571</a></sup> that thou mightest answer<sup><a href="h7725.htm">7725</a></sup> the words<sup><a href="h0561.htm">561</a></sup> of truth<sup><a href="h0571.htm">571</a></sup> to them that send<sup><a href="h7971.htm">7971</a></sup> unto thee? </p>
 <p>22  Rob<sup><a href="h1497.htm">1497</a></sup> not<sup><a href="h0408.htm">408</a></sup> the poor,<sup><a href="h1800.htm">1800</a></sup> because<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> he<sup><a href="h1931.htm">1931</a></sup> is poor:<sup><a href="h1800.htm">1800</a></sup> neither<sup><a href="h0408.htm">408</a></sup> oppress<sup><a href="h1792.htm">1792</a></sup> the afflicted<sup><a href="h6041.htm">6041</a></sup> in the gate:<sup><a href="h8179.htm">8179</a></sup> </p>
 <p>23  For<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> the LORD<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> will plead<sup><a href="h7378.htm">7378</a></sup> their cause,<sup><a href="h7379.htm">7379</a></sup> and spoil<sup><a href="h6906.htm">6906</a></sup> the soul<sup><a href="h5315.htm">5315</a></sup> of <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> those that spoiled<sup><a href="h6906.htm">6906</a></sup> them. </p>
 <p>24  Make no<sup><a href="h0408.htm">408</a></sup> friendship<sup><a href="h7462.htm">7462</a></sup> with<sup><a href="h0854.htm">854</a></sup> an angry<sup><a href="h0639.htm">639</a></sup> man;<sup><a href="h1167.htm">1167</a></sup> and with<sup><a href="h0854.htm">854</a></sup> a furious<sup><a href="h2534.htm">2534</a></sup> man<sup><a href="h0376.htm">376</a></sup> thou shalt not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> go:<sup><a href="h0935.htm">935</a></sup> </p>
 <p>25  Lest<sup><a href="h6435.htm">6435</a></sup> thou learn<sup><a href="h0502.htm">502</a></sup> his ways,<sup><a href="h0734.htm">734</a></sup> and get<sup><a href="h3947.htm">3947</a></sup> a snare<sup><a href="h4170.htm">4170</a></sup> to thy soul.<sup><a href="h5315.htm">5315</a></sup> </p>
 <p>26  Be<sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> not<sup><a href="h0408.htm">408</a></sup> thou one of them that strike<sup><a href="h8628.htm">8628</a></sup> hands,<sup><a href="h3709.htm">3709</a></sup> or of them that are sureties<sup><a href="h6148.htm">6148</a></sup> for debts.<sup><a href="h4859.htm">4859</a></sup> </p>
 <p>27  If<sup><a href="h0518.htm">518</a></sup> thou hast nothing<sup><a href="h0369.htm">369</a></sup> to pay,<sup><a href="h7999.htm">7999</a></sup> why<sup><a href="h4100.htm">4100</a></sup> should he take away<sup><a href="h3947.htm">3947</a></sup> thy bed<sup><a href="h4904.htm">4904</a></sup> from under<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h8478.htm">8478</a></sup> thee? </p>
 <p>28  Remove<sup><a href="h5253.htm">5253</a></sup> not<sup><a href="h0408.htm">408</a></sup> the ancient<sup><a href="h5769.htm">5769</a></sup> landmark,<sup><a href="h1366.htm">1366</a></sup> which<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> thy fathers<sup><a href="h0001.htm">1</a></sup> have set.<sup><a href="h6213.htm">6213</a></sup> </p>
 <p>29  Seest<sup><a href="h2372.htm">2372</a></sup> thou a man<sup><a href="h0376.htm">376</a></sup> diligent<sup><a href="h4106.htm">4106</a></sup> in his business?<sup><a href="h4399.htm">4399</a></sup> he shall stand<sup><a href="h3320.htm">3320</a></sup> before<sup><a href="h6440.htm">6440</a></sup> kings;<sup><a href="h4428.htm">4428</a></sup> he shall not<sup><a href="h1077.htm">1077</a></sup> stand<sup><a href="h3320.htm">3320</a></sup> before<sup><a href="h6440.htm">6440</a></sup> mean<sup><a href="h2823.htm">2823</a></sup> men. </p>

 <p align="center"><a href="../sep/pro022.htm">Sep</a></p><p>1 αἱρετώτερον ὄνομα καλὸν ἢ πλοῦτος πολύς ὑπὲρ δὲ ἀργύριον καὶ χρυσίον χάρις ἀγαθή </p>
 <p>2 πλούσιος καὶ πτωχὸς συνήντησαν ἀλλήλοις ἀμφοτέρους δὲ ὁ κύριος ἐποίησεν </p>
 <p>3 πανοῦργος ἰδὼν πονηρὸν τιμωρούμενον κραταιῶς αὐτὸς παιδεύεται οἱ δὲ ἄφρονες παρελθόντες ἐζημιώθησαν </p>
 <p>4 γενεὰ σοφίας φόβος κυρίου καὶ πλοῦτος καὶ δόξα καὶ ζωή </p>
 <p>5 τρίβολοι καὶ παγίδες ἐν ὁδοῖς σκολιαῖς ὁ δὲ φυλάσσων τὴν ἑαυτοῦ ψυχὴν ἀφέξεται αὐτῶν </p>
 <p>7 πλούσιοι πτωχῶν ἄρξουσιν καὶ οἰκέται ἰδίοις δεσπόταις δανιοῦσιν </p>
 <p>8 ὁ σπείρων φαῦλα θερίσει κακά πληγὴν δὲ ἔργων αὐτοῦ συντελέσει  <br>
  ἄνδρα ἱλαρὸν καὶ δότην εὐλογεῖ ὁ θεός ματαιότητα δὲ ἔργων αὐτοῦ συντελέσει </p>
 <p>9 ὁ ἐλεῶν πτωχὸν αὐτὸς διατραφήσεται τῶν γὰρ ἑαυτοῦ ἄρτων ἔδωκεν τῷ πτωχῷ  <br>
  νίκην καὶ τιμὴν περιποιεῖται ὁ δῶρα δούς τὴν μέντοι ψυχὴν ἀφαιρεῖται τῶν κεκτημένων </p>
 <p>10 ἔκβαλε ἐκ συνεδρίου λοιμόν καὶ συνεξελεύσεται αὐτῷ νεῖκος ὅταν γὰρ καθίσῃ ἐν συνεδρίῳ πάντας ἀτιμάζει </p>
 <p>11 ἀγαπᾷ κύριος ὁσίας καρδίας δεκτοὶ δὲ αὐτῷ πάντες ἄμωμοι χείλεσιν ποιμαίνει βασιλεύς </p>
 <p>12 οἱ δὲ ὀφθαλμοὶ κυρίου διατηροῦσιν αἴσθησιν φαυλίζει δὲ λόγους παράνομος </p>
 <p>13 προφασίζεται καὶ λέγει ὀκνηρός λέων ἐν ταῖς ὁδοῖς ἐν δὲ ταῖς πλατείαις φονευταί </p>
 <p>14 βόθρος βαθὺς στόμα παρανόμου ὁ δὲ μισηθεὶς ὑπὸ κυρίου ἐμπεσεῖται εἰς αὐτόν  <br>
  εἰσὶν ὁδοὶ κακαὶ ἐνώπιον ἀνδρός καὶ οὐκ ἀγαπᾷ τοῦ ἀποστρέψαι ἀπ᾽ αὐτῶν ἀποστρέφειν δὲ δεῖ ἀπὸ ὁδοῦ σκολιᾶς καὶ κακῆς </p>
 <p>15 ἄνοια ἐξῆπται καρδίας νέου ῥάβδος δὲ καὶ παιδεία μακρὰν ἀπ᾽ αὐτοῦ </p>
 <p>16 ὁ συκοφαντῶν πένητα πολλὰ ποιεῖ τὰ ἑαυτοῦ δίδωσιν δὲ πλουσίῳ ἐπ᾽ ἐλάσσονι </p>
 <p>17 λόγοις σοφῶν παράβαλλε σὸν οὖς καὶ ἄκουε ἐμὸν λόγον τὴν δὲ σὴν καρδίαν ἐπίστησον ἵνα γνῷς ὅτι καλοί εἰσιν </p>
 <p>18 καὶ ἐὰν ἐμβάλῃς αὐτοὺς εἰς τὴν καρδίαν σου εὐφρανοῦσίν σε ἅμα ἐπὶ σοῖς χείλεσιν </p>
 <p>19 ἵνα σου γένηται ἐπὶ κύριον ἡ ἐλπὶς καὶ γνωρίσῃ σοι τὴν ὁδὸν αὐτοῦ </p>
 <p>20 καὶ σὺ δὲ ἀπόγραψαι αὐτὰ σεαυτῷ τρισσῶς εἰς βουλὴν καὶ γνῶσιν ἐπὶ τὸ πλάτος τῆς καρδίας σου </p>
 <p>21 διδάσκω οὖν σε ἀληθῆ λόγον καὶ γνῶσιν ἀγαθὴν ὑπακούειν τοῦ ἀποκρίνεσθαι λόγους ἀληθείας τοῖς προβαλλομένοις σοι </p>
 <p>22 μὴ ἀποβιάζου πένητα πτωχὸς γάρ ἐστιν καὶ μὴ ἀτιμάσῃς ἀσθενῆ ἐν πύλαις </p>
 <p>23 ὁ γὰρ κύριος κρινεῖ αὐτοῦ τὴν κρίσιν καὶ ῥύσῃ σὴν ἄσυλον ψυχήν </p>
 <p>24 μὴ ἴσθι ἑταῖρος ἀνδρὶ θυμώδει φίλῳ δὲ ὀργίλῳ μὴ συναυλίζου </p>
 <p>25 μήποτε μάθῃς τῶν ὁδῶν αὐτοῦ καὶ λάβῃς βρόχους τῇ σῇ ψυχῇ </p>
 <p>26 μὴ δίδου σεαυτὸν εἰς ἐγγύην αἰσχυνόμενος πρόσωπον </p>
 <p>27 ἐὰν γὰρ μὴ ἔχῃς πόθεν ἀποτείσῃς λήμψονται τὸ στρῶμα τὸ ὑπὸ τὰς πλευράς σου </p>
 <p>28 μὴ μέταιρε ὅρια αἰώνια ἃ ἔθεντο οἱ πατέρες σου </p>
 <p>29 ὁρατικὸν ἄνδρα καὶ ὀξὺν ἐν τοῖς ἔργοις αὐτοῦ βασιλεῦσι δεῖ παρεστάναι καὶ μὴ παρεστάναι ἀνδράσι νωθροῖς </p>

 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎7 ‏עָ֭שִׁיר בְּרָשִׁ֣ים יִמְשׁ֑וֹל וְעֶ֥בֶד ל֝וֶֹ֗ה לְאִ֣ישׁ מַלְוֶֽה׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎8 ‏זוֹרֵ֣עַ עַ֭וְלָה <font color="brown">יקצור־</font> <font color="blue">יִקְצָר־</font> אָ֑וֶן וְשֵׁ֖בֶט עֶבְרָת֣וֹ יִכְלֶֽה׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎9 ‏טֽוֹב־עַ֭יִן ה֣וּא יְבֹרָ֑ךְ כִּֽי־נָתַ֖ן מִלַּחְמ֣וֹ לַדָּֽל׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎10 ‏גָּ֣רֵֽשׁ לֵ֭ץ וְיֵצֵ֣א מָד֑וֹן וְ֝יִשְׁבֹּ֗ת דִּ֣ין וְקָלֽוֹן׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎11 ‏אֹהֵ֥ב <font color="brown">טהור־</font> <font color="blue">טְהָר־</font> לֵ֑ב חֵ֥ן שְׂ֝פָתָ֗יו רֵעֵ֥הוּ מֶֽלֶךְ׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎12 ‏עֵינֵ֣י יְ֭הוָה נָ֣צְרוּ דָ֑עַת וַ֝יְסַלֵּ֗ף דִּבְרֵ֥י בֹגֵֽד׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎13 ‏אָמַ֣ר עָ֭צֵל אֲרִ֣י בַח֑וּץ בְּת֥וֹךְ רְ֝חֹב֗וֹת אֵֽרָצֵֽחַ׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎14 ‏שׁוּחָ֣ה עֲ֭מֻקָּה פִּ֣י זָר֑וֹת זְע֥וּם יְ֝הוָ֗ה <font color="brown">יפול־</font> <font color="blue">יִפָּל־</font> שָֽׁם׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎15 ‏אִ֭וֶּלֶת קְשׁוּרָ֣ה בְלֶב־נָ֑עַר שֵׁ֥בֶט מ֝וּסָ֗ר יַרְחִיקֶ֥נָּה מִמֶּֽנּוּ׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎16 ‏עֹ֣שֵֽׁק דָּ֭ל לְהַרְבּ֣וֹת ל֑וֹ נֹתֵ֥ן לְ֝עָשִׁ֗יר אַךְ־לְמַחְסֽוֹר׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎17 ‏הַ֥ט אָזְנְךָ֗ וּ֭שְׁמַע דִּבְרֵ֣י חֲכָמִ֑ים וְ֝לִבְּךָ֗ תָּשִׁ֥ית לְדַעְתִּֽי׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎18 ‏כִּֽי־נָ֭עִים כִּֽי־תִשְׁמְרֵ֣ם בְּבִטְנֶ֑ךָ יִכֹּ֥נוּ יַ֝חְדָּ֗ו עַל־שְׂפָתֶֽיךָ׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎19 ‏לִהְי֣וֹת בַּ֭יהוָה מִבְטַחֶ֑ךָ הוֹדַעְתִּ֖יךָ הַיּ֣וֹם אַף־אָֽתָּה׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎20 ‏הֲלֹ֤א כָתַ֣בְתִּי לְ֭ךָ <font color="brown">שלשום</font> <font color="blue">שָׁלִישִׁ֑ים</font> בְּמ֖וֹעֵצֹ֣ת<font color="red"><sup>3</sup></font> וָדָֽעַת׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎21 ‏לְהוֹדִֽיעֲךָ֗ קֹ֭שְׁטְ<font color="red"><sup>3</sup></font> אִמְרֵ֣י אֱמֶ֑ת לְהָשִׁ֥יב אֲמָרִ֥ים אֱ֝מֶ֗ת לְשֹׁלְחֶֽיךָ׃ פ</span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎22 ‏אַֽל־תִּגְזָל־דָּ֭ל כִּ֣י דַל־ה֑וּא וְאַל־תְּדַכֵּ֖א עָנִ֣י בַשָּֽׁעַר׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎23 ‏כִּֽי־יְ֭הוָה יָרִ֣יב רִיבָ֑ם וְקָבַ֖ע אֶת־קֹבְעֵיהֶ֣ם נָֽפֶשׁ׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎24 ‏אַל־תִּ֭תְרַע אֶת־בַּ֣עַל אָ֑ף וְאֶת־אִ֥ישׁ חֵ֝מוֹת לֹ֣א תָבֽוֹא׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎25 ‏פֶּן־תֶּאֱלַ֥ף <font color="brown">ארחתו</font> <font color="blue">אֹֽרְחֹתָ֑יו</font> וְלָקַחְתָּ֖ מוֹקֵ֣שׁ לְנַפְשֶֽׁךָ׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎26 ‏אַל־תְּהִ֥י בְתֹֽקְעֵי־כָ֑ף בַּ֝עֹרְבִ֗ים מַשָּׁאֽוֹת׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎27 ‏אִם־אֵֽין־לְךָ֥ לְשַׁלֵּ֑ם לָ֥מָּה יִקַּ֥ח מִ֝שְׁכָּבְךָ֗ מִתַּחְתֶּֽיךָ׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎28 ‏אַל־תַּ֭סֵּג גְּב֣וּל עוֹלָ֑ם אֲשֶׁ֖ר עָשׂ֣וּ אֲבוֹתֶֽיךָ׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎29 ‏חָזִ֡יתָ אִ֤ישׁ׀ מָ֘הִ֤יר בִּמְלַאכְתּ֗וֹ לִֽפְנֵֽי־מְלָכִ֥ים יִתְיַצָּ֑ב בַּל־יִ֝תְיַצֵּב לִפְנֵ֥י חֲשֻׁכִּֽים׃ פ</span></p>

 <p align="center"><a href="../vul/pro022.htm">Vul</a></p><p>1 [Melius est nomen bonum quam divitiæ multæ;<br> super argentum et aurum gratia bona.<br></p>
 <p>2 Dives et pauper obviaverunt sibi:<br> utriusque operator est Dominus.<br></p>
 <p>3 Callidus vidit malum, et abscondit se;<br> innocens pertransiit, et afflictus est damno.<br></p>
 <p>4 Finis modestiæ timor Domini,<br> divitiæ, et gloria, et vita.<br></p>
 <p>5 Arma et gladii in via perversi;<br> custos autem animæ suæ longe recedit ab eis.<br></p>
 <p>6 Proverbium est: adolescens juxta viam suam;<br> etiam cum senuerit, non recedet ab ea.<br></p>
 <p>7 Dives pauperibus imperat,<br> et qui accipit mutuum servus est fnerantis.<br></p>
 <p>8 Qui seminat iniquitatem metet mala,<br> et virga iræ suæ consummabitur.<br></p>
 <p>9 Qui pronus est ad misericordiam benedicetur:<br> de panibus enim suis dedit pauperi.<br> Victoriam et honorem acquiret qui dat munera;<br> animam autem aufert accipientium.<br></p>
 <p>10 Ejice derisorem, et exibit cum eo jurgium,<br> cessabuntque causæ et contumeliæ.<br></p>
 <p>11 Qui diligit cordis munditiam,<br> propter gratiam labiorum suorum habebit amicum regem.<br></p>
 <p>12 Oculi Domini custodiunt scientiam,<br> et supplantantur verba iniqui.<br></p>
 <p>13 Dicit piger: Leo est foris;<br> in medio platearum occidendus sum.<br></p>
 <p>14 Fovea profunda os alienæ:<br> cui iratus est Dominus, incidet in eam.<br></p>
 <p>15 Stultitia colligata est in corde pueri,<br> et virga disciplinæ fugabit eam.<br></p>
 <p>16 Qui calumniatur pauperem ut augeat divitias suas,<br> dabit ipse ditiori, et egebit.]<br>
 </p>
 <p>17 [Inclina aurem tuam, et audi verba sapientium:<br> appone autem cor ad doctrinam meam,<br></p>
 <p>18 quæ pulchra erit tibi cum servaveris eam in ventre tuo,<br> et redundabit in labiis tuis:<br></p>
 <p>19 ut sit in Domino fiducia tua,<br> unde et ostendi eam tibi hodie.<br></p>
 <p>20 Ecce descripsi eam tibi tripliciter,<br> in cogitationibus et scientia:<br></p>
 <p>21 ut ostenderem tibi firmitatem et eloquia veritatis,<br> respondere ex his illis qui miserunt te.<br></p>
 <p>22 Non facias violentiam pauperi quia pauper est,<br> neque conteras egenum in porta:<br></p>
 <p>23 quia judicabit Dominus causam ejus,<br> et configet eos qui confixerunt animam ejus.<br></p>
 <p>24 Noli esse amicus homini iracundo,<br> neque ambules cum viro furioso:<br></p>
 <p>25 ne forte discas semitas ejus,<br> et sumas scandalum animæ tuæ.<br></p>
 <p>26 Noli esse cum his qui defigunt manus suas,<br> et qui vades se offerunt pro debitis:<br></p>
 <p>27 si enim non habes unde restituas,<br> quid causæ est ut tollat operimentum de cubili tuo?<br></p>
 <p>28 Ne transgrediaris terminos antiquos,<br> quos posuerunt patres tui.<br></p>
 <p>29 Vidisti virum velocem in opere suo?<br> coram regibus stabit, nec erit ante ignobiles.]</p>
