 <p align="center"><a href="../kjv/psa132.htm">KJV</a></p><p>1  A Song<sup><a href="h7892.htm">7892</a></sup> of degrees.<sup><a href="h4609.htm">4609</a></sup> LORD,<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> remember<sup><a href="h2142.htm">2142</a></sup> David,<sup><a href="h1732.htm">1732</a></sup> and <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> his afflictions:<sup><a href="h6031.htm">6031</a></sup> </p>
 <p>2  How<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> he swore<sup><a href="h7650.htm">7650</a></sup> unto the LORD,<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> and vowed<sup><a href="h5087.htm">5087</a></sup> unto the mighty<sup><a href="h0046.htm">46</a></sup> God of Jacob;<sup><a href="h3290.htm">3290</a></sup> </p>
 <p>3  Surely<sup><a href="h0518.htm">518</a></sup> I will not come<sup><a href="h0935.htm">935</a></sup> into the tabernacle<sup><a href="h0168.htm">168</a></sup> of my house,<sup><a href="h1004.htm">1004</a></sup> nor<sup><a href="h0518.htm">518</a></sup> go up<sup><a href="h5927.htm">5927</a></sup> into<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> my bed;<sup><a href="h6210.htm">6210</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3326.htm">3326</a></sup> </p>

 <p>5  Until<sup><a href="h5704.htm">5704</a></sup> I find out<sup><a href="h4672.htm">4672</a></sup> a place<sup><a href="h4725.htm">4725</a></sup> for the LORD,<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> a habitation<sup><a href="h4908.htm">4908</a></sup> for the mighty<sup><a href="h0046.htm">46</a></sup> God of Jacob.<sup><a href="h3290.htm">3290</a></sup> </p>

 <p>8  Arise,<sup><a href="h6965.htm">6965</a></sup> O LORD,<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> into thy rest;<sup><a href="h4496.htm">4496</a></sup> thou,<sup><a href="h0859.htm">859</a></sup> and the ark<sup><a href="h0727.htm">727</a></sup> of thy strength.<sup><a href="h5797.htm">5797</a></sup> </p>

 <p>15  I will abundantly bless<sup><a href="h1288.htm">1288</a></sup> <sup><a href="h1288.htm">1288</a></sup> her provision:<sup><a href="h6718.htm">6718</a></sup> I will satisfy<sup><a href="h7646.htm">7646</a></sup> her poor<sup><a href="h0034.htm">34</a></sup> with bread.<sup><a href="h3899.htm">3899</a></sup> </p>
