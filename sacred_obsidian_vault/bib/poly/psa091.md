 <td valign="top">
 <p align="center"><a href="../kjv/psa091.htm">KJV</a></p><p>1  He that dwelleth<sup><a href="h3427.htm">3427</a></sup> in the secret place<sup><a href="h5643.htm">5643</a></sup> of the most High<sup><a href="h5945.htm">5945</a></sup> shall abide<sup><a href="h3885.htm">3885</a></sup> under the shadow<sup><a href="h6738.htm">6738</a></sup> of the Almighty.<sup><a href="h7706.htm">7706</a></sup> </p>
 <p>2  I will say<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> of the LORD,<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> He is my refuge<sup><a href="h4268.htm">4268</a></sup> and my fortress:<sup><a href="h4686.htm">4686</a></sup> my God;<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> in him will I trust.<sup><a href="h0982.htm">982</a></sup> </p>
 <p>3  Surely<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> he<sup><a href="h1931.htm">1931</a></sup> shall deliver<sup><a href="h5337.htm">5337</a></sup> thee from the snare<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h6341.htm">6341</a></sup> of the fowler,<sup><a href="h3353.htm">3353</a></sup> and from the noisome pestilence.<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h1698.htm">1698</a></sup> <sup><a href="h1942.htm">1942</a></sup> </p>
 <p>4  He shall cover<sup><a href="h5526.htm">5526</a></sup> thee with his feathers,<sup><a href="h0084.htm">84</a></sup> and under<sup><a href="h8478.htm">8478</a></sup> his wings<sup><a href="h3671.htm">3671</a></sup> shalt thou trust:<sup><a href="h2620.htm">2620</a></sup> his truth<sup><a href="h0571.htm">571</a></sup> shall be thy shield<sup><a href="h6793.htm">6793</a></sup> and buckler.<sup><a href="h5507.htm">5507</a></sup> </p>
 <p>5  Thou shalt not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> be afraid<sup><a href="h3372.htm">3372</a></sup> for the terror<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h6343.htm">6343</a></sup> by night;<sup><a href="h3915.htm">3915</a></sup> nor for the arrow<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h2671.htm">2671</a></sup> that flieth<sup><a href="h5774.htm">5774</a></sup> by day;<sup><a href="h3119.htm">3119</a></sup> </p>
 <p>6  Nor for the pestilence<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h1698.htm">1698</a></sup> that walketh<sup><a href="h1980.htm">1980</a></sup> in darkness;<sup><a href="h0652.htm">652</a></sup> nor for the destruction<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h6986.htm">6986</a></sup> that wasteth<sup><a href="h7736.htm">7736</a></sup> at noonday.<sup><a href="h6672.htm">6672</a></sup> </p>
 <p>7  A thousand<sup><a href="h0505.htm">505</a></sup> shall fall<sup><a href="h5307.htm">5307</a></sup> at thy side,<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h6654.htm">6654</a></sup> and ten thousand<sup><a href="h7233.htm">7233</a></sup> at thy right hand;<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3225.htm">3225</a></sup> but it shall not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> come nigh<sup><a href="h5066.htm">5066</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> thee. </p>
 <p>8  Only<sup><a href="h7535.htm">7535</a></sup> with thine eyes<sup><a href="h5869.htm">5869</a></sup> shalt thou behold<sup><a href="h5027.htm">5027</a></sup> and see<sup><a href="h7200.htm">7200</a></sup> the reward<sup><a href="h8011.htm">8011</a></sup> of the wicked.<sup><a href="h7563.htm">7563</a></sup> </p>
 <p>9  Because<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> thou<sup><a href="h0859.htm">859</a></sup> hast made<sup><a href="h7760.htm">7760</a></sup> the LORD,<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> which is my refuge,<sup><a href="h4268.htm">4268</a></sup> even the most High,<sup><a href="h5945.htm">5945</a></sup> thy habitation;<sup><a href="h4583.htm">4583</a></sup> </p>
 <p>10  There shall no<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> evil<sup><a href="h7451.htm">7451</a></sup> befall<sup><a href="h0579.htm">579</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> thee, neither<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> shall any plague<sup><a href="h5061.htm">5061</a></sup> come nigh<sup><a href="h7126.htm">7126</a></sup> thy dwelling.<sup><a href="h0168.htm">168</a></sup> </p>
 <p>11  For<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> he shall give his angels<sup><a href="h4397.htm">4397</a></sup> charge<sup><a href="h6680.htm">6680</a></sup> over thee, to keep<sup><a href="h8104.htm">8104</a></sup> thee in all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> thy ways.<sup><a href="h1870.htm">1870</a></sup> </p>
 <p>12  They shall bear thee up<sup><a href="h5375.htm">5375</a></sup> in<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> their hands,<sup><a href="h3709.htm">3709</a></sup> lest<sup><a href="h6435.htm">6435</a></sup> thou dash<sup><a href="h5062.htm">5062</a></sup> thy foot<sup><a href="h7272.htm">7272</a></sup> against a stone.<sup><a href="h0068.htm">68</a></sup> </p>
 <p>13  Thou shalt tread<sup><a href="h1869.htm">1869</a></sup> upon<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> the lion<sup><a href="h7826.htm">7826</a></sup> and adder:<sup><a href="h6620.htm">6620</a></sup> the young lion<sup><a href="h3715.htm">3715</a></sup> and the dragon<sup><a href="h8577.htm">8577</a></sup> shalt thou trample under feet<sup><a href="h7429.htm">7429</a></sup> </p>
 <p>14  Because<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> he hath set his love<sup><a href="h2836.htm">2836</a></sup> upon me, therefore will I deliver<sup><a href="h6403.htm">6403</a></sup> him: I will set him on high,<sup><a href="h7682.htm">7682</a></sup> because<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> he hath known<sup><a href="h3045.htm">3045</a></sup> my name.<sup><a href="h8034.htm">8034</a></sup> </p>
 <p>15  He shall call upon<sup><a href="h7121.htm">7121</a></sup> me, and I will answer<sup><a href="h6030.htm">6030</a></sup> him: I<sup><a href="h0595.htm">595</a></sup> will be with<sup><a href="h5973.htm">5973</a></sup> him in trouble;<sup><a href="h6869.htm">6869</a></sup> I will deliver<sup><a href="h2502.htm">2502</a></sup> him, and honor<sup><a href="h3513.htm">3513</a></sup> him. </p>
 <p>16  With long<sup><a href="h0753.htm">753</a></sup> life<sup><a href="h3117.htm">3117</a></sup> will I satisfy<sup><a href="h7646.htm">7646</a></sup> him, and show<sup><a href="h7200.htm">7200</a></sup> him my salvation.<sup><a href="h3444.htm">3444</a></sup> </p>

 <p align="center"><a href="../sep/psa091.htm">Sep</a></p><p>1 ψαλμὸς ᾠδῆς εἰς τὴν ἡμέραν τοῦ σαββάτου </p>
 <p>2 ἀγαθὸν τὸ ἐξομολογεῖσθαι τῷ κυρίῳ καὶ ψάλλειν τῷ ὀνόματί σου ὕψιστε </p>
 <p>3 τοῦ ἀναγγέλλειν τὸ πρωὶ τὸ ἔλεός σου καὶ τὴν ἀλήθειάν σου κατὰ νύκτα </p>
 <p>4 ἐν δεκαχόρδῳ ψαλτηρίῳ μετ᾽ ᾠδῆς ἐν κιθάρᾳ </p>
 <p>5 ὅτι εὔφρανάς με κύριε ἐν τῷ ποιήματί σου καὶ ἐν τοῖς ἔργοις τῶν χειρῶν σου ἀγαλλιάσομαι </p>
 <p>6 ὡς ἐμεγαλύνθη τὰ ἔργα σου κύριε σφόδρα ἐβαθύνθησαν οἱ διαλογισμοί σου </p>
 <p>7 ἀνὴρ ἄφρων οὐ γνώσεται καὶ ἀσύνετος οὐ συνήσει ταῦτα </p>
 <p>8 ἐν τῷ ἀνατεῖλαι τοὺς ἁμαρτωλοὺς ὡς χόρτον καὶ διέκυψαν πάντες οἱ ἐργαζόμενοι τὴν ἀνομίαν ὅπως ἂν ἐξολεθρευθῶσιν εἰς τὸν αἰῶνα τοῦ αἰῶνος </p>
 <p>9 σὺ δὲ ὕψιστος εἰς τὸν αἰῶνα κύριε </p>
 <p>10 ὅτι ἰδοὺ οἱ ἐχθροί σου ἀπολοῦνται καὶ διασκορπισθήσονται πάντες οἱ ἐργαζόμενοι τὴν ἀνομίαν </p>
 <p>11 καὶ ὑψωθήσεται ὡς μονοκέρωτος τὸ κέρας μου καὶ τὸ γῆράς μου ἐν ἐλαίῳ πίονι </p>
 <p>12 καὶ ἐπεῖδεν ὁ ὀφθαλμός μου ἐν τοῖς ἐχθροῖς μου καὶ ἐν τοῖς ἐπανιστανομένοις ἐπ᾽ ἐμὲ πονηρευομένοις ἀκούσεται τὸ οὖς μου </p>
 <p>13 δίκαιος ὡς φοῖνιξ ἀνθήσει ὡσεὶ κέδρος ἡ ἐν τῷ Λιβάνῳ πληθυνθήσεται </p>
 <p>14 πεφυτευμένοι ἐν τῷ οἴκῳ κυρίου ἐν ταῖς αὐλαῖς τοῦ θεοῦ ἡμῶν ἐξανθήσουσιν </p>
 <p>15 ἔτι πληθυνθήσονται ἐν γήρει πίονι καὶ εὐπαθοῦντες ἔσονται </p>
 <p>16 τοῦ ἀναγγεῖλαι ὅτι εὐθὴς κύριος ὁ θεός μου καὶ οὐκ ἔστιν ἀδικία ἐν αὐτῷ </p>

 <p>7 Vir insipiens non cognoscet,<br> et stultus non intelliget hæc.<br></p>

 <p>10 Quoniam ecce inimici tui, Domine,<br> quoniam ecce inimici tui peribunt;<br> et dispergentur omnes qui operantur iniquitatem.<br></p>
 <p>11 Et exaltabitur sicut unicornis cornu meum,<br> et senectus mea in misericordia uberi.<br></p>
 <p>12 Et despexit oculus meus inimicos meos,<br> et in insurgentibus in me malignantibus audiet auris mea.<br></p>
 <p>13 Justus ut palma florebit;<br> sicut cedrus Libani multiplicabitur.<br></p>
 <p>14 Plantati in domo Domini,<br> in atriis domus Dei nostri florebunt.<br></p>
