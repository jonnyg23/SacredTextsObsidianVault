 <p>2  For,<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> lo,<sup><a href="h2009.htm">2009</a></sup> thine enemies<sup><a href="h0341.htm">341</a></sup> make a tumult:<sup><a href="h1993.htm">1993</a></sup> and they that hate<sup><a href="h8130.htm">8130</a></sup> thee have lifted up<sup><a href="h5375.htm">5375</a></sup> the head.<sup><a href="h7218.htm">7218</a></sup> </p>
 <p>3  They have taken crafty<sup><a href="h6191.htm">6191</a></sup> counsel<sup><a href="h5475.htm">5475</a></sup> against<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> thy people,<sup><a href="h5971.htm">5971</a></sup> and consulted<sup><a href="h3289.htm">3289</a></sup> against<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> thy hidden ones.<sup><a href="h6845.htm">6845</a></sup> </p>
 <p>4  They have said,<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> Come,<sup><a href="h1980.htm">1980</a></sup> and let us cut them off<sup><a href="h3582.htm">3582</a></sup> from being a nation;<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h1471.htm">1471</a></sup> that the name<sup><a href="h8034.htm">8034</a></sup> of Israel<sup><a href="h3478.htm">3478</a></sup> may be no<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> more<sup><a href="h5750.htm">5750</a></sup> in remembrance.<sup><a href="h2142.htm">2142</a></sup> </p>
 <p>5  For<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> they have consulted together<sup><a href="h3289.htm">3289</a></sup> with one consent:<sup><a href="h3820.htm">3820</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3162.htm">3162</a></sup> they are confederate<sup><a href="h1285.htm">1285</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3772.htm">3772</a></sup> against<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> thee: </p>
 <p>6  The tabernacles<sup><a href="h0168.htm">168</a></sup> of Edom,<sup><a href="h0123.htm">123</a></sup> and the Ishmaelites;<sup><a href="h3459.htm">3459</a></sup> of Moab,<sup><a href="h4124.htm">4124</a></sup> and the Hagarenes;<sup><a href="h1905.htm">1905</a></sup> </p>
 <p>7  Gebal,<sup><a href="h1381.htm">1381</a></sup> and Ammon,<sup><a href="h5983.htm">5983</a></sup> and Amalek;<sup><a href="h6002.htm">6002</a></sup> the Philistines<sup><a href="h6429.htm">6429</a></sup> with<sup><a href="h5973.htm">5973</a></sup> the inhabitants<sup><a href="h3427.htm">3427</a></sup> of Tyre;<sup><a href="h6865.htm">6865</a></sup> </p>
 <p>8  Assur<sup><a href="h0804.htm">804</a></sup> also<sup><a href="h1571.htm">1571</a></sup> is joined<sup><a href="h3867.htm">3867</a></sup> with<sup><a href="h5973.htm">5973</a></sup> them: they have<sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> helped<sup><a href="h2220.htm">2220</a></sup> the children<sup><a href="h1121.htm">1121</a></sup> of Lot.<sup><a href="h3876.htm">3876</a></sup> Selah.<sup><a href="h5542.htm">5542</a></sup> </p>
 <p>9  Do<sup><a href="h6213.htm">6213</a></sup> unto them as unto the Midianites;<sup><a href="h4080.htm">4080</a></sup> as to Sisera,<sup><a href="h5516.htm">5516</a></sup> as to Jabin,<sup><a href="h2985.htm">2985</a></sup> at the brook<sup><a href="h5158.htm">5158</a></sup> of Kison:<sup><a href="h7028.htm">7028</a></sup> </p>
 <p>10  Which perished<sup><a href="h8045.htm">8045</a></sup> at Endor:<sup><a href="h5874.htm">5874</a></sup> they became<sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> as dung<sup><a href="h1828.htm">1828</a></sup> for the earth.<sup><a href="h0127.htm">127</a></sup> </p>
 <p>11  Make<sup><a href="h7896.htm">7896</a></sup> their nobles<sup><a href="h5081.htm">5081</a></sup> like Oreb,<sup><a href="h6159.htm">6159</a></sup> and like Zeeb:<sup><a href="h2062.htm">2062</a></sup> yea, all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> their princes<sup><a href="h5257.htm">5257</a></sup> as Zebah,<sup><a href="h2078.htm">2078</a></sup> and as Zalmunna:<sup><a href="h6759.htm">6759</a></sup> </p>
 <p>12  Who<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> said,<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> Let us take<sup><a href="h3423.htm">3423</a></sup> to ourselves <sup><a href="h0853.htm">853</a></sup> the houses<sup><a href="h4999.htm">4999</a></sup> of God<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> in possession. </p>
 <p>13  O my God,<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> make<sup><a href="h7896.htm">7896</a></sup> them like a wheel;<sup><a href="h1534.htm">1534</a></sup> as the stubble<sup><a href="h7179.htm">7179</a></sup> before<sup><a href="h6440.htm">6440</a></sup> the wind.<sup><a href="h7307.htm">7307</a></sup> </p>
 <p>14  As the fire<sup><a href="h0784.htm">784</a></sup> burneth<sup><a href="h1197.htm">1197</a></sup> a wood,<sup><a href="h3293.htm">3293</a></sup> and as the flame<sup><a href="h3852.htm">3852</a></sup> setteth the mountains on fire;<sup><a href="h2022.htm">2022</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3857.htm">3857</a></sup> </p>
 <p>15  So<sup><a href="h3651.htm">3651</a></sup> persecute<sup><a href="h7291.htm">7291</a></sup> them with thy tempest,<sup><a href="h5591.htm">5591</a></sup> and make them afraid<sup><a href="h0926.htm">926</a></sup> with thy storm.<sup><a href="h5492.htm">5492</a></sup> </p>
 <p>16  Fill<sup><a href="h4390.htm">4390</a></sup> their faces<sup><a href="h6440.htm">6440</a></sup> with shame;<sup><a href="h7036.htm">7036</a></sup> that they may seek<sup><a href="h1245.htm">1245</a></sup> thy name,<sup><a href="h8034.htm">8034</a></sup> O LORD.<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> </p>
 <p>17  Let them be confounded<sup><a href="h0954.htm">954</a></sup> and troubled<sup><a href="h0926.htm">926</a></sup> forever;<sup><a href="h5704.htm">5704</a></sup> <sup><a href="h5703.htm">5703</a></sup> yea, let them be put to shame,<sup><a href="h2659.htm">2659</a></sup> and perish:<sup><a href="h0006.htm">6</a></sup> </p>
 <p>18  That men may know<sup><a href="h3045.htm">3045</a></sup> that<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> thou,<sup><a href="h0859.htm">859</a></sup> whose name<sup><a href="h8034.htm">8034</a></sup> alone<sup><a href="h0905.htm">905</a></sup> is JEHOVAH,<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> art the most high<sup><a href="h5945.htm">5945</a></sup> over<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> the earth.<sup><a href="h0776.htm">776</a></sup> </p>

 <p>3 ἐπιποθεῖ καὶ ἐκλείπει ἡ ψυχή μου εἰς τὰς αὐλὰς τοῦ κυρίου ἡ καρδία μου καὶ ἡ σάρξ μου ἠγαλλιάσαντο ἐπὶ θεὸν ζῶντα </p>
 <p>4 καὶ γὰρ στρουθίον εὗρεν ἑαυτῷ οἰκίαν καὶ τρυγὼν νοσσιὰν ἑαυτῇ οὗ θήσει τὰ νοσσία αὐτῆς τὰ θυσιαστήριά σου κύριε τῶν δυνάμεων ὁ βασιλεύς μου καὶ ὁ θεός μου </p>

 <p>6 μακάριος ἀνήρ οὗ ἐστιν ἡ ἀντίλημψις αὐτοῦ παρὰ σοῦ κύριε ἀναβάσεις ἐν τῇ καρδίᾳ αὐτοῦ διέθετο </p>
 <p>7 ἐν τῇ κοιλάδι τοῦ κλαυθμῶνος εἰς τόπον ὃν ἔθετο καὶ γὰρ εὐλογίας δώσει ὁ νομοθετῶν </p>
 <p>8 πορεύσονται ἐκ δυνάμεως εἰς δύναμιν ὀφθήσεται ὁ θεὸς τῶν θεῶν ἐν Σιων </p>
 <p>9 κύριε ὁ θεὸς τῶν δυνάμεων εἰσάκουσον τῆς προσευχῆς μου ἐνώτισαι ὁ θεὸς Ιακωβ διάψαλμα </p>
 <p>10 ὑπερασπιστὰ ἡμῶν ἰδέ ὁ θεός καὶ ἐπίβλεψον ἐπὶ τὸ πρόσωπον τοῦ χριστοῦ σου </p>
 <p>11 ὅτι κρείσσων ἡμέρα μία ἐν ταῖς αὐλαῖς σου ὑπὲρ χιλιάδας ἐξελεξάμην παραρριπτεῖσθαι ἐν τῷ οἴκῳ τοῦ θεοῦ μᾶλλον ἢ οἰκεῖν ἐν σκηνώμασιν ἁμαρτωλῶν </p>
 <p>12 ὅτι ἔλεον καὶ ἀλήθειαν ἀγαπᾷ κύριος ὁ θεός χάριν καὶ δόξαν δώσει κύριος οὐ στερήσει τὰ ἀγαθὰ τοὺς πορευομένους ἐν ἀκακίᾳ </p>
