 <p>2  Blessed<sup><a href="h0835.htm">835</a></sup> is the man<sup><a href="h0120.htm">120</a></sup> unto whom the LORD<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> imputeth<sup><a href="h2803.htm">2803</a></sup> not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> iniquity,<sup><a href="h5771.htm">5771</a></sup> and in whose spirit<sup><a href="h7307.htm">7307</a></sup> there is no<sup><a href="h0369.htm">369</a></sup> guile.<sup><a href="h7423.htm">7423</a></sup> </p>
 <p>3  When<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> I kept silence,<sup><a href="h2790.htm">2790</a></sup> my bones<sup><a href="h6106.htm">6106</a></sup> waxed old<sup><a href="h1086.htm">1086</a></sup> through my roaring<sup><a href="h7581.htm">7581</a></sup> all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> the day<sup><a href="h3117.htm">3117</a></sup> long. </p>
 <p>4  For<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> day<sup><a href="h3119.htm">3119</a></sup> and night<sup><a href="h3915.htm">3915</a></sup> thy hand<sup><a href="h3027.htm">3027</a></sup> was heavy<sup><a href="h3513.htm">3513</a></sup> upon<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> me: my moisture<sup><a href="h3955.htm">3955</a></sup> is turned<sup><a href="h2015.htm">2015</a></sup> into the drought<sup><a href="h2725.htm">2725</a></sup> of summer.<sup><a href="h7019.htm">7019</a></sup> Selah.<sup><a href="h5542.htm">5542</a></sup> </p>
 <p>5  I acknowledged<sup><a href="h3045.htm">3045</a></sup> my sin<sup><a href="h2403.htm">2403</a></sup> unto thee, and mine iniquity<sup><a href="h5771.htm">5771</a></sup> have I not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> hid.<sup><a href="h3680.htm">3680</a></sup> I said,<sup><a href="h0559.htm">559</a></sup> I will confess<sup><a href="h3034.htm">3034</a></sup> my transgressions<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> <sup><a href="h6588.htm">6588</a></sup> unto the LORD;<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> and thou<sup><a href="h0859.htm">859</a></sup> forgavest<sup><a href="h5375.htm">5375</a></sup> the iniquity<sup><a href="h5771.htm">5771</a></sup> of my sin.<sup><a href="h2403.htm">2403</a></sup> Selah.<sup><a href="h5542.htm">5542</a></sup> </p>
 <p>6  For<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> this<sup><a href="h2063.htm">2063</a></sup> shall every<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> one that is godly<sup><a href="h2623.htm">2623</a></sup> pray<sup><a href="h6419.htm">6419</a></sup> unto<sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> thee in a time<sup><a href="h6256.htm">6256</a></sup> when thou mayest be found:<sup><a href="h4672.htm">4672</a></sup> surely<sup><a href="h7535.htm">7535</a></sup> in the floods<sup><a href="h7858.htm">7858</a></sup> of great<sup><a href="h7227.htm">7227</a></sup> waters<sup><a href="h4325.htm">4325</a></sup> they shall not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> come nigh<sup><a href="h5060.htm">5060</a></sup> unto<sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> him. </p>
 <p>7  Thou<sup><a href="h0859.htm">859</a></sup> art my hiding place;<sup><a href="h5643.htm">5643</a></sup> thou shalt preserve<sup><a href="h5341.htm">5341</a></sup> me from trouble;<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h6862.htm">6862</a></sup> thou shalt compass me about<sup><a href="h5437.htm">5437</a></sup> with songs<sup><a href="h7438.htm">7438</a></sup> of deliverance.<sup><a href="h6405.htm">6405</a></sup> Selah.<sup><a href="h5542.htm">5542</a></sup> </p>
 <p>8  I will instruct<sup><a href="h7919.htm">7919</a></sup> thee and teach<sup><a href="h3384.htm">3384</a></sup> thee in the way<sup><a href="h1870.htm">1870</a></sup> which<sup><a href="h2098.htm">2098</a></sup> thou shalt go:<sup><a href="h1980.htm">1980</a></sup> I will guide<sup><a href="h3289.htm">3289</a></sup> <sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> thee with mine eye.<sup><a href="h5869.htm">5869</a></sup> </p>
 <p>9  Be<sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> ye not<sup><a href="h0408.htm">408</a></sup> as the horse,<sup><a href="h5483.htm">5483</a></sup> or as the mule,<sup><a href="h6505.htm">6505</a></sup> which have no<sup><a href="h0369.htm">369</a></sup> understanding:<sup><a href="h0995.htm">995</a></sup> whose mouth<sup><a href="h5716.htm">5716</a></sup> must be held in<sup><a href="h1102.htm">1102</a></sup> with bit<sup><a href="h4964.htm">4964</a></sup> and bridle,<sup><a href="h7448.htm">7448</a></sup> lest<sup><a href="h1077.htm">1077</a></sup> they come near<sup><a href="h7126.htm">7126</a></sup> unto thee.<sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> </p>
 <p>10  Many<sup><a href="h7227.htm">7227</a></sup> sorrows<sup><a href="h4341.htm">4341</a></sup> shall be to the wicked:<sup><a href="h7563.htm">7563</a></sup> but he that trusteth<sup><a href="h0982.htm">982</a></sup> in the LORD,<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> mercy<sup><a href="h2617.htm">2617</a></sup> shall compass him about.<sup><a href="h5437.htm">5437</a></sup> </p>
 <p>11  Be glad<sup><a href="h8055.htm">8055</a></sup> in the LORD,<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> and rejoice,<sup><a href="h1523.htm">1523</a></sup> ye righteous:<sup><a href="h6662.htm">6662</a></sup> and shout for joy,<sup><a href="h7442.htm">7442</a></sup> all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> ye that are upright<sup><a href="h3477.htm">3477</a></sup> in heart.<sup><a href="h3820.htm">3820</a></sup> </p>

 <p align="center"><a href="../sep/psa032.htm">Sep</a></p><p>1 τῷ Δαυιδ ἀγαλλιᾶσθε δίκαιοι ἐν τῷ κυρίῳ τοῖς εὐθέσι πρέπει αἴνεσις </p>
 <p>2 ἐξομολογεῖσθε τῷ κυρίῳ ἐν κιθάρᾳ ἐν ψαλτηρίῳ δεκαχόρδῳ ψάλατε αὐτῷ </p>
 <p>3 ᾄσατε αὐτῷ ᾆσμα καινόν καλῶς ψάλατε ἐν ἀλαλαγμῷ </p>
 <p>4 ὅτι εὐθὴς ὁ λόγος τοῦ κυρίου καὶ πάντα τὰ ἔργα αὐτοῦ ἐν πίστει </p>
 <p>5 ἀγαπᾷ ἐλεημοσύνην καὶ κρίσιν τοῦ ἐλέους κυρίου πλήρης ἡ γῆ </p>
 <p>6 τῷ λόγῳ τοῦ κυρίου οἱ οὐρανοὶ ἐστερεώθησαν καὶ τῷ πνεύματι τοῦ στόματος αὐτοῦ πᾶσα ἡ δύναμις αὐτῶν </p>
 <p>7 συνάγων ὡς ἀσκὸν ὕδατα θαλάσσης τιθεὶς ἐν θησαυροῖς ἀβύσσους </p>
 <p>8 φοβηθήτω τὸν κύριον πᾶσα ἡ γῆ ἀπ᾽ αὐτοῦ δὲ σαλευθήτωσαν πάντες οἱ κατοικοῦντες τὴν οἰκουμένην </p>
 <p>9 ὅτι αὐτὸς εἶπεν καὶ ἐγενήθησαν αὐτὸς ἐνετείλατο καὶ ἐκτίσθησαν </p>
 <p>10 κύριος διασκεδάζει βουλὰς ἐθνῶν ἀθετεῖ δὲ λογισμοὺς λαῶν καὶ ἀθετεῖ βουλὰς ἀρχόντων </p>
 <p>11 ἡ δὲ βουλὴ τοῦ κυρίου εἰς τὸν αἰῶνα μένει λογισμοὶ τῆς καρδίας αὐτοῦ εἰς γενεὰν καὶ γενεάν </p>
 <p>12 μακάριον τὸ ἔθνος οὗ ἐστιν κύριος ὁ θεὸς αὐτοῦ λαός ὃν ἐξελέξατο εἰς κληρονομίαν ἑαυτῷ </p>
 <p>13 ἐξ οὐρανοῦ ἐπέβλεψεν ὁ κύριος εἶδεν πάντας τοὺς υἱοὺς τῶν ἀνθρώπων </p>
 <p>14 ἐξ ἑτοίμου κατοικητηρίου αὐτοῦ ἐπέβλεψεν ἐπὶ πάντας τοὺς κατοικοῦντας τὴν γῆν </p>
 <p>15 ὁ πλάσας κατὰ μόνας τὰς καρδίας αὐτῶν ὁ συνιεὶς εἰς πάντα τὰ ἔργα αὐτῶν </p>
 <p>16 οὐ σῴζεται βασιλεὺς διὰ πολλὴν δύναμιν καὶ γίγας οὐ σωθήσεται ἐν πλήθει ἰσχύος αὐτοῦ </p>
 <p>17 ψευδὴς ἵππος εἰς σωτηρίαν ἐν δὲ πλήθει δυνάμεως αὐτοῦ οὐ σωθήσεται </p>
 <p>18 ἰδοὺ οἱ ὀφθαλμοὶ κυρίου ἐπὶ τοὺς φοβουμένους αὐτὸν τοὺς ἐλπίζοντας ἐπὶ τὸ ἔλεος αὐτοῦ </p>
 <p>19 ῥύσασθαι ἐκ θανάτου τὰς ψυχὰς αὐτῶν καὶ διαθρέψαι αὐτοὺς ἐν λιμῷ </p>
 <p>20 ἡ ψυχὴ ἡμῶν ὑπομένει τῷ κυρίῳ ὅτι βοηθὸς καὶ ὑπερασπιστὴς ἡμῶν ἐστιν </p>
 <p>21 ὅτι ἐν αὐτῷ εὐφρανθήσεται ἡ καρδία ἡμῶν καὶ ἐν τῷ ὀνόματι τῷ ἁγίῳ αὐτοῦ ἠλπίσαμεν </p>
 <p>22 γένοιτο τὸ ἔλεός σου κύριε ἐφ᾽ ἡμᾶς καθάπερ ἠλπίσαμεν ἐπὶ σέ </p>

 <p align="center"><a href="../vul/psa032.htm">Vul</a></p><p>1 Psalmus David. [Exsultate, justi, in Domino;<br> rectos decet collaudatio.<br></p>
 <p>2 Confitemini Domino in cithara;<br> in psalterio decem chordarum psallite illi.<br></p>
 <p>3 Cantate ei canticum novum;<br> bene psallite ei in vociferatione.<br></p>
 <p>4 Quia rectum est verbum Domini,<br> et omnia opera ejus in fide.<br></p>
 <p>5 Diligit misericordiam et judicium;<br> misericordia Domini plena est terra.<br></p>
 <p>6 Verbo Domini cæli firmati sunt,<br> et spiritu oris ejus omnis virtus eorum.<br></p>
 <p>7 Congregans sicut in utre aquas maris;<br> ponens in thesauris abyssos.<br></p>
 <p>8 Timeat Dominum omnis terra;<br> ab eo autem commoveantur omnes inhabitantes orbem.<br></p>
 <p>9 Quoniam ipse dixit, et facta sunt;<br> ipse mandavit et creata sunt.<br></p>
 <p>10 Dominus dissipat consilia gentium;<br> reprobat autem cogitationes populorum,<br> et reprobat consilia principum.<br></p>
 <p>11 Consilium autem Domini in æternum manet;<br> cogitationes cordis ejus in generatione et generationem.<br></p>
 <p>12 Beata gens cujus est Dominus Deus ejus;<br> populus quem elegit in hæreditatem sibi.<br></p>
 <p>13 De cælo respexit Dominus;<br> vidit omnes filios hominum.<br></p>
 <p>14 De præparato habitaculo suo<br> respexit super omnes qui habitant terram:<br></p>
 <p>15 qui finxit sigillatim corda eorum;<br> qui intelligit omnia opera eorum.<br></p>
 <p>16 Non salvatur rex per multam virtutem,<br> et gigas non salvabitur in multitudine virtutis suæ.<br></p>
 <p>17 Fallax equus ad salutem;<br> in abundantia autem virtutis suæ non salvabitur.<br></p>
 <p>18 Ecce oculi Domini super metuentes eum,<br> et in eis qui sperant super misericordia ejus:<br></p>
 <p>19 ut eruat a morte animas eorum,<br> et alat eos in fame.<br></p>
 <p>20 Anima nostra sustinet Dominum,<br> quoniam adjutor et protector noster est.<br></p>
 <p>21 Quia in eo lætabitur cor nostrum,<br> et in nomine sancto ejus speravimus.<br></p>
 <p>22 Fiat misericordia tua, Domine, super nos,<br> quemadmodum speravimus in te.]</p>
