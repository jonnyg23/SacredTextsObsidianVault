 <p>2  I sink<sup><a href="h2883.htm">2883</a></sup> in deep<sup><a href="h4688.htm">4688</a></sup> mire,<sup><a href="h3121.htm">3121</a></sup> where there is no<sup><a href="h0369.htm">369</a></sup> standing:<sup><a href="h4613.htm">4613</a></sup> I am come<sup><a href="h0935.htm">935</a></sup> into deep<sup><a href="h4615.htm">4615</a></sup> waters,<sup><a href="h4325.htm">4325</a></sup> where the floods<sup><a href="h7641.htm">7641</a></sup> overflow<sup><a href="h7857.htm">7857</a></sup> me. </p>
 <p>3  I am weary<sup><a href="h3021.htm">3021</a></sup> of my crying:<sup><a href="h7121.htm">7121</a></sup> my throat<sup><a href="h1627.htm">1627</a></sup> is dried:<sup><a href="h2787.htm">2787</a></sup> mine eyes<sup><a href="h5869.htm">5869</a></sup> fail<sup><a href="h3615.htm">3615</a></sup> while I wait<sup><a href="h3176.htm">3176</a></sup> for my God.<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> </p>
 <p>4  They that hate<sup><a href="h8130.htm">8130</a></sup> me without a cause<sup><a href="h2600.htm">2600</a></sup> are more<sup><a href="h7231.htm">7231</a></sup> than the hairs<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h8185.htm">8185</a></sup> of mine head:<sup><a href="h7218.htm">7218</a></sup> they that would destroy<sup><a href="h6789.htm">6789</a></sup> me, being mine enemies<sup><a href="h0341.htm">341</a></sup> wrongfully,<sup><a href="h8267.htm">8267</a></sup> are mighty:<sup><a href="h6105.htm">6105</a></sup> then<sup><a href="h0227.htm">227</a></sup> I restored<sup><a href="h7725.htm">7725</a></sup> that which<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> I took not away.<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> <sup><a href="h1497.htm">1497</a></sup> </p>
 <p>5  O God,<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> thou<sup><a href="h0859.htm">859</a></sup> knowest<sup><a href="h3045.htm">3045</a></sup> my foolishness;<sup><a href="h0200.htm">200</a></sup> and my sins<sup><a href="h0819.htm">819</a></sup> are not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> hid<sup><a href="h3582.htm">3582</a></sup> from<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> thee. </p>
 <p>6  Let not<sup><a href="h0408.htm">408</a></sup> them that wait on<sup><a href="h6960.htm">6960</a></sup> thee, O Lord<sup><a href="h0136.htm">136</a></sup> GOD<sup><a href="h3069.htm">3069</a></sup> of hosts,<sup><a href="h6635.htm">6635</a></sup> be ashamed<sup><a href="h0954.htm">954</a></sup> for my sake: let not<sup><a href="h0408.htm">408</a></sup> those that seek<sup><a href="h1245.htm">1245</a></sup> thee be confounded<sup><a href="h3637.htm">3637</a></sup> for my sake, O God<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> of Israel.<sup><a href="h3478.htm">3478</a></sup> </p>
 <p>7  Because<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> for thy sake<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> I have borne<sup><a href="h5375.htm">5375</a></sup> reproach;<sup><a href="h2781.htm">2781</a></sup> shame<sup><a href="h3639.htm">3639</a></sup> hath covered<sup><a href="h3680.htm">3680</a></sup> my face.<sup><a href="h6440.htm">6440</a></sup> </p>
 <p>8  I am become<sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> a stranger<sup><a href="h2114.htm">2114</a></sup> unto my brethren,<sup><a href="h0251.htm">251</a></sup> and an alien<sup><a href="h5237.htm">5237</a></sup> unto my mother's<sup><a href="h0517.htm">517</a></sup> children.<sup><a href="h1121.htm">1121</a></sup> </p>
 <p>9  For<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> the zeal<sup><a href="h7068.htm">7068</a></sup> of thine house<sup><a href="h1004.htm">1004</a></sup> hath eaten me up;<sup><a href="h0398.htm">398</a></sup> and the reproaches<sup><a href="h2781.htm">2781</a></sup> of them that reproached<sup><a href="h2778.htm">2778</a></sup> thee are fallen<sup><a href="h5307.htm">5307</a></sup> upon<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> me. </p>
 <p>10  When I wept,<sup><a href="h1058.htm">1058</a></sup> and chastened my soul<sup><a href="h5315.htm">5315</a></sup> with fasting,<sup><a href="h6685.htm">6685</a></sup> that was<sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> to my reproach.<sup><a href="h2781.htm">2781</a></sup> </p>
 <p>11  I made<sup><a href="h5414.htm">5414</a></sup> sackcloth<sup><a href="h8242.htm">8242</a></sup> also my garment;<sup><a href="h3830.htm">3830</a></sup> and I became<sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> a proverb<sup><a href="h4912.htm">4912</a></sup> to them. </p>
 <p>12  They that sit<sup><a href="h3427.htm">3427</a></sup> in the gate<sup><a href="h8179.htm">8179</a></sup> speak<sup><a href="h7878.htm">7878</a></sup> against me; and I was the song<sup><a href="h5058.htm">5058</a></sup> of the drunkards.<sup><a href="h8354.htm">8354</a></sup> <sup><a href="h7941.htm">7941</a></sup> </p>
 <p>13  But as for me,<sup><a href="h0589.htm">589</a></sup> my prayer<sup><a href="h8605.htm">8605</a></sup> is unto thee, O LORD,<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> in an acceptable<sup><a href="h7522.htm">7522</a></sup> time:<sup><a href="h6256.htm">6256</a></sup> O God,<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> in the multitude<sup><a href="h7230.htm">7230</a></sup> of thy mercy<sup><a href="h2617.htm">2617</a></sup> hear<sup><a href="h6030.htm">6030</a></sup> me, in the truth<sup><a href="h0571.htm">571</a></sup> of thy salvation.<sup><a href="h3468.htm">3468</a></sup> </p>
 <p>14  Deliver<sup><a href="h5337.htm">5337</a></sup> me out of the mire,<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h2916.htm">2916</a></sup> and let me not<sup><a href="h0408.htm">408</a></sup> sink:<sup><a href="h2883.htm">2883</a></sup> let me be delivered<sup><a href="h5337.htm">5337</a></sup> from them that hate<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h8130.htm">8130</a></sup> me, and out of the deep<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h4615.htm">4615</a></sup> waters.<sup><a href="h4325.htm">4325</a></sup> </p>
 <p>15  Let not<sup><a href="h0408.htm">408</a></sup> the waterflood<sup><a href="h7641.htm">7641</a></sup> <sup><a href="h4325.htm">4325</a></sup> overflow<sup><a href="h7857.htm">7857</a></sup> me, neither<sup><a href="h0408.htm">408</a></sup> let the deep<sup><a href="h4688.htm">4688</a></sup> swallow me up,<sup><a href="h1104.htm">1104</a></sup> and let not<sup><a href="h0408.htm">408</a></sup> the pit<sup><a href="h0875.htm">875</a></sup> shut<sup><a href="h0332.htm">332</a></sup> her mouth<sup><a href="h6310.htm">6310</a></sup> upon<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> me. </p>
 <p>16  Hear<sup><a href="h6030.htm">6030</a></sup> me, O LORD;<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> for<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> thy lovingkindness<sup><a href="h2617.htm">2617</a></sup> is good:<sup><a href="h2896.htm">2896</a></sup> turn<sup><a href="h6437.htm">6437</a></sup> unto<sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> me according to the multitude<sup><a href="h7230.htm">7230</a></sup> of thy tender mercies.<sup><a href="h7356.htm">7356</a></sup> </p>
 <p>17  And hide<sup><a href="h5641.htm">5641</a></sup> not<sup><a href="h0408.htm">408</a></sup> thy face<sup><a href="h6440.htm">6440</a></sup> from thy servant;<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h5650.htm">5650</a></sup> for<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> I am in trouble:<sup><a href="h6862.htm">6862</a></sup> hear<sup><a href="h6030.htm">6030</a></sup> me speedily.<sup><a href="h4116.htm">4116</a></sup> </p>
 <p>18  Draw nigh<sup><a href="h7126.htm">7126</a></sup> unto<sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> my soul,<sup><a href="h5315.htm">5315</a></sup> and redeem<sup><a href="h1350.htm">1350</a></sup> it: deliver<sup><a href="h6299.htm">6299</a></sup> me because of<sup><a href="h4616.htm">4616</a></sup> mine enemies.<sup><a href="h0341.htm">341</a></sup> </p>
 <p>19  Thou<sup><a href="h0859.htm">859</a></sup> hast known<sup><a href="h3045.htm">3045</a></sup> my reproach,<sup><a href="h2781.htm">2781</a></sup> and my shame,<sup><a href="h1322.htm">1322</a></sup> and my dishonor:<sup><a href="h3639.htm">3639</a></sup> mine adversaries<sup><a href="h6887.htm">6887</a></sup> are all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> before<sup><a href="h5048.htm">5048</a></sup> thee. </p>
 <p>20  Reproach<sup><a href="h2781.htm">2781</a></sup> hath broken<sup><a href="h7665.htm">7665</a></sup> my heart;<sup><a href="h3820.htm">3820</a></sup> and I am full of heaviness:<sup><a href="h5136.htm">5136</a></sup> and I looked<sup><a href="h6960.htm">6960</a></sup> for some to take pity,<sup><a href="h5110.htm">5110</a></sup> but there was none;<sup><a href="h0369.htm">369</a></sup> and for comforters,<sup><a href="h5162.htm">5162</a></sup> but I found<sup><a href="h4672.htm">4672</a></sup> none.<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> </p>
 <p>21  They gave<sup><a href="h5414.htm">5414</a></sup> me also gall<sup><a href="h7219.htm">7219</a></sup> for my meat;<sup><a href="h1267.htm">1267</a></sup> and in my thirst<sup><a href="h6772.htm">6772</a></sup> they gave me vinegar<sup><a href="h2558.htm">2558</a></sup> to drink.<sup><a href="h8248.htm">8248</a></sup> </p>
 <p>22  Let their table<sup><a href="h7979.htm">7979</a></sup> become<sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> a snare<sup><a href="h6341.htm">6341</a></sup> before<sup><a href="h6440.htm">6440</a></sup> them: and that which should have been for their welfare,<sup><a href="h7965.htm">7965</a></sup> let it become a trap.<sup><a href="h4170.htm">4170</a></sup> </p>
 <p>23  Let their eyes<sup><a href="h5869.htm">5869</a></sup> be darkened,<sup><a href="h2821.htm">2821</a></sup> that they see not;<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h7200.htm">7200</a></sup> and make their loins<sup><a href="h4975.htm">4975</a></sup> continually<sup><a href="h8548.htm">8548</a></sup> to shake.<sup><a href="h4571.htm">4571</a></sup> </p>
 <p>24  Pour out<sup><a href="h8210.htm">8210</a></sup> thine indignation<sup><a href="h2195.htm">2195</a></sup> upon<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> them, and let thy wrathful<sup><a href="h2740.htm">2740</a></sup> anger<sup><a href="h0639.htm">639</a></sup> take hold<sup><a href="h5381.htm">5381</a></sup> of them. </p>
 <p>25  Let their habitation<sup><a href="h2918.htm">2918</a></sup> be<sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> desolate;<sup><a href="h8074.htm">8074</a></sup> and let<sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> none<sup><a href="h0408.htm">408</a></sup> dwell<sup><a href="h3427.htm">3427</a></sup> in their tents.<sup><a href="h0168.htm">168</a></sup> </p>
 <p>26  For<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> they persecute<sup><a href="h7291.htm">7291</a></sup> him whom<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> thou<sup><a href="h0859.htm">859</a></sup> hast smitten;<sup><a href="h5221.htm">5221</a></sup> and they talk<sup><a href="h5608.htm">5608</a></sup> to<sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> the grief<sup><a href="h4341.htm">4341</a></sup> of those whom thou hast wounded.<sup><a href="h2491.htm">2491</a></sup> </p>

 <p>31  This also shall please<sup><a href="h3190.htm">3190</a></sup> the LORD<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> better than an ox<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h7794.htm">7794</a></sup> or bullock<sup><a href="h6499.htm">6499</a></sup> that hath horns<sup><a href="h7160.htm">7160</a></sup> and hooves.<sup><a href="h6536.htm">6536</a></sup> </p>
 <p>32  The humble<sup><a href="h6035.htm">6035</a></sup> shall see<sup><a href="h7200.htm">7200</a></sup> this, and be glad:<sup><a href="h8055.htm">8055</a></sup> and your heart<sup><a href="h3824.htm">3824</a></sup> shall live<sup><a href="h2421.htm">2421</a></sup> that seek<sup><a href="h1875.htm">1875</a></sup> God.<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> </p>

 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎7 ‏אַל־יֵ֘בֹ֤שׁוּ בִ֨י׀ קוֶֹיךָ֮ אֲדֹנָ֥י יְהוִ֗ה צְבָ֫א֥וֹת אַל־יִכָּ֣לְמוּ בִ֣י מְבַקְשֶׁ֑יךָ אֱ֝לֹהֵ֗י יִשְׂרָאֵֽל׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎8 ‏כִּֽי־עָ֭לֶיךָ נָשָׂ֣אתִי חֶרְפָּ֑ה כִּסְּתָ֖ה כְלִמָּ֣ה פָנָֽי׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎9 ‏מ֖וּזָר<font color="red"><sup>1</sup></font> הָיִ֣יתִי לְאֶחָ֑י וְ֝נָכְרִ֗י לִבְנֵ֥י אִמִּֽי׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎10 ‏כִּֽי־קִנְאַ֣ת בֵּיתְךָ֣ אֲכָלָ֑תְנִי וְחֶרְפּ֥וֹת ח֝וֹרְפֶ֗יךָ נָפְל֥וּ עָלָֽי׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎11 ‏וָאֶבְכֶּ֣ה בַצּ֣וֹם נַפְשִׁ֑י וַתְּהִ֖י לַחֲרָפ֣וֹת לִֽי׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎12 ‏וָאֶתְּנָ֣ה לְבוּשִׁ֣י שָׂ֑ק וָאֱהִ֖י לָהֶ֣ם לְמָשָֽׁל׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎13 ‏יָשִׂ֣יחוּ בִ֭י יֹ֣שְׁבֵי שָׁ֑עַר וּ֝נְגִינ֗וֹת שׁוֹתֵ֥י שֵׁכָֽר׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎14 ‏וַאֲנִ֤י תְפִלָּתִֽי־לְךָ֨׀<font color="red"><sup>3</sup></font> יְהוָ֡ה עֵ֤ת רָצ֗וֹן אֱלֹהִ֥ים בְּרָב־חַסְדֶּ֑ךָ עֲ֝נֵ֗נִי בֶּאֱמֶ֥ת יִשְׁעֶֽךָ׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎15 ‏הַצִּילֵ֣נִי מִ֭טִּיט וְאַל־אֶטְבָּ֑עָה אִנָּצְלָ֥ה מִ֝שֹּֽׂנְאַ֗י וּמִמַּֽעֲמַקֵּי־מָֽיִם׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎16 ‏אַל־תִּשְׁטְפֵ֤נִי׀ שִׁבֹּ֣לֶת מַ֭יִם וְאַל־תִּבְלָעֵ֣נִי מְצוּלָ֑ה וְאַל־תֶּאְטַר־עָלַ֖י בְּאֵ֣ר פִּֽיהָ׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎17 ‏עֲנֵ֣נִי יְ֭הוָה כִּי־ט֣וֹב חַסְדֶּ֑ךָ כְּרֹ֥ב רַ֝חֲמֶ֗יךָ פְּנֵ֣ה אֵלָֽי׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎18 ‏וְאַל־תַּסְתֵּ֣ר פָּ֭נֶיךָ מֵֽעַבְדֶּ֑ךָ כִּֽי־צַר־לִ֝֗י מַהֵ֥ר עֲנֵֽנִי׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎19 ‏קָרְבָ֣ה אֶל־נַפְשִׁ֣י גְאָלָ֑הּ לְמַ֖עַן אֹיְבַ֣י פְּדֵֽנִי׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎20 ‏אַתָּ֤ה יָדַ֗עְתָּ חֶרְפָּתִ֣י וּ֭בָשְׁתִּי וּכְלִמָּתִ֑י נֶ֝גְדְּךָ֗ כָּל־צוֹרְרָֽי׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎21 ‏חֶרְפָּ֤ה׀ שָֽׁבְרָ֥ה לִבִּ֗י וָֽאָ֫נ֥וּשָׁה וָאֲקַוֶּ֣ה לָנ֣וּד וָאַ֑יִן וְ֝לַמְנַחֲמִ֗ים וְלֹ֣א מָצָֽאתִי׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎22 ‏וַיִּתְּנ֣וּ בְּבָרוּתִ֣י רֹ֑אשׁ וְ֝לִצְמָאִ֗י יַשְׁק֥וּנִי חֹֽמֶץ׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎23 ‏יְהִֽי־שֻׁלְחָנָ֣ם לִפְנֵיהֶ֣ם לְפָ֑ח וְלִשְׁלוֹמִ֥ים לְמוֹקֵֽשׁ׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎24 ‏תֶּחְשַׁ֣כְנָה עֵ֭ינֵיהֶם מֵרְא֑וֹת וּ֝מָתְנֵ֗יהֶם תָּמִ֥יד הַמְעַֽד׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎25 ‏שְׁפָךְ־עֲלֵיהֶ֥ם זַעְמֶ֑ךָ וַחֲר֥וֹן אַ֝פְּךָ֗ יַשִּׂיגֵֽם׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎26 ‏תְּהִי־טִֽירָתָ֥ם נְשַׁמָּ֑ה בְּ֝אָהֳלֵיהֶ֗ם אַל־יְהִ֥י יֹשֵֽׁב׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎27 ‏כִּֽי־אַתָּ֣ה אֲשֶׁר־הִכִּ֣יתָ רָדָ֑פוּ וְאֶל־מַכְא֖וֹב חֲלָלֶ֣יךָ יְסַפֵּֽרוּ׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎28 ‏תְּֽנָה־עָ֭וֹן עַל־עֲוֹנָ֑ם וְאַל־יָ֝בֹ֗אוּ בְּצִדְקָתֶֽךָ׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎29 ‏יִ֭מָּחֽוּ מִסֵּ֣פֶר חַיִּ֑ים וְעִ֥ם צַ֝דִּיקִ֗ים אַל־יִכָּתֵֽבוּ׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎30 ‏וַ֭אֲנִי עָנִ֣י וְכוֹאֵ֑ב יְשׁוּעָתְךָ֖ אֱלֹהִ֣ים תְּשַׂגְּבֵֽנִי׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎31 ‏אֲהַֽלְלָ֣ה שֵׁם־אֱלֹהִ֣ים בְּשִׁ֑יר וַאֲגַדְּלֶ֥נּוּ בְתוֹדָֽה׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎32 ‏וְתִיטַ֣ב לַֽ֭יהוָה מִשּׁ֥וֹר פָּ֗ר מַקְרִ֥ן מַפְרִֽיס׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎33 ‏רָא֣וּ עֲנָוִ֣ים יִשְׂמָ֑חוּ דֹּרְשֵׁ֥י אֱ֝לֹהִ֗ים וִיחִ֥י לְבַבְכֶֽם׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎34 ‏כִּֽי־שֹׁמֵ֣עַ אֶל־אֶבְיוֹנִ֣ים יְהוָ֑ה וְאֶת־אֲ֝סִירָ֗יו לֹ֣א בָזָֽה׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎35 ‏יְֽ֭הַלְלוּהוּ שָׁמַ֣יִם וָאָ֑רֶץ יַ֝מִּ֗ים וְֽכָל־רֹמֵ֥שׂ בָּֽם׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎36 ‏כִּ֤י אֱלֹהִ֨ים׀ י֘וֹשִׁ֤יעַ צִיּ֗וֹן וְ֭יִבְנֶה עָרֵ֣י יְהוּדָ֑ה וְיָ֥שְׁבוּ שָׁ֝֗ם וִירֵשֽׁוּהָ׃ </span></p>
 <p align="RIGHT"><span lang="he">‎37 ‏וְזֶ֣רַע עֲ֭בָדָיו יִנְחָל֑וּהָ וְאֹהֲבֵ֥י שְׁ֝מ֗וֹ יִשְׁכְּנוּ־בָֽהּ׃ </span></p>
 </td>
