 <td valign="top">
 <p align="center"><a href="../kjv/isa034.htm">KJV</a></p><p>1  Come near,<sup><a href="h7126.htm">7126</a></sup> ye nations,<sup><a href="h1471.htm">1471</a></sup> to hear;<sup><a href="h8085.htm">8085</a></sup> and hearken,<sup><a href="h7181.htm">7181</a></sup> ye people:<sup><a href="h3816.htm">3816</a></sup> let the earth<sup><a href="h0776.htm">776</a></sup> hear,<sup><a href="h8085.htm">8085</a></sup> and all that is therein;<sup><a href="h4393.htm">4393</a></sup> the world,<sup><a href="h8398.htm">8398</a></sup> and all things<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> that come forth<sup><a href="h6631.htm">6631</a></sup> of it. </p>
 <p>2  For<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> the indignation<sup><a href="h7110.htm">7110</a></sup> of the LORD<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> is upon<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> nations,<sup><a href="h1471.htm">1471</a></sup> and his fury<sup><a href="h2534.htm">2534</a></sup> upon<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> their armies:<sup><a href="h6635.htm">6635</a></sup> he hath utterly destroyed<sup><a href="h2763.htm">2763</a></sup> them, he hath delivered<sup><a href="h5414.htm">5414</a></sup> them to the slaughter.<sup><a href="h2874.htm">2874</a></sup> </p>
 <p>3  Their slain<sup><a href="h2491.htm">2491</a></sup> also shall be cast out,<sup><a href="h7993.htm">7993</a></sup> and their stink<sup><a href="h0889.htm">889</a></sup> shall come up<sup><a href="h5927.htm">5927</a></sup> out of their carcasses,<sup><a href="h6297.htm">6297</a></sup> and the mountains<sup><a href="h2022.htm">2022</a></sup> shall be melted<sup><a href="h4549.htm">4549</a></sup> with their blood.<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h1818.htm">1818</a></sup> </p>
 <p>4  And all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> the host<sup><a href="h6635.htm">6635</a></sup> of heaven<sup><a href="h8064.htm">8064</a></sup> shall be dissolved,<sup><a href="h4743.htm">4743</a></sup> and the heavens<sup><a href="h8064.htm">8064</a></sup> shall be rolled together<sup><a href="h1556.htm">1556</a></sup> as a scroll:<sup><a href="h5612.htm">5612</a></sup> and all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> their host<sup><a href="h6635.htm">6635</a></sup> shall fall down,<sup><a href="h5034.htm">5034</a></sup> as the leaf<sup><a href="h5929.htm">5929</a></sup> falleth off<sup><a href="h5034.htm">5034</a></sup> from the vine,<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h1612.htm">1612</a></sup> and as a falling<sup><a href="h5034.htm">5034</a></sup> fig from the fig tree.<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h8384.htm">8384</a></sup> </p>
 <p>5  For<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> my sword<sup><a href="h2719.htm">2719</a></sup> shall be bathed<sup><a href="h7301.htm">7301</a></sup> in heaven:<sup><a href="h8064.htm">8064</a></sup> behold,<sup><a href="h2009.htm">2009</a></sup> it shall come down<sup><a href="h3381.htm">3381</a></sup> upon<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> Idumea,<sup><a href="h0123.htm">123</a></sup> and upon<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> the people<sup><a href="h5971.htm">5971</a></sup> of my curse,<sup><a href="h2764.htm">2764</a></sup> to judgment.<sup><a href="h4941.htm">4941</a></sup> </p>
 <p>6  The sword<sup><a href="h2719.htm">2719</a></sup> of the LORD<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> is filled<sup><a href="h4390.htm">4390</a></sup> with blood,<sup><a href="h1818.htm">1818</a></sup> it is made fat<sup><a href="h1878.htm">1878</a></sup> with fatness,<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h2459.htm">2459</a></sup> and with the blood<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h1818.htm">1818</a></sup> of lambs<sup><a href="h3733.htm">3733</a></sup> and goats,<sup><a href="h6260.htm">6260</a></sup> with the fat<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h2459.htm">2459</a></sup> of the kidneys<sup><a href="h3629.htm">3629</a></sup> of rams:<sup><a href="h0352.htm">352</a></sup> for<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> the LORD<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> hath a sacrifice<sup><a href="h2077.htm">2077</a></sup> in Bozrah,<sup><a href="h1224.htm">1224</a></sup> and a great<sup><a href="h1419.htm">1419</a></sup> slaughter<sup><a href="h2874.htm">2874</a></sup> in the land<sup><a href="h0776.htm">776</a></sup> of Idumea.<sup><a href="h0123.htm">123</a></sup> </p>
 <p>7  And the unicorns<sup><a href="h7214.htm">7214</a></sup> shall come down<sup><a href="h3381.htm">3381</a></sup> with<sup><a href="h5973.htm">5973</a></sup> them, and the bullocks<sup><a href="h6499.htm">6499</a></sup> with<sup><a href="h5973.htm">5973</a></sup> the bulls;<sup><a href="h0047.htm">47</a></sup> and their land<sup><a href="h0776.htm">776</a></sup> shall be soaked<sup><a href="h7301.htm">7301</a></sup> with blood,<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h1818.htm">1818</a></sup> and their dust<sup><a href="h6083.htm">6083</a></sup> made fat<sup><a href="h1878.htm">1878</a></sup> with fatness.<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h2459.htm">2459</a></sup> </p>
 <p>8  For<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> it is the day<sup><a href="h3117.htm">3117</a></sup> of the LORD's<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> vengeance,<sup><a href="h5359.htm">5359</a></sup> and the year<sup><a href="h8141.htm">8141</a></sup> of recompenses<sup><a href="h7966.htm">7966</a></sup> for the controversy<sup><a href="h7379.htm">7379</a></sup> of Zion.<sup><a href="h6726.htm">6726</a></sup> </p>
 <p>9  And the streams<sup><a href="h5158.htm">5158</a></sup> thereof shall be turned<sup><a href="h2015.htm">2015</a></sup> into pitch,<sup><a href="h2203.htm">2203</a></sup> and the dust<sup><a href="h6083.htm">6083</a></sup> thereof into brimstone,<sup><a href="h1614.htm">1614</a></sup> and the land<sup><a href="h0776.htm">776</a></sup> thereof shall become<sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> burning<sup><a href="h1197.htm">1197</a></sup> pitch.<sup><a href="h2203.htm">2203</a></sup> </p>
 <p>10  It shall not<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> be quenched<sup><a href="h3518.htm">3518</a></sup> night<sup><a href="h3915.htm">3915</a></sup> nor day;<sup><a href="h3119.htm">3119</a></sup> the smoke<sup><a href="h6227.htm">6227</a></sup> thereof shall go up<sup><a href="h5927.htm">5927</a></sup> forever:<sup><a href="h5769.htm">5769</a></sup> from generation<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h1755.htm">1755</a></sup> to generation<sup><a href="h1755.htm">1755</a></sup> it shall lie waste;<sup><a href="h2717.htm">2717</a></sup> none<sup><a href="h0369.htm">369</a></sup> shall pass through<sup><a href="h5674.htm">5674</a></sup> it forever<sup><a href="h5331.htm">5331</a></sup> and ever.<sup><a href="h5331.htm">5331</a></sup> </p>
 <p>11  But the cormorant<sup><a href="h6893.htm">6893</a></sup> and the bittern<sup><a href="h7090.htm">7090</a></sup> shall possess<sup><a href="h3423.htm">3423</a></sup> it; the owl<sup><a href="h3244.htm">3244</a></sup> also and the raven<sup><a href="h6158.htm">6158</a></sup> shall dwell<sup><a href="h7931.htm">7931</a></sup> in it: and he shall stretch out<sup><a href="h5186.htm">5186</a></sup> upon<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> it the line<sup><a href="h6957.htm">6957</a></sup> of confusion,<sup><a href="h8414.htm">8414</a></sup> and the stones<sup><a href="h0068.htm">68</a></sup> of emptiness.<sup><a href="h0922.htm">922</a></sup> </p>
 <p>12  They shall call<sup><a href="h7121.htm">7121</a></sup> the nobles<sup><a href="h2715.htm">2715</a></sup> thereof to the kingdom,<sup><a href="h4410.htm">4410</a></sup> but none<sup><a href="h0369.htm">369</a></sup> shall be there,<sup><a href="h8033.htm">8033</a></sup> and all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> her princes<sup><a href="h8269.htm">8269</a></sup> shall be<sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> nothing.<sup><a href="h0657.htm">657</a></sup> </p>
 <p>13  And thorns<sup><a href="h5518.htm">5518</a></sup> shall come up<sup><a href="h5927.htm">5927</a></sup> in her palaces,<sup><a href="h0759.htm">759</a></sup> nettles<sup><a href="h7057.htm">7057</a></sup> and brambles<sup><a href="h2336.htm">2336</a></sup> in the fortresses<sup><a href="h4013.htm">4013</a></sup> thereof: and it shall be<sup><a href="h1961.htm">1961</a></sup> a habitation<sup><a href="h5116.htm">5116</a></sup> of dragons,<sup><a href="h8565.htm">8565</a></sup> and a court<sup><a href="h2681.htm">2681</a></sup> for owls.<sup><a href="h1323.htm">1323</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3284.htm">3284</a></sup> </p>
 <p>14  The wild beasts of the desert<sup><a href="h6728.htm">6728</a></sup> shall also meet<sup><a href="h6298.htm">6298</a></sup> with<sup><a href="h0854.htm">854</a></sup> the wild beasts of the island,<sup><a href="h0338.htm">338</a></sup> and the satyr<sup><a href="h8163.htm">8163</a></sup> shall cry<sup><a href="h7121.htm">7121</a></sup> to<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> his fellow;<sup><a href="h7453.htm">7453</a></sup> the screech owl<sup><a href="h3917.htm">3917</a></sup> also<sup><a href="h0389.htm">389</a></sup> shall rest<sup><a href="h7280.htm">7280</a></sup> there,<sup><a href="h8033.htm">8033</a></sup> and find<sup><a href="h4672.htm">4672</a></sup> for herself a place of rest.<sup><a href="h4494.htm">4494</a></sup> </p>
 <p>15  There<sup><a href="h8033.htm">8033</a></sup> shall the great owl<sup><a href="h7091.htm">7091</a></sup> make her nest,<sup><a href="h7077.htm">7077</a></sup> and lay,<sup><a href="h4422.htm">4422</a></sup> and hatch,<sup><a href="h1234.htm">1234</a></sup> and gather<sup><a href="h1716.htm">1716</a></sup> under her shadow:<sup><a href="h6738.htm">6738</a></sup> there<sup><a href="h8033.htm">8033</a></sup> shall the vultures<sup><a href="h1772.htm">1772</a></sup> also<sup><a href="h0389.htm">389</a></sup> be gathered,<sup><a href="h6908.htm">6908</a></sup> every one<sup><a href="h0802.htm">802</a></sup> with her mate.<sup><a href="h7468.htm">7468</a></sup> </p>
 <p>16  Seek<sup><a href="h1875.htm">1875</a></sup> ye out of<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> the book<sup><a href="h5612.htm">5612</a></sup> of the LORD,<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> and read:<sup><a href="h7121.htm">7121</a></sup> no<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> one<sup><a href="h0259.htm">259</a></sup> of these<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h2007.htm">2007</a></sup> shall fail,<sup><a href="h5737.htm">5737</a></sup> none<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0802.htm">802</a></sup> shall want<sup><a href="h6485.htm">6485</a></sup> her mate:<sup><a href="h7468.htm">7468</a></sup> for<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> my mouth<sup><a href="h6310.htm">6310</a></sup> it<sup><a href="h1931.htm">1931</a></sup> hath commanded,<sup><a href="h6680.htm">6680</a></sup> and his spirit<sup><a href="h7307.htm">7307</a></sup> it<sup><a href="h1931.htm">1931</a></sup> hath gathered<sup><a href="h6908.htm">6908</a></sup> them. </p>
 <p>17  And he<sup><a href="h1931.htm">1931</a></sup> hath cast<sup><a href="h5307.htm">5307</a></sup> the lot<sup><a href="h1486.htm">1486</a></sup> for them, and his hand<sup><a href="h3027.htm">3027</a></sup> hath divided<sup><a href="h2505.htm">2505</a></sup> it unto them by line:<sup><a href="h6957.htm">6957</a></sup> they shall possess<sup><a href="h3423.htm">3423</a></sup> it forever,<sup><a href="h5704.htm">5704</a></sup> <sup><a href="h5769.htm">5769</a></sup> from generation<sup><a href="h1755.htm">1755</a></sup> to generation<sup><a href="h1755.htm">1755</a></sup> shall they dwell<sup><a href="h7931.htm">7931</a></sup> therein. </p>

 <p align="center"><a href="../sep/isa034.htm">Sep</a></p><p>1 προσαγάγετε ἔθνη καὶ ἀκούσατε ἄρχοντες ἀκουσάτω ἡ γῆ καὶ οἱ ἐν αὐτῇ ἡ οἰκουμένη καὶ ὁ λαὸς ὁ ἐν αὐτῇ </p>
 <p>2 διότι θυμὸς κυρίου ἐπὶ πάντα τὰ ἔθνη καὶ ὀργὴ ἐπὶ τὸν ἀριθμὸν αὐτῶν τοῦ ἀπολέσαι αὐτοὺς καὶ παραδοῦναι αὐτοὺς εἰς σφαγήν </p>
 <p>3 οἱ δὲ τραυματίαι αὐτῶν ῥιφήσονται καὶ οἱ νεκροί καὶ ἀναβήσεται αὐτῶν ἡ ὀσμή καὶ βραχήσεται τὰ ὄρη ἀπὸ τοῦ αἵματος αὐτῶν </p>
 <p>4 καὶ ἑλιγήσεται ὁ οὐρανὸς ὡς βιβλίον καὶ πάντα τὰ ἄστρα πεσεῖται ὡς φύλλα ἐξ ἀμπέλου καὶ ὡς πίπτει φύλλα ἀπὸ συκῆς </p>
 <p>5 ἐμεθύσθη ἡ μάχαιρά μου ἐν τῷ οὐρανῷ ἰδοὺ ἐπὶ τὴν Ιδουμαίαν καταβήσεται καὶ ἐπὶ τὸν λαὸν τῆς ἀπωλείας μετὰ κρίσεως </p>
 <p>6 ἡ μάχαιρα κυρίου ἐνεπλήσθη αἵματος ἐπαχύνθη ἀπὸ στέατος ἀρνῶν καὶ ἀπὸ στέατος τράγων καὶ κριῶν ὅτι θυσία κυρίῳ ἐν Βοσορ καὶ σφαγὴ μεγάλη ἐν τῇ Ιδουμαίᾳ </p>
 <p>7 καὶ συμπεσοῦνται οἱ ἁδροὶ μετ᾽ αὐτῶν καὶ οἱ κριοὶ καὶ οἱ ταῦροι καὶ μεθυσθήσεται ἡ γῆ ἀπὸ τοῦ αἵματος καὶ ἀπὸ τοῦ στέατος αὐτῶν ἐμπλησθήσεται </p>
 <p>8 ἡμέρα γὰρ κρίσεως κυρίου καὶ ἐνιαυτὸς ἀνταποδόσεως κρίσεως Σιων </p>
 <p>9 καὶ στραφήσονται αὐτῆς αἱ φάραγγες εἰς πίσσαν καὶ ἡ γῆ αὐτῆς εἰς θεῖον καὶ ἔσται αὐτῆς ἡ γῆ καιομένη ὡς πίσσα </p>
 <p>10 νυκτὸς καὶ ἡμέρας καὶ οὐ σβεσθήσεται εἰς τὸν αἰῶνα χρόνον καὶ ἀναβήσεται ὁ καπνὸς αὐτῆς ἄνω εἰς γενεὰς ἐρημωθήσεται καὶ εἰς χρόνον πολύν </p>
 <p>11 καὶ κατοικήσουσιν ἐν αὐτῇ ὄρνεα καὶ ἐχῖνοι καὶ ἴβεις καὶ κόρακες καὶ ἐπιβληθήσεται ἐπ᾽ αὐτὴν σπαρτίον γεωμετρίας ἐρήμου καὶ ὀνοκένταυροι οἰκήσουσιν ἐν αὐτῇ </p>
 <p>12 οἱ ἄρχοντες αὐτῆς οὐκ ἔσονται οἱ γὰρ βασιλεῖς αὐτῆς καὶ οἱ ἄρχοντες αὐτῆς καὶ οἱ μεγιστᾶνες αὐτῆς ἔσονται εἰς ἀπώλειαν </p>
 <p>13 καὶ ἀναφύσει εἰς τὰς πόλεις αὐτῶν ἀκάνθινα ξύλα καὶ εἰς τὰ ὀχυρώματα αὐτῆς καὶ ἔσται ἔπαυλις σειρήνων καὶ αὐλὴ στρουθῶν </p>
 <p>14 καὶ συναντήσουσιν δαιμόνια ὀνοκενταύροις καὶ βοήσουσιν ἕτερος πρὸς τὸν ἕτερον ἐκεῖ ἀναπαύσονται ὀνοκένταυροι εὗρον γὰρ αὑτοῖς ἀνάπαυσιν </p>
 <p>15 ἐκεῖ ἐνόσσευσεν ἐχῖνος καὶ ἔσωσεν ἡ γῆ τὰ παιδία αὐτῆς μετὰ ἀσφαλείας ἐκεῖ ἔλαφοι συνήντησαν καὶ εἶδον τὰ πρόσωπα ἀλλήλων </p>
 <p>16 ἀριθμῷ παρῆλθον καὶ μία αὐτῶν οὐκ ἀπώλετο ἑτέρα τὴν ἑτέραν οὐκ ἐζήτησαν ὅτι κύριος ἐνετείλατο αὐτοῖς καὶ τὸ πνεῦμα αὐτοῦ συνήγαγεν αὐτάς </p>
 <p>17 καὶ αὐτὸς ἐπιβαλεῖ αὐτοῖς κλήρους καὶ ἡ χεὶρ αὐτοῦ διεμέρισεν βόσκεσθαι εἰς τὸν αἰῶνα χρόνον κληρονομήσετε εἰς γενεὰς γενεῶν ἀναπαύσονται ἐπ᾽ αὐτῆς </p>

 <p align="center"><a href="../vul/isa034.htm">Vul</a></p><p>1 [Accedite, gentes, et audite;<br> et populi, attendite:<br> audiat terra, et plenitudo ejus;<br> orbis, et omne germen ejus.<br></p>
 <p>2 Quia indignatio Domini super omnes gentes,<br> et furor super universam militiam eorum:<br> interfecit eos, et dedit eos in occisionem.<br></p>
 <p>3 Interfecti eorum projicientur,<br> et de cadaveribus eorum ascendet ftor;<br> tabescent montes a sanguine eorum.<br></p>
 <p>4 Et tabescet omnis militia cælorum,<br> et complicabuntur sicut liber cæli:<br> et omnis militia eorum defluet,<br> sicut defluit folium de vinea et de ficu.<br></p>
 <p>5 Quoniam inebriatus est in cælo gladius meus;<br> ecce super Idumæam descendet,<br> et super populum interfectionis meæ, ad judicium.<br></p>
 <p>6 Gladius Domini repletus est sanguine,<br> incrassatus est adipe,<br> de sanguine agnorum et hircorum,<br> de sanguine medullatorum arietum:<br> victima enim Domini in Bosra,<br> et interfectio magna in terra Edom.<br></p>
 <p>7 Et descendent unicornes cum eis,<br> et tauri cum potentibus;<br> inebriabitur terra eorum sanguine,<br> et humus eorum adipe pinguium.<br></p>
 <p>8 Quia dies ultionis Domini,<br> annus retributionum judicii Sion.<br></p>
 <p>9 Et convertentur torrentes ejus in picem,<br> et humus ejus in sulphur;<br> et erit terra ejus in picem ardentem.<br></p>
 <p>10 Nocte et die non extinguetur,<br> in sempiternum ascendet fumus ejus,<br> a generatione in generationem desolabitur,<br> in sæcula sæculorum non erit transiens per eam.<br></p>
 <p>11 Et possidebunt illam onocrotalus et ericius;<br> ibis et corvus habitabunt in ea:<br> et extendetur super eam mensura, ut redigatur ad nihilum,<br> et perpendiculum in desolationem.<br></p>
 <p>12 Nobiles ejus non erunt ibi; regem potius invocabunt,<br> et omnes principes ejus erunt in nihilum.<br></p>
 <p>13 Et orientur in domibus ejus spinæ et urticæ,<br> et paliurus in munitionibus ejus;<br> et erit cubile draconum,<br> et pascua struthionum.<br></p>
 <p>14 Et occurrent dæmonia onocentauris,<br> et pilosus clamabit alter ad alterum;<br> ibi cubavit lamia,<br> et invenit sibi requiem.<br></p>
 <p>15 Ibi habuit foveam ericius, et enutrivit catulos,<br> et circumfodit, et fovit in umbra ejus;<br> illuc congregati sunt milvi, alter ad alterum.<br></p>
 <p>16 Requirite diligenter in libro Domini, et legite:<br> Unum ex eis non defuit,<br> alter alterum non quæsivit;<br> quia quod ex ore meo procedit, ille mandavit,<br> et spiritus ejus ipse congregavit ea.<br></p>
 <p>17 Et ipse misit eis sortem,<br> et manus ejus divisit eam illis in mensuram:<br> usque in æternum possidebunt eam;<br> in generationem et generationem habitabunt in ea.]</p>
