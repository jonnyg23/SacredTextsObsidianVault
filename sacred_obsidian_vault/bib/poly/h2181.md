  <p>H2181<br>  זנה  <br> זָנָה  ‎  zânâh  <br><i>zaw-naw‘ </i><br>A primitive root (highly <i>fed</i> and therefore <i>wanton</i>); to <i>commit</i> <i>adultery</i> (usually of the female, and less often of simple forniciation, rarely of involuntary ravishment); figuratively to <i>commit</i> <i>idolatry</i> (the Jewish people being regarded as the spouse of Jehovah): - (cause to) commit fornication, X continually, X great, (be an, play the) harlot, (cause to be, play the) whore, (commit, fall to) whoredom, (cause to) go a-whoring, whorish.<br></p>
