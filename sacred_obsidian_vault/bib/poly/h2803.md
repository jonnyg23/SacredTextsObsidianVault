  <p>H2803<br>  חשׁב  <br> חָשַׁב  ‎  châshab  <br><i>khaw-shab‘ </i><br>A primitive root; properly to <i>plait</i> or interpenetrate, that is, (literally) to <i>weave</i> or (generally) to <i>fabricate</i>; figuratively to <i>plot</i> or contrive (usually in a malicious sense); hence (from the mental effort) to <i>think</i>, <i>regard</i>, <i>value</i>, <i>compute: - </i>(make) account (of), conceive, consider, count, cunning (man, work, workman), devise, esteem, find out, forecast, hold, imagine, impute, invent, be like, mean, purpose, reckon (-ing be made), regard, think.<br></p>
