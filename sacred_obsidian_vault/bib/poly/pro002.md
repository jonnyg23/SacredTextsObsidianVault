 <p align="center"><a href="../kjv/pro002.htm">KJV</a></p><p>1  My son,<sup><a href="h1121.htm">1121</a></sup> if<sup><a href="h0518.htm">518</a></sup> thou wilt receive<sup><a href="h3947.htm">3947</a></sup> my words,<sup><a href="h0561.htm">561</a></sup> and hide<sup><a href="h6845.htm">6845</a></sup> my commandments<sup><a href="h4687.htm">4687</a></sup> with<sup><a href="h0854.htm">854</a></sup> thee; </p>
 <p>2  So that thou incline<sup><a href="h7181.htm">7181</a></sup> thine ear<sup><a href="h0241.htm">241</a></sup> unto wisdom,<sup><a href="h2451.htm">2451</a></sup> and apply<sup><a href="h5186.htm">5186</a></sup> thine heart<sup><a href="h3820.htm">3820</a></sup> to understanding;<sup><a href="h8394.htm">8394</a></sup> </p>
 <p>3  Yea,<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> if<sup><a href="h0518.htm">518</a></sup> thou criest<sup><a href="h7121.htm">7121</a></sup> after knowledge,<sup><a href="h0998.htm">998</a></sup> and liftest up<sup><a href="h5414.htm">5414</a></sup> thy voice<sup><a href="h6963.htm">6963</a></sup> for understanding;<sup><a href="h8394.htm">8394</a></sup> </p>
 <p>4  If<sup><a href="h0518.htm">518</a></sup> thou seekest<sup><a href="h1245.htm">1245</a></sup> her as silver,<sup><a href="h3701.htm">3701</a></sup> and searchest<sup><a href="h2664.htm">2664</a></sup> for her as for hid treasures;<sup><a href="h4301.htm">4301</a></sup> </p>
 <p>5  Then<sup><a href="h0227.htm">227</a></sup> shalt thou understand<sup><a href="h0995.htm">995</a></sup> the fear<sup><a href="h3374.htm">3374</a></sup> of the LORD,<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> and find<sup><a href="h4672.htm">4672</a></sup> the knowledge<sup><a href="h1847.htm">1847</a></sup> of God.<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> </p>
 <p>6  For<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> the LORD<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> giveth<sup><a href="h5414.htm">5414</a></sup> wisdom:<sup><a href="h2451.htm">2451</a></sup> out of his mouth<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h6310.htm">6310</a></sup> cometh knowledge<sup><a href="h1847.htm">1847</a></sup> and understanding.<sup><a href="h8394.htm">8394</a></sup> </p>
 <p>7  He layeth up<sup><a href="h6845.htm">6845</a></sup> sound wisdom<sup><a href="h8454.htm">8454</a></sup> for the righteous:<sup><a href="h3477.htm">3477</a></sup> he is a buckler<sup><a href="h4043.htm">4043</a></sup> to them that walk<sup><a href="h1980.htm">1980</a></sup> uprightly.<sup><a href="h8537.htm">8537</a></sup> </p>
 <p>8  He keepeth<sup><a href="h5341.htm">5341</a></sup> the paths<sup><a href="h0734.htm">734</a></sup> of judgment,<sup><a href="h4941.htm">4941</a></sup> and preserveth<sup><a href="h8104.htm">8104</a></sup> the way<sup><a href="h1870.htm">1870</a></sup> of his saints.<sup><a href="h2623.htm">2623</a></sup> </p>
 <p>9  Then<sup><a href="h0227.htm">227</a></sup> shalt thou understand<sup><a href="h0995.htm">995</a></sup> righteousness,<sup><a href="h6664.htm">6664</a></sup> and judgment,<sup><a href="h4941.htm">4941</a></sup> and equity;<sup><a href="h4339.htm">4339</a></sup> yea, every<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> good<sup><a href="h2896.htm">2896</a></sup> path.<sup><a href="h4570.htm">4570</a></sup> </p>
 <p>10  When<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> wisdom<sup><a href="h2451.htm">2451</a></sup> entereth<sup><a href="h0935.htm">935</a></sup> into thine heart,<sup><a href="h3820.htm">3820</a></sup> and knowledge<sup><a href="h1847.htm">1847</a></sup> is pleasant<sup><a href="h5276.htm">5276</a></sup> unto thy soul;<sup><a href="h5315.htm">5315</a></sup> </p>
 <p>11  Discretion<sup><a href="h4209.htm">4209</a></sup> shall preserve<sup><a href="h8104.htm">8104</a></sup> <sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> thee, understanding<sup><a href="h8394.htm">8394</a></sup> shall keep<sup><a href="h5341.htm">5341</a></sup> thee: </p>
 <p>12  To deliver<sup><a href="h5337.htm">5337</a></sup> thee from the way<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h1870.htm">1870</a></sup> of the evil<sup><a href="h7451.htm">7451</a></sup> man, from the man<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0376.htm">376</a></sup> that speaketh<sup><a href="h1696.htm">1696</a></sup> froward things;<sup><a href="h8419.htm">8419</a></sup> </p>
 <p>13  Who leave<sup><a href="h5800.htm">5800</a></sup> the paths<sup><a href="h0734.htm">734</a></sup> of uprightness,<sup><a href="h3476.htm">3476</a></sup> to walk<sup><a href="h1980.htm">1980</a></sup> in the ways<sup><a href="h1870.htm">1870</a></sup> of darkness;<sup><a href="h2822.htm">2822</a></sup> </p>
 <p>14  Who rejoice<sup><a href="h8056.htm">8056</a></sup> to do<sup><a href="h6213.htm">6213</a></sup> evil,<sup><a href="h7451.htm">7451</a></sup> and delight in<sup><a href="h1523.htm">1523</a></sup> the frowardness<sup><a href="h8419.htm">8419</a></sup> of the wicked;<sup><a href="h7451.htm">7451</a></sup> </p>
 <p>15  Whose<sup><a href="h0834.htm">834</a></sup> ways<sup><a href="h0734.htm">734</a></sup> are crooked,<sup><a href="h6141.htm">6141</a></sup> and they froward<sup><a href="h3868.htm">3868</a></sup> in their paths:<sup><a href="h4570.htm">4570</a></sup> </p>
 <p>16  To deliver<sup><a href="h5337.htm">5337</a></sup> thee from the strange woman,<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h0802.htm">802</a></sup> <sup><a href="h2114.htm">2114</a></sup> even from the stranger<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h5237.htm">5237</a></sup> which flattereth<sup><a href="h2505.htm">2505</a></sup> with her words;<sup><a href="h0561.htm">561</a></sup> </p>
 <p>17  Which forsaketh<sup><a href="h5800.htm">5800</a></sup> the guide<sup><a href="h0441.htm">441</a></sup> of her youth,<sup><a href="h5271.htm">5271</a></sup> and forgetteth<sup><a href="h7911.htm">7911</a></sup> the covenant<sup><a href="h1285.htm">1285</a></sup> of her God.<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> </p>
 <p>18  For<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> her house<sup><a href="h1004.htm">1004</a></sup> inclineth<sup><a href="h7743.htm">7743</a></sup> unto<sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> death,<sup><a href="h4194.htm">4194</a></sup> and her paths<sup><a href="h4570.htm">4570</a></sup> unto<sup><a href="h0413.htm">413</a></sup> the dead.<sup><a href="h7496.htm">7496</a></sup> </p>
 <p>19  None<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> that go unto<sup><a href="h0935.htm">935</a></sup> her return again,<sup><a href="h7725.htm">7725</a></sup> neither<sup><a href="h3808.htm">3808</a></sup> take they hold<sup><a href="h5381.htm">5381</a></sup> of the paths<sup><a href="h0734.htm">734</a></sup> of life.<sup><a href="h2416.htm">2416</a></sup> </p>
 <p>20  That<sup><a href="h4616.htm">4616</a></sup> thou mayest walk<sup><a href="h1980.htm">1980</a></sup> in the way<sup><a href="h1870.htm">1870</a></sup> of good<sup><a href="h2896.htm">2896</a></sup> men, and keep<sup><a href="h8104.htm">8104</a></sup> the paths<sup><a href="h0734.htm">734</a></sup> of the righteous.<sup><a href="h6662.htm">6662</a></sup> </p>
 <p>21  For<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> the upright<sup><a href="h3477.htm">3477</a></sup> shall dwell in<sup><a href="h7931.htm">7931</a></sup> the land,<sup><a href="h0776.htm">776</a></sup> and the perfect<sup><a href="h8549.htm">8549</a></sup> shall remain<sup><a href="h3498.htm">3498</a></sup> in it. </p>

 <p align="center"><a href="../sep/pro002.htm">Sep</a></p><p>1 υἱέ ἐὰν δεξάμενος ῥῆσιν ἐμῆς ἐντολῆς κρύψῃς παρὰ σεαυτῷ </p>
 <p>2 ὑπακούσεται σοφίας τὸ οὖς σου καὶ παραβαλεῖς καρδίαν σου εἰς σύνεσιν παραβαλεῖς δὲ αὐτὴν ἐπὶ νουθέτησιν τῷ υἱῷ σου </p>
 <p>3 ἐὰν γὰρ τὴν σοφίαν ἐπικαλέσῃ καὶ τῇ συνέσει δῷς φωνήν σου τὴν δὲ αἴσθησιν ζητήσῃς μεγάλῃ τῇ φωνῇ </p>
 <p>4 καὶ ἐὰν ζητήσῃς αὐτὴν ὡς ἀργύριον καὶ ὡς θησαυροὺς ἐξερευνήσῃς αὐτήν </p>
 <p>5 τότε συνήσεις φόβον κυρίου καὶ ἐπίγνωσιν θεοῦ εὑρήσεις </p>
 <p>6 ὅτι κύριος δίδωσιν σοφίαν καὶ ἀπὸ προσώπου αὐτοῦ γνῶσις καὶ σύνεσις </p>
 <p>7 καὶ θησαυρίζει τοῖς κατορθοῦσι σωτηρίαν ὑπερασπιεῖ τὴν πορείαν αὐτῶν </p>
 <p>8 τοῦ φυλάξαι ὁδοὺς δικαιωμάτων καὶ ὁδὸν εὐλαβουμένων αὐτὸν διαφυλάξει </p>
 <p>9 τότε συνήσεις δικαιοσύνην καὶ κρίμα καὶ κατορθώσεις πάντας ἄξονας ἀγαθούς </p>
 <p>10 ἐὰν γὰρ ἔλθῃ ἡ σοφία εἰς σὴν διάνοιαν ἡ δὲ αἴσθησις τῇ σῇ ψυχῇ καλὴ εἶναι δόξῃ </p>
 <p>11 βουλὴ καλὴ φυλάξει σε ἔννοια δὲ ὁσία τηρήσει σε </p>
 <p>12 ἵνα ῥύσηταί σε ἀπὸ ὁδοῦ κακῆς καὶ ἀπὸ ἀνδρὸς λαλοῦντος μηδὲν πιστόν </p>
 <p>13 ὦ οἱ ἐγκαταλείποντες ὁδοὺς εὐθείας τοῦ πορεύεσθαι ἐν ὁδοῖς σκότους </p>
 <p>14 οἱ εὐφραινόμενοι ἐπὶ κακοῖς καὶ χαίροντες ἐπὶ διαστροφῇ κακῇ </p>
 <p>15 ὧν αἱ τρίβοι σκολιαὶ καὶ καμπύλαι αἱ τροχιαὶ αὐτῶν </p>
 <p>16 τοῦ μακράν σε ποιῆσαι ἀπὸ ὁδοῦ εὐθείας καὶ ἀλλότριον τῆς δικαίας γνώμης </p>
 <p>17 υἱέ μή σε καταλάβῃ κακὴ βουλὴ ἡ ἀπολείπουσα διδασκαλίαν νεότητος καὶ διαθήκην θείαν ἐπιλελησμένη </p>
 <p>18 ἔθετο γὰρ παρὰ τῷ θανάτῳ τὸν οἶκον αὐτῆς καὶ παρὰ τῷ ᾅδῃ μετὰ τῶν γηγενῶν τοὺς ἄξονας αὐτῆς </p>
 <p>19 πάντες οἱ πορευόμενοι ἐν αὐτῇ οὐκ ἀναστρέψουσιν οὐδὲ μὴ καταλάβωσιν τρίβους εὐθείας οὐ γὰρ καταλαμβάνονται ὑπὸ ἐνιαυτῶν ζωῆς </p>
 <p>20 εἰ γὰρ ἐπορεύοντο τρίβους ἀγαθάς εὕροσαν ἂν τρίβους δικαιοσύνης λείους </p>
 <p>21 χρηστοὶ ἔσονται οἰκήτορες γῆς ἄκακοι δὲ ὑπολειφθήσονται ἐν αὐτῇ ὅτι εὐθεῖς κατασκηνώσουσι γῆν καὶ ὅσιοι ὑπολειφθήσονται ἐν αὐτῇ </p>
 <p>22 ὁδοὶ ἀσεβῶν ἐκ γῆς ὀλοῦνται οἱ δὲ παράνομοι ἐξωσθήσονται ἀπ᾽ αὐτῆς </p>

 <p>9 Tunc intelliges justitiam, et judicium,<br> et æquitatem, et omnem semitam bonam.]<br>

 <p>10 [Si intraverit sapientia cor tuum,<br> et scientia animæ tuæ placuerit,<br></p>
 <p>11 consilium custodiet te,<br> et prudentia servabit te:<br></p>
 <p>12 ut eruaris a via mala,<br> et ab homine qui perversa loquitur;<br></p>
 <p>13 qui relinquunt iter rectum,<br> et ambulant per vias tenebrosas;<br></p>
 <p>14 qui lætantur cum malefecerint,<br> et exsultant in rebus pessimis;<br></p>
 <p>15 quorum viæ perversæ sunt,<br> et infames gressus eorum.<br></p>
 <p>16 Ut eruaris a muliere aliena,<br> et ab extranea quæ mollit sermones suos,<br></p>
 <p>17 et relinquit ducem pubertatis suæ,<br></p>
 <p>18 et pacti Dei sui oblita est.<br> Inclinata est enim ad mortem domus ejus,<br> et ad inferos semitæ ipsius.<br></p>
 <p>19 Omnes qui ingrediuntur ad eam non revertentur,<br> nec apprehendent semitas vitæ.<br></p>
 <p>20 Ut ambules in via bona,<br> et calles justorum custodias:<br></p>
 <p>21 qui enim recti sunt habitabunt in terra,<br> et simplices permanebunt in ea;<br></p>
 <p>22 impii vero de terra perdentur,<br> et qui inique agunt auferentur ex ea.]</p>
