  <p>H3363<br>  יקע  <br> יָקַע  ‎  yâqa‛  <br><i>yaw-kah‘ </i><br>A primitive root; properly to <i>sever</i> oneself, that is, (by implication) to <i>be</i> <i>dislocated</i>; figuratively to <i>abandon</i>; causatively to <i>impale</i> (and thus allow to drop to pieces by <i>rotting</i>): - be alienated, depart, hang (up), be out of joint.<br></p>
