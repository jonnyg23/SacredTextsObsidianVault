 <p align="center"><a href="../kjv/psa097.htm">KJV</a></p><p>1  The LORD<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> reigneth;<sup><a href="h4427.htm">4427</a></sup> let the earth<sup><a href="h0776.htm">776</a></sup> rejoice;<sup><a href="h1523.htm">1523</a></sup> let the multitude<sup><a href="h7227.htm">7227</a></sup> of isles<sup><a href="h0339.htm">339</a></sup> be glad<sup><a href="h8055.htm">8055</a></sup> thereof. </p>
 <p>2  Clouds<sup><a href="h6051.htm">6051</a></sup> and darkness<sup><a href="h6205.htm">6205</a></sup> are round about<sup><a href="h5439.htm">5439</a></sup> him: righteousness<sup><a href="h6664.htm">6664</a></sup> and judgment<sup><a href="h4941.htm">4941</a></sup> are the habitation<sup><a href="h4349.htm">4349</a></sup> of his throne.<sup><a href="h3678.htm">3678</a></sup> </p>
 <p>3  A fire<sup><a href="h0784.htm">784</a></sup> goeth<sup><a href="h1980.htm">1980</a></sup> before<sup><a href="h6440.htm">6440</a></sup> him, and burneth up<sup><a href="h3857.htm">3857</a></sup> his enemies<sup><a href="h6862.htm">6862</a></sup> round about.<sup><a href="h5439.htm">5439</a></sup> </p>
 <p>4  His lightnings<sup><a href="h1300.htm">1300</a></sup> enlightened<sup><a href="h0215.htm">215</a></sup> the world:<sup><a href="h8398.htm">8398</a></sup> the earth<sup><a href="h0776.htm">776</a></sup> saw,<sup><a href="h7200.htm">7200</a></sup> and trembled.<sup><a href="h2342.htm">2342</a></sup> </p>
 <p>5  The hills<sup><a href="h2022.htm">2022</a></sup> melted<sup><a href="h4549.htm">4549</a></sup> like wax<sup><a href="h1749.htm">1749</a></sup> at the presence<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h6440.htm">6440</a></sup> of the LORD,<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> at the presence<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h6440.htm">6440</a></sup> of the Lord<sup><a href="h0113.htm">113</a></sup> of the whole<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> earth.<sup><a href="h0776.htm">776</a></sup> </p>

 <p>7  Confounded<sup><a href="h0954.htm">954</a></sup> be all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> they that serve<sup><a href="h5647.htm">5647</a></sup> graven images,<sup><a href="h6459.htm">6459</a></sup> that boast themselves<sup><a href="h1984.htm">1984</a></sup> of idols:<sup><a href="h0457.htm">457</a></sup> worship<sup><a href="h7812.htm">7812</a></sup> him, all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> ye gods.<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> </p>
 <p>8  Zion<sup><a href="h6726.htm">6726</a></sup> heard,<sup><a href="h8085.htm">8085</a></sup> and was glad;<sup><a href="h8055.htm">8055</a></sup> and the daughters<sup><a href="h1323.htm">1323</a></sup> of Judah<sup><a href="h3063.htm">3063</a></sup> rejoiced<sup><a href="h1523.htm">1523</a></sup> because of<sup><a href="h4616.htm">4616</a></sup> thy judgments,<sup><a href="h4941.htm">4941</a></sup> O LORD.<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> </p>
 <p>9  For<sup><a href="h3588.htm">3588</a></sup> thou,<sup><a href="h0859.htm">859</a></sup> LORD,<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> art high<sup><a href="h5945.htm">5945</a></sup> above<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> the earth:<sup><a href="h0776.htm">776</a></sup> thou art exalted<sup><a href="h5927.htm">5927</a></sup> far<sup><a href="h3966.htm">3966</a></sup> above<sup><a href="h5921.htm">5921</a></sup> all<sup><a href="h3605.htm">3605</a></sup> gods.<sup><a href="h0430.htm">430</a></sup> </p>
 <p>10  Ye that love<sup><a href="h0157.htm">157</a></sup> the LORD,<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> hate<sup><a href="h8130.htm">8130</a></sup> evil:<sup><a href="h7451.htm">7451</a></sup> he preserveth<sup><a href="h8104.htm">8104</a></sup> the souls<sup><a href="h5315.htm">5315</a></sup> of his saints;<sup><a href="h2623.htm">2623</a></sup> he delivereth<sup><a href="h5337.htm">5337</a></sup> them out of the hand<sup><a href="h4480.htm">4480</a></sup> <sup><a href="h3027.htm">3027</a></sup> of the wicked.<sup><a href="h7563.htm">7563</a></sup> </p>
 <p>11  Light<sup><a href="h0216.htm">216</a></sup> is sown<sup><a href="h2232.htm">2232</a></sup> for the righteous,<sup><a href="h6662.htm">6662</a></sup> and gladness<sup><a href="h8057.htm">8057</a></sup> for the upright<sup><a href="h3477.htm">3477</a></sup> in heart.<sup><a href="h3820.htm">3820</a></sup> </p>
 <p>12  Rejoice<sup><a href="h8055.htm">8055</a></sup> in the LORD,<sup><a href="h3068.htm">3068</a></sup> ye righteous;<sup><a href="h6662.htm">6662</a></sup> and give thanks<sup><a href="h3034.htm">3034</a></sup> at the remembrance<sup><a href="h2143.htm">2143</a></sup> of his holiness.<sup><a href="h6944.htm">6944</a></sup> </p>
