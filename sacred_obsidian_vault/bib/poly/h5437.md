  <p>H5437<br>  סבב  <br> סָבַב  ‎  sâbab  <br><i>saw-bab‘ </i><br>A primitive root; to <i>revolve</i>, <i>surround</i> or <i>border</i>; used in various applications, literally and figuratively: - bring, cast, fetch, lead, make, walk, X whirl, X round about, be about on every side, apply, avoid, beset (about), besiege, bring again, carry (about), change, cause to come about, X circuit, (fetch a) compass (about, round), drive, environ, X on every side, beset (close, come, compass, go, stand) round about, remove, return, set, sit down, turn (self) (about, aside, away, back).<br></p>
