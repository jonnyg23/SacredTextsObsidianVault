  <p>H5203<br>  נטשׁ  <br> נָטַשׁ  ‎  nâṭash  <br><i>naw-tash‘ </i><br>A primitive root; properly to <i>pound</i>, that is, <i>smite</i>; by implication (as if beating out, and thus expanding) to <i>disperse</i>; also, to <i>thrust</i> <i>off</i>, down, out or upon (including <i>reject</i>, <i>let</i> <i>alone</i>, <i>permit</i>, <i>remit</i>, etc.): - cast off, drawn, let fall, forsake, join [battle], leave (off), lie still, loose, spread (self) abroad, stretch out, suffer.<br></p>
