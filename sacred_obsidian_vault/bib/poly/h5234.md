  <p>H5234<br>  נכר  <br> נָכַר  ‎  nâkar  <br><i>naw-kar‘ </i><br>A primitive root; properly to <i>scrutinize</i>, that is, look intently at; hence (with <i>recognition</i> implied), to <i>acknowledge</i>, <i>be</i> <i>acquainted</i> <i>with</i>, <i>care</i> <i>for</i>, <i>respect</i>, <i>revere</i>, or (with <i>suspicion</i> implied), to <i>disregard</i>, <i>ignore</i>, <i>be</i> <i>strange</i> toward, <i>reject</i>, <i>resign</i>, <i>dissimulate</i> (as if ignorant or disowning): - acknowledge, X could, deliver, discern, dissemble, estrange, feign self to be another, know, take knowledge (notice), perceive, regard, (have) respect, behave (make) self strange (-ly).<br></p>
