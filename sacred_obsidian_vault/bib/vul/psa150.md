 <h3 align="CENTER">Vulgate (Latin): Psalms Chapter 150</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Alleluja. [Laudate Dominum in sanctis ejus;<br> laudate eum in firmamento virtutis ejus.<br></p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Laudate eum in virtutibus ejus;<br> laudate eum secundum multitudinem magnitudinis ejus.<br></p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Laudate eum in sono tubæ;<br> laudate eum in psalterio et cithara.<br></p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Laudate eum in tympano et choro;<br> laudate eum in chordis et organo.<br></p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Laudate eum in cymbalis benesonantibus;<br> laudate eum in cymbalis jubilationis.<br></p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Omnis spiritus laudet Dominum!<br> Alleluja.]</p>
