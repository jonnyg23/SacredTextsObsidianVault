 <p align="CENTER">CV. <i>God and the Messiah to dwell with Man</i>.</p> <h3 align="CENTER">CHAPTER CV.</h3> 
 <p>1. In those days the Lord bade (them) to summon and testify to the children of earth concerning their wisdom: Show (it) unto them; for ye are their guides, and a recompense over the whole earth. 2. For I and My son will be united with them for ever in the paths of uprightness in their lives; and ye shall have peace: rejoice, ye children of uprightness. Amen.</p>
