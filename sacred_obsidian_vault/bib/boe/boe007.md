 <p><a name="page_33"><font size="1" color="green">p. 33</font></a></p> <h3 align="CENTER">CHAPTER IV.</h3> 
 <p>And again, observe ye the days of summer how the sun is above the earth over against it. And you seek shade and shelter by reason of the heat of the sun, and the earth also burns with growing heat, and so you cannot tread on the earth, or on a rock by reason of its heat.</p>
