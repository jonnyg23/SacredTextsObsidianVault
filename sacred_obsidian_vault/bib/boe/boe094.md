 <p align="CENTER">XCII. XCI. 1-10. 18-19 <i>Enoch's Book of Admonition for his Children</i>.</p> <h3 align="CENTER">CHAPTER XCII.</h3> 
 <p>1. The book written by Enoch--<font color="GREEN">[</font>Enoch indeed wrote this complete doctrine of wisdom, (which is) praised of all men and a judge of all the earth<font color="GREEN">]</font> for all my children who shall dwell on the earth. And for the future generations who shall observe uprightness and peace.</p><dir> <dir>  
 <p>2. Let not your spirit be troubled on account of the times;<br> For the Holy and Great One has appointed days for all things.</p> 
 <p>3. And the righteous one shall arise from sleep,<br> <font color="GREEN">[</font>Shall arise<font color="GREEN">]</font> and walk in the paths of righteousness,<br> And all his path and conversation shall be in eternal goodness and grace.</p> 
 <p>4. He will be gracious to the righteous and give him eternal uprightness,<br> And He will give him power so that he shall be (endowed) with goodness and righteousness.<br> And he shall walk in eternal light.</p> 
 <p>5. And sin shall perish in darkness for ever,<br> And shall no more be seen from that day for evermore.</p></dir> </dir>
