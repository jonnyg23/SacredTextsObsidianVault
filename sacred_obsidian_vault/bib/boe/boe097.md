 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Book of Enoch</i>, by R.H. Charles, [1917], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> 
 <p align="CENTER">XCI. 12.-17. <i>The Last Three Weeks</i>.</p> <h3 align="CENTER">CHAPTER XCI.</h3><dir> <dir>  
 <p>12. And after that there shall be another, the eighth week, that of righteousness,<br> And a sword shall be given to it that a righteous judgement may be executed on the oppressors,<br> And sinners shall be delivered into the hands of the righteous. <a name="page_134"><font size="1" color="green">p. 134</font></a></p> 
 <p>13 And at its close they shall acquire houses through their righteousness,<br> And a house shall be built for the Great King in glory for evermore,<br> 14<i>d</i>. And all mankind shall look to the path of uprightness.</p> 
 <p>14<i>a</i>. And after that, in the ninth week, the righteous judgement shall be revealed to the whole world,<br> <i>b</i>. And all the works of the godless shall vanish from all the earth,<br> <i>c</i>. And the world shall be written down for destruction.</p> 
 <p>15. And after this, in the tenth week in the seventh part,<br> There shall be the great eternal judgement,<br> In which He will execute vengeance amongst the angels.</p> 
 <p>16. And the first heaven shall depart and pass away,<br> And a new heaven shall appear,<br> And all the powers of the heavens shall give sevenfold light.</p> 
 <p>17. And after that there will be many weeks without number for ever,<br> And all shall be in goodness and righteousness,<br> And sin shall no more be mentioned for ever.</p></dir> </dir>
