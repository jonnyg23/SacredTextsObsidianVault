 <p align="CENTER">XLII. <i>The Dwelling-places of Wisdom and of Unrighteousness</i>.</p> <h3 align="CENTER">CHAPTER XLII.</h3><dir> <dir>  
 <p>1. Wisdom found no place where she might dwell;<br> Then a dwelling-place was assigned her in the heavens.</p> 
 <p>2 Wisdom went forth to make her dwelling among the children of men,<br> And found no dwelling-place:</p> 
 <p>Wisdom returned to her place,<br> And took her seat among the angels.</p> 
 <p>3 And unrighteousness went forth from her chambers:<br> Whom she sought not she found,<br> And dwelt with them, <a name="page_62"><font size="1" color="green">p. 62</font></a></p> 
 <p>As rain in a desert<br> And dew on a thirsty land.</p>
