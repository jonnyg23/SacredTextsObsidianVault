 <p align="CENTER">XXVII. <i>The Purpose of the Accursed Valley</i>.</p> <h3 align="CENTER">CHAPTER XXVII.</h3> 
 <p>1. Then said I: 'For what object is this blessed land, which is entirely filled with trees, and this accursed valley <font color="GREEN">⌈⌈</font>between<font color="GREEN">⌉⌉</font>?' 2. <font color="GREEN">⌈⌈</font>Then Uriel, one of the holy angels who was with me, answered and said: 'This<font color="GREEN">⌉⌉</font> accursed valley is for those who are accursed for ever: Here shall all <font color="GREEN">⌈</font>the accursed<font color="GREEN">⌉</font> be gathered together who utter with their lips against the Lord unseemly words and of His glory speak hard things.</p> <table border="0" width="638"> <tr><td valign="TOP" align="TOP" width="50%"> 
 <p align="CENTER"><b>E</b> </p> 
 <p>Here shall they be gathered together, and here shall be their place of judgement. 3. In the last days there shall be upon them the spectacle of righteous judgement in the presence of the righteous for ever: here shall the merciful bless the Lord of glory, the Eternal King.</p></td> <td valign="TOP" align="TOP" width="50%"> <b>
 </b><p align="CENTER">G<sup>g</sup> </p> 
 <p>Here shall they be gathered together, and here shall be the place of their habitation. 3. In the last times, in the days of the true judgement in the presence of the righteous for ever: here shall the <b>godly</b> bless the Lord of Glory, the Eternal Kin.</p></td> </tr> </table>  
 <p> </p> 
 <p>4. In the days of judgement over the former, they shall bless Him for the mercy in accordance with which He has assigned them (their lot).' 5. Then I blessed the Lord of Glory and set forth His <font color="GREEN">⌈</font>glory<font color="GREEN">⌉</font> and lauded Him gloriously.</p>
