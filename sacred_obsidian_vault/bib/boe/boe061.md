 <p align="CENTER">LVIII-LXXI. <b>The Third Parable</b>.</p> 
 <p align="CENTER">LVIII. <i>The Blessedness of the Saints</i>.</p> <h3 align="CENTER">CHAPTER LVIII.</h3><dir> <dir>  
 <p>1. And I began to speak the third Parable concerning the righteous and elect. <a name="page_75"><font size="1" color="green">p. 75</font></a></p> 
 <p>2. Blessed are ye, ye righteous and elect,<br> For glorious shall be your lot.</p> 
 <p>3. And the righteous shall be in the light of the sun.<br> And the elect in the light of eternal life:<br> The days of their life shall be unending,<br> And the days of the holy without number.</p> 
 <p>4. And they shall seek the light and find righteousness with the Lord of Spirits:<br> There shall be peace to the righteous in the name of the Eternal Lord.</p> 
 <p>5. And after this it shall be said to the holy in heaven<br> That they should seek out the secrets of righteousness, the heritage of faith:<br> For it has become bright as the sun upon earth,<br> And the darkness is past.</p> 
 <p>6. And there shall be a light that never <b>endeth</b>,<br> And to a limit (lit. 'number') of days they shall not come,<br> For the darkness shall first have been destroyed,<br> <font color="GREEN">[</font>And the light established before the Lord of Spirits<font color="GREEN">]</font><br> And the light of uprightness established for ever before the Lord of Spirits.</p>
