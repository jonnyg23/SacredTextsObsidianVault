 <p align="center"><small><i>Precepts</i>.</small></p>  
 <p>AND he said unto them, Is a candle brought to be put under a bushel, or under a bed? and not to be set on a candlestick?</p>  
 <p>1 For there is nothing hid, which shall not be manifested; neither was any thing kept secret, but that it should come abroad.</p>  
 <p>2 If any man have ears to hear, let him hear.</p>
