 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Life and Morals of Jesus of Nazareth (the Jefferson Bible)</i>, by Thomas Jefferson, [1902], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">XXXI.</h3>  
 <p align="center"><small><i>To be Born Blind No Proof of Sin</i>.</small></p>  
 <p>AND as <i>Jesus</i> passed by, he saw a man which was blind from <i>his</i> birth.</p>  
 <p>2 And his disciples asked him, saying, Master, who did sin, this man, or his parents, that he was born blind?</p>  
 <p>3 Jesus answered, Neither hath this man sinned, nor his parents: but that the works of God should be made manifest in him.</p>
