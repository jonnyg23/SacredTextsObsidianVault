 <p><a name="page_24"><font size="1" color="green">p. 24</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">VI.</h3>  
 <p align="center"><small><i>Drives the Traders Out of the Temple</i>.</small></p>  
 <p>AFTER this he went down to Capernaum, he, and his mother, and his brethren, and his disciples: and they continued there not many days.</p>  
 <p>2 ¶ And the Jews’ passover was at hand, and Jesus went up to Jerusalem;</p>  
 <p>3 And found in the temple those hat sold oxen and sheep and doves, and the changers of money sitting:</p>  
 <p>4 And when he had made a scourge of small cords, he drove them all out of the temple, and the sheep, and the oxen; and poured out the changers’ money, and overthrew the tables;</p>  
 <p>5 And said unto them that sold doves, Take these things hence; make not my Father's house an house of merchandise.</p>
