 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 6</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> O LORD, rebuke me not in thine <a href="../ebd/ebd024.htm#002">anger</a>, neither chasten me in thy hot displeasure.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Have <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> upon me, O LORD; for I am weak: O LORD, heal me; for my bones are vexed.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> My soul is also sore vexed: but thou, O LORD, how long?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Return, O LORD, deliver my soul: oh save me for thy mercies' sake.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> For in death there is no remembrance of thee: in the <a href="../ebd/ebd154.htm#008">grave</a> who shall give thee thanks?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> I am weary with my groaning; all the night make I my bed to swim; I water my <a href="../ebd/ebd091.htm#002">couch</a> with my tears.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Mine eye is consumed because of grief; it waxeth old because of all mine enemies.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> Depart from me, all ye workers of iniquity; for the LORD hath heard the voice of my weeping.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> The LORD hath heard my supplication; the LORD will receive my <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#000">prayer</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> Let all mine enemies be ashamed and sore vexed: let them return and be ashamed suddenly.<br>
 </p>
