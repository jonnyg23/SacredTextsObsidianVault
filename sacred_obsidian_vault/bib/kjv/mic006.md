 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Micah Chapter 6</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Hear ye now what the LORD saith; Arise, contend thou before the mountains, and let the hills hear thy voice.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Hear ye, O mountains, the LORD's controversy, and ye strong foundations of the earth: for the LORD hath a controversy with his people, and he will plead with <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> O my people, what have I done unto thee? and wherein have I wearied thee? testify against me.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> For I brought thee up out of the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a>, and redeemed thee out of the house of servants; and I sent before thee <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd256.htm#003">Miriam</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> O my people, remember now what <a href="../ebd/ebd042.htm#006">Balak</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd258.htm#005">Moab</a> consulted, and what <a href="../ebd/ebd042.htm#003">Balaam</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd052.htm#009">Beor</a> answered him from <a href="../ebd/ebd339.htm#009">Shittim</a> unto <a href="../ebd/ebd149.htm#001">Gilgal</a>; that ye may know the <a href="../ebd/ebd313.htm#004">righteousness</a> of the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Wherewith shall I come before the LORD, and <a href="../ebd/ebd063.htm#003">bow</a> myself before the high God? shall I come before him with burnt offerings, with calves of a year old?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Will the LORD be pleased with <a href="../ebd/ebd364.htm#008">thousands</a> of rams, or with ten <a href="../ebd/ebd364.htm#008">thousands</a> of rivers of oil? shall I give my firstborn for my transgression, the <a href="../ebd/ebd139.htm#000">fruit</a> of my body for the <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> of my soul?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> He hath shewed thee, O man, what is good; and what doth the LORD require of thee, but to do justly, and to love <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a>, and to walk humbly with thy God?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> The LORD's voice crieth unto the city, and the man of wisdom shall see thy name: hear ye the rod, and who hath appointed it.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> Are there yet the treasures of wickedness in the house of the wicked, and the scant measure that is abominable?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> Shall I count them pure with the wicked balances, and with the <a href="../ebd/ebd041.htm#008">bag</a> of deceitful <a href="../ebd/ebd380.htm#006">weights</a>?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> For the rich men thereof are full of violence, and the inhabitants thereof have spoken lies, and their tongue is deceitful in their mouth.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> Therefore also will I make thee sick in smiting thee, in making thee desolate because of thy sins.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> Thou shalt eat, but not be satisfied; and thy casting down shall be in the midst of thee; and thou shalt take hold, but shalt not deliver; and that which thou deliverest will I give up to the <a href="../ebd/ebd354.htm#003">sword</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> Thou shalt sow, but thou shalt not reap; thou shalt tread the olives, but thou shalt not <a href="../ebd/ebd024.htm#008">anoint</a> thee with oil; and sweet <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#000">wine</a>, but shalt not <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#000">wine</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> For the statutes of <a href="../ebd/ebd278.htm#006">Omri</a> are kept, and all the works of the house of <a href="../ebd/ebd013.htm#002">Ahab</a>, and ye walk in their counsels; that I should make thee a desolation, and the inhabitants thereof an hissing: therefore ye shall bear the reproach of my people.<br>
 </p>
