 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Leviticus Chapter 17</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> And the LORD spake unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Speak unto <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a>, and unto his sons, and unto all the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, and say unto them; This is the thing which the LORD hath commanded, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> What man soever there be of the house of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, that killeth an <a href="../ebd/ebd281.htm#007">ox</a>, or <a href="../ebd/ebd223.htm#003">lamb</a>, or <a href="../ebd/ebd151.htm#001">goat</a>, in the <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#002">camp</a>, or that killeth it out of the <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#002">camp</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And bringeth it not unto the door of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>, to offer an <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> unto the LORD before the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the LORD; <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> shall be imputed unto that man; he hath shed <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a>; and that man shall be cut off from among his people:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> To the end that the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> may bring their sacrifices, which they offer in the open field, even that they may bring them unto the LORD, unto the door of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>, unto the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a>, and offer them for peace offerings unto the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> shall sprinkle the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> of the LORD at the door of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>, and burn the <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#005">fat</a> for a sweet savour unto the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And they shall no more offer their sacrifices unto devils, after whom they have gone a whoring. This shall be a statute for ever unto them throughout their generations.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And thou shalt say unto them, Whatsoever man there be of the house of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, or of the strangers which sojourn among you, that offereth a burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> or <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#002">sacrifice</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And bringeth it not unto the door of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>, to offer it unto the LORD; even that man shall be cut off from among his people.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And whatsoever man there be of the house of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, or of the strangers that sojourn among you, that eateth any manner of <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a>; I will even set my face against that soul that eateth <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a>, and will cut him off from among his people.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> For the life of the <a href="../ebd/ebd135.htm#007">flesh</a> is in the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a>: and I have given it to you upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> to make an <a href="../ebd/ebd036.htm#004">atonement</a> for your souls: for it is the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> that maketh an <a href="../ebd/ebd036.htm#004">atonement</a> for the soul.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> Therefore I said unto the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, No soul of you shall eat <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a>, neither shall any <a href="../ebd/ebd351.htm#009">stranger</a> that sojourneth among you eat <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> And whatsoever man there be of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, or of the strangers that sojourn among you, which hunteth and catcheth any <a href="../ebd/ebd049.htm#001">beast</a> or fowl that may be eaten; he shall even pour out the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> thereof, and cover it with <a href="../ebd/ebd109.htm#008">dust</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> For it is the life of all <a href="../ebd/ebd135.htm#007">flesh</a>; the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> of it is for the life thereof: therefore I said unto the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, Ye shall eat the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> of no manner of <a href="../ebd/ebd135.htm#007">flesh</a>: for the life of all <a href="../ebd/ebd135.htm#007">flesh</a> is the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> thereof: whosoever eateth it shall be cut off.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And every soul that eateth that which died of itself, or that which was torn with beasts, whether it be one of your own country, or a <a href="../ebd/ebd351.htm#009">stranger</a>, he shall both wash his clothes, and bathe himself in water, and be unclean until the even: then shall he be <a href="../ebd/ebd084.htm#005">clean</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> But if he wash them not, nor bathe his <a href="../ebd/ebd135.htm#007">flesh</a>; then he shall bear his iniquity.<br>
 </p>
