 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 131</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Lord, my heart is not haughty, nor mine eyes lofty: neither do I exercise myself in great matters, or in things too high for me.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Surely I have behaved and quieted myself, as a child that is weaned of his mother: my soul is even as a weaned child.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Let <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> hope in the LORD from henceforth and for ever.<br>
 </p>
