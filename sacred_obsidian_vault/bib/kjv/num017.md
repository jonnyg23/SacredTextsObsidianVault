 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Numbers Chapter 17</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> And the LORD spake unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Speak unto the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, and take of every one of them a rod according to the house of their fathers, of all their princes according to the house of their fathers twelve rods: write thou every man's name upon his rod.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And thou shalt write Aaron's name upon the rod of <a href="../ebd/ebd227.htm#006">Levi</a>: for one rod shall be for the head of the house of their fathers.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And thou shalt lay them up in the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> before the <a href="../ebd/ebd362.htm#008">testimony</a>, where I will meet with you.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And it shall come to pass, that the man's rod, whom I shall choose, shall blossom: and I will make to cease from me the murmurings of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, whereby they murmur against you.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> spake unto the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, and every one of their princes gave him a rod apiece, for each <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#004">prince</a> one, according to their fathers' houses, even twelve rods: and the rod of <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> was among their rods.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> laid up the rods before the LORD in the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#008">witness</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And it came to pass, that on the morrow <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> went into the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#008">witness</a>; and, behold, the rod of <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> for the house of <a href="../ebd/ebd227.htm#006">Levi</a> was budded, and brought forth buds, and bloomed blossoms, and yielded almonds.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> brought out all the rods from before the LORD unto all the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>: and they looked, and took every man his rod.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And the LORD said unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, Bring Aaron's rod again before the <a href="../ebd/ebd362.htm#008">testimony</a>, to be kept for a token against the rebels; and thou shalt quite take away their murmurings from me, that they die not.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> did so: as the LORD commanded him, so did he.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> spake unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, saying, Behold, we die, we perish, we all perish.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> Whosoever cometh any thing near unto the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the LORD shall die: shall we be consumed with dying?<br>
 </p>
