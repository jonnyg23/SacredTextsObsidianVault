 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Isaiah Chapter 15</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> The <a href="../ebd/ebd066.htm#007">burden</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd258.htm#005">Moab</a>. Because in the night Ar of <a href="../ebd/ebd258.htm#005">Moab</a> is laid waste, and brought to silence; because in the night <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#007">Kir</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd258.htm#005">Moab</a> is laid waste, and brought to silence;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> He is gone up to <a href="../ebd/ebd042.htm#000">Bajith</a>, and to <a href="../ebd/ebd103.htm#005">Dibon</a>, the high places, to weep: <a href="../ebd/ebd258.htm#005">Moab</a> shall howl over <a href="../ebd/ebd268.htm#004">Nebo</a>, and over <a href="../ebd/ebd246.htm#000">Medeba</a>: on all their heads shall be <a href="../ebd/ebd042.htm#008">baldness</a>, and every <a href="../ebd/ebd049.htm#000">beard</a> cut off.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> In their streets they shall gird themselves with <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#001">sackcloth</a>: on the tops of their houses, and in their streets, every one shall howl, weeping abundantly.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd176.htm#009">Heshbon</a> shall cry, and <a href="../ebd/ebd114.htm#009">Elealeh</a>: their voice shall be heard even unto <a href="../ebd/ebd195.htm#005">Jahaz</a>: therefore the armed soldiers of <a href="../ebd/ebd258.htm#005">Moab</a> shall cry out; his life shall be grievous unto him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> My heart shall cry out for <a href="../ebd/ebd258.htm#005">Moab</a>; his fugitives shall flee unto <a href="../ebd/ebd395.htm#004">Zoar</a>, an <a href="../ebd/ebd171.htm#006">heifer</a> of three years old: for by the mounting up of <a href="../ebd/ebd233.htm#001">Luhith</a> with weeping shall they go it up; for in the way of <a href="../ebd/ebd182.htm#005">Horonaim</a> they shall raise up a cry of <a href="../ebd/ebd102.htm#004">destruction</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> For the waters of Nimrim shall be desolate: for the <a href="../ebd/ebd168.htm#002">hay</a> is withered away, the <a href="../ebd/ebd154.htm#005">grass</a> faileth, there is no green thing.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Therefore the abundance they have gotten, and that which they have laid up, shall they carry away to the <a href="../ebd/ebd065.htm#006">brook</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd381.htm#006">willows</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> For the cry is gone round about the borders of <a href="../ebd/ebd258.htm#005">Moab</a>; the howling thereof unto <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#007">Eglaim</a>, and the howling thereof unto Beerelim.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> For the waters of Dimon shall be full of <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a>: for I will bring more upon Dimon, <a href="../ebd/ebd230.htm#001">lions</a> upon him that escapeth of <a href="../ebd/ebd258.htm#005">Moab</a>, and upon the remnant of the land.<br>
 </p>
