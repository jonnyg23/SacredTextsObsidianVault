 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Proverbs Chapter 10</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> The proverbs of <a href="../ebd/ebd347.htm#007">Solomon</a>. A <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#004">wise</a> son maketh a glad father: but a foolish son is the heaviness of his mother.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Treasures of wickedness profit nothing: but <a href="../ebd/ebd313.htm#004">righteousness</a> delivereth from death.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> The LORD will not suffer the soul of the righteous to famish: but he casteth away the substance of the wicked.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> He becometh <a href="../ebd/ebd298.htm#006">poor</a> that dealeth with a slack hand: but the hand of the diligent maketh rich.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> He that gathereth in summer is a <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#004">wise</a> son: but he that sleepeth in <a href="../ebd/ebd165.htm#008">harvest</a> is a son that causeth shame.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Blessings are upon the head of the just: but violence covereth the mouth of the wicked.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> The memory of the just is blessed: but the name of the wicked shall rot.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> The <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#004">wise</a> in heart will receive commandments: but a prating fool shall fall.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> He that walketh uprightly walketh surely: but he that perverteth his ways shall be known.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> He that winketh with the eye causeth sorrow: but a prating fool shall fall.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> The mouth of a righteous man is a well of life: but violence covereth the mouth of the wicked.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd167.htm#002">Hatred</a> stirreth up strifes: but love covereth all sins.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> In the lips of him that hath understanding wisdom is found: but a rod is for the back of him that is void of understanding.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#004">Wise</a> men lay up knowledge: but the mouth of the foolish is near <a href="../ebd/ebd102.htm#004">destruction</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> The rich man's wealth is his strong city: the <a href="../ebd/ebd102.htm#004">destruction</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd298.htm#006">poor</a> is their poverty.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> The labour of the righteous tendeth to life: the <a href="../ebd/ebd139.htm#000">fruit</a> of the wicked to <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> He is in the way of life that keepeth instruction: but he that refuseth reproof erreth.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> He that hideth <a href="../ebd/ebd167.htm#002">hatred</a> with lying lips, and he that uttereth a slander, is a fool.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> In the multitude of words there wanteth not <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a>: but he that refraineth his lips is <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#004">wise</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> The tongue of the just is as choice <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#000">silver</a>: the heart of the wicked is little worth.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> The lips of the righteous feed many: but fools die for want of wisdom.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> The blessing of the LORD, it maketh rich, and he addeth no sorrow with it.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> It is as sport to a fool to do mischief: but a man of understanding hath wisdom.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> The fear of the wicked, it shall come upon him: but the desire of the righteous shall be granted.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> As the whirlwind passeth, so is the wicked no more: but the righteous is an <a href="../ebd/ebd127.htm#007">everlasting</a> foundation.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> As <a href="../ebd/ebd377.htm#006">vinegar</a> to the teeth, and as smoke to the eyes, so is the sluggard to them that send him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> The fear of the LORD prolongeth days: but the years of the wicked shall be shortened.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="028">28</a> The hope of the righteous shall be gladness: but the expectation of the wicked shall perish.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="029">29</a> The way of the LORD is strength to the upright: but <a href="../ebd/ebd102.htm#004">destruction</a> shall be to the workers of iniquity.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="030">30</a> The righteous shall never be removed: but the wicked shall not inhabit the earth.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="031">31</a> The mouth of the just bringeth forth wisdom: but the froward tongue shall be cut out.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="032">32</a> The lips of the righteous know what is acceptable: but the mouth of the wicked speaketh frowardness.<br>
 </p>
