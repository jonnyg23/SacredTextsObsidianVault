 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 125</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> They that trust in the LORD shall be as <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#007">mount</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd394.htm#002">Zion</a>, which cannot be removed, but abideth for ever.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> As the mountains are round about <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>, so the LORD is round about his people from henceforth even for ever.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> For the rod of the wicked shall not rest upon the lot of the righteous; lest the righteous put forth their hands unto iniquity.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Do good, O LORD, unto those that be good, and to them that are upright in their hearts.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> As for such as turn aside unto their crooked ways, the LORD shall lead them forth with the workers of iniquity: but peace shall be upon <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>.<br>
 </p>
