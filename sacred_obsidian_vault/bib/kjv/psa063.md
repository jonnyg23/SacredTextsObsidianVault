 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 63</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> O God, thou art my God; early will I seek thee: my soul thirsteth for thee, my <a href="../ebd/ebd135.htm#007">flesh</a> longeth for thee in a dry and thirsty land, where no water is;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> To see thy power and thy <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a>, so as I have seen thee in the <a href="../ebd/ebd321.htm#005">sanctuary</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Because thy lovingkindness is better than life, my lips shall praise thee.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Thus will I <a href="../ebd/ebd060.htm#008">bless</a> thee while I live: I will lift up my hands in thy name.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> My soul shall be satisfied as with marrow and fatness; and my mouth shall praise thee with joyful lips:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> When I remember thee upon my bed, and meditate on thee in the night <a href="../ebd/ebd379.htm#003">watches</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Because thou hast been my help, therefore in the <a href="../ebd/ebd330.htm#002">shadow</a> of thy wings will I rejoice.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> My soul followeth hard after thee: thy right hand upholdeth me.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> But those that seek my soul, to destroy it, shall go into the lower parts of the earth.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> They shall fall by the <a href="../ebd/ebd354.htm#003">sword</a>: they shall be a portion for foxes.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> But the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> shall rejoice in God; every one that sweareth by him shall <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a>: but the mouth of them that speak lies shall be stopped.<br>
 </p>
