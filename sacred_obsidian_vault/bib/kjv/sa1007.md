 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: 1 Kings (1 Samuel) Chapter 7</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> And the men of Kirjathjearim came, and fetched up the <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#000">ark</a> of the LORD, and brought it into the house of <a href="../ebd/ebd004.htm#001">Abinadab</a> in the hill, and sanctified <a href="../ebd/ebd115.htm#000">Eleazar</a> his son to keep the <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#000">ark</a> of the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> And it came to pass, while the <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#000">ark</a> abode in Kirjathjearim, that the time was long; for it was twenty years: and all the house of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> lamented after the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd321.htm#001">Samuel</a> spake unto all the house of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, saying, If ye do return unto the LORD with all your hearts, then put away the strange gods and <a href="../ebd/ebd034.htm#004">Ashtaroth</a> from among you, and prepare your hearts unto the LORD, and serve him only: and he will deliver you out of the hand of the <a href="../ebd/ebd294.htm#002">Philistines</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Then the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> did put away <a href="../ebd/ebd039.htm#007">Baalim</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd034.htm#004">Ashtaroth</a>, and served the LORD only.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd321.htm#001">Samuel</a> said, Gather all <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> to Mizpeh, and I will pray for you unto the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And they gathered together to Mizpeh, and drew water, and poured it out before the LORD, and fasted on that day, and said there, We have sinned against the LORD. And <a href="../ebd/ebd321.htm#001">Samuel</a> judged the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> in Mizpeh.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And when the <a href="../ebd/ebd294.htm#002">Philistines</a> heard that the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> were gathered together to Mizpeh, the lords of the <a href="../ebd/ebd294.htm#002">Philistines</a> went up against <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>. And when the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> heard it, they were afraid of the <a href="../ebd/ebd294.htm#002">Philistines</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> said to <a href="../ebd/ebd321.htm#001">Samuel</a>, Cease not to cry unto the LORD our God for us, that he will save us out of the hand of the <a href="../ebd/ebd294.htm#002">Philistines</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd321.htm#001">Samuel</a> took a sucking <a href="../ebd/ebd223.htm#003">lamb</a>, and offered it for a burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> wholly unto the LORD: and <a href="../ebd/ebd321.htm#001">Samuel</a> cried unto the LORD for <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>; and the LORD heard him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And as <a href="../ebd/ebd321.htm#001">Samuel</a> was <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> up the burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, the <a href="../ebd/ebd294.htm#002">Philistines</a> drew near to battle against <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>: but the LORD thundered with a great <a href="../ebd/ebd365.htm#003">thunder</a> on that day upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd294.htm#002">Philistines</a>, and discomfited them; and they were smitten before <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And the men of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> went out of Mizpeh, and pursued the <a href="../ebd/ebd294.htm#002">Philistines</a>, and smote them, until they came under Bethcar.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> Then <a href="../ebd/ebd321.htm#001">Samuel</a> took a <a href="../ebd/ebd351.htm#005">stone</a>, and set it between Mizpeh and <a href="../ebd/ebd335.htm#001">Shen</a>, and called the name of it Ebenezer, saying, Hitherto hath the LORD helped us.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> So the <a href="../ebd/ebd294.htm#002">Philistines</a> were subdued, and they came no more into the coast of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>: and the hand of the LORD was against the <a href="../ebd/ebd294.htm#002">Philistines</a> all the days of <a href="../ebd/ebd321.htm#001">Samuel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> And the cities which the <a href="../ebd/ebd294.htm#002">Philistines</a> had taken from <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> were restored to <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, from <a href="../ebd/ebd114.htm#001">Ekron</a> even unto <a href="../ebd/ebd143.htm#009">Gath</a>; and the coasts thereof did <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> deliver out of the hands of the <a href="../ebd/ebd294.htm#002">Philistines</a>. And there was peace between <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> and the <a href="../ebd/ebd021.htm#006">Amorites</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd321.htm#001">Samuel</a> judged <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> all the days of his life.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> And he went from year to year in <a href="../ebd/ebd083.htm#006">circuit</a> to <a href="../ebd/ebd055.htm#006">Bethel</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd149.htm#001">Gilgal</a>, and Mizpeh, and judged <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> in all those places.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> And his return was to <a href="../ebd/ebd306.htm#005">Ramah</a>; for there was his house; and there he judged <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>; and there he built an <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> unto the LORD.<br>
 </p>
