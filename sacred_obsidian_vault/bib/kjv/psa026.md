 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 26</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd213.htm#005">Judge</a> me, O LORD; for I have walked in mine integrity: I have trusted also in the LORD; therefore I shall not slide.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Examine me, O LORD, and prove me; try my <a href="../ebd/ebd310.htm#001">reins</a> and my heart.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> For thy lovingkindness is before mine eyes: and I have walked in thy truth.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> I have not sat with vain persons, neither will I go in with dissemblers.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> I have hated the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> of evil doers; and will not sit with the wicked.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> I will wash mine hands in innocency: so will I compass thine <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>, O LORD:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> That I may publish with the voice of thanksgiving, and tell of all thy wondrous works.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> LORD, I have loved the <a href="../ebd/ebd156.htm#007">habitation</a> of thy house, and the place where thine honour dwelleth.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> Gather not my soul with sinners, nor my life with bloody men:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> In whose hands is mischief, and their right hand is full of bribes.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> But as for me, I will walk in mine integrity: redeem me, and be merciful unto me.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> My foot standeth in an even place: in the congregations will I <a href="../ebd/ebd060.htm#008">bless</a> the LORD.<br>
 </p>
