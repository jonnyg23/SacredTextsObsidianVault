 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Ephesians Chapter 6</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Children, obey your parents in the Lord: for this is right.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Honour thy father and mother; which is the first commandment with promise;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> That it may be well with thee, and thou mayest live long on the earth.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And, ye fathers, provoke not your children to wrath: but bring them up in the nurture and admonition of the Lord.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Servants, be obedient to them that are your masters according to the <a href="../ebd/ebd135.htm#007">flesh</a>, with fear and trembling, in singleness of your heart, as unto <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Not with eyeservice, as menpleasers; but as the servants of <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a>, doing the will of God from the heart;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> With good will doing service, as to the Lord, and not to men:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> Knowing that whatsoever good thing any man doeth, the same shall he receive of the Lord, whether he be <a href="../ebd/ebd062.htm#001">bond</a> or free.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And, ye masters, do the same things unto them, forbearing threatening: knowing that your Master also is in <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#006">heaven</a>; neither is there respect of persons with him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> Finally, my brethren, be strong in the Lord, and in the power of his might.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> Put on the whole <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#006">armour</a> of God, that ye may be able to stand against the wiles of the <a href="../ebd/ebd102.htm#007">devil</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> For we <a href="../ebd/ebd384.htm#004">wrestle</a> not against <a href="../ebd/ebd135.htm#007">flesh</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a>, but against principalities, against powers, against the rulers of the <a href="../ebd/ebd097.htm#008">darkness</a> of this world, against spiritual wickedness in high places.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> Wherefore take unto you the whole <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#006">armour</a> of God, that ye may be able to withstand in the evil day, and having done all, to stand.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> Stand therefore, having your loins girt about with truth, and having on the <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#007">breastplate</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd313.htm#004">righteousness</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And your feet shod with the preparation of the <a href="../ebd/ebd153.htm#003">gospel</a> of peace;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> Above all, taking the <a href="../ebd/ebd337.htm#001">shield</a> of faith, wherewith ye shall be able to quench all the fiery darts of the wicked.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> And take the <a href="../ebd/ebd173.htm#004">helmet</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd320.htm#003">salvation</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd354.htm#003">sword</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd349.htm#009">Spirit</a>, which is the word of God:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> Praying always with all <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#000">prayer</a> and supplication in the <a href="../ebd/ebd349.htm#009">Spirit</a>, and watching thereunto with all perseverance and supplication for all saints;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> And for me, that utterance may be given unto me, that I may open my mouth boldly, to make known the <a href="../ebd/ebd263.htm#006">mystery</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd153.htm#003">gospel</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> For which I am an <a href="../ebd/ebd019.htm#008">ambassador</a> in bonds: that therein I may speak boldly, as I ought to speak.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> But that ye also may know my affairs, and how I do, <a href="../ebd/ebd373.htm#008">Tychicus</a>, a beloved brother and <a href="../ebd/ebd130.htm#005">faithful</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd255.htm#007">minister</a> in the Lord, shall make known to you all things:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> Whom I have sent unto you for the same purpose, that ye might know our affairs, and that he might comfort your hearts.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> Peace be to the brethren, and love with faith, from God the Father and the Lord <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd154.htm#000">Grace</a> be with all them that love our Lord <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a> in sincerity. <a href="../ebd/ebd020.htm#001">Amen</a>.<br>
 </p>
