 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 75</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Unto thee, O God, do we give thanks, unto thee do we give thanks: for that thy name is near thy wondrous works declare.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> When I shall receive the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> I will <a href="../ebd/ebd213.htm#005">judge</a> uprightly.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> The earth and all the inhabitants thereof are dissolved: I bear up the pillars of it. <a href="../ebd/ebd326.htm#008">Selah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> I said unto the fools, Deal not foolishly: and to the wicked, Lift not up the <a href="../ebd/ebd182.htm#003">horn</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Lift not up your <a href="../ebd/ebd182.htm#003">horn</a> on high: speak not with a stiff <a href="../ebd/ebd269.htm#000">neck</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> For promotion cometh neither from the <a href="../ebd/ebd111.htm#000">east</a>, nor from the west, nor from the south.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> But God is the <a href="../ebd/ebd213.htm#005">judge</a>: he putteth down one, and setteth up another.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> For in the hand of the LORD there is a <a href="../ebd/ebd094.htm#000">cup</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#000">wine</a> is red; it is full of mixture; and he poureth out of the same: but the <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#007">dregs</a> thereof, all the wicked of the earth shall wring them out, and <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a> them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> But I will declare for ever; I will sing praises to the God of <a href="../ebd/ebd194.htm#007">Jacob</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> All the horns of the wicked also will I cut off; but the horns of the righteous shall be exalted.<br>
 </p>
