 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Ezekiel Chapter 4</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Thou also, son of man, take thee a tile, and lay it before thee, and pourtray upon it the city, even <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> And lay siege against it, and build a fort against it, and cast a <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#007">mount</a> against it; set the <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#002">camp</a> also against it, and set battering rams against it round about.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Moreover take thou unto thee an <a href="../ebd/ebd189.htm#004">iron</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd283.htm#009">pan</a>, and set it for a wall of <a href="../ebd/ebd189.htm#004">iron</a> between thee and the city: and set thy face against it, and it shall be besieged, and thou shalt lay siege against it. This shall be a sign to the house of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Lie thou also upon thy left side, and lay the iniquity of the house of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> upon it: according to the number of the days that thou shalt lie upon it thou shalt bear their iniquity.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> For I have laid upon thee the years of their iniquity, according to the number of the days, three hundred and ninety days: so shalt thou bear the iniquity of the house of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And when thou hast accomplished them, lie again on thy right side, and thou shalt bear the iniquity of the house of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a> forty days: I have appointed thee each day for a year.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Therefore thou shalt set thy face toward the siege of <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>, and thine arm shall be uncovered, and thou shalt prophesy against it.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And, behold, I will lay <a href="../ebd/ebd043.htm#003">bands</a> upon thee, and thou shalt not turn thee from one side to another, till thou hast ended the days of thy siege.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> Take thou also unto thee <a href="../ebd/ebd381.htm#000">wheat</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd045.htm#003">barley</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd048.htm#008">beans</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd227.htm#001">lentiles</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd255.htm#003">millet</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd135.htm#000">fitches</a>, and put them in one vessel, and make thee <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#006">bread</a> thereof, according to the number of the days that thou shalt lie upon thy side, three hundred and ninety days shalt thou eat thereof.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And thy meat which thou shalt eat shall be by weight, twenty shekels a day: from time to time shalt thou eat it.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> Thou shalt <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a> also water by measure, the sixth part of an hin: from time to time shalt thou <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And thou shalt eat it as <a href="../ebd/ebd045.htm#003">barley</a> cakes, and thou shalt <a href="../ebd/ebd042.htm#001">bake</a> it with <a href="../ebd/ebd109.htm#003">dung</a> that cometh out of man, in their sight.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> And the LORD said, Even thus shall the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> eat their defiled <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#006">bread</a> among the <a href="../ebd/ebd146.htm#001">Gentiles</a>, whither I will drive them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> Then said I, Ah Lord GOD! behold, my soul hath not been polluted: for from my youth up even till now have I not eaten of that which dieth of itself, or is torn in <a href="../ebd/ebd295.htm#002">pieces</a>; neither came there abominable <a href="../ebd/ebd135.htm#007">flesh</a> into my mouth.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> Then he said unto me, Lo, I have given thee cow's <a href="../ebd/ebd109.htm#003">dung</a> for man's <a href="../ebd/ebd109.htm#003">dung</a>, and thou shalt prepare thy <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#006">bread</a> therewith.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> Moreover he said unto me, Son of man, behold, I will break the staff of <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#006">bread</a> in <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>: and they shall eat <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#006">bread</a> by weight, and with care; and they shall <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a> water by measure, and with astonishment:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> That they may want <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#006">bread</a> and water, and be astonied one with another, and consume away for their iniquity.<br>
 </p>
