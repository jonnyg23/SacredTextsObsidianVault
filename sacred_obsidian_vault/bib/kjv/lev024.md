 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Leviticus Chapter 24</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> And the LORD spake unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Command the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, that they bring unto thee pure oil <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#009">olive</a> beaten for the light, to cause the lamps to burn continually.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Without the vail of the <a href="../ebd/ebd362.htm#008">testimony</a>, in the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>, shall <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> order it from the evening unto the morning before the LORD continually: it shall be a statute for ever in your generations.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> He shall order the lamps upon the pure <a href="../ebd/ebd071.htm#001">candlestick</a> before the LORD continually.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And thou shalt take fine <a href="../ebd/ebd136.htm#001">flour</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd042.htm#001">bake</a> twelve cakes thereof: two tenth deals shall be in one <a href="../ebd/ebd068.htm#009">cake</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And thou shalt set them in two rows, six on a row, upon the pure table before the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And thou shalt put pure <a href="../ebd/ebd138.htm#004">frankincense</a> upon each row, that it may be on the <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#006">bread</a> for a memorial, even an <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> made by fire unto the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> Every <a href="../ebd/ebd317.htm#003">sabbath</a> he shall set it in order before the LORD continually, being taken from the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> by an <a href="../ebd/ebd127.htm#007">everlasting</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd091.htm#008">covenant</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And it shall be Aaron's and his sons'; and they shall eat it in the holy place: for it is most holy unto him of the offerings of the LORD made by fire by a perpetual statute.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And the son of an Israelitish woman, whose father was an Egyptian, went out among the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>: and this son of the Israelitish woman and a man of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> strove together in the <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#002">camp</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And the Israelitish woman's son blasphemed the name of the Lord, and cursed. And they brought him unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>: (and his mother's name was Shelomith, the daughter of Dibri, of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd096.htm#009">Dan</a>:)<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And they put him in <a href="../ebd/ebd379.htm#001">ward</a>, that the mind of the LORD might be shewed them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> And the LORD spake unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> Bring forth him that hath cursed without the <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#002">camp</a>; and let all that heard him lay their hands upon his head, and let all the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd351.htm#005">stone</a> him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And thou shalt speak unto the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, saying, Whosoever curseth his God shall bear his <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> And he that blasphemeth the name of the LORD, he shall surely be put to death, and all the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> shall certainly <a href="../ebd/ebd351.htm#005">stone</a> him: as well the <a href="../ebd/ebd351.htm#009">stranger</a>, as he that is born in the land, when he blasphemeth the name of the Lord, shall be put to death.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> And he that killeth any man shall surely be put to death.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> And he that killeth a <a href="../ebd/ebd049.htm#001">beast</a> shall make it good; <a href="../ebd/ebd049.htm#001">beast</a> for <a href="../ebd/ebd049.htm#001">beast</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> And if a man cause a <a href="../ebd/ebd060.htm#007">blemish</a> in his neighbour; as he hath done, so shall it be done to him;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#005">Breach</a> for <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#005">breach</a>, eye for eye, tooth for tooth: as he hath caused a <a href="../ebd/ebd060.htm#007">blemish</a> in a man, so shall it be done to him again.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> And he that killeth a <a href="../ebd/ebd049.htm#001">beast</a>, he shall restore it: and he that killeth a man, he shall be put to death.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> Ye shall have one manner of law, as well for the <a href="../ebd/ebd351.htm#009">stranger</a>, as for one of your own country: for I am the LORD your God.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> spake to the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, that they should bring forth him that had cursed out of the <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#002">camp</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd351.htm#005">stone</a> him with stones. And the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> did as the LORD commanded <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>.<br>
 </p>
