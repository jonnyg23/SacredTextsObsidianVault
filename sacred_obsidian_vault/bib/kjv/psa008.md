 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 8</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> O LORD, our Lord, how excellent is thy name in all the earth! who hast set thy <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a> above the heavens.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Out of the mouth of babes and sucklings hast thou ordained strength because of thine enemies, that thou mightest still the enemy and the avenger.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> When I consider thy heavens, the work of thy fingers, the <a href="../ebd/ebd259.htm#004">moon</a> and the <a href="../ebd/ebd350.htm#008">stars</a>, which thou hast ordained;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> What is man, that thou art mindful of him? and the son of man, that thou visitest him?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> For thou hast made him a little lower than the angels, and hast crowned him with <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a> and honour.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Thou madest him to have dominion over the works of thy hands; thou hast put all things under his feet:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> All <a href="../ebd/ebd333.htm#005">sheep</a> and oxen, yea, and the beasts of the field;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> The fowl of the air, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd134.htm#005">fish</a> of the sea, and whatsoever passeth through the paths of the seas.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> O LORD our Lord, how excellent is thy name in all the earth!<br>
 </p>
