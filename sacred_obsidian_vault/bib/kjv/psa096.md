 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 96</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> O sing unto the LORD a new song: sing unto the LORD, all the earth.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Sing unto the LORD, <a href="../ebd/ebd060.htm#008">bless</a> his name; shew forth his <a href="../ebd/ebd320.htm#003">salvation</a> from day to day.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Declare his <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a> among the <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#005">heathen</a>, his wonders among all people.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> For the LORD is great, and greatly to be praised: he is to be feared above all gods.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> For all the gods of the nations are idols: but the LORD made the heavens.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Honour and majesty are before him: strength and beauty are in his <a href="../ebd/ebd321.htm#005">sanctuary</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Give unto the LORD, O ye kindreds of the people, give unto the LORD <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a> and strength.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> Give unto the LORD the <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a> due unto his name: bring an <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, and come into his courts.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> O <a href="../ebd/ebd384.htm#002">worship</a> the LORD in the beauty of <a href="../ebd/ebd180.htm#006">holiness</a>: fear before him, all the earth.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> Say among the <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#005">heathen</a> that the LORD reigneth: the world also shall be established that it shall not be moved: he shall <a href="../ebd/ebd213.htm#005">judge</a> the people righteously.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> Let the heavens rejoice, and let the earth be glad; let the sea roar, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd139.htm#007">fulness</a> thereof.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> Let the field be joyful, and all that is therein: then shall all the trees of the <a href="../ebd/ebd383.htm#003">wood</a> rejoice<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> Before the LORD: for he cometh, for he cometh to <a href="../ebd/ebd213.htm#005">judge</a> the earth: he shall <a href="../ebd/ebd213.htm#005">judge</a> the world with <a href="../ebd/ebd313.htm#004">righteousness</a>, and the people with his truth.<br>
 </p>
