 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Philemon Chapter 1</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd287.htm#002">Paul</a>, a prisoner of <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd367.htm#002">Timothy</a> our brother, unto <a href="../ebd/ebd293.htm#005">Philemon</a> our dearly beloved, and fellowlabourer,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> And to our beloved <a href="../ebd/ebd027.htm#002">Apphia</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd029.htm#005">Archippus</a> our fellowsoldier, and to the church in thy house:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd154.htm#000">Grace</a> to you, and peace, from God our Father and the Lord <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> I thank my God, making mention of thee always in my prayers,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Hearing of thy love and faith, which thou hast toward the Lord <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a>, and toward all saints;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> That the communication of thy faith may become effectual by the acknowledging of every good thing which is in you in <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> For we have great joy and consolation in thy love, because the <a href="../ebd/ebd063.htm#004">bowels</a> of the saints are refreshed by thee, brother.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> Wherefore, though I might be much bold in <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a> to enjoin thee that which is convenient,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> Yet for love's sake I rather beseech thee, being such an one as <a href="../ebd/ebd287.htm#002">Paul</a> the aged, and now also a prisoner of <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> I beseech thee for my son <a href="../ebd/ebd278.htm#009">Onesimus</a>, whom I have begotten in my bonds:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> Which in time past was to thee unprofitable, but now profitable to thee and to me:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> Whom I have sent again: thou therefore receive him, that is, mine own <a href="../ebd/ebd063.htm#004">bowels</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> Whom I would have retained with me, that in thy stead he might have ministered unto me in the bonds of the <a href="../ebd/ebd153.htm#003">gospel</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> But without thy mind would I do nothing; that thy benefit should not be as it were of necessity, but willingly.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> For perhaps he therefore departed for a season, that thou shouldest receive him for ever;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> Not now as a servant, but above a servant, a brother beloved, specially to me, but how much more unto thee, both in the <a href="../ebd/ebd135.htm#007">flesh</a>, and in the Lord?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> If thou count me therefore a partner, receive him as myself.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> If he hath wronged thee, or oweth thee ought, put that on mine account;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> I <a href="../ebd/ebd287.htm#002">Paul</a> have written it with mine own hand, I will repay it: albeit I do not say to thee how thou owest unto me even thine own self besides.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> Yea, brother, let me have joy of thee in the Lord: refresh my <a href="../ebd/ebd063.htm#004">bowels</a> in the Lord.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> Having confidence in thy obedience I wrote unto thee, knowing that thou wilt also do more than I say.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> But withal prepare me also a lodging: for I trust that through your prayers I shall be given unto you.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> There salute thee <a href="../ebd/ebd122.htm#000">Epaphras</a>, my fellowprisoner in <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd241.htm#008">Marcus</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd030.htm#008">Aristarchus</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd101.htm#004">Demas</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd232.htm#005">Lucas</a>, my fellowlabourers.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> The <a href="../ebd/ebd154.htm#000">grace</a> of our Lord <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a> be with your <a href="../ebd/ebd349.htm#009">spirit</a>. <a href="../ebd/ebd020.htm#001">Amen</a>.<br>
 </p>
