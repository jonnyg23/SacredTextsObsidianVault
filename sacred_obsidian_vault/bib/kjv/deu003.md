 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Deuteronomy Chapter 3</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Then we turned, and went up the way to <a href="../ebd/ebd046.htm#003">Bashan</a>: and <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#002">Og</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd046.htm#003">Bashan</a> came out against us, he and all his people, to battle at <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#002">Edrei</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> And the LORD said unto me, Fear him not: for I will deliver him, and all his people, and his land, into thy hand; and thou shalt do unto him as thou didst unto <a href="../ebd/ebd343.htm#002">Sihon</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd021.htm#006">Amorites</a>, which dwelt at <a href="../ebd/ebd176.htm#009">Heshbon</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> So the LORD our God delivered into our hands <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#002">Og</a> also, the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd046.htm#003">Bashan</a>, and all his people: and we smote him until none was left to him remaining.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And we took all his cities at that time, there was not a city which we took not from them, threescore cities, all the region of <a href="../ebd/ebd030.htm#003">Argob</a>, the kingdom of <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#002">Og</a> in <a href="../ebd/ebd046.htm#003">Bashan</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> All these cities were fenced with high walls, gates, and bars; beside unwalled towns a great many.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And we utterly destroyed them, as we did unto <a href="../ebd/ebd343.htm#002">Sihon</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd176.htm#009">Heshbon</a>, utterly destroying the men, women, and children, of every city.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> But all the <a href="../ebd/ebd074.htm#002">cattle</a>, and the spoil of the cities, we took for a prey to ourselves.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And we took at that time out of the hand of the two kings of the <a href="../ebd/ebd021.htm#006">Amorites</a> the land that was on this side <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#004">Jordan</a>, from the river of <a href="../ebd/ebd032.htm#000">Arnon</a> unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#007">mount</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd175.htm#006">Hermon</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> (Which <a href="../ebd/ebd175.htm#006">Hermon</a> the Sidonians call <a href="../ebd/ebd345.htm#006">Sirion</a>; and the <a href="../ebd/ebd021.htm#006">Amorites</a> call it <a href="../ebd/ebd335.htm#002">Shenir</a>;)<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> All the cities of the <a href="../ebd/ebd297.htm#002">plain</a>, and all <a href="../ebd/ebd148.htm#009">Gilead</a>, and all <a href="../ebd/ebd046.htm#003">Bashan</a>, unto Salchah and <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#002">Edrei</a>, cities of the kingdom of <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#002">Og</a> in <a href="../ebd/ebd046.htm#003">Bashan</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> For only <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#002">Og</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd046.htm#003">Bashan</a> remained of the remnant of <a href="../ebd/ebd147.htm#006">giants</a>; behold his <a href="../ebd/ebd049.htm#009">bedstead</a> was a <a href="../ebd/ebd049.htm#009">bedstead</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd189.htm#004">iron</a>; is it not in Rabbath of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd021.htm#002">Ammon</a>? nine cubits was the length thereof, and four cubits the breadth of it, after the <a href="../ebd/ebd093.htm#006">cubit</a> of a man.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And this land, which we possessed at that time, from <a href="../ebd/ebd032.htm#001">Aroer</a>, which is by the river <a href="../ebd/ebd032.htm#000">Arnon</a>, and half <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#007">mount</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd148.htm#009">Gilead</a>, and the cities thereof, gave I unto the Reubenites and to the Gadites.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> And the rest of <a href="../ebd/ebd148.htm#009">Gilead</a>, and all <a href="../ebd/ebd046.htm#003">Bashan</a>, being the kingdom of <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#002">Og</a>, gave I unto the half <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd240.htm#002">Manasseh</a>; all the region of <a href="../ebd/ebd030.htm#003">Argob</a>, with all <a href="../ebd/ebd046.htm#003">Bashan</a>, which was called the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd147.htm#006">giants</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd196.htm#001">Jair</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd240.htm#002">Manasseh</a> took all the country of <a href="../ebd/ebd030.htm#003">Argob</a> unto the coasts of Geshuri and Maachathi; and called them after his own name, Bashanhavothjair, unto this day.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And I gave <a href="../ebd/ebd148.htm#009">Gilead</a> unto <a href="../ebd/ebd235.htm#006">Machir</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> And unto the Reubenites and unto the Gadites I gave from <a href="../ebd/ebd148.htm#009">Gilead</a> even unto the river <a href="../ebd/ebd032.htm#000">Arnon</a> half the <a href="../ebd/ebd376.htm#008">valley</a>, and the border even unto the river <a href="../ebd/ebd193.htm#006">Jabbok</a>, which is the border of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd021.htm#002">Ammon</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> The <a href="../ebd/ebd297.htm#002">plain</a> also, and <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#004">Jordan</a>, and the coast thereof, from <a href="../ebd/ebd081.htm#000">Chinnereth</a> even unto the sea of the <a href="../ebd/ebd297.htm#002">plain</a>, even the <a href="../ebd/ebd319.htm#008">salt</a> sea, under Ashdothpisgah eastward.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> And I commanded you at that time, saying, The LORD your God hath given you this land to possess it: ye shall pass over armed before your brethren the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, all that are meet for the war.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> But your wives, and your little ones, and your <a href="../ebd/ebd074.htm#002">cattle</a>, (for I know that ye have much <a href="../ebd/ebd074.htm#002">cattle</a>,) shall abide in your cities which I have given you;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> Until the LORD have given rest unto your brethren, as well as unto you, and until they also possess the land which the LORD your God hath given them beyond <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#004">Jordan</a>: and then shall ye return every man unto his possession, which I have given you.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> And I commanded <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#006">Joshua</a> at that time, saying, Thine eyes have seen all that the LORD your God hath done unto these two kings: so shall the LORD do unto all the kingdoms whither thou passest.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> Ye shall not fear them: for the LORD your God he shall fight for you.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> And I besought the LORD at that time, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> O Lord GOD, thou hast begun to shew thy servant thy greatness, and thy mighty hand: for what God is there in <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#006">heaven</a> or in earth, that can do according to thy works, and according to thy might?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> I pray thee, let me go over, and see the good land that is beyond <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#004">Jordan</a>, that goodly mountain, and <a href="../ebd/ebd226.htm#001">Lebanon</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> But the LORD was wroth with me for your sakes, and would not hear me: and the LORD said unto me, Let it suffice thee; speak no more unto me of this matter.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> Get thee up into the top of <a href="../ebd/ebd296.htm#004">Pisgah</a>, and lift up thine eyes <a href="../ebd/ebd380.htm#008">westward</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd275.htm#002">northward</a>, and southward, and eastward, and behold it with thine eyes: for thou shalt not go over this <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#004">Jordan</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="028">28</a> But charge <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#006">Joshua</a>, and encourage him, and strengthen him: for he shall go over before this people, and he shall cause them to inherit the land which thou shalt see.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="029">29</a> So we abode in the <a href="../ebd/ebd376.htm#008">valley</a> over against Bethpeor.<br>
 </p>
