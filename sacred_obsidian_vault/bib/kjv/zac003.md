 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Zechariah Chapter 3</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> And he shewed me <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#006">Joshua</a> the high <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> standing before the <a href="../ebd/ebd024.htm#001">angel</a> of the LORD, and <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#000">Satan</a> standing at his right hand to resist him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> And the LORD said unto <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#000">Satan</a>, The LORD rebuke thee, O <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#000">Satan</a>; even the LORD that hath <a href="../ebd/ebd081.htm#008">chosen</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a> rebuke thee: is not this a brand plucked out of the fire?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Now <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#006">Joshua</a> was clothed with filthy garments, and stood before the <a href="../ebd/ebd024.htm#001">angel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And he answered and spake unto those that stood before him, saying, Take away the filthy garments from him. And unto him he said, Behold, I have caused thine iniquity to pass from thee, and I will clothe thee with change of raiment.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And I said, Let them set a fair <a href="../ebd/ebd257.htm#006">mitre</a> upon his head. So they set a fair <a href="../ebd/ebd257.htm#006">mitre</a> upon his head, and clothed him with garments. And the <a href="../ebd/ebd024.htm#001">angel</a> of the LORD stood by.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd024.htm#001">angel</a> of the LORD protested unto <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#006">Joshua</a>, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Thus saith the LORD of hosts; If thou wilt walk in my ways, and if thou wilt keep my charge, then thou shalt also <a href="../ebd/ebd213.htm#005">judge</a> my house, and shalt also keep my courts, and I will give thee places to walk among these that stand by.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> Hear now, O <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#006">Joshua</a> the high <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a>, thou, and thy fellows that sit before thee: for they are men wondered at: for, behold, I will bring forth my servant the <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#002">BRANCH</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> For behold the <a href="../ebd/ebd351.htm#005">stone</a> that I have laid before <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#006">Joshua</a>; upon one <a href="../ebd/ebd351.htm#005">stone</a> shall be <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> eyes: behold, I will engrave the <a href="../ebd/ebd155.htm#000">graving</a> thereof, saith the LORD of hosts, and I will remove the iniquity of that land in one day.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> In that day, saith the LORD of hosts, shall ye call every man his neighbour under the <a href="../ebd/ebd377.htm#004">vine</a> and under the <a href="../ebd/ebd133.htm#001">fig</a> tree.<br>
 </p>
