 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: 2 Timothy Chapter 1</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd287.htm#002">Paul</a>, an <a href="../ebd/ebd026.htm#008">apostle</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a> by the will of God, according to the promise of life which is in <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> To <a href="../ebd/ebd367.htm#002">Timothy</a>, my dearly beloved son: <a href="../ebd/ebd154.htm#000">Grace</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a>, and peace, from God the Father and <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a> our Lord.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> I thank God, whom I serve from my forefathers with pure <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#006">conscience</a>, that without ceasing I have remembrance of thee in my prayers night and day;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Greatly desiring to see thee, being mindful of thy tears, that I may be filled with joy;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> When I call to remembrance the unfeigned faith that is in thee, which dwelt first in thy grandmother <a href="../ebd/ebd231.htm#004">Lois</a>, and thy mother <a href="../ebd/ebd126.htm#009">Eunice</a>; and I am persuaded that in thee also.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Wherefore I put thee in remembrance that thou stir up the <a href="../ebd/ebd148.htm#005">gift</a> of God, which is in thee by the putting on of my hands.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> For God hath not given us the <a href="../ebd/ebd349.htm#009">spirit</a> of fear; but of power, and of love, and of a sound mind.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> Be not thou therefore ashamed of the <a href="../ebd/ebd362.htm#008">testimony</a> of our Lord, nor of me his prisoner: but be thou partaker of the <a href="../ebd/ebd011.htm#006">afflictions</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd153.htm#003">gospel</a> according to the power of God;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> Who hath saved us, and called us with an holy <a href="../ebd/ebd069.htm#007">calling</a>, not according to our works, but according to his own purpose and <a href="../ebd/ebd154.htm#000">grace</a>, which was given us in <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a> before the world began,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> But is now made manifest by the appearing of our <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#003">Saviour</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a>, who hath abolished death, and hath brought life and <a href="../ebd/ebd187.htm#009">immortality</a> to light through the <a href="../ebd/ebd153.htm#003">gospel</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> Whereunto I am appointed a preacher, and an <a href="../ebd/ebd026.htm#008">apostle</a>, and a teacher of the <a href="../ebd/ebd146.htm#001">Gentiles</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> For the which cause I also suffer these things: nevertheless I am not ashamed: for I know whom I have believed, and am persuaded that he is able to keep that which I have committed unto him against that day.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> Hold <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#004">fast</a> the form of sound words, which thou hast heard of me, in faith and love which is in <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> That good thing which was committed unto thee keep by the Holy <a href="../ebd/ebd147.htm#005">Ghost</a> which dwelleth in us.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> This thou knowest, that all they which are in <a href="../ebd/ebd034.htm#008">Asia</a> be turned away from me; of whom are <a href="../ebd/ebd294.htm#008">Phygellus</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd175.htm#005">Hermogenes</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> The Lord give <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> unto the house of <a href="../ebd/ebd279.htm#000">Onesiphorus</a>; for he oft refreshed me, and was not ashamed of my <a href="../ebd/ebd075.htm#008">chain</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> But, when he was in <a href="../ebd/ebd315.htm#007">Rome</a>, he sought me out very diligently, and found me.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> The Lord grant unto him that he may find <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> of the Lord in that day: and in how many things he ministered unto me at <a href="../ebd/ebd122.htm#007">Ephesus</a>, thou knowest very well.<br>
 </p>
