 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Proverbs Chapter 1</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> The proverbs of <a href="../ebd/ebd347.htm#007">Solomon</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> To know wisdom and instruction; to perceive the words of understanding;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> To receive the instruction of wisdom, <a href="../ebd/ebd214.htm#007">justice</a>, and judgment, and equity;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> To give subtilty to the simple, to the young man knowledge and discretion.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> A <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#004">wise</a> man will hear, and will increase learning; and a man of understanding shall attain unto <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#004">wise</a> counsels:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> To understand a <a href="../ebd/ebd301.htm#002">proverb</a>, and the interpretation; the words of the <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#004">wise</a>, and their dark sayings.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> The fear of the LORD is the beginning of knowledge: but fools despise wisdom and instruction.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> My son, hear the instruction of thy father, and forsake not the law of thy mother:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> For they shall be an ornament of <a href="../ebd/ebd154.htm#000">grace</a> unto thy head, and chains about thy <a href="../ebd/ebd269.htm#000">neck</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> My son, if sinners entice thee, consent thou not.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> If they say, Come with us, let us lay wait for <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a>, let us lurk privily for the innocent without cause:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> Let us <a href="../ebd/ebd353.htm#009">swallow</a> them up alive as the <a href="../ebd/ebd154.htm#008">grave</a>; and whole, as those that go down into the <a href="../ebd/ebd296.htm#007">pit</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> We shall find all precious substance, we shall fill our houses with spoil:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> Cast in thy lot among us; let us all have one <a href="../ebd/ebd302.htm#008">purse</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> My son, walk not thou in the way with them; refrain thy foot from their path:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> For their feet run to evil, and make haste to shed <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> Surely in vain the <a href="../ebd/ebd271.htm#002">net</a> is spread in the sight of any <a href="../ebd/ebd059.htm#000">bird</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> And they lay wait for their own <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a>; they lurk privily for their own lives.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> So are the ways of every one that is greedy of gain; which taketh away the life of the owners thereof.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> Wisdom crieth without; she uttereth her voice in the streets:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> She crieth in the chief place of concourse, in the openings of the gates: in the city she uttereth her words, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> How long, ye simple ones, will ye love simplicity? and the scorners delight in their scorning, and fools hate knowledge?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> Turn you at my reproof: behold, I will pour out my <a href="../ebd/ebd349.htm#009">spirit</a> unto you, I will make known my words unto you.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> Because I have called, and ye refused; I have stretched out my hand, and no man regarded;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> But ye have set at nought all my counsel, and would none of my reproof:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> I also will laugh at your calamity; I will mock when your fear cometh;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> When your fear cometh as desolation, and your <a href="../ebd/ebd102.htm#004">destruction</a> cometh as a whirlwind; when distress and anguish cometh upon you.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="028">28</a> Then shall they call upon me, but I will not answer; they shall seek me early, but they shall not find me:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="029">29</a> For that they hated knowledge, and did not choose the fear of the LORD:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="030">30</a> They would none of my counsel: they despised all my reproof.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="031">31</a> Therefore shall they eat of the <a href="../ebd/ebd139.htm#000">fruit</a> of their own way, and be filled with their own devices.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="032">32</a> For the turning away of the simple shall slay them, and the prosperity of fools shall destroy them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="033">33</a> But whoso hearkeneth unto me shall dwell safely, and shall be quiet from fear of evil.<br>
 </p>
