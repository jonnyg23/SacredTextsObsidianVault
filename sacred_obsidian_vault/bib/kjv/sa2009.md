 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: 2 Kings (2 Samuel) Chapter 9</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> said, Is there yet any that is left of the house of <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#002">Saul</a>, that I may shew him kindness for Jonathan's sake?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> And there was of the house of <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#002">Saul</a> a servant whose name was <a href="../ebd/ebd392.htm#003">Ziba</a>. And when they had called him unto <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a>, the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> said unto him, Art thou <a href="../ebd/ebd392.htm#003">Ziba</a>?  And he said, Thy servant is he.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> said, Is there not yet any of the house of <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#002">Saul</a>, that I may shew the kindness of God unto him? And <a href="../ebd/ebd392.htm#003">Ziba</a> said unto the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#000">Jonathan</a> hath yet a son, which is lame on his feet.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> said unto him, Where is he? And <a href="../ebd/ebd392.htm#003">Ziba</a> said unto the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a>, Behold, he is in the house of <a href="../ebd/ebd235.htm#006">Machir</a>, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd020.htm#006">Ammiel</a>, in Lodebar.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Then <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> sent, and fetched him out of the house of <a href="../ebd/ebd235.htm#006">Machir</a>, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd020.htm#006">Ammiel</a>, from Lodebar.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Now when <a href="../ebd/ebd248.htm#006">Mephibosheth</a>, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#000">Jonathan</a>, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#002">Saul</a>, was come unto <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a>, he fell on his face, and did reverence. And <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> said, <a href="../ebd/ebd248.htm#006">Mephibosheth</a>. And he answered, Behold thy servant!<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> said unto him, Fear not: for I will surely shew thee kindness for <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#000">Jonathan</a> thy father's sake, and will restore thee all the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#002">Saul</a> thy father; and thou shalt eat <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#006">bread</a> at my table continually.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And he bowed himself, and said, What is thy servant, that thou shouldest look upon such a dead dog as I am?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> Then the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> called to <a href="../ebd/ebd392.htm#003">Ziba</a>, Saul's servant, and said unto him, I have given unto thy master's son all that pertained to <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#002">Saul</a> and to all his house.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> Thou therefore, and thy sons, and thy servants, shall till the land for him, and thou shalt bring in the fruits, that thy master's son may have food to eat: but <a href="../ebd/ebd248.htm#006">Mephibosheth</a> thy master's son shall eat <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#006">bread</a> alway at my table. Now <a href="../ebd/ebd392.htm#003">Ziba</a> had fifteen sons and twenty servants.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> Then said <a href="../ebd/ebd392.htm#003">Ziba</a> unto the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a>, According to all that my lord the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> hath commanded his servant, so shall thy servant do. As for <a href="../ebd/ebd248.htm#006">Mephibosheth</a>, said the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a>, he shall eat at my table, as one of the king's sons.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd248.htm#006">Mephibosheth</a> had a young son, whose name was <a href="../ebd/ebd252.htm#008">Micha</a>. And all that dwelt in the house of <a href="../ebd/ebd392.htm#003">Ziba</a> were servants unto <a href="../ebd/ebd248.htm#006">Mephibosheth</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> So <a href="../ebd/ebd248.htm#006">Mephibosheth</a> dwelt in <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>: for he did eat continually at the king's table; and was lame on both his feet.<br>
 </p>
