 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: 2 Kings (2 Samuel) Chapter 8</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> And after this it came to pass that <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> smote the <a href="../ebd/ebd294.htm#002">Philistines</a>, and subdued them: and <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> took Methegammah out of the hand of the <a href="../ebd/ebd294.htm#002">Philistines</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> And he smote <a href="../ebd/ebd258.htm#005">Moab</a>, and measured them with a line, casting them down to the ground; even with two <a href="../ebd/ebd229.htm#007">lines</a> measured he to put to death, and with one full line to keep alive. And so the Moabites became David's servants, and brought gifts.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> smote also <a href="../ebd/ebd157.htm#001">Hadadezer</a>, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd309.htm#006">Rehob</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd395.htm#005">Zobah</a>, as he went to recover his border at the river <a href="../ebd/ebd127.htm#001">Euphrates</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> took from him a thousand chariots, and <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> hundred horsemen, and twenty thousand footmen: and <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> houghed all the <a href="../ebd/ebd077.htm#008">chariot</a> horses, but reserved of them for an hundred chariots.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And when the Syrians of <a href="../ebd/ebd096.htm#007">Damascus</a> came to succour <a href="../ebd/ebd157.htm#001">Hadadezer</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd395.htm#005">Zobah</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> slew of the Syrians two and twenty thousand men.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Then <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> put garrisons in <a href="../ebd/ebd355.htm#002">Syria</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd096.htm#007">Damascus</a>: and the Syrians became servants to <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a>, and brought gifts. And the LORD preserved <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> whithersoever he went.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> took the shields of <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a> that were on the servants of <a href="../ebd/ebd157.htm#001">Hadadezer</a>, and brought them to <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And from <a href="../ebd/ebd054.htm#002">Betah</a>, and from Berothai, cities of <a href="../ebd/ebd157.htm#001">Hadadezer</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> took exceeding much <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#003">brass</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> When <a href="../ebd/ebd369.htm#006">Toi</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd160.htm#005">Hamath</a> heard that <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> had smitten all the <a href="../ebd/ebd183.htm#007">host</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd157.htm#001">Hadadezer</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> Then <a href="../ebd/ebd369.htm#006">Toi</a> sent <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#003">Joram</a> his son unto <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a>, to salute him, and to <a href="../ebd/ebd060.htm#008">bless</a> him, because he had fought against <a href="../ebd/ebd157.htm#001">Hadadezer</a>, and smitten him: for <a href="../ebd/ebd157.htm#001">Hadadezer</a> had wars with <a href="../ebd/ebd369.htm#006">Toi</a>. And <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#003">Joram</a> brought with him vessels of <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#000">silver</a>, and vessels of <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a>, and vessels of <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#003">brass</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> Which also <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> did dedicate unto the LORD, with the <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#000">silver</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a> that he had dedicated of all nations which he subdued;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> Of <a href="../ebd/ebd355.htm#002">Syria</a>, and of <a href="../ebd/ebd258.htm#005">Moab</a>, and of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd021.htm#002">Ammon</a>, and of the <a href="../ebd/ebd294.htm#002">Philistines</a>, and of <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#009">Amalek</a>, and of the spoil of <a href="../ebd/ebd157.htm#001">Hadadezer</a>, son of <a href="../ebd/ebd309.htm#006">Rehob</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd395.htm#005">Zobah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> gat him a name when he returned from smiting of the Syrians in the <a href="../ebd/ebd376.htm#008">valley</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd319.htm#008">salt</a>, being eighteen thousand men.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> And he put garrisons in <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#001">Edom</a>; throughout all <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#001">Edom</a> put he garrisons, and all they of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#001">Edom</a> became David's servants. And the LORD preserved <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> whithersoever he went.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> reigned over all <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>; and <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> executed judgment and <a href="../ebd/ebd214.htm#007">justice</a> unto all his people.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd207.htm#006">Joab</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd391.htm#009">Zeruiah</a> was over the <a href="../ebd/ebd183.htm#007">host</a>; and <a href="../ebd/ebd201.htm#006">Jehoshaphat</a> the son of Ahilud was <a href="../ebd/ebd308.htm#005">recorder</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd386.htm#008">Zadok</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd015.htm#001">Ahitub</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd014.htm#004">Ahimelech</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd002.htm#005">Abiathar</a>, were the priests; and <a href="../ebd/ebd328.htm#003">Seraiah</a> was the scribe;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd052.htm#003">Benaiah</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd200.htm#009">Jehoiada</a> was over both the Cherethites and the <a href="../ebd/ebd289.htm#000">Pelethites</a>; and David's sons were chief rulers.<br>
 </p>
