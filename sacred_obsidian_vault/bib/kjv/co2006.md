 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: 2 Corinthians Chapter 6</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> We then, as workers together with him, beseech you also that ye receive not the <a href="../ebd/ebd154.htm#000">grace</a> of God in vain.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> (For he saith, I have heard thee in a time accepted, and in the day of <a href="../ebd/ebd320.htm#003">salvation</a> have I succoured thee: behold, now is the accepted time; behold, now is the day of <a href="../ebd/ebd320.htm#003">salvation</a>.)<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Giving no offence in any thing, that the ministry be not blamed:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> But in all things approving ourselves as the ministers of God, in much patience, in <a href="../ebd/ebd011.htm#006">afflictions</a>, in necessities, in distresses,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> In <a href="../ebd/ebd352.htm#005">stripes</a>, in imprisonments, in tumults, in labours, in <a href="../ebd/ebd379.htm#004">watchings</a>, in fastings;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> By pureness, by knowledge, by longsuffering, by kindness, by the Holy <a href="../ebd/ebd147.htm#005">Ghost</a>, by love unfeigned,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> By the word of truth, by the power of God, by the <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#006">armour</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd313.htm#004">righteousness</a> on the right hand and on the left,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> By honour and dishonour, by evil report and good report: as deceivers, and yet true;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> As unknown, and yet well known; as dying, and, behold, we live; as chastened, and not killed;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> As sorrowful, yet alway rejoicing; as <a href="../ebd/ebd298.htm#006">poor</a>, yet making many rich; as having nothing, and yet possessing all things.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> O ye Corinthians, our mouth is open unto you, our heart is enlarged.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> Ye are not straitened in us, but ye are straitened in your own <a href="../ebd/ebd063.htm#004">bowels</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> Now for a recompence in the same, (I speak as unto my children,) be ye also enlarged.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> Be ye not unequally yoked together with unbelievers: for what <a href="../ebd/ebd132.htm#002">fellowship</a> hath <a href="../ebd/ebd313.htm#004">righteousness</a> with unrighteousness? and what <a href="../ebd/ebd087.htm#004">communion</a> hath light with <a href="../ebd/ebd097.htm#008">darkness</a>?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And what concord hath <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a> with <a href="../ebd/ebd051.htm#007">Belial</a>? or what part hath he that believeth with an infidel?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> And what agreement hath the <a href="../ebd/ebd361.htm#004">temple</a> of God with idols? for ye are the <a href="../ebd/ebd361.htm#004">temple</a> of the living God; as God hath said, I will dwell in them, and walk in them; and I will be their God, and they shall be my people.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> Wherefore come out from among them, and be ye separate, saith the Lord, and touch not the unclean thing; and I will receive you.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> And will be a Father unto you, and ye shall be my sons and daughters, saith the Lord Almighty.<br>
 </p>
