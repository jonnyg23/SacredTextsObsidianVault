 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Numbers Chapter 1</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> And the LORD spake unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> in the <a href="../ebd/ebd381.htm#005">wilderness</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#009">Sinai</a>, in the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>, on the first day of the second month, in the second year after they were come out of the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a>, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Take ye the sum of all the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, after their families, by the house of their fathers, with the number of their names, every male by their polls;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> From twenty years old and upward, all that are able to go forth to war in <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>: thou and <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> shall number them by their armies.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And with you there shall be a man of every <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a>; every one head of the house of his fathers.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And these are the names of the men that shall stand with you: of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd311.htm#009">Reuben</a>; Elizur the son of Shedeur.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Of <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#002">Simeon</a>; Shelumiel the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd396.htm#007">Zurishaddai</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>; <a href="../ebd/ebd265.htm#007">Nahshon</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd020.htm#008">Amminadab</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> Of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#002">Issachar</a>; <a href="../ebd/ebd271.htm#003">Nethaneel</a> the son of Zuar.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> Of <a href="../ebd/ebd389.htm#003">Zebulun</a>; <a href="../ebd/ebd115.htm#008">Eliab</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd173.htm#005">Helon</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> Of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#005">Joseph</a>: of <a href="../ebd/ebd123.htm#000">Ephraim</a>; <a href="../ebd/ebd118.htm#001">Elishama</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd020.htm#007">Ammihud</a>: of <a href="../ebd/ebd240.htm#002">Manasseh</a>; <a href="../ebd/ebd142.htm#006">Gamaliel</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd288.htm#000">Pedahzur</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> Of <a href="../ebd/ebd052.htm#008">Benjamin</a>; <a href="../ebd/ebd002.htm#008">Abidan</a> the son of Gideoni.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> Of <a href="../ebd/ebd096.htm#009">Dan</a>; <a href="../ebd/ebd013.htm#008">Ahiezer</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd021.htm#000">Ammishaddai</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> Of <a href="../ebd/ebd033.htm#008">Asher</a>; <a href="../ebd/ebd282.htm#005">Pagiel</a> the son of Ocran.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> Of <a href="../ebd/ebd140.htm#007">Gad</a>; Eliasaph the son of Deuel.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> Of <a href="../ebd/ebd266.htm#006">Naphtali</a>; <a href="../ebd/ebd014.htm#008">Ahira</a> the son of Enan.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> These were the renowned of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>, princes of the tribes of their fathers, heads of <a href="../ebd/ebd364.htm#008">thousands</a> in <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> took these men which are expressed by their names:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> And they assembled all the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> together on the first day of the second month, and they declared their pedigrees after their families, by the house of their fathers, according to the number of the names, from twenty years old and upward, by their polls.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> As the LORD commanded <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, so he numbered them in the <a href="../ebd/ebd381.htm#005">wilderness</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#009">Sinai</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> And the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd311.htm#009">Reuben</a>, Israel's eldest son, by their generations, after their families, by the house of their fathers, according to the number of the names, by their polls, every male from twenty years old and upward, all that were able to go forth to war;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> Those that were numbered of them, even of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd311.htm#009">Reuben</a>, were forty and six thousand and five hundred.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> Of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#002">Simeon</a>, by their generations, after their families, by the house of their fathers, those that were numbered of them, according to the number of the names, by their polls, every male from twenty years old and upward, all that were able to go forth to war;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> Those that were numbered of them, even of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#002">Simeon</a>, were fifty and nine thousand and three hundred.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> Of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd140.htm#007">Gad</a>, by their generations, after their families, by the house of their fathers, according to the number of the names, from twenty years old and upward, all that were able to go forth to war;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> Those that were numbered of them, even of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd140.htm#007">Gad</a>, were forty and five thousand six hundred and fifty.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> Of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>, by their generations, after their families, by the house of their fathers, according to the number of the names, from twenty years old and upward, all that were able to go forth to war;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> Those that were numbered of them, even of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>, were threescore and fourteen thousand and six hundred.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="028">28</a> Of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#002">Issachar</a>, by their generations, after their families, by the house of their fathers, according to the number of the names, from twenty years old and upward, all that were able to go forth to war;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="029">29</a> Those that were numbered of them, even of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#002">Issachar</a>, were fifty and four thousand and four hundred.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="030">30</a> Of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd389.htm#003">Zebulun</a>, by their generations, after their families, by the house of their fathers, according to the number of the names, from twenty years old and upward, all that were able to go forth to war;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="031">31</a> Those that were numbered of them, even of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd389.htm#003">Zebulun</a>, were fifty and <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> thousand and four hundred.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="032">32</a> Of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#005">Joseph</a>, namely, of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd123.htm#000">Ephraim</a>, by their generations, after their families, by the house of their fathers, according to the number of the names, from twenty years old and upward, all that were able to go forth to war;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="033">33</a> Those that were numbered of them, even of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd123.htm#000">Ephraim</a>, were forty thousand and five hundred.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="034">34</a> Of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd240.htm#002">Manasseh</a>, by their generations, after their families, by the house of their fathers, according to the number of the names, from twenty years old and upward, all that were able to go forth to war;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="035">35</a> Those that were numbered of them, even of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd240.htm#002">Manasseh</a>, were thirty and two thousand and two hundred.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="036">36</a> Of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd052.htm#008">Benjamin</a>, by their generations, after their families, by the house of their fathers, according to the number of the names, from twenty years old and upward, all that were able to go forth to war;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="037">37</a> Those that were numbered of them, even of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd052.htm#008">Benjamin</a>, were thirty and five thousand and four hundred.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="038">38</a> Of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd096.htm#009">Dan</a>, by their generations, after their families, by the house of their fathers, according to the number of the names, from twenty years old and upward, all that were able to go forth to war;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="039">39</a> Those that were numbered of them, even of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd096.htm#009">Dan</a>, were threescore and two thousand and <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> hundred.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="040">40</a> Of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd033.htm#008">Asher</a>, by their generations, after their families, by the house of their fathers, according to the number of the names, from twenty years old and upward, all that were able to go forth to war;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="041">41</a> Those that were numbered of them, even of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd033.htm#008">Asher</a>, were forty and one thousand and five hundred.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="042">42</a> Of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd266.htm#006">Naphtali</a>, throughout their generations, after their families, by the house of their fathers, according to the number of the names, from twenty years old and upward, all that were able to go forth to war;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="043">43</a> Those that were numbered of them, even of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd266.htm#006">Naphtali</a>, were fifty and three thousand and four hundred.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="044">44</a> These are those that were numbered, which <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> numbered, and the princes of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, being twelve men: each one was for the house of his fathers.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="045">45</a> So were all those that were numbered of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, by the house of their fathers, from twenty years old and upward, all that were able to go forth to war in <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="046">46</a> Even all they that were numbered were six hundred thousand and three thousand and five hundred and fifty.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="047">47</a> But the Levites after the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of their fathers were not numbered among them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="048">48</a> For the LORD had spoken unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="049">49</a> Only thou shalt not number the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd227.htm#006">Levi</a>, neither take the sum of them among the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="050">50</a> But thou shalt appoint the Levites over the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd362.htm#008">testimony</a>, and over all the vessels thereof, and over all things that belong to it: they shall bear the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a>, and all the vessels thereof; and they shall <a href="../ebd/ebd255.htm#007">minister</a> unto it, and shall <a href="../ebd/ebd120.htm#003">encamp</a> round about the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="051">51</a> And when the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> setteth forward, the Levites shall take it down: and when the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> is to be pitched, the Levites shall set it up: and the <a href="../ebd/ebd351.htm#009">stranger</a> that cometh nigh shall be put to death.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="052">52</a> And the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> shall <a href="../ebd/ebd296.htm#008">pitch</a> their tents, every man by his own <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#002">camp</a>, and every man by his own standard, throughout their hosts.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="053">53</a> But the Levites shall <a href="../ebd/ebd296.htm#008">pitch</a> round about the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd362.htm#008">testimony</a>, that there be no wrath upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>: and the Levites shall keep the charge of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd362.htm#008">testimony</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="054">54</a> And the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> did according to all that the LORD commanded <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, so did they.<br>
 </p>
