 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Leviticus Chapter 6</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> And the LORD spake unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> If a soul <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a>, and commit a trespass against the LORD, and lie unto his neighbour in that which was delivered him to keep, or in <a href="../ebd/ebd132.htm#002">fellowship</a>, or in a thing taken away by violence, or hath deceived his neighbour;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Or have found that which was lost, and lieth concerning it, and sweareth falsely; in any of all these that a man doeth, sinning therein:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Then it shall be, because he hath sinned, and is guilty, that he shall restore that which he took violently away, or the thing which he hath deceitfully gotten, or that which was delivered him to keep, or the lost thing which he found,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Or all that about which he hath sworn falsely; he shall even restore it in the principal, and shall add the fifth part more thereto, and give it unto him to whom it appertaineth, in the day of his trespass <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And he shall bring his trespass <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> unto the LORD, a <a href="../ebd/ebd306.htm#003">ram</a> without <a href="../ebd/ebd060.htm#007">blemish</a> out of the flock, with thy estimation, for a trespass <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, unto the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> shall make an <a href="../ebd/ebd036.htm#004">atonement</a> for him before the LORD: and it shall be forgiven him for any thing of all that he hath done in trespassing therein.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And the LORD spake unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> Command <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> and his sons, saying, This is the law of the burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>: It is the burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, because of the burning upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> all night unto the morning, and the fire of the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> shall be burning in it.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> shall put on his <a href="../ebd/ebd229.htm#008">linen</a> garment, and his <a href="../ebd/ebd229.htm#008">linen</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#008">breeches</a> shall he put upon his <a href="../ebd/ebd135.htm#007">flesh</a>, and take up the <a href="../ebd/ebd034.htm#000">ashes</a> which the fire hath consumed with the burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> on the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>, and he shall put them beside the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And he shall put off his garments, and put on other garments, and carry forth the <a href="../ebd/ebd034.htm#000">ashes</a> without the <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#002">camp</a> unto a <a href="../ebd/ebd084.htm#005">clean</a> place.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And the fire upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> shall be burning in it; it shall not be put out: and the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> shall burn <a href="../ebd/ebd383.htm#003">wood</a> on it every morning, and lay the burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> in order upon it; and he shall burn thereon the <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#005">fat</a> of the peace offerings.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> The fire shall ever be burning upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>; it shall never go out.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> And this is the law of the meat <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>: the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> shall offer it before the LORD, before the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And he shall take of it his handful, of the <a href="../ebd/ebd136.htm#001">flour</a> of the meat <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, and of the oil thereof, and all the <a href="../ebd/ebd138.htm#004">frankincense</a> which is upon the meat <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, and shall burn it upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> for a sweet savour, even the memorial of it, unto the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> And the remainder thereof shall <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> and his sons eat: with unleavened <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#006">bread</a> shall it be eaten in the holy place; in the court of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> they shall eat it.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> It shall not be baken with <a href="../ebd/ebd226.htm#000">leaven</a>. I have given it unto them for their portion of my offerings made by fire; it is most holy, as is the <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, and as the trespass <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> All the males among the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> shall eat of it. It shall be a statute for ever in your generations concerning the offerings of the LORD made by fire: every one that toucheth them shall be holy.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> And the LORD spake unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> This is the <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> and of his sons, which they shall offer unto the LORD in the day when he is anointed; the tenth part of an <a href="../ebd/ebd122.htm#003">ephah</a> of fine <a href="../ebd/ebd136.htm#001">flour</a> for a meat <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> perpetual, half of it in the morning, and half thereof at night.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> In a <a href="../ebd/ebd283.htm#009">pan</a> it shall be made with oil; and when it is baken, thou shalt bring it in: and the baken <a href="../ebd/ebd295.htm#002">pieces</a> of the meat <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> shalt thou offer for a sweet savour unto the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> of his sons that is anointed in his stead shall offer it: it is a statute for ever unto the LORD; it shall be wholly burnt.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> For every meat <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> for the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> shall be wholly burnt: it shall not be eaten.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> And the LORD spake unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> Speak unto <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> and to his sons, saying, This is the law of the <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>: In the place where the burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> is killed shall the <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> be killed before the LORD: it is most holy.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> The <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> that offereth it for <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> shall eat it: in the holy place shall it be eaten, in the court of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> Whatsoever shall touch the <a href="../ebd/ebd135.htm#007">flesh</a> thereof shall be holy: and when there is sprinkled of the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> thereof upon any garment, thou shalt wash that whereon it was sprinkled in the holy place.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="028">28</a> But the earthen vessel wherein it is sodden shall be broken: and if it be sodden in a brasen pot, it shall be both scoured, and rinsed in water.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="029">29</a> All the males among the priests shall eat thereof: it is most holy.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="030">30</a> And no <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, whereof any of the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> is brought into the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> to reconcile withal in the holy place, shall be eaten: it shall be burnt in the fire.<br>
 </p>
