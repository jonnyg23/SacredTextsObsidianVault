 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Job Chapter 28</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Surely there is a vein for the <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#000">silver</a>, and a place for <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a> where they fine it.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd189.htm#004">Iron</a> is taken out of the earth, and <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#003">brass</a> is molten out of the <a href="../ebd/ebd351.htm#005">stone</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> He setteth an end to <a href="../ebd/ebd097.htm#008">darkness</a>, and searcheth out all <a href="../ebd/ebd290.htm#001">perfection</a>: the stones of <a href="../ebd/ebd097.htm#008">darkness</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd330.htm#002">shadow</a> of death.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> The <a href="../ebd/ebd136.htm#000">flood</a> breaketh out from the inhabitant; even the waters forgotten of the foot: they are dried up, they are gone away from men.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> As for the earth, out of it cometh <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#006">bread</a>: and under it is turned up as it were fire.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> The stones of it are the place of sapphires: and it hath <a href="../ebd/ebd109.htm#008">dust</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> There is a path which no fowl knoweth, and which the vulture's eye hath not seen:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> The lion's whelps have not trodden it, nor the fierce lion passed by it.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> He putteth forth his hand upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd315.htm#001">rock</a>; he overturneth the mountains by the roots.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> He cutteth out rivers among the rocks; and his eye seeth every precious thing.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> He bindeth the floods from overflowing; and the thing that is hid bringeth he forth to light.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> But where shall wisdom be found? and where is the place of understanding?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> Man knoweth not the price thereof; neither is it found in the land of the living.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> The depth saith, It is not in me: and the sea saith, It is not with me.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> It cannot be gotten for <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a>, neither shall <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#000">silver</a> be weighed for the price thereof.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> It cannot be valued with the <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd279.htm#007">Ophir</a>, with the precious <a href="../ebd/ebd279.htm#004">onyx</a>, or the <a href="../ebd/ebd322.htm#002">sapphire</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> The <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a> and the <a href="../ebd/ebd093.htm#005">crystal</a> cannot equal it: and the exchange of it shall not be for jewels of fine <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> No mention shall be made of <a href="../ebd/ebd089.htm#008">coral</a>, or of pearls: for the price of wisdom is above rubies.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> The <a href="../ebd/ebd370.htm#005">topaz</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd126.htm#005">Ethiopia</a> shall not equal it, neither shall it be valued with pure <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> Whence then cometh wisdom? and where is the place of understanding?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> Seeing it is hid from the eyes of all living, and kept close from the fowls of the air.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd102.htm#004">Destruction</a> and death say, We have heard the fame thereof with our ears.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> God understandeth the way thereof, and he knoweth the place thereof.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> For he looketh to the ends of the earth, and seeth under the whole <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#006">heaven</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> To make the weight for the <a href="../ebd/ebd381.htm#009">winds</a>; and he weigheth the waters by measure.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> When he made a decree for the <a href="../ebd/ebd305.htm#008">rain</a>, and a way for the <a href="../ebd/ebd229.htm#002">lightning</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd365.htm#003">thunder</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> Then did he see it, and declare it; he prepared it, yea, and searched it out.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="028">28</a> And unto man he said, Behold, the fear of the LORD, that is wisdom; and to depart from evil is understanding.<br>
 </p>
