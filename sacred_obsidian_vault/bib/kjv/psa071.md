 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 71</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> In thee, O LORD, do I put my trust: let me never be put to confusion.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Deliver me in thy <a href="../ebd/ebd313.htm#004">righteousness</a>, and cause me to escape: incline thine <a href="../ebd/ebd110.htm#004">ear</a> unto me, and save me.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Be thou my strong <a href="../ebd/ebd156.htm#007">habitation</a>, whereunto I may continually resort: thou hast given commandment to save me; for thou art my <a href="../ebd/ebd315.htm#001">rock</a> and my fortress.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Deliver me, O my God, out of the hand of the wicked, out of the hand of the unrighteous and cruel man.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> For thou art my hope, O Lord GOD: thou art my trust from my youth.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> By thee have I been holden up from the womb: thou art he that took me out of my mother's <a href="../ebd/ebd063.htm#004">bowels</a>: my praise shall be continually of thee.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> I am as a wonder unto many; but thou art my strong refuge.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> Let my mouth be filled with thy praise and with thy honour all the day.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> Cast me not off in the time of old age; forsake me not when my strength faileth.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> For mine enemies speak against me; and they that lay wait for my soul take counsel together,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> Saying, God hath forsaken him: persecute and take him; for there is none to deliver him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> O God, be not far from me: O my God, make haste for my help.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> Let them be confounded and consumed that are adversaries to my soul; let them be covered with reproach and dishonour that seek my hurt.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> But I will hope continually, and will yet praise thee more and more.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> My mouth shall shew forth thy <a href="../ebd/ebd313.htm#004">righteousness</a> and thy <a href="../ebd/ebd320.htm#003">salvation</a> all the day; for I know not the numbers thereof.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> I will go in the strength of the Lord GOD: I will make mention of thy <a href="../ebd/ebd313.htm#004">righteousness</a>, even of thine only.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> O God, thou hast taught me from my youth: and hitherto have I declared thy wondrous works.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> Now also when I am old and greyheaded, O God, forsake me not; until I have shewed thy strength unto this <a href="../ebd/ebd145.htm#008">generation</a>, and thy power to every one that is to come.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> Thy <a href="../ebd/ebd313.htm#004">righteousness</a> also, O God, is very high, who hast done great things: O God, who is like unto thee!<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> Thou, which hast shewed me great and sore troubles, shalt quicken me again, and shalt bring me up again from the depths of the earth.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> Thou shalt increase my greatness, and comfort me on every side.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> I will also praise thee with the <a href="../ebd/ebd301.htm#006">psaltery</a>, even thy truth, O my God: unto thee will I sing with the <a href="../ebd/ebd165.htm#001">harp</a>, O thou Holy One of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> My lips shall greatly rejoice when I sing unto thee; and my soul, which thou hast redeemed.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> My tongue also shall talk of thy <a href="../ebd/ebd313.htm#004">righteousness</a> all the day long: for they are confounded, for they are brought unto shame, that seek my hurt.<br>
 </p>
