 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Numbers Chapter 6</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> And the LORD spake unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Speak unto the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, and say unto them, When either man or woman shall separate themselves to vow a vow of a <a href="../ebd/ebd267.htm#008">Nazarite</a>, to separate themselves unto the LORD:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> He shall separate himself from <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#000">wine</a> and strong <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a>, and shall <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a> no <a href="../ebd/ebd377.htm#006">vinegar</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#000">wine</a>, or <a href="../ebd/ebd377.htm#006">vinegar</a> of strong <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a>, neither shall he <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a> any liquor of grapes, nor eat moist grapes, or dried.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> All the days of his separation shall he eat nothing that is made of the <a href="../ebd/ebd377.htm#004">vine</a> tree, from the kernels even to the <a href="../ebd/ebd185.htm#004">husk</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> All the days of the vow of his separation there shall no <a href="../ebd/ebd307.htm#009">razor</a> come upon his head: until the days be fulfilled, in the which he separateth himself unto the LORD, he shall be holy, and shall let the locks of the hair of his head grow.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> All the days that he separateth himself unto the LORD he shall come at no dead body.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> He shall not make himself unclean for his father, or for his mother, for his brother, or for his sister, when they die: because the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#007">consecration</a> of his God is upon his head.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> All the days of his separation he is holy unto the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And if any man die very suddenly by him, and he hath defiled the head of his <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#007">consecration</a>; then he shall shave his head in the day of his cleansing, on the seventh day shall he shave it.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And on the eighth day he shall bring two turtles, or two young pigeons, to the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a>, to the door of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> shall offer the one for a <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, and the other for a burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, and make an <a href="../ebd/ebd036.htm#004">atonement</a> for him, for that he sinned by the dead, and shall <a href="../ebd/ebd160.htm#001">hallow</a> his head that same day.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And he shall consecrate unto the LORD the days of his separation, and shall bring a <a href="../ebd/ebd223.htm#003">lamb</a> of the first year for a trespass <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>: but the days that were before shall be lost, because his separation was defiled.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> And this is the law of the <a href="../ebd/ebd267.htm#008">Nazarite</a>, when the days of his separation are fulfilled: he shall be brought unto the door of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> And he shall offer his <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> unto the LORD, one he <a href="../ebd/ebd223.htm#003">lamb</a> of the first year without <a href="../ebd/ebd060.htm#007">blemish</a> for a burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, and one ewe <a href="../ebd/ebd223.htm#003">lamb</a> of the first year without <a href="../ebd/ebd060.htm#007">blemish</a> for a <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, and one <a href="../ebd/ebd306.htm#003">ram</a> without <a href="../ebd/ebd060.htm#007">blemish</a> for peace offerings,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And a <a href="../ebd/ebd046.htm#009">basket</a> of unleavened <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#006">bread</a>, cakes of fine <a href="../ebd/ebd136.htm#001">flour</a> mingled with oil, and <a href="../ebd/ebd378.htm#003">wafers</a> of unleavened <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#006">bread</a> anointed with oil, and their meat <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, and their <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a> offerings.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> shall bring them before the LORD, and shall offer his <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, and his burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> And he shall offer the <a href="../ebd/ebd306.htm#003">ram</a> for a <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#002">sacrifice</a> of peace offerings unto the LORD, with the <a href="../ebd/ebd046.htm#009">basket</a> of unleavened <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#006">bread</a>: the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> shall offer also his meat <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, and his <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd267.htm#008">Nazarite</a> shall shave the head of his separation at the door of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>, and shall take the hair of the head of his separation, and put it in the fire which is under the <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#002">sacrifice</a> of the peace offerings.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> shall take the sodden shoulder of the <a href="../ebd/ebd306.htm#003">ram</a>, and one unleavened <a href="../ebd/ebd068.htm#009">cake</a> out of the <a href="../ebd/ebd046.htm#009">basket</a>, and one unleavened wafer, and shall put them upon the hands of the <a href="../ebd/ebd267.htm#008">Nazarite</a>, after the hair of his separation is shaven:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> shall wave them for a wave <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> before the LORD: this is holy for the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a>, with the wave breast and heave shoulder: and after that the <a href="../ebd/ebd267.htm#008">Nazarite</a> may <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#000">wine</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> This is the law of the <a href="../ebd/ebd267.htm#008">Nazarite</a> who hath vowed, and of his <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> unto the LORD for his separation, beside that that his hand shall get: according to the vow which he vowed, so he must do after the law of his separation.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> And the LORD spake unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> Speak unto <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> and unto his sons, saying, On this <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#004">wise</a> ye shall <a href="../ebd/ebd060.htm#008">bless</a> the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, saying unto them,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> The LORD <a href="../ebd/ebd060.htm#008">bless</a> thee, and keep thee:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> The LORD make his face shine upon thee, and be gracious unto thee:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> The LORD lift up his countenance upon thee, and give thee peace.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> And they shall put my name upon the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, and I will <a href="../ebd/ebd060.htm#008">bless</a> them.<br>
 </p>
