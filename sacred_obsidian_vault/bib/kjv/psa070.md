 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 70</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> MAKE HASTE, O GOD, TO DELIVER ME; MAKE HASTE TO HELP ME, O LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Let them be ashamed and confounded that seek after my soul: let them be turned backward, and put to confusion, that desire my hurt.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Let them be turned back for a reward of their shame that say, <a href="../ebd/ebd013.htm#001">Aha</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd013.htm#001">aha</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Let all those that seek thee rejoice and be glad in thee: and let such as love thy <a href="../ebd/ebd320.htm#003">salvation</a> say continually, Let God be magnified.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> But I am <a href="../ebd/ebd298.htm#006">poor</a> and needy: make haste unto me, O God: thou art my help and my deliverer; O LORD, make no tarrying.<br>
 </p>
