 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: 1 Timothy Chapter 1</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd287.htm#002">Paul</a>, an <a href="../ebd/ebd026.htm#008">apostle</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a> by the commandment of God our <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#003">Saviour</a>, and Lord <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a>, which is our hope;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Unto <a href="../ebd/ebd367.htm#002">Timothy</a>, my own son in the faith: <a href="../ebd/ebd154.htm#000">Grace</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a>, and peace, from God our Father and <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a> our Lord.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> As I besought thee to abide still at <a href="../ebd/ebd122.htm#007">Ephesus</a>, when I went into <a href="../ebd/ebd235.htm#003">Macedonia</a>, that thou mightest charge some that they teach no other doctrine,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Neither give heed to fables and endless genealogies, which <a href="../ebd/ebd255.htm#007">minister</a> questions, rather than godly edifying which is in faith: so do.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Now the end of the commandment is <a href="../ebd/ebd077.htm#009">charity</a> out of a pure heart, and of a good <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#006">conscience</a>, and of faith unfeigned:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> From which some having swerved have turned aside unto vain jangling;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Desiring to be teachers of the law; understanding neither what they say, nor whereof they affirm.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> But we know that the law is good, if a man use it lawfully;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> Knowing this, that the law is not made for a righteous man, but for the lawless and disobedient, for the ungodly and for sinners, for unholy and profane, for murderers of fathers and murderers of mothers, for manslayers,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> For whoremongers, for them that defile themselves with mankind, for menstealers, for liars, for perjured persons, and if there be any other thing that is contrary to sound doctrine;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> According to the glorious <a href="../ebd/ebd153.htm#003">gospel</a> of the blessed God, which was committed to my trust.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And I thank <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a> our Lord, who hath enabled me, for that he counted me <a href="../ebd/ebd130.htm#005">faithful</a>, putting me into the ministry;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> Who was before a blasphemer, and a persecutor, and injurious: but I obtained <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a>, because I did it ignorantly in unbelief.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd154.htm#000">grace</a> of our Lord was exceeding abundant with faith and love which is in <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> This is a <a href="../ebd/ebd130.htm#005">faithful</a> saying, and worthy of all acceptation, that <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a> came into the world to save sinners; of whom I am chief.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> Howbeit for this cause I obtained <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a>, that in me first <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a> might shew forth all longsuffering, for a pattern to them which should hereafter believe on him to life <a href="../ebd/ebd127.htm#007">everlasting</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> Now unto the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">King</a> eternal, immortal, invisible, the only <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#004">wise</a> God, be honour and <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a> for ever and ever. <a href="../ebd/ebd020.htm#001">Amen</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> This charge I commit unto thee, son <a href="../ebd/ebd367.htm#002">Timothy</a>, according to the prophecies which went before on thee, that thou by them mightest war a good warfare;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> Holding faith, and a good <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#006">conscience</a>; which some having put away concerning faith have made shipwreck:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> Of whom is Hymenaeus and <a href="../ebd/ebd016.htm#009">Alexander</a>; whom I have delivered unto <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#000">Satan</a>, that they may learn not to blaspheme.<br>
 </p>
