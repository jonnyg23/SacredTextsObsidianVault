 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 110</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> The LORD said unto my Lord, Sit thou at my right hand, until I make thine enemies thy <a href="../ebd/ebd136.htm#009">footstool</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> The LORD shall send the rod of thy strength out of <a href="../ebd/ebd394.htm#002">Zion</a>: rule thou in the midst of thine enemies.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Thy people shall be willing in the day of thy power, in the beauties of <a href="../ebd/ebd180.htm#006">holiness</a> from the womb of the morning: thou hast the <a href="../ebd/ebd102.htm#008">dew</a> of thy youth.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> The LORD hath sworn, and will not repent, Thou art a <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> for ever after the order of <a href="../ebd/ebd247.htm#003">Melchizedek</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> The Lord at thy right hand shall strike through kings in the day of his wrath.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> He shall <a href="../ebd/ebd213.htm#005">judge</a> among the <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#005">heathen</a>, he shall fill the places with the dead bodies; he shall wound the heads over many countries.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> He shall <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd065.htm#006">brook</a> in the way: therefore shall he lift up the head.<br>
 </p>
