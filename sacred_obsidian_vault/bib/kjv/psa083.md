 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 83</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Keep not thou silence, O God: hold not thy peace, and be not still, O God.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> For, lo, thine enemies make a tumult: and they that hate thee have lifted up the head.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> They have taken crafty counsel against thy people, and consulted against thy hidden ones.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> They have said, Come, and let us cut them off from being a nation; that the name of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> may be no more in remembrance.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> For they have consulted together with one consent: they are confederate against thee:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> The tabernacles of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#001">Edom</a>, and the Ishmaelites; of <a href="../ebd/ebd258.htm#005">Moab</a>, and the Hagarenes;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd144.htm#005">Gebal</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd021.htm#002">Ammon</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#009">Amalek</a>; the <a href="../ebd/ebd294.htm#002">Philistines</a> with the inhabitants of <a href="../ebd/ebd374.htm#001">Tyre</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> Assur also is joined with them: they have holpen the children of Lot. <a href="../ebd/ebd326.htm#008">Selah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> Do unto them as unto the Midianites; as to <a href="../ebd/ebd345.htm#007">Sisera</a>, as to <a href="../ebd/ebd194.htm#000">Jabin</a>, at the <a href="../ebd/ebd065.htm#006">brook</a> of Kison:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> Which perished at <a href="../ebd/ebd120.htm#006">Endor</a>: they became as <a href="../ebd/ebd109.htm#003">dung</a> for the earth.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> Make their nobles like <a href="../ebd/ebd280.htm#001">Oreb</a>, and like <a href="../ebd/ebd389.htm#009">Zeeb</a>: yea, all their princes as <a href="../ebd/ebd388.htm#006">Zebah</a>, and as <a href="../ebd/ebd387.htm#002">Zalmunna</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> Who said, Let us take to ourselves the houses of God in possession.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> O my God, make them like a <a href="../ebd/ebd381.htm#001">wheel</a>; as the stubble before the wind.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> As the fire burneth a <a href="../ebd/ebd383.htm#003">wood</a>, and as the flame setteth the mountains on fire;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> So persecute them with thy tempest, and make them afraid with thy storm.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> Fill their faces with shame; that they may seek thy name, O LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> Let them be confounded and troubled for ever; yea, let them be put to shame, and perish:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> That men may know that thou, whose name alone is <a href="../ebd/ebd201.htm#009">JEHOVAH</a>, art the most high over all the earth.<br>
 </p>
