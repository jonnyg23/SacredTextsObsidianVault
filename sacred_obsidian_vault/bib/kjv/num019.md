 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Numbers Chapter 19</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> And the LORD spake unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> and unto <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a>, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> This is the ordinance of the law which the LORD hath commanded, saying, Speak unto the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, that they bring thee a red <a href="../ebd/ebd171.htm#006">heifer</a> without spot, wherein is no <a href="../ebd/ebd060.htm#007">blemish</a>, and upon which never came <a href="../ebd/ebd384.htm#009">yoke</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And ye shall give her unto <a href="../ebd/ebd115.htm#000">Eleazar</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a>, that he may bring her forth without the <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#002">camp</a>, and one shall slay her before his face:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd115.htm#000">Eleazar</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> shall take of her <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> with his finger, and sprinkle of her <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> directly before the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> times:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And one shall burn the <a href="../ebd/ebd171.htm#006">heifer</a> in his sight; her skin, and her <a href="../ebd/ebd135.htm#007">flesh</a>, and her <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a>, with her <a href="../ebd/ebd109.htm#003">dung</a>, shall he burn:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> shall take <a href="../ebd/ebd074.htm#007">cedar</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd383.htm#003">wood</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd185.htm#007">hyssop</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#005">scarlet</a>, and cast it into the midst of the burning of the <a href="../ebd/ebd171.htm#006">heifer</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Then the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> shall wash his clothes, and he shall bathe his <a href="../ebd/ebd135.htm#007">flesh</a> in water, and afterward he shall come into the <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#002">camp</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> shall be unclean until the even.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And he that burneth her shall wash his clothes in water, and bathe his <a href="../ebd/ebd135.htm#007">flesh</a> in water, and shall be unclean until the even.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And a man that is <a href="../ebd/ebd084.htm#005">clean</a> shall gather up the <a href="../ebd/ebd034.htm#000">ashes</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd171.htm#006">heifer</a>, and lay them up without the <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#002">camp</a> in a <a href="../ebd/ebd084.htm#005">clean</a> place, and it shall be kept for the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> for a water of separation: it is a <a href="../ebd/ebd302.htm#007">purification</a> for <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And he that gathereth the <a href="../ebd/ebd034.htm#000">ashes</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd171.htm#006">heifer</a> shall wash his clothes, and be unclean until the even: and it shall be unto the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, and unto the <a href="../ebd/ebd351.htm#009">stranger</a> that sojourneth among them, for a statute for ever.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> He that toucheth the dead body of any man shall be unclean <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> days.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> He shall purify himself with it on the third day, and on the seventh day he shall be <a href="../ebd/ebd084.htm#005">clean</a>: but if he purify not himself the third day, then the seventh day he shall not be <a href="../ebd/ebd084.htm#005">clean</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> Whosoever toucheth the dead body of any man that is dead, and purifieth not himself, defileth the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the LORD; and that soul shall be cut off from <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>: because the water of separation was not sprinkled upon him, he shall be unclean; his uncleanness is yet upon him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> This is the law, when a man dieth in a <a href="../ebd/ebd361.htm#009">tent</a>: all that come into the <a href="../ebd/ebd361.htm#009">tent</a>, and all that is in the <a href="../ebd/ebd361.htm#009">tent</a>, shall be unclean <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> days.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And every open vessel, which hath no covering bound upon it, is unclean.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> And whosoever toucheth one that is slain with a <a href="../ebd/ebd354.htm#003">sword</a> in the open fields, or a dead body, or a bone of a man, or a <a href="../ebd/ebd154.htm#008">grave</a>, shall be unclean <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> days.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> And for an unclean person they shall take of the <a href="../ebd/ebd034.htm#000">ashes</a> of the burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd171.htm#006">heifer</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd302.htm#007">purification</a> for <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a>, and running water shall be put thereto in a vessel:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> And a <a href="../ebd/ebd084.htm#005">clean</a> person shall take <a href="../ebd/ebd185.htm#007">hyssop</a>, and dip it in the water, and sprinkle it upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd361.htm#009">tent</a>, and upon all the vessels, and upon the persons that were there, and upon him that touched a bone, or one slain, or one dead, or a <a href="../ebd/ebd154.htm#008">grave</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd084.htm#005">clean</a> person shall sprinkle upon the unclean on the third day, and on the seventh day: and on the seventh day he shall purify himself, and wash his clothes, and bathe himself in water, and shall be <a href="../ebd/ebd084.htm#005">clean</a> at even.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> But the man that shall be unclean, and shall not purify himself, that soul shall be cut off from among the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>, because he hath defiled the <a href="../ebd/ebd321.htm#005">sanctuary</a> of the LORD: the water of separation hath not been sprinkled upon him; he is unclean.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> And it shall be a perpetual statute unto them, that he that sprinkleth the water of separation shall wash his clothes; and he that toucheth the water of separation shall be unclean until even.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> And whatsoever the unclean person toucheth shall be unclean; and the soul that toucheth it shall be unclean until even.<br>
 </p>
