 <p><a name="028">28</a> And the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd321.htm#001">Samuel</a>; the firstborn Vashni, and Abiah.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="029">29</a> The sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#000">Merari</a>; Mahli, <a href="../ebd/ebd228.htm#005">Libni</a> his son, <a href="../ebd/ebd338.htm#002">Shimei</a> his son, <a href="../ebd/ebd376.htm#000">Uzza</a> his son,<br>

 </p>

 </p>
 <p><a name="033">33</a> And these are they that waited with their children. Of the sons of the <a href="../ebd/ebd222.htm#002">Kohathites</a>: <a href="../ebd/ebd173.htm#009">Heman</a> a singer, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd208.htm#005">Joel</a>, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd335.htm#000">Shemuel</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="034">34</a> The son of <a href="../ebd/ebd118.htm#005">Elkanah</a>, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#001">Jeroham</a>, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd116.htm#006">Eliel</a>, the son of Toah,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="035">35</a> The son of <a href="../ebd/ebd396.htm#003">Zuph</a>, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd118.htm#005">Elkanah</a>, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd237.htm#007">Mahath</a>, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd019.htm#004">Amasai</a>,<br>

 </p>
 <p><a name="039">39</a> And his brother <a href="../ebd/ebd033.htm#002">Asaph</a>, who stood on his right hand, even <a href="../ebd/ebd033.htm#002">Asaph</a> the son of Berachiah, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd338.htm#000">Shimea</a>,<br>

 <p><a name="040">40</a> The son of <a href="../ebd/ebd252.htm#009">Michael</a>, the son of Baaseiah, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd239.htm#001">Malchiah</a>,<br>

 </p>
 <p><a name="042">42</a> The son of <a href="../ebd/ebd126.htm#002">Ethan</a>, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd393.htm#007">Zimmah</a>, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd338.htm#002">Shimei</a>,<br>

 <p><a name="050">50</a> And these are the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a>; <a href="../ebd/ebd115.htm#000">Eleazar</a> his son, <a href="../ebd/ebd294.htm#003">Phinehas</a> his son, <a href="../ebd/ebd004.htm#006">Abishua</a> his son,<br>

 </p>
 <p><a name="054">54</a> Now these are their dwelling places throughout their castles in their coasts, of the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a>, of the families of the <a href="../ebd/ebd222.htm#002">Kohathites</a>: for theirs was the lot.<br>

 </p>
 <p><a name="058">58</a> And Hilen with her <a href="../ebd/ebd352.htm#007">suburbs</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd099.htm#007">Debir</a> with her <a href="../ebd/ebd352.htm#007">suburbs</a>,<br>

 </p>
 <p><a name="063">63</a> Unto the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#000">Merari</a> were given by lot, throughout their families, out of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd311.htm#009">Reuben</a>, and out of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd140.htm#007">Gad</a>, and out of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd389.htm#003">Zebulun</a>, twelve cities.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="064">64</a> And the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> gave to the Levites these cities with their <a href="../ebd/ebd352.htm#007">suburbs</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="065">65</a> And they gave by lot out of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>, and out of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#002">Simeon</a>, and out of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd052.htm#008">Benjamin</a>, these cities, which are called by their names.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="066">66</a> And the residue of the families of the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd222.htm#001">Kohath</a> had cities of their coasts out of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd123.htm#000">Ephraim</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="067">67</a> And they gave unto them, of the cities of refuge, <a href="../ebd/ebd333.htm#003">Shechem</a> in <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#007">mount</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd123.htm#000">Ephraim</a> with her <a href="../ebd/ebd352.htm#007">suburbs</a>; they gave also <a href="../ebd/ebd147.htm#004">Gezer</a> with her <a href="../ebd/ebd352.htm#007">suburbs</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="068">68</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd210.htm#001">Jokmeam</a> with her <a href="../ebd/ebd352.htm#007">suburbs</a>, and Bethhoron with her <a href="../ebd/ebd352.htm#007">suburbs</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="069">69</a> And Aijalon with her <a href="../ebd/ebd352.htm#007">suburbs</a>, and Gathrimmon with her <a href="../ebd/ebd352.htm#007">suburbs</a>:<br>
 </p>
