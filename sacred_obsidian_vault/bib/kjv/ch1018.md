 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: 1 Chronicles Chapter 18</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Now after this it came to pass, that <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> smote the <a href="../ebd/ebd294.htm#002">Philistines</a>, and subdued them, and took <a href="../ebd/ebd143.htm#009">Gath</a> and her towns out of the hand of the <a href="../ebd/ebd294.htm#002">Philistines</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> And he smote <a href="../ebd/ebd258.htm#005">Moab</a>; and the Moabites became David's servants, and brought gifts.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> smote <a href="../ebd/ebd157.htm#004">Hadarezer</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd395.htm#005">Zobah</a> unto <a href="../ebd/ebd160.htm#005">Hamath</a>, as he went to stablish his dominion by the river <a href="../ebd/ebd127.htm#001">Euphrates</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> took from him a thousand chariots, and <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> thousand horsemen, and twenty thousand footmen: <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> also houghed all the <a href="../ebd/ebd077.htm#008">chariot</a> horses, but reserved of them an hundred chariots.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And when the Syrians of <a href="../ebd/ebd096.htm#007">Damascus</a> came to help <a href="../ebd/ebd157.htm#004">Hadarezer</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd395.htm#005">Zobah</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> slew of the Syrians two and twenty thousand men.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Then <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> put garrisons in Syriadamascus; and the Syrians became David's servants, and brought gifts. Thus the LORD preserved <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> whithersoever he went.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> took the shields of <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a> that were on the servants of <a href="../ebd/ebd157.htm#004">Hadarezer</a>, and brought them to <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> Likewise from Tibhath, and from <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#009">Chun</a>, cities of <a href="../ebd/ebd157.htm#004">Hadarezer</a>, brought <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> very much <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#003">brass</a>, wherewith <a href="../ebd/ebd347.htm#007">Solomon</a> made the brasen sea, and the pillars, and the vessels of <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#003">brass</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> Now when Tou <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd160.htm#005">Hamath</a> heard how <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> had smitten all the <a href="../ebd/ebd183.htm#007">host</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd157.htm#004">Hadarezer</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd395.htm#005">Zobah</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> He sent <a href="../ebd/ebd158.htm#001">Hadoram</a> his son to <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a>, to enquire of his welfare, and to congratulate him, because he had fought against <a href="../ebd/ebd157.htm#004">Hadarezer</a>, and smitten him; (for <a href="../ebd/ebd157.htm#004">Hadarezer</a> had war with Tou;) and with him all manner of vessels of <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#000">silver</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#003">brass</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> Them also <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> dedicated unto the LORD, with the <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#000">silver</a> and the <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a> that he brought from all these nations; from <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#001">Edom</a>, and from <a href="../ebd/ebd258.htm#005">Moab</a>, and from the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd021.htm#002">Ammon</a>, and from the <a href="../ebd/ebd294.htm#002">Philistines</a>, and from <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#009">Amalek</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> Moreover <a href="../ebd/ebd004.htm#005">Abishai</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd391.htm#009">Zeruiah</a> slew of the Edomites in the <a href="../ebd/ebd376.htm#008">valley</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd319.htm#008">salt</a> eighteen thousand.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> And he put garrisons in <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#001">Edom</a>; and all the Edomites became David's servants. Thus the LORD preserved <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> whithersoever he went.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> So <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> reigned over all <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, and executed judgment and <a href="../ebd/ebd214.htm#007">justice</a> among all his people.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd207.htm#006">Joab</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd391.htm#009">Zeruiah</a> was over the <a href="../ebd/ebd183.htm#007">host</a>; and <a href="../ebd/ebd201.htm#006">Jehoshaphat</a> the son of Ahilud, <a href="../ebd/ebd308.htm#005">recorder</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd386.htm#008">Zadok</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd015.htm#001">Ahitub</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd004.htm#000">Abimelech</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd002.htm#005">Abiathar</a>, were the priests; and <a href="../ebd/ebd332.htm#002">Shavsha</a> was scribe;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd052.htm#003">Benaiah</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd200.htm#009">Jehoiada</a> was over the Cherethites and the <a href="../ebd/ebd289.htm#000">Pelethites</a>; and the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> were chief about the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a>.<br>
 </p>
