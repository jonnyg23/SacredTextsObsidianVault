 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: 2 Kings (2 Samuel) Chapter 23</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Now these be the last words of <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a>. <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#005">Jesse</a> said, and the man who was raised up on high, the anointed of the God of <a href="../ebd/ebd194.htm#007">Jacob</a>, and the sweet psalmist of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, said,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> The <a href="../ebd/ebd349.htm#009">Spirit</a> of the LORD spake by me, and his word was in my tongue.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> The God of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> said, the <a href="../ebd/ebd315.htm#001">Rock</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> spake to me, He that ruleth over men must be just, ruling in the fear of God.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And he shall be as the light of the morning, when the <a href="../ebd/ebd353.htm#001">sun</a> riseth, even a morning without clouds; as the tender <a href="../ebd/ebd154.htm#005">grass</a> springing out of the earth by clear shining after <a href="../ebd/ebd305.htm#008">rain</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Although my house be not so with God; yet he hath made with me an <a href="../ebd/ebd127.htm#007">everlasting</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd091.htm#008">covenant</a>, ordered in all things, and sure: for this is all my <a href="../ebd/ebd320.htm#003">salvation</a>, and all my desire, although he make it not to grow.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> But the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd051.htm#007">Belial</a> shall be all of them as thorns thrust away, because they cannot be taken with hands:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> But the man that shall touch them must be fenced with <a href="../ebd/ebd189.htm#004">iron</a> and the staff of a spear; and they shall be utterly burned with fire in the same place.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> These be the names of the mighty men whom <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> had: The <a href="../ebd/ebd357.htm#002">Tachmonite</a> that sat in the seat, chief among the captains; the same was <a href="../ebd/ebd009.htm#001">Adino</a> the Eznite: he lift up his spear against eight hundred, whom he slew at one time.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And after him was <a href="../ebd/ebd115.htm#000">Eleazar</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd105.htm#005">Dodo</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd015.htm#004">Ahohite</a>, one of the three mighty men with <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a>, when they defied the <a href="../ebd/ebd294.htm#002">Philistines</a> that were there gathered together to battle, and the men of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> were gone away:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> He arose, and smote the <a href="../ebd/ebd294.htm#002">Philistines</a> until his hand was weary, and his hand clave unto the <a href="../ebd/ebd354.htm#003">sword</a>: and the LORD wrought a great victory that day; and the people returned after him only to spoil.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And after him was <a href="../ebd/ebd331.htm#002">Shammah</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd012.htm#002">Agee</a> the Hararite. And the <a href="../ebd/ebd294.htm#002">Philistines</a> were gathered together into a troop, where was a piece of ground full of <a href="../ebd/ebd227.htm#001">lentiles</a>: and the people fled from the <a href="../ebd/ebd294.htm#002">Philistines</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> But he stood in the midst of the ground, and defended it, and slew the <a href="../ebd/ebd294.htm#002">Philistines</a>: and the LORD wrought a great victory.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> And three of the thirty chief went down, and came to <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> in the <a href="../ebd/ebd165.htm#008">harvest</a> time unto the <a href="../ebd/ebd074.htm#006">cave</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd010.htm#007">Adullam</a>: and the troop of the <a href="../ebd/ebd294.htm#002">Philistines</a> pitched in the <a href="../ebd/ebd376.htm#008">valley</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd311.htm#000">Rephaim</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> was then in an hold, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd143.htm#007">garrison</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd294.htm#002">Philistines</a> was then in <a href="../ebd/ebd056.htm#005">Bethlehem</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> longed, and said, Oh that one would give me <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a> of the water of the well of <a href="../ebd/ebd056.htm#005">Bethlehem</a>, which is by the <a href="../ebd/ebd143.htm#008">gate</a>!<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> And the three mighty men brake through the <a href="../ebd/ebd183.htm#007">host</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd294.htm#002">Philistines</a>, and drew water out of the well of <a href="../ebd/ebd056.htm#005">Bethlehem</a>, that was by the <a href="../ebd/ebd143.htm#008">gate</a>, and took it, and brought it to <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a>: nevertheless he would not <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a> thereof, but poured it out unto the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> And he said, Be it far from me, O LORD, that I should do this: is not this the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> of the men that went in jeopardy of their lives? therefore he would not <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a> it. These things did these three mighty men.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd004.htm#005">Abishai</a>, the brother of <a href="../ebd/ebd207.htm#006">Joab</a>, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd391.htm#009">Zeruiah</a>, was chief among three. And he lifted up his spear against three hundred, and slew them, and had the name among three.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> Was he not most honourable of three? therefore he was their captain: howbeit he attained not unto the first three.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd052.htm#003">Benaiah</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd200.htm#009">Jehoiada</a>, the son of a valiant man, of <a href="../ebd/ebd215.htm#002">Kabzeel</a>, who had done many acts, he slew two lionlike men of <a href="../ebd/ebd258.htm#005">Moab</a>: he went down also and slew a lion in the midst of a <a href="../ebd/ebd296.htm#007">pit</a> in time of <a href="../ebd/ebd347.htm#000">snow</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> And he slew an Egyptian, a goodly man: and the Egyptian had a spear in his hand; but he went down to him with a staff, and plucked the spear out of the Egyptian's hand, and slew him with his own spear.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> These things did <a href="../ebd/ebd052.htm#003">Benaiah</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd200.htm#009">Jehoiada</a>, and had the name among three mighty men.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> He was more honourable than the thirty, but he attained not to the first three. And <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> set him over his <a href="../ebd/ebd155.htm#009">guard</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd033.htm#001">Asahel</a> the brother of <a href="../ebd/ebd207.htm#006">Joab</a> was one of the thirty; <a href="../ebd/ebd115.htm#006">Elhanan</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd105.htm#005">Dodo</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd056.htm#005">Bethlehem</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd331.htm#002">Shammah</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd164.htm#009">Harodite</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd117.htm#000">Elika</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd164.htm#009">Harodite</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd172.htm#008">Helez</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd283.htm#007">Paltite</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd189.htm#000">Ira</a> the son of Ikkesh the Tekoite,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd003.htm#000">Abiezer</a> the Anethothite, <a href="../ebd/ebd245.htm#006">Mebunnai</a> the Hushathite,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="028">28</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd387.htm#000">Zalmon</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd015.htm#004">Ahohite</a>, Maharai the Netophathite,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="029">29</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd172.htm#003">Heleb</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd040.htm#007">Baanah</a>, a Netophathite, <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#007">Ittai</a> the son of Ribai out of <a href="../ebd/ebd147.htm#008">Gibeah</a> of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd052.htm#008">Benjamin</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="030">30</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd052.htm#003">Benaiah</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd296.htm#003">Pirathonite</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd177.htm#008">Hiddai</a> of the brooks of <a href="../ebd/ebd140.htm#004">Gaash</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="031">31</a> Abialbon the <a href="../ebd/ebd028.htm#008">Arbathite</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd038.htm#000">Azmaveth</a> the Barhumite,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="032">32</a> Eliahba the Shaalbonite, of the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd197.htm#008">Jashen</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#000">Jonathan</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="033">33</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd331.htm#002">Shammah</a> the Hararite, <a href="../ebd/ebd013.htm#007">Ahiam</a> the son of Sharar the Hararite,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="034">34</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd117.htm#007">Eliphelet</a> the son of Ahasbai, the son of the Maachathite, <a href="../ebd/ebd116.htm#001">Eliam</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd015.htm#000">Ahithophel</a> the Gilonite,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="035">35</a> Hezrai the Carmelite, <a href="../ebd/ebd282.htm#002">Paarai</a> the Arbite,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="036">36</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd186.htm#009">Igal</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd267.htm#002">Nathan</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd395.htm#005">Zobah</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd043.htm#004">Bani</a> the Gadite,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="037">37</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd390.htm#001">Zelek</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd021.htm#003">Ammonite</a>, Nahari the Beerothite, armourbearer to <a href="../ebd/ebd207.htm#006">Joab</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd391.htm#009">Zeruiah</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="038">38</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd189.htm#000">Ira</a> an <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#006">Ithrite</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd143.htm#002">Gareb</a> an <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#006">Ithrite</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="039">39</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd375.htm#002">Uriah</a> the Hittite: thirty and <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> in all.<br>
 </p>
