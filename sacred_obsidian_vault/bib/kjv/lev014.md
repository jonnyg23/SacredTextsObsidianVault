 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Leviticus Chapter 14</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> And the LORD spake unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> This shall be the law of the leper in the day of his cleansing: He shall be brought unto the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> shall go forth out of the <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#002">camp</a>; and the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> shall look, and, behold, if the <a href="../ebd/ebd297.htm#001">plague</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd227.htm#003">leprosy</a> be healed in the leper;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Then shall the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> command to take for him that is to be cleansed two birds alive and <a href="../ebd/ebd084.htm#005">clean</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd074.htm#007">cedar</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd383.htm#003">wood</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#005">scarlet</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd185.htm#007">hyssop</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> shall command that one of the birds be killed in an earthen vessel over running water:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> As for the living <a href="../ebd/ebd059.htm#000">bird</a>, he shall take it, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd074.htm#007">cedar</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd383.htm#003">wood</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#005">scarlet</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd185.htm#007">hyssop</a>, and shall dip them and the living <a href="../ebd/ebd059.htm#000">bird</a> in the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd059.htm#000">bird</a> that was killed over the running water:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And he shall sprinkle upon him that is to be cleansed from the <a href="../ebd/ebd227.htm#003">leprosy</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> times, and shall pronounce him <a href="../ebd/ebd084.htm#005">clean</a>, and shall let the living <a href="../ebd/ebd059.htm#000">bird</a> loose into the open field.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And he that is to be cleansed shall wash his clothes, and shave off all his hair, and wash himself in water, that he may be <a href="../ebd/ebd084.htm#005">clean</a>: and after that he shall come into the <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#002">camp</a>, and shall tarry abroad out of his <a href="../ebd/ebd361.htm#009">tent</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> days.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> But it shall be on the seventh day, that he shall shave all his hair off his head and his <a href="../ebd/ebd049.htm#000">beard</a> and his eyebrows, even all his hair he shall shave off: and he shall wash his clothes, also he shall wash his <a href="../ebd/ebd135.htm#007">flesh</a> in water, and he shall be <a href="../ebd/ebd084.htm#005">clean</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And on the eighth day he shall take two he lambs without <a href="../ebd/ebd060.htm#007">blemish</a>, and one ewe <a href="../ebd/ebd223.htm#003">lamb</a> of the first year without <a href="../ebd/ebd060.htm#007">blemish</a>, and three tenth deals of fine <a href="../ebd/ebd136.htm#001">flour</a> for a meat <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, mingled with oil, and one <a href="../ebd/ebd231.htm#003">log</a> of oil.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> that maketh him <a href="../ebd/ebd084.htm#005">clean</a> shall present the man that is to be made <a href="../ebd/ebd084.htm#005">clean</a>, and those things, before the LORD, at the door of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> shall take one he <a href="../ebd/ebd223.htm#003">lamb</a>, and offer him for a trespass <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd231.htm#003">log</a> of oil, and wave them for a wave <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> before the LORD:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> And he shall slay the <a href="../ebd/ebd223.htm#003">lamb</a> in the place where he shall kill the <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> and the burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, in the holy place: for as the <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> is the priest's, so is the trespass <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>: it is most holy:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> shall take some of the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> of the trespass <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> shall put it upon the tip of the right <a href="../ebd/ebd110.htm#004">ear</a> of him that is to be cleansed, and upon the thumb of his right hand, and upon the great toe of his right foot:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> shall take some of the <a href="../ebd/ebd231.htm#003">log</a> of oil, and pour it into the palm of his own left hand:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> shall dip his right finger in the oil that is in his left hand, and shall sprinkle of the oil with his finger <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> times before the LORD:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> And of the rest of the oil that is in his hand shall the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> put upon the tip of the right <a href="../ebd/ebd110.htm#004">ear</a> of him that is to be cleansed, and upon the thumb of his right hand, and upon the great toe of his right foot, upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> of the trespass <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> And the remnant of the oil that is in the priest's hand he shall pour upon the head of him that is to be cleansed: and the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> shall make an <a href="../ebd/ebd036.htm#004">atonement</a> for him before the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> shall offer the <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, and make an <a href="../ebd/ebd036.htm#004">atonement</a> for him that is to be cleansed from his uncleanness; and afterward he shall kill the burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> shall offer the burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> and the meat <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>: and the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> shall make an <a href="../ebd/ebd036.htm#004">atonement</a> for him, and he shall be <a href="../ebd/ebd084.htm#005">clean</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> And if he be <a href="../ebd/ebd298.htm#006">poor</a>, and cannot get so much; then he shall take one <a href="../ebd/ebd223.htm#003">lamb</a> for a trespass <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> to be waved, to make an <a href="../ebd/ebd036.htm#004">atonement</a> for him, and one tenth deal of fine <a href="../ebd/ebd136.htm#001">flour</a> mingled with oil for a meat <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, and a <a href="../ebd/ebd231.htm#003">log</a> of oil;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> And two turtledoves, or two young pigeons, such as he is able to get; and the one shall be a <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, and the other a burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> And he shall bring them on the eighth day for his cleansing unto the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a>, unto the door of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>, before the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> shall take the <a href="../ebd/ebd223.htm#003">lamb</a> of the trespass <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd231.htm#003">log</a> of oil, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> shall wave them for a wave <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> before the LORD:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> And he shall kill the <a href="../ebd/ebd223.htm#003">lamb</a> of the trespass <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> shall take some of the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> of the trespass <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, and put it upon the tip of the right <a href="../ebd/ebd110.htm#004">ear</a> of him that is to be cleansed, and upon the thumb of his right hand, and upon the great toe of his right foot:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> shall pour of the oil into the palm of his own left hand:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> shall sprinkle with his right finger some of the oil that is in his left hand <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> times before the LORD:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="028">28</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> shall put of the oil that is in his hand upon the tip of the right <a href="../ebd/ebd110.htm#004">ear</a> of him that is to be cleansed, and upon the thumb of his right hand, and upon the great toe of his right foot, upon the place of the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> of the trespass <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="029">29</a> And the rest of the oil that is in the priest's hand he shall put upon the head of him that is to be cleansed, to make an <a href="../ebd/ebd036.htm#004">atonement</a> for him before the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="030">30</a> And he shall offer the one of the turtledoves, or of the young pigeons, such as he can get;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="031">31</a> Even such as he is able to get, the one for a <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, and the other for a burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, with the meat <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>: and the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> shall make an <a href="../ebd/ebd036.htm#004">atonement</a> for him that is to be cleansed before the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="032">32</a> This is the law of him in whom is the <a href="../ebd/ebd297.htm#001">plague</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd227.htm#003">leprosy</a>, whose hand is not able to get that which pertaineth to his cleansing.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="033">33</a> And the LORD spake unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> and unto <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a>, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="034">34</a> When ye be come into the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#005">Canaan</a>, which I give to you for a possession, and I put the <a href="../ebd/ebd297.htm#001">plague</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd227.htm#003">leprosy</a> in a house of the land of your possession;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="035">35</a> And he that owneth the house shall come and tell the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a>, saying, It seemeth to me there is as it were a <a href="../ebd/ebd297.htm#001">plague</a> in the house:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="036">36</a> Then the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> shall command that they empty the house, before the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> go into it to see the <a href="../ebd/ebd297.htm#001">plague</a>, that all that is in the house be not made unclean: and afterward the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> shall go in to see the house:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="037">37</a> And he shall look on the <a href="../ebd/ebd297.htm#001">plague</a>, and, behold, if the <a href="../ebd/ebd297.htm#001">plague</a> be in the walls of the house with hollow strakes, greenish or reddish, which in sight are lower than the wall;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="038">38</a> Then the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> shall go out of the house to the door of the house, and shut up the house <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> days:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="039">39</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> shall come again the seventh day, and shall look: and, behold, if the <a href="../ebd/ebd297.htm#001">plague</a> be spread in the walls of the house;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="040">40</a> Then the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> shall command that they take away the stones in which the <a href="../ebd/ebd297.htm#001">plague</a> is, and they shall cast them into an unclean place without the city:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="041">41</a> And he shall cause the house to be scraped within round about, and they shall pour out the <a href="../ebd/ebd109.htm#008">dust</a> that they scrape off without the city into an unclean place:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="042">42</a> And they shall take other stones, and put them in the place of those stones; and he shall take other morter, and shall plaister the house.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="043">43</a> And if the <a href="../ebd/ebd297.htm#001">plague</a> come again, and break out in the house, after that he hath taken away the stones, and after he hath scraped the house, and after it is plaistered;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="044">44</a> Then the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> shall come and look, and, behold, if the <a href="../ebd/ebd297.htm#001">plague</a> be spread in the house, it is a fretting <a href="../ebd/ebd227.htm#003">leprosy</a> in the house; it is unclean.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="045">45</a> And he shall break down the house, the stones of it, and the timber thereof, and all the morter of the house; and he shall carry them forth out of the city into an unclean place.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="046">46</a> Moreover he that goeth into the house all the while that it is shut up shall be unclean until the even.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="047">47</a> And he that lieth in the house shall wash his clothes; and he that eateth in the house shall wash his clothes.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="048">48</a> And if the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> shall come in, and look upon it, and, behold, the <a href="../ebd/ebd297.htm#001">plague</a> hath not spread in the house, after the house was plaistered: then the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> shall pronounce the house <a href="../ebd/ebd084.htm#005">clean</a>, because the <a href="../ebd/ebd297.htm#001">plague</a> is healed.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="049">49</a> And he shall take to cleanse the house two birds, and <a href="../ebd/ebd074.htm#007">cedar</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd383.htm#003">wood</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#005">scarlet</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd185.htm#007">hyssop</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="050">50</a> And he shall kill the one of the birds in an earthen vessel over running water:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="051">51</a> And he shall take the <a href="../ebd/ebd074.htm#007">cedar</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd383.htm#003">wood</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd185.htm#007">hyssop</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#005">scarlet</a>, and the living <a href="../ebd/ebd059.htm#000">bird</a>, and dip them in the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> of the slain <a href="../ebd/ebd059.htm#000">bird</a>, and in the running water, and sprinkle the house <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> times:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="052">52</a> And he shall cleanse the house with the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd059.htm#000">bird</a>, and with the running water, and with the living <a href="../ebd/ebd059.htm#000">bird</a>, and with the <a href="../ebd/ebd074.htm#007">cedar</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd383.htm#003">wood</a>, and with the <a href="../ebd/ebd185.htm#007">hyssop</a>, and with the <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#005">scarlet</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="053">53</a> But he shall let go the living <a href="../ebd/ebd059.htm#000">bird</a> out of the city into the open fields, and make an <a href="../ebd/ebd036.htm#004">atonement</a> for the house: and it shall be <a href="../ebd/ebd084.htm#005">clean</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="054">54</a> This is the law for all manner of <a href="../ebd/ebd297.htm#001">plague</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd227.htm#003">leprosy</a>, and scall,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="055">55</a> And for the <a href="../ebd/ebd227.htm#003">leprosy</a> of a garment, and of a house,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="056">56</a> And for a rising, and for a scab, and for a bright spot:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="057">57</a> To teach when it is unclean, and when it is <a href="../ebd/ebd084.htm#005">clean</a>: this is the law of <a href="../ebd/ebd227.htm#003">leprosy</a>.<br>
 </p>
