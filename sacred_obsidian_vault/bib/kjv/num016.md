 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Numbers Chapter 16</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Now <a href="../ebd/ebd222.htm#003">Korah</a>, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd192.htm#001">Izhar</a>, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd222.htm#001">Kohath</a>, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd227.htm#006">Levi</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#002">Dathan</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd004.htm#003">Abiram</a>, the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd115.htm#008">Eliab</a>, and On, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd288.htm#009">Peleth</a>, sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd311.htm#009">Reuben</a>, took men:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> And they rose up before <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, with certain of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, two hundred and fifty princes of the assembly, famous in the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>, men of renown:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And they gathered themselves together against <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> and against <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a>, and said unto them, Ye take too much upon you, seeing all the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> are holy, every one of them, and the LORD is among them: wherefore then lift ye up yourselves above the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> of the LORD?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And when <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> heard it, he fell upon his face:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And he spake unto <a href="../ebd/ebd222.htm#003">Korah</a> and unto all his company, saying, Even to morrow the LORD will shew who are his, and who is holy; and will cause him to come near unto him: even him whom he hath <a href="../ebd/ebd081.htm#008">chosen</a> will he cause to come near unto him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> This do; Take you censers, <a href="../ebd/ebd222.htm#003">Korah</a>, and all his company;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And put fire therein, and put <a href="../ebd/ebd188.htm#002">incense</a> in them before the LORD to morrow: and it shall be that the man whom the LORD doth choose, he shall be holy: ye take too much upon you, ye sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd227.htm#006">Levi</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> said unto <a href="../ebd/ebd222.htm#003">Korah</a>, Hear, I pray you, ye sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd227.htm#006">Levi</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> Seemeth it but a small thing unto you, that the God of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> hath separated you from the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, to bring you near to himself to do the service of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the LORD, and to stand before the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> to <a href="../ebd/ebd255.htm#007">minister</a> unto them?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And he hath brought thee near to him, and all thy brethren the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd227.htm#006">Levi</a> with thee: and seek ye the priesthood also?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> For which cause both thou and all thy company are gathered together against the LORD: and what is <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a>, that ye murmur against him?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> sent to call <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#002">Dathan</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd004.htm#003">Abiram</a>, the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd115.htm#008">Eliab</a>: which said, We will not come up:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> Is it a small thing that thou hast brought us up out of a land that floweth with <a href="../ebd/ebd255.htm#000">milk</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd181.htm#001">honey</a>, to kill us in the <a href="../ebd/ebd381.htm#005">wilderness</a>, except thou make thyself altogether a <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#004">prince</a> over us?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> Moreover thou hast not brought us into a land that floweth with <a href="../ebd/ebd255.htm#000">milk</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd181.htm#001">honey</a>, or given us inheritance of fields and vineyards: wilt thou put out the eyes of these men? we will not come up.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> was very wroth, and said unto the LORD, Respect not thou their <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>: I have not taken one <a href="../ebd/ebd035.htm#001">ass</a> from them, neither have I hurt one of them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> said unto <a href="../ebd/ebd222.htm#003">Korah</a>, Be thou and all thy company before the LORD, thou, and they, and <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a>, to morrow:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> And take every man his <a href="../ebd/ebd075.htm#002">censer</a>, and put <a href="../ebd/ebd188.htm#002">incense</a> in them, and bring ye before the LORD every man his <a href="../ebd/ebd075.htm#002">censer</a>, two hundred and fifty censers; thou also, and <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a>, each of you his <a href="../ebd/ebd075.htm#002">censer</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> And they took every man his <a href="../ebd/ebd075.htm#002">censer</a>, and put fire in them, and laid <a href="../ebd/ebd188.htm#002">incense</a> thereon, and stood in the door of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> with <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd222.htm#003">Korah</a> gathered all the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> against them unto the door of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>: and the <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a> of the LORD appeared unto all the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> And the LORD spake unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> and unto <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a>, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> Separate yourselves from among this <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>, that I may consume them in a moment.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> And they fell upon their faces, and said, O God, the God of the spirits of all <a href="../ebd/ebd135.htm#007">flesh</a>, shall one man <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a>, and wilt thou be wroth with all the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> And the LORD spake unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> Speak unto the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>, saying, Get you up from about the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd222.htm#003">Korah</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#002">Dathan</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd004.htm#003">Abiram</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> rose up and went unto <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#002">Dathan</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd004.htm#003">Abiram</a>; and the elders of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> followed him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> And he spake unto the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>, saying, Depart, I pray you, from the tents of these wicked men, and touch nothing of their's, lest ye be consumed in all their sins.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> So they gat up from the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd222.htm#003">Korah</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#002">Dathan</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd004.htm#003">Abiram</a>, on every side: and <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#002">Dathan</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd004.htm#003">Abiram</a> came out, and stood in the door of their tents, and their wives, and their sons, and their little children.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="028">28</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> said, Hereby ye shall know that the LORD hath sent me to do all these works; for I have not done them of mine own mind.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="029">29</a> If these men die the common death of all men, or if they be visited after the visitation of all men; then the LORD hath not sent me.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="030">30</a> But if the LORD make a new thing, and the earth open her mouth, and <a href="../ebd/ebd353.htm#009">swallow</a> them up, with all that appertain unto them, and they go down quick into the <a href="../ebd/ebd296.htm#007">pit</a>; then ye shall understand that these men have provoked the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="031">31</a> And it came to pass, as he had made an end of speaking all these words, that the ground clave asunder that was under them:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="032">32</a> And the earth opened her mouth, and swallowed them up, and their houses, and all the men that appertained unto <a href="../ebd/ebd222.htm#003">Korah</a>, and all their goods.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="033">33</a> They, and all that appertained to them, went down alive into the <a href="../ebd/ebd296.htm#007">pit</a>, and the earth closed upon them: and they perished from among the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="034">34</a> And all <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> that were round about them fled at the cry of them: for they said, Lest the earth <a href="../ebd/ebd353.htm#009">swallow</a> us up also.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="035">35</a> And there came out a fire from the LORD, and consumed the two hundred and fifty men that offered <a href="../ebd/ebd188.htm#002">incense</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="036">36</a> And the LORD spake unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="037">37</a> Speak unto <a href="../ebd/ebd115.htm#000">Eleazar</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a>, that he take up the censers out of the burning, and scatter thou the fire yonder; for they are hallowed.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="038">38</a> The censers of these sinners against their own souls, let them make them broad plates for a covering of the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>: for they offered them before the LORD, therefore they are hallowed: and they shall be a sign unto the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="039">39</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd115.htm#000">Eleazar</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> took the brasen censers, wherewith they that were burnt had offered; and they were made broad plates for a covering of the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="040">40</a> To be a memorial unto the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, that no <a href="../ebd/ebd351.htm#009">stranger</a>, which is not of the seed of <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a>, come near to offer <a href="../ebd/ebd188.htm#002">incense</a> before the LORD; that he be not as <a href="../ebd/ebd222.htm#003">Korah</a>, and as his company: as the LORD said to him by the hand of <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="041">41</a> But on the morrow all the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> murmured against <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> and against <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a>, saying, Ye have killed the people of the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="042">42</a> And it came to pass, when the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> was gathered against <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> and against <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a>, that they looked toward the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>: and, behold, the <a href="../ebd/ebd085.htm#001">cloud</a> covered it, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a> of the LORD appeared.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="043">43</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> came before the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="044">44</a> And the LORD spake unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="045">45</a> Get you up from among this <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>, that I may consume them as in a moment. And they fell upon their faces.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="046">46</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> said unto <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a>, Take a <a href="../ebd/ebd075.htm#002">censer</a>, and put fire therein from off the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>, and put on <a href="../ebd/ebd188.htm#002">incense</a>, and go quickly unto the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>, and make an <a href="../ebd/ebd036.htm#004">atonement</a> for them: for there is wrath gone out from the LORD; the <a href="../ebd/ebd297.htm#001">plague</a> is begun.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="047">47</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> took as <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> commanded, and ran into the midst of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>; and, behold, the <a href="../ebd/ebd297.htm#001">plague</a> was begun among the people: and he put on <a href="../ebd/ebd188.htm#002">incense</a>, and made an <a href="../ebd/ebd036.htm#004">atonement</a> for the people.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="048">48</a> And he stood between the dead and the living; and the <a href="../ebd/ebd297.htm#001">plague</a> was stayed.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="049">49</a> Now they that died in the <a href="../ebd/ebd297.htm#001">plague</a> were fourteen thousand and <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> hundred, beside them that died about the matter of <a href="../ebd/ebd222.htm#003">Korah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="050">50</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> returned unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> unto the door of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>: and the <a href="../ebd/ebd297.htm#001">plague</a> was stayed.<br>
 </p>
