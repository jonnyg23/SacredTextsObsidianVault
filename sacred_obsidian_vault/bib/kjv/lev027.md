 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Leviticus Chapter 27</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> And the LORD spake unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Speak unto the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, and say unto them, When a man shall make a singular vow, the persons shall be for the LORD by thy estimation.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And thy estimation shall be of the male from twenty years old even unto sixty years old, even thy estimation shall be fifty shekels of <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#000">silver</a>, after the <a href="../ebd/ebd333.htm#009">shekel</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd321.htm#005">sanctuary</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And if it be a female, then thy estimation shall be thirty shekels.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And if it be from five years old even unto twenty years old, then thy estimation shall be of the male twenty shekels, and for the female ten shekels.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And if it be from a month old even unto five years old, then thy estimation shall be of the male five shekels of <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#000">silver</a>, and for the female thy estimation shall be three shekels of <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#000">silver</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And if it be from sixty years old and above; if it be a male, then thy estimation shall be fifteen shekels, and for the female ten shekels.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> But if he be poorer than thy estimation, then he shall present himself before the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> shall value him; according to his ability that vowed shall the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> value him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And if it be a <a href="../ebd/ebd049.htm#001">beast</a>, whereof men bring an <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> unto the LORD, all that any man giveth of such unto the LORD shall be holy.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> He shall not alter it, nor change it, a good for a bad, or a bad for a good: and if he shall at all change <a href="../ebd/ebd049.htm#001">beast</a> for <a href="../ebd/ebd049.htm#001">beast</a>, then it and the exchange thereof shall be holy.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And if it be any unclean <a href="../ebd/ebd049.htm#001">beast</a>, of which they do not offer a <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#002">sacrifice</a> unto the LORD, then he shall present the <a href="../ebd/ebd049.htm#001">beast</a> before the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> shall value it, whether it be good or bad: as thou valuest it, who art the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a>, so shall it be.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> But if he will at all redeem it, then he shall add a fifth part thereof unto thy estimation.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> And when a man shall sanctify his house to be holy unto the LORD, then the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> shall estimate it, whether it be good or bad: as the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> shall estimate it, so shall it stand.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And if he that sanctified it will redeem his house, then he shall add the fifth part of the money of thy estimation unto it, and it shall be his.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> And if a man shall sanctify unto the LORD some part of a field of his possession, then thy estimation shall be according to the seed thereof: an <a href="../ebd/ebd181.htm#000">homer</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd045.htm#003">barley</a> seed shall be valued at fifty shekels of <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#000">silver</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> If he sanctify his field from the year of jubile, according to thy estimation it shall stand.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> But if he sanctify his field after the jubile, then the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> shall reckon unto him the money according to the years that remain, even unto the year of the jubile, and it shall be abated from thy estimation.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> And if he that sanctified the field will in any <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#004">wise</a> redeem it, then he shall add the fifth part of the money of thy estimation unto it, and it shall be assured to him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> And if he will not redeem the field, or if he have sold the field to another man, it shall not be redeemed any more.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> But the field, when it goeth out in the jubile, shall be holy unto the LORD, as a field devoted; the possession thereof shall be the priest's.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> And if a man sanctify unto the LORD a field which he hath bought, which is not of the fields of his possession;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> Then the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> shall reckon unto him the worth of thy estimation, even unto the year of the jubile: and he shall give thine estimation in that day, as a holy thing unto the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> In the year of the jubile the field shall return unto him of whom it was bought, even to him to whom the possession of the land did belong.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> And all thy estimations shall be according to the <a href="../ebd/ebd333.htm#009">shekel</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd321.htm#005">sanctuary</a>: twenty gerahs shall be the <a href="../ebd/ebd333.htm#009">shekel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> Only the firstling of the beasts, which should be the LORD's firstling, no man shall sanctify it; whether it be <a href="../ebd/ebd281.htm#007">ox</a>, or <a href="../ebd/ebd333.htm#005">sheep</a>: it is the LORD's.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> And if it be of an unclean <a href="../ebd/ebd049.htm#001">beast</a>, then he shall redeem it according to thine estimation, and shall add a fifth part of it thereto: or if it be not redeemed, then it shall be sold according to thy estimation.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="028">28</a> Notwithstanding no devoted thing, that a man shall devote unto the LORD of all that he hath, both of man and <a href="../ebd/ebd049.htm#001">beast</a>, and of the field of his possession, shall be sold or redeemed: every devoted thing is most holy unto the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="029">29</a> None devoted, which shall be devoted of men, shall be redeemed; but shall surely be put to death.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="030">30</a> And all the <a href="../ebd/ebd368.htm#005">tithe</a> of the land, whether of the seed of the land, or of the <a href="../ebd/ebd139.htm#000">fruit</a> of the tree, is the LORD's: it is holy unto the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="031">31</a> And if a man will at all redeem ought of his tithes, he shall add thereto the fifth part thereof.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="032">32</a> And concerning the <a href="../ebd/ebd368.htm#005">tithe</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd174.htm#009">herd</a>, or of the flock, even of whatsoever passeth under the rod, the tenth shall be holy unto the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="033">33</a> He shall not search whether it be good or bad, neither shall he change it: and if he change it at all, then both it and the change thereof shall be holy; it shall not be redeemed.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="034">34</a> These are the commandments, which the LORD commanded <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> for the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> in <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#007">mount</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#009">Sinai</a>.<br>
 </p>
