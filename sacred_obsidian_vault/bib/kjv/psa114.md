 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 114</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> When <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> went out of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a>, the house of <a href="../ebd/ebd194.htm#007">Jacob</a> from a people of strange language;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a> was his <a href="../ebd/ebd321.htm#005">sanctuary</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> his dominion.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> The sea saw it, and fled: <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#004">Jordan</a> was driven back.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> The mountains skipped like rams, and the little hills like lambs.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> What ailed thee, O thou sea, that thou fleddest? thou <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#004">Jordan</a>, that thou wast driven back?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Ye mountains, that ye skipped like rams; and ye little hills, like lambs?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Tremble, thou earth, at the presence of the Lord, at the presence of the God of <a href="../ebd/ebd194.htm#007">Jacob</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> Which turned the <a href="../ebd/ebd315.htm#001">rock</a> into a standing water, the <a href="../ebd/ebd135.htm#009">flint</a> into a <a href="../ebd/ebd138.htm#000">fountain</a> of waters.<br>
 </p>
