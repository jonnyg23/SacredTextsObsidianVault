 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Numbers Chapter 2</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> And the LORD spake unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> and unto <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a>, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Every man of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> shall <a href="../ebd/ebd296.htm#008">pitch</a> by his own standard, with the <a href="../ebd/ebd121.htm#008">ensign</a> of their father's house: far off about the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> shall they <a href="../ebd/ebd296.htm#008">pitch</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And on the <a href="../ebd/ebd111.htm#000">east</a> side toward the rising of the <a href="../ebd/ebd353.htm#001">sun</a> shall they of the standard of the <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#002">camp</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd296.htm#008">pitch</a> throughout their armies: and <a href="../ebd/ebd265.htm#007">Nahshon</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd020.htm#008">Amminadab</a> shall be captain of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And his <a href="../ebd/ebd183.htm#007">host</a>, and those that were numbered of them, were threescore and fourteen thousand and six hundred.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And those that do <a href="../ebd/ebd296.htm#008">pitch</a> next unto him shall be the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#002">Issachar</a>: and <a href="../ebd/ebd271.htm#003">Nethaneel</a> the son of Zuar shall be captain of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#002">Issachar</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And his <a href="../ebd/ebd183.htm#007">host</a>, and those that were numbered thereof, were fifty and four thousand and four hundred.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Then the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd389.htm#003">Zebulun</a>: and <a href="../ebd/ebd115.htm#008">Eliab</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd173.htm#005">Helon</a> shall be captain of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd389.htm#003">Zebulun</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And his <a href="../ebd/ebd183.htm#007">host</a>, and those that were numbered thereof, were fifty and <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> thousand and four hundred.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> All that were numbered in the <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#002">camp</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a> were an hundred thousand and fourscore thousand and six thousand and four hundred, throughout their armies. These shall first set forth.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> On the south side shall be the standard of the <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#002">camp</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd311.htm#009">Reuben</a> according to their armies: and the captain of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd311.htm#009">Reuben</a> shall be Elizur the son of Shedeur.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And his <a href="../ebd/ebd183.htm#007">host</a>, and those that were numbered thereof, were forty and six thousand and five hundred.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And those which <a href="../ebd/ebd296.htm#008">pitch</a> by him shall be the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#002">Simeon</a>: and the captain of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#002">Simeon</a> shall be Shelumiel the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd396.htm#007">Zurishaddai</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> And his <a href="../ebd/ebd183.htm#007">host</a>, and those that were numbered of them, were fifty and nine thousand and three hundred.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> Then the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd140.htm#007">Gad</a>: and the captain of the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd140.htm#007">Gad</a> shall be Eliasaph the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd312.htm#001">Reuel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And his <a href="../ebd/ebd183.htm#007">host</a>, and those that were numbered of them, were forty and five thousand and six hundred and fifty.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> All that were numbered in the <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#002">camp</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd311.htm#009">Reuben</a> were an hundred thousand and fifty and one thousand and four hundred and fifty, throughout their armies. And they shall set forth in the second rank.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> Then the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> shall set forward with the <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#002">camp</a> of the Levites in the midst of the <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#002">camp</a>: as they <a href="../ebd/ebd120.htm#003">encamp</a>, so shall they set forward, every man in his place by their standards.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> On the west side shall be the standard of the <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#002">camp</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd123.htm#000">Ephraim</a> according to their armies: and the captain of the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd123.htm#000">Ephraim</a> shall be <a href="../ebd/ebd118.htm#001">Elishama</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd020.htm#007">Ammihud</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> And his <a href="../ebd/ebd183.htm#007">host</a>, and those that were numbered of them, were forty thousand and five hundred.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> And by him shall be the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd240.htm#002">Manasseh</a>: and the captain of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd240.htm#002">Manasseh</a> shall be <a href="../ebd/ebd142.htm#006">Gamaliel</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd288.htm#000">Pedahzur</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> And his <a href="../ebd/ebd183.htm#007">host</a>, and those that were numbered of them, were thirty and two thousand and two hundred.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> Then the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd052.htm#008">Benjamin</a>: and the captain of the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd052.htm#008">Benjamin</a> shall be <a href="../ebd/ebd002.htm#008">Abidan</a> the son of Gideoni.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> And his <a href="../ebd/ebd183.htm#007">host</a>, and those that were numbered of them, were thirty and five thousand and four hundred.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> All that were numbered of the <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#002">camp</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd123.htm#000">Ephraim</a> were an hundred thousand and eight thousand and an hundred, throughout their armies. And they shall go forward in the third rank.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> The standard of the <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#002">camp</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd096.htm#009">Dan</a> shall be on the north side by their armies: and the captain of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd096.htm#009">Dan</a> shall be <a href="../ebd/ebd013.htm#008">Ahiezer</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd021.htm#000">Ammishaddai</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> And his <a href="../ebd/ebd183.htm#007">host</a>, and those that were numbered of them, were threescore and two thousand and <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> hundred.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> And those that <a href="../ebd/ebd120.htm#003">encamp</a> by him shall be the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd033.htm#008">Asher</a>: and the captain of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd033.htm#008">Asher</a> shall be <a href="../ebd/ebd282.htm#005">Pagiel</a> the son of Ocran.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="028">28</a> And his <a href="../ebd/ebd183.htm#007">host</a>, and those that were numbered of them, were forty and one thousand and five hundred.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="029">29</a> Then the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd266.htm#006">Naphtali</a>: and the captain of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd266.htm#006">Naphtali</a> shall be <a href="../ebd/ebd014.htm#008">Ahira</a> the son of Enan.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="030">30</a> And his <a href="../ebd/ebd183.htm#007">host</a>, and those that were numbered of them, were fifty and three thousand and four hundred.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="031">31</a> All they that were numbered in the <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#002">camp</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd096.htm#009">Dan</a> were an hundred thousand and fifty and <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> thousand and six hundred. They shall go hindmost with their standards.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="032">32</a> These are those which were numbered of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> by the house of their fathers: all those that were numbered of the camps throughout their hosts were six hundred thousand and three thousand and five hundred and fifty.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="033">33</a> But the Levites were not numbered among the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>; as the LORD commanded <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="034">34</a> And the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> did according to all that the LORD commanded <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>: so they pitched by their standards, and so they set forward, every one after their families, according to the house of their fathers.<br>
 </p>
