 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 137</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> By the rivers of <a href="../ebd/ebd041.htm#001">Babylon</a>, there we sat down, yea, we wept, when we remembered <a href="../ebd/ebd394.htm#002">Zion</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> We hanged our harps upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd381.htm#006">willows</a> in the midst thereof.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> For there they that carried us away <a href="../ebd/ebd072.htm#001">captive</a> required of us a song; and they that wasted us required of us mirth, saying, Sing us one of the <a href="../ebd/ebd348.htm#002">songs</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd394.htm#002">Zion</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> How shall we sing the LORD's song in a strange land?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> If I forget thee, O <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>, let my right hand forget her cunning.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> If I do not remember thee, let my tongue cleave to the roof of my mouth; if I prefer not <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a> above my chief joy.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Remember, O LORD, the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#001">Edom</a> in the day of <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>; who said, Rase it, rase it, even to the foundation thereof.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> O daughter of <a href="../ebd/ebd041.htm#001">Babylon</a>, who art to be destroyed; happy shall he be, that rewardeth thee as thou hast served us.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> Happy shall he be, that taketh and dasheth thy little ones against the stones.<br>
 </p>
