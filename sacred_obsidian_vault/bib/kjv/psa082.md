 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 82</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> God standeth in the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> of the mighty; he judgeth among the gods.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> How long will ye <a href="../ebd/ebd213.htm#005">judge</a> unjustly, and accept the persons of the wicked? <a href="../ebd/ebd326.htm#008">Selah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Defend the <a href="../ebd/ebd298.htm#006">poor</a> and fatherless: do <a href="../ebd/ebd214.htm#007">justice</a> to the afflicted and needy.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Deliver the <a href="../ebd/ebd298.htm#006">poor</a> and needy: rid them out of the hand of the wicked.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> They know not, neither will they understand; they walk on in <a href="../ebd/ebd097.htm#008">darkness</a>: all the foundations of the earth are out of course.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> I have said, Ye are gods; and all of you are children of the most High.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> But ye shall die like men, and fall like one of the princes.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> Arise, O God, <a href="../ebd/ebd213.htm#005">judge</a> the earth: for thou shalt inherit all nations.<br>
 </p>
