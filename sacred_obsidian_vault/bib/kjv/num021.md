 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Numbers Chapter 21</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> And when <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd028.htm#000">Arad</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#008">Canaanite</a>, which dwelt in the south, heard tell that <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> came by the way of the <a href="../ebd/ebd349.htm#007">spies</a>; then he fought against <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, and took some of them prisoners.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> vowed a vow unto the LORD, and said, If thou wilt indeed deliver this people into my hand, then I will utterly destroy their cities.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And the LORD hearkened to the voice of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, and delivered up the <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#007">Canaanites</a>; and they utterly destroyed them and their cities: and he called the name of the place <a href="../ebd/ebd182.htm#002">Hormah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And they journeyed from <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#007">mount</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd181.htm#008">Hor</a> by the way of the Red sea, to compass the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#001">Edom</a>: and the soul of the people was much discouraged because of the way.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And the people spake against God, and against <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, Wherefore have ye brought us up out of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a> to die in the <a href="../ebd/ebd381.htm#005">wilderness</a>? for there is no <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#006">bread</a>, neither is there any water; and our soul loatheth this light <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#006">bread</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And the LORD sent fiery serpents among the people, and they <a href="../ebd/ebd059.htm#006">bit</a> the people; and much people of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> died.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Therefore the people came to <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, and said, We have sinned, for we have spoken against the LORD, and against thee; pray unto the LORD, that he take away the serpents from us. And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> prayed for the people.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And the LORD said unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, Make thee a fiery <a href="../ebd/ebd328.htm#009">serpent</a>, and set it upon a pole: and it shall come to pass, that every one that is bitten, when he looketh upon it, shall live.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> made a <a href="../ebd/ebd328.htm#009">serpent</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#003">brass</a>, and put it upon a pole, and it came to pass, that if a <a href="../ebd/ebd328.htm#009">serpent</a> had bitten any man, when he beheld the <a href="../ebd/ebd328.htm#009">serpent</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#003">brass</a>, he lived.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> set forward, and pitched in <a href="../ebd/ebd276.htm#008">Oboth</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And they journeyed from <a href="../ebd/ebd276.htm#008">Oboth</a>, and pitched at Ijeabarim, in the <a href="../ebd/ebd381.htm#005">wilderness</a> which is before <a href="../ebd/ebd258.htm#005">Moab</a>, toward the sunrising.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> From thence they removed, and pitched in the <a href="../ebd/ebd376.htm#008">valley</a> of Zared.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> From thence they removed, and pitched on the other side of <a href="../ebd/ebd032.htm#000">Arnon</a>, which is in the <a href="../ebd/ebd381.htm#005">wilderness</a> that cometh out of the coasts of the <a href="../ebd/ebd021.htm#006">Amorites</a>: for <a href="../ebd/ebd032.htm#000">Arnon</a> is the border of <a href="../ebd/ebd258.htm#005">Moab</a>, between <a href="../ebd/ebd258.htm#005">Moab</a> and the <a href="../ebd/ebd021.htm#006">Amorites</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> Wherefore it is said in the book of the wars of the LORD, What he did in the Red sea, and in the brooks of <a href="../ebd/ebd032.htm#000">Arnon</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And at the stream of the brooks that goeth down to the dwelling of Ar, and lieth upon the border of <a href="../ebd/ebd258.htm#005">Moab</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> And from thence they went to <a href="../ebd/ebd050.htm#002">Beer</a>: that is the well whereof the LORD spake unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, Gather the people together, and I will give them water.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> Then <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> sang this song, Spring up, O well; sing ye unto it:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> The princes digged the well, the nobles of the people digged it, by the direction of the lawgiver, with their staves. And from the <a href="../ebd/ebd381.htm#005">wilderness</a> they went to <a href="../ebd/ebd243.htm#008">Mattanah</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> And from <a href="../ebd/ebd243.htm#008">Mattanah</a> to <a href="../ebd/ebd265.htm#000">Nahaliel</a>: and from <a href="../ebd/ebd265.htm#000">Nahaliel</a> to <a href="../ebd/ebd043.htm#001">Bamoth</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> And from <a href="../ebd/ebd043.htm#001">Bamoth</a> in the <a href="../ebd/ebd376.htm#008">valley</a>, that is in the country of <a href="../ebd/ebd258.htm#005">Moab</a>, to the top of <a href="../ebd/ebd296.htm#004">Pisgah</a>, which looketh toward <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#002">Jeshimon</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> sent messengers unto <a href="../ebd/ebd343.htm#002">Sihon</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd021.htm#006">Amorites</a>, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> Let me pass through thy land: we will not turn into the fields, or into the vineyards; we will not <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a> of the waters of the well: but we will go along by the king's high way, until we be past thy borders.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd343.htm#002">Sihon</a> would not suffer <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> to pass through his border: but <a href="../ebd/ebd343.htm#002">Sihon</a> gathered all his people together, and went out against <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> into the <a href="../ebd/ebd381.htm#005">wilderness</a>: and he came to <a href="../ebd/ebd195.htm#005">Jahaz</a>, and fought against <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> smote him with the edge of the <a href="../ebd/ebd354.htm#003">sword</a>, and possessed his land from <a href="../ebd/ebd032.htm#000">Arnon</a> unto <a href="../ebd/ebd193.htm#006">Jabbok</a>, even unto the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd021.htm#002">Ammon</a>: for the border of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd021.htm#002">Ammon</a> was strong.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> took all these cities: and <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> dwelt in all the cities of the <a href="../ebd/ebd021.htm#006">Amorites</a>, in <a href="../ebd/ebd176.htm#009">Heshbon</a>, and in all the <a href="../ebd/ebd377.htm#003">villages</a> thereof.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> For <a href="../ebd/ebd176.htm#009">Heshbon</a> was the city of <a href="../ebd/ebd343.htm#002">Sihon</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd021.htm#006">Amorites</a>, who had fought against the former <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd258.htm#005">Moab</a>, and taken all his land out of his hand, even unto <a href="../ebd/ebd032.htm#000">Arnon</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> Wherefore they that speak in proverbs say, Come into <a href="../ebd/ebd176.htm#009">Heshbon</a>, let the city of <a href="../ebd/ebd343.htm#002">Sihon</a> be built and prepared:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="028">28</a> For there is a fire gone out of <a href="../ebd/ebd176.htm#009">Heshbon</a>, a flame from the city of <a href="../ebd/ebd343.htm#002">Sihon</a>: it hath consumed Ar of <a href="../ebd/ebd258.htm#005">Moab</a>, and the lords of the high places of <a href="../ebd/ebd032.htm#000">Arnon</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="029">29</a> Woe to thee, <a href="../ebd/ebd258.htm#005">Moab</a>! thou art undone, O people of <a href="../ebd/ebd078.htm#007">Chemosh</a>: he hath given his sons that escaped, and his daughters, into <a href="../ebd/ebd072.htm#002">captivity</a> unto <a href="../ebd/ebd343.htm#002">Sihon</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd021.htm#006">Amorites</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="030">30</a> We have shot at them; <a href="../ebd/ebd176.htm#009">Heshbon</a> is perished even unto <a href="../ebd/ebd103.htm#005">Dibon</a>, and we have laid them waste even unto <a href="../ebd/ebd275.htm#000">Nophah</a>, which reacheth unto <a href="../ebd/ebd246.htm#000">Medeba</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="031">31</a> Thus <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> dwelt in the land of the <a href="../ebd/ebd021.htm#006">Amorites</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="032">32</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> sent to spy out <a href="../ebd/ebd193.htm#002">Jaazer</a>, and they took the <a href="../ebd/ebd377.htm#003">villages</a> thereof, and drove out the <a href="../ebd/ebd021.htm#006">Amorites</a> that were there.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="033">33</a> And they turned and went up by the way of <a href="../ebd/ebd046.htm#003">Bashan</a>: and <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#002">Og</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd046.htm#003">Bashan</a> went out against them, he, and all his people, to the battle at <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#002">Edrei</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="034">34</a> And the LORD said unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, Fear him not: for I have delivered him into thy hand, and all his people, and his land; and thou shalt do to him as thou didst unto <a href="../ebd/ebd343.htm#002">Sihon</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd021.htm#006">Amorites</a>, which dwelt at <a href="../ebd/ebd176.htm#009">Heshbon</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="035">35</a> So they smote him, and his sons, and all his people, until there was none left him alive: and they possessed his land.<br>
 </p>
