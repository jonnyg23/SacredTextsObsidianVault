 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Numbers Chapter 29</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> And in the seventh month, on the first day of the month, ye shall have an holy <a href="../ebd/ebd089.htm#003">convocation</a>; ye shall do no servile work: it is a day of blowing the <a href="../ebd/ebd373.htm#001">trumpets</a> unto you.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> And ye shall offer a burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> for a sweet savour unto the LORD; one young <a href="../ebd/ebd066.htm#003">bullock</a>, one <a href="../ebd/ebd306.htm#003">ram</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> lambs of the first year without <a href="../ebd/ebd060.htm#007">blemish</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And their meat <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> shall be of <a href="../ebd/ebd136.htm#001">flour</a> mingled with oil, three tenth deals for a <a href="../ebd/ebd066.htm#003">bullock</a>, and two tenth deals for a <a href="../ebd/ebd306.htm#003">ram</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And one tenth deal for one <a href="../ebd/ebd223.htm#003">lamb</a>, throughout the <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> lambs:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And one kid of the goats for a <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, to make an <a href="../ebd/ebd036.htm#004">atonement</a> for you:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Beside the burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> of the month, and his meat <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, and the daily burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, and his meat <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, and their <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a> offerings, according unto their manner, for a sweet savour, a <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#002">sacrifice</a> made by fire unto the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And ye shall have on the tenth day of this seventh month an holy <a href="../ebd/ebd089.htm#003">convocation</a>; and ye shall afflict your souls: ye shall not do any work therein:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> But ye shall offer a burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> unto the LORD for a sweet savour; one young <a href="../ebd/ebd066.htm#003">bullock</a>, one <a href="../ebd/ebd306.htm#003">ram</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> lambs of the first year; they shall be unto you without <a href="../ebd/ebd060.htm#007">blemish</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And their meat <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> shall be of <a href="../ebd/ebd136.htm#001">flour</a> mingled with oil, three tenth deals to a <a href="../ebd/ebd066.htm#003">bullock</a>, and two tenth deals to one <a href="../ebd/ebd306.htm#003">ram</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> A several tenth deal for one <a href="../ebd/ebd223.htm#003">lamb</a>, throughout the <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> lambs:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> One kid of the goats for a <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>; beside the <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd036.htm#004">atonement</a>, and the continual burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, and the meat <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> of it, and their <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a> offerings.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And on the fifteenth day of the seventh month ye shall have an holy <a href="../ebd/ebd089.htm#003">convocation</a>; ye shall do no servile work, and ye shall keep a <a href="../ebd/ebd132.htm#000">feast</a> unto the LORD <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> days:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> And ye shall offer a burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, a <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#002">sacrifice</a> made by fire, of a sweet savour unto the LORD; thirteen young bullocks, two rams, and fourteen lambs of the first year; they shall be without <a href="../ebd/ebd060.htm#007">blemish</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> And their meat <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> shall be of <a href="../ebd/ebd136.htm#001">flour</a> mingled with oil, three tenth deals unto every <a href="../ebd/ebd066.htm#003">bullock</a> of the thirteen bullocks, two tenth deals to each <a href="../ebd/ebd306.htm#003">ram</a> of the two rams,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And a several tenth deal to each <a href="../ebd/ebd223.htm#003">lamb</a> of the fourteen lambs:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> And one kid of the goats for a <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>; beside the continual burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, his meat <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, and his <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> And on the second day ye shall offer twelve young bullocks, two rams, fourteen lambs of the first year without spot:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> And their meat <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> and their <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a> offerings for the bullocks, for the rams, and for the lambs, shall be according to their number, after the manner:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> And one kid of the goats for a <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>; beside the continual burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, and the meat <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> thereof, and their <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a> offerings.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> And on the third day eleven bullocks, two rams, fourteen lambs of the first year without <a href="../ebd/ebd060.htm#007">blemish</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> And their meat <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> and their <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a> offerings for the bullocks, for the rams, and for the lambs, shall be according to their number, after the manner:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> And one <a href="../ebd/ebd151.htm#001">goat</a> for a <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>; beside the continual burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, and his meat <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, and his <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> And on the fourth day ten bullocks, two rams, and fourteen lambs of the first year without <a href="../ebd/ebd060.htm#007">blemish</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> Their meat <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> and their <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a> offerings for the bullocks, for the rams, and for the lambs, shall be according to their number, after the manner:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> And one kid of the goats for a <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>; beside the continual burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, his meat <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, and his <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> And on the fifth day nine bullocks, two rams, and fourteen lambs of the first year without spot:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> And their meat <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> and their <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a> offerings for the bullocks, for the rams, and for the lambs, shall be according to their number, after the manner:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="028">28</a> And one <a href="../ebd/ebd151.htm#001">goat</a> for a <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>; beside the continual burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, and his meat <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, and his <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="029">29</a> And on the sixth day eight bullocks, two rams, and fourteen lambs of the first year without <a href="../ebd/ebd060.htm#007">blemish</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="030">30</a> And their meat <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> and their <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a> offerings for the bullocks, for the rams, and for the lambs, shall be according to their number, after the manner:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="031">31</a> And one <a href="../ebd/ebd151.htm#001">goat</a> for a <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>; beside the continual burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, his meat <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, and his <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="032">32</a> And on the seventh day <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> bullocks, two rams, and fourteen lambs of the first year without <a href="../ebd/ebd060.htm#007">blemish</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="033">33</a> And their meat <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> and their <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a> offerings for the bullocks, for the rams, and for the lambs, shall be according to their number, after the manner:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="034">34</a> And one <a href="../ebd/ebd151.htm#001">goat</a> for a <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>; beside the continual burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, his meat <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, and his <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="035">35</a> On the eighth day ye shall have a solemn assembly: ye shall do no servile work therein:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="036">36</a> But ye shall offer a burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, a <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#002">sacrifice</a> made by fire, of a sweet savour unto the LORD: one <a href="../ebd/ebd066.htm#003">bullock</a>, one <a href="../ebd/ebd306.htm#003">ram</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> lambs of the first year without <a href="../ebd/ebd060.htm#007">blemish</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="037">37</a> Their meat <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> and their <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a> offerings for the <a href="../ebd/ebd066.htm#003">bullock</a>, for the <a href="../ebd/ebd306.htm#003">ram</a>, and for the lambs, shall be according to their number, after the manner:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="038">38</a> And one <a href="../ebd/ebd151.htm#001">goat</a> for a <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>; beside the continual burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, and his meat <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, and his <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="039">39</a> These things ye shall do unto the LORD in your set feasts, beside your <a href="../ebd/ebd378.htm#001">vows</a>, and your freewill offerings, for your burnt offerings, and for your meat offerings, and for your <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a> offerings, and for your peace offerings.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="040">40</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> told the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> according to all that the LORD commanded <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>.<br>
 </p>
