 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 130</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Out of the depths have I cried unto thee, O LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Lord, hear my voice: let thine ears be attentive to the voice of my supplications.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> If thou, LORD, shouldest mark iniquities, O Lord, who shall stand?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> But there is forgiveness with thee, that thou mayest be feared.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> I wait for the LORD, my soul doth wait, and in his word do I hope.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> My soul waiteth for the Lord more than they that watch for the morning: I say, more than they that watch for the morning.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Let <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> hope in the LORD: for with the LORD there is <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a>, and with him is plenteous <a href="../ebd/ebd308.htm#009">redemption</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And he shall redeem <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> from all his iniquities.<br>
 </p>
