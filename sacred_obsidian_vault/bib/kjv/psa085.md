 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 85</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Lord, thou hast been favourable unto thy land: thou hast brought back the <a href="../ebd/ebd072.htm#002">captivity</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd194.htm#007">Jacob</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Thou hast forgiven the iniquity of thy people, thou hast covered all their <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a>. <a href="../ebd/ebd326.htm#008">Selah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Thou hast taken away all thy wrath: thou hast turned thyself from the fierceness of thine <a href="../ebd/ebd024.htm#002">anger</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Turn us, O God of our <a href="../ebd/ebd320.htm#003">salvation</a>, and cause thine <a href="../ebd/ebd024.htm#002">anger</a> toward us to cease.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Wilt thou be angry with us for ever? wilt thou draw out thine <a href="../ebd/ebd024.htm#002">anger</a> to all generations?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Wilt thou not revive us again: that thy people may rejoice in thee?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Shew us thy <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a>, O LORD, and grant us thy <a href="../ebd/ebd320.htm#003">salvation</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> I will hear what God the LORD will speak: for he will speak peace unto his people, and to his saints: but let them not turn again to folly.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> Surely his <a href="../ebd/ebd320.htm#003">salvation</a> is nigh them that fear him; that <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a> may dwell in our land.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">Mercy</a> and truth are met together; <a href="../ebd/ebd313.htm#004">righteousness</a> and peace have kissed each other.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> Truth shall spring out of the earth; and <a href="../ebd/ebd313.htm#004">righteousness</a> shall look down from <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#006">heaven</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> Yea, the LORD shall give that which is good; and our land shall yield her increase.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd313.htm#004">Righteousness</a> shall go before him; and shall set us in the way of his steps.<br>
 </p>
