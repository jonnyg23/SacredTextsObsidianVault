 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Exodus Chapter 27</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> And thou shalt make an <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd339.htm#009">shittim</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd383.htm#003">wood</a>, five cubits long, and five cubits broad; the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> shall be foursquare: and the height thereof shall be three cubits.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> And thou shalt make the horns of it upon the four corners thereof: his horns shall be of the same: and thou shalt overlay it with <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#003">brass</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And thou shalt make his pans to receive his <a href="../ebd/ebd034.htm#000">ashes</a>, and his shovels, and his basons, and his fleshhooks, and his firepans: all the vessels thereof thou shalt make of <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#003">brass</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And thou shalt make for it a <a href="../ebd/ebd154.htm#007">grate</a> of network of <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#003">brass</a>; and upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd271.htm#002">net</a> shalt thou make four brasen rings in the four corners thereof.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And thou shalt put it under the compass of the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> beneath, that the <a href="../ebd/ebd271.htm#002">net</a> may be even to the midst of the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And thou shalt make staves for the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>, staves of <a href="../ebd/ebd339.htm#009">shittim</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd383.htm#003">wood</a>, and overlay them with <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#003">brass</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And the staves shall be put into the rings, and the staves shall be upon the two sides of the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>, to bear it.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> Hollow with boards shalt thou make it: as it was shewed thee in the <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#007">mount</a>, so shall they make it.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And thou shalt make the court of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a>: for the south side southward there shall be hangings for the court of fine twined <a href="../ebd/ebd229.htm#008">linen</a> of an hundred cubits long for one side:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And the twenty pillars thereof and their twenty sockets shall be of <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#003">brass</a>; the hooks of the pillars and their <a href="../ebd/ebd133.htm#002">fillets</a> shall be of <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#000">silver</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And likewise for the north side in length there shall be hangings of an hundred cubits long, and his twenty pillars and their twenty sockets of <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#003">brass</a>; the hooks of the pillars and their <a href="../ebd/ebd133.htm#002">fillets</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#000">silver</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And for the breadth of the court on the west side shall be hangings of fifty cubits: their pillars ten, and their sockets ten.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> And the breadth of the court on the <a href="../ebd/ebd111.htm#000">east</a> side eastward shall be fifty cubits.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> The hangings of one side of the <a href="../ebd/ebd143.htm#008">gate</a> shall be fifteen cubits: their pillars three, and their sockets three.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And on the other side shall be hangings fifteen cubits: their pillars three, and their sockets three.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> And for the <a href="../ebd/ebd143.htm#008">gate</a> of the court shall be an <a href="../ebd/ebd163.htm#001">hanging</a> of twenty cubits, of <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#003">blue</a>, and purple, and <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#005">scarlet</a>, and fine twined <a href="../ebd/ebd229.htm#008">linen</a>, wrought with needlework: and their pillars shall be four, and their sockets four.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> All the pillars round about the court shall be filleted with <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#000">silver</a>; their hooks shall be of <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#000">silver</a>, and their sockets of <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#003">brass</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> The length of the court shall be an hundred cubits, and the breadth fifty every where, and the height five cubits of fine twined <a href="../ebd/ebd229.htm#008">linen</a>, and their sockets of <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#003">brass</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> All the vessels of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> in all the service thereof, and all the pins thereof, and all the pins of the court, shall be of <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#003">brass</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> And thou shalt command the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, that they bring thee pure oil <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#009">olive</a> beaten for the light, to cause the <a href="../ebd/ebd223.htm#007">lamp</a> to burn always.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> In the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> without the vail, which is before the <a href="../ebd/ebd362.htm#008">testimony</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> and his sons shall order it from evening to morning before the LORD: it shall be a statute for ever unto their generations on the behalf of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>.<br>
 </p>
