 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 24</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> The earth is the LORD's, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd139.htm#007">fulness</a> thereof; the world, and they that dwell therein.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> For he hath founded it upon the seas, and established it upon the floods.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Who shall ascend into the hill of the LORD? or who shall stand in his holy place?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> He that hath <a href="../ebd/ebd084.htm#005">clean</a> hands, and a pure heart; who hath not lifted up his soul unto vanity, nor sworn deceitfully.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> He shall receive the blessing from the LORD, and <a href="../ebd/ebd313.htm#004">righteousness</a> from the God of his <a href="../ebd/ebd320.htm#003">salvation</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> This is the <a href="../ebd/ebd145.htm#008">generation</a> of them that seek him, that seek thy face, O <a href="../ebd/ebd194.htm#007">Jacob</a>. <a href="../ebd/ebd326.htm#008">Selah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Lift up your heads, O ye gates; and be ye lift up, ye <a href="../ebd/ebd127.htm#007">everlasting</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd105.htm#009">doors</a>; and the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">King</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a> shall come in.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> Who is this <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">King</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a>? The LORD strong and mighty, the LORD mighty in battle.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> Lift up your heads, O ye gates; even lift them up, ye <a href="../ebd/ebd127.htm#007">everlasting</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd105.htm#009">doors</a>; and the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">King</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a> shall come in.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> Who is this <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">King</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a>? The LORD of hosts, he is the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">King</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a>. <a href="../ebd/ebd326.htm#008">Selah</a>.<br>
 </p>
