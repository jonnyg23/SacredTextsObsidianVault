 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Nehemiah Chapter 3</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Then <a href="../ebd/ebd116.htm#003">Eliashib</a> the high <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> rose up with his brethren the priests, and they builded the <a href="../ebd/ebd333.htm#005">sheep</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd143.htm#008">gate</a>; they sanctified it, and set up the <a href="../ebd/ebd105.htm#009">doors</a> of it; even unto the tower of <a href="../ebd/ebd245.htm#001">Meah</a> they sanctified it, unto the tower of <a href="../ebd/ebd162.htm#002">Hananeel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> And next unto him builded the men of <a href="../ebd/ebd203.htm#008">Jericho</a>. And next to them builded <a href="../ebd/ebd386.htm#004">Zaccur</a> the son of Imri.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> But the <a href="../ebd/ebd134.htm#005">fish</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd143.htm#008">gate</a> did the sons of Hassenaah build, who also laid the beams thereof, and set up the <a href="../ebd/ebd105.htm#009">doors</a> thereof, the locks thereof, and the bars thereof.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And next unto them repaired <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#008">Meremoth</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd375.htm#004">Urijah</a>, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd222.htm#007">Koz</a>. And next unto them repaired <a href="../ebd/ebd251.htm#001">Meshullam</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd053.htm#003">Berechiah</a>, the son of Meshezabeel. And next unto them repaired <a href="../ebd/ebd386.htm#008">Zadok</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd040.htm#006">Baana</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And next unto them the Tekoites repaired; but their nobles put not their necks to the work of their LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Moreover the old <a href="../ebd/ebd143.htm#008">gate</a> repaired <a href="../ebd/ebd200.htm#009">Jehoiada</a> the son of Paseah, and <a href="../ebd/ebd251.htm#001">Meshullam</a> the son of Besodeiah; they laid the beams thereof, and set up the <a href="../ebd/ebd105.htm#009">doors</a> thereof, and the locks thereof, and the bars thereof.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And next unto them repaired Melatiah the Gibeonite, and <a href="../ebd/ebd195.htm#000">Jadon</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd250.htm#004">Meronothite</a>, the men of <a href="../ebd/ebd148.htm#002">Gibeon</a>, and of <a href="../ebd/ebd258.htm#000">Mizpah</a>, unto the <a href="../ebd/ebd365.htm#001">throne</a> of the governor on this side the river.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> Next unto him repaired <a href="../ebd/ebd376.htm#005">Uzziel</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd164.htm#001">Harhaiah</a>, of the goldsmiths. Next unto him also repaired <a href="../ebd/ebd162.htm#004">Hananiah</a> the son of one of the apothecaries, and they fortified <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a> unto the broad wall.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And next unto them repaired Rephaiah the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd184.htm#009">Hur</a>, the ruler of the half part of <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And next unto them repaired <a href="../ebd/ebd199.htm#006">Jedaiah</a> the son of Harumaph, even over against his house. And next unto him repaired <a href="../ebd/ebd167.htm#006">Hattush</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd166.htm#002">Hashabniah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> Malchijah the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd164.htm#003">Harim</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd166.htm#005">Hashub</a> the son of Pahathmoab, repaired the other piece, and the tower of the furnaces.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And next unto him repaired <a href="../ebd/ebd330.htm#008">Shallum</a> the son of Halohesh, the ruler of the half part of <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>, he and his daughters.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> The <a href="../ebd/ebd376.htm#008">valley</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd143.htm#008">gate</a> repaired <a href="../ebd/ebd163.htm#004">Hanun</a>, and the inhabitants of <a href="../ebd/ebd387.htm#004">Zanoah</a>; they built it, and set up the <a href="../ebd/ebd105.htm#009">doors</a> thereof, the locks thereof, and the bars thereof, and a thousand cubits on the wall unto the <a href="../ebd/ebd109.htm#003">dung</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd143.htm#008">gate</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> But the <a href="../ebd/ebd109.htm#003">dung</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd143.htm#008">gate</a> repaired <a href="../ebd/ebd239.htm#001">Malchiah</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd308.htm#002">Rechab</a>, the ruler of part of Bethhaccerem; he built it, and set up the <a href="../ebd/ebd105.htm#009">doors</a> thereof, the locks thereof, and the bars thereof.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> But the <a href="../ebd/ebd143.htm#008">gate</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd138.htm#000">fountain</a> repaired Shallun the son of Colhozeh, the ruler of part of <a href="../ebd/ebd258.htm#000">Mizpah</a>; he built it, and covered it, and set up the <a href="../ebd/ebd105.htm#009">doors</a> thereof, the locks thereof, and the bars thereof, and the wall of the pool of Siloah by the king's garden, and unto the stairs that go down from the city of <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> After him repaired <a href="../ebd/ebd269.htm#007">Nehemiah</a> the son of Azbuk, the ruler of the half part of <a href="../ebd/ebd057.htm#004">Bethzur</a>, unto the place over against the sepulchres of <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a>, and to the pool that was made, and unto the house of the mighty.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> After him repaired the Levites, <a href="../ebd/ebd309.htm#009">Rehum</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd043.htm#004">Bani</a>. Next unto him repaired <a href="../ebd/ebd166.htm#001">Hashabiah</a>, the ruler of the half part of <a href="../ebd/ebd216.htm#009">Keilah</a>, in his part.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> After him repaired their brethren, Bavai the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd174.htm#004">Henadad</a>, the ruler of the half part of <a href="../ebd/ebd216.htm#009">Keilah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> And next to him repaired <a href="../ebd/ebd129.htm#004">Ezer</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#003">Jeshua</a>, the ruler of <a href="../ebd/ebd258.htm#000">Mizpah</a>, another piece over against the going up to the <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#008">armoury</a> at the turning of the wall.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> After him <a href="../ebd/ebd046.htm#001">Baruch</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd385.htm#006">Zabbai</a> earnestly repaired the other piece, from the turning of the wall unto the door of the house of <a href="../ebd/ebd116.htm#003">Eliashib</a> the high <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> After him repaired <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#008">Meremoth</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd375.htm#004">Urijah</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd222.htm#007">Koz</a> another piece, from the door of the house of <a href="../ebd/ebd116.htm#003">Eliashib</a> even to the end of the house of <a href="../ebd/ebd116.htm#003">Eliashib</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> And after him repaired the priests, the men of the <a href="../ebd/ebd297.htm#002">plain</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> After him repaired <a href="../ebd/ebd052.htm#008">Benjamin</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd166.htm#005">Hashub</a> over against their house. After him repaired <a href="../ebd/ebd037.htm#005">Azariah</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd234.htm#007">Maaseiah</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd023.htm#000">Ananiah</a> by his house.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> After him repaired Binnui the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd174.htm#004">Henadad</a> another piece, from the house of <a href="../ebd/ebd037.htm#005">Azariah</a> unto the turning of the wall, even unto the corner.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> Palal the son of Uzai, over against the turning of the wall, and the tower which lieth out from the king's high house, that was by the court of the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#006">prison</a>. After him <a href="../ebd/ebd288.htm#001">Pedaiah</a> the son of Parosh.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> Moreover the Nethinims dwelt in <a href="../ebd/ebd279.htm#006">Ophel</a>, unto the place over against the water <a href="../ebd/ebd143.htm#008">gate</a> toward the <a href="../ebd/ebd111.htm#000">east</a>, and the tower that lieth out.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> After them the Tekoites repaired another piece, over against the great tower that lieth out, even unto the wall of <a href="../ebd/ebd279.htm#006">Ophel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="028">28</a> From above the horse <a href="../ebd/ebd143.htm#008">gate</a> repaired the priests, every one over against his house.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="029">29</a> After them repaired <a href="../ebd/ebd386.htm#008">Zadok</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd187.htm#008">Immer</a> over against his house. After him repaired also <a href="../ebd/ebd334.htm#005">Shemaiah</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd333.htm#002">Shechaniah</a>, the keeper of the <a href="../ebd/ebd111.htm#000">east</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd143.htm#008">gate</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="030">30</a> After him repaired <a href="../ebd/ebd162.htm#004">Hananiah</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd334.htm#001">Shelemiah</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd163.htm#004">Hanun</a> the sixth son of Zalaph, another piece. After him repaired <a href="../ebd/ebd251.htm#001">Meshullam</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd053.htm#003">Berechiah</a> over against his <a href="../ebd/ebd076.htm#003">chamber</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="031">31</a> After him repaired <a href="../ebd/ebd239.htm#001">Malchiah</a> the goldsmith's son unto the place of the Nethinims, and of the merchants, over against the <a href="../ebd/ebd143.htm#008">gate</a> Miphkad, and to the going up of the corner.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="032">32</a> And between the going up of the corner unto the <a href="../ebd/ebd333.htm#005">sheep</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd143.htm#008">gate</a> repaired the goldsmiths and the merchants.<br>
 </p>
