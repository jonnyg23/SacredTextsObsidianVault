 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 84</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> How amiable are thy tabernacles, O LORD of hosts!<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> My soul longeth, yea, even fainteth for the courts of the LORD: my heart and my <a href="../ebd/ebd135.htm#007">flesh</a> crieth out for the living God.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Yea, the <a href="../ebd/ebd349.htm#003">sparrow</a> hath found an house, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd353.htm#009">swallow</a> a nest for herself, where she may lay her young, even thine altars, O LORD of hosts, my <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">King</a>, and my God.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Blessed are they that dwell in thy house: they will be still praising thee. <a href="../ebd/ebd326.htm#008">Selah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Blessed is the man whose strength is in thee; in whose heart are the ways of them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Who passing through the <a href="../ebd/ebd376.htm#008">valley</a> of Baca make it a well; the <a href="../ebd/ebd305.htm#008">rain</a> also filleth the pools.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> They go from strength to strength, every one of them in <a href="../ebd/ebd394.htm#002">Zion</a> appeareth before God.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> O LORD God of hosts, hear my <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#000">prayer</a>: give <a href="../ebd/ebd110.htm#004">ear</a>, O God of <a href="../ebd/ebd194.htm#007">Jacob</a>. <a href="../ebd/ebd326.htm#008">Selah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> Behold, O God our <a href="../ebd/ebd337.htm#001">shield</a>, and look upon the face of thine anointed.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> For a day in thy courts is better than a thousand. I had rather be a doorkeeper in the house of my God, than to dwell in the tents of wickedness.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> For the LORD God is a <a href="../ebd/ebd353.htm#001">sun</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd337.htm#001">shield</a>: the LORD will give <a href="../ebd/ebd154.htm#000">grace</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a>: no good thing will he withhold from them that walk uprightly.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> O LORD of hosts, blessed is the man that trusteth in thee.<br>
 </p>
