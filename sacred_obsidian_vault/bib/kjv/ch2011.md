 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: 2 Chronicles Chapter 11</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> And when <a href="../ebd/ebd309.htm#007">Rehoboam</a> was come to <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>, he gathered of the house of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd052.htm#008">Benjamin</a> an hundred and fourscore thousand <a href="../ebd/ebd081.htm#008">chosen</a> men, which were warriors, to fight against <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, that he might bring the kingdom again to <a href="../ebd/ebd309.htm#007">Rehoboam</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> But the word of the LORD came to <a href="../ebd/ebd334.htm#005">Shemaiah</a> the man of God, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Speak unto <a href="../ebd/ebd309.htm#007">Rehoboam</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd347.htm#007">Solomon</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>, and to all <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> in <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd052.htm#008">Benjamin</a>, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Thus saith the LORD, Ye shall not go up, nor fight against your brethren: return every man to his house: for this thing is done of me. And they obeyed the words of the LORD, and returned from going against <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#000">Jeroboam</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd309.htm#007">Rehoboam</a> dwelt in <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>, and built cities for defence in <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> He built even <a href="../ebd/ebd056.htm#005">Bethlehem</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd125.htm#008">Etam</a>, and Tekoa,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd057.htm#004">Bethzur</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd340.htm#008">Shoco</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd010.htm#007">Adullam</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd143.htm#009">Gath</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd241.htm#009">Mareshah</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd394.htm#004">Ziph</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And Adoraim, and <a href="../ebd/ebd222.htm#009">Lachish</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd037.htm#008">Azekah</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd396.htm#002">Zorah</a>, and Aijalon, and <a href="../ebd/ebd171.htm#004">Hebron</a>, which are in <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a> and in <a href="../ebd/ebd052.htm#008">Benjamin</a> fenced cities.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And he fortified the strong holds, and put captains in them, and store of victual, and of oil and <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#000">wine</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And in every several city he put shields and spears, and made them exceeding strong, having <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd052.htm#008">Benjamin</a> on his side.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> And the priests and the Levites that were in all <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> resorted to him out of all their coasts.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> For the Levites left their <a href="../ebd/ebd352.htm#007">suburbs</a> and their possession, and came to <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>: for <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#000">Jeroboam</a> and his sons had cast them off from executing the priest's office unto the LORD:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And he ordained him priests for the high places, and for the devils, and for the calves which he had made.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> And after them out of all the tribes of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> such as set their hearts to seek the LORD God of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> came to <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>, to <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#002">sacrifice</a> unto the LORD God of their fathers.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> So they strengthened the kingdom of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>, and made <a href="../ebd/ebd309.htm#007">Rehoboam</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd347.htm#007">Solomon</a> strong, three years: for three years they walked in the way of <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd347.htm#007">Solomon</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd309.htm#007">Rehoboam</a> took him <a href="../ebd/ebd237.htm#002">Mahalath</a> the daughter of <a href="../ebd/ebd203.htm#009">Jerimoth</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> to wife, and <a href="../ebd/ebd003.htm#003">Abihail</a> the daughter of <a href="../ebd/ebd115.htm#008">Eliab</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#005">Jesse</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> Which bare him children; <a href="../ebd/ebd206.htm#003">Jeush</a>, and Shamariah, and Zaham.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> And after her he took <a href="../ebd/ebd234.htm#004">Maachah</a> the daughter of <a href="../ebd/ebd005.htm#008">Absalom</a>; which bare him <a href="../ebd/ebd003.htm#006">Abijah</a>, and Attai, and <a href="../ebd/ebd395.htm#001">Ziza</a>, and Shelomith.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd309.htm#007">Rehoboam</a> loved <a href="../ebd/ebd234.htm#004">Maachah</a> the daughter of <a href="../ebd/ebd005.htm#008">Absalom</a> above all his wives and his concubines: (for he took eighteen wives, and threescore concubines; and begat twenty and eight sons, and threescore daughters.)<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd309.htm#007">Rehoboam</a> made <a href="../ebd/ebd003.htm#006">Abijah</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd234.htm#004">Maachah</a> the chief, to be ruler among his brethren: for he thought to make him <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> And he dealt wisely, and dispersed of all his children throughout all the countries of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd052.htm#008">Benjamin</a>, unto every fenced city: and he gave them victual in abundance. And he desired many wives.<br>
 </p>
