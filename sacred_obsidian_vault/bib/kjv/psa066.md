 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 66</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Make a joyful noise unto God, all ye lands:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Sing forth the honour of his name: make his praise glorious.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Say unto God, How terrible art thou in thy works! through the greatness of thy power shall thine enemies submit themselves unto thee.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> All the earth shall <a href="../ebd/ebd384.htm#002">worship</a> thee, and shall sing unto thee; they shall sing to thy name. <a href="../ebd/ebd326.htm#008">Selah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Come and see the works of God: he is terrible in his doing toward the children of men.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> He turned the sea into dry land: they went through the <a href="../ebd/ebd136.htm#000">flood</a> on foot: there did we rejoice in him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> He ruleth by his power for ever; his eyes behold the nations: let not the rebellious exalt themselves. <a href="../ebd/ebd326.htm#008">Selah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> O <a href="../ebd/ebd060.htm#008">bless</a> our God, ye people, and make the voice of his praise to be heard:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> Which holdeth our soul in life, and suffereth not our feet to be moved.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> For thou, O God, hast proved us: thou hast tried us, as <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#000">silver</a> is tried.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> Thou broughtest us into the <a href="../ebd/ebd271.htm#002">net</a>; thou laidst affliction upon our loins.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> Thou hast caused men to ride over our heads; we went through fire and through water: but thou broughtest us out into a wealthy place.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> I will go into thy house with burnt offerings: I will pay thee my <a href="../ebd/ebd378.htm#001">vows</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> Which my lips have uttered, and my mouth hath spoken, when I was in trouble.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> I will offer unto thee burnt sacrifices of fatlings, with the <a href="../ebd/ebd188.htm#002">incense</a> of rams; I will offer bullocks with goats. <a href="../ebd/ebd326.htm#008">Selah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> Come and hear, all ye that fear God, and I will declare what he hath done for my soul.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> I cried unto him with my mouth, and he was extolled with my tongue.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> If I regard iniquity in my heart, the Lord will not hear me:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> But verily God hath heard me; he hath attended to the voice of my <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#000">prayer</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> Blessed be God, which hath not turned away my <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#000">prayer</a>, nor his <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> from me.<br>
 </p>
