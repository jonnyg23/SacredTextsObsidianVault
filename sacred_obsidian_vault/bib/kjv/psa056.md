 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 56</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Be merciful unto me, O God: for man would <a href="../ebd/ebd353.htm#009">swallow</a> me up; he fighting daily oppresseth me.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Mine enemies would daily <a href="../ebd/ebd353.htm#009">swallow</a> me up: for they be many that fight against me, O thou most High.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> What time I am afraid, I will trust in thee.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> In God I will praise his word, in God I have put my trust; I will not fear what <a href="../ebd/ebd135.htm#007">flesh</a> can do unto me.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Every day they wrest my words: all their thoughts are against me for evil.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> They gather themselves together, they hide themselves, they mark my steps, when they wait for my soul.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Shall they escape by iniquity? in thine <a href="../ebd/ebd024.htm#002">anger</a> cast down the people, O God.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> Thou tellest my wanderings: put thou my tears into thy <a href="../ebd/ebd063.htm#002">bottle</a>: are they not in thy book?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> When I cry unto thee, then shall mine enemies turn back: this I know; for God is for me.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> In God will I praise his word: in the LORD will I praise his word.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> In God have I put my trust: I will not be afraid what man can do unto me.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> Thy <a href="../ebd/ebd378.htm#001">vows</a> are upon me, O God: I will render praises unto thee.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> For thou hast delivered my soul from death: wilt not thou deliver my feet from falling, that I may walk before God in the light of the living?<br>
 </p>
