 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Leviticus Chapter 2</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> And when any will offer a meat <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> unto the LORD, his <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> shall be of fine <a href="../ebd/ebd136.htm#001">flour</a>; and he shall pour oil upon it, and put <a href="../ebd/ebd138.htm#004">frankincense</a> thereon:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> And he shall bring it to Aaron's sons the priests: and he shall take thereout his handful of the <a href="../ebd/ebd136.htm#001">flour</a> thereof, and of the oil thereof, with all the <a href="../ebd/ebd138.htm#004">frankincense</a> thereof; and the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> shall burn the memorial of it upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>, to be an <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> made by fire, of a sweet savour unto the LORD:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And the remnant of the meat <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> shall be Aaron's and his sons': it is a thing most holy of the offerings of the LORD made by fire.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And if thou bring an oblation of a meat <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> baken in the <a href="../ebd/ebd281.htm#005">oven</a>, it shall be unleavened cakes of fine <a href="../ebd/ebd136.htm#001">flour</a> mingled with oil, or unleavened <a href="../ebd/ebd378.htm#003">wafers</a> anointed with oil.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And if thy oblation be a meat <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> baken in a <a href="../ebd/ebd283.htm#009">pan</a>, it shall be of fine <a href="../ebd/ebd136.htm#001">flour</a> unleavened, mingled with oil.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Thou shalt part it in <a href="../ebd/ebd295.htm#002">pieces</a>, and pour oil thereon: it is a meat <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And if thy oblation be a meat <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> baken in the fryingpan, it shall be made of fine <a href="../ebd/ebd136.htm#001">flour</a> with oil.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And thou shalt bring the meat <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> that is made of these things unto the LORD: and when it is presented unto the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a>, he shall bring it unto the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> shall take from the meat <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> a memorial thereof, and shall burn it upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>: it is an <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> made by fire, of a sweet savour unto the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And that which is left of the meat <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> shall be Aaron's and his sons': it is a thing most holy of the offerings of the LORD made by fire.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> No meat <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, which ye shall bring unto the LORD, shall be made with <a href="../ebd/ebd226.htm#000">leaven</a>: for ye shall burn no <a href="../ebd/ebd226.htm#000">leaven</a>, nor any <a href="../ebd/ebd181.htm#001">honey</a>, in any <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> of the LORD made by fire.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> As for the oblation of the firstfruits, ye shall offer them unto the LORD: but they shall not be burnt on the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> for a sweet savour.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> And every oblation of thy meat <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> shalt thou season with <a href="../ebd/ebd319.htm#008">salt</a>; neither shalt thou suffer the <a href="../ebd/ebd319.htm#008">salt</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd091.htm#008">covenant</a> of thy God to be lacking from thy meat <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>: with all thine offerings thou shalt offer <a href="../ebd/ebd319.htm#008">salt</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> And if thou offer a meat <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> of thy firstfruits unto the LORD, thou shalt offer for the meat <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> of thy firstfruits green ears of <a href="../ebd/ebd090.htm#006">corn</a> dried by the fire, even <a href="../ebd/ebd090.htm#006">corn</a> beaten out of full ears.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And thou shalt put oil upon it, and lay <a href="../ebd/ebd138.htm#004">frankincense</a> thereon: it is a meat <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> shall burn the memorial of it, part of the beaten <a href="../ebd/ebd090.htm#006">corn</a> thereof, and part of the oil thereof, with all the <a href="../ebd/ebd138.htm#004">frankincense</a> thereof: it is an <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> made by fire unto the LORD.<br>
 </p>
