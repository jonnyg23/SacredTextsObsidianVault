 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Leviticus Chapter 10</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd264.htm#008">Nadab</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd003.htm#004">Abihu</a>, the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a>, took either of them his <a href="../ebd/ebd075.htm#002">censer</a>, and put fire therein, and put <a href="../ebd/ebd188.htm#002">incense</a> thereon, and offered strange fire before the LORD, which he commanded them not.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> And there went out fire from the LORD, and devoured them, and they died before the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Then <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> said unto <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a>, This is it that the LORD spake, saying, I will be sanctified in them that come nigh me, and before all the people I will be glorified. And <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> held his peace.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> called <a href="../ebd/ebd256.htm#006">Mishael</a> and Elzaphan, the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd376.htm#005">Uzziel</a> the uncle of <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a>, and said unto them, Come near, carry your brethren from before the <a href="../ebd/ebd321.htm#005">sanctuary</a> out of the <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#002">camp</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> So they went near, and carried them in their coats out of the <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#002">camp</a>; as <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> had said.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> said unto <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a>, and unto <a href="../ebd/ebd115.htm#000">Eleazar</a> and unto <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#005">Ithamar</a>, his sons, Uncover not your heads, neither rend your clothes; lest ye die, and lest wrath come upon all the people: but let your brethren, the whole house of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, bewail the burning which the LORD hath kindled.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And ye shall not go out from the door of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>, lest ye die: for the anointing oil of the LORD is upon you. And they did according to the word of <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And the LORD spake unto <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a>, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> Do not <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#000">wine</a> nor strong <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a>, thou, nor thy sons with thee, when ye go into the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>, lest ye die: it shall be a statute for ever throughout your generations:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And that ye may put difference between holy and unholy, and between unclean and <a href="../ebd/ebd084.htm#005">clean</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And that ye may teach the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> all the statutes which the LORD hath spoken unto them by the hand of <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> spake unto <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a>, and unto <a href="../ebd/ebd115.htm#000">Eleazar</a> and unto <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#005">Ithamar</a>, his sons that were left, Take the meat <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> that remaineth of the offerings of the LORD made by fire, and eat it without <a href="../ebd/ebd226.htm#000">leaven</a> beside the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>: for it is most holy:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> And ye shall eat it in the holy place, because it is thy due, and thy sons' due, of the sacrifices of the LORD made by fire: for so I am commanded.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> And the wave breast and heave shoulder shall ye eat in a <a href="../ebd/ebd084.htm#005">clean</a> place; thou, and thy sons, and thy daughters with thee: for they be thy due, and thy sons' due, which are given out of the sacrifices of peace offerings of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> The heave shoulder and the wave breast shall they bring with the offerings made by fire of the <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#005">fat</a>, to wave it for a wave <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> before the LORD; and it shall be thine, and thy sons' with thee, by a statute for ever; as the LORD hath commanded.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> diligently sought the <a href="../ebd/ebd151.htm#001">goat</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, and, behold, it was burnt: and he was angry with <a href="../ebd/ebd115.htm#000">Eleazar</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#005">Ithamar</a>, the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> which were left alive, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> Wherefore have ye not eaten the <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> in the holy place, seeing it is most holy, and God hath given it you to bear the iniquity of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>, to make <a href="../ebd/ebd036.htm#004">atonement</a> for them before the LORD?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> Behold, the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> of it was not brought in within the holy place: ye should indeed have eaten it in the holy place, as I commanded.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> said unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, Behold, this day have they offered their <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> and their burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> before the LORD; and such things have befallen me: and if I had eaten the <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> to day, should it have been accepted in the sight of the LORD?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> And when <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> heard that, he was content.<br>
 </p>
