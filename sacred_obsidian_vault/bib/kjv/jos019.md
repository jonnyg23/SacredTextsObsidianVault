 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Joshua Chapter 19</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> And the second lot came forth to <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#002">Simeon</a>, even for the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#002">Simeon</a> according to their families: and their inheritance was within the inheritance of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> And they had in their inheritance <a href="../ebd/ebd050.htm#008">Beersheba</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd332.htm#006">Sheba</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd258.htm#008">Moladah</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And Hazarshual, and <a href="../ebd/ebd042.htm#005">Balah</a>, and Azem,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And Eltolad, and Bethul, and <a href="../ebd/ebd182.htm#002">Hormah</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd393.htm#003">Ziklag</a>, and Bethmarcaboth, and Hazarsusah,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And Bethlebaoth, and Sharuhen; thirteen cities and their <a href="../ebd/ebd377.htm#003">villages</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Ain, Remmon, and Ether, and Ashan; four cities and their <a href="../ebd/ebd377.htm#003">villages</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And all the <a href="../ebd/ebd377.htm#003">villages</a> that were round about these cities to Baalathbeer, Ramath of the south. This is the inheritance of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#002">Simeon</a> according to their families.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> Out of the portion of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a> was the inheritance of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#002">Simeon</a>: for the part of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a> was too much for them: therefore the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#002">Simeon</a> had their inheritance within the inheritance of them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And the third lot came up for the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd389.htm#003">Zebulun</a> according to their families: and the border of their inheritance was unto Sarid:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And their border went up toward the sea, and <a href="../ebd/ebd241.htm#004">Maralah</a>, and reached to Dabbasheth, and reached to the river that is before <a href="../ebd/ebd210.htm#002">Jokneam</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And turned from Sarid eastward toward the sunrising unto the border of Chislothtabor, and then goeth out to <a href="../ebd/ebd095.htm#007">Daberath</a>, and goeth up to <a href="../ebd/ebd197.htm#003">Japhia</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> And from thence passeth on along on the <a href="../ebd/ebd111.htm#000">east</a> to Gittahhepher, to Ittahkazin, and goeth out to Remmonmethoar to <a href="../ebd/ebd267.htm#009">Neah</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> And the border compasseth it on the north side to Hannathon: and the outgoings thereof are in the <a href="../ebd/ebd376.htm#008">valley</a> of Jiphthahel:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd216.htm#002">Kattath</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd265.htm#001">Nahallal</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd338.htm#008">Shimron</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd186.htm#004">Idalah</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd056.htm#005">Bethlehem</a>: twelve cities with their <a href="../ebd/ebd377.htm#003">villages</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> This is the inheritance of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd389.htm#003">Zebulun</a> according to their families, these cities with their <a href="../ebd/ebd377.htm#003">villages</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> And the fourth lot came out to <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#002">Issachar</a>, for the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#002">Issachar</a> according to their families.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> And their border was toward <a href="../ebd/ebd206.htm#008">Jezreel</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd079.htm#009">Chesulloth</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd342.htm#001">Shunem</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> And Haphraim, and <a href="../ebd/ebd337.htm#003">Shihon</a>, and Anaharath,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> And Rabbith, and <a href="../ebd/ebd220.htm#008">Kishion</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd002.htm#001">Abez</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd310.htm#004">Remeth</a>, and Engannim, and Enhaddah, and Bethpazzez;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> And the coast reacheth to <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#007">Tabor</a>, and Shahazimah, and Bethshemesh; and the outgoings of their border were at <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#004">Jordan</a>: sixteen cities with their <a href="../ebd/ebd377.htm#003">villages</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> This is the inheritance of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#002">Issachar</a> according to their families, the cities and their <a href="../ebd/ebd377.htm#003">villages</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> And the fifth lot came out for the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd033.htm#008">Asher</a> according to their families.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> And their border was <a href="../ebd/ebd173.htm#001">Helkath</a>, and Hali, and Beten, and <a href="../ebd/ebd007.htm#002">Achshaph</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> And Alammelech, and Amad, and <a href="../ebd/ebd256.htm#009">Misheal</a>; and reacheth to <a href="../ebd/ebd072.htm#006">Carmel</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd380.htm#008">westward</a>, and to Shihorlibnath;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> And turneth toward the sunrising to Bethdagon, and reacheth to <a href="../ebd/ebd389.htm#003">Zebulun</a>, and to the <a href="../ebd/ebd376.htm#008">valley</a> of Jiphthahel toward the north side of Bethemek, and <a href="../ebd/ebd270.htm#002">Neiel</a>, and goeth out to <a href="../ebd/ebd068.htm#001">Cabul</a> on the left hand,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="028">28</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd171.htm#004">Hebron</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd309.htm#006">Rehob</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd161.htm#002">Hammon</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd215.htm#006">Kanah</a>, even unto great <a href="../ebd/ebd393.htm#000">Zidon</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="029">29</a> And then the coast turneth to <a href="../ebd/ebd306.htm#005">Ramah</a>, and to the strong city <a href="../ebd/ebd374.htm#001">Tyre</a>; and the coast turneth to <a href="../ebd/ebd183.htm#005">Hosah</a>; and the outgoings thereof are at the sea from the coast to <a href="../ebd/ebd007.htm#003">Achzib</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="030">30</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd374.htm#005">Ummah</a> also, and Aphek, and <a href="../ebd/ebd309.htm#006">Rehob</a>: twenty and two cities with their <a href="../ebd/ebd377.htm#003">villages</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="031">31</a> This is the inheritance of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd033.htm#008">Asher</a> according to their families, these cities with their <a href="../ebd/ebd377.htm#003">villages</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="032">32</a> The sixth lot came out to the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd266.htm#006">Naphtali</a>, even for the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd266.htm#006">Naphtali</a> according to their families.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="033">33</a> And their coast was from <a href="../ebd/ebd172.htm#007">Heleph</a>, from <a href="../ebd/ebd017.htm#007">Allon</a> to <a href="../ebd/ebd385.htm#003">Zaanannim</a>, and Adami, <a href="../ebd/ebd270.htm#003">Nekeb</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd194.htm#001">Jabneel</a>, unto Lakum; and the outgoings thereof were at <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#004">Jordan</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="034">34</a> And then the coast turneth <a href="../ebd/ebd380.htm#008">westward</a> to Aznothtabor, and goeth out from thence to <a href="../ebd/ebd184.htm#003">Hukkok</a>, and reacheth to <a href="../ebd/ebd389.htm#003">Zebulun</a> on the south side, and reacheth to <a href="../ebd/ebd033.htm#008">Asher</a> on the west side, and to <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a> upon <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#004">Jordan</a> toward the sunrising.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="035">35</a> And the fenced cities are <a href="../ebd/ebd392.htm#008">Ziddim</a>, Zer, and <a href="../ebd/ebd160.htm#007">Hammath</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd306.htm#001">Rakkath</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd081.htm#000">Chinnereth</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="036">36</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd008.htm#000">Adamah</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd306.htm#005">Ramah</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd169.htm#006">Hazor</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="037">37</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd216.htm#006">Kedesh</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#002">Edrei</a>, and Enhazor,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="038">38</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd189.htm#004">Iron</a>, and Migdalel, <a href="../ebd/ebd182.htm#000">Horem</a>, and Bethanath, and Bethshemesh; nineteen cities with their <a href="../ebd/ebd377.htm#003">villages</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="039">39</a> This is the inheritance of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd266.htm#006">Naphtali</a> according to their families, the cities and their <a href="../ebd/ebd377.htm#003">villages</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="040">40</a> And the seventh lot came out for the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd096.htm#009">Dan</a> according to their families.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="041">41</a> And the coast of their inheritance was <a href="../ebd/ebd396.htm#002">Zorah</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd125.htm#002">Eshtaol</a>, and Irshemesh,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="042">42</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#007">Shaalabbin</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd016.htm#002">Ajalon</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#009">Jethlah</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="043">43</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd119.htm#000">Elon</a>, and Thimnathah, and <a href="../ebd/ebd114.htm#001">Ekron</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="044">44</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd119.htm#002">Eltekeh</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd147.htm#007">Gibbethon</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd038.htm#006">Baalath</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="045">45</a> And Jehud, and Beneberak, and Gathrimmon,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="046">46</a> And Mejarkon, and <a href="../ebd/ebd306.htm#002">Rakkon</a>, with the border before <a href="../ebd/ebd197.htm#004">Japho</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="047">47</a> And the coast of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd096.htm#009">Dan</a> went out too little for them: therefore the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd096.htm#009">Dan</a> went up to fight against Leshem, and took it, and smote it with the edge of the <a href="../ebd/ebd354.htm#003">sword</a>, and possessed it, and dwelt therein, and called Leshem, <a href="../ebd/ebd096.htm#009">Dan</a>, after the name of <a href="../ebd/ebd096.htm#009">Dan</a> their father.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="048">48</a> This is the inheritance of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd096.htm#009">Dan</a> according to their families, these cities with their <a href="../ebd/ebd377.htm#003">villages</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="049">49</a> When they had made an end of dividing the land for inheritance by their coasts, the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> gave an inheritance to <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#006">Joshua</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd275.htm#006">Nun</a> among them:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="050">50</a> According to the word of the LORD they gave him the city which he asked, even Timnathserah in <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#007">mount</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd123.htm#000">Ephraim</a>: and he built the city, and dwelt therein.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="051">51</a> These are the inheritances, which <a href="../ebd/ebd115.htm#000">Eleazar</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#006">Joshua</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd275.htm#006">Nun</a>, and the heads of the fathers of the tribes of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, divided for an inheritance by lot in <a href="../ebd/ebd337.htm#008">Shiloh</a> before the LORD, at the door of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>. So they made an end of dividing the country.<br>
 </p>
