 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 51</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Have <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> upon me, O God, according to thy lovingkindness: according unto the multitude of thy tender mercies <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#002">blot</a> out my transgressions.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Wash me throughly from mine iniquity, and cleanse me from my <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> For I acknowledge my transgressions: and my <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> is ever before me.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Against thee, thee only, have I sinned, and done this evil in thy sight: that thou mightest be justified when thou speakest, and be clear when thou judgest.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Behold, I was shapen in iniquity; and in <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> did my mother conceive me.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Behold, thou desirest truth in the inward parts: and in the hidden part thou shalt make me to know wisdom.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Purge me with <a href="../ebd/ebd185.htm#007">hyssop</a>, and I shall be <a href="../ebd/ebd084.htm#005">clean</a>: wash me, and I shall be whiter than <a href="../ebd/ebd347.htm#000">snow</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> Make me to hear joy and gladness; that the bones which thou hast broken may rejoice.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> Hide thy face from my sins, and <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#002">blot</a> out all mine iniquities.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> Create in me a <a href="../ebd/ebd084.htm#005">clean</a> heart, O God; and renew a right <a href="../ebd/ebd349.htm#009">spirit</a> within me.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> Cast me not away from thy presence; and take not thy holy <a href="../ebd/ebd349.htm#009">spirit</a> from me.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> Restore unto me the joy of thy <a href="../ebd/ebd320.htm#003">salvation</a>; and uphold me with thy free <a href="../ebd/ebd349.htm#009">spirit</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> Then will I teach transgressors thy ways; and sinners shall be converted unto thee.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> Deliver me from bloodguiltiness, O God, thou God of my <a href="../ebd/ebd320.htm#003">salvation</a>: and my tongue shall sing aloud of thy <a href="../ebd/ebd313.htm#004">righteousness</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> O Lord, open thou my lips; and my mouth shall shew forth thy praise.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> For thou desirest not <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#002">sacrifice</a>; else would I give it: thou delightest not in burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> The sacrifices of God are a broken <a href="../ebd/ebd349.htm#009">spirit</a>: a broken and a contrite heart, O God, thou wilt not despise.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> Do good in thy good pleasure unto <a href="../ebd/ebd394.htm#002">Zion</a>: build thou the walls of <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> Then shalt thou be pleased with the sacrifices of <a href="../ebd/ebd313.htm#004">righteousness</a>, with burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> and whole burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>: then shall they offer bullocks upon thine <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>.<br>
 </p>
