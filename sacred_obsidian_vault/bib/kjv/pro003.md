 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Proverbs Chapter 3</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> My son, forget not my law; but let thine heart keep my commandments:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> For length of days, and long life, and peace, shall they add to thee.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Let not <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> and truth forsake thee: bind them about thy <a href="../ebd/ebd269.htm#000">neck</a>; write them upon the table of thine heart:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> So shalt thou find favour and good understanding in the sight of God and man.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Trust in the LORD with all thine heart; and lean not unto thine own understanding.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> In all thy ways acknowledge him, and he shall direct thy paths.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Be not <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#004">wise</a> in thine own eyes: fear the LORD, and depart from evil.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> It shall be health to thy navel, and marrow to thy bones.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> Honour the LORD with thy substance, and with the firstfruits of all thine increase:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> So shall thy barns be filled with plenty, and thy presses shall burst out with new <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#000">wine</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> My son, despise not the chastening of the LORD; neither be weary of his correction:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> For whom the LORD loveth he correcteth; even as a father the son in whom he delighteth.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> Happy is the man that findeth wisdom, and the man that getteth understanding.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> For the merchandise of it is better than the merchandise of <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#000">silver</a>, and the gain thereof than fine <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> She is more precious than rubies: and all the things thou canst desire are not to be compared unto her.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> Length of days is in her right hand; and in her left hand riches and honour.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> Her ways are ways of pleasantness, and all her paths are peace.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> She is a tree of life to them that lay hold upon her: and happy is every one that retaineth her.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> The LORD by wisdom hath founded the earth; by understanding hath he established the heavens.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> By his knowledge the depths are broken up, and the clouds drop down the <a href="../ebd/ebd102.htm#008">dew</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> My son, let not them depart from thine eyes: keep sound wisdom and discretion:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> So shall they be life unto thy soul, and <a href="../ebd/ebd154.htm#000">grace</a> to thy <a href="../ebd/ebd269.htm#000">neck</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> Then shalt thou walk in thy way safely, and thy foot shall not stumble.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> When thou liest down, thou shalt not be afraid: yea, thou shalt lie down, and thy sleep shall be sweet.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> Be not afraid of sudden fear, neither of the desolation of the wicked, when it cometh.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> For the LORD shall be thy confidence, and shall keep thy foot from being taken.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> Withhold not good from them to whom it is due, when it is in the power of thine hand to do it.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="028">28</a> Say not unto thy neighbour, Go, and come again, and to morrow I will give; when thou hast it by thee.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="029">29</a> Devise not evil against thy neighbour, seeing he dwelleth securely by thee.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="030">30</a> Strive not with a man without cause, if he have done thee no harm.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="031">31</a> Envy thou not the oppressor, and choose none of his ways.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="032">32</a> For the froward is <a href="../ebd/ebd005.htm#003">abomination</a> to the LORD: but his secret is with the righteous.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="033">33</a> The <a href="../ebd/ebd094.htm#003">curse</a> of the LORD is in the house of the wicked: but he blesseth the <a href="../ebd/ebd156.htm#007">habitation</a> of the just.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="034">34</a> Surely he scorneth the scorners: but he giveth <a href="../ebd/ebd154.htm#000">grace</a> unto the lowly.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="035">35</a> The <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#004">wise</a> shall inherit <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a>: but shame shall be the promotion of fools.<br>
 </p>
