 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Genesis Chapter 5</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> This is the book of the generations of <a href="../ebd/ebd007.htm#007">Adam</a>. In the day that God created man, in the likeness of God made he him;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Male and female created he them; and blessed them, and called their name <a href="../ebd/ebd007.htm#007">Adam</a>, in the day when they were created.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd007.htm#007">Adam</a> lived an hundred and thirty years, and begat a son in his own likeness, and after his image; and called his name <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#003">Seth</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And the days of <a href="../ebd/ebd007.htm#007">Adam</a> after he had begotten <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#003">Seth</a> were eight hundred years: and he begat sons and daughters:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And all the days that <a href="../ebd/ebd007.htm#007">Adam</a> lived were nine hundred and thirty years: and he died.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#003">Seth</a> lived an hundred and five years, and begat <a href="../ebd/ebd121.htm#005">Enos</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#003">Seth</a> lived after he begat <a href="../ebd/ebd121.htm#005">Enos</a> eight hundred and <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> years, and begat sons and daughters:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And all the days of <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#003">Seth</a> were nine hundred and twelve years: and he died.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd121.htm#005">Enos</a> lived ninety years, and begat <a href="../ebd/ebd068.htm#008">Cainan</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd121.htm#005">Enos</a> lived after he begat <a href="../ebd/ebd068.htm#008">Cainan</a> eight hundred and fifteen years, and begat sons and daughters:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And all the days of <a href="../ebd/ebd121.htm#005">Enos</a> were nine hundred and five years: and he died.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd068.htm#008">Cainan</a> lived seventy years and begat <a href="../ebd/ebd237.htm#001">Mahalaleel</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd068.htm#008">Cainan</a> lived after he begat <a href="../ebd/ebd237.htm#001">Mahalaleel</a> eight hundred and forty years, and begat sons and daughters:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> And all the days of <a href="../ebd/ebd068.htm#008">Cainan</a> were nine hundred and ten years: and he died.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd237.htm#001">Mahalaleel</a> lived sixty and five years, and begat <a href="../ebd/ebd197.htm#005">Jared</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd237.htm#001">Mahalaleel</a> lived after he begat <a href="../ebd/ebd197.htm#005">Jared</a> eight hundred and thirty years, and begat sons and daughters:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> And all the days of <a href="../ebd/ebd237.htm#001">Mahalaleel</a> were eight hundred ninety and five years: and he died.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd197.htm#005">Jared</a> lived an hundred sixty and two years, and he begat <a href="../ebd/ebd121.htm#004">Enoch</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd197.htm#005">Jared</a> lived after he begat <a href="../ebd/ebd121.htm#004">Enoch</a> eight hundred years, and begat sons and daughters:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> And all the days of <a href="../ebd/ebd197.htm#005">Jared</a> were nine hundred sixty and two years: and he died.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd121.htm#004">Enoch</a> lived sixty and five years, and begat <a href="../ebd/ebd251.htm#009">Methuselah</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd121.htm#004">Enoch</a> walked with God after he begat <a href="../ebd/ebd251.htm#009">Methuselah</a> three hundred years, and begat sons and daughters:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> And all the days of <a href="../ebd/ebd121.htm#004">Enoch</a> were three hundred sixty and five years:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd121.htm#004">Enoch</a> walked with God: and he was not; for God took him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd251.htm#009">Methuselah</a> lived an hundred eighty and <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> years, and begat <a href="../ebd/ebd223.htm#004">Lamech</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd251.htm#009">Methuselah</a> lived after he begat <a href="../ebd/ebd223.htm#004">Lamech</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> hundred eighty and two years, and begat sons and daughters:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> And all the days of <a href="../ebd/ebd251.htm#009">Methuselah</a> were nine hundred sixty and nine years: and he died.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="028">28</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd223.htm#004">Lamech</a> lived an hundred eighty and two years, and begat a son:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="029">29</a> And he called his name <a href="../ebd/ebd274.htm#002">Noah</a>, saying, This same shall comfort us concerning our work and toil of our hands, because of the ground which the LORD hath cursed.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="030">30</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd223.htm#004">Lamech</a> lived after he begat <a href="../ebd/ebd274.htm#002">Noah</a> five hundred ninety and five years, and begat sons and daughters:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="031">31</a> And all the days of <a href="../ebd/ebd223.htm#004">Lamech</a> were <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> hundred seventy and <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> years: and he died.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="032">32</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd274.htm#002">Noah</a> was five hundred years old: and <a href="../ebd/ebd274.htm#002">Noah</a> begat <a href="../ebd/ebd334.htm#002">Shem</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd160.htm#003">Ham</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd197.htm#002">Japheth</a>.<br>
 </p>
