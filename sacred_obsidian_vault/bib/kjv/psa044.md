 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 44</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> We have heard with our ears, O God, our fathers have told us, what work thou didst in their days, in the times of old.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> How thou didst drive out the <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#005">heathen</a> with thy hand, and plantedst them; how thou didst afflict the people, and cast them out.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> For they got not the land in possession by their own <a href="../ebd/ebd354.htm#003">sword</a>, neither did their own arm save them: but thy right hand, and thine arm, and the light of thy countenance, because thou hadst a favour unto them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Thou art my <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">King</a>, O God: command deliverances for <a href="../ebd/ebd194.htm#007">Jacob</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Through thee will we push down our enemies: through thy name will we tread them under that rise up against us.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> For I will not trust in my <a href="../ebd/ebd063.htm#003">bow</a>, neither shall my <a href="../ebd/ebd354.htm#003">sword</a> save me.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> But thou hast saved us from our enemies, and hast put them to shame that hated us.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> In God we boast all the day long, and praise thy name for ever. <a href="../ebd/ebd326.htm#008">Selah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> But thou hast cast off, and put us to shame; and goest not forth with our armies.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> Thou makest us to turn back from the enemy: and they which hate us spoil for themselves.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> Thou hast given us like <a href="../ebd/ebd333.htm#005">sheep</a> appointed for meat; and hast scattered us among the <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#005">heathen</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> Thou sellest thy people for nought, and dost not increase thy wealth by their price.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> Thou makest us a reproach to our neighbours, a scorn and a derision to them that are round about us.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> Thou makest us a byword among the <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#005">heathen</a>, a shaking of the head among the people.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> My confusion is continually before me, and the shame of my face hath covered me,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> For the voice of him that reproacheth and blasphemeth; by reason of the enemy and avenger.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> All this is come upon us; yet have we not forgotten thee, neither have we dealt falsely in thy <a href="../ebd/ebd091.htm#008">covenant</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> Our heart is not turned back, neither have our steps declined from thy way;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> Though thou hast sore broken us in the place of dragons, and covered us with the <a href="../ebd/ebd330.htm#002">shadow</a> of death.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> If we have forgotten the name of our God, or stretched out our hands to a strange god;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> Shall not God search this out? for he knoweth the secrets of the heart.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> Yea, for thy sake are we killed all the day long; we are counted as <a href="../ebd/ebd333.htm#005">sheep</a> for the slaughter.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> Awake, why sleepest thou, O Lord? arise, cast us not off for ever.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> Wherefore hidest thou thy face, and forgettest our affliction and our oppression?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> For our soul is bowed down to the <a href="../ebd/ebd109.htm#008">dust</a>: our <a href="../ebd/ebd052.htm#000">belly</a> cleaveth unto the earth.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> Arise for our help, and redeem us for thy mercies' sake.<br>
 </p>
