 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 101</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> I will sing of <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> and judgment: unto thee, O LORD, will I sing.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> I will behave myself wisely in a perfect way. O when wilt thou come unto me? I will walk within my house with a perfect heart.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> I will set no wicked thing before mine eyes: I hate the work of them that turn aside; it shall not cleave to me.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> A froward heart shall depart from me: I will not know a wicked person.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Whoso privily slandereth his neighbour, him will I cut off: him that hath an high look and a proud heart will not I suffer.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Mine eyes shall be upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd130.htm#005">faithful</a> of the land, that they may dwell with me: he that walketh in a perfect way, he shall serve me.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> He that worketh deceit shall not dwell within my house: he that telleth lies shall not tarry in my sight.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> I will early destroy all the wicked of the land; that I may cut off all wicked doers from the city of the LORD.<br>
 </p>
