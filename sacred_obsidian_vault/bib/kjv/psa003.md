 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 3</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Lord, how are they increased that trouble me! many are they that rise up against me.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Many there be which say of my soul, There is no help for him in God. <a href="../ebd/ebd326.htm#008">Selah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> But thou, O LORD, art a <a href="../ebd/ebd337.htm#001">shield</a> for me; my <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a>, and the lifter up of mine head.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> I cried unto the LORD with my voice, and he heard me out of his holy hill. <a href="../ebd/ebd326.htm#008">Selah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> I laid me down and slept; I awaked; for the LORD sustained me.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> I will not be afraid of ten <a href="../ebd/ebd364.htm#008">thousands</a> of people, that have set themselves against me round about.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Arise, O LORD; save me, O my God: for thou hast smitten all mine enemies upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd078.htm#004">cheek</a> bone; thou hast broken the teeth of the ungodly.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd320.htm#003">Salvation</a> belongeth unto the LORD: thy blessing is upon thy people. <a href="../ebd/ebd326.htm#008">Selah</a>.<br>
 </p>
