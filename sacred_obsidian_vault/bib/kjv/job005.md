 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Job Chapter 5</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Call now, if there be any that will answer thee; and to which of the saints wilt thou turn?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> For wrath killeth the foolish man, and envy slayeth the silly one.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> I have seen the foolish taking root: but suddenly I cursed his <a href="../ebd/ebd156.htm#007">habitation</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> His children are far from safety, and they are crushed in the <a href="../ebd/ebd143.htm#008">gate</a>, neither is there any to deliver them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Whose <a href="../ebd/ebd165.htm#008">harvest</a> the hungry eateth up, and taketh it even out of the thorns, and the robber swalloweth up their substance.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Although affliction cometh not forth of the <a href="../ebd/ebd109.htm#008">dust</a>, neither doth trouble spring out of the ground;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Yet man is born unto trouble, as the sparks <a href="../ebd/ebd136.htm#004">fly</a> upward.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> I would seek unto God, and unto God would I commit my cause:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> Which doeth great things and unsearchable; marvellous things without number:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> Who giveth <a href="../ebd/ebd305.htm#008">rain</a> upon the earth, and sendeth waters upon the fields:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> To set up on high those that be low; that those which <a href="../ebd/ebd261.htm#004">mourn</a> may be exalted to safety.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> He disappointeth the devices of the crafty, so that their hands cannot perform their enterprise.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> He taketh the <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#004">wise</a> in their own craftiness: and the counsel of the froward is carried headlong.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> They meet with <a href="../ebd/ebd097.htm#008">darkness</a> in the day time, and grope in the noonday as in the night.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> But he saveth the <a href="../ebd/ebd298.htm#006">poor</a> from the <a href="../ebd/ebd354.htm#003">sword</a>, from their mouth, and from the hand of the mighty.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> So the <a href="../ebd/ebd298.htm#006">poor</a> hath hope, and iniquity stoppeth her mouth.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> Behold, happy is the man whom God correcteth: therefore despise not thou the chastening of the Almighty:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> For he maketh sore, and bindeth up: he woundeth, and his hands make whole.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> He shall deliver thee in six troubles: yea, in <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> there shall no evil touch thee.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> In <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#000">famine</a> he shall redeem thee from death: and in war from the power of the <a href="../ebd/ebd354.htm#003">sword</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> Thou shalt be hid from the scourge of the tongue: neither shalt thou be afraid of <a href="../ebd/ebd102.htm#004">destruction</a> when it cometh.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> At <a href="../ebd/ebd102.htm#004">destruction</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#000">famine</a> thou shalt laugh: neither shalt thou be afraid of the beasts of the earth.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> For thou shalt be in <a href="../ebd/ebd225.htm#005">league</a> with the stones of the field: and the beasts of the field shall be at peace with thee.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> And thou shalt know that thy <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> shall be in peace; and thou shalt visit thy <a href="../ebd/ebd156.htm#007">habitation</a>, and shalt not <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> Thou shalt know also that thy seed shall be great, and thine offspring as the <a href="../ebd/ebd154.htm#005">grass</a> of the earth.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> Thou shalt come to thy <a href="../ebd/ebd154.htm#008">grave</a> in a full age, like as a shock of <a href="../ebd/ebd090.htm#006">corn</a> cometh in in his season.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> Lo this, we have searched it, so it is; hear it, and know thou it for thy good.<br>
 </p>
