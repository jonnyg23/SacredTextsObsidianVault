 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Exodus Chapter 40</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> And the LORD spake unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> On the first day of the first month shalt thou set up the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd361.htm#009">tent</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And thou shalt put therein the <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#000">ark</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd362.htm#008">testimony</a>, and cover the <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#000">ark</a> with the vail.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And thou shalt bring in the table, and set in order the things that are to be set in order upon it; and thou shalt bring in the <a href="../ebd/ebd071.htm#001">candlestick</a>, and light the lamps thereof.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And thou shalt set the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a> for the <a href="../ebd/ebd188.htm#002">incense</a> before the <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#000">ark</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd362.htm#008">testimony</a>, and put the <a href="../ebd/ebd163.htm#001">hanging</a> of the door to the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And thou shalt set the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> of the burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> before the door of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd361.htm#009">tent</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And thou shalt set the <a href="../ebd/ebd224.htm#009">laver</a> between the <a href="../ebd/ebd361.htm#009">tent</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> and the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>, and shalt put water therein.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And thou shalt set up the court round about, and hang up the <a href="../ebd/ebd163.htm#001">hanging</a> at the court <a href="../ebd/ebd143.htm#008">gate</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And thou shalt take the anointing oil, and <a href="../ebd/ebd024.htm#008">anoint</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a>, and all that is therein, and shalt <a href="../ebd/ebd160.htm#001">hallow</a> it, and all the vessels thereof: and it shall be holy.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And thou shalt <a href="../ebd/ebd024.htm#008">anoint</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> of the burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, and all his vessels, and sanctify the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>: and it shall be an <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> most holy.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And thou shalt <a href="../ebd/ebd024.htm#008">anoint</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd224.htm#009">laver</a> and his foot, and sanctify it.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And thou shalt bring <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> and his sons unto the door of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>, and wash them with water.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> And thou shalt put upon <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> the holy garments, and <a href="../ebd/ebd024.htm#008">anoint</a> him, and sanctify him; that he may <a href="../ebd/ebd255.htm#007">minister</a> unto me in the priest's office.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> And thou shalt bring his sons, and clothe them with coats:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And thou shalt <a href="../ebd/ebd024.htm#008">anoint</a> them, as thou didst <a href="../ebd/ebd024.htm#008">anoint</a> their father, that they may <a href="../ebd/ebd255.htm#007">minister</a> unto me in the priest's office: for their anointing shall surely be an <a href="../ebd/ebd127.htm#007">everlasting</a> priesthood throughout their generations.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> Thus did <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>: according to all that the LORD commanded him, so did he.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> And it came to pass in the first month in the second year, on the first day of the month, that the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> was reared up.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> reared up the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a>, and fastened his sockets, and set up the boards thereof, and put in the bars thereof, and reared up his pillars.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> And he spread abroad the <a href="../ebd/ebd361.htm#009">tent</a> over the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a>, and put the covering of the <a href="../ebd/ebd361.htm#009">tent</a> above upon it; as the LORD commanded <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> And he took and put the <a href="../ebd/ebd362.htm#008">testimony</a> into the <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#000">ark</a>, and set the staves on the <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#000">ark</a>, and put the <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> seat above upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#000">ark</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> And he brought the <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#000">ark</a> into the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a>, and set up the vail of the covering, and covered the <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#000">ark</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd362.htm#008">testimony</a>; as the LORD commanded <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> And he put the table in the <a href="../ebd/ebd361.htm#009">tent</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>, upon the side of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd275.htm#002">northward</a>, without the vail.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> And he set the <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#006">bread</a> in order upon it before the LORD; as the LORD had commanded <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> And he put the <a href="../ebd/ebd071.htm#001">candlestick</a> in the <a href="../ebd/ebd361.htm#009">tent</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>, over against the table, on the side of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> southward.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> And he lighted the lamps before the LORD; as the LORD commanded <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> And he put the golden <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> in the <a href="../ebd/ebd361.htm#009">tent</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> before the vail:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> And he burnt sweet <a href="../ebd/ebd188.htm#002">incense</a> thereon; as the LORD commanded <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="028">28</a> And he set up the <a href="../ebd/ebd163.htm#001">hanging</a> at the door of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="029">29</a> And he put the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> of burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> by the door of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd361.htm#009">tent</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>, and offered upon it the burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> and the meat <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>; as the LORD commanded <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="030">30</a> And he set the <a href="../ebd/ebd224.htm#009">laver</a> between the <a href="../ebd/ebd361.htm#009">tent</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> and the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>, and put water there, to wash withal.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="031">31</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> and his sons washed their hands and their feet thereat:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="032">32</a> When they went into the <a href="../ebd/ebd361.htm#009">tent</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>, and when they came near unto the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>, they washed; as the LORD commanded <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="033">33</a> And he reared up the court round about the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> and the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>, and set up the <a href="../ebd/ebd163.htm#001">hanging</a> of the court <a href="../ebd/ebd143.htm#008">gate</a>. So <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> finished the work.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="034">34</a> Then a <a href="../ebd/ebd085.htm#001">cloud</a> covered the <a href="../ebd/ebd361.htm#009">tent</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a> of the LORD filled the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="035">35</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> was not able to enter into the <a href="../ebd/ebd361.htm#009">tent</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>, because the <a href="../ebd/ebd085.htm#001">cloud</a> abode thereon, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a> of the LORD filled the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="036">36</a> And when the <a href="../ebd/ebd085.htm#001">cloud</a> was taken up from over the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a>, the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> went onward in all their journeys:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="037">37</a> But if the <a href="../ebd/ebd085.htm#001">cloud</a> were not taken up, then they journeyed not till the day that it was taken up.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="038">38</a> For the <a href="../ebd/ebd085.htm#001">cloud</a> of the LORD was upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> by day, and fire was on it by night, in the sight of all the house of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, throughout all their journeys.<br>
 </p>
