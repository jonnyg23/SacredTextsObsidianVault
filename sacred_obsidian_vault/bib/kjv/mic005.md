 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Micah Chapter 5</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Now gather thyself in troops, O daughter of troops: he hath laid siege against us: they shall smite the <a href="../ebd/ebd213.htm#005">judge</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> with a rod upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd078.htm#004">cheek</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> But thou, <a href="../ebd/ebd056.htm#005">Bethlehem</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd123.htm#006">Ephratah</a>, though thou be little among the <a href="../ebd/ebd364.htm#008">thousands</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>, yet out of thee shall he come forth unto me that is to be ruler in <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>; whose goings forth have been from of old, from <a href="../ebd/ebd127.htm#007">everlasting</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Therefore will he give them up, until the time that she which travaileth hath brought forth: then the remnant of his brethren shall return unto the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And he shall stand and feed in the strength of the LORD, in the majesty of the name of the LORD his God; and they shall abide: for now shall he be great unto the ends of the earth.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And this man shall be the peace, when the Assyrian shall come into our land: and when he shall tread in our palaces, then shall we raise against him <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> shepherds, and eight principal men.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And they shall waste the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd035.htm#005">Assyria</a> with the <a href="../ebd/ebd354.htm#003">sword</a>, and the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd273.htm#004">Nimrod</a> in the entrances thereof: thus shall he deliver us from the Assyrian, when he cometh into our land, and when he treadeth within our borders.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And the remnant of <a href="../ebd/ebd194.htm#007">Jacob</a> shall be in the midst of many people as a <a href="../ebd/ebd102.htm#008">dew</a> from the LORD, as the showers upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd154.htm#005">grass</a>, that tarrieth not for man, nor waiteth for the sons of men.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And the remnant of <a href="../ebd/ebd194.htm#007">Jacob</a> shall be among the <a href="../ebd/ebd146.htm#001">Gentiles</a> in the midst of many people as a lion among the beasts of the <a href="../ebd/ebd137.htm#006">forest</a>, as a young lion among the flocks of <a href="../ebd/ebd333.htm#005">sheep</a>: who, if he go through, both treadeth down, and teareth in <a href="../ebd/ebd295.htm#002">pieces</a>, and none can deliver.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> Thine hand shall be lifted up upon thine adversaries, and all thine enemies shall be cut off.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And it shall come to pass in that day, saith the LORD, that I will cut off thy horses out of the midst of thee, and I will destroy thy chariots:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And I will cut off the cities of thy land, and throw down all thy strong holds:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And I will cut off witchcrafts out of thine hand; and thou shalt have no more soothsayers:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> Thy graven images also will I cut off, and thy standing images out of the midst of thee; and thou shalt no more <a href="../ebd/ebd384.htm#002">worship</a> the work of thine hands.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> And I will pluck up thy groves out of the midst of thee: so will I destroy thy cities.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And I will execute vengeance in <a href="../ebd/ebd024.htm#002">anger</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd140.htm#002">fury</a> upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#005">heathen</a>, such as they have not heard.<br>
 </p>
