 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 50</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> The mighty God, even the LORD, hath spoken, and called the earth from the rising of the <a href="../ebd/ebd353.htm#001">sun</a> unto the going down thereof.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Out of <a href="../ebd/ebd394.htm#002">Zion</a>, the <a href="../ebd/ebd290.htm#001">perfection</a> of beauty, God hath shined.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Our God shall come, and shall not keep silence: a fire shall devour before him, and it shall be very tempestuous round about him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> He shall call to the heavens from above, and to the earth, that he may <a href="../ebd/ebd213.htm#005">judge</a> his people.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Gather my saints together unto me; those that have made a <a href="../ebd/ebd091.htm#008">covenant</a> with me by <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#002">sacrifice</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And the heavens shall declare his <a href="../ebd/ebd313.htm#004">righteousness</a>: for God is <a href="../ebd/ebd213.htm#005">judge</a> himself. <a href="../ebd/ebd326.htm#008">Selah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Hear, O my people, and I will speak; O <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, and I will testify against thee: I am God, even thy God.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> I will not reprove thee for thy sacrifices or thy burnt offerings, to have been continually before me.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> I will take no <a href="../ebd/ebd066.htm#003">bullock</a> out of thy house, nor he goats out of thy folds.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> For every <a href="../ebd/ebd049.htm#001">beast</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd137.htm#006">forest</a> is mine, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd074.htm#002">cattle</a> upon a thousand hills.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> I know all the fowls of the mountains: and the wild beasts of the field are mine.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> If I were hungry, I would not tell thee: for the world is mine, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd139.htm#007">fulness</a> thereof.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> Will I eat the <a href="../ebd/ebd135.htm#007">flesh</a> of bulls, or <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> of goats?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> Offer unto God thanksgiving; and pay thy <a href="../ebd/ebd378.htm#001">vows</a> unto the most High:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And call upon me in the day of trouble: I will deliver thee, and thou shalt <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#005">glorify</a> me.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> But unto the wicked God saith, What hast thou to do to declare my statutes, or that thou shouldest take my <a href="../ebd/ebd091.htm#008">covenant</a> in thy mouth?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> Seeing thou hatest instruction, and casteth my words behind thee.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> When thou sawest a thief, then thou consentedst with him, and hast been partaker with adulterers.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> Thou givest thy mouth to evil, and thy tongue frameth deceit.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> Thou sittest and speakest against thy brother; thou slanderest thine own mother's son.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> These things hast thou done, and I kept silence; thou thoughtest that I was altogether such an one as thyself: but I will reprove thee, and set them in order before thine eyes.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> Now consider this, ye that forget God, lest I tear you in <a href="../ebd/ebd295.htm#002">pieces</a>, and there be none to deliver.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> Whoso offereth praise glorifieth me: and to him that ordereth his <a href="../ebd/ebd089.htm#001">conversation</a> aright will I shew the <a href="../ebd/ebd320.htm#003">salvation</a> of God.<br>
 </p>
