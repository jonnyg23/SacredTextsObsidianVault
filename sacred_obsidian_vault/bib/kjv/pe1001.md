 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: 1 Peter Chapter 1</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd291.htm#002">Peter</a>, an <a href="../ebd/ebd026.htm#008">apostle</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a>, to the strangers scattered throughout <a href="../ebd/ebd298.htm#003">Pontus</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd141.htm#004">Galatia</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd071.htm#009">Cappadocia</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd034.htm#008">Asia</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd059.htm#008">Bithynia</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Elect according to the foreknowledge of God the Father, through <a href="../ebd/ebd321.htm#004">sanctification</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd349.htm#009">Spirit</a>, unto obedience and sprinkling of the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a>: <a href="../ebd/ebd154.htm#000">Grace</a> unto you, and peace, be multiplied.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Blessed be the God and Father of our Lord <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a>, which according to his abundant <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> hath begotten us again unto a lively hope by the resurrection of <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a> from the dead,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> To an inheritance incorruptible, and undefiled, and that fadeth not away, reserved in <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#006">heaven</a> for you,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Who are kept by the power of God through faith unto <a href="../ebd/ebd320.htm#003">salvation</a> ready to be revealed in the last time.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Wherein ye greatly rejoice, though now for a season, if need be, ye are in heaviness through manifold temptations:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> That the trial of your faith, being much more precious than of <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a> that perisheth, though it be tried with fire, might be found unto praise and honour and <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a> at the appearing of <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> Whom having not seen, ye love; in whom, though now ye see him not, yet believing, ye rejoice with joy unspeakable and full of <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> Receiving the end of your faith, even the <a href="../ebd/ebd320.htm#003">salvation</a> of your souls.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> Of which <a href="../ebd/ebd320.htm#003">salvation</a> the prophets have enquired and searched diligently, who prophesied of the <a href="../ebd/ebd154.htm#000">grace</a> that should come unto you:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> Searching what, or what manner of time the <a href="../ebd/ebd349.htm#009">Spirit</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a> which was in them did signify, when it testified beforehand the sufferings of <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a> that should follow.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> Unto whom it was revealed, that not unto themselves, but unto us they did <a href="../ebd/ebd255.htm#007">minister</a> the things, which are now reported unto you by them that have preached the <a href="../ebd/ebd153.htm#003">gospel</a> unto you with the Holy <a href="../ebd/ebd147.htm#005">Ghost</a> sent down from <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#006">heaven</a>; which things the angels desire to look into.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> Wherefore gird up the loins of your mind, be sober, and hope to the end for the <a href="../ebd/ebd154.htm#000">grace</a> that is to be brought unto you at the <a href="../ebd/ebd312.htm#002">revelation</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> As obedient children, not fashioning yourselves according to the former lusts in your ignorance:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> But as he which hath called you is holy, so be ye holy in all manner of <a href="../ebd/ebd089.htm#001">conversation</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> Because it is written, Be ye holy; for I am holy.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> And if ye call on the Father, who without respect of persons judgeth according to every man's work, pass the time of your sojourning here in fear:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> Forasmuch as ye know that ye were not redeemed with corruptible things, as <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#000">silver</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a>, from your vain <a href="../ebd/ebd089.htm#001">conversation</a> received by <a href="../ebd/ebd371.htm#005">tradition</a> from your fathers;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> But with the precious <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a>, as of a <a href="../ebd/ebd223.htm#003">lamb</a> without <a href="../ebd/ebd060.htm#007">blemish</a> and without spot:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> Who verily was foreordained before the foundation of the world, but was manifest in these last times for you,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> Who by him do believe in God, that raised him up from the dead, and gave him <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a>; that your faith and hope might be in God.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> Seeing ye have purified your souls in obeying the truth through the <a href="../ebd/ebd349.htm#009">Spirit</a> unto unfeigned love of the brethren, see that ye love one another with a pure heart fervently:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> Being born again, not of corruptible seed, but of incorruptible, by the word of God, which liveth and abideth for ever.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> For all <a href="../ebd/ebd135.htm#007">flesh</a> is as <a href="../ebd/ebd154.htm#005">grass</a>, and all the <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a> of man as the flower of <a href="../ebd/ebd154.htm#005">grass</a>. The <a href="../ebd/ebd154.htm#005">grass</a> withereth, and the flower thereof falleth away:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> But the word of the Lord endureth for ever. And this is the word which by the <a href="../ebd/ebd153.htm#003">gospel</a> is preached unto you.<br>
 </p>
