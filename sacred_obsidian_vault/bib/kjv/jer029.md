 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Jeremiah Chapter 29</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Now these are the words of the letter that <a href="../ebd/ebd203.htm#006">Jeremiah</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#008">prophet</a> sent from <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a> unto the residue of the elders which were carried away captives, and to the priests, and to the prophets, and to all the people whom <a href="../ebd/ebd268.htm#005">Nebuchadnezzar</a> had carried away <a href="../ebd/ebd072.htm#001">captive</a> from <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a> to <a href="../ebd/ebd041.htm#001">Babylon</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> (After that Jeconiah the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd303.htm#007">queen</a>, and the eunuchs, the princes of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>, and the carpenters, and the smiths, were departed from <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>;)<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> By the hand of <a href="../ebd/ebd114.htm#004">Elasah</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd331.htm#004">Shaphan</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd145.htm#007">Gemariah</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd178.htm#006">Hilkiah</a>, (whom <a href="../ebd/ebd389.htm#008">Zedekiah</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a> sent unto <a href="../ebd/ebd041.htm#001">Babylon</a> to <a href="../ebd/ebd268.htm#005">Nebuchadnezzar</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd041.htm#001">Babylon</a>) saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Thus saith the LORD of hosts, the God of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, unto all that are carried away captives, whom I have caused to be carried away from <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a> unto <a href="../ebd/ebd041.htm#001">Babylon</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Build ye houses, and dwell in them; and plant <a href="../ebd/ebd143.htm#001">gardens</a>, and eat the <a href="../ebd/ebd139.htm#000">fruit</a> of them;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Take ye wives, and beget sons and daughters; and take wives for your sons, and give your daughters to husbands, that they may bear sons and daughters; that ye may be increased there, and not diminished.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And seek the peace of the city whither I have caused you to be carried away captives, and pray unto the LORD for it: for in the peace thereof shall ye have peace.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> For thus saith the LORD of hosts, the God of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>; Let not your prophets and your diviners, that be in the midst of you, deceive you, neither hearken to your dreams which ye cause to be dreamed.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> For they prophesy falsely unto you in my name: I have not sent them, saith the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> For thus saith the LORD, That after seventy years be accomplished at <a href="../ebd/ebd041.htm#001">Babylon</a> I will visit you, and perform my good word toward you, in causing you to return to this place.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> For I know the thoughts that I think toward you, saith the LORD, thoughts of peace, and not of evil, to give you an expected end.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> Then shall ye call upon me, and ye shall go and pray unto me, and I will hearken unto you.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> And ye shall seek me, and find me, when ye shall search for me with all your heart.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> And I will be found of you, saith the LORD: and I will turn away your <a href="../ebd/ebd072.htm#002">captivity</a>, and I will gather you from all the nations, and from all the places whither I have driven you, saith the LORD; and I will bring you again into the place whence I caused you to be carried away <a href="../ebd/ebd072.htm#001">captive</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> Because ye have said, The LORD hath raised us up prophets in <a href="../ebd/ebd041.htm#001">Babylon</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> Know that thus saith the LORD of the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> that sitteth upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd365.htm#001">throne</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a>, and of all the people that dwelleth in this city, and of your brethren that are not gone forth with you into <a href="../ebd/ebd072.htm#002">captivity</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> Thus saith the LORD of hosts; Behold, I will send upon them the <a href="../ebd/ebd354.htm#003">sword</a>, the <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#000">famine</a>, and the pestilence, and will make them like vile figs, that cannot be eaten, they are so evil.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> And I will persecute them with the <a href="../ebd/ebd354.htm#003">sword</a>, with the <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#000">famine</a>, and with the pestilence, and will deliver them to be removed to all the kingdoms of the earth, to be a <a href="../ebd/ebd094.htm#003">curse</a>, and an astonishment, and an hissing, and a reproach, among all the nations whither I have driven them:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> Because they have not hearkened to my words, saith the LORD, which I sent unto them by my servants the prophets, rising up early and sending them; but ye would not hear, saith the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> Hear ye therefore the word of the LORD, all ye of the <a href="../ebd/ebd072.htm#002">captivity</a>, whom I have sent from <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a> to <a href="../ebd/ebd041.htm#001">Babylon</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> Thus saith the LORD of hosts, the God of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, of <a href="../ebd/ebd013.htm#002">Ahab</a> the son of Kolaiah, and of <a href="../ebd/ebd389.htm#008">Zedekiah</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd234.htm#007">Maaseiah</a>, which prophesy a lie unto you in my name; Behold, I will deliver them into the hand of <a href="../ebd/ebd268.htm#006">Nebuchadrezzar</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd041.htm#001">Babylon</a>; and he shall slay them before your eyes;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> And of them shall be taken up a <a href="../ebd/ebd094.htm#003">curse</a> by all the <a href="../ebd/ebd072.htm#002">captivity</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a> which are in <a href="../ebd/ebd041.htm#001">Babylon</a>, saying, The LORD make thee like <a href="../ebd/ebd389.htm#008">Zedekiah</a> and like <a href="../ebd/ebd013.htm#002">Ahab</a>, whom the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd041.htm#001">Babylon</a> roasted in the fire;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> Because they have committed villany in <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, and have committed <a href="../ebd/ebd010.htm#009">adultery</a> with their neighbours' wives, and have spoken lying words in my name, which I have not commanded them; even I know, and am a <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#008">witness</a>, saith the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> Thus shalt thou also speak to <a href="../ebd/ebd334.htm#005">Shemaiah</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd269.htm#006">Nehelamite</a>, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> Thus speaketh the LORD of hosts, the God of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, saying, Because thou hast sent letters in thy name unto all the people that are at <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>, and to <a href="../ebd/ebd390.htm#008">Zephaniah</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd234.htm#007">Maaseiah</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a>, and to all the priests, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> The LORD hath made thee <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> in the stead of <a href="../ebd/ebd200.htm#009">Jehoiada</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a>, that ye should be officers in the house of the LORD, for every man that is mad, and maketh himself a <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#008">prophet</a>, that thou shouldest put him in <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#006">prison</a>, and in the stocks.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> Now therefore why hast thou not reproved <a href="../ebd/ebd203.htm#006">Jeremiah</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd023.htm#004">Anathoth</a>, which maketh himself a <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#008">prophet</a> to you?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="028">28</a> For therefore he sent unto us in <a href="../ebd/ebd041.htm#001">Babylon</a>, saying, This <a href="../ebd/ebd072.htm#002">captivity</a> is long: build ye houses, and dwell in them; and plant <a href="../ebd/ebd143.htm#001">gardens</a>, and eat the <a href="../ebd/ebd139.htm#000">fruit</a> of them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="029">29</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd390.htm#008">Zephaniah</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> read this letter in the ears of <a href="../ebd/ebd203.htm#006">Jeremiah</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#008">prophet</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="030">30</a> Then came the word of the LORD unto <a href="../ebd/ebd203.htm#006">Jeremiah</a>, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="031">31</a> Send to all them of the <a href="../ebd/ebd072.htm#002">captivity</a>, saying, Thus saith the LORD concerning <a href="../ebd/ebd334.htm#005">Shemaiah</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd269.htm#006">Nehelamite</a>; Because that <a href="../ebd/ebd334.htm#005">Shemaiah</a> hath prophesied unto you, and I sent him not, and he caused you to trust in a lie:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="032">32</a> Therefore thus saith the LORD; Behold, I will punish <a href="../ebd/ebd334.htm#005">Shemaiah</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd269.htm#006">Nehelamite</a>, and his seed: he shall not have a man to dwell among this people; neither shall he behold the good that I will do for my people, saith the LORD; because he hath taught rebellion against the LORD.<br>
 </p>
