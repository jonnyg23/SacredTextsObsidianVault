 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Joshua Chapter 3</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#006">Joshua</a> rose early in the morning; and they removed from <a href="../ebd/ebd339.htm#009">Shittim</a>, and came to <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#004">Jordan</a>, he and all the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, and lodged there before they passed over.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> And it came to pass after three days, that the officers went through the <a href="../ebd/ebd183.htm#007">host</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And they commanded the people, saying, When ye see the <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#000">ark</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd091.htm#008">covenant</a> of the LORD your God, and the priests the Levites bearing it, then ye shall remove from your place, and go after it.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Yet there shall be a space between you and it, about two thousand cubits by measure: come not near unto it, that ye may know the way by which ye must go: for ye have not passed this way heretofore.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#006">Joshua</a> said unto the people, Sanctify yourselves: for to morrow the LORD will do wonders among you.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#006">Joshua</a> spake unto the priests, saying, Take up the <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#000">ark</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd091.htm#008">covenant</a>, and pass over before the people. And they took up the <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#000">ark</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd091.htm#008">covenant</a>, and went before the people.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And the LORD said unto <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#006">Joshua</a>, This day will I begin to magnify thee in the sight of all <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, that they may know that, as I was with <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, so I will be with thee.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And thou shalt command the priests that bear the <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#000">ark</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd091.htm#008">covenant</a>, saying, When ye are come to the brink of the water of <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#004">Jordan</a>, ye shall stand still in <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#004">Jordan</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#006">Joshua</a> said unto the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, Come hither, and hear the words of the LORD your God.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#006">Joshua</a> said, Hereby ye shall know that the living God is among you, and that he will without fail drive out from before you the <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#007">Canaanites</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd179.htm#006">Hittites</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd179.htm#007">Hivites</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd290.htm#006">Perizzites</a>, and the Girgashites, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd021.htm#006">Amorites</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd199.htm#004">Jebusites</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> Behold, the <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#000">ark</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd091.htm#008">covenant</a> of the LORD of all the earth passeth over before you into <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#004">Jordan</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> Now therefore take you twelve men out of the tribes of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, out of every <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> a man.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> And it shall come to pass, as soon as the soles of the feet of the priests that bear the <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#000">ark</a> of the LORD, the LORD of all the earth, shall rest in the waters of <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#004">Jordan</a>, that the waters of <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#004">Jordan</a> shall be cut off from the waters that come down from above; and they shall stand upon an <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#000">heap</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> And it came to pass, when the people removed from their tents, to pass over <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#004">Jordan</a>, and the priests bearing the <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#000">ark</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd091.htm#008">covenant</a> before the people;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And as they that bare the <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#000">ark</a> were come unto <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#004">Jordan</a>, and the feet of the priests that bare the <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#000">ark</a> were dipped in the brim of the water, (for <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#004">Jordan</a> overfloweth all his banks all the time of <a href="../ebd/ebd165.htm#008">harvest</a>,)<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> That the waters which came down from above stood and rose up upon an <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#000">heap</a> very far from the city <a href="../ebd/ebd007.htm#007">Adam</a>, that is beside <a href="../ebd/ebd387.htm#007">Zaretan</a>: and those that came down toward the sea of the <a href="../ebd/ebd297.htm#002">plain</a>, even the <a href="../ebd/ebd319.htm#008">salt</a> sea, failed, and were cut off: and the people passed over right against <a href="../ebd/ebd203.htm#008">Jericho</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> And the priests that bare the <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#000">ark</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd091.htm#008">covenant</a> of the LORD stood firm on dry ground in the midst of <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#004">Jordan</a>, and all the Israelites passed over on dry ground, until all the people were passed <a href="../ebd/ebd084.htm#005">clean</a> over <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#004">Jordan</a>.<br>
 </p>
