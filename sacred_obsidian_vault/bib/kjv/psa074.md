 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 74</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> O God, why hast thou cast us off for ever? why doth thine <a href="../ebd/ebd024.htm#002">anger</a> smoke against the <a href="../ebd/ebd333.htm#005">sheep</a> of thy pasture?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Remember thy <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>, which thou hast purchased of old; the rod of thine inheritance, which thou hast redeemed; this <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#007">mount</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd394.htm#002">Zion</a>, wherein thou hast dwelt.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Lift up thy feet unto the perpetual desolations; even all that the enemy hath done wickedly in the <a href="../ebd/ebd321.htm#005">sanctuary</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Thine enemies roar in the midst of thy congregations; they set up their ensigns for signs.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> A man was famous according as he had lifted up axes upon the thick trees.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> But now they break down the carved work thereof at once with axes and hammers.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> They have cast fire into thy <a href="../ebd/ebd321.htm#005">sanctuary</a>, they have defiled by casting down the dwelling place of thy name to the ground.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> They said in their hearts, Let us destroy them together: they have burned up all the synagogues of God in the land.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> We see not our signs: there is no more any <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#008">prophet</a>: neither is there among us any that knoweth how long.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> O God, how long shall the <a href="../ebd/ebd011.htm#001">adversary</a> reproach? shall the enemy blaspheme thy name for ever?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> Why withdrawest thou thy hand, even thy right hand? pluck it out of thy <a href="../ebd/ebd062.htm#008">bosom</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> For God is my <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">King</a> of old, working <a href="../ebd/ebd320.htm#003">salvation</a> in the midst of the earth.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> Thou didst divide the sea by thy strength: thou brakest the heads of the dragons in the waters.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> Thou brakest the heads of <a href="../ebd/ebd227.htm#007">leviathan</a> in <a href="../ebd/ebd295.htm#002">pieces</a>, and gavest him to be meat to the people inhabiting the <a href="../ebd/ebd381.htm#005">wilderness</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> Thou didst cleave the <a href="../ebd/ebd138.htm#000">fountain</a> and the <a href="../ebd/ebd136.htm#000">flood</a>: thou driedst up mighty rivers.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> The day is thine, the night also is thine: thou hast prepared the light and the <a href="../ebd/ebd353.htm#001">sun</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> Thou hast set all the borders of the earth: thou hast made summer and winter.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> Remember this, that the enemy hath reproached, O LORD, and that the foolish people have blasphemed thy name.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> O deliver not the soul of thy turtledove unto the multitude of the wicked: forget not the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> of thy <a href="../ebd/ebd298.htm#006">poor</a> for ever.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> Have respect unto the <a href="../ebd/ebd091.htm#008">covenant</a>: for the dark places of the earth are full of the habitations of cruelty.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> O let not the oppressed return ashamed: let the <a href="../ebd/ebd298.htm#006">poor</a> and needy praise thy name.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> Arise, O God, plead thine own cause: remember how the foolish man reproacheth thee daily.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> Forget not the voice of thine enemies: the tumult of those that rise up against thee increaseth continually.<br>
 </p>
