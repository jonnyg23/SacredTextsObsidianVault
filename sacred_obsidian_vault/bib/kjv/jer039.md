 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Jeremiah Chapter 39</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> In the ninth year of <a href="../ebd/ebd389.htm#008">Zedekiah</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>, in the tenth month, came <a href="../ebd/ebd268.htm#006">Nebuchadrezzar</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd041.htm#001">Babylon</a> and all his army against <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>, and they besieged it.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> And in the eleventh year of <a href="../ebd/ebd389.htm#008">Zedekiah</a>, in the fourth month, the ninth day of the month, the city was broken up.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And all the princes of the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd041.htm#001">Babylon</a> came in, and sat in the middle <a href="../ebd/ebd143.htm#008">gate</a>, even Nergalsharezer, Samgarnebo, Sarsechim, <a href="../ebd/ebd304.htm#009">Rabsaris</a>, Nergalsharezer, <a href="../ebd/ebd304.htm#008">Rabmag</a>, with all the residue of the princes of the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd041.htm#001">Babylon</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And it came to pass, that when <a href="../ebd/ebd389.htm#008">Zedekiah</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a> saw them, and all the men of war, then they fled, and went forth out of the city by night, by the way of the king's garden, by the <a href="../ebd/ebd143.htm#008">gate</a> betwixt the two walls: and he went out the way of the <a href="../ebd/ebd297.htm#002">plain</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> But the Chaldeans' army pursued after them, and overtook <a href="../ebd/ebd389.htm#008">Zedekiah</a> in the plains of <a href="../ebd/ebd203.htm#008">Jericho</a>: and when they had taken him, they brought him up to <a href="../ebd/ebd268.htm#005">Nebuchadnezzar</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd041.htm#001">Babylon</a> to <a href="../ebd/ebd313.htm#002">Riblah</a> in the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd160.htm#005">Hamath</a>, where he gave judgment upon him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Then the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd041.htm#001">Babylon</a> slew the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd389.htm#008">Zedekiah</a> in <a href="../ebd/ebd313.htm#002">Riblah</a> before his eyes: also the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd041.htm#001">Babylon</a> slew all the nobles of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Moreover he put out Zedekiah's eyes, and bound him with chains, to carry him to <a href="../ebd/ebd041.htm#001">Babylon</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And the Chaldeans burned the king's house, and the houses of the people, with fire, and brake down the walls of <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> Then <a href="../ebd/ebd268.htm#008">Nebuzaradan</a> the captain of the <a href="../ebd/ebd155.htm#009">guard</a> carried away <a href="../ebd/ebd072.htm#001">captive</a> into <a href="../ebd/ebd041.htm#001">Babylon</a> the remnant of the people that remained in the city, and those that fell away, that fell to him, with the rest of the people that remained.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> But <a href="../ebd/ebd268.htm#008">Nebuzaradan</a> the captain of the <a href="../ebd/ebd155.htm#009">guard</a> left of the <a href="../ebd/ebd298.htm#006">poor</a> of the people, which had nothing, in the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>, and gave them vineyards and fields at the same time.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> Now <a href="../ebd/ebd268.htm#006">Nebuchadrezzar</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd041.htm#001">Babylon</a> gave charge concerning <a href="../ebd/ebd203.htm#006">Jeremiah</a> to <a href="../ebd/ebd268.htm#008">Nebuzaradan</a> the captain of the <a href="../ebd/ebd155.htm#009">guard</a>, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> Take him, and look well to him, and do him no harm; but do unto him even as he shall say unto thee.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> So <a href="../ebd/ebd268.htm#008">Nebuzaradan</a> the captain of the <a href="../ebd/ebd155.htm#009">guard</a> sent, and <a href="../ebd/ebd268.htm#007">Nebushasban</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd304.htm#009">Rabsaris</a>, and Nergalsharezer, <a href="../ebd/ebd304.htm#008">Rabmag</a>, and all the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of Babylon's princes;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> Even they sent, and took <a href="../ebd/ebd203.htm#006">Jeremiah</a> out of the court of the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#006">prison</a>, and committed him unto <a href="../ebd/ebd144.htm#009">Gedaliah</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd014.htm#001">Ahikam</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd331.htm#004">Shaphan</a>, that he should carry him home: so he dwelt among the people.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> Now the word of the LORD came unto <a href="../ebd/ebd203.htm#006">Jeremiah</a>, while he was shut up in the court of the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#006">prison</a>, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> Go and speak to Ebedmelech the Ethiopian, saying, Thus saith the LORD of hosts, the God of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>; Behold, I will bring my words upon this city for evil, and not for good; and they shall be accomplished in that day before thee.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> But I will deliver thee in that day, saith the LORD: and thou shalt not be given into the hand of the men of whom thou art afraid.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> For I will surely deliver thee, and thou shalt not fall by the <a href="../ebd/ebd354.htm#003">sword</a>, but thy life shall be for a prey unto thee: because thou hast put thy trust in me, saith the LORD.<br>
 </p>
