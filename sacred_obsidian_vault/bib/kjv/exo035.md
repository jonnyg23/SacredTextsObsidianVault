 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Exodus Chapter 35</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> gathered all the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> together, and said unto them, These are the words which the LORD hath commanded, that ye should do them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Six days shall work be done, but on the seventh day there shall be to you an holy day, a <a href="../ebd/ebd317.htm#003">sabbath</a> of rest to the LORD: whosoever doeth work therein shall be put to death.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Ye shall kindle no fire throughout your habitations upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd317.htm#003">sabbath</a> day.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> spake unto all the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, saying, This is the thing which the LORD commanded, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Take ye from among you an <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> unto the LORD: whosoever is of a willing heart, let him bring it, an <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> of the LORD; <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#000">silver</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#003">brass</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#003">blue</a>, and purple, and <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#005">scarlet</a>, and fine <a href="../ebd/ebd229.htm#008">linen</a>, and goats' hair,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And rams' skins dyed red, and badgers' skins, and <a href="../ebd/ebd339.htm#009">shittim</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd383.htm#003">wood</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And oil for the light, and <a href="../ebd/ebd349.htm#005">spices</a> for anointing oil, and for the sweet <a href="../ebd/ebd188.htm#002">incense</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd279.htm#004">onyx</a> stones, and stones to be set for the <a href="../ebd/ebd122.htm#008">ephod</a>, and for the <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#007">breastplate</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And every <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#004">wise</a> hearted among you shall come, and make all that the LORD hath commanded;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> The <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a>, his <a href="../ebd/ebd361.htm#009">tent</a>, and his covering, his <a href="../ebd/ebd357.htm#001">taches</a>, and his boards, his bars, his pillars, and his sockets,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> The <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#000">ark</a>, and the staves thereof, with the <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> seat, and the vail of the covering,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> The table, and his staves, and all his vessels, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd336.htm#008">shewbread</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> The <a href="../ebd/ebd071.htm#001">candlestick</a> also for the light, and his furniture, and his lamps, with the oil for the light,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd188.htm#002">incense</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>, and his staves, and the anointing oil, and the sweet <a href="../ebd/ebd188.htm#002">incense</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd163.htm#001">hanging</a> for the door at the entering in of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> The <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> of burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, with his brasen <a href="../ebd/ebd154.htm#007">grate</a>, his staves, and all his vessels, the <a href="../ebd/ebd224.htm#009">laver</a> and his foot,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> The hangings of the court, his pillars, and their sockets, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd163.htm#001">hanging</a> for the door of the court,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> The pins of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a>, and the pins of the court, and their cords,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> The cloths of service, to do service in the holy place, the holy garments for <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a>, and the garments of his sons, to <a href="../ebd/ebd255.htm#007">minister</a> in the priest's office.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> And all the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> departed from the presence of <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> And they came, every one whose heart stirred him up, and every one whom his <a href="../ebd/ebd349.htm#009">spirit</a> made willing, and they brought the LORD's <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> to the work of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>, and for all his service, and for the holy garments.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> And they came, both men and women, as many as were willing hearted, and brought bracelets, and <a href="../ebd/ebd110.htm#007">earrings</a>, and rings, and tablets, all jewels of <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a>: and every man that offered offered an <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a> unto the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> And every man, with whom was found <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#003">blue</a>, and purple, and <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#005">scarlet</a>, and fine <a href="../ebd/ebd229.htm#008">linen</a>, and goats' hair, and red skins of rams, and badgers' skins, brought them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> Every one that did offer an <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#000">silver</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#003">brass</a> brought the LORD's <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>: and every man, with whom was found <a href="../ebd/ebd339.htm#009">shittim</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd383.htm#003">wood</a> for any work of the service, brought it.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> And all the women that were <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#004">wise</a> hearted did spin with their hands, and brought that which they had spun, both of <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#003">blue</a>, and of purple, and of <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#005">scarlet</a>, and of fine <a href="../ebd/ebd229.htm#008">linen</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> And all the women whose heart stirred them up in wisdom spun goats' hair.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> And the rulers brought <a href="../ebd/ebd279.htm#004">onyx</a> stones, and stones to be set, for the <a href="../ebd/ebd122.htm#008">ephod</a>, and for the <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#007">breastplate</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="028">28</a> And spice, and oil for the light, and for the anointing oil, and for the sweet <a href="../ebd/ebd188.htm#002">incense</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="029">29</a> The children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> brought a willing <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> unto the LORD, every man and woman, whose heart made them willing to bring for all manner of work, which the LORD had commanded to be made by the hand of <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="030">30</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> said unto the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, See, the LORD hath called by name <a href="../ebd/ebd057.htm#009">Bezaleel</a> the son of Uri, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd184.htm#009">Hur</a>, of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="031">31</a> And he hath filled him with the <a href="../ebd/ebd349.htm#009">spirit</a> of God, in wisdom, in understanding, and in knowledge, and in all manner of workmanship;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="032">32</a> And to devise curious works, to work in <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a>, and in <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#000">silver</a>, and in <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#003">brass</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="033">33</a> And in the <a href="../ebd/ebd095.htm#000">cutting</a> of stones, to set them, and in carving of <a href="../ebd/ebd383.htm#003">wood</a>, to make any manner of cunning work.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="034">34</a> And he hath put in his heart that he may teach, both he, and <a href="../ebd/ebd015.htm#006">Aholiab</a>, the son of Ahisamach, of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd096.htm#009">Dan</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="035">35</a> Them hath he filled with wisdom of heart, to work all manner of work, of the <a href="../ebd/ebd121.htm#001">engraver</a>, and of the cunning workman, and of the embroiderer, in <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#003">blue</a>, and in purple, in <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#005">scarlet</a>, and in fine <a href="../ebd/ebd229.htm#008">linen</a>, and of the weaver, even of them that do any work, and of those that devise cunning work.<br>
 </p>
