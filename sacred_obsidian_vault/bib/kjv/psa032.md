 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 32</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Blessed is he whose transgression is forgiven, whose <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> is covered.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Blessed is the man unto whom the LORD imputeth not iniquity, and in whose <a href="../ebd/ebd349.htm#009">spirit</a> there is no guile.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> When I kept silence, my bones waxed old through my roaring all the day long.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> For day and night thy hand was heavy upon me: my moisture is turned into the <a href="../ebd/ebd108.htm#005">drought</a> of summer. <a href="../ebd/ebd326.htm#008">Selah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> I acknowledge my <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> unto thee, and mine iniquity have I not hid. I said, I will confess my transgressions unto the LORD; and thou forgavest the iniquity of my <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a>. <a href="../ebd/ebd326.htm#008">Selah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> For this shall every one that is godly pray unto thee in a time when thou mayest be found: surely in the floods of great waters they shall not come nigh unto him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Thou art my hiding place; thou shalt preserve me from trouble; thou shalt compass me about with <a href="../ebd/ebd348.htm#002">songs</a> of deliverance. <a href="../ebd/ebd326.htm#008">Selah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> I will instruct thee and teach thee in the way which thou shalt go: I will guide thee with mine eye.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> Be ye not as the horse, or as the <a href="../ebd/ebd262.htm#001">mule</a>, which have no understanding: whose mouth must be held in with <a href="../ebd/ebd059.htm#006">bit</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd065.htm#002">bridle</a>, lest they come near unto thee.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> Many sorrows shall be to the wicked: but he that trusteth in the LORD, <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> shall compass him about.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> Be glad in the LORD, and rejoice, ye righteous: and shout for joy, all ye that are upright in heart.<br>
 </p>
