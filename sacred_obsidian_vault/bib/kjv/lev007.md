 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Leviticus Chapter 7</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Likewise this is the law of the trespass <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>: it is most holy.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> In the place where they kill the burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> shall they kill the trespass <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>: and the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> thereof shall he sprinkle round about upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And he shall offer of it all the <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#005">fat</a> thereof; the rump, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#005">fat</a> that covereth the inwards,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And the two kidneys, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#005">fat</a> that is on them, which is by the flanks, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd074.htm#003">caul</a> that is above the <a href="../ebd/ebd230.htm#004">liver</a>, with the kidneys, it shall he take away:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> shall burn them upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> for an <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> made by fire unto the LORD: it is a trespass <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Every male among the priests shall eat thereof: it shall be eaten in the holy place: it is most holy.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> As the <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> is, so is the trespass <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>: there is one law for them: the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> that maketh <a href="../ebd/ebd036.htm#004">atonement</a> therewith shall have it.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> that offereth any man's burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, even the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> shall have to himself the skin of the burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> which he hath offered.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And all the meat <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> that is baken in the <a href="../ebd/ebd281.htm#005">oven</a>, and all that is dressed in the fryingpan, and in the <a href="../ebd/ebd283.htm#009">pan</a>, shall be the priest's that offereth it.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And every meat <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, mingled with oil, and dry, shall all the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> have, one as much as another.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And this is the law of the <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#002">sacrifice</a> of peace offerings, which he shall offer unto the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> If he offer it for a thanksgiving, then he shall offer with the <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#002">sacrifice</a> of thanksgiving unleavened cakes mingled with oil, and unleavened <a href="../ebd/ebd378.htm#003">wafers</a> anointed with oil, and cakes mingled with oil, of fine <a href="../ebd/ebd136.htm#001">flour</a>, fried.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> Besides the cakes, he shall offer for his <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> leavened <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#006">bread</a> with the <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#002">sacrifice</a> of thanksgiving of his peace offerings.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> And of it he shall offer one out of the whole oblation for an heave <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> unto the LORD, and it shall be the priest's that sprinkleth the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> of the peace offerings.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd135.htm#007">flesh</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#002">sacrifice</a> of his peace offerings for thanksgiving shall be eaten the same day that it is offered; he shall not leave any of it until the morning.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> But if the <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#002">sacrifice</a> of his <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> be a vow, or a voluntary <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, it shall be eaten the same day that he offereth his <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#002">sacrifice</a>: and on the morrow also the remainder of it shall be eaten:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> But the remainder of the <a href="../ebd/ebd135.htm#007">flesh</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#002">sacrifice</a> on the third day shall be burnt with fire.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> And if any of the <a href="../ebd/ebd135.htm#007">flesh</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#002">sacrifice</a> of his peace offerings be eaten at all on the third day, it shall not be accepted, neither shall it be imputed unto him that offereth it: it shall be an <a href="../ebd/ebd005.htm#003">abomination</a>, and the soul that eateth of it shall bear his iniquity.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd135.htm#007">flesh</a> that toucheth any unclean thing shall not be eaten; it shall be burnt with fire: and as for the <a href="../ebd/ebd135.htm#007">flesh</a>, all that be <a href="../ebd/ebd084.htm#005">clean</a> shall eat thereof.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> But the soul that eateth of the <a href="../ebd/ebd135.htm#007">flesh</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#002">sacrifice</a> of peace offerings, that pertain unto the LORD, having his uncleanness upon him, even that soul shall be cut off from his people.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> Moreover the soul that shall touch any unclean thing, as the uncleanness of man, or any unclean <a href="../ebd/ebd049.htm#001">beast</a>, or any abominable unclean thing, and eat of the <a href="../ebd/ebd135.htm#007">flesh</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#002">sacrifice</a> of peace offerings, which pertain unto the LORD, even that soul shall be cut off from his people.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> And the LORD spake unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> Speak unto the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, saying, Ye shall eat no manner of <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#005">fat</a>, of <a href="../ebd/ebd281.htm#007">ox</a>, or of <a href="../ebd/ebd333.htm#005">sheep</a>, or of <a href="../ebd/ebd151.htm#001">goat</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#005">fat</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd049.htm#001">beast</a> that dieth of itself, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#005">fat</a> of that which is torn with beasts, may be used in any other use: but ye shall in no <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#004">wise</a> eat of it.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> For whosoever eateth the <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#005">fat</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd049.htm#001">beast</a>, of which men offer an <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> made by fire unto the LORD, even the soul that eateth it shall be cut off from his people.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> Moreover ye shall eat no manner of <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a>, whether it be of fowl or of <a href="../ebd/ebd049.htm#001">beast</a>, in any of your <a href="../ebd/ebd110.htm#001">dwellings</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> Whatsoever soul it be that eateth any manner of <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a>, even that soul shall be cut off from his people.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="028">28</a> And the LORD spake unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="029">29</a> Speak unto the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, saying, He that offereth the <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#002">sacrifice</a> of his peace offerings unto the LORD shall bring his oblation unto the LORD of the <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#002">sacrifice</a> of his peace offerings.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="030">30</a> His own hands shall bring the offerings of the LORD made by fire, the <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#005">fat</a> with the breast, it shall he bring, that the breast may be waved for a wave <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> before the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="031">31</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> shall burn the <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#005">fat</a> upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>: but the breast shall be Aaron's and his sons'.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="032">32</a> And the right shoulder shall ye give unto the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> for an heave <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> of the sacrifices of your peace offerings.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="033">33</a> He among the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a>, that offereth the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> of the peace offerings, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#005">fat</a>, shall have the right shoulder for his part.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="034">34</a> For the wave breast and the heave shoulder have I taken of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> from off the sacrifices of their peace offerings, and have given them unto <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> and unto his sons by a statute for ever from among the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="035">35</a> This is the portion of the anointing of <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a>, and of the anointing of his sons, out of the offerings of the LORD made by fire, in the day when he presented them to <a href="../ebd/ebd255.htm#007">minister</a> unto the LORD in the priest's office;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="036">36</a> Which the LORD commanded to be given them of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, in the day that he anointed them, by a statute for ever throughout their generations.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="037">37</a> This is the law of the burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, of the meat <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, and of the <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, and of the trespass <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, and of the consecrations, and of the <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#002">sacrifice</a> of the peace offerings;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="038">38</a> Which the LORD commanded <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> in <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#007">mount</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#009">Sinai</a>, in the day that he commanded the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> to offer their oblations unto the LORD, in the <a href="../ebd/ebd381.htm#005">wilderness</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#009">Sinai</a>.<br>
 </p>
