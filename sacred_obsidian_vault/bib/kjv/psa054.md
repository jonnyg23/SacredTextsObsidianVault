 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 54</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Save me, O God, by thy name, and <a href="../ebd/ebd213.htm#005">judge</a> me by thy strength.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Hear my <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#000">prayer</a>, O God; give <a href="../ebd/ebd110.htm#004">ear</a> to the words of my mouth.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> For strangers are risen up against me, and oppressors seek after my soul: they have not set God before them. <a href="../ebd/ebd326.htm#008">Selah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Behold, God is mine helper: the Lord is with them that uphold my soul.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> He shall reward evil unto mine enemies: cut them off in thy truth.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> I will freely <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#002">sacrifice</a> unto thee: I will praise thy name, O LORD; for it is good.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> For he hath delivered me out of all trouble: and mine eye hath seen his desire upon mine enemies.<br>
 </p>
