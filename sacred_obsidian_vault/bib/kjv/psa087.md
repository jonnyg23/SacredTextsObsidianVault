 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 87</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> His foundation is in the holy mountains.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> The LORD loveth the gates of <a href="../ebd/ebd394.htm#002">Zion</a> more than all the <a href="../ebd/ebd110.htm#001">dwellings</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd194.htm#007">Jacob</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Glorious things are spoken of thee, O city of God. <a href="../ebd/ebd326.htm#008">Selah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> I will make mention of <a href="../ebd/ebd305.htm#006">Rahab</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd041.htm#001">Babylon</a> to them that know me: behold <a href="../ebd/ebd294.htm#001">Philistia</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd374.htm#001">Tyre</a>, with <a href="../ebd/ebd126.htm#005">Ethiopia</a>; this man was born there.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And of <a href="../ebd/ebd394.htm#002">Zion</a> it shall be said, This and that man was born in her: and the highest himself shall establish her.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> The LORD shall count, when he writeth up the people, that this man was born there. <a href="../ebd/ebd326.htm#008">Selah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> As well the singers as the players on instruments shall be there: all my springs are in thee.<br>
 </p>
