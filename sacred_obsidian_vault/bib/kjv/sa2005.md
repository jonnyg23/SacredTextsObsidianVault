 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: 2 Kings (2 Samuel) Chapter 5</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Then came all the tribes of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> to <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> unto <a href="../ebd/ebd171.htm#004">Hebron</a>, and spake, saying, Behold, we are thy bone and thy <a href="../ebd/ebd135.htm#007">flesh</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Also in time past, when <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#002">Saul</a> was <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> over us, thou wast he that leddest out and broughtest in <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>: and the LORD said to thee, Thou shalt feed my people <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, and thou shalt be a captain over <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> So all the elders of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> came to the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> to <a href="../ebd/ebd171.htm#004">Hebron</a>; and <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> made a <a href="../ebd/ebd225.htm#005">league</a> with them in <a href="../ebd/ebd171.htm#004">Hebron</a> before the LORD: and they anointed <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> over <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> was thirty years old when he began to reign, and he reigned forty years.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> In <a href="../ebd/ebd171.htm#004">Hebron</a> he reigned over <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> years and six months: and in <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a> he reigned thirty and three years over all <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> and his men went to <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a> unto the <a href="../ebd/ebd199.htm#004">Jebusites</a>, the inhabitants of the land: which spake unto <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a>, saying, Except thou take away the <a href="../ebd/ebd060.htm#009">blind</a> and the lame, thou shalt not come in hither: thinking, <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> cannot come in hither.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Nevertheless <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> took the strong hold of <a href="../ebd/ebd394.htm#002">Zion</a>: the same is the city of <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> said on that day, Whosoever getteth up to the <a href="../ebd/ebd156.htm#003">gutter</a>, and smiteth the <a href="../ebd/ebd199.htm#004">Jebusites</a>, and the lame and the <a href="../ebd/ebd060.htm#009">blind</a> that are hated of David's soul, he shall be chief and captain. Wherefore they said, The <a href="../ebd/ebd060.htm#009">blind</a> and the lame shall not come into the house.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> So <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> dwelt in the fort, and called it the city of <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a>. And <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> built round about from <a href="../ebd/ebd255.htm#004">Millo</a> and inward.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> went on, and grew great, and the LORD God of hosts was with him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd179.htm#003">Hiram</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd374.htm#001">Tyre</a> sent messengers to <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd074.htm#007">cedar</a> trees, and carpenters, and masons: and they built <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> an house.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> perceived that the LORD had established him <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> over <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, and that he had exalted his kingdom for his people Israel's sake.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> took him more concubines and wives out of <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>, after he was come from <a href="../ebd/ebd171.htm#004">Hebron</a>: and there were yet sons and daughters born to <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> And these be the names of those that were born unto him in <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>; Shammuah, and <a href="../ebd/ebd340.htm#001">Shobab</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd267.htm#002">Nathan</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd347.htm#007">Solomon</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd185.htm#008">Ibhar</a> also, and <a href="../ebd/ebd118.htm#004">Elishua</a>, and Nepheg, and <a href="../ebd/ebd197.htm#003">Japhia</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd118.htm#001">Elishama</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd115.htm#009">Eliada</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd117.htm#004">Eliphalet</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> But when the <a href="../ebd/ebd294.htm#002">Philistines</a> heard that they had anointed <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> over <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, all the <a href="../ebd/ebd294.htm#002">Philistines</a> came up to seek <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a>; and <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> heard of it, and went down to the hold.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> The <a href="../ebd/ebd294.htm#002">Philistines</a> also came and spread themselves in the <a href="../ebd/ebd376.htm#008">valley</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd311.htm#000">Rephaim</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> enquired of the LORD, saying, Shall I go up to the <a href="../ebd/ebd294.htm#002">Philistines</a>? wilt thou deliver them into mine hand? And the LORD said unto <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a>, Go up: for I will doubtless deliver the <a href="../ebd/ebd294.htm#002">Philistines</a> into thine hand.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> came to Baalperazim, and <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> smote them there, and said, The LORD hath broken forth upon mine enemies before me, as the <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#005">breach</a> of waters. Therefore he called the name of that place Baalperazim.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> And there they left their images, and <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> and his men burned them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd294.htm#002">Philistines</a> came up yet again, and spread themselves in the <a href="../ebd/ebd376.htm#008">valley</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd311.htm#000">Rephaim</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> And when <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> enquired of the LORD, he said, Thou shalt not go up; but fetch a compass behind them, and come upon them over against the <a href="../ebd/ebd262.htm#000">mulberry</a> trees.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> And let it be, when thou hearest the sound of a going in the tops of the <a href="../ebd/ebd262.htm#000">mulberry</a> trees, that then thou shalt bestir thyself: for then shall the LORD go out before thee, to smite the <a href="../ebd/ebd183.htm#007">host</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd294.htm#002">Philistines</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> did so, as the LORD had commanded him; and smote the <a href="../ebd/ebd294.htm#002">Philistines</a> from <a href="../ebd/ebd144.htm#004">Geba</a> until thou come to Gazer.<br>
 </p>
