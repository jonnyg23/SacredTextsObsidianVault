 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 36</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> The transgression of the wicked saith within my heart, that there is no fear of God before his eyes.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> For he flattereth himself in his own eyes, until his iniquity be found to be hateful.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> The words of his mouth are iniquity and deceit: he hath left off to be <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#004">wise</a>, and to do good.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> He deviseth mischief upon his bed; he setteth himself in a way that is not good; he abhorreth not evil.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Thy <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a>, O LORD, is in the heavens; and thy faithfulness reacheth unto the clouds.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Thy <a href="../ebd/ebd313.htm#004">righteousness</a> is like the great mountains; thy judgments are a great <a href="../ebd/ebd100.htm#008">deep</a>: O LORD, thou preservest man and <a href="../ebd/ebd049.htm#001">beast</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> How excellent is thy lovingkindness, O God! therefore the children of men put their trust under the <a href="../ebd/ebd330.htm#002">shadow</a> of thy wings.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> They shall be abundantly satisfied with the fatness of thy house; and thou shalt make them <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a> of the river of thy pleasures.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> For with thee is the <a href="../ebd/ebd138.htm#000">fountain</a> of life: in thy light shall we see light.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> O continue thy lovingkindness unto them that know thee; and thy <a href="../ebd/ebd313.htm#004">righteousness</a> to the upright in heart.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> Let not the foot of pride come against me, and let not the hand of the wicked remove me.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> There are the workers of iniquity fallen: they are cast down, and shall not be able to rise.<br>
 </p>
