 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Isaiah Chapter 20</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> In the year that <a href="../ebd/ebd359.htm#005">Tartan</a> came unto <a href="../ebd/ebd033.htm#006">Ashdod</a>, (when <a href="../ebd/ebd322.htm#009">Sargon</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd035.htm#005">Assyria</a> sent him,) and fought against <a href="../ebd/ebd033.htm#006">Ashdod</a>, and took it;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> At the same time spake the LORD by <a href="../ebd/ebd189.htm#007">Isaiah</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd021.htm#008">Amoz</a>, saying, Go and loose the <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#001">sackcloth</a> from off thy loins, and put off thy <a href="../ebd/ebd340.htm#009">shoe</a> from thy foot. And he did so, walking <a href="../ebd/ebd266.htm#003">naked</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd044.htm#008">barefoot</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And the LORD said, Like as my servant <a href="../ebd/ebd189.htm#007">Isaiah</a> hath walked <a href="../ebd/ebd266.htm#003">naked</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd044.htm#008">barefoot</a> three years for a sign and wonder upon <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a> and upon <a href="../ebd/ebd126.htm#005">Ethiopia</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> So shall the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd035.htm#005">Assyria</a> lead away the Egyptians prisoners, and the Ethiopians captives, young and old, <a href="../ebd/ebd266.htm#003">naked</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd044.htm#008">barefoot</a>, even with their buttocks uncovered, to the shame of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And they shall be afraid and ashamed of <a href="../ebd/ebd126.htm#005">Ethiopia</a> their expectation, and of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a> their <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And the inhabitant of this isle shall say in that day, Behold, such is our expectation, whither we flee for help to be delivered from the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd035.htm#005">Assyria</a>: and how shall we escape?<br>
 </p>
