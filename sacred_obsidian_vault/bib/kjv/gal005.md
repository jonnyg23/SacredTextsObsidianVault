 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Galatians Chapter 5</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Stand <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#004">fast</a> therefore in the liberty wherewith <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a> hath made us free, and be not entangled again with the <a href="../ebd/ebd384.htm#009">yoke</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd062.htm#002">bondage</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Behold, I <a href="../ebd/ebd287.htm#002">Paul</a> say unto you, that if ye be circumcised, <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a> shall profit you nothing.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> For I testify again to every man that is circumcised, that he is a <a href="../ebd/ebd100.htm#000">debtor</a> to do the whole law.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a> is become of no effect unto you, whosoever of you are justified by the law; ye are fallen from <a href="../ebd/ebd154.htm#000">grace</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> For we through the <a href="../ebd/ebd349.htm#009">Spirit</a> wait for the hope of <a href="../ebd/ebd313.htm#004">righteousness</a> by faith.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> For in <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a> neither <a href="../ebd/ebd083.htm#007">circumcision</a> availeth any thing, nor uncircumcision; but faith which worketh by love.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Ye did run well; who did hinder you that ye should not obey the truth?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> This persuasion cometh not of him that calleth you.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> A little <a href="../ebd/ebd226.htm#000">leaven</a> leaveneth the whole lump.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> I have confidence in you through the Lord, that ye will be none otherwise minded: but he that troubleth you shall bear his judgment, whosoever he be.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And I, brethren, if I yet preach <a href="../ebd/ebd083.htm#007">circumcision</a>, why do I yet suffer <a href="../ebd/ebd290.htm#007">persecution</a>? then is the offence of the <a href="../ebd/ebd093.htm#000">cross</a> ceased.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> I would they were even cut off which trouble you.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> For, brethren, ye have been called unto liberty; only use not liberty for an occasion to the <a href="../ebd/ebd135.htm#007">flesh</a>, but by love serve one another.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> For all the law is fulfilled in one word, even in this; Thou shalt love thy neighbour as thyself.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> But if ye bite and devour one another, take heed that ye be not consumed one of another.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> This I say then, Walk in the <a href="../ebd/ebd349.htm#009">Spirit</a>, and ye shall not fulfil the <a href="../ebd/ebd233.htm#005">lust</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd135.htm#007">flesh</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> For the <a href="../ebd/ebd135.htm#007">flesh</a> lusteth against the <a href="../ebd/ebd349.htm#009">Spirit</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd349.htm#009">Spirit</a> against the <a href="../ebd/ebd135.htm#007">flesh</a>: and these are contrary the one to the other: so that ye cannot do the things that ye would.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> But if ye be led of the <a href="../ebd/ebd349.htm#009">Spirit</a>, ye are not under the law.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> Now the works of the <a href="../ebd/ebd135.htm#007">flesh</a> are manifest, which are these; <a href="../ebd/ebd010.htm#009">Adultery</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd137.htm#008">fornication</a>, uncleanness, lasciviousness,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd186.htm#007">Idolatry</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#007">witchcraft</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd167.htm#002">hatred</a>, variance, emulations, wrath, strife, seditions, heresies,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> Envyings, murders, drunkenness, revellings, and such like: of the which I tell you before, as I have also told you in time past, that they which do such things shall not inherit the kingdom of God.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> But the <a href="../ebd/ebd139.htm#000">fruit</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd349.htm#009">Spirit</a> is love, joy, peace, longsuffering, gentleness, <a href="../ebd/ebd153.htm#000">goodness</a>, faith,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd246.htm#003">Meekness</a>, temperance: against such there is no law.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> And they that are Christ's have crucified the <a href="../ebd/ebd135.htm#007">flesh</a> with the affections and lusts.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> If we live in the <a href="../ebd/ebd349.htm#009">Spirit</a>, let us also walk in the <a href="../ebd/ebd349.htm#009">Spirit</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> Let us not be desirous of vain <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a>, provoking one another, envying one another.<br>
 </p>
