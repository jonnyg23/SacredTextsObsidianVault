 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Exodus Chapter 34</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> And the LORD said unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, Hew thee two <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#006">tables</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd351.htm#005">stone</a> like unto the first: and I will write upon these <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#006">tables</a> the words that were in the first <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#006">tables</a>, which thou brakest.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> And be ready in the morning, and come up in the morning unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#007">mount</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#009">Sinai</a>, and present thyself there to me in the top of the <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#007">mount</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And no man shall come up with thee, neither let any man be seen throughout all the <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#007">mount</a>; neither let the flocks nor herds feed before that <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#007">mount</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And he hewed two <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#006">tables</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd351.htm#005">stone</a> like unto the first; and <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> rose up early in the morning, and went up unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#007">mount</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#009">Sinai</a>, as the LORD had commanded him, and took in his hand the two <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#006">tables</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd351.htm#005">stone</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And the LORD descended in the <a href="../ebd/ebd085.htm#001">cloud</a>, and stood with him there, and proclaimed the name of the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And the LORD passed by before him, and proclaimed, The LORD, The LORD God, merciful and gracious, longsuffering, and abundant in <a href="../ebd/ebd153.htm#000">goodness</a> and truth,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Keeping <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> for <a href="../ebd/ebd364.htm#008">thousands</a>, forgiving iniquity and transgression and <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a>, and that will by no means clear the guilty; visiting the iniquity of the fathers upon the children, and upon the children's children, unto the third and to the fourth <a href="../ebd/ebd145.htm#008">generation</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> made haste, and bowed his head toward the earth, and worshipped.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And he said, If now I have found <a href="../ebd/ebd154.htm#000">grace</a> in thy sight, O LORD, let my LORD, I pray thee, go among us; for it is a stiffnecked people; and <a href="../ebd/ebd285.htm#001">pardon</a> our iniquity and our <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a>, and take us for thine inheritance.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And he said, Behold, I make a <a href="../ebd/ebd091.htm#008">covenant</a>: before all thy people I will do marvels, such as have not been done in all the earth, nor in any nation: and all the people among which thou art shall see the work of the LORD: for it is a terrible thing that I will do with thee.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> Observe thou that which I command thee this day: behold, I drive out before thee the Amorite, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#008">Canaanite</a>, and the Hittite, and the Perizzite, and the Hivite, and the Jebusite.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> Take heed to thyself, lest thou make a <a href="../ebd/ebd091.htm#008">covenant</a> with the inhabitants of the land whither thou goest, lest it be for a <a href="../ebd/ebd346.htm#009">snare</a> in the midst of thee:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> But ye shall destroy their altars, break their images, and cut down their groves:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> For thou shalt <a href="../ebd/ebd384.htm#002">worship</a> no other god: for the LORD, whose name is Jealous, is a jealous God:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> Lest thou make a <a href="../ebd/ebd091.htm#008">covenant</a> with the inhabitants of the land, and they go a whoring after their gods, and do <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#002">sacrifice</a> unto their gods, and one call thee, and thou eat of his <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#002">sacrifice</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> And thou take of their daughters unto thy sons, and their daughters go a whoring after their gods, and make thy sons go a whoring after their gods.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> Thou shalt make thee no molten gods.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> The <a href="../ebd/ebd132.htm#000">feast</a> of unleavened <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#006">bread</a> shalt thou keep. <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">Seven</a> days thou shalt eat unleavened <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#006">bread</a>, as I commanded thee, in the time of the month <a href="../ebd/ebd002.htm#006">Abib</a>: for in the month <a href="../ebd/ebd002.htm#006">Abib</a> thou camest out from <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> All that openeth the matrix is mine; and every firstling among thy <a href="../ebd/ebd074.htm#002">cattle</a>, whether <a href="../ebd/ebd281.htm#007">ox</a> or <a href="../ebd/ebd333.htm#005">sheep</a>, that is male.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> But the firstling of an <a href="../ebd/ebd035.htm#001">ass</a> thou shalt redeem with a <a href="../ebd/ebd223.htm#003">lamb</a>: and if thou redeem him not, then shalt thou break his <a href="../ebd/ebd269.htm#000">neck</a>. All the firstborn of thy sons thou shalt redeem. And none shall appear before me empty.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> Six days thou shalt work, but on the seventh day thou shalt rest: in <a href="../ebd/ebd110.htm#005">earing</a> time and in <a href="../ebd/ebd165.htm#008">harvest</a> thou shalt rest.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> And thou shalt observe the <a href="../ebd/ebd132.htm#000">feast</a> of weeks, of the firstfruits of <a href="../ebd/ebd381.htm#000">wheat</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd165.htm#008">harvest</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd132.htm#000">feast</a> of ingathering at the year's end.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> Thrice in the year shall all your menchildren appear before the LORD God, the God of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> For I will cast out the nations before thee, and enlarge thy borders: neither shall any man desire thy land, when thou shalt go up to appear before the LORD thy God thrice in the year.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> Thou shalt not offer the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> of my <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#002">sacrifice</a> with <a href="../ebd/ebd226.htm#000">leaven</a>; neither shall the <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#002">sacrifice</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd132.htm#000">feast</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd286.htm#005">passover</a> be left unto the morning.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> The first of the firstfruits of thy land thou shalt bring unto the house of the LORD thy God. Thou shalt not <a href="../ebd/ebd326.htm#001">seethe</a> a kid in his mother's <a href="../ebd/ebd255.htm#000">milk</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> And the LORD said unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, Write thou these words: for after the tenor of these words I have made a <a href="../ebd/ebd091.htm#008">covenant</a> with thee and with <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="028">28</a> And he was there with the LORD forty days and forty nights; he did neither eat <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#006">bread</a>, nor <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a> water. And he wrote upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#006">tables</a> the words of the <a href="../ebd/ebd091.htm#008">covenant</a>, the ten commandments.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="029">29</a> And it came to pass, when <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> came down from <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#007">mount</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#009">Sinai</a> with the two <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#006">tables</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd362.htm#008">testimony</a> in Moses' hand, when he came down from the <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#007">mount</a>, that <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> wist not that the skin of his face shone while he talked with him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="030">30</a> And when <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> and all the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> saw <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, behold, the skin of his face shone; and they were afraid to come nigh him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="031">31</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> called unto them; and <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> and all the rulers of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> returned unto him: and <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> talked with them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="032">32</a> And afterward all the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> came nigh: and he gave them in commandment all that the LORD had spoken with him in <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#007">mount</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#009">Sinai</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="033">33</a> And till <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> had done speaking with them, he put a vail on his face.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="034">34</a> But when <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> went in before the LORD to speak with him, he took the vail off, until he came out. And he came out, and spake unto the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> that which he was commanded.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="035">35</a> And the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> saw the face of <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, that the skin of Moses' face shone: and <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> put the vail upon his face again, until he went in to speak with him.<br>
 </p>
