 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 112</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Praise ye the LORD. Blessed is the man that feareth the LORD, that delighteth greatly in his commandments.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> His seed shall be mighty upon earth: the <a href="../ebd/ebd145.htm#008">generation</a> of the upright shall be blessed.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Wealth and riches shall be in his house: and his <a href="../ebd/ebd313.htm#004">righteousness</a> endureth for ever.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Unto the upright there ariseth light in the <a href="../ebd/ebd097.htm#008">darkness</a>: he is gracious, and full of compassion, and righteous.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> A good man sheweth favour, and lendeth: he will guide his affairs with discretion.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Surely he shall not be moved for ever: the righteous shall be in <a href="../ebd/ebd127.htm#007">everlasting</a> remembrance.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> He shall not be afraid of evil tidings: his heart is fixed, trusting in the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> His heart is established, he shall not be afraid, until he see his desire upon his enemies.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> He hath dispersed, he hath given to the <a href="../ebd/ebd298.htm#006">poor</a>; his <a href="../ebd/ebd313.htm#004">righteousness</a> endureth for ever; his <a href="../ebd/ebd182.htm#003">horn</a> shall be exalted with honour.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> The wicked shall see it, and be grieved; he shall <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#008">gnash</a> with his teeth, and melt away: the desire of the wicked shall perish.<br>
 </p>
