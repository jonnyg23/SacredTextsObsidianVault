 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Jeremiah Chapter 28</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> And it came to pass the same year, in the beginning of the reign of <a href="../ebd/ebd389.htm#008">Zedekiah</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>, in the fourth year, and in the fifth month, that <a href="../ebd/ebd162.htm#004">Hananiah</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd038.htm#003">Azur</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#008">prophet</a>, which was of <a href="../ebd/ebd148.htm#002">Gibeon</a>, spake unto me in the house of the LORD, in the presence of the priests and of all the people, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Thus speaketh the LORD of hosts, the God of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, saying, I have broken the <a href="../ebd/ebd384.htm#009">yoke</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd041.htm#001">Babylon</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Within two full years will I bring again into this place all the vessels of the LORD's house, that <a href="../ebd/ebd268.htm#005">Nebuchadnezzar</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd041.htm#001">Babylon</a> took away from this place, and carried them to <a href="../ebd/ebd041.htm#001">Babylon</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And I will bring again to this place Jeconiah the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd201.htm#001">Jehoiakim</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>, with all the captives of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>, that went into <a href="../ebd/ebd041.htm#001">Babylon</a>, saith the LORD: for I will break the <a href="../ebd/ebd384.htm#009">yoke</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd041.htm#001">Babylon</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Then the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#008">prophet</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd203.htm#006">Jeremiah</a> said unto the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#008">prophet</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd162.htm#004">Hananiah</a> in the presence of the priests, and in the presence of all the people that stood in the house of the LORD,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Even the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#008">prophet</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd203.htm#006">Jeremiah</a> said, <a href="../ebd/ebd020.htm#001">Amen</a>: the LORD do so: the LORD perform thy words which thou hast prophesied, to bring again the vessels of the LORD's house, and all that is carried away <a href="../ebd/ebd072.htm#001">captive</a>, from <a href="../ebd/ebd041.htm#001">Babylon</a> into this place.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Nevertheless hear thou now this word that I speak in thine ears, and in the ears of all the people;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> The prophets that have been before me and before thee of old prophesied both against many countries, and against great kingdoms, of war, and of evil, and of pestilence.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> The <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#008">prophet</a> which prophesieth of peace, when the word of the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#008">prophet</a> shall come to pass, then shall the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#008">prophet</a> be known, that the LORD hath truly sent him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> Then <a href="../ebd/ebd162.htm#004">Hananiah</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#008">prophet</a> took the <a href="../ebd/ebd384.htm#009">yoke</a> from off the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#008">prophet</a> Jeremiah's <a href="../ebd/ebd269.htm#000">neck</a>, and brake it.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd162.htm#004">Hananiah</a> spake in the presence of all the people, saying, Thus saith the LORD; Even so will I break the <a href="../ebd/ebd384.htm#009">yoke</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd268.htm#005">Nebuchadnezzar</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd041.htm#001">Babylon</a> from the <a href="../ebd/ebd269.htm#000">neck</a> of all nations within the space of two full years. And the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#008">prophet</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd203.htm#006">Jeremiah</a> went his way.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> Then the word of the LORD came unto <a href="../ebd/ebd203.htm#006">Jeremiah</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#008">prophet</a>, after that <a href="../ebd/ebd162.htm#004">Hananiah</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#008">prophet</a> had broken the <a href="../ebd/ebd384.htm#009">yoke</a> from off the <a href="../ebd/ebd269.htm#000">neck</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#008">prophet</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd203.htm#006">Jeremiah</a>, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> Go and tell <a href="../ebd/ebd162.htm#004">Hananiah</a>, saying, Thus saith the LORD; Thou hast broken the yokes of <a href="../ebd/ebd383.htm#003">wood</a>; but thou shalt make for them yokes of <a href="../ebd/ebd189.htm#004">iron</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> For thus saith the LORD of hosts, the God of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>; I have put a <a href="../ebd/ebd384.htm#009">yoke</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd189.htm#004">iron</a> upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd269.htm#000">neck</a> of all these nations, that they may serve <a href="../ebd/ebd268.htm#005">Nebuchadnezzar</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd041.htm#001">Babylon</a>; and they shall serve him: and I have given him the beasts of the field also.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> Then said the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#008">prophet</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd203.htm#006">Jeremiah</a> unto <a href="../ebd/ebd162.htm#004">Hananiah</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#008">prophet</a>, Hear now, <a href="../ebd/ebd162.htm#004">Hananiah</a>; The LORD hath not sent thee; but thou makest this people to trust in a lie.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> Therefore thus saith the LORD; Behold, I will cast thee from off the face of the earth: this year thou shalt die, because thou hast taught rebellion against the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> So <a href="../ebd/ebd162.htm#004">Hananiah</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#008">prophet</a> died the same year in the seventh month.<br>
 </p>
