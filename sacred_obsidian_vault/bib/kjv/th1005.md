 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: 1 Thessalonians Chapter 5</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> But of the times and the <a href="../ebd/ebd325.htm#003">seasons</a>, brethren, ye have no need that I write unto you.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> For yourselves know perfectly that the day of the Lord so cometh as a thief in the night.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> For when they shall say, Peace and safety; then sudden <a href="../ebd/ebd102.htm#004">destruction</a> cometh upon them, as travail upon a woman with child; and they shall not escape.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> But ye, brethren, are not in <a href="../ebd/ebd097.htm#008">darkness</a>, that that day should overtake you as a thief.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Ye are all the children of light, and the children of the day: we are not of the night, nor of <a href="../ebd/ebd097.htm#008">darkness</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Therefore let us not sleep, as do others; but let us watch and be sober.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> For they that sleep sleep in the night; and they that be drunken are drunken in the night.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> But let us, who are of the day, be sober, putting on the <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#007">breastplate</a> of faith and love; and for an <a href="../ebd/ebd173.htm#004">helmet</a>, the hope of <a href="../ebd/ebd320.htm#003">salvation</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> For God hath not appointed us to wrath, but to obtain <a href="../ebd/ebd320.htm#003">salvation</a> by our Lord <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> Who died for us, that, whether we wake or sleep, we should live together with him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> Wherefore comfort yourselves together, and edify one another, even as also ye do.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And we beseech you, brethren, to know them which labour among you, and are over you in the Lord, and admonish you;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> And to esteem them very highly in love for their work's sake. And be at peace among yourselves.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> Now we exhort you, brethren, warn them that are unruly, comfort the feebleminded, support the weak, be patient toward all men.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> See that none render evil for evil unto any man; but ever follow that which is good, both among yourselves, and to all men.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> Rejoice evermore.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> Pray without ceasing.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> In every thing give thanks: for this is the will of God in <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a> concerning you.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> Quench not the <a href="../ebd/ebd349.htm#009">Spirit</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> Despise not prophesyings.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> Prove all things; hold <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#004">fast</a> that which is good.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> Abstain from all appearance of evil.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> And the very God of peace sanctify you wholly; and I pray God your whole <a href="../ebd/ebd349.htm#009">spirit</a> and soul and body be preserved blameless unto the coming of our Lord <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd130.htm#005">Faithful</a> is he that calleth you, who also will do it.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> Brethren, pray for us.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> Greet all the brethren with an holy <a href="../ebd/ebd221.htm#000">kiss</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> I charge you by the Lord that this epistle be read unto all the holy brethren.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="028">28</a> The <a href="../ebd/ebd154.htm#000">grace</a> of our Lord <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a> be with you. <a href="../ebd/ebd020.htm#001">Amen</a>.<br>
 </p>
