 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: 1 Chronicles Chapter 13</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> consulted with the captains of <a href="../ebd/ebd364.htm#008">thousands</a> and hundreds, and with every leader.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> said unto all the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, If it seem good unto you, and that it be of the LORD our God, let us send abroad unto our brethren every where, that are left in all the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, and with them also to the priests and Levites which are in their cities and <a href="../ebd/ebd352.htm#007">suburbs</a>, that they may gather themselves unto us:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And let us bring again the <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#000">ark</a> of our God to us: for we enquired not at it in the days of <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#002">Saul</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And all the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> said that they would do so: for the thing was right in the eyes of all the people.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> So <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> gathered all <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> together, from <a href="../ebd/ebd337.htm#004">Shihor</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a> even unto the entering of <a href="../ebd/ebd174.htm#000">Hemath</a>, to bring the <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#000">ark</a> of God from Kirjathjearim.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> went up, and all <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, to <a href="../ebd/ebd038.htm#005">Baalah</a>, that is, to Kirjathjearim, which belonged to <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>, to bring up thence the <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#000">ark</a> of God the LORD, that dwelleth between the cherubims, whose name is called on it.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And they carried the <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#000">ark</a> of God in a new <a href="../ebd/ebd073.htm#001">cart</a> out of the house of <a href="../ebd/ebd004.htm#001">Abinadab</a>: and <a href="../ebd/ebd376.htm#000">Uzza</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd014.htm#007">Ahio</a> drave the <a href="../ebd/ebd073.htm#001">cart</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> and all <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> played before God with all their might, and with singing, and with harps, and with psalteries, and with timbrels, and with <a href="../ebd/ebd095.htm#001">cymbals</a>, and with <a href="../ebd/ebd373.htm#001">trumpets</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And when they came unto the threshingfloor of <a href="../ebd/ebd080.htm#001">Chidon</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd376.htm#000">Uzza</a> put forth his hand to hold the <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#000">ark</a>; for the oxen stumbled.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd024.htm#002">anger</a> of the LORD was kindled against <a href="../ebd/ebd376.htm#000">Uzza</a>, and he smote him, because he put his hand to the <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#000">ark</a>: and there he died before God.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> was displeased, because the LORD had made a <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#005">breach</a> upon <a href="../ebd/ebd376.htm#000">Uzza</a>: wherefore that place is called Perezuzza to this day.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> was afraid of God that day, saying, How shall I bring the <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#000">ark</a> of God home to me?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> So <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> brought not the <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#000">ark</a> home to himself to the city of <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a>, but carried it aside into the house of Obededom the <a href="../ebd/ebd149.htm#009">Gittite</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#000">ark</a> of God remained with the family of Obededom in his house three months. And the LORD blessed the house of Obededom, and all that he had.<br>
 </p>
