 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: 1 Corinthians Chapter 1</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd287.htm#002">Paul</a> called to be an <a href="../ebd/ebd026.htm#008">apostle</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a> through the will of God, and <a href="../ebd/ebd348.htm#009">Sosthenes</a> our brother,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Unto the church of God which is at <a href="../ebd/ebd090.htm#002">Corinth</a>, to them that are sanctified in <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a>, called to be saints, with all that in every place call upon the name of <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a> our Lord, both their's and our's:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd154.htm#000">Grace</a> be unto you, and peace, from God our Father, and from the Lord <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> I thank my God always on your behalf, for the <a href="../ebd/ebd154.htm#000">grace</a> of God which is given you by <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> That in every thing ye are enriched by him, in all utterance, and in all knowledge;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Even as the <a href="../ebd/ebd362.htm#008">testimony</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a> was confirmed in you:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> So that ye come behind in no <a href="../ebd/ebd148.htm#005">gift</a>; waiting for the coming of our Lord <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> Who shall also confirm you unto the end, that ye may be blameless in the day of our Lord <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> God is <a href="../ebd/ebd130.htm#005">faithful</a>, by whom ye were called unto the <a href="../ebd/ebd132.htm#002">fellowship</a> of his Son <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a> our Lord.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> Now I beseech you, brethren, by the name of our Lord <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a>, that ye all speak the same thing, and that there be no divisions among you; but that ye be perfectly joined together in the same mind and in the same judgment.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> For it hath been declared unto me of you, my brethren, by them which are of the house of <a href="../ebd/ebd081.htm#005">Chloe</a>, that there are contentions among you.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> Now this I say, that every one of you saith, I am of <a href="../ebd/ebd287.htm#002">Paul</a>; and I of <a href="../ebd/ebd026.htm#006">Apollos</a>; and I of <a href="../ebd/ebd075.htm#005">Cephas</a>; and I of <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> Is <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a> divided? was <a href="../ebd/ebd287.htm#002">Paul</a> crucified for you? or were ye baptized in the name of <a href="../ebd/ebd287.htm#002">Paul</a>?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> I thank God that I baptized none of you, but <a href="../ebd/ebd092.htm#009">Crispus</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd141.htm#003">Gaius</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> Lest any should say that I had baptized in mine own name.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> And I baptized also the household of <a href="../ebd/ebd351.htm#001">Stephanas</a>: besides, I know not whether I baptized any other.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> For <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a> sent me not to baptize, but to preach the <a href="../ebd/ebd153.htm#003">gospel</a>: not with wisdom of words, lest the <a href="../ebd/ebd093.htm#000">cross</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a> should be made of none effect.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> For the preaching of the <a href="../ebd/ebd093.htm#000">cross</a> is to them that perish foolishness; but unto us which are saved it is the power of God.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> For it is written, I will destroy the wisdom of the <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#004">wise</a>, and will bring to nothing the understanding of the prudent.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> Where is the <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#004">wise</a>? where is the scribe? where is the disputer of this world? hath not God made foolish the wisdom of this world?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> For after that in the wisdom of God the world by wisdom knew not God, it pleased God by the foolishness of preaching to save them that believe.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> For the Jews require a sign, and the Greeks seek after wisdom:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> But we preach <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a> crucified, unto the Jews a stumblingblock, and unto the Greeks foolishness;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> But unto them which are called, both Jews and Greeks, <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a> the power of God, and the wisdom of God.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> Because the foolishness of God is wiser than men; and the weakness of God is stronger than men.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> For ye see your <a href="../ebd/ebd069.htm#007">calling</a>, brethren, how that not many <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#004">wise</a> men after the <a href="../ebd/ebd135.htm#007">flesh</a>, not many mighty, not many noble, are called:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> But God hath <a href="../ebd/ebd081.htm#008">chosen</a> the foolish things of the world to confound the <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#004">wise</a>; and God hath <a href="../ebd/ebd081.htm#008">chosen</a> the weak things of the world to confound the things which are mighty;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="028">28</a> And base things of the world, and things which are despised, hath God <a href="../ebd/ebd081.htm#008">chosen</a>, yea, and things which are not, to bring to nought things that are:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="029">29</a> That no <a href="../ebd/ebd135.htm#007">flesh</a> should <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a> in his presence.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="030">30</a> But of him are ye in <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a>, who of God is made unto us wisdom, and <a href="../ebd/ebd313.htm#004">righteousness</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd321.htm#004">sanctification</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd308.htm#009">redemption</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="031">31</a> That, according as it is written, He that glorieth, let him <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a> in the Lord.<br>
 </p>
