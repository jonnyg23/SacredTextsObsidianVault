 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Zechariah Chapter 9</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> The <a href="../ebd/ebd066.htm#007">burden</a> of the word of the LORD in the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd158.htm#002">Hadrach</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd096.htm#007">Damascus</a> shall be the rest thereof: when the eyes of man, as of all the tribes of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, shall be toward the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd160.htm#005">Hamath</a> also shall border thereby; Tyrus, and <a href="../ebd/ebd393.htm#000">Zidon</a>, though it be very <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#004">wise</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And Tyrus did build herself a strong hold, and heaped up <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#000">silver</a> as the <a href="../ebd/ebd109.htm#008">dust</a>, and fine <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a> as the mire of the streets.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Behold, the LORD will cast her out, and he will smite her power in the sea; and she shall be devoured with fire.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd034.htm#001">Ashkelon</a> shall see it, and fear; <a href="../ebd/ebd144.htm#003">Gaza</a> also shall see it, and be very sorrowful, and <a href="../ebd/ebd114.htm#001">Ekron</a>; for her expectation shall be ashamed; and the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> shall perish from <a href="../ebd/ebd144.htm#003">Gaza</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd034.htm#001">Ashkelon</a> shall not be inhabited.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And a <a href="../ebd/ebd047.htm#000">bastard</a> shall dwell in <a href="../ebd/ebd033.htm#006">Ashdod</a>, and I will cut off the pride of the <a href="../ebd/ebd294.htm#002">Philistines</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And I will take away his <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> out of his mouth, and his abominations from between his teeth: but he that remaineth, even he, shall be for our God, and he shall be as a governor in <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd114.htm#001">Ekron</a> as a Jebusite.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And I will <a href="../ebd/ebd120.htm#003">encamp</a> about mine house because of the army, because of him that passeth by, and because of him that returneth: and no oppressor shall pass through them any more: for now have I seen with mine eyes.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> Rejoice greatly, O daughter of <a href="../ebd/ebd394.htm#002">Zion</a>; shout, O daughter of <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>: behold, thy <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">King</a> cometh unto thee: he is just, and having <a href="../ebd/ebd320.htm#003">salvation</a>; lowly, and riding upon an <a href="../ebd/ebd035.htm#001">ass</a>, and upon a colt the foal of an <a href="../ebd/ebd035.htm#001">ass</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And I will cut off the <a href="../ebd/ebd077.htm#008">chariot</a> from <a href="../ebd/ebd123.htm#000">Ephraim</a>, and the horse from <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>, and the battle <a href="../ebd/ebd063.htm#003">bow</a> shall be cut off: and he shall speak peace unto the <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#005">heathen</a>: and his dominion shall be from sea even to sea, and from the river even to the ends of the earth.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> As for thee also, by the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> of thy <a href="../ebd/ebd091.htm#008">covenant</a> I have sent forth thy prisoners out of the <a href="../ebd/ebd296.htm#007">pit</a> wherein is no water.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> Turn you to the strong hold, ye prisoners of hope: even to day do I declare that I will render double unto thee;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> When I have bent <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a> for me, filled the <a href="../ebd/ebd063.htm#003">bow</a> with <a href="../ebd/ebd123.htm#000">Ephraim</a>, and raised up thy sons, O <a href="../ebd/ebd394.htm#002">Zion</a>, against thy sons, O <a href="../ebd/ebd155.htm#003">Greece</a>, and made thee as the <a href="../ebd/ebd354.htm#003">sword</a> of a mighty man.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> And the LORD shall be seen over them, and his arrow shall go forth as the <a href="../ebd/ebd229.htm#002">lightning</a>: and the LORD God shall blow the trumpet, and shall go with whirlwinds of the south.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> The LORD of hosts shall defend them; and they shall devour, and subdue with <a href="../ebd/ebd346.htm#005">sling</a> stones; and they shall <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a>, and make a noise as through <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#000">wine</a>; and they shall be filled like bowls, and as the corners of the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> And the LORD their God shall save them in that day as the flock of his people: for they shall be as the stones of a <a href="../ebd/ebd093.htm#001">crown</a>, lifted up as an <a href="../ebd/ebd121.htm#008">ensign</a> upon his land.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> For how great is his <a href="../ebd/ebd153.htm#000">goodness</a>, and how great is his beauty! <a href="../ebd/ebd090.htm#006">corn</a> shall make the young men cheerful, and new <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#000">wine</a> the maids.<br>
 </p>
