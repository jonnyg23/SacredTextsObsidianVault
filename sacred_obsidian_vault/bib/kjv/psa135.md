 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 135</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Praise ye the LORD. Praise ye the name of the LORD; praise him, O ye servants of the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Ye that stand in the house of the LORD, in the courts of the house of our God.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Praise the LORD; for the LORD is good: sing praises unto his name; for it is pleasant.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> For the LORD hath <a href="../ebd/ebd081.htm#008">chosen</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd194.htm#007">Jacob</a> unto himself, and <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> for his <a href="../ebd/ebd287.htm#008">peculiar</a> treasure.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> For I know that the LORD is great, and that our Lord is above all gods.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Whatsoever the LORD pleased, that did he in <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#006">heaven</a>, and in earth, in the seas, and all <a href="../ebd/ebd100.htm#008">deep</a> places.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> He causeth the vapours to ascend from the ends of the earth; he maketh lightnings for the <a href="../ebd/ebd305.htm#008">rain</a>; he bringeth the wind out of his treasuries.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> Who smote the firstborn of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a>, both of man and <a href="../ebd/ebd049.htm#001">beast</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> Who sent tokens and wonders into the midst of thee, O <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a>, upon <a href="../ebd/ebd292.htm#005">Pharaoh</a>, and upon all his servants.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> Who smote great nations, and slew mighty kings;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd343.htm#002">Sihon</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd021.htm#006">Amorites</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#002">Og</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd046.htm#003">Bashan</a>, and all the kingdoms of <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#005">Canaan</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And gave their land for an heritage, an heritage unto <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> his people.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> Thy name, O LORD, endureth for ever; and thy memorial, O LORD, throughout all generations.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> For the LORD will <a href="../ebd/ebd213.htm#005">judge</a> his people, and he will repent himself concerning his servants.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> The idols of the <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#005">heathen</a> are <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#000">silver</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a>, the work of men's hands.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> They have mouths, but they speak not; eyes have they, but they see not;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> They have ears, but they hear not; neither is there any breath in their mouths.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> They that make them are like unto them: so is every one that trusteth in them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd060.htm#008">Bless</a> the LORD, O house of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>: <a href="../ebd/ebd060.htm#008">bless</a> the LORD, O house of <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd060.htm#008">Bless</a> the LORD, O house of <a href="../ebd/ebd227.htm#006">Levi</a>: ye that fear the LORD, <a href="../ebd/ebd060.htm#008">bless</a> the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> Blessed be the LORD out of <a href="../ebd/ebd394.htm#002">Zion</a>, which dwelleth at <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>. Praise ye the LORD.<br>
 </p>
