 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Numbers Chapter 7</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> And it came to pass on the day that <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> had fully set up the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a>, and had anointed it, and sanctified it, and all the instruments thereof, both the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> and all the vessels thereof, and had anointed them, and sanctified them;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> That the princes of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, heads of the house of their fathers, who were the princes of the tribes, and were over them that were numbered, offered:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And they brought their <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> before the LORD, six covered wagons, and twelve oxen; a <a href="../ebd/ebd378.htm#005">wagon</a> for two of the princes, and for each one an <a href="../ebd/ebd281.htm#007">ox</a>: and they brought them before the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And the LORD spake unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Take it of them, that they may be to do the service of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>; and thou shalt give them unto the Levites, to every man according to his service.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> took the wagons and the oxen, and gave them unto the Levites.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Two wagons and four oxen he gave unto the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd146.htm#009">Gershon</a>, according to their service:<br>
 </p>

 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> But unto the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd222.htm#001">Kohath</a> he gave none: because the service of the <a href="../ebd/ebd321.htm#005">sanctuary</a> belonging unto them was that they should bear upon their shoulders.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And the princes offered for dedicating of the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> in the day that it was anointed, even the princes offered their <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> before the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>.<br>
 </p>
