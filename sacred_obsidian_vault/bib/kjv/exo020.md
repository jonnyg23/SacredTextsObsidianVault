 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Exodus Chapter 20</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> And God spake all these words, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> I am the LORD thy God, which have brought thee out of the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a>, out of the house of <a href="../ebd/ebd062.htm#002">bondage</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Thou shalt have no other gods before me.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Thou shalt not make unto thee any graven image, or any likeness of any thing that is in <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#006">heaven</a> above, or that is in the earth beneath, or that is in the water under the earth.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Thou shalt not <a href="../ebd/ebd063.htm#003">bow</a> down thyself to them, nor serve them: for I the LORD thy God am a jealous God, visiting the iniquity of the fathers upon the children unto the third and fourth <a href="../ebd/ebd145.htm#008">generation</a> of them that hate me;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And shewing <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> unto <a href="../ebd/ebd364.htm#008">thousands</a> of them that love me, and keep my commandments.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Thou shalt not take the name of the LORD thy God in vain; for the LORD will not hold him guiltless that taketh his name in vain.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> Remember the <a href="../ebd/ebd317.htm#003">sabbath</a> day, to keep it holy.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> Six days shalt thou labour, and do all thy work:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> But the seventh day is the <a href="../ebd/ebd317.htm#003">sabbath</a> of the LORD thy God: in it thou shalt not do any work, thou, nor thy son, nor thy daughter, thy manservant, nor thy maidservant, nor thy <a href="../ebd/ebd074.htm#002">cattle</a>, nor thy <a href="../ebd/ebd351.htm#009">stranger</a> that is within thy gates:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> For in six days the LORD made <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#006">heaven</a> and earth, the sea, and all that in them is, and rested the seventh day: wherefore the LORD blessed the <a href="../ebd/ebd317.htm#003">sabbath</a> day, and hallowed it.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> Honour thy father and thy mother: that thy days may be long upon the land which the LORD thy God giveth thee.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> Thou shalt not kill.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> Thou shalt not commit <a href="../ebd/ebd010.htm#009">adultery</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> Thou shalt not steal.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> Thou shalt not bear false <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#008">witness</a> against thy neighbour.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> Thou shalt not covet thy neighbour's house, thou shalt not covet thy neighbour's wife, nor his manservant, nor his maidservant, nor his <a href="../ebd/ebd281.htm#007">ox</a>, nor his <a href="../ebd/ebd035.htm#001">ass</a>, nor any thing that is thy neighbour's.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> And all the people saw the thunderings, and the lightnings, and the noise of the trumpet, and the mountain smoking: and when the people saw it, they removed, and stood afar off.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> And they said unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, Speak thou with us, and we will hear: but let not God speak with us, lest we die.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> said unto the people, Fear not: for God is come to prove you, and that his fear may be before your faces, that ye <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> not.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> And the people stood afar off, and <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> drew near unto the thick <a href="../ebd/ebd097.htm#008">darkness</a> where God was.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> And the LORD said unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, Thus thou shalt say unto the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, Ye have seen that I have talked with you from <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#006">heaven</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> Ye shall not make with me gods of <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#000">silver</a>, neither shall ye make unto you gods of <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> An <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> of earth thou shalt make unto me, and shalt <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#002">sacrifice</a> thereon thy burnt offerings, and thy peace offerings, thy <a href="../ebd/ebd333.htm#005">sheep</a>, and thine oxen: in all places where I record my name I will come unto thee, and I will <a href="../ebd/ebd060.htm#008">bless</a> thee.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> And if thou wilt make me an <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd351.htm#005">stone</a>, thou shalt not build it of hewn <a href="../ebd/ebd351.htm#005">stone</a>: for if thou lift up thy tool upon it, thou hast polluted it.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> Neither shalt thou go up by steps unto mine <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>, that thy nakedness be not discovered thereon.<br>
 </p>
