 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: 1 Chronicles Chapter 14</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Now <a href="../ebd/ebd179.htm#003">Hiram</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd374.htm#001">Tyre</a> sent messengers to <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a>, and timber of cedars, with masons and carpenters, to build him an house.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> perceived that the LORD had confirmed him <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> over <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, for his kingdom was lifted up on high, because of his people <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> took more wives at <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>: and <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> begat more sons and daughters.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Now these are the names of his children which he had in <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>; <a href="../ebd/ebd331.htm#003">Shammua</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd340.htm#001">Shobab</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd267.htm#002">Nathan</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd347.htm#007">Solomon</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd185.htm#008">Ibhar</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd118.htm#004">Elishua</a>, and Elpalet,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd274.htm#008">Nogah</a>, and Nepheg, and <a href="../ebd/ebd197.htm#003">Japhia</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd118.htm#001">Elishama</a>, and Beeliada, and <a href="../ebd/ebd117.htm#004">Eliphalet</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And when the <a href="../ebd/ebd294.htm#002">Philistines</a> heard that <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> was anointed <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> over all <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, all the <a href="../ebd/ebd294.htm#002">Philistines</a> went up to seek <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a>. And <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> heard of it, and went out against them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd294.htm#002">Philistines</a> came and spread themselves in the <a href="../ebd/ebd376.htm#008">valley</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd311.htm#000">Rephaim</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> enquired of God, saying, Shall I go up against the <a href="../ebd/ebd294.htm#002">Philistines</a>? And wilt thou deliver them into mine hand? And the LORD said unto him, Go up; for I will deliver them into thine hand.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> So they came up to Baalperazim; and <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> smote them there. Then <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> said, God hath broken in upon mine enemies by mine hand like the breaking forth of waters: therefore they called the name of that place Baalperazim.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And when they had left their gods there, <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> gave a commandment, and they were burned with fire.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd294.htm#002">Philistines</a> yet again spread themselves abroad in the <a href="../ebd/ebd376.htm#008">valley</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> Therefore <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> enquired again of God; and God said unto him, Go not up after them; turn away from them, and come upon them over against the <a href="../ebd/ebd262.htm#000">mulberry</a> trees.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And it shall be, when thou shalt hear a sound of going in the tops of the <a href="../ebd/ebd262.htm#000">mulberry</a> trees, that then thou shalt go out to battle: for God is gone forth before thee to smite the <a href="../ebd/ebd183.htm#007">host</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd294.htm#002">Philistines</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> therefore did as God commanded him: and they smote the <a href="../ebd/ebd183.htm#007">host</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd294.htm#002">Philistines</a> from <a href="../ebd/ebd148.htm#002">Gibeon</a> even to Gazer.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> And the fame of <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> went out into all lands; and the LORD brought the fear of him upon all nations.<br>
 </p>
