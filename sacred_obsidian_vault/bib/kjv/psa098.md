 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 98</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> O sing unto the LORD a new song; for he hath done marvellous things: his right hand, and his holy arm, hath gotten him the victory.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> The LORD hath made known his <a href="../ebd/ebd320.htm#003">salvation</a>: his <a href="../ebd/ebd313.htm#004">righteousness</a> hath he openly shewed in the sight of the <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#005">heathen</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> He hath remembered his <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> and his truth toward the house of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>: all the ends of the earth have seen the <a href="../ebd/ebd320.htm#003">salvation</a> of our God.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Make a joyful noise unto the LORD, all the earth: make a loud noise, and rejoice, and sing praise.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Sing unto the LORD with the <a href="../ebd/ebd165.htm#001">harp</a>; with the <a href="../ebd/ebd165.htm#001">harp</a>, and the voice of a psalm.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> With <a href="../ebd/ebd373.htm#001">trumpets</a> and sound of <a href="../ebd/ebd090.htm#009">cornet</a> make a joyful noise before the LORD, the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">King</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Let the sea roar, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd139.htm#007">fulness</a> thereof; the world, and they that dwell therein.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> Let the floods clap their hands: let the hills be joyful together<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> Before the LORD; for he cometh to <a href="../ebd/ebd213.htm#005">judge</a> the earth: with <a href="../ebd/ebd313.htm#004">righteousness</a> shall he <a href="../ebd/ebd213.htm#005">judge</a> the world, and the people with equity.<br>
 </p>
