 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Micah Chapter 1</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> The word of the LORD that came to <a href="../ebd/ebd252.htm#005">Micah</a> the Morasthite in the days of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#000">Jotham</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd013.htm#005">Ahaz</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd177.htm#003">Hezekiah</a>, kings of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>, which he saw concerning <a href="../ebd/ebd320.htm#004">Samaria</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Hear, all ye people; hearken, O earth, and all that therein is: and let the Lord GOD be <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#008">witness</a> against you, the LORD from his holy <a href="../ebd/ebd361.htm#004">temple</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> For, behold, the LORD cometh forth out of his place, and will come down, and tread upon the high places of the earth.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And the mountains shall be molten under him, and the valleys shall be cleft, as <a href="../ebd/ebd380.htm#000">wax</a> before the fire, and as the waters that are poured down a steep place.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> For the transgression of <a href="../ebd/ebd194.htm#007">Jacob</a> is all this, and for the sins of the house of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>. What is the transgression of <a href="../ebd/ebd194.htm#007">Jacob</a>? is it not <a href="../ebd/ebd320.htm#004">Samaria</a>? and what are the high places of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>? are they not <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Therefore I will make <a href="../ebd/ebd320.htm#004">Samaria</a> as an <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#000">heap</a> of the field, and as plantings of a vineyard: and I will pour down the stones thereof into the <a href="../ebd/ebd376.htm#008">valley</a>, and I will discover the foundations thereof.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And all the graven images thereof shall be beaten to <a href="../ebd/ebd295.htm#002">pieces</a>, and all the hires thereof shall be burned with the fire, and all the idols thereof will I lay desolate: for she gathered it of the hire of an <a href="../ebd/ebd164.htm#005">harlot</a>, and they shall return to the hire of an <a href="../ebd/ebd164.htm#005">harlot</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> Therefore I will wail and howl, I will go stripped and <a href="../ebd/ebd266.htm#003">naked</a>: I will make a wailing like the dragons, and mourning as the owls.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> For her wound is incurable; for it is come unto <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>; he is come unto the <a href="../ebd/ebd143.htm#008">gate</a> of my people, even to <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> Declare ye it not at <a href="../ebd/ebd143.htm#009">Gath</a>, weep ye not at all: in the house of Aphrah <a href="../ebd/ebd315.htm#004">roll</a> thyself in the <a href="../ebd/ebd109.htm#008">dust</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> Pass ye away, thou inhabitant of <a href="../ebd/ebd322.htm#000">Saphir</a>, having thy shame <a href="../ebd/ebd266.htm#003">naked</a>: the inhabitant of <a href="../ebd/ebd385.htm#002">Zaanan</a> came not forth in the mourning of Bethezel; he shall receive of you his standing.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> For the inhabitant of <a href="../ebd/ebd242.htm#003">Maroth</a> waited carefully for good: but evil came down from the LORD unto the <a href="../ebd/ebd143.htm#008">gate</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> O thou inhabitant of <a href="../ebd/ebd222.htm#009">Lachish</a>, bind the <a href="../ebd/ebd077.htm#008">chariot</a> to the swift <a href="../ebd/ebd049.htm#001">beast</a>: she is the beginning of the <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> to the daughter of <a href="../ebd/ebd394.htm#002">Zion</a>: for the transgressions of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> were found in thee.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> Therefore shalt thou give presents to Moreshethgath: the houses of <a href="../ebd/ebd007.htm#003">Achzib</a> shall be a lie to the kings of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> Yet will I bring an <a href="../ebd/ebd171.htm#007">heir</a> unto thee, O inhabitant of <a href="../ebd/ebd241.htm#009">Mareshah</a>: he shall come unto <a href="../ebd/ebd010.htm#007">Adullam</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> Make thee bald, and poll thee for thy delicate children; enlarge thy <a href="../ebd/ebd042.htm#008">baldness</a> as the <a href="../ebd/ebd110.htm#003">eagle</a>; for they are gone into <a href="../ebd/ebd072.htm#002">captivity</a> from thee.<br>
 </p>
