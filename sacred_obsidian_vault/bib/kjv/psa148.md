 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 148</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Praise ye the LORD. Praise ye the LORD from the heavens: praise him in the heights.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Praise ye him, all his angels: praise ye him, all his hosts.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Praise ye him, <a href="../ebd/ebd353.htm#001">sun</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd259.htm#004">moon</a>: praise him, all ye <a href="../ebd/ebd350.htm#008">stars</a> of light.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Praise him, ye heavens of heavens, and ye waters that be above the heavens.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Let them praise the name of the LORD: for he commanded, and they were created.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> He hath also stablished them for ever and ever: he hath made a decree which shall not pass.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Praise the LORD from the earth, ye dragons, and all deeps:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> Fire, and <a href="../ebd/ebd159.htm#002">hail</a>; <a href="../ebd/ebd347.htm#000">snow</a>, and vapours; stormy wind fulfilling his word:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> Mountains, and all hills; fruitful trees, and all cedars:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> Beasts, and all <a href="../ebd/ebd074.htm#002">cattle</a>; creeping things, and flying fowl:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> Kings of the earth, and all people; princes, and all judges of the earth:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> Both young men, and maidens; old men, and children:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> Let them praise the name of the LORD: for his name alone is excellent; his <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a> is above the earth and <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#006">heaven</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> He also exalteth the <a href="../ebd/ebd182.htm#003">horn</a> of his people, the praise of all his saints; even of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, a people near unto him. Praise ye the LORD.<br>
 </p>
