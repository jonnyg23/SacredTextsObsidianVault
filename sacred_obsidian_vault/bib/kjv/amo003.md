 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Amos Chapter 3</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Hear this word that the LORD hath spoken against you, O children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, against the whole family which I brought up from the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a>, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> You only have I known of all the families of the earth: therefore I will punish you for all your iniquities.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Can two walk together, except they be agreed?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Will a lion roar in the <a href="../ebd/ebd137.htm#006">forest</a>, when he hath no prey? will a young lion cry out of his <a href="../ebd/ebd101.htm#007">den</a>, if he have taken nothing?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Can a <a href="../ebd/ebd059.htm#000">bird</a> fall in a <a href="../ebd/ebd346.htm#009">snare</a> upon the earth, where no <a href="../ebd/ebd149.htm#004">gin</a> is for him?  shall one take up a <a href="../ebd/ebd346.htm#009">snare</a> from the earth, and have taken nothing at all?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Shall a trumpet be blown in the city, and the people not be afraid?  shall there be evil in a city, and the LORD hath not done it?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Surely the Lord GOD will do nothing, but he revealeth his secret unto his servants the prophets.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> The lion hath roared, who will not fear? the Lord GOD hath spoken, who can but prophesy?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> Publish in the palaces at <a href="../ebd/ebd033.htm#006">Ashdod</a>, and in the palaces in the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a>, and say, Assemble yourselves upon the mountains of <a href="../ebd/ebd320.htm#004">Samaria</a>, and behold the great tumults in the midst thereof, and the oppressed in the midst thereof.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> For they know not to do right, saith the LORD, who store up violence and <a href="../ebd/ebd315.htm#000">robbery</a> in their palaces.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> Therefore thus saith the Lord GOD; An <a href="../ebd/ebd011.htm#001">adversary</a> there shall be even round about the land; and he shall bring down thy strength from thee, and thy palaces shall be spoiled.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> Thus saith the LORD; As the <a href="../ebd/ebd335.htm#006">shepherd</a> taketh out of the mouth of the lion two legs, or a piece of an <a href="../ebd/ebd110.htm#004">ear</a>; so shall the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> be taken out that dwell in <a href="../ebd/ebd320.htm#004">Samaria</a> in the corner of a bed, and in <a href="../ebd/ebd096.htm#007">Damascus</a> in a <a href="../ebd/ebd091.htm#002">couch</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> Hear ye, and testify in the house of <a href="../ebd/ebd194.htm#007">Jacob</a>, saith the Lord GOD, the God of hosts,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> That in the day that I shall visit the transgressions of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> upon him I will also visit the altars of <a href="../ebd/ebd055.htm#006">Bethel</a>: and the horns of the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> shall be cut off, and fall to the ground.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And I will smite the winter house with the summer house; and the houses of <a href="../ebd/ebd192.htm#000">ivory</a> shall perish, and the great houses shall have an end, saith the LORD.<br>
 </p>
