 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Matthew Chapter 23</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Then spake <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a> to the multitude, and to his disciples,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Saying The <a href="../ebd/ebd324.htm#003">scribes</a> and the <a href="../ebd/ebd292.htm#008">Pharisees</a> sit in Moses' seat:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> All therefore whatsoever they bid you observe, that observe and do; but do not ye after their works: for they say, and do not.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> For they bind heavy burdens and grievous to be borne, and lay them on men's shoulders; but they themselves will not move them with one of their fingers.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> But all their works they do for to be seen of men: they make broad their <a href="../ebd/ebd294.htm#009">phylacteries</a>, and enlarge the borders of their garments,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And love the uppermost rooms at feasts, and the chief seats in the synagogues,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And greetings in the markets, and to be called of men, <a href="../ebd/ebd304.htm#006">Rabbi</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd304.htm#006">Rabbi</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> But be not ye called <a href="../ebd/ebd304.htm#006">Rabbi</a>: for one is your Master, even <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a>; and all ye are brethren.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And call no man your father upon the earth: for one is your Father, which is in <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#006">heaven</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> Neither be ye called masters: for one is your Master, even <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> But he that is greatest among you shall be your servant.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And whosoever shall exalt himself shall be abased; and he that shall humble himself shall be exalted.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> But woe unto you, <a href="../ebd/ebd324.htm#003">scribes</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd292.htm#008">Pharisees</a>, hypocrites! for ye shut up the kingdom of <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#006">heaven</a> against men: for ye neither go in yourselves, neither suffer ye them that are entering to go in.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> Woe unto you, <a href="../ebd/ebd324.htm#003">scribes</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd292.htm#008">Pharisees</a>, hypocrites! for ye devour widows' houses, and for a pretence make long <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#000">prayer</a>: therefore ye shall receive the greater <a href="../ebd/ebd096.htm#008">damnation</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> Woe unto you, <a href="../ebd/ebd324.htm#003">scribes</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd292.htm#008">Pharisees</a>, hypocrites! for ye compass sea and land to make one <a href="../ebd/ebd301.htm#001">proselyte</a>, and when he is made, ye make him twofold more the child of <a href="../ebd/ebd173.htm#003">hell</a> than yourselves.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> Woe unto you, ye <a href="../ebd/ebd060.htm#009">blind</a> guides, which say, Whosoever shall swear by the <a href="../ebd/ebd361.htm#004">temple</a>, it is nothing; but whosoever shall swear by the <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd361.htm#004">temple</a>, he is a <a href="../ebd/ebd100.htm#000">debtor</a>!<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> Ye fools and <a href="../ebd/ebd060.htm#009">blind</a>: for whether is greater, the <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a>, or the <a href="../ebd/ebd361.htm#004">temple</a> that sanctifieth the <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a>?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> And, Whosoever shall swear by the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>, it is nothing; but whosoever sweareth by the <a href="../ebd/ebd148.htm#005">gift</a> that is upon it, he is guilty.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> Ye fools and <a href="../ebd/ebd060.htm#009">blind</a>: for whether is greater, the <a href="../ebd/ebd148.htm#005">gift</a>, or the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> that sanctifieth the <a href="../ebd/ebd148.htm#005">gift</a>?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> Whoso therefore shall swear by the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>, sweareth by it, and by all things thereon.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> And whoso shall swear by the <a href="../ebd/ebd361.htm#004">temple</a>, sweareth by it, and by him that dwelleth therein.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> And he that shall swear by <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#006">heaven</a>, sweareth by the <a href="../ebd/ebd365.htm#001">throne</a> of God, and by him that sitteth thereon.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> Woe unto you, <a href="../ebd/ebd324.htm#003">scribes</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd292.htm#008">Pharisees</a>, hypocrites! for ye pay <a href="../ebd/ebd368.htm#005">tithe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd256.htm#001">mint</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd024.htm#005">anise</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd093.htm#009">cummin</a>, and have omitted the weightier matters of the law, judgment, <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a>, and faith: these ought ye to have done, and not to leave the other undone.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> Ye <a href="../ebd/ebd060.htm#009">blind</a> guides, which strain at a <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#009">gnat</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd353.htm#009">swallow</a> a <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#000">camel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> Woe unto you, <a href="../ebd/ebd324.htm#003">scribes</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd292.htm#008">Pharisees</a>, hypocrites! for ye make <a href="../ebd/ebd084.htm#005">clean</a> the outside of the <a href="../ebd/ebd094.htm#000">cup</a> and of the platter, but within they are full of extortion and excess.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> Thou <a href="../ebd/ebd060.htm#009">blind</a> Pharisee, cleanse first that which is within the <a href="../ebd/ebd094.htm#000">cup</a> and platter, that the outside of them may be <a href="../ebd/ebd084.htm#005">clean</a> also.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> Woe unto you, <a href="../ebd/ebd324.htm#003">scribes</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd292.htm#008">Pharisees</a>, hypocrites! for ye are like unto whited sepulchres, which indeed appear beautiful outward, but are within full of dead men's bones, and of all uncleanness.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="028">28</a> Even so ye also outwardly appear righteous unto men, but within ye are full of hypocrisy and iniquity.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="029">29</a> Woe unto you, <a href="../ebd/ebd324.htm#003">scribes</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd292.htm#008">Pharisees</a>, hypocrites! because ye build the <a href="../ebd/ebd370.htm#001">tombs</a> of the prophets, and <a href="../ebd/ebd143.htm#006">garnish</a> the sepulchres of the righteous,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="030">30</a> And say, If we had been in the days of our fathers, we would not have been partakers with them in the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> of the prophets.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="031">31</a> Wherefore ye be witnesses unto yourselves, that ye are the children of them which killed the prophets.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="032">32</a> Fill ye up then the measure of your fathers.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="033">33</a> Ye serpents, ye <a href="../ebd/ebd145.htm#008">generation</a> of vipers, how can ye escape the <a href="../ebd/ebd096.htm#008">damnation</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd173.htm#003">hell</a>?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="034">34</a> Wherefore, behold, I send unto you prophets, and <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#004">wise</a> men, and <a href="../ebd/ebd324.htm#003">scribes</a>: and some of them ye shall kill and crucify; and some of them shall ye scourge in your synagogues, and persecute them from city to city:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="035">35</a> That upon you may come all the righteous <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> shed upon the earth, from the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> of righteous <a href="../ebd/ebd001.htm#004">Abel</a> unto the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd386.htm#006">Zacharias</a> son of <a href="../ebd/ebd044.htm#004">Barachias</a>, whom ye slew between the <a href="../ebd/ebd361.htm#004">temple</a> and the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="036">36</a> Verily I say unto you, All these things shall come upon this <a href="../ebd/ebd145.htm#008">generation</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="037">37</a> O <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>, thou that killest the prophets, and stonest them which are sent unto thee, how often would I have gathered thy children together, even as a <a href="../ebd/ebd174.htm#002">hen</a> gathereth her chickens under her wings, and ye would not!<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="038">38</a> Behold, your house is left unto you desolate.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="039">39</a> For I say unto you, Ye shall not see me henceforth, till ye shall say, Blessed is he that cometh in the name of the Lord.<br>
 </p>
