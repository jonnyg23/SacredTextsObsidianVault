 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 61</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Hear my cry, O God; attend unto my <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#000">prayer</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> From the end of the earth will I cry unto thee, when my heart is overwhelmed: lead me to the <a href="../ebd/ebd315.htm#001">rock</a> that is higher than I.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> For thou hast been a shelter for me, and a strong tower from the enemy.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> I will abide in thy <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> for ever: I will trust in the covert of thy wings. <a href="../ebd/ebd326.htm#008">Selah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> For thou, O God, hast heard my <a href="../ebd/ebd378.htm#001">vows</a>: thou hast given me the heritage of those that fear thy name.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Thou wilt prolong the king's life: and his years as many generations.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> He shall abide before God for ever: O prepare <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> and truth, which may preserve him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> So will I sing praise unto thy name for ever, that I may daily perform my <a href="../ebd/ebd378.htm#001">vows</a>.<br>
 </p>
