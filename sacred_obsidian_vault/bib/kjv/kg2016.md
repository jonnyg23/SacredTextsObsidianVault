 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: 4 Kings (2 Kings) Chapter 16</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> In the seventeenth year of <a href="../ebd/ebd288.htm#002">Pekah</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd310.htm#003">Remaliah</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd013.htm#005">Ahaz</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#000">Jotham</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a> began to reign.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Twenty years old was <a href="../ebd/ebd013.htm#005">Ahaz</a> when he began to reign, and reigned sixteen years in <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>, and did not that which was right in the sight of the LORD his God, like <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> his father.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> But he walked in the way of the kings of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, yea, and made his son to pass through the fire, according to the abominations of the <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#005">heathen</a>, whom the LORD cast out from before the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And he sacrificed and burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd188.htm#002">incense</a> in the high places, and on the hills, and under every green tree.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Then <a href="../ebd/ebd312.htm#006">Rezin</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd355.htm#002">Syria</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd288.htm#002">Pekah</a> son of <a href="../ebd/ebd310.htm#003">Remaliah</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> came up to <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a> to war: and they besieged <a href="../ebd/ebd013.htm#005">Ahaz</a>, but could not overcome him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> At that time <a href="../ebd/ebd312.htm#006">Rezin</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd355.htm#002">Syria</a> recovered <a href="../ebd/ebd114.htm#005">Elath</a> to <a href="../ebd/ebd355.htm#002">Syria</a>, and drave the Jews from <a href="../ebd/ebd114.htm#005">Elath</a>: and the Syrians came to <a href="../ebd/ebd114.htm#005">Elath</a>, and dwelt there unto this day.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> So <a href="../ebd/ebd013.htm#005">Ahaz</a> sent messengers to Tiglathpileser <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd035.htm#005">Assyria</a>, saying, I am thy servant and thy son: come up, and save me out of the hand of the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd355.htm#002">Syria</a>, and out of the hand of the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, which rise up against me.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd013.htm#005">Ahaz</a> took the <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#000">silver</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a> that was found in the house of the LORD, and in the treasures of the king's house, and sent it for a present to the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd035.htm#005">Assyria</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd035.htm#005">Assyria</a> hearkened unto him: for the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd035.htm#005">Assyria</a> went up against <a href="../ebd/ebd096.htm#007">Damascus</a>, and took it, and carried the people of it <a href="../ebd/ebd072.htm#001">captive</a> to <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#007">Kir</a>, and slew <a href="../ebd/ebd312.htm#006">Rezin</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd013.htm#005">Ahaz</a> went to <a href="../ebd/ebd096.htm#007">Damascus</a> to meet Tiglathpileser <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd035.htm#005">Assyria</a>, and saw an <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> that was at <a href="../ebd/ebd096.htm#007">Damascus</a>: and <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd013.htm#005">Ahaz</a> sent to <a href="../ebd/ebd375.htm#004">Urijah</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> the fashion of the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>, and the pattern of it, according to all the workmanship thereof.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd375.htm#004">Urijah</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> built an <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> according to all that <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd013.htm#005">Ahaz</a> had sent from <a href="../ebd/ebd096.htm#007">Damascus</a>: so <a href="../ebd/ebd375.htm#004">Urijah</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> made it against <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd013.htm#005">Ahaz</a> came from <a href="../ebd/ebd096.htm#007">Damascus</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And when the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> was come from <a href="../ebd/ebd096.htm#007">Damascus</a>, the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> saw the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>: and the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> approached to the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>, and offered thereon.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> And he burnt his burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> and his meat <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, and poured his <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, and sprinkled the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> of his peace offerings, upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> And he brought also the brasen <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>, which was before the LORD, from the forefront of the house, from between the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> and the house of the LORD, and put it on the north side of the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd013.htm#005">Ahaz</a> commanded <a href="../ebd/ebd375.htm#004">Urijah</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a>, saying, Upon the great <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> burn the morning burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, and the evening meat <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, and the king's burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#002">sacrifice</a>, and his meat <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, with the burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> of all the people of the land, and their meat <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, and their <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a> offerings; and sprinkle upon it all the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> of the burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, and all the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#002">sacrifice</a>: and the brasen <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> shall be for me to enquire by.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> Thus did <a href="../ebd/ebd375.htm#004">Urijah</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a>, according to all that <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd013.htm#005">Ahaz</a> commanded.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd013.htm#005">Ahaz</a> cut off the borders of the bases, and removed the <a href="../ebd/ebd224.htm#009">laver</a> from off them; and took down the sea from off the brasen oxen that were under it, and put it upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd287.htm#003">pavement</a> of stones.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> And the covert for the <a href="../ebd/ebd317.htm#003">sabbath</a> that they had built in the house, and the king's entry without, turned he from the house of the LORD for the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd035.htm#005">Assyria</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> Now the rest of the acts of <a href="../ebd/ebd013.htm#005">Ahaz</a> which he did, are they not written in the book of the <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#003">chronicles</a> of the kings of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd013.htm#005">Ahaz</a> slept with his fathers, and was buried with his fathers in the city of <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a>: and <a href="../ebd/ebd177.htm#003">Hezekiah</a> his son reigned in his stead.<br>
 </p>
