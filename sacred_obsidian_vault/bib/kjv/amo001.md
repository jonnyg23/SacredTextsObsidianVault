 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Amos Chapter 1</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> The words of <a href="../ebd/ebd021.htm#007">Amos</a>, who was among the herdmen of Tekoa, which he saw concerning <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> in the days of <a href="../ebd/ebd376.htm#004">Uzziah</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>, and in the days of <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#000">Jeroboam</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd208.htm#000">Joash</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, two years before the <a href="../ebd/ebd110.htm#009">earthquake</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> And he said, The LORD will roar from <a href="../ebd/ebd394.htm#002">Zion</a>, and utter his voice from <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>; and the habitations of the shepherds shall <a href="../ebd/ebd261.htm#004">mourn</a>, and the top of <a href="../ebd/ebd072.htm#006">Carmel</a> shall wither.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Thus saith the LORD; For three transgressions of <a href="../ebd/ebd096.htm#007">Damascus</a>, and for four, I will not turn away the <a href="../ebd/ebd302.htm#005">punishment</a> thereof; because they have threshed <a href="../ebd/ebd148.htm#009">Gilead</a> with <a href="../ebd/ebd364.htm#009">threshing</a> instruments of <a href="../ebd/ebd189.htm#004">iron</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> But I will send a fire into the house of <a href="../ebd/ebd168.htm#003">Hazael</a>, which shall devour the palaces of Benhadad.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> I will break also the bar of <a href="../ebd/ebd096.htm#007">Damascus</a>, and cut off the inhabitant from the <a href="../ebd/ebd297.htm#002">plain</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd036.htm#009">Aven</a>, and him that holdeth the <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#006">sceptre</a> from the house of <a href="../ebd/ebd112.htm#009">Eden</a>: and the people of <a href="../ebd/ebd355.htm#002">Syria</a> shall go into <a href="../ebd/ebd072.htm#002">captivity</a> unto <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#007">Kir</a>, saith the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Thus saith the LORD; For three transgressions of <a href="../ebd/ebd144.htm#003">Gaza</a>, and for four, I will not turn away the <a href="../ebd/ebd302.htm#005">punishment</a> thereof; because they carried away <a href="../ebd/ebd072.htm#001">captive</a> the whole <a href="../ebd/ebd072.htm#002">captivity</a>, to deliver them up to <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#001">Edom</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> But I will send a fire on the wall of <a href="../ebd/ebd144.htm#003">Gaza</a>, which shall devour the palaces thereof:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And I will cut off the inhabitant from <a href="../ebd/ebd033.htm#006">Ashdod</a>, and him that holdeth the <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#006">sceptre</a> from <a href="../ebd/ebd034.htm#001">Ashkelon</a>, and I will turn mine hand against <a href="../ebd/ebd114.htm#001">Ekron</a>: and the remnant of the <a href="../ebd/ebd294.htm#002">Philistines</a> shall perish, saith the Lord GOD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> Thus saith the LORD; For three transgressions of Tyrus, and for four, I will not turn away the <a href="../ebd/ebd302.htm#005">punishment</a> thereof; because they delivered up the whole <a href="../ebd/ebd072.htm#002">captivity</a> to <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#001">Edom</a>, and remembered not the brotherly <a href="../ebd/ebd091.htm#008">covenant</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> But I will send a fire on the wall of Tyrus, which shall devour the palaces thereof.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> Thus saith the LORD; For three transgressions of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#001">Edom</a>, and for four, I will not turn away the <a href="../ebd/ebd302.htm#005">punishment</a> thereof; because he did pursue his brother with the <a href="../ebd/ebd354.htm#003">sword</a>, and did cast off all pity, and his <a href="../ebd/ebd024.htm#002">anger</a> did tear perpetually, and he kept his wrath for ever:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> But I will send a fire upon <a href="../ebd/ebd361.htm#001">Teman</a>, which shall devour the palaces of <a href="../ebd/ebd063.htm#009">Bozrah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> Thus saith the LORD; For three transgressions of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd021.htm#002">Ammon</a>, and for four, I will not turn away the <a href="../ebd/ebd302.htm#005">punishment</a> thereof; because they have ripped up the women with child of <a href="../ebd/ebd148.htm#009">Gilead</a>, that they might enlarge their border:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> But I will kindle a fire in the wall of <a href="../ebd/ebd304.htm#005">Rabbah</a>, and it shall devour the palaces thereof, with shouting in the day of battle, with a tempest in the day of the whirlwind:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And their <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> shall go into <a href="../ebd/ebd072.htm#002">captivity</a>, he and his princes together, saith the LORD.<br>
 </p>
