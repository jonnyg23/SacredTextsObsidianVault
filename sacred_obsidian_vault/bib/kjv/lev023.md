 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Leviticus Chapter 23</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> And the LORD spake unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Speak unto the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, and say unto them, Concerning the feasts of the LORD, which ye shall proclaim to be holy convocations, even these are my feasts.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Six days shall work be done: but the seventh day is the <a href="../ebd/ebd317.htm#003">sabbath</a> of rest, an holy <a href="../ebd/ebd089.htm#003">convocation</a>; ye shall do no work therein: it is the <a href="../ebd/ebd317.htm#003">sabbath</a> of the LORD in all your <a href="../ebd/ebd110.htm#001">dwellings</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> These are the feasts of the LORD, even holy convocations, which ye shall proclaim in their <a href="../ebd/ebd325.htm#003">seasons</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> In the fourteenth day of the first month at even is the LORD's <a href="../ebd/ebd286.htm#005">passover</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And on the fifteenth day of the same month is the <a href="../ebd/ebd132.htm#000">feast</a> of unleavened <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#006">bread</a> unto the LORD: <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> days ye must eat unleavened <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#006">bread</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> In the first day ye shall have an holy <a href="../ebd/ebd089.htm#003">convocation</a>: ye shall do no servile work therein.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> But ye shall offer an <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> made by fire unto the LORD <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> days: in the seventh day is an holy <a href="../ebd/ebd089.htm#003">convocation</a>: ye shall do no servile work therein.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And the LORD spake unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> Speak unto the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, and say unto them, When ye be come into the land which I give unto you, and shall reap the <a href="../ebd/ebd165.htm#008">harvest</a> thereof, then ye shall bring a sheaf of the firstfruits of your <a href="../ebd/ebd165.htm#008">harvest</a> unto the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And he shall wave the sheaf before the LORD, to be accepted for you: on the morrow after the <a href="../ebd/ebd317.htm#003">sabbath</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> shall wave it.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And ye shall offer that day when ye wave the sheaf an he <a href="../ebd/ebd223.htm#003">lamb</a> without <a href="../ebd/ebd060.htm#007">blemish</a> of the first year for a burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> unto the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> And the meat <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> thereof shall be two tenth deals of fine <a href="../ebd/ebd136.htm#001">flour</a> mingled with oil, an <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> made by fire unto the LORD for a sweet savour: and the <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> thereof shall be of <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#000">wine</a>, the fourth part of an hin.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> And ye shall eat neither <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#006">bread</a>, nor parched <a href="../ebd/ebd090.htm#006">corn</a>, nor green ears, until the selfsame day that ye have brought an <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> unto your God: it shall be a statute for ever throughout your generations in all your <a href="../ebd/ebd110.htm#001">dwellings</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And ye shall count unto you from the morrow after the <a href="../ebd/ebd317.htm#003">sabbath</a>, from the day that ye brought the sheaf of the wave <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>; <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> sabbaths shall be complete:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> Even unto the morrow after the seventh <a href="../ebd/ebd317.htm#003">sabbath</a> shall ye number fifty days; and ye shall offer a new meat <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> unto the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> Ye shall bring out of your habitations two wave loaves of two tenth deals; they shall be of fine <a href="../ebd/ebd136.htm#001">flour</a>; they shall be baken with <a href="../ebd/ebd226.htm#000">leaven</a>; they are the firstfruits unto the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> And ye shall offer with the <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#006">bread</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> lambs without <a href="../ebd/ebd060.htm#007">blemish</a> of the first year, and one young <a href="../ebd/ebd066.htm#003">bullock</a>, and two rams: they shall be for a burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> unto the LORD, with their meat <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, and their <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a> offerings, even an <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> made by fire, of sweet savour unto the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> Then ye shall <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#002">sacrifice</a> one kid of the goats for a <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, and two lambs of the first year for a <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#002">sacrifice</a> of peace offerings.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> shall wave them with the <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#006">bread</a> of the firstfruits for a wave <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> before the LORD, with the two lambs: they shall be holy to the LORD for the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> And ye shall proclaim on the selfsame day, that it may be an holy <a href="../ebd/ebd089.htm#003">convocation</a> unto you: ye shall do no servile work therein: it shall be a statute for ever in all your <a href="../ebd/ebd110.htm#001">dwellings</a> throughout your generations.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> And when ye reap the <a href="../ebd/ebd165.htm#008">harvest</a> of your land, thou shalt not make <a href="../ebd/ebd084.htm#005">clean</a> riddance of the corners of thy field when thou reapest, neither shalt thou gather any gleaning of thy <a href="../ebd/ebd165.htm#008">harvest</a>: thou shalt leave them unto the <a href="../ebd/ebd298.htm#006">poor</a>, and to the <a href="../ebd/ebd351.htm#009">stranger</a>: I am the LORD your God.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> And the LORD spake unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> Speak unto the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, saying, In the seventh month, in the first day of the month, shall ye have a <a href="../ebd/ebd317.htm#003">sabbath</a>, a memorial of blowing of <a href="../ebd/ebd373.htm#001">trumpets</a>, an holy <a href="../ebd/ebd089.htm#003">convocation</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> Ye shall do no servile work therein: but ye shall offer an <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> made by fire unto the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> And the LORD spake unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> Also on the tenth day of this seventh month there shall be a day of <a href="../ebd/ebd036.htm#004">atonement</a>: it shall be an holy <a href="../ebd/ebd089.htm#003">convocation</a> unto you; and ye shall afflict your souls, and offer an <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> made by fire unto the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="028">28</a> And ye shall do no work in that same day: for it is a day of <a href="../ebd/ebd036.htm#004">atonement</a>, to make an <a href="../ebd/ebd036.htm#004">atonement</a> for you before the LORD your God.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="029">29</a> For whatsoever soul it be that shall not be afflicted in that same day, he shall be cut off from among his people.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="030">30</a> And whatsoever soul it be that doeth any work in that same day, the same soul will I destroy from among his people.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="031">31</a> Ye shall do no manner of work: it shall be a statute for ever throughout your generations in all your <a href="../ebd/ebd110.htm#001">dwellings</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="032">32</a> It shall be unto you a <a href="../ebd/ebd317.htm#003">sabbath</a> of rest, and ye shall afflict your souls: in the ninth day of the month at even, from even unto even, shall ye celebrate your <a href="../ebd/ebd317.htm#003">sabbath</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="033">33</a> And the LORD spake unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="034">34</a> Speak unto the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, saying, The fifteenth day of this seventh month shall be the <a href="../ebd/ebd132.htm#000">feast</a> of tabernacles for <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> days unto the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="035">35</a> On the first day shall be an holy <a href="../ebd/ebd089.htm#003">convocation</a>: ye shall do no servile work therein.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="036">36</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">Seven</a> days ye shall offer an <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> made by fire unto the LORD: on the eighth day shall be an holy <a href="../ebd/ebd089.htm#003">convocation</a> unto you; and ye shall offer an <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> made by fire unto the LORD: it is a solemn assembly; and ye shall do no servile work therein.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="037">37</a> These are the feasts of the LORD, which ye shall proclaim to be holy convocations, to offer an <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> made by fire unto the LORD, a burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, and a meat <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, a <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#002">sacrifice</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a> offerings, every thing upon his day:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="038">38</a> Beside the sabbaths of the LORD, and beside your gifts, and beside all your <a href="../ebd/ebd378.htm#001">vows</a>, and beside all your freewill offerings, which ye give unto the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="039">39</a> Also in the fifteenth day of the seventh month, when ye have gathered in the <a href="../ebd/ebd139.htm#000">fruit</a> of the land, ye shall keep a <a href="../ebd/ebd132.htm#000">feast</a> unto the LORD <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> days: on the first day shall be a <a href="../ebd/ebd317.htm#003">sabbath</a>, and on the eighth day shall be a <a href="../ebd/ebd317.htm#003">sabbath</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="040">40</a> And ye shall take you on the first day the boughs of goodly trees, branches of palm trees, and the boughs of thick trees, and <a href="../ebd/ebd381.htm#006">willows</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd065.htm#006">brook</a>; and ye shall rejoice before the LORD your God <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> days.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="041">41</a> And ye shall keep it a <a href="../ebd/ebd132.htm#000">feast</a> unto the LORD <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> days in the year. It shall be a statute for ever in your generations: ye shall celebrate it in the seventh month.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="042">42</a> Ye shall dwell in booths <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> days; all that are Israelites born shall dwell in booths:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="043">43</a> That your generations may know that I made the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> to dwell in booths, when I brought them out of the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a>: I am the LORD your God.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="044">44</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> declared unto the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> the feasts of the LORD.<br>
 </p>
