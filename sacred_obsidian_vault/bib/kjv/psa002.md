 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 2</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Why do the <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#005">heathen</a> rage, and the people imagine a vain thing?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> The kings of the earth set themselves, and the rulers take counsel together, against the LORD, and against his anointed, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Let us break their <a href="../ebd/ebd043.htm#003">bands</a> asunder, and cast away their cords from us.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> He that sitteth in the heavens shall laugh: the LORD shall have them in derision.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Then shall he speak unto them in his wrath, and vex them in his sore displeasure.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Yet have I set my <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> upon my holy hill of <a href="../ebd/ebd394.htm#002">Zion</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> I will declare the decree: the LORD hath said unto me, Thou art my Son; this day have I begotten thee.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> Ask of me, and I shall give thee the <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#005">heathen</a> for thine inheritance, and the uttermost parts of the earth for thy possession.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> Thou shalt break them with a rod of <a href="../ebd/ebd189.htm#004">iron</a>; thou shalt dash them in <a href="../ebd/ebd295.htm#002">pieces</a> like a potter's vessel.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> Be <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#004">wise</a> now therefore, O ye kings: be instructed, ye judges of the earth.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> Serve the LORD with fear, and rejoice with trembling.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd221.htm#000">Kiss</a> the Son, lest he be angry, and ye perish from the way, when his wrath is kindled but a little. Blessed are all they that put their trust in him.<br>
 </p>
