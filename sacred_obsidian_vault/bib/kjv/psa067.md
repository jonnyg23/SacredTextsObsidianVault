 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 67</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> God be merciful unto us, and <a href="../ebd/ebd060.htm#008">bless</a> us; and cause his face to shine upon us; <a href="../ebd/ebd326.htm#008">Selah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> That thy way may be known upon earth, thy saving health among all nations.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Let the people praise thee, O God; let all the people praise thee.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> O let the nations be glad and sing for joy: for thou shalt <a href="../ebd/ebd213.htm#005">judge</a> the people righteously, and govern the nations upon earth. <a href="../ebd/ebd326.htm#008">Selah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Let the people praise thee, O God; let all the people praise thee.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Then shall the earth yield her increase; and God, even our own God, shall <a href="../ebd/ebd060.htm#008">bless</a> us.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> God shall <a href="../ebd/ebd060.htm#008">bless</a> us; and all the ends of the earth shall fear him.<br>
 </p>
