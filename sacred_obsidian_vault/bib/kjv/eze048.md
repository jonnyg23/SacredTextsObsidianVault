 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Ezekiel Chapter 48</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Now these are the names of the tribes. From the north end to the coast of the way of <a href="../ebd/ebd177.htm#002">Hethlon</a>, as one goeth to <a href="../ebd/ebd160.htm#005">Hamath</a>, Hazarenan, the border of <a href="../ebd/ebd096.htm#007">Damascus</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd275.htm#002">northward</a>, to the coast of <a href="../ebd/ebd160.htm#005">Hamath</a>; for these are his sides <a href="../ebd/ebd111.htm#000">east</a> and west; a portion for <a href="../ebd/ebd096.htm#009">Dan</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> And by the border of <a href="../ebd/ebd096.htm#009">Dan</a>, from the <a href="../ebd/ebd111.htm#000">east</a> side unto the west side, a portion for <a href="../ebd/ebd033.htm#008">Asher</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And by the border of <a href="../ebd/ebd033.htm#008">Asher</a>, from the <a href="../ebd/ebd111.htm#000">east</a> side even unto the west side, a portion for <a href="../ebd/ebd266.htm#006">Naphtali</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And by the border of <a href="../ebd/ebd266.htm#006">Naphtali</a>, from the <a href="../ebd/ebd111.htm#000">east</a> side unto the west side, a portion for <a href="../ebd/ebd240.htm#002">Manasseh</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And by the border of <a href="../ebd/ebd240.htm#002">Manasseh</a>, from the <a href="../ebd/ebd111.htm#000">east</a> side unto the west side, a portion for <a href="../ebd/ebd123.htm#000">Ephraim</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And by the border of <a href="../ebd/ebd123.htm#000">Ephraim</a>, from the <a href="../ebd/ebd111.htm#000">east</a> side even unto the west side, a portion for <a href="../ebd/ebd311.htm#009">Reuben</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And by the border of <a href="../ebd/ebd311.htm#009">Reuben</a>, from the <a href="../ebd/ebd111.htm#000">east</a> side unto the west side, a portion for <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And by the border of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>, from the <a href="../ebd/ebd111.htm#000">east</a> side unto the west side, shall be the <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> which ye shall offer of five and twenty thousand reeds in breadth, and in length as one of the other parts, from the <a href="../ebd/ebd111.htm#000">east</a> side unto the west side: and the <a href="../ebd/ebd321.htm#005">sanctuary</a> shall be in the midst of it.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> The oblation that ye shall offer unto the LORD shall be of five and twenty thousand in length, and of ten thousand in breadth.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And for them, even for the priests, shall be this holy oblation; toward the north five and twenty thousand in length, and toward the west ten thousand in breadth, and toward the <a href="../ebd/ebd111.htm#000">east</a> ten thousand in breadth, and toward the south five and twenty thousand in length: and the <a href="../ebd/ebd321.htm#005">sanctuary</a> of the LORD shall be in the midst thereof.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> It shall be for the priests that are sanctified of the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd386.htm#008">Zadok</a>; which have kept my charge, which went not astray when the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> went astray, as the Levites went astray.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And this oblation of the land that is offered shall be unto them a thing most holy by the border of the Levites.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> And over against the border of the priests the Levites shall have five and twenty thousand in length, and ten thousand in breadth: all the length shall be five and twenty thousand, and the breadth ten thousand.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> And they shall not sell of it, neither exchange, nor alienate the firstfruits of the land: for it is holy unto the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And the five thousand, that are left in the breadth over against the five and twenty thousand, shall be a profane place for the city, for dwelling, and for <a href="../ebd/ebd352.htm#007">suburbs</a>: and the city shall be in the midst thereof.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> And these shall be the measures thereof; the north side four thousand and five hundred, and the south side four thousand and five hundred, and on the <a href="../ebd/ebd111.htm#000">east</a> side four thousand and five hundred, and the west side four thousand and five hundred.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd352.htm#007">suburbs</a> of the city shall be toward the north two hundred and fifty, and toward the south two hundred and fifty, and toward the <a href="../ebd/ebd111.htm#000">east</a> two hundred and fifty, and toward the west two hundred and fifty.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> And the residue in length over against the oblation of the holy portion shall be ten thousand eastward, and ten thousand <a href="../ebd/ebd380.htm#008">westward</a>: and it shall be over against the oblation of the holy portion; and the increase thereof shall be for food unto them that serve the city.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> And they that serve the city shall serve it out of all the tribes of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> All the oblation shall be five and twenty thousand by five and twenty thousand: ye shall offer the holy oblation foursquare, with the possession of the city.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> And the residue shall be for the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#004">prince</a>, on the one side and on the other of the holy oblation, and of the possession of the city, over against the five and twenty thousand of the oblation toward the <a href="../ebd/ebd111.htm#000">east</a> border, and <a href="../ebd/ebd380.htm#008">westward</a> over against the five and twenty thousand toward the west border, over against the portions for the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#004">prince</a>: and it shall be the holy oblation; and the <a href="../ebd/ebd321.htm#005">sanctuary</a> of the house shall be in the midst thereof.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> Moreover from the possession of the Levites, and from the possession of the city, being in the midst of that which is the prince's, between the border of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a> and the border of <a href="../ebd/ebd052.htm#008">Benjamin</a>, shall be for the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#004">prince</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> As for the rest of the tribes, from the <a href="../ebd/ebd111.htm#000">east</a> side unto the west side, <a href="../ebd/ebd052.htm#008">Benjamin</a> shall have a portion.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> And by the border of <a href="../ebd/ebd052.htm#008">Benjamin</a>, from the <a href="../ebd/ebd111.htm#000">east</a> side unto the west side, <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#002">Simeon</a> shall have a portion.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> And by the border of <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#002">Simeon</a>, from the <a href="../ebd/ebd111.htm#000">east</a> side unto the west side, <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#002">Issachar</a> a portion.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> And by the border of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#002">Issachar</a>, from the <a href="../ebd/ebd111.htm#000">east</a> side unto the west side, <a href="../ebd/ebd389.htm#003">Zebulun</a> a portion.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> And by the border of <a href="../ebd/ebd389.htm#003">Zebulun</a>, from the <a href="../ebd/ebd111.htm#000">east</a> side unto the west side, <a href="../ebd/ebd140.htm#007">Gad</a> a portion.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="028">28</a> And by the border of <a href="../ebd/ebd140.htm#007">Gad</a>, at the south side southward, the border shall be even from <a href="../ebd/ebd358.htm#003">Tamar</a> unto the waters of strife in <a href="../ebd/ebd215.htm#003">Kadesh</a>, and to the river toward the great sea.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="029">29</a> This is the land which ye shall divide by lot unto the tribes of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> for inheritance, and these are their portions, saith the Lord GOD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="030">30</a> And these are the goings out of the city on the north side, four thousand and five hundred measures.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="031">31</a> And the gates of the city shall be after the names of the tribes of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>: three gates <a href="../ebd/ebd275.htm#002">northward</a>; one <a href="../ebd/ebd143.htm#008">gate</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd311.htm#009">Reuben</a>, one <a href="../ebd/ebd143.htm#008">gate</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>, one <a href="../ebd/ebd143.htm#008">gate</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd227.htm#006">Levi</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="032">32</a> And at the <a href="../ebd/ebd111.htm#000">east</a> side four thousand and five hundred: and three gates; and one <a href="../ebd/ebd143.htm#008">gate</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#005">Joseph</a>, one <a href="../ebd/ebd143.htm#008">gate</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd052.htm#008">Benjamin</a>, one <a href="../ebd/ebd143.htm#008">gate</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd096.htm#009">Dan</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="033">33</a> And at the south side four thousand and five hundred measures: and three gates; one <a href="../ebd/ebd143.htm#008">gate</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#002">Simeon</a>, one <a href="../ebd/ebd143.htm#008">gate</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#002">Issachar</a>, one <a href="../ebd/ebd143.htm#008">gate</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd389.htm#003">Zebulun</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="034">34</a> At the west side four thousand and five hundred, with their three gates; one <a href="../ebd/ebd143.htm#008">gate</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd140.htm#007">Gad</a>, one <a href="../ebd/ebd143.htm#008">gate</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd033.htm#008">Asher</a>, one <a href="../ebd/ebd143.htm#008">gate</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd266.htm#006">Naphtali</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="035">35</a> It was round about eighteen thousand measures: and the name of the city from that day shall be, The LORD is there.<br>
 </p>
