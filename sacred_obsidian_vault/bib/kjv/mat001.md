 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Matthew Chapter 1</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> The book of the <a href="../ebd/ebd145.htm#008">generation</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a>, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a>, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd005.htm#005">Abraham</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd005.htm#005">Abraham</a> begat <a href="../ebd/ebd189.htm#006">Isaac</a>; and <a href="../ebd/ebd189.htm#006">Isaac</a> begat <a href="../ebd/ebd194.htm#007">Jacob</a>; and <a href="../ebd/ebd194.htm#007">Jacob</a> begat <a href="../ebd/ebd213.htm#001">Judas</a> and his brethren;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd213.htm#001">Judas</a> begat Phares and Zara of Thamar; and Phares begat Esrom; and Esrom begat <a href="../ebd/ebd028.htm#001">Aram</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd028.htm#001">Aram</a> begat Aminadab; and Aminadab begat Naasson; and Naasson begat <a href="../ebd/ebd319.htm#005">Salmon</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd319.htm#005">Salmon</a> begat Booz of <a href="../ebd/ebd305.htm#002">Rachab</a>; and Booz begat <a href="../ebd/ebd276.htm#004">Obed</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd316.htm#008">Ruth</a>; and <a href="../ebd/ebd276.htm#004">Obed</a> begat <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#005">Jesse</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#005">Jesse</a> begat <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a>; and <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> begat <a href="../ebd/ebd347.htm#007">Solomon</a> of her that had been the wife of Urias;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd347.htm#007">Solomon</a> begat Roboam; and Roboam begat <a href="../ebd/ebd002.htm#002">Abia</a>; and <a href="../ebd/ebd002.htm#002">Abia</a> begat <a href="../ebd/ebd033.htm#000">Asa</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd033.htm#000">Asa</a> begat Josaphat; and Josaphat begat <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#003">Joram</a>; and <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#003">Joram</a> begat <a href="../ebd/ebd282.htm#000">Ozias</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd282.htm#000">Ozias</a> begat Joatham; and Joatham begat Achaz; and Achaz begat <a href="../ebd/ebd129.htm#000">Ezekias</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd129.htm#000">Ezekias</a> begat Manasses; and Manasses begat <a href="../ebd/ebd021.htm#005">Amon</a>; and <a href="../ebd/ebd021.htm#005">Amon</a> begat Josias;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And Josias begat Jechonias and his brethren, about the time they were carried away to <a href="../ebd/ebd041.htm#001">Babylon</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And after they were brought to <a href="../ebd/ebd041.htm#001">Babylon</a>, Jechonias begat <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#009">Salathiel</a>; and <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#009">Salathiel</a> begat Zorobabel;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> And Zorobabel begat Abiud; and Abiud begat <a href="../ebd/ebd116.htm#000">Eliakim</a>; and <a href="../ebd/ebd116.htm#000">Eliakim</a> begat Azor;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> And Azor begat <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#004">Sadoc</a>; and <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#004">Sadoc</a> begat Achim; and Achim begat Eliud;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And Eliud begat <a href="../ebd/ebd115.htm#000">Eleazar</a>; and <a href="../ebd/ebd115.htm#000">Eleazar</a> begat <a href="../ebd/ebd244.htm#001">Matthan</a>; and <a href="../ebd/ebd244.htm#001">Matthan</a> begat <a href="../ebd/ebd194.htm#007">Jacob</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd194.htm#007">Jacob</a> begat <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#005">Joseph</a> the husband of <a href="../ebd/ebd242.htm#009">Mary</a>, of whom was born <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a>, who is called <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> So all the generations from <a href="../ebd/ebd005.htm#005">Abraham</a> to <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> are fourteen generations; and from <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> until the carrying away into <a href="../ebd/ebd041.htm#001">Babylon</a> are fourteen generations; and from the carrying away into <a href="../ebd/ebd041.htm#001">Babylon</a> unto <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a> are fourteen generations.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> Now the <a href="../ebd/ebd059.htm#002">birth</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a> was on this <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#004">wise</a>: When as his mother <a href="../ebd/ebd242.htm#009">Mary</a> was espoused to <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#005">Joseph</a>, before they came together, she was found with child of the Holy <a href="../ebd/ebd147.htm#005">Ghost</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> Then <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#005">Joseph</a> her husband, being a just man, and not willing to make her a publick example, was minded to put her away privily.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> But while he thought on these things, behold, the <a href="../ebd/ebd024.htm#001">angel</a> of the LORD appeared unto him in a <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#005">dream</a>, saying, <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#005">Joseph</a>, thou son of <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a>, fear not to take unto thee <a href="../ebd/ebd242.htm#009">Mary</a> thy wife: for that which is conceived in her is of the Holy <a href="../ebd/ebd147.htm#005">Ghost</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> And she shall bring forth a son, and thou shalt call his name <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">JESUS</a>: for he shall save his people from their sins.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> Now all this was done, that it might be fulfilled which was spoken of the Lord by the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#008">prophet</a>, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> Behold, a <a href="../ebd/ebd377.htm#009">virgin</a> shall be with child, and shall bring forth a son, and they shall call his name <a href="../ebd/ebd120.htm#000">Emmanuel</a>, which being interpreted is, God with us.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> Then <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#005">Joseph</a> being raised from sleep did as the <a href="../ebd/ebd024.htm#001">angel</a> of the Lord had bidden him, and took unto him his wife:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> And knew her not till she had brought forth her firstborn son: and he called his name <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">JESUS</a>.<br>
 </p>
