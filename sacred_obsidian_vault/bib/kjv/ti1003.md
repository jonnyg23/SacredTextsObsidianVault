 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: 1 Timothy Chapter 3</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> This is a true saying, If a man desire the office of a <a href="../ebd/ebd059.htm#005">bishop</a>, he desireth a good work.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> A <a href="../ebd/ebd059.htm#005">bishop</a> then must be blameless, the husband of one wife, vigilant, sober, of good behaviour, given to hospitality, apt to teach;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Not given to <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#000">wine</a>, no striker, not greedy of filthy <a href="../ebd/ebd232.htm#008">lucre</a>; but patient, not a brawler, not covetous;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> One that ruleth well his own house, having his children in subjection with all gravity;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> (For if a man know not how to rule his own house, how shall he take care of the church of God?)<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Not a novice, lest being lifted up with pride he fall into the condemnation of the <a href="../ebd/ebd102.htm#007">devil</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Moreover he must have a good report of them which are without; lest he fall into reproach and the <a href="../ebd/ebd346.htm#009">snare</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd102.htm#007">devil</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> Likewise must the deacons be <a href="../ebd/ebd154.htm#008">grave</a>, not doubletongued, not given to much <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#000">wine</a>, not greedy of filthy <a href="../ebd/ebd232.htm#008">lucre</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> Holding the <a href="../ebd/ebd263.htm#006">mystery</a> of the faith in a pure <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#006">conscience</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And let these also first be proved; then let them use the office of a <a href="../ebd/ebd099.htm#001">deacon</a>, being found blameless.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> Even so must their wives be <a href="../ebd/ebd154.htm#008">grave</a>, not slanderers, sober, <a href="../ebd/ebd130.htm#005">faithful</a> in all things.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> Let the deacons be the husbands of one wife, ruling their children and their own houses well.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> For they that have used the office of a <a href="../ebd/ebd099.htm#001">deacon</a> well purchase to themselves a good degree, and great boldness in the faith which is in <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> These things write I unto thee, hoping to come unto thee shortly:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> But if I tarry long, that thou mayest know how thou oughtest to behave thyself in the house of God, which is the church of the living God, the <a href="../ebd/ebd295.htm#007">pillar</a> and ground of the truth.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> And without controversy great is the <a href="../ebd/ebd263.htm#006">mystery</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd151.htm#007">godliness</a>: God was manifest in the <a href="../ebd/ebd135.htm#007">flesh</a>, justified in the <a href="../ebd/ebd349.htm#009">Spirit</a>, seen of angels, preached unto the <a href="../ebd/ebd146.htm#001">Gentiles</a>, believed on in the world, received up into <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a>.<br>
 </p>
