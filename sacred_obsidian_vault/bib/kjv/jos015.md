 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Joshua Chapter 15</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> This then was the lot of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a> by their families; even to the border of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#001">Edom</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd381.htm#005">wilderness</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd394.htm#000">Zin</a> southward was the uttermost part of the south coast.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> And their south border was from the shore of the <a href="../ebd/ebd319.htm#008">salt</a> sea, from the <a href="../ebd/ebd048.htm#001">bay</a> that looketh southward:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And it went out to the south side to Maalehacrabbim, and passed along to <a href="../ebd/ebd394.htm#000">Zin</a>, and ascended up on the south side unto Kadeshbarnea, and passed along to <a href="../ebd/ebd177.htm#007">Hezron</a>, and went up to <a href="../ebd/ebd008.htm#002">Adar</a>, and fetched a compass to <a href="../ebd/ebd215.htm#008">Karkaa</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> From thence it passed toward Azmon, and went out unto the river of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a>; and the goings out of that coast were at the sea: this shall be your south coast.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd111.htm#000">east</a> border was the <a href="../ebd/ebd319.htm#008">salt</a> sea, even unto the end of <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#004">Jordan</a>. And their border in the north quarter was from the <a href="../ebd/ebd048.htm#001">bay</a> of the sea at the uttermost part of <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#004">Jordan</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And the border went up to Bethhogla, and passed along by the north of Betharabah; and the border went up to the <a href="../ebd/ebd351.htm#005">stone</a> of Bohan the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd311.htm#009">Reuben</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And the border went up toward <a href="../ebd/ebd099.htm#007">Debir</a> from the <a href="../ebd/ebd376.htm#008">valley</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd007.htm#000">Achor</a>, and so <a href="../ebd/ebd275.htm#002">northward</a>, looking toward <a href="../ebd/ebd149.htm#001">Gilgal</a>, that is before the going up to <a href="../ebd/ebd011.htm#000">Adummim</a>, which is on the south side of the river: and the border passed toward the waters of Enshemesh, and the goings out thereof were at Enrogel:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And the border went up by the <a href="../ebd/ebd376.htm#008">valley</a> of the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd179.htm#002">Hinnom</a> unto the south side of the Jebusite; the same is <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>: and the border went up to the top of the mountain that lieth before the <a href="../ebd/ebd376.htm#008">valley</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd179.htm#002">Hinnom</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd380.htm#008">westward</a>, which is at the end of the <a href="../ebd/ebd376.htm#008">valley</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd147.htm#006">giants</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd275.htm#002">northward</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And the border was drawn from the top of the hill unto the <a href="../ebd/ebd138.htm#000">fountain</a> of the water of <a href="../ebd/ebd270.htm#006">Nephtoah</a>, and went out to the cities of <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#007">mount</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd123.htm#008">Ephron</a>; and the border was drawn to <a href="../ebd/ebd038.htm#005">Baalah</a>, which is Kirjathjearim:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And the border compassed from <a href="../ebd/ebd038.htm#005">Baalah</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd380.htm#008">westward</a> unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#007">mount</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd326.htm#004">Seir</a>, and passed along unto the side of <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#007">mount</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd199.htm#002">Jearim</a>, which is <a href="../ebd/ebd079.htm#004">Chesalon</a>, on the north side, and went down to Bethshemesh, and passed on to <a href="../ebd/ebd366.htm#005">Timnah</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And the border went out unto the side of <a href="../ebd/ebd114.htm#001">Ekron</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd275.htm#002">northward</a>: and the border was drawn to Shicron, and passed along to <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#007">mount</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd038.htm#005">Baalah</a>, and went out unto <a href="../ebd/ebd194.htm#001">Jabneel</a>; and the goings out of the border were at the sea.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And the west border was to the great sea, and the coast thereof. This is the coast of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a> round about according to their families.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> And unto <a href="../ebd/ebd069.htm#003">Caleb</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd203.htm#004">Jephunneh</a> he gave a part among the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>, according to the commandment of the LORD to <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#006">Joshua</a>, even the city of <a href="../ebd/ebd028.htm#007">Arba</a> the father of <a href="../ebd/ebd022.htm#005">Anak</a>, which city is <a href="../ebd/ebd171.htm#004">Hebron</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd069.htm#003">Caleb</a> drove thence the three sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd022.htm#005">Anak</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd336.htm#002">Sheshai</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd014.htm#003">Ahiman</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd358.htm#001">Talmai</a>, the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd022.htm#005">Anak</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And he went up thence to the inhabitants of <a href="../ebd/ebd099.htm#007">Debir</a>: and the name of <a href="../ebd/ebd099.htm#007">Debir</a> before was Kirjathsepher.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd069.htm#003">Caleb</a> said, He that smiteth Kirjathsepher, and taketh it, to him will I give <a href="../ebd/ebd007.htm#001">Achsah</a> my daughter to wife.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd281.htm#003">Othniel</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd217.htm#003">Kenaz</a>, the brother of <a href="../ebd/ebd069.htm#003">Caleb</a>, took it: and he gave him <a href="../ebd/ebd007.htm#001">Achsah</a> his daughter to wife.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> And it came to pass, as she came unto him, that she moved him to ask of her father a field: and she lighted off her <a href="../ebd/ebd035.htm#001">ass</a>; and <a href="../ebd/ebd069.htm#003">Caleb</a> said unto her, What wouldest thou?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> Who answered, Give me a blessing; for thou hast given me a south land; give me also springs of water. And he gave her the upper springs, and the nether springs.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> This is the inheritance of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a> according to their families.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> And the uttermost cities of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a> toward the coast of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#001">Edom</a> southward were <a href="../ebd/ebd215.htm#002">Kabzeel</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#000">Eder</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd195.htm#002">Jagur</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd218.htm#009">Kinah</a>, and Dimonah, and Adadah,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd216.htm#006">Kedesh</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd169.htm#006">Hazor</a>, and Ithnan,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd394.htm#004">Ziph</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd360.htm#007">Telem</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd048.htm#006">Bealoth</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd169.htm#006">Hazor</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd157.htm#007">Hadattah</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd217.htm#008">Kerioth</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd177.htm#007">Hezron</a>, which is <a href="../ebd/ebd169.htm#006">Hazor</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> Amam, and <a href="../ebd/ebd334.htm#003">Shema</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd258.htm#008">Moladah</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> And Hazargaddah, and <a href="../ebd/ebd177.htm#000">Heshmon</a>, and Bethpalet,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="028">28</a> And Hazarshual, and <a href="../ebd/ebd050.htm#008">Beersheba</a>, and Bizjothjah,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="029">29</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd038.htm#005">Baalah</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd187.htm#000">Iim</a>, and Azem,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="030">30</a> And Eltolad, and <a href="../ebd/ebd079.htm#006">Chesil</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd182.htm#002">Hormah</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="031">31</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd393.htm#003">Ziklag</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd235.htm#009">Madmannah</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd321.htm#008">Sansannah</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="032">32</a> And Lebaoth, and <a href="../ebd/ebd337.htm#006">Shilhim</a>, and Ain, and <a href="../ebd/ebd313.htm#005">Rimmon</a>: all the cities are twenty and nine, with their <a href="../ebd/ebd377.htm#003">villages</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="033">33</a> And in the <a href="../ebd/ebd376.htm#008">valley</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd125.htm#002">Eshtaol</a>, and Zoreah, and Ashnah,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="034">34</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd387.htm#004">Zanoah</a>, and Engannim, <a href="../ebd/ebd358.htm#008">Tappuah</a>, and Enam,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="035">35</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd197.htm#007">Jarmuth</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd010.htm#007">Adullam</a>, Socoh, and <a href="../ebd/ebd037.htm#008">Azekah</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="036">36</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd331.htm#007">Sharaim</a>, and Adithaim, and <a href="../ebd/ebd145.htm#001">Gederah</a>, and Gederothaim; fourteen cities with their <a href="../ebd/ebd377.htm#003">villages</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="037">37</a> Zenan, and <a href="../ebd/ebd157.htm#005">Hadashah</a>, and Migdalgad,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="038">38</a> And Dilean, and Mizpeh, and <a href="../ebd/ebd210.htm#005">Joktheel</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="039">39</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd222.htm#009">Lachish</a>, and Bozkath, and <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#008">Eglon</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="040">40</a> And Cabbon, and Lahmam, and <a href="../ebd/ebd221.htm#002">Kithlish</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="041">41</a> And Gederoth, Bethdagon, and <a href="../ebd/ebd263.htm#008">Naamah</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd238.htm#006">Makkedah</a>; sixteen cities with their <a href="../ebd/ebd377.htm#003">villages</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="042">42</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd228.htm#004">Libnah</a>, and Ether, and Ashan,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="043">43</a> And Jiphtah, and Ashnah, and <a href="../ebd/ebd272.htm#001">Nezib</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="044">44</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd216.htm#009">Keilah</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd007.htm#003">Achzib</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd241.htm#009">Mareshah</a>; nine cities with their <a href="../ebd/ebd377.htm#003">villages</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="045">45</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd114.htm#001">Ekron</a>, with her towns and her <a href="../ebd/ebd377.htm#003">villages</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="046">46</a> From <a href="../ebd/ebd114.htm#001">Ekron</a> even unto the sea, all that lay near <a href="../ebd/ebd033.htm#006">Ashdod</a>, with their <a href="../ebd/ebd377.htm#003">villages</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="047">47</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd033.htm#006">Ashdod</a> with her towns and her <a href="../ebd/ebd377.htm#003">villages</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd144.htm#003">Gaza</a> with her towns and her <a href="../ebd/ebd377.htm#003">villages</a>, unto the river of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a>, and the great sea, and the border thereof:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="048">48</a> And in the mountains, <a href="../ebd/ebd331.htm#001">Shamir</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd198.htm#004">Jattir</a>, and Socoh,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="049">49</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd097.htm#004">Dannah</a>, and Kirjathsannah, which is <a href="../ebd/ebd099.htm#007">Debir</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="050">50</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd022.htm#003">Anab</a>, and Eshtemoh, and <a href="../ebd/ebd024.htm#003">Anim</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="051">51</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd153.htm#002">Goshen</a>, and Holon, and <a href="../ebd/ebd149.htm#002">Giloh</a>; eleven cities with their <a href="../ebd/ebd377.htm#003">villages</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="052">52</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd027.htm#007">Arab</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd109.htm#001">Dumah</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd125.htm#001">Eshean</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="053">53</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd197.htm#001">Janum</a>, and Bethtappuah, and Aphekah,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="054">54</a> And Humtah, and Kirjatharba, which is <a href="../ebd/ebd171.htm#004">Hebron</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd394.htm#003">Zior</a>; nine cities with their <a href="../ebd/ebd377.htm#003">villages</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="055">55</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd241.htm#001">Maon</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd072.htm#006">Carmel</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd394.htm#004">Ziph</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd215.htm#001">Juttah</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="056">56</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd206.htm#008">Jezreel</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd209.htm#009">Jokdeam</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd387.htm#004">Zanoah</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="057">57</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd068.htm#007">Cain</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd147.htm#008">Gibeah</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd366.htm#005">Timnah</a>; ten cities with their <a href="../ebd/ebd377.htm#003">villages</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="058">58</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd159.htm#007">Halhul</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd057.htm#004">Bethzur</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd145.htm#003">Gedor</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="059">59</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd234.htm#006">Maarath</a>, and Bethanoth, and Eltekon; six cities with their <a href="../ebd/ebd377.htm#003">villages</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="060">60</a> Kirjathbaal, which is Kirjathjearim, and <a href="../ebd/ebd304.htm#005">Rabbah</a>; two cities with their <a href="../ebd/ebd377.htm#003">villages</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="061">61</a> In the <a href="../ebd/ebd381.htm#005">wilderness</a>, Betharabah, <a href="../ebd/ebd253.htm#006">Middin</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd325.htm#006">Secacah</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="062">62</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd272.htm#003">Nibshan</a>, and the city of <a href="../ebd/ebd319.htm#008">Salt</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd120.htm#009">Engedi</a>; six cities with their <a href="../ebd/ebd377.htm#003">villages</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="063">63</a> As for the <a href="../ebd/ebd199.htm#004">Jebusites</a> the inhabitants of <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>, the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a> could not drive them out; but the <a href="../ebd/ebd199.htm#004">Jebusites</a> dwell with the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a> at <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a> unto this day.<br>
 </p>
