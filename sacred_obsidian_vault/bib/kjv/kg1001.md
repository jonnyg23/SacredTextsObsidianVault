 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: 3 Kings (1 Kings) Chapter 1</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Now <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> was old and stricken in years; and they covered him with clothes, but he gat no heat.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Wherefore his servants said unto him, Let there be sought for my lord the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> a young <a href="../ebd/ebd377.htm#009">virgin</a>: and let her stand before the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a>, and let her cherish him, and let her lie in thy <a href="../ebd/ebd062.htm#008">bosom</a>, that my lord the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> may get heat.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> So they sought for a fair damsel throughout all the coasts of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, and found <a href="../ebd/ebd004.htm#004">Abishag</a> a <a href="../ebd/ebd342.htm#000">Shunammite</a>, and brought her to the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And the damsel was very fair, and cherished the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a>, and ministered to him: but the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> knew her not.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Then <a href="../ebd/ebd009.htm#006">Adonijah</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd158.htm#009">Haggith</a> exalted himself, saying, I will be <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a>: and he prepared him chariots and horsemen, and fifty men to run before him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And his father had not displeased him at any time in saying, Why hast thou done so? and he also was a very goodly man; and his mother bare him after <a href="../ebd/ebd005.htm#008">Absalom</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And he conferred with <a href="../ebd/ebd207.htm#006">Joab</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd391.htm#009">Zeruiah</a>, and with <a href="../ebd/ebd002.htm#005">Abiathar</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a>: and they following <a href="../ebd/ebd009.htm#006">Adonijah</a> helped him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> But <a href="../ebd/ebd386.htm#008">Zadok</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd052.htm#003">Benaiah</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd200.htm#009">Jehoiada</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd267.htm#002">Nathan</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#008">prophet</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd338.htm#002">Shimei</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd310.htm#000">Rei</a>, and the mighty men which belonged to <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a>, were not with <a href="../ebd/ebd009.htm#006">Adonijah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd009.htm#006">Adonijah</a> slew <a href="../ebd/ebd333.htm#005">sheep</a> and oxen and <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#005">fat</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd074.htm#002">cattle</a> by the <a href="../ebd/ebd351.htm#005">stone</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd395.htm#007">Zoheleth</a>, which is by Enrogel, and called all his brethren the king's sons, and all the men of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a> the king's servants:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> But <a href="../ebd/ebd267.htm#002">Nathan</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#008">prophet</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd052.htm#003">Benaiah</a>, and the mighty men, and <a href="../ebd/ebd347.htm#007">Solomon</a> his brother, he called not.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> Wherefore <a href="../ebd/ebd267.htm#002">Nathan</a> spake unto Bathsheba the mother of <a href="../ebd/ebd347.htm#007">Solomon</a>, saying, Hast thou not heard that <a href="../ebd/ebd009.htm#006">Adonijah</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd158.htm#009">Haggith</a> doth reign, and <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> our lord knoweth it not?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> Now therefore come, let me, I pray thee, give thee counsel, that thou mayest save thine own life, and the life of thy son <a href="../ebd/ebd347.htm#007">Solomon</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> Go and get thee in unto <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a>, and say unto him, Didst not thou, my lord, O <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a>, swear unto thine <a href="../ebd/ebd162.htm#008">handmaid</a>, saying, Assuredly <a href="../ebd/ebd347.htm#007">Solomon</a> thy son shall reign after me, and he shall sit upon my <a href="../ebd/ebd365.htm#001">throne</a>? why then doth <a href="../ebd/ebd009.htm#006">Adonijah</a> reign?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> Behold, while thou yet talkest there with the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a>, I also will come in after thee, and confirm thy words.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And Bathsheba went in unto the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> into the <a href="../ebd/ebd076.htm#003">chamber</a>: and the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> was very old; and <a href="../ebd/ebd004.htm#004">Abishag</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd342.htm#000">Shunammite</a> ministered unto the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> And Bathsheba bowed, and did <a href="../ebd/ebd276.htm#006">obeisance</a> unto the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a>. And the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> said, What wouldest thou?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> And she said unto him, My lord, thou swarest by the LORD thy God unto thine <a href="../ebd/ebd162.htm#008">handmaid</a>, saying, Assuredly <a href="../ebd/ebd347.htm#007">Solomon</a> thy son shall reign after me, and he shall sit upon my <a href="../ebd/ebd365.htm#001">throne</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> And now, behold, <a href="../ebd/ebd009.htm#006">Adonijah</a> reigneth; and now, my lord the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a>, thou knowest it not:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> And he hath slain oxen and <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#005">fat</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd074.htm#002">cattle</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd333.htm#005">sheep</a> in abundance, and hath called all the sons of the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd002.htm#005">Abiathar</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd207.htm#006">Joab</a> the captain of the <a href="../ebd/ebd183.htm#007">host</a>: but <a href="../ebd/ebd347.htm#007">Solomon</a> thy servant hath he not called.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> And thou, my lord, O <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a>, the eyes of all <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> are upon thee, that thou shouldest tell them who shall sit on the <a href="../ebd/ebd365.htm#001">throne</a> of my lord the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> after him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> Otherwise it shall come to pass, when my lord the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> shall sleep with his fathers, that I and my son <a href="../ebd/ebd347.htm#007">Solomon</a> shall be counted offenders.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> And, lo, while she yet talked with the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd267.htm#002">Nathan</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#008">prophet</a> also came in.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> And they told the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a>, saying, Behold <a href="../ebd/ebd267.htm#002">Nathan</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#008">prophet</a>. And when he was come in before the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a>, he bowed himself before the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> with his face to the ground.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd267.htm#002">Nathan</a> said, My lord, O <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a>, hast thou said, <a href="../ebd/ebd009.htm#006">Adonijah</a> shall reign after me, and he shall sit upon my <a href="../ebd/ebd365.htm#001">throne</a>?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> For he is gone down this day, and hath slain oxen and <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#005">fat</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd074.htm#002">cattle</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd333.htm#005">sheep</a> in abundance, and hath called all the king's sons, and the captains of the <a href="../ebd/ebd183.htm#007">host</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd002.htm#005">Abiathar</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a>; and, behold, they eat and <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a> before him, and say, God save <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd009.htm#006">Adonijah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> But me, even me thy servant, and <a href="../ebd/ebd386.htm#008">Zadok</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd052.htm#003">Benaiah</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd200.htm#009">Jehoiada</a>, and thy servant <a href="../ebd/ebd347.htm#007">Solomon</a>, hath he not called.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> Is this thing done by my lord the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a>, and thou hast not shewed it unto thy servant, who should sit on the <a href="../ebd/ebd365.htm#001">throne</a> of my lord the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> after him?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="028">28</a> Then <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> answered and said, Call me Bathsheba. And she came into the king's presence, and stood before the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="029">29</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> sware, and said, As the LORD liveth, that hath redeemed my soul out of all distress,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="030">30</a> Even as I sware unto thee by the LORD God of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, saying, Assuredly <a href="../ebd/ebd347.htm#007">Solomon</a> thy son shall reign after me, and he shall sit upon my <a href="../ebd/ebd365.htm#001">throne</a> in my stead; even so will I certainly do this day.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="031">31</a> Then Bathsheba bowed with her face to the earth, and did reverence to the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a>, and said, Let my lord <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> live for ever.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="032">32</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> said, Call me <a href="../ebd/ebd386.htm#008">Zadok</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd267.htm#002">Nathan</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#008">prophet</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd052.htm#003">Benaiah</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd200.htm#009">Jehoiada</a>. And they came before the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="033">33</a> The <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> also said unto them, Take with you the servants of your lord, and cause <a href="../ebd/ebd347.htm#007">Solomon</a> my son to ride upon mine own <a href="../ebd/ebd262.htm#001">mule</a>, and bring him down to <a href="../ebd/ebd148.htm#007">Gihon</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="034">34</a> And let <a href="../ebd/ebd386.htm#008">Zadok</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd267.htm#002">Nathan</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#008">prophet</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd024.htm#008">anoint</a> him there <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> over <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>: and blow ye with the trumpet, and say, God save <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd347.htm#007">Solomon</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="035">35</a> Then ye shall come up after him, that he may come and sit upon my <a href="../ebd/ebd365.htm#001">throne</a>; for he shall be <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> in my stead: and I have appointed him to be ruler over <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> and over <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="036">36</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd052.htm#003">Benaiah</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd200.htm#009">Jehoiada</a> answered the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a>, and said, <a href="../ebd/ebd020.htm#001">Amen</a>: the LORD God of my lord the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> say so too.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="037">37</a> As the LORD hath been with my lord the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a>, even so be he with <a href="../ebd/ebd347.htm#007">Solomon</a>, and make his <a href="../ebd/ebd365.htm#001">throne</a> greater than the <a href="../ebd/ebd365.htm#001">throne</a> of my lord <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="038">38</a> So <a href="../ebd/ebd386.htm#008">Zadok</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd267.htm#002">Nathan</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#008">prophet</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd052.htm#003">Benaiah</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd200.htm#009">Jehoiada</a>, and the Cherethites, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd289.htm#000">Pelethites</a>, went down, and caused <a href="../ebd/ebd347.htm#007">Solomon</a> to ride upon <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> David's <a href="../ebd/ebd262.htm#001">mule</a>, and brought him to <a href="../ebd/ebd148.htm#007">Gihon</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="039">39</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd386.htm#008">Zadok</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> took an <a href="../ebd/ebd182.htm#003">horn</a> of oil out of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a>, and anointed <a href="../ebd/ebd347.htm#007">Solomon</a>. And they blew the trumpet; and all the people said, God save <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd347.htm#007">Solomon</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="040">40</a> And all the people came up after him, and the people piped with pipes, and rejoiced with great joy, so that the earth <a href="../ebd/ebd310.htm#007">rent</a> with the sound of them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="041">41</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd009.htm#006">Adonijah</a> and all the guests that were with him heard it as they had made an end of <a href="../ebd/ebd111.htm#006">eating</a>. And when <a href="../ebd/ebd207.htm#006">Joab</a> heard the sound of the trumpet, he said, Wherefore is this noise of the city being in an uproar?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="042">42</a> And while he yet spake, behold, <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#000">Jonathan</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd002.htm#005">Abiathar</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> came; and <a href="../ebd/ebd009.htm#006">Adonijah</a> said unto him, Come in; for thou art a valiant man, and bringest good tidings.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="043">43</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#000">Jonathan</a> answered and said to <a href="../ebd/ebd009.htm#006">Adonijah</a>, Verily our lord <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> hath made <a href="../ebd/ebd347.htm#007">Solomon</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="044">44</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> hath sent with him <a href="../ebd/ebd386.htm#008">Zadok</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd267.htm#002">Nathan</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#008">prophet</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd052.htm#003">Benaiah</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd200.htm#009">Jehoiada</a>, and the Cherethites, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd289.htm#000">Pelethites</a>, and they have caused him to ride upon the king's <a href="../ebd/ebd262.htm#001">mule</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="045">45</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd386.htm#008">Zadok</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd267.htm#002">Nathan</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#008">prophet</a> have anointed him <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> in <a href="../ebd/ebd148.htm#007">Gihon</a>: and they are come up from thence rejoicing, so that the city rang again. This is the noise that ye have heard.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="046">46</a> And also <a href="../ebd/ebd347.htm#007">Solomon</a> sitteth on the <a href="../ebd/ebd365.htm#001">throne</a> of the kingdom.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="047">47</a> And moreover the king's servants came to <a href="../ebd/ebd060.htm#008">bless</a> our lord <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a>, saying, God make the name of <a href="../ebd/ebd347.htm#007">Solomon</a> better than thy name, and make his <a href="../ebd/ebd365.htm#001">throne</a> greater than thy <a href="../ebd/ebd365.htm#001">throne</a>. And the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> bowed himself upon the bed.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="048">48</a> And also thus said the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a>, Blessed be the LORD God of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, which hath given one to sit on my <a href="../ebd/ebd365.htm#001">throne</a> this day, mine eyes even seeing it.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="049">49</a> And all the guests that were with <a href="../ebd/ebd009.htm#006">Adonijah</a> were afraid, and rose up, and went every man his way.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="050">50</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd009.htm#006">Adonijah</a> feared because of <a href="../ebd/ebd347.htm#007">Solomon</a>, and arose, and went, and caught hold on the horns of the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="051">51</a> And it was told <a href="../ebd/ebd347.htm#007">Solomon</a>, saying, Behold, <a href="../ebd/ebd009.htm#006">Adonijah</a> feareth <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd347.htm#007">Solomon</a>: for, lo, he hath caught hold on the horns of the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>, saying, Let <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd347.htm#007">Solomon</a> swear unto me today that he will not slay his servant with the <a href="../ebd/ebd354.htm#003">sword</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="052">52</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd347.htm#007">Solomon</a> said, If he will shew himself a worthy man, there shall not an hair of him fall to the earth: but if wickedness shall be found in him, he shall die.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="053">53</a> So <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd347.htm#007">Solomon</a> sent, and they brought him down from the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>. And he came and bowed himself to <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd347.htm#007">Solomon</a>: and <a href="../ebd/ebd347.htm#007">Solomon</a> said unto him, Go to thine house.<br>
 </p>
