 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Numbers Chapter 13</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> And the LORD spake unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Send thou men, that they may search the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#005">Canaan</a>, which I give unto the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>: of every <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of their fathers shall ye send a man, every one a ruler among them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> by the commandment of the LORD sent them from the <a href="../ebd/ebd381.htm#005">wilderness</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd284.htm#006">Paran</a>: all those men were heads of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And these were their names: of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd311.htm#009">Reuben</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd331.htm#003">Shammua</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd386.htm#004">Zaccur</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#002">Simeon</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd331.htm#005">Shaphat</a> the son of Hori.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd069.htm#003">Caleb</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd203.htm#004">Jephunneh</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#002">Issachar</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd186.htm#009">Igal</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#005">Joseph</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> Of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd123.htm#000">Ephraim</a>, Oshea the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd275.htm#006">Nun</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> Of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd052.htm#008">Benjamin</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd283.htm#005">Palti</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd307.htm#007">Raphu</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> Of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd389.htm#003">Zebulun</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd141.htm#001">Gaddiel</a> the son of Sodi.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> Of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#005">Joseph</a>, namely, of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd240.htm#002">Manasseh</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd141.htm#000">Gaddi</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd353.htm#008">Susi</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> Of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd096.htm#009">Dan</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd020.htm#006">Ammiel</a> the son of Gemalli.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> Of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd033.htm#008">Asher</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#004">Sethur</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd252.htm#009">Michael</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> Of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd266.htm#006">Naphtali</a>, Nahbi the son of Vophsi.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> Of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd140.htm#007">Gad</a>, Geuel the son of Machi.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> These are the names of the men which <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> sent to spy out the land. And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> called Oshea the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd275.htm#006">Nun</a> Jehoshua.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> sent them to spy out the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#005">Canaan</a>, and said unto them, Get you up this way southward, and go up into the mountain:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> And see the land, what it is, and the people that dwelleth therein, whether they be strong or weak, few or many;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> And what the land is that they dwell in, whether it be good or bad; and what cities they be that they dwell in, whether in tents, or in strong holds;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> And what the land is, whether it be <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#005">fat</a> or lean, whether there be <a href="../ebd/ebd383.htm#003">wood</a> therein, or not. And be ye of good courage, and bring of the <a href="../ebd/ebd139.htm#000">fruit</a> of the land. Now the time was the time of the firstripe grapes.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> So they went up, and searched the land from the <a href="../ebd/ebd381.htm#005">wilderness</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd394.htm#000">Zin</a> unto <a href="../ebd/ebd309.htm#006">Rehob</a>, as men come to <a href="../ebd/ebd160.htm#005">Hamath</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> And they ascended by the south, and came unto <a href="../ebd/ebd171.htm#004">Hebron</a>; where <a href="../ebd/ebd014.htm#003">Ahiman</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd336.htm#002">Sheshai</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd358.htm#001">Talmai</a>, the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd022.htm#005">Anak</a>, were. (Now <a href="../ebd/ebd171.htm#004">Hebron</a> was built <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> years before <a href="../ebd/ebd395.htm#003">Zoan</a> in <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a>.)<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> And they came unto the <a href="../ebd/ebd065.htm#006">brook</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd125.htm#000">Eshcol</a>, and cut down from thence a <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#002">branch</a> with one cluster of grapes, and they bare it between two upon a staff; and they brought of the pomegranates, and of the figs.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> The place was called the <a href="../ebd/ebd065.htm#006">brook</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd125.htm#000">Eshcol</a>, because of the cluster of grapes which the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> cut down from thence.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> And they returned from searching of the land after forty days.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> And they went and came to <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, and to <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a>, and to all the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, unto the <a href="../ebd/ebd381.htm#005">wilderness</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd284.htm#006">Paran</a>, to <a href="../ebd/ebd215.htm#003">Kadesh</a>; and brought back word unto them, and unto all the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>, and shewed them the <a href="../ebd/ebd139.htm#000">fruit</a> of the land.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> And they told him, and said, We came unto the land whither thou sentest us, and surely it floweth with <a href="../ebd/ebd255.htm#000">milk</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd181.htm#001">honey</a>; and this is the <a href="../ebd/ebd139.htm#000">fruit</a> of it.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="028">28</a> Nevertheless the people be strong that dwell in the land, and the cities are walled, and very great: and moreover we saw the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd022.htm#005">Anak</a> there.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="029">29</a> The Amalekites dwell in the land of the south: and the <a href="../ebd/ebd179.htm#006">Hittites</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd199.htm#004">Jebusites</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd021.htm#006">Amorites</a>, dwell in the mountains: and the <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#007">Canaanites</a> dwell by the sea, and by the coast of <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#004">Jordan</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="030">30</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd069.htm#003">Caleb</a> stilled the people before <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, and said, Let us go up at once, and possess it; for we are well able to overcome it.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="031">31</a> But the men that went up with him said, We be not able to go up against the people; for they are stronger than we.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="032">32</a> And they brought up an evil report of the land which they had searched unto the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, saying, The land, through which we have gone to search it, is a land that eateth up the inhabitants thereof; and all the people that we saw in it are men of a great stature.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="033">33</a> And there we saw the <a href="../ebd/ebd147.htm#006">giants</a>, the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd022.htm#005">Anak</a>, which come of the <a href="../ebd/ebd147.htm#006">giants</a>: and we were in our own sight as grasshoppers, and so we were in their sight.<br>
 </p>
