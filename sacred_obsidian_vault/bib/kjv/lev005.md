 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Leviticus Chapter 5</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> And if a soul <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a>, and hear the voice of swearing, and is a <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#008">witness</a>, whether he hath seen or known of it; if he do not utter it, then he shall bear his iniquity.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Or if a soul touch any unclean thing, whether it be a <a href="../ebd/ebd072.htm#004">carcase</a> of an unclean <a href="../ebd/ebd049.htm#001">beast</a>, or a <a href="../ebd/ebd072.htm#004">carcase</a> of unclean <a href="../ebd/ebd074.htm#002">cattle</a>, or the <a href="../ebd/ebd072.htm#004">carcase</a> of unclean creeping things, and if it be hidden from him; he also shall be unclean, and guilty.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Or if he touch the uncleanness of man, whatsoever uncleanness it be that a man shall be defiled withal, and it be hid from him; when he knoweth of it, then he shall be guilty.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Or if a soul swear, pronouncing with his lips to do evil, or to do good, whatsoever it be that a man shall pronounce with an <a href="../ebd/ebd276.htm#000">oath</a>, and it be hid from him; when he knoweth of it, then he shall be guilty in one of these.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And it shall be, when he shall be guilty in one of these things, that he shall confess that he hath sinned in that thing:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And he shall bring his trespass <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> unto the LORD for his <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> which he hath sinned, a female from the flock, a <a href="../ebd/ebd223.htm#003">lamb</a> or a kid of the goats, for a <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>; and the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> shall make an <a href="../ebd/ebd036.htm#004">atonement</a> for him concerning his <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And if he be not able to bring a <a href="../ebd/ebd223.htm#003">lamb</a>, then he shall bring for his trespass, which he hath committed, two turtledoves, or two young pigeons, unto the LORD; one for a <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, and the other for a burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And he shall bring them unto the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a>, who shall offer that which is for the <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> first, and wring off his head from his <a href="../ebd/ebd269.htm#000">neck</a>, but shall not divide it asunder:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And he shall sprinkle of the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> upon the side of the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>; and the rest of the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> shall be wrung out at the bottom of the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>: it is a <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And he shall offer the second for a burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, according to the manner: and the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> shall make an <a href="../ebd/ebd036.htm#004">atonement</a> for him for his <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> which he hath sinned, and it shall be forgiven him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> But if he be not able to bring two turtledoves, or two young pigeons, then he that sinned shall bring for his <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> the tenth part of an <a href="../ebd/ebd122.htm#003">ephah</a> of fine <a href="../ebd/ebd136.htm#001">flour</a> for a <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>; he shall put no oil upon it, neither shall he put any <a href="../ebd/ebd138.htm#004">frankincense</a> thereon: for it is a <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> Then shall he bring it to the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> shall take his handful of it, even a memorial thereof, and burn it on the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>, according to the offerings made by fire unto the LORD: it is a <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> shall make an <a href="../ebd/ebd036.htm#004">atonement</a> for him as touching his <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> that he hath sinned in one of these, and it shall be forgiven him: and the remnant shall be the priest's, as a meat <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> And the LORD spake unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> If a soul commit a trespass, and <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> through ignorance, in the holy things of the LORD; then he shall bring for his trespass unto the LORD a <a href="../ebd/ebd306.htm#003">ram</a> without <a href="../ebd/ebd060.htm#007">blemish</a> out of the flocks, with thy estimation by shekels of <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#000">silver</a>, after the <a href="../ebd/ebd333.htm#009">shekel</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd321.htm#005">sanctuary</a>, for a trespass <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> And he shall make amends for the harm that he hath done in the holy thing, and shall add the fifth part thereto, and give it unto the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a>: and the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> shall make an <a href="../ebd/ebd036.htm#004">atonement</a> for him with the <a href="../ebd/ebd306.htm#003">ram</a> of the trespass <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, and it shall be forgiven him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> And if a soul <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a>, and commit any of these things which are forbidden to be done by the commandments of the LORD; though he wist it not, yet is he guilty, and shall bear his iniquity.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> And he shall bring a <a href="../ebd/ebd306.htm#003">ram</a> without <a href="../ebd/ebd060.htm#007">blemish</a> out of the flock, with thy estimation, for a trespass <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, unto the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a>: and the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> shall make an <a href="../ebd/ebd036.htm#004">atonement</a> for him concerning his ignorance wherein he erred and wist it not, and it shall be forgiven him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> It is a trespass <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>: he hath certainly trespassed against the LORD.<br>
 </p>
