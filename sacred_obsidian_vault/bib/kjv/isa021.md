 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Isaiah Chapter 21</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> The <a href="../ebd/ebd066.htm#007">burden</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd102.htm#000">desert</a> of the sea. As whirlwinds in the south pass through; so it cometh from the <a href="../ebd/ebd102.htm#000">desert</a>, from a terrible land.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> A grievous <a href="../ebd/ebd378.htm#000">vision</a> is declared unto me; the treacherous dealer dealeth treacherously, and the spoiler spoileth. Go up, O <a href="../ebd/ebd114.htm#003">Elam</a>: besiege, O <a href="../ebd/ebd246.htm#001">Media</a>; all the sighing thereof have I made to cease.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Therefore are my loins filled with pain: pangs have taken hold upon me, as the pangs of a woman that travaileth: I was bowed down at the hearing of it; I was dismayed at the seeing of it.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> My heart panted, fearfulness affrighted me: the night of my pleasure hath he turned into fear unto me.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Prepare the table, watch in the watchtower, eat, <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a>: arise, ye princes, and <a href="../ebd/ebd024.htm#008">anoint</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd337.htm#001">shield</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> For thus hath the LORD said unto me, Go, set a watchman, let him declare what he seeth.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And he saw a <a href="../ebd/ebd077.htm#008">chariot</a> with a couple of horsemen, a <a href="../ebd/ebd077.htm#008">chariot</a> of asses, and a <a href="../ebd/ebd077.htm#008">chariot</a> of camels; and he hearkened diligently with much heed:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And he cried, A lion: My lord, I stand continually upon the watchtower in the daytime, and I am set in my <a href="../ebd/ebd379.htm#001">ward</a> whole nights:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And, behold, here cometh a <a href="../ebd/ebd077.htm#008">chariot</a> of men, with a couple of horsemen. And he answered and said, <a href="../ebd/ebd041.htm#001">Babylon</a> is fallen, is fallen; and all the graven images of her gods he hath broken unto the ground.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> O my <a href="../ebd/ebd364.htm#009">threshing</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd090.htm#006">corn</a> of my floor: that which I have heard of the LORD of hosts, the God of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, have I declared unto you.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> The <a href="../ebd/ebd066.htm#007">burden</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd109.htm#001">Dumah</a>. He calleth to me out of <a href="../ebd/ebd326.htm#004">Seir</a>, Watchman, what of the night? Watchman, what of the night?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> The watchman said, The morning cometh, and also the night: if ye will enquire, enquire ye: return, come.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> The <a href="../ebd/ebd066.htm#007">burden</a> upon <a href="../ebd/ebd027.htm#009">Arabia</a>. In the <a href="../ebd/ebd137.htm#006">forest</a> in <a href="../ebd/ebd027.htm#009">Arabia</a> shall ye <a href="../ebd/ebd231.htm#002">lodge</a>, O ye travelling companies of <a href="../ebd/ebd100.htm#006">Dedanim</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> The inhabitants of the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd361.htm#000">Tema</a> brought water to him that was thirsty, they prevented with their <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#006">bread</a> him that fled.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> For they fled from the swords, from the drawn <a href="../ebd/ebd354.htm#003">sword</a>, and from the bent <a href="../ebd/ebd063.htm#003">bow</a>, and from the grievousness of war.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> For thus hath the LORD said unto me, Within a year, according to the years of an <a href="../ebd/ebd179.htm#004">hireling</a>, and all the <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd216.htm#003">Kedar</a> shall fail:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> And the residue of the number of archers, the mighty men of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd216.htm#003">Kedar</a>, shall be diminished: for the LORD God of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> hath spoken it.<br>
 </p>
