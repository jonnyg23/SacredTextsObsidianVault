 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Job Chapter 25</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Then answered <a href="../ebd/ebd058.htm#006">Bildad</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd341.htm#008">Shuhite</a>, and said,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Dominion and fear are with him, he maketh peace in his high places.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Is there any number of his armies? and upon whom doth not his light arise?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> How then can man be justified with God? or how can he be <a href="../ebd/ebd084.htm#005">clean</a> that is born of a woman?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Behold even to the <a href="../ebd/ebd259.htm#004">moon</a>, and it shineth not; yea, the <a href="../ebd/ebd350.htm#008">stars</a> are not pure in his sight.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> How much less man, that is a <a href="../ebd/ebd384.htm#000">worm</a>? and the son of man, which is a <a href="../ebd/ebd384.htm#000">worm</a>?<br>
 </p>
