 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 92</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> IT IS A GOOD THING TO GIVE THANKS UNTO THE LORD, AND TO SING PRAISES UNTO THY NAME, O MOST HIGH:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> To shew forth thy lovingkindness in the morning, and thy faithfulness every night,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Upon an instrument of ten strings, and upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd301.htm#006">psaltery</a>; upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd165.htm#001">harp</a> with a solemn sound.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> For thou, LORD, hast made me glad through thy work: I will triumph in the works of thy hands.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> O LORD, how great are thy works! and thy thoughts are very <a href="../ebd/ebd100.htm#008">deep</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> A brutish man knoweth not; neither doth a fool understand this.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> When the wicked spring as the <a href="../ebd/ebd154.htm#005">grass</a>, and when all the workers of iniquity do flourish; it is that they shall be destroyed for ever:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> But thou, LORD, art most high for evermore.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> For, lo, thine enemies, O LORD, for, lo, thine enemies shall perish; all the workers of iniquity shall be scattered.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> But my <a href="../ebd/ebd182.htm#003">horn</a> shalt thou exalt like the <a href="../ebd/ebd182.htm#003">horn</a> of an <a href="../ebd/ebd374.htm#007">unicorn</a>: I shall be anointed with fresh oil.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> Mine eye also shall see my desire on mine enemies, and mine ears shall hear my desire of the wicked that rise up against me.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> The righteous shall flourish like the palm tree: he shall grow like a <a href="../ebd/ebd074.htm#007">cedar</a> in <a href="../ebd/ebd226.htm#001">Lebanon</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> Those that be planted in the house of the LORD shall flourish in the courts of our God.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> They shall still bring forth <a href="../ebd/ebd139.htm#000">fruit</a> in old age; they shall be <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#005">fat</a> and flourishing;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> To shew that the LORD is upright: he is my <a href="../ebd/ebd315.htm#001">rock</a>, and there is no unrighteousness in him.<br>
 </p>
