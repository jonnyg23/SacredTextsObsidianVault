 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: 2 Chronicles Chapter 13</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Now in the eighteenth year of <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#000">Jeroboam</a> began <a href="../ebd/ebd003.htm#006">Abijah</a> to reign over <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> He reigned three years in <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>. His mother's name also was <a href="../ebd/ebd253.htm#000">Michaiah</a> the daughter of <a href="../ebd/ebd375.htm#003">Uriel</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd147.htm#008">Gibeah</a>. And there was war between <a href="../ebd/ebd003.htm#006">Abijah</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#000">Jeroboam</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd003.htm#006">Abijah</a> set the battle in array with an army of valiant men of war, even four hundred thousand <a href="../ebd/ebd081.htm#008">chosen</a> men: <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#000">Jeroboam</a> also set the battle in array against him with eight hundred thousand <a href="../ebd/ebd081.htm#008">chosen</a> men, being mighty men of valour.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd003.htm#006">Abijah</a> stood up upon <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#007">mount</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd390.htm#004">Zemaraim</a>, which is in <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#007">mount</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd123.htm#000">Ephraim</a>, and said, Hear me, thou <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#000">Jeroboam</a>, and all <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Ought ye not to know that the LORD God of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> gave the kingdom over <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> to <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> for ever, even to him and to his sons by a <a href="../ebd/ebd091.htm#008">covenant</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd319.htm#008">salt</a>?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Yet <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#000">Jeroboam</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd268.htm#003">Nebat</a>, the servant of <a href="../ebd/ebd347.htm#007">Solomon</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a>, is risen up, and hath rebelled against his lord.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And there are gathered unto him vain men, the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd051.htm#007">Belial</a>, and have strengthened themselves against <a href="../ebd/ebd309.htm#007">Rehoboam</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd347.htm#007">Solomon</a>, when <a href="../ebd/ebd309.htm#007">Rehoboam</a> was young and tenderhearted, and could not withstand them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And now ye think to withstand the kingdom of the LORD in the hand of the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a>; and ye be a great multitude, and there are with your golden calves, which <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#000">Jeroboam</a> made you for gods.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> Have ye not cast out the priests of the LORD, the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a>, and the Levites, and have made you priests after the manner of the nations of other lands? so that whosoever cometh to consecrate himself with a young <a href="../ebd/ebd066.htm#003">bullock</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> rams, the same may be a <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> of them that are no gods.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> But as for us, the LORD is our God, and we have not forsaken him; and the priests, which <a href="../ebd/ebd255.htm#007">minister</a> unto the LORD, are the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a>, and the Levites wait upon their business:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And they burn unto the LORD every morning and every evening burnt sacrifices and sweet <a href="../ebd/ebd188.htm#002">incense</a>: the <a href="../ebd/ebd336.htm#008">shewbread</a> also set they in order upon the pure table; and the <a href="../ebd/ebd071.htm#001">candlestick</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a> with the lamps thereof, to burn every evening: for we keep the charge of the LORD our God; but ye have forsaken him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And, behold, God himself is with us for our captain, and his priests with sounding <a href="../ebd/ebd373.htm#001">trumpets</a> to cry <a href="../ebd/ebd016.htm#007">alarm</a> against you. O children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, fight ye not against the LORD God of your fathers; for ye shall not prosper.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> But <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#000">Jeroboam</a> caused an ambushment to come about behind them: so they were before <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>, and the ambushment was behind them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> And when <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a> looked back, behold, the battle was before and behind: and they cried unto the LORD, and the priests sounded with the <a href="../ebd/ebd373.htm#001">trumpets</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> Then the men of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a> gave a shout: and as the men of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a> shouted, it came to pass, that God smote <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#000">Jeroboam</a> and all <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> before <a href="../ebd/ebd003.htm#006">Abijah</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> And the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> fled before <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>: and God delivered them into their hand.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd003.htm#006">Abijah</a> and his people slew them with a great slaughter: so there fell down slain of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> five hundred thousand <a href="../ebd/ebd081.htm#008">chosen</a> men.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> Thus the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> were brought under at that time, and the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a> prevailed, because they relied upon the LORD God of their fathers.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd003.htm#006">Abijah</a> pursued after <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#000">Jeroboam</a>, and took cities from him, <a href="../ebd/ebd055.htm#006">Bethel</a> with the towns thereof, and <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#008">Jeshanah</a> with the towns thereof, and <a href="../ebd/ebd123.htm#000">Ephraim</a> with the towns thereof.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> Neither did <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#000">Jeroboam</a> recover strength again in the days of <a href="../ebd/ebd003.htm#006">Abijah</a>: and the LORD struck him, and he died.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> But <a href="../ebd/ebd003.htm#006">Abijah</a> waxed mighty, and married fourteen wives, and begat twenty and two sons, and sixteen daughters.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> And the rest of the acts of <a href="../ebd/ebd003.htm#006">Abijah</a>, and his ways, and his sayings, are written in the story of the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#008">prophet</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd186.htm#005">Iddo</a>.<br>
 </p>
