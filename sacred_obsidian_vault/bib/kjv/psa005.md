 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 5</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Give <a href="../ebd/ebd110.htm#004">ear</a> to my words, O LORD, consider my meditation.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Hearken unto the voice of my cry, my <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">King</a>, and my God: for unto thee will I pray.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> My voice shalt thou hear in the morning, O LORD; in the morning will I direct my <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#000">prayer</a> unto thee, and will look up.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> For thou art not a God that hath pleasure in wickedness: neither shall evil dwell with thee.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> The foolish shall not stand in thy sight: thou hatest all workers of iniquity.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Thou shalt destroy them that speak <a href="../ebd/ebd225.htm#008">leasing</a>: the LORD will abhor the bloody and deceitful man.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> But as for me, I will come into thy house in the multitude of thy <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a>: and in thy fear will I <a href="../ebd/ebd384.htm#002">worship</a> toward thy holy <a href="../ebd/ebd361.htm#004">temple</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> Lead me, O LORD, in thy <a href="../ebd/ebd313.htm#004">righteousness</a> because of mine enemies; make thy way straight before my face.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> For there is no faithfulness in their mouth; their inward part is very wickedness; their throat is an open <a href="../ebd/ebd328.htm#001">sepulchre</a>; they flatter with their tongue.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> Destroy thou them, O God; let them fall by their own counsels; cast them out in the multitude of their transgressions; for they have rebelled against thee.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> But let all those that put their trust in thee rejoice: let them ever shout for joy, because thou defendest them: let them also that love thy name be joyful in thee.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> For thou, LORD, wilt <a href="../ebd/ebd060.htm#008">bless</a> the righteous; with favour wilt thou compass him as with a <a href="../ebd/ebd337.htm#001">shield</a>.<br>
 </p>
