 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 94</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> O Lord God, to whom vengeance belongeth; O God, to whom vengeance belongeth, shew thyself.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Lift up thyself, thou <a href="../ebd/ebd213.htm#005">judge</a> of the earth: render a reward to the proud.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> LORD, how long shall the wicked, how long shall the wicked triumph?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> How long shall they utter and speak hard things? and all the workers of iniquity boast themselves?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> They break in <a href="../ebd/ebd295.htm#002">pieces</a> thy people, O LORD, and afflict thine heritage.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> They slay the widow and the <a href="../ebd/ebd351.htm#009">stranger</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd262.htm#002">murder</a> the fatherless.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Yet they say, The LORD shall not see, neither shall the God of <a href="../ebd/ebd194.htm#007">Jacob</a> regard it.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> Understand, ye brutish among the people: and ye fools, when will ye be <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#004">wise</a>?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> He that planted the <a href="../ebd/ebd110.htm#004">ear</a>, shall he not hear? he that formed the eye, shall he not see?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> He that chastiseth the <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#005">heathen</a>, shall not he correct? he that teacheth man knowledge, shall not he know?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> The LORD knoweth the thoughts of man, that they are vanity.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> Blessed is the man whom thou chastenest, O LORD, and teachest him out of thy law;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> That thou mayest give him rest from the days of adversity, until the <a href="../ebd/ebd296.htm#007">pit</a> be digged for the wicked.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> For the LORD will not cast off his people, neither will he forsake his inheritance.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> But judgment shall return unto <a href="../ebd/ebd313.htm#004">righteousness</a>: and all the upright in heart shall follow it.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> Who will rise up for me against the evildoers? or who will stand up for me against the workers of iniquity?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> Unless the LORD had been my help, my soul had almost dwelt in silence.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> When I said, My foot slippeth; thy <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a>, O LORD, held me up.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> In the multitude of my thoughts within me thy comforts delight my soul.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> Shall the <a href="../ebd/ebd365.htm#001">throne</a> of iniquity have <a href="../ebd/ebd132.htm#002">fellowship</a> with thee, which frameth mischief by a law?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> They gather themselves together against the soul of the righteous, and condemn the innocent <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> But the LORD is my defence; and my God is the <a href="../ebd/ebd315.htm#001">rock</a> of my refuge.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> And he shall bring upon them their own iniquity, and shall cut them off in their own wickedness; yea, the LORD our God shall cut them off.<br>
 </p>
