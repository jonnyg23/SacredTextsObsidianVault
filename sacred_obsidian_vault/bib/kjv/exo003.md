 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Exodus Chapter 3</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Now <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> kept the flock of <a href="../ebd/ebd206.htm#000">Jethro</a> his father in law, the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd253.htm#007">Midian</a>: and he led the flock to the backside of the <a href="../ebd/ebd102.htm#000">desert</a>, and came to the mountain of God, even to <a href="../ebd/ebd181.htm#009">Horeb</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd024.htm#001">angel</a> of the LORD appeared unto him in a flame of fire out of the midst of a <a href="../ebd/ebd067.htm#000">bush</a>: and he looked, and, behold, the <a href="../ebd/ebd067.htm#000">bush</a> burned with fire, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd067.htm#000">bush</a> was not consumed.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> said, I will now turn aside, and see this great sight, why the <a href="../ebd/ebd067.htm#000">bush</a> is not burnt.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And when the LORD saw that he turned aside to see, God called unto him out of the midst of the <a href="../ebd/ebd067.htm#000">bush</a>, and said, <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>. And he said, Here am I.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And he said, Draw not nigh hither: put off thy shoes from off thy feet, for the place whereon thou standest is holy ground.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Moreover he said, I am the God of thy father, the God of <a href="../ebd/ebd005.htm#005">Abraham</a>, the God of <a href="../ebd/ebd189.htm#006">Isaac</a>, and the God of <a href="../ebd/ebd194.htm#007">Jacob</a>. And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> hid his face; for he was afraid to look upon God.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And the LORD said, I have surely seen the affliction of my people which are in <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a>, and have heard their cry by reason of their taskmasters; for I know their sorrows;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And I am come down to deliver them out of the hand of the Egyptians, and to bring them up out of that land unto a good land and a large, unto a land flowing with <a href="../ebd/ebd255.htm#000">milk</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd181.htm#001">honey</a>; unto the place of the <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#007">Canaanites</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd179.htm#006">Hittites</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd021.htm#006">Amorites</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd290.htm#006">Perizzites</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd179.htm#007">Hivites</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd199.htm#004">Jebusites</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> Now therefore, behold, the cry of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> is come unto me: and I have also seen the oppression wherewith the Egyptians oppress them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> Come now therefore, and I will send thee unto <a href="../ebd/ebd292.htm#005">Pharaoh</a>, that thou mayest bring forth my people the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> out of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> said unto God, Who am I, that I should go unto <a href="../ebd/ebd292.htm#005">Pharaoh</a>, and that I should bring forth the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> out of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a>?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And he said, Certainly I will be with thee; and this shall be a token unto thee, that I have sent thee: When thou hast brought forth the people out of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a>, ye shall serve God upon this mountain.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> said unto God, Behold, when I come unto the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, and shall say unto them, The God of your fathers hath sent me unto you; and they shall say to me, What is his name? what shall I say unto them?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> And God said unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, I AM THAT I AM: and he said, Thus shalt thou say unto the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, I AM hath sent me unto you.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And God said moreover unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, Thus shalt thou say unto the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, the LORD God of your fathers, the God of <a href="../ebd/ebd005.htm#005">Abraham</a>, the God of <a href="../ebd/ebd189.htm#006">Isaac</a>, and the God of <a href="../ebd/ebd194.htm#007">Jacob</a>, hath sent me unto you: this is my name for ever, and this is my memorial unto all generations.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> Go, and gather the elders of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> together, and say unto them, The LORD God of your fathers, the God of <a href="../ebd/ebd005.htm#005">Abraham</a>, of <a href="../ebd/ebd189.htm#006">Isaac</a>, and of <a href="../ebd/ebd194.htm#007">Jacob</a>, appeared unto me, saying, I have surely visited you, and seen that which is done to you in <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> And I have said, I will bring you up out of the affliction of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a> unto the land of the <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#007">Canaanites</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd179.htm#006">Hittites</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd021.htm#006">Amorites</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd290.htm#006">Perizzites</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd179.htm#007">Hivites</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd199.htm#004">Jebusites</a>, unto a land flowing with <a href="../ebd/ebd255.htm#000">milk</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd181.htm#001">honey</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> And they shall hearken to thy voice: and thou shalt come, thou and the elders of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, unto the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a>, and ye shall say unto him, The LORD God of the <a href="../ebd/ebd171.htm#001">Hebrews</a> hath met with us: and now let us go, we beseech thee, three days' <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#001">journey</a> into the <a href="../ebd/ebd381.htm#005">wilderness</a>, that we may <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#002">sacrifice</a> to the LORD our God.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> And I am sure that the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a> will not let you go, no, not by a mighty hand.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> And I will stretch out my hand, and smite <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a> with all my wonders which I will do in the midst thereof: and after that he will let you go.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> And I will give this people favour in the sight of the Egyptians: and it shall come to pass, that, when ye go, ye shall not go empty.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> But every woman shall <a href="../ebd/ebd062.htm#007">borrow</a> of her neighbour, and of her that sojourneth in her house, jewels of <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#000">silver</a>, and jewels of <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a>, and raiment: and ye shall put them upon your sons, and upon your daughters; and ye shall spoil the Egyptians.<br>
 </p>
