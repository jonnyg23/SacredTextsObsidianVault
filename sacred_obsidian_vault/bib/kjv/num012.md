 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Numbers Chapter 12</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd256.htm#003">Miriam</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> spake against <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> because of the Ethiopian woman whom he had married: for he had married an Ethiopian woman.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> And they said, Hath the LORD indeed spoken only by <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>? hath he not spoken also by us? And the LORD heard it.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> (Now the man <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> was very meek, above all the men which were upon the face of the earth.)<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And the LORD spake suddenly unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, and unto <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a>, and unto <a href="../ebd/ebd256.htm#003">Miriam</a>, Come out ye three unto the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>. And they three came out.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And the LORD came down in the <a href="../ebd/ebd295.htm#007">pillar</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd085.htm#001">cloud</a>, and stood in the door of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a>, and called <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd256.htm#003">Miriam</a>: and they both came forth.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And he said, Hear now my words: If there be a <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#008">prophet</a> among you, I the LORD will make myself known unto him in a <a href="../ebd/ebd378.htm#000">vision</a>, and will speak unto him in a <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#005">dream</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> My servant <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> is not so, who is <a href="../ebd/ebd130.htm#005">faithful</a> in all mine house.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> With him will I speak mouth to mouth, even apparently, and not in dark speeches; and the similitude of the LORD shall he behold: wherefore then were ye not afraid to speak against my servant <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd024.htm#002">anger</a> of the LORD was kindled against them; and he departed.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd085.htm#001">cloud</a> departed from off the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a>; and, behold, <a href="../ebd/ebd256.htm#003">Miriam</a> became leprous, white as <a href="../ebd/ebd347.htm#000">snow</a>: and <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> looked upon <a href="../ebd/ebd256.htm#003">Miriam</a>, and, behold, she was leprous.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> said unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, Alas, my lord, I beseech thee, lay not the <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> upon us, wherein we have done foolishly, and wherein we have sinned.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> Let her not be as one dead, of whom the <a href="../ebd/ebd135.htm#007">flesh</a> is half consumed when he cometh out of his mother's womb.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> cried unto the LORD, saying, Heal her now, O God, I beseech thee.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> And the LORD said unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, If her father had but spit in her face, should she not be ashamed <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> days? let her be shut out from the <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#002">camp</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> days, and after that let her be received in again.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd256.htm#003">Miriam</a> was shut out from the <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#002">camp</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> days: and the people journeyed not till <a href="../ebd/ebd256.htm#003">Miriam</a> was brought in again.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> And afterward the people removed from <a href="../ebd/ebd169.htm#003">Hazeroth</a>, and pitched in the <a href="../ebd/ebd381.htm#005">wilderness</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd284.htm#006">Paran</a>.<br>
 </p>
