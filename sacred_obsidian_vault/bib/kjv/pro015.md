 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Proverbs Chapter 15</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> A soft answer turneth away wrath: but grievous words stir up <a href="../ebd/ebd024.htm#002">anger</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> The tongue of the <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#004">wise</a> useth knowledge aright: but the mouth of fools poureth out foolishness.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> The eyes of the LORD are in every place, beholding the evil and the good.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> A wholesome tongue is a tree of life: but perverseness therein is a <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#005">breach</a> in the <a href="../ebd/ebd349.htm#009">spirit</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> A fool despiseth his father's instruction: but he that regardeth reproof is prudent.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> In the house of the righteous is much treasure: but in the revenues of the wicked is trouble.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> The lips of the <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#004">wise</a> disperse knowledge: but the heart of the foolish doeth not so.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> The <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#002">sacrifice</a> of the wicked is an <a href="../ebd/ebd005.htm#003">abomination</a> to the LORD: but the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#000">prayer</a> of the upright is his delight.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> The way of the wicked is an <a href="../ebd/ebd005.htm#003">abomination</a> unto the LORD: but he loveth him that followeth after <a href="../ebd/ebd313.htm#004">righteousness</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> Correction is grievous unto him that forsaketh the way: and he that hateth reproof shall die.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd173.htm#003">Hell</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd102.htm#004">destruction</a> are before the LORD: how much more then the hearts of the children of men?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> A scorner loveth not one that reproveth him: neither will he go unto the <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#004">wise</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> A merry heart maketh a cheerful countenance: but by sorrow of the heart the <a href="../ebd/ebd349.htm#009">spirit</a> is broken.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> The heart of him that hath understanding seeketh knowledge: but the mouth of fools feedeth on foolishness.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> All the days of the afflicted are evil: but he that is of a merry heart hath a continual <a href="../ebd/ebd132.htm#000">feast</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> Better is little with the fear of the LORD than great treasure and trouble therewith.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> Better is a dinner of herbs where love is, than a stalled <a href="../ebd/ebd281.htm#007">ox</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd167.htm#002">hatred</a> therewith.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> A wrathful man stirreth up strife: but he that is slow to <a href="../ebd/ebd024.htm#002">anger</a> appeaseth strife.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> The way of the slothful man is as an hedge of thorns: but the way of the righteous is made <a href="../ebd/ebd297.htm#002">plain</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> A <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#004">wise</a> son maketh a glad father: but a foolish man despiseth his mother.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> Folly is joy to him that is destitute of wisdom: but a man of understanding walketh uprightly.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> Without counsel purposes are disappointed: but in the multitude of counsellors they are established.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> A man hath joy by the answer of his mouth: and a word spoken in due season, how good is it!<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> The way of life is above to the <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#004">wise</a>, that he may depart from <a href="../ebd/ebd173.htm#003">hell</a> beneath.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> The LORD will destroy the house of the proud: but he will establish the border of the widow.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> The thoughts of the wicked are an <a href="../ebd/ebd005.htm#003">abomination</a> to the LORD: but the words of the pure are pleasant words.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> He that is greedy of gain troubleth his own house; but he that hateth gifts shall live.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="028">28</a> The heart of the righteous studieth to answer: but the mouth of the wicked poureth out evil things.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="029">29</a> The LORD is far from the wicked: but he heareth the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#000">prayer</a> of the righteous.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="030">30</a> The light of the eyes rejoiceth the heart: and a good report maketh the bones <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#005">fat</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="031">31</a> The <a href="../ebd/ebd110.htm#004">ear</a> that heareth the reproof of life abideth among the <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#004">wise</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="032">32</a> He that refuseth instruction despiseth his own soul: but he that heareth reproof getteth understanding.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="033">33</a> The fear of the LORD is the instruction of wisdom; and before honour is <a href="../ebd/ebd184.htm#007">humility</a>.<br>
 </p>
