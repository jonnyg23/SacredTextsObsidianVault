 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Amos Chapter 7</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Thus hath the Lord GOD shewed unto me; and, behold, he formed grasshoppers in the beginning of the shooting up of the latter growth; and, lo, it was the latter growth after the king's mowings.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> And it came to pass, that when they had made an end of <a href="../ebd/ebd111.htm#006">eating</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd154.htm#005">grass</a> of the land, then I said, O Lord GOD, forgive, I beseech thee: by whom shall <a href="../ebd/ebd194.htm#007">Jacob</a> arise? for he is small.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> The LORD repented for this: It shall not be, saith the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Thus hath the Lord GOD shewed unto me: and, behold, the Lord GOD called to contend by fire, and it devoured the great <a href="../ebd/ebd100.htm#008">deep</a>, and did eat up a part.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Then said I, O Lord GOD, cease, I beseech thee: by whom shall <a href="../ebd/ebd194.htm#007">Jacob</a> arise? for he is small.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> The LORD repented for this: This also shall not be, saith the Lord GOD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Thus he shewed me: and, behold, the LORD stood upon a wall made by a plumbline, with a plumbline in his hand.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And the LORD said unto me, <a href="../ebd/ebd021.htm#007">Amos</a>, what seest thou? And I said, A plumbline. Then said the LORD, Behold, I will set a plumbline in the midst of my people <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>: I will not again pass by them any more:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And the high places of <a href="../ebd/ebd189.htm#006">Isaac</a> shall be desolate, and the sanctuaries of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> shall be laid waste; and I will rise against the house of <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#000">Jeroboam</a> with the <a href="../ebd/ebd354.htm#003">sword</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> Then <a href="../ebd/ebd019.htm#007">Amaziah</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd055.htm#006">Bethel</a> sent to <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#000">Jeroboam</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, saying, <a href="../ebd/ebd021.htm#007">Amos</a> hath conspired against thee in the midst of the house of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>: the land is not able to bear all his words.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> For thus <a href="../ebd/ebd021.htm#007">Amos</a> saith, <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#000">Jeroboam</a> shall die by the <a href="../ebd/ebd354.htm#003">sword</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> shall surely be led away <a href="../ebd/ebd072.htm#001">captive</a> out of their own land.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> Also <a href="../ebd/ebd019.htm#007">Amaziah</a> said unto <a href="../ebd/ebd021.htm#007">Amos</a>, O thou <a href="../ebd/ebd326.htm#000">seer</a>, go, flee thee away into the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>, and there eat <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#006">bread</a>, and prophesy there:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> But prophesy not again any more at <a href="../ebd/ebd055.htm#006">Bethel</a>: for it is the king's <a href="../ebd/ebd077.htm#003">chapel</a>, and it is the king's court.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> Then answered <a href="../ebd/ebd021.htm#007">Amos</a>, and said to <a href="../ebd/ebd019.htm#007">Amaziah</a>, I was no <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#008">prophet</a>, neither was I a prophet's son; but I was an herdman, and a gatherer of sycomore <a href="../ebd/ebd139.htm#000">fruit</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And the LORD took me as I followed the flock, and the LORD said unto me, Go, prophesy unto my people <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> Now therefore hear thou the word of the LORD: Thou sayest, Prophesy not against <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, and drop not thy word against the house of <a href="../ebd/ebd189.htm#006">Isaac</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> Therefore thus saith the LORD; Thy wife shall be an <a href="../ebd/ebd164.htm#005">harlot</a> in the city, and thy sons and thy daughters shall fall by the <a href="../ebd/ebd354.htm#003">sword</a>, and thy land shall be divided by line; and thou shalt die in a polluted land: and <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> shall surely go into <a href="../ebd/ebd072.htm#002">captivity</a> forth of his land.<br>
 </p>
