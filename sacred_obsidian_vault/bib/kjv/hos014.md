 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Hosea Chapter 14</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> O <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">israel</a>, return unto the LORD thy God; for thou hast fallen by thine iniquity.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Take with you words, and turn to the LORD: say unto him, Take away all iniquity, and receive us graciously: so will we render the calves of our lips.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd035.htm#002">Asshur</a> shall not save us; we will not ride upon horses: neither will we say any more to the work of our hands, Ye are our gods: for in thee the fatherless findeth <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> I will heal their backsliding, I will love them freely: for mine <a href="../ebd/ebd024.htm#002">anger</a> is turned away from him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> I will be as the <a href="../ebd/ebd102.htm#008">dew</a> unto <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>: he shall grow as the <a href="../ebd/ebd229.htm#005">lily</a>, and cast forth his roots as <a href="../ebd/ebd226.htm#001">Lebanon</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> His branches shall spread, and his beauty shall be as the <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#009">olive</a> tree, and his smell as <a href="../ebd/ebd226.htm#001">Lebanon</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> They that dwell under his <a href="../ebd/ebd330.htm#002">shadow</a> shall return; they shall revive as the <a href="../ebd/ebd090.htm#006">corn</a>, and grow as the <a href="../ebd/ebd377.htm#004">vine</a>: the scent thereof shall be as the <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#000">wine</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd226.htm#001">Lebanon</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd123.htm#000">Ephraim</a> shall say, What have I to do any more with idols? I have heard him, and observed him: I am like a green <a href="../ebd/ebd133.htm#005">fir</a> tree. From me is thy <a href="../ebd/ebd139.htm#000">fruit</a> found.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> Who is <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#004">wise</a>, and he shall understand these things? prudent, and he shall know them? for the ways of the LORD are right, and the just shall walk in them: but the transgressors shall fall therein.<br>
 </p>
