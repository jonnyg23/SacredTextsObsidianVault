 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: 1 Kings (1 Samuel) Chapter 27</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> said in his heart, I shall now perish one day by the hand of <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#002">Saul</a>: there is nothing better for me than that I should speedily escape into the land of the <a href="../ebd/ebd294.htm#002">Philistines</a>; and <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#002">Saul</a> shall despair of me, to seek me any more in any coast of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>: so shall I escape out of his hand.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> arose, and he passed over with the six hundred men that were with him unto <a href="../ebd/ebd006.htm#008">Achish</a>, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd241.htm#000">Maoch</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd143.htm#009">Gath</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> dwelt with <a href="../ebd/ebd006.htm#008">Achish</a> at <a href="../ebd/ebd143.htm#009">Gath</a>, he and his men, every man with his household, even <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> with his two wives, <a href="../ebd/ebd014.htm#006">Ahinoam</a> the Jezreelitess, and <a href="../ebd/ebd003.htm#002">Abigail</a> the Carmelitess, Nabal's wife.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And it was told <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#002">Saul</a> that <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> was fled to <a href="../ebd/ebd143.htm#009">Gath</a>: and he sought no more again for him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> said unto <a href="../ebd/ebd006.htm#008">Achish</a>, If I have now found <a href="../ebd/ebd154.htm#000">grace</a> in thine eyes, let them give me a place in some town in the country, that I may dwell there: for why should thy servant dwell in the royal city with thee?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Then <a href="../ebd/ebd006.htm#008">Achish</a> gave him <a href="../ebd/ebd393.htm#003">Ziklag</a> that day: wherefore <a href="../ebd/ebd393.htm#003">Ziklag</a> pertaineth unto the kings of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a> unto this day.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And the time that <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> dwelt in the country of the <a href="../ebd/ebd294.htm#002">Philistines</a> was a full year and four months.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> and his men went up, and invaded the <a href="../ebd/ebd147.htm#002">Geshurites</a>, and the Gezrites, and the Amalekites: for those nations were of old the inhabitants of the land, as thou goest to <a href="../ebd/ebd342.htm#002">Shur</a>, even unto the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> smote the land, and left neither man nor woman alive, and took away the <a href="../ebd/ebd333.htm#005">sheep</a>, and the oxen, and the asses, and the camels, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd027.htm#000">apparel</a>, and returned, and came to <a href="../ebd/ebd006.htm#008">Achish</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd006.htm#008">Achish</a> said, Whither have ye made a road to day? And <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> said, Against the south of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>, and against the south of the Jerahmeelites, and against the south of the <a href="../ebd/ebd217.htm#004">Kenites</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> saved neither man nor woman alive, to bring tidings to <a href="../ebd/ebd143.htm#009">Gath</a>, saying, Lest they should tell on us, saying, So did <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a>, and so will be his manner all the while he dwelleth in the country of the <a href="../ebd/ebd294.htm#002">Philistines</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd006.htm#008">Achish</a> believed <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a>, saying, He hath made his people <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> utterly to abhor him; therefore he shall be my servant for ever.<br>
 </p>
