 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: 2 Thessalonians Chapter 1</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd287.htm#002">Paul</a>, and Silvanus, and <a href="../ebd/ebd367.htm#001">Timotheus</a>, unto the church of the Thessalonians in God our Father and the Lord <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd154.htm#000">Grace</a> unto you, and peace, from God our Father and the Lord <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> We are bound to thank God always for you, brethren, as it is meet, because that your faith groweth exceedingly, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd077.htm#009">charity</a> of every one of you all toward each other aboundeth;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> So that we ourselves <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a> in you in the churches of God for your patience and faith in all your persecutions and tribulations that ye endure:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Which is a manifest token of the righteous judgment of God, that ye may be counted worthy of the kingdom of God, for which ye also suffer:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Seeing it is a righteous thing with God to recompense <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#005">tribulation</a> to them that trouble you;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And to you who are troubled rest with us, when the Lord <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a> shall be revealed from <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#006">heaven</a> with his mighty angels,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> In flaming fire taking vengeance on them that know not God, and that obey not the <a href="../ebd/ebd153.htm#003">gospel</a> of our Lord <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> Who shall be punished with <a href="../ebd/ebd127.htm#007">everlasting</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd102.htm#004">destruction</a> from the presence of the Lord, and from the <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a> of his power;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> When he shall come to be glorified in his saints, and to be admired in all them that believe (because our <a href="../ebd/ebd362.htm#008">testimony</a> among you was believed) in that day.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> Wherefore also we pray always for you, that our God would count you worthy of this <a href="../ebd/ebd069.htm#007">calling</a>, and fulfil all the good pleasure of his <a href="../ebd/ebd153.htm#000">goodness</a>, and the work of faith with power:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> That the name of our Lord <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a> may be glorified in you, and ye in him, according to the <a href="../ebd/ebd154.htm#000">grace</a> of our God and the Lord <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a>.<br>
 </p>
