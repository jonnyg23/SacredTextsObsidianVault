 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 116</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> I love the LORD, because he hath heard my voice and my supplications.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Because he hath inclined his <a href="../ebd/ebd110.htm#004">ear</a> unto me, therefore will I call upon him as long as I live.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> The sorrows of death compassed me, and the pains of <a href="../ebd/ebd173.htm#003">hell</a> gat hold upon me: I found trouble and sorrow.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Then called I upon the name of the LORD; O LORD, I beseech thee, deliver my soul.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Gracious is the LORD, and righteous; yea, our God is merciful.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> The LORD preserveth the simple: I was brought low, and he helped me.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Return unto thy rest, O my soul; for the LORD hath dealt bountifully with thee.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> For thou hast delivered my soul from death, mine eyes from tears, and my feet from falling.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> I will walk before the LORD in the land of the living.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> I believed, therefore have I spoken: I was greatly afflicted:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> I said in my haste, All men are liars.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> What shall I render unto the LORD for all his benefits toward me?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> I will take the <a href="../ebd/ebd094.htm#000">cup</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd320.htm#003">salvation</a>, and call upon the name of the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> I will pay my <a href="../ebd/ebd378.htm#001">vows</a> unto the LORD now in the presence of all his people.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> Precious in the sight of the LORD is the death of his saints.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> O LORD, truly I am thy servant; I am thy servant, and the son of thine <a href="../ebd/ebd162.htm#008">handmaid</a>: thou hast loosed my bonds.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> I will offer to thee the <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#002">sacrifice</a> of thanksgiving, and will call upon the name of the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> I will pay my <a href="../ebd/ebd378.htm#001">vows</a> unto the LORD now in the presence of all his people.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> In the courts of the LORD's house, in the midst of thee, O <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>. Praise ye the LORD.<br>
 </p>
