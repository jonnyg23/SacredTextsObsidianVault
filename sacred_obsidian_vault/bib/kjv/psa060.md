 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 60</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> O God, thou hast cast us off, thou hast scattered us, thou hast been displeased; O turn thyself to us again.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Thou hast made the earth to tremble; thou hast broken it: heal the breaches thereof; for it shaketh.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Thou hast shewed thy people hard things: thou hast made us to <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#000">wine</a> of astonishment.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Thou hast given a <a href="../ebd/ebd043.htm#005">banner</a> to them that fear thee, that it may be displayed because of the truth. <a href="../ebd/ebd326.htm#008">Selah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> That thy beloved may be delivered; save with thy right hand, and hear me.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> God hath spoken in his <a href="../ebd/ebd180.htm#006">holiness</a>; I will rejoice, I will divide <a href="../ebd/ebd333.htm#003">Shechem</a>, and mete out the <a href="../ebd/ebd376.htm#008">valley</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd352.htm#008">Succoth</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd148.htm#009">Gilead</a> is mine, and <a href="../ebd/ebd240.htm#002">Manasseh</a> is mine; <a href="../ebd/ebd123.htm#000">Ephraim</a> also is the strength of mine head; <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a> is my lawgiver;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd258.htm#005">Moab</a> is my washpot; over <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#001">Edom</a> will I cast out my <a href="../ebd/ebd340.htm#009">shoe</a>: <a href="../ebd/ebd294.htm#001">Philistia</a>, triumph thou because of me.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> Who will bring me into the strong city? who will lead me into <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#001">Edom</a>?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> Wilt not thou, O God, which hadst cast us off? and thou, O God, which didst not go out with our armies?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> Give us help from trouble: for vain is the help of man.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> Through God we shall do valiantly: for he it is that shall tread down our enemies.<br>
 </p>
