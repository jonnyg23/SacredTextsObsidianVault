 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Esther Chapter 7</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> So the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd160.htm#004">Haman</a> came to <a href="../ebd/ebd043.htm#006">banquet</a> with <a href="../ebd/ebd125.htm#006">Esther</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd303.htm#007">queen</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> said again unto <a href="../ebd/ebd125.htm#006">Esther</a> on the second day at the <a href="../ebd/ebd043.htm#006">banquet</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#000">wine</a>, What is thy petition, <a href="../ebd/ebd303.htm#007">queen</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd125.htm#006">Esther</a>? and it shall be granted thee: and what is thy request? and it shall be performed, even to the half of the kingdom.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Then <a href="../ebd/ebd125.htm#006">Esther</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd303.htm#007">queen</a> answered and said, If I have found favour in thy sight, O <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a>, and if it please the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a>, let my life be given me at my petition, and my people at my request:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> For we are sold, I and my people, to be destroyed, to be slain, and to perish. But if we had been sold for bondmen and bondwomen, I had held my tongue, although the enemy could not countervail the king's damage.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Then the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd013.htm#003">Ahasuerus</a> answered and said unto <a href="../ebd/ebd125.htm#006">Esther</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd303.htm#007">queen</a>, Who is he, and where is he, that durst presume in his heart to do so?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd125.htm#006">Esther</a> said, The <a href="../ebd/ebd011.htm#001">adversary</a> and enemy is this wicked <a href="../ebd/ebd160.htm#004">Haman</a>. Then <a href="../ebd/ebd160.htm#004">Haman</a> was afraid before the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> and the <a href="../ebd/ebd303.htm#007">queen</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> arising from the <a href="../ebd/ebd043.htm#006">banquet</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#000">wine</a> in his wrath went into the <a href="../ebd/ebd282.htm#008">palace</a> garden: and <a href="../ebd/ebd160.htm#004">Haman</a> stood up to make request for his life to <a href="../ebd/ebd125.htm#006">Esther</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd303.htm#007">queen</a>; for he saw that there was evil determined against him by the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> Then the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> returned out of the <a href="../ebd/ebd282.htm#008">palace</a> garden into the place of the <a href="../ebd/ebd043.htm#006">banquet</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#000">wine</a>; and <a href="../ebd/ebd160.htm#004">Haman</a> was fallen upon the bed whereon <a href="../ebd/ebd125.htm#006">Esther</a> was. Then said the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a>, Will he force the <a href="../ebd/ebd303.htm#007">queen</a> also before me in the house? As the word went out of king's mouth, they covered Haman's face.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And Harbonah, one of the chamberlains, said before the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a>, Behold also, the <a href="../ebd/ebd142.htm#005">gallows</a> fifty cubits high, which <a href="../ebd/ebd160.htm#004">Haman</a> had made for <a href="../ebd/ebd259.htm#005">Mordecai</a>, who spoken good for the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a>, standeth in the house of <a href="../ebd/ebd160.htm#004">Haman</a>. Then the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> said, Hang him thereon.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> So they hanged <a href="../ebd/ebd160.htm#004">Haman</a> on the <a href="../ebd/ebd142.htm#005">gallows</a> that he had prepared for <a href="../ebd/ebd259.htm#005">Mordecai</a>. Then was the king's wrath pacified.<br>
 </p>
