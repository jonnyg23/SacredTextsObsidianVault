 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: 4 Kings (2 Kings) Chapter 13</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> In the three and twentieth year of <a href="../ebd/ebd208.htm#000">Joash</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd013.htm#006">Ahaziah</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd200.htm#006">Jehoahaz</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd202.htm#007">Jehu</a> began to reign over <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> in <a href="../ebd/ebd320.htm#004">Samaria</a>, and reigned seventeen years.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> And he did that which was evil in the sight of the LORD, and followed the sins of <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#000">Jeroboam</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd268.htm#003">Nebat</a>, which made <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> to <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a>; he departed not therefrom.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd024.htm#002">anger</a> of the LORD was kindled against <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, and he delivered them into the hand of <a href="../ebd/ebd168.htm#003">Hazael</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd355.htm#002">Syria</a>, and into the hand of Benhadad the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd168.htm#003">Hazael</a>, all their days.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd200.htm#006">Jehoahaz</a> besought the LORD, and the LORD hearkened unto him: for he saw the oppression of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, because the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd355.htm#002">Syria</a> oppressed them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> (And the LORD gave <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> a <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#003">saviour</a>, so that they went out from under the hand of the Syrians: and the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> dwelt in their tents, as beforetime.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Nevertheless they departed not from the sins of the house of <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#000">Jeroboam</a>, who made <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a>, but walked therein: and there remained the <a href="../ebd/ebd155.htm#008">grove</a> also in <a href="../ebd/ebd320.htm#004">Samaria</a>.)<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Neither did he leave of the people to <a href="../ebd/ebd200.htm#006">Jehoahaz</a> but fifty horsemen, and ten chariots, and ten thousand footmen; for the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd355.htm#002">Syria</a> had destroyed them, and had made them like the <a href="../ebd/ebd109.htm#008">dust</a> by <a href="../ebd/ebd364.htm#009">threshing</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> Now the rest of the acts of <a href="../ebd/ebd200.htm#006">Jehoahaz</a>, and all that he did, and his might, are they not written in the book of the <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#003">chronicles</a> of the kings of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd200.htm#006">Jehoahaz</a> slept with his fathers; and they buried him in <a href="../ebd/ebd320.htm#004">Samaria</a>: and <a href="../ebd/ebd208.htm#000">Joash</a> his son reigned in his stead.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> In the thirty and seventh year of <a href="../ebd/ebd208.htm#000">Joash</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a> began <a href="../ebd/ebd200.htm#007">Jehoash</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd200.htm#006">Jehoahaz</a> to reign over <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> in <a href="../ebd/ebd320.htm#004">Samaria</a>, and reigned sixteen years.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And he did that which was evil in the sight of the LORD; he departed not from all the sins of <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#000">Jeroboam</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd268.htm#003">Nebat</a>, who made <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a>: but he walked therein.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And the rest of the acts of <a href="../ebd/ebd208.htm#000">Joash</a>, and all that he did, and his might wherewith he fought against <a href="../ebd/ebd019.htm#007">Amaziah</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>, are they not written in the book of the <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#003">chronicles</a> of the kings of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd208.htm#000">Joash</a> slept with his fathers; and <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#000">Jeroboam</a> sat upon his <a href="../ebd/ebd365.htm#001">throne</a>: and <a href="../ebd/ebd208.htm#000">Joash</a> was buried in <a href="../ebd/ebd320.htm#004">Samaria</a> with the kings of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> Now <a href="../ebd/ebd117.htm#009">Elisha</a> was fallen sick of his sickness whereof he died. And <a href="../ebd/ebd208.htm#000">Joash</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> came down unto him, and wept over his face, and said, O my father, my father, the <a href="../ebd/ebd077.htm#008">chariot</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, and the horsemen thereof.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd117.htm#009">Elisha</a> said unto him, Take <a href="../ebd/ebd063.htm#003">bow</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd032.htm#005">arrows</a>. And he took unto him <a href="../ebd/ebd063.htm#003">bow</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd032.htm#005">arrows</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> And he said to the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, Put thine hand upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd063.htm#003">bow</a>. And he put his hand upon it: and <a href="../ebd/ebd117.htm#009">Elisha</a> put his hands upon the king's hands.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> And he said, Open the window eastward. And he opened it. Then <a href="../ebd/ebd117.htm#009">Elisha</a> said, Shoot. And he shot. And he said, The arrow of the LORD's deliverance, and the arrow of deliverance from <a href="../ebd/ebd355.htm#002">Syria</a>: for thou shalt smite the Syrians in Aphek, till thou have consumed them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> And he said, Take the <a href="../ebd/ebd032.htm#005">arrows</a>. And he took them. And he said unto the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, Smite upon the ground. And he smote thrice, and stayed.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> And the man of God was wroth with him, and said, Thou shouldest have smitten five or six times; then hadst thou smitten <a href="../ebd/ebd355.htm#002">Syria</a> till thou hadst consumed it: whereas now thou shalt smite <a href="../ebd/ebd355.htm#002">Syria</a> but thrice.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd117.htm#009">Elisha</a> died, and they buried him. And the <a href="../ebd/ebd043.htm#003">bands</a> of the Moabites invaded the land at the coming in of the year.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> And it came to pass, as they were burying a man, that, behold, they spied a band of men; and they cast the man into the <a href="../ebd/ebd328.htm#001">sepulchre</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd117.htm#009">Elisha</a>: and when the man was let down, and touched the bones of <a href="../ebd/ebd117.htm#009">Elisha</a>, he revived, and stood up on his feet.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> But <a href="../ebd/ebd168.htm#003">Hazael</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd355.htm#002">Syria</a> oppressed <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> all the days of <a href="../ebd/ebd200.htm#006">Jehoahaz</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> And the LORD was gracious unto them, and had compassion on them, and had respect unto them, because of his <a href="../ebd/ebd091.htm#008">covenant</a> with <a href="../ebd/ebd005.htm#005">Abraham</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd189.htm#006">Isaac</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd194.htm#007">Jacob</a>, and would not destroy them, neither cast he them from his presence as yet.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> So <a href="../ebd/ebd168.htm#003">Hazael</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd355.htm#002">Syria</a> died; and Benhadad his son reigned in his stead.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd200.htm#007">Jehoash</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd200.htm#006">Jehoahaz</a> took again out of the hand of Benhadad the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd168.htm#003">Hazael</a> the cities, which he had taken out of the hand of <a href="../ebd/ebd200.htm#006">Jehoahaz</a> his father by war. Three times did <a href="../ebd/ebd208.htm#000">Joash</a> beat him, and recovered the cities of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>.<br>
 </p>
