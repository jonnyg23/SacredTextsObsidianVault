 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Joshua Chapter 21</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Then came near the heads of the fathers of the Levites unto <a href="../ebd/ebd115.htm#000">Eleazar</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a>, and unto <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#006">Joshua</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd275.htm#006">Nun</a>, and unto the heads of the fathers of the tribes of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> And they spake unto them at <a href="../ebd/ebd337.htm#008">Shiloh</a> in the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#005">Canaan</a>, saying, The LORD commanded by the hand of <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> to give us cities to dwell in, with the <a href="../ebd/ebd352.htm#007">suburbs</a> thereof for our <a href="../ebd/ebd074.htm#002">cattle</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> gave unto the Levites out of their inheritance, at the commandment of the LORD, these cities and their <a href="../ebd/ebd352.htm#007">suburbs</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And the lot came out for the families of the <a href="../ebd/ebd222.htm#002">Kohathites</a>: and the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a>, which were of the Levites, had by lot out of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>, and out of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#002">Simeon</a>, and out of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd052.htm#008">Benjamin</a>, thirteen cities.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And the rest of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd222.htm#001">Kohath</a> had by lot out of the families of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd123.htm#000">Ephraim</a>, and out of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd096.htm#009">Dan</a>, and out of the half <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd240.htm#002">Manasseh</a>, ten cities.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd146.htm#009">Gershon</a> had by lot out of the families of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#002">Issachar</a>, and out of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd033.htm#008">Asher</a>, and out of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd266.htm#006">Naphtali</a>, and out of the half <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd240.htm#002">Manasseh</a> in <a href="../ebd/ebd046.htm#003">Bashan</a>, thirteen cities.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> The children of <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#000">Merari</a> by their families had out of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd311.htm#009">Reuben</a>, and out of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd140.htm#007">Gad</a>, and out of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd389.htm#003">Zebulun</a>, twelve cities.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> gave by lot unto the Levites these cities with their <a href="../ebd/ebd352.htm#007">suburbs</a>, as the LORD commanded by the hand of <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And they gave out of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>, and out of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#002">Simeon</a>, these cities which are here mentioned by name.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> Which the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a>, being of the families of the <a href="../ebd/ebd222.htm#002">Kohathites</a>, who were of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd227.htm#006">Levi</a>, had: for theirs was the first lot.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And they gave them the city of <a href="../ebd/ebd028.htm#007">Arba</a> the father of <a href="../ebd/ebd022.htm#005">Anak</a>, which city is <a href="../ebd/ebd171.htm#004">Hebron</a>, in the hill country of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>, with the <a href="../ebd/ebd352.htm#007">suburbs</a> thereof round about it.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> But the fields of the city, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd377.htm#003">villages</a> thereof, gave they to <a href="../ebd/ebd069.htm#003">Caleb</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd203.htm#004">Jephunneh</a> for his possession.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> Thus they gave to the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd171.htm#004">Hebron</a> with her <a href="../ebd/ebd352.htm#007">suburbs</a>, to be a city of refuge for the slayer; and <a href="../ebd/ebd228.htm#004">Libnah</a> with her <a href="../ebd/ebd352.htm#007">suburbs</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd198.htm#004">Jattir</a> with her <a href="../ebd/ebd352.htm#007">suburbs</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd125.htm#003">Eshtemoa</a> with her <a href="../ebd/ebd352.htm#007">suburbs</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And Holon with her <a href="../ebd/ebd352.htm#007">suburbs</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd099.htm#007">Debir</a> with her <a href="../ebd/ebd352.htm#007">suburbs</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> And Ain with her <a href="../ebd/ebd352.htm#007">suburbs</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd215.htm#001">Juttah</a> with her <a href="../ebd/ebd352.htm#007">suburbs</a>, and Bethshemesh with her <a href="../ebd/ebd352.htm#007">suburbs</a>; nine cities out of those two tribes.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> And out of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd052.htm#008">Benjamin</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd148.htm#002">Gibeon</a> with her <a href="../ebd/ebd352.htm#007">suburbs</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd144.htm#004">Geba</a> with her <a href="../ebd/ebd352.htm#007">suburbs</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd023.htm#004">Anathoth</a> with her <a href="../ebd/ebd352.htm#007">suburbs</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#000">Almon</a> with her <a href="../ebd/ebd352.htm#007">suburbs</a>; four cities.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> All the cities of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a>, the priests, were thirteen cities with their <a href="../ebd/ebd352.htm#007">suburbs</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> And the families of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd222.htm#001">Kohath</a>, the Levites which remained of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd222.htm#001">Kohath</a>, even they had the cities of their lot out of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd123.htm#000">Ephraim</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> For they gave them <a href="../ebd/ebd333.htm#003">Shechem</a> with her <a href="../ebd/ebd352.htm#007">suburbs</a> in <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#007">mount</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd123.htm#000">Ephraim</a>, to be a city of refuge for the slayer; and <a href="../ebd/ebd147.htm#004">Gezer</a> with her <a href="../ebd/ebd352.htm#007">suburbs</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd218.htm#006">Kibzaim</a> with her <a href="../ebd/ebd352.htm#007">suburbs</a>, and Bethhoron with her <a href="../ebd/ebd352.htm#007">suburbs</a>; four cities.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> And out of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd096.htm#009">Dan</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd119.htm#002">Eltekeh</a> with her <a href="../ebd/ebd352.htm#007">suburbs</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd147.htm#007">Gibbethon</a> with her <a href="../ebd/ebd352.htm#007">suburbs</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> Aijalon with her <a href="../ebd/ebd352.htm#007">suburbs</a>, Gathrimmon with her <a href="../ebd/ebd352.htm#007">suburbs</a>; four cities.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> And out of the half <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd240.htm#002">Manasseh</a>, Tanach with her <a href="../ebd/ebd352.htm#007">suburbs</a>, and Gathrimmon with her <a href="../ebd/ebd352.htm#007">suburbs</a>; two cities.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> All the cities were ten with their <a href="../ebd/ebd352.htm#007">suburbs</a> for the families of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd222.htm#001">Kohath</a> that remained.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> And unto the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd146.htm#009">Gershon</a>, of the families of the Levites, out of the other half <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd240.htm#002">Manasseh</a> they gave <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#000">Golan</a> in <a href="../ebd/ebd046.htm#003">Bashan</a> with her <a href="../ebd/ebd352.htm#007">suburbs</a>, to be a city of refuge for the slayer; and Beeshterah with her <a href="../ebd/ebd352.htm#007">suburbs</a>; two cities.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="028">28</a> And out of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#002">Issachar</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd220.htm#009">Kishon</a> with her <a href="../ebd/ebd352.htm#007">suburbs</a>, Dabareh with her <a href="../ebd/ebd352.htm#007">suburbs</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="029">29</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd197.htm#007">Jarmuth</a> with her <a href="../ebd/ebd352.htm#007">suburbs</a>, Engannim with her <a href="../ebd/ebd352.htm#007">suburbs</a>; four cities.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="030">30</a> And out of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd033.htm#008">Asher</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd256.htm#007">Mishal</a> with her <a href="../ebd/ebd352.htm#007">suburbs</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd001.htm#002">Abdon</a> with her <a href="../ebd/ebd352.htm#007">suburbs</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="031">31</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd173.htm#001">Helkath</a> with her <a href="../ebd/ebd352.htm#007">suburbs</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd309.htm#006">Rehob</a> with her <a href="../ebd/ebd352.htm#007">suburbs</a>; four cities.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="032">32</a> And out of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd266.htm#006">Naphtali</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd216.htm#006">Kedesh</a> in <a href="../ebd/ebd141.htm#008">Galilee</a> with her <a href="../ebd/ebd352.htm#007">suburbs</a>, to be a city of refuge for the slayer; and Hammothdor with her <a href="../ebd/ebd352.htm#007">suburbs</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd216.htm#001">Kartan</a> with her <a href="../ebd/ebd352.htm#007">suburbs</a>; three cities.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="033">33</a> All the cities of the Gershonites according to their families were thirteen cities with their <a href="../ebd/ebd352.htm#007">suburbs</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="034">34</a> And unto the families of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#000">Merari</a>, the rest of the Levites, out of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd389.htm#003">Zebulun</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd210.htm#002">Jokneam</a> with her <a href="../ebd/ebd352.htm#007">suburbs</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd216.htm#000">Kartah</a> with her <a href="../ebd/ebd352.htm#007">suburbs</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="035">35</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd103.htm#007">Dimnah</a> with her <a href="../ebd/ebd352.htm#007">suburbs</a>, Nahalal with her <a href="../ebd/ebd352.htm#007">suburbs</a>; four cities.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="036">36</a> And out of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd311.htm#009">Reuben</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd058.htm#001">Bezer</a> with her <a href="../ebd/ebd352.htm#007">suburbs</a>, and Jahazah with her <a href="../ebd/ebd352.htm#007">suburbs</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="037">37</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd216.htm#005">Kedemoth</a> with her <a href="../ebd/ebd352.htm#007">suburbs</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd248.htm#005">Mephaath</a> with her <a href="../ebd/ebd352.htm#007">suburbs</a>; four cities.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="038">38</a> And out of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd140.htm#007">Gad</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd307.htm#002">Ramoth</a> in <a href="../ebd/ebd148.htm#009">Gilead</a> with her <a href="../ebd/ebd352.htm#007">suburbs</a>, to be a city of refuge for the slayer; and <a href="../ebd/ebd237.htm#005">Mahanaim</a> with her <a href="../ebd/ebd352.htm#007">suburbs</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="039">39</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd176.htm#009">Heshbon</a> with her <a href="../ebd/ebd352.htm#007">suburbs</a>, Jazer with her <a href="../ebd/ebd352.htm#007">suburbs</a>; four cities in all.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="040">40</a> So all the cities for the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#000">Merari</a> by their families, which were remaining of the families of the Levites, were by their lot twelve cities.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="041">41</a> All the cities of the Levites within the possession of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> were forty and eight cities with their <a href="../ebd/ebd352.htm#007">suburbs</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="042">42</a> These cities were every one with their <a href="../ebd/ebd352.htm#007">suburbs</a> round about them: thus were all these cities.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="043">43</a> And the LORD gave unto <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> all the land which he sware to give unto their fathers; and they possessed it, and dwelt therein.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="044">44</a> And the LORD gave them rest round about, according to all that he sware unto their fathers: and there stood not a man of all their enemies before them; the LORD delivered all their enemies into their hand.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="045">45</a> There failed not ought of any good thing which the LORD had spoken unto the house of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>; all came to pass.<br>
 </p>
