 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Leviticus Chapter 9</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> And it came to pass on the eighth day, that <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> called <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> and his sons, and the elders of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> And he said unto <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a>, Take thee a young <a href="../ebd/ebd069.htm#004">calf</a> for a <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, and a <a href="../ebd/ebd306.htm#003">ram</a> for a burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, without <a href="../ebd/ebd060.htm#007">blemish</a>, and offer them before the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And unto the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> thou shalt speak, saying, Take ye a kid of the goats for a <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>; and a <a href="../ebd/ebd069.htm#004">calf</a> and a <a href="../ebd/ebd223.htm#003">lamb</a>, both of the first year, without <a href="../ebd/ebd060.htm#007">blemish</a>, for a burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Also a <a href="../ebd/ebd066.htm#003">bullock</a> and a <a href="../ebd/ebd306.htm#003">ram</a> for peace offerings, to <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#002">sacrifice</a> before the LORD; and a meat <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> mingled with oil: for to day the LORD will appear unto you.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And they brought that which <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> commanded before the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>: and all the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> drew near and stood before the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> said, This is the thing which the LORD commanded that ye should do: and the <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a> of the LORD shall appear unto you.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> said unto <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a>, Go unto the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>, and offer thy <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, and thy burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, and make an <a href="../ebd/ebd036.htm#004">atonement</a> for thyself, and for the people: and offer the <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> of the people, and make an <a href="../ebd/ebd036.htm#004">atonement</a> for them; as the LORD commanded.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> therefore went unto the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>, and slew the <a href="../ebd/ebd069.htm#004">calf</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, which was for himself.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> brought the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> unto him: and he dipped his finger in the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a>, and put it upon the horns of the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>, and poured out the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> at the bottom of the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> But the <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#005">fat</a>, and the kidneys, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd074.htm#003">caul</a> above the <a href="../ebd/ebd230.htm#004">liver</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, he burnt upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>; as the LORD commanded <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd135.htm#007">flesh</a> and the hide he burnt with fire without the <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#002">camp</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And he slew the burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>; and Aaron's sons presented unto him the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a>, which he sprinkled round about upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> And they presented the burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> unto him, with the <a href="../ebd/ebd295.htm#002">pieces</a> thereof, and the head: and he burnt them upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> And he did wash the inwards and the legs, and burnt them upon the burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> on the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And he brought the people's <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, and took the <a href="../ebd/ebd151.htm#001">goat</a>, which was the <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> for the people, and slew it, and offered it for <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a>, as the first.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> And he brought the burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, and offered it according to the manner.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> And he brought the meat <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, and took an handful thereof, and burnt it upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>, beside the burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#002">sacrifice</a> of the morning.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> He slew also the <a href="../ebd/ebd066.htm#003">bullock</a> and the <a href="../ebd/ebd306.htm#003">ram</a> for a <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#002">sacrifice</a> of peace offerings, which was for the people: and Aaron's sons presented unto him the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a>, which he sprinkled upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> round about,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#005">fat</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd066.htm#003">bullock</a> and of the <a href="../ebd/ebd306.htm#003">ram</a>, the rump, and that which covereth the inwards, and the kidneys, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd074.htm#003">caul</a> above the <a href="../ebd/ebd230.htm#004">liver</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> And they put the <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#005">fat</a> upon the breasts, and he burnt the <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#005">fat</a> upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> And the breasts and the right shoulder <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> waved for a wave <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> before the LORD; as <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> commanded.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> lifted up his hand toward the people, and blessed them, and came down from <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, and the burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, and peace offerings.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> went into the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>, and came out, and blessed the people: and the <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a> of the LORD appeared unto all the people.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> And there came a fire out from before the LORD, and consumed upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> the burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> and the <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#005">fat</a>: which when all the people saw, they shouted, and fell on their faces.<br>
 </p>
