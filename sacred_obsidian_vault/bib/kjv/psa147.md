 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 147</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Praise ye the LORD: for it is good to sing praises unto our God; for it is pleasant; and praise is comely.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> The LORD doth build up <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>: he gathereth together the outcasts of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> He healeth the broken in heart, and bindeth up their wounds.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> He telleth the number of the <a href="../ebd/ebd350.htm#008">stars</a>; he calleth them all by their names.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Great is our Lord, and of great power: his understanding is infinite.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> The LORD lifteth up the meek: he casteth the wicked down to the ground.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Sing unto the LORD with thanksgiving; sing praise upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd165.htm#001">harp</a> unto our God:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> Who covereth the <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#006">heaven</a> with clouds, who prepareth <a href="../ebd/ebd305.htm#008">rain</a> for the earth, who maketh <a href="../ebd/ebd154.htm#005">grass</a> to grow upon the mountains.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> He giveth to the <a href="../ebd/ebd049.htm#001">beast</a> his food, and to the young ravens which cry.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> He delighteth not in the strength of the horse: he taketh not pleasure in the legs of a man.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> The LORD taketh pleasure in them that fear him, in those that hope in his <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> Praise the LORD, O <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>; praise thy God, O <a href="../ebd/ebd394.htm#002">Zion</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> For he hath strengthened the bars of thy gates; he hath blessed thy children within thee.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> He maketh peace in thy borders, and filleth thee with the finest of the <a href="../ebd/ebd381.htm#000">wheat</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> He sendeth forth his commandment upon earth: his word runneth very swiftly.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> He giveth <a href="../ebd/ebd347.htm#000">snow</a> like <a href="../ebd/ebd383.htm#005">wool</a>: he scattereth the hoarfrost like <a href="../ebd/ebd034.htm#000">ashes</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> He casteth forth his <a href="../ebd/ebd186.htm#001">ice</a> like morsels: who can stand before his cold?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> He sendeth out his word, and melteth them: he causeth his wind to blow, and the waters flow.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> He sheweth his word unto <a href="../ebd/ebd194.htm#007">Jacob</a>, his statutes and his judgments unto <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> He hath not dealt so with any nation: and as for his judgments, they have not known them. Praise ye the LORD.<br>
 </p>
