 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 12</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Help, LORD; for the godly man ceaseth; for the <a href="../ebd/ebd130.htm#005">faithful</a> fail from among the children of men.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> They speak vanity every one with his neighbour: with flattering lips and with a double heart do they speak.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> The LORD shall cut off all flattering lips, and the tongue that speaketh proud things:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Who have said, With our tongue will we prevail; our lips are our own: who is lord over us?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> For the oppression of the <a href="../ebd/ebd298.htm#006">poor</a>, for the sighing of the needy, now will I arise, saith the LORD; I will set him in safety from him that puffeth at him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> The words of the LORD are pure words: as <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#000">silver</a> tried in a <a href="../ebd/ebd140.htm#000">furnace</a> of earth, purified <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> times.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Thou shalt keep them, O LORD, thou shalt preserve them from this <a href="../ebd/ebd145.htm#008">generation</a> for ever.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> The wicked walk on every side, when the vilest men are exalted.<br>
 </p>
