 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 132</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Lord, remember <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a>, and all his <a href="../ebd/ebd011.htm#006">afflictions</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> How he sware unto the LORD, and vowed unto the mighty God of <a href="../ebd/ebd194.htm#007">Jacob</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Surely I will not come into the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of my house, nor go up into my bed;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> I will not give sleep to mine eyes, or slumber to mine eyelids,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Until I find out a place for the LORD, an <a href="../ebd/ebd156.htm#007">habitation</a> for the mighty God of <a href="../ebd/ebd194.htm#007">Jacob</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Lo, we heard of it at <a href="../ebd/ebd123.htm#006">Ephratah</a>: we found it in the fields of the <a href="../ebd/ebd383.htm#003">wood</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> We will go into his tabernacles: we will <a href="../ebd/ebd384.htm#002">worship</a> at his <a href="../ebd/ebd136.htm#009">footstool</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> Arise, O LORD, into thy rest; thou, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#000">ark</a> of thy strength.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> Let thy priests be clothed with <a href="../ebd/ebd313.htm#004">righteousness</a>; and let thy saints shout for joy.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> For thy servant David's sake turn not away the face of thine anointed.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> The LORD hath sworn in truth unto <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a>; he will not turn from it; Of the <a href="../ebd/ebd139.htm#000">fruit</a> of thy body will I set upon thy <a href="../ebd/ebd365.htm#001">throne</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> If thy children will keep my <a href="../ebd/ebd091.htm#008">covenant</a> and my <a href="../ebd/ebd362.htm#008">testimony</a> that I shall teach them, their children shall also sit upon thy <a href="../ebd/ebd365.htm#001">throne</a> for evermore.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> For the LORD hath <a href="../ebd/ebd081.htm#008">chosen</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd394.htm#002">Zion</a>; he hath desired it for his <a href="../ebd/ebd156.htm#007">habitation</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> This is my rest for ever: here will I dwell; for I have desired it.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> I will abundantly <a href="../ebd/ebd060.htm#008">bless</a> her provision: I will satisfy her <a href="../ebd/ebd298.htm#006">poor</a> with <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#006">bread</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> I will also clothe her priests with <a href="../ebd/ebd320.htm#003">salvation</a>: and her saints shall shout aloud for joy.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> There will I make the <a href="../ebd/ebd182.htm#003">horn</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> to bud: I have ordained a <a href="../ebd/ebd223.htm#007">lamp</a> for mine anointed.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> His enemies will I clothe with shame: but upon himself shall his <a href="../ebd/ebd093.htm#001">crown</a> flourish.<br>
 </p>
