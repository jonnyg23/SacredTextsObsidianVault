 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Jeremiah Chapter 45</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> The word that <a href="../ebd/ebd203.htm#006">Jeremiah</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#008">prophet</a> spake unto <a href="../ebd/ebd046.htm#001">Baruch</a> the son of Neriah, when he had written these words in a book at the mouth of <a href="../ebd/ebd203.htm#006">Jeremiah</a>, in the fourth year of <a href="../ebd/ebd201.htm#001">Jehoiakim</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#008">Josiah</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Thus saith the LORD, the God of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, unto thee, O <a href="../ebd/ebd046.htm#001">Baruch</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Thou didst say, Woe is me now! for the LORD hath added grief to my sorrow; I fainted in my sighing, and I find no rest.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Thus shalt thou say unto him, The LORD saith thus; Behold, that which I have built will I break down, and that which I have planted I will pluck up, even this whole land.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And seekest thou great things for thyself? seek them not: for, behold, I will bring evil upon all <a href="../ebd/ebd135.htm#007">flesh</a>, saith the LORD: but thy life will I give unto thee for a prey in all places whither thou goest.<br>
 </p>
