 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Ezra Chapter 2</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Now these are the children of the province that went up out of the <a href="../ebd/ebd072.htm#002">captivity</a>, of those which had been carried away, whom <a href="../ebd/ebd268.htm#005">Nebuchadnezzar</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd041.htm#001">Babylon</a> had carried away unto <a href="../ebd/ebd041.htm#001">Babylon</a>, and came again unto <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>, every one unto his city;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Which came with <a href="../ebd/ebd391.htm#008">Zerubbabel</a>: <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#003">Jeshua</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd269.htm#007">Nehemiah</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd328.htm#003">Seraiah</a>, Reelaiah, <a href="../ebd/ebd259.htm#005">Mordecai</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd058.htm#009">Bilshan</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd258.htm#001">Mizpar</a>, Bigvai, <a href="../ebd/ebd309.htm#009">Rehum</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd040.htm#007">Baanah</a>. The number of the men of the people of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> The children of Parosh, two thousand an hundred seventy and two.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> The children of <a href="../ebd/ebd335.htm#005">Shephatiah</a>, three hundred seventy and two.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> The children of Arah, <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> hundred seventy and five.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> The children of Pahathmoab, of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#003">Jeshua</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd207.htm#006">Joab</a>, two thousand eight hundred and twelve.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> The children of <a href="../ebd/ebd114.htm#003">Elam</a>, a thousand two hundred fifty and four.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> The children of <a href="../ebd/ebd388.htm#001">Zattu</a>, nine hundred forty and five.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> The children of <a href="../ebd/ebd386.htm#002">Zaccai</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> hundred and threescore.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> The children of <a href="../ebd/ebd043.htm#004">Bani</a>, six hundred forty and two.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> The children of Bebai, six hundred twenty and three.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> The children of Azgad, a thousand two hundred twenty and two.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> The children of <a href="../ebd/ebd009.htm#007">Adonikam</a>, six hundred sixty and six.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> The children of Bigvai, two thousand fifty and six.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> The children of <a href="../ebd/ebd008.htm#009">Adin</a>, four hundred fifty and four.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> The children of <a href="../ebd/ebd036.htm#001">Ater</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd177.htm#003">Hezekiah</a>, ninety and eight.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> The children of Bezai, three hundred twenty and three.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> The children of Jorah, an hundred and twelve.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> The children of <a href="../ebd/ebd166.htm#007">Hashum</a>, two hundred twenty and three.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> The children of Gibbar, ninety and five.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> The children of <a href="../ebd/ebd056.htm#005">Bethlehem</a>, an hundred twenty and three.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> The men of <a href="../ebd/ebd271.htm#006">Netophah</a>, fifty and six.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> The men of <a href="../ebd/ebd023.htm#004">Anathoth</a>, an hundred twenty and eight.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> The children of <a href="../ebd/ebd038.htm#000">Azmaveth</a>, forty and two.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> The children of Kirjatharim, <a href="../ebd/ebd079.htm#000">Chephirah</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd050.htm#006">Beeroth</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> hundred and forty and three.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> The children of <a href="../ebd/ebd306.htm#005">Ramah</a> and Gaba, six hundred twenty and one.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> The men of Michmas, an hundred twenty and two.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="028">28</a> The men of <a href="../ebd/ebd055.htm#006">Bethel</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd015.htm#009">Ai</a>, two hundred twenty and three.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="029">29</a> The children of <a href="../ebd/ebd268.htm#004">Nebo</a>, fifty and two.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="030">30</a> The children of Magbish, an hundred fifty and six.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="031">31</a> The children of the other <a href="../ebd/ebd114.htm#003">Elam</a>, a thousand two hundred fifty and four.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="032">32</a> The children of <a href="../ebd/ebd164.htm#003">Harim</a>, three hundred and twenty.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="033">33</a> The children of Lod, <a href="../ebd/ebd157.htm#009">Hadid</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd279.htm#002">Ono</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> hundred twenty and five.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="034">34</a> The children of <a href="../ebd/ebd203.htm#008">Jericho</a>, three hundred forty and five.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="035">35</a> The children of <a href="../ebd/ebd327.htm#001">Senaah</a>, three thousand and six hundred and thirty.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="036">36</a> The priests: the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd199.htm#006">Jedaiah</a>, of the house of <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#003">Jeshua</a>, nine hundred seventy and three.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="037">37</a> The children of <a href="../ebd/ebd187.htm#008">Immer</a>, a thousand fifty and two.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="038">38</a> The children of <a href="../ebd/ebd286.htm#002">Pashur</a>, a thousand two hundred forty and <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="039">39</a> The children of <a href="../ebd/ebd164.htm#003">Harim</a>, a thousand and seventeen.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="040">40</a> The Levites: the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#003">Jeshua</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd215.htm#004">Kadmiel</a>, of the children of Hodaviah, seventy and four.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="041">41</a> The singers: the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd033.htm#002">Asaph</a>, an hundred twenty and eight.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="042">42</a> The children of the porters: the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd330.htm#008">Shallum</a>, the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd036.htm#001">Ater</a>, the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd358.htm#002">Talmon</a>, the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd016.htm#003">Akkub</a>, the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd167.htm#005">Hatita</a>, the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd340.htm#003">Shobai</a>, in all an hundred thirty and nine.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="043">43</a> The Nethinims: the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd393.htm#002">Ziha</a>, the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd166.htm#009">Hasupha</a>, the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd355.htm#007">Tabbaoth</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="044">44</a> The children of Keros, the children of Siaha, the children of Padon,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="045">45</a> The children of Lebanah, the children of Hagabah, the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd016.htm#003">Akkub</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="046">46</a> The children of Hagab, the children of Shalmai, the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd162.htm#001">Hanan</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="047">47</a> The children of Giddel, the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd141.htm#002">Gahar</a>, the children of Reaiah,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="048">48</a> The children of <a href="../ebd/ebd312.htm#006">Rezin</a>, the children of Nekoda, the children of Gazzam,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="049">49</a> The children of <a href="../ebd/ebd376.htm#000">Uzza</a>, the children of Paseah, the children of Besai,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="050">50</a> The children of Asnah, the children of Mehunim, the children of Nephusim,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="051">51</a> The children of Bakbuk, the children of Hakupha, the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd164.htm#002">Harhur</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="052">52</a> The children of Bazluth, the children of Mehida, the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd165.htm#003">Harsha</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="053">53</a> The children of <a href="../ebd/ebd045.htm#002">Barkos</a>, the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd345.htm#007">Sisera</a>, the children of Thamah,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="054">54</a> The children of <a href="../ebd/ebd272.htm#000">Neziah</a>, the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd167.htm#004">Hatipha</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="055">55</a> The children of Solomon's servants: the children of Sotai, the children of Sophereth, the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd291.htm#001">Peruda</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="056">56</a> The children of Jaalah, the children of Darkon, the children of Giddel,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="057">57</a> The children of <a href="../ebd/ebd335.htm#005">Shephatiah</a>, the children of Hattil, the children of Pochereth of <a href="../ebd/ebd388.htm#007">Zebaim</a>, the children of Ami.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="058">58</a> All the Nethinims, and the children of Solomon's servants, were three hundred ninety and two.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="059">59</a> And these were they which went up from Telmelah, Telharsa, <a href="../ebd/ebd079.htm#003">Cherub</a>, Addan, and <a href="../ebd/ebd187.htm#008">Immer</a>: but they could not shew their father's house, and their seed, whether they were of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="060">60</a> The children of <a href="../ebd/ebd101.htm#001">Delaiah</a>, the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd369.htm#001">Tobiah</a>, the children of Nekoda, six hundred fifty and two.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="061">61</a> And of the children of the priests: the children of Habaiah, the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd222.htm#007">Koz</a>, the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd046.htm#002">Barzillai</a>; which took a wife of the daughters of <a href="../ebd/ebd046.htm#002">Barzillai</a> the Gileadite, and was called after their name:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="062">62</a> These sought their register among those that were reckoned by genealogy, but they were not found: therefore were they, as polluted, put from the priesthood.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="063">63</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd368.htm#001">Tirshatha</a> said unto them, that they should not eat of the most holy things, till there stood up a <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> with <a href="../ebd/ebd375.htm#005">Urim</a> and with <a href="../ebd/ebd365.htm#002">Thummim</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="064">64</a> The whole <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> together was forty and two thousand three hundred and threescore,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="065">65</a> Beside their servants and their maids, of whom there were <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> thousand three hundred thirty and <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a>: and there were among them two hundred singing men and singing women.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="066">66</a> Their horses were <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> hundred thirty and six; their mules, two hundred forty and five;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="067">67</a> Their camels, four hundred thirty and five; their asses, six thousand <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> hundred and twenty.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="068">68</a> And some of the chief of the fathers, when they came to the house of the LORD which is at <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>, offered freely for the house of God to set it up in his place:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="069">69</a> They gave after their ability unto the treasure of the work threescore and one thousand drams of <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a>, and five thousand <a href="../ebd/ebd299.htm#008">pound</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#000">silver</a>, and one hundred priests' garments.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="070">70</a> So the priests, and the Levites, and some of the people, and the singers, and the porters, and the Nethinims, dwelt in their cities, and all <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> in their cities.<br>
 </p>
