 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 25</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Unto thee, O LORD, do I lift up my soul.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> O my God, I trust in thee: let me not be ashamed, let not mine enemies triumph over me.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Yea, let none that wait on thee be ashamed: let them be ashamed which transgress without cause.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Shew me thy ways, O LORD; teach me thy paths.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Lead me in thy truth, and teach me: for thou art the God of my <a href="../ebd/ebd320.htm#003">salvation</a>; on thee do I wait all the day.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Remember, O LORD, thy tender mercies and thy lovingkindnesses; for they have been ever of old.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Remember not the sins of my youth, nor my transgressions: according to thy <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> remember thou me for thy goodness' sake, O LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> Good and upright is the LORD: therefore will he teach sinners in the way.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> The meek will he guide in judgment: and the meek will he teach his way.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> All the paths of the LORD are <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> and truth unto such as keep his <a href="../ebd/ebd091.htm#008">covenant</a> and his testimonies.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> For thy name's sake, O LORD, <a href="../ebd/ebd285.htm#001">pardon</a> mine iniquity; for it is great.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> What man is he that feareth the LORD? him shall he teach in the way that he shall choose.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> His soul shall dwell at ease; and his seed shall inherit the earth.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> The secret of the LORD is with them that fear him; and he will shew them his <a href="../ebd/ebd091.htm#008">covenant</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> Mine eyes are ever toward the LORD; for he shall pluck my feet out of the <a href="../ebd/ebd271.htm#002">net</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> Turn thee unto me, and have <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> upon me; for I am desolate and afflicted.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> The troubles of my heart are enlarged: O bring thou me out of my distresses.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> Look upon mine affliction and my pain; and forgive all my sins.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> Consider mine enemies; for they are many; and they hate me with cruel <a href="../ebd/ebd167.htm#002">hatred</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> O keep my soul, and deliver me: let me not be ashamed; for I put my trust in thee.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> Let integrity and uprightness preserve me; for I wait on thee.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> Redeem <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, O God, out of all his troubles.<br>
 </p>
