 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Hosea Chapter 6</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Come, and let us return unto the LORD: for he hath torn, and he will heal us; he hath smitten, and he will bind us up.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> After two days will he revive us: in the third day he will raise us up, and we shall live in his sight.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Then shall we know, if we follow on to know the LORD: his going forth is prepared as the morning; and he shall come unto us as the <a href="../ebd/ebd305.htm#008">rain</a>, as the latter and former <a href="../ebd/ebd305.htm#008">rain</a> unto the earth.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> O <a href="../ebd/ebd123.htm#000">Ephraim</a>, what shall I do unto thee? O <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>, what shall I do unto thee? for your <a href="../ebd/ebd153.htm#000">goodness</a> is as a morning <a href="../ebd/ebd085.htm#001">cloud</a>, and as the early <a href="../ebd/ebd102.htm#008">dew</a> it goeth away.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Therefore have I hewed them by the prophets; I have slain them by the words of my mouth: and thy judgments are as the light that goeth forth.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> For I desired <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a>, and not <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#002">sacrifice</a>; and the knowledge of God more than burnt offerings.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> But they like men have transgressed the <a href="../ebd/ebd091.htm#008">covenant</a>: there have they dealt treacherously against me.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd148.htm#009">Gilead</a> is a city of them that work iniquity, and is polluted with <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And as troops of robbers wait for a man, so the company of priests <a href="../ebd/ebd262.htm#002">murder</a> in the way by consent: for they commit <a href="../ebd/ebd228.htm#002">lewdness</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> I have seen an horrible thing in the house of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>: there is the whoredom of <a href="../ebd/ebd123.htm#000">Ephraim</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> is defiled.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> Also, O <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>, he hath set an <a href="../ebd/ebd165.htm#008">harvest</a> for thee, when I returned the <a href="../ebd/ebd072.htm#002">captivity</a> of my people.<br>
 </p>
