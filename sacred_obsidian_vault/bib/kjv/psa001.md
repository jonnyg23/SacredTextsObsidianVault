 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 1</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Blessed is the man that walketh not in the counsel of the ungodly, nor standeth in the way of sinners, nor sitteth in the seat of the scornful.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> But his delight is in the law of the LORD; and in his law doth he meditate day and night.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And he shall be like a tree planted by the rivers of water, that bringeth forth his <a href="../ebd/ebd139.htm#000">fruit</a> in his season; his <a href="../ebd/ebd225.htm#004">leaf</a> also shall not wither; and whatsoever he doeth shall prosper.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> The ungodly are not so: but are like the <a href="../ebd/ebd075.htm#007">chaff</a> which the wind driveth away.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Therefore the ungodly shall not stand in the judgment, nor sinners in the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> of the righteous.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> For the LORD knoweth the way of the righteous: but the way of the ungodly shall perish.<br>
 </p>
