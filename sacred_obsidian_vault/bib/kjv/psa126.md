 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 126</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> When the LORD turned again the <a href="../ebd/ebd072.htm#002">captivity</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd394.htm#002">Zion</a>, we were like them that <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#005">dream</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Then was our mouth filled with laughter, and our tongue with singing: then said they among the <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#005">heathen</a>, The LORD hath done great things for them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> The LORD hath done great things for us; whereof we are glad.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Turn again our <a href="../ebd/ebd072.htm#002">captivity</a>, O LORD, as the streams in the south.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> They that sow in tears shall reap in joy.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> He that goeth forth and weepeth, bearing precious seed, shall doubtless come again with rejoicing, bringing his sheaves with him.<br>
 </p>
