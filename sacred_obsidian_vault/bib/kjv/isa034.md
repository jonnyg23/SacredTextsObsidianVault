 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Isaiah Chapter 34</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Come near, ye nations, to hear; and hearken, ye people: let the earth hear, and all that is therein; the world, and all things that come forth of it.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> For the indignation of the LORD is upon all nations, and his <a href="../ebd/ebd140.htm#002">fury</a> upon all their armies: he hath utterly destroyed them, he hath delivered them to the slaughter.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Their slain also shall be cast out, and their stink shall come up out of their carcases, and the mountains shall be melted with their <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And all the <a href="../ebd/ebd183.htm#007">host</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#006">heaven</a> shall be dissolved, and the heavens shall be rolled together as a scroll: and all their <a href="../ebd/ebd183.htm#007">host</a> shall fall down, as the <a href="../ebd/ebd225.htm#004">leaf</a> falleth off from the <a href="../ebd/ebd377.htm#004">vine</a>, and as a falling <a href="../ebd/ebd133.htm#001">fig</a> from the <a href="../ebd/ebd133.htm#001">fig</a> tree.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> For my <a href="../ebd/ebd354.htm#003">sword</a> shall be bathed in <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#006">heaven</a>: behold, it shall come down upon Idumea, and upon the people of my <a href="../ebd/ebd094.htm#003">curse</a>, to judgment.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> The <a href="../ebd/ebd354.htm#003">sword</a> of the LORD is filled with <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a>, it is made <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#005">fat</a> with fatness, and with the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> of lambs and goats, with the <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#005">fat</a> of the kidneys of rams: for the LORD hath a <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#002">sacrifice</a> in <a href="../ebd/ebd063.htm#009">Bozrah</a>, and a great slaughter in the land of Idumea.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And the unicorns shall come down with them, and the bullocks with the bulls; and their land shall be soaked with <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a>, and their <a href="../ebd/ebd109.htm#008">dust</a> made <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#005">fat</a> with fatness.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> For it is the day of the LORD's vengeance, and the year of recompences for the controversy of <a href="../ebd/ebd394.htm#002">Zion</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And the streams thereof shall be turned into <a href="../ebd/ebd296.htm#008">pitch</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd109.htm#008">dust</a> thereof into <a href="../ebd/ebd065.htm#005">brimstone</a>, and the land thereof shall become burning <a href="../ebd/ebd296.htm#008">pitch</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> It shall not be quenched night nor day; the smoke thereof shall go up for ever: from <a href="../ebd/ebd145.htm#008">generation</a> to <a href="../ebd/ebd145.htm#008">generation</a> it shall lie waste; none shall pass through it for ever and ever.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> But the <a href="../ebd/ebd090.htm#005">cormorant</a> and the <a href="../ebd/ebd060.htm#000">bittern</a> shall possess it; the <a href="../ebd/ebd281.htm#006">owl</a> also and the <a href="../ebd/ebd307.htm#008">raven</a> shall dwell in it: and he shall stretch out upon it the line of confusion, and the stones of emptiness.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> They shall call the nobles thereof to the kingdom, but none shall be there, and all her princes shall be nothing.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> And thorns shall come up in her palaces, nettles and brambles in the fortresses thereof: and it shall be an <a href="../ebd/ebd156.htm#007">habitation</a> of dragons, and a court for owls.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> The wild beasts of the <a href="../ebd/ebd102.htm#000">desert</a> shall also meet with the wild beasts of the <a href="../ebd/ebd190.htm#009">island</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#001">satyr</a> shall cry to his fellow; the screech <a href="../ebd/ebd281.htm#006">owl</a> also shall rest there, and find for herself a place of rest.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> There shall the great <a href="../ebd/ebd281.htm#006">owl</a> make her nest, and lay, and hatch, and gather under her <a href="../ebd/ebd330.htm#002">shadow</a>: there shall the vultures also be gathered, every one with her mate.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> Seek ye out of the book of the LORD, and read: no one of these shall fail, none shall want her mate: for my mouth it hath commanded, and his <a href="../ebd/ebd349.htm#009">spirit</a> it hath gathered them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> And he hath cast the lot for them, and his hand hath divided it unto them by line: they shall possess it for ever, from <a href="../ebd/ebd145.htm#008">generation</a> to <a href="../ebd/ebd145.htm#008">generation</a> shall they dwell therein.<br>
 </p>
