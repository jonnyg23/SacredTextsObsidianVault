 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: 1 Chronicles Chapter 11</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Then all <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> gathered themselves to <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> unto <a href="../ebd/ebd171.htm#004">Hebron</a>, saying, Behold, we are thy bone and thy <a href="../ebd/ebd135.htm#007">flesh</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> And moreover in time past, even when <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#002">Saul</a> was <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a>, thou wast he that leddest out and broughtest in <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>: and the LORD thy God said unto thee, Thou shalt feed my people <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, and thou shalt be ruler over my people <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Therefore came all the elders of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> to the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> to <a href="../ebd/ebd171.htm#004">Hebron</a>; and <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> made a <a href="../ebd/ebd091.htm#008">covenant</a> with them in <a href="../ebd/ebd171.htm#004">Hebron</a> before the LORD; and they anointed <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> over <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, according to the word of the LORD by <a href="../ebd/ebd321.htm#001">Samuel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> and all <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> went to <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>, which is <a href="../ebd/ebd199.htm#003">Jebus</a>; where the <a href="../ebd/ebd199.htm#004">Jebusites</a> were, the inhabitants of the land.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And the inhabitants of <a href="../ebd/ebd199.htm#003">Jebus</a> said to <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a>, Thou shalt not come hither. Nevertheless <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> took the <a href="../ebd/ebd073.htm#008">castle</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd394.htm#002">Zion</a>, which is the city of <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> said, Whosoever smiteth the <a href="../ebd/ebd199.htm#004">Jebusites</a> first shall be chief and captain. So <a href="../ebd/ebd207.htm#006">Joab</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd391.htm#009">Zeruiah</a> went first up, and was chief.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> dwelt in the <a href="../ebd/ebd073.htm#008">castle</a>; therefore they called it the city of <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And he built the city round about, even from <a href="../ebd/ebd255.htm#004">Millo</a> round about: and <a href="../ebd/ebd207.htm#006">Joab</a> repaired the rest of the city.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> So <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> waxed greater and greater: for the LORD of hosts was with him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> These also are the chief of the mighty men whom <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> had, who strengthened themselves with him in his kingdom, and with all <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, to make him <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a>, according to the word of the LORD concerning <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And this is the number of the mighty men whom <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> had; <a href="../ebd/ebd198.htm#000">Jashobeam</a>, an Hachmonite, the chief of the captains: he lifted up his spear against three hundred slain by him at one time.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And after him was <a href="../ebd/ebd115.htm#000">Eleazar</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd105.htm#005">Dodo</a>, the <a href="../ebd/ebd015.htm#004">Ahohite</a>, who was one of the three mighties.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> He was with <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> at Pasdammim, and there the <a href="../ebd/ebd294.htm#002">Philistines</a> were gathered together to battle, where was a parcel of ground full of <a href="../ebd/ebd045.htm#003">barley</a>; and the people fled from before the <a href="../ebd/ebd294.htm#002">Philistines</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> And they set themselves in the midst of that parcel, and delivered it, and slew the <a href="../ebd/ebd294.htm#002">Philistines</a>; and the LORD saved them by a great deliverance.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> Now three of the thirty captains went down to the <a href="../ebd/ebd315.htm#001">rock</a> to <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a>, into the <a href="../ebd/ebd074.htm#006">cave</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd010.htm#007">Adullam</a>; and the <a href="../ebd/ebd183.htm#007">host</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd294.htm#002">Philistines</a> encamped in the <a href="../ebd/ebd376.htm#008">valley</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd311.htm#000">Rephaim</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> was then in the hold, and the Philistines' <a href="../ebd/ebd143.htm#007">garrison</a> was then at <a href="../ebd/ebd056.htm#005">Bethlehem</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> longed, and said, Oh that one would give me <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a> of the water of the well of <a href="../ebd/ebd056.htm#005">Bethlehem</a>, that is at the <a href="../ebd/ebd143.htm#008">gate</a>!<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> And the three brake through the <a href="../ebd/ebd183.htm#007">host</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd294.htm#002">Philistines</a>, and drew water out of the well of <a href="../ebd/ebd056.htm#005">Bethlehem</a>, that was by the <a href="../ebd/ebd143.htm#008">gate</a>, and took it, and brought it to <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a>: but <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> would not <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a> of it, but poured it out to the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> And said, My God forbid it me, that I should do this thing: shall I <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> of these men that have put their lives in jeopardy? for with the jeopardy of their lives they brought it. Therefore he would not <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a> it. These things did these three mightiest.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd004.htm#005">Abishai</a> the brother of <a href="../ebd/ebd207.htm#006">Joab</a>, he was chief of the three: for lifting up his spear against three hundred, he slew them, and had a name among the three.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> Of the three, he was more honourable than the two; for he was their captain: howbeit he attained not to the first three.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd052.htm#003">Benaiah</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd200.htm#009">Jehoiada</a>, the son of a valiant man of <a href="../ebd/ebd215.htm#002">Kabzeel</a>, who had done many acts; he slew two lionlike men of <a href="../ebd/ebd258.htm#005">Moab</a>: also he went down and slew a lion in a <a href="../ebd/ebd296.htm#007">pit</a> in a snowy day.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> And he slew an Egyptian, a man of great stature, five cubits high; and in the Egyptian's hand was a spear like a weaver's <a href="../ebd/ebd048.htm#007">beam</a>; and he went down to him with a staff, and plucked the spear out of the Egyptian's hand, and slew him with his own spear.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> These things did <a href="../ebd/ebd052.htm#003">Benaiah</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd200.htm#009">Jehoiada</a>, and had the name among the three mighties.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> Behold, he was honourable among the thirty, but attained not to the first three: and <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> set him over his <a href="../ebd/ebd155.htm#009">guard</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> Also the valiant men of the armies were, <a href="../ebd/ebd033.htm#001">Asahel</a> the brother of <a href="../ebd/ebd207.htm#006">Joab</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd115.htm#006">Elhanan</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd105.htm#005">Dodo</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd056.htm#005">Bethlehem</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> Shammoth the Harorite, <a href="../ebd/ebd172.htm#008">Helez</a> the Pelonite,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="028">28</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd189.htm#000">Ira</a> the son of Ikkesh the Tekoite, <a href="../ebd/ebd003.htm#000">Abiezer</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd025.htm#006">Antothite</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="029">29</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd342.htm#005">Sibbecai</a> the Hushathite, <a href="../ebd/ebd187.htm#003">Ilai</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd015.htm#004">Ahohite</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="030">30</a> Maharai the Netophathite, <a href="../ebd/ebd172.htm#004">Heled</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd040.htm#007">Baanah</a> the Netophathite,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="031">31</a> Ithai the son of Ribai of <a href="../ebd/ebd147.htm#008">Gibeah</a>, that pertained to the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd052.htm#008">Benjamin</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd052.htm#003">Benaiah</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd296.htm#003">Pirathonite</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="032">32</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd185.htm#000">Hurai</a> of the brooks of <a href="../ebd/ebd140.htm#004">Gaash</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd002.htm#009">Abiel</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd028.htm#008">Arbathite</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="033">33</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd038.htm#000">Azmaveth</a> the Baharumite, Eliahba the Shaalbonite,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="034">34</a> The sons of Hashem the <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#001">Gizonite</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#000">Jonathan</a> the son of Shage the Hararite,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="035">35</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd013.htm#007">Ahiam</a> the son of Sacar the Hararite, Eliphal the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd375.htm#001">Ur</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="036">36</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd174.htm#006">Hepher</a> the Mecherathite, <a href="../ebd/ebd014.htm#000">Ahijah</a> the Pelonite,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="037">37</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd177.htm#006">Hezro</a> the Carmelite, <a href="../ebd/ebd264.htm#002">Naarai</a> the son of Ezbai,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="038">38</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd208.htm#005">Joel</a> the brother of <a href="../ebd/ebd267.htm#002">Nathan</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd252.htm#002">Mibhar</a> the son of Haggeri,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="039">39</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd390.htm#001">Zelek</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd021.htm#003">Ammonite</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd265.htm#002">Naharai</a> the Berothite, the armourbearer of <a href="../ebd/ebd207.htm#006">Joab</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd391.htm#009">Zeruiah</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="040">40</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd189.htm#000">Ira</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#006">Ithrite</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd143.htm#002">Gareb</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#006">Ithrite</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="041">41</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd375.htm#002">Uriah</a> the Hittite, <a href="../ebd/ebd385.htm#005">Zabad</a> the son of Ahlai,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="042">42</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd009.htm#000">Adina</a> the son of Shiza the Reubenite, a captain of the Reubenites, and thirty with him,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="043">43</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd162.htm#001">Hanan</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd234.htm#004">Maachah</a>, and Joshaphat the Mithnite,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="044">44</a> Uzzia the Ashterathite, Shama and <a href="../ebd/ebd200.htm#003">Jehiel</a> the sons of Hothan the Aroerite,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="045">45</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd199.htm#007">Jediael</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd338.htm#006">Shimri</a>, and Joha his brother, the Tizite,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="046">46</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd116.htm#006">Eliel</a> the Mahavite, and Jeribai, and Joshaviah, the sons of Elnaam, and Ithmah the <a href="../ebd/ebd258.htm#006">Moabite</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="047">47</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd116.htm#006">Eliel</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd276.htm#004">Obed</a>, and Jasiel the Mesobaite.<br>
 </p>
