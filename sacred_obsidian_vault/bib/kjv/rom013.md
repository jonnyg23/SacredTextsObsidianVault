 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Romans Chapter 13</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Let every soul be subject unto the higher powers. For there is no power but of God: the powers that be are ordained of God.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Whosoever therefore resisteth the power, resisteth the ordinance of God: and they that resist shall receive to themselves <a href="../ebd/ebd096.htm#008">damnation</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> For rulers are not a terror to good works, but to the evil. Wilt thou then not be afraid of the power? do that which is good, and thou shalt have praise of the same:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> For he is the <a href="../ebd/ebd255.htm#007">minister</a> of God to thee for good. But if thou do that which is evil, be afraid; for he beareth not the <a href="../ebd/ebd354.htm#003">sword</a> in vain: for he is the <a href="../ebd/ebd255.htm#007">minister</a> of God, a revenger to execute wrath upon him that doeth evil.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Wherefore ye must needs be subject, not only for wrath, but also for <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#006">conscience</a> sake.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> For for this cause pay ye <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#006">tribute</a> also: for they are God's ministers, attending continually upon this very thing.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Render therefore to all their dues: <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#006">tribute</a> to whom <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#006">tribute</a> is due; <a href="../ebd/ebd094.htm#008">custom</a> to whom <a href="../ebd/ebd094.htm#008">custom</a>; fear to whom fear; honour to whom honour.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> Owe no man any thing, but to love one another: for he that loveth another hath fulfilled the law.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> For this, Thou shalt not commit <a href="../ebd/ebd010.htm#009">adultery</a>, Thou shalt not kill, Thou shalt not steal, Thou shalt not bear false <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#008">witness</a>, Thou shalt not covet; and if there be any other commandment, it is briefly comprehended in this saying, namely, Thou shalt love thy neighbour as thyself.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> Love worketh no ill to his neighbour: therefore love is the fulfilling of the law.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And that, knowing the time, that now it is high time to awake out of sleep: for now is our <a href="../ebd/ebd320.htm#003">salvation</a> nearer than when we believed.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> The night is far spent, the day is at hand: let us therefore cast off the works of <a href="../ebd/ebd097.htm#008">darkness</a>, and let us put on the <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#006">armour</a> of light.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> Let us walk honestly, as in the day; not in rioting and drunkenness, not in <a href="../ebd/ebd076.htm#004">chambering</a> and wantonness, not in strife and envying.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> But put ye on the Lord <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a>, and make not provision for the <a href="../ebd/ebd135.htm#007">flesh</a>, to fulfil the lusts thereof.<br>
 </p>
