 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 123</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Unto thee lift I up mine eyes, O thou that dwellest in the heavens.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Behold, as the eyes of servants look unto the hand of their masters, and as the eyes of a maiden unto the hand of her mistress; so our eyes wait upon the LORD our God, until that he have <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> upon us.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Have <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> upon us, O LORD, have <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> upon us: for we are exceedingly filled with contempt.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Our soul is exceedingly filled with the scorning of those that are at ease, and with the contempt of the proud.<br>
 </p>
