 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Ezra Chapter 7</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Now after these things, in the reign of <a href="../ebd/ebd032.htm#006">Artaxerxes</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd290.htm#009">Persia</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd129.htm#006">Ezra</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd328.htm#003">Seraiah</a>, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd037.htm#005">Azariah</a>, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd178.htm#006">Hilkiah</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> The son of <a href="../ebd/ebd330.htm#008">Shallum</a>, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd386.htm#008">Zadok</a>, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd015.htm#001">Ahitub</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> The son of <a href="../ebd/ebd019.htm#002">Amariah</a>, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd037.htm#005">Azariah</a>, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd248.htm#009">Meraioth</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> The son of Zerahiah, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd376.htm#003">Uzzi</a>, the son of Bukki,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> The son of <a href="../ebd/ebd004.htm#006">Abishua</a>, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd294.htm#003">Phinehas</a>, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd115.htm#000">Eleazar</a>, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> the chief <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> This <a href="../ebd/ebd129.htm#006">Ezra</a> went up from <a href="../ebd/ebd041.htm#001">Babylon</a>; and he was a ready scribe in the law of <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, which the LORD God of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> had given: and the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> granted him all his request, according to the hand of the LORD his God upon him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And there went up some of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, and of the priests, and the Levites, and the singers, and the porters, and the Nethinims, unto <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>, in the seventh year of <a href="../ebd/ebd032.htm#006">Artaxerxes</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And he came to <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a> in the fifth month, which was in the seventh year of the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> For upon the first day of the first month began he to go up from <a href="../ebd/ebd041.htm#001">Babylon</a>, and on the first day of the fifth month came he to <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>, according to the good hand of his God upon him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> For <a href="../ebd/ebd129.htm#006">Ezra</a> had prepared his heart to seek the law of the LORD, and to do it, and to teach in <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> statutes and judgments.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> Now this is the copy of the letter that the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd032.htm#006">Artaxerxes</a> gave unto <a href="../ebd/ebd129.htm#006">Ezra</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a>, the scribe, even a scribe of the words of the commandments of the LORD, and of his statutes to <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd032.htm#006">Artaxerxes</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of kings, unto <a href="../ebd/ebd129.htm#006">Ezra</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a>, a scribe of the law of the God of <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#006">heaven</a>, perfect peace, and at such a time.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> I make a decree, that all they of the people of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, and of his priests and Levites, in my realm, which are minded of their own freewill to go up to <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>, go with thee.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> Forasmuch as thou art sent of the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a>, and of his <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> counsellors, to enquire concerning <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>, according to the law of thy God which is in thine hand;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And to carry the <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#000">silver</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a>, which the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> and his counsellors have freely offered unto the God of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, whose <a href="../ebd/ebd156.htm#007">habitation</a> is in <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> And all the <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#000">silver</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a> that thou canst find in all the province of <a href="../ebd/ebd041.htm#001">Babylon</a>, with the freewill <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> of the people, and of the priests, <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> willingly for the house of their God which is in <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> That thou mayest buy speedily with this money bullocks, rams, lambs, with their meat offerings and their <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a> offerings, and offer them upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> of the house of your God which is in <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> And whatsoever shall seem good to thee, and to thy brethren, to do with the rest of the <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#000">silver</a> and the <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a>, that do after the will of your God.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> The vessels also that are given thee for the service of the house of thy God, those deliver thou before the God of <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> And whatsoever more shall be needful for the house of thy God, which thou shalt have occasion to bestow, bestow it out of the king's treasure house.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> And I, even I <a href="../ebd/ebd032.htm#006">Artaxerxes</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a>, do make a decree to all the treasurers which are beyond the river, that whatsoever <a href="../ebd/ebd129.htm#006">Ezra</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a>, the scribe of the law of the God of <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#006">heaven</a>, shall require of you, it be done speedily,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> Unto an hundred talents of <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#000">silver</a>, and to an hundred measures of <a href="../ebd/ebd381.htm#000">wheat</a>, and to an hundred <a href="../ebd/ebd047.htm#004">baths</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#000">wine</a>, and to an hundred <a href="../ebd/ebd047.htm#004">baths</a> of oil, and <a href="../ebd/ebd319.htm#008">salt</a> without prescribing how much.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> Whatsoever is commanded by the God of <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#006">heaven</a>, let it be diligently done for the house of the God of <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#006">heaven</a>: for why should there be wrath against the realm of the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> and his sons?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> Also we certify you, that touching any of the priests and Levites, singers, porters, Nethinims, or ministers of this house of God, it shall not be lawful to impose <a href="../ebd/ebd370.htm#000">toll</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#006">tribute</a>, or <a href="../ebd/ebd094.htm#008">custom</a>, upon them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> And thou, <a href="../ebd/ebd129.htm#006">Ezra</a>, after the wisdom of thy God, that is in thine hand, set magistrates and judges, which may <a href="../ebd/ebd213.htm#005">judge</a> all the people that are beyond the river, all such as know the laws of thy God; and teach ye them that know them not.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> And whosoever will not do the law of thy God, and the law of the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a>, let judgment be executed speedily upon him, whether it be unto death, or to banishment, or to confiscation of goods, or to imprisonment.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> Blessed be the LORD God of our fathers, which hath put such a thing as this in the king's heart, to beautify the house of the LORD which is in <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="028">28</a> And hath extended <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> unto me before the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a>, and his counsellors, and before all the king's mighty princes. And I was strengthened as the hand of the LORD my God was upon me, and I gathered together out of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> chief men to go up with me.<br>
 </p>
