 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Philippians Chapter 1</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd287.htm#002">Paul</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd367.htm#001">Timotheus</a>, the servants of <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a>, to all the saints in <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a> which are at <a href="../ebd/ebd293.htm#009">Philippi</a>, with the bishops and deacons:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd154.htm#000">Grace</a> be unto you, and peace, from God our Father, and from the Lord <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> I thank my God upon every remembrance of you,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Always in every <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#000">prayer</a> of mine for you all making request with joy,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> For your <a href="../ebd/ebd132.htm#002">fellowship</a> in the <a href="../ebd/ebd153.htm#003">gospel</a> from the first day until now;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Being confident of this very thing, that he which hath begun a good work in you will perform it until the day of <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Even as it is meet for me to think this of you all, because I have you in my heart; inasmuch as both in my bonds, and in the defence and confirmation of the <a href="../ebd/ebd153.htm#003">gospel</a>, ye all are partakers of my <a href="../ebd/ebd154.htm#000">grace</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> For God is my record, how greatly I long after you all in the <a href="../ebd/ebd063.htm#004">bowels</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And this I pray, that your love may abound yet more and more in knowledge and in all judgment;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> That ye may approve things that are excellent; that ye may be sincere and without offence till the day of <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> Being filled with the fruits of <a href="../ebd/ebd313.htm#004">righteousness</a>, which are by <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a>, unto the <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a> and praise of God.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> But I would ye should understand, brethren, that the things which happened unto me have fallen out rather unto the furtherance of the <a href="../ebd/ebd153.htm#003">gospel</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> So that my bonds in <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a> are manifest in all the <a href="../ebd/ebd282.htm#008">palace</a>, and in all other places;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> And many of the brethren in the Lord, waxing confident by my bonds, are much more bold to speak the word without fear.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> Some indeed preach <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a> even of envy and strife; and some also of good will:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> The one preach <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a> of contention, not sincerely, supposing to add affliction to my bonds:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> But the other of love, knowing that I am set for the defence of the <a href="../ebd/ebd153.htm#003">gospel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> What then? notwithstanding, every way, whether in pretence, or in truth, <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a> is preached; and I therein do rejoice, yea, and will rejoice.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> For I know that this shall turn to my <a href="../ebd/ebd320.htm#003">salvation</a> through your <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#000">prayer</a>, and the supply of the <a href="../ebd/ebd349.htm#009">Spirit</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> According to my <a href="../ebd/ebd110.htm#006">earnest</a> expectation and my hope, that in nothing I shall be ashamed, but that with all boldness, as always, so now also <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a> shall be magnified in my body, whether it be by life, or by death.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> For to me to live is <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a>, and to die is gain.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> But if I live in the <a href="../ebd/ebd135.htm#007">flesh</a>, this is the <a href="../ebd/ebd139.htm#000">fruit</a> of my labour: yet what I shall choose I wot not.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> For I am in a strait betwixt two, having a desire to depart, and to be with <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a>; which is far better:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> Nevertheless to abide in the <a href="../ebd/ebd135.htm#007">flesh</a> is more needful for you.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> And having this confidence, I know that I shall abide and continue with you all for your furtherance and joy of faith;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> That your rejoicing may be more abundant in <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a> for me by my coming to you again.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> Only let your <a href="../ebd/ebd089.htm#001">conversation</a> be as it becometh the <a href="../ebd/ebd153.htm#003">gospel</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a>: that whether I come and see you, or else be absent, I may hear of your affairs, that ye stand <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#004">fast</a> in one <a href="../ebd/ebd349.htm#009">spirit</a>, with one mind striving together for the faith of the <a href="../ebd/ebd153.htm#003">gospel</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="028">28</a> And in nothing terrified by your adversaries: which is to them an evident token of perdition, but to you of <a href="../ebd/ebd320.htm#003">salvation</a>, and that of God.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="029">29</a> For unto you it is given in the behalf of <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a>, not only to believe on him, but also to suffer for his sake;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="030">30</a> Having the same conflict which ye saw in me, and now hear to be in me.<br>
 </p>
