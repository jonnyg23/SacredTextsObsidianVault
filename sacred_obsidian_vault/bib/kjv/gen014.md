 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Genesis Chapter 14</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> And it came to pass in the days of <a href="../ebd/ebd022.htm#002">Amraphel</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of Shinar, <a href="../ebd/ebd030.htm#007">Arioch</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd118.htm#007">Ellasar</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd078.htm#003">Chedorlaomer</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd114.htm#003">Elam</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd366.htm#000">Tidal</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of nations;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> That these made war with <a href="../ebd/ebd053.htm#000">Bera</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd347.htm#004">Sodom</a>, and with <a href="../ebd/ebd059.htm#001">Birsha</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#007">Gomorrah</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd339.htm#001">Shinab</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd009.htm#003">Admah</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd334.htm#007">Shemeber</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of Zeboiim, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd051.htm#006">Bela</a>, which is <a href="../ebd/ebd395.htm#004">Zoar</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> All these were joined together in the vale of Siddim, which is the <a href="../ebd/ebd319.htm#008">salt</a> sea.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Twelve years they served <a href="../ebd/ebd078.htm#003">Chedorlaomer</a>, and in the thirteenth year they rebelled.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And in the fourteenth year came <a href="../ebd/ebd078.htm#003">Chedorlaomer</a>, and the kings that were with him, and smote the Rephaims in Ashteroth Karnaim, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd396.htm#008">Zuzims</a> in <a href="../ebd/ebd160.htm#003">Ham</a>, and the Emins in Shaveh Kiriathaim,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd182.htm#001">Horites</a> in their <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#007">mount</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd326.htm#004">Seir</a>, unto <a href="../ebd/ebd119.htm#001">Elparan</a>, which is by the <a href="../ebd/ebd381.htm#005">wilderness</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And they returned, and came to Enmishpat, which is <a href="../ebd/ebd215.htm#003">Kadesh</a>, and smote all the country of the Amalekites, and also the <a href="../ebd/ebd021.htm#006">Amorites</a>, that dwelt in Hazezontamar.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And there went out the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd347.htm#004">Sodom</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#007">Gomorrah</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd009.htm#003">Admah</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of Zeboiim, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd051.htm#006">Bela</a> (the same is <a href="../ebd/ebd395.htm#004">Zoar</a>;) and they joined battle with them in the vale of Siddim;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> With <a href="../ebd/ebd078.htm#003">Chedorlaomer</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd114.htm#003">Elam</a>, and with <a href="../ebd/ebd366.htm#000">Tidal</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of nations, and <a href="../ebd/ebd022.htm#002">Amraphel</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of Shinar, and <a href="../ebd/ebd030.htm#007">Arioch</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd118.htm#007">Ellasar</a>; four kings with five.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And the vale of Siddim was full of slimepits; and the kings of <a href="../ebd/ebd347.htm#004">Sodom</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#007">Gomorrah</a> fled, and fell there; and they that remained fled to the mountain.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And they took all the goods of <a href="../ebd/ebd347.htm#004">Sodom</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#007">Gomorrah</a>, and all their victuals, and went their way.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And they took Lot, Abram's brother's son, who dwelt in <a href="../ebd/ebd347.htm#004">Sodom</a>, and his goods, and departed.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> And there came one that had escaped, and told <a href="../ebd/ebd005.htm#004">Abram</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#009">Hebrew</a>; for he dwelt in the <a href="../ebd/ebd297.htm#002">plain</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd239.htm#008">Mamre</a> the Amorite, brother of <a href="../ebd/ebd125.htm#000">Eshcol</a>, and brother of <a href="../ebd/ebd024.htm#000">Aner</a>: and these were confederate with <a href="../ebd/ebd005.htm#004">Abram</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> And when <a href="../ebd/ebd005.htm#004">Abram</a> heard that his brother was taken <a href="../ebd/ebd072.htm#001">captive</a>, he armed his trained servants, born in his own house, three hundred and eighteen, and pursued them unto <a href="../ebd/ebd096.htm#009">Dan</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And he divided himself against them, he and his servants, by night, and smote them, and pursued them unto <a href="../ebd/ebd180.htm#001">Hobah</a>, which is on the left hand of <a href="../ebd/ebd096.htm#007">Damascus</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> And he brought back all the goods, and also brought again his brother Lot, and his goods, and the women also, and the people.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd347.htm#004">Sodom</a> went out to meet him after his return from the slaughter of <a href="../ebd/ebd078.htm#003">Chedorlaomer</a>, and of the kings that were with him, at the <a href="../ebd/ebd376.htm#008">valley</a> of Shaveh, which is the king's dale.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd247.htm#003">Melchizedek</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd319.htm#001">Salem</a> brought forth <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#006">bread</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#000">wine</a>: and he was the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> of the most high God.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> And he blessed him, and said, Blessed be <a href="../ebd/ebd005.htm#004">Abram</a> of the most high God, possessor of <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#006">heaven</a> and earth:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> And blessed be the most high God, which hath delivered thine enemies into thy hand. And he gave him tithes of all.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd347.htm#004">Sodom</a> said unto <a href="../ebd/ebd005.htm#004">Abram</a>, Give me the persons, and take the goods to thyself.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd005.htm#004">Abram</a> said to the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd347.htm#004">Sodom</a>, I have lift up mine hand unto the LORD, the most high God, the possessor of <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#006">heaven</a> and earth,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> That I will not take from a thread even to a shoelatchet, and that I will not take any thing that is thine, lest thou shouldest say, I have made <a href="../ebd/ebd005.htm#004">Abram</a> rich:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> Save only that which the young men have eaten, and the portion of the men which went with me, <a href="../ebd/ebd024.htm#000">Aner</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd125.htm#000">Eshcol</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd239.htm#008">Mamre</a>; let them take their portion.<br>
 </p>
