 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Isaiah Chapter 4</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> And in that day <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> women shall take hold of one man, saying, We will eat our own <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#006">bread</a>, and wear our own <a href="../ebd/ebd027.htm#000">apparel</a>: only let us be called by thy name, to take away our reproach.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> In that day shall the <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#002">branch</a> of the LORD be beautiful and glorious, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd139.htm#000">fruit</a> of the earth shall be excellent and comely for them that are escaped of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And it shall come to pass, that he that is left in <a href="../ebd/ebd394.htm#002">Zion</a>, and he that remaineth in <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>, shall be called holy, even every one that is written among the living in <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> When the Lord shall have washed away the filth of the daughters of <a href="../ebd/ebd394.htm#002">Zion</a>, and shall have purged the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a> from the midst thereof by the <a href="../ebd/ebd349.htm#009">spirit</a> of judgment, and by the <a href="../ebd/ebd349.htm#009">spirit</a> of burning.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And the LORD will create upon every dwelling place of <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#007">mount</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd394.htm#002">Zion</a>, and upon her assemblies, a <a href="../ebd/ebd085.htm#001">cloud</a> and smoke by day, and the shining of a flaming fire by night: for upon all the <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a> shall be a defence.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And there shall be a <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> for a <a href="../ebd/ebd330.htm#002">shadow</a> in the day time from the heat, and for a place of refuge, and for a covert from storm and from <a href="../ebd/ebd305.htm#008">rain</a>.<br>
 </p>
