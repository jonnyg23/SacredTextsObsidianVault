 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Hosea Chapter 10</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> is an empty <a href="../ebd/ebd377.htm#004">vine</a>, he bringeth forth <a href="../ebd/ebd139.htm#000">fruit</a> unto himself: according to the multitude of his <a href="../ebd/ebd139.htm#000">fruit</a> he hath increased the altars; according to the <a href="../ebd/ebd153.htm#000">goodness</a> of his land they have made goodly images.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Their heart is divided; now shall they be found faulty: he shall break down their altars, he shall spoil their images.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> For now they shall say, We have no <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a>, because we feared not the LORD; what then should a <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> do to us?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> They have spoken words, swearing falsely in making a <a href="../ebd/ebd091.htm#008">covenant</a>: thus judgment springeth up as <a href="../ebd/ebd174.htm#001">hemlock</a> in the furrows of the field.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> The inhabitants of <a href="../ebd/ebd320.htm#004">Samaria</a> shall fear because of the calves of Bethaven: for the people thereof shall <a href="../ebd/ebd261.htm#004">mourn</a> over it, and the priests thereof that rejoiced on it, for the <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a> thereof, because it is departed from it.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> It shall be also carried unto <a href="../ebd/ebd035.htm#005">Assyria</a> for a present to <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> Jareb: <a href="../ebd/ebd123.htm#000">Ephraim</a> shall receive shame, and <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> shall be ashamed of his own counsel.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> As for <a href="../ebd/ebd320.htm#004">Samaria</a>, her <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> is cut off as the <a href="../ebd/ebd136.htm#005">foam</a> upon the water.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> The high places also of <a href="../ebd/ebd036.htm#009">Aven</a>, the <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, shall be destroyed: the <a href="../ebd/ebd364.htm#006">thorn</a> and the <a href="../ebd/ebd364.htm#004">thistle</a> shall come up on their altars; and they shall say to the mountains, Cover us; and to the hills, Fall on us.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> O <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, thou hast sinned from the days of <a href="../ebd/ebd147.htm#008">Gibeah</a>: there they stood: the battle in <a href="../ebd/ebd147.htm#008">Gibeah</a> against the children of iniquity did not overtake them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> It is in my desire that I should chastise them; and the people shall be gathered against them, when they shall bind themselves in their two furrows.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd123.htm#000">Ephraim</a> is as an <a href="../ebd/ebd171.htm#006">heifer</a> that is taught, and loveth to tread out the <a href="../ebd/ebd090.htm#006">corn</a>; but I passed over upon her fair <a href="../ebd/ebd269.htm#000">neck</a>: I will make <a href="../ebd/ebd123.htm#000">Ephraim</a> to ride; <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a> shall plow, and <a href="../ebd/ebd194.htm#007">Jacob</a> shall break his clods.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> Sow to yourselves in <a href="../ebd/ebd313.htm#004">righteousness</a>, reap in <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a>; break up your fallow ground: for it is time to seek the LORD, till he come and <a href="../ebd/ebd305.htm#008">rain</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd313.htm#004">righteousness</a> upon you.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> Ye have plowed wickedness, ye have reaped iniquity; ye have eaten the <a href="../ebd/ebd139.htm#000">fruit</a> of lies: because thou didst trust in thy way, in the multitude of thy mighty men.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> Therefore shall a tumult arise among thy people, and all thy fortresses shall be spoiled, as <a href="../ebd/ebd330.htm#009">Shalman</a> spoiled Betharbel in the day of battle: the mother was dashed in <a href="../ebd/ebd295.htm#002">pieces</a> upon her children.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> So shall <a href="../ebd/ebd055.htm#006">Bethel</a> do unto you because of your great wickedness: in a morning shall the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> utterly be cut off.<br>
 </p>
