 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 15</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Lord, who shall abide in thy <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a>? who shall dwell in thy holy hill?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> He that walketh uprightly, and worketh <a href="../ebd/ebd313.htm#004">righteousness</a>, and speaketh the truth in his heart.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> He that backbiteth not with his tongue, nor doeth evil to his neighbour, nor taketh up a reproach against his neighbour.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> In whose eyes a vile person is contemned; but he honoureth them that fear the LORD. He that sweareth to his own hurt, and changeth not.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> He that putteth not out his money to <a href="../ebd/ebd375.htm#006">usury</a>, nor taketh reward against the innocent. He that doeth these things shall never be moved.<br>
 </p>
