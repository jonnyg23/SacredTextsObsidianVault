 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 52</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Why boastest thou thyself in mischief, O mighty man? the <a href="../ebd/ebd153.htm#000">goodness</a> of God endureth continually.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> The tongue deviseth mischiefs; like a sharp <a href="../ebd/ebd307.htm#009">razor</a>, working deceitfully.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Thou lovest evil more than good; and lying rather than to speak <a href="../ebd/ebd313.htm#004">righteousness</a>. <a href="../ebd/ebd326.htm#008">Selah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Thou lovest all devouring words, O thou deceitful tongue.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> God shall likewise destroy thee for ever, he shall take thee away, and pluck thee out of thy dwelling place, and root thee out of the land of the living. <a href="../ebd/ebd326.htm#008">Selah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> The righteous also shall see, and fear, and shall laugh at him:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Lo, this is the man that made not God his strength; but trusted in the abundance of his riches, and strengthened himself in his wickedness.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> But I am like a green <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#009">olive</a> tree in the house of God: I trust in the <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> of God for ever and ever.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> I will praise thee for ever, because thou hast done it: and I will wait on thy name; for it is good before thy saints.<br>
 </p>
