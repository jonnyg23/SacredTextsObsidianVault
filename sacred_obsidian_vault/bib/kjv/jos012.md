 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Joshua Chapter 12</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Now these are the kings of the land, which the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> smote, and possessed their land on the other side <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#004">Jordan</a> toward the rising of the <a href="../ebd/ebd353.htm#001">sun</a>, from the river <a href="../ebd/ebd032.htm#000">Arnon</a> unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#007">mount</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd175.htm#006">Hermon</a>, and all the <a href="../ebd/ebd297.htm#002">plain</a> on the <a href="../ebd/ebd111.htm#000">east</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd343.htm#002">Sihon</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd021.htm#006">Amorites</a>, who dwelt in <a href="../ebd/ebd176.htm#009">Heshbon</a>, and ruled from <a href="../ebd/ebd032.htm#001">Aroer</a>, which is upon the bank of the river <a href="../ebd/ebd032.htm#000">Arnon</a>, and from the middle of the river, and from half <a href="../ebd/ebd148.htm#009">Gilead</a>, even unto the river <a href="../ebd/ebd193.htm#006">Jabbok</a>, which is the border of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd021.htm#002">Ammon</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And from the <a href="../ebd/ebd297.htm#002">plain</a> to the sea of Chinneroth on the <a href="../ebd/ebd111.htm#000">east</a>, and unto the sea of the <a href="../ebd/ebd297.htm#002">plain</a>, even the <a href="../ebd/ebd319.htm#008">salt</a> sea on the <a href="../ebd/ebd111.htm#000">east</a>, the way to Bethjeshimoth; and from the south, under Ashdothpisgah:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And the coast of <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#002">Og</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd046.htm#003">Bashan</a>, which was of the remnant of the <a href="../ebd/ebd147.htm#006">giants</a>, that dwelt at <a href="../ebd/ebd034.htm#004">Ashtaroth</a> and at <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#002">Edrei</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And reigned in <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#007">mount</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd175.htm#006">Hermon</a>, and in <a href="../ebd/ebd319.htm#000">Salcah</a>, and in all <a href="../ebd/ebd046.htm#003">Bashan</a>, unto the border of the <a href="../ebd/ebd147.htm#002">Geshurites</a> and the Maachathites, and half <a href="../ebd/ebd148.htm#009">Gilead</a>, the border of <a href="../ebd/ebd343.htm#002">Sihon</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd176.htm#009">Heshbon</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Them did <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> the servant of the LORD and the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> smite: and <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> the servant of the LORD gave it for a possession unto the Reubenites, and the Gadites, and the half <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd240.htm#002">Manasseh</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And these are the kings of the country which <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#006">Joshua</a> and the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> smote on this side <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#004">Jordan</a> on the west, from Baalgad in the <a href="../ebd/ebd376.htm#008">valley</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd226.htm#001">Lebanon</a> even unto the <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#007">mount</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd159.htm#006">Halak</a>, that goeth up to <a href="../ebd/ebd326.htm#004">Seir</a>; which <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#006">Joshua</a> gave unto the tribes of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> for a possession according to their divisions;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> In the mountains, and in the valleys, and in the plains, and in the springs, and in the <a href="../ebd/ebd381.htm#005">wilderness</a>, and in the south country; the <a href="../ebd/ebd179.htm#006">Hittites</a>, the <a href="../ebd/ebd021.htm#006">Amorites</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#007">Canaanites</a>, the <a href="../ebd/ebd290.htm#006">Perizzites</a>, the <a href="../ebd/ebd179.htm#007">Hivites</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd199.htm#004">Jebusites</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> The <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd203.htm#008">Jericho</a>, one; the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd015.htm#009">Ai</a>, which is beside <a href="../ebd/ebd055.htm#006">Bethel</a>, one;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> The <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>, one; the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd171.htm#004">Hebron</a>, one;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> The <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd197.htm#007">Jarmuth</a>, one; the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd222.htm#009">Lachish</a>, one;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> The <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#008">Eglon</a>, one; the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd147.htm#004">Gezer</a>, one;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> The <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd099.htm#007">Debir</a>, one; the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd145.htm#000">Geder</a>, one;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> The <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd182.htm#002">Hormah</a>, one; the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd028.htm#000">Arad</a>, one;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> The <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd228.htm#004">Libnah</a>, one; the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd010.htm#007">Adullam</a>, one;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> The <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd238.htm#006">Makkedah</a>, one; the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd055.htm#006">Bethel</a>, one;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> The <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd358.htm#008">Tappuah</a>, one; the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd174.htm#006">Hepher</a>, one;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> The <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of Aphek, one; the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of Lasharon, one;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> The <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd236.htm#003">Madon</a>, one; the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd169.htm#006">Hazor</a>, one;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> The <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of Shimronmeron, one; the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd007.htm#002">Achshaph</a>, one;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> The <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd355.htm#005">Taanach</a>, one; the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd246.htm#004">Megiddo</a>, one;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> The <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd216.htm#006">Kedesh</a>, one; the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd210.htm#002">Jokneam</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd072.htm#006">Carmel</a>, one;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> The <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd106.htm#003">Dor</a> in the coast of <a href="../ebd/ebd106.htm#003">Dor</a>, one; the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of the nations of <a href="../ebd/ebd149.htm#001">Gilgal</a>, one;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> The <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of Tirzah, one: all the kings thirty and one.<br>
 </p>
