 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: 1 Chronicles Chapter 2</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> These are the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>; <a href="../ebd/ebd311.htm#009">Reuben</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#002">Simeon</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd227.htm#006">Levi</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#002">Issachar</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd389.htm#003">Zebulun</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd096.htm#009">Dan</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#005">Joseph</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd052.htm#008">Benjamin</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd266.htm#006">Naphtali</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd140.htm#007">Gad</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd033.htm#008">Asher</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> The sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>; Er, and <a href="../ebd/ebd278.htm#008">Onan</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd334.htm#000">Shelah</a>: which three were born unto him of the daughter of <a href="../ebd/ebd341.htm#005">Shua</a> the Canaanitess. And Er, the firstborn of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>, was evil in the sight of the LORD; and he slew him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd358.htm#003">Tamar</a> his daughter in law bore him <a href="../ebd/ebd292.htm#007">Pharez</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd391.htm#001">Zerah</a>. All the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a> were five.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> The sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd292.htm#007">Pharez</a>; <a href="../ebd/ebd177.htm#007">Hezron</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd161.htm#008">Hamul</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd391.htm#001">Zerah</a>; <a href="../ebd/ebd393.htm#009">Zimri</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd126.htm#002">Ethan</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd173.htm#009">Heman</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd069.htm#002">Calcol</a>, and Dara: five of them in all.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd072.htm#007">Carmi</a>; Achar, the troubler of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, who transgressed in the thing accursed.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd126.htm#002">Ethan</a>; <a href="../ebd/ebd037.htm#005">Azariah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> The sons also of <a href="../ebd/ebd177.htm#007">Hezron</a>, that were born unto him; <a href="../ebd/ebd203.htm#005">Jerahmeel</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd306.htm#003">Ram</a>, and Chelubai.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd306.htm#003">Ram</a> begat <a href="../ebd/ebd020.htm#008">Amminadab</a>; and <a href="../ebd/ebd020.htm#008">Amminadab</a> begat <a href="../ebd/ebd265.htm#007">Nahshon</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#004">prince</a> of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd265.htm#007">Nahshon</a> begat Salma, and Salma begat <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#006">Boaz</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#006">Boaz</a> begat <a href="../ebd/ebd276.htm#004">Obed</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd276.htm#004">Obed</a> begat <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#005">Jesse</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#005">Jesse</a> begat his firstborn <a href="../ebd/ebd115.htm#008">Eliab</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd004.htm#001">Abinadab</a> the second, and Shimma the third,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd271.htm#003">Nethaneel</a> the fourth, Raddai the fifth,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd281.htm#009">Ozem</a> the sixth, <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> the seventh:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> Whose sisters were <a href="../ebd/ebd391.htm#009">Zeruiah</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd003.htm#002">Abigail</a>. And the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd391.htm#009">Zeruiah</a>; <a href="../ebd/ebd004.htm#005">Abishai</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd207.htm#006">Joab</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd033.htm#001">Asahel</a>, three.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd003.htm#002">Abigail</a> bare <a href="../ebd/ebd019.htm#003">Amasa</a>: and the father of <a href="../ebd/ebd019.htm#003">Amasa</a> was <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#007">Jether</a> the Ishmeelite.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd069.htm#003">Caleb</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd177.htm#007">Hezron</a> begat children of <a href="../ebd/ebd038.htm#002">Azubah</a> his wife, and of Jerioth: her sons are these; <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#001">Jesher</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd340.htm#001">Shobab</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd029.htm#009">Ardon</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> And when <a href="../ebd/ebd038.htm#002">Azubah</a> was dead, <a href="../ebd/ebd069.htm#003">Caleb</a> took unto him Ephrath, which bare him <a href="../ebd/ebd184.htm#009">Hur</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd184.htm#009">Hur</a> begat Uri, and Uri begat <a href="../ebd/ebd057.htm#009">Bezaleel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> And afterward <a href="../ebd/ebd177.htm#007">Hezron</a> went in to the daughter of <a href="../ebd/ebd235.htm#006">Machir</a> the father of <a href="../ebd/ebd148.htm#009">Gilead</a>, whom he married when he was threescore years old; and she bare him <a href="../ebd/ebd326.htm#003">Segub</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd326.htm#003">Segub</a> begat <a href="../ebd/ebd196.htm#001">Jair</a>, who had three and twenty cities in the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd148.htm#009">Gilead</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> And he took <a href="../ebd/ebd147.htm#001">Geshur</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd028.htm#001">Aram</a>, with the towns of <a href="../ebd/ebd196.htm#001">Jair</a>, from them, with <a href="../ebd/ebd217.htm#002">Kenath</a>, and the towns thereof, even threescore cities. All these belonged to the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd235.htm#006">Machir</a> the father of <a href="../ebd/ebd148.htm#009">Gilead</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> And after that <a href="../ebd/ebd177.htm#007">Hezron</a> was dead in Calebephratah, then Abiah Hezron's wife bare him Ashur the father of Tekoa.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> And the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd203.htm#005">Jerahmeel</a> the firstborn of <a href="../ebd/ebd177.htm#007">Hezron</a> were, <a href="../ebd/ebd306.htm#003">Ram</a> the firstborn, and Bunah, and <a href="../ebd/ebd280.htm#003">Oren</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd281.htm#009">Ozem</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd014.htm#000">Ahijah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd203.htm#005">Jerahmeel</a> had also another wife, whose name was Atarah; she was the mother of Onam.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> And the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd306.htm#003">Ram</a> the firstborn of <a href="../ebd/ebd203.htm#005">Jerahmeel</a> were, Maaz, and Jamin, and Eker.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="028">28</a> And the sons of Onam were, Shammai, and Jada. And the sons of Shammai; <a href="../ebd/ebd264.htm#008">Nadab</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd004.htm#007">Abishur</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="029">29</a> And the name of the wife of <a href="../ebd/ebd004.htm#007">Abishur</a> was <a href="../ebd/ebd003.htm#003">Abihail</a>, and she bare him Ahban, and Molid.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="030">30</a> And the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd264.htm#008">Nadab</a>; Seled, and Appaim: but Seled died without children.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="031">31</a> And the sons of Appaim; <a href="../ebd/ebd190.htm#004">Ishi</a>. And the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd190.htm#004">Ishi</a>; Sheshan. And the children of Sheshan; Ahlai.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="032">32</a> And the sons of Jada the brother of Shammai; <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#007">Jether</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#000">Jonathan</a>: and <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#007">Jether</a> died without children.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="033">33</a> And the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#000">Jonathan</a>; <a href="../ebd/ebd288.htm#009">Peleth</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd388.htm#002">Zaza</a>. These were the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd203.htm#005">Jerahmeel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="034">34</a> Now Sheshan had no sons, but daughters. And Sheshan had a servant, an Egyptian, whose name was Jarha.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="035">35</a> And Sheshan gave his daughter to Jarha his servant to wife; and she bare him Attai.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="036">36</a> And Attai begat <a href="../ebd/ebd267.htm#002">Nathan</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd267.htm#002">Nathan</a> begat <a href="../ebd/ebd385.htm#005">Zabad</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="037">37</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd385.htm#005">Zabad</a> begat Ephlal, and Ephlal begat <a href="../ebd/ebd276.htm#004">Obed</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="038">38</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd276.htm#004">Obed</a> begat <a href="../ebd/ebd202.htm#007">Jehu</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd202.htm#007">Jehu</a> begat <a href="../ebd/ebd037.htm#005">Azariah</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="039">39</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd037.htm#005">Azariah</a> begat <a href="../ebd/ebd172.htm#008">Helez</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd172.htm#008">Helez</a> begat Eleasah,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="040">40</a> And Eleasah begat Sisamai, and Sisamai begat <a href="../ebd/ebd330.htm#008">Shallum</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="041">41</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd330.htm#008">Shallum</a> begat Jekamiah, and Jekamiah begat <a href="../ebd/ebd118.htm#001">Elishama</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="042">42</a> Now the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd069.htm#003">Caleb</a> the brother of <a href="../ebd/ebd203.htm#005">Jerahmeel</a> were, <a href="../ebd/ebd250.htm#006">Mesha</a> his firstborn, which was the father of <a href="../ebd/ebd394.htm#004">Ziph</a>; and the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd241.htm#009">Mareshah</a> the father of <a href="../ebd/ebd171.htm#004">Hebron</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="043">43</a> And the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd171.htm#004">Hebron</a>; <a href="../ebd/ebd222.htm#003">Korah</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd358.htm#008">Tappuah</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd310.htm#002">Rekem</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd334.htm#003">Shema</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="044">44</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd334.htm#003">Shema</a> begat <a href="../ebd/ebd305.htm#007">Raham</a>, the father of Jorkoam: and <a href="../ebd/ebd310.htm#002">Rekem</a> begat Shammai.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="045">45</a> And the son of Shammai was <a href="../ebd/ebd241.htm#001">Maon</a>: and <a href="../ebd/ebd241.htm#001">Maon</a> was the father of <a href="../ebd/ebd057.htm#004">Bethzur</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="046">46</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd122.htm#003">Ephah</a>, Caleb's <a href="../ebd/ebd087.htm#007">concubine</a>, bare <a href="../ebd/ebd163.htm#007">Haran</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd261.htm#007">Moza</a>, and Gazez: and <a href="../ebd/ebd163.htm#007">Haran</a> begat Gazez.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="047">47</a> And the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd195.htm#007">Jahdai</a>; Regem, and <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#000">Jotham</a>, and Gesham, and <a href="../ebd/ebd288.htm#008">Pelet</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd122.htm#003">Ephah</a>, and Shaaph.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="048">48</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd234.htm#004">Maachah</a>, Caleb's <a href="../ebd/ebd087.htm#007">concubine</a>, bare Sheber, and Tirhanah.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="049">49</a> She bare also Shaaph the father of <a href="../ebd/ebd235.htm#009">Madmannah</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd336.htm#007">Sheva</a> the father of Machbenah, and the father of Gibea: and the daughter of <a href="../ebd/ebd069.htm#003">Caleb</a> was Achsa.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="050">50</a> These were the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd069.htm#003">Caleb</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd184.htm#009">Hur</a>, the firstborn of <a href="../ebd/ebd123.htm#006">Ephratah</a>; <a href="../ebd/ebd340.htm#004">Shobal</a> the father of Kirjathjearim.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="051">51</a> Salma the father of <a href="../ebd/ebd056.htm#005">Bethlehem</a>, Hareph the father of Bethgader.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="052">52</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd340.htm#004">Shobal</a> the father of Kirjathjearim had sons; Haroeh, and half of the Manahethites.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="053">53</a> And the families of Kirjathjearim; the Ithrites, and the Puhites, and the Shumathites, and the Mishraites; of them came the Zareathites, and the Eshtaulites,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="054">54</a> The sons of Salma; <a href="../ebd/ebd056.htm#005">Bethlehem</a>, and the Netophathites, <a href="../ebd/ebd036.htm#000">Ataroth</a>, the house of <a href="../ebd/ebd207.htm#006">Joab</a>, and half of the Manahethites, the Zorites.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="055">55</a> And the families of the <a href="../ebd/ebd324.htm#003">scribes</a> which dwelt at <a href="../ebd/ebd193.htm#009">Jabez</a>; the Tirathites, the Shimeathites, and Suchathites. These are the <a href="../ebd/ebd217.htm#004">Kenites</a> that came of <a href="../ebd/ebd174.htm#000">Hemath</a>, the father of the house of <a href="../ebd/ebd308.htm#002">Rechab</a>.<br>
 </p>
