 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: 2 Kings (2 Samuel) Chapter 16</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> And when <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> was a little past the top of the hill, behold, <a href="../ebd/ebd392.htm#003">Ziba</a> the servant of <a href="../ebd/ebd248.htm#006">Mephibosheth</a> met him, with a couple of asses saddled, and upon them two hundred loaves of <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#006">bread</a>, and an hundred bunches of <a href="../ebd/ebd306.htm#000">raisins</a>, and an hundred of summer fruits, and a <a href="../ebd/ebd063.htm#002">bottle</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#000">wine</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> said unto <a href="../ebd/ebd392.htm#003">Ziba</a>, What meanest thou by these? And <a href="../ebd/ebd392.htm#003">Ziba</a> said, The asses be for the king's household to ride on; and the <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#006">bread</a> and summer <a href="../ebd/ebd139.htm#000">fruit</a> for the young men to eat; and the <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#000">wine</a>, that such as be faint in the <a href="../ebd/ebd381.htm#005">wilderness</a> may <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> said, And where is thy master's son? And <a href="../ebd/ebd392.htm#003">Ziba</a> said unto the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a>, Behold, he abideth at <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>: for he said, To day shall the house of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> restore me the kingdom of my father.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Then said the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> to <a href="../ebd/ebd392.htm#003">Ziba</a>, Behold, thine are all that pertained unto <a href="../ebd/ebd248.htm#006">Mephibosheth</a>. And <a href="../ebd/ebd392.htm#003">Ziba</a> said, I humbly beseech thee that I may find <a href="../ebd/ebd154.htm#000">grace</a> in thy sight, my lord, O <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And when <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> came to <a href="../ebd/ebd041.htm#009">Bahurim</a>, behold, thence came out a man of the family of the house of <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#002">Saul</a>, whose name was <a href="../ebd/ebd338.htm#002">Shimei</a>, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd146.htm#003">Gera</a>: he came forth, and cursed still as he came.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And he cast stones at <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a>, and at all the servants of <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a>: and all the people and all the mighty men were on his right hand and on his left.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And thus said <a href="../ebd/ebd338.htm#002">Shimei</a> when he cursed, Come out, come out, thou bloody man, and thou man of <a href="../ebd/ebd051.htm#007">Belial</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> The LORD hath returned upon thee all the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> of the house of <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#002">Saul</a>, in whose stead thou hast reigned; and the LORD hath delivered the kingdom into the hand of <a href="../ebd/ebd005.htm#008">Absalom</a> thy son: and, behold, thou art taken in thy mischief, because thou art a bloody man.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> Then said <a href="../ebd/ebd004.htm#005">Abishai</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd391.htm#009">Zeruiah</a> unto the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a>, Why should this dead dog <a href="../ebd/ebd094.htm#003">curse</a> my lord the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a>? let me go over, I pray thee, and take off his head.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> said, What have I to do with you, ye sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd391.htm#009">Zeruiah</a>?  so let him <a href="../ebd/ebd094.htm#003">curse</a>, because the LORD hath said unto him, <a href="../ebd/ebd094.htm#003">Curse</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a>. Who shall then say, Wherefore hast thou done so?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> said to <a href="../ebd/ebd004.htm#005">Abishai</a>, and to all his servants, Behold, my son, which came forth of my <a href="../ebd/ebd063.htm#004">bowels</a>, seeketh my life: how much more now may this Benjamite do it? let him alone, and let him <a href="../ebd/ebd094.htm#003">curse</a>; for the LORD hath bidden him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> It may be that the LORD will look on mine affliction, and that the LORD will requite me good for his cursing this day.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> And as <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> and his men went by the way, <a href="../ebd/ebd338.htm#002">Shimei</a> went along on the hill's side over against him, and cursed as he went, and threw stones at him, and cast <a href="../ebd/ebd109.htm#008">dust</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a>, and all the people that were with him, came weary, and refreshed themselves there.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd005.htm#008">Absalom</a>, and all the people the men of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, came to <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd015.htm#000">Ahithophel</a> with him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> And it came to pass, when <a href="../ebd/ebd185.htm#003">Hushai</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd029.htm#006">Archite</a>, David's friend, was come unto <a href="../ebd/ebd005.htm#008">Absalom</a>, that <a href="../ebd/ebd185.htm#003">Hushai</a> said unto <a href="../ebd/ebd005.htm#008">Absalom</a>, God save the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a>, God save the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd005.htm#008">Absalom</a> said to <a href="../ebd/ebd185.htm#003">Hushai</a>, Is this thy kindness to thy friend? why wentest thou not with thy friend?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd185.htm#003">Hushai</a> said unto <a href="../ebd/ebd005.htm#008">Absalom</a>, Nay; but whom the LORD, and this people, and all the men of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, choose, his will I be, and with him will I abide.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> And again, whom should I serve? should I not serve in the presence of his son? as I have served in thy father's presence, so will I be in thy presence.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> Then said <a href="../ebd/ebd005.htm#008">Absalom</a> to <a href="../ebd/ebd015.htm#000">Ahithophel</a>, Give counsel among you what we shall do.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd015.htm#000">Ahithophel</a> said unto <a href="../ebd/ebd005.htm#008">Absalom</a>, Go in unto thy father's concubines, which he hath left to keep the house; and all <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> shall hear that thou art abhorred of thy father: then shall the hands of all that are with thee be strong.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> So they spread <a href="../ebd/ebd005.htm#008">Absalom</a> a <a href="../ebd/ebd361.htm#009">tent</a> upon the top of the house; and <a href="../ebd/ebd005.htm#008">Absalom</a> went in unto his father's concubines in the sight of all <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> And the counsel of <a href="../ebd/ebd015.htm#000">Ahithophel</a>, which he counselled in those days, was as if a man had enquired at the <a href="../ebd/ebd280.htm#000">oracle</a> of God: so was all the counsel of <a href="../ebd/ebd015.htm#000">Ahithophel</a> both with <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> and with <a href="../ebd/ebd005.htm#008">Absalom</a>.<br>
 </p>
