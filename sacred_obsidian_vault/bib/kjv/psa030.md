 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 30</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> I will extol thee, O LORD; for thou hast lifted me up, and hast not made my foes to rejoice over me.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> O LORD my God, I cried unto thee, and thou hast healed me.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> O LORD, thou hast brought up my soul from the <a href="../ebd/ebd154.htm#008">grave</a>: thou hast kept me alive, that I should not go down to the <a href="../ebd/ebd296.htm#007">pit</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Sing unto the LORD, O ye saints of his, and give thanks at the remembrance of his <a href="../ebd/ebd180.htm#006">holiness</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> For his <a href="../ebd/ebd024.htm#002">anger</a> endureth but a moment; in his favour is life: weeping may endure for a night, but joy cometh in the morning.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And in my prosperity I said, I shall never be moved.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> LORD, by thy favour thou hast made my mountain to stand strong: thou didst hide thy face, and I was troubled.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> I cried to thee, O LORD; and unto the LORD I made supplication.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> What profit is there in my <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a>, when I go down to the <a href="../ebd/ebd296.htm#007">pit</a>? Shall the <a href="../ebd/ebd109.htm#008">dust</a> praise thee? shall it declare thy truth?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> Hear, O LORD, and have <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> upon me: LORD, be thou my helper.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> Thou hast turned for me my mourning into dancing: thou hast put off my <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#001">sackcloth</a>, and girded me with gladness;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> To the end that my <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a> may sing praise to thee, and not be silent. O LORD my God, I will give thanks unto thee for ever.<br>
 </p>
