 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Numbers Chapter 15</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> And the LORD spake unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Speak unto the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, and say unto them, When ye be come into the land of your habitations, which I give unto you,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And will make an <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> by fire unto the LORD, a burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, or a <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#002">sacrifice</a> in performing a vow, or in a freewill <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, or in your solemn feasts, to make a sweet savour unto the LORD, of the <a href="../ebd/ebd174.htm#009">herd</a> or of the flock:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Then shall he that offereth his <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> unto the LORD bring a meat <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> of a tenth deal of <a href="../ebd/ebd136.htm#001">flour</a> mingled with the fourth part of an hin of oil.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And the fourth part of an hin of <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#000">wine</a> for a <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> shalt thou prepare with the burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> or <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#002">sacrifice</a>, for one <a href="../ebd/ebd223.htm#003">lamb</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Or for a <a href="../ebd/ebd306.htm#003">ram</a>, thou shalt prepare for a meat <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> two tenth deals of <a href="../ebd/ebd136.htm#001">flour</a> mingled with the third part of an hin of oil.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And for a <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> thou shalt offer the third part of an hin of <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#000">wine</a>, for a sweet savour unto the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And when thou preparest a <a href="../ebd/ebd066.htm#003">bullock</a> for a burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, or for a <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#002">sacrifice</a> in performing a vow, or peace offerings unto the LORD:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> Then shall he bring with a <a href="../ebd/ebd066.htm#003">bullock</a> a meat <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> of three tenth deals of <a href="../ebd/ebd136.htm#001">flour</a> mingled with half an hin of oil.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And thou shalt bring for a <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> half an hin of <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#000">wine</a>, for an <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> made by fire, of a sweet savour unto the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> Thus shall it be done for one <a href="../ebd/ebd066.htm#003">bullock</a>, or for one <a href="../ebd/ebd306.htm#003">ram</a>, or for a <a href="../ebd/ebd223.htm#003">lamb</a>, or a kid.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> According to the number that ye shall prepare, so shall ye do to every one according to their number.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> All that are born of the country shall do these things after this manner, in <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> an <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> made by fire, of a sweet savour unto the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> And if a <a href="../ebd/ebd351.htm#009">stranger</a> sojourn with you, or whosoever be among you in your generations, and will offer an <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> made by fire, of a sweet savour unto the LORD; as ye do, so he shall do.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> One ordinance shall be both for you of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>, and also for the <a href="../ebd/ebd351.htm#009">stranger</a> that sojourneth with you, an ordinance for ever in your generations: as ye are, so shall the <a href="../ebd/ebd351.htm#009">stranger</a> be before the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> One law and one manner shall be for you, and for the <a href="../ebd/ebd351.htm#009">stranger</a> that sojourneth with you.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> And the LORD spake unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> Speak unto the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, and say unto them, When ye come into the land whither I bring you,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> Then it shall be, that, when ye eat of the <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#006">bread</a> of the land, ye shall offer up an heave <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> unto the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> Ye shall offer up a <a href="../ebd/ebd068.htm#009">cake</a> of the first of your <a href="../ebd/ebd106.htm#006">dough</a> for an heave <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>: as ye do the heave <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> of the threshingfloor, so shall ye heave it.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> Of the first of your <a href="../ebd/ebd106.htm#006">dough</a> ye shall give unto the LORD an heave <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> in your generations.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> And if ye have erred, and not observed all these commandments, which the LORD hath spoken unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> Even all that the LORD hath commanded you by the hand of <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, from the day that the LORD commanded <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, and henceforward among your generations;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> Then it shall be, if ought be committed by ignorance without the knowledge of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>, that all the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> shall offer one young <a href="../ebd/ebd066.htm#003">bullock</a> for a burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, for a sweet savour unto the LORD, with his meat <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, and his <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, according to the manner, and one kid of the goats for a <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> shall make an <a href="../ebd/ebd036.htm#004">atonement</a> for all the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, and it shall be forgiven them; for it is ignorance: and they shall bring their <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, a <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#002">sacrifice</a> made by fire unto the LORD, and their <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> before the LORD, for their ignorance:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> And it shall be forgiven all the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd351.htm#009">stranger</a> that sojourneth among them; seeing all the people were in ignorance.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> And if any soul <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> through ignorance, then he shall bring a she <a href="../ebd/ebd151.htm#001">goat</a> of the first year for a <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="028">28</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> shall make an <a href="../ebd/ebd036.htm#004">atonement</a> for the soul that sinneth ignorantly, when he sinneth by ignorance before the LORD, to make an <a href="../ebd/ebd036.htm#004">atonement</a> for him; and it shall be forgiven him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="029">29</a> Ye shall have one law for him that sinneth through ignorance, both for him that is born among the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, and for the <a href="../ebd/ebd351.htm#009">stranger</a> that sojourneth among them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="030">30</a> But the soul that doeth ought presumptuously, whether he be born in the land, or a <a href="../ebd/ebd351.htm#009">stranger</a>, the same reproacheth the LORD; and that soul shall be cut off from among his people.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="031">31</a> Because he hath despised the word of the LORD, and hath broken his commandment, that soul shall utterly be cut off; his iniquity shall be upon him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="032">32</a> And while the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> were in the <a href="../ebd/ebd381.htm#005">wilderness</a>, they found a man that gathered sticks upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd317.htm#003">sabbath</a> day.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="033">33</a> And they that found him gathering sticks brought him unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a>, and unto all the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="034">34</a> And they put him in <a href="../ebd/ebd379.htm#001">ward</a>, because it was not declared what should be done to him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="035">35</a> And the LORD said unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, The man shall be surely put to death: all the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> shall <a href="../ebd/ebd351.htm#005">stone</a> him with stones without the <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#002">camp</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="036">36</a> And all the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> brought him without the <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#002">camp</a>, and stoned him with stones, and he died; as the LORD commanded <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="037">37</a> And the LORD spake unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="038">38</a> Speak unto the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, and bid them that they make them fringes in the borders of their garments throughout their generations, and that they put upon the fringe of the borders a ribband of <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#003">blue</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="039">39</a> And it shall be unto you for a fringe, that ye may look upon it, and remember all the commandments of the LORD, and do them; and that ye seek not after your own heart and your own eyes, after which ye use to go a whoring:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="040">40</a> That ye may remember, and do all my commandments, and be holy unto your God.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="041">41</a> I am the LORD your God, which brought you out of the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a>, to be your God: I am the LORD your God.<br>
 </p>
