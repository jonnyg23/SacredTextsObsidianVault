 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Ezekiel Chapter 30</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> The word of the LORD came again unto me, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Son of man, prophesy and say, Thus saith the Lord GOD; Howl ye, Woe worth the day!<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> For the day is near, even the day of the LORD is near, a cloudy day; it shall be the time of the <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#005">heathen</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd354.htm#003">sword</a> shall come upon <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a>, and great pain shall be in <a href="../ebd/ebd126.htm#005">Ethiopia</a>, when the slain shall fall in <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a>, and they shall take away her multitude, and her foundations shall be broken down.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd126.htm#005">Ethiopia</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd228.htm#006">Libya</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd234.htm#000">Lydia</a>, and all the mingled people, and <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#008">Chub</a>, and the men of the land that is in <a href="../ebd/ebd225.htm#005">league</a>, shall fall with them by the <a href="../ebd/ebd354.htm#003">sword</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Thus saith the LORD; They also that uphold <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a> shall fall; and the pride of her power shall come down: from the tower of <a href="../ebd/ebd354.htm#008">Syene</a> shall they fall in it by the <a href="../ebd/ebd354.htm#003">sword</a>, saith the Lord GOD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And they shall be desolate in the midst of the countries that are desolate, and her cities shall be in the midst of the cities that are wasted.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And they shall know that I am the LORD, when I have set a fire in <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a>, and when all her helpers shall be destroyed.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> In that day shall messengers go forth from me in <a href="../ebd/ebd339.htm#003">ships</a> to make the careless Ethiopians afraid, and great pain shall come upon them, as in the day of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a>: for, lo, it cometh.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> Thus saith the Lord GOD; I will also make the multitude of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a> to cease by the hand of <a href="../ebd/ebd268.htm#006">Nebuchadrezzar</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd041.htm#001">Babylon</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> He and his people with him, the terrible of the nations, shall be brought to destroy the land: and they shall draw their swords against <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a>, and fill the land with the slain.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And I will make the rivers dry, and sell the land into the hand of the wicked: and I will make the land waste, and all that is therein, by the hand of strangers: I the LORD have spoken it.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> Thus saith the Lord GOD; I will also destroy the idols, and I will cause their images to cease out of <a href="../ebd/ebd274.htm#009">Noph</a>; and there shall be no more a <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#004">prince</a> of the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a>: and I will put a fear in the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> And I will make <a href="../ebd/ebd286.htm#007">Pathros</a> desolate, and will set fire in <a href="../ebd/ebd395.htm#003">Zoan</a>, and will execute judgments in No.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And I will pour my <a href="../ebd/ebd140.htm#002">fury</a> upon <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">Sin</a>, the strength of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a>; and I will cut off the multitude of No.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> And I will set fire in <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a>: <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">Sin</a> shall have great pain, and No shall be <a href="../ebd/ebd310.htm#007">rent</a> asunder, and <a href="../ebd/ebd274.htm#009">Noph</a> shall have distresses daily.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> The young men of <a href="../ebd/ebd036.htm#009">Aven</a> and of Pibeseth shall fall by the <a href="../ebd/ebd354.htm#003">sword</a>: and these cities shall go into <a href="../ebd/ebd072.htm#002">captivity</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> At Tehaphnehes also the day shall be darkened, when I shall break there the yokes of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a>: and the pomp of her strength shall cease in her: as for her, a <a href="../ebd/ebd085.htm#001">cloud</a> shall cover her, and her daughters shall go into <a href="../ebd/ebd072.htm#002">captivity</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> Thus will I execute judgments in <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a>: and they shall know that I am the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> And it came to pass in the eleventh year, in the first month, in the seventh day of the month, that the word of the LORD came unto me, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> Son of man, I have broken the arm of <a href="../ebd/ebd292.htm#005">Pharaoh</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a>; and, lo, it shall not be bound up to be healed, to put a roller to bind it, to make it strong to hold the <a href="../ebd/ebd354.htm#003">sword</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> Therefore thus saith the Lord GOD; Behold, I am against <a href="../ebd/ebd292.htm#005">Pharaoh</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a>, and will break his arms, the strong, and that which was broken; and I will cause the <a href="../ebd/ebd354.htm#003">sword</a> to fall out of his hand.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> And I will scatter the Egyptians among the nations, and will disperse them through the countries.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> And I will strengthen the arms of the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd041.htm#001">Babylon</a>, and put my <a href="../ebd/ebd354.htm#003">sword</a> in his hand: but I will break Pharaoh's arms, and he shall groan before him with the groanings of a deadly wounded man.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> But I will strengthen the arms of the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd041.htm#001">Babylon</a>, and the arms of <a href="../ebd/ebd292.htm#005">Pharaoh</a> shall fall down; and they shall know that I am the LORD, when I shall put my <a href="../ebd/ebd354.htm#003">sword</a> into the hand of the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd041.htm#001">Babylon</a>, and he shall stretch it out upon the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> And I will scatter the Egyptians among the nations, and disperse them among the countries; and they shall know that I am the LORD.<br>
 </p>
