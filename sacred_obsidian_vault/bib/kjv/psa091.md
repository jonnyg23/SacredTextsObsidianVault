 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 91</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> He that dwelleth in the secret place of the most High shall abide under the <a href="../ebd/ebd330.htm#002">shadow</a> of the Almighty.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> I will say of the LORD, He is my refuge and my fortress: my God; in him will I trust.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Surely he shall deliver thee from the <a href="../ebd/ebd346.htm#009">snare</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd138.htm#002">fowler</a>, and from the noisome pestilence.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> He shall cover thee with his feathers, and under his wings shalt thou trust: his truth shall be thy <a href="../ebd/ebd337.htm#001">shield</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd066.htm#000">buckler</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Thou shalt not be afraid for the terror by night; nor for the arrow that flieth by day;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Nor for the pestilence that walketh in <a href="../ebd/ebd097.htm#008">darkness</a>; nor for the <a href="../ebd/ebd102.htm#004">destruction</a> that wasteth at noonday.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> A thousand shall fall at thy side, and ten thousand at thy right hand; but it shall not come nigh thee.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> Only with thine eyes shalt thou behold and see the reward of the wicked.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> Because thou hast made the LORD, which is my refuge, even the most High, thy <a href="../ebd/ebd156.htm#007">habitation</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> There shall no evil befall thee, neither shall any <a href="../ebd/ebd297.htm#001">plague</a> come nigh thy dwelling.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> For he shall give his angels charge over thee, to keep thee in all thy ways.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> They shall bear thee up in their hands, lest thou dash thy foot against a <a href="../ebd/ebd351.htm#005">stone</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> Thou shalt tread upon the lion and <a href="../ebd/ebd008.htm#005">adder</a>: the young lion and the <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#000">dragon</a> shalt thou trample under feet.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> Because he hath set his love upon me, therefore will I deliver him: I will set him on high, because he hath known my name.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> He shall call upon me, and I will answer him: I will be with him in trouble; I will deliver him, and honour him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> With long life will I satisfy him, and shew him my <a href="../ebd/ebd320.htm#003">salvation</a>.<br>
 </p>
