 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: 1 Peter Chapter 5</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> The elders which are among you I exhort, who am also an <a href="../ebd/ebd114.htm#008">elder</a>, and a <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#008">witness</a> of the sufferings of <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a>, and also a partaker of the <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a> that shall be revealed:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Feed the flock of God which is among you, taking the oversight thereof, not by constraint, but willingly; not for filthy <a href="../ebd/ebd232.htm#008">lucre</a>, but of a ready mind;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Neither as being lords over God's heritage, but being ensamples to the flock.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And when the chief <a href="../ebd/ebd335.htm#006">Shepherd</a> shall appear, ye shall receive a <a href="../ebd/ebd093.htm#001">crown</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a> that fadeth not away.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Likewise, ye younger, submit yourselves unto the <a href="../ebd/ebd114.htm#008">elder</a>. Yea, all of you be subject one to another, and be clothed with <a href="../ebd/ebd184.htm#007">humility</a>: for God resisteth the proud, and giveth <a href="../ebd/ebd154.htm#000">grace</a> to the humble.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Humble yourselves therefore under the mighty hand of God, that he may exalt you in due time:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Casting all your care upon him; for he careth for you.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> Be sober, be vigilant; because your <a href="../ebd/ebd011.htm#001">adversary</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd102.htm#007">devil</a>, as a roaring lion, walketh about, seeking whom he may devour:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> Whom resist stedfast in the faith, knowing that the same <a href="../ebd/ebd011.htm#006">afflictions</a> are accomplished in your brethren that are in the world.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> But the God of all <a href="../ebd/ebd154.htm#000">grace</a>, who hath called us unto his eternal <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a> by <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a>, after that ye have suffered a while, make you perfect, stablish, strengthen, settle you.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> To him be <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a> and dominion for ever and ever. <a href="../ebd/ebd020.htm#001">Amen</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> By Silvanus, a <a href="../ebd/ebd130.htm#005">faithful</a> brother unto you, as I suppose, I have written briefly, exhorting, and testifying that this is the true <a href="../ebd/ebd154.htm#000">grace</a> of God wherein ye stand.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> The church that is at <a href="../ebd/ebd041.htm#001">Babylon</a>, elected together with you, saluteth you; and so doth <a href="../ebd/ebd241.htm#008">Marcus</a> my son.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> Greet ye one another with a <a href="../ebd/ebd221.htm#000">kiss</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd077.htm#009">charity</a>. Peace be with you all that are in <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a>. <a href="../ebd/ebd020.htm#001">Amen</a>.<br>
 </p>
