 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Nehemiah Chapter 11</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> And the rulers of the people dwelt at <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>: the rest of the people also cast lots, to bring one of ten to dwell in <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a> the holy city, and nine parts to dwell in other cities.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> And the people blessed all the men, that willingly offered themselves to dwell at <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Now these are the chief of the province that dwelt in <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>: but in the cities of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a> dwelt every one in his possession in their cities, to wit, <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, the priests, and the Levites, and the Nethinims, and the children of Solomon's servants.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And at <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a> dwelt certain of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>, and of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd052.htm#008">Benjamin</a>. Of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>; Athaiah the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd376.htm#004">Uzziah</a>, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd389.htm#006">Zechariah</a>, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd019.htm#002">Amariah</a>, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd335.htm#005">Shephatiah</a>, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd237.htm#001">Mahalaleel</a>, of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd289.htm#009">Perez</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd234.htm#007">Maaseiah</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd046.htm#001">Baruch</a>, the son of Colhozeh, the son of Hazaiah, the son of Adaiah, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd209.htm#008">Joiarib</a>, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd389.htm#006">Zechariah</a>, the son of Shiloni.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> All the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd289.htm#009">Perez</a> that dwelt at <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a> were four hundred threescore and eight valiant men.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And these are the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd052.htm#008">Benjamin</a>; <a href="../ebd/ebd319.htm#004">Sallu</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd251.htm#001">Meshullam</a>, the son of Joed, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd288.htm#001">Pedaiah</a>, the son of Kolaiah, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd234.htm#007">Maaseiah</a>, the son of Ithiel, the son of Jesaiah.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And after him Gabbai, <a href="../ebd/ebd319.htm#003">Sallai</a>, nine hundred twenty and eight.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd208.htm#005">Joel</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd392.htm#007">Zichri</a> was their overseer: and <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a> the son of Senuah was second over the city.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> Of the priests: <a href="../ebd/ebd199.htm#006">Jedaiah</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd209.htm#008">Joiarib</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd194.htm#004">Jachin</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd328.htm#003">Seraiah</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd178.htm#006">Hilkiah</a>, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd251.htm#001">Meshullam</a>, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd386.htm#008">Zadok</a>, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd248.htm#009">Meraioth</a>, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd015.htm#001">Ahitub</a>, was the ruler of the house of God.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And their brethren that did the work of the house were eight hundred twenty and two: and Adaiah the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#001">Jeroham</a>, the son of Pelaliah, the son of Amzi, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd389.htm#006">Zechariah</a>, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd286.htm#002">Pashur</a>, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd239.htm#001">Malchiah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> And his brethren, chief of the fathers, two hundred forty and two: and <a href="../ebd/ebd019.htm#005">Amashai</a> the son of Azareel, the son of Ahasai, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd251.htm#000">Meshillemoth</a>, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd187.htm#008">Immer</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> And their brethren, mighty men of valour, an hundred twenty and eight: and their overseer was <a href="../ebd/ebd385.htm#009">Zabdiel</a>, the son of one of the great men.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> Also of the Levites: <a href="../ebd/ebd334.htm#005">Shemaiah</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd166.htm#005">Hashub</a>, the son of Azrikam, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd166.htm#001">Hashabiah</a>, the son of Bunni;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd330.htm#000">Shabbethai</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#002">Jozabad</a>, of the chief of the Levites, had the oversight of the outward business of the house of God.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd243.htm#009">Mattaniah</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd252.htm#008">Micha</a>, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd385.htm#008">Zabdi</a>, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd033.htm#002">Asaph</a>, was the principal to begin the thanksgiving in <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#000">prayer</a>: and Bakbukiah the second among his brethren, and <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#008">Abda</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd331.htm#003">Shammua</a>, the son of Galal, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd199.htm#009">Jeduthun</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> All the Levites in the holy city were two hundred fourscore and four.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> Moreover the porters, <a href="../ebd/ebd016.htm#003">Akkub</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd358.htm#002">Talmon</a>, and their brethren that kept the gates, were an hundred seventy and two.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> And the residue of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, of the priests, and the Levites, were in all the cities of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>, every one in his inheritance.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> But the Nethinims dwelt in <a href="../ebd/ebd279.htm#006">Ophel</a>: and <a href="../ebd/ebd393.htm#002">Ziha</a> and Gispa were over the Nethinims.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> The overseer also of the Levites at <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a> was <a href="../ebd/ebd376.htm#003">Uzzi</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd043.htm#004">Bani</a>, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd166.htm#001">Hashabiah</a>, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd243.htm#009">Mattaniah</a>, the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd252.htm#008">Micha</a>. Of the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd033.htm#002">Asaph</a>, the singers were over the business of the house of God.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> For it was the king's commandment concerning them, that a certain portion should be for the singers, due for every day.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd291.htm#005">Pethahiah</a> the son of Meshezabeel, of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd391.htm#001">Zerah</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>, was at the king's hand in all matters concerning the people.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> And for the <a href="../ebd/ebd377.htm#003">villages</a>, with their fields, some of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a> dwelt at Kirjatharba, and in the <a href="../ebd/ebd377.htm#003">villages</a> thereof, and at <a href="../ebd/ebd103.htm#005">Dibon</a>, and in the <a href="../ebd/ebd377.htm#003">villages</a> thereof, and at Jekabzeel, and in the <a href="../ebd/ebd377.htm#003">villages</a> thereof,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> And at <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#003">Jeshua</a>, and at <a href="../ebd/ebd258.htm#008">Moladah</a>, and at Bethphelet,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> And at Hazarshual, and at <a href="../ebd/ebd050.htm#008">Beersheba</a>, and in the <a href="../ebd/ebd377.htm#003">villages</a> thereof,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="028">28</a> And at <a href="../ebd/ebd393.htm#003">Ziklag</a>, and at <a href="../ebd/ebd247.htm#001">Mekonah</a>, and in the <a href="../ebd/ebd377.htm#003">villages</a> thereof,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="029">29</a> And at Enrimmon, and at Zareah, and at <a href="../ebd/ebd197.htm#007">Jarmuth</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="030">30</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd387.htm#004">Zanoah</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd010.htm#007">Adullam</a>, and in their <a href="../ebd/ebd377.htm#003">villages</a>, at <a href="../ebd/ebd222.htm#009">Lachish</a>, and the fields thereof, at <a href="../ebd/ebd037.htm#008">Azekah</a>, and in the <a href="../ebd/ebd377.htm#003">villages</a> thereof. And they dwelt from <a href="../ebd/ebd050.htm#008">Beersheba</a> unto the <a href="../ebd/ebd376.htm#008">valley</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd179.htm#002">Hinnom</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="031">31</a> The children also of <a href="../ebd/ebd052.htm#008">Benjamin</a> from <a href="../ebd/ebd144.htm#004">Geba</a> dwelt at <a href="../ebd/ebd253.htm#002">Michmash</a>, and Aija, and <a href="../ebd/ebd055.htm#006">Bethel</a>, and in their <a href="../ebd/ebd377.htm#003">villages</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="032">32</a> And at <a href="../ebd/ebd023.htm#004">Anathoth</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd274.htm#003">Nob</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd023.htm#000">Ananiah</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="033">33</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd169.htm#006">Hazor</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd306.htm#005">Ramah</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd149.htm#008">Gittaim</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="034">34</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd157.htm#009">Hadid</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd388.htm#009">Zeboim</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd268.htm#002">Neballat</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="035">35</a> Lod, and <a href="../ebd/ebd279.htm#002">Ono</a>, the <a href="../ebd/ebd376.htm#008">valley</a> of craftsmen.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="036">36</a> And of the Levites were divisions in <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>, and in <a href="../ebd/ebd052.htm#008">Benjamin</a>.<br>
 </p>
