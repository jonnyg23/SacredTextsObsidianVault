 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 138</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> I will praise thee with my whole heart: before the gods will I sing praise unto thee.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> I will <a href="../ebd/ebd384.htm#002">worship</a> toward thy holy <a href="../ebd/ebd361.htm#004">temple</a>, and praise thy name for thy lovingkindness and for thy truth: for thou hast magnified thy word above all thy name.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> In the day when I cried thou answeredst me, and strengthenedst me with strength in my soul.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> All the kings of the earth shall praise thee, O LORD, when they hear the words of thy mouth.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Yea, they shall sing in the ways of the LORD: for great is the <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a> of the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Though the LORD be high, yet hath he respect unto the lowly: but the proud he knoweth afar off.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Though I walk in the midst of trouble, thou wilt revive me: thou shalt stretch forth thine hand against the wrath of mine enemies, and thy right hand shall save me.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> The LORD will perfect that which concerneth me: thy <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a>, O LORD, endureth for ever: forsake not the works of thine own hands.<br>
 </p>
