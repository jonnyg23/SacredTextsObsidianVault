 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 62</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Truly my soul waiteth upon God: from him cometh my <a href="../ebd/ebd320.htm#003">salvation</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> He only is my <a href="../ebd/ebd315.htm#001">rock</a> and my <a href="../ebd/ebd320.htm#003">salvation</a>; he is my defence; I shall not be greatly moved.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> How long will ye imagine mischief against a man? ye shall be slain all of you: as a <a href="../ebd/ebd063.htm#005">bowing</a> wall shall ye be, and as a tottering <a href="../ebd/ebd132.htm#003">fence</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> They only consult to cast him down from his excellency: they delight in lies: they <a href="../ebd/ebd060.htm#008">bless</a> with their mouth, but they <a href="../ebd/ebd094.htm#003">curse</a> inwardly. <a href="../ebd/ebd326.htm#008">Selah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> My soul, wait thou only upon God; for my expectation is from him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> He only is my <a href="../ebd/ebd315.htm#001">rock</a> and my <a href="../ebd/ebd320.htm#003">salvation</a>: he is my defence; I shall not be moved.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> In God is my <a href="../ebd/ebd320.htm#003">salvation</a> and my <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a>: the <a href="../ebd/ebd315.htm#001">rock</a> of my strength, and my refuge, is in God.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> Trust in him at all times; ye people, pour out your heart before him: God is a refuge for us. <a href="../ebd/ebd326.htm#008">Selah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> Surely men of low degree are vanity, and men of high degree are a lie: to be laid in the <a href="../ebd/ebd042.htm#007">balance</a>, they are altogether lighter than vanity.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> Trust not in oppression, and become not vain in <a href="../ebd/ebd315.htm#000">robbery</a>: if riches increase, set not your heart upon them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> God hath spoken once; twice have I heard this; that power belongeth unto God.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> Also unto thee, O Lord, belongeth <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a>: for thou renderest to every man according to his work.<br>
 </p>
