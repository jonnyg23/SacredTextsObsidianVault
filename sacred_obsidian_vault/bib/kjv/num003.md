 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Numbers Chapter 3</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> These also are the generations of <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> in the day that the LORD spake with <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> in <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#007">mount</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#009">Sinai</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> And these are the names of the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a>; <a href="../ebd/ebd264.htm#008">Nadab</a> the firstborn, and <a href="../ebd/ebd003.htm#004">Abihu</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd115.htm#000">Eleazar</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#005">Ithamar</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> These are the names of the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a>, the priests which were anointed, whom he consecrated to <a href="../ebd/ebd255.htm#007">minister</a> in the priest's office.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd264.htm#008">Nadab</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd003.htm#004">Abihu</a> died before the LORD, when they offered strange fire before the LORD, in the <a href="../ebd/ebd381.htm#005">wilderness</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#009">Sinai</a>, and they had no children: and <a href="../ebd/ebd115.htm#000">Eleazar</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#005">Ithamar</a> ministered in the priest's office in the sight of <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> their father.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And the LORD spake unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Bring the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd227.htm#006">Levi</a> near, and present them before <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a>, that they may <a href="../ebd/ebd255.htm#007">minister</a> unto him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And they shall keep his charge, and the charge of the whole <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> before the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>, to do the service of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And they shall keep all the instruments of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>, and the charge of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, to do the service of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And thou shalt give the Levites unto <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> and to his sons: they are wholly given unto him out of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And thou shalt appoint <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> and his sons, and they shall wait on their priest's office: and the <a href="../ebd/ebd351.htm#009">stranger</a> that cometh nigh shall be put to death.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And the LORD spake unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And I, behold, I have taken the Levites from among the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> instead of all the firstborn that openeth the matrix among the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>: therefore the Levites shall be mine;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> Because all the firstborn are mine; for on the day that I smote all the firstborn in the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a> I hallowed unto me all the firstborn in <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, both man and <a href="../ebd/ebd049.htm#001">beast</a>: mine shall they be: I am the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> And the LORD spake unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> in the <a href="../ebd/ebd381.htm#005">wilderness</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#009">Sinai</a>, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> Number the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd227.htm#006">Levi</a> after the house of their fathers, by their families: every male from a month old and upward shalt thou number them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> numbered them according to the word of the LORD, as he was commanded.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> And these were the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd227.htm#006">Levi</a> by their names; <a href="../ebd/ebd146.htm#009">Gershon</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd222.htm#001">Kohath</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#000">Merari</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> And these are the names of the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd146.htm#009">Gershon</a> by their families; <a href="../ebd/ebd228.htm#005">Libni</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd338.htm#002">Shimei</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> And the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd222.htm#001">Kohath</a> by their families; <a href="../ebd/ebd022.htm#001">Amram</a>, and Izehar, <a href="../ebd/ebd171.htm#004">Hebron</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd376.htm#005">Uzziel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> And the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#000">Merari</a> by their families; Mahli, and <a href="../ebd/ebd262.htm#005">Mushi</a>. These are the families of the Levites according to the house of their fathers.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> Of <a href="../ebd/ebd146.htm#009">Gershon</a> was the family of the Libnites, and the family of the Shimites: these are the families of the Gershonites.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> Those that were numbered of them, according to the number of all the males, from a month old and upward, even those that were numbered of them were <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> thousand and five hundred.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> The families of the Gershonites shall <a href="../ebd/ebd296.htm#008">pitch</a> behind the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd380.htm#008">westward</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> And the chief of the house of the father of the Gershonites shall be Eliasaph the son of Lael.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> And the charge of the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd146.htm#009">Gershon</a> in the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> shall be the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd361.htm#009">tent</a>, the covering thereof, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd163.htm#001">hanging</a> for the door of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> And the hangings of the court, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd094.htm#004">curtain</a> for the door of the court, which is by the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a>, and by the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> round about, and the cords of it for all the service thereof.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> And of <a href="../ebd/ebd222.htm#001">Kohath</a> was the family of the Amramites, and the family of the Izeharites, and the family of the Hebronites, and the family of the Uzzielites: these are the families of the <a href="../ebd/ebd222.htm#002">Kohathites</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="028">28</a> In the number of all the males, from a month old and upward, were eight thousand and six hundred, keeping the charge of the <a href="../ebd/ebd321.htm#005">sanctuary</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="029">29</a> The families of the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd222.htm#001">Kohath</a> shall <a href="../ebd/ebd296.htm#008">pitch</a> on the side of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> southward.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="030">30</a> And the chief of the house of the father of the families of the <a href="../ebd/ebd222.htm#002">Kohathites</a> shall be Elizaphan the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd376.htm#005">Uzziel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="031">31</a> And their charge shall be the <a href="../ebd/ebd031.htm#000">ark</a>, and the table, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd071.htm#001">candlestick</a>, and the altars, and the vessels of the <a href="../ebd/ebd321.htm#005">sanctuary</a> wherewith they <a href="../ebd/ebd255.htm#007">minister</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd163.htm#001">hanging</a>, and all the service thereof.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="032">32</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd115.htm#000">Eleazar</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> shall be chief over the chief of the Levites, and have the oversight of them that keep the charge of the <a href="../ebd/ebd321.htm#005">sanctuary</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="033">33</a> Of <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#000">Merari</a> was the family of the Mahlites, and the family of the Mushites: these are the families of <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#000">Merari</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="034">34</a> And those that were numbered of them, according to the number of all the males, from a month old and upward, were six thousand and two hundred.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="035">35</a> And the chief of the house of the father of the families of <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#000">Merari</a> was <a href="../ebd/ebd396.htm#006">Zuriel</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd003.htm#003">Abihail</a>: these shall <a href="../ebd/ebd296.htm#008">pitch</a> on the side of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd275.htm#002">northward</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="036">36</a> And under the custody and charge of the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#000">Merari</a> shall be the boards of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a>, and the bars thereof, and the pillars thereof, and the sockets thereof, and all the vessels thereof, and all that serveth thereto,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="037">37</a> And the pillars of the court round about, and their sockets, and their pins, and their cords.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="038">38</a> But those that <a href="../ebd/ebd120.htm#003">encamp</a> before the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> toward the <a href="../ebd/ebd111.htm#000">east</a>, even before the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> eastward, shall be <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> and his sons, keeping the charge of the <a href="../ebd/ebd321.htm#005">sanctuary</a> for the charge of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>; and the <a href="../ebd/ebd351.htm#009">stranger</a> that cometh nigh shall be put to death.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="039">39</a> All that were numbered of the Levites, which <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> numbered at the commandment of the LORD, throughout their families, all the males from a month old and upward, were twenty and two thousand.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="040">40</a> And the LORD said unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, Number all the firstborn of the males of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> from a month old and upward, and take the number of their names.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="041">41</a> And thou shalt take the Levites for me (I am the LORD) instead of all the firstborn among the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>; and the <a href="../ebd/ebd074.htm#002">cattle</a> of the Levites instead of all the firstlings among the <a href="../ebd/ebd074.htm#002">cattle</a> of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="042">42</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> numbered, as the LORD commanded him, all the firstborn among the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="043">43</a> And all the firstborn males by the number of names, from a month old and upward, of those that were numbered of them, were twenty and two thousand two hundred and threescore and thirteen.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="044">44</a> And the LORD spake unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="045">45</a> Take the Levites instead of all the firstborn among the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd074.htm#002">cattle</a> of the Levites instead of their <a href="../ebd/ebd074.htm#002">cattle</a>; and the Levites shall be mine: I am the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="046">46</a> And for those that are to be redeemed of the two hundred and threescore and thirteen of the firstborn of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, which are more than the Levites;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="047">47</a> Thou shalt even take five shekels apiece by the poll, after the <a href="../ebd/ebd333.htm#009">shekel</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd321.htm#005">sanctuary</a> shalt thou take them: (the <a href="../ebd/ebd333.htm#009">shekel</a> is twenty gerahs:)<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="048">48</a> And thou shalt give the money, wherewith the odd number of them is to be redeemed, unto <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> and to his sons.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="049">49</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> took the <a href="../ebd/ebd308.htm#009">redemption</a> money of them that were over and above them that were redeemed by the Levites:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="050">50</a> Of the firstborn of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> took he the money; a thousand three hundred and threescore and five shekels, after the <a href="../ebd/ebd333.htm#009">shekel</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd321.htm#005">sanctuary</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="051">51</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> gave the money of them that were redeemed unto <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> and to his sons, according to the word of the LORD, as the LORD commanded <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>.<br>
 </p>
