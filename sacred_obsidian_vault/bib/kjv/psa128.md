 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 128</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Blessed is every one that feareth the LORD; that walketh in his ways.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> For thou shalt eat the labour of thine hands: happy shalt thou be, and it shall be well with thee.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Thy wife shall be as a fruitful <a href="../ebd/ebd377.htm#004">vine</a> by the sides of thine house: thy children like <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#009">olive</a> plants round about thy table.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Behold, that thus shall the man be blessed that feareth the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> The LORD shall <a href="../ebd/ebd060.htm#008">bless</a> thee out of <a href="../ebd/ebd394.htm#002">Zion</a>: and thou shalt see the good of <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a> all the days of thy life.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Yea, thou shalt see thy children's children, and peace upon <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>.<br>
 </p>
