 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 23</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> The LORD is my <a href="../ebd/ebd335.htm#006">shepherd</a>; I shall not want.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> He maketh me to lie down in green pastures: he leadeth me beside the still waters.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> He restoreth my soul: he leadeth me in the paths of <a href="../ebd/ebd313.htm#004">righteousness</a> for his name's sake.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Yea, though I walk through the <a href="../ebd/ebd376.htm#008">valley</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd330.htm#002">shadow</a> of death, I will fear no evil: for thou art with me; thy rod and thy staff they comfort me.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Thou preparest a table before me in the presence of mine enemies: thou anointest my head with oil; my <a href="../ebd/ebd094.htm#000">cup</a> runneth over.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Surely <a href="../ebd/ebd153.htm#000">goodness</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> shall follow me all the days of my life: and I will dwell in the house of the LORD for ever.<br>
 </p>
