 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Numbers Chapter 20</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Then came the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, even the whole <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>, into the <a href="../ebd/ebd102.htm#000">desert</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd394.htm#000">Zin</a> in the first month: and the people abode in <a href="../ebd/ebd215.htm#003">Kadesh</a>; and <a href="../ebd/ebd256.htm#003">Miriam</a> died there, and was buried there.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> And there was no water for the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>: and they gathered themselves together against <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> and against <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And the people chode with <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, and spake, saying, Would God that we had died when our brethren died before the LORD!<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And why have ye brought up the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> of the LORD into this <a href="../ebd/ebd381.htm#005">wilderness</a>, that we and our <a href="../ebd/ebd074.htm#002">cattle</a> should die there?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And wherefore have ye made us to come up out of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a>, to bring us in unto this evil place? it is no place of seed, or of figs, or of vines, or of pomegranates; neither is there any water to <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> went from the presence of the assembly unto the door of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>, and they fell upon their faces: and the <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a> of the LORD appeared unto them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And the LORD spake unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> Take the rod, and gather thou the assembly together, thou, and <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> thy brother, and speak ye unto the <a href="../ebd/ebd315.htm#001">rock</a> before their eyes; and it shall give forth his water, and thou shalt bring forth to them water out of the <a href="../ebd/ebd315.htm#001">rock</a>: so thou shalt give the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> and their beasts <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> took the rod from before the LORD, as he commanded him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> gathered the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> together before the <a href="../ebd/ebd315.htm#001">rock</a>, and he said unto them, Hear now, ye rebels; must we fetch you water out of this <a href="../ebd/ebd315.htm#001">rock</a>?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> lifted up his hand, and with his rod he smote the <a href="../ebd/ebd315.htm#001">rock</a> twice: and the water came out abundantly, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> drank, and their beasts also.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And the LORD spake unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a>, Because ye believed me not, to sanctify me in the eyes of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, therefore ye shall not bring this <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> into the land which I have given them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> This is the water of <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#009">Meribah</a>; because the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> strove with the LORD, and he was sanctified in them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> sent messengers from <a href="../ebd/ebd215.htm#003">Kadesh</a> unto the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#001">Edom</a>, Thus saith thy brother <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, Thou knowest all the travail that hath befallen us:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> How our fathers went down into <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a>, and we have dwelt in <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a> a long time; and the Egyptians vexed us, and our fathers:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> And when we cried unto the LORD, he heard our voice, and sent an <a href="../ebd/ebd024.htm#001">angel</a>, and hath brought us forth out of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a>: and, behold, we are in <a href="../ebd/ebd215.htm#003">Kadesh</a>, a city in the uttermost of thy border:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> Let us pass, I pray thee, through thy country: we will not pass through the fields, or through the vineyards, neither will we <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a> of the water of the wells: we will go by the king's high way, we will not turn to the right hand nor to the left, until we have passed thy borders.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#001">Edom</a> said unto him, Thou shalt not pass by me, lest I come out against thee with the <a href="../ebd/ebd354.htm#003">sword</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> And the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> said unto him, We will go by the high way: and if I and my <a href="../ebd/ebd074.htm#002">cattle</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a> of thy water, then I will pay for it: I will only, without doing anything else, go through on my feet.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> And he said, Thou shalt not go through. And <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#001">Edom</a> came out against him with much people, and with a strong hand.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> Thus <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#001">Edom</a> refused to give <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd286.htm#003">passage</a> through his border: wherefore <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> turned away from him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> And the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, even the whole <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>, journeyed from <a href="../ebd/ebd215.htm#003">Kadesh</a>, and came unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#007">mount</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd181.htm#008">Hor</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> And the LORD spake unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> in <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#007">mount</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd181.htm#008">Hor</a>, by the coast of the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#001">Edom</a>, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> shall be gathered unto his people: for he shall not enter into the land which I have given unto the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, because ye rebelled against my word at the water of <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#009">Meribah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> Take <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd115.htm#000">Eleazar</a> his son, and bring them up unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#007">mount</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd181.htm#008">Hor</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> And strip <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> of his garments, and put them upon <a href="../ebd/ebd115.htm#000">Eleazar</a> his son: and <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> shall be gathered unto his people, and shall die there.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> did as the LORD commanded: and they went up into <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#007">mount</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd181.htm#008">Hor</a> in the sight of all the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="028">28</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> stripped <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> of his garments, and put them upon <a href="../ebd/ebd115.htm#000">Eleazar</a> his son; and <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> died there in the top of the <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#007">mount</a>: and <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd115.htm#000">Eleazar</a> came down from the <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#007">mount</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="029">29</a> And when all the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> saw that <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> was dead, they mourned for <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> thirty days, even all the house of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>.<br>
 </p>
