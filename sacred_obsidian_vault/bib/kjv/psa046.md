 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 46</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> God is our refuge and strength, a very present help in trouble.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Therefore will not we fear, though the earth be removed, and though the mountains be carried into the midst of the sea;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Though the waters thereof roar and be troubled, though the mountains shake with the <a href="../ebd/ebd354.htm#001">swelling</a> thereof. <a href="../ebd/ebd326.htm#008">Selah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> There is a river, the streams whereof shall make glad the city of God, the holy place of the tabernacles of the most High.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> God is in the midst of her; she shall not be moved: God shall help her, and that right early.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> The <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#005">heathen</a> raged, the kingdoms were moved: he uttered his voice, the earth melted.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> The LORD of hosts is with us; the God of <a href="../ebd/ebd194.htm#007">Jacob</a> is our refuge. <a href="../ebd/ebd326.htm#008">Selah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> Come, behold the works of the LORD, what desolations he hath made in the earth.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> He maketh wars to cease unto the end of the earth; he breaketh the <a href="../ebd/ebd063.htm#003">bow</a>, and cutteth the spear in sunder; he burneth the <a href="../ebd/ebd077.htm#008">chariot</a> in the fire.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> Be still, and know that I am God: I will be exalted among the <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#005">heathen</a>, I will be exalted in the earth.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> The LORD of hosts is with us; the God of <a href="../ebd/ebd194.htm#007">Jacob</a> is our refuge. <a href="../ebd/ebd326.htm#008">Selah</a>.<br>
 </p>
