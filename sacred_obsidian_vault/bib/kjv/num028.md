 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Numbers Chapter 28</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> And the LORD spake unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Command the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, and say unto them, My <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, and my <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#006">bread</a> for my sacrifices made by fire, for a sweet savour unto me, shall ye observe to offer unto me in their due season.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And thou shalt say unto them, This is the <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> made by fire which ye shall offer unto the LORD; two lambs of the first year without spot day by day, for a continual burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> The one <a href="../ebd/ebd223.htm#003">lamb</a> shalt thou offer in the morning, and the other <a href="../ebd/ebd223.htm#003">lamb</a> shalt thou offer at even;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And a tenth part of an <a href="../ebd/ebd122.htm#003">ephah</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd136.htm#001">flour</a> for a meat <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, mingled with the fourth part of an hin of beaten oil.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> It is a continual burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, which was ordained in <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#007">mount</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#009">Sinai</a> for a sweet savour, a <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#002">sacrifice</a> made by fire unto the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> thereof shall be the fourth part of an hin for the one <a href="../ebd/ebd223.htm#003">lamb</a>: in the holy place shalt thou cause the strong <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#000">wine</a> to be poured unto the LORD for a <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And the other <a href="../ebd/ebd223.htm#003">lamb</a> shalt thou offer at even: as the meat <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> of the morning, and as the <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> thereof, thou shalt offer it, a <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#002">sacrifice</a> made by fire, of a sweet savour unto the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And on the <a href="../ebd/ebd317.htm#003">sabbath</a> day two lambs of the first year without spot, and two tenth deals of <a href="../ebd/ebd136.htm#001">flour</a> for a meat <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, mingled with oil, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> thereof:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> This is the burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> of every <a href="../ebd/ebd317.htm#003">sabbath</a>, beside the continual burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, and his <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And in the beginnings of your months ye shall offer a burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> unto the LORD; two young bullocks, and one <a href="../ebd/ebd306.htm#003">ram</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> lambs of the first year without spot;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And three tenth deals of <a href="../ebd/ebd136.htm#001">flour</a> for a meat <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, mingled with oil, for one <a href="../ebd/ebd066.htm#003">bullock</a>; and two tenth deals of <a href="../ebd/ebd136.htm#001">flour</a> for a meat <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, mingled with oil, for one <a href="../ebd/ebd306.htm#003">ram</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> And a several tenth deal of <a href="../ebd/ebd136.htm#001">flour</a> mingled with oil for a meat <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> unto one <a href="../ebd/ebd223.htm#003">lamb</a>; for a burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> of a sweet savour, a <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#002">sacrifice</a> made by fire unto the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> And their <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a> offerings shall be half an hin of <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#000">wine</a> unto a <a href="../ebd/ebd066.htm#003">bullock</a>, and the third part of an hin unto a <a href="../ebd/ebd306.htm#003">ram</a>, and a fourth part of an hin unto a <a href="../ebd/ebd223.htm#003">lamb</a>: this is the burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> of every month throughout the months of the year.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And one kid of the goats for a <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> unto the LORD shall be offered, beside the continual burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, and his <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> And in the fourteenth day of the first month is the <a href="../ebd/ebd286.htm#005">passover</a> of the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> And in the fifteenth day of this month is the <a href="../ebd/ebd132.htm#000">feast</a>: <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> days shall unleavened <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#006">bread</a> be eaten.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> In the first day shall be an holy <a href="../ebd/ebd089.htm#003">convocation</a>; ye shall do no manner of servile work therein:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> But ye shall offer a <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#002">sacrifice</a> made by fire for a burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> unto the LORD; two young bullocks, and one <a href="../ebd/ebd306.htm#003">ram</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> lambs of the first year: they shall be unto you without <a href="../ebd/ebd060.htm#007">blemish</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> And their meat <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> shall be of <a href="../ebd/ebd136.htm#001">flour</a> mingled with oil: three tenth deals shall ye offer for a <a href="../ebd/ebd066.htm#003">bullock</a>, and two tenth deals for a <a href="../ebd/ebd306.htm#003">ram</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> A several tenth deal shalt thou offer for every <a href="../ebd/ebd223.htm#003">lamb</a>, throughout the <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> lambs:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> And one <a href="../ebd/ebd151.htm#001">goat</a> for a <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, to make an <a href="../ebd/ebd036.htm#004">atonement</a> for you.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> Ye shall offer these beside the burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> in the morning, which is for a continual burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> After this manner ye shall offer daily, throughout the <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> days, the meat of the <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#002">sacrifice</a> made by fire, of a sweet savour unto the LORD: it shall be offered beside the continual burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, and his <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> And on the seventh day ye shall have an holy <a href="../ebd/ebd089.htm#003">convocation</a>; ye shall do no servile work.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> Also in the day of the firstfruits, when ye bring a new meat <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> unto the LORD, after your weeks be out, ye shall have an holy <a href="../ebd/ebd089.htm#003">convocation</a>; ye shall do no servile work:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> But ye shall offer the burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> for a sweet savour unto the LORD; two young bullocks, one <a href="../ebd/ebd306.htm#003">ram</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> lambs of the first year;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="028">28</a> And their meat <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd136.htm#001">flour</a> mingled with oil, three tenth deals unto one <a href="../ebd/ebd066.htm#003">bullock</a>, two tenth deals unto one <a href="../ebd/ebd306.htm#003">ram</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="029">29</a> A several tenth deal unto one <a href="../ebd/ebd223.htm#003">lamb</a>, throughout the <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> lambs;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="030">30</a> And one kid of the goats, to make an <a href="../ebd/ebd036.htm#004">atonement</a> for you.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="031">31</a> Ye shall offer them beside the continual burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, and his meat <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, (they shall be unto you without <a href="../ebd/ebd060.htm#007">blemish</a>) and their <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a> offerings.<br>
 </p>
