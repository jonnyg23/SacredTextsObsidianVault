 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: 1 Chronicles Chapter 3</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Now these were the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a>, which were born unto him in <a href="../ebd/ebd171.htm#004">Hebron</a>; the firstborn <a href="../ebd/ebd021.htm#004">Amnon</a>, of <a href="../ebd/ebd014.htm#006">Ahinoam</a> the Jezreelitess; the second <a href="../ebd/ebd097.htm#001">Daniel</a>, of <a href="../ebd/ebd003.htm#002">Abigail</a> the Carmelitess:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> The third, <a href="../ebd/ebd005.htm#008">Absalom</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd234.htm#004">Maachah</a> the daughter of <a href="../ebd/ebd358.htm#001">Talmai</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd147.htm#001">Geshur</a>: the fourth, <a href="../ebd/ebd009.htm#006">Adonijah</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd158.htm#009">Haggith</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> The fifth, <a href="../ebd/ebd335.htm#005">Shephatiah</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd004.htm#008">Abital</a>: the sixth, Ithream by <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#006">Eglah</a> his wife.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> These six were born unto him in <a href="../ebd/ebd171.htm#004">Hebron</a>; and there he reigned <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> years and six months: and in <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a> he reigned thirty and three years.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And these were born unto him in <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>; <a href="../ebd/ebd338.htm#000">Shimea</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd340.htm#001">Shobab</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd267.htm#002">Nathan</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd347.htm#007">Solomon</a>, four, of Bathshua the daughter of <a href="../ebd/ebd020.htm#006">Ammiel</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd185.htm#008">Ibhar</a> also, and <a href="../ebd/ebd118.htm#001">Elishama</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd117.htm#007">Eliphelet</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd274.htm#008">Nogah</a>, and Nepheg, and <a href="../ebd/ebd197.htm#003">Japhia</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd118.htm#001">Elishama</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd115.htm#009">Eliada</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd117.htm#007">Eliphelet</a>, nine.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> These were all the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a>, beside the sons of the concubines, and <a href="../ebd/ebd358.htm#003">Tamar</a> their sister.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And Solomon's son was <a href="../ebd/ebd309.htm#007">Rehoboam</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd002.htm#002">Abia</a> his son, <a href="../ebd/ebd033.htm#000">Asa</a> his son, <a href="../ebd/ebd201.htm#006">Jehoshaphat</a> his son,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#003">Joram</a> his son, <a href="../ebd/ebd013.htm#006">Ahaziah</a> his son, <a href="../ebd/ebd208.htm#000">Joash</a> his son,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd019.htm#007">Amaziah</a> his son, <a href="../ebd/ebd037.htm#005">Azariah</a> his son, <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#000">Jotham</a> his son,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd013.htm#005">Ahaz</a> his son, <a href="../ebd/ebd177.htm#003">Hezekiah</a> his son, <a href="../ebd/ebd240.htm#002">Manasseh</a> his son,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd021.htm#005">Amon</a> his son, <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#008">Josiah</a> his son.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#008">Josiah</a> were, the firstborn <a href="../ebd/ebd208.htm#009">Johanan</a>, the second <a href="../ebd/ebd201.htm#001">Jehoiakim</a>, the third <a href="../ebd/ebd389.htm#008">Zedekiah</a>, the fourth <a href="../ebd/ebd330.htm#008">Shallum</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> And the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd201.htm#001">Jehoiakim</a>: Jeconiah his son, <a href="../ebd/ebd389.htm#008">Zedekiah</a> his son.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> And the sons of Jeconiah; Assir, <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#009">Salathiel</a> his son,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> Malchiram also, and <a href="../ebd/ebd288.htm#001">Pedaiah</a>, and Shenazar, Jecamiah, Hoshama, and <a href="../ebd/ebd269.htm#002">Nedabiah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> And the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd288.htm#001">Pedaiah</a> were, <a href="../ebd/ebd391.htm#008">Zerubbabel</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd338.htm#002">Shimei</a>: and the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd391.htm#008">Zerubbabel</a>; <a href="../ebd/ebd251.htm#001">Meshullam</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd162.htm#004">Hananiah</a>, and Shelomith their sister:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd166.htm#006">Hashubah</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#004">Ohel</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd053.htm#003">Berechiah</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd165.htm#009">Hasadiah</a>, Jushabhesed, five.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> And the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd162.htm#004">Hananiah</a>; <a href="../ebd/ebd288.htm#006">Pelatiah</a>, and Jesaiah: the sons of Rephaiah, the sons of Arnan, the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd276.htm#001">Obadiah</a>, the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd333.htm#002">Shechaniah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> And the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd333.htm#002">Shechaniah</a>; <a href="../ebd/ebd334.htm#005">Shemaiah</a>: and the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd334.htm#005">Shemaiah</a>; <a href="../ebd/ebd167.htm#006">Hattush</a>, and Igeal, and <a href="../ebd/ebd044.htm#009">Bariah</a>, and Neariah, and <a href="../ebd/ebd331.htm#005">Shaphat</a>, six.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> And the sons of Neariah; <a href="../ebd/ebd117.htm#003">Elioenai</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd177.htm#003">Hezekiah</a>, and Azrikam, three.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> And the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd117.htm#003">Elioenai</a> were, Hodaiah, and <a href="../ebd/ebd116.htm#003">Eliashib</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd288.htm#005">Pelaiah</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd016.htm#003">Akkub</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd208.htm#009">Johanan</a>, and Dalaiah, and Anani, <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a>.<br>
 </p>
