 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Amos Chapter 8</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Thus hath the Lord GOD shewed unto me: and behold a <a href="../ebd/ebd046.htm#009">basket</a> of summer <a href="../ebd/ebd139.htm#000">fruit</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> And he said, <a href="../ebd/ebd021.htm#007">Amos</a>, what seest thou? And I said, A <a href="../ebd/ebd046.htm#009">basket</a> of summer <a href="../ebd/ebd139.htm#000">fruit</a>. Then said the LORD unto me, The end is come upon my people of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>; I will not again pass by them any more.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd348.htm#002">songs</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd361.htm#004">temple</a> shall be howlings in that day, saith the Lord GOD: there shall be many dead bodies in every place; they shall cast them forth with silence.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Hear this, O ye that <a href="../ebd/ebd353.htm#009">swallow</a> up the needy, even to make the <a href="../ebd/ebd298.htm#006">poor</a> of the land to fail,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Saying, When will the new <a href="../ebd/ebd259.htm#004">moon</a> be gone, that we may sell <a href="../ebd/ebd090.htm#006">corn</a>? and the <a href="../ebd/ebd317.htm#003">sabbath</a>, that we may set forth <a href="../ebd/ebd381.htm#000">wheat</a>, making the <a href="../ebd/ebd122.htm#003">ephah</a> small, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd333.htm#009">shekel</a> great, and falsifying the balances by deceit?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> That we may buy the <a href="../ebd/ebd298.htm#006">poor</a> for <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#000">silver</a>, and the needy for a pair of shoes; yea, and sell the refuse of the <a href="../ebd/ebd381.htm#000">wheat</a>?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> The LORD hath sworn by the excellency of <a href="../ebd/ebd194.htm#007">Jacob</a>, Surely I will never forget any of their works.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> Shall not the land tremble for this, and every one <a href="../ebd/ebd261.htm#004">mourn</a> that dwelleth therein? and it shall rise up wholly as a <a href="../ebd/ebd136.htm#000">flood</a>; and it shall be cast out and drowned, as by the <a href="../ebd/ebd136.htm#000">flood</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And it shall come to pass in that day, saith the Lord GOD, that I will cause the <a href="../ebd/ebd353.htm#001">sun</a> to go down at noon, and I will darken the earth in the clear day:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And I will turn your feasts into mourning, and all your <a href="../ebd/ebd348.htm#002">songs</a> into <a href="../ebd/ebd223.htm#005">lamentation</a>; and I will bring up <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#001">sackcloth</a> upon all loins, and <a href="../ebd/ebd042.htm#008">baldness</a> upon every head; and I will make it as the mourning of an only son, and the end thereof as a <a href="../ebd/ebd059.htm#009">bitter</a> day.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> Behold, the days come, saith the Lord GOD, that I will send a <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#000">famine</a> in the land, not a <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#000">famine</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#006">bread</a>, nor a thirst for water, but of hearing the words of the LORD:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And they shall wander from sea to sea, and from the north even to the <a href="../ebd/ebd111.htm#000">east</a>, they shall run to and fro to seek the word of the LORD, and shall not find it.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> In that day shall the fair virgins and young men faint for thirst.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> They that swear by the <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd320.htm#004">Samaria</a>, and say, Thy god, O <a href="../ebd/ebd096.htm#009">Dan</a>, liveth; and, The manner of <a href="../ebd/ebd050.htm#008">Beersheba</a> liveth; even they shall fall, and never rise up again.<br>
 </p>
