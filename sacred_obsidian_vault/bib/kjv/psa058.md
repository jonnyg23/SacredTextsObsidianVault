 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 58</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Do ye indeed speak <a href="../ebd/ebd313.htm#004">righteousness</a>, O <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>? do ye <a href="../ebd/ebd213.htm#005">judge</a> uprightly, O ye sons of men?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Yea, in heart ye work wickedness; ye weigh the violence of your hands in the earth.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> The wicked are estranged from the womb: they go astray as soon as they be born, speaking lies.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Their <a href="../ebd/ebd297.htm#009">poison</a> is like the <a href="../ebd/ebd297.htm#009">poison</a> of a <a href="../ebd/ebd328.htm#009">serpent</a>: they are like the deaf <a href="../ebd/ebd008.htm#005">adder</a> that stoppeth her <a href="../ebd/ebd110.htm#004">ear</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Which will not hearken to the voice of charmers, charming never so wisely.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Break their teeth, O God, in their mouth: break out the great teeth of the young <a href="../ebd/ebd230.htm#001">lions</a>, O LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Let them melt away as waters which run continually: when he bendeth his <a href="../ebd/ebd063.htm#003">bow</a> to shoot his <a href="../ebd/ebd032.htm#005">arrows</a>, let them be as cut in <a href="../ebd/ebd295.htm#002">pieces</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> As a <a href="../ebd/ebd346.htm#008">snail</a> which melteth, let every one of them pass away: like the untimely <a href="../ebd/ebd059.htm#002">birth</a> of a woman, that they may not see the <a href="../ebd/ebd353.htm#001">sun</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> Before your pots can feel the thorns, he shall take them away as with a whirlwind, both living, and in his wrath.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> The righteous shall rejoice when he seeth the vengeance: he shall wash his feet in the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> of the wicked.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> So that a man shall say, Verily there is a reward for the righteous: verily he is a God that judgeth in the earth.<br>
 </p>
