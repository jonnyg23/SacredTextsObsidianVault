 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Judges Chapter 4</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> And the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> again did evil in the sight of the LORD, when <a href="../ebd/ebd114.htm#000">Ehud</a> was dead.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> And the LORD sold them into the hand of <a href="../ebd/ebd194.htm#000">Jabin</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#005">Canaan</a>, that reigned in <a href="../ebd/ebd169.htm#006">Hazor</a>; the captain of whose <a href="../ebd/ebd183.htm#007">host</a> was <a href="../ebd/ebd345.htm#007">Sisera</a>, which dwelt in Harosheth of the <a href="../ebd/ebd146.htm#001">Gentiles</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> cried unto the LORD: for he had nine hundred chariots of <a href="../ebd/ebd189.htm#004">iron</a>; and twenty years he mightily oppressed the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd099.htm#008">Deborah</a>, a prophetess, the wife of <a href="../ebd/ebd224.htm#001">Lapidoth</a>, she judged <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> at that time.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And she dwelt under the palm tree of <a href="../ebd/ebd099.htm#008">Deborah</a> between <a href="../ebd/ebd306.htm#005">Ramah</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd055.htm#006">Bethel</a> in <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#007">mount</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd123.htm#000">Ephraim</a>: and the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> came up to her for judgment.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And she sent and called <a href="../ebd/ebd044.htm#005">Barak</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd004.htm#002">Abinoam</a> out of Kedeshnaphtali, and said unto him, Hath not the LORD God of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> commanded, saying, Go and draw toward <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#007">mount</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#007">Tabor</a>, and take with thee ten thousand men of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd266.htm#006">Naphtali</a> and of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd389.htm#003">Zebulun</a>?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And I will draw unto thee to the river <a href="../ebd/ebd220.htm#009">Kishon</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd345.htm#007">Sisera</a>, the captain of Jabin's army, with his chariots and his multitude; and I will deliver him into thine hand.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd044.htm#005">Barak</a> said unto her, If thou wilt go with me, then I will go: but if thou wilt not go with me, then I will not go.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And she said, I will surely go with thee: notwithstanding the <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#001">journey</a> that thou takest shall not be for thine honour; for the LORD shall sell <a href="../ebd/ebd345.htm#007">Sisera</a> into the hand of a woman. And <a href="../ebd/ebd099.htm#008">Deborah</a> arose, and went with <a href="../ebd/ebd044.htm#005">Barak</a> to <a href="../ebd/ebd216.htm#006">Kedesh</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd044.htm#005">Barak</a> called <a href="../ebd/ebd389.htm#003">Zebulun</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd266.htm#006">Naphtali</a> to <a href="../ebd/ebd216.htm#006">Kedesh</a>; and he went up with ten thousand men at his feet: and <a href="../ebd/ebd099.htm#008">Deborah</a> went up with him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> Now <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#008">Heber</a> the Kenite, which was of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd180.htm#000">Hobab</a> the father in law of <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, had severed himself from the <a href="../ebd/ebd217.htm#004">Kenites</a>, and pitched his <a href="../ebd/ebd361.htm#009">tent</a> unto the <a href="../ebd/ebd297.htm#002">plain</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd385.htm#001">Zaanaim</a>, which is by <a href="../ebd/ebd216.htm#006">Kedesh</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And they shewed <a href="../ebd/ebd345.htm#007">Sisera</a> that <a href="../ebd/ebd044.htm#005">Barak</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd004.htm#002">Abinoam</a> was gone up to <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#007">mount</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#007">Tabor</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd345.htm#007">Sisera</a> gathered together all his chariots, even nine hundred chariots of <a href="../ebd/ebd189.htm#004">iron</a>, and all the people that were with him, from Harosheth of the <a href="../ebd/ebd146.htm#001">Gentiles</a> unto the river of <a href="../ebd/ebd220.htm#009">Kishon</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd099.htm#008">Deborah</a> said unto <a href="../ebd/ebd044.htm#005">Barak</a>, Up; for this is the day in which the LORD hath delivered <a href="../ebd/ebd345.htm#007">Sisera</a> into thine hand: is not the LORD gone out before thee? So <a href="../ebd/ebd044.htm#005">Barak</a> went down from <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#007">mount</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#007">Tabor</a>, and ten thousand men after him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And the LORD discomfited <a href="../ebd/ebd345.htm#007">Sisera</a>, and all his chariots, and all his <a href="../ebd/ebd183.htm#007">host</a>, with the edge of the <a href="../ebd/ebd354.htm#003">sword</a> before <a href="../ebd/ebd044.htm#005">Barak</a>; so that <a href="../ebd/ebd345.htm#007">Sisera</a> lighted down off his <a href="../ebd/ebd077.htm#008">chariot</a>, and fled away on his feet.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> But <a href="../ebd/ebd044.htm#005">Barak</a> pursued after the chariots, and after the <a href="../ebd/ebd183.htm#007">host</a>, unto Harosheth of the <a href="../ebd/ebd146.htm#001">Gentiles</a>: and all the <a href="../ebd/ebd183.htm#007">host</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd345.htm#007">Sisera</a> fell upon the edge of the <a href="../ebd/ebd354.htm#003">sword</a>; and there was not a man left.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> Howbeit <a href="../ebd/ebd345.htm#007">Sisera</a> fled away on his feet to the <a href="../ebd/ebd361.htm#009">tent</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd195.htm#001">Jael</a> the wife of <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#008">Heber</a> the Kenite: for there was peace between <a href="../ebd/ebd194.htm#000">Jabin</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd169.htm#006">Hazor</a> and the house of <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#008">Heber</a> the Kenite.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd195.htm#001">Jael</a> went out to meet <a href="../ebd/ebd345.htm#007">Sisera</a>, and said unto him, Turn in, my lord, turn in to me; fear not. And when he had turned in unto her into the <a href="../ebd/ebd361.htm#009">tent</a>, she covered him with a <a href="../ebd/ebd240.htm#009">mantle</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> And he said unto her, Give me, I pray thee, a little water to <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a>; for I am thirsty. And she opened a <a href="../ebd/ebd063.htm#002">bottle</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd255.htm#000">milk</a>, and gave him <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a>, and covered him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> Again he said unto her, Stand in the door of the <a href="../ebd/ebd361.htm#009">tent</a>, and it shall be, when any man doth come and enquire of thee, and say, Is there any man here? that thou shalt say, No.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> Then <a href="../ebd/ebd195.htm#001">Jael</a> Heber's wife took a <a href="../ebd/ebd266.htm#000">nail</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd361.htm#009">tent</a>, and took an <a href="../ebd/ebd161.htm#000">hammer</a> in her hand, and went softly unto him, and smote the <a href="../ebd/ebd266.htm#000">nail</a> into his temples, and fastened it into the ground: for he was <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#004">fast</a> asleep and weary. So he died.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> And, behold, as <a href="../ebd/ebd044.htm#005">Barak</a> pursued <a href="../ebd/ebd345.htm#007">Sisera</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd195.htm#001">Jael</a> came out to meet him, and said unto him, Come, and I will shew thee the man whom thou seekest. And when he came into her <a href="../ebd/ebd361.htm#009">tent</a>, behold, <a href="../ebd/ebd345.htm#007">Sisera</a> lay dead, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd266.htm#000">nail</a> was in his temples.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> So God subdued on that day <a href="../ebd/ebd194.htm#000">Jabin</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#005">Canaan</a> before the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> And the hand of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> prospered, and prevailed against <a href="../ebd/ebd194.htm#000">Jabin</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#005">Canaan</a>, until they had destroyed <a href="../ebd/ebd194.htm#000">Jabin</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#005">Canaan</a>.<br>
 </p>
