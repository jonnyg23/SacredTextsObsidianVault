 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Leviticus Chapter 3</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> And if his oblation be a <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#002">sacrifice</a> of peace <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, if he offer it of the <a href="../ebd/ebd174.htm#009">herd</a>; whether it be a male or female, he shall offer it without <a href="../ebd/ebd060.htm#007">blemish</a> before the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> And he shall lay his hand upon the head of his <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, and kill it at the door of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>: and Aaron's sons the priests shall sprinkle the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> round about.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And he shall offer of the <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#002">sacrifice</a> of the peace <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> an <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> made by fire unto the LORD; the <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#005">fat</a> that covereth the inwards, and all the <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#005">fat</a> that is upon the inwards,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And the two kidneys, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#005">fat</a> that is on them, which is by the flanks, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd074.htm#003">caul</a> above the <a href="../ebd/ebd230.htm#004">liver</a>, with the kidneys, it shall he take away.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And Aaron's sons shall burn it on the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> upon the burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#002">sacrifice</a>, which is upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd383.htm#003">wood</a> that is on the fire: it is an <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> made by fire, of a sweet savour unto the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And if his <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> for a <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#002">sacrifice</a> of peace <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> unto the LORD be of the flock; male or female, he shall offer it without <a href="../ebd/ebd060.htm#007">blemish</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> If he offer a <a href="../ebd/ebd223.htm#003">lamb</a> for his <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, then shall he offer it before the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And he shall lay his hand upon the head of his <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, and kill it before the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>: and Aaron's sons shall sprinkle the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> thereof round about upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And he shall offer of the <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#002">sacrifice</a> of the peace <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> an <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> made by fire unto the LORD; the <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#005">fat</a> thereof, and the whole rump, it shall he take off hard by the backbone; and the <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#005">fat</a> that covereth the inwards, and all the <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#005">fat</a> that is upon the inwards,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And the two kidneys, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#005">fat</a> that is upon them, which is by the flanks, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd074.htm#003">caul</a> above the <a href="../ebd/ebd230.htm#004">liver</a>, with the kidneys, it shall he take away.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> shall burn it upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>: it is the food of the <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> made by fire unto the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And if his <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> be a <a href="../ebd/ebd151.htm#001">goat</a>, then he shall offer it before the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> And he shall lay his hand upon the head of it, and kill it before the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>: and the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> shall sprinkle the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> thereof upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> round about.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> And he shall offer thereof his <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, even an <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> made by fire unto the LORD; the <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#005">fat</a> that covereth the inwards, and all the <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#005">fat</a> that is upon the inwards,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And the two kidneys, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#005">fat</a> that is upon them, which is by the flanks, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd074.htm#003">caul</a> above the <a href="../ebd/ebd230.htm#004">liver</a>, with the kidneys, it shall he take away.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> shall burn them upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>: it is the food of the <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> made by fire for a sweet savour: all the <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#005">fat</a> is the LORD's.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> It shall be a perpetual statute for your generations throughout all your <a href="../ebd/ebd110.htm#001">dwellings</a>, that ye eat neither <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#005">fat</a> nor <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a>.<br>
 </p>
