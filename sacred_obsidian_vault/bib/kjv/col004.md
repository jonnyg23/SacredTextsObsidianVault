 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Colossians Chapter 4</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Masters, give unto your servants that which is just and equal; knowing that ye also have a Master in <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#006">heaven</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Continue in <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#000">prayer</a>, and watch in the same with thanksgiving;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Withal praying also for us, that God would open unto us a door of utterance, to speak the <a href="../ebd/ebd263.htm#006">mystery</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a>, for which I am also in bonds:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> That I may make it manifest, as I ought to speak.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Walk in wisdom toward them that are without, redeeming the time.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Let your speech be alway with <a href="../ebd/ebd154.htm#000">grace</a>, seasoned with <a href="../ebd/ebd319.htm#008">salt</a>, that ye may know how ye ought to answer every man.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> All my state shall <a href="../ebd/ebd373.htm#008">Tychicus</a> declare unto you, who is a beloved brother, and a <a href="../ebd/ebd130.htm#005">faithful</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd255.htm#007">minister</a> and fellowservant in the Lord:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> Whom I have sent unto you for the same purpose, that he might know your estate, and comfort your hearts;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> With <a href="../ebd/ebd278.htm#009">Onesimus</a>, a <a href="../ebd/ebd130.htm#005">faithful</a> and beloved brother, who is one of you. They shall make known unto you all things which are done here.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd030.htm#008">Aristarchus</a> my fellowprisoner saluteth you, and <a href="../ebd/ebd241.htm#008">Marcus</a>, sister's son to <a href="../ebd/ebd045.htm#005">Barnabas</a>, (touching whom ye received commandments: if he come unto you, receive him;)<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a>, which is called <a href="../ebd/ebd215.htm#000">Justus</a>, who are of the <a href="../ebd/ebd083.htm#007">circumcision</a>. These only are my fellowworkers unto the kingdom of God, which have been a comfort unto me.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd122.htm#000">Epaphras</a>, who is one of you, a servant of <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a>, saluteth you, always labouring fervently for you in prayers, that ye may stand perfect and complete in all the will of God.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> For I bear him record, that he hath a great <a href="../ebd/ebd388.htm#003">zeal</a> for you, and them that are in <a href="../ebd/ebd223.htm#009">Laodicea</a>, and them in <a href="../ebd/ebd178.htm#001">Hierapolis</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd233.htm#002">Luke</a>, the beloved <a href="../ebd/ebd295.htm#000">physician</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd101.htm#004">Demas</a>, greet you.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> Salute the brethren which are in <a href="../ebd/ebd223.htm#009">Laodicea</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd275.htm#008">Nymphas</a>, and the church which is in his house.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> And when this epistle is read among you, cause that it be read also in the church of the Laodiceans; and that ye likewise read the epistle from <a href="../ebd/ebd223.htm#009">Laodicea</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> And say to <a href="../ebd/ebd029.htm#005">Archippus</a>, Take heed to the ministry which thou hast received in the Lord, that thou fulfil it.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> The <a href="../ebd/ebd320.htm#002">salutation</a> by the hand of me <a href="../ebd/ebd287.htm#002">Paul</a>. Remember my bonds. <a href="../ebd/ebd154.htm#000">Grace</a> be with you. <a href="../ebd/ebd020.htm#001">Amen</a>.<br>
 </p>
