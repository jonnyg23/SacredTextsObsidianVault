 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 47</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> O clap your hands, all ye people; shout unto God with the voice of triumph.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> For the LORD most high is terrible; he is a great <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">King</a> over all the earth.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> He shall subdue the people under us, and the nations under our feet.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> He shall choose our inheritance for us, the excellency of <a href="../ebd/ebd194.htm#007">Jacob</a> whom he loved. <a href="../ebd/ebd326.htm#008">Selah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> God is gone up with a shout, the LORD with the sound of a trumpet.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Sing praises to God, sing praises: sing praises unto our <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">King</a>, sing praises.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> For God is the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">King</a> of all the earth: sing ye praises with understanding.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> God reigneth over the <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#005">heathen</a>: God sitteth upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd365.htm#001">throne</a> of his <a href="../ebd/ebd180.htm#006">holiness</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> The princes of the people are gathered together, even the people of the God of <a href="../ebd/ebd005.htm#005">Abraham</a>: for the shields of the earth belong unto God: he is greatly exalted.<br>
 </p>
