 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 43</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd213.htm#005">Judge</a> me, O God, and plead my cause against an ungodly nation: O deliver me from the deceitful and unjust man.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> For thou art the God of my strength: why dost thou cast me off? why go I mourning because of the oppression of the enemy?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> O send out thy light and thy truth: let them lead me; let them bring me unto thy holy hill, and to thy tabernacles.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Then will I go unto the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> of God, unto God my exceeding joy: yea, upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd165.htm#001">harp</a> will I praise thee, O God my God.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Why art thou cast down, O my soul? and why art thou disquieted within me? hope in God: for I shall yet praise him, who is the health of my countenance, and my God.<br>
 </p>
