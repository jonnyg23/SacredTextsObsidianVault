 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Genesis Chapter 46</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> took his <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#001">journey</a> with all that he had, and came to <a href="../ebd/ebd050.htm#008">Beersheba</a>, and offered sacrifices unto the God of his father <a href="../ebd/ebd189.htm#006">Isaac</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> And God spake unto <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> in the visions of the night, and said, <a href="../ebd/ebd194.htm#007">Jacob</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd194.htm#007">Jacob</a>. And he said, Here am I.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And he said, I am God, the God of thy father: fear not to go down into <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a>; for I will there make of thee a great nation:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> I will go down with thee into <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a>; and I will also surely bring thee up again: and <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#005">Joseph</a> shall put his hand upon thine eyes.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd194.htm#007">Jacob</a> rose up from <a href="../ebd/ebd050.htm#008">Beersheba</a>: and the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> carried <a href="../ebd/ebd194.htm#007">Jacob</a> their father, and their little ones, and their wives, in the wagons which <a href="../ebd/ebd292.htm#005">Pharaoh</a> had sent to carry him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And they took their <a href="../ebd/ebd074.htm#002">cattle</a>, and their goods, which they had gotten in the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#005">Canaan</a>, and came into <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd194.htm#007">Jacob</a>, and all his seed with him:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> His sons, and his sons' sons with him, his daughters, and his sons' daughters, and all his seed brought he with him into <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And these are the names of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, which came into <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd194.htm#007">Jacob</a> and his sons: <a href="../ebd/ebd311.htm#009">Reuben</a>, Jacob's firstborn.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd311.htm#009">Reuben</a>; Hanoch, and <a href="../ebd/ebd292.htm#001">Phallu</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd177.htm#007">Hezron</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd072.htm#007">Carmi</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#002">Simeon</a>; Jemuel, and Jamin, and <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#003">Ohad</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd194.htm#004">Jachin</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd395.htm#006">Zohar</a>, and Shaul the son of a Canaanitish woman.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd227.htm#006">Levi</a>; <a href="../ebd/ebd146.htm#009">Gershon</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd222.htm#001">Kohath</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#000">Merari</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>; Er, and <a href="../ebd/ebd278.htm#008">Onan</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd334.htm#000">Shelah</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd292.htm#007">Pharez</a>, and Zarah: but Er and <a href="../ebd/ebd278.htm#008">Onan</a> died in the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#005">Canaan</a>. And the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd292.htm#007">Pharez</a> were <a href="../ebd/ebd177.htm#007">Hezron</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd161.htm#008">Hamul</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> And the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#002">Issachar</a>; <a href="../ebd/ebd369.htm#007">Tola</a>, and Phuvah, and Job, and <a href="../ebd/ebd338.htm#008">Shimron</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> And the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd389.htm#003">Zebulun</a>; <a href="../ebd/ebd328.htm#005">Sered</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd119.htm#000">Elon</a>, and Jahleel.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> These be the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd225.htm#006">Leah</a>, which she bare unto <a href="../ebd/ebd194.htm#007">Jacob</a> in Padanaram, with his daughter <a href="../ebd/ebd103.htm#008">Dinah</a>: all the souls of his sons and his daughters were thirty and three.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> And the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd140.htm#007">Gad</a>; Ziphion, and Haggi, Shuni, and Ezbon, Eri, and Arodi, and Areli.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> And the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd033.htm#008">Asher</a>; Jimnah, and Ishuah, and Isui, and <a href="../ebd/ebd053.htm#005">Beriah</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd328.htm#002">Serah</a> their sister: and the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd053.htm#005">Beriah</a>; <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#008">Heber</a>, and Malchiel.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> These are the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd393.htm#005">Zilpah</a>, whom <a href="../ebd/ebd222.htm#008">Laban</a> gave to <a href="../ebd/ebd225.htm#006">Leah</a> his daughter, and these she bare unto <a href="../ebd/ebd194.htm#007">Jacob</a>, even sixteen souls.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> The sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd305.htm#004">Rachel</a> Jacob's wife; <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#005">Joseph</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd052.htm#008">Benjamin</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> And unto <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#005">Joseph</a> in the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a> were born <a href="../ebd/ebd240.htm#002">Manasseh</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd123.htm#000">Ephraim</a>, which <a href="../ebd/ebd033.htm#004">Asenath</a> the daughter of <a href="../ebd/ebd299.htm#003">Potipherah</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> of On bare unto him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> And the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd052.htm#008">Benjamin</a> were Belah, and <a href="../ebd/ebd049.htm#005">Becher</a>, and Ashbel, <a href="../ebd/ebd146.htm#003">Gera</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd263.htm#009">Naaman</a>, Ehi, and <a href="../ebd/ebd315.htm#009">Rosh</a>, Muppim, and Huppim, and <a href="../ebd/ebd029.htm#008">Ard</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> These are the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd305.htm#004">Rachel</a>, which were born to <a href="../ebd/ebd194.htm#007">Jacob</a>: all the souls were fourteen.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> And the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd096.htm#009">Dan</a>; Hushim.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> And the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd266.htm#006">Naphtali</a>; <a href="../ebd/ebd195.htm#008">Jahzeel</a>, and Guni, and Jezer, and Shillem.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> These are the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd058.htm#008">Bilhah</a>, which <a href="../ebd/ebd222.htm#008">Laban</a> gave unto <a href="../ebd/ebd305.htm#004">Rachel</a> his daughter, and she bare these unto <a href="../ebd/ebd194.htm#007">Jacob</a>: all the souls were <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> All the souls that came with <a href="../ebd/ebd194.htm#007">Jacob</a> into <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a>, which came out of his loins, besides Jacob's sons' wives, all the souls were threescore and six;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> And the sons of <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#005">Joseph</a>, which were born him in <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a>, were two souls: all the souls of the house of <a href="../ebd/ebd194.htm#007">Jacob</a>, which came into <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a>, were threescore and ten.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="028">28</a> And he sent <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a> before him unto <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#005">Joseph</a>, to direct his face unto <a href="../ebd/ebd153.htm#002">Goshen</a>; and they came into the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd153.htm#002">Goshen</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="029">29</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#005">Joseph</a> made ready his <a href="../ebd/ebd077.htm#008">chariot</a>, and went up to meet <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> his father, to <a href="../ebd/ebd153.htm#002">Goshen</a>, and presented himself unto him; and he fell on his <a href="../ebd/ebd269.htm#000">neck</a>, and wept on his <a href="../ebd/ebd269.htm#000">neck</a> a good while.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="030">30</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> said unto <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#005">Joseph</a>, Now let me die, since I have seen thy face, because thou art yet alive.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="031">31</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#005">Joseph</a> said unto his brethren, and unto his father's house, I will go up, and shew <a href="../ebd/ebd292.htm#005">Pharaoh</a>, and say unto him, My brethren, and my father's house, which were in the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#005">Canaan</a>, are come unto me;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="032">32</a> And the men are shepherds, for their trade hath been to feed <a href="../ebd/ebd074.htm#002">cattle</a>; and they have brought their flocks, and their herds, and all that they have.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="033">33</a> And it shall come to pass, when <a href="../ebd/ebd292.htm#005">Pharaoh</a> shall call you, and shall say, What is your occupation?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="034">34</a> That ye shall say, Thy servants' trade hath been about <a href="../ebd/ebd074.htm#002">cattle</a> from our youth even until now, both we, and also our fathers: that ye may dwell in the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd153.htm#002">Goshen</a>; for every <a href="../ebd/ebd335.htm#006">shepherd</a> is an <a href="../ebd/ebd005.htm#003">abomination</a> unto the Egyptians.<br>
 </p>
