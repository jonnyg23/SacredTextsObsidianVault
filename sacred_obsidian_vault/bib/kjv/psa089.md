 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 89</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> I will sing of the mercies of the LORD for ever: with my mouth will I make known thy faithfulness to all generations.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> For I have said, <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">Mercy</a> shall be built up for ever: thy faithfulness shalt thou establish in the very heavens.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> I have made a <a href="../ebd/ebd091.htm#008">covenant</a> with my <a href="../ebd/ebd081.htm#008">chosen</a>, I have sworn unto <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> my servant,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Thy seed will I establish for ever, and build up thy <a href="../ebd/ebd365.htm#001">throne</a> to all generations. <a href="../ebd/ebd326.htm#008">Selah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And the heavens shall praise thy wonders, O LORD: thy faithfulness also in the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> of the saints.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> For who in the <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#006">heaven</a> can be compared unto the LORD? who among the sons of the mighty can be likened unto the LORD?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> God is greatly to be feared in the assembly of the saints, and to be had in reverence of all them that are about him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> O LORD God of hosts, who is a strong LORD like unto thee? or to thy faithfulness round about thee?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> Thou rulest the raging of the sea: when the waves thereof arise, thou stillest them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> Thou hast broken <a href="../ebd/ebd305.htm#006">Rahab</a> in <a href="../ebd/ebd295.htm#002">pieces</a>, as one that is slain; thou hast scattered thine enemies with thy strong arm.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> The heavens are thine, the earth also is thine: as for the world and the <a href="../ebd/ebd139.htm#007">fulness</a> thereof, thou hast founded them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> The north and the south thou hast created them: <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#007">Tabor</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd175.htm#006">Hermon</a> shall rejoice in thy name.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> Thou hast a mighty arm: strong is thy hand, and high is thy right hand.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd214.htm#007">Justice</a> and judgment are the <a href="../ebd/ebd156.htm#007">habitation</a> of thy <a href="../ebd/ebd365.htm#001">throne</a>: <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> and truth shall go before thy face.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> Blessed is the people that know the joyful sound: they shall walk, O LORD, in the light of thy countenance.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> In thy name shall they rejoice all the day: and in thy <a href="../ebd/ebd313.htm#004">righteousness</a> shall they be exalted.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> For thou art the <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a> of their strength: and in thy favour our <a href="../ebd/ebd182.htm#003">horn</a> shall be exalted.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> For the LORD is our defence; and the Holy One of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> is our <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> Then thou spakest in <a href="../ebd/ebd378.htm#000">vision</a> to thy holy one, and saidst, I have laid help upon one that is mighty; I have exalted one <a href="../ebd/ebd081.htm#008">chosen</a> out of the people.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> I have found <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> my servant; with my holy oil have I anointed him:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> With whom my hand shall be established: mine arm also shall strengthen him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> The enemy shall not exact upon him; nor the son of wickedness afflict him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> And I will beat down his foes before his face, and <a href="../ebd/ebd297.htm#001">plague</a> them that hate him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> But my faithfulness and my <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> shall be with him: and in my name shall his <a href="../ebd/ebd182.htm#003">horn</a> be exalted.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> I will set his hand also in the sea, and his right hand in the rivers.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> He shall cry unto me, Thou art my father, my God, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd315.htm#001">rock</a> of my <a href="../ebd/ebd320.htm#003">salvation</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> Also I will make him my firstborn, higher than the kings of the earth.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="028">28</a> My <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> will I keep for him for evermore, and my <a href="../ebd/ebd091.htm#008">covenant</a> shall stand <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#004">fast</a> with him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="029">29</a> His seed also will I make to endure for ever, and his <a href="../ebd/ebd365.htm#001">throne</a> as the days of <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#006">heaven</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="030">30</a> If his children forsake my law, and walk not in my judgments;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="031">31</a> If they break my statutes, and keep not my commandments;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="032">32</a> Then will I visit their transgression with the rod, and their iniquity with <a href="../ebd/ebd352.htm#005">stripes</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="033">33</a> Nevertheless my lovingkindness will I not utterly take from him, nor suffer my faithfulness to fail.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="034">34</a> My <a href="../ebd/ebd091.htm#008">covenant</a> will I not break, nor alter the thing that is gone out of my lips.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="035">35</a> Once have I sworn by my <a href="../ebd/ebd180.htm#006">holiness</a> that I will not lie unto <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="036">36</a> His seed shall endure for ever, and his <a href="../ebd/ebd365.htm#001">throne</a> as the <a href="../ebd/ebd353.htm#001">sun</a> before me.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="037">37</a> It shall be established for ever as the <a href="../ebd/ebd259.htm#004">moon</a>, and as a <a href="../ebd/ebd130.htm#005">faithful</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#008">witness</a> in <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#006">heaven</a>. <a href="../ebd/ebd326.htm#008">Selah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="038">38</a> But thou hast cast off and abhorred, thou hast been wroth with thine anointed.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="039">39</a> Thou hast made void the <a href="../ebd/ebd091.htm#008">covenant</a> of thy servant: thou hast profaned his <a href="../ebd/ebd093.htm#001">crown</a> by casting it to the ground.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="040">40</a> Thou hast broken down all his hedges; thou hast brought his strong holds to ruin.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="041">41</a> All that pass by the way spoil him: he is a reproach to his neighbours.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="042">42</a> Thou hast set up the right hand of his adversaries; thou hast made all his enemies to rejoice.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="043">43</a> Thou hast also turned the edge of his <a href="../ebd/ebd354.htm#003">sword</a>, and hast not made him to stand in the battle.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="044">44</a> Thou hast made his <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a> to cease, and cast his <a href="../ebd/ebd365.htm#001">throne</a> down to the ground.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="045">45</a> The days of his youth hast thou shortened: thou hast covered him with shame. <a href="../ebd/ebd326.htm#008">Selah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="046">46</a> How long, LORD? wilt thou hide thyself for ever? shall thy wrath burn like fire?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="047">47</a> Remember how short my time is: wherefore hast thou made all men in vain?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="048">48</a> What man is he that liveth, and shall not see death? shall he deliver his soul from the hand of the <a href="../ebd/ebd154.htm#008">grave</a>? <a href="../ebd/ebd326.htm#008">Selah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="049">49</a> Lord, where are thy former lovingkindnesses, which thou swarest unto <a href="../ebd/ebd098.htm#004">David</a> in thy truth?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="050">50</a> Remember, Lord, the reproach of thy servants; how I do bear in my <a href="../ebd/ebd062.htm#008">bosom</a> the reproach of all the mighty people;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="051">51</a> Wherewith thine enemies have reproached, O LORD; wherewith they have reproached the footsteps of thine anointed.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="052">52</a> Blessed be the LORD for evermore. <a href="../ebd/ebd020.htm#001">Amen</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd020.htm#001">Amen</a>.<br>
 </p>
