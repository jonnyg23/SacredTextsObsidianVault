 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 120</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> In my distress I cried unto the LORD, and he heard me.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Deliver my soul, O LORD, from lying lips, and from a deceitful tongue.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> What shall be given unto thee? or what shall be done unto thee, thou false tongue?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Sharp <a href="../ebd/ebd032.htm#005">arrows</a> of the mighty, with coals of <a href="../ebd/ebd214.htm#005">juniper</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Woe is me, that I sojourn in Mesech, that I dwell in the tents of <a href="../ebd/ebd216.htm#003">Kedar</a>!<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> My soul hath long dwelt with him that hateth peace.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> I am for peace: but when I speak, they are for war.<br>
 </p>
