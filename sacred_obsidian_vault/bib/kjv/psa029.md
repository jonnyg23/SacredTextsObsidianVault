 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 29</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Give unto the LORD, O ye mighty, give unto the LORD <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a> and strength.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Give unto the LORD the <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a> due unto his name; <a href="../ebd/ebd384.htm#002">worship</a> the LORD in the beauty of <a href="../ebd/ebd180.htm#006">holiness</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> The voice of the LORD is upon the waters: the God of <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a> thundereth: the LORD is upon many waters.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> The voice of the LORD is powerful; the voice of the LORD is full of majesty.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> The voice of the LORD breaketh the cedars; yea, the LORD breaketh the cedars of <a href="../ebd/ebd226.htm#001">Lebanon</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> He maketh them also to skip like a <a href="../ebd/ebd069.htm#004">calf</a>; <a href="../ebd/ebd226.htm#001">Lebanon</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd345.htm#006">Sirion</a> like a young <a href="../ebd/ebd374.htm#007">unicorn</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> The voice of the LORD divideth the flames of fire.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> The voice of the LORD shaketh the <a href="../ebd/ebd381.htm#005">wilderness</a>; the LORD shaketh the <a href="../ebd/ebd381.htm#005">wilderness</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd215.htm#003">Kadesh</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> The voice of the LORD maketh the hinds to calve, and discovereth the forests: and in his <a href="../ebd/ebd361.htm#004">temple</a> doth every one speak of his <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> The LORD sitteth upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd136.htm#000">flood</a>; yea, the LORD sitteth <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">King</a> for ever.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> The LORD will give strength unto his people; the LORD will <a href="../ebd/ebd060.htm#008">bless</a> his people with peace.<br>
 </p>
