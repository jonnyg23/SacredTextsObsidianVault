 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 40</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> I waited patiently for the LORD; and he inclined unto me, and heard my cry.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> He brought me up also out of an horrible <a href="../ebd/ebd296.htm#007">pit</a>, out of the miry <a href="../ebd/ebd084.htm#004">clay</a>, and set my feet upon a <a href="../ebd/ebd315.htm#001">rock</a>, and established my goings.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And he hath put a new song in my mouth, even praise unto our God: many shall see it, and fear, and shall trust in the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Blessed is that man that maketh the LORD his trust, and respecteth not the proud, nor such as turn aside to lies.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Many, O LORD my God, are thy wonderful works which thou hast done, and thy thoughts which are to us-ward: they cannot be reckoned up in order unto thee: if I would declare and speak of them, they are more than can be numbered.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#002">Sacrifice</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> thou didst not desire; mine ears hast thou opened: burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> hast thou not required.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Then said I, Lo, I come: in the volume of the book it is written of me,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> I delight to do thy will, O my God: yea, thy law is within my heart.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> I have preached <a href="../ebd/ebd313.htm#004">righteousness</a> in the great <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>: lo, I have not refrained my lips, O LORD, thou knowest.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> I have not hid thy <a href="../ebd/ebd313.htm#004">righteousness</a> within my heart; I have declared thy faithfulness and thy <a href="../ebd/ebd320.htm#003">salvation</a>: I have not concealed thy lovingkindness and thy truth from the great <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> Withhold not thou thy tender mercies from me, O LORD: let thy lovingkindness and thy truth continually preserve me.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> For innumerable evils have compassed me about: mine iniquities have taken hold upon me, so that I am not able to look up; they are more than the hairs of mine head: therefore my heart faileth me.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> Be pleased, O LORD, to deliver me: O LORD, make haste to help me.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> Let them be ashamed and confounded together that seek after my soul to destroy it; let them be driven backward and put to shame that wish me evil.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> Let them be desolate for a reward of their shame that say unto me, <a href="../ebd/ebd013.htm#001">Aha</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd013.htm#001">aha</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> Let all those that seek thee rejoice and be glad in thee: let such as love thy <a href="../ebd/ebd320.htm#003">salvation</a> say continually, The LORD be magnified.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> But I am <a href="../ebd/ebd298.htm#006">poor</a> and needy; yet the Lord thinketh upon me: thou art my help and my deliverer; make no tarrying, O my God.<br>
 </p>
