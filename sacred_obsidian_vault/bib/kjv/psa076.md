 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 76</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> In <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a> is God known: his name is great in <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> In <a href="../ebd/ebd319.htm#001">Salem</a> also is his <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a>, and his dwelling place in <a href="../ebd/ebd394.htm#002">Zion</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> There brake he the <a href="../ebd/ebd032.htm#005">arrows</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd063.htm#003">bow</a>, the <a href="../ebd/ebd337.htm#001">shield</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd354.htm#003">sword</a>, and the battle. <a href="../ebd/ebd326.htm#008">Selah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Thou art more glorious and excellent than the mountains of prey.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> The stouthearted are spoiled, they have slept their sleep: and none of the men of might have found their hands.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> At thy rebuke, O God of <a href="../ebd/ebd194.htm#007">Jacob</a>, both the <a href="../ebd/ebd077.htm#008">chariot</a> and horse are cast into a dead sleep.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Thou, even thou, art to be feared: and who may stand in thy sight when once thou art angry?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> Thou didst cause judgment to be heard from <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#006">heaven</a>; the earth feared, and was still,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> When God arose to judgment, to save all the meek of the earth. <a href="../ebd/ebd326.htm#008">Selah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> Surely the wrath of man shall praise thee: the remainder of wrath shalt thou restrain.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> Vow, and pay unto the LORD your God: let all that be round about him bring presents unto him that ought to be feared.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> He shall cut off the <a href="../ebd/ebd349.htm#009">spirit</a> of princes: he is terrible to the kings of the earth.<br>
 </p>
