 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 150</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Praise ye the LORD. Praise God in his <a href="../ebd/ebd321.htm#005">sanctuary</a>: praise him in the <a href="../ebd/ebd134.htm#000">firmament</a> of his power.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Praise him for his mighty acts: praise him according to his excellent greatness.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Praise him with the sound of the trumpet: praise him with the <a href="../ebd/ebd301.htm#006">psaltery</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd165.htm#001">harp</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Praise him with the <a href="../ebd/ebd366.htm#004">timbrel</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd097.htm#000">dance</a>: praise him with stringed instruments and organs.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Praise him upon the loud <a href="../ebd/ebd095.htm#001">cymbals</a>: praise him upon the high sounding <a href="../ebd/ebd095.htm#001">cymbals</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Let every thing that hath breath praise the LORD. Praise ye the LORD.<br>
 </p>
