 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 146</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Praise ye the LORD. Praise the LORD, O my soul.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> While I live will I praise the LORD: I will sing praises unto my God while I have any being.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Put not your trust in princes, nor in the son of man, in whom there is no help.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> His breath goeth forth, he returneth to his earth; in that very day his thoughts perish.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Happy is he that hath the God of <a href="../ebd/ebd194.htm#007">Jacob</a> for his help, whose hope is in the LORD his God:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Which made <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#006">heaven</a>, and earth, the sea, and all that therein is: which keepeth truth for ever:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Which executeth judgment for the oppressed: which giveth food to the hungry. The LORD looseth the prisoners:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> The LORD openeth the eyes of the <a href="../ebd/ebd060.htm#009">blind</a>: the LORD raiseth them that are bowed down: the LORD loveth the righteous:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> The LORD preserveth the strangers; he relieveth the fatherless and widow: but the way of the wicked he turneth upside down.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> The LORD shall reign for ever, even thy God, O <a href="../ebd/ebd394.htm#002">Zion</a>, unto all generations. Praise ye the LORD.<br>
 </p>
