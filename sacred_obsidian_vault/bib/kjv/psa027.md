 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 27</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> The LORD is my light and my <a href="../ebd/ebd320.htm#003">salvation</a>; whom shall I fear? the LORD is the strength of my life; of whom shall I be afraid?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> When the wicked, even mine enemies and my foes, came upon me to eat up my <a href="../ebd/ebd135.htm#007">flesh</a>, they stumbled and fell.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Though an <a href="../ebd/ebd183.htm#007">host</a> should <a href="../ebd/ebd120.htm#003">encamp</a> against me, my heart shall not fear: though war should rise against me, in this will I be confident.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> One thing have I desired of the LORD, that will I seek after; that I may dwell in the house of the LORD all the days of my life, to behold the beauty of the LORD, and to enquire in his <a href="../ebd/ebd361.htm#004">temple</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> For in the time of trouble he shall hide me in his <a href="../ebd/ebd287.htm#004">pavilion</a>: in the secret of his <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> shall he hide me; he shall set me up upon a <a href="../ebd/ebd315.htm#001">rock</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And now shall mine head be lifted up above mine enemies round about me: therefore will I offer in his <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> sacrifices of joy; I will sing, yea, I will sing praises unto the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Hear, O LORD, when I cry with my voice: have <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> also upon me, and answer me.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> When thou saidst, Seek ye my face; my heart said unto thee, Thy face, LORD, will I seek.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> Hide not thy face far from me; put not thy servant away in <a href="../ebd/ebd024.htm#002">anger</a>: thou hast been my help; leave me not, neither forsake me, O God of my <a href="../ebd/ebd320.htm#003">salvation</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> When my father and my mother forsake me, then the LORD will take me up.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> Teach me thy way, O LORD, and lead me in a <a href="../ebd/ebd297.htm#002">plain</a> path, because of mine enemies.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> Deliver me not over unto the will of mine enemies: for false witnesses are risen up against me, and such as breathe out cruelty.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> I had fainted, unless I had believed to see the <a href="../ebd/ebd153.htm#000">goodness</a> of the LORD in the land of the living.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> Wait on the LORD: be of good courage, and he shall strengthen thine heart: wait, I say, on the LORD.<br>
 </p>
