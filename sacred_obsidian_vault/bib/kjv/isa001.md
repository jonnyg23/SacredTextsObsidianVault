 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Isaiah Chapter 1</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> The <a href="../ebd/ebd378.htm#000">vision</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd189.htm#007">Isaiah</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd021.htm#008">Amoz</a>, which he saw concerning <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a> in the days of <a href="../ebd/ebd376.htm#004">Uzziah</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#000">Jotham</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd013.htm#005">Ahaz</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd177.htm#003">Hezekiah</a>, kings of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Hear, O heavens, and give <a href="../ebd/ebd110.htm#004">ear</a>, O earth: for the LORD hath spoken, I have nourished and brought up children, and they have rebelled against me.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> The <a href="../ebd/ebd281.htm#007">ox</a> knoweth his owner, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd035.htm#001">ass</a> his master's crib: but <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> doth not know, my people doth not consider.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Ah sinful nation, a people laden with iniquity, a seed of evildoers, children that are corrupters: they have forsaken the LORD, they have provoked the Holy One of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> unto <a href="../ebd/ebd024.htm#002">anger</a>, they are gone away backward.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Why should ye be stricken any more? ye will revolt more and more: the whole head is sick, and the whole heart faint.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> From the sole of the foot even unto the head there is no soundness in it; but wounds, and bruises, and putrifying sores: they have not been closed, neither bound up, neither mollified with <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#007">ointment</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Your country is desolate, your cities are burned with fire: your land, strangers devour it in your presence, and it is desolate, as overthrown by strangers.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And the daughter of <a href="../ebd/ebd394.htm#002">Zion</a> is left as a <a href="../ebd/ebd091.htm#001">cottage</a> in a vineyard, as a <a href="../ebd/ebd231.htm#002">lodge</a> in a garden of <a href="../ebd/ebd093.htm#008">cucumbers</a>, as a besieged city.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> Except the LORD of hosts had left unto us a very small remnant, we should have been as <a href="../ebd/ebd347.htm#004">Sodom</a>, and we should have been like unto <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#007">Gomorrah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> Hear the word of the LORD, ye rulers of <a href="../ebd/ebd347.htm#004">Sodom</a>; give <a href="../ebd/ebd110.htm#004">ear</a> unto the law of our God, ye people of <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#007">Gomorrah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> To what purpose is the multitude of your sacrifices unto me? saith the LORD: I am full of the burnt offerings of rams, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#005">fat</a> of fed beasts; and I delight not in the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> of bullocks, or of lambs, or of he goats.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> When ye come to appear before me, who hath required this at your hand, to tread my courts?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> Bring no more vain oblations; <a href="../ebd/ebd188.htm#002">incense</a> is an <a href="../ebd/ebd005.htm#003">abomination</a> unto me; the new moons and sabbaths, the <a href="../ebd/ebd069.htm#007">calling</a> of assemblies, I cannot away with; it is iniquity, even the solemn meeting.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> Your new moons and your appointed feasts my soul hateth: they are a trouble unto me; I am weary to bear them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And when ye spread forth your hands, I will hide mine eyes from you: yea, when ye make many prayers, I will not hear: your hands are full of <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> Wash you, make you <a href="../ebd/ebd084.htm#005">clean</a>; put away the evil of your doings from before mine eyes; cease to do evil;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> Learn to do well; seek judgment, relieve the oppressed, <a href="../ebd/ebd213.htm#005">judge</a> the fatherless, plead for the widow.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> Come now, and let us reason together, saith the LORD: though your sins be as <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#005">scarlet</a>, they shall be as white as <a href="../ebd/ebd347.htm#000">snow</a>; though they be red like <a href="../ebd/ebd092.htm#007">crimson</a>, they shall be as <a href="../ebd/ebd383.htm#005">wool</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> If ye be willing and obedient, ye shall eat the good of the land:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> But if ye refuse and rebel, ye shall be devoured with the <a href="../ebd/ebd354.htm#003">sword</a>: for the mouth of the LORD hath spoken it.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> How is the <a href="../ebd/ebd130.htm#005">faithful</a> city become an <a href="../ebd/ebd164.htm#005">harlot</a>! it was full of judgment; <a href="../ebd/ebd313.htm#004">righteousness</a> lodged in it; but now murderers.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> Thy <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#000">silver</a> is become <a href="../ebd/ebd108.htm#004">dross</a>, thy <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#000">wine</a> mixed with water:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> Thy princes are rebellious, and companions of thieves: every one loveth gifts, and followeth after rewards: they <a href="../ebd/ebd213.htm#005">judge</a> not the fatherless, neither doth the cause of the widow come unto them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> Therefore saith the LORD, the LORD of hosts, the mighty One of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, Ah, I will ease me of mine adversaries, and avenge me of mine enemies:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> And I will turn my hand upon thee, and purely purge away thy <a href="../ebd/ebd108.htm#004">dross</a>, and take away all thy <a href="../ebd/ebd367.htm#005">tin</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> And I will restore thy judges as at the first, and thy counsellors as at the beginning: afterward thou shalt be called, The city of <a href="../ebd/ebd313.htm#004">righteousness</a>, the <a href="../ebd/ebd130.htm#005">faithful</a> city.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd394.htm#002">Zion</a> shall be redeemed with judgment, and her converts with <a href="../ebd/ebd313.htm#004">righteousness</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="028">28</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd102.htm#004">destruction</a> of the transgressors and of the sinners shall be together, and they that forsake the LORD shall be consumed.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="029">29</a> For they shall be ashamed of the oaks which ye have desired, and ye shall be confounded for the <a href="../ebd/ebd143.htm#001">gardens</a> that ye have <a href="../ebd/ebd081.htm#008">chosen</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="030">30</a> For ye shall be as an <a href="../ebd/ebd275.htm#009">oak</a> whose <a href="../ebd/ebd225.htm#004">leaf</a> fadeth, and as a garden that hath no water.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="031">31</a> And the strong shall be as <a href="../ebd/ebd371.htm#001">tow</a>, and the maker of it as a spark, and they shall both burn together, and none shall quench them.<br>
 </p>
