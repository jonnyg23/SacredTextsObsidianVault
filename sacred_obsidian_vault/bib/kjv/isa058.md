 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Isaiah Chapter 58</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Cry aloud, spare not, lift up thy voice like a trumpet, and shew my people their transgression, and the house of <a href="../ebd/ebd194.htm#007">Jacob</a> their sins.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Yet they seek me daily, and delight to know my ways, as a nation that did <a href="../ebd/ebd313.htm#004">righteousness</a>, and forsook not the ordinance of their God: they ask of me the ordinances of <a href="../ebd/ebd214.htm#007">justice</a>; they take delight in approaching to God.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Wherefore have we fasted, say they, and thou seest not? wherefore have we afflicted our soul, and thou takest no knowledge? Behold, in the day of your <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#004">fast</a> ye find pleasure, and exact all your labours.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Behold, ye <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#004">fast</a> for strife and debate, and to smite with the fist of wickedness: ye shall not <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#004">fast</a> as ye do this day, to make your voice to be heard on high.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Is it such a <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#004">fast</a> that I have <a href="../ebd/ebd081.htm#008">chosen</a>? a day for a man to afflict his soul? is it to <a href="../ebd/ebd063.htm#003">bow</a> down his head as a <a href="../ebd/ebd066.htm#004">bulrush</a>, and to spread <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#001">sackcloth</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd034.htm#000">ashes</a> under him? wilt thou call this a <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#004">fast</a>, and an acceptable day to the LORD?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Is not this the <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#004">fast</a> that I have <a href="../ebd/ebd081.htm#008">chosen</a>? to loose the <a href="../ebd/ebd043.htm#003">bands</a> of wickedness, to undo the heavy burdens, and to let the oppressed go free, and that ye break every <a href="../ebd/ebd384.htm#009">yoke</a>?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Is it not to deal thy <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#006">bread</a> to the hungry, and that thou bring the <a href="../ebd/ebd298.htm#006">poor</a> that are cast out to thy house? when thou seest the <a href="../ebd/ebd266.htm#003">naked</a>, that thou cover him; and that thou hide not thyself from thine own <a href="../ebd/ebd135.htm#007">flesh</a>?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> Then shall thy light break forth as the morning, and thine health shall spring forth speedily: and thy <a href="../ebd/ebd313.htm#004">righteousness</a> shall go before thee; the <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a> of the LORD shall be thy <a href="../ebd/ebd311.htm#004">rereward</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> Then shalt thou call, and the LORD shall answer; thou shalt cry, and he shall say, Here I am. If thou take away from the midst of thee the <a href="../ebd/ebd384.htm#009">yoke</a>, the putting forth of the finger, and speaking vanity;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And if thou draw out thy soul to the hungry, and satisfy the afflicted soul; then shall thy light rise in obscurity, and thy <a href="../ebd/ebd097.htm#008">darkness</a> be as the noon day:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And the LORD shall guide thee continually, and satisfy thy soul in <a href="../ebd/ebd108.htm#005">drought</a>, and make <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#005">fat</a> thy bones: and thou shalt be like a watered garden, and like a spring of water, whose waters fail not.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And they that shall be of thee shall build the old waste places: thou shalt raise up the foundations of many generations; and thou shalt be called, The repairer of the <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#005">breach</a>, The restorer of paths to dwell in.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> If thou turn away thy foot from the <a href="../ebd/ebd317.htm#003">sabbath</a>, from doing thy pleasure on my holy day; and call the <a href="../ebd/ebd317.htm#003">sabbath</a> a delight, the holy of the LORD, honourable; and shalt honour him, not doing thine own ways, nor finding thine own pleasure, nor speaking thine own words:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> Then shalt thou delight thyself in the LORD; and I will cause thee to ride upon the high places of the earth, and feed thee with the heritage of <a href="../ebd/ebd194.htm#007">Jacob</a> thy father: for the mouth of the LORD hath spoken it.<br>
 </p>
