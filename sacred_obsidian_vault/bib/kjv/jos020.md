 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Joshua Chapter 20</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> The LORD also spake unto <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#006">Joshua</a>, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Speak to the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, saying, Appoint out for you cities of refuge, whereof I spake unto you by the hand of <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> That the slayer that killeth any person unawares and unwittingly may flee thither: and they shall be your refuge from the avenger of <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And when he that doth flee unto one of those cities shall stand at the entering of the <a href="../ebd/ebd143.htm#008">gate</a> of the city, and shall declare his cause in the ears of the elders of that city, they shall take him into the city unto them, and give him a place, that he may dwell among them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And if the avenger of <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> pursue after him, then they shall not deliver the slayer up into his hand; because he smote his neighbour unwittingly, and hated him not beforetime.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And he shall dwell in that city, until he stand before the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> for judgment, and until the death of the high <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> that shall be in those days: then shall the slayer return, and come unto his own city, and unto his own house, unto the city from whence he fled.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And they appointed <a href="../ebd/ebd216.htm#006">Kedesh</a> in <a href="../ebd/ebd141.htm#008">Galilee</a> in <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#007">mount</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd266.htm#006">Naphtali</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd333.htm#003">Shechem</a> in <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#007">mount</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd123.htm#000">Ephraim</a>, and Kirjatharba, which is <a href="../ebd/ebd171.htm#004">Hebron</a>, in the mountain of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And on the other side <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#004">Jordan</a> by <a href="../ebd/ebd203.htm#008">Jericho</a> eastward, they assigned <a href="../ebd/ebd058.htm#001">Bezer</a> in the <a href="../ebd/ebd381.htm#005">wilderness</a> upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd297.htm#002">plain</a> out of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd311.htm#009">Reuben</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd307.htm#002">Ramoth</a> in <a href="../ebd/ebd148.htm#009">Gilead</a> out of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd140.htm#007">Gad</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#000">Golan</a> in <a href="../ebd/ebd046.htm#003">Bashan</a> out of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd240.htm#002">Manasseh</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> These were the cities appointed for all the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, and for the <a href="../ebd/ebd351.htm#009">stranger</a> that sojourneth among them, that whosoever killeth any person at unawares might flee thither, and not die by the hand of the avenger of <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a>, until he stood before the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>.<br>
 </p>
