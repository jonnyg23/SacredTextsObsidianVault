 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Haggai Chapter 1</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> In the second year of <a href="../ebd/ebd097.htm#007">Darius</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a>, in the sixth month, in the first day of the month, came the word of the LORD by Haggai the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#008">prophet</a> unto <a href="../ebd/ebd391.htm#008">Zerubbabel</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd332.htm#003">Shealtiel</a>, governor of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>, and to <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#006">Joshua</a> the son of Josedech, the high <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a>, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Thus speaketh the LORD of hosts, saying, This people say, The time is not come, the time that the LORD's house should be built.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Then came the word of the LORD by Haggai the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#008">prophet</a>, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Is it time for you, O ye, to dwell in your cieled houses, and this house lie waste?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Now therefore thus saith the LORD of hosts; Consider your ways.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Ye have sown much, and bring in little; ye eat, but ye have not enough; ye <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a>, but ye are not filled with <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a>; ye clothe you, but there is none warm; and he that earneth <a href="../ebd/ebd378.htm#004">wages</a> earneth <a href="../ebd/ebd378.htm#004">wages</a> to put it into a <a href="../ebd/ebd041.htm#008">bag</a> with holes.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Thus saith the LORD of hosts; Consider your ways.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> Go up to the mountain, and bring <a href="../ebd/ebd383.htm#003">wood</a>, and build the house; and I will take pleasure in it, and I will be glorified, saith the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> Ye looked for much, and, lo it came to little; and when ye brought it home, I did blow upon it. Why? saith the LORD of hosts. Because of mine house that is waste, and ye run every man unto his own house.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> Therefore the <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#006">heaven</a> over you is stayed from <a href="../ebd/ebd102.htm#008">dew</a>, and the earth is stayed from her <a href="../ebd/ebd139.htm#000">fruit</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And I called for a <a href="../ebd/ebd108.htm#005">drought</a> upon the land, and upon the mountains, and upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd090.htm#006">corn</a>, and upon the new <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#000">wine</a>, and upon the oil, and upon that which the ground bringeth forth, and upon men, and upon <a href="../ebd/ebd074.htm#002">cattle</a>, and upon all the labour of the hands.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> Then <a href="../ebd/ebd391.htm#008">Zerubbabel</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd332.htm#003">Shealtiel</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#006">Joshua</a> the son of Josedech, the high <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a>, with all the remnant of the people, obeyed the voice of the LORD their God, and the words of Haggai the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#008">prophet</a>, as the LORD their God had sent him, and the people did fear before the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> Then spake Haggai the LORD's <a href="../ebd/ebd251.htm#005">messenger</a> in the LORD's message unto the people, saying, I am with you, saith the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> And the LORD stirred up the <a href="../ebd/ebd349.htm#009">spirit</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd391.htm#008">Zerubbabel</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd332.htm#003">Shealtiel</a>, governor of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd349.htm#009">spirit</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#006">Joshua</a> the son of Josedech, the high <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd349.htm#009">spirit</a> of all the remnant of the people; and they came and did work in the house of the LORD of hosts, their God,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> In the four and twentieth day of the sixth month, in the second year of <a href="../ebd/ebd097.htm#007">Darius</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a>.<br>
 </p>
