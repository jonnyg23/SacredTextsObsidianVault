 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Exodus Chapter 29</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> And this is the thing that thou shalt do unto them to <a href="../ebd/ebd160.htm#001">hallow</a> them, to <a href="../ebd/ebd255.htm#007">minister</a> unto me in the priest's office: Take one young <a href="../ebd/ebd066.htm#003">bullock</a>, and two rams without <a href="../ebd/ebd060.htm#007">blemish</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> And unleavened <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#006">bread</a>, and cakes unleavened tempered with oil, and <a href="../ebd/ebd378.htm#003">wafers</a> unleavened anointed with oil: of wheaten <a href="../ebd/ebd136.htm#001">flour</a> shalt thou make them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And thou shalt put them into one <a href="../ebd/ebd046.htm#009">basket</a>, and bring them in the <a href="../ebd/ebd046.htm#009">basket</a>, with the <a href="../ebd/ebd066.htm#003">bullock</a> and the two rams.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> and his sons thou shalt bring unto the door of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>, and shalt wash them with water.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And thou shalt take the garments, and put upon <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd085.htm#004">coat</a>, and the robe of the <a href="../ebd/ebd122.htm#008">ephod</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd122.htm#008">ephod</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#007">breastplate</a>, and gird him with the curious <a href="../ebd/ebd149.htm#005">girdle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd122.htm#008">ephod</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And thou shalt put the <a href="../ebd/ebd257.htm#006">mitre</a> upon his head, and put the holy <a href="../ebd/ebd093.htm#001">crown</a> upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd257.htm#006">mitre</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Then shalt thou take the anointing oil, and pour it upon his head, and <a href="../ebd/ebd024.htm#008">anoint</a> him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And thou shalt bring his sons, and put coats upon them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And thou shalt gird them with girdles, <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> and his sons, and put the bonnets on them: and the priest's office shall be theirs for a perpetual statute: and thou shalt consecrate <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> and his sons.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And thou shalt cause a <a href="../ebd/ebd066.htm#003">bullock</a> to be brought before the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>: and <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> and his sons shall put their hands upon the head of the <a href="../ebd/ebd066.htm#003">bullock</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And thou shalt kill the <a href="../ebd/ebd066.htm#003">bullock</a> before the LORD, by the door of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And thou shalt take of the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd066.htm#003">bullock</a>, and put it upon the horns of the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> with thy finger, and pour all the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> beside the bottom of the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> And thou shalt take all the <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#005">fat</a> that covereth the inwards, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd074.htm#003">caul</a> that is above the <a href="../ebd/ebd230.htm#004">liver</a>, and the two kidneys, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#005">fat</a> that is upon them, and burn them upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> But the <a href="../ebd/ebd135.htm#007">flesh</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd066.htm#003">bullock</a>, and his skin, and his <a href="../ebd/ebd109.htm#003">dung</a>, shalt thou burn with fire without the <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#002">camp</a>: it is a <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> Thou shalt also take one <a href="../ebd/ebd306.htm#003">ram</a>; and <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> and his sons shall put their hands upon the head of the <a href="../ebd/ebd306.htm#003">ram</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> And thou shalt slay the <a href="../ebd/ebd306.htm#003">ram</a>, and thou shalt take his <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a>, and sprinkle it round about upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> And thou shalt cut the <a href="../ebd/ebd306.htm#003">ram</a> in <a href="../ebd/ebd295.htm#002">pieces</a>, and wash the inwards of him, and his legs, and put them unto his <a href="../ebd/ebd295.htm#002">pieces</a>, and unto his head.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> And thou shalt burn the whole <a href="../ebd/ebd306.htm#003">ram</a> upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>: it is a burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> unto the LORD: it is a sweet savour, an <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> made by fire unto the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> And thou shalt take the other <a href="../ebd/ebd306.htm#003">ram</a>; and <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> and his sons shall put their hands upon the head of the <a href="../ebd/ebd306.htm#003">ram</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> Then shalt thou kill the <a href="../ebd/ebd306.htm#003">ram</a>, and take of his <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a>, and put it upon the tip of the right <a href="../ebd/ebd110.htm#004">ear</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a>, and upon the tip of the right <a href="../ebd/ebd110.htm#004">ear</a> of his sons, and upon the thumb of their right hand, and upon the great toe of their right foot, and sprinkle the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> round about.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> And thou shalt take of the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> that is upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>, and of the anointing oil, and sprinkle it upon <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a>, and upon his garments, and upon his sons, and upon the garments of his sons with him: and he shall be hallowed, and his garments, and his sons, and his sons' garments with him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> Also thou shalt take of the <a href="../ebd/ebd306.htm#003">ram</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#005">fat</a> and the rump, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#005">fat</a> that covereth the inwards, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd074.htm#003">caul</a> above the <a href="../ebd/ebd230.htm#004">liver</a>, and the two kidneys, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#005">fat</a> that is upon them, and the right shoulder; for it is a <a href="../ebd/ebd306.htm#003">ram</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#007">consecration</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> And one loaf of <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#006">bread</a>, and one <a href="../ebd/ebd068.htm#009">cake</a> of oiled <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#006">bread</a>, and one wafer out of the <a href="../ebd/ebd046.htm#009">basket</a> of the unleavened <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#006">bread</a> that is before the LORD:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> And thou shalt put all in the hands of <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a>, and in the hands of his sons; and shalt wave them for a wave <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> before the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> And thou shalt receive them of their hands, and burn them upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> for a burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, for a sweet savour before the LORD: it is an <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> made by fire unto the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> And thou shalt take the breast of the <a href="../ebd/ebd306.htm#003">ram</a> of Aaron's <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#007">consecration</a>, and wave it for a wave <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> before the LORD: and it shall be thy part.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> And thou shalt sanctify the breast of the wave <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, and the shoulder of the heave <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, which is waved, and which is heaved up, of the <a href="../ebd/ebd306.htm#003">ram</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#007">consecration</a>, even of that which is for <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a>, and of that which is for his sons:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="028">28</a> And it shall be Aaron's and his sons' by a statute for ever from the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>: for it is an heave <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>: and it shall be an heave <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> from the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#002">sacrifice</a> of their peace offerings, even their heave <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> unto the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="029">29</a> And the holy garments of <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> shall be his sons' after him, to be anointed therein, and to be consecrated in them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="030">30</a> And that son that is <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> in his stead shall put them on <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> days, when he cometh into the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> to <a href="../ebd/ebd255.htm#007">minister</a> in the holy place.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="031">31</a> And thou shalt take the <a href="../ebd/ebd306.htm#003">ram</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#007">consecration</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd326.htm#001">seethe</a> his <a href="../ebd/ebd135.htm#007">flesh</a> in the holy place.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="032">32</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> and his sons shall eat the <a href="../ebd/ebd135.htm#007">flesh</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd306.htm#003">ram</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#006">bread</a> that is in the <a href="../ebd/ebd046.htm#009">basket</a> by the door of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="033">33</a> And they shall eat those things wherewith the <a href="../ebd/ebd036.htm#004">atonement</a> was made, to consecrate and to sanctify them: but a <a href="../ebd/ebd351.htm#009">stranger</a> shall not eat thereof, because they are holy.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="034">34</a> And if ought of the <a href="../ebd/ebd135.htm#007">flesh</a> of the consecrations, or of the <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#006">bread</a>, remain unto the morning, then thou shalt burn the remainder with fire: it shall not be eaten, because it is holy.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="035">35</a> And thus shalt thou do unto <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a>, and to his sons, according to all things which I have commanded thee: <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> days shalt thou consecrate them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="036">36</a> And thou shalt offer every day a <a href="../ebd/ebd066.htm#003">bullock</a> for a <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> for <a href="../ebd/ebd036.htm#004">atonement</a>: and thou shalt cleanse the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>, when thou hast made an <a href="../ebd/ebd036.htm#004">atonement</a> for it, and thou shalt <a href="../ebd/ebd024.htm#008">anoint</a> it, to sanctify it.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="037">37</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">Seven</a> days thou shalt make an <a href="../ebd/ebd036.htm#004">atonement</a> for the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>, and sanctify it; and it shall be an <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> most holy: whatsoever toucheth the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> shall be holy.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="038">38</a> Now this is that which thou shalt offer upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>; two lambs of the first year day by day continually.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="039">39</a> The one <a href="../ebd/ebd223.htm#003">lamb</a> thou shalt offer in the morning; and the other <a href="../ebd/ebd223.htm#003">lamb</a> thou shalt offer at even:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="040">40</a> And with the one <a href="../ebd/ebd223.htm#003">lamb</a> a tenth deal of <a href="../ebd/ebd136.htm#001">flour</a> mingled with the fourth part of an hin of beaten oil; and the fourth part of an hin of <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#000">wine</a> for a <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="041">41</a> And the other <a href="../ebd/ebd223.htm#003">lamb</a> thou shalt offer at even, and shalt do thereto according to the meat <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> of the morning, and according to the <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> thereof, for a sweet savour, an <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> made by fire unto the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="042">42</a> This shall be a continual burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> throughout your generations at the door of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> before the LORD: where I will meet you, to speak there unto thee.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="043">43</a> And there I will meet with the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> shall be sanctified by my <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="044">44</a> And I will sanctify the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>: I will sanctify also both <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> and his sons, to <a href="../ebd/ebd255.htm#007">minister</a> to me in the priest's office.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="045">45</a> And I will dwell among the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, and will be their God.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="046">46</a> And they shall know that I am the LORD their God, that brought them forth out of the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a>, that I may dwell among them: I am the LORD their God.<br>
 </p>
