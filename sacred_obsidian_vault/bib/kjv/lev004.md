 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Leviticus Chapter 4</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> And the LORD spake unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Speak unto the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, saying, If a soul shall <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> through ignorance against any of the commandments of the LORD concerning things which ought not to be done, and shall do against any of them:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> If the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> that is anointed do <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> according to the <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> of the people; then let him bring for his <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a>, which he hath sinned, a young <a href="../ebd/ebd066.htm#003">bullock</a> without <a href="../ebd/ebd060.htm#007">blemish</a> unto the LORD for a <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And he shall bring the <a href="../ebd/ebd066.htm#003">bullock</a> unto the door of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> before the LORD; and shall lay his hand upon the bullock's head, and kill the <a href="../ebd/ebd066.htm#003">bullock</a> before the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> that is anointed shall take of the bullock's <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a>, and bring it to the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> shall dip his finger in the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a>, and sprinkle of the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> times before the LORD, before the vail of the <a href="../ebd/ebd321.htm#005">sanctuary</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> shall put some of the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> upon the horns of the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> of sweet <a href="../ebd/ebd188.htm#002">incense</a> before the LORD, which is in the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>; and shall pour all the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd066.htm#003">bullock</a> at the bottom of the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> of the burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, which is at the door of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And he shall take off from it all the <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#005">fat</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd066.htm#003">bullock</a> for the <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>; the <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#005">fat</a> that covereth the inwards, and all the <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#005">fat</a> that is upon the inwards,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And the two kidneys, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#005">fat</a> that is upon them, which is by the flanks, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd074.htm#003">caul</a> above the <a href="../ebd/ebd230.htm#004">liver</a>, with the kidneys, it shall he take away,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> As it was taken off from the <a href="../ebd/ebd066.htm#003">bullock</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#002">sacrifice</a> of peace offerings: and the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> shall burn them upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> of the burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And the skin of the <a href="../ebd/ebd066.htm#003">bullock</a>, and all his <a href="../ebd/ebd135.htm#007">flesh</a>, with his head, and with his legs, and his inwards, and his <a href="../ebd/ebd109.htm#003">dung</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> Even the whole <a href="../ebd/ebd066.htm#003">bullock</a> shall he carry forth without the <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#002">camp</a> unto a <a href="../ebd/ebd084.htm#005">clean</a> place, where the <a href="../ebd/ebd034.htm#000">ashes</a> are poured out, and burn him on the <a href="../ebd/ebd383.htm#003">wood</a> with fire: where the <a href="../ebd/ebd034.htm#000">ashes</a> are poured out shall he be burnt.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> And if the whole <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> through ignorance, and the thing be hid from the eyes of the assembly, and they have done somewhat against any of the commandments of the LORD concerning things which should not be done, and are guilty;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> When the <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a>, which they have sinned against it, is known, then the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> shall offer a young <a href="../ebd/ebd066.htm#003">bullock</a> for the <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a>, and bring him before the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And the elders of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> shall lay their hands upon the head of the <a href="../ebd/ebd066.htm#003">bullock</a> before the LORD: and the <a href="../ebd/ebd066.htm#003">bullock</a> shall be killed before the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> that is anointed shall bring of the bullock's <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> to the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> shall dip his finger in some of the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a>, and sprinkle it <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> times before the LORD, even before the vail.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> And he shall put some of the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> upon the horns of the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> which is before the LORD, that is in the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>, and shall pour out all the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> at the bottom of the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> of the burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, which is at the door of the <a href="../ebd/ebd356.htm#003">tabernacle</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> And he shall take all his <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#005">fat</a> from him, and burn it upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> And he shall do with the <a href="../ebd/ebd066.htm#003">bullock</a> as he did with the <a href="../ebd/ebd066.htm#003">bullock</a> for a <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, so shall he do with this: and the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> shall make an <a href="../ebd/ebd036.htm#004">atonement</a> for them, and it shall be forgiven them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> And he shall carry forth the <a href="../ebd/ebd066.htm#003">bullock</a> without the <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#002">camp</a>, and burn him as he burned the first <a href="../ebd/ebd066.htm#003">bullock</a>: it is a <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> for the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> When a ruler hath sinned, and done somewhat through ignorance against any of the commandments of the LORD his God concerning things which should not be done, and is guilty;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> Or if his <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a>, wherein he hath sinned, come to his knowledge; he shall bring his <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, a kid of the goats, a male without <a href="../ebd/ebd060.htm#007">blemish</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> And he shall lay his hand upon the head of the <a href="../ebd/ebd151.htm#001">goat</a>, and kill it in the place where they kill the burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> before the LORD: it is a <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> shall take of the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> with his finger, and put it upon the horns of the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> of burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, and shall pour out his <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> at the bottom of the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> of burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> And he shall burn all his <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#005">fat</a> upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>, as the <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#005">fat</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#002">sacrifice</a> of peace offerings: and the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> shall make an <a href="../ebd/ebd036.htm#004">atonement</a> for him as concerning his <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a>, and it shall be forgiven him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> And if any one of the common people <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> through ignorance, while he doeth somewhat against any of the commandments of the LORD concerning things which ought not to be done, and be guilty;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="028">28</a> Or if his <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a>, which he hath sinned, come to his knowledge: then he shall bring his <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, a kid of the goats, a female without <a href="../ebd/ebd060.htm#007">blemish</a>, for his <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> which he hath sinned.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="029">29</a> And he shall lay his hand upon the head of the <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, and slay the <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> in the place of the burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="030">30</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> shall take of the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> thereof with his finger, and put it upon the horns of the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> of burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, and shall pour out all the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> thereof at the bottom of the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="031">31</a> And he shall take away all the <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#005">fat</a> thereof, as the <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#005">fat</a> is taken away from off the <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#002">sacrifice</a> of peace offerings; and the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> shall burn it upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> for a sweet savour unto the LORD; and the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> shall make an <a href="../ebd/ebd036.htm#004">atonement</a> for him, and it shall be forgiven him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="032">32</a> And if he bring a <a href="../ebd/ebd223.htm#003">lamb</a> for a <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, he shall bring it a female without <a href="../ebd/ebd060.htm#007">blemish</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="033">33</a> And he shall lay his hand upon the head of the <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, and slay it for a <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> in the place where they kill the burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="034">34</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> shall take of the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> with his finger, and put it upon the horns of the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> of burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, and shall pour out all the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> thereof at the bottom of the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="035">35</a> And he shall take away all the <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#005">fat</a> thereof, as the <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#005">fat</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd223.htm#003">lamb</a> is taken away from the <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#002">sacrifice</a> of the peace offerings; and the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> shall burn them upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>, according to the offerings made by fire unto the LORD: and the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a> shall make an <a href="../ebd/ebd036.htm#004">atonement</a> for his <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> that he hath committed, and it shall be forgiven him.<br>
 </p>
