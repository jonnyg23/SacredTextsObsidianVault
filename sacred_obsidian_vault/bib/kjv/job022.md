 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Job Chapter 22</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Then <a href="../ebd/ebd117.htm#005">Eliphaz</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd361.htm#002">Temanite</a> answered and said,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Can a man be profitable unto God, as he that is <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#004">wise</a> may be profitable unto himself?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Is it any pleasure to the Almighty, that thou art righteous? or is it gain to him, that thou makest thy ways perfect?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Will he reprove thee for fear of thee? will he enter with thee into judgment?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Is not thy wickedness great? and thine iniquities infinite?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> For thou hast taken a <a href="../ebd/ebd297.htm#005">pledge</a> from thy brother for nought, and stripped the <a href="../ebd/ebd266.htm#003">naked</a> of their clothing.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Thou hast not given water to the weary to <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a>, and thou hast withholden <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#006">bread</a> from the hungry.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> But as for the mighty man, he had the earth; and the honourable man dwelt in it.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> Thou hast sent <a href="../ebd/ebd381.htm#003">widows</a> away empty, and the arms of the fatherless have been broken.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> Therefore snares are round about thee, and sudden fear troubleth thee;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> Or <a href="../ebd/ebd097.htm#008">darkness</a>, that thou canst not see; and abundance of waters cover thee.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> Is not God in the height of <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#006">heaven</a>? and behold the height of the <a href="../ebd/ebd350.htm#008">stars</a>, how high they are!<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> And thou sayest, How doth God know? can he <a href="../ebd/ebd213.htm#005">judge</a> through the dark <a href="../ebd/ebd085.htm#001">cloud</a>?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> Thick clouds are a covering to him, that he seeth not; and he walketh in the <a href="../ebd/ebd083.htm#006">circuit</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#006">heaven</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> Hast thou marked the old way which wicked men have trodden?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> Which were cut down out of time, whose foundation was overflown with a <a href="../ebd/ebd136.htm#000">flood</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> Which said unto God, Depart from us: and what can the Almighty do for them?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> Yet he filled their houses with good things: but the counsel of the wicked is far from me.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> The righteous see it, and are glad: and the innocent laugh them to scorn.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> Whereas our substance is not cut down, but the remnant of them the fire consumeth.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> Acquaint now thyself with him, and be at peace: thereby good shall come unto thee.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> Receive, I pray thee, the law from his mouth, and lay up his words in thine heart.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> If thou return to the Almighty, thou shalt be built up, thou shalt put away iniquity far from thy tabernacles.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> Then shalt thou lay up <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a> as <a href="../ebd/ebd109.htm#008">dust</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd279.htm#007">Ophir</a> as the stones of the brooks.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> Yea, the Almighty shall be thy defence, and thou shalt have plenty of <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#000">silver</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> For then shalt thou have thy delight in the Almighty, and shalt lift up thy face unto God.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> Thou shalt make thy <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#000">prayer</a> unto him, and he shall hear thee, and thou shalt pay thy <a href="../ebd/ebd378.htm#001">vows</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="028">28</a> Thou shalt also decree a thing, and it shall be established unto thee: and the light shall shine upon thy ways.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="029">29</a> When men are cast down, then thou shalt say, There is lifting up; and he shall save the humble person.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="030">30</a> He shall deliver the <a href="../ebd/ebd190.htm#009">island</a> of the innocent: and it is delivered by the pureness of thine hands.<br>
 </p>
