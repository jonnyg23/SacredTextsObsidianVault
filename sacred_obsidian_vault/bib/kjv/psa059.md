 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 59</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Deliver me from mine enemies, O my God: defend me from them that rise up against me.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Deliver me from the workers of iniquity, and save me from bloody men.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> For, lo, they lie in wait for my soul: the mighty are gathered against me; not for my transgression, nor for my <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a>, O LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> They run and prepare themselves without my fault: awake to help me, and behold.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Thou therefore, O LORD God of hosts, the God of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, awake to visit all the <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#005">heathen</a>: be not merciful to any wicked transgressors. <a href="../ebd/ebd326.htm#008">Selah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> They return at evening: they make a noise like a dog, and go round about the city.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Behold, they belch out with their mouth: swords are in their lips: for who, say they, doth hear?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> But thou, O LORD, shalt laugh at them; thou shalt have all the <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#005">heathen</a> in derision.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> Because of his strength will I wait upon thee: for God is my defence.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> The God of my <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> shall prevent me: God shall let me see my desire upon mine enemies.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> Slay them not, lest my people forget: scatter them by thy power; and bring them down, O Lord our <a href="../ebd/ebd337.htm#001">shield</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> For the <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a> of their mouth and the words of their lips let them even be taken in their pride: and for cursing and lying which they speak.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> Consume them in wrath, consume them, that they may not be: and let them know that God ruleth in <a href="../ebd/ebd194.htm#007">Jacob</a> unto the ends of the earth. <a href="../ebd/ebd326.htm#008">Selah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> And at evening let them return; and let them make a noise like a dog, and go round about the city.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> Let them wander up and down for meat, and grudge if they be not satisfied.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> But I will sing of thy power; yea, I will sing aloud of thy <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> in the morning: for thou hast been my defence and refuge in the day of my trouble.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> Unto thee, O my strength, will I sing: for God is my defence, and the God of my <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a>.<br>
 </p>
