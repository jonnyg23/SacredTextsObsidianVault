 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Exodus Chapter 17</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> And all the <a href="../ebd/ebd088.htm#004">congregation</a> of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> journeyed from the <a href="../ebd/ebd381.htm#005">wilderness</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">Sin</a>, after their journeys, according to the commandment of the LORD, and pitched in <a href="../ebd/ebd311.htm#002">Rephidim</a>: and there was no water for the people to <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Wherefore the people did chide with <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, and said, Give us water that we may <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a>. And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> said unto them, Why chide ye with me? wherefore do ye tempt the LORD?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And the people thirsted there for water; and the people murmured against <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, and said, Wherefore is this that thou hast brought us up out of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a>, to kill us and our children and our <a href="../ebd/ebd074.htm#002">cattle</a> with thirst?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> cried unto the LORD, saying, What shall I do unto this people? they be almost ready to <a href="../ebd/ebd351.htm#005">stone</a> me.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And the LORD said unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, Go on before the people, and take with thee of the elders of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>; and thy rod, wherewith thou smotest the river, take in thine hand, and go.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Behold, I will stand before thee there upon the <a href="../ebd/ebd315.htm#001">rock</a> in <a href="../ebd/ebd181.htm#009">Horeb</a>; and thou shalt smite the <a href="../ebd/ebd315.htm#001">rock</a>, and there shall come water out of it, that the people may <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a>. And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> did so in the sight of the elders of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And he called the name of the place <a href="../ebd/ebd243.htm#006">Massah</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#009">Meribah</a>, because of the chiding of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, and because they tempted the LORD, saying, Is the LORD among us, or not?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> Then came <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#009">Amalek</a>, and fought with <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> in <a href="../ebd/ebd311.htm#002">Rephidim</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> said unto <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#006">Joshua</a>, Choose us out men, and go out, fight with <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#009">Amalek</a>: to morrow I will stand on the top of the hill with the rod of God in mine hand.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> So <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#006">Joshua</a> did as <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> had said to him, and fought with <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#009">Amalek</a>: and <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd184.htm#009">Hur</a> went up to the top of the hill.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And it came to pass, when <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> held up his hand, that <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> prevailed: and when he let down his hand, <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#009">Amalek</a> prevailed.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> But <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> hands were heavy; and they took a <a href="../ebd/ebd351.htm#005">stone</a>, and put it under him, and he sat thereon; and <a href="../ebd/ebd000.htm#001">Aaron</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd184.htm#009">Hur</a> stayed up his hands, the one on the one side, and the other on the other side; and his hands were steady until the going down of the <a href="../ebd/ebd353.htm#001">sun</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#006">Joshua</a> discomfited <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#009">Amalek</a> and his people with the edge of the <a href="../ebd/ebd354.htm#003">sword</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> And the LORD said unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, Write this for a memorial in a book, and rehearse it in the ears of <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#006">Joshua</a>: for I will utterly put out the remembrance of <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#009">Amalek</a> from under <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#006">heaven</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> built an <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>, and called the name of it Jehovahnissi:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> For he said, Because the LORD hath sworn that the LORD will have war with <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#009">Amalek</a> from <a href="../ebd/ebd145.htm#008">generation</a> to <a href="../ebd/ebd145.htm#008">generation</a>.<br>
 </p>
