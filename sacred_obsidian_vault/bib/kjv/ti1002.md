 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: 1 Timothy Chapter 2</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> I exhort therefore, that, first of all, supplications, prayers, intercessions, and giving of thanks, be made for all men;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> For kings, and for all that are in authority; that we may lead a quiet and peaceable life in all <a href="../ebd/ebd151.htm#007">godliness</a> and honesty.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> For this is good and acceptable in the sight of God our <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#003">Saviour</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Who will have all men to be saved, and to come unto the knowledge of the truth.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> For there is one God, and one <a href="../ebd/ebd246.htm#002">mediator</a> between God and men, the man <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#006">Jesus</a>;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Who gave himself a <a href="../ebd/ebd307.htm#005">ransom</a> for all, to be testified in due time.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Whereunto I am ordained a preacher, and an <a href="../ebd/ebd026.htm#008">apostle</a>, (I speak the truth in <a href="../ebd/ebd082.htm#000">Christ</a>, and lie not;) a teacher of the <a href="../ebd/ebd146.htm#001">Gentiles</a> in faith and verity.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> I will therefore that men pray every where, lifting up holy hands, without wrath and doubting.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> In like manner also, that women adorn themselves in modest <a href="../ebd/ebd027.htm#000">apparel</a>, with shamefacedness and sobriety; not with broided hair, or <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a>, or pearls, or costly array;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> But (which becometh women professing <a href="../ebd/ebd151.htm#007">godliness</a>) with good works.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> Let the woman learn in silence with all subjection.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> But I suffer not a woman to teach, nor to usurp authority over the man, but to be in silence.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> For <a href="../ebd/ebd007.htm#007">Adam</a> was first formed, then <a href="../ebd/ebd127.htm#005">Eve</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd007.htm#007">Adam</a> was not deceived, but the woman being deceived was in the transgression.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> Notwithstanding she shall be saved in childbearing, if they continue in faith and <a href="../ebd/ebd077.htm#009">charity</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd180.htm#006">holiness</a> with sobriety.<br>
 </p>
