 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Numbers Chapter 34</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> And the LORD spake unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Command the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, and say unto them, When ye come into the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#005">Canaan</a>; (this is the land that shall fall unto you for an inheritance, even the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#005">Canaan</a> with the coasts thereof:)<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Then your south quarter shall be from the <a href="../ebd/ebd381.htm#005">wilderness</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd394.htm#000">Zin</a> along by the coast of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#001">Edom</a>, and your south border shall be the outmost coast of the <a href="../ebd/ebd319.htm#008">salt</a> sea eastward:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And your border shall turn from the south to the ascent of <a href="../ebd/ebd016.htm#004">Akrabbim</a>, and pass on to <a href="../ebd/ebd394.htm#000">Zin</a>: and the going forth thereof shall be from the south to Kadeshbarnea, and shall go on to Hazaraddar, and pass on to Azmon:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And the border shall fetch a compass from Azmon unto the river of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a>, and the goings out of it shall be at the sea.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And as for the western border, ye shall even have the great sea for a border: this shall be your west border.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And this shall be your north border: from the great sea ye shall point out for you <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#007">mount</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd181.htm#008">Hor</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> From <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#007">mount</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd181.htm#008">Hor</a> ye shall point out your border unto the entrance of <a href="../ebd/ebd160.htm#005">Hamath</a>; and the goings forth of the border shall be to <a href="../ebd/ebd389.htm#007">Zedad</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And the border shall go on to <a href="../ebd/ebd394.htm#006">Ziphron</a>, and the goings out of it shall be at Hazarenan: this shall be your north border.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And ye shall point out your <a href="../ebd/ebd111.htm#000">east</a> border from Hazarenan to <a href="../ebd/ebd335.htm#004">Shepham</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And the coast shall go down from <a href="../ebd/ebd335.htm#004">Shepham</a> to <a href="../ebd/ebd313.htm#002">Riblah</a>, on the <a href="../ebd/ebd111.htm#000">east</a> side of Ain; and the border shall descend, and shall reach unto the side of the sea of <a href="../ebd/ebd081.htm#000">Chinnereth</a> eastward:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And the border shall go down to <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#004">Jordan</a>, and the goings out of it shall be at the <a href="../ebd/ebd319.htm#008">salt</a> sea: this shall be your land with the coasts thereof round about.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> commanded the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, saying, This is the land which ye shall inherit by lot, which the LORD commanded to give unto the nine tribes, and to the half <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> For the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd311.htm#009">Reuben</a> according to the house of their fathers, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd140.htm#007">Gad</a> according to the house of their fathers, have received their inheritance; and half the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd240.htm#002">Manasseh</a> have received their inheritance:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> The two tribes and the half <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> have received their inheritance on this side <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#004">Jordan</a> near <a href="../ebd/ebd203.htm#008">Jericho</a> eastward, toward the sunrising.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> And the LORD spake unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, saying,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> These are the names of the men which shall divide the land unto you: <a href="../ebd/ebd115.htm#000">Eleazar</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#003">priest</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#006">Joshua</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd275.htm#006">Nun</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> And ye shall take one <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#004">prince</a> of every <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a>, to divide the land by inheritance.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> And the names of the men are these: Of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd069.htm#003">Caleb</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd203.htm#004">Jephunneh</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> And of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#002">Simeon</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd335.htm#000">Shemuel</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd020.htm#007">Ammihud</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> Of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd052.htm#008">Benjamin</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd116.htm#005">Elidad</a> the son of Chislon.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#004">prince</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd096.htm#009">Dan</a>, Bukki the son of Jogli.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> The <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#004">prince</a> of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#005">Joseph</a>, for the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd240.htm#002">Manasseh</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd163.htm#003">Hanniel</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd122.htm#008">Ephod</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#004">prince</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd123.htm#000">Ephraim</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd217.htm#001">Kemuel</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd339.htm#006">Shiphtan</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#004">prince</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd389.htm#003">Zebulun</a>, Elizaphan the son of Parnach.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#004">prince</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#002">Issachar</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd283.htm#006">Paltiel</a> the son of Azzan.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#004">prince</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd033.htm#008">Asher</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd013.htm#009">Ahihud</a> the son of Shelomi.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="028">28</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#004">prince</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd372.htm#004">tribe</a> of the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd266.htm#006">Naphtali</a>, <a href="../ebd/ebd287.htm#009">Pedahel</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd020.htm#007">Ammihud</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="029">29</a> These are they whom the LORD commanded to divide the inheritance unto the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> in the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd070.htm#005">Canaan</a>.<br>
 </p>
