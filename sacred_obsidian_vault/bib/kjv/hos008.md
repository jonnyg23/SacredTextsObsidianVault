 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Hosea Chapter 8</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Set the trumpet to thy mouth. He shall come as an <a href="../ebd/ebd110.htm#003">eagle</a> against the house of the LORD, because they have transgressed my <a href="../ebd/ebd091.htm#008">covenant</a>, and trespassed against my law.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> shall cry unto me, My God, we know thee.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> hath cast off the thing that is good: the enemy shall pursue him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> They have set up kings, but not by me: they have made princes, and I knew it not: of their <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#000">silver</a> and their <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a> have they made them idols, that they may be cut off.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Thy <a href="../ebd/ebd069.htm#004">calf</a>, O <a href="../ebd/ebd320.htm#004">Samaria</a>, hath cast thee off; mine <a href="../ebd/ebd024.htm#002">anger</a> is kindled against them: how long will it be ere they attain to innocency?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> For from <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> was it also: the workman made it; therefore it is not God: but the <a href="../ebd/ebd069.htm#004">calf</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd320.htm#004">Samaria</a> shall be broken in <a href="../ebd/ebd295.htm#002">pieces</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> For they have sown the wind, and they shall reap the whirlwind: it hath no stalk; the bud shall yield no meal: if so be it yield, the strangers shall <a href="../ebd/ebd353.htm#009">swallow</a> it up.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> is swallowed up: now shall they be among the <a href="../ebd/ebd146.htm#001">Gentiles</a> as a vessel wherein is no pleasure.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> For they are gone up to <a href="../ebd/ebd035.htm#005">Assyria</a>, a wild <a href="../ebd/ebd035.htm#001">ass</a> alone by himself: <a href="../ebd/ebd123.htm#000">Ephraim</a> hath hired lovers.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> Yea, though they have hired among the nations, now will I gather them, and they shall sorrow a little for the <a href="../ebd/ebd066.htm#007">burden</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of princes.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> Because <a href="../ebd/ebd123.htm#000">Ephraim</a> hath made many altars to <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a>, altars shall be unto him to <a href="../ebd/ebd344.htm#006">sin</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> I have written to him the great things of my law, but they were counted as a strange thing.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> They <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#002">sacrifice</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd135.htm#007">flesh</a> for the sacrifices of mine offerings, and eat it; but the LORD accepteth them not; now will he remember their iniquity, and visit their sins: they shall return to <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> For <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> hath forgotten his Maker, and buildeth temples; and <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a> hath multiplied fenced cities: but I will send a fire upon his cities, and it shall devour the palaces thereof.<br>
 </p>
