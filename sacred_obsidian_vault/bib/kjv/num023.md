 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Numbers Chapter 23</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd042.htm#003">Balaam</a> said unto <a href="../ebd/ebd042.htm#006">Balak</a>, Build me here <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> altars, and prepare me here <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> oxen and <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> rams.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd042.htm#006">Balak</a> did as <a href="../ebd/ebd042.htm#003">Balaam</a> had spoken; and <a href="../ebd/ebd042.htm#006">Balak</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd042.htm#003">Balaam</a> offered on every <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> a <a href="../ebd/ebd066.htm#003">bullock</a> and a <a href="../ebd/ebd306.htm#003">ram</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd042.htm#003">Balaam</a> said unto <a href="../ebd/ebd042.htm#006">Balak</a>, Stand by thy burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, and I will go: peradventure the LORD will come to meet me: and whatsoever he sheweth me I will tell thee. And he went to an high place.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And God met <a href="../ebd/ebd042.htm#003">Balaam</a>: and he said unto him, I have prepared <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> altars, and I have offered upon every <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a> a <a href="../ebd/ebd066.htm#003">bullock</a> and a <a href="../ebd/ebd306.htm#003">ram</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And the LORD put a word in Balaam's mouth, and said, Return unto <a href="../ebd/ebd042.htm#006">Balak</a>, and thus thou shalt speak.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And he returned unto him, and, lo, he stood by his burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#002">sacrifice</a>, he, and all the princes of <a href="../ebd/ebd258.htm#005">Moab</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And he took up his <a href="../ebd/ebd284.htm#003">parable</a>, and said, <a href="../ebd/ebd042.htm#006">Balak</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd258.htm#005">Moab</a> hath brought me from <a href="../ebd/ebd028.htm#001">Aram</a>, out of the mountains of the <a href="../ebd/ebd111.htm#000">east</a>, saying, Come, <a href="../ebd/ebd094.htm#003">curse</a> me <a href="../ebd/ebd194.htm#007">Jacob</a>, and come, defy <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> How shall I <a href="../ebd/ebd094.htm#003">curse</a>, whom God hath not cursed? or how shall I defy, whom the LORD hath not defied?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> For from the top of the rocks I see him, and from the hills I behold him: lo, the people shall dwell alone, and shall not be reckoned among the nations.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> Who can count the <a href="../ebd/ebd109.htm#008">dust</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd194.htm#007">Jacob</a>, and the number of the fourth part of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>? Let me die the death of the righteous, and let my last end be like his!<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd042.htm#006">Balak</a> said unto <a href="../ebd/ebd042.htm#003">Balaam</a>, What hast thou done unto me? I took thee to <a href="../ebd/ebd094.htm#003">curse</a> mine enemies, and, behold, thou hast blessed them altogether.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And he answered and said, Must I not take heed to speak that which the LORD hath put in my mouth?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd042.htm#006">Balak</a> said unto him, Come, I pray thee, with me unto another place, from whence thou mayest see them: thou shalt see but the utmost part of them, and shalt not see them all: and <a href="../ebd/ebd094.htm#003">curse</a> me them from thence.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> And he brought him into the field of Zophim, to the top of <a href="../ebd/ebd296.htm#004">Pisgah</a>, and built <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> altars, and offered a <a href="../ebd/ebd066.htm#003">bullock</a> and a <a href="../ebd/ebd306.htm#003">ram</a> on every <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And he said unto <a href="../ebd/ebd042.htm#006">Balak</a>, Stand here by thy burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, while I meet the LORD yonder.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> And the LORD met <a href="../ebd/ebd042.htm#003">Balaam</a>, and put a word in his mouth, and said, Go again unto <a href="../ebd/ebd042.htm#006">Balak</a>, and say thus.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> And when he came to him, behold, he stood by his burnt <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a>, and the princes of <a href="../ebd/ebd258.htm#005">Moab</a> with him. And <a href="../ebd/ebd042.htm#006">Balak</a> said unto him, What hath the LORD spoken?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> And he took up his <a href="../ebd/ebd284.htm#003">parable</a>, and said, Rise up, <a href="../ebd/ebd042.htm#006">Balak</a>, and hear; hearken unto me, thou son of <a href="../ebd/ebd394.htm#007">Zippor</a>:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> God is not a man, that he should lie; neither the son of man, that he should repent: hath he said, and shall he not do it? or hath he spoken, and shall he not make it good?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> Behold, I have received commandment to <a href="../ebd/ebd060.htm#008">bless</a>: and he hath blessed; and I cannot reverse it.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="021">21</a> He hath not beheld iniquity in <a href="../ebd/ebd194.htm#007">Jacob</a>, neither hath he seen perverseness in <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>: the LORD his God is with him, and the shout of a <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> is among them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="022">22</a> God brought them out of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a>; he hath as it were the strength of an <a href="../ebd/ebd374.htm#007">unicorn</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="023">23</a> Surely there is no enchantment against <a href="../ebd/ebd194.htm#007">Jacob</a>, neither is there any <a href="../ebd/ebd104.htm#009">divination</a> against <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>: according to this time it shall be said of <a href="../ebd/ebd194.htm#007">Jacob</a> and of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, What hath God wrought!<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="024">24</a> Behold, the people shall rise up as a great lion, and lift up himself as a young lion: he shall not lie down until he eat of the prey, and <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> of the slain.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="025">25</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd042.htm#006">Balak</a> said unto <a href="../ebd/ebd042.htm#003">Balaam</a>, Neither <a href="../ebd/ebd094.htm#003">curse</a> them at all, nor <a href="../ebd/ebd060.htm#008">bless</a> them at all.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="026">26</a> But <a href="../ebd/ebd042.htm#003">Balaam</a> answered and said unto <a href="../ebd/ebd042.htm#006">Balak</a>, Told not I thee, saying, All that the LORD speaketh, that I must do?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="027">27</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd042.htm#006">Balak</a> said unto <a href="../ebd/ebd042.htm#003">Balaam</a>, Come, I pray thee, I will bring thee unto another place; peradventure it will please God that thou mayest <a href="../ebd/ebd094.htm#003">curse</a> me them from thence.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="028">28</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd042.htm#006">Balak</a> brought <a href="../ebd/ebd042.htm#003">Balaam</a> unto the top of <a href="../ebd/ebd289.htm#006">Peor</a>, that looketh toward <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#002">Jeshimon</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="029">29</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd042.htm#003">Balaam</a> said unto <a href="../ebd/ebd042.htm#006">Balak</a>, Build me here <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> altars, and prepare me here <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> bullocks and <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> rams.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="030">30</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd042.htm#006">Balak</a> did as <a href="../ebd/ebd042.htm#003">Balaam</a> had said, and offered a <a href="../ebd/ebd066.htm#003">bullock</a> and a <a href="../ebd/ebd306.htm#003">ram</a> on every <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>.<br>
 </p>
