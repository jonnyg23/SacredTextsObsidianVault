 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 143</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Hear my <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#000">prayer</a>, O LORD, give <a href="../ebd/ebd110.htm#004">ear</a> to my supplications: in thy faithfulness answer me, and in thy <a href="../ebd/ebd313.htm#004">righteousness</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> And enter not into judgment with thy servant: for in thy sight shall no man living be justified.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> For the enemy hath persecuted my soul; he hath smitten my life down to the ground; he hath made me to dwell in <a href="../ebd/ebd097.htm#008">darkness</a>, as those that have been long dead.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Therefore is my <a href="../ebd/ebd349.htm#009">spirit</a> overwhelmed within me; my heart within me is desolate.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> I remember the days of old; I meditate on all thy works; I muse on the work of thy hands.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> I stretch forth my hands unto thee: my soul thirsteth after thee, as a thirsty land. <a href="../ebd/ebd326.htm#008">Selah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Hear me speedily, O LORD: my <a href="../ebd/ebd349.htm#009">spirit</a> faileth: hide not thy face from me, lest I be like unto them that go down into the <a href="../ebd/ebd296.htm#007">pit</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> Cause me to hear thy lovingkindness in the morning; for in thee do I trust: cause me to know the way wherein I should walk; for I lift up my soul unto thee.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> Deliver me, O LORD, from mine enemies: I flee unto thee to hide me.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> Teach me to do thy will; for thou art my God: thy <a href="../ebd/ebd349.htm#009">spirit</a> is good; lead me into the land of uprightness.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> Quicken me, O LORD, for thy name's sake: for thy righteousness' sake bring my soul out of trouble.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And of thy <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> cut off mine enemies, and destroy all them that afflict my soul: for I am thy servant.<br>
 </p>
