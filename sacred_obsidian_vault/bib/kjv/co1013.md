 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: 1 Corinthians Chapter 13</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Though I speak with the tongues of men and of angels, and have not <a href="../ebd/ebd077.htm#009">charity</a>, I am become as sounding <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#003">brass</a>, or a tinkling cymbal.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> And though I have the <a href="../ebd/ebd148.htm#005">gift</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#007">prophecy</a>, and understand all mysteries, and all knowledge; and though I have all faith, so that I could remove mountains, and have not <a href="../ebd/ebd077.htm#009">charity</a>, I am nothing.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And though I bestow all my goods to feed the <a href="../ebd/ebd298.htm#006">poor</a>, and though I give my body to be burned, and have not <a href="../ebd/ebd077.htm#009">charity</a>, it profiteth me nothing.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd077.htm#009">Charity</a> suffereth long, and is kind; <a href="../ebd/ebd077.htm#009">charity</a> envieth not; <a href="../ebd/ebd077.htm#009">charity</a> vaunteth not itself, is not puffed up,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Doth not behave itself unseemly, seeketh not her own, is not easily provoked, thinketh no evil;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Rejoiceth not in iniquity, but rejoiceth in the truth;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Beareth all things, believeth all things, hopeth all things, endureth all things.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd077.htm#009">Charity</a> never faileth: but whether there be prophecies, they shall fail; whether there be tongues, they shall cease; whether there be knowledge, it shall vanish away.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> For we know in part, and we prophesy in part.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> But when that which is perfect is come, then that which is in part shall be done away.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> When I was a child, I spake as a child, I understood as a child, I thought as a child: but when I became a man, I put away childish things.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> For now we see through a glass, darkly; but then face to face: now I know in part; but then shall I know even as also I am known.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> And now abideth faith, hope, <a href="../ebd/ebd077.htm#009">charity</a>, these three; but the greatest of these is <a href="../ebd/ebd077.htm#009">charity</a>.<br>
 </p>
