 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Isaiah Chapter 11</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> And there shall come forth a rod out of the stem of <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#005">Jesse</a>, and a <a href="../ebd/ebd064.htm#002">Branch</a> shall grow out of his roots:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd349.htm#009">spirit</a> of the LORD shall rest upon him, the <a href="../ebd/ebd349.htm#009">spirit</a> of wisdom and understanding, the <a href="../ebd/ebd349.htm#009">spirit</a> of counsel and might, the <a href="../ebd/ebd349.htm#009">spirit</a> of knowledge and of the fear of the LORD;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And shall make him of quick understanding in the fear of the LORD: and he shall not <a href="../ebd/ebd213.htm#005">judge</a> after the sight of his eyes, neither reprove after the hearing of his ears:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> But with <a href="../ebd/ebd313.htm#004">righteousness</a> shall he <a href="../ebd/ebd213.htm#005">judge</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd298.htm#006">poor</a>, and reprove with equity for the meek of the earth: and he shall smite the earth: with the rod of his mouth, and with the breath of his lips shall he slay the wicked.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd313.htm#004">righteousness</a> shall be the <a href="../ebd/ebd149.htm#005">girdle</a> of his loins, and faithfulness the <a href="../ebd/ebd149.htm#005">girdle</a> of his <a href="../ebd/ebd310.htm#001">reins</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> The <a href="../ebd/ebd383.htm#001">wolf</a> also shall dwell with the <a href="../ebd/ebd223.htm#003">lamb</a>, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd227.htm#002">leopard</a> shall lie down with the kid; and the <a href="../ebd/ebd069.htm#004">calf</a> and the young lion and the <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#008">fatling</a> together; and a little child shall lead them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd092.htm#001">cow</a> and the bear shall feed; their young ones shall lie down together: and the lion shall eat <a href="../ebd/ebd352.htm#000">straw</a> like the <a href="../ebd/ebd281.htm#007">ox</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And the sucking child shall play on the hole of the <a href="../ebd/ebd035.htm#000">asp</a>, and the weaned child shall put his hand on the cockatrice' <a href="../ebd/ebd101.htm#007">den</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> They shall not hurt nor destroy in all my holy mountain: for the earth shall be full of the knowledge of the LORD, as the waters cover the sea.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And in that day there shall be a root of <a href="../ebd/ebd205.htm#005">Jesse</a>, which shall stand for an <a href="../ebd/ebd121.htm#008">ensign</a> of the people; to it shall the <a href="../ebd/ebd146.htm#001">Gentiles</a> seek: and his rest shall be glorious.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And it shall come to pass in that day, that the Lord shall set his hand again the second time to recover the remnant of his people, which shall be left, from <a href="../ebd/ebd035.htm#005">Assyria</a>, and from <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a>, and from <a href="../ebd/ebd286.htm#007">Pathros</a>, and from <a href="../ebd/ebd094.htm#005">Cush</a>, and from <a href="../ebd/ebd114.htm#003">Elam</a>, and from Shinar, and from <a href="../ebd/ebd160.htm#005">Hamath</a>, and from the islands of the sea.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And he shall set up an <a href="../ebd/ebd121.htm#008">ensign</a> for the nations, and shall assemble the outcasts of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>, and gather together the dispersed of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a> from the four corners of the earth.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> The envy also of <a href="../ebd/ebd123.htm#000">Ephraim</a> shall depart, and the adversaries of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a> shall be cut off: <a href="../ebd/ebd123.htm#000">Ephraim</a> shall not envy <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a> shall not vex <a href="../ebd/ebd123.htm#000">Ephraim</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> But they shall <a href="../ebd/ebd136.htm#004">fly</a> upon the shoulders of the <a href="../ebd/ebd294.htm#002">Philistines</a> toward the west; they shall spoil them of the <a href="../ebd/ebd111.htm#000">east</a> together: they shall lay their hand upon <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#001">Edom</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd258.htm#005">Moab</a>; and the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd021.htm#002">Ammon</a> shall obey them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> And the LORD shall utterly destroy the tongue of the Egyptian sea; and with his mighty wind shall he shake his hand over the river, and shall smite it in the <a href="../ebd/ebd329.htm#005">seven</a> streams, and make men go over dryshod.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> And there shall be an <a href="../ebd/ebd178.htm#005">highway</a> for the remnant of his people, which shall be left, from <a href="../ebd/ebd035.htm#005">Assyria</a>; like as it was to <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> in the day that he came up out of the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a>.<br>
 </p>
