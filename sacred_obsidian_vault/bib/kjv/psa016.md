 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 16</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Preserve me, O God: for in thee do I put my trust.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> O my soul, thou hast said unto the LORD, Thou art my Lord: my <a href="../ebd/ebd153.htm#000">goodness</a> extendeth not to thee;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> But to the saints that are in the earth, and to the excellent, in whom is all my delight.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Their sorrows shall be multiplied that hasten after another god: their <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a> offerings of <a href="../ebd/ebd061.htm#000">blood</a> will I not offer, nor take up their names into my lips.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> The LORD is the portion of mine inheritance and of my <a href="../ebd/ebd094.htm#000">cup</a>: thou maintainest my lot.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> The <a href="../ebd/ebd229.htm#007">lines</a> are fallen unto me in pleasant places; yea, I have a goodly heritage.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> I will <a href="../ebd/ebd060.htm#008">bless</a> the LORD, who hath given me counsel: my <a href="../ebd/ebd310.htm#001">reins</a> also instruct me in the night <a href="../ebd/ebd325.htm#003">seasons</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> I have set the LORD always before me: because he is at my right hand, I shall not be moved.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> Therefore my heart is glad, and my <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a> rejoiceth: my <a href="../ebd/ebd135.htm#007">flesh</a> also shall rest in hope.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> For thou wilt not leave my soul in <a href="../ebd/ebd173.htm#003">hell</a>; neither wilt thou suffer thine Holy One to see corruption.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> Thou wilt shew me the path of life: in thy presence is <a href="../ebd/ebd139.htm#007">fulness</a> of joy; at thy right hand there are pleasures for evermore.<br>
 </p>
