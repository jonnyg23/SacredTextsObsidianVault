 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 113</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Praise ye the LORD. Praise, O ye servants of the LORD, praise the name of the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Blessed be the name of the LORD from this time forth and for evermore.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> From the rising of the <a href="../ebd/ebd353.htm#001">sun</a> unto the going down of the same the LORD's name is to be praised.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> The LORD is high above all nations, and his <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a> above the heavens.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Who is like unto the LORD our God, who dwelleth on high,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> Who humbleth himself to behold the things that are in <a href="../ebd/ebd170.htm#006">heaven</a>, and in the earth!<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> He raiseth up the <a href="../ebd/ebd298.htm#006">poor</a> out of the <a href="../ebd/ebd109.htm#008">dust</a>, and lifteth the needy out of the dunghill;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> That he may set him with princes, even with the princes of his people.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> He maketh the <a href="../ebd/ebd045.htm#007">barren</a> woman to keep house, and to be a joyful mother of children. Praise ye the LORD.<br>
 </p>
