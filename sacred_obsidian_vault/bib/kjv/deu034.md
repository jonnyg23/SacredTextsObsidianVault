 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Deuteronomy Chapter 34</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> went up from the plains of <a href="../ebd/ebd258.htm#005">Moab</a> unto the mountain of <a href="../ebd/ebd268.htm#004">Nebo</a>, to the top of <a href="../ebd/ebd296.htm#004">Pisgah</a>, that is over against <a href="../ebd/ebd203.htm#008">Jericho</a>. And the LORD shewed him all the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd148.htm#009">Gilead</a>, unto <a href="../ebd/ebd096.htm#009">Dan</a>,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> And all <a href="../ebd/ebd266.htm#006">Naphtali</a>, and the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd123.htm#000">Ephraim</a>, and <a href="../ebd/ebd240.htm#002">Manasseh</a>, and all the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd212.htm#007">Judah</a>, unto the utmost sea,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> And the south, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd297.htm#002">plain</a> of the <a href="../ebd/ebd376.htm#008">valley</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd203.htm#008">Jericho</a>, the city of palm trees, unto <a href="../ebd/ebd395.htm#004">Zoar</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And the LORD said unto him, This is the land which I sware unto <a href="../ebd/ebd005.htm#005">Abraham</a>, unto <a href="../ebd/ebd189.htm#006">Isaac</a>, and unto <a href="../ebd/ebd194.htm#007">Jacob</a>, saying, I will give it unto thy seed: I have caused thee to see it with thine eyes, but thou shalt not go over thither.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> So <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> the servant of the LORD died there in the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd258.htm#005">Moab</a>, according to the word of the LORD.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And he buried him in a <a href="../ebd/ebd376.htm#008">valley</a> in the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd258.htm#005">Moab</a>, over against Bethpeor: but no man knoweth of his <a href="../ebd/ebd328.htm#001">sepulchre</a> unto this day.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> was an hundred and twenty years old when he died: his eye was not dim, nor his natural force abated.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> And the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> wept for <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> in the plains of <a href="../ebd/ebd258.htm#005">Moab</a> thirty days: so the days of weeping and mourning for <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> were ended.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd211.htm#006">Joshua</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd275.htm#006">Nun</a> was full of the <a href="../ebd/ebd349.htm#009">spirit</a> of wisdom; for <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> had laid his hands upon him: and the children of <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> hearkened unto him, and did as the LORD commanded <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> And there arose not a <a href="../ebd/ebd300.htm#008">prophet</a> since in <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a> like unto <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a>, whom the LORD knew face to face,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> In all the signs and the wonders, which the LORD sent him to do in the land of <a href="../ebd/ebd113.htm#009">Egypt</a> to <a href="../ebd/ebd292.htm#005">Pharaoh</a>, and to all his servants, and to all his land,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And in all that mighty hand, and in all the great terror which <a href="../ebd/ebd260.htm#003">Moses</a> shewed in the sight of all <a href="../ebd/ebd191.htm#000">Israel</a>.<br>
 </p>
