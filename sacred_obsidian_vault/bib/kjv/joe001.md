 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Joel Chapter 1</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> The word of the LORD that came to <a href="../ebd/ebd208.htm#005">Joel</a> the son of <a href="../ebd/ebd291.htm#007">Pethuel</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Hear this, ye old men, and give <a href="../ebd/ebd110.htm#004">ear</a>, all ye inhabitants of the land. Hath this been in your days, or even in the days of your fathers?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Tell ye your children of it, and let your children tell their children, and their children another <a href="../ebd/ebd145.htm#008">generation</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> That which the palmerworm hath left hath the <a href="../ebd/ebd231.htm#000">locust</a> eaten; and that which the <a href="../ebd/ebd231.htm#000">locust</a> hath left hath the <a href="../ebd/ebd071.htm#004">cankerworm</a> eaten; and that which the <a href="../ebd/ebd071.htm#004">cankerworm</a> hath left hath the caterpiller eaten.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Awake, ye drunkards, and weep; and howl, all ye drinkers of <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#000">wine</a>, because of the new <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#000">wine</a>; for it is cut off from your mouth.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> For a nation is come up upon my land, strong, and without number, whose teeth are the teeth of a lion, and he hath the <a href="../ebd/ebd078.htm#004">cheek</a> teeth of a great lion.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> He hath laid my <a href="../ebd/ebd377.htm#004">vine</a> waste, and barked my <a href="../ebd/ebd133.htm#001">fig</a> tree: he hath made it <a href="../ebd/ebd084.htm#005">clean</a> bare, and cast it away; the branches thereof are made white.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> Lament like a <a href="../ebd/ebd377.htm#009">virgin</a> girded with <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#001">sackcloth</a> for the husband of her youth.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> The meat <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> and the <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> is cut off from the house of the LORD; the priests, the LORD's ministers, <a href="../ebd/ebd261.htm#004">mourn</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> The field is wasted, the land mourneth; for the <a href="../ebd/ebd090.htm#006">corn</a> is wasted: the new <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#000">wine</a> is dried up, the oil languisheth.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> Be ye ashamed, O ye husbandmen; howl, O ye vinedressers, for the <a href="../ebd/ebd381.htm#000">wheat</a> and for the <a href="../ebd/ebd045.htm#003">barley</a>; because the <a href="../ebd/ebd165.htm#008">harvest</a> of the field is perished.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> The <a href="../ebd/ebd377.htm#004">vine</a> is dried up, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd133.htm#001">fig</a> tree languisheth; the <a href="../ebd/ebd298.htm#000">pomegranate</a> tree, the palm tree also, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd027.htm#004">apple</a> tree, even all the trees of the field, are withered: because joy is withered away from the sons of men.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> Gird yourselves, and lament, ye priests: howl, ye ministers of the <a href="../ebd/ebd018.htm#006">altar</a>: come, lie all night in <a href="../ebd/ebd318.htm#001">sackcloth</a>, ye ministers of my God: for the meat <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> and the <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd277.htm#001">offering</a> is withholden from the house of your God.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> Sanctify ye a <a href="../ebd/ebd131.htm#004">fast</a>, call a solemn assembly, gather the elders and all the inhabitants of the land into the house of the LORD your God, and cry unto the LORD,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="015">15</a> Alas for the day! for the day of the LORD is at hand, and as a <a href="../ebd/ebd102.htm#004">destruction</a> from the Almighty shall it come.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="016">16</a> Is not the meat cut off before our eyes, yea, joy and gladness from the house of our God?<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="017">17</a> The seed is rotten under their clods, the garners are laid desolate, the barns are broken down; for the <a href="../ebd/ebd090.htm#006">corn</a> is withered.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="018">18</a> How do the beasts groan! the herds of <a href="../ebd/ebd074.htm#002">cattle</a> are perplexed, because they have no pasture; yea, the flocks of <a href="../ebd/ebd333.htm#005">sheep</a> are made desolate.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="019">19</a> O LORD, to thee will I cry: for the fire hath devoured the pastures of the <a href="../ebd/ebd381.htm#005">wilderness</a>, and the flame hath burned all the trees of the field.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="020">20</a> The beasts of the field cry also unto thee: for the rivers of waters are dried up, and the fire hath devoured the pastures of the <a href="../ebd/ebd381.htm#005">wilderness</a>.<br>
 </p>
