 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Esther Chapter 5</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> Now it came to pass on the third day, that <a href="../ebd/ebd125.htm#006">Esther</a> put on her royal <a href="../ebd/ebd027.htm#000">apparel</a>, and stood in the inner court of the king's house, over against the king's house: and the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> sat upon his royal <a href="../ebd/ebd365.htm#001">throne</a> in the royal house, over against the <a href="../ebd/ebd143.htm#008">gate</a> of the house.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> And it was so, when the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> saw <a href="../ebd/ebd125.htm#006">Esther</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd303.htm#007">queen</a> standing in the court, that she obtained favour in his sight: and the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> held out to <a href="../ebd/ebd125.htm#006">Esther</a> the golden <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#006">sceptre</a> that was in his hand. So <a href="../ebd/ebd125.htm#006">Esther</a> drew near, and touched the top of the <a href="../ebd/ebd323.htm#006">sceptre</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Then said the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> unto her, What wilt thou, <a href="../ebd/ebd303.htm#007">queen</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd125.htm#006">Esther</a>? and what is thy request? it shall be even given thee to the half of the kingdom.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd125.htm#006">Esther</a> answered, If it seem good unto the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a>, let the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd160.htm#004">Haman</a> come this day unto the <a href="../ebd/ebd043.htm#006">banquet</a> that I have prepared for him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> Then the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> said, Cause <a href="../ebd/ebd160.htm#004">Haman</a> to make haste, that he may do as <a href="../ebd/ebd125.htm#006">Esther</a> hath said. So the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd160.htm#004">Haman</a> came to the <a href="../ebd/ebd043.htm#006">banquet</a> that <a href="../ebd/ebd125.htm#006">Esther</a> had prepared.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> said unto <a href="../ebd/ebd125.htm#006">Esther</a> at the <a href="../ebd/ebd043.htm#006">banquet</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#000">wine</a>, What is thy petition? and it shall be granted thee: and what is thy request? even to the half of the kingdom it shall be performed.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> Then answered <a href="../ebd/ebd125.htm#006">Esther</a>, and said, My petition and my request is;<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> If I have found favour in the sight of the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a>, and if it please the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> to grant my petition, and to perform my request, let the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> and <a href="../ebd/ebd160.htm#004">Haman</a> come to the <a href="../ebd/ebd043.htm#006">banquet</a> that I shall prepare for them, and I will do to morrow as the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> hath said.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> Then went <a href="../ebd/ebd160.htm#004">Haman</a> forth that day joyful and with a glad heart: but when <a href="../ebd/ebd160.htm#004">Haman</a> saw <a href="../ebd/ebd259.htm#005">Mordecai</a> in the king's <a href="../ebd/ebd143.htm#008">gate</a>, that he stood not up, nor moved for him, he was full of indignation against <a href="../ebd/ebd259.htm#005">Mordecai</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> Nevertheless <a href="../ebd/ebd160.htm#004">Haman</a> refrained himself: and when he came home, he sent and called for his friends, and <a href="../ebd/ebd391.htm#006">Zeresh</a> his wife.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> And <a href="../ebd/ebd160.htm#004">Haman</a> told them of the <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a> of his riches, and the multitude of his children, and all the things wherein the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> had promoted him, and how he had advanced him above the princes and servants of the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd160.htm#004">Haman</a> said moreover, Yea, <a href="../ebd/ebd125.htm#006">Esther</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd303.htm#007">queen</a> did let no man come in with the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> unto the <a href="../ebd/ebd043.htm#006">banquet</a> that she had prepared but myself; and to morrow am I invited unto her also with the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> Yet all this availeth me nothing, so long as I see <a href="../ebd/ebd259.htm#005">Mordecai</a> the <a href="../ebd/ebd206.htm#004">Jew</a> <a href="../ebd/ebd345.htm#009">sitting</a> at the king's <a href="../ebd/ebd143.htm#008">gate</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="014">14</a> Then said <a href="../ebd/ebd391.htm#006">Zeresh</a> his wife and all his friends unto him, Let a <a href="../ebd/ebd142.htm#005">gallows</a> be made of fifty cubits high, and to morrow speak thou unto the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> that <a href="../ebd/ebd259.htm#005">Mordecai</a> may be hanged thereon: then go thou in merrily with the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> unto the <a href="../ebd/ebd043.htm#006">banquet</a>. And the thing pleased <a href="../ebd/ebd160.htm#004">Haman</a>; and he caused the <a href="../ebd/ebd142.htm#005">gallows</a> to be made.<br>
 </p>
