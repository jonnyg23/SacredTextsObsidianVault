 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Psalms Chapter 21</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> The <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> shall joy in thy strength, O LORD; and in thy <a href="../ebd/ebd320.htm#003">salvation</a> how greatly shall he rejoice!<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> Thou hast given him his heart's desire, and hast not withholden the request of his lips. <a href="../ebd/ebd326.htm#008">Selah</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> For thou preventest him with the blessings of <a href="../ebd/ebd153.htm#000">goodness</a>: thou settest a <a href="../ebd/ebd093.htm#001">crown</a> of pure <a href="../ebd/ebd152.htm#001">gold</a> on his head.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> He asked life of thee, and thou gavest it him, even length of days for ever and ever.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> His <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a> is great in thy <a href="../ebd/ebd320.htm#003">salvation</a>: honour and majesty hast thou laid upon him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> For thou hast made him most blessed for ever: thou hast made him exceeding glad with thy countenance.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> For the <a href="../ebd/ebd219.htm#001">king</a> trusteth in the LORD, and through the <a href="../ebd/ebd249.htm#005">mercy</a> of the most High he shall not be moved.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> Thine hand shall find out all thine enemies: thy right hand shall find out those that hate thee.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> Thou shalt make them as a fiery <a href="../ebd/ebd281.htm#005">oven</a> in the time of thine <a href="../ebd/ebd024.htm#002">anger</a>: the LORD shall <a href="../ebd/ebd353.htm#009">swallow</a> them up in his wrath, and the fire shall devour them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> Their <a href="../ebd/ebd139.htm#000">fruit</a> shalt thou destroy from the earth, and their seed from among the children of men.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> For they intended evil against thee: they imagined a mischievous device, which they are not able to perform.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> Therefore shalt thou make them turn their back, when thou shalt make ready thine <a href="../ebd/ebd032.htm#005">arrows</a> upon thy strings against the face of them.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="013">13</a> Be thou exalted, LORD, in thine own strength: so will we sing and praise thy power.<br>
 </p>
