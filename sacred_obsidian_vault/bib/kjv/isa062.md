 <h3 align="CENTER">King James Version: Isaiah Chapter 62</h3><p><a name="001">1</a> For Zion's sake will I not hold my peace, and for Jerusalem's sake I will not rest, until the <a href="../ebd/ebd313.htm#004">righteousness</a> thereof go forth as brightness, and the <a href="../ebd/ebd320.htm#003">salvation</a> thereof as a <a href="../ebd/ebd223.htm#007">lamp</a> that burneth.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="002">2</a> And the <a href="../ebd/ebd146.htm#001">Gentiles</a> shall see thy <a href="../ebd/ebd313.htm#004">righteousness</a>, and all kings thy <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a>: and thou shalt be called by a new name, which the mouth of the LORD shall name.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="003">3</a> Thou shalt also be a <a href="../ebd/ebd093.htm#001">crown</a> of <a href="../ebd/ebd150.htm#006">glory</a> in the hand of the LORD, and a royal <a href="../ebd/ebd102.htm#009">diadem</a> in the hand of thy God.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="004">4</a> Thou shalt no more be termed Forsaken; neither shall thy land any more be termed Desolate: but thou shalt be called <a href="../ebd/ebd174.htm#007">Hephzibah</a>, and thy land <a href="../ebd/ebd057.htm#006">Beulah</a>: for the LORD delighteth in thee, and thy land shall be married.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="005">5</a> For as a young man marrieth a <a href="../ebd/ebd377.htm#009">virgin</a>, so shall thy sons marry thee: and as the bridegroom rejoiceth over the <a href="../ebd/ebd065.htm#001">bride</a>, so shall thy God rejoice over thee.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="006">6</a> I have set watchmen upon thy walls, O <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a>, which shall never hold their peace day nor night: ye that make mention of the LORD, keep not silence,<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="007">7</a> And give him no rest, till he establish, and till he make <a href="../ebd/ebd204.htm#005">Jerusalem</a> a praise in the earth.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="008">8</a> The LORD hath sworn by his right hand, and by the arm of his strength, Surely I will no more give thy <a href="../ebd/ebd090.htm#006">corn</a> to be meat for thine enemies; and the sons of the <a href="../ebd/ebd351.htm#009">stranger</a> shall not <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a> thy <a href="../ebd/ebd382.htm#000">wine</a>, for the which thou hast laboured:<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="009">9</a> But they that have gathered it shall eat it, and praise the LORD; and they that have brought it together shall <a href="../ebd/ebd107.htm#009">drink</a> it in the courts of my <a href="../ebd/ebd180.htm#006">holiness</a>.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="010">10</a> Go through, go through the gates; prepare ye the way of the people; cast up, cast up the <a href="../ebd/ebd178.htm#005">highway</a>; gather out the stones; lift up a standard for the people.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="011">11</a> Behold, the LORD hath proclaimed unto the end of the world, Say ye to the daughter of <a href="../ebd/ebd394.htm#002">Zion</a>, Behold, thy <a href="../ebd/ebd320.htm#003">salvation</a> cometh; behold, his reward is with him, and his work before him.<br>
 </p>
 <p><a name="012">12</a> And they shall call them, The holy people, The redeemed of the LORD: and thou shalt be called, Sought out, A city not forsaken.<br>
 </p>
