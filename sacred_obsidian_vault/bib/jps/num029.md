 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Tanach</i>, Jewish Publication Society tr. [1917], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">NUMBERS CHAPTER 29</h3>  
 <p><a name="an_num:29:1">1</a> And in the seventh month, on the first day of the month, ye shall have a holy convocation: ye shall do no manner of servile work; it is a day of blowing the horn unto you. <a name="an_num:29:2">2</a> And ye shall prepare a burnt-offering for a sweet savour unto the LORD: one young bullock, one ram, seven he-lambs of the first year without blemish; <a name="an_num:29:3">3</a> and their meal-offering, fine flour mingled with oil, three tenth parts for the bullock, two tenth parts for the ram, <a name="an_num:29:4">4</a> and one tenth part for every lamb of the seven lambs; <a name="an_num:29:5">5</a> and one he-goat for a sin-offering, to make atonement for you; <a name="an_num:29:6">6</a> beside the burnt-offering of the new moon, and the meal-offering thereof, and the continual burnt-offering and the meal-offering thereof, and their drink-offerings, according unto their ordinance, for a sweet savour, an offering made by fire unto the LORD.</p>  
 <p><a name="an_num:29:7">7</a> And on the tenth day of this seventh month ye shall have a holy convocation; and ye shall afflict your souls; ye shall do no manner of work; <a name="an_num:29:8">8</a> but ye shall present a burnt-offering unto the LORD for a sweet savour: one young bullock, one ram, seven he-lambs of the first year; they shall be unto you without blemish; <a name="an_num:29:9">9</a> and their meal-offering, fine flour mingled with oil, three tenth parts for the bullock, two tenth parts for the one ram, <a name="an_num:29:10">10</a> a several tenth part for every lamb of the seven lambs; <a name="an_num:29:11">11</a> one he-goat for a sin-offering; beside the sin-offering of atonement, and the continual burnt-offering, and the meal-offering thereof, and their drink-offerings.</p>  
 <p><a name="an_num:29:12">12</a> And on the fifteenth day of the seventh month ye shall have a holy convocation: ye shall do no manner of servile work, and ye shall keep a feast unto the LORD seven days; <a name="an_num:29:13">13</a> and ye shall present a burnt-offering, an offering made by fire, of a sweet savour unto the LORD: thirteen young bullocks, two rams, fourteen he-lambs of the first year; they shall be without blemish; <a name="an_num:29:14">14</a> and their meal-offering, fine flour mingled with oil, three tenth parts for every bullock of the thirteen bullocks, two tenth parts for each ram of the two rams, <a name="an_num:29:15">15</a> and a several tenth part for every lamb of the fourteen lambs <a name="an_num:29:16">16</a> and one he-goat for a sin-offering; beside the continual</p>  
 <p><a name="page_201"><font size="1" color="green">p. 201</font></a></p>  
 <p>burnt-offering, the meal-offering thereof, and the drink-offering thereof.</p>  
 <p><a name="an_num:29:17">17</a> And on the second day ye shall present twelve young bullocks, two rams, fourteen he-lambs of the first year without blemish; <a name="an_num:29:18">18</a> and their meal-offering and their drink-offerings for the bullocks, for the rams, and for the lambs, according to their number, after the ordinance; <a name="an_num:29:19">19</a> and one he-goat for a sin-offering; beside the continual burnt-offering, and the meal-offering thereof, and their drink-offerings.</p>  
 <p><a name="an_num:29:20">20</a> And on the third day eleven bullocks, two rams, fourteen he-lambs of the first year without blemish; <a name="an_num:29:21">21</a> and their meal-offering and their drink-offerings for the bullocks, for the rams, and for the lambs, according to their number, after the ordinance; <a name="an_num:29:22">22</a> and one he-goat for a sin-offering; beside the continual burnt-offering, and the meal-offering thereof, and the drink-offering thereof.</p>  
 <p><a name="an_num:29:23">23</a> And on the fourth day ten bullocks, two rams, fourteen he-lambs of the first year without blemish; <a name="an_num:29:24">24</a> their meal-offering and their drink-offerings for the bullocks, for the rams, and for the lambs, according to their number, after the ordinance; <a name="an_num:29:25">25</a> and one he-goat for a sin-offering; beside the continual burnt-offering, the meal-offering thereof, and the drink-offering thereof.</p>  
 <p><a name="an_num:29:26">26</a> And on the fifth day nine bullocks, two rams, fourteen he-lambs of the first year without blemish; <a name="an_num:29:27">27</a> and their meal-offering and their drink-offerings for the bullocks, for the rams, and for the lambs, according to their number, after the ordinance; <a name="an_num:29:28">28</a> and one he-goat for a sin-offering; beside the continual burnt-offering, and the meal-offering thereof, and the drink-offering thereof.</p>  
 <p><a name="an_num:29:29">29</a> And on the sixth day eight bullocks, two rams, fourteen he-lambs of the first year without blemish; <a name="an_num:29:30">30</a> and their meal-offering and their drink-offerings for the bullocks, for the rams, and for the lambs, according to their number, after the ordinance; <a name="an_num:29:31">31</a> and one he-goat for a sin-offering; beside the continual burnt-offering, the meal-offering thereof, and the drink-offerings thereof.</p>  
 <p><a name="an_num:29:32">32</a> And on the seventh day seven bullocks, two rams, fourteen he-lambs of the first year without blemish; <a name="an_num:29:33">33</a> and their meal-offering and their drink-offerings for the bullocks, for the rams, and for the lambs, according to their number, after the ordinance; <a name="an_num:29:34">34</a> and one he-goat for a sin-offering; beside the continual burnt-offering, the meal-offering thereof, and the drink-offering thereof.</p>  
 <p><a name="an_num:29:35">35</a> On the eighth day ye shall have a solemn assembly: ye shall do no manner of servile work; <a name="an_num:29:36">36</a> but ye shall present a burnt-offering, an offering made by fire, of a sweet savour unto the LORD: one bullock, one ram, seven he-lambs of the first year without blemish; <a name="an_num:29:37">37</a> their meal-offering and their drink-offerings for the bullock, for the ram, and for the lambs, shall be according to their number, after the ordinance; <a name="an_num:29:38">38</a> and one he-goat for a sin-offering; beside the continual burnt-offering, and the meal-offering thereof, and the drink-offering thereof.</p>  
 <p><a name="an_num:29:39">39</a> These ye shall offer unto the LORD in your appointed seasons, beside your vows, and your freewill-offerings, whether they be your burnt-offerings, or your meal-offerings, or your drink-offerings, or your peace-offerings.</p>
