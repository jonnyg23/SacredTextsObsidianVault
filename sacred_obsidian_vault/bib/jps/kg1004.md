 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Tanach</i>, Jewish Publication Society tr. [1917], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">I Kings Chapter 4</h3>  
 <p><a name="an_kg1:4:1">1</a> And king Solomon was king over all Israel. <a name="an_kg1:4:2">2</a> And these were the princes whom he had: Azariah the son of Zadok, the priest; <a name="an_kg1:4:3">3</a> Elihoreph and Ahijah, the sons of Shisha, scribes; Jehoshaphat the son of Ahilud, the recorder; <a name="an_kg1:4:4">4</a> and Benaiah the son of Jehoiada was over the host; and Zadok and Abiathar were priests; <a name="an_kg1:4:5">5</a> and Azariah the son of Nathan was over the officers; and Zabud the son of Nathan was chief minister and the king's friend; <a name="an_kg1:4:6">6</a> and Ahishar was over the household; and Adoniram the son of Abda was over the levy.</p>  
 <p><a name="an_kg1:4:7">7</a> And Solomon had twelve officers over all Israel, who provided victuals for the king and his household: each man had to make provision for a month in the year. <a name="an_kg1:4:8">8</a> And these are their names: The son of Hur, in the hill-country of Ephraim; <a name="an_kg1:4:9">9</a> the son of Deker, in Makaz, and in Shaalbim, and Beth-shemesh, and Elon-bethhanan; <a name="an_kg1:4:10">10</a> the son of Hesed, in Arubboth; to him pertained Socoh, and all the land of Hepher; <a name="an_kg1:4:11">11</a> the son of Abinadab, in all the region of Dor; he had Taphath the daughter of Solomon to wife; .12Baana the son of Ahilud, in Taanach and Megiddo, and all Beth-shean which is beside Zarethan, beneath Jezreel, from Bethshean to Abel-meholah, as far as beyond Jokmeam; .13the son of Geber, in Ramoth-gilead; to him pertained the villages of Jair the son of Manasseh, which are in Gilead; even to him pertained the region of Argob, which is in Bashan, threescore great cities with walls and brazen bars; .14Ahinadab the son of Iddo, in Mahanaim; <a name="an_kg1:4:15">15</a> Ahimaaz, in Naphtali; he also took Basemath the daughter of Solomon to wife; <a name="an_kg1:4:16">16</a> Baana the son of Hushai, in Asher and Bealoth; <a name="an_kg1:4:17">17</a> Jehoshaphat the son of Paruah, in Issachar; <a name="an_kg1:4:18">18</a> Shimei the son of Ela, in Benjamin; <a name="an_kg1:4:19">19</a> Geber the son of Uri, in the land of Gilead, the country of Sihon king of the Amorites and of Og king of Bashan; and one officer that was [over all the officers] in the land. <a name="an_kg1:4:20">20</a> Judah and Israel were many, as the sand which is by the sea in multitude, eating and drinking and making merry.</p>
