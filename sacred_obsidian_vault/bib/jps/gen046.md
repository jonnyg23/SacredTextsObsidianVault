 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Tanach</i>, Jewish Publication Society tr. [1917], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">GENESIS CHAPTER 46</h3>  
 <p><a name="an_gen:46:1">1</a> And Israel took his journey with all that he had, and came to Beer-sheba, and offered sacrifices unto the God of his father Isaac. <a name="an_gen:46:2">2</a> And God spoke unto Israel in the visions of the night, and said: 'Jacob, Jacob.' And he said: 'Here am I.' <a name="an_gen:46:3">3</a> And <a name="page_58"><font size="1" color="green">p. 58</font></a> He said: 'I am God, the God of thy father; fear not to go down into Egypt; for I will there make of thee a great nation. <a name="an_gen:46:4">4</a> I will go down with thee into Egypt; and I will also surely bring thee up again; and Joseph shall put his hand upon thine eyes.' <a name="an_gen:46:5">5</a> And Jacob rose up from Beer-sheba; and the sons of Israel carried Jacob their father, and their little ones, and their wives, in the wagons which Pharaoh had sent to carry him. <a name="an_gen:46:6">6</a> And they took their cattle, and their goods, which they had gotten in the land of Canaan, and came into Egypt, Jacob, and all his seed with him; <a name="an_gen:46:7">7</a> his sons, and his sons’ sons with him, his daughters, and his sons’ daughters, and all his seed brought he with him into Egypt.</p>  
 <p><a name="an_gen:46:8">8</a> And these are the names of the children of Israel, who came into Egypt, Jacob and his sons: Reuben, Jacob's first-born. <a name="an_gen:46:9">9</a> And the sons of Reuben: Hanoch, and Pallu, and Hezron, and Carmi. <a name="an_gen:46:10">10</a> And the sons of Simeon: Jemuel, and Jamin, and Ohad, and Jachin, and Zohar, and Shaul the son of a Canaanitish woman. <a name="an_gen:46:11">11</a> And the sons of Levi: Gershon, Kohath, and Merari. <a name="an_gen:46:12">12</a> And the sons of Judah: Er, and Onan, and Shelah, and Perez, and Zerah; but Er and Onan died in the land of Canaan. And the sons of Perez were Hezron and Hamul. <a name="an_gen:46:13">13</a> And the sons of Issachar: Tola, and Puvah, and Iob, and Shimron. <a name="an_gen:46:14">14</a> And the sons of Zebulun: Sered, and Elon, and Jahleel. <a name="an_gen:46:15">15</a> These are the sons of Leah, whom she bore unto Jacob in Paddan-aram, with his daughter Dinah; all the souls of his sons .and his daughters were thirty and three. <a name="an_gen:46:16">16</a> And the sons of Gad: Ziphion, and Haggi, Shuni, and Ezbon, Eri, and Arodi, and Areli. <a name="an_gen:46:17">17</a> And the sons of Asher: Imnah, and Ishvah, and Ishvi, and Beriah, and Serah their sister; and the sons of Beriah: Heber, and Malchiel. <a name="an_gen:46:18">18</a> These are the sons of Zilpah, whom Laban gave to Leah his daughter, and these she bore unto Jacob, even sixteen souls. <a name="an_gen:46:19">19</a> The sons of Rachel Jacob's wife: Joseph and Benjamin. <a name="an_gen:46:20">20</a> And unto Joseph in the land of Egypt were born Manasseh and Ephraim, whom Asenath the daughter of Poti-phera priest of On bore unto him. <a name="an_gen:46:21">21</a> And the sons of Benjamin: Bela, and Becher, and Ashbel, Gera, and Naaman, Ehi, and Rosh, Muppim, and Huppim, and Ard. <a name="an_gen:46:22">22</a> These are the sons of Rachel, who were born to Jacob; all the souls were fourteen. <a name="an_gen:46:23">23</a> And the sons of Dan: Hushim. <a name="an_gen:46:24">24</a> And the sons of Naphtali: Jahzeel, and Guni, and Jezer, and Shillem. <a name="an_gen:46:25">25</a> These are the sons of Bilhah, whom Laban gave unto Rachel his daughter, and these she bore unto Jacob; all the souls were seven. <a name="an_gen:46:26">26</a> All the souls belonging to Jacob that came into Egypt, that came out of his loins, besides Jacob's sons’ wives, all the souls were threescore and six. <a name="an_gen:46:27">27</a> And the sons of Joseph, who were born to him in Egypt, were two souls; all the souls of the house of Jacob, that came into Egypt, were threescore and ten.</p>  
 <p><a name="an_gen:46:28">28</a> And he sent Judah before him unto Joseph, to show the way before him unto Goshen; and they came into the land of Goshen. <a name="an_gen:46:29">29</a> And Joseph made ready his chariot, and went up to meet Israel his father, to Goshen; and he presented himself unto him, and fell on his neck, and wept on his neck a good while. <a name="an_gen:46:30">30</a> And Israel said unto Joseph: 'Now let me die, since I have seen thy face, that thou art yet alive.' <a name="an_gen:46:31">31</a> And Joseph said unto his brethren, and unto his father's house: 'I will go up, and tell Pharaoh, and will say <a name="page_59"><font size="1" color="green">p. 59</font></a> unto him: My brethren, and my father's house, who were in the land of Canaan, are come unto me; <a name="an_gen:46:32">32</a> and the men are shepherds, for they have been keepers of cattle; and they have brought their flocks, and their herds, and all that they have. <a name="an_gen:46:33">33</a> And it shall come to pass, when Pharaoh shall call you, and shall say: What is your occupation? <a name="an_gen:46:34">34</a> that ye shall say: Thy servants have been keepers of cattle from our youth even until now, both we, and our fathers; that ye may dwell in the land of Goshen; for every shepherd is an abomination unto the Egyptians.'</p>
