 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Tanach</i>, Jewish Publication Society tr. [1917], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">DEUTERONOMY CHAPTER 33</h3>  
 <p align="center">‏וזאת הברכה‎</p>  
 <p><a name="an_deu:33:1">1</a> And this is the blessing, wherewith Moses the man of God blessed the children of Israel before his death. <a name="an_deu:33:2">2</a> And he said:</p>  
 <p>The LORD came from Sinai,<br> And rose from Seir unto them;<br> He shined forth from mount Paran,<br> And He came from the myriads holy,<br> At His right hand was a fiery law unto them.<br> <a name="an_deu:33:3">3</a> Yea, He loveth the peoples,<br> All His holy onesthey are in Thy hand;<br> And they sit down at Thy feet,<br> Receiving of Thy words.<br> <a name="an_deu:33:4">4</a> Moses commanded us a law,<br> An inheritance of the congregation of Jacob.<br> <a name="an_deu:33:5">5</a> And there was a king in Jeshurun,<br> When the heads of the people were gathered,<br> All the tribes of Israel together.</p>  
 <p><a name="an_deu:33:6">6</a> Let Reuben live, and not die<br> In that his men become few.</p>  
 <p><a name="an_deu:33:7">7</a> And this for Judah, and he said:<br> Hear, LORD, the voice of Judah,<br> And bring him in unto his people;<br> His hands shall contend for him,<br> And Thou shalt be a help against his adversaries.</p>  
 <p><a name="an_deu:33:8">8</a> And of Levi he said:<br> Thy Thummim and Thy Urim be with Thy holy one,<br> Whom Thou didst prove at Massah,<br> With whom Thou didst strive at the waters of Meribah;<br> <a name="an_deu:33:9">9</a> Who said of his father, and of his mother: `I have not seen him';<br> Neither did he acknowledge his brethren,<br> Nor knew he his own children;<br> For they have observed Thy word,<br> And keep Thy covenant.<br> <a name="an_deu:33:10">10</a> They shall teach Jacob Thine ordinances,<br> And Israel Thy law;<br> They shall put incense before Thee,<br> And whole burnt-offering upon Thine altar.<br> <a name="an_deu:33:11">11</a> Bless, LORD, his substance,<br> And accept the work of his hands;<br> Smite through the loins of them that rise up against him,<br> And of them that hate him, that they rise not again.</p>  
 <p><a name="an_deu:33:12">12</a> Of Benjamin he said:<br> The beloved of the LORD shall dwell in safety by Him;<br> He covereth him all the day,<br> And He dwelleth between his shoulders.</p>  
 <p><a name="an_deu:33:13">13</a> And of Joseph he said:<br> Blessed of the LORD be his land;<br> For the precious things of heaven, for the dew,<br> And for the deep that coucheth beneath,<br> <a name="an_deu:33:14">14</a> And for the precious things of the fruits of the sun,<br> And for the precious things of the yield of the moons,<br> <a name="an_deu:33:15">15</a> And for the tops of the ancient mountains,<br> And for the precious things of the everlasting hills,<br> <a name="an_deu:33:16">16</a> And for the precious things of the earth and the fulness thereof,<br> And the good will of Him that dwelt in the bush;<br> <a name="page_257"><font size="1" color="green">p. 257</font></a><br> Let the blessing come upon the head of Joseph,<br> And upon the crown of the head of him that is prince among his brethren.<br> <a name="an_deu:33:17">17</a> His firstling bullock, majesty is his;<br> And his horns are the horns of the wild-ox;<br> With them he shall gore the peoples all of them, even the ends of the earth;<br> And they are the ten thousands of Ephraim,<br> And they are the thousands of Manasseh.</p>  
 <p><a name="an_deu:33:18">18</a> And of Zebulun he said:<br> Rejoice, Zebulun, in thy going out,<br> And, Issachar, in thy tents.<br> <a name="an_deu:33:19">19</a> They shall call peoples unto the mountain;<br> There shall they offer sacrifices of righteousness;<br> For they shall suck the abundance of the seas,<br> And the hidden treasures of the sand.</p>  
 <p><a name="an_deu:33:20">20</a> And of Gad he said:<br> Blessed be He that enlargeth Gad;<br> He dwelleth as a lioness,<br> And teareth the arm, yea, the crown of the head.<br> <a name="an_deu:33:21">21</a> And he chose a first part for himself,<br> For there a portion of a ruler was reserved;<br> And there came the heads of the people,<br> He executed the righteousness of the LORD,<br> And His ordinances with Israel.</p>  
 <p><a name="an_deu:33:22">22</a> And of Dan he said:<br> Dan is a lion's whelp,<br> That leapeth forth from Bashan.</p>  
 <p><a name="an_deu:33:23">23</a> And of Naphtali he said:<br> O Naphtali, satisfied with favour,<br> And full with the blessing of the LORD:<br> Possess thou the sea and the south.</p>  
 <p><a name="an_deu:33:24">24</a> And of Asher he said:<br> Blessed be Asher above sons;<br> Let him be the favoured of his brethren,<br> And let him dip his foot in oil.<br> <a name="an_deu:33:25">25</a> Iron and brass shall be thy bars;<br> And as thy days, so shall thy strength be.</p>  
 <p><a name="an_deu:33:26">26</a> There is none like unto God, O Jeshurun,<br> Who rideth upon the heaven as thy help,<br> And in His excellency on the skies.<br> <a name="an_deu:33:27">27</a> The eternal God is a dwellingplaee,<br> And underneath are the everlasting arms;<br> And He thrust out the enemy from before thee,<br> And said: `Destroy.'<br> <a name="an_deu:33:28">28</a> And Israel dwelleth in safety,<br> The fountain of Jacob alone,<br> In a land of corn and wine;<br> Yea, his heavens drop down dew.<br> <a name="an_deu:33:29">29</a> Happy art thou, O Israel, who is like unto thee?<br> A people saved by the LORD,<br> The shield of thy help,<br> And that is the sword of thy excellency!<br> And thine enemies shall dwindle away before thee;<br> And thou shalt tread upon their high places.</p>
