 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Tanach</i>, Jewish Publication Society tr. [1917], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">GENESIS CHAPTER 5</h3>  
 <p><a name="an_gen:5:1">1</a> This is the book of the generations of Adam. In the day that God created man, in the likeness of God made He him; <a name="an_gen:5:2">2</a> male and female created He them, and blessed them, and called their name Adam, in the day when they were created. <a name="an_gen:5:3">3</a> And Adam lived a hundred and thirty years, and begot a son in his own likeness, after his image; and called his name Seth. <a name="an_gen:5:4">4</a> And the days of Adam after he begot Seth were eight hundred years; and he begot sons and daughters. <a name="an_gen:5:5">5</a> And all the days that Adam lived were nine hundred and thirty years; and he died.</p>  
 <p><a name="an_gen:5:6">6</a> And Seth lived a hundred and five years, and begot Enosh. <a name="an_gen:5:7">7</a> And Seth lived after he begot Enosh eight hundred and seven years, and begot sons and daughters. <a name="an_gen:5:8">8</a> And all the days of Seth were nine hundred and twelve years; and he died.</p>  
 <p><a name="an_gen:5:9">9</a> And Enosh lived ninety years, and begot Kenan. <a name="an_gen:5:10">10</a> And Enosh lived after he begot Kenan eight hundred and fifteen years, and begot sons and daughters. <a name="an_gen:5:11">11</a> And all the days of Enosh were nine hundred and five years; and he died.</p>  
 <p><a name="an_gen:5:12">12</a> And Kenan lived seventy years, and begot Mahalalel. <a name="an_gen:5:13">13</a> And Kenan lived after he begot Mahalalel eight hundred and forty years, and begot sons and daughters. <a name="an_gen:5:14">14</a> And all the days of Kenan were nine hundred and ten years; and he died.</p>  
 <p><a name="an_gen:5:15">15</a> And Mahalalel lived sixty and five years, and begot Jared. <a name="an_gen:5:16">16</a> And Mahalalel lived after he begot Jared eight hundred and thirty years, and begot sons and daughters. <a name="an_gen:5:17">17</a> And all the days of Mahalalel were eight hundred ninety and five years; and he died.</p>  
 <p><a name="an_gen:5:18">18</a> And Jared lived a hundred sixty and two years, and begot Enoch. <a name="an_gen:5:19">19</a> And Jared lived after he begot Enoch <a name="page_8"><font size="1" color="green">p. 8</font></a> eight hundred years, and begot sons and daughters. <a name="an_gen:5:20">20</a> And all the days of Jared were nine hundred sixty and two years; and he died.</p>  
 <p><a name="an_gen:5:21">21</a> And Enoch lived sixty and five years, and begot Methuselah. <a name="an_gen:5:22">22</a> And Enoch walked with God after he begot Methuselah three hundred years, and begot sons and daughters. <a name="an_gen:5:23">23</a> And all the days of Enoch were three hundred sixty and five years. <a name="an_gen:5:24">24</a> And Enoch walked with God, and he was not; for God took him.</p>  
 <p><a name="an_gen:5:25">25</a> And Methuselah lived a hundred eighty and seven years, and begot Lamech. <a name="an_gen:5:26">26</a> And Methuselah lived after he begot Lamech seven hundred eighty and two years, and begot sons and daughters. <a name="an_gen:5:27">27</a> And all the days of Methuselah were nine hundred sixty and nine years; and he died.</p>  
 <p><a name="an_gen:5:28">28</a> And Lamech lived a hundred eighty and two years, and begot a son. <a name="an_gen:5:29">29</a> And he called his name Noah, saying: 'This same shall  <a name="fr_14"></a><a href="#fn_13"><font size="1">a</font></a>
 comfort us in our work and in the toil of our hands, which cometh from the ground which the LORD hath cursed.' <a name="an_gen:5:30">30</a> And Lamech lived after he begot Noah five hundred ninety and five years, and begot sons and daughters. <a name="an_gen:5:31">31</a> And all the days of Lamech were seven hundred seventy and seven years; and he died.</p>  
 <p><a name="an_gen:5:32">32</a> And Noah was five hundred years old; and Noah begot Shem, Ham, and Japheth.</p>
