 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Tanach</i>, Jewish Publication Society tr. [1917], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">EXODUS CHAPTER 27</h3>  
 <p><a name="an_exo:27:1">1</a> And thou shalt make the altar of acacia-wood, five cubits long, and five cubits broad; the altar shall be four-square; and the height thereof shall be three cubits. <a name="an_exo:27:2">2</a> And thou shalt make the horns of it upon the four corners thereof; the horns thereof shall be of one piece with it; and thou shalt overlay it with brass. <a name="an_exo:27:3">3</a> And thou shalt make its pots to take away its ashes, and its shovels, and its basins, and its flesh-hooks, and its fire-pans; all the vessels thereof thou shalt make of brass. <a name="an_exo:27:4">4</a> And thou shalt make for it a grating of network of brass; and upon the net shalt thou make four brazen rings in the four corners thereof. <a name="an_exo:27:5">5</a> And thou shalt put it under the ledge round the altar beneath, that the net may reach halfway up the altar. <a name="an_exo:27:6">6</a> And thou shalt make staves for the altar, staves of acacia-wood, and overlay them with brass. <a name="an_exo:27:7">7</a> And the staves thereof shall be put into the rings, and the staves shall be upon the two sides of the altar, in bearing it. <a name="an_exo:27:8">8</a> Hollow with planks shalt thou make it; as it hath been shown thee in the mount, so shall they make it.</p>  
 <p><a name="an_exo:27:9">9</a> And thou shalt make the court of the tabernacle: for the south side southward there shall be hangings for the court of fine twined linen a hundred cubits long for one side. <a name="an_exo:27:10">10</a> And the pillars thereof shall be twenty, and their sockets twenty, of brass; the hooks of the pillars and their fillets shall be of silver. <a name="an_exo:27:11">11</a> And likewise for the north side in length there shall be hangings a hundred cubits long, and the pillars thereof twenty, and their sockets twenty, of brass; the hooks of the pillars and their fillets of silver. <a name="an_exo:27:12">12</a> And for the breadth of the court on the west side shall be hangings of fifty cubits: their pillars ten, and their sockets ten. <a name="an_exo:27:13">13</a> And the breadth of the court on the east side eastward shall be fifty cubits. <a name="an_exo:27:14">14</a> The hangings for the one side [of the gate] shall be fifteen cubits: their pillars three, and their sockets three. <a name="an_exo:27:15">15</a> And for the other side shall be hangings of fifteen cubits: their pillars three, and their sockets three. <a name="an_exo:27:16">16</a> And for the gate of the court shall be a screen of twenty cubits, of blue, and purple, and scarlet, and fine twined linen, the work of the weaver in colours: their pillars four, and their sockets four. <a name="an_exo:27:17">17</a> All the pillars of the <a name="page_98"><font size="1" color="green">p. 98</font></a> court round about shall be filleted with silver; their hooks of silver, and their sockets of brass. <a name="an_exo:27:18">18</a> The length of the court shall be a hundred cubits, and the breadth fifty every where, and the height five cubits, of fine twined linen, and their sockets of brass. <a name="an_exo:27:19">19</a> All the instruments of the tabernacle in all the service thereof, and all the pins thereof, and all the pins of the court, shall be of brass.</p>  
 <p align="center">‏תצוה‎</p>  
 <p><a name="an_exo:27:20">20</a> And thou shalt command the children of Israel, that they bring unto thee pure olive oil beaten for the light, to cause a lamp to burn continually. <a name="an_exo:27:21">21</a> In the tent of meeting, without the veil which is before the testimony, Aaron and his sons shall set it in order, to burn from evening to morning before the LORD; it shall be a statute for ever throughout their generations on the behalf of the children of Israel.</p>
