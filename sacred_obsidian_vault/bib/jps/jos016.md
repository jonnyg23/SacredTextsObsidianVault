 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Tanach</i>, Jewish Publication Society tr. [1917], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">JOSHUA CHAPTER 16</h3>  
 <p><a name="an_jos:16:1">1</a> And the lot for the children of Joseph went out from the Jordan at Jericho, at the waters of Jericho on the east, going up from Jericho through the hill-country to the wilderness, even to Beth-el. <a name="an_jos:16:2">2</a> And it went out from Beth-el-luz, and passed along unto the border of the Archites to Ataroth. <a name="an_jos:16:3">3</a> And it went down westward to the border of the Japhletites, unto the border of Beth-horon the nether, even unto Gezer; and the goings out thereof were at the sea. <a name="an_jos:16:4">4</a> And the children of Joseph, Manasseh and Ephraim, took their inheritance. <a name="an_jos:16:5">5</a> And the border of the children of Ephraim according to their families was thus; even the border of their inheritance eastward was Atroth-addar, unto Beth-horon the upper. <a name="an_jos:16:6">6</a> And the border went out westward, Michmethath being on the north; and the border turned about eastward unto Taanath-shiloh, and passed along it on the east of Janoah. <a name="an_jos:16:7">7</a> And it went down from Janoah to Ataroth, and to Naarah, and reached unto Jericho, and went out at the Jordan. <a name="an_jos:16:8">8</a> From Tappuah the border went along westward to the brook of Kanah; and the goings out thereof were at the sea. This is the inheritance of the tribe of the children of Ephraim according to their families; <a name="an_jos:16:9">9</a> together with the cities which were separated for the children of Ephraim in the midst of the inheritance of the</p>  
 <p><a name="page_281"><font size="1" color="green">p. 281</font></a></p>  
 <p>children of Manasseh, all the cities with their villages. <a name="an_jos:16:10">10</a> And they drove not out the Canaanites that dwelt in Gezer; but the Canaanites dwelt in the midst of Ephraim, unto this day, and became servants to do taskwork.</p>
