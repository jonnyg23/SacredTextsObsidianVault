 <p><a name="000"><b>Zetham</b></a>     Olive planter, a Levite (<a href="../kjv/ch1023.htm#008">Ch1 23:8</a>). 
 </p>
 <p><a name="001"><b>Zethan</b></a>     A Benjamite (<a href="../kjv/ch1007.htm#010">Ch1 7:10</a>). 
 </p>
 <p><a name="002"><b>Zia</b></a>     Fear, a Gadite (<a href="../kjv/ch1005.htm#013">Ch1 5:13</a>). 
 </p>
 <p><a name="003"><b>Ziba</b></a>     Post; statue, "a servant of the house of Saul" (<a href="../kjv/sa2009.htm#002">Sa2 9:2</a>), who informed David that Mephibosheth, a son of Jonathan, was alive. He afterwards dealt treacherously toward Mephibosheth, whom he slanderously misrepresented to David. 
 </p>
 <p><a name="004"><b>Zibeon</b></a>     Robber; or dyed.     (1.) A Hivite (<a href="../kjv/gen036.htm#002">Gen 36:2</a>). (2.) A Horite, and son of Seir (<a href="../kjv/gen036.htm#020">Gen 36:20</a>). 
 </p>
 <p><a name="005"><b>Zibia</b></a>     Gazelle, a Benjamite (<a href="../kjv/ch1008.htm#009">Ch1 8:9</a>). 
 </p>
 <p><a name="006"><b>Zibiah</b></a>     The mother of King Joash (<a href="../kjv/kg2012.htm#001">Kg2 12:1</a>; <a href="../kjv/ch2024.htm#001">Ch2 24:1</a>). 
 </p>
 <p><a name="007"><b>Zichri</b></a>     Remembered; illustrious.     (1.) A Benjamite chief (<a href="../kjv/ch1008.htm#019">Ch1 8:19</a>). (2.) Another of the same tribe (<a href="../kjv/ch1008.htm#023">Ch1 8:23</a>). 
 </p>
 <p><a name="008"><b>Ziddim</b></a>     Sides, a town of Naphtali (<a href="../kjv/jos019.htm#035">Jos 19:35</a>), has been identified with Kefr-Hattin, the "village of the Hittites," about 5 miles west of Tiberias. 
 </p>
 <p><a name="009"><b>Zidkijah</b></a>     The Lord is righteous, one who sealed the covenant with Nehemiah (<a href="../kjv/neh010.htm#001">Neh 10:1</a>). 
 </p>
