 <p><a name="an_5:13">5:13</a> [D] . . . . jah qaþ alla gamainþs: amen. jah hazidedun fraujan jah gatawidedun þata waurd alla so managei.</p>

 <p><a name="an_6:16">6:16</a> [D] jah warþ, swe hausidedun fiands unsarai allai, jah ohtedun allos þiudos þos bisunjane unsis, jah atdraus agis in au(g)ona ize abraba; jah ufkunþedun þatei fram guda unsaramma warþ usfulliþ þata waurstw.</p> 
 <p><a name="an_6:17">6:17</a> [D] jah in dagam jainaim managai weisun þize reikjane Iudaie, þaiei sandidedun aipistulans du Tobeiin, jah Tobeias du im.</p>
