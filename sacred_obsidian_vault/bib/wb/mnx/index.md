 <p>
 This is a partial Manx translation of the Bible.
 While the publication date of this version is unknown,
 the Bible in Manx was published in 1772, and it is unlikely
 that there were other editions.
 Hence, it is likely that this text is in the public domain in the US
 because it was published prior to 1923.
