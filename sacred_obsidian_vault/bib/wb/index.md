 <p>
 This page has links to non-English translations
 of the Bible at sacred-texts in (primarily) modern languages.
 Most of these pages require that your browser
 support <a href="../../unicode.htm">Unicode</a>.
 Some of these translations are (partial), or of the New Testament (NT) only.
 Many of these files, derived from data provided by the
 <a href="https://www.unboundbible.org/index.cfm?method=downloads.showDownloadMain">The Unbound Bible</a>
 site, are of unknown publication date,
 and hence of unknown copyright status.
 We have identified known editions where possible.
 Editions in the public domain in the US are marked with (*).
 </p>
