 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible</i>, by John Wesley, [1754-65], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa143.htm#001">Psalms Chapter 143</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa143.htm#002">Psalms 143:2</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_002">psa 143:2</a></font></span></p>Justified - Upon terms of strict justice. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa143.htm#003">Psalms 143:3</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_003">psa 143:3</a></font></span></p>For - This is not a reason of what he last said, but an argument to enforce his petition delivered, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa143.htm#001">Psa 143:1</a>. Soul - My life; nothing less will satisfy him. Dead - I am in as hopeless a condition in the eye of man, as those that have lain long in the grave. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa143.htm#005">Psalms 143:5</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_005">psa 143:5</a></font></span></p>The days - What thou hast done for thy servants in former times. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa143.htm#006">Psalms 143:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">psa 143:6</a></font></span></p>As land - Thirsteth for rain. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa143.htm#008">Psalms 143:8</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_008">psa 143:8</a></font></span></p>Morning - Seasonably and speedily.
