 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible</i>, by John Wesley, [1754-65], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro014.htm#001">Proverbs Chapter 14</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro014.htm#001">Proverbs 14:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">pro 14:1</a></font></span></p>Buildeth - Maintains and improves her family and estate. Her hands - By her idleness or sin. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro014.htm#003">Proverbs 14:3</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_003">pro 14:3</a></font></span></p>A rod - His insolent speeches as they are like a rod, offensive to others, so, they make a rod for their own backs. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro014.htm#004">Proverbs 14:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">pro 14:4</a></font></span></p>The crib - The stable is empty of food for cattle, and much more for man. In the same sense cleanness of teeth is put for famine, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/amo004.htm#006">Amo 4:6</a>. The strength - By their labours, or by diligence in husbandry, which then was principally managed by oxen. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro014.htm#006">Proverbs 14:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">pro 14:6</a></font></span></p>Findeth it not - Because he doth not seek it aright. That understandeth - That is honest and pious. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro014.htm#008">Proverbs 14:8</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_008">pro 14:8</a></font></span></p>His way - Consists not in vain speculations, but in a diligent study of his duty, and of the way to eternal happiness. The folly - The wit of ungodly men, which is really their folly, is employed only in finding out ways of deceiving. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro014.htm#009">Proverbs 14:9</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_009">pro 14:9</a></font></span></p>Favour - They find favour both with God and men. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro014.htm#010">Proverbs 14:10</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_010">pro 14:10</a></font></span></p>Bitterness - The inward griefs and joys of mens hearts, are not known to any but a man's self. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro014.htm#012">Proverbs 14:12</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_012">pro 14:12</a></font></span></p>Right - There are some evil courses which men may think to be lawful and good. The end - The event shews that they were sinful and destructive. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro014.htm#013">Proverbs 14:13</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_013">pro 14:13</a></font></span></p>In laughter - The outward signs of joy are often mixed with real sorrow. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro014.htm#014">Proverbs 14:14</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_014">pro 14:14</a></font></span></p>In heart - He who departs from God inwardly. Filled - With the fruit of his ways, the punishment of his sins. Satisfied - From the pious temper of his own heart, which cleaves to the Lord, he shall receive unspeakable comfort. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro014.htm#015">Proverbs 14:15</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_015">pro 14:15</a></font></span></p>The simple - A foolish man. Looketh - He orders his dealings in the world with due circumspection, not considering so much what other men say as what he ought to do. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro014.htm#016">Proverbs 14:16</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_016">pro 14:16</a></font></span></p>Feareth - Trembles at God's judgments when they are either inflicted or threatened. Rageth - Frets against God; or is enraged against his messengers. Confident - Secure and insensible of his danger. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro014.htm#017">Proverbs 14:17</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_017">pro 14:17</a></font></span></p>Foolishly - His passion hurries him into foolish speeches and actions. Wicked devices - One who suppresses his passion, but meditates revenge. Hated - Both by God and men. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro014.htm#018">Proverbs 14:18</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_018">pro 14:18</a></font></span></p>Inherit - They possess it as their inheritance, holding it fast, and glorying in it. Knowledge - The saving knowledge of God and of their own duty. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro014.htm#019">Proverbs 14:19</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_019">pro 14:19</a></font></span></p>The evil - Good men shall have the pre - eminency over the wicked. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro014.htm#020">Proverbs 14:20</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_020">pro 14:20</a></font></span></p>Hated - Despised and abandoned. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro014.htm#021">Proverbs 14:21</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_021">pro 14:21</a></font></span></p>Despiseth - That does not pity and relieve the poor. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro014.htm#023">Proverbs 14:23</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_023">pro 14:23</a></font></span></p>The talk - Idle talking will bring a man to poverty. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro014.htm#024">Proverbs 14:24</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_024">pro 14:24</a></font></span></p>Riches - They are a singular advantage and ornament to them. But - As for rich fools, their folly is not cured, but made worse and more manifest by their riches. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro014.htm#025">Proverbs 14:25</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_025">pro 14:25</a></font></span></p>Souls - Such as are innocent, from false accusations. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro014.htm#027">Proverbs 14:27</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_027">pro 14:27</a></font></span></p>To depart - To preserve men from destructive courses. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro014.htm#029">Proverbs 14:29</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_029">pro 14:29</a></font></span></p>Exalteth - Heb. lifteth up, like a banner, makes it known and visible to all men. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro014.htm#030">Proverbs 14:30</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_030">pro 14:30</a></font></span></p>A sound heart - Free from envy and inordinate passions. Is life - Procures and maintains the health and vigour of the body. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro014.htm#031">Proverbs 14:31</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_031">pro 14:31</a></font></span></p>His maker - Whose image the poor man bears, by whose providence he is made poor, and who hath declared himself to be their protector and avenger. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro014.htm#032">Proverbs 14:32</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_032">pro 14:32</a></font></span></p>Driven away - In his death, from God's favour and presence. Death - In his greatest dangers and distresses, yea even in death itself. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro014.htm#033">Proverbs 14:33</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_033">pro 14:33</a></font></span></p>Resteth - Is laid up and hid there. In the midst - In the heart. Made known - They will publish in all times and companies. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro014.htm#035">Proverbs 14:35</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_035">pro 14:35</a></font></span></p>Shame - Both to himself, by his foolish management of the king's affairs, and to the king who made so foolish a choice of a servant.
