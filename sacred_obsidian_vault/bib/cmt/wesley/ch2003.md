 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible</i>, by John Wesley, [1754-65], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch2003.htm#001">2 Chronicles Chapter 3</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch2003.htm#001">2 Chronicles 3:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">ch2 3:1</a></font></span></p>Moriah - It was the belief of the ancient Jews, that the temple was built on that very place, where Abraham offered up Issac. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch2003.htm#003">2 Chronicles 3:3</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_003">ch2 3:3</a></font></span></p>Instructed - By David, and by the Spirit of God. The measure - According to he measure which was first fixed. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch2003.htm#004">2 Chronicles 3:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">ch2 3:4</a></font></span></p>The height - This being a kind of turret to the building. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch2003.htm#005">2 Chronicles 3:5</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_005">ch2 3:5</a></font></span></p>Greater house - The holy place, which was thrice as large as the holy of holies. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch2003.htm#009">2 Chronicles 3:9</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_009">ch2 3:9</a></font></span></p>Nails - Each of the nails, screws, or pins, by which the golden plates were fastened to the walls, weighed, or rather was worth, fifty shekels, workmanship and all. Upper chambers - Rather, the roof. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch2003.htm#010">2 Chronicles 3:10</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_010">ch2 3:10</a></font></span></p>Image work - Or, of moveable work, not fixed to the mercy - seat, as the Mosaical cherubim, but in a moving posture. It seems, they were designed to represent the angels, who attend the Divine Majesty. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch2003.htm#013">2 Chronicles 3:13</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_013">ch2 3:13</a></font></span></p>Inward - Heb. towards the house, that is, the most holy house. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch2003.htm#014">2 Chronicles 3:14</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_014">ch2 3:14</a></font></span></p>The veil - The inner veil before the most holy place. This denoted the darkness of that dispensation and the distance at which the worshippers were kept. But at the death of Christ this veil was rent; for thro' him we are brought nigh, and have boldness, or liberty, not only to look, but to enter into the holiest. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch2003.htm#017">2 Chronicles 3:17</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_017">ch2 3:17</a></font></span></p>Jachin - That is, He shall establish. Boaz - That is, In it is strength.
