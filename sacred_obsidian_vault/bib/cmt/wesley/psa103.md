 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible</i>, by John Wesley, [1754-65], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa103.htm#001">Psalms Chapter 103</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa103.htm#005">Psalms 103:5</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_005">psa 103:5</a></font></span></p>The eagles - Which lives long in great strength and vigour. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa103.htm#011">Psalms 103:11</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_011">psa 103:11</a></font></span></p>So great - So much above their deserts and expectations. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa103.htm#014">Psalms 103:14</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_014">psa 103:14</a></font></span></p>Knoweth - The weakness and mortality of our natures, and the frailty of our condition, so that if he should let loose his hand upon us, we should be irrecoverably destroyed. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa103.htm#021">Psalms 103:21</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_021">psa 103:21</a></font></span></p>His hosts - A title often given to the angels, in regard of their vast numbers, mighty power, unanimous concurrence, and exquisite order. Ministers - This Hebrew word is commonly used of the highest and most honourable sort of servants,
