 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible</i>, by John Wesley, [1754-65], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro015.htm#001">Proverbs Chapter 15</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro015.htm#002">Proverbs 15:2</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_002">pro 15:2</a></font></span></p>Aright - Expressing what he knows prudently and gracefully. Poureth out - Plentifully, continually, and vehemently, as a fountain does waters. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro015.htm#004">Proverbs 15:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">pro 15:4</a></font></span></p>Wholesome - Which utters sound and useful counsels. Tree of life - Is greatly useful to preserve the present life, and to promote the spiritual and eternal life, both of the speaker and hearers. Perverseness - False or corrupt speeches. A breach - Disturbs and wounds the spirits, both of the speaker and hearers. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro015.htm#006">Proverbs 15:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">pro 15:6</a></font></span></p>Revenues - Though he may obtain great revenues, yet they are attended with much trouble. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro015.htm#007">Proverbs 15:7</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_007">pro 15:7</a></font></span></p>Disperse - Freely communicate to others what they know. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro015.htm#008">Proverbs 15:8</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_008">pro 15:8</a></font></span></p>Sacrifice - All the religious services, yea, the most costly; one kind being put for all the rest. Prayer - The cheapest and meanest services. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro015.htm#011">Proverbs 15:11</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_011">pro 15:11</a></font></span></p>Destruction - The place of destruction, of which men know nothing but by Divine revelation. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro015.htm#014">Proverbs 15:14</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_014">pro 15:14</a></font></span></p>Foolishness - Wickedness is meat and drink to them. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro015.htm#015">Proverbs 15:15</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_015">pro 15:15</a></font></span></p>Afflicted - Of the troubled in mind. Evil - Tedious and uncomfortable. Chearful - Heb. of a good heart. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro015.htm#016">Proverbs 15:16</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_016">pro 15:16</a></font></span></p>Trouble - Tumultuous lusts and passions, vexatious cares, and horrors of conscience. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro015.htm#019">Proverbs 15:19</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_019">pro 15:19</a></font></span></p>An hedge - As a way hedged up with thorns, troublesome, perplexed, and full of difficulties. Plain - Is easy and pleasant to him, notwithstanding all its difficulties. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro015.htm#022">Proverbs 15:22</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_022">pro 15:22</a></font></span></p>Counsel - When men do not seek, or will not receive advice from others. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro015.htm#023">Proverbs 15:23</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_023">pro 15:23</a></font></span></p>The answer - By a wise, or good and seasonable answer. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro015.htm#024">Proverbs 15:24</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_024">pro 15:24</a></font></span></p>The way - The way a wise man takes to obtain life, is to place his heart, and treasure, and conversation on things above. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro015.htm#025">Proverbs 15:25</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_025">pro 15:25</a></font></span></p>The proud - Of the mighty oppressors. The border - The estate, the border being often used, for the land within the borders. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro015.htm#026">Proverbs 15:26</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_026">pro 15:26</a></font></span></p>Pleasant - Acceptable to God. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro015.htm#027">Proverbs 15:27</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_027">pro 15:27</a></font></span></p>Troubleth - Brings God's curse upon himself and family. Gifts - Bribes given to corrupt judgment. Live - Shall preserve himself and family from ruin. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro015.htm#028">Proverbs 15:28</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_028">pro 15:28</a></font></span></p>Studieth - He speaks considerately. The mouth - Not the heart; for he rashly speaks what comes into his mouth, without the direction of his heart or conscience. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro015.htm#030">Proverbs 15:30</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_030">pro 15:30</a></font></span></p>A good report - A good name. Fat - Not only chears a man for the present, but gives him such stable comfort as revives his soul, and gives vigour to his body. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro015.htm#031">Proverbs 15:31</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_031">pro 15:31</a></font></span></p>The ear - The man that hearkens to that reproof which leads to life, seeks and delights in the company of the wise. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro015.htm#033">Proverbs 15:33</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_033">pro 15:33</a></font></span></p>The instruction - Doth instruct men in true wisdom.
