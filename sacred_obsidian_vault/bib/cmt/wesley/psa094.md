 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible</i>, by John Wesley, [1754-65], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa094.htm#001">Psalms Chapter 94</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa094.htm#004">Psalms 94:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">psa 94:4</a></font></span></p>Utter - Or pour forth freely, constantly, abundantly, as a fountain doth waters (so this Hebrew word signifies.) Hard things - Insolent, and intolerable words against thee and thy people. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa094.htm#007">Psalms 94:7</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_007">psa 94:7</a></font></span></p>Of Jacob - He who takes that name to himself, but has no regard to his people. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa094.htm#009">Psalms 94:9</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_009">psa 94:9</a></font></span></p>Planted - The word is emphatical, signifying the excellent structure of the ear, or the several organs belonging to the sense of hearing. Formed - By which word he intimates the accurate and curious workmanship of the eye. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa094.htm#010">Psalms 94:10</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_010">psa 94:10</a></font></span></p>Know - Mens thoughts and words and actions. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa094.htm#011">Psalms 94:11</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_011">psa 94:11</a></font></span></p>Thoughts - Yes, he knoweth all things, even the most secret things, as the thoughts of men; and in particular your thoughts; much more your practices. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa094.htm#012">Psalms 94:12</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_012">psa 94:12</a></font></span></p>Blessed - Those afflictions which are accompanied with Divine instructions are great and true blessings. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa094.htm#015">Psalms 94:15</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_015">psa 94:15</a></font></span></p>But - God will declare himself to be a righteous judge, and will again establish justice in the earth. Follow - They will all approve of it, and imitate this justice of God in all their actions. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa094.htm#016">Psalms 94:16</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_016">psa 94:16</a></font></span></p>Rise - To defend and help me. I looked hither and thither, but none appeared; God alone helped me. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa094.htm#017">Psalms 94:17</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_017">psa 94:17</a></font></span></p>Dwelt - In the place of silence, the grave. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa094.htm#019">Psalms 94:19</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_019">psa 94:19</a></font></span></p>Thoughts - While my heart was filled with various and perplexing thoughts, as this Hebrew word signifies. Comforts - Thy promises, and the remembrance of my former experience of thy care and kindness. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa094.htm#020">Psalms 94:20</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_020">psa 94:20</a></font></span></p>Shall - Wilt thou take part with the unrighteous powers of the world who oppress thy people. A law - By virtue of those unrighteous decrees which they have made.
