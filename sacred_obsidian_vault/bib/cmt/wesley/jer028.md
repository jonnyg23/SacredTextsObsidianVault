 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible</i>, by John Wesley, [1754-65], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer028.htm#001">Jeremiah Chapter 28</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer028.htm#001">Jeremiah 28:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">jer 28:1</a></font></span></p>The fourth year - Perhaps the fourth year of the sabbatical course is here intended. Of Gibeon - it is probable from the place where he lived, which was one of the cities of the priests; that he was a priest. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer028.htm#012">Jeremiah 28:12</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_012">jer 28:12</a></font></span></p>Then - Some time after. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer028.htm#013">Jeremiah 28:13</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_013">jer 28:13</a></font></span></p>But - Thou hast further incensed God against them, and provoked him to make their judgment heavier. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer028.htm#017">Jeremiah 28:17</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_017">jer 28:17</a></font></span></p>Died - Within two months after Jeremiah had thus prophesied; so dangerous a thing it is for ministers to teach people contrary to the revealed will of God.
