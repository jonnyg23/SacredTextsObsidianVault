 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible</i>, by John Wesley, [1754-65], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa027.htm#001">Psalms Chapter 27</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa027.htm#002">Psalms 27:2</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_002">psa 27:2</a></font></span></p>Light - My counsellor in all my difficulties, and my comforter and deliverer in all my distresses. Strength - The supporter and preserver of my life. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa027.htm#003">Psalms 27:3</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_003">psa 27:3</a></font></span></p>In this - That God is my light. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa027.htm#004">Psalms 27:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">psa 27:4</a></font></span></p>Dwell. &amp;c. - Have opportunity of constant attendance upon God. To behold - That there I may delight myself, in the contemplation of thy amiable and glorious majesty, and of thy infinite wisdom, holiness, justice, truth, and mercy. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa027.htm#005">Psalms 27:5</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_005">psa 27:5</a></font></span></p>The secret - In his tabernacle, into which mine enemies cannot come. He alludes to the ancient custom of offenders, who used to flee to the tabernacle or altar. Rock - A place high and inaccessible. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa027.htm#009">Psalms 27:9</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_009">psa 27:9</a></font></span></p>Away - From thy face or presence, or from the place of thy worship. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa027.htm#011">Psalms 27:11</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_011">psa 27:11</a></font></span></p>Because of - That I may neither fall into their hands by my folly, nor give them any occasion of triumphing over me. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa027.htm#013">Psalms 27:13</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_013">psa 27:13</a></font></span></p>The living - David was thus earnestly desirous of this mercy in this life, not because he placed his portion in these things; but because the truth and glory of God, were highly concerned in making good the promise of the kingdom to him.
