 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible</i>, by John Wesley, [1754-65], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa106.htm#001">Psalms Chapter 106</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa106.htm#004">Psalms 106:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">psa 106:4</a></font></span></p>Me - He speaks here in the name, and on the behalf of the whole nation. With - With those favours which thou dost usually and peculiarly give to thy people. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa106.htm#005">Psalms 106:5</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_005">psa 106:5</a></font></span></p>See - Enjoy. Chosen - Of thy chosen people; such as are Israelites indeed. Gladness - Such joy as thou hast formerly afforded unto thy beloved nation. Glory - That we may have occasion to glory in God's goodness towards us. Inheritance - In the congregation of thy people. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa106.htm#006">Psalms 106:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">psa 106:6</a></font></span></p>Glory - As our fathers did. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa106.htm#007">Psalms 106:7</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_007">psa 106:7</a></font></span></p>At the sea - When those wonders were but newly done, and fresh in memory. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa106.htm#008">Psalms 106:8</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_008">psa 106:8</a></font></span></p>Saved them - That he may vindicate his name from the blasphemous reproaches, which would have been cast upon it, if they had been destroyed. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa106.htm#009">Psalms 106:9</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_009">psa 106:9</a></font></span></p>Led them - As securely as if they had walked upon the dry land. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa106.htm#013">Psalms 106:13</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_013">psa 106:13</a></font></span></p>Soon - Even within three days, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo015.htm#022">Exo 15:22-23</a>. Waited not - They did not wait patiently upon God for supplies, in such manner and time as he thought fit. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa106.htm#014">Psalms 106:14</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_014">psa 106:14</a></font></span></p>Lusted - For flesh. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa106.htm#015">Psalms 106:15</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_015">psa 106:15</a></font></span></p>Souls - Into their bodies. So their inordinate desire of pampering their bodies, was the occasion of destroying them. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa106.htm#016">Psalms 106:16</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_016">psa 106:16</a></font></span></p>The saint - So called, because he was consecrated by God for that sacred office of the priesthood, in which respect all the priests are said to be holy, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev021.htm#006">Lev 21:6-8</a>. Hereby he intimates, that their envy and rebellion was not only against Aaron, but against God himself. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa106.htm#019">Psalms 106:19</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_019">psa 106:19</a></font></span></p>A calf - When they were but just brought out of Egypt by such wonders, and had seen the plagues of God upon the Egyptian idolaters, and when the law of God was but newly delivered to them in such a tremendous manner. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa106.htm#020">Psalms 106:20</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_020">psa 106:20</a></font></span></p>Their glory - God, who was indeed their glory. Into - Into the golden image of an ox or calf, which is so far from feeding his people, as the true God did the Israelites, that he must be fed by them. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa106.htm#023">Psalms 106:23</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_023">psa 106:23</a></font></span></p>Breach - God had made a wall about them; but they had made a breach in it by their sins, at which the Lord, who was now justly become their enemy, might enter to destroy them; which he had certainly done, if Moses by his prevailing intercession had not hindered him. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa106.htm#024">Psalms 106:24</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_024">psa 106:24</a></font></span></p>Despised - Preferring Egypt, and their former bondage, before it, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/num014.htm#003">Num 14:3-4</a>. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa106.htm#025">Psalms 106:25</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_025">psa 106:25</a></font></span></p>The voice - To God's command, that they should boldly enter into it. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa106.htm#026">Psalms 106:26</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_026">psa 106:26</a></font></span></p>Lifted up - He sware. Of this dreadful and irrevocable oath of God, see <a href="../../../bib/kjv/num014.htm#011">Num 14:11-12</a>. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa106.htm#027">Psalms 106:27</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_027">psa 106:27</a></font></span></p>Overthrow - He sware also (tho' not at the same time) that he would punish their sins, not only in their persons, but in their posterity. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa106.htm#028">Psalms 106:28</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_028">psa 106:28</a></font></span></p>Joined - They had communion with him, as God's people have with God in acts of his worship. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa106.htm#031">Psalms 106:31</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_031">psa 106:31</a></font></span></p>And - It was accepted and rewarded of God as an act of justice and piety. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa106.htm#037">Psalms 106:37</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_037">psa 106:37</a></font></span></p>Devils - They did not worship God as they pretended, but devils in their idols; for those spirits, which were supposed by the Heathen idolaters to inhabit in their images, were not good spirits, but evil spirits, or devils. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa106.htm#043">Psalms 106:43</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_043">psa 106:43</a></font></span></p>Counsel - By forsaking God's way, and following their own inventions. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa106.htm#045">Psalms 106:45</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_045">psa 106:45</a></font></span></p>Repented - Changed his course and dealing with them.
