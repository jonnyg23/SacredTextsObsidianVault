 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible</i>, by John Wesley, [1754-65], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/co2009.htm#001">2 Corinthians Chapter 9</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/co2009.htm#001">2 Corinthians 9:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">co2 9:1</a></font></span></p>To write to you - Largely. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/co2009.htm#002">2 Corinthians 9:2</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_002">co2 9:2</a></font></span></p>I boast to them of Macedonia - With whom he then was. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/co2009.htm#003">2 Corinthians 9:3</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_003">co2 9:3</a></font></span></p>I have sent the above mentioned brethren before me. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/co2009.htm#005">2 Corinthians 9:5</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_005">co2 9:5</a></font></span></p>Spoken of before - By me, to the Macedonians. Not as a matter of covetousness - As wrung by importunity from covetous persons. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/co2009.htm#006">2 Corinthians 9:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">co2 9:6</a></font></span></p>He that soweth sparingly shall reap sparingly; he that soweth bountifully shall reap bountifully - A general rule. God will proportion the reward to the work, and the temper whence it proceeds. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/co2009.htm#007">2 Corinthians 9:7</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_007">co2 9:7</a></font></span></p>Of necessity - Because he cannot tell how to refuse. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/co2009.htm#008">2 Corinthians 9:8</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_008">co2 9:8</a></font></span></p>How remarkable are these words! Each is loaded with matter and increases all the way it goes. All grace - Every kind of blessing. That ye may abound to every good work - God gives us everything, that we may do good therewith, and so receive more blessings. All things in this life, even rewards, are, to the faithful, seeds in order to a future harvest. <a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro022.htm#009">Pro 22:9</a> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/co2009.htm#009">2 Corinthians 9:9</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_009">co2 9:9</a></font></span></p>He hath scattered abroad - (A generous word.) With a full hand, without any anxious thought which way each grain falls. His righteousness - His beneficence, with the blessed effects of it. Remaineth for ever - Unexhausted, God still renewing his store. <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa112.htm#009">Psa 112:9</a> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/co2009.htm#010">2 Corinthians 9:10</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_010">co2 9:10</a></font></span></p>And he who supplieth seed - Opportunity and ability to help others. And bread - All things needful for your own souls and bodies. Will continually supply you with that seed, yea, multiply it to you more and more. And increase the fruits of your righteousness - The happy effects of your love to God and man. <a href="../../../bib/kjv/isa055.htm#010">Isa 55:10</a> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/co2009.htm#011">2 Corinthians 9:11</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_011">co2 9:11</a></font></span></p>Which worketh by us thanksgiving to God - Both from us who distribute, and them who receive, your bounty. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/co2009.htm#013">2 Corinthians 9:13</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_013">co2 9:13</a></font></span></p>Your avowed subjection - Openly testified by your actions. To all men - Who stand in need of it. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/co2009.htm#015">2 Corinthians 9:15</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_015">co2 9:15</a></font></span></p>His unspeakable gift - His outward and inward blessings, the number and excellence of which cannot he uttered.
