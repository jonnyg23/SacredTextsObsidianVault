 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible</i>, by John Wesley, [1754-65], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/neh001.htm#001">Nehemiah Chapter 1</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/neh001.htm#001">Nehemiah 1:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">neh 1:1</a></font></span></p>The words - Or rather, the acts, as the word often signifies. Chisleu - Which is the ninth month, containing part of November, and part of December. Year - Of Artaxerxes. Shushan - The royal city of Persia. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/neh001.htm#003">Nehemiah 1:3</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_003">neh 1:3</a></font></span></p>The province - In Judea, now a province under the Persian monarchs. The wall, &amp;c. - The walls and gates continue as Nebuchadnezzar left them; the Jews not being in a condition to rebuild them, nor having commission from the kings of Persia to do so. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/neh001.htm#004">Nehemiah 1:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">neh 1:4</a></font></span></p>The God of heaven - Who seeth in secret; secret; having no opportunity of doing it openly. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/neh001.htm#006">Nehemiah 1:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">neh 1:6</a></font></span></p>Which I pray, &amp;c. - He refers to all the prayers, which he had for some time been putting up. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/neh001.htm#011">Nehemiah 1:11</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_011">neh 1:11</a></font></span></p>To fear thy name - Those who truly desire to fear his name, shall be graciously accepted of God. This man - The king: who is but a man and therefore his heart is wholly at thy disposal. Favour with men is then comfortable, when we see it springing from the mercy of God. Cup - bearer - Whereby I had opportunity to speak to him, and some favour with him.
