 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible</i>, by John Wesley, [1754-65], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa109.htm#001">Psalms Chapter 109</a></h3> God - The author and matter of all my praises. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa109.htm#004">Psalms 109:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">psa 109:4</a></font></span></p>Adversaries - They requite my love with enmity, as it is explained <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa109.htm#005">Psa 109:5</a>. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa109.htm#006">Psalms 109:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">psa 109:6</a></font></span></p>A wicked man - Who will rule him with rigour and cruelty. Satan - To accuse him; for this was the place and posture of accusers in the Jewish courts. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa109.htm#007">Psalms 109:7</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_007">psa 109:7</a></font></span></p>Sin - Because it is not from his heart. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa109.htm#010">Psalms 109:10</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_010">psa 109:10</a></font></span></p>Desolate places - Into which they are fled for fear and shame. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa109.htm#011">Psalms 109:11</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_011">psa 109:11</a></font></span></p>Catch - Heb. ensnare, take away not only by oppression but also by cunning artificers. Stranger - Who hath no right to his goods. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa109.htm#017">Psalms 109:17</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_017">psa 109:17</a></font></span></p>Delighted not - In desiring and promoting the welfare of others. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa109.htm#018">Psalms 109:18</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_018">psa 109:18</a></font></span></p>Garment - Which a man wears constantly. Like water - Water in the cavity of the belly, between the bowels, is almost certain death. And oil soaking into any of the bones, will soon utterly destroy it. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa109.htm#020">Psalms 109:20</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_020">psa 109:20</a></font></span></p>That speak evil against my soul - With design to take away my life. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa109.htm#021">Psalms 109:21</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_021">psa 109:21</a></font></span></p>Is good - Above the mercy of all the creatures. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa109.htm#023">Psalms 109:23</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_023">psa 109:23</a></font></span></p>When - Towards the evening, when the sun is setting. The locust - Which is easily driven away with every wind.
