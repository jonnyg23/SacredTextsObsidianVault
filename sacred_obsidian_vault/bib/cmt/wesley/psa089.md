 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible</i>, by John Wesley, [1754-65], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa089.htm#001">Psalms Chapter 89</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa089.htm#001">Psalms 89:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">psa 89:1</a></font></span></p>Sing - He prefaces this, lest the following complainers of present miseries should argue ingratitude for former mercies. Faithfulness - Whatsoever hath befallen us, it proceeded not from thy unfaithfulness. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa089.htm#002">Psalms 89:2</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_002">psa 89:2</a></font></span></p>Establish - As firmly and durably as the heavens themselves. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa089.htm#003">Psalms 89:3</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_003">psa 89:3</a></font></span></p>Chosen - With David; whom I have chosen to the kingdom. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa089.htm#004">Psalms 89:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">psa 89:4</a></font></span></p>Build up - I will perpetuate the kingdom to thy posterity; which was promised upon condition, and was literally accomplished in Christ. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa089.htm#005">Psalms 89:5</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_005">psa 89:5</a></font></span></p>Heavens - The inhabitants of heaven. Faithfulness - Understand, shall be praised; which supplements are usual in scripture. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa089.htm#006">Psalms 89:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">psa 89:6</a></font></span></p>Among - The highest angels. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa089.htm#007">Psalms 89:7</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_007">psa 89:7</a></font></span></p>Feared - With a fear of reverence. Saints - The whole society of angels. All that - That encompass his throne. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa089.htm#008">Psalms 89:8</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_008">psa 89:8</a></font></span></p>Round thee - Like a girdle encompassing thee. It appears in all thy words and works. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa089.htm#010">Psalms 89:10</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_010">psa 89:10</a></font></span></p>Rhahab - Egypt. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa089.htm#012">Psalms 89:12</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_012">psa 89:12</a></font></span></p>Tabor - The several parts of the land of Canaan, both within Jordan, where mount Tabor is; and without it, where Hermon lies. Rejoice - Shall be fruitful and prosperous, and so give their inhabitants cause to rejoice. In - By thy favour. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa089.htm#014">Psalms 89:14</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_014">psa 89:14</a></font></span></p>Justice and judgment - Justice in judging. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa089.htm#015">Psalms 89:15</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_015">psa 89:15</a></font></span></p>Know - Who enjoy the presence of God and his ordinances, to which they are called by the sound of trumpets. Walk - Under the comfortable influences of thy favour. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa089.htm#016">Psalms 89:16</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_016">psa 89:16</a></font></span></p>Name - In the knowledge and remembrance of thy name, of thy infinite power and goodness. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa089.htm#017">Psalms 89:17</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_017">psa 89:17</a></font></span></p>The glory - To thee alone belongs the glory of all their valiant achievements. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa089.htm#019">Psalms 89:19</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_019">psa 89:19</a></font></span></p>Vision - Which then was the usual way by which God spake to the prophets. Holy one - To thy holy prophets; the singular number being put for the plural; especially to Samuel and Nathan. Laid help - I have provided help for my people. Upon - Upon a person of singular courage and wisdom. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa089.htm#022">Psalms 89:22</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_022">psa 89:22</a></font></span></p>Exact - Not conquer him or make him tributary. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa089.htm#025">Psalms 89:25</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_025">psa 89:25</a></font></span></p>Set - Establish his power and dominion. The sea - The mid - land sea. The rivers - Euphrates, called rivers, in regard of divers branches of it, and rivers which flow into it. So here is a description of the uttermost bounds of the promised land. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa089.htm#027">Psalms 89:27</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_027">psa 89:27</a></font></span></p>My first - born - As he calls me father, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa089.htm#026">Psa 89:26</a>, so I will make him my son, yea my first - born; who had divers privileges above other sons. This and the following passage in some sort agree to David, but are properly accomplished in Christ. Higher - This also was in some sort accomplished in David, but more fully in the Messiah. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa089.htm#029">Psalms 89:29</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_029">psa 89:29</a></font></span></p>For ever - To sit upon the throne for ever, as the next words explain it. This was accomplished only in Christ. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa089.htm#037">Psalms 89:37</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_037">psa 89:37</a></font></span></p>A witness - The rainbow, which is God's faithful witness, a token of God's everlasting covenant between God and every living creature for perpetual generations, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/gen009.htm#012">Gen 9:12</a>, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/gen009.htm#016">Gen 9:16</a>. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa089.htm#038">Psalms 89:38</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_038">psa 89:38</a></font></span></p>But - Having hitherto declared the certainty of God's promises, he now humbly expostulates with God about it. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa089.htm#045">Psalms 89:45</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_045">psa 89:45</a></font></span></p>Youth - The youthful and flourishing estate of David's kingdom was very short, and reached not beyond his next successor. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa089.htm#047">Psalms 89:47</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_047">psa 89:47</a></font></span></p>Short - Our time, the time of our king and kingdom, in whose name the psalmist puts up this petition. Wherefore - Wherefore hast thou made us and our king (and consequently all other men, whose condition is nothing better than ours) in vain, or to so little purpose? Didst thou raise us and him, settle the crown upon David's head by a solemn covenant, and vouchsafe so many and great promises and privileges, and all this but for a few years, that our crown and glory should be taken from us, within a little time after it was put upon our heads? 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa089.htm#048">Psalms 89:48</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_048">psa 89:48</a></font></span></p>What man - All men at their best estate are mortal and miserable; kings and people must unavoidably die by the conditions of their natures; and therefore, Lord, do not increase our affliction. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa089.htm#050">Psalms 89:50</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_050">psa 89:50</a></font></span></p>How I - We thy servants; our king and his people; of whom he speaks as of one person. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa089.htm#051">Psalms 89:51</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_051">psa 89:51</a></font></span></p>Anointed - By whom he seems to understand either first the kings of Judah, the singular number being put for the plural; and by their footsteps may be meant either their ways or actions, or the memorials of their ancient splendor; or secondly the Messiah, whom the Jews continually expected for a long time, which being well known to many of the Heathens, they reproached the Jews, with the vanity of this expectation. And by the footsteps of the Messiah, he may understand his coming. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa089.htm#052">Psalms 89:52</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_052">psa 89:52</a></font></span></p>Blessed - Let thine enemies reproach thee and thy promises concerning the sending of the Messiah, I will heartily bless and praise thee for them, and encourage myself with them.
