 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible</i>, by John Wesley, [1754-65], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa030.htm#001">Psalms Chapter 30</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa030.htm#005">Psalms 30:5</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_005">psa 30:5</a></font></span></p>Cometh - Speedily and in due season. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa030.htm#007">Psalms 30:7</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_007">psa 30:7</a></font></span></p>Mountain - My kingdom: kingdoms are usually called mountains in prophetical writings. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa030.htm#009">Psalms 30:9</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_009">psa 30:9</a></font></span></p>Profit - What wilt thou gain by it? The dust - Shall they that are dead celebrate thy goodness in the land of the living? Or, shall my dust praise thee? 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa030.htm#011">Psalms 30:11</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_011">psa 30:11</a></font></span></p>Sackcloth - Given me occasion to put off that sackcloth, which they used to wear in times of mourning, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/est004.htm#001">Est 4:1</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa035.htm#013">Psa 35:13</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/isa032.htm#011">Isa 32:11</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/joe001.htm#013">Joe 1:13</a>. Girded - With joy, as with a garment, surrounding me on every side. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa030.htm#012">Psalms 30:12</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_012">psa 30:12</a></font></span></p>My glory - My tongue.
