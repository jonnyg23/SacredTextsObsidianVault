 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible</i>, by John Wesley, [1754-65], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa073.htm#001">Psalms Chapter 73</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa073.htm#001">Psalms 73:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">psa 73:1</a></font></span></p>A clean heart - To all true Israelites, who love God, and serve him in spirit and truth. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa073.htm#002">Psalms 73:2</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_002">psa 73:2</a></font></span></p>My feet - My faith in God's providence, was almost overthrown. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa073.htm#004">Psalms 73:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">psa 73:4</a></font></span></p>No bands - They are not dragged to death, by the sentence of the magistrate, which they deserve. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa073.htm#005">Psalms 73:5</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_005">psa 73:5</a></font></span></p>As other men - As good men frequently are. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa073.htm#008">Psalms 73:8</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_008">psa 73:8</a></font></span></p>And speak - Boasting of their oppressions. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa073.htm#009">Psalms 73:9</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_009">psa 73:9</a></font></span></p>Against - Against God, blaspheming his name, and deriding his providence. Walketh - Using all manner of liberty, reproaching all sorts of persons. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa073.htm#010">Psalms 73:10</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_010">psa 73:10</a></font></span></p>Turn - To this wicked company. Waters - And partake of the same prosperity with their leaders. God seems to give them a full cup of consolation, as if he would wring out all his blessings upon them. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa073.htm#012">Psalms 73:12</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_012">psa 73:12</a></font></span></p>Behold - These seem to be the words of the psalmist, summing up the matter. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa073.htm#013">Psalms 73:13</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_013">psa 73:13</a></font></span></p>In vain - Hence I was sometimes tempted, to think that religion was a vain, unprofitable thing. True religion is here described by its two principal parts, the cleansing of the heart, and the hands. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa073.htm#015">Psalms 73:15</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_015">psa 73:15</a></font></span></p>Offend - By grieving, discouraging and tempting them to revolt from God. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa073.htm#016">Psalms 73:16</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_016">psa 73:16</a></font></span></p>To know - To find out the reason of this providence. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa073.htm#017">Psalms 73:17</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_017">psa 73:17</a></font></span></p>Until - 'Till I consulted the word of God. He alludes to the practice of those times, which was, in difficult cases to resort to God's sanctuary, and the oracle therein. Their end - There I learned that their prosperity was short. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa073.htm#019">Psalms 73:19</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_019">psa 73:19</a></font></span></p>Terrors - With God's dreadful judgements unexpectedly seizing upon them. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa073.htm#020">Psalms 73:20</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_020">psa 73:20</a></font></span></p>They awake - Out of the pleasant dream of this vain life. Despise - Thou shalt make them despicable both to themselves and to all others; raise them to shame, and everlasting contempt. Image - All their felicity and glory, which shall be evidently discerned to be, no real or substantial thing, but a mere image or shadow. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa073.htm#021">Psalms 73:21</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_021">psa 73:21</a></font></span></p>Pricked - I was deeply wounded with disquieting thoughts. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa073.htm#022">Psalms 73:22</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_022">psa 73:22</a></font></span></p>Nevertheless - Although I gave thee just cause to cast me off, yet thou didst continue thy care and kindness to me. Hast held - That my faith might not fail. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa073.htm#027">Psalms 73:27</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_027">psa 73:27</a></font></span></p>Go a whoring - Those who revolt from thee, to work wickedness; which is called whoredom in scripture.
