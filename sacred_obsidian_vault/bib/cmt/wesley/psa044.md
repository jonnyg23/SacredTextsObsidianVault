 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible</i>, by John Wesley, [1754-65], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa044.htm#001">Psalms Chapter 44</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa044.htm#004">Psalms 44:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">psa 44:4</a></font></span></p>My king - The whole people speak as one man, being united in one body. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa044.htm#011">Psalms 44:11</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_011">psa 44:11</a></font></span></p>Scattered - Those who were not slain are carried into captivity, and dispersed in several places. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa044.htm#016">Psalms 44:16</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_016">psa 44:16</a></font></span></p>Avenger - Who executeth both God's and his own vengeance upon me. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa044.htm#017">Psalms 44:17</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_017">psa 44:17</a></font></span></p>Yet - Although we cannot excuse ourselves from many other sins, yet through thy grace we have kept ourselves from apostacy and idolatry, notwithstanding all examples and provocations. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa044.htm#018">Psalms 44:18</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_018">psa 44:18</a></font></span></p>Turned - From thy worship to idols. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa044.htm#019">Psalms 44:19</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_019">psa 44:19</a></font></span></p>Broken us - By inflicting upon us one breach after another, thou hast at last brought us to this pass. The place - A place extremely desolate, such as dragons love, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/isa013.htm#021">Isa 13:21-22</a>, and therefore full of horror, and danger. Covered us - With deadly horrors and miseries. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa044.htm#022">Psalms 44:22</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_022">psa 44:22</a></font></span></p>Yea - Because we are constant in thy worship, which they abhor. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa044.htm#025">Psalms 44:25</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_025">psa 44:25</a></font></span></p>Our soul - Our persons. Our belly - We are not only thrown down to the earth, but we lie there like dead carcases.
