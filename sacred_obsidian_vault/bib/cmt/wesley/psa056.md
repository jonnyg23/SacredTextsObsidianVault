 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible</i>, by John Wesley, [1754-65], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa056.htm#001">Psalms Chapter 56</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa056.htm#004">Psalms 56:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">psa 56:4</a></font></span></p>Will praise - I will praise the Lord for his word, for his promises of protection and deliverance, made to his people. Flesh - Infirm and mortal men, called flesh by way of contempt, as <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa078.htm#039">Psa 78:39</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/isa031.htm#003">Isa 31:3</a>. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa056.htm#006">Psalms 56:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">psa 56:6</a></font></span></p>They gather - After they have severally employed their thoughts against me, they meet together to compare them, and to put them in execution. Hide - They lurk secretly, that they may pry into my most private actions. Steps - That they may find some occasion to reproach me. Soul - Or, life, to take it away. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa056.htm#007">Psalms 56:7</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_007">psa 56:7</a></font></span></p>Escape - Shall they secure themselves by injurious and malicious practices. The people - These who are mine enemies. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa056.htm#008">Psalms 56:8</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_008">psa 56:8</a></font></span></p>Wanderings - How I have been hunted from place to place. Put - Regard and pity them. Are they not - But why do I pray to God to do that which he hath already done? 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa056.htm#012">Psalms 56:12</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_012">psa 56:12</a></font></span></p>Thy vows - I had made vows to express my gratitude, and resolve to perform them. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa056.htm#013">Psalms 56:13</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_013">psa 56:13</a></font></span></p>Walk - That I may serve and glorify thee. The light - ln this life.
