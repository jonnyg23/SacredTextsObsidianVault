 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible</i>, by John Wesley, [1754-65], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa112.htm#001">Psalms Chapter 112</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa112.htm#002">Psalms 112:2</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_002">psa 112:2</a></font></span></p>Generation - The posterity. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa112.htm#003">Psalms 112:3</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_003">psa 112:3</a></font></span></p>Righteousness - The fruit or reward of his righteousness, which is God's blessing upon his estate. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa112.htm#004">Psalms 112:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">psa 112:4</a></font></span></p>Darkness - In the troubles and calamities of life. He - The upright man. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa112.htm#005">Psalms 112:5</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_005">psa 112:5</a></font></span></p>Lendeth - Gives freely to some, and lends to others according to the variety of their conditions. Affairs - His domestick affairs. Discretion - Not getting his estate unjustly, nor casting it away prodigally, nor yet withholding it from such as need it. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa112.htm#006">Psalms 112:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">psa 112:6</a></font></span></p>Moved - Though he may for a season be afflicted, yet he shall not be eternally destroyed. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa112.htm#007">Psalms 112:7</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_007">psa 112:7</a></font></span></p>Evil tidings - At the report of approaching calamities. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa112.htm#009">Psalms 112:9</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_009">psa 112:9</a></font></span></p>Dispersed - His goods, freely and liberally. Righteousness - His liberality, or the reward of it. Ever - What he gives is not lost, but indeed is the only part of his estate, which will abide with him to all eternity. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa112.htm#010">Psalms 112:10</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_010">psa 112:10</a></font></span></p>The desire - Either of the misery of good men; or of his own constant prosperity.
