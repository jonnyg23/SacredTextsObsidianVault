 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible</i>, by John Wesley, [1754-65], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer043.htm#001">Jeremiah Chapter 43</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer043.htm#007">Jeremiah 43:7</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_007">jer 43:7</a></font></span></p>Tahpanhes - It was at this time the place where the king of Egypt made his residence. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer043.htm#012">Jeremiah 43:12</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_012">jer 43:12</a></font></span></p>Carry them - He shall carry away both the idols, and the inhabitants of Egypt captive. With the land - With the spoils of the land of Egypt, he shall clothe his army. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer043.htm#013">Jeremiah 43:13</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_013">jer 43:13</a></font></span></p>Beth - shemesh - Beth - shemesh is the name of a city, which had its name from a famous temple of the sun.
