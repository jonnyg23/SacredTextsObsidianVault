 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible</i>, by John Wesley, [1754-65], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/amo007.htm#001">Amos Chapter 7</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/amo007.htm#001">Amos 7:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">amo 7:1</a></font></span></p>Thus - This is the first of five prophetic representations of what was coming upon this people. The latter growth - The shooting up of the first growth being too luxuriant, they often eat it down with cattle; but if the second growth were eat up, it marred the whole harvest. Mowed - It is supposed the first mowing of the corn in the blade was for the king's use; and after this the second springing grew up to the harvest. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/amo007.htm#002">Amos 7:2</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_002">amo 7:2</a></font></span></p>It came to pass - In the vision. By whom - How shall any of Jacob escape, if thou dost cast him down? 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/amo007.htm#003">Amos 7:3</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_003">amo 7:3</a></font></span></p>Repented - This is spoken after the manner of men. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/amo007.htm#004">Amos 7:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">amo 7:4</a></font></span></p>Shewed - In vision. Called - Commanded fire from heaven. A part - Of the land too. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/amo007.htm#007">Amos 7:7</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_007">amo 7:7</a></font></span></p>By a plumb - line - Strongly and beautifully built. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/amo007.htm#008">Amos 7:8</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_008">amo 7:8</a></font></span></p>Set a plumb - line - I will exactly measure the whole ten tribes. Pass by them - I will no more forbear, but will pull down all that is faulty. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/amo007.htm#009">Amos 7:9</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_009">amo 7:9</a></font></span></p>The high places - The temples on high mountains built to idols. Of Isaac - The seed of Isaac. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/amo007.htm#010">Amos 7:10</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_010">amo 7:10</a></font></span></p>In the midst - Openly, and publickly, endeavouring to stir up Israel to sedition or rebellion. The land - The people cannot bear all his harsh predictions. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/amo007.htm#011">Amos 7:11</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_011">amo 7:11</a></font></span></p>Jeroboam shall die - He nowhere said so, but spake of his house distinguished from his person, as <a href="../../../bib/kjv/amo007.htm#009">Amo 7:9</a>. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/amo007.htm#013">Amos 7:13</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_013">amo 7:13</a></font></span></p>The king's court - To which therefore thou oughtest to shew more respect, and not thus to affront the king in his own house. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/amo007.htm#014">Amos 7:14</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_014">amo 7:14</a></font></span></p>No prophet - Not originally, or by study, or by any human designation. An herdman - By breeding and occupation I was and still am, an herdman. Sycamore fruit - This fruit was good food for man, or cattle. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/amo007.htm#016">Amos 7:16</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_016">amo 7:16</a></font></span></p>The house of Isaac - The posterity of Isaac. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/amo007.htm#017">Amos 7:17</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_017">amo 7:17</a></font></span></p>In a polluted land - Among the Heathen. Israel - The ten tribes.
