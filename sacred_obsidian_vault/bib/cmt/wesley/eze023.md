 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible</i>, by John Wesley, [1754-65], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze023.htm#001">Ezekiel Chapter 23</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze023.htm#002">Ezekiel 23:2</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_002">eze 23:2</a></font></span></p>Two women - Judah, and Israel, two kingdoms. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze023.htm#003">Ezekiel 23:3</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_003">eze 23:3</a></font></span></p>Whoredoms - Idolatry. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze023.htm#004">Ezekiel 23:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">eze 23:4</a></font></span></p>Aholah - That is, his own tabernacle; for Israel falling off from the house of David, fell off from the tabernacle, or temple of God; so that all the temple they had was of their own making. The elder - Greater for number of tribes, and for power, wealth, and for multitudes of people. Aholibah - That is, my tabernacle in her: the two tribes had the temple of God with them. Mine - By solemn marriage - covenant. Bare sons - Were fruitful and brought forth children to me; they increased in numbers of people; and among these, some there were that were children of God by faith, love, and obedience. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze023.htm#005">Ezekiel 23:5</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_005">eze 23:5</a></font></span></p>Played the harlot - United in idolatry, with the Assyrians. Mine - When under my government, and protection. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze023.htm#006">Ezekiel 23:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">eze 23:6</a></font></span></p>Horsemen - Skillful in riding, and well furnished with choice horses. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze023.htm#007">Ezekiel 23:7</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_007">eze 23:7</a></font></span></p>With all - Other nations, with whom she had commerce. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze023.htm#010">Ezekiel 23:10</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_010">eze 23:10</a></font></span></p>Discovered - Stript her naked, and exposed her to shame. Took her sons - Captives. Slew her - The kingdom of Israel, under Hoshea, was by Salmanesar utterly destroyed. They - The Assyrians, had executed God's just displeasure upon her. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze023.htm#015">Ezekiel 23:15</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_015">eze 23:15</a></font></span></p>Girded - With soldiers belts, which includes the rest of the habit of soldiers. In dyed attire - Both rich, comely, large, and of divers colours. Princes - Of princely aspect and majesty. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze023.htm#017">Ezekiel 23:17</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_017">eze 23:17</a></font></span></p>Alienated - She grew weary of the Chaldeans. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze023.htm#018">Ezekiel 23:18</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_018">eze 23:18</a></font></span></p>Discovered - Made it appear to all, far and near. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze023.htm#019">Ezekiel 23:19</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_019">eze 23:19</a></font></span></p>By - Remembering her idolatries in Egypt, her alliance with it in days past, which she now resolved to act over again. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze023.htm#020">Ezekiel 23:20</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_020">eze 23:20</a></font></span></p>Paramours - The nations, that were confederate with the Egyptians. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze023.htm#023">Ezekiel 23:23</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_023">eze 23:23</a></font></span></p>Pekod - Pekod is the province between Tigris, and Lycus; in this was old Nineveh. Shoa - Either Sia in Armenia, or the Sohia, among which were the Adiabeni, and this contained the middle part of the kingdom of Babylon. Koa - This bordered upon Media, the inhabitants were called Kohai, and dwelt about Arbela. And all - All subjects of the Assyrian monarchy. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze023.htm#024">Ezekiel 23:24</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_024">eze 23:24</a></font></span></p>And wheels - Lest in their march the carriage wheels should break, a store of these were provided. An assembly - A mighty confluence of people. I will set - Give them a power in right of conquest over their rebels, as well as mine, and I will give them a spirit of judgment to discern the greatness of this people's sins. Judge - Condemn, and execute sentence upon thee. According - To their will, power, wrath, and custom, against rebels; for these are their rules of judgment. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze023.htm#025">Ezekiel 23:25</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_025">eze 23:25</a></font></span></p>I will set my jealousy - As a jealous provoked husband, I will be as much against thee as they are. Thy residue - Either the people, who hid themselves in vaults and cellars, or what the Chaldeans cannot carry away, all this shall be devoured by fire. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze023.htm#029">Ezekiel 23:29</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_029">eze 23:29</a></font></span></p>Take away - Deprive thee of the comfortable use of all thy labour, which they will exact of thee in captivity. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze023.htm#032">Ezekiel 23:32</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_032">eze 23:32</a></font></span></p>It - Is large, and contains what will last many years, even 'till the seventy years be expired. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze023.htm#034">Ezekiel 23:34</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_034">eze 23:34</a></font></span></p>Thou - Shalt stagger with sorrows, that shall intoxicate, and astonish. Suck it out - The dregs shalt thou drink, and multiply thine own sorrows. Break the sheards - To suck out what remains. And pluck - Revenging thyself upon thyself. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze023.htm#035">Ezekiel 23:35</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_035">eze 23:35</a></font></span></p>Bear thou - The guilt, I will impute it, the punishment, I will not pardon it. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze023.htm#038">Ezekiel 23:38</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_038">eze 23:38</a></font></span></p>In the same day - When they had newly polluted themselves with idolatry and murder, they thrust into the temple. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze023.htm#039">Ezekiel 23:39</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_039">eze 23:39</a></font></span></p>My house - Nay, these things have been in my house. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze023.htm#040">Ezekiel 23:40</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_040">eze 23:40</a></font></span></p>Wash thyself - After the manner of harlots. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze023.htm#041">Ezekiel 23:41</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_041">eze 23:41</a></font></span></p>Sattest - Prepared to feast them. A stately bed - A magnificent bed, on which women sat to feast, when men leaned on their sides. Incense - Offered to their idols. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze023.htm#042">Ezekiel 23:42</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_042">eze 23:42</a></font></span></p>A voice - A shout for joy, that there was a treaty of peace between the Jews, and the Chaldeans. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze023.htm#045">Ezekiel 23:45</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_045">eze 23:45</a></font></span></p>Righteous men - Men that keep the law of their God. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze023.htm#046">Ezekiel 23:46</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_046">eze 23:46</a></font></span></p>Upon them - Against the Jews, the children of this Aholibah. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze023.htm#047">Ezekiel 23:47</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_047">eze 23:47</a></font></span></p>The company - The Babylonian army. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze023.htm#048">Ezekiel 23:48</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_048">eze 23:48</a></font></span></p>Lewdness - Idolatry. And indeed we do not read of any after their return out of this captivity. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze023.htm#049">Ezekiel 23:49</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_049">eze 23:49</a></font></span></p>They - The Babylonians. The sins - The guilt of worshipping idols; and you shall bear the punishment of idolaters.
