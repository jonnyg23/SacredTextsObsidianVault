 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible</i>, by John Wesley, [1754-65], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze025.htm#001">Ezekiel Chapter 25</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze025.htm#003">Ezekiel 25:3</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_003">eze 25:3</a></font></span></p>Aha - When thou shouldest have pitied, thou didst proudly insult over my people. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze025.htm#004">Ezekiel 25:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">eze 25:4</a></font></span></p>The men of the east - The Arabians, associates of Nebuchadnezzar, who recompensed their service, with giving them this country when it was conquered, as it was five years after the desolation of Jerusalem. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze025.htm#005">Ezekiel 25:5</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_005">eze 25:5</a></font></span></p>Rabbah - The royal city, called since Philadelphia from the king of Egypt who built it. The Ammonites - The land they dwelt in. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze025.htm#007">Ezekiel 25:7</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_007">eze 25:7</a></font></span></p>Know - Thus God will bring those that were strangers to him into an acquaintance with him, and it will be a blessed effect of their calamities. How much better is it, to be poor and know God, than to be rich, and ignorant of him? 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze025.htm#008">Ezekiel 25:8</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_008">eze 25:8</a></font></span></p>Seir - The seed of Esau, the Edomites. Seir was the mountain where they first planted themselves. Is like - Are no more a select people than others. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze025.htm#009">Ezekiel 25:9</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_009">eze 25:9</a></font></span></p>The side - That part of his country which was best fortified. Bethjeshimoth - An ancient city; it was a fortress toward the desert, which watched lest any should make an inroad on the country. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze025.htm#010">Ezekiel 25:10</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_010">eze 25:10</a></font></span></p>With the Ammonites - As I have given Ammon, so I will with them give Moab to the Chaldeans, who will give it to the Arabians. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze025.htm#013">Ezekiel 25:13</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_013">eze 25:13</a></font></span></p>Teman - A country in the southern coast of Edom. Dedan - Adjoining to Edom. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze025.htm#015">Ezekiel 25:15</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_015">eze 25:15</a></font></span></p>It - Israel. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze025.htm#016">Ezekiel 25:16</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_016">eze 25:16</a></font></span></p>The Cherethim - The bowmen, the strength of Philistia. The remnant - Who had escaped the sword of Samuel, David, Hezekiah, and of Psammetichus king of Egypt.
