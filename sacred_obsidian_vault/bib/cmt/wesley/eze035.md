 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible</i>, by John Wesley, [1754-65], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze035.htm#001">Ezekiel Chapter 35</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze035.htm#002">Ezekiel 35:2</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_002">eze 35:2</a></font></span></p>Mount Seir - The Edomites, who inhabited it. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze035.htm#005">Ezekiel 35:5</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_005">eze 35:5</a></font></span></p>Their iniquity - When their iniquity was punished on them, which brought them to final ruin. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze035.htm#006">Ezekiel 35:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">eze 35:6</a></font></span></p>And blood - Thy guilt, and my just revenge of innocent blood. Hast not hated - Thou hast loved, rather than hated, blood - shed; therefore vengeance for it follows thee. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze035.htm#007">Ezekiel 35:7</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_007">eze 35:7</a></font></span></p>That passeth out - All travellers that go to or from Edom. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze035.htm#009">Ezekiel 35:9</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_009">eze 35:9</a></font></span></p>Return - To their former glory. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze035.htm#010">Ezekiel 35:10</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_010">eze 35:10</a></font></span></p>Though - Though God was with Israel. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze035.htm#011">Ezekiel 35:11</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_011">eze 35:11</a></font></span></p>Judged - Punished thee. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze035.htm#014">Ezekiel 35:14</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_014">eze 35:14</a></font></span></p>The whole earth - The inhabitants of all the countries round about thee. Rejoiceth - Is in peace and plenty.
