 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible</i>, by John Wesley, [1754-65], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1006.htm#001">1 Chronicles Chapter 6</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1006.htm#010">1 Chronicles 6:10</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_010">ch1 6:10</a></font></span></p>The priest's office - So did all the rest, but it is implied that he did it worthily, he filled his place, and valiantly discharged his office in Uzziah's time. Solomon built - In Solomon's temple; so called to distinguish it from the second temple which was built or in building when these books were written. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1006.htm#044">1 Chronicles 6:44</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_044">ch1 6:44</a></font></span></p>Ethan - Called also Jeduthun, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1009.htm#016">Ch1 9:16</a>, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch2035.htm#015">Ch2 35:15</a>, and in the titles of divers psalms. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1006.htm#054">1 Chronicles 6:54</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_054">ch1 6:54</a></font></span></p>Castles - So called, not only because, walled and well guarded by the country; but because they and their possessions were in a particular manner the care of divine providence. As God was their portion, so God was their protector. And a cottage will be a castle to those that abide under the shadow of the Almighty. The lot - Or, the first lot. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1006.htm#066">1 Chronicles 6:66</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_066">ch1 6:66</a></font></span></p>Coasts - Or, of their borders, of their country contained within its borders.
