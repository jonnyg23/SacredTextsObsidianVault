 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible</i>, by John Wesley, [1754-65], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa096.htm#001">Psalms Chapter 96</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa096.htm#001">Psalms 96:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">psa 96:1</a></font></span></p>O sing - Upon this new and great occasion, not the removal of the ark, but the coming of the Messiah. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa096.htm#004">Psalms 96:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">psa 96:4</a></font></span></p>Gods - The gods of the nations, as the next verse expounds it. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa096.htm#006">Psalms 96:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">psa 96:6</a></font></span></p>Before him - In his presence. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa096.htm#009">Psalms 96:9</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_009">psa 96:9</a></font></span></p>Beauty - Cloathed with all the gifts and graces, which are necessary in God's worship. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa096.htm#010">Psalms 96:10</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_010">psa 96:10</a></font></span></p>Reigneth - God hath now set up his kingdom in the world. Established - The nations of the world shall by the means of it enjoy an established and lasting peace. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa096.htm#013">Psalms 96:13</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_013">psa 96:13</a></font></span></p>Before - At the presence and approach of their Lord and Maker. Cometh - To set up his throne among all the nations of the earth.
