 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible</i>, by John Wesley, [1754-65], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa012.htm#001">Psalms Chapter 12</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa012.htm#004">Psalms 12:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">psa 12:4</a></font></span></p>Prevail - By raising and spreading evil reports concerning him. Our own - At our own disposal to speak what we please, who can control or restrain us? 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa012.htm#005">Psalms 12:5</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_005">psa 12:5</a></font></span></p>Puffeth - From him that despises him, and hopes to destroy him with a puff of breath. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa012.htm#006">Psalms 12:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">psa 12:6</a></font></span></p>Pure - Without the least mixture of falsehood; and therefore shall infallibly be fulfilled. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa012.htm#007">Psalms 12:7</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_007">psa 12:7</a></font></span></p>Thou shalt keep them - Thy words or promises: these thou wilt observe and keep, both now, and from this generation for ever. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa012.htm#008">Psalms 12:8</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_008">psa 12:8</a></font></span></p>Walk - They fill all places, and go about boldly and securely.
