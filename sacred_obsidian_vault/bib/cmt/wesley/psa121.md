 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible</i>, by John Wesley, [1754-65], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa121.htm#001">Psalms Chapter 121</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa121.htm#001">Psalms 121:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">psa 121:1</a></font></span></p>Hills - To Sion and Moriah, which are called the holy mountains. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa121.htm#005">Psalms 121:5</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_005">psa 121:5</a></font></span></p>Shade - To keep thee from the burning heat of the sun. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa121.htm#006">Psalms 121:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">psa 121:6</a></font></span></p>Smite - With excessive heat. Moon - With that cold and moisture which come into the air by it. Intemperate heats and colds are the springs of many diseases.
