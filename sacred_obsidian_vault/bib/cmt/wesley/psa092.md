 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible</i>, by John Wesley, [1754-65], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa092.htm#001">Psalms Chapter 92</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa092.htm#005">Psalms 92:5</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_005">psa 92:5</a></font></span></p>Thoughts - Thy counsels in the government of the world and of thy church. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa092.htm#006">Psalms 92:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">psa 92:6</a></font></span></p>A brutish man - Who is led by sense, not by reason and faith. This - The depth of God's counsels and works. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa092.htm#010">Psalms 92:10</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_010">psa 92:10</a></font></span></p>Anointed - I shall have cause of testifying my joy by anointing myself, as the manner was at all joyful solemnities. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa092.htm#012">Psalms 92:12</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_012">psa 92:12</a></font></span></p>Palm - tree - Which is constantly green and flourishing. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa092.htm#013">Psalms 92:13</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_013">psa 92:13</a></font></span></p>Planted - Whom God by his gracious providence has fixed there. The house - In its courts; he means in the church of God, whereof all good men are living members. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa092.htm#014">Psalms 92:14</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_014">psa 92:14</a></font></span></p>Old age - Their last days shall be their best days, wherein they shall grow in grace, and increase in blessedness.
