 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible</i>, by John Wesley, [1754-65], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro005.htm#001">Proverbs Chapter 5</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro005.htm#003">Proverbs 5:3</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_003">pro 5:3</a></font></span></p>The lips - It concerns thee to get and to use discretion, that thou mayest be able to resist those temptations to which thou art exposed. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro005.htm#004">Proverbs 5:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">pro 5:4</a></font></span></p>But - The effect of that to which she entices men, is destruction. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro005.htm#005">Proverbs 5:5</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_005">pro 5:5</a></font></span></p>Feet - Her manner of life. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro005.htm#006">Proverbs 5:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">pro 5:6</a></font></span></p>Lest - To prevent thy serious consideration. Moveable - She transforms herself into several shapes, and has a thousand arts to ensnare. Know - Thou canst not discover all her practice. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro005.htm#009">Proverbs 5:9</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_009">pro 5:9</a></font></span></p>Honour - Thy dignity and reputation, the strength of thy body and mind. Years - The flower of thine age. The cruel - To the harlot, who though she pretends love, yet in truth is one of the most cruel creatures in the world, wasting thy estate and body without pity, and damming thy soul for ever. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro005.htm#010">Proverbs 5:10</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_010">pro 5:10</a></font></span></p>Strangers - Not only the strange women themselves, but others who are in league with them. Labors - Wealth gotten by thy labours. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro005.htm#014">Proverbs 5:14</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_014">pro 5:14</a></font></span></p>A moment - In how little a time am I now come into remediless misery! Assembly - And that in the congregation of Israel, where I was taught better things. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro005.htm#015">Proverbs 5:15</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_015">pro 5:15</a></font></span></p>Drink - Content thyself with those delights which God alloweth thee in the sober use of the marriage - bed. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro005.htm#016">Proverbs 5:16</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_016">pro 5:16</a></font></span></p>Fountains - Thy children proceeding from thy wife and from thyself. Fountains are here put for rivers flowing from them. Dispersed - They shall in due time appear abroad to thy comfort, and for the good of others. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro005.htm#018">Proverbs 5:18</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_018">pro 5:18</a></font></span></p>Fountain - Thy wife. Blessed - With children; for barrenness was esteemed a curse among the Israelites. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro005.htm#019">Proverbs 5:19</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_019">pro 5:19</a></font></span></p>Satisfy thee - At all convenient times: for that there may be excess in the marriage - bed is manifest. Ravished - Love her fervently. It is an hyperbolical expression. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro005.htm#022">Proverbs 5:22</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_022">pro 5:22</a></font></span></p>Holden - He is in perfect bondage to his lusts, and is neither able nor wiling to set himself at liberty. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro005.htm#023">Proverbs 5:23</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_023">pro 5:23</a></font></span></p>Die - He shall die in his sins. Astray - From the way of life, and from eternal salvation.
