 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible</i>, by John Wesley, [1754-65], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/amo005.htm#001">Amos Chapter 5</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/amo005.htm#002">Amos 5:2</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_002">amo 5:2</a></font></span></p>The virgin - So she was, when first espoused to God. Upon her land - Broken to pieces upon her own land, and so left as a broken vessel. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/amo005.htm#003">Amos 5:3</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_003">amo 5:3</a></font></span></p>By a thousand - That sent out one thousand soldiers. An hundred - Shall lose nine parts of them. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/amo005.htm#004">Amos 5:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">amo 5:4</a></font></span></p>Ye shall live - It shall be well with you. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/amo005.htm#005">Amos 5:5</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_005">amo 5:5</a></font></span></p>Seek not - Consult not, worship not the idol at Bethel, Gilgal, or Beersheba. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/amo005.htm#006">Amos 5:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">amo 5:6</a></font></span></p>The house of Joseph - The kingdom of the ten tribes, the chief whereof was Ephraim, the son of Joseph. In Beth - el - If once this fire breaks out, all your idols in Beth - el shall not be able to quench it. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/amo005.htm#007">Amos 5:7</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_007">amo 5:7</a></font></span></p>Ye - Rulers and judges. Judgment - The righteous sentence of the law. To wormwood - Proverbially understood; bitterness, injustice and oppression. Leave off - Make to cease in your courts of judicature. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/amo005.htm#008">Amos 5:8</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_008">amo 5:8</a></font></span></p>The seven stars - A constellation, whose rising about September was usually accompanied with sweet showers. Orion - Which arising about November brings usually cold, rains and frosts intermixt very seasonable for the earth. The shadow of the earth - The greatest adversity into as great prosperity. Dark with might - Changes prosperity into adversity. That calleth - Commands the vapour to ascend, which he turns into rain; and then pours from the clouds to make the earth fruitful. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/amo005.htm#009">Amos 5:9</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_009">amo 5:9</a></font></span></p>The strong - The mighty, victorious and insolent. Shall come - Shall rally and form a siege against their besiegers. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/amo005.htm#010">Amos 5:10</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_010">amo 5:10</a></font></span></p>They - The judges and people. In the gate - Where judges sat, and where the prophets many times delivered their message. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/amo005.htm#011">Amos 5:11</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_011">amo 5:11</a></font></span></p>Your treading - You utterly oppress the helpless. Ye take - Ye extort from the poor great quantities of wheat, on which he should live. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/amo005.htm#012">Amos 5:12</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_012">amo 5:12</a></font></span></p>In the gate - In their courts of justice. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/amo005.htm#013">Amos 5:13</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_013">amo 5:13</a></font></span></p>Shall keep silence - Be forced to it. Evil - Both for the sinfulness of it, and for the troubles, wars, and captivity now at hand. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/amo005.htm#014">Amos 5:14</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_014">amo 5:14</a></font></span></p>With you - To bless and save you yet. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/amo005.htm#015">Amos 5:15</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_015">amo 5:15</a></font></span></p>The evil - All evil among the people, and yourselves. Love - Commend, encourage, defend: let your heart be toward good things, and good men. Remnant - What the invasions of enemies, or the civil wars have spared, and left in Samaria and Israel. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/amo005.htm#016">Amos 5:16</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_016">amo 5:16</a></font></span></p>Therefore - The prophet foreseeing their obstinacy, proceeds to denounce judgment against them. The husbandman - This sort of men are little used to such ceremonies of mourning, but now such also shall be called upon; leave your toil, betake yourselves to publick mourning. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/amo005.htm#017">Amos 5:17</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_017">amo 5:17</a></font></span></p>Vineyards - In these places were usually the greatest joy. Pass through - To punish all every where. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/amo005.htm#018">Amos 5:18</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_018">amo 5:18</a></font></span></p>That desire - Scoffingly, not believing any such day would come. To what end - What do you think to get by it? Is darkness - All adversity, black and doleful. Not light - No joy, or comfort an it. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/amo005.htm#019">Amos 5:19</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_019">amo 5:19</a></font></span></p>And a bear - You may escape one, but shall fall in another calamity. Into the house - At home you may hope for safety, but there other kind of mischief shall meet you. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/amo005.htm#021">Amos 5:21</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_021">amo 5:21</a></font></span></p>I hate - Impure and unholy as they are. Will not smell - A savour, of rest or delight, I will not accept and be pleased with. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/amo005.htm#023">Amos 5:23</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_023">amo 5:23</a></font></span></p>Thy songs - Used in their sacrifices, and solemn feasts; herein they imitated the temple - worship, but all was unpleasing to the Lord. Will not hear - Not with delight and acceptance. Thy viols - This one kind of musical instrument is put for all the rest. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/amo005.htm#024">Amos 5:24</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_024">amo 5:24</a></font></span></p>Let judgment - Let justice be administered constantly. Righteousness - Equity. Stream - Bearing down all that opposes it. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/amo005.htm#025">Amos 5:25</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_025">amo 5:25</a></font></span></p>Have ye - Their fathers and they, tho' at so great a distance of time, are one people, and so the prophet considers them. Unto me - Was it to me, or to your idols, that you offered, even in the wilderness? 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/amo005.htm#026">Amos 5:26</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_026">amo 5:26</a></font></span></p>Ye have borne - Ye carried along with you in the wilderness; the shrine, or canopy in which the image was placed. Moloch - The great idol of the Ammonites. Chiun - Another idol. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/amo005.htm#027">Amos 5:27</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_027">amo 5:27</a></font></span></p>Therefore - For all your idolatry and other sins, in which you have obstinately continued.
