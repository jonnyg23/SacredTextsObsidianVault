 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible</i>, by John Wesley, [1754-65], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro007.htm#001">Proverbs Chapter 7</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro007.htm#002">Proverbs 7:2</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_002">pro 7:2</a></font></span></p>Live - Thou shalt live. Eye - With all possible care and diligence. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro007.htm#003">Proverbs 7:3</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_003">pro 7:3</a></font></span></p>Bind them - As a ring which is continually in a man's eye. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro007.htm#004">Proverbs 7:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">pro 7:4</a></font></span></p>And call - Acquaint and delight thyself with her. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro007.htm#008">Proverbs 7:8</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_008">pro 7:8</a></font></span></p>Passing - Idle and careless, near the corner of the street in which her house stood. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro007.htm#012">Proverbs 7:12</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_012">pro 7:12</a></font></span></p>Without - Without the door of her house. Corner - Of the streets, where she might either conceal or discover herself, as she saw fit. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro007.htm#014">Proverbs 7:14</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_014">pro 7:14</a></font></span></p>Offerings - I have paid my peace - offerings which I had vowed. Whereby she signifies, that she had plentiful provisions at her house for his entertainment. For the peace - offerings were to be of the best flesh, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev022.htm#021">Lev 22:21</a>, and a considerable part of these offerings fell to the offerer's share. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro007.htm#020">Proverbs 7:20</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_020">pro 7:20</a></font></span></p>Hath taken - Which is an evidence that he designs to stay a considerable time. At the day - So that we need not fear any surprise. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro007.htm#022">Proverbs 7:22</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_022">pro 7:22</a></font></span></p>As an ox - Going to it securely, as if it were going to a good pasture. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro007.htm#023">Proverbs 7:23</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_023">pro 7:23</a></font></span></p>His liver - His vital parts, 'till his life be lost.
