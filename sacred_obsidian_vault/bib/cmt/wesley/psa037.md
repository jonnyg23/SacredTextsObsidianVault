 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible</i>, by John Wesley, [1754-65], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa037.htm#001">Psalms Chapter 37</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa037.htm#001">Psalms 37:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">psa 37:1</a></font></span></p>Fret not - Because they prosper in their wicked enterprizes. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa037.htm#005">Psalms 37:5</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_005">psa 37:5</a></font></span></p>Commit - All thy cares and business, and necessities, commend to God by fervent prayer. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa037.htm#006">Psalms 37:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">psa 37:6</a></font></span></p>Judgment - It shall be as visible to men, as the light of the sun, at noon - day. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa037.htm#007">Psalms 37:7</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_007">psa 37:7</a></font></span></p>Rest - Do not repine at his dealings, but quietly submit to his will, and wait for his help. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa037.htm#008">Psalms 37:8</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_008">psa 37:8</a></font></span></p>Fret not - Either against the sinner for his success; or against God. Do evil - If grief arise in thee, take care that it do not transport thee to sin. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa037.htm#009">Psalms 37:9</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_009">psa 37:9</a></font></span></p>The earth - This for the most part was literally fulfilled in that state of the church. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa037.htm#010">Psalms 37:10</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_010">psa 37:10</a></font></span></p>Not be - He shall be dead and gone. Diligently - Industriously seeking him. His place - His place and estate, and glory. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa037.htm#011">Psalms 37:11</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_011">psa 37:11</a></font></span></p>But - Those who patiently bear God's afflicting hand, and meekly pass by injuries. Peace - Partly of outward peace and prosperity, which God in his due time will give them: but principally of inward peace, in the sense of God's favour and the assurance of endless happiness. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa037.htm#013">Psalms 37:13</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_013">psa 37:13</a></font></span></p>His day - The day appointed by God for his punishment or destruction. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa037.htm#018">Psalms 37:18</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_018">psa 37:18</a></font></span></p>Knoweth - Observes with care and affection. The days - All things which befal them, their dangers and fears, and suffering. For ever - To them and their seed for ever: and when they die their inheritance is not lost, but exchanged for one infinitely better. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa037.htm#020">Psalms 37:20</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_020">psa 37:20</a></font></span></p>Fat - Which in an instant melts before the fire. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa037.htm#023">Psalms 37:23</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_023">psa 37:23</a></font></span></p>Established - So that he shall not fall into mischief. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa037.htm#024">Psalms 37:24</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_024">psa 37:24</a></font></span></p>Fall - Into trouble. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa037.htm#025">Psalms 37:25</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_025">psa 37:25</a></font></span></p>Forsaken - These temporal promises, were more express to the Jews in the times of the Old Testament, than to Christians. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa037.htm#026">Psalms 37:26</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_026">psa 37:26</a></font></span></p>Blessed - Not only with spiritual, but with temporal blessings. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa037.htm#027">Psalms 37:27</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_027">psa 37:27</a></font></span></p>Dwell - Thou shalt dwell in the land, and afterwards in heaven. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa037.htm#030">Psalms 37:30</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_030">psa 37:30</a></font></span></p>The mouth - Having shewed, God's singular care over the righteous, he proceeds to give a character of them. Judgment - Of God's judgment, word or law. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa037.htm#031">Psalms 37:31</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_031">psa 37:31</a></font></span></p>Heart - His thoughts, meditations and affections are fixed upon it. Slide - Slide, or swerve, from the rule, from God's law. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa037.htm#035">Psalms 37:35</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_035">psa 37:35</a></font></span></p>Bay - tree - Which is continually green and flourishing even in winter. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa037.htm#036">Psalms 37:36</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_036">psa 37:36</a></font></span></p>Yet - He was gone in an instant. But - There was no monument or remainder of him left. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa037.htm#037">Psalms 37:37</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_037">psa 37:37</a></font></span></p>Peace - Though he may meet with troubles in his way, yet all shall end well. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa037.htm#038">Psalms 37:38</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_038">psa 37:38</a></font></span></p>Together - All without exception.
