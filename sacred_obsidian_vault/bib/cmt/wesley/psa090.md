 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible</i>, by John Wesley, [1754-65], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa090.htm#001">Psalms Chapter 90</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa090.htm#001">Psalms 90:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">psa 90:1</a></font></span></p>Dwelling place - Although we and our fathers, for some generations, have had no fixed habitation, yet thou hast been instead of a dwelling - place to us, by thy watchful and gracious providence. And this intimates that all the following miseries were not to be imputed to God but themselves. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa090.htm#002">Psalms 90:2</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_002">psa 90:2</a></font></span></p>Thou - Thou hadst thy power, and all thy perfections, from all eternity. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa090.htm#003">Psalms 90:3</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_003">psa 90:3</a></font></span></p>Turnedst - But as for man, his case is far otherwise, though he was made by thee happy. and immortal, yet for his sin thou didst make him mortal and miserable. Saidst - Didst pronounce that sad sentence, return, O men, to the dust out of which ye were taken, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/gen003.htm#019">Gen 3:19</a>. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa090.htm#004">Psalms 90:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">psa 90:4</a></font></span></p>Past - Indeed time seems long when it is to come, but when it is past, very short and contemptible. A watch - Which lasted but three or four hours. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa090.htm#005">Psalms 90:5</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_005">psa 90:5</a></font></span></p>Them - Mankind. Away - Universally, without exception or distinction. A sleep - Short and vain, as sleep is, and not minded 'till it be past. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa090.htm#007">Psalms 90:7</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_007">psa 90:7</a></font></span></p>Are consumed - Thou dost not suffer us to live so long as we might by the course of nature. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa090.htm#008">Psalms 90:8</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_008">psa 90:8</a></font></span></p>Hast set - Thou dost observe them, as a righteous judge, and art calling us to an account for them. Secret sins - Which though hid from the eyes of men, thou hast brought to light by thy judgments. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa090.htm#010">Psalms 90:10</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_010">psa 90:10</a></font></span></p>Our years - Of the generality of mankind, in that and all following ages, some few persons excepted. Flee - We do not now go to death, as we do from our very birth, but flee swiftly away like a bird, as this word signifies. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa090.htm#011">Psalms 90:11</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_011">psa 90:11</a></font></span></p>Thy fear - According to the fear of thee; according to that fear which sinful men have of a just God. So - It bears full proportion to it, nay indeed doth far exceed it. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa090.htm#012">Psalms 90:12</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_012">psa 90:12</a></font></span></p>Teach us - To consider the shortness of life, and the certainty and speediness of death. That - That we may heartily devote ourselves to true wisdom. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa090.htm#013">Psalms 90:13</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_013">psa 90:13</a></font></span></p>Return - To us in mercy. How long - Will it be before thou return to us? Repent thee - Of thy severe proceedings against us. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa090.htm#014">Psalms 90:14</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_014">psa 90:14</a></font></span></p>Early - Speedily. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa090.htm#017">Psalms 90:17</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_017">psa 90:17</a></font></span></p>The beauty - His gracious influence, and glorious presence. In us - Do not only work for us, but in us,
