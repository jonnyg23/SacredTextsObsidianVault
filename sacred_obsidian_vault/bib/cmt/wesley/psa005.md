 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible</i>, by John Wesley, [1754-65], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa005.htm#001">Psalms Chapter 5</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa005.htm#001">Psalms 5:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">psa 5:1</a></font></span></p>Meditation - My prayer accompanied with deep thoughts and fervent affections of soul. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa005.htm#003">Psalms 5:3</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_003">psa 5:3</a></font></span></p>Morning - Every morning. As soon as I wake, I am still with thee, as he saith, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa139.htm#018">Psa 139:18</a>. The first thing that I do is to pray to thee. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa005.htm#004">Psalms 5:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">psa 5:4</a></font></span></p>Surely - Thou dost not approve of, nor delight in them, or in their prayers. Dwelt - Have any fellowship with thee. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa005.htm#007">Psalms 5:7</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_007">psa 5:7</a></font></span></p>Come - With holy boldness and confidence. Mercy - Trusting only in thy great mercy. Fear - With an holy dread and reverence of thy majesty. Towards - Looking towards it, when I cannot come to it. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa005.htm#008">Psalms 5:8</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_008">psa 5:8</a></font></span></p>Righteousness - In thy righteous laws. Because - That I may give them no occasion of slandering me, or religion for my sake. The way - The way wherein thou wouldst have to one walk. Plain - That I may clearly discern it, and readily walk in it. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa005.htm#009">Psalms 5:9</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_009">psa 5:9</a></font></span></p>Throat - Wide opened ready to devour all that come within their reach. A metaphor from wild beasts gaping for the prey. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa005.htm#010">Psalms 5:10</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_010">psa 5:10</a></font></span></p>Destroy - Condemn and punish them. Cast - Out of thy land, and from among thy people.
