 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible</i>, by John Wesley, [1754-65], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro028.htm#001">Proverbs Chapter 28</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro028.htm#002">Proverbs 28:2</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_002">pro 28:2</a></font></span></p>But - By a wise and good prince. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro028.htm#003">Proverbs 28:3</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_003">pro 28:3</a></font></span></p>It like - Is like a violent rain or flood, which washes away the very seeds in the earth. He is the worst of all oppressors. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro028.htm#004">Proverbs 28:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">pro 28:4</a></font></span></p>Contend - Are so far from praising them, that they severely reprove them. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro028.htm#005">Proverbs 28:5</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_005">pro 28:5</a></font></span></p>Judgment - What is just and right. All things - Which are necessary to be known by them. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro028.htm#006">Proverbs 28:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">pro 28:6</a></font></span></p>Better - In a much happier condition. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro028.htm#010">Proverbs 28:10</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_010">pro 28:10</a></font></span></p>Causeth - That by evil counsel, or example, or artifice, draws them into evil. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro028.htm#012">Proverbs 28:12</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_012">pro 28:12</a></font></span></p>Rejoice - Are promoted. Glory - In that common - wealth. Rise - Are advanced to authority. Hidden - Wise and good men, who only are worthy of the name of men, withdraw themselves into obscure places. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro028.htm#014">Proverbs 28:14</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_014">pro 28:14</a></font></span></p>Alway - In all times, companies, and conditions. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro028.htm#016">Proverbs 28:16</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_016">pro 28:16</a></font></span></p>Oppressor - The tyranny of a prince, is a manifest sign of folly. Prolong - By God's favour, the peace and satisfaction of his own mind, and the hearty love of his people. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro028.htm#017">Proverbs 28:17</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_017">pro 28:17</a></font></span></p>Violence - That sheddeth any man's blood. Shall flee - Shall speedily be destroyed. Shall slay - None should desire or endeavour to save him from his deserved punishment. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro028.htm#018">Proverbs 28:18</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_018">pro 28:18</a></font></span></p>At once - Once for all; so he shall never rise more. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro028.htm#020">Proverbs 28:20</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_020">pro 28:20</a></font></span></p>Faithful - Who deals truly and justly in all his transactions. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro028.htm#021">Proverbs 28:21</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_021">pro 28:21</a></font></span></p>For - When a man hath once accustomed himself to take bribes, a very small advantage will make him sell justice. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro028.htm#022">Proverbs 28:22</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_022">pro 28:22</a></font></span></p>Evil eye - Is uncharitable to persons in want, and envious to those who get any thing besides him. Poverty - And consequently that he shall need the pity and help of others. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro028.htm#024">Proverbs 28:24</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_024">pro 28:24</a></font></span></p>The same - Is a thief and robber. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro028.htm#025">Proverbs 28:25</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_025">pro 28:25</a></font></span></p>Fat - Shall live happily and comfortably. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro028.htm#026">Proverbs 28:26</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_026">pro 28:26</a></font></span></p>Wisely - Distrusting his own judgment, and seeking the advice of others, and especially of God. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro028.htm#028">Proverbs 28:28</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_028">pro 28:28</a></font></span></p>Men - Righteous men are afraid to appear publickly.
