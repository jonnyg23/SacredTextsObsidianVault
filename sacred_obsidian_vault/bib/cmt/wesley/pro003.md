 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible</i>, by John Wesley, [1754-65], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro003.htm#001">Proverbs Chapter 3</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro003.htm#001">Proverbs 3:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">pro 3:1</a></font></span></p>My law - The law of God, which might be called his law, as the gospel is called Paul's gospel, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/ti2002.htm#008">Ti2 2:8</a>, because delivered by him. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro003.htm#003">Proverbs 3:3</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_003">pro 3:3</a></font></span></p>Mercy - Mercy denotes all benignity, charity, and readiness to do good to others: truth or faithfulness respects all those duties which we owe to God or man, which we have special obligation from the rules of justice. Bind them - Like a chain, wherewith persons adorn their necks. Table - In thy mind and heart, in which all God's commands are to be received and engraven. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro003.htm#004">Proverbs 3:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">pro 3:4</a></font></span></p>Understanding - Whereby to know thy duty, and to discern between good and evil. Of God - Grace or favour with God, and that understanding which is good in God's sight. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro003.htm#005">Proverbs 3:5</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_005">pro 3:5</a></font></span></p>Trust - Wholly rely upon God's promises and providences. Lean not - Under this one kind of carnal confidence, he understands all other confidence in bodily strength, wealth, or friends. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro003.htm#008">Proverbs 3:8</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_008">pro 3:8</a></font></span></p>Navel - To thy body, which is signified by one part of it. Marrow - Which is the nourishment and strength of the bones. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro003.htm#009">Proverbs 3:9</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_009">pro 3:9</a></font></span></p>Substance - Lay out thy estate not to please thyself, but to glorify God. First - fruits - Or, with the chief or best; which answers to the first - fruits under the law. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro003.htm#010">Proverbs 3:10</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_010">pro 3:10</a></font></span></p>So - This is not the way to diminish thy estate, but rather to increase it. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro003.htm#011">Proverbs 3:11</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_011">pro 3:11</a></font></span></p>Despise not - Either by making light of it, or not being duly affected with it; or by accounting it an unnecessary thing: but rather esteem it a privilege and favour from God. Weary - Neither think it tedious or hard, but endure it with patience and chearfulness. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro003.htm#013">Proverbs 3:13</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_013">pro 3:13</a></font></span></p>Findeth - Which supposes his diligent searching for it. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro003.htm#017">Proverbs 3:17</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_017">pro 3:17</a></font></span></p>Peace - Procure a blessed tranquility in a man's mind and conscience. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro003.htm#018">Proverbs 3:18</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_018">pro 3:18</a></font></span></p>A tree - A pledge of everlasting life. He alludes to the tree of life, and intimates, that this is the only restorer of that life which we have lost by sin. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro003.htm#019">Proverbs 3:19</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_019">pro 3:19</a></font></span></p>Wisdom - Either by Christ, or by that Divine perfection of wisdom, which is the fountain of wisdom in man. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro003.htm#020">Proverbs 3:20</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_020">pro 3:20</a></font></span></p>The depths - That great abyss contained in the bowels of the earth, breaks forth into fountains and rivers. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro003.htm#021">Proverbs 3:21</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_021">pro 3:21</a></font></span></p>Eyes - The eyes of thy mind. Constantly and seriously meditate upon them. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro003.htm#022">Proverbs 3:22</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_022">pro 3:22</a></font></span></p>Grace - Like a beautiful chain or ornament. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro003.htm#025">Proverbs 3:25</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_025">pro 3:25</a></font></span></p>Be not - Thou shalt not be afraid. Sudden - For sudden and unexpected evils are most frightful. And fear is here put for the evils feared. Desolation - Which cometh upon the wicked. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro003.htm#026">Proverbs 3:26</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_026">pro 3:26</a></font></span></p>Shall be - A sure ground of confidence to thee. Taken - In the snares either of sin or mischief. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro003.htm#027">Proverbs 3:27</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_027">pro 3:27</a></font></span></p>With - hold not - Do not deny it, but readily and chearfuly impart it. Good - Any thing which is good, either counsel, comfort, reproof, or the good things of the present life. Due - That is, to all men, by that great and sovereign law of love. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro003.htm#028">Proverbs 3:28</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_028">pro 3:28</a></font></span></p>Say not - The former verse forbad the denial, and this forbids the delay of this duty. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro003.htm#029">Proverbs 3:29</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_029">pro 3:29</a></font></span></p>Securely - Relying upon thine integrity. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro003.htm#031">Proverbs 3:31</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_031">pro 3:31</a></font></span></p>Envy not - For his impunity and success. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro003.htm#032">Proverbs 3:32</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_032">pro 3:32</a></font></span></p>Abomination - Therefore sooner or later he must be miserable. The righteous - They are God's friends, to whom he imparts the favours and comforts to which other men are strangers. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro003.htm#033">Proverbs 3:33</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_033">pro 3:33</a></font></span></p>The house - Not only upon his own person, but also upon his posterity. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro003.htm#035">Proverbs 3:35</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_035">pro 3:35</a></font></span></p>Shame - Instead of that glory which they seek.
