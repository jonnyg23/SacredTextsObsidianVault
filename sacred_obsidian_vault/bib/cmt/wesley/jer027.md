 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible</i>, by John Wesley, [1754-65], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer027.htm#001">Jeremiah Chapter 27</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer027.htm#002">Jeremiah 27:2</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_002">jer 27:2</a></font></span></p>Thus saith - God commands the prophet to procure some yokes with bonds to make them more fast; and to put one of them upon his own neck, that therein he might be a type both to his own people, and also the people afterward mentioned, that they should be in bondage to the king of Babylon. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer027.htm#003">Jeremiah 27:3</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_003">jer 27:3</a></font></span></p>Of Edom - These nations were neighbours to the Jews, and their princes had their ambassadors resident in Jerusalem. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer027.htm#007">Jeremiah 27:7</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_007">jer 27:7</a></font></span></p>All nations - That is, all these nations. His son - And Evil - Merodach his son, and Belshazzar his grand - child. Until - Until the period of his kingdom shall come, which was after seventy years, according to <a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer029.htm#010">Jer 29:10</a>.
