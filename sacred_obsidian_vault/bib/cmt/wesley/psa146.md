 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa146.htm#004">Psalms 146:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">psa 146:4</a></font></span></p>That day - As soon as ever he is dead. Thoughts - All his designs and endeavours either for himself or for others. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa146.htm#006">Psalms 146:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">psa 146:6</a></font></span></p>For ever - Both because he liveth for ever to fulfil his promises, and because he is eternally faithful.
