 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible</i>, by John Wesley, [1754-65], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa080.htm#001">Psalms Chapter 80</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa080.htm#001">Psalms 80:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">psa 80:1</a></font></span></p>Joseph - The children of Joseph or Israel. The name of Joseph, the most eminent of the patriarchs, is elsewhere put for all the tribes. Cherubim - Which were by the mercy seat above the ark. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa080.htm#002">Psalms 80:2</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_002">psa 80:2</a></font></span></p>Before Ephraim - Here is an allusion to the ancient situation of the tabernacle in the wilderness, where these tribes were placed on the west - side of the tabernacle, in which the ark was, which consequently was before them. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa080.htm#003">Psalms 80:3</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_003">psa 80:3</a></font></span></p>Turn us - To thy self. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa080.htm#009">Psalms 80:9</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_009">psa 80:9</a></font></span></p>Preparedst - Thou didst root out the idolatrous nations. Deep root - Thou gavest them a firm settlement. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa080.htm#010">Psalms 80:10</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_010">psa 80:10</a></font></span></p>The hills - They filled not only the fruitful valleys, but even the barren mountains. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa080.htm#011">Psalms 80:11</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_011">psa 80:11</a></font></span></p>The river - They possessed the whole land, from the mid - land sea to the river Euphrates. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa080.htm#012">Psalms 80:12</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_012">psa 80:12</a></font></span></p>Hedges - Taken away thy protection. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa080.htm#016">Psalms 80:16</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_016">psa 80:16</a></font></span></p>They - Thy people, signified by the vine. So now he passes from the metaphor to the thing designed by it. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa080.htm#017">Psalms 80:17</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_017">psa 80:17</a></font></span></p>Be - To protect and strengthen him. Right - hand - Benjamin signifies the son of the right hand, a dearly beloved son, as Benjamin was to Jacob. Son of man - The people of Israel, who are often spoken of as one person, as God's son and first - born. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa080.htm#018">Psalms 80:18</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_018">psa 80:18</a></font></span></p>Go back - Revolt from thee to idolatry or wickedness. Quicken - Revive and restore us to our tranquility.
