 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible</i>, by John Wesley, [1754-65], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/zac002.htm#001">Zechariah Chapter 2</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/zac002.htm#001">Zechariah 2:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">zac 2:1</a></font></span></p>With a line - Ready and prepared to lay out the platform of Jerusalem. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/zac002.htm#002">Zechariah 2:2</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_002">zac 2:2</a></font></span></p>To measure - To take the exact dimensions of it, that it may answer God's promise, and be capable to receive its inhabitants. Jerusalem - The city which was to be built hereafter. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/zac002.htm#003">Zechariah 2:3</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_003">zac 2:3</a></font></span></p>The angel - Christ, who had so long talked with Zechariah. Went forth - From the midst of the myrtle - trees. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/zac002.htm#004">Zechariah 2:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">zac 2:4</a></font></span></p>And he said - Christ to that angel who came to meet him. Run - Hasten and tell Zechariah. As towns - The suburbs of it shall be as towns unwalled, for extent and for safety. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/zac002.htm#005">Zechariah 2:5</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_005">zac 2:5</a></font></span></p>The glory - My presence and favour shall make her glorious. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/zac002.htm#006">Zechariah 2:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">zac 2:6</a></font></span></p>Ho, ho - Ye sleepy Jews. Come forth - Come out from your prisons. Flee - Make all the haste you can. From the land - Babylon, which lay north of Canaan. For I have spread you abroad - As I executed my threats in scattering you, so I will perform my promise, and gather you. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/zac002.htm#007">Zechariah 2:7</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_007">zac 2:7</a></font></span></p>Deliver thyself - Accept of thy deliverance. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/zac002.htm#008">Zechariah 2:8</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_008">zac 2:8</a></font></span></p>After the glory - After that he is become your glory, I am to avenge you of your enemies. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/zac002.htm#009">Zechariah 2:9</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_009">zac 2:9</a></font></span></p>Upon them - Against the nation that doth violence to my people. Unto their servants - Unto the Jews, who were first spoiled by, and then made servants to them. Hath sent me - To inform them of my father's will. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/zac002.htm#010">Zechariah 2:10</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_010">zac 2:10</a></font></span></p>I come - To execute judgments on thine adversaries, and to compleat thy deliverance and salvation. I will dwell - This was fulfilled in part to the Jews, but more fully to the gospel church. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/zac002.htm#011">Zechariah 2:11</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_011">zac 2:11</a></font></span></p>Shall be joined - Shall worship the God of Israel. In that day - When Christ shall come in the flesh, and take down the partition wall. Sent me - The Messiah. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/zac002.htm#012">Zechariah 2:12</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_012">zac 2:12</a></font></span></p>Shall inherit - Claim, recover, possess, and delight in, as a man doth in his paternal inheritance. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/zac002.htm#013">Zechariah 2:13</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_013">zac 2:13</a></font></span></p>Be silent - Reverence and adore God, and expect the accomplishment of his word. All flesh - Both Jew and Gentile. He is raised up - God is on this work already, and he will not sit down again, 'till he has accomplished his whole work.
