 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible</i>, by John Wesley, [1754-65], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa135.htm#001">Psalms Chapter 135</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa135.htm#002">Psalms 135:2</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_002">psa 135:2</a></font></span></p>Ye - Ye priest and Levites. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa135.htm#006">Psalms 135:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">psa 135:6</a></font></span></p>Seas - In the visible seas, and in the invisible depths both of the earth and of the waters. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa135.htm#007">Psalms 135:7</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_007">psa 135:7</a></font></span></p>From - From all parts of the earth, from one end to another. Rain - An eminent instance of his good providence. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa135.htm#014">Psalms 135:14</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_014">psa 135:14</a></font></span></p>Judge - Will in due time plead the cause of his people. Repent - He will recall that severe sentence which for their sins he had passed upon them.
