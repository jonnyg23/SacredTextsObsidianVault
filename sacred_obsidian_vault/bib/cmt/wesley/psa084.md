 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible</i>, by John Wesley, [1754-65], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa084.htm#001">Psalms Chapter 84</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa084.htm#004">Psalms 84:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">psa 84:4</a></font></span></p>They - That constantly abide in thy house; the priests and Levites, or other devout Jews who were there perpetually, as Anna, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk002.htm#036">Luk 2:36-37</a>. They will - They are continually employed in that blessed work. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa084.htm#005">Psalms 84:5</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_005">psa 84:5</a></font></span></p>Whose strength - Who trusteth in thee as his only strength. Thy ways - Blessed are they whose hearts are set upon Zion and their journey is thither. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa084.htm#006">Psalms 84:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">psa 84:6</a></font></span></p>Baca - A dry valley in the way to Jerusalem, here put for all places of like nature. Make a well - They dig divers little pits or wells in it for their relief. The rain - God recompenses their diligence with his blessing, sending rain wherewith they may be filled. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa084.htm#007">Psalms 84:7</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_007">psa 84:7</a></font></span></p>They go - They grow stronger and stronger. Appeareth - This is added as the blessed fruit of their long and tedious journey. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa084.htm#009">Psalms 84:9</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_009">psa 84:9</a></font></span></p>Look - Cast a favourable eye towards him. Anointed - Of me, who though a vile sinner, am thine anointed king. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa084.htm#010">Psalms 84:10</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_010">psa 84:10</a></font></span></p>Than dwell - In the greatest glory and plenty. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa084.htm#011">Psalms 84:11</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_011">psa 84:11</a></font></span></p>A sun - To enlighten and quicken, and direct and comfort his people. Shield - To save his people from all their enemies. Grace - His favour, which is better than life. Glory - The honour which comes from God here, and eternal glory.
