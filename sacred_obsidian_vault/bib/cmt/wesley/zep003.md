 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible</i>, by John Wesley, [1754-65], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/zep003.htm#001">Zephaniah Chapter 3</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/zep003.htm#001">Zephaniah 3:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">zep 3:1</a></font></span></p>To her - Jerusalem. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/zep003.htm#002">Zephaniah 3:2</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_002">zep 3:2</a></font></span></p>The voice - Of God by his mercy and judgments crying aloud. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/zep003.htm#003">Zephaniah 3:3</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_003">zep 3:3</a></font></span></p>Her princes - Persons of principal place and authority. Lions - Which hunt for prey, and are ever affrighting or devouring. Wolves - Insatiable and cruel, like wolves of the evening, whetted with hunger. Gnaw not the bones - They leave nothing but the bones to be eaten on the morrow. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/zep003.htm#004">Zephaniah 3:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">zep 3:4</a></font></span></p>Her prophet - So called, false prophets. Light - Unstable and inconstant. Violence to the law - Wresting it by perverse interpretation. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/zep003.htm#005">Zephaniah 3:5</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_005">zep 3:5</a></font></span></p>In the midst - Observing all. Not do iniquity - He will judge them righteously. Every morning - Daily he discovers his displeasure against the wicked. Faileth not - Lets no season slip to convince them, by public and visible punishments. The unjust - But the wicked Jews proceed without shame, and without fear. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/zep003.htm#006">Zephaniah 3:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">zep 3:6</a></font></span></p>The nations - Of old, the Canaanites, lastly the ten tribes, and later yet, the Assyrians. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/zep003.htm#007">Zephaniah 3:7</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_007">zep 3:7</a></font></span></p>I said - I thought (speaking after the manner of men). Thou - O Jerusalem. Fear me - For the many and great judgments executed upon others. I punished them - In some measure. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/zep003.htm#008">Zephaniah 3:8</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_008">zep 3:8</a></font></span></p>Therefore - Since you will not be amended. Wait ye - Attend my resolution. Until - Until I rise up to destroy first, and next to take the spoil. Upon them - The incorrigible Jews. Devoured - Consumed as if burnt up. My jealousy - That jealousy wherewith God is jealous for his own glory. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/zep003.htm#009">Zephaniah 3:9</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_009">zep 3:9</a></font></span></p>A pure language - I will give them a pure way of worshipping me, the issue of a pure heart. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/zep003.htm#010">Zephaniah 3:10</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_010">zep 3:10</a></font></span></p>My dispersed - The praying remnant of the scattered Jews shall return to their own land, and bring themselves an offering unto the Lord. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/zep003.htm#011">Zephaniah 3:11</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_011">zep 3:11</a></font></span></p>Thy doings - Thy sins formerly committed. In thy pride - Proud formalists. No more be haughty - Ye shall no more boast, because of the city, or the temple. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/zep003.htm#012">Zephaniah 3:12</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_012">zep 3:12</a></font></span></p>Of thee - In Judea and Jerusalem. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/zep003.htm#013">Zephaniah 3:13</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_013">zep 3:13</a></font></span></p>Shall feed - Shall enjoy peace and plenty. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/zep003.htm#015">Zephaniah 3:15</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_015">zep 3:15</a></font></span></p>Taken away - Abolished, and put an end to the judgments thy sins brought upon thee. Thine enemy - The Babylonian. Is in the midst - He is returned to redeem and govern thee. Any more - While thy carriage is as becomes my presence with thee, thou shalt neither fear, nor feel the like evils. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/zep003.htm#018">Zephaniah 3:18</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_018">zep 3:18</a></font></span></p>Sorrowful - That mourn their distance from the solemn worship of God. Who are of thee - Thy children. Reproach - The taunts of their enemies. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/zep003.htm#019">Zephaniah 3:19</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_019">zep 3:19</a></font></span></p>Undo - I will break their power and dissolve their kingdom. That halteth - Who is in trouble and ready to fall. Driven out - Into remote countries. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/zep003.htm#020">Zephaniah 3:20</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_020">zep 3:20</a></font></span></p>A praise - So the universal church of the first - born will be, in the great day. And then the Israel of God be made a name and a praise to all eternity.
