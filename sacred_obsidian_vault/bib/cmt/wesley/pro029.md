 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible</i>, by John Wesley, [1754-65], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#001">Proverbs Chapter 29</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#004">Proverbs 29:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">pro 29:4</a></font></span></p>Judgment - By the free and impartial exercise of justice. Gifts - Bribes. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#008">Proverbs 29:8</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_008">pro 29:8</a></font></span></p>Wrath - The wrath of God or of men, who were enraged against it. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#009">Proverbs 29:9</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_009">pro 29:9</a></font></span></p>Whether - Whether he, the wise man, deal sharply with him, or mildly, there is no rest, no end or fruit of the debate. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#010">Proverbs 29:10</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_010">pro 29:10</a></font></span></p>Seek - To preserve it. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#015">Proverbs 29:15</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_015">pro 29:15</a></font></span></p>Left - Suffered to follow his own will without restraint and chastening. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#018">Proverbs 29:18</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_018">pro 29:18</a></font></span></p>No vision - No prophecy; no publick preaching of God's word. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#019">Proverbs 29:19</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_019">pro 29:19</a></font></span></p>A servant - Who is so not only in condition, but also in the temper of his mind, disingenuous, and stubborn. Words - By mere words. Tho' - Though he know thy mind, and his own duty. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#020">Proverbs 29:20</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_020">pro 29:20</a></font></span></p>Hasty - Who is rash and heady in the management of his affairs. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#021">Proverbs 29:21</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_021">pro 29:21</a></font></span></p>Delicately - Allowing him too much freedom, and familiarity. A son - Will find him at last grow insolent, and forgetful of his servile condition. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#024">Proverbs 29:24</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_024">pro 29:24</a></font></span></p>Bewrayeth it not - Which he is bound to do for the publick good. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#025">Proverbs 29:25</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_025">pro 29:25</a></font></span></p>A snare - Is an occasion of many sins. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#026">Proverbs 29:26</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_026">pro 29:26</a></font></span></p>Judgment - The decision of his cause, and the success of all his endeavours.
