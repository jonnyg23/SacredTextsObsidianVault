 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible</i>, by John Wesley, [1754-65], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa083.htm#001">Psalms Chapter 83</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa083.htm#003">Psalms 83:3</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_003">psa 83:3</a></font></span></p>Hidden ones - Thy people of Israel, who are called God's hidden or secret ones, to intimate the respect which God has to them, as to his peculiar treasure. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa083.htm#006">Psalms 83:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">psa 83:6</a></font></span></p>The tabernacles - The people dwelling in them. Ishmaelites - Some of the posterity of Ishmael, called by their father's name, as others of them are supposed to be called Hagarens from their grandmother Hagar. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa083.htm#007">Psalms 83:7</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_007">psa 83:7</a></font></span></p>Gebal - An Arabian people so called by ancient writers dwelling in the southern border of Canaan, where most of the people here mentioned had their abode. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa083.htm#008">Psalms 83:8</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_008">psa 83:8</a></font></span></p>Of Lot - Moab and Ammon. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa083.htm#013">Psalms 83:13</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_013">psa 83:13</a></font></span></p>A wheel - Whereas they promise to themselves a sure possession, let them be like a wheel, which is very unstable, and soon removed. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa083.htm#014">Psalms 83:14</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_014">psa 83:14</a></font></span></p>The mountains - The woods upon the mountains, which in those hot countries, when they have once taken fire, burn with irresistible violence. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa083.htm#016">Psalms 83:16</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_016">psa 83:16</a></font></span></p>May seek - May own and worship thee as the only true God.
