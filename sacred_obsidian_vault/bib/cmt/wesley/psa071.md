 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa071.htm#007">Psalms 71:7</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_007">psa 71:7</a></font></span></p>A wonder - For my many and sore calamities. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa071.htm#015">Psalms 71:15</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_015">psa 71:15</a></font></span></p>The numbers - Of thy salvations and mercies vouchsafed to me. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa071.htm#016">Psalms 71:16</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_016">psa 71:16</a></font></span></p>Make mention - To support and comfort myself with the remembrance of it. Righteousness - Of thy faithfulness in making good all thy promises. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa071.htm#019">Psalms 71:19</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_019">psa 71:19</a></font></span></p>Very high - Most eminent. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa071.htm#020">Psalms 71:20</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_020">psa 71:20</a></font></span></p>Bring me - From the grave.
