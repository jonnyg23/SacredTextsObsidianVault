 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible</i>, by John Wesley, [1754-65], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/kg2015.htm#001">4 Kings (2 Kings) Chapter 15</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/kg2015.htm#001">4 Kings (2 Kings) 15:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">kg2 15:1</a></font></span></p>To reign - Solely and fully to exercise his regal power. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/kg2015.htm#005">4 Kings (2 Kings) 15:5</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_005">kg2 15:5</a></font></span></p>A leper - The cause whereof see <a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch2026.htm#016">Ch2 26:16</a>. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/kg2015.htm#008">4 Kings (2 Kings) 15:8</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_008">kg2 15:8</a></font></span></p>Six months - After the throne had been vacant several years, thro' the dissentions that were in the kingdom. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/kg2015.htm#013">4 Kings (2 Kings) 15:13</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_013">kg2 15:13</a></font></span></p>Full moon - That dominion seldom lasts long, which is founded in blood and falsehood. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/kg2015.htm#030">4 Kings (2 Kings) 15:30</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_030">kg2 15:30</a></font></span></p>Twentieth year - The meaning is, that he began his reign in the twentieth year after the beginning of Jotham's reign; or, which is the same thing, in the fourth year of Ahaz, son of Jotham. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/kg2015.htm#033">4 Kings (2 Kings) 15:33</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_033">kg2 15:33</a></font></span></p>To reign - Alone: for he had reigned before this, as his father's deputy. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/kg2015.htm#035">4 Kings (2 Kings) 15:35</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_035">kg2 15:35</a></font></span></p>Gate - Not of the temple, but of one of the courts of the temple, probably that which led to the king's palace.
