 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible</i>, by John Wesley, [1754-65], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa149.htm#001">Psalms Chapter 149</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa149.htm#004">Psalms 149:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">psa 149:4</a></font></span></p>The Lord - He rejoiceth over them to do them good. Beautify - Heb. adorn, make them amiable and honourable in the eyes of the world, who now hate and despise them. The meek - All true Israelites are such. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa149.htm#005">Psalms 149:5</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_005">psa 149:5</a></font></span></p>In glory - For the honour which God putteth upon them. Beds - By night as well as by day. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa149.htm#007">Psalms 149:7</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_007">psa 149:7</a></font></span></p>Vengeance - For all their cruelties and injuries towards God's people. This was literally accomplished by David upon the Philistines, Ammonites, Syrians and other neighbouring nations. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa149.htm#009">Psalms 149:9</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_009">psa 149:9</a></font></span></p>Written - Appointed and declared in the holy scripture.
