 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Explanatory Notes on the Whole Bible</i>, by John Wesley, [1754-65], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa026.htm#001">Psalms Chapter 26</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa026.htm#001">Psalms 26:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">psa 26:1</a></font></span></p>Trusted - I have committed my cause and affairs to thee. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa026.htm#003">Psalms 26:3</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_003">psa 26:3</a></font></span></p>For - I dare appeal to thee, because thou knowest I have a deep sense of thy loving - kindness, by which I have been led to love and obey thee. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa026.htm#004">Psalms 26:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">psa 26:4</a></font></span></p>Sat - Continued with them. I have been so far from an imitation of their wicked courses, that I have avoided their company. Vain - With false and deceitful persons. Go in - Into their company. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa026.htm#006">Psalms 26:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">psa 26:6</a></font></span></p>Compass - Approach to thine altar with my sacrifices: which I could not do with any comfort, if I were conscious of those crimes, whereof mine enemies accuse me. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa026.htm#008">Psalms 26:8</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_008">psa 26:8</a></font></span></p>House - Thy sanctuary and worship. Honour - Thy glorious and gracious presence. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa026.htm#009">Psalms 26:9</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_009">psa 26:9</a></font></span></p>Gather not - Do not bind me up in the same bundle, or put me into the same accursed condition with them. 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa026.htm#012">Psalms 26:12</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_012">psa 26:12</a></font></span></p>Standeth - I stand upon a sure and solid foundation, being under the protection of God's promise, and his almighty and watchful providence. Congregations - I will not only privately, but in the assemblies of thy people celebrate thy praise.
