 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/kg1001.htm#001">3 Kings (1 Kings)</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_000">kg1 0:0</a></font></span></p>
 <p>The history now before us accounts for the affairs of the kingdoms of Judah and Israel, yet with special regard to the kingdom of God among them; for it is a sacred history. It is earlier as to time, teaches much more, and is more interesting than any common histories.
