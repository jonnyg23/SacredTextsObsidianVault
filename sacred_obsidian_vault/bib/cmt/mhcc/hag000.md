 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Complete Commentary on the Whole Bible</i>, by Matthew Henry, at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/hag001.htm#001">Haggai Introduction</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/hag001.htm#001">Haggai</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_000">hag 0:0</a></font></span></p>
 <p>After the return from captivity, Haggai was sent to encourage the people to rebuild the temple, and to reprove their neglect. To encourage their undertaking, the people are assured that the glory of the second temple shall far exceed that of the first, by the appearing therein of Christ, the Desire of all nations.
