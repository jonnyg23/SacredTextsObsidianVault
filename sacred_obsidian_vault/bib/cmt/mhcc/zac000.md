 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Complete Commentary on the Whole Bible</i>, by Matthew Henry, at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/zac001.htm#001">Zechariah Introduction</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/zac001.htm#001">Zechariah</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_000">zac 0:0</a></font></span></p>
 <p>This prophecy is suitable to all, as the scope is to reprove for sin, and threaten God's judgments against the impenitent, and to encourage those that feared God, with assurances of the mercy God had in store for his church, and especially of the coming of the Messiah, and the setting up his kingdom in the world.
