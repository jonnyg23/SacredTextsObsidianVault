 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/mic001.htm#001">Micah</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_000">mic 0:0</a></font></span></p>
 <p>Micah was raised up to support Isaiah, and to confirm his predictions, while he invited to repentance, both by threatened judgments and promised mercies. A very remarkable passage, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/mic005.htm#001">Mic 5:1-15</a> contains a summary of prophecies concerning the Messiah.
