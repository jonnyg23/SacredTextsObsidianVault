 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jdg001.htm#001">Judges</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_000">jdg 19:0</a></font></span></p>
 <p>The wickedness of the men of Gibeah. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jdg019.htm#001">Judges 19:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">jdg 19:1</a></font></span></p>
 <p>The three remaining chapters of this book contain a very sad history of the wickedness of the men of Gibeah, in Benjamin. The righteous Lord permits sinners to execute just vengeance on one another, and if the scene here described is horrible, what will the discoveries of the day of judgment be! Let each of us consider how to escape from the wrath to come, how to mortify the sins of our own hearts, to resist Satan's temptations, and to avoid the pollutions there are in the world.
