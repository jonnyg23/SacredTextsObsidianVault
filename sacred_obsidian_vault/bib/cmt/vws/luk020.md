 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Vincent's Word Studies</i>, by Marvin R. Vincent, [1886], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk020.htm#001">Luke Chapter 20</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk020.htm#005">Luke 20:5</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_005">luk 20:5</a></font></span></p>
 <p>They reasoned (συνελογίσαντο)
 </p><p>Only here in New Testament. The preposition, σύν, together, and the additional with themselves, denote a very close conference. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk020.htm#006">Luke 20:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">luk 20:6</a></font></span></p>
 <p>Will stone (καταλιθάσει)
 </p><p>Only here in New Testament. "Stone us down" (κατά); i.e., stone us to death.
 </p><p>They be persuaded (πεπεισμένος ἐστιν)
 </p><p>Lit., It (the people collectively) is hawing been persuaded. Denoting a long-standing and settled persuasion.
 </p><p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk020.htm#009">Luke 20:9</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_009">luk 20:9</a></font></span></p>
 <p>Let it out
 </p><p>See on <a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat021.htm#033">Mat 21:33</a>.
 </p><p>Went into a far country
 </p><p>Not necessarily far, but as Rev., another country. See on <a href="../../../bib/kjv/mar013.htm#034">Mar 13:34</a>.
 </p><p>A long time (ἱκανούς)
 </p><p>See on ch. <a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk007.htm#006">Luk 7:6</a>. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk020.htm#010">Luke 20:10</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_010">luk 20:10</a></font></span></p>
 <p>Of the fruit
 </p><p>See on <a href="../../../bib/kjv/mar012.htm#002">Mar 12:2</a>. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk020.htm#011">Luke 20:11</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_011">luk 20:11</a></font></span></p>
 <p>He sent yet (προσέθετο πέμψαι)
 </p><p>Lit., he added to send. A Hebrew form of expression. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk020.htm#012">Luke 20:12</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_012">luk 20:12</a></font></span></p>
 <p>Wounded (τραυματίσαντες)
 </p><p>Only here and <a href="../../../bib/kjv/act019.htm#016">Act 19:16</a>. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk020.htm#013">Luke 20:13</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_013">luk 20:13</a></font></span></p>
 <p>It may be (ἴσως)
 </p><p>Only here in New Testament. The adverb of ἴσος, equal. It expresses more than perhaps, implying rather a strong probability. Compare the phrase, it is an even chance that.
 </p><p>Reverence
 </p><p>See  on <a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat021.htm#037">Mat 21:37</a>. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk020.htm#016">Luke 20:16</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_016">luk 20:16</a></font></span></p>
 <p>Destroy
 </p><p>See on <a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat021.htm#041">Mat 21:41</a>.
 </p><p>God forbid (μὴ γένοιτο)
 </p><p>Lit., may it not be. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk020.htm#017">Luke 20:17</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_017">luk 20:17</a></font></span></p>
 <p>The stone, etc
 </p><p>See on <a href="../../../bib/kjv/pe1002.htm#004">Pe1 2:4-7</a>. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk020.htm#018">Luke 20:18</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_018">luk 20:18</a></font></span></p>
 <p>Shall be broken (συνθλασθήσεται)
 </p><p>Rev., rightly, broken to pieces. See on <a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat021.htm#044">Mat 21:44</a>.
 </p><p>Grind him to powder (λικμήσει)
 </p><p>See on <a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat021.htm#044">Mat 21:44</a>
 </p><p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk020.htm#020">Luke 20:20</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_020">luk 20:20</a></font></span></p>
 <p>Watched
 </p><p>See on <a href="../../../bib/kjv/mar003.htm#002">Mar 3:2</a>.
 </p><p>Spies (ἐγκαθέτους)
 </p><p>Only here in New Testament. From ἐγκαθίμηι, to send in, as a garrison into a city. Hence of persons sent in for the purpose of espionage.
 </p><p>Which should feign (ὑποκρινομένους)
 </p><p>Lit., feigning. Rev., which feigned. Only here in New Testament. See on hypocrites, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat023.htm#013">Mat 23:13</a>.
 </p><p>The power and authority (τῇ ἀρχῇ καὶ τῇ ἐξουσίᾳ)
 </p><p>The former, the Roman power in general; the latter, the specific authority of the official. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk020.htm#021">Luke 20:21</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_021">luk 20:21</a></font></span></p>
 <p>Acceptest not the person
 </p><p>See on <a href="../../../bib/kjv/jam002.htm#001">Jam 2:1</a>. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk020.htm#022">Luke 20:22</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_022">luk 20:22</a></font></span></p>
 <p>Tribute (φόρον)
 </p><p>From φέρω, to bring. Something, therefore, which is brought in by way of payment. Luke uses the Greek word instead of the Latin κῆνσον, census, in Matthew and Mark. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk020.htm#023">Luke 20:23</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_023">luk 20:23</a></font></span></p>
 <p>Perceived
 </p><p>See on considerest, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat007.htm#003">Mat 7:3</a>.
 </p><p>Craftiness (πανουργίαν)
 </p><p>From πᾶν, every, and ἔργον, deed. Readiness for every and any deed. Hence unscrupulousness, and so, generally, knavery. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk020.htm#024">Luke 20:24</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_024">luk 20:24</a></font></span></p>
 <p>Penny
 </p><p>See on <a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat020.htm#002">Mat 20:2</a>.
 </p><p>Image and superscription
 </p><p>See on <a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat022.htm#020">Mat 22:20</a>. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk020.htm#026">Luke 20:26</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_026">luk 20:26</a></font></span></p>
 <p>His words (ῥήματος)
 </p><p>Singular number. Rev., properly, saying. See on <a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk001.htm#037">Luk 1:37</a>.
 </p><p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk020.htm#027">Luke 20:27</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_027">luk 20:27</a></font></span></p>
 <p>Asked
 </p><p>See on <a href="../../../bib/kjv/mar012.htm#018">Mar 12:18</a>. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk020.htm#036">Luke 20:36</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_036">luk 20:36</a></font></span></p>
 <p>Equal unto the angels (ἰσάγγελοι)
 </p><p>Only here in New Testament. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk020.htm#037">Luke 20:37</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_037">luk 20:37</a></font></span></p>
 <p>Shewed (ἐμήνυσεν)
 </p><p>Originally to disclose something secret. Hence, generally, to make known.
 </p><p>At the bush (ἐπὶ τῆς βάτου)
 </p><p>Wrong. Render as Rev., in the place concerning the bush. See on <a href="../../../bib/kjv/mar012.htm#026">Mar 12:26</a>.
 </p><p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk020.htm#043">Luke 20:43</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_043">luk 20:43</a></font></span></p>
 <p>Of thy feet (τῶν ποδῶν σου)
 </p><p>A. V. omits. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk020.htm#046">Luke 20:46</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_046">luk 20:46</a></font></span></p>
 <p>Chief rooms
 </p><p>Rev., correctly, chief places. See on <a href="../../../bib/kjv/mar012.htm#039">Mar 12:39</a>. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk020.htm#047">Luke 20:47</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_047">luk 20:47</a></font></span></p>
 <p>Widows' houses
 </p><p>See on <a href="../../../bib/kjv/mar012.htm#040">Mar 12:40</a>.
