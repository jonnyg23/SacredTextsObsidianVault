 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1001.htm#001">1 Chronicles</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_000">ch1 14:0</a></font></span></p>INTRODUCTION TO 1 CHRONICLES 14 
 <p>
 </p><p>This chapter gives an account of the message of Hiram king of Tyre to David, and of David's taking him more wives, with the names of his children by them; and of two battles he fought with the Philistines, in which he had the victory over them; the same are recorded in <a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa2005.htm#011">Sa2 5:11</a> see the notes there; See Gill on <a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa2005.htm#011">Sa2 5:11</a>. See Gill on <a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa2005.htm#012">Sa2 5:12</a>. See Gill on <a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa2005.htm#013">Sa2 5:13</a>. See Gill on <a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa2005.htm#014">Sa2 5:14</a>. See Gill on <a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa2005.htm#015">Sa2 5:15</a>. See Gill on <a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa2005.htm#016">Sa2 5:16</a>. See Gill on <a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa2005.htm#017">Sa2 5:17</a>. See Gill on <a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa2005.htm#018">Sa2 5:18</a>. See Gill on <a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa2005.htm#019">Sa2 5:19</a>. See Gill on <a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa2005.htm#020">Sa2 5:20</a>. See Gill on <a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa2005.htm#021">Sa2 5:21</a>. See Gill on <a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa2005.htm#022">Sa2 5:22</a>. See Gill on <a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa2005.htm#023">Sa2 5:23</a>. See Gill on <a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa2005.htm#024">Sa2 5:24</a>. See Gill on <a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa2005.htm#025">Sa2 5:25</a>, and the chapter is concluded with observing, that the fame of David, on account of his victories, was spread in all countries, and they were struck with a panic from the Lord, and were in dread of him.
