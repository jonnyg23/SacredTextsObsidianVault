 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Commentary on the Bible</i>, by Adam Clarke, [1831], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1025.htm#001">1 Chronicles Chapter 25</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1001.htm#001">1 Chronicles</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_000">ch1 25:0</a></font></span></p>
 <p>The number and offices of the singers and players on musical instruments; and their division by lot into twenty-four courses, vv. 1-31. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1025.htm#001">1 Chronicles 25:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">ch1 25:1</a></font></span></p>
 <p>David and the captains of the host - The chiefs of those who formed the several orders: not military captains.
 </p><p>Should prophesy - Should accompany their musical instruments with prayer and singing. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1025.htm#002">1 Chronicles 25:2</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_002">ch1 25:2</a></font></span></p>
 <p>Which prophesied - Sung hymns and prayed. But the Targum understands this of prophesying in the proper sense of the term; and therefore says, "Who prophesied by the Holy Spirit." Jarchi is of the same opinion and quotes the case of Elisha, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/kg2003.htm#015">Kg2 3:15</a>; While the minstrel played, the hand of the Lord [i.e., the spirit of prophecy] was upon him. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1025.htm#003">1 Chronicles 25:3</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_003">ch1 25:3</a></font></span></p>
 <p>The sons of Jeduthun - six - That is, six with their father, otherwise, there are but five. Hence it is said, they were under the hands of their father Jeduthun, who prophesied with a harp, etc. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1025.htm#005">1 Chronicles 25:5</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_005">ch1 25:5</a></font></span></p>
 <p>To lift up the horn - "The horn of prophecy," says Jarchi; "to sound with the trumpet in the words of prophecy before the Lord." - T.
 </p><p>Three daughters - These also were employed among the singers. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1025.htm#007">1 Chronicles 25:7</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_007">ch1 25:7</a></font></span></p>
 <p>Two hundred fourscore and eight - That it twelve classes of twenty-four Levites each; for two hundred and eighty-eight divided by twelve quotes twenty-four. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1025.htm#009">1 Chronicles 25:9</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_009">ch1 25:9</a></font></span></p>
 <p>For Asaph to Joseph - His first-born.
 </p><p>The second to Gedaliah - The first-born of Jeduthun. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1025.htm#010">1 Chronicles 25:10</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_010">ch1 25:10</a></font></span></p>
 <p>The third to Zaccur - The first-born of Asaph. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1025.htm#011">1 Chronicles 25:11</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_011">ch1 25:11</a></font></span></p>
 <p>The fourth to Izri - The second son of Jeduthun. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1025.htm#012">1 Chronicles 25:12</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_012">ch1 25:12</a></font></span></p>
 <p>The fifth to Nethaniah - The third son of Asaph. Thus we find the lot did not run in any particular kind of order. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1025.htm#014">1 Chronicles 25:14</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_014">ch1 25:14</a></font></span></p>
 <p>Jesharelah - Supposed to be the same with Uzziel, son of Heman. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1025.htm#031">1 Chronicles 25:31</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_031">ch1 25:31</a></font></span></p>
 <p>Romamti-ezer - Both these names belong to the same person. He is mentioned also <a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1025.htm#004">Ch1 25:4</a>.
 </p><p>With this immense parade of noise and show, (David's own invention), Christianity has nothing to do.
