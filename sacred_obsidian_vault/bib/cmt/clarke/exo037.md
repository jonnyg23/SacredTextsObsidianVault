 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Commentary on the Bible</i>, by Adam Clarke, [1831], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo037.htm#001">Exodus Chapter 37</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo001.htm#001">Exodus</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_000">exo 37:0</a></font></span></p>
 <p>Bezaleel and Aholiab make the ark, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo037.htm#001">Exo 37:1-5</a>. The mercy-seat, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo037.htm#006">Exo 37:6</a>. The two cherubim, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo037.htm#007">Exo 37:7-9</a>. The table of the shew-bread, and its vessels, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo037.htm#010">Exo 37:10-16</a>. The candlestick, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo037.htm#017">Exo 37:17-24</a>. The golden altar of incense, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo037.htm#025">Exo 37:25-28</a>. The holy anointing oil and perfume, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo037.htm#029">Exo 37:29</a>. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo037.htm#001">Exodus 37:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">exo 37:1</a></font></span></p>
 <p>And Bezaleel made the ark, etc. - For a description of the ark, see <a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo025.htm#010">Exo 25:10</a> (note), etc. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo037.htm#006">Exodus 37:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">exo 37:6</a></font></span></p>
 <p>He made the mercy-seat - See this described <a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo025.htm#017">Exo 25:17</a> (note). 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo037.htm#010">Exodus 37:10</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_010">exo 37:10</a></font></span></p>
 <p>He made the table - See <a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo025.htm#023">Exo 25:23</a>. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo037.htm#016">Exodus 37:16</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_016">exo 37:16</a></font></span></p>
 <p>He made the vessels - See all these particularly described in the notes on <a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo025.htm#029">Exo 25:29</a> (note). 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo037.htm#017">Exodus 37:17</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_017">exo 37:17</a></font></span></p>
 <p>He made the candlestick - See this described in the note on <a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo025.htm#031">Exo 25:31</a> (note). 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo037.htm#025">Exodus 37:25</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_025">exo 37:25</a></font></span></p>
 <p>He made the incense altar - See this described <a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo030.htm#001">Exo 30:1</a> (note). 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo037.htm#029">Exodus 37:29</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_029">exo 37:29</a></font></span></p>
 <p>He made the holy anointing oil - See this and the perfume, and the materials out of which they were made, described at large in the notes on <a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo030.htm#023">Exo 30:23-25</a> and <a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo030.htm#034">Exo 30:34-38</a>. As this chapter also is a repetition of what has been mentioned in preceding chapters, the reader is desired to refer to them.
