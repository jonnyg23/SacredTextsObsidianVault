 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Commentary on the Bible</i>, by Adam Clarke, [1831], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1014.htm#001">1 Chronicles Chapter 14</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1001.htm#001">1 Chronicles</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_000">ch1 14:0</a></font></span></p>
 <p>Hiram sends artificers and materials to David, to build him a house, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1014.htm#001">Ch1 14:1</a>, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1014.htm#002">Ch1 14:2</a>. David's wives and children, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1014.htm#003">Ch1 14:3-7</a>. He defeats the Philistines in two battles: one in the valley of Rephaim, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1014.htm#008">Ch1 14:8-12</a>; and the other at Gibeon and Gazer, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1014.htm#013">Ch1 14:13-16</a>. His fame goes out into all the surrounding nations, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1014.htm#017">Ch1 14:17</a>. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1014.htm#001">1 Chronicles 14:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">ch1 14:1</a></font></span></p>
 <p>Now Hiram king of Tyre - See the transactions of this chapter related <a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa2005.htm#011">Sa2 5:11-25</a> (note). 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1014.htm#004">1 Chronicles 14:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">ch1 14:4</a></font></span></p>
 <p>These are the names of his children - In <a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa2005.htm#014">Sa2 5:14-16</a> (note), eleven persons only are mentioned in the Hebrew text, but the Septuagint has twenty-four, here there are thirteen, and all the versions have the same number, with certain varieties in the names. - See the notes there. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1014.htm#008">1 Chronicles 14:8</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_008">ch1 14:8</a></font></span></p>
 <p>The Philistines went up to seek David - See on <a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa2005.htm#017">Sa2 5:17</a> (note). 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1014.htm#010">1 Chronicles 14:10</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_010">ch1 14:10</a></font></span></p>
 <p>David inquired of God - "David consulted the Word of the Lord." - T. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1014.htm#011">1 Chronicles 14:11</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_011">ch1 14:11</a></font></span></p>
 <p>Like the breaking forth of waters - "And David said, The Lord hath broken the enemies of David like to the breaking of a potter's vessel full of water." - T. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1014.htm#015">1 Chronicles 14:15</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_015">ch1 14:15</a></font></span></p>
 <p>A sound of going - "When thou shalt hear the sound of the angels coming to thy assistance, then go out to battle; for an angel is sent from the presence of God, that he may render thy way prosperous." - T. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1014.htm#017">1 Chronicles 14:17</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_017">ch1 14:17</a></font></span></p>
 <p>Into all lands - That is, all the surrounding or neighboring lands and nations, for no others can possibly be intended.
