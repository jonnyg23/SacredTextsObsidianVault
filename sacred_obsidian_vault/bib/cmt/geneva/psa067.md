 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Geneva Bible Translation Notes</i>, [1599], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa067.htm#001">Psalms Chapter 67</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa067.htm#001">Psalms 67:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">psa 67:1</a></font></span></p>"To the chief Musician on Neginoth, A Psalm [or] Song." God be merciful unto us, and bless us; [and] (a) cause his face to shine upon us; Selah. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(a) That is, move our hearts with his Holy Spirit, that we may feel his favour toward us.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa067.htm#002">Psalms 67:2</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_002">psa 67:2</a></font></span></p>That (b) thy way may be known upon earth, thy saving health among all nations. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(b) That both Jews and Gentiles may know God's covenant made with them.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa067.htm#004">Psalms 67:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">psa 67:4</a></font></span></p>(c) O let the nations be glad and sing for joy: for thou shalt judge the people righteously, and govern the nations upon earth. Selah. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(c) By these repetitions he shows that the people can never rejoice and give thanks enough for the great benefits that they will receive under the kingdom of Christ.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa067.htm#006">Psalms 67:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">psa 67:6</a></font></span></p>[Then] shall (d) the earth yield her increase; [and] God, [even] our own God, shall bless us. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(d) He shows that where God favours there will be abundance of all other things.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa067.htm#007">Psalms 67:7</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_007">psa 67:7</a></font></span></p>God shall bless us; and all the ends of the earth (e) shall fear him. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(e) When they feel his great benefits both spiritual and corporal toward them.
