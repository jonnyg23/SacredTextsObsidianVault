 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Geneva Bible Translation Notes</i>, [1599], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1002.htm#001">1 Chronicles Chapter 2</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1002.htm#003">1 Chronicles 2:3</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_003">ch1 2:3</a></font></span></p>The sons of (a) Judah; Er, and Onan, and Shelah: [which] three were born unto him of the daughter of Shua the Canaanitess. And Er, the firstborn of Judah, was evil in the sight of the LORD; and he slew him. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(a) Though Judah was not Jacob's eldest son, yet he first begins with him, because he would come to the genealogy of David, of whom came Christ. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1002.htm#006">1 Chronicles 2:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">ch1 2:6</a></font></span></p>And the sons of Zerah; Zimri, and (b) Ethan, and Heman, and Calcol, and Dara: five of them in all. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(b) Of these read (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/kg1004.htm#031">Kg1 4:31</a>). 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1002.htm#009">1 Chronicles 2:9</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_009">ch1 2:9</a></font></span></p>The sons also of Hezron, that were born unto him; Jerahmeel, and (c) Ram, and Chelubai. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(c) Whom Matthew calls Aram, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat001.htm#003">Mat 1:3</a>). 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1002.htm#010">1 Chronicles 2:10</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_010">ch1 2:10</a></font></span></p>And Ram begat Amminadab; and Amminadab begat Nahshon, (d) prince of the children of Judah; 
 <p>
 </p><p>(d) That is, chief of the family. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1002.htm#018">1 Chronicles 2:18</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_018">ch1 2:18</a></font></span></p>And (e) Caleb the son of Hezron begat [children] of Azubah [his] wife, and of Jerioth: her sons [are] these; Jesher, and Shobab, and Ardon. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(e) Who was called Chelubai the son of Hezron, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1002.htm#009">Ch1 2:9</a>). 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1002.htm#021">1 Chronicles 2:21</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_021">ch1 2:21</a></font></span></p>And afterward Hezron went in to the daughter of Machir the father of (f) Gilead, whom he married when he [was] threescore years old; and she bare him Segub. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(f) Who was prince of mount Gilead, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/num032.htm#040">Num 32:40</a>). 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1002.htm#023">1 Chronicles 2:23</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_023">ch1 2:23</a></font></span></p>And he took Geshur, and Aram, with the towns of Jair, (g) from them, with Kenath, and the towns thereof, [even] threescore cities. All these [belonged to] the sons of Machir the father of Gilead. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(g) That is, the Geshurites and Syrians took the towns from Jair's children. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1002.htm#024">1 Chronicles 2:24</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_024">ch1 2:24</a></font></span></p>And after that Hezron was dead in (h) Calebephratah, then Abiah Hezron's wife bare him Ashur the (i) father of Tekoa. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(h) Which was a town named for the husband and wife, also called Bethlehem Ephratah. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(i) Meaning, the chief and prince. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1002.htm#031">1 Chronicles 2:31</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_031">ch1 2:31</a></font></span></p>And the sons of Appaim; Ishi. And the sons of Ishi; Sheshan. And the children of Sheshan; (k) Ahlai. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(k) Who died while his father was alive, and therefore it is said in (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1002.htm#034">Ch1 2:34</a>) that Sheshan had no sons. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1002.htm#042">1 Chronicles 2:42</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_042">ch1 2:42</a></font></span></p>Now the sons of Caleb the brother of Jerahmeel [were], Mesha his firstborn, which was the (l) father of Ziph; and the sons of Mareshah the father of Hebron. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(l) That is, the chief governor or prince of the Ziphims, because the prince should have a fatherly care and affection for his people. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1002.htm#046">1 Chronicles 2:46</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_046">ch1 2:46</a></font></span></p>And Ephah, Caleb's (m) concubine, bare Haran, and Moza, and Gazez: and Haran begat Gazez. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(m) The difference between the wife and the concubine was that the wife was taken with a ceremony of marriage and her children inherited, while the concubine had no marriage ceremony, neither did her children inherit, but had a portion of goods or money given to them. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1002.htm#054">1 Chronicles 2:54</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_054">ch1 2:54</a></font></span></p>The sons of Salma; Bethlehem, and the Netophathites, Ataroth, the (n) house of Joab, and half of the Manahethites, the Zorites. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(n) The chief and principle of the house of Joab. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1002.htm#055">1 Chronicles 2:55</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_055">ch1 2:55</a></font></span></p>And the families of the (o) scribes which dwelt at Jabez; the Tirathites, the Shimeathites, [and] Suchathites. These [are] the (p) Kenites that came of Hemath, the father of the house of Rechab. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(o) Who were men learned and expert in the law. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(p) Read (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/num010.htm#029">Num 10:29</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/jdg001.htm#016">Jdg 1:16</a>).
