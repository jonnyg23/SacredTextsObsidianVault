 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Geneva Bible Translation Notes</i>, [1599], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo022.htm#001">Exodus Chapter 22</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo022.htm#001">Exodus 22:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">exo 22:1</a></font></span></p>If a man shall steal an (a) ox, or a sheep, and kill it, or sell it; he shall restore five oxen for an ox, and four sheep for a sheep. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(a) Either a great beast of the herd, or a small beast of the flock.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo022.htm#002">Exodus 22:2</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_002">exo 22:2</a></font></span></p>If a thief be found (b) breaking up, and be smitten that he die, [there shall] no blood [be shed] for him. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(b) Breaking a house to enter in, or undermining.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo022.htm#003">Exodus 22:3</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_003">exo 22:3</a></font></span></p>If the sun be risen upon him, [there shall be] (c) blood [shed] for him; [for] he should make full restitution; if he have nothing, then he shall be sold for his theft. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(c) He shall be put to death that kills him.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo022.htm#008">Exodus 22:8</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_008">exo 22:8</a></font></span></p>If the thief be not found, then the master of the house shall be brought unto the judges, [to see] whether he have (d) put his hand unto his neighbour's goods. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(d) That is, whether he has stolen.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo022.htm#011">Exodus 22:11</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_011">exo 22:11</a></font></span></p>(e) [Then] shall an oath of the LORD be between them both, that he hath not put his hand unto his neighbour's goods; and the owner of it shall accept [thereof], and he shall not make [it] good. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(e) They should swear by the name of the Lord.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo022.htm#013">Exodus 22:13</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_013">exo 22:13</a></font></span></p>If it be torn in pieces, [then] let him bring (f) it [for] witness, [and] he shall not make good that which was torn. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(f) He shall show some part of the beast or bring in witnesses.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo022.htm#015">Exodus 22:15</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_015">exo 22:15</a></font></span></p>[But] if the owner thereof [be] with it, he shall not make [it] good: if it [be] an hired [thing], it (g) came for his hire. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(g) He that hired it shall be free by paying the hire.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo022.htm#024">Exodus 22:24</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_024">exo 22:24</a></font></span></p>And my wrath shall wax hot, and I will kill you with the sword; and your (h) wives shall be widows, and your children fatherless. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(h) The just plague of God on the oppressors.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo022.htm#027">Exodus 22:27</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_027">exo 22:27</a></font></span></p>For that [is] his covering only, it [is] his raiment for his skin: wherein shall he sleep? and it shall come to pass, when he (i) crieth unto me, that I will hear; for I [am] gracious. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(i) For cold and necessity.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo022.htm#029">Exodus 22:29</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_029">exo 22:29</a></font></span></p>Thou shalt not delay [to offer] the (k) first of thy ripe fruits, and of thy liquors: the firstborn of thy sons shalt thou give unto me. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(k) Your abundance of your corn.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo022.htm#031">Exodus 22:31</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_031">exo 22:31</a></font></span></p>And ye shall be holy men unto me: neither shall ye eat [any] flesh [that is] torn of beasts in the field; ye shall cast it (l) to the dogs. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(l) And so have nothing to do with it.
