 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Geneva Bible Translation Notes</i>, [1599], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa011.htm#001">Psalms Chapter 11</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa011.htm#001">Psalms 11:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">psa 11:1</a></font></span></p>"To the chief Musician, [A Psalm] of David." In the LORD put I my trust: how say ye to my soul, (a) Flee [as] a bird to your mountain? 
 <p>
 </p><p>(a) This is the wicked counsel of his enemies to him and his companions to drive him from the hope of God's promise.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa011.htm#003">Psalms 11:3</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_003">psa 11:3</a></font></span></p>If the (b) foundations be destroyed, what can the (c) righteous do? 
 <p>
 </p><p>(b) All hope of help is taken away. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(c) Yet am I innocent and my cause good.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa011.htm#004">Psalms 11:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">psa 11:4</a></font></span></p>The LORD [is] in his holy temple, the LORD'S throne [is] in heaven: his eyes (d) behold, his eyelids try, the children of men. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(d) Though all things in earth are out of order, yet God will execute judgment from heaven.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa011.htm#006">Psalms 11:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">psa 11:6</a></font></span></p>Upon the wicked he shall rain snares, (e) fire and brimstone, and an horrible tempest: [this shall be] the (f) portion of their cup. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(e) As in the destruction of Sodom and Gomorrah. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(f) Which they will drink even to the dregs, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze023.htm#034">Eze 23:34</a>).
