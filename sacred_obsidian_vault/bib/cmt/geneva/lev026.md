 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Geneva Bible Translation Notes</i>, [1599], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev026.htm#001">Leviticus Chapter 26</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev026.htm#004">Leviticus 26:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">lev 26:4</a></font></span></p>Then I will give you (a) rain in due season, and the land shall yield her increase, and the trees of the field shall yield their fruit. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(a) By promising abundance of earthly things, he stirs the mind to consider the rich treasures of the spiritual blessings.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev026.htm#006">Leviticus 26:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">lev 26:6</a></font></span></p>And I will give peace in the land, and ye shall lie down, and none shall make [you] afraid: and I will rid evil beasts out of the land, neither shall the (b) sword go through your land. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(b) You shall have no war.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev026.htm#009">Leviticus 26:9</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_009">lev 26:9</a></font></span></p>For I will have respect unto you, and make you fruitful, and multiply you, and (c) establish my covenant with you. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(c) Perform that which I have promised.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev026.htm#011">Leviticus 26:11</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_011">lev 26:11</a></font></span></p>And I will set my (d) tabernacle among you: and my soul shall not abhor you. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(d) I will be daily present with you.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev026.htm#013">Leviticus 26:13</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_013">lev 26:13</a></font></span></p>I [am] the LORD your God, which brought you forth out of the land of Egypt, that ye should not be their bondmen; and I have broken the (e) bands of your yoke, and made you go upright. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(e) I have set you at full liberty, while before you were as beasts tied in bands.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev026.htm#015">Leviticus 26:15</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_015">lev 26:15</a></font></span></p>And if ye shall despise my statutes, or if your soul abhor my judgments, so that ye will not do all my commandments, [but] that ye break my (f) covenant: 
 <p>
 </p><p>(f) Which I made with you in choosing you to be my people.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev026.htm#017">Leviticus 26:17</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_017">lev 26:17</a></font></span></p>And I will set (g) my face against you, and ye shall be slain before your enemies: they that hate you shall reign over you; and ye shall flee when none pursueth you. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(g) Read (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev017.htm#010">Lev 17:10</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev026.htm#018">Leviticus 26:18</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_018">lev 26:18</a></font></span></p>And if ye will not yet for all this hearken unto me, then I will punish you (h) seven times more for your sins. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(h) That is, more extremely.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev026.htm#019">Leviticus 26:19</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_019">lev 26:19</a></font></span></p>And I will break the pride of your power; and I will make your heaven as (i) iron, and your earth as brass: 
 <p>
 </p><p>(i) You shall have drought and barrenness.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev026.htm#021">Leviticus 26:21</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_021">lev 26:21</a></font></span></p>And if ye walk (k) contrary unto me, and will not hearken unto me; I will bring seven times more plagues upon you according to your sins. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(k) Or as some read, by fortune, imputing my plagues to chance and fortune.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev026.htm#022">Leviticus 26:22</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_022">lev 26:22</a></font></span></p>I will also send wild beasts among you, which shall (l) rob you of your children, and destroy your cattle, and make you few in number; and your (m) [high] ways shall be desolate. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(l) Read (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/kg2017.htm#025">Kg2 17:25</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(m) Because no one dares to pass by it for fear of the beasts.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev026.htm#026">Leviticus 26:26</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_026">lev 26:26</a></font></span></p>[And] when I have broken the (n) staff of your bread, ten women shall bake your bread in one (o) oven, and they shall deliver [you] your bread again by weight: and ye shall eat, and not be satisfied. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(n) That is, the strength by which life is sustained, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze004.htm#016">Eze 4:16</a>, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze005.htm#016">Eze 5:16</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(o) One oven will be sufficient for ten families.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev026.htm#031">Leviticus 26:31</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_031">lev 26:31</a></font></span></p>And I will make your cities waste, and bring your sanctuaries unto desolation, and I (p) will not smell the savour of your sweet odours. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(p) I will not accept your sacrifices.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev026.htm#035">Leviticus 26:35</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_035">lev 26:35</a></font></span></p>As long as it lieth desolate it shall rest; because it did not rest in your (r) sabbaths, when ye dwelt upon it. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(r) Which I commanded you to keep.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev026.htm#036">Leviticus 26:36</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_036">lev 26:36</a></font></span></p>And upon them that are left [alive] of you I will send a faintness into their hearts in the lands of their enemies; and the sound of a shaken leaf shall chase them; and they shall (s) flee, as fleeing from a sword; and they shall fall when none pursueth. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(s) As if their enemies chased them.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev026.htm#039">Leviticus 26:39</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_039">lev 26:39</a></font></span></p>And they that are left of you shall pine away in their iniquity in your enemies' lands; and also in the iniquities of their fathers shall they pine away with (t) them. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(t) In that, as they are blameworthy of their fathers faults, they shall be punished as well as their fathers.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev026.htm#043">Leviticus 26:43</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_043">lev 26:43</a></font></span></p>(u) The land also shall be left of them, and shall enjoy her sabbaths, while she lieth desolate without them: and they shall accept of the punishment of their iniquity: because, even because they despised my judgments, and because their soul abhorred my statutes. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(u) While they are captives, and without repentance.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev026.htm#045">Leviticus 26:45</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_045">lev 26:45</a></font></span></p>But I will for their sakes remember the (x) covenant of their ancestors, whom I brought forth out of the land of Egypt in the sight of the heathen, that I might be their God: I [am] the LORD. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(x) Made to their forefathers.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev026.htm#046">Leviticus 26:46</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_046">lev 26:46</a></font></span></p>These [are] the statutes and judgments and laws, which the LORD made between him and the children of Israel in mount (y) Sinai by the hand of Moses. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(y) Fifty days after they came out of Egypt.
