 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Geneva Bible Translation Notes</i>, [1599], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo025.htm#001">Exodus Chapter 25</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo025.htm#002">Exodus 25:2</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_002">exo 25:2</a></font></span></p>(a) Speak unto the children of Israel, that they bring me an offering: of every man that giveth it willingly with his heart ye shall take my offering. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(a) After the moral and judicial law he gives them the ceremonial law that nothing should be left to man's invention.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo025.htm#003">Exodus 25:3</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_003">exo 25:3</a></font></span></p>And this [is] the offering which ye shall (b) take of them; gold, and silver, and brass, 
 <p>
 </p><p>(b) For the building and use of the tabernacle.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo025.htm#005">Exodus 25:5</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_005">exo 25:5</a></font></span></p>And rams' skins dyed red, and badgers' skins, and (c) shittim wood, 
 <p>
 </p><p>(c) Which is thought to be a kindred of Cedar, which will not rot.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo025.htm#006">Exodus 25:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">exo 25:6</a></font></span></p>Oil for the light, spices for (d) anointing oil, and for sweet incense, 
 <p>
 </p><p>(d) Ordained for the priest.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo025.htm#008">Exodus 25:8</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_008">exo 25:8</a></font></span></p>And let them make me a (e) sanctuary; that I may dwell among them. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(e) A place both to offer sacrifice, and to hear the law.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo025.htm#016">Exodus 25:16</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_016">exo 25:16</a></font></span></p>And thou shalt put into the ark the (f) testimony which I shall give thee. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(f) The stone tables, the rod of Aaron and manna which were a testimony of God's presence.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo025.htm#017">Exodus 25:17</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_017">exo 25:17</a></font></span></p>And thou shalt make a (g) mercy seat [of] pure gold: two cubits and a half [shall be] the length thereof, and a cubit and a half the breadth thereof. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(g) There God appeared mercifully to them: and this was a figure of Christ.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo025.htm#029">Exodus 25:29</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_029">exo 25:29</a></font></span></p>And thou shalt make the (h) dishes thereof, and spoons thereof, and covers thereof, and bowls thereof, to cover withal: [of] pure gold shalt thou make them. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(h) To set the bread upon.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo025.htm#031">Exodus 25:31</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_031">exo 25:31</a></font></span></p>And thou shalt make a candlestick [of] pure gold: [of] beaten (i) work shall the candlestick be made: his shaft, and his branches, his bowls, his knops, and his flowers, shall be of the same. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(i) It shall not be molten, but beaten out of the lump of gold with the hammer.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo025.htm#039">Exodus 25:39</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_039">exo 25:39</a></font></span></p>[Of] a (k) talent of pure gold shall he make it, with all these vessels. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(k) This was the talent weight of the temple, and weighed 120 pounds.
