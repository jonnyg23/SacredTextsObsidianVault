 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Geneva Bible Translation Notes</i>, [1599], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa087.htm#001">Psalms Chapter 87</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa087.htm#001">Psalms 87:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">psa 87:1</a></font></span></p>"A Psalm [or] Song for the sons of Korah." His (a) foundation [is] in the holy mountains. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(a) God chose that place among the hills to establish Jerusalem and his temple.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa087.htm#003">Psalms 87:3</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_003">psa 87:3</a></font></span></p>(b) Glorious things are spoken of thee, O city of God. Selah. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(b) Though your glorious estate does not yet appear, wait with patience and God will accomplish his promise.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa087.htm#004">Psalms 87:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">psa 87:4</a></font></span></p>I will make mention of (c) Rahab and Babylon to them that know me: behold Philistia, and Tyre, with Ethiopia; (d) this [man] was born there. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(c) That is, Egypt and these other countries will come to the knowledge of God. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(d) It will be said of him who is regenerate and come to the Church, that he is as one who was born in the Church.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa087.htm#005">Psalms 87:5</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_005">psa 87:5</a></font></span></p>And of Zion it shall be said, (e) This and that man was born in her: and the highest himself shall establish her. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(e) Out of all quarters they will come to the Church, and be counted as citizens.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa087.htm#006">Psalms 87:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">psa 87:6</a></font></span></p>The LORD shall count, when he (f) writeth up the people, [that] this [man] was born there. Selah. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(f) When he calls them by his word into the Church, whom he had elected and written in the book.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa087.htm#007">Psalms 87:7</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_007">psa 87:7</a></font></span></p>As well the singers as the players on instruments [shall be there]: all my (g) springs [are] in thee. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(g) The prophet sets his whole affections and comfort in the church.
