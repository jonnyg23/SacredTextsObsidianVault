 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Geneva Bible Translation Notes</i>, [1599], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze045.htm#001">Ezekiel Chapter 45</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze045.htm#001">Ezekiel 45:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">eze 45:1</a></font></span></p>Moreover, when ye shall divide by lot the land for inheritance, ye shall offer an oblation to the LORD, an (a) holy portion of the land: the length [shall be] the length of five and twenty thousand [reeds], and the breadth [shall be] ten thousand. This [shall be] holy in all its borders on every side. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(a) Of all the land of Israel the Lord only requires this portion for the temple and for the priests for the city and for the prince.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze045.htm#009">Ezekiel 45:9</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_009">eze 45:9</a></font></span></p>Thus saith the Lord GOD; Let it (b) suffice you, O princes of Israel: remove violence and spoil, and execute judgment and justice, take away your exactions from my people, saith the Lord GOD. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(b) The prophet shows that the heads must be first reformed before any good order can be established among the people.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze045.htm#010">Ezekiel 45:10</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_010">eze 45:10</a></font></span></p>Ye shall have just balances, and a just (c) ephah, and a just bath. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(c) Ephah and Bath are the same quantity save that an Ephah is a dry measure and a Bath liquid, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev005.htm#011">Lev 5:11</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/kg1005.htm#011">Kg1 5:11</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze045.htm#012">Ezekiel 45:12</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_012">eze 45:12</a></font></span></p>And the shekel [shall be] twenty gerahs: twenty shekels, (d) five and twenty shekels, fifteen shekels, shall be your maneh. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(d) That is, sixty shekels make a weight called Mina, for he joins these three parts to a Mina.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze045.htm#018">Ezekiel 45:18</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_018">eze 45:18</a></font></span></p>Thus saith the Lord GOD; In the first [month], in the first [day] of the (e) month, thou shalt take a young bull without blemish, and cleanse the sanctuary: 
 <p>
 </p><p>(e) Which was Nisan containing part of March and part of April.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze045.htm#024">Ezekiel 45:24</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_024">eze 45:24</a></font></span></p>And he shall prepare a meat offering of an ephah for a bull, and an ephah for a ram, and an (f) hin of oil for an ephah. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(f) Read (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo029.htm#040">Exo 29:40</a>).
