 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Geneva Bible Translation Notes</i>, [1599], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo011.htm#001">Exodus Chapter 11</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo011.htm#001">Exodus 11:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">exo 11:1</a></font></span></p>And the LORD said unto Moses, Yet will I bring one plague [more] upon Pharaoh, and upon Egypt; afterwards he will let you go hence: when he shall let [you] go, he shall (a) surely thrust you out hence altogether. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(a) Without any condition, but with haste and violence.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo011.htm#005">Exodus 11:5</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_005">exo 11:5</a></font></span></p>And all the firstborn in the land of Egypt shall die, from the firstborn of Pharaoh that sitteth upon his throne, even unto the firstborn of the maidservant that [is] behind (b) the mill; and all the firstborn of beasts. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(b) From the highest to the lowest.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo011.htm#008">Exodus 11:8</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_008">exo 11:8</a></font></span></p>And all these thy servants shall come down unto me, and bow down themselves unto me, saying, Get thee out, and all the people that (c) follow thee: and after that I will go out. And he went out from Pharaoh in a great anger. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(c) That is, under the power and government.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo011.htm#009">Exodus 11:9</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_009">exo 11:9</a></font></span></p>And the LORD said unto Moses, Pharaoh shall not hearken unto you; (d) that my wonders may be multiplied in the land of Egypt. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(d) God hardens the hearts of the reprobate, that his glory by this might be set forth even more, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom009.htm#017">Rom 9:17</a>).
