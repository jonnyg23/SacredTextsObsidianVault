 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Geneva Bible Translation Notes</i>, [1599], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/neh011.htm#001">Nehemiah Chapter 11</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/neh011.htm#001">Nehemiah 11:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">neh 11:1</a></font></span></p>And the rulers of the people dwelt at Jerusalem: the rest of the people also cast lots, (a) to bring one of ten to dwell in Jerusalem the holy city, and nine parts [to dwell] in [other] cities. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(a) Because their enemies dwelt round about them, they provided that it might be replenished with men, and used this policy because there were few who offered themselves willingly.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/neh011.htm#004">Nehemiah 11:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">neh 11:4</a></font></span></p>And at Jerusalem dwelt [certain] of the children of Judah, and of the children of Benjamin. Of the children of Judah; Athaiah the son of Uzziah, the son of Zechariah, the son of Amariah, the son of Shephatiah, the son of Mahalaleel, of the children of (b) Perez; 
 <p>
 </p><p>(b) Which came from Perez the son of Judah.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/neh011.htm#011">Nehemiah 11:11</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_011">neh 11:11</a></font></span></p>Seraiah the son of Hilkiah, the son of Meshullam, the son of Zadok, the son of Meraioth, the son of Ahitub, (c) [was] the ruler of the house of God. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(c) That is, was the high priest.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/neh011.htm#012">Nehemiah 11:12</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_012">neh 11:12</a></font></span></p>And their brethren (d) that did the work of the house [were] eight hundred twenty and two: and Adaiah the son of Jeroham, the son of Pelaliah, the son of Amzi, the son of Zechariah, the son of Pashur, the son of Malchiah, 
 <p>
 </p><p>(d) That served and ministered in the temple.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/neh011.htm#017">Nehemiah 11:17</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_017">neh 11:17</a></font></span></p>And Mattaniah the son of Micha, the son of Zabdi, the son of Asaph, [was] the principal to (e) begin the thanksgiving in prayer: and Bakbukiah the second among his brethren, and Abda the son of Shammua, the son of Galal, the son of Jeduthun. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(e) That is, he began the psalm and was the chanter.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/neh011.htm#019">Nehemiah 11:19</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_019">neh 11:19</a></font></span></p>Moreover the porters, Akkub, Talmon, and their brethren that kept the (f) gates, [were] an hundred seventy and two. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(f) Meaning of the temple.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/neh011.htm#020">Nehemiah 11:20</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_020">neh 11:20</a></font></span></p>And the (g) residue of Israel, of the priests, [and] the Levites, [were] in all the cities of Judah, every one in his inheritance. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(g) Of them who dwelt not in Jerusalem.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/neh011.htm#024">Nehemiah 11:24</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_024">neh 11:24</a></font></span></p>And Pethahiah the son of Meshezabeel, of the children of Zerah the son of Judah, (h) [was] at the king's hand in all matters concerning the people. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(h) Was chief about the king for all high affairs.
