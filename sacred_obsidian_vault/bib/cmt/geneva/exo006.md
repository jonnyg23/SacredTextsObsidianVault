 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Geneva Bible Translation Notes</i>, [1599], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo006.htm#001">Exodus Chapter 6</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo006.htm#003">Exodus 6:3</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_003">exo 6:3</a></font></span></p>And I appeared unto Abraham, unto Isaac, and unto Jacob, by [the name of] God Almighty, but by my name (a) JEHOVAH was I not known to them. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(a) By which he signifies that he will perform indeed that which he promised to their fathers: for this name declares that he is constant and will perform his promise.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo006.htm#007">Exodus 6:7</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_007">exo 6:7</a></font></span></p>And I will (b) take you to me for a people, and I will be to you a God: and ye shall know that I [am] the LORD your God, which bringeth you out from under the burdens of the Egyptians. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(b) He means, concerning the outward calling, the dignity of which they lost later by their rebellion: but as for election to life everlasting, it is unchangeable.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo006.htm#009">Exodus 6:9</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_009">exo 6:9</a></font></span></p>And Moses spake so unto the children of Israel: but they 
 <p>
 </p><p>hearkened (c) not unto Moses for anguish of spirit, and for cruel bondage. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(c) So hard a thing it is to show true obedience under the cross.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo006.htm#012">Exodus 6:12</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_012">exo 6:12</a></font></span></p>And Moses spake before the LORD, saying, Behold, the children of Israel have not hearkened unto me; how then shall Pharaoh hear me, who [am] of (d) uncircumcised lips? 
 <p>
 </p><p>(d) Or barbarous and rude in speech and by this word 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(uncircumcised) is signified the whole corruption of man's nature.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo006.htm#014">Exodus 6:14</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_014">exo 6:14</a></font></span></p>These [be] the heads (e) of their fathers' houses: The sons of Reuben the firstborn of Israel; Hanoch, and Pallu, Hezron, and Carmi: these [be] the families of Reuben. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(e) This genealogy shows whom Moses and Aaron came from.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo006.htm#016">Exodus 6:16</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_016">exo 6:16</a></font></span></p>And these [are] the names of the sons of Levi according to their generations; Gershon, and Kohath, and Merari: and the years of the life of Levi [were] an hundred (f) thirty and seven years. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(f) For he was 42 years old when he came into Egypt and lived there 94 years.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo006.htm#020">Exodus 6:20</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_020">exo 6:20</a></font></span></p>And Amram took him Jochebed his (g) father's sister to wife; and she bare him Aaron and Moses: and the years of the life of Amram [were] an hundred and thirty and seven years. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(g) This type of marriage was later forbidden in the law; (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev018.htm#012">Lev 18:12</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo006.htm#021">Exodus 6:21</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_021">exo 6:21</a></font></span></p>And the sons of Izhar; (h) Korah, and Nepheg, and Zichri. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(h) Moses and he were cousins, whose rebellion was punished in (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/num016.htm#001">Num 16:1</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo006.htm#023">Exodus 6:23</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_023">exo 6:23</a></font></span></p>And Aaron took him Elisheba, daughter of Amminadab, sister of (i) Naashon, to wife; and she bare him Nadab, and Abihu, Eleazar, and Ithamar. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(i) Who was a prince of Judah, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/num001.htm#007">Num 1:7</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo006.htm#026">Exodus 6:26</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_026">exo 6:26</a></font></span></p>These [are] that Aaron and Moses, to whom the LORD said, Bring out the children of Israel from the land of Egypt according to their (k) armies. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(k) For their families were so great, that they might be compared to armies.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo006.htm#030">Exodus 6:30</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_030">exo 6:30</a></font></span></p>And Moses said before the LORD, Behold, I [am] of (l) uncircumcised lips, and how shall Pharaoh hearken unto me? 
 <p>
 </p><p>(l) The disobedience both of Moses and of the people, shows that their deliverance came only from God's free mercy.
