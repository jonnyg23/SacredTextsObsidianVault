 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Geneva Bible Translation Notes</i>, [1599], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev021.htm#001">Leviticus Chapter 21</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev021.htm#001">Leviticus 21:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">lev 21:1</a></font></span></p>And the LORD said unto Moses, Speak unto the priests the sons of Aaron, and say unto them, There shall none be (a) defiled for the dead among his people: 
 <p>
 </p><p>(a) By touching the dead, lamenting, or being at their burial.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev021.htm#003">Leviticus 21:3</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_003">lev 21:3</a></font></span></p>And for his sister a (b) virgin, that is nigh unto him, which hath had no husband; for her may he be defiled. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(b) For being married she seemed to be cut off from his family.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev021.htm#004">Leviticus 21:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">lev 21:4</a></font></span></p>[But] he shall not defile himself, [being] a (c) chief man among his people, to profane himself. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(c) The priest was permitted to mourn for his next kindred only.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev021.htm#007">Leviticus 21:7</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_007">lev 21:7</a></font></span></p>They shall not take a wife [that is] a whore, or (d) profane; neither shall they take a woman put away from her husband: for he [is] holy unto his God. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(d) Which has an evil name or is defamed.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev021.htm#008">Leviticus 21:8</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_008">lev 21:8</a></font></span></p>Thou shalt (e) sanctify him therefore; for he offereth the (f) bread of thy God: he shall be holy unto thee: for I the LORD, which sanctify you, [am] holy. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(e) You shall count them holy and reverence them. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(f) The showbread.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev021.htm#010">Leviticus 21:10</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_010">lev 21:10</a></font></span></p>And [he that is] the high priest among his brethren, upon whose head the anointing oil was poured, and that is consecrated to put on the garments, shall not (g) uncover his head, nor rend his clothes; 
 <p>
 </p><p>(g) He shall use no such ceremonies as the mourners observed.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev021.htm#012">Leviticus 21:12</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_012">lev 21:12</a></font></span></p>Neither shall he go out of the (h) sanctuary, nor profane the sanctuary of his God; for the (i) crown of the anointing oil of his God [is] upon him: I [am] the LORD. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(h) To go to the dead. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(i) For by his anointing he was preferred above the other priests and therefore could not lament the dead, least he should have polluted his holy anointing.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev021.htm#014">Leviticus 21:14</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_014">lev 21:14</a></font></span></p>A widow, or a divorced woman, or profane, [or] an harlot, these shall he not take: but he shall take a virgin of his own (k) people to wife. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(k) Not only of his tribe, but of all Israel.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev021.htm#015">Leviticus 21:15</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_015">lev 21:15</a></font></span></p>Neither shall he profane his (l) seed among his people: for I the LORD do sanctify him. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(l) By marrying any unchaste or defamed woman.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev021.htm#018">Leviticus 21:18</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_018">lev 21:18</a></font></span></p>For whatsoever man [he be] that hath a blemish, he shall not approach: a blind man, or a lame, or he that hath (m) a flat nose, or any (n) thing superfluous, 
 <p>
 </p><p>(m) Which is deformed or bruised. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(n) As not of equal proportion, or having in number more or less.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev021.htm#020">Leviticus 21:20</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_020">lev 21:20</a></font></span></p>Or crookbackt, or a dwarf, (o) or that hath a blemish in his eye, or be scurvy, or scabbed, or hath his stones broken; 
 <p>
 </p><p>(o) Or that has a web or pearl.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev021.htm#021">Leviticus 21:21</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_021">lev 21:21</a></font></span></p>No man that hath a blemish of the seed of Aaron the priest shall come nigh to offer the offerings of the LORD made by fire: he hath a blemish; he shall not come nigh to offer the (p) bread of his God. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(p) As the show bread, and meat offerings.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev021.htm#022">Leviticus 21:22</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_022">lev 21:22</a></font></span></p>He shall eat the bread of his God, [both] of the (q) most holy, and (r) of the holy. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(q) As of sacrifice for sin. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(r) As of the tithes and first fruits.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev021.htm#023">Leviticus 21:23</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_023">lev 21:23</a></font></span></p>Only he shall not go in unto the (s) vail, nor come nigh unto the altar, because he hath a blemish; that he profane not my sanctuaries: for I the LORD do sanctify them. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(s) Into the sanctuary.
