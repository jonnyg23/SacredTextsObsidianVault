 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Geneva Bible Translation Notes</i>, [1599], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num017.htm#001">Numbers Chapter 17</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num017.htm#001">Numbers 17:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">num 17:1</a></font></span></p>And the LORD spake unto (a) Moses, saying, 
 <p>
 </p><p>(a) While he was in the door of the tabernacle.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num017.htm#005">Numbers 17:5</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_005">num 17:5</a></font></span></p>And it shall come to pass, [that] the man's rod, whom I shall (b) choose, shall blossom: and I will make to cease from me the murmurings of the children of Israel, whereby they murmur against you. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(b) To be the chief priest.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num017.htm#006">Numbers 17:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">num 17:6</a></font></span></p>And Moses spake unto the children of Israel, and every one of their princes gave him a rod apiece, for each prince one, according to their fathers' houses, [even] twelve rods: and the rod (c) of Aaron [was] among their rods. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(c) Though Joseph's tribe was divided into two in the distribution of the land, yet here it is but one and Levi makes a tribe.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num017.htm#008">Numbers 17:8</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_008">num 17:8</a></font></span></p>And it came to pass, that on the morrow Moses went into the tabernacle of witness; and, behold, the rod of Aaron (d) for the house of Levi was budded, and brought forth buds, and bloomed blossoms, and yielded almonds. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(d) To declare that God chose the house of Levi to serve him in the tabernacle.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num017.htm#010">Numbers 17:10</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_010">num 17:10</a></font></span></p>And the LORD said unto Moses, Bring Aaron's rod again before the testimony, to be kept for a token against the rebels; and thou shalt quite take away their (e) murmurings from me, that they die not. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(e) Grudging that Aaron should be high priest.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num017.htm#012">Numbers 17:12</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_012">num 17:12</a></font></span></p>And the children of Israel spake unto Moses, saying, Behold, (f) we die, we perish, we all perish. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(f) The Chaldea text describes their complaining in this way; "We die by the sword, the earth swallows us up, the pestilence consumes us."
