 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Geneva Bible Translation Notes</i>, [1599], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1006.htm#001">1 Chronicles Chapter 6</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1006.htm#008">1 Chronicles 6:8</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_008">ch1 6:8</a></font></span></p>And Ahitub begat (a) Zadok, and Zadok begat Ahimaaz, 
 <p>
 </p><p>(a) Who was high priest after Abiathar was deposed, according to the prophecy of Eli the priest, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa1002.htm#031">Sa1 2:31</a>, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa1002.htm#035">Sa1 2:35</a>). 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1006.htm#010">1 Chronicles 6:10</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_010">ch1 6:10</a></font></span></p>And Johanan begat Azariah, (he [it is] that executed the (b) priest's office in the temple that Solomon built in Jerusalem:) 
 <p>
 </p><p>(b) And valiantly resisted king Uzziah, who would have usurped the priest's office (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch2026.htm#017">Ch2 26:17-18</a>). 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1006.htm#015">1 Chronicles 6:15</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_015">ch1 6:15</a></font></span></p>And (c) Jehozadak went [into captivity], when the LORD carried away Judah and Jerusalem by the hand of Nebuchadnezzar. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(c) That is, he was led into captivity with his father Seraiah the high priest, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/kg2025.htm#018">Kg2 25:18</a>). 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1006.htm#022">1 Chronicles 6:22</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_022">ch1 6:22</a></font></span></p>The sons of Kohath; (d) Amminadab his son, Korah his son, Assir his son, 
 <p>
 </p><p>(d) Who seems to be called Izhar, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo006.htm#021">Exo 6:21</a>). 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1006.htm#028">1 Chronicles 6:28</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_028">ch1 6:28</a></font></span></p>And the sons of Samuel; the firstborn (e) Vashni, and Abiah. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(e) Who is also called Joel, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa1008.htm#002">Sa1 8:2</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1006.htm#033">Ch1 6:33</a>). 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1006.htm#031">1 Chronicles 6:31</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_031">ch1 6:31</a></font></span></p>And these [are they] whom David set over the service of song in the house of the LORD, after that the ark had (f) rest. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(f) After it was brought to the place where the temple would be built and was no longer carried to and fro. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1006.htm#039">1 Chronicles 6:39</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_039">ch1 6:39</a></font></span></p>And his brother (h) Asaph, who stood on his right hand, [even] Asaph the son of Berachiah, the son of Shimea, 
 <p>
 </p><p>(h) Meaning, the son of Heman, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1006.htm#033">Ch1 6:33</a>). 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1006.htm#048">1 Chronicles 6:48</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_048">ch1 6:48</a></font></span></p>Their (i) brethren also the Levites [were] (k) appointed unto all manner of service of the tabernacle of the house of God. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(i) The Levites are called the singers brothers because they came from the same stock. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(k) Read (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/num004.htm#004">Num 4:4</a>). 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1006.htm#054">1 Chronicles 6:54</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_054">ch1 6:54</a></font></span></p>Now these [are] their (l) dwelling places throughout their castles in their coasts, of the sons of Aaron, of the families of the Kohathites: for theirs was the (m) lot. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(l) Or, cities which were given to the Levites. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(m) They were first appointed, and prepared for. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1006.htm#055">1 Chronicles 6:55</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_055">ch1 6:55</a></font></span></p>And they gave them (n) Hebron in the land of Judah, and the suburbs thereof round about it. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(n) Which was also called Kirjatharba, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/gen023.htm#002">Gen 23:2</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/jos021.htm#011">Jos 21:11</a>). 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1006.htm#057">1 Chronicles 6:57</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_057">ch1 6:57</a></font></span></p>And to the sons of Aaron they gave the cities of Judah, [namely], Hebron, [the city] of (o) refuge, and Libnah with her suburbs, and Jattir, and Eshtemoa, with their suburbs, 
 <p>
 </p><p>(o) That he that had killed a man might flee to it for help till his case was tried, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/deu019.htm#002">Deu 19:2</a>). 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1006.htm#058">1 Chronicles 6:58</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_058">ch1 6:58</a></font></span></p>And (p) Hilen with her suburbs, Debir with her suburbs, 
 <p>
 </p><p>(p) Which Joshua called Holon, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/jos015.htm#051">Jos 15:51</a>, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/jos021.htm#015">Jos 21:15</a>). 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1006.htm#060">1 Chronicles 6:60</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_060">ch1 6:60</a></font></span></p>And out of the tribe of Benjamin; Geba with her suburbs, and (q) Alemeth with her suburbs, and Anathoth with her suburbs. All their cities throughout their families [were] thirteen cities. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(q) Or, Almon (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/jos021.htm#018">Jos 21:18</a>). 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1006.htm#061">1 Chronicles 6:61</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_061">ch1 6:61</a></font></span></p>And unto the sons of (r) Kohath, [which were] left of the family of that tribe, [were cities given] out of the half tribe, [namely, out of] the half [tribe] of Manasseh, by lot, ten cities. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(r) That is, they gave a portion to the Kohathites, who were the remnant of the tribe of Levi, out of the half tribe of Manasseh and out of Ephraim, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1006.htm#066">Ch1 6:66</a>). 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1006.htm#071">1 Chronicles 6:71</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_071">ch1 6:71</a></font></span></p>Unto the sons of (s) Gershom [were given] out of the family of the half tribe of Manasseh, Golan in Bashan with her suburbs, and Ashtaroth with her suburbs: 
 <p>
 </p><p>(s) Who in the first verse is also called Gershon.
