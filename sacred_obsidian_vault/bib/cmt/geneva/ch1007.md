 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Geneva Bible Translation Notes</i>, [1599], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1007.htm#001">1 Chronicles Chapter 7</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1007.htm#001">1 Chronicles 7:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">ch1 7:1</a></font></span></p>Now the sons of Issachar [were], Tola, and Puah, (a) Jashub, and Shimron, four. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(a) Who also is called Job, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/gen046.htm#013">Gen 46:13</a>). 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1007.htm#002">1 Chronicles 7:2</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_002">ch1 7:2</a></font></span></p>And the sons of Tola; Uzzi, and Rephaiah, and Jeriel, and Jahmai, and Jibsam, and Shemuel, heads of their father's house, [to wit], of Tola: [they were] valiant men of might in their generations; (b) whose number [was] in the days of David two and twenty thousand and six hundred. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(b) That is, their number was found to be this big when David counted the people, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa2024.htm#001">Sa2 24:1</a>). 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1007.htm#003">1 Chronicles 7:3</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_003">ch1 7:3</a></font></span></p>And the sons of Uzzi; Izrahiah: and the sons of Izrahiah; Michael, and Obadiah, and Joel, Ishiah, (c) five: all of them chief men. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(c) Meaning, the four sons and the father. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1007.htm#006">1 Chronicles 7:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">ch1 7:6</a></font></span></p>[The sons] of Benjamin; Bela, and Becher, and (d) Jediael, (e) three. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(d) Also called Ashbel, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/gen046.htm#021">Gen 46:21</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/num026.htm#038">Num 26:38</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(e) Who were the chief: or else there were seven in all as it appears in (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/gen046.htm#021">Gen 46:21</a>). 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1007.htm#012">1 Chronicles 7:12</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_012">ch1 7:12</a></font></span></p>Shuppim also, and Huppim, the children of Ir, [and] Hushim, the sons of (f) Aher. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(f) Meaning that he was not the son of Benjamin, but of Dan (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/gen046.htm#023">Gen 46:23</a>). 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1007.htm#013">1 Chronicles 7:13</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_013">ch1 7:13</a></font></span></p>The sons of Naphtali; Jahziel, and Guni, and Jezer, and Shallum, (g) the sons of Bilhah. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(g) These came from Dan and Naphtali, who were the sons of Bilhah, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/gen046.htm#023">Gen 46:23-25</a>). 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1007.htm#018">1 Chronicles 7:18</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_018">ch1 7:18</a></font></span></p>And (h) his sister Hammoleketh bare Ishod, and Abiezer, and Mahalah. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(h) Meaning, the sister of Gilead. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1007.htm#021">1 Chronicles 7:21</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_021">ch1 7:21</a></font></span></p>And Zabad his son, and Shuthelah his son, and Ezer, and Elead, whom the men of (i) Gath [that were] born in [that] land slew, because they came down to take away their cattle. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(i) Which was one of the five principal cities of the Philistines and who slew the Ephraimites. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1007.htm#025">1 Chronicles 7:25</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_025">ch1 7:25</a></font></span></p>And Rephah [was] his (k) son, also Resheph, and Telah his son, and Tahan his son, 
 <p>
 </p><p>(k) That is, of Ephraim.
