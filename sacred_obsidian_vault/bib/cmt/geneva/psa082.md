 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Geneva Bible Translation Notes</i>, [1599], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa082.htm#001">Psalms Chapter 82</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa082.htm#001">Psalms 82:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">psa 82:1</a></font></span></p>"A Psalm of Asaph." God standeth in the congregation of the (a) mighty; he judgeth among the gods. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(a) The prophet shows that if princes and judges do not do their duty, God whose authority is above them will take vengeance on them.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa082.htm#002">Psalms 82:2</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_002">psa 82:2</a></font></span></p>How long will ye judge unjustly, and accept the persons of the (b) wicked? Selah. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(b) For thieves and murderers find favour in judgment when the cause of the godly cannot be heard.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa082.htm#004">Psalms 82:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">psa 82:4</a></font></span></p>Deliver the poor and (c) needy: rid [them] out of the hand of the wicked. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(c) Not only when they cry for help, but when their cause requires aid and support.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa082.htm#005">Psalms 82:5</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_005">psa 82:5</a></font></span></p>They know not, neither will they understand; they walk on in darkness: all the (d) foundations of the earth are out of course. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(d) That is, all things are out of order either by their tyranny or careless negligence.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa082.htm#007">Psalms 82:7</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_007">psa 82:7</a></font></span></p>(e) But ye shall die like men, and fall like one of the princes. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(e) No title of honour will excuse you, but you will be subject to God's judgments, and tender account as well as other men.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa082.htm#008">Psalms 82:8</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_008">psa 82:8</a></font></span></p>Arise, O God, judge the earth: for thou shalt inherit (f) all nations. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(f) Therefore no tyrant will pluck your right and authority from you.
