 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Geneva Bible Translation Notes</i>, [1599], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1011.htm#001">1 Chronicles Chapter 11</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1011.htm#001">1 Chronicles 11:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">ch1 11:1</a></font></span></p>Then all Israel (a) gathered themselves to David unto Hebron, saying, Behold, we [are] thy bone and thy flesh. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(a) This was after the death of Ishbosheth Saul's son, when David had reigned over Judah seven years and six months in Hebron, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa2005.htm#005">Sa2 5:5</a>). 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1011.htm#011">1 Chronicles 11:11</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_011">ch1 11:11</a></font></span></p>And this [is] the number of the mighty men whom David had; Jashobeam, an Hachmonite, the (b) chief of the captains: he lifted up his spear against three hundred slain [by him] at one time. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(b) Meaning, the most excellent and best esteemed for his valiantry: some read, the chief of the princes. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1011.htm#014">1 Chronicles 11:14</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_014">ch1 11:14</a></font></span></p>And they set themselves in the midst of [that] parcel, (c) and delivered it, and slew the Philistines; and the LORD saved [them] by a great deliverance. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(c) This act refers to Shammah, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa2023.htm#011">Sa2 23:11</a>), who it seems was the chiefest of these. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1011.htm#015">1 Chronicles 11:15</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_015">ch1 11:15</a></font></span></p>Now three of the (d) thirty captains went down to the rock to David, into the cave of Adullam; and the host of the Philistines encamped in the valley of Rephaim. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(d) That is, Eleazar and his two companions. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1011.htm#019">1 Chronicles 11:19</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_019">ch1 11:19</a></font></span></p>And said, My God forbid it me, that I should do this thing: shall I drink the (e) blood of these men that have put their lives in jeopardy? for with [the jeopardy of] their lives they brought it. Therefore he would not drink it. These things did these three mightiest. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(e) That is, this water, for which they risked their lives. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1011.htm#025">1 Chronicles 11:25</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_025">ch1 11:25</a></font></span></p>Behold, he was honourable among the thirty, but attained not to the (f) [first] three: and David set him over his guard. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(f) Meaning, those three who brought the water to David. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1011.htm#027">1 Chronicles 11:27</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_027">ch1 11:27</a></font></span></p>(g) Shammoth the Harorite, Helez the Pelonite, 
 <p>
 </p><p>(g) Called also Shammah, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa2023.htm#025">Sa2 23:25</a>). 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1011.htm#029">1 Chronicles 11:29</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_029">ch1 11:29</a></font></span></p>(h) Sibbecai the Hushathite, Ilai the Ahohite, 
 <p>
 </p><p>(h) He is also called Mebunnai, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa2023.htm#027">Sa2 23:27</a>).
