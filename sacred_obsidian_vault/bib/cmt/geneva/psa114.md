 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Geneva Bible Translation Notes</i>, [1599], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa114.htm#001">Psalms Chapter 114</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa114.htm#001">Psalms 114:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">psa 114:1</a></font></span></p>When Israel went out of Egypt, the house of Jacob from a people of (a) strange language; 
 <p>
 </p><p>(a) Or, barbarous.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa114.htm#002">Psalms 114:2</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_002">psa 114:2</a></font></span></p>Judah was his (b) sanctuary, [and] Israel his dominion. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(b) The whole people were witnesses to his holy majesty in adopting them, and of his mighty power in delivering them.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa114.htm#004">Psalms 114:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">psa 114:4</a></font></span></p>The (c) mountains skipped like rams, [and] the little hills like lambs. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(c) Seeing that these dead creatures felt God's power and after a sort saw it, much more his people ought to consider it, and glorify him for the same.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa114.htm#007">Psalms 114:7</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_007">psa 114:7</a></font></span></p>Tremble, thou (d) earth, at the presence of the Lord, at the presence of the God of Jacob; 
 <p>
 </p><p>(d) Ought then his people to be insensible when they see his power and majesty?  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa114.htm#008">Psalms 114:8</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_008">psa 114:8</a></font></span></p>Which (e) turned the rock [into] a standing water, the flint into a fountain of waters. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(e) That is, miraculously caused water to come out of the rock in great abundance, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo017.htm#006">Exo 17:6</a>).
