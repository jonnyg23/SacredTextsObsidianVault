 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Geneva Bible Translation Notes</i>, [1599], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo029.htm#001">Exodus Chapter 29</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo029.htm#003">Exodus 29:3</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_003">exo 29:3</a></font></span></p>And thou shalt put them into one basket, and (a) bring them in the basket, with the bullock and the two rams. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(a) To offer them in sacrifice.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo029.htm#005">Exodus 29:5</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_005">exo 29:5</a></font></span></p>And thou shalt take the garments, and put upon Aaron the coat, and the (b) robe of the ephod, and the ephod, and the breastplate, and gird him with the curious girdle of the ephod: 
 <p>
 </p><p>(b) Which was next under the Ephod.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo029.htm#010">Exodus 29:10</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_010">exo 29:10</a></font></span></p>And thou shalt cause a bullock to be brought before the tabernacle of the congregation: and Aaron and his sons shall (c) put their hands upon the head of the bullock. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(c) Signifying that the sacrifice was also offered for them, and that they approved it.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo029.htm#018">Exodus 29:18</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_018">exo 29:18</a></font></span></p>And thou shalt burn the whole ram upon the altar: it [is] a burnt offering unto the LORD: (d) it [is] a sweet savour, an offering made by fire unto the LORD. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(d) Or, savour of rest, which causes the wrath of God to cease.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo029.htm#020">Exodus 29:20</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_020">exo 29:20</a></font></span></p>Then shalt thou kill the ram, and take of his blood, and put [it] (e) upon the tip of the right ear of Aaron, and upon the tip of the right ear of his sons, and upon the thumb of their right hand, and upon the great toe of their right foot, and sprinkle the blood upon the altar round about. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(e) Meaning the soft and lower part of the ear.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo029.htm#021">Exodus 29:21</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_021">exo 29:21</a></font></span></p>And thou shalt take of the blood that [is] (f) upon the altar, and of the anointing oil, and sprinkle [it] upon Aaron, and upon his garments, and upon his sons, and upon the garments of his sons with him: and he shall be hallowed, and his garments, and his sons, and his sons' garments with him. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(f) With which the Altar must be sprinkled.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo029.htm#022">Exodus 29:22</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_022">exo 29:22</a></font></span></p>Also thou shalt take of the ram the fat and the rump, and the fat that covereth the inwards, and the caul [above] the liver, and the two kidneys, and the fat that [is] upon them, and the right shoulder; for it [is] a (g) ram of consecration: 
 <p>
 </p><p>(g) Which is offered for the consecration of the high priest.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo029.htm#026">Exodus 29:26</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_026">exo 29:26</a></font></span></p>And thou shalt take the breast of the ram of Aaron's consecration, and wave it (h) [for] a wave offering before the LORD: and it shall be thy part. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(h) This sacrifice the priest did move toward the East, West, North, and South.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo029.htm#027">Exodus 29:27</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_027">exo 29:27</a></font></span></p>And thou shalt sanctify the breast of the wave offering, and the shoulder of the (i) heave offering, which is waved, and which is heaved up, of the ram of the consecration, [even] of [that] which [is] for Aaron, and of [that] which is for his sons: 
 <p>
 </p><p>(i) So called because it was not only shaken to and fro, but also lifted up.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo029.htm#028">Exodus 29:28</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_028">exo 29:28</a></font></span></p>And it shall be Aaron's and his sons' by a statute for ever from the children of Israel: for it [is] an heave offering: and it shall be an heave offering from the children of Israel of the sacrifice of their (k) peace offerings, [even] their heave offering unto the LORD. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(k) Which were offerings of thanksgiving to God for his benefits.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo029.htm#033">Exodus 29:33</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_033">exo 29:33</a></font></span></p>And they shall eat those things (l) wherewith the atonement was made, to consecrate [and] to sanctify them: but a stranger shall not eat [thereof], because they [are] holy. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(l) That is, by the sacrifices.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo029.htm#036">Exodus 29:36</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_036">exo 29:36</a></font></span></p>And thou shalt offer every day a bullock [for] a sin offering for (m) atonement: and thou shalt cleanse the altar, when thou hast made an atonement for it, and thou shalt anoint it, to sanctify it. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(m) To appease God's wrath that sin may be pardoned.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo029.htm#040">Exodus 29:40</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_040">exo 29:40</a></font></span></p>And with the one lamb a (n) tenth deal of flour mingled with the fourth part of an (o) hin of beaten oil; and the fourth part of an hin of wine [for] a drink offering. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(n) That is, an Omer, read (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo016.htm#016">Exo 16:16</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(o) Which is about a pint.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo029.htm#043">Exodus 29:43</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_043">exo 29:43</a></font></span></p>And there I will meet with the children of Israel, and [the tabernacle] shall be sanctified by my (p) glory. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(p) Because of my glorious presence.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo029.htm#046">Exodus 29:46</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_046">exo 29:46</a></font></span></p>And they shall know that I [am] the LORD their God, that brought them forth out of the land of Egypt, that I may dwell among them: (q) I [am] the LORD their God. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(q) It is I the Lord, that am their God.
