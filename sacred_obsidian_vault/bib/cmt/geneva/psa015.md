 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Geneva Bible Translation Notes</i>, [1599], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa015.htm#001">Psalms Chapter 15</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa015.htm#002">Psalms 15:2</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_002">psa 15:2</a></font></span></p>He that (a) walketh uprightly, and worketh righteousness, and speaketh the truth in his heart. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(a) He shows that we cannot call on God unless we trust in him.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa015.htm#004">Psalms 15:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">psa 15:4</a></font></span></p>(b) In whose eyes a vile person is contemned; but he honoureth them that fear the LORD. [He that] sweareth to [his own] hurt, and changeth not. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(b) He who flatters not the ungodly in their wickedness.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa015.htm#005">Psalms 15:5</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_005">psa 15:5</a></font></span></p>[He that] (c) putteth not out his money to usury, nor taketh reward against the innocent. He that doeth these [things] (d) shall never be moved. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(c) To the hinderance of his neighbour. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(d) That is, will not be cast from the Church as a hypocrite.
