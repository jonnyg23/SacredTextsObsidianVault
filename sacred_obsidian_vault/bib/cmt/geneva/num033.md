 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Geneva Bible Translation Notes</i>, [1599], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num033.htm#001">Numbers Chapter 33</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num033.htm#001">Numbers 33:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">num 33:1</a></font></span></p>These [are] the (a) journeys of the children of Israel, which went forth out of the land of Egypt with their armies under the hand of Moses and Aaron. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(a) From which they departed, and where they came.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num033.htm#004">Numbers 33:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">num 33:4</a></font></span></p>For the Egyptians buried all [their] firstborn, which the LORD had smitten among them: upon their (b) gods also the LORD executed judgments. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(b) Either meaning their idols, or their men of authority.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num033.htm#007">Numbers 33:7</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_007">num 33:7</a></font></span></p>And they removed from Etham, and turned again unto (c) Pihahiroth, which [is] before Baalzephon: and they pitched before Migdol. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(c) At the commandment of the Lord in (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo014.htm#002">Exo 14:2</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num033.htm#038">Numbers 33:38</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_038">num 33:38</a></font></span></p>And Aaron the priest went up into mount Hor at the commandment of the LORD, and died there, in the fortieth year after the children of Israel were come out of the land of Egypt, in the first [day] of the (d) fifth month. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(d) Which the Hebrews call Ab, and contains part of July and part of August.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num033.htm#052">Numbers 33:52</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_052">num 33:52</a></font></span></p>Then ye shall drive out all the inhabitants of the land from before you, and destroy all their (e) pictures, and destroy all their molten images, and quite pluck down all their high places: 
 <p>
 </p><p>(e) Which were set up in their high places to worship.
