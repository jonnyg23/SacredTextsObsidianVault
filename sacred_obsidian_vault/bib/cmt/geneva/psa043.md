 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Geneva Bible Translation Notes</i>, [1599], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa043.htm#001">Psalms Chapter 43</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa043.htm#001">Psalms 43:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">psa 43:1</a></font></span></p>Judge (a) me, O God, and plead my cause against an ungodly (b) nation: O deliver me from the deceitful and unjust man. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(a) He desires God to undertake his cause against the enemies but chiefly that he would restore him to the tabernacle. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(b) That is, the cruel company of my enemies.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa043.htm#003">Psalms 43:3</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_003">psa 43:3</a></font></span></p>O send out thy (c) light and thy truth: let them lead me; let them bring me unto thy holy hill, and to thy tabernacles. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(c) That is, your favour which appears by the performance of your promises.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa043.htm#004">Psalms 43:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">psa 43:4</a></font></span></p>Then (d) will I go unto the altar of God, unto God my exceeding joy: yea, upon the harp will I praise thee, O God my God. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(d) He promises to offer a solemn sacrifice of thanksgiving in token of his great deliverance.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa043.htm#005">Psalms 43:5</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_005">psa 43:5</a></font></span></p>Why art thou cast down, O my soul? and why art thou disquieted within me? (e) hope in God: for I shall yet praise him, [who is] the health of my countenance, and my God. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(e) By which he admonishes the faithful not to relent but constantly to wait on the Lord, though their troubles are long and great.
