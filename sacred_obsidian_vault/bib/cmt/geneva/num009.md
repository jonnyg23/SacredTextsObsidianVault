 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Geneva Bible Translation Notes</i>, [1599], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num009.htm#001">Numbers Chapter 9</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num009.htm#003">Numbers 9:3</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_003">num 9:3</a></font></span></p>In the fourteenth day of this month, at even, ye shall keep it in his appointed season: according to (a) all the rites of it, and according to all the ceremonies thereof, shall ye keep it. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(a) Even in all points as the Lord has instituted it.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num009.htm#006">Numbers 9:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">num 9:6</a></font></span></p>And there were certain men, who were defiled (b) by the dead body of a man, that they could not keep the passover on that day: and they came before Moses and before Aaron on that day: 
 <p>
 </p><p>(b) By touching a corpse, or being at the burial.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num009.htm#007">Numbers 9:7</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_007">num 9:7</a></font></span></p>And those men said unto him, We [are] defiled by the dead body of a man: wherefore are we kept back, that we may not (c) offer an offering of the LORD in his appointed season among the children of Israel? 
 <p>
 </p><p>(c) Or, celebrate the Passover the fourteenth day of the first month.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num009.htm#010">Numbers 9:10</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_010">num 9:10</a></font></span></p>Speak unto the children of Israel, saying, If any man of you or of your posterity shall be unclean by reason of a dead body, or [be] in a journey afar off, yet (d) he shall keep the passover unto the LORD. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(d) And cannot come where the tabernacle is, when others keep it.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num009.htm#011">Numbers 9:11</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_011">num 9:11</a></font></span></p>The fourteenth day of the (e) second month at even they shall keep it, [and] eat it with unleavened bread and bitter [herbs]. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(e) So that the unclean and those who are not at home, have a month longer granted to them.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num009.htm#013">Numbers 9:13</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_013">num 9:13</a></font></span></p>But the man that [is] clean, and is not in a (f) journey, and forbeareth to keep the passover, even the same soul shall be cut off from among his people: because he brought not the offering of the LORD in his appointed season, that man shall bear his sin. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(f) When the Passover is celebrated.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num009.htm#015">Numbers 9:15</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_015">num 9:15</a></font></span></p>And on the day that the tabernacle was reared up the cloud covered the tabernacle, [namely], the tent of the testimony: and at even there was upon the tabernacle as it were the (g) appearance of fire, until the morning. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(g) Like a pillar, read (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo013.htm#021">Exo 13:21-22</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num009.htm#018">Numbers 9:18</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_018">num 9:18</a></font></span></p>At the commandment of the (h) LORD the children of Israel journeyed, and at the commandment of the LORD they pitched: as long as the cloud abode upon the tabernacle they rested in their tents. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(h) Who taught them what to do by the cloud.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num009.htm#019">Numbers 9:19</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_019">num 9:19</a></font></span></p>And when the cloud tarried long upon the tabernacle many days, then the children of Israel kept the (i) charge of the LORD, and journeyed not. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(i) They waited when the Lord would signify either their departure, or their abode by the cloud.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num009.htm#023">Numbers 9:23</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_023">num 9:23</a></font></span></p>At the commandment of the LORD they rested in the tents, and at the commandment of the LORD they journeyed: they kept the charge of the LORD, at the commandment of the LORD by the (k) hand of Moses. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(k) Under the charge and government of Moses.
