 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Geneva Bible Translation Notes</i>, [1599], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jos013.htm#001">Joshua Chapter 13</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jos013.htm#001">Joshua 13:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">jos 13:1</a></font></span></p>Now Joshua was old [and] (a) stricken in years; and the LORD said unto him, Thou art old [and] stricken in years, and there remaineth yet very much land to be (b) possessed. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(a) Being almost a hundred and ten years old. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(b) After the enemies are overcome.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jos013.htm#006">Joshua 13:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">jos 13:6</a></font></span></p>All the inhabitants of the hill country from Lebanon unto (c) Misrephothmaim, [and] all the Sidonians, them will I drive out from before the children of Israel: only divide thou it by lot unto the Israelites for an inheritance, as I have commanded thee. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(c) Read (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/jos011.htm#008">Jos 11:8</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jos013.htm#013">Joshua 13:13</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_013">jos 13:13</a></font></span></p>Nevertheless the children of Israel (d) expelled not the Geshurites, nor the Maachathites: but the Geshurites and the Maachathites dwell among the Israelites until this day. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(d) Because they had not destroyed all as God had commanded they that remainder were snares and pricks to hurt them, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/num033.htm#035">Num 33:35</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/jos023.htm#013">Jos 23:13</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/jdg002.htm#003">Jdg 2:3</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jos013.htm#014">Joshua 13:14</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_014">jos 13:14</a></font></span></p>Only unto the tribe of Levi he gave none inheritance; the sacrifices of the LORD God of Israel made by fire [are] (e) their inheritance, as he said unto them. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(e) Levi will live by the sacrifices, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/num018.htm#021">Num 18:21</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jos013.htm#022">Joshua 13:22</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_022">jos 13:22</a></font></span></p>(f) Balaam also the son of Beor, the soothsayer, did the children of Israel slay with the sword among them that were slain by them. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(f) So that both they who obeyed wicked counsel and the wicked counsellor perished by the just judgment of God.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jos013.htm#027">Joshua 13:27</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_027">jos 13:27</a></font></span></p>And in the valley, Betharam, and Bethnimrah, and Succoth, and Zaphon, the rest of the kingdom of Sihon king of Heshbon, Jordan and [his] border, [even] unto the edge of the sea of Chinnereth on the (g) other side Jordan eastward. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(g) That is, in the land of Moab.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jos013.htm#031">Joshua 13:31</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_031">jos 13:31</a></font></span></p>And half Gilead, and Ashtaroth, and Edrei, cities of the kingdom of Og in Bashan, [were pertaining] unto the (h) children of Machir the son of Manasseh, [even] to the one half of the children of Machir by their families. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(h) Meaning, his nephews and posterity.
