 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Geneva Bible Translation Notes</i>, [1599], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/sol008.htm#001">Song of Solomon (Canticles) Chapter 8</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/sol008.htm#001">Song of Solomon (Canticles) 8:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">sol 8:1</a></font></span></p>O (a) that thou [wert] as my brother, that was nourished at the breasts of my mother! [when] I should find thee outside, I would kiss thee; yea, I should not be despised. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(a) The Church called of the Gentiles speaks thus to the Church of Jerusalem.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/sol008.htm#003">Song of Solomon (Canticles) 8:3</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_003">sol 8:3</a></font></span></p>(b) His left hand [should be] under my head, and his right hand should embrace me. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(b) Read (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/sol002.htm#006">Sol 2:6</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/sol008.htm#004">Song of Solomon (Canticles) 8:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">sol 8:4</a></font></span></p>(c) I charge you, O daughters of Jerusalem, that ye stir not, nor awake [my] love, until he please. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(c) Read (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/sol003.htm#005">Sol 3:5</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/sol008.htm#006">Song of Solomon (Canticles) 8:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">sol 8:6</a></font></span></p>(d) Set me as a seal upon thy heart, as a seal upon thy arm: for love [is] strong as death; jealousy [is] cruel as the grave: the coals of it [are] coals of fire, [which hath a] most vehement flame. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(d) The spouse desires Christ to be joined in perpetual love with him.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/sol008.htm#008">Song of Solomon (Canticles) 8:8</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_008">sol 8:8</a></font></span></p>(e) We have a little sister, and she hath no breasts: what shall we do for our sister in the day when she shall be spoken for? 
 <p>
 </p><p>(e) The Jewish Church speaks this of the Church of the Gentiles.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/sol008.htm#009">Song of Solomon (Canticles) 8:9</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_009">sol 8:9</a></font></span></p>(f) If she [is] a wall, we will build upon her a palace of silver: and if she [is] a door, we will inclose her with boards of cedar. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(f) If she is sure and fast, she is fit for her husband to dwell in.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/sol008.htm#010">Song of Solomon (Canticles) 8:10</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_010">sol 8:10</a></font></span></p>(g) I [am] a wall, and my breasts like towers: then was I in his eyes as one that found favour. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(g) The Church promises fidelity and constancy.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/sol008.htm#011">Song of Solomon (Canticles) 8:11</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_011">sol 8:11</a></font></span></p>(h) Solomon had a vineyard at Baalhamon; he let out the vineyard to keepers; every one for the fruit of it was to bring a thousand [pieces] of silver. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(h) This is the vineyard of the Lord hired out, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat021.htm#033">Mat 21:33</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/sol008.htm#013">Song of Solomon (Canticles) 8:13</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_013">sol 8:13</a></font></span></p>Thou that dwellest in the (i) gardens, the companions hearken to thy voice: cause me to hear [it]. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(i) Christ dwells in his Church, whose voice the faithful hear.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/sol008.htm#014">Song of Solomon (Canticles) 8:14</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_014">sol 8:14</a></font></span></p>(k) Make haste, my beloved, and be thou like a roe or a young hart upon the mountains of spices. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(k) The Church desires Christ that if he depart from them, yet he would haste to help them in their troubles.  
 </p><p></p>
