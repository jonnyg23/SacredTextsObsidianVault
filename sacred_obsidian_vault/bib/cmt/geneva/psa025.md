 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Geneva Bible Translation Notes</i>, [1599], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa025.htm#001">Psalms Chapter 25</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa025.htm#001">Psalms 25:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">psa 25:1</a></font></span></p>"[A Psalm] of David." Unto thee, (a) O LORD, do I lift up my soul. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(a) I did not put my trust in any worldly thing.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa025.htm#002">Psalms 25:2</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_002">psa 25:2</a></font></span></p>O my God, I (b) trust in thee: let me not be ashamed, let not mine enemies triumph over me. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(b) That you will take away my enemies, which are your rods.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa025.htm#004">Psalms 25:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">psa 25:4</a></font></span></p>(c) Shew me thy ways, O LORD; teach me thy paths. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(c) Retain me in the faith of your promise that I swore not on any side.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa025.htm#005">Psalms 25:5</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_005">psa 25:5</a></font></span></p>Lead me in thy truth, and teach me: for thou [art] the God of my salvation; on thee do I wait (d) all the day. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(d) Constantly and against all temptations.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa025.htm#007">Psalms 25:7</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_007">psa 25:7</a></font></span></p>Remember not the (e) sins of my youth, nor my transgressions: according to thy mercy remember thou me for thy goodness' sake, O LORD. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(e) He confesses that his many sins were the reason that his enemies persecuted him, desiring that the cause of the evil may be taken away, so that the effect may cease.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa025.htm#008">Psalms 25:8</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_008">psa 25:8</a></font></span></p>Good and upright [is] the LORD: therefore will he (f) teach sinners in the way. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(f) That is, call them to repentance.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa025.htm#009">Psalms 25:9</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_009">psa 25:9</a></font></span></p>The meek will he (g) guide in judgment: and the meek will he teach his way. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(g) He will govern and comfort them that are truly humbled for their sins.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa025.htm#011">Psalms 25:11</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_011">psa 25:11</a></font></span></p>For thy (h) name's sake, O LORD, pardon mine iniquity; for it [is] great. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(h) For no other respect.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa025.htm#012">Psalms 25:12</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_012">psa 25:12</a></font></span></p>What (i) man [is] he that feareth the LORD? him shall he teach in the way [that] he shall (k) choose. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(i) Meaning, the number is very small. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(k) He will direct such with his spirit to follow the right way.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa025.htm#013">Psalms 25:13</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_013">psa 25:13</a></font></span></p>His soul shall dwell at (l) ease; and his seed shall inherit the earth. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(l) He will prosper both in spiritual and corporal things.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa025.htm#014">Psalms 25:14</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_014">psa 25:14</a></font></span></p>The (m) secret of the LORD [is] with them that fear him; and he will shew them his covenant. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(m) His counsel contained in his word, by which he declares that he is the protector of the faithful.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa025.htm#017">Psalms 25:17</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_017">psa 25:17</a></font></span></p>The troubles of my heart (n) are enlarged: [O] bring thou me out of my distresses. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(n) My grief is increased because of my enemies cruelty.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa025.htm#019">Psalms 25:19</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_019">psa 25:19</a></font></span></p>Consider mine (o) enemies; for they are many; and they hate me with cruel hatred. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(o) The greater that his afflictions were and the more that his enemies increased, the more near he felt God's help.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa025.htm#021">Psalms 25:21</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_021">psa 25:21</a></font></span></p>Let (p) integrity and uprightness preserve me; for I wait on thee. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(p) As I have behaved myself uprightly toward my enemies, let them know that you are the defender of my just cause.
