 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Geneva Bible Translation Notes</i>, [1599], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa054.htm#001">Psalms Chapter 54</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa054.htm#001">Psalms 54:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">psa 54:1</a></font></span></p>"To the chief Musician on Neginoth, Maschil, [A Psalm] of David, when the Ziphims came and said to Saul, Doth not David hide himself with us?" Save me, O God, (a) by thy name, and judge me by thy strength. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(a) He declares that when all means fail, God will deliver even by miracle, they who call to him with an upright conscience.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa054.htm#003">Psalms 54:3</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_003">psa 54:3</a></font></span></p>For (b) strangers are risen up against me, and (c) oppressors seek after my soul: they have not set God before them. Selah. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(b) That is, the Ziphims. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(c) Saul and his army who were like cruel beasts, could not be satisfied except by his death.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa054.htm#004">Psalms 54:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">psa 54:4</a></font></span></p>Behold, God [is] mine helper: the Lord [is] with (d) them that uphold my soul. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(d) No matter how few, as he was with Jonathan.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa054.htm#005">Psalms 54:5</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_005">psa 54:5</a></font></span></p>He shall reward evil unto mine enemies: cut them off in thy (e) truth. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(e) According to your faithful promise for my defence.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa054.htm#006">Psalms 54:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">psa 54:6</a></font></span></p>I will (f) freely sacrifice unto thee: I will praise thy name, O LORD; for [it is] good. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(f) For hypocrites serve God out of fear or on conditions.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa054.htm#007">Psalms 54:7</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_007">psa 54:7</a></font></span></p>For he hath delivered me out of all trouble: and mine eye hath (g) seen [his desire] upon mine enemies. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(g) We may lawfully rejoice for God's judgments against the wicked, if our affections are pure.
