 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Geneva Bible Translation Notes</i>, [1599], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze010.htm#001">Ezekiel Chapter 10</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze010.htm#001">Ezekiel 10:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">eze 10:1</a></font></span></p>Then I looked, and, behold, in the firmament that was above the head of the (a) cherubim there appeared over them as it were a sapphire stone, as the appearance of the likeness of a throne. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(a) Which in (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze001.htm#005">Eze 1:5</a>) he called the four beasts.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze010.htm#002">Ezekiel 10:2</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_002">eze 10:2</a></font></span></p>And he spoke to the man clothed with linen, and said, Go in between the wheels, [even] under the cherub, and fill thy hand with coals of fire from between the cherubim, and scatter [them] over (b) the city. And he entered in my sight. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(b) This signified that the city would be burnt.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze010.htm#004">Ezekiel 10:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">eze 10:4</a></font></span></p>Then the glory of the LORD (c) went up from the cherub, [and stood] over the threshold of the house; and the house was filled with the cloud, and the court was full of the brightness of the LORD'S glory. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(c) Meaning that the glory of God would depart from the temple.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze010.htm#005">Ezekiel 10:5</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_005">eze 10:5</a></font></span></p>And the (d) sound of the cherubim's wings was heard [even] to the outer court, as the voice of the Almighty God when he speaketh. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(d) Read (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze001.htm#024">Eze 1:24</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze010.htm#009">Ezekiel 10:9</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_009">eze 10:9</a></font></span></p>And when I looked, behold the four wheels by the cherubim, one wheel by one cherub, and another wheel by another cherub: and the appearance of the wheels [was] as the colour of a (e) beryl stone. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(e) Read (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze001.htm#016">Eze 1:16</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze010.htm#011">Ezekiel 10:11</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_011">eze 10:11</a></font></span></p>When they went, they went upon their four sides; they turned not as they went, but to the place where the head looked they followed it; they (f) turned not as they went. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(f) Until they had executed God's judgments.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze010.htm#017">Ezekiel 10:17</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_017">eze 10:17</a></font></span></p>When they stood, [these] stood; and when they were lifted up, [these] lifted up themselves [also]: for the (g) spirit of the living being [was] in them. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(g) There was one consent between the cherubims and the wheels.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze010.htm#018">Ezekiel 10:18</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_018">eze 10:18</a></font></span></p>(h) Then the glory of the LORD departed from off the threshold of the house, and stood over the cherubim. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(h) Read (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze009.htm#003">Eze 9:3</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze010.htm#020">Ezekiel 10:20</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_020">eze 10:20</a></font></span></p>This [is] the (i) living being that I saw under the God of Israel by the river of Chebar; and I knew that they [were] the cherubim. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(i) That is, the whole body of the four beasts or cherubims.
