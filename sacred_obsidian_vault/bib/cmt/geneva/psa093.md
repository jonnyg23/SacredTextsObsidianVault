 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Geneva Bible Translation Notes</i>, [1599], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa093.htm#001">Psalms Chapter 93</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa093.htm#001">Psalms 93:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">psa 93:1</a></font></span></p>The LORD (a) reigneth, he is clothed with majesty; the LORD is clothed with strength, [wherewith] he hath girded himself: the world also is stablished, that it cannot be moved. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(a) As God by his power and wisdom has made and governed the world, so must the same be our defence against all enemies and dangers.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa093.htm#002">Psalms 93:2</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_002">psa 93:2</a></font></span></p>Thy (b) throne [is] established of old: thou [art] from everlasting. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(b) In which you sit and govern the world.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa093.htm#003">Psalms 93:3</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_003">psa 93:3</a></font></span></p>(c) The floods have lifted up, O LORD, the floods have lifted up their voice; the floods lift up their waves. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(c) God's power appears in ruling the furious waters.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa093.htm#005">Psalms 93:5</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_005">psa 93:5</a></font></span></p>Thy (d) testimonies are very sure: holiness becometh thine house, O LORD, for ever. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(d) Besides God's power and wisdom in creating and governing his great mercy also appears in that he has given his people his word and covenant.
