 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Geneva Bible Translation Notes</i>, [1599], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo027.htm#001">Exodus Chapter 27</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo027.htm#001">Exodus 27:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">exo 27:1</a></font></span></p>And thou shalt make an (a) altar [of] shittim wood, five cubits long, and five cubits broad; the altar shall be foursquare: and the height thereof [shall be] three cubits. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(a) For the burnt offering.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo027.htm#002">Exodus 27:2</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_002">exo 27:2</a></font></span></p>And thou shalt make the horns of it upon the four corners thereof: his horns shall be of (b) the same: and thou shalt overlay it with brass. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(b) Of the same wood and matter not fastened to it.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo027.htm#009">Exodus 27:9</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_009">exo 27:9</a></font></span></p>And thou shalt make the (c) court of the tabernacle: for the south side southward [there shall be] hangings for the court [of] fine twined linen of an hundred cubits long for one side: 
 <p>
 </p><p>(c) This was the first entry into the tabernacle, where the people abode.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo027.htm#010">Exodus 27:10</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_010">exo 27:10</a></font></span></p>And the twenty pillars thereof and their twenty sockets [shall be of] brass; the hooks of the pillars and their (d) fillets [shall be of] silver. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(d) They were certain hoops or circles to beautify the pillar.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo027.htm#013">Exodus 27:13</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_013">exo 27:13</a></font></span></p>And the breadth of the court on the east side eastward [shall be] (e) fifty cubits. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(e) Meaning curtains of fifty cubits.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo027.htm#014">Exodus 27:14</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_014">exo 27:14</a></font></span></p>The hangings of one (f) side [of the gate shall be] fifteen cubits: their pillars three, and their sockets three. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(f) Of the door of the court.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo027.htm#019">Exodus 27:19</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_019">exo 27:19</a></font></span></p>All the vessels of the tabernacle in all the service thereof, and all the (g) pins thereof, and all the pins of the court, [shall be of] brass. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(g) Or stakes, with which the curtains were fastened to the ground.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo027.htm#020">Exodus 27:20</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_020">exo 27:20</a></font></span></p>And thou shalt command the children of Israel, that they bring thee pure oil olive (h) beaten for the light, to cause the lamp to burn always. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(h) Such as comes from the olive, when it is first pressed or beaten.
