 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Geneva Bible Translation Notes</i>, [1599], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo030.htm#001">Exodus Chapter 30</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo030.htm#001">Exodus 30:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">exo 30:1</a></font></span></p>And thou shalt make an altar (a) to burn incense upon: [of] shittim wood shalt thou make it. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(a) Upon which the sweet perfume was burnt, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo030.htm#034">Exo 30:34</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo030.htm#002">Exodus 30:2</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_002">exo 30:2</a></font></span></p>A cubit [shall be] the length thereof, and a cubit the breadth thereof; foursquare shall it be: and two cubits [shall be] the height thereof: the horns thereof [shall be] (b) of the same. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(b) Of the same wood and matter.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo030.htm#006">Exodus 30:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">exo 30:6</a></font></span></p>And thou shalt put it (c) before the vail that [is] by the ark of the testimony, before the mercy seat that [is] over the testimony, where I will meet with thee. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(c) That is, in the sanctuary, and not in the holiest of all.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo030.htm#007">Exodus 30:7</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_007">exo 30:7</a></font></span></p>And Aaron shall burn thereon sweet incense every morning: when he (d) dresseth the lamps, he shall burn incense upon it. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(d) Meaning, when he trims them, and refreshes the oil.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo030.htm#009">Exodus 30:9</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_009">exo 30:9</a></font></span></p>Ye shall offer no (e) strange incense thereon, nor burnt sacrifice, nor meat offering; neither shall ye pour drink offering (f) thereon. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(e) Otherwise made them this, which is described. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(f) But it must only serve to burn perfume.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo030.htm#012">Exodus 30:12</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_012">exo 30:12</a></font></span></p>When thou takest the sum of the children of Israel after their number, then shall they give every man (g) a ransom for his soul unto the LORD, when thou numberest them; that there be no plague among them, when [thou] numberest them. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(g) By which he testified that he redeemed his life which he had forfeit, as is declared by David, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa2024.htm#001">Sa2 24:1</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo030.htm#013">Exodus 30:13</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_013">exo 30:13</a></font></span></p>This they shall give, every one that passeth among them that are numbered, half a shekel after the (h) shekel of the sanctuary: (a shekel [is] twenty gerahs:) an half shekel [shall be] the offering of the LORD. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(h) This shekel was worth two common shekels: and the gerah about 12 pence at a rate of five shillings sterling to an ounce of silver.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo030.htm#015">Exodus 30:15</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_015">exo 30:15</a></font></span></p>The rich shall not give more, and the poor shall not give less than half a shekel, when [they] give an offering unto the LORD, (i) to make an atonement for your souls. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(i) That God should be merciful to you.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo030.htm#019">Exodus 30:19</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_019">exo 30:19</a></font></span></p>For Aaron and his sons shall (k) wash their hands and their feet thereat: 
 <p>
 </p><p>(k) Signifying that he that comes to God must be washed from all sin and corruption.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo030.htm#021">Exodus 30:21</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_021">exo 30:21</a></font></span></p>So they shall wash their hands and their feet, that they die not: and it shall be a statute (l) for ever to them, [even] to him and to his seed throughout their generations. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(l) So long as the priesthood shall last.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo030.htm#023">Exodus 30:23</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_023">exo 30:23</a></font></span></p>Take thou also unto thee principal spices, of pure myrrh five hundred (m) [shekels], and of sweet cinnamon half so much, [even] two hundred and fifty [shekels], and of sweet (n) calamus two hundred and fifty [shekels], 
 <p>
 </p><p>(m) Weighing so much. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(n) It is a type of reed with a very sweet savour within, and it is used in powders and odours.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo030.htm#026">Exodus 30:26</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_026">exo 30:26</a></font></span></p>And thou shalt anoint the (o) tabernacle of the congregation therewith, and the ark of the testimony, 
 <p>
 </p><p>(o) All things which belong to the tabernacle.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo030.htm#032">Exodus 30:32</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_032">exo 30:32</a></font></span></p>Upon (p) man's flesh shall it not be poured, neither shall ye make [any other] like it, after the composition of it: it [is] holy, [and] it shall be holy unto you. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(p) Neither at their burials nor otherwise.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo030.htm#033">Exodus 30:33</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_033">exo 30:33</a></font></span></p>Whosoever compoundeth [any] like it, or whosoever putteth [any] of it upon (q) a stranger, shall even be cut off from his people. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(q) Either a stranger or an Israelite, save only the priests.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo030.htm#034">Exodus 30:34</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_034">exo 30:34</a></font></span></p>And the LORD said unto Moses, Take unto thee sweet spices, stacte, and (r) onycha, and galbanum; [these] sweet spices with pure frankincense: of each shall there be a like [weight]: 
 <p>
 </p><p>(r) In Hebrew, Sheheleth: which is a sweet kind of gum and shines as the nail.
