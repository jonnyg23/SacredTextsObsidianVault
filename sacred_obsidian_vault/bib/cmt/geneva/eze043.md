 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Geneva Bible Translation Notes</i>, [1599], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze043.htm#001">Ezekiel Chapter 43</a></h3> And [it was] according to the appearance of the vision which I saw, [even] according to the vision that I saw (a) when I came to destroy the city: and the visions [were] like the vision that I saw by the river Chebar; and I fell upon my face. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(a) When I prophesied the destruction of the city of the Chaldeans.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze043.htm#004">Ezekiel 43:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">eze 43:4</a></font></span></p>And the (b) glory of the LORD came into the house by the way of the gate whose prospect [is] toward the east. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(b) Which was departed before, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze010.htm#004">Eze 10:4</a>, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze012.htm#022">Eze 12:22</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze043.htm#007">Ezekiel 43:7</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_007">eze 43:7</a></font></span></p>And he said to me, Son of man, the place of my throne, and the place of the soles of my feet, where I will dwell in the midst of the children of Israel for ever, and my holy name, shall the house of Israel no more (c) defile, [neither] they, nor their kings, by their harlotry, nor by the carcases of (d) their kings in their high places. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(c) By their idolatries. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(d) He alludes to Amon and Manasseh, who were buried in their gardens near the Temple and there had erected monuments to their idols.
