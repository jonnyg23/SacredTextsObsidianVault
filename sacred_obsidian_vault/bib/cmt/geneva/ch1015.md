 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Geneva Bible Translation Notes</i>, [1599], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1015.htm#001">1 Chronicles Chapter 15</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1015.htm#001">1 Chronicles 15:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">ch1 15:1</a></font></span></p>And [David] made him houses in the (a) city of David, and prepared a place for the ark of God, and pitched for it a tent. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(a) That was in Zion (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa2005.htm#007">Sa2 5:7,9</a>). 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1015.htm#003">1 Chronicles 15:3</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_003">ch1 15:3</a></font></span></p>And David gathered all Israel together to Jerusalem, to bring up the (b) ark of the LORD unto his place, which he had prepared for it. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(b) From the house of Obed Edom, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa2006.htm#010">Sa2 6:10</a>, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa2006.htm#012">Sa2 6:12</a>). 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1015.htm#008">1 Chronicles 15:8</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_008">ch1 15:8</a></font></span></p>Of the sons of (c) Elizaphan; Shemaiah the chief, and his brethren two hundred: 
 <p>
 </p><p>(c) Who was the son of Uzziel, the fourth son of Kohath, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo006.htm#018">Exo 6:18</a>, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo006.htm#022">Exo 6:22</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/num003.htm#030">Num 3:30</a>). 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1015.htm#009">1 Chronicles 15:9</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_009">ch1 15:9</a></font></span></p>Of the sons of (d) Hebron; Eliel the chief, and his brethren fourscore: 
 <p>
 </p><p>(d) The third son of Kohath, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo006.htm#018">Exo 6:18</a>). 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1015.htm#012">1 Chronicles 15:12</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_012">ch1 15:12</a></font></span></p>And said unto them, Ye [are] the chief of the fathers of the Levites: (e) sanctify yourselves, [both] ye and your brethren, that ye may bring up the ark of the LORD God of Israel unto [the place that] I have prepared for it. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(e) Prepare yourselves and be pure, abstain from all things by which you might be polluted, and so not able to come to the tabernacle. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1015.htm#013">1 Chronicles 15:13</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_013">ch1 15:13</a></font></span></p>For because ye [did it] not at the first, the LORD our God made a breach upon us, for that we sought him not after the due (f) order. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(f) According as he has appointed in the law. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1015.htm#016">1 Chronicles 15:16</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_016">ch1 15:16</a></font></span></p>And David spake to the chief of the Levites to appoint their brethren [to be] the singers with (g) instruments of musick, psalteries and harps and cymbals, sounding, by lifting up the voice with joy. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(g) These instruments and other ceremonies which they observed, were instructions of their infancy, which continued to the coming of Christ. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1015.htm#018">1 Chronicles 15:18</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_018">ch1 15:18</a></font></span></p>And with them their brethren of the (h) second [degree], Zechariah, Ben, and Jaaziel, and Shemiramoth, and Jehiel, and Unni, Eliab, and Benaiah, and Maaseiah, and Mattithiah, and Elipheleh, and Mikneiah, and Obededom, and Jeiel, the porters. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(h) Which were inferior in dignity. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1015.htm#020">1 Chronicles 15:20</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_020">ch1 15:20</a></font></span></p>And Zechariah, and Aziel, and Shemiramoth, and Jehiel, and Unni, and Eliab, and Maaseiah, and Benaiah, with psalteries on (i) Alamoth; 
 <p>
 </p><p>(i) This was an instrument of music or a certain tune, to which they sang psalms. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1015.htm#021">1 Chronicles 15:21</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_021">ch1 15:21</a></font></span></p>And Mattithiah, and Elipheleh, and Mikneiah, and Obededom, and Jeiel, and Azaziah, with harps on the (k) Sheminith to excel. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(k) Which was the eighth tune, over which he that was most excellent had charge. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1015.htm#022">1 Chronicles 15:22</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_022">ch1 15:22</a></font></span></p>And Chenaniah, chief of the Levites, (l) [was] for song: he instructed about the song, because he [was] skilful. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(l) That is, to appoint Psalms and songs to them that sang. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1015.htm#024">1 Chronicles 15:24</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_024">ch1 15:24</a></font></span></p>And Shebaniah, and Jehoshaphat, and Nethaneel, and Amasai, and Zechariah, and Benaiah, and Eliezer, the priests, did blow with the trumpets before the ark of God: and Obededom and Jehiah [were] doorkeepers (m) for the ark. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(m) With Berechiah and Elkanah, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1015.htm#023">Ch1 15:23</a>). 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1015.htm#026">1 Chronicles 15:26</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_026">ch1 15:26</a></font></span></p>And it came to pass, when God (n) helped the Levites that bare the ark of the covenant of the LORD, that they offered (o) seven bullocks and seven rams. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(n) That is, gave them strength to execute their office. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(o) Besides the bullock and the fat beast which David offered at every sixth pace, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa2006.htm#013">Sa2 6:13</a>). 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1015.htm#027">1 Chronicles 15:27</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_027">ch1 15:27</a></font></span></p>And David [was] clothed with a robe of fine linen, and all the Levites that bare the ark, and the singers, and Chenaniah the master of the song with the singers: David also [had] upon him an (p) ephod of linen. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(p) Read (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa2006.htm#014">Sa2 6:14</a>). 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1015.htm#029">1 Chronicles 15:29</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_029">ch1 15:29</a></font></span></p>And it came to pass, [as] the ark of the (q) covenant of the LORD came to the city of David, that Michal the daughter of Saul looking out at a window saw king David dancing and playing: and she despised him in her heart. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(q) It was so called because it reminded the Israelites of the Lord's covenant made with them.
