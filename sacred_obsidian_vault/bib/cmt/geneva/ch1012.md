 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Geneva Bible Translation Notes</i>, [1599], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1012.htm#001">1 Chronicles Chapter 12</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1012.htm#001">1 Chronicles 12:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">ch1 12:1</a></font></span></p>Now these [are] they that came to David to (a) Ziklag, while he yet kept himself close because of Saul the son of Kish: and they [were] among the mighty men, helpers of the war. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(a) To take his part against Saul: who persecuted him. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1012.htm#002">1 Chronicles 12:2</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_002">ch1 12:2</a></font></span></p>[They were] armed with bows, and could use both the right hand and the left in [hurling] stones and [shooting] arrows out of a bow, [even] of Saul's (b) brethren of Benjamin. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(b) That is, of the tribe of Benjamin, of which Saul was, and in which were excellent throwers with slings, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/jdg020.htm#016">Jdg 20:16</a>). 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1012.htm#008">1 Chronicles 12:8</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_008">ch1 12:8</a></font></span></p>And of the Gadites there separated themselves unto David into the hold to the wilderness men of might, [and] men of war [fit] for the battle, that could handle shield and buckler, whose faces [were like] the faces of (c) lions, and [were] as swift as the roes upon the mountains; 
 <p>
 </p><p>(c) Meaning, fierce and terrible. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1012.htm#015">1 Chronicles 12:15</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_015">ch1 12:15</a></font></span></p>These [are] they that went over Jordan in the (d) first month, when it had overflown all his banks; and they put to flight all [them] of the valleys, [both] toward the east, and toward the west. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(d) Which the Hebrews called Nisan, or Abib, containing half March and half April, when Jordan was wont to overflow its banks, read (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/jos003.htm#015">Jos 3:15</a>). 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1012.htm#018">1 Chronicles 12:18</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_018">ch1 12:18</a></font></span></p>Then the (e) spirit came upon Amasai, [who was] chief of the captains, [and he said], Thine [are we], David, and on thy side, thou son of Jesse: peace, peace [be] unto thee, and peace [be] to thine helpers; for thy God helpeth thee. Then David received them, and made them captains of the band. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(e) The spirit of boldness and courage moved him to speak thus. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1012.htm#019">1 Chronicles 12:19</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_019">ch1 12:19</a></font></span></p>And there fell [some] of Manasseh to David, when he came with the Philistines against Saul to battle: but they (f) helped them not: for the lords of the Philistines upon advisement sent him away, saying, He will fall to his master Saul to [the jeopardy of] our heads. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(f) They came only to help David, and not to help the Philistines, who were enemies of their country. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1012.htm#021">1 Chronicles 12:21</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_021">ch1 12:21</a></font></span></p>And they helped David against the (g) band [of the rovers]: for they [were] all mighty men of valour, and were captains in the host. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(g) That is, of the Amalekites who had burned the city of Ziklag, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa1030.htm#001">Sa1 30:1</a>, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa1030.htm#009">Sa1 30:9</a>). 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1012.htm#022">1 Chronicles 12:22</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_022">ch1 12:22</a></font></span></p>For at [that] time day by day there came to David to help him, until [it was] a great host, like the host of (h) God. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(h) Meaning, mighty or strong, for the Hebrews say a thing is of God when it is excellent. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1012.htm#027">1 Chronicles 12:27</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_027">ch1 12:27</a></font></span></p>And Jehoiada [was] the leader of the (i) Aaronites, and with him [were] three thousand and seven hundred; 
 <p>
 </p><p>(i) Of the Levites who came by the descent of Aaron. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1012.htm#029">1 Chronicles 12:29</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_029">ch1 12:29</a></font></span></p>And of the children of Benjamin, the kindred of Saul, three thousand: for hitherto the greatest part of them had (k) kept the ward of the house of Saul. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(k) That is, the greatest number took Saul's side. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1012.htm#032">1 Chronicles 12:32</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_032">ch1 12:32</a></font></span></p>And of the children of Issachar, [which were men] that had understanding of the (l) times, to know what Israel ought to do; the heads of them [were] two hundred; and all their brethren [were] at their commandment. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(l) Men of good experience, who knew at all times what was to be done. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1012.htm#038">1 Chronicles 12:38</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_038">ch1 12:38</a></font></span></p>(m) All these men of war, that could keep rank, came with a perfect heart to Hebron, to make David king over all Israel: and all the rest also of Israel [were] of one heart to make David king. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(m) So that his whole host were 322,222. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1012.htm#039">1 Chronicles 12:39</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_039">ch1 12:39</a></font></span></p>And there they were with David three days, eating and drinking: for their (n) brethren had prepared for them. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(n) The rest of the Israelites.
