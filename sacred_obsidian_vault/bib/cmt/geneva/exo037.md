 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Geneva Bible Translation Notes</i>, [1599], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo037.htm#001">Exodus Chapter 37</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo037.htm#002">Exodus 37:2</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_002">exo 37:2</a></font></span></p>And he overlaid it with pure gold within and without, and made a (a) crown of gold to it round about. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(a) Like battlements.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo037.htm#008">Exodus 37:8</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_008">exo 37:8</a></font></span></p>One cherub on the end on this side, and another cherub on the [other] end on that side: out (b) of the mercy seat made he the cherubims on the two ends thereof. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(b) Of the same material that the mercyseat was.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo037.htm#024">Exodus 37:24</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_024">exo 37:24</a></font></span></p>[Of] a (c) talent of pure gold made he it, and all the vessels thereof. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(c) See (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo025.htm#039">Exo 25:39</a>).
