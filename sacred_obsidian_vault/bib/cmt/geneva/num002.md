 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Geneva Bible Translation Notes</i>, [1599], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num002.htm#001">Numbers Chapter 2</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num002.htm#002">Numbers 2:2</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_002">num 2:2</a></font></span></p>(a) Every man of the children of Israel shall pitch by his own standard, with the ensign of their father's house: far off about the tabernacle of the congregation shall they pitch. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(a) In the twelve tribes were four principle standards, so that every three tribes had their standard.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num002.htm#005">Numbers 2:5</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_005">num 2:5</a></font></span></p>And those that do pitch next unto him [shall be] the tribe (b) of Issachar: and Nethaneel the son of Zuar [shall be] captain of the children of Issachar. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(b) Judah, Issachar and Zebulun the sons of Leah were of the first standard.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num002.htm#009">Numbers 2:9</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_009">num 2:9</a></font></span></p>All that were numbered in the (c) camp of Judah [were] an hundred thousand and fourscore thousand and six thousand and four hundred, throughout their armies. These shall first set forth. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(c) Of those who were contained under that name.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num002.htm#010">Numbers 2:10</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_010">num 2:10</a></font></span></p>On the south side [shall be] the standard of the camp (d) of Reuben according to their armies: and the captain of the children of Reuben [shall be] Elizur the son of Shedeur. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(d) Reuben and Simeon, the sons of Leah, and Gad, the son of Zilpah her maid, were of the second standard.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num002.htm#017">Numbers 2:17</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_017">num 2:17</a></font></span></p>Then the tabernacle of the congregation shall set forward with the camp of the Levites in the (e) midst of the camp: as they encamp, so shall they set forward, every man in his place by their standards. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(e) Because it might be an equal distance from each one, and all indifferently have recourse to it.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num002.htm#018">Numbers 2:18</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_018">num 2:18</a></font></span></p>(f) On the west side [shall be] the standard of the camp of Ephraim according to their armies: and the captain of the sons of Ephraim [shall be] Elishama the son of Ammihud. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(f) Because Ephraim and Manasseh took the place of Joseph their father, they are taken as Rachel's children, so they and Benjamin make the third standard.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num002.htm#025">Numbers 2:25</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_025">num 2:25</a></font></span></p>The standard of the camp of (g) Dan [shall be] on the north side by their armies: and the captain of the children of Dan [shall be] Ahiezer the son of Ammishaddai. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(g) Dan and Naphtali the sons of Bilhah Rachel's maid with Asher the son of Zilpah make the fourth standard.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num002.htm#032">Numbers 2:32</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_032">num 2:32</a></font></span></p>These [are] (h) those which were numbered of the children of Israel by the house of their fathers: all those that were numbered of the camps throughout their hosts [were] six hundred thousand and three thousand and five hundred and fifty. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(h) Which were of twenty years and above.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num002.htm#034">Numbers 2:34</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_034">num 2:34</a></font></span></p>And the children of Israel did according to all that the LORD commanded Moses: so they pitched by their (i) standards, and so they set forward, every one after their families, according to the house of their fathers. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(i) For under every one of the four principal standards, were various signs to keep every band.
