 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Geneva Bible Translation Notes</i>, [1599], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro013.htm#001">Proverbs Chapter 13</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro013.htm#002">Proverbs 13:2</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_002">pro 13:2</a></font></span></p>A man shall eat good by the fruit (a) of [his] mouth: but the soul of the transgressors [shall eat] violence. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(a) If he uses his tongue to God's glory, and the profit of his neighbour, God will bless him.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro013.htm#004">Proverbs 13:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">pro 13:4</a></font></span></p>The soul of the sluggard (b) desireth, and [hath] nothing: but the soul of the diligent shall be made fat. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(b) He always desires, but takes no pains to get anything.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro013.htm#008">Proverbs 13:8</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_008">pro 13:8</a></font></span></p>The ransom of a man's life [is] his riches: but the poor (c) heareth not rebuke. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(c) For his poverty, he is not able to escape the threatenings, which the cruel oppressors use against him.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro013.htm#010">Proverbs 13:10</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_010">pro 13:10</a></font></span></p>Only by pride (d) cometh contention: but with the well advised [is] wisdom. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(d) When as every man contends to have preeminence, and will not give place to another.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro013.htm#011">Proverbs 13:11</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_011">pro 13:11</a></font></span></p>(e) Wealth [gotten] by vanity shall be diminished: but he that gathereth (f) by labour shall increase. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(e) That is, goods evil gotten. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(f) That is, with his own labour.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro013.htm#013">Proverbs 13:13</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_013">pro 13:13</a></font></span></p>Whoever despiseth (g) the word shall be destroyed: but he that feareth the commandment shall be rewarded. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(g) Meaning the word of God, by which he is admonished of his duty.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro013.htm#017">Proverbs 13:17</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_017">pro 13:17</a></font></span></p>A wicked messenger falleth (h) into mischief: but a faithful ambassador [is] health. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(h) Brings many inconveniences both to himself and to others.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro013.htm#020">Proverbs 13:20</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_020">pro 13:20</a></font></span></p>He that walketh with wise [men] shall be wise: but a companion of fools shall be (i) destroyed. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(i) As he is partaker of their wickedness, and bears with their vices, so will he be punished alike as they are.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro013.htm#022">Proverbs 13:22</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_022">pro 13:22</a></font></span></p>A good [man] leaveth an inheritance to his children's children: and the (k) wealth of the sinner [is] laid up for the just. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(k) Read (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/job027.htm#016">Job 27:16-17</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro013.htm#023">Proverbs 13:23</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_023">pro 13:23</a></font></span></p>Much food [is in] the fallow ground of the (l) poor: but there is [that is] destroyed for lack of judgment. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(l) God blesses the labour of the poor, and consumes their goods who are negligent, because they think they have enough.
