 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Geneva Bible Translation Notes</i>, [1599], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa031.htm#001">Psalms Chapter 31</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa031.htm#001">Psalms 31:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">psa 31:1</a></font></span></p>"To the chief Musician, A Psalm of David." In thee, O LORD, do I put my trust; let me never be ashamed: deliver me in thy (a) righteousness. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(a) For then God declares himself just, when he preserves his as he has promised.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa031.htm#004">Psalms 31:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">psa 31:4</a></font></span></p>Pull me out of the (b) net that they have laid privily for me: for thou [art] my strength. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(b) Preserve me from the crafty counsels and subtle practise of my enemies.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa031.htm#005">Psalms 31:5</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_005">psa 31:5</a></font></span></p>Into thine (c) hand I commit my spirit: thou hast redeemed me, O LORD God of truth. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(c) He desires God not only to take care of him in this life, but that his soul may be saved after this life.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa031.htm#006">Psalms 31:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">psa 31:6</a></font></span></p>I have hated them that regard lying vanities: but I (d) trust in the LORD. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(d) This affection should be in all God's children to hate whatever is not grounded on a sure trust in God, as deceitful and vain.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa031.htm#008">Psalms 31:8</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_008">psa 31:8</a></font></span></p>And hast not shut me up into the hand of the enemy: thou hast set my feet in a (e) large room. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(e) Largeness signifies comfort, as straitness, sorrow and peril.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa031.htm#009">Psalms 31:9</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_009">psa 31:9</a></font></span></p>Have mercy upon me, O LORD, for I am in trouble: mine (f) eye is consumed with grief, [yea], my soul and my belly. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(f) Meaning, that his sorrow and torment had continued a great while.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa031.htm#011">Psalms 31:11</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_011">psa 31:11</a></font></span></p>I was a (g) reproach among all mine enemies, but especially among my neighbours, and a fear to mine acquaintance: they that did see me without fled from me. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(g) My enemies had drawn all men to their part against me, even my chief friends.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa031.htm#013">Psalms 31:13</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_013">psa 31:13</a></font></span></p>For I have heard the slander of (i) many: fear [was] on every side: while they took counsel together against me, they devised to take away my life. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(i) They who were in authority condemned me as a wicked doer.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa031.htm#014">Psalms 31:14</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_014">psa 31:14</a></font></span></p>But I trusted in thee, O LORD: I said, (k) Thou [art] my God. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(k) I had this testimony of conscience, that you would defend my innocence.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa031.htm#015">Psalms 31:15</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_015">psa 31:15</a></font></span></p>My (l) times [are] in thy hand: deliver me from the hand of mine enemies, and from them that persecute me. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(l) Whatever changes come, you govern them by your providence.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa031.htm#017">Psalms 31:17</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_017">psa 31:17</a></font></span></p>Let me not be ashamed, O LORD; for I have called upon thee: let the wicked be ashamed, [and] let them be (m) silent in the grave. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(m) Let death destroy them to the intent that they may hurt no more.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa031.htm#019">Psalms 31:19</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_019">psa 31:19</a></font></span></p>[Oh] how great [is] thy goodness, which thou (n) hast laid up for them that fear thee; [which] thou hast wrought for them that trust in thee before the sons of men! 
 <p>
 </p><p>(n) The treasures of God's mercy are always laid up in store for his children, even at all times they do not enjoy them.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa031.htm#020">Psalms 31:20</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_020">psa 31:20</a></font></span></p>Thou shalt hide them in the (o) secret of thy presence from the pride of man: thou shalt keep them secretly in a pavilion from the strife of tongues. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(o) That is, in a place where they will have your comfort, and be hid safely from the enemies pride.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa031.htm#021">Psalms 31:21</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_021">psa 31:21</a></font></span></p>Blessed [be] the LORD: for he hath shewed me his marvellous kindness in a (p) strong city. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(p) Meaning, there was no city strong enough to preserve him, as the defence of God's favour.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa031.htm#022">Psalms 31:22</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_022">psa 31:22</a></font></span></p>For I said in my (q) haste, I am cut off from before thine eyes: nevertheless thou heardest the voice of my supplications when I cried unto thee. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(q) And so by my rashness and infidelity deserved to have been forsaken.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa031.htm#024">Psalms 31:24</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_024">psa 31:24</a></font></span></p>Be of good courage, and he shall (r) strengthen your heart, all ye that hope in the LORD. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(r) Be constant in your calling, and God will confirm you with heavenly strength.
