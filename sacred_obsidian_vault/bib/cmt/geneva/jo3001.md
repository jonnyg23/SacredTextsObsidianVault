 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Geneva Bible Translation Notes</i>, [1599], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jo3001.htm#001">3 John Chapter 1</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jo3001.htm#001">3 John 1:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">jo3 1:1</a></font></span></p>The (1) elder unto the wellbeloved Gaius, whom I love in the truth. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(1) An example of a Christian greeting. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jo3001.htm#004">3 John 1:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">jo3 1:4</a></font></span></p>I have no greater joy than (a) to hear that my children walk in truth. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(a) Than these joys. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jo3001.htm#005">3 John 1:5</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_005">jo3 1:5</a></font></span></p>Beloved, thou doest (b) faithfully whatsoever thou doest to the brethren, and to strangers; 
 <p>
 </p><p>(b) As becomes a believer and a Christian. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jo3001.htm#006">3 John 1:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">jo3 1:6</a></font></span></p>Which have borne witness of thy charity before the church: whom if thou (c) bring forward on their journey after a godly sort, thou shalt do well: 
 <p>
 </p><p>(c) He commends to Gaius, either those same men whom he had entertained before returning to him, about the affairs of the Church, or else some other who had similar business. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jo3001.htm#008">3 John 1:8</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_008">jo3 1:8</a></font></span></p>We therefore ought to receive such, that we might be (d) fellowhelpers to the truth. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(d) That we ourselves may help the preaching of the truth. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jo3001.htm#009">3 John 1:9</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_009">jo3 1:9</a></font></span></p>(2) I wrote unto the church: but Diotrephes, who loveth to have the preeminence among them, receiveth us not. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(2) Ambition and covetousness, two pestilent sins (especially in those who have any church responsibilities) are condemned in Diotrephes. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jo3001.htm#011">3 John 1:11</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_011">jo3 1:11</a></font></span></p>Beloved, follow not that which is evil, but that which is good. He that doeth good is of God: but he that doeth evil hath not (e) seen God. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(e) Has not known God.  
 </p><p></p>
