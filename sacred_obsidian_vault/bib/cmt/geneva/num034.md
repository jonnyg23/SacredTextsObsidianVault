 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Geneva Bible Translation Notes</i>, [1599], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num034.htm#001">Numbers Chapter 34</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num034.htm#002">Numbers 34:2</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_002">num 34:2</a></font></span></p>Command the children of Israel, and say unto them, When ye come into the land of Canaan; (this [is] the (a) land that shall fall unto you for an inheritance, [even] the land of Canaan with the coasts thereof:) 
 <p>
 </p><p>(a) Meaning, the description of the land.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num034.htm#005">Numbers 34:5</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_005">num 34:5</a></font></span></p>And the border shall fetch a compass from Azmon unto the (b) river of Egypt, and the goings out of it shall be at the sea. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(b) Which was Nilus, or as some think Rhinocotura.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num034.htm#006">Numbers 34:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">num 34:6</a></font></span></p>And [as for] the western border, ye shall even have the (c) great sea for a border: this shall be your west border. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(c) Which is called the Mediterranean.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num034.htm#007">Numbers 34:7</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_007">num 34:7</a></font></span></p>And this shall be your north border: from the great sea ye shall point out for you mount (d) Hor: 
 <p>
 </p><p>(d) Which is a mountain near Tyre and Sidon, and not that Hor in the wilderness where Aaron died.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num034.htm#011">Numbers 34:11</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_011">num 34:11</a></font></span></p>And the coast shall go down from Shepham to Riblah, on the east side of Ain; and the border shall descend, and shall reach unto the side of the sea of (e) Chinnereth eastward: 
 <p>
 </p><p>(e) Which in the Gospel is called the lake of Gennesaret.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num034.htm#018">Numbers 34:18</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_018">num 34:18</a></font></span></p>And ye shall take one (f) prince of every tribe, to divide the land by inheritance. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(f) One of the heads or chief men of every tribe.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num034.htm#029">Numbers 34:29</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_029">num 34:29</a></font></span></p>These [are they] whom the LORD commanded to (g) divide the inheritance unto the children of Israel in the land of Canaan. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(g) And be judges over every piece of ground that should fall to any by lot, to the intent that all things might be done orderly and without contention.
