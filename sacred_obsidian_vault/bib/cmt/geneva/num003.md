 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Geneva Bible Translation Notes</i>, [1599], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num003.htm#001">Numbers Chapter 3</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num003.htm#001">Numbers 3:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">num 3:1</a></font></span></p>These also [are] the (a) generations of Aaron and Moses in the day [that] the LORD spake with Moses in mount Sinai. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(a) Or, families and kindreds.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num003.htm#004">Numbers 3:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">num 3:4</a></font></span></p>And Nadab and Abihu died (b) before the LORD, when they offered strange fire before the LORD, in the wilderness of Sinai, and they had no children: and Eleazar and Ithamar ministered in the priest's office in the (c) sight of Aaron their father. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(b) Or, before the altar. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(c) While their father lived.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num003.htm#006">Numbers 3:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">num 3:6</a></font></span></p>Bring the tribe of Levi near, and (d) present them before Aaron the priest, that they may minister unto him. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(d) Offer them to Aaron for the use of the tabernacle.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num003.htm#007">Numbers 3:7</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_007">num 3:7</a></font></span></p>And they shall keep his charge, and the charge of the whole congregation (e) before the tabernacle of the congregation, to do the service of the tabernacle. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(e) Which belonged to the executing of the high priests commandment, to the oversight of the people, and the service of the tabernacle.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num003.htm#009">Numbers 3:9</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_009">num 3:9</a></font></span></p>And thou shalt give the Levites unto Aaron and to his (f) sons: they [are] wholly given unto him out of the children of Israel. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(f) Aaron's sons the priests served in the sanctuary in praying for the people and offering sacrifice: the Levites served for the inferior uses of the same.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num003.htm#010">Numbers 3:10</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_010">num 3:10</a></font></span></p>And thou shalt appoint Aaron and his sons, and they shall wait on their priest's office: and the (g) stranger that cometh nigh shall be put to death. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(g) Any that would minister not being a Levite.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num003.htm#022">Numbers 3:22</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_022">num 3:22</a></font></span></p>Those that were numbered of them, (h) according to the number of all the males, from a month old and upward, [even] those that were numbered of them [were] seven thousand and five hundred. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(h) Only numbering the male children.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num003.htm#025">Numbers 3:25</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_025">num 3:25</a></font></span></p>And the charge of the sons of Gershon in the tabernacle of the congregation [shall be] the (i) tabernacle, and the tent, the covering thereof, and the hanging for the door of the tabernacle of the congregation, 
 <p>
 </p><p>(i) Their charge was to carry the covering and hangings of the tabernacle.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num003.htm#028">Numbers 3:28</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_028">num 3:28</a></font></span></p>In the number of all the males, from a month old and upward, [were] eight thousand and six hundred, keeping the (k) charge of the sanctuary. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(k) Everyone doing his duty in the sanctuary.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num003.htm#031">Numbers 3:31</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_031">num 3:31</a></font></span></p>And their charge [shall be] the (l) ark, and the table, and the candlestick, and the altars, and the vessels of the sanctuary wherewith they minister, and the hanging, and all the service thereof. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(l) The main things within the sanctuary were committed to the Kohathites.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num003.htm#036">Numbers 3:36</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_036">num 3:36</a></font></span></p>And [under] the custody and charge of the sons of Merari [shall be] (m) the boards of the tabernacle, and the bars thereof, and the pillars thereof, and the sockets thereof, and all the vessels thereof, and all that serveth thereto, 
 <p>
 </p><p>(m) The wood work and the rest of the instruments were committed to their charge.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num003.htm#038">Numbers 3:38</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_038">num 3:38</a></font></span></p>But those that encamp before the tabernacle toward the east, [even] before the tabernacle of the congregation eastward, [shall be] Moses, and Aaron and his sons, keeping the charge of the sanctuary (n) for the charge of the children of Israel; and the stranger that cometh nigh shall be put to death. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(n) That no one should enter into the tabernacle contrary to God's appointment.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num003.htm#039">Numbers 3:39</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_039">num 3:39</a></font></span></p>All that were numbered of the Levites, which Moses and Aaron numbered at the commandment of the LORD, throughout their families, all the males from a month old and upward, [were] twenty and two (o) thousand. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(o) So that the first born of the children of Israel were more by 273, as in (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/num003.htm#043">Num 3:43</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num003.htm#041">Numbers 3:41</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_041">num 3:41</a></font></span></p>And thou shalt take the Levites for me (I [am] the LORD) (f) instead of all the firstborn among the children of Israel; and the cattle of the Levites instead of all the firstlings among the cattle of the children of Israel. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(f) So that now the Levites should satisfy the Lord for the first born of Israel, excepting the 273 which were more than the Levites for whom they paid money.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/num003.htm#050">Numbers 3:50</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_050">num 3:50</a></font></span></p>Of the (q) firstborn of the children of Israel took he the money; a thousand three hundred and threescore and five [shekels], after the shekel of the sanctuary: 
 <p>
 </p><p>(q) Or the two hundred seventy and three which were more than the Levites.
