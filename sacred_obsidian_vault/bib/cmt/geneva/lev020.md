 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Geneva Bible Translation Notes</i>, [1599], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev020.htm#001">Leviticus Chapter 20</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev020.htm#002">Leviticus 20:2</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_002">lev 20:2</a></font></span></p>Again, thou shalt say to the children of Israel, Whosoever [he be] of the children of Israel, or of the strangers that sojourn in Israel, that giveth [any] of his seed unto (a) Molech; he shall surely be put to death: the people of the land shall stone him with stones. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(a) By Molech he means any type of idol, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev018.htm#021">Lev 18:21</a>)  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev020.htm#003">Leviticus 20:3</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_003">lev 20:3</a></font></span></p>And I will (b) set my face against that man, and will cut him off from among his people; because he hath given of his seed unto Molech, to defile my sanctuary, and to profane my holy name. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(b) Read (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev017.htm#010">Lev 17:10</a>, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev018.htm#021">Lev 18:21</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev020.htm#004">Leviticus 20:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">lev 20:4</a></font></span></p>And if the (c) people of the land do any ways hide their eyes from the man, when he giveth of his seed unto Molech, and kill him not: 
 <p>
 </p><p>(c) Though the people be negligent to do their duty, and defend God's right, yet he will not allow wickedness to go unpunished.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev020.htm#006">Leviticus 20:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">lev 20:6</a></font></span></p>And the soul that turneth after such as have familiar spirits, and after wizards, to go a (d) whoring after them, I will even set my face against that soul, and will cut him off from among his people. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(d) To esteem sorcerers or conjurers is spiritual whoredom, or idolatry.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev020.htm#009">Leviticus 20:9</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_009">lev 20:9</a></font></span></p>For every one that curseth his father or his mother shall be surely put to death: he hath cursed his father or his mother; (e) his blood [shall be] upon him. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(e) He is worthy to die.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev020.htm#014">Leviticus 20:14</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_014">lev 20:14</a></font></span></p>And if a man take a wife and her mother, (f) it [is] wickedness: they shall be burnt with fire, both he and they; that there be no wickedness among you. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(f) It is an abominable and detestable thing.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev020.htm#020">Leviticus 20:20</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_020">lev 20:20</a></font></span></p>And if a man shall lie with his uncle's wife, he hath uncovered his uncle's nakedness: they shall bear their sin; they shall die (g) childless. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(g) They shall be cut off from their people, and their children shall be taken as bastards, and not counted among the Israelites.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev020.htm#021">Leviticus 20:21</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_021">lev 20:21</a></font></span></p>And if a man shall take his brother's wife, it [is] an unclean thing: he hath uncovered his brother's (h) nakedness; they shall be childless. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(h) Read (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev018.htm#016">Lev 18:16</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev020.htm#024">Leviticus 20:24</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_024">lev 20:24</a></font></span></p>But I have said unto you, Ye shall inherit their land, and I will give it unto you to possess it, a land that (i) floweth with milk and honey: I [am] the LORD your God, which have separated you from [other] people. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(i) Full of abundance of all things.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev020.htm#025">Leviticus 20:25</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_025">lev 20:25</a></font></span></p>Ye shall therefore put difference between clean beasts and unclean, and between unclean fowls and clean: and ye shall not make your souls (k) abominable by beast, or by fowl, or by any manner of living thing that creepeth on the ground, which I have separated from you as unclean. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(k) By eating them contrary to my commandment.
