 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1010.htm#010">1 Chronicles 10:10</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_010">ch1 10:10</a></font></span></p>And they put his armour in the house of their gods, and fastened his head in the temple of (a) Dagon. 
 <p>
 </p><p>(a) Which was the idol of the Philistines, and from the belly downward had the form of a fish, and upward of a man.
