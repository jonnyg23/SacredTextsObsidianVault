 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1001.htm#001">1 Chronicles</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_000">ch1 13:0</a></font></span></p>
 <p>Compare <a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa2006.htm#001">Sa2 6:1-11</a> and notes. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1013.htm#001">1 Chronicles 13:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">ch1 13:1</a></font></span></p>
 <p>The captains ... - Such an organisation had probably been established generally through the tribes prior to the time of David: but David seems to have been the first to recognize in these officers of the host representatives of the people, to consult them on public affairs, and to give them a certain political position.
