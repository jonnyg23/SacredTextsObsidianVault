 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Notes on the Bible</i>, by Albert Barnes, [1834], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch2005.htm#001">2 Chronicles Chapter 5</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch2005.htm#001">2 Chronicles 5:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">ch2 5:1</a></font></span></p>
 <p>This chapter contains one important addition only to the narrative of Kings (see the marginal references); namely, the account of the circumstances under which the manifestation of the Divine Presence took place <a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch2005.htm#011">Ch2 5:11-13</a>. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch2005.htm#004">2 Chronicles 5:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">ch2 5:4</a></font></span></p>
 <p>The Levites took up the ark - i. e., such of the Levites as were also priests (compare <a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch2005.htm#007">Ch2 5:7</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/kg1008.htm#003">Kg1 8:3</a>). 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch2005.htm#009">2 Chronicles 5:9</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_009">ch2 5:9</a></font></span></p>
 <p>From the ark - Or, according to a different reading here and according to <a href="../../../bib/kjv/kg1008.htm#008">Kg1 8:8</a>, some read, "the ends of the staves were seen from the Holy place."
 </p><p>There it is unto this day - This should be corrected as in the margin. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch2005.htm#013">2 Chronicles 5:13</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_013">ch2 5:13</a></font></span></p>
 <p>Even the house of the Lord - Or, according to another reading (Septuagint), which removes the superfluousness of these words - "The house was filled with a cloud of the glory of the Lord."
