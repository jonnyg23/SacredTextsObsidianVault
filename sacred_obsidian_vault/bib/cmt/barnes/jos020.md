 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Notes on the Bible</i>, by Albert Barnes, [1834], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jos020.htm#001">Joshua Chapter 20</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jos020.htm#004">Joshua 20:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">jos 20:4</a></font></span></p>
 <p>As soon as the manslayer presented himself at the city of refuge, the elders of the city were to hold an inquiry, and receive him provisionally into the city. Afterward, when the avenger of blood should have tracked his victim to the city, and appear to claim him, a more formal and thorough investigation <a href="../../../bib/kjv/jos020.htm#006">Jos 20:6</a> was to be made. Consult the marginal references.
