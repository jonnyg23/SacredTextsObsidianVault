 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa134.htm#002">Psalms 134:2</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_002">psa 134:2</a></font></span></p>1-2. The pilgrim bands arriving at the sanctuary call on the priests, who (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa134.htm#001">Psa 134:1-3</a>)
 <p>
 </p><p>Lift up your hands--(Compare <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa028.htm#002">Psa 28:2</a>).
 </p><p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa134.htm#003">Psalms 134:3</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_003">psa 134:3</a></font></span></p>After the manner directed (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/num006.htm#023">Num 6:23</a>). 
 <p>
 </p><p>out of Zion--the Church, as His residence, and thus seat of blessings. Thus close the songs of degrees.
