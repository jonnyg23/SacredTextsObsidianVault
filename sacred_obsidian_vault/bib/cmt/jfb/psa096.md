 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa096.htm#001">Psalms 96:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">psa 96:1</a></font></span></p>The substance of this Psalm, and portions of the ninety-seventh, ninety-eighth, and hundredth, are found in 1Ch. 16:7-36, which was used by David's directions in the dedication of the tabernacle on Mount Zion. The dispensation of the Messiah was typified by that event, involving, as it did, a more permanent seat of worship, and the introduction of additional and more spiritual services. Hence the language of these Psalms may be regarded as having a higher import than that pertinent to the occasion on which it was thus publicly used. (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa096.htm#001">Psa 96:1-13</a>)
 <p>
 </p><p>All nations are invited to unite in this most joyful praise. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>new song--literally, "fresh," or new mercies (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa033.htm#003">Psa 33:3</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa040.htm#003">Psa 40:3</a>).
 </p><p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa096.htm#002">Psalms 96:2</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_002">psa 96:2</a></font></span></p>show forth--literally, "declare joyful tidings." 
 <p>
 </p><p>salvation--illustrates His glory in its wonders of love and mercy.
 </p><p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa096.htm#004">Psalms 96:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">psa 96:4</a></font></span></p>For He is not a local God, but of universal agency, while idols are nothing.
 <p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa096.htm#006">Psalms 96:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">psa 96:6</a></font></span></p>Honour and majesty--are His attendants, declared in His mighty works, while power and grace are specially seen in His spiritual relations to His people.
 <p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa096.htm#007">Psalms 96:7</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_007">psa 96:7</a></font></span></p>Give--or, "ascribe" (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa029.htm#001">Psa 29:1</a>) due honor to Him, by acts of appointed and solemn worship in His house.
 <p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa096.htm#008">Psalms 96:8</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_008">psa 96:8</a></font></span></p>offering--of thanks.
 <p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa096.htm#009">Psalms 96:9</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_009">psa 96:9</a></font></span></p>beauty of holiness-- (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa029.htm#002">Psa 29:2</a>). 
 <p>
 </p><p>fear . . . him-- (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa002.htm#011">Psa 2:11</a>).
 </p><p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa096.htm#010">Psalms 96:10</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_010">psa 96:10</a></font></span></p>Let all know that the government of the world is ordered in justice, and they shall enjoy firm and lasting peace (compare <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa072.htm#003">Psa 72:3</a>, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa072.htm#007">Psa 72:7</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/isa009.htm#006">Isa 9:6-7</a>).
 <p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa096.htm#011">Psalms 96:11</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_011">psa 96:11</a></font></span></p>For which reason the universe is invoked to unite in joy, and even inanimate nature (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom008.htm#014">Rom 8:14-22</a>) is poetically represented as capable of joining in the anthem of praise.
