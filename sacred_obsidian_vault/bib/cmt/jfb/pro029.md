Fausset and David Brown [1882] at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#001">Proverbs Chapter 29</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#001">Proverbs 29:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">pro 29:1</a></font></span></p>(Pro. 29:1-27)
 <p>
 </p><p>hardeneth . . . neck--obstinately refuses counsel (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/kg2017.htm#014">Kg2 17:14</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/neh009.htm#016">Neh 9:16</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>destroyed--literally, "shivered" or "utterly broken to pieces." 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>without remedy--literally, "without healing" or repairing.
 </p><p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#002">Proverbs 29:2</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_002">pro 29:2</a></font></span></p>(Compare <a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro011.htm#010">Pro 11:10</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro028.htm#028">Pro 28:28</a>). 
 <p>
 </p><p>in authority--(Compare Margin), increased in power.
 </p><p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#003">Proverbs 29:3</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_003">pro 29:3</a></font></span></p>(Compare <a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro004.htm#006">Pro 4:6-7</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro010.htm#001">Pro 10:1</a>, &amp;c.).
 <p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#004">Proverbs 29:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">pro 29:4</a></font></span></p>by judgment--that is, righteous decisions, opposed to those procured by gifts (compare <a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro028.htm#021">Pro 28:21</a>), by which good government is perverted. 
 <p>
 </p><p>land--for nation.
 </p><p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#005">Proverbs 29:5</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_005">pro 29:5</a></font></span></p>(Compare <a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro026.htm#028">Pro 26:28</a>). 
 <p>
 </p><p>spreadeth . . . feet--By misleading him as to his real character, the flatterer brings him to evil, prepared by himself or others.
 </p><p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#006">Proverbs 29:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">pro 29:6</a></font></span></p>In--or, "By" 
 <p>
 </p><p>the transgression--he is brought into difficulty (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro012.htm#013">Pro 12:13</a>), but the righteous go on prospering, and so sing or rejoice.
 </p><p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#007">Proverbs 29:7</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_007">pro 29:7</a></font></span></p>considereth--literally, "knows," as <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa001.htm#006">Psa 1:6</a>. 
 <p>
 </p><p>the cause--that is, in courts of justice (compare <a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#014">Pro 29:14</a>). The voluntary neglect of it by the wicked (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro028.htm#027">Pro 28:27</a>) occasions oppression.
 </p><p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#008">Proverbs 29:8</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_008">pro 29:8</a></font></span></p>Scornful men--those who contemptuously disregard God's law. 
 <p>
 </p><p>bring--(Compare Margin), kindle strife. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>turn away wrath--that is, "abate wrath."
 </p><p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#009">Proverbs 29:9</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_009">pro 29:9</a></font></span></p>contendeth--that is, in law. 
 <p>
 </p><p>whether . . . laugh--The fool, whether angry or good-humored, is unsettled; or referring the words to the wise man, the sense is, that all his efforts, severe or gentle, are unavailing to pacify the fool.
 </p><p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#010">Proverbs 29:10</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_010">pro 29:10</a></font></span></p>bloodthirsty--(Compare Margin), murderers (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa005.htm#006">Psa 5:6</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa026.htm#009">Psa 26:9</a>). 
 <p>
 </p><p>hate, &amp;c.-- (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro001.htm#011">Pro 1:11</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/gen003.htm#004">Gen 3:4</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>seek . . . soul--that is, to preserve it.
 </p><p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#011">Proverbs 29:11</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_011">pro 29:11</a></font></span></p>(Compare <a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro012.htm#016">Pro 12:16</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro016.htm#032">Pro 16:32</a>). 
 <p>
 </p><p>mind--or, "spirit," for anger or any ill passion which the righteous restrain.
 </p><p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#012">Proverbs 29:12</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_012">pro 29:12</a></font></span></p>His servants imitate him.
 <p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#013">Proverbs 29:13</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_013">pro 29:13</a></font></span></p>(Compare <a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro022.htm#002">Pro 22:2</a>). 
 <p>
 </p><p>deceitful man--literally, "man of vexations," an exactor. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>the Lord . . . their eyes--sustains their lives (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa1014.htm#027">Sa1 14:27</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa013.htm#003">Psa 13:3</a>); that is, both depend on Him, and He will do justice.
 </p><p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#014">Proverbs 29:14</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_014">pro 29:14</a></font></span></p>(Compare <a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro020.htm#028">Pro 20:28</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro025.htm#005">Pro 25:5</a>). Such is the character of the King of kings (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa072.htm#004">Psa 72:4</a>, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa072.htm#012">Psa 72:12</a>).
 <p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#015">Proverbs 29:15</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_015">pro 29:15</a></font></span></p>(Compare <a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro013.htm#024">Pro 13:24</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro023.htm#013">Pro 23:13</a>).
 <p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#016">Proverbs 29:16</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_016">pro 29:16</a></font></span></p>(Compare <a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#002">Pro 29:2</a>, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#012">Pro 29:12</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa012.htm#001">Psa 12:1-8</a>). 
 <p>
 </p><p>shall see . . . fall--and triumph in it (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa037.htm#034">Psa 37:34-38</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa058.htm#010">Psa 58:10-11</a>).
 </p><p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#017">Proverbs 29:17</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_017">pro 29:17</a></font></span></p>(Compare <a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#003">Pro 29:3</a>, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#015">Pro 29:15</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro019.htm#018">Pro 19:18</a>). 
 <p>
 </p><p>give thee rest--peace and quiet (compare <a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#009">Pro 29:9</a>).
 </p><p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#018">Proverbs 29:18</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_018">pro 29:18</a></font></span></p>no vision--instruction in God's truth, which was by prophets, through visions (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa1003.htm#001">Sa1 3:1</a>). 
 <p>
 </p><p>people perish--(Compare Margin), are deprived of moral restraints. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>keepeth the law--has, and observes, instruction (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro014.htm#011">Pro 14:11</a>, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro014.htm#034">Pro 14:34</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa019.htm#011">Psa 19:11</a>).
 </p><p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#019">Proverbs 29:19</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_019">pro 29:19</a></font></span></p>A servant--who lacks good principle. 
 <p>
 </p><p>corrected--or discovered. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>will not answer--that is, will not obey.
 </p><p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#020">Proverbs 29:20</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_020">pro 29:20</a></font></span></p>(Compare <a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro021.htm#005">Pro 21:5</a>). 
 <p>
 </p><p>hasty in . . . words?--implying self-conceit (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro026.htm#012">Pro 26:12</a>).
 </p><p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#021">Proverbs 29:21</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_021">pro 29:21</a></font></span></p>become his son--assume the place and privileges of one.
 <p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#022">Proverbs 29:22</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_022">pro 29:22</a></font></span></p>(Compare <a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro015.htm#018">Pro 15:18</a>). Such are delighted by discord and violence.
 <p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#023">Proverbs 29:23</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_023">pro 29:23</a></font></span></p>(Compare <a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro016.htm#018">Pro 16:18</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro018.htm#012">Pro 18:12</a>). 
 <p>
 </p><p>honour . . . spirit--or, "such shall lay hold on honor" (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro011.htm#016">Pro 11:16</a>).
 </p><p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#024">Proverbs 29:24</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_024">pro 29:24</a></font></span></p>hateth . . . soul--(Compare <a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro008.htm#036">Pro 8:36</a>). 
 <p>
 </p><p>heareth cursing-- (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev005.htm#001">Lev 5:1</a>), risks the punishment, rather than reveal truth.
 </p><p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#025">Proverbs 29:25</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_025">pro 29:25</a></font></span></p>The fear . . . snare--involves men in difficulty (compare <a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#006">Pro 29:6</a>). 
 <p>
 </p><p>shall be safe--(Compare Margin; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro018.htm#010">Pro 18:10</a>).
 </p><p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#026">Proverbs 29:26</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_026">pro 29:26</a></font></span></p>(Compare Margin; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa027.htm#008">Psa 27:8</a>). God alone will and can do exact justice.
 <p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#027">Proverbs 29:27</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_027">pro 29:27</a></font></span></p>(Compare <a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro003.htm#032">Pro 3:32</a>). On last clause, compare <a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro029.htm#016">Pro 29:16</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa037.htm#012">Psa 37:12</a>.
