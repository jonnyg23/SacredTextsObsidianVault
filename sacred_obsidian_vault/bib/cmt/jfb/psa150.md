 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa150.htm#001">Psalms 150:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">psa 150:1</a></font></span></p>This is a suitable doxology for the whole book, reciting the "place, theme, mode, and extent of God's high praise." (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa150.htm#001">Psa 150:1-6</a>)
 <p>
 </p><p>in his sanctuary--on earth. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>firmament of his power--which illustrates His power.
 </p><p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa150.htm#002">Psalms 150:2</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_002">psa 150:2</a></font></span></p>mighty acts-- (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa145.htm#004">Psa 145:4</a>). 
 <p>
 </p><p>excellent greatness--or, abundance of greatness.
 </p><p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa150.htm#003">Psalms 150:3</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_003">psa 150:3</a></font></span></p>trumpet--used to call religious assemblies;
 <p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa150.htm#004">Psalms 150:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">psa 150:4</a></font></span></p>organs--or pipe, a wind instrument, and the others were used in worship.
 <p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa150.htm#005">Psalms 150:5</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_005">psa 150:5</a></font></span></p>cymbals--suited to loud praise (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/neh012.htm#027">Neh 12:27</a>).
 <p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa150.htm#006">Psalms 150:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">psa 150:6</a></font></span></p>LIVING VOICES SHALL TAKE UP THE FAILING SOUNDS OF DEAD INSTRUMENTS, AND AS THEY CEASE ON EARTH, THOSE OF INTELLIGENT RANSOMED SPIRITS AND HOLY ANGELS, AS WITH THE SOUND OF MIGHTY THUNDERS, WILL PROLONG ETERNALLY THE PRAISE, SAYING: "ALLELUIA! SALVATION, AND GLORY, AND HONOR, AND POWER, UNTO THE LORD OUR GOD;" "ALLELUIA! FOR THE LORD GOD OMNIPOTENT REIGNETH." AMEN!
