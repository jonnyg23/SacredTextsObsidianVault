 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa067.htm#001">Psalms 67:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">psa 67:1</a></font></span></p>A prayer that, by God's blessing on His people, His salvation and praise may be extended over the earth. (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa067.htm#001">Psa 67:1-7</a>)
 <p>
 </p><p>cause his face to shine--show us favor (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/num006.htm#024">Num 6:24-25</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa031.htm#016">Psa 31:16</a>).
 </p><p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa067.htm#002">Psalms 67:2</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_002">psa 67:2</a></font></span></p>thy way--of gracious dealing (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/isa055.htm#008">Isa 55:8</a>), as explained by-- 
 <p>
 </p><p>saving health--or literally, "salvation."
 </p><p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa067.htm#003">Psalms 67:3</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_003">psa 67:3</a></font></span></p>Thanks will be rendered for the blessings of His wise and holy government (compare <a href="../../../bib/kjv/isa002.htm#003">Isa 2:3-4</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/isa011.htm#004">Isa 11:4</a>).
 <p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa067.htm#006">Psalms 67:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">psa 67:6</a></font></span></p>The blessings of a fruitful harvest are mentioned as types of greater and spiritual blessings, under which all nations shall fear and love God.
