 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa099.htm#001">Psalms 99:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">psa 99:1</a></font></span></p>God's government is especially exercised in and for His Church, which should praise Him for His gracious dealings. (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa099.htm#001">Psa 99:1-9</a>)
 <p>
 </p><p>sitteth . . . cherubim--(compare <a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa1004.htm#004">Sa1 4:4</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa080.htm#001">Psa 80:1</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>tremble . . . be moved--inspired with fear by His judgments on the wicked.
 </p><p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa099.htm#002">Psalms 99:2</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_002">psa 99:2</a></font></span></p>great in Zion--where He dwells (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa009.htm#011">Psa 9:11</a>).
 <p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa099.htm#003">Psalms 99:3</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_003">psa 99:3</a></font></span></p>thy . . . name--perfections of justice, power, &amp;c. 
 <p>
 </p><p>great and terrible name--producing dread (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/deu010.htm#017">Deu 10:17</a>), and to be praised by those over whom He is exalted (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa097.htm#009">Psa 97:9</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>it is holy--or, "He is holy" (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa099.htm#005">Psa 99:5</a>, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa099.htm#009">Psa 99:9</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/isa006.htm#003">Isa 6:3</a>).
 </p><p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa099.htm#004">Psalms 99:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">psa 99:4</a></font></span></p>To His wise and righteous government all nations should render honor. 
 <p>
 </p><p>king's . . . judgment--His power is combined with justice. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>he is holy--(compare <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa022.htm#003">Psa 22:3</a>).
 </p><p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa099.htm#006">Psalms 99:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">psa 99:6</a></font></span></p>The experience of these servants of God is cited for encouragement. 
 <p>
 </p><p>among . . . priests, among . . . upon the Lord [and] He spake . . . pillar--may be referred to all three (compare <a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo018.htm#019">Exo 18:19</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev008.htm#015">Lev 8:15</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/deu005.htm#005">Deu 5:5</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa1009.htm#013">Sa1 9:13</a>).
 </p><p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa099.htm#007">Psalms 99:7</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_007">psa 99:7</a></font></span></p>cloudy pillar--the medium of divine intercourse (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo033.htm#009">Exo 33:9</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/num012.htm#005">Num 12:5</a>). Obedience was united with worship. God answered them as intercessors for the people, who, though forgiven, were yet chastened (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo032.htm#010">Exo 32:10</a>, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo032.htm#034">Exo 32:34</a>).
