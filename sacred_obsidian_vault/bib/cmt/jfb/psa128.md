 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa128.htm#001">Psalms 128:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">psa 128:1</a></font></span></p>The temporal blessings of true piety. The eighth chapter of Zecariah is a virtual commentary on this Psalm. Compare <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa128.htm#003">Psa 128:3</a> with <a href="../../../bib/kjv/zac008.htm#005">Zac 8:5</a>; and <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa128.htm#002">Psa 128:2</a> with <a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev026.htm#016">Lev 26:16</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/deu028.htm#033">Deu 28:33</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/zac008.htm#010">Zac 8:10</a>; and <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa128.htm#006">Psa 128:6</a> with <a href="../../../bib/kjv/zac008.htm#004">Zac 8:4</a>. (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa128.htm#001">Psa 128:1-6</a>)
 <p>
 </p><p>(Compare <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa001.htm#001">Psa 1:1</a>).
 </p><p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa128.htm#002">Psalms 128:2</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_002">psa 128:2</a></font></span></p>For thou shalt eat--that is, It is a blessing to live on the fruits of one's own industry.
 <p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa128.htm#003">Psalms 128:3</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_003">psa 128:3</a></font></span></p>by the sides--or, "within" (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa048.htm#002">Psa 48:2</a>). 
 <p>
 </p><p>olive plants--are peculiarly luxuriant (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa052.htm#008">Psa 52:8</a>).
 </p><p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa128.htm#005">Psalms 128:5</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_005">psa 128:5</a></font></span></p>In temporal blessings the pious do not forget the richer blessings of God's grace, which they shall ever enjoy.
 <p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa128.htm#006">Psalms 128:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">psa 128:6</a></font></span></p>Long life crowns all other temporal favors. As <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa125.htm#005">Psa 125:5</a>, this Psalm closes with a prayer for peace, with prosperity for God's people.
