Fausset and David Brown [1882] at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro003.htm#001">Proverbs Chapter 3</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro003.htm#001">Proverbs 3:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">pro 3:1</a></font></span></p>The study of truth commended. God must be feared, honored, and trusted, and filial submission, under chastisement, exhibited. The excellence of wisdom urged and illustrated by its place in the divine counsels. Piety enforced by a contrast of the destiny of the righteous and the wicked. (Pro. 3:1-35)
 <p>
 </p><p>law [and] commandments--all divine instructions (see on <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa119.htm#001">Psa 119:1</a> and <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa119.htm#004">Psa 119:4</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>let thine heart keep--or sincerely observe (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro004.htm#013">Pro 4:13</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro005.htm#002">Pro 5:2</a>).
 </p><p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro003.htm#002">Proverbs 3:2</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_002">pro 3:2</a></font></span></p>length . . . life--often promised as blessings (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa021.htm#004">Psa 21:4</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa091.htm#016">Psa 91:16</a>). 
 <p>
 </p><p>peace--includes prosperity (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa125.htm#005">Psa 125:5</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>add--abound to thee.
 </p><p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro003.htm#003">Proverbs 3:3</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_003">pro 3:3</a></font></span></p>mercy and truth--God's faithfulness to His promises is often expressed by these terms (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa025.htm#010">Psa 25:10</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa057.htm#003">Psa 57:3</a>). As attributes of men, they express integrity in a wide sense (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro016.htm#006">Pro 16:6</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro020.htm#028">Pro 20:28</a>). 
 <p>
 </p><p>bind . . . write . . . heart--outwardly adorn and inwardly govern motives.
 </p><p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro003.htm#004">Proverbs 3:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">pro 3:4</a></font></span></p>favour--grace, amiability (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro022.htm#011">Pro 22:11</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa045.htm#002">Psa 45:2</a>); united with this, 
 <p>
 </p><p>a good understanding--(Compare Margin), a discrimination, which secures success. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>in the sight . . . man--such as God and man approve.
 </p><p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro003.htm#005">Proverbs 3:5</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_005">pro 3:5</a></font></span></p>Trust . . . heart--This is the center and marrow of true wisdom (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro022.htm#019">Pro 22:19</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro028.htm#025">Pro 28:25</a>). The positive duty has its corresponding negation in the admonition against self-confidence.
 <p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro003.htm#006">Proverbs 3:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">pro 3:6</a></font></span></p>ways-- (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa001.htm#001">Psa 1:1</a>). 
 <p>
 </p><p>acknowledge--by seeking His wise aid (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro016.htm#003">Pro 16:3</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa037.htm#005">Psa 37:5</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer009.htm#023">Jer 9:23-24</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>direct--literally, "make plain" (compare <a href="../../../bib/kjv/heb012.htm#013">Heb 12:13</a>).
 </p><p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro003.htm#007">Proverbs 3:7</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_007">pro 3:7</a></font></span></p>(Compare <a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro027.htm#002">Pro 27:2</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom012.htm#016">Rom 12:16</a>). 
 <p>
 </p><p>fear . . . evil--reverentially regarding His law.
 </p><p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro003.htm#008">Proverbs 3:8</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_008">pro 3:8</a></font></span></p>It--This conduct. 
 <p>
 </p><p>health--(Compare Margin). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>to thy navel--for all the organs of nourishment. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>marrow--(Compare Margin). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>bones--frame of body. True piety promotes bodily health.
 </p><p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro003.htm#009">Proverbs 3:9</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_009">pro 3:9</a></font></span></p>(Compare <a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro011.htm#025">Pro 11:25</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo023.htm#019">Exo 23:19</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/deu018.htm#004">Deu 18:4</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/isa032.htm#008">Isa 32:8</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/co2009.htm#013">Co2 9:13</a>).
 <p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro003.htm#010">Proverbs 3:10</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_010">pro 3:10</a></font></span></p>presses--or wine fats (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/joe002.htm#024">Joe 2:24</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/joe003.htm#013">Joe 3:13</a>).
 <p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro003.htm#011">Proverbs 3:11</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_011">pro 3:11</a></font></span></p>The true intent of afflictions considered; they do not contradict the assertion of the blessed state of the pious (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/job005.htm#017">Job 5:17</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/heb012.htm#005">Heb 12:5-6</a>).
 <p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro003.htm#012">Proverbs 3:12</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_012">pro 3:12</a></font></span></p>he delighteth--or receiveth as denoting reconciliation regarding the offense which produced chastisement.
 <p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro003.htm#013">Proverbs 3:13</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_013">pro 3:13</a></font></span></p>findeth--literally, "reaches," or "obtains by seeking." 
 <p>
 </p><p>getteth--literally, "draws out," as metals by digging.
 </p><p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro003.htm#014">Proverbs 3:14</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_014">pro 3:14</a></font></span></p>The figure of <a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro003.htm#013">Pro 3:13</a> carried out. 
 <p>
 </p><p>it--that is, wisdom. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>merchandise of silver--acquisition by trading. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>fine gold--dug gold, solid as a nugget.
 </p><p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro003.htm#015">Proverbs 3:15</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_015">pro 3:15</a></font></span></p>rubies--gems, or pearls.
 <p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro003.htm#016">Proverbs 3:16</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_016">pro 3:16</a></font></span></p>Wisdom personified as bringing the best blessings (compare <a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat006.htm#033">Mat 6:33</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/ti1004.htm#008">Ti1 4:8</a>).
 <p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro003.htm#017">Proverbs 3:17</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_017">pro 3:17</a></font></span></p>Her ways--such as she directs us to take.
 <p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro003.htm#018">Proverbs 3:18</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_018">pro 3:18</a></font></span></p>Wisdom allegorized as 
 <p>
 </p><p>a tree of life-- (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/gen002.htm#009">Gen 2:9</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/gen003.htm#022">Gen 3:22</a>) whose fruit preserves life, gives all that makes living a blessing.
 </p><p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro003.htm#019">Proverbs 3:19</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_019">pro 3:19</a></font></span></p>The place of wisdom in the economy of creation and providence commends it to men, who, in proportion to their finite powers, may possess this invaluable attribute, and are thus encouraged by the divine example of its use to seek its possession.
 <p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro003.htm#021">Proverbs 3:21</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_021">pro 3:21</a></font></span></p>sound wisdom--(compare <a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro002.htm#007">Pro 2:7</a>). 
 <p>
 </p><p>let . . . eyes--that is, these words of instruction.
 </p><p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro003.htm#022">Proverbs 3:22</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_022">pro 3:22</a></font></span></p>assign reasons in their value for happiness and ornament, guidance and support in dangers, both when waking and sleeping.
 <p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro003.htm#025">Proverbs 3:25</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_025">pro 3:25</a></font></span></p>Be not--or, "You shall not be." 
 <p>
 </p><p>sudden fear--what causes it (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro001.htm#027">Pro 1:27</a>), any unlooked-for evil (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa046.htm#003">Psa 46:3</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa091.htm#012">Psa 91:12</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/pe1003.htm#014">Pe1 3:14</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>desolation-- (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro001.htm#027">Pro 1:27</a>).
 </p><p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro003.htm#026">Proverbs 3:26</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_026">pro 3:26</a></font></span></p>The reason; such as are objects of God's favor. 
 <p>
 </p><p>be thy confidence--literally, "in thy confidence," in the source of thy strength (compare <a href="../../../bib/kjv/nah003.htm#009">Nah 3:9</a>, for the same construction, Hebrew).
 </p><p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro003.htm#027">Proverbs 3:27</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_027">pro 3:27</a></font></span></p>Promptly fulfil all obligations both of justice and charity (compare <a href="../../../bib/kjv/jam002.htm#015">Jam 2:15-16</a>).
 <p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro003.htm#029">Proverbs 3:29</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_029">pro 3:29</a></font></span></p>Do not abuse confidence and avoid litigation.
 <p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro003.htm#031">Proverbs 3:31</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_031">pro 3:31</a></font></span></p>oppressor--or man of mischief. The destiny of successful evildoers warns against desiring their lot (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa037.htm#001">Psa 37:1-2</a>, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa037.htm#035">Psa 37:35-36</a>).
 <p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro003.htm#032">Proverbs 3:32</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_032">pro 3:32</a></font></span></p>Reasons for the warning. 
 <p>
 </p><p>froward-- (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro002.htm#015">Pro 2:15</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>secret . . . righteous--in their communion (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/amo003.htm#007">Amo 3:7</a>).
 </p><p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro003.htm#033">Proverbs 3:33</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_033">pro 3:33</a></font></span></p>curse . . . wicked--It abides with them, and will be manifested.
 <p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro003.htm#034">Proverbs 3:34</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_034">pro 3:34</a></font></span></p>The retribution of sinners, as in <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa018.htm#026">Psa 18:26</a>.
 <p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/pro003.htm#035">Proverbs 3:35</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_035">pro 3:35</a></font></span></p>inherit--as a portion. 
 <p>
 </p><p>shame--or disgrace, as opposed to honor. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>promotion--(Compare Margin); as honor for well-doing makes men conspicuous, so fools are signalized by disgrace.
 </p><p>
