 <p>Overview
 </p><p><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer007.htm#001">Jer 7:1</a>, Jeremiah is sent to call to true repentance, to prevent the Jews' captivity; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer007.htm#008">Jer 7:8</a>, He rejects their vain confidence, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer007.htm#012">Jer 7:12</a>. by the example of Shiloh; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer007.htm#017">Jer 7:17</a>, He threatens them for their idolatry; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer007.htm#021">Jer 7:21</a>, He rejects the sacrifices of the disobedient; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer007.htm#029">Jer 7:29</a>, He exhorts to mourn for their abominations in Tophet; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer007.htm#032">Jer 7:32</a>, and the judgments for the same.
