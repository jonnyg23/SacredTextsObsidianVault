 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>Treasury of Scriptural Knowledge</i>, by R.A. Torrey, [ca. 1880], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa1031.htm#001">1 Kings (1 Samuel) Chapter 31</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa1001.htm#001">1 Kings (1 Samuel)</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_000">sa1 31:0</a></font></span></p>
 <p>Overview
 </p><p><a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa1031.htm#001">Sa1 31:1</a>, Saul, having lost his army, and his sons slain, he and his armour-bearer kill themselves; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa1031.htm#007">Sa1 31:7</a>, The Philistines possess the forsaken towns of the Israelites; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa1031.htm#008">Sa1 31:8</a>, They triumph over the dead carcases; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa1031.htm#011">Sa1 31:11</a>, They of Jabesh-gilead recovering the dead bodies by night, burn them at Jabesh, and mournfully bury their bones. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa1031.htm#001">1 Kings (1 Samuel) 31:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">sa1 31:1</a></font></span></p>
 <p>the Philistines:  <a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa1028.htm#001">Sa1 28:1</a>, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa1028.htm#015">Sa1 28:15</a>, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa1029.htm#001">Sa1 29:1</a> 
 </p><p>fell down:  <a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa1012.htm#025">Sa1 12:25</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1010.htm#001">Ch1 10:1-12</a> 
 </p><p>slain:  Heb. wounded
 </p><p>Gilboa:  Eusebius and Jerome place this mountain six miles west from Bethshan, where was a large place called Gelbus.  The natives still call it Djebel Gilbo. <a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa1028.htm#004">Sa1 28:4</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa2001.htm#021">Sa2 1:21</a> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa1031.htm#002">1 Kings (1 Samuel) 31:2</a></p>

 </p><p>Jonathan:  <a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa1013.htm#002">Sa1 13:2</a>, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa1013.htm#016">Sa1 13:16</a>, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa1014.htm#001">Sa1 14:1-14</a>, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa1014.htm#049">Sa1 14:49</a>, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa1018.htm#001">Sa1 18:1-4</a>, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa1023.htm#017">Sa1 23:17</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1008.htm#033">Ch1 8:33</a>, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1009.htm#039">Ch1 9:39</a>

 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_003">sa1 31:3</a></font></span></p>
 <p>went sore:  <a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa2001.htm#004">Sa2 1:4</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/amo002.htm#014">Amo 2:14</a> 
 </p><p>archers hit him:  Heb. shooters, men with bows, found him, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/gen049.htm#023">Gen 49:23</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/kg1022.htm#034">Kg1 22:34</a>

 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">sa1 31:4</a></font></span></p>
 <p>Draw:  <a href="../../../bib/kjv/jdg009.htm#054">Jdg 9:54</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1010.htm#004">Ch1 10:4</a> 
 </p><p>uncircumcised:  <a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa1014.htm#006">Sa1 14:6</a>, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa1017.htm#026">Sa1 17:26</a>, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa1017.htm#036">Sa1 17:36</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa2001.htm#020">Sa2 1:20</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer009.htm#025">Jer 9:25</a>, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer009.htm#026">Jer 9:26</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze044.htm#007">Eze 44:7-9</a> 
 </p><p>abuse me:  or, mock me
 </p><p>he was sore:  <a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa2001.htm#014">Sa2 1:14</a> 
 </p><p>Saul:  <a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa2001.htm#009">Sa2 1:9</a>, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa2001.htm#010">Sa2 1:10</a>, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa2017.htm#023">Sa2 17:23</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/kg1016.htm#027">Kg1 16:27</a> 
 </p><p>a sword:  Eth hacherve, rather, "the sword," i.e., his armour- bearer's, who, according to the Jews, was Doeg; and if so, then Saul and his executioner fell by the same sword with which they massacred the priests of God.
