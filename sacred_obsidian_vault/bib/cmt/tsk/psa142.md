 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">psa 142:1</a></font></span></p>
 <p>A Prayer:  David was twice in great peril in caves.  on one occasion, in the cave of Adullam, when he fled from Achish king of Gath; and on another, in the cave of Engedi, where he had taken refuge from the pursuit of Saul.  It is not certain to which of these events this Psalm refers; though probably to the former.
