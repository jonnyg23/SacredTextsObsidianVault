 </p><p><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer009.htm#001">Jer 9:1</a>, Jeremiah laments the Jews for their manifold sins; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer009.htm#009">Jer 9:9</a>, and for their judgment; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer009.htm#012">Jer 9:12</a>, Disobedience is the cause of their bitter calamity; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer009.htm#017">Jer 9:17</a>, He exhorts to mourn for their destruction; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer009.htm#023">Jer 9:23</a>, and to trust not in themselves, but in God; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer009.htm#025">Jer 9:25</a>, He threatens both Jews and Gentiles.
