 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_010">psa 77:10</a></font></span></p>
 <p>This is:  etc. Or, as Dr. Waterland renders, "This my affliction is a change of the right hand of the Most High," i.e., it proceeds from a change of God's conduct towards me.  De Dieu renders, Precari, hoc meum est; mutare dextram Altissimì  "To pray, this is my business.  to change the right hand of the Most High."  I can do nothing else than pray:  God is the Ruler of events.  
 </p><p>Mr. N. M. Berlin translates, Dolere meum hoc est: mutare est dextre Altissimi.  "To grieve is my portion.  to change (my condition) belongs to the right hand of the Most High." <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa031.htm#022">Psa 31:22</a>, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa073.htm#022">Psa 73:22</a>, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa116.htm#011">Psa 116:11</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/job042.htm#003">Job 42:3</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/lam003.htm#018">Lam 3:18-23</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/mar009.htm#024">Mar 9:24</a>
