 <p>Overview
 </p><p><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa030.htm#001">Psa 30:1</a>, David praises God for his deliverance; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa030.htm#004">Psa 30:4</a>, He exhorts others to praise him by example of God's dealings with him.
 </p><p>Or, "A psalm or song of David, at the dedication of the house;" by which is supposed to be meant the place he built on the threshing floor of Araunah, after the grievous plague which had nearly desolated the kingdom. <a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa2024.htm#025">Sa2 24:25</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch1021.htm#006">Ch1 21:6</a>
