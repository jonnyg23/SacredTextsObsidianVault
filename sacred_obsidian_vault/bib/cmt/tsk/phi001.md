 <p>Overview
 </p><p><a href="../../../bib/kjv/phi001.htm#001">Phi 1:1</a>, Paul testifies his thankfulness to God, and his love towards them, for the fruits of their faith, and fellowship in his sufferings; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/phi001.htm#009">Phi 1:9</a>, daily praying to him for their increase in grace; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/phi001.htm#012">Phi 1:12</a>, he shows what good the faith of Christ had received by his troubles at Rome; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/phi001.htm#021">Phi 1:21</a>, and how ready he is to glorify Christ either by his life or death; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/phi001.htm#027">Phi 1:27</a>, exhorting them to unity; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/phi001.htm#028">Phi 1:28</a>, and to fortitude in persecution.
