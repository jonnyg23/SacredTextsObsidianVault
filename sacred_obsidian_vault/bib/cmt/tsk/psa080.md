 <p>The boar:  This wild boar, chazir, is the parent stock of our domestic hog.  He is much smaller, but stronger, and more undaunted, colour, an iron grey inclining to black; snout, longer than that of the common breed.  ears comparatively short; tusks, very formidable; and habits, fierce and savage.  He is particularly destructive to corn-fields and vineyards. 2Kings 18:1-19:37, 24:1-25:30; 2Chr. 32:1-33, 36:1-23; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer004.htm#007">Jer 4:7</a>, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer039.htm#001">Jer 39:1-3</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer051.htm#034">Jer 51:34</a>, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer052.htm#007">Jer 52:7</a>, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer052.htm#012">Jer 52:12-14</a>
