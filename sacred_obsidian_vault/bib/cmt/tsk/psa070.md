 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa001.htm#001">Psalms</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_000">psa 70:0</a></font></span></p>
 <p>Overview
 </p><p><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa070.htm#001">Psa 70:1</a>, David solicits God to the speedy destruction of the wicked, and preservation of the godly.
 </p><p>This Psalm is almost word for word the same as the five <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa040.htm#013">Psa 40:13</a> of Psalm 40; and it is written as a part of the succeeding Psalm in about 27 manuscripts.  Both Psalms evidently appear to have been written by David during the rebellion of Absalom, and probably at the crisis when he heard of the sanguinary counsel which Ahithophel had given respecting him; or, as some suppose, when beyond Jordan. 2Sam. 17:1-21
