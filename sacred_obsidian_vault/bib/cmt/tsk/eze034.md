 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_002">eze 34:2</a></font></span></p>
 <p>the shepherds:  The shepherds of Israel, signify their kings and princes, priests and prophets; the flock, the whole of the people; the fat and wool, the tithes and offerings, taxes and imposts: these they exacted with great rigour, and even oppressed and destroyed the people to enrich themselves; but they bestowed no pains to provide for the welfare of the state, or for the souls of those entrusted to them.
