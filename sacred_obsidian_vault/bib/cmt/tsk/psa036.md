 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_000">psa 36:0</a></font></span></p>
 <p>Overview
 </p><p><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa036.htm#001">Psa 36:1</a>, The grievous estate of the wicked; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa036.htm#008">Psa 36:8</a>, The excellency of God's mercy; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa036.htm#010">Psa 36:10</a>, David prays for favour to God's children.
 </p><p>This Psalm is supposed by some to have been composed by David at the beginning of Saul's persecutions; but Calmet and others, on good grounds, are of opinion that it was written during the Babylonian captivity.
