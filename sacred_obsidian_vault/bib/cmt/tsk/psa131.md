 <p>Overview
 </p><p><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa131.htm#001">Psa 131:1</a>, David, professing his humility, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa131.htm#003">Psa 131:3</a>, exhorts Israel to trust in God.
