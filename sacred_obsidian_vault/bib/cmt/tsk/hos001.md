 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/hos001.htm#001">Hosea</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_000">hos 1:0</a></font></span></p>
 <p>Overview
 </p><p><a href="../../../bib/kjv/hos001.htm#001">Hos 1:1</a>, Hosea, to shew God's judgment for spiritual whoredom, takes Gomer, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/hos001.htm#004">Hos 1:4</a>, and has by her Jezreel; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/hos001.htm#006">Hos 1:6</a>, Loruhamah; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/hos001.htm#008">Hos 1:8</a>, and Lo-ammi; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/hos001.htm#010">Hos 1:10</a>, The restoration of Judah and Israel under one head.
