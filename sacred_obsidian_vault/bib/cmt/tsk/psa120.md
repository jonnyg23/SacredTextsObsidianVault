 <p>Overview
 </p><p><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa120.htm#001">Psa 120:1</a>, David prays against Doeg; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa120.htm#003">Psa 120:3</a>, reproves his tongue; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa120.htm#005">Psa 120:5</a>, complains of his necessary conversation with the wicked.
