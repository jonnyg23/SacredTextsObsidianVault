 <p>Overview
 </p><p><a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat020.htm#001">Mat 20:1</a>, Christ, by the similitude of the labourers in the vineyard, shows that God is debtor unto no man; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat020.htm#017">Mat 20:17</a>, foretells his passion; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat020.htm#020">Mat 20:20</a>, by answering the mother of Zebedee's children, teaches his disciples to be lowly; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat020.htm#029">Mat 20:29</a>, and gives two blind men their sight.
