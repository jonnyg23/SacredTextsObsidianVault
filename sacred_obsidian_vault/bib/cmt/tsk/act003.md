 <p>Overview
 </p><p><a href="../../../bib/kjv/act003.htm#001">Act 3:1</a>, Peter preaching to the people that came to see a lame man restored to his feet, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/act003.htm#012">Act 3:12</a>. professes the cure not to have been wrought by his or John's own power or holiness, but by God, and his son Jesus, and through faith in his name; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/act003.htm#013">Act 3:13</a>, withal reprehending them for crucifying Jesus; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/act003.htm#017">Act 3:17</a>, which because they did it through ignorance, and that thereby were fulfilled God's determinate counsel, and the Scriptures, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/act003.htm#019">Act 3:19</a>. he exhorts them by repentance and faith to seek remission of their sins, and salvation in the same Jesus.
