 </p><p>yet:  A man may possess much earthly goods, and yet have neither the heart nor power to enjoy them.  Possession and fruition are not necessarily joined together; and this is also among the vanities of life, and was and still is a very "common evil among men."  It belongs to God as much to give the power to enjoy, as it does to give earthly blessings.
