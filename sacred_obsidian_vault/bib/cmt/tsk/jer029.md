 </p><p><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer029.htm#001">Jer 29:1</a>, Jeremiah sends a letter to the captives in Babylon to be quiet there, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer029.htm#008">Jer 29:8</a>, and not to believe the dreams of their prophets; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer029.htm#010">Jer 29:10</a>, and that they shall return with grace after seventy years; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer029.htm#015">Jer 29:15</a>, He foretells the destruction of the rest for their disobedience; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer029.htm#020">Jer 29:20</a>, He shews the fearful end of Ahab and Zedekiah, two lying prophets; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer029.htm#024">Jer 29:24</a>, Shemaiah writes a letter against Jeremiah; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer029.htm#030">Jer 29:30</a>, Jeremiah foretells his doom.
