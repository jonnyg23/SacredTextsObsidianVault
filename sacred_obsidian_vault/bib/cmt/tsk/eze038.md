 </p><p>it shall be:  Though it is not generally agreed what people or transactions are here predicted, yet it seems evident that the prophecy is not yet accomplished.  Nothing occurred in the wars of Cambyses, or Antiochus Epiphanes with the Jews, that answers to it; and the expression here used, in the latter days, plainly implies that there should be a succession of many ages between the publication of the prediction and its accomplishment.
