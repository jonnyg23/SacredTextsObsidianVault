 <p>Overview
 </p><p><a href="../../../bib/kjv/isa020.htm#001">Isa 20:1</a>, A type prefiguring the shameful captivity of Egypt and Ethiopia. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/isa020.htm#001">Isaiah 20:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">isa 20:1</a></font></span></p>
 <p>Tartan:  Tartan was one of the generals of Sennacherib, who, it is probable, is here called Sargon, and in the book of Tobit, Sacherdonus and Sacherdan, against whom Tirhakah, king of Cush or Ethiopia, was in league with the king of Egypt. <a href="../../../bib/kjv/kg2018.htm#017">Kg2 18:17</a>
