 <p>Overview
 </p><p><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa057.htm#001">Psa 57:1</a>, David in prayer fleeing unto God, complains of his dangerous case; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa057.htm#007">Psa 57:7</a>, He encourages himself to praise God.
 </p><p>not.  A golden Psalm. This Psalm is supposed to have been called al tashcheth, or "destroy not," because David thus addressed one of his followers when about to kill Saul in the cave; and michtam, or "golden," because written, or worthy to be written, in gold. <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa058.htm#001">Psa 58:1</a>, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa059.htm#001">Psa 59:1</a> *titles
