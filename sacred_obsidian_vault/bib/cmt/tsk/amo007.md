 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/amo007.htm#001">Amos 7:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">amo 7:1</a></font></span></p>
 <p>showed:  <a href="../../../bib/kjv/amo007.htm#004">Amo 7:4</a>, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/amo007.htm#007">Amo 7:7</a>, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/amo008.htm#001">Amo 8:1</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer001.htm#011">Jer 1:11-16</a>, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer024.htm#001">Jer 24:1</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze011.htm#025">Eze 11:25</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/zac001.htm#020">Zac 1:20</a> 
 </p><p>he:  <a href="../../../bib/kjv/amo004.htm#009">Amo 4:9</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo010.htm#012">Exo 10:12-16</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/isa033.htm#004">Isa 33:4</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/joe001.htm#004">Joe 1:4</a>, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/joe002.htm#025">Joe 2:25</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/nah003.htm#015">Nah 3:15-17</a> 
 </p><p>grasshoppers:  or, green worms, Govai in Arabic gabee "locusts," probably in their caterpillar state, in which they are most destructive.  This is supposed to have been an emblem of the first invasion of the Assyrians.
 </p><p>mowings:  Or rather, feedings or grazings, as the people of the East make no hay.  This was probably in the month of March, which is the only time of the year that the Arabs to this day feed their horses with grass.
