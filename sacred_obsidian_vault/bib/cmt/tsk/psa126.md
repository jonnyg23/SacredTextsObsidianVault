 <p>Overview
 </p><p><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa126.htm#001">Psa 126:1</a>, The church, celebrating her incredible return out of captivity, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa126.htm#004">Psa 126:4</a>, prays for, and prophesies the good success thereof.
