 <p>Overview
 </p><p><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa060.htm#001">Psa 60:1</a>, David, complaining to God of former judgment, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa060.htm#004">Psa 60:4</a>, now upon better hope, prays for deliverance; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa060.htm#006">Psa 60:6</a>, Comforting himself in God's promises, he craves that help whereon he trusts.
 </p><p>Probably a hexachord harp, or lute; for aiduth appears to be the same as the Arabic ood, a harp or lute; concerning shushan.
