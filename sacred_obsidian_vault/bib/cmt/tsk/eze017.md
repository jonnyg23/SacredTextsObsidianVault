 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze001.htm#001">Ezekiel</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_000">eze 17:0</a></font></span></p>
 <p>Overview
 </p><p><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze017.htm#001">Eze 17:1</a>, Under the parable of two eagles and a vine, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze017.htm#011">Eze 17:11</a>. is shewn God's judgment upon Jerusalem for revolting from Babylon to Egypt; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze017.htm#022">Eze 17:22</a>, God promises to plant the cedar of the Gospel. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze017.htm#002">Ezekiel 17:2</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_002">eze 17:2</a></font></span></p>
 <p><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze020.htm#049">Eze 20:49</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/jdg009.htm#008">Jdg 9:8-15</a>, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/jdg014.htm#012">Jdg 14:12-19</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa2012.htm#001">Sa2 12:1-4</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/hos012.htm#010">Hos 12:10</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat013.htm#013">Mat 13:13</a>, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat013.htm#014">Mat 13:14</a>, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat013.htm#035">Mat 13:35</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/mar004.htm#033">Mar 4:33</a>, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/mar004.htm#034">Mar 4:34</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/co1013.htm#012">Co1 13:12</a> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze017.htm#003">Ezekiel 17:3</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_003">eze 17:3</a></font></span></p>
 <p>A great:  Nebuchadnezzar, so called from his towering ambition and rapaciousness. <a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze017.htm#007">Eze 17:7</a>, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze017.htm#012">Eze 17:12-21</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/deu028.htm#049">Deu 28:49</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer004.htm#013">Jer 4:13</a>, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer048.htm#040">Jer 48:40</a>, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer049.htm#016">Jer 49:16</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/lam004.htm#019">Lam 4:19</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/hos008.htm#001">Hos 8:1</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat024.htm#028">Mat 24:28</a> 
 </p><p>great wings:  Extensive empire, both in length and breadth. <a href="../../../bib/kjv/dan002.htm#038">Dan 2:38</a>, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/dan004.htm#022">Dan 4:22</a>, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/dan007.htm#004">Dan 7:4</a> 
 </p><p>full:  Numerous subjects, of various nations, and of different languages and manners.
 </p><p>divers colours:  Heb. embroidering
 </p><p>came:  Came against Judah and Jerusalem. <a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze017.htm#012">Eze 17:12</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/kg2024.htm#010">Kg2 24:10-16</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch2036.htm#009">Ch2 36:9</a>, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch2036.htm#010">Ch2 36:10</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer022.htm#023">Jer 22:23-28</a>, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer024.htm#001">Jer 24:1</a> 
 </p><p>the highest:  Jeconiah, whom he took captive to Babylon.
 </p><p>the cedar:  The royal and ancient family of David. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze017.htm#004">Ezekiel 17:4</a></p>

 <p>the top:  The princes of Judah.
 </p><p>into:  <a href="../../../bib/kjv/isa043.htm#014">Isa 43:14</a>, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/isa047.htm#015">Isa 47:15</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer051.htm#013">Jer 51:13</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/rev018.htm#003">Rev 18:3</a>, <a href="../../../bib/kjv/rev018.htm#011">Rev 18:11-19</a> 
 </p><p>a land:  Chaldea
 </p><p>a city:  Babylon, which by means of the Euphrates and Tigris, had communications with the richest and most distant nations.

 </p><p>whose:  The Jewish state had then no height of dominion; and Zedekiah was wholly dependent on Nebuchadnezzar. 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze017.htm#007">Ezekiel 17:7</a></p>
