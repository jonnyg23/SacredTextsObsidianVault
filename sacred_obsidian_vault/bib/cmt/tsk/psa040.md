 <p>am 2970, bc 1034 (Title), This psalm is supposed to have been composed by David about the same time, and on the same occasion, as the two preceding; with this difference, that here he magnifies God for have obtained the mercy which he sought there.  It also contains a remarkable prophecy of the incarnation and sacrifice of Jesus Christ.
