It is now called Gibyle, of Djebail, situated about a day's journey south of Tripoli.  Its walls are about a mile in circumference, with square towers about every forty yards' distance.  Anciently it must have been a place of no mean extent and of considerable beauty, from the ruins still visible. <a href="../../../bib/kjv/kg1005.htm#018">Kg1 5:18</a> *marg. <a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa083.htm#007">Psa 83:7</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze027.htm#009">Eze 27:9</a>
