 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Scofield Bible Commentary</i>, by Cyrus Ingerson Scofield, [1917], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk017.htm#001">Luke Chapter 17</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk017.htm#004">Luke 17:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">luk 17:4</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>trespass 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Sin 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom003.htm#023">Rom 3:23</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk017.htm#020">Luke 17:20</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_020">luk 17:20</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>observation 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Or, outward show.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk017.htm#021">Luke 17:21</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_021">luk 17:21</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>within you 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>in the midst of. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(Greek, "entos", means "in the midst"). It could not be said of a self-righteous, Christ rejecting pharisee, that the kingdom of God, as to its spiritual content, was within him. Our Lord's whole answer, designedly enigmatic to the Pharisees (cf) (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat013.htm#010">Mat 13:10-13</a>) had a dispensational meaning. The kingdom in its outward form, as covenanted to David (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa2007.htm#008">Sa2 7:8-17</a>) and described by the prophets 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/zac012.htm#008">Zac 12:8</a>) 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>had been rejected by the Jews; so that, during this present age, it would not "come with observation" (literally, "outward show"), but in the hearts of men. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Compare (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk019.htm#011">Luk 19:11</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk019.htm#012">Luk 19:12</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/act001.htm#006">Act 1:6-8</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/act001.htm#006">Act 1:6</a>). 
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom014.htm#017">Rom 14:17</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Meantime, the kingdom was actually "in the midst" of the Pharisees in the persons of the King and His disciples.
