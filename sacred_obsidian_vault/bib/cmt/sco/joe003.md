 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/joe003.htm#009">Joel 3:9</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_009">joe 3:9</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>Prepare war 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Verses 9-14 (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/joe003.htm#009">Joe 3:9-14</a>) refer to Armageddon; verses 15, 16 (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/joe003.htm#015">Joe 3:15-16</a>) are parallel with (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/joe002.htm#030">Joe 2:30-32</a>). From verses 9 to 16 (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/joe003.htm#009">Joe 3:9-16</a>), we have a resume of (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/joe002.htm#009">Joe 2:9-32</a>).
