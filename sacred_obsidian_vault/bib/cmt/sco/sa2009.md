 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Scofield Bible Commentary</i>, by Cyrus Ingerson Scofield, [1917], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa2009.htm#001">2 Kings (2 Samuel) Chapter 9</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa2009.htm#001">2 Kings (2 Samuel) 9:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">sa2 9:1</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>kindness 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>A lovely picture of salvation by grace. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(1) What grace is -- kindness to a helpless one for another's sake, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa2001.htm#001">Sa2 1:1-3</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/jo1002.htm#012">Jo1 2:12</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(2) Grace gives the highest place, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa2001.htm#011">Sa2 1:11</a>). (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/eph001.htm#001">Eph 1:1-6</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(3) Grace keeps the saved one, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa2001.htm#013">Sa2 1:13</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/joh010.htm#028">Joh 10:28</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/joh010.htm#029">Joh 10:29</a>).
