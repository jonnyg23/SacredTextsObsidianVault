 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/dan010.htm#004">Daniel 10:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">dan 10:4</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>first month 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>That is, April.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/dan010.htm#006">Daniel 10:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">dan 10:6</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>His body 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>The theophanies. (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/dan010.htm#010">Dan 10:10</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/dan010.htm#018">Dan 10:18</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze040.htm#003">Eze 40:3</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/gen012.htm#007">Gen 12:7</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rev001.htm#009">Rev 1:9</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/dan010.htm#010">Dan 10:10</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>
 </p><p>beryl 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Chrysolite. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Compare (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze001.htm#016">Eze 1:16</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/dan010.htm#010">Daniel 10:10</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_010">dan 10:10</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>And, behold 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Verses (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/dan010.htm#010">Dan 10:10-15</a>); introduce an angel. The theophany begins again at (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/dan010.htm#016">Dan 10:16</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/dan010.htm#013">Daniel 10:13</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_013">dan 10:13</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>prince of (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/dan010.htm#020">Dan 10:20</a>) 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>The intimation is clear that as the holy angels are sent forth in behalf of the heirs of salvation, so demons are concerned in behalf of the world-system of Satan. (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/joh007.htm#007">Joh 7:7</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rev013.htm#008">Rev 13:8</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/dan010.htm#018">Daniel 10:18</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_018">dan 10:18</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>a man 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>The theophanies. (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/dan008.htm#015">Dan 8:15</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze040.htm#003">Eze 40:3</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/gen012.htm#007">Gen 12:7</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rev001.htm#009">Rev 1:9</a>).
