 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Scofield Bible Commentary</i>, by Cyrus Ingerson Scofield, [1917], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jam001.htm#001">James Chapter 1</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jam001.htm#004">James 1:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">jam 1:4</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>perfect 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Mature and complete. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat005.htm#048">Mat 5:48</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jam001.htm#014">James 1:14</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_014">jam 1:14</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>tempted 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>"Temptation" is used in two senses: 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(1) Solicitation to evil, (for example, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/gen003.htm#001">Gen 3:1-6</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat004.htm#001">Mat 4:1</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/co1010.htm#013">Co1 10:13</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/co2011.htm#003">Co2 11:3</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/co2011.htm#004">Co2 11:4</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/jam001.htm#014">Jam 1:14</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(2) Testing under trial, (for example, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/gen022.htm#001">Gen 22:1</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk022.htm#028">Luk 22:28</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Compare (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk004.htm#002">Luk 4:2</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Compare (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat006.htm#013">Mat 6:13</a>); (solicitation to evil) and (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/pe1001.htm#006">Pe1 1:6</a>); (testing under trial).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jam001.htm#015">James 1:15</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_015">jam 1:15</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>sin 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Sin 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom003.htm#023">Rom 3:23</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jam001.htm#017">James 1:17</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_017">jam 1:17</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>gift 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Two words are used in the original for "gift": 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>the first meaning the act of giving; 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>the second meaning the thing given.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jam001.htm#020">James 1:20</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_020">jam 1:20</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>righteousness 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom003.htm#021">Rom 3:21</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jam001.htm#021">James 1:21</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_021">jam 1:21</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>save 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom001.htm#016">Rom 1:16</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jam001.htm#026">James 1:26</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_026">jam 1:26</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>religious 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(Greek, "thrēskos", means "outwardly religious").  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jam001.htm#027">James 1:27</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_027">jam 1:27</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>world 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Greek, "kosmos" means "world-system". (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/jam004.htm#004">Jam 4:4</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/joh007.htm#007">Joh 7:7</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rev013.htm#008">Rev 13:8</a>).
