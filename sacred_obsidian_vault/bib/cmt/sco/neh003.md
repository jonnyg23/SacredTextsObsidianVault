 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Scofield Bible Commentary</i>, by Cyrus Ingerson Scofield, [1917], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/neh003.htm#001">Nehemiah Chapter 3</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/neh003.htm#001">Nehemiah 3:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">neh 3:1</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>sheep 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>The sheep for sacrifice were brought in here. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/joh005.htm#002">Joh 5:2</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>
 </p><p>tower 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>The towers appear to have been on either side the sheep gate.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/neh003.htm#026">Nehemiah 3:26</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_026">neh 3:26</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>Nethinims 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>That is, Dedicated (persons), probably descendants of the Gibeonites (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa2021.htm#001">Sa2 21:1-3</a>) devoted to the service of the Levites. But see (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/jos009.htm#017">Jos 9:17-21</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/neh003.htm#027">Nehemiah 3:27</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_027">neh 3:27</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>Ophel 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>translated, "tower," (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/kg2005.htm#024">Kg2 5:24</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch2027.htm#003">Ch2 27:3</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch2033.htm#014">Ch2 33:14</a>). Perhaps part of the fort called Millo, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/kg1009.htm#015">Kg1 9:15</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch2032.htm#005">Ch2 32:5</a>).
