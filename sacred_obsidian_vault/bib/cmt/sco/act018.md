 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Scofield Bible Commentary</i>, by Cyrus Ingerson Scofield, [1917], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/act018.htm#001">Acts Chapter 18</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/act018.htm#005">Acts 18:5</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_005">act 18:5</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>pressed 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Or, constrained by the Word. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Compare (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/co2005.htm#014">Co2 5:14</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/act018.htm#007">Acts 18:7</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_007">act 18:7</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>Justus 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Titus Justus.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/act018.htm#017">Acts 18:17</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_017">act 18:17</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>cared for 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Contrast (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/joh019.htm#013">Joh 19:13-16</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/act024.htm#026">Act 24:26</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/act024.htm#027">Act 24:27</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/act018.htm#025">Acts 18:25</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_025">act 18:25</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>instructed 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Taught by word of mouth, or, hearsay, that is, not by revelation. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Compare (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/gal001.htm#011">Gal 1:11</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/gal001.htm#012">Gal 1:12</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>The New Testament Scriptures were not then written.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/act018.htm#028">Acts 18:28</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_028">act 18:28</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>that Jesus 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Apollos' ministry seems to have gone no further; Jesus was the long expected Messiah. Of Paul's doctrine of justification through the blood, and sanctification through the Spirit, he seems at that time to have known nothing. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>See (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/act019.htm#003">Act 19:3-6</a>).
