 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom010.htm#001">Romans 10:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">rom 10:1</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>saved 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom001.htm#016">Rom 1:16</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom010.htm#003">Romans 10:3</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_003">rom 10:3</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>righteousness 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>The word "righteousness" here, and in the passages having marginal references to this, means "legal", or "self-righteousness"; the futile effort of man to work out under law a character which God can approve. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rev019.htm#008">Rev 19:8</a>). 
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom003.htm#021">Rom 3:21</a>).

 <p>
 </p><p>saved 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom001.htm#016">Rom 1:16</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom010.htm#010">Romans 10:10</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_010">rom 10:10</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>righteousness 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Righteousness here, and in the passages which refer to (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom010.htm#010">Rom 10:10</a>); means that "righteousness of God" which is judicially reckoned to all who believe on the Lord Jesus Christ; believers are the "righteous". 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom003.htm#021">Rom 3:21</a>).
