 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Scofield Bible Commentary</i>, by Cyrus Ingerson Scofield, [1917], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer007.htm#001">Jeremiah Chapter 7</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer007.htm#001">Jeremiah 7:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">jer 7:1</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>The word that came 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>The general character of the message in the temple gate is, like the first and second messages, one of rebuke, warning, and exhortation, but this message is addressed more to such in Judah as still maintaining outwardly the worship of Jehovah; it is a message to religious Judah, for example (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer007.htm#002">Jer 7:2</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer007.htm#009">Jer 7:9</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer007.htm#010">Jer 7:10</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer009.htm#010">Jer 9:10</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer009.htm#011">Jer 9:11</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer007.htm#008">Jeremiah 7:8</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_008">jer 7:8</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>trust 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa002.htm#012">Psa 2:12</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer007.htm#022">Jeremiah 7:22</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_022">jer 7:22</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>nor commanded 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo020.htm#004">Exo 20:4</a>), note 2, 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>the threefold giving of the law. The command concerning burnt-offerings and sacrifices was not given to the people till they had broken the decalogue, the law of obedience.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer007.htm#033">Jeremiah 7:33</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_033">jer 7:33</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>carcases 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>See (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer019.htm#011">Jer 19:11</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/kg2023.htm#010">Kg2 23:10</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze006.htm#005">Eze 6:5</a>). Fulfilled in part in all the destructions of Jerusalem, but with a final look toward (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rev019.htm#017">Rev 19:17-21</a>).
