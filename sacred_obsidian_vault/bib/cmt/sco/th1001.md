 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Scofield Bible Commentary</i>, by Cyrus Ingerson Scofield, [1917], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/th1001.htm#001">1 Thessalonians Chapter 1</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/th1001.htm#003">1 Thessalonians 1:3</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_003">th1 1:3</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>work of faith 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Literally, operative faith, and laborious love, and hope-filled patience.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/th1001.htm#009">1 Thessalonians 1:9</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_009">th1 1:9</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>how ye turned 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>The tenses of the believer's life here indicated are logical and give the true order. They occur also in (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/th1001.htm#003">Th1 1:3</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>The "work of faith" is to "turn to God from idols"; 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(compare (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/joh006.htm#028">Joh 6:28</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/joh006.htm#029">Joh 6:29</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>The "labour of love" is to "serve the living and true God"; and 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>The "patience of hope" is to "wait for his Son from heaven". 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(compare (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat024.htm#042">Mat 24:42</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat025.htm#013">Mat 25:13</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk012.htm#036">Luk 12:36-48</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/act001.htm#011">Act 1:11</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/phi003.htm#020">Phi 3:20</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/phi003.htm#021">Phi 3:21</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Paul repeats this threefold sequence in (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/tit002.htm#011">Tit 2:11-13</a>).
