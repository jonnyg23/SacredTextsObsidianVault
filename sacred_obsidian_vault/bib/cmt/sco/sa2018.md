 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa2018.htm#018">2 Kings (2 Samuel) 18:18</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_018">sa2 18:18</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>I have no son 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>The pillar mentioned must have been reared before the birth of sons to Absalom. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Compare (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa2014.htm#027">Sa2 14:27</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Another view is that his sons died in youth. They are not mentioned in the genealogies.
