 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo040.htm#002">Exodus 40:2</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_002">exo 40:2</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>first month 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>That is, April.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo040.htm#034">Exodus 40:34</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_034">exo 40:34</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>glory 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Compare (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/eph002.htm#022">Eph 2:22</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>What the shekinah glory was to tabernacle and temple, that the Spirit is to the "holy temple," the Church, and to the temple which is the believer's body. (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/co1006.htm#019">Co1 6:19</a>).
