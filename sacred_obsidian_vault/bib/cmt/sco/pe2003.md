 <p>
 </p><p>grace 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Grace (imparted). Summary (see "Grace"). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/joh001.htm#017">Joh 1:17</a>) 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Grace is not only dispensationally a method of divine dealing in salvation, 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/joh001.htm#017">Joh 1:17</a>), 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>but is also the method of God in the believer's life and service. As saved, he is "not under the law, but under grace". (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom006.htm#014">Rom 6:14</a>). Having by grace brought the believer into the highest conceivable position. (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/eph001.htm#006">Eph 1:6</a>). God ceaselessly works through grace, to impart to, and perfect in him, corresponding graces. (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/joh015.htm#004">Joh 15:4</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/joh015.htm#005">Joh 15:5</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/gal005.htm#022">Gal 5:22</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/gal005.htm#023">Gal 5:23</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Grace, therefore, stands connected: 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>with service; (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom012.htm#006">Rom 12:6</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom015.htm#015">Rom 15:15</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom015.htm#016">Rom 15:16</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/co1001.htm#003">Co1 1:3-7</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/co1003.htm#010">Co1 3:10</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/co1015.htm#010">Co1 15:10</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/co2012.htm#009">Co2 12:9</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/co2012.htm#010">Co2 12:10</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/gal002.htm#009">Gal 2:9</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/eph003.htm#007">Eph 3:7</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/eph003.htm#008">Eph 3:8</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/eph004.htm#007">Eph 4:7</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/phi001.htm#007">Phi 1:7</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/ti2002.htm#001">Ti2 2:1</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/ti2002.htm#002">Ti2 2:2</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/pe1004.htm#010">Pe1 4:10</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>with Christian growth; (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/co2001.htm#012">Co2 1:12</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/eph004.htm#029">Eph 4:29</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/col003.htm#016">Col 3:16</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/col004.htm#006">Col 4:6</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/th2001.htm#012">Th2 1:12</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/heb004.htm#016">Heb 4:16</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/heb012.htm#028">Heb 12:28</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/heb012.htm#029">Heb 12:29</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/heb013.htm#009">Heb 13:9</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/jam004.htm#006">Jam 4:6</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/pe1001.htm#002">Pe1 1:2</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/pe1003.htm#007">Pe1 3:7</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/pe1005.htm#005">Pe1 5:5</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/pe1005.htm#010">Pe1 5:10</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/pe2003.htm#018">Pe2 3:18</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/jde001.htm#004">Jde 1:4</a>); and 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>with giving; (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/co2004.htm#015">Co2 4:15</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/co2008.htm#001">Co2 8:1</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/co2008.htm#006">Co2 8:6</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/co2008.htm#007">Co2 8:7</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/co2008.htm#019">Co2 8:19</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/co2009.htm#014">Co2 9:14</a>).
