 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_012">joh 16:12</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>I have yet 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Christ's pre-authentication of the New Testament: 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(1) he expressly declared that He would leave "many things" unrevealed (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/joh016.htm#012">Joh 16:12</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(2) He promised that this revelation should be completed ("all things") after the Spirit should come, and that such additional revelation should include new prophecies (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/joh016.htm#012">Joh 16:12</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(3) He chose certain persons to receive such additional revelations, and to be His witnesses to them (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat028.htm#019">Mat 28:19</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/joh015.htm#027">Joh 15:27</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/joh016.htm#013">Joh 16:13</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/act001.htm#008">Act 1:8</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/act009.htm#015">Act 9:15-17</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(4) he gave to their words when speaking for Him in the Spirit precisely the same authority as His own (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat010.htm#014">Mat 10:14</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat010.htm#015">Mat 10:15</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk010.htm#016">Luk 10:16</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/joh013.htm#020">Joh 13:20</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/joh017.htm#020">Joh 17:20</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>See, for example, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/co1014.htm#037">Co1 14:37</a>) and

 </p><p>"Inspiration,"; (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo004.htm#015">Exo 4:15</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rev022.htm#019">Rev 22:19</a>).
