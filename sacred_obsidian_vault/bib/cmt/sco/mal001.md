 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Scofield Bible Commentary</i>, by Cyrus Ingerson Scofield, [1917], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/mal001.htm#001">Malachi Chapter 1</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/mal001.htm#004">Malachi 1:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">mal 1:4</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>Edom 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>That is, Esau's descendants. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>See (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/gen025.htm#030">Gen 25:30</a>). 
 </p><p> 
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/mal001.htm#006">Malachi 1:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">mal 1:6</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>if then I be a father 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/isa063.htm#016">Isa 63:16</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>The relationship here is national, not personal (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer003.htm#018">Jer 3:18</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer003.htm#019">Jer 3:19</a>) here, apparently, the Jews were calling Jehovah, "Father," but yielding Him no filial obedience. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>See; (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/joh008.htm#037">Joh 8:37-39</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom009.htm#001">Rom 9:1-8</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/mal001.htm#010">Malachi 1:10</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_010">mal 1:10</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>Who is there 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Or, I would that one among you would shut the doors of the temple that no more vain fire should kindle on mine altar. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Compare (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/isa001.htm#011">Isa 1:11-15</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/mal001.htm#011">Malachi 1:11</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_011">mal 1:11</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>For from the raising 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>That is, so it would have been had Israel been true. (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/isa045.htm#005">Isa 45:5</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/isa045.htm#006">Isa 45:6</a>). So it shall be despite Israel's failure.
