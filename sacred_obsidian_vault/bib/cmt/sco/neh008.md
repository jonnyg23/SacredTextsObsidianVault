 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Scofield Bible Commentary</i>, by Cyrus Ingerson Scofield, [1917], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/neh008.htm#001">Nehemiah Chapter 8</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/neh008.htm#002">Nehemiah 8:2</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_002">neh 8:2</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>seventh month 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>That is, October; (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/neh008.htm#002">Neh 8:2</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/neh008.htm#014">Neh 8:14</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/neh008.htm#017">Nehemiah 8:17</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_017">neh 8:17</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>for since the days of Jeshua 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>It is not meant that there had not been some formal observance of the feast of tabernacles (cf) (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch2008.htm#013">Ch2 8:13</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze003.htm#004">Eze 3:4</a>), but that the people had not dwelt in booths since Joshua's days.
