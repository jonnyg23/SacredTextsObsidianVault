 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/amo004.htm#004">Amos 4:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">amo 4:4</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>Beth-el 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Compare (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/kg1012.htm#025">Kg1 12:25-33</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Any altar at Beth-el, after the establishment of Jehovah's worship at Jerusalem was of necessity divisive and schismatic. (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/deu012.htm#004">Deu 12:4-14</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Compare (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/joh004.htm#021">Joh 4:21-24</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat018.htm#020">Mat 18:20</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/heb013.htm#010">Heb 13:10-14</a>).
