 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze043.htm#019">Ezekiel 43:19</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_019">eze 43:19</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>and thou shalt 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>doubtless these offerings will be memorial, looking back to the cross, as the offerings under the old covenant were anticipatory, looking forward to the cross. In neither case have animal sacrifices power to put away sin. (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/heb010.htm#004">Heb 10:4</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom003.htm#025">Rom 3:25</a>).
