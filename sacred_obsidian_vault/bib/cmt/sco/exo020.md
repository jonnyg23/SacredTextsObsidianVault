 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Scofield Bible Commentary</i>, by Cyrus Ingerson Scofield, [1917], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo020.htm#001">Exodus Chapter 20</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo020.htm#004">Exodus 20:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">exo 20:4</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>shalt not make 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>There is a threefold giving of the law. First, orally, in (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo020.htm#001">Exo 20:1-17</a>). This was pure law, with no provision of priesthood and sacrifice for failure, and was accompanied by the "judgments"; (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo021.htm#001">Exo 21:1-23</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo021.htm#013">Exo 21:13</a>); relating to the relations of Hebrew with Hebrew; to which were added (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo023.htm#014">Exo 23:14-19</a>) directions for keeping three annual feasts, and (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo023.htm#020">Exo 23:20-33</a>) instructions for the conquest of Canaan. These words Moses communicated to the people. (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo024.htm#003">Exo 24:3-8</a>). Immediately, in the persons of their elders, they were admitted to the fellowship of God. (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo024.htm#009">Exo 24:9-11</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Second, Moses was then called up to receive the tables of stone. (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo024.htm#012">Exo 24:12-18</a>). The story then divides. Moses, in the mount, receives the gracious instructions concerning the tabernacle, priesthood, and sacrifice (Exodus 25-31) Meantime (Exodus 32), the people, led by Aaron, break the first commandment. Moses, returning, breaks the tables "written with the finger of God."; (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo031.htm#018">Exo 31:18</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo032.htm#016">Exo 32:16-19</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Third, the second tables were made by Moses, and the law again written by the hand of Jehovah (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo034.htm#001">Exo 34:1</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo034.htm#028">Exo 34:28</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo034.htm#029">Exo 34:29</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/deu010.htm#004">Deu 10:4</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo020.htm#018">Exodus 20:18</a></p>
