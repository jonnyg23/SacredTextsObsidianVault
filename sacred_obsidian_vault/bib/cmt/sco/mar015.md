 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/mar015.htm#016">Mark 15:16</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_016">mar 15:16</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>the hall 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Or, the court which is the judgment-hall.

 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_017">mar 15:17</a></font></span></p>

 </p><p>platted 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>plaited.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/mar015.htm#021">Mark 15:21</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_021">mar 15:21</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>Rufus 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>It is possible that this may be the same Rufus mentioned in (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom016.htm#013">Rom 16:13</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/mar015.htm#023">Mark 15:23</a></p>

 <p>
 </p><p>drink 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>The stupefying drink usually given to those crucified.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/mar015.htm#024">Mark 15:24</a></p>

 </p><p>See note for order of events at the crucifixion.

 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat027.htm#033">Mat 27:33</a>).

 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_025">mar 15:25</a></font></span></p>

 </p><p>third hour 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Compare (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/joh019.htm#014">Joh 19:14</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>John used the Roman, Mark the Hebrew, computation of time.
