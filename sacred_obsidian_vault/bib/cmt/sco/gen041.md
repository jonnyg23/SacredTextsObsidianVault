 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/gen041.htm#045">Genesis 41:45</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_045">gen 41:45</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>Zaphnath-paaneah 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Asenath, the Gentile bride espoused by Joseph the rejected one (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/joh019.htm#015">Joh 19:15</a>) type of the Church, called out from the Gentiles to be the bride of Christ during the time of His rejection by His brethren, Israel. (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/act015.htm#014">Act 15:14</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/eph005.htm#031">Eph 5:31</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/eph005.htm#032">Eph 5:32</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/gen037.htm#002">Gen 37:2</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Zaphnath-paaneah Coptic, revealer of secret things.
