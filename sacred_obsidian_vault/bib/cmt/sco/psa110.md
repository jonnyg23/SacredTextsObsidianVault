 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Scofield Bible Commentary</i>, by Cyrus Ingerson Scofield, [1917], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa110.htm#001">Psalms Chapter 110</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa110.htm#001">Psalms 110:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">psa 110:1</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>The Lord said unto my Lord 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>The importance of Psalm 110 is attested by the remarkable prominence given to it in the New Testament . 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(1) It affirms the deity of Jesus, thus answering those who deny the full divine meaning of his New Testament Title of "Lord." (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat022.htm#041">Mat 22:41-45</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mar012.htm#035">Mar 12:35-37</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk020.htm#041">Luk 20:41-44</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/act002.htm#034">Act 2:34</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/act002.htm#035">Act 2:35</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/heb001.htm#013">Heb 1:13</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/heb010.htm#012">Heb 10:12</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/heb010.htm#013">Heb 10:13</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(2) This Psalm announces the eternal priesthood of Messiah -- one of the most important statements of Scripture (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa110.htm#004">Psa 110:4</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/gen014.htm#018">Gen 14:18</a>). 
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/heb005.htm#006">Heb 5:6</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/heb007.htm#001">Heb 7:1-28</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/ti1002.htm#005">Ti1 2:5-6</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/joh014.htm#006">Joh 14:6</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(3) Historically, the Psalm begins with the ascension of Christ (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa110.htm#001">Psa 110:1</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/joh020.htm#017">Joh 20:17</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/act007.htm#056">Act 7:56</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rev003.htm#021">Rev 3:21</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(4) Prophetically, the Psalm looks on 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>    (a) to the time when Christ will appear as the Rod of Jehovah's strength, the Deliverer out of Zion. (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom011.htm#025">Rom 11:25-27</a>) and the conversion of Israel; (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa110.htm#003">Psa 110:3</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/joe002.htm#027">Joe 2:27</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/zac013.htm#009">Zac 13:9</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/deu030.htm#001">Deu 30:1-9</a> 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/deu030.htm#003">Deu 30:3</a>), 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>and 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>
 </p><p>    (b) to the judgment upon the Gentile powers which precedes the setting up of the kingdom (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa110.htm#005">Psa 110:5</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa110.htm#006">Psa 110:6</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/joe003.htm#009">Joe 3:9-17</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/zac014.htm#001">Zac 14:1-4</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rev019.htm#011">Rev 19:11-21</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>See "Armageddon" (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rev016.htm#014">Rev 16:14</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rev019.htm#017">Rev 19:17</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>"Israel" (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/gen012.htm#002">Gen 12:2</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/gen012.htm#003">Gen 12:3</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom011.htm#026">Rom 11:26</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>"Kingdom" 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/zac012.htm#008">Zac 12:8</a>). 
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/co1015.htm#024">Co1  15:24</a>). 
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa002.htm#006">Psa 2:6</a>). 
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa118.htm#022">Psa 118:22</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>See Psalm 118, last in order of the Messianic Psalms.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa110.htm#004">Psalms 110:4</a></p>

 </p><p>
