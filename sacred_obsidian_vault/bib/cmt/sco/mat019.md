 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat019.htm#004">Matthew 19:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">mat 19:4</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>Have ye not read 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>See (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/gen001.htm#027">Gen 1:27</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/gen002.htm#023">Gen 2:23</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/gen002.htm#024">Gen 2:24</a>).

 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat019.htm#028">Matthew 19:28</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_028">mat 19:28</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>regeneration 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(Greek, "palingensia", means "re-creation," "making new)." The word occurs once again in (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/tit003.htm#005">Tit 3:5</a>). There it refers to the new birth or a believing person; here to the re-creation of the social order, and renewal of the earth; (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/isa011.htm#006">Isa 11:6-9</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom008.htm#019">Rom 8:19-23</a>) when the kingdom shall come. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See "Kingdom (Old Testament)," 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/zac012.htm#008">Zac 12:8</a>). 
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/co1015.htm#024">Co1 15:24</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>
 </p><p>judging 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>disclosing how the promise (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/isa001.htm#026">Isa 1:26</a>) will be fulfilled when the kingdom is set up. The kingdom will be administered over Israel through the apostles, according to the ancient theocratic judgeship. (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/jdg002.htm#018">Jdg 2:18</a>).
