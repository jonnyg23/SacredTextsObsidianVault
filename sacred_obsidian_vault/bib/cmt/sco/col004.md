 <p>
 </p><p>Epaphras 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>A touching illustration of priestly service, 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/pe1002.htm#009">Pe1 2:9</a>), 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>as distinguished from ministry of gift. Shut up in prison, no longer able to preach, Epaphras was still, equally with all believers, a priest. No prison could keep him from the throne of grace, so he gave himself wholly to the priestly work of intercession. 
 </p><p>
