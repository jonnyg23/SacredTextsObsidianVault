 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Scofield Bible Commentary</i>, by Cyrus Ingerson Scofield, [1917], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/isa015.htm#001">Isaiah Chapter 15</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/isa015.htm#001">Isaiah 15:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">isa 15:1</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>Moab 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>This "burden" had a precursive fulfilment in Sennacherib's invasion, B.C. 704, three years after the prediction (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/isa016.htm#014">Isa 16:14</a>) but the words have a breadth of meaning which includes also the final world-battle. (See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rev019.htm#017">Rev 19:17</a>), (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/isa016.htm#001">Isa 16:1-5</a>) which is a continuation of this "burden," shows the "tabernacle of David" set up, the next event in order after the destruction of the Beast and his armies. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Compare the order in; (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/isa010.htm#028">Isa 10:28-34</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/isa011.htm#001">Isa 11:1-10</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/act015.htm#014">Act 15:14-17</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rev019.htm#017">Rev 19:17-21</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rev020.htm#001">Rev 20:1-4</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>
 </p><p>burden 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>See note 1; 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/isa013.htm#001">Isa 13:1</a>).
