 </p><p>

 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/co2005.htm#010">2 Corinthians 5:10</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_010">co2 5:10</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>For we must all appear 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>The judgment of the believer's works, not sins, is in question here. These have been atoned for, and are "remembered no more forever" (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/heb010.htm#017">Heb 10:17</a>) but every work must come into judgment, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat012.htm#036">Mat 12:36</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom014.htm#010">Rom 14:10</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/gal006.htm#007">Gal 6:7</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/eph006.htm#008">Eph 6:8</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/col003.htm#024">Col 3:24</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/col003.htm#025">Col 3:25</a>). The result is "reward" or "loss" (of the reward), "but he himself shall be saved". (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/co1003.htm#011">Co1 3:11-15</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>This judgment occurs at the return of Christ (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat016.htm#027">Mat 16:27</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk014.htm#014">Luk 14:14</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/co1004.htm#005">Co1 4:5</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/ti2004.htm#008">Ti2 4:8</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rev022.htm#012">Rev 22:12</a>).
