 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Scofield Bible Commentary</i>, by Cyrus Ingerson Scofield, [1917], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/th2002.htm#001">2 Thessalonians Chapter 2</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/th2002.htm#002">2 Thessalonians 2:2</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_002">th2 2:2</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>day of 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/isa002.htm#012">Isa 2:12</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/th2002.htm#003">2 Thessalonians 2:3</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_003">th2 2:3</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>for that day 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>The order of events is: 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(1) The working of the mystery of lawlessness under divine restraint which had already begun in the apostle's time (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/th2002.htm#007">Th2 2:7</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(2) the apostasy of the professing church (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/th2001.htm#003">Th2 1:3</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk018.htm#008">Luk 18:8</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/ti2003.htm#001">Ti2 3:1-8</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(3) the removal of that which restrains the mystery of lawlessness (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/th2002.htm#006">Th2 2:6</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/th2002.htm#007">Th2 2:7</a>). The restrainer is a person -- "he," and since a "mystery" always implies a supernatural element, 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat013.htm#011">Mat 13:11</a>), 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>this Person can be none other than the Holy Spirit in the church, to be "taken out of the way"; (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/th2002.htm#007">Th2 2:7</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/th1004.htm#014">Th1 4:14-17</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(4) the manifestation of the lawless one (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/th2002.htm#008">Th2 2:8-10</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/dan007.htm#008">Dan 7:8</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/dan009.htm#027">Dan 9:27</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat024.htm#015">Mat 24:15</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rev013.htm#002">Rev 13:2-10</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(5) the coming of Christ in glory and the destruction of the lawless one (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/th2002.htm#008">Th2 2:8</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rev019.htm#011">Rev 19:11-21</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(6) the day of Jehovah (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/th2002.htm#009">Th2 2:9-12</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/isa002.htm#012">Isa 2:12</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/th2002.htm#007">2 Thessalonians 2:7</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_007">th2 2:7</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>mystery 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat013.htm#011">Mat 13:11</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/th2002.htm#010">2 Thessalonians 2:10</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_010">th2 2:10</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>saved 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom001.htm#016">Rom 1:16</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/th2002.htm#013">2 Thessalonians 2:13</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_013">th2 2:13</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>salvation 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom001.htm#016">Rom 1:16</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/th2002.htm#016">2 Thessalonians 2:16</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_016">th2 2:16</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>through grace 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Grace (in salvation). (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/ti1001.htm#014">Ti1 1:14</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/ti1001.htm#015">Ti1 1:15</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom003.htm#024">Rom 3:24</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/joh001.htm#017">Joh 1:17</a>).
