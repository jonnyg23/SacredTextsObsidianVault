 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Scofield Bible Commentary</i>, by Cyrus Ingerson Scofield, [1917], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo014.htm#001">Exodus Chapter 14</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo014.htm#019">Exodus 14:19</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_019">exo 14:19</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>angel 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/heb001.htm#004">Heb 1:4</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo014.htm#030">Exodus 14:30</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_030">exo 14:30</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>Thus the Lord saved Israel 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Redemption: (Exodus type) Summary. Exodus is the book of redemption and teaches: 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(1) redemption is wholly of God (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo003.htm#007">Exo 3:7</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo003.htm#008">Exo 3:8</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/joh003.htm#016">Joh 3:16</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(2) redemption is through a person. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo002.htm#002">Exo 2:2</a>). 
 </p><p>(<a href="../../../bib/kjv/joh003.htm#016">Joh 3:16</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/joh003.htm#017">Joh 3:17</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(3) redemption is by blood (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo012.htm#013">Exo 12:13</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo012.htm#023">Exo 12:23</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo012.htm#027">Exo 12:27</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/pe1001.htm#018">Pe1 1:18</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(4) redemption is by power (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo006.htm#006">Exo 6:6</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo013.htm#014">Exo 13:14</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom008.htm#002">Rom 8:2</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/isa059.htm#020">Isa 59:20</a>). 
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom003.htm#024">Rom 3:24</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>The blood of Christ redeems the believer from the guilt and penalty of sin. (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/pe1001.htm#018">Pe1 1:18</a>) as the power of the Spirit delivers from the dominion of sin. (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom008.htm#002">Rom 8:2</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/eph002.htm#002">Eph 2:2</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo014.htm#031">Exodus 14:31</a></p>
