 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/joh011.htm#026">John 11:26</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_026">joh 11:26</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>never die 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>That is, the "second death." 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Compare (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rev002.htm#001">Rev 2:1</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rev020.htm#006">Rev 20:6</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/joh011.htm#027">John 11:27</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_027">joh 11:27</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>world 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Greek, "kosmos", means "mankind". 
 </p><p>
