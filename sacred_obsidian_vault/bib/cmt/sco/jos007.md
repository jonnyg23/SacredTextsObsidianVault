 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Scofield Bible Commentary</i>, by Cyrus Ingerson Scofield, [1917], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jos007.htm#001">Joshua Chapter 7</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jos007.htm#011">Joshua 7:11</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_011">jos 7:11</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>Israel 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>The sin of Achan and its results teach the great truth of the oneness of the people of God, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/jos007.htm#011">Jos 7:11</a>). "Israel hath sinned." See in illustration; (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/co1005.htm#001">Co1 5:1-7</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/co1012.htm#012">Co1 12:12-14</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/co1012.htm#026">Co1 12:26</a>). The whole cause of Christ is injured by the sin, neglect, or unspirituality of one believer.
