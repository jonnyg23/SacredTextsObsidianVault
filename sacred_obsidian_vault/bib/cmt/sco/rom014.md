 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom014.htm#001">Romans 14:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">rom 14:1</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>to doubtful 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>For decisions of doubts, that is, doubts about meats, etc. The church has no authority to decide questions of personal liberty in things not expressly forbidden in Scripture. (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom014.htm#002">Rom 14:2-6</a>).
