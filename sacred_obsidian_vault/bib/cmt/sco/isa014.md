 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/isa014.htm#012">Isaiah 14:12</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_012">isa 14:12</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>son of the morning 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Verses 12-14 (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/isa014.htm#012">Isa 14:12-14</a>) evidently refer to Satan, who, as prince of this world-system (see "World," (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/joh007.htm#007">Joh 7:7</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rev013.htm#008">Rev 13:8</a>) 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>is the real unseen ruler of the successive world-powers. Tyre, Babylon, Medo-Persia, Greece, Rome, etc. (see (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze028.htm#012">Eze 28:12-14</a>) Lucifer, "day-star," can be none other that Satan. This tremendous passage marks the beginning of sin in the universe. When Lucifer said, "I will," sin began. See 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rev020.htm#010">Rev 20:10</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>See other instances of addressing Satan through another, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/gen003.htm#015">Gen 3:15</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat016.htm#022">Mat 16:22</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat016.htm#023">Mat 16:23</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/isa014.htm#015">Isaiah 14:15</a></p>

 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/isa014.htm#026">Isaiah 14:26</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_026">isa 14:26</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>whole earth 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>This universality is significant and marks the whole passage as referring, not merely to a near judgment upon Assyria, but in a yet larger sense to the final crash of the present world-system at the end of the age. (See "Times of the Gentiles," (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk021.htm#024">Luk 21:24</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rev016.htm#014">Rev 16:14</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/dan002.htm#044">Dan 2:44</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/dan002.htm#045">Dan 2:45</a>) "Armageddon,"; (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rev016.htm#014">Rev 16:14</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rev019.htm#017">Rev 19:17</a>). No other such universal catastrophe on the nations is known to Scripture.
