 <p>
 </p><p>Removed our transgression from us 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Three Hebrew words are translated, forgive, forgiven: kaphar, to cover; nasa, to lift away; salach, to send away (cf). (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev016.htm#021">Lev 16:21</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev016.htm#022">Lev 16:22</a>) the fundamental Old Testament idea of forgiveness being not the remission of penalty, but the separation of the sinner from his sin. 
 </p><p>(<a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa103.htm#012">Psa 103:12</a>) expresses this.
