 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Scofield Bible Commentary</i>, by Cyrus Ingerson Scofield, [1917], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze009.htm#001">Ezekiel Chapter 9</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze009.htm#003">Ezekiel 9:3</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_003">eze 9:3</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>was gone 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>It is noteworthy that to Ezekiel the priest was given the vision of the glory of the Lord 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(1) departing from the Cherubim to the threshold of the temple (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze009.htm#003">Eze 9:3</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze010.htm#004">Eze 10:4</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(2) from the threshold (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze010.htm#018">Eze 10:18</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(3) from temple and city to the mountain on the East of Jerusalem (Olivet, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze011.htm#023">Eze 11:23</a>) and 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(4) returning to the millennial temple to abide. (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze043.htm#002">Eze 43:2-5</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze009.htm#004">Ezekiel 9:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">eze 9:4</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>set a mark 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer015.htm#021">Jer 15:21</a>).
