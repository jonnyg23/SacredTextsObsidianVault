 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Scofield Bible Commentary</i>, by Cyrus Ingerson Scofield, [1917], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/act021.htm#001">Acts Chapter 21</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/act021.htm#004">Acts 21:4</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_004">act 21:4</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>go up to 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Literally, set foot in. Not, as in (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/act020.htm#023">Act 20:23</a>) a warning of danger, but now an imperative command. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>See (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/act022.htm#017">Act 22:17</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/act022.htm#018">Act 22:18</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/act021.htm#023">Acts 21:23</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_023">act 21:23</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>vow 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Probably according to (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/num006.htm#001">Num 6:1-7</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Compare (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/col002.htm#014">Col 2:14-17</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/act021.htm#024">Acts 21:24</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_024">act 21:24</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>charges with them 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Literally, spend something on them. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>
 </p><p>walkest orderly 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Literally, art keeping in the ranks, guarding the law. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Compare (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom010.htm#001">Rom 10:1-12</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/act021.htm#026">Acts 21:26</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_026">act 21:26</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>Paul took 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Contrast (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/act021.htm#004">Act 21:4</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/gal002.htm#002">Gal 2:2-6</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>See (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom003.htm#009">Rom 3:9</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom003.htm#010">Rom 3:10</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom003.htm#019">Rom 3:19</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom003.htm#020">Rom 3:20</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom003.htm#028">Rom 3:28</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom004.htm#003">Rom 4:3-5</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom005.htm#001">Rom 5:1-2</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom006.htm#014">Rom 6:14</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom007.htm#001">Rom 7:1-4</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom007.htm#006">Rom 7:6</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom008.htm#003">Rom 8:3</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom008.htm#004">Rom 8:4</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/gal002.htm#015">Gal 2:15</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/gal002.htm#016">Gal 2:16</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/gal002.htm#018">Gal 2:18</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/gal002.htm#019">Gal 2:19</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/gal003.htm#010">Gal 3:10</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/gal003.htm#024">Gal 3:24</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/gal003.htm#025">Gal 3:25</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/gal004.htm#009">Gal 4:9-11</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/gal004.htm#021">Gal 4:21-31</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/phi003.htm#007">Phi 3:7-9</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/heb009.htm#014">Heb 9:14</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/heb009.htm#015">Heb 9:15</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/heb009.htm#028">Heb 9:28</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/heb010.htm#001">Heb 10:1-4</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/heb010.htm#017">Heb 10:17</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/heb010.htm#018">Heb 10:18</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/heb010.htm#013">Heb 10:13</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/heb010.htm#011">Heb 10:11-14</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/act021.htm#030">Acts 21:30</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_030">act 21:30</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>drew 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>See (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/act014.htm#019">Act 14:19</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/act016.htm#019">Act 16:19</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/act021.htm#037">Acts 21:37</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_037">act 21:37</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>chief 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(Greek, "chiliarchos", "the Roman tribune"). There were six such "chief captains" in each legion of 6000 men.
