 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Scofield Bible Commentary</i>, by Cyrus Ingerson Scofield, [1917], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat015.htm#001">Matthew Chapter 15</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat015.htm#005">Matthew 15:5</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_005">mat 15:5</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>gift 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>That is, dedicated to God. (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat005.htm#023">Mat 5:23-24</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mar007.htm#011">Mar 7:11</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mar007.htm#011">Mar 7:11</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat015.htm#021">Matthew 15:21</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_021">mat 15:21</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>departed 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>The Syrophenician woman. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>For the first time, the rejected Son of David ministers to a Gentile. It is a precursive fulfilment of (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat012.htm#018">Mat 12:18</a>). Addressed by a Gentile as Son of David, He makes no reply, for a Gentile has no claim upon Him in that character. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat002.htm#002">Mat 2:2</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/eph002.htm#012">Eph 2:12</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Addressing him as "Lord," she obtained an immediate answer. (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom010.htm#012">Rom 10:12-13</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat015.htm#024">Matthew 15:24</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_024">mat 15:24</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>lost sheep 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(Greek, "apollumi"). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/joh003.htm#016">Joh 3:16</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat015.htm#028">Matthew 15:28</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_028">mat 15:28</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>great is thy faith 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>See (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk007.htm#007">Luk 7:7-9</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mar006.htm#006">Mar 6:6</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Faith honours God, knowing that he is faithful. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Compare (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/jo1005.htm#010">Jo1 5:10</a>).
