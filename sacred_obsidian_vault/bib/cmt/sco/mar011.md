 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Scofield Bible Commentary</i>, by Cyrus Ingerson Scofield, [1917], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/mar011.htm#001">Mark Chapter 11</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/mar011.htm#001">Mark 11:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">mar 11:1</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>Bethphage 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Meaning, "house of unripe figs", (see (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mar011.htm#012">Mar 11:12</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mar011.htm#020">Mar 11:20</a>), probably so called after the fig tree was cursed.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/mar011.htm#010">Mark 11:10</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_010">mar 11:10</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>kingdom 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat003.htm#002">Mat 3:2</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/mar011.htm#013">Mark 11:13</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_013">mar 11:13</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>leaves 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Fig trees which have retained their leaves through the winter usually have figs also. It was still too early for new leaves or fruit.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/mar011.htm#019">Mark 11:19</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_019">mar 11:19</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>when even 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>whenever, that is, every day when evening came.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/mar011.htm#022">Mark 11:22</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_022">mar 11:22</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>Have faith 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Have the faith of God; that is, the faith which God gives. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Compare (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/co1012.htm#009">Co1 12:9</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/eph002.htm#008">Eph 2:8</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/mar011.htm#023">Mark 11:23</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_023">mar 11:23</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>whosoever 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>See (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat017.htm#020">Mat 17:20</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk011.htm#001">Luk 11:1</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk011.htm#001">Luk 11:1</a>). (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk017.htm#006">Luk 17:6</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/joh014.htm#013">Joh 14:13</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/joh014.htm#014">Joh 14:14</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/mar011.htm#026">Mark 11:26</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_026">mar 11:26</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>if ye 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat006.htm#012">Mat 6:12</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Verse 26 (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mar011.htm#026">Mar 11:26</a>) is omitted from the best manuscripts.
