 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ti2003.htm#001">2 Timothy 3:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">ti2 3:1</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>know 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Apostasy, Summary: 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Apostasy, "falling away," is the act of professed Christians who deliberately reject revealed truth 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(1) as to the deity of Jesus Christ, and 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(2) redemption through His atoning and redeeming sacrifice (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/jo1004.htm#001">Jo1 4:1-3</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/phi003.htm#018">Phi 3:18</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/pe2002.htm#001">Pe2 2:1</a>). Apostasy differs from error concerning truth, which may be the result of ignorance (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/act019.htm#001">Act 19:1-6</a>) or heresy, which may be due to the sphere of Satan (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/ti2002.htm#025">Ti2 2:25</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/ti2002.htm#026">Ti2 2:26</a>) both of which may consist with true faith. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>The apostate is perfectly described in (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/ti2004.htm#003">Ti2 4:3</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/ti2004.htm#004">Ti2 4:4</a>). Apostates depart from the faith, but not from the outward profession of Christianity (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/ti2003.htm#005">Ti2 3:5</a>). Apostate teachers are described in; (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/ti2004.htm#003">Ti2 4:3</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/pe2002.htm#001">Pe2 2:1-19</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/jde001.htm#004">Jde 1:4</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/jde001.htm#008">Jde 1:8</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/jde001.htm#011">Jde 1:11-13</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/jde001.htm#016">Jde 1:16</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Apostasy in the church, as in Israel (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/isa001.htm#005">Isa 1:5</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/isa001.htm#006">Isa 1:6</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/isa005.htm#005">Isa 5:5-7</a>) is irremediable, and awaits judgment; (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/th2002.htm#010">Th2 2:10-12</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/pe2002.htm#017">Pe2 2:17</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/pe2002.htm#021">Pe2 2:21</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/jde001.htm#011">Jde 1:11-15</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rev003.htm#014">Rev 3:14-16</a>).
