 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Scofield Bible Commentary</i>, by Cyrus Ingerson Scofield, [1917], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze025.htm#001">Ezekiel Chapter 25</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze025.htm#008">Ezekiel 25:8</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_008">eze 25:8</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>Thus 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>The prophecies upon Gentile powers, extending to (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze032.htm#032">Eze 32:32</a>) have doubtless had partial fulfilments of which history and the present condition of those cities and countries bear witness, but the mention of the day of Jehovah (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/eze030.htm#003">Eze 30:3</a>) makes it evident that a fulfilment in the final sense is still future. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>See "Day of Jehovah" (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/isa002.htm#010">Isa 2:10-22</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rev019.htm#019">Rev 19:19</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Also "Armageddon" (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rev016.htm#014">Rev 16:14</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rev019.htm#017">Rev 19:17</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Those countries are once more to be the battle ground of the nations.
