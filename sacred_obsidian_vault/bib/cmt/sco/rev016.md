 </p><p>Babylon 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Summary: 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>The Times of the Gentiles is that long period beginning with the Babylonian captivity of Judah, under Nebuchadnezzar, and to be brought to an end by the destruction of Gentile world-power by the "stone cut out without hands", (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/dan002.htm#034">Dan 2:34</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/dan002.htm#035">Dan 2:35</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/dan002.htm#044">Dan 2:44</a>), that is, the coming of the Lord in glory (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rev019.htm#011">Rev 19:11</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rev019.htm#021">Rev 19:21</a>) until which time Jerusalem is politically subject to Gentile rule. (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk021.htm#024">Luk 21:24</a>).
