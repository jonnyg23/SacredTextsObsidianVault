 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Scofield Bible Commentary</i>, by Cyrus Ingerson Scofield, [1917], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/co1005.htm#001">1 Corinthians Chapter 5</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/co1005.htm#002">1 Corinthians 5:2</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_002">co1 5:2</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>And ye are puffed up 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>What contempt this pours upon the divisions among the Corinthians: "Apollonians," and "Paulinians," and "Cephasites," all alike indifferent to this instance of gross sin!  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/co1005.htm#005">1 Corinthians 5:5</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_005">co1 5:5</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>destruction 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(Greek, "olethros", used elsewhere, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/th1005.htm#003">Th1 5:3</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/th2001.htm#009">Th2 1:9</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/ti1006.htm#009">Ti1 6:9</a>); never means "annihilation"). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>
 </p><p>saved 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom001.htm#016">Rom 1:16</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Jesus 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Some ancient authorities omit "Jesus."  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/co1005.htm#010">1 Corinthians 5:10</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_010">co1 5:10</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>world 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Greek, "kosmos", means "mankind". 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat004.htm#008">Mat 4:8</a>).
