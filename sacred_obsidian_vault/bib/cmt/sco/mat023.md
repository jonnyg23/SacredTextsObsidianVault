 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Scofield Bible Commentary</i>, by Cyrus Ingerson Scofield, [1917], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat023.htm#001">Matthew Chapter 23</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat023.htm#002">Matthew 23:2</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_002">mat 23:2</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>sit in 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Compare (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/ezr007.htm#006">Ezr 7:6</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/ezr007.htm#025">Ezr 7:25</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/ezr007.htm#026">Ezr 7:26</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Jesus' disciples were to honour the law, but not the hypocritical teachers of it.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat023.htm#005">Matthew 23:5</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_005">mat 23:5</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>phylacteries 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Passages of Scripture enclosed in a small case, bound upon arm or forehead, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/deu006.htm#008">Deu 6:8</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat023.htm#014">Matthew 23:14</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_014">mat 23:14</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>Woe 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>The best manuscripts omit verse 14. (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat023.htm#014">Mat 23:14</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat023.htm#015">Matthew 23:15</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_015">mat 23:15</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>hell 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Gehenna. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat005.htm#022">Mat 5:22</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat023.htm#016">Matthew 23:16</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_016">mat 23:16</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>debtor 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Or, bound; also (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat023.htm#018">Mat 23:18</a>). "guilty:"  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat023.htm#028">Matthew 23:28</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_028">mat 23:28</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>iniquity 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Literally, Lawlessness. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom003.htm#023">Rom 3:23</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat023.htm#034">Matthew 23:34</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_034">mat 23:34</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>send unto you 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>The Jews' treatment of the apostles is proved, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat023.htm#031">Mat 23:31-33</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat023.htm#035">Matthew 23:35</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_035">mat 23:35</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>Abel 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Jesus' confirmation of (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/gen004.htm#008">Gen 4:8-10</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Compare (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/heb012.htm#024">Heb 12:24</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat023.htm#036">Matthew 23:36</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_036">mat 23:36</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>come upon 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>See (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rev018.htm#021">Rev 18:21-24</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>It is the way also of history; judgment falls upon one generation for the sins of centuries. The pediction was fulfilled in the destruction of Jerusalem, A.D. 70.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat023.htm#039">Matthew 23:39</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_039">mat 23:39</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>till 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>The three "untils" of Israel's blessing: 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(1) Israel must say, "blessed is He" (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat023.htm#039">Mat 23:39</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom010.htm#003">Rom 10:3</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom010.htm#004">Rom 10:4</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(2) Gentile world-power must run its course. (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk021.htm#024">Luk 21:24</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/dan002.htm#034">Dan 2:34</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/dan002.htm#035">Dan 2:35</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(3) The elect number of Gentiles must be brought in. Then "the Deliverer shall come out of Zion." etc. (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom011.htm#025">Rom 11:25-27</a>).  
 </p><p></p><div class="filenav">
