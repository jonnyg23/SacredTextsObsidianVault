 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/job011.htm#001">Job 11:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">job 11:1</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>Zophar 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Zophar is a religious dogmatist who assumes to know all about God; what God will do in any given case, why He will do it, and all His thoughts about it. Of all forms of dogmatism this is most irreverent, and least open to reason.
