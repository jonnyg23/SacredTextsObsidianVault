 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Scofield Bible Commentary</i>, by Cyrus Ingerson Scofield, [1917], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/th1004.htm#001">1 Thessalonians Chapter 4</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/th1004.htm#017">1 Thessalonians 4:17</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_017">th1 4:17</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>caught up 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Not church saints only, but all bodies of the saved, of whatever dispensation, are included in the first resurrection 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/co1015.htm#052">Co1 15:52</a>) 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>as here described, but it is peculiarly the "blessed hope" of the Church. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Compare (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat024.htm#042">Mat 24:42</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat025.htm#013">Mat 25:13</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk012.htm#036">Luk 12:36-48</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/act001.htm#011">Act 1:11</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/phi003.htm#020">Phi 3:20</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/phi003.htm#021">Phi 3:21</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/tit002.htm#011">Tit 2:11-13</a>).
