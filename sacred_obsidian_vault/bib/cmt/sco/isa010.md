 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Scofield Bible Commentary</i>, by Cyrus Ingerson Scofield, [1917], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/isa010.htm#001">Isaiah Chapter 10</a></h3> 
 <p>
 </p><p>that when 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>A permanent method in the divine government of the earth. Israel is always the centre of the divine counsels earthward (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/deu032.htm#008">Deu 32:8</a>). The Gentile nations are permitted to afflict Israel in chastisement for her national sins, but invariably and inevitably retribution falls upon them. See; (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/gen015.htm#013">Gen 15:13</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/gen015.htm#014">Gen 15:14</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/deu030.htm#005">Deu 30:5-7</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/isa014.htm#001">Isa 14:1</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/isa014.htm#002">Isa 14:2</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/joe003.htm#001">Joe 3:1-8</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mic005.htm#007">Mic 5:7-9</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat025.htm#031">Mat 25:31-40</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/isa010.htm#020">Isaiah 10:20</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_020">isa 10:20</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>that day 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>"That day": often the equivalent of "the day of the Lord" (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/isa002.htm#010">Isa 2:10-22</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rev019.htm#011">Rev 19:11-21</a>). The prophecy here passes from the general to the particular, from historic and fulfilled judgments upon Assyria to the final destruction of all Gentile world-power at the return of the Lord in glory. (See "Armageddon,"; (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rev016.htm#014">Rev 16:14</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rev019.htm#021">Rev 19:21</a>) "Times of the Gentiles,"; (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk021.htm#024">Luk 21:24</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rev016.htm#019">Rev 16:19</a>); "The great tribulation,"; (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa002.htm#005">Psa 2:5</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rev007.htm#014">Rev 7:14</a>); and 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/isa013.htm#019">Isa 13:19</a>).
