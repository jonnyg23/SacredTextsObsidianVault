 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Scofield Bible Commentary</i>, by Cyrus Ingerson Scofield, [1917], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/mar012.htm#001">Mark Chapter 12</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/mar012.htm#001">Mark 12:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">mar 12:1</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>vineyard 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Israel. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(<a href="../../../bib/kjv/isa005.htm#001">Isa 5:1-7</a>). Israel was not fruitless, but brought forth only wild grapes. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Compare (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/joh003.htm#006">Joh 3:6</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/hos010.htm#001">Hos 10:1</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Contrast (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/hos014.htm#008">Hos 14:8</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/mar012.htm#002">Mark 12:2</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_002">mar 12:2</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>sent to 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mar012.htm#002">Mar 12:2-5</a>); The prophets and John the Baptist.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/mar012.htm#006">Mark 12:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">mar 12:6</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>son 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Jesus Himself. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Compare (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/heb001.htm#001">Heb 1:1-3</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/mar012.htm#009">Mark 12:9</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_009">mar 12:9</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>destroy 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Fulfilled in the destruction of Jerusalem, A.D. 70. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Compare (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk021.htm#020">Luk 21:20-24</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/mar012.htm#010">Mark 12:10</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_010">mar 12:10</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>The stone 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>See (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa118.htm#022">Psa 118:22</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa118.htm#023">Psa 118:23</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Compare (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/pe1002.htm#008">Pe1 2:8</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/mar012.htm#011">Mark 12:11</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_011">mar 12:11</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>Lord's 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Jehovah. Verses (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mar010.htm#010">Mar 10:10</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mar010.htm#011">Mar 10:11</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa118.htm#022">Psa 118:22</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa118.htm#023">Psa 118:23</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/mar012.htm#018">Mark 12:18</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_018">mar 12:18</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>Sadducees 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat003.htm#007">Mat 3:7</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Also (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mar012.htm#022">Mar 12:22-23</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/mar012.htm#024">Mark 12:24</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_024">mar 12:24</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>said unto them 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>See margin reference, (See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat022.htm#029">Mat 22:29</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/mar012.htm#025">Mark 12:25</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_025">mar 12:25</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>from the dead 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Literally, from amongst; 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Compare (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/phi003.htm#011">Phi 3:11</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Here it is the first resurrection. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/co1015.htm#052">Co1 15:52</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>
 </p><p>angels 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/heb001.htm#004">Heb 1:4</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/mar012.htm#026">Mark 12:26</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_026">mar 12:26</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>read in the book 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Jesus affirms the historic truth and inspiration of Exodus 3.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/mar012.htm#034">Mark 12:34</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_034">mar 12:34</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>far 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>That is, not far in knowledge. He knew the very law which utterly condemns the best man -- its true office. (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom003.htm#019">Rom 3:19</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom010.htm#003">Rom 10:3-5</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/gal003.htm#010">Gal 3:10</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/gal003.htm#022">Gal 3:22-24</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/mar012.htm#035">Mark 12:35</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_035">mar 12:35</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>son of 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>That is, David's Son only. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Compare (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom001.htm#003">Rom 1:3</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom001.htm#004">Rom 1:4</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/mar012.htm#036">Mark 12:36</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_036">mar 12:36</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>For David 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Inspiration. (Jesus affirms the inspiration and Davidic authorship of Psa 110) (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat012.htm#036">Mat 12:36</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk001.htm#003">Luk 1:3</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo004.htm#015">Exo 4:15</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rev022.htm#019">Rev 22:19</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>
 </p><p>The Lord 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Jehovah. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>
 </p><p>my Lord 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Adonai, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa110.htm#001">Psa 110:1</a>).
