 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Scofield Bible Commentary</i>, by Cyrus Ingerson Scofield, [1917], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/zac003.htm#001">Zechariah Chapter 3</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/zac003.htm#001">Zechariah 3:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">zac 3:1</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>Joshua 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>The fifth vision discloses: 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(1) The change from self-righteousness to the righteousness of God 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom003.htm#021">Rom 3:21</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>of which Paul's experience, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/phi003.htm#001">Phi 3:1-9</a>) is the illustration, as it is also the foreshadowing of the conversion of Israel. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(2) In type, the preparation of Israel for receiving Jehovah's "BRANCH" 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/isa004.htm#002">Isa 4:2</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>The refusal of the Jews to abandon self-righteousness for the righteousness of God blinded them to the presence of the BRANCH in their midst at His first advent (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom010.htm#001">Rom 10:1-4</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom011.htm#007">Rom 11:7</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom011.htm#008">Rom 11:8</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Compare (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/zac006.htm#012">Zac 6:12-15</a>) which speaks of the manifestation of the BRANCH in glory (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/zac003.htm#013">Zac 3:13</a>) as the Priest-King, when Israel will receive Him. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/heb005.htm#006">Heb 5:6</a>). 
 </p><p>

 </p><p>
