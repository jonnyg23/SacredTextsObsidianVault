 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer039.htm#001">Jeremiah 39:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">jer 39:1</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>tenth month.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer039.htm#002">Jeremiah 39:2</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_002">jer 39:2</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>fourth month 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>That is, July.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer039.htm#007">Jeremiah 39:7</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_007">jer 39:7</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>Babylon 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Here began the "times of the Gentiles," the mark of which is that Jerusalem is "trodden down of the Gentiles," that is, Under Gentile overlordship. This has been true from the time of Nebuchadnezzar to this day. 
 </p><p>
