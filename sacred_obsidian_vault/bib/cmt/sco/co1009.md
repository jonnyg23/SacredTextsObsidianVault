 <p>
 </p><p>sister 
 </p><p>

 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/co1009.htm#011">1 Corinthians 9:11</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_011">co1 9:11</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>carnel 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>That is, things for the body.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/co1009.htm#021">1 Corinthians 9:21</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_021">co1 9:21</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>without 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>The expression is peculiar and might be literally rendered, "not lawless toward God, but inlawed to Christ." 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>See "Law (of Christ)," (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/gal006.htm#002">Gal 6:2</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/jo2001.htm#005">Jo2 1:5</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>It is another way of saying, "not under the law, but under the rule of grace." (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom006.htm#014">Rom 6:14</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/co1009.htm#022">1 Corinthians 9:22</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_022">co1 9:22</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>save 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom001.htm#016">Rom 1:16</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/co1009.htm#024">1 Corinthians 9:24</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_024">co1 9:24</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>race 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Race-course.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/co1009.htm#027">1 Corinthians 9:27</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_027">co1 9:27</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>castaway 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(Greek, "adokimos", "disapproved"). Greek, "dokimos", without the private, "a", is translated "approved" in (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom014.htm#018">Rom 14:18</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom016.htm#010">Rom 16:10</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/co1011.htm#019">Co1 11:19</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/co2010.htm#018">Co2 10:18</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/co2013.htm#007">Co2 13:7</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/ti2002.htm#015">Ti2 2:15</a>); and in (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/jam001.htm#012">Jam 1:12</a>); by the word "tried." The prefix simply changes the word to a negative, that is, not approved, or, disapproved. The apostle is writing of service, not of salvation. He is not expressing fear that he may fail of salvation but of his crown. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>See "Rewards"; (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/dan012.htm#003">Dan 12:3</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/co1003.htm#014">Co1 3:14</a>).
