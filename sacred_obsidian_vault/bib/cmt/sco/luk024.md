 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk024.htm#051">Luke 24:51</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_051">luk 24:51</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>while he blessed them 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>The attitude of our Lord here characterizes this age. It is one of grace; an ascended Lord is blessing a believing people with spiritual blessings. The Jewish age was marked by temporal blessings as the reward of an obedient people. (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/deu028.htm#001">Deu 28:1-15</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>In the kingdom-age, spiritual and temporal blessings unite. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>
 </p><p>heaven 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>The Scriptures distinguish three heavens: 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>first, the lower heavens, or the region of the clouds; 
 </p><p>second, the second or planetary heavens; and, 
 </p><p>third, the heaven of heavens, the abode of God.
