 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ti1003.htm#009">1 Timothy 3:9</a></p>

 <p>
 </p><p>mystery 
 </p><p>(<a href="../../../bib/kjv/ti1003.htm#016">Ti1 3:16</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat013.htm#011">Mat 13:11</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ti1003.htm#015">1 Timothy 3:15</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_015">ti1 3:15</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>church 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Church (visible), Summary: 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>The passages under this head (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/co1010.htm#032">Co1 10:32</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/ti1003.htm#015">Ti1 3:15</a>) refer to that visible body of professed believers called, collectively, "the Church," of which history takes account as such, though it exists under many names and divisions based upon differences in doctrine or in government. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Within, for the most part, this historical "Church" has existed the true Church, "which is his body, the fulness of him that filleth all in all (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/eph001.htm#022">Eph 1:22</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/eph001.htm#023">Eph 1:23</a>); 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/heb012.htm#023">Heb 12:23</a>) 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>like the believing Remnant within Israel. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom011.htm#005">Rom 11:5</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>The predicted future of the visible Church is apostasy (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk018.htm#008">Luk 18:8</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/ti2003.htm#001">Ti2 3:1-8</a>) of the true Church, glory; (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat013.htm#036">Mat 13:36-43</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom008.htm#018">Rom 8:18-23</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/th1004.htm#014">Th1 4:14-17</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/ti1003.htm#016">1 Timothy 3:16</a></p>
