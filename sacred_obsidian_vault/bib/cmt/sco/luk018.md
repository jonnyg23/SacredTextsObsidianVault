 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Scofield Bible Commentary</i>, by Cyrus Ingerson Scofield, [1917], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk018.htm#001">Luke Chapter 18</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk018.htm#008">Luke 18:8</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_008">luk 18:8</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>faith 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>The reference is not to personal faith, but to belief in the whole body of revealed truth. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Compare (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom001.htm#005">Rom 1:5</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/co1016.htm#013">Co1 16:13</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/co2013.htm#005">Co2 13:5</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/col001.htm#023">Col 1:23</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/col002.htm#007">Col 2:7</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/tit001.htm#013">Tit 1:13</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/jde001.htm#003">Jde 1:3</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>See "Apostasy," above, in margin reference of (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk018.htm#008">Luk 18:8</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/ti2003.htm#001">Ti2 3:1</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>

 <p>
 </p><p>merciful 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Greek, "hilaskomai", used in the Septuagint and New Testament in connection with the mercy-seat (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo025.htm#017">Exo 25:17</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo025.htm#018">Exo 25:18</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/exo025.htm#021">Exo 25:21</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/heb009.htm#005">Heb 9:5</a>). As an instructed Jew, the publican is thinking, not of mere mercy, but of the blood-sprinkled mercy seat. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/lev016.htm#005">Lev 16:5</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>"Propitiation," 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom003.htm#025">Rom 3:25</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>His prayer might be paraphrased, "Be toward me as thou are when thou lookest upon the atoning blood." The Bible knows nothing of divine forgiveness apart from sacrifice. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat026.htm#028">Mat 26:28</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>
