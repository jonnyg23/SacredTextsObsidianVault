 <p>
 </p><p>high places 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Compare (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/ch2014.htm#003">Ch2 14:3</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>It appears that local sacrifices to Jehovah (though not according to the divine order) were offered in the times of the kings upon "high places" (cf) (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/sa1009.htm#012">Sa1 9:12</a>). Apparently Asa's mother had defiled one of these with an idol, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/kg1015.htm#013">Kg1 15:13</a>). Asa destroyed the idol and the idolatrous (but not the Jehovistic) "high places." But see "high places,"
