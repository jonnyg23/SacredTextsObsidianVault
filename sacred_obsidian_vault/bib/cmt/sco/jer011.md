 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Scofield Bible Commentary</i>, by Cyrus Ingerson Scofield, [1917], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer011.htm#001">Jeremiah Chapter 11</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer011.htm#001">Jeremiah 11:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">jer 11:1</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>The word 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>This, like the other messages, is made up of rebuke, exhortation, and warning, but in this instance these are based upon the violation of the Palestinian Covenant (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/deu028.htm#001">Deu 28:1-30</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/deu028.htm#009">Deu 28:9</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/deu030.htm#003">Deu 30:3</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>The Assyrian and Babylonian captivities of Israel and of Judah were the execution of the warning, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/deu028.htm#063">Deu 28:63-68</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer011.htm#015">Jeremiah 11:15</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_015">jer 11:15</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>holy flesh 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>See (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/hag002.htm#012">Hag 2:12</a>) that is, to what purpose the "holy flesh" of sacrifices? Its efficacy is "passed from thee" who rejoicest in evil. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Compare (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/isa001.htm#013">Isa 1:13-15</a>).
