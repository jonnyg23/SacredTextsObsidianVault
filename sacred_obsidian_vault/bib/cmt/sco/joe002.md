 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Scofield Bible Commentary</i>, by Cyrus Ingerson Scofield, [1917], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/joe002.htm#001">Joel Chapter 2</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/joe002.htm#011">Joel 2:11</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_011">joe 2:11</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>his army 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>To verse 10 (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/joe002.htm#010">Joe 2:10</a>) inclusive the invading army is described; at verse 11 (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/joe002.htm#011">Joe 2:11</a>) Jehovah's army. This "army" is described, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rev019.htm#011">Rev 19:11-18</a>). The call to repentance is based upon the Lord's promise of deliverance, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/joe002.htm#012">Joe 2:12-17</a>). At verses (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/joe002.htm#018">Joe 2:18-20</a>), we have the deliverance (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/joe002.htm#020">Joe 2:20</a>); see "Armageddon," 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rev016.htm#014">Rev 16:14</a>), 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>and kingdom blessing in verses (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/joe002.htm#021">Joe 2:21-27</a>). Verses 28-32 (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/joe002.htm#028">Joe 2:28-32</a>) give the outpouring of the Spirit, and verses 29-32 (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/joe002.htm#029">Joe 2:29-32</a>), the cosmical signs preceding the day of the Lord. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rev019.htm#011">Rev 19:11</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/joe002.htm#013">Joel 2:13</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_013">joe 2:13</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>repenteth 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/zac008.htm#014">Zac 8:14</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/joe002.htm#028">Joel 2:28</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_028">joe 2:28</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>afterward 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Compare (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/act002.htm#017">Act 2:17</a>) which gives a specific interpretation of "afterward" (Hebrew, "Acherith", means "latter," "last"). "Afterward" in Joel (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/joe002.htm#028">Joe 2:28</a>) means "in the last days" (Greek, "eschatos", and has a partial and continuous fulfilment during the "last days" which began with the first advent of Christ (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/heb001.htm#002">Heb 1:2</a>) but the greater fulfilment awaits the "last days" as applied to Israel. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/act002.htm#017">Act 2:17</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>for phrase, "the last days." 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/act002.htm#017">Act 2:17</a>).
