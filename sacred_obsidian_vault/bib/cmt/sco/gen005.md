 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Scofield Bible Commentary</i>, by Cyrus Ingerson Scofield, [1917], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/gen005.htm#001">Genesis Chapter 5</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/gen005.htm#001">Genesis 5:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">gen 5:1</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>Adam 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Adam, as the natural head of the race (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk003.htm#038">Luk 3:38</a>) is a contrasting type of Christ, the Head of the new creation. See (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom005.htm#014">Rom 5:14</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/co1015.htm#021">Co1 15:21</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/co1015.htm#022">Co1 15:22</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/co1015.htm#045">Co1 15:45-47</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/gen005.htm#022">Genesis 5:22</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_022">gen 5:22</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>Enoch 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Enoch, "translated that he should not see death" (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/heb011.htm#005">Heb 11:5</a>) before the judgment of the Flood, is a type of those saints who are to be translated before the apocalyptic judgments (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/th1004.htm#014">Th1 4:14-17</a>). Noah, left on the earth, but preserved through the judgment of the Flood, is a type of the Jewish people, who will be kept through the apocalyptic judgments; (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer030.htm#005">Jer 30:5-9</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rev012.htm#013">Rev 12:13-16</a>) and brought as an earthly people to the new heaven and new earth; (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/isa065.htm#017">Isa 65:17-19</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/isa066.htm#020">Isa 66:20-22</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rev021.htm#001">Rev 21:1</a>).
