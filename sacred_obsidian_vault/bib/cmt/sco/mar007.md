 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Scofield Bible Commentary</i>, by Cyrus Ingerson Scofield, [1917], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/mar007.htm#001">Mark Chapter 7</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/mar007.htm#005">Mark 7:5</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_005">mar 7:5</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>tradition 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>That is, The so-called "oral law" alleged to have been handed down from Moses; really a traditional interpretation of the written law. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>See margin reference, (See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mar007.htm#007">Mar 7:7</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/mar007.htm#007">Mark 7:7</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_007">mar 7:7</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>for doctrines 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>as authoritative the precepts of men. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Compare (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mar007.htm#005">Mar 7:5</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>See "Pharisees" (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat003.htm#007">Mat 3:7</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat003.htm#007">Mat 3:7</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Compare (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/col002.htm#008">Col 2:8</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/col002.htm#016">Col 2:16</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/col002.htm#018">Col 2:18</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/col002.htm#020">Col 2:20</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/col002.htm#023">Col 2:23</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/mar007.htm#011">Mark 7:11</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_011">mar 7:11</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>a gift 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Or, I have dedicated to God that which would relieve your need; (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mar007.htm#012">Mar 7:12</a>). No longer do you permit him to use it for his father or mother. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Compare (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat015.htm#005">Mat 15:5</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat015.htm#006">Mat 15:6</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/mar007.htm#019">Mark 7:19</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_019">mar 7:19</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>Because it entereth 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Because it does not enter into the heart of him, but into the bowels is passed -- purifying all the food.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/mar007.htm#026">Mark 7:26</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_026">mar 7:26</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>Greek 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat015.htm#021">Mat 15:21</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/mar007.htm#028">Mark 7:28</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_028">mar 7:28</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>And she 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>She, however, answered, saying, "True, Lord! And yet the little dogs under the table eat from the children's crumbs." (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom011.htm#024">Rom 11:24</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/eph002.htm#011">Eph 2:11-22</a>).
