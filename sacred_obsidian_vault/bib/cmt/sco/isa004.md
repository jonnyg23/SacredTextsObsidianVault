 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Scofield Bible Commentary</i>, by Cyrus Ingerson Scofield, [1917], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/isa004.htm#001">Isaiah Chapter 4</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/isa004.htm#002">Isaiah 4:2</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_002">isa 4:2</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>branch 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>A name of Christ, used in fourfold way: 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(1) "The Branch of Jehovah" (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/isa004.htm#002">Isa 4:2</a>), that is, the "Immanuel" character of Christ (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/isa007.htm#014">Isa 7:14</a>) to be fully manifested to restored and converted Israel after His return in divine glory (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat025.htm#031">Mat 25:31</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(2) the "Branch of David" (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/isa011.htm#001">Isa 11:1</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer023.htm#005">Jer 23:5</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer033.htm#015">Jer 33:15</a>) that is, the Messiah, "of the seed of David according to the flesh" (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom001.htm#003">Rom 1:3</a>) revealed in His earthly glory as King of kings, and Lord of lords; 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(3) Jehovah's "Servant, the Branch" (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/zac003.htm#008">Zac 3:8</a>) Messiah's humiliation and obedience unto death according to; (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/isa052.htm#013">Isa 52:13-15</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/isa053.htm#001">Isa 53:1-12</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/phi002.htm#005">Phi 2:5-8</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(4) the "man whose name is the Branch" (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/zac006.htm#012">Zac 6:12</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/zac006.htm#013">Zac 6:13</a>) that is His character as Son of man, the "last Adam," the "second Man" (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/co1015.htm#045">Co1 15:45-47</a>) reigning, as Priest-King, over the earth in the dominion given to and lost by the first Adam. Matthew is the Gospel of the "Branch of David"; Mark of "Jehovah's Servant, the Branch"; Luke of "the man whose name is the Branch"; John of "the Branch of Jehovah."
