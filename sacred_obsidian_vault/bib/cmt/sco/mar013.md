 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/mar013.htm#002">Mark 13:2</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_002">mar 13:2</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>And Jesus 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat024.htm#003">Mat 24:3</a>) on the Olivet discourse: 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat024.htm#003">Mat 24:3</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/mar013.htm#008">Mark 13:8</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_008">mar 13:8</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>sorrows 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>birthpangs. Answering to the "seals." (Revelation 6) (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rev006.htm#001">Rev 6:1-17</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>The death-agony of this age is the birth-agony of the next.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/mar013.htm#010">Mark 13:10</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_010">mar 13:10</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>And 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Compare (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat024.htm#014">Mat 24:14</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>"Gospel of the Kingdom". 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>See note, (See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rev014.htm#006">Rev 14:6</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/mar013.htm#013">Mark 13:13</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_013">mar 13:13</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>end 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Not the end of the believer's life, but the end of the great tribulation. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>
 </p><p>saved 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>In the sense of (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rev013.htm#008">Rev 13:8</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rev020.htm#004">Rev 20:4</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/mar013.htm#014">Mark 13:14</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_014">mar 13:14</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>when ye 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>See "Beast." (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/dan007.htm#008">Dan 7:8</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rev019.htm#020">Rev 19:20</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>
 </p><p>then let 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Compare (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/luk021.htm#020">Luk 21:20-24</a>); which is a prophecy fulfilled in the destruction of Jerusalem, A.D. 70, when the Christians escaped, and which foreshadowed the more terrible day here described. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>See "Great Tribulation"; (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa002.htm#005">Psa 2:5</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rev007.htm#014">Rev 7:14</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa002.htm#005">Psa 2:5</a>). 
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rev007.htm#014">Rev 7:14</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/mar013.htm#019">Mark 13:19</a></p>

 <p>
 </p><p>affliction 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Tribulation. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Compare (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat024.htm#021">Mat 24:21</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>See "Tribulation"; (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa002.htm#005">Psa 2:5</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rev007.htm#014">Rev 7:14</a>).

 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat008.htm#020">Mat 8:20</a>).
