 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_000">lam 0:0</a></font></span></p>
 <p>Book Introduction - Lamentations
 </p><p>The touching significance of this book lies in the fact that it is the disclosure of the love and sorrow of Jehovah for the very people who He is chastening--a sorrow wrought by the Spirit in the heart of Jeremiah (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/jer013.htm#017">Jer 13:17</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat023.htm#036">Mat 23:36</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/mat023.htm#038">Mat 23:38</a>; <a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom009.htm#001">Rom 9:1-5</a>). 
 </p><p>The chapters indicate the analysis, viz., five lamentations.
