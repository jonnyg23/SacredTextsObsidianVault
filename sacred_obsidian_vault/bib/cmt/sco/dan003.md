 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Scofield Bible Commentary</i>, by Cyrus Ingerson Scofield, [1917], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/dan003.htm#001">Daniel Chapter 3</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/dan003.htm#001">Daniel 3:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">dan 3:1</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>an image of gold 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>The attempt of this great king of Babylon to unify the religions of his empire by self-deification will be repeated by the beast, the last head of the Gentile world-dominion (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rev013.htm#011">Rev 13:11-15</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>See note on "Beast, the" 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/dan007.htm#008">Dan 7:8</a>). 
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rev019.htm#020">Rev 19:20</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>It has repeatedly characterized Gentile authority in the earth, for example; (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/dan006.htm#007">Dan 6:7</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/act012.htm#022">Act 12:22</a>) and the later Roman emperors.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/dan003.htm#008">Daniel 3:8</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_008">dan 3:8</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>Chaldeans 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Compare the conduct of Daniel, (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/dan002.htm#024">Dan 2:24</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/dan003.htm#017">Daniel 3:17</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_017">dan 3:17</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>and he will deliver us 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>The three Jews, faithful to God while the nation of Israel far from their land bear no testimony, are a fit type of the Jewish remnant in the last days (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/isa001.htm#009">Isa 1:9</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom011.htm#005">Rom 11:5</a>) who will be faithful in the furnace of the great tribulation; (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/psa002.htm#005">Psa 2:5</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rev007.htm#014">Rev 7:14</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/dan003.htm#028">Daniel 3:28</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_028">dan 3:28</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>angel 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/heb001.htm#004">Heb 1:4</a>).
