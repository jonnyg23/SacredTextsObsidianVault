 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><i>The Scofield Bible Commentary</i>, by Cyrus Ingerson Scofield, [1917], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/gal006.htm#001">Galatians Chapter 6</a></h3> 
 <p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/gal006.htm#001">Galatians 6:1</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_001">gal 6:1</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>fault 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>That is, sin. 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rom003.htm#023">Rom 3:23</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/gal006.htm#006">Galatians 6:6</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_006">gal 6:6</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>communicate 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Or, share with him. (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/co1009.htm#007">Co1 9:7-15</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/ti1005.htm#018">Ti1 5:18</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/gal006.htm#011">Galatians 6:11</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_011">gal 6:11</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>large 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Greek, "with how large letters ... mine own hand." The apostle was, it appears from many considerations, afflicted with ophthalmia, a common disease in the East, to the point almost of total blindness, (for example), (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/gal004.htm#013">Gal 4:13-15</a>). Ordinarily, therefore, he dictated his letters. But now, having no amanuensis at hand, but urged by the spiritual danger of his dear Galatians, he writes, we cannot know with what pain and difficulty, with his own hand, in the "large letters" his darkened vision compelled him to use.  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/gal006.htm#012">Galatians 6:12</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_012">gal 6:12</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>constrain you to be circumcised 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>"Circumcision" stands here for externality in religion -- form rather than spirit. (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/col002.htm#016">Col 2:16-23</a>).  
 </p><p align="center"><a href="../../../bib/kjv/gal006.htm#014">Galatians 6:14</a></p> 
 <p><span class="margnote"><font color="GREEN" size="-1"><a name="an_014">gal 6:14</a></font></span></p>
 <p>
 </p><p>world 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>Greek, "kosmos", means "world-system". (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/eph002.htm#002">Eph 2:2</a>); (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/joh007.htm#007">Joh 7:7</a>). 
 </p><p>
 </p><p>(See Scofield) - (<a href="../../../bib/kjv/rev013.htm#008">Rev 13:8</a>).
