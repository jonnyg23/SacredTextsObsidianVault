 </i><p align="CENTER">Joy and light.</p> 
 <p>1 To the blessed there is joy from their hearts, and light from Him that dwells in them:<br> 2 And words from the Truth, who was self-originate: for He is strengthened by the holy power of the Most High: and He is unperturbed for ever and ever. Hallelujah.</p>
