 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Forgotten Books of Eden</i>, by Rutherford H. Platt, Jr., [1926], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="CENTER">LV.</h3> <i>
 </i><p>Here Enoch shows his sons, telling them with tears: 'My children, the hour has approached for me to go up on to heaven; behold, the angels are standing before me.'</p> 
 <p>MY children, behold, the day of my term and the time nave approached.</p> 
 <p>2 For the angels who shall go with me are standing before me and urge me to my departure from you; they are standing here on earth, awaiting what has been told them.</p> 
 <p>3 For to-morrow I shall go up on to heaven, to the uppermost Jerusalem to my eternal inheritance.</p> 
 <p>4 Therefore I bid you do before the Lord's face all <i>his</i> good pleasure.</p>
