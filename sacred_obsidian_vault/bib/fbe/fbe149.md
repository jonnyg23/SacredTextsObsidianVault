 </i><p align="CENTER">Of how Enoch saw the key-holders and guards of the gates of hell standing.</p> 
 <p>SAW the key-holders and guards of the gates of hell standing, like great serpents, and their faces like extinguished lamps, and their eyes of fire, their sharp teeth, and I saw all the Lord's works, how they are right, while the works of man are some <i>good</i>, and others bad, and in their works are known those who lie evilly.</p>
