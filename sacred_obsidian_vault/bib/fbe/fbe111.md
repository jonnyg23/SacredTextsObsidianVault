 <p><a name="page_83"><font size="1" color="green">p. 83</font></a></p> <h3 align="CENTER">IV.</h3> <i>
 </i><p align="CENTER">Of the Angels ruling the stars.</p> 
 <p>THEY brought before my face the elders and rulers of the stellar orders, and showed me two hundred angels, who rule the stars and <i>their</i> services to the heavens, and fly with their wings and come round all those who sail.</p>
