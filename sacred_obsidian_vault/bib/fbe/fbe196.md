 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg"></a><br><i>The Forgotten Books of Eden</i>, by Rutherford H. Platt, Jr., [1926], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr> <h3 align="CENTER">ODE 1.</h3> 
 <p>1 The Lord is on my head like a crown, and I shall not be without Him.<br> 2 They wove for me a crown of truth, and it caused thy branches to bud in me.<br> 3 For it is not like a withered crown which buddeth not: but thou livest upon my head, and thou hast blossomed upon my head.<br> 4 Thy fruits are full-grown and perfect, they are full of thy salvation.</p>
