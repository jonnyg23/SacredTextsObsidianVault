 </i><p align="CENTER">A strange little Ode.</p> 
 <p>1 Behold! the Lord is our mirror: open the eyes and see them in Him: and learn the manner of your face:<br> 2 And tell forth praise to His spirit: and wipe off the filth from your face: and love His holiness and clothe yourselves therewith:<br> 3 And be without stain at all times before Him. Hallelujah.</p>
