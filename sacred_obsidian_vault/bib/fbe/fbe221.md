 </i><p>The human body makes a cross when a man stands erect in prayer with arms outstretched.</p> 
 <p>1 I stretched out my hands and sanctified my Lord:<br> 2 For the extension of my hands is His sign:<br> 3 And my expansion is the upright tree [or cross].</p>
