 <p align="CENTER"><font size="-1" color="GREEN"><a href="index.htm"><img src="img/tease.jpg" border="0"></a><br><i>The Complete Sayings of Jesus</i>, by Arthur Hinds, [1927], at sacred-texts.com</font></p><hr>  <h3 align="center">C</h3>  <h3 align="center">TO THE CHURCH IN SARDIS—"BE WATCHFUL: I WILL COME AS A THIEF"</h3>  
 <p align="center">A.D. 96.</p>  
 <p align="center"><font size="-1">Revelation 3, 1-6.</font></p>  
 <p></p><div class="jesussaid">AND unto the angel of the church in Sardis write;  
 <p>These things saith he that hath the seven Spirits of God, and the seven stars;</p>  
 <p>I know thy works, that thou hast a name that thou livest, and art dead. Be watchful, and strengthen the things which remain, that are ready to die: for I have not found thy works perfect before God.</p>  
 <p>Remember therefore how thou hast received and heard, and hold fast, and repent. If therefore thou shalt not watch, I will come on thee as a thief, and thou shalt not know what hour I will come upon thee.</p>  
 <p>Thou hast a few names even in Sardis which have not defiled their garments; and they shall walk with me in white; for they are worthy. He that overcometh, the same shall be clothed in white raiment; and</p></div>  
 <p><a name="page_155"><font size="1" color="green">p. 155</font></a></p>  
 <p></p><div class="jesussaid"><span class="contnote"><font size="-2" color="GREEN">[paragraph continues]</font></span> I will not blot out his name out of the book of life, but I will confess his name before my Father, and before his angels.</div>  
 <p>He that hath an ear, let him hear what the Spirit saith unto the churches.</p>
