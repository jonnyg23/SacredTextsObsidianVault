 <p><a name="page_153"><font size="1" color="green">p. 153</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">XCVII</h3>  <h3 align="center">TO THE CHURCH IN SMYRNA—"BE THOU FAITHFUL UNTO DEATH"</h3>  
 <p align="center">A.D. 96</p>  
 <p align="center"><font size="-1">Revelation 2, 8-11.</font></p>  
 <p></p><div class="jesussaid">AND unto the angel of the church in Smyrna write;  
 <p>These things saith the first and the last, which was dead, and is alive;</p>  
 <p>I know thy works, and tribulation, and poverty (but thou art rich); and <i>I know</i> the blasphemy of them which say they are Jews, and are not, but <i>are</i> the synagogue of Satan.</p>  
 <p>Fear none of those things which thou shalt suffer: behold, the devil shall cast <i>some</i> of you into prison, that ye may be tried; and ye shall have tribulation ten days: be thou faithful unto death, and I will give thee a crown of life.</p></div>  
 <p>He that hath an ear, let him hear what the Spirit saith unto the churches;</p>  
 <p></p><div class="jesussaid">He that overcometh shall not be hurt of the second death.</div>
