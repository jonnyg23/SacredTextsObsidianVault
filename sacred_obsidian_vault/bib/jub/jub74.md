 <p><a name="page_184"><font size="1" color="GREEN">p. 184</font></a></p>  <h3 align="center">The Kings of Edom (xxxviii. 15-24; cf. Gen. xxxvi. 31-39).</h3>  
 <p>15. And these are the kings that reigned in Edom before there reigned any king over the children of Israel [until this day] in the land of Edom. 16. And Bâlâq, <a name="fr_830"></a><a href="#fn_822"><font size="1">1</font></a> the son of Beor, reigned in Edom, and the name of his city was Danâbâ. <a name="fr_831"></a><a href="#fn_823"><font size="1">2</font></a> 17. And Bâlâq died, and Jobab, the son of Zârâ of Bôsêr, <a name="fr_832"></a><a href="#fn_824"><font size="1">3</font></a> reigned in his stead. 18. And Jobab died, and Asâm, <a name="fr_833"></a><a href="#fn_825"><font size="1">4</font></a> of the land of Têmân, reigned in his stead. 19. And Asâm died, and Adâth, <a name="fr_834"></a><a href="#fn_826"><font size="1">5</font></a> the son of Barad, <a name="fr_835"></a><a href="#fn_827"><font size="1">6</font></a> who slew Midian in the field of Moab, reigned in his stead, and the name of his city was Avith. 20. And Adâth died, and Salman, <a name="fr_836"></a><a href="#fn_828"><font size="1">7</font></a> from Amâsêqâ, <a name="fr_837"></a><a href="#fn_829"><font size="1">8</font></a> reigned in his stead. 21. And Salman died, and Saul of Râabôth <a name="fr_838"></a><a href="#fn_830"><font size="1">9</font></a> (by the) river, reigned in his stead. 22. And Saul died, and Baêlûnân, <a name="fr_839"></a><a href="#fn_831"><font size="1">10</font></a> the son of Achbor, reigned in his stead. 23. And Baêlûnân, the son of Achbor, died, and Adâth <a name="fr_840"></a><a href="#fn_832"><font size="1">11</font></a> reigned in his stead, and the name of his wife was Maiṭabîth, <a name="fr_841"></a><a href="#fn_833"><font size="1">12</font></a> the daughter of Mâṭarat, <a name="fr_842"></a><a href="#fn_834"><font size="1">13</font></a> the daughter of Mêtabêdzâab. <a name="fr_843"></a><a href="#fn_835"><font size="1">14</font></a> 24. These are the kings who reigned in the land of Edom.</p>
