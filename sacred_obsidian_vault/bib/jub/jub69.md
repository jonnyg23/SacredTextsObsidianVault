   <h3 align="center">The Wives of Jacob's Sons (xxxiv. 20-21).</h3>  
 <p>20. And after Joseph perished, the sons of Jacob took unto themselves wives. The name of Reuben's wife is Adâ <a name="fr_797"></a><a href="#fn_790"><font size="1">1</font></a> and the name of Simeon's wife is Adîbâa, a Canaanite; <a name="fr_798"></a><a href="#fn_790"><font size="1">1</font></a> and the name of Levi's wife is Mêlkâ, <a name="fr_799"></a><a href="#fn_791"><font size="1">2</font></a> of the daughters of Aram, of the seed of the sons of Terah; and the name of Judah's wife, Bêtasûêl, <a name="fr_800"></a><a href="#fn_792"><font size="1">3</font></a> a Canaanite; and the name of Issachar's wife, Hêzaqâ; and the name of Zebulon's wife, †Nîîmân†; <a name="fr_801"></a><a href="#fn_793"><font size="1">4</font></a> and the name of Dan's wife, Êglâ; and the name of Naphtali's wife, Rasûû, of Mesopotamia; and the name of Gad's wife, Mâka; and the name of Asher's wife, Îjônâ; and the name of Joseph's wife, Asenath, <a name="fr_802"></a><a href="#fn_794"><font size="1">5</font></a> the Egyptian; and the name of Benjamin's wife, Îjasaka. 21. And Simeon repented, and took a second wife from Mesopotamia as his brothers.</p>
